Full HD camcorder Toshiba Camileo S20



What interests us most when buying a video camera? Picture quality, usability, weight, size and design. Well, the price, of course. Recently, against the backdrop of the great success of Sony Bloggie, a large number of similar models of very different designs have appeared.
One of these cameras is the Toshiba Camileo S20. I should note that Toshiba has released the widest range of such devices. Then why this one?

The most compact and lightweight camera with the ability to record in FullHD resolution


Technically, everything is extremely simple: a 5-megapixel CMOS matrix with a fixed focal length, a 3-inch LCD flicker, a connector for SD (HC) cards and a Nokia BL4C battery. Available connectors: HDMI, RCA (standard AV), USB. And, actually, everything.



How does all this work and what allows you to do?


The lack of optics as such indicates that there is only a digital zoom that works on resolutions less than the maximum possible.
