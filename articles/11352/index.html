Simulator burrowing pimples

There are some activities that are hard to break away from, for example, eating peanuts or snapping pimples from plastic packaging. The extrusion of air from small air cavities is accompanied by a fascinating sound, it is practically impossible to distract until you burst the last pimple.


Especially for fans of this process, the Japanese company Bandai presented the latest technological achievement Bubble Wrap Simulator (simulator of pimples). This small keychain can replace more than one square meter of packaging! When you press a plastic button with a characteristic shape, the sound of a bursting pimple is made. And then, taking the keys to the front door, it will be possible to stand on the street for a good few hours, until the dream finally falls to its feet.
