What devices do 2can owners use?

Hello! Since we are working with a small business and our technology startup, we have on hand some statistics on the use of mobile devices. And we thought that you would also be interested to see how many and what devices are used with our terminals. The information was curious. We give it in the form of a light infographic.



While American Square mainly displays Apple devices in its promotional materials, our statistics demonstrate the dominance of smartphones and tablets based on Android in Russia. Of course, this is the first data, rather based on the experience of using 2can mobile terminals by early technology followers. It may well be that the situation will change when there are tens of thousands of customers, not thousands. Hmm ... Or will it not change?
