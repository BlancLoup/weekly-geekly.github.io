Who want to be a millionaire?

In Balakovo a payment terminal was stolen from the store. As it turned out, the intruders had a simple and even brilliant plan:

the device was stolen to transfer money to pre-created virtual wallets registered in other regions of the country. According to the preliminary version, the attackers spent about a thousand times through a machine, a thousand-ruled bill, which resulted in a total of 1 million rubles in various virtual wallets.


But what more paradoxically, the entire office was burned by a certain resident of Samara, on whose mobile phone, apparently by mistake, 1 thousand rubles was transferred, which she immediately reported to the police.

But there are still so-called street terminals that are simply attached to the wall or to the pole. It's amazing that this is the first known case, to withdraw electronic money without a trace is quite simple, if you know how ...
