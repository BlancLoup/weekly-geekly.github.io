WebMoney and Yandex.Money: not again, but again

From today, the long-awaited direct exchange between the leading payment systems WebMoney and Yandex.Money has been earned. Now, those wishing to exchange one for the other do not need to stretch their left foot to the right ear and perform such complex and unreliable manipulations. More under the cut.

The exchange works on a new algorithm, it is available only to trusted users of both systems. That is, those clients who received a formal certificate (or higher) in WebMoney and identified in Yandex.Money can exchange funds. Immutable exchange rule: a person who transfers money from one payment system to another must be the same person. Roughly speaking, money can be transferred only to oneself, and not to anyone else.

How does the binding itself go? You go to the site of one of the payment systems ( banks.webmoney.ru or money.yandex.ru ), perform a series of sequential actions - log in, specify the number of the WebMoney ruble wallet and Yandex.Money accounts, get a special code with which you confirm the link to sites of payment systems. After that, the payment systems check your passport information in order to make sure that the sender and the recipient of the money are one and the same person.

After establishing a direct gateway, funds from Yandex.Money to WebMoney and back can be transferred instantly and safely. To simplify the work in the WebMoney Keeper client application and in the Yandex.Money user interface, you will automatically receive icons for fast exchange of funds. A commission of 4.5% of the amount transferred to one or the other is charged for the exchange transaction.

As for the exchange points. Since the exchange and withdrawal of funds in favor of third parties is prohibited in the WebMoney system, in the case of exchanging WebMoney to Yandex.Money exchange points will play the role of an intermediary. Those. using a special interface, they will be able to exchange electronic money as before, but only to a verified user from his account in one system, for his account in another system.
