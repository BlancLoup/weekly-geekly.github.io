Meetup Stack Overflow in Moscow

Good day!

I found out tonight that the next Stack Overflow Meetup will take place this year in Moscow.

The event is scheduled for April 28, meeting on Pushkin Square. You can get more detailed information about the event, as well as register for it, on the official meetup page in Moscow.

In this regard, I propose to discuss the format of the meeting. In my opinion, it would be great if everyone could have 2-3 minutes, so that he could tell about the most interesting (and difficult) situation that he encountered during development. Well, a few full reports also do not interfere.
