This service, as the name implies, works with RSS. How can he be useful?

Even without registering, you can get enough interesting information about the RSS of any blog. You can find out how many backlinks were received from any article from any blogger. For example, the post 5 principles of effective web design received 42 backlinks, which can be seen in the screenshot:



The most interesting opportunity in my opinion is that you can only subscribe to the best posts! Those. You will receive only the best AideRSS posts in any RSS reader.

If you have accumulated a lot of unread posts, or you decide to clean the rss-reader, then the service here will also help. You can import your opml file into it, and the service will show which posts are the most popular. It is also possible to get an opml-file that will be configured to receive only the most popular posts from the blogs you read.
