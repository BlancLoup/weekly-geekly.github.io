Nexus 4 reappeared on sale from $ 300

Just an hour ago, sales of the smartphone from Google, Nexus 4, began again in the US. For more than a month, the phone was unavailable for sale in the Google Play online store. Demand for the Nexus 4 has remained steadily high for the past three months, and the first batch was sold out in less than 1 hour.





At the moment, the device can be ordered in the US and Germany. In Germany, the price is higher.



I bought a 16 GB version, so this is the receipt. Delivery only in the USA, will be sent to the intermediary. For the purchase used the American VPN www.tunnelbear.com



In Russia, the price of the device is 19,990 p.
The Nexus 4 smartphone comes with the new Android 4.2 Jelly Bean operating system. The 4.7-inch True HD IPS Plus screen with a resolution of 1280x768 does not blind under direct sunlight, there is a protective glass display Corning Gorilla Glass 2, which prevents the appearance of scratches. The smartphone is equipped with a Qualcomm 4-core Snapdragon S4 Pro 4-core processor with an Adreno 320 graphics core, 2 GB of RAM, an 8-megapixel main camera, a 1.3-megapixel front camera, GPS module, NFC and wireless charging.
