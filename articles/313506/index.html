Power OK: Schneider Electric Galaxy G300

Any company, regardless of its size, working with critical applications, needs reliable power protection in order to avoid data loss in unforeseen situations.

The Galaxy G300 power protection system is a simple and reliable solution that allows you to effectively protect the important equipment of small and medium-sized enterprises, commercial buildings and technical facilities. The solution is designed to ensure the protection of power supply organizations and is characterized by ease of installation, as well as the optimal ratio of price and functionality. It is worth noting that in the new line modifications are available with the possibility of using in power supply systems for emergency lighting and smoke removal.


Galaxy G300 has a built-in galvanic isolation output transformer, which allows reaching an efficiency level of 90.5%. This indicator of efficiency is higher than that of other power protection systems with similar characteristics. This makes it possible to reduce operating and cooling costs in the medium and long term.

In the architecture of the Galaxy G300, a double transformation topology is applied, which provides high voltage quality for the load. Increased availability of power is achieved by the galvanic isolation transformer already mentioned, as well as by an internal mechanical bypass. The latter allows you to protect the equipment not only from power failures, but also from possible problems in the UPS itself. These features of the device in combination with a compact design and ease of installation make it the optimal choice for customers who need a three-phase UPS in the power range of 10 - 80 kVA.

The Galaxy G300 can be remotely controlled via the Web / SNMP interface. Convenient graphic display supports 18 languages, including Russian. The simplicity of UPS maintenance is additionally provided by front access to the device - this is an additional advantage of the solution in the limited space of the server room. The case of the model has increased strength, in addition, the UPS is equipped with an anti-dust filter - this allows you to use the Galaxy G300 in conditions close to extreme. For convenience of power distribution, configurations with a three-phase input and a three-phase output, as well as a three-phase input and a single-phase output are offered.
