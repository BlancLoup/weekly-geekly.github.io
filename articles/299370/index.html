What IT specialists are currently in demand in Germany, the Netherlands and Malta (part 2)

More than a month has passed since the publication of the first part of the demand review for IT specialists in Germany.

What's new:
  • Added countries: the Netherlands and Malta.
  • Companies start using ItRabota services (without intermediaries).

Demand by country:
  • Germany:
  • Senior / Middle: PHP, JavaScript, Java, NodeJs, iOS / Andorid, .NET, as well as QA and devOps
  • Netherlands:
  • Senior: PHP, JavaScript, Java, NodeJs
  • Malta:
  • Senior / Middle: PHP, JavaScript

Malta will be especially interesting to all who love a lot of sun and sea. Another very important feature of this small island is a sense of security. I am just starting with Malta, and everything will depend on the interest of specialists in this country.

Working directly with companies has a very important advantage - companies are much better than agents know what kind of specialists they are looking for. In addition, agents do not accept candidates who work at the same workplace for less than 2 years. Of course, this may alert the company, but the fact that in the case of working with a project the company will not lay out a full amount for a candidate will substantially smooth out this shortcoming.

Jobs from a specific company from Germany:
Front-end (AngularJS), Java, SysOps / DevOps, QA and Support Engineers. The company's activities are strongly tied to databases and everything connected with it. The level of candidates from the middle and above. Also, some vacancies include business trips to EU countries.
So if databases, SQL, Vagrant, Docker, Ansible, Chef, Puppet sound familiar and if you can find solutions to complex problems, then welcome.
