Mighty Mouse - Squeaky Mouse

Meet
Mighty Mouse - the most unfortunate product from Apple.

Everything annoys me, except for the appearance:
- lack of ergonomics as such (take in your hands at least MX 620 ).
- constantly stuck and stuck scroll, which is quickly clogged with dirt. (I recently ordered to live a long time - I had to google for help. I found an interesting way to clean the dirt from the scroll with a thin strip of scotch, driven inside the mouse)
- terribly uncomfortable side buttons. to press the right one - you need to bend your finger behind the left ear and through the neck with great effort, cheerfully, push by :)
- the revolutionary system "let me guess what button you poke" right now! The mouse consists of one button, under the plastic sensor, which determines which button to trigger - right or left. The sensor is triggered only when the finger falls to the surface, and is not on it. Thus, the chance of triggering the right mouse button comes down to 40% ...

Yes, a handy scroll of 360 degrees (useful thing when working with Photoshop).
Yes, bluetooth.
Yes, white color and apple on the back.

With all the advantages - a terrible device.
100 bucks to the wind.

UPD: At the request of workers lay out a link to the guide for a painless scrolling using a scotch tape.
