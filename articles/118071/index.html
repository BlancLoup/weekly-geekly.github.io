Linux InstallFest May 1st

On May 1, the Linux InstallFest 11.04 Linux and Free Software Festival will be held in Nizhny Novgorod for the 7th time.

As always, the event will act:

  • Installation section, where everyone will get a chair, table, outlet and access to a wi-fi network with a local repository. Anyone can get help from more experienced users of the Nizhny Novgorod Group Linux User (look for people with badges)
  • Presentation section. Here, throughout the day, there will be presentations and master classes on various topics related to Linux and Free Software.
  • Demo area. Here in the free access will be several computers with already installed and configured Linux. If you want to try or ask a question about using Linux - you can safely go there
  • Corner of the 3D Schnick. Here, throughout the day, 3D modeling and animation techniques will be demonstrated in Blender. Master guru Mikhail Soluyanov aka Crantisz


One of the presentations will be a story about the activities of the Nizhny Novgorod Group of Linux Users and a story about planned projects.



The following seminar topics are expected:
Theme Leading
Gnome3 and Unity. What is the difference, what's new, how it will look.
Azovtsev Yuri, NNLUG
How to get a Master of Design degree using Free Software. Personal experience. Demonstration of projects.
Mikhail Soluyanov, studio "Pink Elephant"
Nizhny Novgorod User Group Linux. Who we are, what we have done, our future plans, how to join.
Azovtsev Yuri, NNLUG
Console applications for everyday use
Vlad Zhitnev aka inkvizitor68sl, Moscow, MLUG
IPv6 to Linux
Vadim Apenko aka Kamasak, Moscow, MLUG


We are waiting for everyone. You can come and just chat on some Linux-related topics with interesting people, get to know each other, and maybe find people for your free projects.

Details can be found on the Wiki of the Nizhny Novgorod Group of Linux Users.
