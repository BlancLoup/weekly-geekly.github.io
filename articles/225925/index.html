Civilization V with all additions released for Linux



Rumors about the release of Civilization V and XCOM on Linux appeared in October last year, but then they remained rumors based only on incorrectly displayed goods on Amazon. The release of XCOM was confirmed not so long ago, fans of the 4X strategy counted only on the new Civilization: Beyond Earth and the next part of the main series.

But yesterday, Civilization V along with all the additions really came out for Linux (including Steam OS, of course). Anyone can purchase it on Steam with a 75% discount.
