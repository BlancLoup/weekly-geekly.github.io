How much do testers cost and what do their salaries depend on? Build a portrait of a successful QA-specialist


At the beginning of 2019, we (together with the Portals Software-testing.ru and Dou.ua) conducted a study of the level of remuneration of QA specialists. Now we know how much tester services cost in different parts of the world. And we also know what kind of knowledge and experience a QA specialist should possess in order to change a stuffy office and a modest salary, to a beach chaise longue and a thick wad of currency. Want to know more about everything? Read our article.

So ... Imagine the situation, you have come for an interview and you are asked a completely standard question about "Expected salary level". How can you not lose the answer? Someone will start pushing off from the RFP at the last place of work, someone from the average RFP for this job in Moscow, someone will take as a basis the level of salary, which your friend QA engineer bragged yesterday for a glass of tea. But you must admit, somehow this is all vague, I would like to know for myself the price for sure.

Therefore, any tester interested in money sometimes asks the following questions:

  • How much do I stand as a specialist?
  • What skills need to be developed to increase your value to the employer?
  • Will I not get more by changing my office work in Barnaul to the remote in Moscow?

Salary aka cash compensation is a kind of universal equivalent to the success of a hired professional in his professional field. If we discard the subjective personal and social factors, perhaps it will say nothing better than the salary about the qualifications and level of competencies of the hired specialist. But if we know everything about our level of income, then in what direction to develop, in order to increase this income, we can only guess.

According to the Pareto principle, 80% of the funds the employer / customer is willing to pay for 20% of our skills. The only question is what skills in modern realities are included in these 20%. And today we will try to pick up that key to success.

Part one. The level of remuneration of QA-specialists in Russia and the World


To begin with, we will determine the level of RFP QA specialists in Russia and how it depends on the format of work.

How does the level of remuneration of a QA-specialist depend on his work format (Russia)?



Approximately half of all QA-specialists (48.9%) work in the office on a payroll from $ 601 to $ 1,500. Another third also works in an office format, almost equally divided into two camps: with salary <$ 600 (17.3%) and salary 1500 - 2300 $ (18.1%).

Interesting: The percentage of highly paid professionals is much higher among adherents of flexible office and remote schedules than among testers who are constrained by tight work schedules. As for freelancing, all of its few representatives indicated their level of income as <$ 600.

These indicators are peculiar not only to the Russian market of QA services. Similar trends can be traced internationally.

Comparison of the average wage of QA-specialists (Russia vs the World)



The salary benefits of flexible remote graphics are even better visible when compared to the global level. Probably, this is affected by the absence of the cost of the employer on the org. equipment, infrastructure and organization of the employee‚Äôs workplace, which are partially converted into his salary. So, if you dream of drinking cocktails on the ocean and earning 24% more than your colleagues fighting for the air conditioner remote from 9 to 18, you now have additional motivation.

Interesting: The RF salary in Russia lags behind the world in the case of the remote hard format (35.7%) and freelance (58.1%), and the freelance itself is at least as poorly paid, but much better developed abroad than in Russia.

The percentage of juna (junior) is highest among people with humanitarian, economic and secondary special education.
Good leads (lead) are obtained from students of technical specialties, lawyers, people with a degree and, attention logic, specialists with specialized management education.
Good senior (senior) out of techies and, notably, either people with school education, or specialists with two higher.
But the middle (middle) enough everywhere, except that among lawyers and ostepenennyh them a little less.

Interesting: Our statistics collected for the year of work of the online institute of testers (POINT), fully confirms the above-mentioned data on the formation of June. And the internal statistics of the company shows that technical specialists are growing the fastest on the career ladder.

How many disputes go around the classification of QA-specialists and wages by grades. Junior (junior), who receive as senior (senior), lead (lead) on the salary of the middle (middle) - a common practice in our days. Let's try to figure it out.

How does the level of remuneration of a QA-specialist depend on his position / category?



Let's start with the destruction of the main myth about the growth of seniors in managers. Going into leads is not a step up, but to the side! All the experience gained during the long years of work as a QA-specialist hardly helps in a new role, because you have to work not with code, but with people and plans. All this is well understood by the leadership, and in fact we see that neither salaries nor their structure among seniors and leads are fundamentally different.

The difference between the junas and middles is also not catastrophic. Yes, on average, the middle one more often earns $ 1500-2300 instead of $ 600. But like the junas, half of all middles receive salaries from the range of $ 601-1500.

Interesting: Where the salary jump is really visible is when comparing middles and seniors. salaries less than $ 600 are a thing of the past, and 57% of all salaries go into the range of $ 1500‚Äì3000. Part Three Dependence of the remuneration level of QA-specialists on the level of testing skills


Remember, at the very beginning of this article we tried to understand our value as a specialist. And now for the testing skills analysis. What skills do QA specialists have and how does this affect their salary level?

What skills do QA specialists have the best skills?



Consider the necessary minimum of skills, without which we in the profession can not do.

What should every QA specialist know?

  1. Skill localization and institution defects - The most common skill. 4 people do not own it at all, 16 - poorly own. And 98% of respondents have the skill well and perfectly.
  2. Possession of bug tracking systems (Jira, Redmine, YouTrack, Bugzilla) - also, only 6 people are completely unfamiliar with this skill.
  3. Web-based client testing - 81% of respondents have a good or perfect knowledge of them.
  4. Possession of knowledge management systems and repositories of test cases (wiki, confluence, etc.) - the same 81%, but only 27% of them are perfect.
  5. Possession of test-analysis techniques, test design and test combinatorics - this skill 58% of the specialists have good skills and another 18% are perfect. Should I get behind them?

Now consider the skills that can be considered scarce and, therefore, well paid in our profession.

What can boast to the employer / colleagues?

  1. Experience in developing load testing scripts in JMeter or similar applications is the rarest skill. 467 people do not possess this skill at all (46.4%). 197 people own it at a sufficient level (19.6%). Only 49 people own it perfectly, and, 36 of them earn more than $ 1,500.
  2. Possession of autotest results reporting systems (Allure, etc.) - 204 specialists have a sufficient level.
  3. Ownership of drivers and add-ins for test automation - 241 specialists.
  4. Possession of test frameworks for automation (TestNG, JUnit, etc.) - 272 specialists.

Interesting: The skills of load testing and automation were expected to be the rarest skills, which confirms the current situation on the QA-services labor market. The lack of automation and workloads is well traced by the level of their wages compared to other specialists.

What skills are paid best?




The most modest of all (up to $ 1410 per month) paid for the basic skills of bug tracking, skills in the field of web / mobile applications, test analysis and layout / adaptability.

Not far from them (up to $ 1560 per month) , the skills of testing integration and databases, knowledge of version control systems and logging have gone. On average, they are paid 10-15% better.

Even better (up to $ 1660 per month.) Skills of managing storage of test cases, possession of tools for monitoring traffic and the basic skill of localization and the establishment of defects are paid.

Well, and if you like the figure of $ 1770 , then, as mentioned earlier, welcome to the league of auto testers, workloads and continuous integrators, these are the skills that are best paid for in our research.

Interestingly, possession of the skills of load testing and automation increases the size of your salary, by an average of 20-25%, with an equal position and work experience.
A QA specialist with only one or even 2-3 skills is most likely a rarity in the profession. It is more correct to evaluate the qualifications and salary of a tester by the number of skills that he totally owns.

How does the level of remuneration of a QA-specialist depend on the number of skills he has mastered?



The myth of the profitability of specialization in testing did not justify itself. The number of skills in the tester's arsenal directly affects his salary. Each additional 5-6 skills in a specialist's money-box lead to an increase in wages by 20-30%. Most noticeable is the increase in salaries for specialists who have mastered more than 20 skills. Such "geeks" receive an average of 62% more than narrow-profile specialists with 5 skills in luggage.

Interesting: Only 12 people out of 1006 possess all the skills. All of them have a high level of RFP. All 12 people work in the office, all have extensive experience (only one respondent has experience of 2-3 years, the others are evenly distributed according to experience 4-6, 7-10 and more than 10 years).
