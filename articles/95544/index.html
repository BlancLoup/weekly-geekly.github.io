Open beta testing of free fonts PingWi Typography (PWT) begins

On LORE ( Linux.org.Ru ) today there was news that the company PingWin Software ( PingWinSoft.Ru ) published an announcement about the start of open beta testing of its free font package PingWi Typography (PWT), created since 2009 specifically to solve problems with a distortion of the formatting of documents created by MS Office, when opening these documents in OpenOffice.org.

The headsets included in the PWT package were developed from scratch as metric analogs of Tahoma, Arial, Courier, Verdana and Times New Roman Windows fonts (and are similarly called: PWT Tahion, PWT Arion, PWT Courant, PWT Verde, PWT Timer) ; In addition, PWT fonts are designed to improve the display of text on low-resolution monitors.

The PWT test font package is distributed under a special free license . Headsets are in TTF-form inside the ZIP-archive .
