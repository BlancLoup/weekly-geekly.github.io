Dubbee - listen to your favorite sites for free


Dubbee is a service for translating news sites and blogs into audio format, where all articles are read by the live voices of announcers. You choose the sources that interest you and create your own personal information radio, which can be listened to from a computer, laptop or mobile device.

From today, all Dubbee audio recordings are available absolutely free.
