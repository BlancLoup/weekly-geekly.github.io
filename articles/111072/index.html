"Procons" - choose electronics

Shalom, Habr! Taki ventured to introduce you to his child - the result of overwork and lack of sleep. What is it?

Imagine that you decide to buy a device. After looking through the reviews, we found several candidates for the role of a future purchase. They went to the Market, read a dozen or two of the same type of reviews - tired. We went to the forum, say, iXBT - on the fifth page of the flame we were desperate to learn something useful. Wait, do not make a blind purchase! Probably Procorcons can help you .



How can he help?


First, every review at Procons is collective. Reviews can be edited and supplemented, allowing you to keep them up to date. Of course, you can edit other people's reviews if you have enough karma reputation . This allows you to weed out not quite adequate personalities. By the way, there is a revision system, therefore, in which case, any edits can be rolled back.

Secondly, each review concerns only one plus or minus of the product. Thus, it is possible to get rid of duplication of the same information in different reviews. In addition, this naturally leads us to vote for a particular plus or minus. Which, in turn, leads us to a rating system of products.

All product information is presented on one page; It can be captured, if not with one glance, then at least with one brain. In general, I think, it turned out a logical and harmonious platform for conducting a kind of Wikipedia about products.

Thank you for your attention, I invite everyone to try it - suddenly like it.
