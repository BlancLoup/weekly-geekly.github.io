"Deals 24" - a typewriter for small businesses


Launched at the end of 2009, the Internet service " Transactions24 " was initially positioned as a tool for quickly and easily filling in sales documents: contracts, invoices for payments, acts of completed works, invoices, invoices.

After 4 months of testing, we released the service for free navigation - the official distribution began in April 2010. And over the course of the year, the service was free for everyone - through trial and error, the design was changed several times, it was possible to maintain expenditure documents, unload in 1C (for cases when the company is serviced by a remote accountant), reports, import payments from a bank statement, and much another.

Since April 2011 we have been spreading according to the freemium model - the basic functions are free, goodies and a large number of counterparties - 200 rubles per month.
