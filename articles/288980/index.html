"Level 90" (LeveL90) will be implemented in the form of an interactive diverse educational platform, available as a web service and mobile applications.

The main "theme" of the project is already set by the name, the learning process is identical to the game. The user selects the course of the desired subject, passes the test that determines the current level of the user-player, and then follows the course using a stepwise system, mastering the subject and developing relevant skills up to a maximum level of 90 skill.

Three components of any course on the platform LeveL90:
  1. terms and definitions that the student must master, and without which understanding of the subject is impossible,
  2. educational multimedia content (books, songs, videos),
  3. cases, practicing skills of behavior in imaginary situations in the life of a manager, yoga, physics, to successfully solve similar situations in real life.


All courses are distributed in 5 areas:
  • basic knowledge (school program);
  • professional knowledge and entrepreneurship;
  • social roles and relationships;
  • health, sport, food and lifestyle;
  • culture, art, philosophy.
