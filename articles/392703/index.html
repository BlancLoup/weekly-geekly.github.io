It's nice that the headset allows you to connect directly to two audio sources wirelessly. For example, I connected directly to two smartphones. This is true if one is a working device, and the second is personal. And while listening to music, you can skip an important call. Control and sound
The control keys of the wireless headphones are located on the back of the right ear and are extremely convenient when controlling the thumb of the right hand. In this case, the buttons themselves are at a sufficient distance, which allows to avoid mistakes. The buttons are located from top to bottom in the following order: power on, volume up, volume down. With a long press on the power button, the headset goes into search mode and is available for connecting to new devices. In the operation mode, the same button with a short press puts the melody on pause. Double short press starts the call to the last number dialed. At the time of receipt of the call, a short press on the button will allow you to take a call, long-reject. A short press of the volume up button switches the player to play the next tune, a short press on the volume down button - and the player rolls back to the previous tune in the track list. By long pressing these buttons, the volume is adjusted directly.

And when I connected to ZTE Axon Mini and turned on my favorite EQ profile, and as a test track I chose a melody in lossless quality, then I felt the beauty of this headset. Channel separation feels good, low frequencies sound bold and tangible. The mids fill the sound picture well and let you feel the voice and the basic instruments.
