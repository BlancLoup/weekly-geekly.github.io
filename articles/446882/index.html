MIPT won the right to host the ICPC World Programming Championship in 2020 in Moscow



Yesterday, the Alfandega Congress Center in Porto announced that Moscow had been chosen as the venue for the world's oldest and most prestigious sports programming championship of the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) in June next year. The organizer of the final will be MIPT with the support of RDI.Creative.

Shanghai, Sydney and Orlando struggled for the right to take the final.
