Vox Imperium: Civilization, done on Ajax

Bill Renner created Vox Imperium , an analogue of "civilization", ported to Ajax.



Vox - cross-browser, multiplayer, strategy.


Players compete for the resources and territory of huge worlds in which hundreds of players can simultaneously be located.

Vox is based on a rich history of game strategies, combining elements from Civilization, Settlers of Catan.

Qualified players can win access to Vox "map editor", which will allow him to create a battlefield for the next fight.
