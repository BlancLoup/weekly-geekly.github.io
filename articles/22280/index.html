Coworking / Near Moscow

I will take out the thought from my own comment to one of the previous entries:
How interesting is it for Muscovites to work on the principles of co-work in the near suburbs?

In the first place, I am addressing the question to the residents of the west of the capital, since there are some very interesting places in mind.

Among the advantages of an office in the suburbs:
  • Fresh air
  • Birds, butterflies, trees, leaves - in short, nature
  • Opportunity to drive in traffic jams
  • The opportunity to find an interesting place for sane money


cons, in turn:
  • Friday Residents
  • Without a car is more difficult to get (bus, train, etc.)
  • Similar to the previous point - a pedestrian is much more difficult to reach.
  • If the office is located on the near-government road, they may not let it in time, blocking the road


Transportation minuses, however, can be solved by organizing your own shuttle (which, alas, will affect the price).
