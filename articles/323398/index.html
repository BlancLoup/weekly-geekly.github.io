Why the February update HPE 3PAR StoreServ - the most important since the launch line

The announcement of the new features of HPE 3PAR StoreServ on February 13, 2017 was named by some commentators on the corporate blog as the most important since launch.
Families 3PAR 7000 four years ago. This is a very loud statement. During this time, much has changed in the industry, flash drives have overtaken hard drives in terms of sales, and some storage companies have lost their independence or even collapsed .

Interestingly, on February 13, only programmatic improvements were announced. The only hardware novelty is the four-port adapters for the 3PAR 8000 family. This is an important thing for those who use File Persona or iSCSI, but does not deserve the title of ‚Äúthe most important announcement‚Äù. So what's the deal?



Back in August 2016 and look at the Gartner magic quadrant in all-flash arrays. Despite the fact that HPE was among the leaders with the 3PAR line, many noticed that the company's position was far from the first place (although, judging by sales, HPE 3PAR is the No. 1 array in this segment). At the same time, Gartner analysts noted the strengths of the array - performance, scalability, a set of tools for managing data, and intelligent work with NAND memory. The report even appreciated the fact that HPE 3PAR is the only platform that is present both in the quadrant for all-flash systems and in the quadrant of traditional arrays ( "familiarity and channel partners" ). Analysts thought it was insufficient to put the company in a higher position. Here are the Cautions list:

  • Lack of compression
  • Complex licensing scheme
  • Insufficient analytical capabilities of managing software in comparison with competitors.

As far as these points are critical, one can argue. The implementation of compression in some solutions on the market does not allow convincingly to prove the great benefit of this technology (see the results of last year‚Äôs competition , where 3PAR without compression showed the best storage cost in competition with IBM and EMC solutions). The licensing scheme is the concern of presale at the procurement stage, which does not affect the efficiency of the array. As for analytic capabilities, do they have to be in the cloud? To do this, the system must send statistics to the cloud. To many Russian customers, this seems like a bad idea. In addition, it is possible that analysts did not take into account the presence of such an analytical tool as the HPE StoreFront Remote , which we will discuss later .

However, all these "problems" are fixed in the announcement of February 13.

HPE 3PAR Adaptive Data Reduction


New term in the HPE 3PAR dictionary. This is not just compression, it is a set of measures for the efficient storage of data. It all starts with Zero Detect, when the 3PAR ASIC chip analyzes the Fiber Channel stream and does not write zeros. Further data deduplitsiruyutsya. In the new version of the operating system 3PAR algorithm of this process has been reworked. As it turned out as a result of practical operation, some of the data is deduplicated superbly - up to 12: 1, while the rest are not deduplicating at all. To optimize the operation of the array, part of the code was rewritten, and, according to the developers, we can expect significant performance improvements. After deduplication has detected and processed repeating blocks of 16KB in size, the array tries to determine if the data inside these unique blocks can be compressed. For example, it is pointless to spend processor time on video file compression, and 3PAR will not try to do it. If data is compressed, useful information may take up a volume of 0 to 16 KB. It is important to properly pack it before writing to the SSD in such a way as not to lose the effectiveness of compression and to avoid a situation familiar to users of Windows 10, when the computer is so passionate about background processes that it cannot do anything useful. Competitive arrays require periodic garbage collection, since overwriting may change the data compression ratio, logical volumes may be deleted ‚Äî and, in addition, free space cannot be used. The developers of HPE 3PAR Data Packing claim that their algorithm is efficient and does not overload the processor.

3PAR All Inclusive Licenses


All 3PAR arrays are now shipped with a complete set of data management software within one system: HPE 3PAR Operating System Software, Virtual Copy, Dynamic Optimization, Adaptive Optimization, Priority Optimization, Virtual Domains, Virtual Lock, File Persona, Smart SAN, Online Import, Host Explorer, System Reporter, Recovery Manager Central. In that case, when you need to organize the interaction of two or more arrays, you need HPE 3PAR All Inclusive Multi-System Software, including HPE 3PAR Peer Motion, HPE 3PAR Remote Copy, HPE 3PAR Peer Persistence and HPE 3PAR Cluster Extension. Do you remember that most all-flash arrays offered by competitors have an ambiguous replication situation? 3PAR arrays do not require virtualizers to communicate with each other, but a federated storage or building fault-tolerant solutions requires a license (to try, you just need to get a temporary one - please contact).

The new licensing scheme simplifies planning when purchasing and deploying storage, as well as encourages users to move away from the traditional model of using the first-generation flash arrays "one application - one storage". 3PAR is very efficient when consolidating data, and given the HPE 3PAR Adaptive Data Reduction's stated efficiency of 4: 1, you can get a significant gain in storage costs.

The RMC license is included in the HPE 3PAR All Inclusive Single System base package.
