Key report of the first day


After a short introduction, where participants are told about the order of the conference, and where the halls and key objects such as the Kinect toilet and coffee breaks are located, Dmitry Shimkiv, general manager of Microsoft Ukraine, takes the stage. Arguments about the future begin, historical background on the development of cloud computing and cloud experience.
In particular, Dimtriy began with a story about a car project into which systems are integrated that allow you to control the systems of the car itself through ... a mobile phone, including the possibility of authorization through the phone. These phones, united in one network, can interact with each other, warn about traffic jams and so on. Twitter began to seethe.
In the end, we come to level 5 of building data centers. In particular, the Microsoft data center in Chicago is shown. Servers are located in large containers, similar to those transported by vans. All communications, power and cooling are carried out to the containers, standing in even rows. What was especially unusual: some of the servers are in the open air and use natural resources for cooling and obtaining ‚Äúgreen‚Äù energy, which makes it possible to save a lot on server maintenance - up to 70% (although it was not entirely clear from the context, costs are reduced by ‚Äú or ‚Äúup to 70%? In any case, the numbers are impressive).
After general words about the interaction of devices and applications in terms of cloud development, the most important part of the presentation begins: Microsoft Azure is a new platform for creating and managing distributed cloud systems and web applications. Several examples were cited, among them: a certain French company engaged in market analysis and forecasting, all of which is calculated in the cloud running Azure. Azure also participated in the rendering of Avatar and the new Toy Story. There were also examples when it is much more profitable to rent a cloud instead of building your own data center: a pizza ordering service over the Internet, renting a cloud running Azure per day in the US super bowl; Football Broadcasting in Spain: The Cloud and Silverlight Client.
The development of clouds occurs in two directions: for mere mortals (for example, Xbox LIVE) and for business.
