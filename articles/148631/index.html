Summer Olympics on Yandex

Here is the long-awaited event of the summer - the opening of the thirties Summer Olympics. Two and a half weeks, not only sports fans, but also people far from sports, will closely monitor developments in London - check the weather, monitor discounts on hotels and tickets, but the main thing is to count the number of medals earned by the national team every morning.

You can follow the medals, records and not miss the most interesting competitions for you on our special site about the Olympics . Here you can find the schedule of competitions, see the overall medal standings and achievements of teams in various sports. Also on the site are available live video and text broadcasts, fresh videos on Olympic topics, news and photos. For fans who want to immediately learn about new records, an automatic Twitter account @ yandexsport works .

And, of course, the results of each day of the competition will be displayed on the main page of Yandex.
