July 16-17, the 2nd StartupPoint will be held in Yekaterinburg



On July 16-17, the 2nd StartupPoint will take place in Yekaterinburg at the INNOPROM-2010 industry and innovation forum .

What is INNOPROM?

At the Exhibition and Forum "INNOPROM-2010" the direction of small IT companies of the Sverdlovsk region is planned to be shown with a single stand. It will feature 20 start-up projects at the commercial implementation stage and 30 start-up projects at the prototyping or idea stage. Also, 150 representatives of IT companies working on their own projects, 10 venture funds and 4 invited experts will take part in the stand.
