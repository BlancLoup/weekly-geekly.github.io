1. Casetext


American start-up for lawyers Casetext has developed a system of smart search for judicial precedents - CARA. The mechanics are very simple: the user loads his document into the system, AI searches in the database for everything that seems to be appropriate for this case. Casetext differs from Consultant Plus and its analogs in the intelligence of the search: Casetext tries to use the exact meaning of the document without breaking the request into separate keywords.



The basic cost of a subscription without discounts - $ 120 per month. If we compare it with the cost of a man-hour of an American lawyer, the investments will be repulsed in just a couple of successful searches a month.


The project is technologically complex, it develops relatively slowly. Investments are also not very large by American standards - in the amount of $ 20 million. The potential startup market is 1.3 million lawyers in the United States.


2. Farmers Business Network



American start-up Farmers Business Network offers its customers to unite in the fight against nature, buyers and suppliers. There are two ways to combine:


  • Simple exchange of information. Farmers enter into the information system everything related to their fields: from yield to purchase prices. This allows comparisons with average and ideal results of colleagues, adjusted for the region and other conditions. It is easier to choose seeds and fertilizers, bargain. The more the farmer provides information, the more data becomes available to him. The accuracy of the input data is controlled using manual moderation and special algorithms.
  • Fighting suppliers. FBN organizes joint purchases for its farmers and, at the expense of volumes, knocks out additional discounts - in fact, this is the work of a regular distributor with low margins, the occupation is not very profitable. But farmers get a more substantial reason to pay a subscription: not for some information, but for the opportunity to buy something tangible.

Knowing everything about its customers, FBN can offer them additional services. Now, for example, a ‚Äúclub loan‚Äù has been implemented: a startup helps clients attract financing at a reduced rate.


In less than three years, FBN received about $ 80 million in investment. The last estimate is about 400 million. The potential market is huge: there are two million farms in the USA, $ 600 a year from each is about a billion dollars in revenue excluding other sources of income.


3. Turnkey Vacation Rentals



Turnkey Vacation Rentals for 18% of the commission saves its customers from all the worries associated with the daily delivery of houses and apartments: from placing ads on various websites to organizing the reception of guests and cleaning the house after them. The owner need only give the keys once.


In addition to simplifying life, TVR increases the influx of guests, and therefore the income: the startup staff will take a photo of the house correctly, make the ad correctly, place it on a couple of dozen, not on a couple of sites, promptly answer questions. Another important service is dynamic pricing: a startup can automatically raise and lower prices, depending on demand and the average employment of houses in the area. But it is important to understand that most of the additional profit comes from the removal of tourists from homeowners and competitors, rather than a general increase in tourist flow. And when all homeowners switch to professional management, everyone will earn 18% less.


Turnkey works in the upper-middle price segment: the maintenance is taken at home, corresponding to fairly stringent requirements. Very cheap options to the intermediary are unprofitable: fuss with them as much, and check below.


Among homeowners, a startup is already popular, in the cities of its presence in its price range in terms of the number of offers it is only several times inferior to Airbnb. These results were achieved in four years and approximately $ 20 million of investment spent. The other day Turnkey got the same amount.
