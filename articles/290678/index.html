In recent years, the situation is changing, but not fast enough. Most of the sites, especially the old sites, built in the zero, were created on the principle of the famous experiment with five monkeys:

The essence of the experiment
There are five monkeys in the cage. A bunch of bananas is suspended from the ceiling, and under them stands a staircase. Soon a monkey walks up the stairs to climb behind the banana. As soon as she tries to rise, suddenly all the monkeys (and those who are sitting on the sidelines) under the pressure are doused with icy water. When the monkeys calmed down, the other one tries to get the bananas again - and again all the monkeys are drenched. After several repetitions, a conditioned reflex is produced, and the monkeys are no longer interested in bananas on the ceiling - they are more expensive.

A few days later, in the cage, one monkey was replaced by a new one. A new monkey saw a banana, and naturally wanted to get it. However, this attempt was severely suppressed by the old-timers of the cage. After a good bashing, the novice understood that it is impossible to touch the bananas on the ceiling, and if she tries again, she will be severely punished. Notice, at this stage, the water is no longer used.

The experience continued until all five monkeys were replaced by new ones (which were never watered). But all the newbies have already been taught that for some reason you cannot touch a banana. And when a new, sixth one was added to the ‚Äúnot drenched‚Äù monkeys, the effect was predictable: when a new girl immediately climbed onto the stairs behind a banana, she was beaten by her congeners. Because you can't touch bananas.

After replacing all of the original monkeys, none of the remaining monkeys were sprayed with cold water. However, none of them came up to the stairs to get a banana. Every time a new monkey tried to approach the stairs, it was stopped. Many monkeys who beat her did not even know why they were not allowed to climb this ladder and why they were beating a new monkey. They beat her simply because it is impossible!


Many business sites are made on the same principle: it already has a competitor. We will make the site beautiful, there will be a location map, a description of how good we are, what services we provide - and after all, all this is PR and business cost optimization (for example, the operator does not need to waste time explaining the location map to the company's office).

Well, something is changing. The main trend of the tenth years in site building is the use of landing pages. Landing idea has grown from presentations (a slide with a description of the problem, its solution, etc.), but its implementation is distinguished by a high density of contact information: the pages are simply stuffed with feedback forms, phone numbers, etc. Small businesses have already realized that the most important thing is to get a customer, a call, a letter, a completed order form. Medium and large business is just on the way.

It is the elements of interaction with visitors that are the point to which you need to exert maximum efforts: you need to bring them to the contact, call, write to the chat. This should be on all pages, in the header of the page and in the basement, in the middle of the page, in the menu - everything should call the client to action.
