The next solution to the problem of wires

The topic of fighting the chaos of wires under the table has a long and glorious history, but the monstrous tangles continue and continue to grow under our tables, getting tangled under our feet, falling under the legs of chairs and tying up in sea knots.
A separate part of the problem is the chargers of the ever-growing fleet of gadgets: phones, cameras, tablets and other things.

The solution I propose is aimed at organizing and reducing this chaos in a single apartment.





The idea is simple: a box of such a size is taken so that an extension cord for five outlets gets into it. And two such extension cords are better. Extenders are placed in the box, numerous chargers are stuck into them. On top of the box is closed with a lid with a hole in which the ends of all chargers are displayed. The wire from the extension cable is discharged through the hole in the casing. To charge the gadgets are connected to their s / y and placed on top of the cover.
Thus, the chaos is packed in a box. At the same time, compared with the traditional solution in the form of tying wires with cable ties, there is a big advantage: any charger can be easily removed and put back.

The implementation of the idea can be a great many.

In my case, it was a small plastic box,
