The use of 3D printers in Russia: real cases Top 3D Shop



The media is often called 3D printing as a very interesting and promising area, but communicating with the visitors of our stands, potential customers and just acquaintances, we understand that there are still very, very few people who have an idea about the use of additive technologies.

We are often asked: why do we need a 3D printer ? What can print on it in addition to toys and trinkets?

Over 2 years of work, we have accumulated a fairly large customer base throughout Russia, which includes both b2b and b2c customers, and we are ready for a separate section on real-life histories of using 3D printers and 3D scanners in Russia.

Today we will start with 3D printing :

1) The company "Monitoring"

Specialization: implementation of integrated work on instrumentation, methodological and metrological support of analytical measurement technologies in various fields of science and production

Website: http://www.ooo-monitoring.ru/

Monitoring LLC purchased the Picaso 3D Designer 3D printer in December 2014 to print prototype cases for new devices. As an example, the photographs provided by the company show products created using this 3D printer.



In the photos from left to right: a housing for a remote sensor, mounting the sensor to the cuvette and mounting mount for the pistons, different versions of the housing to the ‚Äúpocket‚Äù gas analyzer.

After three months of confident work with the printer, representatives of the company told us that the 3D printing technology met their expectations: in addition to saving time and money when designing the cases and parts of new products, in some positions the printer replaced traditional machining machines: transitional and mounting boards, fastenings, the whole parts of products now grow on eyes for very short, in comparison with the order "on the side" time.

The device not only paid off, but already paid off. The company is already thinking of purchasing a second printer, since sometimes you have to wait for the print queue. There are a lot of ideas and directions in this area, therefore in the near future this area will develop rapidly.

2) The company LLC "LED Microsensor NT"

Specialization: development and production of optoelectronic devices of the middle infrared range.

Website: ru.lmsnt.com

A team of scientists and engineers with more than 15 years of experience in the field of research and development of LEDs and LED-matrices works in LLC LED Microsensor NT.



LLC "LED Microsensor NT" has a complete technological cycle, necessary for the creation of LEDs and photodiodes - from the initial stage to the output of the final product.

Any design activity implies the possibility of quickly implementing the idea from the drawing to the finished layout, and one of the problems that any design engineer faces is the production of product models with different geometry of the elements.

Thanks to rapid prototyping right on the desktop, you can quickly make adjustments to the design, which allows you to try all the ideas in action at the location of certain components.

The presence of such a powerful tool as a 3D printer contributes both to increasing the speed of creating new projects and developing the engineering skills of the engineer himself.

LLC LED Microsensor NT is the owner of the printer Picaso 3D Designer for about six months. According to the employees, one should pay tribute, few of them could have imagined before buying how useful it would be, and sometimes it was necessary.
Despite the relatively low price, the printer provides acceptable print quality and speed.





As you can see from these two cases, the 3D printer can be used in almost any production. It can be both cases, fastenings, and the whole parts of products. This significantly shortens the cycle between creating a drawing and getting the finished part.
At the end we will give the use of a 3D printer in a 3D printing studio. In our vision, such studios can be cost-effective over time in all more or less large cities, such as photo studios and copy centers. Any person who has an idea or desire to print out his model will be able to contact such a studio and get the finished part. Undoubtedly, this will contribute to the development of a culture of "meikkerstva" in Russia.
