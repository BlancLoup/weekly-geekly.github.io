Introducing Evernote Clearly: reading from the screen is now convenient


We like to feel most comfortable while reading. Put a cup of tea next to you, get your feet in your favorite chair and then open the book. Comfort and lack of distractions help to concentrate. Alas, when you read in the browser, this is not always the case. Not having time to finish reading the first paragraph, you already click on the links, after five minutes you already buy a new player in the online store and wonder how you got there.

Now there is a better way. We are introducing a new browser extension, Evernote Clearly , which is already available for Google Chrome (it will soon appear for other browsers).

Download Evernote Clearly for Google Chrome

With a single click, Clearly will hide all the clutter, allowing you to calmly read a blog or article.



How it works


Click on the Clearly icon while on a blog page or article. Clearly will show you a modified view of the page, in which there will be no navigation elements, ads and links. When you finish reading, click on the return arrow and Clearly returns the standard web page view so you can continue to browse the site.

Multi-page articles

If you launch Clearly on an article that occupies several web pages, they will all be automatically summarized into one, and you will no longer have to click on the buttons to go to the next page.

Save for the future


We do not always have time to read an interesting text at the same moment as we stumbled upon it. Therefore, we have embedded the Evernote web clipper right into Clearly. Click on the familiar icon of the elephant in the sidebar, and the page will be saved to your Evernote account. You can even set labels for all your content copied with Clearly.



The automatic labeling feature allows you to significantly simplify the creation of lists for reading in Evernote, which will make them available from any computer, phone or tablet you use.

Make reading comfortable


Clearly offers three ready-made themes for text. You can choose the most convenient reading mode.
  • Printed: newspaper presentation of the material
  • Simple: clean modern look.
  • Midnight: dark background, ideal for night reading

Didn't like the preset modes? You can make your own. Go to the extension settings and create your own theme. You can customize almost everything - from fonts to color and text alignment.



You can also set shortcut keys for Quickly launching and copying to Evernote.

Everything in your browser


Clearly performs all its actions directly in the browser. He never sends content to Evernote or anywhere else, until you choose this option.

Great companion for web clipper


With Clearly, you now have two Evernote buttons in your browser panel. Our web clipper helps you save any information you find on the Internet, and Clearly gives you the opportunity to read it comfortably. Hope you enjoy.
