New details about the Courier project

On the popular resource Engaget new interesting materials appeared about Courier - the project of an electronic booklet from Microsoft. Below is a new concept video:



The device has a size of 2.5 cm in thickness, weight about 400 grams. Powered by the Tegra 2 platform and running Windows CE 6, the same system running Zune HD devices, the expected Pink smartphone and future Windows Phone 7 smartphones. According to rumors, Courier may enter the market in Q3 or Q4 2010 .

The device contains a camera and headphone jack. Another detail: the video shows that the device can work with telephone networks (receive SMS) and supports integration with social services, like Facebook. Courier will probably have a built-in 3G module.
