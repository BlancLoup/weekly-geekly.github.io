An advertisement that does not respect users

An advertisement that does not respect the users.

It was this thought that flashed through my head, after reading the next spam letter from a well-known global company.

This article is nothing more than a discussion of the advertising market, in particular the Internet advertising market, I am writing it in order to share my thoughts and hear the opinions of others on this matter. Maybe even this article will inspire someone to create a new startup.

Today, the Internet advertising market is huge (we are talking about hundreds of billions of US dollars) and it continues to grow at a steady pace.
