How much is now "Yandex"? History of one sale


Photo: Gleb Shchelkunov / Kommersant

The other day in Moscow, the former Yandex employee Dmitry Korobov was convicted. The case turned out to be quite interesting - Korobov tried to sell the source code and algorithms of the company's main service, Yandex.Search. At the same time, the former employee estimated the source code quite inexpensively - $ 25 thousand and 250 thousand rubles, respectively, Kommersant writes . The money raised from the sale, Korobov wanted to spend on creating his own startup.

At the time of the transaction, the unlucky merchant was arrested by employees of the UFSB of Russia in Moscow and the Moscow Region. For Yandex, everything ended well, since the leakage of the source code could have resulted in millions of losses, a large number of court proceedings and, as a result, a fall in capitalization.
