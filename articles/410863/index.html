Conference on Digital Production Top 3D Expo - April 10



Friends! Already on April 10, our annual exhibition and conference Top 3D Expo will take place ! We invite everyone who is interested in the topic of additive technologies and their applications, especially in industry.



Theme of the conference


The theme of this year's expo is Digital Production 2018. We will discuss and consider from all sides the use of additive technologies and robotics in production, right up to full-scale industrial.











Location


The third annual exhibition and conference of 3D technologies, where the leaders of the Russian market will present their latest products and developments in the field of 3D printing, 3D scanning, digital design and robotics, will share their experience and answer all questions, will be held in Technopolis MOSCOW - the center of development technologies on Volgogradsky Avenue, 3 minutes walk from Tekstilshchiki metro station.



Free VIP-parking is available to the first 50 participants, in the vicinity there are also many municipal parking lots.



The most current topics, the best speakers, a huge amount of useful practical information and the exchange of personal experience in invaluable communication with colleagues are waiting for you.



Speakers


The conference will feature leading experts from companies in the additive industry who will highlight the problems and prospects for the application of new technologies in production, share ideas and experiences.









Goals


The goal of Top 3D Expo is to bring together specialists from the engineering field. Practicing speakers will share their experience using digital production tools (3D printing, 3D scanning, 3D modeling, Industrial Robotics).

The conference is important to go to the owners and managers of manufacturing companies, representatives of educational institutions, makers, journalists, analysts and anyone who wants to expand their horizons and get a lot of interesting and useful information about modern tools of digital production.
