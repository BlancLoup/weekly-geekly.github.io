Lumo Lift: a miniature fitness tracker and "controller" posture



Now in the role of fitness trackers, various kinds of ‚Äúsmart‚Äù watches, bracelets and other similar gadgets usually act. Not so long ago, another device for sports fans appeared on the horizon, it is called Lumo Lift . For the first time it was shown live at CES 2014, after which many resources published information about an interesting little one. Let's see what a device can do.

As already mentioned, Lumo Lift is very small, but it copes well with its functions. This device can count the calories spent per day, monitor physical exertion, and also give a signal (vibrate) if the carrier of this device assumes an incorrect posture. In general, the control of posture can be called the main function of the device, and, you see, this function is quite important.

In this case, the device allows you to count the time during which the posture was correct.

As you can see, the gadget does not have a display, interaction with it is carried out using smartphones (iOS and Android).



Lumo Lift is supposed to be worn on the reverse side of the clothes, and it is fastened with a magnetic clip (the clip is just held on the outside).



On one battery charge, the Lumo Lift can last for about five days, and it takes two hours to charge.



The price of the question is $ 79 (this is a pre-order, the full price will be $ 99). In addition to the Lumo Lift, you can purchase magnetic clips of various colors, in sets of 2 and 5 pieces ($ 5 and $ 10, respectively).

Pre-order can be issued on the manufacturer's website, Lumo for $ 79, or on the company's website Medgadgets for 3990 rubles.
