BlackBerry Technical Seminar 2008

Company Research in Motion (RIM) conducts Technical Workshop Intelligent Mobility BlackBerry 2008 . All those who develop and actively use mobile technologies can take part in it. Of course, most of the seminar will be devoted to the BlackBerry product, but it is also expected to consider the prospects and problems of developing mobile technologies. It is also planned to discuss the system Push to Save Time and mobile Microsoft.Net applications. In addition, the issues of mobile management will be considered.

The seminar will be held in the form of live broadcast, webcasts, one-to-one chat with leading BlackBerry developers, exhibitions of the latest developments of RIM. The time of July 9 is from 9:00 am to 11:00 pm EST (from 5:00 pm, 9.07 to 7:00 am, 10.07 Moscow time). The exhibition opens at 8:30 (16:30 Moscow time). There are no requirements (except for a minimum knowledge of English) and no fees or moves are needed. All you need is interest, Internet connection and registration.
