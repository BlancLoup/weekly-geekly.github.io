Right to make mistakes

Back in 2007, I realized what annoys me are the fancy books on management, finding a way in life and making a million. These books do not leave me any margin for error. They do not recognize this right for me.

The authors of all the mentioned meaningless paper are afraid of the word failure with all their might. They are afraid of this word for themselves, for their readers - for everything that surrounds them. They try to look perfect and happy, and make their readers feel stupid and uncomfortable. And the only appreciative audience of these books is fools .

By becoming successful , successful in terms of management books, you stop being happy . Because your only concern is running. Running only forward, only in a straight line and without the possibility of rest, respite and the right to take careless steps.

Fortunately, people in this world are very different. Different in their abilities and capabilities. And there is nothing shameful in the fact that you are not the best employee, there is nothing shameful even in the fact that you are the worst. You are what you are. Everybody has a place for life. To be perfect and successful is in my opinion the only right in which I want to deny people. I want to refuse for one simple reason: people are much better than they think of themselves in those moments when they feel successful.
