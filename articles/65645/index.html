Compatibility test: HTC Diamond 2

The blog has already mentioned a couple of times about those or other devices on which the use of mobile Evernote becomes as comfortable as possible. This time we found such a device under Windows Mobile. Since our developers (concurrently extremely advanced and active users of the service), having consistently examined the communicator, gave a complacent assessment of the possibilities of symbiosis of this device with Evernote, we decided to mention it here. This is the HTC Touch Diamond 2.





Detailed technical specifications will easily prompt Yandex, so here we will not dwell on them particularly. We only note the processor that has noticeably matured since the first Touch - Qualcomm MSM7200A 528 MHz, with which Evernote works in almost any situation smartly: when scrolling through notes, while photographing, while drawing and creating handwritten notes.

As the authors of the mobile application that uses photography, we are always especially sensitive to the quality of the camera and the resulting images. It's no secret that mobile photos are still far from perfect in terms of clarity - but Diamond 2 has exceeded all expectations. The fact is that the 5-megapixel camera can automatically focus approximately 7 cm from the object. Well, since in Evernote most often photograph mostly business cards, writing on stickers and other text, size does matter. Less is better. And the focal length of 7 cm is probably a record today.
