Droidcon Moscow 2014

If you are working with the Android platform, it will be reckless to miss one of the largest Eurasian conferences Droidcon , which will be held in Moscow for the first time on April 11-12, 2014. Annual Droidcon conferences have been held since 2009 around the world. They gather under one roof Android-developers and experts of the telecom and IT-industry, united by the general idea of ‚Äã‚Äãdeveloping new forms of application of the Android platform.

In April, our program manager for interaction with developers in Central and Eastern Europe, David Ostrovsky (+ Dawid Ostrowski ), will arrive in Moscow specifically on Droidcon from the Polish office of Google.

The program of the two-day conference: lectures and reports from market experts and developers from Russia, Great Britain, Germany, France, Poland, Romania, Macedonia, as well as barcamp, hackathon and gadget show.


On the first day at Droidcon Moscow 2014, you can hear speeches from leading IT corporations on the following topics:
  1. Business applications (Mikhail Dudarev, founder of the jCardSim project, co-founder of the Licel company).
  2. Monetization of Android applications (Tim Messerschmidt, leading development evangelist, PayPal Europe).
  3. Integrating applications into Google services (Dawid Ostrowski, Program Manager, Developer Relations in Central and Eastern Europe, Google).
  4. Innovation and entrepreneurial ecosystems (Thibaut Rouffineau, Wireless Industry Partnership and Apptual).
  5. Development of cross-platform applications (Jose L Ugia, Head of Android at 6Wunderkinder and co-founder of Momenta).
  6. Mobile advertising in Android applications: platforms, monetization, management (Alex Negara, a serial entrepreneur and founder of the Google Developer Group Moldova).
  7. Adaptive design (Joanna Chwastowska, senior Google engineer from Poland).
  8. Augmented reality (Vitaliy Zasadny, mobile developer, one of the founders of the Google Developers Group and Gamedev Community communities in Lviv).
  9. Google Glass and applications for them (Alexey Isachenko, founder of the Google Apps Russian community and representative of the Google Developers Group community in Moscow).
  10. Robotics (Suvorov Dmitry Andreevich, co-founder and Director of IT companies Vikron).
  11. Google Community Ecosystem (Aigul Zagidullina, community manager, Google Developer Group in Germany and Russia).

The program of the first day (April 11) will end the competition for Droidcon Moscow developers Hackaton . Presentation of projects, the final hakaton, the award ceremony will be held April 12.

The organizers of Droidcon Moscow in Digital October were the company Apps4All in partnership with the non-profit community of developers of the Google Developers Group .

Register for the conference and follow the events of Droidcon Moscow 2014 via the hashtag #DroidconMoscow.
