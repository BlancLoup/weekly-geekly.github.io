The world's first digital camera that shoots in YouTube format

The company Casio Computers has developed the world's first digital camera with support for shooting movies, optimized for YouTube. The device comes bundled with specialized software that allows you to upload videos to YouTube with just one click. We owe the appearance of the Exilim EX-S880 to a contract between Casio and Google (the owner, YouTube, if someone is not up to date), which gave Casio exclusive rights to use all the features of YouTube until the end of this year.
