Positive Development User Group Picnic: safe development for everyone



The company Positive Technologies organizes the Positive Development User Group Picnic - a closed informal meeting for developers and other IT specialists. On it, we will be happy to see those who are just planning to learn how to develop securely or who already have experience creating secure applications.

The technical program of the meeting will be the reports of our positive speakers:

  • Timur Yunusov, head of banking systems security, will talk about how hackers hack web applications, point out typical mistakes and share interesting cases;
  • Vladimir Kochetkov, head of the Application Inspector source code development system, will present ways to protect applications and practical techniques for safe development;
  • Valery Boronin, the head of the development team for analyzing the source codes of Application Inspector SSDL Edition, will tell you about the features and benefits of the Secure Software Development Lifecycle.

In addition, the program will be a couple of surprises. Guess what they are connected with, you will be helped by the recent habratopic Volodya Kochetkova :)

After the technical section, the participants will have an entertainment part with a barbecue, beer and the opportunity to share experiences or just chat with like-minded people.

The seminar will be held on August 10 at 15:30 . Participation is free, but you must first register . Hurry up, the number of places is limited! The address will be communicated to participants after confirmation of registration.
