Ask questions to the experts. How can developers get help from Intel?

Not much is known about the Intel Developer Relations Division. Meanwhile, it does a very necessary and useful business - it provides technical and marketing support to software developers. Much attention is paid to cooperation with developers in porting existing and developing new applications for Windows 8 and Android x86. New programs are constantly being opened, within the framework of which Intel provides devices for development, offers participation in co-marketing companies and so on.
