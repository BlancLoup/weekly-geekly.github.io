Samsung Startup Membership - a new program for startups in Russia

Samsung Electronics opens the Samsung Startup Membership (SSM) program in Russia, which will allow Russian technology startups to establish cooperation with a global electronics manufacturer at the local and international level.

Reception of applications for participation is open on the official website of the program.



This initiative is not the first in the framework of Samsung's interaction with startups.

So, in November last year, Samsung Connection Day took place at the Samsung Artificial Intelligence Center, a meeting with Russian start-ups to assess the possibilities for further cooperation with the company's business units in Russia and Korea. The meeting was attended by Samsung experts (including from headquarters in Korea) and representatives of Russian and international venture capital companies.

For example, here are some startups presented there:

  • Humanteq - assessment of the user's personal portrait and preferences
  • Mishka.ai - a platform for creating "smart" toys
  • Rate & Goods - information service for products available on the market
  • Total Vision - eye tracking technology for VR / AR devices
  • CGF Studio - recognition of facial expressions and human movements and their transfer to virtual avatars
  • Speereo - speech recognition for embedded solutions
  • Last.Backend - automation of building, deploying and managing applications
  • Printeltech - gas sensor for mass use
  • Dioram - ultra-precise positioning for devices of virtual and augmented reality
  • Expasoft - optimization of artificial intelligence solutions for embedded devices
  • Sense2Beat - diagnosis and monitoring of cardiac arrhythmias
  • VitaGraph - measurement and monitoring of a person‚Äôs pulse by video.



Participating startups will receive:

  • An assessment from the side - a look at the technology and its prospects in the ecosystem of Samsung products
  • Support in the promotion of technology and its presentation to key persons of the company
  • Priority participation in the next Samsung Connection Day and other events with leading experts and engineers of the company, as well as invited guests



You can leave a request at ssm.russia@samsung.com
