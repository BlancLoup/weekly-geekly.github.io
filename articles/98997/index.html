Yandex.Direct VS Carpet Bombing Sniper Rifle

ICQ receives a lot of questions from beginners (and not only) advertisers. The question that I hear from every second advertiser concerns the lack of understanding of the basic principle of working in context - the need to target their advertising to the target audience as clearly as possible.

Again, bad.
