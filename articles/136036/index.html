Motorola Mobility will soon begin selling its new Android smartphone, which solved the main problem of this OS. The new DROID RAZR MAXX will have a battery with a capacity of 3300 mAh! And this is a very solid increase in the duration of the Android-based smartphone.





Technical characteristics of DROID RAZR MAXX by Motorola:

  • Support communications standards: LTE B13 700 MHz; CDMA800 / 1900 MHz; GSM900
  • Dimensions: 130.7 x 68.9 x 8.99 mm
  • Weight: 145 gr.
  • Platform: Android 2.3.5 Gingerbread (with a planned upgrade to Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich)
  • Processor: Dual-core, 1.2 GHz
  • Display: 4.3-inch, Super AMOLED Advanced, 540x960 resolution
  • Main camera: 8 MP with autofocus and LED backlighting
  • Front camera: 1.3 MP
  • Memory: 1 GB of RAM, 16 GB (Built-in), 11.5 GB available to the user, slot for microSD memory cards
  • Communications: Wi-Fi 802.11b / g / n, Bluetooth v4.0 LE + EDR, USB 2.0 HS, Corporate Sync, DLNA, FOTA, 3.5 mm + EMU, aGPS, sGPS, GLONASS, Electronic compass
  • Battery: 3300 mAh
