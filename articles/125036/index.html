Finally - a bonus for those who will celebrate the Day of the Sysadmin at home. Recipe sisadminskogo cake " Beer mug":



Make this cake is easy - it is going from ready-made sources.

Ingredients:
Packing ready biscuits for the cake - 1 pc.
Shortbread cookies in the form of sticks - 1 pack.
Sour cream - 500 gr
Sugar - 1 cup
Whipped cream - 1 bottle
Drying "Shuttle" - 1 piece

Assembly:

A wide round glass cut circles from the finished biscuits.

Collect the "tower" from biscuit circles, coating each layer with sour cream and sugar.

Cover the sides of the cylinder with sour cream, glue the sticks in the manner of the ribs of the mug.



Glue the handle from the dryer. Decorate the top with "foam" from the can.

It is not forbidden to add a little rum to the cream from sour cream.
