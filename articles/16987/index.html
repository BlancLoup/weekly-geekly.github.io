Developers lifestyle

In this post I want to share links to interesting in my opinion videos that I found in Russian Channel 9 . These are not quite ordinary videos that we used to see there, there are no interviews with developers about how this or that product is implemented. These videos tell you exactly how staff live / live off campus.

The first video is an interview with Yuri Starikov, the first Soviet citizen at Microsoft. He talks about how he worked on Russian DOS, he wrote the Autluk Express (it turns out he was created by a team of 10 developers, 4 of which are Russians :) and many more interesting things. By the way, the uncle reminded Steve Wozniak with his appearance, apparently a beard :)

The second video was shot by three employees Igor Bekelman, Lyudmila Fokina and someone else. They make a small tour of Redmond and show how developers live :) Especially liked the moment about Ballmer on Boydarka :)

Taking this opportunity, I want to ask, can anyone know if Apple or Google have something like that?
