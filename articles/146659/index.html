Robot wins in stone-paper-scissors in 100% of cases

Engineers from Tokyo University have combined a machine vision system and a high-speed robot. It was a funny experiment , in which the robot plays the stone-paper-scissors game, recognizing the movement of the enemy.



Recognition of the shape of the hand is carried out in 1 millisecond. It is not surprising that with such speed the robot wins 100% of the time.
