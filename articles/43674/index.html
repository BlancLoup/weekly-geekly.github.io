Russian university Opera Software Tour begins



So, the schedule of the Russian university tour of Opera Software, organized as part of the Opera Education Program, is almost complete - there are few days before it begins. This is by far the largest event held by a Norwegian company in Russia: Opera representatives will visit seven cities and hold seminars at ten universities. The route is as follows: Vladivostok - Tomsk - Novosibirsk - Yekaterinburg - Perm - Moscow - St. Petersburg. Here is a table with a list of all universities with the date, time, and venue:

The topics of the seminars are as follows:
- Web technologies of tomorrow
- Web standards as a guarantor of the best Internet
- Features of the development of new generation browsers

Seminar participants from Opera Software will be:
Charles McKatiNeville - Head of Web Standards
Alexey Feldgendler - Opera developer for desktops
Ilya Shpankov - development manager

Everyone is invited to the seminars, admission is free (just in case it is better to clarify in advance the access control at universities where seminars will take place).

Also during this university tour, meetings with users will be held in Vladivostok (November 4, details ), Yekaterinburg (November 9, details are being clarified), in Perm (November 10, details are being clarified) and in Moscow (November 11, details are being specified).
