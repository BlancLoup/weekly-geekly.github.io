Regional Internet media: Part 4. Attracting visitors to the site, promotion

Attendance for online media is not exactly the same as circulation for a paper newspaper. The newspaper receives money from each sold number and advertising, and the Internet media, in general, only from advertising.

At the same time, in large advertisers, press officers and marketing specialists report, as a rule, according to formal criteria - numbers are important for them. For small companies, the real state of affairs is important, which they assess subjectively.

I assume that the number on the counter is an indicator, not a goal.


Before you begin to consider specific methods of attracting visitors to the site, I want to note that since we are doing regional media, the project‚Äôs target audience is local residents. They regularly visit your site, advertisers are guided by them.

In practice, the share of local visitors on popular regional sites is about 55% per week. We have 65%, our closest competitors have 56%, and the leading media attendance has 43%. At the most visited site in the region - ... 3%. In the new rating li.ru all this is visible.
