Devbar: news from the fields


We were silent for a long time, but this time was not in vain. The first negotiations with investors took place, but we had to refuse most of the proposals, due to unacceptable conditions for us. Over the past period, we have compiled a menu of an institution, started negotiations with design studios and finalized a business plan.

Previous Discussions: Discussion , Location Choice , Survey Results



Menu


In drawing up the menu, we focused on your advice, comments and survey results. We tried to take into account most of the wishes, but, naturally, we could miss something or not notice, so we will be extremely grateful for the comments in the comments. Basically, they were guided not by quantity but by quality, several well-prepared dishes are much better than a long list

The drinks

Let's start with the most important component of the menu - beer. Five sorts of beer are planned: Hugarden, Guiness, Bellevue Creek, Heinicen, and Vasileoostrovskoye Dark. According to my observations, the first two are the most adequate and pleasant to the taste, the third was added at the request of the beautiful half of the IT industry, while the latter are focused on IT-youth.
Approximate prices for beer: 120-250 rubles

Naturally, we will have wine, brandy, whiskey, tequila and champagne (how not to celebrate the release). The range will not be huge, but we promise that everything will be of high quality and well chosen.

There were a lot of requests for good, not from tea bags, tea and coffee. Naturally, we could not ignore requests from future visitors (especially since I myself like to sit with a cup of good tea or coffee). As a result, we expect to include in the menu 3 types of teas (including Chinese, for which a lot of people called for), classic coffee options (espresso, lat, American, etc.) and hot chocolate.
Our knowledge in this area is not very great, would be grateful to people who are ready to advise coffee machines and dishes for tea, as well as help with the choice of specific varieties of teas. So far we have draft options, but I would like to hear the opinion of experts.
Approximate prices for tea: 180 - 250 per teapot
For coffee: 90 - 180 rubles

Of course, we will have juice and fruit juice. It will probably be even water.

Food

Based on our "bar" experience, we decided to dwell on a pair of classic steaks (chicken, salmon, pork) and salads (Caesar, Greek, fruit). Naturally, there will be dumplings "Odminskie", without them nowhere.
Food prices: 200-350 rubles
Prices for salads: 200-250 rubles

Desserts will be a little (ice cream, cocktails), without exotics.

Snacks

All that you used to see in bars will be with us. Cheese sticks (in the Tower, by the way, are wonderful, if anyone knows), croutons and chicken wings. We will try to cook everything at home, I hope, we will do without an ambulance.

Facilities Map


As we promised, we lay out a map , on the basis of which we were looking for a suitable place for our bar.

We will be grateful if you help us to expand and supplement it.

A couple of words about the logo



There were many questions about the logo of our institution. Why Guy Fox? What does he have to do with it? First, we were looking for a well-known mark in the IT community. Secondly, our logo should reflect the spirit of freedom that will be in our bar. As a result, we stopped at the mask of Guy Fawkes, who now, in my opinion, personifies the free Internet and is closest to our ideals.

The implementation of the volumetric logo was made by inteo , for which many thanks to him and tea.
