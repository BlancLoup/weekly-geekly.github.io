Liveblog Camera

If you had some kind of trouble on the street, for example, a car splashed on you or slipped and fell unsuccessfully, and you see that a passer-by is watching and smilingly pressing a button near the ear - know that in a minute a funny video will be published on the Internet. This is how the new Looxcie video camera , designed specifically for real-time blogging, works.

The video camera is fixed on the ear and is constantly working. Built-in memory is enough for five hours of shooting in MP4 format, and then it starts to be overwritten over the old one. At any time, you can click the Instant Clip button - and the last 30 seconds of the video via Bluetooth and 3G on your smartphone will be sent to Facebook or YouTube.

At the moment, Looxcie works only with smartphones on the Android 2.0 platform, although the manufacturer promises to add support for the iPhone within two months.
