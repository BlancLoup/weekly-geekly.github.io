East is a delicate matter. Insane Japanese Gadgets Review

The Japanese, one of the most technologically advanced nations, are renowned for their extraordinary approach to many things, ranging from humor, advertising and entertainment to gadgets. But if you can get acquainted with the masterpieces of Japanese advertising or show business without any problems on YouTube, then with gadgets, which are often intended solely for the domestic market, things are much more complicated.



Fortunately, there is such a thing as an eBay auction. On it you can get acquainted with the goods from all over the world not only visually, but also physically. And if today we are talking about Japan, then we present to your attention a very unusual shop JapanTrends , in sections of which you stumble upon Unique Japan . This section is the best way to demonstrate the uniqueness of modern Japanese culture. Here are a couple of examples:

Robot Robovie-X
Let me, but what Japanese shop without robots in stock? For a rather impressive $ 1237 you are offered a real robot. More precisely, the buyer will have to assemble the robot independently. Funny Robovie-X is endowed with a set of all kinds of sensors, can dance, play football and perform many other actions, but most importantly, if desired, the user can re-buy additional sensors and independently modify Robovie-X.



Toy for french fries and chips Calbee Jagarico Panic
If everything is more or less clear with the robot, the device called Calbee Jagarico Panic clearly shows the gap between the Russian and Japanese games. The meaning of Calbee Jagarico Panic is that an open package with french fries or chips is placed under the giraffe's upper jaw.



Further, each player at the table opens the jaw cover and pulls one piece of potatoes. If nothing happens to the toy, then the next player makes his move. If chips are thrown away, the person leaves the game. That's the whole idea :) Feel for yourself all the excitement of the Japanese game Calbee Jagarico Panic for $ 59.00 . Potatoes, unfortunately, are not included.

Sticker Printer Rilakkuma Label Writer
Have you always dreamed of printing labels for rolling up, jars with spices and other things not on a sheet torn out of a notebook, but on a sticky tape with bears? Then the Rilakkuma Label Writer portable printer is just what you need.



It is clear that all the stickers are printed in Japanese hieroglyphs, so bears are not enough here alone. You can glue printed labels on pencils, markers, various containers (for example, with spices), etc. But in order to use the mini-printer for its intended purpose, we advise, nevertheless, to deal with the Japanese language. Price nyashnogo printer - $ 115 .

Yozora Oekaki digital drawing pen for iPhone and iPad
This device can be called a digital marker, although its functions are not quite normal. The device interacts with a special application for the iPhone and iPad and allows you to draw luminous lines on the display of your smartphone / tablet without physical touch.



With the help of such digital markers you can create new or edit old photos. Similar effects can be achieved using the same Photoshop, but the creators of Yozora Oekaki argue that their development is much easier and more interesting to use. The price of one marker is $ 70.00 .
