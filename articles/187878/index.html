B1 Basis Band: watch-bioregistrar. Gamification of a healthy lifestyle (first review in Russian)

The dream, perhaps, of any IT specialist is to automate life and put monitoring on everything. There is still a long way off, and there are already a clock that allows you to record a whole range of bio-indicators and send them to the ‚Äúcloud‚Äù for processing and analysis, and I happened to purchase them recently.

.

The B1 Basis Band gadget, one of the nominees of the CES-2012 exhibition, is the first portable bio-recorder that monitors health with the help of sensors measuring human heart rhythm, skin moisture, movement, temperature. They monitor the time spent on sleep, count the number of calories burned. Data via USB and bluetooth are transferred to a computer or mobile phone and recorded in an online profile. In the future we will call this bioregistration clock for simplicity. Moreover, it is also really a watch.

B1 Basis band continues the line of Fitbit, Jawbone, Nike Fuelband, complementing the usual with completely new features that have not been in any portable devices yet. Is it worth buying money spent?



After ordering on the MyBasis.com website , the watch arrived pretty quickly, in this box:





About two days from the time of the order to the shipper in the States, and about two weeks for delivery to Russia by express mail. The total cost is $ 199 for the watch itself and $ 73 for shipping. For comparison, the Jawbone Up in Russia costs 5-7 thousand with a significantly weaker functionality, that is, the Basis B1 band is significantly more expensive.

To start working hours require activation via the Internet. Until then, an indication is displayed on the LCD to go to mybasis.com/go. The activation program put the driver on the laptop, and activated the clock.



When you connect the watch to a USB port or via bluetooth to an android phone, the gadget resets the log of changes in body temperature, pulse, skin moisture, and pedometer indicators to its ‚Äúcloud‚Äù. The clock itself is quite ‚Äústupid‚Äù, like the applications on Windows / Mac / Android: all the ‚Äúsmart‚Äù functionality is available in the personal account on the site. It is possible to extract a lot of knowledge from the collected information, it would be inconvenient to constantly update the software, and there will be more capacities in the ‚Äúcloud‚Äù.

Included with the clock is a very simple booklet and cradle.



By the way, it's funny that the watch has no time setting function. Nowhere. Neither the program nor the clock itself. Just because they don‚Äôt know how much time they can‚Äôt use, their use is useless without the Internet, and they synchronize time with the server when they access the network. When registering, the time zone is indicated - this is enough.

Cradle

To charge the clock is such an original cradle. You can wear it on your watch without removing it from your hands.





The cradle is put on the watch from above and snaps gently, and the watch can be not removed from the hands. It is not very tight so that in case of a jerk it is easy to unfasten oneself - in this case the risk of damaging the cradle or pulling the laptop off the table is reduced.

Design

The design of the watch is, of course, controversial. It is very concise and slightly old-fashioned. This has some charm. But I would not say that the watches are too big, and certainly they are not heavy. The weight of the watch is a little over 40 grams.

Software for Android / iOS is extremely pleasant, as is the design of your personal account. It is clearly seen that the interface was worked out carefully.

Clock itself

Strap

The strap is made of soft-touch plastic. The kit includes a strap of a single color. At the moment when I bought the watch, only models with black straps were available for order. You can buy straps in addition, with the author's design. Latch strap on the watch does not look very reliable, but I have not spontaneously unfastened (people on the network write that it happens).

Screen

Information on the clock is displayed in inverse form on a one-inch transflective LCD screen. Like other LCDs, the sun goes blind, the backlight is very economical, read - weak.

By the way, this is not written anywhere, but when you raise your hand to see how much time, in most cases, the clock understands this gesture and turns on the backlight for a couple of seconds.

On the front side we see a 1-inch LCD screen, four touch buttons on the edges and one mechanical on the right side. Touch buttons are responsible for 1) backlight 2) switching to the date-day of the week 3) scrolling up 4) scrolling down. Through scrolling, HEART RATE (pulse), STEPS (pedometer), CALORIES (calories) are switched. The temperature of the body and the humidity of the skin from the watch screen cannot be viewed only in the personal account. The side button opens the bluetooth settings menu and the ‚ÄúSYNC NOW‚Äù item. Synchronization via bluetooth is carried out with an Android / iOS phone.

On the screen in the clock mode, the actual time, the sign of connecting to bluetooth, the battery charge and the progress bar are displayed.

Turn over the clock

On the reverse side of the clock are biosensors. In the very center there are two green LEDs that constantly shine in the hand, and between them a sensor that analyzes reflection. This is enough to read the pulse. Sensors on the edges are designed to collect information about the temperature and humidity of the skin.





By the way, the presence of pulse sensors in the clock is one of the distinctive "chips" of this gadget. This is nowhere else. Other fitness watches that can measure the pulse require you to wear special straps on your chest that hold the sensors against the heart. This makes sense during training, but not during the day. Watch B1 Basis Band is different in that it collects this information during the day constantly, until the owner removes them from his hands.

Battery

It looks like the average battery life is several days with bluetooth enabled. Convenience charging makes it perfectly acceptable. After the warning, LOW BATTERY is about half a day to find charging.

Pedometer

For the pedometer, a three-axis gyro is built into the clock, which is why the accuracy is rather high.

Soft

As I wrote, the whole "mathematics" is in the "cloud". That is, the accumulated data of the clock is sent to the server, and then they are visualized there in the form of interactive diagrams, grouped, analyzed, the system tries to group the activity according to characteristic data from the sensors.

Sleep tracking

For example, if you walk for half an hour, and then sit down at a computer, the clock (more precisely, the server system) understands when the period of the walk began and ended. If you fall asleep with them, then the watch understands that it is not just a person lying on the bed, but asleep - there is a characteristic decrease in the pulse, moisture of the skin, movements become more rare, etc. If the dream is uneven and the owner of the clock wakes up several times at night, then they are ‚Äúwhipped‚Äù and are shown in the morning in the reports. The duration of sleep is recorded with an accuracy of minutes, and the average pulse during sleep is also shown. On the basis of this and other information collected, the indicator of sleep quality is calculated on a scale from 0 to 100. It remains only to understand why I need to know all this :-)

The system automatically groups by activity. For example, he understands when sleep began, when it ended, when the walk began, when it ended. Everything in exact numbers and chronological order is displayed in the personal account.
