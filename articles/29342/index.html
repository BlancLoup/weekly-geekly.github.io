Funny USB Heater

The regiment of entertaining USB gadgets, which in their diversity are represented at workplaces in numerous offices, has arrived. Any light bulbs, refrigerators and fans - is complete. But they lack conceptualism and zest. But the device Heat Me from designer Mary Kawamura is simply awesome. If not in practical terms, then simply because of its unusualness.

Cool coffee - press the button on the heater, which is a cup. Trite? And if the heater is made in the form of a boiler? Which one should stick in a cup? Yeah. This reminded me too of Soviet childhood and numerous trips on business trips. Of course, this is not a mega-classic in the form of a kettle of blades. But the "toy" is great.

It is a pity that really boil the water will not work. Just reheat. And thanks for that.
