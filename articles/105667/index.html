Word for word, crossword puzzle on the table



Every word has a definition, but not every definition has its own word. Therefore, there is now the Word War , which is designed to restore the balance between justice and interjections. Between common sense and celery, overgrown. By the way, between the legs.

The Word War project is a warm-up for copywriters and all those who have unencumbered creativity and time to be brutally killed.

The essence is simple - the words here need not guess, but invent. New brands, slogans, non-existent concepts, ministries, public associations, numerals and interjections.



The goal of the project, created in the now fashionable timekilling genre, is to train in the naming of non-existent companies, compete in wit, slightly enrich the Russian language and neutralize competitors, distracting them from work.



There are two versions - simple and ultra-hyper-super-extra.
In simple copywriters are not limited to inventing anything other than their own imagination, wit and limitation.
Well, for the guys with steel biceps prepared something more, chrome shining - a crossword puzzle of non-existent words.



Intersecting words that do not exist should fit into a randomly generated grid. In order not to climb on the wall at all, the experienced copywriter has assistants in the form of a saw, glue and a supercursor, which helps to change the task in emergency situations.



It is important that participate in the "Wordward" can be different. First, naturally, invent new words. Do not like guessing - invent new puzzles.
Completely lazy - register and vote for other people's answers. Or against.



A thousand puzzles are already waiting for their saviors, and the passive part of humanity craves for new words and definitions. Center for the study of nostrils and brand of cigarettes with a taste of life . The name of the airship Fyodor Konyukhov and the name of a secret society of music fans stealing car radios . The spell that stops the bus , and the name of the cartoon about the USB-socket . New element of the periodic table and the eighth note . All this requires names. New.
