Screenplay for a new horror movie "Impossible Things" written with the participation of AI



Artificial intelligence (the so-called weak form of it) has recently been closely engaged in art. AI processes photos , draws pictures , creates an arrangement for musical works, generates room designs and people's faces. Now artificial intelligence is engaged in writing a script for a horror movie.

Why attract AI? According to the authors of the project, now so many lines of the plot have been developed for films, that it is difficult to invent something new. But you can make a movie of the elements of the plot lines of existing works. The authors of the new scenarios usually do this, but they use, firstly, only a small part of the material - what they remember. Secondly, it is difficult for people to objectively evaluate both individual storylines and the resulting scenario as a whole. As a result, 87% of new films fail at the box office.

Artificial intelligence can analyze huge amounts of data, ranking information according to various criteria. The problem with AI is that it cannot compose a whole plot, attractive to a person and logical in general. An example is the short film "Sunspring", the script to which is entirely written by the AI.
