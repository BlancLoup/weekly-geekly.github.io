The little robot controls the plane like a real pilot.

Hi, Habr!

Perhaps unmanned aircraft in the future will take us on vacation, but this is still far away. Autopilots, on the other hand, are not uncommon. But after all, aircraft for passengers most often were created in such a way as to be controlled by a person.

The PIBOT robot pilot from South Korean developers uses data from the sensors of the aircraft, and what he sees with the camera. He controls the plane with his own hands. Its creators claim that in some areas it even surpasses the person - for example, it keeps the plane smoother on takeoff and landing.

Under the cut - a video about how Piotb works.



So far this robot is piloted only by an airplane simulator, so that we will not be able to see it as a pilot for international flights.

In addition to improving piloting skills, the PIBOT creators are working on remote control. It is not so difficult to imagine a future in which robots controlled from the ground will be in place of pilots of flesh and blood.
