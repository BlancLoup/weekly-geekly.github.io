Facts and figures about Web Ready 2012

At the end of November in St. Petersburg the annual competition of innovative projects WebReady was completed . Start-ups from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Kazan and Perm became the winners of the competition.
The first place among Internet projects was taken by the St. Petersburg startup Qbaka - a service for automatic collection and analysis of JavaScript errors on web services.
Penxy Moscow project was recognized as the best startup in the field of mobile technologies.
The second and third places among Internet startups were taken by the RealSpeaker project from Kazan and RealtimeBoard from Perm. In the nomination ‚ÄúThe Best Startup in the Field of Mobile Technologies‚Äù, the St. Petersburg project MedM was the second , and Moscow NormSahar was the third.

This year, 584 start-ups from 90 cities and 13 countries fought to win the competition. Competition is growing from year to year.


Directions of 584 projects of the Web Ready-2012 participants:


The most popular areas of projects were SaaS (189 applications) and Everyday Services (186). More than half of the projects - 389 - are developed at their own expense. At the same time, over 30% (182 startups) have already received investments. Mostly submitted projects among the ways to monetize choose a subscription to services or content, as well as the sale of advertising.


Some statistics about the 38 semi - finalists of the competition:
27 startups that entered the semifinals were launched in 2012. More than 85% of them are already running a business. 13 projects are developing at their own expense. Over 50% in search of investment.
