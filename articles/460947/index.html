WASM Meet Up 1/08

We invite you to the meeting dedicated to WebAssembly technology, which will be held on August 1 at the Raiffeisenbank site in Nagatino. We listen to the reports of Raiffeisen Digital, Parity Technologies and Mozilla directly from San Francisco.



Ilya Chertorylsky, Raiffeisenbank
Webassembly SPA frameworks. Overview. And blazor based examples


Consider the existing WA SPA and their capabilities. Let's talk about the possible future of such decisions.

Nikolay Wolf, Parity
Lightbeam compiler


At Parity Techologies, we have big plans for WebAssembly. We will tell you why we need such a compiler, and why others may need it.

Dan Gohman, Mozilla
WASI: An API for non-Web WebAssembly


the report will be read in English via teleconference

To help take WebAssembly outside the browser, we're building WASI, the WebAssembly System Interface. It's a standard API for WebAssembly applications to use outside of the Web, which extends WebAssembly's portability and sandboxing philosophy into the API space. I'll talk about how we got here, what you can do with it today, and where we're going!
