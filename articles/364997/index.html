How many users actually have Google+

In 2015, only 0.3% of profiles wrote


Edward Morbius conducted a small study of the activity of Google+ profiles and came to the conclusion that very few people leave entries on this social networking site. According to his estimate, only about 4-6 million users have written anything since the beginning of the year. Without being particularly cautious, some publications cited his findings, stating that no one uses the site at all.

The total number of users was determined by sitemap files, it is about 2.2 billion profiles. Only about 9.2% showed at least some activity on the site, that is, approximately 200 million people left records of any kind at least once. About 37% have recent comments on YouTube. The fact is that after combining some functions, the comment under any video gets on Google+. Even at 8.1% of the last entry, there is a change of photo - when you upload a new avatar, a message appears in the tape about it.
