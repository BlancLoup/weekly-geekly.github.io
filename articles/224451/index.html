How much is the apple market today?

The Apple app market, or Apple App Store , consumes and begets money. The more you invest, the higher the return. However, earned is not always more spent. Recently, a typical schedule of spent / received funds has suddenly formed in my head.



Green line - a graph of the money spent. What is spent on? For the purchase of traffic . As a result, your application goes to the TOP100, TOP25 or ( wow !!) TOP10 USA and starts to make money. The red line is earned. If you earned more, then the red line on the graph is above the green and you are resting in Hawaii, pouring the white sand of Waikiki between your palms.
-What is the point W?, - the smart reader will ask.

In 2011, it was equal to $ 5,000, this was enough to enter the TOP10 . The application brought at least $ 1,000 per day. Those times are gone. Now in 2014, you need to spend $ 50,000, applications from the TOP10 earn at least $ 10,000 per day per day.

I want to note that we are talking about successful applications, which, after an artificial jump in the TOP , themselves begin to raise their rating and stay on the crest of success for at least 5 days. Mathematically, a red parabola can be characterized by an exponent. If the exponent is greater than 1 - this is cool, you and your application are great. If less is 1, we get a blue graph and a donut hole. That is, how much money you do not invest - the tree will not grow.

How to determine the exponent for your application? You do not need to spend 50,000 dollars.
