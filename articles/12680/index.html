Google Intranet Search Screenshots

All of us at least once used search from Google - the world leader among search engines. Have you ever wondered how the internal search engine of this largest (12 thousand employees, 56 offices around the world and 100 million documents on the intranet) company looks like?

Naturally, all the latest search technologies are used and tested around ...

According to Google‚Äôs official corporate blog, the company uses the Google Search Appliance (its own integrated hardware and software for corporate search) with a bunch of unique and interesting things to search the intranet.

On the internal search, Google is running around new technologies - for this, there is a special flag under the search field that allows employees to search for information using an alternative interface, where, in fact, experiments are conducted, such as different search results, a new interesting implementation of OneBox ... After a successful internal testing, technology has every chance to get to the main page.
