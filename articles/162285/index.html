Arduino Esplora

New Arduino released, in gamepad form factor

Passport photo:




Link to the controller page.

What is on it:



Esplora has the following on board:


  • Analog joystick (two X and Y axes) with a button in the center.
  • 4 buttons on the right side of the board.
  • Linear potentiometer.
  • Microphone.
  • Light sensor.
  • Thermometer
  • accelerometer (three axes X, Y, and Z)
  • Buzzer.
  • Tri-color LED (RGB).
  • 2 inputs for connecting TinkerKit sensors.
  • 2 outputs for TinkerKit.
  • Connector for connecting TFT displays, SD cards and any other devices using SPI protocol.
