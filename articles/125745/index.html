Mobile Developer Day in Odessa, August 19, 2011



August 19 in Odessa will be Mobile Developer Day UA - the first Ukrainian day of the developer of mobile applications, games and services. At this conference, those who write code for iOs, Android, Bada and other mobile device platforms gather. Meet and share the experience of those who think through marketing and monetize mobile applications and services. Within 2 years the event takes place in Russia, in Ukraine it is held for the first time.

On MDDAY, there are always heated discussions about platforms, features of code, methods of promotion, social responsibility, and even the ethics of creating and promoting mobile software. As part of MDDAY UA, speakers and participants will sort out cases and share experiences in creating and promoting mobile applications. Representatives of Nokia, Microsoft, Samsung, i-Free, talented programmers for mobile platforms, project owners, promotion specialists in the AppStore and Android Market, publishers, start-ups, investors, and leading mobile operators in Russia and Ukraine are invited to participate in the program.

Mobile Developer Day - a narrowly themed event. At the conference there are developers for whom mobile applications are a business, as well as creators of client mobile applications for banks, federal media, large companies and even private organizations. Separate reports are devoted to mobile games - what trends are on the rise and how to make money on a free mobile game. The subject of the conference is divided into several sections - development, management, promotion, monetization, information security, etc. - all aspects of the creation of mobile applications, which allows you to cover the whole theme in general.

Mobile Developer Day UA Conference will be held in Odessa on August 19 at the Londonskaya Hotel .

The event is designed for 200 people. Registration fee - 450 hryvnia (1 575 rubles).

For startups participation is free. Read more at http://ua.mdday.ru/startapps/

To register now and get more information, please visit ua.mdday.ru

If you want to speak at the conference with a report, send your application with the topic of the report to md@mdday.ru , and you will receive a prompt response as soon as possible .
