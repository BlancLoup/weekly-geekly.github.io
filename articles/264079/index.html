How much does InfoboxCloud cost?


You can calculate the cost of your IT infrastructure in the cloud on the InfoboxCloud calculator . The calculation will be performed for a month, if the servers are constantly on, you do not change the amount of resources and do not enable autoscaling. Payment is made on the fact of allocated resources to servers in the control panel. Thus, if you turn off the server, you do not pay for the processor and RAM resources. If you change the amount of available resources for servers, this will be taken into account: as the load increases, resources will be enough, and if you decrease, you will not overpay for unused resources.
