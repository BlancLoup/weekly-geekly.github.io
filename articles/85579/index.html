Update news for HTC Hero on Android 2.1

Stumbling once again on an interesting program for Android, which does not start on Android 1.5, which is standard on the HTC Hero, decided to monitor the news about the long-promised update to 2.0-2.1.

Initially, HTC promised to release 2.0-2.1 update before the new year, and now there is no news on the official website.
As it turned out, a little more than a week ago at the Mobile World Congress it was announced that the firmware update for HTC Hero, containing Android 2.1 + updated Sense (HTC proprietary interface), will be released soon.

The deadlines are not exactly defined, but according to fonehome.co.uk and slashgear.com, the update is worth the wait in March.
There is also information from T-Mobile that for their versions of Hero and G2 they will release an update in late March - early April (possibly after the release of a common firmware).
