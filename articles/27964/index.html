The best music search engine

I think for many it will be old, but I will risk it)

Most recently came across an awesome resource - worryaboutyou.com .
It allows you to find almost any of the existing music tracks (published officially and not only), listen to it in a convenient interface and (!) Completely free and with a normal download speed. Restrictions also not noticed; The bitrate of the tracks (subjectively) is excellent. Design made in a minimalist style (adore).

In fact, the site is a huge pile of links to various open sources. Often used vkontakte.

So far I have found everything that I requested - and the little known noize mc in the world and relatively popular Blank and Jones ...
