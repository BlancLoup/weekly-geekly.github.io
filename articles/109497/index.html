Racios iPhone version!

The project Racios.com has its first mobile application! Now you can at any time add a fresh idea to your Racios plan or just check it out!




For those who are not familiar with the project Racios.com , a short description: Racios is an online time management service that is focused on helping to achieve long-term goals.

Racios.com is a tool that will allow you to:
  • Visually plan your time;
  • Focus the person on the most important goals;
  • Collect and systematize information for decision making;
  • Manage your plan - online web from any computer + from your mobile phone (iPhone, iPad, Android);
  • Have personal and "social" tools of motivation (based on the social network in which users share TM information and declare their goals);
  • Participate in webinars telling about the Time Management philosophy (TM) and its tools (most of them are implemented in Racios.com);

Racios iPhone version


  • Racios iPhone works autonomously and synchronizes with the web version on demand.
  • The basic functionality of the web version for iPhone is duplicated: all plans are available (annual, monthly, weekly and daily), a task box, projects and missed tasks.
  • Implemented the system of setting child tasks and tracking the dynamics of execution in progress of the parent goal bar
  • Integration of time management applications: pinery, regular business, timekeeping, corporate version, and geo task binding - are planned for the 2nd phase of development.
  • In the near future, an update will be released with internal reminders of planned events and a cleaned-up design.


Of course, there is something else to improve both in usability and in introducing new features, and we are working on it!
Within 2-3 months, iPhone synchronization with the web version will be available to all categories of Racios users. In the Apple Store, the application will remain free and you will be able to use all its functionality offline.
