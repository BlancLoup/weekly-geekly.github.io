We want the standards governing the senders and recipients to appear in our market, so we urge all newsletters to work in a civilized manner and not to send letters without being able to unsubscribe. This step will not affect the senders in which it is in any way. They will be able, as before, to manage their relationships with subscribers. We only added to our standards for mass mailings .
