Service "Yandex.Taxi" earned in Minsk


Moscow "Yandex.Taxi" with lightboxes on the roofs. In Minsk, cars usually look normal and are no different from Capital 135 cars; this is the technological partner of Yandex.Taxi.

Good news for all who use taxi services in Minsk. Yesterday at 19:00, the Yandex.Taxi service officially started work here, and its prices are even lower than those of the recently launched UberX . There is no additional charge during the trip. True, there is no free first trip.

The cost of the minimum trip is 40,000 rubles (141 RUB). This includes landing and the first 3 km, each subsequent kilometer costs 5900 (21 RUB) in the city, 8900 (31 RUB) - outside the city.

The service automatically locates the customer and sends the nearest taxi to him. After the trip, the client assesses the driver on a five-point scale.

The calculation takes place in cash. In the near future, the possibility of non-cash payment for travel will appear - it will be possible to tie a bank card to a mobile application.

Minsk became the first city outside of Russia, where Yandex.Taxi worked. In total, the taxi aggregator from Yandex is available in 16 cities, including Moscow, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, and Sochi. In total, more than 35 thousand cars out of 450 taxi stations and control rooms are connected to Yandex.Taxi. In Moscow, Yandex takes 11% of its income from taxis, in St. Petersburg - 9%, while in Minsk - while 0%.
