How many earned Google and AdSense-sites for 2006.

As expected , yesterday Google announced its financial results for the last quarter of last year and the whole of 2006 as a whole.

So, in the fourth quarter, the company earned $ 3.21 billion, that is, 67% more than in the fourth quarter of 2005, and 19% more than the third quarter of 2006.

This total revenue, which does not include traffic acquisition costs, which amounted to 976 million dollars, that is, almost a third of the total amount.
The cost of acquiring traffic is a part of the revenue paid to Google partners, mostly related to payment sites with AdSense, which amounted to 916 million dollars.

Income sources are as follows:
62% (1.98 billion) - revenue from their own sites,
37% (1.20 billion) - revenue from partner sites (through the AdSense program).
As in the third quarter, the company received 44% of all revenues from sources outside the United States.

The total number of paid clicks (on their own sites and partner sites) increased by 61% compared to Q4. 2005 and 22% to III square. 2006

For the whole of 2006, the company's revenues amounted to $ 10.6 billion, which is 72% more than in 2005.

The company's net profit for the fourth quarter of 2006 amounted to 1.03 billion, and for the entire 2006 - 3.077 billion dollars.
