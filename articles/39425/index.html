Google Chrome as an Apple ideas promoter.

Most of the new ideas for Google Chrome are taken from Apple Safari:
- In 2007, with the release of the iPhone, Apple made an attempt to attract users to use only Web-based applications. Then Apple announced that only Web applications would work on the iPhone (this was actively discussed in the article ‚ÄúWeb-apps & Apple Safari‚Äù )! Likely time of full-fledged Web-Apps has not come yet.

- The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãattracting users to use Web-Apps exclusively has been bothering for a long time and for the last few years Apple has been actively implemented:
