Minuses


Index numbering

The biggest disadvantage is the numbering of pages with post titles: the oldest page of posts should have the number 1, and with the most recent posts - the maximum number. While browsing and opening in the new tabs of the article, reading them, in the meantime new posts are added, and the page number with the headings is already changing, and it turns out not very nice.



Looping

The logo on the main page is a link to the main page. It has long been known that references to themselves are harmful. Besides, it is confusing. This also applies to the PDA version .


Non-obvious search

There is a separate search for posts, questions and answers, blogs, etc., but on the search page there is no possibility to specify the scope of the search. Immediately you can not guess that such a possibility actually is - there is in each of the relevant sections.


Readability

Habrahabr is, first of all, a resource for reading. However, a regular means of increasing or decreasing the font was not found. At the moment, you can be content with only the means of browsers. In addition, Sans serif is not the best font solution for text articles. Look and compare the readability and reduction of eye strain with slightly increased font.

In the general list of the "People" section, sorting is by rating only. What about karma? And alphabetically? And in chronological order? In the lists by city - obviously, sorting by registration date was done. And why is the main list not sorted by date of registration? Why are the lists by city not sorted by rating? ..


pros


Now - about the pros: a) of which there are more, b) they are much fatter than the deadly minuses listed above.

The site has little advertising, and it is unobtrusive.
