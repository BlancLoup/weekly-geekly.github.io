PISA (Program for International Assessment) is an international program conducted by the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) to monitor the level of basic knowledge of young people aged 15 years from 65 countries, including Russia. The study is conducted every three years, it assesses skills and knowledge in three main areas - reading, scientific literacy and mathematics (by "reading" refers to the ability to read and understand what is written).
