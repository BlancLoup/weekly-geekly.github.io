01/24/08 - Presentation of the winners of the HSE business plans competition {5k} 2007

What is it all about?

The HSE Business Plan Competition is similar to Western universities (Harvard, Stanford, MIT, Oxford). The leader (the initiator of the business idea) assembles a team that works on a business plan (an investment presentation form familiar to the investor) and tries to convince the jury of the success of their project. The competitive process is as close as possible to real communication between investors and businessmen. Work on your project and immersion in the atmosphere of business shows students the reality and attractiveness of an entrepreneur's career. In addition, it is an opportunity to teach business - after all, you will not learn entrepreneurship at your desk.
In February 2007, the Burtniki brothers studied at the Graduate School of Economics at the Higher School of Economics. They learned about the competition of business projects and decided to send their application, not really counting on success. The guys from their team worked in different companies, but in the process of the competition they were so inspired by the idea that when it was completed, they quit to focus on the project. Team

At the stage of the competition in our original team was 8 people. Now 12. The team changed and sifted. Everyone in the team should have one goal - to do business. If the goals do not coincide, then after the completion of the competition it is necessary to look for a replacement for those who simply studied. In a team, ideally, there should be people with different backgrounds, with different knowledge, specialists in their field. For example, a team of three programmers should find an economist if they want to finally attract an investor.
