The app RosYama for iPhone


At the end of May, lawyer and social activist Alexei Navalny presented a project to combat the disgusting quality of Russian roads and the inaction of Russian officials - RosYam. The principle of the service is simple: on the website rosyama.ru, anyone can post a photo and data of the road defect (pit, protruding rails, open hatches, etc.) that violate GOST 50597‚àí93 (Roads and streets).


After that, the site automatically generates a complaint to the traffic police, and if no action is taken, then to the prosecutor's office. The complaint itself must be saved or printed, and then sent to the desired recipient.

To take advantage of RosYam, a user must register or log in using one of his social media accounts.

The RosYam application for the iPhone, which appeared on iTunes on July 15, completely exempts the registration procedure and the need to go to the post office. This application allows you to send a complaint about the state of the road to the mailbox of the relevant department of traffic police in writing with notification. Notification of its receipt by traffic police representatives will come to the applicant at the specified mailing address. The price includes the tariffs set by ‚ÄúMail of Russia‚Äù and the AppStore commission.

How it works?




The user only needs to take a picture of the defect, fill in the information and data of the applicant. After this, the complaint text will be available in pdf format. It can be printed out or sent to an email address through an email client. The most important thing is that the complaint text is automatically sent to the traffic police office mailbox, which is responsible for the quality of the road surface in the specified area. You do not need to go to the post office. The application will send a complaint immediately after its creation.

When using the rosyama.ru website, the applicant pays for the service of sending a complaint at the post office. The RosYam application for the iPhone saves time: the payment of a complaint is simply deducted from the account in the AppStore.

Results of the first days



In addition to those users who are waiting for feedback, there are active fighters with the current state of the roads. During the first week, more than 1,000 iPhone owners from different regions of the Russian Federation installed the application. Representatives of the traffic police of the cities of Yoshkar-Ola, Yekaterinburg, Lyubertsy, Moscow and Tyumen were the first recipients of complaints from users of the RosYam application.

Notice of receipt of the complaint by representatives of the traffic police comes to the applicant's mailbox. Application developers track mail complaint handling, according to which none of them was ignored.
