Manufacturing:
First we need to choose a place where the fan will be, I recommend to choose as close as possible to the end of the folder, and of course we take into account the location of the ventilation holes of the laptop and more, it is advisable to install the fan right under the hottest parts of the laptop, this is usually a processor or a video card, or both in one person (video card integrated into the processor), such as mine. Someone will probably ask if you have an integrated video card, then most likely there can be no overheating, then why bother with an additional cooling system? The answer is simple - purely sporting interest, the idea is very simple, and everything except for the folder I had was available.

After we have chosen the optimal location of the fan, or fans (yes, the folder size allows you to install two 120 mm fans, if there is such a need, besides, this will give the whole structure stiffness, which is obviously not enough here, but more on that later) Mark the part that needs to be cut:
