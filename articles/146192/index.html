PaymentGate: PaySto Universal Payments

When the payment service used by an enterprise or individual entrepreneur is universal, all participants in the workflow remain in an advantageous and comfortable position. Indeed, in this case, payments can be realized in one fell swoop, and it does not matter who the company pays for - with contractors, suppliers or other partners.


This payment format is offered by the PaySto service as part of its PaymentGate product. In addition to the fact that payments to partners are fully automated, they are also universal. This means that payments can be implemented in different forms at the same time. Suppose different contractors and partners prefer to receive payment in various forms. It is more convenient for someone when they transfer funds to a bank account or a plastic card, someone chooses electronic money, while others prefer a money transfer.

Both the minimum / maximum payment amount and the number of payments in general are not limited to PaymentGate. That is, an enterprise can pay with any number of people and amounts needed, and all this at the same time and without unnecessary work. The representative of the company (or the entrepreneur himself) will not have to send the money to his partners in time for the payments, spending time and effort on converting currencies and going to different payment transfer services. All this for one run will make PaymentGate.



There are two ways to make payments. The first is using an XML interface with two-way encryption and information exchange over a secure channel based on SSL. This is an automated way. The second is manual, and it consists in the need to create a special CSV file with the names of the recipients and upload it to the PaySto system in the user's personal account. A prerequisite for the implementation of the fee is the availability of the sum for all payments on the user's account in the PaySto system.



Fees for using PaymentGate differ slightly depending on the method of payment (automated or manual). However, it is important to remember that in any case, the entrepreneur who is the payer pays for the service of sending funds through PaySto itself.

PaymentGate can be used by online stores, social networks and other Web 2.0 projects, gaming systems and portals, remote financial accounting services and various services that work with financial management on the Internet (such as payment centers or exchange offices). In addition, any entrepreneur who works with remote employees would appreciate being able to pay all his contractors in one fell swoop, regardless of the methods they have chosen to receive funds to pay for their work.

Access to PaymentGate is available to the entrepreneur around the clock, and the Cabinet stores the entire history of payments made. The system guarantees control over the status of money transfers in real time.
