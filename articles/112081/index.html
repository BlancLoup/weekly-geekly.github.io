miniOS


As I promised, the next review of innovations will be when miniOS turns out to be successfully launched and finalized. With the tremendous efforts of our team, help from the Chinese, hours of tests and more than 500 messages, we are pleased to present to the owners of Vogue player a full firmware, exactly the kind that is put on regular players. At the moment, the firmware can play mp3 files, play videos, and some files that are buggy on standard firmware are now playing successfully. For example, the standard 175MB rip of the new series of the favorite series will no longer be buggy. Checked for Family Guy :) Also, the translation of the firmware menu was improved, unnecessary functions were removed, management was fixed, now you can even play the built-in Tetris! tronix286 meanwhile, managed to even write a couple of native demos for minios, and embed them in return for not working FM radio.

Instead of the TV module, the Flash playback module was turned on, it has no control, so it will not be possible to play the games. There is a problem with the E-book module - it is made very casually by the Chinese, and initially did not even know about the line breaks, if there are volunteers - it needs to be rewritten, there are a lot of problems with it, and it is impossible to use it normally.
