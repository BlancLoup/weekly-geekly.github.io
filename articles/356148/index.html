Sites BlackBerry, Imo and Line messengers are in the registry of prohibited resources



The sites of three instant messengers, BlackBerry, Imo and Line, were listed by Roskomnadzor in the registry of prohibited resources. Now officials are trying to register all popular messaging services as organizers of information dissemination. The management of some messengers refuses to register and provide user data, after which Roskomnadzor blocks their sites.

As for the programs mentioned above, Imo.im got into the registry on April 19, and the rest of the services from April 28-29. In addition, IP addresses were entered into the same registry. Earlier, Roskomnadzor tried to block the Zello online radio service and its technical resources .

Now these services, despite blocking, work in Russia without any problems. Probably the reason is that the registry does not have as many IP addresses as, for example, in the case of the Zello Internet radio. Most likely, officials are trying to make it clear to the owners of the companies that developed these services, that if they continue to try to counteract the laws, even more stringent measures can be taken, after which the messengers simply cannot work in Russia.
