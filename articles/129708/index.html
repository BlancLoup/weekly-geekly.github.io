Leadership Philosophy



For over 100 years, various people, organizations, private companies have turned to IBM for help. All of them were looking for, in fact, one thing - the perfect combination and application of existing capabilities, developments and skills. In attempts to imagine what this world could be and build it, many people have spent more than one year, not one million.

This process, let's call it, the optimization of reality, can not be imagined without one more, parallel - mental. People think constantly, therefore, for any company that is trying to achieve success, it is very important to understand what employees and customers think, how they think and, most importantly, what conclusions they draw from their own reasoning.

When we talk about any organization headed by a person, we understand that his thoughts and actions are of fundamental importance at all levels of government. Good managers, charismatic and responsible, we used to call "leaders" - these are people who lead, more precisely, their thoughts, followers. What you think is also worth talking about. Not only to convey your own thoughts to others, but also to hear the opinions of other people, which may affect your model of world perception. It is for this reason that IBM holds meetings of various representatives of business, science, and government organizations in order to discuss problems and issues related to leadership. After all, the person who stands at the head must not only effectively cope with current processes, but also look to the future himself in order not to remain in the past.

In fact, it is possible to summarize the main questions that ask people who are responsible: How will a product or company behave tomorrow in a changed world?
