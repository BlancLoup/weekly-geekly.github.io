Nostalgia. Norton. 91 year

My first memories of computers are inextricably linked with Norton Commander. My mom and dad still worked on it, and probably even my grandmother ... and when computers came to school, they all had Norton Commander ...

And recently I had to dig in the history and in the old warehouses in order to find out how it all began. Peter Norton's biography will not be given here, everyone already knows him. I'm more interested in the history of the arrival of Symantec programs in Russia (or rather, in the USSR). It happened more than 20 years ago, in 1991.
