Webinar "SAP Applications on Fujitsu Infrastructure: Lifecycle Management and Development Strategy"

March 26, 2014, we invite you to participate in the joint webinar of Fujitsu and SAP: "SAP applications on the Fujitsu infrastructure: life cycle management and development strategy."

Fujitsu has concentrated all its years of experience in developing infrastructure for SAP in a number of specialized developments. This is also a FlexFrame solution, which has many years of positive experience in industrial operation worldwide and has proved its economic efficiency. This is also a relatively new infrastructure development for carrying out In-Memory calculations, intended for the deployment of SAP HANA databases.

As part of the webinar, Fujitsu for SAP product line updates will be presented, and typical scenarios for their use in particular tasks will be proposed.

Speakers from Fujitsu will share current company news and announce the direction of further development. The event may be of interest to professionals and managers who are responsible for IT efficiency and for the reliable and uninterrupted operation of SAP systems.

A SAP representative will talk about SAP HANA's innovative In-Memory platform and its business scenarios for building productive information systems.

Date: March 26, 2014
Time: from 13:00 to 14:00 (Moscow time).



Julia Seregina, Head of Database & Technology SAP Expertise Center,
Nikolai Grishin, Fujitsu, leading business application consultant,
Sergey Maslenkov, Fujitsu Solutions Promotion Manager.
