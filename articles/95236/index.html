Your WebMoney under the protection of E-NUM

Hi, Habr!

For a long time we did not write anything, and did not notify you about events and news that occur in our company.
In the meantime, our developers come up with more and more new ways to deal with fraudsters (from which both you and we are tired), malicious programs, and also write programs to keep your money in complete safety.
E-NUM authorization service is one of such solutions. When using it, users' wallets become almost inaccessible to fraudsters, thanks to a special system of confirmation according to the question-answer scheme. In addition, a prerequisite for using the service is the presence of a mobile phone or reader, through which the user confirms that he is he, and not a robot or a hacker.

In this post, we would like to introduce you to several new features, which everyone has long dreamed of, as well as to thank all users of the Habr who provided food for the mind, advised and waited ...

So, new WebMoney Keeper Classic has been released, which is equipped with all the additional ways to confirm transactions through E-NUM:

What is it and how does it work?
Now it is possible to confirm transactions through E-NUM in various ways.
With the help of the authorization service, you can now not only enter applications (WebMoney Keeper Classic, Light, Mini) and the System sites, but also confirm any transactions involving the transfer of funds, including payment for goods and services.

In more detail, all methods of confirming transactions can be viewed here , I just want to say about one interesting feature - confirmation via E-NUM in question-answer mode with transaction parameters can be displayed as a QR code. And this means that, using QR code reading software, the data in the E-NUM client can be entered using a mobile phone camera.

Any user of E-NUM has the opportunity to enable SMS authorization to confirm the transaction. This option can be turned on and off in your account on your own. For new users of WebMoney Keeper Classic, when you first start the application, an account in the authorization service is created automatically via SMS. Upon confirmation of payment and transfer of funds a fee is charged - 5 cents in any currency equivalent.
Recall that the entrance to the keeper through E-NUM using SMS is a service that has been operating for a long time and for free, but at the same time has a quantitative limitation: thus, you can enter the WebMoney Keeper Classic application no more than three times a day.

How to avoid additional costs and remove restrictions on the number of entries via SMS per day?

One move - and the operation is confirmed.
