ADOBE European Tour in Minsk. Open Lecture on Flex 4, Flex Builder 4, Flash Catalyst and ColdFusion June 26

June 26, Friday, in Minsk, in the 4th building of BSUIR (Gikalo St., 9), in 104 auditoriums, an open Adobe lecture will take place as part of the European tour, which will be held in 33 countries and 119 cities around the world.

Speaker: Piotr Walczyszyn, Piotr Valczycin, an evangelist from the Polish office of Adobe Systems.

During his speech, Peter will review the new features of products such as Flex 4, Flex Builder 4, Flash Catalyst and ColdFusion. Visitors will learn how to create complex RIA applications, with a division of labor between designers and programmers of the server and client sides.

Participation in the event is free, but by prior registration.
