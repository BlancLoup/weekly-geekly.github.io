Xperia themes

One of the main advantages of the Android operating system can be called the rich possibilities of customization, both in the functional part and in appearance, but not all manufacturers provide users with enough room for modifications, at least using standard tools. Sony strongly supports freedom of expression and launches a new project Xperia Themes.



The owners of Xperia smartphones with Android 4.3 Jelly Bean can now do without third-party shells, and those who are saddened by changing the background of messages in the next update can rejoice! Xperia Themes provides the user with a huge selection of a wide variety of interface design options using 270 graphic elements - buttons, icons, scroll bars, backgrounds, etc.



New themes are available for download in the Google Play and Sony Select stores, where one of the most popular themes is Triflat , which appeared some time ago.
