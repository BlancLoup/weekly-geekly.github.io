Chaos Constructions 2013



Today, August 10, began a two-day "Festival of computer creativity" (demopati) Chaos Constructions 2013 .

You are waiting for excellent sound and light, demoscentersky (and not only) contests, hack quest, exhibition, game zone, retro computer and modern music, thematic seminars, as well as communication with old and new friends and great mood.

This year's contests:

  1. 32k EXE Music - Music as an executable file (32kb limit)
  2. Multichannel Traditional Music - Traditional multichannel music in tracker formats
  3. Oldschool Tiny Music - Music in tracker size (limit 64kb)
  4. Tiny MP3 Music - Music Fragments (30 second limit)
  5. ASCII / ANSI Graphics - ASCII / ANSI Graphics
  6. HandDrawn Graphics - Hand-drawn graphics (on the computer)
  7. 4k Procedural Graphics - A program that generates a static image (4kb limit)
  8. Rendered Graphics - Rendered Graphics (3DS, Maya, Lightwave, etc.)
  9. Combined Photo - Photos obtained using a digital or film (!) Camera
  10. Animation / Video - Short Film and Animation
  11. ZX Spectrum Beeper Music - Beeper music on ZX Spectrum
  12. ZX AY-Music - AY music on the ZX Spectrum
  13. ZX Graphics - Drawing graphics on the ZX Spectrum (including Gigascreen)
  14. ZX Spectrum 4k Intro - Intro on ZX Spectrum compatible computers (4kb limit)
  15. ZX Spectrum 640k Demo - Demo on ZX Spectrum compatible computers (640kb limit)
  16. ZX Spectrum Game - Game on ZX Spectrum compatible computers (640kb limit)
  17. Tiny Intro - Intro for PC, Amiga with accelerators and comparable systems (256b limit)
  18. 4k Intro - Intro for PC, Amiga with accelerators and comparable systems (4kb limit)
  19. 64k Intro - Intro for PC, Amiga with accelerators and comparable systems (64kb limit)
  20. Mobile Demo - Demo for mobile (phones, PDAs, etc., except laptops) devices
  21. HTML5 Demo - Demo for modern browsers
  22. Wild Demo - demo on non-standard equipment
  23. Demo - Demo for PC, Amiga with accelerators and comparable systems
