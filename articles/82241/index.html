Results of the press conference Apple. Meet the iPad



Well, the long-awaited for many day has come - January 27, 2009. Someone was waiting for a new firmware for this day for the iPhone, someone for a new version of the iLife package, someone for another device from Apple, a revolution, a new re-invention of the next device, as it was with the iPod, iPhone ... Maybe someone will say that there is nothing outstanding in these devices, but without any doubt they gave impetus to the industry as a whole. What will surprise us apple company at this time? For several years, everyone has been waiting for the tablet from Apple. Rumors about this go before each event with the participation of this company. This year they have reached the maximum heat.

And then, finally, it happened.
