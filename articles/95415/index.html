Computex 2010: ten tablets based on Windows 7

At the world exhibition Computex 2010, six more tablets based on Windows 7 were presented - summarizes Engadget.



Previously, they also reported on the tablet family Eee Pad on Windows 7 and Windows CE 7 . Tablet from LG UX10 . In addition, a promising ExoPC tablet was previously shown.

In total, at least 10 Windows-based tablets were presented at Computex. Under the cut description of the new six devices.



Viliv X10


Tablet based on Intel Atom Z520 / Z530, 10 inches, 1GB RAM, 32 / 64GB SSD, 1.3-megapixel webcam, USB, MicroSD

Compal NEZ00


Atom N450-based tablet, 10 inches, 1GB / 2GB RAM

CZC P10T


Atom N450, 10-inch tablet, 1GB RAM, 2 USB, 2-megapixel webcam, miniVGA

CZC P10T


Tablet based on Intel Atom N455, 1GB RAM

Fic tycoon


Atom N455 based tablet, available in Broadcom Crystal HD accelerator, 2700mAH battery

Malata PC-A10001


Tablet based on Atom N450, HDD 160GB / 250GB / 320GB, 1.3-megapixel webcam
