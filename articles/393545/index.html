[Video] Visualization of Kung Fu movements



German multimedia designer Tobias Grimmler visually recorded the kung-fu kinetics. Video created by order of the International Association of Chinese martial arts.

Grimmler materializes the physics of the human body through a numerical analysis of Kung Fu exercise movements. Each movement of the body of the soldier is tracked, according to information from the author of the video, with the help of the C4D software package .

Comprehensive digital scanning collects data on the movements of a fighter in terms of speed, time and position in space, formulating visualization parameters.
