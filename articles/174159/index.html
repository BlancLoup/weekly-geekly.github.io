Canadian sells his house for Bitcoins



The fact that someone sells their home is not surprising. The fact that the house is sold through the publication of ads on the resource for the sale / purchase of real estate, is also not unusual. Now, probably, more real estate is being sold and bought this way than by traditional methods. But the currency for which the house is sold is already unusual - at least for transactions of this kind. The fact is that a Canadian sells a house for Bitcoin, a cryptocurrency about which Habr√© was written more than once or twice.

One would think that this is an experiment, that the house is worth a little. But no - the price of this property is 405 thousand Canadian dollars (it is clear that the house is sold for a Bitcoin equivalent. In rubles it is 12 182 996). By the way, at the current rate the house can be purchased for 6700 bitcoins. So this is quite a big deal in the world of cryptocurrency, now there are still rarely pass payments of this size.

Why Bitcoin? There is a logical explanation that the seller himself gives. He is going to implement a couple of projects (presumably, on the Web), and therefore he needs the cryptocurrency. In addition, according to the seller, the transaction will avoid costs in the form of interest on the transfer of a large amount from one account to another (and if the transaction is with the face of another country, the percentage will be even higher). If you use Bitcoin, you can save a very significant amount.

It may well be that this is only the first such deal, and in the not too distant future, someone from the representatives of the community will buy or sell real estate for Bitcoin.
