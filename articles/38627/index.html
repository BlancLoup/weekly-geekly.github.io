Skype closed skykkasty

From now on Skype skykasting service is closed. On the tab "On Air" you can now read the following statement:

Skypecasts is not available

On Monday, September 1, 2008, we decided to close the Skypecasts conference calls in the near future.

Skypecasts was one of the many products we developed to make negotiations worldwide possible. From what we learned as a result of monitoring the use of this product and the feedback received, it follows that Skypecasts did not quite meet our high standards and requirements that we make for communication and convenience for all Skype users.

We apologize for the inconvenience to those who regularly used hosting and participated in Skypecasts. We hope to be able to release a new enhanced product Skypecasts in the near future.

We still believe in the concept of Skypecasts - communication in interest groups. To do this, we will look for ways to re-present this functionality so that they meet the high standards that we and all Skype users have.

Still in force
