Black White

Inspired by the article about the new project to combat corruption in Kazakhstani universities.

I want to talk about the Black-and-White lists, to recall my experience fifteen years ago and today, to listen to community opinion on this matter.

Who cares - please go.

My first experience on such lists has been for 15 years already - for a year and a half since March 1996, the Black & White log was conducted on the basis of materials from the Fido conference pvt.exch.black.log . Archive of the last log remained in the annals of history . At that time, it was a promising initiative - an attempt to use the opinion of the community for real purchases. Now, from a distance, you can draw some conclusions.
  1. The opinion of each person - informally and emotionally. Echo-conference was just an outlet where you can talk. As a result, this log reflected the emotional attitude of a living person to the company (in fact, to a soulless machine)
  2. The main members of the community are students, there was more radicalism in their opinion than justifications.
  3. To put the company a reasonable score of plus or minus, you need the opinion of at least a few people. Firms with such reviews were units.
  4. There were no removal procedures from the list - only positive or negative reviews.
  5. The white list turned out much less than Black. And for planning purchases, it is more interesting to have positive impressions - of which you can savor negative ones.
It is quite logical that I was disappointed in the result when, after a year of work, I tried on the basis of accumulated statistics to give recommendations at the place of purchase. The quality of work of firms on these notes was actually determined by the quality of work of specific people, and the smaller the company, the more personalized the service - which was what the community members liked. Any other indicators were simply ignored.

Then they tried to establish contacts with the consumer society for the exchange of information - this did not interest them either, for the same reasons. Now this is history.

Today I actively use another list - inadequate buyers of online stores from livejournal.com. There are lists of buyers with delivery by mail cash on delivery, which confirmed and did not buy the order. Quite a real return - before there were up to 25% of such orders, now - no more than 5%. Although those on the list are offended ...
