IQLab challenge: prove that you are the best

Today IQLab opens the registration for one of the biggest PHP challenges in Ukraine . This project is designed for professionals of different levels and different backgrounds. The IQLab team will be glad to absolutely all project participants. This is your opportunity to show your knowledge and prove that you are the best.



Project format:
From 4th to 15th of August on the IQLab website registration of participants of the Challenge is held. Also, the IQLab team will play the IQLab kit (branded T-shirt, cup, notebook and pen) every day among social users. networks (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) that use the hashtag #IQLab. Tell us about your participation in the project, and get the opportunity to win a gift. And from August 16 to 17 you will have 24 hours to solve an interesting problem. The site will include authorization, and you will be able to familiarize yourself with the task and upload your solution.

Results:
On August 26, the winners of the Challenge will be announced, and after the results are announced, the finalists will be awarded. You can learn about judges and gifts directly on the project website .

The Astound Commerce team wishes you good luck and we hope to see you among the winners!
