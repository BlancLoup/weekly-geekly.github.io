May 23 at 10.00 live broadcast launch RAD Studio XE4

Dear developers!

For those who can not attend the launch of RAD Studio XE4 in Moscow, Minsk or Kiev, we offer to join the live broadcast, which will begin on May 23 at 10 am.

You can watch the broadcast directly from the event website softwarepeople.ru/delphixe4 .

This year, we specifically invited representatives of the development center as speakers, which will allow you to get information about new opportunities first-hand, and Dmitry Arefyev, the creator of AnyDAC, re-branded to FireDAC, after the Embarcadero acquisition this year will speak at the launch.

As you probably already guessed, cross-platform development will become the main theme of launching XE4 and, to the already familiar Windows, we have added MAC OS and iOS - the most monetized platform. We will also discuss the development plans of RAD Studio.

In addition, you will see many demonstrations for creating applications for iOS and MAC OS.

Do not miss!
