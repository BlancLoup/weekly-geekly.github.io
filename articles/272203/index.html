Not everything is so cool


In addition to the advantages of using IaaS, there are certain difficulties. Here, companies are faced with the need to revise a variety of business processes that have lost relevance since the transition to the cloud IT infrastructure.

We already talked about this earlier when we looked at how the transition to the cloud changes the role of the internal IT service . The Weather Channel came up with a similar situation, and decided to go on a hybrid campaign.

As a result, more than 400 IT professionals had to learn new technologies, some of them chose to quit right away. The remaining ones also dealt with hardware, which the company no longer needed, and applications that were based on its own IT infrastructure.
