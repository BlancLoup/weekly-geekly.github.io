EeePC and DVD


;)

The new ASUS EeePC 1004DN is a neat, simple-styled netbook, in combination - the first car in the EeePC evolutionary series, equipped with a built-in DVD drive.



The drive can not only read but also burn discs. In addition to reading and writing DVD, the netbook has a very convenient almost full-size keyboard - its dimensions are only 8% smaller than a standard keyboard (almost all new ASUS notebooks and netbooks are equipped with similar ones), for which a special thank you to the company's designers. Inside there is an Intel Atom processor with a clock speed of 1.66 GHz, which can not only add speed to a netbook, but also manages to reduce its appetites, reducing power consumption.

Also, the computer uses a new graphics chipset Intel GN40 , perfectly coping with the playback of high-definition 720p video. A clearer 1080p format will not work, but it is not necessary.
