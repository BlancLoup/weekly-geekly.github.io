Imagine Cup: online meeting in the Innovations category



Imagine Cup is the world's largest annual technology competition, sponsored by Microsoft. It is a great honor for us and a very responsible task to host the international final of the Imagine Cup in Russia. In addition, this year will be special for the competition itself.

This year, for the first time, the focus of the competition has shifted from projects to solve social problems (medicine, ecology, etc.) and has reached a new level. We no longer limit you to the so-called Millenium Goals . Starting this year, you should demonstrate your product as efficiently as possible, prove its value and present a working business model.

We organized a series of webinars to help you deal with all the subtleties of each nomination. The first online meeting will be held November 22 at 17:00 (MSC) and will be devoted to the category " Innovation ", probably the most creative in the entire competition.


Time : today (November 22) at 17:00 (Moscow time)
Connection : to connect to the webinar, you must install LyncAttendee in advance and follow the link meet.lync.com/rusdpe/webinar/9DTJBP9R at the scheduled time.
