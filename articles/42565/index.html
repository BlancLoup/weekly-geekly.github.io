Teachmate.org


Good day. Here is my startup: teachmate.org
I made this service to help people when they have problems like this:
  • I need a teacher.
  • I want to teach others what I can do.
  • I want to exchange experience with other people of my own or close to my profession.

The essence

Teaching is not always obliged to contain economic relations. Why not try teaching each other something in exchange? These may be completely different things (for example, I can teach a person to program, and he can play the piano for me), and they can be very close (for example, I can teach a person to program in Ruby, and he can teach me in c ++). The second case is a very real application of this idea and I hope that people will take advantage of it in order to exchange invaluable experiences with each other.

Details about the project

Now only the English version of the service is made to reach the maximum audience. Unicode is supported, so all fields can be filled in Russian.

Thanks for attention. Tell friends, acquaintances and pets if you like the project.
