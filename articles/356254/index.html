Kazakhstan banned commenting on websites without authorization by EDS or phone number

Suddenly! Before the New Year in the Republic of Kazakhstan (not to be confused with the subject of the Russian Federation, the Republic of Tatarstan), the law was passed on December 28, 2017, which introduced changes to the existing ones. Now you can leave comments only on the identification by EDS or via SMS.

Among other uninteresting, there were very interesting details, for example:

"The provision by the owner or owner of a publicly available electronic information resource for the service of placing information by the user is carried out on the basis of an agreement concluded in writing (including electronic), with identification on the portal of the" electronic government "or by using the subscriber number registered on the public information electronic resource user‚Äôs communication with sending a short text message containing a one-time password to send agreement.
