How to keep bookmarks in secret?

The explosive development of social bookmarking services such as Delicious (formerly del.icio.us), ma.gnolia , etc. revealed a very large number of users who are willing to share their bookmarks. These services have gathered whole communities. However, today we are not talking about them, but about those who need a convenient and safe tool to access their bookmarks. There are links that need to be preserved and not advertised. These can be links to pages with financial data or links to the administrative parts of the site or even porn links;). It is necessary to hide these links from everyone, but not to depend on a specific computer. The following is a review of two methods, for users of social bookmarking and for adherents of the old school of storing bookmarks in the browser.

Social bookmarks.
Social bookmarking services are a very convenient tool for storing your bookmarks and a way to share with others. Just because they make it easy to share bookmarks does not mean that you need to do it. In theory, you could keep all your bookmarks closed. In this way, bookmarks can be kept confidential in all social bookmarking services. To do this, when you save a bookmark, do not forget to bookmark a bookmark as closed to others.


In the del.icio.us plugin for Firefox, do not share should be noted.

Old school =)
