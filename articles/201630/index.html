The world's first Bitcoin ATM processed transactions for C $ 100,000 in a week

After the story with Silk Road, even lazy heard about Bitcoins. But this does not mean that Bitcoins can only be used for illegal purposes.

Recently, in the coffee shop in downtown Vancouver, the world's first ATM exchanger for bitcoins was installed, through which transactions for more than one hundred thousand Canadian dollars were made in a very short period of time. While the ATM accepts only cash, but soon the developers are planning to add accepting payments from debit cards.



During the week after installing the ATM, the price per Bitcoin increased by $ 126.
