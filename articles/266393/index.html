HighLoad ++ is a highly loaded dish.



Perhaps the main difference between the HighLoad ++ high-load system developers conference and many others is the absence of hidden targets. There is not a single person or organization behind us that would impose the rules of the game or hunt at an event, such as:
  • Large company
  • Tolstosuma sponsor
  • State structures

For many years, HighLoad ++ remains an event that some developers organize for other developers .

Nine years ago, we adopted several strict rules for ourselves, which we are trying to strictly follow. We will not list them all - for this time will come, let's call only the main ones.

The main thing is that the conference budget is drawn up in such a way that there is always the opportunity to hold it even in the absence of partners. It would seem - what does it give?

As a result - we have no sponsorship reports. Any speech is selected by the Program Committee on a par with all reports. This practice shocks the marketing departments of many companies trying to introduce their own speakers: ‚ÄúHow did we pay, now also prepare a report?‚Äù

Work with the speaker


We always work directly with the speaker and only with him - any letters from other interested parties (including HR and PR-directors) are sent to ‚ÄúProcessed‚Äù. Why?

Because no intermediaries advertising their own employers will be able to submit a good report. This can only be done by an author deeply versed in the subject.

It is useless to ask us - "On what topic would you like to hear the report?". The answer is obvious - to the one in which you understand better, to which you will be interested in reporting.

A live person will be in front of a large audience during a performance. If he is an expert and reads a report for other specialists - then why is the company here, no matter how famous it is? We honestly believe that if you are a good specialist, it is not so important where you live and work, because if your performance is bad, nothing will help you.

Program committee


The program committee, like the rules, has not changed for several years. This is a team of serious professionals and recognized practitioners, each of whom has an excellent command of his own subject. Together they gather a couple of times a year to select a few dozen of the best reports from hundreds of applications.

Last year


From the point of view of the preparation of the conference program, using the example of 2014, it can be reported that we selected 86 best speeches from more than two hundred incoming applications. Reception of reports, of course, begins many months before each conference.

The expert zone, which debuted on HighLoad ++ last year, was the main highlight of the conference. This can be seen without any problems at major Western events, but we are glad that in Russia it was HighLoad ++ that first appeared the opportunity to communicate directly with knowledge carriers.

As our experts have characterized the two-day experiment: ‚ÄúWe could not pee to move.‚Äù

Because every conference visitor had the opportunity to communicate directly with technology developers and consultants on these technologies. Accordingly, it was possible to ask any questions - often it is, experts often come to the conference with a specific task: how to do something, optimize, scale.

Last year, you could explore everything on issues related to MySQL and PostgreSQL, Tarantool, Sphinx, NGINX, Erlang, video, and high-load architectures.



Why democracy does not work


Democracy is something that definitely does not work when organizing IT conferences.

After all, if you give the opportunity to choose listeners of speakers (and we tried this way, what a separate publication will be devoted to), then people choose reports on the number of familiar ‚Äúkeywords‚Äù in theses. As a result, the program does not consist of interesting speakers, but of those speakers who have managed better than others to write abstracts of their own speech.

The program committee approaches the choice of reports in a completely different way.

Each of the PC participants is a professional with experience, managing other people and, most importantly, people who read the resume. Participants of the Program Committee will not be spent with beautifully written text, since the essence for them is the main thing.

Most members of the Program Committee attend not only Western conferences, but almost all Russian events. And therefore, even if the speaker is presented as a ‚Äúnewcomer‚Äù - we know about him the way he performs and behaves on the stage.

After collecting all applications and closing them, the Program Committee meets in person and makes a collective decision on each of the speakers - this is the only way to form a balanced and competent program from the best applications, which will not have a hundred NoSQL reports with a sufficient number of MySQL.
