Room maps appear on Google Maps



A year ago, plans for some public premises appeared in the Google Maps for Android application, but now they can be seen on the web, Mashable writes . So far, individual locations are available in Belgium, Great Britain, Denmark, Canada, USA, France, Switzerland, Sweden and Japan.

Among the available premises - more than 10 thousand shops, shopping centers, airports, train stations, libraries and museums. Plans can be useful in order not to get lost in the shopping center or to make a route from a taxi to the gate at the airport, so as not to run through the entire airport.
