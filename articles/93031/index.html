Do not forget: the video of the war

It is no secret that almost every year new interesting projects on the history of the Great Patriotic War appear in the network. On the eve of Victory Day, I want to present you another interesting resource dedicated to preserving the memory of the history and events of the Great Patriotic War. The project " Do not forget " tells about the history, key events and phenomena of war in the format of documentary videos.



Everything new is, as we know, a long-forgotten old: in the 40s, when television was already invented, but not yet widely used, people learned news about the war from newsreels filmed by front-line operators and broadcast in cinemas before, and sometimes instead of film shows (well, almost like advertising now).

Now you can familiarize yourself with the video chronicles of the key events of the Great Patriotic War and eyewitness recollections in a more familiar way - without looking up from the computer.

The information is presented clearly: plots dedicated to key events and phenomena of the Great Patriotic War can be watched over the years - or you can use a common tape, in a row. Also, all videos are uploaded to the project channel on Youtube , and the channel will be updated - including due to videos uploaded by the users themselves.

By the way, you can add videos in the Video Contest section: videos skipped by moderators participate in the overall rating and the authors of the best will receive prizes , including Acer Aspire One, HTC Hero, ABBYY Lingvo x3 and Windows 7 Pro.
