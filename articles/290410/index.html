Basic principles of the freelancing industry

Freelance is a promising direction in the field of employment and small business. In many Western countries, it has already become widespread, while in Russia it continues to grow rapidly. In this article I will discuss the basic principles of the freelancing industry.



First we introduce the basic concepts:

1) Freelance Exchange


Exchanges are sites that organize the interaction of freelancers and customers. They are essential elements in the work of the freelancing industry. Without them, there would be no organized interaction between freelancers and customers.

2) Freelancers


Freelancers - people who are looking for jobs and projects for the purpose of employment and profit. They are the target audience (on a par with customers) for freelancing exchanges.

3) Customers


Customers are people whose goal is to implement the project using all the advantages of working with freelancers.

4) Information sites


Sites providing information to both freelancers and customers. The goal is to attract interest in the field of freelancing.

Scheme of work in the sphere of freelancing:



In the established market, freelancers are always more than customers.

Freelance Exchange Operation Scheme:



It is worth noting that the exchange has a percentage of transactions conducted on it - this is one of the parts of the income. Also, the income can be obtained from the granting of various privileges to freelancers and customers.

Now we define the main tasks for market participants:

The most optimal task for a freelancer is to gain access to the implementation of the most profitable and promising projects. In a real situation, it comes down to gaining access to the implementation of fairly profitable projects. For many low-skilled and inexperienced, it comes down to the ability to carry out any paid project that they are supposed to cope with.

For the customer, the main task is to implement the project in the most qualitative and profitable way. In different situations, different customers may emphasize profitability or quality.

The task of the exchange is to get the largest number of clients (customers and freelancers) and provide them with the service at the best price.
