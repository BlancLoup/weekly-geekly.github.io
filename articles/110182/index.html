Rambler-News for iPhone

Following the trend of the iPhone in the domestic media, the Rambler has also created its own client for the iPhone. The application allows you to get quick access to Rambler-News materials on the slowest Internet connection: the application uses a special API, so it does not download tons of unnecessary information.

The Rambler News application allows you to create a filter by news headings and fully customize your news flow. In addition, useful features such as increasing / decreasing the font, adding news to favorites, searching for news feeds and dynamic loading of news (only headers are initially downloaded) are available.

Added to Favorites articles are available offline, which is extremely convenient for trips to the subway and airplane flights.



Any interesting news, being in touch, you can instantly share with friends through posting on Facebook, Twitter or sending an e-mail.



The application works quite quickly and in terms of convenience is not inferior to specialized applications for reading electronic books. That's just offline mode is not so functional - there is something to work on.
