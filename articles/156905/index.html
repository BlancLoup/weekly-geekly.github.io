New Brief: Samsung and Google launch Nexus 10 for sale

October 29 was announced the release of a new tablet, created jointly by international technology giants Google and Samsung - Nexus 10. It is a powerful device that combines the best Samsung hardware stuffing, the latest version of Android 4.2 Jelly Bean and all the best from mobile software that may suggest google. The tablet has the highest resolution of the display (2560x1600, 300 PPI - more than 4 million pixels) among all existing similar mobile devices, a 9000 mAh battery (9 hours of non-stop video viewing or 500 hours in standby mode), new multi-user modes, support for HD content and the best apps from Google. Definitely, the Nexus 10 will have something to offer gadget lovers.



Nexus 10 carries powerful components inside a thin (8.9 mm) and light (603 g) case, which makes the device an ideal companion on a sofa or subway. The tablet is equipped with a 1.7 GHz dual-core Exynos 5250 processor on the latest Cortex-A15 architecture, 2 GB of RAM and a powerful graphics accelerator ARM Mali-T604 MP4. Outside, the device will delight 10 "LCD display (Active, Color, Backlit), 5 megapixel main camera and 1.9 megapixel front camera.



Nexus 10 was created for convenience and pleasure. Simply turn on the tablet and click on your photo to enter your own home screen, applications, email, photos and file storage services. New multiplayer modes allow each user to have their own space and save their personal data, files in applications and records in games on the same tablet.

Nexus 10 will be available on November 13 in two versions: 16 and 32 GB. About when the appetizing tablet comes to Russia and how much it will cost, official sources still hold back.
