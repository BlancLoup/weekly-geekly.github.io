Video: 4-year-old girl was entrusted with a Volvo truck on the control panel

The car manufacturer Volvo removed an advertisement in which he entrusted the management of a four-year-old girl to a truck at a safe distance from the site. For two days, the video scored 1.2 million views. While driving a truck, Sofia turned the truck over and destroyed the building. No harm done.



Volvo uses the system to control trucks in difficult conditions: it is an all-wheel drive with automatic control of the front axle connection for a Volvo FMX truck. The front axle is used only when necessary, which reduces fuel consumption and improves handling.

In the video, the company tried to show that a child can drive a truck with this system, and that a truck that has moved into a ditch and turned over several times can go further and destroy a one-story building.

In 2013, the company advertised the handling of its vehicles by shooting a video for Youtube, in which Jean-Claude Van Damme sat on a string between two trucks that drove backwards. Video for two years gained more than 81 million views.
