Many social networks offer users such a paid service as "gifts". Usually, these are symbolic attention signs presented in the form of pictures that can be presented to another user by paying for a gift with sms. The typical cost of gifts ranges from a dollar to $ 7 per piece.



We thought: what can we invent a new, unusual, interesting in this area? And dedicated to the topic of gifts recent meeting of the club Brain Exchange . The meeting participants generated new ideas in a brainstorming format, so some of the ideas may seem strange or not suitable - but surely they can be developed into something useful. We share these ideas with everyone. So, what we came up with (in the order of ideas):

  • You can give users other paid services - to pay an additional rating, a paid account, etc.
  • Material gifts are an opportunity to present an item that is sold through the Internet: books, toys, home appliances, etc. You can realize it by entering into partnership agreements with online stores. Moreover, the user receives a virtual picture in the profile (‚Äúthey gave me a book‚Äù) and a real gift.

  • Integration with wishlist- s of users. In this case, each friend of the user can make a small part of the cost of SMS-Coy.
  • Opportunity to give gifts - to give a bored gift to another.
  • Gifts that operate for a limited period of time - for example, flowers that fade ("fade") after 3-5 days.
  • Interactive gifts are animals that need to be fed, flowers that need to be watered so that they do not wilt. As an option - paid interactive, the user must support their livelihoods by sending SMS or a waste of points on the site.
  • A useful widget is the ability to donate additional blocks to a profile - a weather widget, exchange rates, anecdotes or traffic jams.
  • Gift incentives - gave 10 bears, got a flower (or just a rating)
