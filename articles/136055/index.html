Duck Hunt based on Arduino



There are more and more skilled craftsmen creating interesting projects on the basis of various platforms. Accordingly, the number of the most interesting projects is also growing every day. This time the lover of the game Duck Hunt, who created a real analogue of this game, distinguished himself. True, ducks do not fly here, and there is no mocking partner dog either. But there is a moving platform with duck figures, and these ducks can and should be fired from a laser pistol.

Unfortunately, the holes in the ducks do not appear, the guns still have not reached the required power, but the hit is fixed by the system, and the blue light comes on. By the way, ducks are not rubber, but glass, their creator of this project created with his own hands and lungs (he is also engaged in glass-blowing craft, this is a person's hobby).

In general, the project turned out to be quite interesting, so spectacular. Below you can look at the video, which clearly shows the principle of the entire system. It turned out a kind of laser shooting range, a system that allows you to have fun without computer or video games.
