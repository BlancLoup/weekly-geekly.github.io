Tesla Motors presented the new improved autopilot



Tonight, at 3:00 am Moscow time, there was a presentation of Tesla Motors, at which the head of the company Ilon Mask presented a new function of all Tesla cars - a full autopilot.

Tesla full autopilot presentation video

After the presentation, all the cars produced by the company (including the Model 3) will be equipped with the necessary equipment to support the work of the new autopilot.

The system is based on laser and IR sensors, which provide a 360-degree view. Improvements in the system make it possible to analyze the situation around the car at a distance of up to 250 meters, which, according to the manufacturer, is almost twice as large as the previous autopilots on the market.

The on-board computer equipment was also updated and the system based on the neural network was developed, which is responsible for the recognition of objects (cars, pedestrians, obstacles, road signs) on the road.

To activate the new autopilot you will need to confirm the installation of the appropriate software. Refinement of the system will be based on the data collected from all Tesla cars with the function of the new autopilot.

Unfortunately, it is impossible to install a novelty on already sold or assembled cars. As Musk himself stated, the cost of the upgrade works exceeds the cost of the new Tesla car:

Should mention that it is very difficult. Cost delta is more than buying a new car. Wish it weren't.

- Elon Musk (@elonmusk) October 20, 2016
