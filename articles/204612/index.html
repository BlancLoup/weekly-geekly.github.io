Design and design of applications for Windows: look online today at 10:00 (MSK)

December 3 at 10:00 (Moscow time) join the virtual event " Design and design of applications for Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone ". A well-designed user interface is one of the key components of a successful Windows or Windows Phone application. Good design, thoughtful structure of the application and navigation in it, ease of use - all this is extremely important to make your users happy.

During the event, we will discuss both general issues of designing mobile applications, as well as individual subtleties and nuances associated specifically with the Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone platforms.
