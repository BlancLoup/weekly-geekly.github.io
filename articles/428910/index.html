Light phone II - a phone that respects your time.

Technology Company "Light" , Brooklyn, USA - the developers of minimalistic technologies that allow the use of devices for their intended purpose. After the successful release of the first phone, the Original Light Phone , which was supported by more than 10,000 people, the team is at the prototyping stage of its second Light Phone II model. Since the launch of the campaign on April 22, 2018, by November 2018, 615% of the requested amount - $ 2,036,234 - was collected on the Indiegogo crowdfunding platform. The release is scheduled for April 2019, pre-order the phone can be purchased for $ 300, with a stated market value of $ 400.

Light Products


Original Light Phone

Light Phone II


A simple, 4G / LTE phone with a black and white matte display and electronic ink technology.



At the moment of prototyping, its functionality is expanded and allows you to exchange messages, set an alarm, but the device still remains a phone that respects your time. Developers lead an open discussion with users about adding other tools that do not contradict the original concept.

Original Light Phone vs Light Phone II

A noticeable change in the phone in its second version was a matte black and white screen that allows you to clearly see the contents on the street in daylight. This solution is energy efficient, and shades of gray are a healthy limitation to vision.

A larger microphone and speaker, a USB-C, a zoom sensor, and simple up / down buttons were also added. The team is exploring the possibility of using an aluminum case for higher strength characteristics. Water resistance is in the plan, but does not promise to be implemented in this phone model.
