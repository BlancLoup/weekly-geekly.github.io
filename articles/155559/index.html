ZTE V970 - strong middle peasants from China

On September 27, the Chinese company ZTE, known to the Russian consumer primarily through routers, held a presentation on which she presented several models of her smartphones, which she brings to the Russian market. The most interesting of them personally seemed to me the flagship ZTE Grand Era. However, in the process of drawing after the presentation, I won not him, but ZTE V970, which I would like to report on the experience of using in this review.





So, ZTE V970 is a strong middle peasant in the line of smartphones presented by ZTE. It is based on a 2-core NVIDIA Tegra 2 processor, has a 4.3 dim display with a resolution of 960x540. The truth is that the RAM is only 512 MB, which is for Android ICS, on which the device works. Yes, and the built-in flash memory of 4 GB, but there is a slot for microSD, with which flash memory can be expanded by another 32 GB.



However, the full characteristics of the smartphone can be viewed on Yandex.Market , so I will not dwell on them so much, and I will immediately turn to my experience of using this device.

What I liked literally in the first hours of using the device was its ergonomic qualities. The smartphone is beautifully assembled. In the hand, it feels like a solid and self-sufficient object. Due to the back cover of textured rubberized plastic - the device does not slide at all in the hands. There are no even hints of any backlash and creaks. Everything is fitted tightly, like stones in the megalithic masonry of the pyramids.
