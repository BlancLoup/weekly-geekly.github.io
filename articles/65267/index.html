Investors and investments - bad experience

Goodnight% username%
Recently, startups have written a lot about investments in a blog.
I want to share my unsuccessful experience of communication with investors.
Without mentioning names and company names -

let's look at my mistakes

Perhaps it will be useful to someone.

To begin, briefly about what I turned to investors.
E-commerce project.
The software part is 99% ready, a business plan, a small market survey (already outdated), an understandable monetization scheme (not selling advertising), a return to self-sufficiency in a year.


What did I need from investors
I think it would be possible to omit this item.
What do you need? Of course, money (or services).
usability, marketing, offline costs (sal. employee, hardware, etc.)


To whom addressed
4 investment funds 1 business angel and 1 more subject of this market which I would identify as a person who has money


What happened in the end
Fund # 1 responded immediately that they were not interested in this topic (and even briefly described why).
Fund # 2 has proposed such an investment scheme.
they give a loan at 15% per annum with the ability to start the first payments on it in a year
for this they wanted a very large% of the $ 1 project - such conditions seemed unprofitable to me.
Fund # 3 interested in the idea asked for a more detailed description (it was made)
then there was a meeting at which investment options were specified (they came to a mutually convenient scheme)
further, within 2 weeks, constant clarifications and clarifications were requested.
after which the contact person (general producer) disappeared without explanation of any reasons.
Fund # 4 immediately said that they want 51% and will be able to invest only 30% of the required amount - negotiations are underway.
The business angel showed interest, requested additional information and disappeared safely.
the subject with money corresponded for a long time, figuring out the essence of the project, agreed on a meeting that he overslept ...
Yes, I also could not believe it, but on that end of the tube a sleepy voice spoke something not intelligible, after that he probably decided that it was not worth talking at all and was also gone.

I lost time, did not find the money, told a lot of people about my project who shouldn‚Äôt be told. 



  By the word gone, I mean ignoring letters or calls.
 all the people (funds) to whom I turned pretty well-known and major players.
 For my part provided all the required materials.
 I do not understand the fact of how to conduct your business in this way, but I was very surprised when I discovered that the spirit of the dashing entrepreneurship of the 90s was still alive in the web era.
