Any idea can be expressed through five types of comparisons: component-wise, positional, temporal, frequency, and correlation. Thanks to Jean Zelazny for this sacred knowledge - I myself did not realize that this area can at least try to formalize and classify. So, looking at the formulated idea , we select the appropriate type of comparison.

1) exploded comparison . Shows the percentage of each component as a percentage of the whole.

- Two customers brought us half the profit.
- The boys in our faculty this year, only 90%.
