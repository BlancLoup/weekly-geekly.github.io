Yandex.Market - to be or not?

We decided to make an online store with our friends (before that, they only traded offline), to trade professional audio equipment. Comrades - representatives of one Chinese brand (Biema), and one domestic (Digilab). Both companies are more or less known among professional sound engineers. Comrades trade, of course, not only these brands, for the range there are goods and more famous manufacturers, but of course, there are fewer of them.

In general, we made a store, launched it, some orders appeared, but not many. I will give the address in the comments - hosting will not vydezhit if the load increases greatly. We decided to accommodate, for a start, in Yandex.Market. This required a module for our CMS (there was no ready one). Paid the money made. It also took a module to quickly update the price list - also ordered, done. Have applied. The rest of the list is a store of printers and accessories, several medical equipment shops, several shops of knives, cameras, and so on. True, most of them, except for the profiling positions, sell: who are household synthesizers, who are karaoke microphones, who are car amplifiers. Some time passed, it turned out that the fax did not pass, sent again, already in electronic form. At the same time, a search in Yandex.Market for the word Biema yields 1 result, for the word Digilab - 0 results (there are more products of this brand in our assortment than any other, and this can be seen in the YML file).

I ask for help and advice: where to be and what to do? It is clear that Yandex is big and strong, and has the right to set its own rules. On the other hand, such an answer is akin to sentencing. In other systems, we will also be served, but it is not clear what to do in this case ...
