WebMoney got sanctioned in Ukraine

The National Security and Defense Council (NSDC) of Ukraine has compiled a new sanctions list for Russia, which entered into force by presidential decree of May 14 and will be valid for 3 (three) years.

The list was 756 points . In addition to WebMoney and all its subsidiaries and representative offices, such companies were included in it:

  • PayMaster (representative office in Ukraine - PayMaster.UA)
  • Open Joint-Stock Company Concern Air Defense "Almaz-Antey"
  • public joint stock company "Aeroflot - Russian Airlines"
  • Open Joint Stock Company "First Channel. World Wide Web
  • RTR-Planet TV channels, Russia-24 information channel
  • and many others...


Regarding WebMoney, the sanctions include restricting trade operations, blocking company assets, preventing the withdrawal of capital from Ukraine, suspending the fulfillment of economic and financial obligations, restricting or suspending licenses and other permits, and blocking access to the company's websites and subdomains by providers.

By the evening of May 24, a large number of online stores had already turned off the possibility of buying using WebMoney, and some users experienced errors in the service.
