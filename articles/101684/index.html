Hulu VS Zipping: Who will have the last word?

The service is certainly not universal, but it is definitely a step forward for the entire television industry.

Zipping - once this word defined the scrolling in the accelerated mode of video cassettes, then - the scrolling of advertising on the same cassettes, and in the present century - the cyclical switching of a large number of channels on TVs. In the yellow press, this phenomenon has already been baptized as a disease, while you and I, of course, understand that this is a banal non-optimized process of viewing and selecting channels.

So how to be? The answer, like all ingenious, is simple - you need to create a company that will provide some kind of solution to help you navigate the variety of TV channels on the TV.
