"Write about what will be interesting"

Some clustering by interests is characteristic of any human community, when there are more than three people in it. There is no exception and the online community, where almost every user has their favorite thematic areas in which information and communication belong to a particular thematic area. The site Roem.ru serves as a similar platform for communication of experts in the field of Internet technologies and has already become a recognized authoritative news expert resource of the Russian Internet. Its founder and editor-in-chief, Yuri Synodov, shared the secrets of promoting thematic sites at his lecture at the State University of Management.

Thematic, especially news, Internet resource requires constantly updated content that is interesting to the user. Therefore, Mr. Synodov also spoke about the search for topics for news and articles on the site.
