New countries in Search Ads, the possible output of the counter "number of installations for the search query" in the Play Store, etc.

ASO news for October 2017


The 4th of October


Thomas Petit , as usual, was the first to report to ASO Stack that Apple will launch Search Ads in Mexico (Spanish), Canada (French and English) and Switzerland (French, German and Italian). Search Ads was released in these countries on October 17th. The choice of countries is probably due to the fact that Apple plans to take on the Latin American market in Spanish (Spanish is also important in the US), as well as the French-speaking market in Canada. Switzerland will also be an interesting test site for Apple, given the diversity of languages ‚Äã‚Äã- it can be a test site for the German-speaking and Italian-speaking market. It seems that the expansion of Search Ads is a priority for the Apple App Store team, and soon we will see their presence in new markets.

October 5


According to information from 9to5Google , Google tested the display of application positions in the top. Currently the application position is displayed under the install button. It looks like this was a temporary A / B test that is not yet complete. This change will be useful, as it provides information about the ranking of the application to the page visitor. True, it will also mean that the brief description and screenshots of the application will be moved further down.
