New devices with IFA 2018

Last week IFA 2018 was held in Berlin. We and our partners presented their newest devices on Windows 10 on it. Under the cat you will find a story about some of them. Join now!



The next Windows update, Windows 10 October 2018 Update, will receive about 700 million Windows 10 devices that help people make the most of their time.



Erin Chapl, Microsoft Corporate Vice President, speaks at keynote IFA 2018

New PC, new features


We are very pleased to share with you the new features of Windows, which allow people to achieve more and that have come to life thanks to the new modern PC, announced this week.

One of our top priorities is the ability to work together from multiple devices. Earlier this week, we took another step in the development of PC capabilities: we announced the first Always Connected PC with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 850 processor - Lenovo Yoga C630 WOS (Windows on Snapdragon) . Economical, lightweight, made of aluminum, the Yoga C630 WOS is a laptop with LTE and battery life up to 25 hours *. Equipped with an extra stylus, the Lenovo Yoga C630 WOS will help you in any situation.


Lenovo Yoga C630 WOS

Beautiful devices, such as, combine the functionality of Windows 10 and light weight, allowing users to stay connected even on the go.

Lenovo also announced the Lenovo Yoga Book C930 - the result of years of work. The device was created on the Windows 10 platform and expanded with Microsoft Office tools. Press the button on the Yoga Book C930 - and the second E-Ink display will turn on and will work as a keyboard, notepad or e-book. The ultra-thin lightweight device contains technologies such as Windows Ink and Windows Hello, and is also equipped with an optional 4G feature - to maintain your productivity anywhere.


Lenovo Yoga Book C930

Another PC is the Dell Inspiron 13 7000 2-in-1. A narrow frame on three sides of the screen made it possible to free up all the space for an IPS touch screen that is compatible with the Active Pen. The Inspiron 13 7000 has the Modern Standby feature for quick power up, as well as the ability to unlock a laptop with one touch, using the Windows Hello fingerprint sensor.


Dell Inspiron 13 7000 2-in-1

One of the devices that paid special attention this week was the new Surface Go. It combines the convenience of a laptop with the advantages of a 10-inch tablet. Do you like this color?


Surface go

And, of course, we have news for fans of games! The recently updated Acer Predator Triton 900 is now equipped with an 8th generation Generation Intel Core i7 processor: maximum power and speed! In this case, the hinges of the laptop allow you to change the tilt of the screen so that you can choose the most suitable angle for the game. Thanks to the garage door technology, you can use an Xbox controller. A specially designed dual ventilation system cools the device perfectly, and the back-lit RGB-keyboard allows you to experiment with customizing the device.


Acer Predator Triton 900

This is just a small overview of the innovations that our partners brought to Berlin. To learn more about other devices announced this week at the IFA, read articles about Dell , Acer , Lenovo and MSI .
