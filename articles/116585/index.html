New sights appeared in Google Street View

The Google Street View, which allows you to view various panoramic views, has some historical and architectural sights of Italy and France, including UNESCO World Heritage sites in Rome, the center of Florence and French castles.

For example, the Colosseum can now be explored both outside and inside . The same applies to the Imperial Forum ( outside and inside ).


Inside the Colosseum

In a few clicks you can go through centuries of history. Start from the place of origin of Rome - from the Palatine Hill , where, according to legend, the founders of the city, Romulus and Remus, were discovered and rescued by a she-wolf and where the most ancient buildings of the city are located . Follow the Appian Way , a small path that has become one of the most strategically important roads of ancient Rome.
