The first batch was hastily sewn in the kitchen and distributed among its patients. And the very first reviews turned out to be quite impressive: many refused sleeping pills, many returned to the family bed, despite the snoring of one of the spouses, and in 2007, headphones appeared under the AcousticSheep brand, whose logo adorned the stereotypical lamb.



Studies show the results of experiments where, for example, listening to binaural sounds in theta and delta bands reduces anxiety and anxiety and allows you to relax.
