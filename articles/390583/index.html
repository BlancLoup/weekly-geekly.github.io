15 little things from China. Part 2

We have already published a review of goods from the Middle Kingdom, in which were presented quite interesting gizmos. The emphasis is on non-standard, on creative, as well as on affordability, of course. That is - our goal - gadgets that allow us to live simplify our lives.

Today, the next in turn are the devices that will, if not survive the zombie apocalypse, then simplify many operations - exactly. Let's start, perhaps.

1. Handheld 3D Printer for $ 30 *




Thanks to this pen, you have an open space for creativity, because now you can draw not only to the left or to the right horizontally, but upwards through the air. Perfect for family creativity and the creation of various figures and toys, as well as for the creation of plastic parts and designs.

2. Iron Man recommends a pen for $ 22.49 *




Tony Stark would love it. It can be understood, because the cunning Chinese figured out how to avoid the total disappearance of ink on the ground by developing an endless ink-free pen-pen, which writes ... with metal. Metal, Carl!
They promise that it will serve more than 100 years. Of course, in 100 years you will be neither cold nor hot, but the gadget does not become less attractive for this!

3. Run, Alarm Clock, Run! Elusive alarm for $ 10.56 *




The Chinese offer us a runaway alarm clock. The idea, in general, is logical, given the mood of the awakened person from the annoying chuck.
Now it's so easy to turn every morning into a quest, when the main task is to find where this asshole hid!

4. Toothpaste Dispenser for $ 4.89 *




One more thing, essential in every home. That's right - this is a toothpaste dispenser. He will measure, squeeze the brush. It remains only poelozit teeth. But it is already easier. How much power is saved, how many achievements ahead!

5. Stereo for smartphone for $ 3.68




Excellent portable speakers, easily and simply connect to your smartphone turning it into a stereo system. Useful when traveling on kebabs, fishing or picnic.

6. Portable projector for $ 46 *




With this device you can easily watch a slideshow or watch a movie in an improvised home theater, as it connects to almost any device, including your smartphone.

7. Magnifier for smartphone for $ 6.59 *




Now you can enjoy the full size of the reproduced picture from your smartphone while watching a movie. All you need to put the device in front of you on the table and secure your phone behind the lens, then simply lean back in your chair and enjoy the video. Perfectly fit into a set of things for people who often spend long trips on trains.

8. Wi-fi robot spy for $ 113.13 *




It is like a lunar rover, but only controlled by you from a smartphone or tablet. On it, you can spy on friends, thanks to the almost silent move, or just explore the world around us.

Multifunctional camera, it can be used as:

  • Video surveillance at home.
  • Camcorder - usb webcam computer.
  • In the car as an auto-DVR (enough to fix the bracket-sucker on the windshield).
  • Install in a store or office to monitor the work of employees - as a video surveillance system.

For example, an online camera was installed in a barber shop, car service center, shop, kindergarten, so that people could see the quality of work, service, and so on.

10. USB soldering iron for $ 7.48 *




A good option for people who often solder something, for example, for those who are keen on quadcopters or other R / C models. The soldering iron is powered by usb, which is very convenient if only a laptop is at hand.

11. Big fan for a small smartphone for $ 1.80 *




This fan is conveniently fixed on the smartphone, has no extra parts. Now the stuffiness is not such a problem.

12. Portable usb fridge for $ 36.10 *




Perfect fit in the office or car. Thanks to him, you can always cool your favorite drink without leaving your computer, as it is powered by usb. There is also a more compact and cheaper version ( $ 16.60 * )

If you have a weak cooler and the laptop is constantly heated, and carrying a cooling pad is not an option, then this device is for you. All you need is to fix the gadget in the ventilating compartment of your computer and connect the power cord via usb.

14. USB keyboard vacuum cleaner for $ 16 *




A useful thing when you need to quickly and efficiently clean the keyboard from dust and crumbs. An excellent mini vacuum cleaner for those who save their time and love cleanliness in the workplace.
