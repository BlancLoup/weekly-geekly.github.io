Meet MDNA UG: master Silverlight 5 and understand the clouds


May 11 at 19:00 in the office of Microsoft Russia, after a pleasant holiday holiday, the UG meeting will be held, dedicated to the most innovative technologies of our time: Silverlight 5 and the clouds.

schedule
1. 5 reasons to use Silverlight 5 to create business applications (19:00 - 20:00)
2. Break (20:00 - 20:05)
3. What is cloud computing? (20:05 - 20:35)
4. Review of Windows Azure Toolkit for Windows Phone 7 (20:35 - 21:05)
5. Pizza and pleasant communication (21:05 - 21:30)

Report Information

5 reasons to use Silverlight 5 to create business applications, Sergey Pugachev

The report on practical examples discusses the capabilities of Silverlight Fifth and previous versions, which help to quickly create full-featured business (Line-of-Business) applications. Special attention is paid to the new features of Silverlight 5 and the use of WCF RIA Services.

What is cloud computing, Ilya Sparrow

Come find out about what is cloud computing and technology. The report will discuss the main concepts, ideas and models that will clarify and help you use them in practice.

Overview of Windows Azure Toolkit for Windows Phone 7, Stanislav Pavlov

For a long time engaged in mobile and embedded technologies. Restrictions on resources, both of the device itself and its manufacturer, may impose certain restrictions on the services available to the end user of the mobile device. Windows Azure Toolkit for Windows Phone 7 is designed to simplify the use of the capabilities of the Windows Azure cloud technology on the Windows Phone 7 platform.

How to get there?

Microsoft office is located at ul. Krylatskaya, 17, business center "Krylatsky Hills".

From the subway go special route buses. Ford buses (or similar), on buses sign "Krylatsky Hills".

Stop at the metro: Krylatskaya metro station, exit the metro head car, then exit the metro from the door to the left, go to the right. The stop will be 10 meters from the metro exit. Buses run every 10-15 minutes. Since everyone cannot fit in one bus, it is better to arrive at the Krylatskoye metro station at 18: 20-18: 30 (the meeting starts at 19:00).

After you enter the building with the Microsoft sign on the roof, turn left - this will take you to the Microsoft office. Then you will be shown how to get to the meeting venue.
