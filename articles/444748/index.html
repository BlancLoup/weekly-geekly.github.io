- I would single out the fourth quality - this is leadership. He should be able to ignite the team and lead the product in the right direction, not to deflate. He will have a lot of obstacles on his way. This is exactly the leadership skill. But, again, in a sense, it is derived from the first three. I can not imagine a person who is an expert in his field, an excellent communicator and a good boss, but not a leader. It is very strange.
