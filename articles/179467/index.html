Author Hydra, Amap and SuSEFirewall to Speak at PHDays



One of the keynote speakers at the Positive Hack Days III forum starting in May will be the famous information security researcher Mark Heuze, also known as van Hauser.

Mark has been involved in security research since 1993, his track record is an impressive number of vulnerabilities found in well-known products. In addition, he has developed a number of popular security tools, such as Hydra, Amap, THC-IPV6, THC-Scan and SuSEFirewall.

In 1995, van Hauser founded The Hacker's Choice (THC) security research group, among whose achievements - breaking the A5 GSM in just one minute in 2006. Since 1997, Mark has been working as a security consultant in one of the five largest international consulting companies. Since 2007, he has been an independent security consultant.

The topic of the report is kept secret. To personally see the performance of the famous researcher, register and come on May 23 and 24 at Positive Hack Days!
