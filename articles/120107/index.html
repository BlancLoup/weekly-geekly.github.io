The company Envision introduced the gaming console Evo 2 on Android



The Android mobile operating system is becoming more and more popular - it gradually moves from tablets and smartphones to other types of devices. Recently, the company Envision introduced the game console Evo 2, the software platform for which just serves Android OS 2.2. The console itself is a kind of entertainment center, and is hardly a competitor to giants like the Xbox 360 and PS3. You can play Evo 2 only in games designed for Android (hello again, Angry Birds). In principle, under Android there are quite a few emulators of regular game consoles, so it won't be boring.

According to the developers, EVO 2 is the next version of the EVO console, which was introduced a couple of years ago, and worked on Linux. Then the project just died, and no one could appreciate the benefits of an ‚Äúopen‚Äù gaming platform. Now the situation is a bit different - the project is actively developing, and the final cost of the product is already known, as well as the release date. The price of the Evo 2 is $ 250, and the console should be out this fall. By the way, the site is available EVO 2 SDK for developers, for only $ 149.



You can already make a reservation if anyone can not wait to get this device. The kit will include the console itself, an HDMI cable, and a game controller.

The configuration of the device, of course, is not particularly impressive: 512 MB DDR2, a processor operating at 1.2 GHz (by the way, a Samsung processor). The size of the device is 170 * 111 * 30 mm. There is no other information about the configuration, except for the information that the company is going to release something like Microsoft Kinect.

In order to expand the functionality of its device, Envizions is working to create a cloud service where users' files will be stored.
