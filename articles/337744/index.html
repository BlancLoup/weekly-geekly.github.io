Anonymize photos using Vision. Timofey Khomutnikov, Avito


Avito has a well-developed tradition of internal hakatons, which we already wrote about in our blog. On one of them, the mobile developers team decided to have a known pain of users - when they post ads, they try to hide the faces and numbers of the machines in all possible ways. Someone puts on packages, someone covers with their hands, the most advanced do it in a graphic editor, but the essence remains - to ensure confidentiality, people spend too much effort.

And so that the report does not boil down to a simple use of the system SDK, Timofey conducts educational campaign on the existing methods and algorithms for determining license plates.
