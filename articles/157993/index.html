What to do if you are tired of advertising SMS

In continuation of the articles that the FAS collects additional information about the "short" numbers, I want to tell you how this service can help you get rid of unwanted SMS advertising.

According to Part 1 of Article 18 of the Federal Law "On Advertising", the distribution of advertising over telecommunication networks, including through the use of telephone, facsimile, and mobile radiotelephone communications, is allowed only with the prior consent of the subscriber or addressee to receive advertising.

So, having unsubscribed from all the advertising mailings of my operator, I naively believed that advertising SMS would no longer distract me. But marketers of various companies decided that without advertising I was bored and quickly corrected the situation. As a result, a year ago, I received several advertisements almost every week, mostly messages from the taxi of our city. The company was especially active before the New Year.
