Yota made a profit

The Scartel company, operating under the Yota brand at frequencies provided by MegaFon, made an operating profit in 2014, having tripled its own revenue.

The first financial report of Yota under RAS, issued after the acquisition of MegaFon and the launch of its own mobile operator, reports that the company's revenue in 2014 amounted to 10.4 billion rubles (a year earlier: 3.9 billion rubles). The share of Yota in the aggregate revenue of MegaFon was 3.5%

Also, according to the results of activities in 2014, Yota recorded a profit of 3.8 billion rubles.
