App Store - a goldmine for developers

While some independent developers boycott the App Store , others earn hundreds of thousands of dollars by selling their software through this store. For example, Steve Demeter (Steve Demeter, pictured), the developer of the game Trism earned $ 250,000 in two months .

A simple game like Tetris and costs only five dollars in the store. Steve Dimeter created it alone, paying only designer services in the amount of $ 500. It took quite a bit of time, but now Steve may no longer work until the end of his life. If the game is sold at the same pace, it will become a multimillionaire within a year.

By the way, Demeter's example is far from the only one. According to his estimates, all programs from the list of the most popular in the App Store rating bring their creators from $ 5,000 to $ 10,000 per day.
