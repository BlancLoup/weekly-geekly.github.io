And we vote again - now for MyOpera Community



From time to time, various publications and Internet projects conduct polls among users, figuring out the popularity of a particular browser. Naturally, not a single vote was complete without the Opera browser, which rightfully took prizes. Now the turn has reached the portal of the opera community - MyOpera Community. Voting takes place on the dedicated WebBlogAwards website, and the nominees are websites of various communities, from simple chat lovers to Michael Jackson fans. According to the results of the preliminary voting, the portal MyOpera Community entered the top ten. At the moment, there is a final vote, according to the results of which the best website of the last 2008 community will be determined. Anyone can give a vote, and not once, but every 24 hours. Despite the fact that this style of collecting votes does not pretend to the special accuracy of the results, you can probably spend a couple of minutes voting and supporting the portal of the community of users of an excellent browser.
