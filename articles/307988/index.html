Director of b2c-products of RBC Kirill Titov told about the redesign of the VC.ru edition .

What changed?

Created thematic blocks with interesting materials:

‚ÄúWe assumed that the reader did not initially come to RBC for auto, sport or real estate news, but only goes to the thematic pages if he is hooked by the headline.
