Meeting with Opera in Perm

At the request of shpankov :

The schedule of the Russian university tour of Opera Software, organized as part of the Opera Education Program, is almost complete - there are few days before it begins. This is by far the largest event held by a Norwegian company in Russia: Opera representatives will visit seven cities and hold seminars at ten universities.

The meeting in Perm will be held at PSTU on 10.11.08 from 15:00 to 18:00 in the auditorium 402 of Building A (Complex).

Within
The topics of the seminars are as follows:
- Web technologies of tomorrow
- Web standards as a guarantor of the best Internet
- Features of the development of new generation browsers

Seminar participants from Opera Software will be:
Charles McKatiNeville - Head of Web Standards
Alexey Feldgendler - Opera developer for desktops
Ilya Shpankov - development manager

Everyone is invited to the seminars, admission is free.

Further, there will be a meeting in an informal setting with representatives of Opera:

Date - November 10, 19-00
Place - cafe "Locos Gringos", Komsomolsky Prospect, 27

As always, I ask everyone who exactly comes to the meeting, check in at habrahabr.ru/blogs/opera/43723 - so that I can adjust the number of reserved seats in the cafe.
