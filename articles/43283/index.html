rubymag - Ruby Language Magazine in Russian

Today, our team has opened another project - rubymag . This is an electronic journal dedicated to the Ruby language and everything related to it.

The idea of rubymag came about thanks to our previous project rubyflow.ru . Rubyflow - Social news about the Ruby language, selected by the developer community. For two months of operation, this site has acquired several dozen users and every day it is read by several hundred people.

But the problem is that the format of the article on rubyflow.ru is limited only by the announcement in a couple of paragraphs, and very often there are interesting materials with a more extensive description. In addition, there are useful articles in English that are inaccessible to some developers due to ignorance of this language.

Summing up all this, we decided to open a separate resource, where we will publish articles about projects, libraries and events. Every week on rubymag.ru several new articles, as well as on Fridays a mandatory digest of events for the week.

You can find out about the updates of the magazine on the rubymag.ru website itself , via RSS, subscribe by email or Twitter .

We wish you a pleasant reading.
