Gateway LT2016u netbook for $ 150



Mobile operator Verizon began selling a netbook 3G netbook Gateway LT2016u for $ 150, subject to a contract for two years. Without a contract, a netbook can be bought for $ 300.

By and large, the model is a standard (1.6 GHz Intel Atom processor, 1 GB of RAM, 160 GB hard drive) netbook. Zest only in the presence of the 3G module and the cost, taking into account the contract.

Among other things, we note the 10.1-inch screen with LED backlighting and a resolution of 1024x600 pixels, a Wi-Fi module, a webcam, a card reader and built-in speakers. Managed by the machine Windows XP SP3, weighs 1.3 kg.
