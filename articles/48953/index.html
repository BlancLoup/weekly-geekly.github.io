Federal Online Exam - bugs and features

In 2008, my university became a member of the Federal Internet Exam in higher professional education.

What is the Federal Online Exam?



> FEPO is testing of students of a set of educational programs or a single educational program of all universities of the Russian Federation using the Internet in the off-line mode or in the on-line mode. - quote from the official site.

Yes, indeed, if we are talking about online testing, then a group of students are driven into the computer room, they open the website of the Federal Internet Exam and issue logins and passwords for access to the exam.

After that, it is assumed that the student is not taking notes and textbooks on his own and passes this test.

But the very idea of ‚Äã‚Äãan online exam was not approved by the teachers, at least in my university. As a result, faculty members look at the fact that students use third-party materials.

Also FEPO is used to check the residual knowledge of students. Those. A third-year student may be given a test in a subject that he took as a freshman.

How does this go?



After logging in to the page with the exam, the following window opens before the student:

A question, answer options and a ticking timer.
The test treats students rather sparingly - allows you to skip questions to return to them later. Even if you answered the question, there is an opportunity to return to it and choose a new answer.
Tasks include not only the questions where you need to choose the answer options, but also where you need to type the answer on the keyboard.

The window with Google and Wikipedia opens before the exam, i.e. until you are logged in to the Federal Internet Exam Site.

In the test is possible from 24 to 32 questions. Time is given from 45 minutes to 90 minutes. findings



Federal Internet Exam - already introduced in most universities, as an alternative to the usual. As you can see, he is far from perfect. There are too many critical points that affect the objective assessment of student knowledge.
It is good that our teachers give you the opportunity to retake the exams in the usual format, if the test results did not satisfy you.
On the other hand, troeshnik who have scored a decent amount of points by the ‚Äúspear‚Äù method have the opportunity to get a good grade without straining.
All data from the exams are stored in a special register, apparently in the Min. Education, from where they are sent to the dean of your university.

The idea itself pleases - finally, high technologies come into our ordinary life, exam time is reduced by several times, student knowledge is evaluated objectively, because communication does not occur with a teacher, but with a soulless machine that cannot be bribed.
But the result is deplorable. Students are not serious about this test, they blatantly use didactic materials, use the Internet and even the teacher‚Äôs help, with the result that students get high scores, which are worthy of being questioned, i.e. objective evaluation does not occur.
