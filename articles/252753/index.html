DaData.ru prompts email and determines the city by IP

DaData.ru is a service for automatically checking and correcting contact information (name, addresses, phone numbers, email). Plus javascript-widget and API prompts for entering the address, name and organization.

From the previous issue, Dadata learned:
  • prompt email as you type,
  • determine the city by IP address,
  • recognize car models.

Features are available through the user interface and HTTP API.


Email Tips


Help the user to enter email quickly and without typos.

They know the popular names of mailboxes (ivan, mail, home), common mail services (mail, Yandex, gmail) and top runet domains.

Connect as jQuery-plugin to any text field. Or via HTTP API. Free up to 10 thousand requests per day.

City by IP address


HTTP GET-method detectAddressByIp determines the city by IP-address in Russia. Uses the client‚Äôs IP address or a value from the ip parameter.



Recognizing car models


Dadata brings the names of cars to canonical form. Transliterates if needed; typos is not correcting.



New integrations


  • 1C Enterprise: get company details by TIN or parse the address in one line (by Chizik05 and Anton Abazin).

  • Node.js: analysis of name, addresses, phone numbers and email through the API Dadata (by Denis Zhbankov).
