Why does the Ministry of Industry and Trade prohibit the storage of data on foreign equipment?

A draft resolution on imposing a ban on the admission of software and hardware systems of data storage systems (DSS) of foreign origin for participation in procurement for state and municipal needs has been published on the Federal portal of draft regulatory acts. It is written that in order to ensure the security of the critical information infrastructure (CII) of Russia and for national projects. The CII includes, for example, information systems of government agencies, defense and energy enterprises, credit and financial organizations, telecom operators with a large number of subscribers. Confirmation of the country of origin of goods is the conclusion issued by the Ministry of Industry and Trade. The decree must be valid for two years from the date of its entry into force.


The explanatory note states that these measures are aimed at protecting the domestic market , developing the national economy and supporting Russian producers.
