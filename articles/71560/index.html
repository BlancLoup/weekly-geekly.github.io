The activity of all visitors (or a certain segment of visitors) on the selected page for a specified time interval is shown in the system in terms of the Clicks Map, the Scrolls Map and the Attention Map. In contrast to the generally accepted approximate methods of constructing maps based on binding events to screen coordinates, this implementation of maps in WebVisor links events not to screen coordinates, but to individual tags on the page. As a result, differences in the display of the page by different browsers, differences in the screen resolution of visitors and small changes in the page over time, do not reduce the accuracy of the map.
