Imminence
Once upon a time, in 2004, my friend and I made a project - a children's book of fairy tales.



Sleepless nights, a huge number of illustrations and illustrators, negotiations with printers, communication with sponsors, nerves and the joy of the work done - all this can not be described in two words. To say that we enjoyed the process - to say nothing. With the help of sales of this circulation, we were unable to even recoup the seal, but it is impossible to transfer the state of satisfaction from the benefits brought. The most pleasant thing was to receive feedback from grateful and happy parents who bought this book.

I have long wanted to do something similar: a project that will benefit people, and creators - pleasure. And last year I was lucky to get into a startup (although this word already sounds like a curse) Event Finder. I want to attract new participants to the project and attention in general, and here I will tell about my experience of participation in it and about the results.

Who needs it
Among our friends there are many artists, fashion designers, musicians and other creative people. Most of them are constantly worn with ideas to create an event, an event where they can express themselves, and then give their colleagues to express themselves.

Those who are looking for events are mostly brand managers, PR managers, marketing directors of large companies. Companies with an active position, good advertising budgets and a desire to be on the ear.

Motivation
There is a reasonable question: "Why does my armpit itch?" Ugh. Not this one. This is from another startup. A reasonable question - WHY?

We want to do a good and useful thing. We are pleased that we can make the necessary project for society, which helps ideas to find money for implementation.

Mission
Our mission is to develop the cultural life of Russia and support talented people on the path of creative creation.

Targets and goals
Our goal is to make a convenient resource for presenting ideas. And we want to create a bridge between the people who organize events and those who need a presence at such events.

It is important to make sure that the event organizers have a tool in their hands.



For these purposes, we decided to make a website. Class?

For example, on the site, in our opinion, everything should be in order to describe your event with dignity and in detail. And we came up with a configurator.



Monetization
Now the site is created on personal funds, and we would not refuse income. But the goal is to make the site profitable now.

We were advised by several monetization systems.

Make the site closed, with access by subscription. That is, companies pay to access the site. We believe that this is a barrier. And then we will not reach our goal.

Earn on banner advertising. Unfortunately, our project is designed for a very narrow audience, and we assume that the attendance of the resource will be no more than 20,000 - 50,000 people per month.

Earn interest on sponsored packages. This is interesting, but we are unlikely to find a mechanism for honestly receiving this money.

So far this is all that has occurred to us.
