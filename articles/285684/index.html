Amazon offers $ 1,000 per AWS account to those who successfully complete the edX entrepreneurs course



Hosting Amazon Web Services has decided to take part in helping startups. If you have a well-developed IT startup and you understand this topic, you can try your luck in the competition. To do this, you just need to successfully complete one of two business courses on the edX portal (in English). As a reward you will be given $ 1,000 to the AWS account, which you can pay for hosting your project. The amount will be available for two years.

In addition, Amazon offers a free one-day web-based course on using the service, a month of business class support, several small courses for improving administrative skills, and bonuses from other companies. Details of the proposal read here .

According to TechCrunch , the courses provide quite serious material, so do not hope to easily and quickly replenish your account with AWS. To complete the course, at least you have to come to grips with it and spend several dozens of hours on it.
