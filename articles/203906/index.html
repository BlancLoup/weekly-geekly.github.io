December 7-8 - XII selection of projects in the Kazan IT Park Business Incubator

You are an aspiring IT entrepreneur. We have already decided on the cause of the future and are confident that he will surely shoot soon. The case remains for the small - the initial capital and launch pad ...




The Kazan IT Park business incubator offers not just office space, but also housing for the first time for non-resident companies and infrastructure assistance for the development of the project. For each project, a business incubator allocates $ 40,000 of infrastructure investments per year. And this is not to mention the workplaces, services of its own data center of the IT park, educational programs, accounting and legal services, a developed mentoring network, as well as lined up relations with venture market players in Russia, the CIS and abroad.

Residents and graduates of the Business Incubator have already received more than 200 million rubles of investments. Your turn has come!

Just fill out an application for participation in the 12th selection of the Business Incubator and start building the future of your startup with the team of the Kazan IT Park Business Incubator.
