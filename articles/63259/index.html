Laptops Acer Aspire 3935, 5935 and 8935 - already in Russia

The press service of Acer has recently announced that the new multimedia notebooks Aspire 3935, Aspire 5935 and Aspire 8935, based on the Intel Core 2 Duo processors, have entered the Russian market.



The 13.3-inch Acer Aspire 3935 notebook, which won the gold award at the Design & Innovation awards ceremony (Computex 2009), is made of a metal case with a matte finish. The thickness of the device is less than 2.5 cm, and the laptop weighs about 1.9 kg. If desired, the Aspire 3935 can be equipped with HDD or solid state drive. It will be possible to purchase a model in domestic retail at a price of 44 thousand rubles.



Laptops Acer Aspire 5935 and Acer Aspire 8935 are made in the style of Gemstone new and are equipped with displays with a diagonal of 15.6 "and 18.4", respectively (16: 9 aspect ratio). The user is given the opportunity to choose the most suitable graphic solution: Aspire 5935G can be equipped with NVIDIA GeForce GT 130M graphics or NVIDIA GeForce GT240M graphics with 1 GB of video memory. At the same time, the Aspire 8935G laptop can boast an ATI Radeon Mobility HD 4670 card with 1 GB GDDR3. For data storage in the Aspire 5935G provides a storage capacity of 500 GB.



Buy Acer Aspire 5935 will be at a price of 43 thousand rubles, and Acer Aspire 8935 - 48 thousand rubles.
