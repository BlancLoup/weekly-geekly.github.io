Picstag.ru - photo tagging or interactive photo service

Hello, dear habravchane!
Let me tell you about the new service for creating interactive images Picstag.ru . It allows you to leave on the image interactive tags with text or links. Now, when you hover on the photo, a small window will pop up with a commentary that you specified. Restrictions on the number of tags, upload photos - no.




How to use

We tried to simplify uploading pictures and placing tags, as well as to diversify the code options for inserting pictures on websites, blogs and forums.

Upload images in two ways:

1. Drag'n'Drop - just drag and drop pictures into the form on the site
2. Select pictures from computer

Placing tags is also easy. Just click on the photo and enter your comment and / or hyperlink in the window that opens.

After saving the tags, you can either share the image on social networks and popular services, or send it by e-mail, or select a code to paste on the website or blog. The user can choose from 7 options: direct link, preview + link, BBCode, JavaScript iFrame, Flash link, Flash iFrame or Flash HTML.



For whom

Options for using phototagging are limited only by the user's imagination. This may be news, culinary, automotive resources; sites about fashion, children, beauty; Pictures with tags can be inserted into blogs of popular services like LJ and Ya.ru.

Monetization

Frankly, we are not altruistic to endlessly pay for traffic, servers, wages from our pockets, and we understand that you will not go too far with enthusiasm. And there are a lot of plans for the development of the service. Therefore, we are now developing a monetization model, under which we will not have to veil the site with teasers, banners and other things.

Plans

Plans for the development of a lot. Here are some points:

- Adding audio. You can add sound from a microphone or from a file to a photo (already implemented, but for the time being we are testing)
- Creation of galleries and albums by users
- Improve usability when adding tags to the photo: the ability to choose an anchor for the link, the ability to edit individual tags
- Plugins for downloading images via browsers
- Applications for Iphone and Android

For partners:

For owners of sites with high traffic who wish to use Picstag.ru, we are ready to provide special conditions - writing the necessary features, plug-ins, etc.

I am pleased to answer any of your questions and listen to criticism and suggestions in the comments. You can also write to mail@picstag.ru
