Welcome to Top 3D Expo in September



Friends! We invite you to the next exhibition-conference on additive technologies and digital production Top 3D Expo , which will be held on September 20 in Technopolis "Moscow" on Volgogradsky Avenue.



Top 3D Expo - the largest international diversified exhibition and conference on additive technologies and digital production in Russia. More than 30 domestic and international speakers will share their experience and practical cases on the use of 3D technologies for solving business problems.

Business program sections


During the conference, you will be addressed by professionals from leading companies in the country and the world in the field of digital production and additive technologies, who will share their experience and knowledge, cases from practice and their vision of the industry's prospects for the near future.

Representatives of companies actively working in the following areas will speak at the conference.

3D printing and additive manufacturing technologies



Voenmekh , TYAZHMASH , ODK-Aviadvigatel , Technopark Ingria

- Equipment for 3D printing: what is offered by domestic and foreign manufacturers.
- The introduction of additive technologies in industrial production. Risks and benefits.

Bioprinting and metal printing in medicine



Rokit , Association of Specialists in 3D Printing in Medicine , Sechenov University , LOGIKS Medical Systems

- Practical cases of application of additive 3D printing technologies in medicine.
- Bioprint and biofabrication - present and future.

Automation of digital production. Robotics



SPECIALIA , Top 3D Group , Agency for Strategic Initiatives , Advanced Research Foundation

Round table



In the framework of the round table, experts from leading Russian companies specializing in additive technologies and using them will discuss the following topics:

- The most promising niches of 2019;
- What projects have the shortest payback period;
- What technologies will change the market and where to invest in 2020;
- How to make money on FDM, SLM and SLS-printing;
- What is the difference between Russian, European, American and Chinese developments - which ones are the most profitable and reliable.
