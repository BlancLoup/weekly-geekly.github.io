Meeting of the Innovators Club in St. Petersburg (medicine and IT in medicine)

On March 26, the 18 meeting of the Club of Innovators will be held in the secret hall in St. Petersburg on the Birzhevoy line, 4.

On Saturday noon we will discuss the latest trends in innovative technologies in healthcare and the application of IT in medicine.

Since personal well-being, directly related to health, concerns everyone, we will try to present at the upcoming meeting those technologies and promising areas that will help us live longer and overcome internal adversity with minimal loss.

The head physicians of several St. Petersburg hospitals are invited to the meeting, from them we learn what technologies and IT solutions are not enough for healers today and what are the current trends in diagnosis and treatment.

We will have the opportunity from practitioners to learn about the features of the introduction of medical systems. Inna Aschenbrenner will talk about the experience of Corus-Consulting in the implementation of such systems in Russia, and Maxim Ignatov ( e-Legion ) on their implementation in the United States. From him we also learn how the clinic of the future will look like.

Together with invited experts from medical universities and research institutes, we will evaluate several innovative projects.
