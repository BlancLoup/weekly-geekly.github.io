Google Phone: photo, functionality, platform features

The video demonstrates the capabilities of the Google mobile platform, presents two prototypes of the phone, based on Google Android. One of them was discovered on the platform developer site. His image appears when you start the application in the emulator.





Judging by the documentation for developers, all devices based on the Google platform will come with a set of the most necessary software, which includes an email client, an SMS program, a calendar, maps, a browser based on the WebKit engine, and a contact manager. This kit can be expanded with the appropriate hardware.



The platform itself supports 3D graphics, SQL, vector and raster images, audio and video files in MPEG4, H.264, MP3, AAC, AMR, accelerometer, GPS, compass, camera and, of course, with Bluetooth wireless standards and Wi-Fi. In addition, second and third generation cellular networks are supported.
