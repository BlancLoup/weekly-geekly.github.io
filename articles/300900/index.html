What does the authorized capital mean for a company?

The company has a share capital, even small children know about it now. However, not every adult will be able to tell why the company needs this capital, how to pay it, and how these funds can be used.

Our legislation is rapidly developing, and not so long ago, the registration procedure for an LLC was eased. Now the authorized capital can be paid after registration. How and when to pay?


Authorized capital (UK) - the first money of the company. However, this is not exactly the same as the starting capital. These funds can be compared more with the nominal value of the company at the dawn of its activities. Founders are thrown into a common "piggy bank", which, before the law and people, will be the minimum guarantor for future contractors.

It is possible to make funds in the authorized capital in the following ways:

First , in money. You deposit them in the company's cash desk or transfer by bank transfer to its savings or settlement account.
The scheme with a savings account is extremely rare. Such an account is opened before registration in the name of the company. Most often, payment is made after the company is registered on its bank account.

And one more important point: if we do not pay money, we will have to involve an independent appraiser to confirm the amount. Previously, an appraiser had to be attracted at a property value of more than 20,000 rubles, and now always. Given that these services are not cheap, the non-monetary contribution loses some of its attractiveness.

There is quite a lot of time for payment by the founders. When creating a company, founders usually have to pay up the authorized capital within four months from the date of registration . But in the decision on the establishment or the agreement on the establishment can be set a shorter period, so as not to relax and not forget.
For example, a company is registered on January 1, which means that it is necessary to pay up the authorized capital no later than May Day of the same year. And in the agreement on the establishment, you can specify that the Criminal Code should be paid no later than March 1. Extend the term, for example, until July 1 or November is impossible.

And here is the long-awaited "BUT!". The law states that the minimum size of the Criminal Code must be paid in cash. Then, if the authorized capital of 20 000 thousand, then ten of them need to pay it in money, and the rest can be property.

As you understood, with payment everything is quite simple. Difficulties are only the evaluation of intangible assets, so before making a decision on the contribution of something other than money, think a few times.

And if you do not pay?


The law does not provide for any penalties for non-payment of the authorized capital within the prescribed period, allowing the founders themselves to write down penalties and fines in the contract of establishment.
