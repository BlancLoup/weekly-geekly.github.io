Yandex and Western intelligence services




The company "Yandex" announced that at the end of 2018 it was subjected to attacks by intruders . This attack consisted of attempts by cybercriminals to infiltrate a rare type of malware into the company's infrastructure. Subject to the successful introduction of it into the network, the malware operator can monitor user accounts for the company's services.

Cybercriminals hunted for technical information that provides user authorization. If the attack was successful, the hackers could easily get a large amount of personal data of the company's users.

The Ministry of Transport will regulate the work of online aggregators of joint trips on cars


The Russian Ministry of Transport plans to regulate online aggregators . The largest service of its kind is BlaBlaCar. The project imposes a number of restrictions on the capabilities of those users who offer transportation services.

In particular, the project prohibits the use of cash to pay for driver services, plus a limit is imposed on the number of flights per day (no more than two) and the number of fellow travelers - there should not be more than five.

As for the draft law, this is a document representing a new federal law; it also supplements the existing norms. It was sent on June 28 to the members of the working group of the Ministry of Transport, which regulates legal relations on such trips.
