Round table with representatives of investment companies

Within the framework of the first professional conference of web developers Russian Internet Technologies 2007, we plan to conduct an expert assessment and presentation of the most interesting projects in the field of web technologies developed by its participants.

Pre-selection and expert evaluation of projects by the program committee will be held in preparation for the conference. The program of the conference itself has two round tables:

Expert advice - in its framework, the authors of the projects will receive an assessment of the technological component, comments and recommendations. It will be a truly highly professional peer review, defining the potential of the project in terms of technology.

Round table with representatives of investment companies - the authors will be able to present the business model of their project, get its assessment from professionals in the field of investment in Internet projects. Also, participants of the round table will be able to gain an understanding of the requirements, terms of cooperation and business schemes for investment projects.

To participate in the program of evaluation and presentation of projects, you must send a request to the organizers of the conference by e-mail (at the moment), or fill in the form on the website (from 01.02.2007)
