Twisted pair rug

Yes, the name of the person who invented a permanent fetish and an endless source of joy for almost all admins is famous for centuries - the color differentiation of the lived twisted pair.
This Friday evening I want to show you another handicraft from the category of wooden flash drives.


It all started with the fact that 5 twisted-pair meters were lying around at home, and the new work did not have a cup stand (from a wet mug, you know, such awful stains remain on the table). Without further ado, I set about gutting the cable.
Frankly, I never knitted before in my life, I have no idea how a scarf or a mitten is obtained from a large round skein of threads. Actually had to learn in the field.
For everything about everything, it took me five nights of leisurely knitting in front of a laptop display and under the mocking gaze of my wife.
Next, I will describe the technical process, subtleties and errors. Those who are not interested, can not continue to read. And the fingers will tell you a huge thank you, if you do not repeat my experience.

On the first row already received, we loosen the last knot and make a loop into which we push the folded cable.




We are on the right track, but bad luck: there is already a loop into which a folded cable can be inserted to create a new loop, into which we will extend a folded cable to create a loop, into which ... But how will this be connected with the first row? The answer to the surface - we relax now the penultimate node from the first row and fold the cable twice in two loops.


First, the rows are connected and we have a new cycle. We repeat it to the end and by the same principle go in the opposite direction. It is better to tighten each loop to a certain extent, otherwise they will stick out. And it is better to tighten everywhere the same way, otherwise it will be weak somewhere, but somewhere it will be strong because of what the rug will lead and it will not be even, but slightly convex, as I did.
Now the first trick, which I did not pay enough attention to, and the rug at my end greatly narrowed. Reaching the very last node of the third row, do not rush to go in the opposite direction. You see a tightly tightened eyelet sticking out from the previous row, you need to relax it and push the cable into it, otherwise each next row will be shorter than the previous one.
By eye we estimate how many rows you need to go through in order to get a square and work - monotonous work, which at some moments starts to run out, especially when the fingers are already burning from friction and pressure.
<Spoiler> to loosen tightly knotted knots, you can use mother's knitting needles or paste from a pen. </ Spoiler>
Note that the second end of the core should be on the opposite side if you want to make a patchcord like me. Starting a row of a different color, you must leave sufficient margin.
If you want to compress it, as I did, note that you must have an odd number of rows in order for the tails of the veins to be on all sides.
