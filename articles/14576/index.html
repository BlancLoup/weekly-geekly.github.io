Startup - Obzograf - Service reviews and reviews of any goods and services

Before buying a product or service (not necessarily via the Internet), you almost always want to know (hear, read, ask) the opinions and opinions of people who have already acquired it and have real experience of owning or interacting with the product. Positive or negative, this experience is exactly what interests us. To form a final opinion on your choice based on the feedback received. It happens that having a certain need, we do not imagine what product this need can satisfy. In this case, we also often have to rely on the recommendations of friends, acquaintances or those whom we trust.

At the same time, there are many topical sites in Runet (afisha.ru, market.yandex.ru, ixbt.com, tury.ru, etc.) that reflect the opinions of ordinary people in one form or another. Someone pays more attention to this and creates convenient tools for this, someone is limited only to the function of adding a comment to the product description, but such sites do not solve the full Problem, or partially solve it within their own topics. Thus, at the moment in Runet there is a great need for high-quality and fairly objective reviews and consumer reviews, as well as in the general platform, where you can get to know the opinions of consumers and express your own.
