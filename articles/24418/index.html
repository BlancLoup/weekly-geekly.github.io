Uz-Translations: foreign languages, online education



Dear users, I want to bring to your attention my language project: Uz-Translations - an information blog for philologists and translators. The site was launched in 2004.

The purpose of the site is to create a unique resource on the Internet, where materials will be collected both for students and those who teach foreign languages. Every day the site contains articles on the art of translation, linguistics, regional geography, as well as reviews of programs, educational materials, dictionaries and online courses that are now gaining popularity all over the world!
