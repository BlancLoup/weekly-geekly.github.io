The solar panel turns the sun

The sunflower one-octane, or oilseed sunflower, has an interesting property: heliotropism. This property of plants to change their position under the influence of light was first described by Leonardo da Vinci, and the concept itself was introduced in the 19th century by Augustin the Senior. This property has inspired the creators of the solar battery Smartflower - for good reason it is consonant with the Sunflower.


The device is relatively compact and able to emerge independently. Relatively - because it can be transported to another place in the truck. It works autonomously - you only need to turn on, after which the "flower" monitors the position of the sun and turns to it to get the maximum light.

What Smartflower can do:


- Monitor the position of the sun using GPS, even in cloudy weather
- Self cleaning.
- Feel the power of the wind and turn into a safe position.
- To develop and unfold.

Specifications:


Rated power: 2.13 kilowatt peak.
Rated power, taking into account the biaxial tracking of the position of the sun: 3.2 kilowatt peak.
Panel type: monocrystalline.
Weight: 1000 kg
