Universal reader of electronic books from the monitor and from mobile devices - a revised version 2

Colleagues, have a nice day!
Thank you all so much for the criticism and suggestions after this post . Now in the list of improvements and improvements 34 positions.

The essence of the enreader.com service is simple:
1. Load the book to the server (support TXT | RTF | DOC | PDF | EPUB | LIT | FB2 | DJVU;
2. Read a book on the desktop, or on the iPhone-iPad-iPod, or on the android device.

Wherever you read a book, it will always open where it was closed the last time (including on another device). Bookmarks are also the same on all devices.

Here is what has been done in the last two weeks:
