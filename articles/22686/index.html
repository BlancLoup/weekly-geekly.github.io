Yandeks.Metrika opened reports - for advertisers and partners

The metric, a tool for analyzing site traffic, successfully works in Yandex.Direct as a means of evaluating the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, and is also used to determine the quality of the audience of partner sites, which, in turn, affects the price of conversions on advertisements.
