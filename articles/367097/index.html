3D Print Conference. St. Petersburg

Here they will talk about how to build a car, make an extruder for magnetic spraying, model a duck and perform casting on investment casting using 3D printing.



The main thing at the conference is, of course, the speakers. Therefore, we will tell about these people and the reports that they prepared for 3D Print Conference. St. Petersburg.


Belosludtsev Evgeny Vladimirovich, CEO of 3D Experts.
The company was the first in Russia to start mass production of kits for the assembly of printers. Great help for enthusiasts who have long dreamed of putting together their own 3D printer in the garage. It is better for everyone than to drag parts from abroad. In addition, the guys have created an excellent knowledge base on 3D printing.



However, the speaker‚Äôs speech will not be about the frail bored home printers. Eugene will tell about his experience in the development of industrial models. His track record includes the creation of inert extruders for printing with medical PLA-plastic with an eye to maxillofacial surgery, as well as working with ceramic chips for dentistry and magnetic sputtering for a near-war plant.

In addition, the speaker will talk about the subtleties of interaction with customers: with whom it is worth working, what to avoid and, in general, about market conditions and the creation of a professional portfolio. This part of the presentation will be useful first of all for startups who have already mastered 3D printing, but cannot move further than plastic handicrafts due to the lack of standing orders.



The trio of developers - Nikita Mokhirev, Anton Vedishin, Ivan Gaidamakin - from the company Secured3D.

The company lives on two continents: in San Francisco and Tallinn. It develops a convenient cloud service for storing and protecting 3D models - the key component that was not enough for the colorful future of 3D printing and home production.

For example: you have created a beautiful blanket ring, you want to sell it freelancing. Yes, but only if you download someone to a model one, no one else will buy it from you, but just save it. And you have to model a new ring.



Therefore, Secured3D created a cloud service that transmits information directly to the customer‚Äôs 3D printer, without first downloading the file to a PC. It works with all OSes, and the list of compatible printers is also huge.
