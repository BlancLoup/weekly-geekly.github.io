Algorithm Conference on Strings

This year, the 25th anniversary conference of Combinatorial Pattern Matching , the main event in the world of algorithms on strings, will take place in the Moscow office of Yandex.

The conference will begin with open lectures by well-known scientists who are the founding fathers of a series of conferences and have made a huge contribution to the field of algorithms on the lines:

  • Alberto Apostolico (see, for example, textbooks and conference proceedings at Amazon)
  • Maxim Kroshmor ( again books on Amazon )
  • Eugene Myers (one of the authors of suffix arrays and BLAST, see Wikipedia ).


Registration for the conference is already closed, but you have a chance to get to open lectures. For this you need to submit an application . Lectures are free, they will be held on June 16, from 9:00 to 12:40. For those who will not be able to attend in person, an online broadcast will be organized and videos of all reports will be published. Will be broadcast here: tech.yandex.ru/events/yagosti/CPM
