Steve Wozniak on the future and the iPhone

Apple has already made its way into the living room people, as well as in the field of entertainment. They have gained a huge advantage in these areas. They have an operating system, hardware, software, and online services that work together. Companies like Hewlett-Packard can make a computer. They have a lot of great ideas, but it all comes down to software, and here they are limited to the operating system that Microsoft sells.
