SIM card for tablet


In this case, users offered to release for tablets a separate type of SIM-cards, which have no download speed limit when using mobile Internet. As they say, it is said - done .

Using a smartphone as an access point to the Internet for a fee


Yota has always sought to give its customers the most convenient product. If you need unlimited Internet only for large screens (laptop, stationary computer), then the client can choose a modem ; if you only need internet on your smartphone or tablet - mobile operator. At the launch of the operator, we provided a free tethering option at a speed of 1 Mbit, which allowed us to ensure stable network operation, while customers distributed the Internet unlimitedly. However, the distribution of the Internet via Wi-Fi is not the intended use of Internet access from a tablet or smartphone, although we understand that sometimes our clients may have situations when it is necessary, and at more than 1 Mbps speed. Therefore, when an offer came from a user, we implemented a short-term access to tethering service. If it is necessary to distribute unlimited Internet to other devices, a client using a Yota SIM card for a tablet or smartphone can choose the time he needs - two hours or a day, without any restrictions on speed and traffic. This service is demanded by users who, for a number of reasons, are inconvenient to use other solutions for distributing the Internet.

Select a number in the mobile application


The Yota mobile application is convenient for several reasons, and one of its advantages for the client is that it allows you to provide convenient service management without visiting the point of sale. So, it would be unfair to deprive our users of the opportunity to choose a certain number when ordering a SIM card (if it is not already busy, of course), as it can be done in any cellular phone salon. Chat push notifications and saving its history


The main channel of communication with customer support for customers of a mobile operator is a chat, which can be accessed both from our portal www.yota.ru and from the application. Some time after its launch, users asked to implement the preservation of the history of communication with consultants. Previously, when the window was closed, all correspondence was reset. But there are situations when the client needs to return to discussing the issue with a consultant from the support service, and the chat is already completely empty, since the session has been completed. And if a person also changed, he had to explain the situation anew. Naturally, this was inconvenient, so we recorded the history of communication with customer support.

Also, at the request of users, we added a push-notification stating that the consultant had written something in the chat. After all, do not sit looking at the screen while waiting for an answer.

Binding cards for payment


A simple and obvious thing in the age of electronic payments and high technologies is to save the bank card data in a mobile application . Conveniently, you do not need to drive in details every time. Moreover, very few people remember them by heart, they are not always at hand and each time you have to go into wallets. Today, about 30% of our customers pay for services using linked bank cards, and this figure continues to grow. We cannot fail to note that this is a record figure for the Russian telecom market, and such a high proportion indicates that our audience keeps up with the times.
