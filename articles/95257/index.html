Minsk Mobile Tech Meetup - June 5

On June 5, at 15.00 (Saturday), we are waiting for everyone who is interested in mobile technologies in the Institute of Journalism of the BSU (Kalvariyskaya St., 9, room 414).

The first MitAp - Minsk Mobile Tech Meetup - will be devoted to the development of applications for mobile phones. Moreover, geolocation services, applications and projects will be discussed first.

The event will be attended by both experienced developers of programs for mobile phones, and representatives of various projects and organizations interested in such applications. Separately, will be invited to present existing projects (programmers, project managers) specializing in geolocation. The agreements were reached with maps.interfax.by , Opera, journalists of the specialized online edition Nomobile.ru and other specialists.


Among the listeners and speakers of our event are planned employees of such companies and projects as:
  • Mts
  • life :)
  • Interfax (project site maps.interfax.by )
  • Viaden media
  • Agency of mobile marketing STREAMLINE
  • TUT.by and IT.TUT.by
  • it-job.by
  • other...

The entrance to the mitap is free, by prior registration here .
