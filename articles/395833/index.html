Smartphone, are you all right?

Today, I got another device based on the Android platform. Not that I was engaged in reviews of gadgets and quite a lot of them passed through me, not that I would repair them, and all the devices that passed through me would have been knowingly with a marriage, no. But despite this, I have to state the fact that all, even new, devices over the past 5 years that have fallen into my hands on the setup had some flaws.

And the flaws were the most different - problems with the case, the battery controller, the driver modules and sensors, embedded applications, the collapse of Google Apps is generally a common thing. And not only cheap Chinese smartphones are susceptible to this, the period of support for which ends earlier than the devices fall on store shelves - even the flagships of well-known brands sin this.

In general, I caught myself thinking that on any device that fell into my hands would not be such that everything worked fine. Therefore, this survey may be interesting to many, to understand how everything is good / bad in the market of Android-smartphones.
