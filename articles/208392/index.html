Considering that success in the IT industry, in theory, can also be judged by the fact that the company is included in the NASDAQ-100 index (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NASDAQ-100). The index itself is NDX, but investors usually track the price of so-called QQQ power shares, that is, securities that weightedly reflect the price of 100 largest companies' shares (minus financial companies), which are actually traded on the NASDAQ (http: //en.wikipedia .org / wiki / NASDAQ with a total capitalization of over $ 4 trillion). For example, the current price for QQQ fluctuates around $ 85 ($ 65.96 is the minimum price and $ 87.96 is the maximum price for the last 52 weeks). The NASDAQ and QQQ index is considered the main measure of the situation in the IT industry, despite the fact that it includes several non-technology companies.
