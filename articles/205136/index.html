Anki in brief


Anki Drive is a toy whose main feature is artificial intelligence. Its essence is very simple: it is an ordinary track for racing toy cars. What makes it unique is that these cars are equipped with advanced AI, which allows them to quickly analyze the situation during the race and show enviable results. To overtake the robot is really not easy!
Do not want to play against the computer, play with a friend - this mode is also provided. In any case, fun is guaranteed!

You can control cars only with the help of Apple devices. There is no application for Android yet, but perhaps it is worth the wait. The wording of the developers is as follows: "Anki DRIVE does not currently work with Android devices."

And Anki will work only with relatively new apple gadgets. Here is the official list of supported devices:

  • iPhone 4s and newer
  • iPod touch 5th generation
  • iPad 3rd generation and newer
  • iPad mini

The application can be found on the App Store . By the way, it brings a fairly large element of interactivity into the game. Here you can buy weapons and improve the characteristics of your car, earning points for the "destruction" of the opponent.



In general, the game is practically no different from modern computer arcade racing, except that the action takes place not on the screen of a computer or tablet, but on the floor of your apartment! A kind of experimental breath of fresh air in the digital entertainment industry.
