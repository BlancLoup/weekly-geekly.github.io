Magic bubble for the revival of technology

Someone who has tried to vacuum the system unit, especially in a neglected state, knows that this is a dreadful job, and the result is so-so. The dust between the plates of the radiator and on the blades of the cooler pulls out the vacuum cleaner badly, and this, in fact, is the most important thing for which cleaning is needed.

At first, I, too, had vacuumed everything I could, and far from the worst Kirby vacuum cleaner. It turned out clean, but far from perfect.

Then he took an open sistemnik and spray in the bathroom, so as not to breed dirt. "Bubble" is initially quite heavy, probably more than a pound. The seller said that there should be enough for a dozen cleansing. But I had a very neglected case - I didn‚Äôt clean it for 2 years, from the very purchase. Therefore, I did not regret the air and tried to blow everything up to which I reached.

The result exceeded expectations. Now everything looks like new and, importantly, the fan on the processor has ceased to buzz nasty. I was about to change it, but now it is not necessary. In places, there remained a strong dust stuck to the blades, but not much at all. For those with dust allergies, I think it is better to wear a bandage for the duration of the purge.

Thus cleared two sistemnik, spending 2/3 cartridges. I think if cleaned in a timely manner, it really should be enough for 10 times.
