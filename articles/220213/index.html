Welcome to Skyforge closed beta.

News for all fans of MMORPG: Allods Team is pleased to announce the start of recording for the closed beta testing of Skyforge .



This game was developed by the Allods Team, a member of the Mail.Ru Group, in collaboration with Obsidian Entertainment, USA. The presentation of the game for all fans of online multiplayer role-playing games was held at the international conference Game Developers Conference, held from March 17 to 21 in San Francisco.

Skyforge is the best tradition and innovation of the MMORPG industry. One of the main differences and advantages of the game is a unique game world, which combines the achievements of high technology and familiar fantasy elements.

The developers have put special emphasis on the combat system, turning each battle into a real performance. Allods Team modernized the usual target system, transforming a monotonous battle into a dynamic and beautiful battle: dodge enemy attacks, conduct crushing combos, finish the enemy with spectacular finishers.

In Skyforge, players can go from an immortal hero to a powerful god. Collecting faithful followers, the immortal multiplies his strength, becoming even closer to the cherished abilities of God.

Another Skyforge feature is the ability to change the class on the fly. This makes it easy to find a group for yourself and not to pump additional characters of various classes.

You can leave a request to participate in beta testing here . Later, all participants will be informed of the start date.
