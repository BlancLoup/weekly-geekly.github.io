GDG DevFest Gorky 2017


Hi, Habr and habrozhiteli! Have you ever been to Nizhny Novgorod? Have you observed the merging of the Oka and the Volga against the background of the sunset from the wall of the ancient Nizhny Novgorod Kremlin ? Enjoyed the panorama from Embankment Fedorovsky ? Did you ride one of the longest cable cars in Europe over the Volga? Right now!



November 18, 2017 in Nizhny Novgorod will host the third international conference GDG DevFest Gorky 2017 , dedicated to Google technologies.


What is GDG?

What is GDG DevFest ??


This is a series of IT conferences for developers. Held around the world and organized by the community of Google Developer Groups.


What is GDG DevFest Gorky ??


This is an international conference for developers, held annually by the GDG Nizhny Novgorod community in one of the most beautiful cities in Russia - Nizhny Novgorod.


This grand event will take place within the walls of the Nizhny Novgorod University. N.I. Lobachevsky and will bring together a huge number of developers and other IT-specialists!


This year the conference will be truly international , within its framework there will be presentations by speakers from the Google Developers Group community from different countries of the world - Russia, Great Britain, India, Poland, Turkey, Serbia, Ukraine ... Here are some of them:


  • Alexey Korovyansky (Mobilatorium, Omsk, Russia) - Google Developer Expert, actively promoting VR / AR capabilities among mobile developers. Founder of the GDG community in Omsk. At the moment, Alexey is organizing the Mobilatorium (creative VR / AR laboratory) and is developing innovative VR / AR projects together with the Effective team.
  • Alexey Rybakov (DataArt, Kharkov, Ukraine) - Senior Software Engineer, Technical Evangelist DataArt, organizer of GDG Kharkiv-Center, conference speaker at GDG DevFest, Droidcon, Mobius, IT NonStop, IT Talk. For more than 12 years he was engaged in programming games for various mobile systems (from Palm OS, Windows CE to iOS)
  • Milos Marinkovic (CounterPath, Novi Sad, Serbia) - Developer. Organizer GDG Novi Sad. Interests: Android, Modern Web, IoT. Loves to learn new things about innovation and programming. In his free time he works with various opensource projects.
  • Resul Kaner Yildirim (InnovationBox, Istanbul, Turkey) - Google Developer Expert, UX designer and technological geek. Professional in UX, IoT and Android. Led GDG Istanbul since 2013, organizes, participates and speaks at various events, such as Droidcon, GDG DevFest and so on.

Speakers will make presentations on Android, Web, Firebase, Google Cloud, VR / AR, IoT, Kotlin (for example, Android Auto, Instant Apps, Android Things, ARCore, Google Assistant will be mentioned), as well as neural networks and machine learning.
Besides, this year, besides technical experts in the field of development using Google technologies, professionals in the field of project promotion and development will speak at the conference, as well as, for the first time, at the DevFest in Nizhny Novgorod, experts in the field of iOS will all share their experience and best practices. For example:


  • Adam Wesolowski (GrowthEngine.xyz, Wroclaw, Poland) is an experienced marketer who helps start-ups grow and scale online marketing activities. Founder and CEO of GrowthEngine.xyz marketing agency, Google Developer Developer and Google Launchpad mentor in San Francisco and Warsaw. He worked in companies such as Google, PizzaPortal, SecurionPay, Nasza-klasa. She likes to work in sprint and A / B testing.
  • Marat Ishmeev (Tinkoff, Moscow, Russia) - Master of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. He has published a number of papers on differential equations in Russian and foreign journals. Since 2011, engaged in mobile development on iOS.
