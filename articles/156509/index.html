Some impressions of Surface

Yesterday, I went to the Microsoft Store and twisted Surface a bit in my hands to decide whether to buy it or not. It was "Surface with Windows RT", built on an ARM processor. A x86-compatible Pro version will only be available in a few months.

It is noticeable that Surface enjoys a certain popularity, there are as many visitors in the Microsoft Store as in the Apple Store, located very close. It took me a few minutes to find a free device and a free consultant.

Near Surface makes a more pleasant impression than you might think, after reading some reviews. That's where the interface of Windows 8 in its place - so it is here, very thoughtful and convenient! In addition, Surface has a killer feature - full USB support! But, nevertheless, Surface has some drawbacks. Here are a few things that I noticed ...





1. Build quality.


Surface looks brutal - sharp corners, rows of screws are visible under the folded stand; "Content before Chrome" in action! It does not scare me, for example, I love Lenovo ThinkPad notebooks, which by their appearance are capable of bringing someone to hysterics. But the presence of small, but quite noticeable eyes, gaps between the elements of the case somewhat spoils the impression - Apple products in this regard look better.

2. Flip stand.


Unlike the iPad, which necessarily requires a cover to set it on the table, Surface has a built-in stand. The body material is hard, the stand does not bend and does not falter. But what will happen to it if I drop the Surface on the floor? .. I must say that I dropped my iPad several times.

3. Type Cover.


It seemed to me that tactile sensations could be better. However, this is a matter of taste. But I categorically did not like the fact that when folded, the keyboard is not fixed in any way. By the way, the consultant at the store said that I was far from being the first to pay attention to this, and perhaps this flaw will be corrected.

There are other problems. For example, the lack of 4G and GPS confuses me. In addition, I noticed that when typing in Word, significant time passes between pressing a button and the appearance of a character on the screen ... Is there not enough processor power?

In general, the device turned out quite funny, but now it is unclear whether to buy it or not. It is possible that the tablets on Windows 8 from other vendors will be more interesting.
