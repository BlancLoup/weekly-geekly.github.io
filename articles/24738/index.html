Come off on monday morning

Have you ever had an early, cloudy Monday morning, you have no desire to get up, and an annoying alarm clock rings and rings. And here you are in a fit of anger, grab him and throw him against the wall, or by careless sleepy sharp movement of your hand you knock him off the bedside table or bedside table, and of course he is broken. Well!? A familiar story? Now this can be avoided!

There are special watches for those who are very difficult to get up in similar situations. This watch is equipped with an LCD screen and all the necessary functions of a normal alarm clock. They are presented in 3 different versions: a cricket ball, a golf ball and a football ball. In turn, the ball for football comes in two colors: standard white and silver. There is also a stand with the clock in the set so that they do not roll off the table at night.

This watch is made specifically so that the alarm is turned off only when a sharp movement or throw. So now you can easily throw your alarm clock against the wall, and get a charge of cheerfulness for the whole day for only 10 pounds, and you can buy it on the Boys Stuff website.
