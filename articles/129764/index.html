AlterRussia Virtual Republic continues to evolve



The Internet platform for the development of laws "Virtual Republic of Alter Russia" continues to evolve. The new version of the portal represents a new mechanism for developing and adopting initiatives, which, theoretically, can be projected onto a real model of the state.

The principal difference between the new version is the refusal from the post of President, the revision of the functions of Ministers and the introduction of the posts of Experts. In the past, incompetent populists could be at the head of a virtual republic who are not well versed in issues, but managed to please the community. Having received high posts once, they turned off their activity on the portal, leaving their fellow citizens to fend for themselves.


Now populism will not pass! Experts and Ministers of AlterRussia are those who have shown more awareness, who have caused more trust from other citizens. If the Expert or the Minister stops working or loses trust, they automatically lose their status. They are replaced by other people.
