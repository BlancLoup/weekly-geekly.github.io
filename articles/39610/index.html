Antivirus: Results of tests lab AV-Test

Andreas Marks once again published the results of antivirus tests in the "wild" environment. The top five antiviruses in terms of malware definition looks like this:

1. AVK 2009 and Avira AntiVir - 99.8%
2. WebWasher - 99.7%
3. Ikarus and TrustPort - 99.5%
4. Avast! - 99.3%
5. AVK 2008 - 99/2%

Unfortunately, the test results for domestic anti-virus packages look disappointing. Kaspersky was ranked 10th with a score of 98.4%, Dr Web - 32, with 84.9% of the malware detected.

A full report on the test results can be found here - www.secureblog.info/articles/270.html
