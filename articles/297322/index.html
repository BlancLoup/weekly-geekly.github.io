Yandex.Money began working directly with MasterCard to issue its own plastic cards

Yandex.Money became the first payment service in Russia, which was able to work directly without intermediaries with the international payment system MasterCard. The company's license equalized Yandex.Money in the MasterCard system with seventy of the largest Russian banks.


MasterCard with PayPass technology issued by Tinkoff Credit Systems in 2013

Yandex.Money has received a license of Principal Principal Principal Member of the MasterCard system. This is the highest category of participation of banks in the international payment system, which indicates compliance with security standards, transparency of financial activities and high rates of return and liquidity. And this license gives the right to issue international cards.

According to Kommersant sources, in Russia only seventy of the largest banks in the country have such a license, while there are seven hundred banks on the market.

Yandex.Money has been involved in card products since 2012 in cooperation with Tinkoff Bank. The service has produced four hundred thousand plastic and ten million virtual cards. From February 2016, the service will issue only its own plastic cards.
The card balance will be equal to the Yandex.Koshelka balance. The company will be the first to issue World MasterCard cards offering various benefits, including discounts at restaurants, theaters, museums and shops.

Yandex.Money is 25% plus 1 ruble owned by Yandex, 75% minus one ruble by Sberbank. The company's net profit in 2014 was 98.8 million rubles. By the end of 2015, 25 million wallets were registered in the system, about 12 thousand new ones are created every day. Own cards will allow Yandex.Money users to save on currency conversion, since 30% of payments in the system fall on online payments, including for making purchases abroad .
