FSB explains the difference between consumer and spy devices


A few days ago, the State Duma in the first reading adopted amendments to the Criminal Code and the Code on Administrative Offenses , in paragraphs, which says about the definition of "spy" gadgets. Deputies consider the definition of such devices not very clear, so by the second reading they decided to finalize the documentation.

The amendments are being developed in order to avoid situations where buyers of GPS trackers or video glasses are prosecuted.

According to the clarification, a spy gadget can be called ‚Äúdevices, systems, complexes, devices, special tools and software for electronic computers and other electronic devices, regardless of their appearance, technical characteristics, and operating principles, which are intentionally given qualities and properties for providing the function of covert (secret, non-obvious) information acquisition or access to it (without the knowledge of its owner). "

The FSB received a comment that the same pen with a hidden camera itself is not a spy gadget. But if you use it for covert shooting, it will already be a violation: the shooting cannot be conducted without the consent of the person.

Apple and Qualcomm have settled a patent dispute




Apple and Qualcomm were able to resolve a $ 27 billion patent dispute . The conflict began in 2017 and lasted until the end of March 2019. Now the partners have entered into a license agreement for a period of six years, it entered into force on April 1 of the current year.

Now, Qualcomm will supply its chips for iPhones, and Apple will be able to easily sell devices in any country of the world. Earlier, the courts of China and Germany have banned the sale of Apple smartphones that violate patent agreements.
