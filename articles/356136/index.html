After several months of blocking, PornHub is again available to Russians. Now open access to the international version of the site. The site on the domain. Ru is still blocked. In order to get to the resource, the user must specify the date of his birth and accept responsibility for his actions on the site, as well as take responsibility for "compliance with social and social norms."

A spokesman for Roskomnadzor, Vadim Ampelonsky, said that the decision to release the lock was issued on April 7. It turns out that the site has again become available to users from Russia on the birthday of Runet. Still, such porn sites as Brazzers and YouPorn remain blocked on the territory of the Russian Federation.

PornHub blocking decisions were made on September 13 , YouPorn on September 14, 2016. Interestingly, in its time, Roskomnadzor even gave advice on arranging personal lives to users who were dissatisfied with the blocking of porn resources in Russia.

The decision to block this site was made on the basis of an application filed with the court by Ruslan Okhlopkov. Perhaps he is associated with "Antimaydan" and Roskomnadzor. Okhlopkov has been fighting for morality for several years. He also applied for other porn sites, including Pornolab.
