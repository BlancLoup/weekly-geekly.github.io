Wikipedia loves openstreetmap



In the past few months, several changes have been implemented on Wikipedia.
  • If you click on geographical coordinates in the English and Russian Wikipedia, a convenient page will open with links to various cartographic portals . The main links are Google Maps and OpenStreetMap . Previously, there was only Google Maps, but for a free project it was somehow strange.
  • In Russian Wikipedia, to the right of geographic coordinates, along with quick links to Google Maps and Yandex.Maps (optional), a link to OpenStreetMap has also appeared.
  • And in Russian Wikipedia as in German, Norwegian and French, the OpenStreetMap map with links to other Wikipedia articles was embedded directly on the page . To display the map, click on the "Show geographic map" link next to the geographic coordinates.
I hope that it will be even more convenient for you to use Wikipedia.

Wikipedia and OpenStreetMap are friendly projects and a collaboration program has been launched between them.

Oh yeah, and do not forget that anyone can contribute to the OSM card and improve it . And you can be sure that your work will not disappear and will not be assigned to any commercial company - OpenStreetMap data is distributed under the free and copyleft license CC-BY-SA 2.0 Generic .
