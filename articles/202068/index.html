Motorola introduced the "low-cost alternative to the flagship": the Moto G smartphone



Yes, today Google Motorola officially unveiled a new smartphone, Moto G , on its blog. In this case, the developers claim that the device is quite productive, works quickly, so that it is not very different in speed from the flagships of the mobile market.

As for the characteristics, they are quite interesting: a 4.5-inch display, with a resolution of 1280 by 720, a quad-core Snapdragon 400 processor, 1 GB of RAM. There are several wireless modules, including 3G, Wi-Fi. Installed and two cameras, front and rear (1.3 and 5 MP, respectively). Separately, it is worth noting that the company promised to release a modification of this smartphone with two SIM cards.

The price of the device is really small: for example, in the USA the model with 8 GB of internal memory will be sold at $ 179, and from 16 GB - at $ 199. As far as can be understood, it is about the price of the device without a contract. At the same time, residents of the United States will be able to buy this device directly on the Motorola.com website.
