Mobile phone based laboratory microscope

At Kickstarter , a project was launched to create a miniature Micro Phone Lens 150x lens that turns a mobile phone camera into a full-fledged microscope.





The author of the project is an American, Thomas Larson , who had previously launched a similar project . Micro Phone Lens 150x differs from its predecessor in that it can use 150-fold increase (against 15-fold in its predecessor). The visible resolution of the prototype is 2 microns, which is 10 times less than the thickness of a human hair. The commercial sample, in turn, the resolution will be 1.5‚Äì1.6 microns.

Thus, we can say that this device turns an ordinary phone into a good laboratory microscope. For comparison, the author cites pictures obtained with the help of his attachment and a professional microscope Motic AE2000 with a 2 megapixel nozzle Moticam, the cost of the whole structure is $ 2,723.


On the left in the picture is a picture with Micro Phone Lens 150x, on the right a microscope with a photo attachment.

The diameter of the lily is 6 mm, and the top is a rubber nozzle. To focus, it is enough to press the glass with the sample under consideration to the accessory.



It will work on phones with a camera 5MP and above.

Also, for higher quality, you will need a source of additional lighting, which will be bundled for an additional $ 39 , the lens itself will cost $ 29 .
