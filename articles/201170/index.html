Transformation of ideas in the development process of the project Meople

Be smart at what you like!

Like it or not, but to start any business you need this very idea. Even if it is not quite conscious at the very beginning, at the subconscious level. Usually the impetus for her appearance is the desire to do something convenient and useful, and in the case of a startup, also with your own brains and hands.

According to research, more than 2.8 billion accounts are registered on social networks around the world. 4 out of 5 Internet users check their sources 1-2 times a day. About 60% of them have more than 2 active accounts and 2-3 that visit from time to time. And it seems to us that users are limited in carrying out cross-network operations.

If you have accounts in two networks, you need to open two windows in the browser, or two applications on the device. And here, sooner or later, the question of convenience arises. Plus, there are more often visited networks and those in which our visit is not so frequent. But even there may appear information that is very important to you, I want to at least be aware of events.
So I wanted to make being in several social networks as convenient as possible.
And now the idea has been transformed into a project that allows you to accumulate and manage information received and transmitted from one social network to another. This implements the ability to operate information for us, as for users.

Our social networking aggregator Meople.Net is a handy application for social networking. The user receives input directly into his account without transferring data to a third-party resource and this already guarantees security at the usual level. The functionality of the features is determined by the social-interface API parameters; at present, it is practically no different from native applications. But beyond this, we have tried to supplement the user's capabilities with some unique functions.

Reshare - transfer news from one network to another, at your choice.

Cross-post - placing your information simultaneously in all tapes of networks to which you are connected or to any one of your choice.


Favorites - create a filter of priority sources of information

Matching - search your friends' accounts or corporate resources in all networks at once



We believe that the user should have the right to choose the most convenient way for him to manage his resources in social networks. And the Meople.Net aggregator is an information panel that accumulates information from various sources in a user-friendly form.
