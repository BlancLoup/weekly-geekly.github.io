New project Free-lance.Ru: photo without intermediaries

The Free-lance.Ru team has opened its third independent project 10mpx.ru . Like the others, it is entirely created by freelancers. At 10mpx.ru photographers will be able to sell their work without the mediation of agencies. In addition to photos, illustrations and collages are exhibited.

The mechanism of the service is as follows: the author uploads an image, assigns keywords to it and assigns a price. After moderation, the work is added to the database and is waiting for its buyer. The site has a statistics query, according to which photographers can judge the most popular themes of customers photos.

Speaking about the benefits for the potential buyer, the project creators note low prices and constant updating of the work base.
