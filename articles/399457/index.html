Russian FinTech Meetup # 2: Trading in the Era of Modern Digital Technologies

Trading systems are closely intertwined and develop together with fintech industry. The blockchain technology, machine learning and big data processing systems are improving both the approaches to analyzing the situation and the process of making deals on the exchange and over-the-counter markets.

On November 29, the Russian FinTech community will hold a second fintech-mitap dedicated to trends in trading at the center of DigitalOctober. Young trading solutions that can challenge industry giants gradually strengthen their positions on the market.

One of these projects in the Russian market is TRData , a platform for monitoring emerging markets, which can compete in its segment with Bloomberg and Thomson Reuters products due to a larger number of data providers and a lower connection cost.

In order to form effective trading strategies, market participants need to take into account the enormous amount of information. For a qualitative analysis of large data arrays, expertise is required, which in the field of trading is provided by independent consulting companies, such as Datalogia .

New technologies affect not only securities markets, but also foreign exchange markets. The border between fiat and cryptocurrency is gradually obliterated, and technological solutions of independent blockchain platforms gave impetus to the development of a cryptocurrency exchange infrastructure. Trade in tokens - digital units that can be tied to both fiat and cryptocurrency, opens up new prospects for participants of the exchange market. An example of such a service with Russian roots - Waves Platform . Blockchain technologies underlying cryptographic trading can change the usual industry processes by eliminating the intermediary link of brokers and analysts.

Program Russian FinTech Meetup # 2:

  • "Algo Revolution 2.0 - the evolution of approaches to the construction of algo-strategies." Fedor Pshirkov, Managing Partner of Datalogia
  • "The evolution of information and trading systems for professional participants, current trends in the Russian market." Pavel Fedorov, TRDATA
  • "Is cryptocurrency trading an alternative or harmonious addition to traditional stock exchange instruments?" Alexander Ivanov, Waves Platform

At the end of a series of speeches by speakers, there will be a discussion on the impact on trading of modern technologies, such as the blockchain, big data analysis systems and machine learning.

Time: November 29, 19:30
Location: Moscow, Bersenevskaya nab., 6, p. 3 Digital October. Conference hall. Follow the signs.

Participation is free. Registration - by reference .
