300 thousand.ua

The development of the Ukrainian segment of the Internet was marked by another event - the three-thousandth Ukrainian domain was registered.

The most popular and dynamic public domains are still com.ua, kiev.ua, org.ua and .ua. Each of the leaders has a specific, specific target audience. Thus, in the domains .ua and com.ua, registrants are legal entities (67 and 65%, respectively).

At the same time, in the kiev.ua domain, among registrants there are already a few more individuals than legal entities, and in the org.ua domain, the vast majority of registrants (82%) are individuals.

This is largely due to the difference in cost and terms of service for domain registration. Only three of these domains are located on 17,427 thousand unique name-servers and 36.966 thousand admin-c are administered.

In addition, the popularity of most regional domains has grown. Over the last year, the dp.ua and dnepropetrovsk.ua domains demonstrate a consistently high positive trend.

During the development of the .ua domain, the growth rate of registered domain names has significantly changed. Thus, it took almost 11 years for the Ukrainian domain to reach the first 100 thousand (1992-2003), the next 100 thousand were overcome by the domain in less than 3 years (2003-2006), and it took a little more than a year to overcome the third hundred. Positive growth rates are typical for the vast majority of domains in the .ua domain. So, not far off and half a million milestone.
