Comparative overview of Russian bookmarking services

Bookmarking services are sites where you can link to your favorite sites and that will be accessible to everyone. These services are also used to get traffic to the site. There are a lot of bookmarking services in runet, where you can safely add your site, but which one (s) is better?

The first three ( BeaverGoods , Memori , Mister-Wong ) is almost at the same level, breaking far forward from their competitors :) The services they have are about the same, when registering, all three services do not need to enter a captcha, but are asked to confirm the e-mail. Also, all three have a convenient tag cloud by which you can track the most popular topics for posting in them. And their rating (except the last one) is powerful enough.

The second three ( MyMesto , RuMarkz , RuCity ) is also at about the same level, and for ease of use lags significantly behind the leaders. On RuMarkz require to enter captcha. By the way, RuMarkz and RuCity are similar to each other like two drops of water ... Either these are projects of the same people, or this is one engine.

Well, the latest services ( MyScoop , 100 bookmarks ) ... You can safely send them to the basket and never open them :( The monstrous interface, the requirement to enter captcha, the lack of a tag cloud, the lack of any amenities ... They are not worth adding sites;)

The table shows that in the first three services you should add your sites, and you need to have a pumped up and active profile. Sites in My Place and RuCity can be easily upgraded (although there is no normal rating for these services, unlike the top three). In RuMarkz it is a bit more difficult to do (you will have to enter a captcha), but there is still a rating there :) Well, in the last two services, you can not even look.

I deliberately did not do a traffic analysis, since it mainly depends on the quality of the description, name, choice of tags, profile promotion, etc., which naturally does not depend on the bookmark service :) Even with MyScoop, you can have traffic, and with Beaver for example, not to get a single user at all ... Although, in principle, all other things being equal, the number of traffic will win, I think all the same leaders;)
