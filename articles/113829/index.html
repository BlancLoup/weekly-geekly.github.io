Yota offers free service internet access and new rates.

March 15, 2011 Yota introduces free service access to the Internet and new tariffs.

Connection to the Yota Day tariff will be suspended. The cost of a week of unlimited access to Yota will be 500 rubles, a month - 1,400 rubles. When paying for Yota services for six months in advance, the cost of access will be 5,400 rubles (that is, a month of access will, as before, cost 900 rubles). The cost of annual access will be 9000 rubles - that is, 750 rubles per month.

Simultaneously with the introduction of new tariffs, Yota will not take money for slow Internet access - comparable in quality to that of outdated technologies. Therefore, from March 15, 2011, all customers will receive free service access to the Internet at speeds up to 64 kilobits per second for two months after the end of the paid period. This speed is enough to use most of the information resources of the Internet, make calls via Skype or send mail.
