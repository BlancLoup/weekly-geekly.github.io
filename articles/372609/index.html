Tango tracks movement - this is the first way to transfer data to a smartphone. Just take tango for granted


Until now, the only way the smartphone could interact with the world was GPS technology. Think about when the device needs GPS. Usually the cards come first. It may seem that positioning is generally not necessary in most cases. But how would you use Fitbit, Periscope, Uber, or at least Tinder? Without this technology, many of our favorite applications and functions of smartphones would not work as intended, or were not implemented at all.

Tango will change our lives in the same way that GPS has transformed the way we communicate with the world using smartphones. The number of applications and usage scenarios will grow, and convenience will become increasingly apparent.

Tango's task is to create spatial information. This means that all objects and places in the world will be available directly from your smartphone, just like now you are making requests to Google. Ordered and wait for the car Uber? Tango through augmented reality will mark the car that is coming to you, and it will be easy to see in the stream.
