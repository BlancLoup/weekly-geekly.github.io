bb-mobile Techno 9.7 3G - iPad Air Clone Budget

It's no secret that Chinese brands are more and more trying to be like their more successful counterparts. As a rule, all attempts end on copying the appearance of devices of past generations. But bb-Mobile decided to make a knight's move and presented us a device that almost completely copies the design of the iPad Air, is inexpensive and has a budget filling.



This is not the first such experience for this manufacturer. For example, bb-Mobile Techno 7.85 3G is a clone of the previous generation iPad Mini. But there is also its own development, for example, bb-Mobile Techno 9.0 3G. All products are of good quality, as well as proven, classic lines. Tablets can not boast of high performance, but their capabilities are quite enough, and the price of all products is not very biting.


‚ñå TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS


bb-mobile Techno 9.7 3G is equipped with a 9.7-inch IPS-screen with a resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels. The bb-mobile Techno 9.7 3G tablet is based on a MediaTek MT8382 quad-core processor with a clock speed of 1.3 GHz, 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of internal flash memory, and a microSD memory card slot of up to 32 GB. The capacity of the built-in battery is 6,000 mAh.

The tablet has a set of wireless modules - Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS, 3G. The modem for cellular networks is designed not only for data transmission, but also for voice communication and SMS exchange. Among other features of the tablet, I note the camera at 0.3 and 5 megapixels, as well as Android version 4.2.2 Jelly Bean.
