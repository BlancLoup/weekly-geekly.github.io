LaCie introduced the first USB hard drive with USB Type-C connector


After the Apple MacBook was introduced with a USB Type-C connector, gadget manufacturers began to focus on this connector. For example, LaCie announced an update on the Porsche Design Mobile Drive hard drive lineup. Hard drives of this series will now be released with a new USB Type-C connector, but they also have USB 3.0 interface, which is much slower than its successor, USB 3.1.

LaCie did not report on the price of new models of portable hard drives, but existing models cost from 70 to 140 dollars depending on the volume. New LaCie hard drives with USB Type-C will come with an adapter cable that allows you to connect the drive to laptops with standard USB 2.0 and 3.0 connectors. The device will be released in late June
