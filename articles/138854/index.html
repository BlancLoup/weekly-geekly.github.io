LG launches 4-core smartphone



LG has surprised many competitors with its quickness: it was LG that recently announced its 4-core smartphone LG Optimus 4X HD. According to the manufacturers, this is the first smartphone in the world with such a processor. True, the announcement - this is not all, the sale of the device will begin only in the second quarter of this year. The mobile device, as far as can be judged, is really nothing: it has a powerful stuffing, a 4.7-inch display with a native resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels. The OS uses a new Android 4.0.

The announcement of the smartphone was timed specifically for the Mobile World Congress exhibition, and the company prepared six devices at once for this exhibition. As a platform in the LG Optimus 4X HD chip used Nvidia Tegra 3. As far as can be judged, in this system there is a fifth core, which is designed to save energy. The fifth core works only when it is necessary to perform simple tasks, while the entire power of the device is not involved.

As for the other specifications, there are LG developers here. what to be proud of. In addition to the display, there is also 1 GB of RAM, plus 16 GB of internal memory available to the user. There are two cameras, including one rear and one front camera. The rear camera comes with a resolution of 8 MP, and the front - 1.3. Naturally, such a device consumes a lot of energy, and a 2150 mAh battery is installed in the device.

The processor runs at 1.5 GHz, which is quite enough for a mobile device. Interestingly, the thickness of the device is not so great, as one might think - 8.9 mm. Since the smartphone is the flagship in the model range of smartphones from LG, then the body here is made of the most modern materials that can protect the device. Unfortunately, the cost of LG Optimus 4X HD, even approximate, is not reported.
