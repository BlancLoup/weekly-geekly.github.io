AngelHack: the second international hackathon in Moscow. Who has won?



Last weekend, July 11 and 12, 2015, the Russian stage of the world's largest hackathon, AngelHack Moscow, took place. The event brought together more than 200 programmers, designers and authors of ideas, who presented to the Russian and American experts 40 developed application prototypes from scratch in 5 nominations. The winner of the main nomination - the House Keeper team - will go to represent Russia in the final AngelHack, which will be held in San Francisco.

AngelHack is called the largest and most diversified global hackathon. One of the objectives of the event is to help the international technology community to build ties with Silicon Valley. And in Russia, in Moscow, this competition of minds is being held for the second time.

The partners of AngelHack Moscow 2015 were: IBM, KORUS Consulting CIS, Microsoft, Mail.Ru Group, Clusterpoint, Amazon, Branch. IBM as one of the tasks for developers proposed to create a web or mobile application that demonstrates the capabilities of the IBM Bluemix platform in full. The team that completed this task received a great prize.

Grand Prize


The main prize - a trip home to AngelHack - was won by the House Keeper team.



House Keeper is a project that manages appliances. It allows you to search for instructions by taking a picture of the barcode of the device, and also reminds you of the need, for example, to replace the filter in the washing machine and tell you how best to do it.

The project developers will take part in the global Demo Day along with the winners AngelHack from all participating countries. The winning team is also given the opportunity to participate in the HACKcelerator 12-week training program.
