List of reports and final registration ZFConf 2011



The main preparatory work on the conference is over. Reports are generated. Representatives of KnpLabs, Magento, JetBrains, Sphinx and other well-known companies are among the speakers.

The final registration is open!

If you have been pre-registered , then to complete the final registration you need to follow the special link sent to the e-mail. The information entered earlier will only need to be confirmed. If the link did not come to you, you can request it through a special form .

Please note that the location has changed ! The event will be held at last year's site - in the assembly hall of the LGTC (Lengiprotrans-Center), at the following address: Moskovsky pr., 143, Art. m. "Electrosila". Coffee breaks and flipchart discussions will take place in the lobby.

List of reports:

Flexible Zend Framework architecture of applications using Dependency Injection
Alexey Kachaev ( Cogniance , Senior PHP Developer)

Fighting for resources: Improving the performance of a Zend Framework application using phpDaemon, Varnish and ESI
Alexey Kachaev ( Cogniance , Senior PHP Developer)

Behavior Driven Development in PHP and Zend Framework

Division of Labor: Organizing a multitasking, distributed system in the Zend Framework using Job Queue
Alexander Gotgelf ( Magento , PHP developer)

How can the development environment help with writing an application on the Zend Framework?
Nikolay Matveyev ( JetBrains , developer)

Creating a REST-API for third-party developers and mobile devices with OAuth 1.0 authorization
Dmitry Chizhevsky ( Zen-mani , developer)

Thick model: The history of developing its own ORM
Mikhail Shamin ( Geometry , Lead Developer)

What is Sphinx, why is it needed at all and how to use it with PHP (from a simple index to a search cluster)
Vladimir Fedorkov ( Sphinx , Director of Professional Services)

The cost of participation in the event will be 1500 rubles. This amount includes: participation in the conference itself, coffee breaks (from an external catering company), a package of participant materials and access to professional video recordings of reports (after the event). There are many payment methods available .

Hurry up, the number of places is limited !
