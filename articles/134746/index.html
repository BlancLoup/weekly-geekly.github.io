Petersburg has identified the best Internet startups in 2011

December 2 in St. Petersburg on the investment forum ended the annual competition of Internet projects Web Ready .

More than 10 projects from St. Petersburg, Moscow, Novosibirsk, Perm, Yekaterinburg and Kazan became winners and prize-winners of the competition.

Statistics of applications submitted, project directions can be found here .

In the main nominations of the competition, the best ones were recognized:

- St. Petersburg project ResumUp - the creation of a visual summary and a light job search or candidate (nomination "Best Startup"; prize 500 thousand rubles)



- the Moscow project Shop Points is a mobile application that works as contextual advertising for the on-line world (nomination ‚ÄúThe best project at the seed stage‚Äù; a prize of 250 thousand rubles).

At the exhibition organized within the framework of the competition, 38 semi-finalists presented their projects, from which 12 participants of the final were selected.





This year the final of the Web Ready contest was held in the format of the Investment Forum. While the jury determined the best projects among the Web Ready finalists.
