Green 2-terabyte from WD

Western Digital will soon (perhaps before the end of this week) start selling the new Caviar GreenPower hard disk with a capacity of 2 TB.


The new model is characterized by an average search time of 8.9 ms, automatically controlled spindle speed from 5,400 to 7,200 revolutions per minute and the presence of 32 MB of cache.

Reducing the spindle speed to 5400 revolutions will reduce energy consumption. For this, in fact, HDD from WD and received the prefix "green".

The cost of the drive is $ 224.
