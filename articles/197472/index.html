7 useful books for the head



Hi, Habr!

We in RIK are a bit retrograde - we still read books and advise our students to do this. Let the bourgeois talk about clip thinking, but we believe that Russia, as it was the most reading country in the world, will remain that way :)

Today I want to share a selection of books that has been formed over the long years of work as the head of me and my partners. This is the basic library for anyone who wants to become a manager or is already such: managers, team leaders, art directors, department heads, and even generals. Personally, I reread something from this list a couple of times a year, I hope you will find it useful.

For me, this is a handbook for the past five years. It has a huge number of business analogies that still work today. I reread random quotes from this book almost every month.

Since this book is no longer one thousand years old, it can be considered its national wealth and without hesitation to publish a link to the free version .
