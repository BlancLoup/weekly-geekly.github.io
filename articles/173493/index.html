The Internet Protection League will announce threats to the freedom of the Web, following the example of Batman

This summer, the Internet Protection League (to be confused with the Internet Security League) began a project of emergency notification of threats to network freedom in a way that is typical for the network - the dissemination of information and organization symbols by automated means. In particular, an image of a black kitten with a psychedelic look was chosen as an attribute of the organization.

As it turned out, the activists' fantasy did not end there, but gradually turned into real life, and in a very effective way! The organization installed searchlights in San Francisco and New York and began to broadcast an image to the clouds or walls of a tall building - a symbol of their organization, just as Batman did in the Dark Knight movie series.
