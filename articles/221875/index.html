Mobile Promotion Market Research

In the modern world, most people cannot imagine themselves without the Internet and mobile technologies. Today, more than 2 billion people around the world regularly use the mobile Internet. In this regard, every day the popularity of mobile applications is increasing, and their number is steadily increasing. Today, the mobile application market is one of the most competitive. We decided to find out how the mobile market looks today?

In order to understand this, we will consider the following topics below:
- Current market condition
- what are the types of applications
- Basic prerequisites for market growth
- Market Obstacles
- Opinions of experts and the main theses



Types of applications

- Content Applications
They gained particular popularity among users of mobile applications. Watching movies, photos, listening to music, as well as reading books in digital format has become a convenient and affordable advantage for the happy owner of a mobile gadget. This is what reveals the demand for this segment of mobile applications.

- Business Applications
This type of mobile application has become a necessary tool for a large number of users, helping to simplify their office work. Today, business applications are preferable for investors, but sometimes there are some difficulties in moving business tasks to mobile devices.

- Mobile Games
Mobile games have a huge market demand. Developers are constantly creating new games or improving existing ones. Games attract most of the attention of a huge audience and become a familiar part of the lives of many people.

- Social networks
Every day, mobile social networks are becoming increasingly popular, winning fans around the world. Social network users are becoming more and more, because this is influenced by the increase in the number of smartphone users. Many people now have the opportunity to visit their pages more often from mobile devices.

- Sale of services
Ordering services via mobile applications is quite profitable for service providers. Order a taxi or food delivery through the application in your mobile phone is incredibly convenient, besides it allows you to save a lot of free time.

The volume of the mobile application market and its dynamics



In 2012, according to the research of J`son & Partners Consulting, the market of mobile applications in the world amounted to 7.83 billion dollars, and by 2016 it will reach 65.79 billion dollars. Thus, we see that the mobile application market is growing in all major regions. It should be noted that the rapid pace of such growth is due to the expansive nature of the market. Its volume in the world has increased twenty-five times from 2009 to 2012. It shows one of the highest growth rates for intelligent products and is quite interesting for investors. Only such a powerful player as Microsoft can afford to enter the market infrastructure.

J`son & Partners Consulting experts predict the development of the volume of this market along with the growth of the mobile technology market. Here it will be appropriate to recall the mobile game Angry Birds. This game has captured a huge part of the audience and identified a full-fledged media brand, initially creating it in mobile devices.

Basic prerequisites for market growth

- Increasing the impact of mobile social networks
At the end of 2012, the number of people using Facebook through their mobile phones was about six hundred million people. This social network has focused on the monetization of its mobile version, which became the main task of this company.

- Growth in the introduction of tablet computers
In 2012, the number of such devices in circulation worldwide amounted to 177.5 million units.

- Development of mobile banking and mobile payment systems.
The main global trends in the mobile application market are the growth in the number of mobile devices, tablet computers, the emergence of operating systems such as iOS, Android, Windows 8, the spread of mobile banking and mobile payment systems. For users, smartphones become much more convenient in solving everyday tasks than ordinary computers.

- The cost of communicating via mobile phones is reduced.
Smartphones are often used to complement the functions of other digital technology (when watching TV or using a computer).

- Geolocation.
Due to the growing number of smartphone users, there has been a significant increase in the popularity of GPS navigation, which is tied to Facebook Places, Foursquare, Twitter.

- The introduction of LTE and its impact on the mobile application market.
For this market, LTE will become a new growth driver. High-speed Internet connection allows you to apply more and more resource-intensive advertising technologies.

- E-commerce.
All the benefits of e-commerce become acceptable in a handheld device, and they add several useful features that simplify the process of searching for goods. Mobile commerce is changing the adopted for us application model, giving the opportunity to realize the full functionality of e-commerce in personal devices.

Barriers to the growth of the mobile application market



- Uninformed users.
Some users of mobile devices still do not use most of the useful functions of smartphones, and more often use it as a regular cell phone for making calls and sending SMS. It should be noted that a similar format of using digital services is characteristic of emerging markets, where the need of users for these service offers has not yet been identified.

- The difficulties of payment.
For most users of emerging markets, payment complexity is a major barrier to their development. People are not used to trust mobile systems, not wanting to leave their data in them. Thus, the main obstacle to the growth of monetization and the development of the market as a whole is still some difficulty of payments in mobile applications.

Overview of the mobile applications market in Russia



Experts believe that 2009 was the year of birth for the Russian market of mobile applications. According to experts, the market for mobile applications in 2013 reached one hundred and sixty million dollars, an increase of two hundred and fifty-six percent compared with 2011.
