Freelance School



In recent years, I have been cautious with Russian freelance exchanges. They reminded me more of a vanity fair. But the announcement of Freelance was not able to miss, at least out of curiosity, and posted several projects there. The first dozen or two reviews inspired a terrible melancholy and desire to escape. But literally on the threshold I stopped. After all, most of the people who responded (and this is confirmed later) have very little experience in this area. At school, no one taught them freelancing skills. This was not taught at the institute and this experience cannot be gathered at work.

Since my sense of citizen has been seriously aggravated lately, I decided to open a freelance school. This loud statement, I do not want to say that I am the exclusive carrier of knowledge in this area. I just hope that my initiative will be supported by everyone who can somehow give something to the younger generation of freelancing.

As for my experience, I executed my first order on the stock exchange in late spring 2005, and in the winter of 2006 I left work and never returned there. Nobody goes to the exchange, because everything suits. Therefore, all that you should aim at is creating a mutually beneficial and, as a result, mutually effective environment. Epilogue

Very often I get questions from people who doubt whether freelancing will work for them. Understand freelancing is not easy. Even now, living 300m from the sea and at the same time in the elite city center in a four-room luxury apartment with a fireplace, I often wonder if I acted correctly? After all, my head falls a thousand times more than any office worker. And every time I see a person in it ... who quit smoking, but sometimes looks askance at a cigarette, which is engaged in fitness, but looks askance for sweets. Temptations, including a more lazy lifestyle, will always be inherent to us. We are well aware of all the positive consequences of the regular use of a sports simulator, but we are afraid of the victims, to which we have to go. Here is the same choice. Freelance, like fitness, will give a lot of advantages, but will require the manifestation of strength.

Do not forget that the age of IT person is not long. Chiefs and leaders will not be everything, and when you are over 40, then twenty years will come on the heels. If you have been busy with your routine lately and your tools have become blunt, then the chances of being kept afloat will quickly melt. The freelancer is forced to keep his sword always sharpened.

I will add that many freelance problems that are often voiced, such as reduced communication, deterioration of fitness, etc. - not a lack of freelancing, but a consequence of the misuse of those unusual opportunities that it gives or personal inefficiency. His only "flaw" - in front of you will constantly be challenges that you have to answer.

In general, the term "freelancer" in itself is very capacious. It united freedom and warrior. You will have to become both. If you answer this question, consider that you already know if you want to be a freelancer. If you are not sure, just take a chance.
