Grasp - instructor on the shoulder

The designer from Copenhagen Grasp is a tool that allows your mentor or instructor to watch you from anywhere in the world and help you in any business, be it cooking or fishing. The gadget is mounted on the shoulder, a camera, microphone and laser pointer are built into the device.



For various reasons, including lack of discipline, some of us find it difficult to learn new skills on our own. Need help instructor and his tips. To give people the opportunity to work with an instructor at any distance, Akarsh Sanghi from the Copenhagen Institute of Interactive Design created Grasp.

Grasp is a gadget that is mounted on the shoulder with the help of two ribbons. It consists of a camera, a speaker, a microphone and a laser pointer. The instructor sees the work from the first person and directs it, pointing to the necessary objects with a beam and talking to the ward. The obvious drawback seems a bulky size.

The device is based on the Arduino microcontroller .
