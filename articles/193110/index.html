Studying the pyramids

As part of the project, he will have to write several modules for the new system, which should interact with the ridiculous monsters of the past. The government introduced an amnesty for inheritance over the last thousand years; now it was necessary to provide people with a clear interface that could get data from the mythical system of the RPF, which according to legends was subordinate only to 1C priests.

As a novice, Denis, of course, was thrown onto the dirtiest site. It was necessary to think over the visible code of the lower language and send it block-by-block to the elder in a neurovariable form. It was a rather tedious job, even ten-minute breaks every 20 seconds of work did not help.

This hell lasted all 3 months while Denis's higher education program continued. Now he has become a graduate student, now dozens of young talents have fallen into his hands, who could be sent to brain work.
