The history of the game "Snake" began a few years before the first mobile phones. To win, you had to get more points than your opponent, blocking his way to new goals (in the case of a multiplayer game) as the game progressed, or just beat the record set on the slot machine. In 1984, Gremlin Industries was forced to close, but the game Hustle began to gain momentum: first there was a port for computers TRS-80, then for Commodore PET and Apple II.
