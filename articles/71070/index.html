Starcode. Who are AJAX sites?

Hello! We are a team of programmers and we are turned on JavaScript and AJAX.

We can:
- create an AJAX website from scratch;
- act as a subcontractor and make the program part of the project;
- perform any layout, JavaScript and / or PHP task.

AJAX is our true friend. And in this article we will prove to you that AJAX sites are better than usual .



How are AJAX sites better than usual?



Speed. When clicking on the internal links of the site, the user from the server is not given a full web page (as in the usual approach), but only the content requested by him. This can significantly reduce the waiting time. In regions where fast Internet is not yet considered the norm, this can be a good advantage. For example, in Krasnodar .

Saving traffic. For those site users who have a mobile (or just not unlimited) Internet, it will be quite significant that starting from the second page viewed, the server will receive not 110Kb (the whole page), but, for example, 8Kb (the text of the article from missing pictures and styles).

Animation. Since the front end of our sites is completely controlled by javascript, we can add animation effects. The capabilities of our JavaScript framework (in combination with the power of jQuery) allow us to raise the presentation component of the web interface to a new level.

Perception. Our sites look holistic due to the fact that the asynchronous data exchange mechanism eliminates the effect of "blinking" pages. This is no longer a website consisting of static pages, it is a modern dynamic web application.

Originality. If your field of activity is related to IT (for example, if you are engaged in the design of applications for the iPhone ), the presence of a high-tech, attractive, not like other site will be a weighty argument in your favor in the eyes of the client.
