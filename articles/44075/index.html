Report from the past Startup Meeting in Novosibirsk

The second meeting on startups was held on November 2 in Novosibirsk, a city with a real Siberian soul.

In November, two more meetings will be held in Yekaterinburg and Samara. Who wants, still have the opportunity to sign up.

Now closer to the topic: guys, do not hesitate to openly discuss the projects that you are developing. If your idea is a secret for us, then this is a secret for your users, remember.

A few more people were discussing with me complex technologies that carry enormous potential. It was really felt that fire was burning in their eyes.

The further format of the meetings will still be more rigid, so that there are no ridiculous pauses. Initially, if you have something to say, get ready so that there is no one-way communication. If there is no project, think about the questions.
Further, in order to get rid of the heap of people and loud music, I suggest holding meetings at an earlier time, around 11 o'clock in the afternoon. Then, firstly, it will be easier to find us inside, secondly, no one will interfere with us, thirdly - they will bring food faster.
