Oakley airwave goggles



Since the street is already warm, lovers of coolness begin to look at the proposals of tour operators related to skiing, snow and mountains. In order to effectively engage in skiing, pay attention to the goggles Oakley Airwave Goggles.

This is a real technological complex, with many features and capabilities. In particular, it is:
  • Airwave provides clear, widescreen graphics;
  • The prism technology allows the displayed information to appear as if you are looking at a 14-inch display from a distance of one and a half meters;
  • Integrated GPS and barometric pressure measurements accurately show your speed when you slide down the slope;
  • Measure and track the distance, altitude and time in flight of your jumps;
  • Track your vertical walking, during the day and during the season;
  • A built-in altimeter tracks your height to the top and back;
  • Determine your location on the map of the resort, find trails for the run that you like;
  • Find and track friends who have Oakley Airwave glasses or an app on their smartphones;
  • Connect to your iPhone to view incoming calls and read text messages as they arrive;
  • Playlist mode puts you at the head of managing your music (headphones are required);
  • Switchlock technology allows you to easily and quickly change lenses, so you can use other Oakley lenses;


Oakley Airwave Goggles are excellent sports video glasses that can not only help you play sports, but also serve as a media combine or a guide.

Watch - Sigma PC 10.11 heart rate monitor



In order to monitor your heart rate during skiing (or cycling, fitness), you should use small devices that will not interfere with your activities. This, for example, a watch is the Sigma PC 10.11 heart rate monitor.



This watch is waterproof, the number of function buttons is minimal, the interface is simple. The heart rate monitor is quite accurate, the accuracy of its readings is comparable to the accuracy of ECG readings. To measure the pulse, you can use one of three modes: current, medium and maximum.

In addition, the clock pulsometer Sigma PC 10.11 is:
  • program manager with workouts;
  • pulse measurement modes;
  • calories burned;
  • fixing training time;
  • presence of a visual and audio indicator.

Sport stereo headphones AfterShokz Sportz M3 with microphone



AfterShokz Sportz M3 headphones with a microphone are a bone sound system that does not cover the user's ear shells. Instead, sound is transmitted through the bones of the skull directly into the inner ear.

In this case, the headphones are equipped with a microphone, which turns them into a comfortable stereo headset, which can be used to make telephone calls.

On a single charge, headphones can work for about 12 hours, which is enough for most crossings / flights. AfterShokz Sportz M3 headphones reproduce high-quality sound without distorting it. For the convenience of the user added function buttons.

Microradar Backtracker



A system called Backtracker will always warn the cyclist that a car is moving closer to the bike. In addition, the motorist will see a warning - if the car gets close to the bike, Backtracker will show a bright red light, visible from afar.



Backtracker system consists of two parts: the first is mounted on the steering wheel, and the second - on the rear wing.
