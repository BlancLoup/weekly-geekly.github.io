Ezviz S5 and S5 +: high-definition action cameras

The true companion of any traveler is a photo or video. The more pleasant was the moment when the video camera began to fit in your pocket, and the shooting was no worse than a regular camera. Moreover, such an action camera is not afraid of vibrations, falls and even diving to quite serious depths. The company Ezviz, which recently entered the Russian market with a whole complex of cameras for the house, could not leave aside lovers of extreme recreation. So, let's see how we will be pleased with the two models of action cameras Ezviz S5 and Ezviz S5 +.



Let's start with the differences. Externally, the camera is quite easy to distinguish by the red ring on the lens. Right - Ezviz S5 +.



Also, the differences relate to the battery compartment cover.



To understand the differences of cameras, it is better to look at the specifications. The first, and, perhaps, the main difference is the frame rate in 4K resolution. The Ezviz S5 model can shoot at 4K at a frequency of 15 frames per second, while the S5 + shoots full 30 frames per second with a resolution of 3840 x 2160 pixels. All Ezviz action cameras use aspherical lenses. Briefly I will give the features and differences of spherical and aspherical lenses:


  • spherical lens has a different shape and structure;
  • a lens with a spherical shape is distinguished by a geometric type of rotation;
  • an aspherical lens is shaped like a parabola or ellipse. It is this form that further enhances the transmission capacity of the light rays and the possibilities of the lens;
  • in an aspherical lens, the curvature of the surface changes with distance from the highest point, this helps to significantly reduce and correct the aberrations that occur when using conventional spherical lenses with high dioptries;
  • a change in the geometry of an aspherical lens increases the radius of view (its zone).

Detailed inspection


Let's start with an external inspection. The camera comes in a translucent tube.



In the upper part, the camera itself is mounted in a waterproof box. And in the bottom - the instruction with all the equipment. We will consider the bundling version of Ezviz S5 +.



The kit includes: a pair of velcro mounts, a kit for attaching the camera to the quadcopter hanger, a mount adapter, a spare aquabox door, a removable part for attaching to the velcro, a cap for an aquabox lens, a cap for a camera lens, a battery.


The manufacturer claims that aquabox can withstand depths of up to 40 meters. So far, no such test has been conducted, but the opening of the diving season is nearing and it will be possible to check for sure. For now we will examine boxing from all directions. It is equipped with a standard mount and has three buttons. What is nice, on and off is displayed on a separate button. She also switches camera modes.



It's time to get acquainted with the device. On the right side are two large buttons. They are also available in the aquabox: the power button and the settings menu. With a long press of the settings button, the Wi-Fi access point is turned on and you can connect to it from your smartphone.



At the top there is a button for recording and speaker. If you wish, you can watch and listen to the recording, although it is more convenient to do this, of course, on a computer screen.



On the left, under the plug, there are microSD, miniHDMI connectors and a microSD card slot. Maps up to 128 GB are supported, which should be enough for 5.5 hours of video at a maximum resolution of 4K.
