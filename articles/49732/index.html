Noise around Visual Studio 2010 and ReSharper

Some time ago, Microsoft announced Visual Studio 2010, its CTP version can even be downloaded (for example, from here ). But not about this, but about the rumors about the inclusion of ReSharper in the composition of VS 2010.

Immediately in several blogs there appeared surprised notes that, they say, everyone's favorite ReSharper will be included in Visual Studio 2010. There is no official information about this, but the conclusion was made on the basis of attentively looking at the page devoted to this new version. Here, someone James Kovacs even lists a screenshot of this page, where you can really see the ReSharper item in the studio menu (and the back picture is Resharper's Options window).
But the most curious thing is that on the official product page there are now completely different pictures, where Resharper, alas, no. It seems that when choosing screenshots someone screwed up hurried. :)

I have not had time to download and try the 2010th studio, so the question is to those who managed: how about?
