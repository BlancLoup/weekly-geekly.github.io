5 April startups


5 interesting startups from Startup-news.ru


  • Starmaker.ru
  • Points
  • SEUMKA.RU
  • A.nnotate
  • Loudcloud.fm



one.


Starmaker.ru is a new musical social network. In this social network, can register as talented artists who want to succeed in the field of show business, and their fans. The unusual feature of the project is that the starmaker gives a good chance to get into the show business a budding young talent, spin up and record their own album at the expense of fans.


2


Points - a service that will help you build the best route in Moscow and St. Petersburg, taking into account traffic jams. You can use the service either by sending SMS from the phone or by selecting points on the site, create a schedule by which messages with the route will arrive. The estimated time and the list of streets to be passed are indicated in the sent route.

Scheduled emails are now free. The cost of messages sent from the phone at the moment range from 4.17 rubles. up to 5.00 rub.

3


SEUMKA.RU - position checking service is designed to monitor the position of a site in search engines. Seumka checks on what position your site is located for a given search phrase. For clarity, Seumka builds reports in the form of a table or a graph, export to Excel is supported. Seumka can determine the position of the site in search engines Yandex, Google, Rambler, as well as Yandex UA and Google UA. Check the position of the site occurs daily. Determining the position of the site occurs to a depth of 200 positions.

four.



A.nnotate is a service that allows you to upload documents and add notes to them. Documents that can be in RTF, HTML, PDF, DOC, DOCX, xls, OpenOffice, PNG, GIF, JPG formats are converted to PDF format. You can easily add snapshots of sites and work with them in the same way as regular documents. A.nnotate provides the ability to publish documents online.


five.



Loudcloud.fm - web service for musicians and music lovers. Here you can find new music albums, find out information about concerts and new music groups. On the site LoudCloud.FM, groups and albums are created to place music - the thing for which fans / listeners visit the resource. Groups that do not post music on the site will be deleted. The service assumes that any music placed on the site should be available for download free of charge in full (without limiting the track length).
