New control without touching the screen

Once again, Japanese scientists from the University of Tokyo are surprised by their developments. A new kind of control allows you to capture the movement of your fingers using a high-speed camera (154 FPS). And do not use any additional devices, do not require any marks on the finger. The video shows an example of keyboard input, viewing photos and drawing in 3D space.
