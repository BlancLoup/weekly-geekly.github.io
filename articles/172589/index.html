What does the coming GALAXY cook for me?

Good day, Habr!

As you know, Samsung recently revealed cards about the announcement of the "fourth dimension of the Galaxy" (we have no confirmed data that the new flagship will be called Samsung GALAXY S IV, so we will not use it in the post): the presentation of the advanced smartphone will take place March 14 at Radio City Music Hall, New York City! The event will be broadcast live on Youtube. In the first-cell smartphone will be shown a day later - on March 15. In the meantime, we offer to plunge into the abyss of rumors, conjectures, fantasies and frank fakes, which the Internet is full of on the eve of the launch of the new device.



So, what did the yellow (and not so) pages come up with since the first news about the development of the new Samsung flagship?

Screen

It's no secret that Samsung Electronics is actively working on flexible displays. Prototypes of mobile devices ( ONCE and TWO ) based on these technologies were already presented in January this year at Mobile World Congress 2013. Of course, after this, rumors began to circulate that the company plans to use this technology in the new flagship GALAXY.

‚ÄúFlexible displays are great, they can be twisted, worn on the wrist - many possibilities, limited only by our imagination. Plus, the flexibility of the screen allows for a high degree of durability by using a plastic substrate, which is thinner, lighter and much more flexible than any LCD technology-based screens, ‚Äùsaid a Samsung spokesman in an interview with the BBC.

It is expected that the flexible OLED screen on the Fourth Galaxy will be equipped with Full HD-resolution (1920x1080) or even higher, and its diagonal will be as much as five inches (on the face of a violation of the Samsung GALAXY Note II niche). More modest and realistic expectations on the part of the screen are reduced to the usual LCD Full HD, Super AMOLED (RGB, like GALAXY Note II) and the screen diagonal is not more than GALAXY S III.

The Hi-Tech.mail.ru portal at the MWC 2013 exhibition came across a curious stand demonstrating the evolution of Super AMOLED-matrices. The 4.99-inch display with Full HD resolution and pixel density of 440 ppi became the extreme point of the development of Samsung technologies. It would be logical to assume that this is the same screen for the very same smartphone.

Specifications

In recent months, there has been no shortage of supposedly leaks in the specifications of the Fourth Galaxy: they have not announced any secret data about Samsung extracted from the camp, except, lazy.

According to the Korean site Ddaily, the Fourth Galaxy will carry a quad-core Exynos processor, built on the 28-nm standard. process. It will be smaller in size, and faster in performance than the processor hidden inside the Samsung GALAXY S III. New processors, along with an increase in speed, should take a focus on improving the efficiency of interaction between the cores. And this, in turn, means that the Fourth Galaxy will be able to quickly and simultaneously handle many tasks and work for a long time from an autonomous power source.

There are suggestions that the new smartphone will be equipped with WiFi 802.11ac technology, also known as 5G + WiFi. Roughly speaking, with such mobile Internet capabilities, web surfing speed can be equal to a wired connection. Another source, androidgeeks , claims the device has a Broadcom BCM4335 chip, which includes WiFi 802.11ac along with Bluetooth 4.0 and FM radio.

Some people are convinced that the Fourth Galaxy can be equipped with the new eight-core Samsung Exynos 5 Octa processor ... of course, if you can believe these screenshots of comparative benchmark tests . The model numbers SHV-E300S and GT-i9500 were previously mentioned as possible identifiers for the new GALAXY smartphone, and screenshots were taken in the popular benchmark application AnTuTu. They show that the processor under test has a speed of 1.8 GHz, which corresponds to the capabilities of the eight-core Samsung Exynos 5 processor. In addition to the higher power and speed of the new product, it is possible to offer a longer battery life.

There is another unconfirmed leak of benchmark results from the portal LazyTechGuys.com. If this is not a fake, then the results are really impressive.
