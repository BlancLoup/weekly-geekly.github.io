How to advertise in Google Adwords

My experience is advertising in Adwords. It will be useful rather for beginners, but perhaps experienced advertisers will find something new.

What should be done before starting work with Advords?
  • Add a card for payment on the Internet and find out its CVV-code in the bank. Put some amount on it, for example, $ 50.
  • Very useful to read the help on Adwords .

Registration in Advords and setting up payment information

Go to http://adwords.google.com and sign up.

If you already have a mailbox on Gmail, then it is better to specify it as a login. If not, enter any other valid email.

Set up billing information. Go to the Payment tab - Billing Settings . Choose the main payment method (for Ukraine, I choose postpay by credit card and write with the first name, surname, address as they are filled in at the bank when receiving the credit card, the expiration date of the card and its CVV code).
