IFA 2013: action cameras

This year, by chance, I was able to visit the exhibition IFA 2013 - the second largest thematic IT exhibition in Germany and one of the four largest (CES, CeBIT, Computex, IFA) in the world. There are a lot of impressions, even more photos and thoughts. And I decided to start the review of the new IFA 2013 with action cameras, to which my colleagues pay a lot of attention in their posts.



They started talking about action cameras relatively recently, but these devices are rapidly gaining popularity. High demand creates a corresponding offer: almost every company, one way or another connected with the release of IT-products, seeks to replenish the range of "sports" camera. And if a year or two ago, only the devices of American Woodman Labs, the very same GoPro, were heard, now there are many analogues on the market. IFA 2013 only witnessed the fact that action cameras are interesting not only for local B-brands (ie German), but also well-known global companies. With them, and start a review of new products.

ADIXXION is a branch of the JVC camcorder line, and this is easily guessed in appearance by the GC-XA1 and GC-XA2. I must say that there is nothing particularly outstanding in the characteristics of the GC-XA2. The protected case allows you to dive with a camera into water to a depth of 5 m, to work with it at temperatures up to -10 degrees Celsius. Also, protection against dust and drops from a height of 2 m is declared. Is this anyone you surprise now? It differs from its predecessor GC-XA2 with a better matrix (8 Mp BSI CMOS versus the usual 5 Mp CMOS), a different equivalent focal length (20.5 mm against 25.4 mm) and a different aperture (F / 2.4 against F / 2.8) of the lens. I note that the quality of the JVC GC-XA2 is simply excellent. The camera may not be too well cut, but it is stitched tightly together. The abundance of metal in the design inspires confidence that the JVC GC-XA2 is really thoroughly prepared for situations for which action cameras are mainly purchased.





European prices JVC GC-XA2 - about 300 euros.

Rollei

Rollei, a company well-known in Germany, performed very powerfully in the segment of action cameras. Actually, almost all of its exposition was devoted to these devices.



Rollei now has about ten different action cameras, different in shape, size and even color! A catalog of accessories for models 3S, 4S, 5S, 5S WiFi, S-50 WiFi has 26 items: from banal stickers 3M on the helmet and additional batteries to special mounting on the surfboard and magnetic pad! Neither Sony nor JVC can boast such a variety of fixtures. In general, I had the feeling that the products of second-tier companies have more functions and greater opportunities at naturally lower prices.











I will not list all the camera models shown by Rollei, I‚Äôll briefly discuss only a couple.

Very interesting specimens - Sunglasses Cam 100 and Cam 200. In fact, these are sunglasses with an integrated camera, aimed primarily at motorcyclists. "Points" are equipped with a 5-megapixel matrix, allow you to record Full-HD video and stereo sound. The handles have a microSD card slot and a battery that provides the device with up to an hour of autonomous work. They only upset unimportant viewing angles: only 63 degrees in the Cam 100 model and 135 degrees in the Cam 200 model.



Rollei Youngster is focused mainly on young people, who - and this is totally in Europe - prefer to move around the city on a bicycle. Characteristics average (720p video recording, viewing angle of 135 degrees, 2-inch screen, protective box included), but the price is low and there are several body colors.

In general, I must say that bright colors or interchangeable panels are a new trend in action cameras. The second-tier manufacturers are well aware of how great the demand is, and they are trying to offer the user maximum opportunities, including customization of appearance. For example, the new fall, Rollei S-50 WiFi, comes with three sockets: black, blue and yellow.



The S-50 WiFi model is the flagship Rollei action camera, which can be judged by the characteristics: a 14 megapixel matrix, a box that provides immersion to a depth of 60 m, a glass lens, and an extended operating temperature - from +60 to -20 degrees Celsius, viewing angle of 175 degrees, 2-inch screen, the ability to control from a smartphone via Wi-Fi. The set of this camera, in addition to replaceable, panels includes two batteries.
