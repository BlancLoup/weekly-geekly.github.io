Monitoring company presented data on Google+ users activity



Less than a month has passed since the opening of the social network from Google for everyone. I must say that this time the company acted somewhat differently than in the case of Google Wave, and everyone who wanted to, sooner or later, got the opportunity to register on the new social network. After the introduction of free registration, Google+ attendance went off scale, but now traffic has dropped by about 60%, Chitika experts say.

Google+ traffic measurement was carried out by the company before the introduction of free registration. According to analysts, immediately after the company announced free registration, Google+ traffic increased by 1200% last month, as a result of which the social sphere almost fell. But the developers of the corporation responded quickly, and nothing bad happened.

But it seems that not so many users registered in the new social network, there remain as active users. Many simply register for the sake of curiosity, and then forget about registration. Analysts believe that there are several reasons. And one of them - Google+ does not provide any very special features that are not found in other social networks. And if there is, then it all starts to be quickly introduced by competitors, and users see no reason to stay on Google+, where there are not so many users compared to other similar resources.

But in any case, not so much time has passed since the opening of the new social network so that you can confidently talk about success, or, conversely, about the failure of Google. Users continue to register, and the attendance of the social network is in any case higher than in any month of this summer - after all, open registration continues to attract all new users.
