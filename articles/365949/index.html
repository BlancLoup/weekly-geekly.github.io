Ilon Musk on competition and price for Tesla Model III



Tesla Motors executive director Ilon Musk does not think that Chevy Bolt should be considered a competitor to teslamobiles.

Bolt, an electric car from Chevrolet with a power reserve of about 320 km, will cost $ 30,000 after taking into account tax incentives. Tesla Model III, which is expected soon, is estimated at $ 35,000, without taking into account any state or federal benefits related to electric vehicles, Musk explains.

According to the Jalopnik publication, the total price of the Tesla Model III will be $ 27,500 ($ 7,500 less than the cost without taking into account tax benefits), which is not at all expensive for the advanced technologies that Tesla Motors is working on.
