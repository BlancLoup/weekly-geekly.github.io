CPU
The new family of AMD Fusion - dual-core processors, which are combined with a memory controller and integrated graphics core. In our review we are talking about the C-50 processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, with which the AMD Radeon HD 6250 chip is located. The L2 cache size is 512 KB.
The processor is a typical netbook version, the representative of the younger series in the new family. Tests of the Brazos platform show that the platform copes better with graphics than purely processor computing, but the netbook is well suited for ‚Äúoffice‚Äù work and web surfing.
