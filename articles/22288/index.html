Details about the 10 startups that were selected in the Y Combinator


Greetings to our readers. Today I want to talk about one event, which would have been lost in the depths of similar, if not even scandalous, fame and policies of its culprit. The Internet startup startup incubator Y Combinator professes an interesting and even in many ways original model of attraction and development - it offers a small initial funding and team relocation for the period of development for them in a room with all the infrastructure. Of course, ordinary business incubators work in a similar way (often), but in this variant it is a temporary move for a short time in order to probably be able to directly lead and control the company's work, and besides, this approach allows to unite the team not only the startup itself, and all united under the roof, and therefore achieve a synergistic effect at the design and development stage. And this is often much more important than even money and connections.

And so, the incubator selected ten projects that it will promote and market. What are these lucky projects? After all, there were a lot of applications, but then they managed to win in a fight for money, and then perhaps fame (and much more money). Let's take a closer look and analyze at the same time.
