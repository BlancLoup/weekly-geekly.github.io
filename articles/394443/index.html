Survey of online shoppers in Russia


A survey of online shoppers in Russia showed that the most convenient payment methods for Russians are cash and bank card payments at the time of delivery. Prepaid by credit card is the 2nd most popular, prepayment from an e-wallet is 3rd. However, these payment methods are considered convenient only by 19% and 10% of users, respectively.



Russians leave more money in domestic online stores than in foreign ones. The average purchase amount in Russia is about 6 thousand rubles. In Moscow - a little less than 8 thousand rubles.



Most often, Russian online stores bought small household appliances, clothes and shoes. Slightly less mobile phones, tablets, cosmetics, perfumes and electronics.



When choosing an online store, buyers are often guided by the price of the product, the experience of buying in this shop, the cost of delivery and the availability of a convenient way to pay for the goods.
