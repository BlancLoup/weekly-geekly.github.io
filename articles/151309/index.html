The Windows Store is open to everyone on Developer Day!

Today, on the official Developer Day, on the 256th day of the year, the Windows Store officially opened for all developers!
Starting today, developers from 120 countries can register and publish their applications for users in 200 countries!
Let me remind you that before this opportunity was only from key partners of Microsoft, companies and past Application Excellence Labs.

As previously announced, annual subscription for developer developers costs $ 49 per year , for companies - $ 99 per year.
In addition, under the DreamSpark program , students receive a year's subscription for free .
In addition, under the BizSpark program, startups receive a subscription for a year for free .
Paid MSDN subscribers also receive a one-year subscription for free.

Now you can go to your Personal Account , download free development tools , SDK and a large number of technical materials .
Let me remind you that before October 20, we launched a special application contest for Windows 8 with valuable prizes - ultrabooks and Nokia Lumia 920 smartphones.

If you have previously been registered as a Windows Phone developer, you can use the same account to simplify registration. Also the name of the developer in this case is already reserved for you.

Useful links for developing applications for Windows Store:
  • Application Certification Requirements - http://aka.ms/StoreReq
  • Correction of detected errors - http://aka.ms/StoreFix
  • Problem Solving - http://aka.ms/StoreSupport
  • Escalation to customer support - https://getsupport.microsoft.com

And do not forget, today on Developer Day we are launching Visual Studio 2012 !

Congratulations to all on your professional holiday and I wish to use this unique opportunity to win in the largest user ecosystem in the world!
