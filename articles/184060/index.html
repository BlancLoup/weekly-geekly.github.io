NeoQUEST-2013: the confrontation

The spring-summer season of hack quests continues with the second of the NeoQUEST series: NeoQUEST-2013 . NeoQUEST is an annual competition for specialists in the field of information security, whose participants on a competitive basis solve exciting practical problems related to the protection of modern information technologies.
As planned, on February 18, 2013, we completed the qualifying round, which lasted for two weeks. Now it's time to talk about the next stage of NeoQUEST-2013, during which the best participants in the qualifying round will compete for the main prize, which this year is a trip to one of the largest international conferences on information security in the autumn of 2013, to choose one.



The face-to-face tour NeoQUEST-2013 will take place on July 10, 2013, within the framework of the 22nd scientific and technical conference ‚ÄúMethods and technical means of ensuring information security‚Äù , the general sponsor of which is NeoBIT.
The quest scenario has become even more extreme than before: to get the coveted prize, the best of the participants admitted to the full-time stage will have to escape from prison. All they have is 8 hours to avoid being electrocuted, and the whole range of their information security skills. As in the past year, we will try to present to the participants in an unusual way the tasks related to the security of non-standard devices, cloud computing, virtualization, cryptography, and other most pressing problems of cybersecurity. And also something else that will come as a surprise to most participants.

The main quest will be accompanied by many interesting events. While the participants are busy escaping, everyone (and you too!) Will be able not only to follow the competition in real time, but also to demonstrate their high level of ITsec IQ. Guests NeoQUEST'a waiting for:
  • several multidirectional contests: you will be able to compete for prizes that will be useful on the farm for anyone who cares about their cybersecurity;
  • reports on how to write a security hypervisor, and how this hypervisor can be used by an attacker; how to detect a virtualized system; specific scenarios of attacks on Android devices, websites and large-scale information systems will be considered;
  • the ability to apply newly acquired defensive skills at our stands with the necessary infrastructure.

As it has been said many times, the modern world appreciates the ability to solve problems that have suddenly arisen, it requires to quickly understand new technologies and make decisions instantly. NeoQUEST will allow you to find out if you are able to accept this challenge, if you want to increase and use your knowledge. If you are ready, we will be waiting for you on July 10, 2013 at 10 o'clock in the conference hall of the New Peterhof Hotel. We remind you that admission is free for all comers, but preliminary registration is required on the website neoquest.ru . For the most fruitful work and the opportunity to take part in competitions, it is recommended to have a laptop or a replacement device with you .
