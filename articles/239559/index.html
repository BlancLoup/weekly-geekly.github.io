IBM invites to the conference "Infrastructure 2014"



IBM regularly holds conferences and seminars on various topics, technologies, and areas of work. October 23 IBM invites everyone to the conference "Infrastructure 2014".

This is a major event, at which prominent corporation experts will talk about interesting announcements of this fall, as well as share relevant news. In particular, conference participants will learn how new items from IBM, for example, Power Systems, System Storage and System z, can be used to create the optimal infrastructure for any company. High-End POWER8 server and more will also be announced.

The conference will feature world-class experts, heads of various areas of IBM:

Satya Sharma

CTO of Power Systems IBM USA
