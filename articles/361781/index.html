Sloboda: discovery and future plans

The past few weeks, life in the Sloboda has been constantly boiling and seething. We finished repairs, conducted two start-up schools in a row at once and organized the grand opening of our center.




In full compliance with the 80/20 rule, 20% of the improvements gave 80% of the site impressions, but they also required - well, if not 80% of the effort and time, then definitely not less than 60%. For example, noise insulation foam on the ceiling and walls has dramatically improved the acoustics of our rooms and gave the interior a new look. Curtains began to create the necessary twilight for presentations on the projector. The signboard made our building more visible. The crushed stone in the territory allowed to place on the street several recreation areas and smoking areas. All this was not 2 weeks ago.

We built, built ...



Despite the minor difficulties, we did not pull and began to host major events. The first was the startup school "Sprint" , which was organized by IIDF. For two days, September 19-20, theoretical and practical classes were held on our site for those who want to start their own business on the Internet or find an idea for a project.





Two weeks later, on October 3-5, we received participants from another school: the IT schools of the Crimean Federal District . It was intended for those who want to run their IT business, and represented three days of practical training and group work. The participants discussed a variety of topics, from the most basic (how the innovation ecosystem of Russia is organized) to more specific (how to look for and evaluate business ideas, what a ‚Äúfrugal startup‚Äù is and how to conduct an effective presentation).





To this school, we timed the long-awaited event - the grand opening of the Sloboda. But we will not write about him, because everything has already been written here .



... and finally built



The venue we have got together combines coworking, a conference area and office space.

Coworking is designed for 70 residents who are provided with jobs and all the necessary infrastructure. All residents have unlimited access to high-speed Internet , personal workspace (fixed or floating, depending on the tariff), the right to use office equipment. Plus, they can attend most events for free, use the kitchen (there is always tea, coffee, water, fruit and cookies), a library, car parking and bicycle parking. For an additional fee, we will provide a meeting room and organize an event.

The conference area is two rooms, large and small, which we rent for a variety of events. Of course, as a center for technology entrepreneurship, we are more inclined to host technology and business conferences, master classes, schools, and other business and educational events. But not only: we will be happy to host the events of any other directions. By additional agreement, we will help with the placement of participants in the event, catering, additional equipment, stationery and even blankets.



Office space is 3 offices on the second floor of our building, which can be removed entirely and put a start-up team or a local office / department of a large company there. Thus, you will not have to look for a room on your own, the office will be cheaper than a regular office, and we will take over the entire infrastructure. In addition, with the necessary isolation and privacy you are at the same time remaining in the thick of things and can take part in our events.
