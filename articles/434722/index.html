The next aspect is the number of technical gadgets needed. Among the triathletes known formula: any gadget should be N + 1 piece, where N is such that N + 1 <M, and M is the number of pieces of equipment at which you are expelled from the house. In swimming, in principle, everything is quite sparing. We need a pool for training, swimming trunks, goggles, balls, flippers, snorkel, and wetsuit.
