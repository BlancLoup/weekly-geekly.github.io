We invite to the webinars for beginners Android programmers on Delphi

Friends!

We listened to your wishes and requests and decided to launch a series of webinars, but for the very beginner Android programmers in Delphi . We will start with basic things: where to start, how to set up, how to create your first Android application, main components, properties, interface design, common mistakes of novice programmers, etc.

We plan to conduct webinars every 2nd and 4th Friday of the month at 4 pm Moscow time. Of course, if this time does not coincide with my business trip or other event, about which you will be notified in advance.
Registration for the first four webinars is already open.
