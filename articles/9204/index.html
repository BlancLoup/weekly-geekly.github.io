AP starts to fight for their rights

AP news agency announced increased efforts and new measures to protect their copyright on the Internet. It is not excluded that additional posts will be introduced in which agency assistants who control the information space will work.

The AP has entered into an agreement with Attributor, which will track where agency materials appear. Currently, the Attributor technology allows indexing about 13 billion web pages.

Initially, under the scrutiny of the "supervisors" will be materials that have a high interest among users.
