Who wants to be a millionaire: 7 ways to get rich without creating a company

Millionaires are usually associated with businessmen, owners of their own companies, and startupers. In Russia, however, some segments of the population still consider businessmen to be thieves, but the conversation is not about that now. Millionaires can be outstanding politicians, actors, musicians, athletes and other "showmen."

However, the discussion below will focus on how you can make a big fortune with mere mortals, as they say, in your free time. If you do not want to create a company, do not want to be known outside the online and put your life on public display, then the following ways to enrich you will be interested.

The publication Business Insider took care of your well-being and told the world about seven such ways.

Personal blog


In the middle of 2013, Scott DeLong launched the personal blog ViralNova on each page placed several advertisements Google ad. After 8 months, his annual revenue was estimated at more than $ 1 million. Further, her estimate ranged between $ 1.2 and $ 4.8 million, depending on the level of traffic on the site. DeLong achieved this by working alone, without third-party investments.

In a blog, he published various stories with catchy headlines that caused a resonance and immediately fell on Facebook. So, the number of monthly blog visitors has grown to 100 million.

In 2015, DeLong sold its website to Zealot Networks for $ 100 million.

A few years ago, in 2011, Amanda Hawking became the best-selling writer in the Amazon Kindle store. She was an independent author and had no contract with any publisher, so she got 70% of the sales of her books. About 100 thousand copies were bought from her monthly for $ 1- $ 3. As a result, the writer earned several million dollars.

Monetizing your own YouTube channel


Making money on YouTube is not easy. Even the "stars" with a million subscribers sometimes barely have enough money to make ends meet. But if you manage to consistently produce really outstanding content and acquire a multimillion army of fans, consider that the job is done.

One of the most successful figures in this regard, PewDiePie (his real name is Felix Chelberg), earned $ 12 million on YouTube. Many other such personalities earn $ 1 million a year from advertising. For example, a 9-year-old boy who does toy video reviews has a million subscribers and a billion views.

Crowdfunding


Kickstarter and Indiegogo - crowdfunding platforms that allow you to raise money to implement their ideas. If the community likes the idea, then there is a chance to collect an impressive amount.

Many people repeatedly collected more than $ 1 million in crowdfunding. One of the most successful crowdfunding campaigns is a fundraising to create a Pebble Watch . Developers received $ 10 million in a few weeks.

Now, Pebble watches compete with such smart watch manufacturers as Samsung and Apple .

Of course, the collected funds can not then be put in his pocket. Recently, crowdfunding platforms have paid particular attention to controlling the targeted use of funds and the fulfillment of other promises made by the project authors to backers.

Curriculum sale


In 2008, kindergarten teacher Dianna Jump started selling her own curriculum to other teachers through the TeachersPayTeachers website. She made 99 training programs and sold them for $ 8.

In 2012, its revenues exceeded $ 1 million. Every month she received $ 100 thousand from her small business. This amount was almost twice her salary in kindergarten.
