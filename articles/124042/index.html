Conference #OSCON on the nose!


The OSCON Conference (Open Source Convention) is, if not the most, then one of the largest IT conferences in the world, Open-Source, where more than two thousand leading developers and leaders of the IT industry gather in July each year.

This year's conference will be held from July 25th to 29th. And we once again go there, and this time we will conduct the session in 40 minutes. The exact date and time of our presentation is as follows:
  • Date: July 28th, 2011
  • Location: Oregon Convention Center, Portland, Oregon.
  • Hall: E142
  • Time: 13:40.
  • Language: English (questions can be asked in Russian).

The topic of conversation


This year we will talk about how to create persistent, highly-accessible web services using technology CUBRID HA (High Availability). Therefore, the presentation will mainly take place on the high availability of CUBRID.

Who should attend?



If you are working on a service that brings or will bring you real income, it will be useful for you to come and listen to our presentation, since 100% availability of your service and distributed load balancing are most important for you, and to make it all easy to configure, maintain and also free. Thus, in this presentation you will learn why and how CUBRID HA guarantees the eternity of your service.
