Australian government promised to abolish cryptocurrency tax



The Australian government announced a decision to exclude Bitcoin and other digital currencies from the list of objects that are levied on goods and services tax (GST). The announcement also describes other programs, the implementation of which will give Australia the opportunity to become a leader in the field of financial technologies of the new millennium.

In addition to the abolition of the tax, the government promises to support start-ups operating in the field of financial technology. One of the advantages for potential startups and investors, the government calls the work to provide a launching pad for business expansion in Asia. It is planned that Australia will become the market leader in technology and investment in Asia by 2017. It is worth noting that 55% of those entering the country for permanent residence are immigrants from China .
