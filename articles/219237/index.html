Navigator campus travels to Volgograd, Taganrog, Krasnodar, Yaroslavl, St. Petersburg to communicate with the iron workers

Navigator campus goes to one of the stages of Skolkovo Startup Tour . Starting on Monday, I ( Vasil Zakiev ) and Denis Kislukhin will spend one day in Volgograd, Taganrog, Krasnodar, Yaroslavl, St. Petersburg. Who wants to meet - write VKontakte, to the post office or SMS +79376151828 (there will be many meetings, I can not always pick up the phone).

Why meet with us?

  1. We can see and help in the development of almost any hardware project, especially in the field of robotics, 3D printing, smart devices (including smart houses) and clothing electronics. Denis and I are two IT startups, retraining in zhelezvyachnik-entrepreneurs and builders of private hardware technology parks (to which the Prime Minister of Russia comes a month after the opening). We have our own angel investment fund with another partner, a laboratory for prototyping electronics and a lot of engineers, entrepreneurs, investors who are ready to form teams with people who are close to them in spirit.
  2. We can advise and help with the discovery / development of hackspaces, fab labs, CMIITs. We will be happy to talk about the possibility of opening a private technopark or creative space in your city. This area relates to our strategy for creating a global network of infrastructure sites with an over-geographic community of engineers and entrepreneurs. Our goal is to achieve such an organizational and technological level so that the community of engineers created by us can implement projects of the highest level, such as creating a private space program. We believe that Russia and all of humanity need this.

We also have a smart suggestion for event organizers who want to gather around the hardworking community around themselves / their organization. Who wants to do this - let's meet.
