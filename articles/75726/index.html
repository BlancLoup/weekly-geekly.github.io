PDC second day, a wave of announcements

the phenomenon of Internet Explorer 9, office 2010 beta has been released, Silverlight 4 has broken Flash, each with a laptop



The second day of the PDC conference passed. This day brought a lot of interesting announcements, news and technical details. First things first and foremost impressed. Many things have not yet been tested, something is known only from conference materials. But I will try to put everything together.

Internet Explorer 9


So, Microsoft shared their plans for IE9 and even showed it in action. In short, the news is:
  • HTML 5 and CSS3 support
  • CSS3 selectors are now 99% supported by test results.
  • JavaScript speed is already at Firefox 3.5 level
  • ACID3 compared to IE8 has grown and now = 32 points, they will cut further
  • hardware acceleration of text display using Direct2D, demonstrations convincingly showed its advantage
  • border-radius is now
Accelerated, with demanded chips and still instantly starting in Windows, IE9 can potentially significantly press FF. Hardware acceleration of graphics and text is a fat plus. Direct2D also offers significantly better font smoothing. But in general, there is nothing to look at. Assemblies are not available when they are available - unknown. Now there are several videos about IE9 and an article about improving it.

Silverlight 4


The demonstration of Silverlight 4 caused a wild rush. It seems that everything that can only be imagined was immediately added to it. Enumeration of features is the topic of a separate article, here I will only write about what I remember:

  • support for microphone and video cameras, with simultaneous shader effects
  • elevated privileges, full access to the system, access to COM objects
  • HTML rendering in applications, from which you can render inside Flash and even break it into puzzles, as was done at one of the presentations
  • support for scrolling out of the box and right-click menu
  • all kinds of improvements in media, support for DRM-content in x264
  • notifications, in one of the examples, the speaker connected the camera via USB and SL; the software determined this and offered to import the pictures, which he did well
  • copying and pasting objects, such as tables from SL to Excel

SharePoint 2010


When everyone ran away to download Silverlight materials, a speaker appeared on the scene with a report on SharePoint. SharePoint, who does not know, is an extremely successful commercial product. By profitability for Microsoft among server solutions, it comes in second place after Windows Server. A million licenses sold, 100,000 people in the world are developing applications on SharePoint.

With the release of SharePoint 2010, everything will change. It will be much more rosy. Disadvantages removed, the growth will be avalanche. There are no analogues to the system in terms of convenience, integration with Microsoft-stack, availability of development tools, integration with .NET-environment and other advantages. SharePoint is already a de facto standard, like Exchange, and there is also a significant improvement. Thousands of implementation companies are already raising money on the platform. The number of SharePoint developers is expected to grow to 1 million within two to three years after the release of SharePoint 2010.
