Three ASUS Waveface Concept Gadgets

In addition to all sorts of working devices, ASUS has brought to the exhibition CES 2010, the opening of which will take place tomorrow, several of its new concepts. At least three of them are combined in the Waveface series and symbolize simplicity of execution and exceptional functionality, complete with a convenient user interface.





Device Waveface Light - this is a very unusual notebook, the body of which can bend at unimaginable angles and not break. The display in the Waveface Light, of course, is also flexible, but this is no longer fiction - these screens have existed for a couple of years and are actively developing. The principal feature of this laptop is that if you fully open it, you get a full-fledged tablet computer, since its keyboard is actually another touch screen.



Concept number two, ASUS Waveface Casa - this is not quite clear device. On the one hand, it is very similar to a widescreen display, which it is, but at the same time, the developers report that it can work as a home media center.



The third concept from ASUS is called Waveface Ultra. Judging by the design, it was developed specifically for the beautiful half of humanity. It looks like a bracelet, but is a communicator, controlled by gestures. All three prototypes may well go into mass production if ASUS makes the appropriate effort.
