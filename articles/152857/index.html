Incubator live (under the lens of the ip-camera). And how we did it ...


For more than 2 weeks already (a bookmark was made on September 14) in our homemade incubator there are five chicken eggs. These eggs have names. Their hatching ( if the experience is successful ) can be observed online . According to our calculations, about 3-4 days are left before hatching, which will be held as amended by the GTV channel.

Online


An ip-camera is watching the incubator, the broadcast of which can be seen on the website hi-testing.ru



For all the news about what is happening in the incubator, you can follow Vkontakte or Facebook . And according to the results of the experiment, a video will be posted on youtube. Those wishing to subscribe!



Incubator Requirements



Over the entire incubation period, the temperature inside the incubator should change. At the beginning of the bookmark, the temperature should be 38.2 C. After 4-5 days, the temperature should be reduced to 37.5 C. On day 19, the temperature should be 37.1-37.2 C.

Humidity regulates the heat transfer eggs. On the advice of an expert, Evgeny Bazarkin, we put two containers with water inside the incubator.

The eggs laid in the incubator must be turned 180 degrees so that they warm up evenly and the fruit develops normally. The number of coups - 5-10 times a day.



Incubator making



It took us 3 days to manufacture this incubator. During this time we made the insulation box itself, placed the mechanism for turning the eggs, connected the lamp to warm the air and connected everything to the controller.








When the incubator itself was ready, we connected all the electrics to the controller and introduced a program that would fulfill the incubation conditions (maintaining the set temperatures and turning the eggs 8 times a day).

Eggs


The embryo begins its development with a bundle of capillaries on the yolk, which grow in squirrels. With their help, the embryo breathes. In two weeks the egg is completely penetrated by the vessels.

Stages of development

How the embryo breathes and feeds inside the egg at different stages of development:
  1. 30-36 hours The fetus feeds and breathes due to organic substances dissolved inside the egg;
  2. 2-6 days . Gradually, the embryo dissolves a network of blood capillaries inside the egg. Through them, he gets food and oxygen from the yolk sac.
  3. 6-10 day . The embryo feeds through the yolk sac, through it and a special embryonic organ - allantois receives oxygen.
  4. 10-16 days . The embryo breathes through the allantois, feeds through the allantois and the yolk sac.
  5. 16-19 days . The future chick receives oxygen through the allantois and already feeds only from the yolk sac.
  6. 20-21 days . A couple of days before hatching, the chick already breathes air from the egg's air chamber and feeds from the yolk sac.
