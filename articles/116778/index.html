St. Petersburg Startup Weekend - business in exchange for technology

On April 15-17 in St. Petersburg we will hold the second St. Petersburg Startup Weekend .
And we have great news - now you can come to the Weekend not only with a ready-made idea. If you have technology, but you have no idea what to do with it - our experts will figure out how to use it and implement it in your own project.

Now on Ukande we are waiting for participants with thoughtful ideas, with bare technological solutions, with applications for social networks and with prototypes of services.

What awaits you:
  • The best experts of the country will be bracing to help you capitalize on your ideas.
  • Ekaterina Skorobogatova will give a lecture and with specific examples she will analyze the features of creating, promoting and using applications for Facebook
  • Arkady Moreynis will offer investments to the most interesting projects on behalf of Glavstart .
  • And of course - cookies!
Schedule.

Friday
17.00 - registration of participants and projects
18.00 - opening remarks
18.15 - project presentations
20.00 - announcement of the program and format of work

Saturday
10.30 - gathering participants, registering representatives of projects participating in speed-networking with experts
11.00 - presentation of experts
11.15 - speed-networking with experts
13.00 - personal expert advice, teamwork
18.00 - Ekaterina Skorobogatova (Facebook) seminar on the integration of your projects with the Facebook platform
20.30 - summing up

Sunday
12.00 - lecture by Ekaterina Skorobogatova (Facebook) about the development and promotion of non-gaming social applications
13.00 - personal consultations with experts and preparation of final presentations
16.00 - identification of candidates for mentors, collection of final presentations
18.00 - final presentations
19.30 - summing up, the definition of projects that Glavstart will offer its investments

Participation is free. Registration
To participate with your project, technology or application, you need to communicate your desire to the email idea@glavstart.ru . We accept applications until April 13th .

Questions can be asked by phone +7 (915) 338-52-71 and by mail info@glavstart.ru
See you in St. Petersburg! :)
