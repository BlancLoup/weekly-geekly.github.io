Informal Club Outreach Event DCExpedition for Data Center Managers



We know that Habru is read by professionals from all areas of IT, including those who directly manage the design, construction and operation of data centers.

Therefore, we decided to write in our blog that, together with Mastertel, we are creating the Data Center Expedition Club, which will allow like-minded people in the data center industry to get together and travel around the world, combining leisure and their professional development.

The first event of the series will be a high-tech expedition to the Vladimir region, on the river Lukh. Rafting on boats will be accompanied not only by qualified instructors, but also by unmanned drones fixing our adventures. The parking areas will have recreation areas with a projector and acoustic system, where interesting thematic lectures and discussions will take place in the evening.

In addition to the business part, all participants of the event are expected to have such attributes of outdoor recreation as rafting on the Luh River, numerous recreation sites with beautiful landscapes, fishing, songs around the fire and, of course, mosquitoes - in short, everything without which a full-fledged wild rest is impossible.

As a result of the event at the auto-party, all participants will be presented with a film about the past event.

We will be very happy if you join as a member of our small Tsodovo community!

Registration for the event is open until July 18, so if you want to go camping, then you should hurry!

To get acquainted with the detailed program of the event and register, go to dcexpedition.com
