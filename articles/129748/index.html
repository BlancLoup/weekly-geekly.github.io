Evernote for iOS Update: Major Improvements and iOS 5 Support

In today's Evernote for iOS update (version 4.1.2), a number of things have been implemented that you often asked us about. We improved the work with the text, added the ability to create task flags, made more functional teamwork and much more. Details on these innovations below.

Improved formatting


Our past iOS update was one of the most significant, thanks in part to the addition of a feature like editing notes with formatting. Today we have improved this functionality in several ways:
  • Improved format simplification feature. When you try to edit a complex note with elements that cannot be edited, Evernote offers you to simplify the text, that is, to keep the formatting that cannot be edited and to clean the rest (tables, fonts and CSS styles). In today's update, this functionality has been significantly reworked, and now you will receive a simplified version as close as possible to the original, including saved images, files, and encrypted items.
  • More correct display of line breaks. Now, when editing notes or viewing records created on other platforms, the padding between lines will correspond to what you see on other platforms.
  • Display of encrypted fragments. If you decide to view the encrypted fragment, its contents will be displayed right inside the note, as on Evernote ‚Äúlarge‚Äù clients.

Editing notes in shared notebooks


As you remember, premium account holders can give other users permission to edit their notebooks, even if they are not premium subscribers. Now you can edit other people's notes not only from your computer, but also from your iPhone or iPad. Just open someone else's notebook on your mobile device and edit an existing note or create a new one. After synchronization, all users who have access to this note will be able to see the changes made.

Checkboxes


Now you have the ability to create task flags on your iOS device. Keep a list of to-do items, purchases and anything else. To do this, just need to click on the appropriate button in the editor notes.


iOS 5


This version supports the new version of iOS 5. Very soon you will see many new features and improvements made possible by this new operating system.

Much work


In this update, many significant improvements have been implemented, but we have even more interesting things in development. And please tell us about your impressions in the comments. This is very important for our work.
