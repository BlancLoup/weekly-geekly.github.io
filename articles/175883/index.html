Facebook Game On in Moscow on April 15

Facebook Game On will take place on Monday, April 15, at Digital October.





This is a unique opportunity to meet with the entire EMEA Gaming Facebook team, learn everything about the Facebook gaming platform, how to create successful games on Facebook.com and the mobile version, as well as how to promote your games and reach a large audience.

Every month, more than 250 million people around the world play games on Facebook (almost every fifth of daily users!). More than 200 Facebook games have over a million players per month. And of the 400 most profitable iOS apps, 55% are integrated with Facebook.

The participants of the event will be the head of Facebook EMEA Gaming, Julien Cordonho. We will also hear speeches from companies such as Game Insight, Crazy Panda and Social Quantum. They will tell you about the successful experience of creating Candy Crush, Megapolis and Slotomania games, which have become the most profitable on Facebook and the mobile version.

Since Facebook last held an event for developers in Moscow, several companies have already started, starting from scratch, have achieved great success with the help of the Facebook platform. For example, the Megapolis application, developed by Social Quantum, became one of the most successful applications on Facebook, and then for the month since its launch, it took 5th place in the US ranking of the highest-grossing Apple applications.
