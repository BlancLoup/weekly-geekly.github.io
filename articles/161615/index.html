Room plans are now available for Google Maps / Germany



Not long ago, floor plans appeared in the web version of Google maps. Of course, the plans were not available for all places, but for a limited number of regions in the UK, USA, Denmark, Japan, Sweden, France, Belgium. However, the number of plans is quite high, and exceeds 10 thousand objects, including museums, libraries, shopping centers and other places. Now, to all this, space plans have been added for some facilities in Germany.

Now, according to the developers of the service, Google is going to work actively in Europe to expand the number of places for which room plans will be available. Work is underway all the time, and developers are talking about a rapid expansion in the number of places available for viewing. In general, a commendable initiative, one cannot but agree.

And yes, in addition to the web application, these locations are available for the Google Maps mobile application. If anyone is interested, then the news "first-hand", in German, is available here .
