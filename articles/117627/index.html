YouTube Copyright School

An interesting course was taken by the Youtube service, which I hurried to report on in my blog . Now users who have in any way violated someone's copyright (and not very) rights, send ... no, not to the ban, but for retraining in the "YouTube Copyright School"

Re-training is to watch the "video course" . In this case, the user must answer the test questions and after the correct answer to which you can continue your activity on the trash ... filling the service with content.



The video itself is a cartoon in the form of the well-known Happy Tree Friends series in English with the ability to view with subtitles and in other languages.

Such a step was forced by the situation when a completely respectable user for one reason or another could violate copyrights and in order not to block her account (sometimes quite old and popular) they decided to send to correctional courses.
