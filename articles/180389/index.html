Opera 14 for Android has been released!

Hooray! No, even more, OMG! Final Opera 14 for Android is out! We were waiting for this release no less than you to show you all our ideas, implemented from scratch on a new engine.

Opera 14 for Android is built on Chromium 26 , on top of which lies the interface completely rewritten by the platform, perfectly complying with all the design principles for Android. You can download the new version from Google Play or open the m.opera.com mobile link and try the browser in action.

Great browser for Android

Opera 14 runs on Chromium 26, which does not yet include Blink and is still running on WebKit.
