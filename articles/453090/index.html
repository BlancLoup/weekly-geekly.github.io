MERLION has become the official distributor of Zextras, the world's leading developer of extensions for the Zimbra mail server.

MERLION , the leading Russian VAD distributor, and Zextras , a global developer of extensions for the Zimbra open source mail server, announced a partnership agreement to sell products and services of the vendor in Russia and the CIS.



This agreement will make Zextras solutions (for organizing a postal service, chats, document collaboration, data backup and storage, mobile device support, personal storage) available to a wide range of Russian users through MERLION partners in the form of license purchases. Zextras solutions are based on open source software, which makes customers less dependent on developers and helps to easily expand and modify their information systems.
