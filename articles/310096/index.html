Experiment. How we violated the speed of the mobile Internet

Monday, early morning, autodrome near Moscow. Chef Instructor of the Academy of Safe Driving, a popular IT blogger and experts in the field of telecommunications MegaFon gathered around the sports car.

What made this motley company come together? And made them the first in a series of "destruction of the myths" of cellular communication experiment. As part of these experiments, the quality of the mobile Internet will be tested in unusual and often extreme situations: during peak loads on equipment.



As you know, MegaFon PJSC was the first company to provide Russian subscribers with high-speed mobile Internet connection (both 3G and 4G), and at the moment, with his direct participation, work is underway to switch to 5G.
The quality of the mobile Internet depends on many factors. Among them:

  1. the distance from the base station (BS) and its load (do not forget that voice traffic takes precedence over data transmission);
  2. the presence of obstacles between the BS and the subscriber;
  3. the work of programs that filter traffic, with the possibility of limiting the channel (firewalls, antiviruses, etc.);
  4. the hardware platform of the smartphone (often the internal limitations of the chips do not allow using 4G at full capacity);
  5. in a car, the quality of communication is also affected by toning - the metallized film screens the waves.



According to the specification, 4G Internet should work when driving at speeds up to 300 km / h.
