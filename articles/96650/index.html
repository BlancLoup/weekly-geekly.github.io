Why all this?




The main problem of the global demoscene is the lack of new blood. The guy in the photo on the right is one of the participants of CC'09 realtime coding compo. Without knowing anything about the Z80 assembler, he was able to deal with the SDK in 1 evening (if such a concept is generally applicable to a debugger and assembler on a 5'25 disk) and, along with venerable participants, ‚Äúnavigate‚Äù the procedure for the contest task. I have never seen such interest in programming and mastering the architecture of "new" platforms like his. But there are thousands of such hidden nuggets in Russia!

I do not encourage you to write code for computers made 30 years ago! Try to take into your own hands the existing hardware and go a little deeper than ready-made frameworks, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, etc. Try to get into the very essence of programming and reach the heights of enlightenment in the hardware. And if you succeed, you will experience the deepest motivation in your life. Motivation to learn about the world through code (graphics or music).

We will be glad to see you on our domestic demopati. Russia is waiting for your releases!
