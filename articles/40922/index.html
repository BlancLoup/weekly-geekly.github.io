MaxSite CMS - first impressions

Yesterday on one of my blogs I put MaxSite CMS 0.22 . First impressions are only positive. The author of this system, Maxim , has been engaged in supporting the Russian version of WordPress for a long time, and by September of this year he released his own production system, for which he has an unequivocal respect. What can be noted first:

1. From the stagnation of Wordpress is not left a trace. The system is lightweight and eats very little memory.

2. In the system there is absolutely nothing superfluous, at least, such a feeling immediately arises.

3. Settings and generally work with CMS are very convenient and obvious. It took me no more than an hour to lift the site, set it up and copy my old texts on the subject there.

4. Support at a very good level: Maxim answers some questions or comments very quickly. Yesterday I complained that the plug-in that creates the XML Sitemap does not work correctly, and the author posted the corrected version literally an hour later.

Well, I will use further and test other features. In the near future I plan to start creating my own template.
