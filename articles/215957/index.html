Create a future that you can put on yourself!


This year, Intel is holding a worldwide competition for innovators Make It Wearable , whose goal is to create ideas and concepts for new wearables. Realize your creative potential and win a trip to San Francisco, expert assistance in creating your product or a cash award from a total prize pool of $ 1.3 million.

You can take part in the competition in two different ways. The first on schedule is a competition of futuristic ideas describing wearable devices ( Visionary Track ). Offer your original idea that can improve the world in the future, issue it in the form of a minute video clip and send it to the experts. Call for applications has already begun! Winners of the competition will receive paid invitations to the final event of the competition in San Francisco and cash prizes of $ 5,000.

In the summer of 2014, a competition of developers of wearable devices ( Development Track ) will begin within the competition. Participants will be required to submit an application and a promotional video describing an innovative conceptual product, which will be evaluated by the jury in terms of its creativity, feasibility and market potential. Those who qualified for the semi-final will receive expert assistance from the best specialists of the company. Prizes in the competition will be a variety of support (marketing and technical) of the product being created by Intel, as well as cash prizes of $ 10,000 and the main prize - half a million dollars!

To participate you must register on the contest website . Do not forget to follow the news!
