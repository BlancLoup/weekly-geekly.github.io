Marketplace launched in Ukraine, Kazakhstan and 21 countries

This night the opportunity to publish applications for Windows Phone users in Ukraine, Kazakhstan and another 21 new countries has opened. This makes it possible to reach 60% more potential users with your applications.

Please make your applications available for new countries, especially for residents of Ukraine and Kazakhstan, who can enjoy Russian-language applications as well.

A complete list of new countries Bahrain, Bulgaria, China, Costa Rica, Croatia, Estonia, Iceland, Iraq, Israel, Kazakhstan, Latvia, Lithuania, Qatar, Romania, Saudi Arabia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, Ukraine, Venezuela, Vietnam.

Detailed manual is available on the website . I draw attention to the fact that for some of the new countries an additional check is required for compliance with local laws (paragraph 3.10 by reference). In any case, the expansion of geography requires new certification of applications - due to the launch of new countries, including China, this process currently takes at least a week, so hurry.

From you it requires only 3 steps:

1. Go to your profile on the App Hub and select the application



2. Select change application data in the directory



3. Select all countries at once or individual items.



The Windows Phone ecosystem is developing rapidly, do not miss the opportunity to be the first to enter new markets!
