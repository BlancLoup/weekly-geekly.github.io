Patent Wars: Digging the Pits for Yourself?

To this end, Microsoft and Apple bought patents from Nortel, a bankrupt company. What will happen next is not difficult to understand: multi-million dollar or even multi-billion dollar lawsuits against Google, incriminating it in copyright infringement. This is the latest information about patent wars, which flare up like a fire, and already not even in the forest, but at a gas storage facility. Still would!
