Weather in the house and on the street - how to ensure a healthy microclimate around you?



Weather conditions outside, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, are not very difficult to recognize. To do this, you can either turn on the appropriate weather application, or at least look at the thermometer and look out the window (of course, you will not know the humidity in this way, but the main indicators are completely).

But what if you want to learn more about the conditions in your own home or office? After all, we all know that the concentration of CO and CO2, humidity and temperature, the combination of all these factors have a very strong effect on performance and health. In this case, electronic assistants can come to the rescue, who can provide all the necessary information in a matter of minutes.

Weather Station Netatmo




Netatmo has released a universal device that can measure indoor temperature, carbon dioxide concentration, atmospheric pressure, noise pollution and indoor humidity. If it's time to air the room, the meteering station will report it. As you know, it is often necessary to ventilate the room ‚Äî this is necessary both to maintain normal well-being and to work productively for a long time.

If, for example, there is a large amount of carbon dioxide in the room (which, as we know, it is not detected by organoleptic methods - it is transparent and odorless), the person begins to feel not very well, the ability to concentrate disappears, the person quickly gets tired.

Netatmo station transmits all data to a mobile device, to the application, where the weather forecast for the entire current region is also displayed. Plus, you can control the station by voice.

Nest Learning Thermostat




Many people know about this thermostat, since the device perfectly controls the room microclimate, interacting with some smart devices. According to the developers, the thermostat provides not only a comfortable stay of people in the room, but also an energy saving of 20%.

Nest can control the temperature in the house, depending on weather conditions and temperature changes outside. At the same time, the thermostat is capable of self-learning, and works in full accordance with the schedule, daily routine and preferences of each family (of course, Nest can be installed not only in an apartment or a house, but also in the office). For example, Nest recognizes that there is no one at home and automatically adjusts the temperature to avoid overheating or cooling an empty house.



The Nest thermostat works with 95 percent of various heating and cooling systems, including systems running on gas, electricity, using a heat pump, systems of forced air circulation, systems running on solar and geothermal energy, etc.

Nest protect





And this is a smart smoke detector and a carbon monoxide detector. The manufacturer of the device is Nest - the same company that produces and smart thermostat. One of the advantages of the system is that it can determine the concentration of carbon monoxide (carbon monoxide). Carbon monoxide is much more dangerous than carbon dioxide, as it actively reacts with blood hemoglobin, leading to blockage of the supply of oxygen to the body.

In addition, the device can determine the humidity. Inside the detector are the following modules:
  • photoelectric smoke detector
  • carbon monoxide sensor
  • thermal sensor
  • three violation detection sensors
  • ambient light sensor
  • humidity sensor


Like all the devices described above, Nest Protect transmits all data wirelessly to a mobile device.
