The novel, written by the Japanese program, reached the final of the literary contest



During the development of computer technology, machines learned how to perform a wide range of tasks previously available only to humans. This work in the workplace, and data analysis, and driving a car. It got to the point that people begin to fear the loss of work - after all, robots do not get tired and do not go on strike, but function around the clock.
