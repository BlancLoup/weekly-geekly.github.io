FabLab UrFU: when state initiatives are useful



It seems that very soon Ekaterinburg will become an ideal city for the start of hardware projects. Here, even the government initiative can bring considerable benefits to the emerging business. It is only necessary to know the passwords and appearance. We are happy to open one of such appearances for you today. Under the cut more than 25 clickable photos (traffic!). Ready?

"Innovation-innovative center for prototyping and prototyping of Ural Federal University" is located in Yekaterinburg at ul. Komsomolskaya, d.61. We pass through the hall, past the guard, we need office number 102.



Turn left.



We enter a large bright room. It all starts pretty simple and harmless. Two tables. Soldering stations ERSA 2000A Micro + ERSA I-CON2000A. Microscope.







But behind them there is already heavy artillery. Site of manufacturing prototypes of printed circuit boards.

LPKF Laser & Electronics Equipment Complex
