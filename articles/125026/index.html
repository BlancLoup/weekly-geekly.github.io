Application Dress (Useful links)



When we conceived Sarafan

Each of us many times faced with the fact that we need a recommendation of a trusted reliable person or company. You have to search, call friends and acquaintances, etc. We thought that people needed a service that would allow them not to waste time on unnecessary communications and would allow them to quickly and easily find the right people: a housekeeper, a tutor, a trainer, a plumber, etc. We thought and decided to make a sarafan or in other words a word of mouth on a social network.


Sarafan



A simple service that helps users find recommendations and give their recommendations for trusted people and companies. The Internet and social communications are developing rapidly, we want them to bring people the benefit not only of communication and acquaintance, but also more everyday benefits for real life and solving real issues. Therefore, we made a dress - a service of real good.

Ideology

Share with friends and find what you need! Open and active people who understand the value of their social circle and acquaintances can easily use the connections and acquaintances of their friends, as well as friends of their friends. We laid the foundation of a very simple principle - share your recommendations with friends and they will share theirs with you.

Usability

We tried not to overload the application with unnecessary functionality, to make it as simple, clear and accessible as possible. The application contains two main tabs "MY SARAFAN" and "FIND SARAFAN". Additionally, we have placed the rating of your friends according to their activity in the Sundress. Rating will allow you to understand the activity of your friends and their willingness to recommend their links.

Users can add and remove their Sarafana, search for what they need in the Sarafana of their friends and kick their friends if they need a contact found.

We hope that users will appreciate the simplicity and benefit of this application and will use it every time when they need to find something.

We are waiting for constructive criticism. Thank!
