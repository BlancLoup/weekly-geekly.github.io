Open Technology Forum II in Yekaterinburg

April 18, 2010 at 14.00 in the USTU-UPI (Ekaterinburg, Mira str., 21), the second in a row, but not by value, Open Technologies Forum will open. The event focuses on the benefits of using open technologies in a business environment.
If you are interested in promoting free software products, use them in your professional activities or are just starting to get acquainted with them, this forum is for you.


Planned topics for discussion


  • Launching 1C: Enterprise platform in Linux environment
  • Free licenses
  • Life projects Open-Life.org and Fireforge.net
  • Other hot topics


Will there be guests?


Invited guests from Moscow, Nizhny Novgorod.

Who does all this?


The event is organized and supported.

  • Public organization "GNU / Linux User Group of Ekaterinburg", www.e-lug.ru
  • MyOOo.ru Internet project - available and easy about OpenOffice.org, myooo.ru
  • company GNU / LinuxCenter, linuxcenter.ru


Information Partners

  • LinuxFormat magazine, linuxformat.ru
  • portal about free software NIXP.ru, nixp.ru
  • Internet project RUSNIX.RU, rusnix.ru
  • OpenNET - open source and Linux portal, www.opennet.ru
  • JustMedia news site, www.justmedia.ru


Where?


The forum will be held in the main educational building of the USTU-UPI, in the Hall of the Academic Council. Some changes are also possible, follow the information on the site.
