Coursera launched specialization

Now you can not just get the verified certificate for the subject, but by taking a certain set of courses and receiving a certificate for each of them, as a result, become the holder of a certificate of completion of specialization. It includes from 3 to 9 courses plus the final project. For the project is supposed the same payment as for the verified certificate - $ 49.
