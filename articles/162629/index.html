Reading books in English and learning the language is easier with WordMemo

I want to talk about the non-commercial service wordmemo.ru , as well as the related program WordMemo for devices with Android OS. Our service is designed for people who want to read English texts (for example, fiction). I myself belong to those people who can easily read small fragments of ‚Äútechnical literature‚Äù, but a paragraph from the classics is impossible. The basis of the service, which helps me a lot in this business, is the "reader" with the ability to watch translations of words, write out their meanings, and - most importantly! - simulators that allow you to effectively memorize written words. For example, for each unfamiliar word contexts are saved in which it occurs.
The main idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe service is that it is best to learn words not taken separately, as in dictionaries, but as part of real sentences. Something like an English-Russian phrasebook and a textbook that you create yourself!

Book selection


The site has a choice of several classic books that are not covered by copyright law, but you can download your book, which will be used only by you. While supported formats TXT, RTF and DOC.



The book will appear on your list. In the same list, set it to mark it current so that it opens for reading.

If you now click on the word being studied, in any other text, you will receive a hint from the system in the form of the previous sentence in which this word you encountered is the context.
