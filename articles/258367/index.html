Dadata.ru checks documents and knows all banks

DaData.ru is a service for automatic verification and correction of contact information (name, addresses, telephone numbers, email, passports). Plus javascript-widget and API prompts when entering the address, name, organization and bank details.

Since the previous release, we have become even steeper to work with the data:
  • suggest bank details
  • we return geocoordinates in prompts by addresses,
  • check the validity of the passport
  • determine the value of the apartment.


Bank Details Tips


They help you enter bank details quickly and without typos. Source - the website of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation, updated every day.

Search by name, BIC and SWIFT. Return all the basic details, including a correspondent account and RCC, to automatically substitute them in the act or contract.

Hints are connected as jQuery-plugin to any text field. Or via HTTP API . Free up to 10 thousand requests per day.

Address hints return geo-coordinates


Accurate to home. If the house is not found, the street coordinates are returned; if the street is not found - the coordinates of the city center.



Checking passports


Dadata checks the series and number of the passport according to its own algorithms and directory of invalid passports of the FMS:



Works in files and through API .

Determine the cost of an apartment


We return the average market value of apartments for Russian millionaire cities . Sources - 4 most popular sites for the sale of real estate, covering 20-40% of homes. We recalculate annually.



It works with standardization through API (square_meter_price, flat_price fields) and in files.
