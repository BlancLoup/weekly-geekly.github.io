Vivace - the concept of a synthesizer with a touchscreen



Hairy hands of designers got to the music synthesizers. In the concept of Chinese Young-Shin Lee and Hae-Jin Jung there is no keyboard, but there is the possibility of a wireless connection to the Internet. And the LCD monitor? Well, while synthesizers were previously equipped with LCD monitors, take at least the same Yamaha DGX-520 with a display of 320x240 pixels.

To be fair to the end, the Vivace is not just a synthesizer, but a whole combine for sound processing. Here and the equalizer, and sound editing and siphoning new samples from the Network. All this is beautifully displayed on a huge screen by the standards of synthesizers, packed in a miniature package and ...

... and, apparently, will not be too comfortable to use. Personally, I do not really imagine how you can normally play the phono without pressing keys. Tactile sensations in music - no less important than the sound, which is obtained at the output. But as a toy, Vivace looks like a very nice development.
