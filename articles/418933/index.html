Camera


The main sensor has a resolution of 16 megapixels, the physical size is 1 / 2.6 ", and the lens aperture is f / 1.7. There is also optical stabilization. Apparently, the cameras are the same as in the OnePlus 5T, that is, the Sony IMX519 and Sony IMX376K.

Battery life


Compared to the OnePlus 5T, the sixth version of the screen has increased and the hardware platform has changed, but the battery capacity remained the same as it was - 3300 mAh. And although fans of "bricks" from companies like Oukitel twist their nose, in fact this is enough for normal work due to an economical screen.

My smartphone worked in a balanced mode for about a day, which approximately corresponded to the battery life of OnePlus 5T. According to the results of the iXBT.com standard battery test, the battery life in reading mode was 23 hours and 30 minutes, in online video mode it was 14 hours and 30 minutes, and in 3D game mode it was only 3 hours.



Charging Dash Charge allows you to fully charge a discharged smartphone in one hour and 20 minutes.

Total


In the pros - a powerful platform, a good (but not great) camera, an excellent screen.
In neutral features - appearance (in my opinion, rather boring)
Of the minuses - the lack of microSD

In general, it is really a very worthy Chinese smartphone. Of course, official Russian prices, as always, are striking in their inadequacy (45, 49 and 55 thousand rubles for 6/64, 8/128 and 8/256 GB, respectively), and it is better for "white" to buy P20 Pro or Samsung Galaxy S9.
