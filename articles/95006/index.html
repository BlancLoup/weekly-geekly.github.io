Mobical out of beta


The beta testing of the Mobical project, a free synchronization server with SyncML support and an impressive list of synchronized data (contacts, calendar, bookmarks, tasks, notes and SMS), has quietly and quietly ended.

Along with the release of the beta, the project developers decided to please their users with a new minimalist design in the spirit of Google products. And this is primarily due to the tight integration with Google Sync. In addition, users can appreciate the improved support for mobile devices and the ability to synchronize on devices that are difficult to synchronize via HTTPS.

The service is completely free and has only three limitations:
  • Resale of services or other benefits are prohibited.
  • The administration reserves the right to allow / prohibit the registration of new users from individual countries.
  • The administration reserves the right to delete accounts on the complaint without the right of appeal


Happy sync!
