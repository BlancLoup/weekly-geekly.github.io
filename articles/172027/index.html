New service from MegaFon - reading remote SMS


Foreword


I have always been sure that mobile operators store sent / received SMS for some time. But this confidence was theoretical. Today I was once again convinced.

Why do you need remote SMS?

UMS interface with open SMS sent from outside the service

How does it work


I stumbled upon this quite by accident. On December 11 last year, when I first read about UMS, I signed up for this service just for the sake of interest. I played a little with Flash-sms and forgot about it. Today there was a need to send Flash again, I went to the ums interface and ... saw ALL my SMS correspondence from December 2012 until today. And from that time on, I sent and received messages only to the phone. Since then, I have changed 3 devices, a lot of firmware, i.e. It's not about any service installed in the OS.

How to connect / disconnect?


You can connect and disconnect the service through the service guide or by calling the Call Center. The service is called - UMS (Change Service Set-> Popular Services-> UMS). It clings when registering with the UMS web interface.
How to find a service in the Service Guide

findings


On the one hand, the service is useful. With its help, all your messages are collected in one, fairly user-friendly interface. You can freely change phones, make wipes (hello to droid leads!), Do firmware restores. All your SMS will not be lost. On the other hand, I was always sure that if I deleted the SMS from the device, only the competent authorities can read it (and the spam that comes to me is not interesting for them). Plus, there is stored sensitive information - sms from a mobile bank, indicating the balance on the account.
In general, you decide. I will disconnect this service from sin away.
