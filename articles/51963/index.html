Planet Mozilla Russia is open

In the world of Mozilla there are many interesting events. We reflect the main ones in the news, which are shown not only on the forum, but also on the site. You can subscribe to them in the form of RSS-feed, being aware of the process of development of software products.
However, in the framework of the news format, many interesting things are still missing. Some events, at first glance, do not deserve to be published as a separate article. But after all, a small picture of what is happening is formed from trifles.
The events around Mozilla can be imagined in the form of a certain large planet, where, perhaps, not everyone even knows each other. We decided to create a resource that would unite all references to Mozilla in a single place called Planet Mozilla Russia. Articles from blogs and news feeds will be collected here. Planet Mozilla opens for visitors, welcome!
