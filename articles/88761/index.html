Changing the location of the Second International Maemo Conference

I remind you that tomorrow, March 25, 2010 at 9.30, the Second International Maemo Conference starts.

Yesterday, the organizers made changes to the conference.

Due to the large number of registered, we had to change the venue of the 2nd MAEMO conference. The new place is more spacious and conveniently located for people traveling by public transport.

New venue: conference hall of the MSU medical center, Lomonosovsky pr. 27, Moscow.

The time remains the same - from 09.30 to 19.00. The final version of the conference program will be published on 24.03 to 12.00 Moscow time at http://fruct.org/maemo2 .

We are waiting for you at the conference.
