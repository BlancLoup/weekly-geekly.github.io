2. Familiarity with certification


CompTIA Cloud + certification is designed to test understanding of the essence of the cloud model of IT services consumption, as well as basic knowledge and skills in the implementation of the reference cloud model developed by the National Institute of Standardization and Technology (NIST) of the USA. Certification is designed for a wide audience, including all specialists in IT / IB.
To obtain status, it is enough to pass the exam with code CV0-001 (for 2016) as a computer test in a controlled environment of the VUE testing center. The exam consists of 100 questions in English that must be answered within 90 minutes (+30 minutes if you pass the exam in a non-English speaking country). Questions are presented in Multiple-choice format and test your knowledge and skills in 9 domains , namely:

1.0 Cloud Concepts and Models 12%
2.0 Virtualization 19%
3.0 Infrastructure 21%
4.0 Resource Management 13%
5.0 Security 16%
6.0 Systems Management 11%
7.0 Business Continuity in the Cloud 8%

3. Method of preparation


As an alternative to expensive full-time study, NIST self-study, publications officially recommended by the operator of the self-instruction manual certification, as well as numerous online training courses for the certification of Cloud + will be fine. Among the suitable ones, it is worth highlighting the free online course Explore the Modern Data Center , which clearly reveals the role of virtualization technologies and hyperconverted components in creating a software-defined data center (consider most of the Cloud). Other materials can be easily found on such portals as certcollection.org or techexams.net.

A wide list of topics tested at the exam unambiguously presupposes the existence of practical experience in administering a heterogeneous network in the scope of Network + certification, an understanding of computing virtualization technologies (Type 1, Type 2 hypervisors), networks (VLAN, VxLAN), and storage (VSAN, vSAN), as well as the basics information security in the scope of certification Security + .

4. Result


After passing the exam, certification status is given for 3 years and within the framework of the concept of lifelong learning ( Continuous Education ) requires annual membership fees and reporting in the personal account of the certification operator confirming that you maintain your qualifications up to date.

The most interesting thing is that passing an exam, you just confirmed the understanding of the reference cloud model of NIST. The next step will be to study the methodology for designing cloud-based automated systems, the knowledge and experience of which are vital for working as a design engineer and / or technical writer.

If you are interested in the work of the architect of cloud systems, then you need to learn how to describe economic business models in the form of business processes, write feasibility studies on the costs of automation and organize the work of a team of design engineers to create a cloud-based automated system as a product .
