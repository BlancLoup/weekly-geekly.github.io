Anyone who wants to try their hand at technological entrepreneurship is invited to Gorky Park for a one-day course on Creating an IT Startup. Participation in the course is free. Registration is required .

During the course of lectures, the issues of developing the project idea, monetization mechanisms, product development planning and product launch into the market will be considered.

Event Forat

The format of the event involves the active involvement of participants and communication among themselves. Ideas are born and finalized together and the goal of the seminar is to bring together people who "will create the next Google, Yandex, Facebook and Instagram".

After the course on July 29, participants will be able to start developing an application for Windows 8 (if their idea can be implemented as an application, of course) at the summer school on Windows 8, which will take place on August 5 .

Course program

09:00 - 10:00 Registration
10:00 - 12:00 Product formation: classification of markets and types of products, monetization models, approaches to creating a business model.
12:00 - 13:00 Selection of several ideas from the participants of the seminar, analysis of these ideas with all the participants of the seminar.
13:00 - 14:00 Communication of participants, lunch
14:00 - 16:00 Preparation for the creation of a product: team building, design and preparation of development, stages of product development.
16:00 - 17:00 Investments: why, when and how to attract investments in the project
17:00 - 18:00 Discussion with all the participants of the investment attractiveness of several projects of the seminar participants.
