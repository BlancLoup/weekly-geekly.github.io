Competitor analysis. Other people's mistakes are your advantages.

An analysis of the competitive environment in the market can produce stunning results. Weaknesses of competitors can easily be turned into advantages of your offer. You may be amazed by the reality of the competition of the chosen market, and in some cases, solely on the weakness of competitors, without inventing anything personally, to create a unique UTP for the market.

Any store has a weak spot. Even Ozone, up to a certain time, had a problem with customer feedback, which resulted in 20% of failures. What to say about less developed stores.



What should be the analysis of online shopping competitors?

First , the analysis must be done before you begin to enter information about products, draw design and customize your store. Naturally, after you decide what product you will sell.

Why? It's simple.

Analysis allows you to figure out how to organize the store is not worth it, and how to submit information on the site. It is much easier to do it right than to correct the mistakes, right?

For example, about the information among 5 sites with mobile phones, I did not find even a hint of audience segmentation . What for?

Because, being carried away by the process, some important criterion of site evaluation can easily fly out of my head. And only thinking that it is important to evaluate, you can take into account all the necessary criteria. True, anyway in the course of the analysis, add a couple to the list.

"Contraindications"


But I would also like to caution to draw hasty conclusions. Here, too, the well-known proverb warns, and it is necessary to think about ways to turn other people's mistakes into their advantages thoughtfully.

In some cases, there is a causal link that is not visible to the naked eye. For example, a colorful ad unit on the product page in a prominent place, intriguing visitors to click on it. It may seem to some that it is cool, and in fact interrupts orders.

Undoubtedly, to conduct a good audit, you need the skills of Internet marketing. Whatever the personal feelings, and no matter how objectively you try to approach the assessment, there are statistics, a psychology of human behavior and other wisdom. In addition, the amount of experience and knowledge may be insufficient to identify all the disadvantages, look deeper than the confusing text about the conditions of delivery.
