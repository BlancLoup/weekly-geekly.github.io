AWS: New Prices Simple Storage Service [S3] - even cheaper

Hello!

From December 1, prices for using Amazon S3 cloud storage are reduced. Twenty-four times AWS lowers prices by an average of 24%, and now the first terabyte in Virginia will now cost 9.5 per gigabyte. Compare that now we have a price of 12.5. Not a bad price cut, right? New prices for all volumes and regions can be found on this page .

It is noteworthy that the prices for large volumes have not changed. This means that neither Dropbox nor other services that use S3 as their storage will fall in price. Changes to the giants will not affect at all. But projects that use up to ~ 5 petabytes of cloud storage will now pay a quarter less.

We expect the next time the decline in prices for traffic and storage operations!
