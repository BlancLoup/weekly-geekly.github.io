Survey from Veeam Academy. Do you want to go through intensive C # in the summer?

One cannot learn to have a rest: is there a summer term from Veeam Academy?

This summer, Veeam Academy is going to launch another intensive programmer in C #. To find out your opinion on how relevant the training in the summer time is, at the end of the article we have put the ballot box. And if you still do not know what Veeam Academy is, then we will be happy to tell you. In short: Veeam Academy is a free evening C # course for novice programmers. Graduates have a real chance of employment in the group of companies Veeam Software. Read more - under the cut.



Now the Veeam Academy is taking a spring course, which started on March 14 of this year, the graduation is scheduled for the end of May. The guys have difficult stages of selection, which included online testing and face-to-face interviews with employees of the Research and Development department.

More than 400 applications were received for online testing. According to its results, we invited 50 most successful candidates to a personal interview at the office. After the face-to-face interview, 11 people remained, of whom the group was formed. The students of the course were students who scored a high score on testing, who had basic knowledge of C # programming and were ready to gain practical team development skills.

Leading teacher of the course - Dmitry Shalymov, Ph.D. in Physics and Mathematics, MCSD, has experience in commercial software development for more than 10 years and teaching experience of more than 5 years, co-author of more than 40 scientific publications.

During training, not only lectures take place, but also seminars and master classes with the participation of leading developers of the Veeam Software group of companies, who share their experience and knowledge and acquaint children with the peculiarities of team development using the Scrum methodology.
