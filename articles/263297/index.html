Online stores deliver correctly using DaData.ru

Customers of online stores are enraged by lost parcels and order forms with dozens of fields. When the customer is angry, he leaves. Therefore, a good shop owner tries to make life easier for both the buyer and himself.

The owners of five online stores told me how they use DaData.ru Tips to send packages without refunds, simplify order forms and segment customers.



1. Receive indexes on which the parcel will reach


Timur Shakhov, creator of deoshop.ru :
Indices are a good thing for orders by Russian Post; many people write indices incorrectly. It seems that a person has filled the address correctly, you give this address to the mail, the mail accepts. But the difference in some PGT (urban-type settlement) and the parcel went the wrong way. Or the person does not remember his index. Or incorrectly writes. Dadata puts down the index and corrects the address - we are now testing this opportunity with great pleasure.



With Dadata.ru Tips, the client specifies the address in one line in free form. And the online store receives an address broken into separate fields, supplemented by missing parts and verified by the address directory.
