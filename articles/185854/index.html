Modular monitor and all-in-one PC

The article proposes the concept of a modular monitor and a PC on an all-in-one basis.

Instead of a preamble and a thousand words, it is easier to show one picture:




What is now on the market for desktop systems:
- huge boxes of system units with a bunch of empty space inside and a bunch of wires
- mini PCs are limited in resources and still with wires
- the monoblocks which are not intended for an upgrade or expansion



What is offered:
- at the heart of the monitor 21.5 inches with LED backlight, IPS and Full HD 1920x1080 resolution (18.5 inches from 1366x768 - cheap model, 27 inches 2560x1440 or 4K - top model)
- housing made of aluminum panels, bolted on the back side
- the side of the monitor is multifunctional with universal mounting for the modules, includes 2 USB ports on each side, built-in speakers, audio jack
- the monitor video controller module is located at the back, to which functional modules are connected via HDMI: PC, TV tuner, Android board, DLNA / MHT adapter
- case completes VESA 100x100 mount for desk stand, wall mounting

Modules



Main functional modules and usage scenarios:
- without module = monitor with HDMI input
- TV tuner = TV
- TV tuner + Android = "smart" TV
- Android = gaming and Internet PC, learning station
- Intel Atom = Internet PC, office PC, learning station
- Intel i3, i5, i7 = home, office, gaming, work PC
- DLNA / MHT / m-HDMI adapter = telephone monitor



Front Panel Modules:
- PCT touch screen
- IR touch screen

At the top of the screen:
- Webcam
- LED notifiers

Sideways:
- speakers
- capture for a smartphone or tablet (USB port for charging and data - in the same sidebar)
- LED miter and other modding pieces
- stack module for second monitor



Behind:
- battery (backup and portable version)
- external HDD
- LED backlight (dynamic similar to lighpack or static like a lamp)

Below:
- docking keyboard for console use
- control buttons or shortcut
- sub woofer

Mounts:
- desktop
- to the pipe (for example, to organize an Internet cafe in 5 minutes)
- wall
- floor stand
