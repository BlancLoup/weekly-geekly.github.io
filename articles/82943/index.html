Putting the music library in iTunes on our Mac OS in order

Having presented his beloved Nokia 5800 XM and knowing that she loves to listen to music, he realized one unpleasant feature - most of the songs have not only the cover of the album, but simply the name of the song.

Tell the habrachelovek, how often have you seen something shapeless in your playlist?



Agree, the spectacle is not a pleasant one.
And everyone had the need to correct this disgrace.

Armed with patience and ingenuity, the following wonderful programs were found under Mac OS (Only one was found under Windows, but she refused to recognize the compositions adequately).

CoverScout 3


This wonderful program, from the same developer (strange, why were they not merged into one program?) Performs the role of searching for the album cover. Also in the settings we specify where to search for music and in the menu we start the search.
