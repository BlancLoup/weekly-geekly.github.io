Upgrade to Android 4.0 will appear in March

- this is just the pre-holiday announcement that just pleased us on the Sony Ericsson grocery blog .

After two announcements about the progress of preparing Ice Cream Sandwich for release on their smartphones, the company finally provided accurate information on the dates of the expected update.

The release will be rolled out in two stages. In late March and early April, Android 4.0 will be received by the first owners of Xperia arc S, Xperia neo V and Xperia ray phones. Shortly after, starting from the end of April and beginning of May, the line will reach just Xperia arc, Xperia PLAY, Xperia neo, Xperia mini and mini pro, Xperia pro, Xperia active and Sony Ericsson Live with Walkman.

Now, however, the process was supplemented with the result, and the picture was fully completed. Sony Ericsson continues to delight with its openness and good news.
