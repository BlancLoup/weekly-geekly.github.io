Aluminum coating of fine grinding and finishing NeoWood

At Computex 2009 , which was recently held in Taipei, ASUS presented to the public several new materials that it intends to use in the decoration of some notebook models. No, not light carbon and not fearless Kevlar - everything is simpler - wood and iron. But!

Imagine that you pick up the metal, and feel the silk, which quickly warms up under your fingers. This range of sensations is so unusual that it can attract the close attention of even true connoisseurs of style.

And here is another innovation. Quite different, but just as attractive. With its decoration, it inspires peace of mind - so much like a tree, but more modern and stylish.

The only difference of the new product is much greater durability and resistance to external influences. This was achieved thanks to new developments in the field of synthetic materials, and, it should be admitted, it looks very unusual and beautiful.



The second material, which will be equipped with new products of the manufacturer, is an aluminum coating ASUS Satin-brushed Aluminum with fine polishing, manufactured using evaporative metallization technology ( NCVM , Non-conductive Vapor Metallization ). The use of this technology for the manufacture of coatings allowed us to achieve an interesting and beautiful texture, as well as good wear resistance in everyday conditions. In particular, the coating is highly resistant to scratches and fingerprints, providing the notebook body with additional mechanical strength and reliability. The new coverage is also notable for the fact that it does not interfere with the operation of wireless communication modules such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, as has happened with other metallized finishes. The coating is a matte metal surface with numerous parallel small "scratches" from polishing, which provides an interesting play of light on the device. The coating will be made in various color shades.
