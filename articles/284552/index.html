Got a problem in the city? Tell about them and help them eliminate



Stumbled on the open spaces of the network on a very useful option of a social network. Streetjournal is a Perm project whose essence is in the collective discussion of the actual problems for the citizens. It would seem that discussion by discussion, so what?

So, the essence is:
The user of this social network describes the actual city problem, other citizens vote for the most of them, and for significant problems notifications are sent to the appropriate state structures.

How exactly it works, I do not know, the corresponding section on the site is in development. However, a little googling, I found positive reviews that the system really works. Here is one of them.

What was surprised, the site has the opportunity to publish problems in other cities: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg, Kirov, Rostov-on-Don, Novosibirsk, Smolensk, Kazan and Perm itself. Statistics briskly reports about 70 problems solved out of 436, however, which is important for me, in Moscow so far there is not a single problem solved.
