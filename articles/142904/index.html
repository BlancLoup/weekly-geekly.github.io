Quick view of site statistics with a LiveInternet counter

Until recently, I did the same thing just through FireBug, replacing the url image of the counter with the desired one. But searching the counter code, substitution is a waste of time. To do it faster and more conveniently - just create a bookmark with js-code instead of the URL on the browser's bookmarks bar and when you click on this bookmark - we get the statistics of the open site. A small and quite obvious life hack, which will be useful to those who do not know :) The code and detailed instructions on the cat. Have a nice day, everyone!

  • Add a new bookmark with the address javascript:document.location='http://counter.yadro.ru/logo;'+document.location.host+'?28.1'
  • Open the site that interests us and click on this tab.
  • If LI statistics is installed on the site - we enjoy abbreviated, but quite informative site statistics
