Launch of Visual Studio 2010 in Russia



The official launch of Visual Studio 2010 and the .NET 4.0 platform in Russia has passed. The Moscow event was one of the first events of the global launch. In total, there will be two such launches in Russia except for Moscow: on April 14 in St. Petersburg and 16 in Yekaterinburg.

What was at the start of Visual Studio 2010.

Carefully, under the cut a lot of photos.






First, it all started with a plenary report, during which a large number of speakers spoke and presented on slightly different aspects of the new studio, SQL Server 2008 R2, Silverlight 4 and related tools.
In the photo below: Roman Zdebsky, Denis Kotlyarov, Vitaly Zayko, Marat Bakirov, Gregory Barkan.



Multi-touch capabilities, support for new interfaces, advanced features of SharePoint, support in Visual Studio and Linq for SharePoint were demonstrated. Plans were announced for the sale of SQl Server 2008 R2, which can be ordered in May. They showed how it became more convenient to work with TSQL in VS2010 through IntelliSense and debugging procedures. Marat Bakirov told about the new features of the Entity Framework 4.



Then Sitronics took the floor and spoke about the capabilities of the testers tool - Test and Lab Managment. An important aspect of development - team development, is also not overlooked in the new studio. This was told by Roman Zdebsky, Vladimir Gusarov and Dmitry Andreev. They talked about the capabilities of Visual Studio 2010 and the new version of Team Foundation Server 2010, which help the team to work more efficiently. Many have told and just how with the help of Test and Lab Managment the testing process in a large team becomes easier and more efficient.



The plenary report was only the beginning of the event. Numerous guests were waiting for three streams of reports on a variety of topics, as well as a report from a guest star - Brian Harry - the team leader of the Team Foundation Server development team.
