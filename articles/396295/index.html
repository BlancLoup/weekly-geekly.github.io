The exhibition "Russian Space" in the Multimedia Art Museum



Admission : 500 p.
Photographing : Free.
General assessment : To go only if you are not at all sorry for time and money, or you love contemporary art.

In the lobby visitors are greeted by the descent module "Vostok-1", on which Gagarin made his flight.



The descent vehicle is permanently stored in the museum of NPO Energia, where it is a little more difficult to get into, and here it is set temporarily. It is very curious that on the lower part of the descent vehicle, where the greatest thermal effect is when braking in dense layers of the atmosphere, the thermal protection is collected from the rings, and not installed in a continuous layer. A similar design was on subsequent ships, for example, when speaking about the flight of the Voskhod-1, Feoktistov mentions these rings. Only on later devices does the thermal protection look solid.
