Online broadcast Microsoft Developer Tour in Novosibirsk April 22



On April 22, the Microsoft Developer Tour expedition welcomes everyone to Novosibirsk. The city of Novosibirsk has a rich potential in the field of IT technologies, because here on the basis of the Academpark there is a Technopark, in which 9 thousand scientists and 6 thousand programmers work.

For such a large audience, we will hold an event with three tracks - about client development, cloud technologies, as well as modern web development. And for those who can not personally attend the event, an online broadcast will be available. During the broadcast you are expected to live from the site and communicate with experts of our expedition. See us online from 10:00 (UTC + 6), it will be interesting!

At 10:00, the conference will be opened by Andrey Ivashentsev - Head of the Department of Strategic Technology Experts, Microsoft Russia Next, the program is divided into 3 tracks.

More information about the speakers can be on the site.
