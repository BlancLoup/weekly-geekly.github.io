Atari, in partnership with Microsoft, launched its old online games.

Today, Atari , the publisher and developer of computer games, has laid out Atari Arcade games specially adapted for web browsers. Among the titles are Asteroids, Centipede, Combat, Lunar Lander, Missile Command, Yars Revenge, Pong and Super Breakout, and others will be added later. The technical partner was Microsoft, which naturally led to the fact that you can get the best gaming experience on Internet Explorer 10 under Windows 8 with touch control.
