February 12 - World of Tanks release: Xbox 360 Edition

It's hard to miss the news from the studio, whose brainchild (according to SuperData research) is in the top five of the most profitable free2play games of 2013, bringing its creators $ 372 million. Apparently, the company is not going to stop there and is entering a new market. So, the official release date of World of Tanks: Xbox 360 Edition was announced yesterday. The global release of the console version of MMO-action will be held on February 12.



The project retained the best features of the original World of Tanks, offering at the same time an updated tank control and a new user interface that will maximize the technical capabilities of the Xbox 360. As in the PC version, players can purchase rare vehicle models as well as a premium account.

Screenshots and some well-known details - under the cut.




World of Tanks: Xbox 360 Edition will provide fans of tank battles more than 100 legendary armored vehicles from the USA, Germany and the UK. Each subsequent update will expand the fleet of armored vehicles, new models of vehicles and whole national lines will become available to users.







World of Tanks: Xbox 360 Edition is installed free of charge through the Xbox Live service and will be available to all Xbox Live Gold subscribers. In addition, Xbox Live account owners will be able to play a seven-day trial version of the console MMO-action.
