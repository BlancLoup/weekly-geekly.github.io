Powerful baby.

If you are going to purchase an entertainment center for your living room, pay attention to the SysMaster Tornado M10 Digital Media Center. This device has a lot of functions with dimensions of only 15 centimeters. The specification of this crumb is full of acronyms, for example: IPTV , VOD , VoIP SIP , PVR , and also allows you to use e-mail and voice mail, chat rooms, video camera for conferences, network video and audio broadcasting, the ability to automatically receive the latest news and weather forecast, many other features.



On the right side of the device is a video camera for conferences.


Using embedded Linux allows the M10 to fully reveal its rich communication capabilities:
  • FXO (RJ-11)
  • FXS (RJ-11)
  • Ethernet (RJ-45)
  • Telephone input (RJ-11)
  • USB 1.1 (Type A)
  • SD (Secure Digital)
  • RCA analog video port
  • Stereo RCA output
  • Infrared Port ( CIR )
  • WiFi

By connecting an external USB drive, the device can be used to record video broadcasts or stream audio and video to the network.




The M10 is an extremely interesting device, especially because of the ability to connect an external drive and support the popular standard for managing smart homes - X10. At the moment, the basic version is available for 170 USD , and the version with WiFi for 200 USD . It is likely that in some markets, the M10 can quite successfully compete with AppleTV
