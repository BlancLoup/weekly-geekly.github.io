Meet preemptive instances


If your computational tasks are flexible enough to run on a large number of short-lived instances, then you can save up to 70% using displaced instances. These instances are no different from normal ones, except that the duration of one instance is limited to 24 hours (and maybe even shorter), and availability depends on supply and demand.

Since these are resources that would otherwise just stand idle, we provide them at an extremely low price. Our customers, such as Descartes Lab , have already appreciated the advantages of a novice for performing such tasks as, for example, Hadoop MapReduce, rendering, financial analysis, and for other computationally complex tasks.

Unlike offers from other cloud providers, the cost of displaced instances in Google Compute Engine is fixed, so you can easily predict the costs of using them. The exact cost of each type of instance in preemptive mode is also available on our website .

For example, for the same instance n1-standard-1, the new regular price is $ 0.050 / hour, and for the displaced one is $ 0.015 / hour, which is 70% lower.

The cost of running many tasks on the Google Cloud Platform is 40% lower than in other clouds


We try to be a leader in price / performance ratio. Using a combination of discounts for the duration of the instance's activity period, the absence of a requirement to purchase any amount in advance, as well as per-minute billing, provides significant savings for real-world applications.

Imagine a typical web application or mobile app backend. Development and test environments consume uneven resources throughout the day. The industrial environment handles real user traffic, with its daily cycle of recessions and load rises, constant growth throughout the life of the application, and significantly higher consumption than the development environment. By quickly starting and stopping the development environment, you can benefit from per-minute billing, and the industrial environment will save up to 30% of the instance cost simply by regular work, and you will not need to make any advance payment in advance.

It is this friendly pricing policy that leads to savings of up to 40% compared with other clouds on the market in real-world tasks.
