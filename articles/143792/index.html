Battery that breathes



IBM continues to look to the future and work on projects that will make a revolutionary change in our lifestyle in the very near future. Such projects include " Battery 500 " - the project of a "breathing" battery, on which the average electric car can travel up to 500 miles (800 km) without recharging. The ratio of the weight of the battery to the amount of energy that it can provide to the engine is still not up to the same ratio in classical ICEs, where on one tank you can make a trip with a distance of more than a thousand kilometers (or even more).
