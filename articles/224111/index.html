Wellograph smart watch: a stylish and functional device for gadget and sports lovers



Now, in most companies, when designing a device, they try to pay attention to both the functionality of the product and its design. In the case of ‚Äúsmart‚Äù watches, companies care more about functionality than about design, and such devices can hardly be called stylish.

But there are exceptions, and one such exception is Wellograph . This watch and fitness bracelet, and much more. Developers position their product as a premium smart watch. By the way, the price of the device is not too high, but the functionality and design are really good.

Immediately it is necessary to highlight one feature of the watch - they can work as an autonomous, self-sufficient device, they do not have to be matched with a smartphone to see the results of training, for example. The same fitness bracelets need to be connected to a smartphone for owner information.

Design


The watch is not only functional, as mentioned above, but also looks great. Stainless steel and aluminum, scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, leather strap - all this gives them charm, so you can wear them not only during jogging, but also wear business meetings.

Among other things, the device can withstand immersion under water to a depth of 50 meters. So a business meeting can be organized on the seabed :)

Functionality


As for the capabilities of the watch, then there are a lot of things worth mentioning. Actually, the clock can not remember, now almost any iron shows time. True, the screen with the clock is not bad - a contrast image, minimal design, everything is fine.

Like all smart gadgets, Wellograph allows you to view SMS and phone calls when paired with a smartphone. In principle, this is a standard feature familiar to many.

Most of all, developers paid attention to fitness functions. The clock determines, for example, the heart rate (the sensor is located on the back of the device). There is also a pedometer, a physical activity tracker, a calorie counter, a distance meter.



The result is not displayed in the form of "bare" numbers, but in the form of diagrams and graphs, for clarity. If desired, you can view the dynamics of indicators / achievements for the day / week / month.



The most interesting function can be called a reminder of the need to start physical activity. For example, you sat behind another difficult project, forgot about the warm-up, and the gadget "remembers" this, and reminds the owner. By the way, the clock gives points for physical activity, which may serve as additional motivation :)

Wellograph is equipped with two control buttons, which are not particularly highlighted on the body.



The developers have foreseen the presence of a wireless module Bluetooth 4.0, and the watch can synchronize data with smartphones based on iOS, Android, and Windows Phone. Of course, applications for each of the operating systems have already been developed, and the user receives more information when working with a smartphone than in the case of the autonomous work of Wellograph.





The battery of the device is quite capacious, and allows the watch to function for two weeks without recharging.
