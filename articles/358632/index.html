On April 27th we had JavaScript DevDay - we talked about the mobile phone, about the web, and about hybrid applications. Sharing records of three reports.

What is DevDay?


DevDay - open meetings for sociable developers in Novosibirsk. The last meeting was special for three reasons. First, we moved away from the backend and plunged into such a different JavaScript. Just a minute: for the first time in two years! Secondly, we gathered a full house and the whole team of organizers ran together behind folding chairs. Third, they gave out the most active members of the DevDay Golden Pass community - a pass to all 2GIS techno-events without registration and a selection procedure.
