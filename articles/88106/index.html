Simkl launches Promo Manager - a tool for promotion of startups and online stores


Simkl has released a " Promo Manager " - a free service to promote your website, startup or online store on Twitter and blogs in exchange for distributing invites and promo codes to your users.

If you have: a discount coupon, an invite, secret content that is hidden behind a secret link, etc., you can exchange it for a message in the user's twitter account or for a post in his blog.

The system will automatically send a message on behalf of the user to Twitter or blog. Then check the correctness of the sent message and give the user your promo code, invite code, URL, etc., provided that this message was sent correctly.

Just add your promotional codes and enter what you want your users to tweet or blog about. The site will create a special link for your promo code or invite, which you can give to your users. Here is an example of such a page: http://activatepromo.com/59/ .

Why spread invites through Simkl?
  • If you have a private registration, you receive a large number of requests to give an invite code. Using the Promo Manager, you can automatically distribute invitation codes only to those people who are really interested in your service and who are ready to advertise your services. These are the people you really want to give invites to.
  • Automatically find and issue invites to enthusiasts who are ready to test your project if it is not 100% ready.


Why distribute coupons, vouchers using Simkl?
  • Encourage people to tweet and blog about you
  • To increase customer base
  • To increase customer loyalty
  • To promote sales (for example, excess inventory)
  • To raise brand awareness
  • To reward existing customers
  • To showcase new products and / or services
  • To remind customers of seasonal purchases
  • To attract traffic to the company's website
