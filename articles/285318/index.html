What to ask the employer at the interview, so as not to fall into the swamp

Most employers at the interview will give you the opportunity to ask any questions. This is an excellent chance to determine whether an employer is good and whether it is worth working for him. One of the deadly diseases for the team is the imitation of violent activity (hereinafter referred to as IBD). It arises where the leadership is not in coordinating work, but is replaced by formal control, where the team has been marking time, for tens of years not receiving new tasks, where there is no place for your pursuit of new technologies, where you will not learn anything new. The intrigue is intensified by the fact that the simulator never admits that it only imitates work. On the contrary, he will admit the importance of himself and his activities. Only when you boil in a team for a year, you will realize that you should not have come here, that you just lose time. But there are a few simple signs by which you can easily calculate the disease. About them this note.

Of course, conclusions cannot be made on one or two grounds. It is worth thinking only if all signs are present. In addition, you should not build on the absolute values, it is better to use these signs to compare two works (for example, the current and the proposed).

What time do you have to leave work?

Obviously, the team works effectively if it is as harmonious as possible. If one part of the staff works from 9 to 18, and the second from 15 to 24, then you cannot call such work harmonious.

Imitators always leave work late. Indeed, (imitator logic) the later a person stays, the more he works. In addition, after eight, the simulator can safely do nothing - no one will see it, but tomorrow you can proudly say: ‚ÄúI left yesterday after midnight!‚Äù

Of course, it happens that at work Avrah. Then the team goes to extreme measures, people are laid out to the fullest and, indeed, work around the clock. But this mode of operation can not last long.

If it is not about work, but about imitation, then it goes on for years. It is not possible for years to come on time and leave late. The simulator does not come to work before lunch.

(Occasionally there are non-simulators, owls, they start working as soon as they get out of bed, do half the work at home, and only then come to work. They are easy to distinguish from simulators - they leave work, especially not lingering; see the previous paragraph)

Do you have time to spend your vacation?

Do not go on vacation - another feature of the simulator. He is such a valuable employee that he simply cannot leave the business! He is irreplaceable! In addition, the time spent at work does not bother him at all and he does not feel an urgent need for rest.

In a healthy team, people are able to substitute each other (for more, see the penultimate item); and if a person really has a lot of hard work, then he will not postpone vacation.

Most organizations have canteens or arrangements with the nearest cafe. Food staff somehow organized. But the simulator is so busy with work that it either does not eat at all (eats, of course, but after the end of the working day), or takes food in the workplace.
