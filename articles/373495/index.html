5 places to work in Tel Aviv

We all know how sometimes it is difficult to organize your vacation so that the work goes and you can swim at sea. Measured relaxing on the beach under the sound of the waves, sometimes you still have to interrupt working questions, answer mail or make changes to projects and we, hanging our heads, go to the nearest cafe with Wi-Fi, where we spend a good part of the day painstakingly doing our work . Cowork bay



This co-working center was created specifically for creative people who love freedom of expression and are ready to embody their ideas in spite of everything. Specially designed spaces and a unique location with access to 25/7 on the Mediterranean coast allow start-ups, entrepreneurs, and other creative people to contribute to the development of the industry and shape their rhythm of life.

Kaufmann St 6, Tel Aviv-Yafo

Mindspace rothschild



Mindspace is an international network of coworking centers that has spread its offices from Berlin to Tel Aviv and from Herzliya to Warsaw. The coworking Mindspace Rothschild is equipped with a large number of workplaces, meeting rooms, a conference room and lounge areas. Mindspace has a complete package of services for supporting small businesses and start-ups and flexible working conditions.

Rothschild Blvd 45, Tel Aviv-Yafo

Urban place



Urban Place is a completely new co-working center in the very center of a start-up party on Rothschild Boulevard, this is where the main players in the IT industry market gathered. In addition to a fully equipped work space, Urban Place employees will help resolve legal issues, financial issues, and project investors will share their business experience.

Rothschild Blvd 3, Tel Aviv

HubSpace TLV



HubSpace TLV is located near the central bus station in one of the oldest areas of Tel Aviv with 24/7 access. HubSpace - offers its residents uninterrupted access to the Internet, kitchen area, furnished offices and open-space workplaces. Conference room, meeting rooms and a full range of services that will help focus on the development of your business.

Shvil ha-Merets 2, Tel Aviv-Yafo

SOSA



SOSA is a multi-level multi-format space for global startups founded by the pioneers of the Israeli innovative community.

SOSA solves a global challenge in the global market, directly linking start-ups, business accelerators with leading global corporations, supervising creative communities among themselves.

Shoken St 13, Tel Aviv-Yafo
