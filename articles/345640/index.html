4 New Year Cards for Microsoft Developers


This year we decided to recall the New Year cards, which were made especially for you in recent years and, of course, to congratulate you with the new postcard 2018!

Happy New Year! (2015 version)


Three years ago we congratulated you with a New Year's card, in which there was a subtle hint that in the new year we wish you to distribute your forces and find time for work and leisure. Look at these cute hamsters!



Happy New Year! (version 2016)


Ready to train your fingers? In this congratulation, you have to help the monkey to climb on a tall building and deliver the Christmas tree to the New Year corporate party. To do this, just press 2 buttons on the keyboard ... 2 different buttons that change every 5 seconds.



Happy New Year! (version 2017)


Last year, we offered you to go back to 2086 and find out how technology has changed life by then. To do this, we composed several futuristic stories together with science fiction writer Tim Skorenko.



Happy New Year! (Version 2018)


New Year's Eve is not only the time for the fulfillment of desires, but also the moment when we promise ourselves to become better. Therefore, in this new year we invite you to find out with us what exactly you are capable of and pass the test .

First you have to answer a few questions about technologies that were popular last year, so that we can see if you are ready for a new mission. Then you can choose the level of difficulty and get your mission. Let's conquer 2018 together!
