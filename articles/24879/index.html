Net keyboard and mouse

The keyboard and mouse are real hotbeds of infection. For the long life of these devices, an incredible amount of bacteria and microbes accumulates on them. For those who are particularly worried about this problem, Seal Shield has released an unusual set of mouse and keyboard. Both devices have antimicrobial properties.



The cases of manipulators are made of plastic, into which silver ions are mixed. This valuable metal, as is known, has pronounced antiseptic properties. In addition, the mouse and keyboard are waterproof, so that they can be washed.
For a complete set, the manufacturer asks for a sum of about $ 100. If desired, a mouse or keyboard can be purchased separately.
