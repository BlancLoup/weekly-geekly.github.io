New buttons and a single payment solution Yandex.Money: two free seminars in Moscow

Yandex.Money has two new payment tools:

1. The single payment solution is a service that allows merchants connected to the system to accept not only Yandex.Money, but also payments from bank cards (including Maestro) and cash.
2. New collection buttons - with their help, crowdfunds and benefactors can collect money for their projects not only from our users, but also from owners of any bank cards (issued in any country in the world).



To help users deal with these services, Yandex and Money hold free seminars on February 12 and 13 in the Moscow office.

If you are a small or medium Internet business owner, or want to do it at some point, come on February 12th.
If you are a crowdfunding, philanthropist or IT volunteer in a charitable foundation or an orphanage, come on February 13th.


February 12, 19:00
Seminar "How to effectively accept payments on the Internet"

Moderators: Vyacheslav Gladyshev, Product Manager, Translations, Yandex, and Maria Gracheva, Business Development Director.

Here you can learn how to set up payment acceptance on your website, how to make it efficient, and what payment tools Yandex.Money offers for this purpose:

  • how, in 2 minutes, set up on the website receiving money not only from e-wallets, but also from bank cards;
  • how using Yandex.Cashi you can receive payments from bank cards upon delivery of goods;
  • what is autopayments, and how they can be useful for business.

If you already use any of these products - the more you come, this is an opportunity to talk with the creators of the service, ask questions and ask for new features.

The seminar will be held at the Yandex office: Metro Park Kultury, ul. Lev Tolstoy, d. 16, Morozov business center, audience "7 Sky".
Gathering guests - from 18:30. Pre-registration - by mail: pr@yamoney.ru.

February 13, 19:00
Mitap "Payment instruments for social projects"

Moderator: Business Development Director, Maria Gracheva.

At the meeting, you can talk about tools for effective crowdfunding and find out:

  • how to increase the number of donations for your charity project;
  • what tools to collect money exist for websites and blogs;
  • how to raise money in the field (at charity fairs and festivals) using Yandex.Cash and payment leaflets;
  • what car payments are and how to continuously receive funds in support of your promotion from users;
  • how to collect money on Facebook and VKontakte.


The meeting will take place at the Yandex office: Metro Park Kultury, ul. Lev Tolstoy, d. 16, Morozov business center, audience "7 Sky". Gathering guests - from 18:30. Preliminary registration - by mail: pr@yamoney.ru


Managers will be on duty in these comments - if you fail to come to a seminar or a meeting, you can leave questions. And we will put the presentations in the corporate SlideShare .
