Thirteenth Developer Economics Survey

Developer Economics is the world's largest study on global trends in application economics and development. It is based on a survey of developers and companies, which the British company VisionMobile conducts every six months.



This is the 13th developer survey dedicated to tools, training and professional development. Each year, more than 40,000 developers from around the world take part in the Developer Economics survey, so you are offered the chance to be part of a big event and contribute to the developer community.

According to the survey results, a large report is generated, which is available to everyone for free. Filling out the questionnaire will take you no more than 15 minutes, and expressing your opinion in the defining industrial report is very important - Russia is one of the leading IT markets in the world and it is necessary to back it up with facts and figures. In addition, among the past poll are interesting prizes.

The survey is available by reference (including in Russian): http://vmob.me/DE3Q17
