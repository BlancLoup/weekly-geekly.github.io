NGINX walks on the planet

It is always nice to write about the successes of their colleagues and fellow countrymen. Especially when it is a planetary success.

Last January, NGINX became the second server in the world. Confidently overtook Microsoft ISS in popularity among active websites and threatened to force out Microsoft IIS soon, too, from second place to a million top websites.


February netcraft report.

By the way, Apache, too, is not so far away, NGINX remains the only web server that demonstrates an increase in popularity of + 0.53%. Compared to its competitors, this is a rocket take-off, because even the super popular Apache loses half a percent each month.

To consolidate the success of NGINX opened paid support for its users in the United States. As planned last fall, NGINX will provide more and more services for corporate and business clients. At the same time, the server itself will remain completely free, and its source code will be open to all. Free technical support on the forum and in the mailing lists will also not change.

And for those who, due to business criticality, need a guaranteed level of support, there are three levels of service available - Essential, Advanced and Premium, each of which differs accordingly in the speed of reaction to problems and the amount of work. The fee, for support, ranges from 1.1 thousand US dollars to 6 thousand dollars a month.



Considering such news, it is quite predictable that Igor Sysoev will receive the award of the Best Developer of the Year at the Cloud Awards conference held in San Francisco. The award for the best developer is awarded for outstanding technical achievements, innovative solutions to problems and outstanding manifestations of the entrepreneurial spirit.
