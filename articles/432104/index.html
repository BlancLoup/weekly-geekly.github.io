IT digest of December events (part one)

Before the holidays, everyone is trying to finish the unfinished and embrace the unreached - obviously, this also applies to the IT community. Today we have a whole scattering of small meetings on technologies and languages, hackathons from different points of the country interspersed with large conferences on testing, AI and mobile development.



Brno Moscow Python Meetup

When: December 5
Where: Moscow, Small Konyushkovsky Lane, 2, boiling point
Conditions of participation: information is being specified

At the upcoming meeting, the MoscowPython community awaits guests - representatives of the Czech online travel agency Kiwi.com. The company's developers have extensive experience in Python, so they will have something to share with the audience. The methodology for creating a platform of this scale and purpose, the problems of rapid growth, data distribution schemes in a large team, uninterrupted updating of the database, the difference in the code base of a young startup and a large company will all be considered in the speeches of the company's leads. The mitap will end with a small quiz with a prize drawing.

Heisenbug 2018

When: December 6-7
Where: Moscow, Europe Square, 2
Participation conditions: from 14 000 rub.

A concentrated dose of testing insights will be received by participants in a traditional profile conference. As always, topics will cover automatic and load testing and cause practical issues and specific examples. Among the speakers are representatives of such companies as Mail.Ru Group, CIAN, Smashing Magazine, eBay, Badoo, JetBrains, EPAM, Yandex. Different types of testing (layouts, microservices, interface), the creation of useful frameworks, the nuances of using certain tools and, finally, ways of survival in a team and in the market will be considered.

When: December 6

Meeting for all those involved in the administration of the database in Omsk and enjoys the exchange of interesting stories and life hacking.
