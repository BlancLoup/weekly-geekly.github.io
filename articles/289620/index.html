Apple bought LinX Imaging - Israeli startup with Russian roots

The Wall Street Journal announces the purchase of Apple Israeli startup LinX Imaging , specializing in the production of photo sensors for mobile devices.

The amount of the deal has not been officially disclosed, WSJ speculates with a figure of $ 20 million. LinX cameras can work with both 2D and 3D scenes and may be used in the next generation of Apple portable devices.
