IT specialists focus their resume on teamwork, hard work and analytical mind

The social network for searching and establishing business contacts LinkedIn has once again made a list of personal qualities that appear in every second network user profile.



Top 5 "hackneyed" personal qualities of 2012 in the United States according to LinkedIn

1) creative
2) high organizational skills
3) effective
4) motivated
5) highly qualified.

In addition to the United States, the most creative experts, according to LinkedIn, live in Canada, Germany, the Netherlands, Australia, Singapore, New Zealand. The most responsible employees work in France and Italy, and the motivated ones work in the UK, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, and Malaysia.
