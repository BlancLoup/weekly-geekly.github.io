You need to know them in person: top-15 most influential sites

The British newspaper The Observer has compiled a list of the 15 most influential sites today:

2. wikipedia.org - online encyclopedia, 912 thousand visitors per day, founder Jimmy Wales, 2001, USA.

3. napster.com - file sharing site, 500 thousand paid subscribers, founder Sean Fanning, 1999, USA.

4. youtube.com - video sharing site, 100 million clips viewed daily, founders: Chad Hurley, Steve Chen and Jod Karim, 2005, USA.

5. blogger.com - blog posting system (online diaries), 18.5 million personal users, founder Evan Williams, 1999, USA.
