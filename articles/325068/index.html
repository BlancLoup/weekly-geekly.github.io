[Translation of the article] 4 tips that we received while talking to business representatives on 4YFN 2017



4YFN Barcelona 2017 is over: things are packed, stands are dismantled and advertising materials are collected. Now it's time for two important tasks: to connect with potential customers and to summarize the information that we heard during the event.

We would like to share with you four hot tips on business, which we mainly heard, communicating with more than a thousand participants during the conference.

1. Minimum Viable Product is no longer interesting to anyone.


Are you going to launch a new web or mobile app? Instead of creating a minimum viable product (MVP), determine the riskiest assumption and test it (RAT). Replacing MVP with RAT will help you avoid headaches, find sponsors and partners, and also make the first positive impression on end users.
