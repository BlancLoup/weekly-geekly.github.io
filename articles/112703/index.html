Facebook email and impressions

Literally two weeks ago, I read a post about the possibility of starting Facebook mail , but at that time most people still did not have this opportunity, including me. I asked for an invite, and this was stopped.

Today in the morning I discovered that mail was still activated, and according to friends, apparently already activated for the masses:


I decided, without shelving, to research it a little.

First, a small intro appears telling that it can work like a regular email:




Immediately try to write a letter to a foreign address:



As you can see, it became possible to attach files to the letter, though without the functions of drag-n-drop and multi-apload.
An interesting, but as yet untested option is to create a webcam photo or video on the fly that will immediately attach to the letter.

Here, in fact, all the functionality so far, found in this similarity of mail. No labels and folders, no signatures to the letters. Alas, there was not even a forwarding function with which I can aggregate mail from several mailboxes on one gmail account.
