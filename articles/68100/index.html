Russian officials are actively using the iPhone in the work? About iMonitoring application

Wandering through the AppStore I came across an iMonitoring application in the finance category, among top free.

It shows various reports on the budgets of various subjects of the Russian Federation in various sections.



You can select any subject as seen in the screenshot.
All data can also be viewed from a regular computer here (which is very valuable, online such information was not easy to find).

In the program description it is said that it is part of the complexes ANALYTICAL CENTER OF THE HEAD and the FINANCIAL AND ECONOMIC ANALYSIS.

Thus, Russian officials are actively using the iPhone. And of course everyone remembers that Dmitry Anatolyevich Medvedev was sfotkan with iPhone before the start of their deliveries to the Russian Federation - obviously, he is among the users of the program.

In the next version, we are waiting for adding the ability to manage the strategic nuclear forces of the Russian Federation, with the support of Google Maps :-)
