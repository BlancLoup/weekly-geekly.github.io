Xiaomi will release its own laptop



Xiaomi has quickly evolved from a small manufacturer of smartphones to the fourth in size and capitalization of a mobile device manufacturer in the world. Now Xiaomi produces, in addition to smartphones, and tablets, routers and TV set-top boxes. Apparently, the company plans for the near future there is a new item - a laptop .

Rumors about a laptop from Xiaomi circulate is not the first month. But now the rumor was confirmed by laptop images, and quite detailed ones (admittedly, unofficial photos, this is a leak).

In addition to photos, the device configuration information also went to the Web. So, as the processor in the system is installed Intel Haswell i7-4500u with 2 * 8 GB of RAM. Display - 15-inch 1080p. In the implementation of the laptop will go with the OS, most likely, it will be a Linux-distribution, modified in Xiaomi.





As for the price, the price tag for a laptop is about $ 480 (2999 yuan).
