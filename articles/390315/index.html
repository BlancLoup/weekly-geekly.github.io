We need your brains

Hi, Giktayms! Every self-respecting geek knows how to test iron correctly and never misses the opportunity to tell about it! Are we right Then let's go to participate in our new competition.

Your task is to write your own test script for memory, SSD, gaming peripherals - in a word, any Kingston and HyperX products. Think of how interesting and informative to tell readers about our devices, and we will not be in debt and will give you great prizes.



Under the cut conditions of the competition and all the details.

What do we have to do?


  1. Create your own testing script for any Kingston or HyperX products. Submission form - at your discretion.
  2. Leave the script in the comments to this post and wait for the debriefing.



What is important to think about?


  1. The test scenario should be applicable to those devices Kingston and HyperX, which are sold in Russia.
  2. You can edit your comment, but if a controversial situation arises, we will take into account the earlier version.
  3. Only registered community members residing in the Russian Federation can participate in the competition.

Who determines the winner?


We, that is, the administration of the blog Kingston Technology on GeekTimes.ru. But in parallel, we will hold a vote among users, and if the results do not coincide with our opinion, then we will think about additional prizes;)

If your idea is not there, but you want to support someone else?


Put a plus for a comment with a script that you like - we will definitely take this into account.

What can I get?


  1. Modern and fast SSD HyperX Savage with a capacity of 120 gigabytes.
  2. Honor and respect other giktaymovtsev.
  3. And we will publish a test for your script on your blog and be sure to indicate the author of the idea.



Dates of the competition


Comments are accepted until 9:00 pm Moscow time on March 10, 2016. Everything that you wrote or edited after that, we will not take into account.

Thank you for your attention and stay with Kingston on Hiktatimes!
