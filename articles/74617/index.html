The smaller gadget, called SP2000 or Apollo, is designed to charge various small devices such as mobile phones, PDAs, books on electronic ink, etc. It is unlikely to be able to share energy with something more impressive, because its output voltage is low Namely, ‚Äúuesbeshnoye‚Äù - 5 volts, and the battery capacity is only 2000 mAh, that is, one and a half to two battery capacities of the average communicator. For example, for the iPhone, the adapter was not included in the kit, but it began to charge perfectly on its white cord. So if you get a new gadget, which also requires charging on the way, you will not be left empty-handed.
