Auto will understand when you are talking on the phone while driving

Today, no one can be trusted. And nothing! If earlier we were monitored only by websites, surveillance cameras and smartphones, then soon even cars that are too ‚Äúsmart‚Äù and stuffed with electronics to the top will be able to inform the road services about the chatter by mobile phone while driving.

The development team of the Brazilian University of Saint Katharina has designed the appropriate solution to help the car determine when the driver is talking instead of devoting his full attention to the situation on the road.

In the US, 85 percent of drivers talk on the phone while driving. Just five seconds are needed to dial the number ... But the car at a speed of 60 miles per hour in these five seconds will travel (almost uncontrollable!) 140 meters. And more than 80 percent of accidents in the same states occur when the driver is distracted from the road for just three seconds.



To combat lovers talk camera built into the dashboard, will monitor the eyes of the driver. As a rule, while driving, a person continuously scans the road, paying attention to signs, cars and holes. And during a conversation, the gaze is directed at one point - in front of you. The system works in three stages: first it takes a picture of the driver and cuts off the frame, leaving only the face and areas around it. The point is to find a hand raised to the face with a mobile. Then the system ‚Äúnotes‚Äù all the pixels of skin color and estimates the probability that the driver has a phone in his hand. Finally, the car issues a warning.



Testing was conducted using a camera with a resolution of 320 x 240. There were also unpleasant moments in the system: for example, if a bright light falls on the driver's face, there are difficulties in determining the desired skin color pixels.

It is easy to develop this topic a little bit and add automatic sending of messages to the road services. Unless, of course, the driver agrees to drive a car with such a system.
