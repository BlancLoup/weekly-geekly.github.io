Special economic zones: myths and / or reality?



For the third year already, our company is a resident of a special economic zone, and I want to share with all interested colleagues my opinion on building a business on this site.

Why such a topic? Many of our colleagues and partners ask us if it makes sense to become residents? I cannot answer this question, it all depends on the company's development goals, but to give information for reflection - always, please.

Details under the cut.

What is a special economic zone? For those who have not encountered this phrase, I will explain: special economic zones (SEZ) is a large-scale federal project aimed at developing regions by attracting direct Russian and foreign investments in high-tech industries, import-substituting production, shipbuilding and tourism. Special economic zones are divided into 4 types: industrial, tourist, technological and logistic.
