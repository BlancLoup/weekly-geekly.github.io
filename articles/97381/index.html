Thunderbird 3.1 released



Thunderbird 3.1 windows Russian , windows English

Details under the cut

- Improved initial setup - now all you need to provide is the name, e-mail address and password, and the new e-mail account setup wizard will send a request to our database and find these settings for you.



- Reminder about a forgotten attachment - the attachment reminder function searches for the word attachment (and other words like file types) in the message body and reminds you to add an attachment before sending the message.



- Tabs and search - the ability to work with letters in tabs and a new set of tools for quick search of mail messages. The new quick filter panel will help you to filter your mail even faster. Start typing words in the quick filter panel and search results will instantly appear in front of you.



If you think that you will need this letter in the future, but you want to remove it from the Inbox folder without deleting it, archive it! Selecting the Archive button or pressing the 'A' key will put your mail in the archive.



- Flexible setup of work with mail - with the help of Personas, easy "themes", you can instantly change the appearance of Thunderbird. Smart Folders helps you manage multiple email accounts by putting together special folders such as Inbox, Sent Mail or Archives. Instead of opening your inbox for each of your email accounts, you can see all your inboxes in the same inbox.

- You can search and install add-ons directly from Thunderbird. You no longer need to visit the add-ons website ‚Äî instead simply launch the add-ons manager.



- New manager saved files displays all the files you downloaded from the mail on your computer. New Migration Assistant gives Thunderbird 2 users a way to choose between using the new features of Thunderbird 3.1 and preserving the usual features and settings.



- Security and protection of your mail - popular anti-spam tools built into Thunderbird have been updated to combat the latest spam technologies. Every email you receive passes through Thunderbird modern anti-spam filters. Thunderbird 3.1 provides user privacy support and protection from external images. To ensure user privacy, Thunderbird 3.1 automatically blocks the loading of external images in email messages. Thunderbird protects you from fraudulent emails that attempt to lure their personal and confidential information from users, indicating that the message is a potential attempt at fraud. As a second line of defense, Thunderbird warns you when you click on a link that leads you to a site other than the one specified in the URL.
