Triumph Add to cart (GLONASS + GPS) public transport

All Novosibirsk transport (and minibuses, and buses, and trolley buses) equipped with satellite satellite coordinates and their subsequent broadcast. Now (as I learned from reading one of the LJ blogs) any owner of a laptop or netbook with Internet access can know about the proximity of public transport uninterruptedly - using a special card running on the Google Maps engine.

Thank God! Glory to Novosibirsk! Glory to Russia!

But this map site has a noticeable flaw: instead of updating only the position of the markers (via AJAX, for example), the author of this site, as far as I can see, programmed stupidly to update the entire map. With each update, the mosaic map (and not just the vehicle markers) is downloaded from the Google geodatabase again (again, as far as I can see), so that about a megabyte is otzhiraetsya. I saw with my own eyes that not just the markers move: it is the whole map that suddenly disappears, comes to its initial form (gray rectangle) and starts to swing again.

Therefore, for owners of very limited and (or) paid traffic on the mobile Internet (say, GPRS from any MTS without wholesale prices and discounts) such waste will not be acceptable, so I am sure.

Fortunately, this shortcoming is not an insurmountable one.
