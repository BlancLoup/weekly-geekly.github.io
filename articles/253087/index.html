Announcement of the course "Veeam Certified Engineer (VMCE) v8"

On 01-03 April 2015, an authorized course of Veeam Certified Engineer (VMCE) v8 will be held at the Training Center MUK at the address: Kyiv, ul. Donetsk, 16/2.

VMCE is a deep technical course that allows you to study in detail the functionality and capabilities of Veeam products.
VMCE certification - documentary evidence that the engineer has the necessary level of knowledge in the design, implementation and configuration of the Veeam software.

The Veeam Certified Engineer (VMCE) course is a 3-day in-depth technical course that is designed to comprehensively train engineers in the design, implementation, search optimization and troubleshooting of Veeam software products. As a result of training, resellers will be able to provide professional services, and customers will be able to increase the efficiency of investments in Veeam solutions, achieving significantly higher ROI and quality of service within the SLA (Service Level Agreement).

The VMCE course includes both theoretical and practical parts to provide students with the opportunity to become familiar with the technology and features of its configuration. In addition, every engineer can try to customize the system using realistic scenarios.

To register, please send an application to training@muk.com.ua specifying the name, position, e-mail, contact phone number, name and specialization of the company.
