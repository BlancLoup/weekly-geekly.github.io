Current market dynamics of flexible and curved displays according to analysts

If in January of this year, quite authoritative analysts from IHS did not dare to give an unequivocal answer about the prospects for the flexible and curved display market, even for 2016. Despite the interest shown by users, the skepticism of specialists was precisely due to the lack of functional displays with high-quality characteristics that could bend the way the user needed. The profile of those devices that are already on the market today cannot be changed at will. Analysts share a certain optimism in connection with the prospects for the development of the direction of flexible displays based on OLED matrices, including displays and TVs with curved screens, the growing demand for which became noticeable last year.

As for the curved display market, in 2015, according to IHS estimates, a total of 55 million devices with curved displays were sold for a total of about $ 2.4 billion and 99% of such devices have curved OLED displays.

In accordance with the forecast timed to the beginning of this year, the growth of the market for devices with flexible displays in the amount of $ 5.26 billion was planned for 2016. That is, the market will almost double, reaching 122 million devices. And in the coming years, sales in this segment will grow to 353 million units of products annually, reaching an average of $ 15.7 billion.

As you understand, when building their predictions, analysts from IHS were not guided by the opinion of the miniature Chinese startup Moxi, but by the sales data provided by the giants of the industry. And the change of the absolute leaders in the production of flexible OLED-displays today is not expected. Moreover, their plans include not only the expansion and restructuring of existing capacities, but also the construction of new factories entirely focused on this area. According to preliminary estimates, the volume of investments planned by each of the leading companies will amount to $ 7-8 billion.

Graphene based display

Graphene as the main material of the touch flexible display of promising Moxi smartphones was not chosen by chance. First, the specialists of the company have been studying the properties and capabilities of this material for quite some time. And secondly, it is graphene that has the unique combination of qualities that are necessary to create thin, fairly sensitive displays of increased flexibility. In addition to its valuable physical properties, graphene is extremely durable, light, has the necessary transparency to create high-quality images.



The prototype of the smartphone has already been created. The first batch of 100,000 smartphones will be sold in China this year. If the real demand confirms the expectations of the team, the production will be expanded and flexible smartphones with a graphene-based display will go beyond the limits of the Middle Kingdom.

Despite the increased interest of the curious public, it is necessary to clarify that the first batch will come out monochrome. And this, of course, is the fat minus of development. As Chunsheng Yu specified, the creation of a full-color flexible graphene panel in smartphones of a new generation is already underway, but the first models with color displays can be expected no earlier than 2018. And, in the light of the already emerging competition for the right of the first night in this segment, this date is, of course, not happy.
