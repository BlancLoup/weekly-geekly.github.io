Candid photos of Jennifer Lawrence and dozens of other celebrities leaked through iCloud

Personal photos of some celebrities, including Jennifer Lawrence, Kate Upton and Ariana Grande were posted by an anonymous hacker on 4Chan . Apparently, the leaks were made possible by hacking accounts from Apple iCloud cloud storage, into which information is automatically copied from all Apple devices, which most users do not even think about.

While we are not talking about some kind of global vulnerability in the service, it is possible that the girls were the victims of targeted attacks and each account was compromised separately, and the information was accumulated for a long time.
