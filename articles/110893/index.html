Plan4Year - Plan for 2011


The New Year is coming, the time when it is necessary to take stock of the outgoing year and make plans for the coming year.
Some advise to make a public blog post [1] , some print and hang on the wall [2] .
And I will suggest you to use a small Plan4Year service intended for drawing up plans for the coming year.

About the project, technical features and about the rest below.


about the project

Plan4Year is a mini-project designed to create plans for the next year. You can create up to 10 main tasks of the new year for each of which add 5 subtasks. If you are not ready to make a list of tasks, you can simply make a list of what you would like to achieve.
By making this list, you can open public access to it and give a link to friends.
Throughout the year, the service will send you a mail notification of scheduled and completed tasks (usually once a month).

Technical features

  • Project completed on the Yii Framework
  • Development time was about 40 hours
  • Page generation takes an average of 0.02 seconds and requires ~ 2 MB of memory.
  • The project is hosted by Amazon Web Services as a free package.
