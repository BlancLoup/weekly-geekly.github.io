FlexPod - from theory to practice: the word to engineers (webinar)

Surely many of you know or at least heard about such a solution as FlexPod - an architecture for data centers based on Cisco network and server equipment and NetApp storage systems, which makes it easy to move to a private cloud. We invite everyone to join the webinar dedicated to this decision, during which you will learn about the equipment on which it is built, as well as be able to evaluate the benefits of the implementation of FlexPod in your company.


During the webinar, engineers will review the server and switching equipment used in the FlexPod infrastructure, as well as talk about the new Netapp systems. In addition, you'll learn how to deploy a FlexPod solution using Infrastructure Automation in less than an hour

Webex will take place on February 2 at 11-00 am (MSK). To participate in the webinar, you must register by the link .

The link to the webex session will be sent on Wednesday, February 1.
