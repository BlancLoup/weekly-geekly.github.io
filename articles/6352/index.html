The action "Change people for gold" finished

On February 12, 2007, Belarusian News announced the start of a unique and unprecedented campaign, promising a gold bar to a website or blog that will be able to bring the maximum number of visitors to the Internet media site by the end of March.

March 31, after a month and a half, the contest ended. The final position of the participants is as follows (the number of attracted visitors is shown in brackets):
1. Blog Grodno S13 (5077) - gold bar of the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus (10 g).
2. cetki.com (4213) - silver measuring bar of the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus (50 g).
3. mikolka.info (823) - silver measuring bar of the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus (20 g).
