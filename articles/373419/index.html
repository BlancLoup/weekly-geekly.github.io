Today we will talk about one of those Xiaomi gadgets that are not synchronized with anything - the water tester.



Why is this useful?

Water salinity is one of the criteria for both water quality and classification. The study assesses the presence of solids. In the Western tradition, the term " Total dissolved solids " is used, the abbreviation of which gave the name to the Xiaomi gadget (like many others). Depending on the level (degree) of mineralization, it is customary to distinguish several types of water.

At the same time, a low level of water salinity is poor, as well as high. In some notes , it is noted that the systematic use of water with a low level of mineralization is capable of provoking in the long run heart, bone, etc. diseases.
