Geek Picnic: August 6, St. Petersburg, New Holland Island


On August 6 in St. Petersburg on the island of New Holland, we gather representatives of all IT communities. Our task is to pull everyone into the open air and create a comfortable environment for communication and exchange of ideas. On the island you can lie on the grass, sit by the fire, play volleyball and frisbee. There is a cafe and bar.

Picnic guide


12-00 - 16-00
  • Round table "Do you need clusters in Petersburg, and if so, which ones?"
  • Hackathon
17-00 - 20-00
  • Exhibition of startup projects
  • Lightning talks (5-minute speeches)
20-00 - 23-00
  • Bonfire, frisbee, volleyball.

Participation in an exhibition of startup projects


We invite to participate in a picnic all projects and objects of IT and near-IT topics. We need robots, quadcopters, lasers, 3D glasses, 3D printers, mobile applications, Kinect, development in the field of computer graphics, music and games. Send descriptions of your projects and the conditions of your participation Ilya Bolkhovsky .

The organizers


The organization of the picnic is coordinated by Yuri Lifshits and Ilya Bolkhovsky . We will welcome ideas and help in the organization.
