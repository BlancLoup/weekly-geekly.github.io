Salary of developers of different qualifications in general


Now let's see how the salaries of developers of different qualifications in general differ, without breaking down into programming languages.

Starting with an intern with a salary of 26,000 rubles. On average, the salary of each of the following qualifications grows as follows: June - 1.7 times (45,000 rubles), Middle - 1.9 (85,000 rubles), Senor - 1.6 (140,000 rubles), Lead - 1.1 times (160 000 rub.).
