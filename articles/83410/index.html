Swype - interesting keyboard for Android

Swype is an Android keyboard that makes typing fast and convenient, you do not need to type each character separately. You just drive by the right letters connecting them with their line and get the right word. Past versions of the program worked only on WVGA devices, such as NexusOne, and Motorola Droid. Now everyone can try this wonderful keyboard. The installation process and the principle of use can be viewed on the video.



I put a new keyboard "to try." What can I say? The first feeling is not familiar enough to drive a finger over the letters :) But after some training, the printing speed increases. At least the guy on the video, it turns out much faster than me.



The program has one minus so far - there is no support for the Russian language, while the assets of the developers are only English and Spanish.
