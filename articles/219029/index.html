The project of modular smartphone Project Ara is open to third-party developers.



The project of modular smartphone Project Ara, from Motorola Google, is developing more and more actively. Not so long ago, the project team announced that the smartphone should go on sale in 2015 . And now the project has been opened for third-party developers by publishing an 81-page Module Developers Kit (MDK).

In fact, the project team has opened a wide road to the project for third-party developers, in order to create a reliable and extensive ecosystem for their smartphone. "The success of the Ara project is based on a rich, vibrant and extensive ecosystem of modules from myriad developers," the project team said in a statement.
