Chris Sakka. Twitter experiences

Venture investor, one of the first Twitter investors, Chris Sakka, re-entered the Foras Midas list, regularly published by Forbes, this time in third place. How such a guy could achieve such a thing is unclear. He has neither technical nor business education. He does not know how to program, never opened a business and did not work in an investment fund. Previously, he worked at Google, but quitting, quickly broke into the investment ranks, being in the same boat with veterans of venture investments like John Doherr and Michael Moritz.

Against the backdrop of his investment feats, Chris often quarrels with everyone around.
