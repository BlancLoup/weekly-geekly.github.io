Well, I tell you, it was something with something! Not only that, instead of the originally declared 30 people, more came, so also those who came in the mass were well-versed in the subject matter, and literally flooded Peter with questions. The speaker could not get out of the standard presentation set and in the course actively went sketching on flipchart. He was able to fully appreciate Peter's knowledge of how he answered tricky questions from listeners with ease.
