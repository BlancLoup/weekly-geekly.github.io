Bitcoin rate is growing again: Paypal has allowed Bitcoin payments in US online stores

Paypal has been eyeing Bitcoins before, predicting that they will play an important role in the future of their business. Now, when the big players, from Overstock to Wikipedia, accept cryptocurrency as transactions and donations, Paypal has joined the game.



Starting with BitPay, Coinbase and GoCoin, PayPal will allow merchants to accept bitcoins as payment - but so far only in the United States.

PayPal will make transactions at the expense of the commission from the recipients of payments.

The cryptocurrency market reacted unequivocally to this: the bitcoin rate sharply went up , literally a couple of hours after the announcement, already jumping from a little less than $ 400 to $ 420
