Report by Alexey Bragin at the Russian Open Source Summit 2015

On March 16, 2015, the Russian Open Source Summit took place once again in Moscow.

ROSS 2015 was intended to highlight the qualitatively new role of Open Source as the main driver of the development of modern information technologies in the era of clouds, mobility and social networks. Only the ideology of Open Source today allows to put into practice the concept of a universal technological platform that ensures the interoperability of information systems and services of various manufacturers. Open Source projects of the OpenStack cloud operating platform, software-defined networks (SDN), container automation technologies for deploying and managing applications in a virtualization environment (Docker, Rocket, etc.) can serve as vivid examples.

During the event, the project coordinator Alexey Bragin made a report on the experience gained in the development of ReactOS.
