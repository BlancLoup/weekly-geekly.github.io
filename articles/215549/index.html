Robot Lucky at the contest "Miss Russia 2014"

When many beautiful girls gather in one place, every (beautiful and not very) man dreams of getting to this place! The remote robot presence of Webot from Wicron was fortunate enough to attend the Miss Russia 2014 contest, which is why we called him Lucky or in the foreign manner Mister Lucky .
Access to the robot was opened on the contest website from February 13 until the contest finals, which took place on March 1, 2014.

By controlling the robot, one could see the most hidden corners behind the curtains of the competition:

... visit the gym,



... see the work of stylists,


... and, of course, catwalk in swimsuits!


The robot also attended official events, for example, ...

... at the meeting of participants with Miss Russia 2013,


... at the defile competition,


... and at the dress rehearsal!


Avatar robot changed right before your eyes!


Selfie with our robot Webot - new trend of the season!


Webot closely followed the finals of the competition.
During the final performance, the robot stood on the stage next to the participants and allowed dozens of users to admire the Russian beauties.


And here she is, Miss Russia 2014 - Yulia Alipova!
