Sonic & SEGA All-Stars Racing



The other day I came across a demo version of Sonic & SEGA All-Stars Racing at the Sony Store, the essence of the toy in general is clear from the name - arcade racing "celebrities."
I once had a friend playing Mario Kart Wii . Here everything is the same: simple, fun and very exciting, but unlike the Wii, it is also in HD, though only 720p.
Some facts:
  • available on all platforms (PS3, Xbox 360, Wii, Nintendo DS, PC)
  • Release date: February 23rd in the USA, 26th in Europe and Russia
  • exclusive characters available on some platforms
  • network game is available (up to 8 players), there is also an opportunity to play together on one screen (split screen mode)

One track and two characters are available in the demo, the impressions of the gameplay remain very positive, the graphics are noticeably better than Mario Kart, but you could expect more.
Management is not very intricate: the cursor, accelerator, look back, glide, the use of active bonus. But the bonuses are very diverse, there are also unique to different characters: various kinds of means of destruction, acceleration, shields, flight capabilities, and so on.

The cost for the PS3 version in the US is $ 50, in the Czech Republic almost twice as expensive.
