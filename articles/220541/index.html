HP ProLiant Workshop with 65% discount - May 6-7 in Kiev

On April 17-18, on the basis of the MUK training center, for the first time, a unique seminar developed at TC MUK was held on the basic skills of configuring, managing, updating and maintaining HP ProLiant servers . The purpose of the seminar is to provide technicians and system administrators with knowledge and skills about HP ProLiant servers in the most concise, accessible and practically useful form. Participants (among them were representatives of partner companies of HP and financial organizations) received an excellent starting point for further training on servers and blades of HP . Just those where students are initially expected to have a high level of proficiency in the tools and technologies listed in the seminar program.


In the photo - the author of the course represents the use of servers.


Practical exercises were fully provided with HP ProLiant servers installed in the laboratories of the training center. Under the cat a detailed program and a discount on the seminar.
