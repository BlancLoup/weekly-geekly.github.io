Flying car tested in the Netherlands

The Dutch company Pal-V (short for Personal Air and Land Vehicle), which developed the prototype of a flying car, reports that its new development over the next three years will become a familiar sight on the Dutch roads.

A three-wheeled vehicle that turns like a motorcycle uses a technology called a "gyrocopter" to take off.

This morning, the car passed a test drive near the A1 in the Netherlands.
