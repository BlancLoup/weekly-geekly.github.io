Mumburum - music search engine

Hello! Surely you have had difficulty finding your favorite mp3-nis, right? Here I am too.

So here. Once, when my eyes were already sick with fatigue, and my fingers refused to regularly hit the keys, an idea came up to create a system that instead of me would go to work to find music on the Internet.

Having slept well, appreciating your biceps with experience and capabilities, having stocked up with invigorating coffee and after sitting comfortably, I hit the strained limbs on the keyboard. The code was written easily and quickly, the lines harmoniously put into beautiful functions. Days went by, followed by weeks.

And so, one frosty morning on Tuesday, the system was added. It was a simple search string with a ‚ÄúFind‚Äù button. From the previously registered chose the most appropriate domain mumburum.com , the name of the new site was determined. During the day I fed several hundred links to the thematic sites and the work went.

Two months passed, during which time the search and ranking system was significantly modernized, the project was clothed in modest design, equipped with a search for lyrics and fifty radio stations charts . Today there are more than a million tracks in the Mumburum index and at least 20 thousand are added every day. Scheduled forum. Everything is free and there is no need to register.

From what has not yet been implemented, but is planned for the near future:
  1. Composition bitrate and file size (ready, but have not yet found a place to output)
  2. Recycling the player to keep playing as you move through the pages
  3. The personal section of the user including playlists (spelled)
  4. Own forum (spelled)
  5. Search mp3 by words of songs
  6. Downloading playlists in m3u and zip-archive all files
  7. Openid

I am pleased to expand the list according to your wishes.
