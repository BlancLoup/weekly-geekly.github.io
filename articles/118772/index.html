Javascript master classes from Ilya Kantor in Ukraine

Last year, Ilya Kantor ( http://javascript.ru ), a professional in the field of JavaScript, conducted master classes with us.

The themes of his master classes were professional JavaScript-programming, AJAX / COMET, jQuery. Since then, we have received a lot of questions about whether such master classes are planned yet and when.

This year we will again organize master classes, and invite Kiev developers to take part in them.

Master classes will be held in Kiev on May 17-18, in our wonderful hall on the 20th floor.

In addition to Kiev, they will be this year in Kharkiv , Lviv and Kherson .
More detailed information about master classes is available on the host's website http://javascript.ru/mk .

Briefly about the masterclasses:


Master class "Professional javascript programming"
This master class is about what every developer should know.
It is dedicated to professional development, tools that make life easier, and development - easier, as well as numerous features and javascript features.

Master class "Complex AJAX and COMET: subtleties of Web 2.0"
AJAX is not just an XMLHttpRequest request. This is the basis for building services, including support for History and Back / Forward buttons and managing asynchronous events. These are client and server multithreading. This is COMET technology and the future of the Web.
The master class will make modern approaches simple and clear for you, will improve existing applications and move on to a qualitatively new development in the future.

Client Optimization Workshop
How to speed up the page, doing almost nothing. How to speed up the page and site seriously if you do something ;-)

Master class "Secrets of jQuery"
The master class is devoted to the internal features of the jQuery framework and the creation of widgets with jQuery UI.
This is not an introductory course. It is for those who already use jQuery, but want to do it more efficiently. The information presented at the master class allows you to get a deeper insight into the capabilities and operation of the framework.

Ilya considers all topics in detail, with a focus on non-trivial things and features of technical solutions that come with experience.

Participation in master classes is paid, the cost at the moment - 350 UAH, when writing for all 4 master classes - 10% discount. More detailed information - on the site of the leader, on page http://javascript.ru/mk , when choosing the city of Kiev.
