Welcome to Odessa at WebCamp 2016! Sea, relaxation and favorite technologies



From July 29 to July 31, the WebCamp conference will be held in Odessa for the 7th time, which is dedicated to web development technologies, project management and business development. Traditionally, the event consists of several directions. This year it is: Front-End , Python , PHP , DevOps , PM , BizDev .

WebCamp 2016 will bring together more than 600 participants from all over Ukraine and the near abroad. The conference is designed for Middle / Senior level participants, where everyone will have the opportunity to share experiences, share their ideas, have a great time with colleagues on the after-party and relax around the Black Sea.

Our FlyElephant team is now actively working on finalizing the conference program and you still have a chance to join us. Representatives of such companies as Microsoft, Wargaming, Looksery, Upwork, Provectus, Ciklum, DataArt, Zillion Whales, SoftServe, YouScan, WebbyLab, Coderiver, CartFresh, RevJet, FlyElephant, Netpeak, DataRobot, KeepSolid, VertaMedia and others will make presentations.

Agenda


July 29:
  • WebCamp: Front-end - Experts with unique experience will present narrow technical topics and share the latest innovations in front-end development.
  • WebCamp: DevOps - Leading DevOps experts will share practical examples of how to build the development process using the DevOps methodology and the features of the components that are used within their infrastructures.

July 30:
  • WebCamp: PM - Project managers will discuss the subtleties of methodologies, team conflict resolution, motivation and effective planning, customer-manager-team interactions, share cases and interesting experiences.
  • WebCamp: Python - Experts will discuss news on web development in Python, examples of solving non-standard problems, architecture of high-load services, interesting libraries, cases, frameworks and best-practice.

July 31:
  • WebCamp: BizDev - Experts will share the secrets of working in foreign markets and
    on practical examples they will tell about the organization of an effective business for working with B2B and B2C clients.
  • WebCamp: PHP - Web developers will talk about PHP 5 and 7, popular frameworks, code quality, trends and interesting cases.

You can follow the program updates on the conference website or on Facebook and Twitter pages. The official hashtag is #webcampodessa.

Registration and all details on the conference website . Come as a team and get a discount of up to 25% (for readers of our blog there is a 10% discount code: FlyElephantHabrahabr).
