I want to be a freelancer. No, you do not want!

Of the 50 people starting freelancing, only 1 remains in it for more than 2 years.


Unfortunately, whatever the global statistics on the growth trend of purebred freelancers is not present, so I used statistics based on my own observations. And I would like to share my thoughts on this topic.


Let's start with the earliest freelancers (those that appeared 5 years ago, at the dawn of this phenomenon). These brave conquistadors had clear goals in front of them: Down with the stupid directorate, giving you creative freedom, more money, and a sense of independence. According to them, the image of freelancing as a work and as a lifestyle, they were his live advertising. It was looking at them, reading about their life, about how wonderful it was in freelancing, the rest followed. But the paradox of freelancing is like a good, even beautiful and, in general, paradise, and only a few are serious about it. Why? Here, of course, we can talk about difficulties, that freelancing is not for everyone, and it accepts only strong, purposeful and blah blah blah ... It is possible, but I will not. Because this is not the case, or rather, not only this ...

Take, for example, a dozen people. Boys or girls are not the essence, let them be both. And ask them, for example: "Do you want a beautiful sports figure?". Here everyone will nod in unison.
