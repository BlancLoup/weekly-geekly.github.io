F1 2010 from Codemasters


At the recent Gamescom exhibition, Codemasters finally revealed the details of its new F1 2010 game.
The release is scheduled for September 24 for the PlayStation 3, Xbox 360 and PC.
The game has an official FOM license. The game will feature all the teams, tracks and riders acting in the championship in the season 2010. In addition, fans of computer simulators will already be able to chase Silverstone in an updated configuration, as well as the new Korean Grand Prix circuit.

In the game you can walk on the paddock:
  • David Croft (BBC commentator) will ask you questions after the race.
  • There will be press conferences
  • Faces of pilots look photorealistic
  • Pilot managers - generated characters
  • Full control over the management on the pitstop


There will be a variety of game modes such as the Grand Prix, Championship, career, time trial. Also promise us an interesting multiplayer.

And here is a more detailed list of game features:
  • There will be modes of yellow, red and white flags.
  • Strategy will have a key role
  • You can change the strategy of pitstop
  • Full radio with the team
  • Dynamic wear rubber and fuel
  • Fines and penalties
  • Dynamic weather change
  • Celebration of victory on the podium
  • Engine wear
  • Changing the angle of attack splashes
  • Helmet design change
  • Change steering parameters
  • Closed park


At the exhibition GamesCom many gameplay videos were shown, you can find them on YouTube . I liked this one .
I also advise you to look at the trailer .

Of course, it was not done without stones, such as the lack of a safety car , a warm-up lap , etc ... However, we must remember that this is Codemasters first experience in developing a game of this level. They promised to take into account and eliminate all the shortcomings of the game in 2010 in the next game.
