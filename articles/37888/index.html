TinyWM - a different WebMoney Keeper

Some time ago I already acquainted Habr with my project - TinyWM (https://www.tinywm.com).
I am glad that I still had time to develop and improve it.

So, what's new.

WebMoney Keeper features
Transfer of funds, invoicing, replenishment of ATM-cards - without any special external changes.
The main page displays useful information: a balance, whether there are new transactions or unpaid bills, a link to my purchases (for more, see Digital Purchasing <a/>), as well as shortcuts to the main menu items.




Payment for services
The list of services has expanded (you can pay for everything on telepay.wmtransfer.com ) - 119 service providers are now available.



After a successful payment, payment parameters can be saved to a template, and it is very convenient to re-pay from templates.
Simply click on the desired template, change the amount (if necessary) and click OK.



Buying digital goods
Products from stores based on www.digiseller.ru ( www.plati.ru , www.publicant.ru , etc.) can be bought in one click (not all products, but only those sellers who are on the list of trusted).

Those who made purchases in these stores for webmoney can support me: you can make a purchase from your mobile device with that ears, which is accessible only to the most advanced users (you need to use enum , but alas, there is no client for the iPhone).
