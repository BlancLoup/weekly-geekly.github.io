Uber launches UberEats food delivery service in Russia



Uber launches its own food delivery service called UberEats in Russia, Vedomosti reports . UberEats will work as a separate UberEats application. According to the publication for today, February 15, scheduled presentation of the new service.

UberEats was launched in 2014 in the US and is already successfully operating in 20 countries around the world. For example, in New York, the service cooperates with hundreds of cafes and restaurants, and the average delivery time is 30 minutes. UberEats operates in many European capitals, Southeast Asia and South Africa.

Uber did not deliver food from a good life. The company is bogged down in debt and is now eating the money provided by investors. Thus, only in the third quarter of 2016, Uber's net loss amounted to $ 800 million. To keep afloat, the company invests in the development of robotic vehicles, and also searches for new markets. One of them - the market for the delivery of food from cafes and restaurants.

This direction is considered promising in Uber, and company representatives are hoping for an early expansion of the service‚Äôs geography. Testing service began last week in Moscow.

Liberal Uber tariffs can not be called. For establishments that have connected before February 1, the Uber commission for delivery will be 30% of the order amount, for those who connect later - 35%. At the first stage, UberEats plans to operate within the Garden Ring and in the North-West of the capital. One of the first major customers of UberEats will be the Burger King fast-food chain and the Shokoladnitsa coffee chain.

The pricing mechanism for delivery is not yet clear, but it is worth noting that this market has long been divided between other market players. In addition, many institutions have their own delivery services and are unlikely to agree to the enslaving conditions of UberEats.
