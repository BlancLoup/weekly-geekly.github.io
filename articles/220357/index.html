People often ask me: "How did it all begin?" I will try to briefly tell in our first post.

Idea

First customer

The first 5 years were spent studying the subject area and finding sources of investment. Fortunately, life has once again pushed me with a good man, Andrei Fedorov (one of the founders of Digital Design ), who supported me in this endeavor.


Andrey Fedorov (on the left) and I at the Docflow 2005 exhibition

By some incredible coincidences of circumstances, we received an order to develop a full-fledged corporate platform of electronic document management system for a very large customer, from scratch. Docsvision Birthday

These were probably the most fun 7 months in my life! During the project, our very single developer received an invitation from Microsoft and left to develop a SQL server. But, despite all the difficulties, exactly on December 31, 1999, the first version of Docsvision 1.0 was launched into commercial operation at as many as 16 workplaces.


The interface of one of the first versions of Docsvision

Docsvision today

Currently released version Docsvision 5.2, and now it is a powerful platform for creating various solutions for managing documents, tasks and processes. The platform contains a number of tools for creating such solutions: a set of designers, an open API for refinement. We use all these tools ourselves, and also provide to our partners and clients (partners - for free). And, as I have already said, we ourselves do not introduce our product, becoming one of the few Russian companies that have abandoned design practice in order to concentrate on producing the most convenient platform for our integrating partners. This required a tremendous effort from us to transform both our business model and the product manufacturing process. And the process has not yet come to an end, since paperless technologies are developing very quickly and more and more penetrate into our life - the directors are already signing electronic documents on tablets, and any citizen can contact the state through a website on the Internet.

By the way, according to IDC, the market for electronic document management systems (EDMS) is growing by 23% annually, with a software market growth of 15% per year.
