The first interactive online series

About the series:

The series "Immersion" - the first series, whose plot develops depending on the desire of the viewer. Management is done by simple mouse clicks on video objects. For simplicity, at the moment the clickable video object appears, a red indicator is displayed, clicking on which will turn on the pointer of all interactive objects.

To view the series requires installed Silverlight from Microsoft.



As part of the series, the viewer can choose which next action the hero will take on his way. In the course of the development of the series, the viewer will be given hints (for example, the phone number in the second series, by calling which you can get brief instructions on the next step of the hero).

Also, the most attentive viewers, having solved the secrets of some clues, will be able to influence the scenarios of the subsequent series.

Briefly about the storyline:

Waking up in the morning, the hero finds himself in an unfamiliar apartment with a severe headache and strange visions from sleep. In the course of the series he will have to figure out what happened in the past, along the way ... surviving. You can learn more about the plot of the series only by viewing it .

Enjoy your viewing and may not leave you a good reaction.
