The site sales blackfriday2013 not made the flow of those who want to get freebies

Obviously, the marketing preparation of the project turned out to be stronger than the technical one - in other words, all polymers were prodapted. Most of the time, instead of the site hung a CDN stub www.cloudflare.com


An illustration to the story of how Russian love for freebies has run into Russian.

About 3 nights, an announcement appeared on it that immediately after launching about one million people broke into the site at the same time, so the organizers expand the consciousness of server capacity, and the launch is postponed until 8 am.
