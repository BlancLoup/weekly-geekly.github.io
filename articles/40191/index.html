Golden Telecom, Corbin and Beeline merge into one brand

It is hardly a secret for someone that the provider Golden Telecom, which owns the Corbina brand, was bought by VimpelCom in December last year. However, until recently, all these three brands practically did not overlap with each other.

By the end of this year, according to the Vedomosti newspaper, only one brand will remain - Beeline. The services of Golden Telecom will now be known as Beeline Business, and what name will be chosen for Corbina is not yet known. By the way, in Moscow and St. Petersburg, the Korbina brand will last somewhat longer than in other regions.

In addition to combining names, integration will bring the opportunity to pay for all services using a standard Beeline payment card, and subsequently all three accounts will be merged into one.
