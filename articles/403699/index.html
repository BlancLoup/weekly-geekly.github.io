Smart home systems




Perhaps the camera for home surveillance - this is one of the first functions of a smart home. For example, someone calls you at the door, the camera responds and records, and then sends you an event notification.

Among our customers there are several companies involved in smart home technology. For example, in the USA there is a team that develops a system that allows you to open a garage or a separate room, passing one-time generated code access to third parties.

Your home itself will take the parcel for you while you are away: the courier arrives with cargo, receives a one-time code that opens the garage, puts the parcel there, after which the door closes and is locked.

This system lacked the most important thing - video surveillance. What if the courier steals a bike from the garage? Ideally, it was necessary to see that the courier came, took the package and left, without taking anything with him. To do this, you can publish the event in the application directly at the right time, and at the same time record a video. You can also keep a live broadcast all the time right in the mobile application. Such a system is easy to implement with the help of a suitable microcontroller and any camera with Ivideon on board.

Last year, OBLO Living , a home automation company that developed systems based on the ZigBee and Z-wave standards, connected to Ivideon. Devices operating under these protocols can become repeaters, that is, transmit information independently of the controller up to a distance of 25 meters.

OBLO offers a client application for Android / iOS and cloud services (including Ivideon) in combination with its own home devices (central gateway, gate opening sensor, sockets, sensors for doors and windows, rheostat, water leakage sensor, etc. ). The OBLO gateway controls lighting, security systems, power consumption and other aspects of a home automation system.

OBLO, like Ivideon, supports the IF This Then That service (IFTTT). The central gateway combines ZigBee, Z-Wave, and IP home automation protocols, using NFC to quickly connect various devices. It is able to control and interact with all Z-Wave devices, and this is more than 1,700 Z-Wave products (switches, temperature sensors, electronic blinds and many other devices).

The company provides service to telecom operators who want to introduce the ‚Äúsmart home‚Äù service by their smart home subscribers. Having a mobile SDK client allows third-party developers to create applications for integration with OBLO solutions.

With the help of Ivideon, OBLO users can monitor home security and access the camera, watch live broadcasts or archive recordings (in unlimited amounts).



Resident of the Skolkovo IT cluster, Iridium ( iRidium ) plans to provide Ivideon support in its application for automation systems and IoT devices. The product is designed to combine disparate controlled devices into a single automated system with centralized management. GuardSaaS, an online service for access control and time tracking, integrated with Ivideon. What happened: the integration provides the ability to quickly view the video, tied to events at a place and time directly from GuardSaaS. At the same time, GuardSaaS does not connect to cameras directly and does not store video on its server, but receives a video stream directly from a video surveillance system. It turns out quickly, simply and securely record such incidents as:

There are all the standard features of any camera with the Ivideon service - viewing on a smartphone / tablet, an encrypted connection (excludes the invasion of perverts, spying on children), IR illumination, voice communication, etc. But there are differences added to the application:

iSKI - application for skiers and snowboarders




The program group with the prefix "iSKI" includes individual applications for almost all European ski countries: iSKI Austria, iSKI Swiss, iSKI France, iSKI Italia (Czech, Slovakia, Suomi, Deutschland, Slovenija and more). iSKI show not only the condition of the snow, but also a list of restaurants in the mountains and a scheme of resorts, as well as other useful information - useful when you need to quickly find the phone number of a ski school or something like that.

For Russia, too, there is an application that even includes miniature ski points of the country with one or two tracks. iSKI offers a number of sections where you can find out the weather for the near future in the valley and the mountains, the condition of the snow cover, including data on artificial snow, to see data on the work of the tracks and lifts. You are also given information about equipment rental and its cost. The map of the ski resort is attached, which you can save to your phone. There is a functional GPS-tracking descent from the mountain. At the same time, access to the Internet is not needed - it works in offline mode (except for broadcasting from cameras). All applications are free.

Now, almost every ski resort is equipped with a camera showing the situation on the slope. To view the cameras remotely through the application, we provided iSKI with our SDK, and now everyone can see through the application not only the weather forecast, the thickness of the snow cover and the number of open lifts, but also video directly from the slope.
