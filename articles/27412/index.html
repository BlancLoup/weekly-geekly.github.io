Opera 10 Concept

At the request of rendom.habrahabr.ru , for his own authorship.

I made this presentation of my design a year ago. Since I did not like the Opera GUI since version 8.


Of course, in such a short clip I could not fit all the ideas, partly because it took too much time to implement them in a flash, and I worked on it naturally in my free time. Think of it not as a final result but as a kind of prototype in which I tried to demonstrate some ideas. I was hoping that with the release of version 9.5 in the opera, something would change in the GUI area for the better, but it seemed to be hoping in vain. Therefore, I decided to lay out my design in public, at least so that he would not just die in oblivion.

I apologize for the possible excessive pathos in the video
