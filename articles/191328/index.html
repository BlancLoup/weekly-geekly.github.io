It looks like Mt. Bitcoin exchanger. Gox financial problems are deeper than thought. The fact is that previously the US government withdrew about $ 2.9 million from Dwolla accounts. It happened a long time ago. But the other day it became known that US officials also seized 2.1 million US dollars from the Wells Fargo account. All this is done according to the law, since the Mt. Gox was unable to register in the United States as an organization that deals with money transfers.

Back in May, the court ruled that the exchange still works with real money, and it does not matter whether it is paper bills (well, or metal coins), or a virtual monetary unit. And although after a couple of weeks, work with USD has resumed, problems in transactions with this currency still arise.

In general, the explanation is simple. The fact is that with the withdrawal of $ 5 million, the company simply does not have enough USD for all exchange operations.
