Idea! Let's create a university ranking together



We have a great idea! There are a lot of students, youth and universities on Megamind, and what if together we form an open rating of educational institutions based on the real achievements of students, which we take from social. network. The rating will be formed for the second time ; last year, about 50 Russian universities joined the rating. Its formation was supported locally, for example, in the Altai Territory, the rating was supported by the regional office of the Russian Youth Union .

This rating will be based on a non-standard approach, which, of course, will attract the attention of the media and the public. It will allow to look at higher education institutions somewhat from the other side, and also will allow to draw positive attention to higher education institutions, regardless of their material base, federal status, etc.

The place in the ranking will be calculated on the basis of educational and non-educational achievements of the users of the educational institution, which they uploaded to the social network of LevelPride students. The list of all students of the university and access to the achievements published by the student is free and open. This allows you to achieve transparency and look at the university through the prism of real achievements of students.
To get into the rating, you must register at least one student from the university in the levelpride.com system and join the group with the name of the educational institution.

Instruction
To increase the position of the university in the ranking, students from this university should:
1. register in the system at levelpride.com
2. join the group with the name of your school
3. bring all your achievements into the system

Moderation of achievements, carried out by the participants of the system, is effective and does not allow unreliable achievements to enter the system. However, since the rating is based on subjective data, it will be informative and informative. We believe that the rating will be useful in the framework of non-standard assessment, self-examination of universities, identifying talented students and broadcast their achievements to future employers.
According to the results of the formation of the rating, all universities that took part will be provided with a full unloading of data on students in the university, as well as intra-university ratings in various fields of activity of students. Unloading available on request.

results
Data collection for the formation of the rating is carried out until December 31, 2015. The publication of the rating will be timed to the Day of Russian students.
