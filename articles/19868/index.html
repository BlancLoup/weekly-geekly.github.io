embarrassment with agave

It so happened that at the end of 2007 I was freelancing and made one site. plain. business card. everything is placed on agave.

Customers gave me access to FTP, and said that the tariff plan does not support any database. I checked if php works - it turned out to work. go to the site of the agave, see if there is such a TP - without sql and with php, I did not guess.

I wrote a simple scriptbook that collected pages from text files so that later it was more convenient to add new materials.

Today customers are calling - the site has broken, nothing works - error 403, panic, screams.

after long remembering the password and the letter in those. support, I, under pressure, decided to call and not wait for an answer. answered quickly. and quickly found out that php support was turned on randomly on the selected tariff plan, and now it has been turned off, as it should be.
