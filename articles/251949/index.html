Dell PowerEdge Solutions for Movie Content Provider

On the pages of our blog, we talk about cooperation with a variety of companies and organizations - from local online stores to world-famous universities. Alas, the majority of such cases describe working with foreign partners, but sometimes there are interesting examples in Russia. Today is such a case. The hero of our new story is the company CineLAB , which is engaged in the rental of film-making equipment, film processing, digital post-production, satellite delivery of video content and many other interesting tasks in the "cinema" world.



CineLAB delivers digital content to hundreds of cinemas across Russia. The company sends data via satellite communication to its server located in the client‚Äôs cinema, and only then this data is transferred to the client video server for broadcast. Needless to say, CineLAB equipment should always be in working condition - otherwise the company runs the risk of permanently spoiling relations with a partner. Any instability can lead to a breach of contract and huge losses, and in the longer term - damaged reputation and weakening of positions in the market. The head of CineLAB's IT department, Anton Torkunov, admits that the company has a very balanced approach to the choice of providers of server solutions - too much depends on them.

When it was time to upgrade the IT infrastructure to CineLAB, Anton decided to seek help from Dell. Both companies have previously collaborated and even deployed Dell PowerEdge server solutions in Russian cinemas. The productivity of the machines and the service support of Dell Anton and his team were completely satisfactory, but it was necessary to get acquainted with the proposals of competitors. There were not so many of them: the only sensible alternative was the Supermicro solution, but after analyzing the relationship between performance and price, the choice was obvious. As a result, the IT department of CineLAB stopped at Dell servers.

The equipment, which was installed in Russian cinemas, included Dell PowerEdge R520 and R580 servers with Intel Xeon processors. They fully provided all the needs of client cinemas, had sufficient performance and had all the necessary options. The PowerEdge R520 guaranteed excess input and output bandwidth for demanding applications, and the PowerEdge R580 offered up to 3 TB of scalable memory. Today, CineLAB continues to expand data transfer services - and not without the help of Dell technology. The market is aware of the high quality of CineLAB services and the performance of their servers that store the data transmitted via satellite.
