Issuing Yandex on commercial low-frequency queries is a list of doorways. Let me give you the same example as a year ago, the request ‚Äú Sberbank Surgut consumer credit ‚Äù - the situation has not changed. This request is in the base of low-frequency credit requests from doorway vendors.

The easiest method to burn everything on the vine. No affiliate - no doorway. But then there will not be a number of "good sites". The market will become boring and monopolized, and the common man will find it harder to succeed. Road builders will go to social networks and other sources of traffic.
