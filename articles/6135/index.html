Translation of guidance on making profit from free and open projects

Published a Russian translation of guidelines for making a profit from free and open projects. The original author is John Andrews (John Andrews), the developer of Damn Small Linux.

The text describes various ways of making a profit from an open project. The author sets a goal to teach the reader to live, doing only the development of its Open Source project and not starve.
