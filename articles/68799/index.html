Skype <-> SIP gateway for SIPNET users

SIPNET starts testing a new service ‚ÄúCall from SIPNET to Skype and back‚Äù.

SIPNET users of the open standard SIP and proprietary Skype can now call each other for free.
All connections between SIPNET and Skype subscribers are not charged.

Free call from SIPNET to a Skype subscriber:
To call a Skype contact from the SIPNET network, enter the address in the format user@skype.sipnet.ru (or user@skype.com). If your SIP terminal (program or SIP phone) supports digital input only, enter this address in your phonebook and use speed dial codes - * XX.

On the Skype network, the gateway for accessing SIPNET has an identifier skype.sipnet.ru. Advise a Skype user to pre-add this name to his contact list (including feedback).

Transfer of text messages, files and videos is not supported.
Skype OUT is not supported.

Free Skype Call to SIPNET Subscriber:
To call SIPNET contacts from the Skype network, add skype.sipnet.ru to the Skype contact list. To call a SIPNET subscriber, select skype.sipnet.ru from the Skype contact list and press the "Call" key. After the voice greeting system, click on the button "Add a phone number" and enter the digital SIP ID of the called subscriber.

Skype 3.x version allows you to dial numbers from the keyboard immediately after the connection is established. In the Skype 4.x version, after establishing the connection, you should additionally call the dialer. To do this, press the key (triangle) to the right of the volume control and in the drop-down menu, click on the ‚ÄúSet‚Äù section and enter the digital SIP ID of the called subscriber.

Details can be found on the page http://www.sipnet.ru/call/skype.html
