Idea


In connection with these observations, an idea arose of how to improve the situation with roads in the country.

There is an opportunity to send a message about traffic violations

But it is rather difficult to do this, as it is necessary:

  1. Pull out the camera and take a picture on the phone, which when driving a vehicle is at least inconvenient and can also lead to another accident;
  2. Remove the USB flash drive from the video recording camera on the car, insert it into the computer, find the video you need, edit it in order not to send 5 minutes of recording - compress the video file;
  3. Make a message about the accident by attaching a video or snapshot.

It will take a lot of time, and if you want to do video fixing all the time, it puts a fat point on this issue, because it will take you hours of time each time just to make a request.

How to solve the issue quickly and easily: use a ready-made tool for this

Tool


- from the state the structures conclude a contract (so that all this is by law);
- a website is created on which each motorist can register by entering his personal data (preferably with a passport confirmation, etc.) + license + vehicle number;
- a special camera, pre-created by the manufacturer, is purchased, certified camera in the car (based on ordinary cameras, but with modifications) and installed in the car;
- in order not to be distracted from the traffic situation, a separate button is also installed to send data to the dashboard or to the steering wheel if the button is wireless;
- as soon as the driver noticed a violation of traffic rules, he presses the button and the device cuts the last minute of the video recording, converts it into the required format and sends it indicating the driver's id to the video-fixing server.

Technical implementation


- A camera is taken that can shoot video;
- is being finalized before shooting in high resolution so that the numbers of the intruder are visible;
- SIM card or Wi-Fi support is being finalized (a SIM card is needed so that you can send video right away when you press a button, Wi-Fi so that the video is sent, for example, when a car drives to a house: the camera catches Wi-Fi and sends the accumulated video data when connected to the Internet);
- a special software that allows you to find out the id of the camera and associate it with the driver data, as well as update the camera software;
- a site where you can register by entering your data and camera id and get information about the application (how many accidents are recorded, how much money was received, etc.);
