Tvil.ru - Search for accommodation for recreation and travel in Russia for the summer holidays

Hooray! Summer is coming soon! And we with the whole Tvil.ru team want to congratulate the habrasoobschestvo and start-up entrepreneurs with this long-awaited event.

The last days of May and the sun make us look forward to planning a quick vacation. And not everyone will besiege travel agencies in an attempt to snatch hot vouchers to foreign seas or to the Russian Black Sea coast. Yes, and a summer vacation resort tempts not all. Many prefer to rest without vouchers and spa cards. And the main problem is housing.

Especially for those who do not like the sanatorium daily routine, large and expensive crowded hotels and our website was created, because it offers mini-hotels, apartments for rent, hostels, guest houses and cottages for recreation.

By registering on tvil.ru (or logging in with a social network account), a user will be able to fully plan his vacation at any time of the year. Descriptions of cities, beautiful photos, information about attractions, excursions, transport, cafes, restaurants and places for entertainment will help you choose what you need.

Owners of houses, hotels and hotels can independently offer their object on the site. After a thorough check, the accommodation will be available not only for booking directly on the site, but also for reviews of guests who have already visited. The rating system will also play for the benefit of both homeowners and users.




Soon the owners will be able to create a kind of calendar of availability for one or another period of time. This will help eliminate unnecessary calls and make it easier for vacationers to search for free housing in the selected city.

Despite the fact that the site was launched only three months ago, more than 3,500 offers of objects from different parts of Russia have already been posted on it, 1,500 of them are intended for holidays on the Black Sea, which is important on the eve of summer holidays.
