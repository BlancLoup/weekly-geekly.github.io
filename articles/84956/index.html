Sony Ericsson X10 Mini video in Russian

The first video appeared in the network in Russian, about the smallest Android - Sony Ericsson X10 Mini.
The price in Russia is 12-13 rubles. Will appear in May.
Sony Ericsson X10 Mini Pro is exactly the same device, only with QWERTY, will cost 1000-1500 p. expensive.


  • Operating system: Google Android 1.6
  • Screen: 2.55 inch TFT touch screen, 240 x 320 pixels (QVGA), support for 16M colors
  • Camera: 5-megapixel with autofocus, geo-tagging, flash
  • Keyboard: QWERTY (in X10 mini pro model)
  • Processor: 600 MHz Qualcomm MSM7227
  • Communications: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth (A2DP), Micro USB
  • FM radio, GPS, A-GPS
  • Memory: up to 128 MB
  • Memory card support: microSD, up to 16 GB
  • Headphone jack: 3.5 mm
  • Talk time (GSM / GPRS standard): up to 4 hours
  • Standby time (GSM / GPRS standard): up to 285 hours
  • Talk time (UMTS standard): up to 3.5 hours
  • Standby time (UMTS standard): up to 360 hours
  • Dimensions (Xperia X10 mini): 83 x 50 x 16 mm
  • Size (Xperia X10 mini pro): 90 x 52 x 17 mm
  • Weight (Xperia X10 mini): 88 grams
  • Weight (Xperia X10 mini pro): 120 grams
