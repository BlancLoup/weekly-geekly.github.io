The best admin - invisible admin


Today, throughout the world, there is a clear tendency towards the virtualization of many life, business and other processes. As a person close to the IT field, I like this trend very, very much. Personally for myself, I see in this completely new opportunities for work, new perspectives and a banal comfort from the fact that I can work wherever there is internet. Though on the beach in Sharm-Al-Sheikh. Work tools - laptop and portable fridge with beer.

Surprisingly, but today I can already regard it not as the dream of all admins about a bright future, but as a very real task for the coming year.

Because this thought made possible the pilot project HelpMeIn (everyone can get acquainted with it on the site of the same name). The project is still open, I invite everyone to cooperate. Any help, criticism, advice, in general, all constructive thoughts are welcome.

So, the main objectives of the project:

1. In practice, introduce a new work format for the incoming administrator, prove that the idea has the right to life and remote technical support is not inferior to the traditional one.

2. To make a powerful on-line service out of the pilot project that brings good profit.

From this we will make a start.

How it works?

Thanks to such services as LogMeIn, it became possible to get remote access for computer administration without much difficulty, without using anything other than a regular browser. The client only needs to be informed that he is not working and indicate a convenient time for him to complete the task (for example, at night when there is no one in the office). Convenient, isn't it?

For example, to make a regular system administrator work at night, you need to put a lot of effort and even more finance. Such they are capricious.

Setting up software, virus treatment, all kinds of prevention, assistance in the work and training of company employees, etc. etc. All this and much more can and should be done remotely without interrupting the company's main workflow. The system has long been tested, run-in, does not require equipment costs. All you need is an Internet channel with 128 kilobits per second. And this is no longer a problem.

Fortunately for me, there were brave people who went to the experiment. Agreed like this: I try to perform all tasks to the maximum remotely. Departure to the office only in case of emergency. And oddly enough, the semiannual practice of such service speaks for itself unequivocally - the customer is satisfied. As a result, in these offices I appear 2 times a month: visually once to see if everything is in order, and a second time at a salary. What and all I wish)
