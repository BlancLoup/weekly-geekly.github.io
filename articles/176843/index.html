Video: Samsung ATIV Smart PC XE700T Tablet Review



Samsung ATIV Smart PC XE700T is a tablet based on Windows 8 and third-generation low-voltage Intel Core processors. The tablets are equipped with touchscreen PLS-displays with a resolution of 1920x1080, have onboard 4 GB of RAM and solid-state drives with capacities from 64 to 128 GB. A set of ports includes: one USB 3.0 port, micro HDMI and a card reader.

In addition to the tablet itself, it also includes a keyboard dock with a touchpad and two USB 2.0 ports, as well as a Wacom touch sensitive stylus.
