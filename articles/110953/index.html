What is Kopini?


Strangely enough, but only after 4 months of working on the project, we finally realized what a service it is and what value it actually carries to customers. So, Kopini is NOT a collection of ideas . This is a collective knowledge base, a support forum and a way to organize effective feedback in one bottle. Collecting ideas is only a small part of the feedback. We want to make our service a universal means of productive communication of companies with their clients on the Internet.

Kopini allows you to improve the quality of customer service and reduce support costs, increase customer loyalty and attract new ones, receive effective feedback and be closer to customers . This is what distinguishes us from analogs.

Main features


I will briefly talk about the main features and capabilities that have appeared since the start. It makes no sense to consider trivialities, for a couple of months there were dozens of improvements.

Integration into design and transparent authorization allows you to fully integrate the community into the client's infrastructure. FAQ


Here are collected questions that are most often asked to us and which, frankly, are fed up =) If there are more, welcome in the comments.
How are we different from Reformal and analogues? Friends, understand, we are completely different, although at first glance they are very similar. Reformal is a service of collecting ideas, their main goal is to improve your website (as they themselves say). Kopini is a way of effective interaction between customers and company representatives, our main goal is to get maximum benefit from communication (customer service, loyalty, reduction of support costs, etc.)

Why are we paid? It's simple. Kopini is our only project, it is our brainchild to whom we give ourselves completely. All team members work full time. This allows us to develop by leaps and bounds, to provide quality service and support, to ensure reliability and stability. Receiving money, we take legal responsibility for the service, for the data and their safety. When you use a free service, no one owes you anything, and if it closes tomorrow, you lose everything, and there will be nobody to ask.

Are we reliable? Pah-pah-pah there were no failures. Uptime server 100% and it is loaded now no more than 3%. And most importantly, paying for the service, be sure that it will work throughout this period. We give a guarantee and legally arrange it.
