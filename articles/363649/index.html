A prototype deuterium fusion reactor will be built in Novosibirsk



The team of scientists of the Institute of Nuclear Physics of the SB RAS is going to build a prototype of a nuclear reactor. The reactor will be built in the basement of the institute itself, and the implementation period is five years. Creating a prototype reactor is part of the project "Development of the fundamentals and technologies of thermonuclear energy of the future."

The project budget is quite substantial, and leaves 500 million rubles. Scientists have already received several large federal grants.
