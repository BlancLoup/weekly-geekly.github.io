IE9 supports TIFF, JPEG XR and ICC 4 color profiles



Two new demonstrations have appeared in the Internet Explorer 9 demonstration center : TIFF and JPEG XR support and ICC version 4 color profiles. Tests allow you to assess how much your browser supports these formats and see how it all works in IE9.

The first test Image Support offers the output of one image in three forms: plain JPEG (30kb, artifacts are visible), losless TIFF (890 kilobytes) and JPEG XR (30 kb with quality comparable to TIFF). In browsers that do not support TIFF and JPEG XR, PNG will be used for demonstration.

The second Color Profiles test allows you to test which version of ICC your browser supports and see the differences for different versions of ICC. The original image uses ICC 4. Test images are from the International Color Consortium .
