Payoneer: Easy Withdrawals from PayPal and Moneybookers

We have already reported that you can receive payments to Payoneer MasterCard not only from Payoneer partners, but also from many foreign companies that make payments to accounts in American banks. We are pleased to announce that now everyone can withdraw funds to the card from PayPal and Moneybookers accounts!

Order a card here . By registering, you will receive a card in the mail and, in addition, the bank account details in a US bank. Bank transfers to this account will be credited to your card balance as part of our US Payment Service.

As always, our support service is ready to help in Russian at support (a) payoneer.ru and service for working with Russian partners at sales (a) payoneer.ru.
