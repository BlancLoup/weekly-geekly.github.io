System requirements for 2017 games and builds for them


We have chosen three games that will be released or have already been released this year and studied their system requirements.


Resident evil 7




Release date: January 2017


The seventh part of the popular horror shooter came out on January 24 of this year, so we can already evaluate its system requirements for PCs. The developers recommend Intel Core i7 3770 processors running at 3.4 GHz, 8 GB of RAM, a GeForce GTX 1060X video card with 4 GB of video memory.


To assemble a computer for minimum settings, we needed about 33 thousand rubles. It is obvious that the assembly for the recommended settings will be much more expensive: only a video card costs 20 thousand rubles, and the processor - 20-24 thousand rubles.


For honor




Release date: February 14, 2017


The game studio Ubisoft just released, and in the recommended system requirements - a computer based on the processor Intel Core i5-2500K or AMD FX-6350, video card GTX 680 and newer or Radeon R9 280X and newer with 2 GB of video memory. Memory requirements are standard: 8 GB.


For Honor is not the most demanding game. To build a computer that pulls the game at the minimum settings, we had 30 thousand rubles. In order to play the game at the recommended settings, you will need a processor worth 7.5 thousand rubles and a video card, which costs about 9 thousand rubles.

For Battlefield 1, developers recommended an Intel Core i7 4790 or AMD FX-8350 processor, 16 GB of RAM, and a GeForce GTX 1060 video card with 3 GB of video memory or an AMD Radeon RX 480 with 4 GB of video memory.
