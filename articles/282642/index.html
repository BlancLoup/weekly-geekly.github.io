Announcement! DevCon 2016 participants will be available a master class from Intel

Dear readers, we are pleased to inform you that DevCon 2016 participants will be able to participate in a special Intel class on the Internet of Things with Intel Edison boards.

The master class is dedicated to practical work with Intel Edison. You will learn about the technical features and capabilities, options for using and testing the Intel Edison single-board computer. Consider installing drivers and connecting to Edison; Linux operation: launching SSH and connecting to Wi-Fi. During the training, the solution of practical problems in mini-groups will be offered.

Each participant will try to write their own programs to turn on the LED and connect different sensors. Using the Intel Edison platform, participants will be able to assemble such devices as interactive gadgets, elements of a smart home, as well as try to manage objects via the Internet.

The organizers will provide the following equipment: Intel Edison, Grove Starter Kit Plus and other sets of sensors and sensors. Participants need to bring a laptop with a pre-installed Intel XDK IoT Edition environment.

Diana Ilyina and Sergey Ivanov , one of the first Intel Software Innovator program participants in Russia in the direction of Internet of Things, IoT, will conduct a master class at the DevCon 2016 conference.
