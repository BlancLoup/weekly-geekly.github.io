Question about time and settings

Time of publication of the post, as you know, can be specified in two forms - in relative and in absolute. If the creators of the site, especially dynamic, do not want to mess with time zones and user registrations, then the time is indicated simply - so many days / hours / minutes ago. It is very convenient for everyone - you can immediately see how long the news / post has been published, and it is easy to watch the news for the last half day, a day, as many as you like.

If the time is specified absolute, then you want it or not, but it must be local for each specific user. The fact that the news was posted on April 18, 2007 02:52 says absolutely nothing. If, all of a sudden, the time zone for this time is known from somewhere, then it is already easier - it is necessary to recalculate it in local time. The operation is not the most difficult, but it requires some mental strain. By the way, even the absolute time received is also almost useless, because getting a time is immediately followed by an estimate of how long it was back / forward. (For the same reason, by the way, they control the analogue clock - a person always looks at how much time is left until some important moment for him, and in the hands it is immediately clearly visible.)
