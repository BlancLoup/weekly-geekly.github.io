Aava Mobile Debut - the world's first fully open smartphone

Barcelona, ‚Äã‚ÄãSpain - Aava Mobile ( www.aavamobile.com ) launched a completely open project for mobile devices of next-generation smartphones and tablets with personal computer capabilities. The device is based on Intel's next-generation Intel Atom processor platform, code-named Moorestown.





Features and specifications.


Aava Mobile engineers are confident that there can be no restrictions or compromise solutions between a small form factor and the capabilities of a personal computer. Some of the most prominent features of the presented device are:
- Advanced touch screen (full coverage - touch front cover) with tactile / vibro feedback;
- Compatible with Full HD video;
- Micro USB port that supports work in the host mode (you can connect a mouse, keyboard, external drive and docking station);
- HD video conference (front camera);
- 2 microphones with echo cancellation technology;
- 3D user interface and 3D sound for games;
- Support networks GSM (3G) for voice and data;
- Accelerometer, 3D compass, GPS, Wi-Fi
