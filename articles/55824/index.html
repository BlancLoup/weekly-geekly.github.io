StreetEasy (www.streeteasy.com) is a free website giving access to listings of properties sold and leased in New York and North Jersey. In addition to the standard information on prices, area and number of bedrooms, StreetEasy provides housing seekers with the ability to track listings in such aspects as location, building status, distance from schools and other special facilities. Site users have access to photo materials, contacts (e-mail or direct call), the ability to save tracked information and can receive messages about the availability of proposals on the property of interest to them.
