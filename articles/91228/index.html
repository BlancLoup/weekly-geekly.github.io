We are looking for analogues


The first thing I tried to find such a watch in the online store.

And I found them, only the price is not for a poor student, and on the wall there are unsightly clocks from Facdonald left over from the children's era. A little thought, I still decided on the feat. Begin the transformation!

First thing


First of all, you need to disassemble the clock. We remove the hands one by one, they are attached to everyone in different ways, but in most Chinese watches are removed without effort. Put them aside so as not to bend. Turn off the central washer and remove the dial.

Just do not try to disassemble the mechanism itself. On my watch there were such enticing latches that are very easy to open. Gathering back the gears, I lost about half an hour.

Untitled dial 1


A4 sheet is too small, so we take a paper A3 (for your watches, see the situation). Whatman paper is denser and better than plain paper. Circling the circle and cut, yes, yes, like in school. :)


In the middle, make a hole for the axes of the arrows; I made it with a stationery knife, but you can just with a pencil. Now we will paste the new dial over the old one, only the moment was at hand, but maybe it is for the best. Hold will be stronger.

Markup


This item could be done before gluing with the dial, then you do not need to dance with lines. But I still describe this process.
To begin with, we will draw a straight line through the center, then applying a protractor, we make serifs every 30 degrees. I describe this process in such detail so that no stupid questions appear, an adult will cope with this task.


Numbers, numbers, numbers ...


I decided not to copy the dial from the picture but to invent my own, the main thing is to check everything with a calculator so as not to look like a stupid sweat.

After you have checked everything and are sure, we apply the equation with a pencil. Then we circle, I took a simple marker.

It remains to erase the guides and put it all together.

Everything, hours are ready, it is possible to enjoy result and to surprise friends.
