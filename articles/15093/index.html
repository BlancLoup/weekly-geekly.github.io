How to check the customer "for lice"

When performing various orders, I personally and many of my friends
faced with the question - how to start work to determine that after the execution of the order, everyone will be satisfied?

Having been personally on both sides of the barricades, and considering freelancing, namely, short work, and not custom development (there is a very fuzzy line, I agree), we can distinguish the following points and actions that require the freelancer to know the basics of project management:



- try to search customer history and information about it in the internet
you might be surprised

- look at your competitors
if you offer much worse than them conditions - why you? if you do not know - ask the customer :)

- the contract does not mean that you will receive a reward for the work.
they should be read and understood what is written there, so if you are proposing a contract, consult your lawyer, if you are being offered it, all the more. no lawyer? consider your risks

- the customer does not have TZ at all or has it in an indecent form (a couple of paragraphs, some incomprehensibility for you)
ask for clarification. Generally speaking, freelancing should describe the task as much as possible. no questions on your part should arise.
