Weekly assembly Vivaldi 1.0.129.2

Hello!

We present you the next weekly assembly of the Vivaldi browser under the number 1.0.129.2. In short, what they did, what they did and what they broke.

Added the function of closing tabs by double clicking. You asked - we did.

Also one of the popular requests was the addition of a visual indication of loading background tabs. Without this, it was not clear what was happening with the pages that were open in the background - were they already loaded, or is the process still going on? Now we have added an indicator indicating the status of the loaded pages.

The other day we started to implement another popular request - the ability to import data from other browsers and, in particular, from Chrome. But they are immediately forced to warn: the function is still very unstable and can lead to browser crash. In addition, the feature is not available while on Mac platforms. In other words, we are doing this, but for now, it is better not to touch this function with your hands - wait until the situation stabilizes in the following assemblies. Fans of extreme testing can experiment without restrictions, but do not forget to send us messages about errors found .

Another small change was on the bookmarks bar. We have provided the ability to display only the names of bookmarks without icons. Naturally, soon it will be possible to place only icons on the panel, without site names. You asked for this too.

Changes on the bookmarks panel take effect until only after the browser is restarted, but, for example, you can sort the bookmarks on the panel or add new ones by dragging links without reloading.
