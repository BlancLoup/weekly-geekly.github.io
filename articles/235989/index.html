Whistler 118ST Ru radar detector: tested on the roads


No matter how you treat the existence of radar detectors, they are allowed for use in Russia. Today in the market there are dozens of brands that offer several thousand models. Prices for these auto gadgets start at 650 rubles and reach a space of 30 thousand.

And they really work. Naturally, this is not a panacea for all the surprises on the road, but unlike the CDs popular at the time, under the windshield, they actually perform their functions.

Whistler brand is considered one of the best in terms of price-quality ratio among radar detectors. That is why we could not deprive ourselves of the pleasure to try out the youngest model in the line - the budget 118ST Ru. Let's return to our "Arrows". A couple of years ago, these radars with impunity devastated the wallets of lovers of supersonic speeds - at that time, the only means to deal with the Strelka were databases with their GPS coordinates. But if this trick worked with stationary radars, then the Strelka mobile versions were nothing.

Today, radar detectors that see the Strelka are already quite a few, and the 118ST is one of them. All radars detected by the gadget were recorded in databases. But as practice shows, using them to quickly track recently installed or mobile radars is almost impossible. And almost all motorists (and we are not an exception) are faced with such a trick. Draw your own conclusions: a smartphone or a navigator, even with the most recent links to radars, in this case will not help you for sure.

One of the important parameters of a modern radar detector is sensitivity (or noise immunity), otherwise, the ability of a gadget to distinguish important signals from useless - simple models will react, literally, to everything - from sensors on the doors of stores to radar detectors and parking sensors in other cars .



In this regard, Whistler showed average results.
