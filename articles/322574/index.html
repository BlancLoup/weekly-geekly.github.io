Watching user

Personally, I always wanted to have a third eye, a seventh sense, or, at worst, a magic pill, with which I could design the most convenient site.

In my dreams, users fly to him like a butterfly into the world converting into a loyal and loyal audience. But there are no miracles, so you have to do with means that are not so magical and fast, but completely replace magic tablets and the third eye.

I'm talking about usability testing. The path, of course, is not the easiest, but effective.

In this article I want to share the conclusions made in the process of observing the behavior of users. In principle, everything has long been described in smart books, and without me and for someone, this will not be news, but someone can and will find something new and useful for themselves.

Take the maximum


No matter how beautiful your prototypes are, no matter how ingenious the invented solutions are, and no matter how delicious the proposed coffee is, if the user closes, you will not get sensible results from it.

Hence the first conclusion: show that you are on his side of the barricades. Does he not like high prices on your site? I completely agree, give a discount of 99.99%!

He could not understand that the magnifying glass icon means a search? I myself see it for the first time!

Well, that sort of thing. Agree, smile and understand that magic tune, thanks to which the user relaxes, and you get the maximum result.

In the form of a bonus, he can also distribute a couple of good ideas that can be noted.

To know does not mean to do


Who is the owner of the iPhone knows that deletion is possible by swiping the desired file to the left. However, when testing the application, users could not remove the product basket.

They could not, because they were looking for an icon, a word, and any other thing that would clearly let them know that it could be removed like this. Age and tenure of the iPhone did not matter. They simply did not transfer their knowledge in managing the phone to the app.

Conclusion: if the user understands the functions of the system does not mean that he will transfer these functions to the application. Add icons and inscriptions that will help you understand how to perform this or that action.

As you can endlessly look at the fire, and on the topic of captions to the icons you can talk endlessly.

Once again, I am convinced that even the most zazyuzannye icons, which should have been a long time ago to grin the user's brain and develop an unconditioned reflex, they do not understand.

The conclusion is as old as our planet: sign icons, make tooltips on the site and we will have a great and comprehensive happiness.

Freedom for parrots! Svo-bo-doo-poo-ga-yam!


Search must be. Clear field in a prominent place.

Especially if the site is quite a lot of goods. Small icons in the form of a magnifying glass, driven into the right corner of the site is fashionable and sometimes even cool. But, as observations show, a sufficient number of visitors come with a ready-made solution and they need a search for a quick search. Sorry for the tautology. They will not find your search, they will go to competitors.

Conclusion: Many products on the site? Give us a noticeable search field!

You have your own fairy tale, and I have my own


Recommendations are good, even great. We have individual recommendations that are displayed on the main page. There is a separate page with recommendations on topics, events and many other good recommendations.

But there is a problem: many people do not understand that the main page is individual recommendations, and a separate page is often ignored. But notifications sent to the phone or mail with recommendations and updates draw much more user attention to themselves.

Conclusion: it may not be worth spending too much space to show recommendations, and throw more energy into sending mailings and push notifications. They perceive them as something more personal, and accordingly treat them with great interest. Recommendations from friends, colleagues and acquaintances are also much appreciated.
