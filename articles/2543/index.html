Samsung phone equipped with tactile feedback

Samsung, together with Immersion Corporation, released the SCH-W559, the highlight of which is the touch screen with tactile feedback.

This interesting feature is provided by the VibeTonz System technology developed by Immersion Corporation. Its creators claim that trembling as a means of feedback improves the usability of the phone.



Currently, Samsung SCH-W559 is available only to customers of China Unicom, a Chinese mobile operator, writes newswireless.net .
