Blogus will lead Feedburner to Russia

Tomorrow, it is expected that Blogus will resume its work - a service for bloggers that allows you to search by tags, as well as calculating the popularity rating of online blogs. Some time ago it was closed due to problems encountered on the hosting server due to overload. Having completed investment negotiations, service developers promise new features for users.

At the same time, the official blog Feedburner reports that Blogus is now a partner of this service: Russian partners will sell advertising space in RSS feeds Feedburner for Russian-speaking users in Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan and Israel. In addition, at the address www.feedburner.ru in the near future it is worth waiting for the Russian version of Feedburner.
