Apple Store prepares for pre-order iPad

At the moment, the Apple Store is not available for purchase. The inscription on the main page gives every reason to assume that it is being upgraded to pre-order the iPad. And maybe something else new



According to the Mashable website, orders will start working today from 16:30 (Moscow time).

Users, of course, will not be able to touch the device until April 3rd (except for the most ardent fans that they will order the iPad first). However, with the help of the pre-order system, Apple (and maybe even the media) will know exactly how the new product will be used.

Although the iPad has created a huge stir, we should not forget that the experts are still undecided on whether it will be a huge success or become an epic file. This is not only a new gadget, this is a new type of device, and everyone wants to see how it will affect the IT market.
