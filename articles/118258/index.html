Delicious sold to Youtube founders


According to YDN, Youtube founders Chad Hurley and Steve Chen announced today that they purchased Delicious from Yahoo! They plan to further develop the service so that users can save, share and find the most "delicious" content on the web.
For users, the process of moving the service to new owners should remain inconspicuous. Yahoo! will support Delicious for the next two months. During this period, registration of users will not be suspended. After the end of this period, user data will be transferred to the new owners of the service.
Yahoo! decided to sell Delicious in December 2010. The company expects that new owners will continue to develop the service for the benefit of users.
