The KUB-Mini controller - protection and monitoring with reservation

Today we will talk about another popular among our customers - telecom operators - the controller. This is a KUB-Mini controller. Its distinguishing feature is the redundancy of the communication channel and power. When the main Ethernet channel disappears, the backup GSM channel is automatically activated, and the backup power source ensures autonomous operation of the controller for two hours. Therefore, we position this device as a device for the protection and monitoring of particularly important communication facilities, or objects that require GSM work.
