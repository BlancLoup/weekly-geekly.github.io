Yahoo will open a website for IT investors, as in 2000

The new TechTicker website will launch in January 2008. It will be devoted exclusively to shares of technology companies, as well as news of this market. It is assumed that there will be blogs and videos, as well as live reports on major events.

TechTicker is the second site of this kind (video / multimedia for investors and financiers), which opens Yahoo. In 2000, the company introduced FinanceVision, a kind of online version of a 24-hour business channel. That project could not succeed and was closed two years later.

Now the most popular financial site on the Internet Yahoo Finance is placed on the Yahoo portal. A large audience of users has formed around it, so TechTicker is intended primarily for them.

via NY Times
