Corbina Telecom Announces the Launch of IPTV

Corbina Telecom is launching a new pay-TV service based on Microsoft TV, a third-generation solution for IP-television, first introduced in Russia.

The cost of the "Basic" package is 140 rubles per month. In August, two new packages will be launched - Premium (more than 110 channels) and Advanced (more than 99 channels), costing 400 and 250 rubles per month, respectively.

The Corbina Telecom IP-TV service includes several packages, including the ‚ÄúBasic‚Äù package of 56 channels with the most popular satellite channels (including Discovery, Hallmark, Viasat, Eurosport), as well as premium TV packages - more than 100 digital channels . Any Corbina Telecom TV subscriber has the opportunity to use the Video to Order service. Digital recording of gear is available to all owners of set-top boxes with built-in hard disk.

A set top box equipped with a hard disk allows you to program the recording of a TV show on most well-known TV channels at a certain time. You can view the recorded video at a convenient time an unlimited number of times.

Particular attention in the IP-TV "Corbina Telecom" is given to the possibility of showing films and TV shows in the format of HDTV (high definition television) with a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels and multi-channel surround sound Dolby 5.1. Currently this format is supported by LUX TV.

The Video on Demand service provides users with the option of advanced search for movies, broadcasts from the Corbina Telecom catalog, and then order selected content in DVD or HDTV format for a small fee. The user can view the video content he likes an unlimited number of times during the day, paying the amount from 1 to 60 rubles, depending on the content category. Connecting to the new service is carried out through the Corbina Telecom cable already delivered to the apartment using a switch. To connect to a TV, you need to install a Motorola VIP 1216 set-top box with a 160 GB hard disk worth 6950 rubles (rent 230 rubles / month) or a Motorola VIP 1200 set top box without a hard disk cost 4300 rubles (rent 140 rubles / month). In the future, Corbina Telecom plans to expand the range of consoles from other suppliers.
