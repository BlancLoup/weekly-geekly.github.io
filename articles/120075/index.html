In the depths of the conscience of every developer casualgame are moral principles. There are seven of them.

1) I despise advertising. No ads in your product.
2) I do not notice the publishers. No communication with publishers.
3) I play only intellectual games. No pop apps.
4) I do not like beggars. No requests to users inside the author's programs.
5) I am above the press. No paid reviews or purchase interviews.
6) Not a line of someone else's code. I am the greatest magician.
7) And finally, do not steal, crud, music and pictures.

Of course, in six months of development, I committed all 7 deadly sins.

The author is a scoundrel !, exclaim you, and you will be right.
But not a scoundrel. Scoundrel - who does nasty things with pleasure.

Sin is the first. Advertising




Advertising has been tested in five or six games.
I placed it only in the backyard of applications, somewhere on UIWebView and only for residents of the United States of America.

After the release of advertising applications, I looked at the daily reports on advertising revenue in surprise and stupidly - a lot of numbers. Many abbreviations like "Impressions on click". It looks beautiful, but useless. Statistics update time is not amenable to analysis. Poked into advertising reports for a week, completely lost interest in this page.

Final earnings - $ 10-15 per month. The maximum daily earnings of $ 3. He jumps about every seven to ten days. But the standard daily profit is from 1 to 10 cents.
Having slept with advertising, I no longer sin with it.
But $ 12 for April I will drink with pleasure.
