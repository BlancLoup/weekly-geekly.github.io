Ukraine introduced a new procedure for the importation and operation of mobile

In Ukraine, a new procedure for the importation and operation of mobile phones has come into force.



This was announced at a press conference by the director of the GP of the Ukrainian State Center for Radio Frequencies, Pavel Slobodyanyuk.
Slobodyanyuk reminded that the corresponding resolution of the National Commission for the Regulation of Communications was registered with the Ministry of Justice on March 28.
Ukraine introduced a new order of importation of mobile
This document provides for the need to obtain permission from the Ukrainian State Center of Radio Frequencies (UCRF) for the importation of mobile phones, as well as the introduction of the IMEI codes for radio frequency electronic devices operating in Ukraine.

He clarified that a transition period was set before May 30, during which mobile phone distributors have the right to submit a list of IMEI codes to the UCRF. UCRF is obliged to make them free of charge in the register of IMEI codes. Mobile operators until May 30, 2009 have the right to serve the subscribers' telephones, regardless of the presence of IMEI codes in the data registry.

After July 30, mobile operators have the right to connect subscribers' phones only if an IMEI code is registered.

At the same time, by September 30, 2009, mobile operators must provide the UCRF with a list of mobile phone codes that they maintain and do not register. After that, the maintenance of mobile phones by operators can be carried out subject to the availability of a code in the UCRF database.
