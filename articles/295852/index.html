Research and ranking of the largest online stores in Russia

Hi, Megamind! Ruward, together with the agency Data Insight, has just published the results of a study conducted on Russia's largest online stores - " E-Commerce Index Top-100 2015 ".



As part of the study, estimates were made of turnover, the number of orders and the average check of online stores for the first half of 2015.

Exist (35.7 billion rubles), Yulmart (15.9 billion rubles) and Wildberries (14 billion rubles) are among the three largest stores by turnover. And the top three in terms of the number of orders implemented are: Wildberries (9, 65 million orders), Exist (4.15 million orders) and Ozone (2.8 million orders).

In addition to the main indicators, the analytical partners of the project also calculated a number of additional indicators : security index, accessibility index, SEO index (search visibility), analytic index, number of SKUs, online payment methods.

The method of rating and data collection includes the analysis of a whole set of indicators using external monitoring ‚Äúfrom the outside‚Äù by our analysts, as well as the adjustment and step-by-step refinement of this data based on direct communication with representatives of stores.

It should also be noted that the first hundred of the largest online stores in Russia are the basis of electronic commerce in our country. They account for 58% of all orders and a comparable amount of turnover. The largest of them have tens of thousands of orders per day, ahead of the second hundred in the number of orders by two orders of magnitude. These hundred stores actually determine today the future of e-commerce, the emergence of new services, the emergence of new conditions and operating principles. In other words, the stores of the first hundred are so large that the rules of the game in the market depend on them more than on the rest of the market combined.

All data and calculations can be found on the official website of the rating / study .
