Hackers from the Anonymous group promised to "destroy" Facebook

Members of the Anonymous group promised to disable Facebook on November 5, accusing the management of the social network of irresponsible attitude to user data. This is stated in a video posted on YouTube on behalf of Anonymous.



It is curious that this threat appeared on July 16, but was only noticed now.
