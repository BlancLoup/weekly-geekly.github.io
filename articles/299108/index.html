I understood the need to focus on one specific area and narrow down the subject of study, and having a great (and main) interest in the field of information technology and a desire to work in this environment (not a techie), I was faced with a significant problem - almost all of the announced programs in the educational market, related to the IT field, aimed at either hellish techies or comrades with work experience from 2‚Äì3 years and having unrealistic management experience (MBA programs).

Positive aspects of the program


What attracted me to her and what moments are justified in the end?
  • First, the MSU Graduate School of Business is the official partner of SODIT (Union of IT-Directors of Russia) , and the president of this Union is the curator of the IT Management program, which gives it a huge advantage: this is the connection with the business representatives gives the faculty an understanding of the requirements market, on how best to create a program and a learning process. Moreover, the content of its program, so far, the only one in Russia, fully complies with the requirements of the new professional standard "Information Technology Manager" (approved by order of the Ministry of Labor of Russia N 716n of October 13, 2014)
  • Secondly - a combination of technical and managerial disciplines. For me, as a manager, by education, the most interesting are specific subjects such as: enterprise architecture, BPM, basics of programming and system administration, IT standards, etc., which are extensively and intensively taught in HSB. For my colleagues - techies - the opposite value has the main value: financial management, strategic management, organizational behavior, leadership skills, and others, which have the manager's skill pumped through.
  • Thirdly - the possibility of passing international internships and practices in the field of information management. In the summer, a trip to South Korea (Seoul) is planned, and during the academic semester, students who wish to undergo the internship are given the opportunity to intern in one of the partner companies.

Disadvantages of the program


During the first semester, I managed to improve the business processes optimization, programming and understanding of the IT environment, but after studying one semester, the main drawback of the program for me, so far has become one, but an important fact - its experimentation and novelty:
  • Yes, on the one hand, it can be a plus - the novelty causes flexibility of the program, gives the student the opportunity to be heard and influence the learning process, but on the other, there is instability: the main team of practitioners from the IT business environment is just being formed, which brings not only positive results, but also small disappointments.
  • Also, experiment in such educational endeavors often implies a small number of students in the stream. Our 18 people represent a certain imbalance of backgrounds (there are fewer techies than people with economics and humanities education), which limits the ability to transfer heterogeneous experience and jointly achieve synergies in learning. It is clear that this problem will be solved as early as next year - because of the growing interest in the program, the school plans to expand the flow to 35-40 people, but it is a pity that this experiment affected us in particular.

As a result


I will not get ahead of myself and praise the program - life will show how much it will contribute to my professional development. However, the fact that thanks to her I feel a real growth of the skill of managerial and specific skills is a fact.
