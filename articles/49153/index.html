Video of reports from the SQA Days conference (Minsk)

November 17, 2008 The 4th International Conference of Software Quality Assurance Specialists SQA Days has been held in Minsk at the IBB Educational Center. The conference was devoted to issues related to software testing and quality assurance: functional testing, performance testing, test automation and tools, configuration testing, usability and security testing, static quality assurance methods and other areas of interest of QA specialists. The good news is that in 2009 the organizers are planning to hold several more conferences for QA specialists .
