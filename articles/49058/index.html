Clockwork radio



Radio for victims of the energy crisis - differently call Solar FM Radio language does not turn. The toy is representative of the generation of "green" gadgets and is equipped with two independent sources of energy. For daytime listening there is a solar battery, and nighttime fans are invited to turn a special pen (one minute is enough for 20 minutes of FM music). Device for sale here for $ 25.

Specifications:

FM frequency: 87-108MHz
AM frequencies: 530-1600KHz
Battery life: 5000 charge / discharge cycles
Operating time after 5 hours of solar charging: 30 hours
