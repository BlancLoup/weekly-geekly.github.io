Webcast: Dell Virtual Desktop Infrastructure Solutions

Have a good day, colleagues!

We would like to announce the next Dell webcast, which will be held next week, on September 17, at 11:00 Moscow time, and will be devoted to virtualization and solutions that Dell offers in this area.

Dell Desktop Virtualization is an IT infrastructure that allows you to deploy full-fledged workstations based on a data center, in which many virtual machines operate, without limiting their functionality.
We will describe how to build an effective and complete infrastructure of virtual workplaces of users. Completeness means efficiency: high performance, deep integration of hardware with VMware and Microsoft products, flexibility of Dell Software vWorkSpace.

Separately, we will focus on
  • universality of use and flexibility;
  • increasing the level of security and information protection;
  • data center and virtualization convergence;
  • economic model of ownership of infrastructure;
  • Infrastructure Components and Intel Xeon Processors

For those of you who have already been on our previous webcasts, it will be enough for you at an hour X to follow this link and enter the email address and password that you received after registration.

1. Click here.
2. Enter some personal data that we promise not to use against you (including for spam).
3. A letter with a link will fall into your box.
4. September 17 at 11:00 Moscow time click here and turn on.
