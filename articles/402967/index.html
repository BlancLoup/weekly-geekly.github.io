LePlant plants floating in the air




If there is a levitating mug on one side of the table, then why not put a flying cactus or a bonsai tree on the other? Levitating Crealev table lamp

Aoske Magnetic Levitation Platform

Levitating charger for smart watches




Yes, and there is. This thing is called LIFT. Like most previous gadgets, it is very easy to work with. It is necessary to connect the charger to the network, and place the smart watch, for example, Apple Watch, on the levitating module. As in the other cases, the device will begin to rotate. So here is a double benefit - you can admire the clock, charging them. When the system detects the presence of a device, the backlight comes on, which makes the system even more interesting.

Flyte, one of whose products, the light bulb, was shown above, recently introduced one more thing. This is a watch with a levitating hand that hovers in the air, simultaneously moving along the dial.
