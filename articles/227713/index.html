Odessa Innovation Week: In Odessa, create an E-government and make cities "smarter"!


From July 21 to July 27, Odessa Innovation Week will be held in Odessa. We invite you to take part in events: WebCamp2014 , GeeksLab Hackathon and Odessa StartUp Day , as well as visit a number of useful workshops / workshops and after- party .

GeeksLab Hackathon 1.0: E-Government / CityProjects


From July 25-27, within the framework of Odessa Innovation Week, GeeksLab Hackathon1.0 will be held : E-Government / CityProjects - a two-day themed hackathon that will allow participants to bring their ideas to life.

The main directions of the hackathon:
  • E-Government is a project designed to help improve the efficiency of the state and transparent communication of citizens and the state through the development of e-Gov civil services.
  • City Projects - IT projects that will help improve the urban environment or a certain aspect of it. The main topics are the development of civil society, urban activism, urbanism, housing and public utilities, assistance to socially unprotected categories of citizens, ecology, public transport, the use of new media, journalism, attraction to the tourist brand of the city, and more.

The event was supported by such companies as: Yandex Ukraine, Growth UP, World Economic Forum / Global Shapers, DataArt, international network Impact Hub, Social Boost and more.

Well-known figures from Ukraine and Estonia, specialists in urban studies, as well as professionals from the IT, legal, social, and state spheres will become judges and mentors of the hackathon.
