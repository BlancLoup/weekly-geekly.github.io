July 15 - free webinar "Adobe InDesign in the production of electronic books"

Summer is in full swing, so the fact that you can get new information while avoiding long journeys and direct incandescent rays should please fans of webinars. Every Friday, Adobe conducts an online webinar, and tomorrow, July 15, it will be devoted to e-book preparation tools using InDesign.

The main topics of the webinar:
  • Tools for preparing e-books in ePUB format
  • Work with long documents
  • Principles of working with text in e-books
  • Non-destructive styling
  • Automation of design and rules (nested / GREP styles for applying attributes)
  • Search / Replace (GREP for Replace)
  • Text variables
  • Anchored objects - elements fixed in the text
  • Linked text (sync text blocks)

Total: July 15, 2011, on Friday, Oleg Sidorenko (Adobe Systems) conducts a webinar "Adobe InDesign in the production of electronic books." Beginning at 11:00 Moscow time, duration ~ 1 hour 15 minutes along with the session of answers to questions.
