We connect 5 winchesters via sata multiplier with one sat cable



In this article I will tell you a little about the sata multipliers CFI-B53PM based on the SiI3726 chip, which I bought for a large burial site many terabytes. How and where to order them I wrote in this article (at the end of the article I attached a tracking package). Also I will tell you about the speed of work in linux in the 6th raid.


Benefits


1) Fewer sata wires required.
2) Requires less power wires.
3) Cheap multiplier and controllers for them.
4) Fast reading speed.

disadvantages


1) Multipliers are not sold in Russia, although there are lots of controllers at each corner ( sil3132 ).
2) Solid capacitors break off very easily (caution and straight hands are required).
3) Relatively slow write speed.

To connect to a computer, I used a stlab a-410 controller worth 600 rubles.

The write speed of the 6th raid with the xfs file system starts at 100 megabytes and settles at 40 MB. Read speed consistently 320 MB.

I think the slow write speed is related to the command-based switching animation method, in which at one time the controller can send a command to only one hard drive.
