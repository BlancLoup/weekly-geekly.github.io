Wiki for Python programmers

To date, the following translations of books are available on the resource:
  • The Definitive Guide to Plone
  • The Definitive Guide to Pylons
  • WxPython in Action
From the documentation:
  • SQLAlchemy
  • Beautiful soup
Special mention deserves the section "Packages" , which is planned to be filled with descriptions of the most interesting packages used in Python. For convenience, we have divided this section into several categories.
