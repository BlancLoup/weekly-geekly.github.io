Online mobile phone sales will grow by 43% in 2006

The Divizion group of companies, citing the current state of online mobile phone trading in Russia, shows a 43% increase in online sales in 2006. In total, on the Internet, according to the distributor, about 600 thousand phones will be sold during this period.

Analysts say: with the current fall in the mobile phone market in Russia, the share of online commerce will increase to 2.1% in 2006. Last year this share was 1.3% of the market. In total, around 420 thousand mobile phones were sold in 2005, and $ 75.6 million was earned. The entire market, including its offline part, amounted to $ 5.2 billion in 2005 - that is, 31.5 million phones were sold in Russia .

As already mentioned, analysts of the group of companies predict a decline in trade in 2006 - namely, by 12% to 28 million units. At the same time, they say that there will be an increase in sales - up to $ 5.6 billion.

Indicates several factors that contribute to the growth of trade in mobile phones online. The increased level of Internet penetration in the country is also, along with the growth in purchasing power, caused a surge in Internet sales.

In addition, analysts expect an increase in Internet penetration beyond the borders of the Central Federal District and major Russian cities. This, in their opinion, will allow to keep online trading in mobile phones at a good level.
