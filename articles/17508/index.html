Facebook licenses engine: hit on Google OpenSocial

Yesterday, Facebook announced plans to expand its social network to partner sites. As part of the program, friendly projects can license the Facebook engine and, in fact, connect their users to this social network, which is becoming global.

The first project to connect to Facebook will be the largest social network in the UK and one of the largest English-speaking networks in the world Bebo .

The meaning of these gestures is quite obvious. Suffice it to recall that a month ago, during the announcement of Google, OpenSocial was most conspicuous by the absence of participants in this project from Facebook. It is clear that one of the most promising and technological networks wants to create a movement around itself.

Facebook opened its programming interfaces for developers in August 2006. With the opening of the Facebook Platform, relationships with partner sites are reaching a new level. All technical details can be found on the specifications page .
