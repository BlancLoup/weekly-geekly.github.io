Statistics of users and companies of Russia in Europe

Russia is gradually becoming an increasingly prominent player in the Internet industry. What is worth only the IPO of the two largest Internet companies such as Mail.ru Group and Yandex. As well as DST activity in investing in large overseas projects.

Users



The user base of Russia is also growing. ComScore's recent European report for April confirms this. In a month, the company counted 47,853,000 users in Russia. This is the second indicator in Europe after Germany. For comparison, in March in Russia there were 47.417,000, which means an increase of 0.9% for the month.

The average Russian user spent in April 22.3 hours on the Internet. In March, this figure was 22.8, that is, there were more people, but they spent less time on the network. And the pages for visiting the site began to look less (2.355 in April compared with 2.532 in March).

In total in Europe, ComScore counted 365,274,000 users in April, and in the world - 1,362,369,000. This means that Russian users account for 13.1% of all European and 3.5% of the world.




Companies



The most visited projects in Europe were the websites of Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Wikimedia and Yahoo! .. But we are more interested in Russian projects that are also not on the latest roles in Europe.

Sites Mail.ru Group, apparently, it is only Mail.ru services and Odnoklassniki, took 9th place with 69 million users.

Yandex services ranked 15th with 54.1 million.

Closes the top 20 Vkontakte leaders with 46.7 million. And as we see, the audience of the social network No. 1 in Russia is almost equal to the number of users in the country.

So, the websites of three Russian companies are among the twenty most visited resources in Europe. And if Vkontakte becomes part of the Mail.ru Group, the latter can reach the 6th place in Europe, overtaking eBay and Amazon.
