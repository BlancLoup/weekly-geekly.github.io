Mmm ... tasti is a tlogs service. A tlog is something like a journal, diary or blog, but in a different format. Tlog is conducted personally for oneself. Here they are not looking for old friends and do not make new acquaintances. And here everything is done so that you can express yourself the way you want it. Therefore, there are no restrictions. You can do and behave here as you see fit. To be youreself. And we try to do everything so that you have complete freedom here.
Why tlog and what is the difference from LJ or simple blogs?
The log is created and maintained personally. There is no such thing as "friends." And comments, although present, are disabled by default. Vobschem Tlog - this is your personal super-diary, some of which, if you wish, you can share.
Where does all this come from?
This kind of record keeping format appeared for the first time from Christian in his anarchaia diary, after which it was partially taken over by other bloggers.
