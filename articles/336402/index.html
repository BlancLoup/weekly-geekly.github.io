Amsterdam invites to work in the field of AI



Just started project: a - project.amsterdam . As written on the page:

The project is an invitation for programmers, scientists, business analysts and experts in the field of AI and machine learning to come to Amsterdam and help us make the future.

10 top Amsterdam companies told about their projects. All you need to do is choose a project that you like and submit an application. 10 elected will fly to Amsterdam for 5 days, meet with potential employers, take part in the World Summit AI in October. Everything, including flight, accommodation, visa, participation in the forum, will be paid by the inviting party. Very little is required from you :) The submission of applications ends on September 25, and the names of the winners will be announced on the 26th. The project closes on October 12th at the end of the conference. All details can be found on the project website.

Shl. I personally have no relation to the project, but I work in Amsterdam. Therefore, I decided to share the news published in local technical circles.
