Domain.RUS application filed with ICANN

The Russian company "Russian Names" has applied for a root domain. .

In accordance with the rules of ICANN, all the necessary documents were uploaded to the electronic application submission system by April 12 and a fee was paid for the consideration of the application.

Cyrillic domain RUS is designed to promote the language, culture and identity of the world Russian-speaking community.

The string is the three characters that begin the first word for Russian. A language spoken by 300 million people living on Earth. For 170 million, Russian is the main language, for 130 million - the second language.

Russian Language has official status in Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Abkhazia, South Ossetia, Moldova, Norway, Ukraine, and the USA. Russian is spoken by people living in Georgia, Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia, Israel, Mongolia, Finland, Germany, France, Canada, Australia. Russian Language is the official language for international organizations of the United Nations, UNESCO, IAEA, WHO, ICAO, CIS, EvaAzEC, CSTO, SCO, ATS, OSCE, BRICS

The introduction of the domain. RUS has supported the world Russian-speaking community. Letters of support were received from the Coordination Center of the national domain RU, the Internet Technology and Infrastructure Development Assistance Fund, the Ukrainian Network Information Center, the Kazakh Association of IT Companies, the Kazakh Center for Network Information, the Internet Society of Armenia, the Administrator of the root domain of Azerbaijan, the Russian-Canadian Business Club.

Russian ICANN accredited registrars RU-CENTER, Tsentrohost and Regtime also expressed support and willingness to register domains in the zone. RUS for their clients and partners.
