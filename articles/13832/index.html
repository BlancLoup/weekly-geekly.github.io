Interview with a millionaire. Zahar Bowl

I have long wanted to interview my good friend, and now it has happened! Who is he? A dollar millionaire, businessman, investor, excellent companion and just a handsome man in the prime of life. Meet - Zahar Bowl (Velvet Lounge).

Work for you hobby, or heavy necessity?

Work takes up most of my life, so it is always important for me that work bring moral satisfaction. When I start a new business, I try to consider it not only from the point of view of business, but also as an occupation that I will devote a lot of time, and which, therefore, should bring me pleasure. This is not just a desire to make your life more colorful and interesting. I believe that a successful businessman simply has to be passionate about his business, because only this way truly good ideas can be born, in order to later grow into successful large-scale projects that it will be pleasant for a person to put into practice.



How often do you have to curb your emotions for the sake of business success? Do you lose your temper?

In general, I am a rather emotional person, but if I get seriously angry, I still try to curb my emotions without splashing them on others. I give myself time to think about the situation, if possible, and then settle. This is an important aspect of business ethics, and a wonderful way to maintain good relations with business partners and subordinates.

How and when did you earn the first rubles?

I was 11 years old. In the early nineties, it was pretty easy to surprise with a simple service. At the urban vegetable base, I bought Bulgarian pepper, sparing no time selecting the best specimens, then carefully packing the vegetables into bags and selling almost twice as much, but, most importantly, with a smile on my lips and not hanging around, which was very rare at that time. Housewives loved it. He also worked at a gas station. Washed and fueled cars. Perhaps it was then that I learned the basics of a successful business. :)

Therefore, in business I value partners most of all not just with a strong business acumen, but those whom I can trust. Of course, I often have to learn from my mistakes, but in general I pay attention to the reputation of the person with whom I intend to cooperate.
