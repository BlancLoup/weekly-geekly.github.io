The Russian authorities have decided to create a separate department, which will deal with all issues related to copyright. The new service is being created on the basis of Rospatent, it will be headed by the head of this department, Grigory Ivliev (in the photo).

In particular, the current authority of the Ministry of Culture regarding the protection of copyright will be transferred to the service.
