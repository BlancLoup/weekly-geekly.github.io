Lenovo ThinkPad Twist S230u Ultrabook Video Review



Lenovo ThinkPad Twist S230U is a hybrid of a tablet and an ultrabook, made according to the classical scheme that has been used in ThinkPad X series notebooks for a long time. in the usual form, and in the form of a tablet.

ThinkPad Twist S230u works on low-voltage Intel Core i3 / i5 / i7 processors, and the amount of RAM ranges from 4 to 8 GB, depending on the model (you cannot replace the memory yourself). For data storage, either 128 GB solid state drives or hard drives (320/500 GB) with an additional SSD for caching (24 GB) are used.

The body of the laptop is made in the classic strict design of the ThinkPad with a magnesium alloy frame. By itself, in addition to the standard touchpad, the trackpoint is also used in red - a proprietary part of ThinkPad notebooks.
