Logos. Google Design

To end the demonstration of the history of changes in the logos, I want the Google logo. The experience of Google is interesting not only because they themselves are constantly doing something with their logo. In fact, the Google logo has long become a carrier of viral advertising. Especially, given the number of options for the logo, which is created by lovers and walks on the web.
And interestingly, the logo is essentially separated from the services of the company. In my opinion, this is an excellent illustration of the fact that the brand does not make the logo :)
