Game Master Tools



To create a simple game module you need quite a bit: a bit of paper, a pen and your own imagination. Many skilled craftsmen have enough of this for large modules for several game days or even entire lines. Despite this, there are a diverse number of different tools that help to significantly simplify the work of the Game master and give him the opportunity to focus on the main thing. In this article I tried to collect the most useful programs and online services that facilitate our lives.

Card generator.
Many years ago, I got a free copy of the Fractal World Explorer . Since then, to create global maps, I use it only. Allows you to build a geographical map of the planet, create an atmosphere, calculate the river. Allows you to edit the created map by changing the heights, precipitation, temperature, sea level, erosion of winds and rains.
Advantages:
Convenience and simplicity.
The necessary settings are present.
Disadvantages:
Pay. Explorer is included in the Fractal Mapper package and is a paid program. (However, generous people with redundant copies have not yet been translated on the Internet).

Dungeon Generator
There are quite a few such generators on the network, but I use the most obvious of them:
donjon.bin.sh/d20/dungeon/index.cgi
Very convenient service with the right amount of settings. Not only creates a dungeon map, but also sets traps, secret doors, difficulty levels to detect them (d & d 3 and higher), and so on.
Advantages:
Convenient interface.
Availability of useful settings.
Pleasant result output.
Disadvantages:
The service is posted on the website, you can not drag it to your bag.

Real Name Generator
Despite the enormous abundance of various generators of names, I stopped at this
online service.
kurufin.ru/html/namegenerator.html
The service provides a choice of twenty different nationalities, and also provides additional settings when generating Russian names.
Benefits:

Simplicity and convenience.
Disadvantages:
