Utel - Provider vs Legal entities

Prehistory


I want to tell you a story that has been going on for almost the whole of 2008.
Around the end of 2007, I settled for a great federal place of work, after working a little for convenience, I decided to merge all my branches into one vpn network, but of course officially through the provider, because of the big providers in the city the choice is not great, and the Internet was already it is connected to u-tel, I had to stop on it and look at it.
I did everything as it should be: I wrote letters, signed contracts, signed on both sides.

Hard work with U-tel


Next, the hard work began. With each call to ASI, they put everything off for tomorrow, I patiently called every day or every other day, the most surprising thing is that the organization during these 6 months regularly paid for providing allegedly connected services (about 4 tons).
After 6 months, the situation is generally strangely resolved, after the next kick from the authorities, the chief administrator raised the vpn + internet connection, but for some reason, instead of the standard constant, I connected an ultra-megabit, which cost 35000 rubles at that time, I was indignant but he reassured and said that everything will be fine.

Consequences after work


They overdid so much that they connected me with several tariffs at once (Internet, Internet + vpn, ultra megabit) and imagine what was going on in the accounting department after billing, and billing also turned off everything and, accordingly, the work of the entire enterprise for December 2008 rose completely , neither salaries, nor electronic document turnover, however, they could not solve this problem quickly either, they tortured me and the accountants for another 3 months , thus, vpn and the Internet had not worked for 9 months in a row.

Afterword


In the end, the problem was still decided by filing a lawsuit in court, suing a full refund, plus legal and moral costs.
