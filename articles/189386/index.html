Privilege for an arithmetic machine, issued to the honorary citizen Zelik Slonimsky on November 24, 1845 for 10 years

Zelik Slonimsky and his arithmetic machine are well known to computer technology historians, a description of the device is contained in the special literature, but the privilege itself has not been published on the Web - at least I could not find it.
