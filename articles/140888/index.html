Jelastic at RHS ++ 2012

We want to make a small announcement: the technical director of the Russian division of Jelastic, Dmitry Lazarenko, will speak at one of the largest Russian professional conferences of developers in the field of web technologies - RIT ++ 2012 . The report will take place on April 2 in the second hall of the Digital October Conference Center (Moscow, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, 3rd floor) at 12.30. We want to share with you the most intimate: "How we built Jelastic - the cloud platform (PaaS) of the new generation." Dmitry will open the curtain over the Jelastic architecture.
You will learn:
  • what nontrivial problems we had to face and how we solved them in order to ensure high performance and scalability;
  • about the methods of automatic deployment and centralized updating used by us both for the platform and user environments, simultaneously in several data centers;
  • on the mechanisms of continuous load balancing within the data center;
  • about the process of choosing the optimal virtualization technology for java-hosting tasks.

A photo of the speaker will help you find it faster among many other conference participants. You will have the opportunity to talk with a member of our team personally and find out all the details you are interested in about our platform. Looking forward to the meeting!
