Astronomical calendar for 2017


This year we continue the tradition of publishing the Astronomical Calendar, launched a year ago .

Like the previous calendar, the new Astronomical calendar for 2017 you can independently download and print on a printer, in a printing house or take part in the general circulation. The calendar is distributed under the license CC BY-ND 4.0.

As before, you are waiting for exciting cosmic landscapes, as well as daily astronomical events - an eclipse of the sun, the moon, lunar phases, the position of the planets in the solar system, a significant date in the history of astronautics.

This year, at the choice of users, the calendar presents information about scientists who are united not only by their fascination with science, but also the desire and ability to share exciting knowledge about the cosmos and the nature of everything that exists in our wonderful world.
