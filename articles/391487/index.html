Hyperloop plan to launch in Europe by 2020



The project of a futuristic bullet train in a vacuum tube Hyperloop proposed by Ilon Mask can be implemented in Eastern Europe. Yesterday, Hyperloop Transportation Technologies announced an agreement with the Slovak government to build the local Hyperloop transport system, the first segment of which they want to put into operation in 2020.
