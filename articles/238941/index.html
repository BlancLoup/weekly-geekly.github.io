The first metap for mobile developers at Microsoft



Increasingly, developers complain about the lack of available activities in their area, where they could communicate with professionals like themselves, share ideas, experience in the field of mobile development and learn about technology first hand.

We decided to revive the tradition of meetings of the developer community, but slightly changed the format. Now it will be meetings with short reports, time for communication among themselves and discussions.

So, the first metap for mobile developers will be held on October 16, 2014 in the Microsoft office.
The meeting will begin at 18:30 , which will allow to attend the event even to those who wish, who work according to the standard office schedule. At the event, everyone will have the opportunity to communicate not only with us, but also to meet other participants.

The first mitap will be dedicated to our latest announcements of Windows 10 and XBox One and will be truly festive. We plan to hold meetings with regularity once a month , we promise to prepare you various surprises, to invite interesting experts, including from among the participants of the meeting.

You can follow the schedule and the nearest meetings via the link: www.meetup.com/Microsoft-Mobile-Dev-Meetup

Information on the first mitap


Date: October 16, Thursday
Time: 18:30 - 21.00
Location: Moscow, st. Krylatskaya, d. 17, bld.1, Microsoft office

schedule


18.30 - 19.00 - Coffee break, meeting of the participants
19.00 - 19.30 - Open screening of Windows 10 (Mikhail Chernomordikov aka mixen )
19.30 - 20.00 - Discussion, answers to questions
20.00 - 20.30 - Open showing of the Xbox One (Andrey Ivashentsev aka Inc )
20.30 - 21.00 - Discussion, answers to questions
21.00 - 21.30 - Completion of the event, transfer

check in
