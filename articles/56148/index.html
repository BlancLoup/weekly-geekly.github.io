In the reader's regiment arrived: Foxit eSlick

At the end of 2008, Foxit , a developer of a program for reading PDF files, released information about the device (own production) for reading electronic books that will soon appear on the market. A reader, called eSlick , began selling recently.


So, the Foxit reader is only 9 mm thick, and weighs 180 grams. It looks (judging by the screen, of course) more or less decently. 6-inch display in eSlick has a resolution of 800x600 pixels. Well, the board - 128 MB of internal memory, SD card slot (2 GB card included) and USB 2.0 port. Supported file formats: PDF and TXT. Not much. But bundled with the reader comes with software, with which you can convert into PDF any text file. The functions of the device include the ability to play music in MP3 format. One eSlick battery charge is enough to update 8 thousand pages.

Foxit eSlick costs $ 299.99.
