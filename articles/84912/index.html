On the other hand, thanks to this approach, we sometimes have the opportunity in these most junior models to return the goodies inherent to the older ones, but by paying the price of the younger one.

Here it is - the hero of the topic, 32LH3020, recently purchased.



Usb port is at the back. And even though it says service only, something tells us that this is not quite so :) The default menu looks like this:



Here you can find out that in order to get the goodies we need, you need to go to the service menu. On firmware version 3.15, this could be done by pressing the OK buttons on the remote and the TV at the same time for 5 seconds. If your TV has firmware 3.48, then when you try to roll back to 3.15, you can turn the TV into a brick. To this brick, then it will be necessary to flood the com-port through the hyper terminal with the correct firmware or even carry it to the service.

This is not our way. The service console has an ADJUST button that allows you to get into the same menu.
