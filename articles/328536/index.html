Voting started for YiiConf reports

Good afternoon, colleagues! Many applications came to YiiConf - 12 reports and 2 master classes. For the first time, four developers from the Yii core team will speak at one conference.



Carsten Brandt - one of the most active framework developers, known as @cebe, comes from Germany, and can tell you about interesting details about the work of the database abstraction layer. He also gave a report on the implementation of the search by means of Yii and Elastisearch.


Alexander Makarov, samdark , filed applications with reports on future versions of Yii and life experience in open source.


Pavel Klimov, @ klimov-paul, a member of the team and author of many modules of Yii 2, is preparing to fully talk about migrations.


Dmitry Naumenko, SilverFire , gave reports on DDD, profiling, dependency injection and practical use of the framework.


Dmitry Eliseev, ElisDN, the author of educational materials and books on the framework, will tell you where to put the application code.
