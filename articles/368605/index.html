Tesla received twice as many pre-orders for the Model 3 than planned

Tesla Motors received more than 250000 pre-orders for the Model 3. Now the maximum capacity of the plants is 50,000 electric vehicles per year.


Ilon Musk can be pleased with the work of his team

Competent work of Tesla Motors in promoting the new electric vehicle Tesla Model 3 has borne fruit. The company received twice as many pre-orders for the new product as it had expected. In just the first day, Tesla received more than 100,000 pre-orders for an electric car worth $ 35,000 (basic version). Now the number of pre-orders exceeded 250 thousand.

Another surprise for the company was the queues at the Tesla Motors showrooms. As already reported on Geektimes, some potential buyers took their places in the queue a couple of days before the start of pre-orders. Many came on the old Tesla models in order to order a new model. According to estimates of the head of the company Ilona Mask, the total number of people in the queue per one salon was 800 people on average. The calculation was based on 20-30 people.
