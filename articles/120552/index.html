Ukrnames EPP - Universal Domain Registration System

Good afternoon, dear readers habrahabr!

Would you like to talk about the development of the ‚ÄúCenter for Internet Names of Ukraine‚Äù (Ukrnames) - EPP-domain registration system (Ukrnames EPP).

Ukrnames EPP is an automated system that allows you to register and manage domains in multiple domain zones using a single, standardized protocol - EPP ( http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc4930 ).

EPP has long established itself as one of the world's leading domain registries. Today, the EPP protocol registers domains Verisign (.com, .net, .name, .tv), PUBLIC INTEREST REGISTRY (.org), Afilias (.info) and many other popular TLDs.

However, not all domains are registered using standardized and modern protocols. If you consider any 10 domain zones served by different administrators, in order to register a domain in each of them you will have to integrate at least 5-10 different protocols - this could be EPP, RRP, SRS, XML-RPC, Email Protocols and many others. In addition, you need all the time to take care of the balance in each of the domain administrations.



With Ukrnames EPP, partners get a single interface (EPP + Web) to work with more than 120 domain zones, a single balance with convenient replenishment methods, Russian-language support and best prices - Ukrnames is an ICANN-accredited domain name registrar and official registrar in many regional domains (.UA, .COM.UA and others), which guarantees partners receive domains from the "first hand".

Hosting providers are available ready-made integration modules with Ukrnames EPP for popular billing systems: Rootpanel, Bpanel, WHMCS, BILLmanager. If you have your own system, you can use ready-made EPP-libraries and a separate, test Ukrnames EPP, which is provided for developers.

Ukrnames EPP features:
  • Registration in more than 120 domain zones
  • Domain renewal
  • Domain Transfer
  • NS server management
  • Domain host management (child NS / glue records)
  • Domain Contact Management
  • Manage Privacy Protection Options


All operations are performed in real time.

Summarizing the above, it is safe to say that Ukrnames EPP is the best system in its class.

Firstly, Ukrnames EPP offers a wide selection of domains available for registration, without intermediaries, from the official registrar. Secondly, Ukrnames EPP unifies a variety of protocols of multiple domains - you connect and work on a single command once. Thirdly, using EPP Ukrnames - you get qualified support in your native language, convenient and reliable service. It is important for everyone to have reliable partners, whose guarantees and decency can always be counted on - and not only at the best of times.

We understand this and look forward to long-term cooperation.
