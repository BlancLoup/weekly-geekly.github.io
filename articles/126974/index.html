Yet Another Conference 2011. Registration is open!



A little less than a month is left before the start of the second technological conference of Yandex , which will be held on September 19 . The venue of Yet Another Conference remained the same - the World Trade Center in Moscow. Today we open registration for this event.

The format of the conference has not changed: speakers will talk about useful and relevant technologies - about mathematics in computer science, programming techniques, security, mobile technologies, client development, administration, testing, distributed storage systems and computing.

As in the past year, the speakers will include representatives of leading Internet companies such as Google, Opera Software, Kaspersky Lab, loudera, Sturmann, Cloud9 IDE, Nigma and, of course, Yandex . In addition, representatives of the academic science, researchers from the University of Tel Aviv and ISP RAS , will speak at the conference.

The YaC2011 program has become even richer. Unlike the previous event, the reports will be read immediately in three streams, the third of which will be held in the format of master classes, and a technological exhibition will be held in the lobby.

Participation in the conference is free - you only need to register . We are waiting for developers, technical specialists, students of technical universities. Come, it will be interesting!
