5 reasons to buy a new "flagship": Samsung Galaxy S5, Sony Xperia Z2 or HTC One M8?



They lust and hate at the same time. They are beaten to death, and then drowned in water or forgotten in public places. For their sake, they are starving and even commit actions that fall under a whole series of articles of the Criminal Code ... Flagship smartphones, devices standing on the top step of electronic evolution ... And if without pathos, then we have Samsung Galaxy S5, HTC One M8 and Sony Xperia Z2 in the review.

This time we decided to move away from the usual scheme - direct comparison of devices - because you probably already know everything about them. Moreover, these flagships are designed, it can be said, on a single mobile platform, and represent the evolutionary development of smartphones of a particular brand. But, of course, they have differences. For each of the devices, we will list five chips, thanks to which you can make your choice.

Samsung Galaxy S5 - we waited for it for four years - if you count from the moment the first model appeared in the Galaxy S line. As is the case with iPhones, the owners of the Galaxy S4 are unlikely to leave everything and run to exchange their junk for a supernova. But for lovers of antiques like S3, and even more so for the antiquities of the first two generations, we would quite recommend to look at the "five".

On the other - no longer always means better. The new flagship, in fact, is more correct to consider a modified version of the Z1, and not an absolutely new smartphone in the line.

But if you recall the budget matrix Z1, then the updated screen really becomes a chip that can affect the consumer choice.

In general: Samsung is evolving cheerfully, HTC has made a qualitative leap, and Sony has done a decent job with the bugs. What will be their fate? The S5 will be the most popular flagship, the M8 will be acquired by people with plastic allergies, and the Z2 will be beach lovers and brand fans. It's simple.
