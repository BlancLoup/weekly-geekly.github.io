Finam holds online conference "Runet: points of growth"

Today at 13:00 Moscow time on the website of IC "Finam" an interactive conference on the theme " Runet: points of growth " will be held . The purpose of the conference is indicated in its theme - to discuss the prospects and directions for the development of Runet.

Questions are accepted both on-line and in advance .
According to the regulations, you can ask a question to any of the conference participants. The asked questions do not immediately appear on the site - at first they will be authorized by the administrator of the conference. At the same time, it is not necessary to indicate in the question of a specific participant of the conference to whom he is addressed - each participant can answer the question. After someone from the conference participants answers the question, users will be able to express their opinion or leave comments on this topic.

The number of comments is not limited, in addition, each of the conference participants can respond to comments, so the press conference will take the form of a discussion on a specific topic, where users, without asking new questions each time, but only commenting and complementing previous questions and answers will be directly and actively involved.
