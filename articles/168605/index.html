Mini flowmaster overview

This review focuses on the Notsen mini FLOWMASTER model, which provides wireless transmission of audio-video content over a distance of 25 meters through obstacles and is compact in size.

mini FLOWMASTER is popular with laptop owners who have to display the image and sound on a TV or projector screen, and the use of wires is not convenient or possible.

Appearance

Unlike the older brother - FLOWMASTER, this mini FLOWMASTER model has a portable transmitter (transmitter), which allows using it in combination with a laptop or tablet.

Transmitter
Above - network and video indicators, code button (to add multiple devices to the network). Mini USB-input for power supply, HDMI-input for connecting the transmitter to the signal source.
Dimensions: 98x29x14,5 mm.


Receiver.
Front - network and video indicators, infrared sensor. Rear - HDMI-out connector for the power supply.
Receiver dimensions: 148x97x18 mm.



The transmitter and receiver are made of plastic, the surface of the receiver is glossy and smooth, the transmitter body is made of matte soft-touch material, which does not leave fingerprints, and which prevents slipping from the hands.

Connection:

Transmitter - to a laptop

We connect the transmitter to the laptop via the HDMI connector, connect the power cable to the USB connector.



In the case when it is not possible to provide power to the transmitter via the USB connector, you can use a 5V power adapter. If the signal source to which the transmitter is connected does not have a USB, but a micro or mini USB output, then an appropriate cable or adapter will be needed.



Receiver - to TV

The receiver is installed on the stand, we connect the HDMI cable to the TV. We connect the receiver to the network.



Turn on all devices. Select the video input from the TV menu to which the FLOWMASTER is connected. After 10-15 seconds, an image appears.

Network creation: two transmitters + one receiver

All models have the function of creating a network - several transmitters can be connected to one receiver.
We take the second transmitter, connect to the computer, which is located in the bedroom. Add a new device to the network is very simple. In the menu of the FLOWMASTER receiver, select the item ‚ÄúAdd new video source‚Äù.



The message ‚ÄúPlease register on the transmitter‚Äù appears on the screen. To start the registration of the transmitter, you must press the code button on it. To switch sources, which in our case are a laptop and a computer, use the included remote control.
