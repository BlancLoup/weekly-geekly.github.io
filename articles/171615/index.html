Spring warming at DevCon 2013: ticket prices are melting



Dear software developers and testers, on behalf of the team of organizers of the DevCon 2013 conference, I am pleased to announce a campaign dedicated to the first days of spring 2013.

In honor of the first days of Spring, we reduce prices for participation in our conference and offer
special participation price of 13,000 rubles . for all users who have requested an account in the period from 5 to 12 March.

Register on the conference website, request an invoice for payment of tickets
and get access to two days of technical buzz at the lowest price.

About DevCon 2013


DevCon conference is not the first year and has already managed to catch the fancy of Russian developers and guests from other countries. A key feature of this series of conferences is its country format, the venue is carefully chosen from the best guesthouses near Moscow . At the same time, all conference participants drove into a country holiday home and lived there for two days of the conference, receiving all the pleasant advantages of these places.

The key topics for DevCon 2013 will be the following:

  • Modern client applications
  • Mobile development
  • Cloud computing
  • Software Development and Lifecycle Management Tools
  • Development Technologies and Programming Languages
  • Development, monetization and promotion of applications for app stores
  • Modern Design Microsoft
  • Corporate development, data processing

In addition, conference participants will have unique access to various learning activities outside the main program:

  • school of functional programming
  • intensive software testing training
  • master class by Gaidar Magdanurov from Runa Capital
  • master class from guru Agile Askhat Urazbayev and Viktor Strelkov on the subject of theory and practice of Scrum

Details about the DevCon 2013 conference you can read in this announcement or on the official website http://www.msdevcon.ru/ .

Registration for the conference is already open ! Tickets are sold quickly. Hurry up to take your seats!
