The Case of Dr. Lester

Games, like everything else, have their own life. They are born, live, grow old and die. But among them are those that overstep the verge of everydayness. They, like authentic works of art, are out of time and space.

When in 1993 I first met Another World, I immediately made a mistake. Having seen the Delphine Software logo at the beginning of the game, I decided that it was made by a large team of the company. Then it could not have occurred to me that this is an author's project created by a single person named Eric Shayi.

15 years later, I got into the disc with the film where I first saw it.

Eric Shayi, who sits opposite, smiles and fine lines form around his eyes. He looks about 35 years old, thin, short-haired and confidential. In all its appearance modesty is felt, which, it seems, is about to turn into shyness. But as soon as he begins to talk about his work, a gleam of enthusiasm appears in his eyes, and his hands begin to actively gesticulate.

- Actually, I don‚Äôt like telling the story of Another World. - he says. - In my opinion, the game itself makes it much better. I just created the images, but they already create their own history.

Eric was fond of computers since childhood. At school, he got into an electronics study course where he began programming. At his disposal was a computer Oric 1, on which he after a couple of months wrote two very simple games, which, nevertheless, he was able to sell. The hobby turned out to be more serious than it was possible to initially assume. Eric even left his studies for the sake of it and began to develop a game called ‚ÄúLe Pacte‚Äù on the new Amstrad machine. And ahead of him even more ambitious work "Les Voyageurs du Temps". It was the first game released under the Delphine Software label.

By that time, Eric, who had mastered simple arcades, where the main task of the player was picking up the maximum number of points, was engaged in more serious things. Now he was more interested in the atmosphere of the game, how it affects the player, his emotions. Later this will be the basis of his main project - Another World.



Graphics


Work began in 1989. Shayi was looking for a drawing technique in which the game will be created. He made sketches on paper, using a pencil and pen, tried to create images in Deluxe Paint on a computer. Neither the first nor the second satisfied. He wanted to do something unusual, going beyond the generally accepted framework, something that had not existed until now. But the Amiga computer on which the game was programmed had serious technical limitations inherent to the machines of that time. So, the RAM was only 512 kilobytes and it was impossible to fit large raster images into it.

Then Eric decided to use vector graphics in his work, where it was possible to depict anything using a set of polygons using basic coordinates and color numbers, thereby minimizing the amount of computer resources used. There was no suitable toolkit and I had to write an editor program myself in BASIC. She allowed to significantly speed up the work. In the editor, you could change the animation and see the result without recompiling the game code.

The most interesting thing is that for the revitalization of objects and characters, a rotocopic technique was used. Those. literally time-lapse manual redrawing of the video on which Eric captured his own walk. This was supposed to add to the game of cinematic. So Leicester and his Aboriginal friend learned to walk, run and jump. Rotocopy, in particular, helped create the introductory video of the game, which explains how the protagonist found himself in a parallel world.

At that time, Anothe World was the second game in the world, made with the help of this technology. The first, to the best of our knowledge, was Jordan Mahner's "Karatek".

- This is the car I repeated in the game. - Eric removes from the shelf mock black Ferrari. - On it, Lester at the very beginning of the game comes to the laboratory. And this is a model of a laser blaster, which he uses after teleportation. I glued it out of cardboard. It turned out a bit too big, but this is not essential, in this case the shape is more important, not the size. I shot it all on camera and redrawn it in the editor. Some objects I used in different episodes of the game several times. For example, parts of the landscape, doors, openings and walls of buildings.

Erik had only 16 colors at his disposal - the maximum amount his computer could display. It took a whole week to determine the original palette! It is thanks to such a careful selection of colors and a somewhat rough drawing of the game look so picturesque and conceptual.

The main character


Jean Francois continues to talk about the memories of fifteen years ago.

- The main character is a small character Dr. Lester, this is of course Eric. He acts carefully, sometimes falls into melancholy - he is quiet and harmless. But if Eric starts doing something, he will not be stopped.

It is not surprising that almost everyone who meets him on the way, trying to grab him or kill him. Leicester looks like a stranger in this dark and harsh world. But appearances are deceptive.
