Started selling tickets for the conference Droidcon Moscow 2016

On September 22, the third largest Eurasian conference of Android developers Droidcon Moscow 2016 will be held at the Technopolis Moscow Congress Center.



Last year, speakers from Google, Twitter, Facebook, Sports.ru, Intel, Mail.Ru Group and other well-known companies gathered more than 1,000 participants. A unique three-day program, an exhibition of gadgets, DemoCamp for startups, as well as exciting activities from partners Droidcon Moscow 2015 - read the details here .

This year, the business program will be divided into four main areas: Android, Firebase, VR and IoT. Reports will be presented by foreign and Russian developers and experts on work with new technologies and devices from Google.
