First aid for office stress

Some were lucky to work at home. Or in your own office.

Others are less fortunate and have to sit in a cube or a huge noisy room. Disadvantages give rise to stress. The desire to slightly strangle colleagues. Pour it coffee in the keyboard. Or do something else nasty.

The most effective way out is occupational therapy. But in the office there is usually no opportunity to take a shovel and dig up a garden bed. And the attempt to move furniture to relieve stress can be understood very wrong. Therefore, the occupation should be quiet. Kind Do not require complex tools.

Fortunately, there were people who found a solution to the problem. We will need things that are in any office: a printer (better if it is in color), paper, scissors, and glue. Taking the patterns (in PDF), which are located on the wonderful site readymech , you print them, cut them out and after bonding you get double benefit. On the one hand, you have a wonderful toy in your hands, which, depending on the quality of the performance, can be presented to your favorite boss or guard who got nasty with you last week. On the other hand - stress as it happens.
