Developed a device that signals cheating on lessons from the Internet


An eleventh-grade student from the Moscow region, Andrei Kosinets, invented a device that will help teachers identify students using mobile Internet as a cheat sheet. As stated by the young inventor, the device determines the level of electromagnetic radiation that emanates from mobile phones the moment they connect to the Internet. He began to create the device three years ago - it was then that he began to wonder what effect mobile phones have on a person.

Report with Andrey:


At the moment, the details of this miracle device is not so much. It is only known that an ordinary doorbell and a voltmeter were used to create it, which displays the signal strength on the display. Also, the author said that the device "operates at frequencies from 1880 to 450 MHz."

Future plans for Andrei to create a device that will cure people of cancer diseases using electromagnetic radiation.
