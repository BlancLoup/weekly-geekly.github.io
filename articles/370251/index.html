Communication Verification: Xiaomi Portable Radio

Hi, Geektimes! Today, you guessed it, let's not talk about mobile phones. No, smartphones are needed, but imagine this situation: you went to a rally in Europe with two cars, and the connection between them in a certain way needs to be maintained. With different tariffs and roaming, especially moving between countries, you can easily go broke, and the radio will solve this problem without problems even at a distance of a couple of kilometers. And this is just one of many cases of using this device: apparently, therefore, in 2017, Xiaomi decided to release the radio.



In fact, if you sent them to rest long ago, it is in vain - there are many charms in the walkie-talkies that you can feel only when you use them for a while. Firstly, the vast territory of Russia simply forces you to acquire a couple of such devices: cellular communication is far from everywhere, and if you go for mushrooms, go fishing or barbecues a little further than usual, you can risk being left without communication with the outside world in general.

In addition, the material component is also important here. In comparison with smartphones, walkie-talkies are much cheaper, and the only cost for the buyer to communicate with such a device is, in fact, electricity for charging its battery. Even if you use it rarely, in the household, as they say, such a thing is always useful. What does Xiaomi offer us?

The handheld radio has the talker name Walkie-Talkie , the device supports frequencies within 403-470 MHz when selecting the UHF mode. In the VHF band, frequencies in the range of 136-174 MHz will be available to the user. The Xiaomi walkie-talkie itself is simple, but at the same time equipped with everything you need: here is a set of buttons for control (where you can go without them), and a small display where information is displayed for setting up the walkie-talkie. All this is equipped with a snow-white or dark gray minimalist body with a rather long antenna.



Under the upper controls there was a place for a microphone, the speaker was located just below. By the way, using this radio, you can also listen to FM-radio, there is a function of displaying short RDS messages on the screen. The important thing here is the opportunity to share your geoposition with the interlocutor - it will help you out very much in a forest or other unfamiliar terrain, where it is very difficult to explain your location.
