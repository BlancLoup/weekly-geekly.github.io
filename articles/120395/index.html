Yandex.Money now allows you to delete the old account and create a new

An unexpected continuation was my recent history .
I just received a letter from Yandex.Money where it says that I can delete my old account and create a new one with the same login. What a nice surprise! ..

Hello.

We suggest you remove the account to which access is lost, and open a new account with the same login.
You do not have to write an application to restore access - everything you need can be done without leaving your computer.

What should be done?
1. Go to the link: money.yandex.ru/account/cancel.xml
2. Log in to Yandex (enter your username and password).
