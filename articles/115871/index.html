Social Media Optimization smart use

This idea did not let you sleep all night, I share it with you.

Let's start with the concepts. What is Social Media Optimization? (in Russian, optimization of the site for social media in order to attract traffic to the IMHO site)

Many websites have communities in several social networks. networks.


On the site pages are placed a block with a bunch of links to them.

Often transitions to communities this gives little, for several reasons. I propose to improve the situation.

Idea
