Meizu MX2 in the MegaFon network throughout Russia

Dear habrayuzer, we for the sake of sharing with you an important news for us! The Russian telecommunications operator MegaFon and Telecom InfoProekt announce the start of cooperation and the start of sales of Meizu MX2 in the stores of the MegaFon network throughout Russia from May 6, 2013.




April 30, 2013. The Russian telecom operator MegaFon and Telecom InfoProekt announce the start of cooperation and the start of sales of Meizu MX2 in the MegaFon network. The Meizu MX2 smartphone in versions with 32 GB and 64 GB will be available in 535 MegaFon salons throughout Russia from May 6, 2013.

Meizu MX2 cost in MegaFon salons:

  • Meizu MX2 32 GB - 19 990 rubles
  • Meizu MX2 64 GB - 21 990 rubles

The smartphone can be bought in MegaFon stores and in the MegaFon online store .

Meizu MX2 - introduced at the end of 2012, a smartphone with top features, elegant design, high performance and an attractive price. The model is fully localized, certified and has a one-year warranty.

Features Meizu MX2:

  • Operating system: Android 4.1 with Flyme 2 shell
  • Communication: GSM / EDGE 850/900/1800/1900 MHz, UMTS / HSDPA 900/2100 MHz
  • Screen: Sharp New Mode 2 (IGZO), 4.4 ", 1280x800 pixels, touch, capacitive type, aspect ratio 16:10, 347 ppi, 16 million colors, coating Gorilla Glass
  • Platform: MX5S (Samsung Exynos 4412, A9, 1.6 GHz quad-core processor)
  • Graphics processor: Mali-400 MP
  • RAM: 2 GB
  • Memory for data storage: 16/32/64 GB
  • Camera: 8 MP with autofocus, bright LED flash, 1.3 MP front camera, 1080p video recording for the main camera and 720p for the front camera
  • Wolfson Microelectronics music chip for great headphones
  • Interfaces: Wi-Fi (802.11 b / g / n), Bluetooth 4.0, microUSB (USB OTG support), 3.5 mm for headphones and headsets
  • Navigation: GPS, aGPS, Glonass
  • Optional: light sensor, proximity sensor, accelerometer
  • Battery: 1800 mAh
  • Dimensions: 124.9 x 64.9 x 10.2 mm
  • Weight: 142 grams

Benefits and unique features of the Meizu MX2:

  • Stainless steel frame at base
  • Sharp New Mode 2 (IGZO) high-quality, low-power display
  • Unique phone features: call recording "from the line", customizable answering machine for calls, the ability to create a "black list"
  • Supports most common audio and video formats, as well as embedded and external subtitles for video: FLAC, APE, AAC, MKA, OGG, MP3, MIDI, M4A, AMR, WAV for audio; MP4, 3GP, MOV, MKV, AVI, FLV, MPEG, M2TS, TS for video
  • Superuser rights (ROOT) are available through the settings menu
