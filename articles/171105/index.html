VK.Ratsiya: voice messages VKontakte

Millions of people daily communicate with their friends and relatives on the VKontakte network, using either the website or various mobile applications. In both cases, text messages remain the same way to exchange information. Although it is not always convenient. For example, if you are driving and do not want to be distracted from the road, or when you often communicate with friends from different countries and cities (or even from the same city, but with different work schedules or studies), it is difficult for you to get together on Skype to discuss urgent issues. affairs That is why we created a free VK.Ration app for iOS and Android, which allows you to instantly exchange voice messages with VK friends, without being distracted from other matters.

You can chat in private conversations, create group chats and (this is a truly unique feature) use the walkie-talkie in existing VKontakte communities. In addition, the history of messages will be saved on the community wall and each participant can listen to messages in any browser in real time!

Imagine the situation: you left for work and forgot that you closed the cat on the balcony. While the cat freezes, your wife takes a shower. In a familiar situation, you would have called, but she would not hear the bell. Probably, it is better to write a text message or a VKontakte message, but for this you need to look at the road with one eye, and at the phone with the other, and also hit the keys. As a result, you will spend at least half a minute to convey a few words. VK.Ration will allow you to leave a voice message for a couple of seconds, which will be instantly delivered via Push and your wife will see it in the same way as an SMS or an incoming VKontakte message.
