Fly6: HD DVR and LED bike light in one package



Apparently, there are a lot of cyclists on Habr√©, so this small review is devoted to a very useful gadget for cyclists. We are talking about the device Fly6, which is a LED lamp and a DVR.

Fly6 is specially made well noticeable, and the device case is painted in red color. According to the plan, a motorist, seeing a DVR directed at him, will behave more cautiously than usual. In addition, bright LEDs should also play their part.

What can Fly6 do?





Let's start with the camera. HD camera allows you to record the appropriate video quality (720p with a resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels, 30fps). You can write and sound, (16 bit / 32 kHz, mono). To store the video is a microSD card, the volume of 8 GB is enough for 2 hours of video. As soon as there is no more space, a new entry goes, on top of the old file.

The camera lens provides an overview of 130 degrees, which is quite enough for road conditions. The case of the device is waterproof, so you can ride in the rain, there is no problem, there would be a desire :)



The brightness of the LED lights system is 15 lumens. In this case, LEDs can operate in various modes, attracting the attention of motorists.

Of course, the camera can be used not for highways, but during bike rides somewhere in nature, for example, in the mountains. In this case, the video will remain in memory, as a documentary.

The battery of the device lasts for 5 hours of continuous operation of the Fly6, the device is charged via the miniUSB port.
