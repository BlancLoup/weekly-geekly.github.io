Universal bacteria-manufacturers



Scientists from Genomatica Inc. A few days ago they told us that they had managed to grow a special type of bacteria capable of producing plastic without processing oil industry waste or natural gas.
A small, single-celled tube requires only a little more sugar and water to produce 1,4-butanediol (BDO) , which is suitable for the production of everything from plastics and polymer fibers to drugs.
According to the researchers themselves, it will take no more than a year for the natural and eco-friendly process to cost less than current production methods. To do this, it is necessary to achieve a volume of 1.3 million tons of BDO per year.



Today, butanediol is produced exclusively from oil and gas, and the energy consumption of this industrial sector is enormous (and, consequently, pollution).
