The company managed to realize its plans, and users received a unified platform with a single catalog of universal applications.

Most users of the new operating system are satisfied with it. True, not without problems. In particular, this OS is often criticized for the large amount of information collected about the user. The corporation, according to her, collects data in order to improve and improve its products and services. But information gathering is really extensive. This e-mail, and browser log, search history, call log and SMS messages, information on setting up devices and applications. Also collected (not always) data that is entered through the keyboard. Hard disk encryption keys are also transferred to the company, and this option can only be disabled in the Pro version.

Many times, Microsoft was criticized for putting too much pressure on users of previous OSs to upgrade to Windows 10. Users were almost forced to update the system, turned off the ability to remove the update reminder, installed service packs that change user settings , etc. The company even had to pay $ 10,000 for problems encountered when upgrading the PC of one of the users of Windows 7 to Windows 10.

Today it became known that the company received two more claims. The problem is still the same - aggressive tactics of promoting the update of the previous OS on Windows 10. The first lawsuit was filed in Florida. The plaintiff alleges that Microsoft is violating state laws to restrict electronic advertising. In addition, the lawsuit alleges a violation of the rules of the Federal Trade Commission of the United States.

The second lawsuit was filed in Haifa, Israel. The plaintiff claims that the company has updated the OS on his computer without his knowledge. According to a similar lawsuit, as mentioned above, the company paid $ 10,000, plead guilty.

At the same time, the history of the unclosed update window has not yet ended. A number of users claim that clicking on the cross does not close the window while canceling the update, but is perceived by the system as confirmation by the user of the intention to install Windows 10.

All this should stop tomorrow, July 30th. After July 29th, the Get Windows 10 application will be removed from users PCs worldwide. Accordingly, the notification will disappear. True, it may take some time until the application is removed from the computers of all Windows users: from July 30, Microsoft will gradually remove its application from the PC to Windows 7 and Windows 8.1.

The ability to upgrade to Windows 10 for users of Microsoft OS previous versions is not indefinite. The corporation immediately stated that a free upgrade to Windows 10 is available for exactly one year - from July 29, 2015 to July 29, 2016. Today is the last day when Windows 10 is available for free. Tomorrow, users who want to get Windows 10 will have to pay $ 119. Russian users update will cost 7900 rubles.
