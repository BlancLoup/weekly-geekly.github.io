Google+ is even closer integrated with YouTube and Chrome.

Google developers have announced the next update of their social network. This time the update is integration, so to speak. In other words, Google+ is now inextricably linked to YouTube and some Chrome features. About all this, the development team reported on its official blog.

As for YouTube, now the icon of this service can be seen in the upper right corner of the social network Google+. As soon as you hover the mouse over the icon, it appears to be opening up, offering to find and play a video clip at the user's request. As soon as a user enters a query by which he wants to find a relevant video clip, a pop-up window appears with a list of found clips that may be interesting for our user.



If you share videos with friends in the social network of Kindness Corporation, then any user from selected circles (with access to your posts) gets access to the list of relevant videos, and directly from your recording.

In addition to YouTube, the developers have added some extensions to Chrome, these extensions are directly related to Google+. One of the functions of the extension allows you to share with friends and colleagues from various circles of information found on the web. For example, you can send the page where you are now directly to Google+. All your friends / colleagues with the appropriate level of access will find it there.



Another extension for Chrome alerts you about new Google+ events right in your browser. For users of other browsers, developers have released a special toolbar. So, IE users can already install such a toolbar and get access to many of the functions of Google+ directly in the browser.
