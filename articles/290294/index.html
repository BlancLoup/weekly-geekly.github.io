Moscow Transport Department is preparing a car sharing project - a short-term car rental

The Moscow Department of Transport is preparing to launch a car-sharing project: there will be 500 cars in the city that can be used by paying for services from a mobile application.



Carsharing - short-term car rental for trips around the city. Using a mobile application, the user searches for the transport nearest to him, pays for it and gets behind the wheel, after which he leaves it in any place he likes - if parking is not paid and traffic rules allow parking.
