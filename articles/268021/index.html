Microsoft scared users of Windows 7

Last night, Microsoft scared Windows 7 users by accidentally releasing a test update for all users of this OS. Windows Update Center delivered this update, but its information fields were filled with arbitrary characters. The official forum of the company was quickly filled with negative feedback. It was assumed that the update mechanism of the company was compromised.



The size of the update was about 4.3MB, and its status was Important. The company quickly responded and commented on the situation, and also withdrew the update. It is not entirely clear what exactly was in it, but attempts to install it were not successful for users. A screenshot of the update is shown below.
