Some news from the world of Pixar

Future projects of Pixar, what are we waiting for?
Representatives of Walt Disney Studios unveiled an extensive list of upcoming animated releases of the studios Walt Disney Animation and Pixar Animation, which will be released in the period from 2008 to 2012. This was announced at a special conference in New York by the president of Walt Disney Studios Richard Cook and the creative director of Walt Disney Animation and Pixar Animation studios.

Toy robots WALL-E for $ 190
Fresh cartoon from Pixar, as it should be, will be accompanied by a powerful PR campaign, which also includes the release of toys of the same name, robots. Toy robots will be awarded with the ability to move, move hands and head, and also speak some selected quotes from the tape. The price of a copy of the main character will be about $ 190.
