Happy Birthday, Vivaldi !.



Hello! How time flies. You do not have time to look back, and the year has already passed. Behind him - the second, third. And now we are on the threshold of a small anniversary - January 27, three years ago, we released the first public build of the Vivaldi browser!

So what has changed since then? A lot of things. And our team has grown, and the user community also does not remain unchanged - the number of registered users of the vivaldi.net portal has already exceeded 200 thousand. This is very encouraging and forces us to add even more strength to the improvement of our browser, which has also changed a lot over these three years.

Yes, I did not make a reservation - it was ours with you. After all, not only company employees take part in its development, but also all of you, the users. Let's remember how it all began. Three years ago, we asked you to vote for the features you would like to see in the browser. It turned out a list of 78 most popular requests, and you yourself prioritized with your votes , what would you like to see in the browser in the first place. And today you can already see how many of these functions have already been implemented:

Well, for now - congratulations on the anniversary. We have been together for three years, it was a great three years. Moving on at the same pace.
