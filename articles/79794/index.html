The objectives of the site design (Redesigned and finalized specifically for Habr)

Creating a website, a person first of all wants to convey to the public the fact of his existence and the fact that he can be contacted, that he is now also part of the Internet. In turn, the Internet reacts to the emergence of new sites, like a living organism - it either pushes newbies further, provided that the site was created correctly, or leaves unnoticed. It will take a lot of effort to get your website among the billion others to breathe. A lot of effort must be spent on both the code and the design, because the appearance is the first thing that users pay attention to.

The first goal of the site is to keep the visitor as long as possible, the second is to take a step towards attracting attention to their services or products. To realize the first, a special approach to filling the site is required, including a detailed study of all visual materials. This is due to the fact that if you only work on one component, then the person will still leave, because there are no trifles in life that are not important.



If the text on the page is interesting, will be correctly formatted and contain illustrations, then the task can be considered completed. In this case, a person learns something new with interest, he is already yours, and unless you show where to follow him after reading, he most likely will not go anywhere.



It is also worth noting that in our time more and more people grow up on cartoons and comics, we pay less attention to books, from which it can be concluded about the content of your site: it should include many illustrations. Some studios involved in the creation of Internet projects, mainly in Russia for some reason, are focusing on content visualization, not realizing that visitors will notice only a beautiful picture in full screen and, after watching it, will leave the site.

It is necessary to understand the weight of each action you perform, especially when most of your work should appeal to you.
