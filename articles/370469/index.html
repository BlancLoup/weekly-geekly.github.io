REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S Skype Portal Overview: Remote Control of Home Appliances and Work with Smart Security Sensors

Hello! At one time, the official REDMOND blog on Geektimes.ru published a review of the REDMOND SkyPlug RSP-100S smart outlet. It, we recall, can be controlled from a smartphone - both via Bluetooth, while in the apartment, and over the Internet from anywhere in the world. Today we bring to your attention the review of REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S. This is the so-called skyport, that is, it seems to be a socket, but much more advanced. Let's just say: this is the further development of the ideas embodied in the REDMOND SkyPlug RSP-100S. In addition, we will talk about several new REDMOND devices - in particular, the REDMOND SkyCenter 11S smart home center and the REDMOND SkyControl 21S IR remote control.




However, let's start with the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S . All the features of the predecessor, namely the smart outlet REDMOND SkyPlug RSP-100S, in Skype have been fully preserved. Recall - what is at stake. The skyport is plugged into a standard outlet, after which household appliances are connected to the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S. It could be an iron, TV, lamp, water heater, curling iron - anything. After that, they can be turned on and off from any distance using a smartphone with the Ready for Sky app.



Take the same iron. If you suffer from forgetfulness in the operation of this unsafe electrical appliance (do not hesitate, so many suffer from it), REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S can destroy this problem once and for all. Even when the user goes to the subway or minibus to work, he can with one click on the virtual button to cut off the power supply to this very iron. So go back home to disable it is not necessary. Yes, for this you need to get a home smartphone-geytveem, which will be connected via Bluetooth with Skype, and over the Internet - with your personal smartphone, but still it is a great way to solve issues from the category "and I just turned off the iron, or the apartment is already flaming ? ‚ÄùIn addition, you can use the REDMOND SkyCenter 11S device, which we describe at the end of this material, in place of a smartphone gateway.



Another example. If you suspect that children instead of doing their homework, spend time on the Xbox, you can block the power supply to the TV or to the console itself. That's right, better pay attention to mathematics than to some GTA. However, if you adhere to more liberal views on upbringing and you think that games are also needed, then you can set up a schedule of energy supply on the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S. For example, from 14:00 to 15:00, let the children smash the enemies, and then - everything, the TV screen darkens, and it's time for the guys to learn.

Another useful option is the timer: REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S allows you to customize the operation of the connected device in a specific period of time. Water heater thanks to REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S can also work on schedule. Heating water to your return from work or, for example, coming from vacation. The main difference between the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S and the REDMOND SkyPlug RSP-100S smart socket is the ability to interact with various kinds of external sensors REDMOND, which significantly expands the capabilities of the skyport and turns it into one of the centers of the smart home.

What is the point? The REDMOND sensor line includes, among other things, smoke, door opener and motion sensors. Here they are just and can work in conjunction with the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S, being connected to the skyport via radio. If the smoke detector realizes that something is burning in the room, the skyport will instantly turn off the appliance - for example, a stove, heater or kettle. A motion sensor can capture your presence in the room, and the luminaire connected to the REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S will only be lit when someone is in the room. And, of course, turn on automatically. Approximately the same with the door opening sensor: the light will illuminate only when someone enters. Thus, it will not be necessary to grope a switch in the dark with your hand each time.

It should be noted that the skyport itself costs about 2,000 rubles, and each of the sensors is less than 1,000 rubles. So, everyone can afford to equip their housing with such a system.

Also REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S is able to work with the REDMOND SkyClimate climate station. The point is this: the latter monitors the temperature and microclimate in the room, and in the event of a change in the situation, sends to REDMOND SkyPort RSP-121S a command to turn on one or another device. For example, if it became too hot in the room, the fan connected to the skyport is activated, if the heater is too cold, and if the humidity falls below a critical level, a humidifier. The thresholds for temperature and humidity are set in the application on the smartphone.
