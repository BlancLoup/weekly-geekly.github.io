Budget Android, Acer beTouch E110 is already available for 7990 rubles.

Euroset began selling Android smartphone Acer beTouch E110. The smartphone is equipped with a 2.8-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of QVGA (240 x 320), there is no QWERTY keyboard, but there is a five-way joystick. Among the main characteristics of Acer beTouch E110 - 416 MHz processor, 3.2 megapixel camera, 3.5 mm audio jack, GPS receiver, FM, 3G, support for Bluetooth and microSD. Of the disadvantages of the lack of Wi-Fi and Android 1.5.



Included with the smartphone attached "Navitel Region +" with the support of the free service "Navitel. Traffic jams" throughout the country. In addition, 300 MB of GPRS traffic from Beeline are included, which can be used for 3 months (100 MB per month).
