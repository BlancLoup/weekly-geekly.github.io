How to build a studio for shooting video with minimal cost and for three days

Vasily K., producer of NSMedia studio filmed lectures for our project BeSmart.net. In the meantime, he told us the story that shooting a video is not at all difficult. For this, almost nothing is needed except desire and resourcefulness. A studio for shooting video lectures can be assembled on the knee, a documentary film can be made on an iPhone.



The story happened six years ago when I wanted to get a contract for a series of training videos for employees of one production company. The customer has set a condition: to build a studio and shoot the first video, and if he likes the work - my contract. I fired up.

There was no money at all at that time, and all the equipment of our studio was on other shootings. But the customer was not necessarily aware of this, but I had to get out, and I began to call my friends and acquaintances, trying to borrow money. I called everyone in a row. For those who at the moment could borrow only a thousand rubles, I took a thousand.

If there is no money at all

An analogue of any professional equipment can be assembled on the knee from what is at hand. When we shot a video review of routers for the website Overclokers.ru, we did not have a special table for subject shooting, on which gadgets are being filmed. Then we put the router on white A4 sheets and sent the light of two table lamps to it. In a nearby supermarket, we bought light bulbs for them that give cold light. And so that this light was not bright, we closed the lamps with A4 sheets, imitating the diverters.
