Christmas sale on Steam



In the most popular sales service of digital games for Steam PC, another major sale began, this time dedicated to the Christmas holidays. As during past sales, until the sale ends on January 3, Steam will offer nine different games with discounts up to 90% with daily updates of offers, four games with updates every eight hours and an additional one game of community choice from three different options. This offer will also be updated every eight hours.

In addition to the main discounts shown on the main page of the service, Steam now offers big discounts on many other games. The most popular offers can be found in the section "Bestsellers". In total, more than 2000 games participate in the sale.

Steam sales are a great way to clean up the karma that is clogged up with the nineties-zero, when many of us played games that we did not pay for. It is time to pay for games that we may not play after the sale. Inexpensive, fly!
