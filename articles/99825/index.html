Wireless video

Transferring wireless video from a computer to a monitor / TV is perhaps one of the most popular functions on the market today. There is even a number, in my opinion, of very poor devices, such as FM transmitters, only for video transmission: a computer or other other video signal is wrapped in a television broadcast signal and your TV just catches the channel created by you with a special receiver. The signal is limited to the specifications of today's TV standards, for example, for PAL, the maximum screen resolution will always be 720x576 pixels. Similar to the compound tulips. Their only advantage is that the devices are not afraid of any obstacles in the face of walls, furniture, ceilings; have a good range of action - on several rooms / floors.
