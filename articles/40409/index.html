First site at a young age

Hello, my name is Anton. I am 14 years old, I am fond of and love motorcycles. I wanted to create my project and what came out of it, read on.

In the middle of the summer I really wanted to create my website, because in the summer I did not go to the camp, I just sat at home. There were no problems with the choice of topics, immediately decided that the site would be done about motorcycles. Because I know this subject more than any other. I consulted with whitempak, he approved my idea and I was even more firmly established with the desire to create a site about motorcycles.

While reading Habrahabr, I came across a great explay site management system. Immediately realized this is what I need because I am not yet able to do my cms to the extent of my illiteracy in this direction. Without thinking, I downloaded the release. I started testing on localhost , how to test, rather learn not yet familiar with PHP programming. At that time, it seemed ideal for the realization of my desire .

The question arose which domain to register, so that it would be interesting and remembered by users. There were many options, but chose two of them: motorisha (everyone probably remembers the cartoon) and motoyou. I thought that motoyou.ru is more suitable because The main idea was that users wrote about their motorcycles. The next day I registered a domain. Marked site.

Wrote a description to make it clear to users about what the site.

Our Motoyou.ru network is created for motorcyclists and its goal will unite present and future motorcyclists. Our network has a rating system - both articles, comments, and motorcyclists. Each motorcyclist has the opportunity to publish his articles, leave comments, create clubs and meetings, all this gives a good exchange of experience for both beginners and experienced motorcyclists. Beginner motorcyclists can always count on the help of experienced people.




On the first day, June 1, he wrote several publications about motorcycles, notified his friends and they pulled up. On the same day I sent an insider to roem.ru , there I received many positive and negative comments. Many users have registered and wrote about their motorcycles. At the request of users, draw a new design for the site instead of the standard template. But after the level of visits I slept and it made even more time to be engaged in the site, to fill it with interesting publications, which I still do today. Now the site has about 50 users and 150 publications. Of course, you probably say with one voice - that in this such just screwed the engine and crammed articles, for me this is not so. I can not describe that it is for me, but something larger than a set of letters.
