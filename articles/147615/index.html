The project of the game console in less than 2 days collected $ 3 million on Kickstarter



The Kickstarter campaign launched yesterday to raise $ 950,000 in 30 days to release the game console Ouya collected the required amount in just 8 hours. Already, the project has attracted more than $ 3 million and is interested in its investors, what to do with the extra money.

If anyone does not know, Kickstarter is a site for funding creative projects under a crowdfunding scheme. Anyone who wants to receive funding must register and place a project description, and those interested in the project can help him with at least $ 1, at least tens of thousands (depending on the donated amount, you can pre-order the future product at its expense and receive additional bonuses).

So far, the most successful project is the Pebble electronic watch, which collected a total of 10 million dollars. However, the console Ouya , which attracted three times more than the required amount in less than two days, there is every chance to press it.

Having collected 2 million dollars in almost a day, the creators of the project are now asking two tens of thousands of their contributors what to do with the extra money - they also counted on only 950 thousand. The comments are now actively discussing how to supplement the original draft, and there are so many proposals that some ask to arrange a vote.
