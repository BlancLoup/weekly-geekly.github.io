We live and work not in capitals: software development market in Nizhny Novgorod


Nizhny Novgorod. Photo by Stanislav Kaznov

At the event in which I participated, there were presented slides about the possibility of relocating IT specialists in Russia and, in particular, a slide with the cities most attractive for relocation. The first two places in it were obvious, but then there was a place for questions. The third place was taken by Krasnodar, the fourth - by Novosibirsk, followed by Nizhny Novgorod and Kazan. The distribution of the last two cities caused almost indignation among the audience, why Nizhny was higher. I will tell you about Nizhny Novgorod, one of the five largest IT-cities in Russia. Specialization of Nizhny Novgorod companies


Nizhny Novgorod can rightfully be called one of the centers of international IT outsourcing and software development. The city has the widest range of companies by size and geography of work, by specialization and technology. Unlike other locations, Nizhny definitely has its own ‚Äúface‚Äù: the main players in the market are not small web and mobile development studios, not state companies, but big companies developing serious products, developing development tools and research projects. The areas of activity of development centers in Nizhny Novgorod are really diverse: there are projects related to mobile development, incl. development of mobile devices at the level of HW platforms, web development, projects in telecom, banking, billing projects, Big Data and IoT solutions, many automotive projects, computer vision projects, VR and AR, neural networks and machine learning. There is also the development of "hardware" and related software.

Schools and training opportunities


Nizhny Novgorod boasts two excellent institutions of higher education that prepare software developers. The first of them Nizhny Novgorod State University. N. I. Lobachevsky (UNN), the second - Nizhny Novgorod State Technical University. R. E. Alekseeva (NSTU). At UNN, specialized specialists are issued by the Institute of Information Technologies, Mathematics and Mechanics, at NSTU - by the Institute of Radio Electronics and Information Technologies. In addition to these two universities, excellent specialists are graduated from the Higher School of Economics and their Faculty of Informatics, Mathematics and Computer Science, a certain number of specialists are trained by other universities in the region, which have opened corresponding faculties and courses. Just a year, local universities produce more than a thousand technical experts, of whom several hundred choose the work of a developer.


Building of the 2nd building of UNN. Photo Wikipedia NSTU main building. Photo Wikipedia

A significant role in the training and retraining of specialists is played by the largest companies working closely with universities. Back in 2004, the largest MERA organized the Nizhny Novgorod Institute of Information Technologies - the Institute for Advanced Studies and Retraining. Here, according to information on their website, training is provided for schoolchildren, students and adult specialists, 400-500 people graduate a year. Actively develops its cooperation programs with Intel universities, in Nizhny Novgorod - with UNN. Together with the State University, the corporation organized a Competence Center for Wireless Communication Systems. Several dozen students annually do internships at Intel and gain experience and knowledge quoted throughout the world. In addition, Intel holds an annual summer school, which invites not only students of Nizhny Novgorod universities, but also from other regions. Many of them subsequently receive an offer to become part of the Intel team and move to Nizhny Novgorod. Other companies have their own development and testing schools - HARMAN, NetCracker, Auriga, Tecom. By the way, well-known international companies that are not represented by offices in Nizhny Novgorod cooperate and develop their schools: within the framework of cooperation with Microsoft, the Center for High Performance Computing operates, together with NVIDIA the Technology Research Center is established and successfully operates.

Finally, the professional community itself contributes to the training. Perhaps the best example is the free testing schools run regularly by the QA Alliance. This community, organized by enthusiasts, is developing well and is known, including outside Nizhny Novgorod.

Separately, it is worth noting the sports life of the city. Unlike St. Petersburg, where IT-Dominant creates an excellent base for sports competitions, IT-Olympics annually take place in Nizhny Novgorod, organized by one of the industry's enthusiasts. Nikolai Alexandrovich Zelenov once worked in one of the oldest IT companies in the city, Telma (now HARMAN Connected Services), and organized sports events, and after leaving the company, he began to organize the same events on a city scale. The first Olympics was organized ten years ago by three major companies - MERA, Telma and Intel. Today from January to April there are competitions in mini-football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, rifle shooting, chess, darts in which dozens of local companies participate. In addition to this IT Games, other sporting events regularly take place in the city, but without bright IT coloring.

Cost of living and wages


Nizhny Novgorod is far from the most expensive city in Russia and, accordingly, wages are not the highest in the country. By the way, this, along with the number of technicians graduating from universities, and the total number of companies, was one of the factors behind the high-ranking Tholons rating mentioned at the beginning of the article. Nevertheless, if we talk about the salaries of programmers, then we can say with confidence that they are growing at a rapid pace, well ahead of the average salaries of employees of companies from other industries. At the same time, competition between companies led to a wild state of the market, when specialists with a couple of years of experience can get more or the same amount than experienced specialists with five years of experience. Companies opening offices in Nizhny Novgorod, while able to easily offer good conditions and save compared to employees in Moscow. At the same time, newcomers are fueling the growth of supply from long-established companies. Of course, the specialists who work remotely for Western companies can boast the highest salaries.

Instead of conclusion


Despite the serious differentiation of the development market in Russia with a serious bias towards the capitals, it is pleasing that Nizhny Novgorod does not stand still. There are a lot of companies working in the most recent ‚Äúhot‚Äù areas in the city, new players come to the city every year, the connectivity of communities and the IT ecosystem as a whole is growing rapidly. Among the negative factors, I would point out the insufficient attention to the development of the industry by the state and the insufficient development of the city itself as an attractive place of residence. Many recognize that the city has great potential in terms of tourism, but as a resident I will say that it is not yet used sufficiently. It is great to see the emergence of top events of all-Russian and even international scale, such as the Alfa Future People electronic music festival or the Volga Swim sport event, but I would also like to see the work of the state again. I would like to hope that future big events (the World Cup in 2018 and the 800th anniversary of the city in 2021) will transform the city. Nizhny Novgorod in its history has passed a remarkable path - it was a fortress on the outskirts of the country, became a trade capital, turned into the largest industrial center - and now it would be logical to see it as a major IT city. We'll see.
