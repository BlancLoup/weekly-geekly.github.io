Exhibition "WEB-Studio 2009"

October 27 to 30, 2009 at the All-Russian Exhibition Center the WEB-Studio 2009 exhibition will be held. It will be held in the framework of the exhibition "SOFTOOL" .

We offer you to become a participant or a visitor to this bright Internet event of the fall of 2009.

Get a free ticket here:
www.ws2009.ru/visitors

Now about the exhibition itself:

For participants:

The total traffic of the software is about 20-25 thousand in 4 days. Additionally, there will be a campaign to attract people who need a website development. Purposefully.

It is unlikely that someone will leave the exhibition without a few orders. Rather, these will be possible, but here it all depends on you! From the portfolio, the ability to convince and present themselves, technology, etc. For example, we are confident in our abilities. ;)

At this exhibition we are already participating which year (probably 3 years). All these years, at the stand 90% of the time 1-2 managers were continuously employed. Those. There is a constant stream of visitors to the stand. Very exhausting to be honest, but this is the second question ...

In addition, do not forget about the brand component. To get really GOOD orders, the studio must, at a minimum, hear something. Understand that this is not a one-day studio, which appeared yesterday, but will disappear tomorrow.

I think it is worth recalling that so many large customers and government offices use official catalogs of large, specialized exhibitions to select companies during the tender. It happens that companies remove the minimum stand on the same software (especially from the regions), but do not come themselves, just to get into the catalog.

The formation of the market ends.
Creating your own studio from scratch is becoming increasingly difficult.
The NORMAL laws of the market begin to operate. To survive and get good orders you need to seriously deal not only with direct sales. This is not yet felt, but the time is very close.

And whoever started or will start to do it earlier, will take the lead with less investment, and even today!
