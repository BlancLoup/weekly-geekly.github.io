two-books.net - service for those who want to read the best books

Did you have such a situation when you read two or three books at the same time, but somehow everything is very slow, not interesting. Did you think ‚ÄúMaybe I waste my time?‚Äù - there are many books that are much better ... Today millions of works have been written, but choosing the most interesting and useful is not an easy task. All life is not enough to independently, by trial and error, to select the best.

Listening to the recordings of Natalia Grace , I came across an interesting thought: ‚ÄúIf you want to read the best, ask your friends - which two best books they have read . ‚Äù So the idea was born to create two-books.net , an online recommendation service that will allow people to choose books from the list of the best. Two-books.net is a service that will help you read the cream of world literature without spending your time reading uninteresting, primitive, and shallow books.

There are many recommended sites where you can rate a book from best to worst. On two-books.net only the best books for someone are collected, so here you will not see ratings from 1 to 10, or ‚Äú+‚Äù ‚Äú-‚Äù.

On two-books.net each visitor can add only 2 books.
Why two? Because each of us read a lot of good books, but you need to choose only the best of them - only those that changed you. From such books the rating of world literature is formed.

Also, on the site you will not find standard comments. You can write a review only to your book. If it is the best for you, then perhaps you will want to describe what touched you the work, write the reason why you think the book is the best.

When developing the service, I asked the question: why do I need this site?

Options were:

1) For the popularization of literature.

2) To search for like-minded people and to communicate, to exchange recommendations.

3) To revive the tradition when people gathered in book clubs and
shared their impressions about the read literature.

If the site helps to solve these problems, it will be great, but its main goal is to give the opportunity to choose books to read from the list of the best, and not to waste time reading the next set of words in which there is no soul.
