State startup financing

Suppose you have already created a company, you don‚Äôt want to give a 75% stake to investors, but you need to take money somewhere. Help comes completely from an unexpected place - the State. We are talking about 350 000 rubles, which you (attention!) Will give! But you have to pay some bureaucratic fuss and loss of time. We will talk about small enterprises registered in the city of Moscow, as I am writing from my own experience.



Under the program to promote small business development in Moscow (in other regions there are analogues), any small business entity can receive up to 350,000 rubles under the following conditions:
  • It is necessary to invest at least 50% of own funds;
  • You need to have a room and a lease;
  • It is necessary to spend more than 50% of the provided subsidies for the purchase of fixed assets;
  • Reimbursement of rent from the budget - no more than 3,600 rubles. per sq.m. in year;
  • For consumables can spend no more than 20% subsidies.

Conditions are satisfied? Then we act!


ToDo:


  • Register in the register of small pedpriyatiy Moscow;
  • Collect all the necessary documents;
  • Prepare a Feasibility Study and Cost Estimate;
  • Hand over documents and pass a check on the availability of premises;
  • Answer the questions of the expert committee and take the money;
  • After 3 months to report on the funds spent.

It would seem that everything is very simple, but it is not. Throughout the process, there will be a lot of pitfalls, and I‚Äôm going to talk about them in this article, based on the experience of my company (we are implementing OST and technical support).

Registration in the registry


This is the first step towards treasured budget money. To register in the register of small entrepreneurs you need to prepare the following documents:
- Original of all constituent documents;
- Order No. 1 (on the appointment of the head);
- Application for registration in the registry .

After that we go to the department website , find the address and phone number of the branch in the district where your company is registered. Check the phone time and go with all the documents to the branch. If there is no queue, documents will be accepted fairly quickly.
They also gave me information brochures and offered to sign an agreement on the protection of small enterprises. Let me explain what kind of contract. A special headquarters has been created in Moscow to combat illegal checks and raiding.

After signing this agreement, which, by the way, does not oblige you to anything, you can call the hotline at any time and call an emergency crew of lawyers and accountants who will help to sort out the problem. Fortunately, I did not have to use this phone, but it would not hurt to be safe.

So we filed the documents. Registration time is two weeks. In fact, the exchange of documents takes place on Wednesday, so having passed the documents on Tuesday, you will be registered in the registry next week on Wednesday or Thursday.

Collect documents


Now, do not lose time and begin to prepare documents for the application for a grant. The list of documents is very extensive, it took me a little more than two weeks to collect all references and documentary documents, the preparation of the Feasibility Study and estimates. I cite the entire list of documents with comments:
1. Inventory - we do at the very end on the form, nothing complicated.
2. Application for the provision of funds - fill out the form , also nothing complicated. (The image is clickable)

3. The feasibility study is one of the most difficult points, it is necessary to write the feasibility study in the form of the department, taking into account all their wishes. Be sure to consult your district for recommendations on the preparation of a feasibility study. The most important thing is to describe the essence of your business very clearly, to describe it so that any expert, even if he is far from computers, understands. Also a subtle point in the feasibility study is the compilation of a tax table. We must be very careful.
4. Cost estimate with decoding - it is necessary to fill in the cost estimate and decoding on the form . It is advisable to start with decoding, then transfer all figures to the estimates in groups themselves. It is for this estimate that you will spend money and then report - we conclude that this is a very important document.
5. Rental contract - if you have a direct lease, then you must also provide a certificate of ownership of your landlord. If you have sundarend - your landlord's contract and certificate of ownership.
6. List of creditors and debtors - we write in free form. If there are no creditors / debtors, we write a certificate that there are none.
7. Copies of licenses and permits - we photocopy (necessarily b / w), we put a seal, a signature, ‚ÄúThe copy is correct‚Äù. If licenses are not required - we write a certificate that licenses and permits are not required for the activities of the organization.
8. Documents on the appointment of managers - we make copies of orders, if you are the only founder, also decisions on the creation of a society.
9. Personal profile of the manager and accountant - fill in the form . If you are both a manager and an accountant, we duplicate the data and sign in twice.
10. Help from the bank - you can print from the client bank.
11. Documents confirming the availability of own funds - the investor‚Äôs letter of guarantee, funds on the current account, or documents confirming expenses already incurred.
