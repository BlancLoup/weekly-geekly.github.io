In the United States will spend 100 million to fight the Russian and Chinese Internet spies



An interesting program launches the United States this year. The goal of this program is to prevent attackers from taking over various US infrastructure (electrical networks, subways, etc.). It is interesting that after the recent report of the American special services, it became clear what they fear most of all - Russians and Chinese. According to the American fighters against cybercrime, the work of the mentioned infrastructures is constantly monitored by intelligence. services of Russia and China, and the Americans do not know how close they are to the management systems of these infrastructures. So, to clarify the situation and to combat cyber spies, Americans will spend $ 100 million running the program "Perfect Citizen" (Perfect Citizen).

The American special services, in their own words, are not even able to assess the whole situation. As mentioned above, they are very afraid of the fact that the Russians and the Chinese have gotten close to computer control systems for such objects as trains on the subway, airport runways, telephone lines, and so on. The fact is that such objects at the very beginning were created without the backing thought that such networks should be protected from intruders from the Internet.
