The second version of "Tasty"

It is finished! About two weeks ago, the mmm-tasty.ru website was updated . Many have been waiting for this and are now eating the fruits of their patience. For those who do not know, "Mmm ... test" is a service for creating tlogs. Tlogs are a cross between microblogs (for example: tumblr.com) and bookmarks (for example: del.icio.us).

And now more. First, about the ideology of the project. This service helps people write down their thoughts and systematize them, while not straining about how to post a picture or how to correctly quote. All entries are divided into six (in the expanded version nine) categories. It:


just post, link, quote, picture, video, talk



In the extended version (called by simply pressing the "Advanced" button), more is added: music, code, anonymous letter.
Also, the key point of using the service is the simplicity of the posting process. We tried to do everything in the minimum number of clicks.


This is how to add a quote with a preview

The whole process of posting is built in the same way: the images are automatically pinned to the size of the message, the conversation is automatically formatted and arranged, etc. By the way, you can post by sending a picture to an email that Tasty will generate for you, and a picture will automatically appear in your tag.
