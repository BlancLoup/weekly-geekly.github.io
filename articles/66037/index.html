Sysadmin day. How was it

For the fourth time in the last weekend of July, the All-Russian Meeting of Sysadmins takes place. Rally organized Mail.Ru and Softline. The organizers declared three thousand participants, by eye there were more, approximately, by a thousand. At the very first meeting of the system administrators, there were 350 people, and this weekend 10 times more.

Arriving on Friday at a gathering of sysadmins, at one in the morning, we in the dark with the help of flashlights camped, and tried to find firewood at night in the forest, but found beer and new neighbors. Everyone who registered for the rally and confirmed the registration was given T-shirts with the main symbol of the rally - crossed-out kettles.
On Saturday morning, it all started with admin charging. Particularly memorable exercise "chasing lamer" and "fighting strut Heron to fend off users." Those who could withstand exercise - poured kefir. Kefir after Friday was very popular, but they poured it strictly 100 grams in one hand.

The official opening was without long speeches, but with the warm words of the organizers and sponsors. Which was followed by the admin anthem and the raising of the flag.

Director of Softline Internet Business Development, Roman Agafonov, beats a tambourine at the opening of the rally.

The number of computer utensils at the rally exceeded all possible limits. In its various variations, mice, keyboards of various prescription, monitors and other understandable and not very devices.
