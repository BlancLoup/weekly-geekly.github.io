Master in Finland - the experience of the year

Increasing relevance are publications telling about entering, living and working abroad. And since now is the time to try my hand at this - I want to share the experience of my last year‚Äôs admission to Tampere University of Technology.



The story is built linearly - at first I will briefly tell about how life led me to this move. Next, I will describe the stages of receipt, problems and solutions that can help in the coming year. I will also touch on important topics - money and housing.

A bit of history


In the near 2012 I was still studying at St. Petersburg State University of Aerospace Instrumentation and was quietly planning to finish higher education in the direction of Information Security. Along the way, get a job in one small bank. Last year of study inspired optimism, and the bank at least some vague faith in the work in the specialty. One of the New Year's Eve evenings I received a call from my old teacher with a proposal of some interesting work. When I met a man the next day, I learned about a very attractive scientific activity in the city of Tampere, but the main condition was to enter a local university.

Arrival


The application process looked quite clear at that time, and over the past year, I did not notice any special changes. The main charm of studying in Finland is free education. Not every European country can boast such a bonus to youth development.

What was required for admission to the IT program:

  • Complete and print the application form . The admissions office will later require a paper version.
  • Get a notarized translation of the diploma . Or request the original in English at the university.
  • Transcript of records . A similar process is also required for the diploma supplement.
  • Certificate of English surrender.
  • Motivation letter or "why I want to learn from you."
  • Resume , preferably containing at least some experience of scientific activity.
  • Two letters of recommendation from work and / or study. The form is electronic, but again you need to print and sign.
  • Copy of international passport


Details

Since at the time of submission of the documents I was still in high school and did not have a diploma, I had to apply to the dean's office with a request for a current list of grades and an extract ( Letter of information ) that I would receive a diploma in July. It is necessary to have seals of the university and the department. I had to write letters of recommendation in English myself, although by agreement with the signatories. Resume is very useful to prepare using a business CV template.

In parallel, I began to prepare closely for the language exam. The university accepts various certificates for the IT program with the corresponding minimum requirements: TOEFL iBT - 79, TOEFL paper based - 580, IELTS Academic - 6.5, PTE Academic - 62 and CPE / CAE - C. My choice fell on TOEFL iBT and the long return process began the required ability to adequately express and write in English. The minimum threshold seemed low (79 out of 120), but it was decided to spend all my free time on training. The examination is paid, takes place in special centers and must be registered in advance. Even taking into account the training on the book Barron's TOEFL iBT, the exam itself was many times more difficult. Honestly, I was sure I had piled it up. But two weeks later, it turned out that he scored 83 points. It is important that when registering for the exam, you must specify the university code for the e-mail from the exam organizers, but for the actual admission you must submit the original certificate, which is stated to be completed within a month and a half across Europe.

After I prepared all the documents, the question arose of transferring them to the university, which is waiting for them via the same mail. Alas, it leaves much to be desired, so a willful decision was made to go and pass them personally. First impressions of the trip were good - everything was starting to look more real. So, began the long weeks of waiting for a decision. One day, I received an email confirming receipt! Now it was necessary to wait for the original in the mail, which took a few more weeks. With this letter, you can apply for student housing, what a special office - TOAS . The main charm is that housing for students is of rather good quality and two times cheaper than the secondary market.

Visa


To get a study visa to Finland, in addition to the original confirmation letter, you must prove that you will not die of starvation in a foreign country. Namely, an account statement was required. There should be more than 300 thousand rubles (600 euros per month). You also need a completed OLE_OPI form for a study visa from the Finnish immigration service website, an insurance policy for 100 thousand and two photos. After receiving the documents at the consulate and fingerprinting, the longest wait begins - it takes about a month and a half to process the request, then you can start calling and asking about readiness. After confirmation, it's time to go and get your temporary residence permit!
