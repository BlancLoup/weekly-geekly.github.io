Checking mass warning systems in Novosibirsk

Today in the city of Novosibirsk, a routine inspection of mass warning systems was successfully held, from 7.15 to 7.22 in the whole city the sirens sounded.
The exercises successfully coincided with the weather, just at that time there was such a fog in the city that it was difficult to distinguish the neighboring house (the picture was taken later, after washing the underwear).

It seems that the exercises cannot be held at any other time, except at 7 am, besides, the residents were massively notified about the exercises at the time of the exercises.
Thank you for the pleasant awakening and good mood.
