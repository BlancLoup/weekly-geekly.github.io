Testing VZOchat mobile client

I continue to boast about the achievements of VideoPort.

Our company invites everyone to join the beta testing of a new unique video calling application for communicators based on Windows Mobile 5.0 and higher.

VZOchat Mobile application allows you to make a video call to any user of the VZOchat system. To do this, both users must have a computer or a communicator, with the VZOchat application installed, and be connected to the Internet in any possible way - wired Internet, WiFi network, mobile operator network.



For the VZOchat Mobile application, a communicator with a 400 MHz or higher processor running Windows Mobile 5.0 and above is sufficient. It is desirable that the communicator was equipped with a front camera. Currently, the application has been tested on eight devices. VideoPort hopes to expand the list of tested devices as a result of beta testing.

To participate in testing, you need to download and install the application from mobile.VZOchat.com and fill in the feedback form after testing. The most active participants in the testing will receive prizes and gifts from developers.
