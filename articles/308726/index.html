Interview with the first speaker of Moscow Python

The organizers of Moscow Python Conf entrusted me to search for speakers and to draw up a conference program. And I usually make a program so that it would be interesting to go myself. Conference preparation is an interesting thing. When will you have the opportunity to interview other inter-developer developers? Our first hero is Ivan Tsyganov from Positive Technologies . At the conference, he will give a provocative report in which he will tell you why 100% test coverage is bad. Intrigue, of course, no one will reveal. There I learned what high loads are and learned how to solve complex problems when the world collapses around. One day, the technical director approached me and said that I would write in Python.
