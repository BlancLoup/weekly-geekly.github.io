My Circle Results Report for May 2016

Our traditional monthly performance report on My Circle . In May, we had 681 vacancies, for each month, an average of 8.17 responded.

And now in more detail about the responses to vacancies in the most popular areas of activity.



  • As always, we have the easiest way to find a designer , for one design job there were on average 20.7 responses per month.
  • Frontend and management vacancies traditionally occupy second and third places with an average of 11.6 responses per vacancy.
  • At vacancies in backend and administration of about 8 vacancy responses, they occupy their traditional places.
  • This month, traditionally popular vacancies on testing unexpectedly sank, each had on average less than 5 responses.



But what is the overall semi-annual trend in response to the most popular areas of activity.



  • Designers vacancies are answered most by applicants, an average of 20 people per month.
  • On the vacancy of layout designers and managers, on average, 10-11 specialists respond.
  • On the job of administrators, on average, 9 people respond.
  • On vacancies of programmers and testers on average 7 people respond.
  • On average, 5-6 specialists respond to application developer jobs.
