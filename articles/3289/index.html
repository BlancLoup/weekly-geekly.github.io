Communicator BlackBerry 8800 is equipped with a GPS receiver

The company Research In Motion during the congress 3GSM announced the release of the BlackBerry 8800 communicator with a built-in receiver of the global satellite navigation system GPS.



The novelty today is one of the thinnest BlackBerry smartphones: the thickness of the device is only 14 millimeters. The BlackBerry 8800 model is equipped with a display with a resolution of 320 x 240 pixels, 64 MB of internal flash memory and a slot for removable microSD cards.

The novelty is capable of playing MP3, MIDI, AAC audio files and MPEG-4 and H.263 video clips. Among other things, we can mention the speakerphone, the controller Bluetooth 2.0 and a keyboard with a standard layout. The communicator is designed to work in GSM / GPRS / EDGE cellular networks. The preinstalled software package, among other things, includes the BlackBerry Maps mapping application.

The BlackBerry 8800 Communicator will go on sale this month, its estimated cost is not yet specified.
