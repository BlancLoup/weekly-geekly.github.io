Efficient translation

Hello :)!

I had some idea how to improve the translation service from Google ... The idea was this: why not show the first most relevant picture to the text along with the result of the translation? After all, memorizing a text is much better if a visual image is associated with it.

And here comes the Google AJAX Language API . No sooner said than done! Use on health: aLive Dictionary .


Do not be discouraged if the picture proposed by Google is far from the word you want to translate: turn on associative thinking. For example, the word loud you see a computer power supply. Wonderful! Why did it happen? Power supplies create noise. The noise is loud. The association is built. Now, when someone says: ‚ÄúLoud‚Äù, you can easily restore this chain. This is a game.
