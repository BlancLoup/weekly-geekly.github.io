The new Apple iPhone will cost $ 173


The cheapest model of the new phone from the famous company Apple Inc., which according to the plan is about to go on sale, costs about $ 173. In the US, the iPhone will be priced at about $ 199. This updated model of last year's iPhone (Phone) with an improved navigation system, high-speed Internet will be available on July 11, ScienceBlog.Ru reports with reference to Physorg.

Thus, the difference between the retail price and the real value of the new Apple phone is more than $ 26. The retail price is subsidized by exclusive carriers of AT & T Inc., one of the largest telecommunications companies. In fact, this corporation will buy iPhones from Apple at a higher price, then selling them at a loss to itself, but the money will come back with monthly commissions.

In the new iPhone at the price of $ 173 there will be 8 gigabytes of internal memory, a touch screen and a GPS chip, which was not in the first iPhone. The price of the phone does not include the cost of software, packaging, shipping goods or various accessories, for example, headphones. July 11, the new iPhone will go on sale, prices will vary depending on suppliers.
