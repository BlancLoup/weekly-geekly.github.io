The visual language of DRAGON and its application in the rocket and space industry, medicine and other fields. Video report V. Parondzhanova

Wikipedia about DRAGON.

DRAGON is sometimes called the correct flowchart. But first of all, it is great for writing algorithms.
Inside the SPC AP (Scientific-Production Center for Automation and Instrument Making) DRAGON is used with the help of the closed technology GRAFIT-FLOX.

Beyond SPC APC, there are open public environments where real programs can be written in so-called hybrid languages, for example: DRAGON-C, DRAGON-JavaScript, DRAGON-Java, DRACON-C #, DRAGON-Python, DRAGON-Tcl, DRAGON-ASM etc.



The report is presented at the International Scientific and Technical Conference "Systems and complexes of automatic control of aircraft", dedicated to the 105th anniversary of the birth of Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR N.A. Pilyugin. The conference was held on May 21, 2013 at the Institute of Control Sciences of the Russian Academy of Sciences.
