ZFConf 2012 will be held in the spring in Moscow - pre-registration is open

The main innovation of this year is the venue. This year the conference will be held in Moscow , and not in St. Petersburg, as it was before.

Pre-registration is now available. The final registration, as usual, will be opened after the formation of the final list of reports. Pre-registered participants will receive priority notification of this.

A number of experts have already confirmed their participation as speakers. Negotiations are also underway with foreign speakers, including the Zend Framework Core Team.

If you have something to tell the community, write to us through a special form about the proposed report. For non-resident speakers discussed the conditions for reimbursement of travel expenses.

The most relevant topics for reports:
  • Zend Framework 2 - release is coming! Perhaps you are the owner of the invaluable experience of implementation, migration?
  • Symfony 2 and Zend Framework 2 Framework Interactions
  • Stories of real working projects - architecture, implementation, problems solved, development process organization
  • Designing a model in ZF (DDD, BDD, business logic, division into layers, unit testing, ...)
  • Testing projects (Zend_Test, PHPUnit, Selenium, ...)
  • Rich UI applications (JavaScript, Ajax, Flash, Flex, ActionScript, ...)
  • Interesting experience with components from Zend_Services, Zend_Tool, Zend_Search_Lucene, ...
  • Cloud Infrastructure Management with Zend Framework
  • Methodologies and experience in data caching with Zend_Cache
  • Optimization of Zend Framework for work in loaded projects
  • Interesting methodologies, problems, projects with which you had to work? Tell us about them!
Sponsors and information partners are also invited to participate. For all suggestions and questions, please contact us via the feedback form .

Recall also that the site is now available video of the last conference .
