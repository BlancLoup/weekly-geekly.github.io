Picasa starts working with Russian photo services

Google Picasa has finally started working with Russian photo printing services. You can verify this yourself by going to the print ordering section.

Currently, there are two such services - FotoClick and PixArt . The price for printing one 10 x 15 cm photo is 3.50 rubles, plus a 15% discount for every Picasa client. Total out 2.98 rubles.

The benefit in this case is mutual: Picasa is expanding its presence in Russia, and users get the opportunity to conveniently and inexpensively print photos.
