International IT HR conference "find the answer!" # 6

All the most pressing issues for any modern IT company are reduced to personnel issues:
  • how and where to find staff
  • how to evaluate employee qualifications
  • how much to pay employees
  • what to do so that employees do not leave the company
  • how to evaluate employee performance
  • what should the compensation package consist of
  • how should the office of an IT company look like
  • how to create an attractive HR brand
  • ...

We will discuss these and many other questions on October 19-20 in St. Petersburg within the framework of the largest IT HR conference “Find the answer!” # 6
To participate in the conference, we invite HR managers, administrative and technical managers of IT companies.
“Find the answer!” - conferences for people and about people working in IT companies!
