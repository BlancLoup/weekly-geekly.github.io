Mobile Internet tariffs without monthly fees

Recently, many devices using mobile Internet have appeared. These are alarm systems, automation, and car trackers, and navigators with traffic jams. All of these devices consume mobile Internet in very small quantities and irregularly. For example, I have a GSM alarm system that sends a photo of a burglar via GPRS and cannot send a photo on demand. Another example: a car tracker sends several bytes every 30 seconds while the vehicle is moving. Unlimited tariffs for such devices are completely unnecessary. In the usual tariffs of all operators, the mobile Internet costs about 10 rubles per megabyte rounded up to 100 kilobytes, so the same work of a car tracker will be very expensive.



I studied all the tariffs of all three operators in Moscow and found tariffs more or less suitable for devices with SIM cards using mobile Internet. Data relevant to 28.2.2014.


Beeline


Tariff Simple Internet .
Internet: 3.30 rubles for 1 Mb throughout Russia (except for the Far East region) rounded to 1 Kb.
SMS - 1.95 rubles in Moscow and the region, 3.95 rubles in the rest of Russia.
Voice calls are not allowed.

Megaphone


Tariff Moscow Region .
Internet in Moscow: 3 rubles per 1 Mb with hourly rounding up to 1 Kb.
Internet in the Moscow region: 1.60 rubles for 1 MB with hourly rounding up to 1 KB (after 30 MB per day - 0.6 rubles for 1 MB).
Internet in the rest of Russia: 9.90 rubles for 1MB with hourly rounding up to 100 KB.
SMS - 1.60 rubles in Moscow and the region, 3.90 rubles. in the rest of Russia.
Outgoing calls are 2.60 rubles in Moscow, 1.60 rubles in the region, in the rest of Russia - 9 rubles.

MegaFon-Online tariff.
Internet in Moscow and the region: 1.90 rubles per 1 MB with hourly rounding up to 100 KB.
Internet in the rest of Russia: 9.90 rubles for 1MB with hourly rounding up to 100 KB.
SMS - 1.9 rubles in Moscow and the region, 3.90 rubles. across Russia.
Outgoing calls in Moscow and the region: 3 rubles, in other points of Russia - 9 rubles.

Internet - 1.5 rubles per 1 Mb throughout Russia, rounded to 1 Kb.
SMS - 1.95 rubles in Moscow and the region, 3.95 rubles in the rest of Russia.
