Lenovo S750 - a smartphone with IP67 protection class


Lenovo S750 - Lenovo's new smartphone with IP67 protection class.
IP67 protection class is as follows:
  • Dust cannot get into the device. Full contact protection
  • Short dive to a depth of 1m. Permanent work in immersed mode is not expected.

Today, we will test the phone not only with synthetic tests, but also check the water resistance of the phone in practice.

‚ñ∫ Specifications

Screen: 4.5 ", 960x540, IPS, Gorilla Glass 2
Chip: Mediatek MT6589 (Cortex-A7 1.2GHz, 4 cores)
Video accelerator: PowerVR SGX544
Operating system: Android 4.2.1
RAM: 1GB
Permanent memory: 4GB + microSD slot with card support up to 32 GB
Camera: front 0.3MP, rear - 8MP
Connectivity: 2 sim cards (GSM + WCDMA), GPS, Bluetooth, Wifi (802.11 b / g / n)
Battery: 2000mAh

The first in the line of IP67 smartphones from Lenovo was the A660 model. Lenovo A660 and Lenovo S750 unites only IP67 protection class, the other characteristics have become much more interesting.
The S750 has become larger - 4.5 inches, the screen is made using IPS technology and has a resolution of 960x540 pixels. The platform has also changed - now it is a 4-core Mediatek 6589 chip, the amount of RAM has increased to 1Gb.
