March 1 - a free webinar of Softkey and Kaspersky Lab "Kaspersky Open Space - Release 2"

The Softkey software online supermarket and Kaspersky Lab conduct an online seminar on updating applications in the Kaspersky Open Space Security product line, Release 2 version.

The webinar will be useful to sales managers of software in the field of information security, product managers, managers of IT departments, IT specialists.

Date: March 1, 2010.
Time: from 11:00 to 12:00 (Moscow Time).
Topic: "Kaspersky Open Space - Release 2".
Speaker: Aleksey Shlenov, Softkey Expertise Department Specialist.

You can register for the webinar here - www.softkey.ru/webinars/detail.php?ID=1341
The event itself will take place on a special Softkey platform - 217.74.43.246/r86841040

In the webinar program:

* updated application Release 2 in Kaspersky Lab products version 6;
* Main advantages;
* protection technology;
* protection of workstations;
* server protection;
* centralized management tool;
* installing, deploying protection, getting started;
* benefits to customers.

Release 2 version is an update of Kaspersky Open Space Security product line applications for protecting workstations running under the Windows operating system.

Release 2 introduces higher levels of protection and performance, a new administration console, full compatibility with Windows 7.

Note.

To correctly play sound and image on your computer must be:

* Internet access;
* Adobe Flash Player 8 or newer;
* headphones or speakers;
* microphone (if possible).
* You also need to open TCP port 1935 on the firewall.
