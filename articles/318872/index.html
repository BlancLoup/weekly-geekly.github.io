Tips and lifehacks


1. Thursday is the best day to post.


Why?


a) Traffic on weekdays, higher than on weekends. For experienced bloggers - this is not news. And to the rest I will say that there will be no proof, this is insider information (from another IT blog). Although you yourself can compare the performance of your posts in the section Publications ‚Üí Statistics.


b) Voting for the post takes 4 days from the date of publication. That is, the post will cover the audience for Thursday, Friday, receiving the maximum traffic, as well as those people who read Habr at the weekend. Plus for the first two days - the post gains the maximum number of views, and has a chance to fly up to the top of the week.

c) The end of the working week, productivity falls and I want to get distracted. The brain finds a solution: ‚ÄúEnter Habr!‚Äù

d) A lot of companies publish on this day to get into the top of the week in 2 days, and hang there for another 2 days on the weekend. The usual publication time is in the region of 14-16.00 Moscow time.

It is worth doing to add humor, irony, a catch phrase or proof to your post. When surfing, scroll the FB / VK tape - save the picture. We saw an interesting message on the profile blog / forum - save to favorites. A grandiose thought occurred to you - write it in your phone.

6. Accelerate the process of writing an article.


I'll tell you about my personal workflow, as I write articles.


a) SUDDENLY the idea to write an article comes along, and with it also inspiration. Usually this is an early morning on a weekend when the whole family is sleeping, everything is quiet around, there is a car of free time in front, and you are so relaxed in the bath and Her Majesty descends from unknown space. That is why, when most normal people sleep or remove Olivier from the table, I write this post at 7 am on January 1, Sunday.

Ideas may come differently.
There are other triggers that encourage writing: the problem that you often encounter in the process of work, an interesting and high-quality film, book or website, something spied on and overheard on the street, event, corporate party. The options are endless, the main thing is to be toned and motivated in order to have enough strength to arrange everything into a beautiful and high-quality post.

Since I have touched on the topic of motivation , then it is worth writing a few words about it. What to do if it is not? Answer: do not write . Leave a note and a reminder of the idea, but do not exhaust yourself. The quality will be low, there is a chance to push yourself into a corner and eventually burn out .


And what if writing posts is a job ? Money is money, but for me it is still unclear how people on a stream can generate 2 or more posts per day. Perhaps they are very fond of their work and are inspired by the feedback and traffic coming from the article. But what if you are a paid editor? I will be glad to hear your stories in the comments.



Let's return to the accelerated workflow when writing an article.

b) The idea is, the experience is there, it remains to scatter everything and then arrange. When experience is there, then it is necessary to act according to the principle of the NTCCP . Immediately sit down and write your thoughts in a notebook, simultaneously planning the structure of the post. Having typed a critical mass of text, I transfer it to Google Docs . Why? Firstly, a notebook with large amounts of text is inconvenient at all. And secondly, the Word-format will be useful to us later when exporting to HTML. Along the way, I am doing the formatting, highlighting keywords, placing subtitles.

c) The third step is spell checking, formatting, minor editing and search for images for registration (optional). Pictures for the word are saved in \ Dropbox \ Public \ Habr \ Title Articles, so public URLs for them are already available, without hassle with habrastorage. However, dear admins Habra, before publishing an interesting post in their groups in the social. networks, will replace these links to habrastorage, simultaneously formatting the post to shine.


d) And the last step, copying the entire document to wordtohtml.net , putting the <cut /> tag where appropriate , and getting a ready-made layout.

Next, insert the layout in the habr-editor, edit and view the received.
