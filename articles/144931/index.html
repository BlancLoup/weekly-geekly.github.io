Logitech has released a solar-powered wireless keyboard that is compatible with Apple devices.



Earlier this month, Logitech introduced Folio, a keyboard for iPad and iPad2. The recently announced K760 keyboard (also wireless and solar-powered) can work not only with iPads, but also with Macs and iPhones.

The K760 is equipped with a Bluetooth module for pairing with three devices at the same time, it has Poppy-specific Brightness, Command and Eject buttons (seen in the photo).

It is stated that a full charge will be enough for about 3 months of work. The guys from Engadget have tested the keyboard and report that they were satisfied - the stand is stable, the key travel is soft. And externally, it looks quite expensive - a rough silver paint + metal button Power.

The keyboard will cost about $ 80. Start of sales - June 2012 (tentatively).
