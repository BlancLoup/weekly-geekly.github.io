Microsoft acquires Secure Islands: the company is engaged in information security

Secure Islands cybersecurity technologies are already used by a number of large companies, including Vodafone, UBS and Suisse. Microsoft has already worked with this company to improve the Azure Rights Management Service. According to rumors, the amount of the transaction will be about one hundred and fifty million dollars. Secure Islands will continue to offer its services to corporate clients and work with existing customers.



Microsoft has confirmed the purchase of the Israeli company Secure Islands , specializing in information security. A startup is engaged in the protection of data in files of almost any type. The company has been offering its technologies to corporate clients for nine years.
