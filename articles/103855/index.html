Windows Phone 7 Developer Day

We are pleased to invite you to a special seminar dedicated to the development of a new mobile platform Windows Phone 7.
The seminar will be held on September 27 at the Moscow office of Microsoft (17 Krylatskaya St.), beginning at 10:30, participation is free.

During the event, you will be able to look at the new operating system in action on prototype devices , learn technically aspects of developing applications and games, how to monetize and place in the app store.
The workshop is a discussion platform where developers can share their experiences, test their applications and get answers to questions.

We remind you that the launch of the phone in different countries of the world will take place this year, and the application store will open in early October.
From the first day, Russian developers can download their applications, both paid and free, and earn money on sales in the countries launching new phones.

The following reports are planned for the workshop:
  • Overview of the Windows Phone 7 Platform
  • Application Development on the Silverlight Platform
  • Game development on the platform XNA Game Studio
  • Register and distribute applications through the Windows Marketplace
  • Sharing experience: Russian developments for a new platform
The full schedule of the event will be published soon.

Developers are invited to participate in the event on current mobile platforms - Windows Mobile, iPhone, Android, Symbian.
Registration for the event is currently available by invitation only.
To receive an invitation, write to ruwp7dev@microsoft.com information about yourself with examples of your development on mobile platforms.

We strongly recommend that you now download development tools (for free) and register with the Windows Marketplace (for students for free) at developer.windowsphone.com
See you at the bottom of the developer of Windows Phone 7!
