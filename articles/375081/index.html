SmartProgress 3.0 - the next step

The new version has undergone great changes. Not only external, but also internal. Goals, habits, new interface, sub-steps and more are available right now.



Goal Habits



Since our launch in 2012, we have received many requests from you. One wish has haunted us almost since our discovery. These are goal habits.

If the usual goal has many stages and sub-steps, then in the goal-habit there is only a description and a mark panel. The main purpose - to celebrate their progress in any skills and fixing habits.



Rule one: you must set a mark about the fixing of the habit every day (or on the days you have chosen). If there is no mark, then the progress is reset and the habit setting begins from the beginning. The target habit automatically completes successfully after the last mark is set. Early habit can not be completed.

Sub-steps and new format for adding targets



For a long time, only another goal could be made under this step. We have changed this format, now the stage can be made independent sub-steps, while the functionality of the hierarchical goals remained.



When developing the design of a new add-on page, we were guided by the principle of simplicity and universality. Most of all, we wanted to make a convenient mode of displaying the time frame, without any extra clicks and actions. Now, the time frame is always visually with us and helps us to understand the scale of the goal and the duration of different stages.

New interface



This summer we conducted usability testing, which showed that the structure of the site has some problems. Our team decided to combine all the pages related to the user in one place. We hope that this will simplify the work and increase the threshold for new users.



The new structure pulled a whole carriage of changes: new messages, a news feed, a page of friends and of course a personal profile. When developing, we tried to take into account our previous experience.

Platform for Trainers v2.0



Coaches and coaches will be interested in our new product for customer support. We took into account the wishes that we received when testing the first version and did a great job on the interface.
