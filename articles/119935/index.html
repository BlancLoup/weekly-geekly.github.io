Selection of useful foreign hosting resources

Today's selection is devoted to popular English-speaking resources about hosting. We hope that the material will be interesting, as the buyers of hosting services, and suppliers. We tried to make the selection as diverse as possible. On the one hand, it includes resources devoted to issues such as provider selection, evaluations, reviews, on the other hand, they tell about the intricacies of business, data centers, advertising methods, and others are universal and will be equally interesting to customers and hosters.

www.webhostingsearch.com
Service for hosting selection. There is the possibility of an advanced search with various filters. In addition, the site contains ratings of hosts for various categories - budget hosting, wordpress hosting, VPS, dedicated, etc. The ranking of companies is carried out taking into account many different parameters, including editorial reviews, reviews and user ratings, value for money.

Popular rating of hosting companies. There is a large database of reviews and ratings left by the visitors of the project. Hosters in the ranking are conveniently grouped into categories.
