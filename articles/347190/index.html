Since the virtual number from a technical point of view is an IP gateway for calls originating from the public switched telephone network (PSTN), you can connect it to any PBX - virtual or physical. Using virtual number


The features of the virtual number make it possible to use it in various situations, but today we will tell about 4 most important advantages of working with a virtual number:

Work with ad sites

It is no secret that the private ad market is growing rapidly and people often search for various goods and services first of all at such sites as Avito or Yula. Call recording

When working with private customers, especially when it comes to announcements and mobile conversations, it is very difficult to control the quality of service. CRM Integration

Using virtual DEF numbers instead of real mobile phones allows you to integrate your contact center with a CRM system. For example, Hive already supports AmoCRM and several other platforms.
