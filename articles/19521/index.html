Mozilla Mobile Browser UI Samples Published

Against the background of the promotion of Opera Mini and the appearance on the market of new mobile browsers, the Mozilla organization cannot be idle. At least, in this direction there is an active work. In the official wiki, screenshots of the interfaces of two versions of the Mozilla mobile browser are published, with a detailed description of the functionality.

The first browser is supposed to be installed on devices without a touchscreen. It has a corresponding cursor interface.





The second UI is designed for devices with a pressure sensitive screen.
