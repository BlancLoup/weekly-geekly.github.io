When it comes to financing your own startup, many immediately think about attracting investment. But in order to attract investment, the investor needs to give something tangible than just an idea and a presentation. We need real developments, a pilot release, packs of documentation with an analysis of the project and a lot more.

I will not talk about those lucky ones who are just lucky to get into the jet. It is like winning a jackpot.

Let's better talk about how to make a certain model, according to which you can accumulate this initial capital. So, the very first moment that needs to be understood and accepted is time . Since we are not millionaires and not majors, we will need some time to accumulate finances.

The next point is your real income. If your salary is 1 thousand dollars, then it will be impossible to save up enough money to start your project. From here comes the consequence: your work should be well paid, and your profession should be very popular.
