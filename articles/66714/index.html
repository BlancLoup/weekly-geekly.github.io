Stealth NW-2000 - secure laptop with swivel screen



The company Stealth Computer has announced the release of a secure laptop with a swivel monitor and a touch screen.

The NW-2000 is designed for use under extreme conditions and complies with the safety standards of the US Army's MIL-STD-810F for its safety. I will not list in detail how exactly at what temperatures and humidity you can mock at a laptop. Everyone can read it here .

I can only say that the developers claim that the laptop can withstand 26 drops on a board lying on a concrete floor from a height of 90 cm.

The peculiarity of the laptop is a rotating touchscreen (do not poke your fingers, only a stylus) a display with a diagonal of 13.3 inches and a resolution of 1280x800 pixels. Among other characteristics, we note a combination of Intel GME965 chipset and Intel Core 2 Duo ULV processor (1.06 GHz), 2 GB of RAM and 160 GB hard drive.

The laptop is equipped to the maximum with wireless communication modules: 3G, Bluetooth, GPS and Wi-Fi. There is also a fingerprint scanner and three USB ports.

The dimensions of the laptop are 253x344x59 mm, the weight without battery is 4.1 kg. With a battery, the machine works autonomously for up to 6 hours. Sold now. Cost in the minimum complete set of 5 000 dollars.
