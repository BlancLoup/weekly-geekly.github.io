WANHAO DUPLICATOR 8 mini review



The WANHAO DUPLICATOR 8 3D printer, also known as the WANHAO D8, was presented at the exhibition in Chicago. This is a larger and more advanced descendant of WANHAO D7 , in many ways not similar to its predecessor.


WANHAO D8



Specifications


(The characteristics are approximate; the manufacturer has not yet provided the specifications.)

  • Name: Wanhao Duplicator 8
  • Technology: Stereolithography, LCD DLP
  • Illumination: 405 nm (UV), 12.5 inch LCD display
  • Printing area, mm: 277 x 156 x 180
  • Software: Wanhao Slicer
  • Platform: aluminum
  • Accuracy: z = 0.004 mm, xy = 0.05 mm
  • Layer, microns: 35 - 100
  • Printer weight: 12 kg
  • Packing size, mm: 450 x 450 x 600
  • Materials: photopolymers at 405 nm
  • The ability to control via Wi-Fi
  • Price: about 80,000 rubles, stay tuned

About the printer




The development of the D8 is developing the model range of WANHAO D7, one of the best-selling 3D printers in Russia with DLP technology.


The printer has become much larger and more convenient. The working area is enlarged to 277 x 156 X 180 mm, a convenient hinged lid instead of a removable cap, a large user-friendly control screen.

Compound


On the front of the printer is a double USB connector. File uploading and management are also possible via Wi-Fi; an intuitive graphical interface has been developed for this.

Job



Cooling is carried out by two coolers on the sides and through the vents on the back of the unit. Also located behind the power connector and the main switch.


The resolution along the XY axes will be 0.05 mm, along the vertical axis Z - 0.004 mm.



Materials


The printer will print any photopolymer materials polymerized light with a wavelength of 405 nanometers.



This opens up opportunities for the use of inexpensive quality resins.



Price


The exact retail price of WANHAO D8 is still unknown, but it will be one of the cheapest in its class, with a good price-performance ratio.
