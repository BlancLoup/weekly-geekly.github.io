Platform - 2010. Day One

Good evening!

The first day of the technical conference "Platform - 2010" , which takes place in Moscow, has ended. The day was very rich and interesting, only now you can take a little breath.

So, what makes Microsoft's IT community happy today?

First of all, I would like to note the high level of organization - everything was thought out, there were enough places, the Internet worked, the disks were distributed, and tea was drunk. And this despite the fact that about 1500 participants arrived.

A variety of booths, a large amount of materials, the ability to pass a certified exam for free, contests and information from partners, a football competition, xbox - this is not a complete list of what you could do during the day.

The first thing I went to was the recording of an unofficial podcast from Microsoft, which will appear tomorrow morning. It was very interesting to watch the podcast recording process, and, of course, to see the legendary inverted CLR book with my own eyes :-) The podcast is completely devoted to the conference and its participants.

Now about the reports. There were many of them and everyone could choose topics of interest for themselves.

I was on the review of technologies and benefits of Exchange 2010, I got acquainted with SharePoint 2010. I am not familiar with these products closely, but what I heard is impressive.

Most of all I liked the reports on Visual Studio 2010 and Microsoft StreamInsight.
