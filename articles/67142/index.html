Why is it profitable


  1. Do hosters limit on power per rack. 6 microservers eat less than 1 big one. 120W VS 200-350W. Free capacity will be available for expensive servers.
  2. If you take from 6 clients $ 20-30 per colocation of such a ‚Äúcrumb‚Äù, the revenue per 1U will be higher than from $ 80-100 per colocation of a mono server
