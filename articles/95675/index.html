Virtual QIWI VISA has become rechargeable

A virtual QIWI VISA card is a card issue every 30 seconds and a monthly turnover of more than 60 million rubles .

If QIWI Wallet is a payment for services and purchases on the Runet, then QIWI VISA Virtual is online payments all over the world. Virtual Visa has several undeniable advantages:
  • The card can be purchased in one minute at the nearest QIWI terminal - without queues and documents
  • The card is safe, ( there is simply nothing to steal from it ) since it is bought exactly for the amount needed for a particular purchase.
  • Using QIWI VISA, you do not put your credit and payroll cards at risk.
The commission, at first glance, is not the smallest - 5.26%. But if we recall that the purchase of ordinary electronic money often costs more, + to take into account the universality of the card (such a payment window to the World) - the commission becomes quite adequate. And in some transactions, even with this level of commission, we not only do not earn - we lose money.

However, one of the drawbacks of the card, for a long time, was considered the impossibility of its replenishment: the money was deposited on the card 1 time at check-in, and then it was only spent.
