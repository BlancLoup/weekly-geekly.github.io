Internet Marketing School in Nizhny Novgorod Higher School of Economics

The Business Development Group, together with leading Internet companies in Nizhny Novgorod, organizes a full-time Internet Marketing School on the basis of the Faculty of training, retraining and advanced training of specialists of the Higher School of Economics. This is the first and so far the only official program in Nizhny Novgorod, at the end of which students will receive a State Certificate.

School classes begin as early as May 24, 2011. The cost is 8,000 rubles. The training is conducted by the leaders of Nizhny Novgorod and Moscow Internet companies, as well as invited guest speakers from the largest Russian Internet holding companies.

The training course is designed for 72 hours, during which group members do not just get theoretical skills, but learn effective methods and techniques using real examples, receive a lot of valuable recommendations on all issues relating to the methods of online promotion of goods and services.

Anyone with a completed or incomplete higher education can enter the course. Under the terms of admission, students and graduates of HSE are granted a 10% tuition discount.

In addition, training in Internet Marketing School falls under the cost recovery program, according to which physical. A person can return personal income tax at a rate of 13%, and an employer company that has paid for the training of its employee may receive a substantial portion of the funds back. The fact is that the Ministry of support and development of small business of the Government of the Nizhny Novgorod region from February 1, 2011 provides grants for training from the regional budget. Now small and medium-sized businesses, as well as individual entrepreneurs who have paid for the training of their employees, are entitled to reimbursement of expenses - up to 80% of the cost of the program.

Promotion of goods and services today is impossible without Internet marketing tools. PR in social networks, maintaining a corporate blog, website optimization and placement of contextual advertising is only a small fraction of all the activities that make up the Internet promotion complex. However, such specialists are not trained in universities, and to date, the only opportunity to learn Internet marketing has been self-education and practical experience.
