SMS-Banking and Mobile Internet Banking

Welcome, residents of Habr!

We continue to review the possibilities of our internet banking for mobile devices. We have already talked about applications for our Internet Bank on iPhone and Android .

Today, we continue and tell you about two more forms of our Internet bank.

SMS Banking


SMS-Banking is a program that allows you to pay for services from your mobile phone around the clock that you use daily without being connected to the Internet .

To get started, you just need to install the program "MIG24" on your mobile phone.

This system is notable for the fact that payments are made instantly and without commissions , which allows you to save money.

The key feature of the SMS Banking application is that you can work with it without connecting to the Internet. For each SMS sent, you need to pay the mobile operator.

So, SMS-Banking can:
  1. Make a transfer between accounts.
  2. Top up the balance of your mobile phone.
  3. Pay for internet and tv.
  4. Pay for the services of hosting providers and telephony.
  5. Make transfers to other companies.
  6. Receive information about the account balance and view the list of recent transactions.
