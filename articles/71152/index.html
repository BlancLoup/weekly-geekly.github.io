The DomainTimes web professional forum holds a competition

From October 1 to October 15, the DomainTimes web-professionals forum will hold a creative competition among its users.

The theme of the contest: "Improve our forum together."

Users are required to express their ideas and give practical advice, the evaluation of which will be conducted by the moderators and administrators of the forum.
Anyone who wants to participate in the competition should open a separate topic in this section , in which they should state their ideas.
1. Article should not be large
2. show the practical benefits of your ideas
3. You can draw parallels with other forums and what can be taken from there
4. your advice to moderators
5. you can write which forum you see in the future

The forum administration allocates 5-8 invites to Habrahabr as a prize.

In October each user can make a gift to his friend or friend in October in the form of an invitation to this wonderful site.
