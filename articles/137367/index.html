Law enforcement agencies closed ex.ua


According to unverified information, the largest Ukrainian information storage service EX.UA is closed to the Internet audience. Unknown law enforcement officers seized 200 servers with a total of illegal content of more than 6,000 terabytes. In addition, the service and domain EX.UA. According to users, the file hosting service functioned on Monday evening and on Tuesday morning.

Although yesterday, some Ukrainian users could not get to the site, because there was a restriction of access to IP addresses.

It also became known that the domain name EX.UA was removed from service.

This resource was markedly different from other similar ones in that they could easily be used without registration, the download speed was very high. Also convenient was the ability to download files as when using a torrent client (without taking into account any rating), and download the file directly from the server. Moreover, any file added long ago could also be downloaded at high speed.
