What is the most convenient way to monitor energy consumption in your home? Of course, connect the meter to the Internet and monitor updates remotely. This is now possible with the new Tweet-a-Watt device.

The device is an electricity meter to which a WiFi module is added, which sends reports to a nearby computer. With the help of a computer, you can process data, build electricity use charts and send to friends and colleagues. The cost of the device is $ 90, in addition with the help of such a simple idea, the authors of the invention won the Greener Gadgets Design Competition.
