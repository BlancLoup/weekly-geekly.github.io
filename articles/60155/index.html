What did you like


1. For me, the surprise was to discover the full functionality of a regular smartphone (calendar, alarm clock, timer, notes, calculator, voice recorder, FM receiver and even a book reader)
1. Jokes, jokes, but I liked how they look on the hand. Look quite solid.
2. You can talk without a headset or microphone. The clock has a speaker and microphone.
3. Photo and video camera. Chinese craftsmen shoved there even that. Removes the truth is lousy, but it shoots!
4. It was a great surprise for me to discover the folding stylus, which is crammed into the watch strap.
5. Voice quality is indistinguishable from a regular phone.
6. Without recharging, they lasted for about 2 days.
7. Low price (260 USD in Russia)

What did not like


1. Headphones included - sucks. Fortunately, the connector for them is standard and can be easily replaced with any others.
2. Thick rubber strap. Personally, I was not comfortable all day sitting in them at the computer. I took them a couple of times a day because my hand was sweating and it was not becoming comfortable. The loosening of the strap helped, but I personally don‚Äôt like it when the watch is loose on the arm.
3. Bluetooth Headset. Today discovered an interesting thing. Walking down the street, the headset receiver is attached to the collar of the shirt. If you hold the hand "at the seams" - begin interference. If you bend it (as if you look at the clock), then the noise disappears. The headset receiver was to blame for this, since everything works fine with a different headset.
4. Russian language in read only mode. That is, you can read Russian sms, but there is no possibility to write in Russian.
5. Polyphony mentally returned me to the time of midi calls.
6. Buttons for quick response and track switching are hard to press. Honestly, they called me, I tried to click on the green answer (to the left of the touch screen) and in 1 out of 3 cases I did not succeed.
