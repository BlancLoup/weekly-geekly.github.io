Expanding the base of MegaIndex sites by 47 million pieces

Today we have expanded the site base from 64 to 111 million units.

The expansion of the database was due to the addition of all domains for which there are at least two unique links from already existing sites from our database.

The bulk of the new sites are foreign resources.

We plan to complete the full indexation of new domains by March 10.

Further expansion of the number of sites will be made by means of the indexer automatically.



Until the end of March, we are planning to expand the base up to 300 million sites, which will make it possible to obtain a global index with high completeness.

You can follow the index update in real time through the external links search service.
https://www.megaindex.ru/index.php?tab=linkAnalyze&do=linkIn&site=example.com
