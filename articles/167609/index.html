Connecting sites to online payment in Ukraine



Many have already encountered, and many have yet to face connecting their projects to online payment. In this article I will share my experience in this area.
The post does not contain unique information, everything described here can, if desired, be found in other sources. Nevertheless, reading this article is likely to save you a lot of time in the future, as well as tell you about the many little things and opportunities, the existence of which you may not even suspect.

And so for a start, let's consider what methods of online payment exist in principle:
  • SMS
  • Bank cards
  • Banking Payment Systems (PayPal)
  • Non-bank payment systems (Webmoney, BitCoin)
  • Cash terminals

In Ukraine, they are all represented in one way or another. In particular, non-bank payment systems are the absolute leader Webmoney, although its prevalence is significantly inferior to Visa / Mastercard, from banking attention certainly worth Privat24 ...

And so back directly to receive payments. When you connect the payment, we have 2 options:
  • Connect all possible payment systems through the aggregator
  • Connect each payment system separately

Aggregators

At first glance, the aggregator seems to be an extremely attractive offer: you need to install only 1 script, enter into only 1 contract, or even work without it, do not bother with the choice of payment systems, because the aggregator did everything for you.
In reality, everything is a little different. Looking at these advantages, we forget about the disadvantages.
First, connecting the aggregator, we overpay. At best, you will have to pay 3% of the amount paid.
Secondly, an extra element is created in the payment chain, which, on the one hand, potentially reduces the number of users who complete the buying process, and on the other, creates an additional point of failure.
Thirdly, all aggregators in one form or another have limitations on the amounts and terms of withdrawal of funds.
Also, to connect Webmoney payment, you still have to get a personal passport and tie your wallet to the system.

If all of the above doesn‚Äôt scare you, I can recommend Interkas , as a very good aggregator for Ukraine, with an instant connection and a 3% commission.

SMS

Otherwise, we continue consideration. Let's start with payment via SMS, here are its characteristic features:
  • Suitable only for inexpensive goods / services (<20 UAH)
  • High commission 50-64%
  • Available to almost everyone
  • Few ready modules for CMS


Immediately I anticipate questions like: ‚ÄúOn website X, they write a commission of 55%, and you say that less than 60% does not happen.‚Äù So, 99% of SMS sites aggregators write% of the cost of SMS without VAT. Those. Percentage of the real value is 1.2 times lower.
We now turn to the steps necessary to connect such a payment:
First we need to choose a partner to receive payment. We will not consider the option of opening your short number, it will be a little expensive. Nevertheless, few people know that there are 2 types of SMS aggregators:
The first one takes a higher% of the commission, but provides prefixes on short numbers for free. With such an aggregator you will receive an average of 36 kopecks per SMS with the cost of 1 UAH. Most of these aggregators and you can easily find the right one in the search.
But there is also a second type, in which there is a monthly fee of about 100 UAH / month for 1 prefix on 1 number, but the average% increases from 36 to 40 kopecks from 1 UAH, which can be very profitable on large volumes. Of the second type aggregators, I only know Alpha-SMS , unfortunately they work only under a contract and only with legal entities.

Webmoney

Let us proceed to the consideration of non-bank payment systems. I don‚Äôt see any sense in connecting anything other than Webmoney in Ukraine, since the share of users of other systems is seeking IE6‚Äôs share in the browser market. The characteristic features of Webmoney are as follows:
  • Any amount of payment
  • Minimum commission
  • Low availability (Alas, among users of UAnet, the share of WM owners is small)
  • Availability of modules for most CMS


And so, what do we need to connect the payment?
In case you are an individual, you will need a Personal (25-30WMZ) or Initial (2-5WMZ) certificate in the system.

What about legal entities? For those who work on the general taxation system , you will also need to obtain a Personal Certificate for a responsible employee (certificates for legal entities are not issued), and then enter into an agreement with the Ukrainian Guarantee Agency. As a result, the money will go to your wallet, from where you can at any time withdraw it to your current account minus the system commission - 0.8%.
If you are working on a simplified taxation system , then by law you are not allowed to accept payment in a form other than cash, therefore you will need to conclude an agreement with the same Ukrainian Guarantee Agency on accepting payments without a Webmoney wallet directly to your current account. The commission in this case will be 2%.

Payment terminals

Characteristic features of the terminals are:
  • Any amount of payment
  • High availability
  • Relatively high comm (3-5%)
  • The difficulty of connecting without aggregators
  • Lack of modules under CMS

At first glance, it does not look very rosy. However, not many people know that it is also possible to accept payment through Privatbank terminals, which have a low (from 1%) commission, are very easy to connect for payment, and in terms of prevalence they are not inferior to other terminal networks. Connection of such terminals is carried out through acquiring liqpay for individuals and Internet-acquiring of Privatbank for legal entities. We will talk about this below, as part of the discussion of acquiring payment cards, since both services are closely interrelated.

Payment cards for individuals

And so finally we reached the most common payment method on the Internet - payment cards.
For individuals, the only and non-alternative payment acceptance options is the Privatbank liqpay system.
The system has a number of restrictions, namely, accepting cards only from CIS banks, as well as acceptance limits not exceeding $ 250 (or equivalent in another currency) for a one-time payment from the card and no more than $ 500 / month for 1 card.
The maximum amount available for withdrawal in cash is $ 50,000 / month.
From other things:
- inconvenient verification system with SMS password (not to be confused with 3D-secure / SecureCode)
- if the user has not paid with this card through the system before, the payment can be verified up to a day, but I have not noticed this in practice for a long time.
You can also connect the payment through the terminals of Privatbank. But here I will make a small remark, after the introduction of the new payment interface, the choice of payment through the terminals was made so obscure that it is worth choosing the payment method (card or terminal) at the site interface and not providing the acquiring interface.
After payment, the money in full gets into your virtual account in the liqpay system, from where you can either spend it on replenishing your mobile, send it to another system user, or put it on a card (that's what the commission is at this stage).
The commissions and limits for withdrawing to the Privatbank card are rather gloomy, so consider the option with the Privat card.
Option one, simple:
  • We open the free Universal card
  • We withdraw money to her $ 0.55 + $ 0.5
  • We pay in a retail network or we withdraw at any ATM (commission 1%)

Total we have when withdrawing to cash $ 0.55 + 1,495%

Option two, complex, relevant at speeds from 8400grn / month
  • Open a GOLD debit card (service 80 UAH / month)
  • We withdraw money to her $ 0.55 + $ 0.5
  • We transfer money by payment on account details account or card of another bank (for example, Delta or Platinum) 3 UAH
  • Cash withdrawal at ATM - 0 UAH

Total we have when withdrawing to cash $ 0.55 + 0.5% + 3 UAH + 80 UAH / month

It took about 2 weeks to wait for answers from banks and agree on conditions with Pivdenny.

You can certainly do the same and spend a few weeks on approvals and waivers, but I can not help but notice the unique conditions of Privatbank. 99% of Privat24 users have Privatbank cards, so you can safely leave only the acquiring cards and not connect Privat24.
