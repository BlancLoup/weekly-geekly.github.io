Import photos from Picasa and Flickr into albums VKontakte


Summer is nearing its end, and everyone has accumulated a huge amount of photos that need to be disassembled, edited and, of course, shown to everyone. I think everyone has a favorite service for such an occasion. Previously, on a laptop with Windows, Picasa was such a service for me, and with the transition to a Mac, it became more convenient to use Flickr. Today I want to talk about Picrolla . This application is for importing photos from Picasa and Flickr into albums in VK.





What Picrolla does:
  • Imports selected photos or entire albums: you can select an album to import or create a new one.
  • Keeps the original captions to the photo, which is very convenient.
  • Do not limit you to the maximum number of photos uploaded (You can download only 50 photos in one contact at a time).
  • Displays a slideshow of selected photos.
  • Sends photos to the wall.
