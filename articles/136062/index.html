PlayStation 3D Display - a 3D gamer‚Äôs dream

The new PlayStation 3D display is one of the best products in the 24 '' TV / Monitor class, with all the features you need to become the foundation of an excellent home gaming system with the PS3 console.



The 24-inch ultra-thin LED-screen displays video and games in Full HD 1080p (1920x1080) resolution with a color gamut of 16.7 million, a 10: 1 contrast ratio, a viewing angle of 176 degrees and a refresh rate of 240 Hz, while the built-in speakers and subwoofer complement gameplay high-quality sound. SimulView technology specially developed for playing together for one device provides both players with an individual full-screen HD image (the monitor allows two separate stereoscopic pictures in 1080p resolution to be displayed on one screen), which contributes to an even greater immersion into the game world. The display comes with universal glasses (i.e., compatible with all Sony, Samsung, Panasonic, Mitsubishi, Toshiba and Sharp ACTIVE Shutter 3D displays using infrared emitters).




Currently, PlayStation 3D Display is priced from $ 363 (at amazon.com), up to $ 499 (retail sales in stores in North America, Japan and Europe). In the Russian retail device should appear in the near future.
