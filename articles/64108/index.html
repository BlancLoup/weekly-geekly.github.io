FollowMe - our response to Chamberlain


Do you have a need to remember any link and study
her details in a comfortable setting? Or, for a glass of coffee, fluently
view events happened to your friends? Or maybe you
always dreamed of keeping a diary, but it was too lazy to go to sites to
make out your thoughts? Do you want one to tell one thing and another to others?

All this together now you can do with the Inlusoft Followme service.

Do not want to register? No problem! Read your friends blog
just by clicking on their link.

Do not want to use the browser? Send a message to your blog on ICQ!

July 7th, we begin beta testing our service!

Try it, and maybe you
like : followme.inlusoft.com

Main features of the service:
1. Create an infinite number of user groups that are available
or read your microblog.
2. Assigning each group a unique title that will be visible.
user, if he is in this group.
3. Posting links in the message.
4. Displaying links in the form in which you would like to teach them:
in the form of text, image or media stream.
5. Chat with users who are online.
6. Sending messages to your microblog via ICQ messager.
7. View your list of subscribers and easy contact with them.
8. A simple way to maintain a public blog.
