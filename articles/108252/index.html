Nicaragua and Costa Rica may start a war over Google Maps

Since the beginning of the conflict on 3 November between Nicaragua and Costa Rica, the situation has deteriorated significantly. Although Google corrected the maps and indicated the source of the error, but it was too late. Costa Rica mobilized the police (they have no army), and the Organization of American States unsuccessfully addressed the parties to the conflict.

It all started when the Nicaraguan army entered Costa Rica and camped there, removing the flag of Costa Rica and raising the flag of Nicaragua.

The local general said that this was actually their territory and showed Google Maps as evidence.
