DATA CENTER EXPEDITION 2015: Asia Tour

The third industry away event of the DCExpedition Asia Tour starts on October 24! This year, the DCExpedition team will take a course to the East and visit the data centers of China, as well as manufacturers of equipment for the data center market.

The active participation of leading representatives of the data center industry on DCExpedition tours of past years shows the high interest of professionals in overseas data centers, legislative features of doing business in the IT field, as well as examples of successful relationships with clients both in the domestic and international markets. . The tour organizers thoroughly prepared for the expedition and offer industry professionals to become participants in a unique trip! During the week, the doors of the most technologically advanced data centers in Beijing, Shenzhen and Hong Kong (including the data center with the highest TIER IV infrastructure reliability standard) will be open to the participants of DCExpedition: Asia Tour:

  • 21vianet International Data Center,
  • Huawei Basement,
  • Huawei Data Center TIER IV,
  • HGC Global Watson Data Center,
  • Phoenix TV Omnimedia Cloud Data Center, etc.,


Routes were laid in amazing places in the historical part of China, and also provided the opportunity for unlimited communication between the participants of a business tour. The tour will host a one-day conference in Beijing with representatives of leading Chinese companies offering various solutions in the field of data center engineering infrastructure. During the conference, participants of the tour will be presented products and solutions from the leaders of the Chinese market, as well as the opportunity to discuss all questions of interest within the framework of solutions that are of interest.

Detailed program DCExpedition: Asia Tour is available for review on the site http://dcexpedition.com/ .
