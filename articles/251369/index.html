New mouse gestures


First, let me remind you of the old, suddenly someone forgot. Do not forget that in the version for Mac, they must first be enabled in the settings.

New gestures will work when interacting with links. That is, you click the link with the right button and, without releasing it, make magical passes with the mouse:

- open link in new tab

Pictures in the center


Owners of large monitors most of all asked about this possibility: there is no need to huddle beautiful pictures somewhere in the very corner, it‚Äôs better to show them in the center.
