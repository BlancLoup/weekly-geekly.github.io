Welcome to Android Devs Meetup October 20



Next Thursday, we invite Android developers to the fifth Android Devs Meetup, which will be held in the Moscow office of Mail.Ru Group. Experts and developers of mobile applications will talk about mobile development technologies, discuss the features of using new tools and approaches, share personal experience. At the meeting, you will find three reports, read the details under the cut.

- How to forget about the life cycle and break architecture into layers
Alexander Blinov, RedMadRobot

The report is dedicated to one of the methods for implementing a layered architecture for Android applications and the MPVsV approach.

- How we invent VIPER
Yevgeny Kozochkin, Lazada

The report will discuss modern approaches to building the architecture of Android applications. The speaker will tell how they and the team got the VIPER with the help of MVP and several actions. Share experience in implementing TDD based on this architecture and conclusions.

- Traffic Jam
Kirill Filimonov, Alexander Naumov, Mail.Ru Group

In the course of the report, you will learn how Mail.Ru Mail developers minimize the inconvenience of working with an application when it is poorly connected to the Internet, how they determine the quality of the network, and how the application interacts with the network on different types of networks.

The reports start at 19:00. Participation is free, but you must register through IT.Mail.Ru. For those who can not attend, we will organize an online broadcast .
