Ricoh theta S, work test

In the past couple of months, due to its activities, I received calls several times asking customers to make a 3d video. He began to think - why not agree?

There was a technical issue, as there are a lot of cameras with the ability to shoot video in 3d at the moment, but no one was going to pay the corresponding costs. The issue of mounting 4K video is also quite expensive. Therefore, it was decided to look for an inexpensive solution with FHD resolution.

And here I again received another call, customers were satisfied with the FHD resolution video. Of all the pioneers, I knew the Ricoh theta camera the most - it has YouTube compatibility listed in the description, which means there will be fewer problems. At the same time let's see how it can give the quality of photos compared to my kit

The kit consists of a camera, a charger and a case.

The camera is made of a nice touch plastic, it does not slip out of hand under any circumstances, but easily gets dirty and collects prints. Photo quality



I, of course, not shocked, but pleasantly surprised. Panoramas are quite working, the stitching lines can be seen only with very small space (for example, with a panorama in the refrigerator). Light and shadow are great too! There is no strong glare in the bright sun, the shadows are also extended in some programmatic way. The most convenient thing for which I adapted the camera at work is a quick survey of car interiors - if there is a central armrest, this is a matter of one minute.
