It will be designed for school students, it has a rubberized case to increase durability and wear resistance, a bottom without ventilation slots to minimize moisture ingress inside the case and an optional belt for wearing. Also, the model will have a built-in light indication of network activity for control by the teacher.

Dell Latitude 2100 runs on Intel's 945GSE chipset. Several other important characteristics:
- Intel Atom N270 processor
- Up to 1GB fixed RAM; Additional memory slot to accommodate up to 2GB RAM total
- Display: 1024x576 LED screen, optional touch screen
- Storage options: standard hard drive options up to 250GB; SSD drive up to 16GB
- Battery: 3 and 6-cell battery options
- Wired Connectivity: 10/100 / 1000Mb Gigabit Ethernet
- Wireless Connectivity: 802.11g standard, option 802.11n
- Ports: 3 USB, VGA, headphone / speaker out, mic
- Expansion slot: SD / MMC card reader

Several operating systems will be available for pre-installation: Ubuntu 8.10, Windows XP Home or Vista Home Basic.

The Dell Latitude 2100 will be available in 5 colors: red, gold, blue, green and graphite.
