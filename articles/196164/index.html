Silk Road owner detained, $ 3.6 million seized



Recently, the owner of the Silk Road Internet exchange was detained, she specialized in the sale of substances .
Silk Road is an online store in the anonymous network TOR that allows you to make purchases of any substances, on it any person can act as a seller.

In other words, Silk Road is an exchange for the sale of various substances.

As a result of the operation of special services, the store was closed and its owner, Ross William Ulbricht, was arrested on charges of drug trafficking and the creation of illegal services.

Police confiscated Bitcoins from his computer in the amount of $ 3.6 million .

Detention has become known from Agence France-Presse .
