NVIDIA will support start-ups with finances

Yesterday, March 10, NVIDIA announced the launch of a specialized business program, the GPU Ventures Program, which will include the ‚Äúsearch, support and investment of newly formed companies that use the GPU for visual and other computer applications.‚Äù The company will also open the GPU Venture Zone website, which will be designed to showcase innovative GPU applications developed by partners.

NVIDIA is going to "sponsor" startups, investing from $ 500,000 to $ 5 million. In return, the giant expects to receive a limited share in the funded company. The purpose of this whole action is to strengthen the position of NVIDIA in the market of graphics processors and related technologies.

Vice President of Business Development at NVIDIA Jeff Herbst (Jeff Herbst) said that his company's new program "presents tremendous opportunities for young ambitious companies whose business is based on the GPU." He added that NVIDIA will fully support innovators who will work in areas such as video and image improvement, financial analysis and 3D interfaces.

NVIDIA has already invested startups like Elemental Technologies, Acceleware, Keyhole Corporation (acquired by Google for Google Earth), Mental Images (acquired by NVIDIA), MotionDSP, and Right Hemisphere. Now, it seems, the manufacturer of the GPU wants to put the whole thing "on stream."
