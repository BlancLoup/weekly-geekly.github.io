Rubetek: Creating comfort and safety by integrating devices

The Russians began to show an increased interest in the technology of "smart" home. There are quite a few companies on the market for the Internet of Things (IoT) that offer services in the design of smart homes. The Internet of things market in Russia will grow to $ 9 billion in 2020. In 2016, Russian organizations are investing more than $ 4 billion in the Internet of Things (Internet of Things, IoT), including the cost of equipment, software, services and communications, according to TAdviser. The report Russia Internet of Things Market 2016-2020 Forecast, released in September, analysts predict that during 2016-2020. The IoT market will increase by an average of 21.3% and reach $ 9 billion by the end of the forecast period.


We will tell you more about why, by purchasing Rubetek smart devices , the buyer makes a choice in favor of product quality, safety, comfort and future energy savings.



Rubetek was founded in 2010 by two talented engineers. When installing smart home solutions, the company's employees modified the devices and technologies that were on the market at the time. It is important to note that the company's products are fully Russian-made. The company produces competitive import-substituting products and services.
