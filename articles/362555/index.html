Experience using smart scales Fitbit Aria



It seems to me that not everyone understands the advantages of such gadgets and considers them useless toys, this is not so. I bought scales in early 2014 for about 5 thousand rubles in one of the online stores in Moscow. The need was dictated by the control of the change in the weight of his wife, we were waiting for the completion of the family.

A cursory analysis of the market showed the presence of some nameless models from China, or nondescript models, without frills. This model Aria bribed its advancement in the form of Wi-Fi and design:



Well, to be honest, I have a Fitbit Force fitness bracelet (now out of production), which can be combined into one ecosystem through a mobile application or a branded dashboard on the Internet.

Box:



Nothing special, except that when they were carrying it, people on the subway could not understand that it was painted and there was a reflection on their faces.

Inside instruction, four batteries 1,5V AA. Made very high quality. The indicator display looks very good both in the daytime and in the evening. The application starts, synchronization and scales are ready to use.



Here I will make a small digression.



A big surprise was waiting for me, a sticker was attached to the surface of the scales warning that pregnant women should not be used. But for this purpose the gadget was bought! This, it turns out, was due to the fact that measurements are made in the following way: by stepping with bare legs, a certain electrical impulse is sent, after analyzing which data on the body fat content are displayed. Correspondence with the American support did not give anything, the answer came down to the fact that you go to the doctor and he will say how harmful this impulse is. Arguments about what, if weighed in rubber slippers, did not lead to anything, it was very difficult, apparently, for the respondent. As a result, the wife was weighed in ordinary slippers with a polyurethane sole and this had no effect on the health of the child.

Measurements occur literally within 10 seconds. The weight, the percentage of fat and the first three letters of the weighed are consistently displayed, the scales are understood by up to 8 different people.
