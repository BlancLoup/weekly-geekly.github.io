Who goes to your site?

Meet this - www.netchart.ru www.netchart.ru is a new service that gives you the opportunity to see for free a detailed portrait of the audience of any resource in Runet, simply by entering its URL. Please love and respect.


On Netchar - a lot of statistics. For example, you can rate the audience of any site on the Internet by demographic indicators.

Who needs it and why?
Webmasters do not need to explain why it is important to know the audience of your site. It is difficult to imagine that the webmaster wondered what kind of visitors he has more: girls or boys - in what area they work and what their education is. In addition to all kinds of curiosities, such statistics are an indirect tool for selling advertising on a site, as a webmaster, for example, will be able to hang a report on the advertising possibilities of his site.

In addition, there are specific marketing objectives that our data helps to solve. There is, for example, such a problem: in order to choose a suitable platform for displaying advertising on the Internet, the advertiser and his agency are forced to proceed from the positioning of the site, but it does not always coincide with the real picture. I will give an example:

Cosmopolitan positions itself as a publication for successful women, but in fact it is read by mostly undergraduate students, because the materials start to repeat even before the girl graduates from college. Conclusion: the positioning did not coincide with the actual audience, so today in the Cosmopolitan magazine and on the Cosmo.ru website you can see advertising of an expensive spa-salon less and less. But actively advertised Student-project.ru.

Thus, Netchart can serve as an advertiser or media planner of an advertising agency as an alternative source of information about the audience of each site.

Accuracy
One can argue for a long time about how much the error of this research method is higher or lower than that of competitors. But to judge the accuracy of the information in our case, you can simply: a lot of panelists - a good base, few panelists - a bad base. We have several tens of thousands of panelists and their number is growing very quickly, so Netchart statistics are already representative of the resources visited and it becomes more accurate every day.

Bugs
Whatever statistics you use, do not forget to connect common sense. If you understand the specifics of database compilation, it becomes clear, for example, why Netchart, like Alex, is not representative of sites with low traffic. If some page is visited by 100-200 people per day, it is not surprising that only one of them can be included in the number of our panelists, which means that in Netchart statistics 100% of visitors to this site may turn out to be men of 45-54 years old who are not married.
