EGP-WP98 - the first wrist phone running Windows CE 5.0

Just a few days ago an article was published on the mobile watch. And here is another news from the same category.
The company EPOQ Multimedia has released the world's first watch smart phone running the operating system Windows CE 5.0. EGP-WP98 will be available on April 25th. Currently open to accept pre-orders . The cost of the device is 629 dollars 95 cents.
Inside the smartphone is an AM403 DGT ARM926 processor with a clock speed of 266 megahertz. The diagonal of the EGP-WP98 touch screen, equipped with handwriting recognition, is 1.45 inches.
Also, the device is equipped with a 1.3-megapixel camera, multimedia player and slot for a microSD memory card. From wireless interfaces, the smartphone supports Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. EGP-WP98 works in GSM / GPRS / 850/900/1800/1900 networks.
It is reported that EGP-WP98 can work as a VoIP-phone. Included with the watch-phone comes: Bluetooth-headset, 1GB memory card and two batteries.
