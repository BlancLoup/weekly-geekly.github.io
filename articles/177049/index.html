48% of VKontakte shares sold

The United Capital Partners Foundation, whose President and Managing Partner is Ilya Scherbovich, has agreed to purchase a 48% stake in the Vkontakte social network, Bloomberg reported. We are talking about the shares of the co-founders of the network Vyacheslav Mirilashvili (40%) and Lev Leviev (8%). Sources of the agency, citing the private nature of the information, did not disclose the value of the transaction.

Founder and CEO of the social network Pavel Durov says he has not been notified of the deal
