DevConf 2012 - will be held on June 9 (Saturday) in Moscow - 33 applications for reports have already been submitted

DevConf is a professional conference dedicated to leading programming and web development technologies.
DevConf members are provided with a unique opportunity to access immediately all leading web development technologies by listening to reports from the founders of these technologies who came to Moscow from around the world.

In the framework of DevConf 2012 - June 9, 2012 will be held 5 conferences:

DevConf :: PHP ()
DevConf :: Perl ()
DevConf :: Python ()
DevConf :: Ruby ()
DevConf :: JS ()

June 9, 2012 within DevConf a group of unique master classes will be held.

The conference format DevConf provides the first conference day and the second day of the master classes. The second day is optional and requires separate registration.

The conference is aimed at professional web developers and those who want to become them. It unites ALL the most common languages, with each one having its own stream (hall).

It will be possible to immerse yourself in the technology that is of the greatest interest. At the same time the level of any of the areas is the most professional. In each section, reports will be presented by 1-2 direct authors of the language, as well as recognized experts in the field.
