Virtual Tours with Bing: Flickr and a telescope in the starry sky

At the TED conference, the latest innovations in the Bing Maps service were presented. Among them, the close integration of StreetSide with the Flickr service (in more detail in the blog ), the result of which can be tried now and the integration with the project of the world telescope Worldwide Telescope .

Now, StreetSide offers you not only high-resolution panoramas with a smooth 360-degree mouse rotation, but also integrates them with Twitter, Photosynth services, descriptions of services and attractions, local events. Integration with the world telescope service will allow you to look at the night sky from that StreetSide point where you are right now, and from Flickr to see photos of the current location from service members, including archival ones.
