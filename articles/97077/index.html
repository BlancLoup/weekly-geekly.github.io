WEBO CDN


With the release of version 1.2.0, we officially announce the arrival of a consumer CDN (content delivery network) in Russia. Now, to use it, you do not need any technical knowledge, many hours of implementation and testing. Simply load WEBO Site SpeedUp for your favorite platform, install it, get the SaaS key on the Personal Data page and enable WEBO CDN (on the Setup page in any configuration, CDN tab), remembering to specify which files via WEBO CDN to give out.

Charging using CDN is more than modest. The basic version (up to 10 GB of traffic per month) costs about 105 rubles (35 per day, 1 approximately equal to 0.1 ruble), an advanced version - 450 rubles (up to 50 GB of traffic) and extreme - 810 rubles (up to 100 GB of traffic). If you are interested in professional use of CDN in Russia, then additional options can be found here (besides WEBO CDN, WEBO CDN Mirror and WEBO CDN Geo Mirror are available).

Using CDN allows you to:
  • Increase the regional accessibility of your site, reducing the load time resources for end users.
  • Increase website loading speed by reducing the network overhead for loading static data.
  • Remove the burden of servicing users from your main server by mirroring the resources of your site.
  • Increase the resiliency and security of the site by using server capacities that are available 24 hours a day and which cannot be fully loaded during a DDoS attack.

The WEBO CDN product line basically uses the infrastructure of the largest Russian content delivery network NGENIX CDN ( NGENIX Content Delivery Network ). The NGENIX CDN network consists of 14 multiservice platforms deployed in 7 federal districts of the Russian Federation and in Ukraine. Distributed platforms NGENIX interacts through direct peer-to-peer interfaces with more than 350 telecom operators, which allows delivering content to end users via the shortest network route at high speed and without delays.

The NGENIX CDN network provides daily access to popular news and entertainment resources for the multi-million Russian Internet audience, speeds up the download of music, games, movies and software, ensures comfortable viewing of your favorite TV programs, videos and live broadcasts on the Internet. Today, dozens of Russian and Western companies use the services of NGENIX CDN, including Rutube, SUP, VGTRK and Microsoft.
