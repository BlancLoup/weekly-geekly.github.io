Xiaomi Wyatt Meter - what should we do 50 million clicks?


At this time, Xiaomi did not go on its usual path "in the style of Apple." Yes, the keyboard is white. Yes, it is aluminum. But externally, the keyboard MK01 is not at all like the same Magic Keyboard: the keys are convex and angled, moreover, it has folding legs that allow you to set the angle of inclination about 6 degrees.

The keyboard has a shortened layout with 87 mechanical keys - they are made of polycarbonate, and covered with a pleasant-to-the-touch white ABS plastic. A distinctive feature - the buttons are replaceable, you can replace them or change the location, if you suddenly want the A key to be in the middle of the keyboard.





Inside the keys are located LEDs type 3528, which provide six levels of illumination and can shine in either white or orange. Why white is understandable (this is a classic), and the use of orange is probably a reference to the Xiaomi corporate identity, which has been repeatedly noted in its use. The presence of illumination will be appreciated by those who like to work at night, as well as gamers.



Speaking of gamers. The MK01 uses special switches TTC Red Switches, which, according to the manufacturer, are designed for 50 million clicks. The polling frequency is 1000 Hz, so the response time is approximately 1 ms. Even if the user simultaneously presses 11 keys, the keyboard will be able to correctly process his request. Commands are processed using the STMicroelectronics 32-bit microprocessor with ARM architecture.



Aluminum is used as the body material - in this case, H32 alloy, which is characterized by increased endurance and long service life. Since Xiaomi seriously focused on ergonomics, they abandoned the additional digital block of keys - this made it possible to make the keyboard compact while maintaining functionality.

The dimensions of the device are 358x128x31.6 mm, weight - 940 g. However, the weight here is not so important, it is unlikely that you will often rearrange the keyboard from place to place. Xiaomi separately notes a special coating of keys that prevents the abrasion of characters.
