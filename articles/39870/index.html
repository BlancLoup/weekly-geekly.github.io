Prices for work


Work, as elsewhere, is rated either by an hourly rate or a fixed amount for the project (almost everyone prefers to work with a fixed estimate).

My statistics on hourly payment:

From $ 5 / h (lively yoga, powered by the energy of the sun) to $ 20 / h (just high-level specialists or good companies). And, oddly enough, the rates for different technologies are almost the same. So a very good java and php-coder will cost $ 15-20 / h, despite the fact that in my region Javist gets 1.5 times more and higher. Another thing is that different technologies and specialization provide access to projects of different levels.

The majority of Hindus-private traders and very small desks are probably $ 10-12 / h.
Medium-sized companies - $ 15 / h (up to $ 20 / h).
