Conference Flash GAMM Moscow 2011


According to the established tradition, the Flash GAMM Moscow 2011 conference will take place the day before the GDC , May 12 .
This year, the organizers have chosen a new venue - the Congress Center "WTC" . The conference will be attended by over 500 people , including representatives of domestic and foreign companies, as well as independent developers.

Program


The subject of Flash GAMM is dedicated not only to flash-games, but also to social, as well as mobile games for such popular platforms as iPhone, iPad and Android. This is due to the fact that more and more flash developers are trying their hand at mobile development.


4 sections are planned, which consist of 30 reports, 3 round tables, a section of blitz reports and of course game lynch (for which we are waiting for your applications!)

Attention will be paid to topics such as:
- game development (flash, social, iPhone / iPad, Android, 3D, HTML5, porting games to various platforms, etc.)
- monetization (ways of monetization, advertising in games, payment systems, etc.)
- management (management of teams, projects, etc.)
- publisher (social networks, AppleStore, etc.)
- and much more‚Ä¶
More information on the reports can be found on the conference website .

Competition of games


As always, by tradition, the Flash GAMM games contest will be held! , in which games can take part, both from independent developers, and from studios and large companies. Games can be of any genre and degree of readiness.

The main nominations remained the same:
1. The best game (the winner is determined in 3 categories: independent developers, studios, companies).
2. The most creative game
3. Future hit
4. Female choice
5. Audience Award

Prices for participants from last year have not changed. Standard registration costs only $ 50, and premium $ 75.
