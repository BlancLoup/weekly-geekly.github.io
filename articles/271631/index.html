We invite to Azov Developers Meetup - December 12 in Taganrog



The first (and I hope, annual) Azov Developers Meetup 2015 conference will be held on December 12 in Taganrog. Web and mobile development, testing and project management will be discussed.
Come, it will be interesting!

In the conference program:

Alexander Shevnin, Team Lead, .NET / FrontEnd Developer
ASP.NET 5
The first preview release of ASP.NET 1.0 took place almost 15 years ago. ASP.NET 5 is a new cross-platform open-source framework for building modern web applications using .NET. The concept has been completely rethought and the new version was written from scratch. Perhaps this version brings with it the largest set of changes in the entire history of ASP.NET, and there will be a report about them.


Vasily Khatuntsev, .NET / FrontEnd developer
Meteor under the hood
The speaker will talk about one of the full stack JavaScript platforms of Meteor, whose vocation is to speed up and improve the life of web application developers. Well, at least so say its creators. Let's see how it works inside and try to figure out where this platform can be used.


Tatyana Maximova, testlide
Automated UI testing - how to prepare it correctly
Tatyana will give a brief overview of the organization of automatic testing processes: assessing the need for automation, processing documentation, and selecting tools. Practical recommendations will also be given on the automatic deployment of application components and the reporting of test results.


Pavel Krynetsky, PHP / Java developer
Enterprise PHP Applications
A brief insight into the history of the development of PHP as a programming language, from its inception to the present day, an attempt to trace the expansion of the scope of the language and identify the reasons that contributed to this, an overview of the capabilities of modern PHP as a programming language and as a stack of technologies for creating powerful enterprise-level software solutions .

The report will be of interest to both novice programmers and developers with experience who have not yet mastered PHP.


Olga Kazina, Project Manager
Customer management: non-standard cases of relationships with customers of outsourcing projects
The speaker will tell how to achieve mutual understanding, avoid global conflicts and establish partnerships with different types of customers in managing outsourcing projects: from pilot (1-3 people) to large projects with distributed teams from different countries (20-30 people).

The report will be of interest to all who in the process of work closely communicate with customers.
Examples of emerging problems, ways to solve them and generalize these situations will be presented.


Victor Kotov, iOS developer
Mobile development: Native, Hybrid, Cross-Platform - what to choose?
Currently, there are three approaches to mobile development: Native, Hybrid, Cross-platform.
Each of them has its advantages and disadvantages, so in a situation where there is a choice, we need a balanced approach.

The report will discuss the main solutions currently available on the market (including Xamarin 4 and ReactNative), and also give recommendations on choosing the optimal approach depending on the project.

To participate you must register . Participation in the conference is free, the most active participants will receive various gadgets.

Venue: st. Dzerzhinsky, 161, Congress Hotel "Taganrog".
Collection of participants - at 9:45, the beginning of the reports - at 10:00.

Waiting for you!
