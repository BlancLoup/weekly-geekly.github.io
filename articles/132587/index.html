Why sell on social networks profitable?

Surely you have a personal account in any social network. Through him you communicate with friends, make new acquaintances, learn their news, share photos. Many social networks also allow you to listen to music, watch videos. But did you use social networking as an online store? I propose to talk about the most popular ways of selling goods in social networks and consider their most noticeable advantages.



Of the free, the most popular method of selling goods in social networks is a bulletin board .
Bulletin board - a great way to tell the world that you are selling something. The social network offers a very wide range of customers, both goods and services. Are you looking for a buyer for your old phone? You are welcome in the section "Electronics Goods". Do you want to find a new employee? This is for you in the "Jobs", and Vkontakte generally went farthest and created an excellent resource Vshtate.RU . Want to sell your old car profitably? If you want to offer your services, then contextual advertising is for you! The main purpose of contextual advertising is targeted search for suitable clients by specified key parameters. With the help of it, you will be able to choose for yourself the audience of customers who will receive your ad. In a social network, the search criteria are really extensive - you specify the gender, age, interests of the people you are looking for; pick up the country, the city - even the street!
