Synterra earned the first $ 850 thousand in revenue

Synterra announces the results of the commercial operation of the Synterra WiMAX network in the first six months of operation. During this period, more than 800 subscribers have connected to the network. The company's total revenue received from services based on the Synterra WiMAX network for the first six months of work exceeded $ 850 thousand. At the same time, the average monthly income from a network subscriber (ARPU) was about $ 170.

At the moment, the company's network consists of 40 base stations connected to its own fiber-optic network "Synterra", which has a length of more than 2000 km in Moscow. Today, Synterra WiMAX services are available in 80% of the capital. By the end of 2006, Synterra plans to bring the network coverage of the city to 95%.

The most popular with subscribers of the Synterra WiMax network is broadband Internet access at unlimited corporate tariffs - 85% of customers are connected to this service; then there are telephone services (27% of subscribers) and VPN services (5% of subscribers).
