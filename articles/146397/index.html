Regular shots taken by Nokia PureView

The other day I was allowed to play around with the Nokia 808 PureView.
Yes, yes, the one with 41 megapixel sensor and optics from Carl Zeiss. Surely, many readers have seen a promotional photo . But I'm more interested in the raw photos taken by the average user.

So today I went through the streets of Berlin in search of objects for shooting.
And came across a gay parade. Accidentally came, guys, well, what are you doing! :-)
There I took a lot of photos that I publish without processing.

My subjective opinion


Now I use Nokia Lumia 800.
Mostly I read mail, Facebook-tape, take photos and use navigation.

Photos from PureView shared on Facebook and Vkontakte are noticeably different in quality.
Built-in maps are great: fast and work in offline mode (even car and foot routes).

Therefore, my main requests, despite Symbian, are fully satisfied.
