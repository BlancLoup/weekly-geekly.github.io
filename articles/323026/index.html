MTH: hurry to share our achievements

Greetings, dear readers! Long time ago we did not post information about MyTaskHelper in our company's blog . Since then, a lot of water has flowed under the bridge and finally the time-hands-need have reached the point that we are ready to tell you what our service has become in these few years.


First, it is a full-fledged web form designer . Having no programming skills in general and web programming in particular, a user (even a schoolboy or retired person) who owns a computer and access to the Internet will create, configure and be able to embed, for example, a feedback form, an online survey, a registration form, etc.


To the huge choice of fields of almost all possible types (text line, multiline text, e-mail, file, date, radio button, formula - more than 20 types in total) added the ability to customize the design and functionality of the form (add a logo and captcha, change the color-size of the borders and the web form itself). And those who already have a ready-made CSS file are even simpler: we load it into the system and the web form takes on the necessary appearance.


Let's say a simple feedback form posted on the DiamondVita copywriter website looks like this:



Picture 1

Secondly, it is the designer of relational databases . Yes, all information added to the system by means of constructed web forms or directly is stored in a tabular form. And:



  1. Available functions such as search, filtering, grouping, which greatly facilitates the work with information.
  2. Between two or more tables, you can establish relationships (one to one, one to many, many to one, many to many) by obtaining a relational database;
  3. The owner has the right to provide access to his information to any of the users added to the project team. It is possible to assign roles (reading, reading and writing, editing) for EVERY single field on a web form.
  4. If the user has data in Excel or CSV format, they are easily imported into the system. The same applies to export: all information from any selected table with one click of a button is exported to Excel or PDF and then the user operates it at his own discretion.

Thirdly, it is a convenient widget designer . Using MyTaskHelper is easy to generate:


  1. reports generated by one or another criteria;
  2. graphs and charts;
  3. Google Maps.

Any widgets created in MTH are generated based on the data stored in the database, as well as extremely simple to configure and embed in any web site.


Fourth, the MyTaskHelper service offers users a specific list of features inherent in most modern CRM systems , namely the features:


  1. organize SMS and e-mail notifications (about adding, changing, deleting data from tables), confirmation and distribution;
  2. use conditional formatting and add conditional actions.

As you see, we are growing and developing, and therefore we will be glad to see you as users or critics :)
