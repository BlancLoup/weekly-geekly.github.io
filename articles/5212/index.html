Launched the project "Rating TOP-100 of leading web studios Runet"

On March 14, the official launch of the project for researching the development site market in RuNet was announced, and the rating of the leading developers will be formed. From March 14 to March 21, there will be a stage of preliminary registration of representatives of web-studios to participate in the survey. The official site of the project is http://2007.tagline.ru .

Last year, I conducted a study of the site development market by interviewing representatives of the professional community, which resulted in the formation of the first in Runet rating of the TOP-50 leading web studios by market share. This year I decided to approach the study more seriously, highlighting it in a separate project.

Now the project is the stage of preliminary registration of representatives of web studios.



The project will go in several stages:
  • March 14 - 21. Pre-registration of studio representatives.
    March 22 - 30. A survey of registered studio representatives, independent experts.
    April 3rd. Publication of the report on the current state of the market, presentation of the rating.


    about the project

    The study is based on two basic channels for obtaining information:
    • Data obtained by interviewing professional market participants and external experts.
      The data obtained through the own research of the project organizer - the TEGLINE expert group.


      The result of the project will be an analytical report on the current state of the market, as well as a rating of TOP-100 leading studios of the market. We will try to assess its quantitative indicators (volume, number of players, portrait of an average web studio, price niches, average development time of a ‚Äútypical‚Äù website, market segmentation by regions, etc.), as well as give a qualitative description of the current state of the market, identify the main trends of its development.
