Million monkeys are replaced by one site

Everyone knows that a million monkeys, if they put them behind typewriters, will write any literary work. Just according to probability theory.

It's time to save the poor animals from this fate - everything can already do the computer. The era of monkey humanism has come :)
The site Million Monkeys imitates the actions of the long-tailed macaque when typing text.

Everyone can check how many times they need to refresh the page (and some ten years ago they would have to torture a dozen animals) to find their name in a random sequence of letters. Especially if his name is, for example, Maximilian or Reinhold.

And now imagine how much time it would take to generate at least a poem of the type:

"Dropped the bear on the floor,
Torn bear paw.
I won't leave him anyway
Because he is good "
