Nigma gives books

Intellectual search engine Nigma.ru gives users a book on programming or system administration. You can order a book (one person - one order) on the Nigma.ru website, and receive it at the company's office (Moscow) or by mail. Orders are delivered only within the Russian Federation.

According to Viktor Lavrenko, head of the Nigma.ru development team, about 200 orders have already been issued. Popular is the "Perfect Code" by Steve McConnell or a classic like "Computer Networks" by Andrew Tanenbaum. The action is timed to September 1, how long it will last in time - not yet specified.
