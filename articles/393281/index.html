Polaroid cameras in 2016

The famous company Polaroid has come a long way of development, experiencing a lot of ups and downs. The history of modern photographic technology begins with her instant-print cameras. And even in 2016, Polaroid confidently kept afloat, delighting users with quality products and competing with the advanced giants of digital technology.



A bit of history


Polaroid was established in 1937 by the American scientist and inventor Alvin Herbert Land (Edwin Herbert Land). From childhood he experimented with a kaleidoscope and stereoscope. As a student at Harvard University, Land invented the world's first synthetic polarizing material, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich formed the basis of the company.



Initially, Polaroid was engaged in the manufacture of sunglasses, as well as polarized glasses for various purposes for instruments and military equipment. But since 1948, the era of those same cameras with snapshots began. The incredible popularity of such devices quickly made the company the leader in its field.



In the 70s-80s, the turnover from the sale of photographic equipment and Polaroid components reached $ 3 billion a year.

The most successful Polaroid products were the Swinger cameras (because of its cheapness) and the SX-70 * . This is a complete SLR camera, made of steel and glass, with inserts of genuine leather. In the 70s, this camera was the benchmark of industrial design.

In the 90s, the success of the company began to fade away. Actively developing digital photography, which is gradually replacing film cameras. But Polaroid made a breakthrough in 1999 when it released the I-zone * digital camera. The model sold over 10 million copies. True, after the release of this camera for the company came the difficult times.

From 2001 to 2008, Polaroid declared itself bankrupt three times. But in 2013, the situation changed again for the better when powerful Polad Americans (owned by the Marquette Companies investment fund) became the owners of the company.
