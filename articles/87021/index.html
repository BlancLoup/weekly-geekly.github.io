Low cost writing board with marker (Glass WhiteBoard)


The office was missing the board on which you could write tasks to perform.
After shopping, the expediency of buying WhiteBoard boards did not reach a peak, because sizes and prices did not match our needs.
The solution was found very simple.

As a board used ordinary glass 4mm thick.
The back side was glued with a self-adhesive film - they have a sticky base of white color, which provided a good background, the edges were processed - they were made smooth with fine sandpaper.

There were several mounting options for the product:
1. glue to the wall;
2. on the corners;
3. Drill holes in the glass.

We stopped at the corners. Two corners below, and one above securely fix the glass to the wall.
Still, I recommended using splenitis, or another porous material on which you can put between the wall and glass, so that it is more convenient to write - if the wall is not even.
