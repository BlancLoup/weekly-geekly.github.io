Kickstarter: statistics of successful and failed projects

Did you know that 56% of Kickstarter campaigns fail? Moreover, 90% of failed projects do not collect a third of the requested amount. And among successful projects, half is gaining a little more than desired (up to 110%). And only 5% of projects receive two or more times more than they wanted.

So, the facts:
56% of all Kickstarter projects are failures.
90% of failed projects collected less than 30% of the target amount.
97% of failed projects collected less than 50% of the target amount.
50% of successful projects collected 100-110% of the target amount.
5% of successful projects collected more than 200% of the target amount.
