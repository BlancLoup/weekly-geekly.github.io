New issue of UserAndLINUX magazine with prize draw

After a short break, the April issue of UserAndLinux , which has already become quite popular among Linux users, was released. The theme of the issue is: "The IT world of Linux and its achievements", so the number is filled with news about new technologies, hardware, software, and all this is connected with Linux.
Also in the room there is a small prize draw : flash drives, gamepads, joysticks, webcams and an HD TV are played.

View and download the number can be on the page of the magazine , there you can also read the conditions of the prize draw.

As a result of communication with the management of UALinux , which publish the magazine, I found out that the delay in the release of the magazine is associated with financial difficulties, so the purchase of the magazine will be an invaluable help to the team, as well as contribute to the regular release of the magazine and its more interesting content.
