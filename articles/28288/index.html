iPhone 3G for $ 199?

The most recent, but not the most pleasant information on AT & T prices on iPhone 3G, directly from stock exchange news.

With a 2-year contract for those who bought an iPhone before July 11, who adds a new phone line to their contract and / or has the right to discounts when upgrading:
8gb ... $ 199
16gb ... $ 299

For everyone else, with a 2-year contract:
8gb ... $ 399
16gb ... $ 499

It also assumes the option of buying without signing a contract:
8gb ... $ 599
16gb ... $ 699

Sales will begin July 11 at 8 am.

PS: Recently, the scheme of buying an iPhone 3G was discussed by signing a 2-year contract and abandoning it with the payment of a fine. The standard penalty for AT & T in such cases is $ 200. Whether there will be another, a large amount of the fine for contracts with iPhone 3G is unknown, but the total costs with such a scheme are no less than when buying an iPhone 3G without a contract, therefore there is no point in this scheme.
