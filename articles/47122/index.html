Make money twice

Much of the IT industry is still trying frantically to figure out how to make the first money (hint: hire people). But with those who have already perfected this skill, I want to talk about the next step - how to get rich a little second / third / fourth time.

Making money on unique content is not difficult, unless of course you are not afraid to make money in principle. You can sell your content, offer a subscription to it, you can talk about it, etc. But what is much more interesting, and easy, is to squeeze out a bit of green again from what has already earned its income.

Reforming


Different presentation of the material allows you to earn several times on the same content. This is a great way to raise profits without spending huge amounts on costs.

Money times: we publish a huge amount of our ideas on our blog (comment: Signal vs. Noise ). We publish topics that bring traffic. We make money on traffic, as we place ads in the sidebar and footer. Not very much - a couple of thousand a month.

Money two: we put together the best posts from our blog about our software development philosophy and turned it into a PDF book called "Getting Real". We sold it for $ 19. We earned a couple hundred thousand dollars on this.
