SAP Innovation Forum for development companies in Moscow

Hi, Habrahabr community!

On June 6, we are holding the SAP Innovation Forum for development companies in Moscow and invite you to participate.





Participation in the event will allow you to:

  • learn more about the Center for IoT Solutions that opens at the SAP Joint Innovation Lab
  • discuss opportunities for cooperation in creating custom solutions based on SAP technologies and complementing SAP solutions
  • talk to global SAP executives
  • explore the experience of companies creating their products in partnership with the SAP Joint Innovation Lab
  • Assess opportunities for expanding your business with SAP customers and entering international markets
  • familiarize yourself with the scenarios for using SAP technologies in logistics, customer service, equipment monitoring, agriculture and precision farming
  • keep abreast of global technological and business trends


The event will be of interest to SAP partners and software companies interested in the possibility of complementing SAP solutions, as well as using SAP technologies to build their own solutions.
