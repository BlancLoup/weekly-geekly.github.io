Time management and organizers

Time management is a very difficult task for many modern and active people, but the ability to manage their time, as a rule, is the secret of success. The rapidity of the passage of time and the development of technology make proper time allocation a serious problem. Many different courses and seminars can teach you how to use your time more efficiently, competently draw up a plan of your activities, set goals and achieve your goals within the stipulated time.

It is not necessary to take any courses to learn how to manage your time. We offer a simple and affordable way to help structure your schedule and free up some free time.

Past organizers


Perhaps the simplest and most well-known means of self-organization is the nose, on which everything must be hacked. Of course, we are not talking about the organ of smell, but about a simple device for memorization. The nose was a wooden plank, on which it was necessary to make marks, to remind myself of some important matter. Unfortunately, it is not always possible to remember what kind of business the user left a note for himself.



With the development of personal technology, there were needs that ordinary paper diaries and planners could no longer satisfy. Let's see which gadgets and time planning programs existed, say, twenty years ago.
In the market of electronic devices, quite functional personal organizers appeared, which fit in a hand (although rather large). Monochrome LCD-display, a small keyboard and 512 kilobytes of memory was enough for the basic functions of the digital organizer.


The Japanese company Sharp Corporation was engaged in the release of a series of organizers ¬´Sharp Wizard¬ª. One of the models, the OZ9600, was equipped with a pen for writing or drawing. According to the manufacturer, this allowed to solve the problem of "thumbs", which is difficult to hit on the controls. Device memory could be expanded with external memory cards. To connect two Sharp Wizards, the device has an infrared port, which allows you to transfer contacts, documents and notes to another organizer. In addition, using the infrared port, you could connect to a computer and print the necessary information on the printer. And with the help of a connected modem, this little assistant could turn into a small online terminal, with which it was possible to send an email or fax.


Another notable representative of this category of gadgets is the Casio BOSS (Business Organizer Scheduling System - business scheduling system). In addition to the ordinary calendar, to-do list and telephone directory, the device is distinguished by a built-in spreadsheet editor compatible with the popular tabular processor of that time, Lotus 1-2-3. The organizer is really personal: confidential information can be protected with a password. External plug-in modem allows you to connect to the network, check and send e-mail and fax.


The experimental product was the organizer Seiko UC-2000. These seemingly unremarkable watches were in fact a portable mini-computer equipped with an external dock, which, by the way, worked on three AA batteries. As many as two kilobytes of information were placed in this device, and if necessary, he knew how to show time and remind of upcoming events. Content could be managed without a dock, using standard control buttons. In addition, this unusual device could perform the functions of a simple calculator.


Pay attention to the software. For computers running the Macintosh, In control has become widely known. The program is great for team planning and segregation of duties. The main objects of the program are projects and tasks. Tasks can be grouped and prioritized, as well as delegated to specific people. For personal use, the scheduler is more convenient (To Do list). The program's tools allow you to associate any entry in the organizer with a specific file on the computer disk. A convenient presentation of the calendar in the form of a tape of time very clearly demonstrates all your plans for the near future.


Of the organizers for the PC is to provide a paid Lotus Organizer from Lotus Development Corporation. The developers of this application have focused on the compliance of the program interface with the appearance of an ordinary paper planner. Which, frankly, the usability of the program hurt the order (the fake drawn rings, which supposedly hold virtual pages, occupy almost half of the useful space of the window). The program uses a system of tabs with which you can quickly navigate to the calendar, telephone directory or time line. A handy feature of the program is the ability to create cross-references between entries and contacts on the fly.

And how to use it?


So, all the variety of existing tools or techniques does not save you from having to keep in mind the main plans and objectives. There are various techniques, perhaps you already have your own, which allows you to remain self-organized. If not, we can offer you some recommendations on how to use organizers correctly.

If you are a person far from modern information technologies, you have an excellent memory and you do not have a gadget smarter than you, it is quite possible that you should not think about third-party gadgets. Perhaps pen and paper are your best helpers in organizing yourself. On the other hand, if you are a happy owner of a device that is convenient for accessing the Internet, and you want to squeeze more benefit for yourself (and from yourself), then you are able to use any online service if you are not sufficiently collected.

Set yourself a reminder if your short-term memory periodically fails you. This will help not to be late for an important meeting (or date). The main convenience of such a device is that it is always at hand. But be careful: if you are easily distracted, you can easily be not in your favorite organizer, but on VKontakte somewhere. If your organizer has the function of putting the program widget in a prominent place on the desktop, do it. If this is an online service, then make the page of this service the main one. (If after that another browser becomes your favorite browser - well, you have to make this page the main one there.)

Try to get in the habit of recording your interesting thoughts and plans and review your notes every week. After some of them you can send to the archive or further develop. Free up your memory for more important things. And do not forget to back up!

And yet, all modern services and gadgets will be useful only if you really want to use your time to the maximum and do more. It is unlikely that the to-do list for the week will be useful if it remains dead in kilobytes in the memory of your organizer.

In the event that you still have not found a suitable time management technique, we advise you to pay attention to such time management technologies as MindMap , GTD , Do Less . Experiment and choose what suits you best, and someday you will become the true owner of your time.
