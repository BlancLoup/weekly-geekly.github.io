Want to know about cutting-edge Computer Science? Take part in a series of webinars from Innopolis University!

The first open webinar on Artificial Intelligence will be held on February 11, 2014 at 18:00 Moscow time. He will be held by Associate Professor of Innopolis University Samir Belhauari .

As you probably already know, Innopolis University announced the start of a competition among students of 3-5 courses from leading universities in Russia and abroad in the field of IT. The winners of the competition - a group of 30-40 students will receive a target grant for training under this program.

We received many letters concerning the bachelor‚Äôs program at Innopolis University, and decided to respond to them in an interactive way by organizing a series of open webinars. Our leading teachers will share their experience in Computer Science and Software Engineering and will be happy to answer your questions.

Each webinar will consist of a 20-minute presentation of the course and a question-answer session of 15-20 minutes. A total of 7 such events are planned. A record of each webinar will be available on the Innopolis University page.

S. Belhauari - Associate Professor at Innopolis University, Associate Professor at Mathematics Faculty of Science at Al-Faisal University, Saudi Arabia. He received a PhD in Mathematics (2006) at the Polytechnic University of Lausanne, Master (2000) at the National University of France. Research interests include signal and image processing, pattern recognition and their application in biomedicine, probability theory. He is the author of more than 100 publications.
