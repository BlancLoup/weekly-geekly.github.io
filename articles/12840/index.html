DOMAIN IS LAUNCHED

The new, long-awaited .mobi domain is finally available online: Indesit Company has launched WWW.INDESITCOMPANY.MOBI, which is a version of the corporate page for mobile communicators - smartphones, PDAs and BlackBerry.

The WWW.INDESITCOMPANY.MOBI address is available as an optimized mobile version of the company's corporate website, created to inform users about the latest news from the Indesit Company, wherever they are. First of all, the site is focused on the business and media community. The page is available in English and Italian, and is divided into four sections: general information about the company (history, brands, products, financial information), press releases, information about the Indesit Company in the stock market (data on changes in the value of shares in real time ) and corporate events (information on meetings of the board of directors and shareholders).

Sites of the domain .mobi are specially created for access from smartphones and PDAs. The content on these pages is specially adapted for quick and convenient access to the information necessary for daily work. WWW.INDESITCOMPANY.MOBI is the answer to the growing needs of users to access website content from mobile devices. The site interface is designed to work most effectively on a small screen.

The .mobi version is a significant step forward in communication and the development of interactivity. A large set of tools and functions are available on the site, for example, an email alert service and a company's stock price chart in real time.

The new site Indesit Company, which meets the latest requirements for news and information resources, is another confirmation of the company's leadership in creating innovative solutions. WWW.INDESITCOMPANY.MOBI is an Indesit Company communication style, that is, speed, clarity and transparency of information. The site was created for dynamic people who are constantly on the move and need access to information in real time, wherever they are.

The site is available at the following addresses:

www.indesitcompany.mobi - full address

www.indesitco.mobi - short address to facilitate dialing from a mobile phone
