iLab announces autumn call for ideas

The Expert Council summed up the preliminary results of the competition of ideas initiated by the Innovation Laboratory in the midst of summer . Preliminary results can not be called stunning.

In total, 18 people took part in the competition, presenting a little more than two dozen ideas to the experts for further development for BlackBerry‚Äôs PlayBook tablets.

For the whole of Russia, there were only two dozens not only brave, but also able to offer something new and unseen? Ready to not just offer an idea, but to compete for a significant prize and help in its implementation and promotion.

After a long and painful debate, a group of candidates was selected whose ideas were deemed worthy of inclusion on the shortlist: Yevgeny Sinitsyn, Maxim Kulintsov, Jane Dukhopelnikova (it looks like the only girl), Artem Vasilenko, Tefery and Pavel P. Bezrukikh, who provided a number ideas.

If for some reason you did not find yourself on this list, but sent your idea to the competition, then contact us by email konkurs@innovationlab.ru

The listed participants also have time for a deeper study of the proposals for development already submitted by them and accepted by us.

It was decided to fill the remaining four places in the top ten by announcing an additional set, a kind of autumn appeal, which will last only a month, until September 25, 2011.

We remind you that the competition of ideas concerns applications for BlackBerry Playbook tablets, not smartphones . It should also be remembered that the degree and depth of their study at this stage are not as important as their innovativeness and commercial attractiveness, but it is desirable to nevertheless present them in a complete form.

All applications are also accepted at: konkurs@innovationlab.ru

The Expert Council of the Innovative Laboratory will identify the contestants and put them to a vote, which will be held on our Facebook page .

Winning a vote does not guarantee winning a contest, but seriously increases the chances.
