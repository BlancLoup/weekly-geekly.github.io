SocialWatch.ru - 7 week results

Hello! It's been 7 weeks since the first presentation of our project. We tell, than he became better.



SocialWatch.ru is a tool for tracking the activity of users of social networks.

The main task of the project is to show to interested persons at what time users appeared on the sites. At the moment, you can follow the users: Odnoklassniki, VKontakte, Dating Mamba, Loveplanet, Dating@mail.ru, Rambler-dating and Dating 24open - and now moskva.ru.
