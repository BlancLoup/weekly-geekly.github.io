Inbrics M1 - the thinnest Android-smartphone with a QWERTY-keyboard. Do you still want Milestone?

The little-known today Inbrics company presented an interesting novelty - the Inbrics M1 smartphone on the Android 1.5 platform with a full-size QWERTY-keyboard.

The main feature of the device is its thickness, against which the Nokia N900, Moto Milestone look like monsters.
