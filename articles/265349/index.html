Overview of payment aggregators for receiving payments

If you are an online store owner and you want the buyer to pay for goods in a convenient way, you have two ways: connect and set up payment for each payment system separately - suffer with integration, signing contracts ... or do everything quickly and without unnecessary red tape. use the services of a company that is a payment aggregator. When choosing the latter option, you will need to sign only one contract, and your buyer will be able to choose a convenient payment method from the dozens available. This and offline ways, and e-wallets and, of course, payment by credit card. Payment aggregators set.

This study, conducted over several months, covers companies that provide services for accepting payments not only by bank cards, but also through e-wallets, as well as other payment methods.

What is the difference between payment aggregators and what should I pay attention to?


Each company was decided to consider the ten parameters. Below is a detailed description of the parameter and why it is worth paying attention to.

1. From what year does the company work in the market. The pioneers of the market could earn a reputation and can boast of stability. This does not mean that young companies are unreliable. But on the date of entry into the market, the company should pay attention.

2. Who can connect. Legal entities, individual entrepreneurs, individuals - everyone can use the services of payment aggregators. But not all companies cooperate with individuals or non-residents. For individuals, other conditions are usually offered. Differences may be in fees or in the set of documents provided.

3. Fees . They are all different, depend on the monthly turnover, the type of activity of your company and, possibly, will be made individually for you. Commission may be charged both from the buyer when making the payment, and from you, when withdrawing funds. Do not chase the lowest rates. Perhaps such a company has some significant drawbacks. Some companies do not post pricing information on their website. I note that there are very few sites that contain all the necessary information. At the time of writing this review, I often had to call and communicate with managers to clarify details.

4. Chips . The most interesting section. This is what the payment aggregator can stand out, except for tariffs. These are functions that competitors do not have, or few of them have.

Consider some of them:
4.1 Manual Billing. This function allows you to send the buyer a link to email / sms / icq, clicking on which he will only have to choose a payment method and pay the bill. The amount and description of the goods will already be indicated. This service will be useful to those people who do not have their own site, or they have a group in the social network, and they want to accept payment.

4.2 SMS informing - notification of the buyer and the owner of the online store about successful payment. Cost of this service may not be cheap. If you are not satisfied with the cost of the service, then it may be worth considering to use the services of third-party companies whose price for sms will be lower.

4.3 Logistics is a service for delivering your goods to customers. Payment aggregators who cooperate with companies engaged in the delivery of goods may offer you special conditions for the delivery of your goods.

4.4 Provision of CMS. You can use the engine for the online store, which will provide you with a payment aggregator. You just have to fill in information about the goods (photos, description, prices). The disadvantage of such a proposal is that the possibilities of the CMS offered by the payment aggregator are not unlimited and may not correspond to your requests.

4.5 Acceptance of Bitcoin and other exotic currencies. Only a few companies can boast of this, but if their conditions do not suit you, you can always contact companies that only receive such currency, and to connect other payment methods, contact another company.

5. Cons . They have all. Big and small. So we look, whether it is a minus for you.

6. Support service . 24-hour support service is mainly only for payers, although there are exceptions. For those aggregators on whose site an online chat is set, the waiting time for the connection with the expert did not exceed 5 seconds. Contact customer support will have to contact at the stage of connection and in case of emergency situations. Therefore, the time to respond to customer requests is very important. If you are a large VIP client, you can contact your personal manager, and if not? I did some research. I sent a message with a simple question and waited for an answer.

Request response time:

* You can call support and try to persuade employees to consider the application out of turn.

7. Services . At this point, I noted only the presence of three services: recurring payments, mass payments and WS-integration:
7.1 Recurrent payments - periodic payments made in automatic mode, provided that the first payment was successful. Confirmation of the cardholder is only necessary when making the first payment. Map data is not stored on the side of the online store. For recurring payment, the script of the online store transmits the id of the parent (first) payment using the API. This service will be useful for Internet providers to automatically replenish the accounts of their customers. Some companies connect this possibility from the very beginning, some require a large turnover and work for a certain period without complaints from buyers.

7.2 Mass payments is a service that allows you to make payments to your customers. You replenish the balance, and payments are made from it according to specified details. Not supported by all companies and on special conditions.

7.3 WS-integration - transparent integration of payment scripts into an online store website. The buyer at the time of payment is not redirected to the page of the payment aggregator. The advantages of this solution include the fact that the buyer remains on his usual online store website. This increases conversion because the buyer is not afraid of the ‚Äúnew‚Äù and ‚Äúunfamiliar‚Äù interfaces of payment aggregators. Among the disadvantages: the owner of an online store needs to get a PCI DSS certificate, since the buyer will enter his card details not on the site of the payment aggregator. Not all companies and not all clients can offer such a technological solution.

8. Customization of the payment page . After clicking the "Pay" button on your website, the buyer is redirected to the payment page of the payment aggregator. Customization allows you to change the design of this page under the design of your site: place the logo, change the background and much more. Each aggregator offers different possibilities for changing the design, and some do not allow doing this at all.

9. VIP customers . Which of the large companies cooperates with this payment aggregator? This can be found in this section. Some large companies cooperate with several payment aggregators at once.

10. Comment - important information about the company, which does not fit into any section above. A small useful note.

FAQ
Is this a customized review?
No, objectivity is paramount. Each company has pros and cons. And this is not a aggregator rating.

Why is this company written more than that?
Perhaps the first company can boast a large set of chips and interesting features, and the second does not stand out among competitors.

I know the% Name%% aggregator, but it is not in the review, why?
These are 2-3 companies. Some companies expressed their disagreement to participate in the review, with others it was not possible to contact and talk.

Has the review text been agreed with the PR managers of the companies?
Some PR-managers have made small additions to the description of their company. As it turned out, in most cases, call-centers provided incorrect information. Most often, it was reported that there is no WS-integration or recurring payments. But nobody removed the "Cons" section.

Why there is little information about tariffs, and more information about technical capabilities?
This review shows the presence or absence of technical capabilities in one or another aggregator.

On what basis are payment aggregators located in a review?
Random order. Rather, not entirely random, but does not affect the perception of information.

How did the information gather?
Information gathering began with a visit to the site, then a call to the support service. The last step is to send the finished text to PR managers.
