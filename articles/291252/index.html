Seller without experience: to take or not to take?

Sellers or, as it is now fashionable to say, sales managers, are needed by every business: from a stall selling vegetables and seeds to a large IT company that turns zeros and ones into someone's income. And any competent leader understands that it is not enough just to do SEO-optimization of the site, pay for the context, buy links and wait for customers to hurry to bring the company their money. Accordingly, a volitional decision is made at one or another stage of the company's expansion: to organize a sales department.

It would seem that half the work is done, it remains only to hire competent specialists who know exactly how to offer / explain / persuade.

This article is about whether to hire experienced sellers and how to organize the correct work in the sales department.
