FTS acquired the right to block anonymizers and VPN without trial

The bill that prohibits work in Russia with anonymizers, VPN, Tor and other "technologies that allow access to blocked information resources" has been adopted by the State Duma only in the first reading , but this did not prevent Roskomnadzor, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Federal Tax Service and Rospotrebnadzor to issue an order ( pdf ), which allows blocking anonymizers out of court. This right has been obtained by the Federal Tax Service (FTS) with the help of Roskomnadzor, Izvestia writes .

The order clearly defines the purpose for which anonymizers are blocked - to deal with means of bypassing locks.

The wording of the order on the criteria for blocking anonymizers (paragraph 4.1.7) almost literally repeats the wording in the law, which is currently being reviewed in the State Duma: ‚ÄúThe availability of information and / or programs for electronic computers on a website page that allow access to the website on the Internet, pages on the Internet that host prohibited information that meets the criteria 4.1.1‚Äì4.1.6 of these Criteria. ‚Äù

According to the legislation, Roskomnadzor finds and blocks content with child pornography and suicide propaganda, the Ministry of Internal Affairs prepares for blocking addresses of sites with materials about drugs and trafficking in them, and the FTS is engaged in blocking an online casino. Now the FTS acquires the right to block anonymizers, VPNs and other "programs for electronic computers that allow access to a [prohibited] website on the Internet."

According to the authority, the Federal Tax Service will block only anonymizers and VPNs through which you can get access to illegal online casinos. But today there are no anonymizers on the Internet that are specifically designed to access only blocked online casinos. Anonymizers and VPNs usually allow access not only to online casinos, but also to any other sites. That is, the Federal Tax Service with Roskomnadzor, in fact, have the right to block any anonymizer, VPN and Tor gates.
