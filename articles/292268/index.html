New MongoDB CFO

Today it became known the name of the new CFO MongoDB , it was Michael Gordon (Michael Gordon), who previously worked at Procter & Gamble, Zscaler, Yodle and Merrill Lynch. In the investment bank, Gordon has been closely involved in mergers and acquisitions for more than a decade, and in Yodle, he has been preparing for an IPO. In 2013, MongoDB reached a total capitalization of over $ 1 billion. Appointment of a new CFO continues to form a management team, including the last appointments of Carlos Delatorre as CRO and Megan Eisenberg as CMO. As the company's CEO noted, appointing Gordon should lead to more aggressive scaling of the MongoDB business.
MongoDB is a document-oriented open source DBMS that does not require a description of the table schema.

According to Crunchbase, the company raised $ 311.1 million in 8 rounds from 13 investors, with the last $ 80 million round of the G series in January of this year.

Employees of the company declined to comment in detail and today no one knows when MongoDB will be ready to take the next step, but the reception of a specialist with such experience shows that in the near future the company may announce preparations for an IPO.
