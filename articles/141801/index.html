Technical customer support via the Internet

Good afternoon, Habrayuzer! You know that good automation systems are needed to simplify the lives of users? For example, to speed up the process or improve the interaction between participants in a process.

Our SmartNut (SaaS Service Desk system for supporting customers of IT companies) is a good automation system that simplifies life. At least, we are doing everything to ensure that it is. We already wrote to Habr that we taught SmartNut to automatically disassemble email from the technical support box and register tickets based on this. And the other day in SmartNut, a full-fledged personal client account, accessible via the web, finally appeared.

In short: now you can give contact persons of your clients access to the web-office, where they will be able to submit new applications, look old and correspond in the comments with the engineers and employees of your company.
