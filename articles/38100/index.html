Epoq EGP-PP01. Commercial telephone and projector.

With just not built into mobile phones ... But still there are those who lack something. Someone would like to see an opener for beer in his mobile phone, someone would like his phone to have a penknife in the case. Someone lacked a projector :)
That's just for the last Chinese released device Epoq EGP-PP01 .

In short, the Chinese released the first commercially available GSM phone with a built-in projector. The Epoq EGP-PP01 model costs $ 550 , but for this money the buyer gets a phone with a touchscreen display, a camera, 0.5-watt speakers, and most importantly a projector that creates a 30-inch VGA (640 x 480) image . The gadget can show photos and videos, and using the USB port, you can connect it to a DVD player, desktop computer or laptop. The battery capacity is enough for 2-3 hours of active work, which is enough for a presentation or film.

The Epoq EGP-PP01 interface is a copy of the iPhone UI : even when taking photos with a 1.3 megapixel camera, the same animation is used as in the iPhone.

It is known that Epoq EGP-PP01 works in GSM networks (900/1800/1900 MHz) , 2.4-inch TFT LCD display has a resolution of 240x320 pixels, the battery with a capacity of 1800 mAh provides 2-3 hours in talk mode and 200-250 hours standby time The device supports stereo Bluetooth, there is an audio player. The case dimensions are 115.6x48.6x23.3 mm .
