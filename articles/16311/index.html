The creators of "Balachki" launched an image site and Internet quest

On Friday at 14:00, the creators of the first Ukrainian Internet-messenger "Balachka" launched an image site in support of the product. The site is designed as an entertainment mini-portal, and was created primarily for 10 thousand existing users of the messenger and new users.

The site is located at balachky.com , and contains a number of entertainment services, including a funny video, photos and an interactive flash-game.



The site also announced an online quest from the popular Ukrainian youth group TNMK.

Internet quest started on the same day at 16:00. The game consists of several stages. Constant leaders of the popular group of Ukraine "Tanok on Independence of the Congo" Fagot and Fozzy will lead the participants through a maze of questions and tasks that will be asked in the process of virtual communication.

The creators of the Internet messenger promise the winners of the contest valuable prizes, which they will receive directly from the hands of the musicians of TNMK in the real world :)
