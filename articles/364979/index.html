Initiation of the battle: you need to shoot at each other once

Rules of victory and defeat


You lose if:
a. Drone Fighter will catch 6 missiles he will land automatically.
b. Drone Fighter will make an emergency landing.
c. Drone Fighter can not fly.

You won if:
Drone Fighter landed safely and stopped the motors.

Control head shot
If you lose, you should restart the remote and launch the missile at the winner. (The winner is still on.) This will confirm the victory. From that moment, victory and defeat is awarded and the battle diary is updated.
a. Drone Fighter, who lost, gets +1 loss.
b. Drone Fighter, which won, gets +1 victory.

You can see how many times you won / lost in the battle diary.

Superudary


Using a combination of movements of joysticks on the remote, you can get additional upgrades to the base rocket (machine gun). (Superudars will be added in place with firmware updates.)



SHIELD
1. Turn the left joystick 2 times.
2. Drone Fighter gets protection from enemy attacks for 10 seconds.



DEMON
1. Turn both joysticks 2 times.
2. If you hit a rocket at the enemy within 10 seconds, its control is inverted



WATER BOMB
1. Rotate the left joystick 2 times.
2. If you hit the enemy for 10 seconds, then you will neutralize the enemy attack.



Booster
1. Push and pull both joysticks.
2. If you hit the enemy within 10 seconds, the sensitivity of his console will greatly increase, which will lead to inconvenience of control



HEADING LOCK
1. Double-click both joysticks left and right.
2. If you hit the enemy within 10 seconds, you will block the enemy from yawing for 10 seconds.
