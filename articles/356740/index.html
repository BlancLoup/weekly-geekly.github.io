The program will protect your computer from physical abuse.



The user hephaest0s posted on GitHub the sources of the usbkill project written in Python. The program resides in memory, checking USB devices connected to the computer. As soon as she detects changes in the connected devices (the flash drive is removed), she immediately turns off the computer. Why might such an unusual behavior be required?

Suppose you are looking for the FBI, the NSA, the CIA, or any other three-letter agency, and you fear that government agents will grab your working laptop right out of your hands. Then, if it remains in an unblocked state, no data encryption program will simply help. This is exactly how Ross Ulbricht punctured - FBI agents unexpectedly snatched a laptop from him in the library while he entered the Silk Road administrator's account .
