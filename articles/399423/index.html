Roskomnadzor began negotiations with Opera about filtering prohibited sites



The state regulator in the face of Roskomnadzor began negotiations with the company Opera Software, the manufacturer of the popular Opera browser, about filtering (blocking) websites prohibited on the territory of the Russian Federation at the software level, Kommersant reports .

The product Opera Software is known for having built-in VPN and Turbo mode, which make it easy for the general consumer to bypass the site lockout from the territory of the Russian Federation.

So far, no results have been achieved.
