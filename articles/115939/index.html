Google announced Nexus S 4G and Google Voice integration in Sprint

Google announced a 4G version of its Nexus S smartphone for the Sprint network.

The Nexus S has a 4-inch touchscreen display, a 1 GHz Hummingbird processor, 16 GB of memory, dual cameras and NFC support. Of course, its most interesting feature is the WiMAX chip, which provides fast data transfer.

The device will be available this spring online and in Sprint retail stores, as well as in the stores Best Buy and Best Buy Mobile in the United States at a price of $ 199.99.
