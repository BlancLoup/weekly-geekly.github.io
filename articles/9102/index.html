Alarmed 2 points.

1) "The company management spoke about the transition of the system to a single accounting unit equivalent to the Russian ruble." Does this mean that the amount in my account will be transferred from the dollar equivalent to the ruble one? If so, at what rate? Given my extensive experience with this system, I can assume that the rupees will not have the opportunity to earn money on this exchange transaction, that is, they will receive their 3-5% commission "just like that."
