Smart tape VK - the killer of your conversions, but telegrams are not the way out

Many now began to create channels for themselves in the telegraph in order to convey information to users with greater efficiency. Many people understand that VKontakte groups have a half-dead audience. Even if you have 100 thousand subscribers, this does not mean that you will receive several thousand clicks on the links in your post, even in the next hour.



This arises from the fact that one person has several hundred subscriptions at once, and in order for him to see your post, it must happen:
  • By happy coincidence, go to VKontakte at the moment when you made the post, or scroll to your post - completely cut off 70-90% of your audience. If your post goes into working hours, and your audience is very busy people, then only a few will see your post.
  • Your post should be interesting and go through the algorithm of smart tape - even if a person enters the tape at the right moment, he will not see your post with some probability.

Thus, your 100 thousand subscribers easily turn into 100-200 live, which often make repost, write comments and so on.
