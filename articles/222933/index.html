Poll - Open data of the Ministry of Finance

In addition to the competitions we hold on the basis of information on contracts, we (the Information Culture) and I personally (Ivan Begtin) continue to convince the Russian ministries to discover new data useful for developers.

In particular, I know that the Ministry of Finance of Russia plans to take further steps in ensuring its own openness and we want to convince colleagues in the Ministry to open the most interesting data for developers.

I know because I am on the open data council and the public council at the Treasury

Therefore, on our own initiative, we are conducting this survey in order to understand what the developers want from the Ministry and from the Federal Treasury.

We ask you to answer the questions in the short survey in this post on HubrHabr and in the full survey by the link - https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/RF78FF3

All answers will be used by us in further work and I will try to bring them to those who are responsible for openness in the Treasury and in the Ministry of Finance.


PS May 20, I will be at two meetings of the Councils of the Rosstat and the Ministry of Economic Development. Topics will be, among other things, open data, and according to the results I will write what was there.

If there are questions that I could ask the staff of these departments, then write them in comments and I will definitely ask them.
