Startup Battle is waiting for its members!

On November 29, 2011, iPRORIV 2.0 will open in the Digital October center - a multiplatform where startups can meet with leading businessmen, founders of venture funds and private investors. A real opportunity to draw their attention to their project is to adequately address the start-up battle, which will be evaluated by well-known businessmen, government officials and Internet users via the hashtag on Twitter #iLiftTV and #iPRORIV.



The judges include 30 Silicon Valley businessmen, including Steve Wozniak from Apple, Kevin Colleran from Facebook, Mike Brown from AOL Capital and Paul Byuheyt Y-Combinator, creator of Gmail and Google Adwords, Alex Debelov from Virool, Bill Tai by number of IPOs and M & A, as well as well-known Russian entrepreneurs - Google Russia CEO Vladimir Dolgov, ABBYY founder David Yan, Rosnano CEO Anatoly Chubais, Skolkovo President Viktor Vekselberg and founder of Runa Capital Sergei Belousov. Such a number of brilliant people rarely get together.

November 23 ends registration of start-up participants in the iPRORIV 2.0, an international multiplatform for innovators. On the website www.i-Lift.TV you can submit an application for participation and use your chance to attract the attention of investors in the Innovative Lift in 2 minutes. Evaluate startups will be business experts from Russia and investors in Silicon Valley.

The winners of the start-up battle will have the opportunity not only to establish business contacts with investors, but also to participate in the Lift project, a reality show about innovations and start-ups. It will be filmed by Japanese director Liu Ryuichi Nemoto, among whose works Saturday Night Live is one of the most popular programs in US television history.

Young companies working in the following areas are invited to participate in the battle of startups: web 2.0, e-commerce, IT, bio-tech, hi-tech, nano-tech, space technologies, energy. More information can be found with the programs on the site http://www.iconvention.ru/program/iproriv/

What is iPRORIV 2.0?
The international multiplatform iPRORIV 2.0 is one of the main elements of the Innovation Convention. 100 investors and 50 startups from Russia and Silicon Valley will share their secrets in the format of sections, panels, sets, workshops, presentations. iPRORIV 2.0 will be held November 29, 2011. in the center of Digital October (Moscow).

What is the project "Elevator"?
i-Lift is a multiplatform (TV + Event + MOB + WEB), registered by FRAPA for America and Europe.
Objective: to show the startup ecosystem in real time. 20 venture capitalists will invest in 150 startups.
