Rostrud launched "Russian LinkedIn"

Today, the Federal Service for Labor and Employment of Russia (Rostrud) made a presentation of the project Skillsnet.ru - ‚Äúthe first all-Russian social network of business contacts‚Äù.

On the site you can already register and fill out your profile. In the "Russian LinkedIn" at the moment 1886 users are registered.

The head of the department Vsevolod Vukolov (pictured) recalled that LinkedIn social network was blocked by Roskomnadzor on the territory of the Russian Federation for non-compliance with the requirements for the storage of personal data of Russians. But the need for such a social network exists. And so the agency launched a social network Skillsnet.ru.

"This is the best social network for professional contacts, and, in our opinion, it will be actively demanded by young people," the official said. Later, a mobile application and instant messenger will be added to the portal. All work on these large projects are carried out at the expense of the federal budget .

The site allows you to find and track job vacancies with employers. At the moment, the database contains 24,795 proposals from 510,199 employers (the main part of employers are only registered, but have not published vacancies), as well as two educational institutions.



According to the official, the resource will be in demand both by young people and by older people. Young people will open their profile here, and large and medium-sized enterprises will post job offers and internships. Also, participants of the social network will have the opportunity to exchange information about where it is better to work, study and train.

Judging by the open statistics , the server on Nginx copes with handling up to 90 requests per second, uptime for several hours.



Vsevolod Vukolov stressed that Rostrud is actively introducing electronic technologies in the field of employment. Unemployed Russians are now much easier and faster to find a job. In particular, now workers and employers are actively using the online service Onlayninspektsiya.rf - by this time more than 200 thousand requests have been reviewed and more than 70 thousand online consultations have been provided. This resource allows you to contact the labor inspectorate, get a free consultation on labor relations issues or conduct a self-check of your organization.
