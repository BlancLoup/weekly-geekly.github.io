SamMobile presented Android 4.3 for the smartphone Galaxy S4 Google Play edition

Most recently, the Samsung Galaxy S4 version was released with Android without changes.



And now an update to version 4.3 is available for the GT-I9505G .
The firmware is without any changes from Samsung. I want to remind that Android 4.3 is a minor update and does not contain any strong differences compared to Android 4.2.2.



Also worth noting that the firmware can not be put on a regular Galaxy S4 ( GT-I9505 / GT-I9500 ). Otherwise, you will get a "brick". However, you can put a ported version for the GT-I9505 . You can flash the device through the Odin3 flasher.

All necessary files for the GT-I9505G are in the archive .
