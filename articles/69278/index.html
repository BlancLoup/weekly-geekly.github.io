Oracle makes promises to Sun customers

Oracle has distributed an ad unit in the European edition of the Wall Street Journal. Leaflet reads:

3. Hold twice as many hardware specialists for sales and service of SPARC / Solaris as Sun today contains;
4. Significantly improve Sun hardware performance by integrating with Oracle software.

We took it to win. IBM, we plan to compete with you in the hardware business. - Larry Ellison "
