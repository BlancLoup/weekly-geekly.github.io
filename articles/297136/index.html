The combination of work and freelancing

I started my career as a programmer a few years ago, trying my skills as a freelancer, while combining this business with my studies. Now I remember those sleepless nights with a hint of nostalgia and a quiver over the skin. In stable work, of course, there are many advantages, among which is the measured pace of life. But one day all this idyll was destroyed by the message of the former colleague who offered to help him with the project for a certain fee. All anything, but his company had to do exactly the same as the company for which I now work. Before me was the question of how to get out of this situation, without questioning your good name, and at the same time gain useful experience.

At first, I was skeptical about this event, because I had long forgotten what remote work is. It was also not clear how my employer would react to this, despite the fact that I have been on good terms with him for more than a year. But when I looked at it from the other side, I realized that a business can become very profitable for all parties - I will get a new (and not least important, well-paid) experience, my employer is an employee‚Äôs development (which just will need to pay), and a colleague who decided to start his own business - a programmer who has already solved his problems of interest.

At first I decided that I had to inform the employer that I would work on the project with a similar focus . Make it clear your loyalty to your current job and explain that this in no way threatens the company's confidential data. Next, perhaps the most important point - understand where the line is located, beyond which are the intellectual property of the campaign . This applies to both the employer and the third-party source of income. In no case do not disclose inside information and details to a third party. Even in an informal conversation. Try not to discuss this type of activity with a third party at all.
Remember, the implementation of the solution of the problem is the property of the company for which it was created. But no one can forbid you to use the same methods and principles to solve a similar problem.

Keep a balance between the already studied and the new. As for me, this is the most pleasant part.
