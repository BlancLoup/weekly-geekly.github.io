We announce the updated application

Despite the March jumps of the Bitcoin rate ( according to Coinmarketcap, the lowest cost of Bitcoin in the past month was $ 908, the highest - $ 1279), against the background of public discussions of its hard forks, the cryptocurrency still retains its relevance throughout the world.

On April 1, a bill came into force in Japan, according to which Bitcoin and all altcoins were recognized as the official means of payment at the state level.

At the end of March, the Swedish investment fund Brave New World Investments AB began to use Bitcoin for investment purposes, namely, to purchase shares of Iranian enterprises. At the moment, the fund has already opened accepting applications from investors.

Positive investors' forecasts regarding the growth of the value of Bitcoin are also due to the tightening of the state policy regarding the movement of capital in China. At the same time, according to JulDia Li, analyst at brokerage company BellDirect, the desire of the Chinese authorities to limit capital outflow from the country only provokes demand for Bitcoin.

Therefore, we, in the Wirex payment blockchain service team , are trying to make the process of using Bitcoin for customers from around the world as convenient as possible. On April 8, all Wirex clients will get access to the updated application for iOS and Android. The new version will be faster and will include a number of improvements that we will implement in the next few weeks.



On Saturday, April 8, we will update the application.

Here is a brief overview of the changes:

  • New design

    We completed the Wirex rebranding, completely moving away from the E-coin brand. All E-coin and Wirex users who have access to e-coin.io via the desktop or mobile device will now be automatically redirected to wirexapp.com.

  • The possibility of buying Bitcoin for the currency inside the application

    Buy bitcoins for US dollars, euros and British pounds can now be inside the application.

  • Advanced Wirex Card Payment Tools

    You will be able to pay for new Wirex cards using an external wallet or an existing Wirex card.

  • Additional recharge functions

    Now you can replenish your Wirex account via bank transfer or online payment.

  • The app is now available in 13 languages.

    After the update, the application will be available in: Italian, Portuguese, Turkish, Russian, Greek, Indonesian and Tagalog. The language of the application is set automatically depending on the local settings of the smartphone.

  • A brighter application icon and logo will appear.



As always, we welcome your ideas, suggestions and feedback.

In order to use the latest version of the application, it will need to be updated on Google Play or the AppStore .
