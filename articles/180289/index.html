Video review tablet Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2



Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2 - a tablet based on the Windows 8 operating system, working on a dual-core Intel Atom Z2760 processor (1.8 GHz) with integrated PowerVR SGX 545 graphics. The RAM capacity is 2 GB, and 64 GB is available for user storage.

The tablet is equipped with two cameras (2 and 8 megapixels), an optional 3G module and an NFC chip, as well as ports: mini HDMI, micro USB, USB 2.0, audio jack and microSD card reader.
