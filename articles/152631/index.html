How to choose an SSL certificate

Due to the abundance of SSL certificates with various "chips" and options, it sometimes becomes difficult to choose the one that is right for you. Yes, so as not to overpay for unnecessary features.

REG.RU in the last year alone has issued several thousand SSL certificates for its clients. We have often seen how the owner of a small online store, without listening to objections, buys a SGC-certificate with a forcedly high level of encryption, because "I read that this is the best."

So many site owners argue their choice, because comparisons of SSL certificates from one manufacturer or by the presence / absence of any function are often found on the network, but a detailed review of all types at once and almost all manufacturers of SSL certificates with the ability to choose the comparison parameters before nowhere else was there.

Therefore, in order to speed up and facilitate the selection process, REG.RU specialists created an interactive comparison table for SSL certificates. Under the cut details.

The table is located on the REG.RU website . The interface is the easiest and most convenient: by ticking and changing the position of the price line slider, you select the necessary options, the desired manufacturer and the amount you would like to meet. The selection process takes place almost instantly and without reloading the page. Here you can order the selected SSL certificate.
