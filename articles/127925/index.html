Telephony panel for 1C

90% of Russian companies use 1C as software for business automation. Binding to 1C telephony (receiving calls to 1C, calls from 1C, opening a card) reduces costs, improves business efficiency and profitability.

And reduces call processing time:
  • up to 15 seconds with outgoing call,
  • up to 35 seconds to process an incoming call,
  • up to 2 minutes when switching within the company.

Market overview of ready-made solutions for the integration of IP-telephony and 1C showed ambiguous
results.

Softphone 1C: Rarus


Softphone from 1C: Rarus is only suitable for working with "iron" PBX. By disappointing news
from the developer, the system will not work with software IP PBX. Plus hardware support
PBX has a lot of limitations on the functionality and choice of equipment.

Other solutions


Communication 1C with other IP-platforms has various inconveniences - the high cost of the solution,
limited functionality, complexity of integration, support for only old 1C platforms.
