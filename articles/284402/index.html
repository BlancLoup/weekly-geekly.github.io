Hotline: Consumer Protection

The format of the hotline continues with a new theme:
Protection of the rights of consumers of goods and services


Rules :
You ask a question in the comments of level 1, in the comments of level 2 competent comrades answer. In the comments of the third and subsequent levels we discuss the answer.

In the course of the discussion, the question and the answers are summarized and are placed in the text of the topic with an indication of the questioner and the respondent.

Example
Question
I believe that my rights have been violated. Where to find out all this? Where to go?

Answer
When you buy something or use the service, you enter into civil law relations, which are regulated by the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (see gk-rf.ru for example).

There is also the Consumer Protection Act (RFP) (see www.consultant.ru/popular/consumerism/37_1.html ), which describes your rights and the mechanism for their implementation. This is a large and quite intelligible document, where you can learn a lot of useful things.

It is better to consult a civil lawyer (since March last year the word "civilist" has been used). In the region there should be an authority responsible for working with the public and the executives (committee or inspection). At the state level, there is the Federal Service for Supervision of Consumer Rights Protection and Human Welfare (website www.rospotrebnadzor.ru , a link to the provision on this service www.rospotrebnadzor.ru/documents/postanovlenia/654 ).

Question
The store disliked me. Can I refuse to sell a product?

Answer
If we are talking about retail trade, then, according to Article 492 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (see www.gk-rf.ru/statia492 ), the retail purchase agreement is public.

Public contract is a special form of contract. A commercial organization is obliged to conclude it with any willing citizen (article 426 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation www.gk-rf.ru/statia426 ).

For more information about the conditions and procedure for concluding a public contract (offer), read article 445 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation www.gk-rf.ru/statia445
