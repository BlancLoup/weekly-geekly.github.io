Preparing an IT demo for MMS and IT Camps - part 1

Greetings, Colleagues!

Today I would like to share my impressions about the preparation for IT Camps on our newest server OS - Windows Server 2012, and the day before this event will be MMS. Events will be held September 26-27, that is, next Thursday at the RIA Novosti site, it will be possible to personally meet and learn all the subtleties of the new OS and other new Microsoft chips ...

Perhaps, besides the reports of the speakers (well, besides the delicious food), the most interesting (if not the most useful) for the participants are always live demonstrations and laboratory work. Demo stand will always stand



We will be consistent, and we will begin with the story about the demonstration of the first day, September 26, MMS.
To demonstrate the joint collegial efforts of representatives of Microsoft and our colleagues from HP (who, by the way, provided us with VERY interesting pieces of hardware - for which many thanks to me personally from me - it is always very cool and interesting to dig deeper with high-quality, powerful and rare equipment) the decision that it will be interesting to demonstrate the deployed quotient, no, not even a hybrid, cloud based on Windows Server 2012 , System Center 2012 SP1 Beta and Windows Azure . Also on this platform is planned to deploy labs for the second day of the event - September 27.

For the demonstration, I was given a c3000 basket, 4 blades BL460 G8 with 64GB of RAM and 2 samples each, a pair of 300 GB 15K SAS disks per blade. Also in the basket there is storage P4000 with 2 TB of disk space. Just the most it is for demonstration of System Center 2012 in conjunction with the new Windows Server 2012. Here it is, my beauty (basket) of my dreams (home))). The display of hard disks is very interesting - if you have a disk configuration in RAID 0, then the LED light on the eject button lights up, warning you not to remove the disk, if you do not find RAID 1, you will not find the difference from the usual button. It is a pity there is no mechanical lock ...



The basket also has an internal network of 10GB / s, which makes it very efficient to exchange data between the blades - believe me, it is a real pleasure to see 150GB of VMs with Windows Server 2012 demo labs fly over the blade at a speed of 500 Mb / s (not forget about optimizing the size of the jumbo frame data packets). And if you remember that Windows Server 2012 can make HA-based configurations on SMB 3.0 ball - then there is a lot of tasty things waiting for us ... But we will not deprive you of the pleasure of personally seeing this and trying it.
