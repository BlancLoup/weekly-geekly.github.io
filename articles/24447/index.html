Encyclopedia Britannica materials are freely available.

Recently, there has been a trend to provide free access to paid content. One after another, various information archives make information available under the pressure of the emergence of a mass of free sources of information that are not much inferior in quality to paid counterparts.

It is the turn encyclopedia Britannica . Recently, one of the most reputable and reliable paid information resources launched a new service on the Internet called Britannica Webshare . With the help of the service, people involved in placing information on their websites (bloggers, owners of portals and news feeds, etc.) will have free access to Britannica materials. However, representatives of the encyclopedia reserve the right to refuse to provide free access to anyone.

For free access to the archives of the service Britannica Webshare, you must go through the registration process and indicate the address of your site with a brief description of the resource. All applications are subject to serious moderation. And only in the case of passing the selection, the user gets access to the content. Such users can post articles from the encyclopedia on their website.

The 32 volumes of the Encyclopedia Britannica, in which more than 65 thousand articles, will cost a rather big amount of $ 1,400. Annual subscription to the online version costs $ 70.
