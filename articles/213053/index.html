Mac Pro: A computer for those who understand why it is needed. And only for them

In response to the refusal, I heard that many thousands of people around the world are waiting for my assessment of Mac Pro. Blatant flattery is an effective tool of persuasion, and after a couple of days, this perhaps the most unusual desktop computer in the world came to the editorial board of Mira PC.

I already wrote a little about him in a report from the October Apple press conference, where Mac Pro was first shown alive , without a glass cap. The screen, the speed of work, the powerful battery, the convenient keyboard - all this is good for solving absolutely any problems.
