Moto Z Play review: flagship younger brother

Hello friends! Today we decided to tell you about the Moto Z Play smartphone. This is a very nice "middling" of glass and metal, which is really impressive for its excellent autonomy. Gadget without any problems is able to last up to 2 days without recharging, and this is confirmed not only by us, but also by many observers of the Russian and foreign media.

However, good autonomy is not the only advantage of the device. Read all the details under the cut. And at the very end of the material is a bonus: instructions on how to buy Moto Z Play and get a gift worth 3,990 rubles.



Appearance


Moto Z Play is definitely a gadget for fans of big smartphones. The frames on the sides are relatively small, but the margins above and below are quite large. This approach made it possible to place a flash on the upper part of the front panel, and a square fingerprint scanner on the lower one.



In addition, Moto Z and Moto Z Play are modular smartphones. On the back of both gadgets there is a special magnetic contact through which you can connect all sorts of useful things that we call the word "mods". One of them turns smartphones into a full-fledged compact camera with a tenfold optical zoom, and in this case, indents at the top and bottom become convenient platforms for placing thumbs.



Along the perimeter, the smartphone leads around an aluminum frame with a matte surface. Looks beautiful, touching such material is very nice.



The back panel is almost entirely covered with glass. In addition to the contact for "mods" there is a camera lens with a double flash. It protrudes slightly from the body. This is necessary in order for the element to become the second point for fixing Moto mod's on the body of the smartphone along with the magnetic platform.

The device is available in two colors - black and white. In the first case, the frame will be silver, in the second - golden.



A replacement panel is included with the Moto Z Play package. It is attached to the same magnetic connector - literally with one flick of the wrist.



Holds the panel tightly, and the appearance of the gadget with it becomes even more noble. In addition, this cover additionally protects the glass back panel and camera lens from accidental damage.



The volume buttons and the power button are located on the top of the right end. To distinguish them from each other by touch is easy - the most important key with notches. It seems to be a trifle, but in practice it is incredibly convenient!



A pull-out card tray is located on the top panel. It is noteworthy that you can install not only two nanoSIMs in it, but also microSD. For the latter there is a separate slot. A rare feature even for flagship devices.



Finish the story about the appearance of a few words about the fingerprint scanner. Let its small size not scare you: the sensor works quickly and accurately, all colleagues using the smartphone are satisfied. It is also very convenient that using a scanner you can not only unlock, but also lock the device. The algorithm is exactly the same: we put the finger when the Z Play screen is on, wait a second, and the system immediately goes into sleep mode.



Display


The screen of the Moto Z Play is 5.5 inches, it is based on an AMOLED matrix. From here are all the advantages arising from this, the most important of which are lower power consumption compared to IPS and a deep, true black color.

Given the resolution of 1920x1080 pixels, we obtain a density of more than 400 dpi. As a result, the effect of PenTile is noticeable only with the closest consideration. If you keep your smartphone at a normal distance of 10-15 or more centimeters from the nose, then there is not even any hint of pixelation.



The oversaturation of colors on AMOLED is, in our opinion, also a myth, a thing of the past. Years go by, and with them the matrices are getting better. But just in case there are two shades display modes in the settings.

The tempered glass of Gorilla Glass 3 with an excellent oleophobic coating protects the matrix from damage. Multitouch recognizes up to 10 simultaneous clicks.

Hardware component and system


Moto Z Play runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 625 processor: it has 8 Cortex-A53 cores with a frequency of 2 GHz and an integrated graphics accelerator Adreno 506. This is not the most productive chip from the middle class, but very reliable: stable and cold. The body of the smartphone, even with the most serious tests, heats up very slightly. The amount of RAM - 3 GB. There is also an additional low-power Hexagon 546 signal processor for speech processing and contextual computing. Performing its functions in sleep mode, it consumes very little energy.

In the overall performance test system AnTuTu Moto Z Play gaining more than 60,000 points. This is exactly the level from which you can safely talk about absolutely comfortable working with the system and almost any applications. Only the most demanding games like World of Tank Blitz can make you slightly lower the graphics settings - from maximum to medium.



In the test of the graphics subsystem 3DMark Sling Shot we get 831 points or 462 points in Extreme mode. In Geekbench 4 processor tests, the following indicators are: 750 points in single-core and 2350 points in multi-core tests.

Camera


The main Moto Z Play camera consists of a matrix with a resolution of 16 MP and a lens with aperture of F / 2.0. Taking photos on it during the day or indoors is a pleasure. The autofocus system combines three methods at once: phase, contrast and laser. So, in addition to high speed and good accuracy, we also get the opportunity to bring the smartphone close to the subjects. The result is interesting macrophotography.
