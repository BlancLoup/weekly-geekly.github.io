Wiki tutorial on web technologies

In the continuation of the article stepan_ovchinnikov ‚Äú Learning web technologies. Necessary, unnecessary, harmful . "

Modern web technologies are not standing still. Website creation technologies are constantly being improved and now the speed of their improvement is so high that textbooks often become significantly outdated before publication. To make learning web programming dynamic, we invite you to participate in the development of a Wiki textbook on web technologies .

The initial basis of this textbook was the lectures on web technologies, compiled by Stepan Ovchinnikov ( stepan_ovchinnikov ).

The implementation of a textbook on web technologies in the form of a Wiki-system allows teachers to share materials and arbitrarily structure an individual selection, as well as to use the developments of specialists in areas of web technologies in their lectures.

We hope that this site will be useful to anyone who leads or listens to training courses, be it lectures or seminars on web technologies.

It is planned to structure the materials in such a way that this system allows a person to understand and master the creation of modern websites and web applications from scratch.

If you know how to use the WackoWiki wiki markup and want to participate in a wiki tutorial, sign up and you can create pages and write comments. If you do not know yet how to use wiki markup, you can familiarize yourself with a brief selection of links to instructions .

Using the Wiki technology will allow you even in the absence of a large amount of free time to contribute to the development of this tutorial.

All questions regarding the functioning of the website of the wiki-tutorial on web technologies you can ask me - Iliagi or the user of Elar9000 on the site of the wiki-textbook.

The tutorial is available at: http://doc.olspire.com/
