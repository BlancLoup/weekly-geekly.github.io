20+ resources to start freelancing

Do you feel ready to leave work, control your day on your own, fight for a client and work only on interesting tasks? It's time to try yourself in freelancing. It is not easy at all, and more often it is more difficult than office routine. In order not to be disappointed in the choice of work format, arrange a test drive for yourself - on holidays or on weekends. Collected sites where you will find tasks for the soul.



For those who have long been in the subject // The largest and most visited


Weblancer.net is a large remote work exchange. After registration, be sure to complete your portfolio - will help get more orders.

Freelance.ru is one of the major freelance exchanges. It was originally a forum.

FL.ru - Exchange for freelancers of different specializations. To effectively promote your services you need to buy a PRO-account.

Freelancehunt.com is a labor exchange for programmers, designers, copywriters, and other professionals.

FreelanceJob.ru - is positioned as an exchange for professional freelancers with a good portfolio.

printf ("hello, world"); // For developers


1clancer.ru - remote work for 1C specialists. Many jobs with good budgets.

Devhuman.com is a service for developers, startups and IT companies. You can quickly assemble a team of any specialists to perform any IT project.

Modber.ru is another exchange for 1C developers.

Freelansim.ru is an exchange for developers with a lot of interesting tasks and projects. Sure, habrovchane well acquainted with her.

FreelanceWebmarket - foreign platform for developers.

Toptal is a closed area, to get started you need to pass a selection consisting of four stages: checking English proficiency level, online coding test, technical screen interview and test project. After that, the freelancer signs a contract and becomes one of the Toptal developers, who is looking for projects for him and trying to download work. It is the stock exchange, not the client, that pays developers for their work on projects.



Play with fonts // For designers and illustrators


Illustrators.ru - work for illustrators, new projects almost every day.

99designs - a competition is held for receiving each order, where participants send their solutions to the problem (example of a logo or a site layout). The winner is chosen by the customer. The winner receives a pre-determined fee, transfers the rights to the project to the client and becomes the priority executor of the subsequent orders of this employer.

Crowd Spring is a great platform for designers and illustrators. Works on the service principle above.

Coroflot - there is a list of vacancies and projects for designers for which you can apply. Coroflot does not charge a commission on either side, but does not give any guarantees.

Krop - many tasks for web designers.

DesignCrowd - freelancers offer customers a quote, after which the client chooses a contractor and pays for services. Then starts work on the project.

LogoMyWay - orders for logo creators. To get an order, you need to win the competition.

Shutterstock.com - exchange of images, illustrations.

Istockphoto.com - exchange of images, illustrations. There are entrance exams on the photo stock. It is necessary to provide three photos and pass the theoretical test.



Any miscellaneous // Sites with different types of tasks and useful services


Programmer Meet Designer - the platform helps designers and programmers to find each other to collaborate on projects.

Workzilla - orders are very different: from video editing to creating one-page sites. The customer forms the task, the performers respond and call their terms and price, after which the customer chooses the performer.

UpWork is a foreign platform with tasks for developers, designers, and marketers.

People Per Hour is mainly a job for copywriters and marketers, but you can find tasks for developing software or creating landing pages.

Guru is another foreign platform with a variety of tasks.

IFreelance
- A large service with a lot of work, you can find tasks for the development of databases and software.

Exchange on bots in Telegram


ArbeitBot - fill out the profile with skills, mark the areas of interest of interest and receive notifications about new tasks to which you can respond.

FreelanceBot - the bot asks who you are: a programmer, designer, artist or manager, and after - who are looking for and what time the application will be relevant. The indicated time will receive variants of orders / offers of performers.

Parselancer - Parse puzzles with freelancer and freelancer . Notifies you of new jobs in selected categories.
