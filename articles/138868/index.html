Offbeatr - an incubator for strawberry startups

Many people know the service for microfinance startups Kickstarter , where an entrepreneur can present an original idea, and users throw off a few dollars for its implementation. Many clones were created from this pattern, and another one appeared - perhaps the most unusual among all.



The new project Offbeatr will work in the same way, but with a specialization in porn startups. Each, so to speak, producer of original content can show its work to users. If they like it, they will vote with a ruble to continue the shooting. In addition to video, start-ups for the production of new original gadgets and some other goods can probably be placed on the site.

By the way, the same Kickstarter last year financed 27086 projects for 99 million dollars , so there is someone to equal, because in the adult industry there is also a lot of money.
