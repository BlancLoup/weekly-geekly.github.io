Does the site work? Customer survey after the delivery of the project

I guess the situation is pretty typical.

We work in Volgograd, and the vast majority of our clients are local small and medium businesses operating in the regional market.
Many regional companies create websites. At the same time, the share of those who are concerned about the attendance and the result (in the form of sales) is small.

The most visited local sites of companies (and they can be counted on the fingers) have no more than 200 visitors per day (Volgograd, correct).
We are not talking about portals ( Volga34 , Volgograd in the network , v1.ru ) and forums (for example, the Volgograd forum ).

On average, this number for a decent "corporate site" ranges between 20 and 90.
It is clear that this is a miser.

To identify developmental needs, information support, search engine optimization and advertising campaigns, we conduct such a survey some time after the project is completed.

I would like to know the opinion of site owners, specialists in promotion, marketing, sociology about how this survey will help to understand the needs of the client.

It is assumed that the questionnaire will be filled in during the conversation with the manager or the person in charge who better understands the tactics and strategy of behavior in the market.
