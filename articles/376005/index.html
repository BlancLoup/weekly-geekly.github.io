Introducing the new generation of Intel SSD for data centers



The Intel SSD family for servers and data centers has been extended by the DC S3610 and DC S3710 families . The S3610 is a mid-level SSD that delivers twice the performance gains compared to budget models. S3710 are designed to ensure the efficiency of disk operations in high-load systems (write speed increased by 25% against the previous generation). The form factor of the SSD is 2.5 "SATA. The S3610 series is also expected to be released in the 1.8" case.
