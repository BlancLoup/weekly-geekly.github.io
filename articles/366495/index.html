63-year-old woman arrested in Romania for using torrents

Romanian police arrested a 63-year-old woman for copyright infringement using BitTorrent. The arrest took place in the city of Cluj-Napoca, a criminal case was opened.

As part of the investigation, the police found out that the suspect had made a significant number of music albums, films and other content publicly available, without the appropriate permission of the copyright holders. Perhaps this is the oldest woman in the world who can be condemned for using torrents. In 2011, a similar case occurred in Scotland: a 58-year-old woman was sentenced to three years probation.

TorrentFreak notes a non-standard situation for Romania: torrent sites operate freely in the country, and such cases of arrests are very rare. The source of the publication said that the police are under pressure from companies-owners.
