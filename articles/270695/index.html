The three best technology companies of the Forum and the Open Innovations Technology Show received IBM cloud grants in the amount of $ 120,000



As part of the Open Innovations Forum and Technology Show, IBM, a special partner of the event, awarded cloud grants to the brightest technology startups. Natalia Galinovskaya, head of the IBM Russia / CIS cloud solutions department, presented five cloud grants, in the amount of $ 12,000 each, to the winners of the Open Stage demo sessions, to use the IBM Bluemix cloud platform. Also special prizes were awarded three projects. The teams of these projects received grants of $ 120,000 each.

UNIM project


It is a service for remote consultation of digitized histological preparations, increases the accuracy of research, reduces the time for issuance of conclusions, is a platform for collecting Medical Big Data and a knowledge base for pathologists.
Service developers call UNIM the standard for oncological diagnostics. Thus, the service offers histological, immunohistochemical and molecular analyzes of expert level in the field of oncological diagnostics, and also offers the development and implementation of software for digital pathomorphology, which allows to solve diagnostic and scientific problems.

The service specialization is as follows:
- Immunohistochemical analyzes;
- Histological analyzes;
- Revision of histological glasses;
- Revision of glasses abroad.

Atlas project


Since 2013, Biomedical Holding Atlas has been developing and introducing personalized medicine tools into clinical practice. The company opened the first in Russia center of personalized medicine and conducts research and development in the field of human genetics.



The project proposes to conduct a DNA analysis of the client, having received detailed information about the features of his body. Among other data, Atlas provides information on 335 different patient health indicators, recommends the fulfillment of certain nutrition and sports regulations, tells the story of ethnic origin.

Surfancy Project


Interactive Surfancy system allows you to create a scalable touch surface in any convenient place. In this way, Surfancy enables a person, by interacting with physical objects, to receive a multimedia response.



A business that uses Surfancy to create interactive shelves and windows, gets the ability to monitor customer interest in real time through the statistics tools and heat map.
