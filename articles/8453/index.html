Imeem.com is finally hit

I hope that many people know such a resource as www.imeem.com . It seems like the usual social. a network where you can upload music, video, photos ... BUT! The trick is that on imeem you can put your song and then everyone who wants it can listen. At the same time, they also allow you to embed this composition in your blog, website, etc. in the form of a player (like YouTube). For me, it was the real and only way to share my favorite music with friends on your LJ.

And finally - everyone puts what he wants, so there are no issues of ownership, etc.
