The player continues to compete with the deceased father and does not want him to win, so as not to erase the ghost

This is quite a touching story, and it seems to me that it is the place here. Because making games is also very important. So imagine: the usual game video posted on youtube. In the comments, the usual discussion and hubbub. But among them comes a comment from user 00WARTHERAPY00, who writes that he continues to play car simulator with his late father.



I give a free translation of the comment :
Well, when I was 4 years old, my father bought a real Xbox.
The one, the first, angular of 2001. We got a lot of fun playing together in all games. And so it was until he died when I was 6.

I could not touch the console for 10 years. But as soon as I did it, I noticed something: we were playing the racing game Rally Sports Challenge, quite advanced for that time. And as soon as I launched it, I discovered a ghost. Well, you know, when the result of the fastest lap is recorded and displayed as a ghost car. Yes, you guessed it, it is still rolling down the road. And so I played, played and played, until I was able to practically defeat the ghost. One day, I was in front, I beat him, but ... I stopped right in front of the finish line, just to make sure I did not remove it.
