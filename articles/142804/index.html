What would our world look like if the Internet had existed for thousands of years

We can hardly imagine the world without modern technologies, they have grown so much into our everyday life that using them we do not even think about how we used to live. The authors of the video decided to give a fantastic opportunity to famous characters from the past to take advantage of what we now have. How would people look in the world with the Internet, popular sites and how they would enjoy these benefits. How would reflect the events of the past on the Internet.

It looks like a viral advertisement of some kind of event, but this video was not particularly affected.

The video is only entertainment and in no case does not claim to be serious.
Outstanding personalities were noticed in the video: Moses, Nicolaus Copernicus, Lev Nikolayevich Tolstoy, Kazimir Malevich, Alexander Sergeevich Pushkin and others.
