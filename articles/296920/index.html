Where it works: contextual advertising, landing pages, promotional pages of brands, grocery pages for SEO, product descriptions in the online store, advertising brochures, marketing kit, commercial offers. In almost any format where the task is to sell, to induce users to the target action.

The technique is simple: in order to master it, you do not need to learn copywriting skills at trainings and pay money for it (for example, Dmitry Kota, intensive, costs 19,000 rubles). It is enough to learn how to formulate your value proposition in 4 elements.

So let's go. Parse step by step on a specific example. Theme - sale of GPS-monitoring systems for vehicles.

Usefulness (Usefulness)

This is the ultimate customer benefit. What he wants to solve with your product or service. Not search promotion (property), but increase in sales through search promotion (benefit). Not real estate services, and the sale of a house or apartment. In other words, not a drill, but holes in the wall.

Utility is a must-have in the 4U model. We begin with it. This is usually a verb - save, reduce, save, protect, return, increase, multiply, double, get, earn. Where appropriate - in the imperative mood (get, save, earn).

‚ÄúPull out‚Äù the main benefit that interests your audience. The options in our case, for which people put the GPS monitoring system - control, safety, saving money (by reducing downtime and left-wing drivers, optimizing routes and speed, customers get reduced fuel costs and T / O).

Since 90% of Central Asia are transport companies, they are interested in financial gain. Hence the usefulness - "Reduce transportation costs . "

Uniqueness

Also required element of the formula. It means, due to what your profit works. Product or service, ideally - with the adjustment of competitors. Just do not ‚Äúshout‚Äù that your product is the best or unique. This is prohibited by the law on advertising, without valid evidence. We have a soft option - "New GPS monitoring system . "

The best

Ultra-specificity (Ultraspecificity)

Expresses favor in measurable units. How much in rubles or interest you will increase sales or reduce costs; how many hours or days will you deliver the product or provide a service and so on. In our heading - "Reduce costs to 32% . "

Urgency

Time parameter Moreover, this is not necessarily a deadline "before January 1" or "only the first 10 customers." In some cases, you can do without it. There is an advantageous time condition - set, no - reduce the circuit to 3U.

In our title - "Test Drive 14 days for free . " Yes, if urgency overloads the proposal, put it in the subtitle.
