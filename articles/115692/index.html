Free GTD Workshop in St. Petersburg

Hello friends!

Last year I wrote on Habrahabr two articles ( one and two ) on the use of the Allen system.

By the reaction of a respected community, it was clear that the topic is relevant to many.

If you are from St. Petersburg and want to learn more about the GTD modification that I use, come to the open and free seminar.

It will be held on Monday, March 21, at 27 Marat Street, in INJEKON.

Register for the event here .

From 19 to 21 hours I will talk about the time management system based on GTD.

Specifically, it will be about the following things:

1. The main problems that provided the emergence of time management.
2. The causes of these problems.
3. How time management system can eliminate the causes and eliminate problems.
4. What are the direct and indirect effects achieved through the use of a time management system?
5. How to increase your efficiency 6 times?
6. How to start a rest after work and stop working at the weekend?
