Emotion AI and Emotional Technologies: Neurodata Lab and NRU ITMO Conference

March 30, 2018 Neurodata Lab LLC and NRU ITMO hold a one-day conference in St. Petersburg (an event in Moscow is also scheduled for the near future) dedicated to the Emotion AI industry, its features and prospects.

Registration is available here .

A preliminary announcement is provided below in the body of the publication.



Announcement


Emotional artificial intelligence, the technology of automatic recognition of emotions and human behavior is an interdisciplinary field of research, which in the 2010s transformed into a full-fledged, rapidly growing industry. In fact, we are talking about a new technology market, which is being developed thanks to the effective interaction of academic science with the technology business and the venture capital environment. The active participation of Russian startups and laboratories in it, as well as open opportunities for research and business collaborations, will make it possible to successfully scale innovative technological solutions and integrate emotional technologies into various sectoral contexts.

Assumed blocks of reports in a number of areas:

  1. Introductory block: Emotion AI from the point of view of science, Emotion AI as a new technology market, state funding programs Neuronet, ITMO technopark and other infrastructure in St. Petersburg;
  2. Project block: brief presentations by representatives of start-ups and laboratories;
  3. Technical unit: Emotion AI through the lens of ML / CV approaches;
  4. Block of open initiatives: Emotion AI and AI hub platform from Neurodata Lab, open source project of NIU ITMO with FEFU ‚ÄúNeurotechnology, VR / AR‚Äù, etc.
  5. Discussion and questions.

The program of the event will be published later on the NRU ITMO website.
