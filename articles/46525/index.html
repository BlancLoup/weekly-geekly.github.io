Spam protection

Inspired by the new captcha idea .

Why not create a system in which any user can register by entering his nickname, email and mobile number. A confirmation code will be sent to your mobile to activate your OpenId account.
It remains to make agreements with large portals to use OpenId for registration / login. You can do api.

In this case, spam will be very small and users will be checked, because finding a new "left" phone number is much more difficult than hacking a captcha. You can develop a system of complaints about the user (from bots).

Of course, the firm must be with good authority, so that users trust it. The offer must state that all information will not be distributed and issued to third parties (to avoid leaving phone numbers in the spam database).

Money can be withdrawn for the provision of services after the base grows.
