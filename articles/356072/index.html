Roskomnadzor proposed a new way to identify connections to public Wi-Fi networks



During the last inspection of 18 thousand public Wi-Fi access points, Roskomnadzor recorded 5.5 thousand violations in the user identification procedure. In relation to telecom operators that provided access to the Internet through these points, administrative cases are initiated under paragraph 3 of Art. 14.1. Administrative Code of the Russian Federation (Doing business without state registration or without a special permit (license)).

The regulator also took the initiative to tighten control over access to the Internet using public access points.
