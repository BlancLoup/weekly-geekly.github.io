JavaFX on Raspberry Pi

Simon Ritter managed to launch a JavaFX application on the Raspberry Pi platform.

To run it, he used the commercial version of Open JDK for ARM processors. He failed to rebuild JavaFX (due to laziness, as he says) and he used the non-public JavaFX build for the Beagle Board platform.
