This is HTC Desire Eye: 5.2 inches, 13 MP front camera and waterproof case

Four years later, after the release of its legendary smartphone HTC Desire, a Taiwanese company decided to dust the glorious name and showed today in New York a phone called HTC Desire Eye.



The chip, which the manufacturer considers key, provokes grumpy remarks about the decline of morals: this is a 13-megapixel front-facing camera with a 22-millimeter wide-angle lens, specially designed for better quality selfies. Hence the word Eye in the title. Cyclops, it turns out - the camera is located exactly in the center of the "forehead" With his only (front) eye, he can still shoot FullHD video and even dazzle victims with a dual LED flash. The Desire Eye rear camera has the same 13 megapixels hidden behind a 28mm lens and its own personal dual flash.



Let us turn to more sensible parameters: the Desire Eye's screen diagonal is 5.2 inches, which is bigger than the original Desire, but by today's standards such a smartphone can be considered medium-sized. Display resolution - 1920x1080. The case dimensions are 151.773.88.5 mm, the degree of protection is IPX67, which means dust tightness and the possibility of a short dive into the water to a depth of one meter.



And instead of the heart of our Cyclops quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 801, overclocked to 2.3 GHz, two gigabytes of RAM, 16 gigabytes of internal memory and a slot for microSD up to 128 GB and a battery of only 2400 mAh (something hilovato cyclolopik).
