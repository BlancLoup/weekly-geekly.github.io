Google decided to bury the RSS?

A few days ago, the RSS Subscription extension for Chrome stopped working for me. This is an official extension from Google that adds an RSS icon to the address bar, by clicking on which you can quickly subscribe to the feeds of the open site.
A quick search on the Internet showed that I am not alone . Moreover, this extension has been removed from the Google Chrome store!
Comparing this with the recent sad news about the closure of Google Reader , we can conclude that Google decided to completely abandon RSS.
What do you think about that?

UPD (03/19/2013 09:40): The original extension was returned to the web store. Thanks liaren for the info.
