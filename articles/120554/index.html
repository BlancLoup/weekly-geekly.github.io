BackupDay is testing Acronis True Image Home 2011

The LiveJournal initiative BackupDay , which was reported here, has already attracted two and a half hundred readers and continues to grow. The topic of timely backup did turn out to be in demand among the masses - especially the female part of it, which cares about family photo archives.

Acronis noticed the project and offered a contest - test and write a review on Acronis True Image Home 2011 or Acronis Disk Director 11 Home and get its free activation for 2 years for personal use.



How to participate?

1 . You tell me what product you want to write (you can do both at once) - my mail: roman.ravve (at) gmail.com

2 I send you activation keys for 30 days to test

3 You write a review and publish it somewhere on your blog with a link to the LiveJournal announcement and tell me about it (you can email). By the way, reviews that test tech support on the website - www.acronis.ru/support - will receive additional weight - there is a simple registration, answers to questions, a chat with support staff and so on.

4 I think that within 2 weeks the best reviews will be selected, and their authors will be awarded free activations.

Note : the review should be honest - if you see I get on well and write !

How is the best review chosen?

This is a question ... I would like to contact him to you - what are the ideas, how to choose the best post? The easiest option - the Acronis team chooses to your taste. But I would like to arrange something more social, with a vote, maybe. How could this be done?
