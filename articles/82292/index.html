SETI goes to open source



Yesterday, on January 27, the creation of a new project, setiQuest, an open community of the SETI project (search for signs of intelligent life in distant space), was announced .

It is reported that the source code of the SETI project will be posted in open source in the second quarter of this year. The code will be uploaded to the modules so that the community can discuss and try it, in addition, it will allow you to quickly prepare high-quality documentation.

The setiQuest project invites everyone to join the community setiquest.org .
