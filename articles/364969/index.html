Friday jewelery DIY



I had a penny silver sterling silver ring with a loose and lost stone. It would seem, where does the radio?

Despite the fact that the price of this ring is three rubles per market day, I wanted to fix it. Tried to insert various pieces of ornamental stones - did not succeed. They are very difficult to handle Dremel because of the small size. Moreover, the landing site for a stone with a spherical groove is difficult to grind the insert. Although superglue can glue anything, the stones did not want to live in a ring for more than a couple of days.

Tell me, do you unsolder the failed boards on the radio components? I suffer from this disease in a chronic form. And otpaivaya with the late motherboard another mosfeta, I suddenly looked at the ceramic SMD capacitors with different eyes.

It took half an hour to experiment, it took tweezers, file and superglue (which could have been done without it). It turned out, in my opinion, concisely and original.
Ring


A week has passed - the flight is normal. I wear a ring around the clock, without removing it when washing hands, etc. The plans (if the construction does not fall apart) are to polish the frame with a nozzle for Dremel and GOI paste.
