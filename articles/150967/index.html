"All day phone" - Motorola has updated the line of RAZR

On the same day, when Nokia presented its new smartphones, Motorola held its press conference, at which it presented an updated lineup of the RAZR family with the following names - RAZR Maxx HD, RAZR HD and RAZR M and with the slogan " All day phone ". Common to all models is the support of fourth-generation networks, Android 4.0 with the promised addition to a newer version of Jelly Bean (if something suddenly goes wrong, the manufacturer promises to every buyer 100 USD) and a solid operating time without recharging.



RAZR Maxx HD

This model is primarily characterized by an increased battery capacity of up to 3,300 mAh, which, according to Motorola engineers, can allow it to work up to 21 hours in talk mode, 27 and 10 hours in streaming music and video, respectively. The dimensions of the smartphone are 131.9 x 67.9 x 9.3 mm, weight 157 g, the back panel is made of Kevlar. Also, this model supports the WebTop technology, which allows you to connect it through a docking station to a computer and work with the device's OS on a large screen.

With the Android version of this device, the situation is not very clear - although the announcement indicates that all devices have Android 4.0 installed, however, the specifications give another - Android 2.3.6 (Gingerbread) Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) upgradeable .

In general, the hardware characteristics of the model are as follows:

  • Qualcomm Krait 2-core processor
  • graphics Adreno 225
  • 1 GB RAM
  • 16 GB of internal memory
  • 4.7 "Super AMOLED 720p display
  • 8 MP camera, recording FullHD video
  • microSD slot
  • wireless interfaces Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0, NFC
  • GPS
  • microHDMI
