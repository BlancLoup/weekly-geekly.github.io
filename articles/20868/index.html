Startup - is it when you quit your job and invest all the power and balance of your last salary in your site, hoping to have time to buy an island in the Pacific by summer? Then I have a typical startup :) True, there is something that distinguishes my startup from the classic ones - I did not bother and invent a new idea (or rather, I was tired of bothering and inventing new ideas).
