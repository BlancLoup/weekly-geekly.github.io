Do good

The article was written by the CrazyPit habraiser , but it lacks the karma to publish.

Each of us is pleased to do good. True, many are embarrassed to think about it.

I had an idea how the owners of highly visited resources can help people without special expenses.

There are a large number of charitable organizations, as well as many who accept donations via the Internet. On the other hand, there are many websites that can provide informational coverage to such organizations. If you hang a banner with a link to a similar fund, you will practically lose nothing, but you will probably help people who need it.

Of course, for resources that make money on advertising, this may be a loss of revenue, but there are often not redeemed shows left. And for resources whose income from internal advertising does not depend (for example, online games), placing such a banner costs nothing at all. Here we did it in our game yesterday, after which the idea was born.



The main problem is that it is not enough to find such organizations, it is also necessary to contact them with a request to make a banner, or even make it yourself.

To solve this problem, you can create a resource where, on the one hand, charitable organizations participate, which provide a description of their goals as well as banners. On the other hand, the owners of Internet resources that can provide impressions.

Thus, anyone who wishes to help can easily do it without wasting time making a banner or searching for organizations.

Of course, there is the problem of checking charitable organizations and advertising among them, and Internet resources, but this is also possible. There are many ways, from word of mouth, to reviews on the same resource.
