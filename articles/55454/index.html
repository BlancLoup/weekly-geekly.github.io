New service MixMarket.BIZ - designer of partner programs Mix-Uni

The partner network MixMarket.BIZ has launched a new service - the designer of partner programs Mix-Uni.

In Mix-Uni, convenient reward payment models are implemented:
- CPC (pay per click);
- CPA (pay per action: filling out the questionnaire; viewing the desired page, etc.);
- CPS (pay per sale:% of sales).
