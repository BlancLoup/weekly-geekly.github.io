Toshiba STOR.E TV +

Toshiba STOR.E TV + - a new full-featured 2 terabyte HD multimedia drive. The device works independently, without the help of a personal computer, and due to the presence of a large number of interfaces, it easily connects to the home network and becomes its control link.


External drives can not only play the role of backup storage. They can serve as multimedia centers that themselves take care of transferring data to various devices connected to them. Thanks to Toshiba STOR.E TV +, you no longer need to do the tedious conversion on a personal computer to play materials on a TV screen. The new device allows you to easily play almost any video.

STOR.E TV + offers a wide range of interfaces, including USB, composite video, Ethernet, and HDMI for transferring uncompressed 1080p content to a TV with a similar connector. In addition, the new multimedia drive supports connectivity to a WLAN network. STOR.E TV + allows you to stream data from video cameras, including the CAMILEO SX900, and external hard drives connected to the device via the USB interface. The user only needs to open the STOR.E TV + menu on the TV screen and select the necessary files for playback.


The drive is designed to support a wide variety of formats, including the most popular audio formats MP3 and AAC and video formats WMV, AVI, MPEG, as well as image formats JPEG, BMP, TIFF and GIF. The device also supports surround sound technology, including Dolby Digital.

Toshiba also offers a version of STOR.E TV + with Wi-Fi connectivity. To do this, use the optional Wi-Fi dongle, which can be placed in one of the two USB ports of the device, thereby integrating the drive into your home wireless network. STOR.E TV + devices are equipped with a module for reading SDHC format memory cards, the capacity of which can reach 64 GB.
