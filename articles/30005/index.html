Online room layout



Floor Planner beta is a very entertaining room planning service. You can take into account in the planning of anything, from the walls and the floor, ending with a sofa and a set of plates.

The interface is simple and intuitive, even a child will understand it. It is possible to view the layouts in both 2D and 3D. It seems that there is even a full-screen mode, although I did not find it :-).


It seems to be nothing, this service will not replace us with AutoCAD and Sims. But the emphasis of this service is social!

On this service you can:
  • Comment on the work of other users.
  • You can open your work for other users.
  • Share your ideas by inserting your work into a blog, forum, anywhere.
In addition, the service can be used without registration (which, by the way, does not take more than 10 seconds) in order to evaluate the service.
Generally plan and share your ideas!
