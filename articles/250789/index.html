Live webcast IT Conference: Cloud in Russia - February 18 at 10:00 (MSK)

Good day to all!
Tomorrow, February 18, 2015, the largest Microsoft technology conference, IT Conference: Cloud in Russia, will be held at the Digital October site in Moscow. Participants will be able to choose between four technology tracks: modernization of the local IT infrastructure, application of a hybrid IT infrastructure, expansion of enterprise mobility, a platform for business analysis and data management. For those who can not personally attend the event, there will be an online broadcast, a link to which is located under the cat. Beginning at 10:00 (MSK).





IT Conference: The Cloud in Russia is designed to answer the most pressing issues and challenges of the modern IT industry:
  • How to use cloud technologies in full compliance with legal requirements?
  • How to maximize the effectiveness of IT infrastructure through the use of advanced virtualization technologies and management tools?
  • How to expand existing capabilities using hybrid structures and transferring part of the load to the cloud?
  • How to ensure secure access to corporate network resources from mobile devices running on different platforms and how to manage such devices?
  • How to manage accumulated data and make informed decisions with their help?


A detailed conference program is available on the event website.
And also do not miss the opportunity to participate in the Microsoft Airlines contest , the results of which will be announced during the online broadcast on February 18 at 13.00 Moscow time .
You can also follow what is happening at a social media conference using the hash tag - #msitconf .
