Production Nokia N810 WiMax Edition - discontinued.

According to the guys from Mobileburn, who have reliable sources at Nokia, the Finnish giant has stopped production of the Nokia N810 WiMax Edition internet tablet, the main difference between which and the usual N810 is having a WiMax module for working with IEEE 802.16 networks.

The main reason for the decommissioning of the N810 WiMax Edition is the slow adoption of WiMax networks in the United States and the low efficiency of network utilization.
