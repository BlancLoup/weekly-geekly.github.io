What is a trailer and how does a trailer differ from video advertising?

We often meet movie trailers - short videos that carry a vivid image. They differ from ordinary advertising in that they transmit capacious and complex content. Two hours of films must be packaged in a couple of minutes. A similar task stands before the site advertising.

The trailer allows you to demonstrate from a profitable side a project with a large internal content, a complex structure, and not obvious advantages at first glance.

Why do you need a site trailer?

Having seen the trailer, the user already knows exactly why he came to the site, and what he will do here. He knows what services there are, which buttons to press and how to place an order. He will not leave in 10 seconds just because he got here by accident, because he could not figure it out. The trailer as if says to the viewer: ‚ÄúThe site is simple and clear, and there is a lot of tasty here - join‚Äù.

How is a site trailer better than other types of advertising on the Internet?

1. The cost of developing a trailer less placement of banner advertising;
