Twitter will actively cooperate with Russian advertising agencies and brands.

Twitter sales director in Russia and China, Peter Greenberger, praised the company's advertising audience in our country. According to him, the Russian audience accessible to advertisers is 26 million unique users. He reported this in Moscow, at an event for the press.

Greenberg noted that the calculation took into account users of the desktop version of Twitter, as well as users of mobile applications. This figure cannot be equated to the direct number of users of the microblogging service in Russia - there are a few more, he said.

Twitter Vice President Shailesh Rao noted that 80% of the audience visits the site from mobile devices, 82% of the audience watches the video, and 90% of all video views are mobile.

One of the most popular themes in the world, he called the Oscar film award, the Ferguson riots, the bloody moon and the debate about the color of the dress. The last topic collected a total of 4.8 million tweets, picking up 303 posts per hour.
