EntroPay - we pay by credit card to the Internet

When paying for services or purchases on the Internet, especially on foreign resources, a person with a credit card issued by a domestic bank has problems:

  • Most Russian banks require the transfer of cvv / cvv2 for each transaction (goodbye PayPal).
  • The bank is located in Russia - some banks do not spend our credit cards.
  • I don‚Äôt want to enter a credit card number on questionable sites. (Although this is true not only for domestic credit cards)

What Entropay allows us to:
  • Create virtual credit cards VISA, which you can pay on almost all sites and use on PayPal.
  • Transfer money from conventional credit cards to virtual ones.
  • Transfer money between virtual credit cards.


Let's say if I need to pay for the service on the site, which causes me to disbelief, I can:

Create a virtual credit card
Transfer to it exactly the amount needed to pay for the service.
Pay for the service.
Destroy a virtual credit card.

Actually Entropay, in addition to solving problems with payment by domestic credit cards, is one of the most powerful, in my opinion, means of protection against phishing.

UPD: Entropay removes 4.95% of the input funds. No more payments, incl. for the maintenance of the card is not taken.
