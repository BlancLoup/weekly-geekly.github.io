Movies.io - glamorous UI to search for torrents

The new site Movies.io shows how the search engine interface for torrents can be. Auto-completion of requests and automatic loading of background wallpapers from the selected movie look very nice.



Registered users can also create lists for viewing and subscribe to them via RSS. These lists can be shared and edited collectively.

The creators of the new search engine did everything possible to avoid the punishing hand of American justice: they registered a domain outside the US in the normal domain zone, they do not post pirated content, do not violate the DMCA, do not sell anything, do not show ads, that is, non-commercial service. This is not even a torrent tracker, but simply a beautiful experimental UI.
