Report on the results of "My Circle" for November 2015

We continue to share the analysis of "My Circle" on the work in the IT industry. Today we will tell you what competition was among specialists in November 2015, and also show the most popular vacancies in the main areas of activity.



In November, about 500 vacancies were posted on My Circle. On average, 6.71 specialists responded to each job. In this regard, nothing has changed compared with October - then, for each vacancy, on average, had 6.73 responses .

In November, on My Circle, the easiest way was to find a designer, layout designer and administrator. On average, 11 specialists responded to each vacancy in these areas. It was also not very difficult to find a manager and sales manager. In these areas of activity, the number of responses for each vacancy averaged 8.5. It was a little harder to find a tester and programmer. The number of responses to such vacancies was 6.5.
