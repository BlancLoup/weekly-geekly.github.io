Training Splunk - training center now in Russia

We are often asked how and where to learn to work at Splunk. We wrote about various books and materials and courses earlier in this article. But today we want to tell you that now you can get official full-time training Splunk in Russian in Moscow!



Recently, the first certified educational center Splunk - NTC (Network Training Center) opened in Russia. About what courses you can listen there and what certification to get read under the cat.

What are the advantages?


What are the advantages of passing full-time courses in a certified center?

  1. Getting knowledge from a certified trainer;
  2. Mastering practical material under the control of an instructor;
  3. Splunk official training materials;
  4. An integrated approach to learning (a wide range of courses);
  5. The possibility of passing all courses in their native language;
  6. Certificate of attendance;
  7. Preparation for the certification exam.

At the moment, the training center offers three basic courses: Splunk Fundamentals 2 (it is assumed that you have already completed Splunk Fundamentals 1, as it is available free of charge online), Splunk System Administration , Splunk Data Administration . These courses are the main foundation of your knowledge about Splunk, and if you already have practical skills, they will help you systematize your knowledge. Below is a brief description of the courses and links to registration. Soon the line of courses available will be expanded.

Splunk Fundamentals 2


This course is focused on search teams and teams for generating statistical reports, as well as on the creation of knowledge objects. Key topics include using transformative commands and visualizations, filtering and formatting results, correlating events, creating knowledge objects, using field aliases and calculated fields, creating tags and types of events, using macros, creating workflow actions (workflow) ) and data models, as well as data normalization using the Common Interface Model (CIM).

Requirements:

Recommended
Splunk Fundamentals 1 (Free)

After completing the course, students will be able to pass the Power User Level Exam and receive a certificate. (Subject to the presence of the User certificate obtained after passing Splunk Fundamentals 1)

More information about the course can be found at this link .

Splunk System Administration


This course is designed for system administrators who are responsible for managing the Splunk Enterprise environment. The course covers the setup, management and monitoring of key components of Splunk Enterprise. During the course, students will gain knowledge of managing Splunk licenses, indexers, and search-oriented instances.

Requirements

Required
Splunk Fundamentals 1 (free)

Recommended
Splunk Fundamentals 2

More information about the course can be found at this link .

Splunk Data Administration


This course is intended for system administrators who are responsible for obtaining data in Splunk Indexers. The course participants will gain fundamental knowledge about Splunk forwarders and methods for obtaining remote data. The course covers the installation, configuration, management, monitoring and troubleshooting of Splunk forwarders and Splunk Deployment Server components.

Requirements

Splunk Fundamentals 1 (free)

After completing the Fundamentals 2, System Administration, Data Administration courses and obtaining a Power User certificate, you can pass the exam and get Splunk Admin certification.
