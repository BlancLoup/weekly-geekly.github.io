Today I would like to share with you information about the annual event that redditgifts spends on the eve of Christmas - Secret Santa . According to the wiki, Secret Santa is a Christmas ceremony for the anonymous exchange of gifts by a group of people.

As indicated above, in the definition, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Secret Santa is very simple - each participant of the ceremony gives a gift to another participant. All this is absolutely free and redditgifts take no payment from the participants.
