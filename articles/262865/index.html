Virusday Launches Social Experiment



Does every paid service have to have a trial period of use? Such that all functionality without restrictions was given for a day, week, or month, and only then offered to pay?

Opinions are divided. We have a free fare, but its functionality is limited. Some users advise not to change anything, the other part is to introduce a trial period with full functionality. Proponents of the "free trial" claim that they are ready to switch to paid protection services even after they use the free site treatment function. We understand those who consider it necessary to try a new service in full before it is fully used. To make sure that webmasters are right, we publicly launch a social experiment and temporarily enter a free trial period.

The experiment will last a week. At this time, it will be possible to try for free the possibilities of the Virusday service. At the end of the experiment, we will announce the results and see whether it is worthwhile to introduce the "trial" in any form on an ongoing basis. Satisfaction from the first use of the service and change in conversion to regular users will be tracked.

The experiment will last from 07/17/2015 to 07/23/2014 .

For new users:


During the experiment, each new registered user will receive 1 day free use of the Virusday service. The function of highlighting the found code, detailed reports, file disinfection and firewall will be available (expert assistance is not available). The number of sites in the account, as always, is not limited. A day after the user's registration on the service, all sites in his list will automatically be transferred to a free tariff (except for those sites that during this time will be transferred to a paid tariff).

For registered users:


It will be unfair if we do not give the opportunity to participate already registered users. We will give the functions of highlighting the found code, detailed reports, file treatment and firewall for all sites in their accounts that are at a free tariff, for 1 day (expert assistance is not available)

So let's go! Already, everyone can try Virusay service for free.

PS And, of course, the wishes and comments of webmasters on the functionality and operation of the service are important to us.
