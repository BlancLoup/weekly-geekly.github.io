We transfer grades from Imhonet to the Must App



Imhonet closed. And people left just with a sign of the films that they watched. We made it possible to transfer all of your movie scores, which you have been collecting for so long, to our most convenient application for collecting watched Must App movies!

In addition to all the films with your ratings, you can leave comments on movies, comment on reviews of other people, keep a list of movies and TV shows that you have not watched, receive information about the latest trailers, movie releases in cinemas, and much more.

How to transfer your grades?


To get started, visit our website .



Click "Login" and choose a convenient way for you to enter.



Select the address of your page.



Your profile has been created. Now you only have to transfer your grades.



Download your ratings from Imhonet . Click on the link Import Movie Library to Must . And load the file with your ratings by dragging or clicking on the Watched list button (to add films to the list of watched ones).



When the films are loaded, we will inform you about it, and we will write how many films we managed to upload.
