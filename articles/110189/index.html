Our New Year's gift - more disk space for notes and premium invitations



Still, the New Year holidays are a wonderful time. Time of fun feasts, the smell of tangerines and sparklers. But the most important thing: it's time to make gifts. In this regard, we decided to make you a special gift, as well as allow our premium users, in turn, to please their loved ones.

More monthly volume for notes!




We have increased the monthly amount of available disk space for notes for all users, both with free and premium accounts:
  • For free users, the volume has grown from 40 to 60 megabytes of new notes monthly.
  • For premium users, the limit has increased from 500 megabytes to 1 gigabyte !

This way you can memorize more notes, more web pages, more photos, more files ... most of all!

What is the monthly volume?

Taking this opportunity, we want to remind you what monthly volume is.

First of all, there is no limit on the total size of your account, only the amount of new content that you can add to Evernote in the current billing month is limited (for each user it starts on a certain day and can be viewed in the account properties). You start each settlement month with 60 megabytes or 1 gigabyte in stock, depending on the type of your account. Anything new that you add to your account within a month, whether creating a new note or changing an existing one, subtracts the corresponding amount from the balance of this monthly limit.

Deleting content does not restore the value to the beginning of the month. On the first day of the new billing month, this value is in any case reset and you get your 60 megabytes or 1 gigabyte again.

Do not forget that if you decide to switch from a premium account to a free one, you will not lose any of your notes.
