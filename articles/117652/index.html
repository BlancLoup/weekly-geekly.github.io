How to promote the game in the AppStore: sharing experience on the example of Wordrive

I want to share my experience in promoting my new game for iPhone / iPad - Wordrive .

The article can be useful not only for promoting iPhone applications, but of any mobile or application software, because some of the tips are quite universal. Much of what I described here I have not found anywhere on the net. I hope this helps to pass this stage without common mistakes.

So, here you have an application ready and it appeared in the AppStore. The first stream of people to it is sent from the iTunes / App Store, by itself - due to the appearance in the "updates". There is nothing to rely on him for - he both came and will leave in a couple of days. During this time, you need to do everything to start writing about the program or game on the network. If the program during this time can also collect an Internet audience, then it will be much easier to go to the tops. And to collect it, at the time of launch in the App Store you need to do a lot already.


To further it is a little clearer than I illustrate tips, a few words about the game itself.

This is a Word puzzle, a puzzle, but at the same time abundantly filled with humor. You need to collect phrases like " Learn to cook soup with meatballs." First you need to clear the dumplings from the peel ... "or" If you really love, then such a woman so that she would not be ashamed to appear in the eyes of his wife "," Reality is not necessary to collect the phrase in a foreign language the language is also useful - in addition to the pleasant feeling of having understood the untranslatable wordplay, you practice, remember the rules of grammar, which articles are put before what, the word order in the sentence, and so on, which is very well absorbed in the game form.



In order for the audience of the game and the reviewers to be happy, even at the development stage I noted for myself the main points:
  • There must be a fan in the game! For mobile phones, the software should be either very useful or entertaining. Everything between - somehow does not go. Humor is one of the ‚Äúinexpensive‚Äù but effective ways to influence emotions.   • Compatible with the entire Apple-iOS product line . IPad, iPhone 4 and iPhone 3G have a different distribution, and for each Wordrive released its own graphics.   • professional design . Very many developers decide on the design issue on their own. For games, it seems so impossible, but rare instances make their way through excellent gameplay. I decided not to save on this issue, and found a designer who specializes in game design for iPhones.
  • original soundtrack . This "trick" is also inexpensive, and gives both the program and reviews additional advantages. I can't listen to this soundtrack - I tested the game for several months every day, but people like it, yes.

Press kit



To promote any product you need to provide the press with high-quality material. Good resolution logo, soundtrack, videos, press releases, application icons in different resolutions. If the target market is the whole world, then the screenshots, the video, and the press releases should be translated into the main languages.
