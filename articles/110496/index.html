Devices of the year



Mobile computing

In general, the popularity of portable devices can be called this year a global trend.



Note, of course, the Galaxy Tab tablet (on Android). During his presentation on the Russian market, Anatoly Wasserman tested it. Smartphones

In the past year, we launched many smartphones on the market, including flagships such as the Galaxy S and Wave.
