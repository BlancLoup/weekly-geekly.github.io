What courses would you like to see in the online training program from Mail.Ru Group?



Over the past year and a half, we regularly post videos of courses from our educational projects - Technopark and Technosphere Mail.Ru to the network, and since February this year we have also posted them in our blog on Habr√©. Methods of testing web applications , in-depth programming in Java , an introduction to Hadoop , methods for building information retrieval systems are just a few examples of the latest topics that we offered everyone to read online.

During this time, we have received from you a lot of advantages, positive reviews, constructive suggestions, and most importantly - about 6,000 (yes, yes, it's true!) Requests to turn the whole story into full-fledged online courses. Now we are actively reflecting on the possibility of implementing such a project.
