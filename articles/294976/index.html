Now Gett is developing new service niches for consumers - from food delivery and manicure to major repairs.

"Megamind" leads the most interesting quotes from the interview .

We implemented an idea that excited the minds of our team. About work plans


Naturally, there are more potential categories than we are able to do now. But to release a new segment of services once a month is a good plan, I assure you. First of all it concerns transport. Our goal is to be an alternative means of transportation for your personal car. Transport will remain the fundamental direction of the company's business: we are talking about expanding the range of car classes, optimizing prices and maintaining high earnings from drivers.

With food - the idea to ensure that the delivery of pizza or sushi took 10 minutes. Some do not believe that this is possible. But the same thing used to be said about our taxi services. On the demand for taxi services


One of the most interesting moments is that the format of using a taxi has changed fundamentally. A person who registered with Gett first makes X trips per month, after three months - 2X, after 12 months - 4X trips. Just think: when a person understands the benefits of the Gett service, discovers this service for himself, understands the comfort that it provides, he begins to use the service more often and this eventually expands the taxi market. The number of jobs increased in proportion to all these factors.

About competitors in the global market


Our transport service is used in Russia, the UK and New York. But the service is, in fact, local, and if the company does not have 10,000 cars, such as Gett in Moscow, but only 1,000 cars, it is impossible to provide the market with its services, no matter how beautiful the idea may be: with a small number of cars, any delivery of the car will still take another time. All markets are important and equivalent. Two markets - Russia and the UK - the largest in Europe.

On launch in New York


The New York taxi market makes up half of America's taxi market, but it is not competitive. Only one company works there, and the monopolist, as is known, usually maximizes profits and does not think about the quality or competitiveness of its services. Entering such a market is usually easy by offering a more profitable service.

At the same time, half of New York's taxi market is demanded by corporations. Gett is known there precisely because of the best product for corporations (b2b). Half of the Fortune 500 are our customers, and we have achieved this in three years. In the spring, we launched the b2b segment in New York. We lower the cost of services, increase the efficiency and transparency of transport services, and companies really appreciate it. Believe, 2500 b2b clients in the corporate world is a good result.
