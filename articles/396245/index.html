Sony Xperia XA Ultra Now Available

Today, sales of the frameless Sony Xperia XA Ultra smartphone officially start in Russia. The device is available at a price of 27,990 rubles in the company's online store Sony Store Online , as well as in the network of Sony Center and partner stores.



Let me remind you, Sony Xperia XA Ultra is equipped with a frameless 2.5D-display with a diagonal of 6 inches and smoothly rounded edges. The smartphone is presented in three colors, namely in white, graphite black and in a new color golden lime.



Inside is an eight-core 64-bit MediaTek MT6755 processor, as well as 3 GB of RAM. The 2700 mAh battery provides about two days of battery life. And if you urgently need to charge the phone, the UCH-12 with PumpExpress fast charging technology comes to the rescue. The amount of internal memory is 16 GB, the available space is expanded using microSD-cards with a capacity of up to 200 GB.



The front-facing camera is equipped with a 16-megapixel sensor and high-aperture optics, so selfies go well both in the daylight and at night. The main camera is not inferior - it has a sensor with a resolution of 21.5 megapixels, and is also equipped with a predictive hybrid autofocus, which allows you to focus on just 0.03 seconds.

Well, in the near future we will prepare a detailed review of the Xperia XA Ultra. Do not switch!
