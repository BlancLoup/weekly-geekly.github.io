SMS scammers get to discount coupons

Discount coupons are becoming more and more popular, more and more people are starting to use them and as a result, an increasing number of entrepreneurs want to make money on it.
Someone is trying to work honestly, rushes into this difficult race by opening another discount site and starting to attract traffic using well-known methods.

And some are trying to openly deceive gullible users.
Surely, many people remember: SMS direction finders, Vkontakte / ICQ interceptors, mobile spies, etc.
Such sites offered to download programs that supposedly allow reading other people's SMS-messages, correspondence in ICQ or Vkontakte, but in reality this turned out to be nothing more than an entertaining game. To receive the program, it was proposed to send a specific SMS message to a short text number.
