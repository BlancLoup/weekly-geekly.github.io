Days of field testing technology iBeacon in Luzhniki

For three days, from this Thursday to Saturday, we (euroshow and voximplant with the support of ibecom) will organize a first-of-its-kind event for developers. In preparation for the Saturday concert, we bury, hang on trees and install more than 100 iBeacon beacons - and give you the opportunity to participate in debugging your software in this area! Well, or just walk around with iBeacon-compatible programs, chat with our engineers and look at the construction of the scene. Details under the cut.



We conduct such large-scale testing primarily for ourselves. Our goal is to understand how iBeacon beacons work in difficult conditions: over large areas, surrounded by metal structures, with a large gathering of people at concerts. The technology is young, and besides using it to make money, we are interested in promoting it and forming a community of developers.
