Earn on your hobby!

Anna Gradil, Partner Relationship Manager

A few years ago, American makeup artist Michelle Fan opened her own YouTube channel, where she began uploading makeup tutorials. In 2008, Michelle applied for the YouTube Partner Program. After her approval, the girl began to receive revenue from displaying advertising on her channel, whose audience continued to grow. Thanks to this source of income, Michelle was able to support her entire family, moved to Los Angeles and received an offer from Lanc√¥me to become a company representative on the Internet. And this is just one of many success stories for YouTube users who have become affiliates.

We are pleased to announce that the expanded YouTube affiliate program is now available for the Russian video hosting audience. Thanks to the additional tools of the program you will make even better and more interesting videos. And - best of all - you can earn revenue from displaying advertisements placed inside, on top of or next to your videos. According to our data, in 2011, the number of partners who earn more than $ 1000 a month on YouTube has doubled!

Already want to become a partner? Pay attention to the minimum set of content requirements. He must:
  • belong to the user himself, that is, to you. In other words, you must have copyright to it. This requirement applies to both audio and video.
  • comply with the YouTube Terms of Use and Community Guidelines
  • regularly updated
  • be popular

Agree, nothing complicated! Participation in the affiliate program provides a number of advantages. You will have the opportunity to:
  • Download high-quality video (up to Full HD 1080p) with an aspect ratio of 16: 9
  • Add videos to YouTube with no limit on file length or file size.
  • use additional design options for channels and much more.


And how to earn income, you ask. Everything is very simple! YouTube places ads on the channels of affiliate users, and you yourself decide which videos to monetize and which videos to leave without ads. Most of the profits from the display of advertisements will be listed to you as the author. You can also choose which ad format you want to see on your own channel.

To become a member of the enhanced version of the YouTube partner program, you just need to fill out a form on the site and get confirmation. In this case, placing videos on video hosting will be for you not just a hobby, but also an additional source of income. Join 30,000 YouTube partners from 27 countries and earn money on your videos!
