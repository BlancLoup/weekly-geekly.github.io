We measure the power consumption of household appliances

Hello colleagues, I want to tell you about my little experience in the real measurement of the power of home appliances and your computer.

Preamble
After billing me with large electricity bills, I decided to audit my electrical appliances and check which appliance actually consumes how much and how much it will cost me. As a result, I decided to purchase a local (i.e. plug-in device, rather than an electrical box) a kWh counting device.

Device selection
What is sold in the shops of my city, to put it mildly, did not suit me at all for the price (from 1k rubles) and even by functionality, therefore I bought something from the Chinese for the EU Version Power Balance Energy Meter for just 600r. A little about the device. The device allows you to measure power from 1 W to 3 kW, stores information about the electricity consumption during the session and for all the time, considers the cost of consumed electricity at a given price of kWh, shows the mains voltage, there is a bunch of alarms for exceeding any threshold. Overall very comfortable.


We measure everything and everyone

Other electrical appliances
Pomeryv comp, set out to measure all electrical appliances in the house. The results surprised. ALL devices that I measured, it turns out, have a power less than that declared by the manufacturer by about 20%! Robbery gentlemen! Were measured at full power: iron, washing machine, microwave, vacuum cleaner.
The only electrical appliance that matched the declared capacity was the 1978 samovar of the year of release. Its heating element on the passport 1kW, in fact 950W.
As a joke, I measured the cost of washing 15 pairs of socks (at the same time) in a washing machine. Out 2 rub.
