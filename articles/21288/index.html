Weborama asks for advice


Weborama is a young, actively developing social network of music and photos. The basic principle that guides us is continuous improvement. Before launching the project, we arranged closed testing, the main purpose of which was to get users' opinions about the service. Based on the information received, we have compiled a list of top-priority changes and implemented them.

And now it is time to upgrade once again. We initially focused on honest, open relations with our users and that is why we want to ask the opinion of habra people regarding the direction in which the Web Frame will change. For this, we have compiled a small survey and want to ask you to take part in it. And we, on the basis of your answers, will try to make it so that the WebMoney becomes even more convenient. Thank you in advance!
