Microsoft's Blue Mouse Sales Announced



The Microsoft SideWinder X8 mouse announced in the fall has finally reached the shelves. The rodent, of course, has never been blue, but a completely red color, but this is not the point. Let me remind you that this model is remarkable in that it uses the BlueTrack technology: instead of a laser, a blue LED sensor is used for positioning.

The sensor has a resolution of 250 to 4000 dpi, while it is able to capture data 13,000 times per second. In addition, the sensitivity of the mouse is maintained at the speed of its movement up to 3 m / s. On the case there are 12 buttons, 7 of which are programmable.

The Microsoft SideWinder X8 Lithium-ion battery lasts for 30 hours of battery life. The mouse on the USB cable is charged, while you can work with it while charging. In addition, the device is equipped with interchangeable soles. Their player can change depending on the surface on which the mouse goes.

The mouse is about to appear on sale in American stores for about $ 100.
