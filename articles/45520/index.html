Bajca - keyboard with a smile



Somehow it is even strange that nothing has been written about this gadget on Habr√©.
Fill the gap. Additional USB-keyboard Bajca - this is one of the most intelligent children's pieces that I recently came across. The developer of the toy is Italian studio Adriano Design . The chip of the device is that it can be used both for its intended purpose: for simplified drawing of smiles, and as a decoration. Smiley buttons are made in such a way that they can be easily reached and put together into beads or a bracelet. And you can also make rings, necklaces and so on.

















In short, if you have a young lady in charge and are mentally prepared, the fact that smiling balls will be scattered throughout the apartment - you can safely prepare for the purchase. The production of Bajca in the near future should be taken by the Italian company Progind. The price is still unknown, but it is unlikely to be high.
