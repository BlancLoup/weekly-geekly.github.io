iPhone 3.0



Apple iPhone 3G is already out, the revolution is over - a smooth evolutionary process has begun to upgrade the standard iPhone. Or is it just an illusion?

A certain artist came up with a new, but very real concept of the device, codenamed iPhone 3.0. The device is equipped with a transparent case and a display (yes, the designers seriously perceived the actions of Apple, which submitted a patent application for transparent displays in mobile devices), support for third-generation networks (and now without it) and A-GPS, full-featured Mac OS X 10.6 Snow Leopard, as well as 64 gigabytes of internal memory.

Beautiful, isn't it?
