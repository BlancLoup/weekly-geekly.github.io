Due to EU and US sanctions, Apple has once again banned Russian iOS developers from withdrawing money to Sberbank

On Saturday, August 29, Apple offered Russian developers of applications for OS X and iOS to change payment details. They will no longer be able to transfer affiliate payments to accounts in Sberbank . Such a decision of the company is connected with the sanctions of the European Commission and the administration of the US president. According to their requirements for European and American companies, remittances to accounts in Sberbank of Russia are prohibited. The relevant US and EU regulations were adopted in mid-December 2014.
