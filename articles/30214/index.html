DVDConverter - DVD Video to DIVX Converter


Hello habrozhiteli!
I wanted to tell you about my program for converting DVD to AVI.
First I want to tell you what made me do this program ... Once I was given (for a while) a bunch of DVDs that contained concerts of my favorite band Muse, which I could not save! By that time, my hard disk did not allow me to write all these disks. Plus, I didn't have a DVD-RW drive either. I decided that I needed to quickly overtake this whole thing in a simple video format (DIVX, MPG4), in connection with which I began to look for software for this business.
Required simplicity and speed of the program.
Having tried a bunch ( Auto Gordian Knot , SimpleDivx , ImTOO AVI to DVD Converter , etc.), it turned out that some converters are slow, others do not have the opportunity to select the desired video interval (let's say one song from the concert), in the third you need to pay money.
But having a little settled in the program SimpleDivx, I found out that it works through Mencoder . With this encoder, you can perform many tasks , which I did.
Unfortunately, Mencoder works only from the command line, which is why I decided to make my DVDConverter program.
I myself of course cannot call myself a programmer, but still the program works and I think it deserves attention :)
The main parameters of the program:
1. Convert DVD video to simple AVI file (DIVX format);
1. Convert compressed (MPG4, DIVX) video to a simple AVI file (DIVX format);
2. Convert images to a simple AVI file (DIVX format);
3. Convert DVD video to one VOB file;

In general, you can evaluate all the features of this program yourself by downloading it at http://ffasoft.ru/dvdconverter
