Is it easy for a person with a disability to find a job in IT

Just over a year ago, we wrote that we started marking vacancies for people with disabilities on hh.ru. With general approval of the initiative, there was also criticism, which boiled down to the fact that vacancies and without special markings should be accessible to everyone. In an ideal world, it probably is, but, unfortunately, ours is not like this, but everyone would like to have a job for the soul.
Time has shown that we have begun the right thing: from November 2012, 49,684 vacancies received such marking, real examples of employment appeared.

IT is the first area today for the number of vacancies available for people with disabilities - 4,050 vacancies, or every tenth of the total number in IT. With that, in the middle of last year, it was only in third place after sales and media.
The growth in the last month of explosive - 375%!
