Announcement Forum Dell EMC Forum 2016



Friends, we invite everyone who is interested in the development of the digital economy, its influence on our life and work, to visit the Dell EMC Forum 2016, which will be held in Moscow on November 9, 2016. Details - under the cut.

At the forum, we will discuss the enormous changes that have occurred and occur around us, due to the growing volume of data, unprecedented connectivity and changing requirements for workplaces that lead to the need for a radical IT modernization.

During the day, you are waiting for an interactive exchange of knowledge, discussions and demonstrations, communication with experts and colleagues. You can gain a deep understanding of the technologies, ways of thinking and ways of providing IT, which will be the driving force in the new digital era.

After registration, you can create your personal Forum program by selecting the sessions that interest you:
