General remarks
All motors weigh about 40 grams, which is the minimum figure for this class.

Components of the motor depends on the manufacturer. You are guaranteed to receive a full set of screws from RCTimer and DYS.

Usually suspensions and motors are designed for 9-12 volts (3s). Higher voltages / currents can easily melt the windings. RCTimer BGM2804 accurately withstands 15 volts (4s), for the rest of the motors it is better to programmatically limit the voltage.
