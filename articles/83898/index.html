The first Blu-ray 3D player

Sony announced the player for Blu-ray 3D discs, and also announced the upgrade of the firmware of existing players, so that they will also be able to play three-dimensional high-definition discs.

The new Sony BDP-S470, like other players after flashing, can play discs recorded in the new Blu-ray 3D standard, which the Blu-ray Association adopted two months ago. With support for WiFi and USB, the Sony BDP-S470 player will cost about $ 200.

As you can see, the IT industry is experiencing a real revolution after the release of the film "Avatar". Now turn for television. By the way, the same Sony has already announced a fully three-dimensional broadcast of all the matches of the 2010 World Cup in South Africa.
