New smart watches from Pebble. Thin, light and round



Today, Pebble has announced a new smart watch with a round metal case and a round color display. Otherwise, it's all the same good old Pebble - constantly active screen and a few days without recharging. Novelty called Pebble Time Round . The clock can already be ordered at a price of $ 250, shipping will begin in 6-8 weeks. There is still no direct delivery to Russia, but Banderolka promises to send this watch for $ 10 per share .


Three body colors are available - silver, gold and black, as well as five options for leather straps. Later metal bracelets will be on sale, although apparently the standard straps and bracelets of 14 or 20 mm width will fit the watch (there are case variants for different widths).

The thickness of the watch is only 7.5 millimeters, and the weight is 28 grams. Today it is the lightest and thinnest smart watch in the world. The resolution of the round display is 180x180 pixels. The remaining hardware characteristics do not differ from the previous generation - a processor with a frequency of 100 megahertz, an accelerometer, a compass, a microphone and four hardware buttons. It supports pairing with a smartphone on iOS or Android.

For those who financed the previous model Kickstarter, Pebble Time Steel, there is an interesting offer. New watches they can buy until November 8 at a discount of $ 50 ( but there are nuances ). After the purchase, a month is given for testing and comparing two hours, and then one of the models can be returned and returned. Alternatively, you can keep both models.

The SDK for the new hardware platform called Chalk is already available, developers can begin to round their dials and applications.
