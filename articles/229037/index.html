LG G Watch became available for purchase on Google Play

... and at the same time in the "offline" around the world. According to LG, you can purchase smart watches both in the store and on the Internet in twelve countries: USA, Canada, France, Ireland, Germany, Italy, Spain, Great Britain, Australia, India, Japan and South Korea. In another fifteen countries, the model is now available only from retailers; among others, Russia, Brazil, Mexico, Singapore and New Zealand are named.



Earlier, on July 1, it was reported that the LG G Watch would become available in Russia in parallel with the LG G3, and it would be possible to purchase them at least in the Svyaznoy stores. At the moment, in the online store "smart watches" are present with the note "The product will soon go on sale," the price is 7 990 rubles (~ $ 228). If you believe GSMArena.com, in the US, LG G Watch sold at a similar price - which is good news.



Let me remind you, LG G Watch has the following characteristics:

  • Chipset: Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 (1.2 GHz)
  • Screen: 1.65 inches, LCD IPS, 280 x 280
  • Memory: 4 GB ROM / 512 MB RAM
  • Battery: 400 mAh
  • Operating system: Android Wear (compatible with devices on Android 4.3 and above)
  • Dimensions: 37.9 x 46.5 x 9.95 mm
  • Weight: 63 grams
  • Wireless connection: Bluetooth 4.0
  • Sensors: gyroscope, accelerometer, compass
  • Color: titanium black or white gold
