Toshiba Netbook


In November of this year, Toshiba is preparing to submit its version of an ultra-portable laptop, if someone likes it more, then the netbook is the Toshiba Satellite NB105. This miracle of hostile technology will be based on the Intel Atom 1,6Ghz. It is equipped with a miracle, 1 gigabyte of RAM, 120 gigabyte hdd, ethernet, wi-fi (of course), 3 usb ports and a slot for sd card. Screen 8'9 inches with a resolution of 1024 * 600. About the capacity of the battery, unfortunately, is not reported. The presentation will take place in Mexico, where the Satellite NB105 is going to sell for $ 550. It is unpleasant that sales in Russia are not reported! For me, this is doubly unpleasant, I have a good relationship with Toshiba laptops, so I really want this unit to go to Moscow so that I can easily get it!
