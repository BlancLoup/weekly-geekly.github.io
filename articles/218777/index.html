The personnel policy of the data center is laid during the design

After the publication of reports on the construction and internal structure of the DataPro data center in Tver, we received a large number of personnel questions that are involved in the facility. Alexey Soldatov, General Director of DataPro, told about the principles by which employees were selected, and what key factors influenced decision making on this issue.

- You recently opened a new data center certified for Tier III fault tolerance in Tver. There were features when hiring workers for such an object in the region, and if so, which ones?

- Requirements for data center personnel are largely determined at design time. At the stage of technological design, we realized that we need to build a data center as simple and reliable in terms of maintenance of engineering infrastructure. Top management will not always be able to personally be present and warn of possible mistakes, so we sought to create an object that is less demanding in terms of personnel qualifications and is resistant to possible personnel errors. To this end, we abandoned super-complex circuit switching circuits and created a fairly simple system with a high degree of redundancy. As soon as you make such an infrastructure, then potentially - I emphasize, potentially! - you will need performers who are not as professional as other objects.

We were looking for a compromise for the creation of engineering infrastructure, in order to build a facility using very reliable equipment that would not require daily maintenance staff. This was difficult due to the underdevelopment of the service infrastructure. But this is a common problem for the entire industry in the Russian Federation.

High-quality equipment in some way lowers the potential level of requirements for service personnel. Take, for example, cars. No one is repairing his car right now, and it doesn't break. Although 70 years ago, cars were like a DIY designer. The same approach was to the maintenance of civilian systems.

But in the military structures this approach was not applied. It was believed, ‚Äúif tomorrow is a war,‚Äù then the qualification of a recruit who hasn‚Äôt seen a weapon for 10 years will not be enough to repair and replace modules. The architecture of the data center was built on the same principle.
This increases costs at the stage of capital construction, but, on the other hand, it requires fewer attendants. This approach is often used in the West. At DataPro, we strive to come closer to Western experience due to the modularity of the design and the standardization of solutions.

- How did you collect the staff in the Tver data center? In your opinion, when opening a modern data center in the region, do you need to take specialists from Moscow or search locally?

- Moscow is a big city, it, like a pump, draws people from all over Russia into it. Or even the entire former Soviet Union. Perhaps this is the wrong approach - to build an object in the region and take people from Moscow. In principle, on the ground, you can find a specialist for any position, but the question arises with payment.
Based on the experience of operating such systems, which are owned by my deputies in IT and production, staff requirements were developed and an attempt was made to hire specialists in Tver. According to the investment agreement with the authorities of the Tver region, we were obliged to create a certain number of jobs for residents of the region. And we created them.
First of all, we filled vacancies for electrical engineering specialties. Were published ads in newspapers and the Internet - on such portals, such as Headhunter.ru. And although the specialists we were looking for to maintain the infrastructure do not have the habit of sitting on Twitter and Facebook, we found such people. Not to say that in a week, but within a reasonable time.

Our substation and generators are imported, the best models: they practically do not break. Uninterruptible power supplies are simple in terms of operation, and complicated in terms of repairs. But we did not plan to engage in repairs on our own.
An obligatory requirement is an experience as an electrician, because our service is a high-voltage substation of medium voltage. The fourth and fifth groups of electrical safety tolerance are required. Need to take the exam in Rostekhnadzor. The service team worked for two weeks - and went home. At this time, another shift arrives.
