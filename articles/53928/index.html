Doshirak: Project One. What is Confiture and what is it eaten with?



Three months ago, we announced our retirement , promising to do three projects in six months. Today we want to present the first of our projects at Habrahabr.

Confiture is a program for backing up and restoring files. We took a boring and routine procedure and made a service that is simple and fun to use. Files added to Confiture can be recovered in months and even years. At the same time, you do not need to buy additional hard drives for data storage - just an Internet connection is enough. All files are stored on Amazon S3 servers.

The creeping tape of time, the whole structure of your files on any of the selected days, the collapsing panels, the addition of files with one click of the mouse is just a must see.



Everything is easy and clear - these are your files; yes, they are saved; Yes, they are on the server; Yes, they can be restored. Confiture saves both files and folders selected by the user, as well as entire groups - documents, photos, music, videos.
Download Confiture from offsite or direct download link . The free plan includes 100 megabytes of server space.

Confiture is almost the only online backup system released for the Russian-speaking market. For some regions, the cost of unlimited access is still too high, but this situation is constantly improving. We believe that after a while online backup will become the mainstream for home users. The internal implementation of the server allows you to easily change the billing system and the file storage server, replacing them, for example, with similar provider systems.

After registration, do not forget to look into the "Spam" folder;)
