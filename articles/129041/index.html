EBay.ru duty calculator

On eBay.ru (a simplified version of the service, which gives access only to goods sold at a fixed price and with delivery to Russia, as well as a local bulletin board), a tariff calculator has appeared.

Now, next to the price of the goods and the cost of delivering them to Russia, the estimated amount of the customs duty that you have to pay is displayed so that when you buy a product you can immediately estimate all the costs:



The amount of duty is calculated on the basis of the assumption that this is your first product received from abroad this month and is based solely on the price of the product (not taking into account its weight / size). If you have already made other purchases from abroad this month, you can follow the link and enter the amount of your previous purchases to recalculate the amount of the duty (as you know, you are not subject to a duty of buying a private person up to 1000 euros per month):



The customs duty calculator is provided by eBay.ru by Bundletech .
