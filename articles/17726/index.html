Nielsen F-pattern when reading web pages

How do users' views move when browsing web pages? It turns out that the trajectory of the movement of the look on the page resembles the letter F.

The visualization of the trajectory of the movement of the sight shows that when
reading the contents of web pages most users view the page
so that the movement of their views resembles the letter F: first, they run over the eyes
two lines horizontally, then the look moves in a vertical
direction.
