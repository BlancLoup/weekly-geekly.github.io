The new bill on VAT for IT-companies: the views of politicians, analysts and businessmen



Today, December 24, a bill was submitted to the State Duma (we also wrote about it earlier), according to which e-services that provide foreign and domestic companies will be subject to VAT. According to the original plan of the creators of the bill, it should come into force in a year - January 1, 2017.
