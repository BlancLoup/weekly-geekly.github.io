Canon EOS 5D Mark III. First look

Recently, habrachelovek mclander thinking about the characteristics of the Canon 5D Mark III. And so it happened! Very soon, the veil of secrecy of this camera will open officially, but for now we will look out from under the floors (and see how good the seer is sitting in mclander).





Specs


  • 22.3 megapixel full frame CMOS sensor
  • New processor DIGIC 5+
  • ISO 100-25 600 (expandable up to 50-102 400)
  • 61-point autofocus
  • Shooting speed 6 frames per second
  • 63-zone iFCL sensor metering
  • Record Full HD video (1080p) in the range of ISO 100-12 800 (expandable up to 25,600)
  • HDR Mode - create HDR from three shots directly in the camera; 5 preset styles (nothing is said about user styles)
  • Intelligent viewfinder (whatever that means) with 100% coverage and 0.71x magnification
  • Display 3.2 '' with a resolution of 1,040,000 pixels
  • Two slots for memory cards: CompactFlash and SD


New accessories


Also became known information about the new Speedlight 600EX and Speedlight transmitter ST-E3-RT. Below are their specifications.

Speedlite 600EX-RT:
  • New system with multiple wireless flashes
  • The enlarged flash head covers a wide range from 20 to 200 mm (maximum 60 meters at ISO 100)
  • 18 preset modes and 3 user modes

Speedlite Transmitter ST-E3-RT Flash Transmitter (Speedlite Transmitter ST-E3-RT)
  • New 2-way radio wave communication provides better synchronization between master and flash unit
  • The transmission distance reaches 30 meters at 360 degrees
  • Up to 15 flashes can be controlled through 1 transmitter.
  • Supports E-TTL II, manual and stroboscopic mode, as well as external metering
