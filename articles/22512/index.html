Innovations for social networks

Many web 2.0 services implemented separately can be introduced as an extension to the basic functionality that social networking sites already possess. And the usefulness and relevance of a service can be easily judged by statistics.
It is difficult to gather people in one place, so that millions use one site, but it is even more difficult for each individual person to spend his time and attention on using many services with one single functionality.
Everywhere you need to register an account, enter personal data, prepay what in speed may become unnecessary. And on a single site for a whole package of services and it is not a pity to pay - something, yes, like it.
Here is a vision of the future development of social networks.

To date, they still serve only to make new acquaintances and maintain old ones. I would call this a convenient e-mail interface. Mail is by nature created for personal correspondence. No matter how many contacts you have, and no matter how much you want to communicate in a group, everyone still needs to send a personal letter and preferably with an address by name :) Only those who already know you will read your letter or, at a glance spam And on the site, each has its own page, which people view at will and when it is convenient for them.
