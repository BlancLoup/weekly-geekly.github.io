Nokia 105 for 15 euros with a flashlight and a 800 mAh battery

At the MWC 2013, Nokia introduced two new phones: Nokia 301 and Nokia 105


Nokia 301 (left), Nokia 105 (right)

Nokia 301


  • Price ~ 65 Euro
  • 2.4 "display
  • 3.2 megapixel camera
  • Two SIM card slots
  • 3.5G network support


Nokia 105


  • Price ~ 15 Euro
  • Color screen
  • FM radio
  • Torch
  • Increased resistance to moisture and dust
  • Body Color: Black, Blue
  • The standby battery life is about 35 days.
