Opera browser is even safer

Today's press release from Opera Software announces a new collaboration that will enhance the safety of users on the Internet. At this time, a partnership agreement was concluded with the American company Haute Secure, which is one of the leading developers of network defense systems. Cooperation with Opera Software means including in the upcoming version of Opera-9.5, in addition to protecting against fake sites, a mechanism for detecting and eliminating so-called malware - this generic name usually refers to all types of malware, ranging from viruses to trojans and rootkits. To put it simply, starting with the version of Opera-9.5, the built-in antivirus for a wide profile will appear in this browser. However, you should not think that the size of Opera will increase significantly because of this: a feature of the technology developed by Haute Secure is that the browser adds only detection and identification tools, and the entire malware base is online and constantly updated with recent entries from millions of users and companies developing antivirus software around the world. At the moment, the client base of Haute Secure is about 20 million users, and starting with Opera-9.5, all owners of the new browser version will be added to them.
