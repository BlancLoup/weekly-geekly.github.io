The most important thing


Finally, we come to the main function of LotCat, for which the most difficult work was done on writing this post.

Suppose you created an application for the Android platform and placed it on the Google Play Market. A link from the Play Market was added to LotCat and received a short URL and a QR code. Now you place them in advertising, on sites, in reviews, etc. If the link comes to you a user with an Android device - he downloads the application and is happy immensely.
