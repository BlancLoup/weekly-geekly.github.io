Tomorrow, April 23, 2016 - see the online broadcast of the Russian final of the Imagine Cup

You have probably already heard that this Saturday will be the final of the student projects competition of the Imagine Cup . Starting from 9:30 Moscow time on the website http://imaginecup.ru and on Channel 9 , an online broadcast of the event will be available. Also at 13:00 you will be able to see an exclusive live broadcast from the exhibition of projects in our facebook group . For my part I promise secret inclusions from behind the scenes in the periscope .

We will be waiting for your questions and comments in the hash tag #imcup !



According to reviews from last year, we will broadcast the speeches of all teams in all categories, as well as lectures. Unfortunately, the format of the master classes does not imply broadcast, although many interesting things will take place there. Details - in the program . Muscovites are not too late to join!

What we definitely recommend to watch (in the broadcast from the second hall):
  • 14:40 - lecture by Andrei Egorov , director of the Open University Skolkovo - The future has a present . Andrew is also a representative of the University of Singularity , so it will be very interesting to hear the forecast of the future of technology from his mouth.
  • 15:20 - Lecture by Dmitry Pavlyuchenkov , Deputy Director of the Internet Initiatives Development Fund . The lecture will tell you what qualities an entrepreneur needs and how to start making a business out of your project.
  • 16:30 - Eugene Romin , the creator of Brainy Studio and the winner of the Imagine Cup 2014, will share the secrets of winning the competition and the experience of further developing the project after the competition.

Also at 4:30 pm in the first room, I will talk about why interest in artificial intelligence is now rising again, and which Microsoft AI products can easily be used in my projects. Including, I will show you how to create simple chat bots using the recently announced Bot Framework .

Come to us online or offline, and invite your friends to the event (the easiest way to do this is to use the VKontakte group )!
