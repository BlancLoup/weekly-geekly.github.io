iGlover.ru - we solve the touchscreen problem in the cold

Hello, Habramir!

If you are the owner of a smartphone (or any other device) with a touch screen, then you are surely faced with the fact that its display does not perceive the touch in gloves. In order for a touch to be noticed by a device, contact with a conductive object (for example, a finger ) is necessary. That is the technology .



Of course, this problem was decided not so long ago - they created a conductive fabric and began to use it in the manufacture of gloves. In the United States and Europe, such a thing will surprise no one. But in the expanses of our country to find such an accessory was a problem.

A group of comrades and I decided to correct this injustice and created iGlover.ru , a store of specialized gloves for touch phones.

So, now you can easily write sms and surf the Internet on a cold street. The cost of one pair - 699 p. There are 4 colors. I hope this news will be useful to you, and many fingers will be saved from the March frosts :)
