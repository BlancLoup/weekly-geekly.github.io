Music search service Vpleer.ru back in service?

Immediately I want to disappoint - not yet, but ...

Vpleer's problems (and other similar sites) began with the fact that the well-known social network VKontakte, by its charter from the parasitic services on the music content of its users, decided to introduce protection.

The essence of the protection lies in the fact that if earlier the link to the mp3-file always remained constant (which search engines enjoyed and used), now, after a certain period of time, the file name changes to something like 5a7b9c4d2e5f.mp3.

Admin Vpleer managed to reanimate the ability to search and download music. However, the function of pre-listening directly in the browser, fell the death of the brave (and still lying around). A pity, she really helped to assess the quality of the composition.
