Power push ups

The idea to tell about this simple, but very effective exercise came after reading this topic.

After reading it, I realized that running might be suitable for many. But looking at the puddles, the wind and rain outside the window five days a week, he doubted that it was suitable for everyone.

Therefore, I want to talk about push-ups, than they are better, from my point of view, well-known standard physical exercises (pull-ups, squats), to create physical activity during the day.

Immediately make a reservation that I am not an expert and all of the following is my personal experience and views.

Initial data


8-10 hours at the computer, lack of desire to go somewhere to the gym due to the remoteness of reasonable prices (in an hour's drive - a total of two hours one way in both directions) and the high cost of the subscription being nearby.

Task


Provide physical exertion during the day with the least time and moral effort on oneself, a quick effect - a feeling of muscle tension.
