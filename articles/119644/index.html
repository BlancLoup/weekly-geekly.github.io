Summer School System Analysis - Recruitment

We, Sergey Nuzhnenko and Denis Beskov , announce the recruitment of a summer system analysis school .

What it is
This is a basic course of applied systems analysis in information technology, which we will conduct in Moscow in person and for free all summer, 12 weeks, every Sunday, from 12 to 15 o'clock in the afternoon.

The course includes lectures, practical classes, homework assignments and monthly mid-term monitoring. The total volume of the course is from 36 to 100 full-time hours (the program is being specified), intensives are possible (weekly training during working hours, if the schedule of all participants allows it). The average workload per student per week (classes and independent work) is from 5 to 10 hours.

What will the participants gain
  • A clear understanding of what is the subject of labor system analyst and how to distinguish a bad result from a good one.
  • Understanding what knowledge and skills are fundamental for a systems analyst.
  • Understanding the specifics of system analytics in 4 main production contexts (internal development, product development, custom development, system integration and implementation).
  • The most basic skills of planning, executing and managing analytical work.

Who can participate
We will be happy to see young professionals with knowledge of basic concepts from the field of information technology, who consciously and convincingly want to master the profession of systems analyst.

What is the size of the group and the severity of training
We want to recruit about 30 people.
In a month, according to the results of control measures, 15-20 people will remain.
In another month, 7-10 people will remain (and, most likely, will reach the end of the course).

Where will be trained
The site is still being clarified, most likely - a university in the center of Moscow or on Semyonovskaya metro station.

How to sign up
Fill out the form . If you are interested in us, we will send you an additional test and questions.
