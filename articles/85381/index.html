Curious displays - a screen that interacts with the environment

An unusual concept was presented by Julia Tsao, creative director of Los Angelo bureau of Fair Enough, Julia Tsao. This device is a lot of small screens that can move on different surfaces and interact with objects. These small screens can work as reminders to a person if he has forgotten or lost something. They will be able to tell for how long the user has a meeting scheduled or remind you to water a flower.




Each small screen can work both separately and in combination with others, creating large objects of interaction with objects in your room. You want to watch football, and your wife wants to watch figure skating? Curious displays will split your screen in two. Want a screen on the ceiling? He will crawl there. In general, very comfortable and attractive. It is a pity that the project is only under development, and there is no physical concept yet. I would not like it to happen as well as with many other projects that have not been implemented.
