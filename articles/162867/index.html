Ainol Launches Novo 9 Firewire Tablet Computer with Quad-Core Processor and Retina Display for $ 230

A description of the new device, Novo 9 Firewire, appeared on the Ainol website. The novelty is striking not only for its characteristics, but also for $ 230.


The core of the system is the Allwinner A31 single-chip platform, the main elements of which are a quad-core ARM Cortex-A7 CPU and an eight-core PowerVR SGX 544MP2 GPU. The amount of RAM is 2 GB, the amount of internal storage - 16 GB. In addition, the device supports TF up to 32 GB. Ainol Novo 9 Firewire received a 9.7-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 2048 x 1536 pixels. The tablet is equipped with two cameras: front - 0.3Mp, rear - 5Mp.
The device supports the standard already WIFI 802.11 b / g / n. In addition, 3G support is promised via external modems (the list includes E1916, ZTE AC2736, HUAWEI E1750, HUAWEI EC122, HUAWEI EM770W).
External ports: standard 3.5mm headphone jack, MINI HDMI and Micro USB ports with OTG support.
It will all be powered by a battery with a capacity of 10,000 mAh. The work time is promised from 7 to 10 hours.
From the strange, in the characteristics of the GPS is not mentioned at all.
This will all work under Android 4.1.1.
Checkout can be in 5-10 days .
