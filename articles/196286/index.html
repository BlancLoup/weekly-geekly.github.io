Ukrainian crowdfunding or laser pointer and blaster duel



In the past year, I have been fascinated by the study of the phenomenon of crowdfunding and even try to use it for peaceful purposes - I am preparing my own project on Kickstarter, but this is not about it today. I would like to talk about the different approaches of developers to enter this site and what happens. Autumn turned out to be generous for the projects of our neighbors in the CIS - the Ukrainians, despite the fact that this summer Kickstarter owners installed very serious filters for foreigners to access their site!

Literally with a difference of several days, two very high-quality, but at the same time very different Ukrainian projects appeared at Kickstarter, which are very interesting to compare with each other, which prompted me to dig their roots deeper. The results of the research led me to thoughts that I decided to share. Both projects are perfectly decorated and have another similarity - a rather long history, since they were started at about the same time - in the spring of 2012. In both was a huge amount of effort and labor, but the authors are so different and moved in completely opposite ways. Actually, the idea is simple - a device in the form of a cube in which a webcam with a microphone, a speaker, and a laser pointer are mounted.
All this connects to the Internet and thanks to this the owner should be able to not only watch his pet, but also communicate with him through voice and even play a simple game - catch up with the laser bunny.

I will not bore the reader with technical analysis of the gadget and thinking about how successful the choice of case was, I would only note that the uncomplicated idea is compensated by a potentially wide range of potential buyers - even if every thousandth user of the network who views pictures of cats and dogs, will think about buying this accessory, then you can become a millionaire. The only question is to introduce the new product to the widest possible audience. Apparently this is how the author of the cube thought, judging by his actions. The work began to boil over - participation in numerous exhibition events, articles in the world press, design of pages in social networks, communication in forums, corporate identity development, company registration ... In a word, the project resembles a carefully planned and tastefully conducted advertising campaign, the successful start of which was supported by the world press fundraising at the Kickstarter site. $ 100,000 for two days is an excellent result, especially for Ukrainian guys.
