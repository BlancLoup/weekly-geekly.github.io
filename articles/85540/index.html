Rumor: Steam ready for Mac OS X?

The fact that Valve Software is testing the updated Steam is no longer news. However, today, users noticed that among other service resources there are icons, menu snippets, etc., used in Mac OS X. Of course, the community immediately suggested that Valve Software plans to launch Steam and for Apple computers.

This assumption is not groundless. In addition to the presence of design elements, for Mac OS X support by the service, in the library of which so far only games for Windows are available, it is said that Valve partners with quite venerable companies that produce games for Macs. EA, Take-Two Interactive, PopCap Games, id Software, etc. obviously will not give up the expansion of the army of Steam users due to the increasing audience of Apple PC owners. So will it really be known soon.
