Internet is my personal space

It all starts with the fact that a person gets on the Internet. He discovers a huge world of information and communication. His contact list in IM begins to fill with contacts. First is the one who installs the IM client and friends. Then classmates / classmates, then colleagues. In parallel with this, the number of acquaintances, persons of the opposite sex, friends is growing.

A person changes jobs, and colleagues become former colleagues. Finishes school or college, and classmates with classmates also turn into ex. Relations between friends deteriorate, and with the people of the opposite sex is also not always part peacefully. And slowly the invisible-list begins to fill.

Annoying colleagues who are familiar with the forums that you once helped, employers, annoying characters constantly climb with stupid questions, send incomprehensible links or anecdotes, you want to hide from them all, but often people do not even know who they are. So it goes into the invisible mode, or even worse, it fills the visible list and becomes open only to the select.

But there is no salvation! A person will be recognized under the original nickname on new resources, and who knows the real name is able to find him in fashionable social networks.
