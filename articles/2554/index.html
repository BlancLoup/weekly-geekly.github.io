Parked.ru spoke about his future

The DomainBlog site has published an interview with the creators of the recently officially announced its opening of the site Parked.ru . This is a domain parking service that allows you to sell ads without creating your own website.

The developers said that parking and domain sales services will be available to Parked.ru users. The service is adapted for the Runet, although anyone can use the western equivalent - for example, Sedo or Namedrive .

This year, the company will launch all the planned functionality, update attendance statistics, provide more options for visual design of parking pages and so on.
