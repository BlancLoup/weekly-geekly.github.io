Acer updates Timeline



According to preliminary information Acer global update line Timeline. Now it will be called TimelineX, and one of the most interesting models - Acer Timeline 1810T will receive instead of energy efficient, but relatively slow Intel ULV Core2 1.4 GHz more advanced Intel Core i5 with 1.06 GHz and support for Hyper Threading technology, which allows you to get 4 parallel computational process. The declared work time increased to 8 hours.

The rest of the stuffing has practically not changed: the SATA hard disk is 250-500 GB, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, a display with a diagonal of 11.6 inches and a resolution of 1366x768 pixels. Optionally planned module 3G.

The official announcement of Acer TimelineX 1830T and the entire TimelineX line will occur next week. For the price, nothing is known yet, but we can assume that it will not differ much from the starting one for Acer Timeline 1810T. Let me remind you that in July 2009 in the United States, Acer requested $ 711 for the youngest, and 853 for the older model of the line.
