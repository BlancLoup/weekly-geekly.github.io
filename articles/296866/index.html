The new bill will punish the ruble of "unscrupulous" online goods aggregators - expert comments

Rospotrebnadzor has agreed with the business a bill on the protection of customers from the unfair work of online aggregators, placing offers online retailers. Such sites will now be responsible to customers affected by deliberately inaccurate information about the product.

Rospotrebnadzor believes that in the Russian online retailer market there are "unscrupulous" stores and aggregators. They do not disclose information about the seller, manufacturer of products, about the product itself or services. They receive money into the account as intermediaries between sellers and buyers, but if problems arise, it is almost impossible to bring them to justice. In July, the ministry offered to introduce the possibility of blocking such sites and consider Internet retailers as an independent subject of responsibility.

Last year, online stores, in turn, demanded to regulate the work of commodity aggregators - sites that do not sell goods themselves, but pick up and compare offers from different online platforms on request. The final bill takes into account the interests of the aggregators - they will not "bear the duties and responsibility of the seller", this clause was deleted. Aggregators are threatened with financial losses if they post deliberately inaccurate information about a product, service or seller: it is from the aggregators that the affected buyer will have the right to demand damages.
