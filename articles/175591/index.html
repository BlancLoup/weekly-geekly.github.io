HPC. Program, live stream and free tickets

April 25 in St. Petersburg, with the support of the portal IT-Evestir.rf , held High Performance Conference # 4 - a conference on high loads.


The purpose of the conference is to enable professional growth in various areas of the design, development, testing, administration and support of high-loaded systems to its participants.


Abstracts


Badoo, Andrey Sas:


Andrei will talk about the technical side of sending large volumes of mail, specific examples of the implementation of the mail cluster, the system of generating and sending mail, quality metrics and monitoring.

Yandex.Money, Dmitry Melnikov


Dmitry will share real-life experiences, life observations, rakes and other things on the development of mobile applications Yandeks.Deneg.

Aviasales.ru, Anton Shcherbakov


Anton in his report will focus on the architecture and technology of data storage systems designed to maintain the performance of aviasales services, such as a map, a calendar of minimum prices, a search service for the best offers in the last 48 hours, customizable mailing of information about price changes in a direction selected by the user .

JetBrains, Phillip Torchinsky


Phillip will tell you how JetBrains developed their own language and database for the sake of high-performance application performance.

Jelastic, Dmitry Lazarenko


Dmitry will talk about the mechanisms for updating the platform and user environments in Jelastic.
