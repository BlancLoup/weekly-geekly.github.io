Report on the first stage of the Geo-Hakaton

So, on May 24, the first stage of the Geo-Hackathon , organized by EST Technologies with the support of IT Loft , took place .
Recall that at this stage, the participants of the geo-hackathon should have proposed ideas on the use of geo-data provided by the organizers.
The event was attended by 16 teams.

The first place was taken by the team "Dream".


Congratulations to the winners: Andrei Levchenko - author of the idea, Dmitry Avatkin, Andrei Alexandrov, Alexey Samsonov.




As announced, the winning team received a prize - iPad mini Retina 64GB. In addition, all participants received certificates for 1000 rubles for trips to the taxi ECT.

Separately, teams and participants were noted with the following ideas:


  • Vyacheslav Vinogradov (Geo-quests and the EST bonus system);
  • SiberianCats (Economically calculated individual fare for each taxi driver based on statistics and geo-data);
  • CAT_IN_THE_DARK_WOOD (Automated system for collecting information about traffic rules);
  • Angry beards (Predictions taxi orders based on data from social networks);
  • Evgeny Tarasenko (Integration of the driver and client taxi applications with the bus station, airport, railway);
  • Green Light (Forecasting taxi orders by city districts);

Presentations of participants are available here .
