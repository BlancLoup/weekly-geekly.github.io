Life in freelance through the eyes of a programmer

For two and a half months I have been working in freelancing, during this time I have gained experience that I want to share with the public.

entrance


First you need to let you know in big ways that you are looking for orders: speak to friends, relatives, former colleagues, customers of former colleagues, publish in social networks, specialized websites. I submitted more than twenty applications on such sites before I worked at least one. It will take you a couple of weeks.

Difficulties



Bust


One of the gag, oddly enough, is the excessive number of orders taken. At the beginning, such a problem will not come close, but then it is quite possible. For me, 4 orders that I work on in parallel is the limit. Even in this case, I try, for example, to do two orders on one day, and two more - on another.

The difficulty here is that if you work on one order for less than two hours in a row, time is wasted inefficiently. While you enter the task and achieve a good pace, it will take half an hour. Hence the figure in 4 orders, cyborg can take more. :)

Evaluation


Prepare for the fact that at first you will incorrectly estimate the amount of time and, consequently, the money required to complete the order. For a more accurate assessment, it is useful to divide the task into subtasks, each of which will require no more than a day to complete. And even in this case, it is worth multiplying the resulting number by X, unaccounted features will probably come up, you will have to spend extra time fine-tuning and fixing bugs, etc. Communication with the customer takes its time.

Act


Unlike regular work, where you are paid a fixed salary, where you can in the morning, slowly reeling, reading a LiveJournal, drinking coffee, and getting to work, the freelancer will receive only for real results. For me, a great time - five hours out of eight, spent only on the execution of orders (minus snacks, chatting, reading mail, ICQ, Skype, etc.).

If some kind of sausage starts, emotional throwing - all this should be put off for the evening, when with a pure heart you can get a good heart out. :)
