Caution: Intruders

Recently, users have complained to us about ads in which they are supposedly offered money as a gift on behalf of Yandex. In this case, the person is asked to enter the mobile number, and then the activation code from the received SMS message. If the user does so, then, without knowing it, connects a paid SMS subscription. They start sending SMS to him, for example, with the weather forecast, and every day they charge him money from his mobile account.



We are not related to such ads, even if they appear on the background of Yandex. And, for our part, we are trying to protect you from the actions of intruders. Now Yandex is actively identifying pages created to lure a phone number. The warning that it is better not to enter these pages appears in the search results, in the Yandex Browser and other software.

In addition, we identify sites where the code is installed that triggers the display of ads of intruders. The presence of such code reduces the quality of sites, so they will appear in the search results lower than better quality resources.

Scammers deliberately fake web pages for well-known sites that people trust. They use the name of not only Yandex, but also other popular services, such as VKontakte or Odnoklassniki.
