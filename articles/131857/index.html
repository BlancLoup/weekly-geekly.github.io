Video review of the set-top box on Android - iconBIT Toucan W

Today, Android can be found not only on smartphones or tablets. More and more gadgets choose the system from Google : watches, players, as well as set-top boxes or, as you can call them, media players. It is about one of these consoles that will be discussed in our today's "Review without Rules":



iconBIT Toucan W looks like a WiFi router, but if you take a closer look, it becomes clear that its functionality is much broader. The device has many different ports: LAN, USB, HDMI, AV output.

The device runs on a 1GHz processor running Android Gingerbread . The bundle includes several applications, including a player that most of the known video formats play without problems: AVI, MOV, DiVx, MKV , etc.



In addition, the console is compatible with many standard Android applications and games. Included is a remote with a full QWERTY keyboard, it can act as a mouse, to do this, just hold down a special key and the cursor on the screen will repeat the movements of your hand.

Specifications of iconBIT Toucan W :
  • Processor and GPU : ARM Cortex A9 1 GHz, ARM Mali 400 3D video core (with support for Open-GL ES 2.0)
  • Hardware decoders : Full-HD 1080p video and sound, HD (up to 1080p)
  • Codecs: H.264, MPEG2, VC-1, WMV9, DivX, Xvid, MPEG4
  • Memory: RAM: DDR2 512 MB, built-in 4 GB NAND Flash, SDHC slot
  • Interfaces: 4 USB 2.0 (2 outside / 2 inside), HDMI, SPDIF, RCA video audio output, RJ45 LAN, Wi-Fi (802.11 b / g / n)
  • USB device support : USB mouse and keyboard, USB flash drives, USB HDD
  • Delivery set : BP, QWERTY remote control, HDMI cable

Onboard iconBIT Toucan W there is 4GB of internal memory, you can connect any USB device to the expansion: a USB flash drive or hard drive, there is also a slot for an SD memory card. Recommended price is $ 199.
