Russian startups in the field of e-government gather on May 13 for Feedback - Gov 2.0

I want to tell you that on May 13 in the Central House of Entrepreneur I (on behalf of our virtual incubator Gov2Project) and the Greenfield Project will organize a special event for civil projects (startups in the field of Government 2.0) on Feedback - Gov 2.0

At the beginning of the event, Peter Tatishchev (GreenfieldProject) will hold an improvised press conference with the participation of Maksut Shadaev (Presidential Administration of the Russian Federation), Ilya Ponomarev (State Duma deputy) and Ivan Begtin (author of the projects govweb.ru , datagov.ru , expert in the field of open state data). The audience will also be able to ask the speakers their questions. We want to discuss such issues:

1. Why do gov 2.0 projects
2. Target audience: is the society ready for such projects? How to get people interested?
3. Does the government need such projects?
4. How can the state save the budget using Gov 2.0 projects?
5. How to commercialize such projects?
6. Features of promotion of gov 2.0 projects
7. Why Gov 2.0 - the project can fail?

In the second part of the event, startups themselves, created to solve real problems of society, will tell about themselves - StreetJournal.org, DalSlovo.ru, Help Card and Jam from the window

Moreover, we decided to give an opportunity to any civil project to come up with elevetor pitch and receive feedback from both experts and the audience. If you have such a civil project and you want to join us, then I will always be happy.
