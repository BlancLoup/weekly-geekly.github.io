What is Web Slice?


Web Slice is an innovation in Internet Explorer 8 that allows you to embed a mini web page in the Favorites panel of the browser. Thus, it simplifies the display of frequently updated information: for example, follow up on new offers for your go-ahead on eBay or the weather forecast .

It may seem to you that this is a more interactive RSS subscription, however, if RSS is in most cases limited to textual information, then the Web Slice may contain, say, your entire Silverlight application.
