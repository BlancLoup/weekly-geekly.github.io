Update on the popularity of CMS-systems, analytics systems and online consultants in RuNet for the 2nd quarter

Hi, Habr! Ruward: Track (our joint project with the guys from iTrack ) updated the sections for the second quarter of 2014. The study was based on an automatic crawler analysis of about 5 million domains of the .RU zone (according to typical service signatures).

Fresh cut on the popularity of boxed CMS




Compared to the first quarter of the interesting points, we can single out a small drop in the shares of the most popular opensource solutions - WordPress and Joomla, still leading in the consolidated list - and the growth in the share of Bitrix, the leader in the paid boxed systems segment. Also in the segment of commercial CMS for the first time in the history of ratings, Ruward / iTrack UMI.CMS slightly surpassed NetCat in the number of installations in the fight for second place.

Distribution of popular meters and analytics systems




The distribution leader is still LiveInternet (a drop in the share compared to the first quarter), Yandex.Metrika and GA are in the second and third place (a growth in the share of both solutions is noted).

Rating distribution of popular online consultants




In the distribution of online consultants, Jivosite walked around Livetex in the ranking, taking into account the rank of sites, and the total number of installations. Both of these decisions, in fact, divide the Russian market with a large margin from all other competitors.
