Belarusian News pays for gold bullion traffic

The Belarusian Internet newspaper Belorusskiye Novosti announced the launch of the campaign ‚ÄúChanging People for Gold‚Äù, in which bloggers and site owners can take part.

The winners will be the participants who will bring the largest number of Belarusian visitors to the site Naviny.by. To do this, you can use the Belarusian News content in any way: reprint materials, cite them, post links, invite to view multimedia and participate in the forum. The top five, together with the links that brought the most visitors to Naviny.by, will be announced weekly.
