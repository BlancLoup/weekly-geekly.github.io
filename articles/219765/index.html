Cryptofon from InfoTeKS and Yota

It happened. we do not often write here about our products. Now there is an opportunity not only to tell, but also to show (so far only in the form of a photo :)) InfoTeX ViPNet Client for Android solution, developed jointly with Yota Devices.




ViPNet Client for Android is a software product that implements the functions of a VPN client and a personal firewall on a mobile workplace. The VPN client in ViPNet Client for Android intercepts any IP traffic, ensuring its transparent encryption using the GOST 28147-89 algorithm, and the personal network screen built into the product allows you to protect your device from network attacks.

ViPNet Client for Android can be built into YotaPhone already at the production stage. Now, to enable an LTE smartphone in a corporate environment protected by ViPNet products, it is enough to initialize the device with personal security keys that can be generated in the control center of a client's ViPNet network.
