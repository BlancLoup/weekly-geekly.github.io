PayPal launched service PayPal.Me



PayPal launched PayPal.Me with which you can create individual links and mini pages for quick money reception.

In a peculiar way, they try to make the word PayPal a verb. Translated from English, "PayPal Me" sounds like "Peapal me" or "Paypalani me." So, by the way, "Google" has long been a verb in many languages.

Currently, the company has allowed registration in 18 countries: Australia, Austria, Belgium, Great Britain, Germany, Denmark, Spain, Italy, Canada, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Russia, the USA, Turkey, France, Switzerland, and Sweden.

While the service is fresh - you can get a short and attractive address.

In the address itself, you can specify the desired amount for the transfer, for example:

https://www.paypal.me/namely/99.99
