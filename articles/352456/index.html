Belarus: blockchain and crypt regulation

The Decree No. 8 on regulation of the blockchain and related infrastructure, which was signed by the President of the Republic of Belarus on December 22, 2017, entered into force the day before yesterday in Belarus. The decree introduces such concepts (and their regulation) as blockchain, cryptocurrency, tokens, mining in Belarus smart contracts. Fixing these concepts through their definition in a legal document is the first experience. in the territory of the former Soviet Union. Of course, it will be taken into account and studied in neighboring countries, including Russia, especially in connection with the development of a common economic space within the EAEU.

In the period between the signing of Decree No. 8 and its entry into force, a number of regulations were adopted to regulate the issues provided for in Decree No. 8.
