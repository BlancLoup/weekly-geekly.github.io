Facebook is repainted again

At the f8 conference, Mark Zuckerberg introduced Facebook, which will be launched in the next few weeks. The idea is that all your events, photos, videos and posts will be divided into periods using the "timeline" (timeline), displayed to the right of the wall. In addition, it will be possible to filter the display of the desired content, such as video, pictures or something else. It will be possible to add apps, likes and other content to the ruler so that everything looks less chaotic so that you can show your life story the way you want it.

Now the feature will be launched for some selected users, and within a few weeks for all. Get ready.
