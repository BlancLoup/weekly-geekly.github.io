MediaInsight - monitoring traditional media

Good ping, Internet having



I want to ask you - do you have a TV? And without waiting for an answer, I will assume that there is :) I myself do not have it, but it does not matter. It is important to have an idea about this kind of media. Why is this necessary? And then what I want to tell you about one very interesting project. I learned here that there are children who are engaged in monitoring the media - television, radio, newspapers. That is, just like Yandex tracks activity on blogs, they monitor the broadcast.

Voice recognition


Yes Yes. To track the mention of anything on television and radio, you must have a powerful voice recognition engine. Unfortunately, I have no information on how it works. But judging by the quality of the recognized text - this is an automated solution, and not Indians, as in a fairly well-known service remind;). I would really like to know how this engine works inside. Or maybe even get at his disposal a more or less relevant copy of it.

Channel monitoring


To keep track of all that is said on numerous channels, you need to have a bunch of tuners, each tuned to its own wave. Or maybe there are some other ways? But I know nothing about them. This part of the project interests me no less than the voice recognition engine.

And how do I know all this?


Not. I have nothing to do with them. Just drank a beer with one good person. Heard a couple of entertaining stories. I looked at it all with my own eyes.

Talk less, work more!


The guys posted on their website a few demonstration examples of the use of their technology. The search in the archive seems to me especially interesting: you can see what they are saying about pirates , or find out how the problem of crisis is brought to the media. It is also interesting to hear about such a phenomenon as mutants - on this word the robot usually makes mistakes :)

Monitors and others like him


I think it is clear - there are many ways to monetize such a project. Not to mention that monitoring itself is quite an interesting thing, because it is still possible to process any conversations with the help of a similar engine. That only is the distillation of the recording of negotiations in a textual form. And if you think about listening devices on phones - mmmm :). A similar project in my opinion looks very attractive for large and medium-sized businesses, as well as for developers of enterprise management systems.
