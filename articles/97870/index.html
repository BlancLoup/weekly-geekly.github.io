The founders of Parallels, Acronis and Almaz have created a seed investment fund.

Sergey Belousov, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Parallels and Acronis, and Alexander Galitsky, the founder of Almaz Capital Partners, created a fund to invest in high-tech startups at an early stage of development. The fund was named Runa Capital . The size of the fund is about $ 30 million.



Directions of investments - software and Internet services. Of particular interest - to projects in the areas of cloud computing, machine translation, virtual presence and software for mobile devices. The main task of Runa Capital is to bring talented Russian-speaking technology teams to world markets.
