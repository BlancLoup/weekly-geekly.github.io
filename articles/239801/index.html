We invite you to the Internet of Things: Intel IoT Roadshow goes to Moscow!



Do you want to get an Intel Galileo fee and start writing apps for the Internet of things right now? Then come to the IoT Roadshow! This is a series of hackathon trainings dedicated to exploring the capabilities of Intel Galileo and developing solutions based on it. The event starts on the morning of November 22 in the DI Telegraph with detailed training on working with the board. A hackathon will follow, after which the 3 best projects will receive support from the Skolkovo Foundation and prizes from Intel.

To participate in the hackathon, you must complete the registration form , and then send a description of your project . Come to our event if you -
  • a startup working on an IoT solution;
  • student of a technical college;
  • a developer with experience with Arduino;
  • or just an amateur enthusiast interested in this topic.

Even if you still do not have the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãan IoT project and a team of like-minded people to implement it, with high probability you will be able to join any project right on the hackathon.

The first 100 participants who come to the IoT Roadshow will receive the Intel IoT Developer Kit . The kit includes boards Intel Galileo Gen 2 and Edison, as well as all the necessary sensors, accessories and software. At the event, the most active and creative participants will receive pleasant prizes and souvenirs.

Skolkovo is a partner of this event. The Internet of Things is a direction actively developed in the Fund. The most interesting and thoughtful projects will be able to become residents of the center and get access to world-class mentors, participation in international conferences and, of course, venture capital investments.

Closer to the date of the meeting, all participants will receive additional technical instructions in order to be 100% ready for the hackathon and not spend too much time downloading, installing programs and studying documentation. You really won't have much time to develop, so please do not ignore our advice.

Questions about participation in the event can be asked in the Intel Developer Zone group on VKontakte , on the Google+ page or via email .

Prepare interesting ideas and store energy for intensive coding!

UPD: Attention! Changed the venue of the event. Saturday morning, November 22, we meet in the Central Telegraph building!
