Emotional discharge

Ahead of the working days, and we will begin to talk about the rest.

Places for rest.


Airports


For me, this is a completely separate world. Rest at the airport can be divided into 2 categories.
The first , which I could not understand for a very long time. People drive up in their cars, stand at the landing / take-off sites of the aircraft and watch the planes fly in / out. It seems nothing special about it, but once I was filled. All the charm comes especially at night when the plane is poured with the color of molten metal (mmm ... well, I hope you understand me correctly) and the sound that shakes the lungs, under the "spatial music", when everything around you freezes, and in the hands of coffee, it is very relaxing, very.
The second is when I just watch at the airport how people fly or fly. Sitting at ease aside, or under the scorching lamp of law enforcement officers , smiles upon meeting, tears at parting, everything is very emotional and kindly somehow.

It is important to note that it is better to observe the aircraft at night , to walk through the terminals - in the afternoon . At night in the terminals of the airport (Sheremetyevo), it is simply unbearably empty.

High-speed highway (MKAD)


No, on the highway we will not run from the cars, this is to active recreation, which will not be considered here. We will talk about driving a car. The best time to drive is the night when there are few cars and there are obviously no traffic jams. You can go at a constant speed, thinking about some pressing issues stuck in your head, to the music, both calm and dynamic, it all depends on the desired rhythm.

Lectures


The best way to unload the brains of current affairs is to load them with something new. Attending lectures on a topic that is far from that which you are doing (for example, a counterbalance to the development is medicine). The effectiveness of this holiday will be much higher, you will spend it with benefit, and learn something new for yourself. At the same time, switch to what your brain does not think about every day.

How do you relax? (besides standard cinema, clubs, computer games, serials, etc.). Do you know any not typical, yet quite feasible ways of emotional unloading?
