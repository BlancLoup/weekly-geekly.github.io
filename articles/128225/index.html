Published a free video course on WebMatrix



On the official Microsoft ASP.NET website , a new video course on developing web applications using the free WebMatrix environment has been published . The course was prepared by the company Pluralsight, who is professionally engaged in the creation of video courses on software development. The language of the video course is English.

In total, there are six videos in the video course:

  • introduction to WebMatrix ;
  • sites and files ;
  • ASP.NET Web Pages programming ;
  • work with data ;
  • reports and deployment ;
  • Work with PHP and MySQL .

All videos are available for viewing from Pluralsight.

Download WebMatrix (2 Mb) and try everything yourself by following the link .
