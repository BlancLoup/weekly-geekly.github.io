Yandex.Taxi for iPhone started accepting Yandex.Money and bank cards

From this evening, users of the Yandex.Taxi application for iPhone can pay for their trips by credit card. At the same time, they do not even need to carry it with them: first, the card must be tied to their Yandex.Money account , and then, paying with the driver, enter the three-digit code of this card (CVV2-CVC2).

If there is no card, but there is money in the account, in the same application you can pay and simply Yandex.Money.



The whole process takes one click, if you pay with Yandex.Money, and one click + three digits, if you pay with a card.

Yandex.Money does not transfer your card data to the taxi company, payment processing takes place entirely on the service side. The service of payment by an attached card is free, the service does not charge additional fees or payment processing fees. You enter card details only once, at the binding stage , on the PCI DSS protected page. Then you only need the CVV2-CVC2 code.

To use the new feature, you need to:

  • call a taxi in Moscow (we start from this city);
  • Download or update the Yandex.Taxi application in the App Store (for now, the Yandex.Money payment function or the linked card is for iPhone only);
  • in the browser, link your bank card to a Yandex.Money account - there are detailed instructions on this page . You can also bind the card in the Yandex.Money application;
  • select the item ‚Äú Payment by Yandex.Money and credit card ‚Äù in the list of requirements for the car.




About limits : with an attached card you can make no more than 15 payments per day and spend no more than 20,000 rubles a day. If you plan to drive a lot, keep this in mind. Only one card can be linked to one account for ‚Äúthrough payments‚Äù.



About the application : Yandex.Taxi - a quick way to order a taxi without calling the dispatcher. It is looking for a free car near you, the average car delivery time is 10 minutes after ordering. For the movement of a taxi you can follow the map. In the application, you can pre-select a driver with a smoking or non-smoking cabin; arrange a car with a child car seat or order a taxi with receipts for reports to the personnel department.



For owners of other phones, residents of other cities and users of the browser-based version of Yandex.Taxi: over time, the possibility of paying with Yandex.Money and tied cards will come to you! This is just the beginning.
