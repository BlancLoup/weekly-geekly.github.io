Video review service HostTracker

Thanks to our partner ProDeloTV, we publish a video review of the cloud service of monitoring web sites HostTracker .



The main function of the monitoring service of accessibility of sites by the Host-tracker is to notify the client via SMS, Email, Skype, Hangouts, Viber, Telegram, HTTP Post or a phone call about problems with his site: the presence or absence of certain content, long response time, entering into Roskomnadzor's lists , a domain or certificate expires, it hits DNSBL databases, database availability, CPU, RAM, HDD load and much more.
