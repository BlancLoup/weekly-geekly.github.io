Died writer Terry Pratchett

Today, March 12, 2015, the popular English writer Sir Terry Pratchett, best known for his satirical fantasy "Flat World", passed away. His publisher said that the writer died in his home, surrounded by relatives and a cat on the bed.
In late 2007, Pratchett discovered a rare form of Alzheimer's disease, and in 2009, he wrote an open letter in the Daily Mail about euthanasia. Because of his illness, in the last years of his life, Pratchett worked by dictating to his assistant Rob Wilkins or using speech recognition software.

On the death of the writer said publisher Larry Finlay :
