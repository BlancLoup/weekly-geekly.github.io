CenterTelecom will reduce headcount by 12%

CenterTelecom OJSC (ESMO) in 2006 will reduce the number of personnel by 12%. It is reported by "Prime-Tass" with reference to the online conference of the deputy general director of the company, Dmitry Karmanov.

According to him, the management of the company has developed a number of measures to increase the average monthly wage for employees of CenterTelecom OJSC. In 2006, the budget of CenterTelecom was approved, in which, while reducing revenues, an increase in labor costs of 6.4% was provided. The Board of Directors also approved a headcount optimization program, which provides for a phased reduction of staff in 2006 by 12%, which will increase the average monthly salary for the company by 14.8%.
