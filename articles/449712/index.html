Samsung postponed the start of sales of the smartphone Galaxy Fold




The South Korean corporation Samsung announced the postponement of the start of sales of the newest smartphone Galaxy Fold with a folding screen . The reason - the discovery of a large number of problems with the display itself, in some cases, it stops working normally after a couple of days.

The reason is the lack of protection against dust penetration under the protective film in the place where the screen is folded. In addition, the company did not warn that the film should not be removed from the screen, it performs a protective function, if you try to remove it by applying force, you can damage the display.
