There is a version of Popcorn Time for iOS



Of course, it would be illogical to expect that an application of this kind will be placed in the App Store - this is contrary to the draconian severe rules of Apple. The Popcorn Time application for iOS can only be installed on a jailbreak phone.

It should be noted that this application is a version of one of the forks of Popcorn Time, namely time4popcorn. The developers of fork are very active in terms of working on their application, and the iOS version clearly demonstrates this activity.

The first release of the application requires a jailbreak, and is available through the Cydia platform. Application developers claim that they are currently working on a solution that allows installing this application on regular phones, without jailbreaking. And the plans of the team are grand, according to the members of the team itself.

By the way, Popcorn Time was announced just 5 and a half months ago, and now there are applications and versions of Popcorn Time for almost all platforms. Unfortunately, the iOS version does not have some of the functions available in the desktop version of the application. So, while there is no support for Chromecast and Apple TV, but work in this direction is underway.

The overall work of the application, in general, is satisfactory. Only a more or less wide channel is needed to stream video through BitTorrent.

The developers have posted the source code of their application , under the license GPL V3. The team continues to work on improving the application code, in order to increase the overall performance and functionality of Popcorn Time for iOS.
