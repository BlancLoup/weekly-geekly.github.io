If architects worked as web designers

The results of the contest "Difficult teenager or the client is not always right . " The winner of the contest only corrected the word "site" to "home", but how beautiful it turned out :)

Dear architect,

Please design and build a house for me. I'm not sure exactly what I want, so act on your own. In my house should be from two to 45 bedrooms. Please plan it so that bedrooms can be easily added and removed. I will make the final decision on what exactly I need when you bring me the finished drawings. Also, bring me estimates for each layout option so that I can choose one in comparison.

Remember that my new home should be cheaper than the one in which I live now. But at the same time, keep in mind that all the flaws that exist in my current home (the floor in the kitchen creaks, and the walls are thin) should not be present in my new home.

When you design, remember that the house should be built so that it does not have to be repaired often. This means that you have to use expensive building materials - aluminum, vinyl or composite materials. If you decide not to use aluminum, be prepared to explain.

Please use modern design solutions and the latest materials. I want my home to be a model of modern architecture. However, I warn you that the kitchen should accommodate my refrigerator, 1952 release.

To be sure that the new house is suitable for my family, please consult with all my children, as well as my wife's parents. For my mother-in-law is very important, what will be our house, as she visits us at least once a year. Carefully weigh the points of view of all members of my family and make the right decision. I, however, reserve the right to reject it.

Let's get along without discussing the small details. Your task now is to create a general drawing of the house. Therefore, it is too early to discuss the color of the carpet. However, keep in mind that my wife's favorite color is blue.

Also, don't think about the construction team yet. Your first priority is to create a detailed plan. But after I approve it, I hope that the house will be built and completed on a turnkey basis within 48 hours.

While you are working on designing a home that satisfies all my needs, it is important to remember that sooner or later I will want to sell it. Therefore, the house should like a wide range of potential buyers. So even before you complete the design, it is important to conduct a survey of the population in the area where I live.

I advise you to drive up and look at the house of my neighbor, which he built last year. We like it very much. For example, we would like to have the same 30-meter swimming pool as his. I am sure you can include the pool in your design without any changes in the estimate.

Please prepare a complete set of drawings. At the moment it is not necessary to carry out a real design, as the drawings are needed only to find builders. However, I notify you that you will be responsible for any increase in construction costs as a result of subsequent changes in the project.

You must be delighted with such an interesting project like this one! Such opportunities for the use of modern technologies and materials and obtaining such creative freedom - believe me, this happens infrequently. Contact me as soon as possible and provide your ideas and plans.

Ps. My wife just said that she did not agree with the many instructions that I accompanied you in this letter. It is your duty as an architect to resolve these differences. I myself have tried many times, but it did not work. If you do not cope with these responsibilities, I will be forced to find another architect.

Pps. Perhaps I need not a house, but a caravan for traveling. If so, please let me know as soon as possible.
