10 free crowdfunding project tools

For successful crowdfunding gathering, it is important that the project is beautifully decorated. In this post I have collected ten services that will help create infographics, arrange audio and video, get statistics on fees and much more. I hope they will be useful to you, since all the services are universal and can be used not only for crowding projects (although we used them in projects that came to us for advice).


Pictochart - a service for creating infographics. There are about 10 free templates and more than 4000 icons. In addition to infographics, you can create beautiful posters and charts. Unlike many other solutions, Pictochart does not ask for money for downloading the resulting image.



Soundcloud is an audio storage service. Like Youtube, but for songs. Soundcloud draws tracks into a beautiful widget that can be inserted into a project description on a crowdfunding website. You can share songs with friends, you can create playlists.



Crowdtrack is a Russian statistics service for crowdfunding projects. You score a link to the project and get data on the collection dynamics, the number of sponsors, rewards purchased, etc. The service also offers a free informer that can be placed on your site.



GIMP is a great alternative to Photoshop. Works under Windows, Mac and Linux. With GIMP, you can create graphics and logos, crop photos, combine images using layers, retouch photos, and more.



Lightworks is a video editor distributed under the freemium model (a free product, with the ability to purchase individual functions). Has more functionality than Windows Live. At present, version number 12 is available. By the way, with the help of Lightworks they mounted ‚ÄúPulp Fiction‚Äù.



Youtube is the most popular video service in the world, founded in 2005 (and it seems it was always). In 2014, Youtube announced the introduction of crowdfunding features, so now you can collect money "without leaving the pipe."



Yandex.Speller - spell check service from you-see-who. Speller checks text in Russian, Ukrainian and English. Mastkhev for everyone.



Google Docs is a document collaboration service. A great solution if you are not preparing the project alone. You can work with text documents, spreadsheets and presentations. There are mobile versions for Android and iOS.



Wordpress is the most popular site engine in the world. Thanks to the huge number of video lessons, everyone can master it, in just a couple of days. In this way, you will have a product site where sponsors will gather after the project is completed.



Buffer is a service of simultaneous posting in several social networks. Supported by Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, Google+. Vkontashi there, so there pens.



Perhaps I missed something. If so, share the free convenient services that you use.
