A device for a language can help deaf people start hearing



In the last article, we learned about various devices that help people with disabilities, phones , tablets and even watches for blind people. Now inventions are beginning to appear that should help deaf people defeat their ailment.

In the future, people with hearing loss may not need surgery to install a cochlear implant . This is the conclusion reached by researchers at the University of Colorado, who invented a device inserted into the mouth and helps to hear.
