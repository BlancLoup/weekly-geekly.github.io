How to write an article about your company?


Hello. Today I want to tell you how to write an article about your company on Wikipedia. At the same time on a specific example, I will introduce the main principles and rules.


Is it worth writing?


So, the first thing to do is to carefully read the criteria for the significance of conventional and commercial organizations for the encyclopedia. It is believed that the criteria of significance came up with terrible people who just like to delete articles =). Your article may also try to delete because of insignificance. But if your company is a recognized leader in its field, if you have dozens of medals for excellent products, and profits exceed the state budget of an average African country, you are invulnerable.
Attention! If the significance is not shown in the article and is not confirmed by third-party sources, the article will be deleted anyway.

Who am I? Where I am?


So, Wikipedia is right for you, and you are Wikipedia. What next? Register!
It is not difficult to register, even it is not necessary to have an e-mail. But there are some significant advantages:
  • Articles of interest to you can be added to the watchlist. Then you can track changes made by other users.
  • You will be able to communicate with you on your discussion page, which is one, in contrast to the discussion pages of anonymous, which change when your provider assigns you a different IP address.
  • Traditionally, the degree of confidence in the registered participant is higher than to the anonymous author.

What name to choose? Roga & Copyta, ltd is clearly not suitable - the degree of confidence in your edits will be zero. And the article you create, even if it complies with all the rules, will be subconsciously perceived as an advertisement. Better something neutral like Vasi Pupkin =)

As the title of the article is usually used the name of the company without specifying the form of ownership. That is, for Roga & Copyta, Ltd. the title of the article will most likely be ‚ÄúRoga & Copyta‚Äù. Content


The subject of the article should be described in literary Russian in a neutral style. Any emotion (‚ÄúRoga and Copyta FOREVER!‚Äù) Is unacceptable. Excessive praise or humiliation without reference to independent authoritative sources, too. All statements in the article must be verified.
The content of Wikipedia articles is governed by several fundamental rules - VP: NTZ , VP: PROV , VP: AI .

Sources


As mentioned above, all statements in the article must be verifiable and confirmed by authoritative independent sources . Publications in newspapers, articles in specialized journals, feedback from experts - all this should be searched before writing an article. The more third-party sources are given, the better. If there are no such sources at all, the article is a candidate for removal. If after each sentence there is a footnote, that's just fine.
Separately are links to your company's website as a source. To confirm the words ‚Äúwe are the best‚Äù, links to the official website are not enough, to confirm the information on the number of employees, the structure of the company, and the management - quite.
