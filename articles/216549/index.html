Open and closed collections


We consider 2 types of collections - open and closed. Closed collections, your personal and no one except yourself has access to them. Open collections, on the contrary, can be available to all users, even those who currently do not have an account in Likeastore. In such collections will be collected information that you want to share with the world.

At the moment we have implemented only the concept of closed collections, but very soon we will add open ones.
