SPB Frontend: September 2 will be a meeting of the front-end community in St. Petersburg

On Tuesday, September 2, in St. Petersburg, the front-end developer community of SPB Frontend, in partnership with Action Zone, opens a new meeting season. Time: 20:00. Address: Ligovsky Prospect, 74, coworking "Area of ‚Äã‚ÄãAction".



The program will include reports about the library of beautiful Famo.us interfaces, webpack (the future of the build system after Grunt) and PostCSS (the future after Sass and LESS).

The stated topics of reports and speakers:

  1. Maxim Ryzhikov from JetBrains will talk about a new and rather sensational Webpack module builder.
  2. Sergey Efremov from ResumUP will complement Maxim and show how to move from rails asset pipeline to webpack without closing the project and rewriting everything from scratch.
  3. Artem Sushchev from Stepic will demonstrate how to work with the new library to create crazy (in a good way) Famo.us interfaces and how to make friends with Ember.js.
  4. Andrey Sitnik from Evil Martians will tell about PostCSS and why the Auto Prefixer would be impossible on Sass or Stylus.

Sign up for a meeting: vk.com/spbfrontend_new_season .

You can subscribe to the announcements of this and future meetings in the SPB Frontend group.
