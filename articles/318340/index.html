Burnout freelancer on Upwork. Causes, tools, solutions

But there was one serious problem, a bug in my mental system - it is a regular burnout from work, which I did not realize. It was bad, nervous, anxious, but the cause could not be found. She sat somewhere deep in the subconscious, buried deeper in early childhood and did not allow to see the real situation. How enlightenment came and what to do to avoid burnout - read under the cut.

Including was the experience of working on the platform as a customer. In total, he hired 20 freelancers of different specializations, mostly developers and designers.

Over these 10 years, many habits have been developed. Formed so-called comfort zone and overall mentality and worldview. This clarification is not accidental, since my main experience as a developer is hourly contracts, with the time tracker turned on. Although most of the recommendations apply to any employee of the organization where they implement time tracking and similar software. And subjectively, this post will be useful for all knowledge workers (developers, administrators, copywriters, designers, marketers, and others, sorry I forgot), since most of it is about health and brain function, and tools to prevent burnout.

Personally, my main specialization is .NET development for desktop and web. But it was necessary, more than once, to impose, draw design, write documentation, design a database, engage in testing. As they say, do not repeat the mistakes of others, make your own.

About brain physiology and mental health


Briefly describe how the work of the brain, namely the prefrontal cortex . The prefrontal cortex is responsible for processing and analyzing abstract concepts, suppresses instinctive reactions such as hit or run, sets goals. Let's just say this is the facade or filter of our subconscious. If the resources of this part of the brain are exhausted, the minimum that awaits us is procrastination, and in terminal cases - increased nervousness, anxiety, irritability. To get rid of this, the subconscious pushes the person to any methods of solving the issue. Including alcohol, nicotine, promiscuous sex and so on.

At the same time, rational thinking is turned off, so the central processor doesn't care about morals and other bodies, it needs to restore the resource. So laid by nature and evolution.

By the way, the brain consciously works by 5-7%, the remaining 95% is your ‚Äúsoftware‚Äù installed during life, that is, the unconscious. The program of behavior works as it pleases, based on patterns, beliefs, following the crowd (the monkey sees - the monkey does), self-preservation instincts and others.

Therefore, for a long time focusing on a single task requiring active brain work, the resources of the prefrontal cortex are exhausted. The maximum time of concentration on one mental task is 30-40 minutes . After that comes an uninvited guest - Aunt Prokra.


We are starting to get distracted, get into social networks, frighten off videos, read habr. This is a natural reaction of the subconscious program, the brain needs rest and it seeks solutions in other places that are less energy consuming. And often he finds it in entertainment content, which temporarily provokes the release of dopamine. But choosing this path, the brain does not really relax. The release of dopamine knocks the reward system and return to the task more difficult, I want to be more distracted.

How to recharge?


(from English. recharge - recharge) I have already written a short tip: tired - rest. The younger you are, the more energy and energy you have in solving mental problems. But still, you need to develop a habit so that a red light lights up in your head when you feel tired and your brain refuses to give a result. If in more detail, the following is a list of recommendations that will help avoid burnout :

- Use the "Tomato" method

In other words, alternate work and rest as often as possible. Let's rest the brain after an active working sprint in order to avoid procrastination and overwork. This is an important tip for this post. It was he who led me to the proper organization of my working time. The technique is based on short sprints up to 40 minutes of work (remember the resource of the prefrontal cortex) and then 5-15 minutes of rest. The default proportion is 25/5 minutes. I use a proportion of 40/10 minutes to fall out of the stream less often and rest more. In addition, it does not affect the money earned, the tracker takes a screenshot on average every 10 minutes or more.

What to do in 5-10 minutes of rest?
The main goal is to rest the brain. Switch to reading social. networks, blogs, books or videos are not an option. Dopamine will add a little, but the effect in terms of rest will be small. Therefore, I personally do one of the following:

  • I turn on the music and throw the tennis ball into the wall
  • I juggle
  • I just lie down on the sofa and listen to 2 songs with a massage ball in my hands.
  • I look out the window. First, in the distance, then closer. Exercises sight to the same.
  • Brew tea
  • Breakfast / lunch in long breaks

What else can you do:

  • Meditation (Deep Inhale / Exhale)
  • Self-massage
  • Push up or pull up
  • With long pauses - take a walk, for example, to the nearest store.

General advice - you need to turn off the prefrontal part, by doing something with your hands that you can do on the machine. Embroider a cross, solder, collect layouts, also suitable, if it does not require mental effort. Thus, your pre-frontal part normalizes its resource and you can continue to encode, write texts, draw designs.

Tool:

- Observe the mode of operation and the mode of rest
In freelancing, chaos is a common occurrence. This is his dignity and his main drawback. To teach oneself to work according to the regime is to live easier. You maintain a rhythm and do not overwork without need. You know when you go to bed and when you have free time. Yes, we have the opportunity to do other things at any time and for the majority - to work at any time. But in priority - try to comply with the regime.

Tool:

  • Any reminder, with a daily repetition. I use the built-in iOS application. I exhibit at 11 am - the beginning of work, and within a week or two, I get involved in the rhythm and adjust my affairs to this time.

- Update the situation and types of tasks
If you add newness to the workflow, the prefrontal cortex remains in good shape. The new brain always reacts, because it is important for survival. "I did not have time to navigate - did not survive." Change the setting and type of tasks.

Situation:

  • If you work at home, you can change the position of the table, change the chair, the room where you work every few months. For example, an interesting option - to equip the office on the balcony . More ideas in home office articles.
  • Alternate work at home and work in coworking / cafe / from parents / your office
  • Alternate work on the PC and work on the laptop, if possible - on the tablet
  • New devices and working environment. For example, you can buy a new keyboard or mouse every few months. Install a new interface for your OS. Even changing the background of the desktop, a beneficial effect on the prefrontal cortex.

Freelancers usually have frequent task rotations. Especially designers, copywriters, on average, have many times more orders and tasks than developers. Therefore, I can give advice to developers, especially for long-term projects:

  • Alternate, code and UI development, code and testing
  • Find and use new libraries and solutions.
  • Try new tools and plugins for your IDE. Look especially at the Productivity category.
  • Change the IDE theme, if possible, update to a newer version if possible.
  • Be sure to learn the hot keys of your IDE. You can print and hang in front of you. This increases productivity at times.

Upwork Tracker Tips


  1. If you are annoyed by notifications, disable them. For example, yes. It is better then to go to Work Diary and remove the excess, than to sweat because of the constant screenshot notifications. In the place of Upwork, I would generally make this window as inconspicuous as possible, that is, semi-transparent and small. By the way, for 10 years, I got only one harmful customer who strictly monitored the screenshots and demanded to remove those that in his opinion do not apply to work. The remaining ~ 50, always treated normally, if I check mail for example, answer in the messenger or go to the forum. Voltage from surveillance - this should reduce.

  2. Use hotkeys to start and stop time tracking. For example, you urgently need to switch to a non-working task, press Ctrl + Alt + PgDown. Again, go back to the task - Ctrl + Alt + PgUp. This is set up in my HotKeys tab, the default shortcut keys are different.


  3. Add time manually . If you have established a trusting relationship with a client, feel free to click Add Manual Time if, for example, the Internet disappeared or you worked outside the desktop (collected by a robot). Or, let's be honest, just zadolbalis from tracking your actions. After all, the result is important for the customer - not screenshots. Of course, the other side of the medal is that Upwork does not guarantee payment of manual time, and the customer himself must be warned by you before he is billed. Therefore, the key word here is trust .

  4. Put a ‚ÄúView Work Diary‚Äù reminder at the end of the workday until it becomes a habit. For example, while I was writing this post, the tracker was suddenly ON. I hide the icon itself from the tray in order to completely ignore the fact that I am working under supervision.

  5. Write a monetary goal for the week and write it on a sticker with a pencil so that it is in front of your eyes. At the end of the day, erase the graph for how many hours or $ is left to the goal, and enter the new numbers taking into account the accumulated. Thus, you daily control your income and workloads, and are motivated for faster fulfillment and overfulfilment of the goal. But do not forget about overwork and rest!

  6. Put the Upwork mobile app . This will keep your finger on the pulse when you are offline. The same applies to the mobile versions of instant messengers that the client and the team use. Be it Skype, Viber, WhatsApp, but more often Slack, Flowdock and the like.

  7. Close the Upwork application on the desktop to finish the job. I have a habit of not turning off the computer at all, so as not to waste time loading.
