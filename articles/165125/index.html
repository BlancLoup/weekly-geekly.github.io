Petition to shorten copyright

On Habr√© there were many articles and stormy discussions about the excessive duration of copyright. Now, those who would like to fix it can do for this a small, but important thing - sign a petition on the page.
petitions.whitehouse.gov/petition/shorten-excessive-copyright-terms/XMc72zjc
Although the petition will be sent to the White House, we are sure that this problem concerns everyone on the planet.
You must collect 25,000 signatures by the end of January.

Translation of the petition under the cut

Today's copyright deadlines are disproportionately longer than progress requires. Excessive copyright protection limits the benefits they bring and does not condone the development and creation of new works.

I ask the government to limit the term of copyright to 10 years without any exceptions. And although, compared with the current copyright terms, 10 years may seem quite modest, nevertheless, 10 years is a lot, apparently, even this period is superfluous. I also ask you to limit existing rights to 10 years.

It is not a concern of society for authors and artists to be rewarded for their work and creativity. And copyright should not be the only incentive for them. The era of exclusive rights must end.
