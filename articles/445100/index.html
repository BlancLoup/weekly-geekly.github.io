Anycubic: Photon S, 4Max Pro and Great Renaming



Shenzhen Anycubic Technology Co., Ltd has announced the release of a new Anycubic Photon S - an improved version of the famous photopolymer 3D printer Anycubic Photon. Also, the new compact desktop FDM printer 4MAX PRO was introduced in absentia - an updated version of the now released Anycubic 4Max .

Features


Consider the main differences of the new printer.



Double Z-axis guide rails for increased accuracy and stability of printing. Less chance of deviations, more positioning accuracy.



Using a steel ball construction for quick and easy platform calibration. Calibration on a sheet of paper is now done with one screw.



Linear texture of the construction platform for better adhesion of the part. The aluminum platform has a textured surface to better hold the part in the printing process.



Convenient and fast self-developed slicer. The speed of work is declared several times higher than that of common freeware solutions.



Improved matrix ultraviolet module. Each UV diode on the matrix is ‚Äã‚Äãequipped with a focusing lens. Higher accuracy, less possibility of parasitic illumination.


The strengthened filtration of air - the double filter with absorbent carbon. Evaporating volatile fractions of the photopolymer fall into the filter, and not into the user's lungs.



Less noise: 45-55 dB (measured with 39 dB background ambient noise).
The printer promises to be very interesting. Accept pre-orders .
