We put a marker in the head of the insect, put the beetle on a large sheet of paper and make it crawl with the joystick. We direct both buttons forward - the ladybug draws a straight line, one button forward - we get a smooth curve, one forward and the other back - the bend will be more sharp. Of course, not the fact that by drawing, for example, a cow, you will not depict a dolphin, but experience and practice will do their work.

The toy is designed for children from 4 years. Included are erasable markers (thank God, otherwise Khan linoleum).
