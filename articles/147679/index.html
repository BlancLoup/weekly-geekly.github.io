Master's degree in "Mathematical methods of computer security" at the department IBKS FTK SPbGPU

This year, for the first time in the St. Petersburg Polytechnic University , a set of undergraduates in full-time free magistracy in the specialty "090900.68.01, mathematical methods of computer security" is announced at the IBS department of the Faculty of Technical Cybernetics. Prior to this, the department produced only under specialty programs, and it's great that finally there are opportunities for people receiving education in a two-step system.

Among the topics covered in the learning process:
- control and security of operating systems
- theoretical foundations of computer security
- security of modern information technologies
- information security management
- special sections of mathematics
- cryptographic methods for protecting information in distributed computing systems
- security of distributed databases
- artificial intelligence in information security systems
- secure information systems

You can read more about the program on the website of the Polytech , there are also requirements for entering the magistracy .

Deadline for submitting documents for the program - until July 25
