Video of speeches from the conference Hack In The Box'2008


HITB (http://www.hackinthebox.org) is a regularly held conference devoted to various aspects of information security. There were quite a lot of interesting reports on Hack In The Box 2008, but two reports stood out from the crowd (at least for me):
1) Decompilers and Beyond
2) Analysis and Visualization of Common Packers
The first report is remarkable for the fact that its author is Ilfak Gulfanov, the author of the not-known disassembler IDA. And the second report is notable for the fact that its author Ero Carrera (the author of the PE-format parser in python (http://code.google.com/p/pefile/)) covers topics related to automating the process of analyzing software protected by protectors using technology code virtualization (vmprotect, themida).
