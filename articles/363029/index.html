A little about automating email marketing for beginners


For those who, by the nature of their activities, have long been associated with mailing lists (no matter what topic and in what quantity you are sending letters), the title MailChimp is more than familiar, perhaps you even know everything that is written in this article.

Our article is intended for those who recently began mastering the art of creating mailings that give conversion. Perhaps they will be interested to learn about the updates from this service, which will help save great time and optimize the workflow. Next is a translation of an article from an MailChimp employee named Chris.

Once upon a time, in one of the very old releases, we added a feature called ‚Äúanswering machine‚Äù. After nearly a million campaigns, where more than 700 million emails were sent in total, it‚Äôs time for a massive update. This is more than just a rebranding: we have thoroughly revised the method of automated mailing, although we have retained the features characteristic of MailChimp.

92 thousand of our customers use automatic messages. With their help, you can carry out various marketing techniques: to attract potential customers, to train readers, to notify about the appearance of goods in stock, etc.

Several years ago we ourselves experimented in this area, providing a wider choice to those who received our letters. In addition, we learned from our customers, asked them questions and conducted extensive research to understand how to improve this function.

We are proud to present you "Automation" - an upgraded program with a single interface. "Automation" is a work with a lot of letters, simple and effective management of workflows (using several automation scripts), advanced management of the time of sending. We invite you to a brief tour of the main functions of "Automation".

Mailing Scripts

There are many reasons for sending automatic messages to your subscribers . Taking into account the main ones, we have developed the Workflows feature. Greeting a new subscriber, creating a training series of letters, even congratulations on a holiday - all this allows you to attract customers more effectively. For eCommerce360 users, we have added scripts to send automatic emails in response to certain purchases. For more information on creating scripts, refer to our Knowledge Base.



Time management

One of the most remarkable features is the ability to control the time of sending your automatic emails. You can set up an instant dispatch in response to one or another action - subscription, purchase, following the link. This allows you to automatically and permanently provide the reader with relevant material. Despite the unpredictability of these actions, the program will always track them for you.



Automatic advanced settings allow you to send a letter at any time of the day, to assign the exact time or time period. Various options will ensure the delivery of the letter at the most advantageous time for you. For more information on automatic distribution settings, refer to the Knowledge Base of the service.



Segmentation and reach

As the company grows and its marketing needs grow, the reach of automation grows with you. Create your own rules with our segmentation tool. Develop your own scenarios using specific criteria, list segmentation, a different number of steps in each group.

Identify recipients by location, amount of purchases, or other criteria specific to your business. Interested? Try the "Find Similar Subscribers" feature from our research team.



Detailed reporting

Automation uses a single interface to manage all your emails. Distribute the messages in the correct order and set the send interval between them. You can track the movement of a separate series of letters, as well as examine summary information about the status of your campaign as a whole. The ability to view a separate send queue has been added to increase the transparency of the process. The built-in accounting system tracks conversion and attraction, so if necessary, you can quickly make the necessary changes (the link contains information on what these changes can be in order to increase the feedback ).
