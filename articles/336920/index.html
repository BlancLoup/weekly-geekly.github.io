From the creator of the first SCS


When we say that CommScope is always at the forefront of technology, this is not boasting. The company's engineers were the first to develop many solutions that later became industry standards.

Take, for example, the actual SCS. The concept of structured cabling systems was born at Bell Laboratories in the mid 80s of the last century. Prior to this, cable connections were organized on a point-to-point basis, based on proprietary technologies, and the replacement of IT equipment often required a complete replacement of the cable system. The main idea of ‚Äã‚ÄãSCS is the formation of a universal cable infrastructure with the installation of connection points (information outlets) throughout the building. This idea was implemented in the PDS (Premises Distribution System) system, which later became shipped under the SYSTIMAX brand.
