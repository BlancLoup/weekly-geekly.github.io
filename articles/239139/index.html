The presentation of the new Tesla electric car will be held on October 9



Yesterday, the head of Tesla Motors, Elon Musk, wrote on his Twitter about an important event in the company's life: the presentation of a new electric vehicle model. Unfortunately, the details were not indicated - it is not even known which particular model will be shown.

Most likely, a presentation of the Model D model will be held, which can be considered the successor of the most popular version of the Tesla electric car, the Model S. nothing.

Among other things, the company announced plans to release a compact electric sedan called Model III.
