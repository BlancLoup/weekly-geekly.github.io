Resuscitation of the old grandfather 386

By chance, the case got an old iron box, and the box turned out to be a good set of antique iron poured into the old case.



And of course I decided to revive the old man :)
Details and many photos under the cut.


So, what we have:



Motherboard with a decoupled processor.



Here it is: AMD386DX40 - 40Mhz frequency. This is a clone of the popular intel 386 authorship AMD.




Math Coprocessor 80387



And the socket to install it, note this is not yet a ZIF socket, the processor is inserted with unfit effort, and gets with a screwdriver.



We put in place.



Also found a memory.
The newest SIMM 30 pin form factor



Install into slots of 4 pieces, because we have a 32 bit processor (386DX).
The four microcircuits on the right are the cache memory.




The epic invention of all times and peoples is a multi-card, it carries onboard IDE, COM, LPT and Midi | Game controllers port.


Equally cool video card Trident with a megabyte of memory on board.

The disk is very thin and bolted through the slide - an adapter to standard hardware 3.5 "disks.



Also found a powerful 200 watt power supply, naturally AT format with a separate brutal switch.
