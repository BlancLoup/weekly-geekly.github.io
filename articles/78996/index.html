Questions to Michael Widenius (MySQL)

Hi guys.

A few days ago, a kind person, namely Mikhail Ushakov , practically accidentally introduced us to the person who stood at the very beginning and to this day is one of the main developers of one of the most famous DBMS - MySQL . This is the legendary Michael Widenius , who is also known by the nickname "Monty". Thanks to this unexpected acquaintance, we all had the opportunity to talk with Mike by asking him questions.

As I believe many people know, until January 2008, Michael was the technical director of MySQL AB, and a year later he left Sun Microsystems to establish his own company. The other day, Widenius in his blog turned for help in order to save MySQL from Oracle Corporation, and now Michael is open for communication, perhaps more than ever before.

If you have questions for Michael, ask them in the comments. After a day or two, we will collect them, filter them and pass them to him. Answers, as usual, will be published in the same blog as soon as we receive them.
