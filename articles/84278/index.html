MTS makes it easy for subscribers to access mobile content using Opera Mini



February 15, 2010 - Oslo, Norway

The company Opera Software has announced an agreement with the Russian telecommunications operator MTS (Mobile TeleSystems OJSC). The partnership involves launching a special version of the popular Opera Mini mobile browser for MTS subscribers.

A special version of Opera Mini for MTS involves placing links to the web resources of the operator - Omlet.ru, music and game portals - on the main page of the browser. This will simplify and speed up user access to music, video, games, as well as provide an opportunity to conveniently purchase content directly from the browser window.

MTS is the largest cellular operator in Russia and the CIS countries. The company's consolidated subscriber base in this region exceeds 97.8 million subscribers, of which 69.3 million are in Russia. The company was the first on the market to introduce a range of innovative data services, user applications, and continues to expand the range of value added services (VAS).

About Mobile TeleSystems OJSC
OJSC Mobile TeleSystems is the largest mobile operator in Russia and the CIS countries. The company's consolidated subscriber base exceeds 97.8 million subscribers.
