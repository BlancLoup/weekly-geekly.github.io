The revolution in evaluating advertising



How to evaluate the effectiveness of an advertising campaign? What type of advertising works best for your business? What is the rate of return on each type of advertising? How much is one application? How to control appeals from anywhere in the world? Want to know for sure: does advertising make a profit or is it a waste of money?

The new unique tool Rekmer will answer these and other questions on October 21, 2010 at the INTERNET 2010 exhibition at the RIW-2010 conference, which will be held at the Expocentre Fairgrounds on Krasnaya Presnya (Pavilion No. 3). At the official presentation of the product, you can not only see, but also evaluate it at work, and also find out why the most progressive direction in Internet marketing is lead-marketing.

All those who care about the profitability of their business and want to invest money efficiently will find out:
  • how to calculate the conversion, ROI and cost not only of each site visitor, but also to find out how much each application costs and even the buyer;
  • how to measure the effect and cost of appeals not only online, but also offline advertising;
  • how not to lose calls when the phone is busy and to better understand customer requests;
  • What is the most effective advertising method for your business?
You can find out about these and other possibilities of an absolutely new tool for evaluating the effectiveness of advertising Rekmermer at the INTERNET-2010 exhibition on October 21-23, 2010 from 10:00 to 18:00, booth number A4.
