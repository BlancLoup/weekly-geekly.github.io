Social Commerce day at SumIT



We invite you to listen to what is social commerce? How does the developer understand it? How does she look in the eyes of an investor? What does the user want to see? Elena Abashkina, director of social commerce development at Mail.ru Group , will talk about these and other equally important issues.

The event will be held today (August 15) as part of the SumIT summer start-up marathon, at Kronverksky 49, St. Petersburg State University ITMO building. Beginning at 18:00.

And for real developers, we will continue to distribute prizes. We invite you to Commerthon - a competition for the development of a social application store.

The project "Count reposts Vkontakte" won the tablet on Hackathon .
Alexander Shtuchkin won iPhone4 on the Social App per Day
Nikolay Chashnikov won the iPad2 in the Javathon competition.

This time you will have 100 hours to create something that can not only win the prize, but also get the opportunity to interest Mail.ru Group as investors.
All the details will be announced at the Social Commerce day: assessment criteria, problem statement, assignment directions, what will be the prize.

Summing up the competition, as well as the awarding of participants will be held at SumIT on Friday, August 19 at 19.00.
