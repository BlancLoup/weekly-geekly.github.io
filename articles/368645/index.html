When to wait for the uprising machines

We all love games and movies about the uprising machines. However, the time allotted by the creators of the "Terminator", have long passed, and the cars still have not risen. Watch the video of our colleagues, and read the text version under the cut.



Already, cars have learned to destroy people morally. Back in 2012, the Japanese robot "Stone, Scissors, Paper" could easily beat a man into his favorite school game. And - with one hundred percent probability.

But it was just fun. Today, artificial intelligence wins man in much more complex games. For example, the AlphaGo program recently overcame one of the world's best players in the ancient go board game. And this is a very important victory for cars. After all, go has so far been one of the few games in which a person played better than a computer. But now it is already in the past. Last year, his company created a program that easily beats up a man in old arcades like Pac-Man . Like any evil robot, she knows how to learn from her mistakes and develop new tactics.

All this was made possible through the use of artificial neural networks. In their organization, they resemble the neural networks of our brain. That is, they contain a lot of low-power processors that can solve quite complex tasks. And what is more important: they know how to learn from past experience.
