Latest Chrome Extensions



In my first topic, I decided to give descriptions of interesting extensions that have come out lately. It may be that they are not enough for those who do not dare to go so far to Google Chrome :) Who knows ...


TabSearch [0.1]




Download TabSearch

Very useful extension if you have a lot of tabs open. This extension allows you to make a search among the open tabs, in a single pop-up window - and select the desired one. I don‚Äôt know how many will find this useful, but I think this is definitely a good addition.

Google Reader Compact & Clean [1.1]




Download Google Reader Compact & Clean

The extension maximizes the area to display entries in Google Reader.

iMacros for Chrome [0.3.2]




Download iMacros for Chrome

The extension allows you to create Macros, that is, automate most routine work.

FlashBlock [1.2.11]




Download FlashBlock

The extension blocks Flash objects on the page, and launches them only after clicking on them.

AdBlock + [1.1.9]




Download AdBlock +

An extension designed to block ads, and should remind everyone of Firefox Adblock Plus.

GoCalendar [0.3.1]




Download GoCalendar

GoCalendar is a simple Google calendar extension and shows the next appointment in your calendar.

Smoothscroll [0.4]




Download Smoothscroll

This extension adds smooth scrolling to your Chrome / Chromium.

ChromeGesture [1.0.7]




Download ChromeGesture

This extension is for managing gestures, you can read more here.

Subscribe in Feed Reader [1.3]




Download Subscribe in Feed Reader

This extension adds a RSS subscription button.

ps: in conclusion, I would like to say that these extensions are recommended for the latest releases of Dev build.
