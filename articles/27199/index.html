Yandex.Maps for iPhone

And another news related to the iPhone.
Yandex introduced the Yandex.Maps version,
specially adapted for Safari (iPhone & iPod Touch)
The service is located at http://i-maps.yandex.ru



In my opinion, Yandex once again shows itself from the excellent side, showing
caring for iPhone / iPod Touch users.
We have already seen a portable version of the search engine and weather.

And features and other screenshots of portable Yandex.Map below


Opportunities:
- search by address
- showing the distance from the point to the nearest metro
- various viewing options - scheme, satellite, hybrid
- showing traffic jams (!), Including in St. Petersburg (+ closed streets)
