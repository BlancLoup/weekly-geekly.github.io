The story of the development of the gadget for shock sports manager of the most middle managers. Under the cat analysis of the market for martial arts trackers and a summary of the progress in the development of KickBrick.

It turned out to be quite difficult to find fitness trackers for percussion martial arts, especially a comparative review. I considered 5 gadgets: Corner, Hykso, Moov, Striketec, SportSense. If you have information about any other martial arts gadgets, please write to me.


Corner




The device was presented at the London's Wearable Technology Show 2016. Athletech, a UK start-up, states that their gadget is the first system in the world that is completely ready for use by athletes at various levels and is able to determine the strength, speed and accuracy of the strike.


The system is based on the performance of a 3-axis accelerometer. The gadget is fixed on the arm. All readings in real time are displayed on the screen of the device associated with the gadget. This device is exactly the activity tracker in its pure form, since it only reflects your activity, without introducing a significant innovation into the training process.



Pre-order is available at a discount of 40% - ¬£ 79.99 (6,100 rubles.)


Hykso



The Hykso gadget is much like the Corner. The gadget is also mounted on the hands under the bandages.



The system consists of small sensors and a mobile application, which in real-time monitors all the important indicators - the number of strikes delivered per battle, their speed, intensity, and so on.


An important difference is gamification and the overall rating, which can be displayed on the monitor. During group training, the rating system is an additional incentive for athletes and amateurs to give their best during the training session. In the US, more than 6 gyms are already equipped with these systems.



The cost of a set of two sensors is $ 249.95.


Mooo



Fitness tracker Moov is a multifunctional tracker of various activities. Information is collected from the accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer.


Acting as a digital personal trainer, Moov can analyze your activities. In this case, the gadget understands what exactly you do: running, weightlifting, boxing, swimming, cycling, boxing and much more, providing real-time audio advice and feedback. This is a significant difference Moov from other trackers. You do not just get information about what you have already done, with you a coach who tells you what to do next and forces you to do another round or another approach ... just one more time.

  • Strap: removable, 2 colors are available to choose from (black and white, depending on the color of the device itself)

Striketec



This device is in many ways similar to Corner and Hykso, while the significant difference is the ability to use the option for wrapping in bandages, and the version on the bracelet. The developers have focused on the spamming gamification, this makes the broadcast of fights more spectacular, and the training process is quite interesting for subsequent analysis of behavior in the ring.


In several publications it is mentioned that this gadget has successfully passed a crowdfunding campaign. In fact, on Indiegogo I did not find information about this project, and on Kickstarter this project closed with the amount of 1% of the required. It may well have been used later another site, but I did not find information about this.



The BTLE information is transmitted in real time to the paired device to display and save information about the training process or the fight.



Since 2014, information on the development of the project has not yet appeared, most likely Striketec has not received funding and proper development.


Sportsense



This system is a Russian development. SportSense consists of two sensors, with one mounted on the wrist, the other on the belt of the athlete. The system registers the speed, strength and type of impact. Data is transmitted to the device to view information about each beat and the progress of training.



An interesting experience of cooperation SportSense with Reebok. It is safe to conclude that it is not the readings themselves that are interesting, in kg or parrots, the comparison with the readings of other athletes and the rating system are interesting.
