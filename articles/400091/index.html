New DJI in Moscow: Inspire 2 and Phantom 4 Pro

Every day we are getting closer and closer to Pelevenskoy Hennelore.



On Wednesday, December 14, 2 new products were presented to the general public in Moscow at once - DJI Inspire 2 and DJI Phantom 4 PRO - both devices are new models designed for professional operators and advanced amateurs. Everyone who wanted to rate the new models was invited - brand fans, aerial photography fans and just photographers, as well as professionals for whom aerial photography and video filming have become the main way of earning and self-expression over the past few years.



DJI Phantom4 PRO




The first, perhaps the most accessible, mass and therefore close to a large number of potential buyers was the DJI Phantom4 PRO , which is generally largely similar to its predecessor, but in reality it has been noticeably reworked.

A small copter is convenient for shooting extreme sports and in general for all who need mobility and high quality at the same time. Kirill Umrikhin told us about new features and changes, as well as about the features of sports shooting.



In addition to the slightly modified hull and powerplant with a new battery, providing up to 30 minutes of flight - there is a significantly expanded collision avoidance system.



Now the cameras are located not only at the front and bottom, but also at the back, which makes it possible to make safe departures back and use new intelligent flight modes, and the side IR sensors are designed to further facilitate indoor flights.

4K Optimized Shooting



Optimized 4K shooting and video processing system allows you to shoot video with movie quality in 4K resolution (4096 x 2160 / 60fps) with a bitrate up to 100 Mbps, allowing you to shoot slow-motion video with the highest resolution. Phantom 4 Pro supports H.265 codec (max resolution 4096X2160 / 30fps). With such a bitrate, H.265 is able to give a quality 2 times higher compared to H.264. Recording with a wide dynamic range in the D-log will give the maximum amount of data for subsequent color correction.

DJI Inspire 2




The second novelty, long awaited by the professional community, is a completely new DJI Inspire 2 , which has so far reached only in the form of a pre-production demo sample. For the presentation of this professional tool, we invited an operator with great experience (more than 2500 missions to DJI Inspire 1 / Pro) - Ruslan Prokhorov.



The official start of sales is scheduled a little later. But already in the demo version we had the opportunity to evaluate the increased flight time - now it can reach 27 minutes (with the X4S camera), a much more stable real-time video transmission, which makes it suitable for live broadcasts. No less significant was the emergence of the heating of batteries - a function extremely relevant for our country.



Now officially quadrocopter can be operated at temperatures down to -20C. And at altitudes up to 5000m. In general, the whole Inspire 2 consists of innovations. Finally, a long-awaited flight camera for a pilot with 2-axis stabilization appeared.
