Jam with chrome



Resource Jam With Chrome will allow you to arrange a jam session with your friends.

It is positioned as one of the Chrome Experiments (web audio and web sockets), so it will not work to run this thing in another browser (Fox offers to download Chrome immediately with this application, but you can continue in it, just stable work is not guaranteed).

You can choose a musical instrument (several guitars, drum sets, keys, drum machines, etc.) and invite friends to play with you.

It is better to immediately switch to the PRO-mode.
