Winamp 5.5 Final


Winamp has turned 10 years old. For its very long history, the program has gained tremendous fame, love of users and mad ratings in the vast file archives. It was decided to consider the bright date as the 10th of the 10th month. The release of the new version of the program is timed to this glorious date. Among the many improvements it is necessary to highlight the following: a new skin called Bento (to activate you need to choose in the settings), Album Art window (in which the disc cover is shown), a single file editor combined, support for iPod and other devices.

The undisputed leader among audio players, a program whose name has been synonymous with MP3 for many years. Excellent sound quality, support for all popular media formats (including video), Internet radio and TV, ripping and recording of CDs, plug-ins, skins ... And everything, everything else.

There are 3 versions. The cardinal differences: Lite is just a player; Full - writes and rips audio CDs only at eight speeds; Pro - writes and rips audio CDs at the highest possible speed.
