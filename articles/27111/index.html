Jifeon - 3d graphics editor online.

I want to tell the community about my service, which allows you to work with three-dimensional graphics right in the browser. His name is Jifeon

More screenshots here .

So far, the service is positioned more as a social network, rather than as a serious tool of 3d modeler. In Jifeon, you can create and discuss small 3d models, collect them in collections and vote for them.

The modeller itself uses the html canvas tag, which allows drawing 2d graphics in the browser. Without any reservation canvas supports browsers Firefox1.5 +, Opera9 + and Safari2 +. All transformations for output 3d graphics are made on JavaScript. So far, the functionality of the modeler is not at all great: you can create primitives and edit their structure by moving, rotating and scaling vertices, faces, polygons and whole figures. Attention!!! At the moment, Jifeon can not be compared with any offline software, so do not compare them in the comments.

After creating the shape, you can save it, and then a small rendered picture will appear for it in a place where everyone who you allow will see it. For differentiation of the rights of users there are groups.
