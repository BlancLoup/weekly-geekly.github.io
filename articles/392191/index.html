The book is a tool for transferring thoughts. Consequently, the more interesting thoughts in the book, the more valuable it is!

But how to highlight all the valuable thoughts in the book? After all, AI is still in development ...

Answer: there are people! And a bunch of quotes from every book on the bookmaker website. By collecting and analyzing which you can make a "heat map" of thoughts - highlighting the background more vividly in the most quoted words. This will allow you to read the literature faster, skipping not significant content, focusing only on important things! I myself am not a programmer, for this I am sending an idea to the world, in the hope that someone will implement it.

With the increasing amount of literature, the acceleration of processes and the increasing lack of time, this method should be useful to many. In the future, you can make a rating of books: by citation and density of thoughts. For each of the topics.

Probably, this method is more suitable for developing literature than for fiction. For this, I suggest taking for tests: bookmate.com/books/ttYdhO0S There are a lot of quotes and a good book.
