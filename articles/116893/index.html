Mangrove, Ventech Capital and ABRT have invested in oktogo.ru

Friends, today we are happy to share with you important and joyful news for us. The venture fund Mangrove CP, the global leader in the investment market, as well as the ABRT fund and the French Ventech fund became shareholders of our online reservation service oktogo.ru . Javier Perez Tenessa, founder and CEO of eDreams, was among the company's shareholders and advisors.

An investment of $ 5 million will allow our company to continue work on signing contracts with Russian hotels and provide online booking options for more than 7,000 Russian hotels . Using invested funds, oktogo.ru will be able to increase growth rates, and become a recognizable Russian brand.

A little more detail you can read about it in Vedomosti .
