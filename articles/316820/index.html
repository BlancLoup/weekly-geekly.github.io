Hello. It happened to me to conduct a small survey of hosting services for the VDS service, I want to share. If someone in response wants to share the experience of hosting mailing services, I will be glad. Prehistory


Once again I didn‚Äôt agree with the management and once again looked for work. When you are looking for a job, you have to meet with different people, who for some reason are not looking for a ready-made software product, but a programmer who will write it.

Someone needs an online store, someone CRM. There is nothing unique in the customer's requirements; they are usually looking for a programmer, because they are not familiar with the software market, but there are exceptions. For example, a customer needs a mailing list service, a database of 500,000 addresses is collected, but there is no possibility to make a distribution. MailChimp services are fabulously expensive, other services take the base, take the money, but do not send the newsletter. Therefore, the customer is forced to do development.

When the task was voiced to me, without thinking, I said what I would do in a week. All that is needed is a web application for managing the subscription base, so that people can sign / unsubscribe and the mechanism for sending emails through the mailing list - it seems nothing complicated.

But later it turned out that there is no mail server, and that for mailing lists it is necessary to configure it in a special way, otherwise the letters will be sent to junk and the server itself will be permanently banned. Configuring a mail server means having your own dedicated server ‚Äî that is, regular hosting for a web site does not fit, you need at least Virtual Dedicated Server.

I am not familiar with the VDS market, so I had to get acquainted.
