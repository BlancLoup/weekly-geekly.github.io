MTS and Google will jointly promote search services and launch the first training project for buyers in Russia

The operator MTS and Google entered into an agreement. MTS will distribute the company's search services in the Russian market. On Android-smartphones that MTS sells, the Google App will be pre-installed on the main screen. In addition, the operator will promote the voice search "Okay, Google" in their advertising campaigns, retail stores.

Partners will receive revenue from search services based on revenue-sharing model.
