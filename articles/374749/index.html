Madrobots invites to TechTrends Expo March 28-29



On March 28-29 in the second pavilion of the Technopolis "Moscow" an international exhibition and conference TechTrends Expo, dedicated to new technologies will be held. Visitors to the event will be among the first to appreciate unusual devices, some of which will only be available for order soon.

What awaits you at the stands Madrobots:

1. 3D Structure Sensor for iPad




The Kickstarter fundraising campaign for an unusual 3D scanner raised 12 times the planned amount - more than $ 1.2 million. The Structure Sensor connects to the iPad and allows you to transfer the real world into virtual space.



An unusual 3D sensor digitizes both individual objects and whole rooms. After the model can be printed on a 3D printer or used in entertainment, educational or even working areas - the possibilities are extremely wide.

2. 3D-pens 3Doodler and 3Doodler 2.0




Drawing pens in space is an unusual invention, which has already been loved by amateurs and professionals of fine art. The creators of 3Doodler are the first to develop such a tool, and it is their pens that are rightfully the most popular.



At TechTrends, we will bring not only the first 3Doodler model, which we successfully sell for more than a year, but also a new product - 3Doodler 2.0. The novelty has become 4 times more compact and 2 times easier, and thanks to the convenient control of the temperature and the plastic feed rate, it is now even easier to create masterpieces.

3. Pebble and Pebble Steel smart watches




In our understanding, Pebble managed to become a classic: interest even to the first version does not fade until now. All thanks to the low price and the right approach to functionality.

We offer to get acquainted with Pebble live to those who for some reason have not done it yet.

4. Pulsometers Mio Fuse and Mio Alpha 2




Mio devices allow you to measure your heart rate with an ECG accuracy without a chest strap for easy tracking of the body load during sports.

By connecting Mio Alpha 2 and Mio Fuse to your smartphone, you will receive detailed information about your activity. And it is not necessary to use the official application: most of the popular programs for sports in the App Store and Google Play support Mio bracelets.

About TechTrends Expo this year




On March 28-29, more than a hundred Russian and foreign companies will gather in the Moscow Technopolis, and a professional conference and show program will be held.

The main topics of the event in 2015:

  • 3D printing;
  • Wearable electronics and gadgets;
  • Smart home and Internet of things;
  • Virtual and Augmented Reality.

Presentations will be made by representatives of global corporations, including Microsoft, Samsung, Autodesk, Mail.Ru Group, as well as Russian startups: Fibrum, Healbe, etc.

You can order tickets on the official website .
