The official release of 3CX Phone System 12

3CX, the developer of the award-winning VoIP PBX for Windows 3CX Phone System, today announced the release of 3CX Phone System 12, which provides unparalleled mobility and communication capabilities from smartphones, allowing the PBX to go beyond offices and personal computers.


3CXPhone - the new universal application for Android, iPhone and Windows


The new 3CXPhone application provides truly universal opportunities for communication, with the functions of establishing subscriber status, organizing one-click conferences, the corporate messaging system and a softphone with integrated PUSH technology. In addition, among the new software features are:
  • The ability to easily hold a call "blindly" or "with accompaniment"
  • Multilinearity - manage multiple calls at once
  • Integrate your corporate phonebook with Exchange or LDAP servers
  • Synchronize your personal phonebook with Microsoft Outlook
  • Simple Call Forwarding Rules
  • The ability to quickly set the presence status
  • The ability to visually check the voice mail box
  • Centralized call history
  • 3CXPhone application can be easily installed and integrated into the PBX, in seconds

3CXPhone for computer allows you to make calls much faster


Users who are in the office can increase their productivity by using the 3CXPhone CTI mode, designed to control their IP phone from a computer, to speed dial in one click of the mouse, instead of manually entering a phone number. Also available is the ability to transfer calls with one click of the mouse, instead of complex transfer of calls manually on the device, despite the fact that this procedure is different for each phone model.

Create conference calls in seconds


In 3CX Phone System 12 you can make a conference in a second, with just a few mouse clicks. In addition, a conference can be made from the usual active call by adding a colleague or an external number from the phone book.

Unsurpassed PUSH Mobility


3CX offers a unique integration with PUSH technology, which allows 3CX to ‚Äúwake up‚Äù the phone during a new VoIP call. Now smartphones on Android and iPhone come out of sleep when you receive a message or when you call, which saves battery power, and at the same time allows you to always be in touch around the world. It also allows to significantly reduce the company's costs for corporate communications, since now the user can always call via VoIP. Since all calls go through 3CX Phone System, users do not need to provide their direct mobile phone number to the callers, which guarantees a high level of privacy.

Boost productivity with incredible universal communications capabilities


3CXPhone is ideal for increasing employee productivity as it is a universal communication client, and not just a softphone. Users can see the presence status of colleagues, send chat messages to them, create a one-touch conference call, view voice mail and call history from both their smartphones and personal computers.

Better than standard PBX and IP phone connectivity


The 3CX partnership with leading manufacturers of IP phones and VoIP gateways ensures that all products have passed the full 3CX quality check before release. It also ensures that there are no compatibility issues with the new Yealink T46G IP Phone and Welltech WellGate 2540 VoIP Gateway with the 3CX telephone system. The 3CX phone system (version 12) will soon support low-cost Gigaset N720 DECT devices.
