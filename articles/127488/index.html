Internet banking on Android from Bank24.ru

Hi, Habr!

In the article about the iPhone-Banking, we promised to tell you about our version of the Internet Bank for Android phones.

So, we fulfill the promise.

The functionality of Android and iPhone-Banking are identical, so we will not describe in detail.





With this application you can:
  1. Become a client of the Bank by sending a request directly from the phone.
  2. View the balance on the cards, if necessary, block the card.
  3. Transfer money between accounts.
  4. Top up the balance of your mobile phone.
  5. Pay for internet and tv.
  6. Pay for the services of hosting providers and telephony.
  7. Make a transfer in favor of other companies.
  8. Get information about the balance on your accounts and cards.

Automation of SMS payment confirmation
The convenience of Android in front of iPhone is that Android can read the contents of SMS. To log in to the application and to confirm each payment, the bank sends you an sms with a code.
