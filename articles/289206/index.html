Interesting international events of April

Dozens, if not hundreds, of IT-oriented conferences, exhibitions and other events take place all over the world every month.

For the third time, we collect all the most interesting international dates of this month in order to present the readers of Megamind in one place.

FailOver Conference will be held in Moscow on April 10th .

April 22-24, the Russian Internet Forum will be held.

NABshow , uniting media and content creators, will be held April 11-16 in Las Vegas.

On April 12-16, HIMSS , entirely devoted to health, will take place in Chicago.

April 13-15, the International Conference on Information Technology will be held in Las Vegas, dedicated primarily to scientific advances related to IT.

On April 21, Atlanta will host RailsConf 2015 , one of the largest Ruby & Rails conferences.

On April 20-25, the 15th RSA Conference will be held in San Francisco on various topics in the general direction of information security.

Dates of Apps World Germany in Berlin: April 22-23.

April 23-24 in Amsterdam will host the anniversary 10 The Next Web Conference Europe .

Interop will be held April 27 - May 1 and will be held in Las Vegas, USA.
