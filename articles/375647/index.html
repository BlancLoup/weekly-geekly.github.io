And the idea was simple: why should a smart watch complement a smartphone or tablet? After all, it can be an independent, powerful gadget, in no way inferior to large devices. So, in fact, Neptune Pine - a ‚Äúsmart watch‚Äù with the functionality of a smartphone - was born.
