Vacancy and project today (May 23-27): the programmer DEEEP.ME needs a programmer (St. Petersburg) for a permanent job with a fairly flexible schedule.


1.) DEEEP.ME


This is a Personal Information Community / PIC Community.
In order for a user to get the most relevant and personalized information, it is necessary to study it very closely from the point of view of psychology, social connections and hobbies. This rests on the problem of privacy, as modern people are afraid of too much openness and availability of personal information on the Internet. To solve this problem, we create an interlayer between the user and our system - a live virtual character, which allows on the one hand to study the user as closely as possible, and on the other hand guarantees the user anonymity.
After using various kinds of complex analysis algorithms, the system can offer the user how specific personalized answers to his questions, for example, which film to watch, where is the car better to repair and how many stars are in the sky, on the one hand? On the other hand, the system is a generator and regulator of high-quality professional and entertainment content on any subject in the form of communities based on a user rating system and content.

deeep.me

Who is needed?

Requires a programmer in St. Petersburg for permanent work with a fairly flexible schedule.
Knowledge required: PHP5 (OOP, MVC), MySQL, Javascript (AJAX, MooTools / jQuery).
A plus will be the experience of working with template engines (ideally Smarty).
Also, the plus will be the experience with version control systems (ideally Subversion).
Experience with well-known frameworks (Zend, Yii, Symfony) will be a big plus.
Salary from 40,000 / month, the prospect of growth in a couple of months due to the expansion of staff.
Work in a young team in the city center, an interesting project.

Description of tasks:

Writing plugins to the system from scratch.
Work with the system kernel, fixes and additions.

Schedule:

Constantly
