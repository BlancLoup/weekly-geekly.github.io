MSI GS60 Ghost Pro laptop video review



MSI GS60 Ghost - new thin and incredibly productive portable gaming laptops with a diagonal of matte Full HD-display 15.6-inch. Laptops run on fourth-generation Intel Core processors - Intel Core i5 or Intel Core i7 with support for discrete graphics from NVIDIA (depending on the configuration, either NVIDIA GeForce GTX 870M with 3 GB of dedicated GDDR5 memory or NVIDIA GeForce GTX 860M with 2 GB GDDR5) .

MSI GS 60 laptops have two slots for RAM and two mSATA slots for solid-state drives (there is also a standard 2.5-inch drive bay with a spacious hard disk with a spindle speed of 7200 rpm). The keyboard (made by SteelSeries) has a multi-color customizable backlight that allows the player to specify hundreds of different key backlight color combinations.
