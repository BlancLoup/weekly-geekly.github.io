I.UA opened a blog service.

Ukrainian startup made its users a gift for the old New Year and opened his blog hosting. (Although it was possible to get into it last year, but it was added to the menu of the site on January 10, and today they sent out invitations to all users.)

Having decided to test, I registered in it. The results of the experiment can be found here .

First impressions are twofold. Of the advantages we can note the simplicity, convenience, the ability to insert not only pictures, but also sound, a convenient WYSIWYG editor. There are already familiar for similar services ratings, tags, ratings, tag cloud (where to go without it :)) avatars, friends feed, favorites. Of the features - sexual segregation, as in dating services, the possibility of background sound, sound comments.
Among the shortcomings I want to note the complete lack of themes and design management capabilities, the lack of support for HTML tags, statistics, an abundance of advertising, and the absence of many features that have already become familiar to other services.

Service users are still mostly defectors from the bigmir.net portal, where, by the way, the I.UA developers left last year.

The service, like everything new, podhlyuchivaet a little, but the authors are very experienced developers and soon, I hope, will bring it in perfect order. And although with its rather modest possibilities, it will be interesting more likely to novice bloggers, for Ukraine, where this phenomenon is not yet very common, it will be very useful.

We wish the creators of the service and its users creative success in order to form a good interesting community. Good luck to you!
