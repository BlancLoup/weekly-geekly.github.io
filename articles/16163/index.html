SEO: How to do keyword research? Define TOP10

Keyword research is one of the main areas of SEO. Additional opportunities to promote an Internet resource, such as Social Media Marketing, paid links certainly play a role, but without effective keywords all efforts will not lead to the expected result.
Consider the option of conducting a study of effective keywords using the Keyword Effectiveness Index ( KEI) formula .
IND = (LP x 1000 / CH) where:
IND is the index of the effectiveness of the keyword (phrase);
LP is the number of monthly requests for this keyword (this phrase);
CH - the number of relevant sites by keyword (phrase) - look at the issue, for example, by request an international audit, this indicator will be ~ 1477 (see the line ‚Äúsites no less‚Äù).
It is recommended to start optimizing the site for each keyword, starting with the word with a higher KEI .

In order to get an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe real "weight" of a keyword, it is necessary to compare it with the average rates of requests and the number of sites.
A formula for calculating the keyword performance rating ( KER - Keyword Effectiveness Rating) is proposed .
KER (keyword) = LOG ( LP) / LOG ( LP cf ) x LOG ( CH cf ) / CH
LP is the number of monthly requests for this keyword (this phrase);
CH - the number of relevant sites by keyword (phrase);
LPsr - the average value of queries in the sample;
CH cf - the average number of relevant sites in the sample.
