Microsoft is suing the world

Piracy on Microsoft products, as you know, is flourishing, and the company intends to fight this evil. The corporation filed more than 50 lawsuits around the world against unlicensed trade in its programs.

Legal prosecution of pirates - a new strategy for Microsoft, reports Prime-TASS . It aims to increase its own profits by stopping the spread of hacked versions of Windows and Microsoft Office. These product groups remain the main source of income for the corporation, which, however, is also trying to enter new markets.

According to ITAR-TASS , lawsuits were filed in the United States, Germany, France, Britain, South Korea and Poland. This is the second stage of the legal offensive of Microsoft, which has already started 275 cases against companies that "incorrectly use software products with the Microsoft logo."
