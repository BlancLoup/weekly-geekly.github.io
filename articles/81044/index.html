Technology

I will not describe in detail all the details, you can easily find a lot of press reviews of these points, where the emphasis is placed on the technical part. In short, Nvidia 3d vision is glasses, instead of glasses of which, are transparent LCD screens. While viewing a three-dimensional image, these small screens alternately close and open. At the same time, the monitor alternately displays an image for one eye, then for another. Those. each eye receives a separate image alternately. All this happens with great frequency, invisible to the eye, and there is a feeling of being in the volumetric space.

Hence the important requirement for the monitor. He should be able to update the picture with a very high frequency so that his eyes do not notice the blinking. Therefore, these glasses will work well only with a small number of monitors that are now on sale (At the time of this writing, this is the Samsung 2233RZ and VIEWSONIC VX2268wm).

You can also connect glasses to CRT monitors that support a frequency of 100 hertz, but this is not recommended - you will most likely notice the flickering of the image and the pain in the eyes over time. From my nvidia points, at least my eyes didn't get sick after a week of games. And sometimes I had to play for 3-4 hours in a row. In general, the main difference between nvivia technology and old glasses from 95 years is the ability to play almost any game and a wider range of applications. The chromaticity has become better, much less artifacts - well, it would be strange if there has been no progress in 15 years.

And today it is the best surround image technology. Virtual reality helmets prohibitively low screen resolution, fast heat and high price. Anaglyph (hereinafter the description) has an inadmissible loss of color. 3D monitors have a prohibitively low horizontal resolution. Glasses with polarization, again, are losing their light, which is why there are two powerful projectors in imax (project at least on the moon) and a silvered screen to somehow compensate for this. Glasses from Nvidia are also not perfect - they darken the image, as if sitting in front of the monitor in light sunglasses - but this is the best thing so far.

In some games there are problems with the sight, which comes to the fore and interferes. In some games there are problems with shadows. But, in general, it will be difficult for you to find a game that would look bad in volume. Although a strategy, at least an action, even a quest.
