Survey: do you work better than your average colleague?

About 7 years ago I came across the results of a study on university teachers in the USA. For some sample, respected teachers were asked a simple question: do you think that you work better than your average colleague?

94% of university professors said that YES, they work better.

(Before writing the article, I tried to find the source, but failed. It seems that I read in the paper Harvard Business Review, but there is no complete certainty.)

Not so long ago, my colleague Slavapankratov and I discussed this topic in relation to career advancement and the adequacy of self-esteem. Honestly, it became very interesting how things are in our industry.
