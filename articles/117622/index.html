Economy IT and Innovation

On May 14 and 15, 2011, the National Research University Higher School of Economics (a branch in St. Petersburg) will host a School Seminar "IT Economics and Innovation".

All interested in the problems of using information technologies as an instrument to support the integration of companies' business in the framework of mergers and acquisitions, building super-organizational structures and joint supply chains (CPF & R, VMI) are invited to participate.

Forms of participation in the school seminar :
1. Plenary report: presentation to the participants of the school-seminar leaders
business structures and scientific schools.
2. Section report: the text of the report is published in the collection of materials of the school seminar and
discussed in roundtables.
3. Poster Session: presentation of research papers of senior students and
undergraduates followed by discussion in round tables.
4. Participation in the business game of teams of students, undergraduates and graduate students.

Participation in the school seminar is free.

Financing the travel of nonresident participants to the venue of the school seminar and
accommodation is not provided. The application for participation in the school seminar will be sent to the email address of the Organizing Committee hse.econ.it@gmail.com until April 25, 2011.
