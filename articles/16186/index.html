Can I invest in projects?

I looked at my webmoney wallet and realized that I had accumulated and was lying a dead weight with a little fat.

I also thought that there are unknown projects that, if there is money, they can unwind strongly and then give a decent income.

Thoughts came together, and the question arose: under what conditions would I play an angel investor? This is when money is not in debt, but for the development of the project. And if the project "did not go," then the author does not have to.

Like that:

Investing for a year, monthly.

In a year: a discussion about whether to continue. Legal registration and other bureaucracy (if it is unprofitable, then the project is easier to close).

100 WMZ per month (if this is not enough, then the business plan is incorrect).

Shares in the project 51/49, but I will not interfere.

At any time the relationship can be terminated. If the author is still interested in the project, he moves to a new domain. Within two months on the old domain hangs a large link to the new address.

The desired result: the sale of the project at least a few hundred thousand conventional units.

Only one thing is required from the project: it is necessary to understand why and who will be able to buy it when it is unwound.

It seems to be all logical. But, alas, those in whom you can invest money, they no longer need them. They have another problem: where to take time to develop the project. And the project is dying. Rip
