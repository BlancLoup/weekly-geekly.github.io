Dave Mendlen transferred from Microsoft to DevExpress

He will work with us as Chief Marketing Officer .

Of course, this news could be ignored, but, you see, not every day one of the leading directors of Microsoft goes to your company.

Dave Mendlen is famous for being a speechwriter for Bill Gates and Steve Ballmer, and after many years working with them, he participated in the development and promotion of many Microsoft products, including Windows XP, Windows Media Center, Web Services and Visual Basic .NET. Recently, he worked as a senior director of Microsoft for the development of Visual Studio and, in particular, led the release of Visual Studio 2010.

If you want to know what he remembered while working at Microsoft, what he is going to do at DevExpress, as well as interesting facts about what Bill Gates and Steve Ballmer really are, you can read about all this in his farewell interview with Microsoft. : blogs.msdn.com/b/msdnmagazine/archive/2011/09/07/10207386.aspx



Well, we can only wish him good luck and hope that together we will be able to make our products even better, and therefore more loved by you.
