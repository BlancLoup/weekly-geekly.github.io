Attention, Habrahabr: IBM opens free access to a large number of its resources



IBM has been publishing materials about its developments at Habrahabr for many years. We are glad that many articles are interesting to readers. Nevertheless, we also repeatedly saw the desire of our readers to start working with IBM services and resources free of charge. And today we are pleased to announce that now many users can get free access to most of the resources of the Academic hub, IBM Bluemix, IBM Watson cognitive technologies and the Internet of things.

This opportunity is given to teachers and students of universities, as well as researchers of academic institutions. We know that scientists are making the world a better place, so many Habrakhabr users can now work with us to achieve this worthy goal.

As always, it all starts with the registration. To simplify the registration process, we added a number of organizations to our database. If you are an employee or student of one of them, you can start working with our services and technologies very quickly.

To do this, go to the Academic hub: Onthehub.com/ibm and in the search box to find your organization. It is recommended to use the name appearing on the organization's website.



If the attempt was unsuccessful, then all is not lost, you can get access to free resources in this case too. All you need to do is to go further by clicking on the hyperlink in the lower right corner.



Choose the last option - registration with presentation of evidence of belonging to an academic organization (you need a scan of a student card, teacher certificate or research assistant).

And now it remains only to receive confirmation. If there are difficulties - you can write to technical support by clicking Help in the upper right corner. The section provides answers to the most common errors. The Contact Us hyperlink opens a button for a phone call, as well as a window for sending a message.

After registration will be available a large number of resources and opportunities. You will get access to popular courses, flagship software products and services of the company, such as container technologies, development tools, cognitive technologies IBM Watson, IBM SPSS, IBM Rational DOORS, ILOG, IBM Spectrum LSF Suite for HPC, IBM Watson Analytics, IBM Watson Explorer , IBM Cognos, IBM Sterling Order Management, IBM Connections, IBM Worklight Studio, IBM Rational Rhapsody, and more.

Successes in their mastering. Alexander Sorokin, manager of university projects in Russia and the CIS, IBM Eastern Europe \ Asia
