Thousands of people will decide the fate of the Belarusian ruble WMB

A month ago, it became known that WM Transfer Ltd. finally found a bank in Belarus, which agreed to act as a guarantor of WMB (Belarusian rubles) electronic currency . The guarantor was Tekhnobank.

In the official tariffs of "Tekhnobank" has long been new services related to the input and output WMB. Despite this, the introduction of WMB currency was delayed. And now the last stage of this procedure has arrived: the official voting of users .

In accordance with clause 2.3.2 of the WebMoney Transfer system code , to approve the Tekhnobank's candidacy, at least half of the users who have voted should vote in favor. Voting is considered successful if at least 10% of users with a certificate no less than a personal and non-zero balance on the wallet at the time of voting announcement took part in it. That is, you need to collect votes of about 1,000 people .

Voting was announced on August 29, 2006 at 13 h 52 min 10 sec. Moscow time and will end on September 10, 2006 at 18:00 pm Moscow time.
