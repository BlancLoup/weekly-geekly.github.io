Registration on the portal of public services: simple step by step instructions

Dear Habrovchane!

The attention of those of you who are not yet registered on the portal gosuslugi.ru , offers the most simple and concise instructions for registering from a mobile device. The instruction is accompanied by screen shots. In this case, used the iPad 3.


The special application "State Services" for access to the portal of public services from mobile devices on iOS and Android platforms can be downloaded from the App Store and Google play / Android Market. Only registered users can use the application. In this case, from any touch device, you can quickly and easily register.

Choose a registration form.

Immediately after clicking the "Register" button, the user opens an interface offering to choose a registration form. As we see, public services are available to both Russian and foreign citizens. In addition, there are services for legal entities. In our example, we will choose registration for citizens of the Russian Federation (as the most demanded).

Next, the user is prompted to choose the type of account, which can be simple and standard. Creating a simple account does not require anything other than email. Consider the creation of a more complex option - a standard even record, giving access to all services.

To create a standard entry, you will need not only mail, but also a SNILS (insurance number of an individual personal account), an TIN, a passport, and a telephone.
