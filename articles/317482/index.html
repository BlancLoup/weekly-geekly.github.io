Cambium brings new life to your overloaded networks on Ubiquiti: a new program for operators


Cambium Networks launches a unique program to solve the problem of upgrading congested networks built on Ubiquiti equipment (and later on from other manufacturers of low-cost fixed radio access systems).

In the history of fixed radio access is a unique case, the network can be transferred to a more productive solution without replacing subscriber equipment.

The ePMP Elevate program offers operators an inexpensive, simple and fast way to significantly increase the performance of the existing wireless broadband access network deployed on 802.11n equipment.

The advantage of ePMP Elevate is that you do not need to change subscriber devices. You change your old access point to a high-performance Cambium ePMP1000 series or ePMP2000 base station, download the ePMP Elevate license and remotely download the ePMP Elevate software to subscriber kits. And that's all - your network is faster, more stable and with ePMP functionality.



At the moment, ePMP Elevate supports the following hardware platforms, and their list will be expanded in the future:



What are the advantages of an ePMP Elevate service provider?

1. Large capacity of the base station sector - more subscribers, more speed for each subscriber.

2. Real TDMA. Equal and customizable medium access for all subscribers without loss of performance. Up to 120 subscribers in the sector!

3. Frequency reuse and sector synchronous operation - stable operation of the base with a lack of frequency resource, saving of frequencies without speed reduction.

4. Intelligent beamforming towards the subscriber (Smart Beamforming) with the ePMP2000 base and an optional smart antenna - better signal at the subscriber, better quality of the receiving signal, work on higher modulations, which means higher speed.

5. Detuning out-of-band interference based on LTE networks and other equipment. Higher signal quality, work on higher modulations, higher speed for subscribers.

6. You get the opportunity to plan network expansion using the only available free point-to-multipoint software - Link Planner.

7. No need to change subscriber devices - serious savings on installation work.

The result of the replacement is that the wireless network starts to work better, the use of ePMP Elevate gives you the opportunity to increase the network capacity and the quality of the services provided by using new higher-speed tariffs for customers, and by increasing the number of subscribers in the sector.
