OnePlus sells its quadrocopter for only $ 20



The company OnePlus recently announced the imminent release of an interesting new device. At the same time, almost no one guessed what kind of device it would be until the company representatives themselves reported that it would be a quadcopter, the OnePlus DR-1 model.

Another big surprise was the launch of the device sales on April 1, plus its price - only $ 20 apiece. Naturally, such a drone cannot deliver a parcel, it also does not have a video recording function. But this is not a hi-end model, but an interesting toy that should appeal to both children and adults. The first batch of devices was bought out in minutes .

By the way, miniature quadcopters are very popular in China now, they can be found in almost every newspaper stall. And the offer from OnePlus just falls "in the stream", so to speak.

OnePlus DR-1 fits in the palm of your hand, it can fly about 8 minutes offline, and takes about 20 minutes to recharge. Now it remains only to wait for the additional devices to be released, and you can begin to have fun.
