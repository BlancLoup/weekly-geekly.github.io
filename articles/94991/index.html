Open Atrium - collaborating in drupalo

Studying the market of project management systems was curious, is there a similar module and all of us, our beloved Drupal. It turned out that there is, and not just a module but a ready-made full-fledged assembly, though for now, alpha.
Open Atrium in itself contains:
  • Atrium Blog - allows you to communicate with your team and other users.
  • Atrium Documents - manage articles and attachments with your group
  • Atrium Calendar - share your events and meetings with the group
  • Atrium Case Tracker - Ticket System
  • Atrium dashboard
  • Atrium intranet
  • Atrium profile
  • Atrium Shoutbox - Intravitter
  • Spaces blog


All this I ask under the cat.

Built-in system capabilities:

The idea is that you can create groups and add members to these groups.

Groups can be either with restricted access (visible only to participants), or fully open.

The calendar

Actually divided into the calendar itself and upcoming events.
If I understand everything correctly, you can import iCal. Still, it would be nice to have a google calendar import, but the system is young + it's open source, so everything is in our hands.

Case tracker
Create an unlimited to do list for your projects, assign priorities and manage statuses!
Create a new task:

By default, the status of the new task is always open, but in addition there is Resolved, Deferred, Duplicate, Closed. Priorities respectively High, Normal and Low. Types of tasks Bug, Feature Request and General Task.

The My cases tab will show us all the tasks

And in the Projects tab we will see the total number of cases for each project.

Modified sections are shown in yellow, deleted sections in gray and added sections in green.
We can mark the sections with the radio button, the differences between which we want to see.
