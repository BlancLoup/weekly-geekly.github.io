Ubuntu users social network

Member of the forum ubuntuforums.org with the nickname Beamerboy (in life - Alex) announced in one thread about his desire to create a social network for Ubuntu users. All users of the network will be allocated a space for a blog with the address username.ubuntu-users.org, as well as a mailbox on the domain ubuntu-users.org.

Alex writes in his blog that the future site will be non-commercial, he has already found developers, and he got the domain as a gift from another Ubuntu user. If you have any suggestions for a future social network or you can help with its creation, Beamerboy asks you to inform him about it all in the same thread of the ubuntuforums.org forum .
