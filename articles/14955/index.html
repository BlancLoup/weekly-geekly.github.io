Adsense: Long live checks, Russia so far without EFT

Yesterday, some Russian publishers of Google Adsense received a letter informing about the emergence of an alternative method of receiving payments, or rather, using the EFT system. However, alas, in the territory of Russia it is impossible to use the service at the moment ...

Official letter from Google Adsense support:

Thanks to fellow mstar from SearchEngines forum.

Hello Michael!

Thank you very much for your letter.

This letter was sent to all publishers who have chosen the Russian language in the interface, and whose accounts were registered to a country that supports EFT, but for some reason, a check was established as the main method of payment.

For Russia, Ukraine and a number of other Eastern European countries where EFT is not available, we still send checks in US dollars until a new method of payment is made available.

If you have additional questions, we suggest visiting the AdSense support center at www.google.com/adsense_help . In addition, you can always ask questions to experienced AdSense publishers in our official AdSense forum: groups.google.com/group/adsense-help-ru .

Respectfully,
The Google AdSense Team
