327 000 rubles from the state? You are welcome!

Discussion of government programs to support start-up entrepreneurs have often been raised on the pages of our collective blog. But, since there are several self-employment subsidy programs, I still want to stay at one of them, which, for some reason, has hardly received any attention here.

At present, the state self-employment subsidy program provides an opportunity for a citizen registered with the employment center as an unemployed to organize his / her own business and receive the largest one-time subsidy (up to 30 minimum wages).

You may be interested to know exactly how an ordinary person from a simple Russian city can take it and get a grant for the development of his business in the amount of 327,000 rubles.
How much time and effort was spent, what obstacles stood in the way of achieving the goal - everything will be briefly discussed under the cut.

In each region, as you well know, the minimum wage is different. Six months ago, the minimum wage in Moscow amounted to 10,900 rubles. 30 MROT - 327,000 rubles.
