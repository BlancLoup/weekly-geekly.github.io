Interview with Stanislav Shalunov - developer of technology used in BitTorrent and Apple

Friends of the Cashback service Kubyshka - FRISCO ( First Russian Incubator in Silicon Valley ) - interviewed Stanislav Shalunov , who founded the company that developed the technology for transferring p2p data that BitTorrent bought. Now this technology is used in the products of BitTorrent and Apple.

Currently, Stanislav is working on the company Open Garden , and the application FireChat , which is used by more than 7 million users to transfer data between nearby devices.
