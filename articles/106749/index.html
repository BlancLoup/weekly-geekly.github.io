Chromium 9 and password synchronization

Today, Chromium again broke out into two versions ahead of a stable version of Google Chrome 7. At the same time, curiously, Chromium 9 came out 2 weeks after the release of Chromium 8. The developers managed to reveal a lot of bugs, but this is not important. Finally, to the delight of users, the ability to synchronize passwords was activated (the second time). Unlike past experience in implementing such synchronization, the transmitted data is now encrypted and transmitted over a secure connection. In addition, added an additional protection mechanism for infobars. Innovations are available in the "Canary".
