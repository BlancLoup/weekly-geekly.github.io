Sberbank Online Homepage Design Contest

In the epic (by the number of comments) post about the new design Sberbank Online, many suggested improving the design. Following the trail, several offers were born to help Sberbank, and we (Dmitry Satin and Andrey Sikorsky) instituted a design contest for the main page:
  • Grand Prix iPad 2
  • Four Extra Prizes - iPod Touch
Attention! We do not buy design rights for iPads and iPads ‚Äî you still have the rights. We give these devices to those who better hit the target, stimulating the creativity of designers.
The rules of the game are very simple ...
  1. You need to register (invited not only designers, but also those who will judge the work submitted) - until March 20
  2. Create your own version of the design, based on the brief
  3. Send the resulting masterpiece to the address sbol@usabilitylab.net - until March 31
  4. To wait with impatience, while the Internet will estimate on the scales the sent works - till April 15
  5. Get iPad 2 or iPod Touch, if your work falls into the top five - April 25th.
Competition schedule
  • March 8 - 20 Collection of applications (for designers and judges) (registration is closed since March 20)
  • March 2 - 31 Design reception (designer's work) - send to sbol@usabilitylab.net
  • April 1 to 15 Design evaluation (work of judges)
  • April 16 to 24 Processing Results
  • April 25 Winners announced, distribution of prizes

  • And where does UsabilityLab and Sberbank? Why are you holding a contest for them? We are not holding a contest for Sberbank, we are holding a contest so that our interfaces have the best design.
