# MBLT13 - International Mobile Conference in Moscow

On May 15, Moscow will host the annual international mobile conference - # MBLT13 !

Visionaries and practitioners from successful world-renowned companies will gather under the Digital October roof to share practical case studies that have helped their companies succeed. Already confirmed their participation:

  • Kate Teare, co-founder of TechCrunch
  • Charles Binrbaum, Director of Development, Foursquare
  • Peter Westerbaka, Marketing Director ROVIO
  • Rory Kenney, TripAdvisor Partnership Director
  • Matteo Penzo, Technical Director, Frog Design Inc.
  • Florian Meissner, co-founder of EyeEm
  • Harald Neidhard, co-founder of Smaato

All information about the conference is available on the official website . Until March 1, discounts for the purchase of "early" tickets.
