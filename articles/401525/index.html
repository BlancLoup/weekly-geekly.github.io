Greenlight is dead. Long live the Direct



Valve announces the closure of Greenlight.

For more than four years that have passed since the launch of Greenlight, the number of published games has increased many times. More than a third of all games was published in 2016.


Valve found that, although this model has shortened the path between developers and players, Steam will not be able to lead to the ultimate goal - the system of direct distribution of games. Therefore, in the spring of 2017, a model with the working title Steam Direct will replace the Greenlight.
