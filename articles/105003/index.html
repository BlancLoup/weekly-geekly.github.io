10 Lenovo netbooks as a gift in honor of the 10th anniversary of e-Style Telecom

September 23, we turned 10 years old!
The company decided to celebrate this holiday in a special way.
During the month, e-Style Telecom will give presents to its customers.
Actually, I want to dedicate this post.



From September 23 to October 23, the company holds a gift promotion for its customers.
The first 10 customers of the data center services will receive Lenovo netbooks as a gift.
The action involves the services of "renting a physical server" and "hosting servers."
The first lucky ones have already decided!

Information on the promotion can be found on the e-Style Telecom website.
