Opera Mini 5 beta for Android


From today, owners of devices running the Android operating system have the opportunity to install and use an updated version of the browser Opera Mini 5 beta. This version completely repeats the functionality of the usual Opera Mini fifth series, and the appearance of the interface. One more step has been taken to ensure that on all devices, users of mobile versions of Opera can work in a single interface without having to change the usual style of browser management and web browsing. Further product information: www.opera.com/mini/next

You can download the new version directly to the device from the address m.opera.com/next
or through the Android Market . Opera Mini 5 beta is presented only in the English version.
