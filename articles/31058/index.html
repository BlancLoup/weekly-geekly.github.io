WebOS evolution


Here is our version of the past present and the future of the future Web.
This graph vividly illustrates our thoughts on the current state of affairs in the Web. It presents the evolution from the PC-era to the possible emergence of a full-fledged WebOS in the future.

I would like to note that the terms Web x.0 are used for the sole purpose of separating different epochs of the Web. We are now (as everyone knows) in the era of Web 2.0, and by about 2010 we will safely reach the era of Web 3.0.

Web 2.0 means only one thing for us - much more possibilities for the end user; he is already taken out of the category of viewers, as was the case with Web 1.0 - now he is a member of the information world. Of course, technologies will change to a large extent by the time Web 3.0 becomes available, but the essence should not change as much as in the case of transition from Web 1.0 to Web 2.0.
