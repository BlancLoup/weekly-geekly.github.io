Service menu in Benq E2220HD

Three months ago, I became quite pleased with the owner of Benq E2220HD. But after the first power outage, I was unpleasantly surprised, when I turned on the power filter again, the monitor did not display its rather unpleasant logo for the eyes. It turned out that every time you need to turn on the monitor, this fact upset me.
After looking at the settings in the menu and not finding anything, I put up with it.
Just the other day I tried to find a solution to this problem and was pleasantly surprised that this problem has a solution.

You need to enter the service menu (turn off the monitor, hold down the Menu, turn it on again, press Menu), you need to set the state of AutoPower to On.
Also on this page, you can disable the display of the logo when enabled, see the number of hours worked, the serial number, firmware version, etc. Other monitors have this menu (at least for the G2200WT).
Why didn't the manufacturer make it by default? Or not brought to the usual menu? For me, this remains incomprehensible.
I hope that someone helped.
