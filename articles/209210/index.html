We are announcing a competition to develop a layout for a 3d kidney print.


Autodesk, 3D Bioprinting Solutions and Can Touch are looking for 3D modeling stars who want to express themselves and write their name in the history of bioprinting in Russia.

Participants in the competition are invited to create a three-dimensional design layout of one of the most complex human organs - the kidneys - in section in color. Contestants will be evaluated according to three main parameters: accuracy of reproduction in the layout of the proportions of the adult kidney; ability to work in Autodesk programs; and also creating a job ready for replication on a 3d printer.

More details about the conditions of the competition can be found on the link.

Works are accepted until 12.00 pm on January 23, 2014 by e-mail: pochka.3d@yandex.ru
