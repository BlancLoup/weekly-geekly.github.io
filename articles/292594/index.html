10 ways to "fill up" the interview

There are a thousand ways to leave a negative impression on the first interview. We have selected the most effective for you.





Method # 1

Come to the interview for half an hour later and do not warn in advance about being late. It is even better to come earlier for an hour and go through every open door, introduce yourself and remind yourself. Let the interviewer understand that you know your worth.



Method # 2

Let you be remembered at first sight! Remember that they meet on the "clothes." Dress up defiantly. The girl will advise more cosmetics, toilet water and jewelry. A young man - to come in a tracksuit. Show that you exercise and stay healthy. To consolidate the effect, run in a tracksuit from home to the office.

Method # 3

During an interview, show interest in a potential employer! First of all, at the interview ask what the company does and what the unfamiliar word in the title of the vacancy means.

Method # 4

Don't even think about taking a printed resume with you! Hint to the interviewer that he should have taken care of this. Well, or any other secretary.



Method # 5

During the interview, do not try to turn off the mobile phone. Suddenly your mom will call you! The family is sacred, and some kind of interview is not a hindrance to devote a few minutes to the conversation. HR must appreciate that you are a family man. Such people are needed in the team! After talking with your mother, do not apologize to a specialist. It's my pleasure!

Method # 6

Embellish your achievements, engrave on education and experience, come up with an amazing story of a career path ... and then confuse the details of your story.



Method # 7

Talk badly about past employer. Use the word "tyrant". Blame the boss for your failures. Pretend to be a cute little lamb - it always works! They will believe you!


Method # 8

Behave arrogantly. Show that they need you more than they need you. Become a leader in the conversation, do not be afraid to interrupt the interviewer, he is here for you, do not forget about it!

Method # 9

When the turn comes to your questions to the HR specialist, first ask what your salary will be and in how many months it will increase. Show yourself as a business man!




Method # 10

Complete the interview before the interviewer does. Report that you have several meetings ahead and are waiting for you elsewhere. Quickly say goodbye, say that you are waiting for their call and are ready to start work on Monday. If you notice bewilderment on HR face, it means that he is discouraged and fascinated! In the bag. Keep it up!
