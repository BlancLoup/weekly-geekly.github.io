Lenovo IdeaPad S10 - another baby



Lenovo has been silent for a long time. And finally, they were delivered and showed us their ultra-portable laptop - Lenovo IdeaPad S10. The company introduced two models of this ultra-portable laptop: a model with 512MB of RAM and an 80GB hard drive will cost $ 399, and a model with 1GB of RAM and a 160GB hard drive will cost $ 499. Both models will work under Windows XP. The case of this ultra-portable laptop will be glossy and made in three colors: red, black and white. All models will have a 10.2 "widescreen display.

The company also said that it will launch models with LInux as an operating system and a screen with a diagonal of 9 to some markets. But in which countries and at what price, it was not announced.


  • Processor - Intel Atom 1.6GHz
  • Chipset - Intel 945 GSE
  • Up to 2GB of RAM
  • Intel GMA 950 integrated video adapter
  • 10.2 "widescreen LCD screen with LED backlight and 1024x600 resolution
  • Hard disk up to 160GB in size 2.5 "and spindle speed - 5400 revolutions per minute
  • Built-in 1.3MP camera
  • 2 stereo speakers
  • Touchpad with multi-touch support (multitouch)
  • Full-format keyboard
  • Built-in bluetooth, Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g and LAN
  • Express Card Slot
  • 4-in-1 card reader
  • Battery life in 3 cells up to 3 hours, on battery in 6 cells up to 6 hours
  • Sizes - from 250x183x22mm to 250x183x28mm
  • Weight - the easiest option will weigh about 1.1kg
