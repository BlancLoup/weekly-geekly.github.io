New Russian music service zvooq.ru

Suddenly, many of my friends and acquaintances began using and advising a new music service - Zvooq.ru . Interested in such a sudden appearance and growing popularity, I went to this service - and was pleasantly surprised.




On the main page we are invited to log in with the help of Facebook - there are apparently no other registration options. In general, the service has a rather tight integration with facebook in all aspects.
After login, we get access to a huge collection of music of various genres and directions. In any case, almost all of my musical preferences were present at the service.

A separate feature of the service is "waves" - streams of songs, sorted by unknown criteria for me. Each wave is like a set of music "in mood."



I liked this mode very much - you can simply turn it on to the background, pick up a wave according to your mood, and continue to go about your business.
Also, the service has the ability to create playlists, add music in unlimited quantities to your music library. There is also the possibility of buying songs and albums at very fair prices - about 80 rubles per album and 10 rubles per song.

Separately, a professional look, I would like to note the technical implementation. Everything works quickly, smoothly, does not use any flash drives, and generally looks very nice and comfortable. Technically.

The service itself on the About page says the following about itself:
Zvooq is a new music format. We are working to ensure that music is always at your fingertips, and most importantly, you would not have to guess what else to listen to. We are at the very beginning of the experiment, and in the coming months Zvooq will change. Music on your computer, mobile phone and social networks will become one. Files and wires will no longer be needed.

Summarizing the above, it seems to me that the service was very personal, and it has a future, a very good future.

PS I have no relation to the creators, I just wanted to share an interesting project with the community.
