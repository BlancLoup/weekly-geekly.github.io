But the fact that it is better in some ways means that competitors are worse in some ways, which means their users already have a problem, but cannot yet formulate it. Decision


From the analysis of the problem and taking into account the current current trends in the world, it is logical to follow one or several solutions. Most likely, the decision will be preliminary first. Over time and in the process of working on the implementation, it may change beyond recognition, become simpler and more elegant, or, on the contrary, more complicated and confusing.

It is also worth noting such an important parameter of any decision as its ‚Äúfullness‚Äù, which shows the degree of usefulness of the solution to the ‚Äúpain‚Äù of the user. After that, you can roughly estimate the ratio: the importance of the problem is the completeness of the solution. And it can often come out that a complete solution to the problem of sorting collections of photos with cats will turn out to be more viable and better for business than, say, a very superficial solution of such global problems as the global economic crisis or frailty of human life.

It is also important when looking for a solution to clearly represent your target audience, its interests and desires. Whether they want to use your decision, whether it is well suited for them, how much your virtual thinking model of a potential target audience corresponds to the real thinking of the audience. Different smart marketers advise to conduct all sorts of polls and focus groups from representatives of Central Asia, although they also say that trusting the results is also not particularly worth it. In any case, I think it will not hurt to get a few representatives of potential users in their environment, to describe to them their own solution to their problem and find out what they think about it.
