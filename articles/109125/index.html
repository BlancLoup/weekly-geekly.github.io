We connect headphones


Many in the post tried to solder the second speaker, for which seemingly exist contact pads, but were disappointed by the lack of sound. A cursory examination shows that for the second channel there are also contact pads for the amplifier. Initially, the idea was to take an amplifier chip and the rest of the distribution from another board and solder it. But having carefully considered the board, I realized that other platforms are designed for sealing the headphone jack. What does this connector go tracks from the processor bypassing the amplifier. I decided to check and soldered to it a headphone jack, uprooted from the old player. And I guessed it - both channels work in stereo mode!

Of course, headphones are wonderful, but I want to regulate the sound. Poking a screwdriver into the board showed that the platforms in the middle of the board are nothing more than a volume control!
