Medicine in step with the times: HealthCareAccelerator runs in IT park

Do you have a medical startup? Find out what foreign and Russian doctors and experts in Kazan IT Park think about at HealthCareAccelerator . Names, chips, secrets - all in the material below.




HealthCareAccelerator in the IT park is held for the second time this year with a special scale: the Russian Venture Company ( RVC ) is the general accelerator partner, and this high-tech event will be held with the support of the IIDF Preaccelerator .

And how will this:

Stage 1. Online accelerator.
At this stage, your project will receive remote consultations on the project, including the basic principles of the LeanStartup methodology, as well as advice from specialized experts. If you do not have a development team, but you have good expertise and knowledge of a scalable problem solution in the medical field, we will help you find like-minded people among IT professionals.

Stage 2. Butcamp-accelerator.
Leading experts in the field of IT, medical industry, as well as an IT park team with successful experience working with over 1000 projects from various industries and attracting investments for dozens of start-ups will work with you and your project.

Stage 3. The final stage of the online accelerator.
During the month we will work with you on all the remaining issues and prepare for the final DemoDay, where we will help you find a specialized Smart-investor.

Stage 4. DemoDay.
The final chord will be a demonstration of the most successful projects to more than 50 investors.

And that's not all - according to the results of training in the HealthCare Accelerator, the best projects will be able to go through the first stage of GenerationS acceleration, bypassing the stage of primary selection.
