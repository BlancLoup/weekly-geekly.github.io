Practical part

It is assumed that you have already passed the registration procedure on the website gosuslugi.ru , waited for invitations to e-mail (up to 2 weeks) and received your account activation code.

Then do it!
First we decide what type of passport we get: an old sample for 5 years for 1000 rubles. or a new sample for 10 years for 2500 rubles. (for children 300 and 1200 respectively)

Not all FMS issued passports for 10 years. It is better to clarify this issue in advance.

Now at the top of the page you need to select "Your location"
Choose a place of registration (sorry, registration), all other options are doomed to failure, regardless of what address you choose or what is listed in your profile. Believe me, I tried everything, the questionnaire was always returned to the FMS at the place of registration on the passport.

If you make out a passport for a child, then the form will come to the FMS at the place ... of your registration. Because after Step 3, where you specify the birth certificate number, there will be Step 4 where you will act as a guarantor and provide your passport information.



So, we got to fill out the questionnaire.
But, do not rush to drive data. You need to do this only when you have all the necessary documents in your hands.

1. Employment record or its copy
2. Original and copy of passport (+ birth certificate for child)
4. Military ID for men from 18 to 27 years
5. Previously issued passport.
6. Payment Receipt

If something is not clear, read offsite
By the way, it is better to make an extract from the labor book at once in 2 copies. I will explain further why.

Now, very carefully. Not. VERY CAREFULLY fill out the questionnaire.
Attach your photo at the end. Do not spend a lot of time on it. This is just a photo for the questionnaire. If the passport is biometric, you will be taken separately; if not, they will be asked to bring 2 photos.
