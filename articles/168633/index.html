The issue of recycling waste from office equipment has been around for a long time, but it was only some time ago that was resolved across the country. There are several reasons for imposing tough sanctions on the disposal of used cartridges: environmental pollution, refilling of used cartridges, as well as state control over the circulation of precious metals contained in cartridge chips.

Toner cartridges for laser printers contain a large amount of toxic substances that are hazardous to both human health and nature in general. This fact gave rise to great concern among environmentalists, which led to stricter control over the disposal of cartridges. In the end, the largest companies producing consumables for their printers, including HP, announced the creation of programs for the disposal of used cartridges.
