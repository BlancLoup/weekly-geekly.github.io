Withdraw money from PayPal to the card: Instructions for Dummies

I needed to withdraw money with PayPal in Russia, but I didn't want to work through intermediaries.
I had to get a Payoneer card to put PayPal on this card. How many did not search, and did not find the full, step by step instructions.
Therefore, I write myself.

  1. Go to Payoneer , and press the big Sing Up button ! . Everything is simple there, and I will not describe in steps.

    Follow the advice on the page, and enter the full name and surname so as not to get a card on Vlad Zaytsev, instead of Vladislav which now does not accept the paper and a couple of stores.

  2. We are waiting for the card. I came from Ukraine in two weeks. Comes in a regular envelope. Sending a card is free. In a piece of paper 3 times it is written that the card must be activated, we will not object. Go to Payoneer , click Activate your Card :

    Log in, drive in the card number. We come up with a pin code. Everything, the card is active. But there is little point in this.

  3. We write to the mail support.ru@payoneer.com a letter of the following content (available in Russian):
    I ask to include on the account (your mail) card (number) US Payment Service

      Gave a link to a couple of lots.
