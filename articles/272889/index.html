Corrected "black" tabs and much more in the assembly Vivaldi 1.0.344.24

Hello!

Today is an extraordinary build that includes several important fixes. As you may have noticed, the frequency of the release of assemblies increases with the approach of some important version. So it is - this time we are preparing to show the second beta version of the Vivaldi browser to the court of users. It will be released very soon, and we strive to do everything possible so that the most serious mistakes are corrected in it.

Today we propose to check whether everything has been corrected with the necessary quality in the following problems:

  • Disappearance of custom sketches; Express panels;
  • "Black" (or white, or not responding to requests) pages on inactive tabs;
  • Shortcut Keys on Mac;
  • Compatible with some popular sites;
  • Dependency errors in Linux packages;
  • Placing the mute button in Windows and Linux;
  • Disabled NPAPI in Windows and Mac (now enabled by default).

Many of these problems were often mentioned in blogs and forums, check to see if everything is working fine now.

That's all for now. A complete list of changes can be found at the end of the article.
