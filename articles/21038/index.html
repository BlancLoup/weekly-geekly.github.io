"Poem" script for music videos on Youtube

On Youtube you can find music videos of almost any popular group. For those who regularly listen to music in this way, there was a pleasant opportunity to view the text of songs on the same page.

This option is implemented in the form of a simple Greasemonkey script . He creates an additional window on the page where the lyrics of the song are automatically loaded. The user can even manually specify the source of the lyrics (they say, it is best to use lyrics.astraweb.com). Of course, automatic downloading of words is possible only if the author and the name of the song are indicated in the description of the video clip.

To use the script, you need to install the Greasemonkey extension in the Firefox browser.
