Boom charitable startups in Russia?

Recently, I constantly come across information about various young guys who run charitable startups across the country. It's great that IT people are trying to use their skills to make the world a better and kinder.

In Russia, everything is bad with charity, in the World Charity Index, it is at 138 out of 153, between Vietnam and Cambodia. That is, if briefly - our people do not help each other. At all.

But the situation is slowly changing, so I decided to make a small review of such projects, so that their creators know: they are not alone. So, let's go:
