Short Review: "Russian" Mozart



As you may know, yesterday the official sale of the first Windows Phone-communicator HTC Mozart in Russia began, the guys from the technical portal rozetked took a short video review.

By the way, even in the marketplace, all prices for applications and games are in rubles .
