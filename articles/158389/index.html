Cray Titan - the most powerful supercomputer of our time



Not so long ago, the leader of the supercomputer rating was Sequoia , created by the efforts of IBM. Now, the most powerful supercomputer in the world, according to the Top500.org list, is Cray Titan , which was recently launched and located in Oakridge National Physics Laboratory in Tennessee.

The Cray Titan supercomputer has a performance of 17.59 petaflops / sec, which is more than the previous ‚Äúchampion,‚Äù Sequoia. The ex-leader shows a result of 16.32 petaflops / sec. The peak performance of Cray Titan, theoretical, is 27.11 petaflops / sec. It should be noted that Cray Titan was created using a hybrid architecture, multi-threaded nVidia graphics chips and traditional x86 processors.

This supercomputer can be called a descendant of the supercomputer Jaguar, which at one time also occupied the first places of the list of the most powerful supercomputers in the world.

The power of Cray Titan will be used to solve a number of scientific tasks, as well as to solve problems posed to this supercomputer by commercial companies that access the supercomputer for a certain amount (rather big). The main work of Cray Titan now is to carry out the most complicated calculations in such areas as climatology, materials science, astronomy, nuclear power engineering. These areas of science and industry, like no other, need additional computing power.
