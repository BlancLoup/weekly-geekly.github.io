HTML5 Camp - web future conference



The future of the web and prospects for the development of browsers and web standards in particular are being increasingly discussed. Standards of the web, I must say, recently an obvious hot topic, and now even differences have arisen in the name of these standards.

Perhaps it's time to meet and talk about the latest events, and also discuss possible ways of web development.

On February 17, HTML5 Camp will be held in Moscow, at the Infospace information exhibition center. Participation in the event is free. You can register on the site . The number of places is limited, hurry to register.

Under the cut the details and the program of the event.

We tried to invite community experts to participate in the event who have something to say and who can share their experience and opinions. The preliminary version of the program is on the website and is listed below, but note that it is still being formed and may change (see the current version on the website).

09:00 - Registration
10:00 - Opening: A More Beautiful web , Microsoft
11:40 - Modern approach to web standards , TBD *
12:30 - Immersion in HTML5 , Gaidar Magdanurov, Microsoft
13:10 - Break
14:00 - Dynamic graphics: Canvas and SVG , Vadim Makeev, Opera Software
14:40 - Maps and SVG , Fedor Golubev, Yandex
15:20 - Game development in HTML5 , TBD
16:00 - How to get closer to users with IE9 , Bogdan Brynza, Microsoft
16:30 - Break
17:00 - Evolution of JavaScript: ECMAScript 5 , Ilya Kantor, JavaScript.ru
17:40 - How to measure web speed , Nikolay Macievsky, WEBO Software
18:20 - TBD
19:00 - Closing

(* Discussed)

Also for the event we are preparing a number of interesting surprises, which you will soon find out;)

For those who can not get to the event in person, we will try to organize a live broadcast at its best.
