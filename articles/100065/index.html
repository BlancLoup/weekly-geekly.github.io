Game consoles could not stand against the PC

Microsoft was forced to close the development of a cross-platform portal, which provided a single gaming space for Xbox Live and Windows Live. Owners of Xbox consoles could fight there against players on personal computers in Unreal, Gears of War and other games that are ported to both platforms.

However, preliminary testing showed that the best players with consoles were simply destroyed by opponents of the average PC-level. As they say, the competitive element was absent in this humiliating spectacle. After several attempts to reanimate the project, developers from Microsoft surrendered and closed it.
