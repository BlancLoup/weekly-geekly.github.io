Spanish police detained a Russian programmer at the request of the FBI



In Spain, a Russian citizen, programmer Stanislav Lisov, was detained on January 13 in Barcelona Airport, RT reported . Lisova is suspected of conducting hacker attacks. The detention was carried out at the joint request of the FBI and Interpol.

The Spanish authorities have notified the Russian diplomatic mission of the arrest of Lisov. No formal accusations against the programmer have yet been made.

At the same time, the detention of a citizen of the Russian Federation on the territory of a third country without clear accusations and prior notification of the Russian side is considered a gross violation of international law.
