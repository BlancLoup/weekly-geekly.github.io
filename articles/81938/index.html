Microloan service - WebMoney project in social networks

About half a year ago WebMoney application appeared on the social network Vkontakte - a special version of WM Keeper that allows you to use a WM-wallet without leaving the social network. And in the new year, a common access was opened to a similar application on Facebook . And quite recently, the functionality of the Vkontakte and Facebook application has been expanded with a new product - the microloans service Mutual Assistance Cashier. Now users of both networks can borrow and lend to their friends.

A prerequisite for using the Mutual Assistance Cash Desk is a friendly status on the social network. In addition, in the Mutual Assistance Cash Desk there is a Rating of Lenders, showing how well-off the user who offers the loan. The more friends the user has allowed to borrow, the larger the allowed amount, the repayment period of the debt and the amount on his wallet, the higher the rating.

The possibilities of social networks in terms of security impose their financial limitations: the maximum amount that can be stored on the wallet and the daily limit on operations is 5000 WMR (rubles in WebMoney). This limit applies to all transfers, payment for services and operations at the Mutual Assistance Cash Desk. The minimum amount you can borrow is 5 WMR. What interest to install on the loan and whether to establish them at all - you decide for yourself.
