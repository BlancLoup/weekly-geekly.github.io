Technology and gardening: how to care for your summer cottage in the summer?



Plots are different - for someone it is a few square meters near the house, and for someone - a vast area with fruit trees, potatoes and other cultivated plants. If you do not care for the plants, they will either die or will not be able to please the owner with their appearance or fruits. And it turns out to take care not always - because many of us have to periodically leave.

In addition, the routine work (and work with the land is almost always a routine) is not everyone likes. What to do? There is an excellent solution that allows you to solve two problems at once - caring for the site in the absence of a person and solving the issue of routine. This solution is technology. Now on the market - a lot of smart solutions, systems that will tell the owner in detail about the condition of the soil, plants, weather conditions. They will be able to water the plant or protect it from adverse weather conditions. What does the market offer today? Let's find out.

Smart garden sensor Edyn





This device will always tell its owner about soil moisture, pH, temperature, light level, nutrients and plant moisture levels desirable for plants. All data collected by the device is then sent to the partner application on the mobile device of the owner.

Energy for Edyn supplies the Sun - the device is equipped with solar panels. The system database contains about 5,000 plant species. About each of them Edyn knows a lot, you can look at this data in the application. The only condition for Edyn to work is the availability of a Wi-Fi network.

This is a small system of automatic watering, which can independently take care of home (or office plants) in your absence. "Autoleigh" automatically determines the amount of water and irrigation time, providing the best mode for plants.

If you leave for a long period or your plants need a more accurate dosage of water - the kit includes special droppers dispensers that turn the Auto-Lane into a micro-drop irrigation system. The timer includes a pump that piped up the water. Using the buttons on the panel, you can adjust the frequency of watering from

Another project dedicated to the creation of an artificial ecosystem. Biopod is an autonomous closed system capable of growing and developing on its own. Here, temperature, light, humidity, ventilation and precipitation are automatically controlled. From the owner you need only one thing - admiring and admiring the system.

The cost of a Biopod is much less than the price of an aqua system - only $ 225. Compared to $ 4,000 + it's just a gift.
