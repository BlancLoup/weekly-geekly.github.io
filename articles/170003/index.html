Ubuntu on tablets


With unique productive multitasking, easy navigation and good security, Ubuntu raises the bar in tablet design and sets a new standard for the post-PC era. Wonderful, beautiful and naturally neat.

Under the spoiler video, which demonstrates all of the following





Attractive multitasking


A unique opportunity to present ubuntu phone and ubuntu tablet applications on one screen and at the same time amazing tablet performance. Real multitasking comes on tablets.

Receive calls from Skype, while you work with the document, take notes while surfing the Internet, make entries in social networks, while you watch the video Or share applications together ‚Äî drag and drop content from one application to another with great productivity. We re-invented the tablet, like a bridge between a phone and a personal computer.


Safe switching between users



Ubuntu-tablet has secure support for multiple users, as well as guest login. Ideal for family and friends, for the office, with secure accounts to share.

Protecting information with full disk encryption, as well as additional data encryption, makes Ubuntu ideal for use in the medical, military, industrial and financial sector.



Easy navigation with magical edges


Use all four edges of the screen to navigate between applications, settings, and controls. There are no buttons on the Ubuntu tablet, you do not have to go back to the main screen every time you want to switch the application, because the switch is always available through a swipe.


Quick start

The left edge contains your preferred applications, which you can get with just one touch. Here you can see what is running at the moment, switch between applications or quickly launch another.




Systems at your service

Swipe across the top for any system service, search or settings. Switch networks, turn on mobile data, change the sound volume, respond to incoming messages, or block alerts without exiting the application. In fact, any system setting can be changed without losing the state of the application. This makes Ubuntu the best for maintaining focus on content.




Focus on content, not management.

Ubuntu interface is cleaner, more spacious and less cluttered, because no buttons are needed on each screen. Your information is the main thing. Swipe up at the bottom of the screen to call the application controllers. Without a swipe, you do not see any control buttons. You are free to focus on the content: photos, web pages, music, messages and applications.

Great gallery for your digital life


The gallery app is a stylish, standard Ubuntu app that allows you to organize and share your photos. This is a great example for application design: one application works on a phone, tablet, PC and is equally beautiful and comfortable in any environment.

On the phone, you can view photos, and on the tablet you can create albums - Ubuntu will organize your photos into events by date, or you can create your own collections.

Your photos can be immediately uploaded to your personal cloud and be available on all your devices, including iOS, Android, Windows. Spread between them with just one touch.



HUD voice control


Ubuntu HUD makes complex application processes simple on touch devices. It brings the full power of the PC to your touch devices. Voice control can be a truly convenient assistant.

Use the tablet, where it has never been used before - for working with images, video editing, document management. CAD. Ubuntu brings powerful software to attention on touch devices.


Smart search



Ubuntu provides hundreds of sources on one page, saving you the trouble of comparing stores and finding the right search engine. You'll see information from your emails and your phone contacts along with friends from Facebook and followers from Twitter.

Looking for music? Ubuntu not only searches for music on your tablet, it also searches online, providing choices for tracks that you don‚Äôt have yet. This is really one search for everything.

Neat home screen



A stylish home screen provides all of your most important information, selected from hundreds of sources - online or from a tablet.

You can customize the screen and search for each content type. See what you want with one touch - no longer need to worry what application to look for all this.



Today, tablets are so powerful, akin to ultra-light notebooks. Ubuntu uniquely supports the new category of devices: add a keyboard and mouse and your Ubuntu-tablet becomes a full-fledged PC and thin client, with access to Windows applications through standard protocols from Microsoft, Citrix, VMware and Wyse. This allows enterprises to deploy unified, secure, portable enterprise devices for all types of applications.

Since this is a clean ubuntu, tablets can be managed through Landscape, giving businesses complete control over users, applications, using the same tool that is used on the desktop and on servers right now.
