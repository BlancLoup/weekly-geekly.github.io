The decision on the fate of WebOS, HP will make within two weeks



After HP decided to abandon the further development of its entire mobile division, not much time passed. The final decision on the operating system webOS was not made, now there are all sorts of rumors about the future of this platform. Nevertheless, HP CEO recently announced (or rather, reported, now the CEO is a woman) the final date when the final decision on the fate of the mobile operating system will be made. This will happen in the next two weeks.

In an interview with one of the French publications, Meg Whiteman also said that about 600 people continue to work in the WebOS division, which further complicates the situation. The fate of the mobile webOS operating system has been hanging in the balance for several months now, and now it seems that the time has come to decide the fate of the HP mobile unit once and for all.

Pending rumors that have circulated earlier, will continue to circulate. In particular, there are such scenarios as the purchase of WebOS by companies such as Amazon or Oracle. In addition, there is the possibility that HP itself will still continue to work on its mobile OS. In any case, in two weeks it will already be clear what awaits a good project, in general, in the future. It would be very good if the project continued to develop, if not in HP, or in any other company.

It is worth recalling that for the first time this mobile OS was introduced by Palm. The company bought HP for 1.2 billion dollars in the first half of last year.
