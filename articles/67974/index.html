New Workstation Concept - Flower Bud


In the modern corporate world, a combination of architectural excellence and ergonomics is necessary, but to achieve such a combination was not easy. This concept of an adjustable workstation is inspired by a kind of a bud of a non-blossoming flower, aimed at users of any gender and age.


Such a mobile solution for workstations is an ideal choice for those who need a computer - a workstation, at the same time providing comfortable working conditions. The seats are designed from modern fiberglass, the concept is painted in the modern HI-Tech style, such a workstation offers security, in addition to comfort and coziness, because when folded, the concept forms a solid impermeable shell.
Something like this, the author of Won Shik YU talks about his development. And I think it is not far from the truth, in the corresponding interior the concept would look really impressive, just imagine an office room with hundreds of jobs equipped with such concepts. Clean, stylish, beautiful! True, I will not be cunning, I do not know how convenient it will be for work, compared to, say, an office desk with an abundance of boxes, a paper stand, a coffee pot (or at least a native coffee mug) and other trifles that make the working space cozy. and "personally yours."
