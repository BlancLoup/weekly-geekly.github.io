In this article, we will focus on the ImageCMS Shop project launched in early 2011, since the last update of the system, by and large, concerned him. From the moment of its appearance, the project has grown into an independent direction of our activity. Today, it allows you to create online stores of the highest level of complexity.

The fact that the number of users is growing inspires us to further work on the development of our product. It must meet the demands of the time, be friendly, easy to use and quick to set up. Therefore, we want to present you the latest innovations of version 2.9.0, as well as future expected v3.0, which are left to wait for very short time.

We understand that the main requirements that face the engine for an online store are:
  1. convenient management of goods and orders;
  2. customer management;
  3. usability of the client part.

Today ImageCMS successfully copes with such requirements. Order management is conveniently thought through using the admin panel, you can sort orders, search, view users. The client part is also functional and convenient, tested on a variety of projects both by us personally and by partners.
