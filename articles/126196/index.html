Job Digest: St. Petersburg

Due to numerous requests, we slightly change the format of the weekly digest. Now with geotargeting! :)


Inside, as always, there are many photos of interesting offices in St. Petersburg.

Murano software

Outsourcing + creating your own products. Murano Software is headquartered in Los Angeles. Also, the company's offices are located in St. Petersburg, Voronezh, Kharkov and Tashkent. Many food awards. There is no bureaucracy, but there are business trips to the United States. Employees are loved: they are fed with meals, fruits and sweets every day, they do not get bored and are watered regularly . English courses, an extensive library, table hockey, xbox, cinema and sofas.
The right people : Java, PHP, Python-developers, web designer, Linux sysadmin.









Nevosoft

Produce casual games for Windows, Mac, iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch., Sold in Russia, USA, Europe, Asia and South Africa.
All teams of the company are individual, therefore their locations also. Someone has simulators, consoles and a bed, and someone has a parrot, a flag with a picture of Obama, a world map and poufs. There is a department where there are babies with horns, bloody hands and Qeo - the Goddess of human height.
They are fed and watered, fruits are freely available. Library, English courses. The office is open around the clock.
The right people : tester, game designer, C ++ programmer, 2D artist.













DINS

Development Center of the American telecommunications company RingCentral, Inc. headquartered in California (USA). The company also has offices in China, Ukraine, Russia and the Philippines. The company received the Winner of the 2010 World Economic Forum Technology Pioneer Award at the IEF in Davos 2010.
Since autumn, for all employees - free English courses during working hours. Long-term business trips in the USA from 0.5 years are possible, in the future - registration of a work visa. Non-resident candidates are granted relocation bonus.
Needless to say - tea, coffee buns, unlimited Coca-Cola, cycle parking.
A lot of the right people .











Studio Mikhail Kechinov

Make a lot of interesting projects. Take care of the black crow in the Leningrad zoo. They walk in slippers and eat delicious drying. Organize HackDay. In the office, you can swear.
The right people : RoR, PHP and Javascript - programmers.









Digital design

IT integrator. Office with access to the roof and a view of the Vasilyevsky Island panorama. Tea with lemon, shower, xbox, delivery from the subway, English courses, gym. Fed meals and not forced to comply with the dress code. They are very proud of the team and consider it awesome :)

The right people : project managers and development teams.
