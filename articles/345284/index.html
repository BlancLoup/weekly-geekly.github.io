Tips for beginners


  • Learn to properly formulate a problem. The problem "does not work for me" can have thousands of reasons. In the process of formulating her decision comes very often.
  • Learn to research the problem. Careful reading of the logs and the ability to debug - the basic skills of the developer. In the logs it is necessary to read the top of the bottommost accessory - there is the cause of the whole footcloth.
  • Properly take time to each problem. Two extremes - immediately rush for help and beat us for hours. Try to solve it yourself, and depending on the problem, set aside a reasonable time for this.
  • If you explain something to the project - be sure to write down.
  • When you get the task - specify everything in great detail.
  • Receive feedback in the decision process - whether you are going in that direction.
  • Do not ignore joint lunches (smoking rooms)
  • Get ready for stand-ups / flyers. Ask them sane questions. Show interest
  • Highlight the most important thing by interviewing the boss and important colleagues. Do not spray on the little things
