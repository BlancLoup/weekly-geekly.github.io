22-inch monitor for $ 17 thousand


Toshiba has released a 22-inch LCD monitor. All anything, yes, there are a couple of nuances in the novelty, which sharply distinguish the device against its like.

The first nuance is the display resolution. It is in the case of this model is 3840 x 2400 pixels! Such a resolution, in the dry language of technical abbreviations, is called WQUXGA. However, the rest of the technical specifications are not at all impressive, and in some places even disappoint: the brightness is 235 cd / sq. m, contrast 300: 1, viewing angles - only 120 and 100 gdusov vertically and horizontally, respectively.

The second nuance follows from the first - this is the price. The cost of this monitor in Japan (at first, apparently, the novelty will not be sold outside this country) is a little over two million yen. This is more than 17 thousand US dollars! With this money, by the way, you can buy fifty Dell-22 inches.

By the way, along with the Toshiba monitor is ready to offer a special PCI-video card. Of course, for extra money. For $ 2,700 to be exact.
