OpenStreetMap conferences in 2017

From the translator: the project OpenStreetMap is developing dynamically, more participants join. Conferences are held in foreign countries at which they discuss what has been done, what remains to be done. There are many OSM participants in Russia, but the local community has yet to be created.

Happy New Year ! The past 2017 was a busy year for OpenStreetMap. In communities around the world, not only the amazing State of the Map conferences were organized, but also a lot of meetings, map cards, cartographic parties, seminars and many other things that unite us, cartographers, together and make you feel the essence of OpenStreetMap.

The State of the Map Conference (SotM) is the annual international conference OpenStreetMap. Organized by the OpenStreetMap Foundation , it has been held every year since 2007 (except 2015). There are also many other regional and local conferences called "State of the Map". Please note that these regional SotM are organized exclusively by local teams that run their own sponsorship programs and have separate budgets from the OpenStreetMap Foundation. The main purpose of these conferences is to become a platform for the exchange of information and experience between old and new or potential users.

In this review, we also included some conferences that are not SotM, but they also pay a lot of attention to OpenStreetMap.

Hello to all the organizers and participants who have made this conference successful, and also contribute to the creation of OpenStreetMap, the way it is!

SotM Asia, September 23-24, 2017


State of the Map Asia , the annual OpenStreetMap regional conference, was held in Kathmandu, Nepal. About 175 Open Mapping enthusiasts from 16 countries in Asia, two North American countries and two European countries took part in the conference. The event provided an opportunity to share experiences, learn from each other and collectively think about the future development of OpenStreetMap in Asia.

The State of the Map US Conference was held in Boulder, Colorado. It was a platform for cartographers, businesses, government agencies, and non-profit organizations that aim to collaborate with OpenStreetMap.

These were some of the many amazing conferences that were held in 2017. Let's make sure that in 2018 there are more such events in OpenStreetMap and we will continue to help communities communicate and increase their number!

Use the name "State of the Map".

Want to hold a regional conference and want to use the name "State of the Map" or "OpenStreetMap"? Please notify the OpenStreetMap Foundation as soon as possible. This is done so that there are no temporary conflicts with the International or other regional SotM passing nearby. This is also related to the protection of our trademarks.

Are you planning your SotM regional conference in 2018? No need to worry, but please let us know immediately. Please note that this request does not apply to people organizing map-parties. In the near future, we will tell you about our updated intellectual property policy, so stay tuned!
