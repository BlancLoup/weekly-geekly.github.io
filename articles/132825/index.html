Student Courses on z / OS OS and DB2 DBMS

Dear students, teachers, and simply everyone who wants to improve their own qualifications - you have a unique opportunity to take a distance learning course on z / OS and DB2 DBMS used in mainframes manufactured by IBM and not only for free.

Moscow State University of Communications (MIIT), together with IBM and the academic organization z / Universities, invite everyone to take a series of training events that will give you the basics of working with IBM mainframes, which are widely known throughout the world for high performance and fault tolerance.

First, the most important thing: it's free, but because the number of seats is limited.

The organizers will provide all registered for the course:
- MIIT e-learning materials used in the training of professional specialists in modern mainframes.
- Access to webinars on the basics of z / OS and DB2.
- Access to forums of teachers and students discussing mainframe technology.
- Online consultations with leading experts MIIT on the subject of courses.

Technical parameters of access to the courses and all the detailed information can be found on the website www.miit-expert.ru

Course Schedule:
  • 14.11 - 21.11 Registration of students in the distance learning system
  • 21.11 - 25.11 Conduct training on distance course "Fundamentals of the z / OS operating system"
  • 28.11 - 02.12 Conduct a webinar on the topic of "z / OS operating system"
  • 28.11 - 09.12 Conducting training in the distance course "Architecture,
  • z / OS principles and capabilities
  • 12.12 - 15.12 Conduct training on the distance course "Administration of DB2 for z / OS DBMS"
  • 12.12 - 15.12 Conduct a webinar on the topic of "DB2 for z / OS DBMS"
