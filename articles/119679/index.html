VS + ReSharper promotion

Hello! This is another news from JetBrains! Remember last year's action, which sold Visual Studio and ReSharper with big discounts? So, the action is repeated!

For those who are interested, the action is held here: http://www.microsoft.com/ru-ru/events/vs60off/use-licensed-software.aspx . Detailed conditions of the action can be read on our website .
