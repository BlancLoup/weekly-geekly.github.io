I depend on domain names


This may seem strange, but my journey did not start with an idea.

In 2014, the domain name VidaliaOnions.com was put up for auction: for some reason, the owner abandoned it. Being born in Georgia, I am a little familiar with the industry and immediately recognized it. I bought expired or abandoned domain names and enjoyed developing them. However, then everything was different - although I made a bet, but only for the sake of fun, having entered with the offer of $ 2,200 and being sure that it would be blocked.

After 5 minutes, I became the proud owner of VidaliaOnions.com and did not understand at all what I should do about it.
