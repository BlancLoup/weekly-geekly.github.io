New announcement: QA: Conference: talk about the reports

Four months later, at the end of April, in five cities of Russia (St. Petersburg, Moscow, Omsk, Novosibirsk and Yekaterinburg) the largest conference in Russia on testing software systems - QA: Conference will be held . Some statistics:
  • 24 full reports in Moscow and St. Petersburg
  • up to 16 reports - in Novosibirsk and Yekaterinburg
  • 8 reports - in Omsk
  • the opportunity to see the records of all reports - to all participants
  • ticket price - from 2,000 to 3,000 rubles

What topics will be covered:
  • Network Penetration Testing - from PentestIt
  • Stress Testing
  • Test automation (any aspects are considered)
  • Integration testing
  • Deploy various systems from scratch
  • Experience both positive and negative

What you get:
  • The baggage of the most interesting knowledge that you apply on subsequent projects and a couple of tips on how to push their use through the manual.

  • Sources of examples from the conference to be able to make a quick start to your project.

Automate Java / .NET Application Testing
