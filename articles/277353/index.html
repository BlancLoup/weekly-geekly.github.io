Examination of SSL certificates, software licenses and backup space

Hello! We received a lot of feedback on previous articles ( first , second ), therefore, continuing the series of reviews about virtual servers and virtual hosting , today we will consider a place for storing backup copies , as well as offering SSL certificates and software licenses from ISPsystem and cPanel.



I also want to note that the number of tariffs for virtual servers has exceeded 2000 , and the tariffs for virtual hosting are approaching 1000 .

I'll start with the backup space . As practice shows, only about 10% of hosters provide such a service (at the moment 143 tariffs from 26 hosters are presented).

The distribution according to the protocols and data transfer methods is as follows (FTP is available on all tariffs):
SFTP - 11 hosters (71 tariff),
SSH - 9 hosters (53 tariffs),
Rsync - 9 hosters (47 tariffs),
HTTPS - 6 hosters (27 tariffs),
SCP - 3 hosters (19 tariffs),
Samba - 1 hoster (1 tariff),
FTPS - 1 hoster (1 tariff).


The amount of disk space allocated to the user ranges from 1 GB to 20 TB . Most hosters do not limit traffic.
Prices range from 20 rubles ( 10 Gb and FH1 ) to 450 dollars ( 20,000 Gb ). To find the most suitable offer, we show the price for 1 GB (for rates with a currency other than the ruble, conversion at the rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation works, so the numbers may change slightly). Price for 1 GB is in the range of 1.34 rubles. up to 115 rubles. Well, on average, the hospital has 3.95 rubles.
