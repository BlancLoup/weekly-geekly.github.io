Review budget audiophile headphones

The time comes when a person, listening to music, realizes that the pleasure from this process can be significantly increased, for example, by purchasing a quality player with headphones. In this article, I decided to make a detailed overview of the headphones, which will be to the novice audiophiles. Of the several hundreds of models on the market today, I tried to select the best, the most top-rated and most popular, but nevertheless fit into a modest budget of 10 thousand rubles. Listening, studying forums, reading reviews and analyzing tests, I performed a detailed analysis of six pairs of headphones that should interest a potential buyer.

Player to listen


All headphones were heard through a portable bundle in the form of a player Hifiman HM-601 and amplifier Meier Audio 3MOVE.



Overview


Sound


Bass (10 Hz - 80 Hz)


This is the sound that resonates the room and the rumble of wires rises. If the headphones do not reproduce these frequencies, then you will feel a loss of saturation and depth of sound. Naturally, when listening, recording or mixing, a similar effect can seriously interfere.

Sennheiser HD25-1-ii - impressive bass, pronounced in the midrange.
Sennheiser HD25-13-ii - a little deeper and cleaner than the model above.
Beyerdynamic T50p - nothing to note.
Beyerdynamic DT1350 - ultra clear bass.
Pioneer HDJ-2000 - good presence, pleasant knock, soft hum.
Pioneer HDJ-2000mod - presence at times better, just fantastic, the buzz a little nicer.
V-Moda M80 - strong, clear top bass.
AiAiAi TMA-1 - very strong, smeared bass, more noticeable in the middle, a narrow scene.

Upper bass (80 Hz - 200 Hz)


These are the top notes of bass instruments and the lowest notes of other stringed musical instruments. With the loss of this register, along with it, the sensation of sound power is lost. As a rule, these frequencies contain the energy of sound, which causes a person to dance to the music. The main consistency of the energy of the rhythm section is located in this range.

Sennheiser HD25-1-ii - remain the same impressive, but slightly worse than in the previous range.
Sennheiser HD25-13-ii - again, deeper and clearer, the punch and banging clear, more detailed.
Beyerdynamic T50p - not natural, color and distortion is felt at sufficient volume.
Beyerdynamic DT1350 - poor, but much stronger than in the register above.
Pioneer HDJ-2000 - a lot of extra noise on the transition to the upper bass.
Pioneer HDJ-2000mod - does not lose presence, more density in sound.
The V-Moda M80 is strong again, but not very clean.
AiAiAi TMA-1 - impressive, strong bass, but too bloated and slow.

Midrange (200 Hz - 2500 Hz)


It houses almost the entire rhythm, mostly it is a guitar register, up to 500 Hz. After comes solo violins, solo guitars, piano and vocals. Music that lacks these frequencies is usually called boring and boring.

Sennheiser HD25-1-ii - typical medium, nothing bad, but there is nothing to highlight, they do not show much.
Sennheiser HD25-13-ii - less deep than the model above, clean, with a colored tint.
Beyerdynamic T50p - not stable, and not everywhere clean, occasionally bright, and sometimes too dark.
Beyerdynamic DT1350 - the absolute winner in this fight, clear, enriched sound.
Pioneer HDJ-2000 - the slope of a warm sound, for the better surprising vocal range.
Pioneer HDJ-2000mod - a smoother transition to high mids.
V-Moda M80 - the best balance in the frequency gaps, the lower averages are more noticeable, the upper ones are more magnificent.
AiAiAi TMA-1 - very sluggish and flat sound, buried in the bass.

High frequencies (2500 Hz - 30000 Hz)


There are few notes in this range, although up to 5,000 Hz there are the highest notes of the piano and some other instruments. Enhancing this part of the spectrum allows you to achieve a bright, sparkling sound that creates an effect of presence, but if the energy of this band is excessive, then it hurts. This is an urgent problem of inexpensive acoustic systems that specifically reinforce this part of the range, thereby tiring the listener with excessive musical brilliance. For most people, the limit of perception is 10,000 Hz, but this is not enough for good sound, and if a bear doesn‚Äôt come to everyone‚Äôs ear, then in the absence of upper highs (10,000 Hz - 20,000 Hz), he will feel some discomfort when listening to records.

Sennheiser HD25-1-ii - clear and sharp; There are not many excessive peaks, sometimes the brilliance turns into a whistle.
Sennheiser HD25-13-ii - restrained, very few peaks, pretty dark and deep.
Beyerdynamic T50p - beautiful liveliness, fits perfectly into the ranges nearby.
Beyerdynamic DT1350 - clean and smooth; rare, not particularly noticeable peak maxima.
Pioneer HDJ-2000 - good articulation; stable, but insufficient energy throughout the range.
Pioneer HDJ-2000mod - articulation is even higher, more smoothness at maximum loads.
V-Moda M80 - the smoothest of all, but not enough accuracy and clarity, felt unnatural.
AiAiAi TMA-1 - more sluggish than in the midrange.

In general, Pioneer and Sennheiser are very similar. In Pioneer HDJ-2000, high frequencies are more detailed and give more presence, while in Sennheiser HD25-1-ii their energy is rarely and concentrated, which will be an advantage for some genres.

Listening experience


Sennheiser HD25-1-ii - headphones with a bias toward the bass, without any loss in the middle and high frequencies. The amount of clarity in both bass and high is surprising. Low and top bass rumbles infrequently, but with minor and minor noises. At high there is a strong peak: at the beginning, right after 2500 Hz, and in another range: from 8000 Hz to 10,000 Hz. This is very typical for closed headphones. After long listening, it seems that mids are slightly drowned and are on the side, relatively high and low.

Sennheiser HD25-13-ii - more neutral, more restrained. Everything is as above, but the sound is clearer and more detailed, even on portable sources. Medium less modest than in the model above.

Beyerdynamic T50p - headphones with a bias for high frequencies with amazing energy and lack of good bass. Connecting an amplifier puts them among the best, adding bass, and showing their decisive importance in the search for good sound along with stunning sound insulation. For the genre of rock music, the thing is, with a good top bass and bright high, which some seem to be too "whistling", and some have become accustomed to this effect and receive only pleasure from it. Not stable, because with different tracks the sound is too bright, then too dark. Upper bass is quieter than normal bass, which increases the audibility, and as a result, the high frequencies are ringing.

Beyerdynamic DT1350 - clean, precise, sterile. It is difficult to find fault with anything listening to these headphones. Frequency sweeps are fantastic and completely true. The sound, as if, the murmur of a streamlet, just as gentle, clear and fast. Midrange is the strength of the headphones, while low and high are felt dry and brittle. The main disadvantage of bass in these headphones is its subtlety and cold-bloodedness, without density and reverberations. For studio use, this can be very useful, but for portable it is a noticeable minus.

Pioneer HDJ-2000 (Pioneer HDJ-2000mod) - headphones with a slope on the bass, which overshadows other frequencies, but nevertheless the overall balance is maintained well. The sound is definitely discreet, with chunks of warm sound, which causes a smile and joy on the face. They have a growling, crumbling bass and it is difficult to consider a minus in their sound. High show themselves perfectly, but they lack the same clarity and brilliance, as in other models. Medium is not worse than high.

V-Moda M80 - well balanced, spacious and attractive. Surprisingly balanced headphones, but the upper frequencies are blurred and suffer from a lack of detail; the lower ones do an excellent job, but with some excessive delay, which is even more pronounced in the middle. Upper bass loses consistency at high volume, which significantly affects the quality.

AiAiAi TMA-1 - coffee is dark, bitter and hot, but people like it. Most bass headphones from all presented. The bass is loud, surround, but sluggish. Even if a person likes an excessive amount of bass, it‚Äôs not a fact that he likes this model, most likely it will appeal to fatal haters of high frequencies. There is no vocal at all, and the instrumental sounds seem detached and artificial.

Sound stage


The definition needs to be understood literally. The stage in the headphones shows the apparent size of the musical sound. If you come to the concert, stand in front of the soloist and close your eyes, you will feel the musicians and the acoustic space of the concert hall. In headphones, ideally, should be the same. The three-dimensional image of the acoustic space should not leave the listener for a second. Similar things are done when recording a composition, but mainly the quality of its reproduction depends on the equipment.

None of the presented headphones are suitable for the qualitative sensation of the scene. The Beyerdynamic T50p and Pioneer HDJ-2000, although they stand out among the competitors, are not enough.
