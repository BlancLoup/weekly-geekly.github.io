Specifications modem 1200KN-01.

Information on the technical characteristics of the modem was found in the two-volume reference book ‚ÄúTechnical means of the automated control system‚Äù edited by G. B. Kezling. Actually, all I know about the 1200KN-01 modem is placed in a small table:



Appearance.

Modem 1200KN-01 is a complete device, made in a metal case.

All controls and displays, as well as connectors are located on the front panel of the modem.



The connector JOINT S2-100 is designed to connect the 1200KN-01 modem with a computer. Two or four wire line is connected to the device using the connector C1. The two-wire telephone line is connected to pins 1 and 2 of connector C1.

The TA socket is intended for connecting a telephone set. LED indicators with markings 106, 107 and 109 are supposed to reflect the states of the signals of the CTS (Clear To Send), DSR (Data Set Ready) and CD (Carrier Detect) circuits, respectively.

The assignment of the five two-way switches is unknown. When you press the KM button, the telephone line is shorted.
