The course for freelancers from Seth Godin launched from today

Anyone who is one way or another watching what Seth Godin is doing may have discovered that today he announced the launch of his course for freelancers at Udemy.

He shared a promotional code with his email subscribers, giving a substantial discount until the end of April 2015. The course consists of 87 lectures with a total duration of 3 hours and tells how to start your freelance career, create a name for yourself, find customers and form a growing market around their services.

A month earlier, Seth Godin, through the same mailing list, addressed his subscribers who, one way or another, spent a full day or only a part of freelancing. In his address, he asked to ask him any question that is of great concern to the reader. Obviously, this was done in order to broaden the base of the course and, among other things, answer both difficult and interesting questions and tell about the rake, which beginners take from year to year.
