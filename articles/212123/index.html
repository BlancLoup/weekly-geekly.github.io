Wonderful news from the guys from Izhevsk, just recently started sending devices to investors with Kickstarter . Today, the new Lightpack has become available in the Russian store at a price of 2999 r. Moreover, the price is lower than international, and the price already includes shipping by EMS. The whole principle of operation is described in detail in the project wiki .

Lightpack is an open-source device for lighting the monitor and enhancing the presence effect when watching TV. Depending on the chosen settings, you can either enhance the effect for movies and games, or keep your eyes safe if you often have to work at the computer in the dark. Anyway, Lightpack in less than 15 minutes is mounted on the back wall of your monitor, TV or laptop and highlights the surface behind it with actual colors.





The software analyzes the image on your monitor and sends data about it to the board via USB, which, in turn, illuminates the wall with the appropriate RGB-LEDs with the appropriate light. Most of all, the effect resembles the illumination of Philips Ambilight TVs.
