Samsung starts selling ultra-thin laptops of the 9th series

In April, the first 13.3-inch swallow of the new 9th series of laptops from Samsung is on sale.



When developing a new premium class, Samsung has tried to provide users with a laptop computer with the most extensive capabilities. This good intention was fully realized: in order to be convinced of this, it is enough to look at the impressive characteristics of the upcoming series. The device is built on the new Intel Huron River platform and is equipped with a central processor Intel Core i5-2537M, operating at a frequency of 1.4 GHz. In addition, under the hood are wireless adapters Bluetooth 3.0, Wi-Fi (802.11b / g / n) and WiMAX module, as well as 4 GB DDR3 RAM, two integrated broadband speakers with a capacity of 1.5 W each, one low-frequency 1.5 W speaker, USB 3.0 and HDMI ports. In the maximum configuration, the laptop will receive a 128 GB SSD. On the built-in optical drive speech, for obvious reasons, is not.

The result of the joint efforts of designers, researchers and developers of the company has become the "aerodynamic" style of the laptop. Samsung 9-series delight the eye with its smoothly curved, "flowing" lines. Sports car from laptops, in a word. In addition to the aesthetic value, the sophisticated design has a practical one: you can firmly and comfortably hold a laptop in your hands.



Laptops of the 9th series are made of duralumin, a light alloy of aluminum and copper, which is widely used in aircraft industry. At the same time, this material is two times more durable than aluminum. Countless times the cases of laptops were thrown, dropped on the floor, beaten with hard objects and subjected to other merciless tests. The laptop steadfastly withstood the full range of possible effects and accidents in everyday life, for which he was highly appreciated in the strength test.



The SuperBright Plus screen, which displays more than 16 million colors, is impressive in itself, but to see it in the thinnest laptop is what impresses even more.

The 9 series of laptops not only increased strength, but also reduced the level of noise and heat. Did you not follow the battery or unplug the power cord when the battery is low? It does not matter: a solid-state drive without loss will save all the data with the latest changes. Technology Fast Booting (start the laptop from the off state) allows you to start working with a computer within 12 seconds after launch.



With battery life, the laptop will last about 7 hours. Enough for watching several movies, a flight from Moscow to Beijing, or on a short workday Friday (excluding lunch). Pre-installed OS - Windows 7 Home Premium.
