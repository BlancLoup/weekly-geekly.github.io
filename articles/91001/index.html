Using video to attract new Wikipedia editors

Quick links: [1] , [2] , [3] . Then you can not read. =))

How can we recruit more people to make Wikipedia a richer, deeper educational resource? First, by simplifying editing. But we also need to make our readers more aware that their help is welcome, and to facilitate their first steps in improving or creating an article. So, we are funding the development of an information resource program, such as brochures and videos, that help people join us, some of which are targeted at specific audiences like teachers and students.

Our partners are 27 organizations - regional branches of Wikimedia and everyone who wants to help. Here are two recent examples.

Wikimedia Italia financed the production of a 7-minute introductory video, ‚ÄúLa Wikiguida di Wikipedia‚Äù. You can watch it on YouTube (with subtitles) below or view or download video in Ogg Theora format . Linked to it on every page of the Italian Wikipedia. The video was produced by Christian Biasco and this year it is planned to produce another video.

Many thanks to Wikimedia Italy and Howcast for a good example. Going forward, we are looking for opportunities to assist and encourage our offices and individual volunteers to create these types of information resources.
