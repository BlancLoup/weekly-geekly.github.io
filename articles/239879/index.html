Presentation of new Lenovo products in London

Hi, Habr!

Today, Lenovo in London presents a number of new products, among which I want to highlight the new Tablet Yoga Tablet 2 Pro with a 13.3-inch QHD + display, an excellent projector and a battery for 18 hours of operation, and the thinnest and lightest 13-inch ultrabook in the world - Yoga 3 Pro.

I already touched these things, therefore I ask under Habrakat.



Yoga Tablet 2 Pro


Let's start with the tablet. 80% of tablet owners use them at home, 52% watch videos or streaming TV on their tablets. And, unlike a phone or PC, a tablet is not an inviolable zone - that is, you will most likely not give your phone to anyone, and a tablet is more likely to be given to a colleague to watch the video. At home, the tablet can even be used by the whole family.



With the expectation of this, the Tablet Pro was created - a home environment in which the diagonal is not so important to you, and it is 13.3 inches. However, with a frankly huge display, the weight of the tablet is not particularly large.

Lenovo Yoga Tablets are distinguished from other rectangles by the presence of a convex battery. This is done to make it easier to hold them in your hands - this time. And two - in order to put a more voluminous battery. The battery of the new tablet can withstand 18 hours of work without recharging - the result with the Quad HD large format display is not bad.

Tablet Pro, among other things, received a slightly modified stand. It has a hole designed ... Yes, exactly! For the hook in the kitchen! To make it easier to recipes while cooking look. With a stand, however, a small problem: everyone is trying to pull it out with a fingernail, although there is a specially designed button for this. Maybe next year they will change that.





Why bother with a projector at all?

Most people will lazily connect a tablet to the TV. Moreover, there is not enough micro HDMI - I accidentally bought it after I bought a tablet for my wife Lenovo. It is much more interesting in the dark in the evening to turn on the projector and watch the series directly on the wall.



Well, a nice addition: this is the first tablet in the world with a sub woofer. The sound is very loud and very high quality.

Yoga 3 Pro


Notepad - a prototype of all ultrabooks Yoga. A notebook is a simple thing; you can twist it as you like, lay it on a table or turn it over. We use it with one hand if desired. This was the original essence of Yoga since 2005, when this idea came, but there was no technology yet to release it.





Why else need yoga? That during a breakfast not to drop food on the keyboard.

New fasteners made for three reasons:
1) more stability
2) for the ultrabook to be completely flat
3) the thickness was reduced to 12.8 mm versus 15.6 for the Yoga 11S

Ultrabook resolution - QuadHD +. It will cost 1349 dollars, while Lenovo froze the ruble and dollar exchange rates for its goods in Russia until the end of the year.

Weight - only 1.17 kg.

My first question was about whether dust would get clogged with fasteners. I was convinced that this is unlikely, because they conducted a wild number of tests on this topic. While the details are unclear to me, maybe some kind of unusual coverage.
