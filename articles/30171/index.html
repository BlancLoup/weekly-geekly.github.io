Kaleidoscope of iPhone 3G Displays

Confirming the earlier news about the different quality of the displays in the Apple iPhone and iPhone 3G phones, as well as between different batches of the iPhone 3G, iLounge.com published a series of photos showing the difference in quality of the first-generation iPhone screen and two iPhone 3G ). On all three tested phones, the latest version of OS X was installed on iPhone 2.0, and the screen brightness was set to maximum.


Above - iPhone 2G, below - iPhone 3G 8GB (on the left) and iPhone 3G 16 GB (on the right).

In the photos, where at the top of the first-generation iPhone, the iPhone 3G 8GB screen (left) when viewed at an angle is noticeably darker than in the 16GB model (right). It can be assumed that the picture quality of the younger model will be worse when watching videos and photos, and especially in games, where the management is connected with the tilts of the phone.



On the other hand, if you look at the 16GB model's screen at a right angle with the brightness level set to maximum, the display seems to be too bright. In comparison with the iPhone 3G 8GB, the ‚Äúbig brother‚Äù image appears more blurry.



In general, the situation with the 16GB model is better, because there is a margin of brightness that can be reduced to an acceptable level. Most likely Apple in the 8GB and 16GB phones installs different screens. Without comparison "side by side" for us, for buyers, it is not obvious what the quality of the display in a particular model is the same, worse, better or somewhere in the middle.
