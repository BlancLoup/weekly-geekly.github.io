New head of Google Glass wants to start a project from scratch


Tony Fadell (still in the first version of Google Glass)

Some time ago, Google announced the temporary suspension of the development of the Google Glass project. Then it was said that the company needs to think about what the second generation of smart glasses will be, taking into account the mistakes and shortcomings of the first generation.

However, after this announcement, many decided that the project was closed not for a while, but forever. Apparently, this is still not the case - the company is going to carry out further work on the project. That's just the head of the project is now Tony Fadell, and the new boss is going to start the project from scratch.
