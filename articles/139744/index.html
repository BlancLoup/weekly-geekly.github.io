Farminers Project Academy presents its projects on the first Co-Investment Day

For the past five months, the first projects drove into the office of the Farminers Academy of Projects. They received their $ 150,000, equipment, jobs and ongoing support and advice from Academics. During this time, each of them has grown and is now preparing to enter the market. For the first time, Farminers residents will perform at the first Russian Co-Investment Day .

Russian Co-Investment Day is a unique event designed to gather in one hall representatives of leading venture funds and business angels of Russia. They will discuss 30 specially selected projects and the possibility of joint investment in them. The event was organized by the Startup Monthly school with the support of the Academy of Farminers projects as a general sponsor and GreenfieldProject as a partner.

The Co-Investment Day program includes a panel discussion, a session of three-minute pitches of selected startups and time for individual communication between investors and projects that are interested in them. Each investor has the right to vote for any project by offering him an investment. Then the TOP-5 projects will be announced - those who received the most offers. Voting results investors will discuss among themselves at the round table.

The event will end with a Startup Socials Mixer party.

Russian Co-Investment Day will be held on March 24, 2012 in the MOD Design presentation hall, located at: Maly Konyushkovsky lane, 2. Please note: admission is by invitation only. To participate, you must leave a request here .
