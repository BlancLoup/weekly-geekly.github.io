The unreleased corporate version of the Google Glass video glasses appeared on e-bay.



Google Inc. has long been announced about the minimization of work in terms of the further development of their video glasses Google Glass. According to the new head of this division, it was decided to create glasses from scratch. A little later, it was announced that they would create video glasses exclusively for the needs of various companies. They could be used by data center employees, builders, engineers to more quickly obtain information, exchange data with colleagues and perform a number of other tasks.

For the time being, nothing was heard about these glasses, and they were almost forgotten. But now one model has surfaced on eBay . Points have undergone a variety of changes compared to the Explorer edition. The most noticeable change is that the glasses can be folded like a regular accessory. You can also see that the glasses have a proprietary port for connecting to power or PC - in the normal version this is a standard Micro USB port.



Apparently, there is also an increased display lens, which allows you to see more information. There is also an LED indicator that turns on when the camera is running. In the corporate version, a more energy efficient processor, a more capacious battery, there is support for 5 GHz WiFi networks. In addition, glasses are waterproof and less fragile than Explorer edition.



Unfortunately, no more information can be obtained now - for example, it is unclear who transferred the device to A to Z Pawnbrokers, which put them up for sale. By the way, the first photos of the second generation of Google Glass appeared at the very end of 2015, on the website of the Federal Communications Commission (Federal Communications Commission).
