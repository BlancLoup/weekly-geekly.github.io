DIY: Favorite paper characters

Hello to all Habraluds!
Today I want to share with the community one very addictive and fun way to kill free time and just get distracted.


To my great joy, this weekend I spend at home with my family, not at work. However, this joy is overshadowed by bad weather outside and a runny nose (you will not go outside with your daughter).

Therefore, I urgently had to look for ways to entertain myself and my child.

Without thinking, I began to googling diligently in search of something interesting. I was looking for something interesting and developing for the child.

After a long search, I stumbled upon one original site: cubeecraft , which contains patterns from the paper of various cartoon characters and just wonderful animals.

I looked at a bunch of pictures, chose the ones I liked, printed out and actually started the assembly process together with my daughter.

Gathering Zoidberg and Mario, I decided to look for something similar and came across some interesting characters speakerdog

Toys are very easy to manufacture, but extremely beautiful and entertaining.


Well, actually I found such an interest in an internship for myself and for my whole family for this weekend. :)
