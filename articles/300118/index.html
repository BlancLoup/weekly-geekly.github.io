Forbes 2016: Two newcomers from the IT industry appeared in the ranking of the 200 richest businessmen in Russia

Today, April 14, 2016, the 13th in a row ranking of the 200 richest businessmen in Russia was published . It turned out two newcomers from the IT-industry. In addition, a new billionaire from the IT industry appeared on the list.

This time the leader of the rating has changed. They became Leonid Mikhelson, chairman of the board of Novatek with a fortune of $ 14.4 billion. Last year, Vladimir Potanin, the president of Interros holding, the general director of the MMC Norilsk Nickel, ranked first. Now he has shifted to the 4th position. The second and third lines were taken by Mikhail Fridman and Alisher Usmanov with a fortune of $ 13.3 and $ 12.5 billion, respectively.

Together with Pavel Durov, the owner of the IBS Group, Anatoly Karachinsky, entered the rating for the first time (No. 91 in the rating, worth $ 0.9 billion).
