Mobile phone with a projector shown "live"

Dear Engadget resource continually sends its residents to various events, since there are quite a few of them in the USA. Those, in turn, bring out a lot of interesting materials. In particular, the other day the guys managed to attend an event called Pepcom, where Texas Instruments demonstrated the working porotype of a mobile phone with a built-in micro-projector.
