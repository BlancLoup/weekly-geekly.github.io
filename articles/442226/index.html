This is not available in your country: news that you can not find out from Russia

One of the easiest ways to block this or that information (read - this or that resource) is to prohibit access to it on a geographic basis.



The blocking itself may be due to a variety of reasons - if it is a media service, then most often these are issues with licenses in different countries. If this is an information portal, then much depends on the content and political orientation.

And finally, favorite torrent trackers.

But in this post we will talk not so much about the reasons for blocking, but about the list of resources that are not available from Russian IPs.

Censorship by Geography


Portal Freedom House annually publishes a rating of "openness" of the Internet in different countries. Places in the ranking of countries are distributed according to the criterion "the smaller, the better." Russia has this year a score of 67 points. Of course, this is not the limit: China has traditionally more 88/100. And then there is Saudi Arabia and Ethiopia, where these figures are 73 and 83, respectively.
