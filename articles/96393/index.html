Ruby meeting in St. Petersburg on June 18th: Rack, Warden, tools from plataformatec

  • Date and time: June 18th (Friday), 18.30
  • Location: Politekhnicheskaya metro station, Avalon Training Center (SPE SPbGPU, Obruchev St., 1).
  • Free admission.


Probably almost all Rails developers know Jose Valim and the Brazilian company Plataforma Tecnologia, if only because they use one or several tools that they developed. They make life much easier, take on most of the routine work, but at the same time are unobtrusive and configurable (for example, for obvious reasons, developed with the mandatory support of I18N). At the meeting it will be possible to look at all the basic tools:

  • Authentication: Devise
  • DRY controllers: Inherited Resources
  • Forms: Simple Form
  • DRY-views: Show For
  • Feedback: Mail Form


Warden, the authentication mechanism for Rack applications on which Devise is built, deserves a separate discussion. About Rack and Warden will tell Denis Lifanov. About the tools from Plataforma Tecnologia tell Maxim Filatov and Evgeny Bolshakov.

Looking forward to seeing anyone interested in Ruby.
