IBM workshops: spring-summer 2019 - artificial intelligence, development in the cloud, chat bots, blockchain and other technologies



Hi, Habr! In April-June of this year, in our client center (Moscow, Presnenskaya Embankment, 10), we hold the next series of seminars on IBM cloud services. We invite all interested developers! Participation in the seminars is absolutely free, and coffee, tea, cakes - at our expense. ) At the end of the seminar, each participant will receive a certificate from IBM. Limited number of seats.

For those who attended our seminars last year, we have prepared an updated program , adjusted according to your wishes. Topics for seminars: development in the cloud, chat bots, blockchain, private clouds, machine learning and data analysis in the cloud. By visiting our seminars, you can quickly realize your innovative ideas in the form of services and / or applications from the IBM cloud, using modern technologies, reduce the time-to-market, create PoC for your customers, or bring your idea to the international market!

Those who are interested - look further.


Why IBM?
- we actively use (and participate in the development!) Open source technologies, in accordance with modern concepts and trends,
- we will help you with advice and advice on our cloud services
- we will help you sell your applications (various support programs, marketplace, etc.)

The seminars are conducted in English by experienced instructors of the IBM European Competence Center. The necessary support in Russian will be provided by the Moscow office of IBM.

The seminar will introduce you to the cloud platform for developing applications of the IBM Cloud Platform . You will learn how to develop and deploy applications in the cloud and learn how to use popular and useful services and technologies.

IBM Watson Studio - a tool for analyzing data in the cloud - May 21-22
