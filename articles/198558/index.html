The obvious advantage: Nokia Asha 500, 502 and 503


Three new Asha 500, 502 and 503 smartphones will join the next generation of Nokia Asha smartphones.

Fastlane Magazine is the key to the new Asha platform. Swipe-record your past, present, and future: quick access to notifications, reminders, and everything else that matters to you. Fastlane magazine has become more convenient: control what appears on your screen, follow the news of your friends on Facebook and Twitter.

And we invite you to evaluate new graphic applications, such as Picfeed, Pictag and Photofunia. With the new camera and an improved interface, working with photos will be a pleasure.
