Intertan PPC10 Netbook (1991)

Hello to all lovers of old iron, this topic will be a pseudo-netbook Intertan PPC10, released in 1991.



Intertan PPC10 - netbook, which was produced in the period from 1991-1992, sold at a price of $ 595.
Initially, it was MS-DOS version 5.0 and software - Microsoft Works and RacePan.
Processor: V30-7.15MHz 16bit
RAM: 1024kB
2 * PCMCIA 1.0
OS: MS-DOS 5.0 in 512kB ROM
Software: MS Works 2.0 and Race Pen II in 1.5 MB ROM



Amazingly small and light for that time - 4.5 "x 9.7" x 1.0 ", weight - 1.3 pounds (~ 600 grams). (In the photo Intertan PPC10 near the Nokia 7710)


Monochrome LCD display has a resolution of 640x200 pixels, the physical size of the screen - 2.75 "x 7".
The netbook has a slot for PCMCIA cards, serial and parallel ports.
Power is produced from two conventional AA batteries (rechargeable batteries).
