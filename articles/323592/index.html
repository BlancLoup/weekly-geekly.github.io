Recruitment to St. Petersburg Academic University

Traditionally we inform about the opening of the recruitment to the department of mathematical and information technologies .



Academic University has existed since 2008. During this time, we managed to open a graduate school, magistracy and undergraduate (yes, in that order); become a National Research University; win a megagrant on bioinformatics and much more. In this post, we will talk about how to act and what has happened with us during the year.

Undergraduate


In 2016, it was decided to increase the enrollment to 80 people, thanks to this, we were able to receive a greater number of applicants on the Unified State Exam (last year almost all the seats were occupied by the winners and prize-winners of the All-Russian Olympiads). As a result, the average score received has fallen slightly, but we are still in the top three for the third year in a row on the results of recruitment.

This year we recruit 60 people - 36 programmers and 24 physicists.

Students from the first set have already grown to the third year and were divided into 7 areas of training:

  • Algorithmic Bioniformatika
  • Machine learning and data analysis
  • Cloud technologies
  • Industrial Programming
  • Programming mobile robots
  • Theoretical Informatics
  • Programming languages

At the same time, it is worth noting that we are trying to introduce an unusual system of education (similar to the western one), in which students do not have compulsory subjects in the schedule. A set of subjects, as well as which semester to listen to, students choose individually, together with the curators.

A year ago we recorded a short video about our undergraduate degree. It seems that during this time he has not lost relevance.
