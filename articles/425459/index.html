Upgrading to Windows 1809 (sometimes) destroys all files in the profile

Colleagues in the overseas shop warn ( 1 , 2 ) of the presence of a serious bug in the installer Windows 1809.

At the moment it is known that you will lose files from your profile (My Documents, Images, Desktop, etc.) if you have profile redirection enabled for your profile to another location or they have been synchronized with OneDrive.
