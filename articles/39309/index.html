Eee PC 901 for $ 500? Give two!

ASUS has lowered the cost of its netbook Eee PC 901 from $ 599 to $ 499. At least you can buy a netbook for this money on Amazon.com.

What caused this step is not clear. The desire to at least a little equal with Acer (price Aspire One fell to $ 349)? Or low demand for the 901s? And maybe this is a short-term action? However, it does not matter. The main thing is that today the 8.9-inch Eee PC 901 with a 1.6-gigahertz Intel Atom, 1 GB of RAM and 12 GB SSD-drive costs $ 499, which is a hundred cheaper than the previous price tag.

I wonder how this cost reduction will affect the price of a netbook in our area?
