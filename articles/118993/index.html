Unbricker for Gemei A330

As you know, our freshly purchased gemei do not have a radio module on board. Maybe because of this, and maybe for other reasons, when flashing the device, there may be trouble that can turn the device into a brick. In my case, after flashing the device hung when trying to write to it any files directly. The copying process just hung, and then completely crashed with an error. So it turned out that I did not have the opportunity to flash to another version of the firmware.

But thanks to the hope of the whole dingoo \ gemei-community - the creator of the alternative Dingux firmware - BooBoo , there was an output in the form of unbrick (by the way, there is no way to do a hard reset without dancing with a tambourine). How to return the rattling handheld to life can be found under the cut.

The first and, perhaps, the most disgusting feature of the anbriker is that it will work only from Windows XP x32, since no one bothered to write drivers for Chinachip USB Loader for other OSes. In general, we rejoice in what we have.
