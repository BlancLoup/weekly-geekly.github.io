Own company in the USA without a headache

One of the leaders in processing plastic cards in the United States Stripe offers developers a super-service: comprehensive business registration in the United States. The service is called Atlas.

What is included:
  • Actually, the new company in the United States.
  • Registration in tax and bank account
  • Naturally, Stripe account for receiving plastic cards online
  • Legal and tax advice
  • AWS loan as much as $ 15,000


Well, and the most important thing: for all this happiness, they ask for only $ 500, and in order to open an account in person in the USA it is not necessary to go!
This is literally a revolution, honestly.

The offer looks tempting to impossibility! Not anyone, but one of the leaders of the near-IT business in the United States offers to solve all the problems of developers who have decided to move to a new level. It is worth noting that this is a complex proposal that allows you to immediately build a legal infrastructure suitable for finding investments in an IT startup. There is everything - and the company is registered in the United States, especially in the state of Delaware, with the most comfortable legislation for the investor / founder. And immediately all issues are solved with the acceptance of payments worldwide.

What will happen with legal advice is an interesting question, I suspect that they will be ready to answer only a couple of some kind of general light questions, and all serious topics for a fee. But even so, it is more than anything! The lawyers there are Orrick and PwC - also super-offices, comments are superfluous.

Another of the interesting things: an account with Silicon Valley Bank is free for 24 months. Usually you have to pay for it ...

The service is not public yet, you need to request an invite .
If there is a startup who wants to try it - he is ready to take part in advice, conversations with bourgeoisie and others, I myself am interested!
