We now turn to one of the most interesting moments - the keyboard. More precisely, to the "keyboard surface". YOGA BOOK is equipped with a smart backlit keyboard surface. Lenovo called it HALO keyboard. When keys are not used, the surface is matt black. Tactile sensations ... honestly? At first it is not very familiar, in the future it is convenient. How? Everything is simple, it turns out, the keyboard is learning. It remembers pressing, position of fingers, speed, typographical errors and adapts to style of printing of the owner.



Over time, printing becomes easier, the speed increases significantly. We conducted tests and it turned out that the speed of printing on HALO is higher than on a conventional keyboard, but not immediately - you need to get used to and train your ‚Äúiron friend‚Äù!

Q: When will YOGA BOOK be on sale?
A: Sales will begin in September.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: The recommended price is $ 599 for the Wi-Fi version (Win 10) and $ 699 for the 4G + Wi-Fi version (Win 10)

Q: Can the stylus and YOGA BOOK translate handwriting into typewritten text?
A: Not yet. This is a question, rather, to software manufacturers.

Q: Can I use the stylus to write on the screen?
A: Real Pen is created for Create Pad, the two of them are able to recognize the strength and frequency of clicks. Of course, Real Pen can be used on the screen, but only as your own finger =)

Q: Why not immediately make it possible to draw / write on the screen? Why these notebooks?
A: Drawing on Create Pad you do not block your screen with your hand. A bunch of paper and Real Pen gives a more natural feeling (and result) than drawing on glass.

A: There is no battery in Real Pen, it does not need to be charged.
