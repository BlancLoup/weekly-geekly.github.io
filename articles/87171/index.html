Let's make applications free for Russia!

Unfortunately, paid applications on the Android Market are not yet available to users from Russia, so you have to either settle for free or ‚Äútake it from other sources.‚Äù

Recently wrote a program that allows you to block the launch on Android of any selected programs . At your request: Gmail, SMS, Google Talk, Mail, Market ... yes, whatever!
Those. for normal operation, you can use the Unlock Pattern or leave only the keypad lock at all, but when it starts up, say, gmail, you will need to enter a password. Repeated entry is required only after turning off the screen.

I wanted to somehow thank myself and release the program for $ 0.99 - see how it goes. It goes well =) Many people write that they have been waiting for such a program for a very long time.

In order not to force Russians to engage in piracy (security-related applications must be regularly updated), I thought up to make a demo-version free for anyone with Russian enabled on the phone .

Just like that - download and use without restrictions. Do not forget to update to the latest versions and leave feedback on the Market - good karma is the most valuable thing there.

Actually, the main thing: a call to the developers . It's so easy! You can come up with many ways to solve the problem and make happy advanced compatriots, allowing you to fully use limited versions. Anyway, there is no possibility to officially buy.

The idea was spied in Far Manager 1.x - there you could enter the current day of the week in Russian letters as a registration key.
