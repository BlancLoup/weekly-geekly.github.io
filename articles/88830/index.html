Today I want to boast of modding my flash drive =)
And it was all so ... I had a free evening, I wanted to eat something delicious, - and only polymer clay was at hand ...





Who does not know - polymer clay - an excellent material for creativity, modding, killing time, kneading hands, and just for the soul. If in childhood, sculpturing from plasticine, you dreamed of making your sculptures more durable - polymer clay is for you.
According to its properties, such clay very much resembles clay and is sold in different colors (there is even translucent and with phosphorus). In order for the clay to harden, it must be baked in a conventional oven or cooked. The finished product according to its qualities (I will not say a chemical formula, chemistry was my least favorite subject) is plastic. After that, the product can be sanded / polished and so on and so on ...

In order to make such a flash drive, first mix the clay in different colors to choose the right one - more or less edible color.



Then we make cookies ourselves and cut out * the door * for the flash drive







After that - bake, so that the cookies become hard. Next * the doors * cut from the cookies are glued to the flash drive itself and paint the flash drive itself in the color of the cream, which will be in the cookie.

Then, we put a light clay filling of suitable thickness between the cookies.
Now you have to bake the cream - and it's ready. Last time we put the dessert in the oven (the main thing is NOT TO FORGET to remove the USB flash drive !!) and after the clay has cooled down - that's it! enjoy your meal (just be careful not to break your teeth))








And finally - a few obvious things that you can not see (For those who decide to do something similar):
1. Thin parts tend to break.
2. You need to do everything naturally - first the dough, then the cakes, and at the very end the cream
3. Different clay baking temperatures - different colors
4. Measure seven times - cut once
5. If you have not measured seven times, and have already begun to cut - cut as if you have measured 70 times =)
6. For an even cut - freeze the clay
7. It is useful to leave some clay of the color that the product is made of.

I will be glad to advice and constructive criticism =)
