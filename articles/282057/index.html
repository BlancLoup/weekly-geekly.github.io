Xamarin Development Workshop: Technology Overview and Dive into Solution Development



Friends, super news for all fans of cross-platform technologies and participants of DevCon 2016 - on the first day of the conference a master class on Xamarin will be held!

Microsoft experts and representatives of the Xamarin-community in Russia - partner companies who have mastered the technology and are ready to share real-world development stories of various cross-platform solutions will take part in the master class.

We remind that this year we offer simplified participation in DevCon 2016 with a new category of Guest Pass tickets for 2500 rubles , which include transfer to the venue, attendance of all reports and workshops of the first day of the conference, and an interactive exhibition!

The Xamarin master class is opened by a Microsoft technology evangelist from Belarus, a certified Xamarin developer - Anton Shunkov .

Anton will tell what is happening with the technology now and where the cross-platform development is moving with Xamarin, what to do to those who still do not believe that the tools are free , and what nuances are lurking for those C # developers who are only planning to try platform development.






Denis Kretov - Technical Director of Notissimus will continue the master class with a story about the development of mobile applications using Xamarin and MvvmCross . In addition to the participants of the master class, Denis prepared a demo, the source code of which will immediately be available on GItHub. So try to repeat the speaker‚Äôs actions and ask any questions you may have without leaving the room.








The head of the development department at Binwell, Vyacheslav Chernikov, will complete the master class by immersing the participants in the advanced features of the Xamarin technology, namely the development of applications for the Apple Watch and Google Wear . Vyacheslav has a long history of developing cross-platform applications on various frameworks and is happy to share his experience and answer the questions of the participants.
