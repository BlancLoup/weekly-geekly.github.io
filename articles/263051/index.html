Federal Business Intensive Development Program GenerationS has selected IBM Cloud to create an online platform



IBM and GVA LaunchGurus have developed a cloud solution that will provide access to a unique educational online platform for more than two thousand entrepreneurs from all over Russia as part of the first stage of the federal accelerator of technology startups GenerationS, organized by RVC. The new solution is deployed on the IBM Cloud cloud infrastructure, the distinctive features of which are flexibility, ease of management and scalability.

Pre-accelerator GenerationS is an educational program in which 2159 participants acquire basic knowledge in the field of technological entrepreneurship and refine their projects. An important part of the pre-accelerator is an online educational program, which consists of eight stages, each of which includes video lectures, test items, work of entrepreneurs on their project, expert advice and mentors. Training takes place through an online educational platform, where each student (startup) has his own personal account, which records the progress in mastering the educational program. The personal cabinets of experts and mentors show the results of those start-ups for which they act as individual teachers. The system was developed by GVA LaunchGurus and RVK specifically for the GenerationS pre-accelerator.

GenerationS is a federal accelerator of technology startups, during which the best projects selected by the results of a multi-stage expertise receive intensive development and wide opportunities for attracting investments. GenerationS has been held by RVC since 2013 with the support of Russian corporations, development institutions, and representatives of venture infrastructure.
Since 2015, GenerationS resources have been used to create corporate and industry accelerators in close cooperation with large Russian corporations. Corporations are directly involved in the selection, examination of projects and acceleration of startups participating in GenerationS, based on their needs for innovative developments.
GenerationS-2015 is conducted in seven industry-specific areas (tracks): Power & Energy, Telecom Idea, Robotics, Aerospace, SmartCity, Oil & Gas, BiotechMed. All participants of corporate accelerators will be given the opportunity to receive a grant in the amount of 1 million rubles from the Foundation for Assistance to the Development of Small Enterprises in the Scientific and Technical Sphere. Three winners of GenerationS will share the prize pool from RVC and partners, the minimum size of the prize fund is 5 million rubles.




IBM is the General Partner of GenerationS Pre-Accelerator in 2015. According to the results of the Preaccelerator, IBM experts will select the 5 most promising startups in the field of cloud technologies to participate in the IBM Global Entrepreneur program. More information about the IBM program can be obtained at the link: ibm.biz/CloudStartup .
