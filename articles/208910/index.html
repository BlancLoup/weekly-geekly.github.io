Torrent server Demonoid back to life



It seems that one of the largest torrent servers in the world Demonoid, has come back to life. This torrent server is associated with approximately 400 thousand torrent files and more than a million peers. So far it is unclear when the entire resource will return to a full life, but the distribution of the torrent, in any case, came to life.

It is worth recalling that the resource went off-line in August 2012, and only recently a page appeared on the domain with the message that Demonoid will soon return to life. So far this stub is hanging, but the torrent tracker is already working.

In addition, the resource owners recently announced their intention to bring the Demonoid back to life by confirming the rumors circulating on the Web. A few hours earlier, the address http: // inferno.demonoid.com.3396/, which now links 388,321 torrent files and 1.3 million people, began to work.

Currently, the work of torrents is only through this url and port. Previously, Demonoid used various addresses and ports to keep the resource running. Hosting provider tracker also changed from Ukrainian to Swedish.

In general, it remains only to wait for the resource to return to full life, including the home page.
