Generosity as a new trend: Jack Dorsey gives Twitter securities of $ 200 million to his employees


Photo: Kimberly White / Getty Images

Head of microblogging service Twitter Twitter Dorsey (Jack Dorsey) decided to give employees the company's shares in the amount of $ 200 million. This is about 1% of all shares issued at the moment. Dorsey wrote about his decision in his account.



At the same time, he also said that it would be better to be content with a small part of something large than with a large part of a small one. Dorsey said he was confident that the whole team could make Twitter big.



This is not the first case of philanthropy on the part of Dorsey. Earlier, he allocated shares for $ 40 million, providing securities to charitable funds.
