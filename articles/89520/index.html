So, HTC has introduced its new communicators. And soon their sales in Russia will begin. HTC Legend starts April 1 at 0:00.
In anticipation of these events - a press release and specifications of new products.



Let's start with the Legend, as it is already waiting for the day to day in Russia.

1) HTC Legend



HTC Legend with an updated HTC Sense interface and cutting-edge design takes Android phones to a higher level.

The HTC Legend compact, single-piece aluminum case incorporates an improved HTC Sense interface. Still organized around contacts, not apps, the enhanced HTC Sense includes a new app and a HTC widget called HTC Friend Stream. Friend Stream seamlessly combines all channels of communication, including Facebook, Twitter and Flickr into a single organized stream of updates. In addition to Friend Stream, contacts can be combined into groups, such as groups of friends, colleagues, or on any principle, convenient to the user. Thus, the user can communicate at the same time only with the group that is relevant for him right now.
In addition, the updated interface HTC Sense offers a new application and widget for reading RSS news, as well as a new desktop interface with seven screens, which are presented on the general screen in the form of small icons, which ensures quick and easy access to a specific screen. Improved a number of existing applications, such as a web browser, email client and others.
The unusual design of the HTC Legend organically fit 3.2-inch AMOLED HVGA display and optical trackball.
