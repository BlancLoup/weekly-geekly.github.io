Mass Effect 2 will be available on January 27 exclusively in the stores of the Hitzona chain.

6 flagship stores of the Hitzona chain a day before the official release will begin selling the second part of the trilogy for the PC platform. Exactly at 18 o'clock Moscow time you can not only become the first happy owners of Mass Effect 2, but also receive as a gift a branded mouse pad with the symbols of the game.

The promotion will take place in the following stores of the Hitzona chain:

* TC Evropeisky, Moscow, Kievsky Station Square, possession 1
* TC Metropolis, Leningradskoye Highway, d.16a, p.4
* TC Ladia Mitino, Moscow, Ul. 34/29 Dubravnaya

* TC Kaluzhsky, Moscow, Profsoyuznaya, d. 61, k. A
* Cinema October, Moscow, st. Novy Arbat, 24
