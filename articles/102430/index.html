Market software for smartphones: 3.9 billion downloads in six months

The volume of the software market for smartphones is growing at an unprecedented rate. According to the German research firm Research2guidance, for the first half. 2010 programs were sold for $ 2.2 billion. This is more than in the whole of 2009 ($ 1.7 billion).

In quantitative terms, the volume of the market in the I floor. 2010 amounted to 3.9 billion downloads, and for the whole of last year - 3.1 billion.

The company predicts that there will be a real boom in this market in the next three years. By 2013, the number of smartphone owners will grow from the current 100 million to 1 billion people, and the software market will increase to $ 15 billion.
