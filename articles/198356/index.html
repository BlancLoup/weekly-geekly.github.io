AppsMafia: Hackathon in Moscow (November 9 - 10)

November 9 - 10, API Moscow platform

At night, when the whole city falls asleep and civilians have cozy dreams, in the center of Moscow, on the territory of Digital October, the lights are lit and wake up ... Mafia? Coders!

AppsMafia works, works on projects, ponders ideas for capturing the world. Designers and programmers unite in small start-up cells and, consulting on their projects with don and don experts, diligently write lines of code and draw pictures and presentations. In the afternoon, the city wakes up and the council of citizens decides who ...

At the end of the second day, teams will demonstrate their projects for the expert committee, leading venture capital funds and investors.

The most successful projects will be awarded with gifts, and those for whom the card will be most successful will receive offers from investors.

Two days and one night - coding, prizes, workshops, fan and networking!

Organized by ITmozg and Microsoft . General partner of the event: Center for Innovative Development of Moscow .

Detailed information and schedule on the website: habrahabr.ru/special/microsoft/appsmafia

Participation is free. We are waiting for your applications for participation here: events.techdays.ru/AppsMafia/2013-11

Those who do not want to sleep, but will not be able to come, will be able to watch the online broadcast.
