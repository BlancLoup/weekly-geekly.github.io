The prospects of the mobile games market in Southeast Asia

The market of games in Southeast Asia over the past years has attracted the attention of analysts, experts and the developers themselves. In this article we will try to tell you why.


Southeast Asia is a macro-region covering the continental and island territories between China, India and Australia. It includes 11 countries: on the continental part are Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Malaysia, and on the island - Brunei, East Timor, Indonesia, Singapore and the Philippines.

As a mobile gaming market, there are usually 6 countries that account for 97% of revenue: Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam.



When analyzing information about these countries, we are faced with the fact that the divergence of data on the mobile gaming markets of Southeast Asia in different sources is quite significant. This concerns the assessment of both the number of users of mobile games and the market profitability.
