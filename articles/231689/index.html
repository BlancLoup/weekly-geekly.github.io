Mimito smart pen




Mimito Smart Pen is an accessory for IPhone, Android, computers and tablets. This is not only a multifunctional smart pen, but also a device that turns your monitor into a touch surface. The device looks and writes like a pen, but it also uses high-frequency sound and special sensors that react to the movement of your hand. The pen starts working when you attach the receiver to the top of a regular sheet, and in real time it starts recording and transferring everything you write to any of your devices. The same device allows you to turn your regular monitor into a touchpad, which allows you to play games, draw and write.

Specifications
Wireless connection, USB, Bluetooth
Letter mode up to 90 hours
2.5 hour recharge time
Monitor coverage up to 26 inches

CYSPO OmniStation




CYSPO OmniStation is a multi-charging wireless station that allows you to charge 7 devices at the same time. You can charge your devices both wirelessly and wired. The station will greatly simplify your life if you charge more than three devices per day. You can also increase the length of the cable if necessary. In addition, on the outside is the display, which means that the device can also be used as a photo frame.

Emberlight: smart ceiling light




Emberlight - the ceiling for light bulbs, the light of which can be controlled through the application on the smartphone. A very simple control system: you connect the cover to the router and insert a light bulb, then everything is regulated through the application. Light can be customized in a variety of ways. For example, it can automatically turn on when you enter the room and turn off when you exit it. You can also set the time on / off light with a timer. Using the application, you can adjust the light, raise or lower it. Price $ 49 for 1 ceiling.

OwnPhones




OwnPhones is an individual headset for a smartphone, which is printed on a 3D printer. What is surprising, the device is developed individually for each person. After downloading the application, you make a short video clip of your ear and send it to the developer. The design is also chosen individually: you can choose the color and design (in gold or silver). On the outside of the device there are LEDs that alert others about your level of employment: red is very busy, yellow is not very busy, green is you can talk to me. An excellent speaker is built in, the battery lasts for 5-6 hours, the connection is made via BlueTooth, the device is charged via mini USB for 1 hour. Cost from $ 200.

Sense




Sense is a smart system that monitors your room in real time. Sense has a rather beautiful and simple design, consumes little energy. The device provides you with information about humidity, light, temperature, and even the level of air pollution. Also, using a smart alarm system, Sense will wake you up at the right time, based on the analysis of your sleep, in order to save you from a depressed mood. With the application, you can find out your behavior during sleep: how you slept, what happened at night. Estimated cost of $ 100.
