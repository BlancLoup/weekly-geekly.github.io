What is this GTD?

What arises in your head when you hear the name GTD? I asked friends around and a funny picture was drawn. Everyone I know, imagine GTD like this:

GTD, Getting Things Done (you can safely read in Russian as Ge-Te-Te) is a system for self-organization, invented by the American author David Allen. Since the release of the corresponding book in 2000, millions of unorganized people have finally found happiness and peace of mind. After all, the system, as stated, allows hello affairs in order.

How does GTD work?

The basic principles of GTD are very simple. It is based on three habits *, to which every effective person must accustom himself.
1. Record your affairs
2. Quickly decide what to do next
3. Do what you wrote down.

1. We write the case (the author - Collect)

Why do many people experience constant stress and do not have time? Because they keep everything in their head. And the human brain is able to simultaneously hold in memory only 7 objects.
