Scam Protection


A bank card is not issued by proxy , it is issued only on the passport .

In case of replacing an old passport with a new one, a new passport will be marked with the details of the old one. But is this mark enough or will it also require a certificate - so far there is no answer .

Conflict Policy


From my point of view, the easiest (non-IT) way for a scammer to get access to a card account is to transfer the SIM card "to yourself" with a fake passport or power of attorney. In the "wild" is already met . Then the fraudster gets the opportunity to reset the password to his personal account, and there - the item "Transfer from the phone account." What if Bank Round or MegaFon goes bankrupt?


The issuing bank Round does not participate in the Deposit Insurance System .
UPD1. Actually - participates , but for cardholders it does not matter, because:


All your money is not stored in the bank, but in MegaFon . After clarifying the question, it turned out that all the same in the bank, more precisely - the Ministry of Finance keeps its accounts in several banks .


UPD2. There is an interesting nuance. A month ago, to a previous article about a deee card, I asked , and MaximMegafon confirmed the position of MegaFon and the Ministry of Communications:


The account of the mobile operator is an advance payment, the minister explained, therefore the money on this account does not belong to the subscriber himself.

UPD13. So, from the point of view of the law, the money in the account of the subscriber belongs to the operator. In some situations this may be an advantage. The practice is such that bailiffs cannot seize these funds, i.e. the cardholder will be able to continue using it, unlike ordinary bank cards.
Money on the account is an advance to the operator. Which can be picked up. This is what happens at the time of payment with this money using a card or via mobile commerce. Without a card, this is much more difficult and expensive .


If the bank Round disappears, turns into a pumpkin or something else, then nothing will happen to your money . They both lay on the account of the Megaphone, and will continue to lie there. All payments through the application will be available to you, and the company will promptly replace your plastic card with a new one (another bank).


MegaFon is not a bank, so they cannot take a license from us. At the same time, we guarantee you the safety of all your funds, and not just 1.4 million . This is governed by the agreement between the Subscriber and MegaFon .


Operators do not need an external guarantor, as there are no precedents for the bankruptcy of cellular companies.
