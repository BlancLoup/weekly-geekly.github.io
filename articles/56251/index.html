Yota for business - commercial launch

Since yesterday, Yota has been offering its Internet access services through its 4G (Mobile WiMAX) network for money. This applies only to corporate users. For individuals, services are still free until May 31.

Network Features:
- speed up to 10 Mbit / s
- roaming-free space in all cities of coverage (while there are two of them - Moscow, St. Petersburg)
- maintaining the connection when driving at speeds up to 120 km / h

For business users, QoS and a traffic prioritization system are also used.
This technology allows video conferencing, video calls, video broadcasting.
An important factor - a high level of security and protection against unauthorized access in Mobile WiMAX networks (unlike Wi-Fi networks) is achieved through the use of mutual authentication, data encryption, periodic change of the encryption key and information integrity control.

Reliability is also provided by the company itself, which conducts round-the-clock monitoring of the quality of the network and, having its own contact center, guarantees customers technical support 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
