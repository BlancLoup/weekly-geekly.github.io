In it, Roskomnadzor asks Telegram Messenger LLP to provide the full and abbreviated name, country of registration, tax identifier and / or identifier in the trade register of the country of registration, location address, postal address, email address, domain name, resource administrator email address, hosting provider and description of the service. All these data are necessary for entering the company into the register of information dissemination organizers.

The register of information dissemination organizers created in 2014 imposes several requirements on resources. Among other things, the ORI must keep user data in the territory of the Russian Federation for one year and provide it at the request of the special services.

In a statement for RNS, Telegram representatives emphasize that not a single government or any special service had previously received and would not receive a single bit of information from the messenger in the future. Telegram says that both secret and cloud chats are encrypted. At the same time, the cryptographic keys are divided into several parts and physically separated in different jurisdictions, so cooperation at the level of several states will be required in order to force Telegram to issue data.

According to Vedomosti, in a letter, Roskomnadzor warns Telegram about the possibility of blocking for the refusal to provide data for registration as ARI. While the management of Telegram evades negotiations and refuses to join the register of AIDs, the source claims. Pavel Durov himself declined to comment on Vedomosti.

Yesterday, Telegram users, administrators and channel authors turned to Pavel Durov in the form of a petition on change.org . Users ask the Telegram team not to block the messenger in Russia.
