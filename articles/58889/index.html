Correct task saves time

I want to share my thoughts on optimizing the communication of an adequate manager and an adequate performer. What should be done and what should not be done in order for the work to be done efficiently and on time?

A normally constructed system of relations between a manager and a subordinate is based on the intellectual superiority of the manager and his ability to predict and prioritize not without regard for the interests of the contractor, which is very important. If this is not the case, and the girl manager plays the role of the employer, and the maniac programmer plays the role of the performer, the manager will not receive the results, and the programmer will receive no money.



1. Clearly assigned task.


A good leader is a sieve that is able to weed out the trash and skip the most important details to the performer. Thus, if a manager cannot formulate a task, then the executor will not do it all the more.
