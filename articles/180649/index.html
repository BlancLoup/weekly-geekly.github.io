Today came the long-awaited application Yandex. Music for smartphones on Android

Tracks of audio streams in the Radio section are also available for saving, and the number of stored songs is limited only by the memory of your phone.

With the application it is easy to open new. If you like the song, and there is no one to ask the name - ask Yandex. Just enable the music recognition function, and the application will find the desired track in the Yandex.Music catalog.



With the release of the Yandex.Music application for Android, the terms of subscription to the service have changed. It still costs 199 rubles per month, but now it is automatically extended, and subscribers of the mobile application for Android receive the first month as a gift. Auto-subscription is designed to make life easier for users, but you can easily turn it off if you wish. How to do this, read the section "Manage Subscriptions" in Google Play and the App Store .

You can subscribe through the Yandex.Music web service according to the old model - pay for one month and decide whether it is worth paying permanently. Subscription via the web service costs 149 rubles per month, and you can subscribe for 6 or 12 months at a discount .
