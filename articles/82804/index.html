In my practice I deal with a micro earpiece. A very useful device for a certain segment of people. The purpose of this mini -review is to familiarize people who do not know about the existence of a micro-earphone with this device.

Let's start with the fact that we stipulate the size of the earpiece. This earpiece is most often a flesh color and falls through the ear. It is not visible if properly installed. That is, with these two sentences, I wanted to say that these are not the headphones that are shown in the films, where the swirling wiring from Soviet phones hangs from the agents' ear.

Schematic diagram of the work. Let us analyze in words. A headset with a battery (AG0 or LR521) receives the signal from the antenna (loop on the neck). The antenna directly connects to the phone.
