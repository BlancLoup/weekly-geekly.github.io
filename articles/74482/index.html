Flash GAMM Kyiv 2009!



December 5-6, 2009 , the third conference of Flash GAMM Kyiv 2009 flash game developers will take place in Kiev ! www.flashgamm.com

The conference will be attended by domestic and foreign companies involved in the development and publication of flash games.
Such topics as the development of flash games, graphics and animation, monetization, flash games in social networks, advertising, 3D in flash and much more will be covered.

This time the conference is organized by Absolutist Ltd.
Venue: Hotel "Lybid", which plans to host 150-200 participants in its hall.

Registration for the conference is already open.
The list of speakers is not yet fully formed, so if you have something to tell, the organizers invite you to make a report.

Unlike previous conferences, this one will adopt a number of innovations.

Tables for sponsors will be organized, a conference catalog will be published, where anyone, even non-conference participants, can tell about their company, specify links to the portfolio, insert screenshots of games, etc.

A contest of flash games is also planned, the winner of which will receive Asus Eee Pc

In short, the third Flash GAMM should never be missed!
More detailed information you can get on the official website - www.flashgamm.com
