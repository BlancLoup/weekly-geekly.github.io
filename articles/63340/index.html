Piracy improves not only music, but also movies

It's no secret that in recent times it has become much more. For example, here is the American statistics : if in 2000, 35,516 new music albums were published, then in 2008 - already 79,695, including 25,159 digital ones. Although sales are falling, but music is becoming more and more, for every taste.

It happened, as it is not difficult to guess, largely due to computers, the Internet and P2P networks. Piracy and the general availability of torrents allowed musicians to significantly expand their musical horizons, learn about new musical directions and hear thousands of new groups that they would never have heard, if not piracy.

As a result, not only the quantity but also the quality of new music, the variety of genres and the general professional level of the performers are improved.
