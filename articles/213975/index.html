The results of a single rating of mobile-developers 2014

Hi, Habr! Today we announce the results of the first single rating of mobile application developers . The ranking was made only by the agent segment - companies developing custom applications.


The method of building our rating is absolutely transparent, and anyone can verify that the calculation of the total points for each company is correct. We tried to make the methodology as relevant as possible and protected from markups - the results of the participants in 5 local niche ratings of the segment formed the basis of a single top.

By the way, in the process of preparation, we discovered a rather curious fact. Unlike players from other digital segments (web studios, SEOs, contextual advertising agencies, etc.) - a significant number of companies that develop custom-made applications have a full English-language version of sites and often indicate it "by default". Thus, Russian mobile developers are much more focused on cooperation with Western companies than their counterparts from other market segments.

Let's tell a little more about the top ten companies of our rating:

  1. Unreal Mojo . The first place of our rating with a comfortable margin from the pursuers. Unreal Mojo is represented in high positions in almost all industry rankings (the first place in the J`son & Partners report can be especially noted). According to Cnews, the company's turnover in 2012 amounted to more than 40 million rubles. The company's client portfolio includes such brands as Alfabank, Sberbank, ABBY, Kommersant, etc. The company specializes only in the mobile segment.

  2. REDMADROBOT . The company occupies the second line of the chart due to the first place in BestInDigital and high positions in the ratings of Cnews and J`son & partners. The company's turnover in 2012 is 25 million rubles, currently 40 people in the state. In the client portfolio - Beeline, financial group Life, ROLF. The company specializes only in the mobile segment.

  3. e-legion . Bronze ranking gets a company from St. Petersburg e-Legion. The high position is largely due to the first place of the CNews chart (the company showed a turnover of 50 million rubles for 2012). The staff of the Legion has more than 70 people, there are offices in Moscow, Krasnodar, Ulyanovsk and Kazan. Among the clients of e-Legion are companies such as RaiffeisenBank, Yandex, Mail.Ru.

  4. iD EAST . On the fourth line of the ranking is one of the largest players in the segment - the company iD EAST (largely due to the permanent first place in the chart Apps4All). The company develops applications not only on iOS, Android and Windows Phone, but also on less common platforms - Blackberry, Symbian MeeGo, Samsung Bada. It should be noted separately that the company is not represented in the main chart on the turnover of Cnews, but stated the highest figures for 2012: 60 million rubles.

  5. Articul Media . On the fifth line is the first player of the not purely mobile segment - the full-service digital-agency Articul Media. The company has significantly increased its activity over the past period, and this is largely due to the maintenance of a significant pool of digital work for the Sochi 2014 Organizing Committee.

  6. East Media . East Media is in the sixth position with a minimal margin from the top five (largely due to the high positions in the Apps4All chart). The company has its own publishing platform for publishing periodicals for iPhone / iPad. The head office of the company is in Yekaterinburg, there is a branch in Moscow.

  7. Infoshell On the seventh line of the chart is Infoshell. The high position is largely due to a fairly large turnover, declared in the framework of the CNews rating - 25 million rubles. for 2012.

  8. 2Nova Interactive . At the eighth position is another integrated digital-agency 2Nova, largely due to the high valuation of the company's service in the BestInDigital rating. By the way, in 2013, 2Nova became the fourth Russian agency admitted to the prestigious international association SoDA.

  9. AppTeka . At the ninth position is a small Moscow company Appteka. In the company's client portfolio: Mediamarkt, ECCO, Sport-Express.

  10. Sebbia With a minimum margin of the ninth line, the top ten closes Sebbia. Worthy result of the company provided a good position in the ratings of Apps4All and BesInDigital. Currently, the company employs about 15 people.

A complete list of participating companies can be found at Ruvard .
