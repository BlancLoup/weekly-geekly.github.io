Create your own keyboard with DX1 System Input

Modern keyboard - the crown of technology, but for gamers, they are still not very perfect. Just remember to yourself how many times you dreamed of rearranging keys in accordance with your requirements, in the right order, or you wanted to assign your aliases to specific keys.

DX1 System Input was made to fulfill these desires! Everything is quite simple, you need to place the keys in a convenient position for you on the substrate and assign each key to its own command. This can be a single command-function or several consecutive commands that can be run; you have complete freedom in implementing your projects.



25 special keys can be attached to the substrate. And if you are confused or want to change the key assignment, simply detach it and position it again in a new position. The substrate is empty, so you have the opportunity to place your background or place labels on it. Any macros can be configured directly on the fly for all keys. Being right in the game, knowing that you need a new key, simply hold it, assign a new team and continue to play on! DX1 works with different programs and easily remembers the commands and macros for each of them.

System requirements:
  • Windows XP / 2000 (with Service Pack 3 or later) - US Version
  • 350 MHZ Pentium III PC (or equivalent)
  • 128 MB RAM
  • 50 MB of free hard drive space
  • Powered USB Port (1.1 or 2.0)
  • US English Keyboard
  • Mouse


Cost in the region of $ 150. An interesting thing for real gamers and lovers of the exotic! :)
