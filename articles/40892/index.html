Sony Ericsson Bluetooth Watch

A new collection of women's watches, equipped with a Bluetooth module for communicating and controlling the phone, is represented by Sony Ericsson. The MBW-200 model range includes three devices that are functionally identical and differ only in design: Sparkling Allure Edition, Contemporary Elegance Edition, and Evening Classic Edition.



Like the previous one, the 150-series, the new items allow the user to control a mobile phone without getting it from a handbag or pocket. The watch is equipped with an OLED display that shows the number of the incoming call. With their help, you can literally one-touch reject / accept a call or adjust the volume. MBW-200 provides player control: tracks are displayed, and you can play / stop the melody with the function buttons or switch between tracks.


It is logical that the watch works well only with devices manufactured by Sony Ericsson. In the case of mobile phones from other manufacturers, most functions do not work. But the product is fashionable, for fans of Sony Ericsson products. So the drawback is difficult to call.

How much will the new watch techno fashionistas not yet known.
