How to patch KDE under FreeBSD or what to expect from master classes at DevCon School on June 1

From 2011 to 2016 inclusive, we did a cool DevCon conference in country format for 2 days. And each time, in the comments in the questionnaires, we were asked more stories about the projects of real customers, more practical work!

And we invented and implemented DevCon School: free for participants of the event with deep immersion . Despite its relatively short history, this name has become a brand and a kind of quality mark. We are asked to conduct them again and again. A special place is occupied by large DevCon School, which we conduct twice a year. Unlike ordinary ones, there are several topics in them, and most importantly, it is possible to choose exactly how to get acquainted with these topics: intensive courses or master classes.
