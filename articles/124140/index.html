Acer Liquid Mini - liquid metal wrapped in plastic

Acer Liquid mini - became the first device of Acer company, which passed full testing in our edition. The device is made in a plastic case and equipped with a 3.2-inch screen with a resolution of 320x480 pixels that is quite suitable for this size. In the heart of the smartphone works Qualcomm processor with a frequency of 600 MHz.



The remaining parameters also meet the requirements for devices in this price category. The average price of Acer Liquid Mini on the Internet fluctuates around 8-9 thousand rubles.

Specifications of Acer Liquid Mini
  • Dimensions : 58x110x13 mm weight 109 g
  • Operating system : Android 2.2 (Froyo)
  • Processor : Qualcomm MSM7227, 600 MHz
  • Screen : 3.2 inches, capacitive, multitouch, TFT, resolution 320x480 pixels
  • Battery : 1300 mAh Li-Po (talk time: 8 hours, standby time: 400 hours)
  • Camera : Rear 5 MP
  • Memory : built-in - 512 MB RAM, 512 MB of RAM, microSD card with 2 GB included
  • Data transmission : 3G, WiFi, Bluetooth, microUSB, GPS, FM tuner
  • Body options : silver, black, blue, purple, gold
  • Optional : built-in compass, carrying case included

The smartphone has built-in GPS, WiFi, compass and FM Tuner. The device weighs 109 grams and lies well in the hand. But what you really need to pay attention to is the software shell of the device. Immediately you can not say that the device works on Android OS 2.2. It is not entirely clear what is the home screen of the device. By pressing the "home" button you get into the widget section.



Do not try to bring the icons of your programs on these desktops. For applications in the phone is a separate space. The 8 most used ones can be brought to the dock, and the list of the others can be opened if you drag your finger from the bottom up. Scrolling the screen to the left you can get to the list of recently launched applications, it is here made in the form of reduced screenshots of programs. Also, the notification line has been significantly changed.

All these innovations look very unusual and some of them are not perfect. But it is worth paying tribute to Acer , in that its developers did not steal interfaces from other manufacturers, and tried to make their interface unique and original.
