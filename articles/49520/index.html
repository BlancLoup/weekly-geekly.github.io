Collection of free and open source software for Windows

Many teachers are very cautious (and often negative) relate to Linux. At the same time, "First Aid" does not cause delight among teachers. Especially for educational institutions and students, I have assembled a disk with free and open software for Windows XP and Vista.

Programs, located on the disk, you can absolutely freely at your discretion install on any number of computers in schools, at home or in the office. The collection is equipped with a convenient installer, which will be the basis for future Edumandriva projects - EDUInstaler . You can get the installer source code here: code.google.com/p/edumandriva-setup

The compilation is based on curricula in computer science and computer-related disciplines, as well as the wishes of teachers.
