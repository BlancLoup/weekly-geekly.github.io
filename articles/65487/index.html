Yandex announced a test of the next update of the algorithm - Arzamas 1.2

Significant was the fact that in the first Arzamas a large part of Russia saw, according to geo-dependent queries, the sites of its region, but the division of requests into geo-dependent and geo-independent was officially seen only in Arzamas 1.2.
The idea of ​​Arzamas is, in general, transparent, why should a Moscow taxi resident by the same name request ...
The improved algorithm of geo-classification of queries is intended to eliminate errors and supplement the already formed query database with geo-binding with new data.
Quotation of Yandex.Soids: It is worth noting that in Arzamas 1.2 the ranking for geo-independent queries within Russia is the same for all users, and for geo-dependent — it differs only for Moscow, St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg.
The division of requests into geo-dependent and geo-independent is available at buki.yandex.ru .
As always, there were many oddities with the definition of the type of request.
The situation with brands is curious - the query “Samsung”, “IBM”, “Apple” is geo-dependent, while “Sony Ericsson”, “Google” are geo-independent.
A similar and incomprehensible situation with requests for pipes - “polymer pipes” - a geo-independent request,
“Plastic pipes” is a geo-dependent query.
