Easy Video Cutter - simple video trimmer

A friend of mine decided to make his own contribution to the development of the open source community. The result of his desire was not great, but, as it seems to him, a useful program, about which he asked me to tell.


The essence of this program is very simple: cut the video pieces. I told him, of course, that there was a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãsuch programs, to which he replied that he wanted to do something more convenient than multifunctional nonlinear editors for his daily life.
And the utility was explained quite lucidly: ‚ÄúHere on your computer, for example, there is a collection of porn, which eats a lot of space on hard, you can cut out your favorite scenes, and delete the rest.‚Äù
In general, it is written in QT and is the front-end for mplayer and mencoder.
You can put it in Archa just:
yarout -Sy evc
Or assemble yourself
For those who do not know how to collect:

wget easyvideocutter.googlecode.com/files/evc-0.1.tar.gz

tar -xvzf evc-0.1.tar.gz

cd evc-0.1

qmake
make
After that, the evc binary will appear, if you wish, you can copy it to / usr / bin
