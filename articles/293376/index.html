In the second quarter, Vimpelcom continued to decline and decreased by 26% compared with last year.

In the first half of 2015, Vimpelcom operator revenue fell by 28% to $ 7.274 billion. In the second quarter, revenues decreased by 26% over the same period last year and amounted to $ 3.759 billion.

EBITDA in the first half amounted to $ 2,907 billion, which is 30% less than a year earlier. In the second quarter, EBITDA decreased by 27% to $ 1.511 billion, the company said in a report .

EBITDA margin in the first half of the year was equal to 40%, in the second quarter - 40.2%. Last year, in the first half and in the second quarter, this figure was 41.3% and 41%, respectively. Net profit of Vimpelcom in April-June amounted to $ 108 million, which is 8% more than in the second quarter of 2014.

The total debt of the holding at the end of June was $ 22.3 billion, a quarterly decline of 8%, including through the redemption of bonds worth $ 1.8 billion in April. Vimpelcom net debt for the past quarter increased by 2% - to $ 17.9 billion, mainly due to the devaluation of the ruble and the euro against the dollar.

The number of mobile subscribers of Vimpelcom at the end of June amounted to 213.4 million, decreasing by 1% in annual terms and by 2.2% - in quarterly terms, reports Investing.com
