A look at Google Keep: a great replacement for Evernote



On Habrahabr there was no news about the appearance of Google Keep, I found only an article about the crash and the restoration of the service. Therefore, I think, it will be interesting to residents of the Habr-country to learn about the new product.



At the moment, the service can only be used by owners of Android devices and ordinary users of desktops, Google Keep is available at drive.google.com/keep/ (or in the Play Market )

What does Google Keep do?

- Writing notes (Web + App);
- Photo upload (Web + App)
- Photo creation (App)
- Creating ToDo-lists (Web + App)
- Create voice memos and convert voice to text (App)

Why not Evernote?

Most likely, I will use the service, as it works very quickly and has a nice design. Therefore, I am waiting for the version for iOS.
