ZeroNights HackQuest 2015



For the fifth anniversary time, we are holding a conference on practical information security (to be held on November 25-26 in Moscow, 2015.zeronights.ru ).

Traditionally, we conduct a hakquest in front of her, inviting everyone to solve tasks related to reverse engineering, web hacking, pentest, protocol analysis, etc. things.

  • The duration of the Quest is 7 days, from 8 pm on November 9 to 8 pm on November 15;
  • Every day - one task. On the decision - 24 hours;
  • Who is the first to solve the task will receive an invitation to the conference;
  • Innovation this year : deciding the second or third task, you can get points. The second speed participant will receive 0.5 points, the third - 0.25. Having earned 1 full point, the participant will receive an invitation (note that 2 or more points are entitled to only one invite).
  • Upon request, the organizers may request a description of the task solution (we are for fairness and honesty);
  • It is forbidden to give hints or communicate answers to other participants;
  • It is forbidden to hand over the answer more than once (under another account).


The quest is available at: hackquest.zeronights.org
Start on Monday, November 9, 20:00 (Moscow time). Pre-registration is not required. Just go to the day of the job and start to decide (there will be a form of sending flags).

To assess the type and level of tasks, you can read the analysis of last year's hackhabest - habrahabr.ru/company/dsec/blog/245203 (under the spoiler "writeups").
