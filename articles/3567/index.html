Google started selling Google Apps

In America, began selling office suite software Google Apps Premier Edition . The offer, as expected , included a set of web services already known from the free versions of Google Apps . Recall it includes:

- mail GMail,
- editor of texts and tables Google Docs & Spreadsheets,
- messenger with the possibility of voice communication Google Talk,
- calendar calendar organizer Google Calendar,
- a platform for creating web pages of Page Creator, etc.

What does Google offer to buy for $ 50 per year?

First, five times more roomy GMail. Under the mail, each client is allocated 10 GB of space on the company's servers. You can view correspondence without advertising - the function of prohibiting its broadcast is present. There is also a mobile version of GMail for correspondence from BlackBarry devices.

Secondly, Google gives 99.9% guarantee of uninterrupted work of web services. Technical support is conducted 24 hours a day.

Thirdly, customers are granted access to the API, which allows them to tailor programs to the specifics of the business.

Google Apps Premier Edition will be useful for both individual users and corporate clients, according to Google. The implication is that companies will pay $ 50 per year for the account of each of the employees working with the package. General Electric and Procter & Gamble have already begun to use this offer, which seems like a pretty significant fact.
