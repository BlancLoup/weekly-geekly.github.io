12 projects will receive $ 25,000 for development from the creator of VKontakte Pavel Durov and Yuri Milner

For the third year already Pavel Durov selects projects worthy of his attention, and Yuri Milner gives each of them $ 25,000 each. This time, the number of funded projects reached a record high of 12. All projects were published and discussed directly on VKontakte - vk.com/startfellows . Many were given valuable advice and ratings from group members and Durov.

List of laureate projects under the cut.


Winners Startfellows



For each project there are 2 links: to the project itself, its description and discussion in Startfellows.

- Planner5d.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4049 - a service for creating layouts for rooms and interiors in 2D / 3D modes.

- Surfingbird.ru , vk.com/wall-30825867_5288 - recommendation system of web pages for the convenience of surfing the sites.

- YouDo.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4529 - a service that allows you to find artists to perform any work or urgent orders.

- Rosalind.info , vk.com/wall-30825867_5112 - a platform for learning programming and bioinformatics with problem solving.

- Astrotheapp.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4118 is a mobile application that helps you easily meet interesting people and develop in their communication circles.

- Sptnik.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4913 - search, booking and organizing excursions and master classes from independent experts.

- Takepin.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4385 - search for useful links through friends.

- Avatrip.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_2088 - remote interactive video travel service.

- Lookinhotels.ru , vk.com/wall-30825867_5134 - video catalog of hotels.

- Dialoggy.ru , vk.com/wall-30825867_3272 - an open book of complaints and suggestions.

- Weheartpics.com , vk.com/wall-30825867_4928 - a full-featured social network based on photos.

- DonorSearch.ru , vk.com/wall-30825867_1865 - search for blood donors through social networks.
