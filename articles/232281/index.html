Falcon 9 launch vehicle successfully put a commercial satellite into orbit



Today (literally an hour ago) SpaceX launched another successful launch of its Falcon 9 launch vehicle. The flight was commercial, its goal was to launch the AsiaSat8 communications satellite into orbit.

For the Falcon 9 launch vehicle, this is a one-way flight: the satellite is heavy, all fuel was needed to launch it, and there was no resources for a soft landing / return of the first stage of the launch vehicle, everything was consumed during launch.
