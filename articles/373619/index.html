One of the "Fathers of the Runet" Anton Nosik died



Today it became known about the death of one of the founders of the Runet, Anton Nosik . He died at the age of 52 due to a heart attack. Nosik is rightly considered a key figure, because he was one of those who formed the Russian-speaking segment of the Internet. For many years, Anton Nosik had a great influence on the evolution of Runet. He created dozens of projects, many of which still exist. Even more projects Nosik participated.

It is interesting that education at Anton Nosik is medical, the profile is dentistry. In addition to medicine, he was interested in economics, and then in technology. This interest, in the end, influenced the formation of Nosik as an Internet actor.

He wrote about medicine and economics as a freelance correspondent for the newspaper Vesti. The most active period of activity of Anton Nosik began in 1997, when he returned to Russia. Then the Russian-speaking segment of the network practically did not exist, since there were very few websites in Russian. Emelyan Zakharov and Demian Kudryavtsev were engaged in the development of Runet, which attracted several people to create websites. These were Artemy Lebedev, Anton Nosik, Alexander Gagin and other people. Initially, the work was carried out only on projects that were related to the activities of the Internet provider "Cityline" and the content provider "Netskate", then other projects appeared.

Being engaged in the development of Runet, Nosik quickly became an expert in networking and media technologies. Entrepreneurs who wanted to invest their money in a new field for themselves - information technology - began to turn to him for help. A year after his return to Russia Nosik founded Gazeta.ru, two years later - Lenta.ru. He remained editor-in-chief of the last resource until 2004. Nosik also participated in the creation of NEWSru and a number of other media projects. It all started after Nosik met Gleb Pavlovsky, President of the Effective Policy Foundation. He supported the creation of several new resources, including the sites of Sergey Kiriyenko and Boris Nemtsov, as well as Forum.ru and Polit.ru. A few years later, Nosik was offered to become vice president and then president of the Rambler. The company then had a certain kind of difficulties due to the fact that neither the owners could attract enough of them, nor could they sell it. In 2004, he left the post of editor-in-chief of Lenta.ru and began work on new and existing projects. One of them was the MosNews website, which was positioned as an information resource about Russia for foreigners.

A year later, in 2005, he and the other co-founders founded the Pomogie.org Foundation. The organization was created so that the money collected for good purposes is really sent to people who need them. In 2006, Nosik became one of the leaders of the company SUP. Just two years later, he left the company, becoming the head of bfm.ru. In 2014, he and his colleagues founded Mokhnaty Cheese LLC, a company whose profile of work is marketing research and public opinion. He also was engaged in social and political work.
