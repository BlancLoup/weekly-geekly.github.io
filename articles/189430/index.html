Overview of the Nexus 7 2nd generation (2013)



Hello, users of Habrahabr. A few days ago I received the 2nd generation Nexus 7 for the test. I came across a 16 GB model without LTE.

From the first minutes of use, it became clear that the updated "seven" is a contender for the title of the best 7-inch tablet on Android.



Nexus 7 2-gen Specifications

- Android 4.3
- Processor: Snapdragon S4 Pro 1.5 GHz (4 cores)
- RAM: 2 GB DDR3
- Built-in memory: 16 GB or 32 GB
- Screen: 7 ', 1920 by 1200 pixels, LCD
- PPI: 323
- Video core: Adreno 320, 400 MHz
- Bluetooth 4.0
- Rear camera 5 MP
- Front camera 2 MP
- Battery capacity: 3950 mAh
- Dimensions (LxWxD): 114 x 200 x 8.65 mm
- Weight: 290 g, 299 g (LTE version)
- NFC
- GPS (even in Wi-Fi version)
