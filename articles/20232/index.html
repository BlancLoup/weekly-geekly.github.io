Polaroid introduced Mobile Photo Printer

Small, fast, useful and with a nice design - this is how you can describe the new gadget presented by Polaroid. This is a mobile photo printer, the original name is Mobile Photo Printer.



A digital printer has two unusual possibilities:

1. For printing photos using technology called ZINK, which means that the ink and paint is not needed.

2. You can connect to the device and transfer the photo to print via Bluetooth or USB port.



Bluetooth in this gadget is most likely needed to make it convenient to connect via a mobile phone. You can carry it with you as a pocket printer, because it will be no larger than a regular camera. In 60 seconds, Mobile Photo Printer prints a 5 x 7 centimeter photo. Of course, the photos will be unusual for us, but the photos will take up less space.

Unfortunately, neither the release date of this novelty nor the approximate price has been named yet. However, now you can see his colorful presentation on the Polaroid website.
