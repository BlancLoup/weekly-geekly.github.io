New AdContext and BloggerKit Blog Content Programs

Significant events occur in the contextual advertising market. The largest online shopping site, eBay, has announced the launch of its own contextual advertising system AdContext. Bloggers will be pleased with one more news about the emergence of a specialized system of contextual advertising BloggerKit.

A new ad network AdContext is being created in spite of Google, with which eBay has recently seriously ruined its relationship. The reason is Google‚Äôs plans to launch GBuy‚Äôs own online payment service, which could be a direct competitor to eBay PayPal . Accidentally or not, even GBuy is consonant with the word eBay.

The new contextual advertising system AdContext will work as follows. Each eBay auction owner automatically receives HTML code that can be immediately integrated into any other site. On the page where this code is placed, the goods offered for sale at eBay auctions will be advertised, and the selection of goods for advertising will be carried out based on the analysis of the context on the web page. Advertisements will be constantly updated, depending on the range of products on eBay auctions. Under the terms of work, the site receives from 40% to 70% of the profits for displaying advertising.

According to eBay's plans, every partner who joins the new AdContext advertising network will receive a discount on the services of the largest online auction site. At first, the main customers will be the owners of eBay auctions themselves. Due to discounts, AdContext should be more profitable for them than the popular content networks of Google and Yahoo . The very same eBay company, thus, will be able to refuse the additional costs associated with advertising on Google and Yahoo.

Another good news for bloggers who are looking for additional opportunities for earnings - the launch of a specialized contextual advertising system for blogs BloggerKit . This system is designed for advertising products that are sold in the online store Amazon .
