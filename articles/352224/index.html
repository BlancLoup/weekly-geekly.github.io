Chapter 1. Brief Background


Back in 1981, the Council of Europe published a convention on the protection of individuals when dealing with personal data. The purpose of the Convention is to ‚Äúensure in the territory of each country every individual, regardless of his nationality and place of residence, to respect his rights and fundamental freedoms, and especially his right to privacy, in the aspect of automated processing of personal data (Article 1).
