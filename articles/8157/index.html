With the permission of Mio, I collected the latest wishes of users regarding habrabra.
I do not think that all of them are realizable at all, but I personally am really looking forward to some, for example, the disappearance of a live broadcast from a live broadcast. Well, correction of registration of votes is waiting, of course;)

Killed habratopiki 27 points pietrovich
Essence: If someone created a topic, Habronarod ran over and wrote comments, and this someone took and closed the topic - not only its content disappears from access, but also those who commented out. Also the page from favorites disappears.
Suggested solutions: when hiding a topic, hide only the text of the topic itself and the possibility of voting for it. Or completely forbid to hide topics.

Explanation of links and elements on pages 16 points tmikwid
The bottom line: Beginners are not always clear what these or other numbers and other interface elements mean.
Suggested solution: Add tooltips to the interface elements.

Number of topics on one page 18 akeeper points
Essence: When the opportunity to go to the Habr rarely accumulates a lot of unread topics and flipping between a bunch of pages is inconvenient
Suggested solution: Add to personal settings the ability to set the number of topics per page

Sort news on the main 48 points genie
Essence: topics on the main page appear only after the required number of votes have been collected, while being sorted in chronological order by the date of preservation, and not in the order in which topics appear on the main page. As a result, the topic may occur in the middle of the main page and be undeservedly forgotten.
Suggested solution: Sort topics on the main page by the first date they appear there.
