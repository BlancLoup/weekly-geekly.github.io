Sony Xperia U Review



Sony Xperia U is the third model in the NXT series of the Xperia line of 2012, which was reviewed by the editor of this blog, and the most frivolous of them. Sony introduces the Xperia U as a compact, attractive smartphone. Externally, it is distinguished by colored interchangeable panels and a backlighting flourishing with all the colors of the rainbow, which fairly dilute the severity of the generic features of the smartphones of this series. And on board - a high-resolution screen and a powerful dual-core processor. And all this - for a very modest price. In general, it should be interesting. We'll see?

Okay, they turned the phone in their hands, you can turn it on now. I will not say anything special about the screen - it is not scratched, covered with anti-reflective coating. In the bright sun a little "blind", because normal TFT. But overall a decent display.



Pleases his resolution, 854 by 480 pixels in three and a half inches - this is very good. Naturally, there is a multitouch that supports gestures up to four fingers at once.
