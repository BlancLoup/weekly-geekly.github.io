New iRiver with OLED display

In Japan, sales of a new multimedia player iRiver P20 with a 4.1-inch OLED display with a resolution of 480x272 pixels will begin soon.



The player is equipped with an 80 GB HDD, FM tuner with the ability to record radio programs, a voice recorder, a digital television receiver and a slot for SD memory cards.


iRiver P20 can play MPEG1 / 2, XviD, MPEG4 SP / ASP, WMV (7, 8 and 9), MP3, WMA, WAV, OGG, APE and FLAC.


According to the manufacturer, the battery life of the P20 is about 12 hours in audio playback mode and about 9 hours in video mode. Watching TV "eats" a little more. In this case, the battery will last for 8 hours.


The Japanese (iRiver P20 so far) shops will arrive on February 27 for about $ 436 for an 80-gigabyte model.
