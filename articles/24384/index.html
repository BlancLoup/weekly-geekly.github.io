Mobile social network - part two

MyPPL - mobile social network, which I wrote about in a previous post, has been successfully updated.
Fixed, as usual, part of the bugs, added new functionality.
Now you can write messages to each other using the internal messenger, add animated emoticons and new status images.



The problems with porting are not completely solved yet, as is the global problem with the slow work of the midlet.
Accordingly, while we continue the closed beta until the solution of the above problems.

Special thanks to those who got into the previous post and took part in testing, writing us comments and suggestions for improving the system. I hope that the activity will not die away and the good initiative will continue. For without testers, we will not get anything decent.
