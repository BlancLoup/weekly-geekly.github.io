E / M Commerce Day - grocery conference on electronic and mobile commerce



E / M Commerce Day , the first product conference on electronic and mobile commerce, will be held in Moscow on May 25. The event is organized by the group of companies DZ Systems and RAEC.

Representatives of the largest online stores, analytical agencies and b2c services will make presentations at the conference. As part of these presentations, the topics of integration of electronic and mobile commerce and offline retail, promotion of b2c services and mobile applications, trends in logistics and payment solutions will be covered.

Speakers include Mikhail Osin (Ozon.ru), Roman Kumar Vias (Qlean.ru), Fedor Virin (Data Insight), Vera Kozlova (Lamoda.ru), Felix Muchnik (SoftKey), Evgeny Tikhomirov (Yandex.Market), Andrey Indirev (QIWI) and many others.

Detailed information about the speakers and the program on the official website .
