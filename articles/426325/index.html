So, considering the TCP sequence numbers, we are actually going to look at two issues that are related to how the TCP handshake works. So let's spend some time looking at how a TCP connection is initially established.

Three packets are sent to establish a new TCP connection. Our client generates a packet to connect to the server, which says that here is my client IP address, I send it to the server. At the same time there is a packet header structure consisting of different areas, but we will be interested in the sequence number area.
