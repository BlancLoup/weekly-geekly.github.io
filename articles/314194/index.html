Interesting bookmarks UX / UI-designer

What thoughts come to you when you see other people's outstanding work? Surely, you admire them, as we do. But more importantly, you may be wondering how people achieved this, how they came to success. Where did they get their inspiration from? What did they search and browse on the Internet? Let's see what interesting things can be in the bookmarks of the UI / UX-designer.

Prototyping


1. Justinmind

A tool for creating high-precision prototypes. It will be more convenient to create prototypes of mobile applications. Gesture support makes it stand out against the background of other tools.



2. Mockplus

Mockplus is a rising star among prototyping tools. In version 2.2, its functionality and usability are significantly improved. It provides support for HTML export, and this suggests that Mockplus has an advantage over other tools. This development trend should not be underestimated.



3. Axure

We mentioned Justinmind and Mockplus and, of course, could not forget Axure. These three tools are constantly being compared. But Axure, being the ‚Äúelder brother‚Äù, retains its leading position.



Graphic design


1. Behance

If you are actively interested in the news of the design community, then you probably know about Behance. This is one of the largest communities in which excellent designers from around the world post their work. Here you can find a lot of great works on graphic design for every taste. First-class work will always be the best tool for studying design.



2. Dribble

Dribble is another useful resource for UI designers. The best designers are actively involved in the life of the site, so it can be considered an excellent option for studying and posting works. To join, you need to get an invitation. You may also find the advice of a designer who has been at Dribble for six years.



Web design


1. Awwwards

Awwwards is an excellent resource dedicated to creative sites with great design. Daily here is selected "site of the day." If you need fresh, creative ideas, on Awwwards you will find them in huge quantities.



2. DesignMoo

Here, the best designers post PSD sources, including buttons, icons, page effects.

The goal of DesignMoo is to create a platform that unites designers, within which they will be able to share sources, look for suitable options and get free access to them.



3. The Web Blend

The Web Blend is a community of designers, programmers and those who are obsessed with modern technology. Here you can find the latest news and trends from the world of web design, as well as find links to useful tools, sites, applications.



4. PrimeLiber

PrimeLiber is a Russian-language resource with a rich set of topics. Here you can find articles on UI / UX-design, SEO-optimization, programming and other issues related to product development and design.



Font design


I love typography

I love typography is a blog whose author shares fonts, templates, information and experience in the field of printing. Here are the best and most interesting articles on the topic of font design.

Inspiration


When inspiration is exhausted, set aside your phone and iPad and read books that describe the techniques and skills of renowned designers. You will visit the mass of creative ideas. By the way, we have already made a selection - The best books and resources for UI / UX-designers.

Selection - Professional resources for designers (This article is in Russian) - will also be a powerful source of inspiration.

Conclusion


Not every designer has these resources in bookmarks, and we are sure that you will benefit from being acquainted with them.
