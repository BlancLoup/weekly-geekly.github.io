CMS Magazine has studied the Russian web development market

Analytical portal “CMS Magazine” summed up the results of the first quarterly survey among professional participants in the web development market ( http://research.cmsmagazine.ru ).



The first survey was attended by 227 web studios, that is, about 16% of the total number of companies registered on the CMS Magazine project. Top managers of web studios were asked 12 questions. The format of the answers made it possible to talk about the situation in 2008, in the first quarter of 2009, and to make a forecast for the second quarter of this year.

Only generalized indicators, segmented by region, and comments of web studio managers are published.

Customers can get information about the average cost of developing a corporate website in Moscow, St. Petersburg and other regions, and answers to other important questions for them. Professional market participants will also find the necessary analytical information for them.

The results of the study are accompanied by public comments by representatives of web studios.

Among those who took part in the survey and left comments, such companies as: “Actis Systems”, “Articul Media”, “DEFA Interaktiv”, “Dot”, “X-Project”, “Grape”, “Aist”, “Atilekt” , Business Advertising and many other companies.

Expert Comments:

Anna Sverdlova , Actis Wunderman Managing Director

The availability of objective analytical information benefits both market participants and customers, contributing to the transparency of the market itself and the adoption of more informed decisions by customers.

Peter Didenko , Microsoft Internet Project Expert

We welcome new web development market research, as such projects make the market more open and understandable. We can say that now we are all entering the period of maturation of this market, since studies do not appear from scratch. This means that there is a market and it has matured to such an extent that it has become noticeable and interesting not only to its participants.
