Scan-Speak speakers, passive emitters and car component speakers
The brand Scan-Speak appeared in 1970 in Denmark, where the production and head office are still located. The company is proud of the quality of its products and notes that all the speakers are assembled only by hand. The company's products are exclusively represented in our stores.

Sica speakers, repair kits and horns
The Italian brand Sica has existed for more than 30 years and is present in the American, European and Asian markets. The company offers speakers for professional acoustics and musical instruments, as well as high quality service and customer feedback. We are the official representatives of the brand in Russia.


The Waterfall Audio brand is unique in the first place by the material from which the enclosures of the systems are made - glass or a combination of glass and aluminum.
