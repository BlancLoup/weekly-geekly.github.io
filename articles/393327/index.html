The Chinese have released a mini PC in the form of a folding Bluetooth keyboard



There are quite a few form factors of modern PCs, and almost every month something new appears. And recently (say, several years), Chinese manufacturers have been acting as an idea generator. One of the Chinese companies, Pipo, introduced the PC as a folding keyboard.

In appearance it is really a keyboard, but inside - the elements of the computer. On the left and right sides of the ‚Äúkeyboard‚Äù there are ports necessary for connecting third-party peripherals. And the characteristics of this PC are pretty good (of course, this is not a gaming system, you need to remember this).

Inside - the Intel Atom x5-Z8300 Cherry Trail processor, 2 or 4 GB of RAM, 32 or 64 GB of internal memory. Plus wireless communication modules 802.11ac WiFi and Bluetooth. As already mentioned, there are ports that the user needs for work. These are USB 3.0, USB 2.0, HDMI port, micro USB port, audio jack.
