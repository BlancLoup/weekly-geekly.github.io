Ultrabook Acer Aspire S7-191 video review



Acer Aspire S7-191 is a model from Acer's flagship ultrabook line, equipped with a 11.6-inch IPS display with a resolution of 1920x1080 and supporting touch input. In addition to the gorgeous display, the ultrabook boasts a very impressive performance for its size. The hardware is represented by low-voltage Intel Core i5 / i7 processors, 4 gigabytes of RAM and a fast SSD of 128 or 256 GB.

The thickness of the ultrabook is only 12 mm, and the weight is just over one kilogram. With amazing portability and high performance, the Aspire S7-191 also boasts long battery life. In addition to this, the package includes an additional external battery, with which you can increase the battery life by almost two times.
