How to get up to $ 85,500 free investment abroad on your startup in Start-Up Chile

Start-Up Chile is Chile 's state program that attracts innovative entrepreneurs from around the world to develop their start-ups using Chile as a platform to enter the world market. The main goal of the program is to transform Chile into an innovative and entrepreneurial hub for Latin America. Chile startup takes from 200 to 250 companies per year and consists of 3 programs depending on the startup stage:

  1. The S Factory is a pre-acceleration program for start-ups at an early stage, supporting women's entrepreneurship. Selected companies receive $ 10 million CLP (about USD $ 14K), do not give up the company's share and go through 3 months of acceleration. Two rounds per year for 20-30 companies each.
  2. Seed is an acceleration program for startups with a functional product and early validation. Selected companies receive $ 20 million CLP (about USD $ 30K), do not give up the company's share and go through 6 months of acceleration. Two rounds per year for 80-100 companies each.
  3. Scale is a follow-up foundation for the most successful startups that are registered in Chile, have traction and want to scale in Latin America and at the global level. Selected companies receive $ 60 million CLP (about USD $ 86K) do not give up the company's share, provided that they operate in Chile, and undergo 1 year of acceleration. Two rounds per year for 20-30 companies each.

Start-Up Chile is perhaps the most recognizable international startup program with entrepreneurs from all over the world, including from the CIS countries!
