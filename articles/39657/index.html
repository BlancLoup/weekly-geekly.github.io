What a startup can do in a month


This is a jubilee post where we want to celebrate the first month of work of our young project Picture for me . Exactly one month ago we reported on our service of bookmarks for Habr images to the community.
And so briefly what was added this month:

RSS (thanks to Paranoid );
PicPower (user power, ala habrasila), by it we determine the most active users;
added protection from "boobs", but there still need to work;
You can trace who watched your pictures, added comments and bookmarks;
for more convenient navigation on the sides of the image added arrows (thanks to EKCTPEMICT );
If you have switched to a picture by tag, you can use the arrows to view all the pictures of this tag; you can try this using the example of the gallery by tag Melissa Theuriau .

It also took a lot of time and effort to promote our project to the network, but it was a pleasant job, because many people support us with a good word or idea or even a beetle found;).

Special thanks to habraylu develop7 for the first post, ido , mad_s for ideas and nooze , Leprechaun , sosedoff , Nickolay , brutaler for bugs and for criticism of explanent .
Well, of course, to all 775 users of our service.
