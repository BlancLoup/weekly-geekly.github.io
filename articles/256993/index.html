Looking for the perfect review system for an online store

Hi, Habr! In the previous post, we talked about our review system for online stores, and today we made a comparison with other similar products. After all, reviews - perhaps the most inexpensive and effective way to increase site conversion. For feedback, you can write your own system or use a ready-made and proven solution.

Among the ready-made solutions in RuNet three systems were noted - Cackle Reviews , Mneniya.Pro and Shoppilot . We made an unbiased comparison of the mentioned systems for a variety of parameters and placed the results in a pivot table, simultaneously making a small overview of each system separately. The result was a kind of guide that will help you better understand the features of each system and select the appropriate product for your site.

1. Year of foundation


Was chosen according to archive.org/web . A small remark about Cackle: on archive.org, the first mentions of cackle.ru are in 2011 - then the comment service was implemented, and the Cackle Reviews system was released in 2013.

2. Download speed


For each product, one page of the site was selected where the solution is used. Then, from several computers (located in different networks), in turn, the selected pages were updated with a reset of the cache (Ctrl + F5) and each time the total average load time of all resources for each system was measured (Chrome DevTools, Network, filter by domain cackle.me , shoppilot.ru, dev.mneniya.pro). From the results obtained, the average time was the result.

4. The ‚ÄúLeave Feedback‚Äù link from the letter leads to the store website


It is about sending follow-up letters after purchase with an invitation to leave a review. Suppose a user receives a follow-up letter asking them to evaluate a newly purchased product. It is important that the link ‚ÄúLeave feedback‚Äù from the letter leads exactly to the page of your site (and not to the site of the feedback collection system) without any redirects. Otherwise, you will receive less feedback, as many users do not trust links to third-party sites.

It is also extremely important that when clicking on the link ‚ÄúLeave a review‚Äù, the form for posting a review is opened and the user is automatically authorized. For example, in the Cackle Reviews system, the ‚ÄúLeave feedback‚Äù link leads to the page of the purchased product of the online store, and all service parameters for opening a review writing form and automatic authorization are transmitted after the hash (#) in the link address. In other systems, links lead to the solution developer's site, in the form of writing a review, without automatic authorization, which, of course, reduces the transitions and the number of comments left.

6. Collect feedback from Yandex.Market


For Cackle Reviews and Shoppilot, several sites were found where reviews downloaded from Yandex.Market are visible (for example, forma-odezhda.ru , delonghi.club , digital.ru , wiki-tovar.ru ).

8. Automatic indexing in Google with micro markup


Micromarking is a special format by which other systems, such as search engines, understand and display data in a certain way. Here we are talking about micromarking reviews schema.org/reviews schema.org/reviews . On Google, it looks like pretty stars reflecting the current page rank:


Automatic indexing of micro-markers of a rating, for example in Cackle Reviews, works simply through JavaScript. Using Cackle Reviews you can forget about the difficult setting of saving reviews to a local database or manipulating Apache (Nginx) to pull external HTML-code.

Example: in Google, find ‚Äúsite: kinderus.ru based on a review‚Äù, go to any page from the search result, say, kinderus.ru/products/7173 , and open the display in HTML-form. In the HTML code, reviews and micro-markup are missing, which means that everything works automatically through JavaScript.

9. Broadcast reviews in social networks


Here the system Cackle Reviews again distinguished. Reviews are broadcast on VKontakte, My World, Facebook, Twitter with a link to the site where the solution is installed. As a result, you get additional traffic and popularity among the friends of the reviewer. You can try the broadcast on the solution demonstration page.


In other social authorization systems there is no, respectively, no cross-posting (broadcast).

12. Single authorization with the site


This is when an authorized user on your site is automatically logged in to the feedback system. Useful when follow-up mailing letters. The user clicks on the "Leave feedback" from the letter and when he goes to the form of writing a review, he is automatically authorized.

19. Moderation reviews


Cackle Reviews has a built-in administration panel for moderating reviews in real time, that is, you choose, approve a review or reject it. Shoppilot and Mneniya.Pro, on the contrary, moderate reviews on their own.

Indexing reviews with a micro-mark ranking on Google


As mentioned above, when installing Cackle Reviews on your site, Google starts scanning pages automatically, indexing reviews with a micro markup rating.

Collect reviews from Yandex.Market


To collect it is necessary through the API (Cackle Reviews) to load products from your online store in Cackle, JSON format. Each item must have a special parameter, ymId, that takes the modelId value from Yandex.Market.

Single authorization with an online store


It is possible to make sure that users registered on your site are automatically logged into Cackle Reviews with their name, avatar and profile link. The possibility greatly simplifies writing reviews, because in this case, the user does not need to enter their data or select a social network for authorization. As a result, you get more feedback.

SPAM protection


The only feedback system with built-in spam protection. Once you mark a review as spam and the system will automatically remember your action. The same or similar next review will be marked as spam automatically. If you approve a spam review, the system will remove it from the database. This is how learning happens. A well-trained SPAM protection system can replace your moderator.

In addition, there is support for Akismet .

Moderation Panel reviews


Unlike Shoppilot and Mneniya.Pro, reviews of Cackle Reviews are moderated by you yourself. Any review can be approved, marked as spam, delete, edit (flaws, advantages, comment - everything except the rating).

Moderation takes place in Real-time mode with Tray and sound notifications. There are group operations, search for reviews by the author, IP, everything on the page, a ban on time with the cause, warnings, information about the browser and other features.
