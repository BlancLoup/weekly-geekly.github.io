0. General


The application works in five main screens (not counting authorization): user profile, post tape, favorites, map and ‚Äúvtykator‚Äù. For each screen, the functionality is transferred to the maximum from the web version.


1. Authorization


The application allows you to work without authorization, but with limited functionality. When you try to go to one of the closed bookmarks, the user will receive an offer to register or log in.

Login and Registration Screens


For an authorized user, all the functionality of the application is fully accessible. You can register directly from the application. They say the first 500 registered through the application donated beer.


2. Profile


A bookmark with a profile accumulates all the information and user tools.
Here you can change and add data, change the avatar (take a picture or choose from an i-gallery), add or delete friends, read the feed of their messages.

First profile page


New entries can be made in two formats - a regular post with photos (you can also take a picture or choose from the gallery) or a hosca. Hoska is a special type of recording similar to tweet (limiting the length plus a set of templates).


3. Feed and Favorites


Feed - a list of posts and hawks sorted by time. The content of the tape depends on how the filter is configured, the main regulator of the tape.

News feed and main filter


Each post on the portal F5.ru has a set of characteristics. Of the usual characteristics - the type of the author (user, or columnist), the type of recording (text / hosca / photopost, videopost).
There is also a very strange parameter - mood-o-meter. It can have one of three values ‚Äã‚Äã- Brain On, Fun, Brain Off. The mood-o-meter parameter depends on the content of the post and is manually estimated by users. The filter collects all the basic parameters of the posts and makes it possible to collect a set of posts in the feed according to the mood and current interests.
For each post, of course, there is the possibility of evaluation and commenting.
Pay special attention to the video - the client has the opportunity to watch it. For this server-side videos are specially encoded. I have already posted one video with a roboporno on the portal, you can watch directly from iPhone.


4. Map


Convenient service for users of the portal. On the map, integrated with Google maps, you can track your location, located near the distribution points of the newspaper.

F5 newspaper distribution map and search filter


For F5 cards, an additional element of sociality was introduced - on the map you can see the location of your friends who are currently on the portal.


5. "Vtykator"


A unique mechanism for choosing a random post. The mechanism is visualized in the form of "floating" random headers. Too lazy to think - stick to the "vtykator."

Page "Vtykator"


The title can be selected by tap on an interesting topic, or you can shake the iPhone. The iPhone's accelerometer tracks movement and the app selects a random post on its own.
