Developer Conference eTalks Summer 2010

This year, on September 4-5, the eTalks Summer 2010 conference will be held in Samara for the second time. The conference is aimed at programmers and sympathizers (testers, project managers ...). On the first day of the reports and communication, in the second master classes.

The site has already announced several reports:
* Manage requirements changes
* Risk Driven Testing
* Cross-platform code for applications under
iPhone and iPad
* ... and not only SQL
* Personal programmer effectiveness
* [Workshop] Introduction to
web programming (http, rest, mvc)
* [Workshop] The basics of git for a programmer

Participation for speakers, students and participants of the last year is free (until August 15). For the rest, a small amount (300r) to reduce the cost of organizing the conference (sponsors, ay! :)).

The number of places is not very large (up to 120 people) and more than half are already filled, therefore,
Actually, registration: summer2010.etalks.ru/registration

Specification in time : registration will begin at 11 o'clock, reports at 12, will end at 18.
