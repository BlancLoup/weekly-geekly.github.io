Information Relations. How to extract experience from the environment

It will be about what kind of informational relations we can enter.

All entities in reality are somewhat dependent on each other. I will call these dependencies relationships. They can be combined into different groups:
  • Link / Property
  • Interaction / Contact
  • Removal / Transfer


Relationships of each group can go into the remaining two. Now I will tell you what their essence is.

Link / Property


This is the most pronounced phenomenon in the properties of the information environment. Example: A child has a relationship with his father. The father is in another part of the galaxy. The father died, the child instantly became an orphan.

Interaction / Contact


The existence of an interaction requires the existence of two entities. If the entity has a reflection, then one is enough (the case when you can talk to yourself). Removal / Transfer


The most difficult and most valuable group of relationships. To remove something means to have in oneself the properties characteristic of another entity, which become part of the subject after contact with this entity. Example: I bought a mug with a pen, you can pour water into it.
