Pliq.me. Hot startup looking for his tester

How often does it turn out to combine business with pleasure? For example, free to use a new convenient IT-service and help developers make it better? Pliq.me is the case.

The team of the previously introduced service for converting speech to text for mobile platforms has recently released versions of the program for Symbian and Windows Mobile in external testing, the purpose of which is to attract and record everyone in a closed test group.

The project included in the top ten of Russian Startup Chellenge 2010 is a voice interface of a mobile phone: all SMS, e-mails, reminders can be simply dictated, and Pliq.me will do the rest - convert it into text and send it to the specified destination. Today the service offers such features as:
