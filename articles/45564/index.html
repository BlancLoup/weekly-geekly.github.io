Apple spends more money on advertising than Microsoft

The answer, as is often the case, was found fairly quickly and it can somewhat dampen the ardor of devoted makovods.

Lindsay Blakely, a former reporter for the American edition of Business 2.0, and now a Bnet journalist, stumbled upon the real numbers of Apple's advertising budget somewhere in the bins of a financial report.

So, for the 2008 fiscal year, which ended with the Yabloko past September, $ 486,000,000 was spent only on advertising. In 2007: $ 467,000,000; in 2006: $ 338,000,000.
