42goals - taking into account personal goals and daily affairs



The Team is happy to introduce a new project: 42goals.com . The new service is designed to account for personal goals and daily affairs.

The principle of operation is very simple: you add a list of goals and note achievements for them. Targets can currently be of three types:
  1. "Piece", for example: the number of cigarettes smoked, cups of coffee, approaches to the bar
  2. ‚ÄúNumerical‚Äù: daily jogging distance, your expenses or income

We have added the most frequent goals to the list of templates, from which you can add a goal to your list with one click.
