Colorful decades of the last century

Zero's youth dreamed of being gadget hung


Yes, just like that: gadget compressed. Here are four gadgets, without which we did not live in the first decade of the new century:


- mobile phone or communicator;
- digital camera;
- flash drive;
- mp3 player.

Her Majesty Mobile


During this decade, the mobile phone has turned from an expensive thing into a daily routine. His visible antenna fell off, the screen increased, it became colored. The phone absorbed the video and photo camera, notebook, Internet connection, mp3 player and USB flash drive. Digital camera


Yes, digital cameras are now for everyone. And it first of all sharply raised the quality of photos. The ability to shoot a lot, the ability to immediately see the composition and exposure, the powerful resources of Photoshop did their job. Today a sensible young photographer shoots better than the wise and experienced. Just because in his hands a new tool.



Flash drive


A flash drive settled everywhere: in a handbag, in a jeans pocket, in an expensive leather briefcase. On it are documents, photos, films, programs. Install operating systems on it. Many are already nostalgic for DVD-RW.



Although the flash drive itself may suffer the fate of extinction - in the future, perhaps the data will be synchronized with storage servers (thanks to lam0x86 ).



MP3 player


Although it is dying off as a separate type of device, because it migrates to a mobile phone, but in this decade, the MP3 player was an important gadget. He discovered a new degree of freedom.

Main gadget: Internet


At the beginning of the decade there was a joke about the fridge with Internet access. Now she is not funny, because:

Fast, mobile and unlimited internet has become a common need. The Internet is not a gadget, of course. But the gadget without going online is becoming something very vintage.
