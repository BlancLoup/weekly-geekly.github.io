The results of the competition startups

Dear habrovchane!
The results of the IT start-up competition, which was held from May 12 to July 1, 2010 by IT-GRAD, were summed up. About 20 applications from start-ups that need the data center infrastructure were submitted to the competition.

Immediately I would like to say that it was very difficult to choose winners, but special attention was paid to those projects that are engaged in the development of technologically innovative solutions. First of all, this is due to global trends in the development of information systems, with the growing popularity of cloud computing and services.

The winners are:


1st place: Hivext - a platform for developing cloud internet applications
2 place: Aximedia Movie Editor - online video editor
3rd place: IPP online printing
4th place: Vicanus browser game

Congratulations to the winners and wish you success in the development of projects!
