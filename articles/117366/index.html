IT in medicine in the Innovators Club

March 26, 2011 was the 18th meeting of the Club Innovators. She was devoted to information technology in medicine.



The experience of introducing information systems in Russian medical institutions, the legal and ethical difficulties of such systems in Russia and the inapplicability of foreign experience to domestic realities was shared with the public by the Deputy Director General of KORUS Consulting Inna Ashenbrenner .

In contrast to domestic difficulties with the informatization of medical services, Maxim Ignatov , a project manager from e-Legion , told the audience about his experience of working with several major hospitals in the United States of America. There, the health care system and medical care are built on a completely different principle than in Russia.

In his speech, Maxim recalled the notorious TV series Doctor House and explained that what we see in this and similar films is indeed the reality of American public health.
