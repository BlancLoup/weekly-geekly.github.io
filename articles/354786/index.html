Obtaining a work permit in the Czech Republic: the case of a dual labor card

Good afternoon, dear readers!

In the comments to my last article on employment in the Czech Republic a question arose about the legal aspects of working in the Czech Republic. In this article I would like to delve a little into this topic and consider one of the cases of obtaining a labor card - the case of the so-called dual card. This case is of particular interest because it is more complicated than obtaining a non-dual card and there are some nuances that are often difficult to solve without the help of a lawyer. Who cares, I ask under the cat.

Introduction


To begin, I will explain what is meant by the dual labor card. Dual employment card involves obtaining a work and residence permit in the country. A non-dual employment card serves only as a residence permit. Immediately clarify that the article discusses the situation for a person who already has a long-term visa or residence permit. Being abroad apply through the embassy or consulate.

I myself found myself in the situation of the need to receive such a card, so I set forth everything in the article based on my own experience. So, a few words about yourself. For this reason, I do not have free access to the labor market and therefore I am considering the option of a dual card.
