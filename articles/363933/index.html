Volunteer programmers save the world!


Right now, my provider continues to block GitHub, and every new news in the feed is more and more amazing. Habr and Giktayms are full of reports of new amazing initiatives by the authorities. Popular initiatives on ROI - with them everything is hopeless.

What to do? Where to go?

I wanted to write that the code of the project IT-volunteer is available on GitHub, but now it is even a funny joke.

However, the blocked githab released several minutes of free time from Habr's coding, make-up and design visitors of Habr, which will soon become even more (see Habr not for this ).

One problem - they do not know how and cannot code / design / impose, they often do not know how and cannot put the simplest website on Wordpress.
