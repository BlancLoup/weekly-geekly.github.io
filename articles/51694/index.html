Yandex. Photos for iPhone / iPod touch

Today, the Yandex.Fotky application for iPhone / iPod touch has become available in the framework of the Yandex.Nano project in the App Store (the link will open in iTunes). The application allows you to upload photos that exist on the phone as well as directly from the camera of the device, if available, to the Yandex.Device service. Before downloading, you can specify a name, select an album or publish a photo in Yarushka. After uploading a photo in one click, you can launch the browser and view the downloaded photo on Yandex.
