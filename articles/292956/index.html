Mobile application developers earned $ 857 million over the year on advertising that users did not see

Thousands of applications for smartphones receive money from advertisers for advertising that users do not see. The company Forensiq, analyzing applications for combating fraud, found that some devices download ads at a rate at which an ordinary person can not view it. Later, the company conducted a study: on every cell of a smartphone in the US, at least one application with invisible advertising was launched.

Forensiq did research for ten days. Invisible ad apps were launched on one percent of smartphones in the United States and on two to three percent of devices in Europe and Asia. The company has identified more than five thousand applications on iOS and Android devices that spend up to two gigabytes of traffic per day, downloading ads. Some applications "show" up to twenty ads per minute, sometimes they scroll through ads after closing an application.

The head of Forensiq noted that the list includes applications used by smartphone owners and performing specific tasks. These applications have hundreds of positive reviews on the App Store and Google Play. The company reported on one of the suspects: this is an application for breastfeeding from the publication American Baby and the developer Sevenlogics. The application with four stars from users "does not show" advertising Olive Garden, Amazon and IBM.
