Yandex - now everything is in Chinese

On the eve of closer cooperation between Russia and China, Yandex wants to explore the Chinese market and plans to open an office in Shanghai. According to a Vedomosti representative, the company does not plan to compete directly with the local search giant Baidu.

At the moment, Yandex has already reached an agreement with China: with the help of Yandex and other advertising platforms, Chinese comrades will be able to enter the Russian market.

Previously, people and companies in China have been told about opening access to attract Chinese advertisers to the Russian market.

There is no official decision to open an office in Shanghai yet; meanwhile, Yandex has an international sales office in Switzerland.
