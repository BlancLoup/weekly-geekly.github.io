In 2006, the entire Far East will be covered with Internet

All settlements in the Far Eastern Federal District (FEFD) in 2006 will be provided with universal telephone services and Internet access. It is reported by "Prime-Tass" with reference to the video conference of the Minister of Information Technology and Communications Leonid Reiman.

According to him, for this purpose, in the framework of the implementation of universal communication services in 2006, it is planned to install more than 2,000 payphones in the DFO and open more than 900 public access points to the Internet. At present, this goal has already been achieved on Kamchatka, as far away as Luna, where 100% of the settlements are provided with universal communication services.
