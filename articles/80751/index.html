Second update for N900



Within days, Nokia will post a second update for its N900 Maemo device. The update will be available as "by air" from the device itself, and through a computer.

Of the main changes:
  • Added support for Microsoft Exchange 2003
  • Improved interface Ovi Maps
  • Fixed a problem with the SIM cards of the Three operator (important for me)
  • Work on bugs and stability

and all sorts of different goodies. Full list of changes here .

The N900 will notify you when the update is available for download.
