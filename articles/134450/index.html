Russian startups in the Spanish village

As it was written earlier, StartupPoint and Priority investment company sent 8 startups for 3 months to the Spanish Village business accelerator in Spain.

On December 14 at the Spanish Village in Moscow , the teams will talk about the work done and all the pros and cons of working abroad. Already we can say that all projects have reached a fundamentally new level, having finished the product and received a lot of advice from accelerator mentors.

One of the most memorable projects in the Spanish countryside was LifeTable .

LifeTable are interactive screens for installation in cafes and restaurants.

LifeTable multifunction and shockproof screens allow visitors to get acquainted with the electronic menu, place an order and control the account.

In addition, inside the institution creates a real social network in which you can chat, give gifts, play games, order music and go online.
