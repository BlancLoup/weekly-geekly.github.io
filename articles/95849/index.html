Deus Ex 3 will be released in early 2011


Eidos has released a new video game Deus Ex: Human Revolution, from which it became known that the release of the project will take place in early 2011. The third part of the legendary series is created on the modified Crystal Dynamics Engine engine and will be released on PC, Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3.

The developers of Eidos Montreal claim that the new game will have a ‚Äúbright and exciting‚Äù single player campaign, the passage of which will become non-linear. In Human Revolution there will be a well-thought-out, branched plot in which goals can be achieved in different ways. Unfortunately, multiplayer modes in Deus Ex 3 is not provided.

Despite the desire to preserve the traditions of the series, some changes will affect the combat system of the game - for example, automatic recovery of health will appear. However, like the first part of the series, the new project is still a mixture of first-person shooter and a role-playing game in the cyberpunk genre.

The Human Revolution plot will take players to the year 2027 - 25 years before the action of the first part of Deus Ex. Nanoimplants have not yet been invented, but mechanical body modifiers are widely used. As the developers said, the main character of the game will be Adam Jensen, a security officer of the corporation engaged in biomechanical modifications.

The first part of the series Deus Ex was released on PC and Mac ten years ago - on June 22, 2000, and two years later a PS2 version appeared. The game was favored by critics and has received many awards and cult status from the players.
