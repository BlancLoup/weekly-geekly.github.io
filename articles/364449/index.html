Gaming Notebook ASUS G751JT Review



Gaming laptops have always been designed for a very specific audience - wealthy gamers. After all, the "game" means the top, the most powerful stuffing. This, of course, dictates a certain price level. In addition, a powerful processor and video card always required an appropriate cooling system, which is not easy to fit into a limited amount of notebook, while maintaining the comfort of working with the device. So gaming laptops have always been a kind of technological frontier, the cutting edge of personal computers. Therefore, not only the characteristics, but also how such machines cope with modern demanding games, always arouse interest. For example, this new model of gaming laptop, ASUS G751JT.

This is the average model from the G751J notebook line. It differs from the older one with a slightly weaker video card (NVidia GTX970M instead of GTX980M) and a processor (Intel Core i7-4710 instead of i7-4860).

Complete set and appearance




The laptop comes in a big, but rather thin (less than 9.5 cm) box. Included are: user manual, cable tie for cables and power supply. It is large and heavy, because it has to feed a very powerful stuffing.



The laptop itself is big, thick and heavy. Taking in hand, you immediately realize that this device is designed for serious battles.



The cover is made of matte dark gray, almost black, soft-touch plastic. It is very pleasant to the touch, not smooth and not rough. In the middle of the lid an inset is made of plastic styled as brushed aluminum with ASUS logos and the Republic of Gamers product line.



The back, where the battery is usually located, is thicker and wider than usual. Here are located powerful plate radiators, covered with decorative grilles of red plastic.





The bottom of the notebook is made of textured plastic and has a complex surface configuration.

Display


The ASUS G751JT display uses an IPS matrix, so color distortion does not occur when the tilt angle changes. The horizontal viewing angle is 178 degrees, the quality of color rendering is high. Particularly pleased with the matte surface of the screen.



With a 17.3-inch diagonal, the resolution of 1920x1080 is the golden mean: the image is fairly smooth, but there is no Windows-specific problem when the interface elements become too small. In addition, a higher resolution would create an additional load on the video card during games.

Performance and work experience


The computing power of the laptop is provided by an Intel Core i7 4710HQ processor operating at 2.5 GHz. The computer's RAM is 16 GB, more than enough for even the most modern and demanding games.

The laptop loads very quickly. I do not know how the developers managed to achieve this, but the login menu appears already after 9 seconds after pressing the power button. Probably, the capabilities of the system SSD are very well used here.

I tested the laptop not only in synthetic tests, but also in Battlefield 4. The settings were twisted to Ultra, while the number of FPS ranged from 40 to 65, an average of 55. At the same time, the laptop worked almost silently during Internet surfing. Only occasionally there was a slight rustle of blown air, but the noise level did not even come close to the level of discomfort. After a few tens of minutes of the game, the laptop case became only slightly warmer. ASUS engineers managed to make a very efficient and relatively compact cooling system.



Regarding the sound of the built-in subwoofer, do not feed any illusions: this is just a small speaker with all the ensuing consequences. There is no talk of any bass, at best, it reproduces trimmed mid frequencies. But when you connect headphones, the sound quality turned out to be very decent and detailed. I did not twist the equalizer and listened with the basic settings. And I was impressed by the sound of games and music of various genres.

It took about two hours to fully charge the battery. In the surfing mode with periodic viewing of videos, the laptop worked all day. And with games and benchmarks, I put the battery in about three hours.

Conclusion


Power notebook allows you to comfortably play the most modern games, do not hesitate to set the maximum settings. The cooling system was surprisingly effective.

Who can recommend this laptop? The answer is obvious - avid gamers. Especially those who do not have too much free space for a powerful desktop gaming computer. Also, ASUS G75JT will appeal to lovers of various gaming tournaments, congresses and LAN parties. Still, this laptop will carry much more convenient than a full-fledged system unit. Actually, three different connectors for connecting external display devices both hint and contribute to the high social activity of the owner of this powerful machine.

The second category of users who may be interested in this device are specialists in 3D graphics and its enthusiasts. Of course, larger displays are preferable for such tasks, but they may be interesting as an auxiliary, relatively compact and to some extent mobile G751JT device.
