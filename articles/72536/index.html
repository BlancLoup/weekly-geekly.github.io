NeoTokyo - New Source Mod



You should start with the fact that this modification was developed by the American studio RADI-8, and began its existence on the Unreal Tornament 2004 engine from October 2004 and now released on the Source engine and is free. This is a beautiful, team shooter, made in the sci-fi style and inspired by the anime "Ghost in the Shell", "AKIRA", and from the game Counter-Strike. Compared to other existing mods for free, the game is not only notable for very beautiful and well-developed maps, weapons and characters, but also a very interesting gameplay. The goal of the game is to capture the " ghost " and take it to the enemy base, or kill all opponents. In achieving this goal you will be helped by 20 different types of weapons, and 3 different classes, each of them is unique and has its own unique instrument of vision.
The owner of the "ghost" (flag) can see all members of the enemy team at a distance of 50m.
