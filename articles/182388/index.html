Voting and likes

Hello! With you again Irina and summer outside the window :) It's time for holidays and long-awaited rest. And so want to do something nice! Friends, it's time to pamper your employees a little and, for example, choose a place for a summer corporate event with them. It would seem - what is there to do: compile lists of employees, appoint a responsible person, pick up 3 options, collect the opinion of employees, evaluate the results and start training.

But even this small task can take a lot of time and, by the way, nerves (endless clarifications, questions, whether to invite Sidorov or not, resentment if someone has been forgotten, every minute Klavochka's mood change: “now I want to go to the sea” I would go skiing ”, in an hour:“ yes, this is his corporate party, I’m not very good in my personal life! ”), etc. etc. We offer you a “few seconds” way to quickly determine the place of the corporate party and give the team a little fun. “Huskies” come to the rescue!

A couple of years ago, you would be puzzled to ask: “And here are the dogs?” And now you won’t surprise anyone: referring to likes of comments (put like or “I like”).

Likes in the comments

The mechanism is very simple:

- You create the task “Summer corporate 2013” ​​in “Simple business” ; in the field of assignment, check all the departments at once or just those people you want to invite. You can make it even easier: after creating a task, right-click on it, select “Access” and place one single hook in front of your organization - the task will open to all employees at once.

- You write that we can choose a place for a corporate party and throw 3 options with three comments and, as they say, "the heat has gone." Employees right-click on the option they like and choose “I like” or “I do not like” or if at least one person has already voted - you can click on the “Class” icon and then the voice will also be taken into account.

- Here you can have a discussion, throw a photo of the places you offer, in general, everything depends on imagination and on the time you set aside for the employees to “have fun” :) The results of the voting are very clear, nothing needs to be considered, everything is automatically displayed in a convenient list.


Friends, I hope I managed to cheer you up a little bit and my today's "trick" will also be useful to you.

See you soon, Irina.
