Get leads through ads on Facebook



I want to tell you about a recent Facebook opportunity advertising for getting leads. She will help to contact with users who have shown interest in your company by filling out the form and providing their data. How does such advertising work and what to do with the collected contacts, read on.

How does it work:

Advertising for lead generation looks like a simple ad in a news feed on Facebook.
Only when a user clicks on an ad does he not go to the landing page, but to the application form directly on the social network.
There he specifies his contact details, for example, full name, e-mail. mail, phone number, date of birth, etc. Moreover, the fields are filled in automatically based on the data from the account (information can be edited, if necessary). This saves time. Especially if the transition was made from a mobile phone.
After the user answers the questions in the questionnaire. You can ask up to 3 questions.



The advertiser can change the application form for themselves, for example, use open-ended questions or multiple-choice questions.
