DevCon 2016. Opening of the conference at 10:00! Connect to the online broadcast today

Today, May 25 at 10:00, the opening of the largest conference for developers, testers, architects, project managers and all interested - Microsoft DevCon 2016 begins .



We are pleased to invite you to the online broadcast of the conference. At 9:30 a direct inclusion from the online studio awaits you, and at 10:00 the opening will begin, within the framework of which all the major latest topics on software development on the platform and using Microsoft tools will be presented.

Immediately after the opening and a short break, you will find many reports on the topics Windows, Azure, Office, reports from community experts on various topics and a startup track specially prepared this year. Examine the schedule and connect to the broadcast of selected reports.

Today, May 25, the reports will be held on all topics from web development to the Internet of Things, from games to deep immersion in the services of a cloud platform. It will be very interesting!
