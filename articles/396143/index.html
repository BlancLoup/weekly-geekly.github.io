Roskomnadzor entered Qiwi and Skrill into the unified register of prohibited sites



Roskomnadzor today added Qiwi and Skrill to the unified register of prohibited sites. The department decided to take action due to the fact that the resources contain information, the dissemination of which is prohibited in Russia, writes "Lenta". Thus, on some pages of payment system sites information is placed on the opportunity to participate in a game of chance, and it also describes the procedure for transferring funds, including electronic payments, to participate in this game of chance.

In July 2014, Russia earned a law obliging all bookmakers working on the Runet to become members of self-regulatory organizations (SRO). Somewhat later, a law on interactive rates was passed, according to which SRO was required to create special centers for accounting and translation of interactive rates (TSUPIS). These centers must monitor all online betting in the country. The first TsUPIS appeared in mid-February 2016.
