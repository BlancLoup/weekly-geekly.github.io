Little man in a box

‚ÄúIndependent‚Äù game developers give a new reason for joy every day.
And today, in one of the blogs, I came across an amazing implementation of one very simple idea. The idea is to combine the real world and the game.

In this case, magic happens literally at your fingertips. More specifically in the box. The game controller is a combination of a web camera and a black cube with markings on the edges for recognition. The computer watches as you turn the cube in your hands and projects an image inside it. The projection takes place on the screen, naturally.

Tilting the cube, you force the little man inside to move through the maze, go through the door, climb the stairs. Turning the cube, you move to a new room.
