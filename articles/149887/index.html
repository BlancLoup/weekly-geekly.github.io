Sleepbox Sleep Cabs On Sale



Almost three years ago on Habrahabr it was reported about the development of a cabin for sleeping (or just relaxing at any time of the day) Sleepbox. A portable unit with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã3.75 m 2 is installed at airports, train stations, shopping centers, in offices - wherever people want to relax. The cabin is equipped with a bed, a ventilation system, an LCD TV, WiFi, sockets for laptops, charging for phones, luggage space, a sliding work desk, a mirror, etc.

Commentators expressed the opinion that this is too good to be true - and they were wrong. It took two years to apply for patents in Europe, the United States and Russia, to start production - and that's it. The first experimental cockpit in Moscow was put in Sheremetyevo a year ago , and now the company Sleepbox LLC has started taking orders for the shipment of goods.



Sleepbox is rented for a period from 15 minutes to several hours with time-based payment. Payment is made through the built-in terminal that controls the lock box.

The owner of the cabin itself sets the price for renting the premises, but it is reasonable to count on $ 50-100 of daily income, that is, the cabin will bring the owner about $ 30,000 per year. The official website says that after ordering you will have to wait for the manufacture of the cabin for 2 months, and then another 2-4 weeks for delivery. Cabin cost is reported upon individual handling. Delivery will cost an additional 10-20% of the cost. When ordering from 100 booths, you can enter into agreements for exclusive service Sleepbox on the agreed territory.

Sleepbox cabin is protected by European patent number 001742719, Russian patent RU2010501345 and US patent (filed).
