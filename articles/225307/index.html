EFF souvenirs for Tor owners

The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) announced the launch of the Tor Challenge campaign to encourage the creation of new nodes on the Tor network. Any new Tor traffic node created on June 4, 2014 or later, as well as any old node that after this date will increase the throughput of RelayBandwidthRate and RelayBandwidthBurst by at least 128 KB / s, compared to the average bandwidth, can participate in the promotion. until this date.

To participate in the Tor Challenge program, you must register by entering a digital fingerprint (in hex) of the Riley. You should first make sure that the nickname of your site appears in the Globe directory. You can register anonymously, that is, without publishing the name in the list of participants, and you can order a gift from the EFF to an anonymous box or receive it in person in order not to declassify your place of residence.

The easiest thing to do is install Linux on Linux: just download Tor, change a few lines in the configuration files, and run the program.

Under Windows, there is an implementation with a Vidalia GUI .





EFF seeks to stimulate long-term work of new rileev, so souvenir stickers will get those whose nodes will still work 12 months after the start of the campaign. Owners of riley on 1+ MB / s in addition to the stickers put T-shirts. To receive a gift, you must specify your email when registering, instructions will arrive there in a year. If the souvenir is not needed, email can be omitted. As for the anonymous mailbox, see above.
