Countryman from Evernote


We like Evernote with its design, ideology and, most importantly, we have long used it ourselves. Therefore, it was interesting to talk with them. We were looking for contact with Evernote among all our friends. As a result, through a chain of 8 people, we managed to meet with Dmitry, one of the founders of the company.
Dmitry gave us a couple of T-shirts with their logo, told a lot of interesting things about the markets of China, Japan and Russia. I will briefly retell his story in my own words:
  • in Japan are ready to try new things and do it yourself. That is, your service can become popular in Japan without promotion, users can come themselves.
  • The Chinese market is almost infinitely large, but under tight censorship. It is necessary to share information carefully. In fact, any service that wants to operate in the Chinese market must create its own separate Chinese fake version.
  • Russia. You can't understand it with your mind: the market is big, but small. Our users need the simplest basic things: online store, search, news, communication. Therefore, services with a new functionality better run in other markets.

In general, we had the feeling that because of the Russian roots, Evernote managers feel something like responsibility for their compatriots: despite the fact that Dmitry did not catch fire with our idea, we communicated mentally and sensibly.
