Report with mitap for back-end developers

On September 6, an informal Mitap for back-end developers was held at the Flacon Design Factory.


We were frightened by hurricane, thunder and cold. It did not work, more than 150 participants joined us!

Hot mulled wine and burgers, warm atmosphere. And also hot topics of speeches:

- How to spend time at the interview
- Why Big Data is Useless
- How to implement Kubernetes and not go crazy
- Why sometimes architecture needs to be changed just like that
- About Docker




Guest speaker Vladimir Yartsev , CTO at Cloud Castle and creator of dockhero.io. In the past - the system programmer and system administrator. In the present - Ruby-on-Rails-developer and adept Heroku. Believes in The Twelve Factors. Which told all about Heroku!

It was cozy and fresh. A lot of live communication, a lot of interesting questions and no less interesting answers.

Well, it was not without HYIP, or rather antihipe! Our invited guest was a Russian hip-hop artist, a member of rap battles Purulent, aka Slava KPSS, Sonya Marmeladova, Valentin Dyadka.
