Survey on search resources

Hello.

There was a reason to consult. In the list of search engines browser Vivaldi is Wikipedia. At the moment, the search is carried out on the English version by default, which is not good for Russia and other countries. But at the same time, the majority of Ukrainian and Belarusian Internet users prefer to look for information in Russian. Hence the question: what is the default link for Ukraine and Belarus - Russian or native? I ask all interested to take part in the survey. Also, a more detailed presentation of their point of view is welcome in the comments.

UPDATE

The issue is resolved - we will add a native search in accordance with the browser locale selected during installation. But please remember that this will only work with a new installation - the search engine update will not change.

Thanks to all the voters and commentators for the help!
