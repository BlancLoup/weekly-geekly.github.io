To help startups

Recently, a fresh newspaper from our government came out to help our entrepreneurs.
A new document on this in many reference systems is still missing. In fact, the topic is a comment on this document + some personal experience.

The document implies that any person can receive 58,800 rubles from the state for the start of their business, plus another 58,800 for each employee hired. Hiring a disabled person, the state will compensate 30 tr. on equipment locations. In general, it sounds very interesting and tempting ...


In practice, it does not look much sadder, you should officially be listed on the labor exchange, if you want to get these 58 tr. then the question arises, and if I want to open my own business, combining with work for hire, then what ?? As I was told at the stock exchange everything breaks off, you have an income, sit and be silent. Well, whoever is already working, he will have to open his own business on his capital.

The second question - they became an individual entrepreneur and decided to get another 58 tr, then you need to hire an official officially, but for a person who is not prepared for finance, this will entail additional difficulties and costs, you must immediately register with the FSS, PF and pay taxes there, taking into account the new laws on raising taxes to 34% from 2011 from the wage fund, these 58 tr. become dubious pleasure. It is easier to work in black at first, which most do.
