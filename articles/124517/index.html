Device Seneye connects the aquarium to the Internet



Well, the fish will not be able to work on the Web, no, but the owner of the aquarium will be able to monitor the main indicators (temperature, ammonia concentration in water, etc.) from anywhere in the world. Seneye is a kind of new word in monitoring the life support system of an aquarium. The device will come in three different types, Seneye Home, Seneye Pond, and Seneye Reef. Older models are designed to track performance on larger systems than a home aquarium.

Seneye is a universal sensor, immersed in water, and connected to the web. As mentioned above, this device reads indicators such as pH, ammonia concentration, light intensity, water temperature. These statistics can be obtained by the owner of the aquarium in any place where there is Internet.

The sensor can also be configured to send a text message to the owner if something goes wrong. Generally. quite a useful device for all aquarists, especially for those who are rarely at home, but want to know what happens to pets and their habitat.

Now Seneye can not be bought, this device will appear a little later, at a price of $ 150.
