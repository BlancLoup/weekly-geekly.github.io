Videophone VZOchat.com

So I decided to tell you about our project. Its essence is the videophone in the Internet. The product is called VZOchat. Currently, about 500 thousand users.

Key features:
- group VIDEO conferences
- video quality on weak communication channel
- availability of a web client (web widget)
- availability of a mobile client (release in the coming days)
- mode 2x120 people
- absolutely free

To start using this miracle it is enough to have a webcam, average computer performance and an Internet connection.

Download the software here: www.vzochat.com

Ps. MAK users, please do not bother :) The version for you is in the process of porting and will be soon!


The first with which VZOchat is compared is Skype, the second with various video chats, etc. I will immediately answer these questions. Skype is a great guys and they have a great product, but it is sharpened for another. The purpose of Skype is IP telephony, and in this guise it is beautiful and unsurpassed. But as soon as it comes to video, architectural constraints immediately appear (p2p is not very effective for distilling video streams, because it is expensive and unpredictable) and the codec used is also not so hot. We can say one thing about video chats - they are all implemented on flash technology and therefore are slow and resource-intensive.

Now we are actively working to make the user interface more convenient. Porting applications to various platforms (first of all - Linux, Mac, Symbian).

Where to apply such a product? I think that the application is limited only by fantasy. The most simple options:
- meetings and meek operational meetings
- communication with contractors / freelancers / customers
- acquaintance through the Internet, etc.
- seminars, tutoring, etc. (why not? :) )
- playing cards or chess :)
- video user support service
- Any other activities where you need to collect from several people to reputable audiences of 100-120 people ... Yes, at least to organize presentations of start-ups, ideas, etc. Why not? Mode 2x120 people is quite suitable for this.


But there is no better way to understand this than to set yourself this program and talk on the video with a friend / colleague / customer. Better 1 time to see! ;)
