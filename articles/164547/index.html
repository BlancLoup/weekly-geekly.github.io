ISS video tour from astronaut Sunita Williams (Russian translation)


ISS video tour with commentary by Sunita Williams (NASA)

We have long wanted to open on the channel SIMPLE-SCIENCE a rubric about space and that turned up an opportunity. NASA astronaut Sunita Williams, before returning to Earth from the ISS, filmed an interesting video tour of the International Space Station. We translated it into Russian.

The video was shot on November 18, 2012 in the last hours of stay on the ISS of the commander of the station Sunita Williams, cosmonaut Yuri Malenchenko and flight engineer Aki Hosido. Then they went to the Soyuz TMA-05M ship for returning to Earth.

The tour includes descriptions of station modules and research laboratories.



You can subscribe to new videos on the Vkontakte page or look at the Simple Science website.
