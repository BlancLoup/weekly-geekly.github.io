OneMillionDollarPage in Russian

Recently, an internet project STENA PO appeared in runet, a bit like the famous OneMillionDollarPage project, the author of which earned $ 1 million. for quite a short period of time.

The project consists of two opposite sites: stenapozora.ru and stenapocheta.ru , where each user can express his indignation caused by other people or events, or, on the contrary, express gratitude.

To place your message on the sites, you need to register and send a registration confirmation using an SMS message, thereby purchasing a part of a wall of 100 pixels in size. The cost of the message is 90 cents.
