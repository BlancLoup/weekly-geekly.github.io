We invite you to GDG DevFest Voronezh 2014

Google fans, developers and enthusiasts are waiting for you at the third annual GDG conference DevFest Voronezh 2014 , which will be held on December 13 and 14 ( not to be confused with GDG DevFest Omsk ) .



The speeches of experts, reports on the most burning issues in Google-world, communication with like-minded people, lots of fun and a charge of positive emotions for the coming year - all this awaits you at GDG DevFest. Let's talk about Wearables, Google Glass, Polymer, Android development, browsers, Material Design, and much more.

About GDG DevFest


The DevFest conference Voronezh has been held for the third year in a row and gathers more than 200 participants: developers, IT specialists, IT students, faculty, everyone who is interested in Google technologies. The GDG DevFest conference in Russia gathers only in three cities: Moscow, Voronezh and Omsk.

For Voronezh, such a conference becomes a unique event - specialists and interesting speakers with author presentations from different countries and cities appear along with Voronezh speakers. For example, last time, IT-specialists from Berlin, Delhi, Moscow, Bucharest, Kharkov and Voronezh told how to develop applications using various Google technologies: Android, Google App Engine, Google Native Client, Google Glass . You can read about the GDG DevFest Voronezh conference in 2012 here or here , the photo report .

Event schedule


The first day of GDG DevFest in Voronezh, we will devote to communication and reports. Eight great speakers will perform in front of the guests. Reports will be divided into several thematic blocks. Artem Markushev and Andrey Yanovsky will talk about the use of Material Design in the development of mobile applications and websites. The report of Alexander Suevalov will be devoted to Polymer. Timur Gaskarov from Yandex will share his vision of the future of browsers. A leitmotif of the conference will be Android Wear. 4 speakers will talk about the platform at once: Alexey Rybakov, Vlad Zotov, Vadim Kotov and Valery Kharitonov. The schedule of speeches can be found on the official conference website .



The second day is a design Sprint tutorial on development technologies for Wearable devices: LG G Watch and Moto 360. You can learn how to create applications for wearable electronics and implement and test it on a real device at the end of the day! Alexey Rybakov, honored expert from DataArt, one of the creators of the unique DeviceHive M2M platform and developer of the smart home control system using Google Glass will take part in the sprint.

Come for knowledge


GDG DevFests is truly a global movement. Thousands of adherents of Google technology gather annually in various parts of the world to talk with colleagues and share experiences. Come to DevFest, join Google Developer Groups and create the future with us.

When and where


The two-day event, moreover, takes place at different sites:
December 13 , 10:00 - Voronezh, Universitetskaya Sq., 1.
December 14 , 10:00 - Voronezh, DataArt office, st. Stankevich, 36, 4th floor.

We are waiting for you! And do not forget to register . Participation is free. Limited number of seats.
