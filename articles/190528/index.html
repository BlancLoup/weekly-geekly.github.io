Testing and comparing Fusion-io ioScale and ioDrive2 cards

Having received a fairly decent amount of positive responses to the cycle of our articles shedding light on the products of the Fusion-io company, ADVANSERV decided not to stop on the achieved result and continued to follow the same course. We are proud to say that this time we have at our disposal a very difficult, until recently, map from the new ioScale line. What does the manufacturer promise us? - If you describe in a few words, you get a short, but capacious - just as quickly, but for less money! It sounds very tempting. Trust, as they say, but check, we will try to figure it out for yourself!


We decided, for clarity, to attach the results of our past testing of ioDrive2 series cards to new tests, so that you have the opportunity to study and compare the performance of both products.

These results are more similar to the truth, which once again reminds us of the importance of keeping track of the updates coming out!
We can say with confidence that we were pleasantly surprised by the test results. The ioScale speed approaches ioDrive2 , and sometimes it catches up with confidence, all at a much lower cost of the product!
By the way, about the cost - the price order starts with a mark of 100k rubles for a 410Gb card, which is comparable to the price tags of the closest competitors. However, for example, a similarly priced OCZ card has a volume of only 300Gb and is significantly inferior to ioScale in speed.
Among the many advantages, there must always be a flaw, and it really is. These wonderful cards are available for purchase in Russia only when ordering in quantities of 10 or more. But we did something to level this flaw!
Possessing the rights of the official partner of Fusion-io in Russia, the company ADVANSERV , was able to order this product in a smaller volume, thereby making it more accessible to buyers in our country. We are glad that we managed to find a compromise by the manufacturer, and we will be happy to recommend this product to our customers.
