Russian address search in Google Maps

In dogonku to this habravosti .

Google recently had a detailed map of Russia. But, unfortunately, at that time, the search for Russian addresses was not supported. Today, by going to google maps and entering st. Bratislavskaya, 3, Moscow , received a marker on house number 3.

After checking the other major cities, my fears were confirmed - Google now fully supports maps in Russia. Well, for the complete happiness of the developers, the Google Maps Geocoding API also began to understand in Russian.

The problems that some Russian web developers have already encountered have been completely resolved.

Now we can expect the appearance of projects using maps in RuNet.
