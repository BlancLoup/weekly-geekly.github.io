Comby.Ru became "free"

On June 14, free registration and access to all the content of users of the Comby service opens , combining a social network and a portal for communication, a service for blogging, creating communities of interest, posting photos, searching for classmates, new and old friends.

Within a month and a half since the launch of the project, Comby worked in active beta testing mode, during which users helped to improve service and correct errors, and also invited their friends. During this period, blogs were improved, a list of contacts in the message system, favorites, import of contacts from all major mail systems, community ratings were added, and the invitation of friends and new members to communities was made easier.
