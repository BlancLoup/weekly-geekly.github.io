Registry Wordpress-specialists



A group of innocent bloggers decided to create a full-fledged registry of wordpress-pro.

It is assumed that the registry includes people who know not only how to write to blogs, but who are able to professionally customize the Wordpress engine. Some of them are ready to help everyone with their advice - and even hosting.

The registry already has its own website , and you can get into it by contacting the creators. This can be done, for example, using the google-group Blogosphere ( in this thread ) or a special form .
