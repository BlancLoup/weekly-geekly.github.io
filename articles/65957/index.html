AvisoSMS

Hello, hello!

Let me introduce to your attention a startup AvisoSMS. Having connected to this service, you will be able to integrate sending sms-messages into your projects, be it an online store, billing, forum or something else. You can do the newsletter by importing CSV or just send a text message from your personal account.



A couple of examples of using sms:
Suppose you move to another office, the action has started, new services have appeared. Send sms, inform customers about it!

Do you own your own online store, but you can not constantly monitor it? When you are offline, a new order will be notified by SMS.

Do you serve several servers in different companies and want to receive information about their condition remotely (spending more time outdoors, eating barbecue :)? There would be an Internet - smskoy you can send yourself any information. (if she did not come at the appointed time, it is likely that something fell :)

And many, many different ways to make your life easier with the help of sms-messages.



We tried to make everything as convenient as possible! Integrating sending SMS in your project is quite simple, using ready-made examples in PHP, Ruby, Perl, Delphi, Java, Python.

You can pay by sms in a variety of ways: starting with electronic currencies, money transfers, and by transferring funds to the settlement account under the contract (by entering into a paper contract you can receive bonuses and all sorts of discounts).

Undoubtedly, the client of your online store is pleased to receive SMS "Your order number 1234 is accepted for processing." And by notifying that the goods are ready for shipment by courier, about the receipt of funds on the account or about delivering the desired position to the warehouse, you will fall in love with your shop hundreds of visitors who are more likely to become your regular customers later, saving them from waiting for an email.



You can specify as the sender of the message the name of the store, phone number, website address, your name in the end (11 Latin characters or numbers). After approval of the sender's address by the administrator (usually a day), the SMS will look something like this:


Having embedded SMS notifications in the billing of your project, you can be reminded of the payment, congratulate on holidays and do a lot of other things.

Moreover, we are interested in distributing modules, add-ons, patches, plug-ins of various CRM, CMS, CMF, forums and others that use SMS sending and are ready to share on the site, thanking their author with a solid SMS account replenishment in our system.



Now the site is undergoing prelaunch testing, so access to your personal account (holy of holies :) is only for those who want to try out our service.

We have 500 invites for such people. Contact in person or by contact information on the site to get one of the invitations and 10 free sms to the account.

The goal is to make available and convenient use of sms-notifications in various projects.

PS At the last moment, the peculiarities in the information exchange protocol have changed; we have not yet had time to edit the examples in java, perl, delphi. You must now hash the password md5 twice. I think everyone will be grateful to those who help fix them (including us;).
