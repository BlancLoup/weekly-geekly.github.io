State Duma presented the deputies' income in a new form

We continue a series of articles on innovations on the website of the State Duma . Previous articles: first and second .

Single site search


As the site is updated and developed, it gradually ‚Äúacquired‚Äù accompanying systems , both external and internal, the number of which already exceeds a dozen: these are databases for storing transcripts of deputies' speeches, informational and analytical materials, bills, videos, and others. The module now allows you to perform a single search not only for the site, but also for the main information systems and databases.



The previous version of the search, which works on the basis of the 1C-Bitrix Search module, is now one of the components of the new search, and returns the results for the News and Video sections. Search in transcripts and informational and analytical materials is carried out using Sphinx , which is used in the corresponding systems, I wrote about this in a previous article . For popular queries, a number of terms, abbreviations a dictionary was entered, the search results for which are displayed above the main issue.



A similar implementation of a uniform search has been prepared in the mobile version of the site.



Updated sections Revenues of deputies and civil servants of the State Duma


Previously, information on the incomes of deputies and civil servants was published in the form of two PDF files of 200 pages each, which was difficult to navigate.
