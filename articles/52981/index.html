Brando Bluetooth Bracelet



Loud melodies, vibrations and even headsets do not always help in time to respond to all calls. No, no, yes and missed missed. Well-known seller of IT-pieces - Brando offers a guaranteed cure for the disease of missed calls.

The gadget is a Bluetooth bracelet made of stainless steel. During a call, the device starts to vibrate, informing the owner that there is an incoming call. At the same time, call data is displayed on a small screen.

The bracelet can not be used to talk - to answer, you have to reach into your pocket for a mobile phone. But with his help, you can reset an unwanted call. The device works at a distance of up to 5 meters from the mobile phone, is charged via USB (charge lasts for 60 hours). And sold here for 50 US dollars.

PS By the way, it is believed that the gadget could be a great alarm clock.
