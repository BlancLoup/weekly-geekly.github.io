10 mistakes to avoid in the mobile office

1. Do not save your data.

Output: Install the fastest broadband you can get.

3. Haltur at work.
A virtual office is still an office, even if you are wearing a bathrobe.
Exit: Keep your focus and remember that you are doing real work. If you forget it yourself, then, of course, others will do it.

4. Isolate yourself.
The absence of a full-fledged office does not mean at all that you do not need to communicate with your clients and colleagues in a traditional manner - at least occasionally.
Output: Communicating face to face with others is important, not only to maintain current relationships, but also to keep your sanity normal. 5. Work without an IT plan.
In a virtual office, many people can offer their ideas, what information technology they would like to use. Some go crazy over Mac, other PC lovers. Some people like the BlackBerry, others choose the Palm Pre or iPhone. And all this love can lead to IT chaos.
Output: If you are a boss, then develop an IT plan. If you create a virtual office with technologies that are not compatible with each other, you will lose a lot of time that could be spent for the benefit of the common cause. Therefore, set the standards and desired results and be clear about why you chose them.

7. Do not pay attention to the management of virtual employees.

Exit: Provide your virtual and mobile employees with clear goals, results and milestones. Encourage them to ask for help if they need it, but in other cases let them carry out the task themselves. And they will do it as it suits them, observing the terms you specified.
