Android + Intel Atom = MegaFon Mint


Looking at the green android, with the Intel processor pressed to your chest with love, you realize that this feeling is serious and for a long time. The wide Russian public learned about this passion on August 22, when the MegaFon Mint communicator was introduced - the first officially sold Android in our country on the Intel Atom platform. The launch of the communicator was devoted to a special event held in Moscow last night. In this post we will briefly describe both the event and the device itself.

... and his culprit


What is Mint? This is MegaFon + Intel. For several months now Intel has been selling this model in Europe in cooperation with the Orange operator - now our turn has come. Both the appearance and the hardware stuffing when changing from operator to operator changes a little less than nothing - they do with a nameplate on the back cover.


Characteristics of the device are as follows:
  • Operating system: Android 2.3
  • 2G standards: GSM / GPRS / EDGE 1800/1900/850/900 MHz
  • 3G standards: UMTS 1900/2100/850/900 MHz, HSPA + 21.1 Mbit / s, HSUPA 5.76 Mbit / s
  • Display: 4.03-in. Capacitive TFT, 1024x600 pixels resolution
  • Processor: Intel Atom Z2460 with support for Intel Hyper-Threading technology, 1600 MHz
  • The amount of RAM: 1024 MB
  • Built-in memory: 16 GB
  • Main camera: 8 MP, 1920x1080 video, autofocus, LED flash, 4x digital zoom
  • Front camera: 1.3 MP, in video 640x480
  • FM radio with RDS
  • Communications and interfaces: Wi-Fi (802.11b / g / n), Bluetooth, NFC
  • Connectors: 3.5 mm audio, Micro-USB, microHDMI
  • Sensors: Accelerometer / Illuminance / Approximation
  • GPS satellite navigation
  • Rechargeable battery: non-removable lithium-ion capacity of 1460 mAh
  • Opening hours: 336 h. / 14 h.
  • SIM card form factor: Micro-SIM
  • Dimensions: 123x63x11 mm
  • Weight: 124 grams
