Start-up tournament Harvest - experts will help startups

Harvest participants face a daunting task. For 2 days to make a project or a prototype of a project or even a business plan of a project so as to get a high mark from the jury and interest investors.
To help them, we invited experts who can be consulted or asked for help. Most of the experts will be present all 2 days of Harvest. Everything will happen very simply - if the expert is free, come and ask a question.
Under the cut list of experts who have already confirmed their participation.
  • Anton Yarusov and Evgeny Kazakov , Popcorn Design house - will help participants on design issues
  • Bayram Annakov, Empatika - will help with questions of teamwork, organizational strategy
  • Dmitry Satin and Andrey Sikorsky , UsabilityLab will help to make projects convenient and understandable for people.
  • Alexey Kopylov , UIDesign Group will help with questions of convenience for users
  • Rustem Bogdanov , karaoke.ru will advise on the promotion of projects
  • Vasily Shmelev, Michael Barablin, Anton Vesnin, Roman Smirnov , Locum.ru - will help with issues related to hosting and development for Linux
  • Sergey Lisitsyn and Vladimir Strelnikov , Parking.Ru will help those participants whose web projects work on Windows hosting

Details about Harvest can be found on the tournament website .
