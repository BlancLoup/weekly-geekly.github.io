First All-Russian Student IT Championship



The educational Internet resource Campus.ru and the Moscow Department of Education present the First All-Russian Student IT Championship .

The main objectives of the competition are to help talented students and graduates in specialties related to information technology, to manifest themselves in the labor market, to attract the attention of employers and find a job. One of the main problems for young people today is a lack of experience, which is why they often cannot immediately get a job in their specialty.

The championship allows students and graduates to understand what specific tasks they will have when working in the largest IT companies. After all, these companies provide their tasks during the competition. They will assess the results of the work and determine the winners, who will receive valuable prizes, as well as the possibility of an internship with subsequent employment.



The championship aims to bring the training process closer to business needs and adapt young people to these needs as early as possible.
