MultiMi is a good aggregator of your life on the Web.



Probably many habravchan have accounts on many different Internet services, including social networks, mailers, calendars, video hosting and image hosting. It is very difficult to keep track of all your accounts in these services, as a result of which the necessary information can simply be lost, remain unseen by the user. And in general, it is not very convenient, rushing from service to service for checking mail or uploading photos there. The development team from Israel has created a social aggregator MultiMi , which can solve such problems.

It links your mailboxes, social networks and various services into a single system. In addition, you can bind and various services, organizers and calendars. This whole system then begins to work as a whole, plus contacts of the same users in different services are connected together. Thus, it is possible to track the correspondence with the person who was conducted in different services.
