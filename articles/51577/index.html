What is the best way to visualize Banana Research?

I stumbled on an interesting study the other day that was published on a blog about marketing .

The results, of course, are impressive. But the visualization of numbers is simply horrible. It takes a lot of time to get the picture in mind. And especially in times of crisis, we need to save staff time.

Let's see how you can make the numbers more visible.

Do people like bananas?
Yes, people love bananas - 73.8%.
And more bananas are loved by the weaker sex (79.3% versus 61.5%).
