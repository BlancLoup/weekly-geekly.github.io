How to correct your posture with the help of Kickstarter: a selection of interesting "neurological" projects

Diseases of the back and spine - a massive problem today, and in the foreseeable future, and each will solve it in his own way, depending on experience, knowledge, advice from doctors or friends. As a preventive measure, we are also free to choose at least electronic correctors, even special breathing sensors, even pillows with a heart rate monitor, or even corsets, and all this was once collected money for crowdfunding sites.

Sedentary lifestyle: in the office or in the car

Furniture - furniture, but there are still special "lining" and "litter" with a corrective function, as well as corrective / supporting devices. A few words about them.

Posture md

The cushion, which is designed to ‚Äúcompensate‚Äù for potholes of modern office chairs or car seats, consists of a soft material mixed with a special gel, which allows you to maintain a smooth back and maintain the comfort of movements.



The creators rely on the theory of muscle memory , when with systematic help, our muscles become more and more independent, getting used to the correct position.

A sedentary lifestyle accompanies us not only at work, but also on the way to and from it, as many have private cars. A similar Posture MD device was suggested by the Backboard team.

Backboard

Mobile support board , which covers the hollow space, not allowing the spine to "fall" into it, thereby maintaining the balance between the vertebrae and preventing the formation of a hernia.



A small, durable and easy to install "helper" can be used even in the living room.

ARC

A device that can be used lying and sitting , fixing on almost any chair or chair. The goal is the same - to support the correct position of a flat back.



The device is designed for any parts of the spine, and, for example, 10-15-minute lying on it by the cervical section helps to relieve pain and relieve muscle tension. At the end of the working day!

DTRAC

A similar exercise cushion helps people with a healthy spine to relieve pain and tension during a ten-minute "exercise."



Under the exercises should be understood simply the process of rest on DTRAC , placing the device under the lumbar.
