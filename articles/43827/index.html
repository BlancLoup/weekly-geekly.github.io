Open Letter to Maslov PR

Now the national vote of the Runet Prize is in full swing. VKontakte is not involved (after known events), and the chances of HabraHabra are low.

The top five looks like this:

1. Odnoklassniki.Ru
2. Quotation Runet (Bashorg)
3. National Bittorrent Tracker
4. Google.Ru
5. Legend: Legacy of the Dragons

Why is the best search engine of the world and Russia in fourth place, and not on the first? Because the participants occupying the three leading lines strongly ask their users to vote on them. And Google didn‚Äôt even deign to inform its users that the voting was taking place.

A few days ago, I decided to organize a flash mob in support of Google. Created a special group on VKontakte, topic on the forum SE. Notify friends and like-minded people on ICQ. And everyone agreed that it was necessary to do something with the current situation, you cannot let Odnoklassniki win.

As part of the flash mob for Google collected about 300 votes. However, given the scale of the vote, this is a drop in the ocean.

In this connection, it was decided to write an open letter to Maslov PR (an agency representing the interests of Google in Russia). The goal is to convince them to post an invitation to vote for a search engine on the Google.Ru home page.

The letter itself is published here .

Copies of the letter were sent to several employees of Maslov PR by e-mail. One of these days their answer should follow.

If you wish, you can join an open letter by subscribing to it.
