Successful things are successful immediately.

Someone said that each of us has only a few things that are good, the rest are very bad.

Meanwhile, thousands of people are trying to develop abilities that are not (for example, programming, hehe). People are trying to patch the gap, fix some of their own weakness. And do not rest on the strengths.

Meanwhile, there is a very simple indication of what needs to be developed. This is when you like a business, it goes right away and you can practice for a long time.

This is contrary to the theory of learning from mistakes and breaking walls with a forehead. But it works! While people persistently spend hours on development drop by drop, try yourself in different things, discover talents! While others will invent their own motivation, digest endless bumps, you will move easily thanks to constant victories. By the way, successful athletes, as a rule, are initially good. The iPod is also good from the first sale.

Just find your talents and cut them down without wasting time dealing with flaws.
And be successful right away.
