LVEE 2009

The international gathering and conference ‚ÄúLinux Vacation / Eastern Europe‚Äù has been held in Belarus for the fifth year already, bringing together free communication and leisure enthusiasts.

The purpose of the LVEE is to exchange experience between specialists in the design, development, implementation, maintenance and development of Open Source solutions, as well as to involve the broader layers of the IT public in a public dialogue about the possibilities of free software, problems arising in the process of its integration and ways to solve them .

The central part of the LVEE is the International Conference of Free Software Developers and Users. The format of the conference includes presentations, workshops and round tables. At the disposal of the speakers conference room, projector, sound. By the beginning of the conference will be released a collection of theses.

The objectives of the conference are to provide participants with the opportunity to exchange experiences, organize a discussion of the most current trends and trends in the development of the industry, and accumulated practical knowledge. The meeting is designed to help establish the necessary contacts, give development to ideas, provide support for the open source community.

The conference covers a wide range of platforms - from workstations and servers, including virtualization-based solutions, to embedded systems and mobile devices. The topics traditionally include the following areas:

* development and testing of free software;
* administration of systems built on its basis;
* training of specialists in the field of free software technologies;
* experience in implementing open source solutions;
* legal and organizational features of the use of free licenses.

Any other areas related to free software and extending the above are welcome.
