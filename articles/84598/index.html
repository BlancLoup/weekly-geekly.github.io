A little bit about Sberbank

Inspired by my trips to Sberbank over the past six months ...

Explain why the main state bank of the country can afford to hire stupid workers and score on their training? Why can different software be installed on computers in different cities (even in different departments in the same city)? Why in general in different branches of the same bank are the principles of work radically different from each other?

The situation is as follows:

Every month I send a small amount of money to my brother, who is studying in St. Petersburg. Since my brother has a Mastercard card from Sberbank, it is quite logical that I put money on it.
