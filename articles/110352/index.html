New Toshiba in 2010

We decided to make a small review of new laptops that Toshiba presented this year and which have already gone on sale. Toshiba thin and light laptops, known for their stylish design and feature set, are designed to allow the user to stay in touch with friends on various social networks and receive all the necessary information in time. Under the cat new Toshiba.

Unique laptop with two touch screens

The Libretto W100 laptop with two 7-inch touch-sensitive displays is designed for users who want to be at the forefront of laptop development and have the opportunity to enjoy the latest web technologies using a unique user interface.



Notebook Libretto W100 is really unique. You can hold it vertically, like a book, to read a newspaper spread, or arrange it horizontally, using the upper screen to read the document, and the lower one to mark it. Among other things, the bottom screen is convenient for placing a virtual keyboard, while the computer itself can be used as a regular laptop. At the same time, a light touch of the finger is enough to drag icons, media files and documents from one screen to another.

3D space

The Satellite A665 is the first 3D laptop from Toshiba, which gave users unique experience of 3D technology. A multimedia portable computer is capable of playing videos using the built-in 3D Blu-ray drive.



Included with the NVIDIA 3D Vision technology are the shutter glasses, which, in combination with a long-range infrared emitter (connected via USB), provide a stereoscopic image of stunning clarity, brightness and depth. The notebook is equipped with a high-performance NVIDIA graphics processor and a 39.6-centimeter (15.6 ‚Äù) TruBrite HD display with LED backlighting (120 Hz). Satellite A665 model is equipped with a premium stereo system Harman Kardon and the latest technology Dolby Advanced Audio, complementing the image with a powerful and lively sound. In addition, the Sleep and Music feature allows you to enjoy music from an MP3 player connected to the Harman Kardon stereo system even when the laptop is turned off.

Functionality in a compact package

Thanks to the powerful processor and cooling technology, Airflow Cooling Technology in the Toshiba Satellite R630 notebook perfectly combines stylish, robust design and best-in-class performance. The 13.3-inch laptop has a rugged magnesium alloy case with a heavy-duty honeycomb body texture under the palms to protect against damage to internal parts. In turn, waterproof keyboard ensures the safety and security of your data in case of accidental ingress of liquid.



The computer can work up to 8 hours without recharging (according to Mobile Mark 2007), which is especially important if you are on the road, and together with a surprisingly thin and light design, this makes the Satellite R630 a perfect laptop that can be carried everywhere. A stylish case with a thickness of 17 mm, a weight of 1.4 kg and an integrated DVD drive will give you the opportunity to enjoy working with a truly compact computing device. Among other things, an impressive design means increased computer life, embodying in itself reliability, comfort and impeccable style.

You can go online from anywhere. The latest ultra-fast WiFi technology allows you to get fast and secure Internet access through any wireless point. Stay connected wherever you are.

Android Mobile Internet Device

Toshiba has entered the mobile Internet device segment, launching the first product in this category, the Toshiba AC100 in a notebook form factor. This ultra-compact computer supports continuous network connectivity, making it an ideal device for networking, surfing the web, accessing e-mail, viewing and editing documents, and playing media files. The battery life of the AC100 in standby mode is up to 7 days.



Thanks to the use of the Android operating system, a computer only needs one second to go from standby mode to fully working mode - like a smartphone, it is always on. In the mode of alternating video playback and web surfing, the battery life of the device is at least 8 hours.

Presented in black and orange color AC100 has an intuitive interface that offers the possibility of maximum personalization of the desktop by downloading and installing applications and widgets. In addition, the device is offered with a number of pre-installed programs, including Documents To Go for viewing and editing Microsoft Office documents and viewing PDF; email client with POP3, IMAP and Exchange support; an instant messaging application; Opera Mobile web browser, as well as Toshiba Media Player for playing music, video files and viewing photos.
