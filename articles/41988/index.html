Portable media player Nikon Media Port UP300

Nikon has presented its view on portable media players to the public. Since Nikon does not specialize in the production of such devices, this announcement is already an event. But the Media Port UP300 itself is quite interesting.



The device is a headset that has a small "personal" display. The size of the monitor is only 0.44 inches, the supported resolution is up to 640 √ó 480 pixels. For the user, he will act as an analogue of a 50-inch display, which is located at a distance of three meters.


Stuffing Media Port UP300 is impressive. The device is equipped with 8 GB of memory, a Wi-Fi 802.11g module and a gesture control system (for the more advanced 300x model). In addition to the media player, Media Port UP300 can also boast a full-featured web browser. Video Headphones can play WMV9, MPEG-1, MPEG-2 video files and AAC, MP3 and WMA audio files.


Powered Media Port UP300 with two AA batteries. The manufacturer claims that they will be enough to play the video for two hours and four and a half hours of playing audio.

At the moment, Nikon is going to sell the player only in Japan. It costs $ 677 (UP300x model with gesture control and 8 GB of memory). The trimmed UP300 model with 4 GB of memory will cost $ 485.
