Preparing an educational resource for Android from XDA-developers


A well-known Android resource XDA Developers began preparing its own Android textbook and everything related to this system.

During the existence of the site itself and the forum has accumulated a lot of materials that can be extremely useful for Android-developers, many of whom use Google to search for the necessary information. However, for a coherent system of learning, which is especially important for beginners, it would be advisable to have some sort of centralized centralized repository, which should be a new project - XDA-University .

The project will be the collective work of many people, which will be available for free the most relevant information from the depths of XDA-Developers. In addition, a PDF book is being prepared for publication, which can also be downloaded and used offline for free. At the moment, all interested persons are invited to leave their email address on the XDA-developers.com website in order to receive a notification about the start of work in time.
