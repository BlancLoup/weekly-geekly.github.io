How to make a good box for software

And although online sales are gaining momentum, in absolute terms it is still insignificant volumes. A similar situation is observed in some other countries. For example, in Italy, Poland, Africa, people also prefer to buy boxes with antivirus. But, let's say, in the USA, Hungary, England, France, more than 60% of anti-virus software sales go through the Internet.
