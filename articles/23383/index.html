SeedCamp in Kiev

Launched website www.seedcamp.com.ua
As you know, at the end of May, SeedCamp will be held in Kiev. The site is already running, the program is being formed. The program will include serious European and Russian-Ukrainian speakers. Applications for presentations are accepted (10 projects will be selected, which will help prepare to make a presentation to investors who invest in the early stages).
In addition, the winners of the 7minut competition (which will be determined at TMT Kiev'08 ) will have the chance to make a presentation without prior selection.
