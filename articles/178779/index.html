Video interview from Silicon Valley. AmBAR is the largest foreign business association of Russian-speaking professionals.


This time we met with members of the Board of Directors of AmBAR - an organization with a center in Palo Alto, which brings together Russian-speaking entrepreneurs and IT professionals across America, and is also a kind of business bridge between Russia and the United States. Prominent US investors and entrepreneurs such as Max Levchin (founder of PayPal), Ron Conway (one of Google‚Äôs first investors), Guy Kawasaki, Accel Partners, 500 Startups, Intel Capital, IBM, Adobe, Evernote, etc. take part in AmBAR events. AmBAR events from Russia are attended by both entrepreneurs and investors (for example, Runa Capital) and representatives of RUSNANO. In 2010, AmBAR helped organize a trip for the US President of the Russian Federation , as well as the arrival of major investors from the US to Moscow ( vctriptomoscow.com ). Under the cut more information and video from the interview.


AmBAR President Anna Dvornikova with a group of well-known American investors at a meeting with the President of the Russian Federation (2010)

AmBAR is the largest business association of Russian- speaking high-tech professionals with headquarters in Palo Alto (USA). AmBAR creates conditions for cooperation, acquaintance, exchange of experience, knowledge and business contacts for Russian-speaking professionals around the world. AmBAR regularly holds investment-oriented technology events in Silicon Valley. The largest of them is the annual technological-investment conference SVOD (Silicon Valley Open Doors), which regularly gathers an amazing audience - over 600 participants - investors, start-ups, entrepreneurs.


Google co-founder Sergey Brin (center) and other eminent participants speak at the AmBAR Event, leftmost - Sergey Burkov , a member of the Board of Directors of AmBAR

This year's SVOD conference will be held June 10-11. Participants will have the opportunity to meet personally with successful entrepreneurs of the Valley, top investors, listen to the speeches of start-ups, learn and build relationships with potential partners. SVOD is a great opportunity for businessmen and investors from Russia to meet with their American colleagues to exchange experience, build the right connections, find profitable deals. Startups can rent a stand for the presentation of their companies, as well as hold a presentation on the stage in front of investors from Russia and the USA.
