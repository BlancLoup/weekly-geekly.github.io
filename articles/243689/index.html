IBM invites everyone to a Bluemix meeting



On December 5, IBM technical experts invite developers and anyone interested in IT technologies to a meeting dedicated to the PaaS proposal from IBM - Bluemix.

Several presentations are planned for the meeting. Interesting discussions and just informal communication are expected. The event will demonstrate the development of a service for node.js, porting existing Java EE applications to the cloud environment, and the recently released Watson cognitive services.

The meeting will be held at 17.00 in the IBM Science and Technology Center in Moscow, at Obrucheva 30/1 (metro station Kaluzhskaya). To participate in the event you need to register by the link .

Bluemix in brief:

The BlueMix platform allows developers, companies and end-users to explore the capabilities on which next-generation cloud applications and services will be based. An application can be traditional, social, mobile, or combine all these aspects - and have the ability to work with big data.
