Google will turn off the hint for "pirated" requests

At the request of the RIAA, Google agreed to introduce some copyright protection measures .

1. All requests for blocking information on the DMCA Act will be reviewed within 24 hours. At the same time, the appeals mechanism for those whose content was unreasonably deleted will be improved.

2. Autocomplete search queries on terms closely related to piracy will be blocked. At the same time, Google recognizes that it is not easy to determine exactly which terms are used to search for pirated content.
