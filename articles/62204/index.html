Fan table under the laptop



As you know, laptops have the unpleasant property of heating up. Of course, you hardly get a knee burn, but all the same, with long work, the sensations are not the most pleasant. In this regard, all kinds of cooler coasters from the WTF category have long ago migrated to the Must Have category. But here the Japanese company Sanwa went ahead and is no longer a cooler, but a full-fledged table for a laptop. The novelty is equipped with as many as two fans that connect to the computer via USB. With the help of a mobile stand, the laptop can be rotated at any convenient angle. On the right there is a place for a mouse or notebook, behind it is a stand for cups or bottles. Sale table here for $ 60.
