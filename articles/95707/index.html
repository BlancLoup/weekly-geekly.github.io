LG Optimus: inexpensive Android phone appears in Russia

Budget Android devices are becoming more! Following Acer , LG starts selling its Android phone, the LG Optimus (GT540). The phone will be successful due to active advertising and low prices.
Unlike the Acer beTouch E110, LG has Wi-Fi, an autofocus camera, a larger screen resolution (320x480) and the price is only 1000 rubles. more - 8990 r.



SNS Manager (Social Networking System) provides convenient access to social networking sites and allows you to manage multiple accounts simultaneously. For the Russian market, Vkontakte, Yandex Maps, the Balda game, as well as links to mobile versions of Odnoklassniki, Afisha, Sport-Express, Yandex Services, Facebook are pre-installed. Following the link LG Music Club, you can install the application LG Music Club. The Vkontakte widget is also installed on the screen, thanks to which you can track your status in the social network and quickly manage it. It is worth noting that the LG Optimus module is installed Wi-Fi, which provides fast Internet access.

Users can customize the interface as it suits them. In LG Optimus, you can configure up to five screens that display links and widgets to frequently used applications, such as news, weather forecast, SNS, and others.

A media player with DivX and Xvid codecs installed allows you to view videos in many formats without re-encoding, and navigating through multimedia content is convenient thanks to 3D viewing gallery.

The display has a diagonal of 3 inches and a resolution of 320x480 pixels. The 3-megapixel camera with autofocus supports geotagging (Geo Tagging), and the Auto Face-Tagging (Face Recognition) and Face-To-Action (Smile Recognition) functions make photography more simple. The built-in video editing application with a storyboard function allows you to mount videos directly on your smartphone.

The capacity of the LG OPTIMUS battery is 1500 mAh, which ensures long battery life without recharging. The audio jack is suitable for standard 3.5-mm headphones (miniJack). GPS-navigator allows you to accurately determine your location on the map. In the smartphone, you can install a microSD memory card up to 32 GB. Price smartphone LG Optimus 8990 rubles.
