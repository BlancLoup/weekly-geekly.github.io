Modern technologies of web-design allow experimenting in terms of the presentation of images. Thus, the Due maternity online store offered something unique to future moms. With the help of ‚Äúdynamic image‚Äù technology, the product was presented from all sides, i.e. rotated 360 degrees. Thanks to this innovation, the conversion rate increased by 27%.

The use of such technologies allows you to stand out among competitors and show your product from all possible sides. Accordingly, dispel all doubts about the product, which in the future, as shown in this example, may affect the conversion. Now this technique is used by many online stores. Of course, this is expensive, and here you have to relate the costs to the potential impact on conversion and sales.

How else is it possible to go beyond the boundaries and achieve an increase in conversion, consider using the case from Brookdale. This company offers different accommodation options for seniors. The problem of the site was that the conversion was minimal due to the lack of graphics and reviews on the main page.



To solve the problem, A / B testing was conducted, during which two variants of pages were shown to users - with a video clip and with photos.

The video version told the clients about how they were lucky to get into the Brookdale company, and the second version was a photo image of a satisfied client

Test results showed that the photo version works best. The conversion was 3.92% instead of 0.85%, which amounted to more than 100 thousand dollars of additional income.

The victory of this option was connected with the peculiarities of the target audience. The target audience of the company is people of small wealth who, for the most part, did not have fast Internet, besides, the majority of elderly people were frightened by the automatic launch of the video. The result - they just left the site.
