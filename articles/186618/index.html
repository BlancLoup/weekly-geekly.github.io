Auto Acceleration: windows marketplace, in-app billing and what else we learned over the year

I already wrote about the project here and here . In a few words, this is a mobile application oriented to car enthusiasts for measuring acceleration time of 0..100 and a website where you can download data, view statistics, etc.

During the year of the project‚Äôs life, we have gained a certain amount of experience that I want to share with readers.

The articles referenced above describe both the application itself and the beginning of its life path: victories and defeats, unexpected successes and deceived expectations. Today I will tell you what happened next. But first I will give a plan that was drawn up even before the publication of the appendix: the plan that we are following. And we are trying to understand whether he is good or not. Here is:

About plans


  1. Development and publication for the Russian-speaking audience of the application on Android, publishing the site (in the ru zone, in Russian).
  2. Promotion of the application in various ways, a comparative analysis of effectiveness.
  3. Development and publication for the Russian-speaking audience of the application on iOS, windows phone.
  4. Testing of various ways of monetization.
  5. Ordering a new site design and applications from a professional designer, changing the appearance of the site and applications to look serious, and not as a collective farm craft.
  6. Analysis of the possibility of attracting an investor, publisher, partner - for competent output of the application to the world market If there is an opportunity - we attract.
  7. Development of a bilingual (Russian and English) version of the site, development of a separate English (or adding a language choice in the current) version of the application, publishing a project for the whole world (taking into account the experience gained in paragraph 2 and 4)

About points 1 and 2 it was a lot written, I summarize: the popularity of the Android version in Russia surpassed my expectations, the best way to advance was the creation of topics in the specialized forums of motorists.

A little ahead of the plan, and therefore unsuccessfully, there were attempts to communicate with investors and business angels. In fact, it is obvious: this project is of little interest to such organizations, because if it is profitable, the scale of this profit may be significant for a team of several people, but for a large company - a drop in the ocean. Most of the organizations to which I wrote, answered kindly: the project is interesting, but not for us. All the more my surprise was when a representative of a venture office contacted me and showed interest in Auto Acceleration. We have overpowered for a month, made a business plan, considered the possible profit, necessary expenses (with a fair amount of optimism, of course), and the project was presented for discussion among partners. Trial of the pen in monetization


From advertising in the application, I immediately refused. In order to have clicks on adMob built-in ads, the user must spend a long time in the application. In our case, this is not the case. The use of Auto Acceleration is episodic, almost one-time, in nature. AirPush type advertising is a great way to kill the rating of even a good application, which I have already seen from the example of my other project. Advertising on the site - why not, but need attendance. At the moment, a day more than a hundred people come to the site. This is negligible. However, we will try: a new design is already being designed with the ability to display ads from Google or something else.

The current trend in the world of mobile software is in app billing. Shopping inside the app, if in our opinion. Check how it works, it was decided on the Android version. On May 20 of this year, an update was posted with an appropriate opportunity: buy a Premium Account for 50 rubles, making available the settings of the upper speed limit, memorizing and resetting the maximum speed via GPS and ... for now. Since then, approximately one person per day makes such a purchase. The number of installations per day is about 80. There are about 50 deletions. Therefore, approximately 30 users install and not immediately remove the application, one of them buys: 3.3% of new active users are a source of income. With an income (minus Google margin) of 30 rubles from the purchase, we have an income of one ruble from one active installation. This is a very approximate calculation, but it is interesting to understand how much you can earn on this program. Simple calculations show: last summer there were more than 10,000 active installations. So, we immediately implemented the in-app purchases, could earn about 10,000 rubles. What would not pay off even the current cost of the designer. By the way, an interesting point: If you take the number of active installations 3 months ago and now, then we will get only 350 users per month, approximately 11-12 per day. The difference of active installations and deletions per day, on which we relied earlier, is 30, which is 2.5 times more. If you use this indicator, then the potential income could already be as much as 25,000 rubles. It is clear that this calculation is extremely approximate, but it allows to evaluate the order and the picture before entering the world stage. Perhaps we should make the cost of a premium account higher!

findings
