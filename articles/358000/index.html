The eighth day of the blockade. Roskomnadzor is trying to block Google. State-owned companies massively rent VPN

On April 23, 2018, the eighth day of the blockade, which Roskomnadzor organized for Russian Internet users , went. As a result of fan blocking of cloud services Amazon, Google and Microsoft suffered dozens of Russian sites. The result of the first week of hostilities of Roskomnadzor: 33 856 Russian domains were blocked, including 31 440 domains in the zone .RU, 1856 in the zone. RF, 560 in the zone .SU. Here is a statistic of how many domains in each of the zones indicate (A record) on blocked subnets.

The telegram channel RKNSHOWTIME automatically publishes information about newly-locked subnets and IP addresses from the registry (uploads are provided by Irrepressible Phil - Philippe Kulin, General Director of the telecommunication company Diphost). According to reports in the channel, it is clear that on April 22 Roskomnadzor changed the blocking tactics - and began to work on small subnets and individual IP addresses. Google, Microsoft and Amazon are mainly affected.

If earlier large subnets of cloud providers were entered into the registry, then since yesterday the regulator began to work on specific IP addresses. Sometimes the updated upload contains only 1 new IP address, sometimes a few hundred addresses. The last major update took place on the morning of April 22, when Roskomnadzor blocked the subnet 174.104.0.0/15 of Time Warner Cable Internet LLC at 131,092 addresses. Since then - only small small ones. But the day before, hundreds of thousands of addresses were added to the registry almost every hour: now, in addition to Amazon, Google and Microsoft, the Digital Ocean, Clouding.io, Hetzner, Online.net, OVH SAS and other hosters are blocked.
