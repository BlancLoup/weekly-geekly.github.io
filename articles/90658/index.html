Philosophical question

Attention question:

Are the following truths true?

A. The relativity of truth is due to the infinity and variability of the world to be grasped.
B. The relativity of truth is due to the limited cognitive abilities of man.

Answer options:
1) only A is true
2) only B is true
3) both judgments are true
4) both judgments are wrong

Where would you think I took this question?

This, dear fellow citizens, is question A-6 from the demonstration version of the test measuring materials of the unified state social science examination of 2010 , you can download here: dwl2.alleng.ru/d_ar/ege2010/soc2010_demo.zip

By the way, in our Chelyabinsk region the examination in social studies was (and, as far as I know, is now) one of the three mandatory exams along with literature and mathematics.
