Emercoin connects to F2Pool

The second largest mining pool of the Chinese planet, F2pool, added Emercoin. This was announced by F2Pool co-founder Wang Chun on his twitter.


However, the announcement on Twitter came out a little later than the actual addition of ‚ÄúEmercoin‚Äù to the F2pool pool. This event radically increased the complexity of mining Emercoin: in just one month, since the end of March, it has increased four hundred times, from 700 million to 300 billion units of complexity, reaching approximately 60% of the Bitcoin capacity.


The growth of complexity has one more plus - the higher the complexity, the lower the commission for creating name entries in the blockchain.
- Evgeny Shumilov, founder and head of Emercoin

F2Pool is one of the largest Chinese mining pools of bitcoin and litecoin. According to blockchain.info, today F2Pool is the second largest pool, second only to Antpool. Recall that in April this year Mergemining.com pool joined Emercoin and Bitcoin joint mining .

Emercoin is a cryptocurrency and fully decentralized platform for distributed digital services based on the blockchain.
