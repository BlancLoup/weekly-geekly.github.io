Lemonade Lab, a developer of fitness trackers, raised $ 5.8 million

Lemonade Lab, a developer of fitness trackers, raised $ 5.8 million. Round headed a subsidiary of foxconn.ru Foxconn. In addition to her investor was the company FIH Mobile .

The funds will be used to develop its own cloud platform. It is necessary for the analysis and processing of data from the sensors of athletes. After processing, the platform will give them feedback. The main audience of Lemonade are professional cyclists and runners.

Since the beginning of 2014, the developer has attracted an investment of $ 10.3 million. Direct competitors of the company are leaders in the sphere of Fitbit , Jawbone and Garmin , reports TechCrunch.
