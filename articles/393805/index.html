Alice in the country Polytech


On May 12, an exhibition of the Alice in the Land of Sciences Polytechnic Museum officially opens in Ufa. It so happened that I was able to look there before the official opening. The exhibition is interactive, much can and should be touched, so I want to share a general impression and specifically mention a couple of exhibits that are unlikely to dwell on excursions in detail, and which are worth seeing separately and without haste. And the organizers of the exhibition want to become the center of gravity of the popular science movement in Ufa and are looking for enthusiastic popularizers.

Ufa has become the third city where this exhibition unfolded. The first version opened in Moscow, at VDNH, in May 2015. Winter 2016 exhibition was in Orsk. And from May to October 2016 Alice will be in Ufa. Here, for example, video from the Moscow version:



The theme of "Alice in the land of science" crosses the famous fairy tale with physics and biology. Three rooms talk about physics and cosmology, biology (evolution and perception) and mathematics with topology.

In the hall of physics and cosmology, you can find out your weight on different planets, become the center of star formation (Kinect fixes the position on the ceiling), look at a visual demonstration of the Bernoulli law. One of the interesting stands behind which to stand longer is the scale of the universe:



By controlling the handle, you can project onto a wall the scale from the size of the visible Universe to the minimum length that makes sense in physics, passing through galaxies, stars, planets, cities, people, insects, bacteria, molecules and atoms.

There are such circles on the walls:


With the help of polarized glasses you can see there additional information. It will be interesting mainly for adults, children should like the fact of hidden inscriptions.

But this exhibit is unlikely to attract much attention. And in vain. The fact is that this is an expression in the material of the physics of orbital motion. Depending on the parameters, the orbit can be a circle, an ellipse, a parabola or a hyperbola. And all this is a section of a cone. Inside the cone there are explanatory labels, do not be lazy to disassemble it and see.
