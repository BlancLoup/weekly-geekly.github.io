Google launched recipe search

Google launched on Thursday a new search function, Recipe View, which makes it easier to find recipes on the web.

The service is now available only in the US and Japan, but will soon be available to users from other countries.

Recipe View allows you to narrow your search results to show only recipes, and helps you choose the most suitable recipe, showing its ratings, ingredients and photos. To use the function, simply click on the ‚ÄúRecipes‚Äù link in the left pane.
