Hanvon announces two Windows 7 tablets

The company Hanvon announces the release of two ten-inch tablets based on Windows 7 .

Hanvon B10:
  • Intel Celeron ULV743 1.3GHz, Chipset GS45, 2GB DDR2 RAM;
  • 10.1-inch capacitive multitouch display with a resolution of 1024 x 600;
  • 1.3 Mpx camera;
  • 250GB HDD;
  • WLAN 802.11b / g, Bluetooth 2.1;
  • Mini HDMI (HDMI or VGA), 2 x USB 2.0, microphone and headphone output;
