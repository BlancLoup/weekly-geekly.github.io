How would the conclusion


It is practically impossible to live in Moscow and not to face the rental market - whoever does not rent, he rents out. And the housing problem is still not included in the list of things that can make us better.

The Moscow rental market in its configuration is a dinosaur from the 90s: it is practically not regulated by law, 99% in the shadows and lives according to some rules, according to which the customer is not always not always right, and certainly not the king, and the cash cow who needs to be raised to the maximum, imposing a minimum of services for the maximum of money. The reasons for this can be analyzed for a long time - not least in the case of the lack of responsibility of realtors for the results of their work. But one thing is clear for sure: the need of intermediaries in the matter of renting housing is greatly exaggerated, and the Internet is the main hope of changing the situation.

The future is behind sites like livebase.ru, providing direct interaction between tenants and landlords. Realtors will remain a niche in which professional mediation is really necessary - this is buying and selling, renting abroad and renting commercial real estate.
