Idea for a startup as a gift

Here is a record of a month ago from a notebook as it is. Perhaps someone will come up with an idea, and he will bring it to life =)

Project "mylovesms"

Domain name, say: mylovesms.ru

Did your girlfriend ever tell you that you pay her little attention?
What do not you call and send it to sms first?

Now it is possible to pay more attention to it, without spending time on it.
I'll tell you how:

You register, confirm your phone number, accept the terms of the agreement - that we can use your phone number ...

You drive in the phone number of the girl, you set up the options and pleasant and exciting sms from the large database of templates are sent from your number without your participation.

Further more.
Suppose that in 3 months there will be some event that you will not remember for sure? Anniversary brother-in-law or birthday of your girlfriend's mom? Congratulate them in advance - write a text message, select the date and time when this text message will be sent.

If you want to be aware of what is sent from your number itself - we can duplicate text messages - 2-3 minutes before sending - and you too!


UPD. For especially scrupulous, I will tell how this idea appeared. To show that there is nothing "plastic" in it (thanks for the word jeje ). I spent with a girl in a cafe just a super date. :) And I wanted to give her a little surprise. The idea came that she would receive a text message from me every day during the next week while she was still in bed. I wrote all the 7 text messages, and connected each of them with something good that was happening between us. I called and asked what time she got up on which days of the week, explaining that with a psychological test. =) And the harsh realities of life were such that she got up on some days at 6 am. Imagine for a second how nice it is to get a girl a text message at 6am in the morning with pleasant words ?! Of course, as a normal programmer, I assigned this task to cron. And then there was an idea. The end. Titles.
