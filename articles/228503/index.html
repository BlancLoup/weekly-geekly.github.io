Infobox opens location in Europe!

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new Infobox for VPS location in Amsterdam. Now Infobox gives customers the opportunity to select a location for a VPS in Russia or Europe. We strive to be closer to users, allowing you to create fast and reliable sites and services around the world.

In total, Infobox now has 4 locations: St. Petersburg, Krasnoyarsk , Moscow and Amsterdam.



AMS1 data center


EvoSwitch data processing center is one of the largest and most reliable data centers in Europe. The use of the most advanced technologies and premium-class infrastructure allows customers to guarantee maximum uptime of 99.999% according to SLA.

Compliance with the standards of infrastructure, operation and security of the data center is confirmed by numerous international certificates:
  • ISO 27001: 2005 - ensuring information security (equivalent to NEN 7510)
  • ISO 9001: 2008 - Quality Management System
  • ISAE3402 (SAS-70) - auditing financial and internal controls
  • NEN 3140; 1010; 50110 - protection of electrical systems
  • Borg Class 4 - Building and Electrical Safety

Features of the European installation Infobox VPS


The Infobox VPS service uses new Dell servers. As a software platform, the enterprise-level solution is Parallels Cloud Server. The technology provides live migration and fast backups, making the service resistant to hardware problems. Used container virtualization allows you to achieve greater performance VPS compared with hypervisor technology. The peculiarity of container virtualization is the inability to control the OS kernel for the user. All necessary kernel modules are installed upon request, or in the case of frequent use, installed by default. If you need to manage the OS kernel in a virtual machine, you can use cloud VPS .


Connectivity and test results

The data center has 45 uplinks and direct access to AMS-ix, LINX NoVa and NL-ix traffic exchange points to which Western and Russian backbone operators are connected. This guarantees our customers excellent connectivity around the world and with Russia. Favorable location and excellent communication allows you to deploy websites and applications for Europe (including Russia and other CIS countries) and the USA in the Infobox's European location. A small ping value is important for high performance sites .

You can independently test ping to a VPS from the Infobox located in Amsterdam from your location.
To do this, you must run the command "ping ams.sandbox.infobox.ru " in the console. You can ping other Infobox for VPS locations: spb.sandbox.infobox.ru and krs.sandbox.infobox.ru and choose the best one for hosting your website or application.
