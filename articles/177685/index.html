The social network aggregator Meople.Net is now available on Windows 8

At the end of last year , the social networking aggregator project Meople.Net was launched . During this time, work has been carried out on errors, the list of supported resources has been expanded (there are 12 of them at the moment) and the Russian version of the aggregator has been launched.

The next stage of evolution is an application for the Windows 8 operating system.



It is already available in the Windows App Store .

The application, like the whole service, is designed for active "social" users and for SMM specialists. For the latter, it is planned to expand the functionality in the next versions.
