Do not forget: the path to victory

Well, the parade all watched? On TV, online broadcast or out of the house somewhere?

In any case, it will not be necessary to sit still: Victory Day is passing, and the jubilee year continues, therefore the Do not Forget project recently announced on Habr√© represents the city game Path to Victory , made after the motives and in the spirit of the popular Patrols, only this time, participants will have to go the way of an ordinary Moscow family - from the beginning of the war to Victory.

Well, the prizes, as without them: Acer Aspire One netbook, HTC Hero communicator, ABBYY Lingvo x3 package, Windows 7 Pro and others. Geeks should like;)

Start of the game - tomorrow, May 10th. Join us!

The prehistory of the game will tell about the life of an ordinary Moscow family on the eve of the war.

On June 22, 1941, the family hears a radio message, which refers to the German attack on the USSR, and in the following days family members have to part: the father and two sons go to different places on the front, the mother and daughter stay in Moscow. Grandfather, a native of St. Petersburg, does not listen to family persuasion and remains in Leningrad.

The game's plot covers various parts of the war: the father fights in the south, the son in the north, the mother works in the rear, the grandfather in Leningrad during the blockade, as well as various types of military equipment (the father is a tankman, the first son is a pilot, the second son is a scout in behind enemy lines).

The outcome of the storyline will be a meeting of father and sons in captured Berlin on the day of victory.
