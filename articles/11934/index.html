Accessories for iPhone from Belkin.

And here are the accessories. Belkin has officially unveiled a range of accessories for the new Apple iPhone cell phone.
Sport Armband is a sports bag that allows you to attach an iPhone to your shoulder and has a pocket in which you can store headphones or keys. It fully protects the screen of the device, while not hindering free access to phone controls. The bag is made of porous material and perfectly tolerates machine wash. The cost of Sport Armband is $ 30.

The case of Acrylic Case is made of a hard transparent material. It is equipped with a belt clip, which can also be used in combination as a stand for setting an iPhone on the table, for example, for watching videos. Acrylic Case provides full access to the screen, dock connector and controls, its cost is $ 30.
Belkin also released two cables for the iPhone - Mini-Stereo Cable (mini-jack - mini-jack, $ 20), Stereo Cable (mini-jack - RCA, $ 15) and the Headphone Adapter ($ 11), allowing you to connect high-quality headphones to the phone.
