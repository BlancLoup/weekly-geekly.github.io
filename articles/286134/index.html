New ways of development of the Russian IT services market during the crisis

In a crisis in Russia, it is necessary to develop IT areas that will give the fastest economic effect: cloud computing, mobile technologies, big data processing, outsourcing and infrastructure optimization.

According to Gartner, the global IT market in 2015 will grow by 4.6%, which will be $ 964 billion. Unfortunately, this cannot be said about the Russian market.

Dynamics of the Russian IT services market, $ billion


Source: IDC, AP KIT, 2014

Until 2014, the Russian IT market successfully developed, but due to the devaluation of the ruble a significant regress could occur.

According to IDC estimates, in 2015 the share of IT services on the market in dollars will decrease by 10%, and the share of the entire Russian IT market - by 18%.

Providers of Russian IT services are trying to respond quickly to changing global trends. Therefore, there is a chance that in the wake of the general rise of mobile technologies, cloud computing, big data processing and the spread of outsourcing, it will be possible to compensate for the general stagnation in the Russian IT market.
