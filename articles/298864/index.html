The director of the nuclear center promised to replace SAP and Microsoft software.

The director of the Russian Federal Nuclear Center, located in the city of Sarov in the Nizhny Novgorod Region, promised Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev to develop a replacement for SAP and Microsoft software.



The nuclear center plans to create an IT system for managing the life cycle of an industrial product. It will include a product design module that can compete with Siemens and PTC solutions, a process control module that is close in functionality to SAP engineering, and an operating system.
