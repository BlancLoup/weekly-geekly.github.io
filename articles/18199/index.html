Netscape navigator died

Today, on the eve of the new year, the official Netscape Navigator blog announced that it was no longer supporting Netscape Suite, Netscape Browser, or Netscape Messenger. The www.netscape.com page should remain a normal Internet portal, and by the end of January, the latest patches to one of the greatest browsers in the Internet history will be released, which finally lost the war of browsers (BrowserWar). That's how sad AOL closed the story with Netscape after buying it in 1999. Errors made by AOL in recent years are incompatible with the life of the famous browser.
