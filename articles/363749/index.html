Today, WiFi has earned on all lines of the Moscow metro

From December 2, the wireless Wi-Fi network became available on the trains of all Moscow Metro lines, the metro press service reported .

The long-suffering project, promised more than three years ago , nevertheless brought to the end.

Recall in brief the history of the project. In September 2011, Mayor Sobyanin and President Medvedev spoke in favor of introducing WiFi in the metro. The Moscow Department of Information Technologies immediately began negotiations with key operators and the metro. In 2012, the operators launched test sections of free WiFi on the ring line, and the authorities planned to hold an auction to determine the general contractor.
