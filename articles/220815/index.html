Publish apps to the Windows Store and Windows Phone Store and win prizes



If you are porting an existing mobile application or creating a new one, you can win a trip to the Formula 1 United States Grand Prix 2014, $ 10,000 to create your own modern developer corner, a MarketBot 3D printer or a Nokia Lumia 1520 smartphone.

All you need is to publish your Windows application or Windows Phone application store.

Categories of winners


In each category, we will award a student, an individual developer, and a company that belongs to a small or medium-sized business (which has less than 100 employees).

Best Windows 8.1 App


Get the opportunity to join the team of Lotus F1 for a three-day unforgettable journey on 2014 FORMULA 1 UNITED STATES GRANS PRIX. The prize will be able to receive one winner from each group (student, individual developer, small or medium-sized business). The trip will take place from October 31, 2014 to November 2, 2014.

The best developers in the top-9 in this category from each group will get the Nokia Lumia 1520

The best app for Windows Phone 8.1


Win $ 10,000 to create your own modern developer corner. The prize will be able to receive one winner from each group (student, individual developer, small or medium-sized business).

The best developers in the top 9 in this category from each group will get the Nokia Lumia 1520 .

The best cross-platform application

The prize will be able to receive one winner from each group (student, individual developer, small or medium-sized business).

The best applications for Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone 8.1 among the participants of DevCon


If you are a participant in the DevCon 2014 conference, then you have a great opportunity to win even more prizes (Nokia phones). All you need is to start developing an application for Windows or Windows Phone.

You can complete the development on the coding night hackathon under the guidance of our experts.
At the end of the hackathon, we will announce the winners and award them with prizes. All applications that won the hackathon can also apply on the site to participate in a global competition and qualify for other prizes.

How to participate?


1. Register on the contest website before June 1, 2014.
2. Develop a Windows application and / or Windows Phone.
3. Publish it in the Windows / Windows Phone app store.
4. The application must be taken to the store before June 1, 2014.
5. Do not forget to add the application to the contest website . Please note that it is necessary to choose the category to which you are adding the application. Have time to do it before June 1, 2014.

Good luck!
