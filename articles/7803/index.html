Yumisoft announces the start of sales of the site management system UMI.CMS PRO.

Yumisoft company announces the release of the "boxed" version of software UMI.CMS Professional. The new line includes three editions - PRO Corporate, PRO Business and PRO Commerce. The PRO family allows you to create websites of any degree of complexity: portals, online catalogs and stores, online media. From April 30, sales of PRO Corporate and PRO Business will begin.

The UMI.CMS PRO system is based on the current PHP5 platform. The product fully meets the needs of professional web developers. The creators of the UMI.CMS PRO sites offer open source code, thanks to which the extraordinary flexibility and versatility of UMI.CMS PRO is achieved. The unique conditions for the cms market, Private Label, allow developers not to mention the UMI.CMS brand when creating websites based on the system, but simply to earn money from their own branded product running on the UMI platform.
Other advantages of UMI. CMS PRO are its convenience, versatility and low resource intensity.

End users will be pleasantly surprised by the intuitive interface of the product. To perform everyday tasks, you do not have to read multi-volume instructions - you can begin work literally 5 minutes after you get acquainted with the system. Another unique feature of the system is that it is protected not only from external attacks, but also from user erroneous actions - unlike some other cms, any operation here can be canceled and the deleted object restored. The highest usability indicators are an important advantage of the new system.

Each edition of UMI.CMS PRO has its own set of modules, which are selected to solve various business problems. Special modules allow you to support file management, effectively manage online advertising and customize banners, store and process all types of images, etc.
