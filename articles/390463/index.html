The warmth of our homes



Preamble


Winter came unexpectedly, a lot of snow piled up, and then it also became cold. So, what am I talking about? Yes, everything is simple, there is one village in the depths of Siberia, on the border of the Kemerovo Region and the Altai Territory. Frosts there are fierce, but people live good there. In winter, there is not much to do, then snow is removed, but it is not a little snowy, then go to work in the city for 50 km to go.

And here in this village my friends live, sometimes you come to them on Friday, a bath, drifts and a bio-toilet on the street at -40 Celsius. So I'm not talking about that, but about the fact that they have a coal boiler in the house with coal supply by buckets.

Here we sit with Vadim and I ask the question:
- Vadik, how many buckets of coal does your fire-breathing monster consume to heat your 200 square meters of pleasure?
- Well ... somewhere before bedtime I will throw a bucket, in the morning I will throw a bucket in the morning, if necessary during the day, but this is at will or if someone is in the house.

And it became interesting to me, but how many times should I specifically sink a house, and how specifically to sink, and many different other issues.

I started thinking.
