Iap packaged by google

I can't tell a lot, but our team encountered a funny thing: on Google Android, payments from the app (in-app-purchases) work frankly one time. Since I am not directly connected with the development, I can only rely on the words of our CEO and some developers who talk about a bug in the system from Google. In a nutshell - the payment of content from the user passes, and not all users unlock the content, respectively, have to reimburse for $ 14.99. Any patterns (regional, for example) could not be identified. Again, based on the words of the CEO (translation of quotes):

We have already reported about billing problems via IAP at Google and they promised to fix them with ASAP. In the meantime, you can clear the IAP process data and try again.

In fact, this is a problem for all developers using the IAP, and large threads have already been created about these problems [in the sacrament forums] ...

Also, there were words that a bug fix may possibly come only with the new build of Android OS itself.

To be honest, I am more surprised not by the fact of what happened, but by the lack of information about this on news sites. No, I do not want a reason for gloating, I want publicity and reporting information. In addition to our forum, I have never found any references anywhere, and even a search on Habr did not give any sane results. Who knows something - share in the comments, if not difficult. Links are welcome. I think someone will be useful.
