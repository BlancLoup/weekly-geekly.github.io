Comic Hack Quest


My friends in St. Petersburg are addicted to playing Encounter . I have never tried it myself, but I heard a lot of reviews. So, they asked me to make a flash level on the Matrix theme for one of their games (I was given a rough script). The game is the most primitive, is a console with a pseudo-operating system, on which it is necessary to "hack" other servers to find the coordinates of the next game "level". Work for the day, but amused me.

Play here!

IMPORTANT! To start typing, you need to click the mouse on the game screen. To understand what to type, type the command help .
PS Do not ask why not use Linux commands. The game was intended for people far from IT.
PSS Players are looking for the coordinates of the next game point on the map, so when you find them - YOU WIN.
