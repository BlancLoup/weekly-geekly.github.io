2GIS launches beta service of traffic jams

The 2GIS maps now have information about the state of the road. The 2GIS Traffic Jams service shows the current speed of traffic, road sections are indicated by three colors depending on the degree of congestion: red if there is a traffic jam (traffic is slower than 20 km / h), yellow if movement is difficult (from 20 to 40 km / h) , green - if nothing prevents the passage and cars go faster than 40 km / h. Data is updated every 5 minutes.



The new service is still in beta testing mode and is available only in the online version of Novosibirsk's 2GIS , but in the future it will work in other cities, especially where the traffic jam situation is particularly relevant.
