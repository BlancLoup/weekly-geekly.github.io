Dell hardware review: U2713HM monitor, Alienware X51 desktop and Alienware TactX Mouse 570-10880 mouse




Personal computer DELL Alienware X51



The novelty looks quite unusual. Elegant black matte desktop finish with dark chrome elements and three backlit zones controlled by AlienFX software give the system an unusual look. X51 can be positioned both horizontally and vertically, which saves a lot of space on the desktop. The shell thickness is less than 10 cm, and the weight of the device is only 5.49 kg.



Alienware X51's hardware is based on a Mini-ITX motherboard based on the Intel H61 Express chipset. Processors can be different, at the user's choice: there are both available Core i5 and very productive Core i7. Note that, despite the compact size of the case, the processor can not be overheated - to cool it, an Alienware High-Performance Air Cooling air cooler is used. The graphic heart of the new product is the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 555 graphics card with 1 GB of GDDR5 memory. The user also has access to up to 8 GB of DDR3 standard memory with a frequency of 1333 MHz. To store personal data, you can choose a configuration with a 1 TB hard drive.



Thanks to the clear internal structure and the use of universal components, the user has the ability to quickly and easily update the hard disk, graphics card and memory on their own, which makes it possible not to be afraid of the moral obsolescence of the system.



An interesting feature of the design is that the humanoid head also rotates depending on the orientation of the block (!) .

The computer can play the role of home theater. To do this, it can be equipped with an optical drive DVD or Blu-ray to choose from. For the creation of high-quality sound integrated multi-channel 7.1 sound chip.

On the front panel, there are two USB 2.0 ports and sound ports for connecting headphones and a microphone.



The rear panel contains HDMI 1.4, SPDIF interfaces, sound connectors for connecting a multi-channel speaker system, four USB 2.0 ports and two USB 3.0 ports.

Network connectivity is the responsibility of the Wi-Fi wireless module and the Gigabit Ethernet port.

The operating system uses Windows 7 Home Premium.

The computer case can be highlighted with 20 colors to choose from.



System data of our copy



Alienware X51 - a good combination of compactness and performance. Many will like the presence of all the components for the transformation of a functional PC into a home theater with wide possibilities. And the possibility of modernization will allow to cope with the most modern game.
