Webalta will promote itself with the help of Altastat

The Russian search engine Webalta announced a partnership with Altastat , which will promote the search engine through affiliate programs.

For site owners (including blogs) three collaboration programs will be offered. The Altalink program involves placing text links to a Webalta search with payment for referrals of visitors to sites that have hosted links to search results. The Context program provides the placement of contextual advertising of the Optimist system on the sites of program participants with payment for visitors to websites of advertisers working with the Webalta contextual advertising system. The Referalta program is aimed at attracting new members of the Altastat system itself.

The main difference from other players in the Internet advertising market in the company is their liberal attitude towards partners: websites hosted on free hosting will be allowed to participate in advertising programs, the webmaster is not required to provide statistics on the attendance of their resource and remove ads from competitors from its pages .
