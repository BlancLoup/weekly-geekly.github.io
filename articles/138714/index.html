Free webinar about new APIs in ASP.NET MVC 4



Dear colleagues, web developers and just interested! I invite you to visit a free webinar (an online event accessible through a browser), where I will tell students about the new features of the ASP.NET MVC 4 platform and new APIs added to it in the recently released beta version.

Within 30 minutes of the webinar, I will introduce you to the innovations in ASP.NET MVC 4, designed to improve the efficiency of the programmer. Your attention will be presented to the new ASP.NET Web API, new opportunities for creating mobile views of sites, a new API for automatically compressing and packaging JavaScript and CSS files to increase the performance of web applications. In addition, some new features of Visual Studio 11 of the new version will be considered.

The webinar will be held on March 1 at 17-00 Moscow time and will be available to everyone. The session will take from 30 to 45 minutes, after which I will be happy to answer any of your questions. The webinar will be recorded, the recording will be available for free viewing a few days after the event.

I ask everyone to register for the webinar at the following address on the Microsoft events page .

See you at the webinar!
