Computer with Windows 7 and XBox 360 - Microsoft offer for students



Starting from May 22 to September 3, Microsoft offers American, Canadian and French students in addition to a Windows 7 computer with a price of $ 699 a free XBox 360 console with a 4GB hard drive. Quite solid vendors are participating in the offer - you can purchase the kit on Dell.com, HP.com and in the Microsoft Store. Students who are interested in the offer can make a purchase through their email address (* .edu), or by providing their student ID (Student ID).

This promotion will be available on the newly opened Microsoft website " Do the math" , on the pages of which the prices for Apple-made computers and computers of other vendors are compared with Windows 7.
