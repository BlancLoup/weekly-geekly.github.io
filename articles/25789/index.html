Good time of day!

It would be interesting to know the opinion of industry professionals, where and how best to obtain basic knowledge for successful promotion of oneself as an expert in the field of Internet technologies. the Internet.
It is clear that due to the lack of clear standards and means, the direct study of this area converges mainly on self-study. But still, it is possible to have universities, faculties, specialties that are closest to this topic and allow people to avoid the well-known rake and fill in the gaps in the necessary knowledge.
It is possible that someone will remember his university with a kind word, but someone will say the opposite, that it was wasted 5-6 years in vain and I had to break myself and learn again.
Any opinion and experience is interesting.
Thank.

Threat 1.1
The question is posed to the fact that a person wants to change the field of activity in life for online technology, but self-study is not one hundred percent of his topic. I can name two reasons at once:
1) Self-study is difficult to organize. At a time when education provides structured clear knowledge or at least methods of their extraction, it is not necessary to collect information on the bottom of the barrel and structure it yourself.
2) When learning, let us say, even as an evening student, you find yourself in a community of real people and you gain real social connections, and not doubtful anonymous representatives of the Internet communities, but those people who consciously chose the direction and possibly future partners, colleagues, etc. I am opposed to stewing in my own juice, even if you have access to such social. networks as Habr, where they can not even objectively talk about IT education, but only to grumble about what is best to sit at night after work and read the Internet and PCP for dummies.

Threat 1.2
I will also add that I am not currently idle, I read the theory, there is practice, but all the same, priority is given to studying in a specially designated institution. Just for the sake of example: you start reading about one thing, another aims, which opens up a whole layer, without understanding of which it is difficult to go further.
