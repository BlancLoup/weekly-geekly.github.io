Want to know everything about web and mobile development? Ask me how: DevCon School IV

DevCon School will start on March 24 - the fourth school from Microsoft, aimed at immersing in technology. This time we will talk about creating cross-platform clients, let's talk about popular architectural solutions from the point of view of creating a backend. We remind you that in order to become a participant, you need to pass a small test, answering a few questions, we will also tell about them inside.



About school


I am responsible for the maintenance of this school, stasus , and maria_gore , Masha Gorelkina.

As speakers and experts, we invited industry representatives who will share their experience in working with clouds in mobile and web development.

School program:

  • Cloud development, modern backend and containers
  • The experience of building microservice and the concept of microservice architecture
  • Load testing in the cloud
  • Digital Content Protection
  • Serverless Architecture and PaaS
  • Real stories from colleagues at Kaspersky Lab, Disney from Aggregion, LinguaLeo, BinBank, Intash insurance from Binwell, Stoloto, etc.
  • Three days of practical work on your projects
  • Or the opportunity to pass laboratory work on the topic of interest.

The school is free only in full-time format (without online) and you can get into it in one of two ways:

  1. You have registered and selected by answering questions during registration.
  2. You have been invited there and you have a promotional code .

DevCon Schools is a new DevCon conference format created by us so that you can gain more practical knowledge on new technologies.
