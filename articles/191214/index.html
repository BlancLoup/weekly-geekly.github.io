Google updated dictionary search function



Yesterday they posted on their Google+ page a description of the update of a useful word search function. I myself often had to look for a dictionary definition, synonyms, or translation of a specific word. Previously, it took time, which was spent on opening an online translator, making requests a la 'what is% object%?' and search online dictionaries, but now this can be done by simply typing a define or definition in the search box before the search word, which is much easier to agree on. Entering a word in a foreign language, we get its translation and definition, by entering a word in your native language, you can get a complete list of definitions from a variety of dictionaries, its synonyms, use in sentences and the origin ... but there is one BUT

So far, the update of this function is available only for users from the USA, even the UK has been cheated.
This is another, and in my opinion, a very successful evolutionary step of the search engine Google, I hope that this function will be updated soon with us.
