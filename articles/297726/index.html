Cookies will have to buy



Going to the dark side is becoming more and more difficult. Very soon you will have to pay for it and dough. A lot of dough.

Judging by a cursory study of the issue, the big galactic war has not yet captured Russia, but I suspect it is a matter of time. Very few and many people and companies using Star Wars images will have to face Disney lawsuits and discuss in court the legitimacy of their right to call their vacancy java-jedi.

The essence of the problem is simple - Disney, who bought the rights to Star Wars! = Lucas. For many years, the cult space around the Force and its generation was very weakly controlled, which, in particular, made it possible for it to bloom so luxuriantly. Actually all appeals to the murdered spirit of Star Wars, with which the social networks are filled, and about which Lucas himself wrote, have to do with how the cult will live outside of cinema space.

What we know for sure - numerous toasters and waffle irons with Darth Vader are dying out in the last days - Disney is famous for its manic legal service, which for years has made the studio horizon protected by studio rights. For you to properly evaluate Disney's capabilities - US copyright terms are reviewed every time Mickey Mouse's rights approach the expiration line. Disney is one of the main lobbyists in the copyright zone.

This, of course, does not mean that they will be lost. Simply, they will become more expensive in the amount of the license, something like this .

Where all this goes, in fact, it is not clear. It is known that Disney relatively recently drew attention to the accompanying business, starting with the princesses (read the detailed layout here ) and it is clear from the process and amounts that they have tasted this part of the pie and will not give it to anyone. How far they can get into litigation is incomprehensible - the images of star wars over the years have really penetrated much where. For example, an excellent incident - ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jedaism . In theory, all these people owe Disney money. But we understand that the chances for this are slim. In any case, there is a good chance that in the near future not only the life of Star Wars will change, but also the life around Star Wars.
