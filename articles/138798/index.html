SEO promotion on Android markets

In the last post we talked about the features of SEO-promotion in the App Store and in the Android Market. Now let's take a closer look at the specifics of other markets for Android applications (Amazon Appstore, Vodafone App Select, V Cast Apps).

At first glance, they do not differ much: everywhere you have to write clear descriptions, post screenshots, add keywords. But there are differences that lie at first glance insignificant details.

1. Amazon Appstore



Unlike the Android Market, here, as in the Apple App Store, there is a special field for keywords. Places for them did not regret, highlighting as much as 500 characters. The search is performed both by keywords and by name.

The peculiarity of the application description is that Amazon employees check and edit them, adjusting them to the format of their market. Therefore, not everything will depend on you here.

Plus, the Amazon Appstore only supports English.

2. Vodafone App Select



Android market of one of the largest European operators. A promising market for developers, opening the "door" to millions of loyal users.

AppSelect offers two fields for the description: one for 280 characters, the second - for 1000 characters. Naturally, all the basic "chips" should be listed in the first.
