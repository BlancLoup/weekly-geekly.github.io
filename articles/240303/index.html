Added Android x64 support to the Android emulator.

Android application developers had the opportunity to test their programs on the 64-bit Intel platform.

Last week, Google added 64-bit images for the Android emulator under Windows, Linux and Mac OS. Images are available for download on the Android site for developers . 64-bit Android applications can address more than 3 GB of RAM, in addition, they have access to additional processor registers.

We add that at least the expected emulator of the new ARMv8 architecture has not yet been released.
