The essence of iTablet

Introduction


It remains less than a day before the moment when we, most likely, will see the long-awaited tablet from Apple. I myself have been very much looking forward to this since the beginning of the year, and now, it seems, everything will soon become clearer.
Once, 2.5 years ago, literally an hour after the start of sales, I wrote the first (?) Russian review of the iPhone, shared my impressions and thoughts. Since then, my ‚Äúapple‚Äù passion has arisen. Revolution


My favorite moment from the presentations of Steve Jobs is when he announces the release of an iPhone, and says that "we reinvented the phone." Then many laughed at this, but the fact remains that, and after that, other phones began to appear with touchscreens, convenient browsers, and so on.

As for this tablet computer ... it will also change the world :)



Just imagine: let's say 30 years ago, in order to get new knowledge, a person needed to go to a special institution - to a vocational school, university, some kind of courses. Something small could be found by going to the library. Although again, you spend more time on the road and searching for the right book.
10-12 years ago it became possible to buy a disc in the direction of interest, on which practically everything needed would be collected.
Now you can go to the computer, open Wikipedia or Yandex / Google and find everything you want, the most relevant, in a matter of seconds. Of course, on Wikipedia, you will not learn nuclear physics, but we are talking about ordinary people with everyday interests, tasks and questions.
Also, sitting, you can send a letter to the other side of the planet or find out the latest news, see fresh photos of classmates. But all the same, for this you need to go to the computer, take a mouse in there and look at it so that the arrow on the screen goes where you need it. This is how the ba does not look very natural. In fact, the basic principles of modern computers have not changed since the 1980s, when that same ePL released the first computers with a mouse. All these decades computers have become more powerful, the interfaces are more beautiful, the mice are more comfortable, BUT everything has remained the same: a person sees a picture, moves the mouse over the screen, selects some elements on the screen, and so on.

Remember, at the presentation of the iPhone, Jobs said that they say who wants to use the stylus when we naturally have the most convenient control device - our fingers. So what do we (presumably) give this new tablet? It can be taken in hand, no matter lying on the couch, sitting at a table or on the way in a train, and reading the new information, like a regular book or newspaper. It will be possible to poke your ‚Äúnatural‚Äù finger at links on sites and program buttons. This is where the most important thing happens - the intermediate link in the contact between the person and the computer is excluded! A completely new stage of evolution, a new level of interactivity, when any sites are felt ‚Äúat your fingertips‚Äù. Purpose


According to rumors, the company plans to sell 10 million of these devices in the first year. To achieve this, you need to offer something really interesting and designed for the mass consumer.
And why are computers or laptops commonly used now? Reading mail, viewing sites, well, photos / videos to watch sometimes. BOOM! These people will have the opportunity to take a small device that fits into any bag, and on this device they will be able to get the maximum usability of those very basic functions - surfing the Internet, watching pictures and videos ... and plus, because the device is as big as a magazine, you can and magazines on it to read. And no longer need to go to the kiosk in order to find the right magazine, and then throw it out. The appearance of aytyuns encouraged people to buy music files instead of buying CDs. Now the same can be done with newspapers and magazines - subscribed, got a fresh release right on your device. Publishers have long been talking about reducing revenues from issuing paper publications - and here again, and you can incredibly reduce the cost of production (no need to print) and the distribution of your products, and revenues can even be increased through better advertising. Well, there is already a rental video in aytyuns - we went somewhere on a business trip, downloaded a new movie and watch it on the tablet on the train. But still, how incredibly convenient will be surfing the Internet. In fact, reading any forum will become as simple and convenient as reading a book.

Equipment


Now a little about the technical stuffing and how it might look.


I remember recently there was even a rumor that Core2Duo, gigabytes of memory and so on would be inside. This, of course, is almost impossible - such a tablet will weigh too much and work too little on the battery. The ARM Cortex A9 based chip, which consumes much less energy than even the intel Atom, can cope with internet surfing, mail, movies, books, documents and music. It's not just that Apple acquired a stake in the chip maker PASemi.
