Decision


I decided to use such a cool tool like tables in GoogleDocs =) It is free, cross-platform and tede - I think it needs no introduction. Before drawing up the frame of the tablet, I tried to understand what fields I needed. They can come up with at least a hundred, but we must make it so that they are convenient to use and do not bother to fill each time. Well, try to take into account further scaling: that it would be convenient to use the tablet in a year or two or three.

I stopped my thoughts on the following set of fields:

  1. Category I analyzed what I photographed and divided it into categories. It turned out like this:

    Cars - cars
    Events - events
    Gadgets - Gadgets
    Girls - well, you understand
    Home - something homely, family
    Life - any movement not falling into the categories above
    - =)
    Travel - travel


    All photosets will be decomposed into these sections. If you are photographing a lot, then each section is more convenient to keep on a separate sheet (at the bottom of the table).


    Important : Try to avoid creating the category ‚ÄúOther‚Äù (Miscellaneous), since it is in it that chaos will arise that will collapse the universe. Maximum - ‚Äú! Temp‚Äù, into which you will merge files for further sorting into other categories.
  2. The name . Inside the category, each photoset has a name - it is necessary to give such names that will be easy to remember or find.   3. Where now . In this column, I indicate where the photos are now stored - on the camera's memory card, on a laptop, on an external disk or in the cloud. If the location of the data changes, the label is updated. It is important to provide information about the photoset immediately, otherwise it will be forgotten later.
  4. Pieces before sorting . It does not always work (or rather, it never happens at all) to take right away and sort the gigabytes of RAWs, usually you just dump them from the memory card. And here it is important to understand how many photos in the photoset - in order to estimate how long it will take to sort and process.

    Life hacking : it will be useful to know the average speed of sorting and processing photos, if you bother with it at all. Just set the timer for 5-10 minutes and then see how much time they have improved. It takes me an average of 2-5 minutes to photograph (assuming that I know the hot keys in Photoshop well). Further see p. 8.
  5. Sorting and processing . Just two columns, the cells of which are filled with either green (= ‚ÄúDone‚Äù) or red (= ‚ÄúNot done‚Äù) color.     By sorting, I mean the selection of the best personnel (removal of repeats and rejects) for further processing, and under the processing itself - their way from rav to jeep (which is not ashamed to show others).   6. Copy in the cloud . Usually there is no point in pouring the unsorted stratum of photos into the cloud, it is a waste of time and space. There it makes sense to back up already sorted photos.   7. Copy to disk . Clouds are considered to be reliable, but something inside suggests that it is better to have a backup also locally (at least for very important data). Well, or if we are talking about some "sensitive" data that I would not want to upload to the Internet.
