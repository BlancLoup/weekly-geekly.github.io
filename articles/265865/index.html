Today, at 12:00 (MSK), see Jump Start broadcast: "Windows 10 in the corporate network"



Do not miss the broadcast of the first technical online event dedicated to the new Windows 10.
On September 2, from 12-00 (Moscow time) , Microsoft technical experts will consider the issues of setting up, migrating, securing, and managing mobile devices on the new Windows 10.

This event will be of interest to all technical specialists and CIOs who are planning to migrate to the new OS.

Windows 10 has entered the market. Many devices with Windows 7 and 8.1 have already received the "top ten" in the form of a free update. Companies are beginning to look closely at the new OS and assess its potential for solving corporate tasks. And if in the future you are planning to switch to Windows 10, it's time to look at the approaches and tools of migration, the ability to manage devices with Windows 10, as well as some of the security technologies of the new OS.
