Total


I am satisfied with the prefix, not one game has been passed, more than one fall has survived, there was more than one day lying at zero, and the main thing is that it is still alive.

Pros:
1) A smart display among similar devices, high-quality IPS matrix with good viewing angles and a decent resolution of 1280x800.
2) A rechargeable battery even after 9 months of use, depending on the load, works from three to five hours. I can say that sometimes I forgot to charge the device, leaving it for a couple of days discharged to zero - without further consequences.
3) The presence of analog sticks, two pairs of keys and additional system keys allows you to comfortably play both through emulators and Android games.
4) Ergonomics - the device looks large and heavy, but in fact it is convenient to hold, small thickenings in places of grip do their work, as a result, playing, holding this device in hands, is pleasant and convenient.
5) Custom firmware, correcting minor problems and the popularity of the device, which means a large community of users.

Minuses:
1) The software part (firmware) has some problems, the fix option is to install custom firmware.
2) Wi-Fi. Due to the design features, namely the location of the antenna behind the metal case of the display - this is a -1 stick at the antenna signal, we put the console on the stand and the problem is solved.
3) The case squeaks like a 3DSK.
