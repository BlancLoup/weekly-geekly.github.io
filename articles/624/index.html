MODx-095-RC2 released


In addition to the fact that a patch was recently released, covering the only hole found so far in this system, the release candidate of version 095 just now appeared.


MODx is, in fact, not a CMS, it is a framework. All ajax, web 2.0 and SEO friends. Well, you know.
And in the new version, our foreign friends have preliminarily packed a bunch of useful and pleasant lotions developed by the community.

And personally, I appreciated the redesigned back-end interface, which was four times quicker and much nicer by sight. A little bit more, and in my humble opinion, one of the best open CMS of modernity will be born. (A little bit, this is when someone puts a bridge between phpbb and MODx in the repository. Maybe it will even be me, who knows?)
