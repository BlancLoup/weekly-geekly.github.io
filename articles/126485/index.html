A new gadget will show where the keys are hiding.



Probably no more secretive things than the keys. Only just put them on the shelf near the front door, looking - and there are no keys anymore. And if the keys need to be found not in an hour or two, but in a couple of days, this is a task worthy of Sherlock Holmes. Once I saw a joking solution to solve this problem: an old mobile phone hooked to a keychain. Lost keys - you can call the phone, everything is instantly located. But modern technology allows you to go even further!

The other day, the Cobra Tag device was introduced, which works with Bluetooth, GPS, allowing you to easily determine exactly where the keys lie. To solve this problem once and for all, you need a Cobra Tag gadget (the size of a keychain), a mobile phone and an application for the Cobra Tag.

Such a system offers several ways to find lost keys. First, you can set the maximum distance from the keys, after which the phone will give a signal, and show where the keys are. Thus, you will never forget the keys to an apartment or car on a table in a cafe. Secondly, you can simply launch the application, press a couple of buttons, and the display will show a diagram where you can see where the keys are.

That is, in the morning it will be possible not to waste time looking for keys that may be in the most unexpected place (especially if it is Monday morning). I woke up, picked up a smartphone, launched the application - the keys were found, there is no problem.

The most interesting thing is that the system works in the forward and reverse direction. Those. the key fob will allow you to find the phone if you forgot it somewhere.
