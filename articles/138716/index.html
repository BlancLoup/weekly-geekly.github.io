Lectures of Microsoft and the Skolkovo Open University within the framework of SumIT, a winter startup school

The winter start-up school of high-tech SumIT, with the support of the Skolkovo Open Innovative University and the vice-governor of St. Petersburg, presents to your attention the second project session to be held on February 24 at Kronverksky 49.



On this day you can listen to lectures:
Gaidar Magdanurov , director of the Microsoft Seed Fund ;
Ekaterina Morozova , Deputy Executive Director of OtUS ;
Albert Efimov , director of projects for the IT cluster of the Skolkovo Foundation ;
Vladimir Vasilyev , Rector of NRU ITMO;
Vasily Kichadzhi , Vice-Governor of St. Petersburg ;
Sam Rosenbalma (USA), leader of the technology entrepreneurship development group ;

And also take part in round tables and master classes with David Yan (Founder of ABBYY) and Natalia Kaspersky (Kaspersky Lab).

Also at the winter school are expected and other prominent figures in the field of high technology.

The purpose of the course: teaching students the basic skills of creating, shaping, working out and implementing business ideas in the field of information technology. Classes are held in the format of theoretical lectures and workshops, which work is carried out in teams.

SumIT is a unique scientific and practical project, the purpose of which is to help young professionals apply the skills acquired during training. School participants will be able to:
  • listen to the lectures of specialists of leading IT-companies;
