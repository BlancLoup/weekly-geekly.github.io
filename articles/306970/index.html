Welcome to the NeuroHack hackathon on August 5



We invite everyone to participate in the hackathon for scientists specializing in the fields of neurology, neuro rehabilitation, studying higher nervous activity, neuroscience for sports, etc., as well as developers, programmers, engineers and marketers involved in the creation of AI, machine learning technologies, neural networks , computer vision and streaming video / audio processing. Hackathon will go 48 hours, from 5 to 7 August. Mail.Ru Group has established one of the hackathon nominations, it is more suitable for the team making a mass product for the average user. Our company will award the winner in the nomination 250,000 rubles for the development of his project. Under the cut - a list of interesting topics and details of participation in the hackathon.

Participants will need to team up and prototype products in the following nominations:

  1. Neurofing camera - a complex from a gadget and a smartphone application that removes what you are interested.
  2. Background removal - in selfie mode, the application changes an arbitrary background to video.
  3. Automatic filters and photo cropping is a photo application that independently frames and selects the most successful filter.
  4. Replacing faces in movies with a user's face is an application similar to MSQRD.
  5. Face search is an application that looks for which characters in the pictures of the classics look like you.
  6. Drawing pictures - an application in which you can choose from the "grass", "water", "sky" palettes, to drive with your finger and - it turns out like an artist.
  7. Application that reveals the peculiarities of the psyche of the photo.
  8. The application, which in real time shows how you or your girlfriend will grow old.
  9. An audio translator application that plays your own voice.
  10. Creating music with neural networks , for example, auto-writing music for a video clip.
  11. An application that changes the spoken text to the voice of a famous person , for example, Stalin.

It will also be necessary to calculate the market volume and create a business model for the product, develop a presentation and a plan for market launch.

Before the start of the hackathon, participants will be able to listen to speeches on topics:

  • Market overview: opportunities and perspectives in neuromarketing, data analysis, brain-computer interfaces.
  • Technologies for the neurotechnology market.
  • Examples of projects created on the basis of data obtained by neuroscience.

Following the results of the competition, the jury will select the best projects, the authors of which will be awarded prizes from our partners and will be able to continue working on projects at the HSE Lab. The hackathon is supported by the Innovation Assistance Foundation, RVC, Mail.Ru Group, the Organization for the Development of Computer Science and Neuronet. The hackathon organizers - SCIENCE GUIDE - a community of young scientists in Russia, with the support of the HSE Lab, Gray Matter and Brain Company.

Location: Moscow, Maly Konyushkovsky Pereulok, 2
Registration starts: at the ASI site at 18:00 on Friday, August 5
Final performance of the teams: at 19:00 on August 7
Registration: leader-id.ru/event/2473
