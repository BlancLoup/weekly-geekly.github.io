The co-owner of Anywayanyday and the president of the sailing federation will invest $ 3 million in an online real estate agency

Co-owner of online services for the purchase of airline tickets and hotel booking of Anywayanyday and online guide Localway Kirill Podolsky and President of the Russian Sailing Federation Vladimir Silkin will jointly invest $ 3 million in the online real estate agency MyHomeDay .

100% of Mykhumdey LLC is owned by Alexander Dasaev. He is the general director of LLC. The Firrma publication assumes that the same person launched the GoTable service for booking free tables in restaurants.

MyHomeDay provides users with a database of offers with descriptions, photos and apartment layouts. Users can also go to the section "Guide to districts of Moscow", in which basic information on the infrastructure of districts of the city is available. The service provides the ability to filter by areas, metro stations, number of rooms, price and other parameters, as well as sorting by price and date of placement. The site allows you to subscribe to the specified parameters of the desired accommodation.

Payment for the contacts of the owners and the agency commission are not charged to the user. The service earns a commission from homeowners, which is 50% of the monthly payment for an apartment for rental transactions, 2% of the price for sale transactions. On MyHomeDay presented Moscow apartments comfort class and higher.

Service exists in the form of a web site and a mobile application. At the moment, developers are expanding the functionality of a personal account for owners and tenants. So, the possibility of paying the rent, ordering cleaning services, buying insurance and preliminary scoring for a mortgage loan will be added. In addition, it is planned to launch a mobile application for agents.
