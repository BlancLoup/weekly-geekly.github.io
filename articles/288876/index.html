How to make an article?

You are ready to accept it and take it adequately if you yourself feel where you made a mistake, and where you frankly faked it. You know that the article does not reach the level of your usual publications. Desperate to fail, you conclude that you should continue to work on your copyright (writing) skills. And perhaps in 2-3 years your articles will finally begin to notice ...

Traditional Books & Online Content


Of course, you heard that the reader‚Äôs behavior on the Internet is significantly different from the offline behavior of the reader. The way we read a paper book is not at all like reading updates from your favorite blogs in a reader.

The fact is that proven and high-quality content usually gets into books. Yes, and books get to us, not all in a row - but only selected ones. Those for whom we were willing to pay money, and therefore we are confident in the value of the information they carry.

With online content - otherwise. Web 2.0 made the Internet accessible to everyone, which could not but affect the quality of the content that is published here. The specificity of the perception of content on the Internet follows from its features. The information to which the reader has free access is much more here than offline. But no one guarantees its quality. The only way to make sure that the selected content is interesting and useful is to check it yourself. The problem is that with all the desire a person cannot read not only several thousand, but even several hundred publications per day. And there remains only one alternative ...

Correct formatting


Here are some other techniques for making your writing apt to scan:

  1. Use short paragraphs and leave at least one blank line between them. This banal and simple technique makes your article much more ‚Äúfriendly‚Äù to the reader.     At its core, the subtitle is no less important than the title of the article and belongs to the type of content that will be read in the first place. Formatting and content (about thoughtful readers)
