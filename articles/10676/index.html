Cutting and cooling


Go to the useless pieces, connected to a computer via USB. Remember the calendar-shredder , about which I dream, it seems, not only me, but also a good half of those who have seen it at least once in the picture? So for simple geeks there was an interesting substitute - a manual USB shredder. Of course, manually destroying calendar sheets is not as high-tech, but as effective as with the red box of Susanna Hertrich. A manual shredder can work on several AA batteries, apparently in case of use away from home. Dimensions of the piece - 25 x 7 x 5 cm, weight 351 grams.


The last thing today will be appreciated in the first place by the office plankton. This is a USB fridge for aluminum cans with soft drinks. It's nice to keep such a thing on your desktop and periodically get out something invigorating and refreshing, especially in summer when the temperature inside the room can be higher than outside.

The cooling rate of an average can, according to the manufacturer, is 8 degrees in 5 minutes. You can buy a piece for only $ 17 in the USB Geek store. The size of the mini fridge is 19.5 x 9 x 8 cm, and the weight is 365 grams.
