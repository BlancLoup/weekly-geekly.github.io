Federal Bailiff Service accepts payment by Yandex.Money

The official website of the Federal Bailiff Service of Russia connected the Yandex.Cassa solution, the agency reports . Users will be able to pay debts in new ways with a commission of 2.5%.

The FSSP reports that 26 thousand debts are repaid monthly using payment systems on the site, in 2014, users made 310,570 payments. Within a few days after payment in one of the ways on the site, the debt is transferred to the account of the recoverer, and enforcement proceedings cease. The average amount of online payment in 2014 amounted to 2116 rubles.

Yandex.Cassa will allow you to pay taxes, utilities and other debts from bank cards, via Sberbank and Alfa Bank Internet banks, in cash through terminals and mobile phone shops and Yandex.Money. For clients of Sberbank is available transfer via mobile bank via SMS. Commission - 2.5% of the payment amount. New payment methods on the site will allow users to avoid penalties for non-payment.

The total amount of penalties of the department last year is 430 billion rubles for 40 million enforcement proceedings. Lawyer Vladimir Zherebenkov noted that the department had achieved this result due to the expansion of powers and the increase in the amount of fines for administrative offenses.
