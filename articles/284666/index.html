Is it necessary to obtain a license from the National Bank of Ukraine to create a company abroad?

Over the past few months, there have been a lot of talk about the need to obtain individual licenses of the National Bank of Ukraine in order to incorporate in the West when receiving foreign investment.

Your humble correspondent with the invaluable support of the author of the blog iFinance will tell about it in more detail :)



Original case


An agreement has been reached on receiving investments in the amount of X USD.

A company is created in which the founders are the project team and the investor (who invests the sum of X and receives a share of Y).
UPD. It was decided that the project will not be incorporated in Ukraine. (to whom the other is relevant - the situation with incorporation in Ukraine is considered in the comments ).

In other words, the team (residents of Ukraine) does not invest real money in the company (this is important ... the reasons are lower).

During the negotiations (at their different stages), the opinion is voiced that in order to create a company abroad, the team needs to obtain individual licenses from the NBU (National Bank of Ukraine), otherwise you may get into trouble with certain penalties.

A detailed study of the issue showed that in the framework of the original case without licensing, nothing fatal will occur, in general, will not.
