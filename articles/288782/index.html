We register electronic media

Sooner or later, site owners begin to think about protecting their rights to content. In addition, sites creating their own content are interested in facilitating the receipt of copyright materials. All this can contribute to the registration of the media, in our case - the electronic.

Media registration is handled by the FEDERAL SERVICE FOR SUPERVISION IN THE FIELD OF COMMUNICATIONS, INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES AND MASS COMMUNICATIONS (ROSCOMNADZOR). Their representatives are present in all regions of Russia, but only Moscow deals with the registration of electronic media. Consider more options for media registration

Registration through intermediaries - the cost of intermediary services in various regions of our vast country ranges from 5 to 35 thousand rubles, you pay all fees yourself. Given this fact, it is at least unwise to pay services to intermediaries.
