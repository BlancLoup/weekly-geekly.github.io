From ideas to solutions with SAP StreamWork and Evernote


We all happened in such a situation: there is a team meeting, high activity of the participants, a lot of ideas, everything is fine, but in the end, the decisions made are not implemented in practice, and you can only regret the useless time spent.

The problem is that in many cases these team meetings are key points for interaction and communication between employees, and they do not always take into account all the ideas and changes that occur and occur in the intervals between these meetings in the real world. Now, with the help of Evernote and SAP StreamWork, it will be easier for your team to go from idea to realization

Integration
SAP StreamWork allows you to collect the collected information together and use it for brainstorming, collaboration and decision-making. Since inspiration can visit you unexpectedly, SAP uses Evernote as an improvised tool for collecting ideas. Thanks to Evernote API, Evernote integration into SAP StreamWork applications has become possible, and now users can take notes, dictate audio recordings, save web pages in Evernote and subsequently view all their notes in StreamWork interface.

Business application
We are particularly pleased with this integration, since this is our first experience of integration with a business-oriented service. And this is surprising: we created Evernote as a tool for users to help them remember important moments of their personal lives, but since launch we have received countless requests for integration with business solutions. This is the first experience, others are expected soon.

Less stress
When you're working on an exciting project, you just can't stop thinking about it. In the office, in the store, in the fitness club, you keep thinking of how to make the project better. Previously, such moments left a certain feeling of anxiety, because you knew that the idea that came to mind would be forgotten with a 95% probability. More of this will happen. It is enough to take the phone and make a text or audio note in Evernote. Thanks to the integration, this entry will immediately become available in StreamWork, where it will wait for your colleagues.

How it works
In StreamWork, select "Add> Collection> Add an item> Evernote". You will be asked to log in to your Evernote account. There you can select a notebook, view your notes and then add them to your project. After that, your colleagues will be able to view notes, make comments and use in the decision-making process. Very comfortably.

Try
SAP StreamWork is available for free. Register your account .

Evernote API
Evernote API allows developers to create desktop, mobile and online applications that interact with Evernote. There are dozens of already released applications , and thousands of others are in development. Use our API in your development today!
