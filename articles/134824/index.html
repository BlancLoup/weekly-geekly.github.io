2GIS - iOS version appeared

They waited! Great reference with the map, now on the iPhone!


2GIS is an excellent domestic mapping service, the main focus of which is on actual data on organizations and institutions. Therefore, the system is mainly known in companies, but in recent years the service has been actively gaining popularity among home users.



The iPhone client works with 3D maps, is familiar with 140 cities in Russia and Ukraine and over 1,257,000 companies. Allows you to find all the necessary information about the company (address, phone, time, etc.), search for companies by activity and more.

All the necessary data is installed on the phone, so the application can work WITHOUT an internet connection.

The application is free and available on the App Store .
