Bitcoin Cryptocurrency Report at the First Forex Congress

April 18, 2014 in Moscow, the first international forex congress was held , organized by the Center for the Control of OTC Financial Instruments and Technologies (CRFIN).

The most eminent Forex offices gathered in order to discuss the adoption of the draft law on licensing activities designed to combat deceivers - "forex-kitchens." On the good side, the result of the adoption of such a law is crowding out to the side of everyone who discredits the market, deceives people, and the side effect is reduced competition. Now forex offices thousands, and potentially will be much less.

The event was organized at the highest level - free participation, handouts, a chic restaurant, a buffet table, etc.

One of the most interesting moments of the congress was the report on cryptocurrency.



In this video you can learn a little about the history of bitcoin and its role in business, margin trading, the history of one of the exchanges - ICBIT, the mathematical model of futures and the proposal to regulate bitcoin in the Russian Federation with the assistance of existing stock and financial associations.
