Homeless in Texas are used as mobile hotspots

Bartle Bogle Hegarty from Texas organized an original campaign: free internet for Austin residents via mobile hotspots that constantly move around the city. At the same time, the company found a way to reduce the cost of transporting equipment: they simply distributed 4G-to-WiFi routers to homeless people in Austin. Those are constantly on the street, and their movements perfectly correspond to such work.
