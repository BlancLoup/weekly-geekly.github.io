  • SPbSPU, optical vision system for the blind based on Kinect. The implemented system is capable of using Kinect and the OpenCV library to recognize certain objects, faces and obstacles in the three-dimensional scene, and to describe the scene to its owner using voice messages.
  • MSU and MIPT, Child Monitor project . The project proposes to automatically monitor the mood of the child using the Kinect sensor and superimpose a map of his moods on the calendar. In this way, parents will be able to trace how various actions affect the mood of their child in order to appropriately change their behavior and the set of proposed activities. It is assumed that the system will be useful for early diagnosis and prevention of serious diseases, such as autism.
  • Irkutsk State University, the project Kiwi. The application for WindowsPhone, analyzes the psychological state of people based on entertainment resources: aphorisms, interesting facts and psychological tests. It proposes to get rid of large-scale problems by solving them even at the source - at the level of each individual. Each player is supplied with a Windows Phone 7 phone, which allows you to organize collective dynamic games with social behavior that can be tied to useful activities such as garbage collection. The project will allow the child to be torn away from computer games and captivate with street entertainment at the intersection of reality and virtuality.
  • Saratov State Technical University, the project "System of mobile robot robots." Students drew attention to an important, but not very noticeable at first glance, problem - the neutralization of minefields left over from the time of hostilities. To do this, they propose to use a system of mobile robots that are capable of collective behavior. They demonstrate the model of collective behavior of robots in the form of a program on XNA, as well as a ready prototype of a sapper robot
  • TUSUR (Tomsk), MDVoice project. The project proposes a method for early diagnosis of laryngeal diseases (such as laryngeal cancer) based on voice analysis. According to the UN, one of the important problems of our time is the problem of motherhood, which is also associated with the problem of reducing the birth rate in our country. It is known that leaks are guilty of a significant percentage of water consumption during its transportation, and the flow rate directly depends on the pressure in the pipes. The implemented prototype of the system allows to flexibly control the pressure in the water supply system, ensuring the minimum allowable pressure depending on the current water consumption by the consumer.
  • TPU and TSU (Tomsk), the project SuperTomsk. The project is focused on improving the literacy level of children based on Kinect.
