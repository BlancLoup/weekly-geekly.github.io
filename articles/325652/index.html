Zabbix Moscow Meetup in the office of Badoo on April 15


Hello! We declare registration at the open. We once again accept Zabbix community in our office . Below is a description of the speeches. The event starts at 12:00.


Program


11:00 - Start registration (come to have time to drink coffee and take the best places)


12:00 - 12:45 - Alexey Vladyshev, Zabbix
12:45 - 13:00 - Questions
13:05 - 13:50 - Ilya Ableev, Badoo
13:50 - 14:05 - Questions


14:10 - 14:45 - Lunch break and excursions to the Badoo office


14:50 - 15:35 - Alexander Zobnin, Grafana Labs

16:30 - 16:45 - Questions

Speakers and Topics


Alexey Vladyshev, Zabbix



Topic: Zabbix: what are we working on?


I will share my thoughts on the direction of Zabbix development. I'll tell you about what has already been implemented for version 3.4, what remains to be done and when it will be ready.


  • Find out if there is a backend in 3.4 NoSQL. And if so, which one?
  • Learn how to manage users and their access rights without touching Zabbix.
  • How can one or two mouse clicks control the frequency of collecting millions of metrics?

And much more, we have something to talk about. And, of course, I will try to answer all the questions of the participants of the mitap. Come, there will be a lot of interesting information. You won't be bored!


Ilya Ableyev, Badoo



Subject: Zabbix in Badoo: we respond quickly and efficiently


In conditions of large infrastructure and a considerable number of critical components, the response time to an incident should be as short as possible. In the report, I will tell you what tools help to increase the speed of reaction and notify about the problem more qualitatively.


  1. Infrastructure / specifics of the company and operations department
  2. Why do we need Zabbix
  3. Flow of the monitoring department
  4. With the help of which we increase the speed / quality of work
    4.1. Triggers in the console
    4.2. Notifications on the desktop
    4.3. Zabbix in Telegram

Alexander Zobnin, Grafana Labs



Topic: How to stop fighting with charts and start living


I will tell you about the integration of Zabbix and Grafana, so that you can improve the possibilities of visualizing monitoring data using Grafana.


  1. Why do we need graphics?
  2. How to draw 100 graphs in 10 seconds? (Query Editor, Regex, Templating)
  3. And then what to do with it? (Max Data Points, Functions, Performance)
  4. Events are also Time Series (Annotations)
  5. Seek & Destroy (Alerting in Grafana)
  6. Bonus: Heatmap

References to the project: 1 and 2 .


Andrey Denisov, IT specialist in a telecommunication company



Topic: Pending Database Monitoring


Probably many have tried using Zabbix solution for monitoring databases. From my report you will learn about our experience in its use, and what we eventually came to.


  1. Regular Zabbix database monitoring: features of implementation / customization on an industrial scale
  2. Advantages / disadvantages of the database monitoring solution from Zabbix SIA
  3. Advantages / disadvantages of existing Zabbix extensions for monitoring databases
  4. Learn more about the DBforBix v2.3 beta extension: configuration, features
  5. Refinement of DBforBix : save the advantages and eliminate the drawbacks of regular monitoring of Zabbix databases
  6. Idea development options
