Yandex and KamAZ are working on the creation of unmanned vehicles


The project of an unmanned minibus on the Shuttle electric drive from KamAZ and NAMI. First presented to the public in August of this year at the Moscow International Automobile Salon

The general trend in the development of unmanned vehicles, which Lyft, Uber, Tesla Motors, General Motors, Google and other companies are currently developing, has not bypassed domestic producers.

Internet giant Yandex has signed an agreement with KamAZ, an automaker, under which companies and the institute will jointly develop unmanned vehicles, including those suitable for use in the metropolis. An example is the retention of a car in a lane, the execution of an emergency braking maneuver and control of the driver.

Also, the press service of Yandex for Geektimes answered a number of questions regarding future cooperation with KamAZ.

On the question of what technologies Yandex already has, the press secretary Dmitry Gorchakov has the technologies that are suitable for creating robotized cars, and said that working together with KamAZ specialists will be carried out in several areas:

The first is the adaptation of Yandex services for cars (including taking into account the characteristics of trucks), the introduction of voice control multimedia system of the car and its integration with a mobile device. It is also about creating high-precision maps and solutions for semi-autonomous driving, based on a set of technologies of artificial intelligence of Yandex: computer vision, machine learning, speech technologies.
