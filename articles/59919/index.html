Callpod Dragon Bluetooth Headsets - Walkie-Talkie Headset



A Bluetooth headset is convenient, and a Bluetooth headset with a walkie-talkie function is doubly convenient. Two Callpod Dragon Bluetooth Headsets can connect to each other. And their owners communicate for free at a distance of 100 meters (328 feet). In closed rooms, the range of the headset is somewhat reduced, but it should be enough for a couple of floors.

Other features of the gadget include the multipoint function, thanks to which the headset can simultaneously connect to two mobile phones or a Skype + mobile phone bundle. In addition, a two-microphone noise reduction system is built into the headset. The battery lasts for 8 hours of talk or 300 hours of standby time. It costs 75 dollars and is sold here .
