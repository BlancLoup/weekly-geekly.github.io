Overview of foreign and Russian domain name registrars. Comparing marketing tricks with us and with them

The following discussion focuses on classic .ru / .com zones and not quite ordinary .xyz, .club, .today, .life, .world, .online, .tech, .store, as well as registrars, who are lured by low prices and As a result, users get big money on the extension of these zones.
