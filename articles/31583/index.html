Donkey in the well

One day a donkey from a farmer fell into a well. The animal cried plaintively for several hours while the farmer tried to figure out how to pull it out. In the end, he decided that the donkey was already so old that it was necessary to fill the well and so that the donkey was not worth the effort that would be needed to help him.
So he called all his neighbors to help him. They all took up shovels and began to fill the well with mud. The donkey realized what was going on and started screaming awfully. After that, surprising everyone with this, he fell silent.

Soon enough, the donkey was able to get out of the well on its own, although no one expected such a result.
