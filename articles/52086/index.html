Effective use of browser bookmarks - action orientation

I apologize in advance for a certain amount of water in the introduction - I want to first explain the motives .

Recently, I once again discovered the sad fact: almost all carefully collected bookmarks in the browser are dead weight. The fact that they are ideally sorted by category does not automatically mean that they will be used once. Although among them there are many very useful and important links.

Already several times there was a desire to delete all bookmarks and use only search engines. But every time I kept from such a radical decision, because, in fact, it is not always easy to find once once found a useful resource. On the other hand, it turns out that the efficiency of the entire collection is not more than one percent.

It turns out that every day I spend time searching for useful materials, which then simply disappear into the black hole of the bookmarks storage.
