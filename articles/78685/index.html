Penumbra / Penumbra 2

A first-person action game that uses the Cutting Edge technology to immerse the player more into its dark world. The physics engine manipulates objects directly with a mouse, enhancing the horrific atmosphere ... so if you hear something outside the door, you put your arm-cursor in there and push it towards you, opening the door. A frontal fight is the last hope for salvation in a battle against creatures that you can meet in Penumbra: they work in groups and profitably use game physics, surpassing the player in this. It will scare the hell out of you!

Galatea

Before us is a non-traditional representative of the games of the genre Interactive Fiction . Galatea is an extremely interesting and complex character (according to legend - a revived statue). Her answers vary with mood (which, in turn, changes with your questions about her). In this game there is no other purpose than to study the mind of a living statue, but this is a fascinating activity that abounds in pathos, existentialism and mythology.

Free
Cross-platform (over browser, java-powered).


Little Fighter 2 & Naruto The Setting Dawn

The Chinese two-dimensional fighting game Little Fighter 2 bribed me with simple controls and all the riot of the bloodless cartoon fight that occurs on the screen between twenty fighters. And the support of Hot-Seat multiplayer was very useful when guests were visiting me :) Naruto The Setting Dawn is a smart addon that changes the game beyond recognition. All characters of the original are replaced, the game mechanics have changed, the abilities of the fighters have become stronger, and the gameplay of the game is generally faster. And do not be fans of the series Naruto, to comfortably play this mod.

Free
Windows
