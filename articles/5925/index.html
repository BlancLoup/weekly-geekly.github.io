OpenOffice.org 2.2.0

OpenOffice.org is one of the largest open-source projects whose goal is to create a free and yet full-fledged office suite that is not inferior in capabilities of Microsoft Office. At the moment, there are 6 main applications in the package:
  1. Writer . Word processor and visual HTML editor. Analogue of MS Word.
  2. Calc . Spreadsheet, analog MS Excel.
  3. Draw . Vector graphics editor. It has a number of features similar to CorelDRAW.
  4. Impress . Presentation program. Analogue MS Powerpoint.
  5. Base . The mechanism of connecting to external DBMS and built-in DBMS HSQLDB. Analog MS Access.
  6. Math Math formula editor like MS Equation Editor.
For users accustomed to MS Office, OpenOffice.org may not seem too fast, a bit unusual in terms of the interface and the implementation of certain functions. All this is more than offset by the absolute free package and the presence of almost all the functions necessary for everyday work.
