The first office of the developer: in which city?

First business is like first love. And the first office is like the first kiss. And every, well, or almost every novice businessman wants a good office for himself: at least, comfortable, and often pontovy :) And of course, I really want him to be as close as possible to home: ‚Äúwell, now I will not stand in traffic jams!
But, unfortunately, a small business is like a small child. He must be looked after, he must be fed, and there can be no talk of any ‚ÄúPonts‚Äù in the early stages. We are important performance, payback and flexibility in making decisions :) Therefore, no matter how much I want the office closer to home, after a brief analysis, I came to the conclusion that the office in Moscow is neither efficient nor flexible, and in terms of payback would have to try.
Therefore, below I will give the results of the analysis on the cost of opening an office in 10 cities of Russia. The results will be summarized on a specific case: a software firm consisting of 5 developers.
