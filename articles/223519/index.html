Getting ready for 2014 Zabbix conference

Zabbix Conference 2014 is rapidly gaining new members and invites you to join!

Only 3.5 months are left before the start of the 4th annual Zabbix conference dedicated to scalability and high performance monitoring!



According to the already established tradition, this year the Zabbix conference will take place on September 12-13, in Riga (Latvia) -
The city, referred to as the "Pearl of the Baltic" and is the cultural capital of Europe in 2014.

Every year, advanced IT experts and professionals come to the conference, as well as experts, users and followers of Zabbix , one of the most popular programs for monitoring with open source. Zabbix conference is the largest event of Zabbix company, which is held at the international level, bringing together IT professionals, developers, as well as representatives of the largest financial, aviation, educational, governmental, health care and other organizations from all over the world to share experiences, establish new relationships and the acquisition of new knowledge.

Official conference program





The official program of the Zabbix conference will last 2 days. During these days, you will hear and see presentations and technical demo of 20 professional speakers from around the world, as well as be able to participate in discussions about monitoring high performance, scalability, performance tuning, distributed monitoring, large-scale installations and other relevant issues.

Time to communicate with each other and exchange experiences





Given the rich program of the conference and the continuous flow of new information during the official part of the event, we offer our guests to join us during the informal / entertaining program of the conference, where everyone will have the opportunity to discuss topics and issues of interest in a relaxed atmosphere. You will be able to communicate with each other, share your experience about the conference being held, and also share your impressions of the presentations you hear and see during:

  • BBQ Welcoming Party - Oncoming evening-BBQ for our arriving guests, which will be held the day (September 11) before the official part of the conference.
  • Zabbix Conference Party - A party in honor of the Zabbix conference to which all participants of the conference, without exception, are invited at the end of the 1st day of the conference, and where pleasant live music, traditional competitions from the Zabbix team, as well as pleasant surprises are expected.
  • Post-Conference Evening - An evening tour of historical places in Latvia, namely a tour with an English-speaking guide through the Secret Soviet Bunker - one of the most strategically important places in Soviet Latvia. The evening will end with a themed hot dinner in the "Soviet style".

Only until May 22 is a favorable price for registration!


Only until May 22, you have the unique opportunity to register at the Zabbix conference at very favorable discount prices. Register now and become one of our respected Zabbix 2014 conference participants.

We are waiting for you, your colleagues, friends and associates in Riga on September 12-13!
