Transcend T.Sonic 320 (8Gb) For music only


Today I want to talk about the player from a well-known manufacturer of flash drives, hard drives, modules and memory cards - Transcend.
Firstly, this company has earned its trust as a manufacturer of goods for data storage, which means that you should not worry about the safety of music.
And secondly, the price of the player is good, because It is the same as that of flash drives of this volume.

And if there is no difference, why get less?

So, to begin with, I decided to choose this particular player.
Purpose : Buy the player only to create music from the radio.
Desired options:
  • > 8 Gb
  • Screen
  • Radio
  • Dictaphone
  • Work time> 12 hours
  • Easy computer connection

Budget: 1500-2000 p.
In general, as a result of such a strict sampling, many players, including iPod and Cowon ( their review ), were dropped out of me, and, as a result, having excluded from the search also all devices with unknown to me, and the whole world, I think, with the names The choice fell on these players:

But, after deliberation, the first two had to be abandoned due to the rather large size, and the screen for watching the video was not needed. Thus, there was only Transcend, which I purchased, and, I will tell you, I was very pleased and will now tell you why.

He pleased me at once with several things:
  1. Built-in USB, which allows not to carry with them the wires.
  2. The presence of all the parameters described earlier, but about them in more detail later.
  3. Ability to use as a USB flash drive (see point 1)
  4. Simple navigation menu.

For such sizes and prices, I believe, perfect.

So after all, is it a player?


Yes, yes, and yes again! This is a real player. And it works just as well as its great fellows, and in some places even better (for example, there is no voice recorder and radio in the iPod) This is exactly the player. Not a media center, but a player. If you do not need to play on the road or read books from the screen of your ‚Äúlittle animal‚Äù, and you just want to enjoy the music - this is what you need.

About the sound: the sound is decent, quality. Of course, Apple and Cowon are far away, but Transcend walks quite confidently. Noise was observed, the bass is playing. There are built-in EQ modes, which are quite suitable for an inexperienced user.
On the recording thereof: you can record about 90 hours of chatter on the recorder. (It is enough to record lectures for the entire semester.) A bit of noise still records, but not critical. Reproduces pretty clean.
About radio: standard receiver interface. Wave and the ability to choose from the recorded stations. In total, you can record 20 stations. Pleased that you can record the track from the radio in the player's memory.
