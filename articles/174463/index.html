Webmaster Academy is entering the international arena

Level of training webmaster: any

Since we launched the Webmaster Academy in English back in May 2012, over a million webmasters of various levels of training have benefited from it.

Webmaster Academy was created to help webmasters create great websites that appear regularly in Google search results. The Academy presents topics for beginners, such as ways to display and analyze your own content and the main functions of Webmaster Tools , as well as resources for experienced webmasters to maximize the benefits of your online business.



We are committed to supporting webmasters around the world and are pleased to announce that today we are launching the Webmaster Academy in 20 languages. You can get to the Academy via Google webmaster .

We will be very glad to know your opinion and invite you to join the discussion on our webmaster forum .
