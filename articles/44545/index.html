The face of Volgograd on the Internet or how competitions are held

The city of Volgograd has an official website: www.volgadmin.ru
This site is the face of the city on the web, solves serious information problems. The site contains a huge amount of information.

The site design should embody the great historical past of the city, show its life and prospects, be convenient for the citizens and for the city administration.
Site design is important for citizens.

At the beginning of autumn 2008, the administration of Volgograd announced a competition for the best new design of the informational portal of Volgograd volgadmin.ru.

It would seem that such a serious question should be discussed with the public of the city, the problems that the new design of the site is intended to solve should be identified and formulated. There was no public discussion on this topic.

The competition was not conducted through the system of public procurement or municipal order, that is, there were no formal criteria and a transparent selection procedure. Who and how selects? Unclear.

The composition of the jury was again announced, which in general is nonsense for such events.
The official announcement of the competition and its conditions can be found here: www.volgadmin.ru/ru2/Information/Claims.aspx?ids=8

Among other things, the announcement says that:
1st prize - 18 thousand rubles
The prize winner, who is awarded the 1st prize, also gets the right
to conclude an agreement with MU "GIC" (city information center) for the development of html-templates for the main and thematic pages of the new version of the portal.

It should be understood that the price of a municipal contract for the development of a portal design will not be at all 18 thousand rubles, but tens and hundreds of times more.

What is MU GIZ? This is a municipal institution, which since 1999 has been coordinating the entire information policy of the city.

In the middle of 2008, its permanent director of the State Educational Center, Elena Mikhailovich Ogol, was dismissed from the post by the head of the city, and a certain Folomeev, Yury Anatolyevich, was appointed to replace her.
