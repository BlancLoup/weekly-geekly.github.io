Calmly.Ru - a year later



In June 2009, our service for the selection of auto-instructors for the first time declared itself to the community , received a lot of useful comments that convinced us that we were on the right path, largely due to them defining our further development.

Those few of you, who during this time our resource was useful for personal purposes, may be interested to know about the financial results of our project, and about the difficulties that we have encountered. For the rest, it may help not to step on a rake, inventing a niche for such a business, without studying the mentality of the ‚Äúwards‚Äù. After all, contact with the audience is very important for a young startup.

So why are we still alive? Carefully, under the cut is really great text.


Problems


1) Low site traffic, as a result, all auto-instructors posted on our site receive few calls



Since the launch, we have grown to 2,000 unique visitors per day, providing about 9,000 hits per day. At the same time, according to Google Analytics, the return percentage is only 30%, which is understandable - it makes sense to use our resource 1-2 times in life, it does not count on a permanent audience. This could be an advantage, for example, in a dialogue with advertisers, but so far we have only negative experience, more details below.

You can speculate on the fact that one of our instructors "did not come out with a mug", tritely uploaded not his most successful photo (and we haven‚Äôt helped him in this yet), set too high a price for training, or just in his area a lot of competition, but in fact, from month to month, for almost half a year, we have already formed a limited number of instructor questionnaires, which is not growing, but which receives calls, and, accordingly, pays us for its placement.

Nevertheless, every day at least 1 new instructor is registered, and only a part of them after passing the free trial period of 1.5 months remain with us on a fee basis. The rest fall off for various reasons.

We spent a lot of effort, now we know what percentage of users actually make calls, found a clear correlation with attendance, a lot of analytical work was done for this, many reports were built, the center worked from morning to night without working on the pipe. As a result, we found out that by slightly increasing the attendance it is possible to provide at least 1 guaranteed call per month to an instructor. And for them it is a criterion of payback and usefulness of our resource, since students take an average of 5 lessons.

The cost of SEO over this period has only grown, and if earlier we were counting on the appearance of natural links, then, by observing the emergence of links of such a plan ‚ÄúI found a great instructor myself here, ‚Äù we quickly parted those hopes.

2) Stereotypes


When we first started, we had a lot of unpleasant conversations with competing sites. On the basis of the fact that we borrowed information about the autoinstructors from their sites, of course, calling around the instructors and asking for permission (remember, personal information about a particular person belongs only to that person). This was perceived, to put it mildly, in hostility, there were provocations and other attempts to deceive naive auto-instructors who were not familiar with the rules of the Network.

What is the result: some of these sites began to cooperate with us, taking the initiative. Among them appeared ‚Äúintermediaries‚Äù, people who collected money from other instructors who were not friends with the Internet, and placed it on our website with a certain discount depending on the number of ‚Äúgiven‚Äù instructors. Everyone benefits, in principle.

Despite this, the majority of instructors never got used to the idea that one site with all instructors is better than 100 personal pages on narod.ru. Some of them, in principle, do not believe in online advertising, they have enough students to fall for at the expense of word of mouth, work in a driving school, someone generally perceives all this in his own way, and rushes to make his personal website. We manage to preserve the technological advantage, so the 3 clone sites that have appeared during this period (already with the trained instructors from our site!) Have so far not achieved anything.

3) Low price


We realized that we rested on the bar - somehow not raising the price, not increasing the number of satisfied instructors, not associating with advertising or cooperation with driving schools that we dreamed of, we could not count on an increase in income.

It is clear that just to raise the price we at least do not allow conscience. I wanted to launch new services, announce a new price only for newly connecting instructors, and under this simple cover make a migration of the population to a new, profitable for us, tariff.

In more detail about our attempts to earn we read in the next section.

After all, we promised a functionality similar to autobeginner.ru at the time of launch. But first it was necessary to work the soil.

A rating of driving schools was made, with which it would make sense to communicate, there were attempts to communicate by phone, e-mail, even through Odnoklassniki directly with the director of the Central Driving School. All attempts to contact - both on the wall. It is time for a personal visit, to break through with the fight, so to speak. Here your humble servant felt all the work of a traveling salesman. We prepared a presentation on the disk, which we expected to leave to the secretary as a last resort if we could not see the persons in charge. Financial results


Considering what we had, and we still have a fairly loyal investor to us, we are still alive. If the studio, the product of which is our project spokoino.ru, would not have been engaged in parallel work, it would have nothing to eat.
