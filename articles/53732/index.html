New version of the client video conferencing service VideoPort online!

The VideoPort team is pleased to present the long-awaited videoservice client release VideoPort online! version 5.5 . Now our clients have even more opportunities for joint group work and communications.



full size screenshot

The main novelty is the joint viewing of slides (slide shows) in a group of video conferencing of any type. If earlier this service was available only in the one-on-one video call mode, now the restrictions have been removed. Who can benefit from this service? First of all, companies that conduct remote presentations. Now you can easily and cost-effectively conduct a group presentation at the same time in several places, showing slides, commenting on them and immediately answering questions. It will be equally useful for marketing research when working with focus groups. Now you do not need to bring people to test any visual image for different regions. You can simultaneously display many pictures and watch the reaction.

The second innovation in the client video service VideoPort online! is full dual monitor support. You can scatter the pictures from your interlocutors on two monitors or, by connecting a projector, control what will be displayed on it.

The bandwidth for the audio stream is also significantly increased. This has improved the quality of the transmitted sound.

All innovations will be immediately added to the VideoPort SBS Plus and VideoPort Enterprise server conferencing products . Organizing videoconferencing within an enterprise becomes an even simpler and less expensive task. The reduction in prices for the base product VideoPort SBS has already attracted the close attention of small business, since for the cost of annual service in the video service VideoPort online! The company gets its own video server.

From the beginning of the year, we offer to test any VideoPort SBS video server for free for two weeks. You can leave the application for the test version on the website of the company videoport.ru .

Plans for the following releases have already been approved:
  • Improving the quality of echo cancellation;
  • Increased options for group video conferencing;
  • Update wizard to create group conferences;
  • Screening - a demonstration of the desktop;
  • Mobile version of the client for the Windows Mobile platform.


VideoPort online! - simple and affordable video conferencing service over the real Internet, which does not require high-speed dedicated channels and powerful computers. The basic account is absolutely free and includes an unlimited number of one-to-one video calls. A webcam, microphone and headphones are all you need to start using video conferencing for your business. Better to see once than hear a hundred times!
