Geode - a dream carders or a new word in the use of credit cards?

Recently, a curious project appeared on the kickstarter that collected the necessary amount to launch just a couple of days.

We are talking about an electronic organizer for your credit and discount cards.

The device is called "Geode" and is a case for the iPhone, which includes a card scanner, a rewritable card and an e-ink display to display bar codes.

The point is simple - you scan all your credit cards, discount and membership cards and leave them at home. When you come to the store and you need your credit card, you use the iPhone app to copy the data of the necessary card to a rewritable one (stored in the same case-cover) and with the help of this clone card you are already paying.

Below, in the video presentation, everything is clearly shown:



The idea, of course, is excellent. No need to carry a pack of cards ...

In addition, if there is a barcode on the card, then in this case a high-contrast e-ink display is provided in the device to display it (apparently barcode scanners cannot read the barcode from the screen of the iPhone itself).

And all this is encrypted by fingerprint.



The project fell in love with the audience. To launch, the author wanted to collect $ 50,000. At the moment, 272 thousand have already been collected (in order to get the device from the first batch, you must donate at least $ 159).

It looks great, but how will it all work in practice?
