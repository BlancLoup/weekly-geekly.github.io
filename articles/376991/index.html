Xiaomi introduced an extreme camera for $ 64: GoPro Inc. shares collapsed by 4.9%

The world's third smartphone maker Xiaomi presented a wearable video camera for extreme sports Yi Action Camera, which costs $ 64 and surpasses GoPro Hero in some characteristics. The capitalization of the manufacturer GoPro decreased by 4.9 percent.



Yi Action Camera shoots video at 1080p (60 frames per second), equipped with a 16 megapixel camera with Sony's Exmor R BSI CMOS image sensor. GoPro Hero shoots at the same resolution of 30 frames per second, or at 720p - 60 frames using a 5 megapixel camera. Xiaomi also beats a competitor in the built-in memory - 64 GB against 32 in Hero - and in weight: Yi weighs 72 grams against 111 grams in Hero. The camera from Xiaomi in combination with a waterproof cover can be used at a depth of 40 meters.

You can control the gadget using the application on your smartphone.
