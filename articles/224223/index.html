Before continuing, I will explain a little why we need a separate class for the note and for the star. The melody can repeat its notes, and each star should be in a single copy. When I first thought up the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe game, I just kept every note inside the star. As a result, either the melody was too simple, or there were too many stars in the sky and it was difficult to complete the level with even eight repetitive notes.
