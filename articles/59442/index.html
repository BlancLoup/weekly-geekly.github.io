Stop WEB-SPAM!


Spamming on forums, blogs, comments, guestbooks, even on social networks, he made his way everywhere, this is no longer a problem of one email. And the very methods of protection against spam often complicate the lives of your visitors, agree that it is inconvenient to enter hard-to-find letters from the image, and it also takes more time ...

Although there are less painful ways, for example, StopForumSpam.com offers an API for checking IP and email for the presence of spam bots in its own database. But none of the above methods is aimed at combating the cause of spam, namely, the extraction of benefits - customer acquisition.
