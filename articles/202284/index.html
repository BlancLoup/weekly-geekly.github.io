A brief overview of the console market



While reading Habr, I was perplexed to notice that Habrovchan was little aware of the console world, sometimes in the comments there are serious inaccuracies, and no one is in a hurry to correct them. I have no right to comment, so I am writing a brief overview of the market, especially relevant in connection with the upcoming arrival of a new generation of consoles. The article will be an excellent addition to the recent history of their development , but will describe only the current situation.

The market for PC games will only touch in passing. Just ignore portable consoles, this is a topic for another conversation.

The article is for informational purposes only. Any subjective comments are hidden under the cath and are optional for reading.


Introduction


The transition from generation to generation in the world of consoles has always been a ‚Äútechnology race‚Äù. Beginning on cartridge systems, where the concept of 16 bits was much cooler than 8 bits, it turned into high-tech developments, culminating in the most complex Cell processor for PS3.

The console turned out to be very powerful (you can see its capabilities in peak condition in projects such as Last of Us and Beyond: Two Souls ), but the price was the complexity of developing for it and the cost of production. As a result, many multi-platform projects on the Microsoft console look even better.

The latter made a bet on services in the current generation, on the online, their work with the community at the beginning of the Xbox 360 life cycle was beyond praise, and Sony was in the role of overtaking the entire current generation. Moreover, the Xbox has already managed to gain a foothold in the market, having come out a year or more earlier. The Microsoft console was created balanced, fairly simple to develop, and only a huge percentage of defects in the first installment (according to some sources up to 60%) overshadowed its output.

Nintendo's Wii was shown at E3 2005 , and it suddenly ‚Äúshot‚Äù. More precisely, everyone knew that the console would have a remote in the kit, allowing it to track its movements, but no one imagined that this idea would be so popular with users. Queues to the stands with the Wii did not decrease until the end of the exhibition. At a cost of $ 250, the console was disassembled at the start as hot cakes, not paying attention to the low power of its iron.

For its new console, Sony radically changed its priorities. The console from an exclusive development turned into a modified PC with a unique set of functions. The bet is made on online services and socialization. PS Vita, Vita TV, Gaikai , Nashe and Torne (in Japan) will ideally create a holistic PlayStation universe covering all end-user needs.

Microsoft continued the already proven policy (to wrest leadership from the invulnerable Sony for several years is a great achievement), forcing an additional direction on TV and video services. Nintendo made a bet on the uniqueness of its console, which worked perfectly with the Wii . Their console sold better on both the PS3 and Xbox 360, thanks in large part to capturing completely new players, mothers and grandparents. It was very popular among children. Wii U, their new console, has a built-in joystick screen, and also offers a unique gameplay.

A bit of history and what can we expect


Microsoft today is in a unique situation. The policy they sounded at E3 2013 caused a sharp rejection of the majority of players. Compulsory internet connection once a day, the inability to freely give the purchased disk to play to a friend, Kinekt , without which the console will not even work and the cost is $ 500 ... a squall of criticism fell upon the company.

The system proposed by the company reminded many people of Steam, it is clear that Microsoft was planning to take a step towards fully digital distribution of games, but hurried. Hastened and took away the players' choice, only playing into the hands of the main competitor.

Sony, behind them, frustrated the hall‚Äôs applause , simply telling them that they would have no such restrictions (video) .

Less than a month later, the Redmond fighters made a 180-degree turn , canceling all restrictions and returning to the usual system, at the same time destroying all the advantages that the new system promised. Unfortunately, the company is very closed for both journalists and ordinary users, and there is little news from them.

The rest of the Xbox One is following in the footsteps of the Xbox 360. Their most successful console still surpasses the PS3 in the US and the UK with a large margin, although it is lagging behind in Europe and the rest of the world.

From interesting facts: Microsoft in January of this year showed a very promising technology IllumiRoom



But representatives of the company said that this technology will be too expensive at the moment. Very, very sorry.

Sony entered the latest race (the decision to add to the GPU console was made almost at the last moment, which delayed the development), giving competitors a huge head start, which only Microsoft could play in the middle of this year. The company did not want to repeat such a story, because the PS3 hit their pockets very painfully, with each console sold they lost more than $ 200. And this is at a price of $ 600 for the top model, and $ 500 for the budget. Therefore, the policy of creating a unique platform that worked well with PS One and PS2 was revised. Times have changed.

When creating a new PlayStation, the company focused primarily on players and developers. Focus groups were created, surveys and many tests were conducted. This practice was very different from the closed, top-secret development of the PS3 with Cell inside. PS4 chief architect Mark Cernie talked about this in much more detail; you can watch his amazing performance in the video below.



Although Sony will continue to lose money on each console, these losses do not compare with the losses caused by the PS3. The company will make a profit if the player acquires at least 1-2 games, or subscribes to PS Plus. Microsoft will sell the console immediately at a profit that previously only Nintendo managed before the release of 3DS.

In addition, Sony said that the pre-orders for the PS4 are much higher than those of previous generations, and have now reached one and a half million consoles. Microsoft gives only approximate figures, but also encouraging: pre-orders are twice as high as they were on the Xbox 360.

Nintendo is in its position. Its iron does not cause enthusiasm for its power, although it is convenient to work with it. But their strength is in the interior studios.

If you look at Sony and Microsoft sales tops, you will see Call of Duty, FIFA, Battlefield and the like in the first places. In the top ten exclusives occupy only three, four positions. In Nintendo, exclusives occupy almost all the lines. They sell the console.

However, they are also trying to improve relations with third-party developers. According to igrodelov themselves, Nintendo is the second most open company for them.

Unfortunately, the current sales of their new console can not be called high. Perhaps the current situation with the Wii U was partly due to 3DS. Favorite games just came out on a portable console, and then a year and a half later there were already new parts, and you also had to buy a separate console. Due to unsuccessful marketing, many Wii owners did not even realize that a new console had been released. A joystick with a large touch screen was taken as an additional peripheral for the old console.

However, leading industry experts have repeatedly warned that Nintendo should not be underestimated. They unexpectedly fired off the Wii, but after the failure of the GameCube, they were not even considered to be their competitors. The company can easily pull out the Wii U by expanding the user base with its own projects, and making the console attractive for large publishers.

A few words about indie

Digital distribution, and how it has changed the previously unshakable world of consoles


Starting with the current generation, it became possible to distribute games over the network. And this seemingly simple fact changes almost everything.

For the first time in history, consoles opened for small projects, which gave rise to the ‚Äúindie‚Äù project boom observed today. A small game, worth a couple of dollars, will only be released on the disc and distributed through stores. Download versions have completely solved this problem.

However, for platform holders, this turned out to be a far from positive transition, breaking the established system for developing and distributing games.

First Valve responded - a small gaming company, not the wise mega-corporations. The success of Steam is no longer discussed by anyone.

At that time, they came to a completely empty market first and provided high-quality service, having received deserved recognition. Digital distribution on the PC was developed before, but it was very chaotic, the high level of piracy and the inconvenience of acquiring games greatly complicated the life of gaming studios.

The recent announcement of SteamOS - a further step in this direction.

GooglePlay is rapidly gaining momentum and even overtaken by the number of applications downloaded by the AppStore, according to the latest information.

Console giants were much less agile. With a delay of several years, Microsoft began to move in the digital direction, soon followed by Sony. Only recently, prospects understood Nintendo.
