As the post rises


Suppose you have already written a post (this is a real feat, some gather for two years). After that, the first thing you need is to get through the selection filter, that is, to get through to the main one. That is, get a rating of +5 minimum. This is one of the most difficult parts for a new author, because you don't have any subscribers yet.

But you go to RSS and other subscriptions of those people who read the hubs, where you put your post. They vote and take you home, where the post is already much better seen by all readers. The first voices are quite aggressive: two factors play here. First, early readers are usually quite experienced Habr users, and therefore actively minus what weakens their position (your competitors), do not like personally, does not correspond to the level of the site (remembered at the time of registration) and so on. The second factor is a banal childish desire to see the effect. The minus immediately after the publication of the material clearly gives a good noticeable effect, and a minus a post with a rating of +200 does nothing at all. Therefore, the ratio of positive and negative votes after going to the main will be different, and a little more in your favor - a wide audience is more peaceful.

The next source of readings is the best rating per day. The higher the post rises, the more people will see it. Better to be in the top ten. Social proof works here - even if the reader does not like the headline, then being on the top of the top says that there is something interesting inside and you should not miss it.
