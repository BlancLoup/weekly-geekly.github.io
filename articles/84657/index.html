SkypeIN is now in Russia

Now on the resource www.skypeinrus.ru , which belongs to the Russian telecommunications provider Telfin , you can register a direct Russian city number for your skype account, which subscribers who call will get directly to your skype conversation.

The price list for direct skype numbers from the Telfin company:
  • Moscow (495) - 850 rubles per month.
  • Moscow (499) - 350 rubles per month.
  • St. Petersburg (812) - 350 rubles per month.
  • Nizhny Novgorod (831) - 350 rubles per month.

Let me remind you that you cannot officially register a Russian number for the SkypeIN service on skype.com.

Thus, all those deprived of MGTS people, can now easily get a direct city number, receive calls to it for free and make outgoing calls at SkypeOUT tariffs.

Also, having paid the package "Unlimited Plan Evropa" unlimited outgoing calls to stationary numbers in Moscow and St. Petersburg will cost you 235 rubles / month + 350 rubles / month for Skype number. Total 585 rubles / month Telfin vs. 405 rubles / month IGAS.

An outgoing call to a mobile number in Russia via skype will cost you 2.6 rubles per minute.

PS Just do not forget that Skype is not a replacement for a regular phone, and therefore it cannot be used to make an emergency call.
