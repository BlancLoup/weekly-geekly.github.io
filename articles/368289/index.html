Mercedes-Benz showed his vision of unmanned vehicles

Mercedes-Benz is not the first automaker to undertake experiments with self-driving cars, but does it stylishly at the Consumer Electronic Show 2015 . Meet the F015 Luxury in Motion.



Spy photos of this concept appeared in October last year.







The concept of a car is designed in such a way as to distract passengers from the road as much as possible, because they will sit opposite each other, face to face, whether they are working while working - all in order to save billions of dollars .

The length of the electric vehicle is 17 feet, height - 5 feet. The car is equipped with 26-inch wheels located as far as possible from the passenger compartment in order to increase its volume - for the convenience of passengers. The seats automatically rotate 30 degrees when the doors open. There are six touch-sensitive displays on the doors inside the cabin that allow you to control them with the help of touches, gestures or eye movements.

F015 Luxury in Motion can be used as an unmanned vehicle or driven by a driver. While the autopilot is working, LED headlights can project a zebra so that a pedestrian can cross the road.
