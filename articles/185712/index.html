Happy Farm Demo Day: happy harvest!

Dear friends!

We invite you to take part in Happy Farm Demo Day , which will be held in our business incubator on July 20. Beginning at 15:00.



As part of DemoDay, HappyFarm resident startups (24 teams) will present projects to investors. Also in the program - startup graduation and first birthday of our Happy Farm!

Format: 3-minute start-up pitch (24 companies), presentation of partners and investors, informal communication.
Happy Farm Demo Day is a happy opportunity:
For investors - to learn about the most promising IT startups in the CIS - from first hand!
For startups - learn about unique opportunities for the development of your business within the HappyFarm incubator and all the details about the third cycle, which starts in September!
For journalists , talk with HappyFarm members of the Board in the stars of entrepreneurship and venture business of Silicon Valley (Igor Shoifot, Igor Ryabenky, Oded Hermoni, John Nordmark), with investors and startups in Ukraine, Russia, Belarus and other CIS countries.
Participants of the event: representatives of international venture funds, investors, business angels, representatives of business incubators in Ukraine and the CIS, startups, journalists, bloggers.

Venue: c. Happy (7 km from Kiev in the direction of Borispol), st. Lesi Ukrainki, 14, HappyFarm Business Incubator. Getting there: http://happyfarm.com.ua/rus/bi/contacts/
Registration is required. Limited number of seats. Please register here: http://happyfarmdemoday.eventbrite.com/

Contacts:
Orgoprosy: Ekaterina Seledets k.seledets@happyfarm.com.ua
For media: Danuta Pisarenko dana@happyfarm.com.ua

See you in Happy!
