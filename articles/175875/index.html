Application Competition on open police data

Friends!

As many, for sure, we remember every year we hold an Apps4Russia contest with prizes, partners and cool applications that people make on open data and not only - this year we, too, will surely hold it, the announcement will be wide and big just about as soon . But I will not get ahead - everything will be.

However, apart from our large project of competition, we are engaged in other interesting and large projects, one of which is the project "Open Police" ( http://www.openpolice.ru )

And, within the framework of this project, we are holding another contest - for those who cannot wait to immediately follow the link - www.openpolice.ru/contest . Small in time - only 2 months, different in content - we have a lot of open data collected here - data.openpolice.ru and available via API - api.openpolice.ru



But we already managed to collect such data:
- law enforcement statistics to the subjects of the federation;
- base of district police officers;
- All police stations and traffic police;
- budget of the Ministry of Interior;
- information about the income of the employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs;
- All MIA government contracts;
- all government contracts that _wink_ structures subordinate to the Ministry of Internal Affairs;
and much more.

Plus, of course, we collect ideas. If you do not know how to program, and want to benefit humanity in another way, then you can offer an idea to those who are ready to make applications.
