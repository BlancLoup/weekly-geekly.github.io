How to attract a client to a website? Choose from 22 ways



Anyone who owns their own website or service is faced with the issue of attracting customers to their website. And if there are a lot of ways to attract people on the Internet, and there are plenty of ways to work with them, then there is practically no information on how to choose only effective methods for your business so as not to get scattered. Let's try to answer this burning question.

We have selected 22 ways to attract customers to your website / service. Each method has its own criteria, they are listed under the description. You need to select three or four ways to attract customers, the most suitable for you. Why three or four? Most of the methods in our list are not one-time, but require constant attention and work. As long as the process of attracting customers through a certain channel is not put on stream, it will be difficult and expensive to use more different channels.

Selection criteria:

Type of purchase.
Extensive . A purchase where a person has already defined any criteria, but he needs additional information to make a decision. Such a purchase, as a rule, is carried out through cold settlement, without emotion. More often they buy building materials, apartments, plastic windows, SEO, etc.
Impulsive . Saw - wanted - bought. A purchase that is made impulsively. The buyer is guided solely by emotions. More often they buy phones, shirts, flowers in beautiful pots, cakes, but hardly an apartment, car or SEO-promotion service. Impulse buying is extremely rare in the b2b market.
