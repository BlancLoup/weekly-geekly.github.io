Away is good, but XIAOMI is better: useful new items for your home

XIAOMI Mi Smart Temperature and Humidity Sensor - temperature and humidity sensor


Literally in April, XIAOMI expanded its family of home accessories with a new XIAOMI Mi Temperature temperature and humidity sensor. This baby determines the temperature with an accuracy of 0.3 ¬∞ C, the error in measuring humidity is 3%.



The device works at temperatures from -20 ¬∞ C to +60 ¬∞ C - we understand that circumstances are different, but it is unlikely that your home will be hotter or colder (unless, of course, you decide to turn everything into a huge freezer). With a weight of only 15 g, this is one of the most compact and yet multifunctional solutions.

XIAOMI Mi Temperature will be an excellent addition to your smart home system: for example, it automatically starts a humidifier if it detects insufficient humidity (too dry air), as well as air conditioning - you yourself set the optimal value.



The sensor sends all the data on temperature and humidity to the proprietary Mi application, so if it detects a serious deviation from normal values, it will warn the owner using a smartphone. This will allow you to quickly localize a small fire or flooding.



If you already have an active smart home system or you just want to create one, XIAOMI Mi Temperature is a real must have. Compact, versatile and reliable, GearBest is also available at a discount .

XIAOMI Smart Mi Air Purifier - to keep it clean


The second version of the air purifier from XIAOMI with reduced dimensions, which did not affect the quality of air purification. It looks interesting and presentable, the design is minimalistic, the suction holes are located at the bottom, and the fan is right on the lid. Dimensions - 520 mm (height) and 240 mm (width), the length of the power cord 1.8 meters.



Here (on top) you will find a button to turn the cleaner on and off and switch to one of three modes.



The characteristics of the device are impressive - every hour the cleaner conducts up to 330 cubic meters of air through itself, the recommended area for cleaning is from 23 to 39 square meters. XIAOMI Mi Air Purifier 2 is able to filter up to 99.7% of unhealthy particles with a diameter of less than 2.5 micrometers and up to 99.5% of particles with a diameter of 0.3 micrometer. As for large particles, 100 percent purification is claimed.

The cleaner, as we said, works in three modes - automatic, in which Mi Air Purifier 2 analyzes the air itself and decides whether to increase or decrease the fan speed, at night, when the fan is almost silent, and also user-defined, you set the speed for application help.



The device cleans the air not only from small particles, but also from harmful gases in the room up to 46.6 square meters. The indicator on the front panel will allow you to understand the condition of the air: green (particle content is normal), yellow (the permissible level is slightly exceeded) and red (critical content of harmful particles). The cleaner also removes dust, odors after smoking and formaldehyde.

Inside, it looks even more interesting - two fans (and not one, as in the previous generation) and the filters themselves - activated carbon, HEPA, PET mesh.


Of the additional features - the timer automatically turn on, turn off the light indicator and sounds, protect against children and, most importantly, update the firmware with one button! And how it looks in the interior!



The XIAOMI Mi Air Purifier 2 is already available at GearBest at a very attractive price.

XIAOMI Yeelight LED Smart Bulb - there is not a lot of light


LED lamp controlled via Wi-Fi using an application for iPhone and iPad and Android devices. It does not require installation of any additional equipment - screwed into a cartridge (standard E27 base, used in many floor lamps), launched the application, and everything is ready! It is possible not only to turn on or off the light, but also to change the brightness of the lighting.



Lamp power is 8 W (very low power consumption), color temperature - 4000K, flux - 600 lumens. Despite such a small power, it is an excellent substitute not only for energy-saving light bulbs, but also for incandescent bulbs. Protection against overheating and overloading is foreseen, the lifetime is about 25,000 hours.



XIAOMI Yeelight LED belongs to the XIAOMI family of smart home devices and is controlled by ... yes, again, Mi Home. Integration with the smart home also allows you to set up automatic lamp shutdown when you leave home.


You can select a preset brightness value (for example, 50%) or customize your own. Warm light is ideal for reading or relaxing in front of the fireplace on a weekend day.



It is noteworthy that you can control the light bulb from the local network or via the Internet. You can also combine several of these lamps into a single network and manage everything at once using the application, which makes this solution very attractive for small cafes. About the house and do not say - the thing is simply irreplaceable. And if you think that you have nothing to surprise you with, look at the Yeelight LED page on GearBest .

XIAOMI Mi TV Box 3 Enhanced - 4K Set-Top Box


The TV set Mi TV Box 3 Enhanced, XIAOMI, introduced in early March, and the device immediately interested users with its characteristics. Incredibly compact console (only 100 x 100 x 25 mm) with a weight of 190 g. This does not prevent it from having an HDMI connector and two USB 2.0 ports.



Under the hood, this baby has a six-core 64-bit SoC MediaTek MT8693 processor (two Cortex-A72 2 GHz and four Cortex-A53 1.6 GHz). Plus 2 GB of RAM and 8 GB of Flash. Imagination PowerVR GX6250 is responsible for the graphics. It does not work on the latest Android (5.1), but with the MIUI shell. An active cooling system is an obvious plus.

AirPlay and Miracast support has not disappeared anywhere - you can play games on the big screen, and in a flash turn a cozy movie viewing into a full-fledged family movie show.

But that's not all - this console also has a remote control with a built-in gyroscope and an accelerometer to use the remote control as a game controller.
