More on WCG
In the past WCG from Russia there were several teams and players:
  • Counter-Strike 1.6: Virtus.pro, CMAX.gg (they are tp.uSports)
  • WarCraft 3 TFT: Happy, Naps
  • StarCRaft: ROX.KIS.BRAT_OK, ROX.KIS.Bruce, ROX.KIS.Advokate
