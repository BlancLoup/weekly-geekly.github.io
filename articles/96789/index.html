Black humor NeverDead



One of the main announcements at the Konami press conference was the third-person shooter NeverDead, developed by the studio Rebellion. Cinta Nojiri (Shinta Nojiri), the producer and chief designer of the Metal Gear Acid for PSP, acted as the producer and chief designer of the project.

The game is striking in its creativity. The main character, Bryce, loses parts of his body as he passes. The main feature of the gameplay lies precisely in this, because lost limbs can be repaired, rebuilt and secured anew to your body.

The venue of NeverDead is a mysterious city in the near future, where fantasy mixed with horror, and people wage endless war with bloodthirsty monsters. In the arsenal of Bryce, in addition to a variety of firearms, there will also be an impressive butterfly sword, which easily cuts the enemy in half. What can I say, the Japanese ...

Release date has not been disclosed. It is known only that the game will be released on the Xbox 360 and PS3.

In a short presentation video, the developers of NeverDead tried to show us everything: weapons, monsters, the city, and even severed legs. Fans of "funny horror" should certainly like it.
