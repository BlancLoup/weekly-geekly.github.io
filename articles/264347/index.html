The third PyCon Russia will be held in Yekaterinburg on September 18-19

The format of the conference remained the same: Pitonists leave for two days outside the city (30 km. From Ekaterinburg), during the day they listen to reports, participate in master classes, and in the evening have fun and relax in nature.

An important part of the conference is after-party, and this year we will do prepati: the participants, who arrived on the evening of September 17, will get to know and chat in an informal setting before the conference. At the very afterparty on September 18 there will be an evening bonfire with songs and chat, board games and entertainment from sponsors. In addition, we plan to make a barbecue on the street with pilaf, sausages and beer. We promise, this time there will not be so many mosquitoes :)

Participate in PyCon Russia

1. We are looking for speakers with interesting experience with Python. If you are one of them, fill out an application for a report, there are places in the program. Applications are accepted until August 20, after which the program committee will select the most useful ones.

2. If you want to advertise your company among pythonists, look at the sponsorship packages . We are always happy partners.

3. Come to participate! The site is already open registration . The cost of participation until September 1 - 9000 rubles.

The ticket price includes: participation in the conference, lunch 18.09, dinner 18.09, lunch 19.09, coffee breaks and afterparty, a bus from the center of Yekaterinburg to the venue and back. Paid separately: the place where you will live (for example, on the Oriole - from 2,700 for a double room per day with breakfast), and alcohol after coffee party.

PyCon Russia - 2015, September 18-19, Yekaterinburg, Oriole Training Center.
