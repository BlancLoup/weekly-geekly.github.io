C & C Russia. Competition "7 days - 7 keys"



Good afternoon, dear Habrovchane! So, what we have been waiting for has happened: the final version of the long-awaited C & C4: Tiberian Twilight has been released ! Everyone cheered, sales went up, and the Electronic Arts community manager, Aaron "EA.Apoc" Kaufman, pulled out 7 keys from his wide-legged trousers and gave them to us, the administration of the C & C Russia portal, so that we could hold contests. All for you, dear readers! ;)

Meet: weekly marathon of contests with a daily prize - a universal key, for all language versions of the game C & C4: Tiberian Twilight !

Every morning starting from Tuesday, at 7.00 am Moscow time, on our website C & C Russia there will be news that describes a detailed task for each contest, your task: to respond to this within 24 hours (until 7 am the next day) and send us your work. We will evaluate all candidates and announce the victory the day after the competition. For example, the results of the Tuesday competition we will announce on Wednesday evening (one of the competitions will probably take more time, more on that below), at 19.00 Moscow time!

  • Tuesday, day one, artistic: competition for the best caricature. Try yourself as the author of cartoons on the subject of C & C!
  • Wednesday, Day Two, Writing: Competition for the best review of any of the parts of C & C, your choice!
  • Thursday, third day, is also a writer: make a review for any fashion on any game of your choice!
  • Friday, fourth day, graphic: just send us the best screenshot of any of the C & C AND WE WILL AWARD YOU!
  • Saturday, fifth day, creative: try to come up with a better ending for the C & C universe.
  • Sunday, the sixth day, Zadrotsk: We are bringing to your attention the first tournament of the eSportZ-events line, which will be a competition for the 6th day of the marathon (the tournament will be held in RA3: Upheaval )
  • Monday, the seventh day, the audience: Competition for the best replay - the cherished key will get the author of the best replay posted on our website for all 7 days of the marathon! (Please refrain from mass replay until Wednesday, get ready, gentlemen!).


So, participate in our contests and win!
