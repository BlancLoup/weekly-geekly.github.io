Linux InstallFest 10.04 in Nizhny Novgorod


On May 3, a real Linux InstallFest will be held on Festive Monday in Nizhny Novgorod already 5 times. Every year, the Nizhny Novgorod Linux Users Group holds this event in a big way, thanks to the support of the Nizhny Novgorod RadioTechnical College.

This year the event will take place at the gymnasium of the NRTK. Everyone will be able to get a chair, power outlet and access to the network with a Ubuntu 10.04 repository and the Internet. If you have any questions during the installation and configuration - you will always be helped by consultants from the number of NNLUG participants, of which there will be much more this year.

For those who have not yet decided and just want to learn about Linux seminars will be held in the presentation area. You will be able to watch and polusat about the installation process and configure Linux, work with different environments. During the day there will be presentations of various free applications. This year we focus on schools and will talk a lot about using Linux in education. The more so, that the two-year experience of using Linux in the educational process of the Nizhny Novgorod RadioTechnical College guarantees the existence of interesting developments in this direction.

When you are ripe and go to try Linux at work - it's time to look into the demo zone where there will be computers ready for work with Ubuntu 10.04 installed and configured and Linux School 5.0. Here you can try everything you hear at seminars and ask questions to our consultants.

Well, for those who want to "pogamat" - the game zone will be open. Competitions in OpenArena, UrbanTerror and other exciting free GNU / Linuxcenter prizes are guaranteed. Start training today!

We are waiting for everyone on May 3
from 10:00 to 16:00
square Lyadov, stop Bus station
Nizhny Novgorod RadioTechnical College (Student st. 5)
