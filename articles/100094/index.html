Impressions of WCG Russia 2010

Today I managed to attend the second day of the All-Russian WCG final - the largest cybersport championship in Russia - and not just with the camera in hand.   • PC: Warcraft III, Starcraft, Counter Strike, FIFA 10, TrackMania, Carom3D

In general, we must pay tribute to the organizers - WCG, despite all its official nature and reality (athletes fought for quite material prizes and, of course, the right to enter the international Grand Final in Los Angeles in September of this year), the WCG looked first of all as a holiday where there was a place for everyone - both professionals, and fans, and just interested, sometimes taking the joystick in hands in the long winter evenings.

The most intense were the battles in FIFA, StarCraft, WarCraft, Point Blank and CS, because it was on them that the finals were held on the second day and it was these disciplines that participated in the draw for the Grand Final.
