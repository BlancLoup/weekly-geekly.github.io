If you have closed the browser, then the text you typed will not be in the memory dump of the newly opened browser. The browser can not be closed!

Briefly about the stages of recovery:

  1. Do not close the browser, open the task manager.
  2. Take a dump.
  3. Download and open Total Commander.
  4. Go to the directory (folder) in which you saved the dump, highlighting it by pressing F3.
  5. In the open window, switch to unicode (Options -> Unicode).
  6. Looking for your text.
