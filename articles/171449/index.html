Chrome Super Sync Sports Browser Game

Google recently launched a special browser game called Chrome Super Sync Sports . But it is different from those games that were known to us earlier.
The new service turns your mobile device into a gaming joystick, which allows you to manage games running in your Chrome browser on your computer. The game allows you to use the unique capabilities of mobile devices when playing on screens with any diagonal.



In order to use Chrome Super Sync Sports , you must follow the link chrome.com/supersyncsports on your computer, select the game and the desired mode (one or more players).

In addition, you need to download a mobile browser from Google to your smartphone. Then go to http://g.co/super . In order for the service to work, you need to connect to Wi-Fi.



Then sync your mobile device with your computer by clicking on the g.co/super link in Chrome browser on your smartphone or tablet and typing in a unique code specified on your computer screen.



After the synchronization is completed, you will only have to choose one of the 50 athletes (the description of each of them and the music will make you smile ) using the control arrows on your smartphone or tablet and get ready for the start.



Your hero on the computer monitor will move as soon as you perform any action with the touch screen of your mobile device.



At the moment, there are three games available in Chrome Super Sync Sports - running, biking and swimming. The graphics in all three games are simple, the management is not complex, and the gameplay is diverse, however, each of the games can be captivating, and for a long time. In Chrome Super Sync Sports, you can play four of us, competing on one screen, using smartphones or tablets to control game characters.



To win the race and see your name on the Hall of Fame (to do this, touch the trophy icon in the upper left corner of the screen), you need to perform certain actions on the touch screen as soon as possible.



At this time, the mobile device synchronizes with the computer and your actions are displayed in the browser on the PC.

System requirements


  • On a smartphone or tablet: Chrome for mobile devices running Android 4.0 and later; Chrome for mobile devices running iOS 4.3 and later; Firefox version 15 and higher; Safari version 5 and above.
  • On the computer: Chrome version 15 and higher; Safari version 5.06 and higher; Firefox version 10 and above
