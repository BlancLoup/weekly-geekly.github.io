Panasonic launches another toughie: Toughbook H2 protected tablet PC



On Habr√© more than once and not two reviews were published about laptops protected from everything and everyone. These laptops withstood the journey to the North Pole, they were thrown, rolled on them, as on a sled. Indeed, these devices have earned the reputation of reliable satellites in conditions where conventional technology refuses to work. This time, the company introduced a secure tablet PC. The name of this hero is Toughbook H2.

By tradition, the device is protected from shock, from dust, moisture, and temperature extremes. It can work both in freezing temperatures and in desert conditions.



As for the configuration, here we have a processor Core i5-2557M ULV with a frequency of 1.7 GHz, a display with a diagonal of 10 inches, 4 GB of RAM, 320 GB - the volume of the hard disk. There are wireless modules Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 2.1. When the battery is fully charged, the tablet can work for about 6.5 hours. Windows 7 is used as the OS. The device is light in weight - 1.58 kilograms.

In the future, the developers plan to release different versions of the device for different customers. Both fingerprint reader tablets and GPS and RFID reader tablets will be developed.

But the price ... Yes, the price rolls over. The cost of this device is 3450 evergreen units of the US monetary system.
