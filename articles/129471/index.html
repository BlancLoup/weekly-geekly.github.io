Google Analytics now shows real-time visitor statistics.



This is not a joke, indeed, the developers of Google Analytics have added a new tool to track the attendance of any resource in real time. The new tool got its own name - Google Analytics Real-Time. The service is only available in the new version of the interface, so if you are still working in the old, change your habits. Of course, if you need real-time tracking.

Developers believe that Google Analytics Real-Time will help webmasters track the popularity of various materials on their resources. Previously, real-time attendance tracking was also possible, but third-party tools and services were used for this, for example, whos.amung.us .



According to the developers, you can now send a tweet with a link to any material on your site, and track transitions in real time. After the same tweet stopped working, you can send a new one.
