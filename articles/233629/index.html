NASA presented a prototype origami system for spacecraft photovoltaic cells

About one such system on Habr√© already wrote : a team of engineers from BYU (Brigham Young University), with the involvement of physicist Robert Lang, a specialist in " scientific origami ", began work on finding effective ways of folding / unfolding solar panels.

At the end of last year, Robert Leng and space device developers began work on a solar panel project, 1 centimeter thick, the diameter of the entire system in the folded state is only 2.7 meters.
