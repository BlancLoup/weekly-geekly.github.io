The task of the number at the beginning of the title is to give the user confidence. The number is a predictable value and it eliminates the frustration of the unknown. Is this what any ad is seeking?

5. Lists can recycle other texts.


You have written a detailed text that tells about the product. But, most likely, it turned out too heavy or difficult. You can always reformat it into a list. Or a few.

Already in the format of the list, you can rank its parts as you need. Even on a serial number, you can speculate and collect additional traffic or comments.

In the form of lists is very convenient to do native advertising. It still remains an advertisement, but it has all the advantages of a list.

6. Lists are easier to structure


Lists are faster and easier to do. A copywriter hired for a couple of texts, who may not fully understand what you are doing, will rather compile a list rather than write a long and competent text on your subject matter. Because a long text needs a good structure, sometimes even intrigue, so that the reader can read it to the end.

Without a clear structure, the reader will decide that you have gone to the roof, and go to competitors. For such a text you need a good author. And, most likely, you are not ready to hire him.

This problem is completely devoid of lists. List as a format is already structured.
