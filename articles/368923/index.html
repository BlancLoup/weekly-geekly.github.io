10 pet gadgets

The rapid development of high technology did not leave aside pets. Highly intelligent gadgets are also created for our four-legged friends. For example, recently the company Pedigree, in conjunction with the advertising agency Saatchi & Saatchi, presented the original Posting Tail device, which allows dogs to upload photos of their favorite stuff on the social network.



In the post we will look at pet gadgets that will make life easier not only for pets, but also for owners.

Auto Feed for Dogs




Auto Feeder * is a useful invention for pet owners. With the help of such a device without much effort, you can determine how much food to give your pet at a certain time.



With such a device you will be able to monitor the regular feeding of the animal, which is the key to its health. This is especially true for those owners who have a fairly tight schedule. In addition, in case of emergency, the automatic feeding machine will allow you to leave your pet alone in the house.

Autodrink




This device * is a great addition to the automatic feeder. It will preserve the health of the animal and improve its quality of life.



Through the circulation of water through the filter, it is cleaned of dirt and bacteria. Thus, the water remains fresh and clean. In addition, running water attracts the attention of animals more than standing water.

GPS collar


This waterproof and shockproof gadget easily locates your pet. GPS clings onto the pet's collar and constantly accompanies it on all travels. Also, the navigator warns the owner if the pet goes beyond a certain radius.



At night, the backlight in the device * is triggered, which serves as a kind of beacon. The battery lasts about 3 days (in standby mode - up to 10).

Claw Clipper




Tattered chairs, wallpaper, curtain - common problems faced by pet owners. The presented gadget * , which replaced the clippers, will help to cope with such troubles. He neatly and painlessly trim the cat's claws. The nozzle of the machine is quite strong, has a diamond coating. Practical design and low weight allow you to easily trim the claws of your pet.

Anti-barking




Sometimes situations arise when the dog starts barking continuously and loudly. And neither the teams nor persuasions to calm it. In this case, you can use the ultrasonic gadget "Anti-barking", which interrupts the annoying barking, allowing you to enjoy absolute peace and quiet.



The device can be presented as a collar * or as a separate gadget * resembling a flashlight.

Electric collector




This electric collar * determines the walk radius for a pet. It is used if the dog can not go outside the yard. For example, in order not to get close to the road, dangerous zones, not to enter foreign territory, etc. The closer a dog approaches the conditioned border, the stronger the electrical impulse becomes.



LED collar




With a LED collar * pet will always be visible during a walk, even at night. Light intensity can be adjusted.

Radio control mouse




Running after a toy mouse is one of the favorite cat fun. And with a radio-controlled model, the game can be made more fun and fun.



Mice * are available in four colors: white, black, gray and brown.

LED flashlight




Another toy for cats of all ages - LED flashlight * . With the help of a laser beam, he reproduces the silhouettes of mice, fish, foot prints and other funny pictures. The visual range reaches 3 meters, so there will be enough space for the game.

Training tray




The training tray * will help you to teach a puppy or kitten to go to the toilet in a certain place or on the street without much difficulty. The design is the most natural, resembles grass cover. The top layer dries quickly, eliminates unpleasant odor and is easy to clean.

There are still many different gadgets for animals, such as racetracks, automatic hammocks, hiking cameras, doorbells, and the like. Perhaps, soon our younger brothers will have the same things as ours.
