5 deadly sins manager

In the epilogue to the book "7 skills of effective managers" I enjoyed listening to a brief interview with Stephen Covey. This person surprisingly simply talks about simple and at the same time complicated things that we often forget. There are 5 bad habits, malignant tumors, which contradict the principles of the 7 skills of effective managers and make a person a victim.

I decided to make a small memo for managers.



5 deadly sins manager:
  • To complain
  • Criticize
  • Compare yourself with others
  • Compete for recognition
  • Furiously defend their case


And if these habits themselves and in ordinary people do not promise anything good, then in the nature and behavior of the manager, they, as I see it, simply put an end to his career, because they build a strong box of stress around such a manager, misunderstanding and professional limitations.
