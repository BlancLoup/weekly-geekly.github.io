Skype + QIWI

Now you can pay for Skype in cash in QIWI terminals as easily as replenishing your phone bill - simply by specifying your (or someone else's) Skypename.

An account can be replenished with any amount of money by choice. The money is credited to the account within a few minutes, and then you can both spend it as needed and pay subscriptions to unlimited tariff plans and Skype minute packages.
