ListenIt - Google Chrome extension to automatically create audio versions of articles.

Hello! My name is Eugene, I created the ListenIt extension for the Google Chrome browser. The extension allows you to automatically create audio versions of articles, and readers to listen to them directly from the site.

Idea


It so happened that I have been using Google Chrome extensions for text dubbing for years, this is very useful for people with poor eyesight or just for those who don‚Äôt want to get stuck on page 1 without being able to do something else. Thanks to such extensions, it is possible to browse through your favorite site or go about your business while listening to some interesting article.

But all such extensions had a minus, basically the principle of their work was - select and listen. And it so happened that sometimes it was necessary to allocate different advertising banners in order to fully highlight and voice the article. Also in many extensions problems with normal Russian voice acting.

So the idea was born to create your own extension that can be adapted for each site separately without the need to select text, but simply by pressing a button and listening in a good Russian voice acting.

Expansion


To date, the extension has only the functionality of the voice of a certain number of sites, namely 5 sites.

The list of currently voiced sites


The extension adds a button for voice in the extreme corner of the site, when you click on it, the voice of an open article begins.

Voice button on meduza.io


For voice acting, Yandex SpeechKit is used , I have not yet found a good alternative for working with the Russian language.

The extension has a fairly simple interface, if the site is ‚Äúvoiced‚Äù, the extension will show with its full appearance that you can listen to the material from this site, or, on the contrary, you cannot.
