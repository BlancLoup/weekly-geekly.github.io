Ilon Mask commented on the situation with floating on a huge puddle of Tesla Model S

The electric car without problems overcame the flooded part of the road in Almaty



After YouTube posted a video demonstrating how Tesla Model S overcomes a flooded stretch of road in Almaty (Kazakhstan), they began to actively discuss this situation online. The fact is that torrential rains occurred in Alma-Ata, as a result of which various parts of the city were flooded. The flood affected the roads: some parts of the roadway were under water.

According to the author of the video, all interchanges were flooded in Almaty. Automobiles with ICE, which tried to overcome the "underwater" sections of the road, stopped. The engines of the cars broke down because the engine simply stuck due to water entering the air intake and the piston system. But Tesla Model S had no problems.



Interestingly, the network contains opinions of opponents of electric transport, who claim that Tesla cars are afraid of water. In fact, most of the water-sensitive elements of an electric vehicle are sealed. And the ingress of water in some systems of such a car, if not excluded, is very unlikely. When released into the water, the Tesla Model S literally begins to swim. Of course, the constant impact of water on an electric vehicle should not be allowed, since no one has yet canceled corrosion. But in a situation similar to that described above, Tesla electric cars come out victorious.

Information about an electric amphibian noticed Ilon Musk. He commented on the situation, saying that the company definitely could not recommend a ride into the water, but the Tesla Model S can quite well float on the water for a short time. The electric car will move at the expense of the rotating wheels.
