Forrester research: Internet users do not want to pay for video

Internet users do not want to pay for the video, experts from Forrester are sure. They have every reason for this confidence: according to a new study, only 9% of the entire adult Internet population pay for downloading movies or TV shows.

This year, sites like Apple iTunes will reach the peak of their popularity (market volume will be $ 279 million), after which a decline will occur, and in the next year the income of resources offering paid video will decrease. The reason for this - a large number of free video services.
