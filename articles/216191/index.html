Develop.re - social link aggregator for programmers



I want to show you Develop.re - a social link aggregator for programmers and geeks. It resembles HackerNews and Reddit, but has interesting features.

All polls are based on a universal algorithm that has no priorities or penalties, unlike, say, HackerNews. The actions of the moderators are public.

Registration is by invitation. This is done to combat substandard content and spam. Guests can request an invitation . The public tree of users allows you to trace the history of invitations.

If someone votes against a topic or comment, he needs to choose one of the reasons for such a decision.

Links are broadcast on twitter @develop_re .

The site works on the Rails application Lobsters .
