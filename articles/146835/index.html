PDF files can be imported into MS Word 2013

Advanced MS Word users probably know that from the Office 2010 version they can save documents in PDF format, there is also a corresponding plug-in for Office 2007. But PDF support was only that: the text editor never knew how to open PDF files, much less edit them , without installing third party software. Everything will change in Word 2013 (Word 15): a converter for converting PDF documents into a Word document will be delivered with it.

According to unofficial information , the converter does not guarantee 100% compliance with the result of the original document and works best with mostly text files.

In addition, as previously reported , Word 15 separately will be a tool for viewing PDF documents, and there is already guaranteed one hundred percent compliance, but without the ability to edit.

Judging by the appearance of documents in full-screen mode (both Word documents and PDF), this function is primarily intended for reading electronic books on tablets.
