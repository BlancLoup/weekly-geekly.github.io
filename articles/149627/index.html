Graphic framework. Social services icons set



Hello dear habrasoobschestvo, this post serves as a continuation of the recently actively discussed the topic of social icons [ 1 ] [ 2 ]. The thought that came to my mind after reading these articles led to the emergence of a new vector of development of this topic. Speaking of the vector, for example, it happens that the design of the developed site or application has its own specifics and it is not possible to integrate standard icons of social services without adaptation. In this case, we take into the hands of Google and take up the search for the right logos, in order to create a suitable set of icons. Oh yeah, I got a little distracted, let's continue on the vector so as not to do such boring actions, I gathered together several popular icons in the vector and now everyone can save some time by using them.

Little about the collection


I always thought that there was a minimal future, and then there was also Windows with its Metro ... In general, this framework was created without changing the love of minimalism and thus following the trend. There are no rounds and gradients, although, however, thanks to the format, this issue is solved in a couple of clicks.



Each file consists of only two fragments, a background and a logo.



Without problems, you can change not only the color scheme, but also the shape.



I hope my efforts will benefit someone.
