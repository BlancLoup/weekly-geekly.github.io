The best way to memorize numbers

So, let's begin.


People have to memorize a large amount of information every day. In this case, some data types are easily remembered, others - difficult. So we can easily remember the word known to us, consisting of a large number of letters, but we face difficulties if the letters do not form a word. A similar problem arises when memorizing numbers, as this is abstract information.

Illumine popular ways to memorize numbers:


Method 1: Using Sound Memory:


1) A phrase is selected in which the number of letters in each word corresponds to the next digit of the number:
"I know and remember this perfectly,
Pi many signs I lose, in vain "
Accordingly, it stands for 3.14159265358

2) Or in which the numbers themselves rhyme:
"To PI remember, brothers,
It is necessary to repeat more often:
Three, fourteen, fifteen,
Nine, twenty-six and five

Advantage: it is easy to select words and make sentences from them, as well as to decipher.

Disadvantage: when memorizing long numeric combinations
(for example: 7-digit phone number) will have to make long sentences. Even with a small number of long sentences you can get confused, not to mention large.

Method 2: Using Shaped Memory

1) One digit:
The unit is a stake, a piece of wood in bits of bark, rough to the touch. Two - swan, white and thick, and so on for all numbers.

2) Two or more:
After creating a figurative series, memorizing numbers turns into a game similar to drawing a cartoon.
For example, to memorize the number 21, one can imagine a swan, on which a count fell, his bruise and a swan hissing angrily and pinching a stake. Thus, instead of abstract figures, moving pictures appear in the memory, which are easily and firmly held in it.

Advantage: a person's fantasy will always find a figurative comparison for any combination of numbers.
