Rumors: iPod, MacBook, MacBook Pro and iMac lines will be updated in the fall

AppleInsider shares with us information, according to which in the next three months almost every Apple product will be updated.



In the second week of September (before the exact date was called - September 9), Apple will call on journalists close to her at the Apple Special Event, during which Steve Jobs will tell about new products: the new iPod nano, which we have already written about , and, possibly, about device with a multi-touch display.

After updating the iPod line, Apple will update its laptops later in September or in early October. MacBook will say goodbye to the plastic case, receiving instead of aluminum-steel, MacBook Pro will also change its appearance - it will become more round. Perhaps we already have a photo of a new MacBook Pro package:





The MacBook Pro will finally have a new battery, and the case will be designed so that you can easily replace the internal hard drive. Now this is not done very simply - it took me a couple of hours to replace the hard drive and demanded a trip to Gorbushka for a special screwdriver.



Perhaps the MacBook and MacBook Pro will be built on a new mystical chipset , which Apple either develops itself, or buys from some other company - not Intel.

A little later, in November, the iMac desktop will also be updated - they will become faster, but their appearance will remain the same. But Mac Pro and Mac mini most likely will not wait for updates until the end of the year.
