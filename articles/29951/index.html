Quick search for music and play lists

In this topic I want to tell you about my new site. The idea in my head was spinning for a long time, but there was no opportunity for implementation. More recently, these features have appeared. More precisely, I learned about their existence. Meet - music search service and playlist compilation OnePage.ru.

What it is.


The new music service will help the habra-user to find almost any song and download it immediately, with a direct link. It is also possible to make playlists of your favorite tracks and listen to them directly from the web. Or share with a friend so that he could evaluate your selection. For example, made a small playlist with famous music for every taste. There are present as modern hits and popular songs of the Soviet era.

Almost all functionality is available without registration. But in order for you to save the created playlists it (registration) will still have to go through.
Actually, what can I say - go, see, listen.

The project was not conceived as a commercial or for squeezing n-th sums. Initially I did for myself and my friends, but then I decided to finalize and show you.
