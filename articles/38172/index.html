NHibernate 2.0

Downloading from SourceForge: http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=73818

New features:
  • Add join mapping
  • <union> tables and <union-subclass> inheritance strategy
  • HQL functions 'current_timestamp', 'str' and 'locate' for PostgreSQL dialect
