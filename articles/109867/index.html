Playstation Phone due out in March 2011



Just a month ago, many doubted that the Playstation Phone was a real device, not a fake. Even the video that got on the Web was the reason for the long trials among fans of such devices - many believed that video was fake. She added fuel to the fire and Sony Ericsson, saying that the company is not going to produce anything like this, and in general, "my moped is not." As it turned out, the phone is still a reality, and in fact such a device can come to the court of many gadget lovers.

The device, which combines a portable game console PSP Go and a smartphone with a mobile OS Android, was named Zeus Z1. So far this is the working title of the project, so the phone can also get another name as soon as mass production begins. The other day it became known (again, not from the words of the developers, but from some anonymous sources) that the official announcement of the device should take place around February 2011, and the launch of sales of this hybrid game portable console and smartphone will take place in March next year. As you can see, the wait is not so long.

It is worth recalling that the device will be made in the form of a slider, but, unlike keyboard sliders, here the lower part of the device represents the functional controls of the gaming device. An interesting point is that on the back of the device there are characteristic for all Sony manipulators shifta.

The device configuration includes a display with a diagonal of 3.7 inches, a processor with a frequency of 1 GHz plus 512 megabytes of RAM, there is also a 5 megapixel camera. Games in the device can be downloaded through the proprietary application from Sony.

Interestingly, in spite of such an abundance of information about the new device, including photos, videos and configuration data, the official release of the gaming device has not yet been confirmed by the company. Well, this line of behavior has become familiar for a long time, so only the most inveterate skeptics doubt the reality of the device.
