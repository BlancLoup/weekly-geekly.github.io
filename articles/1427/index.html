Mail.Ru start Games

At the end of May, a special project dedicated to computer games appeared on Mail.Ru portal - Games@Mail.Ru . The creation of a game section is carried out by Mail.Ru in cooperation with the Territory of Information Technologies company .

The new game section is designed to give users maximum information on the game world (news, reviews, screenshots of new games, video reviews and much more). In addition, the project is focused on communication and search for like-minded people on gaming topics. Any authorized user of the section will be able to participate in game discussions on the forum and even start their own gaming blog.
