What is Socializm.net?

We, Anton Popov and Damir Khalilov, want to talk about our new joint project Socializm.net .

This is a collective blog for professionals and beginners in the field of SMM (social media marketing). Today social networks are experiencing a real boom. From the user's point of view, a social network is primarily a place of communication between people. At the same time, for a marketer, this is a new channel of information transfer, a way of interacting with the consumer, which is not always used skillfully today. ‚ÄúWithout knowing the ford‚Äù and the specifics of social networks, one can break firewood, cause aggression and get the opposite effect, which sometimes happens.



Our project is dedicated to social media marketing - this is a kind of guide to the Russian SMM. The main goal is to gather all web 2.0 marketers under one roof to share experiences, discuss, communicate.

The principle of work: "from each according to his ability, to each according to his work." Anyone can read the blog. Authors can be those who previously wrote about marketing and PR in social networks. Each author is approved by the editorial board: in addition to the content of the articles, clear language and literacy are important. Once every two weeks, as well as for the month, the authors of the most popular articles will be awarded cash prizes. Currently, Ilya Rabchenok, aka Scheller, Vladimir Zhilinsky, Maria Podolyak, Viktor Zakharchenko are among the authors of the blog. The blog already has interesting articles based on the personal experience of internet marketers.
Socializm.net is also the name of the book we are currently working on. She will also be devoted to social media marketing, and the blog will be a good help for us in identifying key points and white spots in the Russian SMM.
