The information signal ADAPTER SENSE through the protective zero resistor R6928 falls on the transient dual transistor Q6920, and from it goes already to the multi-controller (SMC). Such a sophisticated information signal transmission circuitry performs a protective function. The downside of it is that the burning of any element will lead to the rupture of communication.

Diagnostics and repair are reduced to the definition of a burned-out element and the subsequent replacement.
