Preferred time for technical work with a break in the provision of services

We have some internal discussion about what time of work that interrupts the work of services, preferably. It is clear that such work is best done at night. But what night?

Here are the arguments that sounded:
  • If something goes wrong, that on weekdays the employees of the client company will be in the workplace and the total downtime will be less.
  • Weekend downtime for services focused on a 5-day working week is preferable, as more time to recover
  • At night, on weekends, the activity of users of sites is higher than on weekdays
  • The work carried out at night at the weekend makes the employees of the company-client on their days off distract


In other words, there are arguments for different options. Taking into account that the work directly affects customers, we decided to provide the right solutions to customers.
