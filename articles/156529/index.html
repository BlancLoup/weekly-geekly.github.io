Monument web developer


This morning I cleaned the office, dug up old diplomas within the framework. In one of them - a certificate of registration of the monument to a web developer.

This is a reminder of the April 1 drawing of almost three years ago, when in the spring of the year I was struck with the idea: to launch on April 1 news about the creation of the first regional monument to Internet users on the network.

What came out of it:
1. They decided to make a globe covered with a tambourine as a monument.
2. Created a visualization (above) with a view on one of the wide and famous streets in Penza
3. The designer painted the certificate of registration of the monument, we printed it out and took photos with it.
4. In the local media, blogs and other resources with the audience posted the news about the creation of the monument with visualization and photography.
What is the result:
1. The drawing turned out. The news was discussed on the Internet almost the whole day. Several people specifically went to this street to look at the monument.
2. A photo and links to the organizers were posted on the Internet.
3. For about a week we received a steady stream of visitors to our sites and questions about whether this is a joke or a reality.

I think maybe it's time to put this idea into reality?
