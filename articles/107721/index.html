Xmarks are very much alive!



Good news for all those users of Habrasoobshchestvo who works with the Xmarks service, allowing to synchronize bookmarks, passwords and logins, history of work in the Network at different workstations, with different browsers. Xmarks - a truly universal service, with which I have been working since the appearance of its forerunner, has already forgotten how the service was called before renaming. So, the last few months there were rumors, and quite reasonable, that the service ceases to exist due to lack of funding.

Yes, the advertisements placed on the service pages did not even pay back the cost of renting the servers - after all, the capacity had to be constantly increased as a result of the addition of new users. As a result, Xmarks management posted on the company's official blog sad news about the imminent closure of the service. There were two ways of survival: the first is to make the service fully paid, while losing up to 90% of users, the second is to buy the service of a larger company.

All this time, the company was looking for an investor, and there were several proposals that did not fully satisfy the wishes of the Xmarks management.
