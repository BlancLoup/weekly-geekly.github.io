Desktop EEE

Asus introduced the desktop version of the PC series EEE - Asus EEE Top ET1602 \ 1603, previously known as the EEE Monitor.


The new device is equipped with a touchscreen display from 15'6 to 19 inches and an aspect ratio of 16: 9, a 160 GB hard drive, an integrated card reader, a Wi-Fi 802.11n wireless connection, and a 1.3 mpix camera with a microphone.
The brain center is built on the Intel Atom processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz in the 1 GB DDR2 add-on memory.

Unlike the ET1602 model equipped with the integrated Intel GMA 950 video card with 64 MB of memory, the ET1603 will be equipped with an AMD Radeon HD 3450 video card with 256 MB of onboard memory.

The first model comes without a battery, while the older one comes with a built-in battery. With a weight of 4.3Kg, Asus Eee Top ET1602 \ 1603 can always be taken with you to any room. (thanks spyfzm)

The customer can choose from versions based on Microsoft Windows XP Home Edition or Linux.
It also uses the Express Gate technology built on the Linux kernel, which allows you to use the Firefox browser or the Skype dialer without starting the main OS.
Shells will be produced in two forms - black and white. Mouse and keyboard included.
The new model will go on sale approximately on November 20 with an estimated cost of $ 450.
