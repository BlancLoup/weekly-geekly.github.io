VisualSVN 3.0.2 client for Visual Studio is released and has become more accessible!


Today came across great news! New client VisualSVN 3.0.2 , which supports the latest edition of VS 2012 , was released on August 17, 2012 !


The VisualSVN client is not just published, but it is also given the opportunity to use it for free, under a Community license !
The license allows commercial use of VisualSVN and is available free of charge for any computer that is not a member of an Active Directory domain. And is the perfect solution for freelancers, students and amateurs.

I personally personally like this tool, and now it is possible to use it for my own small projects, for free! And this means that more convenience and possibilities of working with SVN are given, right from the studio!

Today I was convinced of the veracity of this news by installing this client!



Thank you very much VisualSVN Limited .
