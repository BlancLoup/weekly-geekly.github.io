The first hackathon Skyeng: 15 working projects in two days



In late September, we conducted our first hackathon for employees. Within two days, our developers, designers, products and analysts, having broken up into small teams, diligently coded, and eventually ended up with fifteen projects to the state of MVP. We tell how it was.

The idea was in the air for a long time. First of all, a hackathon is a great way to conceive and implement a dozen or two projects for which there is simply no time for normal operation. Secondly, many of us work remotely, so a hackathon is an opportunity to finally (often for the first time) see colleagues with whom you communicate, live every day. And finally, we were able to implement this idea.

The theme of the hackathon is education. You could do anything: a web project, a mobile application, a bot, etc., and the binding to Skyeng was not mandatory. Developers, analysts, designers, products were invited; all paid for the road and accommodation. The guys had to break up into teams of 2-4 people, it is desirable that these people represent different divisions of the company.

To save time and improve results, we decided to collect ideas and teams in advance. From the beginning of September, projects and participants were entered into a special tablet in Google Docs ( copy ), and the authors of the ideas actively ‚Äúsold‚Äù to their colleagues in Slack.



There was also a small prize fund (hakaton something internal) - 30, 20 and 10 thousand rubles for the first three places.

We managed to start looking for a place to hold the hackathon a little more than two weeks before the event - never do that. Yes, our organizers got experience for level 80, but it was very nervous. The fact is that the platform for the hackathon is not just "some" conference center or hotel. Among the mandatory requirements (collected, including by interviewing our employees who participated in other hackathons) are the presence of separate zones, the ability to work at night, a large number of extension sockets, round-the-clock access to coffee tea and buns, and so on.

As a result, two suitable sites were found - one in Moscow, the second near Chekhov. Moscow had obvious advantages - no need to go anywhere, no problems with the organization of evening activities, in the end, if it is uncomfortable in the main hall, you can always go to code in the nearest tavern. The drawbacks are also obvious: there is little space, you can always go to code in the nearest tavern, instead of doing team building. Chekhov's version imposed transport difficulties, but offered clean air, a lot of space, an unusual environment and a bath; him and chose.

A little more about Chekhov #APi


Chekhov #APi - creative residence near the eponymous town near Moscow. On a vast territory of 200 hectares there are forests, meadows, a river, several buildings (designed by eminent architects) and even their own farm. The beauty of this magnitude - the ability to work together or retire if desired. The residence is designed for events like ours: visiting sessions, workshops, team building and other activities that require a radical change of environment.



There are all conditions for work here - many places with elegant panoramic views (two buildings have full external glazing) and terraces. Our landing force consisting of 50 people filled the local capacity of sleeping places to capacity, had to come up with additional solutions; in the current state of the place is designed for 40 overnight guests. However, a media center is being built on the territory, which will more than double the capacity.
We are very pleased with the choice and proud to have grown enough to be able to afford such a location for the hackathon.




For two days, almost 50 people coded, steamed in a bath, went in for sports, ran drones, went to the farm and worried about the electricity that had turned off in the whole area, and on the third they gathered in the Moscow office and finally saw what we did. We present 15 projects.

Skyeng music



An iPhone player that works with your Apple Music collection and displays lyrics on the screen. With the text, you can do the usual operations for the student school - watch the translation of words, add them to your dictionary. A free version of the Musixmatch lyrics API is used, so there is no karaoke here.

Skyeng tweets


www.messenger.com/t/skyengtweets
A bot for Facebook, issuing several fresh popular tweets that contain a word just added by a student to his dictionary (you need to link a Skeyng account using a token sent by mail). Unobtrusive tool for memorizing words in context.

Skyeng reader


An iPhone application that works with any browser, opening articles from sites in a separate window, where you can already get translations of words and add them to your dictionary. It works similar to our Chrome extension, only with simpler algorithms; Soon it will be built into the usual words application.

Skeyng social


We see in our students the need for additional communication among themselves in English: Skyeng conversational clubs occur on a schedule once a week, and also require investment from our side. This project is a prototype of a social network for Skyeng students, where they can pick up interlocutors to suit their level and their interests. In order to communicate only in English, all messages are voiced by "Google"; participants see only the test that they write themselves.

Chat roulette




Another solution to the problem of insufficient communication between students is a chat roulette with video available to our students immediately after the hackathon. For the chat, random interlocutors of approximately the same age, one level of language knowledge and similar interests are selected. There is no teacher here, this is not a complete replacement for a talk club, but a good opportunity to practice a little. And if the interlocutor categorically does not like - it can be banned for several hours.

InstaPoll


play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=en.skyeng.poll.poll
instapolly.herokuapp.com
Application for quick surveys. Useful for conferences, seminars or group lessons when it is necessary to arrange a vote without unnecessary labor and technical costs. With it, a vote was taken for the best projects of the hackathon.

Yesno


yesno.gq
Another application for group activities. Participants are divided into two teams, after which they answer a series of questions. In our case, there were 20 questions, they were English phrases, it was necessary to determine if there were any mistakes in them or not. The correct answers are counted in the asset of each team, the result can be observed in real time.

Haiku app


haikuthon.herokuapp.com
The application in which you need to collect Japanese haiku from English words. The requirements are simple: the first line - 5 syllables, the second - 7, the third - again 5, rhyme and meaning are optional. The benefits of this simple project in two things: first, the student learns the correct pronunciation and number of syllables in English words (it is not obvious to everyone, for example, that chocolate has two syllables). Secondly, mnemonic connections are involved here: collecting new, funny and unexpected combinations, the student memorizes the words he learns faster. However, the hackathon version does not have the ability to connect its own dictionary.

Hackaton action




A 3D action game written in two days on Unity, with which no one was familiar before. The basic idea is to allow the user to download his new video card, while memorizing English words. The toy is a labyrinth with cheater gates, through which to run faster, but you need to correctly translate the word. There are two players who first reach the finish line - he won.

guess the word


vimbox.skyeng.ru/speech-game/start

The quiz is fully consistent with its name: the student is shown a picture and an English dictionary definition, he needs to guess and say the hidden word. You can choose a category and your level of knowledge of the language. The player has four tips: the first letter, the translation into Russian, the word in English, the pronunciation of the word in English (here it is enough to simply repeat what was heard). Tips reduce the number of prize points, the task of the quiz is to score as many as possible in 10 words.

Build Words




An iPhone game about building houses, where each floor is a properly translated word. You can play solo or together. In single mode, the height of the house is limited only by your knowledge, the roof at some point is in space and continues to grow - the developers promise there are different surprises. When playing together, the task is to give more correct answers than the opponent, and make it faster.
