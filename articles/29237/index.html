Desktop version of the Asus Eee PC and MSI Wind


Desktop version of the ultrabudget Asus Eee PC laptop, Asus Eee Box B202 desktop, went on sale


Currently, Hong Kong residents can purchase the Eee Box B202. It costs 320 dollars. Previously it was assumed that the price of the Eee Box B202 will not exceed $ 300

Built Eee Box B202 based on Intel Atom processor operating at 1.6 gigahertz. The amount of its RAM is one gigabyte, a hard disk is 80 gigabytes. Works computer running an operating system based on the Linux kernel.

For Internet access, the Eee Box B202 is equipped with a Wi-Fi wireless module and an Ethernet port. Additionally, a Bluetooth module can be installed on the computer.

The dimensions of the Eee Box B202 are comparable to a sheet of A4 paper, the thickness of the computer does not exceed 25 millimeters. The power consumption of the desktop is 20 watts. The computer is available in white, black, red and green cases.



Also in July, should begin selling budget desktop MSI Wind. It will be built based on the Intel Atom processor. The maximum amount of computer memory will be two gigabytes, a hard disk - 160 gigabytes. The cost of the desktop will be from 199 to 299 dollars. Unlike the Eee Box B202, the MSI Wind desktop is equipped with a DVD drive.
