Prostopleer became paid



This morning, going to prostopleer.com , I found a letter to users , which says that the service is no longer fully free. Those who have not purchased a premium account are entitled to listen to no more than 15 tracks per day. For the rest it is proposed to pay from 60 rubles per month to 500 per year.

In general, once it happened with last.fm and now it happened with a simple player. But as I continued to use last.fm, so I will continue with a simple player. I believe that for the convenience in the modern Internet is not shameful and pay. Especially not so much money for most people.

On Twitter , this was the beginning of a serious boil over shit, mainly showing the culture of users who want to rake up everything for free and not ready to pay a minimum of money for a convenient and pleasant service to support developers and continue to use the resource.
