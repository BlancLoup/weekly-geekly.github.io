Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon Ultrabook Video Review



Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon - Lenovo's new ultrabook, a distinctive feature of which is the carbon fiber body - a very durable and lightweight material. Despite the size, similar to 13-inch laptops, X1 Carbon is equipped with a 14-inch matte display, with a resolution of 1600x900.

Laptops run on low-voltage Intel Core i5 / i7 processors, equipped with four or eight gigabytes of RAM and solid-state drives with capacities ranging from 128 to 256 gigabytes.

Other features include the presence of a hardware encryption module (TPM) and an optional 3G module.
