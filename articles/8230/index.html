WebMoney Merchant "made friends" with FireFox and Opera

Many people know that accepting WebMoney on websites is often implemented through Merchant WebMoney .

Due to the fact that for interacting with WebMoney Keeeper Classic a special ActiveX is used, there were difficulties with paying for sites on users of browsers other than Internet Explorer.

Good news for FireFox and Opera users: now everything works in these browsers exactly as in IE (you just need to install additional plugins).

You can verify this here (this is a test page, money from the wallet does not go anywhere).
