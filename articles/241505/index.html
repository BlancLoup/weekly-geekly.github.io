VeeRoute is the winner of the it track

Companies of all sizes involved in shipping and delivery often face various logistical challenges. Sometimes it is quite difficult to calculate the optimal route, while saving time and, accordingly, money. This problem is particularly relevant for large companies that need to calculate hundreds of routes per day.

VeeRoute came to the rescue. This company is developing special algorithms that allow you to build routes in real time. The product is intended for online stores, retailers, distributors, courier services and service companies. It can be used by companies that have up to 10 cars, and those who have a car fleet.
