IP-SIM is an FMC SIM card, but with some differences from those SIMs that are standardly offered by mobile operators. The ‚Äúordinary‚Äù FMC-SIMs have two directions of calls: calls to short numbers within the company's dial plan (addressed to the IP-PBX) and calls to all other (fixed, fixed, etc.) numbers that are addressed in the usual way - via cellular network.
