How is the recruitment at the department ABBYY in MIPT

This year, the ABBYY department at the Faculty of Innovations and High Technologies (FIVT) of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology turned 12 years old. During this time, we have trained more than 240 qualified IT specialists, almost a hundred of whom work in the company. Our developers create unique technologies of intellectual information processing, which are used by more than 50 million users in more than 200 countries of the world. Keeping the tradition, in April we held a set of students for the next year, 2019. Today we will talk about how we are looking for talented guys, what they are taught and why they want to work in our company.



ABBYY employs talented people with innovative thinking and exceptional abilities. They are engaged in complex, interesting and ambitious tasks that no one has solved before. Our goal is to find the same talented guys and develop their abilities. That is why 12 years ago we opened the Department of Image Recognition and Text Processing (RIOT) at MIPT, and 7 years ago - the Department of Computer Linguistics (CL). Our departments at MIPT help prepare specialists in promising and sought-after areas: machine learning and analysis of texts in natural language. During training, we pay attention not only to theory, but also to practical tasks, the development and application of technology in business. More than half of the graduates become employees of the company.

The system of education at MIPT is built in such a way that in the first years students undergo fundamental university training, and in older ones they are distributed in basic departments and are actively involved in practical activities of academic institutions like the Scientific Research Institute of the Russian Academy of Sciences and R & D centers of large companies. Every year dozens of people come to the departments of the RIOT and KL .

In 2015, according to the results of entrance examinations, 30 out of 80 students were enrolled in ABBYY basic departments. In 2016, 37 out of 90 students accepted the departments, and in 2017, 37 out of 110 people. In this year, we assume that the number of those enrolled may exceed 40, as some guys asked to be allowed to have an interview later. Although we do not exclude that in the end we will find only 30, but the most motivated students.

We take students to the department from the third year of study - first to undergraduate and then to the magistracy. Previously, the guys write a motivation letter, where they tell about themselves, their skills, why they want to study at the ABBYY departments, what projects they have already done and what they would like to do after the university.

How is the set


Recruitment is carried out at the end of the second course. Students gather in a large flow audience at MIPT. Traditionally, the head of the group of educational projects, Andrei Ocheretny, talks about the company, technological challenges, prospects for the development of the markets in which we work, as well as where graduates of the departments are arranged, about their relevance in the global labor market, and, of course, about the training program and student research papers.

Then students receive a test with 10-14 tasks, the solution of which is given an hour and a half.

After the test, students communicate with our programmers. This interview lasts about 30 minutes. Our employee checks the solutions of the problems, knowledge and erudition of the student, and also asks him additional questions to check the level of knowledge of programming languages, knowledge of the theory of algorithms and the ability to use various data structures to solve specific problems.



Then the interview with our HR specialists begins. For many students, this is the first interview in life. The guys tell what they are interested in and what they want to do. HR pays attention to things traditional for such conversations and, naturally, asks provocative questions: for example, why choosing a student from several basic departments (1C, Yandex, Sberbank, Cognitive Technologies), the student decided to enter the ABBYY department. We understand that IT is a very important industry and one of the drivers of the country's economy along with the production of raw materials. The success of the industry depends on the availability of personnel, therefore, on the one hand, companies are already competing for undergraduate students, and on the other hand, market players unite to grow the future of IT in Russia together.


Then we summarize the points for solving the problems, take into account the notes made at the interviews, and compile a list of the guys accepted to the department.
