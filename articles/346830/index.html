MageConf 2017 video and conference materials



On December 16, the largest technical conference dedicated to the platform Magento and eCommerce in general was held in Kiev. The conference was held in 4 parallel streams, in which 30 reports and workshops were presented. The conference was attended by 600+ participants.

The main event was preceded by Magento Contribution Day, held in the Magento company's office the day before and gathered more than 100 people who, together with Magento Core, programmers and architects worked to improve the platform and fix bugs. And also on the development of new system functionality - Multi-Source Inventory .



During the event, 40+ Pull Request was delivered to the Magento 2 repository.

In this post I suggest to get acquainted with the video of all the speeches that you find under the cat. The program of the conference MageConf 2017 can be found by clicking on the link.
