Goodbye desktop



On March 23, in the Mediametrics.ru studio Ruslan Kuvaev, the founder and CEO of the BYYD mobile advertising platform, conducted an interview with Maxim Tadevosyan, Deputy General Director of Rambler & Co.

It was about the approaches to mass monetization of media, the formats and mechanics of mobile monetization of various portals and publications, as well as the prospects for the development of the mobile market. Theses of the conversation - in our today's issue.

The average mobile audience share on Rambler & Co resources is 40-42% on average. Today the desktop is no longer relevant - you can read any news in a mobile phone or on a mobile screen. Due to the fact that the news background is quite active today (current economic and political events), this share will grow.

Two main factors of the choice of platform by readers (mobile devices / desktop): socio-demographic indicators of the audience and the characteristics of the resource. For example, the youth audience is ready to pay for content as entertainment, while the older audience prefers to save time and localize news content in one place.

Now there is an active shift of the news audience from the desktop to mobile platforms . This has become especially noticeable since the Olympic Games and the crisis in Ukraine.

Differences in approaches to the monetization of various resources of the group of companies Rambler & Co. The main directions of mobile monetization.

Rambler & Co divides mobile monetization into several parts. The two main streams for mobile versions are advertising and consumer revenues.

Consumer revenues imply paid off of advertising on media resources.

In mobile media monetization, there are two main segments of customers. The first is those who built a business in a mobile phone (GetTaxi, Uber, Delivery Club). It is vital for them to be in the mobile phones of representatives of their audience, so they are willing to pay for the installation of applications through mobile advertising.

The second segment is classic advertising clients that use mobile advertising as one of the channels - these are top customers and investors. The specifics of this segment - the lack of a meter - is the biggest problem in the mobile media market. If the three companies - Yandex. Rambler & Co, Mail Group - will unite their efforts, they will be able to develop a consolidated market position, which does not yet exist.
