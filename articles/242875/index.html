Q: Why on a weekday?

A: Our experience shows that it is easier for young Junior developers to come on a day off: usually bosses are reluctant to let go on weekdays. Q: How does the online broadcast work?

A: A few days before the conference, everyone who bought the broadcast receives a letter with a link. By clicking on this link, the buyer of the online broadcast receives a Flash / HTML5 player, which will start working 15-20 minutes before the start of the conference.
In the process, the user can switch between rooms. In addition, at the end of the event, online broadcast customers will be able to see all the videos in the recording on the very same link the next morning. True, without mounting and cutting into separate reports, because mounting and cutting take time.
