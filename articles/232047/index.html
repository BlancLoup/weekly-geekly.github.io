The Russians took the victory in the final of the Imagine Cup 2014



I was surprised that the international final of the Imagine Cup 2014, which took place on August 1 in Seattle, bypassed the habrasocommunity, and meanwhile a team of students from the Perm National Research Polytechnic University Brainy Studio won, the guys took the 1st place and received 50 thousand dollars for development your project in the "Games" category in this competition!

The guys represented the game "Turn On" about the adventures of an electric spark that saves the city from blackout.

The team was represented by undergraduate and graduate students of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering PNRPU:

Romin Evgeny (graduate student) - Team Leader
Antonov Ilya
Frolov Alexander
Galkin Nikita
Team mentor: Anton Baranov (graduate student)

The full version of the award ceremony can be viewed on the official website of the competition . It is noteworthy that the names of the winners in the Games category were called by the famous Russian programmer, author of Tetris, Alexey Pajitnov .
