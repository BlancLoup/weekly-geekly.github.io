Supercell earned over $ 2.3 billion in just three games in 2015



Supercell , which develops mobile games, recently reported on its financial results for 2015 - the company earned more than $ 2.3 billion during this period. The company's revenue came from just three projects: Clash of Clans, Boom Beach and Hay Day. Net income was $ 964 million compared to $ 592 million in 2014 and revenue of $ 1.7 billion for the entire 2014.

Most of the profits came in Clash of Clans, which was released in 2012. It is noteworthy that the leadership of Supercell does not indicate what proportion of income falls on Clash of Clans, but it is obvious that this project brings most of the profits. In 2015, Clash of Clans was ranked first and second in the list of highest-grossing applications, which made it one of the most successful in the world.

At the same time, over time, the company paid dividends to shareholders in the amount of $ 15.50 per share for a total of $ 669 million, and also initiated a scheme to repurchase shares from its employees in the amount of $ 128 million.



These financial results are especially impressive, considering that the Supercell employs only 180 people, and the studio has only three main projects. The new game Clash Royale, which Supercell launched in 2016, now takes first place in downloads and profits on the App Store in 44 countries. At the same time, the company noted 100 million active users of Clash Royale, so the financial results of 2016 may turn out to be significantly higher.
