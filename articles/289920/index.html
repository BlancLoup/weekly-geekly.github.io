HP has become a FireEye partner



HP is partnering with FireEye, a cybersecurity developer. Reuters notes that within the framework of this transaction one of the most advanced teams in the information security market will appear.

Companies are planning to offer an industry standard architecture that will focus on advanced cyber defense and incident response techniques. Companies will also create a single incident response center that will investigate cyber attacks.

According to Mike Nefkens, executive vice president of HP Enterprise Services, more and more customers want to analyze and improve the level of information protection before colliding with regular cyber attacks, and new proposals created with the support of FireEye will help to satisfy this business need.

Collaboration with FireEye may be beneficial for HP, as the information security market continues to grow due to the increase in the number of companies affected by cyber attacks.
