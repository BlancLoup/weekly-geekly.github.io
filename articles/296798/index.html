What is sammari and why are more and more people reading them?



How to stop postponing life for later? How to become stress resistant? How to resist manipulation and emotional blackmail? How to succeed? How to overcome shyness? Answers to these and many other questions can be found in non-fiction books, that is, books on psychology, business, self-development, personal effectiveness, health, science, and everything that contributes to the broadening of horizons and the personal and professional growth of their readers. Ideas from these books help make life better.

But a modern and active person may have several problems with reading:

  • Where to find time to read all the books of interest?
  • How not to make a mistake and choose a book worthy of attention?
  • Is this particular book worth my time (on average 5 hours) and / or money?

There are many useful and decent books that help to change life for the better, if you read them at the right time, but how to find the time for it? Of course, you can search for information of interest on the Internet, but how often are you satisfied with the answers from the network? The quality of many online articles leaves much to be desired, and everyone who is not lazy can publish articles. Too often, you can get bad advice and load your head with unnecessary information.

From the purchase of books, and many stops the money issue. Books cost a lot, many pirated services are closed due to changes in legislation, and the rest are attacking users' computers with malicious programs and annoying ads.

There is another pressing problem of reading non-fiction books. These are annoying repetitions of the same idea, ‚Äúwater‚Äù and examples that are not close to us. Reading some books, one wonders what the author wanted to say and why is he lingering? Such books want to pass through a sieve and separate the wheat from the chaff.

It is for these reasons that sammaris have recently become increasingly popular - brief reviews of key ideas from non-fiction books, set out on 10-20 pages, which can be read in 10-20 minutes.

Sammari help readers solve several problems at once:

  • they contribute to the broadening of horizons, allowing the reader to develop in several areas at once and keep abreast of ideas and concepts from books of various subjects, from psychology to scientific discoveries;
  • they save time - sammari can be read in just 10-20 minutes;
  • they save money for reading lovers - the cost of a subscription for a year is equivalent to the cost of 7 books, but during this time you will be able to read over 150 summaries;
  • they help to make a truly informed choice of which books are worthy of being in your library;
  • they help to repeat the most useful ideas from the already read books and put everything on the shelves;
  • they are always at hand, as presented in all popular electronic formats.

Sammari is not someone else's idle speculation, but qualitative information from authors' books, which are deeply versed in their subject and have themselves achieved in life what they write about.

Below we want to give answers to the most popular questions about sammarie:

Sammari replace books?


Yes and no. Yes, because, having read a book of any book, you will understand that this is enough for you. In this case, reading sammari will save you from buying a book that you do not need. On the other hand, after reading an interesting sammari, you will understand that you want to study the book on which it is written in more detail. In this case, sammari will facilitate the informed choice of full-length books for your library.

Do sammari copyright?


No, only key ideas and concepts from books are presented in sammari, it is an independent creative product. Moreover, sammari contribute to the dissemination of ideas of the authors, because if you enjoy sammari, it is likely that you will get a book.

What formats do you have sammari in?


In all popular electronic text formats (ePub, mobi, fb2, PDF). Plus, you can read directly on the site thanks to a convenient reader.

Our opinion - sammari is not a replacement for a book, sammari is a most useful tool of personal effectiveness, giving you the opportunity to always be aware of interesting ideas from books about psychology, business, development, science and other non-fiction.
