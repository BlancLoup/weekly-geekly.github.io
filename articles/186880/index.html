We turn the case of a sistemnik into a board for daily planning


Good day, Habr!

In this edition of ‚ÄúSmart Handles,‚Äù I want to tell you how to turn an ordinary black system case into elegant shorts for daily planning. Those. tasks are placed on the ‚Äúboard‚Äù for the current working day (for me, 9:00 - 18:00). In this paper, we will need:
  • chassis system unit - 1 piece;
  • straight arms - 2 pieces;
  • Masking tape 2cm wide - 1 piece;
  • white thread - 1 hank;
  • scissors - 1 piece.

Disclaimer. This option is suitable for those who have a system unit located on the table. Thank you ozver for the comment!


I apologize in advance for the quality of the pictures - I took pictures of what was at hand.

Our goal is to line the wall with cases with threads, where each section will correspond to a time interval, for example, one hour. The task will be a sticker with the appropriate text.

So, we have a black case and a wall without ventilation perforation.


Measure and cut the thread length of the wall with a margin of 3-4 cm at the edges.


Along the edges of the thread fastened to the end of the case with pieces of masking tape.


In the process.


After the wall is lined onto the desired number of sections, we glue the vertical stripes of masking tape along the edges of the casing itself. These bars will serve as a timeline, as well as keep our lining.


Cut off the excess part of the threads. I advise you not to cut off at the root, but with a margin of one centimeter.
I'll explain why
This article owes its appearance to my carelessness. First, I broke the surface into 8 sections, because we have an eight-hour working day. But what about lunch! As a result, I had one hour, and I decided to try to move the threads, because Masking tape allows such an operation. But the length of the trimmed ends did not allow the threads to move normally, so it was decided to redo everything, and at the same time take a picture and describe, in order to share with the community. Therefore it is necessary to leave the ends of sufficient length in case of changes in the number of sections or their size.



So our daily planning board is ready.


It remains only to apply a timeline.


The final result.
