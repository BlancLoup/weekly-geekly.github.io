Forecast: What will be the market for commercial drones by 2022

Market forecast for commercial drones by 2022 from the international analytical agency Interact Analysis .


A huge revenue growth is projected from 1.3 to 15 billion dollars. Including sales of hardware, software / analytics and various services.



More than 620,000 drones will be shipped in 2022. 6 times more than in 2016.

TL; DR : DJI copters dominate and will dominate.



More than 50% of revenue will come from the US and China. In the United States in 2016 accounted for a third of the total market revenue. China will increase its share.



2/3 of the sold drones in 2022 will be cheaper than 2000 dollars, but they will bring only 13% of the total revenue. Loukosterov will dominate, but the most "tasty" will fall on industrial designs.



12,000 non-existent, fully autonomous drones will be sold in 2022. Drona, who does not need a man at all, we will see in 2020.

The dominance of the DJI is obvious. DJI in 2016 controlled more than 50% of the market, which is 2 times more than the next 5 players combined.



Predicted huge growth in all areas. Although media and entertainment dominate today, they will show the smallest growth in the near future. Shipping and logistics will grow at the fastest pace, but in 2022 they will remain one of the smallest industry verticals for UAVs.
