Humanity is in control. 7 useless gadgets that you should not connect with your smartphone

Technologies should simplify, not complicate, life. It is convenient to have access to your music collection and watch movies anywhere, but keeping smart toilets and powerful cutlery at home is a bust.
Never connect these technical innovations to your smartphone.
