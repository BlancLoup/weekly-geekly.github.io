how it works


  1. You have an idea for an application for WP7.
  2. Based on the idea, you make a prototype of the application (sketches, sketches, sketchflow, a partially working application, etc.).
  3. Send an application with a description and a prototype for participation in the marathon to us by email rush4apps@microsoft.com (the sooner the better) - we give feedback on your application, discuss with you how best to make an application, what platform features can be successfully used and others interesting questions.
