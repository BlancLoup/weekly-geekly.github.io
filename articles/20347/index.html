Radiola mood

Amazingly interesting, it turns out, to listen to the radio, in which music is rotated on the principle of mood, not style. Those. users upload their playlists and give them a mood.

So It turns out the radio, in which music is played continuously, but nothing is required to search - start with a random song (recommended under the chosen mood) and enjoy the endlessly unpredictable flow of musical mood.

A kind of analogue of last.fm, in which you can listen to almost any music (there is a search by author / title, of course) - while increasing the number of users will increase the music base.

In short, a great alternative for those who like to listen to music for free! (By the way, the copyrights are not violated.)

Anyone wishing to start their own on nothing like a trip to the stream of positive mood - Wellcome !
