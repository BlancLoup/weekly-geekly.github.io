QIWI Wallet Freelancers

Many are already familiar with the QIWI Wallet service, but few people know and use all its capabilities. Today I want to offer an extended review, relevant, primarily for freelancers , as well as all those who are interested in the ability to quickly transfer money person2person.

When it comes to choosing a payment system for work, the following questions are of primary interest:

- How to enter / receive money in the system?
- How to quickly transfer / receive money?
- How to withdraw the money?
- How to do it quickly and with a minimum commission?

How it looks in practice:

Enter / receive money in the system :
Money in the QIWI Wallet through the QIWI terminals is entered without commission, if the amount of input is from 500 rubles. The term for crediting funds to the account is a few seconds. They know about it.
But how can freelancers use it? Give customers your QIWI wallet, let them replenish it, indicating in the comments the purpose of the transfer:

Instructions: here .
- Transfer speed - a few seconds.
- Commission for both Parties - 0%.

Transfers between users :
QIWI Wallet provides instant transfer / receive money.
There are 2 transfer options: invoicing and transfer by phone number.

1. Invoicing:
This is very convenient if you are freelancing or you have a small online business (store). There is a comment field in the invoice that the recipient will see and by which the payment can be identified. For example, in the comments you can write: Doctoral Thesis. The emergence of black holes.

- Transfer speed - a few seconds.

2. Direct transfer between users:
Create an account is not necessary. If your customer uses QIWI Wallet, he can transfer money to you for work in a few seconds:

- Transfer speed - a few seconds.
- Commission for the customer - 1%.

Both methods work not only on the site, but also in QIWI terminals or in mobile phone applications.

QIWI Wallet provides freelancers with the ability to quickly and profitably withdraw money from the system. Commission - from 0.0% to 2.5% (depending on the method), the term - from online up to 3 days (depending on the method).
