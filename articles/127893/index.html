Work with electronic money

Hello, habrazhiteli!

Our today's post will be devoted to electronic money!

Replenishing wallets and withdrawing funds from them is now simple: many banks provide electronic wallets replenishment services, there are examples when a bank and an electronic money issuing company offer a joint product to a customer when a customer‚Äôs account is linked to his electronic purse.

The indisputable advantage of electronic money at the moment is a simple way of acquiring and using them (citizens, as a rule, do not trust banks - breaking the stereotype of queues at cash registers with gray books is very difficult).

According to rough estimates, the turnover of electronic money is almost equal to the volume of payments passing through the self-service terminals, and makes up one sixth of the total volume of payments by individuals.



Our Bank provides a wide range of opportunities for working with electronic money.

So, our main advantages:

Wide selection

You can replenish the following e-wallets through the Internet bank:
  1. WebMoney (WMR, WMZ)
  2. Yandex money
  3. QIWI Wallet
  4. Wallet One (W1)
  5. RBK-money
  6. Money @ mail.ru
  7. Moneta.Ru
  8. Ukash
  9. Network processing
  10. Sprypay
  11. Z-Payment

Speed

All payments are instantly online. No need to wait a few days, as is the case with other banks.

Tariffs

The bank does not charge commission for payments in favor of EPS. Therefore, in most cases, the replenishment of electronic wallets for customers is completely free, including for the popular Yandex.Money and QIWI Wallet. Some EPS (they are few) themselves take a small percentage of the input money.

Bank account replenishment

Of course, replenishing e-wallets is great. But what to do if it is necessary to replenish a bank account from an electronic wallet? We have such an opportunity.

Top up through QIWI Wallet



The commission for the transaction will be 1.5% of the payment amount, but not less than 50 rubles. The maximum amount of payment including commission is 15,000 rubles. Account replenishment occurs, of course, online.

Opportunities for those who make money on Google Adsense

As you know, Google recently made it possible to receive a reward on the Rapid electronic wallet .

The subsequent withdrawal of money from the Rapida wallet to a bank account usually takes several days. In the case of withdrawal to an account at Bank24.ru, we made it happen instantly!

The commission for the transaction will be 1% of the payment amount, but not less than 50 rubles.

Moneybookers account replenishment

In our Internet bank, you can apply for sending a transfer in a foreign currency in favor of the Moneybookers payment system. Payment in the Bank is processed 1 business day. From two to five days the payment is delivered to the recipient company. When sending a payment, the Bank charges a fee in accordance with the Bank's Tariffs for international payments.
