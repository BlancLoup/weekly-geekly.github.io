Holiday Gift



Users who promptly update Opera's browser to the next stable version may have noticed the appearance in Opera 10 and higher of the links and search from the well-known online store OZON.ru. Naturally, such changes do not happen just like that - Opera Software and OZON.ru concluded an agreement on mutually beneficial cooperation. In fact, this partnership benefits the users of the Opera browser themselves. Moreover, the most real - because, as they say, saved - the same thing that earned.

So, during the month, from February 8 to March 7 of this year, all Opera users who make a purchase in the OZON.ru store will receive a 10% discount on all products (except for consumer electronics and special offers). The number of purchases is not limited. In order to get a discount, you must enter the code word spring in the appropriate section of the checkout page. I also remind you that in order for a discount to be granted to you, you need to access the website of the OZON.ru store using the links pre-installed in the browser or searching for this partner. It is the operatic identifier that will allow the sales system to understand that there is a discount for you.
