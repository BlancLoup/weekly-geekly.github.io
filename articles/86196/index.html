Sales of iPhone 3GS will begin tomorrow night

The previous nightly action with the sale of Snow Leopard turned out to be extremely successful, so the Russian Apple resellers decided to repeat the idea.

On Friday, March 5, exactly at 0:01 Moscow re: Store and Z-Store stores start selling Apple iPhone 3GS phones first in Russia. The action will be held in the Z-Store stores on Belorusskaya (Moscow, 1st Tverskaya-Yamskaya, 28) and on Kutuzovsky (Moscow, Kutuzovsky Prospect, 26), as well as in re: Store stores in Atrium (Moscow, Zemlyanoy St. Val, 33, 1 floor) and in the "European" (square of the Kiev railway station, 4th floor, pav. 4-B7, near the cinema). Only on the night of the start of sales can you buy accessories at a 50% discount.

Last time, the free Snow Leopard disc was buried in a snowdrift near the store. Now these surprises are not expected, but interesting communication with the most loyal Russian fans of Apple you provided.

In all other retail stores re: Store and Z-Store, the new Apple phone will go on sale on the morning of March 5th. The cost of devices for 16 and 32 GB is 29,990 and 34,990 rubles, respectively.
