Put.io out of beta and open for registration

I hasten to please someone, and to upset someone. Wonderful online sitbox Put.io today came out of the beta test and opened its doors to new visitors. Now you do not need to beg for an invite on the Internet or monitor Twitter for days, waiting for hands at which 300 pieces fly away in 10-15 minutes.

I will briefly talk about the project to those who do not know what it is all about.
Put.io is a project that allows you to download torrents to your server at impressive speeds. The maximum that I saw firsthand - 18 MB / s. For me, as a resident of Belarus, this is just cosmic speed. After the download is complete, you can upload files to your hard drive or view the video (if this was, of course, the content of the torrent) in streaming mode using a browser or any player.

In addition to this, despite the fact that Put.io positions itself as a service for downloading torrents, in fact, the range of its services is even wider - the service also supports Rapidshare, and can be used as an online backup tool.
