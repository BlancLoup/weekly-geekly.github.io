Show Sound # 7 - Podcast about audio equipment, components, formats and technologies

Hello! With you, "Show Sound" is a podcast for those who like to live, work, relax and move around, listening to your favorite music, podcasts or audio books at home, at work, in the subway or other modes of transport. Starting from the very beginning, we will help you to get acquainted with the world of devices and technologies that can show you the true sound of musical compositions.

In the 7th issue, we discuss the article by Steve Guttenberg (which we published this week) and the measurement of sound quality with Yuri Fomin, a high-end speaker design engineer, founder of F-Lab, which develops speaker systems for brands "Defender", "Jetbalance", "AVE" and "Arslab" (the most famous of them: "Defender" G-2.1, Volcano 1; "Jetbalance" 371, 381, 391; "AVE" DF 100, 102, 104; "Arslab" AC1, AC3)

Podcast is conducted by Dmitry Kabanov ( dmitrykabanov ).
