Since April 1997, 3D Realms studio announced the beginning of work on the continuation of Duke Nukem 3D, 10 years have passed. Since then there have been many events. Graphic engines were changing, release dates were approved and then postponed, screenshots appeared. There were even official gaming videos at major gaming exhibitions and assurances of the game coming soon. As time went on, our confidence in the reality of the appearance of Duke Nukem Forever slowly melted away. It is possible that some of you, in desperation, bought themselves games from other manufacturers. For example, Orange Box. But today we were given a reason for hope. The official commercial of the long-awaited game has been released. Let's hope that this time the story with the commercials submitted for 2005 E3 will not be repeated.
