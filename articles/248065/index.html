Hosting in Kazakhstan or how to break Jackpot

From today in Kazakhstan the cost of domain names .kz and.kaz soared by more than 2 times. An interesting fact: the cost has risen due to requests from the managers of two hosting providers that control more than 80% of the domain market to the Kazakhstan Association of IT Companies, which is the administrator of the first-level domain .kz i.kaz. Yesterday afternoon, customers of one of the largest hosting providers in Kazakhstan, PS.kz, received notifications about price increases for registration services and the renewal of national domains from 01/15/2014. As arguments, the order of the ‚ÄúAssociation of IT Companies of Kazakhstan‚Äù dated January 13 , 2014 , which sets new prices from 01/15/2014, is proposed. Actually, this order appeared, thanks to the very same PS.kz.

For reference: the direct responsibility for maintaining the efficiency of national domains of Level 1 lies entirely on another organization, namely, the Kazakh Network Information Center .

The establishing part of the document provides information that the increase is due to eliminate the imbalance between the cost of annual maintenance of domain names in the Kazakhstani segment of the Internet, ensuring the further development of the Distributed Domain Name Registration System and the payback period of Registrars.

The basis of the order stated:
1) Letter from the KazNic Institution dated December 29, 2014, No. 141229/1 on 2 pages ;
2) Letter of the Registrar HOSTER.KZ dated December 29, 2014 No. 231 on 1 sheet ;
3) Letter from the Registrar PS.kz dated December 19, 2014 No. 70-1 on 1 sheet ;
4) Certificate of competition in the market for the provision of domain name support services from the KazNic institution from 24 December 12, 2014 on 2 pages ;
5) Comparative analysis of domain name support services by country on 1 sheet .

Interesting fact: both companies Hoster.KZ and PS.kz, which control more than 80% of the domain names market, practiced dumping policies regarding the cost of national domains. Which is understandable, because these are the largest hosting providers that sell high-margin services in the appendix to the domain in the form of hosting a website on a shared hosting or VPS / VDS. Very often, domain names were distributed free of charge when purchasing a hosting. At Hoster.kz, the number of free domains depended on the chosen hosting plan, and their number could reach 10.

It so happened that in almost any business, the sale of basic and related goods and services is carried out. To obtain a competitive advantage, there is a common method of reducing the price of one type of product or service, which will be compensated by selling other goods or services that will be on the buyer's check. Thanks to the dumping of domain prices, hosters attracted customers to host sites and everyone seemed to be happy.

Ironically, but in the statistics provided by the institution, both the main players Hoster.KZ and PS.KZ, insisting on losses from dumping, have the lowest prices for domain name registration / maintenance services than other registrars.
