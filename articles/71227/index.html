The book "Dive into HTML5" (in the process of writing)

Mark Pilgrim started writing a new book in the Dive into series, which already includes such freely available books as Dive Into Python , Dive Into Python 3 , Dive Into Greasemonkey , Dive Into Accessibility and maybe some more (dude productive).

Back in September last year, under the name Dive into HTML5 , the publication of This Week in HTML5 notes for the WHATWG blog began.

Now Mark began to design everything in the form of a full-fledged book . At the moment there are 2 chapters in which it is told about how to determine the HTML5 features available in the browser, as well as a chapter on working with the canvas element.
