6 games in 6 weeks - the fourth game

Why live if you don't laugh at yourself and make fun of your friends



The fourth game is Chapay . I decided to completely redo my ancient application and changed everything I could in the game - pictures, goals, physics, code. But not about the code I want to tell. Not about modeling the irregularities of a chessboard. Not about the algorithm of collision of unbalanced checkers. And not even about the numerical solution of the three-body collision problem. Not.

I have a long-standing favor to the country that I love and to people whom I respect. Once I lived in the USSR and was an exemplary citizen. My country was divided - I tolerated it. I can respond to insults only with a sharp word and a cheeky joke. As a protest, I wrote an ironic game Sea Battle, where two republics fought for the Black Sea.
My crafts were shown in the program Time, and they called me a hawk, inciting war.

It's been 20 years since. I'm used to the loss of the USSR, youth and language FORTRAN. And suddenly. Injustice happened again. And I released a game where injustice is punished, and good overcomes evil.

But at the same time he returned his favor for that old unsuccessful joke program.

Guys, fight and win in the virtual worlds. And leave the world to the world.
