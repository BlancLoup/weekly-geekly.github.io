Seminar "Hybrid Clouds - Pros and Cons: What Business and IT Should Prepare for" - April 25, Moscow

Date: April 25th.
Venue: Linxdatacenter DPC, Moscow, ul. March 8, d. 14.

What will be discussed:

  • Benefits of hybrid infrastructure: scaling, performance, big data analytics.
  • Difficulties and "thin places": migration, customization, configuration and support systems.
  • Lenovo hardware solutions for hybrid clouds.
  • SAP Systems and Virtualization: Migration to S / 4HANA and Client System Support - itelligence Russia.
  • Hybrid cloud: waiting vs reality. Experience Positive Technologies and Virtu Systems.
