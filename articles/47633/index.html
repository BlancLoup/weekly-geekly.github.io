In vkontakte will be RSS?

Recently, when viewing photos on VK.com, an acquaintance turned my attention to the wonderful rss icon in the address bar of my browser. We were somewhat surprised, and decided to try it in action.

Opera normally tape refused to show. Gave us a couple of <no title> labels with broken links and that's it. Akregator, also could not download any of this message. Google reader did not understand anything either.

Well, nothing to do, open the source code of some sort of tape.

For example, this one: http://vkontakte.ru/photos.php?act=rss&id=321290&st=

What is wrong here?
First , the authors did not bother to fill in the title. Well right, the photos have no headlines.
Secondly , there is no description. Here is a description of the photos as it is, it was possible to lay out.
Thirdly , the reference is relative. Nowhere, it seems, it is not written that it should be absolute, but it is not at all clear as to what to consider it.
Fourthly ...

It is not at all clear why they even included tape output if it does not work. Yes, of course, they did not announce RSS support, but we are not blind.
Let's hope that someday they will finish everything and we will have an easy way to get news.
