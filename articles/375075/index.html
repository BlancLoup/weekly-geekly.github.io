The Cronar 2015



Friends, Kruglendar 2015 is ready!

A round calendar is a way to see all the days of the year at the same time.
One needs it when planning, others, like in a diary, mark bright moments on it and keep a calendar in memory of the past year. Third - celebrate the upcoming birthdays of friends and relatives.

The project lives for the third year, previous versions were published in 2013 and 2014 , I continue the tradition.
As before, the clandestine is 99% programmatically generated.
The design is improved: the typography is improved, the extra lines are removed, only two remain: the numbers (sinusoid) and the months ( hypocycloid ).
It became brighter and more spacious.

It is downloaded from the site kruglendar.ru , printed on A1 format, gray fields are cut off.
Community in contact: vk.com/kruglendar

I would be glad to receive feedback, thoughts, wishes, suggestions and photos of the clandestrian in your interior.
Happy New Year!
