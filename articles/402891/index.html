Events in cities on Cosmonautics Day 2017

Traditionally, before Cosmonautics Day, I aggregate festive events that will take place in different cities.



Moscow
The Cosmonautics Museum announced a large program of events from April 6 to 15, from a motor rally, an exhibition of retro cars and a demonstration of the Gagarin KHL Cup to lectures, meetings, quests and even the presentation of an astronomy textbook.

The Moscow Planetarium hosts a traditional Mooncake concert on April 14 and 15.
April 8 will be the third quest "Space Flight".

Since March 31, the exhibition "Dream of Space" in the Central Exhibition Hall "Manege"
On April 12, an observatory will open in Gorky Park, there will also be festive events and a photo exhibition of Roskosmos.

The Pushchino Radio Astronomy Observatory will hold an open day on April 15.

Near Moscow in the park Ethnomir space week .

St. Petersburg
The St. Petersburg Federation of Cosmonautics holds events on April 7 and April 9 in the Peter and Paul Fortress.

There are several events on different days in the Planetarium - lectures and a discussion club.
From April 1 to April 30, in the gallery of Cosmos Market there will be lectures, master classes and a meeting with cosmonaut Alexander Lazutkin.

In the restaurant "Northern Modern" on April 12, Yurina night with popularizers.

Novosibirsk
The Novosibirsk Planetarium is holding the second night for Yurin, and the registration is already closed. But perhaps there are still chances to get into the science cafe "Eureka" , where there will be lectures, music and the screening of the film "The October Sky".

Yekaterinburg
"April 12 - 12 hours - 12 volleys" - the traditional launch of 12 missiles on the Plotinka.

Kazan
There are no specialized events in Kazan this year. The choice is a charity space race on April 8 or a party on April 14.

Chelyabinsk
In Chelyabinsk, enthusiasts regularly build pictures, words and figures from cars. The traditional flash mob will be on April 12th.

Omsk
In Omsk, Cosmonautics Day will be celebrated by turning off hot water and volunteering .

Samara
This year, it seems, running has become very fashionable. If earlier in Samara they organized space Saturday work, then this year and here they run a race.
