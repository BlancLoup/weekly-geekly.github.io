PayPal has released a mobile terminal PayPal Here



As a matter of fact, the mobile terminal itself, through which payments can be paid, is not new. But the fact that such a terminal is released by this payment system is news. Perhaps someone from the representatives of the habrasoobschestva such a terminal may be useful. Well, and it will be simply interesting to someone to learn about a new opportunity.

So, even this Thursday, in San Francisco, the company introduced a new program of mobile payments, announcing under this program the mobile terminal PayPal Here. First of all, the terminal is aimed at small business representatives. Under the new program, the device will be distributed free of charge, along with the appropriate software (for now only for iOS, software for other mobile platforms will soon be distributed).



The company will earn on the commission, making up 2.7% of the payment amount. PayPal Here is a direct competitor of a similar program from Square. This company came up with a mobile terminal in 2009, and since then the device from Square has become very popular in the West. So now Square has a very powerful competitor - after all, PayPal is the main electronic system in many countries, and the emergence of a mobile terminal ‚Äúhoned‚Äù for this system automatically makes PayPal Here popular and in demand.

In addition to making payments by credit card, PayPal Here can be used to pay by check, check financial transactions for a specific period of time. You can also send invoices to the recipient's e-mail through the software from PayPal Here, and the buyer, having received the invoice, will be able to pay it. You can get the terminal here - paypal.com/here (of course, to receive it you need to fit the conditions of the payment system).
