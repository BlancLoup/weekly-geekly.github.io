Rambler Cash Register on iPhone 5 and iOS 6

We also look forward to today's presentation of the iPhone 5 and the release of iOS 6 in the coming days. The fact is that among other new products in the iPhone 5 and in iOS 6, the Passbook application appears. This is such a universal "ticket holder" that allows you to store in one place the confirmation of all important online purchases and armor - coupons, electronic tickets, hotel reservations, electronic cinema and concert tickets, and so on.



As a rule, Russian services are integrated with such applications much later than the official release. But we in the Rambler Cashier (kassa.rambler.ru) decided to do the integration in advance.



So our users who upgrade their firmware or buy an iPhone 5 will immediately be able to send tickets to their Passbook purchased at the Cashier (on the web, from their mobile, from the Poster application, on Yandex.Fish or Kinopoisk - in general , wherever "Rambler Cashier" sells tickets).

This is a small, but pleasant and important news for us.

PS:
How does Passbook work? The user can save all his purchases / bookings / coupons in the Passbook application by clicking on the button in the letter that arrives after the operation (whether it is booking or purchase). All coupons and bookings are grouped in the application by topic, for example - air travel, online shopping, movies and entertainment. At the same time, the Passbook allows you not to miss important things: for example, it reminds you that the coupon‚Äôs action ends, or that the date of the event for which the ticket was purchased has changed.
