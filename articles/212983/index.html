ITunes Connect Update



Suddenly, Apple has updated the Sales and Trends section of iTunes Connect.

The section with the updated interface is available at the reportingitc2.apple.com link.

Main innovations:
  • View download statistics by country, platform, category, content type,
  • Convenient dashboard on the start page,
  • Top content for downloads and purchases (without application division and in-app),
  • Convenient switching to different time intervals.

There is a feeling that in the near future the rest of iTunes Connect sections will also be subject to redesign. Well, it's time.

The old version is available at reportingitc.apple.com .
For those interested, the User Guide for the updated section. Curiously, the document has many Confidential notes.
