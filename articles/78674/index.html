Cave story

This game is a true example of Japanese art. A sad story honed to shine about cute rabbit-like creatures and a robot soldier suffering from amnesia. Daisuki Amaya aka Pixel, as if a true wizard, brought me back to childhood, when an eight-bit processor worked wonders. Actually, for the same reason, some young players do not understand the whole charm of the game and leave it without starting. And after passing only once, you risk seeing not everything;)

Once Derek Yu suffered a brilliant idea - to combine the seeming simplicity of platform arcades with the random generation of levels in the style of bagels. And this he more than succeeded. Believe me, in this game you will not be bored to die - just to die, because she is incredibly complicated. At the moment I have been buried 170 times and have never been congratulated on the victory. And this does not bother you at all - each subsequent game is unique and unlikely to others. To my joy and disappointment of the generation of the PlayStation One - old school music and graphics :)

Windows, the expected version for Xbox 360, will cost money.

A turn-based puzzle game in which you play an ‚Äúextraordinary underground scum fighter‚Äù, burdened to clear King Dugan's dungeons from cockroaches, goblins and other evil spirits. With each move made, the monsters approach and, in order to defeat them, you must crush them with your Real Big Sword, which occupies a whole cage of the playing field. Dungeon crawl

Allegedly based on Nethack, ADOM and other bagels, the game boldly introduces innovations into the genre, offering an expanded set of primary races and classes, as well as a diverse pantheon of gods that you can worship regardless of initial abilities. Dungeon Crawl is more flexible and balanced than its predecessors, and in it the main logic and tactical skill than memorizing all the special features and abilities.

Brutal hardcore game about nude ninja saboteur. Despite the trivial management, you will not even have a training session immediately, let alone a campaign. An interesting feature - the game Ikiki - author Nikujin - always turn out to be either hits or failures. And this game is definitely a hit.

Cortex Command

An amazing game in which you must defend against hordes of enemies from orbit, defending the vital brains needed to control your own units. Exceptionally everything here is drawn in the style of Pixel-Art, and each pixel is a separate object. This opens up a huge number of ways to kill enemies and distort the landscape. For example, your unit may die from bleeding, because his body is left with only a head, or you can crush a dozen enemies, dropping the landing craft on them. Advanced AI, group tactics and an unusual physics engine are the main points of a large list of features that continues to grow. The game is still in development, well, dozens of mods help the game to brighten up a long wait, changing the gameplay beyond recognition. There is the possibility of playing Hot-Seat (up to four players with one computer).

$ 18 .
Windows, Mac OS X.


Focus

Something reminiscent of the notorious N, Focus makes you learn to survive in the wildest conditions of your total destruction task. The only thing that is in your arsenal (except for jumps, of course) is the ability to teleport for a short distance. Like the heroes from the movie "X-Men" 2 and "Jumper", you will disappear, being on the verge of death. But do not think that it will be easy - it will be terrible, terribly difficult!

Free
Windows


Battle for wesnoth

Based on the game for SEGA Genesis Warsong (known in Japan as Langrisser), Battle for Wesnoth is a turn-based strategy game that proves that loyal fans, united by a strong desire, can create great games. Battle for Wesnoth is an open source, cross-platform project in which anyone can add a new map, unit or even a whole company. Most of the works included in the official release, created by the players. Also, the game is translated into many languages, including Russian. The development team includes co-founder of the Open Source Initiative, Eric S. Raymond, and other Open Source pioneers.

Free
Windows, Linux, Mac OS X.

Here you can not die. This is one of my favorite indie games, which I advise everyone to play, regardless of game preferences.
