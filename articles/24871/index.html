UMI.CMS Pro Commerce Trial

Recently, Yumisoft released a trial version of the most complete edition of its engine - UMI.CMS Pro Commerce.
This version is not subject to any restrictions in use, except for the period of validity. For 45 days (one and a half times more than the competition) there is an opportunity to try all the possibilities on a real, working hosting. During this time, you can test how this CMS will work on the selected hosting, which will be the load and stability in general. Well, and most importantly, check and decide on the functionality of the system.
After the expiration of the trial period, you can purchase a full-fledged annual license or a license for younger editions. All data entered for the trial 45 days will be stored in the database and folders on the hosting. The trial version is convenient enough for those who want to insure themselves and check how the system will behave in combat conditions.
