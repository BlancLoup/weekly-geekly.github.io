If DJI did an echo sounder: PowerRay underwater drone

Let us take the liberty to supplement MagisterLudi 's post about submariner drones. In the fifth paragraph , talking about crowdfunding and modern commercial projects, a very curious project bypassed the side - PowerRay.



Of course, it‚Äôs impossible to list all the ‚Äúunderwater‚Äù crowdfunding: a good idea is picked up and replicated instantly, and then you would have to remember the Fathome One project, and Ziphius , and many more ...

PowerRay already has two CES shows, and this year the team arrived with a ready project. Now PowerRay can be bought, and it costs a lot: in the maximum configuration - 159,000 rubles !

He looks cool and premium. Design is clearly one of the strengths of the gadget, and it really looks like what DJI would do underwater drones if they did, of course.


The company considers its target audience to be fishing enthusiasts and independent researchers of the underwater world and underwater photography. This is a kind of miniature submarine with some fishing rod functions, an integrated camera and a removable echo sounder that can be used separately - the PowerSeeker element.



Immersion opportunities are up to 30 meters, and for the entire time PowerRay is shooting in 4K and streaming it to a smartphone in Full HD format (IOS / Android). The locator notices the fish with an accuracy of 10 cm, after which it begins to lure it, which can also be observed in real time.

Moreover, preventing some negative from fishermen who are skeptical about geek fishing, the head of the company emphasizes that the drone does not make it easier to catch fish. PowerRay only shows the bottom and tells you if there is something alive in this place, and then further on.



In calm water, the autonomy of the drone is designed for 4 hours of work, and up to a full battery, the PowerRay charges 2 - 2.5 hours.
