THQ lost half its capitalization

One of the largest publishers of video games on the verge of bankruptcy. During today's trading on the NASDAQ Stock Exchange, the publisher lost almost 50% of its capitalization. Now for one company offer shares, a little more than one and a half dollars.

Experts believe that such a sharp drop in shares, due to a financial report published the day before, judging by which, the company lost about $ 21 million in losses, and the publisher announced a delay of several expected releases.

One of the factors of such a sharp collapse, perhaps, are not very good sales of Darksiders 2, rumors about reductions and hiring a number of companies, such as Centreview Partners, specializing in crisis management.
