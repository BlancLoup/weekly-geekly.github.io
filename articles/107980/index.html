Hot days in the fight for security

Hello!

We want to invite you to spend one day together at the InfoSecurity Russia exhibition. StorageExpo. Documation'2010.

On November 17, from 13:00 to 13:30, a lecture by Raimund Genes, Trend Micro Technical Director, will take place on the Main Stage: The menacing landscape on the threshold of the third millennium.

Raimund constantly investigates the problems of emerging threats, their influence, the landscape at the level of large companies, governments and citizens all over the world. In his lecture, he will present the results of a global study and data that show what actions are being taken in the world in the fight against cybercriminals, will share an overview of international trends and independent indicators that are so important to describe the global threat landscape. The priority is not only technology, but a reasonable combination of functional boundaries, which would include all possible precautions, security policies, as well as increased user awareness.

And from 14:00 to 16:00 Virtualization-2012 session will begin its work in the Perspectives hall. Performance. Regulations. Security.

Together with partners, VMware and Ai-Teko, Veniamin Levtsov (Regional Manager, Trend Micro in Russia and the CIS) will present specific approaches to building an effective information security system in a virtual environment.

See you!
After all, security must be global, multinational, and responsive to all threats.
