New York Stock Exchange broadcasts real-time quotes

The New York Stock Exchange ( NYSE ) plans this year to launch a program to provide real-time stock quotes to Internet sites, reports Reuters .

The cost of the service will not be small - a month for it will have to pay $ 100 thousand. But there are already companies that are willing to lay out that amount. Among them were Google and the business news channel CNBC . In addition, the NYSE is negotiating with Yahoo .
