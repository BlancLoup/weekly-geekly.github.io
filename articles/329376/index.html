Pentestit Security Conference 2017: see you in July



Colleagues and friends! On July 15, 2017, Pentestit Security Conference will be held in Orel - a conference dedicated to practical information security. This will be a meeting of caring people who love information security, not even as a profession, but as a lifestyle.

About the conference


This will be a meeting of people who have something to tell each other, discuss interesting topics, communicate in an informal setting, this is an opportunity to find new friends and like-minded people. These are strong technical reports, from specialists of many areas of practical information security in its various manifestations.

Reports


At the moment, the following reports have been agreed (list is supplemented):

  • Implementation of the GSM protocol stack;
  • Using radare2 when analyzing binary files;
  • Practical techniques for secure network configuration;
  • User identification on the Internet using HTTP cookies;
  • OSINT automation;
  • IoT / embedded devices vulnerabilities;
  • Analysis of malicious code of mobile applications;
  • MorphAES: polymorphic shells;
  • Vulners Burp Suite plugin presentation for vulnerability detection;
  • Forensic analysis of Windows;
  • Topological options for the implementation of WAF;
  • Heuristic malware detection algorithms;
  • Distributed detection systems for computer attacks;
  • FailOpen: how WAFs are out of work when protecting modern web applications;
  • Static analysis of Dalvik bytecode;
  • Symbolic execution - SMT-solver;
  • Identification of vulnerabilities in automotive synchronization.

Speakers


The speakers of the conference are experts in the field of practical security of Pentestit, employees of the Federal Security Service, security experts from Russian and foreign organizations, such as QIWI, MTS, NPO Echelon, ENKI, Radio Monitoring Technologies, Innopolis University.

Laboratory penetration testing "Test lab v.11"




On June 30, 2017, the Pentestit team will launch a new, 11th penetration testing laboratory dedicated to the opening of the conference. As always, the laboratory will be a copy of the real corporate network of the company, containing typical vulnerabilities and configuration errors, and anyone can take part. Currently, more than 15,000 people are registered in the laboratory!

Call for papers


If you have something to tell you - demonstrate a new vector or attack scenario, show an interesting tool in action, share a cool exploration - send requests to info@pentestit.ru (marked with CFP).
