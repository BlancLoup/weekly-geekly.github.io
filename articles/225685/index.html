The most comprehensive video game collection in the world is up for sale.



The video game collection compiled by Michael Thomasson is the largest / most comprehensive video game collection in the world. In any case, the Guinness Book of Records tells us so. In this collection there are more than 10,607 different toys, many of which are not even printed. The games that Michael printed out have all the applications / manuals and original packaging preserved.

Michael, as far as you can understand, a very active gamer, after all, just will not collect video games, right? Nevertheless, he decided to sell his collection. According to the owner, he now needs additional funds, and the games no longer interest him as they used to.

In general, if you have available funds (very considerable ones), then this collection can be bought .

The price is not fixed, the sale is conducted on an auction basis. Now the maximum bid is 90 thousand dollars, and until the end of the auction another 7 days. Along with the games, the auction winner also receives a certificate from the Guinness Book of Records.
