The main concept of the simulator remained - no registration, went to the site, and you can immediately print, it works in all popular operating systems and browsers (including with Russian text).

The basis for the design, proposed in the autumn by olegkrasnov .

Added new modes and dictionaries, such as:
- initial training;
- training (this mode is a copy of the previous version, here are new texts);
- study of foreign words (a phrase and five variants of translation are given, it is necessary to print one correct one);
- training printing numbers (digital series, and a digital unit);

The image of the keyboard has become more functional, prompts have appeared which key which finger to press.
