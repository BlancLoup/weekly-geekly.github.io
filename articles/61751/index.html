Latest information about Max Payne 3



In the latest issue of Game Informer game magazine published exclusive material about Max Payne 3 , the continuation of the misadventures of the policeman who lost his family.

The most interesting of what is published in the article:

- The game takes place in the city of Sao Paulo, in Brazil.
- Most of the screenshots in the article show the ghetto in Sao Paulo (which is very similar to Resident Evil 5)
- The action takes place 12 years after the events of the second part. Max has visibly aged.
- Bullet-time is still in the game, but in the gameplay it will be used somewhat differently. Also present is the kinetic animation and the cover system from GTA IV.
