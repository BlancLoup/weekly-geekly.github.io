The humble voxatron debut

Humble Bundle again pleases us with its sale .




This time, only one game is involved in the sale, but which one. Voxatron is an old school shooter, in a new wrapper. Confusingly, but the developers write about it like this. The main feature of the game is the voxel engine. For those who do not know, voxels are analogous to pixels for three dimensional space. As a result, the world turns out, consisting of a set of color cubes.

More clearly:



In addition to the main gameplay, the game includes a level editor. Everyone can create their own little voxel world.

The rules of the sale are the same. Pay as much as you appreciate the work of developers. It is worth noting that this is the world debut of the game. In the future, the developers promise free updates for everyone who purchased the game.
