Surface RT live

10 days ago I pre-ordered a Surface RT 32GB with a red keyboard. Two hours ago, UPS courier handed me a package. Since I'm still playing with the tablet in the office, here are some short pictures taken on the phone and on the tablet itself. At home I will try to take pictures of better optics.




First impressions:
In the hands is excellent, weighty, monolithic. The surface does not bend, it does not go to a kink, but if you press it down, it crunches. The body looks strict, pleasant to the touch, fingerprints remain. The keyboard is slightly larger in size, and therefore protrudes above the case. The texture is pleasant, smooth, to the touch like a vinyl book cover. The screen is just lovely. It does not glare, you can safely use the street in the sun, the colors are bright and juicy.

Works smartly, does not slow down. Makes a very pleasant impression.
