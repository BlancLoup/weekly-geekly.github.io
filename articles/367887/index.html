From the anime the road flies by or what to see on the way to vacation



August is the sunset of summer and the last chance to get on vacation (by the way, in August Karelia is especially good). It's time to decide on the type of recreation - in a hotel, a tent or at the cottage - and collect things, download useful applications , buy all sorts of gadgets . And in order not to be bored on the road, you need to attend to some kind of entertainment and food for the mind. For example, take along an interesting book . But sometimes you want a break from reading and rest your brain. Usually, here the hand stretches to launch the next series or the full-length movie, but still do not rush: the light did not converge on the cinema. There is another genre that can give no less pleasure - anime.

Fans do not need to explain what it is. But if you are now twisted and remembered "Sailor Moon" and "Pokemon", then do not rush to condemn: the anime is as diverse as the cinema. In this genre there are both very deep and serious full-length films and serials, as well as completely stupid and passable crafts. Just on TV it is almost impossible to watch good and high-quality anime, so many have formed a prejudice regarding Japanese animation. And yet we recommend to get acquainted with this genre on the way to hot countries, to the mountains or to the village.
