Invisible File Mystery



Not so long ago, having finished work on the next article for Habr, I decided to add it to my friend's review. After saving the HTML page with the whole environment (pictures, styles, etc), I packed it into a ZIP archive and sent it to the addressee. Within five minutes I received feedback, which, contrary to my expectations, was connected not with the article itself, but with the fact that the archive was absolutely empty. After scratching my head and deciding that I was blunted with archiving, I repeated the procedure, making sure to select all the files needed for packing. A few minutes later, an acquaintance again broke out surprised, ‚ÄúAre you kidding me?‚Äù While I was not joking at all.
