HungryBread - a startup about a lunch in a pleasant company

Hello to Habra and his inhabitants!

I want to talk about startup Hungry Bread , dedicated to business lunches.

The meaning of the project is to make a business lunch not just a meal in the middle of the day, but a pleasant event filled with new impressions.

First, we help choose a place for lunch. For this we have a detailed Moscow business lunch card.

Secondly, we think that lunch is a great occasion for new acquaintances. And the more dating, the fuller life. Therefore, we have made our project in the form of a social network. On our site you can leave your offer for lunch or respond to an existing offer.

And it is also nice to have lunch with an old friend, who, it turns out, works nearby, and you have not seen him for a hundred years.

Add each other as a friend and dine together at your convenience. You can even create a calendar of pleasant lunches for the week ahead.

We hope that our service will help someone to find a soul mate, someone - new friends or useful acquaintances for business.

Can't wait to get to know you all at business lunches.

We don't have a mobile version yet, it only makes sense to go from the desktop.

How is the site


On the "Restaurants" page there is a map with restaurants where there is a business lunch. See new places near your work, or find lunches where you are hungry.



On the "Lunches" page - a map of already created lunches, to which you can respond. Look at the lunches near you - these are people who are ready to dine. Create your lunch and it will appear on this map.

How to find where to eat


Specify in the search your coordinates, lunch time and how much you are willing to pay for it.



And choose what you like best. You can view detailed information about the restaurant - user comments, phone and a link to the site.
