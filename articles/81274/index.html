Takara Launches Nostalgic Gamers Gadget

I think most habrovchan remembers these Soviet toys " Electronics ", where you had to help the wolf to collect eggs, repel asteroids from the surface of the planet and something else, I already do not remember that. So, probably, you have already heard that these games are plagiarism from similar devices of the Nintendo company, which appeared in 1981. Yes, that's right - in the continuation there is a photo proving such an assertion (and there is nothing to prove, the Nintendo device appeared much earlier than the domestic device).



But it is not about who stole what or from whom. The fact is that the little-known Japanese company Takara decided to release their old devices, especially for nostalgic gamers. The only difference from the old games will be the size and availability of the solar battery.

Three versions of this gaming device will appear on the market - Octopus, Parachute and Chef. The size of all three - pocket, in fact it is key chains, but what! Below is a photo and video of the updated gaming device from Takara. More photos can be found at this link (carefully, Japanese hieroglyphs!).
