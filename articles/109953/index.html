3D Find.ru
Gennady Koveshnikov

The 3D Find.ru project aims to make life easier for people who are closely connected with 3D models. We provide fast and convenient search in the database of models, and also give the opportunity to quickly download them. One of the main features of our service is that we are trying to go as far as possible to the concept of web 2.0, i.e. social web. Our community can influence the results. To do this, we have created 2 methods of regulation - rating models and editing tags. We also provide various ways to display the results. For example, you can display results by date added, rating, or number of downloads.
