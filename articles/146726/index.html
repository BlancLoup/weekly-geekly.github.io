Google Chrome comes to iPhone and iPad

Today at the Google I / O conference the news was announced - the Chrome browser is designed to work on iOS. The interface has been redesigned according to the style of the Android version, there is support for synchronization functions. It will be available in the AppStore today or tomorrow in Moscow.
It's unclear whether this is a regular add-on with buns on Safari or a full-featured Chrome with split processes, Google V8 Engine and its own WebKit.
