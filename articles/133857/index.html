Imagine Cup Meeting in Novosibirsk


Microsoft partner students in Novosibirsk, together with the Lavrentiev Breakthrough Foundation and the Youth Council of the Soviet District, with the support of the NSTU student business incubator, invite students and graduate students to the Imagine Cup Meeting , which will be held on December 10 at the NSTU (Novosibirsk State Technical University)

No matter you are a freshman, or a graduate. Technician or humanities. Boy or girl If you want to
  • broaden your horizons
  • chat with interesting people
  • get to the note to the largest world companies
  • save the world from disaster 2012
  • and go summer in Australia
Then competition Imagine Cup for you.

  • You've heard about the Imagine Cup a long time ago, but you are not confident in your abilities
  • You have not heard of the Imagine Cup, but you want to do something meaningful
  • You already have a project, but you do not know how profitable it is to submit it to the competition.
  • Do you have a team but no idea
  • You know how to save the world, but you need people to help
Then come to the Imagine Cup Meeting!

What awaits you?

We will help you form teams;
share with you the experience of participating in the competition;
show you how to program Kinect and Windows Phone 7
let's tell what the judges pay attention to;
show you what you can add to the project;
We will tell you how to live with it further. Especially for you, we invited the winners of the Russian final of the Imagine Cup and the participants of the international final in New York - the Oricrafter team. In the morning, Moscow time, they will share their experience with you. The finalists of the Russian finals of the past years will also be present at the event.
Prior registration is required. (you do not want you to get the buns?)

Program

10:00 - 10:30 registration
10:30 - 12:00 presentations (technical, advisory), performance by Oricrafter
12:00 - 14:00 division into teams, work in teams, brainstorming
14:00 - 15:00 lunch (independently, at the request of the participants)
15:00 - 16:30 continuation of work in teams
16:30 - 18:15 project presentations
18:15 - 18:30 counting results, coffee break
18:30 - 19:30 solemn end of the event. Award
