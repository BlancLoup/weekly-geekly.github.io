Google hunting season is open

Hi, habrabrattsy. We are new, but we already have a great desire to declare ourselves and get a decent feedback. Today we will talk about how to challenge the most advanced IT companies of our time, and for the year to build a foundation in the form of a search engine Lazerto .



Briefly about the product
Lazerto is a slash tag safe search engine that enables everyone to create their own thematic searches and simplify life using smart teams (convenient and fast alternatives to modern search services).

Search
For starters in Lazerto, you can just search, try, it's not scary. Over the year we have done so that our issuance is not inferior to the results of the largest search giants, and often surpasses them.

Algorithm
The technology is a meta-search on Google, Yandex and Bing in combination with our algorithm. Simply put, we took the issuance of the three best search engines and figured out how to mix them qualitatively and give the most relevant results.

Slashtags
Slashtag is a combination of the "/" sign and the words after it, which gives an order to the search line to do something. There are 2 types of slash tags : Thematic tags and Smart commands .

  • Smart team . We gave people access to useful and simple services immediately through the search box. For example, you can quickly translate a phrase like this: " We want you to be active when commenting us / translate ", and listen and download music like this: " Let it be / music ". A list of all Smart commands can be found by entering the slashtag / commands .
  • Subject tags . We gave everyone the opportunity to quickly create their own thematic search, thereby narrowing the desired area only to high-quality resources, which will increase the relevance of the issue. You can also use the Thematic tag as a search on one site. Register, call your slash tag, add sites that interest you and go ahead. The list of already existing subject tags is available by slash tag.

Briefly about the future
Search is just the foundation. We will create a whole ecosystem that will allow each person to access all useful services on the Internet, having only one account, absolutely free.
