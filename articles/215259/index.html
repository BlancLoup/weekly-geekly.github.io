Telecom operators and providers of Ukraine. Some statistics




Not so long ago, we set out to carry out a personalized mailing by paper mail to all telecom operators and providers of Ukraine with information about their SAP UserSide . This is an .xls-file with data on enterprises - communication operators. It contains the name, contact information, types of licensed activities, the territory of the activity. The register is updated after each meeting of the NKRZI. Actually, for sending, we just needed the names of the companies and their addresses.

However, in the process of processing the document, quite specific ideas emerged about systematization of this document and the implementation of a certain statistical slice about communication operators in Ukraine.

As of February 25, 2014 There were 3,938 entries in the registry. One entry corresponds to one enterprise. If an enterprise has several licenses in the field of telecom (several types of services or territories of activity) , then this will be simply added to the current record of the enterprise.

Of them:
  • 82 records of excluded businesses
  • 1620 entries do not have instructions on the address and territory of work and were of little use for accounting. The data on these enterprises, most likely, were entered when the register still had a different form and did not imply specifics. A long time ago.


There are 2236 records left to process.

Although, according to the administrative division of Ukraine, the cities of Kiev and Sevastopol have a special status - I included them in the Kiev region and the Crimea, respectively - for ease of displaying data.

In the course of information processing, the fact was noted that quite often at the same legal address there are two entries - about two different license holders. As a rule, in order to optimize taxation, telecom operators receive a license for a legal entity and for themselves (director / founder) as a SPD-FL (a business entity is an individual. In Russia, an Individual entrepreneur) .
