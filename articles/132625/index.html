Video Controller DEPO Race ReVision. Video wall of 24 ultra-high resolution panels

DEPO Computers has developed a solution for building video walls from plasma or LCD panels.

DEPO Race ReVision video controllers provide the user with a wide range of options for customizing desktops. Configurations include: shared desktop mode, desktop cloning, independent desktops, and combined mode. In addition to the ability to display internal content, video controllers are equipped with the function of capturing video over IP, via DVI input, as well as with VGA, S-Video, Component and Composite devices.

Each video output has a maximum resolution of 2560x1600 pixels. Therefore, when combining all panels into a single desktop, the final resolution will be summed from the resolutions of each panel. The video controller is based on a powerful fault-tolerant workstation.


DEPO Race ReVision M16

DEPO Race ReVision M16 is a professional video controller for building video walls of any configuration. Up to 16 video outputs are supported with a resolution of 2560x1600 for the Display Port and 1920x1200 for DVI.

The video controller is made in a 4U case, it can be installed both in a 19 ‚Äùrack and on the legs. It has a dual power supply 650W with hot-swappable. Server disks are combined in RAID-1. The model is equipped with an Intel Xeon W3670 processor and RAM with error correction function.



The video controller supports ample opportunities in setting up desktops. The image for example shows one of the possible configurations. Here are two independent panels on top, six panels combined into a single desktop on the bottom, and a process control monitor on the right.




This illustration shows one of the bookmarks management software.




Here are the possibilities for customizing desktops: independent screens, combining panels into a single desktop and dividing one panel into several desktops. In the settings there is a choice of how to open windows "full screen" - either on the entire desktop, or on the entire physical panel. There is also a choice of the main panel and the place to open new windows by default.




This example shows the frame compensation tool. This feature is useful when watching videos. The image that falls on the junction of the panels is not moved apart, but is cut to the thickness of the frame. Thus, the picture is solid, and the objects of the correct proportions.



DEPO Race ReVision M16 video controllers allow you to create video walls for situational centers, presentations and information boards.

DEPO Race ReVision S8

Professional video controller with the function of capturing from external sources a digital / analog video stream and displaying it on multi-screens.



The image display system allows you to combine up to 8 plasma or LCD panels in any rectangular configuration into a single desktop. Connection is made via DVI-I, each video output supports a resolution of 1920x1200.

The image capture system allows you to transfer a video stream received from external sources to the video wall. Supports DVI, VGA, S-Video, Composite, Component capture. The ability to capture up to 8 Full HD video streams via DVI allows you to combine up to 8 high-performance video servers into a single system of control and monitoring of the situation.

A high level of performance is provided by the Intel Xeon W3600 processor, a large amount of RAM with error correction and powerful professional video adapters. High reliability is guaranteed by high-quality component base and backup technologies of vital system nodes.
The high performance of the controller, along with the capture, also allows you to use it for the local solution of complex resource-intensive tasks.

DEPO Race ReVision S24

Functionally similar to the DEPO Race ReVision S8 model. The main difference is the support for up to 24 I / O. The video controller is based on a dual-processor platform with support for Intel Xeon X5600 processors and 96GB of RAM with the ECC function. The disk subsystem includes up to 8 hot-swappable server disks combined in RAID-5 to ensure smooth operation.



Thus, using the video controller DEPO Race ReVision S24, you can build a video wall with a total resolution of 11520x4800.
