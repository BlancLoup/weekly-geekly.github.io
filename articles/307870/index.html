Who is to blame and what to do if an IT person is bored at work. Poll results

Recently, we conducted a survey among IT specialists to find out if they are bored at work and why this is happening. As we thought, most specialists do not know from time to time what to do, and at the same time think about how to avoid it. Many are ready to stay at their current place of work, more than 80% do not want to become heads, but want to develop professionally. Of these, almost 10% see themselves in the field of Big Data and Data Science.
