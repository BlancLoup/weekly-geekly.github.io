Beautiful cars: Lexus LX570 in New York

Lexus unveiled a new generation of premium SUV Leuxs LX570 at the New York International Auto Show.

The new Lexus has exceeded all expectations. It has everything: more torque, more airbags, more cameras, more electric seats, more lotions for a good and easy life - in general, most of all. The car is equipped with the latest V-shaped 8-cylinder engine with a power of 381 hp, which is 110 hp more than the previous version of this SUV, and the maximum torque of the 5.7 liter engine exceeds 55 kgm. In addition, as standard Lexus LX570 is equipped with sequential six-speed transmission. To improve the aerodynamic properties of the machine, the motor was set lower by about 3 centimeters.

LX570 is equipped with the latest hydraulic suspension with active height control system, as well as all-wheel drive. Smart suspension will compensate for the vibration of the irregularities, while at the same time it can independently set the clearance of the car depending on the nature of the road, by adapting it to specific conditions and situations.

- Just a beautiful car, what can I say. At the same time, a lesson to all car designers who design a new generation of well-established cars. The lesson is how not to import, but on the contrary, make the new generation better.
