New NTC Training Center Office - Check Point Training Center

On March 12, a solemn opening of the new office of the NTC Training Center (Network Training Center) - Check Point Certified Training Center in Russia, took place in Moscow. New location of the training center - BC Golden Gate (Blvd Entuziastov, d. 2). At the end of the article is a small gift for our readers.



Why is it necessary and important to be trained in certified centers?


  1. integrated learning approach (CCSA, CCSE, CCSM);
  2. official training materials from the vendor;
  3. certificate of attendance;
  4. preparation for the certification exam;
  5. high demand in the labor market.

Can I study on my own?


Yes, learning from your mistakes is a very good method! But not every company is willing to pay for such errors, especially when it comes to information security. If a specialist is incompetent, then the company loses money twice:
