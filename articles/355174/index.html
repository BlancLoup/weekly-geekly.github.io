3D tours of the Olympic Palaces

The official website of the Winter Olympics in Korea is actively using 3D to visualize sports facilities, terrain and infrastructure.



Conducting the Olympics is a big burden and a serious responsibility for the organizing state. Dozens of sports facilities, support support structures and hundreds of thousands of guests who arrive in an unknown country. Huge help is a variety of information sources on the venues of sports events, where the main component is the official website of the Olympiad. It offers all sorts of maps, tips, timetables, photos, and even 3D. Here's the last and talk.

The main group of informational data is concentrated on the Venues page , where you can select the desired sports object and study the interactive map of the area with schematic symbols of buildings. Also, next to it is easy to find and the button "3D Preview".



For almost all key sports complexes, there are 3D presentations that are made using mixed technology, where three-dimensional objects are adjacent to three-dimensional photos. This allows you to realistically transfer the original appearance of the terrain without a special load on the users' equipment. And this is a definite plus. A regular map is just a schematic drawing, unable to convey the urban beauty of the landscape. Another thing - volumetric immersion in the landscapes of Olympic facilities. This is a fascinating three-dimensional journey, where you can not only understand the location on the ground, but also appreciate the architecture of the complexes .
