The mobile operator is unable to prevent the issuance of duplicate SIM cards to attackers

On the Internet, the story with repeated issuance of a duplicate of the same SIM card to attackers and the subsequent theft of funds is gaining momentum.

In short, the fraudsters, using a false power of attorney, issued a duplicate SIM card of the victim, and then, using a duplicate, stole a considerable amount of money from various mobile and bank accounts. Such cases have certainly occurred before. The highlight of the same situation is that this time the well-known and high-ranking person became the victim of the scam, and a duplicate of the SIM card was issued several times a day in different offices of the operator (and different cities).
