Video from Application Developer Days 2010

I am pleased to announce that a substantial part of the video reports has been published on the Application Developer Days 2010 conference site . Almost all performances are synchronized with screencasts, so you can immediately see both the speaker and his presentation. Stas Fomin ( belonesox ) personally performed all this enormous work, for which we are all immensely grateful to him.

I note some particularly interesting speeches:
  • Elena Sagalaeva " Artificial Intelligence in Games "
  • Andrei Bibichev " On the threshold of augmented reality: what developers should prepare for "
  • Maxim Lapshin " Development of video hosting on Erlang "
  • Nikita Frolov " How to start developing for the iPhone "
  • Dmitry Zavalishin " Phantom-OS "
  • Anton Ovchinnikov " Money and internal clock of the company-developer "
In total, three dozen videos have already been published and the work continues. Reports for which videos have already been uploaded are marked with an icon in the general list of reports .

We hope that it will be interesting both for those who could not come to the conference, and for those who were. After all, to visit all performances live there was no physical ability.

See you in spring in St. Petersburg at Application Developer Days 2011 !
