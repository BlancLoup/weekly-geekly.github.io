Yii extensions for working with QIWI payment service

The customer wished to integrate a certain service with the QIWI payment system.
Since, to add their own button to their terminal, the service clearly did not reach, it was decided to use a personalized QIWI wallet (this is not attached to the mobile phone number).
Since at that time I was studying the Yii framework, it was first decided to look for a ready-made extension. Well, since the search did not produce results, it was decided to write his own.
After examining all the interaction possibilities provided by the service, namely: HTTP protocol, XML protocol and SOAP protocol (QIWI service terms), SOAP was chosen as the most convenient.
You can read more about the QIWI SOAP service in this document.

I want to note that this is my first experience writing extensions for Yii.

Under the cut you will find the source code of the module itself (without spare parts from QIWI, see the links at the bottom of the article).
