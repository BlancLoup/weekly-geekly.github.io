Yahoo! Video Player Music reached the third version

Yahoo! Music announced the release of a new beta version of the player Video 3.0. TechCrunch bloggers decided to share screenshots of the novelty with the whole Internet and also talk about some of its features.



Video 3.0 will work on Macintosh computers and be compatible with Mozilla Firefox browser. In addition, the quality of video playback has increased, the ability to manage the playback queue has been added, the recommendation function has been implemented, which can advise the user to download a particular clip corresponding to his tastes.
