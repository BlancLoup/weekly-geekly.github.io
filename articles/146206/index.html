Smart Start has received investments from Softline Venture Partners

Today is a very important day for us, today we finally announced a deal with Softline Venture Partners, who invested a certain amount in us, thanks to which our project grows and develops.

We are a Smart Start team. Smart Start is an application in social networks for finding work for young professionals. At the moment, the application is integrated into the social network VKontakte. In April, the first version of the service was launched, but it turned out to be crude and did not meet expectations.
Therefore, today launched the second version of the application, which reflects the new design and features for our users. The service will collect offers of starting positions, training internships in Russia and abroad, temporary work, professional internships for young people, and volunteer positions will be presented. Now we have more than 100 clients, vacancies and internships of which can be found in the application.
The service allows the user to use his profile data to start a career, which makes it easier for employers and young professionals to communicate.
