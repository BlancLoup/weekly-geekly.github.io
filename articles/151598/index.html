The crippled bald eagle has received a new beak printed on a 3D printer



It is pleasant to hear that high technologies come to the aid not only of people, but also of animals, in particular, birds. So, the story of the bald eagle Beauty, which received a new beak, created on a 3D printer, was sold out on the Web the other day. Specialists had to make a lot of effort to create such a specific prosthesis, but everything ended very well.

By the way, a whole team of specialists, including engineers, scientists and dentists, worked on creating the prosthesis. It is worth noting that this is the first such prosthetic beak in the world, so that the rescue team of the sea eagle can be pleased. The material of the prosthesis - nylon derivatives.
