New Sony Mobile at MWC 2015

Hello! It's time for announcements! We are glad to inform you that at the MWC 2015 exhibition, which is now taking place in Barcelona right now, Sony Mobile presented the following innovations: an updated Xperia Z4 Tablet and a protected middle-class smartphone Xperia M4 Aqua.





Xperia z4 tablet

Let's start with large forms, namely with the tablet. Xperia Z4 Tablet can be called the thinnest and lightest 10.1-inch tablet in the world. Its thickness is only 6.1 mm, and its weight is 389 g (and 392 for the LTE version). In addition, the Z4 Tablet received an extremely bright display with a resolution of 2560x1600, an eight-core 64-bit Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor, an Adreno 430 video accelerator and a 6000 mAh battery, which lasts for 17 hours of video playback. In addition, the tablet is equipped with a wide-angle 5-megapixel front camera, which can be successfully used not only for video communications, but also for high-quality self-portraits. Like the previous models, Z4 Tablet is protected from dust and moisture in accordance with the standard IP68. The tablet will go on sale in July 2015, the possible price has not yet called.

Xperia m4 aqua

Another update came up for the middle class - Xperia M4 Aqua, which took over some of the features of the current flagship Xperia Z3, for example - a rounded frame with polycarbonate inserts that protect against impact and protection class IP68. Eight-core Snapdragon 615 and 2 GB of RAM are responsible for performance, a 2400 mAh battery allows the device to work without recharging for up to two days (in normal mode), and a 5.2-inch display with an IPS-matrix and a resolution of 720p provides excellent image quality. Among the notable innovations, it is worth noting also the micro-USB connector without a plug (it is protected from water by the inner membrane, so now you can not worry about the plug loosening, will be lost or lose its tightness), 5-megapixel wide-angle front camera for selfi and Android 5.0 operating system.
