The series Doctor Who turned 50 years old

All of time and space
or ever will - where do you want to start?



Today, the 50th anniversary celebrates the longest sci-fi series in the world - Doctor Who.

Doctor Who is a British science fiction series about the adventures of a mysterious time and space traveler who calls himself a Doctor. Moving in a small blue booth and visiting the most diverse places with his companions, the Doctor at the same time eliminates injustice wherever he finds it.

The unique features of the series, for which he still has crowds of fans around the world, were laid at the very beginning, back in 1963:

  • A huge universe. The creators chose as a place of action all the time and space, almost without limiting the imagination of the writers.
    In addition, changing the actor who played the main character, the creators did not make him look like the previous one either externally or by character.   • Focus on the family audience. For all its scale, the series is very kind. The doctor does not shoot crowds of enemies from a laser cannon, does not tolerate weapons, and copes only with his bare hands, sound screwdriver and inhuman arrogance.
