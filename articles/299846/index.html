Online store from scratch. Part 1: Getting Started

First of all, this article is intended for those who are planning or are only considering the option of starting their own business.
Looking back over 15 years experience in IT with business processes, I decided to write this manual. This will be a series of articles, as the material goes voluminous.

The article aims to warn beginner businessmen and, perhaps, will be useful to those who have long been working in the field of Internet commerce, since the proposed model is easily scaled with minor changes and amendments to the volume of trade.



Open to constructive criticism and will be happy to assist if you need it.

The article contains the names of some products and companies, I do not give links as it can be perceived as an advertisement. All products that I specify are work experience and are not advertising.
I would be glad if you share your work with these or other services.

I will not touch on what product you should sell or what services are worth providing. This choice is yours. We take the axiom that the product is already selected and the profit (initial) is calculated.



Choosing a CMS for an online store

I will give some tips on choosing the engine for the store. I personally like PrestaShop, but it is difficult for beginners, and it has a big disadvantage, there is no normal bundle with 1C for, I stress, acceptable money.

There is a fraudulent module 1c-prestashop (they have several sites), beware of these guys, read the reviews.


Working solutions are very expensive for starting a business. Therefore, be sure to choose a CMS for your store with a working bundle with 1C or other accounting systems. Before launching, be sure to test the upload and download of data.

The most common ERP we have 1C, so I am building this article to work with it, but, in principle, everything is relevant for other systems.

In addition to the functionality when choosing a CMS, pay attention to the availability of responsive design for mobile platforms.

Alert customers

SMS: Any self-respecting store keeps its customers informed about the order stages via e-mal, SMS and telephone.
We will have an SMS notification from the CMS store, when ordering, and also from 1C with further changes to the order (order is issued, payment received, transferred to the transport company (sent), arrived at the place of delivery, etc.) order status, and We will also look at the alert scripts in detail.

Email: the easiest way to use one of the free mail providers: Yandex or Google. You should bind your domain to one of them and create the required number of mailboxes. It's free. Detailed instructions are on their sites.

Calculate Shipping Cost

One of the difficulties in online trading, it is correct to calculate the full cost of delivery based on the customer's order, the price includes: insurance for the purchased goods; shipping cost from the transport company itself or, more often, Russian Post (calculated on the basis of the weight and size of the parcel, as well as the delivery address); the cost of packaging materials (cardboard, bubble film and others); the cost of the packaging work (man-hours spent on packaging); delivery of parcels to the terminal of the transport company or post office.

We impose all this cost on the buyer directly, that is, he sees the cost of delivery when ordering or indirectly lays in the price of the goods in full (free shipping) or in part.

We must measure and weigh each item. Otherwise, you can not calculate the shipping cost.
