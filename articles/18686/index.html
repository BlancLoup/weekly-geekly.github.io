Dreamcatcher

Hello! Already without any competition, I want to introduce you to the project I was waiting for about five years, and I almost started writing myself. This is probably the first social network for dreaming Dreamcatcher in runet. In contrast to other similar projects (somn.ru), Dreamcatcher has a flexible system for storing and statistics of our dreams.


Anyone will probably ask the question: why should I write down dreams, when I already write down life on my blog? In fact, this occupation with far-reaching consequences. Dreaming notes will help identify their patterns, increase their brightness and recall, as well as develop a special kind of memory and, finally, recall all our dreams that have ever dreamed of. More details about all this can be found on the Internet, or in the books of Hackers of Dreams.

Given all this, the usefulness of Dreamcatcher is difficult to overestimate. The project is ideally specialized for recording dreams and analyzing them.
