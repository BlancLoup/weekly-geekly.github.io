In Cloud. RF - electronic cloud computing journal



It would seem that quite recently, working with data without attracting your own servers, purchased or rented specifically for these purposes, was something from the realm of fantasy. But time does not stand still, and with it - the desire of a person to optimize workflows. So, among other innovations, cloud services appeared. Thanks to the convenience and ease of use, they have allowed companies to facilitate the work with various services. However, like any innovation, cloud technologies brought with them a lot of questions.

What do modern cloud services face when developing and selling their services? How are cloud technologies being introduced into small and medium business in Russia? What can an enterprise face when transferring its business processes to the cloud area? How to ensure the safety and reliability of personal data storage? Answer these and many other questions, learn about the experience of successful implementation of cloud technologies in the existing business and correctly navigate the world of clouds you can thanks to our new publication - the magazine "In the Cloud. RF".

"In the Cloud. RF" - the first specialized electronic journal in Russian, dedicated to cloud technologies and services.
Our magazine will help:
  • leader - to navigate in the world of cloud environments;
  • a specialist - get the maximum of useful and interesting information about global and private issues of designing, creating and effectively using cloud services;
  • Any interested reader - learn about events, trends and innovations in the cloud world, new services and technologies.


The magazine will be distributed primarily as an application for mobile devices (tablets and smartphones) and will be available in the online stores App Store and Google Play.
In October, the first issue of the magazine comes out, the theme of which is CRM cloud services - one of the most modern solutions, without which the full-fledged work of any business is impossible. In the first issue of the publication: an overview of the most advanced systems integrated with a large number of other cloud services; interview with the director of public services and products of the company "Servionika" and many other interesting and useful information.

"In the Cloud. RF" will help you: to understand the flow of rapidly developing technologies, to determine the choice of the service you need, to find out all the pros and cons of various solutions.
