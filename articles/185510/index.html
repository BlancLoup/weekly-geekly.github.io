Homemade tracking parcels, or there and back

Surely many who ordered goods from abroad, wondered: what happens with the packages from export to import? In what otherworldly space do they sometimes spend the months of their journey? What planes do departures fly to Novosibirsk by airmail to Moscow?

Idea


See how the parcels from abroad move to Moscow, in more detail than the official track. To do this, send a device capable of communicating your coordinates.

A friend of mine sent by post to another friend of mine ...



Implementation


It is necessary to choose a device capable of reporting its coordinates on a schedule and having autonomous power for at least a month of such work.

Smartphone

The most obvious solution.
Pros: you can program almost any behavior, for example, use precise positioning on satellites, measure acceleration, light, temperature, and what else, and immediately report to Twitter.
Cons: without an external battery will not live and the delivery time of international mail , and with it weighs a lot and attracts attention. What if it lingers on the way?

Homemade ‚Äúsmartphone‚Äù

What if you take a battery from a laptop (or car, depending on the projected delivery time), GSM module, ARM processor, GPS chip, put Ivan Kuzmich on the nose ... Such a device has all the advantages of a smartphone, and of the minuses only unusual the appearance (will they be received at the post office?) and the likely price of development and production. Perhaps this will be the next experiment, but for now we have decided to limit ourselves to a simpler version.

Pros: all the advantages of a smartphone, a long battery life, the possibility of disguise, for example, under a brick.
Disadvantages: unusual appearance (will they be received at the post office?) And, probably, price + development and production time.

It should be noted that the included devices with inserted batteries are generally reluctant to receive the shipment. Although all tablets and other iPhones are just such devices.

The best option

How much to reduce battery capacity requirements? To communicate less often (being in a deep sleep most of the trip), and use less accurate positioning (over the cellular network, without using GPS / Glonass satellites).

Pros: geolocation on the honeycomb, even inside an iron car or hangar inaccessible to GPS, long life with a small battery.
Disadvantages: low positioning accuracy compared to GPS, low measurement frequency.

And there is such a device that does not attract too much attention!

On your marks!


There was a ‚Äútraveling‚Äù Philips E102 phone in the table, honored, not working for a long time, but satisfying all the requirements: a super-capacious 650mAh battery, the ability to work with one SIM card, and also to turn on or off up to four times a day. This key feature is rarely found in the description of the device.

We chose the mode: two communication sessions per day for fifteen minutes (with a margin for network search). If just in standby mode, the phone worked for five days, then the maximum could be expected 48 times longer, or eight months. These figures will be different due to poor reception, the cost of switching on and searching for the network, possible frosts, as well as battery self-discharge (about 3% per month on Wikipedia). In general, there was a good chance that the batteries would last for the duration of the journey.

As part of preparing the phone for launch, it was disassembled and reanimated - the contacts of the power button were cleaned in it, which oxidized and did not allow it to turn on.

If the phone could write to the LAC and Cell ID logs of the connection in order to subsequently process and obtain the location of the cells by their identifiers, then it would be a grandfather, we could build a displacement map after arrival (if this happens). The phone could not do this, and it was decided to use an external service to control its location. Yes, and online tracking adds interest.

At the station, a SIM card was purchased, which was added to the trusted list of the MTS search for another number from which the tracking took place. MTS-locator is able to determine the location of the subscriber on a schedule - what is needed for this project! The only drawback is that the service operates only in Russia, so the overseas part of the way cannot be traced.


Similar services of Beeline-Locator and Megafon-Locator have other cellular operators

Right before sending the phone was charged and checked silently on and off on a schedule twice a day. Included also the most economical mode. All settings:
  • inclusion at 7:50 and 19:50
  • shutdown at 8:05 and 20:05
  • minimum ring volume, no vibration
  • minimum backlight brightness
  • backlight time is five seconds
  • disabled greeting sounds


The phone in the opaque box could only be unmasked by a non-time SMS or a call (assuming that the enemy does not know how to find us on the radio). This phone model does not know how to turn off all signals, either a vibrating alert or a melody remains, but there are several ways:
  • A admin: enable SMS ban and incoming operator
  • W a damn: send without turning off the signals, which was done
  • On the ordinary: download a silent mp3 or midi signal via wap
  • Iron: unsolder vibrating alert or pull out the speaker (for this Philips it is not soldered and can be easily removed when disassembling)


One fine day (hereinafter - day 1) in one resort town the phone was packed in a box, the postal workers, upon request to remove the battery, received assurances that it was turned off (true) and discharged (not true), and sent by air mail to Moscow.

Journey


Arrival at Sheremetyevo

Three days after sending, the parcel disappeared from the Ukrainian trekking (in the direction of the international mailing service of Ukraine for the interiors of Ukraine. Dyakoumo, scored by our servants), and soon appeared on the radar!

Tour of Russia

Already on the ninth day, the phone suddenly moved, but not to its destination, but began to steadily move away from Moscow, judging by the points - the airmail gave way to the railway mail, and on the 13th day - the post tortoise did. At first, we thought that the point near Vladimir was a positioning error (how this service works), then that sorting in the neighboring region is cheaper than in Moscow, finally they were betting whether the package would reach Baikal, the Pacific Ocean, or go home to China.

A major stop happened in Novosibirsk, where the phone had lain for up to 29 days, regularly communicating twice a day. However, this is not a record delivery time, and we hoped that the batteries would last until the end. Only one communication session did not take place (did the postal pigeon eat up or was there no cellular coverage?), The configured schedule on the search ended once, and once the ‚Äúlocator‚Äù service was paid.
Perhaps during this time the phone made several more flights by airmail ?

On the thirtieth day, the first record of Russian tracking appeared (Import 630982 Novosibirsk PI-2) , the parcel successfully passed customs, and on the thirty-fifth - Processing 630982 Novosibirsk PI-2 left the place of international exchange - the phone went back home. Five more days later, he ended up at the Kazan railway station, and never left Moscow again.
