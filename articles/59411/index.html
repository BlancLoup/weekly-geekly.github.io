Explosive Video: Beyond Good and Evil 2 Gameplay

A video clip appeared on Youtube that allegedly shows us the Beyond Good and Evil 2 action / adventure gameplay. The minute video shows a heroine amazingly similar to Jade, who is running away from armed pursuers. Most of all, the game resembles a mixture of Mirror's Edge and Assassin's Creed. It looks absolutely incredible, so you can be a little skeptical of this information. Perhaps this is another fan duck.

If this is all true, and BG & E 2 really looks so beautiful, then one of the best games in the world is waiting for us. It is worth noting that the original Beyond Good and Evil did not become particularly commercially successful, but earned a lot of positive feedback from many critics and fans. It's nice that Ubisoft decided to finance the release of the sequel to a financially disastrous masterpiece.

From myself: The video is of course elegant, the main thing is not to spoil the atmosphere of the first part and not to turn the game into a clone of Assassin's Creed
