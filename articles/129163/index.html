Short cut for the smartest: Glavstart now invests weekly

Startup Weekend is an excellent tool to refine your idea within two days with the help of experts attending the event and after receiving the final presentation to receive $ 100,000 of investments from Glavstart.

Weekend is still an excellent tool for hundreds and even thousands (yes, more than a thousand participants were registered on Moscow last weekend) young ideas to grow to the level of projects. But lately, we have begun to notice that ideas are already emerging that require not very significant polishing in order to be able to proceed with their direct implementation. We first invested the first project on the results of Skype interviews and a personal meeting in August, the next project in September, and at the last Siberian Weekend we saw and invested another project in a good readiness stage.

Therefore, we decided to present a chance for the smartest - not to wait for the next Weekend in order to present us our idea. We are now ready to invest weekly in new projects that are at a sufficient stage of development.

The process is organized very simple:
  • You need to send a description of your project to idea@glavstart.ru
  • You have a chance to get an invitation to Skype interview with Arkady Moreynis, which will now be held weekly
  • According to the results of Skype-interviews, the authors of the projects can receive an invitation from Arcadia to conduct weekly personal interviews to the Glavstart office, non-resident authors Glavstart in this case will pay the way to Moscow
  • According to the results of the interview, Arkady will be ready to make a decision on investing in the project - in this way Glavstart switches to the mode of providing investment on a weekly basis.

We will advise the authors of all projects that have not received an invitation for an interview to participate in the next Startup Weekend, which will be held in the city closest to you - since your idea requires a more lengthy and diverse discussion and refinement.

The next Skype interview with Arkady Moreynis will take place on September 30th . In the coming days, if you send a description of your project to idea@glavstart.ru, then you have a chance to take part in a Skype interview.

For ideas we have an address - idea@glavstart.ru
For good ideas, we have $ 100,000
