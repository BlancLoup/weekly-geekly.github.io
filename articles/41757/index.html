Kindle 2?

According to yet unverified data, the Amazon online store is preparing to release the second incarnation of its Kindle e-reader. Inside appeared on the site The Boy Genius . There yesterday were published and pictures of the device, which, according to the source, is the new Kindle 2.


Amazon made some changes to the design of the reading room, and also reworked its stuffing. So, from the new Kindle disappeared slot for SD-cards. But the amount of internal memory has increased. From 256 MB to 1.5 GB. Scroll wheel has changed the joystick. The source claims that the reader will be charged via USB.

But the screen remains the same. 6 inches, monochrome.

Information, again, unverified. But, in the light of the recent announcement of the new reader from Sony , Amazon should really scratch a bit, and offer users at least a small upgrade of the first, popular Kindle.
