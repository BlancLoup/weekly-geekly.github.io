C ++ User Group News

Hello!

I haven't written about community meetings at Habr√© for a long time. And meetings are held regularly. It is time to correct this misunderstanding and tell what was, what will happen , gild the handle . If someone is too lazy to go under the cat, then the news is in one line: meeting in Moscow on November 6, meeting in Nizhny Novgorod on December 9, C ++ Russia in spring 2018, C ++ Siberia in winter 2018 (2019?).

Meeting in Moscow


First, some important news. I really like the way the meetings are held in St. Petersburg and I was really waiting for someone to follow the example of anastasiak2512 and organize such meetings in Moscow. But no one took it. I'll try what else remains. So, if you are in Moscow and want to share wisdom, experience , pain , send an application for talks@cpp-russia.ru . If your company is ready to provide a site, too, write, we will be happy to discuss the details. The first meeting will be held on November 6 at the Align Technlogy office. Sean Parent, Mikhail Matrosov ( @mmatrosov ) and Anton Tokar will perform there. Registration for the meeting on the link . Let me remind you that November 6 is a day off, albeit Monday.

Meeting in Nizhny Novgorod


We continue to travel around the expanses of the immense Motherland. December 9, we will hold a meeting in Nizhny Novgorod. Now agree on the program and location. Follow the announcements on the site, on Twitter or in VK .

If you think that the meeting should be held in your city, write to conf@cpp-russia.ru.

Conferences


We decided to try to move the conference schedule a little. This is due to an attempt to transfer C ++ Russia for a time when the participants did not leave on vacation or left for a long weekend. Now C ++ Russia will be in April, and C ++ Siberia will be in winter (end of January?). It seems logical to go to Siberia in the winter.

See you later!
