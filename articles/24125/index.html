How to build a popular social network?

As is well known at the moment, the most popular social networks in RuNet are vkontakte.ru and odnoklassniki.ru. Let's try to understand why.
Vkontakte.ru was launched in autumn 2006 and as of April 2008 has 11 million users.


Page views over the past six months - vkontakte.ru
The project Odnoklassniki.ru was launched in March 2006 and as of April 2008 has 15 million users.


Page views over the past six months - odnoklassniki.ru
So what is the secret to the success of these social networks? The answer lies on the surface: both social networks exploit real offline social connections between people online . Since the main requirement of these networks is registration under a real name and surname, the search or invitation of the right person is just 30 seconds.
If before the world in the minds of people was divided into online (often associated with something virtual, unnatural, sort of its own microworld) and offline, then thanks to social networks online it became just a continuation of offline.

Consider another interesting point: VKontakte never and never advertised. How did he manage to gain 11 million users in 16 months?


First, according to the creators of the project, they already had several popular student projects, the audience from which was "transferred" to Vkontakte.ru. Thus, the start was given - the community began to live. Secondly, Vkontakte is a technically very advanced resource. Both from the point of view of usability, and from the point of view of quantity and quality of the provided services. The main audience - students, among whom there is the largest number of progressive geeks. That, in fact, led to the fact that users began to invite each other. The site administration only welcomed this and distributed iPods to the right and left.

Please note that VKontakte has a lot of current students, not graduates (and the project was originally planned for graduates). It also shows that the knowledge of Vkontakte was passed from mouth to mouth behind the institute bench.

Now consider the Odnoklassniki.ru promotion model:


Advertisement of the project was actively circulating on the Internet, which is quite natural: you can't tell your former classmates so quickly about the project; after all, we no longer sit at the same desk with them. However, ‚ÄúWord of mouth‚Äù played a significant role here, because even those small drops of communication ‚ÄúHello, mash, how are you, something I can't find you in classmates‚Äù promoted the project word of mouth.

Consider another popular project - LovePlanet.ru


LovePlanet.ru is a dating site, the number of users of which also exceeded 10 million.

Let's remember how the portal was advertised: advertising in elevators, at bus stops, a huge number of banners on the Internet. I am sure that the portal promotion budgets amounted to millions of dollars.


Page views over the past six months - loveplanet.ru

And now let's draw conclusions from the data below:


As you know, the cost of promotion of the project constitute a large part of the project budget.

The last thing I spent on promotion was vkontakte.ru , and we see that the indicator of the number of page views of the project continues to grow.

A significantly larger amount was spent on promotion odnoklassniki.ru , and we see that the indicator of the number of page views has stabilized and stopped growing. Most likely, this suggests that the limit on the number of registered users is close: everyone who wanted to register has already registered.

Apparently, the biggest expenses for promotion incurred project loveplanet.ru , and we see that the indicator of the number of page views falls. Most likely, this indicates that more and more users prefer social networks rather than dating sites.

We also draw attention to the fact that classmates and VKontakte are closed resources.

So, how to make another millionaire social network without investing millions of dollars?


  1. Social network should use social connections of users in the real world
  2. Users must be registered with real names and surnames, otherwise item 1 will not work
  3. You will be able to significantly reduce advertising costs if there is active offline communication between the users of your social network
  4. The network must be closed
