Untethered jailbreak method developed

Geohot once again overcame the barriers exhibited by Apple and laid out a video on which it demonstrates ipod touch 3 generations, which passed the jailbreak procedure (as stated), which loads perfectly and works without connecting to a computer.

Let me remind you that the owners of the iphone 3gs whose devices were released after 41 weeks and have an updated boot, previously could only use tethered jailbreak (when the device was restarted, it was necessary to connect to a computer) by the way developed by the same Geohot.

Geohot promises that this method will work on iphone 3gs, ipod touch 2 \ 3.
You can also hope that it will work with the iPad.

Geohot promises that the utility for jailbreaking will be as simple as blackra1n, but the release date is not reported.
