Kitchen tree Fruit of Life



When developing this concept for studio apartments, Vietnamese designers Hugh Trangoc (Hieu Tranngoc) and Khao Tran (Hao Tran) in their own words were inspired by the usual apple tree. Say, there is a trunk on which hung a whole lot of tasty. In addition to washing and electric stoves, the Fruit of Life concept includes: a refrigerator for wine and fruit, a microwave oven and an LCD monitor that displays recipes taken from the Internet. The design of the "legs" allows you to adjust the height of the "tree" as well as rotate it (why this is, frankly, not very clear).
