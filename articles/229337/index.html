Ritot: smart and stylish projection clock



On Habr√© several times already published articles and notes on smart watches from different manufacturers. Such watches can act as an organizer / calendar, additions to the smartphone, and sometimes also a fitness tracker.

These watches can be stylish or not, but they have one thing in common - the presence of a display. Well, the developers of Ritot decided to go the other way, and removed the display as such. He was replaced by a projector, displaying all the necessary information on the surface of the back of the owner‚Äôs palm. Now, however, only a few prototypes are ready, and a mass launch into production is planned from December of this year.



Design and features



Watches exist in two incarnations: classic and sports. Both versions have a different design and are made of different materials.



The classic model has an aluminum case, which is covered in leather (painted in black or white). Such a modification of the Riot looks quite stylish, so that the watch can be used as decoration.

As for the sports model, its body is made of rubber, plastic and rubber.

Functionally, both models do not differ from each other, and both the usual and sports modifications are waterproof (according to what safety standard, the developers do not write).

Watch features:


  • Ritot watches are equipped with a projector, allowing them to be used both during the day and at night;
  • Ritot watches use sufficiently high-quality materials, so the watches are designed for a long service life.
  • Projection technology is absolutely safe for skin and health.
  • You can change the color of the projection in one click. Available in over 20 colors to choose from.
  • There are two ways to activate the projection: touch the touch button or just shake your wrist.
  • The projection automatically disappears from the hand after 10 seconds (you can set a constant projection mode).
  • The watch is equipped with energy-saving technology: 1 month of work in the standby mode and 150 hours in the projection mode.
  • Waterproof
  • Ability to choose the right size for any hand.

  • Time display mode in 24 or 12 hour format.
  • The projection changes position for the left and right hand.


The title says that this watch is smart, which means that they can be used as an add-on to a smartphone. Ritot can display the following information (synchronization is via Bluetooth):

  • Incoming call with the name of the caller
  • Text messaging
  • Reminders, meetings
  • Emails
  • Alert Calendar
  • Facebook posts
  • Twitter
  • Weather Alerts
  • Silent vibration and timer
  • Notifications from any applications


Equipment




Together with the clock comes a charging base (charging wireless, by the way). The base can be used, besides its direct purpose, as an alarm clock.
