Homeless on Red Square

After a long break connected with the summer holidays, I returned to the family and, I hope, I will please someone with my topics.

News number one - I am now officially Mayovets. Cheers, comrades!

But as they say, closer to the body. Today was on Red Square. At first I walked around the Kremlin and decided that sooner or later I would move there to live - very much so there is quiet and calm. And most importantly - clean :-).

Having examined what was discovered at that time, I got to the Beginning Meridian (well, or what is the name of what is on the picture below?). What spoiled my mood a lot was the gathering of homeless people collecting coins. IMHO, this is already too much. And the policeman who was 10 meters away did not pay any attention to this.
