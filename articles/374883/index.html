MSI GE62 2QE Apache laptop video review



MSI GE62 2QE Apache - gaming laptops, replacing the very popular line of MSI GE60 (remembered for its democratic price and good performance). New items work on fourth-generation Intel Core i7 processors (4720HQ in the review), and the new NVIDIA video card GeForce GTX 965M (Maxwell GM204) with 2 GB GDDR5 video memory is responsible for gaming performance. J RAM is from 8 to 16 GB (two slots available). There is a 2.5-inch bay for data storage and as many as three M.2 slots for installing solid-state drives (raid0 is supported). Like the rest of the MSI gaming laptops, the keyboard is made by SteelSeries, and the sound system is handled by experts from Dynaudio (4 speakers and a built-in subwoofer). The screen diagonal is 15.6 inches, matte FullHD matrix (there are modifications with the display IPS 3840x2160).
