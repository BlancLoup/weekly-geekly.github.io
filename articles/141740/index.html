Modder has equipped a controller for SNES wireless module



Despite the rapid progress of the gaming world and gaming technology, the love of gamers to the old games and old gaming consoles does not fade. There are craftsmen who add to the old equipment what it lacked for many years. For example, a wireless module to a game controller for SNES. This is not a new controller, but a standard one, only with changes.

There are almost no changes from the outside (except for the absence of a wire, added LEDs and a switch). But inside a lot of things have been changed. The creator of this project added a lot of functional units, managing to fit it all into the old case (even the battery fit there). Of course, this is not a particularly practical project, but for old-school console gamers - the very thing.

Yes, and of course, besides the controller itself, the craftsman also needed to make a receiver for the console itself. He also turned out not very large, but, of course, noticeable. Nevertheless, the functionality of all this miracle of technology - at the height.
