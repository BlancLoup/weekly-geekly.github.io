We have checked and axis came!



Not so much time passed, as our service appeared in Russia, but we almost immediately received many requests for the opening of Taxolite in Ukraine. Many things had to be redone urgently due to the specifics of the interaction between the two countries.

In a short time, we prepared Taxolet for launch, and today we are happy to provide our innovative taxi to residents of Ukraine for review and future use.

Soon the site will be posted introductory video on how to use our application. Taxi payment is made as elsewhere - in cash. To use our application, you do not need to create any account in the system. To do this, simply download the application, enter your number and use.


What should I do?


You can download applications, leave an e-mail for a notice about the opening of a taxi in your city, and also leave a request to add a city if it is not already in our list of cities. Opening a taxi in a particular city will follow the same principle as in Russia - as the minimum base of interested users is set.
