Current cryptocurrencies besides bitcoin

With the growth of popularity and the price of Bitcoin, other cryptocurrencies have been pulled up: there are more of them, they are becoming more diverse and supported, and many creators have invested a lot of money. Everyone has their own differences and advantages, their own mining complexity, their own price and services. And until everything settles down it's just a big lottery in which you can try your luck.




Why create your own cryptocurrency and invest in its development (website development, public relations, services to work with it, etc.)?
In general, everything is simple: investing with the expectation of wealth in the case of the popularity of their currency, because the one who has the very first access to the currency can remember it on smart conditions (at the very beginning the lowest complexity and the largest exhaust in coins). And only then it is laid out in the public domain. However, not all currencies are mined by the creators to off. launch, and the lack of pre-mine then plays a good role in agitating the public to its own cryptocurrency.

Of the cryptocurrencies, the most popular at the moment are BTC Bitcoin ($ 1,000), LTC Litecoin ($ 30), NMC Namecoin ($ 7.7), PPC PPcoin ($ 6.1), however, you can still argue about the last two too fast.
