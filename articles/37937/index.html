Open for free registration service tineye.com

I think the screenshot shows that this unusual but very useful service is looking for pictures all over the internet that are similar to the given one.




Moreover, you can set a sample by downloading it from your hard disk or by specifying the URL to the desired file. There is also a plugin to quickly search for similar images directly from web pages in Firefox and Internet Explorer browsers.

The site itself is very convenient, fully maintained in the style of web 2.0 and its capabilities at first really stun the imagination :)

Of the minuses, you can call only a small search index of pictures, I hope over time it will be no less than that of Google.

However, you do not need to rant for a long time, because all the possibilities of the service are excellently shown on the introductory video. And what is most surprising is that the search works almost as well as on this video.
