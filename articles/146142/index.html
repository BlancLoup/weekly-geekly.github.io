PHDays Online HackQuest 2012 Completed



Positive Hack Days 2012 practical information security forum died down three weeks ago, but its echo is still audible. We mean, in particular, the Online HackQuest contest , which not only took place during the forum (May 30 and 31), but also lasted two more weeks after its completion.

Any Internet user could take part in HackQuest. Participants were given access to the VPN gateway, after connecting to which it was necessary to find the target systems and detect vulnerabilities in them. After successful exploitation of the vulnerability, the contestant obtained access to the key (flag) in MD5 format, which had to be sent to the jury using a special form on the participant‚Äôs personal page. For the correct flags were awarded corresponding points. To win the contest, the participant had to score 100 points first.
