Google continue to test Digg-like search interface

Cross-post from blog http://blogrock.ru/



Here are the screenshots of the new search interface, which is being tested by Google. Here you can vote for and against the results of search results. All this is very much like Digg. In addition, added the ability to add comments. In addition, the icons themselves voting for and against the exact same as on Digge. If you click on the username - we go to the Google user account (his profile).

In addition, if you click on the cross (in fact - to bury), then the result of the issue will disappear from the screen.
