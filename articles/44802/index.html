Pure Digital Technologies announced the release of the Flip MinoHD pocket digital camcorder, which allows you to shoot videos in HD quality and, using the FlipShare software bundled, place them on the Internet on YouTube and AOL Video hosting sites, the MySpace social network.


In a small-sized device (100x50x16 mm), the developers placed a 1 / 4.5-inch HD CMOS Sensor, a fixed-focus lens (2x zoom, unfortunately, only digital) and a tiny 1.5-inch TFT display with a resolution of 528x132 pixel.

Flip MinoHD can write video with a resolution of 720p at a frequency of 30 frames per second. For the storage of video used 1 to 4 GB of internal memory. Control is provided by touch buttons located on the front panel. The device is connected to the personal computer via a retractable USB connector.

The cost of a Flip MinoHD with 1 GB is $ 130. A device with 4 GB will cost $ 100 more.
