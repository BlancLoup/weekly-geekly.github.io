Xamarin courses in Ciklum Interactive, Dnepropetrovsk

We are pleased to announce that Ciklum Interactive announces a set of courses for Xamarin developers. The objective of the course is to give students a basic understanding of mobile development for iOS and Android in C #.


Mobile development is a trend. These are interesting projects, crazy startups and startups, the latest technologies. A world in which every few months everything changes! And you can be a part of this world!
Whoever you are - a senior student who is fond of the latest innovations, an experienced developer who decided to change his occupation, or just an iOS / Android fan - we invite you to Xamarin courses!
With you - basic knowledge of C #, fluency in English not lower than the intermediate level, understanding of the principles of the PLO and a great desire to engage in mobile development , with us - interesting lectures twice a week for two months and the opportunity to stay with us after the course .

About the teacher: Alexander Leuschenko , Xamarin TeamLead developer in CI, more than 3 years with Xamarin
About the course program:

1. Introductory lecture. What is Xamarin? Setting up the working environment
2. We recollect C #
3. Remember C # 2, Git Basics
4. Entry into Xamarin.Forms
5. XAML, resources, styles in Xamarin.Forms
6. MVVM and Data Binding
7. Database
8. Animations
9. Answers to test questions

10. REST client
11. iOS native way
12. Android native way
13. Camera
14. Geolocation
15. Custom components
16. Testing
17. Answers to checklist questions

Attention! Lecture plan may vary!
The courses will be held in offline format, in the Dnepropetrovsk office of Syklum. Presence in the classroom is required.

You can sign up for an interview with the organizers of the course by e-mail azab@ciklum.com and skype alice.zabolotnaya.

Classes start in September 2015.
