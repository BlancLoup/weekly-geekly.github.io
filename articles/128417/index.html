HTC Sensation XE with Beats Audio for the most demanding

Our popular HTC Sensation superphone fell into the hands of Beats Electronics. After a long and hard work, we are pleased to present you the end result - Sensation XE! This smartphone, like its progenitor, is based on the Qualcomm MMS8260 platform with a dual-core Qualcomm Scorpion processor, the frequency of which was increased to 1.5 GHz. We also did not forget about the time of the phone and increased the battery capacity to 1730 mAh. In addition, the smartphone has acquired a brighter and more aggressive shade!

When developing the device, we paid particular attention to the sound: the unique Beats Audio technology was integrated into the smartphone. Sensation XE is equipped with urBeats headphones from Dr. Dre, which not only allow you to fully control the playback of music from your phone, but also activate a special sound profile on the device itself.


Whether you listen to music, watch videos, play games - Beats Audio provides you with deep bass and bright tops. For the convenience of users, the smartphone will be equipped with a 16GB microSD card. The size of the internal memory is 4GB, 1GB of which is available for installing applications.

The smartphone will arrive on store shelves in early October of this year. Cost will be reported additionally.
Full specifications are available on the official website .
