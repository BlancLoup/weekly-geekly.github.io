A billion-dollar high-tech software company is created in the USA every 3 months.

Ron Conway ( Ron Conway ) once said: "Previously, a company worth a billion dollars was created every 3 months, now every 2". I have a chance to make my list of "billionaires". I tried to limit this list to only high-tech software companies. I also added several companies with a value of about a billion (~ 0.7 billion and more).


I should have clarified that in the context of the quotation, Ron basically meant the high-tech US software companies. There are many such companies outside the United States (especially in China) - the list could be much longer.

Basically he was right. Everything speaks for it.

Software companies, worth 4 billion, were created every year, except for the period 2000-2002.

Break from 2000-2002 associated with the collapse of dotcoms. Everything began to return to normal at the end of 2003 (Skype in August). 2004 was the year of the return of the dot-com era (Facebook, Palantir, Yelp, Evernote).

Take a look around. In the last 3 months, another billion-dollar company was born.

From this it follows that about 8 companies 2011-2012. in the future will grow significantly. They are still small, but we realize their value in the coming years. I have included several companies founded in 2009-2010, which, I think, have potential. It is much more difficult to arrange the rates for companies 2011-2012.
