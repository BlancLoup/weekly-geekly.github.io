SeoPult system - SEO work automation

Launched in early November, the SeoPult project is successfully developing and after the New Year holidays will be out of beta status.

Those who have been to the latest specialized SEO seminars, and those who are interested in optimizing search results, know the main actual method of promotion - link exchange. There are several exchanges, each in its own way convenient or useful.

If you promote a lot of projects, then a lot of time is spent. The quality of all purchases cannot be traced by hands, and the exchanges are in no hurry to give a reliable tool for verification. As a result, you lose a lot of time or waste budget.

SeoPult system automates exactly this part of the work: checking purchases by filters, removing a marriage, buying additional links, if the required Top has not yet been reached. The first few hundred projects confirmed the time savings and significant - at times! - saving link budget. Now you can without a lot of stress to lead a lot of projects.

It is clear that the rapid withdrawal of marriage is not the only trump card of the System. Seopult and landing pages for keywords will prompt, and will mark internal optimization errors, and will draw reports for the client for you. But these are details.

I'm not trying to advertise the project, but just want to introduce myself to the optimizing people.
