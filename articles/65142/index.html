Chronicle of training for hotkeys


Recently, we have made life easier for mouse havers on Poisk@Mail.Ru and GoGo.Ru projects, making it possible to navigate between search results pages using the arrow keys (+ Ctrl). At the same time, we have provided a combination of Ctrl + Enter to accept search engine recommendations, such as a suggestion to correct a typo in the request, go directly to the site (for example, to request ‚Äú habrahabr.ru ‚Äù) or show the entered address on the map.

And in order to achieve some noticeable effect, we had to think about how to convey to the minds and hearts of users the fact of the existence of a new remarkable feature.

The set of familiarization materials was initially supposed to be modest - they just made inscriptions right next to the page list (see screenshot), and to calculate the effectiveness of the innovation, they added a parameter to the links opened with hotkeys.

First, we decided to show a new version of half of the audience. The effect was unexpected.
