Inno, the first Ukrainian forum of innovative technologies InnoTech Ukraine will be held in April

From 9 to 11 April, the first innovative technology forum in Ukraine, InnoTech Ukraine , will be held in Kiev at the Olimpiysky National Sports Complex. It will present the latest technology and inventions in various fields of science. Scientists, inventors and manufacturers will share their ideas, experiences, as well as assumptions about the future development of technology.



InnoTech Ukraine will unite 5 industry conferences, an exhibition demo-zone, a meeting of investors and developers of start-up projects, entertainment workshops for children and adults on one platform.

1. Robotics will be devoted to robotics and everything connected with it. Here the topic of using robots in various fields and fields of human activity will be raised. Leading experts and project developers will tell all the latest information on the latest trends in the world of smart machines;



2. 3D-printing - will tell about the technology that is rapidly entering our lives - 3D printing, as well as new products and prospects for this industry. Professional speakers, educational workshops, high-tech innovations and much more are waiting for you;



3. Health care (m-health, robotics in medicine, genetic engineering, lifestyle & sports, smart food);



4. Smart technologies (wearable-technologies, augmented reality, smart energy, connected cars, Internet of things, software, mobile applications);



5. Education (e-learning, smart education, robotics in education, educational mobile applications, virtual reality and simulators, innovative libraries). It is on this site that our company will be represented on April 10 and 11 .

At InnoTech Ukraine you will be able to personally familiarize yourself with the projects Smiles.Education, make sure of the advantages of the system, ask all the questions to the developers and see the following modules in work: Electronic Journal Diary and Log In / Out from Smiles.School Card " .
In addition, within the framework of the Smiles.Education conference, it will present the Shkolnik mobile application, which has already been recognized by experts at various international IT events.



Come to InnoTech Ukraine, in which we will be happy to share with you knowledge and experience in the implementation of innovative technologies in education.

More information about the event can be found on the official forum website - www.innotech.kiev.ua .
