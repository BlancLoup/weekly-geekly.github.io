Convenience and ease of AirDroid

Greetings to all. Today I want to share the experience of using, in my opinion, an excellent program AirDroid
Let's not pull the bucket by the cord, let's start :)





About the application

By itself, the program offers access to your smartphone via a Web browser, for easy synchronization of information and quick access to smartphone data, almost without the participation of the device itself. This is quite convenient. I can, for example, download and upload files to my machine without a USB tail.

Receive and send SMS messages directly from the browser window


Manage applications


Use the phone as a camera


View contact list, call, save various kinds of notes.


In the end, find your smartphone if it is lost.


Installation and Setup

It is very easy to connect, just log in to the site using any of the suggested methods and put the application on your smartphone.
Next, set up a sentence (there is a Russian language) and voila, you get a link that you need to enter in the address bar of the browser. Further, I think, it will be interesting to you to rummage.

Of course, it was not done without monetization, and there are premium buns, for example, one of them amused me:
The phone camera of the smartphone photographs an intruder who incorrectly enters the unlock password.


I want to note that Google has almost all the web interfaces of its services, but personally I liked this one, as an aggregator all in one.
Good luck everyone :)
