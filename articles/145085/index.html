Ice Cream Sandwich is already working on 7.1% of all Android devices.



According to the developers of this OS, Android 4.0 now accounts for 7.1 percent of all Android devices. Two months ago, the share of Ice Cream Sandwich was 2.9 percent.

The lion's share is still behind Gingerbread - 65%.
The share of Froyo (OS 2.2) also increased - 19.1%.
But Honeycomb (OS 3.2) is losing ground - 2.7% now against 3.4% a month ago.

Analysts consider growth from 2.9 to 7.1 percent to be quite sharp and successful.
