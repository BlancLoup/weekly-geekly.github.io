Now SkyExpress tickets can be paid for with electronic money.

Since August 21, 2007, the payment system MONETA.RU and the airline SkyExpress launched a new joint project for paying for air tickets through the Internet. Payment from electronic wallets MONETA.RU, Yandex.Money and WebMoney is made through the payment system gateway MONETA.RU , which is available to the passenger at step 5 of booking a ticket on the airline's website. Payment is made without additional fees in real time.
For more information on SkyExpress ticket payments, please visit www.moneta.ru/info/users/skyexpress.htm or visit www.skyexpress.ru .
To date, the monthly turnover in the Russian segment of the Internet reaches more than 7 billion rubles, and the total number of users of electronic wallets is more than 12 million people.
