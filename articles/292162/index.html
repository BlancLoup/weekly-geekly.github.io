You are a person, you can have a unique, beautiful, interesting resume, in which you will convey not only information about yourself, but also emotion, your view of the world. It is wonderful. 3. Lack of specific information about the desired position

In Russian practice, the graph is not too widespread yet, but it seems to us that this can be quite a useful innovation, a trend that you are shaping here and now. Why? Firstly, it is useful for you. This is an important, almost key aspect. However, difficulties may arise here, since the desired position does not always correspond to an open vacancy and you must be ready for adjustment. 5. Still a student?

We are sure that you studied at school and finished it with dignity, but we do not advise you to indicate this. Immediately proceed to higher education, if any. Do not forget about the reverse chronological order: master's, then bachelor (the last education is always indicated in the first place). What you need to select? The university that you graduated if its name says something to your employer and your specialty (How does it sound? For example, a public relations specialist.) Be sure to specify the end years. If you have more than one higher education, still indicate all your merits. You will immediately see that you are an educated person with a broad outlook. This is wonderful.

If you can not boast of experience in a particular area and you have had many internships that did not end with a job, it is not necessary to list them all. List those where you actually got some work experience. You should not waste your time on trifles: 1.5 weeks in a company with a big name are unlikely to bring you much benefit. Describe the places where you not only wore coffee. It must be something you really want to talk about at the interview.
