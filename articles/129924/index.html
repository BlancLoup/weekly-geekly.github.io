The global database of time zones is closed by a lawsuit!

TZ_database , the key source of time zone information in the software world, is closed due to copyright infringement allegations.

The closure was initiated in connection with a lawsuit from Astrolabe Inc (manufacturer of software for astrologers) against 2 nat. persons, Arthur David Olson and Paul R. Eggert, DB managers from the mid-80s, September 30 in Boston - www.scribd.com/doc/67760407/ASTROLABE-INC-Vs-ARTHUR-DAVID-OLSON-and-PAUL-EGGERT- Complaint .

The lawsuit involves the use of historical data on time zones from the books of The American Atlas by Thomas G Shanks and The International Atlas by Shanks and Rique Pottenger.

The claim does not affect the current information in tz_database. In spite of this, Olson reported that the ftp database at elsie.nci.nih.gov is closed, as is the mailing list of the project.

Correct time zone information is necessary for proper event logging and automatic processing at the right time.
