Google Play Movies become available in Belarus

June 20 in Belarus (as well as Albania, Argentina, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Croatia, Czech Republic, Estonia, Greece, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Laos, Latvia, Lithuania, Moldova, Poland, Slovakia, Tajikistan, Thailand, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan) became available Google Play Movies, which allows you to buy movies directly from the Play Store.

In Google Play Books, you can now buy and read books to residents of Bolivia, Costa Rica, the Dominican Republic, Ecuador, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Nicaragua, Panama, Paraguay and Uruguay.

What was my surprise when I went to the Play Store and I saw the red Movies button. Finally, Google remembered a small state between Russia, Ukraine and Poland. Maybe soon we will have Play Music available?
