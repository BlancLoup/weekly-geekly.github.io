Homeopathy: a billion-dollar placebo industry


Photo: Wonderlane

According to a number of studies conducted by scientists from Australia for 68 different diseases, a special commission made the following conclusion: homeopathy is a placebo industry (with billions in revenues). A total of 176 individual studies were conducted , with an additional assessment of 57 scientific reviews. Project manager, Professor Glasi (Glasziou) published the results of his work in the blog British Medical Journal. In particular, he pointed out that with the help of homeopathic remedies aimed at treating 68 different diseases, 0 people were cured, and an effective effect on 0 diseases was provided.
