If the state helps to implement this project, the ticket price should fall to 16.1 thousand rubles, which is still too much for the average passenger.

VAIO introduced a laptop with a bunch of connectors and ports




VAIO introduced a new model of the laptop - SX12 . The diagonal of the display device - 12.5 inches, but the main "chip" of this device is not in the screen. The fact is that the developers have equipped the device with a large number of ports, larger than almost any modern laptop.

As for the latter, the SX12 has almost all the connectors that may be needed in the process. These are three USB-A ports, a USB-C port, a full-sized SD card slot, a headphone port, Ethernet, and even VGA, which are overlooked by an overwhelming number of manufacturers. True, the device is worth a lot. For the basic version you will have to pay a little over $ 1000, for the older version the company asks for twice as much.

Researchers: No, video games don't make people cruel




Recently, researchers from the University of Stetson in the United States presented the results of another study, the purpose of which was to find out the effect of video games on the psychological and emotional state of a person . As it turned out, video games do not make people want to show aggression in real life.

The study was long-term - its duration was about two years. As a result, it turned out that aggressive games are not associated with any of the scenarios of antisocial behavior. "The data from this study do not confirm the conclusion that video games are the cause of aggression or refusal of prosocial behavior among young people," says the article.

Russian software developers will provide 15 billion rubles


Import substitution is sweeping across Russia - now developers of domestic software will receive about 15 billion rubles of support . Funds will be allocated in the framework of the program "Digital Economy". The project is already being prepared by a group in the direction of "Information Security" at the ANO "Digital Economy".

It should be noted that the final amount of the grant may be more, the exact amount of assistance has not yet been determined. A third of the amount is allocated by the federal budget, and another two thirds are declared as extra-budgetary funding.
