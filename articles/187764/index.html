In Android 4.3, there was also support for Bluetooth Smart technology (also known as Bluetooth Low Energy), which will allow smartphones to communicate in devices such as fitness bracelets.

Finally, in version 4.3, there are new DRM APIs that offer standard methods for video encryption.
