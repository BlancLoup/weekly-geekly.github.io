Tanks on the ZX Spectrum

The WOT theme is very popular now. I repent myself a sinner ...

A long time ago, in 1984, when the majority of modern tankers were not even in the project, Tank Duel 3D was released on the ZX Spectrum!

First-person view, three-dimensional graphics, several types of tanks! Of course, there was no multiplayer, but then we only had a tape recorder. A feature of the physics of the game is that the shells are flying slowly. This makes it possible to dodge them, like Neo (press back and left / right).


Tank Duel at www.worldofspectrum.org where you can play right in your browser.
