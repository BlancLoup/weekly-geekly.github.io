Hyperledger project for developers and business - the next Moscow Blockchain Developers Meetup will be held on February 9

Already this Thursday, February 9 at 19:00, the next Moscow Blockchain Developers Meetup will take place. The themes of the previous event were Microsoft blockchain-tools and Proof-of-stake consensus features. The upcoming mitap will be entirely devoted to the Hyperledger project and its capabilities for both developers and business in general.

The event will feature:

  • Alexander Yakovlev, Head of Distributed Accounting Systems, National Settlement Depositary, member of Architecure WG

Theme: "Hyperledger"

As part of the conversation, the history and current state of the Hyperledger initiative, the possibilities and basic principles offered to developers will be discussed.

  • Igor Hapov and Alexander Zemtsov, employees of the IBM Scientific and Technical Center

Topic: Hyperledger Fabric : blockchain for business

The goal of the Hyperledger project is to build an open source framework for creating and executing reliable applications and industry platforms for conducting business transactions. The technical community built around the Hyperledger project is an excellent platform for the exchange of knowledge and suggestions between users and developers, which has a positive effect on the development of the project. In one of the implementations of the blockchain technology within the framework of Hyperledger - Fabric, transaction confirmation algorithms, other than proof-of-work, demonstrate good throughput. This all makes Hyperledger a great contender for the role of a blockchain system for business. As part of the report, employees of the IBM Science and Technology Center will talk about their experience with this platform.

Mitap will be held at the Digital October Center site at Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, p. 3.

The number of seats at the event is limited! If you are planning to visit mitap, please put a Like in our group on Facebook (if you are not on Facebook, just come).
