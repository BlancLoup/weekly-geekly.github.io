The Social Network for the Dead has appeared

Social networks of various kinds continue to be born. The choice is so great, because there are already a lot of networks for various groups of people, but new resources are regularly coming to light.
Recently started the project Respectance , dedicated to the dead. It is assumed that the users of the resource will be people who want to pay tribute to those who are no longer with us. For example, family members will be able to honor their ancestors with warm words, share with each other memories of them. It is likely that the site will be especially attractive to idols who have departed to a different world of stars. There is, for example, a section dedicated to Kurt Cobain , the leader of the Nirvana group, who committed suicide in 1994. Probably if teens stop writing down the walls of buildings with the names of those whose creativity they admire and start doing it in a specially designated place, then all of this will only be better.
