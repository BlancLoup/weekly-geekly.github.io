Tesla solar roof will be cheaper than usual, without taking into account power generation



On November 17, 2016, Tesla Motors held a meeting with shareholders about a possible merger of Tesla Motors and SolarCity through an exchange of shares. We will say in advance that according to the results, more than 85% of Tesla Motors shareholders voted positively, so the question can be considered closed. Documents to the SEC will be submitted shortly.

Ilon Mask, the executive director and the main shareholder of both companies, spoke at the meeting. Ilon made a remarkable statement: according to his calculations, innovative technology - SolarCity solar roofs that generate electricity for the house and car - will be cheaper than conventional roofs already at the installation stage, that is, without taking into account power generation!

Stepping onto the stage in front of the shareholders, Ilon Mask said: he had just returned from a meeting with the engineers of SolarCity, so now he can state this fact with confidence. Solar roofs will indeed be cheaper than usual, even without taking into account power generation.

This is amazing and good news. Moreover, this is an amazingly bold statement. After all, if it turns out to be true, then an ordinary person will have no reason why to install an ordinary roof, if a wonderful solar roof is offered for the same money or even cheaper, and it also generates energy. No wonder, after the start of sales of solar roofs next year, Tesla's Buffalo plant will not cope with the fulfillment of all orders.
