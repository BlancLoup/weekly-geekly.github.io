The thinnest laptop. Voodoo Envy 133

Voodoo, a division of HP, introduced its new laptop, which is currently the thinnest in the world. The thickness of the new Voodoo Envy 133, the body of which is made of carbon fiber, is only 18 mm (with a weight of 1.53 kg), which is 1 mm less than the previous record holder Lenovo ThinkPad X300.


Equipped with a new Envy 13.3-inch display with a resolution of 1280 √ó 800.

On board the laptop has a Core 2 Duo processor of 1.8 GHz (older model) and 1.6 GHz (younger), 2 GB of RAM, X3100 IGP video, 80 GB HDD (or 64 GB SSD to choose) and DVD-RW drive. Of the interfaces are WiFi 802.11 g / n, Bluetooth 2.0 and HDMI.

It works all on Windows Vista. Unfortunately, it is already installed. What entails an increase in cost. Which is $ 2,100.

Voodoo Envy 133 will be available in late June.
