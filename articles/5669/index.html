Free ... Kaspersky

A huge part of the population of mainland Eurasia, and of the whole world, uses Kaspersky Lab products (AVP, KIS).

The activity of the laboratory began comparatively long ago and, as we see, successfully.
Now Kaspersky has gone even further. I do not know exactly how AOL and Kaspersky Lab agreed, the result is more important for us. And the result was a free and, most importantly, free Kaspersky anti-virus 6. I downloaded it in December 2006 - build 308.

Download this miracle, called, by the way, Active Virus Shield, you can from the eponymous official website of the program www.activevirusshield.com . The key (serial number) will be sent to the email address. There are no problems with installation on different computers, because the key can be ordered an unlimited number of times (on mail.ru it really took a very long time, use the Gmail boxes).

A small FAQ in English is on the page www.activevirusshield.info .
