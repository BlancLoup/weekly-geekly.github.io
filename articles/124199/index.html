Heroes ABBYY Cup

About two months ago in our Habroblog, we announced the ABBYY Cup Olympiad , and now we want to introduce you to our heroes and a short story about how we got to know them.

First, a few numbers. 176 people from Russia, Belarus, Ukraine and Kazakhstan took part in the competition. They were offered five tasks, for each of which it was possible to get up to 100 points. The winners were those who scored at least 485 points, there were 26 such people. If you are interested in the conditions of the tasks or the results of the participants, you can familiarize yourself with them on the ABBYY website .

We invited all participants to visit us. In total, we have visited 21 people and only 9 of them are students of Moscow universities. Guys from Minsk, Izhevsk, Saratov, Simferopol, Sevastopol, Ivanovo, Orel came to us. The longest way to our office was done by a participant from Tomsk (almost 3000 km one way).
