I want to note that I see two goals for a business plan :

  • internal goal - the concentration of the most important strategic information for business development.

The same business plan is, in some way, a control for the management of the company / company. A business plan shows how well managers are guided and know the market, how much soberly they can assess the position of a company / firm.
