Peugeot starts selling cars online

The Peugeot automaker, which is part of the PSA Peugeot Citroen concern, is going to sell used cars via the Internet, while trying not to interfere with the work of its dealer network, reports Reuters .

About a hundred cars Peugeot 1007 will be put up for sale on the site www.vente-privee.com . All this, according to the organizers of the venture, is done in order to check whether people are ready to spend large sums on the Internet. There is no strategy for the development of this direction in the company.
