QIWI Wallet: the results of the first quarter of 2011

The first results of 2011 demonstrate that QIWI Wallet accelerates the pace of development. The company's financial turnover in the first quarter of 2011 increased by 154% compared with the first quarter of last year, reaching 11.56 billion rubles. The average payment grew by 63%, reaching 423 rubles, and the average replenishment - by 104%. The number of registered users is close to 40 million, active - to 7 million.
The number of Internet projects that accept payment via QIWI Wallet has reached 1,370 - an increase of more than 2.5 times. The turnover of Internet projects has grown more than five times, exceeding 1.5 billion rubles.

Popular Services

The growth leaders in the first quarter of 2011 were payment of fines and duties (Wallet users pay over 1000 traffic police fines every day in at least 77 regions of Russia), payment for purchases in online stores, repayment of loans and payments with virtual cards, remittances. The total turnover of money transfers made by users of the Wallet in the first quarter of the year exceeded 290 million rubles.
In January 2011, one of the most awaited new products appeared in QIWI Wallet - the QIWI Wallet card. QIWI Visa Card (QVC), existing in the form of details and valid for 1 year, allows you to make purchases and payments on the Internet worldwide directly from the QIWI Wallet account. Users appreciated the convenience of QVC: card turnover amounted to 48.6 million rubles: 12,000 cards were opened in 2 months.
As for the QIWI Visa Virtual virtual card, it is also very popular among users: by the period under review, the turnover amounted to 390 million rubles.

Popular Interfaces

The fastest growth in turnover was demonstrated by payment applications for social networks (426%), web-site (279%) and applications for mobile platforms (210%).
In early 2011, applications for the most popular Android and iPhone platforms caused the maximum interest among users.
