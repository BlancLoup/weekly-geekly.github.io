One- and two-letter domains are allowed in the .travel zone

Traldiance, the administrator of tld .travel, received permission from ICANN to register one and two letter domains. Previously, a domain name in the .travel zone could not be shorter than three characters. So soon companies related to the tourism business will have the opportunity to register domains like we.travel and go.travel.

Travelers will go in two letters :)

Now Tralliance is preparing changes to the rules of registration. Among other things, Tralliance plans to cancel the requirement that the domain should be used within 12 months after registration.
