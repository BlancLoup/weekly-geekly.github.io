Flexible methodology (XP)

The main approach in the contract is to provide staff time to develop the required system for the client.

The approach eliminates the endless development, because the client for infinity pays endlessly and is interested in stopping in the perfection of the beloved child in time.

A brainchild is really becoming loved, because the client is constantly involved in product development, which virtually eliminates his dissatisfaction with the delivery of the project.

The deadlines for the execution of tasks are agreed with the Client, but they are assigned by the employee of the Contractor directly responsible for the implementation.
The part of the contract reflects the XP approach in legal form.

1. The Subject of the Agreement

The client assigns and the Contractor undertakes to create and launch the Information System.
2. Cost of services and payment procedure

The cost of services is determined by the number of employees employed by the Contractor and the duration of their work (including development and work related to testing and acceptance).

The price of the working week of one employee of the Contractor is ________ rubles.
Payment is made after the adoption of development stages. Final payments under the contract are made after the delivery of the Information System.

3. The procedure for delivery and acceptance of work

Works are performed in stages according to the accepted specification. After each stage, the specification may be adjusted to the wishes of the parties. The duration of the iteration is 2 weeks.

The acceptance of the development phase of the Information System shall be carried out by the Client within three working days from the date of notification of the readiness of the development phase of the Information System.

Completion of work on the development of the Information System is executed in a bilateral act of acceptance. After signing the act of acceptance of work is considered completed and changes to the contents of the Information System can be made only under a separate agreement.
