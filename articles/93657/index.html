The results of the survey on the effectiveness of business processes "business" and "IT" - who is more optimistic?

In 2009 - 2010, at a series of trainings for IT companies, we conducted an interesting assessment on our own business process maturity model. We considered various models of maturity of companies by key parameters (labor productivity, process management, development of information exchange). Listeners were asked to fill out questionnaires, on the basis of which they summed up some statistics.

There were 4 sessions in different cities - Moscow, St. Petersburg, Voronezh, Saratov. 92 participants. Score is placed in the range from 1 (bad) to 9 (excellent).
