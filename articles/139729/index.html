Looking for a nice investor

Hello! In honor of the fact that spring has finally arrived, we at Glavstart decided to launch the dating resource Wanted.vc , but we are not going to acquaint men with women, but with startups.



Maybe it will seem strange to someone, but let's think about how this acquaintance is being built. You have your own project and you need investments. Suppose you have already spent the money of your friends and relatives. Where will you go next? In fact, there are 2 options:
  1. You will make a beautiful presentation and start sending it to all funds that you know.
  2. You will go to various events where you will be given 2 - 5 minutes for the presentation. During this time, you will need to have time to interest investors in your project.

In the first case, you will most likely receive a refusal either because the project is too early (most Russian funds enter the project already in the late stages), or simply because the presentation was not correctly emphasized. The second time in the same fund you most likely do not apply.

In the second case, you too can simply not have time to get interested in your project. All such events are like the TV show ‚ÄúLove at First Sight‚Äù - it looks beautiful, there are successful stories, but if relationships were built only this way, the planet would have died out long ago.

Now remember how you met your beloved girlfriend. Perhaps someone instantly fell in love with the unearthly beauty and the heavenly voice, and then she with him, and they lived happily ever after. In real life, everything happens not so: not always feelings arise immediately, they are not always immediately mutual. And if the case does not confront people again and again - on pairs at the university, at work, on Facebook and Vkontakte, then nothing usually happens. So we decided to become such an opportunity for the authors of projects and investors. You have a real opportunity to look at each other and only then understand whether it is worth tying your relationship with the bonds of an investment agreement.
