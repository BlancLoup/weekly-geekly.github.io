VTech has released tablets for kids



In fact, why tablet computers should be designed only for adults (well, let the teenagers)? Why, amid such an abundance of any children's gadgets there is still no tablet intended for the development of a child? VTech introduced three models of children's tablets, designed to work with educational applications and games (some of which develop no worse than some lessons in school). The models are called VTech VTech MobiGo 2, InnoTab 2 and InnoTab 2s.

MobiGo 2 is designed for children from 3 to 8 years old, and is equipped with a touch screen, motion sensor and microphone. The design of the device, "sharpened" under the game, as you can see in the preview photo (left). The developers have pre-installed three well-known games, plus there is an electronic reader, a coloring book, a ‚Äúgraphic editor‚Äù and an image viewer. There are a number of educational programs. Among other things, the developers have provided and the presence of a full qwerty-keyboard. So here both entertainment, and educational programs - the whole car, so to speak.

The second device, InnoTab 2, is designed for children from 3 to 9. This device is already more like a tablet, and is equipped with a 5-inch display. There is a video player, music player, all kinds of applications for "creative" kids. For the user is available 2 GB of memory. You can install and memory card up to 32 GB. The developers have thought about the rotating camera.

As for the InnoTab 2s, this device is almost identical to the InnoTab 2, only there is also a wireless module Wi-Fi.

The cost of the devices is small, for VTech MobiGo 2, InnoTab 2 and InnoTab 2s the company asks for $ 59.99, $ 79.99 and $ 99.99, respectively.
