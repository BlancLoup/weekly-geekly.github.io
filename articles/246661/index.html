Before you - the presentation of the Russian-language online magazine and community dedicated to the frontend. Interesting?

What is this project?


The project consists of a site and a community in the social network VKontakte.

VKontakte community

The community regularly (daily) publishes relevant materials from the world of the front-end: articles, videos, tools, services, books and much more. From the avalanche of information, we select the best, so you just have to subscribe and follow the publications.

It should be noted that on VKontakte there are practically no analogues to our community on this topic. There is another relatively large community that publishes more quotes, jokes, and naked women than useful materials. This is what distinguishes our community from the rest - we publish only useful materials, we have no jokes and other unnecessary content.

Benefits of our community:
  • Regular publication of materials
  • Separating material into headings using hashtags
  • Separation of lessons and articles by difficulty level - for example, for beginners and for advanced
  • All material is manually selected.
  • Established and convenient format of published content


Site

Our site opened just yesterday. Actually, in this regard, we publish this article. Subscribers to our community often complained that they know English at a low enough level to read this or that article. Of course, without English anywhere, but Russian materials will not be superfluous;)

With such thoughts, we registered a domain and made a website on which translations and author articles on front-end development and design will be published.

Site Benefits:
  • Careful selection of published material
  • Professional translation of articles
  • Grammar and typography are important to us. We edit all articles before publication.
  • The absence of any advertising
  • Layout is adaptive, which allows you to comfortably read articles from any device.


Future plans


  • Achieve regular release of new materials.
  • Attract new authors to write articles.
  • Post current news
  • Publish interviews with famous developers.
  • It is possible to translate reports and videos.


I will be glad to hear any suggestions, ideas and comments. Thank!
