Currency markets


Last week began with the weakening of the ruble - on the first day of trading on the stock exchange, the dollar rose above 62 rubles, and the euro - above 70 rubles. However, in the future, a calm geopolitical situation and stable oil prices led to the strengthening of the Russian currency - on Friday, the euro exchange rate on the Moscow Stock Exchange for the first time this year fell below 66 rubles, and the dollar fell below 60 rubles.

From March 6, 2015, the Bank of Russia set the official euro rate at 68.30 rubles. This is more than 80 kopecks. below the previous value. This figure was the lowest since December 27, 2014.



The official dollar rate decreased by only 3 kopecks and amounted to 61.84 rubles.

During Friday's trading on the ICE exchange, the cost of futures for Brent crude for delivery in April 2015 rose to $ 61.03, which is 0.9% higher than the previous day's closing level.

Stock markets


At the beginning of last week, the MICEX index rose above 1,800 points for the first time since February 20, the RTS index also showed growth. The growth of oil prices and the strengthening of the ruble allowed the main Russian stock indexes to remain at the reached levels, however, the week ended with their decrease.



ITinvest analyst Vasily Oleinik in his review described the prospects for changing the MICEX index:

Most of the inflation and devaluation of the Russian ruble MICEX index and Russian stocks have already been discounted, but there are still no serious fundamental reasons for further growth. For many, the most liquid Russian stocks, over the past three weeks, investors have seen an increase in volumes, so we can hardly see any current levels of strong growth without correction. The question is only in the depth of correction. The immediate goal correction on the MICEX index are now in the range of 1620-1640 points. As soon as the price closes below the 1730 points mark, the path to the nearest support range will be opened immediately.

Do not forget about the upcoming quarterly expiration, which will take place in the middle of the month. Even if the Russian stock market is kept artificially before it, then after that, what should happen is all the same - at least removing the overbought, at most, a good correction.
