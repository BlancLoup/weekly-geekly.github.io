IBM and RVC invite young entrepreneurs to participate in the SmartCamp competition



Hi, Habr! IBM and RVC announce the opening of acceptance of applications for participation in the IBM Smart Camp competition, which will be held June 17-18 in Moscow . IBM SmartCamp is a unique international initiative aimed at finding start-up entrepreneurs who are implementing projects that support the concept of "Reasonable Planet". Participation in the program allows you to get free access to IBM software and hardware, technical support for the project, advice and assistance in promoting the finished product to the market.

The event will be attended by five companies whose projects will be selected by an expert committee of IBM and RVC. According to the results of the Russian stage of the IBM SmartCamp, out of five applicants one finalist will be selected who will take part in a three-month in-depth mentoring program from IBM, and will also have the opportunity to present their project at the regional final of SmartCamp in Europe.

During the event, entrepreneurs will be able to present their projects to the jury, hear expert opinion, get advice on further development and discuss options for possible cooperation. The jury will include representatives of the Russian and foreign venture capital industry, business leaders, industry and technical experts, heads of IBM and RVC OJSC.

Representatives of start-up companies that implement projects in the field of information and communication technologies are invited to participate in the competition. To apply, enterprises must meet the following criteria:

  • The age of the participating company does not exceed 5 years with an annual turnover of less than $ 10 million.
  • The implementation of the project involves the use of technical devices (for example, sensors, sensors, video cameras), communication networks and analytics.
  • A prototype / demonstration of the proposed solution is desirable.
  • The project must have the potential for development in the global market.
