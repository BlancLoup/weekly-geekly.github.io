Screen with characteristics


The screen of this smartphone has maximum viewing angles, and the display brightness is enough to clearly see the content in clear weather under direct sunlight. The back cover is made of polycarbonate and has a neat logo.



The camera is framed by a metal ring, which, due to its bulge, reduces the risk of scratching the lens.



There are 2 slots for SIM-cards with 4G support on both (alternately) and a slot for a memory card up to 32 GB.



The first thing that surprised me and mocked me was the front camera. The company's engineers have made the function of determining gender and age in the face of a person by default. True, in my 27th my phone constantly gives me 43-60 years. Apparently, I'm too old for a Chinese.

The rest of the people to whom I showed this chip, indicators of age and sex are the same. Probably something is wrong with me.

I often travel by train and I want to notice that RedMi Note 2 is perfect for you to travel.
