Peter Molyneu pushed two options for monetization in Curiosity

Curiosity's game from Peter Molyneux is a cube from which many users from all over the world pinch off scales, removing layers in the hope of getting to its core, where some secret is hidden.



In the last update, the author added to the built-in purchases the ability not only to delete cube elements, but also vice versa - to increase them back. For different amounts, you can add from 10 to 500 thousand items.

"We do not know exactly how events will develop further," writes studio 22Cans, which created Curiosity. "Perhaps Curiosity will soon reveal its secret secret, or maybe players will want to keep it at all costs."

At the moment, more and more inclined to the first version - the players for a fee removed about 14 million scales, and increased about 5 million.

The game itself has been going on for 164 days, during which time players have removed 260 levels from a cube.
