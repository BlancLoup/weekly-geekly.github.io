Nexus Player - the first Nexus on the Intel Atom


A few days ago, a pre-order began on Google Play for the Nexus Player - a device from the new Nexus lineup, created under the Android OS Lollipop. The prefix Google TV was developed by ASUS based on the 4-core Intel Atom processor - we note that this is the first Nexus on the Intel platform. Nexus Player has 1 GB of RAM, 8 GB of internal storage, is equipped with Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Included with the player is a remote control with voice search function, you can additionally purchase a wireless gamepad.
Unfortunately, the functionality of buying devices through Google Play in our country does not work yet. More lucky foreign users the device will cost $ 99.
