The German "Wikipedia" will improve at the official level

The German Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Consumer Protection intends to finance a project aimed at improving the quality of information in the German version of Wikipedia .

The interest of the ministry, however, is very narrowly focused: the money will be allocated to write or edit articles on renewable resources , which will require finding specialists and paying for their work.

Florian Gerlach, project manager, said that the growing popularity of Wikipedia generates an increase in the number of authors who write and edit articles at their discretion, with the result that the final product is constantly changing.
