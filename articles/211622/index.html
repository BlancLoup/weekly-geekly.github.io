Video review tablet Acer Iconia A3-A11



Acer Iconia A3-A11 - inexpensive tablet with a screen of 10.1 (IPS, 1280x800). The device works on a quad-core MediaTek MT8389 processor with a frequency of 1200 MHz. The amount of RAM is 1 GB, and the capacity of the internal drive - 16 or 32 GB. For wireless communication, the user has access to: 3G, Wi-Fi 802.11n and Bluetooth 4.0. The set of ports includes: micro HDMI, microSDHC card reader (up to 32 GB), micro USB and a combined audio port. Acer Iconia A3-A11 is equipped with two cameras 0.3 and 5 MP. The device is running the operating system Android 4.2
