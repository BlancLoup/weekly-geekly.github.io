"Searchlight Paris Hilton" lured on ivi.ru

Top programs and First Channel films, such as Paris Hilton's Searchlight, Night Watch, Day Watch, will be freely available on the ivi.ru video site. Already signed a license agreement.

For the first time, the main TV channel of Russia legalized its programs on the Internet, Kommersant writes . Previously, they were distributed only through torrents.

The unpopular ivi.ru site (it was launched just a month ago ) now has every chance to compete with the leaders of the video content market in Runet, that is, projects like Smotri.com (29% of the market), Video@mail.ru (26%) , Rutube.ru (21%) and Yandex.Video (12%). These are statistics on the volume of video advertising, according to J`son & Partners Consulting.

A new project ivi.ru is planning to capture 20% of the video advertising market in RuNet in a year, which will increase from $ 5 million in 2009 to $ 9-10 million in 2010 and will grow by 40-50% per year for at least the next five years.
