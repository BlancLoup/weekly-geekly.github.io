The score is calculated according to a clever formula: first, the votes scored by all studios are counted (studios themselves and external experts vote). Then the first 250 companies (voted) are taken, and their votes (voices of the ‚Äúelectors‚Äù) are counted with modifier 1, and the votes of the rest are recorded with the modifier 1/10. The resulting score is multiplied by 7 different coefficients (each in the range from 1 to 1.1), the data for which the studios themselves submitted - and this is how the final rating score is obtained.
