The essence

The game is called " Bloody Gebnya against the Legion of Anonymous ". This is a game for social networks. Game genre - network massfighting. By massfighting, we mean a battle in which a large number of units crush each other, and the meaning of a player‚Äôs action comes down to the generation of fighters. The game mixed the mechanics of the magic of gathering games, zombies against plants, and even starcraft; tower defense genres and massively multiplayer online. Blood, guts and memes are the first signs of the game.


The battle

The player can fight with another person in a battle in real time. His army is a deck of cards, where each card corresponds to a warrior-meme or a politician. Participation of the player in the battle is limited to laying out or removing the card. Each laid out card periodically gives birth to a warrior who is already running to kill enemy fighters without a player. The goal of the battle is to destroy the enemy‚Äôs bunker. The fight is the main advantage and the culmination of the game. It is in it at the same time a rod on each other up to 200 units at the same time, filling the location with blood. Each hit in the unit is accompanied by a spray of blood of a huge radius.



Preparation for battle

If the battle is the culmination of the game, then the road of preparation and formation of the deck goes to it. The player is invited to assemble his own army of Internet memes or famous politicians. 21 memes fight for the Legion and 21 fighters steal and kill for the Bloody Gebnya. The player will take one of the parties and form an army. Cards with fighters can and should be bought for game currency, replenishing their army. Each card gains experience and can be pumped. And having pasted a mark on it, the fighter will receive a special skill. Among the characters are Putin, Surkov, Sobchak, Boyarsky, trollface, pepyakomen, kote, onotole, leonide and anonymus. All warriors are divided into 6 races: political politics, oligarchs, showbiz, feys, upyachki military men, beatards. Races do not affect the characteristics of the cards, divide them only logically. By strength, cards are divided into three types: simple, hardcore and epic.


Maps or warriors

Each card has its own characteristics. These are health, attack power, armor, movement speed, attack radius and projectile impact area. This variation allows you to use different tactics to achieve victory. For example, now tactics have proven themselves well, when a long-range fighter ‚Äúide‚Äù is released, who hits the square. Enough to restrain the enemy in battles at a safe distance from the ide, and they will accumulate and turn into a formidable force.


Stamps

Each card has three brand slots, one of which is immediately occupied by the base mark. Having pasted the mark on the card, the fighter acquires a special skill. Often this skill against a particular race of warriors. Thus, it is possible to adapt to a specific enemy, exposing warriors with marks acting against the prevailing races of the enemy.
In addition to fighting with real opponents, the player can gain experience in quests. Now there are 4 quests: Sparta, Bobruisk, Ostankino and the Kremlin. The meaning of the quest is simple - this is a normal battle against the computer in different locations. Each quest has 10 levels of difficulty. The complexity of the quest depends on the deck of a computer, a set of its cards and the level of each card.


Ranks

The player has two ranks at the same time: for gebniu and for legion. The higher the difference in titles between the two factions, the more pronounced and distinctly belonging to one of the factions. But you can play a double agent, developing for both factions. The title is stuffed in quests. Winning the soldiers of gebni, the player gets points and rank for the legion, losing slightly for gebnya, and vice versa. A total of 11 ranks for each faction, ranging from junior lieutenant to admiral (naval ranks) for the legion and from junior lieutenant to general (army ranks) for gebni. Raising his rank, the player opens to buy a card better in the store.

Domestic currency

There are two currencies in the game: lave and hundreds of oil. Currencies, by and large, are equal. You can earn the first one by playing quests and winning battles. And the second is Donat, it can only be bought for real money. I want to note that the donate does not give any advantages in battle, but only allows you to quickly pump your deck. Both currencies can be spent on the purchase of cards, brands, purchase of VIP status, pumping your bunker, forced pumping of cards in the laboratory.
