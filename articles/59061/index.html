New Kindle DX again only for USA

Amazon introduced the Kindle DX third generation electronic reader. Compared to the Kindle 2, there are quite a few innovations in it. The most important of which is a 9.7 inch E-Ink display with a resolution of 1200 x 824 pixels, giving out 16 shades of gray.


Also, the Kindle DX now has 4 GB of internal memory. PDF format support has been added (besides TXT, HTML, DOC, RTF, JPEG, GIF, PNG, BMP, AAX, MP3). From the functional innovations, the possibility of automatic transition from portrait mode to landscape mode and the ability to adjust the length of lines are noted. In terms of ergonomics, there are also improvements: the navigation keys are moved to the right side of the device. On the whole, the Kindle DX is much nicer than its predecessor.

The Kindle DX has support for EVDO and 3G, as well as a Wi-Fi module. USB connector, of course, also available. You can listen to music by plugging headphones into the usual 3.5 mm jack.

Worth reading $ 489. Unfortunately, you can only buy it in the USA.
