BMW has released a free racing game

A rather unexpected news came to us from Germany. The famous auto giant BMW in collaboration with the German studio 10tacle Studios and the company Blimey Games has released a computer game - the BMW M3 Challenge racing simulator with the BMW M3 car in the lead role.


The best part is that the distribution kit with the game is distributed for free, and it weighs relatively little - 346.5 MB.

The BMW M3 Challenge contains four single player modes, as well as a separate multiplayer mode. The latter will become a platform for quick competitions, which will be around the world: the developers promise to arrange a championship with 1000 players, only 14 of whom will reach the grand final. Nothing has been said about specific prizes yet, but something very valuable is promised. As for the system requirements, they are presented on the official BMW M5 Challenge promo website.
