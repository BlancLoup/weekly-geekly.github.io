Amazon for Christmas has sold more e-books than paper

The largest online store Amazon.com reported that one day the first time sales of electronic books exceeded sales of paper books. It happened on December 25, 2009, at Christmas. After that, Kindle officially became the most popular gift in the history of the Amazon.com store.

Why e-books were bought so much exactly on December 25th is understandable. On this day, tens of thousands of happy recipients of Christmas gifts (Kindle) all went together to buy something to read. At the same time, paper books on this day are sold little (ordinary people are not up to shopping). However, specificity does not make this historical achievement less significant.
