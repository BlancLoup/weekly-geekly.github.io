We Are the Dwarves! - domestic tactical action on Kickstarter

Note: The author of the post has no relation to the developers of the game. He just posted the announced.

Kiev studio Whale Rock Games launched a Kickstarter campaign to raise funds for the development of the game ‚ÄúWe Are the Dwarves!‚Äù.


The plot of the game tells the story of three astronaut gnomes who went to get a new Sun for their fellow tribesmen. Only instead of a spaceship they have a mechanical drill with which they pave the way into the earth's firmament. And the role of the Sun in their world is played by magic stones, emitting magical light.

Depending on the style of the player, the characters can either engage in open battle or hide from enemies. Animals have real "feelings." They can hear you, see, or track by smell.

In the game there are three clan gnomes: Engineer, Warrior and Apprentice. All of them are technically savvy, and not very belligerent, but can stand up for themselves, if necessary.

Engineer



He is a real fan of mechanical devices. In the game you will see how he makes traps, builds guns and barriers. His right hand is also mechanical, to hold a powerful gun.

Warrior



This guy likes to fight. Do not feed him with bread, let him fill someone's face. His native kingdom consists mainly of arenas for battles. But they are not invaders. They are only improved in the art of war. Smashfist is strong and fearless, but at the same time very optimistic in life. It can flare up and strengthen its power, or it can become thick-skinned and gain immunity from any attack.

Pupil



This is the most mystical character of the troika. He is very secretive, but has secret knowledge. In his arsenal, stealth and teleportation. His movements are invisible, enemies never know where their death will come from. And perhaps this character will turn into an owl. But we still do not know for sure.



Features:


Single player - control one, two or three heroes
Real-time tactical battles in a unique fantasy world
Stealth mode or mochilovo - all in the hands of players
Game pause and sequence of commands
Interaction with the environment , natural traps, variability of the battlefield.




Exit We Are the Dwarves! scheduled for November 2015 on Windows and Mac. The developers have requested $ 120,000. The Kickstarter campaign has just started. On the project page there is a short game video, a lot of concept art and details of the future game.
