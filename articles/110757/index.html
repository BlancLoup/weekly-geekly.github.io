Free search service for government and commercial IT tenders

Introduction


www.erumpo.ru is a free service for finding government and commercial tenders and procurement in the field of IT and high technology. Today, tenders are aggregated on erumpo from more than 65 sites on the Russian Internet, including the most popular public sites (zakupki.gov.ru, roseltorg, sberbank-ast and many others) and commercial (telecom sector, banks, industry).

You can search for tenders and purchases on erumpo: by categories (now ~ 80), attached documents, contract price, publication date, etc. Also, the RSS feeds functionality has been implemented according to search criteria. Therefore, it is not necessary to constantly be on the site to get new information, you set up RSS and all new tenders and purchases come to the necessary search queries yourself ...

To gain access, you simply need to register your company and add one or more standard projects that you are ready to undertake. And this may be, for example, the sale of any products, their production or, for example, the provision of services, both temporary (assistance in the implementation of IT systems) and permanent (subsequent support).

Access to tenders and orders, and participation in them for all IT suppliers on erumpo will always be free - this is the company's policy. In the very near future, erumpo plans to launch a functional for self-publishing tenders and orders directly by customers.
