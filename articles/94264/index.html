The draft law, toughening the punishment for plagiarism, contains plagiarism

On 6 May, a member of the lower house of the Argentine Parliament, Geronimo Vargas Aignasse, introduced a bill that toughened the penalty for plagiarism. But what is interesting is that in the project proposal in order to define plagiarism, he copied 331 words from the article about plagiarism on Wikipedia without any reference to the source. In the copied text, there are even some square wiki markup brackets.

However, this is not the first time that Ignasse uses Wikipedia text to write project proposals. In a proposal to ban the arrival of Barra Brava ( barra brava - so called Latin American football fans, known for disorderly conduct) to the World Cup, which will be held this summer in South Africa, he copied 314 words from the relevant article .
