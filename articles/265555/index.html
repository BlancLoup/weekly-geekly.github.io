The benefits of links from social networks

What are links from social networks in terms of search engine?

  1. Confirmation that a certain site is of interest to living people.
  2. Factor increasing the naturalness of the reference profile of the site.
  3. Quickly indexed links that report new pages.
  4. Stable links with a long lifespan (WebArtex checks whether the account owner deleted the link after it was posted).


These four factors make a significant but not decisive contribution to the final ranking calculation. According to experts, the share of social factors so far does not exceed a few percent. However, it should be understood that the benefits are not limited to static parameters, because now Yandex pays great attention to behavioral factors and referrals.

Clicking the link is a sign that the link leads from an adequate placement to the page of interest to people:

  • the link is located where real users see it;
  • The link is surrounded by relevant explanatory text that creates context for the transition.


Behavioral ranking factors reflect the interest of visitors to the site:

  • number of visitors;
  • bounce rate (percentage of visitors who viewed only one page);
  • depth of viewing the site;
  • the amount of time spent on the site.


Obviously, links from posts in social networks bring more transitions from representatives of the interested audience than any other type of link placement.
