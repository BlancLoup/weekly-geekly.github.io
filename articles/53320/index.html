Ultimate 5-in-1 Geek Pen



Gadgets that produce today are increasingly scaring with their functionality. Many engineers have forgotten that the simple is not always the enemy of the complex, but in order for the device to be interesting and useful it is not necessary to top it all off to integrate a digital camera, voice recorder and GPS into it.

And among the great variety of various devices, it is sometimes very difficult to find something simply attractive and, importantly, cheap. Stumbling on the Ultimate Geek Pen in my head immediately clicked: "This is it!", And I give my head to the clipping, that your writing tool does not know everything that this ballpoint pen is capable of (unless you already have one).

In addition to the functionality of an ordinary pen (with replaceable rods), the owner of this gadget gets a stylus (you just need to scroll the pen tip) for portable devices with touch screens. By pressing the button next to the clamp, you can turn on the laser pointer, another one turns on the light bulb on a flexible holder with two modes: ordinary white light and ultraviolet - for spies.

Price: symbolic $ 10 , with which anyone who has not lost the ability to write with his hands will part without regrets.
