Terminator tXt



Hypertext game about "Terminator 4" . I did it just for fun, as Linus Torvalds said. Some graphics from posters, some screenshots from teasers and a pinch of cheek.

He wrote texts himself, collected graphics from available sources, and used a handwritten script in Python to break down text in HTML.

Specifications:
- engine: HTML
- amount of code and graphics: less than 1 mb
- the total amount of clear text (not code) - about 60 KB
- number of nodes (pages) - 132
- endings: three good, the rest more than a dozen
- locations: at least five
- there are gunfights, car chases, knife fights with terminators and a battle with the harvester
And all this is in a text game!

Check it out and curse, suggest an idea or write about a bug.
Do not wait until the future comes. Become John Connor today! :)
