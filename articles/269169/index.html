Amazon's legendary books. Continuing the theme of "Elements of Programming Interviews: The Insiders' Guide"

Good afternoon, Habr!

In the beginning of summer, we already talked about one of the legends of Amazon, "Elements of Programming Interviews: The Insiders' Guide".

The book continues to occupy the first places in the ranking of Amazon, but in September 2015, the authors released another book, which also immediately became a bestseller.

Elements of Programming Interviews in Java: The Insiders' Guide

The success of both books can not be called an accident, because they not only help prepare for an interview in a serious company, but also offer a detailed analysis of the most complex tasks and algorithms.

The difference between these books is only in the language in which the algorithms are implemented: C ++ or Java.

So we want to ask your opinion and understand which book (or which language) will be closer to the needs of the Russian reader.
