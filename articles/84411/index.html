Skype release for Symbian phones

Today I saw good news - on February 15, 2010, Skype released a release version of its application for Symbian phones.



The freebies of Skype-to-Skype and the chat itself, plus the ability to transfer files, are claimed as plushies.

As I understand it, support for video calls will be later.

The list of supported phones has pleased - as I understand it - the entire line of S60v3 FP2 + S60v5. The application is available via the link , or simply log in from your mobile phone at skype.com/m

On my phone (nokia 5800) version 1.00 (3) was downloaded. For the installation asked for almost 6 megabytes.

A full-fledged, real Skype without any intermediate layers there is finally a reality!
