Apple Card - Bonus Credit Card


Tim Cook expects Apple Pay to process more than 10 billion transactions in 2019. It will be connected in more than 40 countries. And to make payment even more convenient, to remove the extra fees and increase reliability, the company now presents the Apple Card. Credit card You register through your iPhone, and in a couple of minutes you will receive your card in the application. Available for payment wherever they work with Apple Pay.




The card will live in the Wallet app, so it automatically calculates costs and budget. Apple says it even uses machine learning technology to eliminate all these strange transaction names. In the history they will always be replaced with the names of stores and specific products.


Apple representatives explained how their offer is better than banks:


Every time you spend with an Apple Card, you get a cashback daily. We call it Daily Cash.

Cashback is 2% (instead of 1% for banks). And if you spent credit card resources to buy at the Apple store, the cashback will be 3%.


The card does not have a number, CVV, signature, end date. All data is stored inside your wallet application.


About the benefits of the card told for quite some time. Apple, apparently, really wants to transfer Americans to their card. Let's see how to take off. A little more you can read at the marks .

The future of television


TV shows and movies will always be popular (until some more advanced form of light entertainment appears). We have more and more free time, and we can devote it to viewing something interesting. Looking at the huge success of Netflix, almost every company that has enough money and ambition wants to grab a piece of tasty market.


But the market for streaming TV services is already very full. Only from cloud Amazon, Netflix, Hulu, plus - dozens of traditional ones. In just a month, Disney + is going to present its site to Disney - with its entire insane collection of films, from Sleeping Beauty to the latest Avengers. According to Reelgood, on average, an American home is subscribed to 4.2 streaming services. One of them is Netflix - in 96% of cases. And if the user has only one video service, in 83% of cases it is Netflix.




Apple has already spent a billion, and attracted a lot of famous people for promotion. But its library is still very limited, and it will be difficult to lure users into a new fledgling service even with a couple of very good TV shows. Netflix has grown its audience over two decades.


In 2019, Netflix will spend $ 15 billion on content production. This is a huge amount. It would be enough for 300 seasons of "Game of Thrones" (in one year!), One season almost every day. To somehow compete with Netflix, HBO raised its annual budget to $ 2.5 billion. And Amazon, with its bottomless pockets, wants to spend $ 1 billion on just one series on The Lord of the Rings, making it the most expensive TV series in history.


And this arms race is not going to stop. Netflix‚Äôs annual spending on content creation is expected to increase to $ 23 billion by the year 2022.
