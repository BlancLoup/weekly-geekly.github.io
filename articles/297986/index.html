How to prepare a newsletter for Valentine's Day

Valentine's Day or Valentine's Day, of course, the most romantic of all holidays. Every year it gains great popularity in the CIS countries. And now is the time to think about the strategy of sales, congratulations and customer engagement. This will help you one of the most powerful tools of communication - email newsletter.



Types of content for Valentine's Day newsletters

1. Tips and ideas for the holiday (what to wear, what to cook, where to celebrate). Such content is extremely useful and interesting on the eve of the holiday, because subscribers seek not only to choose a gift for their beloved, but also to surprise the venue of the holiday, their outfit, delicious dinner, etc.

If you have already chosen this type of content, then make discounts on all products, and not just on the one that can be presented on Valentine's Day.



4. Special collections for the Day of all lovers. Many companies prepare special products for a particular holiday. Such products are distinguished by their symbolism (for example, heart, cupid), color, which corresponds to the holiday, etc.

  • Use the traditional symbolism that is associated with Valentine's Day: hearts, cupids, sweets and flowers. You can also use stylized images.
