Video review laptop Asus G752VT



Asus G752VT - powerful laptops from the Asus gaming division - Republic Of Gamers, replacing the G751 line. New models are equipped with IPS-display with a diagonal of 17.3 inches (matte, 1920x1080) and support for NVIDIA G-SYNC. Laptops run on Intel Core i7 Skylake processors (i7-6700HQ in the model from the review), and for graphics processing, NVIDIA Geforce GTX 970M graphics cards (3 GB GDDR5) are used. Up to 64 GB of DDR4 RAM is supported, the user has two slots. For data storage, two slots for M.2 solid-state drives (PCIE Gen3x4, no support for raid0) and a 2.5-inch hard drive bay are used. The review model has a 1 TB hard disk (5400 rpm) and a 128 GB SSD. The laptop is equipped with a 5200 mAh battery (67 Wh), which allows it to work for about 3-4 hours at low load. It should be noted that in this notebook there is no support for switching technology of NVIDIA Optimus video cards, as it is not compatible with G-SYNC.

Asus G752VT has the following ports and connectors on board:
Earphones, Microphone, Line-in
1x USB 3.1 Type-C Gen. 2 + Thunderbolt 3
4x USB 3.0,
1x Mini Displayport
1x HDMI
1x RJ-45 Gigabit Ethernet
SD card reader
Kensington Lock
