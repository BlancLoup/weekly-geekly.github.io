Video service "Mail.Ru Poster" introduces a recommendation system simultaneously with the restart of "Kinopoisk"

The Mail.Ru Poster is testing a recommendation based on data such as gender, age, and type of activity. In addition, the service takes into account the interests of users by analyzing their ratings and actions on the site. But, according to the press release , in order for the advisory service to work, users will have to evaluate at least 10 movies and TV shows.
