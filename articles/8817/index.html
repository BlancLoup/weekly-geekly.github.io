New project The Video Bay

The popular international website The Pirate Bay, which offers users various downloads, including illegal ones, intends to submit a new project, The Video Bay, in the near future.
The new project in its concept should be one of the YouTube clones, with the only difference that, in addition to static downloads, the server will also have the ability to view streaming video.
Separately, the creators of the site say that at Video Bay the bulk of the content will be generated by users, and, as the developers promise, there will be no censorship and no moral cleansing on the resource.

The main motto of the creators of Video Bay is that users themselves must decide what content is on the server and what content is not.
Also, the creators say that for all users the site will be free. However, the final timing of the project is still unknown.
