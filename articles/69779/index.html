We announce the start of registration of applications for participation in Google Developer Day 2009 in Moscow

Alexey Shelestenko, Google Marketing Russia

We're glad to inform you that we are starting to accept applications for participation in the Google Developer Day annual conference for web developers, which will be held on November 10 in Moscow. Last year, more than 400 web developers attended this conference, where they listened to 12 presentations and participated in 6 workshops devoted to various Google products for web developers. This year we are planning to cover in detail Android , App Engine , Google Wave , Chrome , GWT , AJAX API and much more.

The conference will be attended by our teams of engineers working on services for web developers and APIs, and you can communicate with them directly. We will conduct several in-depth technical sessions with a focus on helping developers write good working applications on Google and open source technologies. Those who are working on creating business applications will also be able to find something useful in the improved and expanded program of our conference. And, of course, developers will have many opportunities to get to know each other and discuss the latest mobile and web technologies during the "open door" and informal communication at the end of the conference.

We make every effort to ensure that the conference was rich, useful and interesting! To learn more about the conference and apply for participation , go to the conference website .

We are waiting for you on Google Developer Day!
