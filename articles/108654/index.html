MegaFon and MTS are testing the fare in the subway using a mobile phone



It has been 2 years since MTS and Megaphone took on testing special SIM-cards that allow you to pay for travel in the Moscow metro using a mobile phone. To implement the service, a short-range wireless high-frequency communication technology - Near Field Communication (NFC) is used . It enables data exchange between devices at a distance of about 5-10 cm.

The press service of MTS reported that the operator and the state unitary enterprise Moscow Metro launched a comprehensive solution for payment of travel on the subway using NFC technology into test operation. In total, the test involves about 300 SIM cards.

MTS is planning a commercial launch of the project at the end of 2010. - the beginning of 2011. On the territory of metro stations, special points will begin to work, where MTS subscribers will be installed an NFC antenna inside the mobile phone. An antenna can also be installed in any MTS salon in Moscow. The cost of the trip will correspond to the current metro tariffs; the subscriber will not need to pay any additional fees. The technical side of the MTS project is being implemented with the support of Express Card.

Megafon also began testing the same technology in the St. Petersburg Metro State Unitary Enterprise, and students and bloggers take part in the tests.
From the MegaFon side, the launch date of the project has not been announced, however, it can be assumed that Megafon will try to keep up with MTS. According to the director of the North-West branch of MegaFon, Georgy Getmanets, payment of travel on the subway using a mobile phone is the first stage, in the future the company intends to realize cashless payment of travel on land transport.

And what about Beeline?

A spokeswoman for VimpelCom (Beeline trademark) Anna Aybasheva told RBC that the company is in talks with several partners to implement a large-scale NFC project. She refused to provide more information about this project.

At the same time, according to A. Aybasheva, the operator intends to reach an agreement with the company Aeroexpress LLC on payment by means of a mobile journey on an electric train plying between Belorussky Station and Sheremetyevo Airport. You will be able to pay for the trip by bringing the phone to a special poster with a radio-frequency tag, after which the radio interface is launched, operating on a frequency with a radius of small action. After that, the subscriber needs to confirm the debiting of money from his account. This project is a test project, but if it is successfully completed, the scope of cooperation with Aeroexpress can be expanded.
