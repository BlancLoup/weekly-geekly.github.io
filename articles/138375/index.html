New Origin PC gaming laptop EON17-X



Rarely found are such powerful laptops as the Origin PC EON17-X. This device is more powerful than most desktops, but, of course, more expensive. So, the laptop boasts a 17.4-inch display, a six-core Core i7-3930K or Core i7-3960X processor, depending on the model. The display supports 1080p resolution. In addition, there is the possibility of adding RAM up to 32 gigabytes (DDR 3, of course).



The internal memory is equipped with a system of hard drives from SSDs. The total capacity of such a system can be a maximum of 3 terabytes. As for the graphics, the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 580M graphics card is installed in the laptop. In the maximum configuration in the laptop is installed Quadro 5010M. The laptop has all kinds of ports, including USB 3.0, FireWire, eSATA, there are HDMI and DVI outputs.



The operating system is Windows 7, 64-bit. It is a miracle from 3159 US dollars, this is for the initial configuration. How much can cost the maximum grade, it is difficult to judge, such information yet.
