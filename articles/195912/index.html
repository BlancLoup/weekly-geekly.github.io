Communigate Pro - a platform for the most popular communication services


Historically, the leaders in mobile product innovation have been the cellular network operators in the US and Europe But in the modern world, new technologies are no longer associated by consumers with an increase in the capacity of the mobile Internet or with its universal availability.

Today, the focus of users' attention is on other companies - companies providing services for communication - Facebook, Skype, Viber. They do not bear the ever-increasing burden of laying a new generation of networks, but at the same time they actively, and most importantly, effectively use them for profit.

But even in the face of these unenviable tendencies, the mobile giants of developed countries are not moving too much - revenues are still high and they have time to determine the direction of development.

Operators


However, in some developing regions where ARPU is much lower, operators have long been looking for additional ways to increase revenue from each subscriber. One of them is building your own cloud services. So did our client Moldcell, the second largest mobile operator in the Republic of Moldova:

Moldcell Comunic is a service built with Pronto! - XIMSS client Communigate Pro for browser, mobile and desktop computers. Comunic allows users to save files, contacts, receive mail and connect with friends and acquaintances through one application.

Of our customers, operators, Tele2 (Sweden) occupies a special place. Already in 2007, they launched a service to provide mail and IP telephony based on the Communigate Pro cluster solution , which currently has 2.5 million mail users and more than 300,000 voice accounts:

The picture simply demonstrates the architecture, the real cluster works on 5 backends and 16 frontends, some of which are dedicated exclusively to telephony.
