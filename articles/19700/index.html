Great service for downloading files

Prehistory

A year ago, when the glorious student days were already coming to an end, another student quest appeared in front of me. One respectable professor, who enjoys unquestioned prestige in our department, was ready to play the role of my diploma supervisor, which practically guaranteed me a free transfer of my diploma. The task was that in return, the professor wanted to get the latest version of one well-known and very expensive mathematical package. An additional complication was the need for a Linux version.

Because of such difficulties, it was not immediately possible to find the necessary distribution kit, and only on one Chinese server. I was even ready to download these 4 gigabytes, pay for traffic from my own pocket, and without worrying about writing a diploma ... but, as it turned out, the server gave the files very slowly, constantly tearing the connection, and, unfortunately, did not support the resume ( it was good at least that the distribution was broken into pieces).
And here, in fact, begins the useful part of the story.

Story.

After several days of torment and many megabytes of dead traffic, one kind comrade led me to the getfirelink.net service. A week later the distribution kit was presented to the professor, and some time later I was already celebrating an excellent diploma defense.

So what is a service that helped me out a year ago and is still in effect? Everything is very simple: the service downloads files from almost any source, and sends them to you in any convenient way. Free, and, most importantly - very quickly and reliably. In short, a sort of intermediate buffer, which can be used for a variety of purposes.

So can he?

The most obvious is to download files. And not only by direct links from http / ftp, but also from many popular file sharing sites. Rapida, ayfolder ... yes, all popular file hosting are supported with a bang. Moreover, you can also merge videos from YouTube / Rutube and other popular services of a similar direction. It is possible to merge entirely, and in parts, specifying the necessary pieces. You can specify a proxy through which the jump should be carried out. You can substitute cookies. You can substitute the referrer. Beauty!

Downloaded files are on the server for some time (now it is a little more than five days), after which they are deleted. This was done deliberately - in order to avoid storage charges. The downloaded good is available through direct links - however, you cannot use the service as a file hosting service (there is a technical possibility, but the developers honestly warn you that this is not good, as it discredits the service and may lead to its closure).
