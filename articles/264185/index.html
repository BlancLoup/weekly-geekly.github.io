6T family.
The main purpose of the appearance of such 6T family of controllers is to eliminate situations where the inflexible part of the cable (part about 2 cm long after the connector) rests on the lid or case of the server, on the processor heatsink or other parts of the server. To do this, on the 6th series of controllers, the ports are arranged so that the inflexible part of the cable does not extend beyond the geometric bounds of the controller.
