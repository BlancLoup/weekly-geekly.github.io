Dictionary online Slovennik.NET

Hello!
This article will be more interesting for the Ukrainian sector of Habr.

I think each of you ever wondered what a word means or how to spell. What do we do in most cases? Go to Google Yandex nigma another (underline the right) and look for the word. If there is a dictionary at home, then we look into it.

Personally, I have been using such a service as slovnyk.net for a long time, and, asking around from my friends, I realized that almost no one knows about it!
The service is developing quite actively, it is nice to have the page and design chosen.
According to data from the ‚ÄúAbout SLOVNIK.net‚Äù section, the database contains over 220,000 entries and 20,000 phraseologies from 28 sources. (as well as the words of users).

If you are registered on the site, you can edit the dictionary entry, add an image or usage examples, word forms and more. You can also add new words.

There is an active active forum where you can ask a question about linguistics.

Chrome search


And a nice addition - in Chrome we add the ability to search in the dictionary :

Keyword: word
Request: slovnyk.net/?swrd=%s

And write in the address bar "word% s"
