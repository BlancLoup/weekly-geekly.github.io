National video chat "My Choice": a view of the developer

In 2010, the advertising agency Danke developed on its own the national video chat My Choice . The basis of the project was the new technology Adobe Stratus, which allows you to assign each flash drive a unique id, which allows you to connect 2 flash drives and transfer video packets directly. Starting with version 10, Adobe Flash Player supports the ability to transfer data directly from one computer to another via UDP protocol without the help of a media server. The task of the server-side service (the so-called peer server) is to communicate the sending and receiving objects on two or more users' computers. This service is Adobe Stratus.

More from the developers (in English of course):

This ensures good communication without the organization of multiple servers and ultra-fast broadband communication channels.

The project itself is different from other similar projects. This is not a roulette chat, because here it is impossible to connect in a few seconds with a random interlocutor. This is not a quick chat, this is a project for live communication of people who meet. Of course, everyone understands that each person may simply not be aware of the existence of other people on the other ends of the country who will make him happier. This project was developed for users who want to find their soul mate, communicating with her live.

Most of the users of the project act on the site as follows: they search for people using various search criteria for the questionnaires, view 2-3 people on the project and write them messages. Some - immediately call. If a person is online, a corresponding message will appear on his screen that they want to connect to it.

In the video application itself, the user can change the quality of the transmitted video, the sound level, select input tools. The project is scheduled subscription fee.
