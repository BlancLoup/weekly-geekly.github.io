Honda and Linux will be friends



Tom Chazain, one of the most famous riders in the Indianapolis 500 competition, owner of the team named after himself, said that the on-board computer of his Panoz / Honda car would be running Linux.

The logo of the Chastain machine, which is capable of accelerating to more than 320 km / h, has a Linux logo already stuck - a good-natured penguin.

The contract with Linux at the command of Partan is simple. Linux is organizing a promotional campaign to raise money in favor of the Chastain team among its fans of this most well-known open-source operating system in favor of Linux advertising. So far, Linux has raised only $ 7,500 out of an estimated $ 25,000.

Collecting money quietly and without a pomp is managed by lobby4linux.com . Money is collected through its social network.
