For 7 Russian technoparks allocated 26.3 billion rubles

The amount of budget funding for the creation and development of 7 technology parks in Russia will amount to 26.3 billion rubles. This was announced today by the Minister of Information Technology and Communications of Russia Leonid Reiman, Prime-TASS reports.

According to the minister, this will be funding from different levels of the budget. 6 billion rubles will be allocated for the creation and development of a technopark in the Moscow region, in 2007 - 170 million rubles; in St. Petersburg - 3.4 billion rubles, in 2007 - 420 million rubles; in the Novosibirsk region - 4.4 billion rubles, in 2007 - 680 million rubles; in the Nizhny Novgorod region - 3.1 billion rubles, in 2007 - 650 million rubles; in the Republic of Tatarstan - 2.9 billion rubles, in 2007 - 420 million rubles; in the Kaluga region - 3.5 billion rubles, in 2007 - 800 million rubles; in the Tyumen region - 3 billion rubles, in 2007 - 600 million rubles.
