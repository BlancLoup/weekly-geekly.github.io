Colloquiums at the faculty of Yandex in HSE: molecular biology

June 20 began accepting documents at the Faculty of Computer Science, which last year with the participation of Yandex was opened at the Higher School of Economics. There were so many people willing to study on it that the number of budget places was then doubled. As a result, 320 people entered the first course.

At the faculty, in addition to classes for students, there are special seminars to which anyone can come. They are held in the framework of the HSE Colloquium of the Faculty of Computer Science. The seminars are conducted by scientists not only from HSE, but from other universities and research centers - Moscow State University, MIPT, the Mathematical Institute and the Institute of Systems Analysis of the Russian Academy of Sciences, MIT, Microsoft Research, the Yandex Data Analysis School. Come to listen to them can not only a student or employee of the FKN, but anyone. From today, we begin to publish records of selected speeches.



The first seminar we chose was conducted by the famous Russian bioinformatist Mikhail Gelfand . From it you will learn about the main informational processes occurring in the cell during the implementation of the genomic program.
