Someone took a step forward in their professional development. Someone left the post of general director of the company in pursuit of their new ideas. Someone left the company, as he did not have enough time to read books and rest.

And someone was offered to promote "the direction of the company with unlimited resources and full support."

1. Former Alfa Bank top manager and Microsoft evangelist Peter Didenko have been appointed editor-in-chief of the Lifehacker publication.
