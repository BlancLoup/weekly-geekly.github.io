Electricity is consumed by both the residential sector and numerous industrial enterprises. The use of billing technology of payment and payment allows you to reduce customer service time, as well as quickly receive information about payments received. Legal entities can automate payments for electricity. What does all this software work on? What server capacities are required for monthly processing of readings of millions of electricity meters? After all, it is calculated on them how much you have to pay for the electricity spent in the last month, what numbers will be in the electricity bill received by you and millions of other consumers. A bit of history


More than 80 years ago, an independent self-supporting organization, Energosbyt, appeared in the Soviet Union, part of the well-known trust, the Moscow Association of State Power Plants. At that time, the power system of Moscow was designed for only 150,000 subscribers.

In 1959, in the process of restructuring the electrical service system in the USSR, the operation of meters and other monitoring equipment was massively introduced. Thus, the need to maintain such equipment and computing equipment appeared, for which a special workshop was created at Energosbyt.

As an independent energy sales company, Mosenergosbyt PJSC began its activities in 2005 after separation from Mosenergo. Being the legal successor of Mosenergo, it supplies electric energy to individuals and legal entities in Moscow and the region.


Mosenergosbyt supplies consumers from the wholesale and retail markets. The annual volume of electricity sold exceeds 82 billion kWh (8% of the electricity generated in the Russian Federation).

Data center


The business of energy companies places particularly high demands on IT. This applies to commercial calculations, and enterprise management, and technological processes. In 2010, Mosenergosbyt implemented a comprehensive project to create a fault-tolerant scalable data processing center that provides the most critical business applications. Creating a new unified data center for centralized service of all business applications required due to the increase in data volume.


The data center of Mosenergosbyt, as the data center is supposed to, meets the technical requirements for the operation of server equipment and other IT infrastructure. In addition, it has growth opportunities.

Now the company's customer base includes more than 7.2 million subscribers: 279 thousand legal entities (including outside the Moscow region) and over 7 million residential consumers. Customer service in more than 47 thousand square meters. kilometers carry more than 100 client offices. Billing alone with such a number of customers turns into a very large-scale task. It is logical that without a reliable data center in this case is not enough.

DPC "Mosenergosbyt" is located in Moscow on the street. Vavilov st. 9 - in the same building as her office. Now it has 29 racks. The data center was built to centralize the network and computing infrastructure, deploying a dedicated storage area network (SAN).

Its creation allowed Mosenergosbyt to abandon server rooms at remote offices of the company, and also simplified the implementation of billing systems. In addition, the company received all the advantages of modern data centers in the form of industrial air conditioning, fault-tolerant power supplies, and fire safety. The risks of downtime and losses during various emergency situations have decreased.

In air conditioning and uninterrupted power supply systems, not only current, but also future needs were taken into account. And of course, the redundancy of the components of engineering systems will ensure the efficiency of the data center if something suddenly fails. In case of an accident in the power grid, five UPSs and two diesel generators can handle the power supply.

Money love the bill


The word "billing" is familiar to many. This is a set of processes and decisions responsible for collecting information about the use of services, their pricing, billing customers, processing payments.

Mosenergosbyt, as an energy sales company, is responsible for settlements with electricity consumers in Moscow and the region. For this, a large amount of data is collected and processed, such as meter readings. During accounting periods, consumers receive a calculation to pay for the services of the energy supplying organization according to the tariffs. The company uses an automated method of charging and accounting for payments.


"Billing" in relation to this field of activity, means a unified system of personal accounts, which allows you to automatically take into account the payment of services for utility services. The use of new technology for calculating and accepting payments from individuals reduced the time for servicing, made it possible to promptly receive information about received payments and automate payments for electricity.
