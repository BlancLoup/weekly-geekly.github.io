Began open testing domain registration service Google Domains



Google Domains has moved from a private beta to a public beta. Now any user from the United States can register with a service that offers domain registration with additional features: free private registration, redirection of domain mail to Gmail, free domain redirection, support for up to 100 subdomains, and various options for first-level domains (including newfangled ones such as .guru and .club - full list ). Registration cost starts at $ 12 / year .

Google agreed with the providers of web services whose services can be immediately accessible to those who used this registration: Shopify (online megazin), Squarespace (CMS for blogs), Weebly (visual editor of sites), Wix (visual editor of sites).
