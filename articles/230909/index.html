Will delivery delivery from eBay with the help of "Post of Russia" now be faster?

July 23, 2014, General Director of the Federal Postal Operator Dmitry Strashnov and Vice President of eBay Inc. Wendy Jones, from eBay, an American online retailer, signed a memorandum of understanding and cooperation.


The signing of this document implies the integration of eBay and Post of Russia information systems, which will simplify tracking of packages and also imply a reduction in customs clearance and delivery times for goods within Russia.

The first country, the delivery of goods from which will be carried out under this project, will be China, it is assumed that the delivery time of the parcel will be about 9 days.

According to the site " Mail of Russia ".
