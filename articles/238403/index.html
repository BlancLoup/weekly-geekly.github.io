The Windows Phone and Windows app store developer accounts have become perpetual, for only $ 19!



This month, Microsoft announced several useful features and bonuses for Windows Phone and Windows developers. All of them are already available in Russia.

Perpetual registration with Dev Center


We are continuing to merge Windows Phone and Windows development. Now the Development Center is also united, from where the developer can manage his applications, receive information and participate in various bonus programs.

Starting from last week, the Development Center requires only a one-time registration fee of $ 19 for an individual developer who allows you to create and publish applications in the Windows Phone Store and Windows Store , with no annual renewal fee.

Each of our 600,000+ registered developers worldwide has already received a corresponding e-mail.
Naturally, this change is also valid for all new developers who are just thinking about registering and creating applications.

New program for application developers


We have a new program Dev Center Benefits - designed to accelerate the success of your applications on our platform. Now we will help develop and promote your applications.

The program is available to all registered developers worldwide.

Depending on the success that you have already achieved, the level of support from our side is growing.



Conditions and how to join the program - look at the site: Dev Center Benefits

Affiliate program for developers of services and components


If you are a professional developer, then most likely you are actively using third-party components and APIs. This saves you time and allows you not to reinvent the wheel again and again.

Last week an affiliate program was announced for developers of components and services for Windows Phone and Windows.

This program will give developers early access to new software, joint marketing, event support, and more.

If you are a developer of any useful components, modules, services and add-ons, then you can register in the Dev Center Partner program.
