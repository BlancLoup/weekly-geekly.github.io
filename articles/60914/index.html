Windows 7 Starter does not impose a limit on the number of applications

A message appeared on the zdnet blog stating that Microsoft will not use the restriction of three simultaneously running applications in Windows 7 Starter.
It is noted that the decision was made taking into account the market of "small" laptops (yes, the word netbook is not persistently spoken by the corporation officials). Starter edition - the youngest of the line of Windows 7, the price of it has not been disclosed.
Earlier it was reported that in this version it will be impossible to work simultaneously in three applications.
