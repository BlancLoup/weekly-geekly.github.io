A secure phone for all occasions - a small selection of the most interesting examples



At any time of the year, users of conventional smartphones often bring their devices into disrepair. Someone will spill some water on the smartphone, someone else will smash the phone bought on credit last week on a concrete floor. Ways to destroy technology, in fact, a lot more than we can imagine.

Naturally, no one likes to break up or drown their favorite communication devices. Knowing this, manufacturers have created a whole range of protected devices that are not afraid of water, dust, or shock. With them you can go fishing, climb the mountains or drink coffee peacefully in a bar - to destroy such a miracle of technology is not so simple. In the continuation - a list of new secure phones. This is the first part of the collection, in fact there are a lot of such devices. Pick decided to make from expensive to cheap.

This is one of the most secure phones in its niche. The manufacturer himself believes that the device should be used as a second smartphone for people who are engaged in active sports such as mountaineering, or work in extreme conditions. I must say that the phone is really not afraid of falling on concrete or asphalt. But for such strength you have to pay in size - it simply does not fit into your pocket.

It really can be worn only as an alternative to a regular phone - for example, in a jacket or a construction suit. The case, by the way, is non-separable, but thanks to this the phone can also live in water if it is dropped there.

The phone has a front and rear camera, LED flash, which can also serve as a flashlight. The screen, of course, is not very - its resolution is only 480 * 800. But for secure smartphones, this is practically the norm that can be understood and accepted.

Memory 16 GB, RAM - 1 GB, pretty well. By the way, there is even an emergency call button - if something happens, you can call for help.
