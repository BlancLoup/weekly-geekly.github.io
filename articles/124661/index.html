Beer in Two Clicks



Hello!

June 5, 2011 Paymentwall held a Bavarian-style party on the eve of the E3 conference, which was held in Los Angeles last month. The party was held at the company's headquarters in San Francisco in honor of the launch of the 2-Click Payments widget.

2-Click Payments widget, designed by Paymentwall, allows you to quickly process credit cards in the simplest way. This small widget can be invoked by JavaScript in the layer above the flash game, or as an independent widget in an arbitrary location of the application. 2-Click Payments widget allows you to link prices in each region or country using the admin panel to avoid price fluctuations in different regions. Also, to monetize users from regions where credit cards are less popular, the widget automatically shows alternative payment options by default. In this case, you can monetize through payment methods such as PayPal, mobile payments, landline payments, e-wallets and prepaid cards, bank transfer and direct debit, as well as payment terminals. 2-Click Payments widget is a global solution with support for 18 languages ‚Äã‚Äãand over 30 currencies from around the world. The widget was originally designed to provide flexible solutions and a convenient interface with a high level of customization.
