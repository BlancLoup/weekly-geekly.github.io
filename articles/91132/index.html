What if the Oscars were given to the sites?

However, site developers do not receive any recognition. Is this not a bit unfair in the Internet era - not getting a bit of glory for online movie presentations?



Most modern movie websites are made in Flash, even if it is not at all justified. When creating websites, standards and requirements for accessing splash pages and accessing content are often not followed. Developers allow themselves the luxury of neglecting general principles and usability standards, since the main task is to draw attention to the film and help unleash it. Suppose, however, that these developers got their Oscars, Bears and Palm Branches. Will this not be a powerful incentive to create bright and convenient sites?
