Fraud scheme



The fraud scheme is pretty simple. This is called " Social Engineering. " The candidate writes to you on one and your contacts, NOT through the site free-lance.ru. It seems another person gives a link to the account of a person with a good positive reputation. He says he can do your job. Ready to work through the transaction without risk.

Since free-lance.ru is actively trying to get freelancers on the SBR, we can expect that many freelancers will want to raise their rating using this method of payment. After reading the description of the service, the novice customer will not see anything wrong in it and will agree. Will create a project on the original interface of the original site.
