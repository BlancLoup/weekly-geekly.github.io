Design camp online: see conference broadcast on December 12 at 10:00 (MSK)

December 12 at 10:00 am (MSK) we invite all those who are not indifferent to modern digital and interactive design to join the online broadcast of the second Design Camp.

This year, Microsoft and the British Higher School of Design are preparing for you even more reports, experts, practices and communication. We will concentrate around the four main blocks: application design, games and websites, graphics, animation and infographics, design thinking and UX, as well as design for modern devices.
Player broadcast under the cut, connect!

Especially for you, we invited experts from Yandex, the Mail.ru Group, the Strelka Institute for Media, Architecture and Design, Scream School, Interactive Lab, Parallels, Futurice Ltd., Enlighted Digital, e-Legion, and the leading teachers of the British Higher School of Design " and etc.
And so that everyone can feel like an active part of the conference, we will add workshops and separate QA-sessions. A special guest will also come to us - Bart Decrem , senior vice president of The Walt Disney Company, and will talk about the company's experience and strategy in the field of mobile games, in particular such hits as Tap Tap Revenge, Where's My Water, Temple Run: Brave and oz. Do not miss!
