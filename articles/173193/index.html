The results of the second week of the competition for BizSpark startups "Tell about yourself"

Monday, which means it's time to announce a new winner of the start-up story contest BizSpark . It so happened that the story that was chosen as the best at the joint meeting of the Microsoft BizSpark team and the RusBase editorial staff was sent to us the very first one last week.

So, the winner of this week is the MACROSCOP project.

MACROSCOP is the first professional Russian solution designed specifically for IP cameras. The startup is a resident of the Skolkovo Technopark and a member of the Microsoft Bizspark program. The service performs the processing, analysis, storage and display of video data, allowing to combine into a single system many servers and monitoring workstations.

An interview with the MACROSCOP team will appear this week on the RusBase pages and remind you that the winner of the week remains a participant in the main competition - for the prize of the month.
