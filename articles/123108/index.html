On the Sony Ericsson phones the opportunity to make video calls via Skype

Skype Day on the main.


Good news finally came from Skype - today they announced an update of the Android application with video chat support in their blog .

Video calls require Android OS version 2.3 and higher and a front camera. Four devices are currently supported, two of which are ours: the Sony Ericsson Xperia neo and the Sony Ericsson Xperia pro (Sony Ericsson Xperia neo on the Market ).

In addition, all new Sony Ericsson smartphones equipped with a front camera - that is, Xperia mini pro and Xperia ray , will support video calls by default - as soon as they go on sale.

Video calls in Skype for Android work both on WiFi and on 3G connection. You can download the application from the Android Market or via the Skype.com/m link if you go there from your phone.

Sony Ericsson has accumulated all the best trends this year: devices on the latest version of Android 2.3 out of the box, an open bootloader , an update for the Xperia X10 , an abundance of partners, including game manufacturers, NFC solutions and, in fact, Skype.
