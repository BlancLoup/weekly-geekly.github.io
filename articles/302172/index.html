Hosting provider RUVDS joins ISIC student discount program partners



RUVDS is the first company to start providing IAAS services to participants of the ISIC international student discount program . IAAS (infrastructure as a service) is a fast-growing IT segment for the provision of VPS computing infrastructure and related services.

The cloud service provider RUVDS and the International Student Organization ISIC will provide holders of international cards with unique conditions for using the provider's infrastructure. All participants of the program will receive a 15% discount on the services of the provider, which will make the services of a cloud low-cost airline even more accessible.

In addition to students, holders of ITIC (international certificate for teachers) and IYTC (international certificate for young people up to 29 years old inclusive), who provide special discounts for teachers and young people around the world, can also take advantage of the discount.

Cardholders can receive special offers for a variety of services from leading companies, in particular in the field of travel and transportation services, such as air travel, bus and rail transport, ferries and car rentals. Other discounts are available for services such as visits to museums and historical monuments, various cultural attractions, bars and restaurants, accommodation in hotels and inns.
Schoolchildren from 12 years old, students of technical schools, colleges and colleges, as well as students of music and sports schools, full-time full-time and evening students, post-graduate students / interns of full-time education can apply for ISIC.

RUVDS is a dynamically developing hosting provider specializing in providing corporate class IAAS services. Among the company's partners are the largest financial institutions: Finam JSC, BCS Company, a number of commercial banks and social projects, including the recently opened largest Coworking 14 in Russia, for which RUVDS provided virtual servers for clients. In March of this year, HUAWEI became a partner of RUVDS, equipping the company's data center with hardware infrastructure and a virtualization platform.
