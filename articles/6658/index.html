Secrets of the East: why did they close access to a religious resource

In Uzbekistan, access to the Russian Internet resource Portal-Credo.ru , which publishes religious news, is closed. This was reported by the Norwegian edition of Forum18 , citing a series of unsuccessful attempts to access this site from Tashkent. The fact that not everything is in order with access to the site from the territory of Uzbekistan can be understood from a comparison of data on the attendance of Portal Credo in the republic and its neighboring Central Asian countries. In three weeks, only 13 visitors came from Uzbekistan to the site, whereas from Kazakhstan - 167, and from Tajikistan - 24 (while the population of Tajikistan is four times less than the population of Uzbekistan).

Of course, the mere fact of closing access to a particular Internet resource in Central Asia is not a sensation. Everybody understands why Uzbekistan cannot get to the Muslim website in Uzbekistan, which tells about the oppression of Muslims in the republic.
