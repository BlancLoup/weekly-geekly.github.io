A bunch of Windows 8 / Office costs for OEMs $ 30

The report says that a $ 120 copy of Windows 8 may cost OEMs only $ 30 while this incredibly generous 75% discount remains in force.

It is worth noting that the source Wall Street Journal does not distinguish between the desktop and the RT-version of Windows 8 and Office.
