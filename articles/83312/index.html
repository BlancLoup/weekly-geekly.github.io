Safari - we block flash

Probably for many Mac OSX users it is not a secret how flash banners-videos load their machines. A simple web site sometimes turns into a processor loaded up to 100%, rotating at 6000 rpm a cooler, forcing you to think about aviation :) You can still accept the flash video, but here the banners are outraged. Firefox has a great Flashblock plugin, but what about Safari? Safari in this regard is also not far behind. Anyone wishing to ease the load during a banal surfing, I ask for habrakat.

The decision to completely disable plugins will not be considered, this is too drastic. Better use the plugins, for our purposes there are two. The first is Clicktoflash , the second is Flashless . Both are free. Both have the same principle - flash on click.

Clicktoflash

Popular plugin. Simple and elegant, there is a white list of sites where the flash will always open, you can view videos in H.264 (Youtube). Excellent and popular solution.



Flashless

Menia is a popular solution, but in terms of functionality it is not inferior to the first. Able to display the video, shorkates (shift + click - play video in H.264, option + click - download video, command + click - play original).
