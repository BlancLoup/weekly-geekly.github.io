Welcome to South DevFest 2017



Large-scale IT events are not the prerogative of Moscow and St. Petersburg. That is why GDG Krasnodar and GDG Rostov-on-Don unite and jointly organize the second South DevFest 2017 conference in Rostov-on-Don. In one day, more than 400 IT specialists and people who are not indifferent to high technologies will gather on the same platform to communicate, discuss and share with each other experiences in mobile, web, VR / AR development.


South DevFest 2017 is part of a global series of events held around the world by the Google Developer Group with the support of Google. The conference will be the largest IT event in the South of Russia and will be held on September 9 in Rostov-on-Don at Pl. Gagarin, 1, Congress Hall DGTU. Participants are expected to interactive, workshops and reports on topics React Native, development for Android, Kotlin, AR / VR, Unity, Golang, DevOps, neural networks, TensorFlow, IoT, Web from speakers from St. Petersburg, Kazan, Omsk, Moscow, Krasnodar, Rostov, New Mexico and Amsterdam.


Some of the speakers are:


  • Bob van Luijt - creative technologist and designer, with a report on the Internet of things, knowledge management, RDF, graphic databases, IoT and the use of cloud technologies
  • Alexey Korovyansky , GDE Android, will talk about the possibilities of augmented reality on the example of Google Tango
  • Ishan Khanna - android developer, Booking.com
  • Cameron Smith - specialist in neural networks, research assistant Artomatix
  • Artem Andreenko - Backend and DevOps at Prisma Labs, host of the GolangShow podcast, organizer of the Russian-speaking Go community, will give a talk on distributed p2p private networks with Meshbird, etc.

Conference tickets are already available on our website . For habrozhiteli we have prepared a special page , clicking on which you can buy a ticket at a special rate. For students throughout the entire period of the sale of tickets there is a special price - 500 rubles. The purchased ticket gives you the opportunity to visit absolutely all sections of the conference. We are also waiting for everyone who wants to socialize in an informal setting, on the afterparty on the next day, September 10 (seats are purchased in advance).


South DevFest 2017 is an opportunity to get invaluable experience from professionals in your field, learn about new technologies, pump skills in the technologies that you are learning now, discuss new products with colleagues, become part of a large IT community.
