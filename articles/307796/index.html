Learning English with cards

A little less than a year ago we talked about how we came up with cards that help in learning English idioms, and collected all the cards in one mobile application.



At the moment, the application is not one, but five, and they all help in learning English using cards on different topics:

  • Idiom Land (idioms) - for iOS , for Android
  • Slang Cards (slang) - for iOS , for Android
  • Phrasal Cards (phrasal verbs) - for iOS , for Android
  • Animal Cards (animals) - for iOS , for Android
  • Food Cards (food and drinks) - for iOS , for Android

We strive to make learning English not boring, so we tried to make applications that would be useful and interesting at the same time.

The application for studying idioms contains 1000 cards, the rest of the applications - 500 cards each. All applications are in English, so to use the first three applications (idioms, phrasal verbs and slang) you need to have a good level of language. The latter two applications (animals and food), on the contrary, are designed for a basic level, although they contain much more cards than are required for basic knowledge, so they broaden their horizons about the animal world and everything related to food.

In August, all applications have special offers that allow you to purchase all the cards at once with a big discount.
