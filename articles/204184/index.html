Smartphone Lexand Vega: a pleasant "budget" on the platform MediaTek MT6572

The budget smartphone from Lexand, the Vega model, got into my catching legs. This brand has gained fame thanks to navigators, it also had (and is) DVRs, readers, routers, tablets ... The first Lexand smartphone was released in May 2013 ( here you can read its review), and in early autumn it added another "Brood" of such devices. Lexand Vega - the most inexpensive of them, the model is estimated at approximately 3,800 rubles. Let's see what we are offered for this money ...



The delivery package is standard: the smartphone itself, a USB-microUSB lanyard, a charger unit, headphones, a brief user manual and a warranty card.

Assembled Lexand Vega carefully, failed to stray. Smartphone looks quite unusual. The first thing I noticed is the lack of touch-sensitive keys for controlling Android functions under the screen. Although starting from the fourth version of the mentioned OS they are an optional element, the overwhelming majority of developers use them. They are absent in Lexand Vega, and I personally don‚Äôt like it very much - I‚Äôm just used to physical buttons. But there is a ‚Äúkeyless‚Äù approach and an advantage: Vega resembles a diminished LG Nexus 4. I heard this statement from two people. I do not see a phenomenal resemblance, but yes, something trimmed can be traced.

Also on the front of the Lexand Vega are a conversational speaker, covered with a mesh, proximity and illumination sensors, a 1.3 megapixel front camera. For Skype and crossbows, its capabilities are enough, and more from such matrices no one requires.



The back of the smartphone, which in combination is the battery cover, is made of rubberized plastic. The material is quite practical: firstly, it does not slip in his hands; secondly, it is not easily soiled; thirdly, if you put a shallow scratch on the back panel, it will hardly be visible. At the bottom of the back side of the device is the speaker, in the center is the inscription Lexand, and at the top is the main camera lens and LED flash.

Under the back panel is the battery capacity of 1450 mAh. By today's standards it is not very much, but Lexand Vega shows a good battery life - about two days in average operation with calls, social networks and mail, but without video. Also under the cover there are two compartments for SIM-cards and a memory expansion slot for microSD cards.



The ends of the Lexand Vega edging silver frame. Controls and connectors are located as usual. The screen lock key is on the right side of the upper end, next to it is a 3.5 mm jack for connecting headphones or a headset. The volume rocker is on the left side, while the right side is empty. Connector microUSB developers placed on the bottom. In general, the smartphone fits well in the hand, I have not identified problems with ergonomics.



Let's go to the screen. It is clear that a smartphone with a price below 4 thousand rubles is unlikely to install an IPS-matrix. Here in Lexand Vega and used the display, made by technology TFT-TN, its diagonal is 4 inches, and resolution - 800 x 480 pixels. The screen has a high brightness, but with viewing angles things are much worse, they are not as wide as we would like. In general, the display is not bad, the weak part of Lexand Vega cannot be called precisely.
