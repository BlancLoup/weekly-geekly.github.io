Professional Bloggers Union

In America, a public association of bloggers and journalists Media Bloggers Association was created .

As in these unions, members of the Media Bloggers Association receive free legal assistance and advice. In addition, association members can take out a BlogInsure insurance that protects against occupational hazards, namely, from accusations of defamation, tampering with another's privacy, copyright infringement, etc. This kind of insurance used to be available only to professional journalists.

MBA membership fees are $ 25 per year. To join the association, you need to take courses and pass the certification exam for the qualification "blogger".
