Laboratory: Industrial Cybersecurity Meetup # 4

May 28 at 19:00 in our Moscow office will host the fourth meeting of cybersecurity experts of the automated process control systems.

Together with Ilya Karpov and Evgeny Druzhinin, we will summarize the results of the standoff in the industrial city - one of the quests of the last Positive Hack Days 8 conference. We will discuss everything step by step - from architecture to attack vectors, evaluate the performance of the teams of opponents and defenders of the cyber city and analyze solutions. We will also look at the most interesting holes and vulnerabilities in the security of urban infrastructure.

Further, Laboratory employee Anton Shipulin will introduce us to the Security PHA Review method and determine the requirements for cybersecurity of an industrial facility (PHA) using the HAZOP method.

After that, we discuss the managed detection and response (MDR) view models in the process control system.

In the fourth report, Sergey Pariev will assess the trends in the development of embedded information security mechanisms in industrial systems and will talk about trusted platforms and cryptography.
