On the eve of the presentation, Tesla Motors provided some details about the new Powerpack 2



Tesla Motors today announced the launch of Powerpack 2, a new model of battery backup power system. It should be noted that the presentation of Tesla, in which the company was going to show their new products, should take place tomorrow.

Powerpack 2 differs from its predecessor by a serious optimization of its design. Due to the revision of the implementation of modules and internal electronics, the company's engineers managed to double the energy storage density, according to the company's official blog .
