Overview


What is Techno Anarchism? Techno-Anarchism is a theory based on giving people the opportunity to lead each other, using technology as an intermediary. The system uses the governing bodies as temporary structures that are built, and then understood. In general, Techno-Anarchism can be described as the right of a people to make their own decisions and, if necessary, to protect their choices.

Scalable invariant holons


At the moment, our system is designed on the principle that allows you to completely depend on others. Imported oil, imported cars and the US dollar are excellent examples of how the United States has built into the global society, but have become dependent on it. Although the USA has its own deposits, for various reasons they insist on using imported oil 2 . They can open up access to energy reserves, but this will not mean anything, because oil pumped from the coast of Florida must be distributed among all residents of the United States. Until we unite, the benefits will be small. But we will not unite as long as the good does not look promising. So there can be no question of unity. This argument shows the lack of the concept of a nation: if one aspect of a nation collapses, then it affects all dependent aspects 3 ; This concept is characteristic of all goods and services. The American dollar is the currency that stands behind everything, but what if it collapses? Since The United States is interconnected with other countries, now it will lead to a global problem, whereas this, in fact, should be a local problem 4 .

How do we solve this problem? Scalable invariant holons - the answer to the question of dependencies, the problem of confidence and the solution of questions of stability. Scalable invariant holons are territories that scale the needs of a country to the needs of the local population. This concept allows holons to be independent, completely stable and relying only on themselves. If the United States were divided into scalable invariant holons, they would not feel the need for imported oil, because each holon would extract oil or use natural energy sources. Each holon will be engaged in production for making money and for ensuring their own needs. The task is to make a small society that would be completely autonomous and could function independently. What is the optimal holon size? As low as possible, to the extent possible, given the 100% stability, but without restrictions on the movement of people inside the holon 5 . For example, the holon creates a new automobile brand "X". This holon is the main producer of orange juice in the states. Perhaps the brand "X" will release a car that rides on orange juice? Let the example be contrived, but the very concept of creation from what is available and studied is that reliable and understandable idea, which is the cornerstone of sustainability. Although every holon is completely independent, this does not mean that it should be isolated. Holons can join forces in tasks, events or even in production, but self-sustainability provides a guarantee of the usefulness of the holon. Crime, Punishment and Judicial System


Techno-Anarchism needs a new judicial system. Anything could be a crime. If someone wants to go to justice, all he needs to do is call the court system using his HUD-glasses and the identifier of another person. The call to the judiciary initializes the search and convening infrastructure. If successful, the claimant and the defendant should choose the desired punishment. The claimant may indicate any punishment he wants, but if it is too severe and, if the defendant is found guilty, the defendant is immediately released. If the defendant is found not guilty, then his desired sentence is carried out in the event that the council finds this to be justified. This state of affairs gives true meaning to justice within society.

If an ordinary crime occurs, you can call the police, but you have to pay for it through insurance or from your pocket.

People can still be arrested. Laws regarding arrests may vary from holon to holon. An extradition process can be implemented between different holons. So a person accused of one holon but residing in another can be extradited to the one where he was accused.
