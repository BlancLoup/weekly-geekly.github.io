Rovio has released a new game Amazing Alex for iOS and Android



The creator of the most popular series of mobile games Angry Birds , Rovio, has released a completely new puzzle for iOS and Android called Amazing Alex , in which players need to complete 100 levels using 35 fully interactive objects, writes TNW . Players will also be able to create levels themselves and share them with other users.

As already told in the announcement of the game at Habr√©, the amazing Alex is an independent boy who loves to invent all sorts of complicated things, which are Goldberg machines - extremely cumbersome and intricate devices that perform very simple functions.

Amazing Alex costs 99 cents for the iPhone , $ 2.99 for the iPad and is available in three versions for Android ( trial , premium and HD ) - just like the Angry Birds games, which for all platforms were downloaded more than a billion times.

Rovio also promises to release games for Windows Phone , Mac and PC in the near future - similar to the release scheme of Angry Birds Space , which was downloaded 100 million times in just 76 days.

TechCrunch writes that Rovio intends to create a new gaming franchise, which will become an additional factor before the planned next year IPO , during which the company can be valued at $ 9 billion. In 2011, Rovio received $ 48 million in profit before tax.
