Salaries of IT professionals in the middle of 2018



We publish the second report of the My Circle salary service. The report is based on data on 7900+ salaries contributed by our users over the past six months. 70% of salaries paid by developers, the rest - by administrators, testers, managers, analysts, designers, eycharami, marketers and support specialists.

This time, we will not only look at current salaries for major IT specializations, programming languages ‚Äã‚Äãand companies, but also see the semi-annual dynamics of some of them.


A little about the salary service "My Circle"


The salary service was launched on the ‚ÄúMy Circle‚Äù at the end of November 2017 with the goal of regular monitoring of salaries in the IT industry. Salaries are left by the specialists themselves, we collect them and provide them in an aggregated and anonymous form to everyone in open access. All data presented in the current report, as well as any other data, anyone can get on their own using the salary calculator .



All salaries are in rubles. These are the salaries received on hands, after deduction of all taxes. Dots indicate specific salaries. A group of points for each sample is visualized using a ‚Äúbox with a mustache‚Äù. Total salary change


Over the past six months, the average median salary throughout the IT industry as a whole grew by 5.9%, from 85,000 to 90,000 rubles. Half of all wages is in the range between 58,000 and 135,000 rubles. The increase was due to the increase in wages, which were below the average in Moscow and St. Petersburg, as well as due to the increase in wages, which were above the average in other cities.




Salaries for major IT specializations




The growth of the average median wage is observed in the field of HR, development, administration, design and management. The fall is observed in marketing, support and analytics.



If you look at the regions, then the growth of the average median wage in all regions is observed only in development, and the fall is only in marketing. In the Eichar and design growth is observed only in Moscow and St. Petersburg.

In other areas there is a movement of salaries in one direction and in the other direction at the same time, depending on the region. For example, you can see that the overall increase in the average salary in the administration comes at the expense of Moscow and the regions, in management - at the expense of Moscow, despite the fall in the average salary in these areas in St. Petersburg.




Software Developer Salaries





For all the specializations of the development, we see an increase in the average median salary, especially strong in game development and the frontend.



When splitting into regions, unequivocal growth is observed only in the frontend in Moscow and St. Petersburg. For all other specializations, there is growth in some regions and a fall in others.

For example, the overall wage growth in game development comes at the expense of St. Petersburg and the regions, despite its fall in Moscow. A wage increase in desktop development - due to its growth in the regions, despite the fall in Moscow and St. Petersburg.
