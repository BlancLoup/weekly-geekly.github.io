Acer at CES 2016: monitors, tablets, gaming laptops and ultra mobile transformer

IT-companies had such a tradition: to show some unusual products at large exhibitions in order to cause more noise in the press. All these unusual features were sold, as a rule, badly, but, according to marketing theories, they drew attention to more ordinary products.



So it is, or not, but the situation in the consumer IT-market in recent years is such that there are neither funds nor human resources for extravagant solutions. All companies need products that can be sold. It is desirable huge circulation. Because money is really very necessary. Perhaps that is why at the Consumer Electronics Show in 2016 there were practically no new products made specifically to attract attention. Well, except that with the exception of the Samsung refrigerator with a tablet integrated into one of the doors. All the others clearly planned to please the buyer without intermediaries, right on the store shelf. And Acer is no exception.

Acer traditionally shows the newest features in the Hard Rock Hotel, among rock and roll artifacts and popular culture. Passing the costumes of The Beatles, the guitar of Johnny Cash and his favorite Elvis robe, you arrive in the secret room a little agitated. Which, of course, on hand to the organizers of the show.

New products, however, this time were relatively few. The performance margin even for quite budget devices is hefty, changing a laptop or tablet a year after buying has long ceased to be really necessary. And that's what Acer found necessary to add to its range.
