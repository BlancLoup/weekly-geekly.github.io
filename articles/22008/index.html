Division of errors

The Jcraft building impressed all of New York. Majestic, spiers of the mysterious creation of architecture, hundreds of floors, dozens of elevators ...

How well to work in such a big company, Fox thought. Stable salary, great career opportunities, social package - what else do you need from work? Let what he was doing was not particularly interesting - what's the difference, the main thing, they pay impressively for it.

- Hey, Fox, come here. The boss is calling you. - said John.
- Yes, I'm coming.
