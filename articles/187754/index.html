The Payoneer app for iOS and Android is ready

Today is a great day for all of us - the long-awaited Payoneer mobile app for Android and iOS is ready and waiting for its users. And we look forward to your feedback and suggestions.


The first version of the mobile application works in read-only mode. The application provides instant access to information, including: payment history, transaction information, current account status, as well as important updates from Payoneer.
We plan to significantly expand the functionality, add push-notifications, integrate the call system into technical support and much more in the next version of the application.
At the request of users, we added the ability to see the account balance in several currencies. We are sure that account holders who live in countries where the US dollar is not used will appreciate the convenience of this option.
Since this is the first version of our application, we would really like to know your opinion, you can leave your comments and suggestions here .
As part of the application promotion, the first 100 people who downloaded the application and logged in will receive gifts.

If you have any questions or suggestions, you can contact our support service in Russian by mail at support (a) payoneer.ru; with the department for working with Russian partner companies - partners (a) payoneer.ru.
