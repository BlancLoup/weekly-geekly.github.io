Entry Requirements


In order to study abroad, it is necessary to pass tests: analytical or on the profile of the chosen specialty ( GRE or GMAT ), knowledge of the English language ( TOEFL or IELTS ), as well as, if necessary, pass an interview at the selected university.

GRE and GMAT - adaptive tests in English, which are designed to assess the analytical skills of the future master or graduate MBA program. Choosing GMAT or GRE, you should pay attention to the mathematical component of the exam. In the first test, this section is more complex and more rigorously evaluated; in the second case, the student will need a general level of knowledge in geometry and algebra corresponding to the basic school curriculum.

A necessary condition for admission to a foreign magistracy is a confirmed high level of knowledge of English. For this purpose, the TOEFL and IELTS tests are used - these are international exams, which determine the level of English proficiency of candidates who are not native English speakers.

To send the entire package of documents, you must register on the website of the selected university, fill out the application form and attach all documents.

Please note that test results are usually sent directly by the organization that conducted the exam in electronic format.

Scholarships


In order to reduce the cost of training in the magistracy abroad, you should try to get a scholarship. They vary by source of funding. So, allocate national, private, university scholarships. There are also international scholarships, as well as payments provided for certain social or national groups of students.
Scholarships are also distinguished by their scope. There are those that allow you to cover the entire cost of studying abroad, and there are those that will allow you to pay only part of the course. Scholarships that cover the entire amount of the master's degree education usually include payments for living, insurance and student meals, which is important for those who want to save money on a magistracy abroad.

In terms of funding for education, look at the Presidential Global Education program. The program is gaining momentum, it is designed to pay for tuition (and additional expenses) of citizens of the Russian Federation admitted to leading foreign universities, subject to returning to Russia and working in the specialty within 3 years.

Internships


Another reason why Russian students choose to study in a magistracy abroad is the opportunity to have an internship in a foreign company or on the basis of the university where the training takes place. This experience is invaluable and opens up new opportunities in terms of career start and growth.

Thus, a magistracy abroad is a new horizon in science, work, business, which you can discover for yourself, making every effort in terms of passing exams and choosing a university to get a master's degree.
