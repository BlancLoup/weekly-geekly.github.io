Video recipes for delicious food or culinary analogues of YouTube.

I was just surprised when I stumbled upon an analogue of youtube, but with a culinary theme. And I was amazed when I was able to find as many as five such highly specialized sites, and at each audience quite a rather big one.

A question arose in my head - when will our culinary sites stop being content only with text and go on the offensive through large channels of perception. Apparently not long, the case is quite troublesome and expensive.

By the way, filming the process of cooking, motivates to clean up the kitchen. Verified by experience.

The links themselves:
Rouxbe.com (step-by-step guide to the preparation of tasty and healthy (?) Food)
FoodTube.net (find the video or pour it yourself, naturally on the topic of cooking)
