Kee4 - a new type of wireless keyboard for mobile devices



We all know how inconvenient it is to type text on a mobile device. It doesn't matter if we type text on a physical keyboard, or work with a virtual keyboard, it is still a bit inconvenient to type text. Of course, there are also such users who have got the hang of typing text on the keyboard of a mobile device at high speed, but for most people, a couple of sms is all that can be sustained. Repeatedly, manufacturers have offered various kinds of keyboards for mobile devices, both wired and wireless. Now there is another solution, and a very original one.

A new type of keyboard, Kee4, includes a total of four buttons. According to the manufacturer, this amount is enough for work, and after a small workout the typing speed becomes quite decent. It is difficult to imagine, of course, that such a keyboard was comfortable, but everything can be.

It works via Bluetooth, and is compatible with almost all devices equipped with a Bluetooth wireless module. You need to type using keyboard shortcuts, Kee4 allows you to type not only letters, but also punctuation marks, rare characters. Among other things, Kee4 allows you to control the cursor, Kee4 keys can also be used as function buttons in some programs or games.

For mounting on the hand, this keyboard is equipped with a special "Velcro", which wraps around the thumb. All this really looks well, very unusual - the keyboard of any smartphone seems super-progressive and super-convenient compared to Kee4. But the developers still believe that their keyboard is more convenient, just need to practice. A video is attached to showcase what a person can achieve after a couple of workouts.

To buy a thing while it is not available - there are only a few working models of the device, and the developers are now actively looking for investors to start production of Kee4. I do not even know whether this will become popular, even if this keyboard appears on the shelves of stores.
