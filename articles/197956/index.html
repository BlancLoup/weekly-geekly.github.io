FlyKly - smart bike wheel

FlyKly is a smart bike wheel with GPS and Bluetooth, a new project seeking funding on KickStarter.



Its main function is an electric motor and a battery that help you drive (up to 25 km / h over a distance of 50 km). The battery can be charged from a normal network (full charge - up to three hours) or using kinetic energy (for example, when moving from a hill), and FlyKly controlled by a smartphone - you need to set the desired speed in a special application and the motor will accelerate the bike in accordance with this .
