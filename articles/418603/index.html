Matrix PowerWatch smart watches that never need to be charged

They are charged from the heat of your body while you carry them, and the accumulated energy is enough to work for a year. How does all this work?



Matrix Powerwatch is perhaps one of the most interesting crowdfunding cases from the point of view of commerce. First, at the time of the appearance of the campaign on Indiegogo, all the largest publications in the world and in Russia, including here, instantly wrote about watches.

Secondly, without any detailed reviews, paid advertising and other similar demand for watches - crazy, and the only one cited everywhere (in general, there is) the advantage of these watches - they have an "eternal" charge. The watch does not need to be charged ever, or rather, they charge while you are wearing them. This feature seems to bring us closer to solving the important problem of modern wearable gadgets, but so far it works only locally - in the Matrix smart watch.

The idea lay on the surface for a long time, and the first prototypes that would work on the thermoelectric principle have been known since 1980 , but at that time the project was not commercially viable. Twenty years later, the idea was returned to Citizen (Eco-Drive Termo), and nowadays Matrix Industries.

Thus, it turns out that our body is really a good docking station, which is fully capable of powering the watch-pedometer.
