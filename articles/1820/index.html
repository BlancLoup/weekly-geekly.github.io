Third of Google Global Code Jam finalists - Russians

The finalists of one of the most respected international programming contests, Google Global Code Jam , have been determined, Google reports. The Russians participating in the competition got 32 positions out of 100 in the ranking of the best programmers. By the number of finalists, Russia surpassed the other 22 countries that reached the final.

Recall that the registered participants in the competition for the creation and hacking of software codes this year were over 20,000 people. Now the best hundred programmers will go to the Google Development Center in New York to compete for a prize fund of $ 155,000 on October 27th.
