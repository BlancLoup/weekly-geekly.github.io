6th place. "External BP" - DimmB









Worklog

The presence of a large number of different modding devices and lights requires adequate power. In order to conveniently and reliably connect them, DimmB decided to make an external power supply unit, from a typical Chinese block of 300 watts.
The essence of the project is to make a sufficient number of connectors at different voltages (3.3v, 5v, 12v).
On the front panel there are 4 buttons on / off connectors, a power button for the power supply unit, spring terminals for 5v and 12v, two RCA connectors. On the back - 4 adjustable RCA connectors and 4 permanent RCA. Cover panels are made of orange plex.
