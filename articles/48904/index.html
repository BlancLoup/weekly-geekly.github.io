IPhone app design: LiveJournal.app icon

The visual design of applications for the iPhone is a new and rather creative business, despite the strict standards set by Apple. I offer you a brief story about how we designed the client for LJ.



LiveJournal.app is a free LiveJournal client for the Apple iPhone that allows you to write to your journal, as well as send and receive private messages. This wonderful product is developed by CosySoftware . We were instructed to draw the main icon and a screensaver based on the corporate identity of LiveJournal.
