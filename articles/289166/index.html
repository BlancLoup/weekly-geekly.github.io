Top managers leave Enter without waiting for a "reset"

According to the newspaper Kommersant, the Acting General Director of Internet retailer Enter Denis Kosenkov and 5 other managers are going to leave the company. Enter representatives say they will soon appoint a new CEO.

Among those who leave the company are also Evgeny Prokhorov, Deputy General Director for Commercial Activities, and Illarion Tsekhanovich, Development Director.

As already reported by Megamind, in the past months, Enter has sued 39 contractors for non-payment of debts. The company's total debt amounted to almost half a billion rubles.

In addition, the conflict between the founder of Enter and the Svyaznoy network of Maxim Nogotkov and their new owner, Oleg Malis, the founder of Solvers, is still not resolved.

Also, taking into account the fact that Enter continues to incur losses, the departure of top managers becomes quite expected.

A source from the Kommersant newspaper reported that the possibility of reorganization and closer integration of Enter is being discussed with Svyaznoy.
