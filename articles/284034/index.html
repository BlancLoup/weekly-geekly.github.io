As a result of the analysis, we identified 10 important changes to which you and your business should be prepared.

Trend 2 - Multichannel Approach


With the increasing number of buyers using mobile phones and tablets, e-commerce opens up new sales channels. But that is not all.
According to Animail experts, an increasing number of e-retailers are beginning to see the benefits of opening small offline stores. Their presence in the shopping malls improves the quality of customer service and strengthens the brand. Moreover, customers can pick up or return goods at such points. Experts agree that the multi-channel approach will be distributed worldwide. As a result, online stores that are used to working online will successfully add the traditional distribution to their portfolio.

Trend 4 - Free Shipping and Returns


Free delivery is not new to e-commerce, but only 10% of online stores in the West offer free returns. According to experts, in 2015 this number will change. Free delivery or exchange of goods makes buyers feel more secure, thus increasing their level of satisfaction, while the satisfied customer is more inclined to continue shopping.
Another uptrend is day-to-day delivery. This service is offered by industry giants such as Amazon, eBay and Google (Google Express), and customers are becoming more and more used to getting what they want quickly. Remember that due to the sharp fall in oil prices, negotiations with courier companies should become easier.

Trend 6 - Convenient Payment Methods


Innovative solutions are often used in the electronic payments sector, they all have one thing in common - the desire to be as convenient as possible for the client. Payment solutions in one click or touch are highly desirable. According to experts, the future belongs to universal e-wallets that do not require filling out the same forms again and again in different online stores.

Trend 7 - Individual Offer


Online shopping websites, apps, newsletters and other forms of online marketing will become personalized. Even the location of the proposal should be adjusted to suit the preferences, the behavior on the site of the analyzed customer and its financial capabilities. Adjust your best to increase your sales, mark-up and customer satisfaction.
