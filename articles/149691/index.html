Pleasant trifle in the "Express Office" - comfortable keyboard

During the discussion of the topic touched on by Angerslave in a post about increasing developer efficiency, they touched upon the problem with a mobile workplace and the presence of an ergonomic keyboard. The problem, in fact, is that a mobile developer moving with a laptop is hardly carrying a keyboard that protects his wrists from the onset of tunnel channel syndrome . Therefore, we have already ordered two Microsoft Ergonomic Keyboard 4000 keypads for testing and as soon as we receive them from the supplier (August 22-23), we will provide them to any interested users of our service.

I can tell from my own experience that in a month and a half after starting to use an ergonomic keyboard, the frequency of pain in the wrist is significantly reduced, and after a couple of months I forgot about the problem altogether.

By the way, by the end of September we will have a rolling-in of this service, so we will not take additional money for renting a keyboard! And I hasten to remind you that our offer to provide services for half the price continues until the end of August. Another two weeks have the opportunity to conclude long-term contracts for the rental of jobs and negotiation at current prices and save a whole year!
