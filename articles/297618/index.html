Regional popularity of social networks

Research of analytical agency SimilarWeb in 2015 showed that social networks generate 5.8% of site transitions in the world. Similar data in Russia. We found out which Russian social networks are more willing to share traffic and whether there is a regional dependence.




Who gives more traffic


Vkontakte and Odnoklassniki are popular social networks in Russia.
The audience of social networks for the month according to the TNS Web Index, April 2015.


With the help of the Liveinternet counter system, let's see which of them gives more traffic to the sites.

Vkontakte leads with a big advantage in the number of clicks to sites. In second place is Facebook. Classmates only on the third, although the size of the audience cherish Facebook. The popularity of OK could be higher, but the transitions from the social network go through a page-layer warning the user to leave the site.
Twitter generates 3.8% of conversions - 4th place, blog service liveinternet.ru on 5th place. Livejournal with a share of 1.1% did not make it to the top 5 social networks. Google + ranks 7th - 0.4% of traffic. MyMir - 0.2%. Other social networks generate less than 0.2% clicks.

Social networks as a traffic-generating channel on sites loses much to search engines. This is confirmed by statistics Liveinternet. If the amount of traffic from search engines and social networks is taken as 100%, then only 8.2% - clicks from social networks. Otherwise: for 1 click from networks, there are 12 clicks from the search.

Who generates traffic in the regions


Let's see if there is a relationship between the number of transitions and regions of users.
The "slices" tool will help you see statistics for 17 regions. Other regions are not available.



Vkontakte is the unequivocal leader in the tracked regions. The most active follow the links from Kazan and from home for VK region of St. Petersburg. Odnoklassniki in St. Petersburg and the capital of the minimum popularity.
Most Facebook users in Moscow. Twitter is used in the capital. In other regions, its popularity as a source of traffic is less than 5%. Classmates are popular among residents of Barnaul, Orenburg and Kemerovo - 40.4, 32.2 and 32.2%.

Users of the Safari browser installed on Apple devices also prefer Vkontakte and Facebook. Apple product owners do not use Odnoklassniki.
