What to do?

Recently, a lot of talk about how to be more efficient, but for some reason it seems to me that this does not work. No, of course, some practices may be useful, it cannot be denied that the advice ‚Äúnot to be distracted by communication while working, requiring concentration‚Äù is useful, although it is obvious and does not require reading books and attending trainings. The bottom line is that if you are engaged in a meaningless activity from your point of view, then no system will help, although no, there is one option, if you are engaged in non-creative work, then a taskmaster with a whip can help, well, or hunger, for example.

If you have stepped into the 21st century together with civilized humanity - you are not in slavery, do not stand on the brink of survival and are engaged in intellectual, at least partially creative work, then motivation with kicks or money does not work , which means you must be internally motivated ( what George Carlin thought about the methods of motivation ), should themselves want to do this work, the one who wants to do will find how to do it ie most likely, he will work out all the techniques for effective work suitable to him. Motivation, the desire to work on this problem are primary, and practices of increasing personal productivity follow, they do not replace motivation, but help to implement it.

Before you think about how to do it, you should answer the question what to do and think about whether we are replacing one with another.

This is where the question ‚ÄúWhat to do?‚Äù Arises, so that the overseer is not required, conceptually the answer to it is simple - to do what you think is important and interesting. Here are relevant and interesting quotes:
Richard Hamming
Unless you are working on an important task, it is unlikely that you will do an important job. This is absolutely clear.

John nash
I think my main scientific achievement is that I have been doing all my life things that really interest me, and have not spent a single day doing any nonsense.

An example is Henry Ford, who used to make his car in his free time, he did not look for methods of motivation and efficiency, his work interfered with his family life and work, but he did it because he considered it important to make equipment accessible to everyone. people's lives (being the son of a farmer, he knew the burden of labor on the land and wanted to make an accessible tractor, and then a car).

I think it is obvious that the one who is engaged in an inappropriate business will never be the best in him, no matter what talents he possesses, for there are those who are not less talented, but are engaged in the same business with much greater enthusiasm.

However, in practice, not everything is so simple, not everyone, like Ford, is enlightened with the idea and is full of energy for its realization, and not all ideas allow one to realize them.

The problem of choosing what to do in order to be in harmony with oneself and achieve goals important for oneself, in theory, psychology should help solve. But in practice, it does not look like she had any serious successes on this front, but managed to find only one similar to the professional MMPI test (there is a version for career guidance, thanks to Stratoplanovtsam for the hint).
