Samsung i900 Omnia Announced



This Windows Mobile PocketPC looks like a good competitor to the 3G iPhone, which was also announced the other day, and also, of course, the HTC Touch Diamond. Still, the HTC TouchFLO 3D looks more impressive than what the Samsung i900 offers.

The device is based on Windows Mobile 6.1 Pro , has a 3.2 "WideQVGA screen (240 x 400 pixels) and an optical mouse (touchscreen), like the Samsung i780. The Samsung i900 Omnia camera has 5 Mpix , auto-zoom and face detection. There is also an interesting Geo-tagging function , thanks to built-in GPS.

The design is also quite good, somewhat reminiscent of the Apple iPhone, with which it also has a large amount of internal memory: 8GB and 16GB with the possibility of expansion with microSD cards.

The Samsung i900 supports GSM / GPRS / EDGE networks plus 7.2Mbps HSDPA as well as Wi-Fi. The built -in motion detector automatically rotates the screen depending on the position of the device body. Very comfortably!

Plus: USB, FM radio with RDS and Bluetooth stereo (A2DP). Exceptionally charged PocketPC!

What about the interface - Samsung ported their original TouchWiz for Windows Mobile. He is pretty good.

Release date Samsung i900 Omnia in Europe - July 2008.
