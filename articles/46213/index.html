12/07/2008 An authorized rally will be held in defense of the Vostok rocket

In the past months, a number of statements by high-ranking government officials were published in the media, from which it followed that the Vostok launch vehicle located on the territory of the All-Russian Exhibition Center in the form of a monument can be dismantled or completely destroyed. These statements took place against the background of public destruction in the territory of the All-Russian Exhibition Center of the Tu-154 aircraft. (see attachments)

The rocket "Vostok" is not just a rare example of engineering art, but it has long been an unofficial symbol of Our Country, marking the brightest and brightest milestone in the history of the USSR, Russia and all of Humanity as a whole - the first manned space flight on April 12 1961 our compatriot - Yuri Alekseevich Gagarin.

Given all this and guided by paragraph 3 of Art. 44 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, obliging every citizen "to take care of the preservation of historical and cultural heritage, to protect historical and cultural monuments", and in accordance with Art. 31 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, we are a group of citizens of the Russian Federation, we announce the holding of a rally on December 7, 2008.

The purpose of the peaceful action is:

To draw public attention to the future fate of the rocket "Vostok" at the All-Russian Exhibition Center and demand assigning it the legal status "Object of cultural heritage (historical and cultural monument) of the peoples of the Russian Federation", status "UNESCO World Heritage Site" and thus exclude any possibility of its destruction or replacement .

We urge all people of goodwill to agree with our goals to join us and to take part in the rally, which will be held from 12.00 to 14.00 on Sunday, December 7, 2008, in Moscow, at the main entrance of the All-Russian Exhibition Center (former VDNH).

The initiative group for the preservation of the rocket "Vostok"
citizens of the Russian Federation A.N. Kalinovsky, R.M. Dyachkov
