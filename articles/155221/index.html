AWS Global Start-Up Challenge

Hello to all!

The sixth start-up contest from Amazon Web Services has begun and is open for candidate registration. Since 2007, the Global Startup Challenge allows young projects to win 100 thousand dollars and show themselves to potential investors. In addition to the main award, all sorts of advertising and other interesting buns from AWS will also be played.

Registration is open from September 14 to November 9, 2012.



More about buns:


Dating
Every year, AWS Global Start-Up Challenge members are given the opportunity to meet face-to-face with representatives of world-leading venture capital funds such as Andreessen Horowitz, Union Square Ventures, NEA, Accel and Madrona Venture Group. For example, last year FantasyShopper received a $ 3.3 million investment from NEA and Accel.

Press coverage
Your startup can become famous thanks to the giants of press releases, such as TechCrunch and GigaOM. You know what effect habrahabr sometimes makes.

AWS Credits
Many worthy projects receive loans of up to $ 2,000 to post their projects on AWS. And the winners of the nominations receive $ 50 thousand in cash and $ 50 thousand credit for hosting. It also draws smaller amounts of loans among all participants.

Expert advice
After passing the qualifying rounds, you will be able to meet with experts who will help you properly present your product. There will be a lot of big names at the event, and getting to know them is already a kind of success.

Last year, I worked on a project that, unfortunately, did not become a prize winner, but still, there was a certain amount of traffic, press reviews and interest in the product.

On this page you can register! Good luck!!!
