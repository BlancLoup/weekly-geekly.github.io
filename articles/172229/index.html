For a long time I tried to understand the meaning and meaning of abbreviations: ERP, ECM, CRM, BPM ...
But all I understood was that I understood nothing. And the more answers I found, the more questions popped up ...
And so it went year after year ... I tried to organize business processes from project to project, on a variety of platforms: 1C, DIRECTUM, IBM Lotus Notes, TeamWox, Megaplan, Basecamp, Google Apps ... and a lot more.
Almost all of these decisions - I like them, but each, for some of their problems and their situations.

A ray of light lit a new ideology


WordPress - I only cited as an example of the possibilities that led to the emergence of a new ideology. It is called - Adaptive Case Managment (ACM).
ACM significantly changes the process management approach, but it took me 7 years to figure it out. Therefore, I do not promise that I will be able to reveal this idea in one article.

Let's start with the opinions about ACM , thesis:
1. Someone thinks that ACM is an extension of mail services (gmail + CMS or gmail based on CMS)
2. Someone thinks ACM is a branch of BPM
3. Someone thinks that ACM is the end of BPM and a fundamentally new approach to process management.

Examples - the following guys came very close to or almost to the point:
1. GitHub (Issue section)
2. ZenDesk.com
3. JIRA

But you try to implement on their base CRM, or at least part of PMBOK, ITIL ... so it turns out that this is close, but not yet ACM.
Or take the same Basecamp or Megaplan, try to implement a critical path technique there (aut. I. Goldratt). I could not get. And this is a key problem in managing large projects in large organizations.

For ACM, these are all seeds, while externally it remains as simple as the page with this article on HabraHabr√©. Our version even looks almost the same.

I had a number of projects where there was an attempt to implement an idea close to ACM on different platforms, but it was all "NOT THAT".
ACM is a consequence of web technologies, and I did not use web systems, the results were good, but not great.

I have been looking for a tool for a long time that would fit the ACM architecture in my vision (my vision may of course be different from the vision of others, especially since there is no single standard).

And here is one respected IT architect expressed in his blog the idea that the spirit of ACM is closest to WordPress. By that time, I had just finished one of my first WordPress web projects (I had suffered from Joomla about six months before) and had a warm feeling for this CMS for its simplicity and convenience.
Seeing that dear people are talking about ACM and WordPress, I decided to try, and so a new project was born that is still going on, but with results that already allow conclusions to be drawn.

How does the ACM lay on the shovels of the rest of the cracks?


Here I will briefly talk about how in one rather large organization (for 1000 employees and 20 offices in the Russian Federation), on the basis of this system, we were able to build very interesting solutions and optimize the following business processes:
1. ITSM \ ITIL - this was the first pilot project on which we tested the performance of our hypothesis
1.1. The number of requests for technical support? Share solutions in terms of SLA? Service catalog? IT support costs in terms of services or facilities? (servers, applications, computers, printers ...) - or any other section of information.
1.2. SCRUM? Backlog? Changes by version? Release dates? Sprints? Discuss any change in the comments ...
2. Projects? Briefcases? Programs? Project teams? Who is RP? Timing and timely closure of projects? - all this is present here and is more transparent than ever.
3. Lawyers? Courts? Claims? Contract work? - easy!
4. Marketing - projects? activity? production of products (banners, badges, business cards) - yes.
5. Staff. Looking for employees? Reception employee? Adaptation? Automatic creation of accounts, registration in information systems? Registration in the training center? All this interdependence and starts automatically as an employee adapts. All departments are coordinated by the system. All information is stored within a single social network.
6. Control Panel. The section that shows a set of widgets, the main part is KPI (as Google Analytiks, only on business process indicators)
7. Own gosuslugi.ru
Has anyone ordered a passport on a new portal of public services? Noticed how convenient all the services to see in one place? Now imagine that every department within a company is a service provider, a service catalog for a hundred, and not just an IT service catalog. And all this is available in one place. Need a business card? Want to apply for an employee search? Agree a contract? Check out a vacation? Apply to IT? - all this on one page. So that you do not want, at least from any department.

And further:
