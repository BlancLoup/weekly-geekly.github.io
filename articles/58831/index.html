Good afternoon, dear habrayuzyra.

Let me introduce you a service that implements a qualitatively new approach to finding reliable information on the Internet. In short, the idea of href.ru is to find reliable sources of information containing the search keyword in its domain name.


If longer, then
The project idea is based on several assumptions:



- domain name - a unique feature of the site, often reflecting its business status;
(for example, sony.com and sonyCarsForSale.com - the degree of confidence in these two resources is different)

- Domain names are often combined from popular keywords that most concisely describe the theme of the site.
- the weight and memorability of each keyword in the domain name is disproportionately higher than any key
words in the text of the site.
- the number of laconic and loaded with domain names (for example, tophotels, webdesign, logomaster, ...) is large enough (and growing) to search for them;

So, we search not for the pages content indexed a week ago, but for the keywords in the domain name.

What is the difference between HREF and Google?
In truth, I would not even compare them if it were not for the search subject.
For the approach to the search is different, different priorities and accents, different and results.

Google is well suited for everyday tasks, such as quickly learning, remembering, finding, quoting ...
With the help of HREF, you can find such resources, about which you even had no idea before. Especially if you turn on the fantasy and remember English)

HREF is looking for a whole collection of domain zones, not limiting itself to the popular .com, .ru, .net, .org
HREF will never give you 100,000,000 results for your query. we are not robots.
HREF checks the status of each site in the search results - working or unavailable right now. If possible, displays the title and favicon.
