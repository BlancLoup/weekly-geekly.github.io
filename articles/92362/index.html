Download and Share Visual Studio Color Schemes

As developers, we spend most of our time writing code in Visual Studio. You are likely, like me, with time he is tired of the standard color theme VS.

Great news - Visual Studio allows you to fully customize the background and text colors as you want, you can choose the color combination that suits you. You can also import or export color schemes in an XML file, through Tools-> Import and Export Settings .

A new site that allows you to easily share and download VS color schemes


Luke Sampson launched http://studiostyles.info/ a week ago (developed on ASP.NET MVC 2, ASP.NET 4 and VS 2010).

Studiostyles.info allows you to easily view and download Visual Studio color schemes created by other people. Color schemes work the same way in VS 2008 and VS 2010 (all versions including Express):



Color schemes are sorted by popularity and votes (you can also vote).

Clicking on any scheme you will see a screenshot with the design of a simple code. Then you can load the color scheme in VS 2010 or VS 2008:



You can upload your own color scheme if you have one, and share it with other developers.

If you have not visited the site yet, do it immediately http://studiostyles.info/ . And Luc, thank you for the work done.
