President of the United States is not very pleased with the cost of launching the Falcon Heavy and SLS



On Thursday, Donald Trump, the current US president, held a meeting in his office, the central element of which was the question of the cost of launching a payload into space. On the table at Trump were several models of modern missiles and calculations of the cost of launching each of them. Trump himself is not against space exploration, he seems to be a sensible idea, and he does not cut the budget for NASA.

However, he is surprised at the radically different price tag for launching the heavy Falcon Heavy launch vehicle and the NASA launch vehicle, called the Space Launch System ( SLS ). It is worth recalling that in the first case the client must pay about $ 90 million, in any case, this is the price indicated on the SpaceX website.

In fact, the money that was spent on the development of SLS and the design of ground systems would be enough for 86 launches of the Falcon Heavy launch vehicle.
