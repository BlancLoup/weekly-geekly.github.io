Everything goes according to plan? Planning in Bitrix24

You can travel light, without plans, maps, food and water supplies. Provide yourself with this on the road, spend time, money and nerves if you suddenly go astray. But with the map and inventory, it‚Äôs simpler, safer, and much less contingent. Does your business have a map, reserves of resources, distributed load? Simply put, how is your planning?



Chaotic movement towards the final goal always takes more time and effort than clearly planned steps. No wonder today, no CRM, ERP, and even no bugtracker can do without the time, task and task scheduling module. A team of experts Bitrix24 collected a brief medical history of companies living off-plan, and developed a method of treatment.

  • Wrong estimate of resources and effort. Symptoms: "stuck" orders, tasks, projects; uneven loading of staff; problems with the material support of the activity (for example, the load testing server is busy, which is required by several groups); extra software licenses and so on.

  • Lack of progress tracking. Symptoms: disruption of the project, especially if several divisions are involved in it at once; violation of terms; relationship problems with the client.

  • The perception of the project as a whole, massive goals. Symptoms: inattention to details; missed trivia demolish the project; lost tasks; deadlines; project budget overrun.

  • Lack of planning roles and responsible. Symptoms: confusion, shifting responsibility; loss of important stages of the project.

  • Zoo timekiller: meetings, scheduling, video calls, seminars about anything, non-urgent business correspondence. Symptoms: time spent, broken deadlines of the project, uncollected employees, lost money.

  • Invalid task prioritization. Symptoms: outstanding tasks, imbalance between major and minor milestones of the project.

  • Resistance to planning and measuring results. Symptoms: a negative reaction to attempts to control, sabotage the introduction of automated systems, uncontrolled working time.

  • Immeasurable result. Symptoms: lack of work results, vague responsibility, difficulties in planning and forecasting future projects, demotivating staff.

  • Time lag. Symptoms: customer dissatisfaction, outflow of customers to competitors, lower profits, loss of business reputation.

  • Unrelated tasks within the project. Symptoms: dilution of responsibility, cost overruns, shifts in project completion dates.

All these problems, unfortunately, do not go one by one and entail a number of serious managerial mistakes, and in the end - a drop in the quality of work and a reduction in profits. The Bitrix24 team knows how to treat these unpleasant corporate symptoms.

Some moral
