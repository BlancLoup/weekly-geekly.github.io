Excellent payment system Webmoney



It is not a secret for anybody that the payment systems market in Russia is rather poor in its essence. What many are waiting for when PayPal comes here, and the rudiments of the 90s like WebMoney will finally die. It would seem that in a sufficient number of smart books it is written that the main thing in any business is communication with the client, that a company that does not provide the proper level of service is doomed to failure. He tried very hard to write more softly, but, apparently, it did not work out. So, get acquainted: arbitration Webmoney. Paid, by the way, service.

Frankly, I wanted to write this habratopik long ago. Based on the cycle of stories "How to take my money from the Yandex wallet . " All waited for the logical conclusion of the story. I waited for exactly 3 months and 14 days, this is exactly the time that WebMoney arbitrage understands and tries to execute the decision on the claim filed by me. At first, I just wanted to devote this topic to comparing the speed of Yandex.Money and WebMoney technical support, but the situation went beyond all imaginable limits of reasonable. So, first things first.

In general, a simple story happened to me: on October 13, 2009, I paid for a certain service worth 1000 rubles through the Yandex.Money payment system. First, the seller came up with additional conditions for the transaction and demanded additional payments, then he disappeared, then he appeared again and openly declared that he would not return the money and would not fulfill his obligations. In short, the usual kidalovo. On this occasion, a letter was sent to Yandex.Money technical support (Ticket # 2009101533003953, for the protocol) with a message about fraud. 2 days of quite lively correspondence, and I get the answer from Yandex:
<...> We received information that the money about which you applied to the security service of PS Yandex.Money LLC was completely or partially blocked on the account R110522500757 of the WebMoney payment system.

For a refund, you should immediately contact the arbitration service of the Webmoney Transfer system at arbitrage@webmoney.ru <...>

Wow. The caller himself made efforts to search for my funds withdrawn to another payment system through the exchanger, probably contacted the exchanger to clarify the beneficiary's account, contacted this payment system, and the stolen money eventually froze.
