Tribewanted.com: Online Recruitment to a Wild Tribe

On September 1, 2006, the first group of volunteers will arrive at their new place of residence - an island in the Fiji archipelago. A total of five thousand people must form a new tribe, which will inhabit the uninhabited territory. The set in a tribe goes through the Internet.

The original project Tribewanted.com , one might say, is a kind of reality TV, but only a new generation. Previously, everything was extremely simple, for example, in the show The Last Hero, a small group of people settled on a desert island and lived under the watchful eye of television cameras, gradually expelling members of the "tribe" from their ranks. This is a typical television show, which differs from the usual studio production unless the place of filming.
