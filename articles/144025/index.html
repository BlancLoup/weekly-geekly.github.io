Summer School of System Programming ksys labs

In the period from July 16 to August 10 in Moscow, we hold the Summer School for System Programming . As part of the Summer School, everyone will be able to listen to lectures on operating system architecture, information security and cryptography, as well as take part in real projects on the development of operating systems.

More details:

The main course of the Summer School is the course on Architectures of Operating Systems, and its main direction is microkernel operating systems. As an additional course, lectures on Cryptography and Information Security will be given.

Participation in the course is free, but participants need a serious level of C programming language, as well as English language proficiency to understand listening lectures and reading documentation - some of the lectures on microkernel operating systems will be read remotely by graduate students of TU Dresden.

Following the results of the Summer School, the most successful participants will be offered to undergo an internship at the Company with the possibility of further employment.

Key lecturers:
  • Bjoern Doebel, PhD Student in Operating Systems at TU Dresden, Germany
  • Sartakov Vasily, ksys labs, Russia
  • Dr.-Ing. Norman Feske, Genode Labs, Germany


Schedule:

The summer school runs from July 16 to August 10. There will be 3-4 lectures and a few practical lessons per week. More accurate information about the schedule will be provided by the first of June, when the circle of participants will be determined.

Selection:

To participate in the Summer School, you must fill out a questionnaire and pass an in- person interview. You will be informed about the date and time of the interview by e-mail.
