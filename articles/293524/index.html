The size of the Internet audience in Russia reached 80.1 million people



Analytical company ComScore has published another study that examines the size and composition of the Internet audience of the Russian segment of the Network. As it turned out, the total size of the Internet audience, with users accessing the Internet from various types of devices, reached 80.1 million people, writes Siliconrus. In June alone, 72.4 million Russians visited news information resources. 68.8 million people viewed video content on the Internet, with an average of 166 videos per user. Detailed statistics on the most visited resources of the RuNet - in continuation.

The maximum audience, up to ComScore, is from the Mail.ru Group Internet resources. The size of this audience is 75.4 million people. A little inferior to Mail.ru is Yandex, with an audience of 71.8 million people, and in third place is Google with 66.4 million users. 40% of Runet users also visit torrent trackers.
