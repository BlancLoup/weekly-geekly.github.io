Punto Switcher 3.0

Hello.
We have released a new version of the popular keyboard switch Punto Switcher 3.0.

Now it supports Windows Vista, including the 64-bit version.
The interface is slightly redone in the direction of simplification and greater clarity.
Now you can override the Break hotkey and others that were previously
rigidly set.
It became possible to switch the layout by the Ctrl + Space, Alt + Space combinations (hi to makovodam!).
Fixed many old bugs and flaws.

For closer interaction with users, we created the Punta Club , where you can make a comment or a suggestion and get an answer to your question from Sergey Moskalev, the creator of the program.
