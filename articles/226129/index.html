Realizing that all this does not suit me, and the prices for internal USB sockets are horse, I decided to make myself an outlet with 4 USB ports and a power supply. As a power supply, I used charging from iPad 2.4. And as the most compact and powerful.

So, for the manufacture of embedded USB sockets for 4 ports, we need:
  1. Power supply from iPad 2.4A (avito.ru - 100 rubles, apple.ru - 800 rubles)
  2. USBA-2J double socket on the board 2 pcs. (voltmaster.ru - 23 x2)
  3. USB-A plug on the cable (voltmaster.ru - 13p)
  4. A piece of breadboard for soldering (found in bins)
  5. Embedded Ethernet socket (found on the market for the stock for 15p, the average price in stores - 300r)


Prepared a set for assembly


Disassembled charging and soldered USB sockets on the breadboard


I squandered a hole for the size of USB ports and soldered 4 USB sockets to one USB plug. When charging, not only +5 and GND are used, but also DATA -, DATA +. iPhone without D + and D- connected to charging is not charging.


Super glue glued 4 USB sockets to the bore hole of the Ethernet, and on top of it all was filled with a glue gun. I plugged in the power supply and tried on the whole thing in a 40 mm depth pan. Even a little space left.
