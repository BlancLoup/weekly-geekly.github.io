Free computer courses for schoolchildren of 7-10 classes (Moscow)

The Palace of Creativity on Sparrow Hills invites pupils of grades 7-10 to IT courses in the following areas:
  • Programming sites: HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL.
  • Video editing and computer graphics: video and film production technologies, editing, video filming, special effects, script and storyboard, work with sound.
  • C / C ++ Programming
Education is free.

The first year of study is the study of technology. The second year is an in-depth study and development of our own projects, a performance with them at competitions and conferences.


Classes in all groups are held 2 times a week : on weekdays and on Saturday afternoons, on Sunday morning or afternoon. The exact schedule of classes will be known after September 5th. Load - 4 hours per week + independent work using distance learning (Moodle).

Age
It is advisable to register in grades 7-9. Those enrolled in grade 11 will not be able to remain in their second year of study due to graduation. But usually we try to take everyone 13-17 years old.

How to join the group
The first option: you can fill out a form on the department website . We will contact you.
The second option: you can come to the Palace (see the address below), chat with the staff of the sector and join the group. Time: from August 30 to September 10 on weekdays from 16 to 19 hours, on Saturday and Sunday from 12 to 16 hours. In the same place, you can enroll in other groups of the Palace of Creativity . There are many areas in the Palace: from the English language and financial management to shooting and military training.

Admission to the group is carried out according to the results of testing for the possession of basic computer skills. Testing is conducted after September 10th.
