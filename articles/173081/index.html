More than 500,000 Google Reader users switched to Feedly in two days

More than 500 thousand users have registered on the Feedly site in the last 48 hours. This is an absolute record in the history of the development of the service. Portal owners are happy to accept new users and welcome everyone.

The main tasks of Feedly for the next 30 days are: 1) to ensure the smooth operation of the service; 2) listen to the advice of new users regarding new features; 3) add new features every week. At least, Feedly developers set such tasks for themselves. In order to serve the increased audience, the bandwidth of the channels from the hosting has already been increased 10 times.

Closing Google Reader will lead to the fact that attendance of many sites will fall July 1, 2013, but the remaining users will have a more convenient and functional RSS aggregator.
