Uncompromising replacement for a 60-watt light bulb

Europe has become at a crossroads: refuse incandescent bulbs or not . Incandescent lamps have no future, but the minuses (high cost, harmfulness + problems of utilization, low light flux) of energy-efficient lamps prevent them from spreading everywhere.

Cree offers an alternative: LED bulbs. And although the LED lamps from Cree are made only in the form of an engineering sample, there is no doubt that they are waiting for a "bright" future.
