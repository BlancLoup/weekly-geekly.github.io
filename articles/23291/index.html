Official websites of manufacturers of household appliances and electronics

The network has a new project: companyinfo.ru

The site contains the web addresses of the official websites of manufacturers of household appliances and electronics.

The site will be useful for both buyers who purchase goods from online stores, as well as by online stores that put up household appliances and electronics for sale.

The goal of the project is to provide buyers with the opportunity to learn from the original source more detailed, and often more accurate information about the product being purchased, and online stores to place on their website a reliable and more complete description of the goods they sell.

PS: This project was made not by me, but by a new haber man with the nickname 7bb . He himself filled out the catalog for the online store brwtehno.ru and, making it, ran into such a problem - where to get descriptions for the goods. In Yandex.Market and in other online stores, product descriptions often contain errors or they are incomplete, and he began collecting them from manufacturers' websites. And now, to help other people, I decided to make a directory of home appliances and electronics manufacturers. I think that this site will be useful to all online sellers.
