Microsoft buys almost all old mobile devices

About a week ago, it became known that Microsoft buys old iPad users from users for checks of $ 200, which seems to have been spent on Surface tablets.

Microsoft began working with Clover Wireless, a company specializing in recycling old electronics, and now the event has changed dramatically: the corporation offers users a maximum of $ 350 for almost everything - iPad, iPhone, Android devices, Blackberry, and even some exotics (in the cage and smartphones and tablets) - so that, the specified amount was refunded to the buyer of one of the Windows devices: Surface, Windows tablet or desktop, or, finally, Windows Phone-smartphones.



The promotion conditions are as follows: the promotion participant must purchase a Windows device, answer several questions on the promotion website - by selecting a model, you must honestly answer regarding the damage to the device being exchanged - and send it by mail to Clover Wireless. In response, a Visa prepaid card with a certain amount of compensation will come. You will also need to provide proof of purchase of a Windows device ‚Äî a serial number or IMEI.

For example - iPhone 5 64 GB is estimated at $ 265, iPad 4 64 GB - $ 325.

Since only the US state can be specified as the address for sending a Visa card, it seems that participation in the action is permissible only for the United States. At the same time, nothing prevents "passing for recycling" several old smartphones or tablets at once.
