How to create an employee from a freelancer

Among the fears of small and not very business, a topic periodically pops up to get an employment contract with outsourcers. Everyone is afraid of labor inspections, tax risks and fines. Your fears of your ignorance. In fact, it is quite difficult to substitute an employee, not a performer. If you yourself in fact have not turned a free counterpart to work force. And what is this your work relationship


Let's dig into the terminology. We have a separate law for labor relations - the Labor Code (TK). And this law gives us almost a check-list, according to which we can recognize the employment contract:

  1. We have an agreement between the employer and the employee.
  2. The employee has a certain function.
  3. The employer must provide the employee with a job, that is, load his function.
  4. He should provide him with conditions for labor.
  5. Pay salary.
  6. The employee must personally carry out the work he has unloaded.
  7. Follow employer orders.
  8. Follow the rules of the employer.

How does the article itself sound
Employment agreement - an agreement between an employer and an employee, in accordance with which an employer undertakes to provide an employee with a job according to a stipulated labor function, to ensure the working conditions stipulated by labor legislation and other regulatory legal acts containing labor law norms, a collective agreement, agreements, local regulations and with this agreement, pay the employee a full and timely wage, and the employee undertakes to personally carry out certain By agreement, the labor function is in the interests, under the control and control of the employer, to comply with the internal labor regulations in force with the employer (Article 56 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation).

So in the end how to determine something?


Formula no ultimate. There is no unconditional sign that will make a contract of employment, or vice versa - to protect against such. Determined comprehensively. And it is significant that the first instances refused to recognize the contract of services as labor relations, and the tower said that you didn‚Äôt understand it enough.

What did the first instance say, refusing to the citizen:

  2. It is not clear why she believes that she was provided with a workplace.
  3. Salary as a salary for the documents she was not charged. Yes, and there is nothing in the TC about the salary, which can be done in 1% of the contract.
  4. The testimony of witnesses that she went to the office to work every day does not confirm the employment relationship.

The logic actually is. But the Supreme Court did say no, guys, you lured something:

  1. The confirmation of the employment relationship cannot depend on formal papers that exist or are missing.
  2. The contract of services, in fact, also had to be checked to see if there was a contract in fact? And whether the services provided under this contract?
  3. The contract of services is not for the performance of work as such, but for the implementation of specific actions or activities on the basis of a specific assignment by an agreed date and for a conditional fee .   4. Under the service contract, the performer maintains his position as an independent entity, and for the labor worker is included in the staff and obeys the rules of the organization and the employer.

And most importantly, all ineradicable doubts about the recognition of relations arising on the basis of a civil law contract as labor relations are interpreted in favor of the existence of labor relations (Article 19.1 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation).
