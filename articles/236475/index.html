Google for Entrepreneurs

Google for entrepreneurs includes a lot of communities, tools and start-up development tools:

- Google Campus for Entrepreneurs includes a network of coworkers in London and Tel Aviv. Recently, three more campuses were launched: Campus Warsaw, Campus Sao Paulo and Campus Seoul. Each campus offers its own programs, in addition, in almost all of them classes are held with business mentors, business incubators and other conditions are created for the successful development of a startup.

- Startup Communities collaborates with companies that educate entrepreneurs and help them in working on their projects. Among the partners of the site you can find Startup Grind, UP Global, Blackbox Connect, American Underground, and Capital Factory.

- Tools for entrepreneurs . Google and General Assembly (a company engaged in the development and distribution of video training courses) entered into a partnership agreement, which resulted in weekly lessons for entrepreneurs. The course allows you to increase your level of understanding the basics of business, marketing and analysis, and also explains how some Google tools work. Scientists, experienced businessmen and experts in the field of entrepreneurship act as teachers.

- Think with Google is an online magazine that will post reports on Google research in the field of entrepreneurship, as well as other information that may be useful for developing and promoting a business, improving the efficiency of a businessman. The site also contains a number of tools for entrepreneurs: these are Brand Impressions, a mobile advertising campaign calculator, Display Benchmarks and much more.

- Google Business Groups - independent groups that are supervised by Google. Such groups consist of experts and enthusiasts. Attendance is absolutely free. For some reason, such groups do not function in Europe and the CIS (see map).

Google Small Business Community

The Google Small Business Community is a group created on Google+ so that small businesses can get the help they need and succeed in e-commerce. The group provides information in text and video formats. Here you can find tips, advice and speeches from successful entrepreneurs, officials and professionals in the field of trade and promotion in the network.

They are a set of offers and tools for companies and organizations. Includes corporate mail that holds 30GB of information; tools for organizing online conferences in HD quality; calendar of events and tasks.
