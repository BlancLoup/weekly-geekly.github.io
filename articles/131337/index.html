If you want to learn more about IPTV, the principles of operation of services, subscriber devices and in general, to understand how the TV has changed in recent years, welcome to Cat.

Let us consider in more detail the features of IPTV functions.


In order for the Beeline home digital television to appear in the apartment, you need to stretch the cable to the apartment through which the signal will be transmitted. This is not a traditional antenna cable, but a twisted pair (Ethernet). One cable can transmit both TV and Internet signals simultaneously. Then a TV set-top box is connected to the TV: this is the device that will output the signal to the TV, decode it, record the air and perform many more functions.

What is IPTV cooler than regular broadcast?


First of all, these are well-known and favorite subscriber control functions of the air: recording, pause and rewind. All TV set-top boxes that are used in our network are equipped with a hard disk with a capacity of 320 or 500 GB: thanks to this, the set-top box can work with a ‚Äúlive‚Äù broadcast and record it.
The subscriber is in no way limited in the number of channels or broadcasts that can be recorded, or in the ability to record several channels simultaneously.

The recording works in real time and can be activated by pressing a single button on the remote. You can assign a record of a future program, having previously performed its search in an electronic TV program, or by searching for it by the first letters of the name. The prefix itself will turn on at the right time, record and turn off. There is a mode in which the console can record a series or series of programs. The prefix itself takes control of the TV program and begins to track all the issues of a given program, methodically recording them. Recorded issues can be viewed at any time. The number of views is also unlimited.

The pause function works quite simply. At any time you press a button on the remote and the image freezes. If you need to urgently answer the phone, or just take a break for a few minutes, then the pause function is very useful for you. The prefix does not just stop the image, it turns on the background recording and as long as the image remains static, the recording of the broadcast will be saved to disk. Accordingly, when viewing is resumed, everything that happens on the screen will be shown to you as if you are watching the broadcast.

The rewind function also works simply, and its functionality also depends on the presence of a hard disk. At the moment when you switch to the channel and start its continuous viewing, the console records everything that happens on the screen in the background. This does not interfere with the transmission viewing, since the recording does not produce itself.
