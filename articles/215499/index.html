New conference for those in the .NET world



Our meetings with .NET activists show that they have no less need for communication than javist colleagues. The latter are traditionally more common: JUG.RU regularly organizes Java Days and specialized conferences. So the conference. NEXT, which will be in St. Petersburg on April 4, promises to be useful: good speakers, fresh topics, maximum technical content.

Conference organizers - Codefreeze and JetBrains . Conference site - dotnext.ru . Among the speakers will be people who rarely appear in public, for example, the head of the .NET development in JetBrains Sergey Shkredov. If you have something to ask these people - come.





Applications for reports are still accepted, so in the comments we will be happy to read what else you want to know at the conference (this will help the speakers), and on the site you can apply yourself.

Below is a list of already accepted reports. Mark which reports are the most interesting for you. We understand that your vote for the report does not mean that you will come, and we will plan the capacity of the halls not only by the results of this survey, but we still want to know your opinion.

Thank!
