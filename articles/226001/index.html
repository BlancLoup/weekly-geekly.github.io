Maker Faire in Chinese (exhibition of the achievements of robotics and electronics)



Maybe some of you have heard about the Maker Faire exhibition, and maybe there are even lucky ones who personally participated.

Maker Faire is an exhibition of innovative projects in robotics and electronics, supported by the famous Make magazine, hackspaces, makers and many hi-tech companies. Unfortunately, as far as I know, the exhibition has not been held in Russia yet. But in China this year was held, and in this post we show what is so interesting there was presented.

The Maker Faire exhibition was held in Shenzhen , the unofficial world capital of electronics.
