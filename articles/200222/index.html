New version of Navitel with a subscription to cards

Today we released the update Navitel Navigator for Android with the implementation of a completely new opportunity - the purchase of cards by subscription. By taking this step, we created more accessible navigation. Now you can buy not only a perpetual license for maps for Navitel Navigator, but also to purchase it for a certain period. There are several subscription plans: for 2 weeks, 1 month and 1 year.

You can subscribe to the card from the program itself. It is easy, reliable and does not take much time.

The cost depends on the subscription period and a set of cards.

When planning your vacation, weekend or business trip in other countries, it is very convenient to use a subscription to the necessary cards. First, the familiar interface will allow you to quickly get used to the new country and use the navigation at home. Secondly, it is profitable! We note an important point: the subscription is valid for all existing packages of navigation maps, with the exception of maps of Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and Kazakhstan, as well as packages that include at least one of the maps of these countries.

At the moment, the Navitel Navigator 8.5 version (with the option of subscription to cards) is available only for Android OS users. Device users on other platforms can expect an update in the near future, developers are already preparing versions.

The idea to add the function of buying cards for a limited period appeared long ago. Including, thanks to responses of our existing and potential users. Thank you for your active participation in the development of software Navitel Navigator! Thanks to the feedback, we are able to make navigation better and more qualitative, taking into account the interests and requests of our users.
