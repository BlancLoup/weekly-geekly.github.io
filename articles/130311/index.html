My Things is a new site that helps owners of the same things find each other



We present to the attention of habr-audience site My Things .

What it is?

Very simple: My Things is a site where you find other owners of the same things as yours.

We bought a new smartphone, laptop, camera - went to My Things and said, "I am the owner." Now you are in touch with other owners!

But a single universal service, allowing owners of the same things to be in direct communication with each other, has not yet been. Previously, other owners had to search "somewhere on the Internet", and useful knowledge - to collect "piece by piece" on dozens of different sites and forums.

Now - we have done My Things.

What we have more interesting, what I would like to draw attention to:

  • Exchange tips. We have made a separate section for advice on each item.
