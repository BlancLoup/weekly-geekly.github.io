The annual route of the Curiosity rover for 2 minutes from NASA (and yes, Curiosity has been on Mars for a year!)



Time always flies, nothing can be done here. As they say "other people's children grow up quickly." This is true, but this can be said not only about children. For example, the Curiosity rover has been on Mars for a full year. And it seems that it was launched only a few weeks ago ... Over the year, Curiosity has done a lot for science, including evidence of the existence of liquid water in the past of the Red Planet. In honor of the "anniversary" date of the Mars rover on Mars in NASA created a two-minute video that shows all the movements and basic actions of the rover.

The video includes compiled images of various rover cameras. There are many images of how the rover manipulator captures rock samples from the surface of the planet. There are also a lot of shots with changes in the outlines of the shadow of Curiosity, which also impresses.

There is no music, so you will have to watch the video in complete silence - and, by the way, this is more of a dignity than a lack of video.
