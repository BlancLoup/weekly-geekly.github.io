Samsung introduced a new smartphone GALAXY Note 4 and its modification with a curved screen - GALAXY Note Edge

Good Friday, Habr!

Samsung Electronics has announced the expansion of its flagship Samsung GALAXY Note line, presenting the new GALAXY Note 4 smartphone at the international exhibition IFA 2014, as well as its modification with a curved screen - GALAXY Note Edge. Both devices are equipped with an improved S Pen and an Quad HD Super AMOLED screen, Exynos 5433 and Qualcomm Snapdragon 805, 3GB RAM, Cat LTE module. 6, high resolution cameras with optical stabilization system and a wide range of different sensors. For the rest of the details we pass under the cat!


Samsung GALAXY Note 4 surpassed all predecessors of the line, and these are not empty words. The smartphone boasts a 5.7-inch Quad HD (2560x1440) Super AMOLED screen, which, in addition to a clearer and brighter picture, provides an impressive response time of one millionth of a second. The new flagship has a main 16-megapixel camera, which is equipped with intelligent optical stabilization (Smart OIS) and a 3.7-megapixel front camera with an F1.9 aperture, providing a 90-degree shooting angle by default or 120 degrees in wide-angle shooting. The technical side is also impressive: the main novelty has acquired a new eight-core processor Exynos 5433, 3 GB of RAM and a 3220 mAh battery.

GALAXY Note 4 offers a number of advanced features, such as Fast Charging, in which the device charges much faster or in Ultra Power Saving Mode, which guarantees the user that he will not be left without communication when needs it. The smartphone is also equipped with several microphones and an improved speaker for good hearing and noise reduction when talking in a noisy environment. The built-in voice recorder offers eight different labels for voice input and makes it possible to highlight the voice of selected interlocutors in a group conversation, while isolating all the others. GALAXY Note 4 got an improved fingerprint scanner, and also received a built-in ultraviolet sensor - an absolute novelty for mobile devices.

The S Pen electronic pen has reached a new level of its evolution - the improved version has become as convenient as possible when handwriting information input, imitating the effect of a ballpoint pen or a pen for calligraphy. The screen of the smartphone now recognizes 2048 degrees of depression, tilts the pen in different directions and other movements. In addition, S Pen allows you to easily manage the intuitive capabilities of the smartphone: Active Notes (Action memo), Instant Comment (Screen write), Quick fragment copy (Image Clip), as well as the Fragment Album function (Smart Select), which allows users not only to collect putting together content from various sources, but also sharing it with friends or colleagues.

GALAXY Note 4 acquired the advanced features of the service for notes S Note. Apart from standard functions, such as creating handwritten notes or using a QWERTY keyboard, as well as converting handwritten text into printed, the updated application now can instantly digitize text (Snap Note function), that is, convert images into an editable format. For example, the user can take a picture of the text on a piece of paper or writing on a blackboard, drive the resulting photo through the digitization process, and then edit it. In addition, S Note will help to literally record thoughts on the go, thanks to the Voice Memo feature for creating quick speech notes.

GALAXY Note 4 is enclosed in a smooth metal frame that blends well with the display device. The protective glass inherited from the previous GALAXY Note 3 model not only provides durability to the smartphone, but also makes the image on the screen more saturated. The soft texture of the back cover gives a good contact with the palm, and the ergonomic design allows you to control the device with one hand.
