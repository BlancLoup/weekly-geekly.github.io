Cyber ​​Monday 2013 in Russia

Today in Russia is held Cyber ​​Monday. Such an event in our country happens for the first time. Unfortunately Cyber ​​Monday supported only 6 stores. Exactly one day, discounts up to 75% for various goods are valid. The list of participants includes the online stores "White Wind Digital", "M.Video", "Eldorado", Enter (part of the Svyaznoy group of companies), "The Tenth Dimension" and Wikimart, which organizes the cyber Monday. In the USA, where cyber monday is spent for many years, in 2011 the company's revenues amounted to $ 1 billion. In 2012, the revenue of retailers increased by a third.



Cyber ​​Monday (English Cyber ​​Monday) is Monday, coming after Black Friday, which gives a start to the sales season in the United States, between Thanksgiving Day and Christmas. If Black Friday is associated with the traditional peak of retail sales in regular stores, then Cyber ​​Monday means a busy day in the online retail segment, in online stores that offer products at discounted prices. Monday is supposed to be the day when most US employees return to work, which gives them plenty of time to shop online from their office computers.
