Facebook Garage in Moscow

On April 12, Facebook will hold its second traditional Facebook Garage developer meeting in Moscow, where Facebook engineers will talk about how to create or improve applications for Facebook, integrate websites with Facebook and use Open Graph.

Also at the event, Russian partners Facebook will present their case studies, already using the new features of Open Graph on their websites and in their applications.

Important: if you are going to take part - do not forget to register on the site

Facebook Garage program:

15:00 - 15:30 Registration
15:30 - 15:40 Welcome speech - Joanna Shields, Vice President, Facebook
15:40 - 16:00 The strength of the social graph - Julien Codornier, Head of Partnership and Platform
16:00 - 16:45 Open Graph - detailed analysis - Tom Eliott, engineer for working with partners
16:45 - 17:00 Break
5:00 pm - 5:45 pm Mobile Platform - Bruce Hazan, Engineer
17:45 - 18:15 Cases of partners (details are being specified)
18:15 - 18:20 Closing remarks - Ekaterina Skorobogatova, Facebook representative in Russia
18:20 - 19:30 The informal part

Important: this is an event for developers. All presentations will be in English with simultaneous translation into Russian.
