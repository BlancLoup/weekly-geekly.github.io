Dear habrareyudi! How long has such a problem? How to get the RSS feed from Gmail in Google Reader?

Gmail allows you to receive the rss stream from the headers of new letters. But not in the way it can be done for the Google calendar, via a secret address, but through server authentication.
Thus, software readers, like FeedDemon, can receive such streams if the login and password are specified in the feed settings. A native Google Reader does not know how, since such settings for the stream is not provided. How did Google overlook this possibility of integrating its products?!?

So it turns out that I open Google Reader more often than Gmail, mail is easier and faster for me to check. Do you know a possible crutch to solve this problem?
