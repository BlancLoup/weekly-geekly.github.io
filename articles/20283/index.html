At seminars of Aaron Marcus will give Microsoft Expression

In February, Aaron Marcus, the world's first professional graphic designer, the author of more than 100 concepts for smartphones, including those used to create the famous iPhone, will visit the author's seminars in Moscow, Kiev and St. Petersburg.

At seminars, Aaron Marcus will talk about the key strategies of Web 2.0, current trends in visual site design, research results of mobile phone users, designing interface sites with a focus on B2B and B2C, globalization and internationalization of products and interfaces and much more.

Also, the seminars will be held a bonus lecture from the general sponsor, the company Microsoft. The topic of the lecture is ‚ÄúMicrosoft Expression - tools for creating new generation interfaces‚Äù.

Each participant of Aaron Marcus seminars in St. Petersburg and Moscow will receive as a gift full-featured versions of Expression Studio, which cost about $ 800.

As part of the seminars, a competition for simple and elegant use of Microsoft Expression products will be held. Valuable prizes will be raffled among the participants.

Aaron Marcus is the founder and president of AM + A, a company that has been consulting and designing for more than 25 years for Microsoft, Oracle, Adobe Systems, eBay, Nokia, Visa, BMW, and other well-known companies. AM + A has developed more than 100 different concepts of improved user interfaces for smartphones in the United States 2-3 years before smartphones entered the market. Back in 2000, Aaron Marcus developed the Apple iPhone visualization concept, a product that appeared in 2007.

It was thanks to the projects of Aaron Marcus, Samsung, being a little-known brand in the USA, managed to rise to the second position after Motorola in the field of mobile phones and to the second position after Sony in the field of consumer goods.

AM + A's customers include Microsoft, Oracle, Adobe Systems, eBay, Nokia, Motorola, Samsung, America Online, Visa, Xerox, Hewlett-Packard, Siemens, BMW, DaimlerChrysler, and many others.

Aaron Marcus Company Projects:

* design of the Circulate application interface for scanning, recognizing and storing documents and images for Adobe Systems;
* design and development of the next generation Mob-i mobile information communicator interface for Samsung;
* unification of the interface and ease of use of the web portal for residents of different countries of Europe's largest Internet provider Tiscali;
* consulting on the design of the user interface of the first communicator Nokia Communicator 9000;
* Designing an HTML training system for CAST (Computer-Assisted Self-Teach).
