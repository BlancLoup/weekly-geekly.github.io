IE8 has become the most popular browser.

According to the Net Applications January report, IE8 has become the most popular browser (22.37% + 2.80% in compatibility mode), having shifted IE6 (20%) from this position.

Firefox 3.5 (17.08%), IE7 (14.53%), Firefox 3.0 (5.24%), Chrome 3.0 (3.85%), Other (16.93%).



Yes Yes! IE6 is no longer the most popular and its position continues to fall, which is good news. By the way, the fall of IE6 correlates well with the fall of Windows XP amid the rise of Windows 7.
