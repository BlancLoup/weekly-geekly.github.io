New Yandex.Market. What happened at the YaC / M conference, and why this could be an important step in the development of Runet

Hello! My name is Alexey Avdey, I am the head of Yandex.Market. Today I want to tell you what the main presentation at the past YaC / m was about and why the updated Yandex.Market will bring the e-commerce market in our country to a new stage of development.

The market is changing the format. Now it will not only be a product search service. We will be the arbiter between sellers and buyers: we will guarantee the integrity of purchases and will make people stop fearing that their money will be stolen. In addition, based on the MultiShip data, we will begin to show the time and cost of delivery for each product in each region. We will allow even small players to use technologies that change the market.



When we launched the product search service in 2000, we had 70 stores. The e-commerce market in Russia received a strong impetus and actively developed. Now more than 15,000 stores are connected to Yandex.Market, 80% of which are small. According to TNS Web-Index, about 20 million customers come to the service per month. The time has come when new tools are needed to continue this growth.

Much has happened over the years: we bought Multiship software and invest in its development , gain experience in quality control of stores and create technologies that will help not only us, but also users and partners. What path we have traveled and what will be further thanks to this, read under the cut.

Yandex.Metrica made web analytics and efficiency calculations available to any store, Yandex.Direct eased access to customers, and Yandex.Webmaster helped newcomers with search engine optimization.

Buyer Protection System


The customer protection service will resolve the disputes between the customer and the store. If the buyer is not satisfied with the response, he will be able to file a claim. Within three days after filing a claim, the store will have to resolve the issue. If this does not happen, the buyer will be able to attract an independent arbiter from Yandex.Market. The arbitrator will communicate with both parties, request documents, additional information and make a decision. If the store is wrong, the buyer is guaranteed to return the money.


Paid goods in Russia is not always easy to return. The Yandex.Market Quality Service, based on customer feedback, has previously paid attention to how stores fulfill laws, and applied sanctions to violators. But now each user will be able to directly contact us through a special interface - immediately after placing the order and another 14 days after delivery. Thanks to her, a person will be able to find out everything necessary about the order or solve a controversial issue by resorting to the help of an independent arbiter.

Buyers will be able to see the history of their orders, their status, history of payments and correspondence with stores.
