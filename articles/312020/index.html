We invite you to the Java-conference Joker 2016, which will be held on October 14 and 15 in St. Petersburg


The best speakers from Russia, the USA and Europe will come to share their thoughts and ideas with you.


At the conference on October 14, the publishing house "Peter" will present books on IT, which can be purchased at a publishing price.


This will be the fourth Joker in St. Petersburg. There will be more speakers and reports, sponsors and stands, participants and experts than in past times, so everyone who came will have something to do!


The conference focuses on participants who are well versed in Java (senior / middle level developers): 2 days, 50 technical reports of 60 minutes each, half an hour breaks so that the head does not overheat - and open discussion zones for communication with experts without restrictions.


Joker 2016 speakers include experts from Oracle, Red Hat, Azul Systems, JetBrains, Yandex, Odnoklassniki, SAP and other leaders of the international IT scene.


A detailed program of the conference, details about the speakers and the conditions of participation can be found on the conference website .
