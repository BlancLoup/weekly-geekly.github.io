Toshiba Qosmio X770 - New Gaming Laptops

The next new Toshiba, which will come to the Russian market, will be the Qosmio X770 game series.
These models are designed for demanding gamers, including those who want to discover the world of computer entertainment in 3D.
Accordingly, the series will be presented by 2 models: Qosmio X770, and Qosmio X770 3D.



Capacities of new laptops are represented by quad-core i5 or i7 quad-core processors and NVIDIA GeForce GTX 560M graphics cards, 1.5GB GDDR5, which is designed to provide high quality images in Full HD resolution (1080p) while maintaining gaming performance.

A hybrid hard drive with 4 GB of flash memory and an adaptive memory technology provides high performance and ultra-fast loading, while the HDMI interface makes it easier to connect a laptop to a large external screen.

The high performance of the Qosmio X770 makes laptops most suitable for converting video stored on DVD from 2D to 3D. Qosmio X770 models support 3D video transmission to the screen via HDMI for watching 3D movies with friends and family.
NVIDIA 3DTV Play support on the Qosmio X770 3D uses the GPU to convert various games into 3D format for playing on a connected 3D TV.

In addition, due to the short response time, the display reproduces flickering scenes without a blurry frame effect. A built-in 3D webcam with two lenses allows you to conduct 3D video conferencing and take 3D photos. RAM, depending on the configuration is 4 GB, 6GB or 8GB.

Communication features include WLAN ports (802.11 b / g / n), Bluetooth 3.0 + HS *, Gigabit LAN, Intel Wireless Display, as well as three USB 2.0 ports and one USB 3.0 port with USB Sleep & Charge function, as well as HDMI modules - CEC, and Multi-Card Reader.

Excellent live sound is essential for high-performance gaming machines. Therefore, the two models of Qosmio X770 are equipped with premium-class harman / kardon micro acoustic systems and a subwoofer to enhance low-frequency sounds. Together with support for Dolby Advanced Audio laptops provide sound quality characteristic of expensive audio systems.
