Measure ROI Optimization

Conversion for the conversion does not make any sense. How many users filled out the form and sent applications - not as important as the number of buyers in the end. With a 10% conversion in the bid, you may have a 1% conversion in the sale. See what happens on the ‚Äúlower‚Äù floors of the sales funnel.

Count profits, not registrations; business conversion, not website conversion.
