Open competition of St. Petersburg in robotics



St. Petersburg's open robotics competition will take place on March 18, 2012 in the Carnival Theater and Concert Complex (Nevsky Prospect, 39, Liter D) based on the St. Petersburg City Youth Creativity Palace.

In a programme:
1. Competitions according to the regulations of the Open Robotic Tournament.
2. Regional stage of the International competitions of robots according to the rules of the WRO 2012 World Olympiad.
3. Competitions for beginners.
4. Football robots.
5. Exhibition of creative projects.

General regulations and a link to the online broadcast under the cat.
