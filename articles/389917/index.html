I love to travel and take pictures. And each time going on vacation or a business trip I try to remember what I need to take with me. As a result, the fees turn into a test of everything and the obligatory forgetting of some trifles.


A power flash drive is an excellent solution that allows you to successfully combine the functions of a fairly weighty set of cords, adapters and batteries in one tiny device.
It also has a battery for emergency charging the phone, and an adapter cable from phone to USB. And a small flash drive for 8 GB and even a small flashlight.
One of the features of the flash drive is an interesting solution for switching the mode of operation of the USB connector.

In the first position of the switch is a normal connector, and the flash drive turns into a small marching charging cord. It is also suitable for data exchange with the connected device. There are three connectors for the old and the new iPhone, the usual micro-usb for androids, windows-phones, bluetooth headphones, children's toys and so on.

In the second position of the switch, the external cords are disconnected and the USB connector turns into a micro USB flash drive adapter, with a standard micro SD card inside. In this mode, the power flash drive is used as a regular flash drive. You can connect to a computer, auto-radio and everywhere where a regular USB flash drive can be used.

Naturally, you can simultaneously charge and power-flash and the phone connected to it.
The advantage of the power flash drive is that the flash drive is just a small micro SD card (already inserted into the USB adapter). And if necessary, for example, when the memory in the camera ran out, you can quickly "take" the card from the flash drive.
