Impartiality

More and more plug-ins are becoming in order to see all the information about the person who left a comment, and at the same time vote not only for the comment, but also for the karma of the person who left it. I went the other way, I propose an extension which, on the contrary, hides all the personal information about the commentator, and at the same time the evaluation of the comment. What will help (for those who want of course) to leave their unbiased opinion about the comments.


The script is placed on Google Chrome 4 without separate gestures. For Firefox, you need to put GreaseMonkey . Under the Opera should also earn without problems. In theory, it will work even under IE7 / IE8 with the addon IE7Pro.
