Conference Agile Eastern Europe


October 8-9, 2010

Kiev, Ukraine

www.agileee.org

Agileee Annual Conference is once again gathering participants and partners!

A rich program - 2 days, 4 streams, 40 reports and workshops!

Key speakers - world-renowned, members of the board of directors of Agile Alliance :
  • Mary Poppendieck ( Mary Poppendieck ) - a pioneer in the use of Lean - a lean approach to software development. Author of a number of books and tutorials, keynote speaker at international conferences.
  • Henrik Kniberg - Agile / Lean with over 15 years experience in IT. The author is Scrum and XP from the Trenches and Kanban and Scrum, making the most of both .


The geography of Agileee goes beyond Eastern Europe! Speakers from around the world:
USA, Canada, Belgium, Finland, France, Germany, Hungary, Israel, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Sweden, Switzerland, Great Britain, Ukraine, Russia and Belarus.

Master classes for key speakers are a great way to get the transfer of professional experience and the official Scrum Alliance certificate (for CSM and CSPO classes).

Unforgettable atmosphere - exchange of experience, professional contacts, a lot of communication and fun in the spirit of Agileee!
