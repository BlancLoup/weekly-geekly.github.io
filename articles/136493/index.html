If we talk about the technical part.

OLED vs. LED / SUPERLED / SUPERLED +
  • OLED has smaller dimensions and weight (which we easily confirm
  • OLED does not need extra backlight, which allows you to get a picture that is incomparable in contrast, the contrast ratio of OLED panels is up to 50 times higher than that of LED panels
  • OLED lacks such a parameter as viewing angle - the image is visible without loss of quality from any angle
  • OLED has an instant response (up to 150 times higher than that of LED) - in fact, the total lack of inertia of the screen, which allows you to get a very realistic picture in the most dynamic films
  • OLED has better color rendering (due to high contrast and instant response)
  • the ability to create flexible screens
  • OLED has a wider operating temperature range (‚àí40 to +70 ¬∞ C)
