Formed a preliminary program WebCamp: Mobile Day


From July 21 to July 24, in the framework of Odessa Innovation Week , organized by the GeeksLab project and RootUA Media , the 5th annual conference of web developers WebCamp 2014 will be held in the following areas: development , project management , design , internet marketing and mobile technologies .

The second day of the conference, July 22, will be devoted to the development, design and promotion of mobile applications ( WebCamp: Mobile Day ). Representatives of such companies as Yandex, MobiDev, DevRain Solutions, DataArt, Artjoker, Capable Bits and others will make presentations.

The formation of the WebCamp: Mobile Day conference program is close to completion. Among the accepted reports include:

Swift is a new programming language for iOS and OS X, advantages and disadvantages. How to move with Objective-C.
Andrey Makarov (iOS Developer, Team Leader at MobiDev).
A brief overview of the new language for development under iOS and OS X from the point of view of iOS developer. Weaknesses and strengths of the language. Compare Swift with Objective-C. Combining Objective-C and Swift code in one project. Smooth and painless transition to use of the Swift language.

Popups, alerts and windows.
Yandex
How beautiful and painless to create a custom popup on iOS based on UIWindow, and at the same time have no problems with the rotation of the device. Peculiarities of UIAlertView behavior. How to make the behavior of a custom popup identical to the system one. All this is considered in the context of existing ready-made third-party solutions, their pros and cons.

Development of universal applications for Windows Phone 8.1 and Windows 8.1.
Alexander Krakovetsky (head of DevRain Solutions).
The latest Windows Phone 8.1 update allows you to develop universal applications for Windows Phone 8.1 and Windows 8.1 platforms. About where to start, what tools / technologies / approaches to use.

Why use Gradle in android projects, what opportunities does it give the developer? Gradle support in Android Studio.

Promotion and popularization of mobile applications.

- How to stimulate the installation in the first days of the application.
- Principles of ranking and promotion of your application in Google.Play.
