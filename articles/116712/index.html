Choosing a domestic cloud hosting provider

Hello, dear readers! In my article, I want to analyze companies that provide cloud hosting services in the territory of the Russian Federation. The analysis is carried out from the point of view of the average user, who was faced with the task of acquiring hosting in the cloud. The main criteria for the analysis will be made - support, cost, flexibility (as one of the main criteria), transparency of pricing policies, etc.

I will consider the following companies:
  • ActiveCloud.ru
  • Slidebar.ru
  • Cloudone.ru
  • Ispserver.com
  • Selectel.ru
  • Clodo.ru
  • Scalaxy.ru


Let's get started

ActiveCloud.ru
This company has been known for quite a long time in the market of hosting services (over 5 years) and has established itself as a very reliable and stable platform for the allocation of resources.   • No payment for used resources (only monthly payment)

However, I want to note that with the order of a more powerful server, the price is reduced. The rule ‚Äúwholesale is cheaper‚Äù.

Advantages:
  • Very competent support and good knowledge
  • Stability and reliability of equipment
  • The ability to replace equipment based on customer needs
  • Deservedly high prestige among hosting companies

Let's put it all together. Unfortunately, as a user of services, it is difficult for me to see the difference between the service ‚ÄúCloudServer. If you know the average (and peak) loads on your server and you are ready to pay for the stability and brand to the detriment of flexibility, then ActiveCloud.ru will be a worthy and competent choice.

Pretty young company that is developing very fast and provides high-level services.

  • Flexibility in all parameters
  • Excellent support and updated knowledge base

  • Hourly payment

Let's put it all together. The only drawback I encountered is the lack of restrictions on the use of CPUs in the Scale Server service.
