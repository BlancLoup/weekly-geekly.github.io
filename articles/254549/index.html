Coub goes to strike

April 10-11, Ulyanovsk will host the largest in the regions of Russia IT conference " Strike ". Coub went to strike last year, could not miss it in this. We will not only be present at it, but we will also bring several reports with us.







We will make presentations in 3 sections.
  • Technical
  • Management and Education
  • Mobile development


Excellent developer rules


On the first day of the conference, Mikhail Tabunov will tell you what it means to be a good developer:

Mikhail Tabunov , CTO

There are very few qualities that share good and excellent developers, however, the difference in the results of their work is sometimes very large. The point here is not in erudition and some personal qualities, but only in the understanding of values. The developer, they are one, his leadership others. It rarely happens when they coincide. His report will try to explain what we should strive for in the work, and what will be behind it.


Overlaying video effects with GPU optimization using OpenGL ES 2.0 with Shaders


Maxim Reshetey , Lead iOS developer

Maxim will talk about how real-time video effects overlay works in the Coub mobile app using OpenGL ES 2.0 with Shaders. With optimization for GPU architecture.

Report Details
1. Digital video and image processing, theoretical foundations and a general algorithm.
3. Examples of calculations of some simplest filters.
4. Gaussian Blur, Theoretical Foundations and General Algorithm.
5. Performance evaluation on the CPU.
6. Graphic processors (GPU) and features of their architecture.
7. Estimation of the effectiveness of Gaussian blur on the GPU.
8. OpenGL ES graphic library and shader programming.
9. Effective implementation of Gaussian blur using a pixel shader.
