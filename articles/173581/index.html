In Russia, a man was first convicted for Xbox 360 firmware, and then acquitted!


Department K , Kaluga subdivision, carried out a special operation to catch a particularly dangerous criminal who carried out illegal actions - Xbox 360 Consoles Firmware .
Firmware - drive firmware change technology that allows you to run unlicensed disks on the console


The special operation proceeded according to the usual scenario; a front officer of department K, posing as a normal person, asked to buy the console upon purchase, for some money, Orhan Abbasov, the seller, agreed.

After the transfer of money, Orhan Abbasov was detained, and later was convicted and received a sentence of 1.5 years of conditional imprisonment with a probation period of 2 years, in addition, Orhan Abbasov received a fine of 45,000 rubles.
