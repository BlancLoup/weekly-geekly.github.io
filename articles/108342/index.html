ASUS PRIDE Contest

As you know, TrendClub often holds contests and gives prizes from ASUS. As an example, this month‚Äôs ‚Äú Dream Robot ‚Äù contest. But this time we are pleased to announce the competition PRIDE from the company ASUS.



Each ASUS motherboard purchased is a worthy story, so the company decided to hold a contest that will enable each user to share such a story with the world. PRIDE (English "Pride") - these are the key characteristics of ASUS products: Performance (Performance), Reliability (Reliability), Innovation (Creativity), Design (Design) and Efficiency (Efficiency). Users of ASUS motherboards will be able to talk about why they are proud of their computer, and get a chance to win great prizes!

‚ÄúWe know what exactly the users want from the motherboard, and we make a lot of efforts so that each of them can be proud of their computer. This competition is designed to inspire people to realize the full potential of our motherboards. ‚Äù
Jackie Syu, vice president of ASUS

The PRIDE competition is held around the world. In Russia, it started on November 15, 2010.

What to do?

To participate in the competition, you must submit a video or photos in one of three categories: Performance, Reliability or Creativity.
The winners will receive valuable prizes, and the main prize will be a free trip to Taiwan to the headquarters of ASUS. And you can win a ASUS G73 3D laptop and more!



Where to send?

The competition began on November 15, 2010. Reception of applications on the site ( event.asus.com/mb/2010/PRIDE/RU ) ends at midnight (23:59, GMT) on December 16th, 2010. Six winners from each participating country will be announced on January 5th. Voting for regional winners will begin on January 5, 2011 on the site ( event.asus.com/mb/2010/PRIDE ) and continue until January 17, 2011.

All voted automatically participate in the daily lottery. Winners of the main prize of the PRIDE competition will be announced by ASUS on January 18, 2011

Additional Information

So, if you are proud of your computer and want to tell everyone about its phenomenal performance, the incredible endurance of your ASUS motherboard or something else on the subject of our competition, we will be glad to hear your story! Choose a category and tell your story! Regional prizes - the latest ASUS motherboards (3 prizes in each category).

Performance

To win the Performance category, you need to talk about how the ASUS motherboard helped you achieve incredible computer speeds. Set a new overclocking record or scored the highest score in the test program? Write!

Reliability

To win the Reliability category, tell us how your motherboard stood the test of harsh working conditions. Have you survived the fire? Bought it in 1992, and it still works? Does your story demonstrate the reliability of the ASUS motherboard? You in this category.

Innovation

To win in the category of Innovation (Creativity), you need to show a little bit of madness, to show a glimpse of genius or throw out a ton of creativity! Show us the most unexpected and innovative way to use your ASUS motherboard.

Good luck!


* - The trip includes a flight from Russia to Taiwan and back, four-day hotel accommodation and breakfast, as well as transit to the hotel. The remaining expenses are not paid.
