Experience successful crowdfunding. Top 5 best projects Indiegogo

Compared to the promoted Kickstarter, the oldest crowdfunding platform Indiegogo has a number of advantages. Firstly, Indiegogo has a wider geography - projects on it can be created not only by Americans and British, like on Kickstarter, but by everyone, including the Russians. Secondly, there are no restrictions on the type of projects - the range is very wide, from technology to educational and entertainment. Another important detail is that the funding model allows a startup to keep the amount collected, even if the aforementioned goal is not achieved. The project continues to evolve, and backers receive their products at a reduced price.



Of course, all these advantages attract to Indiegogo a huge number of investment-seeking projects and a multi-million user audience that is ready to financially support these projects. Watching the activities of colleagues with professional interest, the VCStart team studied the most successful projects of Indiegogo, and made up the TOP-5 startups, who are currently raising funds at the site for the implementation of their projects. Here they are:
