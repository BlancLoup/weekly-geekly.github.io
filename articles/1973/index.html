Asia will get the first level domain

The Corporation for the assignment of addresses and names on the Internet, known as ICANN, has approved the emergence of a new domain zone for online resources in the Asia-Pacific region.

Register in the domain .asia English-language site names will be no earlier than six months, and the owners of trademarks will be able to do it first.

An agreement on the creation of an Asian domain zone will be signed between ICANN and the DotAsia Organization , working with the domains of Vietnam, China, Japan and other countries in the region, in the coming weeks, reports the Associated Press. The cost of registration is not reported.
