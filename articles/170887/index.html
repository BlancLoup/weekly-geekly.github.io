Canadian startup Coinkite Cryptobank from Toronto presents offline payment service for cryptocurrency

Canadian company Coinkite Cryptobank , located in Toronto, offers a full range of services and Bitcoin and Litecoin vertical integration solutions for offline e-commerce consumers and merchants.
For acquiring clients are offered:
  • online banking
  • issue of debit plastic cards
  • POS ‚Äì terminals for making transactions (payment for purchases) in Bitcoin and Litecoin

The company is positioning itself as an inspiration for consumers and sellers to use cryptocurrency in e-commerce. The presented cryptoequiring system was developed by a Canadian firm for its own use and internal use, but subsequently it was decided to make the service public.

POS-terminal Coinkite provides a method of securely accepting Bitcoin payments offline, just like with ordinary credit cards in payment terminals. The device is fully customizable and works exclusively with Bitcoin and Litecoin cryptocurrencies.

POS-terminal features:
  • Making Bitcoin payments without a computer, phone or tablet
  • High-quality laser QR-code reader, for scanning public keys (Bitcoin addresses)
  • Built-in printer for printing QR codes and replenishment receipts
  • Input and output on Coinkite debit cards
  • Accept payments from any Bitcoin user, including the official Bitcoin-QT client, Coinbase , Bitstamp , BIPS and many others.
  • Retail buy / sell your Bitcoin
  • Connect to the World Wide Web anywhere via GSM and Wi-Fi
  • Control currency rates any time (listing on check)
  • Setting your own exchange rate
  • Print invoices
  • and much more ...
