The initiative against reducing the limit of purchases abroad from 1,000 to 150 euros per month



Happy new year friends! Unfortunately, this year the limit of duty-free import of online purchases from abroad in Russia can be reduced from 1,000 euros per month to 150 euros. We have a chance to influence this .

This is undoubtedly a very unpleasant news. The Ministry of Finance of the Russian Federation proposes to limit the amount of duty-free import of goods from abroad, purchased via the Internet from 1,000 Euro per month per person now to 150 Euro per month in the future. There is also a possibility that it will be possible to receive only one parcel per month duty free! If this limit is exceeded, it is also planned to charge 30% of the amount of excess of the established limit per person.
