New tariff plans Skype and video conferencing in the next beta

Last night, Skype announced the introduction of a new system of tariff plans (subscriptions) for calls to fixed and mobile numbers. There are not so many details yet, but we are talking about a flexible system for creating our own personalized tariff plan.

The user can choose himself:
  • countries and regions that should be included in the subscription and whether this plan will include calls to mobile numbers, all over the city, or all in a given region / country
  • tariff plans with unlimited connection time or with packages of minutes (from 60 and more).
  • subscription validity period - from 1 month to a year.
