Ministry of Communications launched a new site and is waiting for comments on its work

The Ministry of Communications and Mass Media of Russia has launched a new version of the site, reports Rossiyskaya Gazeta. You can leave your comments and suggestions on the portal on a special page .

All data from beta.minsvyaz.ru transferred to the main domain, and the old version is available at the link old.minsvyaz.ru . In the "Documents" section are placed over a thousand industry regulations, including orders, orders and decrees of the Ministry. The calls to the Ministry of Communications through the feedback form can now attach one file of no more than 5 megabytes. The new site is divided into six sections and integrated with social networks.

The beta version of the new website of the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media was presented in December last year.
