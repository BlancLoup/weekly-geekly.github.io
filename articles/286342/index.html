5. Now, the main point of the story: Google Adsense as one of the best ways to monetize a website


My only opportunity to monetize a site (in my case, Romanian media) is Google Adsense, since Affiliate programs do not work well on the site. However, I also squeezed from partners for 30-200 euros per month.

My first profit in Google Adsense was 70 euros per month, the money came through Western Union. Perhaps nothing motivates to make more money, like the desire to earn more.

Every month I earned more and more:





To do this, I had to raise the percentage of clicks on advertising and traffic to the site, mainly through FB. Initially, my CTR ranged from 0.40% to 0.80%.

Everyone who uses Google Adsense is trying to find articles about how to raise CTR in this advertising network (if there are 100 people visiting the site, and one of them made a click on the advertisement, then CTR = 1%).

*** So, on the video posts CTR more; for posts where the main photo is hidden and only text is visible, the CTR is also high.

*** The most profitable place for advertising is under the title. How to find out? Leave only one block on the whole site and test it. The most clickable ad unit is 328x280.

MOST IMPORTANT: do not overdo it with CTR, as it happened with ME. (With rage and without a head on my shoulders, I brought him up to 25%)! If you have a site on a variety of topics, do not let the CTR exceed 3-5%. Anything more is free of risk for non-niche sites. If you have a niche website, for example, about furniture products, you can play around with up to 15-25% of CTR.

In June 2013, I reached 15 thousand euros a month, which I never received (and before that I received 2-3 thousand a month for half a year). Hah ... suffered a lot. All that helped me was my sporting life.

Conclusion: Google Adsense is a good tool, use it correctly and watch the CTR level.
