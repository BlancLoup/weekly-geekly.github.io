Anticipation

Going to the section where it was advertised, I fugitively reviewed the order of actions to be performed, namely:
1. Sign in to Skype Manager
From Skype Manager:
2. Pay for call channels and deposit some money into your SIP profile account.
3. Configure your company's PBX or VoIP gateway by specifying SIP profile information.
4. Give SIP profiles money to use Skype.
5. Start calling.
