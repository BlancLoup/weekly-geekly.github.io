The demand for equipment in Russia is falling, despite the holidays



According to Kommersant , the holidays, February 23rd and March 8th, were held in Russia without much growth in demand for equipment. Usually the situation is somewhat different, but this year the demand for equipment was not rising very actively. Probably the reason - the crisis and the desire of buyers to save money. At the same time, the fall in demand is expressed in units of goods sold, but the market volume in money terms is growing. This is due to the devaluation of the ruble.

Different companies selling appliances, the situation is also different. For example, the company Candy sales of washing machines fell by 30%. This was stated by the managing director of the company in Russia, Anton Kharin.
