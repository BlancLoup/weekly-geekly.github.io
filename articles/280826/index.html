Announcement of the Russian-language catalog of solutions of independent developers certified for Microsoft Azure

Dear colleagues, I am pleased to present to your attention the Russian-language catalog of independent developer solutions (ISV) certified for the Azure cloud platform.


This resource will host already popular and brand new, fresh, recently published solutions that have been certified for use in the Azure cloud and published in the Azure Marketplace solution store. We will use this portal as a place to promote solutions of independent developers, helping to acquire new users.

About what these decisions are, what is the Azure Marketplace store and how to get to it you can read below.

Azure Marketplace


We have already talked about the purpose of the Azure Marketplace and the details of using the store for developers and IT professionals to deploy complex third-party solutions, and for independent software developers (ISV) who want to enter new markets for the distribution of their solutions.

The Azure Marketplace is a catalog of solutions, services, images of environments of varying complexity, ready for rapid automatic deployment on the Azure platform.

The main idea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzure Marketplace is to give an independent developer a sales channel for their solutions around the world, and Azure customers are an easy way to deploy functional solutions in their own infrastructure.

Many ISVs have already certified their solutions for Azure and placed them in the Azure Marketplace, among them Red Hat, Oracle, CoreOS, MariaDB, Elasticsearch and hundreds of others. You can read more about some in this article , and the full list of solutions is always available on the official Azure Marketplace website .


One of the most recent additions to the Azure Marketplace is the open-source database Tarantool, which Mail.ru is developing. Details on its use from the store you can find in this article .

Azure Marketplace as a sales channel


We looked at the Azure Marketplace as a powerful tool that makes it easy for developers and IT professionals to deploy solutions and organize environments based on different solutions in the Azure cloud. On the other hand, Azure Marketplace is a powerful sales channel for independent software developers. Today dozens of new solutions from companies from many countries are added to the store every month. These solutions receive a variety of marketing support from Microsoft and additional promotion to the target audience. An example of such a promotion is the Microsoft Partner Apps channel.

With its presence in more than a hundred markets around the world, the Azure Markertplace channel is a great way for any software developer to enter the global scene and start selling themselves anywhere in the world without the hassle of legal and tax issues.

Azure Marketplace in Russia


Today, developers in three dozen countries can publish solutions in Azure Marketplce. While Russia is not included in the list of such countries and the publication for Russian developers is limited. However, several Russian companies with a global presence have already successfully published their solutions in the Azure Marketplace. Among them: Kaspersky Labs , Veeam , Jelastic , NGINX and Mail.ru.


The possibility of publishing solutions from Russia will be added in the near future. In the meantime, Russian developers and IT professionals can freely use the Azure Marketplace and third-party solutions to create new interesting projects based on the Azure cloud.

New Russian-language portal


Our new portal is designed to promote selected solutions from the Azure Marketplace to the Russian-speaking audience.

Using this portal, developers and IT professionals will be able to get acquainted with the solutions from the Azure Marketplace and find the right ones for their tasks.

On the portal we offer the following sections:
  • Coverage of selected solutions
  • Full list of solutions promoted through the catalog
  • Description of Azure Marketplace as a worldwide sales channel for independent developers
  • Azure Marketplace Solutions in Russia
  • Links to useful resources: videos, slides, articles
