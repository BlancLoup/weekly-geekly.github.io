Wall Street Server: VPS for Traders

The list of available technical means for a trader is constantly growing, a lot of technologies and ideas flow from other areas, adapting to the needs of financial market participants. If earlier a wide range of trading tools was available mainly to experienced professionals, now even a beginner can get almost any opportunities if he wishes, but not all traders use these opportunities.

So why should a trader have a virtual server, what are its advantages? Each trader encountered in his difficult work with the problem of a power outage or connection. And it does not matter whether you are trading from home, from the office or from coworking - as a rule, there is one line of food and communication. And when power is turned off in a building, the equipment installed in it is usually turned off. And even if you work from a laptop or tablet, the battery of the mobile device will not save you.


Losses from such failures can be significant. Failure to close a position, especially during the release of serious news, can cost profits in a few months of work, and sometimes just reset the account.

How to be in this situation? Of course, you can go through duplication. Buy a good UPS, which will power your computer for an hour, a professional router with redundant wired internet channels or ‚Äúspare‚Äù wireless access. However, such a solution to the problem is a rather costly and difficult method. If anyone can install a household UPS, even if they are not very well versed in technology, then not everyone can choose the right router and properly configure it.

As a rule, setting up this equipment by a third-party specialist will cost almost the price of the device itself. But in general, the cost of such a reservation can easily amount to 20-30 thousand rubles without taking into account subscriber payments for several communication lines and periodic replacement of batteries in the UPS.

Renting a VPS server is the key to successful trading


There is a more modern, cost-effective solution, and more importantly, a better, more reliable and mobile solution. This is a virtual dedicated server ( VPS / VDS) . Those who start trading professionally, sooner or later think about how to make it more comfortable and convenient. That is what VPS servers for traders are for.

In the case of a virtual server, you rent some of the computer‚Äôs resources on Windows (or Linux): its hard disk capacity, processor power, RAM, etc. This computer is located in the data center of one of the hosting providers and is accessed via the Internet.

You set the parameters of the virtual server when it is created and you can at any time increase its power in accordance with the trading activity.

Redundant Internet channels and serviceability through a single control panel, unlimited traffic - all this will allow you to focus on key tasks.

Remote trading Windows or Linux platform (Ubuntu, Debian, CentOS) will be available around the clock from any device with Internet access (Windows, Mac OS, Linux, iOS, Android), which makes mobile trading possible without installing additional trading applications.


Traders can use Virtual Private Server - a virtual server in the data center of a provider - to host exchange tools, such as trading terminals (QUIK, MetaTrader, Transaq, etc.). You can also install on a remote virtual server of the robot that will trade while you are busy with other things. Since the speed of execution of transactions depends mainly on the broker, the time to react to price changes and decision making is important. The robot needs only a few nanoseconds to respond to the situation.

Automated systems are able to process orders to buy and sell at high speed, so today the largest brokerage companies, banks and hedge funds make deals mainly with the help of robots. They speed up and simplify the process of stock trading, allow you to earn almost every participant in trading. A trading robot is trained to trade according to a certain strategy and can do everything on its own. It is enough to enable it on your terminal.


You can deploy a ready-to-use VPS server, including for algorithmic trading and auto-follow, in a matter of minutes, and only a few mouse actions will be required to change its configuration for current requirements.

A VPS server for trading often provides ready-made environments for traders.

It can be deployed from the template offered by the hosting provider with preinstalled software for stock trading, such as MetaTrader, Transaq or QUIK. The server will be ready to work in a couple of minutes, no need to spend time installing and configuring software tools. All that remains is to place the required files on the server (keys / certificates, etc.) and specify the data for access to the broker's servers.


Key advantages of such a server:

  1. Low cost of ownership. One trading terminal with several advisors / robots will require a configuration costing 500-600 rubles per month.
  2. Multiple redundancy of power supply and communication lines at the data center level.
  3. Absolute mobility. You will get access to the server from any PC, tablet, smartphone from anywhere in the world. At the same time, the speed of interaction of the server itself with the core of the exchange / trading system does not change, which is extremely important when trading.


Thus, the advantages of using virtual servers are the reliable provision of VPS with uninterrupted communication and power supply, its round-the-clock availability from anywhere in the world, high response times and geographical proximity to the exchange. When renting a VPS server, you are no longer tied to one computer and can access your server, your terminal, from any computer with Internet access, no matter where you are. Yes, and keep a lot of running terminals on your home PC is just uncomfortable.

Power outages and the loss of Internet access are not terrible for you. The terminal with advisers is located on the server and will work without interruption due to redundancy and high availability of systems in the data center .

The most up-to- date and powerful equipment is used as a platform for VPS, and they are running the latest versions of the licensed Windows Server OS , which guarantees high quality and reliability. The stability and high speed of trading instruments guarantees a service level agreement (SLA) and financial guarantees of continuous availability.

VPS is just


By connecting a VPS, you have at your disposal a remote virtual computer that functions 24 hours a day. Working with it is no more difficult than with a home PC: in most cases you will have a familiar Windows desktop. You can perform all the usual operations on such a virtual computer. In fact, before you all the same Windows, as on a regular PC. All actions, such as installing programs, copying files, etc., are performed in the same way as on your home computer.

Installation and setup of the trading terminal will take almost as much time as with a regular PC. No less important is the fact that the operation of the terminal and trading robots does not differ from the ‚Äúlocal‚Äù version.

For example, to install the MetaTrader 4 terminal, you need to go to your broker‚Äôs website (or to MetaQuotes, developers of the trading platform), download the installation file and run it, then go through the usual installation steps of the program. After starting the program after installation (if it did not start automatically), you will see the MetaTrader 4 terminal window. Then you can open a demo account and test the operation of the terminal.

Recently, there has been a growing interest in the MT4 terminal in conjunction with Linux systems. For example , you can install Metatrader 4 on a Linux virtual server with access via x2go.


You can disconnect from the VPS, while the trading terminals launched by you and any programs will continue their work. There are unforeseen situations when, due to some force majeure circumstances, or due to the fault of the provider, the VPS server restarts. You can make so that all the most important programs and trading terminals after the restart of the system started automatically and could continue their work. To do this, simply drag the program shortcut to the Startup folder, via the Start menu - All Programs - Startup. A special service like Ping Admin will allow you to always be aware of the server performance.

Thus, using simple steps you get access to a remote desktop. The only difference from a PC is that all your actions are transferred to the provider's server, so some delay in response to your actions is possible. But it does not mean that the virtual server "slows down".
