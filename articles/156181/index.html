Wandboard Freescale i.MX6 - mini PC for a developer for 69 USD



Now miniature PCs appear in a wide variety of options. We have to think that in the near future there will be much more models of such PCs than now, however, and now the number of mini-computers is large. Novelties appear with enviable regularity, and among such novelties there are those models that deserve special attention. Such a device is the Wandboard Freescale i.MX6, positioned by the manufacturer as a mini-PC for the developer.



This device without the body and without the information carrier is on sale. But there is a slot for an SD memory card, so there are no problems. By the way, not so long ago, the device manufacturer promised to start selling in November, and it seems that the promise will be kept. In any case, the first photos of the Wandboard Freescale i.MX6 have already appeared.



Mini-PC is equipped with all kinds of connectors, including Ethernet, HDMI, USB, microSD, audio-plug and serial port. There is also a GPIO connector for connecting additional devices.



Wandboard Freescale i.MX6 has a modular design that allows you to change the system configuration, including the replacement of RAM, processor and other components.

The device manufacturer offers two models. The first is equipped with 512 MB of RAM, a Freescal i.MX6 ARM Cortex-A9 processor, but there are no wireless communication modules.

But the model Wandboard Dual is equipped with a dual-core i.MX6 processor, 1 GB of RAM, wireless communication modules WiFi and Bluetooth.
