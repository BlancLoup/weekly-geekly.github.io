State of Opera in the first quarter of 2008

Recently, the financial results of the first quarter of 2008 were presented. Summarizing- Opera is profitable, it has a lot of money and the profit grows fastest in the entire business segment.

  • Opera is profitable, and profits grow faster than costs
  • Record quarter with a total profit growth of 57%
  • Costs increased by 25%
  • The profit from the Desktop application grew by 56%, respectively, with an increased number of users and a higher average profit per person (Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)) (did you search Google more often from the address bar?)
  • Over 20 Million Desktop Users
  • User base growth of 10% compared to 4th quarter 2007
  • 11.9 million people used Opera Mini in March
  • 20 billion pages viewed in Opera Mini
  • Monthly views in Opera Mini are skyrocketing
  • A big increase in profits from the direction of Opera Mini
