Here are the data that will be provided to the participants of the contest of ideas and geohacathone.


Map and transport network of Omsk. One file in SpatiaLite format, containing: addresses of houses, contours of houses, contours of districts of the city and villages of the region, the transport network of Omsk. Using this file, directly using SpatiaLite (or using your own algorithms) you can: perform direct geocoding (find the coordinates of a house by address), reverse geocoding (find the address by coordinates), build routes.

The map and transport network were carefully drawn and prepared by the cartographic department of EST Technologies. This is what we do for all cities where we work, because the address base and the transport network are very important for the functioning of taxi services.

SpatiaLite is a SQLite extension that adds geographic data types, corresponding indices and operations.

Tracks move taxi in Omsk in 2013. A lot of files in GPX 1.1 format. Tracks include speed, direction of travel (as accurate as possible on smartphones), and a driver ID. By identifier tracks can be compared with taxi orders. The tracks were recorded by the ‚ÄúECT: Driver‚Äù application during the order execution. Due to the nature of data collection, each track is limited to a minute interval: there are 60 second reports in each track, and there are several tracks per order.

GPX is the most universal format for presenting GPS tracks. They can even be displayed, for example, in Google Earth.

The history of real taxi orders in Omsk for 2013. Orders are uploaded to one SQLite file. For each order there are: driver ID, date and time of the beginning and completion of the order, address and geographical coordinates of the taxi address (beginning of the route) and destination (end of the route), distance traveled.
