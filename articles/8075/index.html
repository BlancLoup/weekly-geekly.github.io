Or, some believe, the quarrel itself was part of the negotiations conducted by the companies.

According to Techcrunch , the founders of Photobucket have tried to sell their business since March of this year, when they turned to Lehman Brothers for help. Initially, they estimated the site at 300 million, but probably there were not so many potential buyers who could outbid the price offered by MySpace.
