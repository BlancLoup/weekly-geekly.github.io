Refusal to use ICQ

The post concerns any instant messenger, such as XMPP, AIM, MSN, etc
Last week I have been experimenting on myself - I absolutely do not use ICQ. Despite the fact that I am an ICQ user with experience, that I have a beautiful six-digit sign, despite the fact that quite recently I had a hundred contacts, I decided that such communication should be stopped.

The reasons


  • ICQ is distracting . It doesn't matter if I have eclipse or quake open - the incoming message makes me break away from the process, at least mentally.
  • ICQ is unproductive . There is no guarantee that you will be answered immediately. However, the IM-communication format implies that you need to wait for an answer and spend attention on it.
  • ICQ is not informative . No matter how fast you type on the keyboard, speaking is still faster. In addition, ICQ does not transmit intonation - and after all, nonverbalicus makes up 90% of the flow of information, as they say NLPShniki.
  • ICQ creates the illusion of accessibility . You can always send a quote from Bash, Lalka, the hated "immediately spread it, and not that ...", ask for advice ...
  • ICQ is easier to talk about . A conversation that takes 5-10 minutes on the phone, over ICQ can take hours - did you notice?


Alternatives


For conversations is the phone. Yes, mobile communication is more expensive than ICQ - for me this is a plus , as it helps to appreciate the time.

I have not yet considered the option of VoIP, because few familiar users. Although, it seems to me, ip-telephony is perfect for negotiating with remote customers.

Well, for correspondence was, is and will be e-mail.

Besides...
