Export Gmail and Google Calendar archives

Google has now allowed to export all the information from the Google Calendar. The feature is already activated for 100% of users. Export from Gmail is activated for 1% of users, by the end of January the figure will reach 100%. Mail is exported to MBOX format, which is supported in Microsoft Outlook 2011, Mozilla Thunderbird and Apple Mail.



Link to export Gmail and Calendar data, and here is a complete list of supported products for exporting personal information.

When exporting to zip, archives are created with a maximum of 2 GB, so it is better to choose .tgz or .tbz (50 GB max.). After creating the archive, you can download it no more than five times.
