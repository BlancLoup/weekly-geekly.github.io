Yandex.Money organizes courses for mobile developers in St. Petersburg

Yandex.Money offers free courses for experienced iOS and Android developers. Upon completion of training, each programmer must submit their own final draft. Developers who have successfully completed the training will be issued certificates.

The courses will be taught by leading developers of the company. The development training program for the Android platform includes an overview of development tools, application components, UI principles, ways of storing data, performing tasks in the background, multithreading, networking, and Android Support Repository & Google Play Services.

IOS programmers will increase their knowledge on topics such as Foundation classes, memory management, UI components, application life cycle, creating an adaptive interface using Interface Builder and software tools, as well as blocks, multithreading, networking and data storage.

The start of training is scheduled for September 7, the duration of training is a month. Courses will be held in the St. Petersburg office of Yandex.Money in the evenings.
