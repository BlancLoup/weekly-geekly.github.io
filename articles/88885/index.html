Even among two-dimensional games small pearls come across. Who remembers Worms, Jets'n'Guns and Braid?

Iji is a two-dimensional Doom 2D style shooter . Managing the girl, in whose body alien nanotechnologies have been installed, you will need to fight against the races that have descended to their home planet, and make them fly away. Destroy everything you see on your way - or avoid encounters with them; break doors, become stronger and more secure and achieve your goal with weapons or peace.
