In the REDMOND Smart Home series, there are still multicookers, kettles, coffee makers, humidifiers and air purifiers, floor and kitchen scales, heaters. All devices are distinguished by the fact that they have a built-in wireless module capable of receiving commands from other devices. For submission of these commands developed application Ready for Sky. It is available for iOS and Android and allows you to remotely manage the entire range of equipment of the REDMOND Smart Home line.

The iron is made of soft-touch glossy plastic (it is partially translucent), the assembly is perfect. When the device is turned off automatically, the buzzer beeps, and the LED on the handle flashes. To continue ironing, simply lift the iron, the indicator of the heating of the sole will light up.
