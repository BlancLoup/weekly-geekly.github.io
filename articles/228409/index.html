Expert opinion

What do developers think about the implementation of communications in their business solutions - do you need to integrate everything around e-mail? - I asked this question to the expert of the CEO of the Dental-Cloud service Yaroslav Osetrov.

- Yaroslav, you are creating SaaS service for automating dental clinics and are you planning to add ‚Äúspecialist-client‚Äù communication functionality to the service and what will they be?

- If we talk about the health care industry, with the help of electronic communications, the doctor can constantly communicate with the patient, quickly answer questions, monitor the course of treatment of patients, and possibly conduct a patient's appointment online, instead of visiting the clinic.
E-mail is an attractive way to communicate for both doctors and patients, and the demand for such a service will undoubtedly increase every year. However, one should not forget that many details and non-verbal signals are lost when communicating by mail, and as a result there can be a misunderstanding between the doctor and the patient.
