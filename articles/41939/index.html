Google finally feeds RSS for search results

Google has officially confirmed that in the near future RSS feeds will be connected to the search results, so that you can subscribe to the channel for a specific keyword and track all new links on this topic.

Monitoring the search results at the moment only works through e-mail, through the Google Alerts newsletter, but this is clearly not convenient. Until now, Google remains the only major search engine that does not have a subscription to search feed RSS feeds. Those users who needed this functionality had to use third-party services to monitor Google or write their own scripts.

According to available information , the long-awaited service will be launched within the next month.
