How Yandex and Google sum up the year



Yandex and Google summed up the year and told which topics in 2018 turned out to be more popular than others. The results of the study were divided into several categories. Yandex had more of them - ten, against six of Google. However, some of the rubrics of the two search engines intersect. The more curious to compare the interests of their audiences.

Filed under "Yandex" : events, people who have gone, news, movies, TV shows, TV shows, games, music, memes. The graphs showed what and at what point there was a particular interest among the users of this search engine, and also resulted in the most popular formulations of queries.



Filed under Google in Russia : events, people, losses, the names of the World Cup, how to become ..., memes. At the same time, the global ranking was not six, but nine rubrics, most of which are different: search, events, people, actors, athletes, losses, movies, music, TV shows.


Yandex determined the interest according to a more complex algorithm than simply by the number of requests. Instead, we measured the value of the wave of requests. The algorithm took into account requests with new formulations, as well as the delta of requests in already known formulations between 2017 and 2018. Google says that "the lists are based on the search queries for which the largest increase in traffic was recorded this year compared to last year."
