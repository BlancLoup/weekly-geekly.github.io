Online store of industrial equipment

To begin with, I got a job in a company that sells industrial equipment and components more than a year ago. I worked there part-time, something like an incoming employee. Soon, I suggested to the authorities to develop an online store, as this can increase and simplify sales.
Why am I saying simplify? All due to the fact that there are different types of equipment, some are conditionally 5.000rub, while others are more than 500.000 euros. That's just, expensive equipment to sell via the Internet is difficult, if not impossible, all due to the fact that such equipment is bought by factories and enterprises, and of course, everything goes through the main engineers and kickbacks to them to buy from you, this the simplest scheme, most often everything is much more complicated.


Start


It all started with the fact that I began to choose a management system, a large number of different systems are offered on the market. Since the store did not require particularly complex functions and capabilities, customers often are no longer young people and the simpler it is for them, the better.

The simplicity of the interface ( intuitively clear - all contacts are visible ), how not cool, but people refuse to register and order via the Internet, more than 70% of customers still call and order by phone. We even indicated discounts for registered users, those who make an order through the online store, and not by phone. All because of the fact that to reduce the burden on consultants, as they had to communicate by telephone and via the Internet. And to find experienced and knowledgeable employees of the equipment is expensive. Especially for a developing project, that's why we tried to reduce the load.

Step 2. Cash payment


The problem we faced was the sale of equipment at retail, many of our customers ordered equipment at the garage, to the country, etc. Our company was registered as an LLC and we could not issue an invoice for a part of our clients; they came to the office and took the goods for cash. The head solved this problem simply, in his opinion, he registered another IP, through which he sold for cash.

Step 3. Maintenance


Equipment maintenance, here with it, the most, in our opinion, big problems, as much of it requires constant maintenance. To do this, we held talks with a number of services by region and concluded agreements to accept for maintenance and warranty service for our customers.
