Buster - twitter client for bus

I do not know about you, but I spend more time on twitter than on facebook. Basically, I read it from my phone on the way to or from work (I travel by bus). Through Twitter, I learn the main news, discover new articles and blogs, and also just find a lot of interesting things.

Unfortunately, all the clients that I tried, I somehow did not fit. They work great for those who constantly follow new tweets, but I go there a couple of times a day to see what's new. So I decided to write my ‚Äúthin‚Äù client. I've been using it for a month and a half while I like it, so I decided to share it with the community, maybe someone else will do.

Meet the bus ter ( www.busterhq.com )





Features:
  • Synchronization read tweets, shows only tweets that you have not yet seen.
  • All new tweets from one user are shown together to make it easier to follow the development of the plot.
  • Priority - tweets from your friends will always be shown first (just press the pin next to the friend's name).


In the future, I plan to implement support for twitter cards, unpack short URLs and add previews of pictures.
