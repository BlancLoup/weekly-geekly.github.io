iCamp Russia, the last chance to catch the ship

Friends!
You, of course, remember the Russian project iCamp Russia (who does not remember, goes to icamp2009.ru ).
This year iCamp will again be held not in the capital. This time we chose Nizhny Novgorod and Kazan.
On July 24, the main event (barcamp only a part of it) will be held in Nizhny Novgorod, and the participants will go to Kazan by motor ship, which will also have barcamp sessions and other events.
If, by the will of fate, you will be on July 26 in Kazan - come to the pier and climb aboard our motor ship.

Participation in the event is, of course, free of charge, but you have to pay for self-delivery, housing and food.

On the boat we still have a dozen and a half places. Therefore, if someone wants to spend a week in Russia, of which 4 days sailing on a comfortable boat on the Volga - register on our website, book and buy a voucher for the ship.

Places on the iCamp Russia motor ship of the following categories are currently available :
  • Place in a triple cabin (standard): 9 000 RUR (~ $ 280).
  • Place in a triple cabin (junior suite): 10 000 RUR (~ $ 310).
  • A place in a four-bed cabin (standard): 9 000 RUR (~ $ 280).

The stay includes accommodation in a cabin of the chosen category (4 days), three meals a day, gala dinner in Lukomorye (green parking on July 28).
