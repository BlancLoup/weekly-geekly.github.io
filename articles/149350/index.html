Interactive 3D tour of Versailles from Google



Despite the fact that the vast majority of representatives of the Habr community have a technical mindset, many still are not averse to join the beautiful. Therefore, some of us attend various kinds of exhibitions and museums. Of course, due to lack of time (and / or money) it is not always possible to see what you want. But modern technology comes to the rescue. Want to visit Versailles without getting up from your chair / chair / floor? Then you should take advantage of the new project of Google.

Based on WebGL, the project shows us the most famous sites of the French palace, which is considered one of the most advanced of its kind. The project itself was called " Chaos to Perfection ".

Of course, the corporation, creating such a project, pursued its own interests. One of them is the promotion of Chrome, because it is this browser that perfectly supports WebGL technology. If you have Chrome, consider yourself lucky. If not, you will have to download and install an additional plugin .
