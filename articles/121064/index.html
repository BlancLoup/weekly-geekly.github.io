A little about why the project Actio.tv received international recognition. Interview with Alexey, the founder of the project.

Alexey, in what market does your project work?

Market video and video advertising. The video is developing on the Internet at a tremendous pace. Now for the month on Youtube and Facebook more videos are being uploaded than shown on the TV channels ABC, NBC and CBS in the entire history of their existence.

The video is getting bigger, but there is no effective way to monetize it. We create tools to turn an ordinary video into an interactive one! What became of the text when it turned into hypertext. Large sites are interested in new technologies that will allow them to manage advertising, create new formats, expand the range of services for the advertiser. On the other hand, advertisers are interested in increasing the effectiveness of their advertising costs.
