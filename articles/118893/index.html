Clouds for a regular user

And what did you transfer to the clouds from your everyday life?

About a year ago, I thought about moving into the clouds of everything that surrounds me. For a long time I considered various options and chose Google as my main platform. I changed the phone to an android phone and rushed.

1) E-mail : I completely refused to buy the bat and switched to google mail , set up redirects to the main mail in all mails and gave permission to the main mail to send letters from all my mailboxes. Immediately I will pay attention to security and the ability to bind Google Mail to a cell phone number, which will allow you to regain access to the box in case of loss of control or hacking.

2) Contacts : everything translated into Google contacts , very convenient, automatic synchronization to android phone, impossibility of losing contacts when the phone is lost / broken.

3) Money : for the most part went to the visa / master card, tied the card to the webmoney and poison, convenient, affordable, small percentages. And in general in life I prefer to pay with a card.

4) Reading sites : in fact, before the day began with poking at speeddiale for a dozen other sites + constant monitoring of sites during the day. All that can be transferred to the google-reader subscription to rss, everything has become convenient, comfortable and allows you to save time on unnecessary clicks and does not allow you to miss important things.

5) Reminder-time managment : here I switched to miniplan.ru , there is a day planning, reminders to mail / sms (for free), the only thing that is missing is cyclic tasks like GoogleCalendar.

6) Office documents : of course, the Gugldocs saved, I use it to store important files by means of these ads. Therefore, I do not use the same dropbox.

7) Browser : I refused to use the opera because it slowed down and did not work much in it (same Gugldoki), she couldn‚Äôt synchronize her bookmarks, settings, passwords, and she didn‚Äôt have any plug-ins (it was too late that they all implemented). I chose chrome , can synchronize everything I need, the only negative is that plug-ins are not able to synchronize their data for the most part.

8) Passwords : I use a bundle from passpack.com and lastpass , there are plugins for all browsers, it works under android phone.

9) Music : oh yeah, all the music now I listen to from the Internet, I especially liked the deceased simple player , I even think about paying them, and I also use the grwshark and VKontaktik .

In general, these services allowed me to become significantly more mobile and not be tied to a specific computer.
