4th Innovators Club Meeting in St. Petersburg

Next Wednesday (March 24), the 4th Innovators Club Meeting will take place - a meeting where startups can present the best sides of their projects, get expert evaluation and even funding. The topic of the upcoming meeting: Invest Pitch Investor Presentation .



At the meeting, you can not only learn about the technology of preparing and giving presentations to investors: what to look for when drawing up a business plan and financial model. But also to understand the main points of investment, as well as to understand why it is impossible to give money to everyone who asks, and some even harmful.

Each of the experts will reveal the secret to what he pays attention when he hears the presentation of a startup, and after which phrase he will point to the door, and after which he will sign a cooperation agreement.

The meeting will be held at the Innovation and Technology Center of St. Petersburg State University ITMO on the Exchange Line, 14 .

Invited experts:
  • Alexey Tolmachev , Director of the St. Petersburg Venture Fund
  • Mikhail Podgayets - Chairman of the Expert Council of the Non-Commercial Partnership SOBA (St. Petersburg Organization of Business Angels)

As a guest lecturer, Anatoly Abramovich Shalyto , a well-known to many young IT scholars of St. Petersburg, will be speaking - Professor, Head of the Programming Technologies Department at SPSU ITMO.

The agenda of the meeting will also include the traditional information block from the Zvorykinsky Project and the Innovators Club , containing the results of the past month, the announcement of upcoming events and presentations by representatives of partner programs.

Participation in the meeting is free , but by prior arrangement.
Details about the meeting, read the Club website .

Another good news is that the Innovators Club announced the launch of an affiliate program and invites everyone who has something useful to offer startups to send applications.

If you have interesting suggestions on topics, formats and chips for the next meetings, please write your suggestions in the comments.
