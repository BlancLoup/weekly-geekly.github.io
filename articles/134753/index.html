Lua 5.2.0 released

After a long wait, version 5.2 of the Lua language is finally released.

The official site at the time of this writing has not yet been updated, but here is the announcement in the official newsletter: thread.gmane.org/gmane.comp.lang.lua.general/86552

The list of changes in the language can be found here: www.lua.org/work/doc/manual.html#8 (in English, after updating the site it will most likely be available in another URL).

Download the official implementation here: www.lua.org/ftp/lua-5.2.0.tar.gz

Mirror: www.tecgraf.puc-rio.br/lua/ftp/lua-5.2.0.tar.gz
