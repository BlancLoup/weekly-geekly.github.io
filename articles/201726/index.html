Let me introduce myself: Payler



We present a new project in the field of receiving payments for e-commerce - Payler. Our goal is to simplify the process of accepting payments from bank cards as much as possible and to create a service that not only allows you to receive money, but also provides analytics for all transactions. What solutions do we offer?


Payler is an online payment system with the function of monitoring payments and building business development forecasts. First of all, it is suitable for private online stores and merchants. It allows you to debug the process of accepting card payments as flexibly and conveniently as possible, and also collects detailed analytics on all transactions. Such analytics allows you to track sales conversion and quickly adjust your business strategy.
Payler payments are analyzed, organized and displayed in clear graphs, from which you can understand the overall picture of what is happening with your business. For example, you can trace the growth or decline in profits from the beginning of the activity to the present, track the most popular products, predict a possible decrease or increase in sales.

Payler main features:
- Payments using bank cards via the Internet online 24/7.
- Protection against electronic fraud;
- Individual support service for each client;
- The ability to bind to your virtual wallet an unlimited number of bank cards;
- The possibility of implementing loyalty programs for customers.

At the moment we are in the active development stage: we are completing work on the web version of the service, are being audited for PCI compliance
DSS level 2.0, in order to obtain the right to conduct transactions, in parallel, we are working on a mobile version. Start Payler is scheduled for November of this year, that is, during this month we plan to launch the first version.
