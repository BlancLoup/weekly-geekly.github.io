Social dictionary - learn English together!

It was like this: once I realized that my English vocabulary is rather poor, and this greatly interferes with life and work, because most of the information I am interested in (manuals, blogs, articles) is written in English. So you need to urgently learn the words! But learning everything in alphabetical order is not the best solution, because most texts are a little less than completely composed of a limited number of words. These are most verbs, prepositions and a certain number of nouns. Thus, if we make a selection of a sufficiently large number of sites on various subjects, we will be able to find out which words and parts of speech are used most often.
So this service was born - Social Dictionary

Probably, it would be more correct to call it a ‚Äúmini-service‚Äù, since It consists of only five pages, and is written in a few days.
The essence of his work is very simple - users add links to English-language sites. The system parses these sites and collects some statistics. After some time of work, when the amount of accumulated information will be large enough, you can sum up and make charts of the most popular words that you can safely learn :)
