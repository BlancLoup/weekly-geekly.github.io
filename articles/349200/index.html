Veeam Academy, created on the basis of Veeam Software, launches intensively in March 2018 for C # developers. Intensive will be useful to those who already have a basic knowledge of programming, but who want to get more practice and participate in the team development of a software product.

At the seminars, participants will become familiar with the basic features of the C # language, the .Net platform, and the principles of OOP. They will practice the acquired skills on the example of a pilot project, the theme of which will be chosen at the first lesson all together.

The work will be organized on the principle of Agile to teach participants effective team development. More information about the program: academy.veeam.ru

After the successful completion of Veeam Academy, the best graduates will have a chance to join the company and become part of Veeam Research & Development.
