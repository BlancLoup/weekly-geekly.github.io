Who are the project managers?

In general, the project manager is a responsible employee, whose responsibilities include creating something in the conditions of general uncertainty. Create, within the time, quality and budget constraints in a predetermined environment.

That is, the project manager is a person who realizes some goal qualitatively and on time, using pre-determined resources: temporary, human, organizational and material.

This is where he looks like a small animal to which: given out are a shovel, seeds, a watering can and a support group; and the task was set to grow a Christmas tree for the New Year's mornings.

What to know where to go?

With a set of skills and abilities already fun - in the project manager must be combined perfectly opposite qualities. If a coin has always two sides, then the project manager is generally multi-faceted in its manifestations.

Boring guys from different scientific research institutes of the times of the bubonic plague made up a stack of standards describing the activities up to nausea in detail, but in reality the work of the project manager consists of:
1. Planning
2. Control
3. Communication

In principle, a project manager should be able to do only two things: manage the state of affairs and communicate. The only difference from the present leader is increased stress tolerance. Projects always imply time limits, and in such conditions some members of the team must pass their nerves. Is it contagious.
