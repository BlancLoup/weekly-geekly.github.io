Errrr - Simplest Error Recorder

I make websites. And sometimes it turns out that these sites have shoals: in the layout, spelling, or somewhere, an error pops up. And people write about this error to the mail, to the contact, sms, and in the end something is sure to be lost. And recently, I finally understood! Enough tolerating this! :) That's how the idea was born, to the horror of a simple bugtracker that would help me not to forget about project bugs. And then I thought it was worth making it not only for myself. And here, meet: http://errrr.ru/ - The simplest error logger


How it works?


Everything is very simple. You create a project, then add a form that will be used to collect information. The form has its own link that you place on the pages of the site. Any user who wants to leave an error message will see a report form, and you, in turn, will receive his message. Each message is assigned a status.
