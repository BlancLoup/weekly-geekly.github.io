Yekaterinburg, July 10 - Alfa-Bank's mitap on system analysis

As promised, we continue to hold useful meetings, not only in Moscow, but also abroad.

Yekaterinburg, July 10, 19.00.
BC "Vysotsky" (Malysheva street, 51).



The program includes 4 reports from our guys about auto-tests, success metrics, Pega BPM and internal entrepreneurship.

Program


Ignat Zherlygin, Leading Systems Analyst, Equation //
Land for peasants, factories for workers, autotests for analysts

He will talk about how our processes are organized, whether it is possible to consider a qualitative situation when an analyst and a tester are one person, and how to make it so that auto tests can write everything.

Yana Silacheva, Head of the Central Systems Analytics Competence Center //
System analysis as a methodology for solving problems or how to do it

Yana on real cases will show the secrets of the success of banking products, as well as talk about the metrics of project success and how system analysis helps to achieve them.

Mikhail Markov, and. Director of Innovation and Marketing at UrFU //
Internal business in itself

Oriental business practices and a sense of ownership of the product.

Alexander Tashirov, Head of Pega Competence Center //
Transformation of Bank processes on the Pega BPM platform

Alexander will tell you how to build processes that are ready for change, touch on the topic of the role of the analyst in this and consider other approaches to record keeping.

When and where


July 10 (Wednesday), 19.00.
Yekaterinburg, Malysheva street, 51 (Business center "Vysotsky").
