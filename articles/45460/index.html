All the flaws of non-click sites

There is a new trend to create sites
One of the pioneers in runet steel: VIERA (Panasonic TVs) and Lexus RX350

Yes, of course, everything new attracts, but as for me - this technology has many disadvantages in usability.

1. The number of random navigation through the pages increases by several times, therefore the time spent by the user for viewing the site will increase too, and the time is inefficiently spent.

2. If we look through the gallery of pictures, then before we had to click to view the next picture, now we have to remove the mouse and bring it up again. What is the benefit of technology if you have to make extra moves?

3. Such sites are usually made on a flash.

4. But what about your favorite tips, when you can see a short description of what we get after the transition before clicking on the link?

In general, I believe that this technology will be in demand only for a narrow circle of sites of a narrow circle of webmasters.
