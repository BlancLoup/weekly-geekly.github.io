Master class "Software Architecture". Basic course to prepare for international certification iSAQB

On June 4-6, 2013 in Moscow, international expert Dr. Peter Khrushka will hold a 3-day master-class "Software Architecture". This master class is a basic course to prepare for international certification iSAQB.

Reference:
Dr. Peter Khrushchka is the founder of the Atlantic Systems Guild, an internationally renowned group of experts (www.systemsguild.com), which includes Tom DeMarco and Tim Lister. Included in the editorial board of IT magazines, including the founder and member of the International Council for the Development of Requirements (IREB) and the International Council on the Qualification of Software Architecture (ISAQB). Founder of the Agile community in Germany. One of the developers of the ARC42 systems architectural documentation template. A pioneer in modeling tools for structural and object-oriented methodologies. Frequent speaker at IT conferences, consultant, among his clients are many companies from the Fortune 500 list. During the practical classes you will learn important tools and get the skills you need for a modern architect. Among the key themes of the master class are the construction of flowcharts and the working cycle, effective design and cross-cutting problems, documentation, assessment methods and metrics. You can learn more about the master class program on the website.

The master class is designed for anyone who feels responsible for the structure of the IT system: mainly software architects, technical IT managers, designers, programmers and quality management staff. In addition, project managers will learn the rationale for design decisions. Thus, they are able to optimize the structure of the development team.
