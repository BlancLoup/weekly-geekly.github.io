Kholodilnik.Ru could not sue Holodok.info domain competitors

Trading house "Edil" tried to take away the domain Holodok.info from competitors. Moscow businessmen appealed to international arbitration, but lost the process.

Trading house "Edil" is well known in RuNet. They own an online store Kholodilnik.ru , as well as a number of domain names, including holod.ru, holodilnik.ru, holodilnic.ru, holodilnik.info, holod.info with an aggregate attendance of about 45 thousand hosts per month. In 2003-2006 they have registered the trademarks COLD, HOLOD and HOLODILNIK. This fact was the reason for filing a claim. The plaintiff stated that the disputed domain, although it does not coincide with the registered trademark, is so close to it, so that they can be confused.

As is known, the issues of the transfer of rights to domains that coincide with a registered trademark are handled by the Center for Arbitration and Mediation at the World Intellectual Property Organization. Case No. D2006-0612 at the suit of the Edil trading house was considered by the independent expert of the international center Irina Savelyeva.

The right holders filed their claim to international arbitration on May 17, 2006. The very next day, lawyers appealed to the registrar and received official confirmation of the identity of the owner of the domain, Anton Baranov. The controversial domain holodok.info was registered on January 27, 2005, after which an online refrigerator store was opened on it - a direct competitor to the Kholodilnik.ru store.
