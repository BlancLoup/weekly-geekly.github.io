To do list to capture the world


MyAgenda is a service for keeping to-do lists. It is so simple that it does not require registration.
This is generally the main idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe service, to make simple things really simple.

We all have ever made lists of cases, nothing complicated about it.
Of course, keeping a list on a piece of paper is not always convenient. He can get lost, frayed, he can be eaten by a dog or a cat will play somewhere. And you want something so simple and in electronic form.
That is why this simple electronic to-do list appeared.
No need to register, you can immediately take and write down all the cases. Very easy, very fast.



Since I have been working on this project for quite a long time, I need a fresh look from the outside. Earlier, I had already done 2 small tests and each time it brought new ideas that made it possible to make the service a little better.
At this time, I want to conduct more extensive testing, try to identify typical scenarios of work and the main difficulties.
Therefore, I want to invite Habr's audience to conduct an examination of my service. I would be grateful for any comments, suggestions and discussions.

And now the answer to the question of life, death, and all that which probably is in your head. 42. Why did I make another to-do list of which is already full on the Internet?
It's simple (I love this word! :). For a couple of years I have been looking for a list of cases that would suit me. I tried a lot of different services. Each of them had some nice features, but using lists was generally inconvenient. As a rule, because it is either a 100-in-1 Swiss knife, or it lacked the functions I needed, or they were, but they were made very uncomfortable. In the end, I got sick of it and decided to try to make the to-do lists exactly as I would like.
The work is not finished yet, to be honest, it has just begun. And a great many plans. ;)
