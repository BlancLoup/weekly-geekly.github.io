This ship will be used to deliver cargo and astronauts to the asteroid redirected to the lunar orbit, and then to Mars. Orion will be transported by a heavy booster Delta IV Heavy. It should be noted that the first launch of a new launch vehicle with the new module will take place as early as December of this year.
