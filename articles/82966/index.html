10 predictions about online advertising and promotion in 2010




In this small post I want to give some of the most likely forecasts for the development of Internet promotion and advertising in the coming year. My statements are based on my own observations, experience in this field and some analytical materials. And you, in turn, can use this information as you please.

2010 is the year of SMM & SMO






Everyone is already well aware that Social Media Optimization & Social Media Marketing is far from new to the promotional online projects. But in 2010, these methods and methods will reach a new level and will finally be included in the tools of each modern communication agency.


Banners and mediyka work less






Do you notice banners? Me not. Many of my friends too. Most geeks are banner-cutters in browsers or various adbloki . OK, they are far from everyone, and media advertising works well with proper media planning. But, you see, this kind of advancement is becoming increasingly secondary.

The context is even more personalized.






One of the reasons for the decline of media advertising for a year is the context . So, in 2010 he will become even more meticulous and will know everything about you, thanks to the efforts of our favorite search giants. They already know about us more than we do about yourself. Do you remember what you did a year ago at the same time, what you watched and read about during the past six months? They remember.

Advertising and PR-agencies are increasingly using promotion in social networks.







In early 2009, advertising agencies that resorted to the services of social promotion and viral promo could be counted on the fingers. At the end of the same year, it became a fashion trend. In 2010, every self-respecting communication agency will offer social media promotion services . Let not the main service, but these methods will firmly take root among the PR people, marketers and advertisers of all stripes.
