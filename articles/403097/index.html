Flying car



A few years ago, the AeroMobil prototype was introduced, which at the moment has become a model of a flying car that is almost ready for commercial use. And now other manufacturers began to catch up. The French aviators from Airbus, world-famous for their planes, decided to take up cars, but not simple, but also those who know how to fly.
