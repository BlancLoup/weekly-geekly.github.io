Source: pharmvestnik.ru

Less than six months after the division of Hewlett Packard into two companies, as it became known about the launch of the new venture fund HP Tech Ventures .

New HP Inc. received all the assets for the development and production of personal computing systems and printers. Hewlett Packard Enterprise got the entire HP corporate business.

The team of the fund of eight experts will be headed by Andrew Bolwell, writes Techcrunch. He has worked for HP Inc.16 for years. Over the past few years, he has earned the title of chief expert on "generating original and breakthrough ideas."

The fund plans to focus on seed investments, as well as on Series A rounds. This plan is part of HP Inc. strategy. The company is interested in projects in the areas of 3D printing, augmented and virtual reality, home and industrial robotics, the Internet of Things ( IoT ).
