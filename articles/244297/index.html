All-metal line of smartphones Samsung GALAXY A got to Russia

Good afternoon, Habr!

On December 5, in Moscow, there will be a presentation of a new line of smartphones in the all-metal body of the Samsung GALAXY A, namely, its first ‚Äúswallows‚Äù: the 5-inch GALAXY A5 and the 4.5-inch GALAXY A3. Metal smartphones have a 5-megapixel front camera and are the thinnest mobile devices from Samsung today: only 6.7 mm and 6.9 mm thick, respectively.



The older model of the Samsung GALAXY A5 series is equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 quad-core processor with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz, a 5-inch Super AMOLED screen (1280x720), a 13-megapixel main camera and a 5-megapixel front camera. Also, the smartphone has 2 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal memory, which can be expanded with a slot for microSD-cards up to 64 GB. The device supports LTE networks (Cat4). Battery capacity - 2300 mAh. The smartphone works on Android 4.4 KitKat with TouchWiz. It is worth noting the Multi-Window mode, which allows you to simultaneously display two windows of different applications on the screen. The size of the model: 139.3 x 69.7 x 6.7 mm. Weight - 123 g.
