Hamburger Internet Business

Before the first Siberian Weekend (Novosibirsk, March 25-27) Glavstart held a Communication Tube in Tomsk and Novosibirsk. On the example of projects that spoke, Arkady Moreynis showed the authors of the projects how to look at his idea through the eyes of business, why projects need expert help and what is common between a startup and a hamburger.

The project is a big bag.
The project, which has become a successful business, is similar to Big Mac. It has a cutlet (main product / technology), an upper bun (the audience that needs this product) and a lower bun (like what problems the audience will solve the product).

Investors and the market first want to hear from you: what you offer, to whom you offer it and how you are going to do it. Also, going to the diner, you want to get a well-known and understandable hamburger, and not the nameless patty.

Why can not sell one "cutlet".
One of the projects that performed in Novosibirsk offered another service for a quick taxi call. The ideas are not unique, but the authors.
To start a project, authors must be able not only to give birth to an idea and write code - this is only the very beginning. The success of the next stage depends on the competences and abilities of the team. The main work begins when you have to analyze the needs of the audience, to promote and sell their services.
