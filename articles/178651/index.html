About Unite Nordic and Motivation



Game developers like to go to different conferences to gain experience, look at other people's projects, promote their own, find a publisher, or vice versa, find projects, teams ... Work, fan and communication, in general.

According to a similar formula, since 2007 Unity holds the annual Unite conference in different parts of the world. Until 2013, United was alone; now we have decided to make our own conference in every part of the world. For Europe, this is Unite Nordic , which will be held immediately after the CWI, from May 21 to 22 in the Swedish city of Malmo, which is located near the Copenhagen Airport . Tickets cost around 230 USD for adults and 76 USD for students .

Why go to Unite Nordic?



  • See and hear Unity CEO David Helgason
  • Learn from the head of our Chinese office, how to fill in China
  • See Nintendo and learn about publishing to a new console
  • Blackbury talks about publishing to a new platform
  • Learn about 2D perspectives in Unity
  • Once again find out how they do it on Kickstarter
  • Hear from the creator of profiler Unity, how it works ...
  • Understand the prospects for Linux publishing
  • See and hear a series of smart reports about Unity from the developers themselves
  • Understand that it is time to raise your English :)


In fact, take a look at the schedule , perhaps my list does not match your priorities.

See you at FlashGamm Moscow, KRI, and then at Unite Nordic!
