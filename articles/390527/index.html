3D Systems announced the launch of a new line of devices ProJet MJP 3600 series



More recently, 3D Systems has received a real thrashing from the investor community, who learned about a partnership agreement between the industry giant and 3D Systems' direct competitor in the face of Carbon3D. It would seem that on this game is over, but it was not there: it seems, 3D Systems is not going to curtail their production and announces the launch of a new line of 3D printers for sale.

ProJet MJP 3600 devices are the development of ideas implemented in the last 3500th series, which included a fairly large number of machines for various applications.



New devices have several key differences:

First, they print print objects twice as fast as their predecessors. But if we talk about quality, everything here remains at the same level. Most users will need only one increase in speed, so that they will be able to increase the volume of products several times.



It should also be noted that the 3600th series is equipped with a more powerful processor, which allows processing 3D models that are 2.5 times larger than those that could be loaded into devices of the previous series. And this means that now you can print objects that are even more complex in their structure.



Some design solutions have also been refined - in particular, the cleaning of auxiliary material is now much faster. In the picture above you can see the part in the cleaning process. For greater clarity, you can also view the following video of this process:



In addition, it should say a few words about the appearance of new printers. There are no major differences from the previous series of machines. We can say the same devices. However, the commitment to universality and total standardization cannot be called a negative factor - although the release of models indistinguishable from each other still seems to be a strange move ...

Companies such as 3 D Systems , can not afford to stop there - they must constantly go forward. What we have in this case. The new line of their devices is definitely more effective and offers advantages that all those who have long ago dreamed of setting up a serial production of highly detailed products have long dreamed of.
