Hurricane Electric: why we entrusted our equipment to this company



Our company has been working in the field of hosting for many years. Our technological platforms are in the USA, the Netherlands and Russia. In these countries, we cooperate with a number of major telecommunications companies. One of them is Hurricane Electric. Choosing partners in the US, we wanted to get the maximum protection for our equipment so that at the most inappropriate moment it would not fail all at once for some unknown reason (frankly, there could be no suitable time for such cases).

The choice was quite large, but we decided to start working with Hurricane Electric, and, in general, did not regret it even once. The fact is that the company is one of the largest in the world. The range of services Hurricane Electric includes colocation, and web hosting, and rental of dedicated servers with tunneling and IP-transit. Why did we decide to trust this company? Let's try to explain.

The fact is that the colocation service of this company is very developed. This is facilitated by a number of points, including a deployed backbone infrastructure: the company has its own backbone network of optical channels in North America, Europe and Asia. On the plus side, there are a large number of points of presence, with the ability to connect IP transit services. There are connections with 100 main exchange points, as well as traffic exchange with 4000 large networks. Critical sections of the network infrastructure are duplicated. Well, plus high-speed Internet ports with the ability to move to additional bandwidth.

Equipment placement




With the company you can work with almost any amount of equipment. You can place both a rack and a whole data center. Each year, the usable area increases, so that the equipment fits more and more. And you can place at least one server, at least ten thousand. If necessary, Hurrican Electric specialists can change the configuration of the equipment being placed, or the client can perform this operation.



Protection of server racks is respectable - in addition to monitoring systems, it is possible to lock both rack doors with a key. Regarding the size of the racks, there are the usual racks, with a depth of 30.25 inches, and deep ones, with a depth of 37.5 inches.
