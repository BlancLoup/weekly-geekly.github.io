Keyboard with magnetic cushion keys

Without lengthy introductions and unnecessary words, I propose to evaluate a kind of invention of the Korean company Embotec - the so-called magnetic levitating keyboard, which in the original is called the Magnetic Levitation Keyboard.

The principle of operation of each key, I think, is obvious and does not require unnecessary explanations. I can only say one thing: in my opinion, the idea is original and worthy of at least further development. Interestingly, the know-how can be implemented in the keyboard of any type, whether ordinary or so-called notebook type. The head of the company Embotec claims that the keys on the magnetic cushion will be comfortable enough for typing, and for games.


The first commercial samples of new keyboards will go on sale this year.
