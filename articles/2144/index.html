Attendance at American online casinos has decreased

Attendance of gambling resources on the Internet has declined significantly after the president of the United States George Bush on October 13 signed a law banning online games. This is evidenced by the results of a recent study conducted by Nielsen / NetRatings .

Total attendance of the most popular gambling resources, including Sportsbook.com, PartyPoker.com and Ultimatebet.com, decreased by 56% over the past October, writes AP .

The law forced the owners of many gaming sites to cease the activities of their services or to transfer business to offshore zones.
