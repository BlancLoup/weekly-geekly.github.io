Introduction




In these videos, we will talk with those who promote additive technologies - manufacturers of equipment and materials and consumers of their products, with those who use digital technologies in production and with amateur makers, discuss successes and consider individual application cases.

In addition, we have a service department that deals with commissioning, repair, training and technical support.



In our Moscow showroom, we meet clients, demonstrate to them the possibilities of digital production, advise and solve specific production problems using digital processes.







On the second floor of the office are the sales and logistics departments, administrative and support staff.

Production


The concepts of ‚Äúadditive manufacturing‚Äù and ‚Äú3D printing‚Äù are quite narrow; they do not include many related and related processes, such as modeling, operation of robotics and CNC machines, casting metals using 3D printing forms and master models, 3D scanning, all sorts of software from this area and much more. In my vlog I will call this, collectively, the term ‚Äú Digital Production ‚Äù.

Digital production is a consequence of global digitalization, digital automation of production and related processes; it is growing at a rapid pace throughout the world, including in Russia. Despite the fact that hype and hype around additive technologies that have so raged in the infosphere over the past few years have waned, digital production itself does not even think to slow down.



The development of additive devices in the user segment, for home use by completely unprepared users, did not meet the expectations placed on it - too much was expected from it and the start was too briskly, the technologies are not yet ready for this, but this is a matter for the near future.

Digital production for professionals, on the contrary, continues to develop at an accelerated pace, which the Formnext 2017 exhibition at the end of last year, which grew by a third from the previous one and showed a huge amount of interesting new industrial equipment and integrated solutions, hinted unambiguously.
