How to manage IT-Schnick without competence in this area?

Project manager: you can not be a mega-expert in everything, but you must be a universal. We will talk about this at the master class of Sergey Boronin. After all, hiring good IT professionals does not guarantee a bright future for the project. It is required to organize work, introduce risk management and keep participants motivated until the project is successfully completed.

March 1, Sergey will hold a master class focused on startups, and will try to explain to them without unnecessary complications how to improve their competencies in the IT field. Boronin has a decade of experience in project management and IT-background, so he can tell you about the difficulties that project managers face, the principles of setting and controlling tasks, how to take work in stages, and put the project into operation.

Registration is required and is here . The master class will be held on March 1, 2011 from 19:00 to 22:00 on the campus of the Russian Academy of National Economy and Public Administration under the President of the Russian Federation at the address: Yugo-Zapadnaya, Vernadsky Avenue, 82, bld. 1. For non-residents of the incubator participation is paid, the cost of participation is 1250 rubles.
