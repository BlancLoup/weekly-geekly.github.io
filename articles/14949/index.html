Teamer and his team

Ateve Commercial presents a new project - Teamer.ru . With it, the organization of teamwork will be much easier - both for companies and for teams of freelancers.

The project is intended for use by small groups of people who solve certain problems. When creating it, we focused primarily on working with Internet projects.

People are grouped into projects and set tasks for each other (tickets). In projects there are managers (they see all the tasks created within the project) and executors (they see only those tasks that relate directly to them). All these people can write messages (comments) as part of tasks and attach files to them (screenshots, text documents, etc.).
