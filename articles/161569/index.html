GTA: Vice City came to iOS and Android

In addition to the already released GTA III, Rockstar released the port of its famous game GTA: Vice City for mobile devices on iOS and Android.



In Russian AppStore the game is already available.
UPD: a link appeared on Google Play . For her say thanks ArsNG .
UPD2: game removed from Google Play. Apparently, he will return as soon as they fix the problems with loading the cache and with the support of all the declared devices.
