Route Widget



On the website of almost any company and any office, institute, restaurant or agency there is a page with the address and map. The bus widget allows site owners to provide their customers with a new service: the ability to get directions on public transport to any office of the company using a personal search form.

Installing the widget is very simple:
1. You need to get the widget code and place it on your website.
2. The site user enters his address as a point of departure.
3. Then selects one of the company‚Äôs addresses as a destination.
4. And presses the "Find Route" button.

As a result, the user receives several travel options, accompanied by detailed route statistics and online traffic information.

Everything is very simple and convenient!

If you wish, you can create a widget without a CSS design in order to be able to precisely adjust its appearance to the design of a specific page.

Site developers invite everyone to rate the widget and will be grateful for any suggestions.
