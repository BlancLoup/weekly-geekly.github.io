Yahoo Mail beta - now with chat

Yahoo has kept its promise made in November last year, and announced the introduction of chat in the new interface of Yahoo! Mail - the most popular email service in the world.

The emergence of chat, allowing you to chat with other Yahoo! Messenger directly on the pages of the email service is one of the final stages in testing its new version. Officially, this and all other features will be presented in the coming months. Among them - a convenient AJAX-interface, tabs for viewing messages, dragging messages into folders, working with RSS, etc.
