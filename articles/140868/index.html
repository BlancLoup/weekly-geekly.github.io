HTTP notifications in Yandex.Money - now for p2p transfers

The long-awaited functionality for recipients of p2p transfers is now available to all users of the system. Now, not only your mobile phone or your mailbox can learn about each incoming payment, but also your own software.

Notification of incoming p2p can be configured directly from your wallet at https://sp-money.yandex.ru/myservices/online.xml . Specify the URL to send notifications, copy the sha1-secret and enable any automatic service associated with the receipt of payment.

In the notification parameters, you will receive the amount, date, time and number of the sender's account, and with an additional automatic request you will be able to load comments entered at the time of sending the payment.

More automatic services, good and different!
