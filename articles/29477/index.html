Rusavtobus in your city!



Today, the Rusavtobus urban public transport route search service operates in Moscow , St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg . If you want to create an interactive search service "Routes of the city" in your city - write to us at info@rusavtobus.ru. We will tell you what is needed for this, and provide all the necessary data formats and a convenient admin panel for filling and updating the route database.

Besides the fact that you will create a useful service for your city, you will have the opportunity to receive income thanks to it. Write to us and we will consider your offer!

To create a service in your city, it is necessary that on Google Maps be its map with the accuracy of house numbers. It is possible to use other maps, but you must have your own interface for working with them, allowing you to move around the map with the mouse, zoom in and out, and also apply markers to it.

By the way, you can create new services not only in Russia, but also in any other countries where there are detailed city maps.
