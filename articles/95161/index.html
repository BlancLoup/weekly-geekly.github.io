The battle for the prize 80 000 dollars

Registration of participants of the Automated Trading Championship 2010 has begun today!



This is the fourth automatic trading championship. Within three months, the top three strongest developers of mechanical trading systems will be revealed. The evaluation criterion is absolute profit. The winners will share the prize fund among themselves - 80,000 US dollars!

The championship will begin on October 4th and end on December 24th, 2010. The main purpose of the meeting is to popularize the automatic trading and programming language MetaQuotes Language 5 (MQL5). In the course of the Championship, we will have the opportunity to evaluate what progress the expert writers have achieved and where the limit of achievements in automated trading is today.

As in the three previous Championships - 2006 , 2007 and 2008 , during this competition, hundreds of different experts will compete with each other. You will see how different trading systems behave in real market conditions and which of these trading strategies and tactics are effective.

We invite everyone to register and participate in the competition: participation is free !
