Photos from the KAI Engineering Festival in Kazan

Navigator Campus participates in various events and supports any movement that meets our goals. Today we took part in the opening of the All-Russian Engineering Festival, which organizes a serious technical university of Kazan, under the cut of photos and a story about the exhibition of the Festival.


Engineering Festival is a series of events from September 2, 2014 to April 30, 2015, united for the consistent promotion of engineering professions among schoolchildren and their mentors: teachers, parents. This year it is planned to hold everything mainly on the basis of the Republic of Tatarstan, and then expand the movement throughout Russia.
