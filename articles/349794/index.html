Magento Meetup Kharkov - Videos and Presentations

January 26 in Kharkov was held mitap dedicated to the platform Magento. This is the second such event in the city, and it is gaining momentum. The event was attended by 130 people: developers, testers, designers.

There were three reports on testing, AMP technology and Magento 2 customization. We want to share video and presentations with you. Anyone interested in the platform will find them useful.



Video


Igor Minyaylo - Automated testing in Magento 2 .

Olga Burtyka - Accelerated Mobile Pages in Magento .

Vlad Veselov - Typical mistakes in customizing Magento 2, and how to avoid them .



Presentations


Igor Minyaylo - Automated testing in Magento 2 .

Olga Burtyka - Accelerated Mobile Pages in Magento .

Vlad Veselov - Typical mistakes when customizing Magento 2 .



Mitap is not over. On January 27, Magento Contribution Day took place, where those who wished developed the Multi-Source Inventory. Architects from the Magento Core Team Igor Minyaylo, Valery Naida and Vladimir Kublitsky helped hold the event. On this day in Kharkiv, we created 6 Pull Requests in the Magento 2 repository.



The next mitap is scheduled for mid-April. Event information will appear on the Magecom page.
