From July 1 - duty-free import of goods up to 1000 euros (it was: 5-10 thousand rubles)

C on July 1 in Russia and Kazakhstan comes into force the " Agreement on the procedure for the movement by individuals of goods for personal use across the customs border of the customs union and the performance of customs operations related to their release."

For ordinary mortals, this means the ability to freely import purchases of up to 1000 euros from your favorite online stores abroad, without having to pay a 30% tax, and go through a complicated customs clearance procedure (however, the parcel can get stuck at customs even if you do not need to pay tax) . This innovation can radically affect the technology market, because The cost of buying on Amazon with delivery for most goods may be 2 times cheaper than buying in Russia (even when using fast delivery). For purchases with delivery only within the United States, you can use the redirection services. For example, MYUS.COM .

Be careful! Most customs and courier services are not yet up to date on innovations, so it makes sense to wait some time before running to buy the long-awaited MacBook for 32,000 rubles ($ 999 + shipping via myus) instead of 47,000 in re: Store.
