Office Design SCHOOL # 1



The Moscow office of the English school SCHOOL # 1 is located on the attic floor of the old mansion on Pyatnitskaya.

The author of the corporate style and interior of the school - Alex Mikhailov (Alex Mikhaylov) - designer, author of identity and video for Moscow Eurovision.



















SCHOOL # 1 is different from the traditional image of the school. Its creators wanted to get rid of the stereotypes associated with language courses as much as possible and make a club of interests rather.

Conducting various thematic educational activities in the office of SCHOOL # 1 constantly justifies the functionality of the interior, and also proves that interesting design in many respects contributes to the assimilation of knowledge.

Periodically, the school organizes screenings of films in English, which take place in the lobby: the screen is set, and sometimes the image is simply projected onto the wall. The audience is located on the couch and on the armchairs-pears.

On the walls of the school, instead of the usual symbols of language courses (Big Ben and the alphabet) quotes in English by Muhammad Ali, a half-liter mouse, Ozzy Osborne and other famous characters.

Classes are held in classrooms with brick walls, skylights, colorful chairs and glass on an easel instead of a flipchart.
