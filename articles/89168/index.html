NaviBot


The vacuum cleaner will definitely become interesting to customers who are actively responding to everything new and unusual. From its features, it is possible to distinguish such moments as a touch screen, 38 sensors that allow you to create a virtual picture of an apartment by photographing an apartment with a frequency of up to 30 frames per second, as well as the possibility of designating restricted areas on the apartment map.
