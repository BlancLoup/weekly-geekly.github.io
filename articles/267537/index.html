Registration for the SQLSaturday conference on October 17 is open

PASS and Microsoft are pleased to invite you to the SQLSaturday technology conference, which will be held in Moscow on Saturday, October 17, 2015, from 10.00 to 18.00 at the Golden Ring Hotel at 5, Smolenskaya Street.



Why ?

You will have a unique chance to listen to nearly two dozen reports on key data management platform topics from recognized industry experts, including:
  • Features of the practical use of In-Memory OLTP in SQL Server 2014.
  • Examples of using AlwaysOn to ensure the resiliency of MS applications.
  • Principles and methodology for consolidating MS SQL Server database servers in the enterprise.
  • Analysis of the performance of Windows applications on the example of SQL Server .


A separate set of reports will be devoted to classical and advanced business intelligence, including:

  • Mobile business intelligence: Datazen and Power BI .
  • An overview of advanced analytics usage scenarios ( Azure ML ).
  • SSAS Profiling with Extended Events ( XEvents ).

And, of course, we will lift the veil over SQL Server 2016 , introducing its revolutionary technologies that set new standards in the field of data storage, processing and visualization.

Please note that several reports will be held in English.

By taking part in the event, you can exchange views with industry colleagues and chat with leading experts who will be ready to answer any questions you may have.

Additional information and the full program are available on the event website .

On the day of the conference for those who can not take part in it personally, the site will be organized online broadcast .

For whom?

On SQLSaturday, it will be interesting and useful to all IT professionals facing the data management platform in their work. Do not miss the event if you:
  • database administrator;
  • IT specialist responsible for the database in the organization;
  • architect, analyst, developer and administrator-BI solutions;
  • software developer.
