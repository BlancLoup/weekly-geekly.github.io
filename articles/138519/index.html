World of Tanks - something that is usually silent

World of Tanks or, for short, WOT is one of the most popular online games of the last year in the CIS. Go to their website and there you can see the inscription in big letters: play for free. And, indeed, for registration, client download and online battle with you will not require a penny. And at first, you will probably even be delighted with such a simple, interesting and free game. But, as soon as you get involved, then comes the understanding that free cheese is only in a mousetrap. Who cares - read on about what is usually silent in connection with WOT.


So, who knows what WOT - can skip the first couple of paragraphs, for the rest I will explain. World of Tanks - online, i.e. You can play only with a permanent Internet connection, the game is an arcade tank simulator. Developers of the game is positioned as an MMO-action game with elements of role-playing, shooter and strategy. You need to download and install an online client about 2.6 GB in size, register on the site by entering an email, username and password. Then, when you start the game client, you must enter the e-mail and password for authorization.

It is currently possible to play for five nations: USSR, Germany, France, USA, China. Moreover, the division into nations refers only to the crews, in combat in one team, participation of tanks of any kind is possible, the division occurs only on the level of tanks, but it will not work to transplant the French crew to a US tank. Two teams of 15 people each play on one of the cards chosen randomly. (This all refers to the most common, so-called random mode, there are still clan battles, training mode, etc., but we will not talk about them). Tanks are divided into classes, at the moment - from 1 to 10. Accordingly, tanks of level 10 are the most powerful. Beginners are given a default of 4 tanks, one each from the USSR, USA, Germany, France, all level 1. Choose a tank, press the "To Battle" button - and you are randomly assigned to one or another team. Teams are formed depending on the level of the tank, beginners of the first level will never have to fight with tanks of the 10th, except for the battle mode in the platoon, but we will not talk about this either. Management, gameplay - everything is simple to ugliness - perhaps even a young schoolchild can play, which also attracts beginners and attracts a large audience of schoolchildren. Forward, backward, left, right, shot - this is enough to play.

Tanks need to "pump". And here begins the most interesting. Depending on the outcome of the battle, the tank gets a certain amount of experience and credits. Credits - game currency. Accrue experience and credits for various successful actions, but mainly for causing damage to other tanks. There are two types of experience: personal, for a specific tank and free. Personal experience of the tank can only be used to study the modules of the tank, free - to study the modules of any tank. Where does free experience come from? This is 5% of the "deductions" from the earned experience of each tank, plus you can transfer to a free extra personal tank experience, but ... for money. No, not for game loans, but for real money.
