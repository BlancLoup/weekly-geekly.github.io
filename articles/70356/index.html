Google SketchUp 7.1


Google updated 3d modeling software to version 7.1. Among the changes:

- Support for large models
- Improved import / export
- Improved modeling tools from / using Google Street View photos
- Support for dimensions of models in LayOut

In addition, Product Ideas is open for Google SketchUp, where you can now leave your wishes to the product or vote for other ideas for improvement.
