Form Analytics - new Yandex.Metrics tool

Last night in Metric there was an opportunity to analyze forms for filling on the site. A full analysis can be carried out for any form - registration, ordering or sending an application - and understand what can be improved and how to increase conversion rates.

The tool allows you to find out how many visitors reached the page with the form, how many of them left their data and, most interestingly, how many of them sent their data.



The Form Analysis report displays the time users spend filling out a form, shows which form fields are left blank and which field prompts you to leave the form and leave the page.
