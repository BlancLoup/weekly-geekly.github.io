Selgascano office in the forest


Spanish architects Jose Selgas and Lucia Cano designed and built the office of their company Selgascano . The office is located in a forest in the suburbs of Madrid and is a tunnel, half of which is made of 20-millimeter acrylic glass. The mechanism, installed at one end of the tunnel, periodically opens the flap, through which fresh air passes.
