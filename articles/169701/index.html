KDE Plasma Active launched on Nexus 7

If you are interested in the topic of Ubuntu Phone, then you just have to know about all the features of the Linux-community!

Ruediger Gad - Linux developer - published a video (under the cut), which demonstrates the work of KDE Plasma Active on the Nexus 7. Now you probably thought another Linux, once again launched, but slows down terribly and nothing works ... But no! It demonstrates 3D accelerator support in the glmark2-es2 parrot test, hardware video acceleration on the example of 1080p video, playability in mario in the emulator, support for Bluetooth, QZeeControl as a wireless manipulator.

Plasma Active is a KDE project that provides a modular UX platform for devices of various form factors (tablets, smartphones, transformers, etc.).
