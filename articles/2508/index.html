iRobot turned the vacuum cleaner into a real robot

The smart vacuum cleaner Roomba, which brought fame to iRobot, became even smarter and turned into a real robot, writes Slashdot . The name has changed - now this electronic machine is called Create .



In Create, you can download programs written in C or C ++ (those who are not able to program will have to be content with ten built-in ones), it is equipped with various kinds of sensors, and new devices can be created on its basis - everything depends on the owner's imagination.
