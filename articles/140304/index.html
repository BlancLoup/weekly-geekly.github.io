Work with search queries


The difference between a Twitter search is that an information flow is often searched for on a given topic, and not an answer to a specific question. When the stream is found, the request can not be changed for a long time and just follow the information flow. Extradition is limited to five days from history (restriction introduced recently, I think, because of the high load). It is easier for me to tell the basic techniques using specific examples of search queries.

  • rizzoma OR rizzomacom

On Twitter, you can quickly track what they write about your service. Often users come up with direct questions. We have the name of the service itself, Rizzoma , different from the name of the Twitter account: @rizzomacom. Therefore, the request has both names.

  • #confluence OR wrike OR streamwork OR "cisco quad"

My work is closely connected with the tools of the collaboration. This request allows you to monitor what people are saying about some services that are interesting to me in a given period of time. I use #hashtag very rarely in requests: recently it was captured by vile spammers or the so-called SocialMediaMarket-lolog (SMM-sci). Here is the latest article about this issue (in English).
