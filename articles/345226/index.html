10 RecSys materials on recommendation systems that everyone should read

Hi, Habr! Today we want to share with the community a selection of the best materials of the ACM Recommender Systems (RecSys) conference, which every specialist who works in recommender systems must read.



ACM RecSys is the main international conference where new results of research, methods and technologies are presented annually and trends in the field of recommender systems are defined. Since RecSys brings together major international research groups working on recommendation systems with leading e-commerce companies worldwide, it has become the most important annual conference for the presentation and discussion of recommendations systems research.

Since the first conference in 2007, a huge amount of knowledge, research, reports and books has been accumulated, and 10 best materials in the history of ACM RecSys were recently selected.

The top five are top rated articles of the ACM digital library. ACM Digital Library is a research and networking platform that contains full-text collections of all ACM publications, including conference materials, technical journals, books, etc., a collection of selected full-text publications from selected authors, ACM Guide to Computing Literature - a comprehensive bibliographic database data, focused exclusively on computing, as well as an interrelated set of links between authors, papers, organizations, and specialized communities.

Of the 925 articles published in the library, these 5 account for more than 12% of citations of the RecSys!

  • Performance tasks recommender algorithms on top-n recommendation tasks , 2010. Authors: Paolo Cremonesi , Yehuda Koren , Roberto Turrin
  • Trust-aware recommender systems , 2007. Authors: Paolo Massa , Paolo Avesani
  • A matrix factorization technique with social networks , 2010. Authors: Mohsen Jamali , Martin Ester
  • Multiverse recommendation: n-dimensional tensor factorization for context-aware collaborative filtering , 2010. Authors: Alexandros Karatzoglou , Xavier Amatriain , Linas Baltrunas , Nuria Oliver
  • Hidden rating and understanding topics dimensions 2013, 2013. Authors: Julian McAuley , Jure Leskovec

The second five included the best reports for the last five years. Since 2009, the reports that make the most significant contribution to the annual ACM RecSys conference have been awarded with special awards, and the following list of the top 5 is the winners of the RecSys Best Paper Awards of the last five years. The full list of winners is published on the conference website .

  • Modeling the Assimilation of Contrast Effects in Online Product Rating Systems: 2017. Authors: X. Zhang, J. Zhao, JCS Lui
  • Local Item Item Item Models for Top-N Recommendation , 2016. By: E. Christakopoulou and G. Karypis
  • Context-Aware Event Recommendation in Event-based Social Networks , 2015. Authors: A. Macedo, L. Marinho and R. Santos
  • Beyond Clicks: Dwell Time for Personalization , 2014. Authors: X. Yi, L. Hong, E. Zhong, N. Nan Liu and S. Rajan
  • A Fast Parallel SGD for Matrix Factorization in Shared Memory Systems , 2013. Authors: Y. Zhuang, W. Chin, Y. Juan and C. Lin (Best Paper)

Enjoy reading!
