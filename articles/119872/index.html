SQA Days 9 Conference Continues Online

Especially for those who could not get to the SQA Days 9 conference, which was held on April 22-23, 2011 in Kazan, or could not attend any of the reports that they really wanted to listen to, or did not have time to ask the speakers, to express objections , add your own ideas or experience - the conference continues online!

The online sequel is:
  • access to conference materials (audio, video, slide shows),
  • opportunity to communicate with the speakers online,
  • valuable prizes and gifts from IT-CONF, Testing Software- Testing.Ru portal, and other partners of SQA Days.
How will it go:

The forum SQA Days 9 online is available on the forum of the Software-Testing.Ru testers portal. Each topic of the thread refers to a specific report of the last conference, and contains a brief description, presentation and audio / video materials. The message of each topic is transmitted to the appropriate speaker. Starting from today and for two weeks, almost all the speakers, as much as they can, will appear on the forum and answer your questions (and we will encourage them to do so :)). Participants are invited not only to familiarize themselves with the conference materials and to communicate with the speakers, but also to share their insights on certain / similar topics - interesting conclusions, articles, their experience, links to discussions on similar topics on another resource, etc. etc.

As at the conference held in Kazan, during the online continuation, we intend to hold a series of contests, and we are preparing gifts for the winners and active participants. All news about contests, prizes, content updates and other organizational issues will be published in the news topic of the online continuation of the SQA Days 9 conference .

PS The conference materials were collected and prepared for publication by Felix Zinatullin , for which he was very grateful!
