Microsoft Useful Resources for IT Professionals

Greetings, colleagues!

For me, IT professionals are the main and most valuable audience, people with whom and for whom I work every day. In the end, the meaning of what I do is to make the life of IT people a little better. For example, providing interesting and useful information. Our small team is constantly working to ensure that such information is always at your fingertips.

Over the past six months, we have had several interesting events, the materials of which are available online at any time:
  1. IT Conference: Cloud in Russia . 18th of Febuary
  2. JumpStart: Manage corporate mobile devices . 26 March
  3. JumpStart: Monitoring: from server to application. System Center Operations Manager 2012 R2 . 14th of April
  4. Microsoft TechDay: Windows Server vNext . June 10th

We are sure that recently passed Jump Start on Windows 10 will be of no less demand.

We continue to develop the Microsoft Virtual Academy (MVA) free learning portal, below are the five most popular courses of the past few months:
  • Windows 10: deployment, management, security
  • Hyper-V Basics in Windows Server 2012 R2
  • Computer Security Basics
  • Basics of building a domain network
  • Basics of building a domain network. Part 2

And, of course, do not forget about other resources targeted at technical specialists:
  • The main portal for IT specialists TechNet.ru and the TechNet Forum , where you can find all the most useful and relevant information about Microsoft technology, as well as ask an expert.
  • TechNet Newsletter . By subscribing to our newsletter, you can always receive the most interesting news in a convenient format.
  • Microsoft TechNet Download Center . Here you can download a trial version of the software for free.
  • Video Recorders on Channel 9 - the largest library of video technology for Microsoft, where you can find the answer to almost any question that interests you.
  • The Events section on TechNet , thanks to which you will always be up to date on upcoming Microsoft events.

We are open to comments, suggestions, criticism and simple communication. Perhaps the easiest way to contact us is social networks and Twitter:
  • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/technetru
  • Vkontakte: https://vk.com/technetru
  • Twitter: https://twitter.com/technetru

We constantly conduct various polls to take into account your opinion. Every six months, we do the largest survey, collecting your feedback on all our work and all our solutions, so in the coming days you may receive an email from feedback@e-mail.microsoft.com. For our team, this is not a simple formality. Together with our colleagues, we really study the information collected, and it is the main assessment of our work and the basis for our future plans.

We really appreciate your opinion about Microsoft.

Respectfully,

strategic technology expert
