Launched the Russian version of MSN Video

On Thursday, the promised launch of a local Russian-language version of the MSN Video video portal took place. Unlike the exclusively amateur Soapbox of the same Microsoft and mixed YouTube, MSN Video shows its viewers only the professional content of their own and third-party production: concerts, news programs, entertainment programs, etc. The picture quality is higher than on YouTube.

The official launch of the Video will take place only on Monday, and at the moment the resource is still being filled with content. Already today on the main page of the site you can see videos of the national television company TNT (of course, the omnipresent Comedy Club) and the RIA Novosti news agency in Russian. In addition, there are live recordings and fashion shows in English.

The service is free from advertising. On his "big brother" advertising is placed directly in the videos themselves before they start.
