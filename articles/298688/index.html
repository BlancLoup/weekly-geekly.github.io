Google hires experts in the project of an unmanned vehicle



In the near future, unmanned vehicles may take a significant share of the market, and while they are being tested by some major technology giants. If Tesla has already released some analogue of this functionality for the latest generation of cars, then Google is only developing its car.

To develop such a project, a large number of specialized employees are required - Google recently opened a recruitment of 36 specialists to the Google X project. Among the open vacancies, there are open for engineers related to the development of robotization systems, new sensors and other related elements.

The list of job vacancies includes a description for employees of marketing, use policy analysis from the point of view of legislative practice. Such a number of open vacancies may indicate that Google is actively beginning to develop this area.
