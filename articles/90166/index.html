How to make money on vending machines: VendExpo 2010

In continuation of the cycle of publications on the topic of vending in Russia - some beautiful photo cards from the exhibition of vending new products.

Vending coffee, snacks, or even shoe covers are already more or less familiar to us and are already found almost everywhere, so those who want to occupy a vacant niche for now may be interested in:
  • automatic machine that produces popcorn and cotton candy
  • lunch machines
  • Hot Dogs

And:
  • upgrade to payment terminals (with books and disks)
  • automatic machine with shoe covers that put them right on their feet
  • juicer machine

All pictures and a story about the exhibition - under the cut, well.

But for starters - a small lyrical digression about the meaning of such events and the article itself.

As the exhibition showed, the interest in the vending business is huge and only grows: there were a lot of people at the exhibition, deputies came, and people who want to do new business. And the organizers of VendExpo 2010 have fulfilled this task well: on a business program open for free to visit, almost all significant issues were discussed: taxes, law, laws, business development.

Popularizing such events is one of the keys to the success of the vending business in Russia - and, perhaps, your success in it.

Well, now the actual pictures from the exhibition.

Upgrade to payment terminals - books and CDs


Let's start with the upgrade to the payment terminals. People use such terminals often, a lot and with particular enthusiasm. But who can resist the temptation to do two things at once - to put money on your cell phone and buy an interesting magazine or CD?

The vending machine connects to the payment terminal, after which a program is installed on the terminal, which adds a product selection function to the payment acceptance function. In the machine you can put disks, flash drives, magazines and similar utility - up to 384 pieces.

Fresh cotton candy


In March of this year, a candy wadding machine called Cotton Candy Factory became a hit at the Las Vegas entertainment show. While fresh cotton candy is being prepared in the bowels of the machine, Cotton Candy Factory entertains the customer. One filling of the vending machine will provide people with about three hundred batches of cotton wool - the operator only needs to pour water in time, add sugar and lay chopsticks.

The Russian company "Domino" went further - the developers have created a machine for the preparation of cotton candy with a much larger volume of containers for ingredients. Only one sugar in the domestic machine will be able to fall asleep 8 kg - against 5.5 kg at the Taiwanese Cotton Candy Factory, represented in the United States.

While the machine is preparing fresh cotton candy, the client listens to music. Musical accompaniment selects the owner of the machine - which is especially nice.
