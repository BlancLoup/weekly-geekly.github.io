True, there is nothing on the company's website, except for two screenshots and a list of publications . However, the February alpha version of the program was already demonstrated at the Adobe Engage Event conference.

According to the director of the company Virtual Ubiquity, the use of the Flex 2.0 platform has allowed developers to significantly improve the functionality and appearance of the program compared to competitive developments from Google and Zoho.
