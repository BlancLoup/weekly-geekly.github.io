Passport scan is required: a sudden check of customs officers stopped some of the parcels from abroad

If during the holidays you felt anxiety in the Force, problems with the delivery of parcels from abroad, then you may have come under a large-scale check of the Federal Customs Service (FCS), which decided to make sure that all logistics companies have at their disposal scans of their clients' passports. It was especially hard for the customers of Boxberry and SPSR-Express, which even had to suspend delivery from some online stores.


No passport scan anywhere

Good news right away: according to Medusa , on January 7, the check ended and the FCS stopped requesting additional documents from companies.

What was it


It turns out that according to the rules of the Federal Customs Service, when ordering a parcel from abroad, you must provide the carrier with scans of passport pages with basic data and registration. At least, that is what they meant by "information about the identity document of the recipient of the goods." This interpretation was not obvious to all carriers, therefore some companies asked for scans of only the first turn of the passport or were limited to a series and number. And when the January inspection of the FCS began, they had to urgently request from their clients scans of passports or even cancel delivery.
