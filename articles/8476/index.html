Motorola introduced Razr2

And here is the continuation of the model range of one of the most popular mobile phones. I see them everywhere and in the hands of all kinds of people. It seems that I will see soon more often ...

Motorola has announced a new brand Razr2. So far, it includes only three folding beds: Razr2 V8 for GSM networks, V9m and V9 for CDMA and 3G. The thickness of the phones has decreased by a couple of millimeters, and the size of the external screen has increased. The output of the phones is scheduled for July 2007.



Without exaggeration, the new phones have absorbed the best from Razr. The frame is made of galvanized steel frame, which ensures high resistance of the phone to wear. The front side is covered with a metal layer, and the back - pleasant to the touch rubberized material. The phones use an external screen with a diagonal of 2 inches - this is the largest screen in this class. The resolution of the internal screen is doubled compared to the Razr.

Model Razr2 V8 has a completely redesigned user interface. Changed the phone book - now it excludes entries with the same name, and each of the contacts may have several numbers. The phone automatically recognizes the text message that the user enters, and sends it in the appropriate format. The main menu can now be configured so that any function can be reached in a maximum of 3 clicks. Razr2 phones use a new, faster processor ARM-11, which provides 10 times faster performance than previous models.



A 2-megapixel camera with eight-frame sequential shooting mode and the choice of the best is built into all telephones (similar to BestPic in the Sony Ericsson Cyber-Shot). Depending on the configuration, the phones may have up to 2 GB of internal memory.

Despite the fact that new models will be released for various networks and sold in many countries around the world to ordinary customers and in the corporate segment, according to analysts, the new brand is not enough to compensate for the financial losses that the company announced in the first quarter of 2007. Experts doubt that Razr2 phones will be able to compete with products such as the Nokia N-series and the iPhone.
