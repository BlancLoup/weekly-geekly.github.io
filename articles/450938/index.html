Linux Install Fest 05.19 in Nizhny Novgorod May 18, 2019

Linux Install Fest 05.19 will be held in Nizhny Novgorod on May 18, 2019.

The event is held NNLUG on the basis of the Nizhny Novgorod Radio Engineering College.

Today, with an accessible Internet, it is not difficult to download this or that Linux distribution kit or find the answer to most of the questions arising during the work under this OS.

However, the format of public meetings remains popular among open-source supporters.

On Linux Install Fest, a series of reports are planned at various levels of complexity and on a variety of topics: continued interest in Blender, gaming solutions, and daily workflow cases.
Details can be seen on the event page .

Those who wish to make a report are invited to join (adding topics to the reports is available on the website nnlug.ru).

Time 10:00 - 15:00
Location: N.Novgorod, st. Student, 6 (NRTK), Assembly Hall.
Questions and suggestions can be voiced in t.me/nnlug
