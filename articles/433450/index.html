Grow and teach. How we made friends with PEGA

The group of companies LANIT employs more than ten thousand people. It seems that in such a team there is always a suitable specialist. However, every year more and more new products, methods, solutions appear on the market. IT professionals are interested in new products in the industry, but it takes time and money to study them. Therefore, companies are faced with a situation where there is a promising technology, solution or platform, but there are no specialists who could use them.

From this article, you will learn how the LANIT-BP team was able to master the platform, which at that time had just appeared on the Russian market. The developers of the company will tell you what was the most difficult thing when meeting a new technology and share their experience on how best to proceed to work with an unfamiliar development.

How it all began


Eight years ago, the LANIT team was solving a difficult task - we agreed with PEGA that LANIT would implement its BPM solutions in Russia (more details about the methodology for managing business processes and about PEGA were already written in Habr√©).

Until 2010, a department of integrated business solutions operated in LANIT, employing about 60 people. We implemented Java projects in telecom and banking. In the same year, we learned about the American company PEGA and its BPM system . Business processes are rapidly turning into a "figure." At some point, they began to be perceived as a value that can be transformed and improved, and thus affect the business: for example, to accelerate it. At that time in Russia, the BPM direction was little known, and LANIT decided to deal with it closely.

So, in 2011, we started working with the platform and got a new name - ‚Äú LANIT - BPM ‚Äù. The company was the first to introduce BPM products from PEGA in the country. There were no specialists who understood this decision, neither we nor other players in the Russian market. It was necessary to find people to whom this technology would be interesting. The company turned 16 enthusiasts. They were accomplished professionals, but at some point they wanted to try themselves in a new direction and believed in a new product.

Training


At that time there was not a single Russian-language resource where PEGA and BPM were accessible and understandable. The PEGA Academy (training portal) was already operational, but it was also completely English speaking. Employees themselves were engaged in adapting and translating manuals. Then a company representative came to us and gave lectures; also our student specialists traveled to the vendor‚Äôs representative office in London. Americans developed their own certification and prepared customers for it with the help of paid courses. After a number of LANIT specialists passed the classic PEGA certification, we developed our own testing. Then we sent future professionals to PEGA certification when they passed our test. In the early years, it took place online, literally via Skype. Later, Network Academy LANIT obtained a partnership with a vendor, and the process began to go much easier.

The future PEGA masters mastered most of the materials themselves. Everyone was happy to learn quickly, but it was necessary to start the implementation as soon as possible, and this required even more personnel. So, a group of those who have already mastered the system, urgently took up campaigning and retraining of those interested in PEGA. The dive was already much faster: three weeks instead of two months. For 21 days, people took two 40-hour introductory courses: FastTrack and Bootcamp. They give an overview of the platform and work with it. Usually these courses are mastered with a trainer. Sometimes novice experts do it on their own, but it takes twice as long. Another week was given for running. There were moments when more experienced developers mastered the entire platform in a week. As a result, full-fledged PEGA specialists grew up in LANIT in a year or two. During this time, they managed not only to master the textbooks and pass the tests, but also to apply knowledge in real projects. More about this will tell later.

Training new specialists "LANIT - BPM"

Over the entire period of working with the system, more than 300 people came to learn how to work with PEGA, half of them brought the matter to the end and remained in the company. Everyone who started training had a different experience in IT. On average, the first specialists trained in two months.
