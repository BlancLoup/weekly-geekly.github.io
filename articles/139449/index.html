New life of your postal addresses in Rambler-Mail

Friends!

In June 2011, we began the rebranding of Rambler - we presented our new corporate identity and announced that we will gradually launch and restart our products in it.

At the same time, we initially emphasized: in the simple ‚Äúrepainting‚Äù of services we see no point. Rebranding for us is the creation of new quality products, and this task requires a lot of time.



In July, we restarted Rambler Maps, in February, Rambler-News, each time presenting a set of functions unique in the market in our products. Now it 's Rambler-Mail time . Very soon it will be restarted to the entire audience - which is to say, we are afraid. After all, it will be a new life of one of the oldest postal services runet.

What has changed and what appeared in our new mail?

- the email client interface has changed - now everything in it is very simple and this, like all good things, will probably quickly become addictive;

- at the mailbox appeared 2 types - in two and three columns. In the three-column mode, the ability to visually switch between letters is implemented - such a structure for working with mail saves time;



- Mail on "Rambler" has become a web client that supports multiple email addresses at the same time. That is, you can connect any mailboxes to your account on the "Rambler" and read all your mail in one window;

- in the new mail there are no extra features like news, games or special offers, but there will be easy to configure filters - an easy way to maintain order in the mailbox;

- updated address book;

- Thanks to the new technological platform, the speed of receiving, processing and sending messages will increase.
