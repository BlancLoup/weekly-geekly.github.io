Google+ is not for kids



If you try to enter the new social network of the search giant (which already has its own native Android application, and the iOS version will soon appear) and your age will be less than it should, the system will not let you in, even if you have invitation.
Could not sign you in to Google+. Google+.

By the way, from what age you can start using the service on the page is not written anywhere. One of the representatives of the company in the discussion thread answered this question, saying that while the minimum age at the time of testing will be 18 years old, but in the final version it may change, it will still clearly be at least 13-14 (thanks to CAJAX ). The age data is, of course, checked against the Google Accounts profile, and in particular YouTube (thanks andpey ), through which authorization takes place.

Of course, we understand that children can create a new account with false data about age, but most children would prefer to use Google+ with their old account without changing anything. And what, is it possible to just take and change the data on the age in the profile settings? No you can not. Google removed this field. In any case, Google+ does not replace Facebook and Vkontakte, which they have long been used to, and the loss will not be great.
