I have a blog dedicated to interfaces for iphone and ipad. This blog is not about new appstor, not about textures and glass buttons. I am interested in applications that change reality and help us to become better. Twitter, Foursquare, Dropbox, sports and food trackers, magazines and books on the iPad - more than a well-designed application.
