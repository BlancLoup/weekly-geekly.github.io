DynDNS will soon stop servicing its free dynamic DNS.


The world-famous company Dyn , the owner of such a popular dynamic DNS service as DynDNS, completely collapses its program of free third-level domain names. Disable free service is scheduled for May 7.

The CEO of the company in his blog reports that the product was originally developed for a small group of people who later grew into an exciting technology that could be used by millions of people and they are proud of it. But, unfortunately, they are forced to curtail the program of free dynamic DNS names in order to invest money in support, infrastructure and security system, which will improve the quality of service for existing customers on a paid basis.
They also offer a 25% discount on a paid counterpart to the previously provided free service, which can be arranged here using the FREE2VIP code

Let me remind you that since 2011, the company has ceased to register new free accounts, but has not prohibited old users from continuing to use their free dynamic DNS names, reducing their number from 3 to 2 per account and introducing compulsory confirmation of account activity every 30 days.
