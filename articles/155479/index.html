Jot Touch Stylus for iPad



- Who wants a stylus?
- I do!

Adonit recently updated its stylus line for iPad with the new Jot Touch, a touch -sensitive stylus.

How does he work



If you do not need sensitivity to pressure, you can do nothing and just draw them on the tablet. At the tip there is a so-called precision disc.
Data on pressing the stylus transmits over bluetooth, inside the battery, which lasts for 12 hours; The kit includes a usb charger.
In iOS, there is no support for styluses, so adonit made its API, with which applications can get the amount of pressure.

Specs


Length: 140.7 mm
Radius: 10.9 mm
Weight: 24 gr.
Levels of depression: 256
Battery life: 12 hours
Supported tablets: iPad 2, New iPad

Appearance



Stylus aluminum, a place for grabbing rubber. Color can be chosen or red, or metallic. It has three buttons: undo, on / off, redo - and a light bulb. The kit still has an aluminum cap, in the process of use, you can screw it on the back (as I did on the very first photo). The material of the charger is not distinguishable from the macbook:


Using


Precision disc



He can wipe, for this a spare is included in the kit, if necessary, you can buy more on the site. Comes the same as the tip of the classic stylus.
On the iPad, it's better to stick a film. In general, the stylus will not harm, but the glass can be scratched by small grains of sand. It is not recommended to use these films:
  • Zagg Glossy Invisible Shield (but Smudge-Proof Invisible Shield is possible)
  • Ghost armor
  • Bodyguardz
  • Skinomi
  • Wrapsol

Positioning accuracy is quite high. I thought that this disk would interfere, closing a part of the screen, but in fact it is not.

Battery


Stated that it lasts for 12 hours; in practice, at 8 ~ 10, but most likely depends on how to use. You cannot get it and charge it separately, only through the supplied usb-charger. Connect to the computer, insert the stylus into the charger, when it is charged, the light will turn green. To the charger the stylus is attached to the magnet. The magnet is strong, you can turn it as you please: it will not fall out.
The trouble may be that it is difficult to charge it from some models of keyboards with usb ports or from a system integrator: only via a usb extension cable.

Connection



To connect via bluetooth, you must enter the PIN code 0000, you can not change it. The connection does not fall, at least, I have never lost (in some forums they write reviews that the iPad is losing it, but I was lucky). After it is connected, applications usually need to enable Jot Touch support, then they will use it. In applications without Jot Touch support, it will behave like a regular stylus, that is, without pressure sensitivity.

Sensitivity


Actually, you only need to connect the stylus so that applications can access the amount of depression. The delay in data transfer is not noticed. There are two interesting points:

In general, for a stylus that you can take with you, this is a good stylus, but like with any tool, you also need to get used to it.

Applications



The adonit API is already integrated into applications such as:
  • ProCreate
  • Autodesk sketchbook
  • ArtRage
  • other

In applications (for example, ProCreate and SketchBook, others do not know), you can choose what the pressure will affect: opacity or size (but you can't do it together). In ProCreate, in order to change the size of the brush depending on the click, you need to enable the Glazing option (such an undocumented feature). Buttons usually perform undo-redo functions, but some applications allow you to change their purpose.

Availability



The price of the stylus is $ 99. Unfortunately, adonit does not send it to Russia, but there are many gadget shops that will gladly send it to you. Stylus pen for iPad 2 and New iPad.
