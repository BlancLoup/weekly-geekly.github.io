Studio "Sugar". Tale of a startup.

Ukraine, Nikolaev
www.rafinad.net

There are three of us, but we are in vests. Designer, programmer and editor. Each one has more than 5 years of experience in the web site industry.

The choice of the name was a long and rapid one. For obvious reasons, finding the right domain name was not a trivial task. We decided that we would choose a name, and then think of something with the domain.

And after several months of torment and flight of thought, about 200 various options were born. Most memorable:
- Studio "Bombovoz"
- Studio "Gingerbread"
- Studio "Catastrophe"
- Studio "Rafinad"
- Studio "25 markers"

"Sugar" chosen as the best option. Despite the fact that any normal Russian-speaking person associates this name with lumpy sugar. With the domain, you can say lucky - rafinad.net turned out to be virgin free. The prefix "web" for the word "studio" was decided not to use - is it enough;)

The designer sat down to work. In the course of his work, he invented the country of "Rafinadia", which is inhabited by small "refiners". So now we live together - even on the site they left. It became more fun.


"Along the coast, strange apple-tree trees grow, which fill
the air is the scented smell of its exquisite bloom. And in the middle of the sea
floats a huge chunk of extremely pure refined sugar that could
provide another hundred generations of refiners, only they can not yet
to get to it, for the shipyard where they could make a website and
on it swim to the iceberg, Uncle Seryozha has not yet drawn ... "


A few months before the events described, I became a private entrepreneur. For starters, it seemed quite sufficient. This made it possible to officially rent an office space. Now the country has a refined territory. Let it be only 21 square meters. Growing up!
