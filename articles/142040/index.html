Facebook has updated the Download Your Information feature.



The social network Facebook is trying to follow the promises of its leadership, which once again announced its intention to make the user's work in the Facebook environment more transparent. Previously, the Download Your Information tool was created, allowing the user to view and download many of his data uploaded to a social network during the entire time spent on this resource. Now this tool has been significantly updated.

So, before it was possible to download only the archive from the uploaded photos, messages and some other data. Now you can get additional information regarding your work on this social network. For example, you can view requests from friends, an archive of your nicknames, or IP addresses from which you logged into the system.

This update is already available to everyone, so that any user can view all the information collected by the social network about himself / herself. According to the developers, after a while, the Download Your Information function will be further expanded to make all information about the user and his work on Facebook available to him.
