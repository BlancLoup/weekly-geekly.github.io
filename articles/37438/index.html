Wii is perfect for violent games

According to some experts , the company Nintendo makes a big mistake by not releasing games with a real bloody massacre for Wii, of which there are a lot of for PCs and standard game consoles. If you think about it, then the Wii is the best suited for such a physical interaction: fights, blows with cold weapons, throwing stones, the destruction of surrounding objects by kicking legs, etc. Such games could have unprecedented realism.

Just imagine: you are tearing a road sign out of the ground and from all its scope beating your opponent's head. There is a difference, do it with your mouse or hands. The Wii prefix allows you to do it with your hands, what is its unique feature.

Probably, game developers will sooner or later realize what a unique opportunity they are missing, so that all standard bloody PC toys will finally be ported to the Wii, and then unique games will appear that would in principle be impossible when playing on another platform. Actually, the process has already begun. Sega recently announced the first really cruel Wii game.

Nintendo is unlikely to oppose the huge financial flow that promises the emergence of violent games, because Wii sales will increase many times over. Nintendo will surely go for it, even despite the risk of a state ban on the Wii console in some countries.

As proof that the real fights will be very exciting on the Wii, you can bring a real game - a virtual baseball under the Wii. It requires a bat to hit the ball, and the public really likes it. This game is one of the most popular sports simulators for Wii. If people like to hit the ball with a bat, then other people should like to hit the enemy with a bat on the head. Naturally, we do not discuss here the social aspect of such games, their benefit / harm to society, etc. There are specially trained specialists to discuss such issues. But the reality is that people really like to hit the enemy on the head with a bat, and game developers have long exploited this human craving.

If Wii proves itself as a gaming platform for "adult" games, then you can even think about the transition to the next stage: the creation of sex games, although this will require significantly improving the graphics subsystem.
