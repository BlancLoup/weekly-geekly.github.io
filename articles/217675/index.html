We now turn to the work of the product.


Immediately after turning on the plug, the network reveals a significant drawback, which can often be observed in low-cost soldering stations - the hum of a transformer. This indicates a low quality transformer. Moreover, different batches of the same device can be equipped with transformers of different quality. I was not lucky, in my case the buzz is so strong that it will be difficult to work. Transformer definitely should be changed or modified. Turn on the hair dryer. If it is cold and lies on the stand, nothing happens. If it is removed, the turbine starts working and heating starts to the set temperature, while the display shows the current temperature. Heating is very fast. The temperature can be changed in the range from 100 to 450 degrees Celsius with an accuracy of one degree. With a long press on the buttons, the temperature changes in increments of one degree. This is not very good because, in spite of the rate of change (approximately 5 grams per second), in order to quickly change the temperature from minimum to maximum, you will need to hold the button for about a minute. It is much better to regulate the temperature by a long press in increments of 10 degrees. The set temperature is stored in the EEPROM and is restored after shutdown. The heated hair dryer, placed on the stand, begins to cool. For this, the control unit turns on the maximum speed of the turbine and waits until it cools down to 100 degrees. Now about the internals and improvements


The reluctance to put up with the buzzing, like a whole substation, a transformer and the desire to turn off the station with a switch and not with a plug, was forced to disassemble the device.



The transformer turned out to be small and very hot, which speaks, again, of its low quality.



After an external study, it became clear that the core was weakly pulled. Having tightened it in a yew from all directions, it was possible to pull together more feasibly.



The buzz did not completely disappear, but it became much quieter and noticeable only in complete silence. This could be tolerated. Therefore, it was decided to close the topic of the transformer until another suitable trance appeared.

Now about the switch of the entire station. He is not and that is bad. Lukey 702 type stations cut a hole in the plastic case and insert the missing part. There is also a plastic case. But I decided to go smarter. In the station described, there are already as many as 2 switches - for a hairdryer and a soldering iron. Why not make it so that when you turn on one of them, in addition to the corresponding unit, the power of the station itself is turned on. To do this, do not need to cut a plastic case. It is only necessary to replace one linear switch with two linear ones. The second lines are paralleled and plugged into the ~ 220V power supply circuit. As indicated in the diagram:



Were purchased and installed similar to the old new two line switches KCD1.



Their parameters allow switching the maximum power of the station. Now the disadvantage is completely eliminated. The station turns on when you turn on or hair dryer or a soldering iron and completely turns off when you turn off both blocks.
