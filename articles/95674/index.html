Math exam

Do you know that in order to get a second higher education, it is now necessary to pass the EGE ?
In order to recover at the institute after deduction, you also need to pass the exam .
In order to go to college, you must pass the EGE, even if you graduated from high school before the EGE appeared at all.

And today we can observe a wondrous picture - as with schoolchildren , newly graduated graduates, for whom the exam is obligatory for graduation, adults 25-40 years old sit at the desks to write the unified state examination in mathematics. They are put in the same classrooms as schoolchildren, and they write an exam in the school curriculum ...

Which, of course, for several years since graduation, was completely forgotten, like a bad dream.

For most of the basic knowledge and formulas for the rest of his life may not be needed even once. Another logarithm - all right, they are at least somewhere used, like derivatives. But the sines and cosines, and especially - equations with them, are not needed almost anywhere and ever.

Today is the day of the exam in mathematics . Those who have nothing to do with this - take off your hat . Remember: if you want to get additional education, you also have to sit at the same school desks and remember trigonometry.
