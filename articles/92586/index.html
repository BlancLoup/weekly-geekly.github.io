Asus EEE PC 1005 PE Netbook Review

Keyboard.


The very rough touchpad with the multitouch function from the asus, is a very convenient thing, the hand does not get tired and it is easy to find it in the dark. What can multitouch?
2 fingers: on each other - decrease in scale, in different directions - increase in scale. 1 finger is in place, the 2nd one works as in a compass and we twist it - turn it (checked only in the standard Windows Picture Viewer). 2 fingers simultaneously vertical / horizontal - scroll the page.
3 fingers are used to change the page - slide 3 fingers to the right - the next page, slide to the left - the previous page.

The keyboard itself, in my opinion, is well done, a nice indent from the buttons, has a small stroke when pressed. The advantages of this keyboard in large enter and backspace keys.

A standard set of indicators for most models of books: power indicator, battery charging indicator, hard disk indicator, WiFi indicator, CapsLock indicator.

Immediately after the purchase, I was introduced to the stupor by 2 buttons on the netbook. The first button is responsible for turning on the netbook in the Expres Gate mode, the second one in the normal mode. I will not write about the mode of the express gate, about him there is already a lot said before me.

Lifetime.



How long does your laptop hold? 3-6 hours without charging? And 1005 PE lasts ~ 14 hours! , details:

Example 1
1. Turn on 50% of the brightness of the screen (I have enough of such brightness with my head).
2. Turn off Wifi.
3. We start typing in any office application. Then 14 hours will be absolutely real.

Example 2
1. Turn on the brightness by 50%.
2. Turn off Wifi.
3. We start watching the video (by the way, this netbook, like the others, does not support HD Video, but does an excellent job with DVD5, DVDRip ). In this situation, your will be 10-11 hours with non-stop playback.
UPD. habouriors helped deal with codecs and players, but HD really works as they say in these comments: 1 , 2 .

Example 3
1. We tighten the brightness at your discretion.
2. Open your favorite browser and start surfing. 10 hours of work you are guaranteed!

Example 4
Use a netbook as a music player, you can, I even tried, brightness 10%, sound to maximum - it played for 9 hours non-stop and there was still 22% of the battery charge.

Temperature.


In standby mode, like surfing (watching movies, listening to music), the netbook works silently. The average temperature of the device when surfing the Internet is 39-40, when playing hedgewars for half an hour it reaches 50-55 and the cooler starts to accelerate, it makes a lot of noise when it overheats, but the noise passes quickly because there is a cooler with 3300 RPM in connection with which it cools quickly and again goes into the silence of the mod.
