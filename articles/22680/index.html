UAWEB 2008. Interesting reports.

Salaries of IT-specialists: Analysis. Maxim Ishchenko.

Using a database of special questionnaires filled out by more than two thousand respondents, Maxim described the trends in the IT market, the most popular programming languages, the growth of salaries for programmers using these languages, the difference in pay team lead, project managers, developers, senior developers etc. The fork of salaries of industry specialists was considered depending on work experience. Also touched were the themes of personnel shortage in the Ukrainian IT market and the dependence of wage growth on the amount of knowledge and knowledge of various programming tools.

Social networks, development prospects and ways to monetize. Nikita Semenov.

A general overview of social networks was made, an attempt was made to approximate classification, hypotheses were made about the development trends of social networks in the West and the Russian Internet, a small part of the report was devoted to the history of social networks. The report clearly lacked the depth, structure, and completeness of the terminology, and therefore the report can be classified as an attempt to introduce an introductory lecture on the topic of social networks on the Internet. Separately, it is worth noting that the speaker mentioned a platform for building social networks on which he is currently working.


Features broadcast media on the Internet. Vladimir Mikhalko.

Vladimir is the director of Community8, a company that creates personal and corporate websites, social networks and blogs, online stores, news sites and information portals. Vladimir very clearly showed the audience a lot of problems that all print media face in one way or another, who want to ensure their own presence on the Internet. I mentioned a lot of factors, CMS, the need to match the business models of the offline edition and its online version, the complexity associated with the server part. Showed many fairly illustrative examples.

Using the hidden psychophysical capabilities of web developers for the successful creation and implementation of modern Internet projects. Valery Pavlovich Yurikov.

He introduced the modern bio-computer model of man to the attention of the public. He proposed to consider the programming process as creativity. Stress states. Creating sustainable states of comfort, increasing efficiency and psychological stability of web developers. The state of the speaker is classified, and methods of influence are not described.

Ria in Flex perspective. Alexey Zatvornitsky and Sergey Kovalev.

The concept of Rich Internet Applications has been defined. Many have said that Ria is a great tool for creating projects like Saas. The capabilities of the Flex technology were demonstrated and ways of integrating Flex applications with various server technologies - Java, PHP, Ruby on rails etc. were discussed. I was very impressed by the demonstration of the RIA application (data visualization, interaction with the open Application Programming Interface.

Latest Guidelines for Web Usability. Julia Klimentovskaya.

Very interesting, detailed and well-structured report on the main ways to improve the usability of the site, the various methods of visual display of information on the site (small details) and errors that allow designers and layout designers. Besides a lot of really concrete and useful information there were many interesting examples. Perhaps this report in view of the great practical value of the slides and I want to download the most.
