HDin.tv closes

I regret to inform you that today, on a beautiful spring day, the authors of the HDin.tv resource reported about the imminent closure of their remarkable brainchild. The project is unique in that it can be used to watch popular TV shows, just like in the notorious Turbofilm, only for free and in HD quality. On the main HDin.tv hanging link to the message from the father-creator:

Dear users of the resource.

When this resource was created, everything was rosy and prosaic. There was a time, there were resources, there was an idea. But time passes, enthusiasm fades away, conditions change. Projects of legal online cinemas have appeared and are actively developing. The repression against providers of not entirely legal content intensified. Etc. etc. In general - a lot of things.

Guided by multiple assumptions and a certain number of personal motives, I decided to close this resource. This will be done on April 1 (and this is not a joke), until then the site will function as before. After that, the shutdown will be made, and all content will be eliminated. Perhaps the resource will be revived in a new form, but it will no longer be a collection of TV shows in its current state. Twitter will remain behind the resource, the waves of Google Wave, most likely, will be simply abandoned.

For those who wish, I will post the whole kitchen for preparing such resources in my technology blog on relabs.ru a little later.

Separate information for donators. I understand that this scenario is not the most expected and in some ways even wrong. Therefore, I can offer you as an alternative to register on tulavideo.net , another online video resource, which was also made with my participation. The series there are presented in the traditional SD-quality, but there are many, much more than they were on hdin.tv. Write me, please, at mkrentovskiy@gmail.com, I will send you a code word for registration on the resource.

Special thanks to everyone who helped develop and expand the resource, participated in testing and discussion. If you do not have registration on tulavideo.net - write to me, we will solve this issue.

In general, something like that. Hope not to say goodbye. :)

Best wishes,
Maksim
