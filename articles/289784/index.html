Twitter - Marketing in the Moment
Nlall Horgan, Twitter

The main distinctive features (they are also strengths) of Twitter, according to the speaker, are that:
Alive - events are happening in it right now
Public - it brings together many people
Means to communicate people

It is these features that should be used for promotion. About 3.5 billion tweets are generated weekly within the community. Whatever people are different, they are united by many desires, sensations, actions.

Analysis
Based on a study of tweets, you can find out what time of the day most people get bored. On which day of the week people run the most in the morning or go shopping. Thus, you have the opportunity to get a high spread of your tweet and remind / tell more people about your company.

‚ÄúMoving into a crisis quickly: shifting the point of pleasure to a result‚Äù
Alexander Pervak, "Datamaaster", "Gifterland"

Many people know the feeling when the first chapter of the book read gives the illusion that you have already achieved the result. Most likely you do not read the book in this case.

In order to continue to grow, you need to move the point of satisfaction with yourself / process / project to a later period. To do this, we mark ourselves a period when we allow ourselves to be satisfied, like launching a beta version of a product, for example.

Next, do the following procedure:
1. We break work on the project into stages
2. We visualize these stages, for example, on the wall.
3. Denote the stage at which we allow ourselves to enjoy the result obtained.
4. We cross out the stages
5. We reach the period of "joy" and praise ourselves for the work done.
6. We continue to work until the next reached peak.

Such a method, according to the speaker, allows the team to remain motivated and in good shape, and the project to develop.

You can and should search for your target audience through the means of crowd marketing: look for questions / feedback related to your product / service; install Google Alerts and get keyword alerts; track comments through Disqus; Communicate with potential users through social networks; Search sites that match your subject through LiveInternet.
