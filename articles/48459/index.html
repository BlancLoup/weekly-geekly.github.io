Make your blog unique - create MuzPic!

Looking through such sites as z0r.de and pown.it we thought that loops ( looped animated video ) is a very interesting topic.

Since the autostart is done on z0r.de, the music is looped and loaded for a long time - we decided to improve the technology and make everything much more convenient, as well as expand the possibility of using the interactive video.


Now these videos can be inserted into a blog or page to show your mood, reaction to something or share something interesting with your friends.

Muzpic is interactive, it starts playing if you hover it over with a mouse, but for gourmets we also left the autostart function.

That is, now you create an interactive video yourself, upload a gif, choose music, and insert a ready-made MuzPic anywhere on your page or blog. Also supported by the insertion in the forums and a separate link to the micro-blogs.

Examples of Muzpic, as well as how to use it, you can see in our blog .
