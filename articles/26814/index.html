Announcement of the business smartphone HTC Touch Pro



Just the other day, I began to look for a replacement for my HTC Touch, which I bought last year and stopped arranging in many ways, the main one being the speed of the device. Then I saw that there was an active discussion of such smartphones as HTC Diamond and Sony Ericsson XPERIA X1. The release of the first smartphone was scheduled for June of this year and apparently already on sale.

Htc diamond


SE XPERIA X1


And just today I read the news about the announcement of the business version of the HTC Touch Pro, which is a further development of the HTC Touch Diamond. Unlike its predecessor, the new smartphone has a QWERTY keyboard and is a 3G smartphone running Windows Mobile 6.1 Pro.

HTC pays attention to the fact that even though the Touch Pro got the keyboard, the 2.8-inch touchscreen display of the device still remained and users can use it to navigate. The smartphone is equipped with a GPS module and a 3.2 megapixel camera.

Also optional:
Qualcomm MSM7201A 528 MHz processor;
Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR, Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g;
ROM 512 MB, RAM 288 MB, microSD slot;
FM radio with RDS;
1340 mAh battery.

Personally, I like the characteristics of the new smartphone, I like the QWERTY-keyboard and GPS module. How dynamic the technologies are developing and you have to think a hundred times before buying a new product without fear that in a couple of months it will not be so relevant and interesting.
