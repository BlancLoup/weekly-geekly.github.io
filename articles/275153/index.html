DeepHack.Q & A Starts - International Hackathon for Deep Learning and Machine Intelligence

Rostelecom invites everyone who is interested in data mining, machine learning and neural network technologies to take part in the DeepHack.Q & A International Neural Network Hackathon!
Registration is until January 17, 2016.



The 24/7 non-stop hackathon and scientific school on the most promising methods for constructing question-answer systems and text analysis to solve the The Allen AI Science Challenge problem will be held from January 31 to February 6, 2016.

Among the speakers of the scientific school are specialists from Google, Facebook, openAI, Skoltech and other research institutes about question-answer and conversational systems, methods of machine translation and knowledge processing.

To participate in the hackathon, 50 people will be selected who will unite in 8-12 teams for team assault on one of the most difficult tasks - intellectual answer to questions using classical methods of working with natural language and deep learning algorithms.

Free registration of participants and more information about the event on the official website ( http://qa.deephack.me/ ).

Join now!

The winners will receive a Skolkovo Foundation grant in the amount of 5 million rubles, All-Access participation in the leading technology and research conference - NVIDIA GTC 2016 , an interview for the summer internship at Facebook AI Research and other prizes.

In addition, teams have a chance to win the international kaggle competition with a prize fund of $ 80,000.

We will be glad to see you among the participants!
