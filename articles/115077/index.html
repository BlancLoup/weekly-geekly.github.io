Simple timer for multiple tasks

Golden Minute HQ is a small service to account for working (and not only) time.

At work, we use FreshBooks , and he has a pretty good timer, the problem is that he is alone. Sometimes I have a pretty ragged work schedule (10 minutes in the IDE, 15 minutes to talk with developers, etc.), and you have to record time often enough, and then also remember the wording of the previous task, when you need to return to it.



I did not regret one of the after-work evenings, and wrote what, in my opinion, solves this problem: Golden Minute HQ (it was busy without HQ, I decided to follow the example of BaseCamp :)

One page (HTML + JS), many timers, the launch of any of them automatically stops the rest (it will not work 30 hours today).

Timers are stored in cookies for 10 days - you can reload the page as much as you like.
Esc cancels timer editing.

If someone helps - use on health.
Well, I have not tested for Mac / Linux / IE6, etc., because this is not an end in itself (and there is CSS3). But if something floats somewhere, let me know. It is also worth noting that there is no synchronization with FreshBooks yet, after all, this is not a single evening affair.
