How to take the first steps in robotics?



Robotization and automation are becoming more in demand, and many would like to learn how to create such systems and devices. But where to start, how to master the basics? We have made for you a small selection of Russian-language and English-language YouTube-channels with educational materials and methodical manuals on robotics.

AlexGyver



The channel is led by an engineer who talks about his experience in designing various materials from scrap materials, usually automated ones. We are talking about electronics, robotics, tools and applied experiments. Quite an interesting and accessible material from which you can learn something new.

AmperkaRu



Channel pretty popular store "Ampere". Dedicated to electronics and robotics. It tells about the Arduino, Raspberry Pi and Iskra JS platforms, with which you can create robots and automated systems (such as ‚Äúsmart home‚Äù) even with a minimal set of knowledge.

Universarium, the course "Robotics"



An interesting channel that allows you to get a lot of knowledge in different areas from leading universities. Including contains introductory materials of lectures on robotics, which can be accessed within the framework of the Universarium project.

Easytech



The channel presents available lessons on robotics, programming, as well as interesting materials and coverage of events related to robots. The author is a Candidate of Physical and Mathematical Sciences and a coach of the Russian national team in robotics.

SERVODROID



Contains educational materials on robotics for beginners. Making a robot with your own hands from scratch. From the simplest copies to computers on processors and microcontrollers. Each material contains a description of the robot, instructions for creating it and a list of necessary elements.

Happybot



On the channel you will find training lessons on robotics, in particular, on programming EV3. The first season of lectures is laid out completely. The material is available for beginners. Planned 3 seasons.

Robot on



The channel has a lot of interesting and useful information on the creation of robots, materials from the scene, exhibitions and championships, as well as educational materials, including practical exercises for the course on robotics.

Channel Vladislav Lukyanov



A number of quite interesting training and survey materials on robotics from an employee of the Orenburg Presidential Cadet College. Here you can get basic knowledge that will be useful to anyone interested in robotics.

Entertaining robotics



Robotics lessons for beginners, and not only for children. Step-by-step instructions available for assembling robots from LEGO, based on Arduino, etc. It also contains many other interesting materials on the topic.

I love robotics



Robotics training materials, as well as videos and news from this field. The author has several awards and patents in this area. She teaches her own course ‚ÄúHow to become a robotics engineer‚Äù for undergraduate and graduate students.

Josh Bongard Canal



A very interesting course of lectures on robotics is not for beginners. Covers many interesting aspects and contains many materials in the form of lectures from this and related areas.

Paul McHorter's Channel



A series of training lessons on the Arduino platform. Quite an interesting and simple presentation of the material. Lessons are suitable for beginners.

Programming electronics academy



A series of tutorials on Arduino and basic programming for beginners. It will help make the first steps to get acquainted with the platform and learn the basics.

DPV TECHNOLOGY



Educational lessons on electronics, robotics based on Arduino and much more. Containing interesting materials from the DIY category.

Introduction to Robotics



A collection of lectures with a talking title from Stanford University. Not for beginners.
