3 ways to increase visitor loyalty with content

Do you know how much time the user spends on a preliminary assessment of the site after it is loaded? To assess whether it is worth looking for the information you need on your website here and now? One twentieth of a second. This is a critically small value. Consequently, there are only three things that can keep his attention: a memorable design, high-quality structure, which in one instant will lead the user to the desired point of his journey through the site, or interesting, conspicuous content. Here we will talk about it.

As experience has shown, in our difficult time filled with terrabytes of information, users have become lazy and biased. An article more than a couple of pages should simply be a discovery in its field, so that more than half of users read it to the end, and the joke should be really funny so that it can be sent to each other. Hell has come for content managers and a paradise for sites like news2.ru or digg.com, where lazy users are already waiting for specially approved other users, which means obviously interesting content. (Even if it was not ordinary users who approved it, but SMO-optimizers, hired to promote this or that site in web 2.0 resources). Information is more and less time. <>

If users are not offered solutions to problems, then they are satisfied with a summary of what was said earlier in one place. In this case, it is likely that users will add your page to the "Favorites" or add to the online bookmarks. You will have to spend a little time, however, you will do a useful job, for which users will thank you, because they will not waste time on the same work.
