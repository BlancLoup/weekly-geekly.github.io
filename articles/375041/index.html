Space timelapse: 12 thousand photos of cosmonaut Alexander Gersta in one video



The European Space Agency has prepared a New Year's Eve gift for all lovers of space subjects. The agency's specialists created a timelapse video, which made up of 12,000 photographs taken by German cosmonaut Alexander Gerst.

This cosmonaut lived and worked for the ISS for six months. During this time, the astronaut managed to do a lot, for example - to capture thousands of views of the Earth, stars, take pictures of his colleagues and the ISS. By the way, this video is in 4K quality, so that the beauty of space can be enjoyed while watching it to the full.
