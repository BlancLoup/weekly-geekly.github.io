Own build of Firefox? Easy!!!



Recently found a very nice project. Called Firefox Builder
The service is intended to create Firefox and Thunderbird assemblies customized for your interests.
The choice of versions of Firefox on the basis of which you can make your builds is limited to versions from 3.0.10 to 3.6 a1pre, and Thunderbird from 2.0.0.21 to 3.1 a1.
Addons and themes can be added to builds. They can be selected from the catalog or downloaded by yourself.
