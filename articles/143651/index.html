Universal assessment system (goals, methods, actions, etc.)

We all use different systems for monitoring ourselves and organizing our actions. Someone needs a notebook, and someone has created a whole cloud infrastructure for himself. Point one. Basics of Geometry.
Although I am sure that geometry is remembered by everyone, without it in my concept, nowhere.

The measurement is zero. Point. I am. I am me and nothing more. At the moment - that's all.

Measurement first. Line. I and the coordinate system is good-bad. Evaluating yourself and your actions on a ten-point scale, where 5 is good, -5 is bad. Introduced framework of morality. Each defines for itself the limits of the permissible. This is done as follows: five or more crimes are taken, then five or more good deeds. Each of these cases is assigned a score on the same ten-point scale. Then the invalid is truncated. For example, inadvertent murder is rated on a scale of -4. Allowed personal scope - -1,5. If someone can not immediately call for a framework of permissible - can use the Gauss ratio (0.8-0.2). Important: Do not forget to raise your moral framework as you develop.
