Anonymous will launch their own social network



Despite the fact that law enforcement officers have recently revived, and more and more members of the Anonymous community are arresting (in any case, the arrested are members of this group, the law enforcement officials themselves are sure), the latter are not asleep . So, immediately after the release of the new social network Google+, Anonymous opened its own representative office here, Your Anon account. Google blocked this account in a few days. What are going to do about this Anonymous? Of course, open your own social network.

It is worth noting that Anonymus lost not only the account in the new social network, Google has also disabled the group accounts in Gmail, YouTube, Picasa and other services of the company. Naturally, the proud Anonymous decided not to complain (to whom?), But to act in a more original way by creating their own social network. The announcement has already taken place, and on the anonplus.com domain the description of the future project proudly flaunts.

The project team currently consists of 18 people, so there is someone to work on a new social network. Anonymous promise that the new social network will be completely anonymous, no censorship, no account blocking and so on. In addition, there will be no tyranny :-)

In general, anonplus users will be completely free citizens of the Network, as it should be. At the same time, the rest of the Internet community, oppressed by the evil tyrants of the Network, will envy the free community of Anonymus with envy and slowly move to the social network Anonplus.

In general, jokes are jokes, but a new social network can be quite an interesting project. This project is unlikely to be able to compete with large and malicious social programs, but if everything goes well, a new project will find its niche and its users without difficulty.
