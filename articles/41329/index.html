10 things startups can learn from superheroes

Superheroes never give up

Like Batman, you must fight to the last breath. Challenges are overcome if you resist them tirelessly.



Superheroes always do their jobs until the end

You have no excuse if you did not save the girl from the burning building. There are only results, not reasons why you did not achieve them, and you either achieve them or not. Halftones are for Kafka, not for superheroes.



Superheroes are the best in what they do

Flash is the fastest, and we all know it ... If you are a back end developer, then you are the best developer in the world, and all members of your team know that. Make great talents prosper in areas where their super powers are most needed.



Superheroes are very clear about their goal.

Captain Marvel can do stupid things, but he knows exactly what he is fighting for. Startups die when their mission is unclear.



Superheroes are not without flaws

Superheroes have flaws, and your team members have them too. The goal is not perfection, but the pursuit of it.



Superheroes are not looking for fame ... But they get it anyway

Do not do this to get attention. If you do everything right, you will get it anyway.



Superheroes help others

Superheroes help people solve their problems. Startups should be obsessed not with themselves, but with how they can help people solve their problems.



Superheroes can do everything themselves, but they are especially strong in the team.

You should always feel the support of other members of your team. This is your fight against the rest of the world, and it is vital for you to have your own team of superheroes, mutual respect, loyalty and a sense of camaraderie to win.



The real power of a superhero in his character

It does not matter how much you consider yourself to be brilliant, because your strength is not in your talent, but in your character. Be courageous, polite, noble and disinterested.



Superheroes perform feats

Need to change the world. Your goal is not to make a product and not to get traffic. Your goal - to make an outstanding feat. Make a Product, Save the World.
