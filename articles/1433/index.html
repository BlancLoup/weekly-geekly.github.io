A new website of the Association of Providers of Mobile Services and Content has opened

The association of providers of mobile services and content launches a new version of its website. The site is intended to become an effective information resource promoting the activities of the Association.

On the pages of the updated resource will be posted news relating to the field of mobile content, as well as information about events in the market, and the activities of the Association. Along with the news, the site also presents the Charter of the Association, information about its members and a questionnaire for commercial organizations wishing to join the Association.
