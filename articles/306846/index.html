MVNO adult

The possibilities of a virtual mobile operator are no less wide than those of a regular mobile operator. In previous articles of the Tottoli GSM blog, we talked about different MVNO models , which differ in the functionality available to the operator. In this detail we describe the model of Full MVNO, which this summer has become available for connection.

Having connected the Full MVNO package, the operator receives SIM-cards and API from us. Using the API, he completely independently creates the control system of the virtual mobile operator. Calls pass through his own server and are counted in his billing system.



What functions remain with Tottoli GSM?


When working with the Pro MVNO package, calls go through the Tottoli GSM server, billing is also there. As we have said, when working through Full MVNO, billing is on the server of the virtual mobile operator.
When a subscriber wants to make a call, the signal goes to the Tottoli GSM server, from where the request to the server of the MVNO operator is sent, whether this subscriber can make a call. If the call is allowed, then it is further processed by the server of the MVNO operator in accordance with the specified rules and recorded there in the billing system.
Thus, when calling back Tottoli GSM knows only about the fact of making a call, in case of a direct call, the first shoulder (A-leg) is also on the side of Tottoli GSM.

What features distinguish Full MVNO?



1) Your site with a personal account
The virtual mobile operator creates its website, which has its own user account.
2) Own tariffs
Operators and earlier could form tariffs under conditions which they established. However, to make changes to the existing conditions, it was necessary to contact Tottoli GSM each time. Although the technical specialists who perform the necessary settings are a team of professionals, it is still impossible to make changes instantly. Now operators can change tariffs as often as they like, as they do it themselves. However, their conditions are confidential, business indicators do not fall into the external environment.
3) Own traffic providers (SIP-providers)
Operators can connect new SIP traffic providers. Previously, it was necessary to do it through Tottoli GSM, which took time and was paid separately. Their suppliers provide more opportunities for the formation of favorable tariffs, and the procedure for connecting new trunks has been simplified.
4) The operator does not depend on our resources, capacities
Its technical and professional resources, the process is organized as needed by this virtual mobile operator. Technical support for subscribers 24/7, VIP-lines for quick communication of privileged subscribers, any other tools to improve the quality of work.
5) Own reporting (charts, graphs, other convenient implementation of current indicators)
Operators have the ability to organize the removal of indicators most clearly for their purposes.
6) Independence of the implementation of all decisions
With strong specialists, new solutions are being introduced as quickly as possible.
7) USSD with their teams
Operators have the opportunity to create their own USSD-commands for different functions: management of the SIM-card, options, sending USSD-messages to other subscribers and so on.
8) You can connect your virtual numbers
You could connect your virtual numbers before, but through Tottoli GSM specialists. Operators can now do this on their own.

API technical features.


1) CallBack / Callthru implementation (The operator determines the mechanism by which the call will be organized)
2) Authorization / prohibition of directions, operators, profiles (The operator can open or close various directions that are of interest to him or, on the contrary, are unprofitable according to the business model)
3) Regulation of incoming calls (enable / disable itp)
4) Time limit session (anti-fraud)
5) Substitution of numbers, voices by own rules
6) Incoming and outgoing SMS
7) USSD - any commands

Programmers (the code of the administrative panel can be written in any programming language), at will - the designer, if necessary - the system administrator.

Summary


Full MVNO gives the virtual mobile operator a wide range of opportunities for doing business and managing SIM cards, subscriber calls, and its own statistics.
