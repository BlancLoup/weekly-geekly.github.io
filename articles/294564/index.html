Qiwi plans to create Bitcoin analogue for the Russian market



The Qiwi payment system is currently engaged in the development of bitruble - cryptocurrency, the principle of operation of which will be similar to Bitcoin. Such a system will be designed for the Russian market, as reported by the company's general director and co-owner Sergey Solonin, writes VC. To launch the national cryptocurrency, the company will need several hundred million ordinary rubles, and the bitrubley will start working in 2016.
