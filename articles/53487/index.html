Hypnocubus



In RuNet is very popular hypnotob. Many interesting thoughts arise when you look into a hypnotizing endless stream of multi-colored spheres and ovals.

Inventors delight trance lovers with an interesting new gadget - this is hypnoucube.

The gadget is very useful not only for clubs, cafes, but also will be a cool decoration in the evening in your living room.

In a small cube, 64 tricolor LEDs are installed, which make up unique combinations of multi-colored patterns.

The hypnocube is programmed to reproduce 4096 colors in 51 modes. Color schemes move along 10 specified trajectories and have 10 effects of changing modes. 4096 colors, 51 effects, a random direction of movement of the picture and 10 transition effects add up to the total ... I do not even know how many possible combinations.

In the cube, a system for generating 3D images is implemented, which works smoothly with hand-written effects. For those who like to solve riddles, it is possible to connect a hypnocube to USB, so you can manually set the program modes.
