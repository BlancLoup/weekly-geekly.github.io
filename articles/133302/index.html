Niche choice


As the commentators repeatedly noted in the Habr articles, the class of devices in question is not suitable for serious work - weak processors, small screens, often only the VGA output of the 2nd monitor, the absence of 3D graphics. But it is suitable for a lot of various auxiliary cases - viewing, reading on the road and other autonomous environments, editing materials, simple tests, demonstrations on the road, transferring large archives of tens of gigabytes. To cover the maximum number of simple tasks, Intel‚Äôs architecture is better than the other, because it means the compatibility of programs and data from these programs, the absence of a separate zoo of utilities to perform similar functions on the main and auxiliary computers. It can equally well be installed Windows or Linux or both. In some cases, the computer can play the role of the main - known stories that netbooks were used for web development.

Therefore, it is very advantageous to use netbooks with Intel architecture for such auxiliary tasks. The role of the experimental, with its own rules and utilities, remains devices with telephone OS, rushing into this market on the other hand - with saving space in your pocket by combining it with the phone and expanding the functions of the phone at the same time.
