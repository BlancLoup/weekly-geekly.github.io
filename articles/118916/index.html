Google is experimenting with the design of the search results page



Google Corporation constantly conducts certain tests and experiments that are aimed at improving the interface and usability of the company's services. In general, this is good, since it is clear that the company does not stop there, and is constantly improving its own products, using various fields of science and technology to improve the quality of its services. At this time, Google is conducting a selective testing of the design of the search results google.com

The design of the issue changes a little, but only slightly, and the planned changes will bring in a lot of new things. This, of course, will not affect the issue itself, but its perception by users will be influenced quite strongly. First of all, the color gamut on the page was changed.
