MTS and the Moscow Metro presented a new way to pay for the subway using a mobile phone

The MTS operator (Mobile TeleSystems OJSC, the largest cellular operator in the Russian Federation by the number of subscribers) intends to launch a new method of payment for travel in the Moscow metro in the fourth quarter of 2010 - subscribers can go to the subway by attaching their mobile phone to the turnstile reader, company message.



The project is being implemented jointly with the Moscow Metro State Unitary Enterprise based on NFC technology (Near Field Communication, short-range wireless communication technology). The MTS representatives demonstrated a ready-to-use technological solution for payment of travel in the metro on Friday at the St. Petersburg Economic Forum.
