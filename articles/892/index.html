Christmas toys from Yandex

In anticipation of the New Year holidays, the company Yandex, offers to play to everyone who has an electronic mailbox registered in the system Yandex. Mail.

To start the game you need to get a virtual toy in your account, which you can donate to other Yandex.Mail users, or collect your collection.

It is important not to forget that the New Year's magic ends and virtual toys will turn into pumpkins on the night of January 13-14. To prevent this from happening, you can exchange toys for real Yandex.Money, however, for this you have to collect a certain set of several toys.

All in search of toys, and for detailed rules - to Yandex .
