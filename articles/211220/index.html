20 professions of the future



"60% of the best professions of the next decade have not even been invented yet . " - Thomas Frey, futurist .

Research agency Sparks & Honey offered their vision of the near future. More precisely, the vision of 20 professions, which, according to the authors, will be in great demand in the next decade.

In the past, people built their careers steadily and linearly. Everyone chose one path in life and adhered to it from college to retirement. But in this age of exponential technology development, such a model no longer works. Today, building your career is a complex, fragmented process, we have to specialize in a narrow area and constantly cooperate with others. More than ever, this process requires the involvement of other people. Often our portfolio consists of a list of microcaviers .

Some of the future occupations presented below already exist, some are still in their infancy. The rest have not yet been created, but, according to our daily observations, there is a high probability of their imminent demand.

Productivity consultant (Productivity conselor) . Thanks to the emphasis on "productivity" and "creating differences", as well as new tracking technologies, people will need help in organizing their lives for the sake of increasing their productivity, well-being, effective time management and career counseling.

Personal digital curator (Personal digital curator) . A specialist who will create for you a unique set of applications and information resources for your personal and business needs.

Specialist in microflora (Microbial balancer) . A consultant who selects for you both the external and internal composition of micromiflora, and also gives recommendations on the formation of a sustainable healthy ecosystem.

Corporate disorganizer (Corporate disorganizer) . A specialist who changes the hierarchical relationships in a company in order to create a startup atmosphere or organized chaos.

Curiosity educator (Curiosity tutor) . A personal flaning advisor who not only inspires and supports curiosity in you, but also teaches you the art of independent world exploration.

Alternative currency trader (Alternative currency speculator) . Bitcoin and other virtual currencies are becoming increasingly important for people who do not trust traditional money. This creates prerequisites for the formation of full-fledged exchange trading and investment using alternative currencies.

Urban Shepherd . Micro-farmer and consultant on the creation of living space in the city. These people will specialize in tiny gardens and flower beds, which will be located in the most unusual places.
