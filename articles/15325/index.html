Fluxbuntu 7.10

The ability to create distributions based on Ubuntu does not give people peace of mind. Meet Fluxbuntu

Word Processor - AbiWord
Spreadsheets - GnuNumeric
IM - Pidgin
Browser - Kazehakase

The creators promise that this miracle requires less resources than Ubunt, which should be good news for owners of weak machines.
