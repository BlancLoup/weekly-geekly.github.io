All-Russian final of the Technology Cup Imagine Cup 2009

On April 18, the winning team of the Imagine Cup 2009 technology team will be determined in Moscow, which will receive the right to represent Russia at the international final of the competition in Cairo. The competition will be attended by seven teams.
