Navigon 8110: 3D navigation

At CeBIT 2008, Navigon presented an interesting novelty in the field of GPS navigation - the advanced 8110. The main advantages of the device are a large 4.8-inch WQVGA display, voice control, playback of three-dimensional terrain maps and TMC Pro traffic, allowing you to get free information on traffic jams.

Navigon 8110 is equipped with a powerful processor with a frequency of 533 MHz and 512 MB of RAM. Additional features worth highlighting the presence of MP3-player and FM-radio. Another plus of the device is a set of preinstalled software, among which the most interesting is the panoramic panorama viewer program 3D Panorama View and the voice recognition software Voice Recognition Pro.

According to the company Navigon, in April, the new product will be on sale in Europe and will cost 449 euros. Expensive, of course, but there are a lot of functions in this stylish GPS-navigator.
