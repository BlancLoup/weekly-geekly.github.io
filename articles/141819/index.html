Music players in Android

At some point I was tired of constantly recording discs to listen to them in the car on the road. Of course, to change the radio tape recorder that supports flash drives / cards / bluetooth is an option, but it was a pity to part with the true ‚ÄúPioneer‚Äù, which in terms of sound quality will give odds to many modern low-cost devices. But I have HTC Desire, which with the help of simple manipulations was connected to the radio through the line input and the music is always with me, whether I'm in the car or with my headphones with light, you can always listen to where you left off. And the problem of choice has come - what to play music with ?!


Initial requirements
He did not set strict quality requirements, and such testing is often subjective. Therefore, I will not compare the sound of different players.
I was interested in more convenience of use and some chips, such as: searching for covers on the Internet, controlling from Lockscreen , turning off music when disconnecting a headset, pausing to play when calling or turning on voice recognition, convenient control (rewinding, previous, next track, album) without small elements and a large number of clicks.

As it turned out, there are still a lot of interesting pieces, which I did not even guess. For example, group albums into folders, and not try to present a collection as a whole bunch of different albums.
So, 8 players are loaded, who will win?

PlayerPro Music Player
One of the recommended players in many places. A 7-day trial version, fully functional, is available for free.
Outwardly handsome, the cover downloaded almost everything, besides he also uploaded photos of the performers. In the notification panel can show the cover. The control buttons are large, the scroll bar is clearly visible. The thing is, to control the car. It has a built-in equalizer, though presets to it must be downloaded separately from Google, for free. There are also a number of additional skins and plug-ins.
It contains in its arsenal as many as two types of lock screen, one better than the other. And pieces of 5 widgets for every taste and size.
Of the interesting chips - automatically start playback when a headset is connected. Shaking tracks (in the car, yeah). A huge number of options for setting the lock screen, the appearance of the player and anything else. Do not even talk about the pause mode for calls. The ability to intercept control playback from the standard loxgrin Cyanogen.

Total: you can say almost perfect player. If only he could not show all the songs from the collection, as different albums. And sometimes, after pressing the pause, it reproduces a low rattle. The latter can be attributed to custom firmware.



Winamp
Frankly, I have long believed that Winamp is no longer a cake. But he couldn‚Äôt not look. Outwardly simple, although not obvious in some places, and in some places even confused and incomprehensible. In general, I do not share the opinion of the editor of the resource "Android Police".

Albums shows, like everything, the cover seems to be shaking, loxgrin is simple and clear, there are widgets like everywhere else. If you want an equalizer - please buy the full version. Poweramp
And here he is, here, the player of my dreams. Almost. So, what interesting developers offer? It was the first player who realized that my collections do not need to be shown as a bunch of separate songs, but can be combined into folders. Almost all the covers downloaded independently and only with a few exceptions, you need to go in and manually set a search, so that there would be a large number of options for manual selection. In addition, you can edit the tags of compositions. There are several options for the presentation of albums, folders, compositions - tiles, list, tree. The controls are large and clear, you can switch tracks on the cover by swipe.
The richest sound settings. Here and 10-band equalizer with built-in presets and preamplifier, loudness and limiter, separate tuning of bass and treble, balance and stereo widening.
And most of the settings are available directly from the main screen of the player. And if some controls are pressed and held, then all possible options of this element will appear.
Almost perfect lock screen widget. There is everything you need and even more, just like in the player, a swap works to change tracks. Several widgets of different sizes, which also have everything you need. And the widgets can also be downloaded separately, so that when the player is installed on the card, they continue to work.

You can download a trial full-featured version. Full version for a small fee, but to be honest, it is worth the money.

Total: an excellent player with rich settings and functionality. A small spoon of tar dropped today, when for some reason the player hung the whole system. Still sinning on an unstable firmware version. And during a pause during a call, the volume is somehow not very pleasantly changing - not a smooth fade or immediately off, but a jump in a loud sound. But these little things are compensated by the convenience and subjective sound quality.
