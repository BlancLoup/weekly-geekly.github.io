F-35 fighter helmet costs $ 600,000



F-35 fighter - the most expensive project in the history of the Pentagon. The development of a new generation of aircraft spent $ 300 billion It's time to return the investment.

For example, the Australian government recently signed a contract for the purchase of 72 F-35 fighter jets with a total value of 12.4 billion Australian dollars. According to the Australian media, the pilots' helmets alone cost more than 55 million, that is, up to $ 600 thousand apiece, translated into US dollars (if it was exactly 72 helmets purchased).

It is said that these helmets, as if taken from Tony Stark 's arsenal, are the most technically advanced head-mounted display that engineers have ever created.

Stuffed with electronics and sensors, with a carbon fiber body and a night vision device, a helmet weighing 2.25 kg broadcasts real-time contextual information about the surrounding terrain.
