Odesk-client stopped taking screenshots (OS X Lion)?

If you make money through Odesk , then after updating to OS X Lion, you most likely noticed that the screenshots were no longer taken. It looks like this:


The problem is solved by running the command in the console:
$ defaults write com.odesk.Preferences "TeamCapture.UseCGS" 1

I hope this fix will be useful, and you do not have to spend your precious time writing a ticket in Odesk-Support :)
