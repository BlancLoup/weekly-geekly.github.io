We are looking for speakers at the second Moscow Atlassian Meetup



For 5 years, we have been developing the Mail.Ru Group infrastructure using Atlassian products. All this time we have been constantly confronted with a shortage of specialists in these products, and the community in Russia is practically not developed. Answers to all the questions had to be found by ourselves, fighting with the problems, difficulties, and support of Atlassian.

Then, having already gathered some experience, we organized our community meeting - Moscow Atlassian Meetup. Colleagues from other IT companies were invited to it and spoke on their own. They talked about many things: I showed with a live example how to automate the work of editing content projects; Denis Kulyabin from IT Care - how to integrate telephony with JIRA; Anton Colin explained why they did not implement the Service Desk in Teamlead, and Andrei Larionov from ALM Works told what to do if JIRA slows down. We have recorded all reports on video and published on Habr√©. Then more than 100 people came, and another 200 watched the broadcast online.

Now we want to gather people for the second meeting on Atlassian products (JIRA, Confluence and others). This will finally allow to establish professional communication and develop the market for the use of these products. My colleagues regularly hold similar meetings on other technologies, and their experience is very useful for the participants. Why don't we make it a tradition?

Moscow Atlassian Meetup 2: what will happen?

We will again talk about interesting and discuss sore. In general, speaking at IT parties and even just visiting them is terribly useful for self-development. Speakers also get a set of significant bonuses. For example, this feature:

  • share experiences and learn about others' successes;
  • promote your own name in the community;
  • pump up the speaker's skills;
  • and just get acquainted with your technology colleagues, chat in an informal and cozy atmosphere.

By the way, it's free. Surely, you have cases that will interest others. Tell us about your experience, you need the Russian community! Even with minimal experience of performances and completely without him send applications for participation. We will dispel fears and tell the details.
