A brief overview of significant innovations in the world of Bluetooth beacons



The main use for Bluetooth beacons is to solve indoor positioning problems. Sometimes, a banal finding in the signal radius is enough. Sometimes, you need to apply a Kalman filter . The range of application contexts is also wide, and varies from use in sales (English) to help (English) to people with visual impairments.

Over the past couple of weeks, two of the largest players in this market have released new devices that significantly expand the possibilities for developing solutions based on this technology. It is noteworthy that both firms have a vision that goes beyond the application of this technology in smartphone applications.

Unfortunately, these events passed unnoticed by Habr. Under Habrakat, an attempt by your humble servant to fill this gap.

The card beacon has modest dimensions, only 2mm thick. In addition to the BLE module itself, RFID is also integrated, with the ability to add NFC. The battery life is 8 months.
