Future in event organization

Good afternoon, dear visitors Habr.

In this post I want to tell you about new technologies in organizing events. It just so happened that six years ago I, a certified IT specialist, a system administrator with 10 years of experience, has greatly changed my life and opened my own business, the Knyazev Holidays Agency . How this happened is a long story, and not at all for this post. Let me just say that the new work captured me entirely, and over the years we have grown from a small start-up company to a leading agency for organizing corporate and private events in St. Petersburg. And, despite the fact that the new business was quite far from the IT world, all these years I read. Habr tried to apply in the new work all the innovations that he found to use all the devices and gadgets available to me. I would like to tell you about some of them, for example, about a machine making clouds of a given shape, or about dancing robots.

Dancing robots


Dancing android robots are able to synchronously move to the rhythm of a melody, perform acrobatic stunts, build various figures. Since the size of the robots in height is only about 40 centimeters, the show can be held on any site. Various musical compositions are available to choose from, under which the robots dance.



Polyvizor


Polyvizor is an airborne 3D screen, or scientifically, a pseudo holographic 3D video projector. The technology is based on mist-saturated airflow of small thickness. The image created by the installation, even near it seems to be voluminous, although it is not a hologram. In the context of mass and private events, it is very important that no special glasses are required to view the 3D image on the polyvizor screen.



Video mapping


Video mapping is a technology of projecting video onto the surface of various objects - buildings, structures, taking into account their geometry and location in space. Also does not require special points from the audience.



Interactive floors, walls, tables, bar counters


Interactive projection system allows a person to interact with the surrounding space with the help of body movements, change it, managing the content at its discretion. An infrared camera is responsible for tracking the movement of shadows on the surface of the floor, infrared illumination is responsible for color stabilization. The brain of the whole structure is, of course, a computer that receives a signal from the camera and changes the picture transmitted by the projector.



Projection scenery


Projection walls, standing around the perimeter of the room, allow you to create any decoration atmosphere. The feeling of hitting the invented world, moving in space, interactive with the guest - all this became possible. To organize a party on the moon, to depict a theatrical performance on a mysterious island or go back to the world of the seas and oceans today can be in no time and without any consequences.



Cloud Logos


On a special equipment - a cloud generator - clouds of a given shape, color and size are produced, whether it is your company's logo or heart for your sweetheart.



Live projection


Virtual characters created with the help of a projector can be not only unusual advertising, but also an element of the design of any event, or both.



Light paper


Today, light paper is one of the most convenient, affordable, and highly effective ways to decorate a room, be it a banquet hall or an advertising sign; it is widely used as part of the technology for creating animated light installations that are highlighted by banners and promotional materials. The photo paper is absolutely safe: it does not heat up during operation, does not emit ultraviolet radiation and does not contain fragile parts. Moreover, such paper can be cut and give it the desired shape.



Other


Quite a lot of innovations that we use in organizing events remain outside the scope of this article. Just list some of them. You can order a 3D movie or video about your event, use the tablets as a menu, or for presentation and organizational purposes. Actively use QR codes, including those drawn in the theme of the event, for invitations, to orient guests on the site. And many many others. The main thing to remember is that the most important is the thoughtful concept of the event. And you need to ensure that all the details fit organically into this general concept.

You can easily find information on how we make holidays, photo reports about our events, our clients' reviews, as well as our address, telephone, and many other useful information on our website: www.knyazev-spb.ru

Thank you for attention.
