Top 7 DDoS Protection Services for Better Security

Do not allow DDoS attacks to threaten your business operations with loss of reputation and financial loss . Take advantage of cloud DoS protection to prevent hacking.



According to the latest data Incapsula , DDoS costs the business $ 40,000 per hour .



The following tools will help you calculate losses due to DDoS:

  • Cost of a DDoS attack by Akamai
  • DDoS downtime cost calculator from Incapsula
  • Calculate your DDoS attack cost from Neustar

DDoS attacks are unpredictable, and more recently, terribly dangerous. Power can be in the range of 300 to 500 Gbps .

Crackers have resorted to different methods to attack your business, including:

  • fragmentation of UDP packets;
  • DNS, NTP, UDP, SYN, SSPD, ACK flood;
  • Chargen attack;
  • TCP anomalies.

So you need to defend not only against DDoS attacks at the seventh level , but also to provide protection at all levels.

According to the latest AKAMAI security report , most DDoS attacks are made from China.



Let's look at cloud solutions for small and medium businesses, with which you can connect protection against DDoS attacks in a matter of minutes .

  • spoofing;

I advise you to try Incapsula to protect your business at all levels ( website , infrastructure and DNS ).

AKAMAI


AKAMAI is a market leader in security and CDN services. More recently, AKAMAI set a record by repelling an attack with a capacity of 620 Gbps .



AKAMAI KONA DDoS Defender shields against DoS / DDoS attacks on network peripherals.

KONA is based on the AKAMAI intelligent platform to protect websites , and a global security center that operates around the clock responds to attacks.

This cloud solution protects against all known types of attacks, including traffic encryption. AKAMAI is widely represented worldwide: AKAMAI has more than 1,300 sites in more than 100 countries .

AKAMAI protects the infrastructure of the entire data center with Prolexic Routed or Prolexic Connect.

Cloudflare


Cloudflare provides basic protection against DDoS attacks in FREE and PRO packets. However, for enhanced protection against DDoS attacks (at levels 3, 4 and 7), you need to buy a Business or Enterprise package.

The cost of Cloudflare services is fixed , which means that no matter how large-scale attacks you encounter, you will pay the same amount every month.

Cloudflare is trusted by such famous companies as Nasdaq, DigitalOcean , Cisco, Salesforce, Udacity and others.

CloudFlare network is available in 102 data centers with a total bandwidth of more than 10 Tbps , that is, it will cope with any types of DDoS attacks, including:

  • at levels 3, 4 and 7;
  • DNS amplification;
  • DNS reflection;
  • SMURF;
  • ACK.

If you are under a DDoS attack, you can contact the around-the-clock Cloudflare hotline.

Sucui


SUCURI - a specialized cloud solution to protect a variety of sites, including WordPress, Joomla, Drupal, Magento, Microsoft.Net, etc.



Protection against DDoS attacks is included in the antivirus and firewall package. If you need comprehensive site protection , then Website Antivirus is suitable for you, which protects you against threats on the network, including DDoS attacks, and also includes the following services:

  • detection and removal of malicious code;
  • security control;
  • speed optimization;
  • protection against brute force;
  • protection against zero-day vulnerability;
  • protection from unwanted bots.

SUCURI detects and blocks level 3,4 and 7 attacks. The cost of maintenance starts from 19.88 dollars per month.

Alibaba


Alibaba's Anti-DDoS Pro helps protect against DDoS attacks. Anti-DDoS Pro repels powerful attacks up to 2 Tbps and supports TCP / UDP / HTTP / HTTPS protocols.

Anti-DDoS can be used not only when hosted on Alibaba, but also for AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, etc.

MYRA


Myra DDoS protection is a fully automated solution for websites, DNS servers, web applications and infrastructure. It is fully compatible with all types of CMS and e-commerce systems.
