Land Rover Transparent Hood



Land Rover introduced a prototype technology that allows the driver to see the surface of the road, which under normal conditions is hidden by the hood. This is achieved using webcams and projectors. The effect is simply spectacular, as you can see. Practically indispensable technology in off-road conditions.

The Transparent Hood is part of a package of promising technologies that Land Rover will demonstrate at the 2014 New York Auto Show. In addition to displaying the surface under the hood, the system also displays on the display a number of parameters, including speed, angle, roll angle, steering position and driving mode.
