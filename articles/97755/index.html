Samsung Xcover: $ 100, shockproof, 67 days of battery life

Not everyone is susceptible to expensive phones like the iPhone or the latest HTC smartphones. For some people, priorities are somewhat different - for example, high reliability is important, long battery life and ease of use (I called, I counted on a calculator).



If you belong to the second type of people, you may notice the new Samsung Xcover E2370 handset. The device is completely simple, it is difficult to expect anything from it other than simple calls and SMS-ok, its external part is perfectly strengthened, and the phone itself has an IP54 certificate, indicating the device‚Äôs resistance to dust and moisture. To top it off, the phone provides you with 67 days of standby time and 22 hours, almost 24 hours a day, in continuous talk mode, so that you will not be left without a connection on your next long expedition (or your next business trip).

It is also useful to say that the phone weighs 115 grams, its thickness is 19 millimeters. It supports GSM / EDGE, is equipped with a TFT-display with a resolution of 128 x 160 pixels, Bluetooth, a simple camera, 5 MB of internal memory and support for microSD memory cards (up to 2 GB). Summer residents will be pleased with the presence of FM-radio. :)

The exact release date has not yet been reported, but we can assume that the phone will appear in Europe next month and will cost about $ 100.
