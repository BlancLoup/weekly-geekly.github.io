Extreme Networks Solutions Workshop

Tomorrow there will be a training workshop on Extreme network solutions. Initially, there will be a presentation with a general overview of new products, in particular, the industry's first solutions based on 802.11ac Wave 2, and already from 10.30 (in Kiev) there will be a workshop on setting up Extreme Networks hardware.

10.30 - 11.30 - Configuring the controller (management, configuring the wireless environment VNS, Guest Portal).
11:45 - 13:00 - Controller configuration (access points, Radar, Logs and Reports).

The workshop will be based on real-life cases / projects and focused on helping network engineers work.
