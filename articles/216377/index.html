‚ñå Well, suppose convinced, but why do you need such a huge resolution? Print posters A0?


I dare to suggest that the above examples closed the topic of the impossibility of the existence of such a huge resolution. The logical question is: where are so many megapixels?

In short, the main advantages of this approach are:
  1. Oversampling technology, which allows you to make a 41-megapixel 5MP frame with a photo with an extremely small amount of noise. From 6-7 ‚Äúnoisy‚Äù pixels, 1 ‚Äúclean‚Äù pixel is combined, which contains a kind of arithmetic average of ‚Äúnoisy‚Äù pixels. This is best demonstrated in the following video:


  2. The ability to perform digital zoom up to 5X when shooting photos and videos without losing quality and at the same time not doing a giant Galaxy Zoom
  3. Opportunity to crop a photo after a photograph has been taken
  4. The same oversampling allows you to shoot HD video with 6x zoom without any loss of quality.
