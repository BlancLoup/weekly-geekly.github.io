Google Media: materials of the most interesting media on your mobile device

Massie Shore, Product Manager

We want users to have fast and convenient access to all information on the web, including materials from various media. Now all owners of mobile devices based on Android and iOS can download the Russian-language version of Google Media application (Google Currents) and get free access to online publications, including Russian ones! And for publishers, we have developed a special Google Producer platform that allows you to upload your own texts, videos, images and more to the application.

Google Media for users
Are you interested in popular science topics, sports or fashion - in the Google Media application you will find media materials for every taste. Simply download the app from Google Play or the App Store , log into your Google account and subscribe to the publications that interest you. Among the publications in Russian: cosmo.ru, gazeta.ru, National Geographic, Rossiyskaya Gazeta and many others. In the library of foreign media you will find such publications as Popular Science, CBS Sports, Men's Health, The Atlantic.





In Google Media, you can also add RSS, video and photo feeds, open posts from Google+, as well as your subscriptions from Google Reader. In addition, you will have the opportunity to view not only the content of selected publications, but also popular materials from other media.

As soon as you select the desired publication in the Google Media application, the most recent content begins to be automatically uploaded to your mobile device. In this update takes up a minimum of memory and does not give a large load on the battery. Even if you find yourself without Internet access (for example, on a plane), you can still view the latest content - you can even read pre-downloaded texts and watch pictures in Google Media!

Google Media gives you access to publisher content from around the world. If you see a globe icon on the menu, it means that the publisher has integrated Google Translate into their content. Just click on this icon, and the article will be translated into one of 38 languages, including Russian.

Google Media Publishers
Publishers can independently and free download and process content in Google Media using the special Google Producer platform. It does not matter whether the materials are from a small regional news agency, a public organization website or a leading TV holding.

Publishers determine whether to make content accessible to an international audience and whether to translate it automatically into other languages. Many publications are actively using these features of Google Producer . Among them: Forbes, The Guardian, LaStampa and many others.

In addition, publishers can link their account to the analytics tool Google Analytics. So they can better understand what content is the most popular, from which devices users read articles, in which country readers are and a lot of other data.
