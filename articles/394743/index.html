Intel invites you to Moscow Maker Faire

July 9-10, Moscow will host a real holiday for everyone who loves to create interesting, intellectual things with his own hands or has long dreamed of doing this. Maker Faire is a festival of science and modern technology for inventors, engineers and geeks of all ages. This is a science fair, an exhibition of modern technologies, and an interactive art gallery, but, above all, a place for communication between DIY makers and enthusiasts. For two days, the best representatives of the community will talk about the present and the future, DIY and open source, teach you new skills and show off their developments in all areas where you can put your hands and head on.



Intel invites you to participate in this DIY extravaganza and use the Genuino 101 and Edison boards as the basis for your products. The authors of the most interesting projects will be awarded the Golden Electrical Tape , and the winner will receive a ticket to Berlin for the big Maker Faire festival. Intel will also award their prizes.

Moscow Mini Maker Faire will be held on the campus of NITU "MISiS" (Leninsky Prospekt, 4, metro station "Oktyabrskaya"). Participation is completely free . Register for the festival .
