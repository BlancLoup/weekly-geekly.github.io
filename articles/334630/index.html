How to create a business proposal, based on the result of R & D and technology



The Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, in partnership with eNano (RUSNANO group), opened the recruitment group for the new advanced training program ‚ÄúCommercialization of R & D Results and Technologies‚Äù . The program provides practical tools for assessing the commercial prospects of research and development and creating a business proposal based on them.

Training takes place in an online format from August 21 to October 13.
You will work with your own or educational project under the guidance of the author and teacher of the program:

  • step by step work out the potential market, product / service and its competitive advantages;
  • Perform a patent search on the subject of the project, analyze and create a register and portfolios of intellectual property rights;
  • draw up a diagram of a complex of technological systems ensuring the production of a selected promising product or service;
  • highlight key types of activities and major partners in the stages of product creation and its promotion to the market;
  • Prepare a description of your technology / service in the form of a business proposal to a potential partner.

The author and teacher of the program is Vladimir Alexandrovich Antonets, Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor of the Basic Department of RUSNANO at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology and the Department of Economics of the Faculty of Economics of the RANEPA, leading researcher at the Institute of Applied Physics of the Russian Academy of Sciences, international expert in the field of commercialization of research and development, RF Regional Technology Incubator. He has more than 200 scientific publications, the author of more than 20 educational and methodical works, electronic courses and textbooks.

Training dates: August 21 - October 13, 2017
Tuition: 18 thousand rubles
Program size: 72 academic hours, 8 weeks, 9 academic hours per week
Form of study: correspondence using distance learning systems. Training takes place on the platform partner program www.edunano.ru . Listeners take part in webinars, study the materials of the e-course, perform individual practical work under the guidance of a teacher.
Certification: in remote format.
The final document: MIPT certificate of advanced training of the established sample.
