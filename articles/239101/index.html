Keyboard for iOS 8 will allow typing phrases

Hi, Habr!

The other day, Techcrunch publish the news that a special keyboard Phraseboard will appear for iOS. Doesn't this remind you of anything?



Of course, this is the perfect keyboard!



Many, indeed, often use the same phrases when communicating. This keyboard will offer you expressions depending on the type of interlocutor. Colleagues - some phrases, friends - completely different. It also has a special section into which it will be possible to drive in your email addresses, links to pages in the social network and other data from letters and symbols that will be difficult to constantly type from scratch.

Phraseboard is already available for download from the Apple Store.
