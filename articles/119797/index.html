HackDay: it's time to act


Have you visited five events to improve the business model of your project? The experts have washed your brain, and now your project is completely different from what it began with? Have you even written a business plan?

Now what?

Stop time. Enough presentations in PowerPoint. Stop planning and changing the model.

It's time to act. Show the investor a product. PRODUCT! Make, show and run.

Do you have an idea, but no team? Not sure where to start?

Go from thinking to action:

1. Come to HackDay.
2. Submit your project to the audience.
3. Outfit your team with programmers, designers, marketers, lawyers, chefs. Or join the project of interest.
4. In a short time, develop the first version of your product.
5. Show results to professionals and investors.
6. Start, finally.

HackDay # 16 - May 28-29. St. Petersburg.
Participation is free. People for the team are attached. Investors on demofeste - too.
It is necessary to publish on the site information about the project, and who is required in it.
Professionals of all specializations are in demand at the event. We are waiting for everyone.
