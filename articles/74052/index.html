My Mir@Mail.Ru: 100% of the money to developers

The social network My World@Mail.Ru has great news for application developers. Starting today, a new program of interaction with them starts. Her key points are:

1. Application creators are paid 100% (!) Of the money they earn
2. The authors of the best products will have the opportunity to conduct free advertising campaigns within the social network.
3. In addition, Mail.Ru is ready to make financial investments in interesting projects and teams, as well as provide them with technical and consulting support.

By the way, in the near future, My World@Mail.Ru will release a new version of its API, which will expand the possibilities for the application to interact with the user profile, as well as support for applications created using various technologies (previously it was possible to use only flash). Also in development is an automated system for receiving user payments with convenient integration into applications, a wide range of available methods of depositing funds and detailed statistics.

If you are interested in all this - write to Alexey Terekhov (app@corp.mail.ru).

We will be happy to answer your questions!
