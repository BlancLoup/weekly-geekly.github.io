Mobile legal system ipLex.Profi

Before the new year, the full mobile version of the Ukrainian legislation ipLex.Profi .

ipLex.Profi is designed to work on the following devices:
- Apple iPad tablets with iOS 5.0 and higher;
- Apple iPhone smartphones with iOS 5.0 or higher;
- tablets with the operating system Android 4.0 and above;
- smartphones with the operating system Android 4.0 and higher, the screen resolution of at least 320x480.

The application has free guest access based on the basic legislation of Ukraine, but for full access registration is required on the site iplex.com.ua and a paid login password. Full access includes the entire regulatory framework, court decisions, reference books, analytics, etc., with a volume of more than 8 million documents.

The first release of ipLex.Profi can be downloaded from the App Store and Google Play .

A brief overview of the capabilities of the system.
Search:
- search in one line with highlighting of the found fragments;
- extended request form for requisites;
- filters search results by details;
- Sort search results by date.
Connections
Regulatory documents and court decisions are linked by forward and backward links, that is:
- from the text of a regulatory act or a court decision it is possible to ‚Äúpoint‚Äù go to the necessary article of the mentioned document;
- to the article of the law are selected referring judicial decisions and regulations ("clip" in the margins on the left);
- lists of related decisions are given to the court decision, thus it is possible to track the passage of the case by court instances.
Work with the document.
Document handling service includes:
- details and list of publications of the document;
- previous and future editions of the regulatory act, history of its changes;
- detailed hierarchical structure of the document;
- contextual search inside the text;
- copying the fragment to the buffer for further use in other applications.
Own offline database.
Selected documents can be saved directly on a PC or mobile device and work with them without the Internet inside the application in the offline mode.
