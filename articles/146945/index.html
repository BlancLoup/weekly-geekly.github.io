Official VLC for Android appeared on Google Play



Not so long ago, developers of the VLC cross-platform video player celebrated an important event - a billion downloads of the distribution package of the program. This number includes the download of sources, as well as the download of the already compiled distribution under Windows and MacOS. Now fans of this player have another reason to celebrate - the official VLC application for Android has appeared on Google Play.

Developers warn that this is a beta version, moreover, unstable. But users who have already tested the player claim that the program is fully functional. Now playing video through this player is possible on most Android devices that have Android version 2.1 and higher.

It is worth recalling that the player itself was developed by the student team as an educational project. Now VLC is a very popular GPL player.

Actually, you can download it here: Google Play .
