How to withdraw money from the App Store to Payoneer card

Not three years, but only a few months are waiting for the promised. In our last post, we promised to tell you about the withdrawal from the App Store and, here we are.

Many Payoneer cardholders make a living by the hard work of an application developer. And before each developer (or a company thereof), sooner or later the question arises of the withdrawal of funds earned. Payoneer’s US Payment Service allows you to receive payments from Apple, PayPal and Google and many others directly to your Payoneer MasterCard.
The process is very simple: if you already have a Payoneer card, just contact us and ask to connect to the US Payment Service.

If you still do not have a card, you can apply at www.payoneer.com/ru

With the opening of the US Payment Service, you will receive US payment information (account number and routing number ABA), which you can use to receive funds from many US companies.
It remains only to add the account details to the App Store account and get money on the card.

To get paid from, for example, iTunes you need:
1. Go to the iTunes Connect main page and select the “Contracts, Tax, and Banking” section


2. In the “Bank Info” column, select the “Set Up” menu.


3. Select “Add Bank Account”.
Next, in the line “bank country, select“ United States ”, then enter the number of the routing number.
The routing and account number details are in the US Payment Service menu on the My Account page in your Payoneer account.


4. Enter the account number, name, in the “account type” line, select “Checking”, in the “surrency” line: “USD - US Dollar”.


5. After adding the US Payment Service details, return to the “Contracts, Tax, and Banking” page and select “Set Up” in the “Tax Info” column.

6. It is necessary to fill in the tax return form. Click “Set Up” in the “US Tax Forms” column.


7. Fill in all required fields and click “Confirm”.


All payments sent to your US Payment Service details will automatically be loaded onto your Payoneer MasterCard, which can be used worldwide, wherever MasterCard is accepted for payment.

You can also use the US Payment Service to accept payments from other US companies, such as Google. Owners of developer accounts on Google Play, who have the ability to withdraw to an account in a US bank, can receive payments on their cards.
If you have a Google account, but there is no possibility to withdraw money to the US account, do not forget to visit this topic and leave your feedback for Google.

You can contact our support service in Russian by mail at support (a) payoneer.ru; with the department for working with Russian partners - partners (a) payoneer.ru.
