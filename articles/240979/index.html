How the data was collected

It is logical that the skill is of interest, if the demand for it greatly exceeds the supply and at the same time the demand is also quite important. That is, the importance of the skill depends on two criteria:
  • 1st criterion: the ratio of demand to supply.
  • 2nd criterion: frequent occurrence of the skill in the requirements of customers.
