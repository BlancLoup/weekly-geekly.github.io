Opera 11.50 - what everyone has been waiting for

In fact, it is almost three months at the current rate of release of new versions of browsers. But any wait someday ends with a final release. Therefore - we meet the new version of Opera 11.50. At first glance, there are not so many innovations, as perhaps someone would like to, but everything is simple: firstly, there are still innovations, and very interesting ones, and secondly, users may have noticed that we have they were very closely involved in fixing bugs, improving the stability of the browser, in general, they were actively working with reviews and bug reports of users. And - of course, I almost forgot: the new version is again faster than previous versions, and much more advanced in terms of support for you-know-what web standards. So let's start with the most noticeable changes.

Password synchronization
You asked - we did. Now you can forget about manually transferring wand.dat from machine to machine after each browser update or adding new passwords. It is enough to turn on synchronization and tick the necessary checkbox.

I understand that there are quite a lot of people who relate to the security of their data with the utmost care. Anticipating their question I will say: yes, it is safe enough. You can read more about what and how happens during password synchronization here .

We already had an Express panel (who had forgotten - by the way, also our invention), relatively recently we added extensions (this is not our idea, I will not lie).
