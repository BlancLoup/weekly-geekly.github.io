Mobile application "Reader" is now for Android

About speech synthesis technology
The TTS speech synthesis engines used in the Reader are a proprietary development of the MDGs. They use the original hybrid implementation of several well-known technologies: Unit Selection and statistical modeling of speech parameters based on hidden Markov models (HMM). The first provides the natural timbre of the sound of the synthesized voice, and the second allows you to give the voice the necessary intonation, semantic and emotional coloring.
In addition, the MDG speech synthesizer is able to understand the meaning of the text, depending on the context: the large volume of the dictionary provides the correct interpretation in ambiguous situations and the correct pronunciation of abbreviations and abbreviations.
