Internet users called the most beautiful car


Internet users consider the most beautiful car in the world - Alfa Romeo Spider. This model was given preference by 100 thousand web users who took part in the survey on the popular Italian car portal.

Voting continued from September 1 to December 31, 2006. According to their results, the Alfa Romeo Spider is the absolute winner, having bypassed such competitors as the BMW 3 Series Coupe and Audi TT. This is reported by Prime-Tass .
