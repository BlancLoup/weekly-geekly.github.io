Asus PadFone video review



ASUS PadFone is a new Asus smartphone based on the Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 8260A dual-core processor with a frequency of 1500 MHz, manufactured using the 22 nm process technology. The smartphone uses a Super AMOLED display with a diagonal of 4.3 "and a resolution of 960x540. The smartphone is controlled by Android OS 4, has 1 GB of RAM onboard and 16/32 GB of internal memory.
An interesting novelty makes a special dock PadFone Station, with which the smartphone can be turned into a full-fledged tablet with a diagonal of 10.1 "and a screen resolution of 1280x800.
In addition to this, the keyboard pad ASUS PadFone Station Dock is also on sale, which turns the device into a netbook on the same principle as the Eee Pad Transformer.
