Work with color: useful tools, books, articles for web designers

Working with color is the first thing that any designer should be able to do. On the Internet, a huge amount of scattered information on this topic, I tried to collect the most useful in one collection. Most of the utility with a bias in web design.

Instruments




Color Lovers is an old and functional tool for selecting color schemes. Analogues - Colourcode , Color Scheme Designer and of course Kuler . There are a lot of similar sites, but these, in my opinion, are the most convenient.



Multicolr is a useful search service that can find photos on Flickr (more than 10 million under a Creative Commons license) using one or several colors.

MasterBundles is a service for searching graphic elements, fonts, stock photos, patterns, icons, logos, presentations, bundled with a huge discount.



Check My Colors - a service that checks the readability of the text on any site. On the screenshot - part of the results of Habr.



Sphere - the choice of colors in accordance with the theory of color, almost a scientific approach to the choice of palette

.

0to255 - helps subtly select the desired shade of color.



CSS Color Names - demonstrates all 147 color names in English.



Color Hexa is a small interactive color encyclopedia with useful features.



Color Blender - "mixes" two colors, finding an intermediate

Flat UI Colors is a simple service for choosing colors from the Flat UI kit from Designmodo.
