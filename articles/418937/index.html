Celebrating the 8th anniversary of the first release at GeekUniversity


Today GeekBrains is 8 years old, and over the past year we have been joined by 1.3 million users - the number of registrations on the portal has exceeded 3 million. We have many new curricula and professions, but our main achievement is GeekUniversity online university. It allows you to train middle-developers for a year and employ them in Mail.Ru Group and to our partners. Now 2,000 students are enrolled in 7 faculties, and at the end of August we are waiting for the first 50 graduates.


The project from two faculties launched in April last year. Now there are already seven faculties represented: web development, Python, Java, iOS and Android development, game development and information security. We focus on training in practice, students work on real projects and take electives from partners - MTS Bank, Wildberries and many others.
