What is the best way to participate in Imagine Cup Software Design, go to Australia and win

Approaching February 15, 2012 - the deadline for submission of applications for the competition of program projects Imagine Cup . In this regard, we increasingly hear from students something like the following: ‚ÄúYes, the Imagine Cup is very interesting, and we would like to go to the finals to Australia, but we don‚Äôt know at all where to start. Especially in order to help students avoid such doubts, come up with an idea and participate in the competition most effectively - we hold events Imagine Cup Student Camp. Such an event has already taken place in Moscow , and on February 11 it will be in St. Petersburg . In addition, especially for those who live far away - we will hold an abbreviated version of the event, Imagine Cup Virtual Student Camp on January 26 at 15:00 Moscow time .

Below, I would like to share my thoughts on how and where to start.


Go outside - do something!


First: it is better to do at least something , than to postpone "the next year" or "until the idea appears." Make a simple project this year, take part, do not win - you still get useful competitive experience, and next year it will be easier. Start small , and then gradually improve, bringing the project to a winning one. You will surely like it!

How to come up with an idea?


The theme of the competition is ‚Äúhow technologies help us solve the complex problems of our time‚Äù. If you are already programming for fun, and you have a project, but you do not know how to fit it under the theme of the competition - think three times! If you still do not know - write to me! Almost always you can slightly modify an interesting project for you under the theme of the competition .

If you do not have your own idea, I recommend, following the site of the Imagine Cup, to look at the problems of humanity formulated by the UN . In this case, consider them in the most general way: for example, problems of education, health problems, etc. Next, under each of the common problems, list in a column any specific problems, and below them - already ideas of specific projects.   3. We make a WP7 application to track well-being, doctor recommendations, etc. with a simple expert system and communication with the server, where for money you can get advice from a living doctor.
