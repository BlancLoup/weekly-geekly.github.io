POLL: If I were a Samsung developer, what device / technology / service would I bring to life?


Friends. We want to try new ways to communicate with the community and start with a fairly creative survey. Since Habrahabr is mostly inhabited by versatile, interesting, inventive and creative people, we want you to imagine yourself for a moment in the place of the main developer or creative director of Samsung and think about what device, technology or service our company has been able to make easier, more convenient and more interesting. As you know, Samsung Group is a multi-profile concern, so your offers can be from any sphere, so you can not stop your flight of thought only on gadgets and other electronics. That is why we did not specifically begin to do a traditional survey, so as not to limit your ideas and give you space to describe them. Any, even the most courageous, we are waiting for suggestions in the comments to this survey.
