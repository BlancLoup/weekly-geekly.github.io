Price and prospects Sony Ericsson XPeria X10

The other day, the official twitter of Sony Ericsson announced the final cost and packaging of the flagship smartphone XPeria X10. The device will be available closer to March at a price ... 24990 rubles! For this money, we get an Android-smartphone with 4 "inch widescreen, 1 GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon processor, 1 GB ROM, 256 MB RAM, 8 MPks camera with LED flash and autofocus, 16 GB microSD card, 1500 headset battery mA / h

It seems that this time the SE managed to surprise and keep the price of the device at a very decent level. The closest gigahertz competitor X10 - HTC HD2 costs 29990 rubles, and the Nexus One is not planned to be sold in Russia at all.

XPeria X10 will come with Android OS version 1.6 and its own development of SE - the Nexus software shell, which includes the unique Timespace and Mediascape applications. At the same time on the X10 you can already install a huge number of applications from the Android Market such as: Google Navigation , Google Goggles , Wikitude , Layar augmented reality application and many others. In the future, it is planned to update the firmware to version 2.X.

Whether the device will shoot at the Russian market or not - time will tell, but the starting price for iron of this level is very pleased. It seems that in the future we are waiting for the whole battle for the high-range market of Android-smartphones.
