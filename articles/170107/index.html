From Idea to Business in just 54 hours!

Startup Weekend is an entrepreneurial movement of international scope where developers, designers, businessmen and in general everyone who is interested in setting up their own business can test whether their idea is viable.
Businesspeople who have successfully launched their business have been invited to participate in the Startup Weekend as experts. During the weekend, they will work with the formed teams at the same table. Why is this event so special?
In addition to ordinary listeners, we invite engineers of Ukraine to meet and network for girls , this will allow not only to meet and see each other, but also to meet and unite with girls Facebook engineers for Ukrainian and international initiatives.

About speakers:

Goranka Bjedov is an infrastructure power engineer at Facebook, and she specializes in user server performance and capacity. Before joining the Facebook team, she worked as a performance engineer at Google, a reliability engineer at NetApp (a company specializing in network storage devices), and a manager of performance at AT & T Labs. Goranka has a doctorate degree in engineering from Clarkson University.

Kari Lee is the technical manager of the Facebook Search Division and focuses on the grammatical aspects of the Count Graph. Before taking a place in management, she was an engineer and worked in various teams of search infrastructure and interfaces. Previously worked for Google and Microsoft. Kari has a master's degree in computer science from Stanford University.

Mary Pimenova is an engineer on Facebook who deals with privacy issues, previously worked in a performance team. Mary has a bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of Washington.
