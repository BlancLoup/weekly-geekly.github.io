MBLTdev Live Broadcast

Good news!

Anyone who fails to attend the MBLTdev Second International Mobile Developers Conference on November 17 at Digital October, will be able to watch the event live !

This year's conference program includes reports for mobile developers and for managers of IT companies, as well as workshops for developers. Viewers of the online broadcast will be able to attend a round table on mobile ecosystems and hear reports from the best mobile developers and interviews with the most interesting speakers of the conference.



The conference will feature presentations by experts from Microsoft, Realm, Wunderlist, Parallels, Kaspersky Lab, Rambler & Co, Objc.io, Spotify, Soundcloud and many others. More information about the conference program can be found on the website.

Join the free live stream ! Beginning at 10:00 (MSK).

Conference organizers: e-Legion and RAEC
General Partner: Microsoft Russia
Partners: Kaspersky Lab, Avito.ru, Rambler & Co, reLeadgion
