Google Tech Talk: Crazy Attempt to Rewrite Windows from Scratch



On October 2, another Google Tech Talks event took place in Montreal.

More than 150 people came for the sole purpose - to listen to the report of Alex Ionescu , who is known to the Russian audience, first of all, as a co-author of the books of the Windows Internals series.

Alex's report was called " The Crazy Open Source Attempt To Rewrite Windows From Scratch " and is entirely devoted to the ReactOS operating system. The speech is full of interesting details and contains a practically exhaustive report on the history of creation and the current state of the ReactOS project.
