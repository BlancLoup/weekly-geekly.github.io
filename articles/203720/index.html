MakeItLab & CoolRF at the International Exhibition Center



Want to whistle too in a freshly-3D-printed whistle? Come to our booth at Discover ICT. The exhibition takes place these days (November 26-28, 2013) in Ekaterinburg-Expo.

If you arrive at the exhibition early in the morning, you can catch this deserted view:



At the front desk, our robber head will kindly watch you with his sly, narrowed eyes:



After the check-in desk we go straight ahead, undressing in the wardrobe. We go straight past the cafe, turn left. On the left side of the pavilion, next to 2GIS, we see our tables with two 3D printers:



Interesting at our booth


Today we mainly print whistles and bracelets. Some printed models are given to particularly inquisitive and smiling smiles. On the first day, the MakeItLab handing out is chronically short ; one of two printers is not working. With all this, the stand enjoys considerable popularity.
