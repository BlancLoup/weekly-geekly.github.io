Sports bag for geek

When my sports bag, which is a Chinese noname, finally became useless, I was faced with the task of buying a new one. As one of the main selection criteria, it was possible to carry in a bag not only a sports uniform, but also at least a tablet, and even better, an ultrabook. On Geektimes there are a couple of articles about choosing a backpack for a geek ( 1 and 2 ). And one was even found on Habrahabr . But I just needed a sports bag, and therefore, having discovered the great and terrible, I began to search.

First mistakes and getting on the right track


At first I began to conduct my search by entering the phrase " sport bag " in the search field. However, Amazon immediately told me that the gym bag would be right. Walking through a couple of pages, I realized that the sample should be narrowed down, and asked the search term " gym bag tablet ".
