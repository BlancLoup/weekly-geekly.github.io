Extra-Terrestrial Excavations

The Extra-Terrestrial game, released in 1982 by Atari for its own Atari 2600 console, is recognized as one of the worst games in history. According to the legends, it was decided to dig all unclaimed copies of this product. All data associated with this action was hidden.




This week, thanks to excavations conducted by Microsoft and its Xbox Live division, burial has ceased to be a legend. The first photo with cartridges extracted from the ground was published in its microblog by Xbox Live program director Larry Hryb. A few more shots of the surviving copies appeared in the official blog of Xbox Wire.
