TOP-10 banks whose cards were paid in the Wahren in the IV quarter of 2009

MoneyNews, in conjunction with the Interbank Electronic Delivery and Payment System, Portmone.com conducted a regular quarterly survey to determine the ratings of banks whose cardholders pay for purchases and services in the Ukrainian segment of the Internet.
The study analyzed data on transactions for the IV quarter of 2009.

The rating results are reflected in the TOP-10 Ukrainian banks below.
  1. RaiffeisenBank Aval 33.8% (0)
  2. PrivatBank 18.7% (0)
  3. UkrSotsBank 9.9% (0)
  4. OschadBank 5.6% (+2)
  5. OTR Bank 4.6% (-1)
  6. UkrsibBank 3.5% (0)
  7. Ukreximbank 3.0% (-1)
  8. Finance and Credit 2.4% (0)
  9. PUMB 1.7% (0)
  10. Universal bank 1.4% (+2)

* Note: the numbers in brackets indicate the movement of bank positions compared to the previous rating:
(0) - the position of the bank in the rating has not changed;
(+1) - the position of the bank has risen in the rating;
(-1) - the position of the bank dropped in the rating.
