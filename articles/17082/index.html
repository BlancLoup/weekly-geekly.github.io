Online game developers are joining

Russian online game developers Nival Online , IT Territory , TimeZero and NIKITA.Online announced their merger into Astrum Online Entertainment holding. Using common resources and developments, companies are going to strengthen together in the domestic and foreign markets, justifying the status of the largest developer of online games in the country. The plans of the holding - by the end of next year to increase its market share from 45% to 65%.

"Pie" of the Russian online gaming market is becoming more attractive: now its volume is about $ 80 million with a growth rate of 100% per year. As a plus, it causes a keen interest of investors, as a minus - foreign developers are striving to remove their share of the cream and to compete with them alone is already becoming difficult.
