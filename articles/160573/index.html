Opera, together with TIM Brazil, presented a new mobile application store

According to the agreement on the basis of the Opera Mobile Store, a new Brazilian mobile app store was created - TIM App Shop . To launch a new store, in addition to its own platform, the Opera Mobile Store , whose office opened in Odessa in the summer of 2012, provided the TIM network with a collection of several thousand mobile applications specially selected for Brazilian users.
