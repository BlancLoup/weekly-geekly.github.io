Speaking in a dry technical language, ITAM (IT Asset Management) is a comprehensive solution for physical accounting, financial control and following contractual obligations throughout the entire life cycle of assets: from their acquisition and redistribution to disposal.

Today, when information technologies are the most important part of running a business, this area is expensive. Indeed, one of the main tasks of the company management is cost optimization. Therefore, an understanding of where the money goes when investing in IT assets is now particularly relevant.

One of the solutions to help analyze and reduce costs is ITAM (IT Asset Management) technology. Its component SAM (Software Asset Management) allows you to achieve a really high level of return on investment (ROI) - up to 350%.

IT assets: good and different


Let's see right away what we mean by IT assets in general?

IT assets can be divided into two groups: tangible and intangible assets. Tangible assets are any IT equipment - all PCs, laptops, servers, phones, printers, fax machines, etc. Accordingly, intangible is software with documents confirming its legality and processes ensuring the life cycle of any such solution within the company.

Of course, special attention should be paid to the management of software assets SAM (Software Asset Management). In the classical sense, these are business processes that regulate the life cycle of software in a company, which is not only in the area of ‚Äã‚ÄãIT department management.

At the moment, a large number of companies have finally come to understand that establishing order in the field of software is a necessity for business. The management of medium and large companies are increasingly having questions about the quality and quantity of software being acquired.
