What was first a simple messaging among friends, eventually grew into a separate application, which, in turn, is rapidly becoming the main communication tool not only on a personal level, but also on a business level.

Now there are 600 million Messenger users, new features appear every month - first curtsy towards XMPP, then stickers and the ability to make audio calls, then video calls, and just the other day, the ability to share geolocation and send the interlocutor a place on the map.
