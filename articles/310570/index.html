And finally, a few words about the duality property of polyhedra. If the Schl√§fli Symbol is written in the reverse order, then we also get the correct polyhedron, only dual to the original one. This is a very important feature that helps calculate the size of the edges and angles of regular polyhedra. Geometrically, duality means that the vertices of one polyhedron abut against the centers of the flat faces of the second dual polyhedron to the first.
