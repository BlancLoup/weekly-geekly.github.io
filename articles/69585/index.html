Asus Eee Keyboard in October

Asus has announced that the Asus Eee Keyboard is expected to be on sale from the beginning of the year. According to information received by PCWorld.com from the company's top management, a unique device will appear on the shelves of North America and Europe at the very beginning of October 2009.

Let me remind you that the Eee Keyboard is a keyboard with a built-in computer and a 5-inch touchscreen located to the right of the main panel with buttons. "Keyboard" refers to both wired and wireless connection to a video device. The "bundled" wireless interfaces Eee Keyboard - Wi-Fi 802.11b / g / n, Bluetooth 2.0 and Ultra Wideband HDMI (high-definition multimedia interface). Not forgotten and wired interfaces: USB 2.0, VGA and HDMI, microphone and headphone jacks are present. The Eee Keyboard uses an Intel Atom 1.6 GHz processor, with 1 GB of RAM and SSD (16 or 32 GB).

Despite the fact that before the appearance on the market there are literally a couple of weeks, the price of the Eee Keyboard has not yet been made public.
