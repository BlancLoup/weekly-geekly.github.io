Google Pac-Man on your site



Inspired by the topic of Pac-Man forever and having spent some of the morning playing catch up with malicious ghosts , I thought, why not post the game on my website and please the modest audience.

A short googling led me to the Google Pacman project.

Actually it remained to simply unpack the source in the right place and ready. If you wish, you can embed the game in any place on your site.

Now you can play Pac-Man on your site.

The rules of the game are the same. Option A: wait 10 seconds and the game starts automatically. Option B: click the button "Insert a token" to play in single player mode. Option C: press the button "Insert a token" two times to play together or in two hands (the second Pac-Man's control keys are ASDW).
