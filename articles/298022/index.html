Why should you set goals? 7 reasons



Why should you set goals? What are the benefits of living with clearly defined objectives and a plan? In fact, properly formulated goals give us a lot of advantages, allowing us to eventually make our lives fuller and more interesting, but most importantly, by meeting our dreams, we are not wasting our days.

And just the first step to this is to set a goal.

Strange as it may seem, some people are afraid to make plans and set clearly defined tasks. They probably associate the latter with something new and unknown, with obstacles that will have to be overcome, with difficulties in obtaining new knowledge and skills, with responsibility towards themselves and others, with difficult work on mistakes, as well as with the possible fear of defeat and shame for not being able to achieve their goals.

So, to dispel all possible doubts about the usefulness of goal setting, let's see what advantages we get:


1. Clearly defined tasks allow us to take control of our own lives . The goal gives the direction vector, and, like the navigator, helps to go through life. How are you going to achieve what you want, if you have not even set yourself specific tasks? Moreover, aspirations help us determine what is our priority, and then focus our attention and strength on the most important things, rather than dissipate into unnecessary things. If you set yourself long-term goals, understanding what you want to come to will help you on the path to dreams to make the right decisions and make the right choice.
