Tesla Roadster is already in stores for $ 98,000

Among the investors of Tesla Motors, a startup located in Silicon Valley, are Google founders Sergey Brin & Larry Page, the second employee and the first president of eBay Jeff Skoll. What is so interesting Tesla Roadster?



* 100% electric
* 0-100 km / h - less than 4 seconds, and without changing gears
* more than 300 horsepower
* 356 km from the battery
* fare about a cent per mile

Trains write about the indescribable sensations of a silent, evenest non-decreasing acceleration after touching the gas. And about the indispensable disgrace waiting for any lumbering Ferrari or Porsche, who made a mistake, stopping next to Tesla at a traffic light.

Motor efficiency on average 90%. For comparison, internal combustion engines have an efficiency of about 20%. It is also cool: during braking, kinetic energy is not wasted, but a generator is started, which, braking the machine, generates energy and saves it for the future.

The local ruler, Arnold Schwarzenegger, was one of the first customers. But the very first copy received Elon Musk. He invested the first $ 7.5M into this company.
