TimePad.ru - assistant in organizing events



Hi, habra people! Today I want to tell you about the service, which I have been working with for a long time with my friends, but there was no good reason to talk about it here. Our project is called TimePad , and a good reason is our participation in the RIW Innovation Alley and plans to make RIW more fun :)

But about everything in order.

What is TimePad and who needs it?


Our mission: "We help make events better, and their organization is easier." At the moment, TimePad can:
  • Create event pages
  • Organize the registration process for events
  • Collect and store email addresses of all who may be interested in the event
  • Send invitations and announcements

It seems that there is nothing super-new in this, and there are quite a few services that cope with each of these tasks better than us. But our main feature is not in the tricky functional, but in its refinement for specific tasks (organization of events). The second, no less important feature is clarity and ease of use for any Ksyusha and any Anatoly Prokofyevich, who are involved in organizing events and are not very familiar with the Internet.


We do not set a goal to satisfy the needs of absolutely everyone. Sites, one way or another working with events, appear almost every month and competing with everyone is a rather unpromising occupation. In our developments, we focus on professional event organizers - those who conduct public lectures, seminars, trainings, conferences, and who do it all the time.

At first impressions, this approach is justified. Now the system is used by about 150 organizations that work with almost 30,000 audiences. And this is just the beginning.
