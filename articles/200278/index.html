Several captain principles of bandl formation:
  • a bundle should cost less than all its components separately;
  • a bundle should not be cheaper than its most expensive item;
  • the bundle should be evenly assembled, it should not contain objects that are very different in price in one direction or another;
  • the bundle should be useful to the user, its components should not contradict each other (no substitute products, but no complement products);
  • The pricing principles of the bundle should be shown to the user as transparently as possible;
  • Do not be greedy - the bundle should be cool, and everything else - idle talk.
