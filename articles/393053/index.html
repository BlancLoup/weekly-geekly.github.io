Face the Earth and Uranus? Nothing is easier - use the Universe Sandbox

Simulator creator of stars and planets in action



Universe Sandbox is a realistic space and gravity simulator that allows you to create new worlds, juggle with planets, and arrange disasters of a truly cosmic scale. For the first time this game (was it a game?) Appeared in 2008, and now it has become available on Steam Early Access. This is the latest version, Universe Sandbox 2 , where there is a virtual reality mode.

Here you can study the influence of existing objects of the Solar system on each other. Well, for example, send Earth on a long flight to Uranus, and then see what happens if the Earth collides with this gas giant.
