MiCoach Fit Smart: Adidas minimalistic fitness tracker



Recently, the company Adidas announced the emergence of a new model of fitness tracker, called MiCoach Fit Smart. This is not a smart watch, to which many have already managed to get used to, but a fitness tracker, which, according to the developers, need to be worn on the arm only during workouts.

The device can record the pulse, the number of calories burned during the workout, the running / walking speed, the distance traveled and the average stride length.

Design and features





The tracker is quite light, its weight is only 58 grams. Body material - plastic and silicone. There are two color versions of the tracker: white and black.

The display of the device is diode, no high resolutions, bright colors and just what we got used to. In general, most of the information is transmitted by the tracker to the smartphone, where all this can be seen in the detailed layout. But some information, such as the distance traveled, can also be shown on the device display.

In addition to the ‚Äúdisplay‚Äù, the MiCoach Fit Smart tracker was also equipped with an LED strip placed on the side. The brightness of the glow and the color of the strip show the intensity of the workout.

MiCoach Personal Trainer



It is worth noting that the device can be considered as a personal trainer. The fact is that the base contains about 100 different training plans (all this is on a smartphone that synchronizes with the clock). During the first training session (it is the same for everyone, offered when the device is first started), the watch keeps track of the indicators of the human body, and then makes a recommendation on the type of training.



In general, all this is standard for MiCoach applications.

Price



Sales start in the USA on July 17, and in Russia - on August 15. Estimated price - 199 dollars / 179 pounds / 199 euros (US, UK, Europe, respectively).
