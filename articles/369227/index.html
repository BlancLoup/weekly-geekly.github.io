No matter how much I like to communicate with hints and leave incompleteness in communication, this is not the case now. Therefore, I will tell you what this device is. The carbon dioxide detector allows you to measure the concentration of this gas in the PPM value, which can be interpreted as parts per million.

What does PPM mean, those who wish can google, or maybe a respected sergku1213 will tell us on the fingers in the comments about this value. But in principle, the decoding is more or less clear. Detector capabilities


It is very easy to use the device. Stuck in power, he warmed up for 5 seconds and everything, readings are available. There is an indicator of three states. Comfort (green), can ventilate (orange) and urgently air (red).



States, by the way, customizable. Those. if your rate is up to 1000, you can shift the border to 800 to 1000. The same can be done with the other values. There is also a reset to factory settings, sensor calibration and setting the altitude above sea level.
