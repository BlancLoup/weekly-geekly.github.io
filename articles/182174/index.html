In Humble Indie Bundle 8 added new games

Another sale of the Humble Indie Bundle, some time after the start, has traditionally been replenished with new games. And one of them is not so indie, lovers of good graphics will appreciate. For those who are interested - welcome under the cat;)

Humble Indie Bundle is a sale of cross-platform and DRM-free indie toys that you can pay as much as you want. Project site .

The price for a set is determined by you and it can be any, starting with $ 0.01. However, some games are only available if you pay above the average amount. In addition, the money received is distributed as follows: a part goes to the developers, a part is donated to the Electronic Frontier Foundation and Child's Play organizations and another part goes to the Humble Bundle team. And here you can also indicate to whom and how much to pay. You can pay using PayPal, Google Wallet, Amazon Payments. Starting with this release, Bitcoin is also accepted for payment.

The toys are cross-platform and ported to all popular operating systems: Windows, MacOS and Linux. For Linux, the native version often comes out for the first time.

As I said, games do not contain any DRM, do not require any activation, you can download installers as many times as you like. You can even upload via torrent. Those who paid at least $ 1, also receive a key to activate the game on Steam. The latest bundles can also be activated in the Ubuntu Software Center. In addition to the toys themselves, soundtracks are often included.
