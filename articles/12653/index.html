The number of subscribers to World of Warcraft exceeded 9 million

The company Blizzard Entertainment announced that the number of subscribers to the online game World of Warcraft has exceeded 9 million people. This MMORPG has once again confirmed its title as the most popular in the world.

The list of subscribers includes those who have paid for their account, and those who have just purchased an active prepaid card for playing WoW. This list also includes newbies playing their first free month. Players of computer clubs that have played for the last 30 days are also considered subscribers.

Currently, Blizzard is working on launching the World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade project in China, and in this regard, an influx of new users is expected.
