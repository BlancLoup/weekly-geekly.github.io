RapidShare begins to take domains from satellite sites

RapidShare AG has sent warnings to site owners Rapidshare.net , Rapid.org (formerly Rapidfind), Rapidshare4movies.com and others due to the fact that they encourage users to download pirated content from RapidShare, thus illegally using its trademark.

Owners of the listed sites must immediately cease illegal activities and transfer their domains to RapidShare. In addition, the company, through WIPO arbitration, has already begun to seize domains music-rapidshare.com, rapidshareprime.net, rapidshare-catalog.com, full-rapidshare.com, easyrapidsharedownloads.com, rapidshare-search-tool.com and rapidsharese.com.

Distributing such requirements is part of the new RapidShare strategy, in which it demonstrates an active fight against piracy, collaborating with copyright holders. In the future, the file sharing service plans to start legal actions against the users of the service themselves, who distribute illegal content.
