Mobile aquarium, or another example of augmented reality for children Ocean Pets

The company, familiar to the readers of our blog on interactive children's gadgets ( Pudding , Rubik 's cube for smartphones), has in its assortment another funny children's toy - the so-called ‚Äúmobile aquarium‚Äù.



This example of children's augmented reality allows children to create virtual fish, and then take care of them in his mother's (or father's) smartphone or tablet.

Ocean Pets - what's inside the box:

  • 8 silicone molds for making fish
  • 8 small boxes of plasticine
  • 2 plastic "knife"
  • Small plastic backing



Plasticine does not stick to the hands - it is something like a Play-Doh, and in this sense it comes out very practical: slap something around will not work.

The main game is in an application for IOS or Android , and both there and there is both a Russian and an international version: the main thing is not to confuse.



Despite all the simplicity, there is, as it were, a training mode for creating the first fish by prompts. Then the whole process is reduced to a few simple steps for each mold:



  • 1. Choose a fish from the 8 proposed
  • 2. Take a pattern
  • 3. Step by step to fill the template with clay of the proposed color.

In the final handicrafts must be scanned. At the same stage, the fish can be assigned a name, read the training information about it.
