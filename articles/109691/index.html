Perl Workshop "Saint Perl - 2" in St. Petersburg, December 18

Less than two weeks later, on December 18, the sixth Russian Perl workshop "Saint Perl - 2" will be held in St. Petersburg.

Website: event.perlrussia.org/saintperl2

It is not too late to register or apply for a report. Upd: new reports appeared.

Today we have such performances:

  • Martin Berends. Perl 6 Implementations in December 2010
  • Martin Berends. Perl 6 Database Interfacing
  • Alexey Kapranov (kappa). How not to store sessions
  • Alexander Kotov. Paradigm of aspect-oriented programming and its implementation in Perl
  • Vyacheslav Matyukhin. Morpheus - a new approach to the configuration of anything
  • Maxim Baklanovsky. CODA - new computer security system
  • Naim Shafiev - Perl IDEs

The schedule will be made from day to day, but for now we can inform you that registration will start at 10:30, and the opening of the conference at 11:00.

Those who come from other cities can find a suitable hotel on the event website: event.perlrussia.org/saintperl2/booking.html , the distance from the hotel to the venue is also shown there.

Train ticket - on the website ticket.rzd.ru , on the plane - awad.ru.
