Monitoring of Runet technologies for 2013

The department of information and analytical research RU-CENTER Group published an annual report with the results of monitoring the use of CMS and various technological solutions in Runet in 2013. Each month, the robot http://monoid.nic.ru/ polled the hosts on the list (popular car parks are excluded from it). In total, more than 1 million domains in the .RU zone were surveyed. Read more about the methodology here .

Some of the results are quite interesting. Since the surveys were conducted monthly, it is possible to see not only the final result, but also to evaluate the process over time. In other words, you can track the migration of sites between different CMS.

The robot distinguishes seven leading CMS by the number of sites: Drupal, WordPress, Joomla !, MODx, 1C-Bitrix, NetCat, UMI.CMS. In this case, there are two undisputed leaders - Joomla! and wordpress. These CMSs account for 70% of the websites running one of the detected content management systems.


The June-July anomaly is most likely due to technical problems on communication channels with one or several hosting providers, according to RU-CENTER Group experts

During 2013 between Joomla! and WordPress was a match. At the beginning of the year, Joomla! Was in the lead, but in June WordPress caught up with its rival, and by the end of the year it came out on top.
