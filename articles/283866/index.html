This 3-day course of practical use of general methodology and basic project management tools applied to IT projects, as well as the use of industry best practices for starting, planning, implementing, monitoring and completing IT projects.

Benefits for course participants:
  • Effective methods for launching, planning, implementing, monitoring and closing IT projects
  • Discussion of the pros and cons of various methods of information technology development
  • Methods for determining resource requirements and tools for tracking and controlling a project
  • Best practices for identifying, analyzing and managing risks

Topics covered:
  • IT Project Management Review
  • Project life cycles and their adaptation
  • Launch of the project
  • Requirements Management
  • Planning and coordinating project plans
  • Definition of content
  • Graphics development
  • Budget planning
  • Anti-risk planning
  • Communication planning
  • Project Change Management
  • Project monitoring and control
  • Verification and acceptance of project results
