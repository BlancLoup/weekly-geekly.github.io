according to The New York Times


Just over a year ago, Apple Watch sales started. Over the past few months, a huge number of reviews of the new apple device have been released, and yet for many it still remains a mystery what Apple Watch can do.


To a large extent, the usefulness of Apple Watch is determined by the applications available to them. From this point of view, the picture is quite rosy - the list of applications for watches is growing rapidly. Here are some of the most successful ones:



The Facer app shows a different wallpaper on the screen every time you look at the clock.
