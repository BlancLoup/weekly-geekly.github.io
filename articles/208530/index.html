Escene ES290 IP Phone Review

Today we look at the new IP phone Escene ES290 . In the line of corporate models of the company, the phone ranked between the younger model Escene ES220 and the older model Escene ES320, which was reviewed in a previous review. In terms of its characteristics and functional features, this innovative IP phone is closer to the older model.

When developing the devices, the engineers and designers of Escene used a unified approach to design, functional characteristics, as well as to control interfaces. The line includes models from basic to advanced phones, but they all share similar features - they meet the high requirements and have all the necessary functions of corporate IP-phones.

The phone is made in a strict style and is made of high quality materials, has a large bright screen, two independent lines, an intuitive interface in Russian, a huge set of functions, supports HD audio quality, two Ethernet ports and PoE power (optional).


The device is presented in two models: Escene ES290-PN with PoE support (powered over Ethernet) and Escene ES290-N without PoE support (equipped with an Escene AD200 power supply unit). For a model with a PoE power supply unit is not included, but if necessary it can be purchased separately.
