Digital events in Moscow from November 6 to 11

A selection of key events in Moscow for the week.



HybridConf


  • November 7 (Wednesday)
  • Color Boulevard 11s2
  • 4 500 r.
  • Already the fourth annual conference Hybrid Conf! We again gather all programmatic professionals to discuss the future of the market.

HSE {consult}


  • November 7 (Wednesday)
  • Brick 33
  • is free
  • HSE {onsult} - consulting early-stage startups with experts from the HSE Business Incubator. We will help you to sort out the current problems of the project and ask the most important questions.

Open Day of the new program "Digital Product Management"


  • November 8 (Thursday)
  • Trifonovskaya 57s1
  • is free
  • November 8, 2018, the Open Day of the Higher School of Economics program for product managers "Digital Product Management" will take place. In the program, you will learn about product vision, market analysis, consumer and value proposition, UX / UI, data analysis for decision-making and much more. The second stream of training starts on January 21. At the event, you can ask questions about the program, meet with future teachers and sign up for an interview. Registration by link: http://hsbi.hse.ru/events/open_days/den-otkrytykh-dverey-upravlenie-tsifrovym-produktom-08112018/#.W9bkimgzY2w

MateMarketing 2018


  • November 8 (Thursday)
  • 1st Zachatievsky lane 4
  • 16 000 r.
  • MateMarketing-2018 is a marketing analytics conference. The conference was organized by Alexey Nikushin - the author of the Internet-analytics telegram channel and the founder of the professional community of digital analysts and marketing specialists.

HighLoad 2018


  • November 8 (Thursday)
  • Skolkovo, New Street 100
  • 36 000 r.
  • Annual conference for developers of highload-systems, which will gather 2,700 participants from different regions of Russia and the world. The event is aimed at sharing knowledge about technologies that allow serving simultaneously thousands and millions of users.

Mitap game developers from Gamedev.House


  • November 8 (Thursday)
  • Pyatnitsky lane 2
  • is free
  • This time we will talk about crypto games: what is crypto games, how much they earn, how many players are there, what genres are popular and so on. The speaker, Yury Naydenov - Blockchain Architect from Waves, will tell you about how the Waves ecosystem will look and how it can be used - theory + practice in one report!

Energy Blockchain Hackathon


  • November 9 (Friday)
  • Novoslobodskaya 16
  • is free
  • The Hackathon Energy Blockchain Hack aims to find new innovative energy solutions based on blockchain technology. Prize money 400 000 r.

CyberMarketing-2018


  • November 10 (Saturday)
  • Leningradsky Prospect 39
  • from 6000 p.
  • Strong speakers from leading digital companies, hot topics for business, accurate answers to complex questions - this is the legendary 11th CyberMarketing Conference.

Sberbank Data Science Day


  • November 10 (Saturday)
  • New Arbat 24
  • is free
  • Data Science Day is a one-day conference about science and business, where you can not only learn a lot of new things, but also have a great time with the experts of the region.

CosMosHack


  • November 11 (Sunday) - November 13 (Tuesday)
  • Space Museum, Prospekt Mira 111
  • is free
  • We invite IT-specialists, programmers, designers, analysts, and entrepreneurs to the 48-hour hackathon. This is a chance for all those interested to immerse themselves in the technological and historical get-together, communicate with experts and realize their potential. Prize money of 500 000 r
