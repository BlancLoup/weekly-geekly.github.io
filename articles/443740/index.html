The first release of an open source product test testing tool.

In a few recent posts, a promising and fairly simple way of assessing the quality of product searches using intentions was discussed. We are glad to present an open automated tool for such testing - Intent-based Search Quality . The idea boils down to the use of previously prepared "focused" requests, the value of which lies in their straightforwardness and unambiguous interpretation.
