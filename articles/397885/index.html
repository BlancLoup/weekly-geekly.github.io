fps1000HD - 1000 frames per second




Someone Graham Rowan (Graham Rowan) offered the option of a rapid, and fairly cheap camera. A project called fps1000HD can shoot 1280 x 720 photos at 1,000 fps and 640 x 360 @ 4,000 fps. It is possible to change the exposure and set a variable frame rate. With maximum quality, recording is possible within one minute (60 thousand frames!). Pictures are saved to the internal memory (64 or 128 GB) in 12-bit RAW. After the results can be reset to a computer via USB 3.0 or to a memory card. The device comes with an interchangeable 35mm f1.7 lens on the bayonet C. A 5-inch touchscreen is used for control and preview.

The camera is a bit oak, but compact and promises good functionality. A sufficient amount of funds has already been raised to start production, and there are still 2 days ahead.

Hicat.livera - game learning technology for machine vision




The basis for the team to create your own robot from Team Hicat is the Arduino-compatible open source libraries needed to integrate the functions of machine vision. In addition to the functionally basic, they are attached to those that may be required when creating your own device. Including the provision of video recording, broadcasting and management via Wi-Fi, required with wireless networks, and the base for the web interface. The software comes with a card with a camera and the necessary circuitry. And by paying a small amount, you can purchase a ready-to-assemble set, allowing you to create your own robot.

The cost of the kit is small. And for children, or beginners, it can be a very good start. Due to this, 4 times more is already collected. Until the end of the action there are still 52 days.

Hobby Hand 2.0 - Roboruk to study




Biomechanical Robotics Group Inc. decided to teach the whole world to work with manipulators in the image and likeness of the human hand. Their Hobby Hand 2.0 project is a complete set that includes all the necessary components for building, debugging, as well as studying the work of manipulators of this kind.

The full required amount is still not recruited, but 44 days ahead. The project has all the chances to go into production.
