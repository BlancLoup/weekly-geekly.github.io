A criminal case was filed against a resident of Bratsk for buying a voice recorder in a Chinese online store



Buying gadgets in Chinese online stores, be careful. Russian customs do not sleep, and carefully examines the contents of your parcels. There are a number of products that are quietly sold on the Internet, but their purchase is prohibited by Russian law: camera pens, miniature voice recorders, camera lighters, hidden microphones and much more.

A resident of Bratsk, who bought a voice recorder in the Chinese online store, became a victim of her own carelessness. Very convenient: it is powered via USB and saves sound in WAV or MP3. Here are examples of such gadgets costing $ 6-9 on Aliexpress , on Focalprice , on Dealextreme .

The parcel was detained at the Russian customs, and the police opened a criminal case under article 138.1 of the Criminal Code of the Russian Federation.

Under this article of the Criminal Code, a woman faces a fine of up to 200 thousand rubles, the press service of the Investigation Department of the Investigative Committee of the Russian Federation in the Irkutsk region reported . The article also provides for punishment in the form of restriction of liberty for a term of up to four years and in the form of forced labor for a term of up to four years.
