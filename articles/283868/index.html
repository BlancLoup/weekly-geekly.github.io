Nature has tried its best so that living things enjoy not only the process of food, but also its prey, sometimes in unpleasant conditions. For this, nature has invented dopamine - the pleasure hormone while waiting for a reward.

And the size of the award is of secondary importance, the most important thing is the fact of receipt.
