Beginners self-study tips for Microsoft exams

Hello Habraworld!

In this article I would like to share my little experience in the field of self-preparation for passing Microsoft certification exams using the example of MCITP: Enterprise Administrator

So what do we need?

First of all, Moscow needs to set a goal for itself and go towards it step by step. If you decide to become a certified MCITP - Microsoft Certified IT Professional (formerly MCSA ), then you should study which exams (and these are four mandatory exams and one to choose from) to pass. You can do this on the Microsoft website here or, for example, here . No matter in what order you will pass the exams, the main thing is to pass them! Most of the exams are available for delivery in Russian!

Secondly, literature. There are several options: buy books, download their electronic versions, or take them from friends / colleagues / acquaintances. If you decide to buy books, then we take a denyuzhku and go to Ozon.ru or to a regular bookstore (preferably one that is in a large computer store. Tyk ). Electronic versions of the same books can be found on the Internet, and the images of the attached disks on torrent trackers. Google to the rescue.

Third, practice. Again, we have two options: imaginative physical machines or virtualization. If you have the opportunity to use physical machines (2-3 PCs or more), then this is great! But not always there is such an opportunity. This is where virtualization software comes to the rescue. Here are two of them: Microsoft's Virtual PC and Oracle's VirtualBox . Now everything is ready for a successful, or not , independent study of the material.

Read the book carefully. You need to understand what is at stake. There are a lot of pictures in books. If something is not clear - read again, and more, and more ... Do practical work. Answer the security questions at the end of each of the chapters of the books without looking into the answers ahead of time. On the accompanying discs there is additional content: the electronic version of the book in English, videos, links, as well as software for self-passing the trial exam for the current course of study.
