BlackBerrry sold out Passport Gold Limited Series

BlackBerry yesterday released a limited series of 50 Passport smartphones in gold and black. Included with the device was a soft calfskin cover from the Valextra brand. All smartphones are sold out.



The BlackBerry Passport smartphone is available in black, white, and red, and a version with rounded corners has been developed for AT & T. The device has a QWERTY-keyboard, a touchscreen display with a diagonal of 4.5 inches and a resolution of 1440x1440. The gadget is based on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 MSM8974AA quad-core processor, Adreno 330 is responsible for the graphics. The device has 32 GB of internal and 3 GB of RAM, 13 and 2 megapixel cameras and a 3450 mAh battery. Everything works under the Blackberry 10.3 operating system.

The frame of the smartphone version of Limited Black & Gold is made of stainless steel, not gold. The keys are plastic. All 50 marketed devices have already been purchased.



At CES 2015, BlackBerry introduced the Passport smartphone with rounded corners. Designed for AT & T.
