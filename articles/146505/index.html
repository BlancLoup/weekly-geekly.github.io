Video review of Asus N46 / N56 / N76 laptops



Asus N46, N56 and N76 are new multimedia laptops made in three different form factors. The new Asus N series is based on third-generation Intel Core i7 processors (Ivy Bridge) and Nvidia GeForce GT 650M video cards.
Laptops received a powerful sound system developed in conjunction with Bang & Olufsen and using Asus SonicMaster Premium technology. Moreover, they are equipped with an external subwoofer.
