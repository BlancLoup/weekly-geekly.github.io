Smartphones Sony Xperia Z3 and Z3 Compact went on sale

Hello! Let's get to the point: on September 18, a presentation of the updated flagship line of Xperia smartphones took place in Moscow, marking the official start of sales of the Xperia Z3 and Z3 Compact in Russia. Both devices have already arrived on store shelves at a price of 29990 and 23990 rubles, respectively. You can learn maximum details from upcoming reviews, but for now we offer you to get acquainted with general data.





The main differences between Xperia Z3 include 1 mm thick case, increased heat and moisture protection (complies with IP68 standard), polymer inserts on the side faces (they reduce the risk of damage to the glass when it falls), and increased processor frequency up to 2.5 GHz. The capacity of the Z3 battery has grown to 3100 mAh, and for the Z3 Compact it has grown to 2600 mAh, given the low power consumption of the device itself, this allows the compact to work for two days without recharging. The maximum sensitivity of the camera is now ISO 12800, which allows you to shoot in very low light, the new DSEE HX technology provides the highest sound quality, but thanks to the integration with the PlayStation 4, you can stream games from the console to the screen of the smartphone or tablet Z3 Tablet Compact, about which We will tell you in addition - its sales will begin in October.





Features Xperia Z3:

Processor : 4-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 801, 2.5 GHz
Memory : 3 GB RAM, 16 GB internal, SD card slot
Video accelerator : Adreno 330
Display : 5.2 inches, IPS, 1920x1080
Camera : Exmor RS 1 / 2.3 ", 20.7 megapixel
Battery : 3100 mAh
