GestIC interface - welcome to tomorrow



On the last day of the World Mobile Congress, the Germans from iDent Technology AG shocked the public by demonstrating GestIC, a system that allows you to work with phones, netbooks, cameras and other mobile small things with gestures. But it is not necessary to press the buttons.
