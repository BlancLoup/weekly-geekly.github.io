Control vote for 121 best games according to Habr (2013)


After the post " Collective Intelligence HabraHabra chose the best games of all times and peoples for 2013 ", many disputes arose: can the positions of games be considered objective? And the truth is, the games mentioned in the comment earlier than others mostly collected more votes. There are several obvious reasons for this , and that this can really be seen from the graph drawn from the data from Athari :
In order to get a more honest result, it was decided to take 121 games that collected more than 20 votes (with the blessing of the author of the original post vadimus ) and make a topic-vote with them.

Opinion of other habrazhiteli about games from this vote can be read in the first topic of the series .

Below the games are located in a fair, random order, but after the expiration date, we will still see if this time there is a dependence of the number of votes on the place in the list. As well as compare the position of the games in this vote, with its predecessor. The game range is narrowed down, so the results can be quite interesting.
Since the list still turned out to be quite large, I suggest using the search on the page (Ctrl + F) in order not to reread all the games several times in search of the one that fell between the eyes.

PS I apologize in advance to all those whose favorite games did not get 20 votes in the first topic. Maybe they will be lucky in Habogolosovaniya 2014 :-)

UPD. It seems to me that not everyone understood: this post is, as it were, the third part of the trilogy of posts. In the first post from the user vadimus, habrovans could offer games and vote for them. In the second post from the same vadimus , they collected games from the first post, which gained more than 20 votes. Also, in the same post the top games of the statistics from the first post were compiled. Many people in the comments of the second post began to complain that the Top is not accurate, due to the fact that different games got different conditions. For this reason, I decided to take the games that fell into the second post of vadimus , and offer the habrovans to vote for them again, but were given the most identical conditions for each game.

This means that those games that by the time of writing the second post from vadimus did not get 20 votes do not participate in this vote. If there are no games / the game has scored less than 20 votes, then it will not be here.
