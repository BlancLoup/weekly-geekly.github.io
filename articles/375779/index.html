Xbox One can be streamed to any device with Windows 10

Microsoft has promised that after the release of Windows 10 in 2015, the owners of the Xbox One video game console will be able to play their favorite games on any device with the new OS.

Connection devices will work within the home. After the devices are connected to LAN, it will be possible to configure the interaction between them. Then, by running the Xbox application on your computer, tablet, or phone, you can play any game from the Xbox.
