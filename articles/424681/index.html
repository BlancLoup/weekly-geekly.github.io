IT digest of October events (part one)

The zeal of the IT community this autumn obviously reached its peak, so today we had to restrict ourselves to a review of the first two weeks of October, with an increase in the form of the last September weekend, which turned out to be particularly fruitful. A summary of the rest of the month is expected next week. In the meantime, we have: mobile development, game development, hackathons of various kinds, design courses, as well as sudden charity initiatives.



SmartMail Hack: Pro Welcome

When: September 29-30
Where: Moscow, Leningradsky Prospect, 39, p. 79
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Dual benefits of the hackathon: practice for participants and help for vulnerable groups. The Charitable Foundation ‚ÄúYour Territory‚Äù invites those who wish to have a hand in creating products to support adolescents in difficult life situations of various kinds, from domestic violence and grooming to aggressive online communication. There is a job for programmers, designers, psychologists, emergency centers, marketers and other caring experts. The project being developed may be a panic button, a chat bot for auto-dialing, a post-injury lifting simulator or any other solution within the theme.

memo.id

When: September 29-30
Where: Moscow, st. Karetny Row, 5/10
Participation conditions: free, registration required

In parallel, on another Moscow site, another hackathon will be held for the benefit of society. The International Memorial and Greenhouse of social technologies organize a two-day work day to process data on the victims of terror in the USSR. Specialists in historical computer science, data journalism, programming, web design are invited. Participants can choose and suggest interesting methods and directions for them: content analysis, creation of tools for extracting new meanings from databases, visualization of meaningful queries to big data databases, work on cleaning and normalizing data, or something else. All developments will be used in projects that are being prepared for the presentation on the days of the memory of victims of political repression.

Ryazan IT Workshop 2018

When: September 29
Where: Ryazan, st. Dzerzhinsky, 6
Participation conditions: free, registration required

The initiative, which originated as a meeting of the local community of Perl-developers, but since last year has changed the format with great success, now covers the entire IT field and related topics: backend and front-end development, system administration, leadership and project management. This year, an even greater geographical spread is expected among the speakers and an even higher level of reports - while preserving proprietary lamplight, of course.

CocoaHeads Special Event

When: September 29
Where: Moscow, st. Leo Tolstoy, 16
Participation conditions: free, registration required

This time, the CocoaHeads community seduces iOS and macOS developers with a particularly diverse agenda. There will be an opportunity to listen about the current capabilities of Kotlin and Flutter, get acquainted with the SceneKit engine using Yandex Maps as an example, comprehend micro-interactions, learn the Texture framework, learn about modern standards in general and play a quiz.



Game Dev Secrets Meetup

When: September 29
Where: Rostov-on-Don, Mikhail Nagibin Ave., 3a, Bldg. 6
Participation conditions: 990 rub.

Large-scale event for those who make games and lives in the south of the country. Veterans of the Russian gamedev will talk about different aspects of the workflow (from working with remote workers to choosing a monetization scheme, from communicating with publishers to analyzing the product) and presenting showcases. The most interesting part will be followed by the most interesting part - an impromptu analysis of the projects of all participants interested in feedback.

CODE BATTLE for students 2.0

When: September 29
Where: Izhevsk, st. 30 years of Victory, 43
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Izhevsk students recruit volunteers for the war of tanks. The list of requirements is limited to the availability of a laptop, minimal knowledge of one of the following programming languages: C #, Java, JavaScript, C ++, Python, and belligerent enthusiasm. The teams will work on weapons of mass destruction - a particularly powerful super-tank. All participants, regardless of the outcome of the battle, will be awarded for their merits, including food and afterparty.

Open class of the course "Interface Designer"

When: September 30
Where: st. Lev Tolstoy 1-3, coworking "Yasnaya Polyana"
Participation conditions: 32 900 rub.

Full two-month course of interface design from the company Contented. To master a promising profession is offered even to those who have absolutely no background knowledge. The program covers the creation of a user's portrait and analytics, the creation of interactive prototypes and animation mockups, the design of responsive web interfaces and mobile products, and testing in real conditions. The educational process involves both independent online classes and master classes with teachers. Training is completed with the protection of projects of participants, and potential employers will be present at the ceremony. The course itself begins on October 8, but the introductory lesson, where the curators will be met and the dive case study will be held on September 30th - it can be visited free of charge.

Intel Software: Practical Developer Training

When: October 3-4
Where: Vladivostok, l. Nizhneportovaya, 1, The Marine Restaurant
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Intel Software representatives will demonstrate how the company's tools can be used in high-performance computing and machine learning. Participants will develop skills directly in the course of working with the provided infrastructure with the support of specialists. They will be offered a wide range of tasks, from building a neural network model and ending with the use of optimized deep learning libraries for pattern recognition. The program is divided into two parts: first day themes - Intel processor architecture, Intel compilers, MKL Python math library, Intel Advisor and IntelVtune; themes of the second are machine learning concepts, classical pattern recognition with Intel Distribution for Python, neural networks with Intel Optimized TensorFlow with Keras.

Frontend conf

When: October 4-5
Where: Moscow, 1st Zachatievsky lane., 4, Infospace
Participation conditions: 30 000 rubles.

Specialized conference on frontend in all its manifestations: automation, testing, caching, templating, interface speed, adaptive layout, browser-based APIs, frameworks, ECMAScript 6 standard, Node.js, io.js, and so on. VR / AR-Hackathon

When: October 5

When: October 6

The traditional multi-format event from FRIA, the largest platform for startups. DevParty Developer Conference

SupportFest

AppsConf

3D modeling (Autodesk 3ds Max)

When: October 9
Where: Moscow, Kutuzovsky Prospect 36, p. 4
Participation conditions: 38 200 rub.

The course comprehensively covers the process of working with Autodesk 3ds Max, a platform for 3D modeling, combining a theoretical base with the development of acquired knowledge in practice. Students will learn how to use the platform for various purposes, from marking up the scene to creating a bitmap image, learn how to work with light and create interiors, learn the basics of the composition, practice in creating and animating characters. Classes will be held twice a week, in the evening, it is also assumed independent classes (up to 10 hours per week). The program is divided into four modules (Introduction, Interior Design, Character modeling and texturing, Character animation), modular payment is possible. Upon completion of the course, students receive a certificate and a project in the portfolio.

INDEX TECH 2018

When: October 12
Where: Moscow, Tverskaya Street, 7, DI Telegraph
Participation conditions: 15 000 rub.

IT and HR, let's live together - approximately under such a motto there will be a conference with the general theme of recruiting IT specialists. SECR 2018

Where: Moscow, Digital October Center
Conditions of participation: 27 000 rubles.

A truly large-scale conference with a truly dense program - there will be everything related to software development, and many of those who are now at the edge of this industry in Russia.
