Acer is going to release a "cloud" smartphone



Acer has released so far not many models of mobile phones and smartphones. Its new project may make Acer smartphones recognizable and popular. So, the other day the corporation announced the release of a new smartphone, called Acer CloudMobile, which will be ‚Äúsharpened‚Äù for a cloud service from the same company. The service is called Acer Cloud, there is nothing unexpected. The presentation of the device and service will be held at the 2012 Mobile World Congress.

The exhibition will be held on February 27 of this year, so there will be something to see. In addition to binding to the cloud, the smartphone will work on the basis of the Android 4.1 OS, as far as can be understood, this is the first mobile device that will use this version of the mobile operating system. As for the characteristics of the device, here we have a processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz and a display with a diagonal of 4.3 inches. In addition to the usual communication modules, the developers have also embedded support for HSPA + and 3G.

There is almost no information about the cloud service from Acer. At the moment it is only known that this service will allow the client of the company, i.e. the user of one of the Acer devices, store their files in the cloud, and access to the data can be obtained both from a PC and from a mobile device. Initially, Acer Cloud will work only in North America and China, and then in other countries.

In general, the development is quite interesting, but here the mobile device itself is more interesting, and not the cloud service. However, in conjunction can be a very curious product that will become popular.
