Eye-Fi Cards

The company Eye-Fi today announced two new SD-Wi-Fi cards, and also changed the name of the original device.

Eye-Fi Explore : this map automatically searches for open wi-fi networks near you and makes geotagging of your photos using the Skyhook service. It also automatically connects to Wayport hotspots, which is of no interest to people in Europe, Russia and Asia , but in the USA every McDonalds is . Device price: $ 129 .

Eye-Fi Share : This is the original eye-fi card with a new name. Allows you to automatically upload photos directly to your favorite photo hosting service using wi-fi. Price: $ 99 .

Eye-Fi Home : this card is intended for those ( folks ) who take and copy many pictures to their computer, but do not feel the need to upload them to the network. The card transfers photos only to your hard disk, no cables / card readers / docks or other trash are needed. For only $ 79 .

This is a SD memory card , which allows you to use Wi-Fi on almost any digital camera. In addition, the free Eye-Fi service allows you to automatically upload your photos to 20 different photo sites, such as Flickr! , for example. And, of course, on any computer home network.
In addition to the Wi-Fi adapter, this is also a regular 2 GB SD card.

This accessory works flawlessly. Installation takes only 5 minutes - this is the time it takes to set up through your computer and a simple SD card reader.
