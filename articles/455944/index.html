Rakes in working with freelancers. We learn to bypass

In freelancing advertising, they often say that it is cheap for a customer, because a freelancer does not have a lot of overhead, and a freelancer advertises that he is now his own director and this is a great opportunity to get rid of the yoke of all these managers and managers, to become free and independent . This is partly true, but there is a small amendment - all the same, someone must fulfill the functions of all these leaders and managers. But who?

Often it turns out that no one. And this is exactly what leads to problems both for the customer and the freelancer himself.

What are the pitfalls and how can they be avoided?

Communications


People are different, the style of communication is also different for everyone. Someone more talkative, someone tries not to waste letters. When opposites converge, it can cause discontent and tensions. For example, the customer briefly described the task and believes that this is sufficient. The contractor took and began in the process to ask 100500 clarifying questions. On the one hand, this is good - it means it works and tries to do it well. But what to do if the customer does not have the opportunity to answer so many questions, there is no possibility to be constantly online? Or vice versa - the freelancer took the job and disappeared. Answers questions once a day, at other times does not read messages, it distracts him from work. This is the case when the performer must do the work for two - for himself and for the manager who is constantly in touch.

There is no simple solution, you need to listen to your opponent, try to understand whether his style of communication suits you.

Motivation and control


Since the performer does not have a boss who hangs over him and in his interest to complete the task, the performer must motivate himself. It is not always possible and not all, this is one of the aspects and difficulties of freelancing. One way or another, without self-control and self-discipline, freelancing cannot be and therefore not everyone is suitable for freelancing. Of course, a successful freelancer possesses such skills, otherwise he would not have been successful. But this does not mean that there can be no punctures.

The control of both the process and the final results lies with the customer. To motivate the artist sometimes you also have to. The project can be protracted, boring, burnout, autumn, and in general all ashes.

Unfortunately, many customers deprive themselves of an effective tool in the form of negative feedback. In order to be able to post a review, you must select a freelancer as a performer on the exchange. Do not neglect this, otherwise there will be only gingerbread and a kind word. And a kind word and a whip, as we know, is much more effective than a kind word alone.

Intermediate control will not only allow you to keep abreast of the process, but also to identify problems at an early stage of occurrence and either try to solve problems, agree on new terms or stop cooperation. Here it is important to take into account the features of communication that were described above.

Scammers


Not those who took up the order, did, did half and refused to return the prepayment, but the real ones, whose sole purpose is to take money from you.

The scheme is almost always the same and is used not only for freelancing.

The fraudster entices the customer / buyer with a very attractive price, takes a prepayment and hides. Do not stop and $ 10, because until the customer realizes that he was deceived, you can imitate the activity, take time, take a prepayment from others. As a result, the loss is not only in money, but also in time.

More often, these guys are operating in categories where it is difficult to find a performer, usually these are areas related to programming.

  • Do not neglect the ratings and reviews. Their goal is to protect you. It is safer to work with proven performers. But often more expensive
  • Do not hurry. The scammer has little time, we will calculate it and block
  • Use Secure Payments. The purpose of the fraudster - prepayment. On the prepayment fraudsters insist very sharply and by any means trying to get money in advance.
