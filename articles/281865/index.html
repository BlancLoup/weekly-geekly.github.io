Firebird 3.0 released



Firebird 3.0 , the sixth major release of Firebird DBMS, was released today, and it is also the most significant in scale of changes since the release of version 1 in 2002 ,.
The Firebird 3.0 architecture has been redesigned and now fully supports multithreading with scaling to hundreds of cores, a large amount of RAM is effectively supported. According to the results of OLTP load tests that simulate intensive insertions and changes, the speed of work in scenarios with hundreds of users in Firebird 3.0, compared with 2.5, increased by ~ 5 times.
In addition to scaling and performance, the release of Firebird 3.0 includes database encryption, traffic, and more than 100 innovations in the field of SQL and security - they are described in detail in release notes and documentation on the SQL language (in Russian).
