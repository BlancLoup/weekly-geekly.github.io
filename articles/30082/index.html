Online conference with leading PostgreSQL experts

On July 30, Postgresmen organizes an online conference , Questions to Leading PostgreSQL Experts . Anyone can ask their question related to the implementation, operation, development of PostgreSQL, the world-famous PostgreSQL experts, leading developers and leaders of the PostgreSQL community.

Conference participants:

* Bruce Momdzhan ( Bruce Momjian ) , PostgreSQL development coordinator, PostgreSQL community leader, EnterpriseDB expert.
* Maxim Boguk , leading database administrator for Rambler and Masterhost, a renowned PostgreSQL specialist.
* Fedor Sigaev , developer of the full-text search subsystem, GiST, GIN indexing systems and many additional modules, one of the main members of the PostgreSQL Global Development Group.
* Oleg Bartunov , one of the main members of the PostgreSQL Global Development Group, developer of the full-text search subsystem, GiST, GIN indexing systems, developer of multi-terabyte scientific databases.

Conference start: Wednesday, July 30, 2008, 15:00 Moscow time. Duration: 3 hours . Questions will be received in the jabber channel postgresmen@conference.jabber.org (main mode) and on skype: postgresmen. After the conference, questions and answers will be published on the website http://postgresmen.ru .
