VTB 24


Of course, I myself can walk to the ATM and turn on SMS confirmation and 3D-Secure, if possible. Only here, 3D-Secure was invented not at all to protect the cardholder, by the way, as written on the website of the same VTB24, but to protect retail outlets from friendly fraud.


You probably know that it is extremely difficult to challenge a password-prone transaction on the website of the issuing bank (3DS - we have sms), as fraudulent. But if the channel of the store is 3D-Secure, and the card does not support this technology, then you will win 99% of the challenge (if the bank wants to start it). And VTB24 itself (that is, the issuer) will pay for fraud, and not the acquirer bank (as is the case with the non-3DS channel). This is beautiful in English called the liability shift - the shift of responsibility from the acquirer to the issuer when using 3D-Secure technology.
