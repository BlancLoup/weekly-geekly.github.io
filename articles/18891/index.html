HabraReader - for reading RSS feeds

I present to your attention HabraReader!
HabraReader is an RSS reader that is installed on the user's computer, allows you to read RSS feeds and has additional features. For example, the formation of your own tapes using search engines for a different type of data.
With it, you can get the materials of interest immediately after publication in the source.
