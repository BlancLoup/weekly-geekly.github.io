Wearable device market grew by almost 700%

Analytical agency Canalys calculated how many wearable devices manufacturers shipped this year. The figure is not very impressive yet - in January-July, 6 million smart watches and fitness trackers were produced, which is 684% more than last year. Under the cut - a few words about the market leaders.



Samsung has become the industry leader, largely due to the three new products introduced this year: Gear 2, Gear 2 Neo and Gear Fit. Moreover, in July, the company introduced the fifth wearable device for the year - Gear Live on Android Wear .



Keep the position in the top three smog and manufacturer Pebble thanks to Pebble Steel. Sony closes the top three. So far this is a very fragile rating, as new devices are expected this year, including from Motorola .



If we talk about highly specialized bracelets, the leaders are Jawbone and Fitbit.





However, it is still too early to talk about the overpopular wearable devices. In any case, in the second quarter of this year alone, 300 million units were shipped, which is 50 times more than in half a year - fitness trackers and smart watches.
