Watson Health continues to evolve



IBM has announced plans to expand its Watson Health division. In this regard, the company has already introduced two new cloud services related to IBM Watson, plus agreed to cooperate with a wide range of international companies.

One of the presented services is the IBM Watson Health Cloud for Life Sciences Compliance, it is proposed to use it to facilitate and accelerate the certification of various biomedical developments. The second service is IBM Watson Care Manager, which allows the patient to provide personalized care and treatment.

IBM Watson Health Cloud for Life Sciences Compliance will significantly improve the efficiency and speed of certification of biomedical research. This, in turn, will accelerate the launch of new medical products on the market. Now the emergence of a new medical drug on the market is associated with a huge number of procedural and bureaucratic elements, which slows down this process.

In addition, the service helps companies accelerate the implementation of infrastructure and applications that meet security standards for the use of IT equipment for medical purposes, which will meet stringent requirements for hosting, access and data exchange.

IBM Watson Care Manager is a combination of Watson Health, Apple HealthKit and ResearchKit. The last two platforms are Applt Corporation development, which facilitates research using an iPhone smartphone. The IBM service, which combines all the capabilities of these platforms, opens up a number of prospects for medical companies and medical workers to create an individualized treatment and patient care program.
