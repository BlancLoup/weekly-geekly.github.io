Lenovo IdeaPad U8 - First MID


Lenovo is preparing to launch its Ideapad U8 , a device classified as MID . In fact, these are the same UMPCs , but designed for a wider range of users (unlike UMPCs, which are mainly aimed at business users), work on a more ‚Äúlightweight‚Äù OS, for example, Linux, and are more optimized for multimedia and Internet -functions
Ideapad U8 was introduced at Intel IDF in Shanghai and, as announced at the event, is equipped with the latest Intel processor - Atom .
The main controls will be a 4.8-inch touchscreen display and a special manipulator operating on the principle of an optical mouse, only the sensor reads finger movements.
