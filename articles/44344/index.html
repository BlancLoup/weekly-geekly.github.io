Tikhon Deinekin, head of the online promotion department of the reference service Imkhonet told him about the monetization models of www.imhonet.ru :
Unlike other social networks, Imkhonet has an additional advantage, thanks to which it is much easier for us to monetize our resources than other social networks. imhonet.ru is a symbiosis of a recommendatory service and a social network: the network is built on the basis of recommendatory functionality. Users give marks (make up their own preferences profile), the system compares the profiles of different users and selects like-minded circles.
