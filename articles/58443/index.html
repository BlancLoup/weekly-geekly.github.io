100 applications for the contest from Softline Venture Partners "Dev Generation 2009"

Recently, we overcame the boundary that seems important to us - more than 100 applications for participation in the competition "Dev Generation 2009". Applications were sent by undergraduate and graduate students from more than 100 universities. The competition has become truly international - young developers from Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Ukraine and Estonia took part.

The most active were the developers from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk and Penza - they sent about half of the submitted projects.

We consider all submitted applications very carefully and we will do this until May 19, and we very much hope that the number of applications will grow at the same pace. Such companies as Microsoft, Adobe, VTB, Alliance ROSNO, IT Alliance, Embarcadero Technologies are represented in the competition jury.

The winner of the competition will receive an investment of $ 100,000 for the implementation of its project from Softline Venture Partners. In addition to the main prize, there are gifts from Softline Venture Partners partners in the contest and sponsors.

Learn more and submit an application on the contest page softlinevp.com/vp/DevGeneration2009
