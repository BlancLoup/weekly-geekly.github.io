Miss Croft Highway

When the imagination at the expense of the name of the new roads was exhausted from the municipal authorities of the British city of Derby, they took note of the full power of the Internet and held a general vote. In the list of finalists who reached the vote on the site, was the name Lara Croft Way. In honor of, of course, the famous virtual tomb raider.

The epic with the name of the road began last summer. And recently, Lara Croft Way won an unconditional victory: 89% of the 27,000 voters. It is not surprising, because, first, Lara Croft is an English aristocratic woman. And, secondly, the company Core Design, which founded the Tomb Raider series, worked in Derby from 1998 to 2006.
