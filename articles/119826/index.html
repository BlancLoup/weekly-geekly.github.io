In the wake of Nokia Developer Days

Dear Habr!

May 19 and 20 in Moscow, a conference of developers of mobile applications Nokia Developer Days. We announced this event earlier and hope that everyone who wanted could attend it. The conference was attended by 150 developers and people interested in developing applications for mobile phones.

In the near future, we will post all the presentations from the event so that all those who for one reason or another did not get on Developer Days could familiarize themselves with them in the public domain. By the way, we tried a little for a cozy Habr, and now everyone can access the tag 
  <slideshow> link to the slideshare.net presentation </ slideshow>,
with which you can embed in the habr-topic presentation. So far only slideshare.net is supported, but in the future, we hope, all the most common services for storing presentations will be added.

In the meantime, we would like to ask those who attended the conference for the first time, as well as those who participated in previous Developer Days, what did you like and what would you like to do or organize differently? What presentations did you like the most, and on which ones you wanted to sleep? We will be sincerely happy for constructive criticism so that the next event will be only better!
