How we created "Hotel for this night!"

Hi, Habr!

We launched a new feature in our mobile application - "Hotel for the night!". This is a feature that helps a person to book a hotel at the last minute. Why we did it, how and what came out of it, we decided to tell on Habr√©. We welcome your feedback and comments on what can be further improved in the new function and in general in applications.

History of creation



In December 2012, we launched mobile applications for the iPhone and Android , through which you can book hotels around the world. These are bookings made in advance. After that, we began to think about how to help people who need a hotel urgently, right here and now. This is how the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Hotel for the night! Function appeared. And, since Ostrovok.ru works with 5,000 hotels directly, we have agreed with some of them about connecting to the new option and offering discounts of up to 50%. It‚Äôs convenient for the customer, he can quickly book a room and then enter it, and we help hotels to fill in empty rooms.

The first meeting on the "Hotel for the night!" Was held in late December. To make a seemingly small function in a mobile application it took the work of at least seven departments of the company:
  • Supply - department for work with hotels;
  • Extranet - back office for hotels, management of all hotels and rates for hotels;
  • Content - the department for working with content (in this case, with mobile content);
  • OTA - core product team;
  • API - a team responsible for transferring data using the necessary protocols to partners and / or clients (in this case, mobile applications);
  • Mobile is a mobile team of mobile mobile application developers;
  • Marketing - marketing department.

As a result of the meeting, we decided that:

  • The function should be only in the mobile application (iPhone, Android);
  • The application should provide an opportunity to find hotels for today and standard booking for the future;
  • Special prices and mobile content (information about discounts and bonuses, a brief description of the night rate);
  • At the start there should be at least ten hotels from Moscow and St. Petersburg and several hotels in Kiev;
  • Design requirements: the user must unambiguously understand where the night rate is, and where the standard hotel booking is;
  • Application release - March-April.

In January and February, the main work fell on the shoulders of the Supply Department: they had to agree with the hotels about a new tariff, determine the list of cities and hotels that might be interested in such a tariff. As part of the nightly fare with each hotel, the size of the discount was determined (from 20% to 55% per room) and various conditions from the hotel were approved (for example, the beginning and end of sales, late departure, payment without a card, compliments: champagne, water, bathrobe and even slippers :). Looking ahead, not every hotel went to such conditions, because they did not have such experience and / or understanding that this would bring them additional reservations. As a result, we managed to add 45 hotels: seventeen in Moscow and St. Petersburg and eleven in Kiev.

In early March, the first nightly rates were added to our Extranet system.

During this time, a new feature design has been developed for the iPhone and Android, new mobile API methods and communication with OTA functionality are ready.

A few comments about the most mobile development


In the iPhone version of the application, we tried to simplify the interface of the search for night tariffs. We understood that the number of hotels at such rates in each city is small, so we removed various sorting and filters. In addition to the new feature, we managed to make a number of changes and improvements in the application. So, for example, they added very beautiful and understandable tips (so that all-all users know and understand how to use our application), as well as optimize the download and operation of the application.

In the Android version, due to the particular implementation (the only active search available in the application at a time), we decided to add the search for night tariffs in a separate tab in the search form. We deliberately went for a similar experiment, making different ways to access the "Hotels for the night!" Function on the iPhone and Android.



The name of the new function in the application has changed several times, we have long picked up a short and clear name. Just before the release of the application, we stopped at the name ‚ÄúHotel for this night!‚Äù.

As a result, we managed to release new versions of iPhone and Android applications for the App Store, Google Play and Yandex. Store in one day - March 27, 2013. And by chance, the release of our mobile applications coincided with the announcement of the third round of investments received by the company.

New Feature Development Plans
