And again on the stand for laptop

Akasa company, one of the specializations of which is the production of various external coolers and lotions for laptops, announced the release of new notebook supports.


The new Akasa LIBRA differs from the Logitech product with its greater functionality (due to the presence of a cooler), and from the Cool Table that has also appeared on the pages of the Habr Cool Table with a prettier design and manufacturability.


The dimensions of this thing are 334x275x36 mm (perfect for a 15-inch laptop). The body is made of aluminum and rubber inserts that are needed to ensure that the laptop does not slide. Coolers in stand two. They are characterized by a rotational speed of 15600 revolutions per minute and make a fuss of nothing at all - 19.2 dB. The good thing is that the coolers can be turned on or off (there is a special switch). Power is supplied to the stand via USB.

Made LIBRA in black and silver colors.
