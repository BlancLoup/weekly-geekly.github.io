Overview of the smartphone ASUS ZenFone 3 Max



Battery life is still the cornerstone of modern gadgets. And today, the focus of our attention is a new ASUS ZenFone 3 Max smartphone (ZC520TL) with a high-capacity battery.

Appearance


Included with the smartphone is a USB-charger and a short USB-micro USB adapter.



The case of the smartphone is plastic, but with a very high-quality coating under brushed aluminum. Comes in three colors.





Above the camera is a hole of one of the microphones. The camera module itself is slightly recessed into the case. The flash is plain, monochrome. Under the camera is a fingerprint scanner. Visually, this is not noticeable, but it is tactilely clearly visible that the scanner is recessed into the case more than the camera, so that you can not touch them and wonder why the smartphone will not unlock.



At the bottom is a large mesh dynamics.



The ZenPhone 3 Max has no sharp edges or corners: the case is flattened, licked, and even the edges of the protective glass display are rounded.



Thanks to the ‚Äústreamlined‚Äù shape and smoothness of all elements, the smartphone is very nice in the palm of your hand. Only the volume and power buttons are tactilely highlighted.



On top there is a 3.5 mm combined audio jack.





Below is a micro USB connector and a second microphone hole, and on the left side is a tray for micro and nano SIM cards.







You can insert either two SIM cards (micro and nano), or a SIM (micro) card and a memory card into the tray.

Display


ZenPhone 3 Max is equipped with a display on the IPS matrix, with a diagonal of 5.2 inches and a resolution of 1280 x 720. The image is completely smooth, even on round figures no aliasing is visible. Viewing angles are great, and the color quality is traditionally high. The maximum brightness is 400 cd / m2.

Software


The device is Android 6.0 with a proprietary graphic shell ZenUI 3.0. It allows you to download and install all sorts of themes. The system buttons "Back", "Home" and "Running applications" are not made as a separate touch panel under the screen, but are displayed at the bottom of the screen.


Of course, the smartphone has pre-installed branded applications: ShareLink, ZenCircle, ZenTalk, and others.

Cameras


For the main camera, a matrix with a resolution of 13 megapixels is used. The five-element lens is manufactured by Largan Precision. Front camera resolution - 5 MP.

Performance


ZenFone 3 Max is based on MediaTek's MT6737T platform. It is equipped with two ARM processors: the central 64-bit quad-core Cortex A53 (operating frequency up to 1.5 GHz) and the graphic Mali-T720. The amount of RAM - 2 or 3 GB.

Conclusion


ASUS ZenPhone 3 Max (ZC520TL) is a modern smartphone, comparable in performance to ASUS ZenFone 3 Laser. Even their hulls are almost the same. However, they are not direct competitors: the Laser has a larger screen resolution and an improved camera focusing system, and the Max has a more capacious battery by a third. So if you are not fond of taking pictures in a situation where the bill goes on for a second, if you often stay away from sockets for a long time, then pay attention to ZenFone 3 Max - there are not so many rivals for ‚Äúlongevity‚Äù.
