The seventh "Festival 404" will be held October 4-5, 2014 in Samara

Great news! On October 4-5, 2014, for the seventh time in Samara, the 404 Festival will be held.



This year we decided not to beat records in the number of reports, but to focus on the strengths of the festival. Speeches will last 45 minutes plus 10 minutes for questions. And we plan to implement such an innovative piece as small breaks between reports.

We really appreciate the personal relationship, so the sections at the 404 Festival will be traditionally authored. Here is a list of sections that will go in 4 threads:

  • Managing people (Sergey Kotyrev)
  • UX (Plato Dniprovsky)
  • Design Weekend (Artem Geller, Vyacheslav Pravdzinsky)
  • Marketing for robots (Yuri Marin)
  • Mobile applications (Ruslan Demyanenko)
  • Technologies (Alexander Fitiskin)
  • Startups (Anton Gugla)
  • Information Society (Denis Kortunov)


It is possible that due to the natural greed, we will organize another place for performances in Holiday Inn and let another stream. Write in the comments which sections you would like more. Also this year we will have an afterparty in a new place, better than before.

We are looking for sponsors and partners of the festival. You can be a sponsor, you can put a stand in the hall, or just a pillar, you can still come up with something. Sponsoring the 404 Festival is not only honorable, but also beneficial.

Now the most important thing. If you have no doubts and there is a desire to participate in the festival, then until August 5 you will be able to pay participation at a special low price for the most decisive. Come in Samara to the 404 Festival in the fall! It's great here.
