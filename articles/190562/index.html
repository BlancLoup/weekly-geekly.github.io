Meetings of the Meet Magento Russia 2013 Conference



About the conference


The Meet Magento Russia 2013 conference will take place on August 24 in Moscow (Izmailovo).

Magento is a powerful e-commerce platform owned by eBay / PayPal.
Magento is leading both in the free e-commerce market and in the corporate segment.

Participation in the conference is completely free and requires only pre- registration .
Details about the conference, you can read this post or on the website Meet Magento Russia .
