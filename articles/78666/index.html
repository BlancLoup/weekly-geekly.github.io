AlterGeo iPhone App (aka Wi2Geo)

We finally released an iPhone app. True, under the AlterGeo brand, but this is how our geo-network will be called in the very near future. Download the application in the iTunes Store . The full description of the application can be seen here: http://altergeo.ru/iphone/

For those who are lazy to follow the link, I will describe some screenshots and functionality right in this post.

Firstly, the application can not only Google Maps, but also Yandex.Maps and Open Street Maps. In case you get to places where there are no Google maps.

Secondly, you can communicate with other users of the service using the convenient built-in chat, made on the basis of XMPP.

Under the cut a few screenshots.


So now you can:

Easy to find places right on the map.
Filter places by category
Quickly browse place profiles
Easy to send location coordinates

Find new people on the map And chat with them


In principle, it is possible to describe for a long time, but it seems to us that it is better to touch once than to see a hundred times. We repeat once more that you can read more about the application here: http://altergeo.ru/iphone/ , and you can download the application from the iTunes Store .
