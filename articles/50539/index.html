iPhone and iPod touch for snipers



Walking around the net I found a funny and at the same time intriguing news that a program for snipers appeared in the Apple App Store (yes, I was also surprised), which will help them in aiming. The name BulletFlight, released in the company Runaway Technology (in the picture - the logo of the program).



Users of BulletFlight are invited to attach an iPod to a rifle, enter several parameters and fire a shot following the advice of the program. Among the input parameters are the wind, the type of ammunition used, and the distance to the target (And I would like it to determine the wind, distance, etc.) in the future)



The authors of the program note that their program for the better is different from the competitors, who display the results of ballistic calculations in a tabular form, in that the Runaway application gives the result in the form of a tip.
