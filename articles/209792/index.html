What technology stacks are used more often on the Jelastic platform?

This post tells about the statistics of the used technological stacks on the Jelastic platform for December 2013. The article will allow you to identify the most popular and promising technologies available today on the Jelastic platform in one click.

Database servers


Our regular winner, MySQL, lost 3% of the market share that its younger sister, MariaDB, absorbed (20%). The remaining databases in statistics remained unchanged.

In November, the Netherlands led the way in using MySQL with an almost 80% market share. In December, the Netherlands went down to eighth place on the use of MySQL (54%), sharing it with Switzerland.

Java application servers


Tomcat 7 remains the leader among Java application servers, and in December it became only more popular (83%). Interestingly enough, Tomcat 6 loses its second position in favor of Glassfish. Jetty usage also decreased (2%). Outsider TomEE consistently takes 1%.

Let's see which regions made Tomcat and Glassfish even more popular. The leader of Tomcat 7 in Ukraine in December was 94%. Glassfish should say thanks to Finland for 18% of users. In November, a large percentage of Finnish users used Jetty. It seems that the last months of 2013 most affected the distribution of second place between Glassfish and Jetty.

Apache continues to dominate the server market for PHP applications in December and only 17% is left for NGINX.

PHP 5.4 became even more used in December. As a result, PHP 5.3 and 5.5 lost 2% of the market each.
