MBLTdev 2017 is coming. Registration is open



Hi, Habr! The 4th MBLTdev Mobile Developer Conference will be held on October 27, 2017 in Moscow.

MBLTdev is an annual conference for training and getting to know the professional community of experienced and talented developers. Learn the latest iOS and Android trends and best practices by visiting speakers from Europe, Silicon Valley and Russia. Learn about new tools and approaches to architecture design, ask experts questions, get new knowledge to work on your projects.

Carefully, under the cut spoilers - all the details about the program and tickets.

Program


Traditionally, most reports will be devoted to native iOS and Android development. And for the first time, we will organize Codelabs - a separate room with hourly sessions from experts, where you can learn something new and consolidate your knowledge with a practical task. We promise a hardcore program for developers Middle + and Senior.

Enough space for everyone. 4 halls will be involved simultaneously, plus a video broadcast in the lounge area. Between reports 10-minute breaks, during which the speakers will continue to communicate with you in special discussion areas.

Main topics:


  • Innovations and non-obvious features in new versions of Android and iOS,
  • Advanced swift
  • ARKit, HealthKit,
  • Kotlin,
  • Migrating from CoreData to Realm,
  • Machine learning,
  • Custom animation and controls,
  • ReactiveX
  • CoreML,
  • Memory Efficient tools,
  • Security,
  • Instant apps,
  • Android pay,
  • PWA,
  • Iot.

Applications for speeches


If you have something to share with colleagues - welcome. We accept applications for speeches. Deadline - August 20, 2017.

  1. Fill out the form . Carefully read the presentation rules and the list of recommended topics.
  2. All applications will be reviewed by the program committee. In case of a positive decision, within two weeks it is necessary to provide a full-fledged draft slide report with brief comments.
  3. The program committee will study the materials and give feedback, you need to make edits and go through a test online run. We will also invite you to a public speaking workshop where you can improve your presentation skills.

Yes, this year there will be a rigorous selection of reports. Program committee members, experienced iOS and Android developers, will select the most interesting topics and the most eloquent speakers.

Program committee


If you are an Android developer of mid + or senior level, and you have the time and desire to help create high-quality content for MBLTdev 2017, then we will be glad to see you on the program committee. Fill out an application and join our team!

Also in the comments to this post you can advise Russian and foreign speakers and topics that you want to hear at the conference MBLTdev 2017.

Tickets


This year the number of participants is limited, only 600 tickets will go on sale.

While we are forming a program, tickets are subject to a special Early Bird price.
Register and buy tickets now!



See you at MBLTdev 2017!
