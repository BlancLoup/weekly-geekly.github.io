Upgrade to Windows 8 inexpensively. Opening of the Russian portal

An interesting promotion from Microsoft starts soon. Anyone who purchases a laptop or PC with Microsoft Windows 7 Home Basic, Home Premium, Professional or Ultimate, can upgrade to the corresponding Microsoft Windows 8 Pro edition for only 469.00. rubles. Now, in addition to the international one, the Russian-language portal is also available!

Registration is available at this address. As it is written in the same place, PCs and laptops purchased between June 2, 2012 and January 31, 2013 will participate in the action, and the action itself will last until February 28, 2013.
To register, you must leave your data - (Name, Surname and E-mail), and later you will need additional information, such as the Acquisition Date Manufacturer and model. Also, additional information may be requested to verify registration, including the Windows 7 product key of your compliant PC.
A maximum of 5 updates are available for one user per special. price, with the rule - one update on one PC. In addition, the offer does not cover Windows 7 Starter Edition.
