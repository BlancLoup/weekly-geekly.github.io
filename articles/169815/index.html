Conference "Internet advertising day: from planning to performance evaluation"

For those who are closely connected with the marketing and promotion of the company, we invite you on February 20 to the 5th conference "Internet Advertising Day" , organized by the contextual advertising service eLama.ru , with the support of the portal Ayti-Event .




The purpose of the event:


To acquaint listeners with what difficulties and problems they face when preparing and conducting advertising campaigns, how to resolve and prevent them. The event is aimed at obtaining fresh knowledge, ideas and recommendations from experts in the field of online advertising.

Who is this event for?


Due to the practical orientation of the program, participation in the conference is attractive for owners and directors of companies, heads of departments and practitioners in the field of marketing, advertising and public relations, as well as participants in the professional market of Internet advertising.

What will happen at the conference:


Leading experts in the field of Internet marketing will share their experiences, tips and examples from practice. You will learn how successful projects are implemented, what difficulties each specialist faces, and, most importantly, how to overcome them:
  • How to correctly use strategic thinking in planning advertising campaigns?
  • How to choose a domain for an advertising campaign?
  • How to increase sales without increasing advertising costs?
  • How to avoid typical mistakes in attracting traffic from search engines?
  • How to customize site personalization to the expectations of visitors by means of web analytics in the automated mode?
  • How to see quick results from invested forces and funds?

You can familiarize yourself with the program, select the appropriate material and develop an individual event plan on the website .

Participation format:


  • Participation in the conference is free . Registration is required for participation.
  • Also at the conference provides a format for business participation. More details .
  • If you can not attend the conference in person, you can watch it online , as well as receive videos of all speeches after the event.


Venue:


St. Petersburg, Azimuth Hotel, as well as online .
