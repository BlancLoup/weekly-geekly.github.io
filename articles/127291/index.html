Virtual RBK Money MasterCard Virtual

Bank cards are a popular and convenient payment tool, but not everyone uses it for online payments. Especially for users who are afraid to register their card data on the Internet or for those who do not have a bank card at all, but would like to make purchases online, we begin to issue virtual bank cards MasterCard Virtual .

You can order a virtual card in a professional RBK Money wallet - after a few minutes you receive all the data and you can start shopping. RBK Money e-wallet can be recharged to order a virtual card without any commission, using all the main methods.

To ensure maximum protection, the MasterCard Virtual card has a short lifespan, it is non-replenishable, its account limit is limited to 15,000 rubles. In addition, the sms-information system is immediately connected to the map.

Card issue conditions:

  • Card validity period - from 2 to 3 months depending on the date of issue;
  • The minimum face value of the card is 100 rubles, the maximum one is 15,000 rubles;
  • The card is non-replenished;
  • The commission for issuing a MasterCard Virtual card is 2%;
  • No more than two simultaneously valid cards can be ordered per phone number;
  • The balance of unused funds from the MasterCard Virtual card will be returned to the user on the mobile phone account.
