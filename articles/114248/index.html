Communication Tube: Siberia and the Urals

After the last Moscow Weekend, we decided to try to get acquainted with the projects before the Weekend personally. The nearest Startup Weekend will be in Novosibirsk, so Glavstart is going to Siberia.

As part of a start-up tour, we will conduct 3 Communication Tube in Tomsk, Novosibirsk and Yekaterinburg, where we will get to know the participants and share our experiences with each other.

Let's start with a short lecture by Arkady Moreynis. The topic is investments, projects at the idea stage and, in fact, investments in projects at the idea stage. And we will only be happy if the topic of the lecture changes along the way, depending on the questions of the audience.

During Communication Tube you can:
  • talk about your idea and get a public feedback and advice from the chief analyst "Glavstarta"
  • prepare to speak in front of a huge audience
  • find out what to do at Startup Weekend to make participation in it as useful as possible
  • personally ask questions of interest to Arkady Moreynis


Communication Tube is the first in the line of Glavstarta events.
Next is Startup Weekend , where over the weekend you can get advice from a dozen experienced professionals and involve the famous Runet specialist in your project.
Next is Business Lynch , where, together with our experts, the authors analyze the business models of their projects, proving their viability to business angels and private investors.

Check in:
Tomsk February 28, 6 pm - 9 pm
Novosibirsk March 2, 5 pm - 8 pm
Ekaterinburg March 3, 5 pm - 8 pm

Applications for performances with the project, as always, at idea@glavstart.ru

The event is open. Participation is free.
You can ask your questions here, or in private, at info@glavstart.ru
