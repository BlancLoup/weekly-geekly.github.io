Nmap 6

After 3 years of work since the release of the 5th version of nmap, version 6 of one of the most popular network scanners has finally been released.




Among the major changes in version 6 stated:

  1. Nmap Scripting Engine. More than 300 NSE scripts included in the new release
  2. Improved web crawl
  3. Full IPv6 support
  4. New Nping utility
  5. Improved Zenmap GUI Interface


  6. Increased scanning speed

Download and familiarize yourself with the changes on the official site.
