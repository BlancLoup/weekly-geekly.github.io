Results of the year from the company REG.RU

Hello!

On the eve of the holidays, we are writing our last post this year, in which we sincerely want to congratulate you, to wish you success in the professional field. Let the new year please both you and us with various and interesting technological innovations!

Summing up the year, we could not help remembering our acquaintance with Habr. Somewhere it was successful, in some matters we did not manage to find a common language. But we promise that we will try in the new year, because we know that there are our friends, fans, customers among the residents of Habrovsk. We are grateful to everyone who followed our work in 2010, shared his opinion, helped with advice. This is very important for us, because we are constantly working on ourselves, our main principle is the convenience of all services for both Internet users and market professionals.

Looking after the outgoing year, we would like to recall the main innovations that we launched in 2010, and discuss with you what we have been able to, and that, perhaps, still needs some work.

REG.Panel 3.0

REG.Panel 3.0 we consider one of the important achievements of the outgoing year. This is radically new compared to previous versions of the software for the provision of domain name registration services. The software allows you to create your own dealer network for registering domain names and serve end customers on the basis of your own website in automatic mode. REG.Panel 3.0 introduced new universal user interfaces, more domain zones are available for customers to order (39 + IDN), new services have been added - VPS, Web-forwarding, certificates, internal messaging system, news feed, full-fledged basket, wholesale domain renewal, bulk DNS change, etc. We hope that our developments in this area will help our partners work more efficiently and more conveniently.

STAT.REG.RU

In 2010, the functionality of the unique statistical service STAT.REG.RU was significantly expanded, offering a complete analysis of the market condition and a comprehensive assessment of the data on registered domains in .RU / .SU / . zones. We are pleased that in the past year, not only Internet users and professionals have taken advantage of this service, but also the media, which often refer STAT.REG.RU as a reliable source.

Domain Name Services

In addition to the Reg.Choice service, which allows you to select domains by keywords, we have released the unique REG.Premium advisory service. The system of special filters, a convenient search by various headings, topics, sub-headings, a quick search by keyword make it possible in a few minutes to select a domain name that is suitable for any purpose from among those that are free in one of the national domain zones.

Security

This year, we introduced the Domain Ban Domain Banking service to maximize domain protection against third-party actions or malicious software, as well as Thawte SSL certificates to protect the connection from third-party intrusion and confidential information leakage.

New services

In 2010, we introduced two new key services that our customers were looking forward to: virtual dedicated REG.VPS servers and rental of physical REG.Dedicated servers. For both services, we are pleased to offer you unique tariff plans and a lot of nice bonuses to the bargain, starting with the most prompt service provision and ending with a wide selection of pre-installed operating systems.

Habrovchane, we will be happy to answer any questions and receive any comments on the services and activities of the company REG.RU! And we look forward to next year to present our new ideas to you!
