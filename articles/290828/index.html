The development strategy of the Russian segment of the Internet will be developed until October 1

Russian President Vladimir Putin instructed the Presidential Administration of the Russian Federation, together with the Ministry of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, to develop a development strategy for the RuNet by October 1. ANO Institute for the Development of the Internet, will participate in the development of proposals for the formation of a long-term program for the development of the Russian part of the information and telecommunications Internet, AdIndex reports . The discussion began on May 14th.

The Institute for Internet Development (IRI) was founded in March 2015. Among its founders are organizations such as RAEC, IIDF, ROCIT and ISS.
