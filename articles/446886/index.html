Experts Top 3D Expo: Sunny Wong. Over 25 million sprains can be prevented



On the eve of the upcoming exhibition and conference Top 3D Expo , which will be held on April 19 in Technopolis Moscow, we are talking about the conference participants.


From the company Shining 3D will perform Sunny Wong (Sunny Wong).

Sunny Wong



Sunny Wong has been working in Shining 3D since 2004 and now holds the position of Director of Promotion in Europe, Middle East and Africa, Global Sales Manager.

Theme


Speech by Sunny Wong - 3D Digitizing Solutions: Experience of Shining3D.

Speaker will talk about the experience of Shining 3D, the leader of Chinese industry in 3D scanning, in creating 3D digitization solutions.

About company



Founded in 2004, SHINING 3D is one of the largest Chinese companies in the 3D digitization and 3D printing segment, develops, manufactures and sells 3D scanners and 3D printers, and provides services. The company is based in Hangzhou, China, with offices and representative offices throughout the world, in particular, in Stuttgart, Germany, and San Francisco, California, in the United States.

SHINING 3D develops, manufactures, distributes and applies in its activities a variety of 3D scanners, including industrial and specialized dental, 3D printers for the consumer market and production, materials for 3D printing. The company also offers 3D design and manufacturing services and a cloud platform for storing and using models.


Complex solutions SHINING 3D are successfully applied in many industries: automotive, aerospace, mold and die, electronics, consumer goods, dentistry, orthopedics, museum and art, construction, energy, research, professional education, and so on. .

The company's most sought-after product is the Einscan series of 3D digitizers, dental scanners and metal printers for the professional industrial sector.

Cases


The company recently signed an agreement with the EXO-L team to provide customers with 3D ankle scanning services. A month before, the company announced a collaboration with AGE Solutions on the development of software for digital dentistry.

Orthopedics


More than 25 million ankle sprains are recorded annually in Europe and the USA. Shining 3D and EXO-L have joined forces to make the joint protection individual, which greatly increases its effectiveness.

EXO-L has developed a patented ankle protection against sprains. The company operates in the Netherlands, Belgium and Germany.


EXO-L Ankle Brace - patented ankle protection for sports, work and everyday life. It limits the mobility of the joint in a traumatic direction, while not interfering with its normal functioning.


For effective work of adaptation and the maximum comfort ideal landing is necessary. 3D scanning with Shining 3D EinScan-Pro and Pro + makes the process of adjusting the size and printing of a unique device for each ankle much easier, faster and more accurate.

Cooperation of companies gives customers the ability to scan their ankles anywhere, send scan results for processing and creating digital three-dimensional models and then manufacturing protective devices based on them.


In July 2018, at the invitation of EXO-L, Shining 3D assisted in scanning the players of the German hockey club in Heilbronn, Germany.


The high scanning speed of Einscan-Pro + allows you to scan both ankles in 15 seconds, after which the data is sent to the manufacturer for manufacturing protection.


Dentistry


Digital dentistry professionals lack an integrated solution that contains basic digital instruments for orthodontics: 3D scanning, design, and 3D printing.

Shining 3D is working with AGE Solutions, a dental software provider, to solve this problem. The fruit of the joint efforts of AGE Solutions and Shining 3D is to meet these requirements. It should receive scan information at the input and generate an open standard file that the user can use at his discretion, freely choosing methods for making models and the artist.

Program for orthodontists AGE Solutions Maestro 3D Ortho Studio is integrated into the digital dental solution Shining 3D.


Maestro 3D Ortho Studio is used for orthodontic diagnosis, editing and analysis with the use of high-quality 3D patient jaw scans. The software consists of two main macromodules: Ortho Studio and Dental CAD / Dental Restoration, for diagnostics and treatment planning, including the creation of exact models of crowns and prostheses, respectively.


In addition, the kit includes other modules, such as a virtual installation module, a bracket placement module, a model builder, a PDF3D module, and a Clear Aligner module.
