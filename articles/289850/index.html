Service for recording notes when viewing on-line video

Hello!

Today I discovered this wonderful service made by UniShared (thanks to AdvanTiSS ) for recording abstracts while watching a video and I can not share. The idea is that your outline will be synchronized with video and you can easily return to the relevant moment. All records are stored in Google Drive.

The site is currently supported by Coursera, Udacity, edX, Khan, Vimeo and Youtube.

The Open Source website and its repository are located here: github.com/UniShared/videonotes

All pleasant self-education.
