Pedometer, scales and mobile applications with Lumia 930/925

Introduction

I took for testing the new Nokia Lumia 930, guided solely by my personal interest - is it time to change my own Lumia 925 to a new business flagship? But it just so happened that a simple post about a model turned out to be a post - a description of my organization of a healthy lifestyle using available devices, a phone, and applications running under Windows Phone.


I will tell you more about this, but I'll start with a small comparison of the two Lumia models - 925 and 930. The difference in their entry into the market was about a year.

If we compare the 930 and 925 model, then the first has become much more manly. Rounded edges were replaced by hard edges, and now the phone can be put, and not just put. In theory, the 930 model should be felt in the palm of a more massive - but this does not happen - the geometric parameters are increased in such a way that the difference when wearing and talking is not felt. Although when browsing the pages, it undoubtedly is - the larger screen benefits greatly.

Photo of the phone's end with sharp edges

Design has become more aggressive. On the 930 model added "racing" stripes. Nokia logo is shifted to the right, which seems to be a reasonable decision in the struggle to increase the screen, but preserve dimensions. The camera is removed inside the case and no longer stands out (which was a characteristic feature of the 925 model and, moreover, 1020). What was done quite coarsely in the Lumia 925 is a slot for a SIM card. On the 925 model, it is rather flimsy. This was taken into account - and in the model 930 is done securely.

Requirements

What is needed in order to monitor a healthy lifestyle?

First, it is not strange, you need to walk more. You can jump, crawl and jump, but in a big city it is best to walk. In order to track the movement always and everywhere, you need a pedometer. I can not say that this is a universal device (there are problems, for example, when walking with a stroller or a cart, bicycle), but it is better, perhaps, there is no solution. Thus, we have such an indicator as the number of steps per day.

Secondly, you need to play sports. In my personal case, it's running. Just running - without barriers, without rough terrain, without water barriers - running in the hall and the park. Here you have to combine two solutions at once. On the one hand, it is possible to track the running on the simulator only with a pedometer (if we are talking about recording devices), on the other hand, it will be possible to supplement the pedometer with a spatial reference in the park - gps tracker. Which will help (albeit not always objectively) also with climb and with the correct distance.

An additional required parameter is heart rate tracking. It is necessary, firstly, during the workout itself (it determines the current load on the body), and secondly, when analyzing the distance covered, it was how hard, where, under what conditions. If somewhere the pulse has reached 180 or more, then it may be worthwhile to think in order to reduce the tempo while passing this section.

Third, weight control. Many nutritionists console that he does not solve anything. But nevertheless, there is probably no more objective criterion. Here, probably, any calibrated scales will do. Most often, the fact that you can buy in supermarkets is practically useless. I know only one brand of electronic scales that can be considered as such - it is Tanita. Mechanical in this regard is more reliable.

Fourth, the control of food. Control is not so much the quality of food, how many calories and composition (proteins, fats, carbohydrates). Not all that you can buy is worth eating. In my humble opinion, all diets that work at least as much are built on two principles - either just eat less or eat less fat and more protein. Just eating less is boring. Every day, keeping track of what else you can eat is fun and partly effective.

Fifth, social networks. Of course, many will say - is this something to do with it? My answer is motivation. Run 10 km of the Moscow Marathon and not put in the facebook track? I laid out.

Sixth, photo. It's not even an instagram. You run 5-7-10 km. The sun is shining, smoke is spreading, others are running nearby. There are moments that you want to capture, even on the run.

Like here, when people ran along the Garden Ring, towards the dawn

And finally, the control of the duration of sleep, the amount of water you have drunk, is if everything is already promontory, but something else is not enough. Sleep control in Garmin is implemented crookedly - if you are going to fall asleep you have to hold the button for 6 seconds, and then remember to press it when you wake up. Personally, I almost always forget one or the other, and more often all at once.

That's actually the main part of my story - the realization of all this with the Nokia 930 + Garmin Vivofit + free apps + scales.

Instead of conclusion

Probably, the organizers of the test were not expecting that, but my conclusion is that there is no point in changing the Nokia Lumia 925 to 930. I could conduct my entire test on my own phone. The differences would probably be in the photo, and even then it is unlikely. For almost a year and a half, I have been using the Nokia Lumia 925, and the thought that the phone should be changed, crept in only once, when the back panel jumped out of the impact of the concrete from the aluminum frame. But then this panel was tucked back with a finger, the phone worked, and the thought of the change was gone.

Over the past year and a half, I did not remember what the processor frequency is, and how many cores are there. Unlike platforms on Android, this phone does not accumulate bugs with the duration of use. Maybe someone will disagree, but after a year of using the phone on Android, I could keep a whole list of fun adventures, such as self-dialing the director, screen hangs, or even just trips.

Everything else the manufacturer has updated the operating system to version 8.1 in previous models, so there is no difference.

Often, WP smartphones are charged with a lack of applications.
