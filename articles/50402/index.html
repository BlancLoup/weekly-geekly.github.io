Personal Finance Online

I propose to the court of the habrasoobshchestvo project vKoshelke.ru - online personal finance management system. Initially, the service was planned as a personal financial consultant - an easy-to-use, but at the same time powerful expert system for accounting, monitoring and managing personal finances online.

Concept


Our system is like a compass (rather like a modern GPS navigator), which helps you stay on course and achieve financial independence. Service vKoshelke.ru determines your current financial position by analyzing the information you provide, helps you choose the right, relevant for you, goals and determine their value, as well as recommends the best ways to achieve goals, implement your plans for the future.
The path to achieving a sustainable financial position is not easy, but interesting. To make it much easier and much more interesting, we gathered the best practices, strategies, tips of people who managed to achieve financial independence. These people are our experts and are constantly working to improve the service, the introduction of new methods and effective ways to manage personal finances.
The route from financial insolvency to the sense of freedom in disposing of money is divided into several stages. It is necessary to build the steps of your stairs and climb them. Our system automatically, by several criteria, determines your position on this path, gives hints on reaching the next stage, the construction of the next stage.

First stage


This is the concept of service, then what we are striving for, the direction for further development. After six months of hard work of the team, we can report on the successful completion of the first stage: ‚ÄúCreating a personal finance accounting tool‚Äù
We understand that the tasks we have set are rather laborious and ambitious (and that makes them even more interesting), so it was decided to launch the service functionality step-by-step or iteratively develop it. The first stage of development is aimed at implementing accounting functions.
Accounting for expenses and income is an important component on the path to financial freedom. It helps to assess the current financial condition, creates prerequisites for further successful forecasting, identifying financial problems and difficulties.
However, it is worth remembering that accounting for accounting is a stupid exercise. Accounting allows you to determine your current state vector, so that later you can build your goals.

The following tools are available at this stage:
  • entry into the system of income and expenses;
  • financial goals - accounting for long-term savings;
  • simple accounting of investments: deposits, mutual funds, OMS, work with currencies;
  • planning expenses for the month;
  • construction of various graphs based on the data entered.

What's next?


With such a set of functionality, we have enough competitors, but we will not stop there. In the near future, we will present new features that currently have no analogues in other systems. We also plan to continuously work on improving the quality of the existing functionality.
Your opinion about the project is very important to us, we hope that it will help us to make the service really useful, will guide the development of the project along the right path.
