At the beginning of the first one, all guests were gathered in the conference hall, where the main part of the event began: a presentation of the Galaxy S II and the Galaxy Tab tablets. The first to appear on the stage was the president of the headquarters of Samsung Electronics for the CIS countries June Sang Ho. He noted several of the most important events and achievements that have taken place in the mobile device market and within the company over the past few years:

- In 2010, the smartphone market grew by 60% in the world and by 160% in Russia. Sales of Samsung smartphones over the past year increased by 3 times - by 190%.

- In addition to the flagship Galaxy S, last year a whole line of Galaxy family smartphones was launched, the most prominent representative of which was Galaxy Ace.

- Also in 2010, the Galaxy Tab tablet appeared, which became the sales leader in the tablet market among all competitive devices.
