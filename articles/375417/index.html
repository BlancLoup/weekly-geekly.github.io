It felt like after the electric scooter was quite comfortable, but very slowly. A sort of "turtle extreme". (For comparison, the scooter in my current configuration on asphalt is almost one and a half times faster). Well, in terms of overall dimensions, it is still rather small for visibility on the roadway, which is not the best factor in terms of security. Unfortunately, I really don't have much experience with real off-road or dirts-tracks, so I have enough adrenaline from the forest park so far. Well, as for driving on asphalt roads, it is rather not a cake.

Regarding ergonomics drive - MX650 after the electric scooter is almost fabulous. Landing is more convenient, the steering wheel does not sausage on each small fossa / hummock, the vibration from the road surface is much less noticeable, the position of the hands is more natural. For short trips this is certainly not critical, but when you drive about 30 km per day - the difference is quite impressive.

When replacing the battery, I did not want to bother with high-ampere connectors, but it was not an option for collective farms with twists, so I decided to resort to self-clamping wago-terminal blocks:
