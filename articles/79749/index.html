Little educational program for marking netbooks ASUS


It turned out that many are worried about this issue;) Let's fill the gaps of ignorance using the example of the EeePC 1005HAG.

‚Äú Eee ‚Äù means ‚Äú Easy to learn, Easy to work, Easy to play ‚Äù (Easy to learn, easy to work, easy to play)

The first two digits indicate the diagonal of the device. In this model, it is 10 "(more precisely, 10.1").
The next two digits are the model number.
The letter H means that a hard disk (HDD) is used inside, not an SSD. A - the little motor is the Intel Atom processor . G - availability of WiMAX module. Those. by name alone, it can be understood that this is a very modern model that will be of particular interest to residents of Moscow, St. Petersburg, Ufa and some other million-plus cities.

All letters in one place:
A - The presence on board the processor Intel Atom ;
D - The presence on board the processor Intel Celeron-M ( Dothan );
E - High-capacity battery (for example, in 1000HE as much as 8700 mAh);
H - Hard disk presence instead of SSD-drive;
G - Availability of built-in WiMAX module;
P - Availability on board the new Intel Atom processor ( Pine Trail );
S - SSD-drive instead of a hard disk;

There are also posts 2G , 4G , 8G (in the "old" models - the volume of the built-in SSD-drive in GB), Surf (no webcam) and Go (the presence of a built-in UMTS module).
