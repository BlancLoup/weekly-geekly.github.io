Now you can search for jobs and employees using a mobile phone.



Registration on labor exchanges, job search among acquaintances, buying newspapers with ads go to the distant past. Now, any mobile phone user can easily find a job on a new WAP-portal on employment. For the first time in Ukraine, this project was carried out by the mobile operator Kyivstar, together with the Internet-recruiting server Rabota.ua and the content aggregator Point Com.


Now, in any part of Ukraine, Kyivstar, Ace & Base, DJUICE subscribers can easily find work by going to the ‚ÄúInfo and Services‚Äù section at wap.starport.com.ua . On the WAP portal there are constantly about 4,000 current vacancies of leading companies in Ukraine and Europe, sorted by various specialties and industries. The relevance and freshness of all the information placed on it is achieved by constantly adding and updating from the Rabota.ua database.

An additional service for all visitors is the availability of analytical materials, news, and publications on employment. In addition, users can customize themselves and sending out new vacancies of the section of interest to them by SMS. Also in the near future will be added the possibility of placing a resume of applicants using a mobile phone, which will completely forget about the ads in the printed press.
