BigRep One: A New 3D Printer That Can Print Entire Furniture



Now almost every month new models of 3D printers appear, many of which are modifications of one or another existing model. Nevertheless, sometimes there are also completely original models, which differ from everything that is in the market of printers at the moment.

It is to this class of 3D printers can be attributed BigRep One - a large device that can print all the furniture. The dimensions of such furniture are quite ordinary, for example, 115 * 100 * 125 cm.

The device can work with nylon, special plastic, as well as a material called Laywood - a mixture of polymer and wood fibers. If you ‚Äúprint‚Äù furniture from such material, the result is more or less similar to woodwork.

However, the price of the device is rather big, and it will take a lot of materials. So, BigRep One costs 39 thousand dollars - for this money you can buy excellent furniture from any designer.
