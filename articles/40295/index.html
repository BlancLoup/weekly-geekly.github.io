Want a Mac Pro Workstation?

Take part in the industrial design competition and win the main prize.


JSC "NORD" , the only manufacturer of complex household appliances in Ukraine, invites progressive designers and simply creative personalities to take part in an industrial design competition.
The competition provides 2 categories - a refrigerator and a free-standing stove. The main prize is a Mac Pro Cinema HD Display workstation or a cash equivalent is provided in each category, i.e. participants have 2 chances to win a prize. In addition, in each of the categories, money awards will be given to two more participants, whose works will seem to the organizers the most interesting. The jury of the competition will consist of experts and industry leaders - representatives of the Research Institute of Household Engineering UkrNIIBytMash, as well as progressive practicing designers Design Bureau "KARANDASH", Kiev
The project starts on September 15 and ends on October 31, 2008.
Winners will be announced in each of the 2 categories on November 15, 2008.
If you are interested in the details of the competition or you have already decided to and are ready to fill out an application for participation, go to the website www.dt-nord.donetsk.ua
