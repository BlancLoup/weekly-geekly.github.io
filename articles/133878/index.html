Sony made its own Holodeck



And even more. Using a large number of different technologies and devices, Sony developers have created a virtual reality in which computer images actively interact with the real environment. The result is a very unusual illusions that our brain perceives as reality (well, and what to do, the human brain is so gullible). The results of the work are shown in the video, in the continuation of several videos.

All videos were obtained by a team of specialists from Sony, Studio Output and Marshmallow Laser Feast. It is clear that for the most part all this is an advertising campaign, which should draw the attention of viewers to the PlayStation Store, but everything is done really well. According to the developers, all three clips were obtained in real time, without further processing. All you see are real optical effects taken with a conventional camera.

In the shootings, images projected onto real volumetric objects were used, which were synchronized both between themselves and with objects from real space. The actors, who can be seen, also influenced all this virtual-optical reality. And yes, when shooting used gadgets such as the PlayStation Move, and EyeToy.
