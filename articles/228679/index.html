How I tried to take a bike in a public bicycle rental in St. Petersburg. A look from the IT side

Yesterday in St. Petersburg officially earned a public bike rental with many points in the city. The event was interested, so I was not too lazy to go and try out and find out how everything works. Interest was from the point of view of IT, the quality and level of great interest me much less. Therefore, I think that the article may be of interest to the habrosocommunity.
Ps hubs chose neatly mated articles. Ideally suitable, I think not.



How it all began


Being a supporter of the ecological lifestyle, I have always tried to be interested in cycling infrastructure. There are a lot of rental points in St. Petersburg, the community is large, but until yesterday there was no movement from the state. Got the announcement that on July 3 a public city bike rental opens. Began to study and dig.

The first difficulty was to find out the official website of this bike rental. In one article I found a link to velobikespb.ru, but this domain was not even registered. I wrote to different activists, constantly monitored news from popular media (thanks to Google with its search option for the last hour). As a result, I got a real site - http://velobike-spb.ru/ . Immediately, there was disappointment, because the site was ‚Äúclosed‚Äù in a very primitive way: by redirecting from the main page to the internal one, on which only the logo was located. Not serious, especially considering that the internal pages were available. It was necessary only to know the exact addresses.

  1. If we closed the site, it was necessary to make a normal landing page. With the form of collecting emails, for example, and the further purpose of reporting open registration on the site. Or at least add some text with information when registration becomes publicly available.
  2. Immediately negative impression of the site design. Ala 2000s The feeling that they took the standard template and put the logo. Imkho so now it is impossible


We try to work with the site


When the site was still closed, but it was possible to walk through the internal pages, I tried to register. Did not work, closed. Writes "not enough rights." I called technical support, they said registration will be available on the morning of the official opening. The next day, in the morning, the site was opened for viewing, but you still cannot register. Those. support said that at 16:00 will open.

Ok. Waited registered. Began to deal with pricing to pay. As far as I understood, the principle is the same as in the Moscow bicycle rental (they even say that old Moscow was brought to St. Petersburg). There are two payments:
  • For the right to use. You have to pay to love. If you take the season, the price is very affordable - 950 rubles. Per day - 100 rubles.
  • For the actual travel time. The first half hour is free, more payment appears incrementally


Accordingly, before taking the bike, it is necessary that on the current date you have paid for a subscription for the right to use and at least 50 rubles to pay for the trip.

Payment

He paid, the money in the account did not appear. Instead of 0, it became minus 100. Not understanding what was happening, I tried again to press the "activate tariff" button, I was sent to the payment page (third-party service). I came out, as a result, it became minus 200. I called technical support, telling the situation. While talking money came. They promised to return the fee for the subscription. Ex. Remains the practical part - to test great. By the way, payment notices came in English, which hints at borrowing the entire system from abroad.

Misses the current stage:
  1. Deposit funds should be instant. Users really don't like their money to evaporate.
  2. The personal account menu is intuitively incomprehensible. Below is a screenshot. For example, what is the difference between rental and rental bikes? The first one just shows what I took and where. The second is empty. As if the same.
  3. Found a bug with the repeated withdrawal of the subscription for the already paid period. Today they phoned saying that they fixed it.
  4. The subscription payment interface is also confusing, to be honest
