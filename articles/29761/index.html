MTS: replenish the balance of the phone by credit card

Just yesterday, a friend asked me to throw electronic money on her cellular balance (operator - MTS ). Neither Ya.D. nor WM appeared to me in sufficient quantity, as a result I had to refuse.

I also thought that it would be good for operators to introduce a service to replenish the balance with credit (I would also mean debit cards under them) via the Internet. Agree, it would be convenient? You do not depend on the presence of banknotes in electronic wallets, you do not need to run to an ATM or a payment terminal, there is no link to a specific phone number, etc. Yes, in any case - the more payment methods, the better the subscriber.

And now, I find out that literally this month MTS has already introduced this service.

I even regret that I did not know about it yesterday - I would have tested it with pleasure. We are waiting for similar services from other providers of cellular communication.
