Jailbreak for higher education



The traditional system of higher education is hopelessly behind the times, according to the creators of the startup Degreed. The cost of education grows three times faster than inflation, the debts on educational loans are overwhelming, and the model of lifelong learning in the university that had been formed in the Middle Ages for several years did not at all correspond to modern requirements. Profile on LinkedIn, repositories on GitHub, certificates from serious corporations like Cisco or Microsoft, the results of authoritative online tests today can often replace a formal education certificate.

Degreed is committed to an ambitious goal - to create a single lifelong digital diploma, constantly updated and flexible, which will reflect real knowledge no matter from what source they come from.
