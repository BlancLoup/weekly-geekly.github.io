JustParty.ru. Want a holiday? Do it yourself!

Want a holiday? But tired of paying for the mediocre buffoons? Create your own holiday. Now you do not need to be limited to fantasy. Anything you can think of yourself can be realized. And most importantly - the organization of your imagination will take over JustParty.

That is how one could start a topic about JustParty.ru .

But it is better to just tell why the site is needed and what the value carries. I must say that the project is not mine, but friends.

So now in more detail, why is the JustParty website needed? Probably, you already guessed that the site allows you to create holidays: parties, birthdays, weddings, etc. What is the functionality?
  • The site allows you to control performers and abandon the middlemen.
  • On the site, you can develop a dress code and invitations?
  • With the help of the site you can do only creativity, organizational efforts do not take away forces.
  • If simple - JustParty allows you to simply organize a party.



Functional


The functionality is designed even for the most complex (at first glance) and intricate ideas. Moreover, it can be done independently, without intermediaries. It can be difficult to organize a birthday party for 20 people - they bought little alcohol and food, they did not invite everyone, they missed the place with rent. What to say about the wedding for 500 people?

The interface is intuitive (even very), so everything will be elementary. But a few suggestions:
  • Any event is set up in many ways: place and date, address, invitations, wish-list, notes to clarify the place or description of the dress code and other holiday items.
  • For guests and organizers there is a wonderful section "Gift Ideas".
  • Events can be announced via free SMS-mailing to guests who do not need to register on the site.
  • For each holiday, guests can leave their preferences, and the organizer will immediately calculate the budget.
  • After the event, participants can upload photos and comment on the past.

Financial component


For business, friends have registered LLC "Pati". In total, 20 employees work on the creation of the project. In the next year and a half, friends are planning to invest up to $ 1 million in the development of the project (part of the funds - attracted investors' money).

Market


Friends-entrepreneurs believe that the Russian market of niche social networks is far from saturation. I agree with them. You can even look at this blog (" I am promoting "). If so many narrowly thematic projects are created, then this is necessary for someone.

It is expected that in half a year the number of users registered on the site will exceed 150 thousand. In the event that the popularity of the site grows according to the intended scenario, investments in its development will pay off in two and a half years.
