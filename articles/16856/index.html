Factory of new social web resources

The first products of the project:

1. Platform - a tool for building various social resources Web 2.0 / Web 3.0, ranging from mass and leisure, and ending with professional and business.
2. Business resource - focuses on a variety of high-tech industry without a specific binding to any corporation or a narrow field of knowledge. The resource is created on the basis of the platform and is the first product at the output of the Factory of social web resources, demonstrating its capabilities (see also ) .

Main market:

1. For the Platform and Factory, in general , small and medium-sized companies in various business sectors, web startups of various applications.
2. For the Business resource - specialists, including those that are poorly covered by the existing social networks. These are relatively well-to-do and accomplished individuals who use the resource as a means of mutually beneficial information exchange and professional communication, as well as tools for joint activities that go beyond the narrow corporate interests.

Basic concepts:

- descending SaaS - resource construction scheme;
- focus on the resources of the new wave: more efficient, more intelligent and more motivational.
- a single stream combining various types of information;
- Institute of experts and intellectual search;
- flexible methods for creating interconnected, autonomous and self-governing communities;
- incubator and testing ground for start-up social resources.

Direct prototypes and competitors are not known: neither in terms of market segments, nor in terms of the concept of construction and operation.

Regions of implementation: the project initially focuses on users of the .com domains (primarily the United States) and .ru. It is assumed a slight shift in the implementation phase of these two versions of the project.

The main sources of operating profit are: rent for the platform and hosting resources, fees for Business services and other resources generated by the project, advertising.
