"Text Radio"

Service for periodic publication in the network of long texts in parts, in the format of the rss-channel or just a separate page. After registration, the participant can upload his text to the server and set up the way it is published. The principle of radio broadcasting - the text in the channel appears in parts, and at each particular point in time only a few parts are available, but not all the text. Over time, new parts appear in the broadcast, and the old parts disappear.

The reader receives a small amount of mandatory reading every day, along with news channels. The same text, of course, is available to the reader entirely, but he constantly postpones his reading ‚Äúfor later‚Äù (the analogy with a movie on a dvd-disk, the viewing of which is constantly postponed and showing the same movie on TV).

Such a service is necessary for people suffering from the syndrome of constant information hunger and inability to focus on a long text.

The problem with copyright on texts is solved by the fact that the text is published by the participant and under his responsibility, the service team only provides a technological platform for publication.

Related services - contextual advertising (new books, events, libraries), announcements of all sorts of interesting reading, featured channels (for texts from the public domain).
