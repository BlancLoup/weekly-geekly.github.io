Russian-speaking crowdfunding got a new service similar to the American Patreon.com

Hello, habrazhiteli, I want to introduce you to our new project SkySe .

Skyce is a platform that allows you to carry out active creative work and receive for it the monthly salary formed by your supporters, fans, sponsors and other people with similar interests.

Classic crowdfunding platforms are committed to raising funds for a one-off and rather large project. The main difference between the Skies site and the classic crowdfunding is that the fundraising is not for a one-time project, but for the monthly ongoing support of a person or team.

Thus, musicians, artists, bloggers, inventors, and other figures who have fans or supporters can gradually free themselves from employment at work that does not meet their vocation and receive a steady income from activities interesting to their supporters.



It is enough to have 330 fans or supporters on Skys participating in the ongoing support of 100 rubles so that the monthly income exceeds the official average salary in Russia.

Subscribers of various figures establish a monthly autopayment for the desired amount in support of the creator from the card or account within the system, which provides stable and high-quality support in the long term.

At the moment, a huge number of creative people, activists and groups with varying success collect funds for their activities by publishing e-wallets and other details on pages in social networks, adding them at the end of video content and other ineffective ways. Skays site allows you to bring the quality of collective funding to a new level, combining all participants in a single social network with search, rating, news feed and automatic payment system.

The site team sets itself the task of developing a culture of collective support in Russia. Not only initiation of a crowd project, but also the support itself can be an exciting and interesting activity.
