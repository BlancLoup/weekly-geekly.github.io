Cartoon "Gypsy": If you see in the picture

If you see in the picture
Drawn river
Or spruce and white frost,
Or the garden and the clouds
Or snow plain,
Or a field and a hut, -
Necessarily picture
The landscape is called.


Each of us in his own way represented landscapes, against which the action of the cartoon takes place. This is not a complete list of parts:
- steppe (with wind)
- well (with bucket)
- cemetery (with enclosure)
- forest (dense, there are animals in it and nothing is visible at all)
- field (grains of the first group, hybrid varieties, 25-30 centners per hectare due to drought)
- mountains (with climbers, cable car and Courchevel)
- river (banks are steep, but shallow)
- the abyss (deep and scary)
- the village (there in the distance on a hillock, smoke comes from the pipes and it is not clear what they drown in the summer)
- herd (cows, horses, pigs, tourists)
- monument to the unknown vandal (bronze, monumental)
- A crossroads (with a stone "Wherever you go - KHAN!")
- cabbage beds (with cabbage, watchman and pests)
- garden (apple. Romantic)


In order to bring all the ideas about the landscape together, it was possible to confer and draw or go to nature and take a picture. But the team decided not to go easy ways and therefore decided to embody the landscape in artistic modeling. On the landscape went mountains of plasticine, bundles of matches, a lot of time and a lot of nerves. It turned out, in our opinion, very, very nice.
