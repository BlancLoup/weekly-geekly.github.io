Snom D725 IP Phone Review


We continue a series of articles with reviews of multifunctional office IP-phones by Snom. At this time, the story will be about the model D725, relating to the average price segment.

Model features

The display is monochrome, with adjustable brightness. It is only slightly larger than the youngest models.
