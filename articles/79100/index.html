Store widgets with information and news graphics for online publications

The design bureau of Artyom Gorbunov opened the infograms.ru website through which infograms will be distributed - interactive charts in Flash, each on its own theme. Such an infogram can be freely inserted into a personal blog; for collective blogs, forums and mass media it costs 1000 rub. / piece.

Here is a screenshot of the first swallow (sorry, there were no thousands on the flash drive):



Artyom Gorbunov says and shows:
We made this infographic in the form of a widget, which can be inserted into the site of the publication in the article instead of an illustration. This is the first product in the infogram store that we open in a few hours and this will be the first product to be released here. Further, starting from January 15, we will issue three infograms per week , and we will continue to increase this number. Now interactive infographics is quite a hot topic, the best things are happening in the West right now, in a newspaper, for example, The New York Times - absolutely crazy things are done there. We want this format to appear on the websites of our publications and media.

We work with information partners, the first of which was the Public Opinion Foundation. Together with them, we will be doing infographics in the context of sociology on a variety of topics: politics, society ... on more popular, more popular science topics. For example, we will have things related to scientific visualization ... tell people a little more about what is the flu, how it works, why it is swine, goat, and so on. Another partner is the Wildlife Fund, and together with National Geographic magazine we make infographics on the topic of tiger conservation.
