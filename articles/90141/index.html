Bug-listening bug

I urgently needed to assemble a listening device with my own hands. I started to google wool. There were quite interesting things, from the synopsis of the series "Invisible" to "one hundred ways to hack a VKontakte account."

It was not so easy to solve my problem, although one original recipe was found. On the site of radio amateurs, I found a transmitter circuit with a decent range - up to 300 m. However, in order to adjust this circuit, some experience is needed.





Details:

VT1 - any type of transistor KT315 (KT3102). Pick up depending on the required sensitivity of the microphone.
VT2, VT3 - KT368 (Gain - at least 100). It is advisable to use in a metal case.
M1 - microphone type "pine", FEM-3, or some import.
L1 - 3 turns with 0.5 mm wire on a 5 mm frame.
L2 - 2 turns with 0.5 mm wire on a 5 mm frame.
L3 - 8 turns of wire 0.25 mm on the frame 5 mm.
After assembly, it is desirable to place the entire circuit in a metal case.
