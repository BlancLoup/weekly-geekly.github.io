Wizee shopping center guide: 200 thousand downloads, 100 thousand users and 140 shopping centers

Wizee Shopping is a mobile guide to shopping centers, which allows you to always have on hand maps of shopping centers, as well as be aware of promotions and discounts of shopping centers, search for the necessary shops, restaurants, ATMs and much more.

140 trade centers in 10 cities of Russia and Ukraine are collected in Wizee. The application is available for iPhone , iPad , Android and Windows Phone .

In this article, we will talk about new versions of our application, and also share interesting statistics.





A new version


After the release of the application, we continued to improve it: adding new features and improving usability. But at some point they realized that it was time to seriously review the way of building the interface, then they decided to do Wizee 2. The main feature of the 2nd version was the emergence of a global menu through which it became possible to find the right store, look at the discount section or look through the collections without going into shopping center.



In the second version, some technical improvements were made, for example, maps are saved offline, and updating them will take a few seconds and a few bytes of traffic.

Interesting statistics


We are glad that you have read or at least flipped through the page before this section. In gratitude, we will share interesting facts.

For a start, some pretty numbers. Since its launch, Wizee has downloaded more than 200 thousand people and more than 100 thousand of them have become users of the application. In the six months of the existence of the second version of Wizee, users have viewed 500 thousand times the information on shopping centers and more than 1.5 million times the information on stores in these shopping centers.

The Moscow audience of Wizee is 80%, St. Petersburg - 15%, other cities - 5%

After the introduction of notifications that the user is near the shopping center, our daily audience has grown 3 times, but in the last release we temporarily removed this function for technical reasons.


The most popular shopping center in Wizee is the European shopping and entertainment center, the brand is Zara and the restaurant is McDonalds.

At 70% of our users on iOS geolocation is allowed, and 35% of them use Wizee in close proximity to the mall.
