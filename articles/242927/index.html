Interest in Facebook games is steadily falling


Facebook presented its results for the third quarter of 2014, which even surpassed analysts' expectations and amounted to $ 3.2 billion. Also, DAU's growth to 703 million and MAU to 1.12 billion.

Despite all this, CFO David Wehner described a rather gloomy picture in the gaming direction of Facebook, saying that although there was an overall increase in payments, the players made 2% less money into the games for this quarter, and this percentage will only continue to grow .

However, it is worth noting that on the above chart it is clearly seen that from advertising revenues continue to grow actively and overlap the internal payments of Facebook. Also, there is not a word about Oculus in the report, although the R & D budget has grown to $ 608 million, which is almost two times more than last year.


Million Unity Asset Store users


Not a week without Unity news! Four years ago, the Asset Store was opened. During this time, more than 15,000 assets and ready-made applications created by 3,800 developers were uploaded to the store, which saved a lot of money and time to other developers. According to the creators of the Asset Store, by the end of the year the number of developers using tools and models from the store to create their games will reach a million people!

If all these models and tools were made by the developers themselves, they would spend an additional 6 million working days, which translates into finance - about 1.4 billion dollars.



It also gave developers the opportunity to earn money by selling their tools and assets.

Well-known to Unity-developers, the NGUI and PlayMaker tools brought more than one thousand dollars to their creators, allowing them to earn extra income, and for some people the main income.
