Yandex learned to search on Facebook

Today, Yandex announced the start of a new collaboration with Facebook, in which it will receive data on all new posts by Russian Facebook users.

Thanks to this, with the help of Blog Search, for the first time, you can search by the records of Russian users, taking into account morphology and full-fledged query language. In the future, their comments will also appear in the search.
For example: blogs.yandex.ru/search.xml?text=Yandex&holdres=mark&server=facebook.com



Entries will fall into the search in real time. In the Blog Search, you can find entries for FB users from Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and Kazakhstan. Also, Yandex, as part of this agreement, received a stream with entries from Turkish users.

Yandex has long paid great attention to the search for social services. In particular, in 2004, the first of the big search engines, Yandex launched a separate Blog Search service .

It first appeared real-time search for the records and comments of the Russian part of LiveJournal. Already a few years ago in the Blog Search, it became possible to search for public records of users of the largest Russian social networks, and two years ago, Yandex signed an agreement to receive the full data stream from Twitter.

Thus, Yandex now has real-time data from all major social services that are popular in Russia and have public access to records.

Of course, Facebook data will not only be used in Blog Search. We will improve with the help of these and other services, including the basic search on the Internet - as easy to find content from there, and to improve the ranking, speed and quality of responses.
