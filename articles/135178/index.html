Radeon HD 7970 Graphics Card Announced



AMD has officially announced the Radeon HD 7970 (Tahiti XT chip), which will be the fastest graphics card with a single GPU on the market. The new Tahiti graphics processor is made on the 28 nm process technology, consists of 2048 stream processors with a clock frequency of 925 MHz. The processor is built on the architecture of Graphics Core Next, which replaced the VLIW4. The board also has 3 GB of GDDR5 memory connected via a 384-bit bus.

The card supports DX11.1 rendering and shows record performance in tests, by about 15-20% ahead of the flagship single-processor product Nvidia GeForce GTX 580, and in some tests the Radeon HD 7970 is quite comparable with dual-processor cards.
