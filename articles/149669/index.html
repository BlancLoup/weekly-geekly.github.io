Flash Player goes with Android, users are asked to remove the plugin

Today, a significant event took place: a message appeared on the Flash Player page on Google Play that the program will not be updated anymore, and is not supported for versions above Android 4.0.x.

Thus, the company has put the final point in its decision to abandon the development of Flash Player for mobile devices.

The Flash Player plugin was quite popular on Google Play: almost everyone who downloaded it put the program on top. The formal reason for the refusal of support was that Flash Player was unstable on the latest version of Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean). Now Adobe asks to remove the Flash Player of everyone who has upgraded to the latest version of Android.
