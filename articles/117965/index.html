Innovation Project Competition

April 29, at 6:30 pm Friday in St. Petersburg, in the building of the Council of Rectors (V.O., Exchange Line 4), the first Innovators Project Competition this year will be held this year.

The competition will be held within the framework of the high-tech conference and will bring together a solid composition of experts from various fields.

The intrigue of the event will be that all projects will receive not only weighted assessments of competent experts and feedback from the audience of the competition, but also the opportunity to win valuable prizes for their workings and publish a description of their project in a collection of conference articles.

Invited experts will help the contestants to identify the strengths and weaknesses of the technologies they promote. At the same time, each viewer present will also have the opportunity to influence the results of the assessments and personally participate in the selection of leaders thanks to a collective voting system.

We are sure that the work on a startup usually rests on the first stages only on the ambitions and dedication of the author and his team. And the lack of a positive response and approval of this activity from the external environment becomes the prime cause of the freezing and dying of many projects.
