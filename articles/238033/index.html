Come to KRI 2014



In the calendar of all Russian game developers, the days from 2 to 4 October 2014 are circled in red. At this time, Crocus Expo will host one of the most important annual events in the Russian gaming industry - KRI 2014 conference. The history of the CWI has been taking place since 2003, and therefore bears the proud title of the oldest annual event in Russia for gaming industry professionals. Here, experts from various professions gather to exchange experience: programmers, designers, animators, artists, scriptwriters and many others.

By the way, starting this year, the CWI takes place simultaneously with another event, the locomotive, the IgroMir exhibition. This event is intended primarily for the players themselves, here you can see almost all computer, console and mobile gaming innovations. Big and noisy show. So from the point of view of the gaming industry professionals, the joint holding of the GDC and IgroMir is very convenient, you can show yourself and see people and share your experience.

Of course, our gaming department will take part in this conference. We have prepared a whole troop of speakers, one can say the color of our games. They do not hide their rich professional experience and are eager to share it with the developer community. To make it easier for you to navigate, we have prepared brief announcements for each performance.

Development and trends in IMO (Hall 1, October 3)
Alexander Mishulin, creative director of the studio Allods Team

Any game genre evolves over time, it is a completely natural process. Approaches, unwritten rules, genre standards are changing. And it was especially clearly manifested in the development of the Allods Online and Skyforge projects. With their examples, Alexander will demonstrate how different the approach to the same gaming features that form the basis of the MMORPG genre. Of course, he will also give a detailed account of the reasons why the development team applied certain solutions without blindly repeating the same standardized approaches.

Skyforge: rendering technology (Hall 1, October 3)
Sergey Makeev, technical director of the studio Allods Team

Sergey will share the details of creating a render system that is used in the Skyforge game engine. In particular, we will discuss the physically correct system of materials and lighting, post-processing, deferred shading, interaction with translucency, multiple optimization techniques and many other technologies used in this render system.
