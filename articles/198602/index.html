Increase store conversion through purchases on credit

If you want to achieve maximum store efficiency and sales growth, first of all you should pay attention not to the number of visitors attracted by advertising, SEO and affiliate programs, or even to the quality of traffic, which is conventionally characterized by the number of views per visitor, calls from the site and other targeted actions.

Despite the fact that the number of sales obviously depends on these indicators, it makes sense to increase them (and invest money in it) only when the store site itself, as a sales tool, provides an acceptable conversion for the business. Otherwise, the money spent on advertising and marketing will be wasted. You will sell your visitors the ruble at 90 kopecks. Therefore, absolutely any opportunity to increase the conversion rate is much more important for the store than the opportunity to attract additional traffic to the site.

One of the obvious and effective ways to increase store conversion is credit sales. By giving visitors the opportunity to get a loan for the purchase in your store, you increase the conversion of the site and get additional sales. The possibility of buying on credit actually becomes a mandatory requirement for a modern store with an average check of more than 3,000 rubles, especially as regards appliance stores, electronics and furniture.
