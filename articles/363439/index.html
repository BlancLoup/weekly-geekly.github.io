Suitcase without lightning and charging for devices

Tell me, how often did you lose your suitcase when traveling? Did you have to pay extra for extra weight at check-in because you simply did not have time to weigh your luggage before departure? Do you know any unpleasant situations with lightning, wheels and other telescopic handles?

It seems that at some point all these problems with luggage just got the guys from the New York-based startup project Trunkster , who decided to create a reference sample of a modern suitcase - utilitarian, durable and stylish.




And most importantly, however, not to say that this is so unexpected - there are not so few people with similar problems, as well as the amount of the fund they provide: more than 650 backers and more than $ 230,000 of investment (this is with the stated goal of $ 50,000 !)

But what did the former backpackers buy when they decided to trade their rumpled backpacks for this miracle of design and engineering?

The project presents two types of luggage - Carry-On and Cheked with a capacity of 43 and 86 liters, respectively. The supporting structure and side walls are made of durable and lightweight aircraft-grade aluminum. But the access system inside the baggage is truly revolutionary and made on the principle of sliding curtains of hard and hard polycarbonate, frosted black.



By the way, in order to help all lovers or, rather, lovers cram everything into their luggage, they have developed a special ‚Äútamping‚Äù mechanism, combined with a closing panel.

The emphasis on lovers of traveling with a small luggage is not accidental - the startup emphasizes the versatility of their suitcases. And the marketing strategy of the project is the same backpack, only more reliable, more capacious, more functional and stylish - it finds its bright embodiment in the key details of the product.

Rigid telescopic handle on the entire width of the suitcase - remember how often you yourself suffered with narrow handles and trying to turn over the luggage.

Practical wheels with ceramic bearings and a working surface made of the newest rubber compound - asphalt, paving stones, paving slabs and even steps will not cause them any harm.

Well, so that all these performance characteristics do not remain a proto-declaration, the founders of a startup - a full exclusive among all projects on Kickstarter, provide a 5-year warranty for both their models. Any more questions?

Probably not. But there are additional chips in Trunkster suitcases that make them the absolute exclusive in the luggage market.

So, in order.

First, the display built into the handle at the moment of lifting the suitcase shows its weight - now you will not have problems registering, you can distribute things in advance:



Secondly, a battery is built into the suitcase, on a single charge of which you can charge your phone up to 9 times - now there are no throwings around the airport building and the streets of an unfamiliar city with the extended wire in your hand:



Well, maybe the most important thing - from a value point of view, for sure - is an additional GPS sensor (+40 $), which functions on the Apple Find My Mac principle. That is, in extreme cases, at least you will find a suitcase.

Which, by the way, is not bad in itself, considering how much this miracle costs, more precisely, it will cost after the completion of funding. Namely: Carry-On - $ 500, Cheked - $ 600. However, if you invest now with a delivery date in August 2015, the prices will be $ 255 and $ 295, respectively.

The weight of the suitcase version of the Carry-On is approximately 3.5 kg, with packaging it will pull approximately 4.2 kg. With LiteMF it can be sent to Moscow for $ 59.
