Will the robot do your job?



Machines can do amazing things. But what you are really interested in is whether your work will exist in the future?

Researchers have tried to make a forecast of how technology will affect the labor market in 20 years. Find your profession in the list to see what awaits you in the future.

What is the hardest job for a robot? Social workers who deal with problems of mental health and substance abuse (in the section "Communities and Social Services"). This work has a 0.3% chance of being automated. This is due to the fact that she has a high intelligence rating, the need to negotiate and help others. And what kind of work will be automated most quickly? Telemarketers. No wonder: this is already happening.

Researchers recognize that these estimates are too general and probably not entirely accurate. But think: after all, this may be our future, in the opinion of smart people. If they say that your duties will soon be able to perform robots, you must be warned and prepared.



The test is compiled according to the University of Oxford .
