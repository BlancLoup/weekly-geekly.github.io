New "cute" Shuttle

Shuttle has announced its all-in-one desktop.

The novelty, called the X50, is equipped with an Intel Atom 330 processor, 1 GB of RAM and a 160 GB hard drive. The display in the X50 has a diagonal size of 15.6 inches and a resolution of 1366x768 pixels.

The PC has a Wi-Fi module (IEEE 802.11b / g / n), a 4-in-1 card reader, five USB ports, a VGA output and a 1.3 megapixel camera. There are a pair of speakers with a capacity of 2 watts each.

The PC is preinstalled with Windows XP Home.

The manufacturer notes that the new X50 "eats" quite a bit of electricity - 30 watts and, moreover, it works very quietly. The noise level does not exceed 22 dB.

Unfortunately, there is no exact information about the Shuttle X50 date of sale and its price. But it should cost, according to preliminary data, no more than $ 500.
