In this small topic, I would like to talk about the various ways of withdrawing money earned by overwork. To begin with, it is worth mentioning how my work is organized. I use only two options - oDesk and work directly. It has not been possible to work with clients from Russia yet, so there will not be such familiar withdrawal options as WebMoney and Yandex.Money.

As you know, oDesk offers the following ways to withdraw money:
1) Payoneer Debit Card
2) Moneybookers
3) Paypal
4) Direct Deposit / ACH
5) Wire Transfer
Pay attention to each of the methods.

Payoneer Debit Card . First you need to order the card itself. It is easy to do, you just need to correctly write your address in English. The card will come in the usual letter, it will be simply enclosed in an envelope with accompanying paper. The card is activated through the site, and it can already be used. With Odeska money gets fast enough. In this case, the funds appear in your account, but the card (by default) does not immediately fall. They need to be downloaded there. Option two - download in the usual way (up to two business days), or download quickly (within two hours, but for $ 4.5). You can make the choice of the boot option made automatically when transferring money. During my use of the card, the terms of use changed, this was mainly in the amount of commissions (monthly fee). Currently, such conditions are $ 1 per month, if over the past month there were more than two transactions on the card, if from 0 to 2, then $ 3. The card can be replenished without Odesa (or another Payoneer partner) using another card. At the same time, a commission of 4.5% is taken. Summarize:

Moneybookers . Payment system in which you open a virtual account and use it. Moneybookers is not very common and is known in our country, abroad, its popularity, apparently, is also much inferior to such systems as PayPal, for example. The special feature of Manibukers is a very tight security system. By default, money can be displayed on the card and bank account. But after a while you will be asked to complete the verification procedure. Verification comes down to the following: a small amount is transferred to your bank account, and there is a verification code in the field of payment purpose (reference). You need to go to the bank (or Internet bank, if possible), and ask what is written there in the purpose of payment. Everything works, everyone is happy. Only I just did not work out, because when asked about what was in the reference field, the girl in the bank patted her eyes and said that they did not have this. I had to demand a printout of the translation details, ask to put a seal on it, scan it and send it to Moneybookers support (by the way, they speak Russian, which is undoubtedly a plus). The card to which money can be transferred from the account in Moneybookers can also be verified. Just like in PayPal, a small amount is withdrawn from the card, and for verification you need to enter its exact value (2.56 dollars for example). In addition, there are withdrawal limits. Verification directly affects the outgoing transaction limit. In addition to verification of the card and bank account, you can get a letter to your address and enter the code in the letter. Then the limit of outgoing transactions will increase significantly. By the way, about withdrawal to a plastic card - you should inquire about the possibility of this in your bank in advance, because At some banks, the policy prohibits direct replenishment of cards from abroad.
