Facebook has agreed to purchase Gowalla



The geolocation service Gowalla has recently been purchased by the social network Facebook, as reported by "informed sources". The service itself is a kind of analogue of Foursquare, and at one time these two services competed almost on equal terms. True, some time ago it became clear that Foursquare is still much stronger, so to speak. It is worth noting that Gowalla was founded in 2009, so by today's standards it is almost a veteran of the industry of geolocation services.
