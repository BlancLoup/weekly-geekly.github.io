48-hour online Angularjs Attack hackathon


Angular Attack is a 48-hour online hackathon using Angularjs

How it works?


  1. Put together a team
    Teams consist of 1-4 people. Do not forget to register .
  2. Build the application in 48 hours
    You will have 48 hours to make the application of your dreams. The competition will begin on May 14th UTC .
  3. Voting / Winner Selection
    The judges and the other participants will review the applications, give marks and give feedback to the participants.
  4. Winner Announcement
    Winners will be announced on May 23. Presentation of prizes.

Important Dates


Registration opening - March 1
Application Development - May 14-15
Winners Selection - May 16-22
Winners Announced - May 23
