Video review tablet Asus Google Nexus 7 (2013)



Nexus 7 (2013) is a new generation of tablets jointly developed by Asus and Google. The new generation still uses the 7-inch IPS display, but the resolution has grown to 1920x1200. The filling has also undergone significant changes, now the tablet runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 quad-core processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz, which gives a performance boost of almost two times compared with the previous generation model. The amount of RAM has also been doubled and is now 2 GB.

The new model has acquired a rear camera with a resolution of 5 megapixels. The amount of internal storage is 16 or 32 GB. Also available are models with the 4G LTE module, which operates on the territory of the Russian Federation.
