"Steel Tambourine" All-Ukrainian Conference of System Administrators

We invite everyone to attend the First All-Ukrainian Conference of System Administrators " Steel Tambourine ."
The event will happen in Lviv on November 27, 2010 in the conference hall of the Dnister Hotel, which will be on ul. Mateyka, 6.

The conference will feature presentations by leading Ukrainian specialists in the field of cloud technologies, 1C administration, a presentation of the European experience in implementing ERP systems, an overview of the development of terminal technologies over the past five years.

The conference is focused on a comprehensive exchange of experience of specialists in the field of network solutions.

Beginning to be at 10 am.

They have already promised to come to the IT matinee:
  • Igor Shastitko from Microsoft , with two reports, the topic of which has not yet been disclosed;
  • Andrei Novosad from Peterson Systems LLC , with a saga of terminal solutions (past, future, desktop virtualization, remote access to 3D applications);
  • Yuri Kukharchuk from Adelina call center , with an opus about the everyday life of terminal call center servers;
  • Rostislav Dobriansky and Vladimir Vitkalyuk from Rosan-Agro , with a dialogue on the implementation of the CDN XL ERP accounting system at the Rosana meat-processing complex;
  • Mikhail Garbar from Media Service , with a story about glitches and solutions when installing 1C on the Windows Server OS family.


Who else are waiting for:
  • knowledgeable, willing to share their experience, knowledge, interesting about the specifics of their work;
  • caring, having the opportunity to warn about the rake in the way of implementation and deployment of operating systems, networks and other IT benefits;
  • economic, capable of telling recipes for success in inventory, updating, software support and hardware;
  • as well as all IT-dependent and sympathetic ones - from system administrators to IT managers and technical directors.

The holiday will be held with the support and participation of:
  • Microsoft
  • Aria Group, a group of official dealers of Volkswagen, Honda, Nissan
  • "Asign", web development company

  • "M joy", online-radio
