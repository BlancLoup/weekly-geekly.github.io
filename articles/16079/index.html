Yandex gives advertising network partners $ 2.5 million per month

Yandex has summed up the first results of its advertising network . Over the past year since the launch of a full-scale launch, its audience has increased by 3 times, the monthly number of targeted referrals to advertisers' websites - by 4.3 times, and monthly payments to partners - by 4.5 times.

On average, each partner earns 7,000 rubles a month through the Advertising Network *. In absolute terms, payments are more than 63 million rubles a month.

Participants in the Yandex Advertising Network are the majority of the search sites on the Russian, Ukrainian, and Belarusian Internet. So, the search services Aport.ru, Mail.ru, Gogo.ru, Nigma.ru, Km.ru, Tut.by, All.by, Bigmir.net, Ukr.net, Poisk.ru entered the Advertising Network. In addition, advertisements for Yandex clients are also posted on many popular content resources, including Hand-to-Hand, Livejournal.ru, Liveinternet.ru, Mail.ru news and mail.
