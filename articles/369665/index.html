Chinese LeEco introduced two smartphones to the Russian market, promised to supply electric vehicles in 2018



This evening in Moscow Digital October, the Chinese technology company LeEco (formerly Letv), operating under the brand LeRee (Russia & Eastern Europe), announced the appearance on the domestic market of two Android smartphones, as well as the delivery of the LeSee electric vehicle of the Faraday Future assembly in 2018 .

The LeEco smartphone line is represented by two devices: Le 2 and Le Max 2.



Le 2, the retail price of 14,990 rubles, has the following key characteristics: a 16 megapixel camera, a 8 megapixel front camera, 3 GB of RAM and 32 flash disks, 2 sim card slots. Eight-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 652 processor, proprietary CDLA ( ? ) Audio system. 5.5 "in-cell display in a metal case in two colors (gray and gold).



Le Max 2 is positioned as a flagship model, priced at 29,990 rubles, carries on board: a screen of 5.7 inches with a density of 515 pixels per inch with a resolution of 2k, a Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor, graphics Adreno 530, 6GB of RAM and 64GB of disk. 21 megapixel main camera (4k and slow-motion video recording options) with image stabilization and dual flash, six lenses, 8 megapixel front camera. Ultrasound fingerprint scanner.
