Ergonomic concept for Nokia



Finnish designer Heikki Juvonen began his work on the concept of a mobile phone for Nokia by sitting down and writing out on paper all the non-obvious shortcomings of modern mobile phones. In the first place was the weight distribution and it was her hot Scandinavian guy who tried to fix it. As a result, the concept assumes that the keyboard unit must be the heaviest part of the phone. Due to this, the tube will sit in the palm of your hand like a glove. Well, and the curved shape should contribute to a more convenient set.
