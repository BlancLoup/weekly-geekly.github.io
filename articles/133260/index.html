Hackers made another "climategate"


As a rule, the actions of individual hackers and their groups are perceived by society in a negative context, although, nevertheless, sometimes in their actions you can actually find some element of "ethical" and social protest. As an illustration of the last thought, one can cite the actions of the well-known Anonymous group, which led to the cessation of one of the large servers that distribute child pornography.

In anticipation of the start of the UN Climate Summit, hackers published a correspondence of members of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC), the meaning of which is this: combating global warming is carried out by aggressive investments of incredible size in energy companies, which should bring greenhouse gas emissions to an acceptable level, which is said to be the beginning of positive changes in climate. In particular, hacker (s) argue that, according to the IPCC correspondence, the world should invest $ 37 trillion in energy companies and energy-saving technologies by 2030, which will lead to global poverty, despite the fact that the harmful effects of emissions are not recognized as an absolute factor climate change.
