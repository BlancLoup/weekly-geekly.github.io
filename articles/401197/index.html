The founder of Virtus.pro became the owner of the business of equipment for the interception and storage of traffic


Virtus.pro founder Anton Cherepennikov

Anton Cherepennikov, the founder of the eSports organization Virtus.pro, in which businessman Alisher Usmanov invested $ 100 million in 2015, became the sole owner of Osnova Lab, according to Vedomosti. About the redemption of shares of other shareholders, Egor Falileev and Dmitry Zolotukhin, Cherepennikov himself told the publication.

Osnova Lab is engaged in the development of software and hardware for systems of operational search activities (SORM). Now, according to the current legislation, telecom operators install similar hardware complexes on their channels for interception and storage of information for a period of 12 hours.

State technical experts call the use of SORM systems as one of the ways to implement the anti-terrorism package of Yarovaya-Ozerov laws. Earlier, Osnova Lab was negotiating the purchase of MFI Soft, a developer of information security systems, Internet traffic filtering systems and SORM. On the basis of these two companies can be created production, which will be aimed at meeting the needs of telecom operators, providers and IT companies in equipment for the implementation of the norms of the package of laws of Yarovo-Ozerov.

The representative of the USM stressed that Cherepennikov is precisely the partner of the holding in the field of eSports (and not a subordinate or employee of the USM - Ed.). At the same time, the holding stated that USM had invested "in a fund that funds the creation of IT companies in the field of integration and IT consulting, in which Anton is the managing partner."
