The history of joint trips in Russia and abroad



The first precedents of joint trips are known to a person from time immemorial (hitchhiking) - hitchhiking, when two strangers to each other make a joint trip. The driver, going somewhere , sees the passenger - a person (hitchhiker), who is given a gesture (raised thumb) and ‚Äúpicks up‚Äù him. As a rule, such trips are free (but not always), and the safety of such movements cannot be guaranteed. However, they have spread throughout the world and are used even today. An example of such a trip can be seen in the 2000 American comedy "Road Trip" (road adventure).



In the 1940s , the basic direction of joint travel appeared - carpooling. The appearance was due to the needs of the Second World War, and then in the 1970s in the United States (the oil crisis). The sources from which people learned about such trips were posters, they were of the nature of mass propaganda and served as motivation, along with slogans calling for saving fuel for the good of the Motherland, contributing to the victory in the war , etc.
In connection with the advent of information technologies into our lives, the pace of life naturally increased, the main criterion of which was a limited resource - time. The user has become important simplicity, speed and ease of use of the service (application). As an independent direction, Carpooling mutated and divided into many subspecies:

Vanpool

Vanpool - joint group trips from 7 to 15 people for short or long distances within a certain social group (schoolchildren, students, colleagues , etc. ), which are, as a rule, permanent (as scheduled). Often such trips are organized with the help of a freight forwarding system that has professional drivers and vans (minibuses) at their disposal.
As drivers can also alternately act colleagues themselves. The fee for transportation services is based on the principle of a subscription fee (this includes the cost shared between the participants for paying for gasoline, as well as the subscription fee at the rate of the service providing special software and organizing the trip in this way). The distance of such trips is fixed, the software is responsible for the rational consolidation of trip members. For vanpool vans, as a rule, special parking spaces marked with road signs are reserved at the points of departure and destination.
Such trips in Russia have not yet found application, but in the US they have been used for a long time:

Real-Time Ridesharing

Real-Time Ridesharing - joint travel services, a logical continuation of the classic carpooling, two or more people move in one car. The existence of this type is due to the satisfaction of needs caused by the high rate of human life in modern society. Distinctive features are:

The motivation for using ridesharing-services is based on the rational use of free passenger seats in private

Slugging
Slugging - joint trips of a permanent (commute) character, occurring at the request of a passenger waiting at a specially designated, road sign (slug line)
The incident of the first joint slugging trip occurred about 38 years ago (in 1975) in Washington DC (almost immediately after the HOV bands for carpool and vanpool trips were designed and opened). The main motivation of drivers during such trips is the occupancy of their car (at least 3 people with the driver in mind) for driving along a dedicated speed lane - HOV lane ( High-occupancy vehicle lane). On such lanes, it was possible to move at a speed of 65+ miles / hour instead of a limit of 55 miles / hour. Widely distributed in several US states (Washington DC, San Francisco, etc.)


Passengers wishing to take advantage of this type of joint travel, go to special websites , choose the slug line nearest to them, come to the appointed place and wait for their driver in a live queue, the principle is First Come - First Served (first came - first left) . The fee for such a trip is not charged, the shared expenses are only funds spent on tolls (toll roads).
Such joint trips do not require interaction with special software and services that organize trips. At the same time, the authorities completely remove from themselves any responsibility for what happened, shifting it to the fellow travelers themselves, their instincts and chance. In comparison with hitchhiking, slugging trips are more convenient; By the criterion of security, there are still many questions.


Taxipooling
Taxipooling is a type of joint travel involving the use of a taxi by several (2 or 3) passengers simultaneously. The benefits of such a trip are clear - passengers share the cost of the trip, and the taxi service has guaranteed customers.
The interaction of passengers occurs through the mobile application (the user selects the final and starting points of the trip, sets the trip time, including the interval (5, 10, 15, ... min.) Through which he waits for a taxi; the application selects the desired route for the user, displays suitable car and offers the user a ride). On board a taxi there is a similar software. Organized prior arrangement in the form of instant text messages.
