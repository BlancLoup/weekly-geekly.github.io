A smart oven recognizes food to cook it perfectly

To cook beef well, you first need to spoil a few pounds of meat. Cooks have been learning to make dishes perfect for years, in case of failure, throwing them into the trash can. A San Francisco startup offers another easy way to cook - the smart oven of June . This kitchen ‚Äúmiracle‚Äù recognizes the food in order to find the perfect way to cook it, and broadcasts the video to the smartphone.



According to IKEA, pattern recognition technology will occupy an important place in the kitchen. The company showed how the kitchen will look in 10 years : the system from the table, camera and projector helps to choose a recipe from the products that you have.

A San Francisco startup used pattern recognition technology in the oven. In the device, the developers put a widescreen camera with HD-resolution - thanks to it, the device sees the dish, and the owner can send photos of food to instagram. With the help of algorithms developed by a startup, the device recognizes the dish and selects the optimal temperature regime for its preparation. Now the oven recognizes the most common bakery and meat dishes. Over time, technology will improve, expanding the base of recipes.



Oven Brains is a 2.3 gigahertz NVIDIA Tegra K1 quad-core mobile processor with 192 supercomputer GPU cores that support the CUDA programming language.

"Acceleration" to 176 degrees Celsius is 4 minutes and 15 seconds. Heating elements are made of carbon fiber. External surfaces do not heat up during cooking. The size allows you to cook a pizza with a diameter of 30 centimeters.

The cooking process is reduced to three basic steps. You put the food on a baking sheet, then the oven recognizes it, weighs it and recommends the cooking program. For example, in the case of a steak, it offers degrees of roasting. After that, the device monitors the cooking process, sending all the data to the smartphone.

The smartphone application will allow you to monitor the cooking process and signal its completion. The smartphone plays the role of a remote control to control the temperature and turn off the device. A collection of recipes should help plan your diet.



Now the oven costs $ 1,495 on pre-order, and after July 10, the price will be raised to $ 2995. Sales are scheduled to begin in the spring of 2016.
