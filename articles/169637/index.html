Amazon began to ship Kindle Paperwhite to Ukraine

What many have been waiting for has been accomplished - now Amazon officially supplies Kindle Paperwhite to Ukraine (for Russia, the order is not yet available).


Previously, Kindle Paperwhite could only be bought from resellers, while losing the official warranty from Amazon. And the price did not attract from $ 170 for a book with advertising, which then could be turned off by paying Amazon $ 19. As an option, you could suffer with shipito or similar services and get the book in this way, while again losing the warranty.

Now it has become officially available option without advertising for $ 139. With delivery to Ukraine, it will cost 139 + 21 = $ 160. In Kiev, such a parcel is usually up to two weeks.

Let me remind you the characteristics of this wonderful device:
Screen: 6 ", 212 ppi, 16 shades of gray, backlight technology paperwhite
Size: 169mm x 117mm x 9.1mm
Weight without cover: 213g
Flash memory size: 2 GB (1.25 GB available to the user)
Battery: 8 weeks in reading mode for half an hour a day
Charging time: 4 hours from computer via USB cable
Wi-Fi: 802.11b / g / n with WEP, WPA and WPA2 support with password or WPS
Supported formats: Kindle Format 8 (AZW3), Kindle (AZW), TXT, PDF, unprotected MOBI, PRC; HTML, DOC, DOCX, JPEG, GIF, PNG, BMP with conversion via Amazon cloud service
Warranty: 1 year

I own a Kindle Keyboard 3G and have already appreciated the quality of Amazon e-books. Also, I really liked the lighting in the books from B & N (I managed to evaluate it in dark trains), so I already made an order for the device I was waiting for.
