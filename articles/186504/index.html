Results of the online conference ruHaxe 2



It seemed a long time ago, it would seem, last Friday, but in fact, on July 6, 2013 at 13:00 Moscow time (10:00 GMT), the second online conference ruHaxe was held.
(report of the first conference)

A group of accomplices, with the assistance of the Internet, YouTube, Skype and just common sense, talked online and listened to interesting reports of interesting people about haxe, maven, GUI, Unity3d, and so on. etc.


Alexey Fomkin - Build Projects with Haxemojos
Haxe integration with the world of Java and Flex development. Understandable for an enterprise developer project organization.
At the moment - this is the only development of this kind.



Alexander Kuzmenko - Stablex UI. New. In a good Old. Comfortable
Alexander spoke about a very high-quality library of GUI components, described the installation process and showed online how it is done.
And done, then - easy!



Alexander (AxGord) Gordeyko - Unity3d + Haxe
Unity3d + Haxe - this is unreal, you say. Alexander (AxGord) Gordeyko destroys this myth. Even if you are not going to take on Unity3d or Haxe anytime soon, you still need to watch this report.



Yuri Ketov - Haxe in RuNet
The conference ended with Yuri Kets. There was no code in his report, but there was a lot of useful information that concerns each member of the haxe community. Various issues were raised - from helping newcomers to promoting Haxe in runet.




Links and notes:


  • haxe.org - the official website of Haxe
  • haxe.ru - site of the Russian-speaking Haxe-community

  • haxe.ru/haxe_chat is a Russian-language chat, here you can quickly get answers to questions on Haxe.
