The Chinese claim that the size of the phone is 110x55x14mm, the ruler confirms this in principle. It is comfortable to hold the phone in your hand, it does not slip, all the buttons are at hand.




The front is a bright enough and juicy screen (just do not have to look at it gradients, Windows Mobile does not like them, only 65536 colors). In the sun, almost nothing is visible, but you can set the backlight to a maximum, it will be better.
