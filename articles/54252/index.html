HSE 10K Competition

Our friends from the Higher School of Economics asked to post.

The competition of business plans of the Higher School of Economics starts. HSE {10K} is the first Russian student competition for business projects. It has been held for the 4th year. The main objectives of the competition are to give students the opportunity to try themselves as entrepreneurs, and the most talented and goal-oriented of them is to help implement business ideas. The audience of HSE {10K} - students, graduate students and graduates of the HSE and other leading universities in Russia.
