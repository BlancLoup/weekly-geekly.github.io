Startup Weekend in Kazan VS World

Hello! Our next Startup Weekend in Kazan , which will take place in 2 weeks, will become part of the Global Startup Battle - so the entire global start-up community will follow the participating projects, we wrote about it.

More than 100 cities from Canada, USA, Europe, Japan, etc. will take part in this international battle .
Startup Weekend is a three-day work session whose goal is to assemble a team and involve a mentor into the project: an IT market pros ready to contribute to the project with knowledge and connections, and it is the mentoring system that is the hallmark of Russian weekends.

To show that there are good projects in Russia and to demonstrate the mentoring system in the business to the whole world, we gave Glavstart experts an unusual task - to make ANY idea that participates on the weekend viable and figure out how to bring it to the market.
No more excuses like "It will not work" or "It has already happened." That is to blame - it is forbidden, absolutely. But to invent, search for strengths and new approaches - it is possible and necessary - for each idea.

So, if you want to tell the whole world about your idea and give our experts a reason to break your head over difficult questions - welcome to Kazan on November 19, 20 and 21!
We are waiting for authors with ideas not only from Kazan, but also from all over Russia - an international event.
