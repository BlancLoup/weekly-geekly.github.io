The schools of Dzerzhinsk are provided with classes for the new academic year.


The Nizhny Novgorod Linux Users Group, with the support of the Education Department of the Dzerzhinsk City Administration, ELSIS LLC , GNU / Linuxcenter-NN, Penguin-Software LLC, carried out the installation of the School Linux and Free Software Package in the computer classes of all secondary schools in Dzerzhinsk.

School teachers will be able to start the new school year with ready-to-use classes with customized networks, Internet access, customized Content Filter and iTalc computer class management program. It is also worth noting that the distribution kit includes a set of applications for teaching the whole variety of topics included in the course of school informatics, ranging from office software, programs for working with raster graphics, vector graphics, 3D modeling and animation, working with audio and video. -files, work with the Internet and e-mail.

Details and statistics on the project can be read on the project blog:
