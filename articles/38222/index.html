Google Suggest will work by default.

As expected , the Google Predictions query prediction system will finally appear on the main page of the search site google.com, as announced today in the official Google blog . After four years of development in Google Labs and numerous tests, the system is finally ready for full-scale implementation.

The launch of the system will occur gradually over the next week, but in the end, all visitors of Google will see this feature.



At the time of its development in 2004, the function of Google Suggest was an unprecedented innovation. Auto-completion of the search line using a script that in real-time makes a selection from the database of the most popular queries - this was very unusual for a web service. However, the completion of the functionality took too much time. Now this feature has already been implemented in the Google Toolbar, Youtube and other services. Moreover, even some competitors (Ask.com, Yahoo) had similar developments. So today, this feature is hardly anyone can be surprised.
