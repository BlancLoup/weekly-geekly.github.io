FL Studio 8.5 beta 3 released


Rejoice, composers! A public beta of one of the best programs for creating and processing music has been released.

Major changes:
- Support for multi-core processors both on generators and on processing in a mixer
- Mixer increased from 64 to 99 tracks (total 105)
- Support channel split for VSTi
- Playlist changed for the better without losing old opportunities
- New Autogun plugin, in which 4294967296 Presets (as written in the instructions - to listen to all these Presets, spending 1 second for each, will take 136 years. Who is ready?).
