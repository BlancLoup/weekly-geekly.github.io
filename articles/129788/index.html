Online testing for Linux users


Currently, there are many online tests on the Internet. You could come across such well-known online testing systems as:
  • Brainbench.com
  • Specialist.ru
  • Basic.com.ua

where you can check your level of knowledge in programming, working with Windows, with Microsoft Office and other products under Windows, Web programming, etc.

But for some reason, everyone else forgot other systems, such as Linux. And not only administration, but the usual work in the user graphical interface (which already now has a fairly convenient form) and programs for it.

But how can an ordinary Linux user who works in Desktop Linux and spends most of his time at the mouse, without even realizing that there is a console? Indeed, today the Linux OS is no longer just a server. This and a large number of regular users who prepare documents, work with e-mail, browse web pages, etc ...
Here it is decided to fill this gap.

Assess your level of IT knowledge in the Online Testing Center. Tests from professionals will help you confirm your knowledge.

At the moment there are two sections and four tests:

"Education"


  • Work with the graphic editor GIMP
  • Working with spreadsheets in OpenOffice / LibreOffice Calc

"PC user"


  • Ubuntu Desktop Linux base user
  • An experienced user of Ubuntu Desktop Linux


Stand before the most stringent examiner - before yourself!

Get tested and assess your knowledge here.

PS But this is only a small part. There are so many sections that need to be covered.
Among the readers of this resource there are many teachers, employees of educational centers and people with rich life experience. If you have the desire and opportunity to take part in the creation of new tests, I will wait for your suggestions at pak@ualinux.com
Topics may be different:
  • Work in the text editor "OpenOffice Write".
  • fine-tuning and optimizing Ubuntu Desktop.
  • Work in the vector editor Inkscape.


If there are tests and for administration, then we will open the corresponding rubric.
