Three short rules for promoting a company using content: do not believe, do not be afraid, do not ask

I will not write a long guide to working with content, but I will give three tips. They are short, but cost me a lot of nerves and lost time. I hope that they will help someone to save at least some of these irreplaceable resources.

Do not be afraid to experiment. The first 50 failures in no case should discourage the desire to create content and persistently promote it.
You will not be read. You will write bad reviews (it is better, at least read and give feedback). Network media will render you in the publication.
Do not despair.
Time after time your lyrics will get better, you will learn to feel the audience and its requests. You will become more and more interesting to each other.
