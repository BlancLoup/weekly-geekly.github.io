Fukubukuro in LANIT office: sharing a bag of happiness

The end of the year in all the offices of the country smells not of tangerines and gingerbread, but of deadline and burning projects. And against the background of all this you need to hold a corporate event and it is desirable that everyone survive. Nobody canceled the favorite holiday, it is impossible to transfer it, but to celebrate it is strictly necessary. Otherwise, the next business year will turn out wrong - a sure sign.

We immediately abandoned the traditional scenarios: get together at the festive table, invite artists, go to a cafe opposite. I wanted to go the other way and create a truly memorable festive atmosphere. How about the new year in Japanese style? It turned out interesting, and we decided to share the idea and results with all Habr. Take on weapons. Banzai!



Year of Japan in Russia


To decorate shopping centers and city streets to the main night of the year began in mid-November. And it turns out that in a month and a half, people become overwhelmed with New Year paraphernalia and the anticipation of the New Year miracle disappears. Therefore, we began to look for other ideas - above all, for the mood.

2018 is the year of Japan in Russia. Why not invite your colleagues to plunge into the unique fantastic world of the land of the rising sun and get to know its culture and traditions more closely? Moreover, Japan gave the world a lot of what is now the basis of competent management in the IT sphere. And we began to prepare for the New Year in the Japanese style.

New day - as a gift

According to Japanese philosophy, there are not four, but five elements (or, as they are also called, primary elements): air, water, earth, fire and supreme - emptiness, or contemplation. Therefore, the morning staff began with Zazen meditation. The guys mastered the basic techniques of conducting meditation and learned to keep calm in the raging rhythm of the metropolis.


In the evening there was a time for master classes and seminars.
