meet - Asus eeePC



So we meet - eeePC will soon go to the stores.

Asus eeePC is a portable computer running on WinXP / Linux, with an Intel Pentium M 900Mhz processor, with WiFi, LAN, webcam, a 7 "800x480 screen (a year later a larger diagonal screen is promised). There are vga and usb ports. The case color and white and black

The highlight is that this miracle weighs 890 grams thanks to two memory cards acting as a hard disk. EeePC has two memory cards. One, with a capacity of 2 gigabytes, is designed for the operating system itself, the second 4-16 gigabytes - for user space.
