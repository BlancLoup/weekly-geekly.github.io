My Circle Results Report for April 2016

Our traditional monthly performance report on My Circle . In April, we had 672 vacancies, for each month on average 8 specialists responded.

And now in more detail about the responses to vacancies in the most popular areas of activity.



  • As always, we have the easiest way to find a designer , for one design job there were an average of 21.3 responses per month.
  • An unexpected record for administration vacancies, for the first time these vacancies came in second place with 15.5 responses.
  • Frontend and management vacancies traditionally lead with 11.8 and 9.1 vacancy responses, respectively.
  • At vacancies on backend and testing approximately on 7.3 responses on vacancy, they take the traditional place.



But what is the overall semi-annual trend in response to the most popular areas of activity.



  • Designers vacancies are answered most by applicants, an average of 19 people per month.
  • On average, 10 specialists respond to layout and manager vacancies.
  • On the job of administrators , an average of 9 people respond.
  • On vacancies of programmers and testers on average 7 people respond.
  • On average, 5-6 specialists respond to application developer jobs.
