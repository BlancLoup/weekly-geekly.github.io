Russian SharePoint User Group

On August 18, the Russian SharePoint User Group was launched. This is a meeting place for like-minded people working with SharePoint products and technologies. User groups are a very popular communication format for people working in the field of information technology. It involves regular meetings of participants and the exchange of experience.
RUSUG meetings will be held every three to four weeks. MVP for SharePoint reports are scheduled, including from other countries. In addition, Microsoft Consulting Services employees working with SharePoint technology are expected to attend.
The UG format also implies the possibility of becoming a speaker for any participant. Do you have something to say too? Offer your topic in the group forum or write a letter to the organizer at oksana@prostakova.com . It is worth noting that you do not need to spend considerable time on the preparation of the report, because the meetings will mainly take place in the format of blitz reports of 15-20 minutes each. This will provide an opportunity for one meeting to hear and discuss the story of three or four participants.
The next meeting of Russian SharePoint UG will take place on Monday, September 14. Collection of participants - at 18:30, the beginning of the report at 19 o'clock. Mikhail Nemtsev will read a report on " Recommendations for installing and configuring a farm ." At the meeting, Mikhail will talk about what kind of farms there are, how to deploy and configure the farm from scratch. After listening to this report, you will receive practical recommendations for creating a reliable basis for your portal. At the meeting, you must register .
Speaker: Michael Nemtsev - Microsoft MVP and SharePoint and .NET expert. Michael has extensive experience in software development and design, and has led development projects for industry giants such as IBM and Microsoft since 2000. Mikhail runs the SharePoint Tips & Tricks website, a blog about SharePoint, and actively uses Twitter (@ laflour ).
Join: http://sharepoint.su/UG/ .
