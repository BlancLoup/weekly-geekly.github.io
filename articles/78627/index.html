Google Transliteration

The developers of the Bangalore office of Google finalized the transliteration feature that appeared a month ago on Google Translate , and now it is available as a separate service - Google Transliteration . The service is aimed at users who have a keyboard based only on the Latin alphabet, but who need to type text in a language that uses non-Latin script.
