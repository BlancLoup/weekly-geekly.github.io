Nigma announces volunteer recruitment

Spam has entered our lives tightly. He filled not only our mailboxes (both electronic and real), but also the results of search engines, which often makes it difficult to find the necessary information. Mastodons search online make significant efforts to combat this phenomenon. Nigma.ru as always pleased with an alternative solution. Moreover, the developers of this search engine urge users to stand under their banners and declare a crusade against search spammers. After all, we, decent users, are many times more than adherents of dirty SEO.

In order to enroll in the army of volunteers, you must register on the site and after that you can send any site from the issue to the basket. It will be checked and then, if a fact of spam is detected, it will be mercilessly removed from the search result. Its author will be stoned and tormented by nightmares. Those who help Nigme to ban the largest number of spam sites will receive iPhone 3G as a gift, as well as gingerbread, ubiquitous honor and respect.

At the moment in the army of Nigma consists of more than 165 thousand users who send hundreds of applications every day. In the first days of the new feature, more than a thousand spamming sites were excluded from search results. Combine the forces in the fight against this Internet plague!
