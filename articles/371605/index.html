Wow! Signal source there could be comets and not aliens



One of the most interesting events of all space exploration is the registration of the so-called Wow! Signal! in 1977. Then astronomers were very actively looking for traces of extraterrestrial civilizations in the Universe (they are still looking for them), and it is believed that this signal could be just such a trace / signal from an extraterrestrial civilization.

Since then, astronomy has gone a long way forward, improving the technology of space exploration. Now many scientists have no confidence that the signal Wow! is the work of another civilization. The team of scientists from the United States, in particular, believes that the source of the signal could be two comets that passed through the group of stars Chi constellation Sagittarius from July 27 to August 15, 1977.

The nuclei of these comets, as scientists found out, are surrounded by very large clouds of hydrogen. The radius of such a cloud reaches several million kilometers. Perhaps these clouds were the source of the signal that was recorded many years ago. The comets themselves were discovered only in 2005, and they did not immediately pay special attention to them, they simply cataloged them. However, the source of the signal was located in 1977 precisely in the area where there were comets.

Of course, the stated point of view has opponents. For example, some NASA experts claim that cometary clouds emit at different wavelengths, and not at 21-centimeter waves.
