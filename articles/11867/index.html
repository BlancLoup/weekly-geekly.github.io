UcoZ is moving west !!!

The Russian-language web service UcoZ began to work online in 2005 with the idea to give everyone the opportunity to realize all their ideas about creating their own website. Today, the project team announces the opening version of the site for the English-speaking audience.
Two years of experience www.ucoz.ru has prepared a good ground for creating an English version of the service. Now the number of actively visited and developing sites in the system exceeds 100,000, and more than 2,000 new users are registered daily. According to Alexa, the service is in Top-15 of the most visited Russian resources. The most popular woweb webmaster portal, the Potteroman community site, the actress Anna Nevskaya site and many others are all hosted on Yukoza.

Today, the web service provides each user with free 100 MB of disk space with a real possibility of expanding up to 2 GB, more than 100 professional designs to create a website, each with the ability to change or create your own design style, the ability to link your domain, a convenient CMS system , which includes such standard modules as: Blog, Forum, Bulletin Board, Guestbook, Mini-chat, Photo Album, Catalog of articles and others. These modules can be used in a single bundle to create a full-featured site, or separately to create a blog or forum.

Today, the domestic web service Yukoz is a professional, free service for anyone who wants to declare itself in the world wide web space.
