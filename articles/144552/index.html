Tyumen equipped with free Wi-Fi-connections

Radio "Echo of Moscow" reports that today (May 25, 2012) in Tyumen, a presentation and official opening of the regional network of free access to the Internet - " Tyumen . Free ". Now in the parks and squares of the city there are twenty-two Wi-Fi access points , but by July 1, they plan to open another thirty-two - in cultural centers, in leisure centers, in medical institutions, in sports and youth organizations, and also in station buildings.

For the implementation of this project from the Tyumen regional budget allocated 5 million rubles.

Echo of Moscow calls ITAR-TASS the primary source of this news , but, unfortunately, I could not find this news on the ITAR-TASS website.

Judging by the text of the news, the free wifi network is conceived as regional, but so far its implementation is limited to Tyumen alone. If we compare with the distribution of free Wi-Fi, which began last year in the Krasnodar Territory on the initiative of the governor Tkachev , then the difference is immediately visible: on the site " Wi-Fi Edge " you can see more than forty locations, each of which has free radio access points - agree that this network is geographically much more widespread than if you open up even more than fifty points, but in a single city.

However, one should think that sooner or later the creators of Tyumen.Free will do away with Tyumen and move to the neighboring regional centers. Otherwise, the network would have nothing to call regional.
