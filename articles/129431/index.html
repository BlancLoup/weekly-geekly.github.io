Presented budget smartphone HTC Explorer

The new budget smartphone from HTC with Android 2.3.5 and HTC Sense 3.5 contains only the most necessary and is suitable as the first smartphone not for the most demanding user. (I come up with the idea to present such a child for a holiday.)



The display has a resolution of 480 by 320 pixels, which are placed on a diagonal of 3.2 inches. The device has a processor with a frequency of 600 MHz, 483 MB of ROM memory and 512 MB of RAM, 802.11 b / g / n wireless adapters and Bluetooth 3.0, a GPS receiver and a microSD memory card slot. HTC Explorer has only one camera - 3 MP on the back side. The smartphone can work in 3G, and its battery capacity is 1230 mAh.



The price of the device is still unknown, but it is unlikely to be high. Two primary colors are available: black and blue. Interchangeable panels of blue, orange, white and purple will also be available. Some sources believe that the device will be on the shelves in October.
