Kitchen computer table



In the evenings, having a laptop, but not having a cozy corner to work with him, the only logical way out of the situation seems to be to occupy a favorable position in the kitchen. Do not interfere with anyone and do not interfere with you. I think many will understand me.

The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãtransforming a kitchen table into a computer arose while walking through a famous Swedish store, where I saw a table with a rising lid - saving space, convenience and comfort all in one. But it did not suit me for two reasons: the price (about 7,500 rubles) and the surface of the countertop are clearly not for the kitchen.

Initial conditions


My kitchen table consisted of two pieces of worktops left after the kitchen was assembled, connected by two bars and set on legs. It remained a matter of small - add a little and a little refine. It was planned to perform a miracle over the weekend, but the work dragged on for a month.

Small explanation

All wires 220V are stuck in the extension cord bolted to the bottom of the table.
It took a lot of time to search for materials, which is why the work took almost a month.
There are not enough photos of some stages of work, I apologize for this - I was interested in the process and forgot to photograph the assembly process.
The HDMI adapter is needed because of the peculiar design of the monitor, the outputs of which are perpendicular to the screen. The adapter allows you to run an HDMI cable along the monitor and reduce the distance between the back wall of the monitor and the table top.
The monitor was donated for a birthday and is not included in the total price.

Summing up


Final costs amounted to about 3,500 rubles. Got a lot of pleasure from working with your hands, not your head. And, of course, now there is a cozy computer desk.

Ps. I am pleased to hear your suggestions for where to remove the laptop.

UPD. There are many questions, whether it is convenient to sit at the table. Here are the numbers: 65 cm - the distance from the floor to the bottom of the tabletop, 81 cm - the distance from the floor to the highest point. With my height of 183 cm there is enough space to cross my legs.

UPD2. I got rid of the laptop - under the window sill on the shelf attached to the wall I put the system unit. Now it remains only to open the lid and the working / playing place is ready.
