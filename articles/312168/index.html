[Peter] Meeting with Gil Tene from Azul Systems about application response times

On Wednesday, October 12, at 8:00 pm, a meeting of JUG.ru with Gil Tene , Azul Systems CTO, will take place at the St. Petersburg office of Luxoft. The topic of the meeting is the application response time (latency, responsiveness). The meeting language is English.



Measuring, monitoring and improving application response time - today is the daily routine of many professional developers. Whether you are developing applications or managing their configuration, understanding how response times and the basic mechanisms that influence it are arranged is the key to the success of your application and the happiness of your users.

This Gil review report focuses on different characteristics of response time, including a review of common errors encountered (even too often) here and there. In addition, the conversation will focus on proven methods of research, measurement and reporting of response time.

Participation is free. Limited number of seats.
