Extreme developer: how to relax programmers Veeam

There are many ways to escape from work. The variety of entertainment services offered is sometimes confusing. One like simple and affordable leisure activities like cinema and exhibitions, others like something more active and exciting.

There is an opinion that programmers - people are by their nature calm and low-active - are mostly keen on going to bars and rarely barbecues in nature. In rebuttal, we present you a selection of the most active and extreme hobbies among the developers of Veeam Software.

Today they talk about their hobbies:

The fact that without a hobby - nowhere



Nikolai Kobzarev:
In my opinion, it is important to be able to take a break from work and move away from thoughts related to work tasks, at least a couple of times a week. It does not matter whether there are problems or, conversely, success in work, it is useful to stop thinking about it for a while, reboot.

Alexander Abdullaev:
To have a hobby is to be open, to be in the world, to be interested, to love, to live! More importantly, not just a hobby, but to have interests other than work :)

Maxim Astakhov: Traveling on a bicycle, I am not tied to tickets, nor to fuel, nor to parking lots, nor to train stations, nor to cities. With some experience and preparation, I am not even attached to the roads.
