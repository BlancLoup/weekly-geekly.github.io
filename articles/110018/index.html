Choppers and cars from computer trash

Any computer geek probably has accumulated a whole pile of old iron, which is a pity to throw out: old hard drives, mice and other gadgets. Much often still in working condition, but devices still have no use, because they are godlessly outdated.



But here the Spanish designer Enrique Conde (Enrique Conde) found a great way for what old iron suits. As part of the KikeArts project , he creates highly realistic car and motorcycle models from unnecessary parts. For example, an excellent fuel tank for the chopper comes out of the mouse.

There are dozens of photos and videos on his website.
