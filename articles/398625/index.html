Photo tour of the MakerFaire 2016 exhibition in Shenzhen, part 3 (+ video)



We continue our tour of the exhibition MakerFaire 2016, which ended recently in the city of Shenzhen, China ( part 1 and part 2 ).

Recall - this is a grand DIY event historically based by the magazine MAKE, and with time it acquired an international character and takes place in many countries (including Russia).

Also under the cut will be video of robot musicians, fighting robots, cooking pancakes using a 3D printer and others.

DFRobot




Of course there was the stand of the company DFRobot - a well-known manufacturer and supplier of DIY electronics. For reference - the company was founded in 2008, and is developing OpenHardware modules for robotics. And over the past 5 years - has produced more than 1 million modules for students, teachers and DIY enthusiasts of robotics.
