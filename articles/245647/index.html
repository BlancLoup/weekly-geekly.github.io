How was the Dell Solution Forum 2014 in Moscow

On November 14 in Moscow, the regular, already the third annual Dell Solutions Forum took place. In this post, we will talk about the most interesting, in our opinion, reports that were heard that day, as well as updates from Dell and partners exhibited in the demonstration zones. The traditional venue for the Forum was the hotel and the Radisson SAS Slavyanskaya business center.


The third Forum came in the anniversary year: Dell turned 30 years old. More than 750 participants included representatives from Dell, as well as customers and partners. An exhibition of Dell and partner companies was organized in the spacious lobby. The Forum program was extremely intense: in a large conference room for 550 people, in the morning, a plenary session was held with key presentations from Dell executives and global partners (Intel and Microsoft), as well as representatives from Dell customers. The second half of the day was devoted to thematic discussions that took place in parallel in four conference halls. Depending on interests and needs, visitors could take part in any discussion.

The forum was held in Russia as part of the worldwide series of Dell Solutions Tour events. It presented the whole range of solutions developed by Dell and its partners.

One of the main premieres of the day was the new generation of the transformer tablet Dell Venue 11 Pro 7140 on Intel Core M processors.


The world premiere of transformer took place a week earlier, on November 5th. But on November 14, journalists and guests of the Forum had the opportunity for the first time in Russia to see the model with their own eyes. Venue 11 Pro 7140 is the successor to the previous generation of 7130 tablets, but is ahead of it in all respects:


The novelty combines improvements, like the appearance of LTE, with reasonable conservatism, like the screen of the previous resolution (1920x1080 with the same 10.8 ‚Äùdiagonal, which remains the optimal resolution for Windows devices with screens of this diagonal), as well as the continuity of the docking connector.

Despite the fact that the Venue 11 Pro 7140 has become thinner and lighter than its predecessor, one cannot say that it is chasing dimensions for the sake of dimensions: the thickness of the tablet case allowed to place a full USB 3.0 port.

Powerful stuffing - Intel Core i5 processor, up to 8 GB of RAM, up to 256 GB SSD and a slot for SD-cards - make it an excellent tool for solving work tasks.

Dell Latitude 14 Rugged Extreme and Latitude 12 Rugged Extreme


The line of ultra-secure laptops Dell Latitude Rugged Extreme is designed to automate the work in harsh environmental conditions. The model Latitude 12 Rugged Extreme is presented in the form of "two-in-one" and combines the functionality of a laptop and the mobility of the tablet. The robust attachment mechanism for the new device is borrowed from the premium Dell XPS laptops.

All Dell Latitude Rugged Extreme laptops are made of durable materials, internal drives are placed in sealed containers with rubber seals to protect against vibration, dust and moisture, and the fourth-generation cooling system QuadCool ensures uninterrupted operation of devices at extremely low and high temperatures.

Dell Latitude Rugged Extreme laptops have passed the tests for compliance with military standards MIL-STD-810G (protection against liquid contamination, work at low pressure, resistance to mechanical shock and a fall from a height of human growth, etc.), IEC 60529 (IP-65) ( dustproof shell and water jet protection without pressure), MIL-STD-461F and ANSI / ISA.12.12.01 (compatible with other types of equipment). They are equipped with fourth-generation Intel Core processors and high-speed solid-state drives with a maximum capacity of 512 GB, as well as the latest technology for remote control (Intel vPro) and support mobile Internet access and GPS.

Resistive multi-touch display with anti-reflective coating provides excellent visibility in direct sunlight and allows you to work even in thick gloves and with moisture on the screen. A distinctive feature of the new laptops is a sealed keyboard with customizable RGB backlight for convenient operation in any lighting conditions.
