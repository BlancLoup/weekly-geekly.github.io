- 7 hours I sleep;
- 1 hour going to work in the car (+ 1 hour back);
- 8 hours I sit in the office at the computer;
- 4-5 hours I sit at the computer at home;
Total 21-22 hours sitting / lying. There are of course exceptions on weekends (picnics, walks, etc.). Therefore, 80% of the time only the brain works. Physical activity is almost absent.
