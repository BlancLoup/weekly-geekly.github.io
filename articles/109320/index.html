Who pays for "buns"?

Social portrait of a paying user in the applications of My World


Monetization of social applications is an extremely important issue for any developer.
Naturally, at a certain stage, each studio begins to wonder: how to optimize the application for a paying social network audience?

In order to correctly answer this question, first you need to understand the general portrait of the social network audience and understand exactly who should focus the application.

According to TNS, from 15 to 21 November, the proportion of women in the audience of My World was 52.3%, and 47.7% of men - we observe gender parity. At the same time, the core of the audience is almost evenly distributed by age from 18 to 44 years:
