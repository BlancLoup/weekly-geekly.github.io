Allocacoc PowerHUB: Mobile USB Splitter

Allocacoc is known for its PowerCube product line. It is associated mainly with splitters of predominantly cubic shape, with and without extension.



Today we will talk about PowerHub.

The Dutch manufacturer Allocacoc creates designer electrical accessories . He has several successful crowdfunding campaigns, some of which ended only recently.

The main assortment is cubes-splitters , which differ in the length of the cord and the number of sockets.



Also in the assortment there are splitters of a more familiar form, and this year we also received a Bluetooth speaker and a USB splitter.

At this time, the company slightly violated the proportions of its recognizable shape, and instead of a cube, we have a parallelepiped measuring approximately 6.5 by 4.5 cm and weighing 190 grams (without plug) and 230 (with plug).



Its main task: to give the opportunity from one outlet to charge multiple gadgets.



The design consists of two parts, and this is because the PowerHub can perform the functions of the PowerBank. Capacity of the internal battery 5000 mAh.
