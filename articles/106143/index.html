ASUS Flowers

Remember the flowers from the Avatar movie that moved if you touched them? ASUS offers to enjoy such a spectacle already live. At the Taipei International Flora Expo, which opens on November 6, ASUS engineers collected a flower bed of 438 robotic flowers. Flowers move in time with the music and change the shape of their buds.



Electronic plants are called Asustek FloraBot. They were given a separate room in the "Pavilion of Dreams" operating at the exhibition. Thanks to the movement sensors, the flowers begin to move if you waved your hand next to them. The buds change shape, starting from cylindrical, then gradually expanding and shortening, and finally returning to their original form. The movement of each of the flowers provide small electric motors.



According to the company, color designers have been working on the project for over a year. The exhibition will be valid until April 25 of the following year.
