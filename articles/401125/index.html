SELFLY


This is a case for a smartphone that can turn into a miniature drone equipped with a video camera. The device flies by itself. You pull the drone out of the case, which by the way is only 9 mm thick and let it fly. The drone hangs in the air and waits for commands from the mobile application in your phone.

SELFLY is essentially a remote camera that uses the computing power of your smartphone to provide broad functionality. The device is able to hang in the air, stabilize, i.e. According to the developers, the pictures will be of the same quality as if you used a tripod.

Among the functions are also stated: following the object, voice control, face recognition and smiles, shooting from various angles, including 360g panoramas.
