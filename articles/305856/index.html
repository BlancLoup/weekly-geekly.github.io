Data center cooling market news: air conditioners of a new generation InRow DX

There are many options for cooling data centers. This area is constantly expanding with new solutions, innovations and technologies. The French company Schneider Electric, which is developing energy-efficient solutions for the energy and infrastructure of the data center, has released a series of new-generation In-row conditioners. The company's specialists believe that such units can reduce energy costs.



Air conditioners provide high energy density cooling, have a width of 600 mm. At the same time, they are characterized by reduced energy consumption costs. Of the features: the line has a new user interface; 4.3-inch color touchscreen display and network interface; high performance scroll compressors; additional controls. The possibility of cooling high-loaded racks up to 37 kW with a maximum air flow rate up to 6800 m¬≥ / hour is also increased.

The InRow DX has an adjustable fan speed, which reduces energy consumption during periods of cooling with part-time load. The devices have a built-in humidifier, which provides humidity control at the room level and protects electronic equipment from static discharges. In addition, to prevent hypothermia during the dehumidification process, the humidity is controlled by a dedicated dehumidification circuit and heating system.

In the words of Anne-Marie Gignac, vice-president of the IT Division of the company Anne-Marie Gignac:
There are many obstacles to improving the energy efficiency of data centers. Data center owners are constantly faced with the growing need for high energy density systems. Also become more stringent energy consumption rules, reducing operating costs. The main objectives of in-row InRow air conditioners are efficiency and cost savings for users. With an incomplete load, they provide the most affordable energy savings.


InRow DX has Active Flow Control technology that measures air pressure inside the air distribution system and automatically adjusts the fan speed. This improves the efficiency and accuracy of determining the compliance of air flow in IT equipment.

The design of new generation air conditioners inextricably links cooling with IT equipment. Due to this, it prevents the recirculation of hot air and increases the predictability of cooling. InRow DX air conditioners are available in stand-alone configuration, with liquid and air cooling with or without humidity control.
