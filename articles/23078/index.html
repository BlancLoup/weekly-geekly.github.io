Piwik

Web competition continues to grow. Recently (March 31) an open testing of a direct competitor of my favorite Google Analytics was started. Namely Piwik .
Piwik - is positioned as an open-source project (GPL license). Among its advantages, the creators single out: an open API, the ability to write and add plug-ins, a customizable look, and so on. Read more about all the innovations and ready and upcoming can be read on the blog Piwik.

But it's better than reading yet to see, isn't it? :) For this, the site has already opened a live-demo page. You can click and figure out how it will look.

PS: An interesting feature is that almost any info can be inserted into various sites, it is given via embed.

Tonight I plan to put him to test, but at this stage I think GA is not an opponent.
