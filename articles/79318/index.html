Nigma-feature collection 2009

It's time to celebrate the New Year and take stock of the outgoing year. 2009 for Nigma.ru was even more fruitful than 2008 . We have developed and launched more than a dozen new useful services, and also updated your design at your numerous requests :))

It's time to see off the old year - we present to your attention a collection of the main Nigma-features of 2009:
