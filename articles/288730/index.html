First impressions of the first spring business portal


On the first day of spring, the business portals arrived in the regiment: at bfm.ru, visitors are welcomed by the beta version of the business for me project. What do the authors promise?
The main page of the portal is modular - you can add the necessary information to it. To do this, use the catalog of materials - it contains an extensive collection of its own and third-party information sources. There are also options for quick additions - using the ‚Äúeditors recommends‚Äù block in the left column on the main page. In addition, you can add material to the main page, for example, from a page with information about a company or an industry. We try to simplify work with information as much as possible.

You can configure most of the blocks on the main page. When you hover the cursor on the block, control buttons appear. In the quick setup window of the news feed, you can specify which topics interest you - in the future there will be only them. You can also add and remove stock quotes for companies of interest to you, include scrolling text boxes and customize other elements of the main page.

Most of the blocks can be moved around the page - just like windows on Windows or MacOS. You can also remove unwanted blocks. You can return them through the information catalog - it contains the history of all changes to the main page.
By the way, all changes are saved automatically. The main thing, do not forget to register, so that the settings can be used on any computer.
