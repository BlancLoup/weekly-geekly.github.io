AirDog: Go-Pro camera drone automatically following you




AirDog is a small quadcopter designed for people who are fond of extreme sports. This drone is both a personal trainer and a great photographer who is always with you. Comes complete with a wrist, waterproof AirLeash bracelet that tells your quadcopter your location. Using the bracelet, you can set the distance at which the drone will be located, for example 10 meters from the bracelet, at a height of 5 meters. You can also make the drone hang in the air, keeping the attention of the camera lens on the bracelet. Can be used without a bracelet using the application on the IPhone.

Specifications
It reaches a speed of 40 miles per hour
Wind resistance up to 14 meters per second

Flight time 10-20 minutes, based on flight speed

CA7CH Litebox




The CA7CH Litebox is the smallest portable stream camera - only 1.5 inches, made in a high-quality aluminum case. The camera is designed to change the way you communicate. At any time you can share with your friends a photo or video in real time. The design of the camera is so convenient that it will absolutely not interfere with your daily life, as it is equipped with a powerful magnet. Moreover, it is possible to attach it to any place, be it a shirt, a T-shirt or a belt from a bag. There are 3 buttons on the panel: take a picture, capture a 12 second video or start a live stream. Connects to a smartphone via WiFi or BlueTooth and sends files to cloud services.

iFind




iFind - the world's first key chain without battery, to facilitate the search for objects. The key fob is connected with IOS or Android devices via Bluetooth and absolutely does not need recharging. iFind can be attached to any item and you only need a phone to find it. If you have lost your smartphone, then all you need to do is to shake one of the key fobs and the phone will answer you with a signal or with vibration. Keychain available in a variety of colors. Made from recyclable plastic and completely safe for the environment. Approximate price of $ 10.

Short Start Challenger: Smart Tennis Racket




Short Start Challenger - a device capable of making the racket really smart. Pretty interesting device, which is attached directly to the racket, thus allowing you to track hits. By installing the app on your smartphone (IOS or Android) and synchronizing it with the device via Bluetooth, you will receive accurate results of each of your strokes. For example, you can find out what is the speed of the ball, the impact force, the percentage chance of hitting the ball during the strike. You can also visualize the game on the smartphone display, simply by pointing the lens at the player with the racket.
