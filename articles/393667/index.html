The official trailer for the movie "Snowden"

The premiere will be held September 16, 2016




Almost three years have passed since the former NSA officer Edward Snowden published documents on secret mass surveillance programs. Documents changed the way Americans and the whole world view the US government. Under the threat of being accused of treason, Snowden is now hiding somewhere in Russia. This did not prevent the director Oliver Stone, who met with him several times during the shooting of the feature film.

Yesterday the first official trailer of this exciting picture of the lone hero was published. The young guy just wanted to benefit his homeland. Until I found out the truth.

In the main role of the film " Snowden " - Joseph Gordon-Levitt, remotely similar to his character. The plot is based on real events. Perhaps from this picture we even learn some details of the story, previously unknown. For example, an episode when he hid an SD card with secret documents in the Rubik's Cube and in the original way avoided checking it in a checkpoint scanner at the exit from the NSA building. Very similar to fiction.



But who knows, suddenly such an incredible technique was actually used? Edward himself has not yet commented on this episode .
