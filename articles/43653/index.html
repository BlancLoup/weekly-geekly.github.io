IgroMir 2008 - November 6/7/8/9

IgroMir is the first full-scale exhibition in Russia for all lovers of interactive entertainment: computer games, games for consoles, mobile phones and other platforms. This is a fascinating tour of the virtual worlds and real emotions, the opportunity to evaluate the latest gaming news and see for yourself those who make them before others.

"IgroMir" will open its doors on November 7-9, 2008 - for all comers, and on November 6 - for specialists and the press. In the spacious 57th pavilion of the All-Russian Exhibition Center, on two floors, visitors of all ages will find a variety of gaming platforms and the latest games from leading manufacturers from Russia and from abroad - both already released and still under development. Presentations, show programs, fun contests, prizes and gifts will help to spend an interesting and eventful time - in short, everything you can expect from an exciting journey into the game world.
