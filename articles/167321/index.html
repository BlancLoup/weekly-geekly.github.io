Video review laptop Lenovo IdeaPad Y500



The Lenovo IdeaPad Y500 is a mid-priced gaming laptop with a screen diagonal of 15.6 inches and FullHD resolution. Notebooks run on Intel Core i5 or i7 processors, and the graphics card is responsible for the Nvidia Geforce GT650M graphics card. The main feature of the notebook is the possibility of replacing the optical drive with the second GT650M video card, after which both video cards start to work in pairs (SLI), which leads to a significant increase in gaming performance.

Of the other features of the IdeaPad Y500, you should highlight the keyboard with a backlight of predatory red. In general, the design of the model turned out at the level of the best representatives of expensive gaming laptops, such as Asus G or Dell Alienware. However, unlike them, the Y500 boasts a very attractive price.
