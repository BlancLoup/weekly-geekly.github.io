Especially when this project is quite automated and brings a small, but profit. And then the question arises about choosing your server.

A home server is nice, but how reliable is it? Especially when you are in fairly frequent traveling and you can not constantly control the iron? Plus, fire safety and not least important - uninterrupted Internet. It is not necessary to speak about the latter, it suffices to recall the failure in Corbin of March 25.

Servers at work also have their drawbacks. For example, what to do when you need to give a project to support third-party people?
