SmartProgress. A successful start history and our promotion experience

2 months ago we launched a beta version of our project ( post on Habr√© ), which received good feedback and recommendations here. Honestly, we did not expect such a large influx of users at the start.

In this article, I intend to share the experience of launching and promoting the project in the network.




Launch


I have always believed that start-up projects should be brought to perfection, and only then can they be launched into a long voyage. The ReWork book from the 37signals founders changed my mind about this. A large part of the book told that ‚Äúlicking‚Äù the project is not at all necessary, and even contraindicated. All this confirms the fact that the most important thing in the project is the launch of the main working version, improvements and finishing - only after receiving the feedback from the first users.



Promotion


So, the first 2 weeks we went to the corrections. In parallel, we have been promoting the project in the network. We had little experience in this matter, so things were going quite slowly.
We started with the simplest and most obvious - from forums and blogs. We tried not only to spam (we did not use automatic mailings and bots), but to engage in a dialogue with users, we wanted to convey the essence of the project to those who could be interested in any way.
The most popular forums of the network with the highest attendance were affected, only about 10 large sites. The result - a lot of forces were spent (registration, posting, communication with the forum users), practically no result. From all sites about 300 visitors combined. There was a feeling that the forums are dying out mostly as a format. Regulars who sit there, the conservatives to the depth of the bones and feedback from them can not wait.

The next step we chose appeals to thematic resources, one of them was life hacker.ru.
An interesting feature is that we wrote to them, but received no answer. ‚ÄúStandard situation‚Äù - we thought and safely forgot about them. A week later (maybe a little less), ‚ÄúMetric‚Äù begins to go off-scale ‚Äî suddenly, unexpectedly, a post on lifhacker appeared, which brought us very high-quality traffic, the percentage of failures was minimal, which was very pleasing.

On this free cheese over. Then came the personal investment.



Yandex.Direct - I picked up low-frequency queries, trying to reduce the cost of a click, but on average it was about 20-30 cents, the traffic came in quality, but at this stage it seemed expensive to us. If the keywords were selected less competitive, the traffic became ineffective, the percentage of failures increased several times.

Blog Advertising on Twitter was the cheapest, but also the most ineffective. Blogs yielded results, but also small, until the campaign was stopped.

Vkontakte groups were surprisingly a good way to tell people about the project. On average, groups of about a million people take about 2000 p. for the post with a link to the site and 1500 with a link to a group in the VC. They have such rules - the post hangs for a day, exactly after 24 hours they remove it. Since they did not post in any entertainment groups, but thematic ones, the traffic was of high quality.

Now comes targeted advertising in the VC , which is bearing fruit.

Figures and awareness


According to statistics, the service is inhabited by 75% of men and 25% of women.
90% of users are between 18 and 35 years old.

Already, about 7,000 people have registered on our website. But it is necessary to take into account that 5500 of them used our know-how - a toy account. This account is given for a month so that the user can try the main features of the site, and then is deleted.

Over time, we began to notice that the service had outgrown even our expectations, if initially it was just a site for publishing our goals and communication between users, now new horizons are opening up. Some participants find people with similar goals and unite to help each other, for example, in moving to another country, others find like-minded people in their project, and others - business partners. Also investors who looked for interesting projects with us were noticed.

Since the launch, we have changed a lot in the project, made it more friendly, but do not intend to stop there. This is only the main part of the changes and innovations that occurred in 2 months:
1) Launched the English version of the site.
2) Changed the main page goals. In addition to design, the presentation of goals has also changed. Now we divide the goals into "Interesting" and "Everything."
