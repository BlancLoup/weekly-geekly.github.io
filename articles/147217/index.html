Video tutorials on creating components on Joomla 1.5

I have been developing Joomla extensions for 7 years now. Exactly as much as it exists since I began to develop from another Mambo progenitor Joomla.

About 6 months ago I had an idea to create a comprehensive course on how to program extensions for Joomla 1.5. As a goal, I pursued both possible commercial benefit and internal use in the company. Planned that this would be an introductory course and a guide for new developers, which would significantly raise the quality of the products.



Planned 36 lessons covering almost everything. But I had to stop on the 8th due to the rapid changes in the structure of Joomla. Version 1.5 becomes obsolete and even 2.5 ceases to be supported this year in the fall. So now it only makes sense to do this on Joomla 3.0 if we mean for the long term.
