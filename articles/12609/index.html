Meebo overtakes Google Talk in popularity

The Meebo internet pager audience has grown four and a half times since last August and came close to 2 million (this is only in the USA). In terms of audience growth, no one can match this web program, and by its example Meebo demonstrates a new trend: people increasingly prefer to use online IM pagers, which are more versatile, connect to any IM network and are not blocked by corporate firewall.

Analytical company Nielsen NetRatings has published statistics ( PDF ) on the audience of the fastest growing web pagers on the Internet. For the first time statistics was collected in August 2006, so you can see how the situation has changed over the year. It is clear that Meebo is rapidly rushing into the list of the most popular IM programs and will overtake Google Talk by the fall. And this despite the fact that the program was released a little over a year ago.
