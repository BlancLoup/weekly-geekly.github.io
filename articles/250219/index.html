A small flashback in the history of Technopark. Three years ago, the Mail.Ru Group, together with the Moscow State Technical University named after NE Bauman, opened an educational project that allows senior students and postgraduate students at MGTU to undergo free additional IT training. Technopark grows specialists in our field: web developers and system architects of high-load systems. The main task, in short, is to adapt the student to real life. Speaking in more detail, Technopark provides an opportunity to learn from IT experts, to try their hand at real projects and solve real problems. The output is experience, many have their own product, someone has a job in leading IT companies. The project is working, developing and paying off. This year the Technopark program was included in the ‚ÄúCatalog of the best educational programs of RAEC‚Äù.

How it works?


The main program of the course is designed for 2 years and includes 22 disciplines. The first year of study is devoted to acquiring skills in the development of web services. The second year is a special discipline aimed at developing the competence of a system architect. Teaching at Technopark is mainly employees of Mail.Ru Group. Also last year, we began to attract specialists from other IT companies, for example, from Kaspersky Lab, who supplemented our program of open courses, but more on that later.

How to proceed?


Recruitment to the Technopark takes place 2 times a year: in September and in February. Over the past three years, the competition for a place has grown threefold - from two to six people. The selection takes place in two stages. The first stage is online testing. The second is a technical interview conducted by one of the Technopark teachers. You can find out more details, find examples of assignments and (if you are a student at MSTU) submit an application for entrance examinations on the Technopark official website .

Training courses


Not only students of specialized departments can (and want) to study at the Technopark. The program initially started as an experiment, and following the results of the entrance tests, it became clear that it was a success: Dmitry Merkuriyev, a law student, received the highest passing score.

Competition Russia vs China


By the way, the history of the Mobile University project that won in the Techno Start continued beyond the competition. The Technostart team led by Technopark student Nikolai Chameyev went to the mobile application development contest for students of Russian and Chinese universities. Now we are tracking student championships in which technoparkov and tehnostartov can participate.

Summer school


This year, Technopark held its first summer courses, which were attended by about 80 students. The course on information security was held by Yaroslav Rabovolyuk - head of the information security department at Mail Ru Group. Assignments completed as part of the course, read to students as a summer practice. This summer we plan to supplement the list of departments (and faculties) and expand our summer school.

First two issues


Polishing the Technopark program, we largely focused on students' reviews: asked what they were missing, analyzed the results of tests and exams. The first releases of Technopark made the greatest contribution to the formation of the program - and today we want to thank the guys who became pioneers.

Today, there are 31 graduates in our account. At the first graduation, the whole Technopark team (students, teachers, organizers) went to Sochi. With the second year graduates of the Mail.Ru Group CEO Dmitry Grishin (he, by the way, also a graduate of the Moscow State Technical University named after Bauman) held a round table where everyone could ask his question and get feedback.

For three years we have come a long way. Our graduates work in leading IT companies in Russia, and we are proud of them. Ahead we have new championships, the embodiment of plans and new achievements.
