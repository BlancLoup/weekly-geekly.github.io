Kindle Second Incarnation

Today, Amazon will release its new Amazon Kindle 2 e-reader.



The new model is different from the first Kindle smoother and rounded body shapes. It has also become significantly thinner. Controls have changed somewhat. Buttons for turning pages have decreased, and instead of a sidebar for navigating through the menu a joystick appeared.


The novelty is able to play audiobooks, so it has a pair of speakers located on the back panel.


Detailed specifications have not been made public yet. However, I am sure that immediately after the announcement there will be plenty of them.

Amazon Kindle 2 will be available in late February. It will cost, like its predecessor, $ 359.
