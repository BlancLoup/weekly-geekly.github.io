The Basics of Urho3D

It is no exaggeration to say that Urho3D is comparable to Unity3D in terms of its capabilities and working with it is just as easy. At the same time, it is free without any reservations (MIT license), available for many platforms (Windows, Linux, Mac OS X, Android, iOS, Raspberry Pi, HTML5), incredibly fast and lightweight. Allows you to write games on AngelScript (syntax is similar to C #), LUA and C ++.
