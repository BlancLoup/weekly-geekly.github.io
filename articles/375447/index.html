The most interesting announcements of CES 2015



The year 2015 had barely begun, and the Consumer Electronics Show, one of the most important consumer electronics exhibitions, ended. In fact, CES is an exhibition (or even a show) primarily for consumers, so the stunning new products are almost not shown here, but devices that are ready for commercial production (for example, TVs), processor technologies that set the development vector of smartphones and computers demonstrate year, as well as concepts that show the level of technology in general. That is why the exhibition is not the first year more famous for robots and smart cars, rather than flagship smartphones, which we will see at the MWC in March.

Toshiba


The Japanese were marked by one of the most interesting devices at the exhibition - the robot Aiko Chihira, frighteningly similar to a real person . The robot is able to sing and gesticulate, emotions are displayed on its face. True, in these aspects, imitation of a person is not so impressive, there is still something to work on. However, this is a very impressive development.

Toshiba predicts a wide distribution of such humanoid robots by the years 2025-2030. They are tipped a lot of different ways of use, including information services at stations and airports, concierges, carers, etc.



Also from the interesting gadgets Toshiba smart glasses can be noted. They do not have a prism, and the projector image is mounted on the right hand. This does not end the oddity: glasses do not have their own power source, they work from devices connected to them. Camera and memory card missing. Despite the ascetic feature set, Toshiba offers many different scenarios for the use of glasses. There are three types of frames: universal, sports and for work in industrial environments.

Intel




The company feels great - at the exhibition there were practically no laptops that do not work on an Intel processor. Moreover, the broadest way were presented models on the newest generation of processors, Broadwell. Intel has traditionally introduced a number of processors, including in the framework of the platform Core U, designed to create powerful systems.

Intel has not forgotten about the technology RealSense 3D, and the wireless charging Rezence. However, for the success of Rezence in the market, you need to enlist the broad support of gadget manufacturers. The same can be said about the numerous products for the Internet of things.

Qualcomm





Intel's competitors are not asleep: Qualcomm showed at the exhibition a new standard Wi-Fi 802.11ad, a whole range of solutions based on the Snapdragon 602a processor for automakers, all sorts of components for a smart home, the Internet of things and many other areas. Not all kinds of existing technologies were forgotten, for example, QuickCharge fast charging.





As mentioned above, Qualcomm has paid great attention to the automotive industry . In particular, the company has launched new Maserati Quattroporte GTS and Cadillac XTS 2015, equipped with Snapdragon 602A, 3G / 4G LTE modems, Bluetooth and Wi-Fi from Qualcomm, as well as Android 5.0 (in Cadillac) and QNX (in Maserati) .

Acer






Taiwanese showed at the exhibition a whole range of gadgets. For example, Chromebook 15 turned into a laptop with the largest diagonal among all such devices running ChromeOS. The high-performance multimedia notebook Acer Aspire V17 Nitro has got support of the Intel RealSense 3D technology. Now you can control the device without a touchpad, simply drawing various gestures in the air. And for their registration are responsible IR sensor and built-in camera. This technology even allows you to create a simple three-dimensional model of an object, which can then be sent to a 3D printer.







Also of the new products can be noted compact multimedia center Acer Revo One, as well as smartphones Liquid Jade S and Liquid Z410.

Acer main announcements .

ASUS






The main focus of the exhibition ASUS did on the Zenfone 2 smartphone, equipped with 4 GB of RAM. A cheaper version with 2 GB of memory, a 5.5-inch display and a new Intel Atom Z3580 processor will be available. The cost of the smartphone should be $ 200, it will appear on sale by March.

Interest was also generated by the Zenfone Zoom smartphone, which received a 5-inch display and a 13 megapixel camera with 3x optical zoom and optical stabilization. It will go on sale in the second quarter, and its value should be $ 399.







ASUS also showed at once three updated Transformer Book Chi notebook models. All are equipped with fifth-generation Intel processors.

ASUS main announcements .

Lenovo








At the stand of the Chinese, it was possible to turn in the hands of many new products, including smartphones P90, VIBE X2 Pro and S60. All of them have their own interesting features and will certainly interest buyers.

Lenovo has not remained aloof from the fashion trends and presented a smart bracelet VIBE Band VB10, equipped with a display on electronic ink. The bracelet performs both the function of reporting events on a smartphone and a fitness tracker. Due to its high energy efficiency, the gadget can work up to 7 days with the display and Bluetooth on all the time.







Laptops under the NEC brand, in particular, the NEC LaVie 13 model, have a special attention. With a 13-inch diagonal display, it weighs only 779 grams, today it is the lightest laptop in the world.

Lenovo mobile devices and laptops .

Dell








Three years ago, at CES 2012, Dell introduced the XPS 13 laptop. It was notable for the fact that the manufacturer managed to fit it into the body of a 12-inch laptop. This year a new version of this device was introduced, which is comparable in size to 11-inch laptops. The width of the frame around the screen is only 5.2 mm, the processor Core i5 fifth generation. The weight turned out to be a match for the sizes, 1.18 and 1.26 kg for the versions with a regular and touch display, respectively. Despite its compactness, the laptop, according to Dell, is capable of working up to 15 hours in playback mode Full HD-video.

Dell XPS 13 2015 .

LG








This year, the Koreans' showers were TVs. First of all, of course, these are LCD models with 4K / Ultra HD resolution, supporting technologies of extended color gamut. Also, the manufacturer has significantly expanded the range of OLED-TVs.







However, most of all visitors were interested in the LG G Flex 2 curved smartphone. In addition to its unusual shape, it boasts a self-healing case material. Compared with the first version of the smartphone, the strength increased by 20%, and the recovery rate at room temperature increased from 3 minutes to 10 seconds. But that's not all - LG G Flex 2 became the first commercial device based on the Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor.

LG G Flex 2 and LG TVs .

Sony








This year, Sony introduced an extensive range of new products. Both the hardware and software of the TVs have been significantly improved. Looks fascinated models of the ultra-slim X90C series with the design of Floating Style. With a diagonal of 55 and 65 inches, TVs at the thinnest point are only 4.9 mm thick.





With the onset of 4K TVs, video cameras were pulled up, allowing to shoot content with the appropriate resolution. Now a full 4K camera can be purchased for $ 1,000, which can be called a psychologically important mark.









Sony has not forgotten about the "second plan" devices, having updated its audiophile player NWZ-ZX1 . True, it is more restyling than a full-fledged new model. The price corresponds to the high class of the device - 1120 dollars.

Garmin








The French are actively developing a line of wearable devices. Our attention was attracted by the Epix tourist smart watch with a built-in barometer, altimeter and electronic compass. Thanks to the EXO antenna, the watch is capable of receiving signals from GPS and GLONASS satellites. Built-in 8 GB memory allows you to write detailed maps. It is possible to connect various external sensors, in addition there are ready-made profiles for different kinds of sports and tourism. Also, the watch can be used as a fitness tracker.
