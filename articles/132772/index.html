How to cook blackberry stuffing?
In principle, everything is simple. We take an ARM processor, BES and BIS technologies, mix them with AES encryption standard, and season with GPRS, CDMA or GSM, Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, QWERTY or Touch Pad to taste. Best of all this dish is combined with ties. Serve to middle managers.

But seriously, the filling of the BlackBerry has a fundamental difference from the same Windows Mobile, Symbian Android or iOS. First of all, thanks to its target audience, 99% consisting of corporate clients. This feature and identified the unique characteristics of the platform - the very BES and BIS.

BlackBerry Enterprise Server (BES) is a special server that is installed in a company that wants to give out to its employees on BlackBerry. The pleasure is not cheap: the cost of BES is about $ 10,000, and each device connected to it must be licensed. Of course, not free.

BlackBerry Internet Service (BIS) - equipment that is installed at the cellular providers.

As a result of the interaction between BES and BIS, the BlackBerry has the following features: the BlackBerry email network, where all data is instantly and automatically synchronized, and some sort of BlackBerry Messenger corporate chat for fast and secure communication.

The operation of the BlackBerry mail networks is intuitive: after the device is registered on the BlackBerry network, the system recognizes each device by its individual identification number (PIN). The presence of a unique number allows the device to transfer messages through the peer-to-peer system. The BlackBerry Messenger application also uses a peer-to-peer data transfer system, but the algorithm of the application resembles the principle of operation of Internet messengers, for example, ICQ. In order to save Internet traffic and instant data transmission, data compression algorithms are used. All information transmitted is encrypted with a 256-bit key with the AES / 3DES algorithm.

The convenience of this scheme is that the BlackBerry does not require manual synchronization of the smartphone and the mail server. And if the device is in the network coverage area, it automatically sends and receives all e-mails. In addition, this system provides additional control over staff: the manager can track delivery and read any mail messages in real time.

The user also has the right to use traditional e-mail accounts with access via POP3 or IMAP protocols. However, in this case, the letters will arrive with a slight delay, and confirmation of the delivery status will be unavailable.
