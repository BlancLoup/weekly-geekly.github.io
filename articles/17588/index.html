Book about django published

Last week , a book about the Django framework - "The Definitive Guide to Django: Web Development Done Right" was published . You can buy it on Amazon or read online .

The draft was published online about a year ago. Since then, readers have added about two and a half thousand comments. The authors of the book (Adrian Holovaty and Jacob Kaplan-Moss) appreciate the quality and quantity of comments and are grateful to everyone who participated in the improvement of the text.

Django is a great web framework written in Python. The most important difference between Django and the well-known RoR is that the models are defined in the programming language and the database structure is generated automatically, and not vice versa. From the main fellow language Django differs primarily solidity. (Of course, all differences have their positive and negative sides.)
