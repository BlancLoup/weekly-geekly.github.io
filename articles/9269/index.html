30% of Belarus use the Internet

In May 2007, independent sociologists conducted a public opinion poll (1,530 respondents aged 18 years and older were surveyed by face-to-face interview, the error of representativeness does not exceed 0.03), which was devoted to the most important problems of the life of Belarusians. Among the questions was " Do you use the Internet? "

5.2% of Belarusians answered that they are online every day, 9.9% - several times a week. With the frequency of a month using the Internet - 10.9% , per year - 4% .

58.3% of the population of Belarus do not use the Internet at all, and 10.5% do not know what it is.

According to the results of a sociological survey conducted by IISEPS , 24.6% of Belarusian Internet users answered that they most often visit Russian sites, 20.2% Belarusian, 9.9% European, and 1.3% American.
