New update for Memo "on air"

Today I received a new update for Maemo 5. 3.2010.02-8.002.



This is the first step to the historical moment, this is a minor update that includes fixes, the second step will be an update that will include Qt 4.6.2 on board right out of the box.

The size of the update is 16.2 megabytes.
Why in two steps? Yes, because a very big update will work. When the second one is released is unknown, but it is expected that this month (very soon).

What was noticed is that MicroB (the browser on the device) now scrolls the screen smoother and more pleasant, and the media player is louder.
