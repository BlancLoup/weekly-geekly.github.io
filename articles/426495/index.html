Solo designer. How to build a career when you work alone

My experience of self-development, job search and making money




It so happened that from the first years of my career I was always the only designer in every project that I managed to work on. These were small startups and international organizations, but, regardless of scale, I always worked alone.

Communicating with young designers, I notice that most of them are isolated. Basically, it is self-taught, who are trying to understand a new topic for themselves, faced with uncertainty, lack of experience and knowledge.

Five years ago, I was the same, just starting my way in design. I lacked a lot of knowledge, for example, understanding the process and the essence of our work. In the absence of a mentor, my progress was not so fast, and I had to learn most things from my own experience, analyzing successes and failures.

I started my professional career at 18. Over the years, I managed without special talent and education to establish myself in the company with which I went to New York to take Starta Ventures. I was hired several times by a well-known payment system for designing the financial services of the three largest banks in our country. And on this I earned good money.

I have already described my way in detail in the article ‚ÄúBecoming a Designer‚Äù , so in this article I will focus on practical things that helped me establish myself in the industry ( my interview ) and start my own project.

Prerequisites


I tend to self-analyze and normally spend several hours for me, looking through notes and flipping through my favorite books. I enjoy trying to better understand how our world works and how decisions made have influenced my career.

After reading the books of Nassim Taleb, Daniel Kaneman, Ray Dalio, I was even more absorbed in the analysis of my actions, trying to separate specific solutions from random events.

Somewhere this was quite difficult to do, since many actions are precisely aimed at attracting random events, which is well written in the book "Anti-Fragmentation." It was from the book of Taleb that I learned this strategy.

I prepared this material for myself in order to better understand my path, but in the end I decided that it could be useful to many specialists who are just starting or have recently begun their careers.

If you have a decent experience, then I will be glad to hear your opinion on the things that I will touch on in this article. Perhaps many things will coincide, and some will be able to complement it, and I will be very happy.

I chose to work for a passion, so any of my current activities brings pleasure. But, at the same time, I always tried to earn more.

Therefore, I have divided this material into two parts:

  1. Professional and personal development
  2. Making Money and Finding a Job

I hope that my conclusions will be useful to you.

Some things may seem simple, but over the years I‚Äôve become more and more aware of the power of simple and understandable things and try to avoid complex ones. Not because I do not want to strain my mind, but because I believe that any topic can be explained in simple words. This is what I'll do now.

Personal and professional development


If any of you are familiar with my Instagram profile, then you know that I regularly read. Many people think that this is the only thing that I do in the intervals between coffee cups and writing articles, although I don‚Äôt spend much time on reading.

But despite this, the books really influenced my thinking , and, not to be unfounded, I will tell you how.

The list of my favorite books is always open at iskros.com/favorite-books , so here I will highlight only a few.

Not all readers believed my review of the book Principles by Ray Dalio . Someone considered him a charlatan (possibly against the backdrop of Tony Robbins‚Äôs arrival), although Ray is the founder of the most successful hedge fund in history and one of the richest people in the world. Biographies of entrepreneurs helped me understand how a business works, what every designer should know. Making money


The key idea about making money, I expressed in the design community The Design Times ( Telegram , Instagram ).

The thought is as follows:

‚ÄúMany designers are fighting in the glass ceiling, trying to sell their services more expensive, instead of taking a step to the side and climbing the stairs.

There is one proven way that helps earn money above the market without superhuman efforts:

Choose an area that scales well or with a high cost of goods.

Online services are best scaled, but if you specialize in expensive products, such as cars, you can make good money there.

Think not as part of the value of your hour, but of the value you bring to the client.

If your job helps you sell your customer an extra mug, sit and shut up. But when your design helps sell more cars or brings hundreds of new customers or transactions to the bank, you can (must) increase the value of your work at times. ‚Äù

Perhaps this is the main thing.

A designer and any other specialist are willing to pay above the market, but only if you help a business make more money.

By rounding out the borders and adding shadows you will not help the business, but by simplifying the process of using the service you can increase the company's revenue several times. I prefer to focus on the second.

In simple words, I am ready to sacrifice the beauty and accuracy of the layout, for the sake of development speed, thanks to which the product will enter the market faster and bring the first money.

When a designer breaks out of the framework of the design world and thinks in categories of business problems, his career changes forever.

Search for projects



Office of the project, which I am currently engaged in

I have never been without a job for a long time. Last time changing the project, I had several options, including well-known food companies, design studios and start-up start-ups (I chose the latter).

At first, I thought it was good luck to help me find interesting opportunities at the very time when I was looking for work. But later I looked at this situation differently.

And what if I always managed to find new projects, because I offered the market something valuable?

Despite the popularity of design, intelligent experts are not enough, so they do not stay up long on hh.ru, do not hesitate to raise wages, responding to high demand. So what are these skills?

I have already spoken about the first - this is an understanding of business . When they see in you not only a creator, but also a potential partner, the attitude towards you changes a lot.

The second is professionalism . Each of your projects should be a stepping stone to something more, even if you can‚Äôt talk about it. It can be a project in portfolio, experience, connections or money.

At the start of my career, I could lose interest in the project in the course of work, because of which the final result satisfied few people and could not help me in the future. Now I just feel sorry for my time for useless work and I refuse most projects, preferring to devote time to personal projects aimed at the future.

The third is networking . The biggest clients came to me on the recommendation. It is very difficult to find an intelligent specialist, especially if you need it urgently, so people prefer to contact their colleagues instead of looking through dozens of portfolios. Therefore, it will not hurt to expand your social circle, not only in design, but also in the business sphere.
Many people believe that networking is an ungrateful thing and below the dignity of many professionals.

Here I want to give a short but succinct thought expressed in the article ‚ÄúNetworking Designer's Guide‚Äù:

Professional contacts are not a pull, but an opportunity to realize your skills, attract promising initiatives and accelerate your development using other people's resources.

The fourth is the ability to present their work . I remember how at one workshop I was hired as a ‚Äúhand‚Äù, and this project ended with an in-person presentation to the board of a large bank. Considering that the authority of medium-sized company managers depends on such presentations, the responsibility hangs on me a lot.

But in case of a successful presentation, they will certainly return to you. This is what happened. After a couple of months, I was approached by the product manager of that same bank with a job offer.

Something about errors


I discovered a funny thing by analyzing my experience. All mistakes or failures made were based on lack of experience . Many after the first failures lose confidence in their abilities, although it is not their business that matters, but their ability to continue to practice, no matter what.

As Steve Jobs said, the points can be connected only looking to the past. So, looking back, everything seems to be much simpler and more logical. Understanding that any result takes time greatly helps in new beginnings.

For example, The Design Times began with a Telegram-channel with 0 subscribers, but now, after a couple of months, it already has about 650 designers. Recently, I received the first offer of advertising, although it all started as an experiment.

A similar story was with my first performance. About a year ago I had a desire to speak in public and I thought about how this could be organized. As a result, I decided that my publications on Medium (where I became a top author in several topics) would attract attention to me. Therefore, when half a year later Yury Vetrov offered me to speak at the Dribbble Meetup, I was not surprised, but was glad that my strategy was justified.

Sometimes we delve very much into today, while it is better to cover the big picture and understand where the current road will lead you in a few years . You can not accurately predict the result, but you can assume how much the final result will satisfy you.

Every year I am increasingly convinced that the right way and regular progress, even if it is a small one, is much more important than the daily superhuman efforts.
