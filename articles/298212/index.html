According to Vedomosti, Yandex.Market specialists analyzed the prices of goods at ten large online stores that took part in the sale and posted their offers on the official sale site Cmonday.ru. Among them were such giants as Euroset, Yulmart, Wikimart, Technopark, Media Markt and others.
