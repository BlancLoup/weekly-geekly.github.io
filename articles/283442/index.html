Google I / O Extended 2016

Hi, Habr! May 18-20, 2016 in Mountain View, California, will be held the anniversary, tenth Google I / O conference, dedicated to the most current cutting-edge trends in technology and software from Google.



To fly through the floor of the world for one conference is a pleasure then, and even if there are not enough conference halls for everyone to gather. Therefore, we are pleased to inform you that Google I / O will be held in the largest cities of Russia.

Google I / O Extended is a unique opportunity to become a member of one of the largest gatherings of Google-technology lovers. Everything will happen online, so you can watch the annual presentation of the coolest new products in the IT world with GDG Mountain View representatives! Now I / O Extended includes more than 550 events in 94 countries on 6 continents and covers the entire planet.




Together with millions of other people, you will see Keynote, including a review of key conference trends and a forecast of new products for the coming year, take part in a lively discussion of new technologies with other representatives of the community, see how I / O Extended is going on in other GDG communities around the world.
