Snom C520 and C52 - space design and wide opportunities

It is no secret that all modern business is very much dependent on the stability and quality of communications. Therefore, specialized devices that provide the best quality of newsgroups are always in high demand in the market.

Today we will talk about the two devices of the company Snom, which will help you to make remote communication with colleagues as simple, convenient and effective.



Snom C520-WiMi


Usually the most interesting and advanced solutions are either at the junction of the two technologies, or at the same time combine them in themselves. So, Snom engineers in their new C520 conference module connected together several modern technologies. As a result of this synergy, an interesting product appeared on the market with advanced features and elegant design.

But first things first.

Snom C520 combines two wireless data transmission technologies - DECT and Bluetooth. Thanks to this combination, the C520 is almost the ideal device for holding conference calls. Even if employees are on different floors or at different ends of a large office, this module will help to easily establish mutual communication and conduct negotiations, meetings or brainstorming even in a fairly large room, and participants will not have to gather closely around the device to hear them well. interlocutors at the other end of the line.

Design




The conference module Snom C520 is made in the case of durable high-quality plastic and its appearance resembles a futuristic spaceship. When the lights are off, the similarity with the spacecraft landed on the planet is further enhanced. Three supports with damping plates provide a high stability of the module, it does not slide on the table and cannot be brushed away with a slight movement of the hand.





To control the operating modes of the module, bright LED indicators are placed on the case. Throwing a glance at the device, you can quickly understand which functions are involved and which modes of operation are active.



Snom C520 is equipped with three high-quality and sensitive microphones, two of which are wireless. Installed inside the conference module itself, the main microphone, in combination with powerful speakers, provides full duplex communication.











Wireless microphones are connected to the base station using DECT. They can be carried away to a distance of 50 meters. This ensures maximum freedom of communication with any conference participant, even in the most spacious or crowded rooms. Built-in rechargeable batteries guarantee reliable operation for up to 12 hours in active mode and up to 70 hours in standby mode.



Thanks to the automatic volume control of the speakers and the sensitivity of the microphones, modern noise reduction algorithms and the elimination of the local effect with adaptive feedback, the conversation participants do not need to raise their voice. During testing, the speech was perfectly audible to all interlocutors at the other end of the line, even if the speaker was a few meters from C520 and spoke without raising his voice.

Convenience in work




The module is equipped with a contrasting three-line matrix LCD display with backlight. Its resolution (158x57 pixels) is enough for comfortable work with the system. All navigation through the device menu and the phone book is carried out using the usual four-button joystick. All settings can also be controlled using the password-protected embedded web server.

When using the phone function, the display shows the date and time, as well as the duration of the call. The subscriber identification function helps to find out with whom you are connected. There is also a local phonebook for 200 numbers with the ability to quickly copy the caller's ID. An alphanumeric search will help you easily find the right contact. The phone automatically remembers the last 10 dialed numbers and which you can quickly redial.









The built-in synthesizer generates 10 ringing tones, of which 4 are polyphonic.







For speech processing in the device uses a modern broadband HD-codec G.722. It provides a very clear voice and accurate transmission of emotions and nuances of speech. Snom C520 supports three SIP identifiers with simultaneous call control. Plus, using DECT and Bluetooth, you can easily connect compatible devices to the system, greatly expanding its capabilities. In particular, through Bluetooth you can, for example, use both a smartphone and a headset at the same time, or connect a laptop.







If there seems to be little coverage of the C520, you can easily expand them using various optional equipment. For example, to sound large halls, powerful C52 wireless modules can be connected to the C520.
