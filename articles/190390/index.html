How uLogin forgot to extend the domain and what came of it

The creators of uLogin in 2011 made one good and convenient thing for a webmaster: a widget for authorization through social networks. You did not need to bother with the connection of each of them, it was enough to connect only uLogin





Today, on August 16th, they forgot (at least I want to believe that they forgot) to extend the domain. Reviews webmasters on this subject can read blogs

Obviously, what will happen next: the team will write an apology, the domain will be paid for in a hurry, many will decide for themselves whether they want to continue using uLogin or not. And it was used not only by amateur projects, but also by quite serious ones - for example, our partner AdEasy advertising network, through which Roem.ru sells a part of advertising.
