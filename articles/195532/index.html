How to make the Internet better


So, what came to my mind at 2 am? The idea was to add additional settings to all browsers (a couple of extra checkboxes) in which a person, if desired, could choose the desired item (visually impaired, such and such kind of color blindness). And then this mode / property / attribute could be used by developers on sites. You could use it through JavaScript , but even better, in my opinion, add a new media type for media queries so that you can adapt the content through CSS . By the way, there are already types of aural (Used for speech and sound synthesizers) and braille (Used for braille tactile feedback devices), so why not add a couple more? Here I want to focus on the fact that this approach will help different categories of users, and not just blind and visually impaired.
