The font game

Very interesting and challenging game.


The screen shows the word, each time written in a new font. Under the image you see four options for font names. Your task is to choose the right one. The rules are simple, but everything is very, very not easy. I guessed only 13 out of 34.

We play and win! Good luck.

The only cheat code I know is www.myfonts.com/WhatTheFont

PS By the way, the site ilovetypography.com is very original, beautiful and useful to all who are not indifferent to fonts. Highly recommend.
