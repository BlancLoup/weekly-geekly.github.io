Universal Music Videos at Last.fm

The Last.fm music service and Universal Music Group have entered into a partnership agreement, under which users of Last.fm will receive free access to the full catalog of video clips from Universal.

Now the resource community will be able to view many thousands of clips of popular artists working in different musical directions, including Jay-Z, Nirvana, Def Leppard, Amy Winehouse, The Killers, Duffy, Snow Patrol, Kanye West and many others.

This is not Last.fm first contract. Earlier this year, the service entered into agreements for the distribution of musical materials with the Universal Music Group, Sony / BMG, Warner Music and EMI companies, which allowed Last.fm to increase its user audience by 85% in just a couple of days.
