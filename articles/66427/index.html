ATM - quickly and conveniently looking for ATMs and terminals



A week ago, our project Calmly.Ru added to OSMP. As soon as this pleasant news appeared, we began to prepare for the announcement on the site: checked the billing module, which is responsible for accepting payments, prepared instructions with screenshots of the machine, made a couple of test payments - everything seems to be working, you can send SMS messages with good news to instructors . And then I got the idea to make it on Spokino.Ru, for more convenience, also a card with Kiwi payment terminals in Moscow. Thinking more globally, we came to the concept of "ATM" - all ATMs and terminals in Moscow on one card.


In addition, the team had a great desire to implement the experience gained in the development of a single project . As a result, a week later - a simple and useful service based on Google Maps with our favorite interface.

In the near future, it is planned to add new banks and terminals, special filters for search (for example, display ATMs that work around the clock), an interface for adding and editing points by service users (according to the wiki ideology) and, of course, a version for mobile devices.

The site has a system for reviews "Reformal", so we will be glad to your ideas, tips and suggestions.

http://bankomator.ru

Work on the project (in alphabetical order): bofh , piumosso , Prophet , vorob , Xumepa .
