Really powerful and compact PC

Some new items are very alarming. This is an open laptop without a keyboard and without a disk for $ 1'995 based on a weak ARM processor, then a "universal" and "powerful" PC Tango the size of a smartphone for $ 350, is actually completely useless without a second docking station, which costs as much as half of this PC , in that case, if you still carry a computing module between work and home.

I have been looking for a long time to replace my laptop with something, and laptops have completely ceased to interest me since such a tablet appeared.
I wanted something quite compact, and I started digging in the direction of the nano-ITX and pico-ITX form factors, but nothing was productive enough for reasonable money.
