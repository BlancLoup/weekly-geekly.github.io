Apple may refuse Intel processors

History repeats itself, just the opposite?

In Bloomberg, an article appeared in which they write that Apple is looking for ways to abandon Intel processors in the Mac lineup.
The article talks about the potential possibility of transition to the ARM architecture as being more energy efficient and that in the future ARM processors will have enough performance for such an application.

Goodbye Hakintosh?

Apple began to use Intel processors since 2005 when it abandoned the use of the PowerPC architecture and before that there was no talk of hakintoshs. With the transition to Intel, MacOS has become "more accessible", and it was already "possible" to try.
If Apple still refuses from Intel and it will have its own processors, then you can forget about the "hakintosh".
