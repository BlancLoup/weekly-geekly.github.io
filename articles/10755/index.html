The biggest startup, which is not

Recently, the authors of start-ups are increasingly using the marketing ploy with the launch of the project's blog, even before the appearance of the working version of the service. The guys from bigstartup.ru believe in the success of their offspring and the fact that intrigue will not die before the site is born.

How to achieve profitability, how to communicate with investors, how not to get lost among other innovators - in the living room ‚Äútoodoo + internet.ru‚Äù tells the initiator of the project - Igor Sanin.

- Igor, Do all team members have this first startup startup experience?

- Andrei Zyatikov and I once launched a design studio. There were orders, we put together a good team and successfully implemented several projects. Then I participated in the creation of an Internet service in the hotel sphere: I led the development of the portal for half a year until I left there: my partners and I had different visions of its development.

- Where is the confidence that everything will work out?

- Very vague concept of ‚Äúeverything will turn out‚Äù. Will we reach the planned audience? Will we get the expected returns? Will we become leaders in our field? We have formed and worked out in detail the concept of the project, created an excellent backbone of the team and managed to attract the attention of individual specialists and potential investors. We have repeatedly told our friends about our idea and in one voice received the answer that such a service is really necessary. All this gives us reason to believe that the project has excellent prospects. In order to talk about confidence in success, a much more solid foundation is needed. Perhaps such a foundation for us and could be an investment.

- You have already mentioned that you are planning a short time to bring profitability. Do not somehow fit with statements about financial illiteracy?

- We are sure that it is not enough to rely only on our own and expert conclusions based on personal experience and knowledge in determining the prospects of any project. Many startups do not conduct any research and analytical work to describe their ideas in concrete figures and indicators. The Internet is a very dynamic area and any business project that is organized there is subject to a number of significant risks. And when planning it is necessary to take into account the many uncertainties affecting cash flows.

We, as we can, are trying to determine future profitability: we attract consultants and experts, communicate with economists, and have already conducted valid serious research. So far, the result may not be perfect, and needs some work, but this is only a matter of time.

We can plan the yield on profitability based on indicators of similar services - basically, it is possible to draw conclusions from them regarding the performance of the business model.

- Could it be that you will bring the project to a certain stage, and you will not allocate money for it? What then?

As a rule, the authors are able to start a web service and organize its basic promotion on their own - here the bill goes to hundreds of dollars. Financing at the seed stage is necessary mainly to speed up the work, attract professional specialists and more large-scale promotion.

When a startup functions and has a certain user base, money can serve as fuel for the rapid growth of the audience - either directly, through advertising campaigns, or indirectly - through improving the quality of services. In the case of the emergence of competitors, often, the winner is the one who has the greatest financial potential.

Plus, of course, we need people to support and develop the service.
