How much should your SaaS cost? I analyzed 1530 services and received such graphics



Pricing SaaS-services - a tricky thing. The slightest mistake can easily cost you a fortune in the long run. Therefore, it is difficult to overestimate the need to analyze competitors in your niche and the average price level in a hospital.

But there is good news - I have already done this analysis for you (at least in some niches) and offer you a scattering of color pictures that are drawn on the basis of an analysis of 1,530 prices of various services.

A little about how to read the graphics below. When I try to understand how much my SaaS application should cost users per month, I start by collecting the prices of my competitors and building their distribution histograms. The peaks in this histogram indicate price segments that are in a particular niche.

By the way, the lack of a clear peak where it should be can signal that we have a clear opportunity to enter this market. For example, it might look like this:

Here's the price of SaaS. The small number of players at $ 0-20. pic.twitter.com/ZGkPsmOb9s

- Alex Moskovski (@mskvsk) February 20, 2017

A few words about how the data for graphing were formed. We have compiled lists of global SaaS services grouped by niche. We went through each of them and extracted their minimum price for monthly use. Translated it into dollars, if initially there was something else. Discard free options and those that cost more than $ 500. And got such results.

I hope this small statistic will be useful to you when making decisions, just as it was useful to me.
