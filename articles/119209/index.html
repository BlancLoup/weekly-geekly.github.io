In the web interface Evernote appeared to view the notes in the form of a list

We continue to develop a new web client Evernote. Now and in it you can view the notes in the form of a nice and convenient list. Log in to your Evernote web client to see the changes.

List of notes


Now in the web interface of Evernote there are two modes for displaying notes: fragments and a list. In order to switch to the new version, click on the Settings list link above the list of notes, and then select the display mode ‚ÄúList‚Äù.



When you first turn on the new mode, the list will stretch to the full length of the window. Click on any note, and a panel will appear below to view it. As before, you can edit and send your notes to your friends using the buttons above the list.

You can change the size of the preview pane by dragging the mouse cursor over the horizontal line at the upper border of the pane. To hide the panel, click Close Note, this will allow you to view and scroll the list to the full height of the window.
