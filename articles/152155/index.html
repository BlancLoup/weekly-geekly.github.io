Uanet protests defamation law



On September 25, protest action against amendments to the criminal code, which translate slander from the category of administrative offenses into criminal ones, began in Uanete. The amendments were adopted in the first reading on September 18. In accordance with them, large monetary fines and prison terms of up to five years are imposed for libel. Slander in Ukraine ceased to be a criminal offense in 2001.
