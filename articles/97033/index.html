FAQ for legal startups

MLG Consulting law firm together with lawyer Marina Derevyanko ( St. Petersburg Bar Association (SPbGKA) announces the creation of a legal FAQ for startups.

Questions can be left in the comments, e-mail info@mlgconsult.ru or asked via twitter @ mlgconsulting until July 10, 2010. You can ask any question about creating an IT business, copyright, attracting investments and any other question from the field of IT - businesses.

The answers will come out as a free brochure and will be posted on the website of the law firm MLG Consulting and on the resources of Marina Derevianko . In addition, comments will be given to the questions asked in this post.
