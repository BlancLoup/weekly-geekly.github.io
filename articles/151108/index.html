Competition for radio amateurs - 7400 contest 2012

It's time to uncover the soldering iron! The competition of development on logic chips of the 7400-series began again (as well as 4000 CMOS and Soviet analogs - K155 and their ilk). The deadline for accepting works is October 31, 2012 .

The point is very simple - you need to invent and implement anything in the gland on logical chips: you can go on the breadboard, you can on the printed circuit board, you can use a bunch of wires - whatever you like. You can not only take the simulator work. A whole bunch of prizes, so it will be difficult to leave empty-handed.

Judging criteria are as follows:


  • Hilarity ("noisy wild fun")
  • Originality
  • Quality of documentation
  • Unorthodox use of chips (for example, as an AM transmitter)
  • Technical ingenuity
  • Build quality
  • Imagination and creativity


What to do?


There are no restrictions, but the more original and unexpected - the higher the result will be. It is not necessary to do something on hundreds of chips, you can win on very simple, but original schemes.
