Interesting devices with CES 2015 for January 9



On the last day, CES presented no less unique devices and solutions than in previous days. Probably the most interesting can be considered hybrid devices, including headphones with integrated display. Here is what Medgadgets recommends paying attention to:
  • Headphones with Optinvent ORA-X;
  • 3D printer for chocolate printing from Hershey;
  • An improved version of the smart Parrot Pot;
  • TP-LINK smart home solutions;
  • Pearl - backup battery in the form of a mirror.

Parrot Pot - smart flower pot





Now this flower pot can not just tell the owner how the plant feels, but also water the green pet on its own if there is such a need.

To do this, the pot is equipped with a capacity of approximately 2 liters, into which you need to pour water. Well, and then the pot will use this water as needed.

An excellent solution not only for those people who are often absent from home, but also for all lovers of houseplants - because sometimes you forget to water the plant, but everything is done automatically.

TP-LINK smart home





The manufacturer of network equipment, TP-LINK, decided to enter CES 2015 with three solutions for the smart home: TP-LINK HS100, TP-LINK HS110 and TP-LINK HS200. The first two products are original smart sockets that allow you to control all home appliances (including via the Internet).

And the last device is a smart light switch that allows you to control the lighting in your home or office from anywhere in the world. In this case, a smart light bulb is not required - the switch will do everything on its own.

Backup battery for mobile phones girls





Girls talk on the phone a lot (of course, not all), plus many take selfies, take pictures of all sorts of sweets and generally waste battery energy as they hit :) As a result, the phone can run out after a few hours of active use, as a result, you have to look for a source of energy .

And they can be Pearl - this is a backup battery, made in the mirror form factor. The battery capacity is about 3000 mAh, which is enough for most modern phones and tablets.

Well, the mirror here is the most real, so the device can be considered a 2-in-1 system.
