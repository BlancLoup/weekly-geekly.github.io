Brief about vacancy


Let me remind you briefly about what this vacancy is, and why it is interesting.

Name: Home Agent or Home Agent. The duty is to receive calls from the company's clients and provide advice related to the technical component of the operation of the Iota devices and their interaction with the clients' computers. And also to answer some questions regarding finance, tariffs, payments and other smaller ones. The home agent, as it is not difficult to guess, works at home. Those. no need to go to the office, waste time and effort. He got up in the morning from bed, washed, had breakfast, and you can get to work. Schedule yourself compile yourself. There is a mandatory number of hours that must be worked out. Now it is 120 hours per month, i.e. only 4 hours a day. You can work and more, these hours will pay at a higher rate.



Under the terms of the contract, I can not tell about how much you can get. But I can say that by working more than the minimum rate of hours I can provide myself with a 2-room apartment in St. Petersburg (rented), with good clothes, the opportunity to go to the movies, send money to my bride and eat normally.

New stage of development and new opportunities


One of the parameters limiting the development of interest in vacancies was that half of the training and the entire internship required coming to the office and being there all day. But literally not so long ago everything changed. Now 90% of the time the intern is at home. 30 hours of mandatory testing in the office is no longer required. The mentor and the trainee agree to remotely connect the trainee to the desktop during the internship hours, and the tutor hears the trainee live and sees his desktop: how he works with databases, how and how correctly diagnoses the user's situation, can advise something right during a conversation with a client who, of course, does not hear these tips.

Thanks to such opportunities, the Home Agent vacancy comes to a new level. Now anyone who can talk and hold a mouse in their hands and press keys, has a desire to work and help clients, and has basic ideas about how networks, computers and the Internet work, can become part of a company.

Let me explain why I want to emphasize this fact.
First of all, because such work can be a good help and a chance for those people who find it difficult to leave the house. In our society, there is still the opinion that people with disabilities are no good, besides doing clothespins and sockets. However, this work proves the exact opposite. And this is not just talk, about how great it would be if people with disabilities could work on this specialty. This is the real reality. The company in this position are already working such people. And they work very high quality and have one of the best indicators among domestic agents. And since this is their main job, they, sitting at home, can provide for themselves, and with an increase in the number of hours worked, and their loved ones with everything they need.
