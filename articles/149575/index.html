Grants for women in the computer industry


Elina Igaunis, Daniela Lander, personnel department

We really want the girls who are interested in technology and programming to have as many opportunities as possible to participate in international conferences and express themselves. That is why today we are happy to talk about Google EMEA Travel and Conference Grants - a program of grants operating in the region of Europe, Africa and the Middle East and designed specifically for girls.

Participants who win grants will be able to register free of charge at one of the conferences and up to 1000 euros for travel expenses returned after attending.

A successful grant applicant must:
- To study computer science, computer engineering, other technological areas related to the theme of the conference, or work in these areas.
- Have a specialized education and have leadership skills.
- Take part in a key day / main conference.

To view the list of conferences included in the program, as well as for more detailed information about the rules for applying for a grant, please click here .
