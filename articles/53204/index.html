Universal Tripod



The Monsterpod is not a tripod or a sucker, but a device for holding the attached equipment for quite a long time on almost any surface. Monsterpod uses the principle of chewing gum, which sticks to almost everything and repeats the shape of the surface to which it fits: the more precisely - the stronger it holds.

Monstrpod is a sticky base made of viscose-filled silicone rubber (similar to chewing gum) in a fabric case with adjustable tripod mount. Monsterpod is able to hold up to 566 grams. weight for 10 minutes on virtually any surface other than polyvinyl (and its containing materials and coatings). At low (above-30) temperatures, the holding capacity does not decrease, at high (above +28) - the material becomes fluid, silicone oil is released, and Monsterpod slips vertically. Possible use is not limited to amateur photography with compact digital cameras. Flashes and other electronic multimedia devices mounted on a car dashboard, lightweight video cameras for shooting a snowboarder‚Äôs movement, temporary security cameras, cable suspensions and holders of decorative bouquets - all this and much more can be temporarily fixed using Monsterpod. Monsterpod is separated from the surface by a loop sewn into the device body by a soft and uniform movement from the edge to the center.
Manufacturers claim that they tested it on more than 1000 surfaces and in all cases it was able to hold the camera. Among the surfaces were: brick wall, wood, glass, grating, traffic lights, stone, etc.
