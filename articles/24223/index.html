Philips Xenium 9 @ 9k: two months on one charge

Phones Philips have long established themselves as extremely tenacious and unpretentious devices that combine in addition to many other attractive qualities. True for me, every new model was necessarily deprived of something elusively necessary for me: I did not like the design, or the unbalanced technical stuffing, or the price, which was difficult for me to justify. But it seems that the time has come when Philips has embodied my dreams of a phone for every day and forever. Deliveries of the Xenium 9 @ 9k phone began in Russia.

The main feature of this unit - doubled compared with the previous record holders of the work on one charge. In standby mode, it is 2 months (!), And in talk mode - 17 hours. Also a powerful battery allows you to listen to music in the built-in MP3-player for 35 hours. What in general should be enough to hear the entire contents of a 2 GB flash drive (128 MB MicroSD included). And there is a radio there. And he is cute. And small. And its recommended price is 4,000 rubles.

Now that you have reached the Internet in search of places where you can buy it (you will follow me!), It is worth noting that since the end of 2006, all production of mobile phones under the Philips brand has belonged to China Electronics Corporation. Perhaps the most European brand is Chinese. But well done because, eh!
