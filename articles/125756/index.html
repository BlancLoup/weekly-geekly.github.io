The Noun Project: a free icon for every object in the world



The creators of the project The Noun Project set a goal to collect icons for all objects in the world. The collection is replenished with dozens of designers from different countries. All icons are published in SVG format, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC BY) or Public Domain, divided into thematic categories, there is a search in Russian.
