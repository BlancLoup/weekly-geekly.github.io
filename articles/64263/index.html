Placing a photo on a T-shirt



For a long time, many men buy their own T-shirts with the inscription "The best father in the world." Individual T-shirts are not new, but now you can make them yourself. Nowadays, images that can be placed on a T-shirt are usually stored on a computer. With the help of digital photos, you can print an image on a sheet for thermal transfer, and then put it on a T-shirt.
