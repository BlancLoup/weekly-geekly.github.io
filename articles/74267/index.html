How I built my data center - part two, construction. Many pictures

So, the project is ready, work has begun.
Since I am not a builder, but a signalman, there will be a lot of pictures and few letters in this part.

First of all, the builders threw all the excess and removed the suspended ceilings.

It has become brighter and more fun.

Then, with the crowbars removed all unnecessary, there were perennial layers of asphalt over the concrete.


We laid the mines of non-performing freight elevators (Interestingly, they did not hide anything in the elevator?)


Torn off old insulation


And, slowly, began to build the skeleton of the containment zone


They removed all the pipes from the future containment. We started to make the ceiling from leaks from above. We make a coupler.


The skeleton is made. The ceiling is made. The screed is flooded. It's time to sheathe


We sheathe non-combustible insulation and non-combustible moisture-proof plasterboard.


Why swear, the cashier. Everything will be well done.

Raised floor collected, almost ready tight area :)
