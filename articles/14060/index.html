E-generator has created a social network of "special orders"

The original idea was realized by creatives from E-generator.ru. The new project is called Agency special orders . This is a unique service for Russia, with the help of which users can find themselves an assistant for any business except a criminal one.
The essence of the concept is the outsourcing of any services, within the framework of legislation and ethical standards. Get help on some issue, visit a person or organization, arrange a meeting at the station or at the airport, check information, buy products, create a regional branch of the company, develop a new technology, etc.

The execution of orders in the service involved agents. You can get this status only by recommendation. At the same time, the agent provides a lot of information about himself, including passport data. At the moment, the pool of agency employees is formed from users of the interactive creative system E-generator.ru. Today, Porucheno.ru has at its disposal 303 agents who operate in 138 cities in various countries of the world. Of course, Russian settlements dominate at this stage, but Guangzhou and London are already on the list.

To use the services of the Agency, it is necessary to register on its website as a customer, fill out an order form and pay for the order, the cost of which is determined by a special calculator program and depends on the urgency, complexity and other parameters. The average price of a standard order (for example, to meet a person at a train station and take him to a hotel) is around $ 30.
