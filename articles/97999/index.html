New Kindle DX for $ 379

More recently, Amazon, in response to lower prices for the Nook reader from Barnes & Noble, lowered the price of the Kindle to $ 189. Today, Amazon introduced the new Kindle DX, also lowering its price from $ 489 to $ 379. It is already full advertised on the main page of the store:



The screen size of the Kindle DX is 9.7 inches, which makes it convenient to read large PDF documents, unlike a regular Kindle. In addition to lower prices, the new Kindle DX differs from the old graphite color of the case and 50% more contrast screen.

Surely many, and $ 379 will seem too high a price for a regular electronic ink reader. In this regard, it is interesting to remember that the original Kindle, the sales of which began in 2007, cost $ 20 more - $ 399. Now, a small Kindle costs $ 189, more than twice as cheap as three years ago.

The price of the Kindle DX includes 3G Internet around the world for the entire life of the device, so you can buy a book in the Kindle Store anywhere in the world.

Order Kindle DX is possible today, and its delivery will begin from July 7. True, as I understand it, the new black graphite Kindle DX will be delivered only to US customers. The rest of the world will have to be content with a second-generation white Kindle DX, but also at a reduced price of $ 379. However, white Kindle DX can order and residents of the United States.

Update: be careful with orders, they say some have problems with customs .
