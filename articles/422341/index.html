Forward to a comfortable life, we will go along with IoT. IoT (Internet of Things) is when ordinary physical objects are upgraded to smart ones that are able to manage and control through a certain noosphere (the Internet will do so far).

Things and engineering systems are now able to collect data, share them through various services. In the future, it is a whole system that includes not only data collection and storage, but also security, analytics and other tools. Existing technologies are scattered and chaotic. Earnings on additional and cross-sales


What will be the earnings in the world of IoT? As you know, printer manufacturers do not earn anything on the printers themselves, but earn on cartridges. Similarly, the IoT market model is likely to be profitable due to additional and cross-selling.
