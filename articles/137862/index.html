Webweb technology in Yandex.Metrica


Official information from the site of Yandex :
The web browser records the activities of visitors to your site and allows you to view them in live mode.
Using the player, you will see an exact repetition of all the actions of the visitor on the site, as if looking at his monitor:
- mouse movement;
- clicks;
- scroll the page;
- keystrokes and form filling;
- selection and copying text.

What follows from this.
The user goes to your site, looks at the pages, selects text, fills out feedback forms, clicks on links. At the same time, the WebVizor technology records all these actions (note: of course, if pages are available for the Yandex counter, and the counter is installed on the site) . Problematics


Quote: "Using the player, you will see an exact repetition of all actions of the visitor on the site, as if looking at his monitor." However, this is not quite true. You will see the playback of the recording of the visitor‚Äôs actions only , but not the pages of the site on which he was at the time of saving the session by the web visitor. The page itself is loaded in real time with your cookies on the session site.

Example of erroneous replay

There is an online store with an ordering page (form + composition of the order). You can get to the registration page only if there is at least one item in the basket (read cookies), in other cases there is a redirect to the main one.

The owner of the online store (the owner) has decided to view the visitors' sessions on the checkout page. He selects the desired one from the list of sessions and starts viewing from the moment the visitor came to the site. The visitor looked through several pages, then added Item No. 1 to the basket and went to the checkout page, on which he saw a form for filling out contact information and the composition of the order (Item No. 1). The owner will see the main page, as in his basket (read the owner‚Äôs cookies) on the site is empty. Or he will see his order composition (that is, not Item No. 1).
