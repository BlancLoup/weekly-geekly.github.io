VDS launch took place!

Today, July 1, we launched into commercial operation our new service - VDS on Xen technology !

We prepared for this day for a long 3 months, during which with the help of our active users we conducted thorough testing! Today we open our VDS service to a wide audience!

Our VDS service is based on Xen technology, which divides a physical server into virtual ones strictly according to the amount of physical RAM. This technology has significant advantages over other popular virtualization technologies, since, working at the hardware level, it allows more efficient use of the resources of the entire parent system.

VDS offer includes three tariffs, configurations of which meet the high requirements of customers, and the price is pleasing to the eye and wallet. The cost of the junior line fare is not much different from the cost of the most expensive mass hosting tariff. Renting a VDS, you get all the benefits of a physical dedicated server at no extra cost:

* full administrative root access to the system;
* installation of any server programs within the VDS physical resources;
* the user of the VDS service receives all the resources and capacities that are assigned to him;
* complete independence from other customers;
* the ability to archive the entire system in a single copy.

In addition, VDS owners can enjoy the bonuses that our clients have already enjoyed so much mass shared hosting: discounts for several months and a domain as a gift .
