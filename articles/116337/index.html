Toshiba Satellite C670 / C670D: an inexpensive desktop replacement

What if you want a big screen? But at the same time leave the opportunity to transfer the computer from one room to another, or even take it to a country house? The solution is a laptop with a screen of 17.3 inches - the one created to replace the desktop PC. A wide range of Toshiba laptops includes such models, and now the company is pleased to announce the latest addition - the Satellite C670 / C670D devices.

New items are equipped with bright 17.3-inch TruBrite screens with LED backlighting and a resolution of 1600 x 900 pixels, which is more than enough for comfortable work and, of course, watching movies in high quality.



The Satellite C670 is equipped with an Intel Core i3 or Pentium processor, while the C670D is equipped with an AMD Fusion hybrid chip. Buyers of the first are offered either integrated Intel GMA HD graphics or a more powerful discrete Nvidia GeForce 315M video adapter with 512 MB of video memory. In the C670D, only the AMD Radeon HD 6310 accelerator integrated into the CPU is available.



It is worth noting the weight of laptops - it is only 2.8 kg, which is quite a bit for a 17-inch system. Very soon, new items will be available in Europe. They have not been announced on the Russian market yet. The manufacturer promises extremely attractive prices for the Satellite C670 / C670D. Systems will be available not only in black, but also in a combined white case.
