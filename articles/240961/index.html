Dell Solutions Forum 2014: registration is open

Dear Colleagues!
We are pleased to inform you that our Dell Solution Forum 2014 will take place very soon! This event will be held in Moscow for the third time already and we can, albeit with some caution, call it traditional. Like last year, the Radisson SAS Slavyanskaya Hotel was chosen as the venue for our meeting, the date is November 14 (this is Friday), and we invite everyone to take part in it.




What is the Dell Solution Forum?
Dell Solutions Forum is a large-scale Dell event held in Russia as part of the global series of Dell Solutions Tour events. The Forum will feature the entire range of solutions developed by our company. Parallel thematic sections on methods of storing and protecting information, building IT platforms, infrastructure and cloud solutions will help participants learn about the prospects for IT development and trends in the professional sphere. Sectional reports will focus on four key areas on which the Dell strategy is based:

Transform .
The tasks of organizing the management of applications in such a way as to ensure their quick and secure deployment on new platforms or in a cloud environment. Dell can help you optimize your applications and infrastructure for maximum flexibility and investment protection.

Inform .
With the latest technologies for storing and analyzing increasing amounts and diversity of data sources, Dell can be a source of competitive advantage for your company.

Protect .
A full range of Dell solutions to ensure the security of critical data for each company with the least capital investment.

Connect .
Dell mobile solutions are really designed for business, they will provide you with the necessary security and manageability and provide maximum flexibility regardless of the scale of the tasks - in one office or around the world.

How is the Forum going?
The Forum itself can be divided into two parts. In the first of them, from 10:00 to 13:00, you will be able to hear speeches by General Director of Dell in Russia, Kazakhstan and Central Asia Boris Scherbakov and Vice President of Dell for Corporate Solutions in the EMEA region Tony Parkinson, speeches by our key partners - Intel and Microsoft, as well as hearing Dell‚Äôs views on such important topics today as VDI, the evolution of storage and cloud computing. In the second part, thematic sessions and seminars of specialists of our company and partner companies will be presented, including Citrix, VMWare, Canonical and others.
In addition, you will be able to see for yourself all the key Dell products at the exhibition, as well as get acquainted with the ready-made solutions of our partners in the demonstration area.

Who is this event for?
Our event will be interesting first of all for IT managers: directors, heads of departments, consultants and specialists.
As part of our Forum, you will have an excellent opportunity to talk with Dell experts, their colleagues and colleagues. This is a great opportunity to build partnerships, and often friendly relations with experts in all relevant fields.

Where, when and how to get?
What: Dell Solutions Forum 2014.
When: November 14, 2014, Friday, from 09:00 to 18:30.
Where: Moscow, Radisson SAS Slavyanskaya Hotel, Moscow, pl. Europe, d. 2 (m. "Kiev").
How much: for free.
How to: register here .
