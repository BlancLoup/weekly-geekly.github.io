HackDay # 27 in St. Petersburg

On May 24-26, in St. Petersburg, with the active support of the ITG-Event . mentors and market experts.




HackDay is not a conference, not a party or a start-up party. This is the time and place of work on real projects. For two days you can completely disconnect from everyday affairs and head off into the development of the project.

The best project manager will have the opportunity to spend 2 weeks as a member of Startup Academy students, fully immersed in the unique ecosystem of Silicon Valley, USA, in December 2013.

In addition to the comfortable working conditions of the participants, there is an educational program and consultations from experts from Russia and the USA. Also this year, for the first time, participants will be able to receive online consultations from business angels and experts.

Among the mentors


- Branden Mulligan, a technology entrepreneur, who has five successful technology companies in Silicon Valley. Brenden is a mentor of 500 startups.
- Olga Pavlova, one of the best usability experts in Russia, the founder of the Pavlov Dog Internet Bureau.
- Gary Fowler, an expert in bringing Russian companies to the American market, the founder of Fowler International.
- Mikhail Kechinov, specialist with 12 years of experience in launching large web projects, Internet entrepreneur, co-founder of start-up accelerator CoffeeLab.vc
