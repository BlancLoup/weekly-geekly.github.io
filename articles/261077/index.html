5 internships for IT professionals abroad



Already the end of June, the students closed the session and some just started to think about where to go to work or where to get an internship. We decided to make a small review of foreign opportunities. All of them are paid by employers.

Internship at Daimler

Daimler is a German automaker, one of the largest companies in Germany, headquartered in Stuttgart. Daimler owns such well-known and popular brands, such as, for example, Mercedes-Benz, Smart and others. The company always offers IT-specialists to undergo an internship while studying or officially get a job - first you need to write an e-mail to - tss_career@daimler.com and tell about yourself. As written on the company's official website, Daimler employs engineers, economists, and IT specialists who have completed a bachelor‚Äôs degree or are studying for a master‚Äôs or postgraduate study and have experience abroad. Internship for graduates usually lasts from 15 to 18 months. Practice for students - from 3 to 6 months. Be sure to fluent in English or German. The selection is carried out in several stages: resume, online test, telephone interview and assessment center. There are no specific terms for submitting an application for participation in the internship, new job offers should be monitored on the company's official website in the "Career" section.

More information at www.daimler-tss.com/Careers

Internship at British Petroleum

British Pertroleum is a well-known British oil and gas company headquartered in London. Every year this company employs from 80 from 110 interns who work among highly qualified and experienced colleagues. Paid internships last 12 months or 11 weeks in the summer, which are focused mainly on graduate students. And, of course, the intern must be fluent in English, have experience in the field of information technology and have a professional education in an IT specialty or related field.

More information at www.bp.com

Internship at BlackRock

BlackRock is a well-known largest investment company headquartered in New York. Requirements for participants in the program - age from 21 to 28 years old, university graduate, experience in the field of information technology, good academic performance in the university, free English. BlackRock Company invites to cooperation and internship not only IT-specialists. For all questions concerning paid internship, you can write to the official e-mail of the company - professional.careers@blackrock.com.

More information at www.blackrock.com

Internship at Capital One

Capital One is a renowned American holding company that specializes in credit cards, mortgages, loans and more. According to SNL Financial, Capital One is the eighth of the list of the largest holding companies in the United States. Paid internships in this company last, as a rule, from July to August. Interns can work not only in New York, but also in Texas and Virginia. During practice, interns interact with highly qualified specialists and business leaders, which will help develop professional skills and earn unique work experience.

More info at campus.capitalone.com

Internship at Chevron

Chevron is a well-known integrated energy company in the United States, one of the largest corporations in the world and the second in America after ExxonMobil. The headquarters of the company is located in San Ramone. Thanks to the internship program, IT professionals can gain hands-on experience in the company of highly qualified employees who use advanced systems and tools to create innovative solutions. Application for a paid internship can bachelor students or undergraduates. You can work not only in San Ramon, but also in Houston, Covington, Concorde, Richmond and Bakersfield.

More information at careers.chevron.com
