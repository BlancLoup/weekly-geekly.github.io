WRate.net: a meeting place for customers and performers


Many habras-inhabitants are familiar with the problem of choosing the artist for creating and promoting the site. Customers and studios solved problems in their own way: the first often had to overpay the first best studio, the second to work hard on SEO. Far from the capitals, where web construction is less developed, the problem is more acute. In recent years, I had to visit both skins and as a result, there was a desire to implement a service that would be useful to both parties. It turned out from this catalog web studios and advertising agencies WRate.net .

It was decided that the service will begin life as a directory where customers will be able to choose a suitable artist for themselves. The main selection criteria in our opinion are: the price of the site, examples of work, customer reviews, region and a full list of services. This is all presented in four main sections:
  • ‚ÄúCatalog‚Äù - sorting companies by alphabet, rating (see below), region or comments. The main tool is the choice of the type of service and price;
  • "Works" - the page presents all the works from the studio portfolio;
  • "Prices" - the section will help in the preparatory stage, when the site already wants, but how much it costs we still do not know. The section lists prices for each type of work and a list of services that can be obtained in this region;
  • "Applications" is a section for customers. You can publish your application and collect offers. For executing companies this is all available via RSS.

An interesting innovation was the rating and the method of its calculation. We came to the conclusion that the rating of each studio or agency will depend on several indicators, each of which characterizes the participant from a certain point of view:
  • the number of jobs in the portfolio. This is a quantitative indicator;
  • implementation rate index, i.e. number of new works lately. Indicates the activity of the participant;
  • the duration of participation in the catalog. Something like a studio or agency experience;
  • variety of services provided. The more services the executor provides, the more likely it is that the customer will receive the package of services from one source and he will not have to take the layout from one, the design from the second, and the engine from the third studio;
  • TCI and PR home page directory. These indicators allow you to assess the weight of the participant in the Internet and the ability to unleash themselves;
  • the number of visits from the participant‚Äôs portfolio to the sites created by them. It characterizes the quality and interest in the developed sites;
  • evaluation studio or agency by users. It is an analogue of public opinion.
We assigned the role of rating components to these seven indicators. Considering the fact that they are all very diverse and to varying degrees should influence the rating, we summarize them with different weighting factors, and we also established a logarithmic dependence instead of a direct indicator for the ‚Äúduration of participation in the catalog‚Äù so that newly arrived studios and agencies could compete on equal terms with old-timers. We plan to further optimize the formula and upon completion of work on the formula, it will be published with all the coefficients and explanations. At the moment we can not do this, because the formula is still vulnerable to "cheating". If you have any suggestions for the formula, then be sure to email us on the mail, on the website, on Twitter , or to me personally through Habra Mail.
