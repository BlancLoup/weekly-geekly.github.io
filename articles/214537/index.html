ORANGEMAN: Rusonix distributes servers for free

Hello!

The Orangemen are back in touch! And we continue to distribute servers to good people for good deeds.


And something cardboard? o_o

From the first distribution of servers 9 months have passed. Since then, more than ten enthusiasts and companies have taken part in our Orangeman initiative, putting almost 60 servers, computers and other equipment into safe hands.

We do not stop at what has been achieved and sincerely believe that with your participation we will be able to provide hundreds and thousands of enthusiasts, non-profit organizations and interesting projects throughout the country with equipment!

So, today we want to distribute not much, not enough - 12 servers. All of them really need a careful owner, whom the servers will serve for more than one year.



12 servers for good deeds



Who are we and why are we distributing servers? If you missed everything and did not know about Rusonix and Orangeman‚Äôs initiative, here is a very brief summary:
  1. Rusonyx - hosting provider. For thirteen years of work we have accumulated a lot of very different iron. For new tariff plans, we buy new servers with SSD and distribute free old ones.
  2. We carried out the first distribution of servers in the summer of 2013 and, having received more than a thousand applications, we realized that we could not meet the demand for equipment alone.
  3. Orangeman's initiative is our attempt to make the world a better place. We invite other companies to follow our example and pass on their old, but working equipment to enthusiasts, interesting projects, non-profit organizations.
  4. Since then, with the help of enthusiasts and companies that have joined the initiative, we have spent about a dozen equipment distributions in several cities of Russia, in Kiev and New York.



No, well, really cardboard? o_o

In general, we really give these servers in good hands. Disassemble until you change your mind :)
Of course, there are several conditions, they are quite simple:

  • One server in one hand (if you do not argue a greater number);
  • You have to describe why you need this server;
  • For obvious reasons, we will not give up these servers to the host;
  • Pickup from our office in Moscow (not far from Belorusskaya metro station);
  • Servers without disks at the request of our system administrators (avito and hammer to help you);
  • To the gift horse ... well, you realized that the equipment claims are not accepted, but when dismantling all the servers were working and they were wasting the heating of our data center in Varshavka.


Ready? GO!
