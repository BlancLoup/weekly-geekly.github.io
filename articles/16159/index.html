Fans chipped in the club



Members of the football site MyfootballClub decided that it was not interesting to simply discuss football news and events, so they had a completely crazy idea to buy a football club. No sooner said than done. A few days, and 20,000 registered users, having thrown off $ 73 each, collected the necessary amount for the purchase of Ebbsfleet United Club, located in the middle of the standings of the fifth most prestigious division - Blue Square Premier.

Thus, having a controlling stake, fans will influence the starting line-up, transfer policy and other affairs of the club using a vote.

The president and head coach of the club Ebbsfleet United gladly accepted this information, however, what else could they do?

Apparently, the actions of English fans will give motivation to our football fans.
So, last year, fans of Moscow Spartak dissatisfied with transfer policy, were going to create a website where every self-respecting fan of red and whites had to be thrown into a fund intended for the purchase of players.
