WebMatrix Hosting is back!

More than 7 years ago in 2003, Parking.ru took part in creating the first version of WebMatrix Hosting. The project turned out to be very popular and unprecedented in scale.

WebMatrixHosting.ru is a specialized hosting project for WebMatrix sites, which was tested for compatibility by a team of developers from Microsoft and meets all the necessary performance and technology support requirements, including such an important option as WebDeploy!
