Return to the Moon: New Space Race



Watch the cool video about returning to the moon. If you do not know, then the moon is now in fashion again, and specifically. An adaptation of the award-winning digital film for the planetarium, this 24-minute video tells of 18 teams from all over the world seeking to go down in history by landing an unmanned spacecraft created on private means on the surface of the Moon. This world competition is created to spark the imagination and inspire people to the pursuit of space exploration, not by governments or states, but by citizens of the entire planet.
