At the end of 2014, games and social services are the main points of growth of income of Mail.ru Group

Mail.ru Group continues to increase revenue in the segment of games and user payments for social services, but profits from display advertising are declining.

The total revenue of Mail.ru over the past year increased by 14.8% (to 35,778 million rubles), EBITDA - by 13.5% (to 18,297 million rubles), total net profit - by 11.4% (to 12,518 million rubles). In 2014, the social network VKontakte increased its revenue by 12.9% (to 4,319 million rubles), EBITDA - by 54.9%.

In the fourth quarter of last year, the revenue of VKontakte increased by 22.6% (up to 1,348 million rubles). Income from online advertising increased by 8.4% compared with last year (up to 12.4 billion rubles). Multiplayer games brought 8.4 billion rubles, which is 26.4% more than last year. Revenue from user payments for social services (IVAS) increased by 16.2% (up to 11.8 billion rubles). Other revenues increased by 8.3% to 3 billion rubles.



Mail.ru Group CEO Dmitry Grishin noted that revenues from MMO-games and user payments for social services remained generally as planned. The current "economic and geopolitical situation" still affects the profit from display advertising, as well as the income of the company HeadHunter. From Q2 to Q4 2014, earnings from display advertising fell, but other income, including HeadHunter, increased by 5.4% in the second half of the year.

According to a press release from Mail.ru Group, the option program, which was launched after the company went public, was completed.
