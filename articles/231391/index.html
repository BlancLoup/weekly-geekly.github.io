Welcome to Moscow Django Meetup July 31


If you have not gone on vacation (or have already returned), then we want to invite you on July 31 to the next, already the 21st in a row, Moscow Django Meetup. As usual, the format of the meeting implies a small program of speeches. In total there will be two reports:
  1. Using Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) to build complex web projects.
  2. On the dangers of a priori forms of knowledge in the application of the content of web pages on the example of Django and Yandex.Metrica.

Using Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) to build complex web projects. In this report, Vitaly Glibin from Headhunter will tell:
  • what SOA is, how it can help and what to interfere with;
  • on the preparation of SOA on the example of the portal Headhunter;
  • about the history of creating frontend frameworks based on Python Tornado - Frontik and Tortik.

More Vitaly will stop at Tortik, talk about its architecture, features, features and examples of use. Demonstrates the convenience of debugging and profiling in production and development environments.

On the dangers of a priori forms of knowledge in the application of the content of web pages on the example of Django and Yandex.Metrica. Maxim of Orange will speak on such a great topic. The author is known for such masterpieces as your interviews - sucks and Nahru programmer online store . Listeners will have an unforgettable half-hour story about how to set up split testing in a django project in five minutes.

As usual, the meeting will be held in Mail.Ru Group at 39 Leningradsky Avenue, Building 79, SkyLight Business Center. Do not forget to take your passport or license.

Registration is required to participate. And if you are not able to come in person, then we will have an online broadcast available .
