Thank you for being with us! Achieve goals in the new year!

The number of users of “Simple Business” in 2012 increased by 225%. With such results, the cloud service for managing the “Simple Business” company is coming to 2013. For us, these are not just numbers, but a significant indicator of work and user ratings, an incentive to work even more actively.

2012 was for “Simple business” a year of close and open communication with Russian business, development of cooperation with entrepreneurs of neighboring countries, and also a year of introducing new promising developments.

“Supporting small and medium-sized businesses is the most important area of ​​our work in 2012,” said Valentina Karmaza, General Director of the company. - We took an active part in more than 15 leading events for Russian entrepreneurs, including business forum “Partnership. Leadership. Prospects ”, Russia: Youth and Entrepreneurship Summit, iBusiness Conference, Open Innovations Forum, Creation of Favorable Climate for Youth Entrepreneurship Development: Experience and Prospects, Softool Exhibition, etc. “Simple Business” acted as a partner of RIW-2012 and Business Management 2012 conferences, events of the National Association of Business Angels, INFOBEZ-EXPO, etc. Great attention was paid to the training of entrepreneurs. We organized the Mini-MBA course, the master-class “Secrets of smart startup management”, held our own practical conference and seminar “Optimization of business processes”. In 2012, the company joined the Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Association of Managers of Russia. ”

In 2012, “Simple Business” continued to develop new tools that increase the efficiency of automation in companies of any field of activity. Thus, the following tools were launched: payment accounting, websites, import of contacts, multichat, mail on the prostoy.biz domain, sales funnel. In 2012, customers of the system were given the opportunity to use the bulk mailing module, the planning module, and the video conferencing module. An important update was the new interface design of the program, its web version and the Simple Business portal in the current Metro style.

In the outgoing year, “Simple Business” received new perpetual licenses from the FSB and FSTEC, which will allow the company to expand its work in the field of data protection and the design of information systems.

Confirmation of the effectiveness of the work of “Simple business” and recognition of users was the receipt of several awards at once: the “Soft of the Year” award (nomination “Business Programs”), the award in the field of small and medium-sized business support “The Basis of Growth” (nomination “Innovation of the Year”) “Time of Innovations” (nomination “Innovative Product of the Year”) and prizes in other competitions.

We thank you for being with us in 2012, and congratulations on the upcoming holidays! Stable development in the new year, the achievement of all goals, and we will make every effort to help you with this!
