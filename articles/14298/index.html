What is really the iPhone?


We are dealing with a device that forcibly gives its functions to the side. Web based phone.


What it gives.

  • Stability. Nothing can be put on the phone, so there are no problems with other people's programs. You can supplement the system without accidents.
  • Less load on the device. For example, the mail program. Few functions. I do not like? Google mail at your service. Works. Also with all other questions.
  • Compatibility. IPhone is compatible with any computer that has a browser and an internet connection. That is, there are no compatibility issues at all.
  • Billions of developers. Now even children can program for the web. And it turns out that programs for iPhone are written, for example, by Google. Good such resource of programmers.
  • Zero cost of basic programs. For example, a normal email program costs money. Small, but money. There are as many mail servers in the web as you can find. Is free. And so with most applications. Subject to the use of Wi-Fi, the cost of operation is also zero.
  • Synchronization is no longer needed. Everything is stored on the net. The phone may die, the information will remain.

At the same time look - this is a very open system. You can at any time stop using it and climb onto any other device without losing time. It is enough to launch the browser. Everything.
