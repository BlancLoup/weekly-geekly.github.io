Presented a layout of the new user interface LibreOffice

Someone Paulo Jose drew several LibreOffice user interface templates. He said that his main inspiration was Blender 's interface.

Made in: Inkscape / GIMP
GTK2 Theme: Atolm
Native icons: Faenza-Dark


Pictures are clickable (traffic!).

Dark Theme:




SVG versions of mockup are also available.

SVG1 , SVG2 (I hope, devianart, will not be angry at hotlinking)

It is important to note that Paulo himself is not a member of the LibreOffice development team. That is, the layouts are informal .
But personally, I am sure that such an approach to the office software interface is very, very good. Perhaps the guys at The Document Foundation will look at him?

upd .: I tried to reach them through IRC. One of the developers said that the development mailing list is already talking about this mockup:

SkyManPHP : What and who should do to apply this layout as the real face of LibreOffice?
jcorrius : Do you mean the technical part?
SkyManPHP : Yes. In principle, the point of view from the management too.
jcorrius : First look at the vcl modules and see how they work. Then take the toolbox and cui-modules.

jcorrius : Then you should interest them and substantiate your point of view about this.
SkyManPHP : How much will it cost?

SkyManPHP : You're a LibreOffice developer, aren't you? Can this fit in your personal priorities? :) And it's not how much money? ;)
jcorrius : There are still many bugs in the main features that need to be fixed, so this is probably the most important priority. But I see there is a discussion about this on the mailing list, so you should connect.
