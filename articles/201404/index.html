Age of Empires 2 has an add-on (first in 13 years)



Yes, there are protracted in the game world. To the famous game Age of Empires 2 came the first addition in many years, called The Forgotten. This addition can already be purchased in the corresponding service Steam for 249 rubles (or 7.99 US dollars). It must be borne in mind that the addition is released for the HD version of the toy. The Age of Empires II HD + The Forgotten game costs $ 16.19.

Supplement introduces five more civilizations into the game, besides the existing ones. These are Italians, after the collapse of the Roman Empire, the Indians, the Slavs (immediately after the victory over the Tatar-Mongols), the Hungarians, and also the Incas. The Incans in The Forgotten traditionally fight the Spanish conquistadors.

It is worth noting that the update offers players and new campaigns, with the participation of the well-known Wallachian Prince Dracula, the leader of the Visigoths Alaric and the condottiere Sforza. In addition, there is a campaign where the player will help the Byzantine family to gain power and prosper in the port city of Bari.

The author of the add-on is the group Forgotten Empires LLC. Initially, the addition was developed as an unofficial one, but after the permission of Microsoft Studio, all this came out quite officially.
