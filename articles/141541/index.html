Barcamp Krasnodar + South Russia 4: celebrate tomorrow the 18th anniversary of Runet

April 7, 2012 , that is, tomorrow, the fourth BarCamp Krasnodar + South of Russia will take place in KubSAU , the participants of which will not only be able to see the presentation of the Krasnodar IT-park , but also celebrate the Runet Day .


Almost 1000 people have already been registered on the event website. Traditionally, 50% will actually come, i.e. about 500 people .
The main feature of non-conference (barcamp) is the possibility of informal communication with any person who attended this event. Therefore, the number of participants is so important and worthy of mention.
The organizers define the goal of the fourth barcamp, as the maintenance and development of the IT community that originated in the Southern region. The first two barcamps obviously took part in the birth directly, the third took birth.

It is important that since the first barcamp in Krasnodar (February 2010), local enthusiasts are gathering more and more worthy children. The scale of the event and its level is growing noticeably (I personally was on 2 of 3 barcamps already past).
And this year, RAEC also decided to celebrate the Runet Day (April 7, 2012 - Runet knocks 18 ) as part of BarCamp Krasnodar + South of Russia.
On the occasion, leading industry experts from other cities are invited (more than 20 of them show off in the Speakers section of the BarCamp website).

The presentation of the project of the Krasnodar IT-park will be part of the opening ceremony. And then the participants will run in sections. Among those already announced are sections on design, cloud computing services, development, production and promotion of mobile applications, e-Gov, universities in social networks, media 2.0, the blogosphere, the wage labor market in IT, mobile applications and Internet resources to help travelers , education, as well as iOS-workshop .

At the end of the daily program, the details of which I propose to watch on the site, according to tradition, there will be an after-party - even more informal communication in the bars of the city of Krasnodar (for the most active participants, however, other cities are available).
