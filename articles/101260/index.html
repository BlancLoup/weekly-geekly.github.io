Competition. Future entertainment

Russia met Augustus with heat and smog. This anomalous temperature deprived the inhabitants of both normal work and normal entertainment. In general, everyone is waiting for autumn and hoping that the situation will improve, because even the brain cannot function normally. But we believe in you and hope that you still have the strength and desire to participate in contests and receive prizes.



By the way, in Ireland it is still more fun, they generally consider August as the first month of autumn. But for us, residents of Russia who are not tempted by the Gulf Stream, August is the last month of summer, though not the most pleasant this year, but nonetheless a month of rest and entertainment.

Did you know that a distant relative of the great Caesar, Octavian Augustus , in whose honor this summer month was named, loved science, wrote poems himself, and his name even gave the name to a whole epoch that was known for the flourishing of the sciences and arts of the Roman Empire
