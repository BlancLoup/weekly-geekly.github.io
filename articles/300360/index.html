Productivity for digital transformation

The need to move to digital business today is acutely recognized by many companies. And this task is multifaceted. It is not only about finding the most effective ways to work with data that will help improve workflows, better understand customer desires and build new business models, but also create a flexible infrastructure that can support any business development initiatives. Equally important is the reliable protection of information and processes from ever-growing and increasingly sophisticated threats.



But it would be a huge mistake, immersed in solving the technological problems of digital transformation, to forget about its main driving force - about employees and customers of companies. The way your specialists work today and how customers interact with you to a great extent determines the ability of an enterprise to become a real player in the digital market and succeed in the digital economy.

For most of us, convenient and quick access to the right information or service has become an integral part of everyday life. Do not be late for a meeting if the car broke down, ordering a taxi in Uber or another similar application on a smartphone will help. You can buy the book you are interested in, without spending time on transport, through the online store. It is possible to analyze the current situation in the industry and prepare for an important meeting even on the way to work - just open the business newspaper's mobile application.

People expect the same convenience in obtaining information, analyzing and processing it from systems in their workplace. The necessary data here and now, an interface that is understandable without a long study of instructions, access to relevant information in the office, during a visit to a client or on a business trip are all integral characteristics of the company's working environment, which seeks to make the transition to digital business. Without such opportunities, it is impossible to ensure high productivity of each employee and the entire professional team necessary for successful digital transformation.

Flexible and fast


A user-friendly interface, adaptability of the application to the changing needs of the user, quick correction of the situation in the event of a failure, and ideally anticipating such failures - this is a guarantee of productivity, whether we are talking about company employees or consumers of the services it provides.

It would seem simple conditions, but to fulfill them is not so simple. After all, behind each application or service are complex information systems that are integrated both among themselves and with external solutions. Implementing and controlling changes to such an IT landscape is usually highly formalized, and within these processes it is almost impossible to provide support for flexible user solutions that meet the needs of the digital business. If your complaint about the low speed of access to the analytical system is put in a long line of requests for changes and a new release will have to wait more than one month, there is no need to speak about any successful digital transformation.

HPE sees the solution to the problem in the transition to a flexible (agile) style of work in the process of creating and operating IT services. Many organizations have already adopted the agile-method, allowing not only to quickly produce new services, but also to constantly improve them, promptly taking into account changes in customer requirements. However, the advantages of agile-development are leveled, if they are not supported by equally flexible testing processes and rapid transfer of changes to the production environment. Therefore, today, more and more attention is attracted by the methods of integration of the development, testing and operating divisions - DevOps, of which HPE is an active promoter.

The HPE ALM application lifecycle management suite includes everything you need to create and improve an IT service, from defining requirements to comprehensive testing in accordance with agile methods. HPE Portfolio tools for rapid deployment of test environments and the integration of various life cycle stages, including system operation, enable you to implement end-to-end DevOps processes, and HPE consulting helps customers successfully implement DevOps methods and shape the appropriate corporate culture.
