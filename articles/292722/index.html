Government doubles maximum revenue for small and medium enterprises

Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev signed a decree on doubling the maximum amount of revenue for enterprises. For microenterprises, it will be 120 million rubles, for small businesses - 800 million rubles, for an average - 2 billion rubles without VAT.

Restrictions for the maximum number of employees have not changed - 100 people for small and 250 people for medium-sized businesses. According to the Prime Minister, the change in revenue limits will allow a larger number of companies to access the state order and financial support through various programs, Vedomosti reports.

For Russian entrepreneurs, it would be much more important to increase the thresholds taken into account when choosing tax regimes, says Dmitry Nesvetov, a member of the board of the Moscow branch of Opora Russia . For example, marginal revenue for the simplified tax regime is 60 million rubles.
