In previous publications, we have already considered many important moments for personal growth and development, which Tony Robbins spoke about. And now we offer to understand the types of motivation and important features of teamwork .

Tony Robbins explained that there are two types of motivation:
1) pushing
2) attraction.

Pushing is manifested in the fact that you constantly ‚Äúkick‚Äù and connect discipline and willpower to achieve the goal . But will power and discipline are NOT ETERNAL, and as a result you fade. That is why many people fail: they HIT the result, but it only works for a few days, weeks or months.

The key is to use motivation based on attraction. It manifests itself in the fact that you literally THINK to the goal. It does not require discipline or willpower, because you WANT to do what is necessary to achieve the goal. You are changing the DUTY to do this at DESIRE.

The fact is that in life there are certain things to which we are drawn, which we love to do. For example, I like to go to the gym and do the exercises. For this I need neither discipline nor willpower. In fact, they are required by me to NOT go to the gym - I literally get sick if I have to skip the workout. For me, this is an example of motivation-ATTRACT.

I am drawn to exercise, because:

1) it meets my needs;
2) this coincides with my beliefs / values;
3) I have an attractive dream and an understanding of why I need this.

But there is another thing that can ‚Äúpull‚Äù us to the result or goal. Tony Robbins shared an effective method of creating such motivation-attraction. We form it using the feelings and sensations that would have arisen if we had already achieved a goal or result, and then LOST what we wanted.

The strategy used in the exercise is simple: if we HAVE HAD something, and then it was taken away from us, then naturally we begin to gravitate towards it again. For example, if you are accustomed to a mobile phone, and one day I say: ‚ÄúNo cellular phones, you should use a payphone‚Äù, then you WILL FIND A WAY to reclaim the device, because you already had it.

This method is phenomenal and definitely one of my favorite episodes of the second day.

Team strength


Tony Robbins said that the secret of well-being and happiness lies in becoming a team player.

Speaking of "team", Tony Robbins meant: the key is to perceive each person as part of his team or family. It does not matter if we are talking about a passerby serving you in a restaurant as a waiter or as your abuser. According to Tony, you should acquire two beliefs concerning people:
  • people do everything in their power using the resources they have;
  • There are no mediocre people, there is only an unproductive attitude.

You always want to see good in others and understand that they are trying their best to manage the resources available to them. Moreover, if someone is angry or offends you, you know: this is due to the fact that he is in a NON-PRODUCTIVE CONDITION.

Both of these beliefs are important. Tony notes that most people are miserable and poor because they are focused on themselves. Power always comes to those who are sincere in their ministry. The more actively and often you take care of your team, the more energy you have. Your level of consciousness can be measured by the number of people you care for.

Happiness is always a consequence of progress. Development manifests itself in the form of growth and sacrifice.

Who you spend time with, you become . If you want to change your life, then you should start with those who surround you, because they affect your essence more than anything else. If you strive to grow and get what you want, be sure to gather around you people playing at a higher level. Find a team that will provoke and push you towards development, and not the one in which everything is simple and convenient. If you feel good and comfortable, you will not grow.
