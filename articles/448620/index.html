T + Conf 2019 is around the corner



The second annual conference will be held on June 17 (Monday) in the office of Mail.ru Group
Tarantool Conference, or shortly T + Conf. It is addressed to both newcomers and experienced developers and architects in the corporate sector.

You are waiting for new reports and workshops on the use of in-memory computing, Tarantool / Redis / Memcached, cooperative multitasking and Lua language to create high-load fault-tolerant services in Digital and Enterprise.

The program includes reports on microservice and grid architecture, on the features of enterprise development and how best to implement certain functions. Examples of speeches from last year‚Äôs conference:

  • Create a high-load application for Tarantool from scratch
  • Interior design, tuning and monitoring Tarantool / Vinyl
  • Tarantool Replication: Configuration and Usage
  • VShard - horizontal scaling in Tarantool

Like a year ago, it will be full-time: reports will begin at 10:00 and end at 19:00.

The program of speeches will be published closer to the beginning of the conference. Spoiler: The opening speaker will be Konstantin Osipov with an overview of the most interesting features from the Tarantool roadmap.

If you want to share your experience with the community, then send us a request for a speech, we are open to new speakers.

Deadline for submission of applications is already close - May 1, 2019. At the same time, the price of tickets for conference visitors will also grow. Hurry up to book a place in April! :)

In addition to the speeches themselves, you will find useful handouts, lunch and delicious coffee breaks, and after the conference you will be able to watch videos of the reports that you like the most.

Conference site : conf.tarantool.io/2019
Address : Mail.ru Group office, Leningradsky pr., 39, p. 79.
Duration : 1 full day.
