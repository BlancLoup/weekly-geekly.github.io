The electronic cigarette will count every puff



Smokio e-cigarette is supplied by the manufacturer as the first one that can calculate how much you have smoked, and regulating the level of nicotine in each puff using the application on your smartphone. Even if the connection with the smartphone is not established, the cigarette continues to count each puff. The cost of the gadget is $ 89.9.





Despite the fact that electronic cigarettes are clearly less harmful compared to conventional cigarettes, their effects on the body are not yet fully understood . There are voices among scientists that, instead of facilitating quitting smoking, electronic cigarettes can only worsen addiction.
