Another iPad competitor: X2 iTablet

What makes Apple's iPad uniquely good is that it is in the wake of Jobs and co. the market in the near future will be made literally stuffing similar devices. Already announced ExoPC Slate tablet PC and tablet JooJoo . And now came the information about the X2 iTablet.



The British X2 Computing announced the start of production of iTablet Tablet PCs (there is speculation on the name, isn't it?). The device will be available in two versions, with a 10.2-inch and 12.1-inch touchscreen widescreen display and optional multitouch support (in this case, Windows 7 is also possible on board).

Inside, the tablet has a 1.6 gigahertz Intel processor, a 250-gigabyte HDD, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, three USB ports and HDMI (also optional). And a 1.3 MP webcam into the load.

In the sale of the device should appear in April 2010.
