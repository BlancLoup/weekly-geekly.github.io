Happy Farm selected 8 start-up projects for the third cycle of the acceleration program.

Happy Farm Business Incubator selected residents of the third cycle of its acceleration program. They were 7 IT projects from Ukraine, China, Italy and Uzbekistan:

Neuronsbox (China) is an innovative service for machine learning and statistical data analysis. www.neuronsbox.com

Hashtago (Ukraine) combines a B2B web service that allows you to run and manage cross-platform marketing campaigns with hashtags at the communication center (with the integrated big-data analysis tool), and a B2C hashtag of promotional campaigns for participants. www.hashtago.com

Microtenders (Ukraine) is a service that allows medium and small businesses to quickly and easily search for suppliers of goods and services and place them in a competitive environment to get the best deals. www.microtenders.com

Keypic (Italy) is an anti-spam service without using CAPTCHAs. www.keypic.com

Be healthy (Uzbekistan) is a platform that helps the user to search for lists of drugs in the appropriate pharmacy.
The main advantage is a combination of quick search, lists of drugs, reminders and descriptions, combined in one application.


The seven residents of the third cycle also include two Ukrainian C-class resident startups:

NewZmate is a service that unites news around events based on user interests, as well as allowing you to easily find related news on related topics and most flexibly customize news sources. http://www.newzmate.com/

EyeDoc is software that can reduce the negative impact of a computer on the eyes. The product is based on eye tracking technology using a webcam. EyeDoc detects user deviations from computer hygiene standards by analyzing the image of the eyes and displays notifications. www.sightwatcher.com

These projects passed a two-week training program at Happy Farm in July of this year, after which the International Board of the incubator decided to participate in the third cycle as full-fledged residents. In addition, one of these startups - NewZmate - was selected for a monthly internship in the US, along with startups of the second cycle, which will begin on September 4th.

In addition, one project in class C has already been selected - Dokity is a service for quick and convenient data exchange in business communications www.dokity.com

The set of projects for the third cycle started in May 2013 and officially ended on August 27th. Number of applications submitted this season - 170.

Happy Farm acceleration program for third-cycle start-ups started on September 2.
