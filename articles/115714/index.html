PrestaShop 1.4 - a new version of the online store platform



Probably many are familiar with this fairly functional and user-friendly system.
The other day, or rather on March 16th, a new version was released.

It was nice to read on Twitter tonight:
Here we are guys! PrestaShop v.1.4 Final is out, check it now!
More info tomorrow ;-) Now we're gonna sleep a bit! ->

And the next day, as promised, a list of major changes was posted, the translation of which can be found under the cut.
+ screenshots



More than 65 new features, unsurpassed performance ... The version of Prestashop so expected by our community has finally become available. Upgrade your existing stores and enjoy new features and hundreds of optimizations or join the 50,000 merchants who already trust Prestashop.

Catalog


Product Comparison

Allows buyers to easily compare the characteristics of several products.



Product condition

It provides an opportunity to indicate the condition of the goods (new, used, repaired) and mark the goods as being sold only via the Internet.



Catalog mode (products will be available only for viewing)

The ability to disable the display of prices and payment methods will allow to transform an online store into a catalog.

Product price depending on quantity

Allows you to set the unit cost of goods (kilogram, liters).

Management of UPC product codes has been added (USA, Canada)

An additional way to identify the product (via UPC codes ).

The history of the movement of goods (Sales, Purchases, etc.)

The ability to track the history of the movement of goods.

Advanced merchandise management

The ability to control the number of different modifications of the goods (color, size).
Now not only the product itself, but also its modifications are taken into account.

The minimum amount of goods to order

The ability to set the minimum quantity of goods that can be added to the order.

Buyers / Customers


Customer service integrated into the store's control panel

An interface that integrates messages sent by customers through a contact form and stores order messages in chronological order.


Contact Applications

The ability to attach files when sending a message through the contact form.

Coupons

The ability to create coupons for groups of customers, individual customers.

Orders


Checkout on one page

Increase the number of orders, allowing users to issue it in just 1 step.


Guest registration

Now there is a simpler and faster way to increase conversion. Allows your customers to place an order without registering an account.


Possibility to repeat the order in one click

Provides customers with the opportunity to repeat the order in just one click in your account.

Payments


AUTHORIZE AIM

The most popular payment system in the US is now available in Prestashop out of the box.


Security

A new module that evaluates the reliability of transactions. This module, integrated into Prestashop, helps to fight against fraud.


Rules for pricing

The ability to set prices and their changes depending on the currency, country or group of customers.

Tax management by country, state (region) and county for the United States.

The ability to set taxes depending on the zones.

Delivery


New delivery services: UPS, USPS, FEDEX

New modules allow you to deliver using: UPS, USPS, FedEx in the USA


Shipping cost individually for each product

Ability to set different shipping costs depending on the product

delivery date

Module to display the approximate delivery date depending on the selected delivery method

Setting price ranges for shipping methods

The ability to set shipping costs depending on the price and weight of the order separately for each delivery method.

New MODULECARRIER class for delivery modules

You can choose between 2 ways to calculate shipping costs: by price or weight ranges, or by using an external service.

Statistics

New Dashboard in the back office
A quick way to access statistics

Export statistics

Ability to export statistics to .csv

Integration of Prestashop add-ons directly to the back office

Became available modules for back office.

Export orders and invoices

The ability to export your invoices and orders to PDF using the filter system.

Use of 3 different logos

The ability to use 3 different logos (for email, invoices and the store itself)

Store locations

List and indicate the location of your stores, go to a new level of communication with customers giving them the opportunity to meet with you face to face.


Store interface (front office)


Parameter Navigation (BETA)

Allows customers to select products based on predetermined criteria (price, size, color, etc.)
