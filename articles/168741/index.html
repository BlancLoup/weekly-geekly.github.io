Teradata Information Resources

Communicating with users of our systems, we occasionally receive questions about what information resources exist regarding Teradata and its products. To answer these questions, here too we decided to compile a list of resources that directly relate to Teradata, and where you can find everything from distributions to documentation and forums.

  • Official Teradata documentation: www.info.teradata.com , including the documentation for the DBMS itself
  • Teradata developer community: developer.teradata.com . Here you can find many articles and Q & A.
  • Teradata official forum: forums.teradata.com/forum
  • Distributions: downloads.teradata.com/download You can download utilities, as well as the Express version of the Teradata DBMS (in the form of VMware or EC2). Also available for download is Teradata Aster virtual machine - this is BigData solution from Teradata
  • Not an official, but an active forum on Teradata: www.teradataforum.com - works in the form of a conference.
  • Teradata Region European (EMEA) Blog: blogs.teradata.com/emea
  • Electronic version of Teradata Magazine: www.teradatamagazine.com
  • Well, just the official website of Teradata: www.teradata.com

As well as social accounts on Twitter:
  • twitter.com/teradata
  • twitter.com/teradataemea

And Facebook groups:
  • www.facebook.com/Teradata
  • www.facebook.com/TeradataEMEA
