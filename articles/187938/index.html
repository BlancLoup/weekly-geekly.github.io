If in times of old (already in the severe 70s when the saints Date and Codd worked at IBM, and the 300 MB hard disk weighed like an adult sow), the data was optimized initially for storage at ... yes on anything: the files consisting of the header, the body and the end-of-file icon on the disks of file systems in a modern form did not exist - the data from the database was flopped onto disks already marked for storing data of a given table, that is, the formatting was done by the database itself and for storing its own data.
