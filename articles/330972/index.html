For the first time in Russia at the robotic Olympiad will be held competitions drone



From 23 to 25 June, 589 people from 50 regions of the country will come to Innopolis University to the All-Russian Robotic Olympiad. Competitions are divided into 8 categories, among which two are new - autonomous unmanned aircraft competitions and robotic tetris.

In one of the competitions, participants need to assemble and program an autonomous robot capable of flying in a limited space of the room. The drone must perform tasks for the detection and delivery of goods, accurate passage through control points, around obstacles. The task is performed offline, without the participation of the pilot.
