[Webinar] Representation of hierarchical structures in relational databases

Any experienced developer knows that as the project grows, the complexity of the data structures used and the interrelationship of the elements also increases. Sometimes attempts to independently formalize and present these data structures in relational databases end quite badly in terms of performance.

January 27 (Thursday) Sergey Tomulevich, the head of the UGC services development group of Rambler , will open a series of webinars on the topic of representing hierarchical structures in relational databases. In the first webinar, Sergey will touch on the topic of presenting tree structures in databases and examine in detail the model of the presentation of the Adjacency List .

The time of the seminar is 13: 00-15: 00 . Participation in the webinar is free, but pre- registration is required.
