Kaspersky Industrial CTF 2017 in Shanghai: Everything at the Cyberstorm Oil Refinery



Kaspersky Lab invites those who wish to take part in a large-scale cyber tour of the model of an oil refinery - our industrial CTF 2017. This time the final of the competition will be held on October 24 in Shanghai, right at the GeekPWN conference. This event, dedicated to the new zero-day vulnerabilities in devices of all stripes, is held by the partner of LC - Keen Cloud Tech. So, the four best teams that have overcome the qualifying stage online will have the opportunity not only to attack the refining processes of a small plant with a digital substation, but also to see the business capital of China, as well as communicate with the best industry security specialists. In addition, the finalists will be able to test the strength and several IoT devices connected to each other, including the new version of the Alcokoe LK, which will award burglars with interesting drinks. And all this hack & fun - at the expense of the organizers: the finalists will pay for tickets and accommodation in China.

However, before getting to Shanghai, the participating teams will have to overcome the qualifying stage in the form of task-based CTF, which will be held online from October 6 to 8. Cyberstormists expect the following types of tests: Crypt, Reverse, Web, Network and Fun. After summarizing the four best teams will be selected, which will go to the finals in the Middle Kingdom. To participate in the qualifying competition you need to register on the site . We are waiting for you, friends! And see you in Shanghai!
