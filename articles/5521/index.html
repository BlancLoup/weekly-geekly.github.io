"Raska" offered futures for the upcoming elections to the Duma

In the exchange game "Raska" a new family of instruments appeared - futures for the upcoming parliamentary elections. The participants of the stock simulator got the opportunity to acquire assets, the final cost of which will be determined depending on how many percent this or that political force will receive in the State Duma of the new convocation.

Recall that the Raska project was launched by the FINAM investment holding on April 1, 2005. The number of participants in the game, which is defined as an interactive satirical exchange simulator, now exceeds 8.5 thousand people. Players can buy and sell securities, becoming financial bigwigs. In most cases, the events taking place in Rashka represent the author's interpreted reflection of the political and economic realities in Russia.

According to FINAM, at the first stage six futures were launched in Rashka, each of which, as expected, has its own ticker.
