Photos of the Earth from the ISS from astronaut Terry Werts



Terry Werts is an astronaut who twice visited the ISS (he is at the station now). The first time he went to the ISS in February 2010, and worked at the station for 13 days. The second time he flew into Earth orbit on November 24, 2014, as a flight engineer.

The cosmonaut's terms of reference are wide enough - after all, any astronaut should be able to do a little more than his specialization requires. Well, as a hobby, Terry chose a photograph - and he photographs the Earth from orbit, his colleagues and open space. But he devotes maximum free time to the Earth.

Terry Werts has a Twitter account , where he uploads his pictures in good resolution. Fortunately, the ISS now has high-speed Internet, and there is enough bandwidth for something more than sending text messages.
