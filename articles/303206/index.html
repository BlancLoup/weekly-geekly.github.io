Microsoft machine learning hackathon results in May

Hello!

At the end of May 2016, we successfully completed the third hackathon for machine learning. The topic of advanced data analysis in recent years is gaining increasing popularity, and we are pleased to contribute to its development. For two days we gathered more than 150 people under the roof of our office, who, during a short acquaintance with each other, quickly chose the captains and divided into 24 teams.


The hackathon participants were offered a choice of solving 4 data modeling tasks and 1 visualization task using Microsoft technologies: the Azure Machine Learning machine learning service and the Power BI business intelligence service.

Customers whose business problems the participants had to solve for two days were:
  • Magnitogorsk Metallurgical Plant (to determine the silicon content in the furnace)
  • Business Youth Community of Entrepreneurs (categorize clients hot and cold)
  • Network of clinics "Mother and Child" (to predict the sale of groups of services)
  • And one of the largest Russian banks, which participated anonymously. (estimate future customer turnovers on transactions)

More information about the tasks can be found on the event website .
