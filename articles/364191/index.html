Dragon revenge

At first, when no one tries to kill you (which is about one level out of a million), the game seems simple, but as the location number increases, difficulty gradually increases. For the sake of justice, it should be noted that the player also has new ways of destroying the game world, such as, for example, cobblestones.



Dragon Revenge is perfectly adapted for the wheelbarrow, but it also plays well with the gamepad. Support for vibration in collisions and shooting, smooth control from the sticks, that's all.
