Fitness tracker Mio Fuse


Mio Fuse is the new brainchild of MioGlobal. C company you may be familiar from their previous product - Mio Alpha, we wrote his review , for example, in BoxOverview (by the way, now we live on d3 ). The developers listened to the wishes of users and released not just a heart rate monitor with memory, but a full-fledged fitness tracker with the functionality of the previous generation of devices.

He can also monitor the pulse constantly and send it to applications on the phone, but in addition he also records the pulse and activity in his own memory, which allows him to take only his workout, leaving the phone at home.

Toothbrush Philips Sonicare


The Philips Sonicare Toothbrush is a true geek toothbrush. First, it has a button and indicators. Secondly, there is a PCB inside and a real microcontroller. Thirdly, it must be constantly charged!
But seriously - this is the best brush of all that I have tried. Unlike other electric toothbrushes, which brush their teeth with rotational movements with a frequency of about 50-100 hertz, Sonicare moves with a frequency of five times more, and due to this, perfectly cleans teeth and plaque, darkening and tartar.
Of course, this is just my opinion, but you can also read reviews from other users, for example on the Yandex Market . The main danger of this brush is that after it is impossible to return to the usual brushes.
There are many models of brushes in this Philips shop. Prices for the younger model start at 2 590 rubles - this is HX3110 . I have been using the HX6711 model for 5,290 rubles for several years. Older models such as the HX9352 and HX9332 are more expensive - 8,490 and 13,490 rubles, respectively, but their equipment is better - charging in the form of a glass and travel case with USB charging.
