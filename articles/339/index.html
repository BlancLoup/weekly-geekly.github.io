Hans Reiser arrested on suspicion of murder

Yesterday it became known that Hans Reiser , the developer of the ReiserFS file system, was arrested on suspicion of murdering his wife Nina Reiser.

ReiserFS are haunted by failure. Despite all the efforts of the developers, this FS was never included in the core , in August the team was concerned about finding work , and most recently Suse Labs refused to use ReiserFS as the main file system for their OS. Now here is the arrest of the head.

In connection with these events, the future prospects for the development of ReiserFS look vague.
