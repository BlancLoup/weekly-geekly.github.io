Pen and notebook



The easiest, fastest and most convenient option, but like everything in our world, it is relative. Why? It is very easy to forget at home, to take a whole bag with you, so that only these two things can be put in there. A pen can stop writing, a notebook can be blown away. But in general, what does this mean - the inevitable achievement of goals?

And now we answer the question, what is the inevitable achievement of goals? First of all, this is a state where there is no turning back, bridges are destroyed, all that is left behind does not matter, only what is ahead is important. This is only part of the state, but from the above, I think you understand that writing in notebooks, you certainly approach the goal, but the cost of not achieving the goal is small, you are the only one who knows about the goal, which means the chances of going astray are great.

Any development and change is stressful for the organism, so when you set a goal for yourself, you have two options, either you will do it or you will not, but under stress 80% of people do not achieve their goals. The question arises, how to make the achievement of the goal inevitable?

The first and most important thing is systematic , to do something every day to move forward. But how to make yourself do it? There are several ways, the first is publicity , the second is pledge. There are cases when even the publicity and price of the word will not make you leave the comfort zone and start doing something for the purposes.
