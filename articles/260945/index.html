What you need to know about working with text preview emails



You have spent a lot of time optimizing your emails, and you are sure that the mailing lists are well written, look good and, more importantly, appear normally in most email clients. The problem is that even the most ideal letters with high probability will never even be opened by the recipient. They will simply fall into the Inbox folder, where a huge amount of text is already buried.

The inbox folder is the first thing that a mail program user sees, but it is also often the last factor to be considered when deciding whether to open a letter. All mail clients necessarily show who the letter came from (the ‚Äúfrom‚Äù field) and the subject of the message. Some programs also show a small text preview.

Optimization of this text can bring excellent results and significantly increase the percentage of open letters (sometimes up to 45% ).

What is the text preview


The preview text (or preheader) is a small piece of text that ‚Äúpulls up‚Äù from the message body, and is usually displayed below the fields with the sender's name and the subject of the message.



The concept of the text of the preview is a modern adaptation of the concept of the Johnson Box, a block with a selling proposal that was placed at the very top of the paper advertising messages. The idea is exactly the same - the text of the preview can attract the attention of the recipient of the message and encourage the person to open the letter.

In addition, the preheader can also affect the user's behavior ‚Äî for example, if you interest a person in mentioning an interesting object (the name of the article), he will then look for it in the text of the message. An example of such a manipulation - the company Wedding Wire began to mention in the text of the preview "inspirational wedding ideas of this week", which aroused interest among readers. The percentage of message discoveries during the tests increased by 30%.



Conducting such tests can give a good result (albeit not always fast), plus is the ease of making changes - no need to typeset and change the HTML or template design.
