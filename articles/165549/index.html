Take care of your sight, use f.lux

Almost four years have passed since the f.lux program was mentioned on Habr√©, the real medicine for those who have tired eyes from behind the monitor.



The program changes the color profile of the monitor, depending on the time of day. At night, the eyes are less tired of warm color tones (3400 K), during the day - from cold (6500 K), which correspond more to daylight.

You can adjust the settings to your taste.



If you work in a graphic editor, you can temporarily stop it.

F.lux is a free program, there are versions for Windows, Linux, Mac OS and iOS (iPhone / iPad).
