Lenovo Super Share for Corporate Customers

This time we will tell you not about technical nuances, "iron" updates, software or other usual things. Today's information will be useful to people who are doing business in the IT industry or selling computer systems.



Lenovo is launching a rebate partner program called T2. The point is very simple: when you purchase at least five computers, you get a refund of $ 10 to $ 20 for each of them. Thus, buying five cars, you can save up to $ 100.

There is only one restriction: the offer is valid on the configuration of computers only within the lists, and not between them. This means that you can choose, for example, four 90DA00FCRS and one 90FN005LRS, or three 10KY0039RU and two 10HS008JRU. But to combine 90DA00FCRS and 10HS008JRU will not work.

Characteristics of the systems involved in the action, looks promising. For example, the configuration of the Ideacentre 300-20ISH (90DA00HXRS) consists of an Intel Core i3-6100 processor (3.7 GHz), an NVIDIA GeForce GTX750 video card (2 GB of memory) and 8 GB of RAM, and a 1TB hard drive with a solid-state drive for 8 GB The line of Lenovo S510 is designed specifically for small businesses, the configurations are tailored to the specifics of the segment. Intel Pentium G440-G3900, Core i3-i7, integrated Intel or NVIDIA GeForce GT720 graphics card (1 or 2 GB of memory) are installed on these models, the size of optional memory is up to 32 GB DDR4. The hard disk is 500 GB or 1 TB, plus a 120-256 GB SSD.

Accessing this promotion is simple: you need to indicate your intention to participate in the offer to Lenovo managers at akapustin@lenovo.com , svoina@lenovo.com . In the letter, specify: Lenovo ID (if not, you can enter here ), company name, contact person, email address, distributor for charging rebates.
