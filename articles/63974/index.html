American chemists have invented a new way to create self-destruct documents. They used gold nanoparticles coated with a layer of azobenzene molecules. When exposed to ultraviolet light, these nanoparticles become polarized and form clusters, and the substance of such nanoparticles first changes its color from red to blue, and then becomes completely colorless.
