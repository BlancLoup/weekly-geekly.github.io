Ever2One Converter - the first convenient way to import from Evernote to MS OneNote

On the Internet, holivars do not subside, which is still better - Evernote or Microsoft OneNote, and some people use them in parallel. And if Evernote has built-in tools for importing content from OneNote, there is no similar functionality in OneNote. And until recently, OneNote users had to invent various workarounds to perform a similar operation. But now everything has changed, because Our team wrote Ever2One Converter, a utility that imports notes from Evernote to OneNote.
