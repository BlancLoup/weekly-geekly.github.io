E3 2008: Where to watch live broadcasts



A few hours remain until the start of the main gaming event of the year - the Electronic Entertainment Expo Media & Business Summit, or simply speaking - E3 2008. And of course, one of the most important events of the exhibition are the press conferences of three giants of the console industry - Sony, Microsoft and Nintendo. It is at these events that the loudest announcements are made every year and it takes the form of an industry course for the next year. Naturally, many portals will conduct live broadcasts from conferences. A list of where it will be possible to observe them and is offered to your attention.

Broadcast starts at 21.30 Moscow time


- IGN Live Wire (english)
- GameSpot Live (english)
- G4TV Live (english)
- CNZ Live (Russian broadcast)
