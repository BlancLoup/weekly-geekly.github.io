Wuala closes free accounts

Today I received a letter that on December 14, 2014 all free accounts are closed:
Wuala account:
IF YOU USE FREE WUALA STORAGE, PLEASE READ THIS NOTICE CAREFULLY
DECEMBER 31, 2014, we will be terminating all existing free storage.

What is offered in return?

Well, firstly, they introduced a new tariff plan for 5GB at a price of 0.99 Euro \ month and 9 Euro \ year. And secondly, until December 31, when paying one year for any tariff, Wuala doubles the purchase (they give a promotional code, you can either double the seat, or donate it to someone else).

So here, the freebie is over. In June, the registration of all free accounts was canceled, and by the end of the year they would be completely closed. And it all started quite differently.
