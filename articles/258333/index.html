All publications by Habrahabr, Hiktaims, Megamind in one place



I propose to get acquainted with the service that allows you to read all the publications of Habrahabr, Hiktimes, Megamind in one place.
The service parses the RSS feed of each of the three resources, downloads the articles and puts them into the database. Then the site displays the information received. The interface was made in the style of Material Design. The site is made adaptive and allows you to read equally conveniently and on large monitors and on tablets and phones. The emergence of such a service is associated with the need to see all publications from three resources at once and not to switch between them.

Implemented standard features that are on other similar services. For example, such:

1) display all publications on one page.
2) display of publications for each resource separately.
3) display comments on the publication.



But besides this, there are functions that are not available on all services. For example, such:

1) display publications only with a positive rating.
2) display publications only with a negative rating.
3) displaying publications without rating.
