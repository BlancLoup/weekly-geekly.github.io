"Competition" continues! New competition from InfoTeKS Academy!

Do you know what makes your smartphone at 2 am? Security of mobile platforms, applications and devices is one of the most discussed topics today. That is why the participants of InfoTeKS Academy are increasingly choosing it for their research, and that is why most of the competitions and contests are aimed at studying this issue.
