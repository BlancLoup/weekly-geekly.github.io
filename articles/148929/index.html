Yandex.Money from Europe with a low commission

Foreign Yandex.Money users have the opportunity to replenish their accounts by bank transfer from any country in the world. In 14 European countries, the translation will be considered internal, so the commission will not exceed 1%.

What is needed for this?
1. For the sender - a bank account opened in the UK, France, Italy, Scandinavia, the Baltic States or in other countries of Western and Eastern Europe.
2. Recipient - the status of the identified user in Yandex.Money. If the recipient is anonymous, to replenish his account from abroad is possible only by international transfer.

Everything is very simple. Select the country of departure on the service page , and the system will offer you the cheapest way to top up. For example, to receive money from relatives in Europe, you do not need to open a special bank account. It is enough to inform them of the details obtained on the Yandex.Money site (they are generated specifically for the country and currency in which the sender‚Äôs account is opened). The transfer can be sent via Internet banking or in cash - in any bank branch of the selected country. The money will go to your Yandex.Money account within 2-5 business days.

The maximum amount of one-time replenishment for identified users is 100,000 rubles (or equivalent in another currency), for anonymous users, 15,000 rubles.
