Advertising services "Euroservice"

Client: ROSNO.
Advertised product: Euroservice service.
TSA: motorists.
Weight: for a banner 200x300 - 20 Kb, the rest - 25 Kb.
6th version of flash player.
Note: all pictures are clickable.

He tried to make the most attractive banner for car enthusiasts, to emphasize the main advantages of the Euroservice program.
Road signs clearly and simply display the information necessary for Central Asia.
Rummaged in the network, dug a mountain of road signs. I repeated the traffic rules :)



The first version of the banner was:



Everything except greenery on the background is a vector!
It is necessary to make such an emphasis on this, because in the last article about the advertising of the Incognito service , many people did not understand that I had drawn the box in a flash.

Each text corresponds to a certain character.
The penultimate sign was planned sign "Main road" with the text: "CTP at the European level." The last sign should have been a special sign ‚ÄúROSNO‚Äù.

After some discussion, we decided to remove the ‚ÄúMain road‚Äù sign (it was a pity, but it was necessary to clearly comply with the guidelines), shorten the text, make more greenery in the background and arrange the last frame strictly according to the rules.


Strictly on guidelines.

How did I draw all this?


From left to right, the layers from bottom to top are shown. Because weight restrictions were wild (up to 20 Kb for 200x300), it was necessary to use the same elements. At the same time, so that they do not give the impression of copies. This applies to rivets for signs (there are 2 pairs), a sign for additional information (plates), consisting of 4 layers (bottom of the picture), 3 layers of which are always the same, but only the machine and text change.
When drawing tried to make them as realistic as possible, as in life.

It turned out such a banner .
