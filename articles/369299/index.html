Vkontakte: there was no hacking of the resource, the old database is sold on the network

According to representatives of the social network, in the dark-web sold user base 2011-2012



A few hours ago, the LeakedSource resource, which monitors and analyzes leaks, reported that the Vkontakte user base with a number of accounts exceeding 100 million is being sold on the network. The data is not freely available, but sold for 1 Bitcoin (almost $ 600) current exchange rate). After analyzing the database, it turned out that it contained exactly 100,544,934 records. The database includes e-mail, username, password and telephone (if any).

After the information about what happened was published on Geektimes, many readers found their data in the leaked database. At the same time, the passwords of a number of users were complex, by no means dictionary. However, this did not prevent hackers from obtaining information about such accounts. Now the social network "Vkontakte" commented on what happened.

According to the administration of the resource, we are not talking about hacking into the social network user database.
