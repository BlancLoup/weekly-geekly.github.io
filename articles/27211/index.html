Eco-friendly modular PC. Amateur version

Designer Ben Chase became briefly known to the general public by showing his version of the eco-modular all-in-one computer. Chase made this PC from recycled plastic. The main achievement: completely "invisible" electricity consumption. Only 27 watts.


Chase does not disclose the stuffing of his all-in-one PC. But he assures that there are enough resources for working with texts and surfing the Internet. This still useless environmental monster running Windows.

However, it may not be completely worthless. Such PCs (due to their low cost) can be easily installed in schools and even kindergartens.
