Oxygen Accelerator - Our First Day in London

So, today is the first day of our stay on the startup accelerator in London - Oxygen Accelerator . Earlier, in early September, our web site protection service against DDoS attacks was selected to participate in this accelerator. Our decision to apply for this program was dictated by the fact that Oxygen is one of five accelerators that have been approved by the UK government for obtaining a business visa for 6 months. This, in turn, will extend the stay in England if our startup attracts an investment of 250,000 pounds.



The Oxygen Accelerator program takes 13 weeks and runs at Google Campus in London. Accelerator guide and interested investors select 10 teams from all applications and then invite them to the program.




The program conditions are as follows:

1) You give 8% of shares from your startup to the Oxygen Accelerator team.
2) In return, you receive an investment in your startup in the amount of ¬£ 6,000 per team member. The maximum number of people in a team is 3 people. In our case, we came together, so we‚Äôll get a total of 12,000 pounds.
3) During the first week you also need to register a new company in England and open a bank account.
