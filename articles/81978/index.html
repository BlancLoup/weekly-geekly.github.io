Web studios rating 2009 launched on the Runet Rating project

The rating of leading web studios has been launched, which will be published on the Rating Runet project.



The rating methodology is based on a portfolio of current web studio projects that are developed by the company. The more up-to-date projects in the web studio portfolio and the higher the credibility indexes of these sites from the point of view of search engines, the higher the developer company is in the ranking.

The position of web studios in the local market is of great importance for their clients. Therefore, in addition to the main rating - TOP 100 web studios Runet , ratings will be generated by countries, federal cities of Russia and the largest cities. Also, ratings will be generated by project type (corporate sites, online stores, promotional sites, portals and services, blogs, social networks).

Any web-studio that is professionally engaged in website development and has at least 10 active projects in its portfolio can take part in the ranking. Companies participating in the rating, through their personal account, post information about the sites that they have developed.

In every major city, the Runet Rating project has its own regional accredited partner. This makes it possible to show the most accurate cut of the market not only in Moscow, but in fact in all major cities of Russia and the CIS countries.

Data collection for the rating will be carried out until February 22, 2010.

The final results of the web studios rating will be publicly presented in March 2010. Intermediate results are already available in real time on the page of the Runet Rating project.
