The latest typewriter factory in the world has stopped working



Yes, the history of typewriters has recently ended, and in fairness it should be noted that these devices have been popular for many years - from 1867 to 2011. In general, it is difficult to immediately recall any device that has been in use for so many years or more (except for the printing press). So, the last factory, which produces typewriters, ceased production of new devices, and only 500 pieces of previously manufactured machines remained in the company's warehouses.

The Godrej and Boyce factory is located in Mumbai, and the mass production of typewriters ceased in 2009, and now the factory closes. Only 20 years ago, the company produced and sold 50,000 typewriters a year, but only 800 units were sold last year.
