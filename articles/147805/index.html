View Internet videos on iPad offline

I have been using the Pocket application for a long time (formerly readitlater) and I really like the opportunity to read various articles while I'm offline, such as going to the subway or train. Only one thing was missing - to watch videos offline, for example from youtube. Of course, you can download a separate video, then convert it and only then upload it, but it‚Äôs somehow difficult, but I wanted it just like in a pocket. Looking for a similar application the other day, I found TagDisk HD .

After you download the application everything is very simple:
1. Run the application and go to the Browse tab
2. Find the video we are interested in.

3. Click on the arrow down from the bottom of the screen - a dialog box appears

4. On the Downloads tab, you can track download progress.

5. After downloading the video will be available on the Files tab.

7. Profit!

Enjoy watching!
