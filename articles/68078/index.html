What is Pindikator, or Pinarik in numbers

When we use Pinaric, then periods of "recoil" regularly occur. That is, such moments when working with tablets of goals and time becomes a chore. Well, yes, in the morning and in the evening we mark the passing time. So what? After all, it is better to evaluate the motivating factor of time in some quantitative and descriptive criteria. Many perfectly logical color pinachik. One color for a productive day, the other for a so-so day. However, after a short time, it becomes clear that this kind of assessment leads to a quite predictable result: the ratio of time hovers around 50 to 50. 54% vs. 46%. And is it worth messing with pinarik if the result is so predictable? It is worth, if you add it pindikatorom. First, consider the principle, and then - one of the possible ways of implementation.

1. Every day of our life in one way or another includes elements of "global areas". For example, Bodo Schaefer defines five such areas: "Health, finance, communication, emotions, the meaning of life." But Alex Shalman - already ten: "Health and sports, education, career and money, public relations, home and family, character, emotions, habits, life purpose, spiritual development." I am sure that the essence is clear, and everyone is able to identify such areas for himself personally.

2. Each of the selected areas of life necessarily needs attention and development. Moreover, attention and development are daily activities. It may be insignificant, but daily.

But besides this - also the average score for the last 10 days.
