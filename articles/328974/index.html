Top designs by Behance users

Ksenia Suvorova, the editor of the blog "Netologii", made a selection of some of the coolest works of designers at Behance.

Our editors love Behance. We decided to highlight the coolest designs according to the version of site users in several categories: animation design , branding and illustrations . Watch the compilation, learn and be inspired.

Animation design (motion design)


" Air Max " from ManvsMachine




London studio ManvsMachine specifically for Nike Europe has created amazing video from animated graphics. The magical transformation is so shocking that 83 thousand views account for a little less than 10 thousand likes.


By the way, this is also an example of a great brand book, not for a large corporation, but for a specific product. Yes, it happens too.

" TrickPics " and " Ooohlympics " for Pornhub


Who will best understand the international brand? The management of the company decided that five Russians with production from Officer & Gentelmen. Our compatriots did not suffer when creating a series of cartoons for an adult brand and used Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator and Corel Draw.


To create an animation, they use this particular set of tools. And each of their work gains at least a thousand likes and many views.


Works Eiko Ojala for the media


A graphic designer and illustrator from Tallinn scored over 1.222 million views and about 124,000 likes on Behance. His work is incredibly stylish, minimalist and simple. Beauty, worthy of constant attention and orders of the largest media in the world: Sunday Review, New York Times, La Poste, Wired and others.



Look at non-standard ideas and, especially, execution. Atypical colors, texture and excellent technical work. In simplicity is power.



Branding


" Autistic art "


The project of the Czech team of seven people "Autistic art" has collected more than 82 thousand views, about 12 thousand likes and half a thousand comments. The idea was born from a minimalist image of the window and drawings of people with autism spectrum disorders.


When creating a brand book , Adobe products were used: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign. Work is a great example of how a good idea and execution can be applied on any subject.

The Ramotion team from San Francisco received almost 207,000 likes for all of their work at Behance, and their creations viewed a total of 2,164,000 people. The publication of several works, one of the best, received 134 thousand views and 18 thousand likes.

Studio from Bristol, England, scored likes, comments and views even more than the popular company Ramotion. They have 3.5 million views, 265 thousand likes.

Illustrations


Works Emi Haze


Creations of the Italian designer and artist adore people from all over the world. Only on Behance she has more than 3.15 million views and 317 thousand likes. She loves to combine photography and landscapes.




Her tools are standard Photoshop and Apple iMac, Wacom Cintiq graphics tablet, watercolor and acrylic. She also uses InDesign periodically.

" Kitten Free Typeface Family "


Everyone loves cats and beautiful typography. Italian graphic designers have combined two in one. And a little blew Behance. Only 90 thousand views, but also 22 thousand likes. The ratio is very large.




The team uses Photoshop, Illustrator, After Affects and FontLab Studio.
