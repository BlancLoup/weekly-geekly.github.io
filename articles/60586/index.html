Red5 Solar Designer



red5 is a company that specializes in the sale of "high-tech" toys. One of the most interesting developments is solar-powered roboconstructor. From a set of standard parts, a child can assemble a robo-pack, and then convert it into a walking transformer, and then rearrange the parts in places and turn it into a car, and so on. In total, the kit provides six options: a dog, a robot, a machine, a wind turbine, a boat and an airplane.

The trick is that it all works from the sun or a 50-watt halogen bulb: the dog and the robot are walking, the machine is driving, the windmills are spinning.

The kit consists of 25 parts and is sold here for ridiculous money - $ 22.
