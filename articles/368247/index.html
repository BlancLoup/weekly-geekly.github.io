Android 6.0 Marshmallow for Sony Xperia Z5

Earlier this month, Sony began distributing the Android 6.0 update for its Xperia Z5, Xperia Z5 Compact and Xperia Z5 Premium smartphones in Japan, but now users from other countries, including Russia, have also received the OTA update.





The international versions of the Xperia Z5 series smartphones began to receive an update of Android 6.0 Marshmallow from March 7. This is a bild with an index of 32.1.A.1.163, which includes the February security patches. After the update, the modified camera interface, support for stickers in Xperia Messaging and other innovations will appear.



The list of confirmed countries in which the update has become available includes Thailand, Russia, Algeria, Saudi Arabia, the USA, India, Malaysia, and in the near future the list of countries will expand. A complete list of Sony smartphones that will receive an upgrade to Android 6.0 can be found in one of the past posts .
