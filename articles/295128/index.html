Upvoted news website launched - the mercantile "brother" of the Reddit project

Reddit is a resource that allows you to post a link to interesting information on the Internet. Users of the resource can vote for their favorite materials, thereby promoting them "up". However, on Tuesday, the Reddit team launched another project - Upvoted . This name is translated as "raised up by a majority vote." On the new resource materials will be selected on the basis of the best publications Reddit.

But the new resource has fundamental differences from its predecessor:

1 On Upvoted there is no possibility to vote for published posts.

2 On Upvoted will appear paid publications from advertisers.

In addition, the developers plan to remove the ability to comment on posts.

According to analysts, in this way Reddit management expects to expand its audience and earn money on it. According to Enders Analysis , the target audience of Reddit are young people aged 18 to 24 years. According to Thomas Caldecott of Enders Analysis, the new site was created because of the impossibility of further increasing the audience on the old resource. In addition, due to the uncontrolled nature of post publications, it was not possible to use the new effective advertising formats to monetize Reddit itself.

According to Wired, a new team will be working on Upvoted. Her minimum task is to publish about 20 stories per day. The maximum task is to increase the number of publications to 40.
