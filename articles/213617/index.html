How we caught sea crucians, or review of Huawei OceanStor Dorado2100 G2



At the end of 2012, Huawei introduced the second generation of the OceanStor Dorado2100 G2 storage system. It was interesting for us to compare it with the recently visited IBM FlashSystem 820 flash testing system . But first, a little more about Dorado.


The Dorado2100 G2 architecture is entirely built on SSD solid state drives and uses advanced cache management technology and scheduling algorithms for I / O operations. High reliability of the system is ensured by redundancy of components: power supplies, fans, controllers, interface modules, SSD and BBU are duplicated and allow to avoid the occurrence of single failures, and the used methods of balancing the degree of wear, the restoration of damaged blocks and scrubbing technology providing an average uptime (MTBF) of over 1,200,000 hours. The power of the device 580W saves up to 90% energy consumption compared to traditional disk arrays.
