Update Desktop Client Yota Music

A new version of the Yota Music for PC client application has been released, which allows you to listen to tracks from your Yota library online.

What's new:

* There are functions of searching for music, creating channels and an equalizer.
Now the user can access the advanced search by keyword.

* Now you can create and save your own music channels, compiled by genre, artist name, album year or song name. And, of course, listen to those channels that were created on other Yota Music applications: on the website or on the phone.

* Yota also took care of the sound quality. Any operations with the track (stop, switch, rewind, etc.) are accompanied by spectacular fading - a soft attenuation of the sound. Real music lovers will appreciate the equalizer with preset settings that emphasize the features of different genres (rock, jazz, pop, etc.).

* The new version of the program allows you to register with the Yota Music service directly from the application interface. Now for this to go to the site is not necessary.

The application can be downloaded for free from the website and easily installed on your computer.

Listen to Yota Music online, in the catalog of which there are now more than 600,000 tracks, all registered Yota users can download it for free on the website or using PC and phone apps.

And current Yota subscribers can download tracks for free and listen to them without an internet connection.

PS: The other day there was a discussion of new business models for the sale and distribution of music, Yota just came up with one of them.
