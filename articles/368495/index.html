10 crazy games for Oculus Rift: traveling through the fridge, exploding asses and more

Oculus Rift - the most famous virtual reality glasses and one of the most successful futuristic gadgets. The device began its history with crowdfunding, relatively recently came under the wing of Facebook: Mark Zuckerberg saw the potential in glasses and bought the project for $ 2 billion. Now Oculus Rift has thousands of fans, an interesting future and already several released versions of the device. Especially for augmented reality glasses make games, demo versions of which can be found on specialized forums . Digital Trends editors studied the demos and chose a dozen of the strangest and most memorable games.

The boursin sensorium




The developers of The Boursin Sensorium game created it by request of the eponymous company, the manufacturer of Italian cheese. In the game we fly through the fridge filled with a variety of snacks, including Boursin cream cheese. In flight, the player flies through the ingredients that are present in the cheese. Interactive promo dairy product looks very strange.

Dumpy Going Elephants




This is a fairly well-known game, previously released under Oculus Rift DK 1. With the advent of a new model of augmented reality glasses, the game creators have released a sequel. In a simple arcade you need to play for an elephant running around the city and destroying everything that catches his eye. Neuro




Pictures, "drawn" by Google neural networks, have driven the entire Internet crazy.
