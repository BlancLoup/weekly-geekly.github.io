Ask a question to one of the most experienced cosmonauts in Russia



There was an opportunity to conduct an interview with cosmonaut Pavel Vinogradov , who last year met his sixtieth anniversary aboard the International Space Station and continues his space work in the cosmonaut team.

Pavel flew three times into space, having managed to visit another Mir station in 1997, was a member of the ISS crew in 2006 and 2013. And in 2013, in the role of the station commander. On account of Paul 7 spacewalks, a total duration of 38 hours 25 minutes.

Since 1977, Pavel Vinogradov has been working at RSC (formerly NPO) Energia. First, he trained the crews of the Soyuz and Buran spacecraft, and later joined the cosmonaut corps.



In general: everything you wanted to know about space, but were afraid to ask, you can ask today.
