Social network - a new form of communication


Denoting the subject of further consideration, we will give it a definition: a social network (or social network for short) is a means of organizing and displaying social relationships in a virtual Internet space that satisfies the following requirements: having personal user profiles, the ability to transfer various information between users (photos, music, videos, messages, etc.).

The emergence of social networks was predetermined by the general trend of development of communication methods. Compared to the previous means of communication and information transfer (letter, telephone, fax, audio and video tapes, disks, etc.), the costs of searching and transmitting information between people in social networks are orders of magnitude less. The speed of information exchange is many times higher. In other words, through social networks, it became easier to share information with each other.

The opportunity to share photos, movies and music, find old and new acquaintances, carry on correspondence with them, unite in interest groups, and quickly receive news - all this and much more determined the success of the development of social networks. In the end, it was the presence of these opportunities that led to a big rush (especially among young people) and an explosive growth in the number of users of the first social networks. This required a quick revision of the existing functionality and the development of new features that were added based on the wishes of users and current trends in society.

As social networks developed, attitudes towards them in society changed.
