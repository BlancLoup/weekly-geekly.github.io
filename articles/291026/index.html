All Russian banks working with Visa transferred to NSPC processing

Russian banks that work with the Visa payment system have completely transferred all domestic Russian transactions to the National Payment Card System for processing. Thus, the process of connecting domestic banks (those of them that work with Visa) to the NPCS can be considered complete, reports TASS. The transition process was completed on May 27th.

On May 25, 76 Russian banks were connected to the National Payment Card System. The system itself began operating in the Russian Federation on April 1. The MasterCard payment system was the first to connect to the NSPK system, Visa is expected to connect in June.
