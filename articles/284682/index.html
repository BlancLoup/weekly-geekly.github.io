The United States wants to fine Ukraine for copyright infringement



The International Intellectual Property Alliance ( IIPA ) recommended that the US Chamber of Commerce include Ukraine in the list of countries where copyright infringement is systemic.

According to the IIPA, Ukraine is a key exporter of pirated digital content in the region - where several popular torrent trackers and file sharing sites are located, and the level of use of illegal software exceeds the limit - both from individuals and from government agencies and enterprises. Specific names in the report of the organization do not appear, but it is known that the United States has repeatedly included the demonoid torrent tracker and file hosting service ex.ua in the list of the most pirated resources in the world.

Countries that are on the pirate list of the United States are deprived of the right to economic benefits when importing goods. Thus, Ukrainian exporters, who are in no way associated with copyright infringement, may suffer if they are taxed at a substantial amount - the export volume of only the iron and steel industry and the chemical industry in the United States last year amounted to $ 650 million.

The image of Ukraine among rightholders suffered losses after the last scandal with the closure of file hosting ex.ua , which a week later earned again. In addition, pirated copies of Windows were found on the MVD servers, which also caused a scandal.
