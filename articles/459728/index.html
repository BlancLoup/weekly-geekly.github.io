Smart phone with a breeze: a review of ZTE Red Magic 3

Inside, the best Snapdragon 855 processor today, with 8 + 128 gigabytes of memory, or 12 + 256 and three colors to choose from: absolutely dull black, bright red and camouflage for the 12/256 version.

The 48 megapixel camera from Sony with the popular IMX586 sensor (it will require a separate video), the speakers are double and very loud, two additional buttons on the side for aiming and shooting, a 5000 mAh battery, and inside the main hit is active cooling using tiny, but a very brave fan designed to remove heat from copper and graphite elements inside the case. Let's understand what kind of beast ZTE has turned into as a third gaming smartphone.


The smartphone is huge: the first thing that catches your eye is an elongated 6.65 ‚Äùdisplay, made according to the AMOLED standard (with support for wide dynamic range or HDR). Very similar to modern Samsung - rich colors, high brightness, so that movies, games or viewing photos go with a bang, but reading books is not always convenient.

The system is a naked Android 9 with a small hub for games. Naked Android is dull, nerdy and makes you miss MIUI. Would be a apple maid, probably would hang himself from such beauty.

The game hub is nice - you can customize the control, backlight, performance, notifications, and the fan mode. The hub is turned on by a separate key. If a game from the collection is already running, then side buttons, power and cooler are added to it, and unnecessary notifications are blocked. But aiming, shooting and any other functions (depending on the game) can be assigned to the index fingers. Buttons on the end work best in games that previously seemed inconvenient. Gaming performance is just a buzz, but either temperature and frequency sensors do not work correctly, or there is a catch in the operating modes.
