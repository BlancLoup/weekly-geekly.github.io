Xiaomi Mi Band 2 officially presented: OLED display, heart rate monitor and new design

Welcome to the pages of the blog iCover ! The family of smart bracelets from Xiaomi finally replenished with a third model, which was called the Xiaomi Mi Band 2 . Information about the novelty today appeared on the official website of the company. The main innovation compared to previous models was a small OLED screen with a diagonal of 0.42 inches.




The screen will display various notifications, as well as activity data for the day. Connecting to a smartphone using a wireless interface Bluetooth 4.0 LE.

The bracelet is compatible with smartphones running Android 4.4 and above and with devices running on iOS 7.0 and above. As in the model Xiaomi Mi Band 1S , in the novelty there is a heart rate monitor.



Xiaomi Mi Band 2 can monitor activity, heart rate, count calories, steps, distance, and track sleep. The pedometer of the novelty is improved - the manufacturer promises to significantly improve the accuracy of counting steps.

The design of the strap in Xiaomi Mi Band 2 has undergone changes - now it is adjustable from 155 to 210 mm, and the total length is 235 mm. According to the manufacturer, the main unit is held in a strap even more reliably.



The dimensions of the main unit with electronics are 40.3 x 15.7 x 10.5 mm, and its weight is only 7 grams. It has a touch-sensitive button, and the top panel, under which the screen is located, is now flat. The radial pattern is applied to the button itself.



The bracelet has a built-in battery with a capacity of 70 mAh, and the manufacturer promises up to 20 days of autonomous operation of the device. The bracelet is protected from water and dust in accordance with IP67 protection level - you can wash your hands without any problems and even take a shower.



In addition, the bracelet is able to work in difficult temperature conditions: from +70 to -20 degrees Celsius. In the case of such hypothermia, the battery life will be reduced to 128 hours. Xiaomi Mi Band 2 is able to withstand a fall on a solid surface from a height of 1.2 meters.

Like previous models, Xiaomi Mi Band 2 can be used to unlock the smartphone brought to it - if the bracelet is connected and within Bluetooth range, a password for unlocking is not required.

In addition, Xiaomi Mi Band 2 will learn to remind the user of the need to move during the day - with a long absence of activity, a reminder will be displayed on the screen. Mi Fit program will receive an update and learn how to display summary statistics - for the day, week or month.



Xiaomi Mi Band 2 bracelets will be available in one of four colors - in black, orange, green and blue, at the user's choice. Sales Xiaomi Mi Band 2 in China will start on June 7. The cost of the bracelet in China will be 149 yuan - about $ 23, which is slightly higher than the starting cost of the model Xiaomi Mi Band 1S.
