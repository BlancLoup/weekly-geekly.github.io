DrToller.com - Social Search Engine

For quite a long time I had an idea about implementing a social search engine with the ability to find people who are looking for the same thing as me. Yes, all somehow did not reach the hands. But given the principle of Donald Trump that "There is nothing more criminal for financial well-being than to come up with a great idea and not bother to implement it" we decided to do at least something. By the time it took a week and that's what happened .

Where do we get information from?
We parse the first three pages of search results of eight search engines: altavista.com , aport.ru , gogo.ru , google.com , msn.com , rambler.ru , yahoo.com , yandex.ru .

How does the information merge?
In our output, the result is the higher, the more search engines it is present. On top of that, a registered user can vote for a ‚Äúgood‚Äù result and then the rating will be higher.

Why register?
So that your requests are saved in the database so that you can find like-minded people who are looking for the same thing and chat with them.

What is our social service?
User can be added as a friend. And then there is a tape search friends. The user can also write a personal message.

And if I don't want someone to know what I'm looking for?
Each request has three levels of privacy: visible to everyone (i.e., everyone sees that I was looking for IT), is visible only to friends and is not visible to anyone. Well, your request can be erased from the history of their requests.

Why do you need a request page?
Under each request, you can leave a comment. We hope that users will keep links to resources that were not included in our issue. By the way, there is an RSS subscription to comments.

Why is everything so slow?
We apologize to users. We do not have a dedicated server. If the service will be popular, then of course we will get our own server and maybe not even one;)

Why then re-search is performed faster?
The results are stored in the database and then we do not turn to search engines.
