Open beta test MechWarrior: Living Legends

After more than 3 years of blood, sweat and beer, luck and disappointment, lack of sleep and problems with personal hygiene, it is finally time for MechWarrior: Living Legends to finally reach the finish line. Yes, the guys from Wandering Samurai are pleased to introduce MechWarrior: Living Legends , and want to invite everyone to participate in the beta test.
What is Living Legends , and how does it relate to the Battletech universe? This is a large-scale modification of Crysis, created in the Battletech universe. The developers offer us to fight on the battlefield with the participation of 16 players for both sides. Each player can control one of 28 unique battle furs, 4 unique tanks, 6 original ground combat vehicles, and eight different types of flying machines.
