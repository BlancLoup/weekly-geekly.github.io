How to turn landing into profit?



One of the levers of creating an effective advertising campaign is your landing page. After the user clicks on the advertisement, a landing page enters the business, the main task of which is to convert the interested audience into the buyers of your goods. The financial benefit directly depends on the conversion rate on the page. But what are the typical mistakes of marketers? Today we will try to figure it out.

They use spyware, copy-paste information from landing pages they like and freeze in anticipation of high conversion. But the miracle does not happen. Why?

They do not know about the components of a successful landing page and do not use split testing when developing their own landing pages. They do not interact with the audience at the very beginning, so the results are appropriate. Copy others - this is the last thing you can do for the company. As a rule, this approach does not lead to success, even if the copy object is on the list of top marketing solutions.

So let's start in order.
1. Value of the offer

The most important criterion. Here you should answer the question that you offer to the consumer. To do this, build the scheme:

Value - The advantages of your offer - The cost of obtaining such an offer

Moreover, the cost of receiving includes not only the price of the goods or services, but also the efforts that should be expended to obtain them. Psychologists consider two substances as motivation for something: movement towards a personal goal and avoiding problems and unpleasant moments. As a rule, painkillers (which get rid of trouble in the form of physical pain), people buy more often than vitamins to maintain immunity.

2. Relevance

Your advertisements and landings should be designed so that their message fully conveys the content of the final product and the user does not have any additional questions about the relevance of its acquisition. By clicking on the ad, the user must receive what attracted him initially.

3. Clarity

Landing pages should be clear, without unnecessary elements that distract from the main content. Ornate design may look better, but if due to it the attention of users will dissipate, there will be no benefit from it. It is important to remember the original task of the landing - sale.

More visible call to action

Absolutely every element on your page should perform the task of increasing conversion . Objects that do not meet this requirement, we remove without regret. They will not help us.
