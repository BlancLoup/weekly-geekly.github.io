Yota Egg enters the market

Yota announces the launch of a new network access device - Yota Egg.

This is a mobile access point that connects to the Yota high-speed wireless 4G network and distributes the Internet via Wi-Fi. With the help of Yota Egg, any device with Wi-Fi-access can be connected to the Internet, regardless of the operating system. Laptops, communicators, players, camcorders, game consoles, and so on - to connect any of these devices to the Internet or several, just turn on the Yota Egg in the Yota network coverage area.
Easy, convenient, self-powered, Yota Egg will help you instantly create an Internet zone where you need it - in the office, in the auditorium of the institute, in the car, in the park - anywhere, anytime.

The price of a new device is 5,990 rubles. It can already be ordered today at the Yota online store and from October 7 to buy at the Sales and Customer Care Centers , mini-sales offices , as well as in re: Store and Z-Store networks.

For private users to connect to the Internet using Yota Egg will operate unlimited tariff Yota Mini - 900 rubles per month.
For more information, please read the devices.

Yota Egg has added an extensive line of devices access to Yota. It includes USB modems, Express-cards, several dozen laptop models with a built-in Mobile WiMAX module, the world's first GSM + Mobile WiMAX HTC MAX 4G phone and Mobile WiMAX / Wi-Fi routers.
