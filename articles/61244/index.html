N97 sales will begin in June



Nokia N97 flagship smartphone will go on sale this month at about $ 776 (this is the price in the US without a contract, in Russia it should be about the same). Sales will begin simultaneously in more than 75 countries around the world, Nokia said today.

As you know, this new product boasts a huge 3.5-inch touchscreen, a full QWERTY keyboard and the ability to fully customize the main screen to your taste (for the first time on Nokia phones) using any widgets, for example, showing current currency quotes, weather, and the latest or updates from the social network. Plus, 32 GB of memory, GPS and a 5 megapixel video camera, shooting at 30 fps.

Naturally, the device will be sharpened by instant access to the online catalog of programs Ovi Store . There are a lot of applications for Twitter and anything else.
