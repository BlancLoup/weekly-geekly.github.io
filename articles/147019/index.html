Hello! Here we told you about the UX32VD model with a matt IPS FullHD 1920x1080 display, a discrete NVIDIA GeForce GT 620M graphics card and a third-generation Intel Core processor. If you are interested in the device, then the next news will definitely be to your taste. July 5 and 6, 2012 in all ASUS brand stores in Moscow when you buy any ASUS ZENBOOK Ultrabook based on a second-generation or third-generation Intel Core processor, you will receive as a gift a 500 Zb ASUS Zendisk AS400 hard drive (the one mentioned in the previous post) ).

However, this is not all. On July 5, there will be a raffle and you will have a chance to take away ASUS ZENBOOK Prime UX21A or other gifts. Stop, you still do not know about the UX21A? Next - a little more about the new ultrabooks and the conditions of the action.


July 5 in the ASUS Market shop at: Moscow, st. The trade union d 7/12 from 20:00 to 21:00 will be a competition among visitors, in which it will be necessary to correctly answer questions about the company and the products of ASUS and Intel. Each visitor who correctly answered one of the questions will receive one of the prizes as a gift: wireless keyboard sets with mice, wireless mice, bags, ASUS backpacks or the main prize - an ultrabook.

In addition, every visitor who buys a laptop from the listed models of the ASUS ZENBOOK Ultrabook based on the Intel Core processor of the second or third generation also gets the opportunity to receive one of the above prizes (including the ultrabook) as a gift without participating in the competition.

July 5 and 6, 2012 in all ASUS brand stores in Moscow when buying any laptop of the listed ZENBOOK models (UX21E, UX31E, UX21A, UX31A, UX32A, UX32Vd) based on Intel Core processor of the second or third generation, each customer receives a hard gift drive Zendisk AS400 500 Gb.

Now about the main prize. The UX21A has a display of 11.6 inches, a discrete graphics card replaces the Intel HD Graphics 4000, the screen finish is matte, and the resolution is 1920 x 1080 pixels. However, there are configurations with a resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels. 256 GB SSD (or 128 GB - depending on the configuration). Weight 1.1 kg. Battery life is about 5 hours.

In general, ASUS has now announced five new products of the ZENBOOK series. 11-inch UX21A and UX21A Touch, 13-inch UX31A, UX32A and UX32VD. However, we will tell about all the differences another time, but be sure, with this choice, many will be able to choose the ultrabook exactly for their needs.
