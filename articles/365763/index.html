Amazon is preparing for a giant sale and wants to overshadow Black Friday

Recently, the world's largest online hypermarket Amazon announced the largest sale in its history - Prime Day . The giant of online retail has set an ambitious goal - to eclipse the traditional autumn discount-promotional madness called " Black Friday ." In addition, several lucky ones (one from each country where the action is valid) will receive a prize of $ 10,000 in the form of an Amazon gift card.



To get access to special sales offers of Amazon and get into the number of participants of the draw, you need to fulfill only two conditions:
1) become or already be a subscriber to Amazon Prime until July 15, 2015;
2) on the draw page, upload a photo of the best moment in your life and confirm participation.

Be sure to make a purchase is not required. Moreover, if you have never used a Prime subscription, or it was a long time ago, then you can activate the trial 30-day period of this subscription completely free of charge (and opt out for free if you don‚Äôt need it anymore) on a special page .

* free access to the library of more than 500,000 books;
