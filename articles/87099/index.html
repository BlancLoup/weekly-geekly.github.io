New service from Google, alternative to Google Reader

Google a couple of hours ago launched an alternative version of Google Reader, which is called Google Reader Play.





This is an experimental Google Labs product. Now, each news item is displayed on a black background, almost full screen, and to view the next one, you need to press the arrow. Some kind of photo gallery.



And, as they say in the official blog , this is not a replacement for the usual Google Reader, but just 4 fun service =)

UPD: Since the service is new, in order to try it out, you need to select English in the Google Reader settings. Or in the address bar to add ? Hl = en Thanks Kyller and switchON for tips =)

UPD 2: By default, it is configured to view the recommended news. To view your subscriptions, you need to click on the newsletter in the reader, in the address bar you will get something like ... reader / view / # stream / feed .. copy from # to the end of the line, and then add this parameter to www. google.com/reader/play . Or simply by clicking on the newsletter, in the address bar, change the view to play . tnx ghisguth
