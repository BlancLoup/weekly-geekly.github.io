Mp3spy.ru offers download mp3 days

Online digital music store mp3spy.ru
announces the launch of the campaign "Boundless Hunt" . According to the company, participation in it gives the right to download music with time-based payment. The cost of access to unlimited file downloads for 24 hours is 1 USD. The action will last until December 31, 2006.

Recall that this summer mp3spy.ru was going to become the property of one of the western recording companies. Apparently, these plans were not destined to come true, because the prices on the site are still lower than the European average.
