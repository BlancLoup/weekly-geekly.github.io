September 30 is the last day for submitting applications for participation in the Runet Prize 2007 competition.

The Runet Award 2007 competition, founded by the Federal Agency for Press and Mass Communications, is the main competition of the Russian Internet, where the award is given for contributions to the development of the Russian Internet segment.

Leading organizations and projects that have made a significant contribution to the development of the Runet in the past and the current year will be evaluated as part of the award, and special requirements will be presented to them in this connection.

The prize is awarded in six main and one special nominations:
