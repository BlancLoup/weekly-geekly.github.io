- How is Xperia Z different from ZL?

First and foremost: both models have absolutely identical fillings, including the same 5-inch display, 4-core processor and a 13-megapixel camera with support for photo and video in HDR.

The Xperia Z is stylish and slim. Its back surface is completely flat and made of glass. Case thickness - only 7.9 millimeters. At the same time, it is also waterproof.

- Why is there no wireless charging?

Because it is not needed. Do not be indignant, just read further (: The existing wireless chargers on the market are in fact not fully wireless: anyway, there is a charging station that, in turn, is tied to an outlet. It's as if the wi-fi router was distributing the Internet, only if you put a computer on it. All convenience comes down to not picking up the phone with a charging cord each time. Sony deliberately refused to use wireless charging, because the induction coil required for it makes the case heavier and increases its size, compensation in the standard package Xperia Z will go docking station, in which the phone is placed on the charging of just one movement.

- Why is the power button placed in the center?

There are two reasons: first, it is beautiful . The design of both devices is designed in the new style of Omni Balance, which implies maximum symmetry.

The power button lowered to the middle makes it accessible for pressing without interceptions even by the smallest palm.
