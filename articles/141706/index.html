The free and free Cuprum font is now available in four styles (regular, italic, bold, bold italic)

Fans of Cuprum have come to rejoice: its author, a talented and prolific designer and type designer Ivan Gladkikh , better known as Giovanni Lemonad, today released a revised and greatly augmented version of his brainchild. Instead of one former mark, Cuprum is now endowed with four!



Cuprum font is available for download and free distribution under the SIL OFL 1.1 license ; it is also available on the Google Web Fonts website , which allows (as I have previously reported ) to significantly speed up its shipment to the reader in cases when not all the font is needed, but only a certain subset.
