ASP.NET Day: Announcement of Reports

Friends, C # developers and ASP.NET web programmers!

On February 28, 2015, a conference will be held that is unique in the concentration of specialized information for ASP.NET developers.

This conference is Go # Moscow - ASP.NET Day . All speakers have extensive experience in developing web applications on the .NET platform, and some are holders of the status of Microsoft MVP and Microsoft Regional Director.

8 reports cover topics:
  • future ASP.NET
  • ASP.NET Internals
  • web application performance monitoring
  • architecture of ASP.NET applications
  • client-side

ASP.NET Day is a great opportunity to raise your professional level, to communicate with colleagues in the workshop, as well as with speakers from leading technology companies.




Go # format remains unchanged and will provide maximum professional communication. This is one track from short reports, a lot of coffee breaks and several lunch breaks, which are already included in the ticket price.

As you may have guessed, all sections of the conference are devoted to ASP.NET:
