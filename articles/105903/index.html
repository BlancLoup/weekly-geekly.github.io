Do not miss the presentation of the first devices on Windows Phone 7 (today!)



Today, October 11, in New York, Microsoft will hold a press conference at which the company, together with its partners, will present the first devices based on Windows Phone 7.

The beginning of the press conference is scheduled for 6:30 am PDT (17:30 Moscow time). The whole event, as usual with Microsoft, will be broadcast on the Internet live on this address http://www.microsoft.com/presspass/default.mspx .

It is possible that during the press conference, Microsoft will talk about its future plans for the development of the mobile direction.
