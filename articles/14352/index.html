HeadHunter and Digital Sky Technologies bought at 20% Free-lance.ru

HeadHunter and the direct investment fund Digital Sky Technologies each acquired a 20% stake in the Free-lance.ru project, a leader in remote work services.
Negotiations between the partners were quite long, and as a result an agreement on mutually beneficial cooperation was signed. The personnel portal HeadHunter and the Digital Sky Technologies foundation saw the great potential of the Free-lance.ru resource and decided to invest in its further development.

HeadHunter (hh.ru) has been working in the online recruitment market since 2000. The mission of the company is to provide HR managers and recruiters with convenient tools to search for personnel and assist applicants in finding decent work. More than 40 thousand companies are customers of HeadHunter, more than 1 million resumes and 60 thousand vacancies are presented on hh.ru at a time. HeadHunter offices operate in 11 regions of the Russian Federation, as well as in Ukraine and Kazakhstan. In 2007, HeadHunter joined The Network, a global network of recruiters.

Free-lance.ru is a professional resource created in 2005 to search for a remote performer (freelancer) for a specific type of work. For a fairly short period of existence, Free-lance.ru has become the leading resource for remote work in RuNet. Today, the site provides orders for thousands of web developers, designers, programmers, managers and copywriters. More than 3000 remote work offers appear on the portal every month.
