Andy Grove proposes e-health reforms

Andy Grove, one of the founders and former president of Intel, launched an initiative to reform the US healthcare system.

In his opinion, recording patient information, as well as their case histories on portable microchips, will help reduce costs (for example, on the same paper), and also facilitate the work of doctors. In addition, thanks to the system offered to them, many patients will be able to receive help at home, interacting with doctors through the Internet.

According to Andy Grove, the health care system in its current form is very inefficient.
