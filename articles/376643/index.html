Endless Eyewear: a new service that allows you to change sunglasses and optical glasses every month



There are a lot of different kinds of services and projects now, and very original ones are found among them. There is, for example, a service that sends several pairs of new socks to a subscriber per month for a certain monthly fee. Thus, a busy person may not worry about running out of socks. But it‚Äôs about another service - Endless Eyewear , developed and launched by Ditto. This startup allows you to change glasses (both optical and sun glasses) almost monthly, for a relatively small fee.

The service itself offers not some Chinese know-how products there, but points from quite well-known companies, including Ray-Ban, Oakley, and Burberry. In total, the company works with more than 50 different brands. And in the subscription, you can add and insurance against scratches and scuffs.

The company has two subscription options - one for sunglasses, the second for optical ones. You can pick up glasses using a special technology 3D-fitting. Using the camera, the user can create a three-dimensional image of his face, and then watch which glasses go and which do not. The first pair of glasses you need to buy, and then you can change for $ 19 / month for sunglasses and $ 29 / month for optical glasses (lenses included).

If you are tired of glasses, or it seems to you that you made a wrong choice - you can change it without any problems. The main thing is to avoid cracks and other problems. Well, the small scuffs are covered by insurance, there is no problem here.

By the way, the company received about 5 million US dollars as investments, and now it is going to conquer the world.
