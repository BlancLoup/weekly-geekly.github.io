Hyperloop got the first contract



The company Hyperloop Transportation Technologies, which is promoting the revolutionary public transport system Hyperloop from Ilona Mask, plans to start laying the first commercial route next year.

Hyperpetul 8 km long will be built in central California. Of course, at this distance, passenger capsules will not be able to accelerate to the maximum stipulated speed of 1220 km / h , but this is only the beginning.

Publishing technical specifications in August 2013, Ilon Musk said: ‚ÄúThis is an open project, and anyone can build it. Neither I nor SpaceX will patent Hyperloop. All specifications will be available freely. If a few years later no one succeeds in creating a hyperloop, perhaps I will return to the project. ‚Äù He added that Hyperloop is "unique in its concept of open design like Linux."

The project provides that the capsules with passengers move in the thin air of the tunnel. The project indicated a working pressure in the tunnel of 100 Pa; this is practically a vacuum, as in the upper stratosphere. The acceleration of the capsules is carried out using magnetic accelerators (stators) in the tunnel and the rotors on each capsule. The distance between them in 20 mm is maintained due to the electromagnetic field.

The first loop will be built on an empty area approximately in the middle between San Francisco and Los Angeles. Here in the future it is planned to build from scratch a new city Quay Valley for 150,000 inhabitants. "City of the Future" will fully provide energy to solar-powered power plants. Hyperlove will organically complement the picture of the futuristic settlement.

For the construction of Hyperloop Transportation Technologies plans to raise $ 100 million, releasing shares in the III quarter. 2015

A little hyperloop in Quay Valley is not an experiment for running technologies, but a real project that the developers intend to launch into commercial operation. Everyone can come here, buy a ticket and ride on the transport of the future.

Hyperloop Transportation Technologies employs 200 professional engineers from NASA, Boeing, Airbus and other companies. They all work for free, in their free time, sharing the options and the future profit of the corporation in case of success.
