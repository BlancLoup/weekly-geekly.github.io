Locality

If the information is structured, then it has great potential. In the case of Yolki, this filter became the territory where communication takes place. On the wall, messages are poorly structured into threads, while rooms are an example of thematic communication. Creating rooms is the only paid service that is a privilege, since free can not be due to the fact that everyone in this scenario wants to create an unlimited number of them and there will be a lot of territory without communication. Anonymity

This principle refers to the strong, but not fundamental characteristics of the application. In Yolki there is no registration and authorization. In one click after installing the application, you are immersed in communication.

At the first visit, each user has an anonymous profile, then he can create additional ones with distinctive characteristics. To keep the secret of his identity or not - his right.


Multiple profiles allow you to quickly reincarnate when changing your social circle or use this opportunity for gaming purposes - to speak simultaneously on behalf of a girl and a man, for example.

The main target audience of the application are: students and schoolchildren, as well as office staff. Potential territory for communication are any places where there is a high concentration of people.
