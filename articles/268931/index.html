5 internships for programmers abroad



After a long search for cool opportunities, we have identified the most profitable not only in gaining experience, but also financially internships. In addition, offices of world famous companies are located in major US cities, which will allow you to enjoy not only training, but also the whole environment


HNI Corporation

The HNI Corporation group of companies represents the most well-known office furniture brands involved in home furnishing. Geographically, the corporation is located in the district of Muscatine, USA. The company provides an opportunity to gain experience in a number of disciplines in the field of IT-technologies. Namely: application development, IT infrastructure development, business analysis, database administration and much more. The candidate must be a college graduate or an MBA with a specialization in information technology; with an average score of at least 3.8 out of 5 (GPA), have communication skills, be able to work in a team, take the initiative in different situations. Deadline for submission of applications is not specified.

More information at hnicareers.com/Article.aspx?ID=40

Intel Corporation

Known worldwide company Intel Corporation, which equips the world with electronic devices and various computer components, invites the intern to the position of system programmer. The internship internship will be held in Phoenix, Arizona, USA. It is assumed to gain experience in the field of information technology, namely: system administration, application development, monitoring, updating, testing, troubleshooting; implementation of system transformations, updating applications, their reliability and security. The candidate must be an IT student; Any work experience is possible, perhaps an internship already completed elsewhere, research done. The candidate must have an understanding of network programming, computer communication, operating systems, data structure and programming in general. The internship is temporary, further employment is possible. The deadline for submission of applications is September 18, admission ends December 17, 2015.

More information at www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/homepage.html

iConsultUS INC.

iConsultUS INC. carries out a full cycle of software development, provides services in this area. Provides users with high quality web applications, is one of the best software developers. Develop applications using Java, J2EE platforms. Geographically located in Chicago, Illinois, USA. The company is looking for a Java developer in Los Angeles, California, USA. It is proposed to gain experience in the field of information technology, namely: web development, the creation of mobile versions of websites, supervising several projects simultaneously. This should be a talented person who is willing and able to join a dynamic team of IT specialists. In case of need training, it is paid by the company. The candidate must understand HTML, CSS, and web standards, as well as JavaScript and AJAX, website design. Applications are accepted all year round. The contact number is 630-506-6037 or you can send your resume to the address: rkasyap@iconsultus.com.

More information at www.iconsultus.com/net-developer

App Code Source, LLC

App Code Source, LLC is a company that develops entertainment mobile applications for Apple iPhone, iPod and iPad, for Mac, Android and Amazon devices, the main focus is on iOS and Mac OS X. Also, applications are being developed concerning electronic music, music tracks, Ringtones, radio players and recorders, viewing news. Geographically, the company is located in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA. Internship is offered on good conditions, namely: flexible working hours, getting a loan after completion of the internship. You can also get free apps, promo codes and other benefits while working. Your responsibilities will include: creating, editing and updating applications, learning how to create applications, testing them, distributing to iTunes and Mac, troubleshooting. If you are not a student or graduate in the field of information technology, but have the experience and skills to develop applications, you should send a request. The minimum requirements for the candidate: knowledge of the basics of developing applications for the iPhone, iPod, iPad or Mac, knowledge of the xcode development environment and objective-C, understanding the use of audio, ability to work in a team. The following knowledge will be your advantage: links to samples of your work, development of an application for Mac OS x from the elementary level to the highest. Development of applications for Amazon Kindle or other Android platforms - in Java, in Eclipse; knowledge of HTML5, Adobe Photoshop, sound editing. If your candidature is approved, you will be sent more detailed information about the location of the office. Next you will be required to sign a non-disclosure agreement from the beginning of your internship. Deadline for submitting applications all year round.

More information at www.internships.com/it/las-vegas-application-developer-internship-program-iphone-ipod-touch-ipad-mac-apple-watch

Barracuda Networks

If we talk about the security of your PC and all associated ones, then it is implemented by Barracuda Networks. It provides not only the security of networks, but also the storage of resources based on network devices and cloud storage. The company develops programs to protect email, web surfing from hackers, viruses, spyware and other types of threats. Security is carried out at the highest level, namely: a web traffic filter, application delivery controllers, message archiving, backup services and data protection. Geographically, the company is located in Campbell, California, USA. Looking for an intern who will perform tasks on building software modules that the company uses to protect devices in their cloud infrastructure. Also, work on the integration of data traffic from various components, the implementation of e-mail security in the most important cloud services. Candidate requirements: ability to work with Java, C, C ++ and Lua, an understanding of the Linux system, knowledge of the shell script and the perl language. Applications are accepted all year round.
