UPD: Outcome.
The state of automation in domestic health is deplorable. Not all institutions have computers. The software used is fragmented. As a rule, only reference information is automated. The level of computer training of staff leaves much to be desired.
However, Russia is full of talents. There are very good, at first glance, development with examples of implementation.
Medical information system "Medialog" . Implemented in dozens of institutions in Russia, Ukraine and Kazakhstan.
TrustMed software package . Several dozen implementations.
Evrika Company (St. Petersburg) has a medical IC.
Perm uses the online appointment service for a doctor .
There is even a complex implemented in the medical institutions of Denmark .
At the same time, judging by the article in PC Week , medical information and analysis centers (MIAC) have been created in all regions, which keep their programmers on staff and implement their own developments (from the article, in the Vladimir region this is Medbase).
Conclusion: with the necessary attention from the state, the infusion of the necessary funds, the conduct of a competent tender, I consider the task of finding the optimal software system for scaling to the federal level feasible. We have someone to work with, gentlemen!
