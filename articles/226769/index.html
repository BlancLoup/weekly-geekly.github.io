4 Explore the scope!

Such an option is to get good at something very well, to find out the area from the inside and on the basis of this, ideas for this application will arise.

Example:

Or another option is a robotic agronomist who flies over a hundred hectares of wheat fields per day and takes measurements of soil indicators - humidity, pH, etc. Based on this, fine-tuning is made for irrigation canals in order not to swamp areas of the field and prevent water overspending.
