Beta Adobe Flash Player 10.1 with hardware video acceleration released

Habravchane, it happened!



Adobe has finally been honored to release a beta version of the already announced Flash Player 10.1, about which I have already provided to the habrasoobshchestvu . It is recommended to test this player on ion netbooks and nettops in order to get a noticeable effect to the naked eye.



As always, it was not without limitations. Since this is a test build, it works correctly and effectively only with special drivers from nVidia version 195.50 and higher. You can download this magic version of the drivers in a clever way from the official ftp-server, but if the load on the private ftp is spoiled, I am afraid that I will fly for it ...

Download here:
  • www.nvidia.ru/object/win7_winvista_32bit_195.55_en.html
  • www.nvidia.ru/object/win7_winvista_64bit_195.55_en.html


Unfortunately, at the moment I have no ion devices left in my hands, so the owners of Revo or similar devices - describe the work of Youtube HD in the comments, if not difficult.
