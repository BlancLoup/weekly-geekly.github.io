CROS - 2017: the second call to Sochi



Already today, an event of undoubtedly significant for domestic telecom is starting - the Annual Conference of Telecom Operators, which will be held in Krasnaya Polyana for the second consecutive year in Sochi. Like all previous years, this again broke records in the number of participants. Under the cut a story about how it all began and how much CROS became a landmark event for the industry. Come to anyone interested.

In the middle of the 2000s, the Russian telecom was a kind of foundation for a multi-storey building, where it was necessary to build more labyrinths of floors and communications. The vast expanses of our country did not allow all the enthusiasts of the telecom to get to know each other. Several people led by Pavel Nagibin came to the aid of the industry, deciding to gather in one place telecommunications workers from all over the country in order to communicate in a relaxed atmosphere. This is how the Conference of Russian Telecommunications Operators (CROS) appeared.

The geography of the first conference was wide. Representatives of 55 providers from 42 cities of Russia arrived at the recreation center near Ekaterinburg. Ten years ago, telecom in Russia looked radically different from what it is now, but the development of the industry went at a rapid pace.

Among those present at the first CROS there were people who were still only carrying out plans for the construction and development of communication networks. All that they lacked was the knowledge base, and they got it.

Report part


The most tasty thing on KROS is not kebabs and beer unlimited, but the report part of it. Each year, the topics of the reports are like a slice of the problems that are of interest and concern to the industry. Today's Day


It is incredibly difficult to put in a certain framework a story about what happened to CROS in these more than a dozen years. I would have had to write a book, and in several volumes, because there is a lot of this that is worth and I would like to mention.

CROS is a unique event not so much in scale as due to that unique internal symbiosis where the business part harmoniously intertwines with warm friendly communication. According to the participants themselves, not a single industry conference can boast of such an atmosphere.





Russian telecom is developing in parallel with the development of communication technologies. Network coverage is growing, operators are expanding, the Internet is becoming available to almost everyone. All this cannot but reflect on such a landmark event for the industry as CROS.
