For those who already have the tools (like me) this is certainly not an option (although ...), but if you currently want to buy a guitar or put off this purchase due to lack of space in the apartment, noise, this text will be very useful to you.

In the world there are four companies that produce such instruments: Yamaha, Traveler Guitar, Warr Guitars, and Koopal. Being in Russia, the easiest way is to purchase a tool from Yamaha, which will be discussed further ...


In the model range Yamaha three guitars of this type:
  • Yamaha SLG 100S
  • Yamaha SLG 100N
  • Yamaha SLG 120NW

The difference between the first two - strings (S - steel, N - nylon), the head of the neck and the presence of the lining (it is in 100S). The third guitar is a live version of the model 100N, we will not dwell on it in detail.



The neck of the guitar is made of mahogany, the body of maple, which, as you know, resonates well (in this case it is quite important, given its size). Shell (contours) are made of plastic. It is worth noting that the top can be easily removed for transportation or storage, the truncated cover comes complete with the guitar.



Musician is available to adjust the volume, bass and treble. Also in the guitar there is a built-in reverb with three positions of the knob (off / 1/2).



The output signal can be sent to the monitors or to the headphones (separate output), which are included. When playing through headphones, the above-mentioned reverb and equalizer will help to achieve excellent sound. Guitar pickups with a preamp. Powered by batteries or from the network.



Guitars also have an aux in input. Through it you can connect the player with a backing or with a shock section. The volume of the input is adjustable.
