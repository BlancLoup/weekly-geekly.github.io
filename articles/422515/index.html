September 20 (wherever there is internet)
Webinar Approaches to horizontal scaling of databases: sharding, shared nothing


The webinar is devoted to the features of distributed systems associated with sharding as a method of providing horizontal scaling. As part of the webinar, Artem Shitov, GridGain Solutions Engineer, will talk about:

  • Differences in shared everything from shared nothing
  • Approaches to sharding and its nuances
  • Opportunities for collocation of disparate data
  • Shredding shards, etc.

The webinar will be of interest to architects and developers who plan to use horizontal scaling technology in a business environment, and choose an open-source solution for this.

Registration is required.
