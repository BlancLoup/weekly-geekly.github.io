The touch control has its pros and cons, in general. But you quickly adapt to it - several scenarios:

Call

  • Only one earphone is active during a call.
  • With an incoming call - a short press takes the call, rejects a long
  • Double click to dial the last number
  • During a call - short press disconnects

Music

  • Short press - play / pause
  • Hold for a second - new track
  • Double tap - volume up

That is, for example, by pushing the earpiece carelessly into your ear, you can pause the playback.
