Google is not satisfied with Google Music yet.



Recently it became known that Google is not particularly pleased with the development of its music service Google Music. There were no direct publications from the management of the company, but Google partners received such information from the employees of the Corporation of Good. The company is very unhappy with the fact that the service has not yet become popular at the proper level.

The Google Music service itself was launched relatively recently, and there were no particularly high-profile statements by the corporation about this. At the beginning of the service‚Äôs existence, geeks and fans of everything new began to test it, but after a while, the number of people willing to participate in checking the service‚Äôs performance decreased. As a result, neither attendance nor income reached the level expected by the company. The number of users (or rather, accounts) registered with Google Music is quite large, but there are not as many active users as the developers and management would like.

Currently, it is possible to work with the service from a laptop or computer, or from a mobile device thanks to an Android application. In general, this service has no particular differences compared to other companies' analogues. Perhaps that is why Google Music has not become so popular.

However, the service works only three months, which is not so much. It may well be that over time, Google Music will still gain popularity. And if the development team comes up with some kind of "highlight" for this project, everything will work out. Now the service API is being finalized, in connection with which it is possible to predict an increase in the number of active users of this project.
