OfficeTalk - Microsoft's microblogging for business

Office Labs is preparing its next project - OfficeTalk . In the future, this product will be positioned as a business solution for companies that want to deploy a microblogging service in their corporate environment.



Obviously, OfficeTalk is inspired by the success of Twitter and copies its main successful ideas: followers, the small size of the message, the built-in abbreviation of links, tags and user addressing via @. However, there is a difference: support comments for messages. In addition, it is obvious that the future service will be tightly integrated with other Microsoft solutions, such as Office, SharePoint, Active Directory and others.

In general, it looks like a completely interesting and complete product that will dilute the package of business applications for Microsoft corporate clients with another service.
