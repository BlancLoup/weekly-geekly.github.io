Logo marathon

Greetings. We had a crazy idea) To conduct a 100 days long Logo-Marathon - 100 logos. 100 days is not a fur coat in your pants), it is really hard. But what is the challenge?

The essence of the idea is as follows: the participants of our site publish their applications, the designer (you) choose the one you like and proceed to execution, agree that the creation process should not take more than 3 hours in total.

At 12 o'clock in the afternoon you choose work at 10 am the next day we publish the result and at 12 o'clock in the afternoon you choose the application again.

No additions or corrections are accepted!

It will be great if you also share your drafts, work flow, ideas and thoughts.

Benefit?

You experience, fame, dollars. Experience - I think if you go through this marathon at a good level, or even a simple one, one of the actions you can be proud of for a long time ...

Fame - we will publish a release of the conduct and the results of the marathon on popular sites of the Runet: habr (!), Demiart, dirty, livejournal, lepra.

Dollars - many participants in order to increase the percentage of choice of their application, in a competitive environment will begin to set the price they are willing to pay for the work. Well, for our part, at the finish line, we will reward our hero with an example of the remarkable work of Apple designers.
