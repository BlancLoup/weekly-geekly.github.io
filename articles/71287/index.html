Anlim back up for $ 5 per month

Internet backup services are not new. There are quite a few paid (for example, Carbonite , Memeo or Cuku ), and you can make a free analog yourself, at least based on Gmail. But almost all such services have restrictions on the amount of data transferred. There are very few real anlims on the market.

Kompniya McAfee since yesterday launched just such anlim. For a fixed amount of $ 60 per year ($ 5 per month) you get a backup channel with unlimited traffic. The McAfee Online Backup program is installed on the computer with very advanced settings, through which automatic backup is performed.



The software was created with the help of Mozy , which has long been engaged in the development of such software.

What is interesting, after installation, the program itself selects the folders and files that it wants to send to the remote server (contacts, documents, private data, bookmarks from the browser, photos, email archive, etc.). Of course, you can change the list of files for backup.
