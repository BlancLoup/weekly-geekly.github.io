About feedback


Despite the fact that the subject is clear to everyone and seemingly simple, I want to touch on him in my first post.

We all live in a world where either any answer follows any action we take or no answer follows. If there is no answer, we feel that something is wrong. Perhaps some do not feel discomfort from the lack of feedback. When designing any system, program, device or anything else - feedback is used for its calibration and correct operation.

But what if the feedback is broken? If there is interference in the signal flow? If there is no feedback at all (everything is gone broke)?

In this situation, we do not know what the customer or subordinate thinks about us. The man does not know whether he is hot or cold. The spacecraft, plowing the expanses of the large theater of space, loses its connection with the MCC. Everything is falling apart ...

There are two types of feedback: positive and negative. Positive feedback is a type of communication, where a change in the output signal of the system leads to a change in the input signal, which further deviates the output signal from the original value. Those. it is an explosion or collapse. But in any case, this is a clearly understandable ending and end of the existence of the system / process / society. Negative feedback is a type of communication in which the output signal of the system is transmitted back to the input to cancel part of the input signal. Negative feedback makes the system more resistant to random parameter changes. Those. it is a roly-poly, self-regulating, having a certain standard for correction.

If we look from the point of view of processes and management, then all those processes that diverge into distant distances, or vice versa - collapse to zero (well, we designed them like this) - these are processes with positive feedback. And vice versa - correctly designed processes that allow the system to function the necessary time (laid during the design) is a direct example of negative (corrective) feedback. Renovation in the house, when there are always opinions from the outside, changing your original plan - just the same example. A restaurant with missing / disturbed negative feedback will go bust because the quality of its dishes will constantly go farther from the standard (in any direction). A country ruled with broken or no feedback from the people at all is doomed, just like a project or repair that was managed in the same way.

At the same time, repairing or creating from scratch the necessary feedback can often solve 90% of control problems. In projects, this is often communication. In system design - the right design. In society there are all the same communications and processes that can be controlled by society.
