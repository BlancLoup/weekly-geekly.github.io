Learning to write in the information style

At work, I write texts: posts for habr, email-newsletter, articles in the media. I have been writing for a couple of years, the last six months, at least two texts a week. A significant step in the development of my writing skill was the discovery of the information style and advice of Maxim Ilyahov.

The purpose of the text in the information style - to convey information to the reader. Information style: concise, unambiguous, accurate and objective. In the informational texts there are no unnecessary words, emotions, personal opinion. They are read easily and quickly.

Maxim introduces the concept of stop words. Stop words are not typical for texts in the information style and if you met him - think about and rephrase this place. The stop words are ‚Äúby the way‚Äù, interjections, modal verbs, verbal nouns and others, about 100 in total.

At some point, it became difficult for me to search for these words in texts, so I created for myself to help Test The Text . Test The Text highlights words uncharacteristic for the information style and explains why.




Test The Text will help to check and edit your texts or learn to write in the information style. Clicking on the stop word opens a hint why the word is bad and what to do with it.


Information style is not always suitable. If you remove the stop words and emotions from the text, the text will seem too dry, inhuman. Especially on Habr√©, where posts not only convey information, but also the attitude of the author. But, when you understand what bad words are, all your texts will become better and easier to read.

For example, I will edit the post-translation of an open letter BlackBerry . Original text, stop words highlighted:


BlackBerry is having problems. Sales of the flagship smartphone Z10 failed. The company is sold in parts at auction. Doug Fregin, co-founder of the company, tried to buy it incognito. To reassure the few fans of the brand, BlackBerry has published an open letter in 30 newspapers 9 stan.
