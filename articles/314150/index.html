Hackers stole a billion rubles from four banks with the help of a bot

The intelligence services uncovered a group of cybercriminals who had deceived Promsvyazbank , Trust , Uralsib and the credit institution Zenit .

16 people in Moscow organized a cybercrime group and engaged in online fraud. The group began to work with a million rubles, gradually increasing the amount. In just four months, the team robbed financial institutions for a billion rubles. The scam was a success thanks to the special program they created.
