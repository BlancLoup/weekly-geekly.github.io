Show Sound # 6 - Podcast about audio equipment, components, formats and technologies

Hello! With you, "Show Sound" is a podcast for those who like to live, work, relax and move around, listening to your favorite music, podcasts or audio books at home, at work, in the subway or other modes of transport. Starting from the very beginning, we will help you to get acquainted with the world of devices and technologies that can show you the true sound of musical compositions.

In the 6th issue, we answer questions from the audience that we received in a review of this podcast in iTunes. Given the very different nature of the issues, we were able to offer basic approaches to understanding the everyday aspects of this area.

The podcast are conducted by Dmitry Kabanov ( dmitrykabanov ) and Timofey Shikolenkov ( timshick ).
