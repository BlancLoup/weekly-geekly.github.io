RoverPC G6: a modern 3G communicator

In September of this year, sales of the RoverPC G6 3G Communicator will start. This device is powered by a Marvell PXA 270 processor with a frequency of 520 MHz running Windows Mobile 6.0. RoverPC G6 is made in the form of a compact monoblock with a size of 106.5 x 58.5 x 18.5 mm, and combines in design two classic colors: metallic and black. The model boasts a touch 2.7-inch display that displays 262 thousand colors. The amount of RAM is 64 MB, and the built-in flash memory is 256 MB with the possibility of expansion by means of miniSD cards. In September this year, sales of the 3G communicator RoverPC G6 will start. This device is powered by a Marvell PXA 270 processor with a frequency of 520 MHz running Windows Mobile 6.0.

Among the communication capabilities of the RoverPC G6 are support for the EDGE data transfer standard (class 10), a Wi-Fi module, as well as a Bluetooth interface and an infrared port.

Feature RoverPC G6 - support for 3G-networks of the standard WCDMA (UMTS), which will provide mobile access with network services, high speed data transmission in various formats.
Of the other features of the model, it is worth mentioning the built-in 2-megapixel camera, MP3 player, mail client, organizer. The lithium-ion battery lasts for 4 hours of active work and 150 hours in standby mode.
