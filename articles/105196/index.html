StartupIndex project sessions are closed events for Internet and New Media professionals, where presentations of promising start-ups are presented. Ratings can range from D (totally hopeless) to AAA (maximum appeal). Moreover, only estimates ranging from BBB to AAA belong to the investment class, all that is lower are projects with a high degree of risk.
