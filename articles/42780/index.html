The Latvian government has calculated the damage from the Soviet government. Only numbers

I just read a note on Lenta that the Latvian government somehow somehow managed to assess the damage from the Soviet government. And what turned out? It turned out that "direct demographic losses" amount to 10 million man-years.

I wonder how much is it? The article says that this is the income that one person can generate in 10 million years.

Or income that 10 million people can generate in 1 year. At present, the population of Latvia is approximately 2.3 million people. That is, the damage is approximately equal to the total income of the entire population of Latvia over 4.3 years. Although in the USSR Latvia spent 51 years (1940-1991). Considering that the population of Latvia was about the same in the 20th century , it turns out that the Soviet Union damaged Latvia's income by 8.4%.

Or is it the income that residents of Russia will receive in 0.07 years, that is, in 25 and a half days.
