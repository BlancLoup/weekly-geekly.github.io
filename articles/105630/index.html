Widget Yandex.Probki updated to version 1.2

About six months ago, I wrote my first, albeit small, application for Android - Yandex.Traffic Widget . Everything was quite dignified and interesting - I read feedbacks, released a few updates. But later, the time was dramatically less, the work is much more and I no longer returned to it until now.

However, Yandex recently showed their own widget with the same functionality. This was the impetus for today's post.



The main change, as can be seen from the screenshot, was the addition of a large 2x2 widget with a mini map of traffic jams of a selected city. There is nothing better, in the morning, before going to work, unlock the phone and immediately see the general picture of traffic jams in the city. And if you need more details, the Yandex.Maps application still opens the card to the card. Of course, if it is installed.

Errors that occasionally lead to crashes were also fixed.

I failed to solve the error on launching Yandex.Map immediately after adding the widget, so after the installation it is necessary to restart the phone to activate this functionality.
