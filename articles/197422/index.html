Convenient utilities for android

Phone prices are rising, there is no convenience. Convenience of using basic functions, but not mail and maps.
At some point, my patience ran out, and I decided to change that.

Introductory word


I did not know how to write in java, but for more than 4 years I was tired, and now I decided to take on this business myself.

I plan to systematically introduce un-demanding utilities that simplify the daily use of your device.
I myself am a C ++ programmer, and the new rules of an unfamiliar language do not allow me to guarantee the absence of errors and crashes.
Meanwhile, I make every effort not to bring critical situations to users, and to catch it in the laboratory.

Also, brought up by the assembler, I delete all the objects immediately after using them, and at the right moments I call the Garbage Collector manually. I hope this will reduce the memory usage of my applications, and allow your phone to work as fast as ever.

I still have a simple rule - the amount of internal memory occupied should not be more than 200kb. I will try to comply with it.

How did i start


Of course with what I needed most.
An application that automatically throws a homeless beacon to a friend if your call fails.
Unfortunately, there are no common solutions for this problem, so the offer will be shown after any conversation for less than 10 seconds.
Now it is very convenient to ask to call back: it is enough to reset immediately after the start of the call.
