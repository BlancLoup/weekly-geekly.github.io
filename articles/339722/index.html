User activation


The first acquaintance is the most important scenario that should be thought out with the utmost care. You need to make it as simple and clear as possible, because if at this step the user is confused and / or he was unable to solve his problem, at least partially, then he will no longer use your application.

We put ourselves in the user's place and formulate the problem that he wants to solve by going to the application for the first time. Suppose I want to see what offers in the secondary market for the Mercedes-Benz C-class. Let's see what needs to be done to satisfy this request.
