Diablo game] [got the first patch in 6 years



The company-developer of games Blizzard North has released a new patch version 1.14a for its famous game Diablo] [, for the first time since March 2010, when the patch 1.13d was released . The update mainly concerns the correction of errors related to the work of the game in modern operating systems.

In addition, as developers are joking, you can finally get rid of your old Mac PowerPCs. The patch includes a new program for installing the game on OSX. At the same time, programmers promise not to stop at what has been accomplished and to improve the system of countering cheaters.

Apparently, we should expect further improvements of the game - however, it is not clear how many years later the next update will be visible. The game itself will turn 16 in the summer.

Diablo] [- Action / RPG computer game, continued Diablo. It was released by Blizzard Entertainment for Windows and Mac OS in 2000. Developed by Blizzard North. Won many awards and recognition from players around the world. She was honored with a record in the Guinness Book of Records (in the 2000 edition) as the fastest-selling game ever sold with a score of more than 1 million units sold after the first two weeks of sales.

One of the easter eggs in the game is a secret level with cows - there were so many jokes about its existence in the first part of the game that in the second part the developers decided to really make this level accessible .
