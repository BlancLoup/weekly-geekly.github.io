New method for rendering impossible figures

Hong Kong specialist Tai-Pang Wu has developed the most realistic method for rendering impossible figures to date. Perhaps someday in the virtual space of computer games it will be possible to render the stairs in Escher style, and they will look realistic from any angle, as well as with different lighting. An impossible ladder will even roll a ball under the force of gravity.
