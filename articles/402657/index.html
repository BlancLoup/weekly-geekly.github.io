MTS abolishes tariffs with unlimited mobile Internet


"Unlimited" all.

MTS was the first major telecom operator to refuse to provide mobile unlimited access to the Internet, Vedomosti reports .

Under the reduction got the tariff plan "Unlimited" . Now all subscribers who subscribed to this tariff plan on March 27, 2017 and later will receive 10 GB of incoming traffic instead of unlimited access to the Network. The package price is 550 rubles.

According to Vedomosti, other tariffs of the Smart +, Smart Top and Smart ULTRA operators also have traffic restrictions (7, 15 and 20 GB respectively), but on the MTS official website, the Smart + tariff is still listed as a package with unlimited Internet.

MTS representatives claim that they analyzed the traffic consumption by users and came to the conclusion that subscribers with unlimited access, for the most part, consume no more than 5-6 GB of traffic per month. Accordingly, from these figures the tariff scale of the company was adjusted, which will cover the needs of the vast majority of customers.

For those who need large volumes, MTS suggests choosing a more expensive tariff plan than Unlimited.

Talk about the refusal of cellular operators from unlimited tariffs began in 2016. In December, VimpelCom CEO Schell Morten Johnsen said that the rejection of "unlimited" will not hit ordinary users and will affect only some customers.
