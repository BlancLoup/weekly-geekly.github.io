StartupPoint - 4th Meeting of Moscow Startupers


We remind you that on November 9, on Sunday at 15:00, the next meeting of startups in Moscow begins.

The following startups are announced in the program of the event:
+ Crabiza.ru
+ Megaplan
+ VeryWhy.Ru
+ YourMart.ru
+ Look around.ru (idea stage)
+ news4people.ru ( UPD )

+ Report "Startup: what to go to the investor?" From the investment company AVK-Media ( avkmedia.ru , egenius.ru )

We recommend that you familiarize yourself with startups in advance so that you can have a substantive conversation at the meeting.
Applications are still being accepted as a presenting startup. All the detailed information and answers to any questions on StartupPoint here and on soap mfagu@rambler.ru
Of course, you can come as a spectator (that is, without a startup, but just listen / talk to the people).

For non-Muscovites will be organized audio and video recording, which we post after processing the network.

Venue : Club "Caviar"
Address: Kazakova Street 8a (three-storeyed mansion of the Theater. Gogol)
Getting there : Yandex.Maps + Contacts on the club website

Get the latest news by joining the Google group StartupPoint community , as well as on our website by RSS-subscription.
