Nokia 6700 Classic user test: beauty requires sacrifice

Let's not waste time on lyrical digressions and get right to the point. Nokia 6700 Classic is a phone, the main ridge of which is the look. That is why of the 3 options for the design of the case (black, silver, chrome) you should choose chrome. Chrome 6700 looks not just catchy. Any ray of light in the room is instantly reflected from the surface of the phone and scatters with solar hares. Now, after all the praises of praise, you can stop on a fly in the ointment. The 6700 is definitely a good phone, but far from perfect. Even for his seemingly impeccable beauty will have to pay. The most serious drawbacks of the device lie precisely in appearance. However, first things first.



The phone is compact enough, but weighty (113 grams). Subjective sensations in the hand - a pleasant weight. You see the metal in the case and expect that the phone will not be easy. That is how it turns out. The metal in the body is quite thick and strong. This is clearly seen in the battery cover. This is not a thin aluminum cap Nokia E52, the metal is completely different. The phone is very well assembled. There are simply no gaps, creaks, or gaps. If you find fault very much, you can see a light slit to the left of the function key block, as well as an imperfect fixing of the plastic insert covering the camera. But this may be specific to the specimen that fell into my hands. Now about the shortcomings. Marco case is monstrous. As soon as the phone falls into the hands, it instantly slaps. Even if you wash your hands with soap and water, the phone will immediately become covered with prints. From a distance, the body slapping does not strike the eye, but in the vicinity of the noble brilliance of chromium, it was already gone. You can polish the phone to a mirror shine, but then in order to save it, you have to use the phone in gloves. And the whole surface of the phone is being stained - both the chrome-plated panels, the blocks of digital and function keys, and the screen. The black and silver versions of such problems, naturally, do not exist, but they also look much simpler than chrome, there is no highlight in them.
There are no complaints about the functional and alphanumeric keys for their main functions. All keys are well spaced, have a pleasant white backlight, sufficient to work in the dark, the designations on the keys are clearly visible, pressing a nice, the buttons are well fixed. There are several specific properties about the alphanumeric buttons. If you are a man and do not shave every day, then you will have problems from contact of this part of the phone with bristles.
