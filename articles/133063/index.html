Lomokino Review

Recently, the Lomokino machine came into my hands. As its name implies, the device is designed to shoot video in the style of Lomo.



As a carrier in Lomokino, which is generally characteristic of the Lomo direction, a 35 mm film is used. The camera is fully mechanical and uses a special pen as an energy source, one revolution which corresponds to one captured frame.



On one frame of 35 mm film 3 frames are placed, made with the help of Lomokino. The aspect ratio of the frame is approximately 25: 9.



This frame was obtained on a color negative film with ISO 400. The camera is equipped with a convenient viewfinder and mount for an external flash. Shutter speed is fixed - 1 / 100c. The diaphragm is adjustable from 5.6 to 11 using a special wheel located on the front side of the device. There is also a mount for mounting the camera on a tripod.

The device takes us back to the days of analog gif-animation and gives a lot of positive emotions.
