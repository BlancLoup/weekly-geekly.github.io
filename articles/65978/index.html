The LG CC - headset phone



This concept has become the winner of LG's Design the Future Competition contest, the goal of which is to provide LG with a range of unusual and interesting solutions. The essence of the work The LG CC comes down to the fact that a mobile phone in the unfolded state turns into headphones. This solution saves the user from the need to separately buy a Bluetooth-headset and, in principle, can be implemented already with the current level of technology development. In general, the author of the work Emily Albinski carries home $ 20,000 received for the first place , and we are waiting for the update of the line of phones of the Korean manufacturer.
