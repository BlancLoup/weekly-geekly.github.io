Daripodarki.ru opened the season of hunting for corporate gifts!

Usually, the season of hunting for corporate gifts opens in early November, but according to the old Russian tradition, most companies will start looking for New Year gifts for employees, partners, and customers only in December. For such companies, the gift voucher store Daripodarki.ru (http://daripodarki.ru/) launched the specialized campaign ‚ÄúHunting season for corporate gifts is open!‚Äù (Http://2007.daripodarki.ru).

For every 10,000 rubles in the total amount of the order, the ‚Äúhunter‚Äù receives a personalized gift - a gift certificate to the restaurant. The highlight of the gift is that the buyer himself determines the nominal value of this certificate. Upon delivery of the order, the store's courier offers the buyer to pull out one of the three envelopes. In the envelopes are certificates of three denominations: 100, 300 and 500 rubles. Thus, choosing one of the envelopes, the buyer receives a gift certificate of one or another value.

Gift certificates are ideal as corporate gifts. First, they save time searching for (making) gifts. Secondly, they provide the freedom to choose a gift to its ultimate owner. Thirdly, the delivery time of certificates is a maximum of 3 days from the date of payment.

The organizers of the action expect to attract the attention of a large number of corporate buyers. Daripodarki.ru store specialists conducted a preliminary study, the results of which turned out that 90% of companies this year will celebrate the corporate New Year. At the same time, 74% of companies are going to give presents to their employees and partners. At the time of the survey in 75% of the companies, the employees responsible for the purchase of gifts either knew nothing or had a vague idea about them. After a detailed account of gift certificates, 96.5% of respondents confirmed that they are ready to consider gift certificates as a gift: 71.5% are ready to give certificates to employees; 25.5% to business partners; 28.5% to customers.

As the statistics show, gift certificates themselves are an attractive solution to the problem of New Year's gifts for many companies, and an additional incentive in the form of a personal gift to the customer will only confirm the correctness of his decision and speed up the order.
