MTS fell in St. Petersburg and Pushkin

Petersburgers congratulations on Friday. Almost all over Peter fell MTS.

But when you try to call with your voice - silence, and then - hang up. When you try to call 112 - immediately hang up. The city call center can not get through.

This is proof that the problem is global.
