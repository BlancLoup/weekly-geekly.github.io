Digital Communications Kazakhstan 2011

I was surprised when I did not find a single mention of the largest event in Central Asia. We will fix it.

September 14-16, Astana will host the 3-day Digital Communications Kazakhstan 2011 conference. Among the participants will be 100 exhibiting companies, more than 500 delegates and thousands of visitors from different countries.



The main topics of the conference will be:
  • 1 day: Mobile devices.
  • Day 2: IT and Innovative Economy.
  • Day 3: Internet, Social Media and Interactive Advertising.
The speakers will include such interesting personalities as Alan Oro (President and General Director of IAB Europe), Vladimir Dolgov (Executive Director of Google in Russia), Andrey Kormiltsev (Director General of HTC in Russia), Alexey Katkov ( Vice-President Mail.Ru Group) and many others.

From our company on Digital Communications Kazakhstan will be Alexander Zverev . Using his own case studies as an example, he will talk about creating mobile applications and introducing them into the market within a few months.

A complete list of speakers can be found at the link .

A full participant package, which includes visits to all 3 days of the conference, costs $ 400 and includes:
  • Access to conference sessions for 3 days;
  • Free access to all seminars and exhibitions;
  • Access to the Business Communication System;
  • Lunches and coffee breaks included;
  • Free access to Wi-Fi;
  • Conference Package
Register and come to sunny Kazakhstan at Digital Communications Kazakhstan 2011 . This is a unique opportunity to share experiences and establish business contacts with representatives of major companies from around the world.
