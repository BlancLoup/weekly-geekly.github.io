Twitter bought Smallthought Systems

Every day, millions of people use Twitter to create, find and share information, and as we grow, analytics is becoming an increasingly important part in improving our service.

About a year ago, we started using an online database called Dabble DB to track and share information about our projects within the country. All this time we have maintained close relations with the creators of Dabble DB's - Smallthought Systems. When Smallthought launched Trendly , a tool that helps websites extract a signal from the noise in their data from Google Analytics, we were among the first to try it. Their team has a unique combination of entrepreneurship, creativity, and analytical competence.

Therefore, it is with great pleasure that we announce the purchase of Smallthought Systems. They have joined our analytical team and will focus on integrating Trendly ideas into our current tools and creating innovative, real-time products for our future trading partners. Please welcome Avi Bryant , Andrew Catton , Ben Matasar , and Luke Andrews to Twitter on Twitter.
