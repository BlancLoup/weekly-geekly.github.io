Portable wind generator - take the future with you on the road



I got an interesting concept the other day - a project of a portable wind generator. To be honest, the news about solar batteries, which are getting better every hour, is fed up, so I read the concept description with interest. So, the Eolic portable wind generator is designed for regions where there is no electricity at all, or it is, but comes with interruptions. In addition, the wind generator can be used as an alternative source of energy even for energetically saturated regions. A few photos and a description of the device - in the continuation of the post.

The main condition, of course, is sufficient windiness, in principle, can be calculated from the wind rose, or simply call the nearest meteorological office.

The implementation of the idea is interesting - according to the idea, the wind turbine body will be made of carbon fiber. In addition, aluminum is used in the construction. The height of the "mill" can be adjusted by applying the principle of the telescope in the design of the device.

Well, and among other advantages, this wind generator can be folded for carrying. In general, an interesting idea. Unfortunately, dimensions and other configurations are not listed in the original source.
