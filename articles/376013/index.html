Tesla Model S will be faster due to software update

New firmware will make the car faster, as if it is a smartphone


Ilon Mask announced in his Twitter account that the P85D will be faster by updating the software: the algorithm of the inverter, the device for converting DC to AC will be changed. A specific improvement figure was announced: the car will accelerate from 0 to 97 km / h (60 miles per hour) by about a tenth of a second faster.

The P85D is already a very fast car, it accelerates from zero to 97 km / h in just 3.2 seconds. Here is the extraction of all power from the electric vehicle.



An electric car does not have a gearbox, so it creates a feeling of constant acceleration without shifting gears. Tesla has been posting updates for a long time and adding new features, but this case is interesting in improving the features, which, it seems, cannot be improved by flashing. Updates "over the air" come either via WiFi or via the built-in 3G module. You do not need to visit a service center.

In addition, the version with one P85 engine (the P85D has two) will also be faster, but not so significantly.
