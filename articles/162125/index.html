"Rostelecom" launched a service payment of utility bills on the portal gosuslugi.ru

The portal gosuslugi.ru is open payment of utility bills . More than 30 million Russians in 50 regions of Russia already have the opportunity in real time to find out their public service debt and pay them online on the Public Services Portal. You can pay on the portal Single payment documents, electricity and gas. Until the end of next year, the service of payment for housing and communal services through the portal gosuslugi.ru will work in all regions of the country. A locality is considered connected to the service if 80% of its residents can pay 100% of utility bills through the portal.

Payment of utility bills on the portal can be made both on the invoices and on charges. The service also offers several payment methods. Any utility provider can connect to the service.

The convenience and importance of introducing an electronic system of payment for housing and public utilities is hardly necessary to prove to those who read habrahabr, but we still give a few numbers.

New service allows you to:

1. Recognize your utility bills in real time
2. Receive operational information about new bills in a virtual personal account
3. Pay bills directly on the Public Services Portal using a bank card
4. Store information about paid bills in a virtual personal account

In the near future, it is also planned to add the possibility of paying with electronic money and from a mobile phone account.

But the most interesting. In order to pay for utilities, you do not need to go through the standard registration. In order to make the payment service for housing and communal services more accessible, a simplified procedure has been developed for registering on the Public Services Portal, which requires only an e-mail address.
