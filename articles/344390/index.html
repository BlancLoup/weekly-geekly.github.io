However, thanks to some efforts of state bodies and other organizations, we have the opportunity to fairly quickly collect interesting information about specific cars from open sources on the Internet. How to do it quickly and for free , we will try to tell in this article.

To collect all the available information about the vehicle, you need to know its vin-number. VIN or Vehicle Identification Number (English Vehicle identification number) - a unique vehicle code consisting of 17 characters. The code provides information on the manufacturer and characteristics of the vehicle, and the year of manufacture. The code structure is based on ISO 3779-1983 and ISO 3780 standards. Identification numbers are applied on all-in-one components of the body or chassis and on specially made license plates (nameplates).
