GeForce for $ 59

NVIDIA officially unveiled its budget accelerator GeForce 9400 GT on August 26.


Built a new product based on the G96 chip with an operating frequency of 550 MHz and 16 stream processors. Despite its focus on the low-end market segment, the 9400 GT supports extensions to the CUDA architecture.

By default, nVidia offers a GeForce 9400 GT with 512 MB of GDDR2 on board. And the video adapter (recommended by the manufacturer) will cost only $ 59.

However, all those companies that have already started production of the 9400 GT (there are about a dozen of them headed by ASUS and GIGABYTE) are unlikely to stop at such a cost. Moreover, many have announced the release of the device with 1GB of memory.

I wonder how much the novelty can be bought from us ...
