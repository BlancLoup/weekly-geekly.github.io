The agreement with the FAS obliges Google to resume the work of its services in the Crimea



Google Corporation on April 17 signed an agreement with FAS. In addition to other conditions, this document obliges the company to give the opportunity to select a search system for users throughout Russia, writes Izvestia.

"For devices that are in circulation in the Russian Federation, Google will develop a selection window, which, with the next update, will give users the opportunity to select the default search engine," explained FAS deputy head Alexei Dotsenko. Even more interesting is the nuance regarding the work of Google in Crimea. According to Russian officials, now the American company is obliged to make its services accessible to all residents of the peninsula.
