Python, Perl, PHP, Ruby, .NET, Flex, Silverlight - at DEVCONF 2010 May 17 in Moscow (Hello World!)



DevConf is a professional conference dedicated to leading programming and web development technologies.

DevConf members are given a unique opportunity - to access immediately all the leading technologies of web development by listening to reports from the founders of these technologies - who came to Moscow from all over the world.

The democratic cost of participation: 3,000 rubles for the main day of the conference - will allow us to bring together more than 1,200 developers under the same roof

As part of DevConf, on May 17th, 2010, Crocus Expo (M. Myakinino) will host 5 conferences:

DevConf :: PHP () - Former PHPConf
DevConf :: Perl () - from Perl fans
DevConf :: Python () - from Python fans
DevConf :: Ruby () - From Ruby Fans
DevConf :: ASP.NET () - from .NET fans


On May 18th, 2010 in the area (m. VDNH), DevConf will host a conference DevConf :: RichClient (Javascript, Flash, Flex, Silverlight), as well as a series of unique master classes.

The conference format DevConf provides the first conference day and the second day of the master classes. The second day is optional and requires separate registration.

REGISTRATION on DevConf 2010 is OPEN!

For whom DevConf?

The conference is aimed at professional web developers and those who want to become them. It unites ALL the most common languages, with each one having its own stream (hall).

It will be possible to immerse yourself in the technology that is of the greatest interest. At the same time the level of any of the areas is the most professional. In each section, reports will be presented by 1-2 direct authors of the language, as well as recognized experts in the field.
