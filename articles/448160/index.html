Process and bureaucracy on the road to WeChat


About a year ago, Dodo Pizza announced that it would launch a pizzeria without a box office in China. ‚ÄúIn order to open a website in Russia, you just need to open a website. Hosting, domain and forth. In China, everything is much more complicated. Suppose you need to create an online store.
