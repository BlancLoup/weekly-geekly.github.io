Review of inexpensive in-channel SoundMAGIC headphones (models ES18 and PL11)

Both models belong to the class of dynamic headphones of the plug-in type and are designed to replace the complete headphones of the phone, player or take an empty seat at all.

‚ñ∫ Packing and packaging

Headphones are supplied in white cardboard boxes. In the upper part there is a window, inside of which there are headphones on a kind of podium, and in the lower part there are accessories.

On the right side are the possible options for color design.

Behind - the characteristics, as well as a sticker with a scratch-coating, erasing that, we get an eighteen-digit verification code. To verify the originality of the headphones, enter it (without spaces) on the official website of the company in the Security check section. As a result, you should receive confirmation of the authenticity of products and thanks for the purchase. :)

The set of delivery for both headphones is quite modest, while for ES18 it is completely spartan. In addition to the headphones themselves and the medium-sized nozzles already on them, the box contains a case and additional large and small silicone inserts.

In addition to all of the above, PL11 has a clip that can perform at least two useful functions, which I will discuss later.

The package bundle is directly proportional to the price, but in general, everything that is most needed is, with the exception of the clips from the SoundMAGIC ES18.

‚ñ∫ Appearance

ES18 does not stand out in terms of design, they are made of black plastic, with a tiny metal insert in the middle. Available in 3 color options: red, green and orange. The body itself has the shape of a barrel.

The sound system, like the entire body, is plastic, covered with a metal grid. On the other hand is the company logo, headphone model, as well as the inscriptions L and R.

The cable along the entire length consists of a double wire. Runner and splitter have the same shape, only the latter is fixed with glue and raises doubts in terms of reliability.

PL11 looks more attractive. The main part of the body consists of metal, which is a distinctive feature of this model and can be made in gray, gold, red or blue.

Plastic sound guards, covered with metal mesh. On the other hand, the tails are made of hard rubber, on which the markings L and R are located. Because the marking is made by squeezing on the tails and blends in with the color of rubber, it is simply impossible to distinguish the right earpiece from the left one at night.

In this model, the main part of the cable is rubber-sheathed, and the splitter looks more impressive.

The plugs of both models are similar, they are ‚ÄúG‚Äù -shaped, gilded. This is convenient if the connector on your phone / player is located on the side.
