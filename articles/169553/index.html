The robot is made of gold. Vertu launches Android smartphone


Vertu is a Nokia sovereign fragment recently known for its luxury phones: leather upholstery, buttons made of precious stones, precious metals, sapphire glass ... And this is not a complete list of what you can boast of when purchasing one of the phones of this manufacturer (although, if you have the funds for such a phone, then you can brag a lot more than that, but this is not about that now).
So, as it was said earlier, recently Vertu has passed under the wing of EQT VI, and their new Vertu Ti smartphone is the first after the change of the majority shareholder.
As materials of the case (in the basic configuration), polished titanium, genuine leather, sapphire crystal and some more precious metals for small elements were used. The richer versions are crocodile skin and rose gold. In total, the smartphone has 184 parts, each of them is manually produced by a separate master. The stereo speakers are Bang & Olufsen, they were calibrated with the help of experts from the London Symphony Orchestra. In addition, the device is equipped with two cameras (1.3 megapixels and 8 megapixels), LED flash. Under the hood is a dual-core processor with a clock speed of 1.7 GHz, 1 GB of RAM and 64 GB of permanent memory. Of course, the smartphone is equipped with all the wireless plumes like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and NFC. Screen size - 3.7 inches. And the most unexpected highlight of the luxury phone - it works on the Android 4.0 platform!

Yes, by the way about the price: in the basic configuration it will cost $ 9,600 , and in the "major" one - up to $ 21,700 . Thus, Vertu Ti has the right to be called the most expensive Android-smartphone.
PS One interesting detail: Ti has an index of RM-828V. For comparison: Nokia Lumia 820 - RM-824 and RM-826. It is possible that the hardware was completely optimized for Windows Phone, but for some reason, Android was chosen.
