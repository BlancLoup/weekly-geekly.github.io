A calendar that understands you

The story is simple. I wanted to try out a parsing library of samopisny and I made a parser of Russian-language date and time formats, almost any.
Where to try:
1. JSon API g-calendar.appspot.com/application/parse?message=your_message
2. Demo - google calendar


The original idea was to make a calendar. Because, apart from parsing the date and time, the parser highlights the name of the event and its regularity (day, week) - some things are still raw.
