Getting money from AdSense through Rapida Online to a bank account

Hello.

Not so long ago, faced with the need to get money for the published application on the Android Market. The very first thing I tried was a check. As most people know, it is not easy to cash a check in Russia (the process consists of sending the check back to America by mail and returning the money back by mail and takes up to 60 business days), therefore, disappointed, he began to look for alternatives.

In the AdSense account I was very interested in Count Rapida, where it was written that you can receive money in the mail within 2 days. I went to Rapida and by chance I got not on Rapida, but on Rapida Online. Rapida Online is a kind of electronic wallet that makes it possible to transfer money from it to other wallets:
  • Qiwi
  • Yandex money
  • Web money

or to a bank account in the banks listed on the site. The list is quite long. I was interested in Alfa-Bank, and I found it there.

Registering with Rapida Online

So, registration in Rapida online is quite simple. Go to Rapida Online and fill out the registration form in Russian. You must specify the name, email and phone number without a country code. Then there are two stages of verification - the code on the email, the code on the phone.

After that, your account is ready. You can log in in the future with the help of a pin code that came to your phone and phone number.

Create a binding in adsense

It's still easier here. Go to Account Settings - Edit Payment Method - Setup Rapida. Again, one form, enter the name, address, index. Now pay attention to the latest field - the identifier in Rapid. This is the field, the filling of which will transfer you from receiving money in the mail to receiving money into the account. Enter here your phone number, one to one corresponding to the phone in the rapid rate (without country code).



Result

So, I received money for February 2011. As usual, payments occur at the end of the month. This time, the money went away with AdSense on the 28th.

Of course, I immediately went to Rapida and was horrified - 0. I thought, probably the delay. All the next day, nothing came and began to get a little nervous. But the next day I received an email notification of the transfer and I finally saw the money in the Rapid account. A little disappointed by the fact that the money was there in rubles, converted at an incomprehensible rate, but here, most likely, nothing can be done.
Next - I chose to withdraw money to an account at Alfa-Bank. Entered the account number, identification data and clicked "Done". For such transactions, Rapida takes a fixed commission of 20 rubles.
A day later, the money was in my account.

Total, Rapida Online is seen at the moment as the only truly convenient way to earn money with a minimum of time and money lost. The only losses - 20 p. commission and possible losses on conversion at a rate that is not pleasing to you.

Plus, at the moment, the maximum amount with which Rapida works is 150,000 rubles. That is, if your income has exceeded $ 5000, I am afraid that you will have to suffer to receive money or split it into several payments.

Thanks for attention.
