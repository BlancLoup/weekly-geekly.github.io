Roskomnadzor blocked the largest porn site

Today, the world's largest porn resource, redtube.com (96th most popular web site) has been added to the registry of banned sites.

This case is notable for the fact that, according to information from the antizapret.info site, the reason for blocking was a movie depicting underage characters in a pornographic cartoon (hentai). It is not completely clear how the age of the drawn actors was determined and what consequences this will bring to other artistic materials, however, not so long ago it was reported that Roskomnadzor is going to block similar content.

At the moment the site is already blocked by some providers.
