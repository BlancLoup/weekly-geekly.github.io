Trial versions of Adobe Creative Cloud are available.


I'm glad for the fifth time to bring the good news about the appearance of trial versions of new Adobe products.

Adobe Creative Cloud on assurances of developers, completely changes the workflow. Now all applications are integrated with the cloud service from Adobe and simplify development. Also, subscribers can access from 2 to 20 gigabytes in the cloud storage.


It is also extremely interesting that now you can subscribe to both individual applications and the entire package, and at a very attractive price. Until July 31, a monthly subscription for a separate product will be 599 rubles, a monthly subscription for all products subject to an annual subscription - 1500 rubles. For students and teachers a full package will cost 599 rubles. per month.
Not forgotten users of previous versions. If you have a license for CS3 or later products, then a subscription to an individual product will cost you 299 rubles per month or 899 for everything.
Previously, Adobe was a synonym for "greed." With constant updates, compare with the total cost of a box of the same photoshop about 40 thousand rubles or a suite - 103 thousand rubles.

To download trial (30 days) products, you must register at Creative Cloud . Download will occur through the Adobe Applications Manager.
