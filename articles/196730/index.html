Scrambler - a service for storing and exchanging data with encryption

We present you a service that has not existed until now. This is a scrambler.


A unique service that is designed to provide reliable data storage and transmission in the global world of the Internet. Scrambler reliably protects data using cryptographic encryption algorithms and electronic signature, thereby protecting your private and confidential data from being viewed by unauthorized persons, attackers, including owners of Scrumbler service.

You have a unique opportunity to share data, work with files online and always have access to your data anywhere in the world.
