Pseudo Linking in Axure

Many users of Axure at first encountered such a problem as the design of pseudo links. The fact is that by default, the software does not allow you to make links (or text, which, in principle, is the same) underlined with a dotted line. Therefore, novice creators of prototypes (prototypists?) Periodically can see pseudo-references with a continuous underlining, which causes confusion in the document. In fact, to solve this problem is very simple.

1. In the widget panel, select the link (Hyperlink);



2. We place it in our work area;



3. Remove underlining;





4. In the widget panel, select the horizontal line (Horisontal line);



5. Drag it into our workspace, change the color and make it dotted;



6. Increasing the scale of the workspace, we customize it under our link (now a pseudo link). I usually leave a gap between the text and the line of just one pixel;



7. Enjoying the result
