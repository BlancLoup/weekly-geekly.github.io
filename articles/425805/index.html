DevOops 2018: free live webcast, party and more



Only a few days are left before the DevOops 2018 conference: it will be held at the congress center of the Park Inn by Radisson Pulkovskaya hotel on October 14. You already know about 17 reports and hundreds of participants, but we have prepared something else.


According to tradition, a free online broadcast of the first hall will be opened directly on YouTube. Between presentations, keynote speakers will be interviewed.


Link to broadcast

Only the first four of the seven reports (up to and including Baruch, and an interview after it) will be broadcast. The video link will work on Sunday morning, when DevOops begins. Now there you can see the program of the first hall.


For those who go live, I want to remind you a few extra chips.

After the presentations, you can sign books with the speakers.

Discussion areas


After each report, the speaker goes to a dedicated discussion area, where you can talk with him and ask your questions.


Formally, this can be done in a break between reports. Speakers are not required, but usually stay much longer - for example, for the duration of the next report. Sometimes it makes sense to skip the report from the main program (if you bought a ticket, you still have records) and spend it on focused communication with an important expert.


It is extremely important not to stand on the sidelines, but to participate in the discussion and ask your questions. Popular speakers are often surrounded by a whole crowd of people who want to, so it makes sense to immediately ‚Äúcatch‚Äù the speaker, go into the discussion area with him, stand in the front row, and feel free to ask interesting questions.

Three BoF Sessions


Immediately after the end of the main program, three parallel BoF sessions await you.


BoF is now a traditional format. Something like a round table or discussion group in which everyone can take part.


No division by speaker and participant: everyone is on a par. This is important for at least two reasons:


  • The format of the report is very useful because of its focus on a specific topic and the lack of opportunity to be distracted. Everyone is sitting and listening to the speaker. The BoF session is suitable for full-fledged communication between all participants and allows you to cover a wide range of related topics.
  • Among the participants are many cool specialists. They do not have a report at this conference, but this in no way diminishes their coolness. Often they are speakers at our other conferences, or occupy leading positions in well-known companies.
  • Not everyone has time to ask questions in the discussion area, even if they spent more than an hour with the speaker there.
