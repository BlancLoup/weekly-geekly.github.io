Motorola again began to produce its MOTOFONE F3, an inexpensive phone with an e-ink display.



This "old man", called MOTOFONE F3, is equipped with only basic functions, without any connections to the Network and the ability to synchronize data with online services. No, this device does what the phone should do: it can be used to make calls, send SMS, receive a signal about the arrival of messages and save contacts in the phone book. Yes, there is still an alarm clock, but that's all.

As mentioned above, the display on this phone is not TFT or AMOLED. This is a segmented e-ink that consumes an extremely small amount of energy (compared to a conventional display). It can also be assumed that the display is equipped with a backlight, otherwise using the phone in the dark becomes impossible.
