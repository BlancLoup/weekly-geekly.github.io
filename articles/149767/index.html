What happens to the equipment after lightning

We have small PBXs in different villages with equipment for accessing the Internet. As a rule, they are located in rented premises of village administrations. The whole installation of equipment is always trying to do according to the mind, lightning protection is always done. But practice shows that with a good thunderstorm with an abundance of lightning, protection does not help.
Under the cut there is a little photo of what happened in the PBX room, after a lightning hit the roof of the building and the cable of the air passage over the ravine (it could be a bit more fucking, but that was enough).
