HTC Legend - the successor of HTC Hero in aluminum shell

As HTC, it would not try to keep the veil of secrecy around its upcoming new products on the eve of the World Mobile Congress, but the company's partners continue to merge information into the network (even despite the frank watermarks in the firmware).

As you may have guessed, the photograph is a preproduction sample of the long-awaited replacement of the HTC Hero - the HTC Legend. The elements of the case are made of aluminum, which, according to reviews, sits perfectly in the hand. Trackball replaced by a more aesthetic optical sensor. Also, the novelty will have a 3.2 "AMOLED HVGA screen, a budget version of the SoC Qualcomm MSM7227 with a frequency of 600 MHz, 512 MB of ROM, 256 MB of RAM.
