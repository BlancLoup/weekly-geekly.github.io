The alpha version of the Qwiki service is open.



The other day the alpha version of the Qwiki service was opened.

Qwiki is a startup that combines different sources of information (Wikipedia, Google, Fotopedia, YouTube) into almost real-time video presentations in almost real time. Currently there are about 3 million similar presentations available.

As the founders of a startup say, Qwiki is a new media format and an innovative method of consuming information.

Go to qwiki.com and enter any query. For example, " Russia ". How do you?
