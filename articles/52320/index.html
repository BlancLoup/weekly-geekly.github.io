MegaFon and gifts for loyalty.

Just a couple of minutes ago I received an SMS message from MegaFon with the following content:
You have connected to the MegaFon network on 02/19/03. Thank you for being with us! To activate a gift within 5 days, type% service_number%. Conditions 88003330500.

Discarding Paranoia, and checking the reality of the message - sent a request. In response, I got the opportunity to choose a gift for loyalty: either 20 SMS, or 20 MMS, or 10 MB of traffic gprs.

Since MMS is rarely used, and instead of GPRS lately mostly WiFi, I chose 20 SMS for myself. In response to the request, we received a clarifying message asking that I definitely want a package of 20 SMS on MegaFon all over Russia with a validity period of 3 (!) Days.

So the question arose - is this normal? In my opinion, rather poorly and pitifully, it would be better to have nothing at all than that. Or did I snicker?
