Palm Centro 2 in a new form factor

A poster appeared on the Internet showing the Palm Centro 2 communicator.



Despite the fact that the first model was equipped with a QWERTY-keyboard, the second version will be performed in accordance with the fashion trends.

Palm Centro 2 will be equipped with a large touch screen, through which the user interaction will be carried out. Under the display is a block of control buttons, made in the style of Palm Centro. As the operating system, the company intends to use Windows Mobile instead of its original Palm OS. Information about the release date of the device yet.

UPD: The poster that aroused the journalists turned out to be a hand-made article by designer Andrew Jo (senkyu, YasonBy ). His clarifications about the alleged features of the mythical device: Centro 2 kakby has a sliding QWERTY-keyboard, but did not have enough time to draw it.
