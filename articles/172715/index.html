Video Sitemap


VideoSiteMap is an xml file on your server that transmits information about the video content of your site to the search engine. Surprisingly, 8 out of 10 of our customers ordering videos do not know what a video mapping is, and therefore do not use it for promotion.

If the video is placed on your server, then video sitemap is the only way to tell the search engine about your video; without it, the video will not be included in the search results. In addition to the technical information about the video file, the video sitemap contains the name of the video, its description, tags. Be sure to use in these fields the keywords for which you want to get into the result of search results.

Youtube promotion


Position in the search results of your video posted on Youtube depends on the factors:

  • keywords in title, description and video tags;
  • average video viewing time;
  • the number of views and comments from the video and the channel as a whole;
  • the number of subscribers to the channel;
  • age of the video, age of the channel;
  • the number of "likes", comments, additions to playlists, complaints, video inserts on other sites;
  • the number of external links to the video.

This is not an exhaustive list, but it is with these factors that you should start working and look at the result.

Keywords


Use keywords in the title, in the description (at least twice) and in video tags.
There are keywords that relate only to video and cannot be found using conventional keyword selection tools. Therefore, when selecting keywords, it is necessary to use not only the familiar Google Keyword Tool and Yandex Wordstat, but also to check all the results using the Youtube Keyword Tool .

Tip: put a link to your page in the first 150 characters of the video description. According to statistics, 6% of viewers pass by it.

Average time watching movie


A small average viewing time and a high bounce rate (when users close the page almost immediately) is a very serious ‚Äúsin‚Äù in Google‚Äôs eyes. This suggests that the video is either useless, or uninteresting, or irrelevant to keywords. The chance to get on the first page of search results in such videos is low.

Use only relevant keywords, and by all means try to keep the viewer longer on the page. For example, try starting a discussion in the comments.

And try not to make long videos. The ideal length of the video is: 90-120 seconds for a detailed marketing video and 30-45 seconds for a short video how to. Such videos do not have time to bother the viewer, videos are viewed in full, the pages do not close ahead of time, Google is happy, the video rating is growing.

Video distribution


For a video to have a high rating, it must have many views, comments, subscribers, etc. To do this, distribute the video on all your channels:

  • email distribution (video cannot be remarked into a letter, but you can insert an image imitating a YouTube player window with a large ‚Äúplay‚Äù button);
  • press release (of course, if you think that the topic will be of interest to editors);
  • posts from video on blogs, forums;
  • social networks and twitter;
  • video response to a popular video on your subject.

Cheat views


An alternative to natural advancement is the number of views and subscribers cheat services. We recommend using them only in extreme cases and be very careful: Google requires that all promotion (including video) be natural, so you can get banned for too aggressive browsing.

These recommendations do not guarantee 100% success, but at least you can start with them, and then evaluate the result and draw the appropriate conclusions. If we have missed something, and you have your own ways to increase sales with the help of videos - welcome to the comments.
