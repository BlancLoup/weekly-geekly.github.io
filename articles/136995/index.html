Comparison of several popular Russian cloud / regular Windows VPS providers

The other day it took me to choose a virtual machine for my own project. Because I write on Windows / .Net / Mssql, then, obviously, and hosting needs appropriate. I chose only from Russian providers because of the need for good pings and simplicity of calculations, since it will be necessary to work with jur. the face. After a not very successful Q & A Q. I myself tested several providers.
Updated Added another provider, added a final comparison


So, as I conducted testing:
I made VPS in the minimum acceptable configuration: 1GB of memory, 1 core (or more, if given), 20GB hard drive or more, the issue price is up to 1500r. Everywhere I used test accounts, except for scalaxy (I threw 100 rubles there after the limit was exhausted).
Then:
1) Installed all updates on windows
2) Measure speed using Speedtest.net
3) Measured speed of disk operations
4) Compare subjective feelings when working through RDP with work locally

So let's get started:

The most brake of all that was tried. Give the minimum amount of resources for comparable money.
Subjectively - when working through RDP it slows down everything. Tests of speed of the Internet and disks I will not bring.

Discs. Give 20GB, you can ramp up.

Internet: download ~ 70Mbit / s, return 9.5-9.7Mbit / s
Give one core , min. 1330 MHz warranty, up to 2660 MHz

Subjectively, everything works well, not tupit, updates were made quickly.

Discs. Give only 15GB, more - for the add. fee.

They give 16 (!) Cores , however at the same time they guarantee 450 MHz per slot, as they say, to be honest, it is not clear.
The Internet!

Subjectively - everything flies, except for the Internet.

1Gb.ru


He was a favorite in terms of price / quality ratio (only 1150r per month), if it were not for one fat BUT that crossed all the advantages.
So: Disks - unfortunately the picture did not work, because see BUT. In general, they work satisfactorily, they give immediately 70GB, which is very nice. The speed is slightly worse than scalaxy.
Give 2 x 2400 MHz , it is unclear whether it is guaranteed

Subjectively, everything works well, the only thing is that for some reason the updates were dumb for a long time, then they were quickly installed.
And now the very thing BUT: I am sending a fiery greeting to this wonderful company and thank God that, to my joy, I did not take hosting from them. Note that the stated test period is 10 days. Last night I created a test PPS, and today at 13.00 I received a letter of approximately the following content:
Dear hosting user 1Gb.ru
Your xumix account resources have been blocked by an administrator or system because Account activity violates the rules of using hosting services.
First of all, please log in to your personal account. Most likely, you will receive a message about the reason for blocking.


Naturally, there was no reason in the personal account. Naturally, I did not break anything, all I did was install updates and test the speed of the disks / Internet. After a letter to the support, after 5 hours, an answer was received with the following content:
The test period for your VDS is stopped because resources required paid users. You can continue to work, if you pay for services, VDS is not deleted.

To which I replied with such a letter:
Well, after what happened, I had a little less than any desire to use your services, especially for money. Thank God that I did not buy right away, although you were the first contender for the move. Even more glory to God, that this fact happened right away, and not when you would have the projects. I can only advise to be more attentive to customers, and for example, to warn before disconnecting, and also to write the reason for disconnection.


Thus, from all in favorites Cloud4y was beaten out, since only he got an acceptable quality / price of services. Scalaxy would also look very nice if it were not for a problem with the network.
