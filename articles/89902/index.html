Unpack the iPad

I have been thinking for a long time about whether to preorder me on the Apple website. I decided that I could buy without it. Yesterday, of course, there were small concerns that there would be queues. Especially after reporting from blogs, that here and there people live in tents in front of shops. But, as it turned out, you can buy an iPad absolutely no problem. The seller was even happy with my purchase, as there were about 20 people around the booth, but obviously no one was in a hurry to buy.

Available were all three currently available models: 16, 32 and 64 GB. I bought 64, because there is not much space. Externally, the packaging is no different.

Immediately I wanted to buy a case. I'm not a big fan of cases, and I have never had them before, even for mobile phones.
