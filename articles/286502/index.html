Shopping service PASSENGER.ME started working with Russian users

According to the publication FIRRMA, the PASSENGER.ME service began to work for Russian users, which ensures the ordering and delivery of goods from other countries during the week. Currently, the service has 50 million different types of products, some of which are also included in the Amazon catalog. The cost of the service is formed as the sum of the product price and the price of delivery. It is noteworthy that travelers pay for the delivery of goods.

The service has a feature that relates to the delivery process: all orders are brought by travelers, who, depending on the characteristics of the goods, pay for it from $ 20 to $ 200.

By the beginning of 2015, 237 orders for the sum of $ 67,000 were delivered from the USA. So far the number of registered users on the site totals about 5,000. The average purchase amount is $ 300. Most often in demand are gadgets, products for children and premium products.

From ordering to the transfer of goods to the buyer takes 5-10 days.
