At the same time, such services have long been successfully provided by various Internet services, earning on "clean" IP-telephony. And while IP-services have limitations, IP-telephony is already becoming a full-fledged product for communication, competing with cellular communication.

MTT launched YouMagic IP telephony service, which conveniently combines the main advantages of using data transmission via the Internet protocol, traditional telephony and can use the mobile network as an infrastructure for its work. In addition to the usual conveniences of IP-telephony, YouMagic can receive a city number from Russia or even from another country for free and quickly. In a traditional connection, getting a direct city number is quite a complicated and time-consuming process, which usually takes a lot of money, time and nerves. In YouMagic, this is done quickly and without visits to the operator‚Äôs office. How this opportunity is realized I would like to tell you.





Since MTT is one of the largest telecom operators in Russia, the company has the opportunity to directly own the city numbering, where necessary. To connect the city numbering to the IP telephony of a telecommunications company, a lot of work is required, which is associated with serious costs and bureaucratic red tape. This is not available for every company, and therefore often in IP-telephony services landline numbers are rented from different operators. This does not ensure proper reliability of the service and does not guarantee the possibility of its expansion, therefore MTT prefers to do all such work on its own. Getting the city numbering is not complete without very important steps:

1) It is necessary to obtain a license from the state for the provision of local telephone services;

2) It is important to purchase certified equipment for a local communication hub: A large carrier-class PBX, including several servers and a switch;

3) It is obligatory to rent a technical site, install and configure equipment, with electricity and communication channels with redundancy;

4) It would be good to request and purchase local numbering from a state for a specific pool;

5) There is no way to do without the acceptance procedure and performance check by Roskomnadzor and other departments of the communication center;

6) It should be requested to open access to the selected numbering from other local telephone operators;

7) Now you can safely sell numbers!

Until the end of the year, MTT plans to connect YouMagic to the numbering of all major cities of Russia, as well as expand the numbering capacity connection in other countries.
