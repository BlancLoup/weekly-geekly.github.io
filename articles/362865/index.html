Ilon Musk confirmed plans to launch 700 communications satellites into Earth orbit



At Geektimes, the news was already published that Ilon Mask and his partners are working on their own project of providing access to the Network to the most remote corners of our planet. It is planned to do this by launching into the orbit a group of 700 miniature communication satellites.

In parallel, Facebook and Google are working on similar projects. These companies plan to launch several types of devices at once: balloons, communication satellites, and drones. This is all done with the same goal - to give the opportunity to connect to the Network for residents of regions where it is impossible to deploy a regular network infrastructure.


The other day, Ilon Musk confirmed the intention of his company, SpaceX, to develop functional mini-communications satellites (weighing about 100 kilograms and costing about a million US dollars) for group work. The official announcement of the results of the project will take place in 2-3 months.
