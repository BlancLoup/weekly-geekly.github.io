The second group of learning Java and our news

In April, the first educational course for Java-programmers started on the educational platform Otus.ru, which will last for five months. Classes go on as usual in the online format, programmers are trained in groups through webinars and homework. Projects are as close to real conditions as possible: actual tasks are solved, the bias is made on practical skills. The teacher of the course - a professional developer and experienced teacher Vitaly Chibrikov - such a combination is not often to be found, the more so it is valuable. We consider it very important that in the process of learning a student gets the opportunity to work with a professional in his field.

Now we pay special attention to the quality control of education, conduct surveys and develop an assessment methodology, simultaneously solving the technical nuances of the process. It is very important for us to build the most flexible and effective teaching method and to produce specialists of an excellent middle level. However, we have already stated this before.

It is not for nothing that we declare that it is interesting to learn from us, but not easy. At the end of the first month, a small percentage of students left us. The dropout of students is inevitable and is a kind of indicator of quality - a successful program cannot receive an equal number of students at the entrance and exit, this fact will speak about the rating of training under such a program. In most cases, students made a decision about graduation due to lack of sufficient time for study. Those who have decided to go further, in surveys note the relevance of the material, its novelty and practical benefits, as well as the responsiveness of the teacher. And for us it is a very important assessment.
