Cloud tracking service provider launched



The https://cloudsleuth.net/ site was launched, which represents a service that collects and analyzes information on the parameters of cloud providers.



The service collects information on response time and availability for the following cloud services: Microsoft Azure, Google App Engine, Amazon EC2, OpSource, RackSpace, GoGrid . Information is collected from different distant points around the world. On the site you can see the parameters of a service in one place or another in the world and the overall averaged parameter of the service. You can set the period for the services to be analyzed: 6 hours, day, week and month.

An interesting project, but I would like more geographic data collection points and, of course, the appearance of such points in Russia.
