A schoolboy from Texas got a joke in a computer game

An 18-year-old schoolboy from Texas was jailed for a sarcastic joke in the computer game League of Legends and he faces 8 years.



The story began in February 2013. 18-year-old high school student Justin Carter playing LoL, joked.

During the game he was called crazy. To which he replied:

- Yeah, I'm definitely not friends with my head! I'm going to shoot all the kids in school and eat their still beating hearts.
- lol
- jk (short for just kidding)

After that, a compassionate woman was found who, upon seeing this post, called the police. Guy tied a month. He has been in prison since then and recently celebrated his 19th birthday there.

At the moment, the case is being considered and the guy is being charged with the threat of terrorism and he has been in the shit for 8 years. His parents created a petition for release addressed to Barack Obama, which was already signed by 27 thousand people.

The world has gone mad ... A man is imprisoned for 8 years for an obvious joke.
