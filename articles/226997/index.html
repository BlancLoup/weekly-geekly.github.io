Welcome to Moscow JavaScript Meetup June 26

June 26, 2014, on Thursday, the 12th meeting of javascript developers MoscowJS will take place in Moscow.



The format of the mitap is well established and does not change for a long time. The event is divided into two parts: reports and discussion. Of course, performances are important, but backroom conversations often bring even more benefits.

With each event we try to improve MoscowJS. This time we asked the speakers to stay after the speeches. They will hold master classes on their topic and answer questions. All this in an informal setting, for a slice of pizza and something refreshing.

Topics for scheduled performances:


Anton Belousov, founder and CEO of Tai.st, will make a report Addons for cloud web applications: a developer‚Äôs view

What are addons useful for users and the cloud products themselves? What are the technologies and approaches to their creation? Ivan Chashkin, Mail.ru Group, will present a report on the topic of Mobile Web: What is so slow!

Ivan will talk about what you have to face when you want to make a quick Single-page application. Examples will be given that can be optimized to increase the "responsiveness" of the application, improve its performance.
