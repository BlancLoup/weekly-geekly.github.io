New album McCartney released in the figure without DRM

Paul McCartney released the new album Electric Arguments, and not only on the discs, but immediately in digital form. The album can be purchased on the site and immediately downloaded to your computer. What is characteristic is that not only 320-bit MP3s are available for download, but also files of almost perfect quality: Apple Lossless and even FLAC Lossless. With such characteristics, the sound is close in quality to vinyl records.

Giants of show business ( with rare exceptions ) have not yet matured to upload their new works immediately to free access. But all gradually go to this. At least they are already abandoning DRM. McCartney's new album is not protected in any way, so it can be freely copied from device to device.
