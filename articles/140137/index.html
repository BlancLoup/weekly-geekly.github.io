NetApp FAS2240

Hooray! They waited! NetApp FAS2240-2 arrived at our NetApp Competence Center .

NetApp FAS2240-2 - the new model, which became the oldest in the younger line of FAS2000. Therefore, its appearance as a demo stand will be interesting to those who were looking for an intermediate option between FAS2XXX and FAS3XXX. Speaking of price-quality ratio, FAS2240 - works like FAS3210, but costs like FAS2040 - and this is a key advantage of this storage system.

For this reason, on the FAS2240 model, IT-GRAD formed ready-made sets of equipment for virtualization. The capacity of this model is enough to freely build the entire infrastructure of the company for up to 500 users .


In this post the main idea is that FAS2240 is available for the test! You can conduct load testing of storage systems directly in the Competence Center of IT-GRAD or get the equipment to your site.
