Open registration at Yet another Conference 2012

Today we open registration for the third technology conference of Yandex, which will be held on October 1. This year, the Yet Another Conference will be held in the 75th pavilion of the International Exhibition Center ( VVC / VDNKh ) in Moscow.

We will address the issues of mathematics in computer science, talk about distributed storage systems and computing, programming techniques, mobile technologies, front-end development, discuss the problems of testing, security and administration.

We are thinking of our conference as a platform for the exchange of knowledge and useful information in the field of technology and look forward to an active discussion and, possibly, to bitter disputes. Like last year, representatives of leading Russian and foreign Internet companies will speak at the conference, as well as academic figures.

To learn more about the speakers, follow our news on the site , as well as on Twitter , Facebook and VKontakte .

Reports at the conference will be divided into three large streams, each of which will be held in a separate room. Get ready to listen to speeches in both Russian and English. We organize an exhibition in the hall, where it will be possible not only to get acquainted with new technologies, but also try to work with them.

Participation in the conference is free - you only need to have time to register. We are waiting for developers, technical specialists and students of technical universities. Press representatives are accredited at pr@yandex-team.ru . In the subject line, specify ‚ÄúAccreditation for YaC 2012‚Äù.
