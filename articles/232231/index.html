Job


The laptop should be carried almost always. We serve dozens of call centers throughout the country for hardware and software. Including banking CC. Emergencies sometimes happen, and the laptop helps a lot. There is configured a reliable VPN-tunnel to directly control consoles. The main OS is Windows, but most of the work is done on virtual machines in our data center, or on servers across the country.

Every day we work with business-critical systems and equipment. Calm days happen, but not often. The workflow consists in testing new or faulty hardware, administering the software of customers, analyzing logs and detecting faults, communicating with vendors. Sometimes there are emergency situations, you need to respond quickly, sometimes to prepare equipment for replacement, for example, as with a server today. Often there is a work with documents for all this. Then the same prepared iron sometimes has to be mounted. When calmer days happen, you can read some educational literature, for example.

Our service department provides support for a very large range of traditional telephony systems and call center solutions. Among them are the call distribution system, the support system for automated workplaces of operators, the interactive speech interaction system, the recording system, the human resources management system of the CC. Of course, for each system we have dedicated specialists who are most competent in a particular area, but all the engineers are somehow familiar with all the products.

Plus, another unit deals with the infrastructure itself, data networks. Most of our colleagues have vast experience, there are those who have only been working in this field for 15 years, and we have been working in KROK for 15 years.

When I visit customers' sites, it is impossible to shoot there for obvious reasons. Therefore, I do not publish photos from external sites.
