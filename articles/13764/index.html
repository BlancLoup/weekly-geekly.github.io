Begun and Mail.ru have entered into an agreement under which Begun begins broadcasting contextual advertising in the GoGo.ru search engine.

Ad units "Runner" are placed in the search results in the category "Internet" and contain 10 advertising spots. Ads advertisers are placed according to the auction - from 1 to 10 places.

The Internet portal Mail.ru is the oldest partner of Runner, and many innovations that subsequently became industry standards were first used by Runner on the pages of Mail.ru. For example, for the first time in the history of the market, the Begun mail interface began to use fully behavioral advertising. Also on Mail.ru "Runner" began to place contextual advertising, tied directly to the texts of e-mails.

The GoGo.ru search engine, developed by Mail.ru, is a technological and dynamic search engine. Connecting to the Runner network of a search engine that is rapidly gaining popularity will increase the share of the audience coming to advertisers' websites from search results.
