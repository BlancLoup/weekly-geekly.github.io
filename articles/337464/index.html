We are the IT recruiters IT team at Hays, and we work with a large number of candidates every day.

And despite the fact that we live in an age of modern technology, yet, recruitment, as a process, is an integral part of business processes in companies. After all, every employer is interested in building a "best team", and this is the main task of the recruitment specialists - to find the same candidate.

At the same time, we continue to meet people who are wary of calls to HR specialists, and in general are not ready to engage in dialogue with recruiting companies: someone because of his skeptical attitude, someone is lazy or thinks that this is wasted time. And this article we want to show that we "do not bite" and can really be useful.

Telephone conversation (first contact)

We are well aware that there are times when it is not convenient for you to talk on the phone, so we are ready to offer various options: social and professional networks, various instant messengers, email, skype, after all, we can arrange for a call at a convenient time. Do not give up the opportunity to get acquainted with various vacancies, because often we can offer you 2-5 vacancies from different industries at the same time. And yes, the search can be confidential, and only we can know about this position)

How is the interview?

We are mobile, we are ready to meet not only in person, but also to communicate on Skype. Little time? No question, on average, Skype interview takes 30 minutes.
