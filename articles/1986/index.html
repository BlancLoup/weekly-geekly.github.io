Echelon spy technology began to be used in commerce

Software that recognizes human emotions and key phrases in his speech, is becoming increasingly popular and is increasingly used in call-centers of various companies. This is stated in the Forrester Research report .

Over the past year, the amount of sales of similar software all over the world reached a record high of $ 400 million. Secret technologies that were previously used in listening systems like the American Echelon or Russian SORM are now used to guess the mood of callers and quickly fulfill their moods.

To assess the mood of the subscriber, the system focuses on voice volume. In addition, there is automatic speech recognition and search for keywords and phrases on a given pattern, writes ScienceDaily .
