Perhaps this is such a fashion, but, gentlemen, we want to introduce to you another service notification of the release of new series :-) Over the past couple of weeks, this has become an unspoken trend, I guess.



We believe that you love TV shows. We love them too! We look forward to each new episode, add new release dates to the calendar and very upset when we accidentally miss them. Nobody likes to be upset, right?

Therefore, we wrote Fedor .





And if you are counting the minutes before the release of the new series ‚Äúhere in this size and translation‚Äù, or if you are interested in the fate of some specific distribution on the largest tracker of the runet - Fedor can help you.

What makes Fedor different from other services:
