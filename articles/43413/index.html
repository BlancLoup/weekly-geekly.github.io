Microblogging in Russian - now on the iPhone



The leading Russian microblogging service SMSter.ru has a version for iPhone owners.

The interface of the new version is simplified and optimized for the iPhone, but, nevertheless, it has retained most of the functional features of SMSter.ru - tapes of friends' records and those records that they recommend, comments on the records, thematic channels and much more. The version for iPhone, which is gaining popularity in Russia, is available at smster.ru and complements existing web and wap versions of the service, as well as free SMS notifications.

For those who have not yet got a device from Apple, we remind you that on SMSter.ru until November 15th there is a competition "Karma Race" where the main prize is just the new Apple iPhone 3G! There is still a chance to win it :)
