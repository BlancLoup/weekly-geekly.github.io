‚ñå DISPLAY


The phone is equipped with a 4.5 inch capacitive touchscreen display, with a high contrast IPS matrix. It has a resolution of 540x960 pixels, with a pixel density of 275 PPI.
The display is made by technology OGS. One glass solution is a technology that integrates the display and the sensor / glass into one, which will reduce the thickness of the device, make the image clearer and increase sensitivity. The screen cover is made of tempered glass.



The screen is very bright, the image clarity is good. Also pleased with the viewing angles.





Supports multitouch up to 5 touches inclusive and the ability to transfer an image to another device. A nice bonus is the presence of a light sensor, which very well adjusts the brightness of the lighting.
