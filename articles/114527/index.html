Firefox 4 Enters Final Beta Testing

Yesterday (February 25) the latest (twelfth) beta version of the Firefox 4 browser was published.

The official notes for its release mention only three significant changes from the previous beta version:

1) Increased speed when playing Flash.

2) Improved compatibility with plug-ins in hardware acceleration mode.

3) Hovering the cursor over a hyperlink now leads to displaying its URL in the lower part of the window, and not in the right part of the address bar.

In addition, a number of other changes were made to the twelfth beta , improving its stability, its speed, its compatibility; The full list of them, as usual, is available on a separate page , very extensive.
