The gyroscope in the smartphone will open a window in a new dimension

Gyroscope

The gyroscope in the iPhone 4 will bring a new revolution . In my opinion, this is the most important thing in it. The remaining moments are not so interesting. They anyway have long been in other smarts. A great body and screen is very nice, but not opening.

Window to another world


Imagine you are running an application on a smartphone and its screen becomes a window to another world for you.

For example, this is a 3D game.
You simply move the smartphone in order to see other sides of the world. You lift it up and see the sky. Lower down and see the ground. Turn around to look around.

And you can also make a change in the angle of the image visible in the screen if the user zooms in or removes the smartphone from itself.

The gyroscope just makes us understand how smart is oriented relative to the world and connect it with the virtual world.

And the accelerometer allows the program to know where and how quickly the smart was moved.

Why do I speak smart, instead of the iPhone? Because soon, the rest will also put the gyroscope in their smarts.

Although the first wave of applications will be for the iPhone 4.

I believe that many have already begun to implement this idea. And soon such programs will appear (if not already).

Augmented Reality


As you understand, to connect this with augmented reality is simple and easy. iPhone 4 itself can simply become an association with augmented reality, if others do not hurry. This will also help him a new 5M camera. This and a clear screen, more than enough to create or complement the world with such a level of quality that it will be almost indistinguishable from the present.

Welcome to the new era.
