Samsung Introduces New 845DC EVO SSD Series

Good afternoon, Habr!

Samsung introduced the new Samsung SSD 845DC EVO, designed for use in large data centers and other enterprise-class server systems, including among small and medium-sized businesses. New items carry a proprietary 3-bit NAND flash memory 10-nm class, firmware and controller, which are based on SSD Samsung PM853T.


845DC EVO is available in options with 240 GB, 480 GB and 960 GB on board. The new SSDs provide sequential data read speeds of up to 530 MB / s, and in random read mode the speed reaches 87,000 IOPS. In addition, Samsung provides 5-year drive warranty and up to 600 terabytes of TBW (Total Bytes Written). This measure of reliability reflects the amount of data that can be recorded on the SSD before the exhaustion of the resource of memory cells. The active power consumption of the Samsung 845DC EVO is approximately 4 W - this is only 25% of the volume of energy consumption of equivalent HDD for data centers.

New SSD will be available worldwide at the end of June. Prices for the domestic market so far can only be estimated in mind, based on announced prices for the USA: $ 250, $ 490 and $ 970 for versions with 240 GB, 480 GB and 960 GB, respectively.
