Sports and Android: life-saving applications

All people want to be strong and healthy, and in not so distant times it did not have to put any special effort. In everyday life, there was enough physical labor, and no one puzzled over the organization of additional workload. But times change, and many people devote their lives to mental work.

As a rule, this is accompanied by an increase in concentration on activities far from physical ones. There is nothing wrong with this, but one should not forget about the mortal body, it may still be useful. Fortunately, technologies allow us to find healthy scenarios for the use of the same mobile devices, and we'll take a look at several of them today.


Fitness Buddy: 300+ Exercises


When you first start this program will offer you to download a collection of images and supporting illustrations that make the process of learning the exercises more visual. It is worth getting used to this, since practically all the application-trainers exploit the same data distribution scheme.



The process is not burdensome, and after a few minutes you can explore the collection without connecting to the network. All available exercises, of which there are many, even in the free version, are divided into appropriate muscle groups. In addition to the images, you have a step-by-step description of each activity. After reviewing the list and selecting the appropriate options, you can add them to the list of activities as part of any workout. A fast-running search should help in this, but the interface as a whole may seem somewhat outdated.



For lovers of maximum freedom of choice, it is possible to create a training cycle from scratch, but there are several selections designed for different levels of training. The history of classes, which allows you to track your achievements, is located in the settings according to a strange whim, but for the rest, the use of the program does not cause questions. The full version, in turn, includes an even greater wealth of options for bringing your own body to the desired condition.

Noom Weight Loss Coach


If JEFIT, according to the motto, was created by athletes for athletes, then Noom pursues a more modest, but no less significant goal. This program is designed to help you lose weight, and vigorous statements when you first start to make you believe that the technique really works. With careful study, it becomes clear that Noom is good, and even the most skeptical user is infected with optimism.



In the presence of a game component, which increases the fascination of any process, as well as a modern and stylish interface, thanks to which it is a pleasure to use the program. Noom's weight loss trainer does not contain exercises as such, but its use is not limited to banal motivators and appeals not to eat after six.

On the contrary, nobody restricts the user in habits, it is enough just to write down all the consumed food in a magazine, dividing products by calorie content. The daily ration rate will be calculated automatically by the program based on the biometric data that you specify, and if you accidentally exceed the number of calories, you will be asked to walk or run, fixing movements using GPS.



Thus, Noom is not only a nutritionist and mentor, but also a sports tracker. In terms of stability of work and accuracy of positioning, the program can be compared with Endomondo and other segment leaders, so the service monetization model no longer raises questions such as when we first met. The developers provide an annual subscription fee, but there is also a lifetime license for 30 USD. It seems that this is a small price to keep fit.

Workout trainer


The last figure in our selection today belongs to the type of classic coaching programs, which is clearly indicated in the title. Being formally free, the utility forces you to spend a little time on the selection of training, since most of them are distributed on a commercial basis. Nevertheless, the Workout Trainer can be useful to those who want to not only do their own work, but also do it right.



He has a powerful potential for planning and distributing workouts according to your personal schedule. It is necessary to choose the necessary exercises, as at a certain moment the program will not hesitate to remind you of the need to perform them and will cause a fairly motivating trainer on the screen, accompanying your repetitions with voice prompts.

Each program is designed for a specific period, but many combinations are possible, and there may be as many virtual trainers as possible. In the end, your schedule is easy to clarify by switching to calendar mode and, if necessary, shifting certain classes.



The application interface is quite convenient and modern, except that the settings screen is somewhat out of the general picture. Of course, there are regularly deduced proposals for activating monthly or life-long memberships, but you can not rush with this until you decide exactly whether you need a coach in your pocket or other ways to motivate yourself for feats.
