The fastest home internet is in Japan (2 Gbit / s)



The other day it became known that the Japanese provider So-net Entertainment added to its service package a connection to the Network at speeds up to 2 Gbit / s. And this is the home Internet, and not any pilot project developed by the research institute. In this case, Sony is a partner. The new high-speed connection service was named Nuro and is already available for residents of Tokyo + residents of several neighboring prefectures.

According to the company's management, this package is designed not only for households, but also for owners of the restaurant business and other small companies. At the same time, the cost of the package is not so high: $ 51 per month when concluding a contract for 2 years (plus about the same for installation of equipment, one-time).
