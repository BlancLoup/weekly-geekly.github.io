How does it work in a Google office in Zurich?

Last week, I posted a post about a Google office in Zurich. In the comments, many habrovchan asked about the workflow in Google and about life in Zurich.

Unfortunately, I could not answer these questions, as I was just a guest. But, since there is an interest in this topic - I agreed with Nikolay, who was just my guide to Google office.
He will answer the questions of the habrasoobshchestva directly about working in an evil corporation and about moving from Russia to Switzerland.

UPD. There were more questions than I expected. Therefore, it takes more time to answer. I will post the answers in this thread, and not write in the form of comments - because the questions are often repeated.



Briefly about our hero:

In 2007 he graduated from the Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics of Moscow State University. Lomonosov, almost immediately after graduation, got a job in the Russian office of Google for the position of Software Engineer. After working for 3 years in Russia, he decided to move to Switzerland. The main incentive is raising living standards and wages.
Now he lives in Zurich for 1.5 years with his wife, working in the position of Software Engineer on the project Panoramio. No plans to return to Russia.
