March 24-26, the Innovations for Business Forum will be held in Nizhny Novgorod

March 24-26, 2010 in Nizhny Novgorod a forum will be held, the main topic of which will be a discussion of the role of innovation in the modernization of the economy. Leading Russian and Western top managers, investors, and experts will consider the impact of the crisis on Russian business and ways of post-crisis recovery through innovative implementations. The forum was organized by Marchmont Capital Partners together with UNOVA MEDIA; The general partner of the forum is the Russian Venture Company.

The main purpose of the Innovations for Business Forum is to consider topical issues and formulate innovative proposals that contribute to the development of business structures. The use of the deductive method in solving important problems has already become the hallmark of the forum stages. All questions are considered first on a global scale, then on the example of specific Russian regions and individual projects. Thus, the forum will address the development of business in a crisis, ways of bringing companies to the international market, methods of interaction of innovators with investors, ways to increase the innovation potential of the regions.

The main speakers of the forum will be Marchmont Capital Partners CEO Kendrick White, CEO of UNOVA Media and the BIT Competition Yevgeny Savin, Head of Project Analysis Services RVC Kirill Bulatov, President of the OmniGrade Rating Monitoring Agency (Moscow, Vienna) Mikhail Treivish, President science: connections (Moscow, London) Patrick Fullik, President of the Association of Business Angels of the Volga Federal District ‚ÄúStart Investments‚Äù Eduard Fiyaksel, Chief Expert for the Development of the Innovation Infrastructure of RUSNANO, Anatoly Zaikin.
