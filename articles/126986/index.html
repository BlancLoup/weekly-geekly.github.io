Firefox 7 testday

The quality control team Mozilla QA appealed to everyone who uses the beta version of Firefox 7 to participate in Firefox 7 Testday. This event, in which Mozilla employees participate, is designed to help the testing team confirm bug fixes and check for unconfirmed bugs for reproducibility.
There is a case for everyone: unconfirmed bugs at the moment 108, corrected 90 - and all this must be rechecked.
A list of all the bugs here ( fixed here ).

The event will be held on Friday, August 26 from 1:00 pm to 5:00 am the next day (Moscow time), August 27th. If you have time and desire to help, go at that time to the #testday IRC channel on irc.mozilla.org. This can be done without an irts client, simply by clicking on the link .

Download the beta version in any language here .
Release of Firefox 7 is expected in early October.
