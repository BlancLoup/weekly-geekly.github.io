How to launch and promote your application based on the SAP Cloud Platform using Skolkovo and SAP

This year, SAP is actively talking about the SAP Cloud Platform - our new cloud platform for developing and deploying business applications on the platform as a service or PaaS (platform as a service) model. In October, together with the Skolkovo Foundation, we launched a new program where we help Russian developers launch, develop or transfer their solution to the SAP Cloud Platform. Companies that will go through all stages of the program will be supported in promoting solutions among SAP customers and partners, as well as in marketing and PR.



What is the SAP Cloud Platform


The SAP Cloud Platform is an SAP open cloud platform that provides data storage and processing capabilities in SAP HANA and ASE, as well as in PostgreSQL, MongoDB and Hadoop open source systems. The SAP Cloud Platform is designed to develop mobile and HTML applications that can be easily integrated with any cloud or local systems thanks to the integration service integrated into the SAP Cloud Platform. Currently, the SAP Cloud Platform provides about 70 different services, including the Internet of Things, forecasting algorithms, machine translation and other machine learning methods, has its own WebIDE development environment and a tool for rapid prototyping Build, supports the development of applications in Java, XSJS, C ++ , Python, Ruby, Node.js, Go, PHP, .Net.

The SAP Cloud Platform can be used as an environment for developing your own corporate application, as well as for transferring an existing service from another platform in order to integrate the solution with SAP enterprise systems (ERP, CRM, etc.).
One of the advantages of SCP is its integration with the SAP HANA database. SAP HANA is a powerful big data analysis platform that can quickly load and analyze various types of information.

Startups and companies that qualify for the joint program of SAP and the Skolkovo Foundation will receive preferential access to the SAP Cloud Platform and expert support for developing or postponing their solution.

How to get into the program


The first stage of the program has already started and will last until November 30 - this is accepting applications to the program. To do this, participants need to fill out information about the company and send a presentation about their application through a special form on the registration page. In the presentation it is important to give a detailed description of your solution, both in terms of business and in terms of technical implementation.

The second stage will last from November 2 to December 22 - during this time, experts from the IT cluster of the Skolkovo Foundation and SAP will select the received applications remotely or in the form of in-person meetings and interviews.

The main stage of the program will begin from the end of December and will last for the whole 2018. During this time, participants will go through the stages of solution validation, will be engaged in the transfer and adaptation of their solutions on the SAP Cloud Platform platform. Teams that successfully pass all these stages will receive marketing and PR support from the Skolkovo Foundation and SAP to enter the challenging market of corporate clients.

You can apply for participation on the official website .
