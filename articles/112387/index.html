Hey. Two months ago, I got into a kind of hell with the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa survey service, I succumbed and gave all my free time to development. So twiq.ru was born

The main difference between the service and the competitors is that it is not just a site where you fill in the form and force other people to click on the answer options. Everything is different here. In general, after registering, you can no longer visit us, but nevertheless, you can use almost the entire set of functions successfully.



The secret is simple, after registering, all your special tweets, about it below, will be collected by the bot and beautifully drawn up on the site as a survey, and all the answers to this tweet will be carefully sorted into categories, and after the time you specify, a link to the results will appear on Twitter survey. It is not even necessary for your followers to know how best to respond so that the answer is credited, the bot will try to recognize the answer and send it to the desired category. The poll template on twitter is:

Of course, you can create polls directly on the site. This method has additional advantages:
First, you can specify similar answer options through a slash to increase the chances that the bot recognizes the user's response. For example, in the classic holivar question about operating systems, it would be logical to write Linux / Linux so that both words are considered as one option.
