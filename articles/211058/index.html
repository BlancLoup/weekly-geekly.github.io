How to get a free professional account in Wunderlist

Many use the task manager Wunderlist. It bribes with cross-platform and free of charge, but for the money such interesting functions are offered, such as adding files, delegating tasks, unlimited subtasks and communication. It shouldn't be a pity to give $ 5 a month (or $ 50 a year) for a product that improves productivity, but there is a free way to get a professional account.
It seemed to me that it is not obvious, because I, like most, use desktop and mobile clients, and there is no such function there. But if you visit the web version of Wunderlist, you can find interesting functionality.
Go to www.wunderlist.com and log in. Click on your avatar in the upper left corner of the page and in the menu select "Get Wunderlist Pro for free."

Add the addresses of your friends and get 1 month for each (limit - 12 months).

Yes, an invited friend will also receive 1 month PRO account.
