Concept idealized mobile phone for seniors

In addition to this topic .
Older people need from the phone only two functions - to call and answer the call. It should be noted that when you really need to call the situation, there are quite extreme ones.
For example - the door has snowed in and it is impossible to get home, and a piece of paper with a phone as luck would have it at home. Or something else that requires an urgent call.

And here will help my concept - the phone for an elderly person. The user interface is extremely simple:

There is a screen of high contrast with auto-backlit and large letters, as the elderly are hard to read. The alternative is to display a photo of the subscriber.

Two large backlit buttons:
Green - make a call / answer a call.
Red - end the conversation.

The phone connects to the computer and a list of all subscribers that can call and what will be displayed (number / name / picture) is set there.

When you click on the green button, the number is dialed using an autodial.

The phone itself works for a very long time without recharging, as it is very simple, it has an optional GPRS beacon that works when called.
