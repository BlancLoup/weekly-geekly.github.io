Over the past ten centuries, life expectancy has increased slightly.

I repeatedly came across a mention of the fact that people used to live until the age of 30, gave birth to many, many children, but almost all of them died.
How could this happen at the same time? It is not clear, it would seem that it takes quite a lot of time to give birth to and feed a dozen children, the nursing mother usually does not become pregnant, biologically people could not change much over a thousand years, but few people think about arithmetic.
People in general tend to believe in self-contradictory statements, apparently this is some kind of evolutionary meaning.
I decided to check whether everything was so bad.
It turned out not quite.
