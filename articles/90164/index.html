SunCalc - Solar Calculator

I want to share with you a small but useful projector that I developed at my leisure - the solar calculator SunCalc .



SunCalc shows on the map for the selected location and date the trajectory of the sun and the phase of illumination during the day (and a couple of nice little things).

His idea came after I needed to find a good place in Crimea for shooting a frame of the sun going off into the sea at sunset (for the video, they shot here in the end) - it turned out that all the existing tools for this were very outdated. I wanted to create my own, making it the best one can imagine, found a good page with a bunch of astronomical formulas , took up the development in free evenings and this is the result.

Opportunities


  • draws on the map a beautiful vector graph with the position of the sun at dawn, sunset and at the selected time (yellow, orange and red line), projection of the sun's trajectory (orange arc) and its spread throughout the year (yellow area around it); the closer the point on the graph is to the center of the circle, the higher at this moment the sun is above the horizon
  • hovering over the sunset / dawn line shows the corresponding spread of positions
  • It shows the illumination (darkness, twilight, daylight) in color on the time slider
  • You can see the time not only of dawn and dusk, but also of 3 types of twilight (civil, navigation, astronomical) and total darkness
  • shows a link to the weekly weather forecast (cloudiness, precipitation, fog) in the selected location
  • able to determine the location (click on the appropriate field -> Detect my location)
  • You can also choose a location by searching, auto-detecting, clicking on the map, or dragging a marker.
  • permalinks for a specific place / time and back / forward in the browser work
