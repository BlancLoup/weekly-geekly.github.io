March 9 (Monday) 2009: Ubuntu Testing Day

March 9, 2009 announced the next day of testing Ubuntu.

In Russia, March 9th is a day off . So just like sober up after the Great Women's Holiday (tm), do not take the trouble to download a fresh alpha CD with your favorite u / k / xbuntu and carefully study it.

Burn the disc and boot from it on all computers that you have under the other. Carefully check that all the equipment available to you is recognized by the LiveCD and is working correctly.

After that, put a virtualbox-ose package on the working system, then install fresh Ubuntu into it and check if all the features of the programs you use are in place? Do all the claimed functions work?

Check to see if all the bugs that you ran into during the operation of previous versions of the distribution are corrected.

Carefully write down all the identified shortcomings, create a bug-report in the Launchpad , or at least write it on the forum in the section " Testing Ubuntu ".

If you are in trouble with English, go to the # ubuntu-testing IRC channel and find out if you need any help.
