Flashlight Video Recorder is a flashlight with a built-in video camera and night shooting function. 17 infrared LEDs around the main light source provide night lighting (as a night vision device), which allows you to shoot up to 13 minutes of MPEG4 video with a resolution of 640x480 and a frequency of 30 frames per second in total darkness.
