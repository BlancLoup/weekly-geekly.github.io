How I became a designer in six months

I did not complete the design courses and did not study design at the institute, but I managed to complete my own course of study for 6 months, I would note that in the process of learning I was simultaneously engaged in a full day of my main work. Although I did not think that I was ready to get a new job as a designer, still I was very lucky and managed to find a good place.

Of course, I understand that I do not have the skills that many professional designers have learned for several years and have a great work experience, however, I feel that I can work at a good level, creating a good design. I am currently designing websites.

This article will help novice designers to take the first steps in studying design on their own; here you will find links to those books and resources that helped me in studying and were helpful. I note that the links point to English-language resources that I used for self-study.

Level One: learn to see


The main mistake that novice designers make is an attempt to immediately begin by studying Photoshop and drawing design. But Photoshop will not make you a designer, just as buying paints and brushes will not make you an artist. Therefore, we begin with the development of the very basics of design.

Learning to draw


To accomplish this task, you will not need to make great efforts or sign up for the school of artists, you will not have to paint still lifes and paint oil paintings, we will limit ourselves to studying the basics of drawing, which will be enough to make a sketch on paper. Abracadabra like Lorem Ipsum should alarm, it is a direct pointer to a bad designer. A good designer should be able to communicate well with his users, he sees the whole process of evaluating his design as a whole and does not miss such details as a presentation text. Each word is chosen thoughtfully, wisely, and the text itself is written with the expectation that people will read it. Do not use the abstruse language used by teachers in school.

Level Two: learn to use Photoshop and Illustrator


Having studied the basics you can begin to master the programs for creating a design. Learning is best to start with Illustrator (a program that designers use to draw various icons and logos). A huge amount of educational materials has been written on this topic; I will cite just a few references to books that were especially useful. You can use the resources that seem most appropriate.

It is time to determine the scope of design, which you will be doing in the future. Take time to explore different areas of design (websites, graphics, mobile applications) and choose for yourself what brings the most positive emotions.

Level 4: Portfolio Creation


You do not need to finish art school to get a job as a designer, but you cannot do without a good portfolio.

How to create a newbie portfolio with no work experience? To do this, it is not necessary to have finished projects behind you or to work with real clients. Just do your own projects.

Here is what you can do to gain experience:

- Find a site with a bad design and redo it.
- Find charity projects and offer to make a design for free.
- Practice on sites for designers .
- Join the team at StartupWeekend .
- Perform tasks from this book .

Do not include absolutely all of your work in the portfolio, select only the best.
At the initial stages do not try to be original, it will come with time, with experience, with skill. At first, everyone learns the basics and repeats simple things behind the teacher, and when enough experience has accumulated, you can move on to more advanced and independent work.
