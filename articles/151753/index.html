Humble Indie Bundle 6

A new bundle has been released. Six new (and not so), great games and five sound tracks.

  • DustForce - Super-speed janitor-acrobat
  • Rochard - Miner with a gravity gun
  • Shatter - Vector Hypertext Fidonet Arkanoid
  • SPAZ - Asteroids with megalomania
  • Torchlight - Hack-n-slash in the dungeon
  • Vessel - Everyday plumbing




DustForce goes as a bonus, for which you need to pay more than the average (~ $ 5.65 at the time of writing).
Charity goes for EFF and Child's Play

Vessel is currently available only under Windows, but it will open in a day for Mac with Linux.

Saud tracks are available for every game except Vessel.

Given that recently there are accounts in the Humble Bundle - you can register before purchase. And also add to your account the bundles purchased earlier, to simplify access to them (no need to wool mail).
