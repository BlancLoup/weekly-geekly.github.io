Switching layouts with CapsLock on Ubuntu 14.04

Found a simple solution.
Procedure:

1. Install gnome-tweak-tool.
2. Go to the "Keyboard" section
3. In the "Behavior of the CapsLock key" list item, select the "CapsLock off" item. I emphasize, just such a point, and not just "Off"



4. Go to the settings ubunt and calmly assign CapsLock as a button to switch the layout. It is recognized as 0xff, and not as ‚ÄúCapsLock‚Äù, but now it will work calmly.
