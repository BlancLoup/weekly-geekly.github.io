There are problems with Skype


At the moment, Skype is not working around the world.
In the relevant topic on Reddit, users from different countries have unsubscribed. A little earlier, it was possible to use the web version , but at the moment it also periodically falls under the increased load.


According to the official version , instant messages should work, the Skype team is aware of the problems and is trying to solve them.

Upd. Microsoft Russia apologized to users.

Friends, we are working on troubleshooting issues that prevent users from logging in and using #Skype . We apologize.

- Microsoft Russia (@RusMicrosoft) September 21, 2015

Upd. At 18:00 Moscow time, the Skype team reported that they identified a problem related to the network and are working to fix it.
