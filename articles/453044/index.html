The long-awaited step towards complexly structured documents (+ video)


With this article on Habr√©, we are very pleased to announce to you, friends, that we have moved from patterned rigidly structured documents to the recognition of various complexly structured documents. And this, believe me, is a completely different song! For details, welcome under cat.


In each of our articles on Habr√©, we do not get tired of repeating that our primary goal is to automate data entry from any documents in natural uncontrolled conditions without the need for special equipment. In just a few years, we managed to bring the ID recognition system to the industrial level and now most financial applications (including even some applications of national importance) use our technology to speed up and simplify work with the application.


For this year, our global goal is to recognize any documents, without additional requirements for templates and forms. As always, recognition should be performed directly on the device (be it a mobile device or a powerful server). Having spent most of the time on internal research, reworking almost completely our basic Hieroglyph technology, we created the first version of the universal document recognition program - Smart DocumentReader.


What documents does Smart DocumentReader recognize?


Architecturally, the Smart DocumentReader program does not contain any restrictions on the types of supported documents and allows you to customize the recognition of any complexly structured documents. Documents may contain various semantic elements: tables, checkboxes, handwritten areas, etc. Although, one limitation, rather caused by the hardware features of mobile devices, is present in our program: the maximum physical size of recognizable documents is A4 format. But, you see, this is not a strong limitation from the point of view of bureaucracy in the Russian Federation. All the main financial documents are printed on A4 pages: a certificate in form 2-NDFL, invoice, invoice, act, waybill (TTN), waybill in form TORG12, universal transfer document (DFC), charter, contract , invoice, application form, etc.


Recognition of certificates 2-NDFL


As a first example, we set up the Smart DocumentReader program for recognizing certificates in the form 2-NDFL. From the point of view of practical use, this is a very popular document, which is required, for example, by banks when applying for large loans, by the state to receive tax deductions.


From the point of view of the internal structure, the 2-NDFL reference is an excellent example of a complexly structured document: it contains mandatory and optional fields, several tables, there is a logical connection between individual attributes, a large number of recognized fields.


Smart DocumentReader supports the recognition of multi-page documents. To do this, the program should alternately show all pages of the document. Upon the appearance of new pages, the overall recognition result will be updated with new data.



Like all of our previous products, Smart DocumentReader runs on a wide range of processor architectures running various operating systems. Today we support the Elbrus, Komdiv, SPARC, MIPS, ARM, x86 hardware platforms, Sailfish Mobile OS RUS (Aurora), iOS, Android, Elbrus, Linux, Windows, macOS, Solaris operating systems . As for the recognition speed, on a mobile phone a one-page document 2-NDFL is recognized in 3-5 seconds.
