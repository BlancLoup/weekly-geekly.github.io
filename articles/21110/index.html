Blog spam changes goals and tactics

Probably, there is not a single more or less popular blogger who has not encountered spam in the comments.

The fight against them is quite simple: Wordpress can send for pre-moderation or immediately nail comments with certain words, as well as comments with more than two links. In addition, even a simple captcha (this is such a picture with clumsy letters, as I have in the comments), most spambots stop.

Posting links manually and spamming in the "name" field only works with those who don't follow their blogs at all.
