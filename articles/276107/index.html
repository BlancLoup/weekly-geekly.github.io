Who stayed with us on New Year's Eve

So, on New Year's Eve, about 250,000 foreigners registered with MegaFon networks. We calculated that this year more foreigners came to celebrate holidays in Russia than in the past: it seems that because of the sharply cheaper Russian currency, holidays in our country became financially attractive for foreign guests.
It is expected that most of them came from neighboring countries: Belarus, Kazakhstan, Estonia, Ukraine. However, thousands of people from more distant states, such as Italy and the United States, clinked glasses with our compatriots.

What about us?
And what about our citizens? Where did they catch the 2016? It is logical to assume that not everyone could afford to travel abroad. If a year ago, 756,000 MegaFon subscribers were leaving for holidays to other countries, now there are only 635,000. At the same time, residents of one Russian region began to drive more by 16% than in 2015 - the Far Eastern one. Trips to Asia hit the wallet of Russians not as much as in Western countries.
