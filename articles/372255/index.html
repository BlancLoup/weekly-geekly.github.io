Announced by Sony Xperia XA Ultra

The line of Sony Xperia X, which initially consisted of models of Xperia X, Xperia X Performance and Xperia XA, added to the new device - Xperia XA Ultra. This smartphone has all the advantages of the original model (screen bent at the edges of the 2.5D and the absence of side frames), but comes in a larger package with a larger display diagonal.





Sony Xperia XA Ultra has a six-inch screen with a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels, and the pixel density is 367 ppi. The stuffing is powerful: the heart of the smartphone is the eight-core MediaTek MT6755 processor, plus there is 3 GB of RAM, 16 GB of internal memory. There is an SD-slot for memory cards, NFC-chip and support for Bluetooth 4.1 standard.


Sony Xperia XA Ultra and Sony Xperia XA

The main camera has a resolution of 21.5 megapixels, and the front - 16 megapixels. A front flash will help improve the quality of self in low light. And for a comfortable selfie shooting, gesture control is provided: raising your hand, you will begin the countdown until the shutter is triggered. There is a special shooting mode in flash, Slow Sync, in which the smartphone will allow you to get a well-lit background when shooting in low light.



The battery at 2700 mAh will provide about two days of work. Xperia XA Ultra supports quick charging through the device Quick Charger UCH122 - a ten-minute charge will allow the smartphone to work up to 5.5 hours in stand-alone mode.



The smartphone will be available in three colors - golden, black and white. Price and sales start date will be announced.
