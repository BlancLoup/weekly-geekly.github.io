Vyzhmi MAXIMum from IT

It has long been a secret that IT departments are often perceived by company management simply as serving units, the benefits of which are only stable work of mail and office programs. Have you ever thought that IT departments can be full-fledged participants in a company's business, capable of influencing business processes, optimizing them and reducing costs? Of course, they thought and, perhaps, even looked for and tried to apply some solutions, because we all secretly dream that computers and IT technologies solve as many questions for us as possible, preferably in one place and without any extra effort.

We thought, searched, tried, but each time we faced the same problem - integration. How to integrate databases and programs of different departments, make them understand each other and function normally. There are really many solutions, but most of them cannot solve this problem in a complex way. But storing data in one place, as well as simplicity and clarity in use and operation, is not all. Comprehensive this solution makes the possibility of "seamless" integration of products using a single platform, i.e. organization of uninterrupted interaction of various programs to solve specific problems.



And, finally, on the basis of this platform several products were implemented that greatly simplify and optimize work. These are EAM (corporate asset management - with its implementation, a reduction in time and financial costs by 20-30%), ITAM (IT asset management), Service Desk (quality management of the services provided, both within the company and external - provided to other companies) .

At the same time, Maximo does not require cardinal changes and business restructuring and does not replace other specialized applications that exist in the enterprise (accounting, personnel, etc.).
