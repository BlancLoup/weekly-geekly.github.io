Cooling system and noise level





The cooling system implemented in the laptop, one of the best in its class. It includes a massive radiator and two large fans. Fan speed depends on laptop usage activity. During operation, the heating level of the components is within the normal range. If the temperature rises to critical values, protection will work and the laptop will turn off.



When using a laptop in stress mode, it is absolutely not hot! The only place, significantly heated, was the rear grille. The noise level also impressed us ... Yes, at a processor temperature of 75 ¬∞ C, the fan noise is audible, but comparable to the noise of ordinary office computers.

Battery life

Do not pay attention to the results of third-party tests, the actual battery life at maximum load (playing at max graphics settings) is 50 minutes. In Battery Saving mode and the minimum illumination of the laptop can stretch up to three hours. When viewing Full HD video (including 3D), with a maximum display brightness, the operation time is up to one and a half hours.
