Acer Aspire Revo 3600 Dual Core Nettop



In the near future, Acer is going to start selling a new modification of the Aspire Revo nettop. Externally, the new product does not differ from its predecessor. But inside ...

Unlike the existing model, which is based on the single-core Intel Atom 230 (1.6 GHz), the novelty will be built on the dual-core - Atom 330 (1.6 GHz). At the same time, the platform will remain old: nVidia Ion with the GeForce 9400M.

Compared to the Aspire Revo, the Aspire Revo 3600 also doubles the amount of RAM (up to 4GB) and the capacity of the hard drive (from 160 GB to 320 or 500 GB). In addition, the updated nettop will acquire a Wi-Fi module.

The upgraded Aspire Revo will be demonstrated in two days at the IFA Berlin. Probably at the same time we find out the price and the date of commencement of sales.
