Internet and religion

Any knowledge system, scientific or religious, is designed to give a person a coordinate system, to help navigate the unknown. The Internet is now developing so rapidly that scientific approaches in its study simply do not work. Search engines rules, interfaces, visitors' interests, development tools, etc. - everything is constantly changing. Internet research is simply impossible; you can only watch: statistics, polls, opinions. Not for nothing is trend watching becoming a global trend :)

In such conditions, the best coordinate system is provided by religion, or rather, an object-oriented approach and mass parallels with religions. How to explain what is Yandex or Google, talking about different services, it is easier to show them in action. Do you want to go up in Google - make a sacrifice on Blogspot, in Yandex - on the People. Analyzing the situation is useless - you can do everything you can and wait for the mercy of the gods.
