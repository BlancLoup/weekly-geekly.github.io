Why exactly Office365 business?


Those familiar with Microsoft's Office know that there are several options for acquiring it - for any needs: Office 2013 for home and business, Office 2013 standard and, in fact, Office365 business itself. The main drawback of the Office 2013 standard is the high purchase price, the costs are paid off only after 4 years.
