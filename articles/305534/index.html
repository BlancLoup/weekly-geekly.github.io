Clojure course materials



In 2014, a group of professional developers at Clojure under the leadership of Dmitry Bushenko conducted the first and only online courses on Clojure in Russian.

Especially for the course, we recorded 71 original videos with a total duration of 17 hours 25 minutes and 44 seconds. The course was a success - more than 200 people came, but the materials were not published in the public domain.

This site - those very materials.

We publish them as they are. The lectures themselves are coherent, complete and self-sufficient. They are almost not outdated (as of June 2016), although, of course, many new things have appeared. Tasks can also be resolved - these are exactly the files that were given to the course participants in 2014.
