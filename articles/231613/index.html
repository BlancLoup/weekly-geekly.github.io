SERPClick: Promotion of behavioral factors



The corporate blog of the company ALTWeb Group continues to talk about new products. This time we will talk about SERPClick - a system designed to control behavioral factors. About what it is and how it works, we will tell in this post.


SERPClick solves the problem of promoting behavioral factors.



The company MegaIndex has developed an alternative tool to promote behavioral factors - SERPClick. This tool speaks with search engines in their language and allows you to continue to promote your sites to the top in the face of significantly sophisticated search engine ranking algorithms.

SERPClick system takes into account the requirements of search algorithms, such as the region of users of the region of the visited site, the time spent on the page and the absence of impact traces. SERPClick distributes specific tasks to users, which improve behavioral factors and directly affect the promotion of the site to the top. Tasks are based on a preliminary analysis of your site, which the system conducts automatically, identifying all the requests for which the site is visible and assigning the necessary number of clicks in order to make the promotion natural for the search engine.

The principle of operation of SERPClick is simple: interested users come to you and improve your SEO performance. However, taking into account how many of these indicators search engines now take into account - SERPClick technology is really unique, because it allows you to influence all 5 critical parameters of behavioral factors:

  • CTR
  • Number of views
  • Session duration
  • Session depth
  • Return from your site to search results
