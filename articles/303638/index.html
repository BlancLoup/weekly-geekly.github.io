Joker 2016 Java Conference: Peter, October 14-15

October 14 and 15 in St. Petersburg will be held Java-conference Joker 2016 . Java speakers from Russia, USA and Europe will come to Joker to share their thoughts and ideas with you.

Joker ‚Ññ4. Bigger, stronger, more interesting


This will be the fourth Joker conference in St. Petersburg. Speakers and reports, sponsors and stands, participants and experts will be more than they have been in past times, so everyone who came will have something to do! The format will remain the same: 2 days, reports of 60 minutes, breaks for half an hour - so that the head does not overheat and there is time to calmly communicate with everyone. We tried the format for the first time with long breaks for the past Joker, and everyone liked it so much that we will definitely leave it.

Speakers


The team of Joker speakers is today, undoubtedly, the main pride of our conference. At the moment (June 20) we have received confirmation from 12 speakers - this is approximately 30% of the planned number of speakers.



New to Joker


As always, several interesting English-speaking speakers will come to visit us at once:
  • Sam Aaron is a Cambridge researcher from Sonic Pi , a system for creating music in Live Coding mode;
  • Charles Nutter - Java Champion, Red Hat JVM Engineer, JRuby Development Team Lead;
  • Rafael Winterhalter - Java Champion, a JVM-enthusiast, author of the ByteBuddy library for code generation in runtime;
  • Andres Almiray is Java Champion, a contributor to Groovy, Griffon, JMatter, DbUnit and other famous projects.


Joker veterans


  • Alexey Shipilev is a Performance Engineer at Oracle, the author of a whole series of interesting speeches and articles about the guts of JVM and JDK, one of the strongest (if not the strongest) Russian speakers. Will perform on Joker for the fourth time.
  • Oleg Anastasyev is a leading Odnoklassniki developer, an expert in distributed systems. Will perform on Joker for the third time.
  • Vladimir Krasilshchik is an architect at Luxoft, an expert on Vert.x, logging and monitoring. Will perform on Joker for the fourth time.
  • Vladimir Ivanov - Oracle JVM Engineer, G1 GC expert and invokedynamic. Will perform on Joker for the third time.
  • Vladimir Sitnikov is a Performance Engineer at NetCracker, an expert on full-stack profiling and PostgeSQL. Will perform on Joker for the third time.
  • Alexey Zinoviev - Java / BigData-trainer company EPAM. Will perform on Joker for the third time.
  • Kirill Tolkachev - lead engineer at Alpha Laboratories, a specialist in microservices and DevOps, editor and permanent presenter of the podcast ‚ÄúFlying‚Äù . Will perform on Joker for the second time.
  • Alexander Tarasov is a leading engineer at Alpha Laboratory, a DevOps and containerisation specialist. Will perform on Joker for the second time.


Topics


Traditionally on Joker there will be many reports on the following topics:
  • JVM application performance and optimization;
  • JDK / JVM internals;
  • Alternative JDK / JVM implementations;
  • Useful tools and frameworks;
  • Spring and its components.


In addition, by popular demand, this time we will add several new topics:
  • Java application architecture;
  • Enterprise Java;
  • Problems & Solutions;
  • DevOps & Cloud.




Become a speaker


Reception reports open. If you want to talk about something to the Russian Java-community, rather send us a report ! The advantage will be given to those speakers whose topics will seem the most interesting to the program committee. Other things being equal, preference will be given to those speakers who will provide recordings of their past (successful!) Speeches: cool performances in the past are one of the important criteria that our program committee has traditionally been guided by. Beginners should not be afraid either - we will definitely communicate with everyone.
