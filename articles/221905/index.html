Results of the InterSystems nomination at Golden Byte 2014

On April 26, the final of the International Computer Talent Championship " Golden Byte 2014 ", organized by the Computer Academy "Step", took place in the Kiev City Teacher's House. There were 9 nominations at the competition, and one of them - NoSQL Expert - was devoted to the use of Intersystems products: Cach√© and GlobalsDB.

The theme of the competition this year was ‚ÄúChanging the world‚Äù, i.e. the works submitted to the jury should somehow improve, facilitate, secure, simplify interaction with the outside world. So, let's see how the winners offered to change the world ...

At the finals of the competition 5 works were presented, 4 of which were carried out by development teams and 1 - individual. All the works were interesting, each in its own way. It was clear that the participants spent a lot of time preparing the presentation and tried to present their projects as best as possible, more interesting, more vividly.

Third place went to the project of Nikita Savchenko from Kiev, KPI "GlobalsDB admin" - an interface for the administrator of the GlobalsDB database. A pleasant variety after working with a tabular representation of globals was a graphical representation of trees, which allows you to "travel" from one node to another and at the same time shows the entire set of indices, starting from the root. The application allows, without departing from the cash register, quickly view, edit, add and delete nodes. And all this is implemented in an intuitive interface that can be controlled using gestures or a keyboard. In addition to the desktop version of the admin, an application for Android mobile devices is also available. Presentation of the project. More information about the project can be found at: zitros.github.io/globalsDB-Admin-NodeJS .


The second place was taken by the project team ITHead (Chernyshov Stanislav, Slobodenyuk Zoya, Uchuvatov Vadim) from the branch of the Academy "Step" in Odessa, "uControl" - a mobile application for identifying the location of a mobile phone. It allows the user to add a user of another phone to the list to track his movements, mark control points for the route (for example, from school home), danger zones (for example, construction sites or game clubs) and the time it takes for the route to pass. Thus, it is possible to always know where the owner of a phone is and control its movements along a predetermined route. Of course, adding a new phone for tracking is done exclusively with the consent of its owner. The video .


And finally, the first place was taken by the project of the Needrise team (Gulay Igor, Masliy Eugene, Mirda Arthur) from the branch of the Academy "Step" in Lviv, "Thousand Roads" - a site related to travel and tourism, which is an application for social networks Facebook , Odnoklassniki, VKontakte. This application allows you to create your own routes for "drivers" and friends-passengers join them, thus facilitating the search for routes, fellow travelers and interesting places to visit. Presentation of the project. More information about the project can be found at: th-roads.com
