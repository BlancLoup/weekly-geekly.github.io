E-Style Telecom at the 22nd international communications exhibition

From 11 to 14 May 2010 The 22nd Svyaz-Expocomm 2010 International Exhibition was held at the Expocenter.
E-Style Telecom, a participant in the Svyaz-Expocomm 2010 exhibition, presented a virtual tour of its third data center in Moscow.



e-Style Telecom is a telecom operator with a full range of telecommunication services. The company's presentation at the exhibition was made with a bias on the company's data center services: server colocation, telecommunication racks (cabinets) rental, server leasing, virtual dedicated servers.

The stand of e-Style Telecom - 2275, was located in the second hall of the second pavilion of the exhibition. It was one of the most visited and filled exhibition halls, as its participants and guests noted.

Moreover, more than 500 companies from more than 25 countries of the world were represented at the exhibition.
Most of the participants are from Russia, China, Taiwan and Germany.

"Sviaz-Expocomm 2010" - was held in a big way, starting from luxurious stands to the program of the event. The importance and importance of the event was added by the presence of high-ranking officials of the state.
The first day of the exhibition was the most official and tense, both for the organizers and for the participants. The building of the Expocentre was literally crammed with officials, representatives of the management of the companies - participants, invited. And only in the afternoon, after the completion of the official part, the exhibition earned in its normal mode. The second and third days were very lively, people approached, were interested in services, argued and gave advice. And only the final, fourth day of the exhibition was the most free, specialists, schoolchildren and just amateurs came.
