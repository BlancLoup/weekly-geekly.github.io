The presentation of the BlackBerry Classic will begin today at 18:00



The Canadian company Blackberry (ex-RIM), today officially launches the sale of the BlackBerry Classic. On this occasion, a presentation will be launched in New York, Frankfurt and Singapore. Start start at 10:00 EST (18:00 Moscow time) .

Resolution: 720x720pix.
