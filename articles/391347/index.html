Xiaomi Mi5 - unpacking, benchmarks



The newest smartphone from Xiaomi is Mi5, a white version with 32 GB of memory (glass version, yes, the simplest one) in my hands!

  • Screen: 5.15 inch 1920x1080, 428 PPI
  • RAM: 3 GB LPDDR4
  • Storage: 32 GB, UFS2.0
  • Main camera: 16 megapixel stabilization in 4 axes
  • Front camera: 4 megapixel

Chip Review


Fingerprint scanner works well. To register a finger, you need about 12 touches. Unlock almost instant. Some feared that to activate you will need to hold your finger top down - this is not at all the case, just one touch is enough. However, if you keep your finger very flat all 12 times during registration, then when you touch it at an angle of 45 degrees and more, your finger may not recognize it. In general, work is very comfortable.

The decrease in speed starts only in the Burst shot mode after 40-50 frames - the speed of photographing decreases due to buffer overflow. Otherwise, everything is fine.
