The first step is to want. We are all doing fine with this item, but this is only part of the solution . In fact, if you desire wrong, then aggravate the situation.
Here's how to do it right.

1) Desire (but do not stop at this)


At the beginning of everything are desires. But if this is all - you are really in trouble.
A positive attitude is largely necessary, because a negative point of view increases the likelihood that we will abandon the case, or even do not proceed to it.

But when a positive attitude turns into a fantasy, the situation deteriorates very quickly. Yes, dreaming of success is bad.

Again and again the results were unchanged. Positive fantasies, desires and dreams, divorced from assessments based on previous experience, did not turn into motivation, leading to a more energetic and interesting life. They were transformed into something completely opposite.

Why? Derek Sievers explained this in his remarkable TED talk .

The emotional brain simply cannot distinguish fantasy from reality.

When you dream, the ancient parts of the mind believe that you have already reached the goal. Therefore, motivation does not increase, but decreases.

So: one of the reasons for the low productivity of positive fantasies is that they do not create the energy necessary to strive for the realization of the desired.

Sorry, Sekret fans - it just doesn't work. Dreams transform positive thinking into the usual attempts to pass the desirable to the real.

So if the method does not work, why do we still use it? Simple and clear: it is uplifting (like regular snacking or checking mail 216 times in an hour, this seems significant).

Dreams of a beautiful future seemed protection from sadness for a short time, but only kindled it in the future. They coincided with a short period of pleasure, which inevitably eroded and led to increasing depression.

3) Provide obstacles


Oettingen calls this "mental opposition." You need to leisurely think about the dirty tricks that can stand in the way of achieving the goal.

And now something really interesting: some people do it, and their motivation is growing. At the same time, others stack up with her fall.

Does this mean that the technique is less productive? No, it says she is adorable. Why?
People who did not experience an upswing realized that their current goal was unrealizable.
Mental opposition does not only motivate people. It also helps to realize what goals really worth striving for.

When people looked at difficulties and understood that they had every chance to overcome them (‚ÄúI want to get a promotion‚Äù), the motivation grew.

Mental opposition is so effective, for it allows you to combine desires and reality. This is a stress test for a desired goal.
