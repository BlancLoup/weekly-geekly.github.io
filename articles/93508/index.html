Search for a responsible structure


The Resolution says that the state registration is carried out "by the Ministry of Communications and Informatization or an organization authorized by it ". The Ministry of Communications shifted this function to the BelGIE (State Telecommunications Inspection). On the website of this structure there is an order of registration of legal entities . After filling out and signing all the documents, the time of the visit was agreed upon.

Site registration


To register a site, you must first have a registrant and a data center (also known as a data center). When creating a registrant of an individual, the required fields are the name of the owner of the resource, the country code and e-mail address. It is quite successful that an individual is created without providing passport data, although this contradicts the Appendix to the Regulations.



The data center (Data Processing Center) requires the specification of the full name, network address space of resources and equipment located in the data center, such as this data center and its address. Moreover, as an address, you can specify geographic coordinates.



So, after the institution of the registrant and data center, you can register the site. The screenshot shows which fields you need to fill out.



The worst of all this was the need to register e-mails and the lack of clarity about the need to register domains of the third and deeper levels. Free hosting and mail services were at risk.
