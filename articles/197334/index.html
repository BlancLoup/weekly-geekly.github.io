The process of testing mobile applications


Mobile testing is a complex process: dozens of different screen resolutions, hardware differences, several versions of operating systems, different types of Internet connection, sudden breaks in communications. Therefore, we have 8 people working in the testing department (0.5 testers for a programmer), and a dedicated test lead keeps track of its development and processes.
