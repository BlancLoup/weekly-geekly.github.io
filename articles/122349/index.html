Gipis is a service for creating an individual training and nutrition program.
Site gipis.ru

All physical activity data is collected automatically using a mobile phone and GPS (for remote sports such as running or cycling), as well as using an accelerometer for team sports and training in the gym (football, tennis, fitness, barbell) or heart rate monitor. Activity data is uploaded to the site, where a further training and nutrition program is generated on their basis. Depending on the progress of the person, the program is automatically edited so that the user reaches his goal by a certain date, even if part of the workouts have been missed or the nutrition plan has not been respected for some time. There are mobile versions of the application for the iPhone and Android.

Sergey Vasiliev (StartupPoint) : ‚ÄúIncreasingly, Internet resources and services have begun to appear, aimed at solving the problem of overweight, helping users to eat and distribute the load during sports. A great addition to such services is a mobile device. Gipis is a very good counterpart of such Western services as: Nike +, Runkeeper, Daily Burn, which has good prospects in Russia. ‚Äù

Sergey Ivanenko (Spanish Village) : ‚ÄúA project with a clear target audience: people leading a healthy lifestyle and monitoring their condition. The way to monetize the project is not clear to me. The Codepays project is a payment system for mobile devices based on QR codes.

Site codepays.com

The mechanics of the service are as follows: the seller creates a payment in the form of a QR code, the buyer takes a photo of him on his mobile phone and immediately pays for the service with a bank card. The seller no longer needs to buy additional equipment for making payments with bank cards at points of sale. You also get the opportunity to distribute QR codes in the media or in public places. The buyer gets a safe and fast way to pay for purchases without entering bank card details every time. All mobile platforms are supported for which there is at least one QR code scanner.

Commentary by Sergey Vasiliev (StartupPoint) : ‚ÄúCodepays is a project that is in trend, as they say now, mobile applications, payment systems. In addition, QR codes as a phenomenon is a very hot topic, so the symbiosis of several trend directions will surely give a good result. The main field of application of this service is retail sales. ‚Äù
