Fujitsu LIFEBOOK U772 Ultrabook Video Review



Ultrabook Fujitsu LIFEBOOK U772 its dimensions resemble the standard 13-inch ultrabook, but the novelty is equipped with a frameless matte display with a diagonal of 14 inches and a resolution of 1366x768. Models run on third-generation Intel Core processors (i5-3317U, i5-3427U, Core i7-3667U) with support for up to 8 GB of RAM (one slot is available). For data storage, either hybrid hard drives of 320/500 GB (with an additional solid-state drive of 32 GB) or solid-state 2.5 "drives with a capacity of 128 or 256 GB are used.

The laptop is equipped with all modern means of wireless communication: Wi-Fi (a / b / g / n), Bluetooth 4.0, as well as the 3G / UMTS module (there are modifications with 4G / LTE).

Magnesium alloy body is durable and lightweight, the weight of the laptop is 1400 g, and the thickness is only 16 mm. Two color options are available: bright red and silver gray. The warranty on laptops is 2 years.
