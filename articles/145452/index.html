A small adapter allows you to play on the Xbox with the controller from the PS3 and vice versa



For gamers who are used to a particular game console, the problem is known when you get used to the controller of this console. After, if you have to play on a different type of console, you need to relearn for quite some time. Recently, a new device was introduced, the Cronus USB adapter, which solves this problem. The functionality of the adapter is very limited, but the main task the device performs.

With it, you can play on a specific game console (Xbox 360, PS3, Wii) with an ‚Äúalien‚Äù game controller. Probably not all such a device will seem useful, but there will be gamers who will find this adapter useful.

Cronus itself is a black USB adapter, similar to a USB flash drive, with a USB input and a digital display on its upper part. The guys from engadget checked the device on the example of PS3, Battlefield 3, and the controller from the Xbox 360. In general, you can now come to a friend with a different type of game console than you, with your game controller. Problems should not arise, in any case, this is guaranteed by the developers. The cost of the device is from 50 to 60 US dollars.
