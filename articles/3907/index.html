UltraEdit 13.00

UltraEdit 's developers have released the thirteenth version. Added built-in viewing of HTML files, spell checking on the fly, expanded search and replace capabilities in several files at the same time, and other changes and additions .

Download UltraEdit 13.00: uedit32.zip (10Mb).

Personally, I am a little suspicious about programs whose developers are not shy about using version numbers close to or even larger than ten (although this hardly underestimates the merits of UltraEdit as one of the best text editors for programming). In this particular case, I can predict that not everyone will have time to download UltraEdit 13.00, as versions 13.00a, 13.00b, 13.00 + 1 will start to appear with emergency updates, which are almost inevitable with significant innovations.
