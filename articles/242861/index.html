Creative card file

Card file is an old tool for generating new ideas.



Among the tools for working with information and developing creative thinking one of the most suitable for me was a very archaic thing. I met the most universal and clear description of this tool at the guru of the national time management, comrade Gleb Arkhangelsky, but the idea itself flashes in the noosphere here and there, starting, probably, from writers of the 18th century. Filling

Here we just touch the rules of GTD, which say that any garbage that interests you should be fixed, leaving its description (or reminder of this garbage) in a special drive, and rake this drive with a certain regularity.
