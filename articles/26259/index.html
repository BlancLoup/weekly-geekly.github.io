Social network of shopping lovers



Do you like to go to the supermarket? I love it, but only when every time you buy something new, and not just clog the refrigerator with a standard set of products. Only now, when you buy unfamiliar things, you run the risk of running into something bad or tasteless.

That would be great, before buying a package of juice, see the reviews of those who have already tried it.

But to realize this is not at all difficult: manufacturers carefully put a unique bar code on each product.
Make a product catalog by barcode, add commenting and the social network is ready.

In the west there is a similar project: upcdatabase.com . Visitors to the site add a brief information about the product and its manufacturer, almost 1,000,000 entries have already been collected! It is strange that the author of that site did not guess to add the ability to comment and add photos.

Now phones with WAP-browsers are quite common, so do not forget to make a WAP-version of the site so that your social network can be used directly in the store. :)

Many more ideas can be found on my blog: blog.ugnich.com .
