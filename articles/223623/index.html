Samsung Launches New Samsung Level Audio Series

Good afternoon, Habr!

Earlier this month, Samsung announced a new series of audio products under the Samsung Level brand. Portable news promise users a good sound, ease of use, as well as the ability to seamlessly connect to a smartphone or tablet. The series consists of four models: Level Over, Level On, Level In and Level Box.



Samsung Level Over is the flagship headphone model with HD sound, ergonomic design, touch controls and Bluetooth connectivity. 50mm speakers are designed using neodymium magnets. Biocellulose prevents the occurrence of resonance between the vibrating plate and the frame, guarantees a clear sound without interference Samsung Level Over headphones use hybrid-type active noise cancellation technology, which is equipped with both themselves and an external microphone, which allows the wearer to isolate from the outside world and enjoy only pure music.





Users can download the Samsung Level headphone app from the Google Play Store or Samsung Apps. It activates a special SMART monitor that will prevent hearing loss when playing music at the highest volume for a long time. In addition, using this application, the owners of the headphones will be able to connect voice notifications to read incoming messages, as well as for an alarm clock and calendar. Equalizer SoundAlive will help to optimize the sound for listening to music as well as movies, games, streaming video and audio. With it, you can customize the 3D effect, adjust the depth of the bass and use one of 25 different preset settings: pop, rock, jazz, concert music, etc.

SPECIFICATIONS:
Sound: 50 mm biocell cellulose diaphragm with neodymium magnet / active noise cancellation system
Design: Highly elastic headphone material / leather imitation cover material
Bluetooth: BT 3.0 / Audio Codec apt-X, SBC
Interfaces: Touch control Smart Touch Control / 3.5 mm audio jack
Functions: Voice control S Voice / Bluetooth connection / Two-microphone noise canceling and echo cancellation / Listening / Standby mode with ANC on: 15h / 30h / Listening / Standby mode with ANC off: 30h / 200h
Accessories: Carrying Case / Micro USB Cable / Detachable Audio Cable with Microphone and Remote Control / Airplane Adapter
Dimensions and weight: 175 x 190 x 78 mm / 350 g

Another headphone model, Samsung Level On, has 40-mm dual-aperture speakers.





SPECIFICATIONS:
Sound: 40 mm double layer aperture
Interfaces: 3.5 mm audio jack
Functions: Voice control S Voice
Accessories: Carrying Case / Detachable Audio Cable with Microphone and Remote Control
Dimensions and weight: 161.75 x 181.4 x 70.4 mm / 209.8 g

The Samsung Level In in-ear headphones are equipped with three-channel speakers: two armature drivers with a balanced armature for pure mid and high frequencies and one dynamic driver for rich bass sound.





SPECIFICATIONS:
Sound: Three-channel speakers (2 reinforcement drivers with balanced anchor / 1 dynamic driver)
Design: In-Ear Headphones
Interfaces: 3.5 mm audio jack
Functions: Voice control S Voice
Accessories: Carrying Case / Replaceable Ear Pads: 4 pcs. silicone rubber, 3 pcs. memory foam silicone
Weight: 15.7 g

Completes the new line of audio devices compact Bluetooth column Samsung Level Box, made in a metal case with a 56-mm speaker and a battery for 15 hours of continuous operation. It also has a built-in noise and echo cancellation system. The column can be connected to any NFC-enabled mobile device. Control is carried out using the built-in buttons that allow you to adjust the volume, enable and disable music playback, as well as receive and reject calls.

SPECIFICATIONS:
Sound: 56 mm stereo speaker with passive radiator
Design: Compact metal body
Bluetooth: Fast connection via NFC, Bluetooth
Interfaces: 3.5 mm audio jack
Functions: Voice control S Voice / Hands-free (Conference call) / Built-in microphone, noise and echo cancellation
Accessories: Micro USB cable / Mains charger / 3.5 mm audio cable
Dimensions and weight: 164.3 x 62.3 x 69.2 mm / 600 g
