So, cybersport is not just alluring games with large prize pools, but a whole movement of fans, amateurs and just those present: the audience alone numbers more than 300,000,000 people .



You need to understand that the entertainment of eSports is a concept that you need to understand: lovers devote hundreds of hours to watching streaming, and this is not the time that can be ignored.
