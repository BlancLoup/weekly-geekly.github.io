NOMOBILE.RU at MWC-2010. Last day



The last day at the exhibition was devoted to the inspection of everything that had not previously been possible to see. Moving to Chinatown at the MWC, Nikolay Turubar noticed an interesting gadget from Motorola - Golden-i. This strange-looking thing is a real computer. With the help of a clever attachment, it is put on the head and a small display appears in front of your eyes. Management is completely vocal.




Then there was a booth with phones Emporia - a company that seriously attended to the creation of grandmothers and grandfathers. By the way, they make them very, very cool.
