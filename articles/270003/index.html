JetBrains Toolbox came out with all updated desktop products

Although today is Monday, we have an important day - we launched JetBrains Toolbox , updated all the desktop products included in it and started selling under a new licensing scheme .

Briefly about each of the product updates:

  • ReSharper Ultimate 10 - ReSharper Build for improved build efficiency, continuous testing, timeline profiling inside Visual Studio, more code style controls, NUnit 3.0 support, full C99 support, new C ++ checks, fixes and refactoring, improved JavaScript and TypeScript (including regular expressions and JSX / TSX syntax), decompiling into IL code.
  • IntelliJ IDEA 15 - new features in the debugger, improved user interface, new built-in tools and integration, further improved help with writing code, Kotlin support "out of the box".
  • PhpStorm 10 - improved support for the PHP language, increased ease of editing, debugging, code analysis and many other powerful features.
  • WebStorm 11 Support TypeScript 1.6, Flow and Angular 2, integration with Yeoman and other improvements in all directions.
  • PyCharm 5 - added a number of cool features, including full support for Python 3.5, integration with Docker, visualization of thread parallelism and much more.
  • RubyMine 8 - convenient gems management thanks to Rbenv gems and Bundler improvements, improved debugging speed and much more.
  • AppCode 3.3 - supports Xcode 7, generic Objective-C types, and many Swift 2 features.
  • CLion 1.2 - support for Google Test, more efficient work with CMake, improved performance of the C ++ debugger, improved work with VCS and much more.
  • 0xDBE 1.0 Preview - our IDE for databases is almost ready. Try it and share your impressions - it will greatly help us to bring it ‚Äúup to standard‚Äù to release at the end of this year.

Starting today, all listed products are provided only on a new licensing model, that is, on a subscription.

If you have a valid subscription to updates , you do not need to switch to a new model immediately. You are free to use product updates until the subscription to updates has expired. And after that you can switch to a new model and take advantage of our special offer for existing customers.
