Drag and drop images into documents

When writing a document, it is often necessary to add text to the images. There are already three ways to add images to Google Docs: you can find them on your hard drive, add them by URL and you can find them using Google Image Search.

But sometimes the exact image that you need is on your desktop, and you just want to quickly add it to the document. Starting from yesterday, you can drag images from the desktop directly into your documents.





Now drag and drop images are available in the latest versions of Google Chrome, Firefox and Safari.
