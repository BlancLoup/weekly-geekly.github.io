HP at TechEd Russia 2011

I do not know about you, but I sincerely believe that you should never lose optimism. And now, just 1 day before the start of the colossal Microsoft TechEd Russia 2011, we are finally ready to make an announcement of a colossal competition that will take place all two days of the conference on and around HP booth.



So, if you want to test your knowledge of HP and Microsoft technologies and win one of the prizes from the picture above, be sure to take a laptop with you . Catching a Wi-Fi network in the TechEd exhibition area, you can get to the contest website by typing in your Internet Explorer * address

//hp-konkurs.teched

Register and be sure to include your real email and phone number. We will need the phone to quickly find you for the presentation of the prize, so we made it the main login - it will track your progress in the competition.

After registration you will see a list of tasks for the competition. The tasks are based on real-world practice using HP and Microsoft technologies. Some of them you can solve immediately, others - only after the previous ones. The solutions to each task may differ, but for each of them there is an unequivocal criterion for successful implementation.

Your goal is to solve all the available tasks before everyone else and spend as little time as possible on each of them. At the HP booth, we will show the current progress of all participants and the desktops of all open virtual machines. By the way, all virtual machines will run on HP steep hardware, which is installed directly on the HP booth.

Having solved all the tasks available at the moment (we do not doubt you!), Do not rush to relax! We will constantly replenish the list of tasks, giving you additional chances to prove yourself, consolidate leadership or catch up with rivals. On the second day, TechEd part of the tasks of the first day will be unavailable, so do not delay the decision "for later."

Well, it seems nothing is forgotten. And, yes, I advise you to take a passport - then you can get a prize right at the closing ceremony of TechEd on November 10 and go home with it.

See you at HP booth!

* Technical requirements for the client computer are:

- OS Microsoft Windows XP 3 or higher (32 or 64 bit)
- Internet Explorer 7 or higher (32-bit version only)
- ActiveX is enabled, a single applet installation is required
- the ability to place a computer on your knees while you sit in comfortable ottomans around the stand. Puffs, please do not take away someone else's stands! :)
