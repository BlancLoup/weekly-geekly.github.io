Samsung and Microsoft produce specialized products in honor of the film The Simpsons

Many years have passed until the Simpsons reached the big screen, but it seems that the company 20th Century Fox does not waste time painting products in yellow to unleash the film.

You may have heard about laptops and mobile phones produced in honor of films, just as you heard about movies on mobile phones, but Samsung, together with 20th Century Fox, reveals the secret that they are planning to release a limited edition yellow mobile phone in order to to capture the film The Simpsons. The phone has all chances to get the status of cult in the world of geeks and cartoon fans, although technical specifications have not yet been announced for it, and even the design has not yet been completed, but it is already known that it will be yellow, it will have a special Simpsons logo, and contain exclusive materials.
