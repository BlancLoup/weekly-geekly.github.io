edX / MITx: Introduction to Computer Science and Programming

The course starts on October 1, 2012 and will run until January 14, 2013. As with many edX courses, the approximate workload is 12 hours per week.

The course is designed for people with little programming experience to understand computational approaches to problem solving. The purpose of this course is to provide students with extensive, but not deep knowledge of many aspects of programming.

Requirements for understanding the course: High school algebra and math skills.
The training will use the Python language version 2.7. Knowledge of language is not required.
