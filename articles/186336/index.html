JQuery World Conference comes to Russia

The first and only event in our country dedicated to the famous JavaScript framework - the jQuery Russia conference, with the active support of the IT-Everstiya.rf portal , will be held on October 15 at the Digital October center in Moscow.




The jQuery annual conference was launched in 2007 in Boston, and since then around two dozens of conferences under this brand have been held around the world, including Vienna, Oxford, Seoul, Portland and San Francisco. This fall, for the first time, ITmozg will host the famous conference in Russia.

The jQuery Russia conference will focus on all aspects of Front End development. As part of the program, participants will be able to learn about the latest trends in the use of jQuery and understand in which direction the development of web sites is moving, and get acquainted with the implemented projects of experts.

Conference speakers


The event will feature leading foreign and Russian Front End experts.
The main guest and speaker of the conference is the president of the jQuery Foundation - Dave Methvin (USA).

Conference organizers invite experts and fans of the jQuery library to become speakers of this unique event. The main topics of the conference - jQuery, jQuery UI, jQuery Mobile, expected reports on JavaScript, CSS, testing, tools, teamwork. Apply for a speech here .
