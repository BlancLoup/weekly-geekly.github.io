Cybersport recognized as the official sport in Russia


Photo source: cyber.sports.ru

The Ministry of Sports has published an order according to which eSports is entered in the register of officially recognized sports in Russia. The order on the recognition of "computer sports" will take effect 10 days after the publication of the document.
