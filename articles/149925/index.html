IEEE is preparing a 1Tbps Ethernet standard

The latest 40GbE and 100GbE standards at 40 Gb / s and 100 Gb / s, which the IEEE institute approved in 2010, won't be long enough. IEEE experts have published an expert assessment (pdf) of network bandwidth growth and a forecast for the future. According to their data, the volume of IP traffic is growing exponentially, doubling approximately every 18 months.

For users, the maximum download speed on modems is also increasing by 50% per year for the last 29 years.
