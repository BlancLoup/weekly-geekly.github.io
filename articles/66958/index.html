Evolution in business processes

The process of evolution is irreversible. You can only slow down or speed it up, but not stop it. So it was always and in everything, including trade. Bone bills turned into calculators, and bazaar billbooks into paper price lists, and later into online stores.

Despite the fact that each tool was effective at a certain stage, sooner or later it will be time to improve it!

If we return to trade - in our opinion, now is the time to use Internet technologies in it, which in themselves are more a revolution than an evolution. Standard trading techniques are becoming more costly and less effective than virtual trading platforms. But, built on the principle of social networking business space, give companies a lot of opportunities. For example, any company can reduce non-production costs and increase sales in such a business space, regardless of the type of activity and scale.

So why are many companies still doubt to take advantage of the Internet?

In subsequent articles we will try to reveal the practical possibilities and prospects of companies in the virtual space. Show (using the example of the PROMDEX system ) how the Internet can work for your company.
