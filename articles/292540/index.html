Russian e-commerce features

In May 2015, KupiVIP sales grew by 65%, in June - by 55-60%. KupiVIP CEO Vladimir Kholyaznikov sees this as a profound reason: the crisis in the Russian fashion industry, which began in the fourth quarter of 2013, when people began to think more about savings. Another reason was two warm winters - at that time of the year major money was made. Since 2009, many things in Russia have been sold at a discount in order to revive traffic after the crisis. Later, retail tried to return to full price mode, but in 2013 it all started again. KupiVIP helps to get rid of unsold collections.

In an interview with Kommersant-Money, Vladimir Kholyaznikov told about the peculiarities of Russian Internet commerce.

KupiVIP company is a discooter, to which brands, their franchisees, wholesalers and manufacturers apply. 70% of purchases at the moment are in Russia. European manufacturers made concessions - made a discount, cheapened materials. Otherwise, the rise in prices reaches 70%.

In fact, from the very beginning, KupiVIP is a marketplace. The company sells alien goods from foreign warehouses. Own warehouse provides only 30% of all sales, but mostly it is used as a distribution center.
