Group-IB Webinar: "Forensic Investigation of RDP Artifacts in Windows"

The webinar will begin on November 27, 2018 at 11:00 (Moscow Time), and Alexander Ivanov , the Group-IB leading expert in computer forensics, will hold the meeting.
