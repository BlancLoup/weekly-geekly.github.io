Texas Instruments is preparing a system-on-chip OMAP 5 with 3d support

Specifications platform OMAP 5:

  • 28-nm process technology
  • two ARM Cortex-A15 cores with a frequency of up to 2GHz and two iCortex-M4 cores (dual dual core);
  • 3D graphics accelerator PowerVR SGX544-MPx;
  • 2D graphics accelerator TI 2D graphics core;
  • IVA-HD video accelerator for 1080p HD video;
  • audio processor;
  • C64x DSP;
  • M-Shield security system with a set of cryptographic functions;
  • support up to 4 high resolution displays;
  • support single HDMI 1.4a 3D display;
  • energy efficiency is 60% higher than OMAP 4.
SoC OMAP 5 is designed for future smartphones, tablets, embedded devices and ultraportable laptops.
