Microsoft will launch Xbox Live on iOS and Android


Microsoft is going to develop a platform for the introduction of Xbox Live functionality on iOS and Android. Xbox Live has the smallest user base among other gaming social networks. As of the end of October 2013, 48 million users were registered with the service, while on Steam there were 65 million active accounts, and PlayStation Network - 110 million. For comparison, Apple has more than 200 million registered accounts in the Game Center.

Earlier, Microsoft has already tried to introduce Xbox Live functionality into competitors' mobile platforms. In December 2012, the company released a version of the Wordament game for iOS with the ability to achieve achievements by logging in to Xbox Live. In October 2013, a similar version of Wordament appeared on Android.

Microsoft is still trying to take an increasingly important place in the mobile industry and is taking various steps. Let's see what happens.
