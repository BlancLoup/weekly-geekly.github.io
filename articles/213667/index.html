Nokia has released a line of phones based on Android OS


Information about the release of the line of Nokia phones based on Android OS has been confirmed.
The new line will be called Nokia X.


Judging by the characteristics - the line is a budget and is designed to expand the line Asha.
It is worth noting that the system interface has been redesigned and designed in the style of the Nokia Lumia line, based on the Windows Phone OS.
In addition to the OS, the new phones are also distinguished by the support of two sim-cards and camera parameters, which are modest as by today's standards, from 3 to 5 MP.

Let me remind you that previously there was information about the release of Nokia smartphones based on Android OS:
  • Nokia will introduce its Android phone on February 24
  • There are photos of the Android interface performed by Nokia
  • Nokia may release a smartphone with Android before merging with Microsoft
