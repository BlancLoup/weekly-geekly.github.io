First International StartUp Women Forum 2011

Hello to all. I am the organizer of the first international StartUp Women Forum 2011 , which will be held in Moscow on April 4-5.

His goal is to create a communication platform where women entrepreneurs can teach other women to create their own projects on the Internet and make money from them.

The Forum will feature the best presentations from world and Russian top managers and entrepreneurs. They will share their story and tell about their path to success.

The Forum also holds a competition among start-up projects in the IT and Internet industries with responsive female leadership or participation.

We decided to give the opportunity to get a free ticket to the Forum. To do this, you need to publish your project or business until March 28 .
