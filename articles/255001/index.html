Difficult times for free-to-play, a little more about Apple Watch, console revenue - and other news of the week for a mobile developer



The profit of free-to-play games falls


Nicholas Lovell, founder of Gamesbrief, believes that the profitability of free-to-play games will fall this year. It's hard to disagree. Moreover, this trend has been outlined since the middle of last year. There are several reasons for this.



First, the cost and development time increase. The requirements of the audience of mobile games for the quality of products are growing. Developers need to invest more and more power to succeed.

Secondly, an increase in marketing expenses. It's no secret that the mobile advertising market is very hot. The cost of users is increasing, while the quality does not increase. The main markets (USA, Western Europe) are saturated, the audience is growing very slowly, and in some countries is already close to its maximum. There are more and more applications on the market, the struggle for the user is getting stronger.

Lovell confirms these theses with annual reports of the companies that earned the most last year - Supercell and King.

In 2014, Supercell earned ‚Ç¨ 1.5 billion. This is 3 times more than in 2013. But at the same time, operating expenses increased by almost 3.75 times and amounted to ‚Ç¨ 1.04 billion. The increase in operating expenses is associated primarily with an increase in marketing costs. The company in one year spent about $ 400 million to promote their games.

The situation with King is very similar. King's revenue last year grew by 20%, the company's marketing expenses grew by the same 20%, while the profit growth was not so tangible - about 8%. The reason is the same - $ 455 million spent on marketing.

This year this trend will continue. We will probably not see any excess returns in this market anymore.


What happened to the pricing for Apple products?

Today, Apple releases its watches , which in fact have only 2 modifications depending on the screen diagonal - this is 38 mm or 42 mm, but at the same time as many as 15 models. And most importantly, all these 15 models do not differ in their characteristics, and the price is different:

How much do console games earn


According to SuperData , the revenue of games for consoles in February of this year fell by 3.7% compared with the same month last year and amounted to $ 233 million.


Nevertheless, the console market continues to grow steadily. The end of 2014 was a record in sales, and revenues in December amounted to about $ 350 million. This is due to the release of the new Fifa 15 Ultimate team from EA and, of course, the release of Grand Theft Auto V.

Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare became the most earned game in February. The game brought its developers $ 31.6 million.
