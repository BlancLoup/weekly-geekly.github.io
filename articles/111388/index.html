Evernote is now available in the Mac App Store

We all in Evernote just love the app stores. They play a key role in the growth of Evernote , especially the iTunes app store (iTunes App Store).



Since its launch, iTunes App Store has set the standard for how such directories should be, and its success has helped create a new market. And today, Evernote can be found on dozens of shelves of virtual stores around the world, whether it be iTunes, Android, BlackBerry or WebOS. In recent months, app stores have become the most popular way to spread Evernote. More than 70% of new users of Evernote download Evernote for the first time from there.

Until now, all this concerned only mobile platforms, and that is why today's news is so significant: Evernote has appeared in the newly opened application store for Mac OS - the Mac App Store .

This is a very big event.


Apple has revolutionized user experience with mobile apps. Prior to the iTunes App Store, finding and installing applications for your smartphone was a procedure no more pleasant than, say, walking through broken glass. And nowadays, it would seem, what could be simpler! Imagine now that finding and installing desktop applications can be just as easy! By bringing its know-how to the world of desktop computers, Apple will once again turn our understanding of what should be the search and installation of new software. And we are glad to be involved in these events.

Download Evernote from the Mac App Store


The Mac App Store is available as part of the Mac OS X 10.6.6 Update. When the update is complete, you will see the App Store icon in the dock - find our application there by the word ‚ÄúEvernote‚Äù and follow the installation instructions.
