Google gurus will perform in front of Russian students

The Russian office of Google has launched an educational program. Leading programmers of the company will give lectures to Russian students.

Student meetings with developers are held in eleven leading universities in Moscow and St. Petersburg from March 5 to 13. In the coming days, students of SPSU, MSU, MIPT, MIET and several others will be able to attend lectures and learn about the latest solutions in programming, the latest trends in the web industry and Google products. Of course, pundits from Google will answer all questions.

Video recordings of all lectures will be posted in the public domain on the official blog of the Russian representative office of Google.
