Apple patents gesture management

The US Patent Office granted Apple an incredibly wide patent for control elements using gestures. Sorry, Kinect!

SmartSense, for a minute, is an Israeli startup that developed the Kinect 3D sensors. Gaming movement control began in 1989 with Power Glove for the Nintendo Entertainment System. In 2005, the Nintendo Wii gaming console appeared with the Wii Remote controller. Five years later, the PlayStation Move entered the market, and after a couple of months, Microsoft Kinect followed.
