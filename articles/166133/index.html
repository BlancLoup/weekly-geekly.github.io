Today, Yandex People service has been attached to Yandex Disk, let's see what happened.

Today, Yandex has published a sad, at least for me, news . Briefly, the essence of the news is as follows: before the People could upload an unlimited number of files via the web, and up to 5 GB in size. Now you can upload only 2 GB files via the web, and in order to upload a 5 GB file, you need to install the Disk to your device. Now the space for storing files is strictly limited and initially amounts to 5 GB, after installing the Disk on any device, and uploading any file, the space will increase to 10 GB, and if you share your left email with friends, share up to 20 GB. This is the maximum amount of free space available, i.e. 20 GB. The most annoying thing is that you cannot increase the place even for money, although Yandex says that it will be possible in the future.
