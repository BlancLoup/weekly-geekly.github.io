There is no sense of belonging to a single team, empathy, solidarity. All participants in the race are irreconcilable rivals. The internal ideology of the company is based on extreme individualism, around personal success. There are also managers who are behind middle managers, driving them and pushing them. The entire system of power within the company looks as if it is a series of incessant struggles for survival.
