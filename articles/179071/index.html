About the "Scarlet Mantle"
A small "board" role-playing game from Yasumi Matsuno, creator and ideological mastermind of tactical RPG Final Fantasy Tactics , Vagrant Story and Tactics Ogre . It was created according to the canons of the old-school desktop rolevushkas - cubes d4, d8, etc., text inserts, the traditional role-playing system.


The library of games for 3DS continues to grow. Get at least the latest presentation of Nintendo Direct, where they announced an interesting Mario & Luigi , a new Mario Golf , a Donkey Kong Country Returns remake and a whole heap of crazy downloadable games for the eShop store, whose range is more and more impressive.

It is possible to list big names for a long time, but the essence is simple: 3DS as a gaming system is very impressive now. Nintendo continues to do everything to make their offspring even more attractive. Why disputes about the technical characteristics and performance, when it is already clear to everyone that the best platform becomes the one on which the best games come out. And in 2013, this is 3DS.

As our editor-in-chief remarked , 3DS is worth buying for the sake of Fire Emblem alone : Awakening - a terrific strategy that loads brains more than chess. Or for the sake of Super Mario 3D Land - one of the best Mario games in general. Or for the sake of Zero Escape: Virtue's Last Reward - my favorite game of the past year. Or for the sake of Revelations - the only worthwhile game in the Resident Evil series since hell is the fourth part!

Three or four years ago, the best gaming system on the market was not the Xbox and Playstation. Nintendo DS dominated the video game world. It had everything: puzzles, platformers, role-playing games, quests, many sequels of famous series of various genres. DS was for me a cheaper, more affordable and attractive system than any other. 3DS went the same way and achieved success. It diverges well - Nintendo reports that more than 30 million units have been sold worldwide - and have far exceeded our wildest expectations.

Maybe the reason for this were those very failures at the start. In the summer of 2011, 3DS went to the bottom. How simple is that, yes? "Whistles" and specifications do not matter. Cool platform make the game. There are great games on 3DS. Therefore, 3DS is a cool platform.
