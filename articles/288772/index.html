IT bloggers Runet, where are you?

Just yesterday, I realized that besides Habr in my RSS reader there is not a single Russian-language source of IT information. Some injustice, I thought, and began to compile a list of bloggers who would be interesting to read and for which I would like to actively follow. This task, oddly enough, was not the easiest. Therefore, I appeal for help to Habrachelovekam.

Interest is aroused by specialists and amateurs, programmers, designers, entrepreneurs, and marketing specialists who write analytical articles, reviews on IT and near-byy themes, forecasts and news. Let them be not very famous, the main thing is that the information was fresh and interesting. So if you run an IT blog, or follow an interesting IT blog, please leave a link to it in the comments to this blog (or, if you are not registered on Habr√©, send the URL to me via twitter ) - so I can make the most complete list the most interesting IT bloggers runet and, of course, share it with everyone.
