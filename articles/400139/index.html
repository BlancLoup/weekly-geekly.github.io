Overview of CNC laser engravers up to 1 million rubles



Along with 3D printers and milling machines, laser cutters and engravers are another class of CNC devices, without which it is difficult to imagine modern high-tech manufacturing. The laser beam is capable of processing the widest range of materials with high speed and accuracy, leaving the result without additional finishing.

In this review, we will introduce readers to several samples of laser cutters that can be used in many areas - the production of promotional materials, furniture, various equipment and consumer goods. These engraving cutters do not work properly for metal, stone or mineral glass, they do not have the same purpose and power, but they do their job perfectly well.

Most models have prices, but these prices are approximate - they can change both up and down for different reasons. For more accurate information you can visit the site.

Raylogic (formerly Qualitech)


Laser engraver Raylogic 304 mini


  • Laser power: 30 W
  • Size of the working field: 300 X 400 mm
  • Price: 136,000 rubles
  • Application: labeling, advertising, production of souvenirs.

One of the most inexpensive laser machines. Indispensable in the manufacture of promotional and promotional materials from paper, cloth, plastic. Easily cuts acrylic with a thickness of up to 5 mm.
Not the most powerful laser, but reliable electronic filling and mechanism allow this machine to gain its users.

Laser cutters-engravers of the 11G series are manufactured according to high international standards of industrial equipment and taking into account the standards of Russian manufacturing companies.

The cutters are equipped with the latest generation of DSP-controllers with many new features that increase the speed and performance of work. The basic equipment is equipped with Reci laser tubes with a resource of 10,000 hours of continuous operation. Rack table makes comfortable work with sheet materials.
