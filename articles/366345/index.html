Apple will employ 86 specialists in artificial intelligence and machine learning

The company launched a recruiting program through doctoral programs and places dozens of vacancies, writes Reuters . Apple wants to make its smartphones even smarter, so it will hire at least eighty-six specialists in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Some experts believe that the strict position of the iPhone manufacturer in matters of privacy will prevent it from competing in a rapidly growing area with Google, Amazon and Facebook.

Siri's personal assistant and question-answer system, developed for iOS and first launched in the iPhone 4S, has become a pioneer in its field. In the upcoming Siri update for iOS 9, the system will receive several new features.

Apple plans to further expand the capabilities of Siri, for which the company needs specialists in machine learning. Their work will be complicated by the position of Apple in matters of privacy - for example, the company does not see the content of messages in iMessage.
