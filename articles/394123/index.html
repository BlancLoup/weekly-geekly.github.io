Minecraft Gameband - a bracelet for gamers

The gaming industry has an impact on the development of technical support: millions of dollars are spent on upgrading the PC, new gadgets appear, and all sorts of effects are created to immerse oneself in the story. Companies are constantly looking for ways to make games more exciting. And the increasing popularity is gaining a bracelet Minecraft Gameband.



This accessory does not track fitness activity and does not synchronize with mobile devices. The bracelet allows gamers to save their "worlds" in Minecraft. Gameband is something like portable storage for virtual worlds.

In the silicone strap device is hidden storage capacity of 8 GB, which contains user data. Gameband has a built-in flash drive that runs the saved game on another PC. To do this, it is necessary to connect the bracelet to the computer and create a new world of Minecraft, which will remain in the Gameband and in the cloud. Thus, it will be possible to play Minecraft from any computer, simply by connecting a bracelet to it. Supported operating systems are Windows, OS X and Linux.

Also, the bracelet is equipped with an LED display that can be customized. The clasp is made of stainless steel, and stylized as game elements.



According to the head of the company Mac Conulada, the development has three main advantages: the availability of the necessary technical support, convenient software, and the ability to play on different operating systems (Mac, Windows XP, Windows 10, Linux). Of course, the product does not solve all the problems. When switching to another computer, you will still have to make certain changes (install a package of textures, etc.).



Gameband automatically saves the game, including each modification created. This allows the player to take your Minecraft with you.

You can buy Minecraft Gameband in online stores. Prices start at $ 80 * , including shipping.
