Microsoft PDC10 local in Russia and Ukraine

The main event of the year for .NET developers, the Microsoft PDC10 conference, will be held very soon. Already have a preliminary schedule and announcements from Microsoft Research . But few people know that, in addition to a large event, several smaller ones with the status of local PDCs will also be held this year. The full list can be found at the link .

It should be noted that in Russia and Ukraine there will be only three such events - in Moscow , Kharkov and Vinnitsa .

As the organizer of the event in Vinnytsia, I would like to invite everyone to act as information partners and sponsors, information about which we will post in the "Partners" section.



We also invite specialists to speak at our meeting. Commemorative prizes and good mood are guaranteed. News about the event will appear on Twitter and on the site .
