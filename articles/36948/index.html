Where to go, what to do?

One of the most important decisions in life is what to do? What to shine yourself in
of this life. Under the cut a few ideas and tips.



1) Choose a business that you already know. The hairdresser opens his salon,
The lawyer begins his own practice. Actually - this is a big mistake. People,
who go this way, think about work, not about business. Just because you
good cook, you can not open a restaurant and manage it. And the more so it is not
means that your restaurant will generate income.

2) Choose a product or service that you like. On the other hand, it
may not be profitable.

3) Make sure your product or service will be bought multiple times. It's almost
not the main condition for a long-term successful business. You need your products /
services were in demand.

4) Make sure you have a team. The idea is good, but even the most
a successful idea should have a good team for its implementation.

If there are more tips, write in the comments, I will add them to the general list.
