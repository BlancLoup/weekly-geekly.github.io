PacktPub gives you the opportunity to get free books


PacktPub decided to mark the thousandth published book really widely, congratulating its readers and giving them a pretty good gift, free access to its library for a whole week with the ability to download any e-book.

In order to take advantage of the action, it is enough to log in to an existing one or create a new account from the 28th to the 30th of September.

For reference, PacktLib is the online digital library of Packt, which publishes primarily IT literature.

Enjoy everyone!

UPD: So far PacktPub really lies, the main one works, there is no possibility to log in.

Link to the publisher site: www.packtpub.com
