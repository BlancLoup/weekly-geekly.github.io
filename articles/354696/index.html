Riddles resume. Part 1. Choosing a form

So, you are going to find a new job. Where to begin? With the search for a suitable vacancy that best reflects your wishes for a new position and organization. And then ... Then begins a frantic search for a template that would fit all the best that you could tell about yourself. And here the first difficulties arise. There are many forms of resumes, but you have a favorite one. What to choose?

Of course, it is easier to pick up and fill in some ready-made questionnaire form on one of the employment sites, or copy the accounting sheet, which may be stored in your organization in the personnel department. And can use the resume composing designer from one of the sites of the finished resume? The choice is ambiguous.

Resume is your business card. It should be as attractive as possible to those who will watch it. The person who makes the selection should have an unconscious desire to necessarily invite you for an interview.

Now we come to the most important thing. Do you know that each person is unique and inimitable not only by his fingerprints, type of thinking, way of presenting his thoughts, manner of presenting himself to strangers? Of course you know! Therefore, you should guess that you express your individuality to one degree or another in almost everything: in the clothes you wear, in the way you present yourself at the interview, in speech and in emotions. In the summary, we also express our unique style. We would like to demonstrate all the best, interesting and, most importantly, necessary to a potential employer. And what should not be mentioned - of course, hide!

Resume - this is a matter that, as a rule, do not want to do

Yes, there is another indisputable truth - in every person there is something good and something that he may not like very much in himself. And this is only part of the problem. And if you have something that does not suit your employer? Imagine the following picture: you find an interesting vacancy for yourself, send a resume to this organization, and for some reason you are simply excluded by you, even without letting you pass the interview. Everyone who is engaged in staff recruitment subconsciously evaluates resumes by several parameters. I already wrote about this in the last article. The system I mentioned is based on an assessment of the three main indicators subconsciously estimated by a person - form, content and font.

Some of you will think that this rating system is not worth considering. Since it is not described and is not known to anyone. In something you are right, but in something not. After all, processes that we do not realize are sometimes even stronger than those that we consciously control. Therefore, to consider this or not, you decide.

And for those who are interested, I will start with the first of the resume evaluation factors - with the form in which the potential employee places information about himself. It is important that it is because of the form you choose that your resume will either fall into a pile of documents, to call with an invitation for an interview (it will not be thrown into the dropout folder), or almost immediately you will be mentally labeled "not very suitable." And perhaps they will not consider it at all.

Recruiters on job sites give advice on focusing on a resume on completeness of information, no errors, the suitability of education, and the experience of your chosen job. All this is also important and no one will argue that it is not significant. But this is the content.

You might be surprised, but each person, when confronted with a new document drawn up in an arbitrary form, first of all evaluates unconsciously precisely the form and font. That is why now I started not with the content, but with the form.
