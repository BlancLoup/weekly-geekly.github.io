Carmageddon reincarnates

A series of revivals of the good old games through crowdfunding continues. Following the successfully financed and launched into production Wasteland 2 and Leisure Suit Larry , Kickstarter began to raise funds for the come-back of the legendary bloody racing.



The team is going to bring this ambitious project to life - the same Stainless Games that the original did. Now the office is mainly engaged in additions for MtG, but, apparently, inspired by the example of the above projects, gentlemen still gathered to prove that they were not covered with rust in the 15 years that have passed since the release of the first part, and are capable of doing something serious.
