Google is by far the most expensive brand

For the third year in a row, Google , according to the rating published by research company Millward Brown Optimor , has been recognized as the world's most popular and valuable brand. The value of the Google brand this time amounted to 86 billion dollars, which is 30% more than last year.

The second place in the ranking was taken by General Electric , whose brand value was estimated by Millward Brown experts at $ 71.4 billion.
