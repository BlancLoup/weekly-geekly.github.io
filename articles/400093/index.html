Mamut will buy his share in Rambler & Co from Potanin for $ 295 million



Businessman Alexander Mamut is forced to buy his share in Rambler & Co from his partner, Vladimir Potanin, for $ 295 million, Vedomosti reports . The deal is scheduled to close by the end of this year, after which Mamut will become the sole owner of Rambler & Co, in which he now has half, but he will have to pay almost twice as much for a part of his partner as originally envisaged.

The deal stems from a merger of media assets of Mamut and Potanin back in 2013. According to his terms, Potanin received the right in 2.5 years to sell his share of Mamut for a fixed amount of $ 295 million, which he now used.
