SIP on HTC Hero

Probably I, like many, if any idea came to mind, I will not calm down until I implement it, or make sure that there is no solution. So, after buying HTC Hero and switching to the MTS Connect-2 tariff, I had outgoing calls for 3.5 rubles to mobiles across Peter, and for some reason 3 rubles for calls to Moscow. And then I really wanted to tie the Internet telephony to Android. As it turned out, Skype is somehow not at all suitable, do not call from it, do not call it, well, in general, in any way. On the Android market found the application SIPDroid. After that, I registered for pbxs.org, I registered for PCTEL.ru, put 200 rubles (less can not) into my PCTEL account through MasterCard (yes, by 2009 it became a reality). And then just everything. In the pbxs.org interface, you need to create a channel (trunk) for incoming and outgoing connections (specify sip.pctel.ru as the server, the username and password from your PCTEL account), then create an extension for your HTC Hero, username and password to connect from your phone.

And so, he launched, dialed the number of his beloved mother-in-law in Omsk and - voila! One ruble a minute. Everything is audible, everything is normal. But it was a home Wi-Fi, no questions. Turned off Wi-Fi, turned on GPRS, only EDGE was found at home (3G is not for some reason). Scored a colleague and heard a terrible gurgling.
Mnda, no stone flower comes out. Now we need to find a place where there really is 3G.

PS It looks like MTS does not cut sip traffic yet. Life will show how all this is applicable outside of Wi-Fi.
