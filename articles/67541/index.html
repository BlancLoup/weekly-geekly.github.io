Linux based Lotus Foundations is a convenient solution for small and medium businesses. Get the basic network services ( DHCP, DNS ) and deploy the IT infrastructure in your company from scratch by setting up the File-server , E-mail-server , Print-server , FTP and much more in 30 minutes .

Small and medium-sized companies face the same problems as large corporations. But they are forced to solve with the help of limited IT-resources, or in their absence.

IBM Lotus Foundations is a simple solution that simplifies the delivery of information technology to enterprises with limited time and resources to manage computer and software systems.

The Lotus Foundations package runs on a 100-megabyte Linux kernel, launched from an SSD chip, which allows you to start the system even in case of problems with hard drives. The complex will be able to serve up to 500 users simultaneously.
