As readers of electroname.com report, they were redirected to the fake website charter97.in when they tried to access the Charter website.

The fake website answered for about 5 minutes, and then re-started the original domain charter97.org.

It is possible that there was a failure in the work of DNS servers and this is in no way connected with the upcoming action on October Square. However, it can not but alarm that such fakes were created for other independent opposition sites, for example, belaruspartisan.in and they are registered for one owner, and are hosted in Belarus, electroname.com reports.

Recall that the very possibility of modifying DNS-records at the level of Beltelecom exists. By modifying DNS, you can redirect visitors from Belarus to fake sites.
