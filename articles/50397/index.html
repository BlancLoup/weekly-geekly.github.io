About the personal qualities of the programmer

The programmer must be a fanatic creator. Simple observation - each key press means the appearance of a new piece of information from non-being. Creating a new one is the main occupation of the programmer. The love of creativity is often the main source of energy that allows a programmer to overcome many difficulties.
