Guidelines for completing the personal data operator's notice



In one of our previous articles , which was devoted to preparing Roskomnadzor for checks on meeting the requirements of the law ‚ÄúOn Personal Data‚Äù, we talked about the importance of correctly filling out the notice, about cases when the notice needs to be filled out, and we also promised to tell more about how to fill each notification field.

It would seem that by the names of many fields it should be intuitively clear what exactly to write in them. But practice shows that many personal data operators have a lot of questions, and some fall into a real stupor when trying to fill in all the fields.

We decided to write a detailed instruction here, so many times not to tell the same thing to our customers, and also to make it just always available for everyone.

The notification of the operator of personal data is filled out on the Roskomnadzor personal data portal. Now let's look at each of the fields.



There should be no problems with the first positions. We choose the territorial office of Roskomnadzor to which the notification should be sent. Then select the type of operator. Enter the full and abbreviated name of the operator in accordance with the constituent documents. We indicate the actual and legal addresses of the organization. Choose a region (or regions) in which the organization operates. Fill in the details of the organization (only the TIN and OGRN are required, the rest can not be filled). If the organization has branches, add information about them.

Everything seems to be simple and understandable here, but there may already be questions with the following fields.



In the column ‚ÄúLegal basis for the processing of personal data‚Äù, you can specify all regulatory and internal documents that may in one way or another be associated with PD processing. Usually they start with 152- and the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, continue with legislation relating to the organization‚Äôs field of activity (for example, if it is a medical institution, then we write 323- hereof ‚ÄúOn the basics of public health protection in the Russian Federation‚Äù and other regulations as federal and regional scale related to health) and end the charter of the enterprise.



The ‚ÄúPurpose of processing personal data‚Äù column is one of the most insidious. Completing this field we must not forget that Part 2 of Article 5 of the Federal Law ‚ÄúOn Personal Data‚Äù tells us that PD processing should be limited to achieving specific, predetermined and legitimate goals. It is not allowed to process personal data incompatible with the purposes of collecting personal data.

We give one example of how to do it.

Some employers, when inviting a candidate for a vacant position to an interview, ask to fill out a questionnaire in which, among other things, they are asked to enter their passport information. However, from the point of view of 152-FZ, this is not legal. Since the purpose of processing personal data is to select a candidate for a vacant position and try to come up with a plausible rationale for why you need passport information. Work experience? Yes. Education background? Yes. Age? And here it smacks of discrimination, but we are not going to exploit child labor. But the passport data for the selection of staff is not needed.

No, we are not so naive and we understand that often the employer's passport data is needed by the employer in order to ‚Äúpunch‚Äù the candidate, for example, for debt load or for participation in other unpleasant stories. But once again - from the point of view of the law it is impossible to do that.

Let us return to filling out the field ‚ÄúPurpose of personal data processing‚Äù. Here we must correctly and adequately formulate these goals. And adequately what? Adequate to the list of categories of personal data that we will fill out below. After all, we do not want, that already before checking the RKN on the basis of our notification, there were reasons for issuing a prescription? Here we draw a vicious circle - we will write that we process the passport data of applicants, punish for violating the law on personal data, say that the ‚Äúpassport data‚Äù accidentally fell into the notice, wrote in the verification protocol ‚Äúincomplete / unreliable information is indicated in the notification of the personal data operator ".

As you already understood, the column ‚ÄúThe purpose of personal data processing‚Äù for different organizations can be very different, but for most commercial organizations it will be correct to write ‚ÄúProvision of personnel and accounting, staff recruitment for vacant positions, provision of services [list of services]‚Äù.



The next section is one of the most difficult and incomprehensible. Roskomnadzor wants us to describe the measures taken, provided for in Articles 18.1 and 19 of the Law ‚ÄúOn Personal Data‚Äù. But in fact, this section is one of the most simple, just take the provisions of these articles of the law and write that all of this is done with us. We have done - right?

An example of filling the field ‚ÄúDescription of measures stipulated by Articles 18.1 and 19 of the Federal Law‚Äú On Personal Data ‚Äù

Elimination of the consequences of violations of the legislation of the Russian Federation is carried out in accordance with the current legislation of the Russian Federation, in accordance with the regulation on the processing and protection of personal data, as well as in accordance with the instructions to the personal data security administrator and in accordance with the procedure for backing up and restoring hardware and software data and information security tools. Internal control over the compliance of personal data processing with the legislation of the Russian Federation in this field is carried out in accordance with the internal audit plan, the security administrator‚Äôs instructions and the regulation on personal data processing and protection. For the information system of personal data, a model of threats to the security of personal data has been developed, in which, when determining the danger of threats, an assessment of the harm that can be inflicted to the subjects of personal data in case of violation of the law is carried out. For the information system of personal data, a technical task has been developed for creating an information protection system and a draft design of an information protection system that provides for the implementation of measures defined by legislation for an information system of the third level of protection, as well as measures aimed at neutralizing threats identified as relevant in the security threat model. Employees who are allowed to process personal data are instructed in information security, sign a non-disclosure agreement on personal data, and communicate with personal data protection documents against a signature.

In the information on ensuring the security of personal data, a list of information security tools used in the ISPDN is indicated. Fortunately, this information is not published in the public domain for everyone, unlike other fields, so you can specify all the actually used GIS.



Date of commencement of PD processing usually coincides with the date of foundation of the company (registration).
In the next paragraph, the ‚ÄúCondition for terminating PD processing‚Äù is usually selected and ‚ÄúTermination of the organization‚Äôs activity‚Äù is indicated as a condition.



In the ‚ÄúCategories of personal data‚Äù section, we first check the processed categories by checkboxes, and then in the ‚ÄúOther categories of personal data that are not listed in this list‚Äù field, indicate those PDs that are not in the list, and it‚Äôs better to do it separately for different categories of subjects, for example: ‚ÄúOther categories of PD of workers: [list of PD of workers]. In the section ‚ÄúCategories of subjects whose personal data are processed,‚Äù we indicate a list of categories of persons whose data we have are stored or processed, for example: ‚ÄúTo employees, applicants for vacant positions, contractors, clients‚Äù. In the field ‚ÄúList of actions with personal data‚Äù the easiest way to quote the definition of PD processing from 152-FZ: ‚Äúcollection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, refinement (update, change), extraction, use, transfer (distribution, provision, access) , depersonalization, blocking, removal, destruction ". Next, we indicate the method of processing PD, usually it is ‚Äúmixed, with transfer via the internal network of a legal entity, with transfer via the Internet‚Äù.

If different assets of different classes are used, the form allows you to specify all the necessary information.

The next section of the form appeared on September 1, 2015. Anyone who has filled out a notice a long time ago needs to make changes to it and supplement it with data on the data center. Next, fill in the data of the person who was appointed in the company responsible for organizing the processing of personal data. IMPORTANT! The name of the person in charge, his contact phone number and e-mail will be available to everyone in the register of PD operators. Keep this in mind and, of course, it is better to warn an appointed person about this.

At the very end we indicate the data of the performer. The performer is the person who filled out this notice. This may not be a responsible, but a completely different person.
