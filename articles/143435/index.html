Lenovo will spend 800 million US dollars on the development of mobile devices



Lenovo, whose products are becoming increasingly popular, is going to invest heavily in the creation of a new division. This division will develop new mobile devices, mainly tablets and communicators. It is worth noting that in recent years, Lenovo has made significant progress in the market of mobile devices. To consolidate and strengthen its success, the company will spend 800 million US dollars.

The new plant, or rather, a mobile device development center, will be built in the Chinese city of Wuhan. The center will be open as early as October 2013, and will employ thousands of workers. The main task of the center is the development and implementation of various projects in the field of mobile devices.

By the way, Lenovo is now the second largest manufacturer of PCs and laptops in the world, surpassing even such a giant as Dell in sales. Ahead now only Hewlett-Packard.

Not so long ago, Lenovo began to try their hand at the mobile device market, offering LePhone smartphones and ThinkPad tablets. In the last fiscal quarter, the company sold 6.5 million mobile devices and 400,000 tablets, which is quite good. In sales of tablets, the company became the second in China.
