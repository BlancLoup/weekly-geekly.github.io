Release Mango scheduled for the first of September



The British resource about gadgets Pocket-lint with confidence asserts that the release of OS Mango for WP will happen on the first of September. According to the author of the article on Pocket-lint, this information was exclusively provided to the portal from several reliable sources. Microsoft itself has repeatedly stated that Mango will be released in the fall, but a specific number was not named.

One of the reasons for the release of Mango on the first of September may be the IFA exhibition in Berlin. Having released a new version of the OS before the exhibition, Microsoft will allow companies like Samsung, Acer, ZTE and LG to present their phones with the new OS at this exhibition.

Also, do not forget about Nokia World, which will be held in late October. It is assumed that it is at this exhibition that Nokia will show its first phone on Windows Phone.

Microsoft declined to comment on this information.
