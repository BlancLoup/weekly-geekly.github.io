Hi, Habr!

Our company is called Paymentwall. We are a startup with offices in San Francisco and Kiev. For two years, we deal with online payments and alternative payment methods. The founders of our company have been working together for 7 years, uniting Silicon Valley and Kiev. During this time, we released 4 successful startups within our previous incubator and decided to invest all our time in a new project that seemed to us the most promising.
