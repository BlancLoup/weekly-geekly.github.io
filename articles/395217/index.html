Archaeologists have discovered a previously unknown ancient structure in Jordan, thanks to satellite imagery and drone


Photo: I. LaBianca; J. Blanzy

Many of us think that the Earth has been studied very well, and there is practically no room for discoveries here. First of all, it concerns historical science and archeology in particular. But modern technologies help scientists to work, to make more and more new discoveries. However, sometimes found historical artifacts in practice turn out to be remnants of the vital activity of microorganisms.

Sometimes people doing research just make mistakes, as happened to a schoolboy who discovered an ancient city that turned out to be a hemp field. But now we are talking about a real find, which scientists have made in the ancient city of Peter, Jordan. For more than two thousand years in this region there was a huge structure hidden under a layer of soil - and nobody knew about it. Find an ancient structure was possible through the use of satellite images of Google Earth and photographing the area using drones.

For all the time the archaeological excavations in Petra, scientists have found thousands of structural elements of the city and tens of thousands of artifacts. All this was cataloged and documented. But the platform with a base of 56 * 50 meters remained hidden from the eyes of archaeologists.

According to the scientists who made this discovery, the giant monument was a cult place for residents. So far, excavations have not yet been carried out (no permission), but experts believe that the platform was built in the middle of the second century BC. At the same time, Petra became the trading center of the ancient Arab kingdom of Nabataeus.

Aerial photography also showed the presence of a smaller structure, the size of which is 8.5 * 8.5 meters. Also found the base of the columns and a large staircase.

Many famous objects of the city appeared in the period from 1 century. BC. up to 2 in. ne This period includes, for example, the Nabatean temple of El Hazne. Therefore, the presence of the object, whose age exceeds the age of most of the monuments of the city, causes considerable interest among the scientific community to this place. Now scientists are trying to answer the question, when did people start to build a city here.

Another team of scientists from the University of California is looking for the tomb of Genghis Khan, using drones and satellite imagery. Nobody knows the location of the grave; there are only indirect indications of the place where it is located. According to one of the legends (and there are many of them), a riverbed was laid above his grave so that this place could not be found (the Sumerian king Uruk Gilgamesh was buried in the same way). Nowadays, in addition to historians, thousands of volunteers who search for the satellite images, old texts, any information that could help in the search, are searching for the tomb of Genghis Khan.
