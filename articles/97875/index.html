Smart Online Buyer's Guide

The variety of online stores is extremely high - even in Russia, and in Western countries - even more so. Buying in Western stores is attractive for two reasons: often it allows you to save money, and sometimes it is generally the only way to buy goods. In order to purchase the right product, there is no need to devote days and weeks to independently combing the sites of these stores, choosing the best option and suffering due to the fact that not all stores want to accept cards from Russian banks and even more so deliver their goods to Russia. You can make a purchase in a few minutes and in just two stages - first select a product and store using specialized selection services (similar to the usual Price.ru and Yandex.Market Russians) and then, without thinking about payment and delivery, place an order using the EbayToday service . ru , which works not only with online auction eBay, but almost with any American and British online stores.

TheFind



TheFind also coped quite well in order to find Google Nexus One - though here, like Google, I had to use a trick and sort the output by price after the request: there are no product categories, so the phone is in the same issue with chargers and cases. Casio watches in TheFind were search immeasurably nicer than Google: in the menu to the right of the search results, you can set a lot of parameters that facilitate the selection.

Shopzilla could not find the Google Nexus One. That is, he knew about the goods, of course, but he could not find the stores that they sell.

Disadvantages: the search is clearly imperfect. Shopzilla knows about Nexus One, and about Amazon, Nexus One is being sold on Amazon, but Shopzilla did not find it.
