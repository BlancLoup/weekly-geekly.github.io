Yandeks.Narod: hosting moves to uCoz

Yandex transfers its website hosting to the uCoz web service. In March, all "popular" sites will move to the uCoz platform. Website addresses will remain the same, the content will remain. However, users will have much more opportunities to develop their sites.

The decision to transfer the service is due to the fact that the Internet market and the needs of people have changed. At the beginning of the century, when we opened the People, few had a website. But many wanted to start it. We could do it in five minutes using ready-made templates. No special knowledge was required, only desire. As a result, the People became one of the most popular free hosting sites in runet, where people tried to create their first sites. Someone wanted to tell about their business on the Internet, someone - about themselves. Then users only learned to work with sites, even not always understanding why they are needed.

Now the situation is completely different. Everyone knows why sites are needed, but they have different requirements for them. Many business owners are no longer satisfied with simple web pages. To have a decent representation on the Internet, they turn to the development of websites to professionals. And users who want to tell others about their personal thoughts and impressions, separate personal sites are no longer needed. For this they have enough profiles in social networks. People as a hosting site has ceased to be a massive service. Of course, a certain demand for such services remained. But companies that specialize in services for creating websites do an excellent job with it. It is this company that we decided to transfer to the ‚Äúpopular‚Äù sites and chose uCoz.

uCoz is one of the most popular content management systems. This is a free service, where there is a modern designer, hundreds of templates and dozens of modules for creating web pages. After moving the hosting to uCoz all these tools will be available to users of the People. And those people who are seriously engaged in their sites will be able to develop them further. Now uCoz is completing preparations for the reception of "popular" sites, as the company reports in its blog .

We transfer only hosting sites to uCoz.ru. Files that are stored on the People, remain in the jurisdiction of Yandex. They will be available to users as usual. Emails sent to addresses of the form name@narod.ru will still arrive at Yandex.Mail.
