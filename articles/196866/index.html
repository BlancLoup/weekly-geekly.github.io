Google introduced the 11-inch chromebook from HP



Google released the HP Chromebook 11 - 11-inch laptop on Chrome OS with an IPS-display resolution of 1366 x 768 and a design inspired by the Chromebook Pixel, writes The Verge. It is charged via Micro USB, so that owners of smartphones and tablets on Android will be able to use one charger for all their devices.

Buyers of the new Chromebook will get 100 GB in Google Drive for two years, full access to Google Play Music for 60 days and access to the Internet from GoGo for 12 air travel. It costs $ 279 and is already available for order in the US and the UK through Google Play, Best Buy, Amazon, and HP directly. A 4G LTE version is also planned, but its price and release date are not yet known.

As he writes in his review of The Verge, the design of the Chromebook 11 is very similar to the plastic MacBook that is no longer in production. Ports are located only on the left side, all the speaker grilles and screws are hidden. The laptop is available in black and white with inlays of blue, red, yellow and green. The black version looks almost the same as the original glossy CR-48.

Despite its smaller size, the Chromebook 11 still has a full-sized keyboard and a fully interactive touchpad. A VGA camera is built into the panel around the display. The speakers, as in the Chromebook Pixel, are located under the keyboard - according to Google, this allows you to avoid muting the sound when using the laptop on your lap.

According to Google, the new display in Chromebook 11 is 50% brighter than most laptops on the market. Its IPS panel provides a 176-degree viewing angle and a very wide color gamut. Chromebook body is made of plastic, but the magnesium frame provides strength, and it weighs only 1.04 kg. It works on a Samsung Exynos 5250 dual-core processor and 2 GB of RAM, and also has 16 GB of internal memory. From the ports - two USB 2.0, a slot for Micro-SIM and Micro USB for charging and Slimport. Promised about six hours of battery life.

Google also said that all Chromebooks already occupy 25% of the laptop market is cheaper than $ 300, and Samsung Chromebook remains the most popular laptop in Amazon since its release. The Chromebook 11 retains all the virtues of the Samsung version, and also improved the screen and design, so it looks like it is doomed to success.
