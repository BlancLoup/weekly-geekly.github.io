Now you can run old DOS viruses on Archive.org.



A collection of old viruses has opened on Archive.org - The Malware Museum . There you can find and run malware, even the era of DOS, that is, 80-90-ies.

Viruses of those times after the infection of the system usually showed some kind of animation or displayed a message. Thanks to the use of an emulator and the removal of some malicious instructions from viruses, now everyone can experience what a virus infection under DOS looked like.

Viruses run in full screen mode and with sound.

Judging by the names, some viruses obviously occur in the spaces of the former USSR.
