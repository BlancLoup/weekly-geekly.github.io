How to make the user think about the openness of the software?


Do people really don‚Äôt attach importance to the openness of the products they use, or they just don‚Äôt know about the existence of such things? If the latter, how can we help people get acquainted with open source software?

We are afraid of what we do not know.

This is what Ubuntu is trying to do.
