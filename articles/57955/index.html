5th Kharkov OPEN

For those who not only read the news about acm tournaments, but he himself is not averse to participating in them ...
For those who have a couple of free hours on weekends for interesting tasks ...

This Saturday, April 25, the fifth in a row, open championship of Kharkov on sports programming will be held. Entry to the championship extended until April 24.

The championship is educational, designed primarily for schoolchildren and students of elementary courses, therefore it is unlikely to be of interest to "experienced" athletes from the world of programming.

But this is a great chance to try your hand for those who have long wanted to try themselves in ACM competitions, as well as for those whose experience does not allow them to participate in major international tournaments.

You can evaluate the level of tasks in the tournaments of the past years in the archive .
Note: this is a separate testing system that requires separate registration.

You can participate in the championship both onsite (in Kharkov, Kiev ... ) and online.

Tournament format: team, acm
Allowed languages and used compilers
C (gcc / 4.1 i486-linux-gnu)
C ++ (g ++ / 4.1 i486-linux-gnu)
Pascal (fpc / 2.2 for i386)
Object pascal (fpc / 2.2 Delphi mode)

Approximate distribution of task complexity by division:
  • the first is the main division, the tasks of the qualifying level of the CBOSS tournament
  • second - arrays, strings, structures, textbook algorithms
  • the third one is reading from / writing to a file, basic elements, several correctly written ifs

More information about the championship and its rules, see the link .

Please, after registering correctly and completely fill out the profile before submitting the application for the competition, you can also indicate that you are registered by the link from habrakhabra - this will be accelerated by the moderators.
