International Mobile Conference # MBLT14

Twitter, Line, Facebook, Airbnb, T-Mobile, Samsung, Distimo, Aviasales, Qualcomm, JetBlue Airways, Opera, Paypal. Representatives of these and many other companies will share their experience on May 23 in Moscow at the international mobile conference # MBLT14 .



This is the third MBLT conference, and each time we are trying to bring top foreign speakers to Russia who can share truly unique information about the use of mobile technologies in various business areas. How were MBLT12 and MBLT13 can be viewed in the reports for the last and last year.

And what will happen this year - we read under the cut.


This year, representatives of Opera Software are coming to us - CEO Lars Boylesen and senior vice president of desktop systems Christian Colondra. Head of the international Twitter development team Chris Chabot. Distimo Vice President of EMEA Richard Pijon. Head of Strategic Partnership at Deutsche Telecom AG, Joseph Norona. Development Director LINE in Russia Kohta Fuji. Jonathan Stephen, Head of Mobile and Developing Technologies at JetBlue Airways, and many, many others . The list will be updated until the end of April.

They will talk about mobile solutions in travel, advertising and business. What changes are coming in the near future, how wearable gadgets will change the market and in what direction the mobile world is developing. How are products and services globalized? How do mobile technologies change business processes, what are the strategies of leading companies? Benchmarking cases, wearable gadgets, look to the future - # MBLT14 will inspire you for new ideas and solutions.

Detailed information about the speakers and a tentative schedule is available on the official website .
