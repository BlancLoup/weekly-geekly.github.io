Live Mobile in a week in Moscow

On November 12-13, Live Mobile will be held in Digital October in Moscow ! .

The event will be attended by: App Annie, Flurry, ChartBoost, AviaSales, Game Insight and 20 others.

Under the cut, I'll tell you why it is worth visiting the conference and how to get to it for free.




Live Mobile is mainly devoted to the promotion of applications, mobile games and start-ups. Organizer - Game Insight.

The first reason to attend the conference is to listen, see and chat with representatives from Flurry , App Annie and ChartBoost . These companies make services known all over the world, in fact they influence the way the mobile application market looks and will look. In Russia, they are rare.

By the way, have you already seen the new analytics system for mobile advertising Advertising Analytics from AppAnnie? Most likely they will tell about it. And Simon Podd from Flurry will share the latest data on user behavior in mobile games.



Ivan Kozlov from AviaSales, Kirill Makharinsky from Ostrovka and Ilya Dyer from the Poster will tell about their experience on the mobile services panel. In my opinion, these are the few people in Russia who know how to create mobile products, how to make them comfortable and in demand. Worth to listen.

I recommend watching Anton Shayakhov from AviaSales, where he talks about the current how they created and promoted the mobile application. Interesting report + numbers from various advertising channels.




Nikolay Evdokimov, the founder of SeoPult, will talk about strategies for bringing an application to the top and its new project AppInTop . The service is in closed beta. Personally, I am very interested in what‚Äôs inside and if Nicholas can repeat the success of SeoPult.



On iOS 7, the briefing will talk about iOS7, its new API, and the 10 most important things when developing applications. Briefing closed, only 30 places to get to it after registering with Live Mobile write to iOS7@livemobilecongress.com .

The briefing is conducted by one foreign company which, for some unknown reason, cannot be called. In general, the description sounds boring and strange, but I assure you, you will not regret if you get into these 30 developers.


And of course, afterparty, where you can communicate in an informal setting with mobile investors, mobile services representatives, mobile game creators, Samsung, Huawei, Yota and many others.

(who will be on the afterparty, please ask Yota, why do they release Shine fitness tracker and what is the company's development strategy now after buying with Megaphone)
