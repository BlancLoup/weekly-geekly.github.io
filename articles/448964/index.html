Toaster gives users more rights.

Hello from the Toaster team! Today we are launching a new feature that will allow experienced Toaster users to assume the rights of tag curators. Details under the cut.



To begin with, who are the curators of the tag? The curator is a user with extended rights, he can mark answers as solutions as well as the author of the question, as well as manage the tag description without prior approval from the administration of the resource.

In our vision, the curator is not just a person with additional capabilities, but also an active user of the service who is interested in the technology for which he is responsible.

All users whose contribution to a tag is 100 (or more) points will see a message about the possibility of becoming a curator of the tag. After that, if desired, will be able to apply.

On the tag page we will list all his curators, because the Toaster must know his characters. In addition, the responses and comments of the curators will be highlighted, just as the authors now stand out.



This is a small innovation, but we hope it will improve the Toaster a little more and help you find answers to questions faster. Please write in the comments, would you like to become a curator?
