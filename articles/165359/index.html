Let's calculate the savings.

Let 1 kWh cost 2.3 rubles
Then when using the lamp in the corridor in the mode from 7-00 to 9-00, from 18-00 to 23-00 Mon-Fri
from 10-00 to 23-00 Sat, Sun
we will receive what will be in a month ((2 + 5) * 5 + 13 * 2) * 4 = 244 hours of work
Recalculate in rubles
For incandescent 93 watts. (one is not enough, but suppose that one) 244 * 0.093 * 2.3 = 52 r / month
For incandescent 60 W (2 lamps are needed because dimly) 244 * 0.06 * 2.3 * 2 = 67 p / month
For LED 3W. (about 3-4 is needed very dimly) then 244 * 0.003 * 2.3 * 4 = 6.7 r / month
For LED 9 W (you need 2, because dim) then 244 * 0.009 * 2.3 * 2 = 10.10 p / month
For Energy 26 W (generally shines quite well, but one lamp is not enough, we leave one) 244 * 0.026 * 2.3 = 14.6 p / month

Suppose that the gas, as well as the LED, will work for 2 years in good wiring conditions (I have been burning in the kitchen for 2 years), and the incandescence is 0.5 years
Then we calculate the cost of lighting, taking into account the cost of the lamp

For incandescent 93 watts. 52 * 24 + 12 * 4 = 1296 r / 2 years
For incandescent 60 W (2 pieces) 67 * 24 + 8 * 10 = 1688 r / 2 years
For LED 3W. (4 pieces) 6.7 * 24 + 4 * 300 = 1360r / 2years
For 9W LEDs (2 pieces) 10 * 24 + 2 * 1100 = 2440 r / 2 years (10 * 24 + 2 * 300 = 840 r / 2 years) *
For Energy 26 W 14 * 24 + 150 = 486 r / 2 years
* Price for ordering lamps from China, not buying in the Russian Federation

Conclusion.
Currently, the market for LED lamps in Russia is very poorly developed, and prices leave much to be desired. Switching to them is not profitable and it is not yet known how long the LED lamp will last. The leader of profitability remains a gas energy-saving lamp.
