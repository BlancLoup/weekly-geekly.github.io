Moscow, heat melted AppleTV

Today's Moscow heat has yet finished my prefix AppleTV. Without the possibility of a cool prefix, the prefix began to hang and show the tongue (apple). There was no way for my wife to watch the next series of Desperate Housewives. I had to turn off the minutes for 30-40, so that it was completely cooled, and watch the movie with the cords connected to the plasma and the MacBook receiver.
What conclusions should be drawn to the developers:
1) Still, equip AppleTV with a cooling system or build a circuit with less heat transfer.
2) Improve the system of sleep or passive state of the console. Now it is in such a state of non-cooling and warm all the time - I hope this will be corrected with the next software upgrade.
