Flee camera for on-the-fly shooting

Turkish designer Hakan Bogazpinar, creating a concept camera called Flee, in the literal sense of the word offers to shoot "on the fly." Flee - a badminton shuttlecock with a camera inside - can be thrown like a plate of frisbee. During the flight, the device can take a couple of cool pictures.


Flee takes pictures at a certain interval, which the user can configure himself. The received photo camera can automatically transfer to your mobile phone via Bluetooth.
