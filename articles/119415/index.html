HackDay # 16 in St. Petersburg, May 28-29

We invite you to the final in the spring series HackDay # 16. Subjects - projects with a thoughtful business component. At the presentation of finished projects there will be a lot of investors, get ready in advance.
The goal is to develop a project from idea to prototype in two days.
Since the beginning of the year, HackDay traveled to Moscow, Voronezh, Yaroslavl and Almaty, in June we will go to Novosibirsk. We made a lot of new friends and developed many great projects.
The upcoming HackDay # 16 promises to be extremely fruitful. We invited specialists from leading software companies to conduct master classes and prepared many valuable prizes from sponsors for the best projects.
Traditionally, we provide jobs, wi-fi, tea-coffee-cookies.
