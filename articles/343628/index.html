DevDay about microservices. Record the best reports



Last Friday, we gathered 140 developers to talk about microservices - deployment, coordination, consistency. We share with you a record of the two best, in the opinion of the participants, speeches.

That such DevDay, we told in this article .



Deploy microservices // Alexey Baitov

Engineer Alexey Baitov told how 2GIS continuously delivers microservice architecture, which is already in production. And also about how we changed the traditional approach of connectedness of microservices: removed unnecessary integrations within the team and got rid of the premature legacy of maintaining old versions of the API.



Microservices and data consistency // Denis Ivanov

Denis Ivanov - team leader of the ERM team. His report on how to achieve a guarantee of consistency and maximum protection against failures, if microservices jointly change data in several repositories.


Add-on: here Denis talks about how to make a REST service on ASP.NET Core and run it on Kubernetes. And here is an open-source versioned data storage system on .NET.

Videos from other meetings can be viewed on the DevDay channel on Youtube.
