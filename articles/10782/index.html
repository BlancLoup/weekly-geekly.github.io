Ukraine shows a big growth in the provision of IT outsourcing services

According to the publication informationweek.com , citing a study by the independent agency for monitoring the IT services outsourcing market GoalEurope, Ukraine showed a huge increase - 47% compared with last year.

Of course, as the agency notes, $ 246 million a year cannot be compared with the multi-billion dollar turnover of India in this area, however, the growth dynamics for the country that went to this market more than one year ago is impressive.
According to GoalEurope, the lack of full-fledged integration of Ukraine into the economy of a united Europe is just the impetus for the growth of outsourcing services. This may include the fall in the national currency rate to the increased Euro and the presence of relative administrative barriers for the emigration of specialists to the countries of Western Europe (compared to other Eastern European EU member states). At the same time, GoalEurope predicts an increase in the share of Ukraine in the market in the coming years.
As noted in the study, the main location of Ukrainian companies remains Kiev - up to half of all specialists involved in the provision of IT services are located there. Other important cities are called Lviv and Kharkiv.
According to the agency, it is now important for the Ukrainian market to rally around several main producers, and then the share of Ukraine can increase dramatically and significantly. At the moment, only 7 out of 70 surveyed service providers employed more than 300 people, while in Western European and American companies there is a tendency to use integrated services.
