Yandex. Photos released extension for iPhoto

All Mac OS X users, accustomed to storing all their photos in iPhoto and suffering from the inability to upload them to Yandex. Photos directly from their favorite program, now have the opportunity.

The extension for iPhoto, released by Yandex. Photos, provides the main possibilities for working with the service - creating and editing albums, indicating the level of access to photos, loved by all 18+, hiding the original and, of course, actually downloading one or more photos.

The extension works on both Tiger and Leopard and supports iPhoto 6 and 7 versions.
