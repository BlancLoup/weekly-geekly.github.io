Another 10 TB storage from the Chinese - now distributes Alibaba (upd)

Today, the Chinese group of companies Alibaba Group has announced that it has purchased the Kanbox service (Dropbox clone) for an unnamed amount and now provides account holders with 10 TB of cloud storage for free.

Kanbox - a solid service, according to the creators, which in China are used by about 15 million people. There are both desktop clients and mobile (Windows, Mac, iPhone, Android), allowing users to upload files to the service and synchronize devices with each other.

To get 10 TB, you need to have an account on Kanbox and install the Laiwang mobile app (available for both Android and iOS) - a Chinese instant messenger for a mobile social network. Account in Laiwang will need to be linked to Kanbox, after which the place will be allocated.

UPD : yes, the mechanism works (albeit a precedent from China), the instruction is expected soon.
