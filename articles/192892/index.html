Set in circles 239



Soon starts enrollment in the circles of the famous school 239 (St. Petersburg). For children of Peter and LO, from 6 to 11 classes. All classes are free and are conducted by leading teachers in their field.

I am sure that the following directions will be interesting for Habrovites:
Math Center
Robotic Center
It direction
Nanotechnology (yes, 239 has its own nanolaboratory)


Registration in the circles is carried out by written statements of parents and by compulsory registration on the school website from September 10 to September 15. More information on the link http://239.ru/kruzhok/

Today, September 7, from 16 to 18 there will be a fair where you can get acquainted with the leaders and members of the circles. St. Petersburg, Kirochnaya 8a, second building.

Ps. Well, so as not to get up twice.

On September 8, on Sunday, at 12:00, there will be an open lecture by Pavel Pevzner, a leading expert in the field of bioinformatics and computational biology. The lecture will consist of small parts of 15 minutes (between them you can and need to ask questions) and will last 2 hours. Video recording of this lecture, which is carried out by Lectorium, will become the basis of a distance course, which starts on coursera at the end of this year. The lecture will be held in English. Venue: assembly hall of the second building.

Speaker: Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor, University of Southern California, University of Pennsylvania, University of California, San Diego. He is a member of the Scientific Advisory Board of the Genome Institute (Singapore) and a member of the editorial board of the Journal of Computational Biology - PLOS Computational Biology, the winner of the first 2010 mega-grants competition.
