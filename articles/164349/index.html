Videobook ultrabook (tablet) Lenovo IdeaPad Yoga-13



Lenovo IdeaPad Yoga 13 is a hybrid of ultrabook and tablet, which boasts a 13.3-inch touchscreen IPS display with a resolution of 1600x900. The devices operate on third-generation low-voltage Intel Core processors (i3 / i5 / i7), can carry on board from 4 to 8 GB of RAM and 128 or 256 GB solid-state drive for data storage.

The main feature of IdeaPad Yoga-13 is the mechanism for turning it from a tablet into an ultrabook and back. The display opens to any angle up to 360 degrees, which allows you to use the device not only in the form of a tablet and ultrabook, but also in intermediate configurations.

The machine runs under Windows 8 and is a rather interesting tool both for creating content and for its consumption.
