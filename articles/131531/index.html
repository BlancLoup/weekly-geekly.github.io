October 31 - Especially for IT Park: Master class by famous Russian technological entrepreneur Sergey Belousov!

October 31 (Monday) at 15:00 in the Technopark in the field of high technologies will be held a master class by Sergey Belousov, a senior partner of the seed investment fund Runa Capital , the founder and chairman of the board of directors of Parallels , the world leader in developing solutions for virtualization and automation of service providers , companies and individual users, creator of Rolsen and Acronis .

Sergei IT Park guests will talk about the secrets of creating global companies with Russian roots. Listeners will learn:

how Parallels was created;
how to find the right investor;
what you need to get investments of venture capital fund Runa Capital,
how Runa Capital finds talented teams and grows international companies of them;
how to use global trends when creating an IT company;
how to bring the company and product to the international market;
what is the difference between approaches to entrepreneurship in Russia and abroad.
