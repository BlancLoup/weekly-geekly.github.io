Toaster. Userstyle. Update

A few hours ago, the administration announced the revival of the resource "Toaster". But not in the form it was, but by separating the Q & A section from the habr into a separate site. The first thing that caught my eye was the site left to the left. Maybe on laptops will look good, but on my screen you can place 2 such sites.

Without hesitation, I decided to correct the styles for myself, since I visit the section (now the site) almost every day.
Of the changes:
  • Fixed location of the site header
  • The site is centered, the left panel remains untouched.
  • The main part of the site has become wider
  • Added transparency to tags, so as not to catch the eye and changed the font to Arial
  • Changed the text color in the pop-up window at the tag, made more contrast
  • Just a couple of subtle little things
