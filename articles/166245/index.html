500,000,000 Foursquare check-ins on satellite map

Geolocation service Foursquare has issued a map, on which 500,000,000 check-ins carried out by users over the past three months are marked.



In their post , company employees compare Moscow with a spider:



Recently, Foursquare has significantly changed its concept, and from a scout game with quests and badges, it began to turn into a service that actively uses user feedback. In addition, powerful search tools for finding the right places and places appeared in Foursquare.

In their blog, the Foursquare team writes that they compare the recommendation system of the service with "Google PageRank for the real world." Thus, they consider in Foursquare, thanks to their users, they create a rating of places and institutions around the world, giving them an objective and independent assessment.

This map clearly shows where in our country the largest clusters of Foursquare users are.
