New Mac Pro

Apple has announced a new Mac Pro. There are two models to choose from. With one quad-core Intel Xeon 3500 (Nehalem) processor with a frequency of 2.66 GHz and two Intel Xeon 5500 chips 2.26 GHz.



The initial configuration of a single-processor Mac Pro is 3 GB of RAM, 640 GB of HDD, 512-megabyte (GDDR3) NVIDIA GeForce GT 120 video card and 18-speed SuperDrive (DVD ¬± R DL / DVD ¬± RW / CD-RW).
