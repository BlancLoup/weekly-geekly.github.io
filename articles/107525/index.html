Software for the provision of domain name registration services

REG.RU prepared a new version of the REG.Panel distributed registration system for its partners. REG.Panel is a unique software developed by REG.RU specialists, which allows partners to create their own dealer network for registering and renewing domain names. The system also provides an opportunity to serve end-users on the basis of its own Internet site in automatic mode.

Version 3.0 is completely different from the previous one. It contains more than 200 updates and changes, many of which will be a technological breakthrough in organizing the work of resellers.

REG.Panel 3.0 introduced new universal user interfaces, IDN domains in .COM / .NET / .SU zones, pre-order and order domains. RF. By the way, for partners REG.RU set a special price for registration in the zone. RF - 600 rubles. And all partners working with REG.Panel get the opportunity to use special tariffs in the .RU zone - from 95 rubles per domain.

Also now, in the new version of the program, a larger number of domain zones are available for order (39 + IDN) for customers. In addition, new services were added - VPS, Web-forwarding, certificates, internal messaging system, news feed, full basket, bulk domain renewal, bulk DNS change, and domain transfer to another REG.Panel user and partial transfer of control to REG user. RU. Special attention in the new version of REG.Panel deserves the introduction of personal data protection methods provided for by Federal Law 152 (Federal Law on Personal Data), which will be presented to the company's partners in December of this year.

REG.Panel version 3.0 is already available for direct partners of the company. Even if you are not a partner of REG.RU, you can already get acquainted with all the charms of the system by testing it in the demo version

We, as always, will be glad to all feedback and suggestions, especially from our partners, for whom we have come up with all this.
