Two scroll wheel modes


Here it should be noted that the function is very convenient, especially when surfing the Internet, various documents in Microsoft Office and programming. In one mode - there is a ratchet, like most mice. In the second mode - it rotates freely by inertia. If it is good to twist, then 20-30 seconds turns itself for sure. Modes are switched by the first button above the wheel. By the way, this is the only button that cannot be reprogrammed.

Materials and quality


Very reminiscent of "softtach" on the phones. Pleasant to the touch, does not slip but not sandpaper. Lyuftov, squeaks not observed. Washes perfectly, calmly withstands rubbing with alcohol. Practically does not get dirty, but in the middle of the four side keys dirt sometimes gathers, which is easily wiped off with a thin cloth. Glossy bottom and a few sidewalls - naturally scratched, but this is hardly so important, because it is almost never visible.

Only 1 AA element. If good quality then it is 2500-3000 mAh. I have enough for 6-7 days with 1-2 hours of games and 1-2 hours of surfing the Internet. At the weekend I can play 4-5 hours a day for 3 days in a row for sure, and I will still have 2-5 hours of surfing.

13 buttons x 6 profiles


Actually, 13 is one that switches the wheel modes not counted.
All others: G11 (at the crown of the head), left / right main, 4 at the side under the thumb, 3 to the left of the index finger and the wheel: press down, left, right. And everything can be reprogrammed for any needs. It is possible to set macros, keyboard shortcuts, specific functions, for example: viewing mail.

Do not forget that if you have a little 13 buttons, or you work in several fundamentally different applications / games, then the manufacturer thought and equipped this gadget with built-in memory that can store 6 fully independent profiles, but since you need to use 1 more to switch profiles button, in the end we get 12x6 = 72 buttons on the mouse, which is very, very not bad.

Profiles are created in the corresponding program from the official site. By the way, the disc is not included, so you have to search and download yourself, which today is unlikely to cause any special problems, but still, some negative sediment remains ...

On the side there are three LEDs that show the selected mode (in orange), as well as the battery charge (in green and red).

Sensor


5700 points. Like me just mad stock. It costs 1200, and in games I decrease the sensitivity even more - to about 800.
You can put the dpi switch on the button, and the number of modes and the value for each mode are selected in increments of up to 1 dpi.

About the sensor - I tried on all possible surfaces: colored, transparent, textured, wool, etc. Everywhere works equally well. No comments.

About problems


To be completely honest, the mouse is just over a year old. None of the problems that I met on the forums and in the reviews did not come across. I use it quite carefully: it does not fall, at least once a week dust is wiped off, and once a month it is vacuumed.

Impressions


Not a bit sorry that he bought it. There are no more delays in games, responsiveness is all 101%. True, I use only 1 profile, since I no longer need it. Ease of use and quality - at a height that you would expect when using peripherals from Logitech. Recommend.

At the moment, it is this model that has already been discontinued, and the replacement has been the updated Logitech G700s, in which a nice picture appeared and increased to 8200 dpi, the price has become even higher. The remaining characteristics are identical.
