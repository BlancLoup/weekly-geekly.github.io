New bonuses to AlterGeo mayors in new cafes

Recently, we wrote about cooperation with the Moscow restaurants IL Patio and Planet Sushi , within which AlterGeo users, who became the mayors of these establishments, now receive a mojito, cafe or juice as a gift.

We are glad to announce friendship with new excellent places in Moscow - SQUAT-Cafe, ArteFAQ, FAQ-Cafe and Sisters Grimm.

Becoming the "mayor" of these institutions, you can get a refreshing cocktail:

SQUAT-Cafe : Big Summer Squat (alcoholic or non-alcoholic)
ArteFAQ-cafe : Mojito (alcoholic or non-alcoholic)
FAQ-Cafe : Beer cocktail (alcoholic or alcoholic)
Sisters Grimm : Cucumber lemonade (non-alcoholic, and how else?)
