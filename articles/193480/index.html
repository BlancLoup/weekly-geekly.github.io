The 53rd International Consumer Electronics Show IFA 2013 concluded yesterday in Berlin. Traditionally, a large number of companies of various caliber gathered to show people the latest innovations in technology and technology, starting from vacuum cleaners with an innovative steering mechanism and smart watches and ending with the latest UHD and OLED TVs with a diagonal of 50 inches and above.

Samsung Electronics traditionally announced its latest developments, almost in all the main areas in which the company has been operating for many years. All interested persons we ask to glance under kat to get acquainted with the most interesting of them.

One of the most anticipated new products was the flagship smartphone Samsung GALAXY Note 3 , the last representative of the GALAXY Note family. The mobile device received an increased Full HD Super AMOLED display with a diagonal of 5.7 inches, while it became thinner (8.3 mm) and dropped some weight (up to 168 g). The battery capacity has increased to 3200 mAh. Amateur photographers will appreciate the 13-megapixel camera with smart stabilizer and a powerful CRI LED flash on board. The amount of operating memory is unprecedented for mobile devices - 3 GB.

The novelty, which has expanded the line of technology with a proprietary design of Samsung GALAXY, is equipped with a housing with an already traditional textured back cover. The textured pattern on the side panel of the device reminds of the company's classic notebooks. Samsung GALAXY Note 3 will be available in two colors - black and white. After the start of sales will be available to a variety of accessories for the gadget: dozens of interchangeable back panels and covers, covers of different shades.

Improved electronic pen S Pen, which is included with the smartphone, is designed to further simplify the usual method of handwriting information and cope with a large number of tasks with one click. When you press the S Pen button and hover on the display, the Context Commands menu appears with five useful features. Thanks to this, there is no need to switch between screens, memorize commands or search for information in several menus.

Context Commands provides access to the following functions:
  • The function "Active Notes" allows you to automatically convert handwritten text into a printed in the desired format using a handwritten command, make calls, add contacts, find the address on the map or information online, compile a task list and much more.
  • The Album of Fragments feature will help you collect and organize data from many different sources, including websites, YouTube videos and image galleries, in one place. The user can save a large amount of data and return to working with them after some time, while gaining access not only to information, but also to links to all sources.
  • The "Instant Comments" feature allows you to take a snapshot of the page open on the screen and leave comments directly on the image.
  • The S Finder feature provides access to advanced content search on a device, regardless of its type. Using keywords and filters, for example, dates, location and file type, you can quickly find documents, events, discussion history, which will be collected in one place. In addition, the smartphone supports searching for handwriting data, symbols and formulas.
  • The window-in-window feature expands the capabilities of the digital pen. Thanks to this feature, S Pen can be used not only for drawings and recordings, but also for working with several windows in multitasking mode. It is enough to draw a window of any size on the smartphone screen to automatically open one of the applications in it, for example, YouTube, a calculator or a web browser, without interrupting work with already running programs.

Not one mobile device is rich in Samsung, which the IFA 2013 guests once again saw for themselves as soon as the company introduced its new UHD devices: a 98-inch LFD display for the commercial segment and a 31.5-inch monitor .

The 98-inch new Samsung is the industry‚Äôs largest LFD display with a resolution 4 times higher than that of standard Full HD models, and, of course, an incredibly detailed and realistic image. If necessary, the display can be easily installed in a vertical position. This was amply demonstrated at the exhibition itself in the form of giant 171-inch video walls made up of three vertically arranged UHD displays.



At the exhibition, Samsung also unveiled the first Ultra HD monitor. Undoubtedly, this is the largest model of the released models. The 31.5-inch screen provides a clear and vivid image of UHD-resolution, the number of pixels is 8.3 million and is almost 4 times higher than the performance of Full HD monitors with 2.07 million pixels.



Any such UHD monitor can be calibrated for the most accurate display of colors. The display is conventionally divided into 25 sections, on each of which with the help of an external calibrating device, the correctness of color rendition, uniformity of the backlight and white balance are carefully checked. Calibration with optional software is also available. In addition, due to the display of 1 billion colors and wide color gamut of Adobe RGB 99%, you can get richer shades compared to the image of conventional monitors.

Also close attention of the public was drawn to the new UHD-TVs with a curved display - Samsung Curved UHD TV . Panoramic displays were presented in two versions - with a diagonal of 55 and 65 inches.



The production of UHD TVs with a curved display is a much more complicated process compared to the same OLED screens of a similar shape. Since the UHD-device market is at the initial stage of development, the company intends to carefully analyze consumer reviews after the release of Samsung Curved UHD TV, in order to fully respond to consumer demands in the future.

New items have expanded the series of Samsung UHD TVs, which currently include models with a diagonal of 110, 98, 85, 65 and 55 inches.

Closed the chain of latest displays Samsung new UHD OLED TVs . They provide the same extremely realistic and detailed Ultra HD resolution image with the highest brightness and contrast, achieved thanks to the latest OLED technology, 8 million pixels and 4 times higher resolution than Full HD models.



The combination of OLED technology and self-emitting sub-pixels allowed to bring the image quality and contrast to a new level.
