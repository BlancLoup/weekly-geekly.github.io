Prehistory


You've been sleeping, and I counted you. The other day I decided to try out phpQuery - I read the documentation, I was already preparing to parse something like this, but it turned out that it was not so easy to find a suitable (purely subjective) object for the experiments. (By the way, it is not clear why. Karma, as for me, has a greater value and is an important indicator of Habradush - every +1 is earned by hard work.)

Thus, the task appeared - to make a top according to karma. Along the way, it was decided to take another series of indicators - Habravozrast (how many years on the site) and rating ( until the heap for completeness). The result was a modest habrastatistika by age groups.

Age groups


I will note once again that "age" is understood as the age of a habrasaccount - the number of years spent on the site. Thus, the following groups were taken:
  1. Up to 6 months (inclusive)
  2. From 6 months to a year
  3. <1 year - total for the first year
  4. 1 year - meaning the period from 1 year to 2 years, i.e. rounding to integer
  5. 2 years
  6. 3 years
  7. 4 years
  8. 5 years
  9. The rest - a few people, in the date of registration of the account which is listed in 1970.

The latter were decided to be taken out as a separate group, since these people, apparently, are related to the administration and the date of registration is obviously not correct.
Not counted - 612 people. This includes accounts of administrators, moderators and all those who are not involved in habrarating (not displayed in the "People" section).

The most karmist group are users in a habra age from one to two. Obviously, by the 4th year there is a stabilization of the state and leveling of the situation (karma per person - 4.35).
