Hacked firmware lumix GH1, GH2, G2, GF1, GF2

February 18 Vitaliy_Kiselev announced the release of a new version of the program PTool v3.64d , which allows to modify the original firmware Lumix micro 4/3.

Advantages of the modified firmware:


  1. Interface unlock in all languages ‚Äã‚Äã(if someone bought abroad);
  2. Removal of the 30 minute recording limit (The restriction was made by the manufacturer in order to avoid taxes in Europe on video recorders that record over 30 minutes) ;
  3. Removing ISO restrictions in video mode;
  4. Change of video recording standard PAL / NTSC;
  5. Changes in MJPEG and AVCHD encoder settings; Auto Quantizer - patch for better video quality in poorly detailed scenes;

How to flash:

  • We mark the necessary patches. Version increment should always be marked.
  • Save the modified firmware. Change the last digit to what you want.
  • Copy the firmware to the root of the SD card. We charge the battery. Don't even think about the power supply!
