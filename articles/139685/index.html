As you can see, the payment did not go straight, but was disguised as payment for access to download files from the megashara.24n6.ru file hosting service, to whom the payment was made was determined via the feed specified in the configs.

The 24n6.ru website contains the rules (http://24n6.ru/subscription_rules.php) for the service, where it is stated that the cost is from 175 rubles per day and then descending. And the first day is free. those. about the withdrawal of money a person learns only in a day.

Total we have:
  1. Universal script that allows you to "block" any sites (just change the css), which do not check the work under the frame and do not go out from under the frame.
  2. Payments are carried out secretly, under the guise of paying for access to the content of the file hosting service.
  3. Money starts to take off immediately, so that people do not understand what's the matter.
  4. Judging by the aff_query parameter, there was previously support or planned work through anonymizers.


All this might not have happened if:
  1. Classmates would check the work under the frame or go out from under the frame.
  2. The file hosting service checked 24n6.ru would check the referer or set up a special cookie, before making a payment.
  3. Advertising sites controlled what they advertised and automatically opened.
  4. The billing services would not give anyone the opportunity to receive payment via SMS or through a subscription.




In general, be careful!
