The final of the SAP contest Koder 2017 will be held live

In early April, we announced the competition ‚ÄúSAP Koder‚Äù . Participants had to offer their projects in specified areas, made on the basis of the SAP Cloud Platform. All this time, the participants prepared their decisions - and now it is time to present them. The solutions turned out to be interesting, so we suggest you join the viewing. The live broadcast of the finals of the SAP Coder 2017 competition will take place on June 28 from 10:00 to 14:00 (Moscow time). To not miss the live broadcast, add the event to your calendar .

SAP Coder 2017 is a contest for anyone who seeks to master the most modern development approaches. This year SAP CIS holds this competition for the first time. As part of the competition, participants develop their own applications to solve real business problems provided by Russian SAP customers. Development is conducted on the SAP Cloud Platform, an innovative open SAP cloud platform offering digital services for the Internet of things, machine learning, predictive analytics and other advanced business scenarios. Read more about the competition on the website .

During the broadcast, you will hear about technological trends, get acquainted with the SAP strategy in the field of platform technologies, learn about examples of business development using the SAP cloud platform.

11:30 - Break

11:45 - Performance of the contest finalists

13:30 - Award winners

The SAP Cloud Platform is an SAP open cloud platform that provides data storage and processing capabilities in SAP HANA and ASE, as well as in PostgreSQL, MongoDB and Hadoop open source systems. The SAP Cloud Platform is ideal for developing mobile and HTML applications that can be easily integrated with any cloud or local systems thanks to the integration service integrated into the SAP Cloud Platform. Currently, the SAP Cloud Platform provides about 40 different services, including IoT, forecasting algorithms, machine translation and other machine learning methods, has its own WebIDE development environment and a tool for rapid prototyping Build, supports the development of applications in Java, XSJS, C ++, Python, Ruby, Node.js, Go, PHP, .Net.
