HTC Flyer - a stylish pocket tablet for business and entertainment.

Under reviews without rules, we talk about devices that we personally test to show you their strengths and weaknesses. You have already seen today's exhibit more than once in the Droider Chart and Droider Show releases . This is the first tablet from HTC , which, as manufacturers say: " does not look like the others ." Let's see what makes it so unique:



We have slightly changed the format of our video editors, so we are waiting for your feedback. And, as for the device, the tablet turned out to be quite universal.



Due to its compact form factor with a 7-inch screen and a stylish aluminum case, it is perfect for active people who need an assistant to work with documents, mail and the Internet. In addition, HTC Flyer is equipped, though, with a single-core, but powerful enough Qualcomm Snapdragon processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz . It will be without any brakes to drive all modern games for Android , except those that are adapted for Nvidia Tegra 2.



The tablet works on Android 2.3 Gingerbread and recently there was an update to version 2.3.4, in which support for video calls via Google Talk appeared . By the way, for them, the tablet has a front camera at 1.3 MP, and for shooting video and photos there is a back camera - at 5 MP. The pictures from the camera are not very high quality, it remains the weak side of HTC, but for notes it‚Äôs not necessary anymore.

Well, now let's go to the very part of the tablet, because of which it is called unlike the others. Firstly, this is of course the new HTC Sense with a bunch of adapted applications for a seven-inch screen. We must pay tribute to the developers of the company for a convenient and beautiful software. Secondly, the HTC Scribe special pen comes bundled with the gadget, with which you can draw on the display of the flyer, all kinds of squiggles or mark text and take notes. In addition, it comes in a white leather case with a magnetic snap and pen holder.



More manufacturers promise that in the future the tablet will be updated to the Android version of Honeycomb , the timing is not reported yet. The recommended price for HTC Flyer in the Russian market is 26990 r. for version 3g with 32GB of memory on board.
