How we help startups

Airbnb began with the lease of three mattresses, and six years later, having accumulated $ 10 billion in investment, the project itself promotes the development of new startups. Do you need many resources to start your IT business, if you have a well-coordinated team and remote work skills? Fast internet and comfortable accommodation may not be all that you need, but you just can't do without it. Having handed over your accommodation in one corner of the world, you can start working with a team on any part of the globe. The difference is put in a pocket, and as a bonus, a place is selected next to large start-ups.

Garage co-working spaces and cheap offices in the basements are good. In its own way. But a young startup sometimes needs something more than just a piece of material space, with protection from wind and rain. Most apartments are more spacious than the average hotel room. In the apartments you can live and work at the same time, there is a kitchen, a washing machine, and some owners allow you to keep pets. And prices. At first, startups' incomes are minimal, many refuse to pay, trying to save on any little things. A house in the backwoods for one team can save several thousand dollars a month.

Success stories are built according to different templates, but the most popular scheme looks like this: you have housing and a desire to travel. Business travel is an extremely conservative direction of business tourism, because employees of companies traditionally follow a certain corporate policy that only works with specific hotel operators or booking systems.
