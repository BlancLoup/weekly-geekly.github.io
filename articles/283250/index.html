Sale of PHDays VI tickets closes.



A few days left before the start of the international forum on practical security Positive Hack Days. You have the last chance to participate. Tickets on sale until May 13 - you can buy them at runet-id.com/event/phdays16 .

We remind you that PHDays VI will be held on May 17 and 18, 2016 at the World Trade Center in Moscow. The forum will be attended by leading security experts and hackers from around the world, representatives of government agencies and big business, journalists and young scientists. We have prepared an extensive program - business and technical reports, master classes and round tables. Schedule on the official website of the event .

Also you will find a real battle of hackers and security men - PHDays VI ityF: Confrontation . Almost all competitions will be related to the CityFit infrastructure. Participants will hack the process control system, Internet of Things, Internet banks, GSM and network equipment. Everyone can try to arrange an accident at the hydroelectric power station, disable the smart home and take control of the car. Details - in the competitive program .

And there is also a cultural program - an art exhibition, a cyberpunk night, and even a rock concert (Lucy's First Job group will perform).
We are waiting for you at Positive Hack Days VI. It will be fun!
