Criminal case for key chain

The Security Service of Ukraine opened a criminal case on four articles of the criminal code:

  • Art. 15 - Attempted crime
  • Art. 263 - Illegal handling of weapons, ammunition or explosives
  • Art. 359 - Tacit information retrieval

... for two video cameras in the form of a key fob and a penknife ordered for dealextreme.

A system administrator with a one-year-old child in her arms can go to jail for a good ten, like a full-time criminal.
