Learning 3.0

Everyone who has ever studied at a university, institute and other institutions of higher education is familiar with the standard teaching model: seminar - lecture. Some people like it, some don't, but there's nothing you can do because this is the foundation of education. Although sometimes teachers come across who distort this paradigm. Personally, I got such a copy, and this is the head. our department of information security. He immediately offered us to conduct lectures in the form of a conversation - where he told us, and we did not write anything, but only listened to him and if there was a need, asked questions.

Of course, it was convenient that his personal book was attached to the subject, where everything he said was described in detail.

At home, we all (ideally of course) read in detail, and already at the seminar they told him how they understood everything he was talking about. So I wanted to tell you that such a model of education is much better (and everyone knows that) than the standard one, under which he would mumble a dictation from his book, which can be read anyway.
