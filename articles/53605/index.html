Win a Nintendo Wii with Opera!



The portal of the opera community My Opera Community is not only a convenient place to communicate, it also allows you to get quite tangible gifts and prizes from the company Opera Software. All that is required is to take part in numerous competitions organized with enviable regularity. It should be noted that Russian users of Opera browsers are quite actively involved in such events and often receive well-deserved awards. Therefore, I think all of you will be interested to learn about the new competition organized jointly by Opera Software and the developer of computer games gPotato . The prize of the winner of this event, which will last until March 15, will be a Nintendo Wii game console. The rules for participation are as follows.

All you need to do is to become a member of the IMO "Tales Runner" and score the maximum number of points for completing various levels with various obstacles. The procedure is as follows:

1. Create an account and download the game from the official website
2. Get the individual code of the contest participant on the Opera website
3. Log in to your game account and enter the individual code
4. Find the Opera shirt and put it on (go to My room and check your box of things)
5. Participate in tasks of the game with maximum zeal until March 15, 2009 !

Do not forget to become a member of the Tales Runner group on the My Opera website to chat with other players and have fun.

The winner of this unusual competition will be announced on the site My Opera. If, within seven days from the date of the announcement, no one applies for the prize, the Nintendo Wii game console will be played by a draw.
