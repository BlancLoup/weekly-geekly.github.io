Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon Ultrabook Video Review (2014)



Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon (2014) - an updated version of the acclaimed 14-inch laptop ThinkPad X1 Carbon. The laptop received a new filling in the form of fourth-generation Intel Core processors, as well as a redesigned case, thanks to which the laptop became lighter and thinner. The amount of RAM is from 4 to 8 GB (soldered on the motherboard), and for storage of data using solid-state drives up to 512 GB. It should also be noted the presence of wireless interfaces NFC and WWAN (Micro-SIM).

Depending on the model, the ultrabook is completed with either a matte display with a resolution of 1600x900 (TN) or an IPS display with a resolution of 2560x1440 (there is also an option with a touchscreen).
