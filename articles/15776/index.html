Fell statistics FeedBurner?

Many today have reduced the number of subscribers to FeedBurner indicators, the blogosphere is filled with reports that the statistics of FeedBurner are buggy.

For many blogs, the number of subscribers decreased by 40-50%, Problogger.Net showed a decrease in the number of subscribers on one of its sites from 31.240 to 6.286.

As a result of observations, they found out that the statistics do not include subscribers who read feeds via Google FeedFetcher, hopefully that they will fix it tomorrow.
