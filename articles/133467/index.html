allw.me: your link is in question


With the development of the Internet and social networks as a phenomenon, it becomes increasingly difficult to control the flow of information, including those that we distribute ourselves. I wanted to create a service that will help solve the problem of uncontrolled access to any resource or data, while using clear and simple mechanisms by which everyone can solve other cognitive-entertaining tasks :)

The essence of this small service called allw.me is really simple - the user comes up with a question with the correct answer and adds a link that will open only to the one who answers it correctly. That's the whole mechanism. The rest - for the user's imagination and fantasy, for example, you can:
  • Close by some question a link to a profile in a social network in order to weed out an extra audience.
  • Write a cool article only for the elite who know the answer to the most important question
  • Use in contests
  • Show tits yourself only to those who really deserve it, or at least know the color of your eyes

  • Just put the password on any link (file, image)
Despite the simplicity of the service, there are plans for its development, which I will try to talk about as they are implemented.

I ask you not to take this idea too seriously, all from pure interest and curiosity, well, and then we will see what happens;)
