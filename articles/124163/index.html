Logo toaster in 5 minutes



I saw such a toaster. And he fired up to do something like this with his own hands. In order to please your loved ones every morning with different pictures on toasts. It turned out that it's pretty simple.
We will need: an aluminum can, and simple tools.

Cut the jar and burn it from the paint.



Now we have a flat sheet from which we need to cut the logo.
If you want the logo on the toast to be white, then you need to cut out the profile of the shape you need.
In the case of making a stencil, the whole toast will remain white and the drawing will be browned on the toast.
My choice was Cheburashka.

Pay attention, it is necessary to provide options for mounting your logo to the toaster grate.
