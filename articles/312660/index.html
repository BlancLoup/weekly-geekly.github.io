Free seminar "Operation of the data center: what you need to do yourself", October 27, Moscow

We invite you on October 27 to the seminar "Operation of the data center: what you need to do yourself . "

Kirill Shadsky will tell how to build work of own operation service in a data-center or simple server. From 2012 to 2015, he was in charge of the operation of the largest network of commercial data centers in Russia.

What shall we talk about:

  • Functions and tasks: I cannot outsource myself;
  • State: how many people you need and who all these people are;
  • How to evaluate the work of internal services;
  • Instructions and regulations: what needs to be formalized and how to do it so that it really works.

For all participants at the end of the seminar, an excursion around the OST data center and a delicious lunch :)



To participate in the seminar, send an application for participation and wait for our confirmation. We will answer!

When: October 27, 11.00
Where: Moscow, st. Borovaya, 7, p.10, OST data center. Location map
From Elektrozavodskaya metro there will be a transfer to the seminar and back to the metro.
