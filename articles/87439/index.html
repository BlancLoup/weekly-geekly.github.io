Habraklient for mobile devices


Preamble


Break the search, the Internet and all the blog page "ideas for startups," I did not see a single post associated with the creation of the client Habr for mobile devices.

As a result, I decided to describe my vision of the client, or rather its functionality.

I present a client with a fairly simple 3-click interface with high usability, most likely finger-oriented. It is very useful for fans to read the habr in the morning over a cup of coffee, at work, on the train and on the bench near the free wi-fi point.

Thank you dive for the advice on the implementation of the mobile client. As a matter of fact, the idea smoothly flows into the development of the client for Android / WM / iPhone OS / Symbian OS

If, nevertheless, there was a very similar idea, and even more so the implemented ones, please notify for immediate closure of the topic.

Habraklient's overall functionality


I think that it is not worth overloading the client, I see the functionality as follows:
  • Authorization (for users with ak on habr)
  • Polycolumn. Not so long ago, I came across a client for Twitter using the same AIR shell, namely TweetDeck .
    I really liked their multicolumn structure.

  • The ability to sort thematic blogs and company blogs. Since the structure seems to me to be multi-column, then, as a consequence, you can create a new column and add either a separate thematic blog or select a number of blogs to display in it. The posts themselves will be displayed as headers, with the ability to expand to the first n-characters. Clicking on the title takes you to the post itself. It is also possible to create columns:
    1. Private messages
    2. Favorites
    3. User Profile
    4. My / His (with the display of your or others' blogs and / or comments)
    5. Favorites
    6. Specific Themed Blog
    7. Chosen by you blogs (tick your favorite blogs checkboxes)

  • Plus posts (of course for authorized users only)
  • Editing your profile
  • Add friend
  • Commenting


More about the tape posts



Tape posts includes the following functionality:
  • By clicking on the post (and as it was written above, the posts are displayed in the ribbon headings) go to the post page in the browser
  • Expand and collapse the post. When clicking on a post, it unfolds on the first n characters (up to a maximum of 400, I think)
  • Raising and lowering the ranking of the post (as written above under a big question)
  • Remove post from tape
  • Add post to favorites
  • Tweet
  • Add to bookmarks (again, it can be, although you can specify the service of bookmarks in the same settings and authorize on it, including)
  • Remove post from tape
