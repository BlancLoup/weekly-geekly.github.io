Traincert.ru - my new project

I bring to your attention my new project for distance learning - www.traincert.ru . The main ideas are as follows:
- Information and educational articles will be created by independent authors
- The main focus is on multimedia presentations as the most effective way to deliver training material
- The main feature - virtual laboratory work, allowing visitors to try the technology about which the author writes.

There is a demo video on how to create virtual labs and what happens next.
While the project is at the stage of alpha testing, it is planned to add new services, the design and layout will be redone. There are few server resources at the moment, so several people can simultaneously perform laboratory work.

I would like to hear your comments on this. Thanks for attention.
