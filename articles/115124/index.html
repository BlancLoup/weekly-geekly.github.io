Adobe released Flash to HTML5 Converter



Adobe has released a new experimental tool, code-named Wallaby for converting Flash to HTML5.

Wallaby is an AIR application with a very simple interface that allows you to easily convert fla files to HTML5 code. Converted content can be viewed in browsers on the WebKit engine, including Google Chrome and Safari.

The technology was presented at the Adobe MAX conference last fall. Users can now freely access Wallaby on Adobe Labs.
