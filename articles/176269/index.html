The Pirate Bay [again] want to block



Several online magazines reported that the Swedish government decided to block the domain of the most popular file sharing service in the world: thepiratebay.se.

In 2011, the US government promised to apply similar sanctions to the thepiratebay.org domain.

10 days ago, the official bit-torrent blog jokingly published a post that they will move back to America.

Now the administration of The Pirate Bay plans to move from thepiratebay.se to thepiratebay.gl.

Update [ 2013-04-25 ] site moved to thepiratebay.is.

Update [ 2013-04-30 ] site moved to thepiratebay.sx.
