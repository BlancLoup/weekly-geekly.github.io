Pacsafe backpacks and bags - up to 50% off




Pacsafe was founded in the early 90s by two travelers, obsessed with the desire to make really safe travel bags and backpacks. Such not to cut, not open imperceptibly, not to pull out from under the nose of a scattered tourist, while he orders a cup of coffee in an outdoor cafe.


Over the decades of work, the Pacsafe team has developed various ways to protect baggage. They come down to four categories. The first is anti-opening: the fabric is reinforced with a stiff steel mesh, and the straps are reinforced with thin steel slings. The second is the zippers. These locks and carbines hold the "dogs" in place. So the thief will not be able to unbutton his pocket or branch. Third: locks for lines. Snap off such a lock, wrap a sling around a table leg or fence, snap it. Now the bag will not be stolen unnoticed by you. The fourth category, pockets with protection against RFID attacks, has appeared recently. It is clear that the specialist will roll his eyes when he heard about the rarely skimming, but Pacsafe decided to take care of it.

Wacaco Nanopresso portable coffee machine - 20% discount




Wacaco Nanopresso is a manual espresso machine. The device is mechanical and does not require electricity. To make a cup of espresso, you need only boiling water and ground coffee beans.


The compact espresso machine looks like a capsule and is comparable in size to a small thermos. Inside the capsule is a pump, which is driven by a tight button-piston. The pump creates pressure up to 18 bar.



Preparing espresso this way: you take Nanopresso apart, find a filter cup, pour coffee into it. Then in the water compartment you need to pour hot water. Connect the parts of the machine, turn the neck down over the cup, and begin to pressurize the piston. It will take about 10-15 clicks per serving, depending on the degree of grinding. At the exit coffee with a dense skin turns out.
