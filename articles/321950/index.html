Superjob Data Science Meetup

Superjob invites you to Data Science Meetup. We meet on March 2 in our office on Malaya Dmitrovka.



Topics and speakers:

  • "The use of fuzzy duplicate search algorithms in the search for vacancies"

Dmitry Cojocari, senior developer of Superjob , will talk about the experience of creating an effective algorithm for finding fuzzy duplicates among a large number of semi-structured text records. The report discusses the use of functions from the family locality-sensitive hashing with additional optimizations for identifying similar vacancies and their subsequent clustering.

  • "Automation of selection: assessment of candidates using machine learning methods"

Maxim Savchenko, Head of Model Development at the Sberbank Technologies Research and Development Center of Competence , will talk about the development of HRM models using advanced regression analysis using the example of Sberbank, the problems of integrating analytics into the business environment and prospects for using Social Data Science.

  • "The practice of machine learning: issues and problems when working on an ML-project"

Evgeny Grigorenko, an expert on strategic technologies at Microsoft , will share his accumulated experience in building ML solutions, point out frequently missed questions, and tell about the successful development from scratch and the implementation of a research project in the field of practical medicine.

The program of the meeting is being specified. Stay tuned!

Timing:

18.30-19.00 - gathering participants, drinks and snacks
19.00-20.30 - reports, questions, discussion
20.30-21.00 - free communication

Admission is free, the number of places is limited. Please register here .

On the day of the event, please do not forget to bring your passport or driver's license. This is necessary for access to our business center.
