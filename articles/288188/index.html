How to track copying content?

For most online media, the problem of content theft is acute. I want to draw attention to the service Tynt . It will not save you from rewriting and brazen theft, but it will help you understand what content on your pages is considered the most interesting, what ordinary users are copying and where. In addition, you will attract additional traffic to your site, get more links. How it works?
  1. Go to the site Tynt , register and get html-code.
  2. Paste the code on the pages of your site, somewhere before the closing tag body. This code will load a java script about 12 Kb in size.
  3. After installation, users of your site with the included java-script will automatically add a link to the source when copying text, pictures and with a simple selection. See the demo video here .
  4. After a day you can watch the statistics in your account on Tynt.


I understand that this is not a panacea, but even large media use this thing, for example, the British Daily Mail . Maybe you need it.
