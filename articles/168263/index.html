Idea:
I am trapped inside the crystal prison. I don't know how or why I got here, all I know is that I need your help. I'm very scared. It is like falling between two worlds. I can sometimes hear your voice, feel you close - it helps me to hold on, and at least for a moment to forget from the harsh reality. I know that with the help of your magic I can get out of here. At your disposal:

  • 25 levels (7 of them are available in trial mode);
  • manual graphics;
  • pleasant music;
  • convenient management.

A little bit about how the game was created


Developer - Maiax studio from Argentina. We act as a publisher.

The backbone of the team consisted of three people: the first was engaged in the development and creation of musical accompaniment, the second acted as a designer and helped with the development, the third member of the company also engaged in design and related work. A few more people worked on creating gameplay.

The game was developed using XNA technology using the Farseer physics engine. The development of the game took about a year.
