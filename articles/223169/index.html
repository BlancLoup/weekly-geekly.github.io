New idea. Mad mix of spider and 2048



I bring to your attention a new solitaire.
For programmers and mathematicians.
I've been playing for the third week already, opening up more and more new combinations.

For those who are too lazy to read - there is a link to a 30-second video game.


Rules of the game


Initially on the board 30 bricks.



Bricks can be shifted from place to place, according to two conditions.
  1. Any brick can be put on the ground;
  2. Lightweight bricks can only be put on heavy ones;
  3. Two identical bricks laid on each other turn into one brick with double weight.


Everything. No more rules.

The task is to add solitaire, which means leaving one, the largest, heaviest brick weighing 2048 kg on the board.



In the picture solitaire is complicated in 26 moves. And it was possible to collect rationally.

For those who are too lazy to read - a 30-second video game:



Extra buns


If solitaire is not going - it does not matter. Each alignment has its own unique number from 1 to 1 000 000. By choosing the alignment by number - you can always play it again. If you want - in a minute, if you want - in a year. The uniqueness of layouts generates correspondence competition between players. A more savvy person will collect solitaire better. For fewer moves. Of course, the results are collected on the server and every day a draw of the Yellow Brick Cup is held.



Professionals who find the game too easy, will go to play on the option with two colors of bricks. Here, layouts are just freaky! Try to collect solitaire number 49 for 31 moves! Who can, that is waiting for a surprise.
