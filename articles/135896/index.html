The project "And from our window" is dedicated to renting apartments in Moscow. Every day, the moderators manually select the three best apartments, which are now rented in the center for sane money, as well as looking for apartments on individual requests. The service is useful and promising, with well-thought-out mechanisms and an established infrastructure.



The project is looking for a new owner who wants to manage his own business, develop, improve and promote it.

about the project
