First UMPC on Core 2 Duo

UMPC Haier X6 will not make designers turn their heads, but they will certainly attract a lot of attention with their internal capabilities. Despite the rather mediocre appearance, the device is equipped with a full QWERTY keyboard, made on the Intel 945GU chipset, a 6.5-inch display with a resolution of 800 x 480, 1 GB of RAM, 30 GB of HDD (5400), a front camera for video conferencing (0.3 megapixels), Wi-Fi 802.11b / g, Bluetooth 2.0 and GPS receiver. Windows Vista operating system. But the main advantage of the computer was not this, but the choice of processor. Haier uses 1.83GHz Core 2 Duo L2500 or 1.66GHz L2400 processors in this model. With dimensions of 19 x 12 x 2.8 cm and weight of 720 grams, the device operates on batteries 4-5 hours (3900 mAh). Unfortunately at the moment the release date of the device is not known, however, the price promises to be around 580 pounds sterling (1165 US dollars).
