Opera Mini Browser Nominated for Two GSMA Awards

Winners will be announced at Mobile World Congress in Barcelona

January 20, 2010 - Oslo, Norway

The Opera Mini browser is nominated for two GSMA Global Mobile Awards. GSMA represents the interests of the global telecommunications industry and holds the Mobile World Congress (MWC) in Barcelona every year in February.

The Opera Mini browser is a contender for awards in two categories: Best Mobile Internet Service (Best Mobile Internet Service) and Mobile Technology Breakthrough (Best Mobile Technology Breakthrough). The winners of the Global Mobile Awards will be announced at the MWC event on Tuesday, February 16 at 3:45 pm local time (Hall 5, 3rd level, 1st audience). The famous British writer, actor and technophile Stephen Fry will take part in the solemn ceremony.
