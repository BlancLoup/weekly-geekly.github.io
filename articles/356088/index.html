Relevant instructions on making a resource in the register of prohibited sites sent to Roskomnadzor.

The basis of claims to Linkedin was the failure to comply with two requirements of the law ‚ÄúOn Personal Data‚Äù: failure to comply with the requirements for localization of personal data of Russian citizens registered on the network, and violation of the rules for the order, storage and processing of third party data.
