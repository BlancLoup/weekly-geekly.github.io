Popfly - now and games without a line of code


A new section dedicated to game creation has just opened on Popfly.com.

Let me remind you that Popfly is a Microsoft project written in Silverlight and focused on easy creation of various mashups, that is, applications that can make different services on the Internet.

So, now Popfly allows not only services to be friends, but also casual computer game players :) And even those who have never dealt with such things before can create such games.

Initially, there is a set of more than 15 game templates, hundreds of pictures, animations, sounds, and other elements of your future games. In visual mode, you can create and modify various elements. Well, you can play with it all of course right there.

If you wish, you can customize the game to your needs using JavaScript. And you can share the finished game with your friends, including inserting it somewhere to your site.

Well, finally, a small video showing where to start.
