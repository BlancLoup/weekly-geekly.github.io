November 27th, Kiev. Joint conference ZFConf Ukraine & MageConf

On November 27th, Kyiv will host a free, international joint conference ZFConf Ukraine & MageConf .
Our goal is to bring together the best PHP and Zend Framework specialists in Ukraine, Russia and other CIS countries.

ZFConf Ukraine is the first in Ukraine conference on Zend Framework, held in partnership with ZFConf Russia and the zendframework.ru community. ZFConf Russia was the first conference on the Zend Framework and was held in March this year in St. Petersburg, Russia, bringing together more than 200 participants from Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, Lithuania and other CIS countries

The MageConf conference was first held in May 2010 in Kiev. This was the first conference of the PHP and Zend Framework series in Ukraine. More than 150 specialists visited it.

The conference will be held on November 27 in Kiev
The title sponsor of the conference is Magento
Location: KyivExpoPlaza, ul. Salyutnaya 2b, pavilion 3
Time: November 27, 2010 (from 11:00 to 19:00)
Duration: 1 full day
Format: reports in three streams and flipchart discussions (in breaks)
Reports language: Russian
Participation fee: free of charge
Threads: Zend Framework, PHP, Magento.

Audience: Software developers and Project Management specialists from Ukraine, Russia and nearby CIS countries. The expected number of participants is 500 people.
