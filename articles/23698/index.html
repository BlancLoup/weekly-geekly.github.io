Kojinsha E8



Kojinsha has delighted consumers with an E8 mini-laptop.

This new product has several advantages over Asus. For example, it is equipped with a rotary touch screen with a resolution of 1024x600 pixels.

Also, the model specifications include a Geode LX800 processor from AMD, a 40 GB HDD, 512 MB of RAM, a USB port, a slot for memory cards, built-in stereo speakers and a battery that ensures autonomous operation of the device for up to 3.5 hours.

Also, the laptop will be equipped with a 4GB SSD drive, and an additional hard drive (spoken on 40 GB), an integrated graphics card and a 7-inch display with a resolution of 800 by 480.

They promise that a laptop will work for about 3.5 hours without recharging. Batteries will also be available, allowing you to work up to 8 hours. Of course, bundled WiFi, card reader 3-in-1.
The standard RAM may not be enough (256MB), but if you wish, you can expand to 1GB.

The cost will be within 300 USD.
