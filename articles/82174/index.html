HTC Supersonic and HTC Bravo: features and first photos


The network continues to leak new details about gigahertz Android-smartphones Taiwanese company HTC .

Perhaps the most interesting smartphone is the HTC Supersonic (on the left in the picture). This phone is the closest relative of HTC HD2 and has a large screen size (approximately 3.8 "to 4.3", but more extended than HD2), 1 GHz SoC Qualcomm Snapdragon SoC (with dynamic frequency control), 512 MB of ROM, from 256 MB RAM It also has a 5 megapixel camera, a 1400 mAh battery, WiFi, GPS and, perhaps, the most interesting is the mobile Wimax 802.16e.

So far, it is only known that the device is not intended for mass sale, but will be sold to work in the Wimax network of the Sprint-Clear operator. However, as it is known, Clearwire and Yota work on the same frequency, which means that it remains a theoretical opportunity to teach the HTC Supersonic to get along peacefully with the Yota, as the craftsmen did for the Wimax miniPCI-E series of cards from Intel.



Photos by which the craftsmen created the render:



The second quite interesting Android-smartphone is the HTC Bravo, which you have long known. Bravo is nothing more than an analogue of the Google Nexus One, both in terms of external data and characteristics. The announcement of the device will take place in 3 weeks at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona (via @dryab ), and the phone will go on sale in March-April. HTC Bravo is equipped with a 1 GHz SoC Qualcomm Snapdragon, 512 MB of ROM, 512 MB of RAM, 5 megapixel camera, 3.7 "AMOLED touch screen with a resolution of 800 x 480 pixels, a 1400 mAh battery, etc. information, the cost of the device will be slightly less than the cost of HD2, which means we can count on the amount in the region of 25 000 r.
