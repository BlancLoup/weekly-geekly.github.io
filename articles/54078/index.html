Appeared the first real keygen to WinRAR, the digital signature is no longer relevant

And here I come across sensational news. It turns out that the first working keygen for WinRAR appeared. What does it mean? This means that the meaning is lost in the digital signature, which was the guarantor of the fact that the archive was created by who it was supposed to be.
