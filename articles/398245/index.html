Criterias of choice


For many, the criterion is price, which is not surprising - the prices will be discussed further in the text. The second important factor is the attitude to music and the pretentiousness of the ears. If you are disturbed even by minor distortions, you have listened to your classics live all your life or are used to studio conditions, get ready to pay a lump sum and not just for the phono-corrector. Once again I remind you about the type of head - be sure to pay attention to what type of pickup head you have. Next, you will be faced with the question of choosing a circuit. If you have lamp amplification and you feel a significant difference - your way is a lamp or a hybrid. If you need a relatively budget option and you will not feel sick from the "dead" sound - choose a number for yourself.


Perhaps you will be satisfied with the built-in phono stage, if you have not yet purchased an amplifier and player, pay attention to the combined devices.
