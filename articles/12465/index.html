Search engines podsuzhivayut their owners

Do you seriously think that search engine results are based on impartial algorithms? So it is, but only half. Issuance is really based on algorithms and formulas, but they are not at all impartial.

Analysis of the search results of the three search engines Google, Yahoo and Microsoft shows that they shamelessly condemn their own products and lower competitors (in the literal sense - they omit them as low as possible).


The table shows the position of branded sites in the issuance of three search engines. For example, for the query [mail], Yahoo! Mail, Gmail and Windows Live Mail in three search engines. If the link is missing in the first thirty results, then a dash is put in the table.

Note that with self-testing you can get search results that differ from the ones below. It depends on your personal search history, personal search site settings, etc.
