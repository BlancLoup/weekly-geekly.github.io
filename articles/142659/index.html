SkyDrive cloud storage received a big update

Today, SkyDrive cloud storage has received a large functional update . Added the long-awaited desktop client for Windows and OSX (in addition to existing clients for Windows 8, Windows Phone and iOS). Access to local files via the web.

In addition, the opportunity to purchase additional storage space for information was announced: 20 GB, 50 GB and 100 GB. Prices are quite low, for an additional 100 gigabytes they ask for 1,570 rubles a year.



Everything in more detail under the cut.

Skydrive for windows


The preview version of the new client available today for automatic synchronization of desktop and cloud makes it possible to quickly and easily synchronize the selected folder on the disk with the cloud storage:



All updates happen automatically, user changes in the cloud immediately lead to a change on your desktops and vice versa - by changing something on your computer, you will automatically receive the same change in the cloud, and through it on your other computers.

The new client is miniaturized, downloaded and installed in two clicks, automatically updated to new versions.

Accessing files on computers via the web


By installing the client on your computer, you can enable it to remotely access content via the cloud storage web part. That is, you can get access to the files of your computer (when it is turned on) from any point of access to the Internet, through your SkyDrive cloud storage account.



Acquisition of additional SkyDrive space


By default, the updated SkyDrive offers all new users 7 GB of free storage. Good news for old users! If you have previously used 25 GB storage, then follow this link and hurry to get all your 25 gigabytes for free and forever!

Increase file limit


Those who used SkyDrive know that despite the large storage, it was possible to upload only files no larger than 100 (more recently 300) megabytes. With the update, this limit is increased to 2 gigabytes . Download large files, synchronize them on the desktop.
