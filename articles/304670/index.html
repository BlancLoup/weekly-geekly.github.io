Open WG Talk # 3: New Processes and Product Management

Wargaming's Minsk office regularly hosts meetings at which company employees share experiences and discuss pressing issues of concern to developers, managers, designers, producers, artists ‚Äî in short, all those who work in IT and game development. We call these meetings, WG Talk, and hold it in a private format, only for company employees.
But there are topics and knowledge that can not be shared with a wide audience. Therefore, we organize the Open WG Talk.

Today we want to share with you videos of the speeches of the last Open WG Talk, the theme of which was product management and the introduction of new processes. Under the cut, you will find reports from Sergey Berezhnoy, director of Wargaming development, and Yaropolk Rush, head of Wargaming media services.



Enjoy watching!

"How to introduce new processes in the project and beyond its limits?"
Sergey Berezhnoy, Development Director Wargaming
As part of his speech, Sergey reviewed the Dilts logical level model as applied to business processes. He showed how to create a good check list on its basis for preparing the team for changes, as well as to introduce new processes at the level of simple human emotions.


"How to go to the grocery management without waiting for the go-ahead on top?"
Yaropolk Rush, Head of Media Services Wargaming
Yaropolk told how and why to use the product approach in project management. In particular, it was about:
  • Cast. Who do you need and why?
  • Processes. How is this done and working?
  • Examination. What you need to know and be able to advance?
  • The results. What should and should not be expected in the end?
