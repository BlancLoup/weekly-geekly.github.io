QIWI Visa Virtual - pleasant fare change

Quite recently, QIWI Wallet and Virtual QIWI Visa announced a long-awaited innovation - the card has become rechargeable .

Another good news of the project: from July 1, the commission for the purchase of a card will drop to 3.63% (now 5.26%).

This reduction is planned to be organized in the form of a share - during July 2010 . Although, there is nothing more permanent than something temporary .

The reduction in commission by almost two percentage points forced us to introduce a minimum level of commission - in the amount of 25 rubles.
At present, the average amount of a card purchase is 1,150 rubles . This means that for the overwhelming majority of our users, the introduction of new conditions is an absolute plus! But we have to admit that buying cards with a face value of 300 rubles will become less profitable ...

New rules will apply not only to the purchase of cards , but also to the replenishment of existing ones.

Your recommendations to increase the life of cards at least twice - we hear . Now there are active discussions on this issue.

We will report additional results :)

Information : the project started in August 2009 and in just 9 months the turnover amounted to more than 400 million rubles. The turnover in June 2010 alone is more than 70 million rubles. The average amount of top-up for QIWI VISA Virtual cards continues to increase: compared with August 2009, the figure has grown by more than 50%.
