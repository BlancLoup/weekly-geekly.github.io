AMOcrm and Hive: high-tech business model for online stores

Regional sales are a gold mine for small businesses. But in order to successfully cope with the growing range of products and the flow of calls from different cities, without hiring an entire sales department for this, you need to automate something. And we have an idea how to do it.



Before we talk about the technology that we love to do the most, let's dive a little into the problematic. Due to the rapid development of free classifieds sites in Russia, entrepreneurs have an interesting opportunity: to distribute sales throughout the country, not doing either an online store promotion or creating a special sales department. In general, literally 1-2 people can conduct business, who simply take orders and send them by mail, or by transport companies.

Often, a small business is born from the purchase of some interesting product, ad placement and a gradual increase in the flow of sales. However, if the business is progressing, there are more and more goods and there is a desire to launch sales in new cities, the entrepreneur has to use additional regional sites and post new ads on them. But this is where the problem arises: many sites - the same Avito - do not allow duplicate ads in one account, so each new recurring ad needs its own unique phone number, and customers from Tambov do not have to call Moscow mobile phones (because they can to guess about it by the code 925 or 985 and not to call the ‚Äúintercity‚Äù at all), it is better that the number be regional.

As a result, the business faces a dilemma: get a dozen numbers and redirect to your mobile (expensive and confusing), hire a sales department (expensive), carry a few phones with 3 sim cards each (uncomfortable) or still think of something something more original. In addition, the problem of recording orders becomes a real hell - in the absence of automation equipment, you need to constantly carry around a notebook in which to record that Galina from Samara needs 100 pairs of black socks, and Elena from Syktyvkar - 200 orange stockings.

CRM and Hive integration

Today we will talk about how to solve this issue easily and easily with the help of ready-made cloud solutions in the form of amoCRM CRM service and Hive virtual PBX with Multi IP-SIM technology. Services are already integrated with each other, so that a businessman does not need to invent or order something additionally.
