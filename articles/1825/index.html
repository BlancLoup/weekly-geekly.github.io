BitTorrent's popularity is growing by leaps and bounds

After the formal closure of eDonkey, users of file-sharing networks began to gradually move to other P2P networks. This is most clearly seen in the statistics of BitTorrent sites in recent days .

Four sites connected to the BitTorrent P2P network were included in the list of the 500 most popular Internet sites according to Alexa. In the first thousand ranking there are already six such sites.

205th place. Mininova ;
218. Torrentspy ;
387. Piratebay ;
439. Isohunt ;
573. Torrentz ;
593. Demonoid .

New BitTorrent sites open every day, and their attendance often increases by several orders of magnitude in just a couple of months.
