3D printer creating color paper models



Iris is a new 3D printer model from Mcor Technologies .

Printing is done by gluing together pieces of paper. A pack of paper is laid in the printer, the cutter does its job, the sheets are glued together. In addition, each of these pieces is painted so that the result is a voluminous paper photo-realistic object.
