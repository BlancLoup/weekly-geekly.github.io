Does Response Timing Matter in Online Research?
Jeff Sauro is trying to understand how much the response rate of respondents in surveys affects the quality of the assessment. This will allow the use of data in the course of the study, without waiting for it to end. Previously conducted research either does not show influence, or suggests that those who respond faster are more supportive. The findings from his experiment show that there is no significant influence.
