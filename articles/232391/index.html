Real-time cyber attacks



You can watch cyber war with your own eyes using the Norse Live Attack Map . This map displays data from Norse equipment located in more than 40 countries. Attacks are conducted on specially formed small lure infrastructures with supposedly valuable data. Information is updated every second.

The real attacks on this map are not visible, but with its help you can monitor the most common types and sources of attacks. At the moment, the attack on port 694 is leading. If you look for a while, you can see that most of the threats come from China and Canada, and the main target is the United States.

An interesting point is also the fact that in addition to the common ports ssh, telnet, etc., two unknowns stand out: 53003 and 21320. Perhaps with the help of this tool it will be possible to track the use of new exploits.
