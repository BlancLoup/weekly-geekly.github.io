UnetaPlus conference opens doors for developers



The UnetaPlus conference, where I will give a talk, will be held on October 1 in Kharkov. For their own purposes, the organizers rented the Radmir Expohall exhibition center, a large and roomy venue.

It is not difficult to guess that the conference is entirely devoted to the IT sphere. A rich list of speakers will share experiences in areas such as MANGO, SILVERLIGHT, ODATA, SQL Azure, the Entity Framework, and more.

A total of about 10 reports are planned, each of which highlights a specific topic. Any developer will find for themselves something important, interesting and relevant in the presented topics. Another good news is the ‚Äúexpert zone‚Äù - the speakers very often rush to speak and run away, and asking them questions in an informal setting does not work. On UnetaPlus this will not happen, because they will be available all the time for communication on a special platform. In addition to the speakers, experts (mainly MVP) are invited to it, with whom it will be possible to meet and talk on topics of interest to you.

The company Digital Cloud Technologies, which is the organizer of the event, promises that its level will be European. This is a good room, an excellent composition of speakers, invited experts, excellent sound and video, fresh drinks and food during the event. After the presentations, there will be a reception for the participants, where business contacts and strong partnerships are often established.

Many experts who have confirmed their participation in the conference rarely appear in public, and even less often attend such events in Ukraine. Among the speakers:

- Vagif Abilov, who will talk about the features of working with the OData protocol,

- Alex Golesh, who prepared a report on the possibilities of Windows Phone 7 and Mango in developing hybrid applications for smartphones;

- Dmitry Kostylev, ready to share secrets to speed up the work of SQL SERVER;

- Diego Vega with a report on the Entity Framework;

And many others. A complete list of speakers and their presentations can be found on the conference site Uneta Plus .

My presentation will be devoted to innovations in the ASP.NET MVC framework.

One of the advantages of the conference is its profile and the ability to communicate in the circle of real experts in the field of software development.

I am waiting for you at my speech, which will be held at the conference UnetaPlus on October 1 (beginning at 9.00).
