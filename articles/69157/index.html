SQA Days 2009 Piter: a complete selection of materials from the 5th Testers Conference

On the occasion of the day of the tester , as well as on the eve of the sixth conference of specialists in testing and quality assurance, as well as the Test Labs conference in Kiev , I am publishing a full list of the available materials on the speeches of the previous fifth conference, which took place on April 23-24, 2009 in St. Petersburg .

Published all slides of presentations (in PPT format), audio recordings of round tables, for about half of the speeches - slidecasts (presentations with superimposed sound). Some performances, unfortunately, were not recorded, or the quality of the recording turned out to be too low, so there are not slide shows for everyone.
