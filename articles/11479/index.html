Released RealPlayer recording video from the web

Finally, RealNetworks has released the promised RealPlayer 11 with the ability to download and record video from the Web.

A user who has RealPlayer 11 installed can easily download videos from surfing the Web. To do this, you just need to click on the Download this Video button, which will appear directly in the browser next to the web player. The new version of the player records online video of all major formats, including Flash, Windows Media and QuickTime. By the way, if the video is protected with DRM, the program blocks the recording.
RealNetworks plans to add to the program the ability to download video to portable devices, such as iPod.

RealPlayer 11 is available in both free and paid versions. In the first case, you have the ability to burn the downloaded video to CD in VCD format. In the second case, paying $ 29.99, a DVD recording is available.

You can download the program from the official website .
