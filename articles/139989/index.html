The court recognized that the Russian Union of Rights Holders (RSP) was registered illegally

The Akushinsky District Court of the Republic of Dagestan on March 2 declared illegal the registration of the RSP organization in the Ministry of Justice (a scan of the 40-page solution was posted on the ROSP website ).

The lawsuit demanding recognition of the illegality of the RSP was filed by the singer Kurbanmagomedov, in court his interests were represented by the lawyer of the Russian Society for Related Rights (ROSP) Azamat Shapiyev. Kurbanmagomedov pointed out that, in accordance with the Civil Code, only authors, performers and producers of phonograms, etc., have the right to receive remuneration for the use of copyrights, and there are practically no rightholders among the founders of the RSP.
