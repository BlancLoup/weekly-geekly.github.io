How an online store uses video to increase sales

The use of video content in online stores is gaining popularity every year, and this is not surprising. Buyers can not evaluate all the quality of the product based on photos only, so video comes to the rescue. In addition, using the video, you can quickly and easily tell about the store and its features.

In the Convead service , we analyze quite a lot of online stores and notice that the conversion of sites that use videos is higher than in those with no video. Of course, many factors affect conversion, but research shows that product rolls help customers decide on a product and make a purchase decision.

So, according to a ReelSeo study, 73% of buyers are more likely to make a purchase after watching a video. In addition, 96% of customers say that the video helped them make the final purchase decision.

But the benefits that video can bring are not only sales. Here are some other factors that are affected by the videos:

  • increase the time spent on the site;
  • increase conversion;
  • increase in average check;
  • Improving site seo optimization.

Video is a great way to engage buyers in the buying process. Now let's see what types of videos exist and what tasks they perform.

So, here are some video formats that online stores can use:

  • product demonstration;
  • product story;
  • corporate videos;
  • product presentation;
  • reviews video;
  • promotional videos;
  • video from the owner or manager;
  • video instructions.

So, if you decide that you want to use the video on your website, here are some more tips:

  • Use videos about products from manufacturers. If they are in a foreign language, then you can subtitle or translate videos, but this will require translation and editing work;
  • Offer customers to make a video review of the product. Now every person has a smartphone, and lovers of recording videos will be happy to leave feedback about the product and get into your online store.
  • If you are not sure that the videos will increase the conversion in your online store, then make a couple of videos and evaluate how this will affect the sales of these products. Also, you can understand what time and money resources requires the creation of one video.
  • In order to increase the number of views of your videos, place them on sites such as youtube or vimeo.

Surely one of the video usage options described above is suitable for your online store, so try and evaluate the effectiveness.
