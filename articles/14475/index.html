Yet Another WebMoney Keeper

=================
= UPDATED 07.08.2008 =
=================
https://www.tinywm.com

A lot of new, all rewritten :-)

- You can pay for more services (all that is on telepay.wmtransfer.com )
- Purchase of digital goods (!!!). Products from stores based on www.digiseller.ru (plati.ru, publicant.ru) can be bought in one click (not all products, but only those sellers who are on the trusted list)
- well, the design has changed, so modestly, without frills
- https

It all started like this: I bought a Sony Ericsson P900 smartphone. With all the abundance of its capabilities, I lacked very little: I could not manage my WebMoney wallet from it .
Some time later, the Java client of the www.telepat.ru service (aka GSM Keeper) and the ability to use enum-rating for light appeared, but:
- java client had to reinstall with the release of the new version
- light website was hard for a smartphone
- lacked the ability to normally pay for the service for webmoney

A little later, GSM Keeper has overgrown capabilities:
- it became possible to pay for services
- recharge ATM card from cards.webmoney.ru

When I changed my smartphone to ASUS P535, it turned out that I have ‚Äújava not of the version‚Äù . For some time, GSM Keeper did not want to work on the same java-machine that came with ASUS.

Then I took the WebMoney XML interfaces and made a small website for me personally, where I could look at the balance of my wallets and pay bills.

Then, I came across an interesting feature of WebMoney : managing wallets by proxy , and realized that a service could be made that would allow everyone who would trust their service to their wallet to perform basic operations with their WM-purse.

To simplify access to the site, you can log in by login and password (you first made an entry through enum - simple, more secure - but still more gestures and you need to install the client application).

Here, of course, you can be indignant: the login and password for finance is not good!
Yes it is. But when you trust a wallet, you can set a daily limit , and if a suspicious transaction is detected, you can safely password or revoke trust and write a complaint in arbitrage.webmoney.ru .

But login and password - both will be easy and convenient :-)

Then I screwed up the payment for services and checking the balance of ATM cards ( thanks to WebMoney experts for
additional information on technical issues ) - it has become quite convenient for personal use.

The result is here: http://www.tinywm.com
Those who want to try - you can register .
