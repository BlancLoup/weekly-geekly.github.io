What is tourist tax?


This is when the country earns from tourists directly. Arriving in Greece, or Italy, or the Emirates, you may not know that you pay tourist tax. But you pay it on top of the cost of a hotel room: for each top - from 1 to 6 euros per day. The tour operator does not include this payment in the package, and the tax is paid on the spot upon arrival. Here are the countries:

  • Italy (price depends on the city and stars: from 1 to 6 euros per day per tourist);
