.NET is more valuable than .COM?

Today, Sedo, the owner of the largest domain auction, has published a statistical report for the second quarter of this year.

In general, the state of the domain name market has not undergone major changes - the domain industry is developing, domains are in great demand, sales have increased by 11% over the past year and continue to grow.

But one unusual phenomenon was noted: the average cost of domains in the .NET zone exceeded the cost of domains in the .COM zone by $ 300. The cost of domains in the .NET zone increased by 50% compared with the previous quarter, despite the fact that it was not made particularly large transactions: the most expensive domain for this period was hot.net (56000 euros). The average cost of .COM domains, on the contrary, has slightly decreased, although this zone remains the unchanged leader in money turnover and sales (48% of domain transactions fall within the .COM zone, 15% by .DE, 6% by .NET and 5% - on .co.uk).

For the first time, domains in a zone other than .COM have acquired greater value than names in the zone itself.COM. This event gives cause for optimism to all who want to invest in the development of alternative domain zones, as if creating their own site in these zones or a domain business.

But it should be noted that in recent times many other "trading platforms" for the purchase and sale of domains have appeared, and Sedo gradually gives way to its competitors. Therefore, a statistical calculation that includes all domain transactions may produce a different result.
