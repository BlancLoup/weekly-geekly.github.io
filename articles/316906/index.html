Observations that indicate an enterprise’s determination to change

The bell rings.

- Hello, is this Sergey? My name is <Ηράκλειο> (do not delve into the name, the word that exists, but this is the fruit of a second fantasy), I am the production director at <Κρήτη>. I have a number of problems with production planning. Could we meet with you?

The meeting was attended by Ηράκλειο, the CEO and, by chance, a business owner. I was provided with a list of technical requirements for production planning, the presence of which has a positive effect on the subject matter of the conversation. During the conversation, we met, shared commercial and organizational information, outlined the first steps.

I note that I do not recognize the “rapid survey”, since in the short period that this stage involves, the desired or even intermediate result will not be achieved. The period of "rapid survey" will help you and your partner to evaluate each other, the method of work, the seriousness of intentions. Therefore, I try not to carry out this stage (sometimes I do it for free), but to immediately do a full-fledged survey (depending on the current load, attitude, and interest of the owners). Yes, sometimes there are costs, but I get saturated with the pleasure of learning a new enterprise - I see how they produce toys, special-purpose equipment, building materials and much more.

The next day, Ηράκλειο showed me the enterprise from the inside, technological processes, production sites.

My primary task is to assess the possibility of the project, which includes two components:

1. physical ability
2. administrative readiness.

Physical opportunity


1. With the first item - everything is simple. You should receive the technical requirements of the project (TT) - yourself (having discussed with the customer) or already formulated from the customer. I wrote about the TT document itself here , but I’ll emphasize that you should have a table of this type:




Of course, you and your team must be specialists in order to assess the feasibility of the technical requirements. You must understand each item and see the guideline for its decision. In the process of modeling, when you go into all the details, you will have clarifications. However, they will be in the process of the whole life of the project.

Administrative readiness


2. In administrative readiness, I include everything that is beyond the boundaries of engineering - the interest of the management, the financial capabilities of the customer, the psychological climate of the team, the competence of the company's specialists. All these indicators will be considered in the course of the article.

The interest of leadership.

There are many enterprises where the appointed general director receives a salary according to the formula [the size of the salary plus a percentage of the difference between the income and expenses of the enterprise ]. If the desire to get a higher salary exceeds the essence of the Creator, then you will not start anything investment there. He has a contract for 2 years, and during this time it is more important to pay off the loan for a car recently taken to "fit" the position.

On the very first day I am compiling an “Empty Memorandum”: I request the organizational structure and according to it I draw up a schedule of interviewing key employees. I send this “document” with the request of the signature of the general director and fix the date and time of sending .
I define key persons by comparing technical requirements with posts by organizational structure, plus key users from the category of business owners and co-owners. Business owners can be found in the extracts from the register of unregistered tax services , except when information on shareholders is kept in the registrar organization. For a detailed list of affiliates, I recommend the Spark-Interfax database . Do not count for advertising, but the base gives answers to many questions regarding reliability, financial solvency, holding structure.

  • The answer came with the signature of the CEO, but with a long wait [fast / long is a subjective indicator of personal sensation]. Feature: good !

"Organizational Chess"




In the scheme we will consider 2 important parameters: the interest of a key employee and the level of communications. The latter determines the impact on the employee through the thickness of the line.

The first combination. DG is not interested, DP is interested, CD is not interested, WB is neutral.





Do not forget to answer the following questions:

  • Why such a position of this employee?
  • Does this make it difficult to achieve a local and global result?
  • What are the risks and what are the possibilities to minimize them? (add to your personal risk card)

In this case, Ηράκλειο - DP, which is interested in the implementation of the system, as it requires good production results. DG - not interested in the costs that affect his salary. After the launch, an economic effect is likely to occur, but he is not sure about saving the position by that time. CD is not interested, because the introduction of the system will add him and his department additional work, as in the volume-calendar and operational planning he will need to regularly enter additional calculations and forecasts, to enter information into the system.

Summary. This diagram shows that the enterprise is not ready for change. Only partial cases of small influence are possible: (1) DP is a brother / relative of the State Duma; (2) the presence of contact with the WB, where offer him to exclude from the calculation of the salary of the DG investment costs.
