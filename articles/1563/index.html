Wikipedia has added RSS feeds to each of the articles.

In the folk encyclopedia appeared feeds with a history of edits of each article. Now you can subscribe to topics that interest you most and track content changes, for example, about selected people, companies or brands.

Information channels appeared both in the English version (1.25 million articles) and in Russian (94 thousand articles), as well as in all other versions of Wikipedia.

To see the link to the RSS or Atom feed, you need to go to the History section of the selected article (link at the top of the page). Links to RSS and Atom appear in the left column "Tools". For example, here are the RSS feeds of articles about Yandex , Putin and Ksenia Sobchak .
