Sony Ericsson Xperia Trio Receives Ice Cream Sandwich



Sony Ericsson has a clear position in relation to the community of Android developers: one love . The Ice Cream Sandwich port is available for upgrade to the Sony Ericsson trio, which includes the following Xperia line devices: Arc S, Neo V and Ray. But keep your horses, because for the update you will get only the alpha version, suitable only for "advanced developers". In this version, most things (Google Apps, WiFi, Bluetooth, Google Voice, FM radio and ANT +) simply do not work. In other words, you should dive so deeply only if you want to look at a possible update from Google. Remember that you perform all updating operations at your own peril and risk.
