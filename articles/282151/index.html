DevConf-2016: waiting for applications for reports

Do you drink your co-working smoothie? Buried in the code in the office? Do not oversleep the main event of this summer! Developers, front-endiers, devops, we invite you to take part in the annual international conference on web development, which will be held on June 17, 2016 in Moscow.




This year we have a new conference venue - Skolkovo. Feel the innovation and gain knowledge on advanced technologies. The theme of the conference 2016 is an effective web development. Our speakers are at the forefront of progress. DevConf - open all topics!

We are waiting for applications for reports in the following areas:
- Organization of program code. Architecture, standards, performance, refactoring and library development.
- DevOps. Continuous integration, monitoring, organization of collaboration.
- Architecture. SOA, RPC, ORM, concurrency and concurrency.
- Theoretical basis. OOP, MVC, MVP, functional programming and algorithms, programming paradigms.

The report is an original work, directly related to the theme of the conference, presented in the form of a speech lasting 20-30 minutes, followed by a slide show.

Flip - a short (5-10 minutes) presentation on a topic related to the subject of the conference or close to it, using the board or flip chart and markers, or orally, without using special technical means.



Form and deadlines for submission and consideration of applications

May 31 - the deadline for submitting applications for participation in the form of a master class and a report;
June 10 - deadline for consideration of applications and the formation of the conference program;
June 15 is the deadline for the presentation of slide-ready presenters.

Those who wish to make a report at the conference should submit to the program committee the following abstracts of their report: 1-2 pages of printed text.

The program committee will consider the submitted abstracts, may request additional information from the authors, after which, by June 10, form a conference program on a competitive basis and publish a list of selected reports with a brief annotation.

Speakers are required by June 15 to submit to the program committee ready-to-show slides and / or speech at the rate of no more than 40 minutes for the entire report, including answers to questions from the audience. The program committee may require certain changes, both in form and in substance. The rapporteur has the right to make changes to his report after the deadline, but these changes must be agreed with the program committee.

If the ready-made slides and / or the text of the speech are not submitted by the deadline, the report may be withdrawn from participation and replaced by another report from among those that have not passed the initial selection.

All materials must be sent to the program committee address: http://devconf.ru/ru/offers

Applications for participation in the form of a flip will be accepted without restrictions, including directly on the day of the conference. Flips will be selected directly by conference participants, for which some form of voting will be organized.
