Server

Total

The main problems for general virtualization and cloudization (crooked word - just leave it here) are:
  • lack of broadband and stable Internet access, except for big cities
  • computing and resource requirements for client equipment are small enough, which makes senseless transfer to virtualization
  • Some "retardation" of developers of specialized software regarding new technologies, most likely associated with testing and ensuring maximum reliability
  • The almost complete lack of "computer literacy" of employees of other services
