VCStart startups: cloud CRM QuickOrder for the restaurant business

The blog of the collective investment platform VCStart appeared on Habr√© two weeks ago, simultaneously with the pre-launch of our project. It is time to show the goods face. From this point on, the VCStart blog will regularly cover projects counting on crowdfunding investing, the ideas of which seemed to us promising and interesting. And one of them is the mobile service QuickOrder .

The project is a cloud-based CRM utility that optimizes the relationship between the restaurant business and its client with the help of digital technologies, which ultimately significantly increases the level of service and has a positive effect on sales.

Forrester Research assesses the mobile CRM market as fast-growing. More and more Western companies in the integration of CRM (Customer Relationship Management) into their business are focused on mobile solutions. Such a direction for its activities was chosen by the Russian project QuickOrder, covering at once all the main mobile platforms - iOS, Android and Windows Phone.

As a target audience for integrating their software, QuickOrder developers took the restaurant business and, in the future, a chain of restaurants and fast-food cafes. Applications are electronic menus of cafes and restaurants, with the ability to receive payments. With their help, the service offers a wide range of opportunities for both restaurateurs and customers.

For the first, QuickOrder is an effective communication channel with visitors and a tool to increase the revenue of the establishment. With the help of the software, the institution forms a knowledge base about visitors, improves the quality of service and optimizes the work of the staff. In addition, the software provides tools for collecting statistics and analytics.

Users of the service also have something to attract. Customers of restaurants directly in the application can see information about the institution, contacts, news, photos, as well as browse the menu and order selected dishes.

Recently, the international chain of coffee shops Starbucks announced that it is introducing an ordering system and the subsequent payment of coffee through an application in a smartphone. QuickOrder users got this opportunity even earlier. Having tied a bank card to his account, a client can pay her in a restaurant without any participation of the waiter. Additional motivation to download the app, rather than go to the restaurant in the old fashioned way (wait for the waiter to place an order, then wait again to pay) is also present. Registered users have access to a list of promotions and special offers of restaurants, and in some of them when ordering through QuickOrder, visitors receive discounts ranging from 20% to 50%.

For guests of restaurants working with applications is as follows. Downloading QuickOrder (for free, of course) in the application store of your mobile platform, we register in the service - you can log in via Vkontakte or Facebook, let the application determine the geolocation, go to the search section, find the restaurant of interest, scroll through the menu, select the dishes, find on the table, for which we sit, the QR code for which we place an order.
