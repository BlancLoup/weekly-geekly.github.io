Sony Ericsson will have its own online music store.

Despite the very bad experience of Sony Corporation with the launch of the Connect online music store on the market, the guys from Sony Ericsson want to try their luck in the same field and create a similar service aimed at music mobile phone owners. The corresponding plans were announced today by representatives of the company, who indicated the second quarter of next year as the most likely time for the launch of an ambitious project.

It should be noted that Sony Ericsson already has experience in this business, although it is not gigantic: the company cooperates with Sony BMG, thanks to which phone owners can download music released specifically by this label. However, soon such companies as EMI Group, Universal Music and Warner Music will appear in the list of record companies.

According to Sony Ericsson, the tracks will most likely be distributed in MP3 and WMA formats, but how many users will have to pay for each downloaded song, the authors of the project are not yet recognized.
