Spring School Front-End Programming in St. Petersburg

Spring School Front-End Programming (JS \ CSS \ HTML) is a specialized course from leading IT companies in St. Petersburg and a programming guru.

The school is aimed at developing talents and learning the practical things necessary for successful hiring in an IT organization.
The course is built on examples and tasks. It is not enough one theory, practice is always necessary, and the more - the better. Throughout the course of training there will be many examples and tasks.
Teachers will give only relevant information. Specific examples, how modern projects are made, and not how it was 3 years ago.

The composition of lecturers is formed from leading companies.


Students who successfully complete the course will receive diplomas certified by the companies represented on the course, the best will be offered employment in the company whose representatives have taught the course.
