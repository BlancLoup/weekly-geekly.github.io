How good are the new Intel Skylake processors

What the company says about its chips, which comparisons are correct, and which are not quite - let's try to briefly analyze the situation.


/ photo by Uwe Hermann CC

Marketing marketing, but Intel compares Skylake with chips five years ago. For this, a flurry of criticism struck the company, including from major online magazines .

Of course, against the background of its predecessor, Skylake looks much faster and more energy efficient, but if you put it alongside Broadwell, then the new one no longer looks so impressive. Only 10% increase in speed and only a small increase in working with graphics.

We constantly quote the opinion of Patrick Moorhead, an expert of Moor Insights & Strategies. He says that such a comparison is important not for the experts, but for the audience itself of the new product. It can be tens of millions.

If we look at the situation from this perspective, then Skylake should encourage people to buy new models and replace their gadgets. Intel and its partners in general are not required.
