Nikon introduces new flagship: D3X



Last day, Nikon finally pulled off the veil of secrecy from the new SLR camera, the flagship of the entire line, the D3X, and it turned out that almost everything that went as rumors was true.
The full-size giant has a 24.5-megapixel CMOS matrix with a high signal-to-noise ratio and can take up to 5 frames per second (7 in framing FX mode), the ISO sensitivity range is from 100 to 1600. Since the camera is professional, you can say and the important figures for people who understand: the camera's power on time is 0.12 seconds, and the kevlar shutter lag is 0.04 seconds.

Branded autofocus system (51st point AF), EXPEED image processing system, improved image recognition system, 3-inch VGA display, dual CF card slot, HDMI video output - all this comes as standard, you only need to pay for new camera GPS module.

The price in Russia has not yet been announced, Western resources are talking about availability for a considerable $ 8,000.
