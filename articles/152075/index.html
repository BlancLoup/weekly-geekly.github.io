2 free paper templates for mobile app prototyping

The site Smashing Magazine posted two new useful tools for mobile developers. This is a set of blanks for drawing sketches of interfaces of common types of Outline mobile devices and templates for sizing the elements of the touch interface, taking into account the resolution and physical size of real Tapsize devices. Both sets are designed for printing on paper.



Outline contains 28 PDF for seven types of mobile devices - Android, BlackBerry, iOS (iPad and iPhone), Meego, Symbian, webOS, Windows Phone 7. There are images of devices in portrait and landscape orientations, full-size and 10 per page. As a bonus - Adobe Illustrator file with images of devices.
