Linux Tycoon: Distribution Building Simulator

With the help of the App Store and the games contained in it, you can become, perhaps, everything you wish. Immortal warrior, undercover police officer or the owner of a football club. But if none of these roles suits you - how about a Linux developer?

The new game, developed by Lunduke, gives you the opportunity to take on such a role, which I am sure you have long dreamed of. The company is working on a Linux Tycoon project, a game that is described as ‚Äúthe best Linux distribution simulator in the universe‚Äù. Sounds exciting, doesn't it?



The game, in fact, will be familiar to all lovers of Magnates, but instead of a roller coaster and zoos in it, you will need to create a Linux distribution. While it is not clear what exactly will have to do? Lunduke wisely provided an exemplary list of software mogul cases:

  • Analysis and selection of software packages;
  • Correction of bugs;
  • Management of volunteers and employees;
  • Manage the size of the distribution and maintain it within the specified framework;
  • And much more!


The Linux Tycoon is already available for purchase as a beta on Lunduke.com . While there is a game for Linux, Windows and OS X, the release on iOS is expected. In addition, developers are working on an online version, which will probably become the world's first MMOLDBSG (Massively Multiplayer Online Linux Distro Building Simulation Game).
