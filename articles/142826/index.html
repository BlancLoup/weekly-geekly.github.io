WWDC 2012 will be held from June 11 to 15


On the official Apple website , there was news that WWDC 2012 (Worldwide Developer Conference 2012) will be held from June 11 to 15 in San Francisco and in the banal exhibition center Moscone West. At this conference, we will most likely see a new and updated iOS 6 platform and of course the topic will be touched on Mac OS X Mountain Lion , which is now in beta. It is possible that Siri will also be removed from the beta version.
At the conference, developers will be able to gain experience in creating the development of their applications. To do this, the conference will be Apple engineers who will kindly answer developer questions. Tickets for WWDC 2012 cost $ 1600, but they have already been bought out in less than 2 hours. But that's not all. Developers who did not get to the conference will be able to follow the conference via a special link .
Apple also opened a registration at the Apple Design Awards, which also took place at the WWDC conference. The Apple Design Awards is an annual award that is presented to the best applications for iOS and Mac OS X from the point of view of quality, innovation and design. This event is held in order to identify the most talented, innovative and the best software and hardware development for iOS and Mac OS X operating environments from developers. Developers must submit their applications in the App Store and Mac App Store no later than May 1st. Since 2003, awards in the form of a cube are awarded to each nominee. On the verge of a cube, the Apple logo is applied, which glows when touched. Let me remind you that last year's winners were Chair Entertainment with their excellent game Infinity Blade , Algoriddim GmbH with their Djay app (for iPad), Chillingo along with its Cut the Rope and also Pixelmator UA with Pixelmator for Mac.

I think that the conference will be very interesting indeed.
