Office Plankton Overalls - Washable Suit


From February of next year in Japan, the sale of business suits will begin, which can be in the evening, coming from work, to wash in the shower, leave for the night on a hanger and put on in the morning. The suit will not need to be ironed or dried with any special devices; it will take on its original form without additional effort as soon as it dries. A suit made of wool with the use of the latest technology will be produced for both men and women and will cost from $ 260 USD to $ 492 USD.

I think the idea will appeal to those who actively participate in corporate events and then do not remember where this digested sausage on its costume came from. And yet, a small idea, for especially lazy people or those who constantly do not have enough time - now you can take a shower right in a suit! Two birds with one stone!
