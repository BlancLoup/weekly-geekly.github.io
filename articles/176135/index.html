Announced a new version of Google Play (Google Play 4.0)



Google has launched a phased update of Google Play. The new version, as far as can be judged, has a more or less minimalist design, which is quite different from the previous version. In general, there were no problems with this before, but now it is even easier to quickly find the necessary section, since the interface is quite friendly.

In the Play Store, image sizes are now increased, plus more recommendations are shown. As you scroll, additional applications, books, movies, and other types of content appear.

Among other things, the developers have made several improvements in the mode of purchase, removing some flaws and making a number of corrections. Perhaps after this update, a number of users will disappear all sorts of glitches with purchases inside Google Play.



The updated version of the Google Play Store is available for all Android devices with a version not lower than 2.2. And yes, despite the fact that the update is already happening right now, it is still phased, which means that some users will see the updated version of Google Play in a day or two (or even a week).
