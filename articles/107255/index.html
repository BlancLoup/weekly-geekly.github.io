Virtual choir

A choir of more than 200 people from 12 countries around the world created Eric Whitacre and Scott Haines living in Los Angeles.

The idea to create a choir came to Eric after recording the song online. He decided to create a real virtual choir. Recording each voice separately, he subsequently joined them into one beautiful choir. Great idea and its implementation. Watch Eric Whitacre's interview.



The choir members live in: Austria, Argentina, Canada, England, Germany, Ireland, New Zealand, the Philippines, Singapore, Spain, Sweden, and the United States of America. And you, Habra-man, you can easily join these people.









Project website: ericwhitacre.com/the-virtual-choir
