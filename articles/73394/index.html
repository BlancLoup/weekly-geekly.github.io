In reality, Facebook has always embodied some kind of hacker culture, that is, from the very beginning they set a single goal: to make a good project in terms of programming and performance, in order to appeal to people. The company had no other goals. They served a growing stream of users and bought new servers for $ 85. The expenditure part is always ahead of the revenue.
