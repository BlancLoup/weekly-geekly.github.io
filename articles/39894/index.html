Steps to improve sleep



1. Air out the room before bed.

I agree, trite, but very effective. The presence of fresh air in the room helps our brain to rest normally.

2. Rid yourself of temperature fluctuations.

It often happened that in order to comply with point one, I simply opened the window and then forgot to close it. And at night it usually gets colder than during the day. The result is that you have a bad sleep and in the morning you just can't get out of the blanket.

3. Calm exercises or walk before bedtime.

By calm exercises, I mean light stretching or breathing exercises. It improves the flow of blood and oxygen to the brain. The walk is the same - movement + breath.

4. Do not get enough!

Also a hackneyed fact, but again, very important. Stuffing the stomach before bedtime does not lead to anything good. Tightly eat is necessary for 3-4 hours before bedtime. Drink a lot too. A sandwich and an average cup of tea are possible, but more obviously not worth it. Pity your body, he wants to sleep too, and not to recycle your food.
