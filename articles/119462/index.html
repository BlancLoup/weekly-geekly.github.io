Kiev ALT.NET


Kiev ALT.NET is an informal community of professional developers based on the .NET platform in the glorious city of Kiev. A feature of the meetings is the absence of any restrictions in the choice of topics for discussion, and in the choice of places and beers for the after-party. The only working criterion by which meetings are planned is the presence of interest from the majority of participants.


Meetings



The next meeting will be held this Friday , May 20th. We swung to the sacred: a comparison of Continuous Integration servers: CC.NET, TeamCity, Jenkins (aka Hudson) and ... TFS. Bets are accepted;). After deciding what is best for Continuous Integration, there will be two more bonuses: the first is a demonstration of Continuous Deployment using the ChuckNorris Tools ( UppercuT and RoundhousE ), and the second is a traditional afterparty, where you can learn a lot of interesting things from the speakers and colleagues.

Details in the invitation . We have no registration as such, just write to support@kievalt.net .

Future plans


There are plenty of topics for discussion. Most likely candidates for the following meetings:

1. NoSQL MongoDb / RavenDb, we have not yet decided under what sauce, but we have gained enough experience, so it will be interesting
2. Development of the Continuous Deployment theme is possible: toolling, zero downtime deployment.
3. Distributed Version Control Systems
4. Continue the topic F #
5. And really want AsyncCTP / TPL.
