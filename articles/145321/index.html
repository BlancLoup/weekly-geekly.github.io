Samsung 900X4C




We are already familiar with this comrade: for the first time he appeared on CES 2012. But only by the summer of Samsung did the 900X4C be modified so that it could be shown to everyone and not put it next to a special person.



The 900X4C is Samsung's first 15-inch ultrabook and immediately the thinnest in the world. The device is representative of the 9th series and it is not surprising that it looks very similar to the 13-inch model. The 900X4C aluminum case is 14.9 mm thick and weighs 1.65 kg.

This ultrabook was given a powerful stuffing: processors Core i5 and i7 of the third generation, 8 GB of RAM and SSD-drive. The graphics, however, built-in - Intel HD 4000.

The display of the 900X4C is matte, with a nice thin edging and a resolution of 1600x900.

From the ports: three USB (one of them is version 3.0), microHDMI and miniVGA, headphone jack, LAN through an adapter and a card reader on the bottom under the plug.
