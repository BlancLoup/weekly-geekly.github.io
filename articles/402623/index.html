CampStove 2: Camping Stove, from which you can charge gadgets



Without all sorts of gadgets, many people are already afraid to go out. In fact, how can you spend a whole hour without your favorite smartphone? And what can we say about hiking, fishing or hunting? Despite the fact that this is a rest, which is used for rest from everyday life and work, many of us take at least a telephone with us.

But here is the problem. Far from everywhere and it is not always possible to find a source of electricity. Let's say you set off into the woods with a tent. Sat phone battery, and you need to make an urgent call. Perhaps a spare battery or power bank was successfully forgotten at home or something happened to him. What to do in this case? The company BioLite proposes to use CampStove camping stove charging, and already the second version. With the help of this interesting device, you can cook food (fry fish there) and recharge the phone.

It would seem - a strange combination. The stove and the charger - is it not ridiculous? Not at all. The fact is that a thermoelectric generator is built into a safe, portable stove that is fired with ordinary wood. For the convenience of the user, there is also a fan that supplies oxygen to the fire source. As a result, the fire burns even better and produces energy.

It is fed to the built-in USB-port, from which you can charge anything.


The company improved the performance of the second version of the furnace, raising the total power to 3 watts. The developers added a built-in battery with a capacity of 2600 mAh. If there is nothing to charge, the battery stores energy, which can be used later. In order to make your device even more interesting, the company added LED indicators. They show the temperature of the flame, the speed of the fan and the total charge of the internal battery.

As for the latter, it may have been worth making it more voluminous - there are no restrictions here. But manufacturers did not want to make their device massive. His weight remained the same - 935 grams. In the campaign, of course, every gram counts, so it was decided to dispense with an enlarged battery.



The cost of the device is about $ 130 from the manufacturer. In general, it is a little more than a different kind of stoves and marching stoves for professional tourists.
