Facebook provides the ability to edit comments.



On many sites go screenshots of comments of users of the social network Facebook relating to the genre of "Fail". Probably a fairly large percentage of users of this social network regretted that, until now, developers have not introduced the ability to edit their own user comments (in fact, it exists, but the 30-second interval is uncomfortable when the comment remains editable). Now such an opportunity will appear, and soon.

So, in a couple of weeks, all users will have the opportunity to work with the comment editing function.
