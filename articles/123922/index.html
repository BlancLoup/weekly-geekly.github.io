The world's first video editing editor.

According to the developers of the video editor Novacut Pro Video Editor, their brainchild is the world's first video editor with the ability to co-edit video in real time.

The project is under active development and developers are seeking financial assistance. A good part of the work has already been done, but much more needs to be done, for which the well-known Kickstarter service was attracted to obtain the necessary funds. On the project page, you can contribute to the creation of this unique application, watch the promo video, as well as follow the updates.

The creators give a few promises:
  • your work is priceless, so backward compatibility will be maintained forever
  • roadmap will be published, and the development process will be transparent
  • we will ask you what you need and we will listen to your answers
  • the editor will always be free (FOSS)

More information can be found here .
