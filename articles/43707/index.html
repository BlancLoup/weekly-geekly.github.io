Moscow, Monday, November 3, Usability-Game

On the next Monday, November 3, ErgoPro holds an educational and entertaining Usability Game in Moscow.

The goal of the game: to draw up a plan-scheme of the central interchange hub of the Moscow metro (station of the Library named after Lenin, Alexander Garden, Arbat and Borovitskaya). We will discuss the problems faced by passengers, formulate the requirements for the format of the scheme, thoroughly examine the interchange node and design the scheme. We will tell about the course of the game and the results at a conference dedicated to World Usability Day .

Number of participants: up to 10 people. We will be especially glad to see participants from other cities.

Collection point: Moscow, Gazetny per., 17, McDonalds. 300 meters from Okhotny Ryad metro station, exit to the National Hotel, then up Tverskaya Street to the second turn to the left.
Collection point on the map

Collection time: Monday 3 November, 12:00.
Take with you: (1) A4 notebook or tablet for A4 paper, (2) pen, (3) simple pencil, (4) travel card or money for a ticket on the subway, (5) lunch money.

Those who wish to participate must register by sending their full name and phone number to the address degat [gav] yandex.ru.
