Happy birthday to Google!


According to the Google calendar , Google has turned 10 years old today (Google Inc. appeared on this day). In fact, this miracle is about 13 years old, before the 2005th year, they were celebrated on the 10th, but the domain itself was registered on the 15th . Since then, they celebrate at different dates, for example, in 2002 they celebrated on the 27th. While they are determined by the date, let's congratulate them on their anniversary! During these 10 years, they managed to create a lot of useful programs and services - for which humankind should thank the staff-developers of this company.
