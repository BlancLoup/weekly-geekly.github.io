May 28, Russia celebrates SEO Day

May 28, SEO-optimizers celebrate their professional holiday.


The first SEO specialists appeared in the late 90s of the last century, when the first search engines began to gain popularity, and the revenues of sites directly depended on their positions in the results of search results. Many years have passed since then, ranking algorithms have changed, but the main traffic to the sites still comes from the search. Therefore, today SEO is a powerful industry with multi-million dollar turnover.


Domestic SEO specialists have been celebrating their professional holiday, the Runet Optimizer Day, since 2006. The date of the holiday was chosen by voting on the Searchengines forum and
no binding or symbol does not contain.

4) The question that worries many: what do SEOs, and how to measure the results of their work?

Roman Rublevsky: Covers existing demand. Search engines are the main traffic generator, that is, search engines drive the main share of traffic to sites. Accordingly, each site would like to cover some existing information demand on its topic. To pick up these queries, make the site search for these queries, working closely with the site owner - this is the task of the SEO ... There are a number of metrics that can characterize the work: positions - aggregate indicator, visibility, more correct metric - search traffic volume, even more correct metrics, this is how this traffic is converted into the necessary actions and in what time frame it occurs.
Alexey Korostelev: You can evaluate the results of the SEO work on the basis of the tasks assigned to him. In most cases, this is of course: the growth of site positions in search engines, an increase in site traffic, an increase in sales from the site.


Anton Uryadnikov: Easy. There are qualitative and quantitative indicators of site visitors, they can be seen through Yandex.Metrica, for example. The standard Yandex.Metrica reports are enough to evaluate the performance of the optimizer.

7) Some tips for those who are looking for a contractor for search engine optimization of their site

Try to interview only those companies that have worked with the contractor for more than 1 year.
