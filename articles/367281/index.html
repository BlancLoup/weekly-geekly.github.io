For any Steam game, you can now request a refund "for any reason"



Steam, Valve's gaming platform, has updated some of the terms of the user agreement. At the same time, updates are aimed at improving interaction with users, especially in terms of reimbursement of funds for the purchase of a game or game content. Thus, the new rules suggest the possibility of a refund for almost any game, ‚Äúfor any reason,‚Äù as specified in the agreement. Conditions - the game must be purchased no later than 14 days before contacting support. In addition, the total time of the game should not exceed 2 hours.

Valve will return the money within a week by choosing a user-friendly payment method. If there are problems with payment, the money will go to the Steam Wallet.

Of course, there is no guarantee of a refund. In addition, it is not yet clear for which games it will be possible to demand a refund. Indeed, many games like ‚ÄúGone Home‚Äù can be completed in less than two hours. Probably, all these moments will clear up a little later - after a series of user requests to support.

Until now, in the USA and many other countries for the Valve games it was simply impossible to get a refund. An exception was made for the countries of the European Union and New Zealand, where the policy of protecting the interests of customers is quite strict. Now the rules have changed, and if necessary (for example, the game did not like it), it will be possible to return the funds.

Here is what the rules say:
"You can request a refund for almost all purchases on Steam for any reason. Perhaps your computer does not meet the system requirements of the game. Or you bought the game by mistake. Maybe you spent an hour in the game, and you just did not like it.
It does not matter. Valve will refund funds regardless of any circumstances, if a refund was requested via help.steampowered.com no later than fourteen days after the purchase, and if you spent less than two hours in the game. You can find more detailed information below, but even if your situation does not meet the described rules, you can still request a refund, and we will consider your appeal.
Your purchase will be fully refunded within one week after the return is approved. The funds will be transferred to your Steam wallet or back to the payment method you used when purchasing. If for any reason Steam cannot return the money for the payment method, your Steam wallet will be credited with the appropriate amount. (Some payment methods available in the Steam store in your country may not support returns. Click here to view the full list.) "
