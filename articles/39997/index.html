The Russian State University of Innovation Technologies and Entrepreneurship (RGUITP), within the framework of the Federal Target Program for the Development of Education for 2006-2010, announces an All-Russian competition for the best innovative and technological entrepreneurial project among young people. More about the competition ...

Citizens of the Russian Federation aged from 18 to 30 have the right to participate in the Contest.

The competition is held in the following categories:

1. Medicine, biotechnology and agribusiness
2. Instrumentation
3. IT and telecommunications
4. Transport and engine building
5. Industrial technology
6. Nanotechnology and new materials
7. Energy and energy
8. Ecology and resource saving
9. Electronics
10. Security
