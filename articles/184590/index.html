I have to admit, the study was interesting again. Details and some thoughts - under the cut.



Work for interest

Remember that poll - 32.8% of students expect their salary after university to be more than 150 thousand rubles? Given this, it is interesting that the wages only for 23% of the respondents is key. The rest say that this is not the most, and often - not a significant factor of choice at all. This suggests two conclusions:
