Another minus Kyivstar


Despite the fact that I have chosen Kievstar as the main and only operator for a long time, it never ceases to amaze me with various unpleasant trifles. And sometimes such trifles become not trifles at all, as they can paralyze part of the working day.

Here is another bug that I noticed (or rather I ran into it) a long time ago, I informed the representatives of the call center about it, but they stupidly beat it and this bug is still alive. We are talking about the mobile Internet. The fact is that I use the Internet from my smartphone constantly, 24 hours a day. My smart works even when I sleep!

I order the packages according to the following scheme: I chose for myself the optimal amount of MB in the amount of 5000 and every first day of each month my account is charged for using the Internet during the month , which is approximately 130 UAH. And here's what carp: if my account runs out of funds (in my case, the balance becomes less than -142 hrn.), Then I can not make either outgoing calls, which is logical, but I can not use already paid in 100% the size of the internet !!! That is never logical. Of course, you should always monitor the state of balance, because to remain without an opportunity to call is very bad, but it also happens that a decent and solvent citizen just insisted on replenishing an account. The bottom line is that paid services should not be disconnected in any way!
