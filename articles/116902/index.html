With the assistance of Skype and AdTech and Enterprise Estonia, the world's first booth was installed at Tallinn Airport, which any visitor can use for free for video chat via Skype (naturally, an account is still required in the system). As well as the usual desktop or mobile version, you can make calls and Skype-phone, pre-paid call Skype currency.

The booth is equipped with a 22-inch touchscreen and a handset. However, you can connect your headset.
