Navigator with video recorder SeeMax Navi E540 HD DVR

Under habrakatom review navigator SeeMax Navi E540 HD DVR with integrated DVR, which motorists can appreciate.



The appearance of new items made in a classic design. Most of the front panel takes a color screen. Under it is the logo of the manufacturer. Most of the body is made of matte black plastic. Gloss is the only front panel, which is framed by a golden rim. On the rear panel are the slots of the built-in speaker, the reset button and the main "chip" - the camera.



The power button on the device is on top. Just below the upper left corner of the case on the front panel is an LED indicator that glows in different colors depending on the actions performed by the navigator.



On the left side you can find a mini-USB port, a memory card slot and a headphone jack. A slot for a memory card, which is recorded from the camera, is located on the upper end.



Screen Navi E540 HD DVR has a diagonal of 5 ", its resolution is 800x480 points. The sensor is made by resistive technology. The screen has high-quality color reproduction and good sensitivity to clicks. And due to the brightness brightness margin, working with a navigator in the bright sun will not make any difficulties.

As for the main purpose of the navigator, the SiRF Atlas V chip is installed for this purpose. The processor operates at 664 MHz, there is support for DDR2 memory, which is 128 MB in size and is installed in the navigator. An interesting feature of SiRF Atlas V is the use of SiRF Instant Fix II technology, which allows you to simulate the trajectories of the movement of satellites, thereby reducing the cold start time. Considerable can be called the amount of non-volatile memory, which is equal to 8 GB. The devices are running the operating system Windows CE 6.0. Navitel is the navigation software here. For those who love other software, it is possible to install it.



The DVR records video with a maximum resolution of 1280x720 pixels. The video quality is sufficient to view the numbers of the vehicle in front (at a distance of 5-10 m) and remove the situation in front of the car. Useful functionality in our time.

I would also like to note the presence of additional features. By clicking on the menu item "Books", you will find a program for reading books in txt format. The menu item "Photo" allows you to view photos in the following file formats: jpg, bmp, gif, png and tif.

In the Video submenu, you can view video formats AVI, MPG (mpeg-1 and mpeg-2), AVI (divx), MPG, DAT, ASF, WMV, MOV. In the ‚ÄúFlash‚Äù submenu you can view flash-videos. In the ‚ÄúMusic‚Äù submenu, music formats MP3, WMA and WAV are played.

The package of the device contains a mount on the windshield, car charger, USB cable, stylus, leather carrying case and user manual.

Conclusions: SeeMax Navi E540 HD DVR - a device with a good range of features. Built-in video recorder allows you to remove the situation in front of the car, not covering the cabin with additional devices.

And finally, the existential question: how much? In the retail device will cost about 140 dollars. The price is not bad as for 2 in one: navigator + recorder.

Users fear that it is two-in-one, but it seems that it cannot work simultaneously as a recorder and as a navigator.
