Spring Update "Tutumba"

In April, we updated the interface and added several new features to our Tutumba project . Search and choose places have now become much more convenient, and business owners have access to additional tools to attract an audience.

Now each visitor of the project is greeted by a new page of the city, where he can familiarize himself with popular institutions, read the most interesting reviews and get acquainted with the best reviewers.



The search has become more convenient and clearer at the expense of the map, which displays the companies found at the user's request. Due to this, the user is much easier to choose the nearest and most convenient institution for him.



Significantly improved search for metro stations in Moscow and St. Petersburg. The interface has become more convenient and now you can select several stations at once to search for places near them.

The changes affected the company pages. Information about the current rating of the company has become much more visual, due to the graph showing the distribution of user ratings. Now, even without reading all the reviews, you can understand how the company is valued by its customers.



But the most important innovations concern business owners. All of them got access to the statistics of attendance of their page on Tutumba and the opportunity to comment on user reviews about their company.

In addition, they now have access to features that will attract more attention and get new customers.
