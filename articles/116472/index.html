Google launches Talk Guru chat bot

Google Labs released a new experimental service called Google Talk Guru . In fact, this is a normal chat bot that can handle a limited list of commands and respond to them.

You can just talk, you need to add to Google Talk guru@googlelabs.com

The greeting displays a list of available commands:

Hello! Please enter a query. The following topics are supported: score, weather, define, translate, web. Enter 'help' to learn more, ie 'help score'.

Of all the teams work well weather, define, calculator, currency converter.



According to the score, I could not achieve results other than those proposed in the help Arsenal. The web request seemed to me strange and inconvenient for a chat bot, but it was very cut and uninformative. Translate into Russian does not translate.

In general, the bot responds very quickly, but the convenience of its use for me is still in doubt.
