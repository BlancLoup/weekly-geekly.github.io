ThL W8 - a harmonious combination of price and quality

The Chinese company ThL has long been known in the market of mobile technology. The manufacturer does not stand still and develops with the progress and needs of customers. And this time, the company ThL released its flagship smartphone W8. This model is currently the most technologically advanced in the entire line from the Chinese manufacturer.



The ThL W8 smartphone has all the necessary attributes of a modern mobile communications device. Powerful features, various modules, as well as one of the latest versions of Android. Although the appearance of the device was perfectly copied from the well-known Samsung Galaxy S3, the work of this Chinese company deserves special attention ...

Specifications

ThL W8 smartphone is equipped with the latest novelty of the Chinese telephone industry - the top-end (for the Chinese) MT6589 chipset from MediaTek. The platform includes a quad-core processor built on ARM-cores Cortex A7 with a maximum frequency of 1.2 GHz, and the graphics accelerator PowerVR SGX544. The LED display has a 5-inch diagonal, a resolution of 1280 √ó 720 pixels and a capacitive sensor that recognizes up to 5 touches. The smartphone runs on Android OS 4.1 Jelly Bean. On board is installed 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of internal flash memory. In addition, ThL W8 is equipped with two cameras, the front with a resolution of 5 megapixels and the rear of 13 megapixels with autofocus and flash. As the power source uses a battery capacity of 2000 mAh.

Software platform

Despite the budget of the device, a relatively fresh version of the operating system Google Android 4.1.2 Jelly Bean is installed in this smartphone.



The shell interface is slightly modified (color schemes, wallpapers and icons have been added), but mostly the bare Android everywhere.



ThL has modified the lock screen, which now allows you to get quick access to certain applications when you unlock the phone. Namely: Camera, Phone, Unlock, Messages. You can also change the unlock mechanism - choose between the above method, face recognition, pattern, PIN-code or password.



The notification area contains the basic settings you need. There is a management of SIM-cards. And you can also change the sound profile.



ThL did a good job with the performance and interface functionality so that everything runs smoothly and does not exactly have to complain. The smartphone is already pre-installed Google Play. There is a fairly large number of compatible applications that run without lags.
