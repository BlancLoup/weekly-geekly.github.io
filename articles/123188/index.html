RIPE Visits Selectel Holds Training Courses in St. Petersburg


RIPE (European Internet Registrar, an organization that distributes IP addresses) conducts two training courses in St. Petersburg.

RIPE NCC LIR Training Course

Passing: 07/21/2011
Venue: St. Petersburg, at the Holiday Inn Hotel (see address below)
Topics:
  • The Internet Registry (IR) System
  • Exercise: RIPE and the RIPE NCC
  • The policy development process
  • Exercise: First Day at Work as an LIR Contact
  • The RIPE Database
  • IPv4 Resources
  • Exercise: Making Assignments
  • Other Resources: AS Numbers & IPv6
  • RIPE NCC Services
  • Exercise: Slogan

RIPE NCC IPv6 Training Course

Passing: 07/22/2011
Venue: St. Petersburg, at the Holiday Inn Hotel (ibid., Address below)
Topics:
  • The registry system
  • IPv4?
  • Exercise: Business Case
  • IPv6 Basics
  • Exercise: Addressing Plan
  • Getting it
  • Deploying
  • Exercise: Deployment Challenges

Schedule and location

Schedule:
9: 00-9: 30 - Welcome (coffee)
9: 30-13: 00 - Morning part
13: 00-14: 00 - Lunch
14: 00-16: 30 - Afternoon
16: 30-17: 30 - Free discussion

A place:
St. Petersburg, Moskovsky Prospect, 97B (Holiday Inn Hotel www.hi-spb.com )

At the moment there are still places left. Classes are free for organizations with LIR status (included in the annual fee).

Classes are held in English

If someone wants to participate, submit an application on the page lirportal.ripe.net/training/courses .
