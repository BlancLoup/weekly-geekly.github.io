Video books

Probably start this blog with audio video books! A video book is something between a usual video sequence, an illustration or a photograph and an audio book in the usual sense. Everything is quite simple: take static pictures, add a couple of simple effects (such as smooth input / output, scaling, color transitions) to taste, we put it all on the original audio track. Video book is ready! The final file format does not matter; the main thing is the effect on the viewer.

- Has this already happened?
- I have not met.

- Who needs it?
- Well, first of all, to all who love audio books. You come home, you want to relax, turn on the video book, you start to go about your business and occasionally you look at the big plasma with the latest bestseller. Maybe this will be a new means of expression for future photographers. You come to the gallery where you get into the charming atmosphere created on the basis of magnificent panoramas of nature and fairy tales of the peoples of Polynesia. Or just a documentary video book - "Works of primitive artists."

- I want to look at it!
