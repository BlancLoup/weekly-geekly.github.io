Bring me a blue bathrobe


I sat in my office and worked. Before me and my team was another technical problem. To paint one of the products produced in our company, it was necessary to come up with a new sample. I set this task for myself and my subordinates. I have always practiced this approach. Himself and all. This brought competitive notes to the work and supported the spirit of the inventor in me.

The loading of the curing oven, in which the parts were baked, was insufficient. And the fault was the existing linkage. In addition to low load, they were inconvenient to use. To insert parts into holes and get them after baking - mockery of workers. In addition, the manufacture of the hinges required considerable labor costs. It is flexible a corner, contact welding of these corners, puncture. Two weeks passed after the task was announced. My employees did not have a solution. Bring me a blue robe, I'm going to the workshop.

When we are looking for technical solutions, in the first place there should always be a one-word postulate - simplicity. This rule is valid everywhere. The ideal is not where there is nothing to add, but where there is nothing to take away. Remember.
