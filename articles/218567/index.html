Perfeo 1019-IPS - budget wagon

Today we have on the review of the tablet Perfeo 1019-IPS. Honestly, before he got into my hands, I did not know anything about this brand. The video service concern sells the tablet, and it produces, of course, China.



A quick inspection of its characteristics in conjunction with its price seemed to me quite interesting, and I decided to get to know him better. So, today we have a budget tablet, which is positioned as a universal one.

Screen: 10.1 ‚Äù, 1280x800 px, HD, IPS
Processor: MT8389 (Cortex-A7, 1.2 GHz, 4 cores)
Video accelerator: PowerVR SGX544
Operating system: Android 4.2.2 (Jelly Bean)
RAM: 1 GB
User memory: 8 GB + microSD slot
Camera: front - 0.3 megapixel, rear - 5 megapixel
Connectivity: 2 SIM cards (GSM / GPRS / EDGE + UMTS / HSPA +)

‚ñå PACKING AND COMPLETE SET


The tablet comes in a high quality box. For some reason, I have such a box associated with board games. High-quality printing, the density of the cardboard allows the tablet to survive even sending by mail to Russia :)



On the sides of the box we are waiting for a fascinating listing of the characteristics and awards of devices of this brand ... And on the back of the box - detailed characteristics of our tablet.



Inside we see - OTG cable, microUSB cable, charging 5V 2A, warranty and instruction. Complete rich can not be called, but everything you need is there.





By the way, since the tablet is powered by 5V, a distinctive feature is the ability to charge from USB. But since for normal charging you need 2A, then charging from USB is very slow, for normal charging it is recommended to turn off the device.

‚ñå DESIGN, DIMENSIONS, CONTROL ELEMENTS


Externally, the device looks very compact. Compared to classic tablets, this is achieved at the expense of thinner frames around the screen and a widescreen 16:10 screen. Front view classic for tablets. There are no hardware buttons on the front panel. The screen looks like glass. Of the minuses - collects all the fingerprints and a little glare.



The body is made quite high quality: no squeaks, no gaps. For two weeks of operation, the back surface does not have scratches and scuffs. Fingerprints on the back surface are unobtrusive and easily removed. By the way, the tablet is quite thin - 10 mm.



There is a front camera and proximity sensor.



The reverse side of the tablet is made of aluminum, except for the plastic insert on top, we'll talk about it a little later. There can be two colors - dark indigo (he is judging by the inscription on the box - titanium, our case) or silver. The top of the plastic insert is decorated with a 5 MP camera and under it a flash. In the center is the inscription "Perfeo", model, link to the site and serial number. Left and right are the stereo speakers. The side edges of the tablet have a bevel towards the screen, it is quite convenient. In particular, when installed in the car holder, the bevel prevents the holder from pressing the buttons. In addition, the tablet has 2 stereo speakers, they sound quite loud and do not wheeze at the maximum.



On the right side of the tablet are the hardware volume buttons and the Back button. I still find it difficult to assess how convenient this solution is. In principle, it is duplicated by on-screen buttons, but if you need another button here, then after getting root-rights, you can reassign it to the camera or to the home button with a specialized software (not included). Please note that the buttons are also metallic. All buttons and connectors are clearly marked and catchy.



The left side has only a hole under the microphone.



The bottom line is clean. All the fun is hidden on the top. On the right side, above the volume buttons is the power button.



At the top left are all the main connectors - mini HDMI, microUSB, a standard headphone / headset jack, a charger connector. Decisions to place next to the headphone jack and power seemed to me not very successful - they are easy to confuse.



Now is the time to return to our plastic insert. The upper edge has two notches, thanks to which it is easily removed.



And under it, there were 2 connectors for ordinary (mini) SIM cards, a connector for a microSD card. And on the left is the hardware button - reset. In my opinion, a very elegant solution is to hide the cards under the cover.

The screen supports multitouch 5 touches.

‚ñå SOFTWARE PLATFORM


The tablet uses Android 4.2.2. Typical. No Chinese software. Installed services from Yandex.



In addition to Yandex, there were also a number of programs: clients of social networks VKontakte and Odnoklassniki, Skype, video player, audio player, PDF readers and something else.
Also included is a special version of the program Navitel Navigator with a free test period for 90 days from the date of activation without card restrictions.
Out of habit, the first thing I got on the tablet is root (useful for connecting ntfs disks) - you just need to install the program Framaroot. One click and root rights are obtained.



OTG works without surprises. The mice, the keyboard earned, I did not have flash drives. I connected the hard drive to 1 terabyte. Immediately wound up. I had to connect external power to the hard drive and install the free Paragon NTFS & HFS + program (requires root) to access the NTFS disks.



For external power, the hard drive used the Power Bank at 7800 mAh from the same company (Perfeo PF-7800). It uses 3 SAMSUNG 18650 batteries. I consider it as a road power source, it has 2 ports of 1A + and has a thin-charging wire for tablets. As a result, you can charge an external hard drive and a 5V-powered tablet (such as our client in question) on the road at the same time.

The device has GPS with A-GPS support. It seems that they finally put a good antenna. I saw for the first time that GPS-based GPS was catching satellites in a room. True, only 2-4 pieces, but 2-3 meters from the window. By the way, GPS earned out of the box, no traditional shamanism, as for devices directly purchased in China, was required.
In general, the satellites catches well. Comes with a test 90 day Navitel test version. 90 days are counted from the moment of activation in the program. To activate and download maps you need internet. In the presence of the Internet is available and traffic jams. A test trip of 100 km revealed no problems with GPS, the signal is not lost, the route itself is not rebuilt while driving.
In the beginning, it seems that the tablet is too big for navigation. But on the other hand, it, installed downward of the windshield of the car, does not close the important viewing areas (depending on the height of your landing in the car). Cellular communication, voice and data also work. On one of the SIM-cards (optional), you can activate 3G support. The tablet has a microphone, sound is output to the rear speakers, or there is support for the headset, including a wireless one. It is possible to record conversations.
