Sony will unite all services into a single network Sony Entertainment Network

Now Sony Corporation is going through hard times, but everything is slowly getting better. Hackers seem to have left the company alone, Sony gadgets are sold, and the management of the corporation is planning to merge the company's separate services into a single network. So far, these are only plans, but the plans are quite realistic, so it is unlikely that something will prevent the Japanese company from unifying its services. Sony has Qriocity Music Unlimited music service, PlayStation Network, Sony Music and others.
