CMS for photographers

This topic asked me to publish Anykeys , it does not have enough karma.

Good day.

I wanted to introduce you to another ready-made project, which I initially did for myself, but I can share it.

Some time after I started photographing, my idea came to posting photos either using some CMS on my site or on some popular website.

The only CMS that liked and met many requirements was PixelPost (pixelpost.org), but I wanted a different photo viewing structure.

On the existing sites there was what I wanted, but there was no desire to pay quite a lot of money for premium accounts. And I decided ...

Three days without sleep - and that's what I wanted. Admin is simple to disgrace, of course. There is a form for adding and editing albums and a form for uploading / editing photos and previews. Screenshot CMS .

The rest is pure HTML and CSS.

If someone is interested in the system: write in a personal Anykeys , in a reply letter I will send an archive with a script.
And I would like to hear suggestions about adding any functions to the script, while it is interesting for me to engage in its development.
