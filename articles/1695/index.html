Ukrainian mobile content market took a breather

In Ukraine, in the first six months of 2006, the growth of the content providers market decreased by 10% (compared to the second half of 2005). According to ComNews Research researchers, the volume of this market is $ 30 million. The top three have changed. Among the reasons for the slowdown in growth, analysts call the monotony of products, the lack of new services and piracy.

Nevertheless, Ukrainian experts predict growth for providers specializing in WAP projects. Media projects will also increase their share, they say. In addition, the development of the content market of Ukraine can be safely expected in the segment of Java games and Java applications. Predict sales growth in the segment of "exclusive" content.
