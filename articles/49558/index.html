So, dear habrasoobschestvo, you have the opportunity in the most direct sense to participate in the birth of a startup and help him ... or kill ... :) Nobody ever did this, so why not try it?

Input data:
Financing : my function
Budget : in the range of $ 450,000 to a confident exit to the growth stage.
Duration : 9 months
Idea : I propose, we all develop together
Management : collective intelligence. Postulate: we submit to the reasonable majority.
Execution : recruited command. Options are possible.
Purpose : to create a project that a) will be useful and demanded by users, b) will be financially successful.

Get down to business.

Popular and promising in RuNet : Vkontakte, Classmates, LJ, Habr, Yaroshka, Imhonet, Mamba, GoogleRider
What from this should take:

Circles - communication based on common characteristics (interests, age, region, place of study or work)
Blogs are content, their place on the Web, a tool for self-expression.
Collective content is another strong binding factor, a catalyst for development. (Groups, communities, community blogs?)
Ratings, voting - the opportunity to express their attitude to the phenomenon / fact / action / personality, social recommendations.
Media Galleries - the user should not be constrained by the ability to produce media content - pictures, video, audio.
The format is a platform . Not the site on which admins rule and their livelihoods are consumed by users, namely the platform. Where each user organizes and equips his own habitat.
Niche and subject - you should choose one unoccupied and promising niche. Or create a lot of narrow-topic projects. Considering the previous point, we decide: each user account is a full-fledged thematic project.
Syndication - the ability to both aggregate the necessary user content from external sources (rss, xml, what else?), And to give the outside in a convenient format.
Tags, Folksonomy - high-quality rubrication of materials is necessary as air. No admin can handle this better than the users themselves.
Open platform - API for developers.
User influence - evaluation of ideas, selection of functionality, formation of a project development strategy
Fashion - modern gadgets, without which the project will look like a product of the Soviet era. Ajax, drug & drop, drop-down tips, custom themes, RSS, compatibility with mobile devices, etc.

And all this needs to be combined into an organic and understandable service - something between a webOS and a social service.

Promotion strategy
a) media plans, budgets ... - traditional advertising
b) non-standard, viral advertising, hidden promotion - "Jeans"
c) dirty promotion - spam, buying user databases, etc.
d) Google's strategy - we do not give direct advertising, we make more of a good product in demand.

The third option, as I understand it, works effectively from the point of view of the project owner, but it causes a lot of negativity to users.
Morally ready for the first option, but I would love to go on the third fourth path. If the project builds the whole community - why not? Is it real?

Monetization strategy
1) paid services - unpromising in runet, poorly perceived by users.
2) advertising - contextual advertising based on all the knowledge about the user, his interests and actions - REALLY USEFUL, minimal annoyance, the obligatory observance of complete confidentiality.

I see a more promising second option.

- Preparation of technical documentation, drawing up a business plan.
- Search / collect team
- Technical implementation
- Run
- Promotion, continuous development
- Monetization and capitalization
