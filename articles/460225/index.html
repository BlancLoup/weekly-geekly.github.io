About the standing table, spinal health and personal effectiveness

First, why standing up to work is not a panacea ...


I am 39 years old, I am a programmer, my back problems started at 24. The diagnosis is protrusion of the intervertebral disk L4-L5 about 6-8 mm, there are also protrusions in other departments. Over these 15 years, I apparently became an expert in everything regarding efficiency for people with spinal problems: I tried a lot of money and developed habits that allow you to work 12-14 hours a day and stay away from osteopaths, surgeons and others. make incompatible changes in the design of my body.


In particular, I realized that standing up is not salvation. Standing vertebral muscles tense almost like sitting, just the spine experiences a slightly different load vector. Therefore, the key to long productive work is a regular change of body position.
