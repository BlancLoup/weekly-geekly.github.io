About the Diaspora project already wrote on Habr√© .

Briefly: the project authors are students, active users of facebook.com, who have drawn attention to the lack of security thereof and having decided to make a project that will be free from the flaws of facebook. It is worth paying attention to the fact that Mark Zuckerberg, the head and creator of facebook.com, provided a small financial assistance to the project, as well as the fact that the source code of Diaspora will be available on the network.
