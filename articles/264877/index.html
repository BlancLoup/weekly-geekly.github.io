Yii environment. Inheritance and redefinition of configs

I want to tell you about an interesting experience that I encountered at my last job. A flexible environment system was needed. After some time of experiments, I did achieve the ideal version. Let's get right to the point.

In protected, I created environment.php with these two classes:

environment.php

Same in cli.php. But in Production / Web.php so as not to constantly copy some parameter if you change it, I did this: 
 return CMap::mergeArray(include(PROTECTED_PATH.'config/Base/main.php'), [ 'components'=> [ 'db' => include(dirname(__FILE__) . '/DB/BaseConnect.php'), 'db1c' => include(dirname(__FILE__) . '/DB/1c.php') ] ] );

Thus, I just overwrite the settings of the database, or what I need. Now the fun part. Loading index.php.
