Representatives of companies that are engaged in the development of the Internet of things in Russia have made an official letter to the authors of the initiative. At present, the Association of Participants of the Internet of Things Market includes such operators as Rostelecom, MTS, ER-Telecom, MTT, etc.

The direct threat is that the project will increase the delay in the transmission of data packets for Internet of Things devices on core networks. First of all, we are talking about devices that are used in the systems of "smart cities", transport infrastructure and the industrial Internet.

The fact is that the bill points out the need to restrict access to prohibited resources by controlling the contents of the traffic with the help of special equipment on the networks of operators. ‚ÄúThis may lead to technical failures and degradation of the quality of services, including for IoT devices, which may negatively affect the projects of smart cities,‚Äù said MTS representative Alexei Merkutov.

Now the share of protocols that do not use traditional DNS servers is gradually increasing.
