Gaming destop Dell Studio XPS 435



Dell announced the upgrade of its gaming station Studio XPS 435. The main difference between the new version is the ability to expand the amount of RAM to 24 GB.

XPS 435 is a desktop, the main feature of which is a quick start - the operating system boots in seconds (which is not surprising with such a volume of brains). The computer is designed for hardcore gamers and in the top version is equipped with:

Processor: Core i7 Extreme 3.2GHz;
Video card: Radeon HD 4870 1GB;
Winchester: three disks totaling 4.5 terabytes.

The list of equipment also includes a Blu-ray drive, a Sound Blaster X-Fi audio card, and a module.

The XPS 435 model inherited from the XPS 430 a characteristic body shape with a notch in which you can put an external drive. It also displays three USB-out and audio ports.
