Steve Ballmer finally left Microsoft



Former Executive Director Steve Ballmer left the Microsoft board of directors. About this decision, he said in an open letter to Sate Nadella , the current head of the company.

Steve Ballmer writes that 6 months have passed since his retirement: enough time to think about life. He is proud to have worked for a company like Microsoft. There are many difficult tasks to be solved in the future, but Microsoft has all the possibilities for this.

It will not be easy to switch to a new business model when mobile and cloud services come to the fore. In the "new world" will have to earn money through corporate subscriptions, sales of equipment and advertising. At the same time, it is necessary to preserve the old business of selling software, which is a difficult management task, admits Ballmer.

Ballmer admits that within 6 months of his retirement he devoted not too much time to his own company. He now has a lot of worries, among them - the management of the Clippers basketball team, which he bought for $ 2 billion, social activities, teaching and self-study.
