Wefi - Wi-Fi hotspots around the world

Good day. I want to attract the attention of habra people to one interesting service - Wefi . This is a symbiosis of free software for laptops, netbooks, PDAs, smartphones and a portal with a world map of Wi-Fi access points. The program automatically finds, tests and sets the rating of each of them, and connects you to the one with which the connection will be the best. If WeFi finds a new access point, it will automatically add it to the map. Information on the location of more than 40 million hotspots on planet Earth is available today.
