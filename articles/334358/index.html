First, a few words about certification itself. As Wikipedia says, the system originated in Scotland (Edinburgh) in 2002. The purpose of the appearance is to standardize the knowledge and skills of testers. Now the certificate is recognized in 56 countries .

The system is three-level, the readout comes from below - for clarity, I will give a picture.


In Russia, exams 1 and 2 levels are available for passing. The cost of a single exam is 150 euros, while the level 2 examinations consist of several stages of 150 euros each. In this case, you can pass in Russian only the very first exam of the basic level, which we will talk about later.

Directly exam


The books are notched, the test exam is decided by 95-100%, the candidate is ready for battle - what's next?
To begin, register for the exam in the date of your interest in the desired city. I strongly recommend doing this directly in GASQ, since The link from the RSTQB website (the official representative of certification in the Russian Federation) still leads to the form of GASQ. During registration, it is better not to indicate anything in the ‚ÄúCompany‚Äù field - otherwise, by default you will be considered an employee and will be paid by invoice in a bank. We leave the field empty, the payment method, select "Credit card". About the fact of payment will be slightly lower.

After registration, you will write a charming (hopefully) girl from GASQ confirming your reservation and will warn you that there will be a separate letter if the exam is confirmed on the specified date. Let me dwell here in more detail: yes, registration for a specific date does not mean that the exam will be 100% - there may be a shortage or other force majeure circumstances. In this case, there will be a transfer of the exam to the nearest date, of which you will be notified by letter. Further, 8-10 days before the exam, your chosen date disappears from the registration lists - this only means that registration was closed (again, if you postponed registration for the last moment and ‚Äúflew‚Äù, you still have a chance - my colleague late in registering through the form, I resolved the issue by writing to the GASQ contact address).

The confirmation letter usually arrives 6-7 days before the exam. It shows the date, time and address of the exam. Usually it is either an office of an IT company or an office of a training center.

Why haven‚Äôt I touched my exam fee yet? The fact is that the invoice comes to you exactly 24 hours before the exam. If you choose a payment method, the card will have two letters - an invoice in the form of a pdf document and a separate link to the card payment form (I have a letter with payment url dropped into spam , so keep this in mind). Here, if you accidentally registered as a company representative and understand that 24 hours is not enough to pay through a bank branch, you can ask for a re-invoice for payment by card - the girls in GASQ are responsive and prompt.

Finally, all organizational and transportation questions are closed, materials are repeated, tests are resolved and you are standing in front of the door to the office. As I mentioned above, the exam is taken on a computer. Since we chose to pass the exam in our native language, its duration will be 60 minutes (for a non-native language, an additional time is given). The administrator will check your name on the lists (you need a passport or driver's license with you) and will issue a sheet with a login / password. Login, agree with the license agreement, enter the data (preferably correct, they will then be printed on the certificate). Now you can proceed, the benefit of the interface you have already studied in detail during the preparation.

During the exam, you can not use anything. The permissible maximum is to solve problems on a blank sheet of paper // sheet with a login / password.
