How much does it cost to enter the top cash categories of the app store and is there any point?

How much does it cost to enter the top cash register in a certain category? This question tormented me for a long time. It was always interesting to know: how much do other games earn? If you are also plagued by such questions, then welcome under the cut - there will be some text, no water - just on business



I have a simple quiz game, which is listed in two categories of the Russian app store: "Quiz" and "Puzzle". The game was launched for about six months, in August, I made a change of the icon, the name and perevylozhil this miracle. Recently, I decided to test the performance of my store and made a test purchase for 29 rubles. After a while, I was greatly surprised to find that the application had risen to 34th place in the ranking of top-grossing games of the Quiz category.

It became interesting and I decided to try to find answers to two questions:

  1. How much should you earn to enter the top cash register of your category?
  2. How many organic installations will be in the top cash register in the category "Quizzes"?

1. How much should I earn to enter the Top Box Office of my category?


I decided to conduct a purely scientific experiment - make purchases by myself and see where I can get without significant losses for my personal budget. There is no exact algorithm for calculating top cash registers in the public domain, there are suggestions (for example, here ) that it is built on the basis of sales for several days, taking into account weights. Before the experiment, I was not present in the top cash register, all purchases were made in the Russian app store.
