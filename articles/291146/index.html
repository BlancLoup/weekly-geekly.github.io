Free Holi Day advertisements for Megamozg members

Hi, Megamind! It so happened that we are organizing the Holi Day Holi Day music festival of colors, which will be held on June 12 at the Otkritie Arena stadium in Moscow, near the Spartak metro station. 80,000 people will gather at the festival, and we offer projects from Megamind for free to be promoted there.



How paints are related to Rocket Callback


The fact is that even before we created Rocket Callback , we were already engaged in two major projects - we had a company selling paints and holi and a company producing sweatshirts with a print of universities Univer Club . Rocket Callback was the third project in this chain. This is how we deal with several businesses from different areas in parallel: paint, sweatshirts and IT widget. Our partners are still with us - digital workshop Appatbox .

Previous years we only supplied paint to the festival: in 2013, it was 14 festivals, 10 cities, 8 tons. In 2014, we delivered 58 tons of colors, 87 festivals, 69 cities.

This year we decided to go further and plan to deliver 160 tons of paint for 200 festivals in 600 cities. But the most important difference is that this year we decided not to limit ourselves with delivery, but to hold the holi music festival by ourselves.

What is going to be there


The festival will take place on June 12 in Moscow at the Otkritie Arena stadium, Spartak metro station. The audience is about 80,000 people. There will be 20 tons of paint, 29 big salvos. All the day 18 Russian DJs will perform. Teleconference and 10 joint volleys with other cities of the country.

As organizers, we offer projects with Megamozg to be promoted for free at the festival. Formats - stand, flyers, promo, branding. Since there will be mostly young people aged 18-25 years, we can hold contests with gifts, which we personally think is most effective. If you have ideas on how to use such a crowd for the benefit of your project, then you are ready to listen to them.

You can not only promote alcohol, energy and cigarettes, so IT-projects - welcome. We do this because we know for ourselves how difficult it is sometimes for an IT project to declare itself offline, and therefore we are ready to help all those who are suitable for the audience who will be at the festival. There is also the possibility of advertising and sponsorship, which include a wider choice of sites and opportunities, we can send the price list by mail.

For all questions, including paid advertising and sponsorship, you can contact me at mail valeriya@rocketcallback.com.

Head of Rocket Callback, Valeria Makarova.
