Someone made a $ 160 million bitcoin transaction

A bitcoin address with a history of large transactions last night made another transfer to a record amount of 194,993 BTC , which is just over $ 160 million in equivalent at the current rate.

Tellingly, the owner of this amount paid 0 BTC transfer fees. Just imagine what the commission would be at an international transfer of $ 160 million through the banking system.



Today it is the largest transaction in the Bitcoin network.
