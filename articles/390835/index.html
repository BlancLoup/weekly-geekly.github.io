Opera Presto updated to version 12.18

Many fans of Opera browser were depressed by the change of the engine from Presto to Blink in 2013. The loss of a huge amount of functionality that made Opera Presto a unique browser, a much greater gluttony of RAM in the Blink version - these are two main reasons for the resentment of old users.



The last update Opera Presto version 12.17 was released in 2014 and was like the last. Nothing foreshadowed, especially against the background of the news about the company's purchase by the Chinese , and now, suddenly, our favorite browser is being updated to version 12.18 .

Of the changes can be noted:
  • ECC and GCM encryption support added
  • A set of ciphers, as in other modern browsers
  • TLS RC4 encryption algorithm disabled
  • TLS 1.2 support is enabled by default.


Also, the developers once again urge to upgrade to the latest version of Opera Blink, because with the support of new technologies it is somewhat better than in the lost (unfortunately) full support of the Opera Presto developers.
