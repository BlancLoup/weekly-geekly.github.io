New Android 5.1

Over the air has become available for updating Android 5.1. What is new in it? Maybe they just fixed bugs?




Of course, especially no fundamentally visible functional was added, but there were some useful features.

1. Ring volume


Now while listening to music, you can change the volume of the notification signal. To do this, click on the bell icon.




2. Work with quick settings


The main emphasis in the changes of the visible functionality was made to work with the "curtain".
First, it is now possible to work with quick settings, even when the phone is not unlocked (and there is a password)


Secondly, it is possible to work with settings, for example, wifi right away in quick settings.


Third, some of the quick settings labels can now be hidden. To do this, click on the tab and hold the pen for seconds.


3. Notifications
