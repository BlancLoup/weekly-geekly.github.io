Not all breakage - mutations


It is important to distinguish between DNA damage and mutagenesis. If you imagine cells as small plants, factories or companies, the core in which the DNA is stored is the head office of the enterprise. From time to time, on the eve of some large-scale events, there is a rush job, but one way or another everything falls into place: everyone follows one another and in case of that they help to correct mistakes. In this case, it is a replication error. The mutation, on the contrary, is a change in DNA, which for some reason was not corrected.
