Apple may release Macbook Pro with 2880 x 1800 display



According to DigiTimes, based on data from suppliers of components, it is possible that Apple will release new MacBook Pro notebooks in the second quarter of 2012, the screen resolution of which will be 2880 x 1800 pixels. To date, this parameter is typical for 30-inch monitors.

Retina displays were introduced for the X Lion system earlier this year, they can support resolutions several times larger than they are now - MacBook Pro laptops have screens with resolutions from 1280 x 800 to 1920 x 1200 pixels. The release of new ultra-high-resolution products will allow Apple to emphasize the MacBook Pro more strongly against other brands.



It is emphasized that the cost of new items is unlikely to increase. Now models are priced from $ 1200 to $ 2500. The announcement of the first representatives of the Macbook Pro series with a display of 2880 x 1800 will be held in the second quarter of next year. By this time, ASUS and Acer are planning to introduce laptops with LCD panels of 1920 x 1080 pixels.
