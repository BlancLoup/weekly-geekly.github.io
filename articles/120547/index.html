RentFilter.ru - rent an apartment without intermediaries

Do not want to pay real estate agents a commission?



The new project www.rentfilter.ru confirms: to rent an apartment without the help of realtors is real! Yes, going to any site with ads for renting apartments, you will have to call more than a dozen of the numbers before you find the ad really from the apartment owner, and not from a real estate agency. But this can happen only if you are very lucky.

RentFilter.ru will help to rent an apartment without intermediaries. Our robots filter the rent of premises around the clock and leave only those that are served by the apartment owners themselves. Just call the phone number listed in the ad, look at the apartment, study all the documents on the property, sign the contract and pack your bags. By the way, on RentFilter.ru you can read everything you need to know if you decide to rent an apartment without agent assistance. Here you can download a standard rental agreement for residential premises.

Now RentFilter is working in test mode: there are private ads in open access only from residents of St. Petersburg. In the near future - to make a similar database for the rest of the Russian cities.



You can follow updates and news on our official pages on social networks: Facebook , Vkontakte , Twitter .

In addition, we provide an API for developers. Now he is in closed testing. If you have an ad site, we will help you improve the quality of ad filtering. For cooperation, contact hello@rentfilter.ru .
