Kaspersky for half price and even cheaper

Initial data:
Need KIS 7.0. Hacking methods disappear. The price in Russia is 1600 rubles per year. You can buy a "license renewal", but it still costs 960 rubles, in addition, the lack of a terminating license, which could be extended, somewhat complicates the task.

We are looking, we think. It turns out that Kaspersky Lab has different prices for different countries, all products are sold in local currencies. Very curious, we find and compare the cost of a KIS license in different countries ... Here, what you need, in the USA it costs less rubles in terms of rubles than in Russia. Already good. But wait, in the UK (http://www.kaspersky.co.uk/store) until the end of March, a 50% discount on everything! Cheers, comrades.

Now on the payment page (a bank card is needed, of course) we notice a field for entering a coupon number. What makes a Russian person who does not have a coupon number? Right, looking for the number of this coupon. For some time, a search in the network gives the result ;-) An additional discount on the coupon is another 50%. Now the prices are normal!

Total: 440 rubles.

Anyone who is interested in a successful purchase.
