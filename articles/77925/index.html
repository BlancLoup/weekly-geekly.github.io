Iferta online shopping mall opened in Runet

Web Systems LLC, which owns the WebCreds payment system, launched a new project, the online mall (shopping center) iferta , in which the products of various online stores are presented.

iferta offers an affiliate program for stores. A lot of money is invested in advertising the site and attracting customers.
At the same time, the shops pay only a commission from a really perfect transaction - no subscription fee or payment for going to the store's website. The size of the commission ranges from 2.5% to 15% depending on the price of a particular product, and is retained at the end of the month after the approval of sales reports.

Besides the fact that the Internet mall is a profitable business for its owner, the company also achieves a synergy effect with it, indirectly developing its own payment system.
