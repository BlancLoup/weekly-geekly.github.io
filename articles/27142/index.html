Cronvix: Friendly Blog Service

Recently it was announced the launch of a new blog service "Cronvix" . According to the creators, during its development all weaknesses of other blogging services were taken into account, in particular LJ, Liveinternet and mail. The main goal was to make the most friendly service, in which even an inexperienced user can figure out a few minutes.

The main functionality of the site:
  • Each user's own file archive;
  • the ability to create access groups;
  • the ability to install paid access to certain posts and archives;
  • RSS, crossposting;
  • "Designer" of the left menu;

One of the ‚Äúchips‚Äù of the service is an interesting monetization scheme that almost every blogger can use. Any user can set a price for accessing a diary entry, video, photo, any other file or folder. This opportunity can be useful to freelancers and producers of any content. Or, say, if a user has an exclusive, he can sell it. And no problem to withdraw the money earned.


Among the shortcomings can be noted the lack of communities and the inability to change the design of diaries. The creators of Cronvix argue that the implementation of both points is a matter of the near future. It should also be noted that while the project is much less diaries than on LJ or LI. However, it is difficult to say clearly the dignity or disadvantage On the one hand, it seems to be unprofitable from the point of view of recruiting friends, but on the other, by getting a diary, or at least making crossposting in Cronvix now, after a while you can become a real veteran of the project.
