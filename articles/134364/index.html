In comparison with the matte ASUS P52.

Performance





ASUS UX31E is a very powerful device, thanks to the Intel Core i7-2677M processor (basic frequency: 1.8 GHz, in Turbo Boost mode: up to 2.9 GHz).

Integrated Intel HD 3000 video core. In the 3Dmark 06 test, the model scored 3755 points.



The storage in the laptop - SSD-drive with a capacity of 256 GB.



The amount of non-expandable RAM is 4 GB DDR3.

It is possible to estimate the overall performance only in the aggregate of all technical characteristics. ZENBOOK is a high-performance and fast device that displays results previously available only to stationary computers. The laptop boots up in 16 seconds, and it wakes up from sleep in literally 2 seconds thanks to InstantOn technology. All programs are installed in a few seconds, the simultaneous launch of multiple applications does not kill the system, does not force Windows "thinks".

InstantOn technology provides the ability to find a laptop in sleep mode up to 14 days on one charge. In addition, in sleep mode, when the charge level drops to 5%, the laptop automatically saves the contents of the RAM to disk, preventing data loss when completely disconnected. InstantOn is the next generation of standby mode, combining the advantages of existing modes, and devoid of their shortcomings.



The system simply runs the program and works, as if nothing had happened. However, this is primarily the merit of the ADATA SSD disk, may the Lord keep it for many years. The drive is one of the fastest today (reading speed of over 300 Mb / s), so the user can not worry about system performance in the next few years.
