What else can you type?

At the moment, to find an explanatory description - who and why it is needed is almost impossible.
As my first post (hopefully not the last), I will give an example of the TOP 5 models, on my own version, printed on three-dimensional printers.

The main part of the list is taken from the post of the official blog of the company Stratasys Ltd, (in the past Objet . The two companies merged into one.) But this is not the point. The purpose of the post to show the possibilities of three-dimensional printing.

5th place. Printed corset.



In May of this year, at one of the prestigious art exhibitions in the Center Georges Pompidou in Paris, a whole collection of various works printed on 3D printers was presented.
Video report can be viewed here .

Idea and execution: Professor Neri Oksman. Israel


4th place. Perhaps the smallest typewriter


STL file of this model we received from David San, head of the engineering department of IRIS International Inc. In the letter, he offered to break his record: print out this machine less than 3 cm in length.



The most important condition was to preserve the functionality of the doors (they were supposed to open) and the wheels (on the chassis and should turn)

The call was accepted and we printed a 1.5 cm model.
The wheels are spinning, the doors on the axles with a diameter of 0.9mm open. The thickness of the hull walls is 0.14mm !!!

Our engineers themselves were slightly shocked.

3rd place. Functioning adjustable wrench.



In contrast to the smallest typewriter, we printed the largest adjustable wrench.


Its length is 120cm.
