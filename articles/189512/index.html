Handball Powerball simulator - as a means to prevent tunnel syndrome

Nowadays, a great number of people, if they do not yet have, are subject to the so-called carpal tunnel syndrome. The risk group includes people of various professions and hobbies, for example, drivers, artists, photographers and, of course, people who work at computers a lot, they are also the most susceptible to this ailment. In the advanced stage of CTS (Carpal Tunnel Syndrome), inpatient treatment may be required, up to surgical intervention (special surgery to extract excess fluid from the joints). Well, the best way to solve a problem is never to face it. To do this, you need to engage in prevention through warm-up or specialized devices, such as a gyroscopic simulator.

Today I will talk about 3 models of Powerball - Classic, Fusion and Autostart. This is one of the most popular models at the moment. They can also cover the entire line of wrist simulators, with the exception of metal devices. Read more under the cat.

‚ñ∫ Working principle and gut guts

The principle of operation of the subject is quite simple. The device is based on a gyroscope consisting of a rapidly rotating rotor enclosed in a plastic sphere. To set the rotor in motion, you can use a special rope that comes in the kit, then you need to make circular movements with your hand to increase the rotor speed. The simulator during use must be firmly held in your hand, as the forces of action will try to deflect it in different directions.

During the application of a constant external force to the gyroscope, it begins to rotate around a certain axis that does not coincide in direction with the main axis of the rotating rotor, i.e., precess. In this case, the rotation does not occur in accordance with the direction of the external force. The magnitude of the precession is proportional to the magnitude of the acting force. In the case of termination of external influence, the precession instantly ends, but the rotor continues to rotate.

As soon as the rotor is started, tilting the device will cause one end of the axis to move along the upper side of the groove, and the other end along the lower side. When the axis of the rotating rotor comes into contact with the upper and lower surfaces of the groove, this causes a precession and the axis of the rotor will start a circular movement along it. The friction force between the axis and the surface of the groove can either accelerate or slow down the rotation of the gyroscope. The greatest acceleration is achieved when the rotor axis begins to ‚Äúslide‚Äù along the surface of the groove as smoothly as possible. Since the friction force is very important for this effect, the device should not be lubricated. The maximum speed of rotation of the rotor is achieved while holding the sphere in the hand and constantly maintaining the rotation by the movement of the brush.

‚ñ∫ Quick start

Running the simulator is extremely simple. All that needs to be done is to insert the rope coming in the kit into the groove on the rotor, then pull it out with a sharp movement, thereby giving rotation to the rotor. Next, simply unwind the Powerball with slow circular motions with the hand (with a brush). The main thing is not to start with quick short movements with your wrist, otherwise you will not be able to catch the rhythm, and it will be difficult to unwind the ball correctly. ‚ñ∫ In use

Carpal tunnel syndrome is a lesion of nerve tissue (neuropathy) due to some adverse conditions. Adverse conditions can be very different - usually it is the monotonous work of the brush or a long stay in unnatural positions. Habrahabr has repeatedly discussed this topic. I strongly recommend that you familiarize yourself with the works of my colleague vvzvlad and stabor habrauser that in the first one , that in the second one , the disease was dismantled, as well as methods for its prevention.

In general, the scope of Powerball applications is quite extensive and is not limited to CTS prevention alone. It can also be used to repair damaged ligaments and tendons after injuries to hands, sprains and fractures. In such cases, it is recommended to rotate the Powerball literally several minutes a day, but in such a way that its built-in rotor works at a limited speed, that is, no more than 5-6 thousand revolutions per minute.

But this is not the end of the application. "Balls" equipped with counters enable people to compete in "who will wind up more." Thus, the obligatory warm-up becomes also fascinating. Moreover, the presence of an opponent near is not required. On the official site of NSD (manufacturer of Powerball) there is a special table in which records are made from all over the world (representatives from the CIS countries are also present there).

Now as for the modes of operation of the counter. There are 5 of them here. Switching is performed using the FCN button, and switching on and zeroing via ON / CLR. In the first mode, the number of rotor revolutions that he performed from the moment of the last start to the moment of stopping is considered.

The second mode is responsible for measuring the speed (RPM per minute) in real time.

The third and most frequently used mode is to display the maximum RPM (High score), i.e. This setting captures your record and displays it until you dial a larger score or reset it using the ON / CLR button. As soon as you start to rotate the simulator, the counter automatically switches to the fourth mode (New high score) - the counter shows the highest rotational speed reached since the last start. If you have exceeded your previous record since the last start, the number on the counter will flash, and the new record will be recorded in memory as soon as the rotor rotation stops. If your current result is less than a record, it will be displayed on the screen until the rotor stops, after which the High score mode will turn on again.

And, finally, the last installation is the measurement of the force index, its essence in calculating the number of revolutions made over a certain period of time (30, 60 and 90 seconds). A great way to measure your strength (endurance?) With a friend.

The display turns itself off after 40 seconds of inactivity.
