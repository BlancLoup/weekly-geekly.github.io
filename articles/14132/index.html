User Experience Russia 2007

On November 8, 9 and 10 in Moscow, the Radisson SAS Slavyanskaya Hotel will host the first annual User Experience Russia 2007 conference, timed to the World Usability Day.

User Experience 2007 combines all the technologies and areas designed to bring computer development to the user.

The following sections will work at the conference:
* E-government and accessibility
* Mobile Experience
* Intranet
* Usability methodology
* Usability on the Internet

and will star usability

* Maria Stone (Google)
* Eric Schafer (Human Factors International)
* Susan Fowler (Fast-Consulting)
* Shailesh Manga (Optimal Usability)

For details, see the conference website: userexp.ru
