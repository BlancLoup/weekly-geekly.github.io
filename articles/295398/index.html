The first marketing activity in history. In the history of Ridewithme, of course

In this article, we look at the effectiveness of social media marketing, and a more adult version is the distribution of press releases.

The first was decided to use Vkontakte advertising in two ways:

- Advertising of the application in the news feed, this type of advertising appeared relatively recently, there were not many reports about its effectiveness, and we decided, as they say, to try it out for ourselves.
- Advertising on the stock exchange advertising. The essence of this channel is that communities put their advertising space and set a price.

To test the advertising market, a budget was allocated for a test launch in the news feed, and in groups. 2/3 of the budget - allocated for targeting (advertising in the tape) and 1/3 for advertising in communities.

Next we bring to your attention the graphs of the use of advertising in the feed (Graph 1) and in the communities (Graphs 2)
Schedule 1. Advertising in the tape.

On the graph: 171 transition advertising. We received about 5 downloads in the same period.

Charts 2. Advertising in communities.

On the charts: The total number of transitions was 67, while downloads during the promotional period exceeded 30.

Conclusion: The advertisement in the tape did not justify itself and the funds invested in it, the advertisement in the groups showed more impressive results, considering the coefficient went-downloaded, we get 5/171 = 0.0292 and in groups 30/67 = 0.44. For our application, advertising in communities turned out to be much more efficient than advertising in the news feed.
