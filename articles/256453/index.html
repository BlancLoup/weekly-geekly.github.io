City laboratory of data analysis: we assemble a team

The Department of Information Technologies will have the first specialized unit in Russia, which will be engaged in the analysis of big data in healthcare.


Laboratory analysis of data will predict a surge in incidence, compare the effectiveness of various methods of treatment, to identify unexpected effects from the use of drugs on large samples, identify risk groups, find bottlenecks in the urban health care system, suggest new areas of automation and much more.

The touchstone was the creation of the Situation Center of the Unified Medical Information System, which provides visualization of problems with the availability of medical care in polyclinics.


* Red marked problem clinics, where the waiting period of the desired doctor is higher than the standard. Clinic managers can view the statistics in all sections and reconfigure the schedule of doctors or enable the "roaming" of patients between branches.

It's not too late to be part of the team!
The best of the best are selected on the project website lab.emias.info until May 11 .

What do you need?


Good technical or economic education, team spirit, outstanding analytical skills, the desire to create history in big data.

What to do?


Turn information into value. More specifically, then:

  • analyze large amounts of industry data (search for patterns and points of increase in efficiency);
  • predict and build data models based on the found patterns;
  • look for data errors and ways to optimize their structure;
  • work side by side with business analysts and system analysts on the issues of uploading data and setting new tasks for automating processes in the health care system.


Laboratory staff will be divided into two competing teams, each of which will work experienced mentors. The experiment will last a year, in case of its success, the developments will be extended to other industries.
