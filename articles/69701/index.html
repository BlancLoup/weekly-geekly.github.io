Sales of music in RuNet fall for the third year

For the third year after the closure of the famous Allofmp3.com site, sales of music in RuNet continue to fall, Vedomosti reports, citing data from the research company iKS-Consulting.

The record year was 2006, when Runet‚Äôs online stores sold music files for $ 9 million, and with Allofmp3.com accounting for $ 30 million. Since then, the market has been falling at a catastrophic pace: in 2007, sales were $ 7.5 million , and in 2008 - only $ 3 million.
