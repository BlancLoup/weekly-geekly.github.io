The state will limit the use of foreign electronics

Russian electronics is several times more expensive than imported ones, so you have to ban car terminals on foreign chips

It was discussed how the domestic industry is ready to produce ERA-GLONASS terminals and other equipment on its own element base.

Experts estimated that at the moment it is possible to localize the production of terminals at 30%, and for the import substitution of the remaining 70%, it will take from one to three years, subject to receiving investments in the following amount:

  • Experimental design work on the creation of a Russian GSM modem - 150 million rubles, the release of the product in 2018
  • Accelerometer - 100 million rubles, release in 2018
