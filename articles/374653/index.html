Preliminary version of Microsoft Office for Windows 10

The development team announced in their blog that they have released a technical preview of the next version of Microsoft Office. These will be universal applications that can be run on a computer, tablet and even a Windows 10 phone.

For now, only those who have registered in the beta program and downloaded the Windows 10 Technical Preview for themselves can test Office for Windows 10 in action. Soon promise to release and version for phones.

The new office suite will be pre-installed on phones and tablets. On other platforms, it will be available for download through the Windows Store.

Word for Windows 10



The first thing that catches your eye is a super-compact toolbar. It is not clear how deeply you will have to climb behind the usual functions, but it looks very clean and pleasant.

Of the innovations - a certain feature called Office Insights . Apparently, this is a contextual search for any definitions in the mode of reading the document. It works, of course, through Bing.

Excel for Windows 10



They promise a super-convenient control using the touchscreen that "you will forget about your mouse and keyboard." It sounds ambitious, but I want to believe!

Powerpoint for Windows 10



On the slides, you can make handwritten notes during the presentation of the presentation (apparently, just like in the Spartan browser).

OneNote for Windows 10



Again, handwritten notes. If we are to introduce, then everywhere!
