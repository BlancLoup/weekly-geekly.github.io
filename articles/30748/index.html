What are the tools for teamwork other than Exchange?

As a rule, for an average organization, the question of organizing interaction between employees arises when the number of employees exceeds 2. Typical tasks are to send mail, exchange contacts, set and monitor a task, schedule a meeting. I will try to highlight this topic on the example of the thorny path of our small organization.


Post office.

Here, Google hosted mail is out of competition. At least 50 accounts will be available for your domain name, a huge mailbox size, access via pop3, imap. The ability to access mail from mobile devices.

Unfortunately google hosted does not have the means to work with tasks, and it seems that it does not yet strive to have.
