Pocket has updated the web version and attracted $ 7 million investment

Startup Pocket , which provides a tool to save web pages for deferred reading, today introduced an updated version of the web client, making the website adaptive for all permissions.

In addition to updating the design and interface, Pocket announced the successful closure of the next round of investments of $ 7 million. Among the investors: New Enterprise Associates and Sound Ventures, founded by Ashton Kutcher and Guy Oshiri. The total investment in Pocket is $ 14.5 million.

The company will spend most of the money on hiring new employees, as it is difficult for it to maintain the existing infrastructure, having only 15 people on staff.

The number of registered Pocket users is now 17 million. More than 2 million links are saved per day using the service.
