Lomogram Windows Phone Statistics

On the Internet it is rare to find the latest statistics on the use of mobile applications, especially on Windows Phone. I want to correct this situation a bit by sharing statistics compiled on the basis of the Lomogram application for Windows Phone and the Flurry service. Let me remind you that Lomogram became a finalist in the international competition Windows Phone Next App Star, second only to the winner - Wikipedia, and is also one of the top applications in the photo category.

On 12/05/2013 at Lomogram:

  • sessions (total): 27 918 229;
  • sessions (per day): 180,000 - 200,000;
  • average time of use: 44.2 s;
  • Users: 5,146,884;
  • average number of active users: 452 621;
  • downloads: 1,817,131.
