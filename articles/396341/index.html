Where to get soldering stations and other stuff


The equipment must be at least 200 000 - 300 000 rubles. Although the minimum set (without bga soldering) can reach 70 thousand. It is clear that at the beginning of the experience no one invested in serious tools, since the professional level is still low. Boards are soldered on the knee, the desktop is combined simultaneously with computer and dining, and so on. But if you seriously go to the customers, and not just repair the htc-patches of friends once a month, you need good equipment and a convenient workplace , and this (in descending order of importance):

  • Multimeter
  • Oscilloscope
  • ESR Tester (desirable but not necessary)
  • Laboratory power supply
  • Actually soldering stations: to start a soldering iron + thermoair soldering station, and later (if you want to solder bga) - infrared
  • POST cards
  • Programmer and a set of "beds" under the chip
  • Fluxes, solder paste, solders, board cleaners (liquid) and other useful chemistry
  • Different sets of hand tools
  • Vice
  • Drill (it is desirable for case works, also allows to cut interlayer short circuit on the board)
  • Good lighting
  • Hood in order not to breathe harmful evaporation of fluxes and solders (airing during soldering at the infrared station is prohibited, since the thermal profile may not work out as it should)
  • Comfortable armchair
  • Microscope
  • Storage systems
  • Large table and a lot of shelves. The table can be hung on chains so that the table legs do not interfere and there is free space: cleaning is so simplified and storage systems can be placed.
  • Have a lot of training circuits, do not experiment on the client. You can buy 500 p. Avito faulty, diagnose and pick (for the test you need at least several different processors)
  • BGA Wizard Set: special vice, a set of different stencils for reballing, solder paste and lead balls of different diameters, a spatula, an eraser, a brush.

Most of the infrared soldering stations can be easily bought second-hand on Avito, but before buying you need to thoroughly examine the issue on the Internet. Buying the rest of the staff better in well-established stores such as Chip and Dip , Profi (Siriust). As well as in the technology markets (in St. Petersburg this is the famous Juno) and on Aliexpress (Except for the chips! A lot of remarks and reballs). It is better to take chips on partsdirect.ru and for Peter - chipbuk.ru .

Parting words from the hero of the article


The most difficult repairs come after other services with incompetent craftsmen. For example, I brought a man a fee, I wanted to sell for 500 rubles. after another service (a new one costs 12000 rubles). They wanted to change the video chip, but according to the client, "we did not succeed." The motherboard was literally twisted: the lower heating was used too small and the thermal profile was not followed, it was not wrapped in foil and the cargo was not attached (a mandatory measure for infrared stations with a small lower heating). He offered the client to try to repair.

He warmed up to 300 evenly on a large thermal bench, applied loads in places of deformations, held for 10 minutes and allowed to cool. Straightened up! Soldered the video chip (the motherboard was brought without a video chip) - it started up, but there was no initialization. He drew attention to the north bridge - there are no several capacitors on it, he soldered - without changes, they and his eels seem to be. Put the chip from the donor - zakolosilos. For repairs took 7000 p. (3000 chip and 2000 donor, from which he removed the north; such a new north costs 3200). For the work itself, he took it purely symbolically - it was interesting to raise the mother from the next world and raise his reputation.
