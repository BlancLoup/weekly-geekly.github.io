Humble Weekly Sale: Bastion



Now the Humble Bundle will delight us not only with the sudden sales of entire packs of games, but also with weekly promotions dedicated to any one. For the first time, Bastion ( wiki ), a wonderful indie game that captures a bunch of awards at various festivals, falls under the hand. It is distinguished by hand-drawn graphics, game- like gameplay, an interesting plot, according to which we have to figure out the causes of the Holocaust that destroyed the world, and the pleasant voice of the narrator accompanying us throughout the game. If you have already played it - this is a great way to support developers and at the same time get some additional bonuses. If not, then you can buy the game until next Tuesday, which I, by the way, advise you wholeheartedly. Such wonderful in all respects, indie games now rarely meet.



Under the cut - a brief summary of the additional materials that go along with the game

For $ 1 you will be given a Steam and DRM-free version of the game (for Win, Mac and Linux); for the price above the average, you will also receive a soundtrack (with two new bonus tracks), unpublished artworks for the game, notes of game music and ringtones on the phone; Well, for $ 25 they promise to send a red bandanna with gaming symbols, a soundtrack disc (beautifully designed) and two postcards: one dedicated to Bastion, and the second to Transistor, the future project of the Supergiant Games team.
