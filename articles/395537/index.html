Epson Label Works - what and why


Actually, Epson LabelWorks is a ribbon thermal transfer printers designed for labeling (label printing).


Options for their use - an infinite number:

  • in the office: for inventory, labeling folders, equipment, trays, cables, etc .;
  • in small business: marking of goods in stock, printing bar codes and color price tags;
  • in production: marking of parts, ensuring safety requirements (emergency exits, warning labels);
  • Well, at home, of course: marking products, clothes, tools, all kinds of computer hardware, wires, etc.

The principle of operation is as follows: the cartridge consists of 2 tapes: ink and substrate (woven, adhesive, reflective, luminous, etc.). When the printing head is heated, the image is transferred from the ink ribbon to the substrate under the influence of temperature, thus the inscription is ‚Äúsoldered‚Äù into the ribbon. The highlight of this solution is that in addition to one such cartridge, no additional consumables are required. There is also no need to worry about ‚Äúrefilling‚Äù: change the cartridge and label it on health!
