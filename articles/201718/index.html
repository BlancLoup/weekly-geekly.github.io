Welcome to the World Usability Day 2013



This Saturday, November 16, the new Mail.Ru Group office will host World Usability Day 2013, one of the main events of the year for designers and interface designers. The conference will discuss current topics of creating digital products - web applications and websites, mobile and tablet services, desktop software and games.

The program of this year includes several thematic sections at once:

  • Great Hall , which will talk about the state, media, data presentation and legal aspects of work, the future of mail and rethinking color perception. Presenter - Sergey Kudryashov.
  • The world of games , where industry experts will answer questions on computer gaming interfaces, interaction systems, and other theoretical and practical issues. Moderator - Anton Artemov.
  • Lean UX - question-answer section, where you can talk about professional issues, how to "do well"; see if the Lean UX is more than a buzzword and learn a good selection of evidence. Presenter - Ivan Mikhailov.
  • UX-Environment - the 20th open interface micro-conference.
