Evaluation and analysis of nitrates is carried out by measuring the conductivity of high-frequency alternating current. At once I will make a reservation that this method of measurement has its opponents. It turns out that the device measures the electrical conductivity of salts dissolved in the product, which means not only nitrates, but also magnesium, calcium, potassium, etc. Also, there are complaints that the device allegedly greatly underestimates the testimony that its error is not 30% at all, but 100% and more, etc. But all this can not be checked at home, so here everyone decides for himself what to believe and what not.

Already upon reading the instruction, it is regrettable that a calculator is not built into the device. The nitrate tester measures the nitrate content per kilogram of product mass. Everything. To find out how much nitrate was eaten with this or that product (5 mg of nitrates per day per 1 kg of body weight is considered safe for an adult), additional calculations should be made. For children, of course, the norms are different, and here the tester pleased with a separate menu item ‚ÄúChildren's norm‚Äù.
