Are you ready for a mobile revolution?

Odessa, the city of sun and sand, in the first half of August will turn into a mobile mecca - this is where a series of events for mobile developers and startups will take place on August 8-10.

August 8 - Mobile Developer Day


Mobile Developer Day - a developer day for mobile platforms, which is held once a year in Ukraine, Russia and Belarus. This is a business part in the form of a conference for those whose business is related to the development of mobile applications, special seminars for programmers on development technologies.

More details: http://ua.mdday.ru/

August 9 - training "Deep immersion in the development and promotion of Windows Phone applications"


DevRain Solutions - Alexander Krakovetsky (Microsoft Windows Phone Most Valuable Professional) and Mikhail Galushko (Microsoft ASP.NET/IIS Most Valuable Professional) will conduct the training. The training is dedicated to the development, promotion and monetization of mobile applications for Windows Phone and will contain unique information, as well as real-world experience in using various systems, services and approaches.

More details: http://appclub.im/revolution/

August 10 - AppClub {build, monetize} Revolution




AppClub {build, monetize} Revolution will be held in the cozy office of Provectus IT near the sea and will bring together about a hundred mobile developers, experts, representatives of start-ups and start-up incubators. At the conference, besides the presentations, master classes with speakers and experts, as well as start-up pitchs from participants of the hakafon will be presented.

AppClub {build, monetize} is a club for mobile experts and startups. The club began work in October 2012, since that time 7 meetings were held, of which six - in Kiev and one - in Odessa. Much attention is paid to the design, development and testing of software products for various mobile systems, but the main focus is on promotion and monetization methods. At AppClub meetings, there are both recognized industry experts and young startups who have the opportunity to talk about their product and get feedback in the early stages of development. Such startups as WP7PUBLISH, Tutorle, mockup.io, WithMyFriends.org, Backendless already spoke at the meetings.

If you are a startup and want to talk about it at AppClub {build, monetize} Revolution - send a description of your project and information about speakers / founders to info@devrain.com so that we can include you in the program in advance.

Traditionally, you also informal communication with the participants and speakers of the conference, as well as, if desired, continued communication in one of the night clubs in Odessa!

More details: http://revolution.appclub.im
