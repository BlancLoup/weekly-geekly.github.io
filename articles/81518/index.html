Sony Ericsson Vivaz - a new fighter in the market of Symbian-smartphones

Despite the huge number of rumors, Sony Ericsson not only continues to exist, but also rolls out new products to the market. Recently, a smartphone based on Symbian S60 5th incarnation was introduced - Sony Ericsson Vivaz.

Vivaz is positioned as a multimedia smartphone that can record 720p HD video, and also has outstanding gaming capabilities, because it boasts the following characteristics: 720 MHz Cortex-A8 processor, PowerVR SGX video accelerator, 256 MB RAM, 75 MB ROM . Also, the smartphone has a 8.1 megapixel camera, a 3.2 "touch screen with a resolution of 360x640 pixels, a 1200 mAh battery and a device. The dimensions are 107 x 51.7 x 12.5 mm, and the weight is only 97 g.



This miracle works under control of Symbian S60 and interface add-on from SE. Cost in Russia, according to the official twitter , is 15999 r, which makes this smartphone an excellent competitor to the Samsung i8910, and another set of devices from Nokia. At the same time, Vivaz does not intersect with Satio in any way, since the latter is positioned as a camera phone.
