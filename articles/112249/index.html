IpHoster.ru? NOT



Admins are divided into those who do not make backups and those who already do

Hello, reader! Tell me, did you have such that for some stupid reason you did not have time to pay for hosting and delayed payment for two days? I think everyone has this happened. Probably, you hoster issued a warning or even blocked the site? Yes, it is unpleasant, but not fatal (unless, of course, you have a super-visited portal). So it happened to me - for the first time in a year of using a dedicated server, I delayed payment for two days. But the staff of the brave hosting decided that a simple lock is not enough. iphoster.ru decided to punish me as much as possible, on all points as they could.



Story


Hosting was ordered on December 17, 2009 for a startup that was smoothly and leisurely preparing to launch. We wanted the idea to mature, because a year or two to launch it is not scary. All this time we have been preparing it right on our personal server.

The machine is not the most powerful, but enough with his head:
Processor: Pentium 4
RAM: 512 MB
Disk: 60 GB
IP Addresses: 5
OS: Debian 5

The price at 40 per month was incomparable with the convenience of our own hosting and confidence in their own data.

On the server, besides our future startup, several of my projects were hosted:
The libcanvas framework (http://libcanvas.com) and the console forum (http://freecr.ru). Also, I ‚Äúgave shelter‚Äù to some of my comrades for their projects, for example, nerman
In addition, I used it for work, as well as for many small articles and scripts.
Also, the version control systems of my projects were located there.

I would not say that hosting was very reliable. Sometimes it happened for several hours there was no connection with him. Once an interesting case even happened due to which my server was not working on November 25-26, 2010.

Closer to dinner on the 25th, I receive a text message stating that the server is not working. But I am in another city, I arrive in the evening and begin to understand. Then I write in support. Although this case is only indirectly related to the result, but note how loyal I feel about their problems and how I emphasize the importance of the data.
