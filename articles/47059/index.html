With love from Opera: My Opera portal is now available in Russian

Oslo, Norway - December 16, 2008 - The My Opera portal for browser users, supported by Opera Software, is now available in Russian. Russian-speaking users have long supported Opera, and in gratitude for the support Opera has created a version of My Opera in Russian.

In addition to the fact that hundreds of thousands of Russian-speaking users turn to My Opera services every week, Russia also holds leading positions in the number of Opera Desktop and Opera Mini users. The My Opera community was created to provide an opportunity for self-expression for each person in any language. Russian-speaking users make up the majority of this community and can now easily use the interface in their native language.

In order to get My Opera in Russian, go to www.my.opera.com and select the language in the lower right corner of the page.
