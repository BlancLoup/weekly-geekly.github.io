Google reader close



Google in the official blog , as well as on the site Google Reader announced the closure of the service.

Among the reasons was the desire to focus on core products and improve them.
Service closing date: July 1st, 2013.

Very sad news, especially for the Habr community, which more than ordinary users understand the advantages of RSS feeds.

PS Among online service alternatives I can name Yandex . Lenta and NetVibes .
