CRM plus mobile communications. We strike integration at low sales

This time we will not boast much about the achievements in the field of integration of virtual PBX, Internet telephony and mobile communications, since we have been writing about this for more than half a year. We will try to describe not what has been done (although this too), but what we would like to do in the foreseeable future and what, from our point of view, might be of interest to dear readers. AltegroCloud's virtual PBX honestly performs its tasks and ensures close friendship of mobile phones, people and cloud PBX: records mobile calls, shows statistics of corporate cellular communications in real time and even knows how to find employees on the map using geotags.

A lot of effort was spent on integration with a couple of popular CRM systems and they (these same integrations) gave a clear understanding that our SIM cards + virtual PBX + CRM is a convergent product that allows you to build interesting communication schemes, the complexity of which is limited only by the imagination of developers and possible specific IP.
