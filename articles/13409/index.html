Pay with a Linux Fund Visa card - help the open source movement

The non-profit foundation Linux Fund launched a joint project with the VISA payment system for servicing branded plastic cards for the second time. A similar program was already launched in 1999, and thanks to it, it was possible to raise more than half a million dollars to finance free projects such as Debian, Blender, FreeGeek, the WikiMedia Foundation, and others . Now the validity of those cards has expired, and therefore the fund issues new cards in US Bank (USA) and MBNA Canada Bank (Canada) banks.

The owner of such a Visa card automatically makes charitable donations to the Linux Fund every time it makes a payment transaction from its account ‚Äî these deductions are taken away from the standard bank commission.
