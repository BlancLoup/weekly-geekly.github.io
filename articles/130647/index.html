Record of the seminar from SumIT summer startup marathon


It was a wonderful summer day (August 18, 2011), when Alexander Orlov (independent consultant in the field of project management, Vice-President of the Guild of Software Project Managers) came to the site of the summer Start-up Marathon SumIT (St. Petersburg, Kronverksky pr. 49, NRU ITMO) to to conduct a seminar on the theme ‚ÄúHow are start-up teams‚Äù. The workshop gathered more than a hundred people and lasted until the evening. The speech contained a lot of useful information, so remembering the past SumIT and waiting for the future WinIT, we post the video of this seminar in this topic!




Alexander Orlov - ‚ÄúHow are teams in IT companies?‚Äù

Theses of the seminar:
What is the skill of managing difficult and intelligent people? Why does the team work for some managers like a clock and people solve the most complicated tasks and get real pleasure from it?
The manager and the team can be on one side of the barricade, and then there are practically no organizational and technical problems for this tandem. The success of the project depends on how the manager and engineers can work together in one mechanism and become one team.
We will analyze what mechanisms operate during the life cycle of a team and how we can influence it.

Short biography:
Alexander Orlov is an independent consultant in the field of software development projects and development teams, employee motivation and retention systems, and career building. He is the author of several trainings on team management, managerial skills development and IT career building.
Vice President of the Guild of Software Project Managers.
From 2004 to 2008, he worked at Intel as a team leader for programmers.
From 2000 to 2004, he worked at Elbrus MCST under a contract with Sun Microsystems, Inc.. Since 2002, he has led the Java technology testing team on mobile devices (J2ME).
Founder of the project Happy PM. Author of the book "Secrets of managing programmers." Speaker of more than 30 industrial conferences.
Founder of the online university Stratoplan.ru
In the past, leading corporate training for Intel: New Employee Orientation, Transition to Manager, Effective Listening
