What conclusions did I personally draw from this study?
1. In Yekaterinburg, there are really major players in the development market. In other cities, they are practically absent or, if they exist, they did not participate in the study.
2. The turnover from the promotion and advertising services already exceeds the turnover from the development services (because I know for sure that many promoters did not make it to the rating, because they hardly develop websites).
3. In general, the regions are not so great efficiency in the development of websites - you will not earn much on this if you do not offer clients related services and, of course, promotion. The right column shows the turnover per employee. Maximum - 89.5 thousand. rubles. Medium - about 50 tr. Pay from it office, equipment, turnover taxes, payroll taxes ... and almost nothing will be left for development.
4. If you do not take Yekaterinburg, the average web studio earns less than the average kebab house.
5. Most likely, the future is for inexpensive standard solutions and SaaS services. Because the client is not ready to pay a lot, but developers need to eat. Therefore, you will need to sell a lot of cheap sites and a lot of advertising.

In general, there is something to think about. What do you think?
