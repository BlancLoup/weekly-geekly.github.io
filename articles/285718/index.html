Choosing a master's program in Europe and the USA



For many people, the issue of choosing the optimal way of further improvement and professional development is relevant. It is no secret that choosing a master's program is not so easy , so we would like to share with you what we know about master programs in Europe and the USA. Recall that in one of our past articles we published the most famous world university rankings, do not forget to view them in our blog .

The best universities in Europe - advantages and opportunities



The academic level of the second stage can be obtained at many universities in Europe in any area in one or two years with preparation for the subsequent professional activity.

Master programs can be taken at the Technical University of Madrid , one of the oldest universities in Europe. Some faculties have more than a century of history. More than thirty-five thousand students study here and more than three thousand teachers work. The university became widely known as a school of architecture and engineering of world importance. At the educational institution you can get a master's degree and a doctorate in business, social sciences, modern technologies, engineering and architecture for about one thousand euros per year.

The possibilities of the University of Hamburg in Germany allow you to choose a master's program, corresponding to academic goals, employment and budget, as it provides payment for the semester, an average of 400 euros. On the basis of the educational institution there are six faculties with a full range of all possible disciplines, including economic, social, humanitarian, and natural. Here study computer science and medicine. The university has more than five thousand employees.

In one of the strangest university with a world name - Oxford University in the UK, whose history began in 1096, it employs more than five thousand employees. Here you can study master programs in various disciplines - linguistics, art, engineering, technology, medicine, social sciences, which cost an average of fifteen thousand pounds.

Master's and doctoral degrees can be obtained at the University of Glasgow - the oldest educational institution in the UK, ranked fourth among English-speaking universities around the world. For those who want to study in the following areas - business, social sciences, art, languages, medicine, educational services will cost from fourteen thousand pounds.

Humboldt University of Berlin provides master's programs in all humanities from three hundred euros per semester. The university has the status of the oldest in the world, more than thirty five thousand people study here.

The list of reputable and oldest universities continues at the University of Bologna in Italy, the history of which is the starting point of the European cultural and scientific tradition. Students and undergraduates are offered about two hundred different areas with tuition fees from six hundred euros per semester.

The London School of Economics and Political Science in the UK helps to get a degree in the social sciences. The school received wide acclaim due to the fact that it was completed by 35 heads of various countries and 16 scientists and politicians who became Nobel laureates. Tuition is about seventeen thousand pounds per year.

More than seventy international study programs can be taken at the Catholic University of Leuven in Belgium, founded in 1425. Currently, more than forty thousand students study there, the approximate payment per year is six hundred euros.

One of the best universities in the world - the Swiss Higher Technical School of Zurich , Switzerland offers one of the best masters programs in mathematics, physics and chemistry. The payment for the semester is approximately six hundred and fifty Swiss francs.

The Ludwig-Maximilian University of Munich , Germany has given the world thirty-four Nobel Prize winners; today it trains forty-five thousand students, providing a wide range of master's programs. Education here costs about two hundred euros per semester.

More than one hundred and fifty areas of master's degree can be chosen at the Free University of Berlin in Germany, a well-known research center that maintains close ties with research institutions and universities all over the world. Tuition is from three hundred euros per semester.



US Universities: Master Degree Opportunities



The system of higher education in the United States of America is considered one of the best in the world, it brings together about six thousand institutions of higher education. American universities offer a distinguished master's program, allowing to improve their knowledge and skills and make a new qualitative step in professional development.

You can use the wide range of programs for a master's degree at the Academy of Art University . This educational institution was founded in 1929 in San Francisco by Richard Stevens. The university is located in the heart of the city on Kearny Street. He is deservedly considered an outstanding school of art and design of world importance. There are six Nobel Prize laureates and twenty-two Pulitzer Prize winners among graduates and teachers of the university. The international labor market is in need of specialists with a university degree, so graduates find work within six months after graduation.

One of the leading research universities - Boston University , ranks fourth in terms of size among US higher education institutions, offers more than two hundred and fifty programs, among which masters occupy a significant place. All of them have earned a high reputation, which is ensured by a decent level of teaching and high scientific potential.

Harvard University is the oldest prestigious American university located in Cambridge. He invariably occupies a leading position in world rankings. Among the former and present teachers of the school are forty-four Nobel laureate. The university conducts scientific research, which have an international level, playing an enormous role in raising the level of master's programs that the university offers.

It is possible to acquire a master's degree at Michigan State University - the largest academic, research center, introducing new programs, including for obtaining a master's degree. The educational institution occupies a leading position in the number of programs for exchange and study abroad, and this is more than two hundred offers in sixty countries.

Princeton University , according to the USNews Ranking, in 2013 ranked first in the world. Here studies are conducted that are of great importance for the development of various areas of world science and it is possible to choose promising master programs that allow you to subsequently pursue successful professional activities.

University of San Francisco , the oldest and most prestigious university in California, offers a wide range of graduate programs in a fairly large number of disciplines and areas of study. Here, students from seventy-five countries of the world receive education, who later receive employment support.
