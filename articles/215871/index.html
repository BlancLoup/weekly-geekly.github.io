Why do we need remote presence robots?

In recent times, our company often faces misunderstanding on the Internet, why we need our robot Webot, how it differs from skype and who will buy it. In my post I will try to briefly explain these issues.
A remote presence robot is usually a fully human-controlled robot that may have an auxiliary autonomous mode, for example, to automatically return to the charging point. The main mode of operation of such a robot is human control! Our robot Webot is still completely human-controlled.


The main task of a remote presence robot is to give a person the opportunity not only to inspect the surrounding space, but also to move along it to create a more complete presence effect.

We recently built a service to inspect a luxury hotel, through its website . This service allows you to draw attention to the hotel and demonstrates the concept of openness of the hotel to its customers.
