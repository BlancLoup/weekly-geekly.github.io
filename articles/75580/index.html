Motorola Milestone WCDMA version available in Germany


WCDMA version of Android-smartphone Motorola Milestone (nee Droid) is available for purchase in Germany. The local operator O2 offers Milestone for the price of 1 EUR, subject to the conclusion of a 2-year contract. The cost of a monthly payment for the device is 20 EUR, i.e. The final cost of the phone is 481 EUR. The contract is paid separately. The price of the phone looks more than attractive. It remains to wait for the appearance of Milestone on ebay, and in retail in European countries. Thanks to a powerful advertising campaign, Motorola Droid sold 250,000 copies in its first week of sales.

Briefly about the characteristics:
- Display 3.7 '' with a resolution of 480 x 854.
- Supports HSDPA, WiFi, GPS, USB 2.0 High Speed, Bluetooth v2.1 + EDR
- MicroSD card for 8 gb in the kit.
- Android OS 2.0 Eclair + WebKit-based browser with built-in support for Adobe Flash 10.
