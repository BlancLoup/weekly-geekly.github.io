On the possibility of AI to self-knowledge and knowledge of the creator

I want to offer readers a philosophical task on a computer theme:
Suppose that in a computing environment (built on modern principles) there is an artificial mind. Some algorithms that have reflection and knowledge. Let's try to think about how much these algorithms can learn about the world and its device?
Let's think up a little more interesting than a simple analysis of obvious facts like configuration, memory size, etc. As humankind is now trying to calculate the foundations of the universe very indirectly, pushing particles together, accumulating statistics on recorded events and checking the correctness of their theories on these statistics, an artificial mind can conduct experiments, say, on processor heating, determining power dissipation and the point of increasing errors when overheating.

For example, can such a mind determine the speed of light? Or processor material? Let's try to think in this way, we will feel ourselves physicists - our task is to study the structure of the world, being "inside" of this world.
