A couple of useful sites for choosing photographic equipment

I would like to add a recent topic about the theoretical foundations of photography with a certain amount of practical information. It will be interesting, first of all, to those who choose another camera or lens for themselves (regardless of the qualifications of the photographer and the level of technology):

www.dpreview.com - detailed reviews and comparisons of cameras, rather quickly replenished as new models are released. For people in the history of photographic equipment there is an archive over the past years;

www.photozone.de/Reviews/overview - high-quality and informative lens testing reports for SLR cameras from various manufacturers.
