Results of the contest of IT projects Web & Tech Ready 2015 and statistics on all participants in the competition

On October 20, the final of the VII open competition of IT projects Web & TechReady was held in the Skolkovo Hypercube. Awards this year were awarded to 20 projects in three main and ten special nominations. Including 12 companies received cash grants from the Skolkovo Foundation for a total amount of 48 million rubles.

The main nominations of Seed and Startup differed in the stage of business development; in the Spinoff nomination, projects created within large companies and withdrawn into separate business units participated. In total, 712 teams from 106 cities participated in the competition. The projects presented 65 Russian and foreign business incubators and accelerators.



In the Startup nomination, the Sum & Substance project won over - a platform for verifying the authenticity of digital documents. The jury awarded second place to the Face_IS face recognition cloud service. The third was AppFollow service, which allows developers to analyze the applications of competitors in the App Store and Google Play.

The Best in the Seed nomination was Last.Backend, which created a platform for automating the development and deployment of server applications. Second place - the projector augmented reality for the car Crystal Vision HUD. The third prize was a project Surfancy - a device that allows you to create a touch area on any surface.

In the Spinoff nomination the winner was WorksPad - a solution that allows you to organize a mobile workplace (on a tablet computer) for corporate users. The second place is a tool for user verification in mobile applications by voice and face of VoiceKey.ONEPASS. The third is the brand analytics system for monitoring and analyzing a brand in social media and media in real time.
