Nokia draws attention to the environment

At CES 2008, Nokia introduced a new Eco Sencor device - a set of mobile phones and a special set of sensors that monitor the weather, the environment and the health of the owner.




The sensors are located in a separate case from the phone, which can be worn around the wrist as a bracelet or around the neck. Solar batteries supply them with energy, the measurement results are transmitted to the phone using Near Field Communication technology.
Measured parameters: humidity and air temperature, atmospheric pressure, gas content in the air, wearer's pulse, etc. Thus, at any time the owner will be able to find out any necessary information about the environment and his health.

Even in the manufacture of the device will use new eco-friendly technology. Many electronic circuits will be made by printing elements on a plastic substrate. Plans to actively use recycled materials (for example, only recycled steel will be used in the hull) and biomaterials, such as lactic acid polymers.

At the moment, Eco Sensor - just one of the developments. But already in ready-to-release updates, the company pays attention to the environment. The new phone Nokia 3110 Evolve uses more than half of renewable materials and the power supply has a minimum power consumption, almost twice the requirements of the Energy Star standard.
