Announcement of a free online course DevOps: What, Why, and How



Friends, we invite everyone to attend the free online course for DevOps developers: What, Why and How.

The course program is designed for a very wide audience: software developers, testers, IT specialists, decision makers, as well as students and anyone who is somehow interested in the DevOps methodology, its tools and processes.

Training will be conducted with the help of video tutorials, presentations by teachers, text materials and practical exercises. Course duration - 5 weeks. The start of classes is March 29, 2016.

  • First week: Introduction to DevOps.
  • Second week: DevOps introduction - methodology.
  • Third week: Techniques and tools - Agile development and continuous integration.
  • Fourth week: Techniques and tools - continuous delivery and continuous development.
  • Fifth week: DevOps deployment technologies.


Thanks to the division into small groups, discussion publications and social networks, you will be able to communicate and learn not only from teachers, but also from other participants. For optimal results, you will need to spend 2-3 hours per week on training. Training takes place in English .

At the end of each week will be tested acquired knowledge. Anyone who successfully passes the course will receive certificates.

You can sign up for the course on our resource, educast.emc.com .
