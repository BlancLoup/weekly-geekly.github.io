Gabe Newvel will hold a teleconference in Reddit on March 5 at 2 am Moscow time

Gabe Newvel , CEO of Valve, will hold a newsgroup on Reddit today at 14:00 Moscow time. Teleconference - literally a remote conference.

Initially, the conference was planned for March 3, and light tension prevailed among users of reddit, but when Gabe did not appear by the promised time, the public was tensed up in earnest. Later, he was contacted by journalists, and he replied that he should wait for March 4 at 2:00 PST , 14:00 Moscow time . Apparently it was an allegory to Valve Time , Left4dead 2 and the fact that the campaign did not release a single third of its game titles.

According to recode.net and gamespot.com .
