I want to! Or the world of advertising upside down

Google has learned to select ads according to the needs of the user, but it happens that the user has satisfied the need, and the ads all flash before his eyes.

At the same time, there are stock exchanges on which stocks and even goods are sold. They have someone who wants to sell, and someone who wants to buy.

Why not allow the user to declare their desire (with the parameters of time and price)? And let manufacturers and suppliers of goods and services fight for his choice.
