Alfa-Bank and Visa launch the development program for fintech-startups

On Habr√© have always been interested in programs with which you can not only get training, but also build your business. The best example of such at the moment is the program from Yandex Tolstoy Startup Camp . About her was a good post . But I would like to talk about a new program of this kind, which is focused on finance and payment systems, Alfa Kemp is the first in Russia program for finding and developing FINTECH start-ups. For 5 weeks (August 17 - September 22) of an intensive program, it will be possible to create a basis for the development of your business in the field of financial technologies and, possibly, to receive investments. Participation in the program is free.

Camp participants will be able to consult with AlfaBank specialists (accountants, lawyers, technologists, marketing specialists), MetaBeta start-up accelerator specialists, Visa, and any other specialists you need.

To develop services, Alfa-Bank will provide program participants with access to using their technologies through an API, in particular, an Internet acquiring API for making online payments, an API for organizing P2P transfers, an API for issuing virtual prepaid cards, an API for transferring from accounts of a legal entity to any card of a Russian bank.

Under FINTECH startups, accelerator organizers understand companies that make financial and near-financial services, such as payment systems, online banks, virtual wallets and cards, virtual currencies, personal finance management systems, integration of coupons and bonus programs with financial services, b2b services. Developers of such technologies as biometrics, Big Data, scoring systems, antifraud, p2p solutions, mobile acquiring are also welcome.

Participation in the accelerator implies at least two trainings a week for 4 hours each and three meetings with methodologists, mentors and trackers lasting an hour a week each. On Fridays, the program will summarize preliminary results, the results of which will select teams to continue participating in the event. In the final of the program, a presentation of the developed projects to bank experts and venture investors will be held. The best teams will receive investment proposals from the bank, as well as its partners for the implementation of their project.

Participants from the regions are provided with accommodation and scholarship. Applications for participation in the event are accepted on the website alfacamp.alfabank.ru .
