Online broadcast: the most feminine Microsoft conference



Today, at 10:00 (Moscow time), an online broadcast of Design Camp will begin - our first conference in Russia, dedicated to the design and design of modern applications.


The program includes speeches by experts from Microsoft, the British Higher School of Design, the Scream School, Computer Graphics School, Mail.Ru Group, Yandex, Usabilitylab, UIDesign Group, Parcsis and JetStyle.

We will talk about service design and gamification, usability and testing, typography, animation, grids and industrial design, practice of use and implementation. And also separately about the integration of design when creating real products.

Link to the broadcast: events.techdays.ru/descamp/2012-12
