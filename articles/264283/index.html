Welcome to Windows Camp - September 10, Moscow



Hello!

It has already become a good tradition for us to meet at the beginning of September and tell the latest news to the developers ... Do you remember the Windows Camp 2013 ? In 2015, after a year's break, he returns again ! This year, the tradition of doing a great event specifically for developers will continue - we invite you on September 10 to Windows Camp, which will be held in Moscow, in RIA Novosti. Read more - under the cut!

Four parallel tracks, partner exhibition and all the most relevant from experts (not only) Microsoft on Windows 10 , Visual Studio 2015 , Microsoft Azure and the Internet of Things !

This event is very important for us and the ecosystem - quite recently, at the end of July, a completely new version of the most popular Windows 10 operating system was released , which, perhaps, became the most innovative - this is a direct connection with users, Cortana, and Microsoft Azure, and many other, less noticeable, but no less important details that formed on July 29 into a single puzzle.

Come hear about how to develop applications on Windows 10 ; about the capabilities of the new version of the most powerful development environment for Windows - Visual Studio 2015 , as well as how we see and make the Internet of Things .

At the conference you will find:
  • deep immersion in the latest technology
  • technical reports on Microsoft key products, technologies and solutions
  • master class on building solutions in the concept of the Internet of Things

Hurry up to register for the event! Limited number of seats!
