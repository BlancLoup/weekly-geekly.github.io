Want to go to university - make a presentation

Applicants entering the University of Chicago, starting from this fall, will be required, in addition to passing the main exams, to present four-page PowerPoint presentations on a free topic.

Partly new requirements are related to the fact that PowerPoint is becoming one of the business tools, and besides, the authors of the initiative believe, in the presentation the applicant will be able to reveal their new qualities that will not be able to manifest themselves in traditional exams.

PowerPoint format has become a kind of business language. Every day, according to Microsoft estimates, around 30 million presentations are shown in the world.
