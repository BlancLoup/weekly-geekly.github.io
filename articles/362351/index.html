Short film "Ambition"



Ambitions is a collaboration between Platige Image Studio and the European Space Agency (ESA). The director was Tomek Baginsky, the roles were played by Aidan Gillen and Aisling Franziosi. The film was shot in Iceland and was shown on October 24, 2014 as part of the Sci-Fi: Days of Fear and Wonder festival held by the British Cinema Institute, South Bank, London.

The film was shot in support of the mission "Rosetta", more detailed information here .
