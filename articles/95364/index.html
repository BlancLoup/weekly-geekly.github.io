ESPROS - a service that will help you buy on the Internet easily and profitably! Actual for Ukraine


I'll tell you about a cool Internet service, which is designed to greatly facilitate the process of making purchases on the Internet - ESPROS .

Today, almost everyone knows that buying over the Internet is more profitable than in stationary stores. Almost everything except the avid skeptics. Most of those who know that it is more profitable to buy on the Internet - they buy on the Internet. Let's look at how most of those who buy on the Internet do it!


Most often, the buyer comes to one of the specialized services to compare prices, find the desired item in the product catalog and look at the offers of online stores registered on this service and selling this product. There can be a lot of such offers. The buyer gets acquainted with 10-20 offers, calls the store managers for accurate information, because sometimes the goods are available only on the site, or the price may be higher. All this takes a lot of time and effort, those who often buy on the Internet will understand me. In my experience, about 50% of cases when the confirmed goods turned out to be more expensive than those declared by the manager, or were completely absent.

ESPROS will save you from this job. You simply select a product and place an order. After the time that you choose for yourself, representatives of the online store that has made the lowest price for the necessary goods will contact you! Everything is simple, fast for you!

So far, the resource is at the Beta 1.0 stage, but it is getting better every day, and you can make a purchase via ESPROS today, though only in Ukraine, but we plan to expand the boundaries of the service in the CIS countries. Service address - espros.com.ua
