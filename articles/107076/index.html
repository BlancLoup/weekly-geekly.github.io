Conclusion


As for me, none of the laptops came close enough to the MacBook Air to call it a worthy alternative. Someone pumped up the technical characteristics, someone has the price, and someone has both.

But despite this, I would like to mention MSI X360 and Sony Vaio X as those who came as close as possible. The disadvantages of MSI X360 can only be called the use of HDD instead of SSD. And the Sony Vaio X main disadvantage is the price.
Asus UL30A is far from Air, but for its price is a very interesting offer. The same can be said about the Acer 3810 Timeline.
Dell Adamo XPS, although it has the minimum of all applicants thick (9.99 mm), but has a sky-high price and a controversial support system with the lid open.

The only thing that can be said is that Apple haters PC fans now have plenty to choose from.

Although I do not have anything from Apple products, but in the new MacBook Air I saw a possible ideal laptop. I will wait for full reviews from reputable publications and prices from us, but we'll see there.
