DevCon School schools and other good news for the day of knowledge

Dear our readers! One of the most enjoyable activities in life is to learn! After all, precisely in order to learn throughout life, we choose such areas of knowledge in which everything is constantly changing, isn't it? Therefore, we are happy to congratulate all our readers with the day of knowledge! Students, of course, especially ...

By the way, about knowledge. We, technology evangelists , love to share knowledge, and we do it in different ways. For example, at conferences like DevCon . However, this year we decided to give knowledge more profound and interesting, and to do it periodically, not once a year. From here was born the DevCon Schools format, which you can read more about under the cut. By the way, the first such school on cognitive services, chat bots and artificial intelligence will be held on September 11th .



By the way, under the cut there are a few gifts for the day of knowledge and special opportunities for students, so if you like gifts - look in there too!

DevCon Schools


DevCon Schools are practical events that most resemble a mixture of a master class and a hackathon. During such events, you will be able to dive deeper into the study of the most modern technologies and "try the code with your hands." And lectures ... you can see them online?

DevCon Schools - this will be a series of events of various formats. The nearest will take place on September 11-17, and I will tell about it below. The next school will be in November, and we plan that it will gather more than 500 developers. But about this - in our next posts.

And of course, do not forget that there is still an online learning format! For example, we recently wrote about the most popular courses at the Microsoft Virtual Academy , and in the coming months we hope to please you with even more interesting projects in the field of online learning!

Cognitive school


The nearest school will be devoted to cognitive services and artificial intelligence, and it is intended for those who want to learn how to use the entire arsenal of Microsoft services and technologies related to artificial intelligence in one week. These include:

  • Chat bots and Microsoft Bot Framework
  • Cognitive services, i.e. cloud services for image analysis, natural language, speech, etc.
  • Machine Learning, Azure ML and Neural Networks

The school will be held 2 full days on weekends, and 2 days during the week in the evenings. In the course of the school, you will be asked, in addition to lectures, to do small laboratory work (ie, you need to come with your laptops), as well as a graduation project, the topic of which you can think up on your own. According to the results of the implementation of the graduation project and laboratory works, the school participants will be issued a certificate of its completion, which you can then be proud of.

The event is focused primarily on developers and enthusiasts who want to use the capabilities of artificial intelligence in their applications, but do not yet know how. We will not go deep into the theoretical aspects of this complex and interesting discipline, but show how in practice modern technologies can be used to solve real problems. We also invited partners from Mail.ru, Abbyy, Nanosemantics, CleverData to share their experiences and capabilities of their products.
