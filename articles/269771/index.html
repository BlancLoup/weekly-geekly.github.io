3 days before the increase in the cost of tickets for the conference of mobile developers MBLTdev

Less than a month later, on November 17, in Moscow, the MBLTdev Mobile Developer Conference will be held. The conference program will be divided into several tracks aimed at mid + mobile developers and managers of IT companies.



We have gathered 5 reasons for you to attend this event:

  1. Reports from mobile development experts from companies like Wunderlist, Runkeeper, Spotify, Tinkoff, Rambler & Co., Microsoft and other well-known companies;
  2. Seminars on cross-platform development, virtual reality and security of mobile devices;
  3. Entertaining activities and jokes at partners' stands;
  4. Hundreds of like-minded and professional networking;
  5. After-party in the Coin restaurant, where you can communicate in an informal setting with speakers and conference participants.

The preliminary schedule is published on the official conference website .

Last year's reports are available on our Youtube channel . Follow the conference news on social networks Vk.com , Facebook and Twitter .

See you soon!
