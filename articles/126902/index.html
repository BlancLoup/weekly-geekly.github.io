25 best startups of St. Petersburg



The summer of 2011 for the start-up community of St. Petersburg was marked by SumIT. This project of the Innovators Club, which was sponsored by RVC as a general sponsor, attracted large companies, experienced experts and investors, and also gathered participants from other countries and cities.

Now the organizers and participants enter the home stretch, and we invite you on Wednesday, August 24, at 18:00 to the final Invest Fest event. Participants will be able to get acquainted with the presentations of the 25 best projects and 5 best SumIT ideas , and the organizers of the summer university will sum up, award the winners, tell whose team won the trip to Silicon Valley, and share plans for the coming year.

The list of projects that we managed to collect impressed even ourselves. In several hours, users of the first will be able to familiarize themselves with a brief description of projects that will be performing.

In addition, there will be:
  1. business angels: Yakov Markov (IC "Income"), Samuel Gorelik (Money Money);
  2. Representatives of venture funds: Almaz Capital Partners, Runa Capital, VTB Capital, iTech Capital, RSV Venture Partners, CMD Holding ;
  3. Representatives of large IT companies: Mail.ru Group, Intel, EMC, Oracle ;
  4. Special guest: Igor Rubenovich Agamirzyan , General Director of RVK.

SumIT in numbers:
  1. 38 days marathon;
  2. 30 useful lectures, seminars, workshops, competitions and master classes;
  3. about 200 proposed ideas;
  4. more than 1000 people participated in the events;
  5. 100 projects applicants wishing to get to the investment session Fest Invest.

The event will be held at: St. Petersburg, Kronverksky pr. 49, ITMO.
