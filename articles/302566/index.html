Wolfram Technologies: 4th Russian Conference



On June 6 and 7, 2016, the 4th Russian Wolfram Technology Conference will be held at the St. Petersburg State University of Economics.

You can register here .
Participation in the conference is completely free.

You can get acquainted with how the conference is held in the article on Habr√© Materials of the Third Wolfram Technologies Conference (SPbESU, 2015) .

This year the conference will be held 2 days. You can work with Wolfram technologies and chat with experts:

Galina Mikhalkina (International Business Development Executive, Wolfram Research)

Oleg Marichev (Developer - Integration and Special Functions, Wolfram Research, Doctor of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, creator of widely known tables of integrals, sums, series and products)

Roman Osipov (Wolfram Research Certified Instructor, IT Manager, Balass Publishing House)

Mikael Egibyan (Consultant, Mathematica R & D, Wolfram Research)

Ilya Marchevsky (Candidate of Physics and Mathematics, Department of Applied Mathematics, Bauman Moscow State Technical University)

Mike Remolon (International Business Development Manager, Wolfram Research)

Julia Ratushava (Certified Instructor, Wolfram Research)

Galimzyan Islamov (Head of the Department of High Performance Computing and Parallel Programming at the Institute of Mathematics, Information Technologies and Physics of Udmurt State University)

On June 6, the training will work for you:
  • 9:30 - Introduction to Wolfram Language
  • 10:30 - Master class on image processing
  • 12:30 - Data processing in Wolfram Language: import, parsing, analysis, visualization, infographics

On June 7, experts will deliver their reports for you:
  • 9:45 - Business development in Russia (Galina Mikhalkina)
  • 10:15 - Main report: Mathematical functions and operations with them in the Wolfram Language (Oleg Marichev)
  • 12:45 - Solving urgent multidisciplinary tasks with the help of Wolfram Language (Roman Osipov)
  • 13:45 - Innovations in image and signal processing (Mikael Egibyan)
  • 14:45 - Using Wolfram Mathematica in the development of numerical methods for solving problems of hydro- and gas dynamics (Ilya Marchevsky)
  • 15:45 - Working with the Wolfram Language in the cloud (the working language of the presentation is English) (Mike Remolona)
  • 16:15 - Application of high-performance computing for solving problems from the theory of nonlinear oscillations (Julia Ratushava)
  • 16:45 - Modeling of vacuum displacement fields in the Mathematica system (Galimzyan Islamov)

Come, it will be very interesting!
