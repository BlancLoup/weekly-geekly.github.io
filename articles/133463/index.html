Clothing in exchange for copywriting - is there any point?


In the vastness of the network, there are websites whose visitors can pay for services in non-standard ways, a recent example is SocialWhispers , where tweets and ‚Äúlikes‚Äù can be used instead of money. At this time we will focus on the site where you can pay for the services with used clothes. As you can guess, Clothing for Correspondence is an Australian project that provides letter writing services and asks for a piece of clothing in return for the work done.

Users communicate with the contact center via e-mail and explain what kind of correspondence they need; Letters - the main profile of the site, but earlier they wrote and more, even songs. In any case, users specify all the necessary details - for whom the letter, the main meaning, the style of writing and all the necessary prehistory. Next, the authors carry out the order and send it to the customer, who pays for their work clothes.

The letter writing service is interesting in and of itself in the era of OFF = ON , but such a strange payment adds an element of quirkiness and sympvertising (I did not really understand the meaning of this term - note of the translator), which is difficult to resist. All for inspiration!
