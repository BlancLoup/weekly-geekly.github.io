Foreign companies are still the largest advertisers in Russia

The Sostav.ru publication has published the next annual rating of the largest advertisers in Russia for 2014.

Judging by the data provided by the research company TNS Russia, last year foreign companies remained the largest advertisers - only the distribution of positions among the largest of them changed. Also significantly increased the field of pharmaceuticals and other drug manufacturers.

Leadership belongs to Procter & Gamble, generating over 3.5% of the total amount of funds placed. There are no detailed figures before the official report of the ACAR for 2014.

The second line is Mars, which was pressed in January-September by PepsiCo, slightly inferior to P & G, but at the end of the year it still ranked third in the ranking.
