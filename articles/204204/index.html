Training in web development, web design and internet marketing

Today I want to talk about our project - this is a business school, which was conceived as a place of practical knowledge in the field of digital, a place where Internet professions are taught and skills are raised.

We, like everyone, lack knowledgeable people on the one hand, and on the other, we see so many people who want to learn, but do not know how and where. Universities do not teach web development, web design and internet marketing, at best they are trying to do it, but the results are still poor. Observing this tendency, we decided to get down to business and start teaching what we know, namely: PHP, Python, Ruby, JS, Internet marketing, e-commerce, IT management, etc.

For this important undertaking, we need your help. Yes, it is yours, the help of each of you is useful. Firstly, we need volunteer specialists who can provide training and lectures free of charge (both for the teacher and the student), we will provide the technical platform and all the necessary resources for this, and secondly, we will be happy to cooperate with HR ' These are Internet companies and are ready to send our students to you in your regions absolutely free of charge, and thirdly, we will not be prevented from helping us spread information.

Who cares about the future of the industry, please under the cat. This education is important!

Why can we learn?


A little background. We have been studying for a long time, but earlier we did it mainly for our own internal purposes. A year ago, we had the first free courses on Python / Django, there were two groups for a total of 30 people, of which more than 10 successfully completed the course for 4 months. We still learn daily.

Our concept


We teach practice. This is the meaning and purpose of our training, each course, each training. Every day we ourselves work on different projects, big and not so big, for the CIS, for the West and for the East. We can and are ready to show in our training exactly how we do it, give out professional secrets and even talk about the difficulties that everyone faces in one way or another.

The purpose of this kind of practical training is to prepare specialists who can work independently, even in Junior positions or, if the course is not for beginners, but for specialists, to share experience and raise the level of knowledge. All who are ready to respond to our call will have to follow this principle, and we, of course, will help with this.
