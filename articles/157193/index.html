Independent developer makes a remake of Descent

Descent - the cult game of the 90s - can be reborn in a new guise. The developer is going to implement the dizzying mechanics of this game with six degrees of freedom on the Unreal Engine 3. On Youtube, you can already see demos where angular Descent models fly and shoot on one of the UT3 cards.



Work is still at a very early stage, while everyone is engaged in one person under the nickname Madmax1998. On the project page, he writes that he will gratefully accept any help in creating new detailed models and textures of ships, weapons and Descent levels for the new engine. In addition, he published a PDF with a detailed description of the features of the implementation of the physics of motion in zero gravity with six degrees of freedom on the Unreal engine.

It is not yet known what the legal status of the development will be, whether there will be any problems with copyrights and licenses. I would like to hope that this classic shooter for people with a strong vestibular apparatus and a developed spatial imagination will still delight their fans in the new incarnation.
