We invite you to take part in the Siberian Hackath 1-2 March

On the upcoming weekend in Omsk a two-day programming marathon will take place. The Siberian hackathon, where experts from different areas will immerse themselves in the creation of joint IT projects, is organized by the leading members of the GDG Omsk community, developers Alexey Korovyansky and Serik Beysenov. The GDG Omsk team is part of the global non-profit developer community Google Developer Group , which focuses on Google technology and a number of open technologies: Google Android, Google Chrome, Google App Engine, Google Apps, Google Drive, Google Plus, Google Maps, Google APIs, HTML5, Google TV.




Venue: IT-loft, Omsk, ul. Training 83, office 212.
Participation conditions: Anyone who has gathered a team of 2 or 3 people can become a member of the Siberian hackathon. To take part in the hackathon, you must submit an application for registration and receive confirmation from the organizers. Applications are accepted until March 1. Why did GDG Omsk decide to organize the Siberian hackathon
In the Siberian hackathon it will be possible to create any projects using any technologies. For example, you can make a web application, a game for a smartphone or tablet, and write programs for the Raspberry Pi ‚Äî a single-board computer the size of a bank card. The main thing is the appearance of live and interesting projects for users and the city.

In addition to creating IT projects, the organizers set as their goal the practical development of new and little-known technologies, such as Go, AngularJS, Dart, Rust, Scala, RxJava, Android @ Square, Unreal Engine, Cassandra, Raspberry Pi, Google Glass. Participants can use Erlang, Clojure, Lisp, Brainfuck, Pizza, Chuck and other programming languages.

Event program
March 1, Saturday
11-00 - 12-00: Acquaintance of teams, brief presentations of projects.
12-00 - 15-00: First session of coding.
15-00 - 16-00: Lunch break *.
16-00 - 20-00: The second session of coding.
* Anyone can continue to develop in non-stop mode!

March 2, Sunday
09-00 - 13-00: First session of coding.
13-00 - 14-00: Lunch break.
14-00 - 17-00: Preparation of project presentations.
17-00 - 19-00: Presentations of projects.
19-00 - 19-30: Summing up and rewarding.
19-30 - 21-00: Afterparty for the most resistant.

The main criterion for evaluating the expert jury will be how well the team was able to apply a new technology for itself in the implementation of the idea. Special nominations for student, humanitarian and urban IT projects are also planned.

Organizers prepare a separate surprise for those who like to program micro-controllers. So, at the time of the Siberian hackathon, the authors of interesting ideas will be able to rent a Raspberry Pi Model B with a Bluetooth / WiFi USB module , Android ADK 2012 and other smart hardware. Additional information about all bonuses and surprises for hackathon members is collected in a regularly updated post on the GDG Omsk community blog .

To help participants with the formation of teams and search for really cool ideas, the organizers created a special Idea Moderator .

Keep track of what is happening on the Siberian hackathon on social networks on the hashtag #sibhack and on the event page on Google+ .

If you have any questions, please contact the organizers at org dot team@gdgomsk.org.
