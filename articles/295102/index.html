WebProfy made a move

The WebProfy web design studio (Kokoc Group) developed the design of the All-Russian Day of Walking festival website , which was first held in the country on the initiative of the Russian Olympic Committee on October 3, 2015.



Development of the design project and the creation of the site took place in a short time. Usually it takes two or three months to do this kind of work; in WebProfy they managed in just one. The result was a high-quality image site in a minimalist style with the harmonious use of modern trends of creativity. Simple and intuitive navigation of the site is supported by the logo of the Olympic Committee of Russia.

It was important not only to launch the resource, but also to maximally promote it so that as many people as possible in Russia could learn about the event of federal significance and take part in it. Therefore, WebProfy was faced with the task of envisaging a massive influx of visitors to the resource during the online broadcast of the event, the ability to quickly download and view video content. Before presenting the finished product to the customer, WebProfy conducted multiple load tests in the studio, which instilled in developers confidence that the site would not fall.
