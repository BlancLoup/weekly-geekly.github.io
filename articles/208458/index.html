Introducing Github Attendance Analytics

The weekend is over, and the old mood is returning to us to introduce new functions of the Githab. Based on the desire to start spectacularly in 2014, today we are happy to launch attendance analytics!

Now you can see detailed, analyzed information about visits to those repositories that you own or in which you can directly put the code (push). Just go to the graphs page of a specific repository - and you will see a new link leading to the Traffic page.



Having visited the page of visits, you will see a lot of useful information about your repositories, including where your readers come from, what they are viewing.


Looking at these numbers on our own repositories was fun, sometimes surprising, always interesting. We hope that you will like it as much as we do!
