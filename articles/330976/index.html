Today we will talk about the career in digital: what it is, what are the directions, what you need to know, who will be interested and where you can already start working. Laid out on the shelves and share with you!



1. What is this something?


It's like big data - everyone says, but nobody knows what it really is. Digital is the use of digital resources / channels to achieve company goals. Digital is connected with everything related to information technology: contextual advertising, display advertising, website development, SEO, web design, SMM. The goal of titanic efforts: brand promotion, sales growth, the formation of audience loyalty, attracting employees and many more interesting things.




In fact, everything is simple. Digital is technologies (cap, yes) that are used in marketing to promote products and / or services.
