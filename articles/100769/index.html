But this is in the future, and now many cities in Russia are languishing from heat and smoke, as peat bogs burn somewhere, and entire forests burn out somewhere. Undoubtedly, this is an environmental disaster, but what to do? A person has to adapt to these conditions. And if the smoke can still be tolerated and somehow deal with it, the abnormal heat haunts many, and air conditioners entail many dangers in the form of interesting diseases.
