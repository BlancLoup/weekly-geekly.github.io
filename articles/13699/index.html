Two new items from Samsung in a stylish guise: a laser printer and an MFP



Today, Samsung presented two new models of laser printers at the IFA exhibition in Berlin: the ML-1630 (laser printer) and the SCX-4500 (laser multifunction printer).

A pair of these devices is not only one of the thinnest in the world, but also looks amazing, thanks to the black glossy finish of the Grand Piano.

Samsung, in fact, for the first time in the world released a device for printing, which is based not only on new technologies, increasing the speed of printing and technical stuffing, but also on appealing to consumers.

Given that both devices look amazing, they have all the basic functions of laser devices. A remarkable detail is also the fact that the standard control buttons are completely removed from the surfaces and replaced by a touch panel with a pleasant blue backlight.

On the price of the device is not reported.
