25 books on cloud economics and security



/ photo Perspecsys Photos CC

IT-GRAD offers you a selection of 25 books on how to protect the clouds and save money on their maintenance.

Business case for cloud migration for IaaS model
The book is intended for employees of financial departments involved in justifying the feasibility of the introduction of cloud technologies. The book deals with basic concepts, describes the procedure for calculating the internal and external economic effect, describes the main selection scenarios.

Data Center 2.0: The Sustainable Data Center
The book is about what you need to do to build an effective data center. The situation is viewed from an economic point of view.

Comparing Traditional Data Center and Cloud Data Center Operating Costs
How much money is spent on maintaining the work of the data center? It depends on how big it is, where it is located and what it is intended for.

The Clouds Economy
Atlas of cloud services and tools.

Cloudonomics: The Business Value of Cloud Computing
The complete guide to extracting maximum profit from the cloud. It also considers situations when it is better to avoid using cloud technologies.

Management Strategies for the Cloud Revolution
This work covers the most important concepts and benefits that contribute to the growing popularity of cloud technologies.

Securing the Cloud: Cloud Computer Security Techniques and Tactics
The book describes the advantages and disadvantages of various ways to protect company information.

Critical Infrastructure System Security and Resiliency
Protection of virtualized environments: how to reduce the likelihood of undesirable events and reduce the harm if the perimeter of security was broken.

Securing the Virtual Environment Against Attack
The book shows how virtualization is used in different areas of activity. It also considers the various dangers that it carries.

Virtualization for Security: Including Sandboxing, Disaster Recovery, High Availability, Forensic Analysis, and Honeypotting
Virtualization provides opportunities to search for malware, data recovery and analysis. This book shows how to use virtualization for security.

Computer security handbook
The book for professionals and students on how to minimize the likelihood of gaps in the security systems of computing systems.

Cloud Security and Privacy: An Enterprise Perspective on Risks and Compliance
This book will show you the risks of trusting your data to the cloud, as well as tell you what you can do to secure your virtual infrastructure and web applications. It contains tips from tech-security gurus.

Cloud Computing Security: Foundations and Challenges
The most comprehensive overview of cloud security technologies with real life examples.

Cloud Computing: Theory and Practice
On parallel computing, architectures and distributed systems. The book describes the modern cloud infrastructure, as well as the infrastructure of companies such as Amazon, Google, Apple.

Cloud Computing and Services Science
This is a collection of the best works presented at the CLOSER conference held in the Netherlands in May 2011. The following areas are affected: cloud infrastructure, security, management, etc.

Thinking Security: Stopping Next Year's Hackers
This book is a fresh look at security issues. The author tells how to take measures to counter specific threats.

Virtualization Security: Protecting Virtualized Environments
In this article you will find tips to improve disaster recovery. Also read about the positive effects of VDI on security.

Transitioning the Enterprise to the Cloud: A Business Approach
The book addresses the cost of moving to the cloud, as well as the associated risks and difficulties.

Cloud Systems in Supply Chains
The book discusses the risks that entail the wrong choice of cloud technologies and management errors.

Guidelines for Cloud Computing
A simple guide to how to manage cloud systems. The book provides an excellent base that will allow you to assess the cost and risk of the transition to the cloud.

Building the Infrastructure for Cloud Security: A Solutions View
The author considers the main technological components necessary to create reliable cloud systems. The book also describes the difficulties that companies face when converting business-critical applications to the cloud.

Infrastructure Planning Handbook: Planning, Engineering, and Economics
All concepts and methodologies of infrastructure planning: tools, analysis methods, cases for maintaining infrastructure.

Enterprise Data Center Design and Methodology
Guide to the development of data centers of any size. The book covers all stages: from the choice of location to the structure of the network.

Datacenters in Financial Services: Driving Extreme Cost Savings
The book shows how to manage a virtually infinite amount of data. Each chapter provides the most advanced technological solutions for data management in data centers.

Green IT For Dummies
Here are collected ways that will save money and make your company a leader in the field of "green technology". In the book there are cases of companies that have already become "green" so that you can learn from their example.
