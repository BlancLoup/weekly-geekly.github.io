Driving + Innovation: Microsoft Student Day 2.0 and the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2012

Friends, as you already know , the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2012 will take place on April 14 in Moscow, within the framework of which a student team will be selected, who with his project will go to represent Russia at the international final in Australia.



My colleague Dmitry Soshnikov ( shwars ) briefly wrote that in addition to the presentations of the teams of the participants of the event, an additional interesting program awaits. Today we are pleased to announce the full program of the event .

In parallel with the performances of the teams, interesting technical and non-technical lectures will be given to the participants of the event, including lectures on the development for Windows 8, Kinect and Windows Phone.

Lectures


  • The future of technology , Alexey Fedorov, Microsoft
  • Career building secrets, advice from HR department , Svetlana Razumova, Microsoft
  • Microsoft Research in Russia - opportunities for students , Elena Pavlova, Microsoft
  • How to turn an idea into a business? , Alexey Klimov, PiratePay
  • Speech to the investor , Gaidar Magdanurov, Microsoft
  • Introduction to Metro-design , Konstantin Kichinsky, Microsoft
  • Building balanced teams , Dmitry Stepanov, Poster
  • Master class on PR , Alexander Milkus, Komsomolskaya Pravda


Technical sections


  • Windows 8 application development
  • Windows Phone Application Development
  • Programming for Kinect
