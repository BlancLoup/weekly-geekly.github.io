How to flash:
There are official firmware (you can find them on w3bsit3-dns.com or slatedroid), but there is neither a market, nor work with Google accounts.
But in the unofficial firmware all this joy is there, and we will put it.

We unpack, throw it on the card (no more than 4GB necessarily, otherwise there is a high degree of getting a brick), insert the card into the switched off tablet, press M + Power and hold until the start of the firmware.
