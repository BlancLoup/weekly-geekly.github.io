The new issue of the magazine "Radio Annual" number 35 (2015)



The FLProg project has been fruitfully friendly for quite some time now with the editors of Radio Yearbook. This is due to the common interest and target audience. The magazine has a practical focus and covers issues at the interface of programming, modern microprocessor technology and classical analog-digital electronics.
The topic of the next 35th issue is: Magazines on Electronics - 2014. This review presents 21 periodicals on electronics, published in Russian for 2014. For each of them, the summary thematic content of all numbers for 2014, information on the website of the publication, terms of purchase and subscription is presented. As it turned out from this information, many interesting journals are issued in electronic form, and only simple registration is required for downloading, the prices for the rest of these days are low.

A number of publications on electronics for the PE-35 presented their most interesting articles on a wide variety of topics, which are reproduced in full. There is something to read!

From my point of view, this issue is most convenient to use as a reference book, in which it is convenient to look for an answer to your questions. Analogues of such a collection of information in one place today does not exist.

By agreement, Radio Yearbook also regularly publishes news from the FLProg project. At the end of this release my article was published with an overview of the new features of the program and FLProg.ru website.

Releases of the Radio Yearbook are distributed online without restrictions, and the official version in PDF format can be downloaded for free on the RadioLocman website.
