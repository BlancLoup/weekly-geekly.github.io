Chelyabinsk meteorite: systematized photo and video materials

February 15, 2013 in the Urals, a meteorite fell. Today Chelyabinsk meteorite - if not the most famous, then one of the most famous. An incredible huge amount of photo / video material has played a significant role in such a big fame.


A little about myself - I am a little interested in astronomy, and it suddenly became interesting to me to calculate the elements of the meteoroid orbit based on the photo and video. But for me it was a problem to find a large number of videos. The fact is that when calculating orbits for more accurate results, as many measurements as possible should be taken into account.
On youtube, I found less than five videos that could be put into action, but the problem is that they were not tied to the site, that is, the coordinates of the shooting location were not given. And then I came across the work of one enthusiast who helped me a lot.
His name is Nikita Loskutov ( vk.com ). Actually the result of the work:

Catalog of photos / videos of Chelyabinsk meteorite with shooting coordinates:
http://fotki.yandex.ru/users/ChelyabinskFall

On 09/29/2013 there are more than 300 photos / videos, 182 survey points with coordinates.

The lists of materials of the ChelyabinskFall catalog are here:
http://vk.com/chelyabinskfall - group VKontakte
and here:
http://chelyabinskfall.ya.ru/index_blog.xml - Blog

To view all of these materials with reference to the map by coordinates - GoogleEarth KMZ
ChelyabinskFall_182.kmz
http://vk.com/doc201369230_224635577

ChelyabinskFall_182.doc-MS Word document with a list of all observation sites with coordinates and links to the photo / video.
http://vk.com/doc201369230_224638569

The main feature of this work is the presence of coordinates for each observation, and in this collection you can find a lot of beautiful photos. Enjoy!
I am very glad that there are enthusiasts who spend a lot of time creating such things, because this is part of our history and science. On the other hand, social networks have greatly helped in this project.
