GetTaxi (Gett) will expand the list of services from July

The head of GetTaxi, Shahar Weiser, on his Facebook page, stated that since July 2015, the company has been diversifying its own services and, through a single application, will provide not only a taxi order service, but also food, as well as manicure or massage, as well as any home call service, from cleaning (cleaning) to plumbing.



In an interview with RBC, Weiser explained that for four years the company planned to develop a service capable of satisfying all the daily needs of its own customers.

All the new services of the company will be merged into the application under the common brand Gett, which the company has already produced a rebranding of the company and it is available to users in Moscow under a new name. The main directions of the order will be five:
  • Nutrition
  • Beauty Services
  • Home Services
  • Health Services
  • Passenger Transportation

How Gett will determine and form tariffs for services is still unknown. New services in the updated application will be available to all users of GetTaxi - today it is more than 10 million people in 32 cities of the world. Of these, 2 million - in Moscow and St. Petersburg, in which the company operates.

Weiser emphasizes that, as with the taxi service, a single call center will work for new services, where customers can turn to all questions.
