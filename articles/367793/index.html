Samsung has built-in wireless charging for smartphones in the monitor

When I come to work, the first thing I do is connect an Android smartphone to the Apple all-in-one to make it work as long as possible during the day. Samsung suggests removing wires from this process: a new wireless charger is built into the new SE370 monitor .


Samsung

To use the system, the smartphone must support Qi wireless charging standard - for example, this is the Samsung Galaxy S6 and LG G3. For the Galaxy S5, Samsung has released a removable back panel with support for Qi technology.

It is enough for the user to put the device on a special area in the monitor stand. LED signal confirms charging.

The company offers monitors in two diagonal versions - 23.6 and 27 inches. Resolution - 1920 x 1080 pixels. Viewing angle - 178 degrees. Brightness - up to 300 cd / m2.

Samsung does not disclose pricing information.
