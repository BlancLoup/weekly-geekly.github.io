Good and even better: Tesla Model S updated



Tesla Motors Corporation not only creates new models of electric vehicles, but also updates existing ones. So, recently the updated Model S hatchback was introduced. This model received not only an updated design (somewhat more similar to the Model 3 ), but also several important elements. For example, added an unusual air purification system, which is taken into the cabin.

This is a HEPA-filter, the dimensions of which are about 10 times the size of ordinary automotive filters. It can retain 99.97% of all bacteria and pollutants that would enter the salon without cleaning. According to company representatives, this filter is 300 times more efficient than standard filters. Allergens such a system filters 500 times better, and 700-800 times better eliminates elements of smog and viruses.

In a number of company reports, it was mentioned that this cleaning is a reliable means against biological weapons.


It was-become (image: drive.ru)

As for the technical equipment, then nothing has changed much. Power reserve is still more than 500 kilometers, and the hatchback is equipped with two electric motors 503 and 259 horsepower. The battery is set at 90 kilowatt-hours.

There is another interesting change - the company's charger engineers have upgraded the charger, so that the car will be charged faster than before.

As for the charging stations, by the end of the year it is planned to commission five electric gas stations: near Moscow, in St. Petersburg and Tver, on the M9 highway near the border of the Tver and Pskov regions, as well as on the M10 highway near Veliky Novgorod.
