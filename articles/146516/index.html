Facebook generated email addresses to all users

Facebook in its style took and replaced in the profiles (timelines) of users all the addresses of third-party mail services, such as gmail.com or mail.ru, with their addresses in the facebook.com domain. Who previously did not have a facebook.com email address - the system automatically generated it and made it the main one. The previous e-mail address has gone to the depths of the site and is not shown as contact information.

It turns out that it is very simple - change the postal addresses of several hundred million people. The main thing is to act boldly and decisively.

Facebook launched its own mail service in 2010, but it never gained much popularity. In April, the company synchronized email addresses with the timeline, so that the email address of the username @ facebook.com always matches the URL of the personal page facebook.com/username .
