Myvu Points are for iPod video players. Those who are not too happy with their player (mainly due to the display device parameters) may well come in handy. So if you have an iPod Video in your pocket, $ 200, and it is somehow necessary to pass the time at a lecture or in public transport, then Myvu Solo Edition is a great solution.
