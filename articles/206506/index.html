Keyboards


The main ‚Äúweapon‚Äù of traders when working with a trading terminal is usually the keyboard, on which hot keys are configured to optimize the speed of work. This is enough for most merchants, but some want more and use special (often gaming) keyboards.

TraderTools Keyboard

Known among traders keyboard from the company Bionic Trader. Its main advantage in flexibility is that you can take an empty keyboard, add the necessary buttons to it (there is a special catalog ), program the controllers and get a unique tool that suits you.



Cost: $ 295

TradeMaster

Excellent keyboard for professional traders - nothing superfluous, just the necessary keys for giving orders and reacting to the market situation. For example, with the help of the Turn key, a trader can change the direction of his position with one touch - make the long one short , and vice versa.



Price: on request from the manufacturer

WEY MK06 / RAY06

Professional keyboard from Wey Technologies . Thanks to this device, a trader can control several computers simultaneously, analyze trading information displayed on the touchscreens, and thanks to modularity, the keyboard can be configured differently.



Price: on request from the manufacturer

Optimus popularis

Used by some traders and the famous keyboard from the Studio of Artemy Lebedev. At the top of the keyboard is a small display on which you can display any information, for example, stock price charts. Another plus - the keys can display absolutely any characters, from the letters of the alphabet to special characters, codes and images.



Price: 41 500

Not really the keyboard in the usual sense of the word. Convenience settings allows you to use this module to manage the trading terminal.



Price: 1900
