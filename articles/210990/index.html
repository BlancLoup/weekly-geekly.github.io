LoveQA. Badoo's first mini-conference for testers

UPD: Photos from the conference in FB and Vkontakte .

All last year we wrote a lot on Habr and told at conferences about how we have a testing process in Badoo, how we roll out releases twice a day and about many other things.

We decided to start this year with our small mini-conference - LoveQA (LoveQA) , where we would like to invite specialists who are interested in hearing about test automation, mobile application testing, security, and much more. We would also be interested to talk with colleagues from other companies, answer questions and find out how testing is arranged for them.

In addition to presentations that you have already heard at conferences, we have prepared for you several completely new reports.
We want to make a small cozy conference, and call about 120 people.
The program includes interesting reports, coffee breaks and lunch, a tour of the Badoo office.
We will do broadcast and video reports. Stay tuned for hashtag news: #loveqa
