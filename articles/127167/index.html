Apple Developers Community # 8. School iOs first release

The eighth meeting of the Apple Developers Community will be held on September 2 at 19:00 in St. Petersburg.

The goal of our meetings is the development of those who are already developing for the iPhone and iPad, as well as attracting new developers. To implement it, we collect monthly meetings aimed at obtaining theoretical and practical knowledge.

If you remember, in June we talked about recruiting people to the free school of iOs developers. The summer has passed, September is coming and the training is coming to an end. Half a dozen developers have already received the final tasks in which they need to develop their own application for the iPhone. We decided that our two main initiatives should not be divided and united the eighth meeting with the graduation of the school.

Total, on ADCSpb # 8 , besides the already familiar reports, there will be speeches by graduates with presentations of their programs.

Having talked in the previous meeting about the promotion of applications in the App Store, we return to the technical reports.
  • Gregory Buteyko from DataArt will talk about the features of the new Mac OS X 10.7 Lion operating system.
  • In turn, Andrey Pilyugin from iBearSoft will talk about new technologies that he implements in his popular applications all over the world.
Admission is free, you just need to register .

IOs Developers School


For the summer holidays, we organized free developer courses for iPhone and iPad. From July 9 to September 1, a group of 8-10 people had to learn how to program in Objective-C.

The course program was as follows:
  • Objective c
  • Foundation and Memory Management
  • Views and View Controllers
  • Interface creation in Interface builder
  • Table view
  • Interface Development Principles (HIG)
  • Interaction with Web services server, JSON
  • Image view, Web view
  • Application Data Storage
  • Multithreading
  • Useful Utilities Instruments
At the end of the training, each student received a task for the final project - his own application for the iPhone. September 2, we can see the result of their work. Each student will prepare a small presentation and talk about the project and its development.
