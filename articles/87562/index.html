RSS feed converter to FictionBook format

I have to spend considerable time on the way to work and back, what to do during the road? - Correct, read!
And what is interesting to read? - Right, favorite RSS feeds!

This is how a service was born that allows you to convert RSS feeds into a book in FictionBook format.

How it works
Go to the application page, specify the addresses of rss-channels, specify the width of the device screen from which you will read, press the ‚ÄúRun‚Äù button and wait for the conversion results.
It is possible not to make a list of rss-feeds by hand, but to load them from an opml-file.
So that each time you use the service, do not enter the addresses of the tapes or do not load the opml-file, you can specify a list of tapes in the application settings (the application settings are available from the personal account - you will need to register for this).
Specifying the screen width of the device is required in order to convert large images from the tape to the screen size.

Why FictionBook
This format was not chosen by chance - open, lightweight and flexible; there are a lot of applications for different platforms for reading from this format.

Use on health! And at the same time you can criticize, offer your ideas about the service, or just thank :)
