- Yandex. Photos;

And all these services are free! Why do you ask? Idea Ammonization


The word monetization within the Internet community is most often found when they talk about the monetization of any free service. For example, social networks : Odnoklassniko 's monetization or Vkontakte monetization .

I propose to introduce a new term : Amonetisation.

Ammonization is a process when a company, by providing a free service, pays the user for using it.

Sounds crazy? But let's look at the advantages of monetization. As we already understood, every search engine should dream of getting active email accounts, i.e. users who will be in 90% of cases to choose their services. Let's consider as an experimental - Yandex.

So, the task:
It is given - on Mail.ru and Rambler.ru services there are a lot of users who use them ‚Äúout of habit‚Äù, not wanting to know that there is an excellent mail on Yandex.
The task is to lure as many users as possible to Yandex mail.

Decision:
Yandex introduces a new rule - we will list active users of mail on Yandex from 5 to 15 rubles a month to Yandex. Money!

The degree of your activity will be evaluated by the following parameters:
  1. Number of incoming emails per month
  2. Number of sent letters per month
  3. Number of open (read) letters per month
  4. Register emails in social networks (i.e., social networks will be sent to this email), the more social networks - the greater the degree of activity
  5. Your activity in other services Yandex - Maps, Tape, Photos, Search, etc.
  6. Do not use forwarding filters to other addresses or ‚Äúpick up‚Äù mail from other accounts
  7. Using the WEB interface

Thus, we get really active users.

A side positive effect is the development of Yandex.Money, because people have little money in their purses and a natural desire to spend them, and for this they will have to either save a couple of years or replenish their wallet .

How much will such a pleasure for Yandex cost?

Today, Yandex has about 10M mail users, I think that only 1M is really active users who can receive from 5 rubles. Suppose that in the course of such a campaign we will get a doubling of the audience - i.e. 2M active email users with an average activity estimated by us at 10 rubles.

Total, we get 2M * 10 rubles = 20M rubles per month, i.e. about 10M dollars a year.

Net profit Yandex in 2006 amounted to 30M dollars, which allows us to talk about the reality of such figures.

Obviously, you need to take into account the profits from this project, how much we really increase the audience, and we will not just pay to existing users.

Conclusion


Despite all the difficulties and the high cost of the solution, I believe that email amonetisation is the only way for Yandex to get the active email users it needs.
