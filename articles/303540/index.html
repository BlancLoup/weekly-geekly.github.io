NetApp AltaVault Storage Systems

Traditional cloud solutions for backup and data recovery are often not satisfied with enterprises for a number of reasons. NetApp today offers its customers a range of solutions of the Cloud-Integrated Storage class of the AltaVault family.






First, on the reasons why modern enterprises are no longer satisfied with inherited backup and data recovery systems of the on-premise class (located on the local site of the enterprise itself).

1. They are too slow . Users always expect instant recovery and minimal data loss, but legacy backup and recovery strategies no longer keep up with the times.

2. They are too expensive . Volumes of critical corporate data are growing rapidly. Companies use all available means to struggle with the increasing cost of data protection in their on-premise systems. In addition, as data volumes grow, the costs of providing the necessary bandwidth and combating other constraints are becoming more and more significant.

3. They are subject to too many risks . Many organizations still rely on tape storage systems. This approach seems to be imperfect today because of the potential risks of losing some of the data during write-restore, the relatively large downtime of tape systems, and their limited ability to test.

4. They are too complicated . The number of critical enterprise applications that need protection is also increasing. Increases the complexity of the backup architecture for numerous applications. Inherited technologies are also prone to error accumulation. Therefore, traditional local backup systems become unnecessarily complex.

How AltaVault Solves Backup, Backup and Restore Issues

NetApp AltaVault (formerly SteelStore) provides enterprises with reliable and secure backups in the cloud of any type and any provider, while reducing costs by up to 90% compared to local solutions.


Constructive physical device AVA 400/800

AltaVault gives customers the ability to take full advantage of the cloud economy while protecting their already made investments in corporate backup infrastructure and complying with agreements on the quality of backup and recovery services (Service Level Agreements, SLAs).

How AltaVault Efficiency Is Ensured

Due to the best in the data compression industry . AltaVault uses on-the-fly deduplication and compression, which provides data compression of up to 30: 1. This means that the enterprise stores less data in the cloud in real volume and can move it faster.

By optimizing the network and the cloud . Built-in WAN optimization reduces the amount of transported data, which speeds up the time of data exchange with the cloud up to four times. Thus, AltaVault intelligently squeezes traffic even more, which saves money and time. At the same time, the specified quality of services (Quality of Services, QoS) ensures that the data moves to / from the cloud at the speed required by the business.

Due to the rapid data recovery . AltaVault improves recoverability, since 95% of the data remains in the local cache. With its intelligent prefetching, AltaVault recovers data from the cloud within minutes. So, with AltaVault, customers can recover their data up to 32 times faster than with tape drives.

Using NetApp AltaVault gives businesses the following benefits.

1. Corporate scaling on an open platform

Flexible deployment and scaling . The client chooses the most appropriate deployment option - cloud (Azure, AWS, etc.) virtual (VMware, Hyper-V, etc.) or physical (AVA).

The AltaVault family of devices starts with just 2 TB (the youngest virtual model AVA-v2) and expands to 57 PB (the older physical model AVA-800) protected data in the cloud. This makes AltaVault solutions the most scalable, integrated with the cloud data storage (cloud-integrated storage) on the market.
