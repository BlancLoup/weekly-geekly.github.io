Gadget for catching fish from places where it really is

Winter, speak? And what's stopping you in winter fishing to fish? What do you think is the purpose of the thing that is shown in this photo?



Last week, already familiar with my advertising tests, the company ‚ÄúDadzhet‚Äù pleased me with another useful thing, which I gladly agreed to test. This is a wireless echo sounder. The echo sounder is a smart sonar that can be connected to a phone or tablet. The connection takes place via Bluetooth and is possible for any device based on Android and IOs






The device is about the size of a golf ball and weighs about 50 grams.



In fact, it is a float. But a sonar is built into this float and it has a connection with a smartphone. What is remarkable, he is even able to inform you about the bite.



Brief characteristics

Depth of action: up to 41 meters
Distance from the smartphone: up to 30 meters
Battery life: up to 8 hours in active mode
Standby time: a few weeks
Weight: 47g
Measurement System: Metric and English
Power Source: Battery 3.7 V
Sensor type: Single-beam sonar 118 kHz
Viewing angle: 90 degrees
Bluetooth Version: Bluetooth Smart 4.0

Application for iOS: works with iOS from version 6.0
Application for Android: works with Android version from 4.3, if the smartphone / tablet supports Bluetooth 4.0

This echo sounder works very simply:

We tie it to the end of our fishing line.





And we make a cast.



After entering the water, the echo sounder turns on automatically and immediately starts sending via Bluetooth data with depth, temperature and fish data, synchronizing with the device and outputting data in a special application.



When the manufacturer declared 30 meters of work range, he worked perfectly for me and on 50 meters. Further than 55-60 m, the echo sounder worked intermittently. However, this distance is quite enough to find the most promising place for fishing. The echo sounder shows both the presence of fish and the depth at the sites and the structure of the bottom ...



Slowly stretching the echo sounder, you can watch the change in the depth and topography of the bottom. It is easy to find the edge, flat places or koryazhnik, in which the greatest density of fish is concentrated.

Previously, for this I used a marker, with the help of which the bottom tediously rattled the bottom in order to find the most promising place for fishing. Now, with the help of such an echo sounder, this is really the last century. The picture is more than visual, we immediately see a cluster of fish, we save a lot of time to study the bottom, and most importantly, we increase the effectiveness of the catch several times.



Cats always feel who today will not remain without a catch and will be ready to share. How do they do it? )





In addition to this, the echo sounder has several interesting features, such as a green LED beacon for night fishing, a bite alert system Strike Alert, and an automatic reconnect system that resumes communication even after the sonar has been tapped under water.



This echo sounder can be used both during fishing from the shore and during fishing from a boat.



As a test of this echo sounder and for understanding the principle of operation, this time I fished at sea from the pier. Next week I plan to go with him to the lake and with his help I will try to catch trophy carp. So wish me luck) I am sure that with this device my chances will increase many times!

I fished on January 8 in Sevastopol Bay on the Black Sea.
