A good bank will make a blog and talk about how its services work in life. Tinkoff Credit Systems has launched its own online magazine .

To advertise a mile card, Tinkoff Magazine first names the typical problems of such products , and then works in contrast. What does all of this mean


Rules to help make good branded content:

  • Determine what benefits your product brings to a particular person in everyday life and write about it.
  • Deserve trust and authority, show your expertise.
  • Write articles on related topics.
  • Attract third-party authors. They are not busy with your business every day and will not confuse the company's internal problems with the problems of the buyer.
  • Write yourself because you have experience and know your stuff. And this is a reason to trust you.
  • Feel free to talk simply, joke and be sincere.
