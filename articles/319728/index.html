MIT launched Moral Machine: an experiment to study how unmanned vehicles should be delivered

MIT decided to contribute to the creation of unmanned vehicles in an interesting way - to solve the moral and philosophical question of how an unmanned vehicle should act in a given situation .


The interest of this study is also in the fact that many voters have no rights, and therefore do not know the traffic rules, which will contribute to the results.

The main essence of the test is to find out what is more priority for people or what aspects an unmanned vehicle must take into account:

1) Number of victims (Go straight without breaking traffic rules and kill 4 or break traffic rules and kill one)
2) Save passengers or save pedestrians
3) Effect of red traffic light
4) The importance of people and animals
5) People with obesity \ tucked up people

After passing the test, they will show you how other people vote.

I do not want a test, I want to see the result!
