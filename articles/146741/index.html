Google Now cards

Google published a presentation of the new Google Now service, which was announced at the Google I / O conference as part of the Android 4.1 operating system.



Google Now is a personal search engine that processes voice and text requests, providing information taking into account the user's current GPS coordinates, personal information from the calendar, search history, navigation history, browsing history, and so on. The search widget is on the start screen.

From the user's point of view, the results of such a search are displayed in the form of ‚Äúcards‚Äù (cards): there are only ten types of them developed so far, but new ones will appear in the future. The user can customize the cards to your taste and remove unnecessary.

In theory, the ribbon from the cards is great for a constant update on the monitor of Google Glass glasses, perhaps there it will work that way.

Card List


Weather

In the morning when you turn on the phone Google shows the weather outside the window, as well as in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwork.


Traffic

The route is calculated on the basis of information about the current location of the user and his travel history. The card pops up when it is time to leave (according to the time of the usual schedule of the day), and also if the trip actually began.


Public transport

Shows the schedule of buses, trams and subway trains, if you are approaching a public transport stop.


Next meeting

Card pops up before the meeting (from the calendar). It shows the route to the meeting place and the time is calculated on the way. The Android device will notify you when it is time to leave so that the user does not miss the meeting and arrives on time.


Flights

Displayed for users who have already searched for this information. Shows the schedule, flight status and weather conditions.


Sport

Results of matches live - for sports teams from the history of search queries.


Places

Information about nearby bars, restaurants and other places of entertainment, based on data about the location of the user, search history and travel history.


Transfer

Displayed if the user is in another country.


Currency

Displayed if the user is in another country.


Home time

Displayed if the user is in a different time zone.
