Art and Information Technology

The development of Russian culture is impossible without a clear understanding of the importance of modern information technologies and the opportunities that they represent today. Competent presentation and availability of information on the Internet, about important events and cultural figures helps to create a positive image of academic art, expands and simplifies access to creative resources.

With the help of the Internet, you can avoid the issue of empty halls, because it is often with the help of search engines that people search for the artist they are interested in, and perhaps they are you! Also, your audience will know in time about the place and time of your next concert or theater performance.

Previously, one could complain about the information vacuum, sometimes not allowing even the most ingenious creations to reach the public. But now, interested subscribers, in a few seconds will hear the news about you, and your planned performances. Seats at the concert are booked with one click of the mouse. And you know in advance whether today there will be a full house. All this is more than realistic, if you, of course, made sure that this information was on the World Wide Web.

Information support of art with the help of modern technologies and electronic media, texts that can hold the attention of the Internet user - all this contributes to a balanced development of fundamental art in the regions of Russia and the awakening of public interest.
