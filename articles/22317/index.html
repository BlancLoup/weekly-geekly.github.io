Coworking Center "Vulik". Kiev. Ukraine

The advantages of coworking have been discussed repeatedly and confirmed not only by the positive experience of foreign colleagues, but also by a keen interest in this form of association on the part of Russian-speaking freelancers. Until today, we all, unfortunately, were only able to theoretically evaluate all the positive aspects of the office 2.0. Our team made every effort to change this situation. So, get acquainted: "Vulik" is the first co-working center in the CIS.


We very carefully approached the choice of place, taking into account the numerous wishes of freelancers, current trends and prospects. "Vulyk" is located 5 minutes from the central bus station, 2 minutes walk from the future metro station, directly opposite the Goloseevskaya park area. The presence of a developed infrastructure makes it possible, if necessary, to completely eliminate tiresome trips, as there are a lot of cafes in the park area (discounts are expected for the summer season), the Mir Hotel, the Velikka Kyshenya supermarket, a gym, a hairdressing salon, a medical center. Centre. The windows of the main hall overlook a cozy, quiet, well-kept courtyard, on the territory of which a ping-pong table will be installed, and the presence of an external Wi-Fi access point will allow you to work in the open air in warm weather.
