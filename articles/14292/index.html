Boombox in sneakers


BoomShoe - a new concept of the new boombox. The most unusual thing is that this BoomShoe is installed in a ... sneaker! BoomShoe is able to play audio files thanks to the built-in sneaker CD player, twitter and speakers. In addition, provided and navigation through the tracks.
There are play, pause, forward, back and stop buttons. In addition, the shoe, if you can call it that, has a small built-in LCD display and has some built-in memory, the amount of which is not known.
Unfortunately, this is only a concept so far and this model is not planned to enter the market in the near future. But, I think, there would be a demand for this model.
