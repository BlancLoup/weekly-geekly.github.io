Twitter introduces trend personalization



Many of the representatives of the community have Twitter accounts. Who is for work, who is for communication, and who is for that and for the other. In general, this microblogging service is getting better, it is clear that the developers are trying. But some things, even small things, are still incomplete. Well, for example - the most popular topics of the day, trends. Most users who live, for example, in the CIS, almost all Twitter trends are uninteresting. Exceptions are, for example, when Russian-speaking users draw their own trends. Now the situation has to change - Twitter introduces personalized trends.

Personalization will depend on two factors - your current location, and those you follow. The system will analyze these two factors for each of the users, and display current trends. In general, personalization will be as complete as possible. Developers have provided and option to disable personalization - if desired, this can be done without difficulty. In addition, you can and see the trends for a location other than yours.

However, personalized trends are set by default. So far, this feature is not available to all users, developers will gradually "roll out" the update for the entire system. Users will be notified when connecting to a new feature. By the way, the update applies to both the web version and mobile clients.
