The government of the Russian Federation can reduce up to 40% of budget places in universities next year. The problem is that the Ministry of Education and Science does not have money to pay for admission of students to the first year, writes Gazeta.ru. Budget cuts also lead to a reduction in scholarships for undergraduate and graduate students. By 2019, 10.3 thousand researchers of universities, the Russian Academy of Sciences and the Kurchatov Institute can remain without work.

This is exactly what will happen if the Ministry of Finance leaves in force a reduction in budget allocations for such state programs as Education Development and Science and Technology Development. The government approved the main ways of optimizing state programs on July 7. Total budget expenditures at the same time it was decided to freeze at the level of 15.78 trillion rubles. per year for the years 2017-2019.

The Ministry of Education and Science claims that such a decision will lead to a real reduction in spending on the needs of science and education in Russia. For example, the state program ‚ÄúDevelopment of Education‚Äù was underfunded last year, in 2016 it will be reduced by 11.5% of its passport value. And in 2017, the sequestration of the educational program will amount to 23.4% of the passport of the program. Reductions will continue in 2018, 2019, when expenses will decrease by 28.5% and 35.2%, respectively. In 2019, the share of expenditure on education in the total budget expenditures will fall from 2.75% in 2015 to 2.45% in 2019.

The lack of funds to finance the state program "Development of Science and Technology" will lead to the need for large-scale layoffs among scientists. The share of the scientific state program in the total budget expenditures is reduced from 0.98% in 2015 to 0.87% in 2019.

By 2019, from the payroll sheet of the FANO, the Ministry of Education and Science proposes to remove about 8.3 thousand researchers. In 2017, 3.5 thousand people may already be fired. Next year, 1.5 thousand people will be dismissed from the Kurchatov Institute.

The department is not limited to cuts in budget places. The number of supported scientific research, development activities of the scientific and technical base will be reduced, subsidies for government assignment to educational institutions of all levels will be reduced.

Significantly less money is allocated for training WorldSkills professional skills championships. The Ministry of Finance provides for the allocation of 200.8 million rubles a year for these needs. The Ministry of Education and Science believes that supporting interest in blue-collar occupations, the introduction of an independent qualification system and basic vocational training centers will cost 3.6 billion rubles. in 2017, 4.8 billion - in 2018 and 10.5 billion rubles. - in 2019

The Ministry of Education and Science at the government meeting demanded not only not to cut the budget for the needs of science and education, but even to double the cost of some budget items. For example, additional funding for the development of Moscow State University, St. Petersburg State University, the Higher School of Economics, the Crimean and Sevastopol universities is proposed to increase by 16.9 billion rubles. three years above approved expenses.

Provided that the faculty salaries are indexed, additional expenses will increase by 119.7 rubles from 2017 to 2019. Otherwise, the presidential task of raising public-sector wages to 200% of the average salary in the region will be unfulfilled.

In the same 2017, the postgraduate scholarships were allocated two times less than required.

Unfortunately, the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Education could not reach an agreement at the meeting.
