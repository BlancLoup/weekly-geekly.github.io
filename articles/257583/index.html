Ways to Improve Reference Profile Performance

Recently, all search engine optimization experts have been actively discussing the details of the future Minusinsk algorithm of Yandex. In this article I would like to structure a number of facts and propose strategies for improving reference profiles.



In mid-April (after the Minusinsk algorithm was announced), some sites received notifications about too many SEO links. What could have served as a catalyst for the triggering of the algorithm and which sites came under these warnings?

The MegaIndex team analyzed over 3000 sites to which we have access to Yandex Webmaster. Among them are sites that are both promoted in MegaIndex and never advanced. There are also sites that use other ALTWeb Group services ( PFLink.ru , SERPClick.ru , etc.)

As I wrote earlier , MegaIndex has sufficiently updated the algorithm for purchasing links, which affected a significant change in the reference weight and parameters of the reference profile. Among the sites that are advancing in MegaIndex, no more than 2% of the sites received notifications from Yandex. This is significantly less when compared with commercial sites that are not promoted in the system.

This was due to several reasons:
1. About 20% of project references were purchased on new resources in the last 30 days.
2. In selecting donors, we significantly tightened the criteria for evaluating spamming:
- the ratio of the number of unique external links to the number of documents on the site
- parameters of the sites to which the donor refers (see the HITS algorithm)
3. Most of our customers are actively using technology to improve the quality of reference profiles.

Reinforcing links

It is already obvious that the impact of links in search rankings will increase significantly. Yandex is trying to make the signal from external links less noisy, which will increase its influence in the ranking formula (this is an unavoidable measure, as the machine learning algorithm will notice the correlation of high-quality sites and high-quality reference profiles)

Some sites with a large mass of poor-quality links will gradually reduce their number, which will definitely increase the impact of the remaining links in the whole market. However, it is obvious that the removal of a significant proportion of external links will lead to irreparable problems for sites:
a. Decrease position in Google and Yandex
b. Reduced search traffic
