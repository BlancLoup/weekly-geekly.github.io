Al Qaeda: Hi-Tech and the Information War

It is not only about the most advanced computer hardware, Sony laptops, digital video cameras and strong cryptography using PGP, but also about the unique methods of online propaganda. Some of them are know-how in the field of hacking social networks. A good example of manipulation is the actions of one of the leaders of the organization, Ayman Zavahiri , for which Americans give $ 25 million for his head.
