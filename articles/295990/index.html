Divide and Conquer: the biggest event in the history of Hewlett-Packard since 2002

Hewlett-Packard ( HP ) in the past was the largest manufacturer of PCs and servers in the world. That legendary company that existed until now is no more. The process of dividing HP into two independent businesses is complete.

New HP Inc. received all the assets for the development and production of personal computing systems and printers. Hewlett Packard Enterprise got the entire HP corporate business. For HP, these changes have become the biggest since the takeover since it bought Compaq with 63,700 employees in 2002 for $ 25 billion.

HP Inc. Already announced the listing of Fortune 100 public companies. The head of the company was President and CEO Dion Weisler, who held the post of vice president of Printing and Personal Systems in the former company.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise will focus on the development and production of enterprise IT systems. The company in the status of CEO will be headed by Meg Whitman (Meg Whitman), the former CEO of Hewlett-Packard. Its shares began trading on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) on Monday, November 2.

The existing Hewlett-Packard logo was inherited by HP Inc. In addition to laser and inkjet printers for business and home, laptops, desktop and tablet PCs, workstations, she also plans to develop the legacy patent portfolio in the field of 3D printing and other promising technologies.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise received a new logo.
