Megamind wrote that in February Yandex accused Google of violating fair competition in the mobile systems market and filed a complaint with FAS. After that, the FAS filed a lawsuit against the offender.

According to Yandex, the company violates Article 14 of the Law on Protection of Competition, forcing manufacturers of mobile devices installing the Android operating system to refuse to pre-install competitors' applications.

Last year, three manufacturers of smartphones based on Android OS ( Fly , Explay and Prestigio ) stopped performing the installation of Yandex services on their devices. The reason alleged were the special conditions of the contract with Google. However, the search giant persistently denied accusations.

The trial was to be held in April. However, Google was able to achieve the transfer of the court hearing on May 25. This was due to the fact that the company did not have time to prepare all the necessary documents.
