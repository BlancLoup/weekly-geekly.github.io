ISTQB certification: benefits and features

The success of an IT project depends largely on how well the testing and quality assurance system (Quality Assurance, QA) is organized at all stages of its life cycle.

For a QA specialist, one of the most reliable ways to prove your professional skills is the availability of an international ISTQB certificate. Today we will talk about what such certification gives to an employee, an employer and a business, as well as how we at SimbirSoft support the qualification of QA engineers.

What is ISTQB?


ISTQB (International Software Testing Qualifications Board) is an international organization that certifies QA specialists. Today it is the most authoritative certification system.

As of December 2018, ISTQB has conducted more than 875,000 examinations and has issued over 641,000 certificates that recognize IT companies in 126 countries around the world. Different countries have their own representative offices of this organization, for example, in Russia it is RSTQB.

Despite the territorial "variation", the materials for the preparation, the number of questions and the duration of the exam are the same for everyone. In this case, certification is carried out only in person, in the presence of an examiner from the ISTQB representative office.

The benefits of employee certification for IT companies and customers


When choosing a contractor for an IT project, customers estimate different parameters, including deadlines and cost of work, availability of relevant cases, etc. If QA specialists of an IT company are certified according to ISTQB standards, all other things being equal, this could be an additional competitive advantage for the company.

ISTQB certificates are quoted worldwide, so an IT company with a staff of certified employees can also participate in international tenders.

In addition to the obvious competitive advantages, there are other positive results of certification:

  • All experts who have been trained and passed the exam, have common terminology. The team members speak the same language, there are no problems due to a misunderstanding of each other.
  • Evaluation of the results is impartial, since knowledge is verified by third-party independent experts, for whom Ivanov, Petrov and Sidorov are equal.
  • Certification provides an opportunity to assess in which areas the employee understands the best, and what knowledge should be developed.
