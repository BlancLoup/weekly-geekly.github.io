The Humble Indie Bundle # 2 - "pay what you want" for 5 games



On Habr√© already wrote about The Humble Indie Bundle, the sale of indie games under the slogan "pay how much you want." Today launched the second such action; The kit includes 5 games: Braid, Cortex Command, Machinarium, Osmos and Revenge of the Titans.

You can pay by using Paypal, Google Checkout and Amazon Payments. You also choose which part of the money will go to the developers, which is for charity: EFF and Child's Play (a charitable organization founded by the authors of the Penny Arcade comic book and distributing toys to children's hospitals) and which auction organizers. There is still an opportunity to buy a gift code and (who would doubt) give someone for the New Year, for example.

All games run on Windows, Mac and Linux and are not burdened by DRM. Games have already slipped on the habr and in the description, perhaps, do not need, but still:
  • Braid : "Mario on steroids" crossed with the Prince of Persia. Platform with gameplay tied to time management. Several worlds with a completely diverse "physics of time", this game alone is worth the purchase of the entire set, if not tried.
  • Cortex Command : You play as a giant brain that can control humanoid robots and must defend in order to survive. Remotely similar to Worms, a bunch of weapons, realistic physics, a peculiar, but fascinating damage model and multiplayer.
  • Machinarium : quest from the creator of Samorost. That says it all. Oh yeah, another cool robot.
  • Osmos : we play as a spherical organism, which moves according to the principle of jet motion. We encounter smaller organisms - we devour them, with large ones - they devour us. There are organisms with AI, anti-organisms and attracting spheres (thanks, belk )
  • Revenge of the Titans : tower defense with elements of the RTS and the evil aliens.

It is worth noting that Cortex Command and Revenge of the Titans are in the process of being developed, and by purchasing the beta version now, you will receive all further updates for free.

At the moment, in less than a day, more than 25 thousand copies have been bought, the average purchase price is $ 6.87. Sale will last 7 days.
