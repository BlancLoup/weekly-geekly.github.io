"Building blocks" for corporate networks

The Huawei Quidway Sx700 series switches provide networking of various configurations and complexity that meet the requirements of the IT infrastructure of modern enterprises.

Huawei has long established itself worldwide as one of the leading manufacturers of telecommunications equipment, but for several years now the company has been actively developing its corporate business. This development is facilitated by R & D, which are engaged in more than 45 thousand employees, patenting developments, changes in the organizational structure.

In the past five years, Huawei has declared itself in the corporate sector - the strategic direction of Huawei Enterprise - as a supplier of optical data transmission devices, routers and switches, GSM-R equipment, Telepresence solutions. In 2010, the company entered the world market with this equipment, announced a cloud computing strategy and the development of a cloud platform. Currently, Huawei's corporate business continues to grow rapidly. As expected, Huawei revenues in the corporate segment by 2015 will reach 15 billion dollars.

Huawei is counting on substantial business growth in the Russian corporate segment. Over the years of work in the domestic telecommunications market, it has earned recognition from domestic telecom operators and Internet companies: companies such as Rostelecom, VimpelCom buy its equipment, including switches, in large quantities, which indicates the high quality and reliability of equipment.

For example, in the data center of one large Internet company, more than 1,000 Huawei S5700 switches and over a dozen S9312 switches are deployed, featuring high-performance and high-density ports. Developing its strategy for the corporate market, the company released a new series of Sx700 switches, and its business model now includes both the introduction of corporate solutions through its business partners and the independent implementation of projects at the system integrator level, using its experience and capabilities.

All modern Huawei equipment supports cloud solutions, and the company's cloud platforms are focused both on telecom operators and enterprises - to create a corporate private cloud. In addition, the company customizes its solutions for specific industries.

New Sx700 Ethernet Switch Series

Huawei's high-performance Quidway S family switches, designed for enterprise networks of various sizes, are designed for use at the access, aggregation and campus core levels, as well as for installation in modern virtualized data centers.

These devices, characterized by advanced functionality and energy efficiency, have common architectural and structural components, including the VRP (Versatile Routing Platform) operating system, the command line interface, and power modules. The Huawei VRP universal routing platform software meets the requirements for accessing a variety of services on Ethernet networks. The Sx700 uses energy-saving integrated circuits to ensure uniform heat dissipation. This allows you to do without fans, make the device more reliable and economical. Free ports go into sleep mode, reducing power consumption.

Characteristic features of this family of models include support for IPv4 / IPv6 protocols, which allows them to be used in IPv4 networks, IPv6 networks or networks that use both protocols, non-blocking switching mode, the ability to form stacks, hot backup of the main components, ease of installation and operation, availability versions with PoE and PoE +.



The Quidway Sx700 switch line includes devices of the S1700, S2700, S3700, S5700, S6700, S7700 and S9700 series. Characteristic features of this equipment include advanced security features that include protection against DoS attacks; DHCP snooping based on the analysis of MAC and IP addresses, lease times for IP addresses, VLAN IDs and user access interfaces; protection against attacks using the ARP protocol; centralized MAC address authentication and 802.1x authentication.

To ensure the quality of service (QoS), traffic is classified using information contained in packets, such as 5-tuple, IP-preference, ToS, DSCP, type of IP protocol, type of ICMP, source port TCP, VLAN ID, type of protocol Ethernet, class of service CoS.

Other protocols and features implemented by Quidway S switches include Enhanced Trunk channel duplication technology; Smart Ethernet Protection Protocol; ring network protocol used on the Ethernet link layer; forming a group of devices based on the VRRP protocol.

To manage the switches and their administration, the SNMP v1 / v2 / v3 protocols, the program line interface, and the Web NMS, Telnet, and HGMP protocols are used. There are automatic configuration mode, support for plug-and-play functionality, deployment using a USB flash drive and remote upgrade.

The Intelligent Stack feature (iStack) allows you to combine several Sx700 switches into a stack that works as a single virtual switch and is managed by a single IP address. In this case, the backup switch assumes the load when the main switch fails, minimizing the interruption of services. iStack increases scalability, improves network reliability and simplifies its architecture.

These and many other features make it possible to use the Huawei Quidway Sx700 series switches as universal building blocks for creating various configurations of new generation networks that meet the requirements of the IT infrastructure of modern enterprises.

Access Switches S1700

S1700 Series devices are access switches with 8-48 Gigabit ports supporting optical SFP modules. Their hardware provides high performance, support for a wide range of functions and capabilities to create a secure, reliable and high-speed network. Easy to install and maintain, the S1700 switch is suitable for building networks of small and medium enterprises, can be used in Internet cafes, in hotels and schools.

S2700 Series Switches: Flexible Service Management

The S2700 series switches also include 8, 16, 24, and 48 port models. They are applicable for various scenarios of building a corporate network, simplify installation and maintenance. With flexible VLAN deployments, versatile security and QoS policies, and energy-efficient technologies, the S2700 switches enable corporate customers to build the next generation network. These switches provide 2.7-5.4 million packets per second.

The S2700 1U chassis supports automatic configuration and Plug-and-Play technology. As in the S1700 series, the absence of fans reduces the number of mechanical failures and protects the device from damage caused by condensation of moisture and dust, reducing the amount of maintenance work by 53%.

For flexible service management, port management, and resource scheduling, you can apply ACL rules to a VLAN. The S2700 supports port-based VLAN and MAC address allocation, which is convenient to use in networks with frequent user movements or with high security requirements.

To implement QoS policies, the S2700 switches support sophisticated traffic classification based on VLAN ID, IP, source MAC / IP addresses, destination MAC / IP addresses, priority, or packet port number. By limiting traffic speed based on the results of traffic classification, the S2700 performs transmission at the speed of a line on each of the ports to provide high quality voice, video, and data services.

S7700 and S6700 Intelligent Routing Switches

The Quidway S7700 and S6700 series high-performance intelligent routing switches are capable of aggregating Gigabit channels and transferring 10GbE traffic to devices of the following network layers in large campus networks, can be used to aggregate server traffic into the data center, and be installed as central nodes of medium data centers and relatively small campus networks. The S7700 series consists of the S7703, S7706 and S7712 modular switches with a capacity from 540 to 1320 million packets / s and a maximum backplane capacity of 4.8 Tbit / s. Their modules include, in addition to the access controllers of wireless networks, EPON cards, which simplify the maintenance of passive optical networks.

The 1U switches with fixed configuration ports of the Quidway S6700 series include models with 48 and 24 1 GbE SFP / 10 GbE SFP + ports, USB ports, as well as two connectors for connecting AC power sources. S9700 Switches: Corporate Network Core

Quadway S9700 series terabit routing modular switches are designed for the central segments of high-performance, scalable, managed corporate networks. Devices of this series (models S9703, S9706, S9712) with a capacity from 1440 to 5760 million packages / s provide for the installation of line cards with ports 40 and 100 Gbit / s. The S9700 modules include a wireless LAN controller card.
