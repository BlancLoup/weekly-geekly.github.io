As I understand it, the first part of the presentation was about the introduction of USB 3.0 technology on Gigabyte P55a series motherboards. And about USB 3x Power boost - technology that allows to increase the reliability of the connection and energy efficiency of the ports. And also about Sata 3.0, the greatest effect of the implementation of which will be on the example of arrays of RAID level 0. Also, we talked about the 24-phase VRM-module of the distributed power supply system to ensure high-quality power supply of the CPU for any type of load.

Well, I will say more about the technology Ultra Durable 3 - the thickness of the layers of power and grounding of printed circuit boards is 70 microns.

But the main thing in the presentation was the cooling of motherboards Hybrid Silent-Pipe 2 - this is a combined air and water cooling system with an additional radiator, which contributes to heat dissipation outside the system unit.
