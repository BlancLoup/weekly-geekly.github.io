One island is inhabited by a tribe whose members have only blue-eyed or with brown eyes. A total of 1,000 people live on the island (of which 100 are blue-eyed and 900 brown-eyed). All of them, as one, adhere to a religion that strictly forbids them to know the color of their eyes (they never look at mirrors or water) or discuss the color of the eyes of anyone on the island. Each of them knows the color of the eyes of everyone else, except himself, and if he suddenly finds out the color of his eyes, he is obliged, by his religion, to commit a public ritual suicide in the square in front of the whole island, in the near afternoon.

One day, a traveler from distant countries arrives on the island, and soon becomes friends with all the inhabitants of the island, who completely trust him in everything.

One evening, he convenes them all to say goodbye and thank them for their hospitality. At the end of his speech, he notices, in particular, that for him - and he has blue eyes - it was a pleasant surprise to find a blue-eyed islander in this part of the world.
