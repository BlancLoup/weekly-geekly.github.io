Description of the application itself with partial translation from Estonian


Home page




"Minu asukoht" - the location of the phone
"Auto" - the choice of car
"Parkimiskoht" - selected parking space
This page is the starting point for the user: having determined his location, he will be able to see information about nearby parking lots, and choosing the right one will park and activate the service.

Car selection




You can add a car, erase or change the name.

Parking selection




By default, the nearest parking is shown. You can also locate yourself on the map, and see the services (cost, opening hours) of other parking lots nearby.

Map




Actually here's the map. The circle is your location. Blue squares highlighted the nearest parking, by clicking on them, you can get more detailed information.

Parking




After choosing the parking you like, the parking authorization is made via SMS. To remove the parking set is made on a short mobile parking number. Funds from your account is removed from the calculation of the time difference between the SMS and call.
