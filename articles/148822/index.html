You can play Spelunky in the browser



Have you finished working today? Are you sure? Well, if you have a couple of free hours click here . You play Spelunky in the browser and your productivity is now at zero.

This port was created by Darius Kazemi from Tiny Subversions. He noted that the HTML5 version of Spelunky was made possible thanks to the Gamemaker HTML5 (now GameMaker Studio ), which allows you to compile games created in GameMaker in JavaScript. At the moment there is no sound and added hacks to increase stability, and the delay before running after pressing X is still increased compared to the original - approx. per.
