Return to preinstalled Windows 8

In the article I will describe the procedure for the return of funds for the operating system pre-installed on the laptop that I just purchased, using the example of the recently released Windows 8 as an example. There are plenty of previous versions of information on the internet, including on the official website of laptop manufacturers. Buying a laptop


So, we take the laptop from the store. Be sure to open the box and remove the warranty card from the inside. It is packed in polyethylene along with instructions.

Looks like a little brochure in several languages. Ask the seller to fill out the card and put all the necessary seals and stamps. Without filling out this card, the SC has the right to refuse to return the OS.

It is advisable not to turn on the laptop, because when you first start, there is an initial preparation of the OS for use, which takes considerable time and is absolutely not necessary for us. Moreover, when you first start Windows asks to accept the terms of the license agreement.

In no case should this be done. The SC will again fail if you have already agreed and activated your copy.

Also be sure to have a sticker on the bottom cover of the laptop. Instead of the usual pink label certificate of authenticity now uses a sticker of genuine software, a description of which can be found on the Microsoft website.

The key itself is flashed into the BIOS, which I was able to find out after putting the laptop in here .

Visit service center


You can return the OS within a month from the date of purchase by contacting the service center of Acer's retail partner, if you bought a laptop from him, or to an authorized service center, which in my case is the R-Style Service ( list of SOs in Russia ).

We take with us in the SC a guarantee card, a laptop itself and checks (or other payment documents confirming the fact of purchase). In my opinion, it is better to bring the entire box directly from the store to avoid unnecessary questions.

After the paperwork is issued, a receiving coupon is issued, according to which number you can track the execution of the application.

The approximate formatting time is hard, which is the return procedure, as I was told in the SC, - one or two days.
