Google Nexus 7 - the first look at the first Google Tablet

Who follows the news, probably already aware that on the first day of the Google I / O conference, the search engine presented several new products at once. And one of them has long been rumored on the Internet - about the first branded tablet from Google, which received the unpretentious name Nexus 7 . So, this miracle fell into our hands - look first-look :



The most revolutionary in this device - its price is $ 199 , with a very powerful stuffing (quad-core processor NVidia Tegra 3 , screen 1280 by 800 points and the last Android 4.1 Jelly Bean ).

According to sources on the Internet, Andy Rubin, in an interview, admitted that the gadget is sold at cost. And Google plans to profit from the sale of content: applications, books, music and movies. The main competitor is still the Amazon Kindle Fire tablet, which sells well at the same price and won most of the Android tablet market, but much weaker in terms of hardware.
