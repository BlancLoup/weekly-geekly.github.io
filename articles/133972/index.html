The release of version 0.6.4 led to the blocking of the nodejs.org domain in China

The great Chinese firewall simply blocked access to the nodejs.org domain from the country. This is connected with nothing else, as with the released new version of node.js number 0.6.4. This may seem strange, but the firewall does block all sites containing the numbers 64 and 89. This is due to the unfortunate events that took place in the 1989th year. Then students were shot on Tiananmen Square. The government is trying by any means, even so insane, to limit the access of the modern population to the history of those events.
