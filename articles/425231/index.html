- Who decides who will provide medical care? There are three flight attendants, two doctors and three policemen. Theoretically everyone can and should be able to do this?

And any normal adult, it seems to me, if he does not have adequate training, he will not climb. If there is no doctor, then there is no priority between us and the same policemen. Most often, either the one who first started, that one renders, or very quickly find out who has more experience with certain procedures. We will have more experience with oxygen tanks than with police officers, and with injuries, rather, with them.

No This is a rare case. Depends on the situation. If the question of life and death - we will hold on to the adjacent seats and stay on the floor with the passenger. But in any case, the landing, as a rule, is not so strong and sharp that we flew to the other end of the cabin. To stay on the floor is real, especially since we are familiar.
