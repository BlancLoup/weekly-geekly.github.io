Hackspace in the capital of China, what is it?



Weekend starts - time to take a break from work and work for your pleasure - that is, do your favorite thing. For some it is to create robots, flash microcontrollers, encode copters, and much more - all this can be done in hackspaces - open technical and IT clubs created by enthusiasts in hundreds of cities around the world.

Continuing our series of publications about hackspaces - today we will take a tour of the hackspace in one of the largest megacities of the world in Beijing.

And at the end of the post voiced the winners of the competition for projects on VoCore.

Hackspace in Beijing ( site ) was founded 3 years ago. In the second year of existence, the state joined.

There are about 300 participants in the Hackspace, the minimum membership cost is 100 yuan.

Type of membership depends on the time at which you are going to attend hackspace. Details written in the booklet . But no one checks this - everything remains on the conscience of the participants, the situation is quite informal.

Types of Hackspace Membership:
  • 100 yuan
  • 300 yuan
  • 1000 yuan

Receipts from participants do not cover the costs of hackspaces, additional revenues come from events. Companies such as Lenovo hold their events and pay money for it to hackspace.

Two workshops per week are usually devoted to how to do something with your own hands. Discounts of 70% for members. Plus, the training is conducted to work with the equipment.

Unlike hakspeysov in Shenzhen (about which we told ), where the focus is the embodiment of ideas in the product. In Beijing, hackspace is more a place for communication, development and learning. As a summary

Hackspace in Beijing - is a complete set of equipment, comfortable areas for communication, and for work, a lot of space, a lot of light - come and create with DIY any DIY project.

If you also wanted to visit the hackspace - try searching in your city. If you do not have a hackspace yet, find those like you and think about how to organize. Hakspeys is an amazing phenomenon - this is a place where people, regardless of age, want to work, want to create, want to learn new things, share their experience and knowledge with others - and they like it.
