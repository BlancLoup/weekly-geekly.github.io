Toaster statistics for 2017

Hello everyone from the Toaster team!

The year 2018 has begun, and we want to share with you the results of last year, which turned out to be quite positive and productive for the Toaster.

In 2017, Toaster users asked more than 100 thousand questions and received more than 200 thousand answers to them. Solutions were found for 51% of the questions, that is, every second question on the Toaster finds a solution. But how does all the statistics for the year look like in numbers:

As you can see, most of the questions were devoted to web development, which is not surprising, given its popularity. And the most popular tag turned out to be JavaScript : 13% of all questions were related to it.
