Acer Aspire Timeline 1810T - officially introduced



Acer Aspire Timeline 1810T, announced in early July, was officially unveiled today. Let me remind you that the model should replace the Aspire One 751 and is interesting because it is based on a bunch of Intel GS45 Express + processor Intel ULV SU3500. Due to this, the mini-laptop works much faster than analogs with the Intel Atom.

The main thing that has become known is the price and weight of the new item. The weight of the novelty will be 1.35 kg, and the price, depending on the modification, may be $ 711 or $ 853 (as expected).
