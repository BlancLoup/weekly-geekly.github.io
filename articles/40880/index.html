IPhone sales in Russia start on the night of 2 to 3 October

According to the M.Video press service, iPhone sales in Russia will start at midnight from 2 to 3 October. This date was confirmed by the managers of cellular operators - MTS, Vimpelcom and Megafon, who will buy phones from Apple and sell them independently and through cellular retailers.
As previously reported, the unit price across Russia will be 23 thousand rubles for the iPhone 3G with 8 GB of internal memory and 27 thousand rubles for 16 GB.

The number of pre-orders for one new product collected by the sellers shows that there will be no rush, Vedomosti reports.
