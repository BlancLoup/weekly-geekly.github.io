Monokolis 2018 Market Overview

The rising sun, drops from the southern roofs even on a cold day and the movement of the zero isotherm to the northeast are all signs of the inexorable approaching spring. Soon snow and ice on the sidewalks will replace personal vehicles - bicycles, scooters and monowheels. Now is the time to think about a way to turn a work trip into a pleasant walk. As an enthusiast monowheel, rolling since 2016, I recommend this type of transport, and this review is designed to help you choose the machine that you like the most.

Introduction


Here is a brief story about how the monocolder manufacturers spent 2017, and with which models they meet 2018. I do not deliberately plunge into numerical characteristics - almost all modern non-nome wheels have enough power to drive safely and maximum safe speed above 30 km / h

Necessary disclaimer: I am not associated with any manufacturer or seller of monocovers, advertise none of the models mentioned below, manufacturers or sellers, and present exclusively my opinion based on my own experience and information from specialized forums, chat rooms and websites.

For greater impartiality, manufacturers are listed alphabetically.

Gotway


2017 was a very unfortunate year for Gotway. First of all, in the spring they released a batch of wheels of two models with defects in the controller, because of which, after a small bump, the wheel entered into self-oscillations and dropped the rider. I had to recall and reflash defective devices, which hit the company's image very strongly. Further, on the flat ground, the disappointment was created by the marketers. The name of the new monocolice, Tesla, caused a feeling that Gotway will delight us with something fundamentally new and breakthrough, and it was theoretically possible, given that in 2016 the company became one of the top three. Alas, the novelty was only increased to 16 inches MCM4, to which was attached a retractable handle.


Gotway tesla

Two handles (fixed and retractable) would allow to make a button for conveniently carrying the wheel on, as on the Inmotion V8, but, alas, Gotway engineers were too lazy to realize this possibility.

Contrary to the name, Tesla turned out to be quite an average mono-wheel, which stands out except the largest possible battery (1020 Wh) in the segment of 16-inch models.

Much more avant-garde looks like a 14-inch MCM5 that is expected very soon, the prototypes of which have already appeared in Europe.




Available: a new controller, retractable handle, finally a shutdown button when lifting the wheel and a rather controversial idea of ‚Äã‚Äãventilation holes in the controller compartment. However, the air intakes in the upper part of the body were later versions of the Kingsong KS-18A / S and did not seem to cause serious complaints.

The ten-inch Luffy wheel is modernized and presented under the Mten3 version. The fact is that Luffy was developed by a third party and was produced at the facilities of Gotway. As a result, the design of the wheel contained quite archaic elements that were decided to be corrected in Mten3.


Left Mten3, right Luffy


In principle, they coped with the task, the wheel turned out to be more powerful, with increased water protection, but rather heavy and with a button in an uncomfortable place. The impression spoiled the recall of all models sold in August due to the repetition of the bug with auto-oscillations.

Also available from Gotway are upgraded versions of the 18-inch MSuperV3 wheel, the best (of course, in my opinion) in the design of this diameter in 2016.

Conclusion: Previously, Gotway was known as a manufacturer of fast and powerful wheels with no frills, like music, and managed to acquire its fans. Now the main question is whether they managed to eliminate the problems with the quality of production in 2017.

Inmotion


For the company Inmotion, the year 2017 was quite good. Having released an excellent V8 wheel in the second half of 2016, the company officially entered the American market through the company-inventor of the monowheels Solowheel. V8 received the second name Solowheel Glide 3 and ... increased in price by one and a half times. However, public outrage forced to cancel the last decision, and the price returned to more or less adequate. The characteristics and capabilities of the wheel are perfectly demonstrated by professional acrobat Damien Goma.


From the moment of release, the wheel did not become morally obsolete and is still an interesting option for purchase, differing in stable quality, low weight compared to similar models and excellent design with the most necessary elements - headlight, retractable handle and button that locks the wheel for carrying. The disadvantages include a high price, only one version of the battery, which someone (at least to me) is not enough, and water protection is below average.

In the Inmotion model line there is a 14-inch V5 wheel in various modifications, but it is already morally obsolete - the limit of 25 km / h even for the fastest version now looks too low, the folding handle should be bought separately, and its design does not seem to me successful.

March 17 will be officially announced the model V10 / V10F, which, according to rumors, will be different more battery, wide tire, speaker and brake light, and, alas, more weight. So far, there are only photos that are published to whip up intrigue.



Conclusion: Inmotion is said to have the most advanced production and makes good wheels. If it were not for a limited range, they would, in my opinion, share the pedestal of leaders with Kingsong, but, given the information about the release of the new model, it seems that the company is working on it.

IPS


The IPS company, which some have already buried, pleased us in 2017 with a completely unique model - an ultralight and ultra-compact i5 wheel. The new design made it possible to abandon the pedal pillars, creating almost a folder for papers with a wheel.

However, any technical solution has its price - we had to pay for the lightness and compactness with a small battery, low maximum speed and low engine power. Also in the design revealed a very bad waterproofing. So in the end, the wheel has found quite limited application.

At the end of 2017, it was reported that the s5 model is being prepared for the exit, which will be faster, more powerful and heavier. Also they are going to install two engines on it, but it is not clear whether two motor-wheels are meant, or one engine in the two halves of the hull.



Kingsong


Having released the 16-inch KS-16A wheel with the best design in my opinion, in 2016, KingSong did not begin to fantasize any further, but spread the design to other diameters. In the 16-inch segment, the 16A replaced the 16S (Sport) model in the same package, but with a more powerful engine, an improved algorithm for the controller, new pedals, and an increased maximum safe speed.


On video 16A, but it is the cutest, visual and atmospheric of me known

In the segment of 14-inch wheels, the casing was reduced and made more technologically advanced - plastic-wearing self-tapping screws were replaced with screws. Also fuse carried in a much more easily accessible hatch on the housing. In 2017, we managed to release the base model 14D and the version with a larger battery -14S (in some Russian stores it is designated as -14DS).




In 2018, the budget model is expected -14M with a minimum battery, without speakers and lights, but cheap.

The same case in 2018 increased to a size of 18 inches and called the resulting model -18L. One of the fastest (seriously, 50 km / h after 1000 km) appeared, which is already beyond the limits of a safe for itself and surrounding range) wheels with a very large battery - 1036 Wh and the longest handle in the ruler. Start of production is expected in April.


Conclusion: In my opinion, Kingsong is now the market leader. They offer a large selection of wheels of excellent design of several diameters with batteries of different capacities for different tasks and budget. The design of the wheels has headlights and dimensions, retractable handle, speakers, USB charging (except -16A / S). Also, the models have good water protection, and there were no serious complaints about the quality. The disadvantages include the lack of a button that turns off the wheel when carrying, an unsuccessful new version of the application for smartphones and rumors of a future price increase.

Neinebot


The company Ninebot, having released a revolutionary round design in 2015, was distracted from the mono-wheel market, having engaged, and very successfully, with mini-segways and scooters. In the second half of 2017, it was reported that Ninebot decided to return to the market, while choosing a very unorthodox design. The 18-inch mono-wheel Ninebot One Z in versions Z6, Z8 and Z10, differing in power of the motor and battery, installed an unusually wide tire, and the body shape, according to official developers, was inspired by the Batmobile.




An interesting solution is a retractable handle, the lower part of which serves as a stand. But disputes about the convenience of the selected technical solutions remain theoretical, the production start dates have shifted already in the summer of 2018, i.e. from the announcement to the beginning of the release, almost a year will pass, and this is not a very good sign.

Rockwheel


The company Rockwheel, which several years ago made interesting and very light gear wheels, in 2017 released the model GT16. It differed strange technical solutions like the main power wire, going outside the case, and the first delivery had quality problems. On the second version, you can find positive reviews, but, given the archaic design (seriously, in 2018 to screw the suitcase handle yourself?), It is difficult to consider the GT16 as a competitive alternative.


It is good that the company did not cease to exist at all - you can find, for example, such a concept wheel without pedals.



Also, there was news about the new version with a completely unhealthy top speed of 70 km / h. But in order to rise again to the level of leaders, Rockwheel will have to make considerable efforts.
