New neat three-pane Yandex.Mail for business correspondence interface

Yandex launches Trinity, a new mail interface that saves time in business correspondence.



In the new interface, all letters open on one page, they are immediately grouped by topic. For example, if in the correspondence with the topic ‚ÄúPreparing for a presentation‚Äù there are already many letters and participants, the entire discussion is easy to read as a tape on one page. This interface allows you to focus on the main thing - the rapid reading of letters and work with them.

It was based on the usual form for mail clients: the list of letters and the letters themselves are located on one page. For this, the familiar Yandex.Mail design and its functionality has been completely reworked. We have removed everything that does not relate directly to the work with the mail: advertising, themes, advanced settings, postcards and video writing.

The design was concise and strict. You can choose the way the list of letters is displayed - vertical or horizontal , as well as adjust the width or height of the blocks to your taste.


The new interface is in beta mode, but is available without prompts: anyone can now turn it on in Settings . In the near future, Trinity will be offered by default to all Yandex.Mail users for a domain . For all other changes will occur only at will.

The new interface has been internally tested on Yandex employees - many of us have used it for a long time, for example, for correspondence in corporate mail.
