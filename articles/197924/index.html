October 21-22, we invite you to Tizen Hackathon in Yaroslavl

Hello!

Fond of developing mobile apps? Do you know C ++ or HTML5? Or maybe you already have an application that you would like to port to the Tizen platform? Are you dreaming to take part in the Tizen App Challenge ?

Then this is for you!

On October 21‚Äì22, 2013, we invite you to Yaroslavl State University at Tizen Hackathon with valuable prizes from the organizers.

The workplace, the support of Samsung specialists, cookies and a favorable working atmosphere are guaranteed. You will need a laptop, preferably with the latest Tizen SDK installed.

The event will take place at: Yaroslavl, st. May Day, 2a, "Znamenskaya Tower", Center Internet.

Brief rules of the event:
1. Registration of participants and their projects on October 21 from 9:00 to 12:00.
2. Allowed teams up to 3 people.
3. Project development is carried out on the laptops of the marathon participants.
4. Only projects developed using the Tizen SDK, as well as funds officially supported by the Tizen project, are allowed to participate.
5. The start of the marathon on October 21 at 10:00.
6. Before the end of the marathon on October 22, 16:00, participants must prepare a working application + a presentation of their project.
7. Evaluation of works carried out by the jury In evaluating the following criteria are taken into account: idea, completeness, quality.
8. All rights to created applications remain with the participants.

Come - it will be interesting!
