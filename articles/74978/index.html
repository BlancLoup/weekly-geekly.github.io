Yeti USB Mic - the world's first THX certified microphone

The company known as Bluemicrophone has released a rather interesting device, namely a microphone. But not quite normal, but received a certificate of THX (the certificate is so named in honor of George Lucas's first film "THX 1138").

This device has a low level of distortion, high recording accuracy and cardioid , which can be omnidirectional and bidirectional. Also inside the case is hidden amplifier for headphones.

The price of the device is not to say that it is too small - $ 149.99. According to the manufacturer, it will be possible to buy a microphone in December. It has a USB port and is fully compatible with Mac and Win platforms.
