First photos of Polymer Vision Readius

Not so long ago was published the news about a portable device for reading newspapers and books. The site crave.cnet.co.uk has a review of the device with photos and technical specifications.

The collapsible screen of the new device (made by E Ink technology) has a diagonal of 127 millimeters. In the collapsed state, the device will fit in the jacket pocket and will have the following dimensions:

width 56 mm; height 100 mm; thickness 21 mm.

Readrius will contain 4 gigabytes of internal memory, the screen will be able to display up to 16 shades of gray, the communication capabilities will be GRPS / EDGE, DVB-H and USB. The battery of the device will allow you to work 10 days without recharging.
