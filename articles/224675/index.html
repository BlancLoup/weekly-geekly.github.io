Fira Sans font update


Last weekend, Erik Spiekermann, a well-known specialist, announced the release of a new version of the Fira Sans (3.1) font. Prior to this, the font was available in eight styles: (light, regular, medium and bold) * italic and a separate Fira Mono in regular and bold styles. In the new version, an additional 24 outlines appeared, from hair to ultra. Thus, the font has become one of the richest of all free fonts.

Fira Sans was developed by a group of designers led by Eric Shpikermann in 2013 for Mozilla and its operating system Firefox OS. The basis of the font was the popular commercial font FF Meta , which was also published under the authorship of Eric Spieckermann.

The font is well suited for typing large amounts of text and headings. And light-patterns perfectly fit into the paradigm of flat-design.
