A new crowdfunding platform, focused on offline business, has appeared in runet

It appeared in runet crowdfunding platform Itinvest.su . Its founder, Yury Korkhov, invested his own funds in the development of about $ 10,000.

On this site can register a project from any business areas. Commission service will be 8%. It will be taken from those projects that were able to attract the necessary funds in the framework of the crowdfunding campaign. In addition, as an investment in the Itinvest.su project, 2% of funds successfully raised at the site will be channeled.

Similar to other sites, Itinvest.su will acquire a stake in projects on behalf of the investor. According to the offer contract, representatives of the site act in the interests of the investor.
