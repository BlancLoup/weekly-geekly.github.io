Clone iPhone from HTC soon in Europe


The Taiwanese handset maker NTS has announced the HTC Touch, a new GSM smartphone based on Windows Mobile with a touchscreen - like the future Apple iPhone.

Covered with the same soft-touch soft paint (black or green) as the NTS T-Mobile Wing phone, the HTC Touch is even smaller: 101 x 58 x 12.5 mm and weighs 113 g. It takes almost 2 full widths, 4-inch screen, but unlike the touchless iPhone, the HTC Touch has a navigation bar and a button under the screen.

New technology HTC TouchFlo, based on Windows Mobile 6 Professional (version of Windows Mobile 6 for devices with a touch screen), allows you to solve many common tasks using your fingertips. For example, if you drag your finger up from the navigation bar to the center of the screen, a list of contacts appears on the screen. If you strike to the left, it is replaced by the media control buttons; once again to the left - a set of shortcuts of pre-installed applications appears.
