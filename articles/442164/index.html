Roskomnadzor plans to fine Facebook for non-compliance

Today Roskomnadzor drew up a protocol on administrative violations against Facebook. The social network did not provide the department with the necessary information about the localization of personal data bases of Russian users in Russia, as reported by Vedomosti.

Even on December 17, 2018, Roskomnadzor sent Facebook a request demanding to provide the information required by law about the transfer of Russians to the server, which are stored in Russia. The answer was never received.
