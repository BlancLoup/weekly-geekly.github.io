Nekto.me - anonymous comment service

Let me tell you about our small startup.
Now formspring.me is popular, where you can be asked anonymously.
Almost at the same time, threewords.me appeared with us, where you can be characterized in three words, anonymously.
And here he is Nekto.me , where they write a review about you, change karma and add a label. Yes, anonymously.
