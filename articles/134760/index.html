A variation on the phpmyadmin interface [UPD!]



Hello!

People are different and each has their own tastes. Someone loves everything apple, someone just beautiful, someone do not care for beauty, and give them naked usability, while others do not know what they want. I (I think I'm not alone) treat each type to some extent. In one of the boring evenings, I decided to sketch a layout of my view of the phpmyadmin interface. It has the convenience (IMHO), a little appleiness, and a screen that is not loaded with unnecessary elements.

The layout was drawn on the basis of their classic phpmyadmin 3.2.3 interface (so, at least it is written on my Denver). At first glance, this version is more decently than 3.2.3 , but visually cluttered up. My eyes ran away. And it's not that this is just a new interface.

The purpose of the post? I will give a psd layout to anyone who wants to implement as a theme for phpmyadmin. Drawn only the main page. If someone takes it, I'll draw the rest of the interface.

I tried to stick to minimalism + icons (I love them).
