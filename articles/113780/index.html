Earn on content

Periodically, Habra users turn to me with their ideas of creating websites. Naturally, almost everyone wants to earn on them one way or another.

In this case, people are not afraid of routine work or time-consuming. Most of them want the minimum investment and expect to somehow make money on site content.

They know enough about what methods of advertising to use, but the main question that torments them is how to make a website?

I decided to briefly show how to quickly select a suitable topic and evaluate the competition for such a site, without touching the design, without understanding the intricacies of search engine optimization, usability, text size and presentation, selection of engines and other things.

All this is undoubtedly important, but only after the first step - the choice of the subject of the project.

Or maybe not to strain?


You can of course, especially not bathe, and make a project with a large number of pages and information collected on the basis of volume considerations. For example, any regular reference list. Can.

But only if you have a task to earn only, and not to make a project for which the soul lies, there will be a lot of problems.

Information needs to be kept up to date. Imagine a directory with tens of thousands of addresses and phone numbers. You can ignore this, but in this case your resource will lose the trust of visitors after some time, and for the independent development of the site you need a growing core of regular users.

Among other things, such projects usually have a very vague audience, i.e. Low quality. Therefore, advertising on them works very poorly, and therefore the profit will be less.

Let me remind you once again that we are talking about a project that we want to monetize using content.

Conditions

So, in order for our project to make a profit, its theme should:
1. Be close to you. Ideally, the one you understand. Or you want to understand.
2. Be narrow enough to work well advertising.
3. It must be expensive, so that every action of your visitors cost as much as possible (click on contextual advertising, view banner).
4. Have a small number of competitors.
5. Be popular.

And two more important conditions for the site itself. He must:
1. Have a good attendance with a relatively small volume.
2. To be useful for people to get regular visitors and develop independently in the future.

It is important for us to find a compromise between these conditions.

Be useful
You make a personal project, so you should submit information as your own experience. Based on this, most of the materials on the site should be written by you, and therefore the topic should either be close to your professional activity or hobby.

Another option: choose a theme that has not yet been mastered on the Internet (for example, a new car model club).

As a result, you will have a list of several topics under the heading "I understand."

Narrow niche
Becoming popular in a small area is much easier than in the global one. For example, if you like to cook or prefer a particular kitchen, it is better to choose one product or group. Let's say bread or beer.

So you have another list of topics. Headline "Niche".

Cost and competition
One of the ways to make money on a content site is contextual advertising, so you should find out the cost of a click in your topic by keywords. Most likely, Google Adsense will stand on your site, so you should check the price for the transition in it.
