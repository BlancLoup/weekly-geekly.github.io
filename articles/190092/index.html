Humble Origin Bundle - sale of 8 epic games from EA



Meet the new, without doubt the most generous Humble Bundle - Origin (or Humble Indie Bundle 8).

By themselves, all these games will cost about $ 215, but we are given the opportunity to name our price!

Starting at $ 1, you can purchase 6 games:
  • Dead space 3
  • Crysis 2 Maximum Edition
  • Burnout Paradise: The Ultimate Box
  • Medal of honor
  • Mirror's edge
  • Dead space

Pay more than the average ($ 4.6 at the moment), and you get 2 more:
  • Battlefield 3
  • The Sims 3 (plus 2 DLC from The Sims 3 Starter Pack)

Note:
All games are tied to the client Origin, and 5 of 8 to Steam. Windows-only, except for The Sims 3, which is also available on Mac.
