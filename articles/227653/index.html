Start of construction in the village of programmers

The idea to build a cottage settlement for remote workers in the outback has finally reached the construction stage. We did not manage to get land from the administration and investments from developers, so we bought the land and started building the infrastructure with our own money.


The essence of the idea


Living in a modern Russian city is a dubious pleasure. In an ordinary Russian village, all the more. And moving abroad "with the ends" is suitable only if you are ready to give up the Russian mentality and to talk to your children in Thai before the end of your life.

Therefore, the idea arose to make a cottage village in the nature, ‚Äúsharpened‚Äù for remote workers - with all the necessary infrastructure and adequate prices. More information about the concept of the village can be read on our website .

Just want to make a reservation that we did not focus on proximity to Moscow. Life in such a village is suitable only for those who have really moved to a remote place and are not dependent on regular work meetings.

Initially, we planned to agree on the allocation of land with the local administration, and about investments - with some developer or venture capital fund, but we failed. Having ceased to run with the presentation on different offices, it was possible to concentrate on the main work and earn money for the land and the initial development.

Land


As a result, we bought a small plot of 17 hectares in a couple of kilometers from the town of Slobodskoy, Kirov region. It is a glade on the shore of Skokovsky pond, surrounded on three sides by a pine forest, on the fourth - limited by a coastline. All photos can be viewed in our Instagram , and the location of the site - on Google Maps .







When planning, we wanted to keep the shape of the horseshoe as much as possible, which the plot suggests. The result was 60 residential plots of 12 acres and three large public areas. The site has a detailed description of the layout .



These plots will be sold at cost (cost of land + communications, divided by the number of plots). The price will include road access, water supply, sewage, electricity and wired internet. The exact cost price will be known closer to the beginning of sales, while we can only orient - something around 150-200 tr. for the plot.

The plots will be sold only with a contract - with the purchase you will have to choose a house project and pay for the first stage of construction work.

Construction site


Construction will be carried out by our construction company. The proposed technology is a frame with a mineral wool as a heater. The whole village will be in the same architectural style, but each house will be made on an individual project.

We are building a construction company from scratch - now we are just getting used to each other on the mini-hotel project and infrastructure for a large subsidiary project - IT camping. The preliminary price we hope to offer is from 15 to 20 tr. per sq.m. in a state of "fine finish". This price is due to cheap lumber (Kirov region - the largest supplier of wood) and the low level of margins, which we lay in the company.
