Google and Yahoo are working on new generation social networks.

Thanks to Michael Arrington, the creator and presenter of TechCrunch, it turned out that Google and Yahoo are working (individually) on the new generation of social networks.

For example , Google is sponsoring a project at Carnegie Mellon called Socialstream , while in the depths of Yahoo, the Mosh website is being created. True, it can only be accessed by company employees or by users who connect to the Yahoo guest wireless network.

True, Arrington writes, there is no indication that both projects will ever be launched for ordinary citizens. Perhaps, this is only concept resources created for running in of new technologies.
