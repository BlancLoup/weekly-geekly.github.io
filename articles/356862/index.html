Hacker hacked hacker and sells nahak

In confirmation of the absence of a pirated code on one of the private hacker forums, w0rm.ws, an offer appeared to sell the database of another hacker forum, Monopoly. The latter specializes in stealing credit card numbers, botnets and spam.



Forum w0rm.ws just offers tools for hacking computers and hacking data, so the database fits into its subject. What exactly it contains is not clear from the announcement. Base cost is modest: $ 500.



Knowledgeable people say that the group w0rm is from Russia, and they have already shone several times in the news. In 2013, they hacked the BBC server , then the CNET , VICE and Washington Post sites. The forum often offers zero-day vulnerabilities. Depending on the value of this information, its cost can reach several tens of thousands of dollars. Exploits are sold at auctions, where information is received by the buyer who has offered the most.
