How to choose customers

I recommend working only on the largest freelance exchanges, where hundreds of projects are published per day. For example, free-lance.ru , weblancer.net and freelancejob.ru . Be sure to purchase pro-versions of accounts there - it will be inexpensive, but you and a person will not consider you as a person without them.

Check for adequacy


Always read the ad text carefully. If it is written in such a way that you cannot figure it out without half a liter, and even with a bunch of errors, it is better not to take the work. A customer who cannot clearly express his thoughts immediately, in the process of work will deliver a lot of inconvenience, you will constantly misunderstand each other. In addition, the literacy rate is directly dependent on the overall adequacy of the person.

Collect more information.


Carefully examine the customer profile. We are important registration date, fullness of data and the quality of feedback about the person. A fresh account or lack of feedback should alert you, be careful with such customers. Read the reviews (only from freelancers, reviews of other employers do not interest us), often from 20 positive 19 are written ‚Äúto order‚Äù.

If there are no contact details in the employer's profile at all, we leave immediately. Communicating through private messages is uncomfortable and unprofessional. Even if the customer offers you an icq number or postal address, there is no guarantee that he will not stop responding. You need to know several ways to communicate with a potential client, especially the phone (or Skype). Honest people have nothing to hide, their contacts are always indicated.

It's amazing how rarely it occurs to people to check the data of the person of interest. Just fill in contact information one by one in Google. You will be surprised how dramatic stories you can sometimes find out about your clients, including all their dark deeds. And this is a great reason to refuse to work with them.

Discuss the price


You should be completely satisfied with the project budget. Not every freelancer can afford such luxury as customer selection. When you really want to eat, rush to carry out any order, almost for food. Another moment at the bidding stage, which is issued by an incompetent (or even simply deceitful) customer - when a person asks for a discount, promising to throw a bunch of orders if you work together. Smart people understand that if both parties work together comfortably, their cooperation will continue.

Do not require prepayment


Among professional freelancers and web studios it is considered offensive to receive money only after doing the work. Many simply will not work without prepayment, and some require the full amount at once, and only then they start working.

We will not build a diva from ourselves. Even if you are a very cool specialist, do not ask a person to pay you before you complete at least some of the work. Why should a customer give you money for promises? Only if he offers a prepayment, then you should not refuse.
How, then, to check the solvency of your client? Well, you carefully read the reviews about it, looked at the number of completed projects and the availability of contact / billing information? A customer with a good reputation will not go anywhere. If the employer does not yet have impressive karma on the stock exchange, but it seems to be quite adequate, then ...

Pretend to trust the client




Of course, you can not completely trust anyone, unless you have been working with a partner for several years. In the morning money - evening chairs


When the project comes to an end, the principle of trust is rotated 180 degrees. Send the latest version of the product only after full payment. You have no reason not to send the finished work. All clients understand this and are easy to meet. But if you hesitate to talk about money, after receiving the finished product, the client may have bad thoughts, especially if he is on the other end of the Earth, and you are left with nothing.

Over time, you will gain many good partners with whom you will work constantly. Try to keep the most pleasant clients around you and give up on problematic ones, then your work will be a complete pleasure. I hope that attentive employers will find a lot of useful things for themselves in my article, learn their typical behavior and learn to hunt for real professionals.
