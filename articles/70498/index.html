Student of EF SPSU condemned for hacking faculty site

The pride of the Faculty of Economics of St. Petersburg State University - after 3 years, the hacker of the site was sentenced.

Here is a quote from the official website of the faculty:

According to the Information Technology Center of the Faculty of Economics, on September 15, in the Court of the Moscow District of St. Petersburg, the sentence was pronounced in a criminal case about hacking into the site of the Faculty of Economics in spring 2006.

The accused was found guilty of committing crimes under Art. 273 Part 1 of the Criminal Code "The creation, use and distribution of malicious computer programs."

At the request of the prosecutor, as a punishment, the hacker was assigned two years of imprisonment in a penal colony, a fine of 20 thousand rubles and the confiscation of equipment using which a computer crime was committed.

According to the humane 73rd article of the Criminal Code "Conditional conviction", the penalty was reduced to a suspended sentence (2 years).

Penalty and confiscation left.

Thus, the hooligan trick of a computer attacker received a proper assessment of justice and the honor and dignity of the economic faculty are protected.
