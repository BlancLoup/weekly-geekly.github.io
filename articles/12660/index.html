Wii joystick embedded in Second Life

One of the happy inhabitants of the Second Life universe connected their simulator (treadmill) and joystick from a Wii console to a computer. He made the virtual world much more real.



This video was shot by order of activists of the open source project WiiController4SecondLife , who are developing the appropriate drivers. Their activities were made possible by the fact that the company Linden Labs opened the source code of the client part of SL

The integration of Wii into Second Life is an interesting and relevant problem that other developers are now solving . The resulting hardware and software can be used even in business, for example, for corporate training. It turns out cheap, but very technologically.
