Some facts

  • The game is completely free, contains built-in iAd advertising, rather unobtrusive.
  • To work, you need a firmware device not younger than version 4.0.
  • The game is localized into 2 languages: English and Russian, and this is not just a translation, but in many ways a different set of levels. In addition to the used alphabet, only in the Russian version one can meet some cartoon characters and Internet memes that are not familiar to the western man in the street.
  • On the radar can be not only ASCII or UNICODE characters, but also arbitrary images.
  • OpenFeint is supported, namely their Leaderboards and Achivki.
