Lowcost hosting. Failed startup

I would like to tell you about one interesting project in our opinion that we were ready to finance. Unfortunately, the team promoting this idea fell apart halfway to the beginning.
Below is a description of the project, close to what we received from the authors.
A link to preliminary financial calculations is provided in the body of the note. All figures are given as of 2011 and are not updated.

Almost every hosting company operating in the mass market is faced with the fact that the demand for cheap hosting services is many times greater than the demand for quality, but not cheap service. The main share of costs in this business does not fall on the cost of server hardware, but on the technical support of the data center, power supply and, most importantly, on the high level of those. customer support. But the mass market is not ready to appreciate and pay for it.
If 10 years ago the creation of the site was available to the units, now 5-year-old children create their home pages within 20 minutes. The Internet has become massive and not professional.
According to NETCRAFT , an explosive growth in the number of domains on the network began in 2010.

This growth, of course, led to a change in the structure of customer demand for hosting services. Now the success of a hosting company is determined primarily by the price of the services offered. Accordingly, customer preferences have changed. Why pay more for a brand name rackmount server in a data center with a TIR4 certificate if you can get the same megabytes and megahertz for less money. And what a problem if the site with photos of your favorite cat hangs in hot weather for a couple of hours. Kitty from this will not be scratched.

Just at the very beginning of explosive growth, about 5 years ago, dumping offers for hosting services began to appear.
There is no need to advertise such companies as Hetzner, DirectNet, etc.

We must give the client what he understands, saving on what he does not appreciate:

Why in Europe (Germany):
  • The cost of renting premises in large developed cities is significantly lower than in large cities of Russia.
  • The quality of the premises is higher.
  • There is no corruption component, no cops, sanitary epidemiological stations, and department K. There are rules and laws. If you fulfill them, you are not interested in any supervisory body. In addition to tax In Europe, this is not joking.
  • The quality of communications: electricity, canals, roads - is orders of magnitude higher than in Russia
  • Equipment, on average, is cheaper not significantly, but its choice is greater. Especially with regard to used equipment.
  • The commercial value of the phrase "Made in Germany" I propose to compare independently with the phrase "Made in Russia"

What is worse:
  • S / n The cost of staff.
  • Tax regime. There are, of course, who are trying to deceive the state in terms of taxes. But I would not advise.

How to make such a project:
  1. Rental of industrial premises - from 100 sq.m. at the start of the project
  2. Buy ordinary computers, or buy second-hand from leasing companies, etc.
  3. Upgrading system units with an increase in memory, hard drive volumes, etc.
  4. Room equipment shelving. partitioning into cold / hot zones, organization of forced-air ventilation and simple air conditioning. No precision air conditioning systems, air preparation, etc.
  5. Access restriction - a barn door lock. The calm atmosphere in Germany and the well thought-out insurance make it possible to use this method of protection.
  6. Fire alarm and foam fire extinguishing system. Inert gas, only in tires of the car of the engineer on duty.

The advantages of such a project over competitors are obvious. Having a similar quality of equipment and services, the startup will have a marketing structure oriented towards the Russian-speaking community, will be able to work with Russian payment systems and national currencies of Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, etc. If you fulfill a number of well-known requirements (licensing), you can provide legal services. Persons in Russia and other countries that Western hosters definitely cannot afford.
