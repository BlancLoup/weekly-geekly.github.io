Roskomnadzor: the Internet needs more regulation

The head of Roskomnadzor, Alexander Zharov, spoke at the 4th Worldwide Internet Governance Conference held in Wuzheng (eastern China Zhejiang Province). He spoke about close cooperation with Chinese colleagues on filtering data to be banned and frequency regulation, and outlined the position of Roskomnadzor to strengthen the international regulation of the Internet.

‚ÄúThe international legal mechanisms allowing to assert the sovereign right of states to regulate in the national segment of the Internet are still not established. Zharov noted that the development of mechanisms for the national regulation of the Internet must take into account the national characteristics of each country.

In order to bring order to the global Internet and make it a safer place, Russia developed the concept of a UN convention to institutionalize the regulation of the safety of the functioning and development of the Internet, the rules of responsible behavior of states in the information sphere.
