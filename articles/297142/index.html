Deliveries of servers in the world grow, in Russia - decrease

According to Gartner, in the third quarter of 2015, global shipments of servers increased by 9.2%, while the revenue of their manufacturers increased by 7.5%. In Russia, shipments of servers this year, on the contrary, are falling - for the 2 nd quarter of this year they decreased by 44.7% in units and by 28.4% in dollars according to IDC.



The growth in sales of servers around the world has not touched Russia. In the first quarter of 2015, currency fluctuations and budget uncertainty were to blame. IDC senior analyst Mikhail Popov was expecting a revival of supplies to Russia in the second half of the year, but this did not happen, he told Vedomosti .

Together with the servers , deliveries of personal computers are falling. In the third quarter of 2015, PC shipments fell by 45.5% compared with the same period of 2014 and amounted to 1.2 million units. Notebook shipments fell the hardest.

In the world, the reduction in the supply of personal computers is slower: in the 4th quarter of this year, analysts are waiting for their decline by 10% yoy.

The growth in demand for servers and the reduction in demand for personal computers by IDC analysts explain the transfer of services to the cloud . Virtual terminals are on average three times cheaper than conventional PCs, and their lifespan is twice as long. Corporations duplicate data in the primary and backup data centers. To speed up the delivery of user data to smartphones, Internet companies place them in several data centers around the world.
