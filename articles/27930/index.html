iriver Volcano T7 coming soon in Russia

Iriver has announced the soon appearance of the Volcano player on the Russian market. The novelty will be available to buyers in early July.

Volcano flash player will be released in 5 colors, equipped with a 1 inch display and supports playback of MP3, WMA, ASF and OGG files. In the load of the main function - audio playback - Volcano also has FM radio with the ability to record radio programs, as well as a voice recorder. The amount of internal memory device is from 1 to 4 GB. According to the manufacturer, the Volcano battery lasts for 11 hours of playback.

The cost of Volcano varies from 35 to 50 euros (depending on the amount of memory).
