VimpelCom promises to soon decide on the sale of cellular towers


Photo: pcweek

Russian telecommunications companies, in particular, cellular operators, are faced with the fact that revenues are stagnating - this situation is due to the weakening of the Russian economy. As a result, consumer spending is declining, and so are spending in the corporate sector, Vedomosti reports. But the weakening of the ruble is now leading to an increase in the cost of purchasing foreign equipment.
