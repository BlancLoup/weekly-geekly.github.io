CIKM Cup 2016 - an international competition in the field of Data Science

Big Data comes on all fronts - their role is growing in science, business, medicine, security management and in the economy. Therefore, each of the spheres is ready for a joke to fight for the best minds of specialists. Among the most successful attempts to find the most-most - the most important global competition in the field of data analysis CIKM Cup 2016, which takes place within the framework of the 25th international conference CIKM Conf. 2016. Russian technology company DCA, specializing in work with big data and high-loaded systems, this time became a partner of the conference and competition. DCA has formed a task to be solved by the participants of the CIKM Cup 2016, and also provided the necessary data set for analysis. Want to learn a little more about the task and try your hand at Data Scientist competitions? To whom, if not to us, know how strong are Russian data processing specialists.


We are looking for our Bruce Willis.

The CIKM Cup 2016 competition is part of the international scientific and practical conference CIKM 2016, which will be held on October 24-28 this year for the 25th time and will bring together scientists, academics, information technology professionals and professionals for several days. Traditionally, CIKM takes place in various cities of the world (Baltimore, USA, 1992 - Melbourne, Australia, 2015), thus symbolizing the transboundary nature of science and information technology. In 2016, the conference will take the city of Indianapolis, USA.

CIKM 2016 is located at the junction of three areas of science - information retrieval, databases and knowledge management. CIKM focuses on complex interdisciplinary information problems and global challenges facing the academic community and society as a whole. To strengthen the interaction between science and business, which is necessary to accelerate research in the field of Data Science and innovation in the industry, this year for the first time CIKM will complement the program with a full day devoted to open vacancies (Career Day), as well as present an extensive program with the active participation of industry representatives (Industry Track), which will work for all three days of the main conference, in addition to the Research Track research program, which has already proven its effectiveness for 25 years.

Being, for the most part, an event of the scientific, academic world, CIKM strives for rapprochement with real applied spheres of life. This is exactly where the CIKM Cup competition is held: IT companies set real challenges, and their strongest Data Scientists around the world solve.

The competition is open, anyone can take part from any country in the world - just register on the site and send a request. Each participant is invited to solve two actual problems:

1. Cross-Device Entity Linking for Online Advertising with support from DCA (Data-Centric Alliance)

2. Personalized E-commerce Search with the support of DIGINETICA.

The proposed problems are related to e-commerce and online advertising - perhaps the first industries that caught the commercial value of big data and began to intensively develop in this direction, actively rebuilding both programmatic and targeting, and working with events on the ‚Äúrails‚Äù of big data. That is why internship in companies of such industries is of great value - you can work with data collected both online and through user interaction with elements of the Internet of things (sensors, counters, wearable electronics).

The prize fund of CIKM Cup 2016 is $ 5,000, and as a bonus - the opportunity to communicate with leading world researchers on Entity Linking, Online Advertising and Personalized Search from organizations such as Microsoft Research, Google Research, IBM Research, Stanford University, UIUC, UC Berkeley and others.

The duration of the competition - from August 5 to October 5, 2016. The best of the proposed technological solutions will be presented at the CIKM Cup Workshop at CIKM, open to the entire expert community and on the CIKM Cup website, as well as potentially become part of the solutions of DCA and DIGINETICA.

The tasks are interesting and non-trivial - you should take part at least in order to look at them and turn to the workers ‚Äúliving‚Äù data. Until all is lost, until October 5, you can join the competition and try your hand at the role of Data Scientist. At a minimum, it will be a strong experience, as a maximum - you will join the best minds of Big Data.

About CIKM Conference 2016
CIKM is an ACM conference of the highest level in the field of information retrieval, knowledge management and databases. Since 1992, leading scientists and developers from the three communities have successfully participated in it in order to identify the complex problems facing the development of advanced knowledge and information systems, as well as shaping future research directions through high-quality publications, applied and theoretical research results. With CIKM 2016, we will continue the tradition of promoting cooperation between several areas through broad and interdisciplinary programs at the conference, including seminars, study guides, industry speakers, keynote speakers, research papers, and posters.
