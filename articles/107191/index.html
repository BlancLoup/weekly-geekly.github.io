Conference "Usability Ukraine '10"

November 5 in Kiev will be the first in Ukraine conference on usability. For the first time, the best Ukrainian and Russian interface design experts and representatives of major Internet projects will come together to discuss how Ukrainian sites can become better.





During the reports of leading usability masters, real cases and interesting figures of successful Ukrainian and Russian projects will be considered.
conference program

Unique conference chips


Thematic expert advice

In addition to the speakers, there will be independent usability experts at the conference, who can be recognized by special badges. You can approach any specialist and get answers to your questions in the field of usability and design. This consultation is useful to owners of Internet projects, startups and everyone who has a website and who wants to make it more efficient and better.

Blitz Consulting

During the event, analysts from Turum-Burum and InVooDoo will be in the lobby, showing that your project is online, you can get a 15-minute consultation - a brief analysis of your project, identify the most problematic points and possible directions of development, and, of course, ask questions .

Wall projects

At the conference, we place a huge stand on which everyone can place a page of their project. We will print any page of your site and during the conference speakers and usability experts will glue their stickers with comments on the wall. As a result, you will receive an assessment of your project from leading industry experts.

You can also relax on soft ottomans, have a snack, chat with interesting people, share experiences and just have a good time.

The conference will be held on November 5 in the conference hall of the President Hotel in Kiev.
The cost of participation is 700 hryvnia.

Conference site: usability.ua

Organizers: Turum-Burum , InVooDoo
