Jelastic 2 will have access via SSH

More and more details are becoming known about Jelastic 2. In a significant update of the Jelastic platform, access via SSH will appear. Ruslan Sinitsky, the founder and technical director of Jelastic, wrote about this today on the Facebook page .


At the same time, it will be possible to manage different environments with a single SSH authentication. It is safe and does not even require the allocation of a public IP address. This opportunity was very much requested by many Jelastic users. Release a new platform soon.
