Webnames.ru helps ICANN solve problems with Cyrillic domains

ICANN invited experts from Webnames.ru to take part in researching issues related to the delegation of new Cyrillic root domains (IDN TLDs).

Closer the starting date for filing applications for new root TLDs. ICANN planned to conduct such a study by the end of 2011 with the involvement of experts from six language groups - Arabic, Chinese, Cyrillic, Indian, Greek and Latin.
