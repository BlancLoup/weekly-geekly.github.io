New Evernote website for developers


The ecosystem of applications integrated with Evernote is expanding, and we are trying to launch new tools and resources for partners. The other day we presented a completely redesigned site for developers - dev.evernote.com . The site is in English, but hopefully this is not a big problem for developers.

Visit the Evernote Developer Website ¬ª

Evernote Developer Community


Currently, thousands of developers have shown interest in creating applications and services integrated with Evernote. Using our SDK and code manuals , partners have already managed to create a good collection of products that greatly enhance the capabilities of your Evernote account. These solutions allow our users to control costs, move to paperless workflow, link notes to real-world objects, and much more. View the growing selection of these services and applications in the Evernote Gallery .

Since the first Evernote Trunk conference last year, we keep in touch with our developers, discussing ways to improve our API tools. Over the past year, our team for working with developers has tripled. We organized several meetings with developers all over the world (one of them will be held at the end of April in Moscow - we will tell about the event in more detail later) and completely reworked our technical documentation on working with the Evernote API so that the creation of applications integrated with Evernote becomes more enjoyable.

Our new website for developers should provide partners with all the necessary tools and information to start working on the integration and successfully attracting millions of Evernote users to their project.

Below we will go through the main sections of the new dev.evernote.com :

Documentation


The technical section of our site contains a full overview of the Evernote service, as well as information about what you need to quickly begin development. There you can find materials on authentication , note creation and other key aspects.

news


In addition to launching a new site, we have provided several other ways of tracking community news. You can keep up to date by starting to read our Twitter @evernote_dev . In addition, there is a mailing list and a developer forum . Of course, we will write about important things in Habrahabr too.

Support


We have tried to simplify the process of starting work on the integration with Evernote. On the support page, you will see recent posts from the developer forum , be able to register for an API key, check the status of the API, and contact the support team to solve any problems.

Video


Here we collect materials from conferences and seminars for our developer community.

Showcase


This is our favorite section , where we show the most interesting examples of Evernote integration and talk about how these applications were created.

We will be glad if you take a minute of your time and visit our new developer site . If you have any questions about the development of applications based on the Evernote API, we will be happy to answer them here in Habr√© or in the appropriate section of the Russian-speaking support community.
