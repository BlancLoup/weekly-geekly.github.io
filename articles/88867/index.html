Facebook accused of promoting the spread of syphilis

In the UK, Facebook was accused of the fact that this social network allegedly contributes to the spread of syphilis. This was stated by the head of the health department of the city of Teesside, Peter Kelly. According to him, in recent years in the north-east of England, where Facebook is extremely popular, the number of syphilis has increased fourfold.



Peter Kelly is confident that Facebook, as well as other social networks, has provided people with a new affordable opportunity to find an occasional partner for one night.

A representative from Facebook responded to Peter Kelly's statement. He advised users to take "necessary precautions" when meeting with "friends" from the social network.
