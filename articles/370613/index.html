6 unusual offices of the world


A source


Who thought offices should be stuffy boxes? Choosing work and space for it, we choose a way of life. It is no secret that we spend most of our life in the office. Therefore, it is very important for us to create comfortable working spaces in the company where an employee can not only work, but also relax. And sometimes in our office there is even a skating rink .


Inconvenient offices have a very bad effect on employee motivation. Therefore, the office should become a space for creativity, communication, recreation and productivity. But this is possible only with its detailed forethought. From design to functionality. You probably read about our Moscow office many times, so now we will look at some of the most unusual offices of companies around the world. It is not only convenient to work in them, but sometimes you even want to live. Office can be home. And we will prove it to you.

Merging with nature in the office of Selgas Cano Architecture (Spain, Madrid)



A source


The best motivation for creativity is freedom in all its manifestations. And even if the office is very cool, then most often it is limited by the height and density of the urban jungle. Therefore, the concept of office in nature comes to the rescue.


The office of the architectural bureau is located in a forest near Madrid. From the side, the building resembles a large glass pipe. The creators wanted a lot of sunshine to enter the office, but that the workplaces were not under direct rays. Therefore, we decided to make the building half glass.



A source


Part of the building is buried in the ground, which creates the effect of complete immersion in the forest environment. Due to this, desktops are located almost on the same level with grass. Thanks to the windows, employees can enjoy beautiful views and fresh forest air.

And yet, do we need an office? WordPress costs without it



A source


Automattic management has decided to close its office. Nobody just came to it. And so it was decided to no longer spend money on renting an office and invest it in something more worthwhile. And to transfer all employees to remote work.


Most often, colleagues gathered to discuss joint projects and organize events, the rest of the time they worked at home. The formation of such a habit was influenced by the policy of the company, encouraging work in other places. If an employee likes to spend time in coworking, then he is paid a stipend to cover expenses. If he decided to arrange his office at home, then he also receives payments. If he ran to work at Starbucks, the company will pay for coffee.


Company events are held in different parts of America, so all flights are also paid by the company, and all working meetings are held online. Many employees are remotely hired, without a personal conversation, after an online interview.


In the office or without it, the working atmosphere of the company and good specialists still create the working atmosphere. A properly built workflow can go beyond the usual office, regular schedule and formal relationships. This allows such companies to create a unique and useful product, which is used not only by ordinary consumers, but also by the employees themselves outside the office.
