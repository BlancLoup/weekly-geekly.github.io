Eee Stick. ASUS '"Wii Remote"

ASUS has introduced the Eee Stick, a wireless game controller for its popular Eee PC laptops and Eee Box PCs. With the appearance and capabilities of the Stick, albeit not clean, but lapped with the Wii Remote. The principle of operation is the same.

The Eee Stick comes with three devices: an Activation stick (112 grams), a navigation stick (114 grams), and an RF dongle. Works Eee Stick at 2.4 GHz. The radius is 10 m. Two AA batteries, according to the manufacturer, ensure the operation of the controller for three days.

In theory, the Eee Stick should work with any PC. But, of course, ASUS recommends using it only with those computers (and applications) with which it is compatible. Those. with Eee PC and Eee Box. By the way, it will be possible to buy a controller both separately and in a set with popular ASUS PC models.
