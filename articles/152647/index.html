Organization of the Internet broadcast of the event by budgetary funds

Introductory


The article is intended for event organizers, trainers, teachers who wish to organize online broadcasting of events with minimal expenses for the purchase of equipment and software.

In this article we will describe in detail the steps that need to be taken to organize a free online broadcast. We give a list of necessary equipment and give instructions on how to install it. We describe the main problems that organizers may encounter when organizing online broadcasts, and possible solutions.

Usage scenarios


Online broadcasting can be divided into two categories:

  • Video broadcasts of offline events on the Internet: conferences, seminars, round tables;
  • Web conferencing: simultaneous video chatting of many participants among themselves.


In the first case, the organizer, as a rule, rents a room in which participants gather and at the same time organizes the online broadcast of this event. It is necessary to transmit high-quality sound and video stream, and use video and audio equipment.
In the second case, the participants organize a web conference at home using their home computers or laptops with built-in web or USB cameras.

Equipment


According to the list of equipment for the video broadcast of offline events on the Internet, it is required to use:
  • digital video camera
  • radio microphone system
  • audio mixer
  • video capture card
  • desktop computer
  • wired Internet,
  • sound speakers if you need to output sound to the hall or audience;
  • Cables: audio cable, HDMI cable, balanced cables.


Digital video cameras can be used both professional and home. We recommend that novice organizers first use home camcorders.

Radio microphone system is used to transmit high-quality sound.

An audio mixer is used to amplify and control the volume of the sound (for more information, go to ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%90%D1%83%D0%B4%D0%B8%D0%BE_%D0%BC%D0%B8%D0% BA% D1% 88% D0% B5% D1% 80 .

A video capture card is an electronic device (most often a PCI or PCI-E, less often a USB-compatible card) to convert an analog video signal to a digital video stream (for more, see here (http://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9F%D0%BB % D0% B0% D1% 82% D0% B0_% D0% B2% D0% B8% D0% B4% D0% B5% D0% BE% D0% B7% D0% B0% D1% 85% D0% B2% D0 % B0% D1% 82% D0% B0).

Computer requires a stationary or laptop.

Speakers with speakers (possibly with amplifiers, if it is necessary to do broadcasts in large audiences) (with jack or balance input)

We conditionally all the equipment grouped into three lists, depending on the quality of video broadcasting: broadcasting good quality (let's call it HD quality) and lower quality (conditionally SD quality or Lite version) and webcasting equipment at home.

Equipment for HD video broadcast quality


For HD video broadcast quality, you will need to install the following equipment:
  • home digital video camera (with HDMI output), with support for Full HD;
  • a stationary computer, preferably with a processor not lower than Intel Core i3 (with monitor) with a PCI-Express slot;
  • Integrated Blackmagic Intensity Pro video capture card (with HDMI input);
  • radio microphone system. We recommend Shure brand radio microphones;
  • audio mixer We recommend Behringer brand audio mixers (http://mmag.ru/behringer-xenyx-1002fx.html or a little more professional mmag.ru/behringer-xenyx-qx1202usb.html );
  • a tripod for a video camera (must correspond to the brand of your video camera);
  • Cables: Mini HDMI - HDMI camera for connecting the video camera to the video capture card, audio cable for connecting the audio mixer to the computer, 2 Jack-Jack cables or balance cables for connecting the radio microphone system to the audio mixer.


Features of equipment for HD video broadcast quality

The Blackmagic Intensity Pro video capture card only works on video cameras that have been released for the past two years and that support Full HD. As a rule, you should check if your camcorder supports this video capture card. The advantage of this board is that it can be used to transmit sound from a video camera. For the price of this fee is in the range of 8-9 thousand rubles.



The budget alternative to this board is the TV Tuner - the integrated AVerMedia AVer3D CaptureHD video capture card with HDMI input. The disadvantage of this board is that it does not allow to transmit the sound to the direct video camera (therefore, an external radio microphone system and an audio mixer are required). Also, to capture an image, you need to run the software that comes with this board each time.

The first level of Bit Rate, jumping to 300 Kilobits / s, will allow those users whose Internet access does not exceed, say 400 Kilobits / s (via 3G modems from distant regions of the country). Major problems or frequently asked questions


There is a strong sound noise in the webcast

You may have a poorly connected electrical outlet, in which the ends of electrical cables or contacts that sparkle are poorly connected. Plug all equipment into a different electrical outlet.

After configuring the drivers for the audio capture card, the operating system writes a message that the drivers are not properly connected and may not work.

Remove all drivers of all webcams that were on your computer and repeat the installation. We noticed that sometimes there is a conflict between webcam drivers and video capture cards.

The video capture card drivers are installed correctly, but Adobe Flash Media Live Encoder does not see them.

In this case, you may have to first run the software that came with the video capture card, display the video image on the screen in this software, and then launch Adobe Flash Media Live Encoder. So at least AVerMedia AVer3D CaptureHD can work.

When connecting an audio mixer, the wrong low and high frequencies are transmitted.

Low frequencies (bass) reduce to a minimum. Midrange make a little less balance, high a little more balance.

Users complain that they can not connect to the Internet broadcast

Users must have port 1935 open.

Users complain that they are slowing down the video image and the sound comes with interruption

Encourage users to connect to a more broadband Internet channel. Set two different bit rates in the Adobe Flash Media Live Encoder.
