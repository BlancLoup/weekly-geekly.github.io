Release 01, Actual Start

Presenting your attention to the 1st, in fact, but not in a row, issue of Interitive , we can, with a clear conscience, put a tick in the calendar: "Start has begun." This is a very interesting and, if you think about, a positive experience for all people involved in the compilation, layout and release of our humble journal. There are four of them, that is, us: Unno , Shapelez , va1en0k and Lesch . Kissing is not necessary; all we currently want from readers is sound ideas and suggestions for further improving the PDF version, valid criticism and rays of joy. It will be enough for us, really.
