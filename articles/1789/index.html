IPTV goes to Krasnoyarsk

Global Telecom is launching a project to create a digital television network in Krasnoyarsk with an initial investment of $ 1.5 million, Prime-TASS reports. At the first stage of work (6 months), it is planned to provide access to the IPTV network to 5 thousand residents of the Vzletka microdistrict. To cover the whole of Krasnoyarsk, according to the company, it requires about $ 38 million.
