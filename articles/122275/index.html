So, consider these tricks ... In fact, the tricks for making big and exciting games are pretty simple and reasonable.

1. Uploaded and play

If the player does not have to read the instructional instructions and can immediately start playing, this is a great advantage for any game. Gamers want to play, not read boring manuals ... so give them what they are used to doing. Of course, on more complex levels it makes sense to read the FAK in order to arrive at the best result, but the beginning should be simple and intuitive for everyone.

2. Mentally light

Learn games

Do not just play, and learn the game. Many people are fond of games, but few understand them, and this is the key to creating games. Why is it impossible to break away from some games, while others cause only boredom and a desire to bring them back to the store? Why do some games immediately cause delight, while others - irritation? The difficulty lies in the fact that there are no generally accepted rules for creating games, therefore many projects are developed on the basis of pure intuition. Studying games, trying to understand the reasons for their popularity or unattractiveness allow you to gain experience, which is so necessary to create good games. But remember - the experience just does not come: just spending time behind the monitor screen is not enough. It is necessary to constantly analyze all the games that fall into your field of vision, asking yourself why they were made that way and not otherwise ...
