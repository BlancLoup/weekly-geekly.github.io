What will be important for employees?


The "sanitary and hygienic" minimum for any developer when choosing a company is:


  • convenient location of the office, with large parking and stopping within walking distance;
  • thoughtful office with comfortable workplaces and dedicated areas for meetings and recreation;
  • availability of a cafe, gym, etc.

We turn to the most important issue for business - wages. Here a lot depends on the interestingness of the project. To form the backbone and not lose a useful developer, you need to add 10% to the median of the salary market. This is provided that the project is ambitious and interesting. If we cannot promise such a project, the increase should be at least 20%.
