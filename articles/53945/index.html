Typograph 2.0 - waited!

Editors, proofreaders, web designers, web developers and bloggers have been waiting for this. Tiporgaf was supposed to be updated back in January , but is being updated only today. So glad to introduce you Typograph 2.0 !

Over the past year, I have repeatedly rethought my vision of typography in general and of my typographer in particular . I collected a lot of material, and despite the fact that right now I am announcing the second version, I am already working on the 3rd version.

I congratulate all of us, the main browsers have finally begun to support the characters:, & thinsp, & ensp, & emsp (the exception is Opera, but I will work on this).
