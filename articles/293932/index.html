One skill that always surpasses any talent.



I will tell you a secret. There is a skill that you can master, and which guarantees you the following: everything you do will improve by at least 50%, but, what's even more likely, by 100%, and with time and even more.

The best thing about this skill is it is simple. Take possession of everyone. You do not need to have some natural talent. You do not need any special resources or teachers to master them. As soon as you gain this skill, it will become part of your daily activities, and the pace of your work will increase. You will become more successful, and your reputation will bring you more opportunities.

As Morpheus said from the immortal "Matrix": "Do you want to know what it is?"

Just do all the bullshit you need to do.


Yes it is. Read again. Let rooted in the memory.
