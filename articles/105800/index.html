Xmarks is alive and will live!


Not so long ago, and more precisely on September 27, the Xmarks project team announced that the servers were shutting down and the project was closed due to lack of funds.
The public, namely the users of this bookmark synchronization service for the browser, was very concerned about the problems of the project and expressed their thoughts and suggestions in a specially created topic .
Well, today, the Xmarks team reported positive news to us (users of the service) through its blog . It is written that many offers from various investors and companies have been received. And although no contracts have yet been signed, the management is full of hope that the service will not even ruin its work.
Hurray comrades !!! :)
