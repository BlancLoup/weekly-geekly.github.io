The company Oppo introduced a charge that can charge the phone for 100% in 15 minutes



Gadgets to some extent make our lives more convenient. On the other hand, the person is constantly tied to the mains, because all the time there is a device that needs to be charged. And it would be fine if the charging process took place in a couple of minutes, but you have to wait for 2-3 hours or even more.

Now the company Oppo introduced a charger that can recover the battery power of a smartphone with a capacity of 2500 mAh to 100% in just 15 minutes. This is what the manufacturer promises. According to the company, the technology works with both MicroUSB and USB Type-C cables.

New charging works even faster than the promise of another company, which has long been creating high-speed chargers. We are talking about Qualcomm with its QuickCharge 3.0 (on the market so far there is only charging with Quick Charge 2.0). With this charge, the smartphone recovers the charge by 80% in about 35 minutes. By the way, Huawei is also going to participate in competitions to create the fastest charger in the world.

In some cases, it is possible to fully charge the battery using the new Oppo technology in less than 15 minutes. At the presentation of the device, one of the smartphones charged 100% in just 10 minutes. Of course, while this is only a presentation, the real situation may be somewhat different. But this already shows time and real charges, which should soon get on the market.
