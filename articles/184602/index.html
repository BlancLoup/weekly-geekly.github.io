SmartWatch 2 - even smarter!

For the year of its existence on the market, Sony SmartWatch smart watches have gained a fair number of fans. The number of applications for this unusual gadget has exceeded two hundred, and the recently launched Open Smartwatch Project has opened up the possibility for independent developers to create their own firmware. The time has come for a new model - SmartWatch 2 has become more productive, versatile and, moreover, waterproof!





With SmartWatch 2, you can remotely control your camera and presentations, check routes, keep track of sports statistics, view new letters and messages on Twitter, control music playback, and adjust volume. NFC technology allows you to connect your watch to your smartphone with one touch, an enlarged contrast screen perfectly displays information even in bright sunlight, and the elegant design and the ability to replace the straps ensure that the watch matches your wardrobe.

Specifications:

- Touch 1.6-inch screen with a resolution of 220x176
- Multitouch (supports three simultaneous clicks)
- Compatible with any Android smartphones
- NFC technology support
- Replaceable straps
- Working time: 3-4 days with intensive use
- USB charging
- Bluetooth 3.0
- Moisture protection (compliance to the IP57 standard)
- Support 14 languages, including Russian
