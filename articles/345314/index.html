I want to program, let them teach me! Avito educational projects

Hello! My name is Anna, I am the head of educational projects in Avito. It seems to us very important to encourage beginners (and not only) developers to learn about the coolest technologies on the market. Therefore, we are doing a whole pool of educational online projects in partnership with the best sites. I tell about them in detail under the cut, in the same place I reveal our plans for the future.



Avito collaborative projects with leading online sites


Learning should be fun and convenient. The higher education system does not always keep pace with rapidly developing technologies (but still provides an excellent base), and streaming lectures and one and a half hour handwriting, like a few centuries ago, do not seem to be the most optimal ways of learning information.


So far, no one has invented chips with knowledge, embedded directly into the brain, we will use proven training tools. This year we have done as many as five projects with leading online educational sites (OTUS, LoftSchool, Stepik, GeekBrains, HTML Academy), in which we experiment on the format - we interview the best students, we edit the curriculum taking into account the technologies of the company's development, we pay for training in the case of employment at Avito, our employees become teachers and mentors of courses and much more. This is what our partners tell about these projects.
