So what are we celebrating?

For a proper understanding, we need to at least look at the history of the issue from a distance. Even for a superficial, but correct understanding, you need to find the source. Briefly the essence. Chicago, May 1, 1886


The working day systematically lasts about 15 hours, wages are low, there are no social guarantees.

Today, a worker, having become accustomed to modern working conditions as a given, can imagine himself in the place of 19th century workers. Such is the thought experiment - assess the scale of the problem, approaching the personal, and if you have a family, family tragedy, a person who, having freedom, does not have free time and material resources.

Of course, rallies and strikes began. I would not like to copy the text of an already well-written article, so I suggest those interested to follow the link " Riot to the Haymarket ." There is enough: a rally, police, a provocateur, a bomb, shooting, slander and the death penalty of innocent people.

The American press attacked all leftists indiscriminately. The judges and the jury were opposed to the accused, the bomb thrower did not even try to establish, the petitions for the trial of each defendant were rejected separately. The line of accusation was based on the fact that once the defendants did not take measures to search for a terrorist in their ranks, it means that they were in collusion with him.

Of the defendants, only Fielden and Parsons were ethnic Englishmen, the rest were natives of Germany, of whom only Neebe was born in the United States, while others were immigrants. This circumstance, as well as the fact that the rally itself and the anarchist publications were addressed to German-speaking workers led to the fact that the American public mostly ignored what happened and favorably responded to the following executions, if somewhere there was a revival of the labor movement in support of the defendants, then abroad - in Europe.
In memory of this event, the first Paris Congress of the Second International in July 1889 decided to hold annual demonstrations on May 1. This day was declared an international holiday for all workers.

Sometimes there are opinions that in Russia this holiday was borrowed in the revolutionary period, they say, they can not invent anything from us. I note that, firstly, the Day of International Solidarity of Working People cannot be borrowed, it can only be joined, and secondly, May Day was celebrated for the first time In the Russian Empire in 1890 in Warsaw, a strike of 10 thousand workers was held.

According to some media reports, for most citizens of Russia this day is just a reason for entertainment, an additional day off and the beginning of the garden season. I think that the reason is mainly in the lack of enlightenment in the history of the issue. Social order, the world has become better, the struggle against oppression was given in countries around the world at different prices. Definitely, there is something to be grateful for, to appreciate and cherish.
