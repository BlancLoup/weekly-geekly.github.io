A social network for the debaters Gottabet

For those who like to argue and conclude a bet, the Gottabet website has opened , which is a social network for debaters.

For example, visitors may try to guess how many people Jack Bower will kill in the sixth season of the popular American television show "24". The rate is 30 "nuts" (local currency), the winner takes the entire bank, writes TechCrunch . Accepted bets and in real money.

The rest of Gottabet has all the features of a typical social network. In the future, its creators are planning to create a mobile version of the service.
