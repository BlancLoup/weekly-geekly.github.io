Tools that only introverts will appreciate



Do you consider yourself an introvert? Do you feel that you get tired quickly next to other people and can only recuperate in loneliness? If the answer is "Yes!", Then the following 9 applications , services and tools that can change life, for you! Enjoy tranquility, introverts :)

Avoid Humans



This is a web application that analyzes geographic marks on Foursquare and Instagram, and shows a list of the nearest places with the least amount of people.

Split



Split helps you avoid casual encounters with people you don‚Äôt want to see. It connects to Instagram, Twitter, Foursquare and your own network, and helps you stay calm . And rested from society.

The noise app



Another mobile application - The Noise App - allows you to record an annoying noise and quickly send a record to the municipality or the landlord.

Hush



Hush blocks the annoying noise at night, and wakes up only if something serious happens.

Highbrow



Highbrow is a free mini-course, information about which you are sent every morning. All courses last 10 days, and topics range from art and history to science, language learning and philosophy.

Noizio



This application helps you stay focused and relaxed, filling the space with environmental sounds. You can choose your own sounds, and even make a small mix.

Selfcontrol



SelfControl is an application that blocks access to distracting sites, mail servers, and anything on the Internet that prevents you from concentrating.

Introji



Introji is an application in which the introvert will find a suitable sticker for any situation and state of mind. Everyone who loves his loneliness will love these smiles, because they convey all the states and emotions that can be depicted.

Pinboard



This is a fast and ascetic bookmarking site for introverts and those who appreciate privacy and speed.
