Internet access right

The author of the initiative proposes to legislate the right of every citizen to access the Internet.
In the absence of a legally protected right of access to the Internet, citizens are not guaranteed such a right, which means that law enforcement agencies, providers and other persons who may have malicious intent to stop providing Internet access to a citizen at any time can be guaranteed. At the same time, the speed of data transmission on the Internet today can vary greatly with fixed and mobile providers. Considering that the right of citizens to get access to the Internet in any way today is not fixed by law, there is no possibility to fix the minimum data transfer rate that the state and providers guarantee to every citizen (except for convicts and other categories of persons with restricted rights).
