Comstar-UTS doubled the subscriber base of Stream TV in the 2nd quarter of 2006

Comstar-UTS announces the operating results of the alternative and traditional business segments for the second quarter of 2006. During the reporting period, the growth of the subscriber base of broadband Internet access was 73% (net growth of 126,927 subscribers) to 301,045 compared with the results of the 2nd quarter of 2005. The subscriber base of Stream TV doubled to 28,556 subscribers compared to the first quarter of 2006.
