Summer is coming - it's time to apply to the summer school (internship) of Intel



As it has long been the custom, in early April, we invite you to take part in the traditional summer school of programmers Intel 0x7E1, which will be held in July-August 2017. Are you young, programming and interested in topics such as performance optimization, video and graphics processing, machine vision? Are you attracted to the opportunity to work in the company of cool professionals? Do you want to solve interesting live tasks and see the result of your work? Or maybe you just have nothing to do this summer? Then you definitely under the cat.

So, Intel Software Development Center in Nizhny Novgorod invites you to the Summer Internship . For two months, the interns who have passed the competition will work on tasks in real projects under the guidance of Intel experts. At the same time, leading scientists of various scientific schools and areas, teachers at Russian universities and leading staff of Intel will hold a series of special lectures, trainings and seminars.

The best student trainees of this program will have the predominant opportunity to work at Intel during their studies as interns and after graduation as full-time employees. During the internship, a monetary remuneration is paid, a non-resident intern is given a grant to travel from the place of study to the place of internship and back and stay in a student dormitory.

Dates of internship: from July 3rd to August 25th . Students are invited (starting from the 2nd year at the time of application), undergraduates and graduate students of Russian universities, not older than 27 years old , who have citizenship of the Russian Federation.   • Demo image recognition in the process of video transcoding using the Intel Media SDK and Intel DAAL
  • Optimization of computer vision algorithms in C / C ++
  • Crown and Rabbit: the implementation of Intel Embree support in the Blender TM ray tracing engine
