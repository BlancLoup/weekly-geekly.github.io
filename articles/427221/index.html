What I realized on the way to my dream of artificial intelligence

* The word "general" in the definition of "artificial intelligence" means that the machine can perform any task that a person can perform. In other words, the ability of the machine is not limited to any particular task.



Events in which I participated as a schoolboy contributed to the birth of my dream about AI. I created websites and programs for free and for money. Finally, in my last year of high school, I co-founded a startup as a ‚Äútechnical director‚Äù, and this was a decisive moment for me when I decided to study economics and management.
