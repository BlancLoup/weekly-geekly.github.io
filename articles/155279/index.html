Human key graphs

Not newness for the sake of systematization for. Everyone knows about the social graph, and this is not surprising - social networks are based on it, the largest of which has recently gained a billion users. However, the social graph does not solve all problems. For example, you cannot be sure that in your friend tape you will really read all the relevant materials on topics of interest to you. Hence the concept of interest graph and services based on it. What is the sensible habrastia from karaboz . In part, I tried to bring the same idea to the absurdity of logical conclusion . Few people know that in LJ there is an opportunity to read friends only on certain tags in their blogs. Curiously, the possibility of intersecting the social graph and the graph of interests already existed on such an ancient resource. In a comment on his status on Facebook, Konstantin Sinyushin said that if you are interested in restaurants and some friends write a review about the restaurant, there is no guarantee that this review is of real value, because This friend is not necessarily an expert in this field. In other words, even the intersection of an interest graph with a social graph does not solve all problems.

A problem of this kind can be solved only with the help of the graph of competencies as a set of skills (competencies) associated with a specific person. I googled and it turned out that the concept of a competence graph exists, although in a completely non-expansive form. Just a couple of links.
