Mobile Developer Day - June 9-10 in Moscow



The Mobile Developer Day (MDDAY) conference will take place on June 9-10, 2011 # MoCO is a special program for developers of mobile applications and services within the framework of the World Mobile Content Forum (MoCO).

MoCO is already a traditional event for the industry of producers and aggregators of mobile content, which has been held for over 7 years. The forum is attended by top managers of large companies from Russia, the CIS countries, the European Union, the Middle East and the USA.

And MDDAY (Mobile Developer Day) in its program unites not so much managers as developers for all mobile platforms and all types of mobile applications, both existing and preparing to enter the market.

The joint MDDAY # MoCO program will allow to unite the interests of both vendors and small teams and startups.

The MDDAY program is free to participate, based on registration on the website moco.mdday.ru . As part of MDDAY # MoCO, speakers will sort out cases and share experience in creating and promoting mobile applications. Representatives of Microsoft, nVidia, Unreal Mojo, Data Art, Boston IT-company and dozens of small independent groups of developers have already announced their participation .

You can see the MDDAY # MoCO program here http://moco.mdday.ru/programma/ .
For information on how the previous Mobile Developer Day took place, you can see here http://2010.mdday.ru/
If you want to make a presentation at MDDAY # MoCO, you can send the topic of your speech at md@mdday.ru .
By the way, another project of the organizers of MDDAY is Mobilefest http://www.mobilefest.ru/ , a festival of mobile software developers and innovative projects in the field of mobile Internet. In 2011, Mobilefest will be held at the beginning of autumn near St. Petersburg and according to the established tradition will be the closing barcamp of the season.

In events, we try to move away from the traditional canons of strict conferences, make the program fun and maintain an atmosphere of freedom and creativity. Join now!

Date of event: June 9 - 10, 2011
Venue: Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel (Moscow)
Dates: June 9, 11:00 - 18:30; June 10 11:00 - 17:30
The contact person for cooperation, sponsorship and participation in the festival MDDAY # MoCO - Anna Akimova.
