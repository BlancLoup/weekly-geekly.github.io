Format


PyConRu we especially love for the format: two days in nature, reports and master classes from Russian and foreign speakers, Lightning Talks and afterparty with a bonfire and songs with a guitar.

The conference will again be held in the country hotel "Yakhonty Tarusa" . There will be a transfer from the Annino metro station to the hotel and back.
