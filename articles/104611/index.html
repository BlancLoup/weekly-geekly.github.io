Google has developed a plugin for MS Office

It sounds strange, but it is. At a conference in Paris, Google representatives conducted a demonstration of the DocVerse plugin for MS Office 2007. It was also shown how the formatting of documents looks like, if you save them from one machine and open it to another. That is, supported and functional group editing.

This plugin is based on the product of the company DocVerse, which Google bought in December 2009.
