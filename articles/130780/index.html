Everyone decides on the dilemma of choosing the next film in their own way: someone goes to the Top 250 Best Films of the Film Search, someone searches for a favorite actor or director, someone for the winners of film awards, etc. However, time after time it becomes harder to make a choice.

***
When the time to search and select the next film seemed to be close to the timing of the full-length tape - the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa simple service was born that was supposed to reduce the process of irrational agonies of the movie lover, giving a quick and ingenious answer to the question: "What to see?"



The main page of Chuzimuvi reveals to the visitor the purpose of the project, briefly describing its modest functionality.

By clicking on the big red button in the header of the site (in combination with the logo), a certain film is immediately offered to the user's attention. The output algorithm is a mystery even for the developers themselves , one can be sure of one thing - the service will not recommend a bad one.



Literally all the elements on the movie page are clickable; Links work like filters. If you have already seen the proposed film, you may want to refresh it in your memory, or you want to watch something similar, or filmed by the same director, or with the participation of the same actors, or with the same awards, or just trust Chuzi again ...
