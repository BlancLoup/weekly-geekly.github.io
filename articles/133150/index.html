Unusual hybrid device from Convergence Technologies



The company Convergence Technologies recently introduced a hybrid device of its own design, which combines several gadgets at once. This includes a remote control, a game controller, and a keyboard. Needless to say, such a device can be used for anything - from switching channels on a TV to playing on your favorite game console. The manufacturers do not say anything about whether the device has any wireless modules, but it seems not.

The only negative point in all this is that the company has not yet collected the required amount for the mass release of the device. For everything about everything Convergence Technologies requires about 50 thousand dollars, after which the production of the device will begin. It is clear that just because no one will give money, so the guys from Convergence Technologies are taking pre-orders in the amount of $ 65.



Here is a tablet of device compatibility with various devices:



There are other models of the device, plus different sets of accessories that come with it. For example, for $ 130 you can get two devices and two covers for it. And for $ 1,000, you can get a book with copies of the device development project (not technical documentation, but designers' designs), plus a t-shirt, plus a case, plus a lot more.



The device looks very good, agree. It seems that it works no worse - at least in the photo, everything looks quite comfortable. It would be great if the configuration of the device really included wireless communication modules so that the device could be used as a keyboard (or game controller) for mobile devices.
