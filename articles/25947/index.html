AppleInsider news of the day

All MacBooks will get LED backlighting by 2009
Apple plans to use LED-backlit display in all MacBooks already by 2009, thus completing the care in the line of laptops, from mercury panels, launched last year with the updated MacBook Pro.

Apple blamed for mouse name
A small manufacturer of computer accessories sued Apple and CBS, as he believes that the Mac manufacturer should stop calling mice for desktop computers "Mighty Mouse".

IPhone 3G will still work on 3G
Apple will launch an iPhone for third-generation networks earlier this year than the original model - in the past. However, according to Piper Jaffray, the work on the device will still not be completed.

Mac sales grew 50% in April
The success of Apple personal computers continues to amaze: Mac sales showed a 50 percent annual growth in April, compared with 17 percent growth in the broader market.

IPod nano 1.1.2 firmware released
Despite active preparations for future product updates, Apple did not forget to submit a third-generation iPod 1.1 update for firmware 1.1.2.

A quarter of buyers waiting for the new iPhone
Information from a ChangeWave Research customer survey indicates that exactly a quarter of all potential iPhone buyers postpone a purchase, as they are waiting for the next-generation iPhone.
