Stand or not stand: decide with 2GIS

The other day we reported that we had added a car of various information to the headings related to cars. At this point, the process of improvements 2GIS, as an assistant to the motorist, is not finished. In the August version, a few more useful features appeared.



Let's clarify a bit. We do not seek to become a car navigator. We, as before, are experts on the city. We just try to provide the information that will make your life in this very city a bit more comfortable.

We are glad to announce that now on our maps (in all versions of 2GIS: PC , Online , Mobile , and API 2GIS ), you can find parking places. While they are simply marked on the map with a special symbol "P". But as they say, the first step is the hardest. We promise that information about the cost, time of work and much more will appear soon.

In addition, its direction is indicated on all one-way roads. This information will help orient those who are used to building routes on their own.

More than a year ago we reported that we launched a traffic service in Novosibirsk. It is time to deal with traffic jams Peter and Krasnoyarsk .



Unfortunately, we are not omnipotent, and we cannot remove them. But we will help you to go round. In the mobile 2GIS (except WP), you can see the current situation on the roads, and in 2GIS Online - also with a forecast of the situation for each period of time on this stretch of road.



Instead of the conclusion: parking and direction signs you can see in any version of 2GIS, and traffic jams in 2GIS Online and mobile version for iOS and Android .

We hope that this news will be doubly pleasant to all who use or intend to use API 2GIS . The innovations described are already available to all partners.
