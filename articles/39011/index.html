New WLM 9 details.

Many have seen WLM 9 Milestone 1. There have been enough changes since WLM 8.5 . But now the WLM 9 Milestone 2 screenshots appeared on the network, and new details about this IM client became known.

First, let's take a look at how the new Windows Live Messenger will look like:



What is new? Let's understand:

Personalization.



In the picture on the right, you can see that the title of the main window can now not only be painted in one color, but also put all sorts of different photos, backgrounds and so on.

Simplified title.



Removed all unnecessary buttons, such as Windows Live Call, Windows Live Today, Spaces , etc. No wonder, because these services were needed only 1% of WLM users. But they left and transferred to the right panel the counter of new letters in the Hotmail service. Very convenient, in my opinion.

For comparison, this is how the panel looked like in WLM 8.5:



Personal Status Messages.



The song you are currently listening to and your personal status can now be shown simultaneously.

New buddy icons.



Buddy- icons replaced with colored panels. Take a look at the first screenshot. Now the avatar of a person who is online is highlighted in green on the back. Orange color means that the person has moved away, and red, that the person is not online.

What's new



Now, in the main window of the program, you can see what's new with your friends, who among them added new photos to their album, who uploaded the file to SkyDrive , etc.

Tabs



Tabs are now at the bottom, just above the advertisement. Looks much more aesthetically and functionally than before.

Let me remind you of the main changes introduced by <WLM 9 Milestone 1 compared to WLM 8.5 :

Favorites.



Special group for the most interesting people. It makes it easier to observe what your friends are doing, what their status is at the moment, etc.

Groups



Now chatting with several people at the same time has become much more convenient. Just create the necessary group, add people there and start communicating with them.

Sounds.



For each contact, you can set your own sounds when it appears on the network and sends you a message.
