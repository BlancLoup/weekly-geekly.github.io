Droider Quest - urban game for Android fans

UPD. Due to the climatic conditions in Moscow, the game is postponed to the next week. The new date will be known in the coming days. We apologize for the inconvenience.

Hello,

This Saturday, August 7, Droider.ru is holding a city game for Android fans in Moscow - Droider Quest.



Our task is to hold a meeting among fans and owners of Android, to communicate, to have a great time. But first we offer a little play.

The essence of the game is that you will need to go through a small route through the center of Moscow, while answering with SMS (the cost of your operator) to simple questions about the places that you will meet on the way.

Whoever scores the most points will receive the main prize - a stereo bluetooth headset . In addition, the first 35 players will receive a treat of cold beer or fresh fruit to choose from.

To take part, you need to send SMS #droider to the number +7 (903) 797-6139 (the cost of your operator, for example, I have 1.50 p.). After this, on Saturday you will receive a message with the first question.

The beginning of the game: August 7 at 15:00 at the Polytechnic Museum (m. China-City, Lubyanka). More information here.

I guarantee a great pastime, interesting questions and tasty drinks! See you!
