Battery recycling has become mandatory.

The EU leadership obligated sellers of various batteries to collect them for recycling. According to the new rules, starting from February 1, everyone who sells more than 32 kg of batteries per year (approximately 4 finger-size batteries per day) will have to give buyers the opportunity to bring the used battery back.
