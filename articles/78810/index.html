We go, we go, we go ... 7.5 hours in Norway or a trip to silence


I'll start from far away. There have been moments when everything around you is annoying, when the sound of a neighborhood drill or music center after five hours of listening becomes the pulsating center of your personal universe.
While working in a coworking center or office you can not concentrate on the problem to be solved because of the conversations of your neighbors?
I present an optimal solution under the cut for reasons of price / effect.


There are several ways to protect yourself from annoying sounds from the outside.
  • Soundproof passive - roughly speaking, is determined by the thickness of the walls or headphones that separate you from the outside world.
  • Sound insulation active - a system that actively suppresses external sound vibrations. For ordinary mortals, it is represented by headphones like Sennheiser PXC450, Creative HN-700, Philips SHN9500, Bose Quiet Comfort 3, Jabra, C820s, AKG K28NC, Sony MDR-NC11A
  • Masking is a way that does not eliminate the sound, but hides it.


In order to understand which method is most effective, it is necessary to present various situations in which extraneous sound interferes.

House
Noise factor: neighbors, street, home.
The first way is the most logical and the most expensive is soundproofing an apartment. For those who are not aware of the occupation is very, very expensive. There is one simple rule that determines the effectiveness of sound insulation of a room - in order to isolate the volume as efficiently as possible you must build a room in the room, while in theory your interior should not touch the exterior and also the air gap between the rooms should be open volume and the interior should be airtight closed. No blankets, carpets or foam on the walls will save you, and in the case of foam it is likely to aggravate the problem. The implementation of the theoretically correct set of sound insulation measures is practically not feasible.
