Find my car: autoblockchain HashCoins against hijackings and "not a bit, not beautiful" ads

On Monday, HashCoins , in the blog of which you are reading this news, announced the accounting service for vehicles and their components, built on the blockchain Emercoin . The project has already been presented to the government of one of the Baltic countries and negotiations are underway with two global automakers.


A specially designed computer simulation demonstrates what the car owner will look like, whose car is listed in the autoblockch.

In short, you can keep records and history of any vehicles on autoblockch, from motorcycles and cars to boats and airplanes, as well as their components, including motors and expensive electronic components, history of changes in their owners, insurance claims, etc. - depriving the meaning of virtually any fraud with them from hijacking to "never broken, I swear by my mother" on the car market.

Who needs it


There are a number of organizations that will be interested in the autoblockchain:

  • government agencies involved in the accounting of cars (in Russia it is the traffic police),
  • Insurance companies,
  • automakers and car services,
  • companies with a large number of cars (taxi companies, courier services, retail chains),
  • manufacturers of any movable equipment, up to tractors, engines and aircraft, which are subject to one or another method of accounting.

For example, mopeds with engines of less than 50 cubes in Russia are not required to be considered. And everything that has a passport or technical book can be useful accounting in the blockchain. In Europe and especially in the USA, for example, the subject of light aircraft is relevant, and in Norway, river transport is very popular - and very often small-engine boats are hijacked. They, too, are sometimes hijacked, interrupted by numbers or simply curled up, etc. - in general, everything relevant to cars is relevant for them. For example, insurers can enter data on any insurance claims to the autoblockch.

How it will work


The more organizations adapt the autoblockch in their activities, the more effective its results will be. Therefore, the trick with a hijacked car, according to the broken numbers registered somewhere in the North Caucasus, is no longer a ride - or a ride with very careless customers. Or those who buy a stolen car under the order - but there is not much to be in this, because the very first attempt by the insurance, auto service or the same traffic police to verify the data about the stolen car with the autoblockchain will burn the "impostor" with the broken numbers to the full. Any bona fide purchaser can do the same by using the search directly at the car market, at a car dealership, or even in correspondence with the seller at Avito, simply asking him to drop a link to the car page on the blockchain .

Moreover, if traffic police simply provide protection against frauds with their databases, transferring information to the blockchain, other interested organizations will be able to store additional information important to them: for example, by drivers, mileage - up to taking into account all THAT and replacing branded stickers on bodywork.

The only paid service in the autoblocker is to create a new record there, which costs up to 0.3 emc (8 cents) and less. This board is installed algorithmically and is only needed to protect the Emergency network from spam. Earn HashCoins and Emercoin plan on introducing autoblockchain.
