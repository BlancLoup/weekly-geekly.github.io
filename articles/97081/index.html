Choosing a USB flash drive

About 2 weeks ago we already showed you the speed testing service for flash drives .
During this time we managed to test about 1300 flash drives.


Development


In general, the service was perceived positively, but after trying to recommend it to our friends for choosing flash drives, we realized that it was practically useless for the average user. Today we have taken the first step towards our user to meet. Now you can upload photos of drives , because for most people the main selection criterion is the appearance of the device.
All photos of drives are pre-moderated in order to obtain a catalog with high-quality images. At the moment we have uploaded photos of the most high-speed flash drives and will gradually replenish our base.

Some statistics




Average read speed: 20.4 MB / s
Average write speed: 9.36 MB / s

So far this is all we wanted to say. Use our usbflashspeed.com service, and only then we will develop and become more convenient!
