Astro Lords: Oort Cloud

Astro Lords: Oort Cloud - space shooter. Thirty game projects from the CIS countries were launched at the CS and a good half of them are space shooters with various genres.
Specifically, this project is a space-based multiplayer strategy from Ukraine, which was among the finalists of the Selected Projects contest within the framework of Game Connection Europe 2013.
The game promises everything: war, commerce, research, the search for artifacts and of course the robbing of cows.

The project has already collected ¬£ 7.005 from ¬£ 100,000 and will last 34 days.



Stalin vs. Martians 3

Stalin vs. Martians 3 is a hell of a tresch that promises to rip off all the cream from such hits as X-COM, Cut the Rope, Angry birds, etc. For me, this project will be remembered only by the fact that in his Kickstarter video they speak Russian abusive proverbs. The translation of this proverb into English is especially good.

The project has already collected $ 1,953 out of $ 125,000 and will last 18 days.



KIT ^ N ^ KATE

KIT ^ N ^ KATE is a kids cartoon about kittens from Pavel Muntyan. Yes, yes, from the very Muntyan who was at the origins of Masyani and Mr. Freeman. But, as practice shows, a famous person in Russia is absolutely not enough to raise funds abroad.

The project has already collected $ 1,794 from $ 300,000 and will last 10 days.



LUMINES - make your life brighter

LUMINES is a project of luminous neon clothing for people and animals. Rather, it is clothing with piping from a special glowing cord. The guys themselves are from the city of Odessa, and their charges are going very slowly.

The project has already collected $ 161 from $ 15,000 and will last 40 days.

Pettls - a piece of nature in your interior

Gigs 2 go

Gigs 2 Go is not a domestic project, but a low-quality copy of the concept from the Art. Lebedev Studio. If Tyoma in this concept meant disposable flash drives that will be completely recycled, then the authors of this project simply packed a regular USB flash drive into cardboard and made them into detachable blocks. Since 2011, the concept studio of Lebedev has flown around the network and even got into many foreign print media.
