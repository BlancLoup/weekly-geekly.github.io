CyanogenMod9 Alpha arrives on the path of the HP Touchpad



If you want to run Android 4.0 on your HP Touchpad instead of watching a video like someone else does, then know that your time has come, because You can already try to do this with CyanogenMod9 Alpha, which you just recently rolled out. True, at the moment it is akin to living on the edge of a knife, because alpha will still cause you some discomfort. This includes access to the Android Market, hardware video acceleration, and camera usage.
If you want to roll out your version of Ice Cream Sandwich, then you will undoubtedly be able to do this due to the fact that the sources of this miracle are already here. Also interesting is the fact that on the HP Touchpad you can hold several OSes (CM7, CM9 and HP webOS, for example). All the necessary information and software you can find in one of the threads on RootzWiki .
