GTUGs of Russia with the support of Google invite you to hackathons on Google Chrome and a contest with prizes

What is Chrome Hackathons & Contest?
This is a competition between developers who want to create the world's best extensions for Google Chrome and Chrome Web Apps. The main hackathons will be held on August 27 and 28, on weekends, with key speakers from Google: Ilmari Heikkinen (Developer Programs Engineer) in Moscow and Mike West (Chrome Advocate) in St. Petersburg. Hackathons will also be held on the same days in Ufa, Voronezh and Omsk.

The purpose of the competition?
The goal is to make our favorite Google Chrome browser more convenient for us and for users by creating excellent web applications and extensions based on the requests and wishes collected from Chrome users .

Expansion: the extension should not repeat existing (but may be an improvement to existing extensions). You can rely on the extensions suggested by users , but these extensions should nevertheless fall under the category Browser Actions or Page Actions ; in this technical competition, we do not consider extensions such as Themes or Override Pages . Extensions are evaluated by solving real-world user problems and technical implementation.

Application: the application should ideally not repeat existing ones. Applications are evaluated by solving real-world user problems and technical implementation.

When will hackathons and competition take place?
The hackathons and the contest itself will be organized in 4 stages:
  1. Chrome Hackathons - August 27-28: 2-day event in 5 cities.
  2. Chrome Contest - August 29 - October 8: An online contest that takes place throughout the month, developers complete their applications and extensions and are preparing for the final.
  3. Final - DemoFest - October 9: The event takes place at the Moscow office of Google.
  4. Presentation and awarding of winners at GDD 2011 - October 10: The event takes place at GDD 2011 Russia .

Winners
Three winners receive Mac Book Air laptops, which will be presented during the awards ceremony at Google Developer Day 2011 Russia . Additional 4th and 5th places will be awarded with valuable prizes.

How will Chrome Hackathon be organized in my city?
In each of the cities, the hackathon will be conducted with the active support of the local GTUG group - the Google Technology User Group .
