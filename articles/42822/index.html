Wednesday, Crocus Expo, In-fo-com!

All roads on Wednesday will lead to the Moscow exhibition center "Crocus Expo". The largest congress of information and communication technology professionals in Russia, Infocom-2008 , will open there. As always, until Friday the program of the event is crammed with reports, round tables and exposition presentations with the participation of the most eminent representatives of business and government structures. Infocom is supported by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation.

The entrance to the exhibition part and most of the reports are open to all comers. To do this, you need to register on the site by filling out a special form, and then print a free invitation ticket.

In the framework of Infocom on the same days, the Russian Internet Week (RIW) conference will be organized by ROCIT. Registration for it is combined with the total and is also free.

By the way, precisely because all the roads on Wednesday lead to Crocus, it is worthwhile to plan your time more carefully and be prepared for terrible traffic jams at the entrances / exits to the Moscow Ring Road. The new way of the congress from the inner side of the Koltsevoy is intended to ease the way to the exhibition.

It is recommended to choose a new route through the Strogino station for your own trip through the subway, from where free buses will run on Infocom days (a visitor ticket is required for entry).
