Sketch e01 - 100 seconds about Nokia

Sketch is a new video clip. It is dedicated to everything related to IT, but with one limitation. The length of each sketch is 100 seconds. No greetings, no intro, no razmusolivaniya, only in essence. This is the exact opposite of a video blog! The point of the rubric is to discuss current issues or stories from the IT world.
I'd like to understand if this format is interesting for the community.

The first issue: about Nokia.
We take a low start from 80 seconds.



Text translation, for those who did not have time to hear everything!

More recently, a smart phone has been associated with Nokia.
In 2007, the iPhone appeared, which, on Apple's notice, was ahead of everyone by as much as five years. The army of simbianovodov openly smirked at such statements.
However, after a while, Nokia was already in the position of a catch-up. The developers literally were still iPhone OS. Market usability and high-quality software.
Touch Symbian seriously inferior to the brainchild of Apple and usability, and the number of software. Nokia is releasing Maemo and presenting a few bold smart-tablets on it. This is followed by a partnership with Intel, and their joint brainchild of MeeGo, which to this day has seen the world with only one eye. And then squinting.
Symbian released a new version of the OS, supposedly sharpened by touch control, but the hardware of smartphones is still at a weak level. And the OS itself has not changed much.
Meanwhile, the world is conquering the OS from Google - Android. It has recently become the most popular in the United States and has already bypassed Finns in some other markets for quite some time. Symbian is steadily losing ground.
Perhaps the turning point will be the recently signed partnership between Nokia and Microsoft. Now the top smartphones of the manufacturer will be on the Windows Phone OS, although it is not clear yet which version. Will it save Nokia?
