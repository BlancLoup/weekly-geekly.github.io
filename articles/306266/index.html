RuHaxe 5 Online Conference

Already today, on Sunday, July 24, at 15:00 Moscow time, the fifth online conference of the Russian-speaking Haxe community will be held. As at past conferences, it will be possible not only to watch and listen to presentations on Haxe-related technologies, but also to ask any questions to their authors.

Update : changes in the composition of participants. SlavaRa will not be able to speak due to sudden circumstances. Instead, Yaroslav Sivakov will talk about the creation of NanoFL and the possibilities that this editor provides developers and animators.

If you do not know yet what Haxe is, you can read a brief description , and then watch the slides and the recording of the report from a recent speech by Daniel Korostelev at Nevosoft.
