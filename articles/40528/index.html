Spam will be no more. But letters too. Mail.ru

In my last post, Mail.ru banned Yandex, I reported about blocking one of the Yandex gateways by the mail.ru administration, there were several versions and few emotions.

UPD: I have a mailbox on Yandex, and I send an email to mail.ru

Let me remind you, I sent a request to the mail.ru administration to unlock the Yandex relay, to which I received an answer today:
Good day!

Your IP (77.88.32.20) is closed for spam and cannot be opened.

Sincerely, Mail.ru Customer Support Service

And you, thank you, and you the same ...

77.88.32.20 is the address of the relay forwards4.yandex.ru , through which Yandex sends mail, at least mine.

In my opinion, the fact that one of the largest portals of the Russian Internet blocks mail delivery from another, no less large portal is a significant event.
