What to do if your favorite iPhone drowned?

Or spilled tea on your favorite mouse? Or the keyboard? Or a stereo?

It is better to try to quickly disconnect from the voltage and remove the water. And it flows into any gap, even into the smallest - under the influence of capillary forces.
