The company Crytek announced a new engine CryEngine V


Screenshot image on new CE V using DX 12

This Tuesday, March 15, the company Crytek announced the release of its new engine, CryEngine V, which will be distributed free of charge . Take advantage of the new development of the company will be every user without any restrictions, including developers will have access to the source code of the engine.

Among the main innovations:

  • C # support;
  • DirectX 12 support;
  • Advanced volumetric system, adapted to work on VR projects, which will optimize 3D rendering and minimize drawdowns in performance;
  • The new particle system, which allows you to focus all the load when rendering in real time on the GPU;
  • New launcher and redesigned UI;
  • Support FMOD Studio;
  • Dedicated channel CRYENGINE Q & A for communication developers.



Video from the presentation of Crytek at GDC 2016

The new engine is distributed absolutely free, but users have the opportunity to financially encourage the work of the company. On the page through which CE V is distributed , you can donate funds to Crytek or the Dev Fund, an independent studio support fund.
