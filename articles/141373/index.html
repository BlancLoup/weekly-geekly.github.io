Map Affairs - GIS service placement and search of personal orders

The idea that formed the basis of this service is simple and, we hope, will be in demand by the Internet masses. In short, the essence is as follows. This project helps people to quickly find assistants: teachers, nannies, promoters, couriers, laborers and other professionals who are ready to help you in your urgent (or not so) business for a fee.
