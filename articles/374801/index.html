Eclipse of the Sun and the ISS: a rare photo



Such a phenomenon as an eclipse of the sun has attracted the attention of people since ancient times. Once it was considered a harbinger of all troubles, a manifestation of the evil will of some higher powers. Now we all know that there is nothing mystical here, and calmly admire the eclipse. For astronomers, an eclipse of the Sun is of scientific interest, so many observatories in the world have observed a recent phenomenon.

The Spanish observatory Fregenal de la Sierra was able to take rare pictures - the passage of the ISS on the solar disk. The ISS covered this distance in just 0.6 seconds. The photo turned out to be just wonderful , and the workers of the observatory decided to share the image with the whole world.
