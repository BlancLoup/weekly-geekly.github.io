Windows 8 may appear in two years



Yesterday, a message appeared on the Web, on the official Microsoft blog (not only in English, but for some reason on the Dutch website) that Windows 8 will appear on the market in about two years. Now, as stated in the same post, active work is underway on this OS. Note also the identification of users - the system will now not work with a password system, but face recognition (most likely, the password system will also be there, not everyone has a webcam, right?).
