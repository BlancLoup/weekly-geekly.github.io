DevCon 2014: announcement of the second wave of speakers and reports

Let me remind you that within the framework of the conference we plan to split all the reports into several thematic groups:

  • Development of client applications for the Windows and Windows Phone platforms
  • Cloud Computing and Web
  • Tools, development methodologies and programming languages
  • Corporate development, data processing
  • Virtual international track, combining reports in English

Today we present to you the second portion of the reports and speakers for each of these groups.
