Kazakhstan launched a program of state support sites

KazContent, a program to support Kazakhstan's websites, started its work in Kazakhstan. At this stage of the program, sites are provided with assistance in the form of free VPS. The program is designed to increase the number of quality sites in Kaznet, to enable novice developers to grow and develop their offspring, to assist social projects.



In order to receive assistance, the site must correspond to several points:
  • Being in the zone .kz
  • Have attendance of at least 6000 unique users per month (approximately 200 unique visitors per day)
  • The site should not be present materials violating applicable law.
  • This should not be a corporate site or a business card site or an online store.

and some other points. More information about them can be found in the methodology .

All sites that meet the conditions may apply for participation in the program. Applications sent to kazcontentkz@gmail.com for the same address, you need to share the statistics of your site on Google Analytics.
