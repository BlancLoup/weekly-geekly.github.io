The Big Four are preparing to share 5G frequencies

The Big Four operators, which the so-called LTE Union represents, are ready to support the 3.4-3.8 GHz sharing project for 5G networks. This follows from the letter of the LTE Union to the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media, writes Kommersant. True, the frequencies of the specified range have not yet been allocated, since the development of technology in this spectrum is not coordinated with the security agencies.

So far, the state plans to test 5G in a less attractive range for operators, so the consortium in question may not be formed. Operators themselves believe that the sharing model allows participants to share operating costs for infrastructure development.
