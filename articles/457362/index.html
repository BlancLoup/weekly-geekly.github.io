Action from RUVDS: prepare the server in the summer

Summer is definitely a great time: heat, sun, light clothing and shoes, fruit and pleasant evenings. For most business areas, summer also has a cool advantage: against the background of a downturn in the business season and a decrease in demand, you can catch your breath a little with conditioned office air and put your infrastructure in order. For example, move to new capacities, review the IT management policies in a company, conduct an audit, select optimization points. We in RUVDS were not too lazy prepared for you a discount of 30%, as well as an emotional, a little even nostalgic surprise. Brief details under the cut - read, and we ran to write you another interesting article.



Why?


On June 1, we held the Duke Con festival in St. Petersburg, dedicated to the 22nd anniversary of the release of the Duke Nukem 3D game. Duke Nukem, he is also Nuclear Duke, a legendary computer game, one of the most iconic in the industry.

In honor of this event, we decided to hold a summer promotion for RUVDS clients .

What is the action?


In June, we increase the discount when paying for the year to 30%. All customers who have paid for the year from June 1 to 30 will also be able to participate in the raffle of nuclear prizes from legendary game designer Richard Levelord Gray, who created the best levels of Duke Nukem 3D.

Nuclear Prizes:

  • Dinner at a restaurant in Moscow with Richard Gray
  • 10 copies of the game Duke Nukem in the box with the autograph of Levelord
  • 5 hoodies with the image of Duke Nukem autographed by Levelord

And I do not risk paying a year in advance?


It is a logical question. So you will pay a year, and then it will begin ... No, it will not begin! Because the company RUVDS guarantees:

  • High speed - all data centers have redundancy of communication channels - at least three of 10 Gbit / s each, connected to the largest nodes M-9 and M-10 for Moscow and LD-8, AMX-IX, DE-CIX for European data centers;

  • Convenient tools for managing tariffs and parameters of VDS servers online;
  • 24/7 support, which is not an empty sound, but really works;
  • Steep server configurations;
  • Flexible management of installed software;
  • Reliability and fault tolerance: all equipment is located in data centers of a level not lower than TIER III in Russia, Switzerland, Great Britain and Germany;
  • Analytics and stuff.

The business year flies by unnoticed - because behind a dense grid of work tasks you simply do not notice the change of days and sometimes even the seasons. Why not save a good amount and at the same time use a really worthy solution that won't let you down?
