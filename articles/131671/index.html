Mouse and keyboard for PS3. Pleasant addition or necessary attribute?

These are the mouse and keyboard created specifically for the PS3 company HORI. In principle, the mouse and keyboard for the PS3 were created before, but the HORI product clearly stands out against the others with its convenience and ergonomics. However, only field tests will pass the final verdict. First of all, the new product will appear in stores in Japan and the United States and will be sold there at a suggested retail price of $ 99.



The keyboard presents most of the keys with DualShock'a. The main buttons are visually enlarged and positioned so that they are equally easy to reach with both the index finger and the thumb. Changing default settings is regulated by a switch located at the top of the keyboard. The other 2 switches are responsible for the sensitivity of the mouse. The mouse itself is connected to the keyboard via a cable, and the keyboard itself is connected to the PS3 using mini-USB. The HORI product is exclusively for PS3 and is not compatible with PCs or other mowers.

Equal conditions for users of consoles and PCs are the main requirement for the upcoming multiplatform online shooters, since the mouse and keyboard of the players on the PCs put console gamers in a losing position. Adaptation of the super-weapon RS under the console should erase this difference.
