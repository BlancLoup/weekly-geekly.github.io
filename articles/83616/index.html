Badges on Habrahabr

Hello! Today badges appeared in the profile of most users.
Badges are a system of awards that are available to the user when certain indicators are achieved. Badges are assigned automatically, usually in the morning, when even the most resistant geeks still fall asleep. The list of possible badges is available as sorting on the users page and is not considered complete. By tradition, in the comments to this topic, ideas about the introduction of additional badges are accepted, so to speak, to expand the current range.

So, what are the badges?

  • The author . A user who has published more than 10 topics; Top rating must be more than 50 points.
  • Star A user who has published more than 25 topics; Top rating must be more than 50 points.
  • Legend . A user who has published more than 50 topics; Top rating must be more than 50 points.
  • Commentator . User left 5 comments; Comment rating must be more than 50 points.
  • Old-timers . A user registered more than 3 years ago (at the time of calculation), with a karma greater than 100.
  • Podcaster A user who has published at least 5 zahabrenny podcasts.
  • Translator . A user who has published at least 5 zahabrenny translations.
  • Comedian . The user who published in the blog "Humor on the site" more than 5 topics.
  • Troll . User with karma less than -100.
  • Hacked . User with karma> 0.
  • Off User with karma <0.


Update : There was a bust with trolls and super trolls.
Update2 : Fixed a bug with the accrual of the badge "Author". Thank you for the reports.
