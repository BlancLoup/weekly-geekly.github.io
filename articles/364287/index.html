Google closes the development center in Russia

Google Corporation is closing its engineering office in Russia in connection with the restriction of Internet freedoms regarding the use of data by foreign companies operating in the country, writes the Wall Street Journal.



The publication notes that the largest search engine can save part of the state in the areas of sales, business partnerships, technical support, marketing and communications. And according to Bloomberg, in parallel with the withdrawal of the development department from the country, Google plans to increase investment in business operations in Russia.
