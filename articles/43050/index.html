VGTRK restore order in the web

The media holding VGTRK filed claims for copyright protection against the Internet company Mail.ru and the social network vkontakte. At the same time, the All-Russian State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company promised to sue any resource that posts its content without coordination. The company is trying to ensure that the websites containing video content, without waiting for a request from the copyright holder, independently regulate relations with the owners of all posted videos. Representatives of Internet companies object that they are technically unable to independently track illegal content.
