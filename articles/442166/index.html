Alternative metro scheme of Minsk

My name is Zvyagin Ivan and I am a designer from Minsk. At the end of July, my city had a beautiful metro scheme, which we painstakingly drew in the evenings of 8 months. The task of the art director sounded like this: you need to design such a metro scheme so that other cities envy.



Centre


Showed in detail, used the main landmarks: street names, parks, river and attractions. Refused the numbering of stations, allocated exits. Inside maintained a uniform density of inscriptions and objects. Stations outside the center are located horizontally and without geographic details. Particular attention is paid to trees and buildings that do not interfere with reading information.




Line being built


Marked by a pleasant raster. Indicated the year of the planned opening.
We do not show stations that are only in the plan.
