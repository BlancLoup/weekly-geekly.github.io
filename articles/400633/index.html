Among the new HyperX on CES 2017 were shown:
ALLOY RGB gaming keyboard - the novelty is made in a completely new style with dedicated multimedia control buttons and supports LED backlighting with 16 million colors. Users can control the backlight, save and load various profiles at any time, change colors and modes of operation. In addition, ALLOY RGB is equipped with a special metal frame, which provides it with stability and durability.



Gaming Mouse Pulsefire is the first HyperX mouse created for professional players. The novelty is equipped with an optical sensor and four sensitivity modes with LED indicators (400/800/1600/3200 dpi). In this case, the sensitivity settings can be changed without the use of special software. Pulsefire has everything that real gamers need, and its price will pleasantly surprise them.






ALLOY FPS Red and Brown gaming keyboards - the new HyperX ALLOY FPS mechanical gaming keyboards with Cherry MX Brown / Red switches are equipped with a sturdy steel frame and take up minimal space on the table so that the player can freely move the mouse during network skirmishes. Now gamers have a choice of new types of switches and the ability to put textured red WASD keys on the keyboard, with which it is much more convenient to play first-person shooters. In addition, new items are equipped with a dynamic LED backlight in red, which can be controlled without the use of additional software. And for lovers of LAN parties and trips, there is a removable cable and a special bag for carrying the keyboard.



The Cloud Revolver S headset is the most advanced and perfect HyperX headset to date, with built-in support for 7.1 Dolby Surround Sound surround sound. HyperX used Dolby Headphone technology to create a new USB dongle that delivers top-quality sound with just the touch of a button. Cloud Revolver S will give users maximum comfort, excellent sound quality and realistic sound scene, which is especially important in CS: GO and other three-dimensional shooters.
