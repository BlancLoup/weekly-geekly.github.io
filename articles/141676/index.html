Art positioning


Imagine this situation: you are going to start / launch a startup with a team, be proud of your idea, work day and night, and one day, returning home late at night you meet your old friend. The following dialog takes place:

...
- And what are you doing then?
- Yes, we start a startup with the guys, so cool!
- Oh, cool! And what a startup?
- Well listen, now I will explain. In general, this is a directory with music in which you can evaluate it, like and share it with friends. And most importantly, everything is free!
- Oh, I see. Okay, I went, see you.

And you go and think, why is your idea not appreciated.

Approximately the same thing happens when a user comes to your site and does not get an answer to the question of who you are, how you can help him and what you are better than others. Next, I will try to talk about how to make a correct opinion about yourself and give examples.

Basic principles of positioning


The moment when the user gets to the site of your project for the first time is the most important. That's when you have the only chance to make a first impression about yourself. The more understandable your goals, objectives, competitive advantages, the more likely that the user will have a good impression. By the way, people tend to share with others :)

To create the correct image using 3 elements:
  • Formula
  • Tagline
  • Functional presentation


Formula


This is the very brief description, consisting of 3-8 words, which you will tell friends without risking being misunderstood. From the project formula, each user must understand who you are, what opportunities you provide or what problem you can solve. The formula, by virtue of its brevity, can not describe the entire functionality of the project, but should make an assumption about the user.

Tagline


The slogan and the formula is often confused. The slogan is a concise, easy to remember phrase that expresses the essence of the competitive advantage of the project or some of its functions. The slogan often carries less information load than the formula, and rather contributes to the formation of a general impression of the project.

Functional presentation


Under the presentation of the functional means the description of the capabilities of the service in any form: video, presentation, demo version, product screenshot.
