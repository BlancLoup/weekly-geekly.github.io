- ‚ÄúWhat documents are needed for registration of copyright on the site design?‚Äù
A site is a complex product, various elements of which may be protected by various legal instruments (ie, as trademarks, inventions, industrial designs, computer programs, databases, other objects of copyright).
To document the rights to the RID (results of intellectual activity) that belong to you as a customer, state them explicitly in the contract, and after the work is completed, detail the result as much as possible, for example, as an attachment to the Act of Work Acceptance, which may contain screenshots site flowcharts, listings, user cases, etc.
Then you will have a certain time to decide on the method of patent protection of IP (six months for an invention, twelve months for an industrial design). As inventions, it is possible to patent the algorithms for obtaining and processing information, as industrial designs, graphic design elements.
The rights to textual and some graphic elements of the site can be documented by depositing with a publication or without publishing in the depository objects of copyright (for example, such a service is in the Russian State Library).
Computer programs and databases can be registered with the patent office by partial deposition in text form.
Logos, names and slogans can be tried to be registered in the patent office as trademarks (including, volume, sound, etc.).
However, the main thing here is to decide whether you need it all, since the cost of a set of measures for the protection of rights can repeatedly exceed the amount indicated by you for development (about 120-150k).
