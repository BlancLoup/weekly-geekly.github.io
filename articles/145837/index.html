Crowdfunding (skladchiny) in Russia: Interviews with the coordinators of the project "Tugize"

Many have heard about popular overseas crowdfunding projects, quite often there are publications about their achievements and ideas funded on them.
But what about similar Russian projects and why are they not developing so rapidly?

To understand, I decided to ask about this directly from crowdfunding projects working on the Russian market.

Today I prepared the second publication from the ‚ÄúCrowdfunding (folding) cycle in Russia‚Äù series: an interview with the coordinators of the Tughez project

"Tuguesa" - This is a public association / syndicate of people who want to do something useful.



- Tell us a little about the project.
Tuguesa is a targeted charity society. They threw off 100 rubles each, bought, drove, and reported. We sleep further, or we participate in the current affairs of groups, who wants to.


- Now the prevalence of the method of collective financing is gaining momentum in the US, slightly inferior in Europe. Despite the fact that in Russia the first projects began to appear in 2008, in our country they are still developing slowly and hard. What do you think is the reason?
  1. Each project in Russia, as a rule, is one person, and when it is exhausted, the project stalls.
  2. Project processes are hidden, solved within the project, there is no publicity. As a result, people interested in the project have no idea about what is happening. No comments, no criticism, new blood does not enter the project orbit.
  3. Vagina of the population, especially in the regions. Fear of the Internet and registration, fear of commenting, expressing your opinion. Fear of responsibility and change, with a great desire for these changes. The desire to be only an observer.
  4. The ambitiousness of the project participants: I above all and the common cause in particular, the reluctance to cooperate, the internal struggle for imaginary power, the lack of diplomacy. If in reality a leader easily suppresses one's opinion, then in virtual reality it is extremely difficult. It is necessary to negotiate, but few people know how.
  5. Cooperation and qual. It is difficult to cooperate with other projects. Snobbery in relation to social networks like Odnoklassniki or dating sites. Swing only for a long time already mastered resources. Again, snobbery and elitism towards all.
  6. Fear of responsibility and submission, poor contact.

If to summarize, the development of projects with collective financing is hindered by the excessive ambitions of the participants and the lack of system quality of runet users.

- There is a popular opinion in runet that our mentality simply will not allow crowdfunding projects to truly develop, what do you think about this?
Normal mentality, great people. You just need to report, show results, download and do it regularly.

- Often, the lack of good project results is explained by people's distrust, it can be like distrust of the site itself (all the money is stolen), the method of financing itself (it still does not work to collect any significant amount, therefore I will not invest). How do you think this is relevant and how are you struggling with the distrust of users?
The collection should be organized with maximum convenience and minimum amounts. Again, reports, checks, photos, the current discussion - everything is as open as possible. Russia is a collective consciousness, people want to see examples. It is useless to beat the heel in the chest with a shout of "I AM HONEST", even if it is really honest - the people have been deceived for too long.

- What obstacles to the development of the project do you see from the state (tax, legal and other)? Perhaps there are any other difficulties in the development of the project?
None Tuguesa is an unofficial organization, plus good connections and high-quality advice from Tugazy members. Difficulties are possible, but after 3 years.

- Could you disclose any quantitative indicators achieved by the project?
Everything on the site ( together.ru ). Hundreds of people in groups, thousands of collected amounts, dozens of completed current and related projects, for all projects there are reports for 2 years that are posted in LJ ( ru-the-people.livejournal.com ). Come and see for yourself.
