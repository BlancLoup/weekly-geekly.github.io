Who to entrust your business? Part 1 - Accounting

The Moscow market of business services is extremely rich and rich. We have to carefully study its flora and fauna, because our service company is not the only way to get good service. We recently did a little research, popularized accounting and legal support, studied the proposals of secretaries and business assistants . Typically, marketers keep the results of research in secret, but we decided to tell about them. I want to believe that this will save entrepreneurs a couple of hours in search of a service option suitable for their company. Let's start with accounting.



We explored accounting options that suit businesses from small to medium. It turned out only 7 categories, each of which we tried to give an objective description, determine the price plug and highlight the most noticeable (read, advertised) proposals.

Stuffing documents


These tools are quite simple and perform exactly one task: they help to quickly and almost free of charge prepare standard accounting and legal documents, such as bills, acts or contracts. One of these packs can be shamelessly handed to your employees: customer service managers, accountants, and in case of urgent need - use them yourself, spending a little time to figure out the details and texture.

The program Business Pack , the most well-known in a professional environment, is a time-tested free utility that allows you to work with a bunch of different forms, from familiar to all, to checks, prices and payment requirements. You will have to get used to the interface, but this is rather a common problem of frivolous software. All data at once are exported to 1SK for further processing by an accountant. The only minus but serious enough for 2014 is that the program is installed locally and works only under Windows.

Fashionable cloud services nowadays are deprived of this minus and give access to the created documents from any device and from anywhere in the world. Formz.ru and freshdoc.ru seem to be the most interesting: they allow you to work with a huge number of forms and templates, and the second one, in addition, has a fun papkuyu interface with pleasant animations.

In the rearguard of cloud-based work with documents, single-tasking packing of accounts, such as naoplatu.ru and invoicer.ru, is wobbling . However, they perform their functions perfectly, allowing for a couple of clicks to prepare an invoice for payment and send it to your client. There are probably a whole bunch of such services, we chose the most visible ones.



To suit
Anyone who needs to quickly invoice, create a contract or any other simple document. Will help those whom the chief has bothered to work with documents, and in the Excel lazily poking around.

How much are
Free or for little money: from 100 to 1 000 rubles per month.

Links
Business Pak , formz.ru , freshdoc.ru , naoplatu.ru , invoicer.ru

Accounting software


The name of the main domestic vendor of accounting programs is familiar even to those who are far from bookkeeping, business and computers in general. The great and terrible 1C reigns in this market almost completely, taking advantage of the inescapable demand among professional accountants. What a sin to conceal, we ourselves conduct the accounting of our clients with the help of solutions from the company of Boris Georgievich Nuraliev. 1C is more than a software company; it is a whole phenomenon that gave rise to a separate profession of 1C programmers . There are slightly fewer custom builds of this program than the installed legal copies.

Alas, long-term communication of an ordinary person with 1C threatens to result in sleep disturbances and emotional breakdowns. In addition, the company's sales policy sometimes raises questions , and the cost of implementation and maintenance varies from a couple of thousands to incredible transcendental amounts. 1C is installed locally or on the terminal, but there are companies that provide access to this solution through a browser.

In 1C there are a lot of followers and analogs: Sail , Best , Contour-Accounting Active , but they are attracted only by the price. Unique enterprise solutions like those offered by SAP will find you when you earn your first instagram.



To suit
Professional accountants and especially stubborn business owners who are ready to independently explore the rocky bottom of accounting.

How much is
From 3,000 (for the version for IP on special modes) to infinity plus the cost of maintenance and installation of updates. The full cost will help calculate the regional distributor. Or a favorite torrent tracker :)

Online Accounting


With this increasingly popular phenomenon, we are familiar firsthand. Many Button employees have worked in the past on Elbe , an online accounting office for SKB Kontur. Today, there are a great number of such services: My Business , Sky , AssistantPro , My Finance , ImBoss and their ilk. Their functionality is almost identical: they help to independently keep records, count taxes, submit reports and work with documents. They are suitable for small companies on USN and UTII and are quite cheap.

Online accounting is a great solution for start-ups. If the service is done well (like Elba), then after 15 minutes, a person who is not even familiar with the term ‚Äúaccounting‚Äù will be able to get comfortable with it. Another pleasant side effect: after a couple of months of work, a businessman will begin to understand a little about the intricacies of tax accounting and reporting, which does not hurt when the business starts to grow, and the owner will have to communicate with a live accountant or an accounting company.

Some services, for example, My Business and ImBoss go a little further and offer partial accounting outsourcing to the user. In this case, they themselves keep records and submit reports on the basis of documents and transactions reflected by the user in the personal account. The rest of the online accounting is still limited to expert advice and useful educational content in their blozhikah and social networks.



To suit
Small companies will help save some money at the start, graduates of accounting courses will make them feel like real professionals who do everything exactly and on time.

How much are
  • from 400 to 5 000 rubles per month depending on a set of options (for example, reporting via the Internet, payroll, work with documents);
  • from 1,000 to 10,000 rubles in excess of this amount for accounting outsourcing;
  • many services have free fares for the smallest ones, but we recommend supporting the guys with a specie - they deserve it :)

Links
Elba , My Business , Sky , AssistantPro , My Finance , ImBoss

Accounting company


To outsource non-key business functions is a trend of the twenty-first century. There will definitely be no problems with finding a bukhkompaniya: the market is packed with similar offers a little more than full. Outsourcers are happy to take away your accounting, preparing and sending reports, keep records, help with tax optimization and give you full advice on telephone and email.

Unlike a freelancer, a accounting company will give more confidence, supported by a normal, competent contract, but in most cases it will not be able to provide that very individual approach. A lot of accountants work in such establishments, they exchange experience and help each other, so you will not find yourself in a situation where reporting is tomorrow, and boo went to justify his short name, became ill with inflammation of procrastination, or even disappeared in an unknown direction with the original accounting documents.

State accountant


A caricature aunt with painted tow, busily correcting glasses with a huge diopter to get a better look at the cards in solitaire - this is a myth. These, too, are found, but if you carefully go through the hunting portals, you will find a sweet and sincere person who will devote all his time only to your business. We have become adept in hiring classroom accountants and will easily give a couple of tips if you ask in the comments.

The advantages of an accountant in the state are obvious: he is always at his side, you can talk with him, hold his hand, or hurt him with a keyboard for an untimely report. He (and more often, she) will always tell and advise the winning option, because his emotional balance, stability of position and salary directly depend on the success of your business. A happy accountant will reciprocate. This level of service for other categories, unfortunately, is unattainable :)
