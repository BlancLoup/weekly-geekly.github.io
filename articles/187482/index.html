Why didn't the timed decorator shut down on the second cpuload call?

Recall that the global name timed is associated with a decorated decorator, i.e. with new_decorated function; it means that the timed.enabled = False line timed.enabled = False changes the attribute of the new_decorated function - the common ‚Äúwrapper‚Äù of both decorators.
