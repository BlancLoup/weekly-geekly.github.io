Photo! 3D Screensaver - visit your own photo gallery

Source: myfreesoft.ru Today I was shown a wonderful three-dimensional screen saver Photo! 3D Screensaver, which uses your favorite photos as the main material. Remarkably, the program does not just include a slide show, replacing one frame by another after a specified period of time, but allows you to create virtual three-dimensional exhibition halls. Have you ever walked through an art gallery, where your favorite photos are exhibited instead of paintings? Photo! 3D Screensaver allows you to do this without any problems. You can admire not only your favorite and memorable photos, but also the environment itself. Beautiful 3D-graphics, pleasant and relaxed atmosphere. The template of the gallery can be chosen to your taste. The program site contains a lot of various realistic three-dimensional galleries made in various styles: a modern exhibition hall, a Renaissance courtyard, a mysterious medieval castle or an ancient Greek temple. It is also worth noting that you can walk around the gallery either automatically or manually, moving using the keyboard or mouse. Choosing photos is pretty simple. Just go to the settings Photo! 3D Screensaver, select the folders or files you want to place in the gallery, and then click OK. You can turn on the soundtrack, in this case, a trip to the gallery will be accompanied by your favorite melody.
