I want to share a great service for creating animation on the HTML5 platform called Mugeda . Immediately make a reservation, the service is not mine, but I couldn‚Äôt tell about it and let alone tell about it.





Perhaps something similar already exists, but this project at least deserves your attention.

The first thing that catches the eye is the editor himself. Well, why not Flash, you will say and you will be right. And in fact, why invent something new when there is an old proven way to create animation?



Sooner or later, such a tool had to appear. Of course, there are not so many opportunities at the moment, but it is quite suitable for simple animation. Create the same banner will not be difficult.

The following tools and primitives are available:

I think no need to voice. I am very pleased that you can upload a picture and sound effect. It is possible to create layers, add cameras, use hot keys (read pdf here ).

Well, the most important thing in my opinion is the export of animation. This process is very simple and fast. The service itself will compress and pack everything, and you only need to save the archive to your computer. The archive contains a separate file with your animation, their library for playback (mugeda_player _ *. Js), html file for preview and all the media files that are used (pictures, sounds, etc.). After that you can insert your video or banner wherever you want.

The only note is the size of the scripts. The library for playing mugeda_player_0.1.0.js weighs 184 Kb. The weight of the animation file depends on the complexity of the animation. Perhaps in the future weight will be reduced.

Very pleased that there is no need to download third-party software to work. All data is stored in the service cloud and you can start creating at any time. You can also easily share your work with friends or site visitors simply by publishing your animation.
