Google News has overtaken the NY Times

Rupert Murdoch will definitely be displeased. According to comScore statistics , in the fall of 2009, Google News outperformed all media in the list of the main news sources on the Internet.



If you look at these charts, it becomes clear the anxiety of the bastions of journalism. The news aggregators are clearly ahead of all the major media in attendance and, obviously, take their audience.

However, according to the number of page views and the time spent on the site, aggregators still have much to develop. The same Google News is inferior to CNN for each of these indicators by about three times.

In November 2009, the global audience of Google News was 100 million, more than the NY Times (92 million) and CNN (66 million). Ahead only Yahoo News (138 million). Interestingly, we have never heard of Murdoch complaints about Yahoo.

Next in the ranking of the most popular online media follow the Chinese QQ.com News (monthly audience of 53 million), MSN News and the British BBC (48 million each).
