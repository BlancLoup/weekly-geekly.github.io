Byrobot Drone Fighter - the world's first combat quadcopter. Team battles are like a computer game - taking into account "lives", special attacks, taking into account statistics of victories and defeats. Also, the model is notable for the possibility of fast self-repair - in Byrobot it is easy to replace any part in a minute without a screwdriver (as in the LEGO designer). Ease of control plays a big role - you can learn how to fly in 15-20 minutes, and you can actually manage the control with even one hand (below is a link to proof). Thanks to its compact size, the Byrobot Drone Fighter is suitable for playing indoors - it is sufficient for maneuvers of even a small room and it is absolutely safe for people, animals and things.



The possibility of playing together is not just an interesting activity, but also an important socialization tool for children with disabilities - a joint pastime for family members, classmates, and work colleagues. Teamwork, live communication (which is not a single computer game!), The development of spatial thinking skills - we are sure that Byrobot Drone Fighter is useful for users of any age, especially children.
