500 words per minute without preparation



The usual speed of reading in Russian is 120-180 words per minute. With the help of long workouts you can increase it somewhere up to 600 words per minute. Most recently, a small startup from Boston introduced a new technology called Spritz .

I was able to read 500 words per minute without preparation. The developers claim that you can reach 1000; without prejudice to the meaning. Moreover, it is argued that the use of Spritz also increases the speed of reading ordinary texts.

Probably, now you can expect the appearance of devices with tiny screens that will compete with traditional e-books. I highly recommend to read the demo on the site.
