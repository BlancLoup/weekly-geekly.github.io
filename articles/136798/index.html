Selectel Tour: DC Berzarina

DC Berzarina - our only data center in Moscow. Fire fighting systems


In contrast to the DC at Flower, only support and security engineers are on duty at Berzarin. So automatic fire extinguishing system is required. Of course, it is gas (whether someone is morally prepared to have their server extinguished with powder). By the way, in the same photo you can estimate the height of the raised floor - the door to the room is at the height of the "real" floor "and the staircase leads up. The second entrance is used for lifting weights (on the left in the photo), where there are no stairs, but you can use the loader


Through these nozzles gas is fed into the cold corridor. Then it is under its own pressure and with the help of server fans distributed to the equipment and to the hot corridor.

Cooling





This Uniflair TDCV2700A - precision air conditioners. When possible, we use free cooling (cooling of the refrigerant in the chillers with outside air), since the weather in Moscow allows us to do this for most of the year.



Air conditioners are reserved under the scheme N + 1.


Electricity


Yellow pipes lead to gas balloons (behind screens). The air conditioner is primitive, but the reason is simple: the task of bespereboynik - to keep the server until the diesels are running. To do this, the air in the room must be cold, and the air conditioner is turned off (as it is powered by the bespereboynik). As soon as the diesel engines turn on, the air conditioner will turn on, cooling the performance of the bespereboynik.


This is General Electrics SG-CE PurePulse S1, a total of 6 pieces. They are combined into two groups, in each group there are 2 main and one spare (N + 1 reservation).



In total, the DC provides 800 kW of power.

Optics and communication channels


Optical channels are used to communicate with the world. There are two inputs into the data center, both through wells. Of course, different wells. Optics goes to MMTS-9 (96 fibers), the same to M10. The gigabit channel connects Berzarin with our network in St. Petersburg. Aplinki: Retn, TeliaSonera.
