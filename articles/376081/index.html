A laptop for a programmer and a workplace, and an entertainment center, and a window into the world of the network. But this freedom has one drawback: the laptop is not a small thing, you cannot put it in your pocket. You can, of course, carry it over your shoulder in a bag, but this is not very convenient: you have to hold it with your hand, support the slipping belt, and the bag itself turns out to be wide and stiff. In general, a suitcase without a handle. A backpack is quite another matter. Hands are free, the load is distributed evenly and much more comfortable, nothing dangles from the side. Yes, and the laptop is protected from any conflicts is not an example better.
