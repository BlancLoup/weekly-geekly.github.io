Not so long ago, the phrase ‚Äúsmartphone on the atom‚Äù or the Intel Inside logo on its body could be perceived as a joke of medium order. With the advent of the MegaFon SP-A20i, I had the opportunity to compare my expectations from the Medfield platform with reality, in one of its hardware implementations. What came out of it - under the cut.


This paragraph assumes a description of the kit, but, alas, I got the device in a completely naked form. Nothing wrong. But you can immediately write that the pre-release sample participates in the review, and not everything that will be told about this sample can be compared with those specimens that went to the shop windows with one hundred percent certainty. I warned you.

The case of the device is made of several parts, the assembly is made without gaps and cracks in the places of interface elements. The back panel has a soft-touch coating, due to which the smartphone is quite comfortable in the hand. The case material is the most ordinary plastic, not to say that it is of poor quality, but I have seen better. The quality of painting is also not great, in the process of using the paint will peel off, and the process will start from the microUSB socket, due to the connector retainer. When you press the back panel, the case creaks softly, an unpleasant surprise for an expensive device of 2012 release.









Run through the edges of the hull. Below are two cutouts for the speakers, covered with a steel mesh. The solution is quite convenient, the chance that the device will be heard in the depths of the bag rises slightly. There is also a microUSB connector. On the right side of the MegaFon SP-A20i is a volume rocker, a camera release button and a microSim holder. By the way, the SIM card holder is the only metal part of the case that you can touch. Above - a 3.5mm jack for connecting a headset and a second microphone hole. And finally, on the right, the microHDMI connector. The connectors are not supplied with any plugs, so the presence of dust inside did not cause me any surprise.



The back panel is completely monolithic, non-removable. The only holes in it are cutouts for the camera and LED flash.



For sweet left to inspect the front panel. Along the bottom are four touch buttons. On the upper edge of the eye of the front camera, earpiece, light sensor and proximity sensor. In general, the standard set. The panel is made of plastic, which can not be called scratch-resistant, after looking closely, I found several shallow grooves that arose during operation. When tilting you can see the application of a touch coating.

According to general impressions, the casing was made quite decently, the quality of painting the elements and the plastic as a whole can be recorded as flaws. MegaFon SP-A20i comfortably in the hand, the body does not slip out of the palm. The hardware buttons are arranged correctly, but due to the fact that they practically do not protrude from the surface of the case and have a rather tight course, it is inconvenient to use them. You have to either press them with your fingernail, or make a tangible effort, so that they work.

In general, the volume of the speakers is enough for everyday tasks. Vibrating a medium power, lying in a pocket of clothes, the smartphone is quite capable of attracting attention. By the battery life of the MegaFon SP-A20i I was pleasantly surprised.
