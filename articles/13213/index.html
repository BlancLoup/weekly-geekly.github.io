GoGo.Ru is now looking for video on LiveInternet and MyVi

The GoGo.ru search service developed by Mail.Ru now searches the contents of such Runet video hosting sites as LiveInternet.ru and MyVi . At the moment, GoGo indexes about 835,000 videos. LiveInternet has increased this number by 81 thousand, and Mewie has increased by 15 thousand.

Now GoGo indexes videos on 9 resources: Video@Mail.Ru , RuTube , Loadup , Rambler Vision , Video.I.UA. , LiveInternet and MUVI, as well as on the Russian part of the YouTube and Metacafe content.

The search is carried out in accordance with the original algorithm that allows you to collect information about the video, including its name, tags, description and previews from several web pages.

According to the CEO of Mail.Ru, Dmitry Grishin, video search is a very promising demand and at the same time a niche in the domestic Internet search market that is almost completely uncovered. The volume of user-generated video content on the Russian Internet is growing at a staggering rate, only on Videos@Mail.Ru every day an average of 6,000 new videos appear. To ignore this growth would be, at least, short-sighted. So at the moment, the development of this particular type within the GoGo project is a priority for Mail.RU.
