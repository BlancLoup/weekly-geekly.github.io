Scribblenauts, or why I bought a Nintendo DS

Scribblenauts

Formally, this is a quest - we are wandering, we solve some problems (cross the river, press a button, remove the kitten from the tree). The main difference from other quests, there is no inventory, instead a magic notebook. If you write a word in a notebook, you will get this item. In theory, there are almost no restrictions.
Need a machine gun? We take the machine gun. Need an elephant? Take the elephant. Need Cthulhu? We take Cthulhu. Only he is angry. :)
Accordingly, any task has a lot of solutions. A kitten from a tree can be pulled off with a rope, knocked down with a stone, substitute a ladder, lure it with a mouse, cut down a tree, send a beaver or a pyromaniac into the tree ...

Where to get games

For the selection of games I liked the site www.espalnds.com

For the game, you can see screenshots (the absence of which many sites sin) - click on the words "Post oficial del juego" - send it to the game page on the forum, where there will be several screenshots, a video recording of the gameplay, there may also be information about the performance on different flash drives.
