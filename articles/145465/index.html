The first Russian applications for Windows 8 in the Windows Store



Friends, as you probably know (and, I hope, have already tried), last week Windows 8 Release Preview was released , with which the Windows Store application store was updated, in which many interesting applications appeared.

In this post I want to draw your attention to the first applications from Russia.



First of all, these are two popular applications available from Consumer Preview, now they have been updated and available in RP:

Cut the rope



I think this popular game from ZeptoLab does not need a special introduction, I note only that this is one of the most popular applications in the Windows Store.

News360



News360 is a popular service for selecting and filtering news from around the world on topics of interest to you. By the way, News360 is a member of BizSpark One .

===
New applications available on Windows 8 Release Preview:

Gismeteo



The client for the popular weather forecast service of the same name is Gismeteo.ru . Now the weather outside the window right on your start screen!

Ivi



If you want to watch any movie or series, then this application is exactly what you need. This is the official client for the eponymous video service with licensed content - ivi.ru. Largest online cinema on your couch :)

Film Search



If you are thinking of going to the cinema or want to know the details and reviews about this or that film, then you should definitely put the official client for the site kinopoisk.ru , developed by Actis Wunderman . All announcements and reviews right under your fingers.

MyEnglish



MyEnglish is a great app to learn English and test your knowledge. Spend with the application 10-15 minutes a day - and very soon your level of English will increase markedly.

Doodle god



Feel yourself ... um ... Doodle-god :) Blockbuster from JoyBits now on Windows 8! Cross different items to get new ones.

Treasures of Montezuma 3



The popular game from the company Alawar came to Windows 8. Classics of the genre in a colorful design and with the support of fingers :)
