Gottfried Svartholm, who was arrested earlier, will be extradited to Sweden . Already, documents are being prepared for sending home to the 27-year-old co-founder of The Pirate Bay.



2 years ago, due to illness, he did not appear on appeal in the case of The Pirate Bay. At this time, Svartholm was hospitalized in a hospital in Cambodia.
