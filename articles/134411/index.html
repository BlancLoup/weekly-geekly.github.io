Competition for the best phrase for a T-shirt from a start-up KidsReview.ru. Prize - iPhone 4

Online project KidsReview.ru (Magazine, Poster, Handbook for families with children) launched a mobile application for iOs and Android phones . To attract and retain the interest of the Internet-active audience to the new project and application, we hold a competition for the best phrase for a t-shirt , a phrase about KidsReview.ru The main prize is iPhone 4. Competition phrases are accepted until December 18, open voting will be held from December 20 to December 27, the winner of the competition will be announced on December 29, 2011.

The competition takes place in two stages:
1. To participate in the competition, each phrase must be posted on the author's personal page on the Internet and contain an active link to KidsReview.ru. The link to the participant‚Äôs page containing the text for the T-shirt should be sent to the editor via a special form on the site .

2. The editors of KidsReview.ru will select the 10 most successful, in our opinion, phrases that will take part in the second round - the open voting. The main prize - iPhone 4 - will go to the participant for whose phrase the largest number of users registered through any of the three social networks will vote (vkontakte, facebook, twitter).

Our goals:
1. Entertain and provide a field for rampant imagination of our readers.
2. Get fun and witty phrases for T-shirts that are not ashamed to wear and give to readers and partners
3. Earn inbound links
4. Attract additional traffic to the site at the voting stage.
