Facebook Hacker Cup 2013 - The Qualification Round is taking place right now

Greetings, I did not see the announcement as last year - but I think many will be interested.
The Facebook Hacker Cup 2013 has already started and The Qualification Round is right now - 60 hours out of 72 two left to solve three tasks.

Everything is simple - read the conditions of the problem, write the program that decides, download the file with the test data (once) and you have 6 minutes to fill in the result of the work of your program and the program itself.

Pass qualifying rounds, and in the composition of the 25 winners participate in the finals in the office of Facebook.

The tasks look simple, so this round can be interesting for those who are just starting to program.
