Megaphone Moscow: preferential intra-network roaming!

Finally, the subscribers of Moscow Megaphone waited for preferential on-net roaming!
The site has no official information yet, but the official forum has already posted descriptions of options and their cost. The forum administration confirms that these options will be activated from today.


And so, the package "Roaming at home"
Roaming at Home Package:
connection - 30
monthly fee - 1 ruble per day
The package sets the cost of outgoing and incoming calls and SMS made in intranet roaming (only the Central region), at the level of the cost of calls to the home tariff plan.
The package operates in the Central region in the following regions: Bryansk, Vladimir, Ivanovo, Kaluga, Kursk, Kostroma, Nizhny Novgorod, Oryol, Ryazan, Smolensk, Tver, Tula and Yaroslavl.
The package is saved when changing the tariff plan.
Package connection is made through the Service Guide or employee.
