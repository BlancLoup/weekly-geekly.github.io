A portion of fresh photos and videos for those whom I was able to interest in his clever beginnings. The project is taking shape and is already implemented in a test gland, which soon (hopefully) will come to me from a distant country of Ukraine.

What is now?


Developed and embodied in the gland early developer kit. This is actually a dimmer, as in the photo above (by the way - clickable) and an additional shawl on the basis of the atmega with the implemented software dimming algorithm, which I myself have to transfer to the NRF24LE1.

The current dimensions of the dimmer board are 50x50 mm. The release board will not be different in size, unless it is possible to cut corners a little. The total height of all elements of the dimmer will consume about 2 cm, which is located on the border of the free space behind the switch.
