Windows Phone - what to buy (today)

I wanted to buy a phone with Windows Phone, try it in real life, and not twist it in my hands at exhibitions and in stores. My personal requirements:
1) screen up to 4 "(otherwise it does not fit in the hand)
2) memory of 16 gigabytes (although eight will come down on the phone for "trying")
3) replaceable battery (there are few communicators now)
4) normal sim card (not micro - I have several devices, between which sim cards are roaming)
