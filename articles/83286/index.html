Multiplatform emulators




Gaming consoles, the main purpose of which is to launch games of other consoles - NES, SNES, SMD, NEOGEO, GBA ... Plus additional features - a media player (music, video, images), reading books, radio. The most famous representatives - Dingoo A-320, GP2X Wiz.

Price: on average - around $ 100
Prevalence: small, you can buy in Chinese online stores
The main genres of games: depending on the emulated platform
Advantages: multi-function device, hundreds of great games, portable media player, nostalgia, again
Disadvantages: low prevalence in Russia, which is why the local price is twice as high as the Chinese one, if you can find it.

Gameboy



"Modern" model - GameBoy Advance SP (clamshell). Also met: GameBoy, GameBoy Color, GameBoy Advance, GameBoy Micro. The predecessor of the Nintendo DS, is still quite popular with "casual". In the first place - due to the low price of the device and games, ease of use. By and large - a pocket version of Super Nintendo. Of the additional features - multiplayer (cable and wireless, reader and media player - when sticking specially trained cartridges)

Price: around 2500-3000, games, on average, 200-300 rubles.
Prevalence: High
The main genres of games: almost everything, from platformers to RPGs and strategies.
Advantages: inexpensive, compact, long enough life from batteries
Disadvantages: like all inexpensive devices in this review, outdated





Nintendo ds




Somehow already wrote something like a review. The most popular portable console in the world (but not in Russia;)), a huge number of games, many of which use additional features of the device in the game process - a touchscreen, a microphone, a camera ... there is also a wifi that allows you to play multiplayer games (including and via the Internet). The hardware is outdated (Nintendo promises to announce a replacement soon), but cosmetic updates are coming out. The most popular (and most often commercially available) version is NDS Lite, which is now slowly being replaced by NDSi (NDSi XL). But occasionally there is also the original NDS.
Differences:
Nintendo DS - the original version.
Nintendo DS Lite - reduced size, added the ability to adjust the backlight level, slightly modified ergonomics.
Nintendo DSi - increased screen size, replacing the GBA slot with an SD card slot, the ability to play AAC files, two cameras with a resolution of 640 * 480 (one outside, the other inside), 133 megahertz processor (against 67 in the previous versions) , 16 megabytes of RAM (there were 4), 256 megabytes SSD (there was no previously), updated wifi (supports WPA / 2).
Nintendo DSi LL / XL - Increasing the diagonal of screens up to 4.2 ", more powerful battery.

Price: NDS / NDS Lite BU 3500-4500, new ones still occur around 6000-7000. DSi BU did not see, the new one costs from 9000, on average - 11000. Games cost 1000-1500.
Prevalence: moderate. The console is much easier to buy than the games for it.
The main genres of games: different. Arcade, puzzles, strategies, quests ...
Advantages: interesting gameplay, reasonable price (if you take BU)
Disadvantages: quite expensive games, plus their low prevalence - because you have to buy a specially trained cartridge and pirate games from the Internet. Although some can and buy ... Scribblenauts ... :)

Sony psp




The main competitor of NDS in the world and almost the winner in Russia. Usually when they talk about a portable game console, they mean the PSP. Excellent screen, powerful hardware, comfortable ergonomics, Wi-Fi, support for memory cards. Can be used as a reader, browser, media player. Emulators of old consoles, including the official emulator of the first Playstation.
There are four versions:
PSP 100x - the first models. Was available infrared.
PSP 200x (Slim) - became a little less, easier. The infrared port was gone, the RAM was added, the opportunity to connect to the TV appeared.
PSP 300x (Bright) - a microphone has appeared, a new screen is brighter, more anti-reflective.
PSP Go - slider. Removed miniUSB, removed UMD disk support - you cannot play old licensed games. 16 gigabytes of internal memory, Bluetooth, new memory card format (M2). The ability (or rather the need) to buy games over the Internet (PSN). Smaller sizes.

Price: Used - around 5000-6000, new - from 8000 to 11000 (for PSP Go)
Prevalence: high, the price on the secondary market is high - that is, it is in demand.
The main game genres: arcade, action, racing ... Although there are fighting games, RPGs, and strategies.
Advantages: high prevalence, powerful hardware, excellent graphics, great functionality
Disadvantages: a rather large size, with the release of PSP Go, the platform has almost completely changed - old games and accessories do not fit it. Fun with hacked firmware - if you do not want to be limited to licensed games, you will have to carefully watch what you buy.

Apple Ipod Touch (and Iphone)


Ipod Touch - initially positioned as a media player, but in the end it turned out something like a PDA. A fairly large set of software, one of the best mobile browsers, a good screen, a good speed.
There are a lot of games. Distributed through the online store AppStore. They are not very expensive (on average - $ 5-10), most games have free demo versions. Ported many games from other consoles - NDS and PSP, plus there are emulators of some platforms (NES, SNES ...).
Game control - touchscreen (multitouch) plus accelerometer. It is inconvenient to play part of the games - the ‚Äújoystick‚Äù is drawn on the screen, the fingers are closed during the game. And on the other hand, on the contrary, there are no problems, even despite the same ‚Äújoystick‚Äù on the screen.
In general, it was a very decent gaming platform. According to the schedule - better than NDS, but worse than the PSP. For ease of management - depends on the game. For example, if you take GTA: Chinatown Wars, which appeared on all three platforms, then I would not say where the more convenient control is on NDS or on ipod. But the PSP with this game is an obvious outsider.

Everything said about ipod also applies to iphone.

There are several options for ipod touch and iphone.
Iphone (2G) - original, 4, 8, 16 GB
Iphone 3G - 2G plus support for 3G and GPS, 8 and 16GB
Iphone 3Gs - more powerful CPU, GPU, double the size of RAM. 16 and 32 GB

Ipod Touch 1G - the original version. 8, 16, 32 gigabytes.
Ipod Touch 2G - differs from the first generation in the presence of bluetooth, which was activated when updating the IphoneOS 3.0, 8, 16, 32 gigabytes.
IPod Touch 3G - latest version. 8, 32, 64 gigabytes.
Touch 3G 8GB is, in fact, 2G with iphoneOS 3.0 originally installed
Touch 3G 32 and 64 is really a new platform, more memory, a more powerful processor and a "video card" (similar to the iphone 3Gs).

So if we consider the device as a game console, then the best choice is Ipod Touch 3G 32GB. Because there are already games that slow down on less powerful models. Even seemingly such garbage as Plants and Zombies.

Ipod Touch: Used - 6000-7000, the new one starts from 8500 for 3G 8Gb, 10,000 - 3G 32Gb.
Iphone:
Bootstock of the first generations - approximately 10,000, 3G - from 16,000;
New - 3G somewhere from 20,000, 3Gs - from 30,000
Prevalence: ipod touch - not very, iphone - high
The main genres of games: puzzles, puzzles, arcades. But there are strategies and 3D-action ...
Advantages: wow factor - enjoy nicely, a large number of games, low price of games, ease of purchase (all you need is internet access and a bank card)
Disadvantages: two main disadvantages.
The first is that the back side is very easily scratched (if metallic). For a couple of weeks of wearing without a cover, it seems that it was covered with sandpaper. It is treated by the purchase of a cover.
The second disadvantage is itunes. The disadvantage is huge and almost incurable. Programs and games, of course, can be downloaded via the Internet. But work with a computer, synchronization, downloading music and video (as well as installing pirated software) - only through itunes. What is more, the disadvantage is rather ideological, not technical ...
