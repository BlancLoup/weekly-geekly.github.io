The 80/20 principle. Beginner's Guide

Today we bring to your attention a translation of the article A Beginner's Guide To The 80/20 Principle about the principle of operation and use of the 80/20 rule, in which we hope readers will find useful tools for managing their performance.


Briefly about 80/20.
‚ÄúYou don't need to do everything. You only need to do what gives the result. ‚Äù

You may find this wrong, or take advantage of it. If you are reading this, you are one of those smart people who would rather choose to curb the energy of this rule than fight it.

Here you will learn how to optimize everything you do from the very beginning.

How does 80/20 work
In most cases, about 80% of the results come from 20% of the causes. These numbers can change - sometimes it is 70/30 and sometimes 90/10. But whatever the ratio, the point is that a small number of causes have a disproportionately large effect on the result.

In everything, from attracting clients, blog posts, traveling, fitness, relationships - you will find that the 80/20 rule works everywhere.

But let's go back a little. What is this ‚Äú80/20 rule‚Äù and what does it even mean?

The original wording of the rule:
‚Äú20% of investments lead to 80% of the result‚Äù

The rule comes from Richard Koch‚Äôs Rule 80/20. It is a three-dimensional work that definitely deserves to be read.

Curiously, if you open a book of Koch, you will find, on 25 pages, he mentions that you can get the most important information from any book by following the instructions in three steps. As a good student, I followed his instructions ‚Äî I read the beginning and end of the book, glanced casually at the rest of it, and then read the beginning and the end again. Some practical examples of 80/20

TONGUE
Most languages ‚Äã‚Äãhave about a million words (if not more)

Most people use only a few hundred of these words per day.

If you want to learn a language, you do not need to memorize the entire dictionary. You just need to remember the words that are used most often. Their number does not exceed several thousand words.

You can spend the day and night learning Spanish words - minor aspects of biology for the completeness of the language, but this probably will not be of great use to you only if you do not urgently need to talk about mitochondria.

If you do not need to discuss mitochondria, you can learn the basics much faster. This someone like Benny manages to learn a language in 3 months. He does not focus on his possession in perfection, he just focuses on speaking and is not afraid to look silly.

DIET
If you change 20% of your diet, you will see absolutely huge results, especially at first, when you just started to improve your diet. If you follow the Paleootic diet and exclude from the diet of grain and dairy, you do not need to worry about whether or not the use of legumes paleo rule. I have talked about this in several places, but the exclusion of grain and dairy products is 80% of what you need. All that will be after - this is the usual refinement.

If you are upset and do not see the result when it comes to weight loss, stop thinking that you need to completely rebuild everything. Just change a few things that will make a big difference, and use this difference as an impulse to make big changes in the future.

WHY YOU REALLY FEEL TO USE 80 \ 20
I was brought up to have hardworking midwestern thinking. Work hard and do not stop until the work is done. Do exactly that every time.

This is great advice. He creates integrity and a sense of pride in his work. This is powerful advice that most people can use in their lives and work.

It can also be a waste of time if you thoughtlessly apply it in all directions and to everything you do.

Here is the truth: you should not be perfect. Your work does not have to be perfect. In fact, it will often not be perfect. So instead of chasing the unachievable standards of excellence, focus on getting a ‚Äúgood enough‚Äù result to move on to the next step.

If you are not quite sure how you feel about this, your inner perfectionist probably shouts ‚Äúfoul !!!!‚Äù.

USE 80/20 IN ACTION
If you're tired of the theory, here are some practical things you can do to implement 80/20 today.

BUDGET
Look at your budget. Use Mint to analyze your purchases. Which categories cause the most expenses? Find out which of the big expenses can be cut. Choose something like a cable. Turning off the cable will save you about $ 100 a month, probably as much as you would save on something small that you had all this time, such as your daily cup of coffee ‚Äî and give you time to create something worthwhile. the same time. Double win.

INCOME
Analyze your various sources of income and effort spent on each. There is a chance that you have one milk cow or a larger income stream that takes a minimum of work / is the most fun / is the easiest to do. Get rid of other responsibilities that do not generate the return on investment to the same extent and focus on those flows that give disproportionate results (This is exactly what I did when I refused low-paying client work).

DIET
Do not make a major overhaul of your diet. Do not go to the ‚ÄúPurge‚Äù and do not spend hundreds of dollars on the preparation of juice. Just give up cereals and dairy products for four weeks. See what happens.

FITNESS
Do not buy a gym membership. Do not spend three hours a day on the treadmill. Spend 20 minutes a day doing a 4-week program, or an 8-week program with dead weight and sprints. Combine all this with your new diet and see what happens.

TONGUE
Stop trying to memorize vocabulary and learn 200 words in another language. Take a trip to another country for a week and just chat with people. You will see that you have become more fluent in the language these days, unlike your friends, who spend months face in a book.

SITES AND MARKETING
Instead of focusing on every marketing channel you can imagine, look at your statistics for the past 90 days and find out where most of the traffic comes from. Double bid for these traffic sources and forget about all the others. Do this for 90 days and see what happens.
