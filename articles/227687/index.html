How to take over the world, proving that P = NP

Hypothetically suppose that you managed to prove the equality P = NP. What now needs to be done to gain domination over the whole world?

Firstly, there is a million-dollar Millennium Prize. But this is not the best possible solution, because by accepting this award you need to publish proof of your discovery, which robs you of the trump card too soon.

Instead, you can write an application that uses your proof. Consider what follows from the equality P = NP. The main class of tasks for which a certain algorithm can give an answer in polynomial time is R. In some problems, the method of quickly obtaining an answer is unknown, but the answer can be checked in polynomial time; these problems are NP-related. The equality P = NP may mean that problems, the solution of which was previously considered very difficult, are now solved in polynomial time.

Unfortunately, having only one proof without considerations on how to convert it into a working algorithm, it will be very problematic to capture the world.

The greatest value of P = NP lies in the fields of mathematics, cryptography, artificial intelligence, and even biology.

Starting with cryptography, you can now break open symmetric algorithms. Now you can transfer money from their accounts to yours. It is better to use an account in an offshore, so as not to attract the attention of special services, which these transfers are very interested.
