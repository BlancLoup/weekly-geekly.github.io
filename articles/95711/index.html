Apple iPhone 4 officially presented

The iPhone 4 was officially unveiled at the Apple conference today. IPhone 4 is Smarter, Better, Faster, said Steve from the stage and was right. The appearance of the novelty did not become a surprise for us - it is exactly the same as in the previously leaked prototypes .



The novelty is running System-On-Chip Apple A4, which is installed on the Apple iPad. The iPhone 4 boasts a powerful ARM processor of the Cortex A8 architecture and 1 GHz frequency, a Power SGX video accelerator and 256 MB RAM, WiFi 802.11n, as well as an excellent 3.5 "Retina Display display capable of displaying 326 pixels per inch.
