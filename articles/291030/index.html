The share of Russian online retailers using payment aggregators has grown to almost 50%

The company "Yandex" conducted a study of Russian online retailing. It turned out that 48% of online stores use ready-made online solutions for accepting payments in several ways: card, wallet, mobile payments.

86 thousand sites were analyzed. The most popular payment aggregators are Robokassa (it is used by 21% of online stores), Yandex.Cassa (19%) and Interkassa (5%).

In addition, many online retailers accept payments from e-wallets: Yandex.Money (47%), Webmoney (44%), Qiwi (39%), PayPal (8%) and RBK Money (3%).

46% of sites provide only one method of payment, 3% - two methods, 51% - more than three.
