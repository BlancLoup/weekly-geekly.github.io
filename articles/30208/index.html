Cheoptics 360 - 3D Projector

Peter Simonsen, with a team of engineers from Denmark, developed a new three-dimensional image technology, Cheoptics360, which allows you to project three-dimensional objects or video in the air. Cheoptics demonstration prototypes resemble holographic projectors from science fiction films.

Cheoptics360 uses four 3D-projectors, the projected images are assembled into one inside a transparent pyramid prism that displays the video hanging in the air.

The prototype can show computer animation and movies, and scale them in size from one and a half to thirty meters.
