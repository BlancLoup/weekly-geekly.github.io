The keyboard layout is familiar enough, albeit with nuances - 119 buttons, 15 of which are additional.

Just to the right, there are 6 buttons to control the music player: Play / Pause, Next, Prev, Vol +, Vol- and Mute. If it is still convenient to use the first three, then these 6 buttons, due to the fact that they almost do not protrude from the relief, I had almost no attention. However, they work :)



The F1-F12 buttons are located on the top row, on which functions painted by pressing the Fn key are marked with orange paint. LCD Display


At the top of the noticeably protruding plastic part there is a black and white (more precisely, black and gray) display, on which various information can be displayed. Below it are two buttons ("and") that can switch the functionality of the screen:

2. Ambient temperature;
3. Information from the player;
4. Keystroke counter;
5. Calculator;

All this is configured in the SetPoint program from the kit. Item 1 - fully reassigned.
