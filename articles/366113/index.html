Cognitive Technologies together with KAMAZ will develop an unmanned truck



Cognitive Technologies and KamAZ announced the launch of a project to build an unmanned truck. The total cost of the project is estimated at 390 million rubles - the funds will be needed to develop a production sample of the car. The project is allocated 300 million rubles by the Ministry of Education and Science of Russia. Another 90 million rubles allocated by the company "KAMAZ". The project is long-term - the appearance of an unmanned truck is planned only by 2020 (commercial sample).

As for the company Cognitive Technologies , it is the winner of the competition held earlier Ministry of Education. The competition was held in the framework of the federal target program "Research and development in priority areas of development of the scientific and technological complex of Russia for 2014-2020." Cognitive Technologies in the project acts as a developer of the autonomous vehicle control system, and KamAZ will finalize the truck for such a system. Cognitive Technologies is a highly respected company. The basis of the development team of the company Cognitive Technologies was formed in 1968 under the leadership of V. L. Arlazarov.
