PRO account on free-lance.ru: return unused funds


Recent changes on free-lance.ru have created a lot of discontent among freelancers.
Many voiced their intention to go to other exchanges. Quite often the question was ‚ÄúHow can I get a refund for a PRO account?‚Äù
The rules of the site free-lance.ru state that the funds credited to the account are not returned.

The process is quite simple and intuitive, but, just in case, I describe in detail. Suddenly useful to the doubters and lazy.


Step one. Application for return of unused funds
