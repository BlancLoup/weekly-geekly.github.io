Yahoo! closes part of projects and changes strategy

Yahoo! plans to close several of his projects. One of them is Yahoo Pipes . It allows you to create applications that combine news feeds, web pages and other services. Already on August 30, its functionality will be disabled, and on September 30, Yahoo Pipes will be permanently closed.

In addition, the company will close the map service - Yahoo Maps . True, access to the maps will remain in the search engine Yahoo! and in Flickr . By the end of June, it will cease to exist as a separate service. Together with the maps, GeoPlanet and PlaceSpotter services will leave the horizon.
