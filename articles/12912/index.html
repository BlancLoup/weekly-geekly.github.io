Conceptual clock for the blind

Swatch has developed a concept clock for the blind. This gadget, called Sens Time By Touch, is not only convenient and functional, but also uniquely beautiful in appearance and can serve as an element of decoration on the desktop, even if it is not limited in human capabilities. For example, as an alarm clock.

But, of course, Sens are designed for people with vision problems. The watch is marked with Braille on the outer surface, which serves as the main tool for setting and determining the time. The device consists of three concentric circles: two internal from ceramics and one external from plastic. The time is determined by the distance of the twelve hour marker from the second ceramic ring. The plastic ring is used to set the alarm timer.

The advantages of this device is that the developer took care not only of its functionality. Sens Time by Touch are pleasant and to the touch and outwardly no less elegant.
