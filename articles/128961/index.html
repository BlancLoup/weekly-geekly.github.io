Starts the recruitment of students to the school programmers HeadHunter

The company HeadHunter from September 12, opens the second set of school programmers . The start of training is December 1, the training period is 6 months, leading programmers of the company and invited experts teach, a stipend is paid, the best graduates will get a job in the company.


With the help of the school of programmers, we are looking for talented young developers and grow them for ourselves. For the last year students, our school is an excellent opportunity to prove oneself in one of the largest companies of the Runet and to gain practical knowledge about the development of high-load systems. Last year we received more than a thousand applications for school, 100 people coped with the test task, of which we selected six of the best after an interview, three of whom received a job in the company. This year the set is doubled, to 12 places.

For six months, 12 young programmers, who will be selected on the basis of the results of the test assignment and interview, will learn modern approaches to building high-load systems and the latest software development technologies. In parallel, students of the school will solve the working tasks of the hh.ru site, gaining real industrial programming experience. Leading programmers of HeadHunter and invited experts will give lectures and share practical development experience. Study will consist of one lesson per week in the Moscow office of the company and independent work. Education is free, a monthly stipend of 15,000 rubles is paid. Acceptance of applications for admission is conducted on the school website from September 12 to November 1.
