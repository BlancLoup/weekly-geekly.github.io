Preview of HTC smartphones from the new One family

Sunday evening was marked by a bright presentation of the new line of smartphones from HTC called One . In addition to the title, the three gadgets One X , One S and One V are combined with an excellent camera and support for Beats Audio . Let's look at two of the new products:



Let us see what is the difference between the devices among themselves. The older brother in this family is HTC One X. It is equipped with a 4-core 1.5 GHz Nvidia Tegra 3 processor (the LTE version has a Qualcomm chip), a 4.7-inch HD screen and two cameras (the main 8 MP). All this is hidden inside the polycarbonate case. (Video from the third smartphone line in the continuation).

The second coolest smartphone is called HTC One S. It is more compact, and is made in the unibody form factor from anodized aluminum (very pleasant to the touch). Inside, the Qualcomm dual-core processor works, the screen has a 4.3-inch diagonal and a resolution of 960x540, and the camera set is the same as that of the X model.



The third smartphone of the HTC One V line was endowed with an unusual design (similar to HTC Legend and Hero ), and the stuffing of the gadget was slightly simplified compared to its counterparts: a single-core Qualcomm, 4-inch screen, 5-megapixel camera (but, according to the creators, ).

The main focus of the presentation was on the new camera in smartphones, which, according to first impressions, was indeed a success. This also applies to the hardware component ( aperture F / 2.0 ) and software (updated user-friendly interface, quick shooting, photo mode inside the video).

All new items work on Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich , on top of which is installed the shell HTC Sense 4 . As a result, the interface was very nice and responsive. Also, the device has a lot of updated apps and widgets from HTC . Sales of all three smartphones in Russia should begin as early as April, prices are still being specified.
