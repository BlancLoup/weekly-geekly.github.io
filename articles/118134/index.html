Application Competition - a chance to get WP7

April 15, in preparation for the DevCon conference, a competition of mobile applications started on Windows Phone 7.

Winners in four nominations will receive a free ticket to participate with accommodation at the DevCon conference, as well as the opportunity to win phones on Windows Phone 7 !

To participate in the competition, you need to develop an application and place it either in the Marketplace itself, or as a video on YouTube.

The results of the competition will be announced on May 20. Detailed information is available on the official page of the competition.
