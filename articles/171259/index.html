Characteristics and debriefing

Apple TV 3-gen runs on a dual-core A5 processor (32 nm), but one of its cores is software disabled. For what it is done, I did not find out, but I can say with confidence that even one core copes with the playback of Full HD video. Graphics chip: PowerVR SGX 543MP2.


(Apple A5 on iPhone 4S and Apple TV 3)

Note: iPhone 4S and iPad 2 (released before 2012) have Apple A5 installed using 45nm technology, which allows the device to discharge the battery 15% faster than the same 32nm chip.

In addition, Apple TV is set to 512 MB of RAM and 8 GB of internal memory, which can not be used. Internal memory is intended only for system processes and caching.

AirPlay, iTunes Match, iCloud

If you still do not know what AirPlay is, then I will pleasantly surprise you. This function in real-time mode allows you to stream an image from an iPhone, iPod touch, iPad, Mac or PC to a TV or monitor ... not without the help of Apple TV, of course. The most pleasant thing here is that this technology is brought to mind and the image does not slow down a bit. Here another question may arise: what is it for? Firstly, Mirroring allows you to play games with the iPhone on the big screen, and this is a great pleasure. Secondly, you can listen to music or watch movies.
