Using a mouse and keyboard on consoles is cheating?


Many players believe that XIM Apex is an undetectable cheat device that corrupts video games. But for people with impaired motility, this is the only way to play.

The device, called XIM Apex, costs $ 100 and looks like a USB stick and hub, allowing you to connect my mouse and keyboard from the PC to the Xbox One (or PlayStation 4), while my opponents and partners are likely to play with regular Xbox One controllers.

The most difficult decision
