Free webinar "Creating a business without investment: Studio 3D-modeling and robotics." Lead by Anatoly Garmashov

This Saturday at 18-45, a free 3D webinar on 3D modeling will be held. The webinar will be run by a 3D printing specialist who organized a school course in Yekaterinburg on teaching 3D modeling, creating objects and printing on 3D printers to children, Anatoly Garmashov.



In this school, children from the age of 11 have been studying for more than half a year. They are taught to create simple things first: cup, cube. And gradually transferred to more complex. Many of the children dream of doing different things with their own hands, from a smartphone to a car and a quadcopter. Parents are very satisfied with the work of elective. Through learning, children develop engineering thought, spatial imagination and skills to work with complex devices that they will need very much in the near future. The future, where as a result of the third industrial revolution, production becomes small-scale, additive and networked.



Anatoly Garmashov, together with his colleagues, teaches work on 3D printing and 3D modeling not only of children, but also of students and adults. This free intro webinar is part of the course and training system. And therefore it will be useful for adults. And those who want to do 3D printing themselves, and those who want to give their children for training.
