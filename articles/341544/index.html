Fiztekh launches online courses in higher mathematics to prepare for admission to the magistracy



The MIPT online courses in higher mathematics are intended for independent theoretical and practical preparation for entering the magistracy of the Physics and Technology Institute and other leading technical universities.

  • Already open training courses "Mathematical analysis - 1", "Analytical geometry and linear algebra";
  • On December 1, the courses "Mathematical Analysis - 2", "Differential Equations" and "Theory of Probability" will be available;
  • December 31 will open the course "Theory of Functions of a Complex Variable" and "Equations of Mathematical Physics."

Each of the 7 sections can be held separately. Courses prepared by leading teachers of MIPT, constantly participating in the educational process of students. Head of the Higher Mathematics Department of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Grigory Ivanov, notes that this form of education will make it possible to prepare for the new educational level more effectively: As a rule, students have to urgently repeat everything they learned during their undergraduate studies in order to prepare for the entrance exams and go to the next level of education. Our online courses can help not only refresh the knowledge of the university course of mathematics, but also bring them to a deeper level, thereby preparing for further study.
