NeXTSTEP


NeXTSTEP was a promising UNIX-like operating system, released in 1988 and formally completed its history in 1996. NeXTSTEP is best known as the ancestor of the somewhat famous Mac OS X.

NeXTSTEP had a very distinctive appearance. So characteristic that it served as the basis for a half-port project, called GNUStep, and created in order to bring the look of a long-forgotten operating system to Linux.
