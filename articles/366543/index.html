Officially presented Nexus 5X and Nexus 6P

Today, Google has officially unveiled its new smartphones - Nexus 5X and Nexus 6P.

The first, produced by LG, became the budgetary successor of the Nexus 5 model of 2013, the second, produced by Huawei, a phablet with a very high-speed and modern filling, is the heir of the last year‚Äôs Nexus 6, which was not as popular as the Nexus 5.





Nexus 5X - the fruit of cooperation with LG, with a screen size slightly larger than its predecessor - 5.2 inches, with the same resolution of 1920 * 1080 pixels, equipped with a six-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 808 processor with a clock frequency of 2 GHz, 2 GB of RAM, as well as 16 or 32 GB of internal memory. Equipped with a 12.3 megapixel main camera and a 5 megapixel front camera. The device is equipped with a Nexus Imprint fingerprint scanner and a USB Type-C port - according to the manufacturer, a 10-minute charge should be enough for 4 hours of work. Battery - capacity of 2700 mAh. The device can be made in three colors - white, black, and blue. Video - Adreno 418 GPU, the size of the phone - 147.0 x 72.6 x 7.9 mm, weight - 136 grams.

As for the Nexus 6P, this smartphone has become the first smartphone from the corporation of good, made in a metal case. The manufacturer has become Huawei, the phablet is completed with a 5.7-inch display with a resolution of 2560 * 1440 pixels, covered with a protective glass Corning Gorilla Glass. Inside - a Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 v2.1 processor and 3 GB of RAM. The battery capacity is 3450 mAh, the main camera is also 12.3 megapixels, the front one is 8 megapixels. There is a fingerprint scanner Nexus Imprint and a USB Type-C port. The color scheme is either white or black. There is also NFC. Video - Adreno 430 GPU. Dimensions of the novelty - 159.3 x 77.8 x 7.3 mm, weight - 178 grams.



Both smartphones have the latest version of Android onboard - 6.0 Marshmallow.

Pre-order price in Google Store today for several countries - USA, UK, Ireland and Japan for $ 379 for Nexus 5X and $ 499 for Nexus 6P.
