The automatic system for protection against tayposkvottingu

We all came across this. After all, it was not once that you entered the address of a site, but you made a small mistake in its typing, and then instead of the expected page you got a completely different one?

The so-called cybersquatters make big money on erroneously and incorrectly entered addresses. Owners of such domains, representing the names of addresses of well-known brands written with errors, earn money by displaying advertisements or selling these domains.

CitizenHawk has offered its solution and some owners of famous brands have already subscribed to it. The company today officially announced the launch of its technology TypoSquasher , which has already been used for several months by a number of brand owners during the beta test period.

TypoSquasher is an automated system that checks domains for "tayposkvottinga", and then reports about fraudulent activity to the domain owner.

The regular version of TypoSquasher costs $ 995 / month. The product does not require any special skills and is easy to use.

By the way, now about 20% of manually typed addresses are written with errors.
