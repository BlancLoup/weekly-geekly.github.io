Liberty City Tickets Now Available

Rockstar Games, the developer of the Grand Theft Auto series of games and other equally ambiguous, and therefore iconic, computer entertaining games, announced the launch of pre-orders for GTA IV in all more or less developed countries of the world. Chilling (in places) and forcing a new way to rethink the place of a person in a soulless urban environment, the action thriller will appear on the shelves already on April 29, including in Russia. The game will be released simultaneously for two platforms - Sony Playstation 3 and Microsoft XBox 360, but for the latter - in the augmented and extended versions.

Everyone who ordered the game in advance will receive a DVD with bonus materials, a set of postcards with views of Liberty City (a city similar to New York, location of the IVth part of the game) and a discount coupon for buying a second copy. XBox owners will also get a baseball cap with the console logo. It is possible to place an order in the networks of M.Video, Soyuz, Hit-Zone, White Wind DIGITAL, Mood, GamePark, ION, 1C Interest, StartMaster and " WORLD".

It should be noted that the prices for the licensed edition of the game will significantly bite. An absentee blockbuster does not fall under any appetite reduction programs for game title makers in Russia. It can be understood by reason, but the soul, the soul will be deeply wounded by the price tag ...
