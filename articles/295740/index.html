Huge visualization allows you to find the best for 1 minute

Submit a task. In two minutes, you need to get acquainted with a large group of people, at once, to understand and evaluate who is the leader in this group, who is the strongest in sports, who is the smartest, who has read the most books, and generally identify several key figures. You can certainly study the reports and specifications, but we found a better way, and yes it will take less than two minutes.



Of course this is a visualization. We just decided to submit photos of all the right people on one page, while the size of the photo depends on the user's rank.
