CyberPlat has closed acceptance of credit cards from abroad

Faced the problem of paying for hosting. At the end of December.

My usual CyberPlat has categorically stopped accepting a couple of my cards. Cards are registered in different countries, payments from them are made and I was sure that I did not have the problem.

1. Firm - not our problem - deal with CyberPlat
2. CyberPlat - no answer to e-mal, Skype hotline - no answer. A phone call - everything is fine with us, the card issuer does not give the go-ahead for payment.
3. Card Issuer - yes, we see your previous payments in CyberPlat, we see your yesterday's payment to China, today there were no inquiries about your card.

Attempt to start Yandex.Money ... Cards also do not pass!?

Attempt to start MoneyMail, I enter money. Hooray! Passes by

I pay hosting, and finally, to clear my conscience, I send a letter to the company with a description of the situation and a warning that more clients will come to this rake now.

And I get the answer:

CyberPlat from 28/11/2011, unilaterally set restrictions on
some states for card payments through the payment system. For
Our company is allowed payments for cards issued by banks of the CIS countries:
1. Armenia
2. Belarus
3. Kazakhstan
4. Kyrgyzstan
5. Moldova
6. Russia
7. Tajikistan
8. Turkmenistan
9. Uzbekistan
10. Ukraine
11. Azerbaijan

So, foreign users of CyberPlat - seek backup payment options in advance.
