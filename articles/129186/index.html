Smartphone Huawei Honor officially presented

Most recently, Huawei has introduced a new smartphone Honor U8860. I will try to compile all the information that was found on the Internet.

So, Huawei has officially unveiled the Honor U8860 smartphone in China. It will soon be sold there by the operator China Unicom, however, in other markets, for example, in Europe, the device may also appear, and in the USA, this smartphone is expected to appear under the name Huawei Glory from the operator Cricket. According to some rumors, manufacturers promise a price of $ 300 for this device.
From the author: the information is, of course, highly doubtful, as long as the pre-order for 499 US rubles is only open, but if there really is such a price, then this device will simply not seem to have competitors in this price category

According to the manufacturer, its novelty will be able to work in active mode for up to three days ( which is very good for Android - author's note ) not least due to the presence of a capacious battery (1900 mAh) and support for modern technologies, while Huawei Honor has a small thickness (10.9 mm) with a weight of 140 grams.

Technical characteristics of the device are as follows:

  • Support communications standards: 3G WCDMA / HSPA + 900 / AWS / 2100 MHz; GSM / GPRS / EDGE 850/900/1800/1900 MHz
  • Platform: Android 2.3.5
  • Processor: 1.4 GHz
  • Display: 4-inch, displaying more than 16 million colors, 16: 9, FWVGA resolution
  • Main camera: 8 MP, HDR, with autofocus
  • Front camera: 2 MP
  • FM radio
  • Two microphones
  • Memory: 512 MB RAM, 4 GB ROM, slot for microSD memory cards
  • Communications: WiFi 802.11b / g / n, DLNA, Bluetooth v2.1 (including A2DP profile), Micro USB 2.0, GPS
  • Other: electronic compass, gyroscope, accelerometer, light sensor


The manufacturer intends to start selling Huawei Honor with its own user interface and the provision of 160 GB of "cloud" space in the fourth quarter of this year in China, Russia and the Middle East. Initially, the smartphone will be supplied only in the black gamut, but other colors will soon promise.
