Payler. Summing up 2014!



Dear friends,
We worked day and night, found inspiration in every moment of time, wrote the code at the speed of Schumacher on the Formula 1 track, did the most powerful work and achieved great results. The year was intense and flew by rapidly. It is time to take stock.

Start
After a period of rampant coding, which lasted for almost a year, the final step towards the launch of Payler was the passage of an PCI DSS audit. We officially started on May 1 - on the holiday of spring and labor - and started as a payment gateway, providing only Internet acquiring services. However, we quickly realized that the market required fresh ideas and new tools.

In the first month of work, we had one client and one acquirer bank, and it took 3-4 weeks to connect to the system. But even then, our main priority was to consider the convenience of the client, and therefore continued, as we continue to now, to work on developing new services and searching for new ways to make the client's work easier and more efficient.

results
Today, we have grown from a payment gateway to a multifunctional payment service that is able to comprehensively solve emerging business problems. More than 80 satisfied customers, more than 5,000,000 transactions over the entire period - and this is only the beginning. Today we cooperate with several acquiring banks, and connecting to the system - from preparing documents to the bank to the first combat transaction - takes three to five days, depending on the connected site. During this year, we launched the Billingrad sms-informing service, ITB auto-collection service, Boxberry online delivery service and an online insurance module pilot together with VTB Insurance . The award for our work and diligence was the Runet Award in the nomination "Economy, Business and Investments" .
