JQuery puzzles



Wonderful and very original solution on Jquery.
With this plugin literally one line of code, you can turn any picture into a real puzzle. The weight of the plugin, for its functionality is small - 9 kilobytes.

You ask: Why actually need this plugin? Just play around?
- Think wider! For example, I immediately had the idea to use a small puzzle as a captcha to the site.

jqPuzzle works great in Firefox 1.5+, Internet Explorer 6 + 7, Opera 9 and Safari.

On the website of the author of this plug-in you can find a huge number of examples of working with jqPuzzle, so there will definitely not be any problems with its development.
