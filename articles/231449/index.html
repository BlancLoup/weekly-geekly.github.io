Ecotourism in comfortable conditions

Forget the tent - take the house with you.

Designers of the Spanish company In-Tenta have developed Cabin Drop XL. This modular house in the form of a cylinder the size of a one-piece Moscow Khrushchev: 30 with a small square meters. The structure is made of wood, steel and glass, and is designed in such a way that it can be completely disassembled and taken away with minimal impact on the environment.



The house is on steel legs, which, firstly, minimizes contact with the ground, and secondly, allows you to place the house on an uneven surface.



In the central part of the house there is a room with a bed, a table and chairs. In one of the side parts is a bathroom with toilet, shower and sink.



To increase the area, you can add another module in front of the building.
