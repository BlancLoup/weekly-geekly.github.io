How to make a quality design with a limited budget?

Often when discussing a task with a customer, having learned the cost of the project, the customer disappears, since he had completely different expectations. The customer is looking for another designer, finds, gets a terrible result from him, all lost. Common situation?

I want to tell how you can make high-quality design with a limited budget.

Example. The customer has a budget of 100 thousand for website design and he absolutely needs a super cool designer who would agree to make a design within this budget. Suppose the customer was lucky and he found a professional designer, who estimated the work at 200 thousand rubles. Two scenarios: the first is to bring down the price, the second is the search for another designer.

How does the customer think?


This designer does not suit me, as he is expensive and I am not ready to pay so much. If he pays less, he will work poorly or somehow. What happens next? The customer chooses from the remaining candidates and compares them with each other according to the criterion of price - quality. And chooses within its budget, "acceptable" quality. The customer is satisfied, he thinks he has saved money, the work is in full swing, but the result is not encouraging.

Time for design ends, you need to typeset and program, as a result, they accept the option, just like at the very beginning of ‚Äúacceptable‚Äù quality, just to start. Obvious losses: time is gone, money is spent, there is no result. At the new coil, they begin to search for a new designer, but with the same approach to the search. History repeats itself.

What does a professional do?


He tries to agree to do this project within the budget of the client, but with the same quality. You may have to sacrifice some chips, remove something or postpone it for the next iteration, but the quality remains the same. A professional can not work somehow. Because working poorly is unprofitable, the result is embarrassing to put in the portfolio, the reputation is spoiled, and the customer has to redo everything.

The reality is that if a person does not know how to do something or does something somehow, then how much you do not pay him, he will not do it better, simply because he has not learned. A professional, if he embarked on a project, will not bungle, regardless of the size of the budget, since reputation is worth more.
