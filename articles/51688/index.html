Acer has updated Aspire One to version AOD150



Acer continues to expand its range of netbooks. According to the official press release , the most recent model is the ten-inch Aspire One AOD150.

The model is based on the Intel Atom N270 processor, equipped with 1 gigabyte DDR2 memory, Intel GMA 950 graphics and 160 gigabyte hard drive.

Other features include a 10.1-inch display with a resolution of 1024 by 600 pixels, a WiFi module, a built-in webcam, three USB 2.0 ports, an all-in-one card reader and a six-cell battery, which should be enough for six parts of work (oh, these manufacturers statements).

Sales of Acer Aspire One have already begun in the United States. The recommended price is $ 350 apiece. The model comes in red and black versions.
