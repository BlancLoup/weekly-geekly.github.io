Smartphones crush the GPS navigation market

If you believe the latest research iSuppli smartphones, and not navigators have become the main platform for satellite navigation.

Interestingly, according to iSuppli, in the first half of the year, the leadership was maintained by the navigators, but now smartphones have come to the first place with confidence.

ISuppli analysts believe that by 2014, in terms of navigation, the ratio of smartphones and navigators will be about 3 to 1 (305 million smartphones and 128 million navigators). At the same time, the popularity of smartphones in the future will only increase, as productivity increases, the amount of memory and battery life on a single battery charge.
