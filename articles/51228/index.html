Let's start from afar. Perhaps, not everyone knows about Hiper, and it is simply necessary to put in a few words about its history and market position;)

The full name of the company is Hiper Group , or more precisely, HIgh PERformance Group and it has existed since 2001. As far as I know, the roots of this English company grow from the well-known Cooler Master - where someone Farsh Fallah served as vice president.
