- When do they stop selling tickets?

In electronic form - one hour before departure. At the box office - 15 minutes before departure. At modern stations - in electronic form 15 minutes before departure from the passenger station, if the train has a device for controlling the legitimacy of electronic tickets. At the ticket office you can buy a ticket right before the departure of the train, if the technical capabilities of the station allow it.

- According to what document can I buy a train ticket?

List of documents
For a passport (regular or foreign) or temporary certificate (for example, issued when a passport is lost), birth certificate for a child, a foreign document (for example, if you are a German citizen with a German passport), military ID, temporary certificate, certificate of a soldier, seaman's passport, prisoner's certificate.

Please note that the rules of Russian Railways do not specify information that a ticket must be purchased by the original document, so some cashiers can issue tickets without presenting the original document (by copy or by document). When boarding the train, the passenger must present the original document, which is listed in the ticket.

Boarding is carried out only if there is a genuine identity document of the passenger, on the basis of which the travel document was acquired.

- Can I sit at the intermediate station?

Paragraph 16 of the Rules for the carriage of passengers establishes that the journey of passengers must begin from the station indicated in the travel document (ticket). However, as explained by the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation in the decision dated 01.07.2004 No. GKPI04-875, this only means that the passenger does not have the right to board the train earlier than the departure point indicated in the ticket. This does not deprive the passenger of the right to use the transportation service, starting from any next station on the way.

The passenger can sit at any station following the departure station indicated on the ticket, subject to the validity of the ticket. According to the rules of JSC ‚ÄúRussian Railways‚Äù, if the place is sold, then the conductor must provide either a place a class higher without extra charge, or a place a class lower with paying the difference.

- Do I need a visa to Kaliningrad?

For a one-time trip, a Lithuanian visa to citizens of the Russian Federation is not needed. It is enough to issue a special simplified travel document on the railway (DFD). The FRTD is issued at the ticket offices at the train station, which simultaneously send a request to the consular section of the Embassy of the Republic of Lithuania in Moscow. 28 hours after the submission of the request, the passenger must apply to any railway ticket office and find out whether he has received permission for the right of transit through Lithuania. If permission is not obtained, the passenger can return the ticket and return the money. Registration of FRTD is free of charge.
