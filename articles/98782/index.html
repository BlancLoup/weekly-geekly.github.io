Check PEOPLEnet account in Linux

Most recently became the proud owner of the mobile Internet from the company PEOPLEnet (Ukraine). I will not discuss the quality of communication, but immediately get down to business. To check the account, you need to send SMS to number 906. But the trouble is, I could not find any sensible program that could send SMS via CDMA modem from under Linux. Having a little rummaged, I have found out that on a site it is possible to look at a detailed statistics on sessions. To find out the rest of the services for the current day, you had to take a calculator and count the amount of traffic spent, which did not suit me. Without hesitation, I wrote a small program on Qt, which itself visits the site, checks the amount of funds in the account and calculates the total traffic used per day. I think the program will be useful not only for me, so I decided to share it.
