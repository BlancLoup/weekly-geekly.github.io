The turnover of online games in Russia by 2016 will reach $ 1.89 billion

According to research by J`son & Partners Consulting, by 2016 the turnover of online games in Russia will grow by 16% and reach $ 1.89 billion. Contrary to the predictions of some experts, the online gaming market did not collapse last year.

At the end of 2014, multiplayer online games remain the largest segment of the gaming market in Russia. They occupy about 40% of the market in monetary terms. Moreover, further growth is expected in this niche. This will happen both by increasing the number of Internet users and by attracting new members to the gaming community. The rest of the market is divided between mobile games, PC games, console and browser games.

However, the fastest growing segment of mobile online games. The most famous publishers and developers of such games are Rovio Entertainment Ltd. , Game Insight and HeroCraft . The mobile segment showed an average annual growth rate of 23%.
