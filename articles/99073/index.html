Opera Mini 5.1 for Android



So the dreams of users of devices running Android came true - today the final version of Opera Mini 5.1 for this platform has been released. Compared to the fifth beta, a lot of work was done to improve the browser. In particular, the rendering of pages on devices with high-resolution screens was improved, work was done on errors in terms of displaying text when scaled. Also, many will like the new kinetic scrolling of pages. By the way, the interface of this version of Opera Mini supports more than 90 languages.



You can download the new version both through the Android Market and from the download page on the official website. To install Opera Mini 5.1 directly on your mobile device, use the address m.opera.com .
