Beeline Data School: With the Upcoming



So 2016 is over. For us, he was very active. There were 6 issues of our course for analysts , 5 issues of the course for managers (Data-MBA) . We launched the course in St. Petersburg and have already held the first issue. In partnership, we also taught students of the Higher School of Economics and the Russian Economic School , conducted master classes in Skolkovo , participated in dozens of hackathons across the country, advised leading companies on the use of analytics and data monetization. This year one of our teachers became the first in the world in the Kaggle ranking .

In 2017 we continue and grow. On January 16, we will launch a regular course for data analysts , on February 5, for managers , and on January 30, the second analytical course will start in St. Petersburg .

Also, following numerous requests on February 28, we launch our course for managers in St. Petersburg.

We congratulate you on the upcoming New Year. We wish it to be full of accomplishments and discoveries in the field of Big Data, artificial intelligence, machine learning and not only. We hope that 2017 will even more be a year of startups in the field of Big Data and artificial intelligence, a year of creating ecosystems of data exchange between companies and their joint monetization, a year of cloud services take-off, a year of transition in many companies from pilot projects in the field of Big Data to real monetization.

Happy holidays and see you in the New Year. Sure that it will be fun)
