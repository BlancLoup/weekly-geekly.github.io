Samsung Apps Screenshots

Samsung is trying to promote its own Android application publishing service .



You can compare the functionality of the service with Google Play .

On the main profile page you can see general download statistics and user comments / questions.

The main differences from Google Play:
- Mandatory verification of applications before publication. Verification takes about a week, in case of problems, the certification team creates a report describing the failure and adds a video that reproduces the problem.
- the requirement to make separate startup modules for different groups of devices (apparently they think that it is too difficult to make an application working normally on different screens).

Total


Samsung Apps provides more complete and detailed statistics and allows you to test the application without even having a specific model.
Judging by the statistics, applications are downloaded more than on Google Play (for the application from this article, downloads are 7-8 times more). Moreover, the most active regions are Brazil, India, etc.

You can answer questions from users. This feature has recently appeared on Google Play and is not for everyone.

The terrible usability of the site spoils the impression a bit. It is difficult to make out what and how to do even if there is a FAQ and a detailed description.
