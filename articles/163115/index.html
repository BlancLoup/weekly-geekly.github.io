Sencha announced a contest for HTML5 applications with a prize fund of more than $ 20,000



More details about the competition can be found on the Sencha website.

Competition conditions: create an application using HTML5 and Sencha.
Applications should be optimized for iPad 4, iPhone 5, Galaxy S3 and Microsoft Surface

The start of the competition began on December 17, 2012, the end of February 15, 2013, from February 15 to February 28, the judges will select the winners, and the best will be announced on March 4.

Prizes:

1st place: $ 10k + iPhone5, iPad4, Galaxy S3, Microsoft Surface, Nexus 10 + invitation to take part in SenchaConf 2013
2nd place: 5k $ + invitation to take part in SenchaConf 2013
3rd place: $ 2500 + invitation to take part in SenchaConf 2013

Judges:

Michael Mullani - CEO at Sencha
Pavel Kopachki - Vice President of Marketing at Sencha
Aditya Bansod - VP, Project Management in Sencha
Dion Almaer - VP, Mobile Architecture at Walmart Labs
