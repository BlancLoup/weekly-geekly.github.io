MTS is done with SMS fraudsters

From today, MTS has included additional protection against fraud on short numbers. Now, before the withdrawal of funds, the subscriber will receive an SMS with an offer to confirm his desire to use the service and reliable information about its cost. Sources in the MTS, who wished to remain anonymous, confirm that this filter was installed on the short numbers that were most often heard in the complaints of subscribers. In the future, if the situation with fraud does not change, a similar filter will be installed on all short numbers.
