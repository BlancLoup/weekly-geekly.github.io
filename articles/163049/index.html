Skype Promo: Unlimited Monthly Subscriptions - Free


Skype started distributing elephants for free for 1 month.
(Within 1 month you will be able to make unlimited calls to home, and in some countries to mobile phones.)

Unlimited calls to 34 countries of the world (in Russia only Moscow and St. Petersburg) are included in the subscription, and in the world: America, Argentina, Austria, Austria, Belgium, Chile, Croatia, England and others.

But as always there is a catch, or rather two.
To activate the free no-limit for a month, you need to confirm the availability of a bank card ($ 1 will be charged from you and returned within a month) .
And after a month you will be switched to a paid subscription for 7.99 Euros per month.

Of course, no one bothers you at the end of the month to disable auto-renewal subscriptions.

You can take part in the promotion on the official Skype website:
http://beta.skype.com/en/offers/skype-with-love/
