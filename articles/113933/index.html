3. Statistics

Despite this, service statistics for the Russian market are generally positive (in my opinion, of course). Depending on the day of the week, there are from 15 to 25 registrations per day, and the number of forms actually placed on the working sites of the users of users ranges from 5 to 10 per day. The number has increased dramatically after the introduction of the form creation wizard from a template that allowed many people not to go any further than the promo site: received the code, inserted it into the site and work with e-mail.
The overwhelming majority of users use the free version of the system, although there are paid customers, but they use the system specifically for internal workflow: there is an entry for THAT, there are hospital and holidays, there is an approval of documents. By the way, almost all paid users came from our other projects.

4. New life

However, the problem with the wrong audience was the impetus for repositioning qFlow . Now I would like to designate a new vision of qFlow as an Online document management system (yes, now we are not afraid of this word). The main task was to make the system as clear as possible, but not to go deep into the jungle of workflow and never bring it closer to the SED. However, qFlow is indeed a simple-minded service that allows small workgroups to switch from paper to electronic workflow without additional costs for installation and training (we check it on ourselves, not without problems).
