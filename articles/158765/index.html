TechCrunch Moscow 2012

On December 9-10, the third Moscow TechCranch Tekloblog Conference will be held - TechCrunch Moscow 2012 . This year's event will be devoted to confronting business models, strategies, technologies, as well as the emergence of new markets and industry leaders.



Conference participants are waiting for the presentation of the most promising start-ups on the first day and the business program on the second.

Among the invited speakers there are such IT-bison as Arkady Volozh (Yandex), Mike Butcher (TechCrunch), Dmitry Grishin (Mail.Ru Group), Edward Shenderovich (Kite Ventures), Anton Nosik (SUP Media) and many others.



On the day of the event on the site tc.digitaloctober.ru will be organized a video broadcast in English and Russian languages.
