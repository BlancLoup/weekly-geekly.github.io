Firefox 2.0 is available for download.

New - the second version of the free Firefox browser is available for download.

The final release of Firefox 2.0 took place a week after the publication of the third release candidate. The new version eliminates vulnerabilities, improves RSS support, adds tab recovery after program crashes, etc. Plugins for versions of the 1.x line in version 2.0 will not work.

Over the two years of its existence, Mozilla Corp. managed to become the second most popular in the world: at the moment it is used (according to various estimates) from 10 to 15 percent of Internet users. Leadership remains with Internet Explorer , which accounts for more than 80 percent of web users.
