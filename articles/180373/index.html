Yahoo has confirmed the purchase of Tumblr for 1.1 billion dollars, Marissa Mayer has promised not to screw it up



For several days there were rumors about the purchase of the Tumblr service by Yahoo, and today the parties finally confirmed the fact of the transaction. Yahoo paid 1.1 billion dollars - and almost the entire amount in cash. Tumblr users are not particularly happy and many people even quit on Wordpress, but Yahoo head Marissa Mayer promised not to spoil Tumblr, although she already announced plans to add ads.

Tumblr's weakness is profitability, writes VentureBeat. The site was able to earn only 13 million dollars last year. This is where Yahoo intends to step in and find ways to generate advertising revenue.
