I will cite a few examples from life, the availability of many publications is limited only by large cities of Russia, it is impossible to get a fresh issue of a magazine in Peregrebnoe village in a stall, you can only dream about purchasing a fresh issue of a magazine in the countries of near and far abroad. In such a scenario, the Internet would seem to be the solution that can overcome the problems of delivering a fresh number to anywhere in the world. But as practice shows, it was not there.

To be honest, the impression is created that many publishing houses simply do not look towards the Internet as a tool for delivering their content to the end user, although all the tools and sales schemes already exist and are adjusted, there is no need to reinvent the wheel.

But not everything is so bad among publishers, some of them are beginning to look at the sale of their publications on the Internet. Therefore, it is completely incomprehensible why the state of the market of electronic versions of printed publications is in the state that we all observe.
