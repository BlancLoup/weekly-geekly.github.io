What is the instruction? This is an understandable sequence of actions due to the reasons or pattern of actions to achieve a certain result. The instruction does not accept deviations from the sequence of actions.
The simplest example. Adjust TV channels. You go to the desired menu item, find the autotune and start the channel search. All is ready. Works? - Of course it works.
