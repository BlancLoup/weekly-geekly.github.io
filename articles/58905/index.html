Service color selection car Colorible

Colorible will help to choose the color of the car (and not only).

The project was created to help people who are planning to buy a new car. Now you do not need to visit the websites of auto manufacturers and search for information about the colors of the offered cars - all this is now available on Colorible. By the way, not many car manufacturers offer the possibility of selecting colors for cars on their websites.

The service allows you to see the car in standard (factory) colors, and to find your own color, and then get a link to the car in the selected color, as well as a color code. The catalog is updated, and it is convenient to subscribe to updates via mail or Twitter .

The service has moved from UseTheColor.com to Colorible.com . The service does not support work in Internet Explorer browsers version 6 and earlier. He invented and made the service konoplovek konopko , but he did not have enough karma to promote himself to praise and I undertook to help him.
