You asked to say when this will happen.



So, starting today, December 5, 2013, all owners of iPhone 5S, iPhone 5C, iPad Air and iPad Mini Retina, certified and purchased in Russian retail chains, can connect to the Beeline LTE network. Owners of the iPhone 5S and 5C devices have the opportunity to be the first in Russia to use services based on LTE.

Updating operator settings for subscribers will be held in the coming days. If you want to speed up this process, you can upgrade to the latest version of iOS and go to Settings - Basic - About the device, and then follow the instructions.

Now Beeline has deployed LTE networks in Moscow and in 6 constituent entities of the Russian Federation: Kaliningrad Oblast, Altai Republic, Adygea Republic, Ingushetia Republic, Kalmykia Republic, Astrakhan Oblast. Currently, 4G network coverage is provided in 80% of urban areas. In the near future, there will be an active expansion of the LTE network in the Moscow Region and the launch of the network in St. Petersburg in March 2014. In 2014, the LTE network will be launched in 6 licensed regions and cities of one million people throughout Russia.
