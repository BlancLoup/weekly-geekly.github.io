New mobile phone AU AQUOS from Sharp


In January, the new Sharp phone with a 2.8-inch widescreen display and an incredibly high 2000: 1 contrast ratio will be available in Japan, for example, this contrast is used in modern plasma and LCD TVs. The need to produce such a model was due to the growing popularity of mobile digital television among the Japanese population.

The phone will be available in white and black colors for respectable uncles and in bright pink for disreputable ones. In addition to its screen, the phone is more than surprising, a standard 2 megapixel camera, support for microSD cards and a 300-hour battery in standby mode.
