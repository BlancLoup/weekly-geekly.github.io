Now from the exterior of the device we go to its hardware characteristics. So, the smartphone is based on a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 2-core processor running at 1.5 GHz. In addition to its other interesting characteristics (for example, automatic transition to a single-core mode of operation, when the user just dials SMS and the operation of two cores is clearly not required), this chip has one fundamentally important characteristic: it supports the new generation of communications - LTE, providing the user with speed 10 times faster than 3G connection. So, both Lumia 920 and Lumia 820 will be supported by Russian LTE networks. The Snapdragon S4 is manufactured using the latest 28 nim process and is equipped with an Adreno 225 graphics processor.

Unlike previous smartphones on WP, which worked with 512 MB of RAM, the Lumia 920 and Lumia 820 are equipped with 1 GB of RAM, which I think will be enough for comfortable work that is traditionally rather undemanding to the hardware resources of WP. As a flash drive in the Lumia 920 uses a 32 GB internal flash drive. That is, Lumia 920 will be sold in only one version. Connector for a removable micro-SD card in the device there, because the body is one-piece. But in the Lumia 820, which has 8 GB of internal memory, the back cover is removed and under it is a slot for a removable micro-SD, supporting cards up to 32 GB.





If we talk about the difference between the Lumia 920 and Lumia 820, then it is easy to paint on several points. Displays differ, - differ both in the size of the diagonal and in the manufacturing technology. If the Lumia 920 is an LCD IPS, then the Lumia 820 is an OLED. However, the proprietary Synaptics technology, which recognizes touching with a fingernail, gloved hand, or key, is also present in the Lumia 820 display.

One of the most important differences between the Lumia 920 and Lumia 820 is the camera. The fact is that the Lumia 920 8MP camera is equipped with PureView technology. However, in contrast to the Nokia 808 PureView super-cameraphone 41MP in summer, the essence of the PureView technology lies not in the huge resolution of the matrix, but in the unique optical stabilization system first implemented in a mobile device. The so-called floating lens is built into the lens of the Lumia 920, which, through gyro commands, gives instructions to the sensor and provides image stabilization, unseen until now, both in the photo and in the video. The resolution of the matrix of the camera Lumia 920 is 8.7 megapixels. Also less than the Lumia 820 and the battery capacity. If in Lumia 920 it is 2000 mAh, then in Lumia 820 - 1650 mAh.
