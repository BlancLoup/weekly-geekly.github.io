Over 3 years of work, we have been in a situation many times when even with a competent technical assignment, the result of work is bad, so I pay attention to this particular item.

How do we deal with this problem in Take It? We collect reviews, ask for recommendations and want the person working with us to communicate in the same language: the same words and concepts. Then everything is good and you can work with a person.

If the final goals differ, you are unlikely to come to a good result, and in order to set goals, both parties must understand each other well.

Creative processes from this point of view are no different from the processes of routine, cyclical, planned and predictable.

The secret to success is getting up every day and doing your own thing. Even when you run into a wall - the result can be achieved by circumventing or breaking the wall. The only thing that I saw working as a placebo in any business is persistence. Every day, do the same actions, even in a situation where the result seems subtle or absent.
