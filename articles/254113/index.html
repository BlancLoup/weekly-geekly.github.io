ComfortWay Platform

The ComfortWay Platform is a technological solution for Internet access. Client platform 3G-Wi-Fi router, the platform itself with functions (billing +):
  • activate, deactivate devices
  • setting traffic consumption limits by users
  • tariff setting for users.
  • partner's personal account
  • API for connecting third-party services
  • calls, sending sending SMS

While on the basis of the platform, a line of products is being created for tourists traveling around the world and I already wrote about these solutions.

We create our mobile WI-FI network (b2b product)

We have a platform. How can I apply it for business? Restriction - the platform is built using the channel of GSM operators, and they have a) fairly cheap corporate rates b) have specialized solutions for companies - BYOD, VPN. The answer is obvious - in roaming, and yes, no one has yet canceled it in Russia. Let us designate potential solution partners - these are hotels, taxis, oriented to work with tourists, car rental service - rent cars, international carriers. All options are focused on tourism - moving the client to the roaming zone. It is not advisable to build such networks in the home network due to competition with GSM operators, but if you see a niche, then why not? In fact, we remain in the same niche in which we worked, but at the same time, we repackage the capabilities of the platform for consumers who can earn on resale services for visitors. At the beginning of the development of the project - it was not obvious - everything, including the architecture, was focused on the creation of the device. Partner functionality was not considered.

Building an Intranet Internet Access Network

In companies, it is possible to build MVNO based not on issues of economy, but from the point of view of control - on ComfortWay, all control is transferred to the partner (client) side.
Advantages of corporate access network on the platform

  • Access to the mobile Internet from your smartphone / tablet / laptop around the world without roaming payments. Basic offer: subscription fee of $ 19 per month, 1 Gb included in the world, additional traffic at $ 19 per Gb.
  • Built-in VPN for secure access to the corporate network with a traffic compression algorithm.
  • Built-in encrypted storage of important data with remote management by a system administrator. It is required that users do not save documents on their smartphone / laptop.
  • Built-in traffic checking for viruses.
  • Restriction of access to entertainment resources with the ability to remotely configure black and white lists6. Hardware authorization on key resources (analogue of HASP-key).
  • New features are easily added "in the air", because The device is a mini-computer on Linux.
  • External battery for charging smartphones and tablets - very convenient to travel.
