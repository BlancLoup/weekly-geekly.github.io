Taxi in mobile or Automation of popular business (part 1)

This is the first part of the review of a new startup: Taxolet taxi.



I want to share with all the new technology, which, without any doubt, will soon become more popular than calling a taxi by phone.

It's about an application to call a taxi in a mobile phone. The first part of my review about what it is all about, why it is much more profitable, more convenient and more interesting than calling a taxi by phone number.

Attention, all customers, addresses, telephones and drivers in this review are specifically fictional, any connection with reality is an accident.

To become a client of our taxi, you just need to download the application to your phone, enter your phone number and select your city. You need to do this only once, and then you will immediately proceed to the application. I will make a reservation in advance, that this is a great find for those who often move between cities - no need to look for taxi numbers and talk with dispatchers.



one


The technology is arranged so that there are no more dispatchers. You apply directly to taxi drivers. Few people like to communicate with dispatchers. Caught and impolite, and incompetent, or simply very tired. Although I sometimes come across exceptions, but rarely. :)



2


Next you need to select the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city and fill out an application. Here we went to meet customers by making auto-complete. This is the essence: the application remembers all the entered addresses, and the next time it automatically substitutes the address as soon as you enter the first letter. Substituted also the name of the street, and the house, and the entrance. If several options were previously submitted to the same letter, then an asterisk can be switched between the options.

It turns out that the filing of an application becomes a matter of only 10-15 seconds.

Apply to a regular taxi will take about the same time. If you get a competent and sleepy dispatcher, you quickly speak and get through immediately, then you can even overtake the application by speed, but such a coincidence is unlikely.



3


Further, more interesting. The application is sent directly to taxi drivers who are in your area. And here you have a legitimate and, undoubtedly, a very pleasant right to choose a driver, who will have the honor of taking you (by the way, I, with open testing, really liked this privilege).



As you can see from the example, you can choose a car according to your personal preferences - either the ‚Äúcoolest‚Äù one, or the one that comes the fastest, or for the price, if everything is not so good with money. Each driver rides on his personal rates - they can be listed as a fixed price, either by mileage, or by price per minute. Depends on the city. For example, in Tolyatti they ride mostly at fixed rates, but there are firms operating by mileage. Often it will be possible to meet an economical option, because there will be competition between drivers, and some will carry cheap if they are close to them or for other reasons known only to them.

four


So, we chose a driver. By the way, I personally do not know a single telephone taxi offering a choice of car. I haven't seen anything like this anywhere (except, possibly, capital cities and a vip-taxi).



A counter will appear on the screen with a countdown from the time specified by the driver. You can leisurely gather and know when you come. But let's face it - in most cases, the claimed time is not true. Most often you go out into the street, and there is no car on the horizon. You have to wait, which is not very pleasant - bad weather, for example. Or on the contrary, you leave, and the driver has long been on the spot and demands payment for idle time.
In the application everything is thought out. As soon as the driver arrives at the scene, an inscription appears on the phone, and also it vibrates and beeps ‚Äî in other words, by all possible means it informs the client that the driver is already at the door. While testing was going on, they called taxis even on minor matters, since I really liked these features. Surprisingly, the driver came to the place only once after the end of the counter.

As long as I traveled in an ordinary taxi, it was always the other way around - you go out and wait, even there was a habit of going out 5-7 minutes later than the dispatcher said (I think most people do). Apparently, the motto of our taxi is quite justified.



five


Convenience - now you can call a taxi from any noisy places, for example from clubs. Given that taxis are very often called from clubs, then for such a category of people it will be a real find.
