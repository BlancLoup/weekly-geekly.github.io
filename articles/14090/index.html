Web 2.0 what will happen next? - Web 3.0

What is Web 2.0 - it is believed that the exact description yet. I want to offer it. And for this it is necessary to propose a logic that will show the development - what was before and what will be after.

So Web or Web 1.0 - the Internet, pages, portals, users - while users are passive readers of portals, all information and work is done by the owners of pages and portals.

Web 2.0 - users manage the content of portals - the owners of portals and pages create conditions for the convenience of users. Users work on the basis of their own enthusiasm.

Web 3.0 - the owners of pages and portals create conditions so that users not only work (create content, attendance ...), but earn. Those. technologically set users a narrow task within which they could do the GOODS. Those. quality and popular product that is right there on the portal and would be sold. Thus, users make a product, users buy a product, users receive money, the portal receives a percentage.



Why I divided these categories exactly. Because after Web 1.0 in Web 2.0, the properties of portals have changed - they have become massive and centripetal. Those. users are interested where there are a lot of users, where there is a lot of content, where life is in full swing - and this quality determined the wave of portals that were given the name Web 2.0

Web 3.0 takes users to a new level, they are interested in revenues, therefore in sales of their product, therefore in its high quality. This means that the portal gets more content or goods and it is more qualitative, it attracts additional users and some of them become buyers, and part of them is included in the production of goods ‚Äî both conditions bring the quality and speed of the portal to new quality indicators.

Plus, if the portal in the Web 2.0 system could become commercial only when it gained more traffic ‚Äî which can be converted into advertising revenue ‚Äî then in the Web 3.0 system, the portal is commercial from the moment it was launched, as soon as the first purchase was made - the portal began to receive its percentage - it also allows you to develop at a completely different speed without waiting for investors or not promoting a portal for sale.

So back to the (type of scientific) definition of Web 2.0 and Web 3.0:

Web-2.0 is a set of solutions that creates conditions for creating content with the involvement of a large number of users acting on the basis of enthusiasm.

So Web-3.0 is a complex of solutions in which conditions are created for creating content by a large number of users on a reimbursable basis. Those. users except moral satisfaction receive a material reward.
