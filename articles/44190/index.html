Conclusions :
  • It's hard to work with the Chinese. Highly. If there is no person in China or if there is no possibility to go shopping for the goods, then it is better to think 5 times before deciding to buy. If, nevertheless, they decided to buy something from the Chinese, then it is better to think another 5 times, and so on until this desire disappears (or not get tired of thinking).
  • The supply contract must be required. The contract must specify the allowable percentage of marriage, delivery time and conditions for refund.
  • If there are any problems or misunderstandings, then it is better to find out them BEFORE sending the goods, and it is better BEFORE payment. Then to solve something is almost impossible.
  • If the lot is large, then, nevertheless, it is better to go and control the delivery yourself (or through the auditors to resolve this issue).
  • If you buy something, it is better to indicate in the details of the recipient a private person, and not a company (no names of sites or anything superfluous). Otherwise, the process of customs clearance can be very complicated.
  • It is better to organize sending by the state mail, but not by means of the courier companies. Had conversations with representatives of DHL, UPS and FedEx, in all these companies to receive the goods you need to pay a customs fee. EMS has no such problems.
  • If it turned out that it is necessary to undergo customs control, then it is better to do it yourself, without resorting to the services of customs brokers. It is expensive, but if you wish, you can do everything yourself, the main thing is not to pull with it.
  • Before passing through the controlling authorities, it is necessary, nevertheless, to translate the accompanying documents. It is possible that the translation can be done independently, and in the translation bureau simply put a seal "for cookies".
  • The opening of the parcel / parcel / container must ALWAYS be held IN YOUR PRESENCE . Otherwise, you will not be able to prove that the goods came to you in good condition.
  • When dealing with customs officials, it is highly desirable to have documents confirming the declared value of the goods, otherwise you will have to pay a customs fee based on the average price for this type of goods.

Well, actually everything. From myself I would like to say that working honestly in our country is almost impossible. The only option at the moment to shop on the Internet is if the delivery will be made using EMS. Otherwise, without ties in customs control, the final purchase price may increase dramatically.
