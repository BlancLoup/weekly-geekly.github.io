I want to share good musical news for connoisseurs and producers: Fidel.ru justifies its mission and begins to work closely with small thematic labels, as well as directly with independent musicians. Down with the middlemen!

Immediately interrupt and reveal a big secret. From October this year, each author will be able to upload his works online and legally earn them through the largest digital content retailer in the Russian Federation: we are completing the development of an automated content import and distribution system, Fidel Distribution. And you can download, of course, not only your music, but also video, literature, software, games, animation. The only thing is to have to print and send the signed contract (they say, I authorize Fidel.ru to sell for me this and that). Everything else is online: the data is filled-checked online, documents are generated automatically, in your account there will be online statistics on downloads and fees, the movement of docks can also be tracked online. Stop sales, request a report, get a fee, order advertising - everything is online. Long live the Internet!

For now, our CEO is looking for interesting labels on his own to add them to the Fidel.ru catalog. For example, from September you can find exclusive for Russia albums from London-based CurveMusic on the site. This is a very fresh and quite worthy label, the only one releasing young Brazilian musicians in Europe. The main genres are house, ethno-jazz, electro-pop. Formed in 2004, already in 2005, Curve won MIDEM awards (Cannes) for innovations in the World Jazz category.
