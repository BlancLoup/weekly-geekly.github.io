Idea and business model:
It all started, as always, with a simple one: with a beer with my friend. For several years he has been engaged in NLP practitioners at the trainer level, as well as in private psychological counseling. And if in short, he gave me that the business of providing psychological services in Russia lags behind the west by 8-12 years. For example, only now we have people realize that psychological training is a very profitable business. And in the west, the wave began to rise just in the early 90s. If you notice this, then it becomes clear that a wave of psychological services through the Internet is about to overwhelm us. Well, why not anticipate her in something?


So, our idea was born simple: the provision of psychological services on the Internet.
Here are the following main advantages:
  • Anonymity: it is sometimes desirable to solve your problem anonymously, rather than shuffle through a real consultation and face to face with a living person
  • Price: psychologists from different parts of our country can provide services via the Internet, and therefore the price of consultation for the client will be lower
