We are actively developing internal professional communities in Raiffeisenbank. For each of the directions, we regularly hold meetings and share news about what is happening with someone on the project, who has learned what is interesting and what can be taught.

We know how important it is to communicate with people from other teams and projects, to have the opportunity to ask for advice, discuss newly emerging technologies and share experiences. Therefore, on May 16, in the Moscow office of Raiffeisenbank, we will organize our first open Java MeetUp.
