GT03B GPS - what's inside

Came by mail ordered more than a month ago, the long-awaited GPS tracker GT03B. It was not enough internal struggle is it worth buying this thing. It is not cheap, but where is the guarantee that it will be also "not cheap" to work ... I need a tracker. I have many children, and I would very much like to control their movement around the city, as well as their social circle. The device allows you to see its position on the site www.cootrack.com and receive SMS messages by SMS request or in case of alarm situations when the armed mode is on. Remembers 4 telephone numbers, interacts with them. Understands the format of recording numbers in the form of +3753312344567.



The first thing that caught my eye when I opened the box was its dimensions. For some reason, I expected significantly more. The device seemed to me very compact. In addition to the tracker itself, the box contains: charger, USB interface cable (also known as charging cable), battery, and instructions. The device itself had 2 switches, a mini-USB connector, 2 LEDs and a relatively large red SOS button, which can also be illuminated from the inside with a separate LED. Ie LEDs in the amount of 3. The device is painted with a soft-touch velvet paint that resembles rubber.
Gathering the device, I put it on charge. Until the full charge, it was not distracted by the hour 3. After that, real life began - the period of development and tuning. I inserted an active SIM card free at that time and began to consistently, looking at the instructions, send an SMS to the device, and then carefully study its answers. Then he checked the security functions. It is necessary to admit that while I understood the syntax and its features - I was mistaken more than once. The device either kept silent or sent an error code. But all the same it is necessary to recognize that it is elementary to configure it and further it clearly works with these settings regardless of the change of the SIM card. I tried voice only rates. Then online tracking does not work. I also tried only Internet tariffs - then the voice functions do not work, ie remote listening and dialing by pressing the SOS button. However, SMS is sent and received.
The device is controlled and programmed exclusively via SMS and external buttons / switches. Any attempts to connect it to a computer fail. A known-good USB cable from a video camera was taken (I want to emphasize its quality with this) and viewing of logs in Linux was turned on - not a single line twitched the log under any circumstances. Ie from USB, it can only be charged. How to control the device can be read in the instruction manual , so we will not dwell on this.
The next day, the navigator, which in the evening after the experiments did not turn off and was not charged extra, was sewn into the pocket of one of the children who were going to day camp. It is sewn so as not to get lost. After they left, the site was opened and ... My wife could not find a place for herself with delight :). Throughout the day, she could know exactly where the child is. It should be noted that the device allows you to track not only by GPS, but also by GSM.
