Friday format: how to start working in the music industry (part 2)

In the first part, we talked about how to get on the radio . Today we will continue the story and analyze the myths faced by novice DJs and vocalists.

Photo of possan CC-BY

Myths for DJ


"I will not understand the technique"

A person who is not connected with DJing has gotten eyes from one type of equipment: a huge console, turntables, many buttons, laptops. Of course, you can go the hard way and learn to play vinyl to understand what a lag, delay, or "horses" are (often a deliberate mistake of mixing).

In fact, you can learn everything very quickly if you start with simple tools like Virtual DJ or Traktor. They will help to master the "information". You do not need to buy expensive equipment for training right away, and you definitely shouldn't go to DJing schools.

For mastering the basics there are many clips on the Internet. But it is better to tighten the English, video tutorials on the most advanced "chips", as always, are not translated.

Now the main method of information - from the computer. Both vinyl and CDs can be connected to a laptop, you can just play from several computers, connect utilities and tools for live performance to them. Digital DJing is simple, and this is not the future, but already the present. Well, best to learn from the music that you like.

"I will play my own"

Even if you need to make a start not from a particular style, but from what kind of music you like in principle, then a beginner DJ can get stuck on one thing. It is not right. Playing only deep house or techno, you will not be able to move forward and ‚Äúsell‚Äù yourself. In this case, the eye becomes blurred very quickly, the sets become monotonous and boring.

If you consider DJing as a job, you often have to play not what you like, but something that will require this or that institution. Until the DJ earns a name for himself.

"I know all the trends"

It is not enough just to know what is in fashion now. Music needs to be heard and understood. You will have to constantly update your music base, look for a new one, collect a music library.

At first, when a DJ plays at a disco or corporate party, this is doubly important. The dance floor does not always want what the DJ thinks is ‚Äúright.‚Äù This will have to be tolerated.

For the process of updating the library can be allocated several hours a week. For example, Sunday morning. The musical base is an obligatory attribute of a good DJ.

Paradox - the better the musical taste, the harder it is to choose tracks. To choose the music you should use decent headphones. Ideally, of course, monitors are needed.
