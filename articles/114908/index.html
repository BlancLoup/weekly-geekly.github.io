Sergey Andreev

CEO, President of the ABBYY group of companies.
Winner of the Prize of the Government of the Russian Federation in the field of science and technology.

Born September 12, 1969 in Krasnoyarsk. In 1992 he graduated from the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology (MIPT), Faculty of General and Applied Physics. While studying at the last MIPT courses, Sergey Andreev joined Bit Software (ABBYY since 1997) as commercial director. In 1996, he was appointed executive director, and in 1999, general director of the ABBYY group of companies.

Under the leadership of Sergey Andreev, innovative software products ABBYY have been developed, the distribution network of the company in Russia and abroad has been built, international offices have been opened in Europe, the USA and Asian countries, and a technology licensing system has been developed for third-party developers. Since 1999, Sergey has initiated and carried out a number of large-scale structural reorganizations reflecting the company's organic growth.

In 2007, Sergei initiated the creation of the ABBYY "Image Recognition and Text Processing" department at MIPT.
