Flash for Android for ARMv6 processors



If you remember, Adobe stated that it would not release Flash for Android devices without the support of ARMv7 instructions.

So, rejoice, owners of Android-devices with ARMv6-processors!

The Android Campus development team released the Flash 10.1 port, adapted for multiple Android devices based on processors with the ARMv6 instruction set. Links, a list of supported devices and some details under the cat.

According to the information from the branch of this modification with xda-developers , the authors are the developers of firmware for the Chinese mipad tablet.

Actually the distribution itself was obtained using a modification of apk from Adobe, so it is not in the Market, and this is logical.

Link to download apk from google sites from the developer

QR code - at the beginning of the post. The package is installed if the Android version is 2.2 or higher.

The list of supported models, on which the modification works, is updated in a post on the modification authors blog .

However, it should be noted that on the part of the claimed devices the success of the installation depends on the firmware. So, the modification is not installed on the factory firmware Samsung Galaxy Mini, Samsung Galaxy Ace.

Also some details from personal experience:
- On ZTE Blade (aka Orande San Francisco / Beeline E400), the installation of the application itself is successful on both stock firmware and CyanogenMod;
- Support appears in all webkit-based browsers - stock, SkyFire, Dolphin Browser HD:

- In browsers on own engines, for example in Opera Mobile - there is no flash support:

- As you have already noticed, the version is defined as 10.1.616
- Both pages with small flash elements and videos of more than three megabytes work great, the interactivity is not lost
- In addition to browsers, flash is picked up by numerous swf players, for example, SmartFlash
- There are some flaws in the drawing, for example, sometimes the transparency of the layer during animation is incorrectly handled.

In general,% username%, if the processor of your smartphone is based on the ARMv6 instruction set - I recommend this mod for installation - with such a flash it is definitely better than without a flash in general.
