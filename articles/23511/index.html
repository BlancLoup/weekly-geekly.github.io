Autosubmit to social bookmarking services

Online social bookmarking services can be a good source of links from quality resources and become part of your link building campaign (add links).

The constant addition of your sites to online bookmarking services, of course, needs to be automated (as this is extremely time consuming). To do this, smart people came up with autosubmitters.

Recently, I met a small review of auto-publishers on the maximus325.com/?p=14 blog. I tried all the FREE. Now I give my recommendations ...

The free web services mentioned in the article, which add at least to more than 10 services, did NOT suit me:

The most worthy (maybe someone will appreciate, all the same)
Socialposter.com - uncomfortable interface, it is not clear what and how to do.
Socialmarker.com (bad list, dead and paid services).

I found another autosubmission that gave me 80% - Onlywire.com .

The procedure for automatically adding links is as follows:
- Register first! on all online bookmarking services themselves, you get a login and password (no one has yet figured out how to do this automatically);
- in a special form on the service for each site, fill in the login and password;
- save your data;
- add the auto-submit button to the browser (now you can delete all the buttons furl it, spurl it, fave it, diigo it and others);
- open the site you want to add and click on the button.
