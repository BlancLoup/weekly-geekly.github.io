Fotonotes for Drupal 6. Notes for photos in the style of Flickr.com

Fotonotes is a script intended for creating ‚Äúnotes‚Äù on pictures: select a section of a photo, leave a comment for this section and, later, when you hover the cursor on it, your comment will be displayed. Thus it is very convenient to mark friends on collective photos or nodes on complex schemes. This feature is actively used, for example, on Flickr.com .

Now Fotonotes is available as a module for CMS Drupal 6 .
