What is QuietOn

QuietOn - intelligent electronic ear plugs that are designed to reduce ambient noise at certain frequencies, making work, hobbies and recreation more comfortable. These headphones can be addressed:

  • 1. For those who have problems falling asleep, they are acutely responsive to surrounding sounds. For example, the sounds of snoring.
  • 2. For those who cannot concentrate enough on working in a noisy open office.
  • 3. Representatives of the construction professions.
  • 4. Those who fly frequently.



Who they do not fit:

  • 1. In fact, in bed they will not be very comfortable.
  • 2. Those who are used to working with music

We'll have to admit that these are headphones for those who love silence - such a paradox. Moreover, not absolute silence. Experience of use indicates that they are blocking, of course, not all.

The effect of noise reduction is achieved using the technology of inverting sound waves in the low-frequency range. As a result, you hardly hear the annoying noise and hear the interlocutor's voice well or public information announcements. QuietOn is also used in noisy industries, in open-space offices, in homes near noisy highways, at night next to a snoring person.

How QuietOn Gags work

Innovative noise suppressors allow you to create a personal, quiet space for sleeping or working. Unlike conventional earplugs, they not only block the ear canal, but also invert sound waves, which reduces the noise level by 40 dB!



They are arranged quite simply: there is a microphone that picks up the sound and a processor that determines whether to ‚Äúdampen‚Äù it or not. If the sound needs to be removed, the headphones give out the antiphase of the incoming sound, plus a minus = zero. Approximately the way sound leakage compensation works in bone headphones.



And it works. At first, this seems a bit unusual, but as a result you get used to such noise cancellation quite quickly, and the surrounding sound space becomes quite natural.

Plus to the active noise suppressors, the cushions play the role of passive noise suppressors. The set includes three interchangeable nozzles: two versions of silicone and one foam one.
