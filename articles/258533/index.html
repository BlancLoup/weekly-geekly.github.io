Cocoa Developers Club - Club iOS and OS X developers

It all started with the fact that three months ago we decided to unite into a community of Russian-speaking iOS developers.

As you know, developers love to unite in communities for the purpose of sharing experiences or just to talk on topics of life. There are a lot of similar communities, but, as it seems to me, developers (no matter whether he is a beginner or not) always have a demand for live communication with similar developers.

To meet this demand, we decided to use a modern approach. Currently, new tools have appeared for group communication, such as HipChat or Slack (read more about Slack here ). In addition to being used in the work of a specific team, they can also be used to form communities of interest. We chose Slack.

This is how we looked then:



During these three months, more than 1000 participants from all over the world have joined us (15 time zones). All of them, in varying degrees, are lovers of the case that unites them - this is a development for iOS and OS X.

During this time, we even managed to hold an informal meeting (conversations over a glass of beer) at the same time in three cities: Moscow, St. Petersburg and Minsk.

And now, as it seems to us, we have become not just a community - we have become a club. A club is a place where people meet with common interests. And this is about us, we meet every day in our club.

From this day we are called Cocoa Developers Club.

The Cocoa Developers Club is an open club. We welcome any new member, from absolute beginners who still do not know where to start, to experienced professionals who are ready to share their experience.

In order to get to us, you must fill out a small form on our website . And in response, we will send you an invitation.

Welcome to the club!
