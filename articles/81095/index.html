Cartographic meeting in Kiev


On January 23, 2010 , at 13:00, the next meeting of the Kiev Google Technology Users Group (Kyiv GTUG), dedicated to maps and the Google Maps API, will take place at the Hudgraf cafe located at 33 Taras Shevchenko Boulevard.
Presentations will be made by:
  • Anton Berezhniy, captain of the winning team of the Google Map Maker Latin America contest
  • Vladimir Bilonenko, developer of Maplos.com
  • Anton Linevich and Sergey Polzunov, developers Flatfinder.com.ua
  • Ilya Muzychuk, technology developer wLBS, Glaive Security Group
  • Igor Shubovich, author of the blog Mapping.in.ua , enthusiast of OpenStreetMap .
To participate, please fill out the registration form . First registered participants will receive a free drink. Free admission.
