Project idea


Service focused on user publications, their printing and the encouragement of authors.

Purpose of publication

  1. I want to share ideas - "steal" on health.
  2. Search, but rather not an investor, but - teams, people. People who do not know how much, but who want to learn a lot, in particular, design, programming, implementation. People who will keep me company in self-education. No matter what you know now, it is important that you want to do it. A team capable of great achievements. Altruistic people who understand that copying is the basis for the functioning of culture. Well, everything else. And I want to learn how to practical design of convenient things, programming, drawing (I myself know html / css very well).

Service Services

  • Authors may publish articles, but only under a free commercial license.
  • Users can duplicate articles to my favorites.
  • Users can print " on demand " collections of articles. The system recommends when it is better to do so that collections do not work out too expensive.
  • The authors receive the bulk of the mark-up on print. (the rest of the service + non-print authors, for example, webcast authors)
  • Users can edit cloned articles.
  • Any user has visual access to all edited articles and editing. Authors can thus improve their articles.
  • When adding to favorites (duplication) the user is obliged to specify tags
  • No ads

Streams

  • Total 3 + 1 publication streams
    1. all publications
    2. all but ignored (by author, tag or other parameter)
    3. only selected authors or tags
    4. a thread like a friendfed where people share links (to prevent copy-paste, and this is not all links, but friends, friends of friends, selected authors, and the possibility of separate discussions there)

  • Threads except for all publications are modified duplicates.
  • Publications can be removed from such streams.
  • A way to promote non-print topics
  • There is no ranking as such, there is open statistics on the number of printed, cloned, etc.

Discussions

  • 3 groups of comments related to the publication
  • 1st group is displayed by default
  • The 2nd group is hidden, nothing will be removed from there (taking into account the 3rd, I think - is it necessary?)
  • 3rd group - visual display of user discussions (from ala frendfid stream)

Development (after implementation of the main functionality)

  1. Improved filters and personalization (so that not interesting publications are annoying)
  2. opportunity to work with partners - printing not only on their own
  3. multi-user publishing / editing

Community

  • initially web developers
  • initially not all have the opportunity to publish
  • The opportunity to publish is given by the authors, and also automatically given to users whose edits are popular.
  • fully open after sharpening filters and personalization systems
