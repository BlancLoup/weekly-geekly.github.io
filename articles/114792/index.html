Geek has adapted Kinect for playing Tetris



It is clear that Tetris itself is of no interest to anyone as a game (although this is not entirely correct, many people play Tetris, at least on a mobile phone, standing in line for beer at the theater). However, geeks use this game in order to show their own ability to adapt any device that is unsuitable for a game as a platform for Tetris. You won't surprise anyone with the game itself - even some models of oscilloscopes already have a built-in Tetris, which is to say about mobile phones, watches and, moreover, game consoles. One of the craftsmen combined the old game (Tetris) and the new gadget (Microsoft Kinect), creating something special.

The game does not use the Xbox 360 game console, but a PC with Kinect connected to it. According to the developer, the game Kinetris appeared as a result of experiments with Kinect. Software sources that allow you to run this entire system into a business, you can ask the author of the development, which promises to share with everyone, you just need to refer to it (this can be done here ).

More detailed information about the principles of the system and the software that powers the system, no. Generally speaking, Kinect was not fit for anything except its immediate purpose. Through Kinect, a virtual interior was created in the house, and even fireballs were launched, and almost real, created by means of a media projector. This time we have a game of tetris. What will happen next? Let's wait and see, and we hope that what follows in the future will be even more interesting.
