Mobile Opera on Nokia X Platform



Today at the Mobile World Congress conference in Barcelona, Opera's mobile browser based on the Blink engine debuted on the Nokia X platform. The browser will be pre-installed on all Nokia X devices, at the time of launch they are Nokia X, Nokia X + and Nokia XL.
Being a key player on the debuting platform is always very interesting. And doubly interesting when the project is a company such as Nokia. We have a rich history of cooperation with Nokia, and we are glad that they chose Opera to be included in Nokia X right from the day the platform was launched. Together with Nokia, we intend to provide users with the best surfing experience on the world wide web.
Lars Boylesen, CEO of Opera Software.
