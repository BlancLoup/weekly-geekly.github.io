PC Magazine / RE magazine can be read on mobile devices and e-books

Today, the largest copyright holder for the use of electronic copies of Russian-language books on the Internet "Liters" and the magazine PC Magazine / RE announced the launch of a joint project. Now PC Magazine / RE magazine can be read on mobile devices and e-books - the mobile version of the publication appeared in the online hypermarket "liters". Each issue of the magazine is presented in 12 different formats, which allows you to read it on any mobile devices: smartphones, communicators, electronic books with screens based on electronic ink (e-ink) and many other specialized eBook-devices.

As it is noted in ‚Äúliters‚Äù, the entire volume of the material of the printed number is preserved, despite the complicated process of re-design, which adapts the publication to the mobile format. Maybe due to this, or maybe just due to the novelty of such an initiative, PC Magazine / RE almost immediately entered the top ten of the sales rating. In turn, the chief editor of PC Magazine / RE, Oleg Lebedev, notes that the opportunity to offer the readers of the magazine information in a new format is very timely. However, according to Lebedev, the mobile version of the magazine does not replace the printed edition, but complements it.

The project itself was launched in mid-April. Initially, the mobile version of PC Magazine / RE was conceived as an experiment, no one expected such a dynamic start. Actual issues of the magazine stood next to such regulars of the Liters bestseller rating, like Daria Dontsova and Tatyana Ustinova. Today, all issued numbers for 2009 are available in ‚Äúliters‚Äù, as well as releases of the fourth quarter of 2008. Work is underway to prepare the entire collection of numbers for 2008.

The content of the PC Magazine / RE print edition in the mobile edition is almost complete, the structure of the magazine and materials, including tie-ins, footnotes and infographics, basic illustrations and tables, award logos, etc., are kept. On a CD-ROM supplied with the print edition, additional articles were posted - they are also included in the mobile version. Table of contents is formed by means of the program for reading. At the same time, for obvious reasons, the CD-ROM is not attached to the mobile version (including the keys to a number of software products that buyers of the printed version receive), photos and images are reduced in size, the tables are in the form of graphic images.
