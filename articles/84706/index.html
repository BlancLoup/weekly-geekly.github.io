Design Patterns

Hello, habrochelovek!

It so happened that from this semester in my high school I enrolled in a special course on design patterns. The course takes place in the form of seminars where you (the student) should tell you what you learned in a week (meaning, which patterns you learned and applied in practice).

In turn, I decided to write posts on the habre about the studied patterns. Links to these posts can be seen below.

Structural class / object design patterns


  • Adapter (Adapter)
  • Bridge
  • Linker (Composite)
  • Decorator or Wrapper
  • Facade
  • Flyweight
  • Deputy (Proxy) or Surrogate (Surrogate)
  • Information Expert
  • Low Coupling
  • Resistant to change (Protected Variantions)

Patterns of designing classes / objects behavior


  • Chain of Responsibility
  • Command (Command), Action (Action) or Transaction (Transaction)
  • Iterator or Cursor
  • Mediator
  • Keeper (Memento)
  • Observer, Publish -Subscribe or Delegation Event Model
  • State
  • Strategy
  • Template Method
  • High gearing (High Cohesion)
  • Controller
  • Polymorphism (Polymorphism)
  • Artificial (Pure Fabrication)
  • Redirection (Indirection)
  • Visitor
  • Interpreter

Generating design patterns


  • Singleton
  • Abstract Factory
  • Builder
  • Creator of class instances
  • Factory Method or Virtual Constructor
  • Prototype (Prototype)
