How to "send" the client


Long ago, in our hosting panel of Parallels Plesk Panel, we made such an interesting feature: if you entered the address of the panel with the port (8443), but without specifying encryption (https), then Plesk redirected the user to https://<server_hostname>:8443 . It was comfortable. Someone got used to this behavior. And someone even took into account in their processes. In version 11.5, we replaced the web server that serves Plesk itself, c lighttpd with nginx. And inadvertently broke that little chip. They simply could not think of why it was needed, and did not implement it with the new web server. Users who have accessed http://:8443, 400 - ¬´Bad request¬ª.
