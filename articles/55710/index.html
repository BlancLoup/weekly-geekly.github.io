In the summer of 2008, the GoToury project was very confident about itself. With the help of a simple BTL advertising project broke out in the tops of Yandex. The project competition and the project itself have become one of the most discussed topics of the runet and blogosphere in particular. I personally took a small part in that competition and saw the project a little closer. For 2008 it was a very interesting idea and a completely worthy implementation. The game itself was an adventure with the atmosphere of a tropical island. Colorful, positive, bright. Advance win-win project. But not in this country. More precisely, not at this time.

Russian society is not yet ready to use even a tenth of the progress of the digital world. This should be taken into account when creating any, even the simplest, project designed for mobile phone users. If you think about it, in recent years only ringtones can be called successful in this area. And even then, this should be attributed to the lack of knowledge in the field of new technologies among the population. Or a complete lack of opportunities to use these technologies. Ringtones are usually ordered in three cases.
1. Lack of knowledge on the synchronization of the phone and computer.
2. The absence of a computer with Internet access.
3. Lack of skills in processing audio files (cutting "pieces" from the whole track).

Already today you can count at least 10 failed projects of similar GoToury or Meeting . It is necessary to understand that the domestic consumer will be ready for online games and social services based on gps no earlier than in 2 years.
