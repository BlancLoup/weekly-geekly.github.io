Writers wrote a letter to Medvedev against free licenses and other amendments to the Civil Code

We are talking about the most revolutionary amendments in the Civil Code.

Dear Dmitry Anatolyevich! We appeal to you with a request to stop the process of legalization of book piracy , which is now happening under the good slogans of replenishing the library funds of the country with digitized books. At present, the adoption of amendments to the Fourth Part of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation is underway ... these amendments contradict the meaning and spirit of international agreements on intellectual property adopted by the Russian Federation in the run-up to, we hope, soon WTO accession.

Under the guise of "removing administrative barriers to the innovative development of the economy," it is proposed to introduce completely vague innovations into the legislation, open to any interpretations.
