Android Froyo 2.2 for HTC Hero

Hi, Habravchane!

This morning was marked by good news for me - a well - known team released an unofficial firmware for HTC Hero based on Android Froyo 2.2 - FroydVillain 1.0.0 .

So, in the firmware:
  • Android Froyo 2.2 without Flash 10.1
  • All equipment works as expected (the standard camera program does not work, you can use Camera Magic)
  • Your bootscreen
  • Its core with the possibility of overclocking the processor to 748Mhz
  • Work with EXT section
  • All htc ringtones, messages, notifications, etc ..
  • High performance Open GL and firmware as a whole
  • Fancy Widget (HTC weather widget)
  • The choice - Launcher Pro or Launcher 2


To install the new firmware, of course, requires a full wipe.
Being installed in the morning, the firmware used the process of using it.

Personal impressions:
  • the regular dialer has no active controls during the call, as a result of which there are no accidental clicks when the phone is attached to the ear
  • ADW Launcher instead of HTC Sense is more functional.
  • It works great sorting and searching contacts in Russian letters
  • The regular Lockscreen on the Home button does not fall out on the desktop, which eliminates accidental pressing when carrying the phone in your jeans pocket
  • The new version of Navitel for Android 2.2 stopped stuttering (I don‚Äôt know, really, who to thank for it)
