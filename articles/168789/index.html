TPB AFK



TPB-AFK, a documentary about The Pirate Bay and its founders. The film was created over 4 years and tells about the life of the three founders of the Pirate Bay and the trial of their case in Sweden. Today is the day of the premiere of the film, and you can download and share it for free with anyone

Pirate Bay, represented by its founders, has been a defendant in numerous lawsuits for several years, and director Simon Klose has documented the process of the struggle of rights holders and owners of TPB.

Today the premiere of the film at the prestigious Berlin Film Festival, but for the first time you can join as a spectator. The film is already available on YouTube and Pirate Bay itself.
