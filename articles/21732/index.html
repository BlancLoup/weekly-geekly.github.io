GroovyTel and IPKall - free US phone number

Recently I took a note on the site of the wonderful service gtalk2voip.com to see what new baubles appeared and saw interesting news for January 29th about the service www.groovytel.com
In short - a personalized US phone number with the possibility of redirection to Google Talk, MSN Messenger, Yahoo Messenger, Free World Dialup, Gizmo5. When registering, you can choose a number from 3 offered, they gave me the form + 15672587XXX. It really works checked only on gtalk. When making a call, gtalk receives a call from the subscriber with a phone number (if determined).

Similar services: gobaza, jetnumbers.

Update:

After surfing the Internet, I found another site, www.ipkall.com, which has great advantages over groovytel (for me personally) in its ability to use hardware solutions (supports SIP and IAX). I set up a redirect to the sipnet.ru account which is running on AudioCodes MP-202. Now you do not need a computer to receive calls and you can use a regular phone device =)
