Hi, Habr! Today we want to present a new edition of our infographic series Runet in Pictures. This time we decided to consider the market of bank cards in the world and in Russia. Based on the collected data, we estimated what cards Russians use and how actively, how popular various MPSs are and what trends are noticeable today on the Russian plastic market.





When collecting information, we used data provided by the Central Bank of Russia, international payment systems VISA and MasterCard.



So, what conclusions can be drawn from the above data?

1) We are still significantly lagging behind Europe and America in terms of the number of bank cards per 1 inhabitant of the country (on average, there are 2 bank cards per 1 Russian). However, the growth rates of the number of bank cards are encouraging and demonstrate that the Russian plastic market has a good potential in store.

2) In Russia, an absolute majority consists of debit cards (although an overdraft is open to some of them).
