Package and intermediary: do you need a middleman?

Very often in the comments to our publications we meet questions about the need for a mediator. For example, some US stores send parcels directly to Russia. Why do we need a parcel ? Today we will tell you about it and, of course, we will be happy to answer your new questions in the comments.

As an illustrative example, let's take a simple situation: you decided to buy certain goods in an American store. Suppose this is going to be a popular Amazon. You chose the goods for a long time, settled on 15 items.



Where to deliver?


Here you will find the first surprise. Yes, Amazon sometimes offers direct delivery to Russia, but not all products. And there may well be a situation in which 10 goods can be sent to you, and the remaining 5 - not. What to do?

This is where the Banderolkin service comes in handy. As a rule, delivery is free for the USA, the incoming parcels are free at the Parcel Post, so here you absolutely do not lose anything. Moreover, having received the goods in your account, you can immediately check it for integrity, compliance with the order, size and many other parameters with the help of special queries . It is much more convenient, easier and cheaper than getting the goods in Russia and finding that the seller or a few of them were wrong.

How to deliver?


But it may be that Amazon can send all your selected products to Russia on its own. It would seem - hurray! And the mediator is not needed, and time will be saved a little. But here you can expect another underwater stone: Amazon has dozens of warehouses and as many as six Fulfilment Centers throughout the US. Where exactly is your chosen product?



Well, if your orders are in one such center - in this case, they will be collected in one parcel and sent. Everything is simple here. And if in different? Then they will be collected in different parcels. Moreover, Amazon does everything automatically and, in fact, does not understand very well who is sending the parcel and where. He sees only the client and delivery address. Any product will arrive only tomorrow? Well, Amazon will not wait and today will send all that is, and tomorrow will collect another package.

And here we do not consider the situation when the goods are sold on Amazon, but physically located in stores or in the warehouses of sellers. In this case, expect them in one package certainly not worth it.

Of course, Amazon offers to group all purchases, but promises to do it only if it is available.



Here again Banderolk helps: agree, it's easier to wait for all the parcels in our warehouse, pack them in one and send them in a convenient way, rather than waiting for them all to get to you at different unpredictable times.

What to deliver?


Another question we partially answered above. Banderolka offers such delivery methods as USPS, Qwintry Air and EcoPost.

Amazon, as a rule, uses either USPS or commercial services - UPS, FedEx or DHL Express. The disadvantages in these cases are obvious: the packages are not packaged perfectly and in the case of USPS (and, consequently, Russian Post), there is a great chance to get at least a torn box, and at worst - a package without goods.

For commercial postal services, packaging is no longer so critical, but there is one unpleasant feature: when the cost of goods exceeds 200 euros, customs clearance is necessary. In general, there is nothing terrible here, but you have to be nervous and hurry up. An example of such a design has already been on the pages of Geektimes.
