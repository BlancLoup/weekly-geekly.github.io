Whatsapp not for sale

Recently there was news about the possible sale of WhatsApp messenger for one billion dollars to Google.



An employee of the AllThingsDigital online publication contacted WhatsApp business development manager Neeraj Arora, who denied the very fact of negotiating the sale of WhatsApp to Google.

Neeraj Arora declined to comment on this topic in more detail.

Earlier, in December 2012, there were messages about the purchase of WhatsApp by Facebook, which was later refuted.
