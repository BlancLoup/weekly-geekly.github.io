News from the world of MODX . New additions to MODX, interesting publications and video tutorials.

Add-ons for MODX Evolution

  • FormLister - Snippet for working with forms and users.
  • Jevix - Snippet for filtering and typography.
  • evoAutoFolder - Plugin for creating a tree of folders by id resources.

Add-ons for MODX Revolution

  • MinifyX 1.4.4-pl - Optimize JS and CSS. Munee Library Update, etc.
  • Foundation 6 1.0.0-beta2 - Template on Foundation.
  • virtuNewsletter 2.1.1-pl - News publishing system.
