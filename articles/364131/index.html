Of course, we are interested. And becoming interested - found information about the state program Startup Chile. This is a project to support startups from all over the world who receive grants from the Chilean government, and in exchange for this they live for six months and share their experience with the local IT environment. Thus, Chile strives to become the main technological hub of South America and create its own Silicon Valley.
