Instead of the preface

I made my first attempt to understand what was happening after the publication of the rules for registering domain names in the Cyrillic runet. My interest, idle and childish in its own way, increased after the publication of the so-called. "Stop list" for the zone of the Russian Federation. Butter on coals poured a recent post in the domainer habrablog.

I am aware of the fact that I am risking to turn my garden into a semblance of a Karesansui rock garden. But the number of completely incomprehensible, in my opinion, blunders allowed by Ru-Center when organizing the launch of Cyrillic domains, for my naive consciousness seemed simply monstrous. However, this can be said about any other monopoly that reigns in Russia. You will not have to go far for an example: I personally work in one of the divisions of the Russian Railways.
