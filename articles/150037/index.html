FBI for the first time arrested websites with pirated applications for Android

One large and two small sites involved in the distribution of pirated Android applications were closed during the first-of-its-kind operation of the FBI, the Department of Justice and a number of US government services and other countries.



Closed sites are:

  • applanet.net
  • snappzmarket.com
  • appbucket.net

According to PC World, FBI agents arrested three domains and received 9 search warrants. It is not yet clear whether the FBI and the Ministry of Justice will take any action against website owners or people associated with them.

Applanet, the largest of the seized sites, was represented as a repository of over 15,000 Android applications, according to Google's cache.
