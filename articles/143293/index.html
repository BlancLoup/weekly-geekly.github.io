PSN account


This is probably the easiest. You just need to create another account on the console and register it in PSN. Paypal account


If you already have one, skip this item. We do not carry out any financial frauds, so there are no special tips here - go to paypal.com , register as a law-abiding citizen, fasten your card, verify it.

CD with the US region


Probably, the first thing you will hear your own "ah", having reached this point (more precisely, having reached it still in the list).
