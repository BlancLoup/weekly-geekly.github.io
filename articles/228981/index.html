Fresh sections on the popularity of CMS-systems, meters / systems analytics and online consultants

Hi, Habr! We recently published results for the second quarter on our blog, and now Ruward: Track updated the sections for the third quarter of 2014. The study was based on an automatic crawler analysis of about 5 million domains of the .RU zone (according to typical service signatures).

Fresh cut on the popularity of boxed CMS





In this quarter, we significantly updated the signature database, and also added a number of new systems that could immediately get into the common top - OpenCart, Setup.ru, Wix and a number of others. This affected the redistribution of market shares (due to new players, many old people lost a little as a percentage of the market), but if you look at the absolute number of installations, then from the top three WordPress and Bitrix they added the number of implementations, and Joomla lost a little.

Distribution of popular meters and analytics systems





Yandex.Metrica is growing rapidly (if this pattern continues, it can take the first place in the next quarter), LI and Google Analytics reduce the share a little. There is a hypothesis that migration to Metric is connected with the closure by Yandex of data on search queries for all external systems.

Rating distribution of popular online consultants





Compared to April, the number of sites with established consultants increased by 0.3 points - exactly the same growth as consultants showed in the past period. This time Jivosite turned out to be the absolute growth leader, which grew by 1.66 points, taking away the share of all other participants.
