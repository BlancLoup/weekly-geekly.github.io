As I left the programmers and started making hydroponic installations. DIY non-stop

Hello! I am a programmer from Ekaterinburg. C #, ASP.NET. I have 6 years of development experience. But at some point I realized that this was not mine and decided to start creating hydroponic installations. "... Why? Naaa-naa-na ..." From Zemfira's song "Why"

Hydro ... what?


Actually what is hydroponics? This is a method of growing plants without using land. Plant roots can safely receive nutrients from the water into which these substances can be introduced. Next, the case for technology. You can spray this water on the roots, you can periodically flood them, it is possible that the water flows through the roots. Yes, a lot of things you can.

There are many advantages:

  • Hydroponic yields good yields
  • Lack of need for the earth and picking in it. Say no to dirt under your nails!
  • No pests
  • No weed
  • Fresh air in the apartment, the plants improve it
  • Mini eco-farm for children, as a guide for growing plants and respect for their own and other people's labor

I planted plants, periodically changing the nutrient solution, and that's it . Rejoice and crunch grown cucumbers and bourgeois strawberries from their beds all year round.


Hydroponator G-1000


The first installation came out beautiful, as in the watched video (for some reason I only had a photo of the flooded contour, but on the whole the installation looks like that). I even recorded my video. But it flowed from all the cracks. And speaking seriously, she could kill me with electricity. I smeared these crevices with my sealant, my fingers and everything they touched, but leaks appeared here and there. In the end, I decided that it was necessary to choose a different material, thanks to which the installation could be disassembled and assembled. With a sealant this would not work.
