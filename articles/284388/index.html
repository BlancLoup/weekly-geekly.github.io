What if you want money, but do not give?

I will share one story that happened to me recently, in the hope that someone from competent and respected habra people will share a bit of legal knowledge or simply support a kind word in a difficult life situation. A story in which money and deceived women are involved.

A lot of time has passed since one of the respected companies entered into an agreement with me on the provision of consulting services in the amount of 4 flights on broken-down planes and 32 hours of speaking to managers and engineers who want to absorb the art of optimizing business processes. I will not hide, the pleasure of communicating with people cut off from affectionately beloved work was worth the effort. However, several days of trimming pictures and correcting errors in eight presentations cost more: money.
