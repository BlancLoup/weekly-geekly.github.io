Hetzner lowered the price of installing servers


Today I went to Hetzner and saw that the prices for the installation of some of the servers had fallen. Previously, it was for the entire line of modern servers 149 euros. A little later, he began to save the auction, where the installation cost could be dropped to the cost of a month's rent. A reduction in prices for the installation, in my opinion, killed the need to rent a server through the hetzner auction.

Total we have 4 servers with a reduced installation price.

1) EX4 with new Intel i7-2600 proc, new 2 * 3TB disks (do not forget about the correct alignment when formatting such disks), 16GB of normal memory. Rental price of 49 euros, installation of 49 euros.
2) EX4S is the same only 32GB memory. Rental price of 59 euros, installation of 49 euros.
3) EX5 with old Intel i7-920, 24GB RAM, and two old 750GB screws. And here is the most delicious rental price of 59 euros, the installation price is 0 .
4) EX6 on Xeon e-1245 with 16GB ecc operatives. The rent price is 69 euros, the installation price is 69 euros.
