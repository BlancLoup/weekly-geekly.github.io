Sales of HTC Legend start in Russia!

April 1 at 0:00 start selling HTC Legend in a famous store. The price remains the same as previously announced 22 900 rubles.
Anyone who doubted and did not buy Hero, Legend for you!

HTC Legend has an aluminum case, it is equipped with a 600 MHz processor, a camera with a resolution of 5 megapixels, an AMOLED screen with a 3.2-inch diagonal. The operating system uses Android OS with support for multi-finger touch control, where a wide range of communications - Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, EDGE - will always stay on the forefront of technology.

By the way, the device will be on Android 2.1!
