Comparative scheme of ships from science fiction



DirkLoechel has finally finished work on the largest in the history of the comparative scheme of the spacecraft from science fiction works: feature films, TV series and games. Star Trek, Star Wars, EVE Online and many others (full list under the cut). All cult ships - they are here. Open the original image 4268 x 5690 pixels - and dive.

For each ship its fleet, ship class and length are indicated. On the scheme it is convenient to compare different models with each other.

1 pixel = 10 meters.

By the way, there is also the International Space Station, in the upper left corner, about two dreadnoughts of the Empire. To make it easier to find, the author placed the earth station in a yellow rectangle.

The author explains that, for aesthetic reasons, he placed on the poster only ships from 100 to 24000 meters in size, so the Death Star and some others did not fit. The Doctor's Tardis did not fit because it is both too small and too big at the same time.
