No. 1 storage at an affordable price


Aim: cost optimization and storage system creation

More than once, in the comments to our articles, they wrote that it is very difficult to find second-hand storage systems in the market. High demand for this kind of equipment is absolutely understandable, because the prices for new data storage systems start at 200,000 rubles. and higher. We were approached by our friends from St. Petersburg, who are engaged in hosting - BeGet with a request to find a solution for creating backups. We offered the 4U Huawei OcenStor and OceanSpace expansion shelves and 2 Supermicro 6016T-UF 2x Intel Xeon E5620 32GB servers. By connecting the shelf to the server via a controller or hba-card, we get a full storage system. Each shelf is designed for 24 disks, as much as possible you can install disks of 6TB (total 144TB). After successful testing, 8 more shelves and 8 servers were purchased. Previously, for these purposes, the customer purchased SuperStorage Server 6048R-E1CR24N.
