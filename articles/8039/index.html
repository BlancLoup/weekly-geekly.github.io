i Business card

A wonderful thing for those who meet every day with a lot of new people - a business card scanner . The software allows you to scan and recognize the text on a business card received from a business partner, colleague or friend to synchronize with the program scheduler on a PC or PDA. In addition to maintaining a local notebook, it is also possible to synchronize contacts on-line.

Price: 159.99 USD for CardScan Personal and 259.99 USD for CardScan Executive
