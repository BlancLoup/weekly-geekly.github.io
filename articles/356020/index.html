Internet Archive unlocked in Russia

Roskomnadzor deleted the resource from the registry of prohibited sites



Roskomnadzor removed Internet Archive from the list of banned sites. Now the site can be freely accessed with Russian IP, although there are exceptions: so far not all Russian users have access to Archive. Most likely, these exceptions are connected with the updating of the DNS protocols, and within a day all Internet users from the Russian Federation will be able to work with the specified site.

In the registry Internet Archive stayed for almost a year. Roskomnadzor blocked the resource in June 2015, when an entry on domain blocking and the IP address of web.archive.org (207.241.224.26) was entered in the Register of banned sites.
