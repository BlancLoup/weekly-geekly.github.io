Music that is always with you

There are waterproof speakers that are good only when a person basks in the water. If a person decides to swim, then no columns will help. Usually there are waterproof players for this. But the music lover usually already has a good player, and not everyone wants to buy another one. An interesting alternative is a device case called the H2O Interval Swim System .





This great system allows you to use your own iPod shuffle. The basis of the H2O Interval system is a waterproof case, which is fixed on the back of the swimming goggles (glasses not included). On the outside of the case there are buttons to control the player, so every time you want to switch the track, you don‚Äôt have to open the case. And of course, there is a pair of in-ear headphones, comfortable and streamlined, that do not jump out from the resistance of the aquatic environment during swimming.







The cost of the H2O Interval Swim System is $ 81 or $ 130, depending on the iPod shuffle model.
