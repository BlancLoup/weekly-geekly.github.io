Some technical details

As for the principle of Evapolar, it is based on the natural evaporation of water. Water evaporates, the temperature drops, everything is simple and strictly according to science. According to the authors, their development is one of the most effective in this area. Evaporative air conditioners are designed to cool and humidify hot dry air. If the humidity is above 70%, in this case the effectiveness of Evapolar drops. Since the humidity in most rooms does not exceed 60% (and in most cases much lower), Evapolar is suitable for the vast majority of apartments or offices.

The system can work as an ordinary fan, without water at all.



The weight of the device is 1.68 kg. The evaporation rate, which contributes to the cooling of the air, is adjustable by the user. The maximum temperature drop with Evapolar is 15 degrees Celsius.

Plus, the smallest droplets of water improve the microclimate of the room, they are useful for the skin and respiratory system. These droplets contribute to the purification of air from dust. There was no external smell during operation. Evapolar was not noticed.
