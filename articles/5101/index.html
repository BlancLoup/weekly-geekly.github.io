Moloko + Mag: in the future it will even be possible to lick

Graphic and photo beauty, interviews with interesting and creative people, music in its various manifestations. Moloko + Magazine - the so-called magazine, in which it all abounds. His first issue saw the light on the first day of spring; True, this light is only online so far, because the magazine is published in pdf format.

This issue focuses on experimental art and style mixing. The editorial staff selected the most interesting works of domestic and foreign designers, and in the ‚Äúapplication‚Äù you can find an mp3 mix - the same experimental and mixed.

Let's get acquainted with the editors of the magazine.

How are you?

Things are good, thank you :) We want summer!

First of all, a little about the team that created the magazine.

At the moment, our team consists of three people. This is Revaz , Sasha and Marina . Revaz and Sasha work together as designers in the creative agency Hodusbureau . And Marina, Marina ... well, she is just a beautiful girl student with knowledge of English :)



Why did the magazine appear at all? Is this an attempt to create a creative publication in digital format or a sight for something more extensive, printed and commercial?

Firstly, this is an attempt to create, more precisely, not an attempt, but a digital edition of a creative magazine in order to share with the readers the work of interesting people, to talk about them and show them in order to inspire someone to create something of their own, someone just enjoy leisure time looking at beautiful pictures and photos. And, secondly, of course, I would like to have such a journal in print, so that it can be looked through, lying on the couch in any position, so that it can be viewed in any other place, shown to friends ... And just so that it can be pat, tear if necessary, or lick.



When are you planning next release?

Oh, here we do not undertake to call the exact dates. Let's release the second number when we sleep. As for what is going to be there, for the time being, we can definitely not say either. But what will be very interesting, we promise it!



Who do you focus on when creating a magazine: more on specialists in this field or just curious?

We focus on those and others. But for specialists in this field, most likely, most of the works presented in the journal are known and not new. Although, we hope that they will learn something. And for the curious eye, we think, there will be a lot of interesting and previously unseen.



And who made the mix to the magazine?

Mix did Revaz. He mixed a musical hodgepodge of the best songs from albums from reviews selected by Marina and Sasha, so that it would be nice for readers to flip through the magazine.
