Google, Facebook and other large companies want to buy Twitter



For several days now, rumors have been circulating on the Web that the leadership of various large companies, including Facebook and Google, is in talks with Twitter's microblogging service. The subject of negotiations is the possible purchase of this service. Today The Wall Street Journal has published confirmation of these rumors, so that everything is more or less officially. Remember, you first wanted to buy Twitter for 500 million, then for a couple of billions, and then for 5-6 billion US dollars?
