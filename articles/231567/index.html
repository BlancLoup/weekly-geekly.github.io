Instagram presented Bolt: the fastest way to send a photo to a friend

At first, Instagram got the whole world hooked up with filter games and photo editing, and now they also offer users to send the captured footage as is and in one click. The photo / sharing mechanics are implemented very simply and beautifully: the user selects one of the 20 preset contacts, clicks on his icon - at this moment a picture is taken, which immediately leaves the selected addressee. If you hold your finger on the button, then instead of the photo, it will be photographed and the video will be sent. Total for all - exactly one click.


One click to make and send a photo or video - so much easier?

The function to cancel sending by shaking the phone protects the user's privacy from false positives. The general concept of photo exchange on the principle of "see what is happening right now" makes the application to Snapchat coherent - at least, Western publications perceive Bolt as an answer to this application .

Android | iOS - as long as Bolt is available to a limited number of regions. To register in it, email and registration in other services are not required - the application is tied to the user's phone number. Accordingly, 20 contacts available for presetting should also be in his notebook.
