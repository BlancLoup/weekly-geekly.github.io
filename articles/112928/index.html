Speak To Tweet Service Launched To Help People In Egypt



After the announcement of the closure of the last Egyptian provider, the news that a small team of engineers from Twitter, Google and SayNow (the company acquired by Google last week) developed the service Speak To Tweet was very relevant. It allows you to tweet using only voice connection.

Everyone can use the service by simply sending a voice message to one of these international telephone numbers: +16504194196 or +390662207294 or +97316199855. The service will immediately publish a link to the message with the hashtag #egypt. People can listen to messages by dialing the same phone numbers or by visiting @ speak2tweet .

The developers hope that the service will somehow be able to help people in Egypt stay connected during this very difficult time.
