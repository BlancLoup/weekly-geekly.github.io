Can I get to the top of search engines without financial costs?

Once I wondered if it was realistic to get to the first place of issue in Google / Yandex by any popular search query.
Since I myself am a big fan of the Fallout game, it was decided to create a fan site for this game and dedicate it to reviews of the future of Fallout: New Vegas, and existing additions to Fallout-3.



One of the conditions for the creation of such a site was to spend on all no more than $ 0, or euros, or any other units, in general, do not incur any costs.

Therefore, I will need: free hosting, free domain name, free CMS, design and layout of the template for it (I wanted a unique design), free file storage for downloadable content, preferably one that would not require the site user to buy premium accounts or in some other way to spend money on downloading files, and content, thanks to which the site could get to the top search engines.

I solved these tasks as they arise :)
