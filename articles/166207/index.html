Unified Communication Center - Imo.im


I do not belong to the company that created the service, I do not work there and do not promote them. I just found a convenient service for me and decided to share my impressions with you.



ICQ


I have long since abandoned ICQ, for a couple of years now. Almost all friends can be contacted via Skype, or by mail. But recently it was necessary to communicate with a person who lives only in ICQ. I didn‚Äôt want to set up a ‚Äúfat‚Äù client, and I began to look for a more or less convenient web client.

Customers


First of all, of course, got into "Web-ICQ - the new ICQ2go!". What can I say - this is a classic of clients from ICQ (now mail.ru). This is a bunch of ads, lags in the interface, an attempt to show the web service as a desktop application. Some may like it, but I started looking further.

I dug up almost all the links of Google issue. Some services did not support Russian, through some I basically could not log into the account. MegaFon UMS is generally just something ...
I ended up finding IMO.IM.


Imo.im main window with loaded dialog

Imo.im


In short - a pleasant, developing service for convenient communication. Allows you to connect to a single account a bunch of services:
  • imo (own service account)
  • MSN
  • Skype
  • Yahoo
  • Google talk
  • Facebook
  • Aim
  • ICQ
  • Jabber
  • VKontakte
  • Steam


Add / Manage Accounts window

Service advantages


  • There is no advertising (it should be, but I did not notice it. Even with adblock turned off). Something ran through the left panel, but I closed it and did not find how to open it again :)
  • Very smart interface. There are no lags at all.
  • The interface is actually convenient. The window is not trying to get out of the browser.
  • Integration in one contact list from several social networks / services.
  • There are groups, block sheets
  • Full Russian support in communication
  • Almost complete interface Russification (78% at the time of writing)
  • There are sound alerts / pop-up alerts
  • There is an iOS client.

Also, thanks @AMarkin
  • the ability to quickly share content (voice notes, photos, locations, videos) with those users who have a client
  • the ability to make calls



IOS Client

Minuses


  • No metacontacts. If 1 person is in 4 networks - you will have 4 contacts in the list.
  • Minor flaws (in the iOS client, instead of avatars from social networks, your avatar is pulled up), some of the strings are not Russified.
  • Something else is sure to come out, but for now, everything suits me.

Customers

You can use the service. Many available services, nice interfaces, Russian support and a good mobile client appeal to this service. The lack of advertising adds benefits to the service.
