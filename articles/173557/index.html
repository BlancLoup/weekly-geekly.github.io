HTC Butterfly - beauty at your fingertips

More recently, the abbreviation Full HD caused associations only with modern TVs and monitors for working with graphics. 1920 pixels fit perfectly on 22-27 inches of computer displays, and they felt quite comfortable on the impressive diagonals of home screens. It is all the more surprising to see the same figures relating to the compact display of a mobile device.

Such an impressive resolution came to smartphones quite recently, and the number of models present in the market can be counted on the fingers of one hand. However, one of them has outstripped competitors and is already on sale. As you can guess, we are talking about one of our new products - HTC Butterfly - the first phone with a Full HD-display, which went on sale in the territory of the Russian Federation.




Due to the fact that the interaction with the modern smartphone is mainly done with the help of taps on the screen, it is not difficult to explain such a serious attention to this component. However, the other parameters of the device do not allow him to hit the face in the dirt.



The company's engineers used the updated Qualcomm Snapgragon S4 Pro APQ8064 SoC, supplemented by the Adreno 320 graphics accelerator, to show your favorite games and the necessary applications without delay. HTC Butterfly shows impressive results both in synthetic tests and in actual use, let alone work interface, characteristic for devices on Android previous generations, you can safely forget.
