CES results: new Samsung monitors at the international consumer electronics exhibition

Good evening, Habr!

As part of summing up the international consumer electronics exhibition CES 2013, which was held January 8-11, in the city of Las Vegas, USA, today we will tell about the new monitors that were presented at the Samsung booth.



Touch SC770 monitor of the 7th series

For the first time in the history of the Visual Display Business division, the company expanded the line of monitors with new premium touch screen models.

The 7-inch 24-inch touch monitor SC770 is optimized for Windows 8 and supports 10-finger capacitive multitouch, which will allow you to drag, rewind, select, scale, rotate and carry out the entire set of touch manipulations.



The screen has a widescreen Full HD resolution (1920x1080) and is equipped with MVA-technology, which, in addition to short response time (5 ms), gives a high image contrast (5000: 1) and wide viewing angles (178 degrees). The monitor is also equipped with an HDMI connector. Monitor stand provides screen tilt up to 60 degrees.

Ergonomic monitor SC750 7th series


The main feature of the 7-inch 27-inch SC750 monitor is the ability to rotate the screen 90 degrees, which allows you to work comfortably in the vertical (portrait) mode.


The screen has the same high resolution of 1080p and carries a WVA LCD-matrix with LED-backlit, which gives at least wide viewing angles (178 degrees) and a static contrast ratio of 5000: 1. An HDMI connector is also available. Ergonomic cylindrical stand made of metal.

Professional monitor SB970 9th series


The SB970 9-series 27-inch monitor uses a PLS-matrix and a WQHD screen resolution, which provides four times more detail than traditional HD-resolution. In addition, the integrated color calibration feature allows users to adjust the uniformity, gamma and white balance on the display. This process is carried out using our own technology Samsung Natural Color Expert, through which you can get as close to reality as possible on the monitor screen.


A pleasant moment is the fact that every single monitor of this model, before being sent to you, will undergo the process of manual adjustment of the image by Samsung specialists. The minimalist design of the SB970 monitor has already managed to collect top awards in the design industry, including IDEA Bronze Award 2012 and the Japanese Good Design Award.

New monitors of the 7th and 9th series will be available in Russia in the first quarter of 2013. Recommended prices will be announced later.
