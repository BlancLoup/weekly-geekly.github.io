UA Web Challenge 2012 Report



Last weekend (April 28-29), the final event, called UA Web Challenge, took place in Kiev.

The event is held in the format of the championship and the main goal is to combine cool, promising developers with the best IT companies on the market; help IT professionals get a new useful experience.

This year we held the event for the second time ( announcement of the qualifying round in the events in Habr√© ) and I must say that this time everything that was intended by the organizers was possible (or almost everything;))

All-Ukrainian Championship and held in 2 rounds:

  • online stage (up to 1000 participants)
  • the final part in Kiev (76 winners of the online tour, but this year they took more, because not everyone arrives, as a rule)

In the final, we selected (with the help of our jury ) 71 participants and 5 teams (teams could consist of a maximum of 3 people).

Participants of the final represented 21 cities of Ukraine. At the same time 10 girls got to the final (more than 10% (!) Of the total number of finalists).

What is important for participants - nonresidents (who registered before the specified date for the championship), we settled in hotels, here with such numbers (last year there were hostels):

The venue for the finals was kindly provided by our partner, Ciklum . And we took advantage of this opportunity in full - we occupied as many as 3 floors (18, 19 and 20) :)

The event itself was divided into 2 days, the first day the participants decided the tasks, and the second was the presentations from the partners, the jury spoke, the awards and afterparty took place;)

The first day

After the introductory word from the organizers and partners, the participants received tasks and went on their own flows to solve them.

Time for decisions was allotted 6 hours . Someone coped in advance, someone sat to the last (some tasks were really difficult to do in 6 hours, try for example to create such a layout during this time).

Second day

The Suddey team worked half the night on the decisions of the participants, while one of them, Yuri Artyukh, had a birthday before, and Gennady Osipenko just flew in from a foreign trip. But this did not prevent them from suing their nominations brilliantly.

The participants gathered in the morning (there was a transfer from the hotels to Ciklum) and the day began with the presentations of our partners - each participant could learn more about each company, about open vacancies, prospects, etc.

Those who were not interested in the work (many participants came to test their strength, talk with colleagues in arms, etc.) - talked on the sidelines among themselves, played XBox, Playstation 3, table football or simply took pictures in a Darth Vader mask:



After that, the jury spoke, talked about common mistakes in the qualifying round and final, drew the attention of participants on what to emphasize when solving assignments, in the work and in what direction to develop.

Many participants in the questionnaire on the past event expressed that it was very useful and interesting.
