Feedback of the PVS-Studio team on the C ++ Russia conference, 2015


Two and a half hours on the train is a great time to write thanks to conference organizer Sergey Platonov (@sermp) and his team for finally having a C ++ conference in Russia! Sergey's merits are truly impressive, given that he did not have the budgets of Microsoft and Intel. However, it turned out that the main thing is not the budget, but the desire and enthusiasm. Therefore, the speakers collected by Sergey were beyond all praise, foreign guests were extremely sociable and kind. And at the stands of JetBrains, IncrediBuild, Kaspersky and PVS-Studio gathered not talking heads, but professionals, with whom it was interesting to talk to guests and neighbors on the stand. Our personal impressions are under the cut.



So, the conference was held on February 27-28, 2015 at the Izmailovo-Alpha Hotel. We (Evgeniy Ryzhkov EvgeniyRyzhkov and Andrey Karpov andrey2008 ) decided to raise the mood of the conference participants, having distributed small chocolates in the morning.

By the way, if you saw that someone was going not with one or two chocolates, but with a whole bunch, then know. These are our clients of PVS-Studio, so they were entitled to additional bonuses :-).
