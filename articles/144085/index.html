The Chinese broke into the mini-computer market



Raspberry Pi and Cotton Candy have a Chinese rival . A computer the size of a USB flash drive costs $ 74 - just halfway between the Raspberry Pi ($ 25- $ 35) and Cotton Candy ($ 200). Inside is a single-core ARM Cortex A8 AllWinner A10 1.5GHz processor, a Mali 400 graphics chip, 512 megabytes of DDR3 RAM and 4 gigabytes of internal flash memory. There is a full-size USB slot, microUSB, a micro TF card reader, an HDMI port, and an 802.11b / g wireless WiFi module. The dimensions of the "Chinese" - 8.8 x 3.5 x 1.2 cm, weight - 200 grams.

The computer is running Android 4.0 and is able to play video in Full HD. The main differences from Cotton Candy's more expensive competitor are a weaker single-core CPU and 512 megabytes of memory instead of a gigabyte. In addition, there is no bluetooth, and the HDMI connector of the "mother" type does not allow plugging the computer directly into the TV or monitor - you will have to use a cable.

In addition to the noname version for $ 74, you can order the version under the brand zero-devices . Judging by the photos and specifications, this is the same device. The version with the logo costs $ 2 more.
