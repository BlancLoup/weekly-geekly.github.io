The results of the Android event



The main thing that was said and shown at the last event :

Market
- Android Market is now available from any browser on the market.android.com , and directly from the computer, you can log in, via the cloud, install the software on your smartphone or tablet.
- The programs now have official internal payments. Disney has already integrated their support into its application, although the SDK was provided to it only five days ago.
- On Android now, too, will Tap Tap Revenge :)

Honeycomb and tablets
- Program interfaces will consist of separate blocks, developers will be able to reorder the blocks between portrait and landscape modes (and, it seems, use the same blocks on tablets and smartphones).
- Everything got better with 2D and 3D graphics. Showed a couple of 3D games.
- New notifications with images (similar to Ubuntu and Makovsky Growl), new widgets, new interfaces for standard applications (camera, music, etc.)
- To demonstrate the capabilities of the platform, CNN showed its application with streaming video and a button "to shoot what is happening around and put it on the CNN website."
