Concepts of technology and gadgets of the early 2000s



In early January 2015, reading this publication here about the smart pen, I remembered that I had read about such a thing many years ago in the magazine Around the World. This gave me the idea that really a lot of new things are forgotten and rethought old.

It so happened that I actively read these magazines in the early 2000s, and there among the descriptions of different countries, cities, historical events and other material was the most interesting for me heading "Technoparad", where it was told about modern (at that time) gadgets, new revolutionary devices, concepts. I asked permission from "Around the World" and after a positive response, I decided to share this interesting material with readers.

The images were scanned by me from magazines, the text of the notes is in a revised form, all rights belong to the magazine "Around the World" .

Solar cell phone



What to do if the phone is dead? You just need to put it in the sun. This makes sense if it is equipped with a battery from Sunpower Systems. It is alleged that to fully charge enough five hours in the sun. Similar batteries were issued for all mass phones.
Source: Journal for May 2001, p.40.

Smart House




If an e-mail message came from the washing machine - it means that the future has already come and your home is equipped with an Ariston Digital system. This system connects all appliances in the apartment, and it does not require any additional connections - the connection between them is provided by wiring, and with the outside world - by the Internet and GSM. Each household appliance diagnoses itself and reports any problems to the central Ariston service center. If it is possible to eliminate the failure remotely, then instructions will be sent to the device via e-mail (patch, firmware) and the device will correct its work. If the failure was not resolved, the owner arrives by fax, wired or cell phone, or by email. The smart home control panel is a touchscreen monitor through which a person interacts with the Leon @ rdo control system.
Source: Journal for July 2001, p.96.

Ancestor of smartwatch?




On the image - Siemens Wristphone, an ordinary GSM cell phone, which can also act as a watch and be mounted on the arm. Concept weight - 36 grams.

As far as I was able to figure out, this ancestor of the SmartWatch did not hit the mass market, remaining a beautiful concept.
Source: Journal for July 2001, p.97.
