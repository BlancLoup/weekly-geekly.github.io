Lenovo started selling smartphone in Russia, which can be used as an external battery

Russia launches sales of a two-inch, five-inch Lenovo P70 LTE-smartphone with a 4000 mAh battery. The smartphone can work up to 34 days in standby mode in 2G networks, and the adapter comes with it for use as an external battery for other gadgets.



The smartphone is built on the basis of an eight-core MediaTek 1.7 GHz processor, equipped with 16 GB of internal memory and 2 GB of RAM. Video accelerator Mali-T760-MP2 700 MHz is responsible for the video. Memory can be expanded by microSD. The declared operating time of the P70 with an average load will be two to three days. Autonomous work in standby mode - 34 days. IPS-display has a diagonal of 5 inches and a resolution of 1280x720 pixels. Camera resolution is 13-megapixel and 5-megapixel. Weight - 149 grams, thickness - 8.9 mm.

The device works under the operating system Android 4.4 KitKat. The claimed battery life is achieved through high capacity and a special power saving mode.

The cost of the device will be 19990 rubles.
