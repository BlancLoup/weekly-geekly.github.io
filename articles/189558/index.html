Xbox One: unboxing



Despite the fact that Xbox One will be available only in November, Microsoft decided to hold an additional presentation, with the device being unpacked. The presentation was not made at a press conference, this video is posted recently on the Web. The video shows the equipment and appearance of the device, plus some additional information about the device.

And yes, the Xbox One Day One version is being unpacked. The kit, as you can understand, includes the console itself, Kinect, a game controller. There is also an Xbox One Chat Headset. And the standard version, and Day One have one set of headsets.

It is worth noting that the Xbox One has a separate power supply, HDMI cable that supports 4K, 1080P, and 3D. The same cable will be sold separately, in the US - for $ 5.

The controller can be charged from the micro-USB cable and use AA batteries / rechargeable batteries. If you play with a controller connected via a cable, the wireless module automatically turns off. This allows you to play without a power source in the controller.

Microsoft confirmed that Xbox One is compatible with A, B, G, N Wi-Fi, and supports frequencies of both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz.

This equipment will be available in Canada, Australia, USA and some European countries, starting in November, as mentioned above.
