Corel has released an image editor, AfterShot Pro, similar in functionality to Lightroom, with a version for Linux.



Finally, IT giants are starting to pay attention to such a platform as Linux. Corel released an image editor for the three main platforms, created on the basis of the previously purchased Bible Pro editor.


AfterShot Pro promises the following functionality:

Flexible photo management

Quick review

Easily compare, filter and select the best photos from any number of similar shots.

Reliable search tools



Search for the right photo in a collection of hundreds or thousands of others very quickly with the ability to filter metadata. Add your own keywords, ratings, tags and tags with the ability to search by them. Or search for data that the camera recorded in exif, such as date, camera type, aperture settings, and more.

Advanced non destructive editing

Powerful Correction Tools

Whether you shoot in RAW or JPEG, you can change your photos with a variety of correction tools, including cropping, straightening, straightening, overexposed areas (highlight recovery), noise removal, color correction, and much more.

Multi-versioned editing



It is easy to make changes to several versions of your photo for a virtually unlimited number of ways to experiment with correction options and customizable presets, including black and white, cross processing, and more.

Selective editing



Apply adjustments, exactly where you want, in specific layers and areas, precisely isolating those areas of your photo that you want to change.

Correction and cloning tools



Remove unwanted items from photos with fix and clone tools. Use the patch for small items such as dust or stains. Select cloning to retouch large areas that can be closed with a copy of an object from the adjacent image area.

Perfectly Clear Technology



Quickly optimize your images with Perfectly Clear, award-winning technology from Athentech Technologies Inc., automatically adjusts the lighting for each pixel in the frame, while maintaining true color and zero quality loss. It also eliminates the abnormal shade and restores faded photos.

Noise ninja

Reduce noise while maintaining image detail, with Noise Ninja by PictureCode, Inc. You will get natural looking artifact-free photos with this fantastic technology.

Supported Formats

RAW in the version of the most common photo manufacturers: ARW, CR2, CRW, DCR, DNG, MRW, NEF, NRW, ORF, PEF, RAW, RW2, RWL, SR2
JPEG, TIF
