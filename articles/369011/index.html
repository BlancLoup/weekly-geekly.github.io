List of research reports on robotics for 2015



The number of research reports covering the robotics industry has quadrupled over the past three years. We have reviewed many reports for 2015. The reports that we have chosen for you cover a wide range of segments, as well as the industry as a whole.

These reports are costly, but often represent value only to venture capitalists, companies and their technical and marketing research departments, as well as academia. Many reports overlap, and their forecasts often differ. But the conclusion is the same: they all predict positive growth for most segments of the robotics industry.

Note: Reports, which you can find below, are summarized in 2015.

IN THE SCALES OF INDUSTRY


Global Robotics Market 2015-2019 ,

August 26, 2015, 94 pages, TechNavio .
Technavio believes that the global robotics market will grow at a constant rate of 26% from the average annual growth rate until 2019. The adoption of industrial robots in developing countries is expected to stimulate the development of this market during the forecast period, with huge demands from the automotive and electrical industries, as well as the food and beverage sector in developing countries. This report is largely focused on the industrial sector and lowers the defense and space industries, as well as the rapidly developing services sector.


Global market for robotics ,

May 2015, Allied Market Research.
This report is segmented by types of robots, components, applications and geography. The types of robots include industrial, service and mobile development; components include hardware, software and services; Market applications include healthcare, defense and security, aerospace industry, automotive, electronics, household and other. Forecast charts are drawn up for the period from 2014 to 2025.

SEGMENTS IN THE FRAMEWORK OF INDUSTRY


Patent analysis of medical robotics ,

May 12, 2015, 21 pages, Market Intelligence & Consulting Institute.
Covers key sectors and areas, the top 30 patent assignees and various patent development strategies. Reprogrammable bots, such as diagnostic systems, are also included in the report. Virtual robots like IBM's Watson and some other research systems blur the line between what a robot is.


Smart robots (virtual and physical) ,

July 2015, 143 pages, Markets and Markets.
By providing a definition of autonomous robotic systems, this report analyzes interaction services, consumer and specialized services, as well as reprogrammable robots, predicting a rapid growth of 19.22% of the average annual growth rate in 2020. The list of companies under consideration include Amazon, Google, Honda Motor Co. and 15 others.


Medical Robotics Market ,

April 2015, 180 pages, IndustryARC.
The report speaks of the drivers and growth restrictions, as well as industry growth is projected at 12% from the average annual growth rate until 2020. Companies such as Hansen Medical, Intuitive Surgical, Mazor Robotics, Accuray, Titan Medican, Renishaw, Ekso Bionics, Varian Medical Systems, Stereotaxis and 15 others are being considered.


Household robot vacuum cleaner industry in the USA ,

August 7, 2015, 255 pages, QYResearch.
Provides an overview of the industry, including development trends, competitive landscape analysis and regional growth, but only within the United States. The report covers the following suppliers: LG Electronics, IROBOT, Samsung, Neato Robotics, Philips, Dyson, Panasonic, ECOVACS, Electrolux and 20 others.


Uninhabited underwater vehicles ,

September 16, 2015, 65 pages, Technavio .
Considers stimulating factors and difficulties in the context of market growth, provides profiles of companies such as Fugro, Kongsberg Maritime, Oceaneering, SAAB, Teledyne, Atlas Elekronik, Bluefin Robotic, Boston Engineering and Palmarii Dynamics, and also predicts market growth by an average of 20.65 % until 2019.


Uninhabited Submersibles II ,

July 2015, 185 pages, IndustryARC.
This report with the same name as TechNavio above predicts 11% of the average annual growth rate until 2020 and includes profiles of some other suppliers (along with those already mentioned in the previous report), including Deep Trekkers, Forum Energy Technologies, Subsea7, FMC Schilling Robotics, ECA Robotics and 25 others.


Robotics in the electronic and electrical industry ,

June 2015, 86 villages, TechNavio.
It covers the current state and growth prospects of the global market for industrial robots in the electronic and electrical industries in the period 2015-2019, and also provides an industry outlook for growth by an average of 5.43% until 2019.


The opportunities of the global market of unmanned aerial vehicles / drones ,

June 2015, 126 pages, BIS Research.
This report examines various aspects of the unmanned aerial vehicles market: its development factors, threats that can slow growth and the opportunities that shape its expansion. The report predicts that the market will increase by almost 70% (to $ 11.95 billion) by 2020 with an average annual growth rate of 8.41% for the period from 2015 to 2020.


Agricultural robots market ,

August 26, 2015, 85 pages, TechNavio.
The title of the report is not entirely correct, as it includes only the care of livestock, the feeding of cattle and the preservation of wildlife. However, the report predicts an increase of 11% to 2019 for these limited areas. In the context of these subsystems of the agricultural industry, the following companies are considered in the report: DeLaval, Lely, Yamaha, BouMatic Robotics, Fullwood Automation and several others.


Gearbox market for industrial robotics ,

June 27, 2014, reissued in August 2015, 58 pages, Research in China.
This is an important category. One third of the robot components are represented in this market, which was captured by Japanese manufacturers Nabtesco, Harmonic Drive and Sumitomo. China is taking a step toward producing these necessary components domestically, and therefore is interested in its new producers. Shaanxi Qinchuan Machinery Development Co., Ltd., Nantong Zhenkang Machinery Co., Ltd. and Shuaike Machinery Manufacturing Co., Ltd. The report provides a forecast of a doubling of Chinese production by 2016.
