Visual Studio 2010 contest for Habrahabr!

In connection with the upcoming event - the global launch of Visual Studio 2010 and the .NET 4.0 platform, which will be held on April 12, Habrahabr opens a special page and launches a mega-contest for developers.

Namely: send to Habr with the help of a special page an article reviewing Visual Studio 2010 and win Visual Studio 2010 Ultimate with a MSDN subscription for a year! And by the way, the cost of such a prize is many hundreds of dollars.





Under the cut are some of the details of the competition and links to resources.


The contest will last about a month. In order to participate in the contest, you need to install the pre-final release of Visual Studio 2010 RC, the link to which is available on the contest page . Then you share your experience using Visual Studio 2010 in your own article and send it to a special Habr page . Further, readers choose the best articles and evaluate them, which leads to the selection of the best article, the author of which will be designated the winner.

The winner will receive the maximum edition of Visual Studio 2010 Ultimate with a MSDN subscription, which will provide free access to the main Microsoft software and other bonuses.

Hurry up, although the product is new, many have already felt it and are ready to write at least a dozen articles. In order to become a winner, you need to create really interesting material.



You can also go to the description of different versions of Visual Studio 2010 and read about their features.



In addition, on April 12 you can watch the broadcast of the event for the launch of Visual Studio 2010 in Moscow. And on April 14 and 16, similar events will be held in St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg respectively. So, many of the developers who got involved in a promo code for these events will be able to participate in the launch of a new studio.
