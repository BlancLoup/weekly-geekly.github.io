Motorola introduced the DROID RAZR

So, today, Motorola introduced the android-smartphone from the RAZR series - DROID RAZR with a body that has a Kevlar coating (7.1 mm thickness).






The device is equipped with a 4.3-inch Super AMOLED display with qHD resolution and a protective coating of Corning Gorilla Glass, 1 GB of RAM. The camera has the following characteristics: 8 MP with support for Full HD video.

TX Motorola DROID RAZR:
Platform: Android 2.3.5
Processor: TI OMAP 4430 1.2 GHz dual-core, PowerVR SGX540 video accelerator
Display: 960 x 540 pixels Super AMOLED Advanced
Main camera: 8 MP with LED backlight (FullHD video), LED
Front camera: 1.3 MP
Memory: 1 GB of RAM, 16 GB of ROM
MicroSD support (16GB already in the box)
Communications: Wi-Fi IEEE802.11 b / g / n, Bluetooth v4.0 LE + EDR, 3.5 mm audio jack, GPS (They still write that GLONASS is :))
Battery: 1780 mA / h (12.5 hours talk time)
Communication standards: WCDMA 850/2100/1900/900, GSM 900/850/1900/1800, HSDPA 14.4 Mbps (Category 10), EDGE Class 12
Dimensions: 130.768.97.1 mm, 127 g.

Motorola DROID RAZR is compatible with some branded accessories Motorola Lapdock 500 Pro and Lapdock 100.
