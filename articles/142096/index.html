A very quick and effective way to relax your eyes.

So, one evening I had a great idea that I have been using for 4 years to maintain my vision.
I promise you will feel the effect right away!

Causes of blurred vision



There are 2 theories why a person's eyesight deteriorates. In my opinion and in my experience both of them have a place

The lens of the eye and ciliary muscles

To begin, take a look at why vision deteriorates:
Myopia - corrected with a biconcave (reducing) lens.


Farsightedness - corrected with magnifying lenses


Briefly about the causes: loss of elasticity of the lens, spasm or "failure" of the ciliary muscle - a muscle that changes the curvature of the lens.

Supporting muscles of the eye: lateral, medial and other

Pictured right eye

I will not describe in detail how and what the eye muscles work - it can be easily understood by looking at the picture. I want to say only one thing: due to the uneven load on the muscles, some of them spasm, and either change the shape of the eyeball (and even then, during normal operation of the lens and ciliary muscles, the same picture is obtained as in the images above) or simply ‚Äúlead the eye to the side‚Äù - when you look at the monitor screen, both eyes look at one closely spaced point, and therefore only that muscle (medial) works, which turns / slashes the eye closer to the nose bridge. When you look into the distance, both eyes look exactly straight. So when you try to look into the distance after a long work at the computer, your eyes can not look exactly right because of a spasm of the medial muscle.

Method itself


I don‚Äôt know if someone could have done something like this before me, but this is my idea, never looked at anything. It looks simple: a plate with pairs of balls, a figure is written on each of the balls.

How to use

Download and print the plate . There are 3 files in the archive. One table is immediately ready for printing (table_done.doc), in another (table.doc) - a macro, by changing the parameters of which you can change the appearance of the table for specific needs (hereinafter - more) and the third (table_done.pdf) - pdf (very convenient use on tablet). Print and hang the table on the wall at eye level. Stand at a distance of about 30 cm from her and try to combine pairs of balls into one ball. In other words, each eye should look at a separate ball. The principle is the same as in such pictures . As soon as you managed to combine the top pair into one ball - go down below. So try to go down as low as possible. With each new step down, relax the muscles of the lens, making the image blurry as ‚Äúin gauss‚Äù (to achieve this effect, look as if through a sheet into the distance), but not disconnected.
So go up and go down a few times. Recently, I have been using this tablet on my tablet - very convenient and always at hand. The only thing - I turn down the screen brightness to a minimum.

Some clarifications and notes

There is a famous exercise of changing the focus of view when you look at a point on a window, and then you look at a point somewhere far away. Here is the principle, BUT! If you descend to the lowest pair of balls, your eyes will literally separate to the sides, relaxing the medial, and training the lateral muscle. In addition, because of this force-relaxation, the ciliary muscle of the lens also reflexively relaxes.

About the macro and its parameters. I assumed that if the balls would increase or decrease with each new step, it would be more effective for short-sighted or far-sighted people, but I failed to test my hypothesis - there are no people willing to study regularly. Try it! It may be possible to fix the damaged eyesight. It will not be worse for sure (I checked it on myself for 3 years - I never wore glasses, my vision is excellent, I sit at the computer for hours for days )

And finally, additional quick blinking in a minute, and circular eye movements will also be helpful. Everything!
