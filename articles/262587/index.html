Report on the last meeting of Defcon Moscow



Recently, our office hosted the ninth meeting of information security specialists Defcon Moscow. There were many people willing to attend the meeting, but not everyone had such an opportunity, so we recorded all the performances and shared these videos with you.

Sergey Vishnyakov , coordinator of the DC7499 group, opened the meeting with his story about future plans for the development of the Defcon conference.



Then Timur Yunusov spoke on the intricacies of social engineering as part of penetration testing. Timur specializes in in-depth analysis of the security of web applications and related services, as well as research in the field of information security.



Timur's presentation on Slideshare: www.slideshare.net/MailRuGroup/ss-50379047

The next report was presented by Oleg Kupreev aka 090h, a leading researcher at Digital Security, a company providing consulting services in the field of information security. Oleg's report is devoted to vulnerabilities in telecommunications software.



Oleg's presentation on Slideshare: www.slideshare.net/MailRuGroup/ss-50379378

Mikhail Elizarov aka SapIt shared information on how to conduct a MITM attack based on the vulnerabilities found in network equipment.



Michael's presentation on Slideshare: www.slideshare.net/MailRuGroup/man-in-the-middle-50379661

And at the end of the meeting, Defcon's special guest, Ivan Novikov, aka d0znpp, founder and head of the Wallarm startup, who develops systems to protect websites from hacker attacks through vulnerabilities in web applications, spoke. Ivan is also known as the whitehat hacker.
