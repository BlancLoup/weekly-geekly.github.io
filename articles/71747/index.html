ESET partners in the same boat

Sea air, sun and sailing are wonderful components of not only a successful vacation, but business. Under this motto in the middle of September at the height of the velvet season in Turkey, the partner regatta ESET was held. During this time, ESET employees and partners learned to knit knots, stand at the helm, walk the ladder in the dark and drink tequilla, licking sea salt from the back of a friend .

Under the flags of ESET from the port of the Turkish city of Marmaris, 6 yachts and one judge catamaran, much more comfortable and stable, but certainly not as romantic as a yacht, came out.




referee catamaran

However, the difficulties of marine life came to taste and to the partners and employees of ESET. In the end, driving a yacht is no more difficult than business. However, the first two races had to be mastered and learn not to fall overboard.


We select those who are overboard

Also had to learn the course in rolls. The lower the mast leans towards the sea, the faster the yacht goes. In the photo below the guys go at approximately 6-8 knots. At such a moment, inside the cabin, all the dishes fly out of the open cabinets and crash against the wall. So the habit of closing the boxes developed at the beginning yachtsman pretty soon.


Roll roll



The last crucial race, the most difficult and dynamic, took place in the bay of Marmaris. It finally decided on the leader - the yacht Emirald with Andrei Albitov, head of the Russian representative office of ESET, on board. The crew is confident that the solidarity of the team and, of course, the location of Neptune played a decisive role in the victory. Before the start of the race, the Emiralda team decided to observe the old maritime tradition - pouring rum or cognac into the sea.
