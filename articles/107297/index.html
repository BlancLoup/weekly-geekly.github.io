Asimo is 10 years old


Today, October 31, Honda celebrates the 10th anniversary of ASIMO (short for Advanced Step in Innovative MObility), its most advanced humanoid robot in the world. According to the unofficial version, ASIMO got its name in honor of Isaac Asimov, the famous author of the Three Laws of Robotics. The Japanese company is currently focusing its research on developing the intelligence of ASIMO robots, as well as exploring the possibilities of interaction between humans and humanoid robots.
