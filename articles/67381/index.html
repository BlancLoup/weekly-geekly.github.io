JS strategy that can be played online

A little about the game

It is made in pure javascript, without libraries and flash. Works in Opera 9 and up, ff 3. For IE7, there are still problems
This is of course beta, I wrote it alone, and I will be glad if someone is interested in continuing what they have begun.

http://yarax.ru/lab/evol2/create.php

After someone creates a server, and someone joins him, both players end up on the playing field in different random places. In the presence of one of the peasants, whose actions can be viewed by clicking on it. Each player has an enemy always dark orange. With new buildings, new opportunities appear.



The task, as elsewhere, is to develop faster, create an army and defeat an opponent =)
Tip: use ctrl + wheel to work with the scale, initially it is as large as possible. As well as F11 for playing in full screen mode.

Buildings in order of availability:
Settlement
mill
iron mine
gold mine
forge
military camp
academy
Units:
peasants (born from the settlement)
warriors (from military camp)

There are shoals (and possibly others) in large-scale battles due to lack of synchronization. I will be glad to hear ideas about their elimination. As well as help in hyphas cliparts.
