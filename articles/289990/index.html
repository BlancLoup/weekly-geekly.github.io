Dropbox is testing Notes

Many users of ProductHunt yesterday noticed an invitation to register with the beta service Composer for Dropbox.
A little later, the company itself has updated the page of the new cloud storage function .



So far, there is no information about Dropbox Notes, as a co-creation and text editing service. In addition to the screenshot of the new service, showing the basic functionality, Dropbox does not provide any details.



A year ago, Dropbox absorbed the company HackPad, whose service, most likely, we will soon see in a modified form. In addition, it is easy to assume that Dropbox is interested in the corporate chat market (HipChat, Slack) - not so long ago, the company made a purchase in the form of Zulip , which is engaged in the development of such a solution.
