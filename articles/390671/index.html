Screen in a tube or waiting for the release of a bending smartphone from LG

LG plans to launch the production of smartphones that will bend at any angle, as well as curl into a tube. Helps to achieve this technology P-OLED based on organic LEDs. The display is made entirely of polymeric materials, which made it possible to bring their bending radius to 30 mm.



Matrix LG screens have a brightness of 75 lumens, with a color rendering index in excess of 85 units.

Another LG achievement is display transparency. If the usual LCD-panel has a transparency of about 10%, here LG took the bar at almost 40%.



In the case of a flexible smartphone, alloy strips will be used with the effect of memorizing the shape sensitive to temperature changes. This material converts thermal energy into mechanical energy, due to which the phone can transform.



For example, if you receive a text message, the smartphone bends forward, and the more unread messages, the stronger the curvature will be. About the missed call, the smartphone will notify, bending one of the corners. All functions can be customized.
Also according to the patent application, when tilting the hand with the phone, the body bends so as to shift the center of gravity and prevent the gadget from falling.



The battery will be used as the sun and in the form of a thin film, located on the back of the smartphone.

We expect that the release of LG flexible gadgets will not be limited. The chairman of the company said that after the implementation of their plans, the world will change for the better. This will be the first step to creating multimedia coatings (windows, walls).
