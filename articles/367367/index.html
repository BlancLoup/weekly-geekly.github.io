Free 3D printing of Witcher 3 models in Euroset

Not a month passed since the end of the test drives of the Prism Mini and Prism Uni home 3D printers, as the company began to receive numerous requests for re-testing of new products for those who have not had time to get to know them.
3DQuality always goes to meet its customers, and therefore invites everyone to a test drive of home 3D printers from June 13 to 17, which will be held in Moscow at the Euroset store in the Semenovsky shopping center.

On these dates, from 12 to 19 o'clock, 3DQuality specialists will demonstrate the work of the already well-known Prism Mini and Prism Uni, printing for free to the visitors of the store and everyone such products as a universal pen for the home craftsman, children's toys, baking tins, women's jewelry, key chains and models from the game The Witcher 3 part. Visitors will be able to take the printed models with them.



And of course, visitors will be able to test their skills in drawing volumetric objects with 3D pen Myriwell for free.
During the test drive, when buying 3D Prism Mini and Prism Uni printers, there is a bonus - 4 PLA plastic coils as a gift, or free delivery to the nearest transport company terminal, to choose from. The promotion applies to all regions of Russia.
See you on a test drive!
Information about the location of the action can be found on www.semenovsky.ru
