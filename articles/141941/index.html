Withdrawing Yandex.Money to Visa cards in new countries

Remember, in February, we talked about the possibility of transferring Yandex.Money to any MasterCard cards of our foreign users from several countries? From today, this service is open for Visa cards of any banks that operate in Ukraine, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Moldova, Uzbekistan and Tajikistan. The required amount can be sent both to your card and to the card of another person. It is enough to know her number. Payment will be credited in one to two business days.

You can read about how this service works on the Yandex.Money website .
