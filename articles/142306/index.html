Ya.Subbotnik in Kiev

May 5, 2012 in Kiev will be held the traditional May J. Subbotnik.

Employees of the Moscow, Kiev and Simferopol offices of Yandex will talk about technologies, solutions and useful practices that may be of interest to developers.

Come, listen, ask questions and chat on professional topics over a cup of coffee.

On our J. Subbotnik you will find out:
  • About assessors of Yandex;
  • Pro designers in search;
  • About the Ukrainian interface and API 2.0 Yandex.Maps;
  • About using git;
  • About using jsLint;
  • What is the modern maker-up machine?

In addition, you will find a separate section about BEM, namely:
  • How BEM can create universal applications;
  • bem server: frontend build system;
  • i-bem.js: JavaScript in BEM terms;
  • How to create websites for iOS / Android on BEM.

Bonus:
  • Opening of J. Subbotnik by Bobuk;
  • The story about the development in Ukraine Vitaly Kharisov.

The program of J.Subbotnik in Kiev can be viewed here .
