Harvard CS50 course in Russian: lecture twenty-one



We are introducing the 21st (of 24) lecture at the legendary Harvard CS50 course, which we translate and voice specifically for JavaRush.

In this series of the series, Professor Skazelatti from Yale University talks about natural language processing systems. The lecture discusses speech recognition algorithms, reveals the concept of statistical models of speech recognition, hidden Markov models, question-answer system, semantic text processing, and also touches upon the creation of non-verbal communication systems.
