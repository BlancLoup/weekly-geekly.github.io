The second version of Gmail for iOS has been released, now with support for multiple accounts


Good news for users of Gmail on iOS devices - Google has just released a global update of its Gmail application for iOS.

The new version of the application has a completely redesigned interface and many improvements, such as pictures in messages, animations and endless scrolling in the list of messages. You may also notice improvements in auto-aggregate and integration with Google Calendar and Google+.


Finally, the company added support for multiple accounts - now, like in the Android application, you can add up to five Google accounts and switch between them in a couple of touches.



ITunes Link: itunes.apple.com/app/gmail/id422689480?mt=8

UPD: Judging by Twitter and comments, when you try to install an update, a message appears like "The item you tried to buy is no longer available." However, the habrip user Professore shared a recipe that worked: you need to find it by searching among Google applications and there already click Update .
