The 25 billionth download Apple App will receive a gift of $ 10,000



Apple launched a countdown to the 25 billionth download of apps from the App Store. But this is not the most important news.

The lucky man, besides going down in history, will also receive a gift certificate for $ 10,000. The trick is that these $ 10,000 can only be spent on the App Store. Just think about all those games and utilities without ads.

The most important thing is that you do not need to buy anything. And it is not strange, do not even need to upload. You just need to fill out the form to subscribe to the tote. You can send up to 25 applications per day to one name.

The winner must subscribe to publicity, namely the use of his or her name, photo and list of selected applications in the Apple Community. Yes, it means that everyone will know what you spent won $ 10,000. More details on the official website .
