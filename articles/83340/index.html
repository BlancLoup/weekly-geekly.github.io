Overview of trading robots

Hi, habra people! We all know about Forex, maybe even one of you has tried (or is still trying to) play to earn a living, beer, telephone, car, startup (underline the necessary). But in our life to devote all the time to trading in the foreign exchange market, it is practically unreal and as a result, automated trading experts appeared.
In this post I will talk about some paid advisors that I bought and used on a real account. All graphs and figures are taken from personal experience and in no way belong to the developers of advisers.
So it is proposed to choose a "red" or "blue" pill :)



Foreword



Most brokers use MetaTrader4 as software (by the way, the 5th version with OOP elements is already being actively introduced and tested). It has a built-in API called MQL. Actually, it did not take much time for the first program to appear that would trade on the forex market according to a given strategy (by the way, even the annual international competition of such robots is held). And so the current day on the Internet is full of such programs here, but they differ in price, quality, availability very much. Take the average prices and good "reports".
So I decided to share my modest experience in using these systems. Let's stop first on some developments of our compatriots.
