Speakers' Corner with Anton Utkin - High-speed design and prototyping of projects and products 30.09

More and more product development and start-ups appear in recent years in Ukraine, our developers have already proved that the software Made in Ukraine successfully competes with the whole world. One result of this trend has been a keen interest in UX issues and professional interface design.

Guest Ciklum Speakers' Corner, which will be held in Kiev on September 30, Anton Utkin is co-founder of Thalient Interfaces (Moscow, Russia). He designs interfaces for Intel, works on his own startup and helps other fellow startups. He gives dinner parties, interns with famous Moscow chefs, makes science fiction films and has a regular futurological column in Prime Russian magazine.


But that's not all: Anton teaches UX-strategies at the British Higher School of Design, constantly conducts master classes, including for companies known all over the world, just recently opened a series of lectures and master classes with the first performance in Dream Industries (Moscow ).

The lecture within Ciklum Speakers' Corner is addressed to a wide range of creative specialties: designers, developers, copywriters and journalists - to everyone who is faced with the need to structure a huge amount of information every day and quickly achieve results. We will analyze how the design and prototyping of texts, design, industrial design and interfaces work, and then we will try to come up with several synthetic projects and analyze different problem cases.

Do not miss a unique opportunity: bring your projects at any stage of development for the advice and recommendation of a professional.

When: September 30, 2013
Time: 18: 30-20: 00
Location: Ciklum office, Kiev, st. Amosova 12, BC Horizon, second tower, 20th floor

Participation is free with prior registration: goo.gl/7lou7L
