Lázaro Campos became a member of the Payoneer Advisory Committee

Lázaro Campos, the former CEO of SWIFT, joined the Payoneer Advisory Committee. Mr. Campos has worked at SWIFT for 25 years, including five of them as CEO. Under his leadership, the company increased its profit by almost two times. He will work with the management of Payoneer to develop new services and improve existing services.


His vast experience in international payments and business is unique, and the cooperation with Campos will contribute to the growth and development of Payoneer.



Mr. Kampos joins Payoneer at a very important time for us. Payoneer recently completed a fourth round of $ 25 million in venture financing from Susquehanna Growth Equity, LLC and was chosen by Google as a payment solution for Google Trusted Stores.
