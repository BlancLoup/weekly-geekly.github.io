No More Woof: dog-to-human translator

The No More Woof project, which is being developed by the Nordic Society for Invention and Discovery, organized a fundraiser for Indiegogo - and 47 days before the end of the campaign, he already received one and a half times more money than he wanted. No More Woof is the first device in the world that should adequately translate the thoughts of dogs into human speech.



The whole structure looks like a complex dog headset, but in reality it is an EEG-removal device, a computer for processing (Raspberry PI) and a speaker. While the device can recognize only a few distinct thoughts of dogs - such as fatigue, hunger or curiosity.
