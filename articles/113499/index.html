There is a bit more work here. But once it is done, and then the scheme will be debugged. The main thing is to choose a good bank.

So, the sequence of actions for Ivan:
  1. Choose a bank and open a currency account.

    You must first contact the bank, to warn about the purpose of the visit.
    For a hike you need to have at least a passport with you, an TIN, possibly a certificate of IP registration. In general, it is better to clarify the list of documents in the bank. It is also necessary to find out the amount of the fee for opening an account.

    If you have all the documents, then opening an account will take about 2 days. You must not forget to notify the tax account about opening a currency account. The application can be there and take.

    In the bank, Ivan will open 2 accounts: transit and current. The transit account will receive money from the customer. Therefore, in the bank details you need to specify it. And then, according to currency legislation, you need to transfer money to your current account within a week, or sell it on a currency exchange.

  2. Conclude an agreement with the customer and, if necessary, issue a passport transaction.

    The contract can be issued in Russian. For example, ask the customer for a facsimile signature and seal and impose them on the contract. And to explain to the customer what is written there, and why it is needed.

    Or to draw up a contract in two columns: in Russian and in English (sometimes customers who work with Russia already have such documents). If the signature is in a foreign language, it will have to be notarized.

    The contract must clearly indicate:
    • what to do, point by point;
    • payment currency, payment schedule, conditions (advances, postpay, 50 to 50);
    • how the work will be accepted (how long will the acts be issued);
    • details of the parties.
    If the amount of the contract is more than 5,000 cu, then it will be necessary for the contract to issue a passport transaction. Often this service can be provided free of charge or at an additional cost at the bank where the currency account is opened.

    The contract and passport can be one, even if there are several payments. But it is better to close each payment step by step by act - it will be easier to take into account in taxes.

  3. Deal with the currency that entered the transit account.

    The customer transferred money to Ivan for the sold photos. Now within 7 days you need to issue a certificate of foreign exchange transactions (the bank can also help with it for the first time, and then you can copy-paste;).

    Next, you need to either sell the currency to the bank and transfer the money received to a ruble card or to a current account, or transfer it to a current currency account in order to use it later.

  4. Do not forget to close the deal.

    It is necessary to obtain from the customer an act of work performed in Russian or both languages, and provide a copy of it to the bank along with a certificate. Well, for the tax is useful.

  5. Correctly calculate the tax.

    The fact is that when we deal with currency, it is necessary to take into account the rates of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. They can be taken on the website of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation or in your bank.

    The IFTS and the Ministry of Finance consider that the exchange difference, if any, should be taken into account separately from the principal amount of income. When can it occur?

    A. There is a date when the customer transferred the money to the transit account - this is the date the income was received. We translate into rubles at the rate of the Central Bank and we get income.

    B. There is the date of the act, that is, the date of transfer of ownership of the result of the transaction. Or there is the last date of the month. At this date, you need to recalculate the money received, using the rate for that day.

    Calculate the difference BA. If it is positive, then it must be separately recorded in income as exchange rate difference. Date of income - date B. If negative, then Ivan will not take it into account, since he has a tax system of "income, 6%," and writes down only the amount A with date A.

    A natural way to avoid these calculations is to indicate the date of receipt of money for the transit account by the date of the act.
In general, the action is not very much. And with a good bank, work will be easier.
