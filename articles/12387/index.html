Apple's iRing ring will manage players and phones.


Apple has expanded the list of manufactured accessories to one of its most famous products - the iPod player, as well as to its own mobile phones, releasing an original and stylish ring that allows you to control the playback of audio tracks stored in devices from a distance.

The ring, called iRing, also allows you to control the volume level. Controlled by Bluetooth wireless technology. The ring is equipped with a special touch-sensitive panel with a bright OLED display of status. To access the functions Skip, Pause and Play, you need to touch the icon displayed on the small screen in the form of an Apple logo, and to increase or decrease the volume, simply drag your finger across the sensitive surface in the right direction.

The developers have taken care of the convenience of wearing a ring on the finger, providing its inner surface with two rubber gaskets. The device is powered by a rechargeable battery, which provides up to two days of continuous operation of the device.

According to Ferra.ru, iRing comes with a miniature cradle for recharging the battery and is offered in white or black in the style of an iPod or iPhone.
