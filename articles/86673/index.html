Osu! - music game


Since many people liked the arkady story about worms, then I will tell you about the wonderful game osu !, which is installed in my games folder besides worms and stepmania.
Osu! - a musical game, the essence of which is to hover the target with the mouse to the music and pressing the mouse button or keyboard. In Osu! there are three types of games: Osu !, Taiko and Catch the beat. In the type of Taiko player must press the buttons to the beat of music without ignorance, and in catch the beat you need to catch all sorts of falling fruits man.


I rarely play taiko and even less often in catch the beat, so I'll tell you about the main type of game.
The game has three elements: the usual bells, spinners and sliders. At the top there is a health-bar, if you miss a lot of elements then the game ends, in general, everything is as usual.
The game is incredibly convenient in terms of usability: the tracks are downloaded from a single directory and imported without restarting the game, you can watch the rating both on the site and in the game, watch other replays and comment on them. The game has a built-in track editor. There is a chat and rooms (made on the basis of irc), there is a room #russian.
There are also modifiers, some add complexity (hard rock, hidden, flashlight), some reduce (easy mode, no fail), some utility (no video, autopilot)

What is there to tell, you just look at this video, played with the easy mode modifier!
