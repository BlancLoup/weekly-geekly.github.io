GeekBrains invites beginner designers to an educational game.


Educational portal GeekBrains invites you to participate in the game ‚ÄúOne Day in the Life of a Designer‚Äù, which will be held on March 24 at the Moscow office of Mail.ru Group. For six hours, participants, having broken into teams, will be able to immerse themselves in the work of the designer, acquire basic skills and create a prototype of the site. The game is suitable for beginners or interested in design. Details under the cut.


The whole day at the event will be held educational tracks, during which experienced designers will talk about creating a design concept, developing UI, prototypes and texts, trends in design and how to present their ideas. The speakers will be curators of design courses at GeekBrains: art director of Mail.ru Group Artem Fenelonov, senior designer of Mail.ru Group Arthur Gromadin, lead designer of Invitro Ilya Polyansky and others.

The authors of the best works await prizes. Members of the winning team will be able to study for free at GeekBrains. The award for the second place - graphic tablets. Teams ranked from third to fifth will receive a 50% discount on all design courses in GeekBrains.
