Habr and Microsoft Imagine Cup 2009 final

Probably, many have already heard that from July 3-8, Cairo will host the finals of the world student technology cup Microsoft Imagine Cup , which will be attended by 149 teams. Among them in the final will be our guys: the Vital Lab team from Nizhny Novgorod and the Moscow SoundStreamers team.

The program of the final includes several disciplines : software projects, embedded solutions, games, robotics and algorithms, IT-competition, mashaps, photo-stories, short films, design. Within each of these categories, participants will compete for three prizes. By the way, the prize fund this year is $ 288 thousand, and for first place in some disciplines $ 25 thousand are waiting for the winners - a good extra charge for the scholarship.

By the way, besides the Russian one team from Belarus takes part in the final - BigBang , one from Kazakhstan - Ferrum Logic , and as many as three Ukrainian teams - Baby Guards , Intellectronics and Just 4 fuN . So all of us will be for whom to support the next few days.

So, together with them, I will go to Egypt, too, to closely follow their movement to victory and to report from the event. And it promises to be very interesting. All the most curious things that will happen at the finals of the Microsoft Imagine Cup 2009, I will publish:
