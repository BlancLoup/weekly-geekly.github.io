This article is not about how to protect your computer, but about how to reduce street crime. Below is a project that, if implemented, will increase the security of each of us individually and society as a whole. In addition, it can change for the better and society itself.

Emergency service


The main task and the unique function of the Service is to give a person who is in danger of a criminal nature an opportunity to instantly attract the attention of others by sending an SOS signal. Such an opportunity will naturally increase safety because:
  1. The number of witnesses to any incident will increase dramatically. Increased crime detection. Crime will decrease.
  2. Potential criminals will quickly realize that it has become much more difficult to commit a crime and go unnoticed. They are aware of the increased risks. This will reduce crime.
  3. In many cases, each of us will be able to avoid the harsh effects of an attack simply because the perpetrator, seeing the people who have appeared nearby, will stop the criminal activities and try to escape.
  4. People will start helping each other in emergency situations, and this will change their attitude towards each other and will begin to change the very atmosphere in society from fear to trust.

I would like to once again draw your attention to these four points. They are very simple, but fundamental. It is these mechanisms that represent the core of the Service, on which all additional functionality will be strung.

It is obvious that the SOS signal sent by a person should get not only to others, but also to special services (police, Emergency Situations Ministry, ambulance, etc.) and, if desired, a person, his relatives. All this should happen immediately, because the second signal, not to mention phone calls, may simply not be enough time.
