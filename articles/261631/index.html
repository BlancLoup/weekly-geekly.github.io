Ciklum Kiev invites to Speakers' Corner dedicated to the development for the Apple Watch

On Thursday, July 2 , Ciklum Kiev invites to Speakers' Corner , dedicated to the development for the Apple Watch .



Alexander Pazhin , UI Designer of Ciklum Interactive Solutions, will help to understand the principles of the Apple Watch and the capabilities of widgets. Also, Alexander will share how to prepare materials for further development. The following topics will be discussed during the event:

1. Apple Watch. How do they work?
2. Application structure
3. WatchKit for watchOS 2
4. Widget features
5. Top Case Studies
The event is free, by prior registration .
