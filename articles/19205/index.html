Readius Flexible Screen Device - Technical Details

The Polymer Vision website has details on the long-awaited device with a flexible screen - Readius ( promised at the end of last year ). The device will be presented to the general public in February at the GSMA Mobile World Congress in Barcelona. Readius is expected to go on sale in the middle of this year (nothing is yet known about the price).



Functionality

In addition to the ability to read e-books, Readius functionality will allow you to listen to audiobooks and music (a large number of formats is supported), read RSS news and view e-mail (pop3, imap, attachments), and also use it as a mobile phone (you can call via the contact list which is synchronized with the PC).

Technical details
Weight: 115 gr
Screen: 5 "16 grayscale
Dimensions (folded): 115 x 57 x 21 mm
Battery: 30 hours of continuous reading
Connectivity: Tri Band / 3G HSDPA, USB 2.0, Bluetooth 2.0, ActiveSync
CPU: ARM11 400MHz
Memory Cards: High Capacity Micro SD (up to 8GB)
Control Interface: 8 buttons (SimpleTouch Buttons)

Content

Content will be available through a single Readius portal. Content owners will be provided with tools for publishing content on the portal. What content will be available does not say: "Polymer Vision is in the negotiations with the largest producers of content."
