What information is needed?


Perhaps this is the most important question that should be answered in full.

Any form is: Elements of direct interaction (constrols) with the user and accompanying background information. Let's derive the following rules for ourselves:
  • Form must be indicated. Title, description, explanation. The user, even out of the corner of his eye looking at the form, must immediately understand what he will have to face. This rule is.
  • Legend should be clear. Use concepts within the same language in which the entire site is written, or within the chosen localization.

  • Help users. Think of the easiest ways to fill out forms for users. Remember, saving time of visitors directly increases the loyalty of visitors in the future.
  • Do not overdo it. Do not forget that there is always the opportunity to get rid of any elements in favor of simplifying the form.
