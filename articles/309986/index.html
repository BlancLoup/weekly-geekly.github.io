Reaching goals: 9 life hacking from SmartProgress

1. Write down the purpose


If the goal exists only in the head, then consider that it does not exist.

‚Ä¢ In psychology, the concept of exteriorization is the transfer of internal processes (thoughts, plans, goals) to the outside in oral or written format. Writing a goal on SmartProgress , we just carry out this transfer and the goal takes on real form. Yes, and the brain begins to perceive something recorded as already quite real, prompting them to go to the gym, save money, pump over their skills.

‚Ä¢ Memory and consciousness are not dimensionless. And if you write the goal on paper, then release a certain amount of memory, attention and thinking that you already use to plan actions. 2. Check the target by SMART criteria


This is the English abbreviation of SMART, meaning "smart." Each letter is a criterion for the effectiveness of goals.

‚Ä¢ Specific
The more specifics, the higher the probability of achieving the goal. Determine the desired result as accurately as possible, for example, "Drop 15 kilograms in one year." Avoid inaccuracies and double interpretations.

‚Ä¢ Measurable (measurable)

‚Ä¢ Achievable (achievable)
If you can find resources to achieve the goal, then the goal is achievable. Resources are knowledge, skills, useful contacts, information, time, money. Starting to achieve the goal, analyze what you already have, what you need and where you take it.

‚Ä¢ Relevant (current)
Formulate a goal on SmartProgress and its environmental friendliness (relevance). In a special field to fill in, write why and why you want to achieve exactly this result. Environmental friendliness is external and internal.

External environmental friendliness is the correspondence of your goal to the other goals of your life. For example, you want to breed British cats, but you are allergic to cat fur. In this case, the second goal, which makes it impossible to fulfill the first one, can subconsciously sound like this: ‚ÄúAvoid cats in your apartment.‚Äù Internal environmental friendliness - is the compliance of the goal with your beliefs, values, lifestyle. Are you ready to accept the changes? Will you be comfortable? Are you afraid of possible changes? ‚Ä¢ Time bound (time limited)

After that, the timeline will always be displayed in the goal header, reminding you of commitments and deadlines.

Checking goals by SMART-criteria, you summarize the available information, set adequate deadlines, determine the availability and amount of resources, set clear and specific objectives for yourself or partners.
