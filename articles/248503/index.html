JetBrains products at last year's prices

We are constantly asked: "When will there be discounts?". We answer: "There are discounts!" .

Until February 5, ALL JetBrains products can be purchased with a new license or upgrade to an existing one with a 40% discount from the current price.

This time the action is not international, but only for residents of three countries: Russia, Ukraine and Belarus. We understand that currency rates have greatly shaken and changed prices in recent months, and we hope that our action will somehow compensate for this and allow you not to save on performance and code quality.

How to use the discount?
To purchase or update a personal license you need to use a discount code. If you want to make a purchase on the company, there are two options: contact one of our resellers or directly to our sales department. All the details are on a special page .

A small request: do not make a stir, do not promise the end of the world, there will be enough licenses for everyone, especially since two weeks is a decent time :)
