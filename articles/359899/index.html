News and Releases


  • PhpStorm 2018.2 EAP - PhpStorm early access program has been launched , thanks to which you can use new versions of IDE for free. Among the innovations: support for touch bar, improved autocompletion for neuralspaces, structural search and replacement, simplified installation of CodeSniffer / MessDetector, and other improvements.
