Return of the reinforcement legend: Etymotic ER4SR, ER4XR headphones

There is no such audiophile that the American company Etymotic would not have known. Not only because it is called the ‚Äúreinforcement legend‚Äù at every step (although everyone has flaws), but also during its long history. We will tell you more about everything right now, but today we are gathered primarily to see the second coming of the legend in the face of the ER4SR and ER4XR models - modifications of ER4.



The leader and innovator in the field of sound prostheses, Etymotic in the early 90s released its line of in-ear headphones Etymotic Research ER4. Later, the company began to expand the model range, releasing ER4RB and a special version for traveling (ER4PT), but it became widely known thanks to two models: ER4P and ER4S. The latter are recognized even by picky experts as the best in-channel reinforcement headphones in history with almost linear frequency response and incredible accuracy. One reinforcing emitter and a decent sound source (this is important) can literally give the standard of music reproduction. Those who have ever listened to these headphones will not let them lie.

But there were reasons for her. Did Etymotic reincarnation succeed, and was it worth it for the manufacturer to raise a hand to the holy in general? Let's try to answer this question.



In appearance, ER4XR (Extended Response) and ER4SR (Studio Reference) headphones are no different, except that the inscriptions on the boxes are different. Moreover, the main characteristics of them are also the same - the resistance is 45 Ohms and the sensitivity is 98 dB. For comparison, the old ER4P and ER4S had a resistance of 27 ohms and 100 ohms, and the sensitivity was 102 dB and 90 dB, respectively. In other words, the manufacturer chose average values: compatibility improved with reduced resistance, and ER4S didn‚Äôt really have enough sensitivity.

Appearance and innovations


First of all, it is worth noting the use, as it is now fashionable, of anodized aluminum, thanks to which both models look much more spectacular (and in many respects also because of the inscriptions using laser engraving), the manufacturer used black plastic earlier. At the same time, the headphones almost did not change from the ER4S, Etymotic obviously wanted to keep the concept of their headphones, which was once warmly received by audiophiles around the world.



Significant changes have affected the cable, which has become more subtle, soft and elastic and less susceptible to the microphone effect. Although it remained replaceable, but now it has not its own branded connectors, but standard widely used mmcx. This solution will obviously please the owners of steep, high-quality cables, and the rest will easily be able to replace the wire in a couple of movements if such a desire arises. In addition, the cable sits in the socket is much more reliable, due to the presence of the holder, which prevents the rotation of the plug. It may seem to someone that is too long, but if you use the clip from the kit, the friction of the wire on the clothes does not affect the sensations from listening.





Owners of the previous model will surely remember how the protrusion around the cable plug did not allow to connect headphones to some models of players and mobile phones. In the new versions, the manufacturer listened to its users: the cable is truly universal, there is a ‚Äúslider‚Äù for fixing the headphones in place, and the clip began to look more solid.

We still have to verify this, but looking ahead we say that it took about 2-3 days to get used to it. Much actually depends on the ear structure, which is individual.

Sound




Paired with Astell & Kern AK 70 and ER4SR, and ER4XR proved to be very good, although in general they can be shaken from a more budget player. If you insert the earphones as deeply as possible, in both cases an excellent noise isolation is achieved, so literally you are left alone with the music. In the subway music is heard perfectly, and if you are surrounded by several workers with jackhammers, the situation is unlikely to change.



It is best to start right away with the most interesting - the differences. ER4SR produces a smooth, but at the same time dynamic sound, the legendary accuracy of the ER4 can really be traced in this reincarnation of the model, as well as the characteristic monitorability. The frequency response of this version is smoother than that of the ER4XR, there is a slight rise after 2,000 kHz. Distortion if there is, then already at the very top, only few will be able to catch it.



But ER4XR seemed to be created specifically for those who were unhappy with the lack of bass in previous models. In this version of the bass abound, the fullness of low frequencies is carried out by a smooth transition, and not just the usual rise on bottoms. The frequency response is less even, the owners claim that the XR is slightly larger than the SR, but in order to recognize them, you need to have a really sharp ear. It is impossible to buy blindly for sure: you need to listen to each model in advance and find out which one will be more to your liking. Here, by the way, measurements on XR and SR separately.
