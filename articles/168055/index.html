Announced the first smartphones based on Clover Trail and Lexington

After the release of the first phones based on the x86 platform - Medfield - in the fall of 2012, and their debut on the world market, not much time passed. But already this January, new models of phones with x86 architecture were announced - on new platforms Lexington and Clover Trail .



Accordingly, these solutions are a budget solution - Lexington (a budget version similar to SoC Medfield) and a solution for the mid-top segment based on the dual-core Clover Trail , according to all announcements and forecasts that exceed the power consumption and performance of the Nvidia Tegra3 based solution and can compete with promising SoC.

Lexington





At the moment, announced several devices:

  • Lava Xolo X500 - phone for the Indian market for about $ 170
  • Yolo - the first phone for the African market on the x86 platform for about $ 115
  • Acer Liquid C1 - will go on sale in February on the Thai market for about $ 335


All solutions are very, very low cost, while with very good performance. Models for the US and America have not yet been announced, but most likely, as in the case of Medfield, they will appear with a slight delay in the markets of Europe and Russia.
