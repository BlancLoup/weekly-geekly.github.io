Trolling as a tool for community management

Fighting is a sign of life

Now in this regard, nothing has changed. Communication on the Internet has moved from chat rooms to social networks and forums. And conflicts are still the generator of the much-needed and desired phenomenon. And the struggle in the community is a sign of his life, his pulse.

From theory to practice: why is it necessary to troll yourself


I worked in community management for two companies - in IT Territory in 2006 and more recently in Game Insight. And I did it according to the strategy described above. The main plus - no one can harm you more than you do. In addition, you and your battle trolls are not only content generators for the community, they are also an extra line of defense when unwanted users come to visit.

Remember: the user can always tell the community what the administration representative can never allow himself to say. And imagine what will happen if the community begins to promote the troll in the moderators or in the number of representatives of their interests? This I once achieved. What could be more useful than the phenomenon when people want to make an administrator under the cover of their confidant?

From theory to practice: why not troll yourself is dangerous


If you do not troll your own resource and your users, someone else does it. If this happens is organized, then everything can end very badly.

In my practice there was an indicative episode. In 2008, together with one friend, we joined forces and organized opposition to the administration of a certain project. The goal is to save the community from malpractice employees. The measures that were taken against us and our numerous supporters are bans and moderation of posts (an attempt to hide the problem). Countermeasures on our part - the creation of an independent resource, which published all the evidence of abuse collected. As a result, our activity was the last straw and overflowed the cup of patience top management. After the dismissal of the head of the support service, our campaign achieved its goals.

Now imagine what damage negative figures and opinion leaders can do and what risks to reputation they can pose if you, of course, do not control them. In this case, we fought for a just cause. But for the image of the company and a lie, and the truth can pose a comparable danger if this information is not presented to the community in the way you need it. Therefore, everything could be different if the place of the key negative figure was occupied by its own person.
