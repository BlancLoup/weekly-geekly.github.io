Less is better, but better: airFiber 5X is a new generation of radio channels, or Lutz * for your radio route is now in retail

Let's see what they offer us.



Until today, Ubiquiti has, in essence, two competing equipment lines for building long-range point-to-point long-haul spans: airMAX and airFiber. AirFiber 5 / 5u had an unsurpassed capacity (up to 600 Mbit / s duplex), better real sensitivity, many times higher batch performance. AirMAX, on the other hand, was a solution at times lower cost and, more importantly, with higher gain antennas - however, these solutions have limited packet performance (about 18 thousand packets per second in normal and about 70 thousand packets per second in AC -variant).

Also, AirFiber 5 / 5U within the Russian Federation had a certain problem with the legality of use: in these bands, we are allowed to use only broadband TDD with one frequency band up to 40 MHz inclusive. Thus, it was legally possible to get only 450 Mbit / s simplex from airFiber, which is not so interesting, especially considering the cost of airFiber (about $ 2,000 per span).
Thus, a problem arose, aptly described in Kin-dza-dza ‚ÄúLuts are not sold in parts‚Äù, with the subsequent pushing of a gravitsapa over long distances by building a main canal on airMAX.
Today we meet a novelty - a system that combines the best of two lines: airFiber 5X (AF-5X (EU)).



The case of the device resembles an elongated Rocket case and can be installed on the corresponding antenna, the back wall of the device is a needle radiator.



Cable configuration is familiar with airFiber - a gigabit port of traffic transfer and a control port of 100 Mbps.

Please note that airFiber X can be operated with airMAX parabolic antennas - that is, it can be used to upgrade existing PtP spans built on the equipment of the airMAX line. For new installations, however, specialized -45 antennas are recommended. Unlike airMAX antennas, polarizations of new antennas are tilted (slant), which provides a smaller variation in signal levels on polarizations in the case of signal reflections and / or partial violation of radio visibility and also some isolation from equipment operating in H and V polarizations (the manufacturer introduced antenna gain 24, 30, 34 dBi).

Incomplete list of benefits

Summary


  • The performance of airFiber X in performance significantly exceeds airMAX, including airMAX AC;
  • In actual practice, the use of airFiber 5 in most cases does not have advantages relative to airFiber X;
  • The possibility of using external antennas, including the airMAX line, allows building airFiber X channels with a length of over 100 km with high performance and modernizing previously deployed airMAX channels;
  • The disadvantage of airFiber X is 24 volt power supply, not 802.3at, which, when using third-party switches, can make power management from the switch impossible (the high consumption of the device also attracts attention - it exceeds the capabilities of TOUGHswitch \ EdgeSwitch, forcing to use the complete power injector).
