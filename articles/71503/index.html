Adobe + nVidia or hardware accelerated flash-video by the GPU



Hi habrovchane!

Today, October 5, the embargo on the publication of this information is lifted. At the nVidia press briefing, among other things, the alpha version of Adobe Flash Player 10.1, which differs from the 10th version, was supported by CUDA as one of the key chips. In particular, it will be nice to fans of Youtube and their HD-content.



In the process of demonstrating the capabilities of hp mini 311, video playback from Youtube-HD in 720p format was demonstrated. All this was competently flavored with a large amount of statistical data, according to which it turns out that people watch videos on the network even more often than they use their e-mail. The only good news is that social networks and information search still hold leading positions.



According to comrades from nVidia, today should be published the news about the release of the public beta version of Adobe Flash Player 10.1. I do not know about the news, but you can search for the player on the Adobe Labs page . There is no download link yet, but there is complete information about the upcoming version.
