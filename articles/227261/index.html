The government is ready to pay for Russian studies in foreign universities

The Russians, who independently entered leading foreign universities, will be able to attend them at state expense. According to Izvestia, last Friday, Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev signed a decree.

First, the applicant must already have a bachelor's degree. That is, younger students do not enter the program. Secondly, the student will be required to work after graduation in a Russian company for at least 3 years. Otherwise, he will be obliged to pay a fine of three times the amount that was spent on his training.

The program is designed for 2014‚Äì2016, during which time it is planned to train at least 1.5 thousand Russians at state expense.
