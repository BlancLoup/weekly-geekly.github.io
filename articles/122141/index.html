Watermarks on now.ru

After I learned about the appearance of the cinema now.ru, I wondered what protection measures would be used to avoid pirated replication of paid content.

And today, while watching the Interns series, I saw a watermark in the middle of the screen:



Most likely, this is a unique code for each user. Those. when finding the series in free access, the administration now.ru will be able to determine which user is engaged in piracy.

After that, they will be able to block the pirate account and, possibly, according to the subscription payment data, submit an application to the authorities.

An interesting move. Curious, are there any other types of protection from them?
