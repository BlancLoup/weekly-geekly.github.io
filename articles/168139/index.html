$ 30,000 from Telerik for Windows 8 developers

Telerik has announced Virtual Accelerator for Windows 8 for startups whose products are aimed at the Windows 8 platform.



The winners will receive $ 30,000 (in exchange, the company will qualify for a 4-8% stake), a weekly program in Hong Kong at Accelerator HK, and a three-month participation in the virtual accelerator.

Answers to frequently asked questions:

  • Who can participate? Startups at an early stage of development that believe in the bright future of Windows 8. A team must contain at least one technical founder and one non-technical co-founder. To participate you will need to register a company (if you have not done so before). There are no geographic restrictions.
  • What do you need to apply? This may be a mocap or a schematic representation of a future application.
  • What are the evaluation criteria? Startups will be accepted with a team of 2 to 4 people. Of the requirements: strong technical, design and business skills, a strong concept, a prototype or a working service, openness and a desire to learn.
  • Can I add multiple projects? No one company should be concentrated on only one product.
  • We are a working startup and work with another accelerator. Can we get involved? Yes.
  • We already had investments. Can we participate? Yes.
  • Are we required to use Telerik products in the project? Not.

More details on the official page .
