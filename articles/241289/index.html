VisionMobile Eighth Developer Economics Survey

Developer Economics is the world's largest study on global trends in application economics and mobile development. It is based on a survey of developers and companies, which the British company VisionMobile conducts every six months. In the past, for example, more than 10,000 people from all over the world took part.



The survey finds out almost all the main metrics of application economics - which platforms are used by developers, which tools are used in development, which categories are most popular and which more money they bring, and which motivation drives developers. This year, the study also includes the Internet of Things and the development of applications for it.

According to the survey results, a large report is generated, which is available to everyone for free. Filling out the questionnaire will take you no more than 10 minutes, and express your opinion in the defining industrial report is very important - Russia is one of the leading mobile markets in the world and it is necessary to back it up with facts and figures.
The results of the eighth study will be published in February 2015.
