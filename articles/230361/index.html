LG G3: outside and inside the clearest smartphone in the world

At the end of last year, many were surprised and delighted with the LG G2 smartphone. After many years of production of middling cars and budget devices at all, LG suddenly showed a flagship capable of being on par with fellow Samsung and Sony. Moreover, in terms of a number of parameters - camera capabilities, battery life, sound quality in headphones - G2 exceeded other flagships. Probably the only thing to complain about was the lack of a microSD slot in the Russian version of the device. But 32 gigabytes is not such a small amount. And if you do not carry with you a powerful video library, it is quite enough even for a maniac like me.

Since then, LG has somehow gotten better at the mobile market. A large smartcard came out (or a phablet, if you like) G Pro, an inexpensive, but very worthy G Pad tablet appeared. And so there was no reason to doubt that the heir to the LG G2 would also be sensible. True, considering how powerful and harmonious the apparatus turned out, I did not think that the heir would appear before the fall. It was quite possible to release some kind of improved version, adding, for example, the coveted slot for flash drives. But LG decided differently, and now on all counters of the country lies the new flagship LG G3. And this is not a cosmetic upgrade, but a fundamentally different device.


The screen brightness of the LG G2 (left) and G3 is set at 66%. But for G2 this is clearly not enough, but for G3 - with an excess

LG G3 has become significantly more than its already rather big predecessor. The screen diagonal is 5.46 inches, and, despite the very narrow frames on the sides of the matrix, the device still claims to be the largest smartphone-flagship. At least it's wider than the Sony Xperia Z2 and longer than the Samsung Galaxy S5. Personally, it seems to me that for a smartphone that is constantly in your pocket, such dimensions are redundant. And it is impossible to reach the upper corners of the screen even after the operation on building up the thumb. Of course, for games and movies, the big screen is a joy, but unfortunately or fortunately, we are playing and watching TV shows far from constant.

Before proceeding to the conclusions, let's look at the G3 inside. Suddenly there is something unusual.

Inside

Talking about the internal structure of the LG G2 , I said that it made quite a significant step forward compared to its predecessors. Components there for the first time for LG were normal, and not as in the "Chinese" mediocre. However, in general, the engineering solution was controversial: microphones, charging, vibration motor and a number of other components hung on a huge cable, which made assembly difficult and could be a source of damage during operation.

The LG G3 made another important step: there are no cables at all. All components are located on the motherboard. Some doubts were caused by the long ‚Äúleg‚Äù leading to the bottom of the smartphone, but it was empirically found out that it is very strong and elastic, and also securely fixed in the case. So to break it, accidentally dropping a smartphone with the inserted connector of the charger or headphones, will not work.

In general, the internal device is simple and somehow not elegant in Korean. Understands the LG G3 in seconds, and it does not require anything except a screwdriver. Separately, I want to praise the headphone jack, which is made removable. It is the weak link in all smartphones - then plug the plug roughly, then the water will flow. Evaluating the design of the G3 as a whole, we can say that the Korean engineers almost did not surprise us, but we were pleased. The new flagship is simple and even elegant. Everything in it is sharpened by the production of large editions with minimal costs. It's nice that in the course of numerous simplifications with water they did not throw out the child. Total

Taking into account the high bar set a little more than six months ago, I personally expected a lot from G3. And, as often happens with high expectations, the reality was somewhat more modest.

LG G3 is undoubtedly an intelligent device. But the super-screen, which has become a noticeable performance limiter and reduced battery life, has not turned into a real killer feature. Check in any salon: the brightness and wide viewing angles are much easier to notice than the super-sharpness.



If you like large smartphones and want a modern model with an excellent screen, one of the best mobile cameras on the market and high-quality sound in headphones, you can easily take the LG G3. Have fun. The price, however, is still a bit high in official stores, 29,990 rubles, but in less prestigious places, G3 can easily be taken for 23,000. And this hints at a substantial margin on dumping prices.

But the release of the new flagship does not negate the existence of excellent in every sense of the LG G2, which officially costs 19,990, and unofficially - only 14,000! For six months, it is not obsolete at all, and taking into account the price reduction, it has become a very interesting option. It will be easy to wait for LG G4.
