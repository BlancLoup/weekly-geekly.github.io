SMSCoin - we are 3 years old!


Agregtor celebrates the 3rd anniversary of the SmsCoin project and summarizes its activities over the past year.

Our services are now available in Latin America, the Middle East and East Asia. Almost complete coverage of European and CIS countries was achieved. At the moment, the number of countries is not only unique on the Russian market, but also noticeable on a global scale, which has begun to attract not only Russian but also foreign partners. Along with the increase in the number of countries, we have strengthened positions in those of them in which we have been working for a long time, connecting many new numbers and tariffs with high costs. This was an important achievement against the backdrop of the global crisis, as it gave many of our partners the opportunity to accept payments from new countries, the connection of which was previously irrelevant due to the too low cost of SMS messages.
