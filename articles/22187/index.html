Button.RU instead of .COM on iPhone

1ph0ne.com published a very useful keyboard chip in the set on the iPhone. If you hold any letter on the keyboard, you can select its international version (see screenshot)


And you can do the same with the .COM button in the Safari browser on your iPhone. Hold down the .COM button and you will have the opportunity to quickly select a regional domain suffix. Depends on the regional settings for the keyboard.
