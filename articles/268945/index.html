The program of the second international conference of mobile developers MBLTdev is published.

We have published the program of the event, which you can see on the MBLTdev website. This year the program will be divided into several streams: reports and seminars for mid + mobile developers and reports for mobile development managers and IT directors. The program will be gradually supplemented by new speeches, stay tuned.



Among the confirmed speakers:

  • Chris Eidhof, iOS developer, founder of the objc.io blog
  • Phillip Connaton, iOS developer, Runkeeper
  • Caesar Valiente, Android Developer, Wunderlist
  • Bridget Lyons, QA, Soundcloud
  • Hector Zarate, iOS Cowboy, Spotify
  • Dmitry Soshnikov, expert on strategic technologies, Microsoft Russia
  • Yegor Tolstoy, leading iOS developer, Rambler & Co
  • Alexander Cherny, COO, iOS guru e-Legion
  • Alexander Orlov, Lead iOS Developer, Postforpost

With all the speakers can be found on the official website .

Mike Ash really wanted to come to Russia, but as a result unforeseen circumstances arose and he, alas, refused. But we are looking for a worthy replacement for him!

Please note that from November 1, the price will rise to 7,500 rubles. When buying two or more tickets, a 30% discount is valid.
