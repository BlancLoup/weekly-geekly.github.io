Gunnars - glasses for those who like to sit behind the PC

Every one of you knows that the best way to preserve vision and the organism as a whole is not to sit at your PC for days. If this does not concern you, then there may be a new development of a startup in the field of optics, Gunnar Optiks companies will slightly mitigate the consequences, at least for the eyes.




So, according to the manufacturer, their innovative glasses called Gunnars are designed to prevent the popular "computer vision syndrome". Thanks to branded i-AMP lenses, it becomes possible to relax the eye muscles, which get tired when viewing images for a long time over a short distance.



The glasses also have an anti-glare coating, and their special design creates a more favorable microclimate for the eyes, protecting them from drying out. Thanks to a specially selected color, which is applied gradiently, the glasses filter or block a certain light that is in the spectrum that is not optimal for the eyes. Gunnars cost ranges from $ 90 to $ 200.

You can learn more about glasses on the official website of Gunnar Optiks.
