Forbes ranked the 20 most expensive Runet companies

In 2014, according to Forbes, the record transactions in the market for the first time were not related to foreign investors. Mail.ru consolidated 48% of VKontakte for almost $ 1.5 billion and, as it turned out today , it was going to sell Elbrus Capital an HR-resource for Headhunter for $ 208 million - the deal stopped at the last stages. Yandex acquired Auto.ru for $ 175 million.

These three deals reflect the main trend of the last year and a half on the RuNet: the change of foreign investors by Russian investors, deliverance from non-core assets, consolidation.

At the same time, foreign funds are gradually reducing activity in Russia: Luxembourg Mangrove Capital Partners (KupiVip and Oktogo) suspended activity in Russia, the American Tiger Global reduced its share in Wikimart, where part of the shares went to a group of domestic investors headed by Anatoly Goncharov - Chairman of the Board of Directors of Finaprombank. General Catalyst and Accel Partners came out of Ostrovok.ru. This is not only a matter of politics - over the past year, the portfolios of venture funds depreciated 1.5-2 times.

The current rating of Russian startups Forbes for the first time was made according to a new method - not by revenue, but by cost. For public companies, this is a market capitalization; for non-public companies, the value was calculated based on the multiples of peers, transactions, and expert estimates. Forbes notes that all the figures are Forbes' expert assessment, and not financial documents reflecting the real value of a company.
