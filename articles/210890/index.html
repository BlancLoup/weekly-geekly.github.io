RealMusic.ru now allows you to upload music under Creative Commons 3.0


This picture symbolizes the speed with which I wrote this news, for two months have passed. But better late than even later.

So, RealMusic added the ability to license works under Creative Commons 3.0.

What kind of animal is that?


RealMusic - service for musicians. You can put your songs / videos / poems. They will be available for listening, downloading, embedding in sites using a special code. There are statistics. There are no restrictions on the duration / number / size of the file.

You can blog a group / artist. You can add the announcement of the concert in the poster. You can buy a PRO- account to get additional buns. There is an online radio.

There are many such sites. How is it different from others?


There are quite a few such sites. But this is one of the oldest, if not the oldest, among the existing in RuNet. It appeared in 2001, before Circles , ThankYou, and even such international sites as Jamendo . The main functionality was available back in 2007 - then I first encountered this site. Many novice groups laid out their records on it before the popularization of VKontakte. Yes, before it was decided to post there, after 2007, most beginners began to manage the group in VC.

Now on the site, if you believe the data indicated on it, 89323 musicians, 5917 authors and 711002 tracks.

Clear. So what about old news?


12.12.2013 I received a letter to the mail with the following content:

Starting today, every musician will be able to assign a Creative Commons license to his track!

What it is? What is it for? What for?
You will find the answers in the special section of the Creative Commons License that is dedicated to this unique phenomenon in the field of the protection of musicians' rights.
