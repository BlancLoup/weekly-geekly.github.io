Payment for goods on JD.com can now be carried out using Yandex.Money


Photo: Itreviewer

Online retailer JD.com now accepts payment for goods through Yandex.Cass - the Yandex.Money payment solution. The corresponding agreement between the two companies was signed in June of this year, Itreviewer writes . Buyers can pay for their goods both from a Yandex.Money wallet and from a bank card linked to a wallet.

Soon, Yandex.Cassa plans to add banking support to Sberbank, Alfa-Bank and Promsvyazbank.

Recall that JD.com works in Russia, starting this year. The site offers customers a fairly wide range of products, ranging from components to telephones, and ending with auto parts. The range of products is gradually expanding. Prior to being added as a Yandex.Money payment system, JD.com accepted payment only by bank cards and PayPal.

JD.com also collaborates with Russian Post.
