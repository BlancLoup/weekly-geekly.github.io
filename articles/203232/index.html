Power Panel and Control PPU = Monitoring + UPS + I LIE



We present to your attention a monitoring device for telecommunication cabinets - Power and Control Panel - PPU.


For the first time, the functionality of several devices necessary for the maintenance of the cabinet is combined in one case: a controller for monitoring the cabinet, an uninterruptible power supply and an input-distribution device, which is, in essence, a block of sockets with a counter.

It is thanks to this design that space is saved inside the telecommunications cabinet, the number of wires is reduced, which means that the reliability and maintainability of the system increases and the labor costs for its installation and maintenance are reduced.

BUT, LET'S FIRST, FIRST ...


Setting the task came to us from our special area - telecommunications and communications. For several years now, we have been manufacturing low-cost controllers for lockers that provide broadband Internet access using the Optics to the Home (FTTB) technology. FTTB is one of the most common and efficient technologies that allows a provider to quickly ‚Äúreach‚Äù a client quickly and without large capital investments.

Unfortunately, in some cases, the simplicity of the implementation of technical means turns into a certain simplification when the equipment is assembled ‚Äúfrom the pine forest‚Äù, mounted in a suitable iron box - and it is ready.

In fairness it should be noted that in fact there is no equipment on the market that is required by the operator of this service. Such a bleak picture is explained simply: until recently, no one has developed a technique specifically for this task.

Indeed, what is the modern FTTB terminal equipment located in residential buildings? INTEGRATED OPPORTUNITIES OF PPU


We describe the work of the new device in terms familiar to us, the developers: ‚Äúsetting the task - implementing the function‚Äù.

1. PROTECTION.

Problem Statement: Needless to say about the modern orgy of theft and vandalism ...



Function implementation: In addition to the cabinet door opening and impact (vibration) sensors, the device has an access authorization node (CHIP reader) and an alarm sounder (siren). The siren turns on automatically when the shock sensor triggers or when the cabinet door is opened and the authorization is not executed by the subject. The intensity of the sound pressure developed by the siren is very high - at the level of the pain threshold. Due to the presence of the UPS node, the siren will work even if the cabinet is completely de-energized, the communication channel is broken, etc. As a result, the cabinet acquires the function of self-active protection against theft (vandalism).
In addition, PUF allows you to control the cross-access copper cable. To put the cable under protection, you need to select a free pair in the cable, short it at the opposite end and plug it into the gap of the door sensor. It is done.

2. CLIMATIC MODES AND FIRE SAFETY.

Task setting: As you know, FTTB cabinets are often placed in attics and basements of residential buildings, where they are ‚Äúfrozen‚Äù in winter and overheated in summer. And there and close to the fire.



Function realization: The PUF is completed with a digital temperature sensor with a plug connector and a flexible wiring connector 50 cm long. This design allows the sensor to be placed directly at the point of possible maximum heating. The digital value of the measured temperature is transmitted to the center. In addition, the PUF contains a special ‚Äúclimatic‚Äù controlled outlet of ~ 220 volts, into which either electric fans (cooling) or heating elements (heating) can be included. The threshold values ‚Äã‚Äãof temperatures, upon reaching which heating or ventilation is automatically turned on, can be set and changed by the user from the DC. Thus, the PUF is able to independently regulate the temperature inside the cabinet, preventing overheating or overcooling of the equipment.

3. ORGANIZATION OF QUALITY POWER SUPPLY WITH RESERVATION.

Problem Statement: The state of electrical power inputs in residential buildings is well known. As a rule, they are designed according to the standards of the 60s and do not withstand the stresses created by modern household appliances. As a result - throws and dips in the power supply network supplying the cabinet. And the corresponding quality of the Internet is low, far from reaching the stated speeds. Or its complete (Internet) disappearance.



Implementation of the function: The function of organizing power supply with redundancy is performed by the UPS node built into the PUF. This node is built specifically for specific consumers, which are Fast Ethernet router switches. It is devoid of the disadvantages of a UPS designed for other purposes:
  • The output form of our UPS does not create interference that can reduce the speed of data exchange. At the same time, UPSs that are not specialized for working with switches, have an output signal in the form of a so-called ‚Äúmodified sine wave‚Äù. Under this beautiful name hides the fact that the device, in fact, is a generator of rectangular pulses with an amplitude of 220 volts and a frequency of 50 Hz. The proximity of such a source of interference with Ethernet is highly undesirable, since no one knows in what spectrum and with what amplitude pickups appear that can seriously reduce the speed of data exchange;
  • All parameters of our UPS node are measured and transmitted to the Dispatch Center (DC) in the general signaling package, that is, no interface conversion is required in this case. Non-specialized UPSs, as a rule, work under the RS232 protocol, therefore, an additional ‚Äúintermediary‚Äù is necessary for exchange with DC over Ethernet.
  • the presence of a sufficient number of sockets for connecting all reserved and non-reserved consumers. In the UPS, not designed specifically for the cabinet FTTB, just do not have enough power outlets.

This UPS is designed taking into account the features of the object and is devoid of the disadvantages of the UPS, created for other purposes. When using standard UPS, switching to battery power occurs with a voltage drop, the duration of which is approximately 20-60 ms - this is enough to disrupt the operation of the switch. UPS technology "Tehnotroniks" has a significant advantage - the transition to backup power occurs without the "dips" voltage, which eliminates the possibility of "hang" switches (also see this in the VIDEO ).

4. STRUGGLE AGAINST THE "DEPENDENCE" OF ELECTRONIC "FILLING" OF A CABINET. DISTANCE AND AUTOMATIC CONTROL OF SWITCHES.

Task setting: The ‚Äúfreezing‚Äù of the equipment and, as a result, the temporary loss of its working capacity is partly due to the poor quality of the power supply, and partly to its design flaws. After all, it is not a secret that the main criterion for choosing a specific type of devices is price. And this does not contribute to their reliable operation. The method of "treating" this disease is also well known - short-term withdrawal of the power supply followed by re-feeding. If a visit is required for such a procedure, the ‚Äúpleasure‚Äù is expensive. And for the period of waiting for "resuscitator" all subscribers are left without a service. Thus, it is highly desirable to do a power reboot remotely (if the equipment has not lost the ability to perceive commands) or automatic (if the switches can only be restarted ‚Äúfrom the inside‚Äù).



Function implementation: PUF is capable of performing both listed types of switch power restarting - both external (remote) and internal. As part of the PUF there are controlled sockets ~ 220 Volts, providing the physical implementation of the break / power supply. Switches are included in these sockets. No more external switching elements are required.

5. REMOTE READING OF INDICATIONS FROM ELECTRIC POWER ACCOUNT DEVICES.

Task setting: The usefulness of this function does not require special comments. It is clear that monthly to go around all the houses and entrances where FTTB cabinets are installed and to manually write off the meter readings is unpleasant and troublesome. On the other hand, not to set the counter at all and blindly pay the bills issued by the HOA and the management companies means to repeatedly overpay for invalid figures.



Function implementation: There is a separate modification of the device under the name PPU-S, which contains a built-in electricity meter. The data from the pulse (telemetric) input of the counter are summed up, stored and transmitted in a general packet to the DC.
