For those who do not know, Tumblr is a social network of blogs, which can be in text, picture, animation, or video format. Users publish what they like, share, subscribe to each other, make re-blogs. In general, nothing unusual. However, Tumblr for its 11 years has gained great popularity among fans of adult content. While a friend of the social networks began to actively block such materials, Tumblr users remained untouchable, which only increased the popularity of the portal.
