We work with bookmarks in a new way.



Surely many will say that there are already so many services like this one, why do we need another one? First, the www.juwit.com service is not just a bookmark service, it is a whole community of people who can share their bookmarks on the Internet. Secondly, in addition to bookmarks, it has the function of creating short links of this type www.juwit.com/xyz

But, besides the fact that on our service you can store links to sites, you can also search for the necessary information on it. The search is carried out using the morphology of the Russian language, which allows you to find the necessary information without even knowing exactly what you are looking for. When you add a bookmark, a copy of the page is created on our server, which allows you to find the necessary information, even if the main site is not working or has changed.

After adding a bookmark, other users of the service can rate and comment on it, and this in turn gives more relevant results for those who are looking for information on our site, because bookmarks with a higher rating are displayed above other bookmarks. If you want to follow the bookmarks of a particular person, then this user will be helped by the RSS feed of this user.

But the main difference of our service from others is that it was created as a service that everyone can modify and improve. This means that we accept all comments and suggestions on the functionality of the service and make the appropriate changes, and you, in turn, receive high-quality and reliable service.
