November 6th is the best day of the calendar! Student Day 3.0 with Steve Ballmer!

Hubs and Hubs! We are pleased to invite you to the main Microsoft student event this year - Student Day 3.0 . It will be held on November 6, 2012 at Digital October in Moscow, and will be the brightest in the whole history - after all, we took the best of the first two events held in 2010 and 2011 ! At Student Day 3.0, you will be addressed by Microsoft Executive Director Steve Ballmer , who will tell you about the amazing opportunities that this year will open to Russian students.



Registration | Student-Day.ru | #studay

At the student day everyone will find something interesting for themselves! You will hear about the new Microsoft products that are starting this year - Windows 8 and Windows Phone, the Kinect wonder device, which allows you to see and feel the person, as well as to show amazing technological tricks . And do not just hear - students who can program, right at the event will be able to learn how to develop applications for Windows 8 and Kinect themselves!

But this event will be interesting not only to developers - feel free to bring with you girls humanities! In a very rich program consisting of both lectures and practical sessions, everyone will find something for themselves - including even the possibility of personal career consultation with a coach! Come to Student Day to become better, more experienced, more successful!

We hope that Student Day 3.0 will justify the well-known opinion that third versions of products always prove to be successful , and that after visiting the event you will be charged with enthusiasm for the coming year - a year of amazing student opportunities !

Registration is required to attend the event. Invite friends to an event using the hashtag #studay .
