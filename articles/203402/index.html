ThL W8s - a powerful smartphone with glass protection Gorilla Glass 3

Today, many manufacturers of smartphones have a very wide range of manufactured models, and often there is the option of grouping them by series. In particular, if you look at ThL, the company offers about a dozen smartphones, most of which belong to the W8 or W100 families. The user deals with the same in appearance products, but significantly different in characteristics.



In appearance, the new model is very similar to its older brother ThL W8, its hardware platform is different, but the design is almost not changed. The previous version of the smartphone already has a well-deserved reputation among fans of Chinese products. And the model W8s should not undermine it, because it is equipped with a new processor, extended memory and a camera with high resolution. And also a new highlight - a sheeting of Corning Gorilla Glass 3.

The hero of the review is entirely made of white plastic. ThL W8s plastic does not look and does not feel cheap, as it is not a creaky version of plastic with sagging curved edges dotted with burrs. ThL W8s smartphone is equipped with an IPS sensor matrix with a diagonal of 5 inches. The resulting density of dots per inch was 441 ppi. The picture on the screen of the review hero is really very clear and bright.



The display brightness has both manual and automatic adjustment, the latter is based on the operation of the light sensor. Multitouch technology allows you to simultaneously handle up to ten touches. There is a smartphone and proximity sensor, which locks the screen when you bring the smartphone to your ear.



The screen has good viewing angles without inverting the shades and without significant color shift even with large deviations of the view from the perpendicular to the screen.



In the bright sun, the screen remains readable. Top screen is covered with a protective glass Corning Gorilla Glass 3, resistant to scratches.
