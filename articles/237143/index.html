In the continuation - a brief history of Minecraft and Mojang in numbers and dates. Rather, it is not even a tape, but a chronology of events.

August 2010 . Spontaneous first meeting with toy fans, in Bellevue, Washington.

September 2010 . Minecraft servers fell, unable to withstand the influx of players. Notch temporarily made the toy free, as compensation.
