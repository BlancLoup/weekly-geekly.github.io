New models of radio bridges Siklu EtherHaul 2500 - transfer more, transfer further


It is no secret that the needs of users and applications for bandwidth are growing.
But there are no frequencies on the air, on the contrary, every year and each new frequency assignment of free frequencies remains ever less.

That is why more than 5,000 spans of Siklu radio bridges, E-band Gigabit solutions (70, 80 GHz), which do not require assignments of frequency nominal values ‚Äã‚Äãand payment for them, are already successfully operated in Russia.

Today we are happy to tell you about the new model Siklu EtherHul 2500, offering twice the capacity and one and a half times longer distances than the 1200 series we are used to.

So, what's new for us is the EtherHaul 2500, a solution that, in general, is new for the Russian market, but for almost a year now it has been tested in key customers.

In the first place, of course, this is a large radio channel capacity.

And the big one immediately doubled in comparison with the previous flagship 1200F and 5.5 times in comparison with the most popular in Russia model 1200TL (2 Gbit duplex versus 700 Mbit simplex).

The second is a high transmitter power and better sensitivity, and, accordingly, a large communication range with the same accessibility, or high speed and availability at the same range.

In the new model range, SIKLU switched from the practice of releasing a separate EXT and 1-ft model line (without the possibility of installing an external antenna) in favor of systems with a universal mount that allows you to connect an antenna of any type: 16, 31, 60 cm

Accordingly, the articles for Odu now look like this:

The antenna unit itself with a universal footprint EH-2500F-ODU-H-EXT and a paired EH-2500F-ODU-L-EXT. Antennas at 30 EH-ANT-1ft EtherHaul 1 ft. antenna and 60 cm EH-ANT-2ft EtherHaul 2 ft. antenna. Mounting kits: EH-MK-1ft EtherHaul Mounting Kit for 1 ft. Antenna and EH-MK-2ft EtherHaul Mounting Kit for 2 ft Antenna.

This is extremely positive news for those who use Siklu as a temporary communication channel to connect new territories - there is no longer any need to keep the equipment of different items in the warehouse for different ranges, just enough to have the necessary antennas.

If we talk about less demanding access level channels, where availability of 99.9% is acceptable, then we are talking about 7 km:



However, using the EMM channel redundancy technology, you can build much longer links.

EMM (a simplified version of the ERP implementation ) allows in the case of high attenuation to seamlessly switch traffic to backup low-frequency, rain-resistant, but also relatively low-performing communication channel, while ensuring high channel availability for the most priority traffic.

In this case, both the classic solutions of the range of 5-6.4 GHz and the novelty - AirFiber 11 about which we will soon write a separate material can act as a backup channel.



We combine the best: high performance of the E band and high availability of the channels of the classic bands.
