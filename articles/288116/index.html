Ya.ru finally open

About 7 months have passed since that moment when Yandex launched the beta.Ya.ru service in beta testing. During this time, many innovations appeared in the service compared to the first version, and users began to call it a toy or a toy :). And then the day came when access to the toy was open to anyone.
In April, we launched the Ya.ru service in beta testing mode, by invitation.
Six months have passed since then. Service has changed significantly, it has new features. We are very grateful to all users who helped us test Ya.ru and make it better.

Today we announce that to create a page on Ya.Ru no longer need invitations. Just go to the main page - wow.ya.ru - and register. In addition, the main page shows our "Hall of Fame" - users who have recently received Qu. And you too can be among them! More on wow.ya.ru you can see the mood of users Ya.ru. They are updated in real time.

And the most interesting thing is the appearance of places for communication by interests - clubs. You can select a club in the menu "My Clubs". Using the official position, we invite everyone to the club service Ya.ru! :) By the way, it is there, in the special topic, you can leave requests-requests for the creation of clubs with other topics. Clubs are still being tested and we invite everyone to participate in it.

Communicate, meet, show your photos, share your mood, links, discuss common interests in clubs - and we will make Ya.Ru more convenient and interesting!

According to the minimum estimates, about 18,000 people are registered with the service today.
