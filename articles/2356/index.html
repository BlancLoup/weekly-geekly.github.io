You can pay for Skype in Yandex.Money

Russian internet users can now pay for Skype calls through the Yandex.Money payment system. According to Yandex , there is a corresponding agreement with this global IP-telephony system.

Until now, the development of SkypeOut in Russia was hampered by difficulties in paying for the service, says Evgenia Zavalishin, CEO of Yandex.Money. Skype could only be paid by credit card, and not all Russian banks. Unofficial intermediaries took a noticeable commission, and the payment itself often took several days. ‚ÄúNow Internet users who want to talk to the whole world have a direct, simple and reliable payment method - Yandex.Money,‚Äù Zavalishina says.

Recall, SkypeOut allows you to call from computer to phone at the per minute rate, which is very cheap - for many countries it coincides with the rates for local phone calls.

Currently, Yandex.Money users are offered SkypeOut payment certificates of 5, 10 and 25 euro. Payer enough to specify your Skype ID, and the selected amount will be automatically credited to his virtual account in Skype. You can also buy Skype Voicemail payment certificates of 5 and 15 euro.

During December, the first 15,000 users who pay for SkypeOut using Yandex.Money will receive 1 year of free use of a Skype Voicemail voicemail box.
