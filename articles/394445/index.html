Humorous public MDK blocked in the territory of the Russian Federation



The epic with the blocking of the MDK public has come to an end. Today, at about 3:30 Moscow time, access to this VKontakte group is de facto blocked on the territory of the Russian Federation.

As announced in court, each of the 695 albums of the public contains 400-600 photos that are pornographic in nature and can affect children's mental health. In addition, the community published texts with profanity and pictures that offend the feelings of believers.

The blockage was carried out by Roskomnadzor six months after the decision of the Smolninsky District Court of St. Petersburg dated October 27, 2015. The decision was disputed in court, but in February 2016 the city court of St. Petersburg left it in force .
