Certon Systems has released a protected hard drive.



We are talking about an external hard drive, which often fail their owners. In the sense that sometimes a fall from a very small height is enough for the external hard drive to stop working. In some cases, all this leads to the loss of very valuable data that cannot be recovered. Many companies produce their own solutions, but Certon Systems decided to create a very secure device.

Its hard drive is able to withstand a fall from 7 meters (this is the third floor somewhere, right?), Plus a submersion at a shallow depth of about 1 minute. In general, if such a hard drive falls into the bathroom, then nothing terrible will happen. The question is - why the hard drive in the bathroom? Well, okay, the main thing - you can not be afraid of rain, coffee, tea, beer and other substances that more often than others act as a destructive element.



The company offers three models of models, starting with a volume of 500 GB and ending with 1 TB. In general, quite an interesting proposal. And yes, such a disk is connected to a PC or laptop via USB 3.0. Unfortunately, now this model range of hard drives is sold only in Germany. The cost of the device is 170/200/240 euros, respectively, an increase in capacity. Already in early 2012, these gadgets should begin to be sold worldwide.
