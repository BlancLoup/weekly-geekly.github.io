The term of transfer of servers with personal data to Russia is reduced

A few days ago, there was news about the reduction in the transfer of servers with personal data of Russian citizens to Russia. If earlier nobody wanted to take active actions earlier than in a year and a half, now, probably, it is necessary to hurry. The State Duma proposes to transfer all information until January 2015. Our state explains this decision to reduce the period of "considerations of national security."

I share this news with the community so that no one has any surprises in the new year.
