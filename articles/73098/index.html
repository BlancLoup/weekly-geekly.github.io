Music bios. or how computers sing

Many of those who encounter computer malfunctions have heard various sounds when turned on. These sounds are not simple, but informative - bios gives signals depending on the fault, etc. Usually these sounds are quite simple: for example, 3 signals - a video card malfunction, one long signal - a RAM fault, etc. Each BIOS manufacturer has its own signals.
