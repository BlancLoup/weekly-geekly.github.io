Budget forecast from Yandex.Direct is available to all

Yandex.Direct has opened for everyone a tool for preliminary assessment of the monthly budget of advertising campaigns. Now, each Yandex client can get an approximate forecast of the costs of contextual advertising without spending time and money on a trial order, but only setting the parameters of the upcoming campaign. Previously, such an opportunity was provided only to Yandex partner agencies.


The approximate budget forecast is based on Yandex.Direct statistics. When estimating the budget of an advertising campaign, key phrases are taken into account for which ads will be displayed, region of display, position of ads on the Yandex search results pages.
