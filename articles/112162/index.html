Concentrate as salvation from procrastination



After reading the topic " How to protect yourself from time-sink sites? " I immediately remembered one of the most necessary programs, which really helped me when I was working under Mac OS X.

Meet - Concentrate .

This program allows you to focus on work, banning everything that hinders: websites, programs. Can automatically put the status in IM. Conversely, run the necessary programs and execute scripts.



From the settings, you can build configs that can be useful: for example, if you are writing code, you have nothing to do on deviantart.



We select a config, and how much you want to do business - and productivity increases.


PS If someone advises an analog under Win - I will be very happy. Chrome plug-ins do not offer.
