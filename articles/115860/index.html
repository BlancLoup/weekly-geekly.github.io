Disable SMS confirmation of payments in the Webmoney system

Not so long ago, Webmoney connected me a great payment confirmation function. It is not clear why. Each SMS costs 4 cents and I consider it meaningless to pay them several times a day. I think that those who really need this service and would have connected it themselves. The case on the part of the webman looks quite different - we will turn it on to everyone, let them pay, and whoever doesn‚Äôt want to let them dig for help and turn it off on their own. Here is what the company itself says:

In some cases, confirmation of operations can be enabled automatically based on the results of the operation of the WebMoney security robot.

Plus during the transaction increases the minimum commission. Read more here . Well digging help.

The first thing I turned off is Confirmation of operations in the settings of the light version of the program, in General


and on the Security tab


But it did not help. A little more google and solution found.

Go here - security.webmoney.ru/asp/default.asp go to "Confirmation of operations" and after logging in we disable this function.



I hope someone this will be useful.
