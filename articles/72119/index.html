12 hours without recharging!

ASUStek has announced the upcoming release in Europe of new UnLimited notebooks that can work as long as 12 hours without recharging - 13.3-inch UL30 and 15.6-inch UL50AG and UL50VG. The last of the laptops, by the way, is equipped with a separate graphics adapter. All three laptops are enclosed in aluminum cases and equipped with high-end Altec Lansing speakers. Laptops ASUS UL30, UL50AG and UL50VG are running the new operating system Windows 7 and, which is quite logical, will go on sale on October 22.

Laptops are based on energy-efficient Intel Core 2 Duo CULV SU7300 processors. They are also equipped with a hard disk up to 500 GB, wireless adapters Wi-Fi 802.11 a / g / n and Bluetooth, a web-camera, a touchpad with multi-touch support and a light sensor for automatic adjustment of the display brightness. And to optimize power consumption, the Power4Gear function is provided, which allows you to set 4 different modes from the most productive to the most economical.
