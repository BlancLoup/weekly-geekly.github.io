The best gifts for February 23: version Madrobots.ru

Bracelet with heart rate monitor, backpack for geek and headphones from films about the future - we have compiled a digest of the most useful and unusual gifts for Defender of the Fatherland Day.

Pulsometer Mio Fuse




Chest straps, which are certainly included in the set of ordinary heart rate monitors, are no longer needed: in Mio, they created a tracker that can read the heart rate without additional accessories. At the same time, the accuracy of optical monitoring is comparable to an ECG: this is what the manufacturer of the bracelet says after numerous tests. In general, Mio Fuse is a smart gift for a man who regularly goes to the gym and wants to work on a pulse tracking program.



In addition to measuring heart rate, Mio Fuse is able to do the same thing as other fitness bracelets: it counts steps, measures pace and distance, and also keeps a record of calories burned. With daily classes lasting up to an hour, the Fuse battery will last for a week. The tracker is synchronized with the two most popular mobile operating systems (iOS, Android), so it is important to make sure that the future gift-holder‚Äôs smartphone is not running Windows Phone.

Backpack Thule Crossover TCBP-317




A real gadget man always carries his devices with him, but putting them in a regular bag is suicide: the risk of getting a tablet with a broken screen or a hopelessly tangled cord from charging is too great. Thule developed a special geek backpack, which, despite its rather modest capacity (25 liters), will hold all the necessary devices.



The main compartment of the Crossover TCBP-317 is a laptop: this compartment was originally designed for the MacBook Pro, but another laptop with a screen up to 15 inches will fit in there. The second compartment is given to the tablet (by default with a 10-inch diagonal). Of course, between the gadgets there is a layer that will not allow the devices to fight against each other. In the third compartment, it is proposed to transfer charges, and for the smartphone, the SafeZone unit has been designed: thanks to the solid construction, the device is guaranteed complete safety. However, if someone is used to carry the phone in his pocket, SafeZone quickly removed, increasing the free space of the backpack.

Sensu Brush Stylus




Tablets on iOS and Android have quite a few usage scenarios, but until recently, drawing was not one of them: drawing something highly artistic would not work with a finger, and the usual stylus is only suitable for sketches. The stylish digital brush Sensu Brush adds another function to the gadget: the screen turns into a picture canvas.

Due to the conductive fiber (the pile is made of it), Sensu Brush is also suitable for complex patterns: the sensations from touching the screen are the same as from touching a normal brush to a canvas. Additional advantages: no need to recharge and a cap that turns the brush into a stylus for capacitive displays.

Zendure 4-Port




The bed of adapters sticking out of the socket quickly begins to irritate: it is more convenient to distribute energy to gadgets using a single charger. Zendure 4-Port - from this category: a stylish square-shaped device is equipped with a quartet of USB cable inputs. The device from Zendure weighs only 200 grams: you can take it on any trip to be able to charge your smartphone and tablet at the same time, and not wait until the adapter is free.



But the main function of 4-Port is a panel for wireless charging. There can be placed as smartphones that support this feature (models of Nokia, Motorola, LG, HTC), and other devices (including the iPhone). However, for the second option you need an external Qi-receiver, which is not included in the kit.

Zendure 4-Port at Madrobots.ru

JBL OnBeat Mini lighting




A compact speaker with two powerful broadband speakers is a great gift for a music lover who uses Apple devices. OnBeat Mini works with all devices from Cupertino, released in the fall of 2012 or later (the main thing is the lighting connector). The gadget from JBL can play the role of a docking station (charging) or play music from another audio device via a 3.5mm cable.

From one charge, the speaker gives an 8-hour concert, and the current battery fullness level is indicated by an LED indicator. OnBeat Mini is a useful thing not only at a party, but also in everyday life: it is obvious that listening to jazz from the speaker is more pleasant than from the speaker of the iPhone.

Harman Kardon AE




The design of headphones with an aluminum alloy body was designed specifically for those iPhone owners who want to look ultramodern. "Gags" involuntarily evoke associations with films about the future, but the appearance is far from the only plus of Harman Kardon.



Headphones are equipped with a thunder switch, a 1.2-meter-long wire and a case included in the kit. Also, Harman Kardon AE can operate in the range of 16-20000 Hz and can boast ear pads that do not cause discomfort at all.
