Advisory service KinoEstet



I want to present you my new service - KinoEstet . The purpose of the service is to help the user select a movie to watch.

A brief background to the creation of the service, and how it differs from other similar services:

Once I quit playing WoW :)

I had 4-6 hours of free time per day, which, at first, I began to spend on watching movies. To watch only the best and interesting, I began to use the services of imhonet.ru and kinopoisk.ru .

After I rated about 200 films on Imhonet, he stopped giving me adequate recommendations, and I really didn‚Äôt want to watch what he was offering. I stopped going there.

I used Kinopoisk a little longer, revised everything with awards over the past few years, revised everything imdb above 8, then uninteresting films went on, in the end, I stopped going and going there.

I liked the oral recommendations of friends most of all, because these were not custom-made reviews and not dry assessments, but the opinions of people I know. So the idea arose, to create a small saytik on which my friends and I will write, who watched what the latest movie, based on the forum threads ‚ÄúWhat was the last movie you watched?‚Äù, Only with convenient search and selection mechanisms for films, while simple interface.

4 months have passed since the launch of the service, during which time a lot of things have changed, many features were implemented that users wanted, the design changed several times, much was rethought.

As a result, the following key features are implemented, without any special various bells and whistles:
  • selection of films from the whole heap, on my personal handful according to the principle ‚ÄúI will watch‚Äù, ‚ÄúI will not‚Äù, ‚ÄúI will decide later‚Äù.

  • add the ability to recommend a specific movie to a specific person (special notification)
  • additional sorting (according to the assessment of a particular user, according to the date of feedback of a particular user)
