The second gigapixel panorama of Yekaterinburg

The second panorama, like the first one, was commissioned by the administration of Yekaterinburg.
In my opinion, the second panorama turned out to be better than the first. Less artifacts. Although the author overdone with the horizon.

The panorama also contains links to a number of simple, equally high-quality panoramas. A small virtual tour of the city.


Interestingly, the first panorama last year was made by Cityscanner , and in this competition the unexpectedly won a little-known photographer from Novokuznetsk - Andrey Borisov . Set a price for the work of 88 thousand rubles, beating Citiscanner with the price of 120 thousand rubles.

I wish the author to develop the skill and continue making gigapixel panoramas. More gigapixels, more quality incl. other cities of Russia. Unfortunately, gigapixel panoramas in Russia were found only in Moscow and Yekaterinburg . I would like to see Peter, Novosibirsk, Samara, Kazan, Sochi and other wonderful cities :)
