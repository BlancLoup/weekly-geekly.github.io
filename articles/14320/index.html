How to take a screenshot from the screen of a mobile phone, PDA, smartphone, PDA?

There are 2 ways to take a screenshot from the screen of a mobile phone, PDA, smartphone, PDA:

The first way is trivial and suggests itself. We take the phone and vulgarly photograph it with a digital camera or any other device that can photograph.
Pros:

* quickly
* screenshot can be made from any device

Minuses:

* hard to achieve good quality, you need experience in photography
* The device for photographing should be of good quality.
* you need to edit photos with a graphic editor

The second way is to install the software on your phone or other device. It all depends on the manufacturer of the phone, its model and the operating system used. Not all devices are capable of this!

At the moment I have found 2 programs for smartphones:

* Screenshot for Symbian OS (S60 / UIQ)
* Ximplify SeleQ

With their help, you can take screenshots from some phones Nokia, Sony Ericsson, Motorola

For Windows Mobile:

* HauteCapture 2.0
* SmartControl
* CapturePro
* Pocket Controller Pro

Pros:

* no need for additional equipment
* no need to use a graphic editor

Minuses:

* search and install software for a long time
* not all devices support
