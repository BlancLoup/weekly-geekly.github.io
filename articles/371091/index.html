[CASE] STIGMA SHOW and Raise3D N2 Dual 3D Printer



Hello! We continue the cycle of posts about the use of technology purchased by our customers. This article is about the STIGMA SHOW show team and how they create their costumes using the Raise3D N2 Dual 3D printer.



About what


Stigma Show - is a bright geometric patterns, psychedelic images and powerful energy. For ten years now they have been performing all over the world and giving people light, in the literal and figurative sense. The main directions - light, fire and mirror show.

Example: video announcement of the new program Stigma Show - Pixel Light Show rew 2017 - EKRAN



Here we will talk about how Stigma Show applies 3D printing in its work.



What kind of printer


Raise3D N2 Dual


  • Technology: FDM
  • Printing area, mm: 305 x 305 x 305
  • Layer thickness, from, micron: 10
  • Supported file formats: .stl, .obj
  • Printing temperature, degrees Celsius: 170-300
  • Supported materials: PLA, ABS, PC, PETG, HIPS, NinjiaFlex
  • Diameter of thread, mm: 1.75
  • Nozzle diameter, mm: 0.4 mm
  • Software: IdeaMaker
  • Price: 235,000 rubles.

Raise3D N2 Dual is interesting for its high quality and accuracy of printing, high-quality assembly and accessories, a large work area and the possibility of printing with two materials.

The appearance of this sample of industrial design and the convenience of working with it deserve special attention.

A 3D printer is used to print masks and other elements of costumes that participants use in performances.



Each such suit is a complex structure, assembled from a variety of components, including both 3D printed parts and fabric, as well as electronic or reflective parts.
