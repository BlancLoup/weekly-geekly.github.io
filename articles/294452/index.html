Yandex.Taxi will increase commissions for taxis. Service offered to compensate for the difference by advertising on cars

From October 1, the Yandex.Taxi service will increase commissions for taxi fleet partners by three percentage points. The company offered to compensate for the increase in commission by installing advertising on cars - on light boxes. "For those taxi companies that already have such an advertisement, Yandex.Taxi will not increase the commission.

Commission for taxis when paying in cash will increase by three percent from 8 + VAT to 11% + VAT, with non-cash payments - from 5.99% + 0.01% agency + 2% acquiring + VAT to 8.99% + 0.01% agency + 2% acquiring + VAT .

Increasing the commission will not be the reason for the increase in fares with Yandex.Taxi . The company offers taxis to install advertising in light boxes to compensate for the increase in commission without raising tariffs.
