Getting Started with the Google+ API



Just received the good news from Google:

Welcome developers,


Thank you for your interest in the Google+ platform. We promised to report everything interesting that is happening. Well, well - the time has come ...

Today we are launching the first part of the Google + API. This is the first release of the API focused only on working with public data - the API allows you to access information that users share using Google+. Detailed information you can get in our blog .

Besides We are pleased to introduce a new site for Google+ developers . It will be a place to get acquainted with the conditions of our policy , terms of use , discussions with other developers , access to documentation , as well as tools that make development on the Google+ platform easier and more fun, and, of course, a place where announcements of all new releases will appear.

We look forward to see what you develop using the API. Today it is only the beginning, and your work will influence what comes next, so get down to work.

Happy coding!

- Team Google+ platform
