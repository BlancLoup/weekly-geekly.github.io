Good night with a sound cushion. Sound Asleep

If in a previous post, the robot Mr. Clock helped us wake up, then in this one will get to know someone who will help you fall asleep - the musical cushion. Sound Asleep

Inside this pillow, filled with fiber, are the speakers that allow you to play your favorite sleeping music or the corresponding sound background.

You can connect any playback device to the pillow (be it an MP3 player / radio or music system) that has a 3.5 mm audio jack. In the end to everything: Sound Asleep will not interfere with those who sleep next to you, since only you will hear music alone.
