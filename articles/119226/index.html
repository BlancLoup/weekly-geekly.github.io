Introducing the HTC Sensation multimedia smartphone

Hi, dear Habrachitel. From today we open a blog on Habrahabr and will delight you with new interesting publications. Just over a month ago we announced the HTC Sensation multimedia superphone. It's time to get to know him better!

HTC Sensation is a real toughie, the performance of which is too tough for any existing application. The heart of the smartphone is the latest dual-core processor Qualcomm MSM8260. Using MSM8260 allowed not only to improve the performance of the smartphone, but also to increase the battery life. Do not be afraid of its dual-core power, because The cores in the processor are clocked asynchronously, which means they will not spend precious charge for nothing.



However, high performance is by no means the most important goal. During the design of the HTC Sensation, we took into account all the accumulated experience and brought the usability of the smartphone to a new level! A single piece of aluminum in combination with soft-touch plastic forms a monolithic body of the device, which is not only elegant in appearance, but also has excellent strength. The contour qHD display is easy to use and will not be scratched, even if you put the smartphone face down.

Unlock the full potential of the device will help the new version of HTC Sense: an interactive lock screen, 3D widget carousel, excellent weather effects, polished UI elements and much more.

Detailed characteristics of the device are available on the official website .
The approximate start of sales is the beginning of June. The recommended price will be announced.
