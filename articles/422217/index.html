Not really serious about hosting or how to check the adequacy of the host

During the filling of VPS.today - the directory for selecting virtual servers - the goal was not to add as many hosting companies as possible. On this site, we place increased demands on hosters and are very sensitive to all the shortcomings that we encounter on the site of the hoster and in its work. This allows you to offer service providers who are responsible for their work and allow a minimum of errors.

In this article I will tell you what criteria affect the appearance of the company in the catalog (or the disappearance from it), as well as what you should pay attention to when you first meet the hoster. I will try not to do advertising to anyone or anti-advertising, all coincidences are random. If any company was not found in the catalog, it is not their fault, and our flaw does not mean that it is bad.



So, suppose that we came across the site of host X, about which we had not heard before. What simple steps can be taken to protect yourself from possible troubles in the future?

For starters, you can check the domain with any WhoIs service to see that it is not registered yesterday (there may, of course, be cases when a company changes a domain for various reasons, but they can be neglected). Many hosters create threads on the forums (very popular, for example, searchengines.guru) and assign employees representing their company.
