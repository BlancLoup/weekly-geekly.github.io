Lightmapping is a technology that preserves information about lighting in texture, which allows you to free up computing resources for other needs. FAS has recognized Google as violator of antitrust laws

On February 18, Yandex filed an application with the Federal Antimonopoly Service with a request to check Google for violation of the antimonopoly legislation of Russia. Yandex believes that the American competitor needs to separate the Android operating system from Google search and other services. Otherwise, the principle of free competition in the market of Android-smartphones is violated, where Google can exert pressure on manufacturers wishing to use their own versions of Android-based firmware.
