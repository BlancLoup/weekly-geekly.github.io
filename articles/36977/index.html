Voice strength

Many copies are already broken around innovation with the power of voice. Someone does not like that with one or two voices you can bring the post to the main page or roll it into the asphalt of a peel. Disputes do not stop for several days. But what about the situation in reality?

On the one hand, no one will argue with the fact that the site has a core of users that is as active and authoritative as possible. These users do not have to have a huge karma, karma in this case is not an indicator at all. So what can serve as an indicator? For example, habrasila. Habrasila is an indicator of user activity and utility for the community, according to the community itself. You can have karma -100, and habrasila +100. Theoretically, this is possible.
Depending on a number of indicators, including habrazila and tormozuhi pressure in the UFO brake system, the voice weight of the user changes. Someone votes with a force of 1, and someone with a force of 2, 3, 4 or 5. Is it terrible, not fair, offensive? Perhaps, but let's look at the power distribution statistics.
A voice with a strength of 1 point has 44160 people.

If you look at the thresholds for displaying posts on the main page, you will notice that everything is not so bad. Not enough one or two votes for a fundamental change in the position of the post.
I want to note that the thresholds are not final, the power of the voice can also change. I hope that you will understand the possible changes with understanding.
