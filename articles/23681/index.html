Delaycast.com: Predictions for Air Traveling

There are no airports in the world where travelers would not face cancellation or delay of flights. American startup Delaycast helps people find out in advance whether they will have to sit in the terminal for an hour or two or three. The task of the Delaycast is to provide information about possible difficulties on air routes. Retrospective statistics of this kind are available on many sites, but Delaycast technology allows you to make predictions for the future. The problem is solved by analyzing existing data.
