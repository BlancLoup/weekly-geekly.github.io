nanoCAD Heating 1.0 Beta - a new solution in the product line based on nanoCAD

Dear Colleagues,
Today we released a new software product, which was preceded by a year of development - nanoCAD Heating 1.0. While in beta mode. But absolutely free.


‚ÄúA huge amount of work preceded the release of this product: we understood the need for a solution, but we also understood the amount of research needed to create a truly useful program,‚Äù said Denis Ozhigin, director for strategic development of Nanosoft CJSC. - Now we have taken the first step - version 1.0 is available to all interested users. We are counting on active feedback, which is being transformed into new opportunities for a unique solution for designing heating systems for buildings according to national standards. ‚Äù

Therefore, the goal of this release is to collect as much feedback from users as possible. At the moment, there are not so many solutions for heating design, and even inexpensive ones - generally count on fingers. With nanoCAD Heating, we want to change the situation and provide domestic designers with a good tool for everyday work that maximizes the needs.



What is now in nanoCAD Heating?
In short, this is a three-dimensional drawing machine with the ability to automatically receive specifications, axonometry and design of drawings. It can be said - this is a small BIM within the framework of one specialization (although I understand that BIM is a more complex concept). More precisely, at the moment the functionality of the program provides:
  • drawing floor plans of heating systems;
  • generation of axonometric schemes of heating systems;
  • equipment specification generation;
  • generation of the list of heating devices;
  • the formation of a three-dimensional model of the heating system.

At the moment, in nanoCAD Heating there are no calculations and there is no integration with calculation programs - this is work for the future. But nanoCAD Heating is already a good automation tool for people who draw the Heating section with their hands using blocks and primitives - for example, at the stage of obtaining verified error-free specifications, which are automatically generated from a single model in nanoCAD Heating.

How to get a license?
As with all Nanosoft software products - if there is a registration at nanocad.ru, then in the My Account in the Get license section you must select nanoCAD Heating. Next, select the Get button - and you will be allocated a serial number for installation. After installing the program at your workplace, go through the Registration Wizard and get the license file linked to a computer.

The distribution kit of the program can be downloaded both on the site nanocad.ru (http and ftp), and on rutracker.org .
