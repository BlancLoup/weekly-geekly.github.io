About moving

The one who glances under the cat is waiting for a dramatic story about how in Russia (specifically - in the city of Obninsk, Kaluga region) they move from one office to another. The main goal was to move with the least possible loss for workers. And as quickly as possible.

December 25 (-16 days)
I manage to order in Moscow box and everything you need for installation. I run, sticking my tongue out, shopping, buying everything on the list. From my mobile, I take pictures of the graphics of the stores, in order to be able to buy something if it is needed.

Approximately 60 meters of power cable, 100 meters of a telephone cable, 160 meters of a network cable go into the purchase. 10 outlets for the phone, 10 outlets for the network, 14 outlets for electricians. 34 meters of boxes in the amount. On 30 pieces of network ends, caps. Crimping tools, screwdrivers, screws, etc.

Employees in the office should pack at this time, leaving only furniture and equipment.
