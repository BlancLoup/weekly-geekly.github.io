Passing the interview. Amazon's Legendary Books

Hi, Habr!

And let's talk about one of the legends of Amazon - the book Elements of Programming Interviews: The Insiders' Guide .



EPI was released in 2012, and its ratings are still incredibly high, and not only in the interview section.
# 1 in Books> Computers & Technology> Programming> Algorithms> Data Structures
# 2 in Books> Computers & Technology> Programming> Software Design, Testing & Engineering> Software Development
# 3 in Books> Business & Money> Job Hunting & Careers> Interviewing

This book contains 300 tasks (and detailed solutions) that are most popular when interviewing leading IT companies.

The book begins with the analysis of data structures, algorithms and solving the most popular tasks. Further, more complex issues such as parallelism, the development of algorithms and systems, performance, and even discrete mathematics are considered.

In addition to programming tasks, EPI provides information on general interviewing issues - major errors, interview strategies, career prospects, best financial conditions, etc.

This book is not like a trivial "Reshebnik", but a unique reference book for all sections and areas of programming.

The authors have a wonderful website , the book is constantly being finalized and improved.

And the question, in fact, is simple: do you want to see this book in Russian?
