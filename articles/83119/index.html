Amplifier for 500 rubles. Do it yourself

It was a long time ago, I decided to write just now.

Were available speakers S-30B.
I wanted to make home acoustics to my computer. Not enough amplifier.

I went shopping. The lower price of small-capacity amplifiers / receivers (30 watt speakers at the peak) is about 6,000 rubles.
I decided to do it myself.

Let's start.

Lamp - for audioflov, and a lot of problems. Transistor - for a long time to adjust, and it is necessary to select the element base precisely, for errors in parts sold in markets and in stores sometimes reach 10%
As a result, the choice fell on the chip.
Googling and reading the forums chose the chip TDA2050.

Inclusion scheme:



As you can see, the body kit is minimal.
They also need radiators.

He took 500 rubles. and moved in the direction of radio markets and shops.

The entire element base cost a little more than 100 rubles.

Needed the same power source. I will not go into details of nutrition. Diode bridge + output Conder - about another 100 rubles.

Radiators bought on the market from the old first stump 2 pieces of 20 rubles.

+ bought more stuff, which will be lower.

Came back home. It is necessary to make seals. I decided not to bother for a long time. He took out a piece of textolite from the bins, marked out the tracks, drew them by hand with a marker, which is on the photo below.



Corroded with ferric chloride (again, he took from old stock).

Signet decided not to drill, but to solder parts on top of the track.

To the power supply a little more responsible. Here, the textolite is still drilled, because the conders are large and if you solder them from above, the mechanical load on the copper foil is large, it can peel off. Well, + stabilizers on + -12 volts just in case, for the future I attached on ordinary zener diodes + a transistor with a resistor.

About the transformer later.

All canopy connected. Connect the sound source and speakers. Popper self-excitement. Beeps and that's it. Soldered straight to the feet of the food mikruh Conder film on 100n. The excitement is gone. Chased an hour - it works.
