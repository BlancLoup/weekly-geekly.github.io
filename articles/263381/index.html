Registration for MBLTdev 15 - international conference of mobile developers has begun

We took off our cap, grew a beard, changed black to white, chose the date and place of the Second International Conference for Mobile Developers MBLTdev . Add to calendars!

November 17, Digital October Center, Moscow




The MBLTdev conference, as before, will bring together foreign and Russian gurus of mobile development, from the Philippines to the USA. The program will be divided into several streams: hardcore presentations and seminars for mid + developers and practical presentations for mobile development managers and IT directors.

Program

Speakers who have confirmed their participation at the moment:

- Mike Ash, iOS Developer, Plausible Labs
- Chris Eidhof, iOS developer, founder of the objc.io blog
- Phillip Connaton, iOS developer, Runkeeper
- Jasper Blues, Founder, Typhoon
- Kevin Goldsmith, Vice President Development, Spotify
- Caesar Valiente, Android developer, Wunderlist
- David Grandinetti, iOS Developer, Yahoo Finance
- Konstantin Kichinsky, Technical Evangelist, Microsoft
- Alexander Cherny, iOS developer, e-Legion
- Dmitry Soshnikov, Strategic Technologies Expert, Microsoft Russia

We continue to actively recruit speakers, this list will be updated every week. Follow the news.

Anticipating all the questions, the full program will be available no earlier than a month before the MBLTdev date. As the speakers decide on the topics of their speeches and send us abstracts, we will publish this information in groups on VK.com , Facebook and Twitter .

In the meantime, we are pleased to announce the first seminar on cross-platform development and development for Windows 10 from the Microsoft Russia strategic technology expert Dmitry Soshnikov . This seminar is aimed at Android developers.

How was it last year

A detailed report with the program, photos and video recordings of speeches by MBLTdev 14 speakers is available here .

Please note that until August 19 you can purchase an Early Bird ticket with a 50% discount , details on the official website .

Application for reports

If you have an interesting experience in mobile development that you want to talk about in public, send your applications to ot@mbltdev.ru. In the letter, indicate the preliminary topic, the key theses of the presentation and answer the question: what practical benefits will the participants get after hearing your report?

Applications will be reviewed by the organizers and then put to the general vote.

Conference organizers: e-Legion and RAEC
General Partner: Microsoft Russia
