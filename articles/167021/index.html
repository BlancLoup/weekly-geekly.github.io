Google Apps for Business is easier and more accessible.



In January 2013, REG.RU was the first in Russia and the CIS countries to start distributing Google Apps for Business. Now on the REG.RU website, you can order, renew, configure and pay for any of the apps provided by Google Apps (Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, etc.). In addition, you can try out the 30-day free version of Google Apps for Business, immediately switch from it to the paid extended version, and even link a Google corporate account to your domain. To understand this variety of possibilities, a specially developed REG.RU interface helps to make the process of working with Google Apps for Business as easy as possible.



3 tariff plans are available to users: trial, flexible and annual. The prices do not differ from those offered on the original Google Apps website . In addition, on the REG.RU website, when ordering a service, you can specify the planned number of users and the system will automatically calculate the cost.



You can migrate from the free version of Google Apps to a paid extended or convenient extension of the latter right there - for this you need to enter Transfer Token.



Directly to the Google Apps for Business domain is tied in two ways: directly from the interface of the service order or from the Personal Account. In the first case, this is the next step after choosing a tariff plan.
