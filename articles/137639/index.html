Unicode 6.1 standard released, 732 new characters added

On January 31, a new version of the Unicode standard 6.1.0 was officially introduced; this is a minor release compared to Unicode 6.0.0 ( October 2010 ).

The latest version includes 732 new characters , including seven new scripts: Chinese, Asian and African. Among 110 thousand Unicode characters, you can find glyphs for every taste, for example, here is the LOVE HOTEL (U + 1F3E9) symbol:



Unicode 6.1 adds support for 200 variators for Emoji icons (emoticons), so now these icons can be described both in black and white (text style) and in color (emoji style).

Among other improvements in Unicode 6.1: two new line break properties have been added for Japanese and Hebrew texts; improved segmentation of texts for Thai, Lao and similar languages; improved processing of Chinese texts due to more accurate mapping between simplified and traditional Chinese characters.
