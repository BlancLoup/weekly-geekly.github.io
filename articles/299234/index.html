Countdown counters
+ 10-15% conversion

Let's start with the most well-known tool - the countdown counter, which creates a sense of time pressure and forces the client to make a purchase decision faster. Internet marketers can sigh and say that they personally immediately leave the site, seeing such a counter. But let's not judge the audience on their own. The tool is so effective that it was picked up by email-marketing - the counters report days and hours before the end of the campaign in letters. A new round of development counters - agile-timer, which counts the time since the opening of the letter .



Using such a tool, you need to be honest with your customers. If you reduce the price for the promotion period, then you should not raise it on the eve, as shops often do on the eve of Black Friday. Many major brands have already lost confidence in themselves, using such tricks.

The counter on the site can be implemented using an html-widget for your content management system (CMS) or special services, for example, free and flexible megatimer.ru .



Callback widget (callback service)
+ 70% conversion

When choosing a product or service, customers study several offers at once. The client always has clarifying questions, and who will resolve them first, he will get more chances to close the deal. Here, as in the Wild West, you are either quick or dead. A callback widget is the most efficient way to communicate.

Often, for the sake of experiment, visitors to the site enter their phone number to check if the company really will call them back so quickly. And when the bell rings, it produces a stunning effect on the customer.



Behind the seeming simplicity is a serious technology. As soon as the customer left the phone, the system calls the sales manager, and when he picks up the phone, the customer calls. When the client is on the line, the system connects them to a general conversation.

The system has many nice bonuses to communication efficiency. First, the assessment of the effectiveness of sales managers.



Secondly, analytics promotion channels.



And, thirdly, ease of installation. A widget is an html-code that is installed on the site in three minutes. Pozvonim.com has detailed video instructions for all types of CMS .

Social evidence

The level of confidence in reviews is 12 times higher than the description of the product. To begin, give customers the opportunity to leave feedback on your product or service. Most commercial CMSs have easily customizable plugins that allow you to display customer reviews, edit fields, assign ratings to a product, etc. For example, it looks like a plugin of reviews in the Bitrix management system.



But there are offers on the market for those who do not use commercial CMS - for example, the service SprosiKupi.ru , which not only collects and broadcasts reviews on the site, but also encourages buyers to share their impressions. Pop-up window

This method can be called the "last hope" of a commercial site. It is worth it to use it as a last resort - when you are sure that the person who has come in is not here by chance, and you have something to offer. Some online stores, for example, offer promotional codes for a discount.



In order not to irritate the audience, the appearance of pop-up can be customized. Pop-up can be implemented using special widgets for different CMS or using program code.
