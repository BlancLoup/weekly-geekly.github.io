October 23 in Moscow will be a presentation of the new smartphone Edelweiss.

Specifications
The device was created in Japan by Sharp.
* GSM 850/900/1800/1900 MHz, WCDMA (UMTS) 850/1900/2100 MHz, GPRS, EDGE;
* Dimensions: 115 x 56 x 12.3 mm, weight: 130 g;
* touch screen: 3.5 inches LCD (FWVGA) with tactile feedback, support for 262 thousand colors and a resolution of 854 x 480;
* memory: 8 or 16 GB;
* two processors: 1. Texas Instruments OMAP 3; 2. BB
* 3.2 megapixel autofocus camera with triple (digital) zoom;
* built-in GPS-module;

Software
