Knowledge of HCD (Human-centred Design)

At the beginning of this summer, we shared the materials of our lecture on Microsoft .NET Architecture Day. We talked about the new ISO standard "Human-centred Design" .

Over the summer, we have developed, in continuation of this lecture, a whole CUP (Certified Usability Professional) certification program [announcements of the program can be found here and here ].

Today on the Day of Knowledge, we offer everyone to test their knowledge of HCD . This test is designed as a screening test for CUP participants.

As you remember, HCD describes the process and principles, not buttons and molds.

As approbation, usabilityLab employees passed the test. Our project director Andrei Ponomarev received the highest score - 36 out of 40 !

We decided to establish a prize for the one who beat Andrei's record: the first one who scores more than 36 points on the test will get the opportunity to study under the CUP program for free!

PS Tip - watch the video (first link). All test questions are covered in this lecture.
