What are we planning to do using this money?


This is part of the sponsorship agreement with Google about our commitments.

  • Increasing the number of children's educational institutions of the project partners to 100.
  • Development of teaching aids for the study:
    1. visual programming on Scratch and Code.org for junior and high school;
    2. Processing (java, python, ruby) for high school;
    3. basic web development for high school;
    4. basics of robotics (Arduino, Littlebits, quadcopters) for high school.
  • Conducting quarterly events to share the experience of volunteer teachers in Moscow and St. Petersburg.
  • Conducting a children's conference on programming and game creation.
  • Conduct All-Russian gamified competition for programming with online and offline stages.
  • Creating a website and conducting a set of communication activities in online media and social media to attract new volunteers and teachers.
