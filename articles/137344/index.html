Practical work


The final stroke of the overall picture of the day was a workshop - an experimental format. The idea was as follows: to use the collective intelligence of the speakers and experts of the seminar to solve practical cases of the seminar participants. To this end, all the guests, along with an advertising razdatka, were given questionnaire questionnaires, which they could fill out during the seminar and pass to the organizers during the second break. The speakers, therefore, had time to look at the questions and get ready to answer.
