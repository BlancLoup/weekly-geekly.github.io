eBay collaborates with the Chinese search engine Baidu

The Chinese search portal Baidu and the resource Eachnet, which is a branch of the eBay auction in the Middle Kingdom, concluded an agreement on mutually beneficial cooperation, reports Reuters .

As part of the contract, Baidu will promote the services of the online payment system PayPal Beibao, as well as search the auction pages Eachnet. It is expected that this will help eBay to strengthen its position in China, somewhat shaken over the past year due to serious competition in the face of the online auction Alibaba.com, working with Yahoo !, and Tencent Holdings, enlisted by Google.

New ebay and Baidu alliance plans to begin active operations early next year.
