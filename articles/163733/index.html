The Open Republic portal began work in Bashkortostan

In the wake of the emergence of many websites helping people to correctly make a complaint or petition to state authorities (RosYam, RosZhkh, etc.), the emergence of electronic receptions at all authorities, the launch of the Open Government, in the Republic of Bashkortostan the Open Republic portal began to work " .
The goals and objectives of the project are defined as follows:

The objectives of the project "Open Republic" are
  • creation of mechanisms of public control over the activities of regional authorities, including through an independent assessment of the effectiveness of management decisions based on open data;
  • exclusion of conditions for systemic official abuses by revealing information about the activities of government bodies;
  • the creation of conditions for the free exchange of information between the state, organizations and citizens of the republic (the creation of the information environment);
  • improving the competitiveness of the region in terms of the quality of life of the population and the creation of comfortable conditions for the development of enterprises in the region by expanding access to various information;
  • increasing the investment attractiveness of the Republic of Bashkortostan through the discovery of information in the interaction of authorities and organizations in various fields of activity;
  • creating conditions for the development of civil projects aimed at creating a public good.
The objectives of the project "Open Republic" are:
  • providing public access to these authorities of the Republic of Bashkortostan, which are not a state secret;
  • simplifying interaction with the power of the region;
  • ensuring the transparency of the work of government structures.
The implementation of the project "Open Republic" will allow:
  • increase constructive civic engagement in addressing issues and problems at all levels: from local to regional;
  • to make the work of power structures more transparent for citizens and business, flexible, accountable and efficient;
  • to improve the processes of rendering state and municipal services, the processes of managing state and municipal resources, including through innovation;
  • stimulate the development of the information technology industry in the region due to the large-scale implementation of software products in the work of government departments and municipalities, streamlining the work processes of government bodies, as well as improving computer literacy among government officials and the population
