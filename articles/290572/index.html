Facebook improves working conditions for freelancers

Facebook has announced an increase in the minimum wage, as well as benefits to freelancers. So, now it is proposed to increase the amount of payment to $ 15 per hour, plus provide at least 15 days of paid vacation per year, reports Phys.org . At the same time, employees who cannot go on paid leave to care for a child are paid $ 4,000 one-time.

Changes in working conditions have already entered into force for those freelancers who work in the company's California office.

In this state, from January next year, the minimum hourly wage increases to $ 10, rising by $ 1. On average in the United States this figure is $ 7.25.

In addition to Facebook, Apple, Microsoft, and several other companies have announced improvements in working conditions for their freelancers.
