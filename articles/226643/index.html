The use of 3D printers in the jewelry industry: gold products from the Nervous System



Nervous System is well known in the world of 3D printing. The last project of this company was the Kinematics project, which consisted in printing a compact system, which could then be decomposed, getting a certain thing larger than the printer allows with the usual printing method.

Now this project, or rather, the idea embedded in it, was used to create a gold bracelet. Of course, standard 3D printers do not allow working with gold, so the company used the direct laser sintering of metal (DMLS) in the jewelry industry.

This method, in turn, was developed by Cooksongold .
