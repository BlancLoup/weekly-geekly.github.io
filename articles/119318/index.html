Communicate with those nearby in your iPhone

Hi, Habr!
You may have heard about the conference called South by Southwest (SXSW) , which takes place annually in the United States. It once announced foursquare and twitter. This year, the technological part of SXSW was remembered by the explosion of start-ups of mobile applications implementing group text communication: GroupMe, Youbongo, Beluga and others.
It so happened that at about the same time we began to develop our iPhone application that allows us to communicate with strangers who are nearby. Now we understand that ideas are really in the air :)
Today, for the first time, we publicly announce our application here - and we ask you to evaluate what we have done, comment on the idea and help with advice. We really need and appreciate your opinion - we want to catch up and overtake American startups!
More about the application:
Squeek is a combination of iPhone application and web chat . Why is it needed? Imagine that you miss a lecture, or sit alone in a cafe. Open the application, go to the uni room or cafe, and communicate with those who are sitting in the same place. To discuss a boring teacher or to meet a pretty girl on the contrary - that's why :)

We made a web chat specifically for those who do not have an iPhone: it works on Android, checked :) And we also need facebook authorization - to cut off spam and advertising.
We will be very grateful for feedback. Fingers crossed - first appearance :)

PS By the way - the application is absolutely free. Therefore, there are no barriers to download ;)
