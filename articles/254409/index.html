Payler 2.0 - work on the creation of a new site

Hi, Habravchane! We have not written anything for a long time, but we worked a lot. Today we want to open the veil of secrecy and show you the working outline of the interfaces of the new Payler site. In the meantime, with the speed of flight of the bumblebee the designer hand is conjuring, here are three interesting facts:

  • In developing the structure of the future site, we decided to combine all our products and services in it under the common name Payler;
  • The designer and art director are united in one person, so we abandoned prototyping and immediately started drawing the interfaces, which saved us a lot of time and effort;
  • From the first day, a new website is created with an eye to future seo optimization (because it is not everywhere and not always, as it turned out).

The main page from the extensive landing has evolved into the mission control center:



The blocks have not yet been firmly established in their places, but the idea is clear - when developing the concept of a new site, we tried to simplify navigation through sections as much as possible, bringing the main elements to the main one.

By the way, about the sections. Initially it was meant that when you hover the cursor on the "Features" section, a long list will appear, and when you click on a subsection of which, a page like this opens:



However, looking at the question critically, we refused to be overloaded with text blocks. On the last sketches, the page consists of a header, a heading, three mini-blocks with a summary of the main information, one seo-optimized text and a basement.

Now on our site there is no obvious section with documentation , but in the new version of the site we will pay special attention to it - all information about the working conditions and connection, integration capabilities and requirements for the connected site will be collected and structured in one place.

By the way, the updates affect not only the site, but also the merchant's personal account . There will be a round-the-clock chat with technical support, the ability to manage several companies in one account, a marketing module, the possibility of personalized anti-fraud settings. And we translate a personal account into English - it will be available very soon - Chinese and Arabic . But more on that later. :)

By the way, we now have two blogs - here and on the Megamind . We wrote this post about our latest achievements in the field of development, and shared our experience of entering the European market here .

Friends, we will be happy to hear your wishes and comments on the functionality of the site Payler. What would you like to see there? What information do you think should be placed on the main page?

Stay in touch!
