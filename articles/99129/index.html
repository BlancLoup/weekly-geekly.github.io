Free payment - how it works

About a month ago, a new service appeared in QIWI Wallet - Free payment , which allows you to pay for services or goods, knowing only the bank details of the supplier, make tax and other payments to the budget, pay for paid services of state and municipal enterprises.

After listening to the first comments and looking at the market, it was decided to improve the payment process: templates of common payments were added, with clear tips.

So far, however, only one, but very much in demand: payment of transport tax .
Here's what it looks like.

On the QIWI Wallet website, select the Free Payment provider, and enter the account number indicated on the receipt: 40101810500000010010:


After that, a form appears, where all details are entered with the receipt. If the user does not know what he needs to enter, he can always click on the question mark and see a hint:


That is, now there is no need to go anywhere and stand in lines to pay the debt to the state.
This is the main plus :)

And minuses can be noted:
Payment does not go online, but by transferring money - through a bank, which takes 1-3 business days.
Commission for transfer: 3% and 10 rubles.
But it is convenient, because there are more pluses.

Now only one template has been added. In this regard, I would like to ask you, what other templates need to be added without fail? Tell us if it's not difficult.
