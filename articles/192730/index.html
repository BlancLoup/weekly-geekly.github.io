Sony PRS-T3 - a new reader with a higher resolution display

Following Amazon.com, Sony announced a new generation of readers. Sony PRS-T3 will be equipped with a display with higher resolution and a protective cover.
Sony is a leader in many technology industries, but for a long time it was inferior to Amazon in terms of e-books. Sony PRS-T3 should solve this problem.



The new reader will be sold along with the integrated case, the Chinese brothers will not be able to weld on the sale of additional accessories.
Sony decided to leave the physical buttons and did not succumb to the tendency to design devices under the ‚Äútouch-screen only‚Äù design. The integrated backlight is also missing. Those interested can buy the option with a built-in lamp. Sony has remained true to tradition. This is a classic reader. At the same time, the lack of built-in lighting allows you to win in weight.



Display size remains the same: 6 inches. Resolution increased: 1024x768. This resolution improves the clarity of not only text, but also pictures.



Another interesting update is the fast charge feature. Reader can be charged in 3 minutes. This is enough to read the whole book. The function is relevant if you are going to leave the house, but forgot to charge the reader.



Sales should begin within a few weeks. The price of the device should remain similar to the old model.
