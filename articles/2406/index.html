Blogger out of beta

The creators of the Blogger blogger engine announced the official release of the new version and the end of the beta era.

Bloggers now have the option of using the drag-and-drop feature when editing templates and assigning tags to posts. In addition, the Dashboard has been updated to provide quick access to basic management tools.

Also announced improved support with RSS feeds - in particular, the compatibility with RSS 2.0 and Atom 1.0 has been established, reports Blogger Buzz . In this case, all users can choose - stay with them on the old engine or switch to a new one.
