Stages of research


Further, for each sample, a study was conducted. To begin with, the main elements of web pages were identified that are present on all sites of the sample (for each element a color is defined, with the help of which this element will later be applied to the color map of the template):
  • navigation bar (red);
  • news feed (pink color);
  • school logo or its name (blue);
  • the main graphic image describing an educational institution or its activity in order to attract the attention of users (green);
  • contact information (black);
  • communication resource with third-party resources and social networks (yellow).


The next step is to determine the position of each selected object of the main page on the screen. To do this, translucent shaded areas were superimposed on the white sheet in the places where this object was placed on sites. Subsequently, all these locations were combined on one sheet and an overall picture of the placement of page objects of the web resource was obtained.
After this study, it was found that the sample according to the ranking of the best universities in Russia and the rating of the companies Mail and Rambler give the same results, so the decision was made: the results of the study should consider only the template schemes based on samples of the best foreign and Russian universities. Research results


Based on the results obtained, it can be noted that the schemes in terms of the location of objects give approximately the same result. The difference in terms of location of objects should be noted the ambiguity of the location of the contact panel on the websites of Russian universities, as well as the almost complete absence of links to social resources. The main difference is that foreign universities have a site in the center of the screen with a fixed width, and most Russian universities tend to stretch the site on the whole screen.

Results

From myself I just want to add that for the further design of the template for the developed web resource, I used the template scheme obtained from a sample of the best foreign universities, as this option more suited the customer and is more convenient for display on mobile platforms.
