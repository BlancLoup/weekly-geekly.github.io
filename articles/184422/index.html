Through thorns to Armikrog


What is Neverhud once again do not have to tell. This year, the Habrasoobschestvo named the game the best among the quests, and in the list of the best games of all times and peoples, it took 5th place, overtaking Doom and Civilization.

In 2007, the news was spread on the Internet that a plasticine cartoon based on Neverhud would soon be removed, but there was no continuation. Last year, conversations began that the third part of the game would be created, but again everything died down. And now, the charter to fight with the owners of the company decides to remove the new clay quest with new characters.



New characters are:
- space explorer Tomminawt (Tommynaut, translated by GreatRash ),
- and his dog Bik-Bik (Beak-Beak).

The creators have promised to draw a comic with new heroes and even published one page about what happened right before the game began:



And, of course, Terry Taylor , the same musician who wrote and performed the entire soundtrack for Neverhud, was attracted to the creation of the game.
 

About the amount of donations and that you can get for it


In my opinion, the creators have come up with too many gradations, it could be easier. The most popular was the amount of $ 20, for which they offered the opportunity to download a legal game and that's it. But the limit of this amount has now been exhausted. Here is what the creators offer to the current accountants:

- from $ 5 to $ 25 - just morally support the creators. They will give a wallpaper with a picture of a worm Jimmy.
- $ 25 - wallpaper with a worm and a game!
- $ 30 - all that on top, and 100 pages of pictures about the country Armikrog in PDF and 3 tracks of Terry Taylor.
- $ 35 - everything on top, and 24 pages of a comic book about Armikrog in PDF, and a 20-minute documentary.
- $ 45 - everything on top, and the whole soundtrack for the game.
- $ 50 - everything on top, and 50 pages of sketches and a 60-minute documentary.
- $ 60 - everything on top, and a film with commentaries of the developers to the game and tracks with dance mixes for the songs of Terry Taylor.
- $ 75 - all that on top, and 100 pages of the script to Newhud in PDF and 24 pages of comics to Armikrog on paper.

I will not list all of them, but I will note some of the other suggestions:
- $ 130 - a disc with the game, a disc with a soundtrack in the box with a description, and with an additional level of the game, out of 100 offers are all sold out,
- $ 3,500 - an opportunity to talk on Skype with the creators and tell them your ideas about the world of Armikrog, and these ideas should be displayed in the game, out of 5 all are sold,
- $ 10,000 - the opportunity to come to the studio, create a piece of animation that will enter the game. Of the three, one place is still free. Hurry up! If I had 10 thousand free, I would buy my excursion for my child, he adores Glinka.
