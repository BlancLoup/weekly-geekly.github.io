Google CodeF in Russia!

Nina Tseplyaeva, recruiter for engineering positions

Dear girls,

This year we have prepared for you a new and very interesting educational program - Google CodeF, which has already been launched in Zurich, London and Krakow. Of the more than 500 applicants, about 100 participants were selected, 60 of whom won the right to participate in the CodeF mentor program, and some of them at the end of the program successfully completed our interviews and became employees of Google.

What is this program?
Within 8 weeks, the candidates selected by us will pass practical classes under the guidance of mentors - engineers of the Moscow office of Google, who will be happy to share their experience with you and tell more about the company. Mentors will help you identify and develop your strengths, outline development directions, teach you how to plan and use resources as efficiently as possible. Together you will train to solve complex problems, write beautiful code, create an informative resume, and also improve the skills of interviewing.
