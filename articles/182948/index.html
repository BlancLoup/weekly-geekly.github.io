Dell Alienware M18X laptop video review



The Dell Alienware M18X is Dell's flagship gaming notebook for enthusiastic gamers. This huge device is equipped with a display with a diagonal of 18.4 inches and a resolution of 1920x1080. Works "alien" based on the Intel Core i7 Extreme 3920XM processor with additional features for overclocking. For graphics performance, there are as many as two Nvidia GeForce GTX680M video cards operating in SLI. The amount of RAM is 32 GB, and for storing data using a tandem of SSD for 512 GB and HDD for 1 TB (with the possibility of installing the third drive).

Also noteworthy is the keyboard with adjustable color backlighting and additional keys for creating macros. It would not be superfluous to note the truly comprehensive set of ports and interfaces, of which the ExpressCard-54 slot, the HDMI video input and four audio jacks (microphone + 5.1 analog multichannel sound) are worth mentioning separately.
