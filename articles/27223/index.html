Sipgate - VoIP service and SIP client for iPhone

Sipgate has announced the release of its own SIP client, which adds VoIP support for the popular iPhone communicator.



Sipgate client allows you to make and receive calls using the capabilities of any SIP-based VoIP provider at any time when the client is within range of a Wi-Fi network.

To start working with Sipgate, it is enough to register on the company's website, after which you will receive your own SIP ID in the system and instructions for downloading the client to iPhone. Once logged in to your personal account on the Sipgate website, you will be able to configure access to a VoIP provider whose services you are going to use.

Sipgate is currently in beta status.
