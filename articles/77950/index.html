Virtualka on a smartphone? Soon!

According to Computerworld , VMWare is working on a new software package for its software, this time for smartphones.

The new VMWare will be able to run a second operating system on smartphones and communicators and switch between them with one button. Among the technical problems were not called the hardware parts of smartphones (most of them are already close to computers in terms of power), but the processing of button presses - which OS should process what? Who is responsible for calls, etc.

Microsoft representatives said they did not see the point in this whole undertaking, however, they allowed VMWare to experiment.

The launch of Windows Mobile and Android on the Nokia N900 was demonstrated.
