Announcement of the .NET conference DotNext 2016 Piter



On Friday, June 3, DetNext 2016 Piter .NET conference will be held in St. Petersburg. This will be the fifth DotNext conference, the third in St. Petersburg. The Radisson Pulkovskaya Hotel will host the conference for the second time in a row.

What have we saved for you this time? As always, the coolest topics from the coolest speakers.

In particular, the conference will speak:
  • The authors of the coolest books about .NET Dino Esposito and Sasha Goldshtein ;
  • Developers of ReShaper and a new IDE for C # called Rider ;
  • Microsoft employees, both evangelicals and engineers, and the steepest
  • The best habathomators , leaders of .NET-hub;
  • The best speakers of previous DotNext conferences;
  • Organizers and speakers of the SPb .NET Community ;
  • Traditional sponsors DotNext - JetBrains and Futurice;
  • One of you - we are waiting for applications from you!




We are most interested in the following .NET themes:
  • Developer tools and frameworks;
  • CLR internals;
  • CoreCLR;
  • Performance & GC;
  • Cross-platform Development;
  • Desktop applications development;
  • Mobile applications development;
  • Automated Testing;
  • Dependency Management;
  • Build Tools;

Participation is paid, registration is open.
