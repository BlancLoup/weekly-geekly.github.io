How to win in Vkontakte contests? Another approach



Hi, Habr!
To write this post in many ways we were prompted by one article published a couple of weeks ago on Habr√©. Namely, " How to win in the contests repost Vkontakte "?

The author believes that the answer is obvious.
‚ÄúWe must participate in all contests and according to the theory of probability, the more contests, the greater the chance to win at least something‚Äù

As a result of the experiment, the author managed to win several contests in which the prizes were cheap Chinese souvenirs. For many skeptical people, the question arises: are competitions being conducted by participants of social networks honestly? Let's think and describe this risk (the possibility of fraud).

The main type of selection of the winner is the selection using the program-selector. There are many modifications. The published contest informs the day when the winner will be selected and afterwards it places a screenshot from the screen on which the result of the selection is indicated.

- One screenshot?
- Yes, one screenshot.

- Can the dishonest organizer click "Choose a winner" in such a program until the participant he needs is selected?
- The opportunity is there.

- Will there be a registry of the number of winner selections in such a program?
- About such cases is not known.

As we can see, there are a lot of holes in the contests algorithm. The probability of winning something worthwhile drops sharply due to the risks of fraud, the probability of which increases with increasing motivation. Specialists in this field have described the so-called triangle in which such a situation may lead.



There are cases when the winners were chosen during a live broadcast on YouTube. Such methods also leave the possibility for scammers to influence the results of a competition by simulating the work of, for example, a random number generator on a fake website. Although it is much more difficult to do.

Reject - offer


A reasonable solution is to create certification centers for competitions in a social network. Application in which the organizer must record the mechanics of the competition at the time before its publication.

  1. The terms of participation
  2. The exact time to determine the winner (at this point, the program must choose the winner according to the conditions of participation).
  3. Publication of the results of competitions in the registry.

- Can the dishonest organizer choose the winner of the participant he needs?
- No, it is possible to make changes to the register of results only for administrators of the competition certification center.

The form of interaction with the certification center can also be implemented through the application in a social network, the ability to choose another winner - upon request with an indication of the reason. Registration in such a center will raise the popularity of the competition compared to the unregistered, with sufficient information on the function of the certification authority.
