Upload to YouTube from your site

YouTube Direct is a tool with which visitors from your site can add their content to YouTube.


What good can offer this service :
  • all for free - working with open-source YouTube API
  • possibility of free interface settings
  • users upload videos to YouTube without leaving your site
  • you can upload not only video, but also photo materials
  • you are moderating what is being downloaded from your site (without this option, the service probably could not exist)
  • in YouTube, next to the video there will be a link to the site from which it was downloaded

The platform was originally focused on media interaction with amateur reporters, but I think there are still many interesting ways to use it.
Read more on the project page .
