Official sales of iPad mini and iPhone 5 begin in Russia

Home sales of the iPhone 5 begins on the night of December 14 in some retail networks. The price of these devices became known shortly before the start of sales.


Prices for various models:
iPhone 5
  • 16-gigabyte version - 34,990 rubles
  • 32-gigabyte version - 39 990 rubles
  • 64 GB version - 44 990 rubles

iPad Mini
  • 16-gigabyte version - 13 990 rubles
  • 32-gigabyte version - 17 990 rubles
  • 64 GB version - 21 990 rubles

iPhone 5 in Svyaznoy:
  • 16-gigabyte version - 29 900 rubles
  • 32-gigabyte version - 34 900 rubles
  • 64-gigabyte version - 39 900 rubles

iPhone 5 Connected

iPad Mini will be available a little later, on the evening of December 14. Russian buyers will first have the opportunity to buy the version only with WiFi support, the option with the support of cellular networks will go on sale later .
The iPad Mini will go on sale at 3 retail chains: re: Store , M-Video , White Wind Digital .
IPhone 5 sales start at stores such as: Svyaznoy , re: Store , Beeline , Euroset , White Wind Digital .

A number of stores will begin various promotions to attract customers.

The Apple iPhone 5 smartphone differs from the previous model with an enlarged screen with a diagonal of 4 inches and a resolution of 1136x640 pixels, an aluminum case and 1 GB of RAM. The maximum resolution of the main camera is 8 MP. Despite the fact that iPhone 5 has support for 4G LTE networks, in networks of Russian operators it will only work in 3G mode.
