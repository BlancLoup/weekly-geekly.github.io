Samsung SPH-9200 competitor?


The network has information about the new ultra mobile computer from Samsung . Model Samsung SPH-9200 is made in a folding form factor, with a folding keyboard. Specifications Samsung SPH-9200 are:
- Operating system Windows XP Home edition
- 5-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 800 √ó 480 pixels
- Processor Via C7-M
- 512 MB of RAM
- 1,3-megapixel camera
- Slot for SIM-cards
- Wireless standards Wi-Fi, WiBro, HSDPA
- 2 USB, LAN, VGA ports
- Battery life with a standard battery up to 2.5 hours
- Battery life with optional battery up to 5 hours



So far, Samsung has not officially confirmed the information on the Samsung SPH-9200, so the details are preliminary. The timing of the release, as well as the estimated price is not yet known. It should be noted that this is not the first Samsung experience in devices of this kind. At the beginning of the year, the SPH-P9000 computer was introduced in South Korea. The novelty uses a different processor, more RAM, and instead of CDMA EV-DO, HSDPA support is provided. The latter allows us to hope that this model will appear outside the Korean market.
