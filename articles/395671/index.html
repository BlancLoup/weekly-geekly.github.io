Pac-man in a petri dish

A copy of the labyrinth from the world of the game Pac-Man with a size of less than a millimeter was placed in a favorable environment for microorganisms. Roles play the role of ghosts. The role of Pac-Man is assigned to Euglena Green (lat. Euglena viridis). According to the authors of the work, the labyrinth is much closer to the natural living conditions of protozoa and rotifers than ordinary Petri dishes . And the features of the relationship of hunters and their victims in this labyrinth reflect the real state of affairs.

According to Johannssen, an artificial two-dimensional environment is formed in Petri dishes, in which there are no obstacles, and microorganisms spread in any direction without restrictions. It is not possible to track the natural relationships of predators and preys in such conditions. Introducing an additional component in the form of a three-dimensional maze, experts forced the inhabitants to interact with the environment, and not just with each other. And the fact that this environment has taken the form of a maze from a popular game, according to scientists, will contribute to the popularization of science. The number of video views created by scientists is steadily increasing. It seems that scientists have achieved their goal.



Initially rotifers moved reluctantly and slowly. But after only a day, having become accustomed, they began to actively move through this labyrinth.
