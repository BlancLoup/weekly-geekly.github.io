Test Labs 2014. ONLINE conference for testers and test managers. June 28

The next Soft Labs series event will be held on Saturday, June 28, and will be devoted to automation and test management. Since in summer the motivation to spend a day in front of the monitor is sharply reduced, we decided to make this conference shorter (but not at the expense of the quality of the material and the intensity of the discussions).

We would like to present to your attention three stories based on our own experience in solving complex problems. One report for entry level specialists, one that will be useful to everyone, and one presentation for advanced masters:

  • From manual tests to integration systems. The story of one project . Speaker - Victor Belov;
  • Development through testing using the Google Test Framework . Speaker - Pavel Tsytovich;
  • Requirements in the form of test cases - one wine and three files in a row . Speaker - Alexey Lupan.


The conference starts at 13:00 Moscow time.

Participation conditions remain unchanged - admission is free , but you need to register on the event website. The conference is held on the Webex platform. Video recordings of the reports will be available for registered participants a week after the event.
