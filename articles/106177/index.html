Two more tablets based on Windows 7

Announced two tablet based on Windows 7, one of which has already gone on sale.

A popular blog, Engadget, showed CTgo's 2goPad SL10 tablet that went on sale.



The device comes with a special software shell on top of Windows. On the right side of the panel there are touch keys for easy navigation. The device is equipped with an accelerometer to determine the orientation of the screen and change the resolution. Comes with a case that can be used as a stand. The tablet has the following specifications: 10-inch multi-touch capacitive screen, Intel Atom N450 1.66GHz, 2GB RAM, 250 GB HDD, WiFi b / g / n, 2 USB, SD-card, camera, VGA output.

The price of the device is $ 499 on the manufacturer's website .

The second device is called Zoostorm SL8 it is produced by the same company Zoostorm .

It is expected to go on sale on December 1, 2010. The device will have the following characteristics: a large screen with a diagonal of 11.6 inches, 1.66GHz Intel Atom N450, 32 GB flash drive, 2 GB RAM, thickness - 14 mm. The manufacturer plans to release the next version of the device next year with 3G and GPS.

The device is positioned for the professional sector, business and organizations. The price for the model without 3G is 500 pounds ($ 790), with 3G - 580 pounds ($ 916)
