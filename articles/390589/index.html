Deodorant and socks for February 23? Enough tolerating this



Soon February 23, which means time to think about choosing a gift for your friend or male colleague (well, or female, why not?). Moreover, it is worth thinking about choosing a gift for your relative or loved one. In order to help determine the choice, your attention is invited to a small selection of several interesting devices.

Such a gift would be glad to any boy, man or grandfather. But, of course, you judge better.

Breathalyzer DrinkMate





We have already written about this useful sensor - it can be used to measure the level of alcohol in the blood with a fairly high level of accuracy. How does all this work? Here's what you need to do to make the device work:

1) Download the free application DrinkMate

2) Enable the DrinkMate application on your phone

3) Follow the instructions of the application, how to blow in DrinkMate

4) View measurement results.

The DrinkMate is connected via micro-USB, the power is supplied by a smartphone battery, and the Alcotester does not have its own battery.

Avantree PowerHouse - Desktop USB Charging Station





Avantree PowerHouse USB station is a great solution for those users who constantly need to charge the whole mass of their devices. The station can charge smartphones, tablets, regular phones. The maximum number of gadgets that can be charged at the same time - four.

This solution can be used both at home and for the office - came, put the gadget, charged.

Luminous BT Smart Bulb - smart light





An interesting solution is a smart LED light bulb that connects to a smartphone, through which the Luminous BT Smart Bulb is controlled. A special application allows you to control the brightness of the light bulb, change color, group light sources for simultaneous control.

The remote control of the Luminous BT Smart Bulb smart lamp is performed using Bluetooth wireless communication version 4.0 and later. The range of the radio signal is about 40 meters.

These bulbs are great for both home and office.

ASWY Air Speaker WA-102 wireless levitating speaker





The ASWY Air Speaker WA-102 speaker reproduces high-quality sound, while it looks quite interesting - during operation, the speaker hovers over a magnetic stand. This stand holds the speaker in the air.

The speaker is controlled using a smartphone - via Bluetooth 4.0. The battery life of the speaker is approximately 6 hours. The sound propagates uniformly in all directions, sound waves are not absorbed by the surface on which the ordinary speaker stands.

If you wish, the functions of ASWY Air Speaker can be controlled using the buttons - the button control panel is located on the device. Charging the speaker "over the air" charging wireless.

Rooti Labs CliMate is the world's smallest wireless weather station.





Climate is a weather station that allows you to monitor air humidity, temperature and the level of ultraviolet radiation. The latter function is very important in regions where the sun is very active. The station shows how much you can stay in the sun with the current level of UV radiation.

All data is saved, and then this information can be viewed on the mobile phone screen. Data transfer is via Bluetooth. CliMate is a wireless, wearable, small, lightweight and very smart device. This tiny tracker captures the place and time of data collection. Under normal use, the tracker can work for up to 6 months on a single round battery.
