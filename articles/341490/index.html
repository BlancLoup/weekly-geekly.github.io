Introducing free ordering and extended subscription for freelancers

We have changed the payment system. Now customers do not need to purchase a subscription, you can place orders for free . For freelancers, we have expanded payment options. You can purchase a separate response or subscription for a day, week, or month. So no longer have to choose between the desire to write a response to a single order and 500 rubles.



To customer


Place any number of orders for free. So far, we have used a paid subscription as a tool to ensure the quality of the content. This task remains paramount, while we have a need to increase the number of orders. To do this, we canceled the paid subscription, but we plan to introduce additional tools for placing orders. In the meantime, take advantage of the possibility of free placement and not naughty.

Free placement of orders is accompanied by two nuances in working with the site:

  • Orders older than one month are hidden in the archive if no performer is selected. This is done in order not to clog the tape if you have placed an order and, for example, have forgotten about it. From the archive it can be pulled out and placed again at any time.
  • Free viewing of contacts is available in the profiles of only responding freelancers. If you want to see the contact information of other users, you will have to buy any of the subscriptions available to the freelancer (the purchase of the response does not provide access to contacts).

If you bought a monthly subscription and published an order in the last 30 days, then the funds are returned to your balance. They can be used to pay for our other services.

Freelancer


For freelancers, we have expanded payment options. Until now, the minimum subscription was for a month and cost 500 rubles.

Now you can get one response . It is provided for those who have a narrow specialty and rarely come across profile orders. It costs only 99 rubles. Or buy a subscription to an unlimited number of responses that will be available during the day, week or month. The first two options cost 270 and 490 rubles and are provided for those who quickly find long-term orders. The cost of a monthly subscription increased to 1,200 rubles for those who regularly take short-term orders.

You can buy a response or a subscription directly on the order page or in the usual way through your personal account.


After the expiration of the subscription, correspondence with the customer remains open if you have already left a response to this order. Any subscription can be extended by day, week or month. Choose to taste.

All current paid subscriptions for freelancers retain their relevance for the paid period.

We are always very happy about the feedback. Email us if you have any ideas for improving the service.

Best regards, Freelance Team.
