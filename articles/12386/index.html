YouTube allows you to create custom players

YouTube has a new feature - custom player Custom Player . Now users can customize the player on their own. So, you can change the name of the player, the interface (either standard or with a list of similar clips on the right), the list of videos, as well as the color scheme so that the player harmonizes with the look of your site.

The ability to create a list of videos allows you to add to the player both your own videos and others (from the list of favorites or from a playlist).
