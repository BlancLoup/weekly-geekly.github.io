What can be done from old typewriters

There is such a young man named Jeremy Meyer . He is a sculptor. He is not known to the whole world and does not design monuments on the order of mayors of cities. But he spent many years creating several sculptures from old non-working typewriters. It sounds strange, but it looks very unusual.


The most interesting is that each sculpture was assembled without the use of any glue, welding was also not used. Jeremy just manually assembled and sealed all the details.


All the works of the talented sculptor will be present at the exhibition at the Art Museum of Nevada until December 2.
