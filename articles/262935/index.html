Miracle Puzzles - 10,000 puzzles in one application

In childhood, each of us loved to collect puzzles, and the more pieces there were - the more enthusiasm we felt, finally finishing our masterpiece! And how unpleasant it was to lose one of the pieces and reduce all the hard work to zero. But now such problems are solved simply by downloading the desired application to the phone or tablet. Some may say that electronic puzzles do not give the same emotions, in fact, such applications give more possibilities: you can choose the levels of complexity, collect exactly the pictures you like most, and the new "social features" allow you to collect puzzles the same together, as we used to do this with the whole family.



In itunes you can find quite a lot of applications for collecting puzzles, each of which has its own unique functionality.
Miracle Puzzles from XIMAD differ not only in a huge collection of pictures, but also in a social set of functions and many other services within the game.



The main distinguishing feature of the game is the presence of different levels of interaction.
Developers offer users daily bonuses that motivate to enter the game every day. In addition, several sets of puzzles with tasks appear several times a month, for the performance of which users also receive prizes. Often there are tournaments among players that motivate users to play more often, improving their skills and increasing their rating.



In Miracle Puzzles, each player can show their individuality by creating their own puzzles. The user selects a favorite picture or photo, loads it into the application and the puzzle is ready! If desired, the player can edit the image using the filter system. The field for experiments is quite wide! Photos and pictures can also be easily downloaded from the album using third-party services flickr, dropbox and facebook.



For connoisseurs of classic puzzles, the application presents an impressive collection with more than 10,000 puzzle pictures! For the convenience of users, all the pictures are divided thematically and organized into puzzle sets of 20-50 pieces each. Any player can easily find a package of puzzles that interests him using a keyword search. All puzzle packages are placed in the internal app store - Shop Puzzles. Most of them are free, the rest can be purchased for money or for in-game currency - coins that are credited for successfully completing puzzles.

Now you can not only share the collected puzzles with your friends through social networks, but also collect puzzles of your friends, and they can collect your puzzles, evaluate and comment on them.
A nice bonus is the ability to send your puzzle for consideration to the developers, and then it can fall into the category of "the best in the world" and will be available in the tape puzzles of users from around the world!



This innovation was successfully met by users and soon this feature will be implemented in other versions of Miracle Puzzles. In turn 1 will please fans of Android (versions for tablet and smartphone), but also on Win8 and Facebook is not far off. Literally a month later comes the version of the game for iPhone. This will definitely inspire fans of the iOS platform, as it will give them more leeway.
Follow the news in social support groups on Facebook and VKontakte .
