IT Music Fest: guitar instead of Claudia and music instead of code

On June 5, the first IT Music Fest took place in the Hophead pub in Moscow. The staff of Positive Technologies, who created the Lucy's First Job group, invited for the joint performance musical groups from Mail.Ru (GODCats), Kaspersky Lab (Out Of Office) and ABBYY (ABBYY Band). The special guest of the event was the electronic duet 4beatten, uniting the staff of two IT companies (PT and Luxoft). About what came out of this, read our report.



As befits a rock concert, IT Music Fest began 50 minutes after the time specified in the invitation. It was assumed that the musicians will distribute them to friends and colleagues, but many invited relatives and relatives. The event is important: almost all groups on this day performed for the first time at a real open festival, and not at the corporate party of their company.

- And where to sit down so that it was not loud? - they asked me at the entrance a couple at the age: the parents of one of the musicians.
- I'm afraid today it will be very loud everywhere ...

So it happened. People reached for the dance floor before the first band entered the stage.



Lucy's First Job Positive group started playing less than a year ago. Now consists of eight people. The team was so enthusiastic about creativity that after the first concert in Positiv they decided to organize a festival of musicians from IT companies. While convening participants and looking for a place, played at the sixth PHDays.

The pub crashed under the covers of System of a Down, Three Days Grace, The Offspring, and the support group did not catch their breath even during the melodious Jungle Drum and History Repeating. By the exit of Godcats the dance floor and the air in the close pub were red-hot.



Photo: Nastya Kozlova, VK.com

The backbone of Godcats ("Divine Seals"? "Seals from God"?) - These are artists from Mail.ru GROUP. They found each other within the same department. Creative artists were also talented musicians. The group began to rehearse right in the office, the repertoire was made up of covers of their favorite songs.

On stage, Godcats are really like cats: stylish and emotional. The crowd in front of the stage was raging under the tunes of Dani California, Personal Jesus, Enjoy the Silence, I Think I'm Paranoid.

Brutal Out of Office followed: two beards for four developers.



The Kaspersky group turned out to be older than all the speakers: they have been playing at corporate parties since 2011 and have already performed several times at external venues: at the Rain Major Club and twice at the Glastonbury Pub at the Rock Around Festival of Kaspersky music teams.
