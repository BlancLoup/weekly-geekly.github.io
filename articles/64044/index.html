Easy way to organize video surveillance

Sometimes there is a need to keep track of what is happening at home, but at the same time there is no desire to install expensive hardware or tinkering with setting up complex software. For example, you left the child alone at home or you have minor repairs done by builders, you wonder what your favorite dog / cat does in your absence.
To solve this problem, you just need a computer on the other side, a webcam and Skype.

The scheme of use is as follows: we call on your home Skype and he automatically picks up the phone and starts the video broadcast. Everything is very simple.
