Bluetooth Stereo Headset from Dadget

Convenience associated with the lack of wires has long attracted all gadget lovers. Headphones with occasions are good while you sit in order to listen to music. When moving or just doing something, the wire constantly strives to cling to hands or surrounding objects.
We got rid of the wire, but here another question arises - what to do if the phone rings? And for this to the headphones must be a microphone and control buttons.
In general, the picture is clear, the word for a specific implementation of the problem. (Recall how the implementation of smart watches was assumed a long time ago :) )

In this review, the bluetooth stereo headset FB0035 from the company Dadzhet will be tested for compliance with expectations.

Let's start with the kit.
Beautiful green transparent packaging. Through the transparent top you can see the headphones and the control panel.
A detailed manual (so small, what looks like an A8 format ... is there such a one?), A bag (it is always put on the headphones, but I never use it), a charging wire and a guarantee.

By the way, this is not written in the manual and it is not easy to guess right away.

1. Ergonomics.
In the ears sit well, keep well with attachments. Without nozzles strive to fall out - affects the mass of the built-in battery and the rest of the filling.
On the right earpiece is located the bottom of the microUSB connector. There is also a LED that blinks happily in blue to everyone around you that you are connected to music.

Replaceable liners no. I came up with regular earbuds, but there is something to buy or take off from other headphones.
Unfortunately, the dimensions of the headphones do not allow the use of headgear covering their ears. So in the winter it will be necessary to look for something more suitable.
The control panel under the right hand, thin flat wire thrown back and does not cling to clothes. Everything is good.
Headphones dimensions are a little confusing - at first it seems that they can catch on with something nearby :)


2. Sound.
The music is not the highest quality (music lovers are back in flight), which would be strange to expect from such an inexpensive model, but it is pleasant to listen. Audiobooks and radio plays do not cause any complaints, and the headphones I use most often to listen to the voice.
The volume reserve is large and you don‚Äôt have to push it to the maximum even in the noisiest places like the metro. Noise isolation is good, so be careful - it is better not to use when traveling on a bicycle or in places where it is important to hear the surrounding space!

3. Headset.
Here is a small bummer. It is difficult to use it in noisy places, it is necessary to constantly hold the microphone near the mouth. The only plus is that there is no need to climb the pocket in the phone.
Even in quiet places when walking, it is better to hold the microphone with your hand, otherwise the effect of approaching the deletion of the voice appears.
