HP Logo Evolution

The Internet is flooded with posts about how the logos of the most famous brands have changed over time. See, for example, here - agree, very entertaining.

And I must say that I was always saddened by the absence of the HP logo in these extensive lists. Gathering my strength, I launched my hands into the internal archives of the company, and now I can finally correct this injustice.

So, here is our story:

1939
Over the years since the release of the first product of the company (the sound frequency generator that Walt Disney used to create the fantasy cartoon) to the end of the 60s, HP used two equal logos. The first, consisting of simple lines, was easy to apply to the metal housings of various equipment. And the second one looked good in printed materials.
