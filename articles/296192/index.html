Founders of BlaBlaCar and Plantains - about working in Russia, about the future of ridesharing and unmanned aircraft

Last year, BlaBlaCar acquired the company "Plantain", which provided similar services in Russia and Ukraine. In Russia, the service was launched in February 2014. It was the first country outside of Europe where the company went.
