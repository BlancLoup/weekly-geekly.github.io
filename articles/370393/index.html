Baidu creates an open platform for robots



There are more and more players on the market for autonomous driving. Companies that are engaged in the creation of robomobiles, both hardware and software for such vehicles, are calculated in dozens. Among them are Chinese developers. Now a technogiant from China has announced itself - Baidu Corporation.

She announced her own mobile platform, which Baidu has made open. The initiative was called Project Apollo. According to the company's management, the project combines both hardware and software elements. Baidu is ready to provide its partners with solutions that allow vehicles to detect obstacles, avoid them, plan a route according to various factors.

So far this is only an announcement, access to Project Apollo will be available starting in July. First, the platform will be tested on cars that do not travel outside the test ranges. A little later this year, Project Apollo will be tested on ordinary public roads. According to representatives of the Chinese company, the goal of this project is to create a fully autonomous vehicle.
