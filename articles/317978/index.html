Successful companies in this market do not need additional investments, they have money, and they are happy to invest them in development and development, if they understand that this is really necessary.

For the last year and a half, we have been actively building iGaming expertise, which, I hope, will be officially turned into industrial practice in the near future. About regional differences


The main country for our work is the United Kingdom. Historically, it is the most stable, all the rules are clear there, the legislation rarely changes, and the situation with taxes is absolutely transparent. In addition to Great Britain, the most important regions for us are Western Europe: Sweden, Germany, Malta and Gibraltar. And, of course, we work with multinational corporations headquartered in the United States.

Regional differences depend on existing laws. Both online gambling and traditional land-based-casinos are allowed somewhere, only land-based is legal somewhere, in some countries iGaming can only deal with a regular casino. In America, these issues are regulated at the state level.

In Western Europe in recent years there has been a tendency to liberalize legislation regarding gambling. For example, in Switzerland, gambling is allowed only in land-based casinos, but discussions are already under way in parliament on the legalization of online activities.

In Sweden, the market is monopolized by the state, but even here in parliament there is already a paper on admitting foreign companies to the market. In general, from year to year the market expands, including all new countries. The industry provides a good income to the state, moreover, if this activity is regulated, there is practically no place for dishonest players in it.
