Briefly about the conference:

The All-Ukrainian Conference Steel Tambourine is a professional conference dedicated to system administrators. The conference is held quarterly, on the last Saturday of the month, in Lviv.

As part of the conference, there are speeches by prominent international experts. Professionals share their decisions and secrets of their skills. Learn about the intricacies of the life of system administrators, learn technologies and technological processes, gain experience and consider examples of solving complex and interesting problems in enterprises; jokes and contests, informal conversations and gifts - in all this you can participate in our conference.

We invite you to take part in the conference: system administrators, IT-specialists, everyone who wants to share their experience and knowledge in the field of IT, who wants to tell about the rake of implementing certain solutions (OS, network, etc.), share recipes for success in inventory, updating, software support and hardware.
