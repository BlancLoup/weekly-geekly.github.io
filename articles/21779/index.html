Russian-speaking Coworking Community OfficeShare.ru

Hello!
Coworking in Russia and the countries of the former USSR is alive.
We invite you to the Russian-speaking coworking community OfficeShare.ru



Now the project is at the initial stage, this is beta. We could not wait any longer, we will improve the resource on the go. There is no time, coworking is alive, he needs a home, now he has it.

Our goal.
- Collection of applications for coworking among Russian speakers around the world.
- Creating a base of coworking offices.
- Exchange of information and news, communication on the topic of coworking.
- The solution of questions and problems of coworking: rent, legal issues, finance, discipline, repair, planning, design, furniture, equipment, and more.
- Articles about coworking, the experience of Western coworkers.

Expected Result.
The heyday of coworking among the Russian-speaking world.
Ability to select coworking office in your hometown.
The choice of groups of coworkers by occupation.
The possibility of more simple immigration (Russian-speaking coworking offices in London, New York, Singapore and other global business centers).
The relief of long-term travel. Opportunity to work productively while traveling to other countries.

Gentlemen, who are interested in coworking, who want to develop coworking and be an active participant in the movement, please write . We need your help.

Thank!
