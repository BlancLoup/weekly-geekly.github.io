Evernote Developer Contest: Application Ideas


Evernote is in full swing, hundreds of developers have begun to create the next generation of applications that will work with our service. Many already have great ideas that they are going to bring to life, others are still looking for inspiration. To those who have not yet decided, we would like to suggest some ideas that arose from us or were voiced by our users.

Application ideas with note taking


If you have certain hobbies, hobbies, activities in which you use Evernote to store certain type of notes, then why not create an application with the appropriate functionality for other users with similar hobbies. Surely among the 10 million people there are such. It seems to us that over time there will appear a whole category of products integrated with the service, which will specialize in creating and storing this or that structured information in Evernote. Such applications can be integrated with other services: maps, calendars or social networks to provide the user with additional information.
