Webinar from Kaspersky Lab: Next Generation Security

May 17 at 19:00 we invite you to the Next Generation Security webinar!



Representatives of Kaspersky Lab and GoTech will talk about Next Generation Security technologies, key selection criteria, what opportunities the corporation offers to start-ups, and how to work with projects after passing the examination.

The webinar will be useful for teams developing products in:
  • mobile and cloud security
  • new approaches in information security
  • adaptive security
  • human and machine behavior analysis
  • physical security
  • Internet of things: smart infrastructure of cities and houses, car safety

For successful projects, Kaspersky Lab offers:
  • access to global information security expertise
  • assistance in international business development
  • access to company technologies
  • investment support

The winner of the Next Generation Security nomination at the finals of the GoTech contest will also receive a cash prize of 350,000 rubles.

Speaker: Vartan Minasyan, Head of investment and innovation at Kaspersky Lab.
Vartan came to Kaspersky Lab in 2007 as a business development manager and worked on creating new products in the field of corporate security. Later, he led the creation of Kaspersky Anti-Virus products, Kaspersky Internet Security (Win / OSX). Currently actively developing the direction of corporate investment.

Moderator: Evgenia Barchenko - GoTech Foundation Development Director.
