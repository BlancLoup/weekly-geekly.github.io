News and Releases


  • The end of support for HHVM in popular projects - If some two years ago the alternative PHP virtual machine from Facebook could boast an impressive performance advantage, today the difference is insignificant. According to Composer statistics , the proportion of HHVM users is less than PHP 5.3 and is 0.36%. Nevertheless, thanks to the HHVM team for pushing PHP to develop.
    Starting with the symfony 4 version, the framework will not support HHVM . Laravel has discontinued support since version 5.3 . In Yii, the termination is scheduled in version 2.1 . MongoDB driver support for HHVM stops . Support for Doctrine , Twig (from version 2), Silex, and Swiftmailer also stops.
