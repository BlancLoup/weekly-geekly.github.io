1% of the internet is porn

The study, commissioned by the US government, revealed that approximately one-hundredth of all Internet sites indexed by Google and Microsoft have sexual content.

According to AP , government lawyers and lawyers insist on the restoration of the act of 1998, according to which the owners of erotic and porn sites had to check the age of the visitor, asking for his credit card information. The act was blocked by the US Supreme Court in 2004 on the basis that content filtering technology is more efficient.

Currently, according to experts, software filters provide a delay of up to 90% of unwanted content.
