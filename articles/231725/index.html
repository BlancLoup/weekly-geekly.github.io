nooLite: new PT112 temperature sensor and RX2132 USB receiver

I hasten to share two good news for people interested in home automation.
In fact, this news is a week ago: when I wrote about them last time, I was banned for a week on the site for giving a link to my site. But, since on the habr about it, so far there has been nothing, deleted the extra links and try again.

So:

1. Started the production of PT112 temperature sensors (only temperature, without the possibility of measuring humidity).



The sensor is exactly the same as the PT111 temperature / humidity sensor , but can only measure temperature. Its advantage - lower cost - $ 21.7. I think many people do not need a measurement of humidity and they will be happy with the new model of the sensor.

2. The production of a new model of nooLite RX2164 USB receiver has begun, with the ability to receive information from sensors



The specifications are the same as those of the RX1164, the price is the same. Moreover, there will be an opportunity to exchange the RX1164 adapter previously purchased for a new model for free!
