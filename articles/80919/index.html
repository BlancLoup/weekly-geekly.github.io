Kodak vs. Apple and RIM. New lawsuit

Kodak entered the war lane. The trial of Kodak with Samsung has just ended, as company representatives found new lawbreakers. Now came the turn of companies such as Apple and Research In Motion. Kodak accuses manufacturers of iPhone and BlackBerry of stealing technologies used in the cameras of popular mobile devices. Kodak is demanding to stop violating the law, that is, in essence, stopping the sale of iPhone and BlackBerry, as well as compensating them for the damage with a sum of as yet unnamed value. Kodak owns more than 1,000 patents on digital imaging technology, and almost all cameras today are based on them. Who will win this dispute?
