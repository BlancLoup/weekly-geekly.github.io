Post about the most important thing


Only a little remains ... Literally in a week or two, everything around will plunge into the atmosphere of celebration, and the pre-holiday thrill familiar from childhood will wrap the streets of all the cities of the country.

And on the eve of the New Year's adventures we would like to say about one very important thing.

For our company, New Year and Christmas are definitely the most important holidays. After all, they are considered to be truly family, and Huawei is nothing but a huge family.

And we would very much like you to dedicate the coming holidays to your loved ones. Put things aside and take time for the most important thing!

Everything that we produce, be it communication equipment or smartphones, we do for one purpose only - to bring people closer to each other, so that, no matter what distance, you and your loved ones are close by.

Huawei products do not know peace: 365 days a year, our smartphones continuously control the flow of your information, sending and receiving signals all over the planet, and communication channels ensure reliable transmission.

But on the eve of the New Year, we have a huge request for you. Give your smartphone a day off. Set it aside for one day, once a year, and spend the day with people close to you! They deserve your attention much more.

Tear off the screen, so as not to miss the magic! No matter how good your laptop or mobile phone is, the most amazing things happen outside of it. And our video - confirmation!

Are you ready for miracles?
