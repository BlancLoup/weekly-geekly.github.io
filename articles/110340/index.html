XBMC 10.0 "Dharma" released



Last weekend, after long months of waiting, perhaps the best cross-platform shell player for XBMC 10.0 Dharma came out.

Of the innovations worth noting:
  • absolutely new API for plugins and other functional extensions
  • initial mouse gesture support in the GUI engine
  • Broadcom Crystal HD decoding support
  • Google WebM support
  • playback of unencrypted blu-ray discs


Full list of changes: mirrors.xmission.com/xbmc/releases/10.0-Dharma-changelog.txt

Official website: xbmc.org
Download page: xbmc.org/download
