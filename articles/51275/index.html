Jajah turns iPod Touch into iPhone

Company Jajah, February 5, 2009 announced the software that turns the iPod Touch into a full-fledged mobile phone.



In order to make calls and write messages, the happy owner of the iPod Touch player needs only a headset with a microphone and a Wi-Fi connection.

It is worth noting that the user can save about 98% of the cost, making calls and sending SMS messages through Jajah.

The Jajah app will also be popular with iPhone users.

It is unclear whether Apple will allow the purchase of this application through the App Store.
