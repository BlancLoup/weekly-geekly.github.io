Lego WeDo - robotics for the little ones

The fact that robotics in the future will increasingly penetrate the everyday life of an ordinary person is already clear to many, if not all. And with what opportunities and difficulties humanity will face - the discussion is also open.
But how to prepare the younger generation for these changes, to work out with them the correct attitude to the problem, to acquaint them with the principles and rules of the functioning of robots?



Lego took this issue very seriously and a few years ago released the LEGO Education WeDo special educational design kit. In spite of the fact that the recruitment is already several years old, only now domestic educational institutions began to pay attention to it.



LEGO WeDo was designed for primary school students (grades 1-4), but nothing prohibits its use for older children.
