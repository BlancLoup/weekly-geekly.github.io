How to protect yourself from time-sink sites?

I think many freelancers and not only asked this question. First I wrote a whole paragraph of text, and then I thought why should I pour water? In general, here are the specifics:

1. First, we need to identify the kidnappers of time, this will help us:
rescuetime.com - show time spent on both sites and programs,


or Chrome plugin for "Time Tracker" - will give information only on sites.

2. Now we need to protect ourselves during business hours from distracting websites and programs. Those. need a program that will close us access to these sites from 9:00 to 18:00.
