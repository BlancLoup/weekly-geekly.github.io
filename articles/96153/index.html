Heroes of Might and Magic Online. Play but not us

A few words in the preface ...



Heroes of Might and Magic online combines all the classic elements of our usual Heroes. Character creation and gameplay are fairly predictable for those who have ever played in the MMORPG: When creating a character, you can choose one of 8 races, choose a man or woman, mage or warrior. In the game you can upgrade your own castle, in the future to build and capture others, to hire creatures for the grand battles that take place, as in the third part of the Heroes series. There are battles in the arena against each other (PvP), participation in battles for titles and guild battles. And of course you will be able to complete quests and kill mobs in large quantities. Fulfilling missions and fighting with rivals, you will receive the resources necessary to strengthen the army and build a castle.

And it would seem nothing foreshadows trouble ...



Since the launch of the game on May 7, many players have participated in the game in a closed beta test. The administration conducted interviews with players to identify and eliminate errors, as well as to create an ergonomic and simple interface.
Added adjustments to the gameplay itself to stabilize and balance the game. It turned out a qualitatively new kind of Heroes, combining the characteristics of the previous games of the series and absolutely new features characteristic of the MMORPG. The game turned out to be quite to itself, I will tell you, standing, and the dimensions of the game universe are inspiring (even the most active players could not determine its approximate size for that past month).
What is most amusing - in the closed test I personally observed most of the players from Russia and the CIS countries (Or we were just the noisiest).
