Russian Creators - to designers, illustrators, photoframes and others


I discovered a new Russian design project for designers (as well as artists, illustrators, photographers, musicians and other talented people) - Russian Creators .

In fact, this project is a network for people engaged in creativity and working in the field of design and applied arts.

The project is notable for being at the same time an exchange for freelancers, enabling creative people to find a job, and an employer to find people capable of producing high-quality results; an online gallery of colorful works, which is always a pleasure to visit as a regular user and at the same time a resource that writes about global trends and mainstream.

The most correct, in my opinion, is that the authors of the project Russian Creators have chosen the closedness of their network. Those. Only those who are clearly skilled and can be pre-screened can register and submit their work. Such a tactic, despite the fact that it is a significant deterrent to the content of the project, will, over time, give the project a status of quality and a certain authority.

Now, over 100 participants have already been registered on the project and I hope that everything is just beginning.
