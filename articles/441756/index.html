The operating systems of the Alt family create a safe environment for working with corporate data using built-in information protection tools, the manufacturing company has FSTEC licenses for the development and production of confidential information protection tools, as well as technical protection activities for confidential information.

Start of mass production of a new industrial computer is scheduled for the end of May 2019. The pilot sample HR-IC-01 will be presented on March 5, 2019 in Yakutsk as part of the so-called start-up tour of the Skolkovo Foundation.
