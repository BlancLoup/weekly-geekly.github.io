Promotions: the first serious experience

My first investment experience happened at school, we already had dial-up, and after a while the first companies offering to trade on Forex. At that time, the attitude to all this trade was purely playful - I made deals without much analysis, using the simplest indicators of the levels on the price chart. Years later, I came "adult exchange".

After a few months of playing on currencies, I noticed that the schedule regularly ‚Äúslows down‚Äù, and applications for selling or buying currencies are executed with a delay and are always not in my favor. Having understood the question, I learned that most of the companies that associate themselves with Forex do not actually conduct operations in the real market and, at best, accept bets on currency exchange rates in the same market. Having played enough, I tied up.

I have nothing to do with finance, and therefore only in 2014 I returned to trade, began to read articles and books on this topic. My interest was dictated by the desire to learn not only to save money, but also to protect them from inflation and many other scary words.

Very soon I learned that in reality everything is not so difficult, and the amount of basic knowledge necessary for the rational management of personal money is small.



There are two ways to invest in securities: you can come to a specialized company, choose a portfolio (a set of different stocks, bonds and derivatives) for a certain amount of money and, having signed a contract with the company, transfer the necessary amount of money.

The second way is to conclude an agreement with a broker to trade on the market in person. To do this, you will have to open your personal investment account, replenish it with money, install a trading terminal.

In the first case, simply invest in a fixed product. The company has already considered the possible risks and decided on the type of securities, based on their profitability. Most often, such investments are clearly limited in time.
The positive side: in many products the maximum possible loss is fixed.

In the second case, additional risks are borne by us - we ourselves must understand how much the shares actually cost and at what price they are now sold on the stock exchange, whether their value will increase after a while, how much and when the dividend will be paid.
The positive side: at any time you can withdraw money, independently choose the composition of the portfolio of all available securities.

I decided to access the stock exchange and make my first securities transactions myself. To do this, I chose a broker for several weeks - a company with a specialized license needed to make deals in the markets and provide access to the stock exchanges to my clients.

The selection process was delayed, because there are a lot of offers on the market and each of them causes different concerns, and I was in no hurry to trust any strange companies.

First of all, I came across the Alfa Capital management company, since I was already a client of Alfa Bank. I will briefly tell you about the options for working with Alpha, as they are typical of most banks.



Mutual funds - a ready investment tool in the stock market. The entry threshold is from 5 thousand rubles, but you can invest only for a fixed time, from 6 months.

Alpha Profit is actually a finished product with 100% capital protection.
The minimum investment amount is 50 thousand rubles. The minimum investment period is 3 years.

Structural products - a group of finished products, one of the instruments of which are derivative financial instruments, such as futures, forwards, options, etc. Here I also cannot touch the exchange and transactions, and the instruments themselves are too complicated for me now.

Trust management - the broker will do all the transactions for me. Entry threshold of 15 million rubles. When I become super busy and rich, I will definitely return to this service.

Individual solutions are strategies implemented exclusively for the needs of a unique client. Interesting, but the threshold of entry from 50 million rubles.

Pension management - in short, you can transfer your pension from the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation to the Alfa Capital Special Fund, this will be another time.

Walking through this wonderful list of offers from a well-known investment company, I did not find a suitable offer for me, but I was able to formulate a brief set of conditions for myself:

- Ability to trade independently.
- The minimum entry threshold is 30 thousand rubles.
- Minimum commission for service and transactions.
- The ability to withdraw your money at any time.
- Trade only your own money, without a loan (leverage).

Realizing this list, I began the search for a broker who will be my guide to the stock exchange, in fact, a provider.

The second company was BCS . They have a lot of advertising, in which they often declare themselves as the market leader. I looked through their conditions and found a suitable option for myself - the company is ready to provide access to the exchange, it arranged a minimum amount.



The first thing I encountered in BCS was a huge package of documents of several dozen pages in small print that needed to be signed in order to get started.

I never sign documents without looking, and nobody warned me about their number in advance, so I was stuck on studying papers for more than half an hour.

Second: the service itself. At first, I had to wait a long time for the manager at his empty table, and in parallel with working with me, he solved some difficult situation with another client.

When signing the contract, I was asked for the details of my regular account for withdrawing money to him if necessary. I do not like to produce a million accounts, so as not to encounter various related expenses, such as a fee for maintaining an account, so I provided the details of an existing account with another bank.

After 10 minutes, a lovely woman came, from which she smelled very temptingly of borsch, and invited me to the cashier.

I went back to the manager, filled out a bunch of papers again. I was already in the BCS customer base, so this time I just signed attachments to the contract. Back to the cashier; contributed money by spending another lunch; went to the office.
