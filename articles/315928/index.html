Welcome to Unity Moscow Meetup December 2


On December 2, Friday, for the first time, we invite developers to the Unity Moscow Meetup, which will be held in the Moscow office of Mail.Ru Group. They will be able to share their experience of using Unity3D, ideas and modern approaches to development, as well as make new contacts.

At the first meeting of participants waiting for a developer report from Unity! See the program under the cut.

- Render optimization techniques on the example of Unity: ADAM
ReJ Zioma, Graphics Foundation Team, Tech Lead, Unity Technologies

ReJ Zioma is one of the creators of the ADAM demo, a RealTime movie made on Unity, to demonstrate the graphics capabilities of the engine. The speaker will tell you how to achieve the same level in your project and what is required for this. Here you can download assets of this demo.

- Expansion of the Unity editor to develop gaming artificial intelligence
Grigory Dyadichenko, Lead Unity Developer, VRTech

The speaker will briefly tell what game artificial intelligence is, show how to make convenient tools for game designers inside the Unity editor. You will learn how to simplify your life when developing AI in a game that is written in Unity.
