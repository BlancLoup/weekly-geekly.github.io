BabyBlog.ru fulfills wishes


On June 15, 2010, the social network BabyBlog.ru connected to the Wishop system and provided its users with the new My Wishes service. This service is available to absolutely all registered users and work with it through a personal account.


BabyBlog is a social network uniting future and real moms. More than 2,000,000 users have the opportunity to save a large amount of time when planning online purchases and searching for the best deal.

Now the advertiser can segment the audience according to the specific desire of the user! For example, if you have a store (no matter online or offline), then you can turn to the audience you need with a minimum budget. You can find people who want to buy a stroller for a month, and make them an exact offer.

The essence of the section "My wishes" on BabyBlog for the user is that now when buying any product or service via the Internet, dozens of sites do not have to be bypassed, save and systematize windows, compare prices, specify product parameters, availability in stock and the like. The user simply creates a new desire, indicates the name of what he wants, describes the necessary parameters of the product, delivery, price, and ... And after a while the stores themselves will begin to send their offers for this product, taking into account all the wishes. It remains only to choose the best offer in your personal account and contact the selected store to place an order.

Now a variety of stores are involved in the Wishop system, selling both children's products and all others. Users can order as strollers, cribs, diapers, and TVs, cameras, cell phones, home appliances and much, much more.

Every desire is immediately seen by dozens of stores of the chosen subject and compete with each other, providing you with the best price and delivery time.

It is pleasantly pleased that the term Wish & Buy we entered has taken root. From different people I heard this term, even without reference to our projects.

Store owners and advertising specialists will be grateful for feedback on the Wishop Store.
