On the catcher and the beast runs

Today, an interesting SMS has been received on the mobile phone of one of our virus analysts:



Without thinking twice, he downloaded this application from a specially equipped stand. Who would have thought that there would be a new malware - J2ME / TrojanSMS.Agent.B, which is currently not detected by any of the antivirus solutions, of course, except for ours. :)

As it turned out, the SMS was sent via the Internet service of one of the mobile operators.



By itself, the application does not shine with ingenuity and is the simplest Trojan that sends paid SMS. However, once again we urge everyone to be vigilant and not to succumb to the tricks of fraudsters.

And of course, we will not miss such a convenient moment in order not to remind of our mobile antivirus . After updating the signature database, its users will be protected from J2ME / TrojanSMS.Agent.B.
