Are you going to study in Toulouse?


Back in September 2013, I wrote about how you can sign up to learn French at the University of Toulouse. The recording itself took place in January. And now the people who were selected came to confirm.

Some people asked questions and talked in comments, others preferred to write questions to me personally. But I am sure that the majority simply quietly sent the documents without telling anyone. I would like to use the date of confirmation of the record and count the readers who sent the documents and those who received a positive response.

Just answer the questions of one of those who signed up, the information may be of interest to others. I invite everyone to join the discussion.

I will not reveal his identity, I do not know whether he wants to advertise his plans for departure.

Congratulations! Actually from this message I learned that the recording is over.
If you have not received confirmation today, you will most likely not receive it. Try next year.

There is no need to be afraid of tests. They do not aim to weed out anyone, but determine which year of study and which group you will be recorded in.
Personally, I arrived with zero knowledge of French. In the test answered at random, did not even try to get into the correct answers. Accordingly, I was enrolled in the first course in the weakest group, which suited me perfectly.

Teaching comes in french. But at the first stage, sign language is actively used, so do not worry.

On a visa, information could become outdated, but in my time it was like this: the embassy issued a student visa for 3 months, and after being registered at the university, the prefecture in Toulouse already issued a student residence permit for a year already. Then it is extended annually in the prefecture of the place of residence (you can easily change the university and move to another region).
