Rambler & Co has launched an exchange of services for advertisers based on the LiveJournal blogging platform.

The Livejournal platform (LiveJournal), a member of Rambler & Co , launched a project above the name Bloggers Exchange . With it, advertisers will be able to place ads without intermediaries, referring directly to one or another blogger.

The Bloggers Exchange allows the advertiser to select the desired blogger from the list by special criteria (audience, subject, post cost), set a specific brand promotion task for him, approve the post before publication, and also receive an analytical report on the campaign in his account.

For example, select authors by subject and target audience (the service will offer a base of 5,000 bloggers), determine the format of cooperation, and then follow the entire campaign using the reporting system. Service solution provided by the company DigitalMagic, writes the publication Lenta.ru.
