Artificial blood will be made in plants.



Once about such things told in science fiction. A man whose bones are printed on a printer, and the blood is made in a factory. But now it is almost a reality. The developers of 3D printers have learned how to print individual organs , and now scientists are ready to produce any amount of artificial blood.

Professor Marc Turner (Marc Turner) said that his group was able to start production of blood cells of the first group from iPS cells, i.e. stem cells derived from human organs.
