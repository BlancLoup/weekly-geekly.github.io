Mozilla Foundation is 8 years old



Not just Twitter today is celebrating a birthday. Founded July 15, 2003, the Mozilla Foundation celebrates its 8th anniversary.

Mozilla, as everyone probably knows, is behind the free Firefox browser, which is the most popular alternative to Internet Explorer. At the time Firefox was released, IE 6 occupied 95% of the market.

Firefox quickly gained popularity by adopting and then setting many web standards. Many even link the growing popularity of Firefox and the return of Microsoft to the development of Internet Explorer, which has not developed for a long time.
