DG approved a draft law on blocking websites with any pirated content

The State Duma Committee on Information Policy recommended to adopt in first reading a bill that extends the effect of "anti-piracy" law to all objects of copyright and related rights.


On March 12, it is planned to consider amendments to the law, if everything passes normally, then from July 1, 2014 will come into force. Several implications:

1. You can block any sites for any pirated content.
2. Service providers, hosting and site owners are exempt from liability for blocking.

Hello brave new world.
