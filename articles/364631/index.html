Apple raised prices for apps in the Russian App Store 1.5-2 times



Due to the growth of the dollar against the ruble, Apple has raised prices not only on equipment, but also on applications in the App Store. Previously, applications that cost 0.99 US dollars were valued at 33 rubles in the Russian App Store. Now the price has been changed to 62 rubles.

All other prices are also changed, from the calculation of the ruble rate as 62 rubles to 1 dollar. For example, if the price in dollars is 1.99 dollars, the price in rubles is 123.99. If the application costs 9.99 dollars, its price in the Russian App Store is 618.09 rubles.

Apple has previously sent letters to developers , which indicated the main reason for raising prices in rubles: the change in the dollar exchange rate. It is worth noting that the price of some applications still remains the same, perhaps in the near future they will also be updated. In addition, prices in iTunes are also the same.

As previously reported, Apple prices are also raised, and quite significantly .
