CES 2012 // Dell XPS 13



Taking another six months odds, Dell, along with ultrabooks of the second wave, presented its first representative of this class. Meet the Dell XPS 13 Ultrabook!



First, the body. Rejecting the frame, the company's engineers were able to fit a 13-inch display in an 11-inch form factor. The XPS 13 is indeed smaller in size than the 13-inch MacBook Air, but it looks more like a 12-inch or even 11-inch device. Not bad!



The body of the machine is made of aluminum and carbon fiber, and the thickness of the model at the thinnest point is just over 6 mm and weighs 1.36 kg. (In the thick part - about 16 mm.)



The keys are corporate, dellovskie. Of course, the island type. The touchpad impresses with its precision and softness.


Dell XPS 13 and MacBook Air





Inside the ultrabook has everything you need for operational work. Processors to choose from: Intel Core i3-2367M, i5-2467M or i7-2637M, Intel HD 3000 graphics, 4 GB of RAM and SSD-drive with a capacity of 128 or 256 GB.
