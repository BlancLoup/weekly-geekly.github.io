Windows - over 1000 free legal songs!

Yesterday, the Windows Social Media Team and Reverb Nation launched the Sponsored Songs program. Now anyone can go to MySpace.com/Windows and download more than 1000 songs from there for free!


Sponsored Songs made possible by advertising. Advertising information is displayed while the song is playing in the form of a changing album cover. And no sound ads! I think that this moment is absolutely not critical, because most of the time we listen to the music itself, and not looking at the covers.

I told about the only negative - now let's move on to the pluses:

1. A large number of music of various genres and from various performers. A quick glance in my favorite category Rock immediately found tracks from such famous artists as Underoath, The Answer and Lordi.

2. The program involves more and more artists and groups. Who knows, maybe in a month you can download tracks from the new Placebo or The Black Eyed Peas album.

3. Music can be downloaded in different formats and in good quality - mp3 (256 kbps) and m4a (152 kbps).

4. This is a great way to expand your musical horizons. It often happens that among the strangers for Russia performers, there are those from whom it is impossible to tear their ears off after the first audition. It is possible that among this list there will be those who in a year or two will become world stars.

5. At the moment, it is planned to add 10 songs per week to the collection, which is clearly more than 2-3 free songs in iTunes.

6. You just bought a player and you have nothing to listen to it? Do not defile your purchase with stolen content! It is better to go to MySpace.com/Windows and download yourself completely legal tracks!

7. I do not know if it is possible to cooperate with Russian performers, but I think that if you play high-quality music and at the same time sing in English, then you should try to get into this program.
