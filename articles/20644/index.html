RippleNote T8100 Notebook by Pixel Lab

The company Pixel Lab announced the release of a laptop-transformer RippleNote T8100. This mobile PC has a spectacular and interesting design, rotating 12.1-inch touchscreen display with a WXGA resolution of 1280 √ó 800 pixels and a very good filling.

And the filling is as follows: Intel GM965 + ICH8M chipset, Intel Core 2 Duo T8100 2.1 GHz processor, 1 GB of RAM (expandable up to 4 GB), Intel GMA X3100 video, 120 GB SATA hard drive and DVD drive. In addition, on board the RippleNote T8100 has a Gigabit Ethernet network card, a Wi-Fi 802.11a / b / g adapter, an integrated card reader and a 1.3 megapixel webcam.

According to Pixel Lab, the RippleNote T8100 should be on sale by the end of February. True, so far only in South Korea. The price of the laptop is not yet known.
