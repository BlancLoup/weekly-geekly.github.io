Alchemy. Modern remake

Ladies and Gentlemen!
"Oldschool Infundibulum Sowtware" presents to your attention the game Alchemy, which is a conceptual remake of the old-name DOS game ( Post about DOS game )

And here is a remake.

Picture to attract attention:


The controls are intuitive, but still a couple of tips:
- drag one item to another to trigger a reaction
- double click on the field to get 4 main elements
- double click on the item to clone it
- in the list above open items are displayed. Click on any of them to add it to the field.
- excess elements can be removed by placing them on the black square on the right

Purpose of the game: hmm ... hard to say. Probably try to get something interesting. Create a life to begin with, for example)

This is written in pure JS and, despite being played in a browser, is not an online game.
Who will spy the reaction in the source - the cheater, not the alchemist.
Best at Firefox. In Chrome, not so much; he constantly strives to select something on a double-click.

PS The author of the remake of the game is not in the game, but you can invite him. He will be able to answer all our questions and tell you about the process of creating the game.
