Bitfury: about the team, requirements for candidates and open vacancies

We told BitFury about the company and what it does in the previous post . And today Anna Minets, Chief Human Resources Officer ( The BitFury Group ) will tell about our team.

At the beginning of 2017, the company employs 250 people, and 150 of them joined us during the last year. We continue to grow and invest in highly qualified personnel. On average, every day, somewhere in the world, a new employee comes to the BitFury team.

The composition of the development team


About 20% of our team are programmers. Soon this percentage will increase: now we are paying more and more attention to software products, Data Analytics platform, Digital Assets PaaS, Lightning Network (p2p), therefore we are extremely interested in talented developers. The average age of soft-developers is 28-30 years old, in hardware slightly older employees are closer to 40.

Candidate Requirements


We are engaged in the blockchain technology and, of course, we will evaluate if the candidate is already in the subject. But this is still a new area, in which very few specialists have ‚Äúgrown‚Äù so far, and there will not be a refusal to a person just because he knows little about the blockchain. It is good if the candidate understands related subjects: cryptography, the specifics of data warehousing, system programming, and theory of algorithms.

It so happened that most of our developers are graduates of the Physical-Engineering Institute (MIPT), of course, they quickly found a common language and became a cool team. We monitor the market and successfully compete for the most gifted children from other strong universities: Moscow State University, Baumanka, Taras Shevchenko University and others.

We are always happy olympiadnikam. The figures speak for themselves: 80% of our technical specialists are winners of olympiads, 40% are candidates of science. Our team has a world champion in mathematics - an ambitious company we have gathered.

By the way, about ambitions. If they are healthy, we really appreciate them, as well as non-standard thinking. For example, one of the candidates drew the close attention of the HR department by comparing him with ... a discriminating bride from the well-known optimization task of Martin Gardner , for which at one time defended his doctoral dissertations. A little advice to applicants: if you have imagination and purposefulness, do not be afraid to show them at our interviews.

When ambitious people gather in one place and they are given an interesting task - they do not need to be customized, on the contrary - you will not stop!

Test items


Tasks are prepared and tested by our developers. They are not very voluminous and will not take much time.
