Multidirectional translation

Hello :)!

Google now allows you to translate texts and websites from any language to any. The restriction on English-Kakoy and Kakiy-English directions disappeared, and the translation page was slightly updated.

Interestingly, their AJAX Language API also supports translations in different directions, although they are silent about this in the documentation. Without delay, I decided to use this wonderful fact: my home-grown associative translator got a new interface and features :). A quick language swap (hover over ") and the ability to change safe search'a pictures on the fly appeared.

I would be glad if someone comes in handy :)!
