First Moscow Startup Weekend



Comrades, I am glad to bring to your attention this new event and invite those interested. It‚Äôs great that events where people try to launch new projects in a few days are beginning to gain popularity in Russia.

Recently, in St. Petersburg, was HackDay - a format that is more focused on developers. Startup Weekend will be held in Moscow from October 2nd to 4th, a format focused on creating full-fledged business teams.

About Startup Weekend


The movement began in Silicon Valley, and today weekends are regularly held in 50 US cities and European countries. Startup Weekend format features:
  • The event is not initiated by any big company, it is an informal startup community.
  • It is always held on weekends so that people who work somewhere can try themselves as startups.
  • The event is not focused on development, but on creating a full-fledged business team. Therefore, in addition to IT specialists, marketing experts, project managers, product managers, sales managers, lawyers are invited. Ideally, the first sales should be made by the end of the event;) A detailed list of roles in the teams forming at the event.
  • Discussion of ideas on Friday evening, and active work on Saturday and Sunday. The speeches of experts are held in parallel with the work of the teams; those who find the topic of the speech seem relevant and necessary at the moment are listening. The main goal is to make the project, the speakers are selected to be useful to you in this.
  • The further fate of the projects supported is being monitored. We are now thinking about how to organize this in Russian realities, we are glad to hear your opinions and wishes.

We invite technical experts .

Participation


The most important thing is of course participation. And creative, with the mood to work, and not to listen to the reports. There are no restrictions on the profession and subject area. Marketers, designers, developers, project managers and other professions. Any experience from banking and trading to show business is welcome. We will especially welcome people who are ready to engage in advertising and selling new products. You can come up with your idea and find companions for its implementation, or simply join the project you like. At the same time, nothing prevents to come with one idea, and in the end - to implement another;)

Registration on the site Startup Weekend is open until September 29. Participation is paid - we throw 1000 rubles. for three meals a day for two days.
