Torrent tracker Demonoid moved to Ukraine

As previously reported , a month ago, the torrent tracker Demonoid.com, closed under pressure from the Canadian Recording Industry Association (CRIA), began to show signs of life again. Although the site itself did not resume work, the tracker server began to work. According to Torrentfreak , at the time it was located in Malaysia. The resurrection was short-lived, and after a few days the tracker again went off-line.

And then, a month later, he began working again, this time on the territory of Ukraine. Checking the tracker's ip-address shows that the Demonoid servers are currently serviced by Kiev-based Ukraine Colocall Ltd. At least, it is to her that belongs the ip-address, to which inferno.demonoid.com responds, which ensures the connection of all users of the file-sharing service.
