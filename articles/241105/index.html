Tinkoff Bank at #MBLTDev 2014

Our mobile platform architect Dmitry Tarasov will speak on October 28 at the #MBLTDev mobile developers conference .
Dima will talk about re-using the code on practical examples from the development of mobile applications of the bank and share experiences on how to accelerate the development of the application without losing quality.

In addition, for visitors and participants of the conference Tinkoff Bank organizes a cozy lounge zone with ottomans, sockets and cookies, where you can relax, chat, charge your devices, get souvenirs from Tinkoff and sign up for an open day at the bank.

Together with this conference, we are launching the Tinkoff People movement, in which we will talk about working at a bank, about our employees and offices, and how the project development and management processes are organized. And many other things.
