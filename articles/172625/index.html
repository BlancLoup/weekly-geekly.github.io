Mobile application that allows you to get more from the car

Recently, the topic of developing applications for cars has become quite relevant. The market is rapidly responding to such signals, and online surveys of applications directed in this direction are more and more common.

One of these applications is Automatic (iOS). It consists of two parts - a hardware plug that connects to the on-board computer of the car, and the application itself on the device. The entire kit will cost the car owner $ 70 and allow you to transfer data from the car to the phone via Bluetooth.



How it works: the application analyzes the data and begins to act as a smart helper while driving. The application analyzes all your trips and provides the user with tips on how to drive more carefully, how to reduce fuel consumption and how to increase the overall service life of the car. In addition, Automatic also calculates the average fuel consumption for a particular trip.

The main purpose of the application is that the driver can look at his car in a new way.
