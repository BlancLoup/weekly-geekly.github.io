FriendFeed came close to Web 3.0

FriendFeed , the dream of Internet maniacs, who, before his appearance, had to monitor his Internet maniacs through dozens of different subscriptions and feeds, continues to amaze with its thoughtfulness and atmosphere of total enlightenment. Of course, the system is still far away from the complete guessing of your interests (and thank God, for it is scary when some algorithm knows what we like, and we traditionally do not), but everyone is happy. Anyone who managed to snatch the news from his roaring stream.
