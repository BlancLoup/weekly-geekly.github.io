How to quickly learn speed reading and read 300 words per minute



Many of you have heard about speed reading, courses and methods that help you read faster and perceive large amounts of information in a short time. Now I will tell you how to start reading in a few minutes at a speed of 300 words per minute. It will be about the technology RSVP (Rapid serial visual presentation), which translated into Russian means "Fast serial visual display."

The bottom line is to quickly display text in a specific area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe screen, usually in the center. In this case, the words appear consistently, one after the other. Reading in this way, a person simply does not have the opportunity to think, distract or return to the previous line, since the words on the screen change very quickly. This reduces the reading time, for we spend about 1/6 of the total time on returning to the previous paragraphs and pages.

This method of perception of information allows you to instantly increase the speed of reading, at least 2 times. All applications of this kind have the function of setting the speed of display of words (usually from 100 to 1000 words per minute). From my own experience, using this approach for the first time, I easily read texts at a speed of 300-400 words per minute, despite the fact that the average reading speed of a modern person is ~ 200 words per minute. Further, it all depends on your desire and determination.

This option is ideal for reading and perception of journalistic and fiction literature, but reading scientific and technical texts with the abundant availability of formulas, graphs and diagrams will be very difficult.
