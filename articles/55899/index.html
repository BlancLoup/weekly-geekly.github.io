Gamer Corps Level 10



The motto of the studio DesignworksUSA - items must be surprising. The studio was founded 35 years ago, is now owned by BMW and is mainly engaged in the development of accessories for BMW and MINI brands: bags, cups, seat covers and so on. However, industrial design is not forgotten, which is good news.

The concept of the Level 10 case shown on the past CeBIT is a model specially designed for the anniversary of the Thermaltake brand. It is assumed that Level 10 will be the flagship line of gaming desktops.

Chip case - "open architecture". Instead of a single box, there are separate cases for optical drives, hard drives, a processor and a video card. All this is fixed on the "wall", where the USB ports of the audio outputs and other interfaces for connecting peripherals are brought. In the case there is only one 120 mm fan. It is possible that such a design is not only convenient when upgrading, but also effective in terms of cooling.

It is expected that by the June exhibition Computex the concept will turn into a full-fledged product.
