3D Scanner for iPad

The company 3D Systems , known for its 3D-printers, introduced a removable 3D scanner iSence for tablets iPad.



The scanner was presented at the CES 2014 exhibition. Judging by the video, scanning is performed using multiple laser beams, resulting in a fairly accurate three-dimensional model, which can then be printed on 3D printers that support the Cube format.

The device is planned to be released for sale in the second quarter of this year at a price of $ 499.
