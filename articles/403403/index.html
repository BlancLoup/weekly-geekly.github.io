Onion stops collecting donations for the Omega 2 microcomputer



On April 25, 2017, the Onion company published a letter on the site of the crowdfunding platform Indiegogo, informing that the collection of funds for the development of the Omega 2 microcomputer is stopped.

We are completing a donation campaign.

The project is fully secured, and we want to thank each sponsor for their support and patience. We could not have done it without you!

I made a donation and still have not received a reward.


Those who place an order, we are constantly sending packages, if you have filled the survey forum. Over the next two weeks we will ship 885 packages. This is for those who have already completed the survey form.
