Almost 20% are categorically against it, 15% do not watch it without high-speed Internet access and 4% are watching, perhaps because there is no fast Internet. But the most interesting, noted 137 people (18%), who watch TV, and they have a good channel on the Internet. Some of them, perhaps, mean cable / satellite TV (I have nothing against him), but probably not all. That is, we can conclude that there are people in whose lives TV still takes some place.
