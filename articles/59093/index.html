AMD reorganization

Despite the already good progress (in the falling CPU market, the share of AMD processors has grown ), the company is announcing its reorganization. What should help her to compete with even greater success in the market.

Now the company is divided into four independent departments. The main should be the department for the development of new products, both CPU and GPU. He was headed by Rick Bergman (Rick Bergman). The fact that both the central and graphic processors will be developed by one department is determined by market trends. In the near future, the first solutions should appear, combining the functions of CPU and GPU.

Other departments of the company will be the department for the development of advanced technologies (focus on the development of innovative technologies), the marketing group and the group for working with clients.

In addition to information on the reorganization, AMD also reports a possible rejection of the further use of the ATI trademark, which (together with the company of the same name, of course) was acquired for $ 5.4 billion in 2006. Instead, ATI plans to use the trademark AMD.
