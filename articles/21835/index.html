Movement against spammers.


Spam Fighters Declaration
We, people passionately burning with hatred for spammers, declare:

Spammers send millions of messages, and at our expense impose their goods and services on us. They reduce the effectiveness of our work and poison the rest. They do not respect our rights, they break the law. They do this every day for many years, stealing time and money from us day after day.

But we do not intend to put up with the situation, and we have effective ways to stop their activities. We know how to make a crime unprofitable.

Spam customers are counting on the advertising effect. They expect that good customers will come to them and spend their money.

But we will come to them. Us - the legion.

We will take away from them what they have taken from us all these years: time and effort.

If they sent us a letter, then they need our calls and letters. And we have them!
We'll throw a barrage of calls with idiotic questions on them. We fill them with millions of crazy offers and orders. We will not let them work with real customers.

We do not need to create an organization. We will change ourselves.
Spam will mean a lot of positive emotions for us, because we will fight it in such a way that we can enjoy it ourselves.

We will order delivery of elephants from India from transport companies. For a long time, language learning centers have been able to find out the possibility of learning the state language of the Andromeda nebula.
There are millions of us, our imagination is limitless.

We will have fun ourselves, if possible, record and publish dialogs so that the whole Internet can make fun of spammers.
We have the desire, the tools and all the possibilities.
We are power.

Our actions are legitimate.
We are defending our rights.
Spammers are defenseless before us.
