When communicating with parents, principals and school teachers, the question arose of SMS notifications with grades. The problem has been solved only recently by the development of a special gateway. The implementation scheme is as follows:
  • There is a special sms-gate, which is engaged in receiving messages from a copy of the System of electronic diaries and broadcasts to their parents. By default, you can use my gateway, but you can change it to a third-party, as long as it conforms to the specification . (you must first register a copy of the system on the gate)
  • The student's profile indicates the phone numbers of the parents, which are then transmitted to the gate.
  • Parent pays for SMS in a way that is convenient for him (at the official gateway only payment via SMS is realized)
  • Since then, the parent receives notifications about the adventures of his child by SMS.
