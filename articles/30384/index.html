I did not doubt that the originals work in the E-generator. Though the office does not like it, they know how to choose a niche. They released a new creation - the project Pavlik Morozov . Of course, not for free, but in exchange for a percentage of sales.

Everything would be fine, but as I think the main place to search for such acquaintances for pairing will be social networks, and the result will be that your friend will suddenly be neither a friend nor an enemy - and so (Pavlik). As a result, all efforts to protect against spammers will be meaningless - if you do not choose friends more carefully. The prosperity of the service will probably also be promoted by the problem of forced friends raised not only by me.
