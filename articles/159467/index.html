Asus Zenbook U500VZ Ultrabook Video Review



ASUS Zenbook U500VZ is a new premium ultrabook that combines lightness, portability, powerful stuffing and a stunning matte 15.6-inch IPS display with a resolution of 1920x1080.

Inside the stylish aluminum case, there is a quad-core Intel Core i7 processor and a GeForce GT 650M video card - a duet that can handle almost any task, including even the most advanced video games (albeit not at maximum graphics settings).

Depending on the model, the disk subsystem includes either two solid-state drives in RAID0, or a combination of a hard disk and a solid-state drive.

The laptop is equipped with a lighting sensor that automatically adjusts the brightness of the screen and the keyboard backlight depending on external conditions. The excellent sound of the SonicMaster speaker system complements the external subwoofer. Included is also an external DVD drive.
