Dark patterns in action. WhatsApp explained how to refuse to transfer personal information to Facebook



Yesterday, WhatsApp announced a change to two documents - the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Text of documents in Russian

Changes in these documents are made for the first time in four years. As it is easy to guess, the changes are related to the fact that in 2014 the company WhatsApp became the property of Facebook. It's time to pay back the money spent, that is, to merge the information about your users to the new owner.

The new rules explain that WhatsApp is part of the Facebook group of companies and plans to share information with Facebook in order to ‚Äúimprove coordination and improve the experience of using services.‚Äù As soon as the user accepts the updated Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy, WhatsApp will begin to merge his personal information into the Facebook advertising system.
