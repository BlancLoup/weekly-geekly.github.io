Review of the OPPO Finder X907 smartphone

Good day, Habrahabr!

As you could understand from the first post, OPPO comes with its Android smartphones to Russia. The first model in our market will be the OPPO Finder. In this post you will find an overview of this device, with all its advantages and disadvantages.





Contents of delivery

Two colors will be on sale: black and white. The smartphone comes in a cardboard box with a decorative-looking and pleasant-looking touch. Inside is the smartphone itself, as well as instructions, USB cable, charger, headset, microUSB adapter - 3.5 Jack.







The size of the phone is 125 x 66 x 6.65 mm, the camera module protrudes slightly above the main line of the case. This is the thinnest smartphone on the Russian market. I would like to note that recently the OPPO has reviewed the thickness of the upper back cover and now the camera is noticeably less protruding.



The weight of the device is 125 grams. The back cover of the smartphone is made of metal. It pleasantly cools the hand and tactilely conveys the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãminimalist industrial design embedded in the apparatus. The frame of the case is stylized as metal, but, in fact, made of durable plastic. It is pleasant to hold the smartphone in hands and it is very convenient to carry in a pocket because of its compact sizes. At the same time, it is not felt "fluff".

Comparison of appearance. From left to right: Apple Iphone 4, Apple Iphone 5, Meizu MX, OPPO Finder, Samsung Galaxy S3.







The disadvantages of the appearance include the absence of a 3.5 mm connector. To minimize discomfort while listening to audio content, branded OPPO headphones with headset functions are included with the device. In addition to answering and ending a call, the button on the headset also has a media function: while listening to music in the player, pressing once turns on / off the pause, and double pressing switches to the next track. In addition, the kit includes an adapter to the standard 3.5 mm Jack. Its use does not affect the sound quality.

The justification for the absence of a standard headphone jack is the thickness of the case, which did not allow it to fit. On the left side of the cap is a slot for microSIM-card, just below - 3 pins for branded accessory OPPO - wireless case - charging. On the right side is a paired volume button. The upper end is an on / off / screen lock button, and here is a microUSB connector, also hidden behind the cap. The button is outside the box on the left. You quickly get used to this, as it is pronounced exactly under the use of your index finger. On the rear panel there are: a camera and an LED flash, additional microphone holes for noise cancellation and a speaker. At the bottom there is a hole in the main microphone. On the front panel there is a screen, as well as a front-facing camera for video communication, a light sensor, and touch buttons: Menu, Home, Back.















Overall, the build quality makes a great impression. As soon as the device is in the hands - all questions about the build quality and strength disappear by themselves. The case design does not provide for opening directly by the user. A few words about the stubs: for more than 4 months of exploiting the smartphone, not a single complaint arose to them, they are very durable and have shown their functionality.

Screen

The OPPO Finder is equipped with a Super AMOLED Plus display, made by Samsung, with a diagonal of 4.3 inches and a resolution of 800x480 pixels.
Displays up to 16 million colors. Viewing angles are maximum. The screen remains readable in the sun. Sensor type capacitive, supports up to 10 simultaneous touches.
Also noteworthy is the fact that in the manufacture of the matrix applied to the standard for the LCD RGB scheme: each pixel is formed of three subpixels. In practice, this means no PenTile effect.

The Finder is equipped with a 1500 mAh lithium-ion battery. By the current Andorid standards - the figure is small. But, firstly, the Qualcomm MSM8260 chip has a moderate power consumption. And, secondly, the software is very much optimized. Read about it, below. Claimed working time: talk time - 8 hours and 40 minutes; Standby time - 400 hours. Communication capabilities

Usb USB data transfer speeds of up to 13 Mb / s (USB Mass Storage), multiple PC connection modes supported - charging only, drive mode, OPPO Assistant mode, modem mode. It also supports the MHL standard, which, with the appropriate cable, allows you to display a picture on a TV or other monitor.
Bluetooth Bluetooth 2.0 version, supports a standard set of profiles, including A2DP, in the settings you can specify the automatic shutdown of Bluetooth after a certain time interval.
Wi-Fi. Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g / n is supported, you can use your phone as an access point.
GPS There is a GPS chip, this is part of the Qualcomm chipset, GLONASS is supported. A cold start takes about 15 seconds, A-GPS also performs positioning very quickly.



Camera

The device has an 8 - megapixel camera module, equipped with a flash and autofocus. For photo and video shooting there is a minimum of settings: HDR, timer, flash control. The picture quality is average for 8 megapixels, this can be attributed to the disadvantages of the Finder. But OPPO does not hide it. Moreover, the software is optimized in such a way that it uses the maximum possible of the hardware that is available. In future models, modules from another supplier will be applied.
