Grant Program (100,000 rubles) for students and young scientists

Friends, if you have students, graduate students and young scientists among you, and your interests are in the field of information security and related fields, then we have news for you.



We are conducting an additional set of applications for the 2014 Grant Program. Ready work at the time of application is not required (Kaspersky Lab itself will propose interesting topics for development), we are waiting for you only an application and, of course, a desire to work :)

The selection of applicants will be made on a competitive basis, leaving 15 people as a result. Until the end of autumn 2014, they will work on their projects under the guidance of experts of our company, and on November 28 there will be a general meeting at which each participant will give a presentation. According to the results of the performances, participants will receive grants in the amount of 100,000 rubles!

Hurry up, accepting applications expire March 31! To apply and learn about all the rules of the Program, please visit: academy.kaspersky.ru/networking
