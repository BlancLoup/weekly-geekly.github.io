iPhone Gloss.ua 3.0 with a poster for 16 cities of Ukraine



Today, city guide Gloss.ua introduced a new version of the iPhone application .

Users can now see posters of cinema, music, nightlife, theater, exhibitions in 16 cities of Ukraine - Odessa, Dnepropetrovsk, Donetsk, Kharkov, Lugansk, Zaporozhye, Alushta, Feodosia, Yevpatoria, Kerch, Koktebele, Sevastopol, Simferopol, Sudak, Yalta.

The new version of the iPhone application was made possible thanks to the partnership of Gloss with another Internet project of the Ukrainian Media Holding - a network of city portals Vgorode.ua.

The first version of the application was released in 2009, since then the application has been installed more than 10,000 times according to App figures.

According to Google Analytics, over the past year, the audience of the application has grown by 138% . Over the last month, 8,800 users launched the application 31,700 times . The application continues to grow at a rate of 10-15% per month.

In addition to the additional 15 cities, a new design appeared in the application. Also translated into English interface. If the phone has a non-Russian or Ukrainian locale installed, the data will be displayed in English. Information on the best entertainment establishments in Kiev is also available in English - restaurants, pubs, clubs, which is especially important on the eve of Euro 2012.

Simultaneously with the release of the new version, Gloss has reduced the price of advertising in the application and the minimum period of placement. From now on, promotion in the iPhone costs from 8,000 UAH / month, the minimum placement period is not 3, but 1 month. The full presentation of this offer can be found here . You can read about the experience of Gloss in advertising in articles on AIN here and here .

Recall that in September, Gloss presented a mobile application with a poster in 16 cities for Android , and the City Guide has versions with a poster of Kiev for Samsung Bada and Nokia . And last week, Gloss introduced a paid API with a poster for Kiev .

All mobile applications are connected with the Gloss Pro platform , thanks to which the owners of institutions and event organizers can add information about themselves to Gloss for free.

Gloss for iPhone can automatically determine the location of the user and show him the poster of the desired city. If desired, a person can select the poster of another city in the settings.

The application is developed from the very beginning in Alterplay , so that we gladly accept any of your feedback and suggestions.
