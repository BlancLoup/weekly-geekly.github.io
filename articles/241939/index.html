The game development process can now be broadcast on Twitch



Game development for many is a fascinating process that brings positive emotions no less, and sometimes more, than a video game. It is clear that this is not entertainment, but many developers live their work, trying to share their achievements, and in time to learn about the developments of other specialists.

Now you can share your work, and you can follow the work of others non-stop, thanks to the appearance of a specialized section for game developers on the Twitch service. The new section allows you to broadcast the process of developing another toy directly to the service, where all this will be seen by other people.

In addition, if there is such a desire, you can watch and how other game development specialists work. Now there are already 17 channels in the section with a relatively small number of online viewers (about 700), but the popularity of the section will grow.

Right now in the section there is a channel where the development of a platformer is demonstrated, a channel whose author creates a 3D model of the hero for his game and other channels.



Of course, there is the possibility of commenting channels, so it is worth being prepared for criticism or, conversely, approval of your work. Probably, with the help of this section you can organize various online development courses and other interesting things - there are quite a few ways to use them.
