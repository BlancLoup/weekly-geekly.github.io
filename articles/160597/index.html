Gelius Team Presentation

The Gelius team from St. Petersburg has been formed recently, but on the account of the team members there are already 9 officially registered packages of medical application programs.



The scenario of the MRLs from Gelius: bracelets are put on your hands, a wireless silicone applicator is placed on the eve of the nose (due to the specific material and size, the applicator is not felt, does not fall out and is not visible externally). There is a measurement, the data is transmitted to the smartphone, where the application processes, analyzes and interprets the incoming information for the end user (sick / see a doctor / healthy). For more detailed information you can see all the graphs and parameters. In the mobile application is a personal medical card with dates and results of the examination, which makes it possible to track the dynamics of the disease.

Using the device, it is possible to diagnose diseases of the cardiovascular, respiratory and nervous systems. Monitoring options:
  • arterial blood pressure;
  • electrocardiogram;
  • Body temperature;
  • pulsation of breathing temperature;
  • breathing rate;
  • peak expiratory flow rate;
  • galvanic skin response;
  • pulse oximetry;
  • mechanical activity.

The complexity of the implementation of the concept lies in the fact that a number of research works are needed to develop sensors with characteristic parameters (thin-film), and serial production of sensors developed using a new technology is also needed.
