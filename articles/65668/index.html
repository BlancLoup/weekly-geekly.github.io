Room 3700

I wanted to share with the Ukrainian part of the social community a convenient SMS service (again from Privatbank), number 3700.
You can send any question to it, the operator on that side will find the answer with the help of Google, and will write to you via SMS. Difference from other similar reference services, the price of SMS at the standard operator rate (it is better to clarify, but not more than 35 kopecks.). It is very convenient, when it is not near a computer to find out what movie is and where it is going, to remember what the song is, following the example of words, and so on. I often helped.
Answers come within 3-15 minutes.
