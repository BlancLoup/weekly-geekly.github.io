Do not be afraid of small business


Recently, I have often come across articles where an equal sign is placed between a small business and losers. An example of one of the last such articles: " Sergey Belousov about zombie startups that are of no use, but sorry to get rid of ." Short sense: if the company lives, but does not develop, then this is a failed project and it is time to move on to a new one. I do not agree with this direction of thought. Not all companies can grow and be tremendously successful. And this is nothing bad. If the company lives, that's good. They are not zombies, but simply companies that have found their niche. Small business is great.

Are they all losers? Not. These are people involved in small business. They may never get a star from the sky, but, most likely, their life is much better than sitting out their pants in the office. At least they have more freedom, hope, and potential.

And again, I disagree. Small business does not mean low income and boredom! I will give a couple of examples.

The first of them is closer to me in spirit (see what I do ), although it seems not so clear to most readers. There is such a company EdisonDesignGroup (EDG), which is developing a library for preprocessing and parsing C / C ++ programs. At the same time, their clients are the largest companies, and they themselves in many respects set the development vector of the C ++ language. Here is such a small business.
