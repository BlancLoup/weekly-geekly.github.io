ProductCamp Minsk 2015

On October 17-18, ProductCamp Minsk for experts in the field of product management will be held near Minsk. The event will cover various thematic areas of product management: from marketing research and development to market launch and operation.


What awaits you?
- Reports, blitz-speeches, round tables. You are waiting for two days of intensive professional communication with colleagues in the fresh air in the country club "Festival"

- Are you participating? Be active! Participation in ProductCamp Minsk is free, but everyone present should contribute to the program of the event: actively share information, talk about the practices and projects implemented. In the application for participation it is necessary to indicate the topic on which you are ready to submit a small but informative report. Participants who will offer the most relevant and topical issues will have the opportunity to prepare a blitz performance or hold a round table within the ProductCamp.
- Experts from all over the CIS. The best Product Managers from all over Belarus, Ukraine, Russia and other CIS countries traditionally gather at ProductCamp. Want to make sure? View ProductCamp materials.
The exact program will be published later.

When and where?
Dates: October 17-18
Venue: Country Club "Festival" . A free transfer will be organized to the meeting venue.
Participation fee: free of charge. Active participation is required (report / volunteering).

I want to participate!
To apply for participation in ProductCamp Minsk 2015 fill out the form . Please refer responsibly to the information provided, since it is on the basis of it that the decision on inviting you to the meeting will be made.

Accommodation and meals
Planning to participate two days? Then you can stay overnight in the hotel complex at the club. For booking you have 24 rooms with 3 beds. Room rate - $ 55 (payment in Belarusian rubles). Alcohol and additional food is also paid by the participant.

What is ProductCamp?
ProductCamp is an event in BarCamp's format for product management and marketing managers and is part of a series of global non-conferences productcamp.org .
At the moment, this is practically the only specialized event in Eastern Europe where you can meet such a large number of professionals in the field of product management and marketing.
As participants we invite industry professionals working in positions and roles: Product Director (Product Director), Product Manager (Product Manager), Product Marketing Manager (Product Marketing Manager), Marketing Manager (Marketing Manager), Brand Manager (Brand Manager).

Why do we do it?
We want experienced professionals and beginners in the field of product management to familiarize themselves, establish connections, exchange experiences, improve their qualifications and market qualifications as a result.
What are the performances?
Topics of speeches, round tables, seminars and trainings will be devoted to:
  • market research, competitive analysis,
  • working with innovation
  • creating a product concept, defining a product strategy,
  • positioning, pricing,
  • developing and managing requirements
  • product portfolio management,
  • growing the product expertise in the company,
  • sales support
  • product lifecycle management.
