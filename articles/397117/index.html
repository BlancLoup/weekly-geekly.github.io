Unevisable chips Sailfish OS

Hello, reader!

Today I would like to highlight some of the unobtrusive Sailfish OS chips. The article is more focused on users of Sailfish OS, but the "adherents" of other platforms, I hope, will also be interesting, they can appreciate the degree of convenience and the readiness of the system for the end user. If you are ready, then go under the cut!



Did you know that:


1. If you hold your finger on the application and turn the smartphone, the application will NOT change the orientation.
Imagine by example: you were browsing the Internet from your smartphone while standing, then you decided to lie down (the orientation lock of the phone was turned off) so that the orientation did not change, just hold your finger on the screen while the smartphone changes its position in space.

2. Double clicking on the hardware lock button unlocks your device and you immediately get to the Home Screen, bypassing the Lock screen.
Please note that double clicking does not work if you have a password when unlocking.

3. Since version 2.0.2, Sailfish OS has a call recording feature.
To activate it, go to Settings (Applications) - Applications (Apps) - "Call Recording" (Call Recording).

Note:
In general, in Settings (Settings) - the Applications section (Apps), you can configure many applications authored by Jolla herself, as well as perform some actions on Android applications (clear the cache, stop the program, and so on).

4. Double-pressing the lock button, if the phone is not locked, will lock the phone, followed by vibration, indicating that the phone is locked.
Example of use: the phone is in your pocket and you are not sure if it is locked, but you don‚Äôt want to get it. Then you just need to find the lock button and double-click until you feel the vibration.

5. From the Tutorial application, you can exit immediately, for this, at the very beginning of the hour, click on the corners, starting from the top left.

6. Since version 2.0.2, a simple out-of-the-box file browser has appeared, to find it, go to Settings (Settings) - Storage (Storage) - External memory / Memory card (User data) - swipe from top to bottom - File Manager option (File manager).

7. Since version 2.0.2, it is possible to take screenshots, to do this, perform a half-second press on both volume buttons.

8. For connoisseurs of the Semantic Web : some technologies of the Semantic Web are used in Sailfish OS:

- Change the wallpaper to your own picture only from the gallery. To do this, open the picture in the gallery (Gallery), swipe from top to bottom, then select the option "Create atmosphere" ("Create ambience"). You will be taken to the atmosphere settings page, there you can adjust the colors to your taste or simply leave it as it is and rejoice;

-in Settings (Settings) in the "Text Input" (Text input), you can enable the separation of the keyboard in landscape mode;
