RUVDS and Huawei start working together



In March of this year, RUVDS , a Russian hosting provider providing virtual server services, began cooperation with Huawei, a major supplier of communication solutions and hardware infrastructure for modern data centers. In particular, within the framework of the joint project, Huawei intends to equip the RUVDS data center located in the Moscow region with the latest equipment - this guarantees a faster and more stable operation of VDS / VPS servers located on virtual machines.
RUVDS, which is one of the largest Russian hosting providers, is especially famous in the corporate sphere - more than one Russian organization uses IAAS services. The clients of the hosting provider are commercial banks, social companies, as well as BCS Company, Coworking 14, FINAM JSC and other large financial institutions.
According to Nikita Tsaplin, founder and leader of RUVDS, collaboration with Huawei will allow the company to advance to a new level - and offer its customers even better and more modern services, stable and trouble-free virtual servers .
In the near future, RUVDS and Huawei have close partnerships with the introduction of liquid cooling technology into Russian conditions, which is not yet very popular in Russia. However, the project will optimize the work of the data center and significantly reduce the inevitable costs. And that means - to improve the quality of the final offer, while maintaining reasonable prices for the product.
Recall that the company Huawei, which began work in 1987, is now widely known throughout the world as a supplier of reliable, fast and the most modern equipment for infocommunications. The field of activity of the company covers telecommunications, virtualization, cloud and terminal technologies. Solutions from Huawei are used by enterprises working with television and Internet technologies in more than one hundred and fifty countries of the world.
According to the company, entering the Russian market is a particularly important task for Huawei. Tight cooperation with a well-known Russian provider in the field of virtualization and cloud technologies can be the first step towards a significant expansion of activities. Such mutually beneficial collaboration will allow Huawei to occupy a rather extensive niche in the Russian segment of communication services.
