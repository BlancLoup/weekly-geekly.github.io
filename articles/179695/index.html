Sony refill: waterproof Xperia ZR



Among the 2013 Xperia Sony's waterproof models, the Xperia Z came: after the 5-inch Sony Xperia Z, the 4.6 "Sony Xperia ZR was put into operation. The model has improved waterproof performance from its predecessors: the IP55 / IP58 level allows the Xperia ZR to withstand staying fresh water at a depth of 1.5 m for 30 minutes. A hardware button to turn on the camera allows you to easily take pictures or shoot HD video under water. Burst mode allows you to take pictures at a speed of 10 frames per second.



The main characteristics of the smartphone Xperia ZR:

Dimensions and weight: 131,3x67,3x10,5 mm, 138 g
Display: 4.6 ", 1280x720
Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 Pro 1.5 GHz quad-core
Memory: 8 GB, 2 GB RAM, microSD memory card slot
OS: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean)
Camera: 13 MP with Exmor RS matrix and HDR support
Battery: 2300 mAh
Protection: waterproof and dustproof according to IP55 / IP58 standards
Connectivity: LTE, NFC

For our market, the Xperia ZR will come with a DK28 docking station included. Possible colors: traditional black, white, pink and original mint-green.
