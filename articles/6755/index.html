A person's age can be determined by the history of surfing.

Researchers from the company Xerox found a way to quite accurately determine the age of a person without even asking him - just about the history of surfing. Such a method can be used in online authorization systems, in online stores, as well as everywhere where it is desirable to preserve the anonymity of the user and conduct verification quickly.
