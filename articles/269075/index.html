Web Camp 2015: how it was

Somehow it happened that this event was avoided in the Habr√©. And even m31 either forgot or did not have time to write about it.
I decided to make a contribution and tell about my impressions about Web Camp 2015.
This event was held in the framework of Odessa Innovation Week from 2 to 10 July.



About the event

It took place in Odessa and consisted of 3 directions:

  • Tech direction
  • Business direction
  • StartUp direction

Tech direction included such thematic days:

  • Back-end Developers Day,
  • Frontend Developers Day,
  • Designers Day,
  • Project Management Day,
  • Mobile Day,
  • as well as the Hardware Lab Conference, dedicated to the Internet of things, and Robotics School - a master class in robotics.

Business direction was represented by threads:

  • SalesLab. Product,
  • SalesLab. Outsource,
  • master classes in effective sales and business development BizDevCamp

StartUp direction included:

  • StartUp Lab conference
  • and StartUp Crush Test

According to a report from the organizers, 1,714 participants from 26 cities and 6 countries attended the event.

Since the topics about robotics, project management and sales in IT were transferred to other sections, here I want to dwell on such theme days as Back-end Developers Day, Frontend Developers Day, Designers Day.

Back-end Developers Day

It passed in three parallel threads: Architecture & HighLoad, DevOps & Cloud, and Frameworks & DB. The first covers such aspects of web development as large project architectures, the development of high-load systems and load testing. The second is approaches to the development and operation of software - DevOps, cloud technologies and their capabilities for building reliable backends. The third is the secrets of various frameworks and databases, their comparison and selection for certain tasks.
