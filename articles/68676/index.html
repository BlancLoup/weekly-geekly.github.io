Ruby And Rails barcamp - Kiev, September 26

Despite the growing popularity of the Ruby language and the Rails web framework in the west, these undoubtedly remarkable technologies are still very poorly covered in Ukraine. That is why the coffee'n'code initiative group decided to organize the first in Ukraine barcamp dedicated to Ruby and RoR.

Both experienced Ruby programmers and beginners or interested ones are invited to participate in the barcamp. Accordingly, reports are planned for both advanced topics and more general ones.
Well, of course, we will be very happy to have speakers who will get all the attention and gratitude of the public: have the framework been optimized? Come up with know-how? Did you create your own heme or did you research it before someone else's holes? - Tell us about it!

In order to demonstrate the power of Ruby, we also plan to conduct Show Time projects in Ruby created by the participants of the camp.

In general, go to the site , register, discuss and communicate in the Google-group and of course we are waiting for you on September 26 in person. (we will announce the venue a bit later and notify all registered by e-mail)
