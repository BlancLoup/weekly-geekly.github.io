New multiplayer Star Wars shooter with movie-level graphics



Electronic Arts has released a trailer for its upcoming Star Wars: Battlefront game, featuring strikingly realistic graphics. If you do not take into account the faces of the characters (the work on the engine is not finished yet, so improvements can come), everything that happens on the screen can easily be unknowingly confused with the film. Anyway, such films are made with the help of CGI.

The game is built on the third version of the Frostbite engine, which the developers of EA Digital Illusions CE used in the Battlefield series of games. In fact, SWB will be familiar to fans of Battlefield multiplayer military shooters, transferred to the SW universe. Battles will occur on the surface of the planets, with the possibility of using ground and air vehicles, but without the possibility of going into space.
