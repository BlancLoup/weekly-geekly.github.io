Samsung Galaxy Tab 8.9: millimeters



Curiously: this material is devoted to the Samsung tablet, but I start it with the words about the Apple tablet, because if it were not for the iPad 2 from Cupertino, the new Galaxy Tab in the form in which it is now, we could not have seen!



What is what?


Earlier this year, Samsung introduced a couple of rather curious tablets, on which many pinned high hopes. And our team, having turned them in their hands at one of the exhibitions, was generally pleased with the gadgets. But on March 2, Apple showed the world the second version of the iPad, and then it turned out that the most critical to Samsung products are not journalists, not analysts, and not consumers, but the company's management itself - to the point that it was decided to postpone the release date of new tabs until summer and completely redo them so that they can truly compete with the iPad 2.

Display


The size of the diagonal of the screen Galaxy Tab 8.9 is reflected in its name. Otherwise, I can not say that the display is something particularly remarkable. The usual TFT-matrix, moderately bright and contrast, but after literally oozing the Super AMOLED Plus display color in the Galaxy S II - this, of course, is not that ... However, if Samsung had decided to embed organic LED displays in the tablets, they would soar to the heavens, since in this case they grow in proportion to the increase in the diagonal.

Iron


Despite the fact that Samsung has its own dual-core Orion processor, the Galaxy Tab 8.9 used the development of NVIDIA - Tegra 2. This chipset, as it was already clear from smart phones based on it, allows Android to work without brakes. Indeed, the tablet is absolutely not inclined to think about the tasks. Is that when scrolling through the desktops there is some delay, but I write it off more on the imperfection of the screen sensor than on the lack of processor performance.



It is strange that this tablet scored a total of 1654 points in the Quadrant test. Usually gadgets based on NVIDIA Tegra 2 show at least two pieces with a trifle ...

6000 mAh built into the Galaxy Tab, the battery is enough to drive the tablet literally all day. He started asking me to the outlet only after 11 hours of active testing. The rival of the Galaxy Tab is, of course, the Apple iPad 2. On the side of the ‚Äúapple‚Äù is the prevailing ecosystem, millions of quality applications and nation-wide love. Summary


Samsung did it. Galaxy Tab 8.9 is the first tablet that can really compete on equal terms with the second iPad. But it is in a vacuum, that is, purely technologically. And in the real world, competition is not only between gigahertz, megabytes and millimeters, but also between prices. So, in the pre-order on the Euroset Network site, the Galaxy Tab 8.9 costs 25990. For these (and sometimes smaller) money, many potential buyers will prefer to buy a more popular Apple fashionable tablet than Samsung will decide to try. Only one way out - to reduce the price.
