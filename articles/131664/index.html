November 2 - Start-up gatherings: The procedure for obtaining the status of Skolkovo resident for innovative companies

Skolkovo acts as a point of attraction for science and research and provides project participants with additional benefits and opportunities, opens doors and helps develop.
Participants of Skolkovo can be 2 levels: residents (receive tax and some other benefits) and projects that receive investments from Skolkovo in the form of gratuitous grants. To receive investment, you must first become a resident.

Ildar Abdrashitov, Project Manager of the IT Park Business Incubator, will tell you how to become one.

Theme of the meeting: The procedure for obtaining the status of Skolkovo resident for innovative companies

Summary:

1. The application procedure for obtaining the status of Skolkovo resident

2. The list of documents required for the formation of an application for obtaining the status of Skolkovo resident

3. Conditions for obtaining the Skolkovo resident status

4. Criteria for the selection of companies as residents of Skolkovo

5. Preferences received by the company when becoming a resident of the Skolkovo project
