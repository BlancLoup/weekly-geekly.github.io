cine.com.ua - Movie cinema for mobile



The cine.com.ua service we developed solves one simple problem of choice - which movie to go to in the next couple of hours. The idea was born by itself, when I often walked with a girl around the city and there was a desire to go to the movies.


Here you can:
  • view sessions for the next 2 hours
  • find in which cinema is the film of interest
  • open the poster of the selected cinema for the near future
  • study the short description of the film, see the footage and download the trailer (.3gp format)

Poster is available for the main cinemas of the cities of Ukraine.

Appearance is designed extremely minimalist in order to speed up the process of downloading from mobile phones and save money for traffic.

In fact, the only such service Yandex.Fish (mobile) did not suit for several reasons, although some interface ideas were borrowed from there.

As it turned out, some of our friends use the service even from stationary computers due to the simplicity of the interface and the lack of a heap of intrusive banners.

IPhone Version


At the request of workers a few months after the start, we developed a free iPhone application with the same functionality and, naturally, a much more pleasant interface.
