Protest website closed in Azerbaijan

The public campaign to collect signatures against price increases in Azerbaijan ended before it could begin. Providers of the republic have blocked access to the site susmayaq.biz , which was a protest, addressed to the head of state. This is stated in the statement of the author of the site Bakhtiyar Hajiyev, reports REGNUM . Susmayaq.biz server is located in the Netherlands, and there are no problems with visiting the site outside of Azerbaijan.

On January 8, the price of gasoline in Azerbaijan rose by 50%, the fare became more expensive by 35%, the payment for electricity increased by 3 times, and for water - by 4.
