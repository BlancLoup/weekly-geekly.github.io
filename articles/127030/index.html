Pinpoint - a project for which the whole world is not enough

Already more than half a year, as I got! Hard hit the team of like-minded people. This is a time of hard and quite successful work in the Pinpoint team ( http://pin.pn ). Herodotus, Marco Polo, Vasco da Gama, Christopher Columbus, Francis Drake, Athanasius Nikitin, Fernand Magellan. Almost every one of us, at least once in childhood or adolescence, dreamed of inscribing his name in this series of famous travelers and seafarers. And I am sure that dreams must come true! Therefore, sensing ourselves as the masters of our own destiny, we came up with and realized our dream - the Pinpoint project, which allows each person to discover his own world and show how his life proceeds in a personal coordinate system.

Why reinvent the wheel ...


We have seen many services: different and diverse, with interesting functionality and with unnecessary features, convenient and sometimes mysterious. Why reinvent the wheel, if we have already invented all the details that work reliably? Therefore, in Pinpoint you can find a lot of what services have geolocation information. We just made it easier, clearer and more interesting!
All information that is accumulated on the service in the form of pins (points with semantic content) is formed in a single database and immediately displayed on a single global map of the world.
So, with a pin you can easily mark a house, a cafe, a place for a party, a monument, etc. At the same time, an individual name and a page with information ( http://pin.pn/Manneken-Pis ) are associated with each such object marked with a pin. At the same time, each pin has different levels of privacy (now there are two of them - private and public, but the third is already being tested - the secret), which means you control those who open this information and who do not. In short, you can create your version of the world and dispose of it completely. I think it's cool!



Different places on the map, marked with pins, we can combine into arbitrary thematic groups, sets: visible and invisible to the general public. Conveniently! So, if we continue the theme of the sights of Brussels, then they can be viewed on the set http://pin.pn/set/2613 . Yandex.Maps or Google Maps? Each map service has its own priorities. It is known that Google Maps Europe and North America are developed in more detail than Yandex.Maps. But for Russia, Yandex.Map has a clear handicap. That's why we made it so that in Pinpoint you can easily switch between the two most popular card systems in the world and Russia. Moreover, on which engine the pin is set, it will be used by default for it. Plans to connect Bing as an additional alternative.
