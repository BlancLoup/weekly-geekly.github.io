Browser and machine

To take readings, Firefox 20 was taken on a machine with xUbuntu 12.04. On other machines and browsers, sites may differ, but not by much.

To automate the process, add-ons were taken:

findings


The average size of sites vary in 1-1.5 megabytes. Ideally, if your site weighs slightly less than others. Rising number of views, visits. Therefore, if you have not yet optimized your offspring, see what you can do.

Best to see what is wrong with Page Speed .

The main points about which you can read in any article on optimization:
  • Optimize images. They usually make up a huge part of the weight of the site.
  • Compress CSS and JS. Sometimes it turns out to be bored twice.
  • Use Ajax to load pages and items, not reload.
  • Cache everything you can. The main thing is to monitor the load on the server.
  • Enable gzip compression
  • Use delayed image loading. I connected Lazyload on my sites. I recommend, especially on mobile sites or mobile versions of the site.
  • Css sprites. Group pictures. Both the client and server will be welcome.
  • Remove all the excess! Do not put heavy Flash prikolyuhi, online radio and auto play videos unnecessarily.

PS

The tips I gave above will speed up your site, and users will be more comfortable. However, if your site is super cool, then no megabytes can stop the user from accessing it. If your site is so-so, optimization will do little.
