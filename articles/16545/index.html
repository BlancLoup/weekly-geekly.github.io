Installing Programs on iPod Touch

ATTENTION!
Everything you do - you do at your own peril and risk!

Installing applications for iPod Touch is initially blocked by the manufacturer. To unlock, use the well-known method for unlocking your iPhone - iJailbreak. For this we need to connect to the Internet via Wi-Fi.
