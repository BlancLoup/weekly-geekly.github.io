Classifier Base

After reading this habratopic, I finally decided to tell the public about my non-profit startup and generally open access to it. This project is made by programmers for programmers. Its essence is simple - all classifiers on one site.

Each classifier may contain several tables. The content of any table can be viewed directly on the site or download the entire classifier. The following formats are available for download: XML, SQL-dump or simply in Excel format. In the future, we will attach a graphic diagram with the relationships between the tables to the classifier profile.

All your wishes, suggestions and classifiers , please send to class.upload@gmail.com . You can add to karma by drtoller (i.e. me), derbov , @saratovdae.
