5 activity trackers that motivate: how to walk more, relying on electronics

Tracking and analysis of activity is a useful and necessary thing, but sometimes there is nothing to analyze! We rummaged in the warehouse and chose the top five trackers, which not only count, but also motivate to move. Who cares, I ask under the cat.



Let's start with the simple.

Lifetrak - motivation by hand

The easiest way to get yourself to move, by being independent, is to put on the R450 and start the Workaut mode. In this case, at a specified time, you will receive notifications from your watch that it is time to go. Of course, many trackers have this feature, but the Salutron gadget has several more useful features.



  • There is a heart rate monitor
  • The ability to receive Push-notifications (non-Russified)
  • Smart alarm clock
  • Water resistant
  • Up to 6 months of battery life
  • Light analytics


A curious story with light in general: a well-known fact about the positive effect on the nervous system of shades of blue "added color" to the device, and Salutron, together with the University of Massachusetts, invited 10 volunteers to study. 9 of them as a result noted that they had enough data with Lifetrak R450 to manage the psycho-emotional state more easily, and as a result, the company even patented its gadget as a device contributing to the prevention of seasonal affective disorders.

  • The cost of 7 190 rubles


Runtastic Orbit - like an Orbit with the taste of calories burned

With all the "ordinary" with the calculation of steps, calories and distance, Runtastic added a chip - Happy Tracking, or the ability to memorize and summarize the happy and joyful moments of life. This should, in the opinion, stimulate creators to all sorts of activities and actions in order to find those that are fun!



  • Synchronized via Bluetooth
  • Shows statistics and progress of successes and achievements.
  • Integration with social networks
  • Built-in memory up to 7 days
  • Vibration alarm

  • The cost of 8 490 rubles
