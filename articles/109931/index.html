Teamfinder.ru - a project for those who like to play football

I was asked to bring to your attention the social network "The meeting - football!" - Teamfinder.ru . The developers hope for constructive criticism and comments on the merits that will help make the site better.

In the comments, the authors of the resource will answer questions.

The idea of development is simple and clear - to create a convenient search service for players, teams, and opponents to play amateur football. In addition, the site should be a convenient platform for communication of people united by a common interest.



After a simple registration procedure, the newcomer immediately has the opportunity to find like-minded people, form his team, find a field for the game, set a training or match date, and make a call to the opponent.

The project geography is wide and supports all regions of Russia. That is, in any city you can create a team and announce the recruitment of players. Now, while the project is young, Moscow and St. Petersburg are the most active. But the audience is growing and regions. There are plans to add the CIS (requests come from Kazakhstan and Ukraine), but so far they are developing only in Russia.

We give a description of the main modules on which the site structure is based and the general idea is implemented.

Friend search

You can find people who are ready to play by narrowing down the search by several parameters - city (district), status (independent, team player, captain) and role (goalkeeper, left back, right midfielder, center forward, etc.).

Team Search

You can find the necessary team both by one and by several criteria: by name (in the future, we think it will be convenient), place (city, district), skills, specialization, gender, age, etc. You can view the composition and join.

Search for an opponent

The search mechanism is similar to the search team - only now the opponent can be called to battle according to the game calendar of this team or offer your date and place.

Field search

To help organize matches and training sessions, the site offers the ability to select fields with a filter by key characteristics (city, field type, coverage). Now the field base is partially formed by Moscow and St. Petersburg. In the fill by region, we are still counting on user assistance - there is an opportunity to add new fields to the database.

The project is young, but it is already clear that the idea is claimed by the audience, judging by the responses. The guys want to play football.

We invite you to join and try the service in terms of usability.

Thanks for the comments.
