We will help with engineering analysis and calculations


Dear Habraengineers and Habrakonstruktory!

If you are working on a project of any product or structure, and you need help with calculations and analysis of its properties, then you are welcome to us!
Our specialists (represented by the authorized distributor of Autodesk of the POINT Company and the Research and Development Center Tenzor) will render such consultations absolutely free of charge in the framework of the new project Masters of Simulation.

Anyone can take part in the project: a company representative or an independent designer from Russia. To do this, just need to fill out an application for participation, describing your project, the desired task and the desired result.

As a result, you will receive materials that:

The procedure for participation in the project Masters of Simulation is simple. "At the entrance" the participant provides as input data his 3D or 2D model and description of the tasks, as well as information about himself and his organization. "At the exit" receives video and text materials, which describe the solution of these and related problems, as well as emerging problems. These materials are published online, and links to them are sent to the "customer".

At the same time, POINT Company and Autodesk will be able to use the obtained information (product geometry, problem statement, project results, customer information) for open publications, marketing materials and others.

Those participants who want to see the solution to their problems, but do not want to give out their know-how or violate the terms of contracts, can modify the initial data of the geometry and setting.
