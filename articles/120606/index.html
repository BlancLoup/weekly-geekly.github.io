ASUS at Computex 2011

The era of tablets has come. In Russia, as well as all over the world, prolonged deliveries of the tablet computer ASUS Eee Pad Transformer are waiting. The company said that they managed to cope with the shortage of components, which prevented the increase in supply. As a result, supplies will start to grow in the near future. If in May 100 thousand units of the Eee Pad Transformer were shipped, in June the volume of deliveries will increase to 300 thousand units.



In the meantime, ASUS prepared several new devices, about which, probably, many have already heard, and some have even seen it. All the details and a small spoiler about the Eee Pad Transformer inside.


In winter, at CES 2011, ASUS presented 4 tablet devices, which we have already described. This development tablet computers are not completed. ASUS announced the use of an interesting concept based not least on the success of Eee netbooks, which created a new niche in the computer business. The smartphone and the tablet complement each other: for the phone the tablet is a docking station, a charger, for the tablet a phone is an internet modem and a camera that is activated when the phone is placed in the compartment.
