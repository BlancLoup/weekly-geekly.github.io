Interview with members of the project Birdymail

Introduced at the beginning of April on habre, the online project Birdymail is a platform for communication between users who wish to transfer a parcel to another location, without resorting to the services of Post of Russia and other suppliers.

They represented Birdymail in an interview with TelNews about the features of their project and the financial component of its activities.



What is the reason for the obligation to fill in the fields ‚ÄúFull name‚Äù when registering on the site?

- In fact, this is just an additional opportunity that allows people, communicating on the site, to contact each other by name. In this case, no one requires a person to specify his name and surname. Many registered users simply specify any nicknames. For example, we have a user named Sarancha Ivanovna. Well and good. Specifying your first and last name or not is a user's right.

On the other hand, if a person indicated his name and surname during registration, he, in our opinion, would react to what he writes and does on the site, more responsibly. For our project, the degree of responsibility of its users is important.

- Do you plan to somehow solve the issue of mail delivery guarantees - to a greater extent than is being implemented now? Do you have plans, for example, to cooperate with any insurance company?

- Of course, the question of guarantees is very important. Pay attention, for example, to such sites as Molotok.ru, or Free-lance.ru. On these resources, guarantees are built on the feedback system. After completion of the transaction, counterparties leave feedback to each other. The number of reviews and user rating show the security level of the transaction with him. At the same time, users transfer a lot of money to each other for, say, work that has not yet been completed or goods that have not been received, focusing only on reputation. These resources are successfully developing, and we can say that such a guarantee system has proven itself well and works not only in RuNet, but throughout the world.

As for our security system, it, firstly, provides for exactly the same review system based on the gradual formation of reputation. Well and, besides, and most importantly, people, passing the parcels, meet in person. In this case, the sender can always ask to present documents from the person delivering the parcel, and find out his mobile phone number.

Thus, at present, our guarantee system is even stronger than on other resources based on user interaction. As for cooperation with insurance companies, of course, we plan to do this. In the future, I think every sender will be able to insure his package online on our resource.

- What about the contents of the package? If a customer transfers cocaine or a weapon in a bag with clothes, does the ‚Äúsite bear no responsibility‚Äù or will you still somehow try to help identify such customers?

- So far we have not recorded such cases (and I hope it will not be). In the fight against the possibility of such cases, first of all, the same review system will help. Secondly, the person delivering the parcel, of course, always has the right to know what he delivers, and I think that if he has any doubts about this, he can ask the sender to dispel them. If such cases do occur, we will take steps to compile a blacklist of unscrupulous senders in order to protect those delivering the parcels from any problems.

- Some Birdymail users agree with each other about mail transfer fees. Why not make it easier for them and enter the appropriate fields on the site?

- I think that in the near future we will do it. However, I would like to say that the service was conceived simply as a place where some good people help others with delivery. The remuneration was not at the forefront - therefore, such a field has not been entered so far. Well, the absence of this field does not prevent people from reporting that the delivery will not be free of charge.

If to compare with porucheno.ru, then:

Birdymail:

2) we have everything for free;
3) our website allows you to deliver the parcel at the destination.

Porucheno.ru:
1) not a web2.0 site, users communicate only with the site administration, and not among themselves;
2) the administration provides only paid services;
3) with the help of porucheno.ru you can not send a parcel to the destination.

In fact, there is not a single similar feature of our project with porucheno.ru, which is, in fact, a corporate site on which you can order a service (by the way, you can do this not on the site, but by phone). They have representatives in different cities that perform this service. But they will not deliver the parcel. At best, they will simply go to the post office and send it (or send it by courier Birdymail).
