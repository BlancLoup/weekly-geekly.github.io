Impressed by the first meeting of speakers

On Friday evening, the first meeting of the RIT-2007 conference speakers was held in the conference hall near Tverskaya. It was a truly fascinating sight to see so many venerable developers from leading companies in one place. Under the pretext of organizing sections and the appointment of curators, an active process of familiarizing participants took place, discussing topics for reports. Since the meeting took place in a relaxed atmosphere, the disputes did not subside for a minute, and reached their apogee on a coffee break. Along the way, some topics of the reports disappeared, but immediately new applications took their place. The process of redrawing the themes of the sections continuously went on - some had to be merged, and some had to be beaten into several parts. In the coming days, the results should appear on the conference website RIT2007.Ru .
Obviously, the meeting turned out to be fruitful and timely, because now everyone understands the immediate plans and received a clear guide to action. It seems that we will soon see a list of reports with theses!
