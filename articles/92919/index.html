Discussion of the future of Flash on May 12 in the VMK MSU building

Experienced web developers know that Flash was first a means of animation, then quickly found application in advertising and animated series, and then became a platform for online creative work in various directions. Millions of applications are now using Flash technology - super popular social and casual games, video services and corporate systems, websites and (alas) voracious banners. This variety and quantity became possible due to the speed of development, graphic capabilities and availability of Flash Player on 99% of computers. At the same time, Flash affects the development of neighbors over the network - AJAX, web animation, new techniques in the UI / User Experience Design.

The conference " The Future of the Flash Platform " coincided with the release of Flex 4 and it will consider the new functionality of Flash Builder 4, as well as promising areas of development and Flash monetization. And, of course, let's talk about who wins whom - Flash or HTML5, is there any Flash on the iPad, what will Flash be like in 10 years and much more. The creators of interesting projects - 3D-engine and Facebook Desktop Client with touch-interface - will share the details of the development. Here is the conference program with speakers, and these are Flash experts, living evangelists from Adobe and community leaders of Flash developers in Russia, the CIS and Europe. Within five hours they will be telling and answering questions from participants.

Entrance to the conference is free, and registration is required.

The conference will begin on May 12 at 4:00 pm - and, by the way, the next day Flash Gamm conference will take place in Moscow.

Come chat about Flash and more.
