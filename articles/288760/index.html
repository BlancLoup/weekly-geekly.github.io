Why online news sucks? 10 reasons

Maybe it's not good to mock the wretched, but the current news sites deserve a whipping. Consider the list of their sins.

1. News per thousand words


One of the reasons people go to the Internet is to get news quickly. So, dear candidates for the Pulitzer Prize, we will not scroll your news for half a year. Either break it into pieces, or erase all unnecessary that you have written there.

2. Slideshow on one hundred pages


But let's not rush into extremes. It is not necessary to divide the news into parts in such a way that one page and one sentence each fall on one page. Maybe you need to unscrew more banners, but it also breaks us to click thirty times to read one (!) News.

3. Unfolding rich media advertising


You open the news of the case, you carefully begin to read. Suddenly, bang! From somewhere above or from the corner comes the miracle of interactive animation. You start frantically searching for the ‚ÄúClose‚Äù button or some cross, but it was shoved exactly where no one would guess. All this bothers you and you close the window. Familiar?

4. Links do not smell


Often the news tells about some interesting piece or site, but does not give any links. You have to google yourself. Sometimes it remains on the conscience of the incompetent journalist, but often the reason is different - this is the editors of the site are afraid that we will go to another site. Do not be afraid! We really leave.

5. Registration


Thank God, many are no longer asking everyone to register. However, there are still dinosaurs still requiring mandatory registration. They do not understand that it is easier for us to refuse to read their news than to register on a site that we will never return to anyway.

6. Wacky design


Wherever you go, the first pages look like the HTML wizards are nauseous. Endless columns of text and links, all in porridge, no separation of content. If we are going to sculpt the column, look at the Alltop site. Maybe it will help.

7. Full-screen advertising


You come to read news, and to you advertising left on all screen. And not at all on the topic. We understand that you need to earn money, but we are also annoyed.

8. Looking for firefighters, looking for the police.


Watch the news on TV. Here the reporter has finished the story and says: "You can read more about this on our site." - And they give the site address on the screen. Go to the site and what we see? Nothing. Search for a link to this story will evaporate. It is not even in search form.

9. Popups


You what? Do you still use pop-ups on your site? No, what's the truth? Is it real?

10. Trolls and flame


Well, there are plenty of these guys everywhere, not just on news sites. Since the news itself is sometimes quite contradictory in its essence, there is a place to roam. It starts runaway, threats, disassembly, insults. Try delete, immediately start screaming about freedom of speech. But you still need to clean the forum.
