The best smartphone released in 2007

2007 was especially fruitful for all sorts of innovations in the environment of multimedia mobile computers. The number of smartphones and communicators released exceeded all possible facets! Apple gave us a long-awaited iPhone, Nokia literally flooded us with new items! This year we received the first smartphone with a 5MP camera and an excellent xenon flash. Apple introduced us to a multi-touch screen. Samsung - a phone with two simultaneously working SIM cards, Duos. Nokia began to use built-in 3d video accelerators, thanks to which, the quality of games and videos approached portable game consoles. In general, this year the technical progress of portable mobile devices has gone far forward. I want to ask the respected Habaraludey, as the most advanced users of various Hi-tech devices, which mobile phone, smartphone or communicator impressed you this year? You can also leave your vote in favor of the super-mobile 2007.
