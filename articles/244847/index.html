Experiment: Free translate keywords and application descriptions for the App Store and Google Play in exchange for statistics



We already wrote how translation of keywords for an iOs application increased the number of downloads by 767% , as well as how the localization of the interface helped raise the application to dozens of positions up in local Google Play tops . In both cases, not a cent was spent on advertising applications.

We want to make sure that these are not isolated cases, but a really working way to make the application more popular in local markets. In this regard, we invite all developers to take part in the experiment:
  1. We translate the description and keywords for your application page on Google Play and / or the App Store from English into any of our list of 40+ languages for free .
  2. You do not use other methods of promotion (no advertising, in one word) for a month.
  3. You share with us a schedule of downloads, purchases and rating of your application in the local store.
  4. We share the information received with the world in our blog.


Ready to participate in our experiment and maybe grow up in the tops? Then fill out this form ! We are waiting for your applications until December 10th. However, if there are too many applications, we will not be able to physically transfer all of them. We also reserve the right to refuse translation without giving a reason. Nothing personal, as they say.

We promise to tell about the results here, in our blog - subscribe not to miss.

UPD: Extend acceptance of applications until December 15.
