Meeting of mobile game developers, November 27, Moscow



November 27, we invite all developers of games for mobile devices and those who are only interested in this topic at our meeting.

Meeting mobile game developers is a good opportunity to share experiences and ideas with other participants and experts.

You can discuss your ideas, prototypes, ready-made games, find new people and get expert advice.

We allocate special time for your presentations . Presentations of ideas and projects will be held in the form of short pitches.

Come and bring your scripts, sketches and prototypes with you.

schedule


18.30 - 19.00 - Gathering participants, coffee break
19.00 - 19.30 - Mobile player: who is he? (Konstantin Sakhnov, Senior Game Designer for Mail.Ru Group)
19.30 - 20.00 - Back-end architecture of the game server (Vitaly Chibrikov, Mail.Ru Group)
20.00 - 20.30 - Application as a game or how to lure a user into an application for a long time (Konstantin Degtyarev, Head of Mobile Direction, LinguaLeo)
20.30 - 22.00 - Presentations of your ideas and projects (according to preliminary requests), work with experts.

Location


Address: Moscow, st. Krylatskaya, 17, building 1, Business Center "Krylatsky Hills"
From the metro station "Krylatskoe" runs a free minibus with the identification plate "Krylatsky Hills".

check in


Our meetings are open and accessible to everyone . But, in order to correctly calculate the number of places, food and make the meeting more comfortable for you, we ask, if possible, to leave an application for registration.

Registration is available here .

If you want to tell about your project - send the name and the name of the project to this address or leave in the comments to the post.

We are waiting for your visit!
