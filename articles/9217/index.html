You can watch YouTube videos on Apple TV

At the D: All Things Digital conference, Steve Jobs talked about collaboration with YouTube, so next month you can watch videos on Apple TV from there.

Apple TV is a set-top box that plays, purchased, or downloaded for free via the Internet on a computer on your TV screen. Now users mostly watch TV programs or movies purchased from the iTunes Store. Apple TV does not have a built-in browser through which other videos could be downloaded, although you can download them from a computer.

In a month, Apple TV owners will have access to the most popular YouTube videos, which will be converted to the native H.264 format for the console. A full catalog of clips should also appear this year, but a little later.
