How to write a game in two days

If you like computer games, you probably thought about writing your own game. And if you have not done it yet, then because:
  • failed to put together a team (programmer, designer, screenwriter, animator, artist - underline the necessary);
  • no free time.

Perhaps you had an interesting idea. Maybe you even started to work it out. But then, for everyday problems, bustle and work, this dream somehow forgot itself. And now you make websites, program in java, draw business cards or write articles ... But sometimes, usually out of place, the dream makes itself felt: it prevents you from falling asleep or distracts you from work, and you think: " Ok, I will deal with my affairs, my free time will appear I will assemble a team and do it! " And then again goes into the background.

How to live with it?

March 12-13 (this will be in 3 days) will take place in Moscow HackDay: Games , where we will protect you for two days from external irritants, provide an opportunity to recruit a team and this will allow you, finally, to realize your plans.

You will have two days - the perfect time to try writing a game.
You will have the opportunity to assemble a team to not work alone.
You will have a moment of glory, you will demonstrate your game to a large audience.

Procedure:
  • Register for the event.
  • If you already have an idea, post it to build a team. Or join the existing one .
  • Warn friends and relatives not to be disturbed at this time.
  • Come to HackDay on March 12 at 12:00.

Participation is free, prizes are attached.
There will be two workshops: development for Windows Phone 7 and development of games with augmented reality.

Waiting for you.

The event is held together with the movement. Begin! and with the support of the Russian Venture Company .
