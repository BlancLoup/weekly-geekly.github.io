Beeline and online appeal to the Federal Service for Supervision in the Field of Communications



In the comments to the latest topic about advertising SMS from Beeline, an interesting link to the online appeal to the Federal Service for Supervision in the Field of Communications, Information Technologies and Mass Communications (Roskomnadzor) appeared.

As one of the "victims", he compiled and sent his appeal. For anyone who wants to send a similar message, here is the text below. The text itself was not particularly thought out and was compiled for about 15 minutes, so any comments on its content are welcome.

On March 10, 2010, in violation of clause 1 and 2 of Article 18 of the Federal Law "On Advertising", the following advertising message was sent to my mobile number:

‚ÄúYour number is 89051111111 , you participate in the drawing of 1 million today and 30 million in the final, if you send SMS to 1010 to 22:00. Info 1010 ".

According to the information on the official website of the promotion at 1010.beeline.ru , the organizer of this promotion is Limited Liability Company Mobile Dialogue, registered at the address: 123077, Moscow, ul. 3rd Trunk, house 30, p. 1.

Also, in accordance with the public offer contract, located at the same address, except Mobile Dialogue LLC, the second party entering into this contract with the subscriber is Vympel-Communications OJSC (Beeline trademark). In addition, in the commercials of this lottery on television channels, it was the Beeline trademark owned by Vympel-Communications OJSC that was mentioned.

In accordance with the Statute on the Federal Service for Supervision in the Sphere of Telecommunications, Information Technologies and Mass Communications, this service exercises state control and supervision over the observance of the legislation of the Russian Federation in the sphere of mass media and mass communications, television broadcasting and broadcasting.

I ask you to find out why Mobile Dialogue LLC and Vympel-Communications OJSC violate the Advertising Law and take all necessary measures to prevent such violations from occurring in the future.

None of these companies have my prior consent to receive advertising messages , moreover, I have repeatedly applied to the support service of Vympel-Communications OJSC with a request to stop sending such messages to me .

The highlighted text is highlighted in red.

PPS From bigdogsru : Online application to the Federal Service for Supervision of Consumer Rights Protection and Human Welfare. (Rospotrebnadzor supervises consumer protection).

PPPS According to Uncle_Sam , supervision over the observance of the Federal Law "On Advertising" is entrusted to the Federal Antimonopoly Service (as stated in Chapter 5 of this law). I did not find forms of online circulation there, so they can write paper letters to a public reception office in Moscow (e-mail: delo@fas.gov.ru), or to the addresses of local administrations .

In the letters to the FAS 5 paragraph of the appeal can be replaced by:

In accordance with Chapter 5 of the Federal Law "On Advertising", the Federal Anti-Monopoly Service exercises state control over compliance with advertising legislation of the Russian Federation, including preventing violations of advertising legislation by individuals or legal entities and initiates and deals with cases of violations of the legislation of the Russian Federation about advertising.
