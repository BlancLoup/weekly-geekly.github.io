MI Guitar - guitar for those who want to play the guitar, but do not want to learn it



Startup Magic Instruments raises money on the IndieGoGo crowdfunding platform for its project - a guitar simulator. MI Guitar is an electronic instrument similar to a guitar, with buttons instead of strings on the fingerboard. Each button corresponds to a particular chord, and the creators assure that everyone can learn how to use this tool in 5 minutes.

Anyone who wants to play this instrument launches a mobile application that shows the lyrics - and instead of the names of the chords above the lines are the numbers of the buttons (the number of the button consists of the number of the key and the number of the button in this mode). The application automatically tracks which buttons you have already pressed, and scrolls through the lyrics.

The fingers of the left hand press the buttons, and the right one hits (or sorts) the short strings connected to the sensors.
