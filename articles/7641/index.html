Replacing the good old Newsland.

Those who have been using Pocket PCs for a long time (PDAs, smartphones, communicators) most likely remember that newsland.ru used to look quite different from how you can see it now. And it did not provide the service that is now. Who does not know about it, can read here . And the rest, perhaps, as I very much grieved that such a mega convenient service ordered to live long in connection with the sale of the newsland.ru domain to someone else, who preferred a different business idea to an existing service.

Honestly, I have been looking for quite a long time to no avail what can replace Newsland of previous years. And did not find. I gave up on so much that I decided to do this project with my own hands. And so, quite by chance, today I discovered the Mobilizer.ru service and immediately understood - IT! The mere launch of a client downloaded from a resource immediately causes a fit of nostalgia. One-to-one !!!

In fact, one person has already tried to convince me that only lazy does not know about this service. However, I know (besides myself) at least 10 of the same lazy ones, who together with me grieved at the untimely deceased Newsland. Gentlemen, replacement found! Viva!
