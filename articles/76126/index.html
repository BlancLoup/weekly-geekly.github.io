Intouch IT7150 Wireless Photo Frame



Digital photo frames are a very valuable invention of mankind. And it looks beautiful and donate in which case it is not a pity. In recent years, photo frames have acquired a significant amount of features. In particular, Intouch, here, has released a wireless photo frame with Internet access IT7150. Of course, buying a frame in order to surf the Internet with it is not very smart. But the benefits of compatibility with the global network is still there. For example, with the help of such a framework, it is convenient to view photos on photobanks. On the IT7150, you can send photos from the network or from a computer using WiFi. Characteristics of the frame: 7-inch color screen with a resolution of 800x480, WiFi 802.11 b / g, touch screen with a QWERTY-keyboard. The price is $ 229.99.
