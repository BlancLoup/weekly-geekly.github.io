Live broadcast of the second day of the DevCon 2014 conference


Dear friends!
Do not miss the broadcast of the second day of the conference DevCon 2014 (May 29 from 9:00 Moscow time)!
The main program of the second day of the conference will open with 30 minute interesting mini-reports. You can learn a lot of interesting things from our partners and invited speakers.

The entire second day will be filled with technical reports. Of the 25 reports of the second day of the conference, conference participants and live viewers can learn a lot of interesting things about the most modern approaches and software development tools, here are some of the reports that you can visit online May 29:

  • Create XAML applications for Windows Phone, tablets and desktops using universal application projects . Larry Lieberman, Microsoft, Windows Phone Product Manager
  • Deep immersion in controls for Windows Phone 8.1 . Hoer Tim, Microsoft, lead product manager for the Windows team


The final of the conference will be the official closing ceremony, during which the event will be summed up and the winners of the competitions will be awarded.
And now you can see the reports of the first day of the conference!

A detailed schedule of reports is available at www.msdevcon.ru/schedule , as well as in the official conference application, created specifically for DevCon 2014.

Follow the news on the site www.msdevcon.ru , communicate in the twitter-tape (# msdevcon ) and connect to the broadcast on May 29 at 9:00 (MSK).



Respectfully,
DevCon 2014 conference organizers
