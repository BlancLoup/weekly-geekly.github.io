The complexity of the person in the interaction with technology in different stages of life

A person living a life often changes his attitude to the world. At first, he thought it was necessary, then he realized that it was better in another way. Today I want to talk about the stages of human life and its relationship to the world, to technology. I do not have psychological or philosophical formations, but I think with the below-mentioned schemes many will agree, since they are simple and understandable, and in many respects even known.
I want to raise the old problem of ages and attitudes towards something new. I think that the information will be mainly useful for interface designers.



Periods of life of an ordinary person are divided into 2 stages. I want to create and I have to consume.
In the first stage, a person wants to create, wants to improve the world, wants to work. Everything new is better. Updates, news, activity - these are the words of this time.
In the second case, the person is tired, he does not want anything, but in order to survive he has to act. At the stage of consumption, a person is fed up with everything, he constantly forgets and understands for a long time, it is difficult to perceive new information. Life is more like torment than joy.
