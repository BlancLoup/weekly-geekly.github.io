My two terabyte locker

It so happened that the money from the sale of the old computer (with all the peripherals) was enough only for a two-byte hard drive. This is how technology gets cheaper. Although I have no regrets and even decided to write a review about this little monster. Who cares - I ask under the cat. Caution, traffic at 784 KB. :)




You cannot warm your hands from this hard drive - that‚Äôs a fact. At night, he will not wake you up, as he is practically silent. But data can store, and perfectly fulfills this function. And it is also quite small, and not too heavy (compared to the other 2 TB external disks).
Well, let's learn more about it.

Specifications


Title WDH1CS20000
Ruler My Book Home Edition
The size 2 TB (actually 1.81 TB)
Buffer size 16 mb
Rotational speed 7200 rpm
Average access time 8.9 ms
Interfaces USB, eSATA, FireWire x2
Dimensions 54x166x137 mm
Weight 1.15 kg


Equipment


The box is quite beautiful and not heavy (not more than 1.5 kg). Inside, in a plastic container, are:

- 2 TB hard drive
- USB cable
- Firewire cable
- Network adapter and adapter
- Instruction
- A small reminder to the user

As I saw all the contents, immediately began to call the store. I thought that I forgot to put an eSATA cable. After all, there were cables for USB and FireWire, but not for eSATA. It turned out (much to my chagrin) that it should be so. I had to run to the nearest computer store and buy an eSATA cable. Mentally damning the company Western Digital, their hard drives, employees, and all that is possible up to 5 knees, I ran back and forth in the rain, and purchased a cable. It has a length of 50 cm, and it costs 235 rubles. Almost 5 rubles per centimeter, robbery, what to say. :)



From the software included (all software is on the hard disk):

WD Anywhere Backup


As the name implies, the program for creating backups. There is a data encryption function.
Convenient utility, you can minimize to tray and ask to eat less resources. What is surprising, obeys.

Memeo autosync


As, again, as the name implies - a program to synchronize.
It can encrypt data as well as create flexible synchronization plans and auto-sync.

Google software

Includes Google Desktop , Toolbar and Picasa .
The first can be used to search for files on a computer after it has them indexed.
About Picasa nothing special to tell, a sort of "mini-photoshop."
Allows you to remove the red-eye effect, adjust the contrast and brightness, as well as add some effects.
Well, toolbar can block ads and auto-fill forms. You can put, for IE, for example.

Very upset that all the programs in English. Care about Russian users is not felt. Although, in general, the programs are convenient and quite useful.

Appearance


By itself, the hard disk is very beautiful. Do not be ashamed to put next to a laptop on the table or show friends.
A disk indicator shows when data is being read or written. If there is no disk access, the indicator just lights up.


Size relative to the stick


Morse code top and back vents


2 FireWire connectors, then busy eSATA port, free USB and power adapter connector

Also, at the back there is a Kensington lock slot - so that if they steal it, then along with the table or any other place to which you attach the drive with a steel cable. The case, by the way, is shockproof and specially designed to protect the hard drive from shocks. This feature has not yet been verified, it is necessary to believe the developers of the word.

Measurements


Considering that there is only one disk, it is not necessary to expect high speeds. No matter when 2 disks in RAID 0 are combined. But to download / upload to DC ++ / torrents such speeds is enough. And not just that, by the way. I was able to watch an HD movie weighing about 15 GB and simultaneously distributed in DC ++ and torrents, the speed was quite enough. However, it does not only depend on the disk.

The tests took place when connected via eSATA program HD Tune Pro 3.50 .
The values, unfortunately, do not coincide with the stated ones (access time, for example). Perhaps due to the fact that a couple of days after the purchase, I had already managed to score about 1 TB on the disk and continue my attempts until now.



As you can see from the screenshot, the CPU does not use the disk at all. Tested 3 times, always writes "-1.0%".



Good, but not perfect performance. However, it suits me.

Total


Pros:
- Large volume
- Small size and fairly low weight
- very quiet
- Almost does not heat up (although there is no built-in fan)
- Stylish design
- Good measurements
- A variety of interfaces
- Useful software included
- Reading indicator

Minuses
- Lack of eSATA cable (why not complete the device with an eSATA connector with the necessary cable right away, but!?)
- Initially formatted for FAT32
- Power button in the back (though it is not needed, the hard drive is turned on and off with the laptop)
- There is no support for the Russian language in the programs (and setup, by the way, also in English)
- No direct Ethernet connection
- SMART supported but not working

As you can see, there are more pros than cons. Some cons, even had to specifically invent. Joke. :)
