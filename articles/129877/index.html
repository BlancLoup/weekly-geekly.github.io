Bada developer day

Friends, we are pleased to announce that on October 12, the second Russian conference for mobile application developers on the bada platform - bada Developer Day Russia 2011 will take place in the Digital October center in Moscow in Moscow.


As many of you know, bada Developer Day is a peculiar cycle of events of international scope. To attract the attention of developers to the bada mobile platform and improve interaction with them, Samsung annually holds local bada Developer Day conferences in more than 40 countries around the world. Those who are interested in a full list of bada Developer Day cycle events, can get acquainted with it here .

The speakers of the Moscow session will include key figures such as Kanghyun Kwon, senior vice president of the Media Solution Center at Samsung Electronics (South Korea) and Alexey Dorofeev, director of mobile devices at Samsung Electronics. Samsung representatives will talk about bada mobile platform capabilities and application development tools, share platform development plans and hold technical workshops with application development recommendations for the new version of bada 2.0 platform. During the event, new Wave: Wave 3, Wave Y and Wave M smartphones will also be demonstrated. Each of those present at the event will have the opportunity to ask them questions about bada's prospects in Russia and features of bada 2.0 devices.

In addition, participants in the event will be able to share knowledge and experience with other developers. For example, the conference will be attended by representatives of SPB Software , who will share useful information in the field of application development.

As mentioned above, this year bada Developer Day will be held in Russia for the second time. The first bada conference was held in St. Petersburg in March 2010. More than 150 people took part in its work.



Samsung, as the creator of the bada platform, is not limited to bada Developer Day events alone, and offers developers a whole ecosystem that includes development tools, detailed reference materials, forums and partner sites.
The main platform for developers of applications on the bada platform is the website of Bada Developers . The site is available for developers in different languages, including the Russian-language forum . Useful for Russian developers and people interested in the platform will be our partner blog badablog and the Bada Portal website with daily news and app reviews.

Dear habrayusers who are close and interested in the topic of mobile application development! We are waiting for you on October 12 at our event. Participation in the conference is free, but requires prior registration . Registration is open until Tuesday, October 11, 23:59 MSK.
