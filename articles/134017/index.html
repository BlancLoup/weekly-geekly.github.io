New year with new project: Harvest December 10-11

On December 10-11 in Digital October Harvest from GreenfieldProject will be held working days, where together with experts and other participants you can do a huge amount of work on your startup in two days, find like-minded people in the team or join the team of the project you like.

Thus, those who have ideas of projects or developments, talk about them at Harvest, in order to get feedback from experts and assemble a team for work. And those who do not have a project come to Harvest to listen to lectures, choose an interesting start-up, join the team and sometimes become co-founders of projects (such as happened with the ePythia project).

We are going, as always, on Saturday morning, and this time - in the welcoming walls of the Digital October Technopark. First, we listen to two-minute presentations of selected projects (only pitch, no slides! - bring along with you charisma and persuasiveness). Then the projects are divided into teams and begin work. They will be helped by our experts.

In two days you have to make significant progress in the work on your project. Get ready, waiting for you:
  • understanding what your product is
  • awareness of who your customer is and what the market is
  • creating a marketing, sales and development strategy
  • the emergence of an indicative financial model
  • and finally, excellent practice in conducting presentations.
