The founders of The Pirate Bay negotiated with North Korea regarding the placement of torrent tracker servers


That is the embassy of North Korea in Sweden

Last year, many resources published news that the server of the most famous torrent tracker The Pirate Bay can be transferred (or already transferred) to North Korea. Of course, as some readers of such news assumed, all this turned out to be nothing more than a joke.

Nevertheless, the negotiations of The Pirate Bay team with the representatives of North Korea were indeed conducted, although not last year, but much earlier, in 2004-2005. This was announced by Tobias Andersson in an interview last week.

At the time, according to Andersson, the torrent tracker team began negotiations with the North Korean embassy in Sweden about the possibility of moving their servers to the embassy building.
