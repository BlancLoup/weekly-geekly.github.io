Nook Color officially unveiled

Barnes & Noble presented a new version of the Nook reader called Nook Color. She has a color LCD "with a laminated screen that minimizes reflections," and it is positioned as a hybrid reader and tablet.



OS: Google Android 2.1 without access to the Market, promise an update to 2.2 and its own app
Screen: LCD, 7 inches, 1024x600, touchscreen
Battery life: 8 hours of reading with Wi-Fi turned off.
Built-in memory: 8 gigabytes.
Other: Wi-Fi, microSD.

Price: 250 dollars
Date of commencement of sales: November 19
