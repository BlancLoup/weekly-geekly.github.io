RBK Money - a new competitor Yandex.dengam / Webmoney

Due to Rupay joining RBC Holding, on April 21, 2008 the system will be renamed RBK Money and will receive a new address on the Internet - www.rbkmoney.ru

With the acquisition of the Rupay payment system, RBC gained quick access to the rapidly growing market of electronic payments. The new version of the Rupay payment system will use in its work the infrastructure of OJSC RBC Bank, acquired at the end of 2007. Under the single trademark of RBK Money, the system will offer its customers innovative payment services based on the combination of electronic payments and banking services.

The Rupay system was founded in 2002. and is one of the first Russian systems to provide an automated payment acceptance tool for e-commerce businesses. Rupay serves more than 250 thousand Russian Internet users and 6 thousand online stores.


In addition to changing the brand, they plan to introduce a number of new options in the operation of the system. In particular, it introduces the function of replenishing the electronic wallet using bank cards of international payment systems Visa and MasterCard and the ability to exchange instant messages within the system. During 2008, users of the system will be able to receive RBK Money bank cards (issued by our partner, RBC Bank, linking it to an electronic wallet in our system. This service will allow you to instantly deposit / withdraw funds from an RBK electronic wallet) and link them to your electronic wallet in system.

There will also be introduced new rates for payments within the system in the amount of 0.3% of the payment amount. The tariff for withdrawing funds from the system to a bank account will be 3% + a fixed fee of 10 rubles.
