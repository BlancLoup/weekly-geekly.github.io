Larry Sanger creates another improved Wikipedia

Larry Sanger is known for his suggestion to Jimmy Wales to use the wiki engine to create a free encyclopedia. It was he who invented the name "Wikipedia", which is now known to the whole world.

Larry Sanger was appointed editor in chief of Wikipedia and worked for a little over a year, after which the only paid position was abolished. Sanger left the project and subsequently criticized Wikipedia for lack of public trust, for the poor quality selection of authors, etc. All problems, according to Sanger, stem from the harmful "anti-elitism" of the project, its disdain for everything elite and lack of respect for experts.

In December 2005, it became known that Sanger was involved in the creation of the Digital Universe project , an alternative to Wikipedia. For this startup even $ 10 million investment was raised.
