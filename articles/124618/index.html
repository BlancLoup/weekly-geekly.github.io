Take part in the prophecy winning Web Ready contest

Do you have experience creating a successful project? Can you say with certainty what the market needs? If the answer is yes, then we invite you to participate in a special project of the Web Ready contest on the site HabraHabr. Try to assess in advance the chances of winning the contestants and you will have a nice bonus. All constructive reviews, as well as accurate predictions of victory, are guaranteed to receive an invite to the closed final of Russia's largest innovation project competition, which will be held in St. Petersburg on December 2.

Many interesting projects have already arrived, some of them are presented below. The rest can be found on the site .

1. EvaDesk is a virtual desktop that provides remote access to all applications a user needs via the Internet using the SaaS model. You can quickly organize a virtual workplace.
2. LiveDiscourse - an interesting tool for commenting on the Internet, aggregation service and comparison of opinions.
3. BookMix.ru is another recommendation service, but on the subject of books. It allows you to familiarize yourself with book reviews of all genres, see various ratings, find people with similar tastes and much more.
4. Network Visualization System - a serious project, a software platform that allows you to build powerful and flexible systems for monitoring and analyzing network processes.
5. Podorozhniki.com is a service that unites drivers and passengers who are on the way. It can be used for one-time or regular trips around the city, between cities, countries.
6. DEEEP.ME - something like a personalized Internet. The system can offer the user how specific personalized answers to his questions. For example, which movie to see where it is better to repair a car and how many stars are in the sky. On the other hand, the system is a generator and regulator of professional and entertainment content.
