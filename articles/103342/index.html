Interview with the leader of GTUG Russia

Hello, habra-community!

Google is gaining more and more popularity every day.
Many in their work give preference to Google technologies.
However, not always one person can fully realize their initiatives and ideas.
Without a team of like-minded people here is not enough! Help can be found in GTUG!
The Google Technology User Group is a group of people willing to help, share their experience, or find partners for projects.
The GTUG community is huge, there are about 150 groups around the world, their number is constantly growing. Russia has already established groups in Moscow , St. Petersburg , the increasing activity of these groups is encouraging other cities to create new GTUGs.
At the end of the summer, GTUG-Russia held several events in Moscow and St. Petersburg.
We visited one of them and interviewed Arkady Khachkovanyan, head of the Google Technology User Group in Russia .
