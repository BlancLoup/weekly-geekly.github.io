Two weeks left to take part in the competition of young scientists in the field of information security



The PHDays Young School competition was invented three years ago to help beginning researchers express themselves, showcase their work, and share unexpected ideas. This year we extended the acceptance of applications until March 1, which means that every observant developer reading these lines, whether he is a schoolboy or a student, has a chance at the end of spring to tell about his discoveries from the stands in front of thousands of the best hackers and security specialists.
