Zone 51


Area 51 is the name of the Nellis US military test site located in Nevada, USA. Presumably used for experimental tests in the field of aeronautics and weapons systems. Air traffic over the Zone is prohibited.
Many assume that the remains of UFOs that crashed in Roswell in 1947 are stored here.

Ostankino

Our Ostankino tower looks very beautiful from the satellite, in my opinion (:

The tower is the third tallest structure in the world (540 meters). The mass of the tower, together with the foundation, is 51,000 tons, and the maximum deviation in strong winds is 12 meters.

The highest building is the CN Tower (553.33 meters), located in Toronto, Canada. The weight of the tower is over 130,000 tons, and it was built in 1975.


By the way, CN Tower is almost 2 times higher than the Eiffel Tower, which is the most visited landmark in the world.
