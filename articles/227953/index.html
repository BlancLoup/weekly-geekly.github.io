Energy transfer by water and single wire transmission



Hi habr. Today I want to talk about an unusual phenomenon that I observed as a result of the experiment. I will say right away nothing in common with IT and there will be no scientific explanation.
And there will be a "kacher", a container with water, broken bulbs and a curiosity to learn new things! To whom it became interesting - please, come on, it will be fun.

I will start from afar, namely from Nikola Tesla. From the same person who still can not determine who to rank as the great inventors or charlatans. They say he could transfer energy through a thin tungsten filament, traveled by car without fuel and worked other magic. I do not know what is true and what is not in this, but this is not important because it will be about the discovery of Stanislav Avramenko, who referred to Tesla, therefore I remembered this person.
S. Avramenko was able to transfer energy through a single conductor and use this effect to power the active load. He discovered that if you convert electricity into high-frequency pulses, then a closed circuit is no longer needed and the conductor itself serves as a direction for energy, and not as a transmitter. The most interesting thing is that it doesn‚Äôt matter where the conductor is made and what section it is, the main direction.
I was able to repeat his experiments two years ago. But recently I decided to use water instead of a conductor and the experiment was a success.
