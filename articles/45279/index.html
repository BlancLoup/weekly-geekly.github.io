Redirect from habr.ru to habrahabr.ru

When you go to a site from a clean browser (without history), it is easier to type habr.ru, a redirect occurs, and only habr.ru remains in the history because Temporary redirect is done.

Thus, habrahabr.ru will not appear in the history.

Probably more correct to do Permanent Redirect from habr.ru to habrahabr.ru.
Trifle, but nice.
