Exchange of experience and informal communication on .NET development - all this is Ciklum .NET Subbotnik (22.10)

All-all-all those who are interested in .NET development, new products, best practices and just communicating with colleagues - you will hear all this, receive and be able to participate in Ciklum .Net Subbotnik , which will be held on October 22 at the Kiev office of Ciklum


Recall that in our offices both throughout Ukraine and in Belarus , Ciklum Saturdays are constantly held, gathering developers for various technologies. This Ciklum Subbotnik , like the previous ones , will be held in the format of an informal mini-conference.

At the moment we are actively working on the content of the program, so you can make your own contribution and share your achievements and chips.

The program will be supplemented and updated so follow our announcements in our habra blog , twitter (using the hashtag #netsat) and on the page on facebook

Currently, the following speakers have confirmed their presentations:
  • Dmitry Mindra , RnD Technical Lead developer at Lohika will talk about Code Generation in .Net
  • Nikolai Basyuk , Asp.NET developer in the IntraNote team (Ciklum) will teach how to create a SOLID code
  • Peter Afanasyev , Senior .Net developer in the Bettson Payment team (Ciklum) will share .Net development experience
  • Sergey Koshel , .Net developer in the Betsson Component team (Ciklum company) ponders on the topic I see dead code (Code Quality + Continuous Integration (TeamCity), Code Quality tools (FxCop + StyleCop)
.

Participation in Ciklum Subbotnik is free, by prior registration . The final program of the event with organizational details will be sent on Friday, October 21, by e-mail to all who register.

According to the tradition of our Subbotnik, participants of the event will be able to informally communicate, exchange knowledge and share experience not only during presentations on technology topics on software development, but also throughout the event.
