Tell us a little about yourself.

I am 22 years old, born in Moscow. I have been interested in information technology since childhood (from 8-9 years old). I graduate from the institute with a degree in information security, I work as a programmer in

How long have you been involved in the gaia project? Why did you find this interesting?

It all started with the desire to use the maps offline (having previously downloaded them) under FreeBSD (there was no client for it). A couple of evenings were poking around with colleagues on Google Maps, then suddenly they changed maps (before that, better images were available - apparently those that are now used in Google Earth; now the quality is lower and watermarks are added).
