Ways to promote mobile applications

There are many ways to promote mobile applications, but which of them will be most effective for a developer can only be understood through trial and error. Applead decided to share its experience in this matter. Today, there are more than enough applications in the markets and many of them use the same proven channels.


Before you make your promotion plan, which should include at least two components, you need to answer a few questions:

1) What is the quality of the application?
2) To whom the application is directed, the target audience (this question had to be asked before starting development)
3) Who are the main competitors?

By answering these questions, you can clearly define the path of the mobile application.

For promotion, you can use the following channels:

1. Offline advertising
2. Contextual advertising
3. Targeted advertising in social. networks
4. Social networks
5. Press releases
6. Reviews on thematic resources
7. Reviews on youtube channels
8. CPI network
9. Motivated traffic

Let us examine each of them, we define all the pros and cons.

CPI (cost per install) - payment for the installation. CPI network takes responsibility for the quality of traffic. Initially, it is important to put a clear technical task, for which installations will be paid, and for which not. Motivated traffic is the installation of the application for a fee. There are special services that help to purchase the required number of installations, for example, CashPump. You can distribute the installation by day and time. Such installations help to quickly get into the TOP.
