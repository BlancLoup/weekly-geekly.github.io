The latest mission of the shuttle - look online

Today, in just a few hours, a historical event will occur - the last launch of the shuttle.


Four astronauts will take part in the 12-day STS-135 mission: spacecraft commander Chris Ferguson, pilot Doug Hurley and specialists from Sandy Magnus, Rex Walheim.
They will conduct a series of experiments as well as deliver a payload to the ISS, in particular the Raphaello multi-purpose logistics module.

You can watch the live broadcast here:
www.nasa.gov/multimedia/nasatv
There you can also find links to broadcasts for mobile devices.
