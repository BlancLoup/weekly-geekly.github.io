Google launched Friend Connect platform

As expected, Google launched Friends Connect , which supports three major social standards - OpenID, oAuth, and OpenSocial. Initially, the main members of the OpenSocial Alliance (Facebook, Google Talk, Hi5, Orkut and Plaxo) will be able to provide data to their users for third-party sites.

No programming knowledge is required to work with Friend Connect. Simply fill in the required information, select the necessary social applications, copy the code, paste and save. There are also features such as user registration, sending out invitations, posting messages and reviews.
