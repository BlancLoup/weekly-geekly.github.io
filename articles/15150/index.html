Google has released a lightweight interface for Google Docs mobile phones

Google has released a lightweight interface Google Docs, optimized for mobile phone screens - Google Docs Mobile . True, you cannot edit files in this interface, but you can view a list of recent documents and open them (viewing presentations is not supported). In addition, there is a search for documents, as well as the ability to view documents located in folders (and not located in folders) or marked with an asterisk; There is also the ability to view the basket.

As mentioned earlier, presentations in the mobile version of Google Docs are not supported. More precisely, they are supported, but only with the iPhone, at least Google's help is at least saying that. There are no incompatibility problems with tables and text documents - they are opened by all devices. Text documents are shown as is, and the tables are proposed to either be saved in xls (Microsoft Excel) format, or shown as HTML.

The table in the form of HTML is a special reformatted page that is well displayed on the screens of mobile phones. By default, single-column lists are displayed; it is possible to move to the next column or to a specific cell (if you know its location). There is also the option of viewing a single line, which is also displayed vertically, rather than horizontally.
