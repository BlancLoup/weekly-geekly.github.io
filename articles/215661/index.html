In the Indian police for 8 years did not respond to complaints because they lost the password from the database

The police of the city of Delhi for 8 years did not receive complaints about the work of this very police. As it turned out, the matter is not at all that the police are so wonderful, but simply no one opened this database with complaints - the password was simply lost from her.



In India, there is a Central Vigilance Committee, in fact, the body responsible for fighting political corruption. In 2006, this committee created a portal that allows you to write complaints and forward them to the police for response. After 8 (!) Years, the committee decided to inquire as to why the police are silent. The police summoned to the carpet made a surprised face and could not open the base or remember the password.

By now, the password has already been restored, and now the police have to clear 667 complaints that have accumulated over nearly a decade.
