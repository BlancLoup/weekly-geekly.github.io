MeeGo 1.0 released

The weather today may have let us down, but for real good news, all this is not a hindrance. The developers of the promising MeeGo platform launched the first release of their new operating system!



MeeGo 1.0 was released for several devices, in particular for netbooks and ... for the Nokia N900 ! And you can download now and absolutely free!



In the future, the number of supported devices will only grow - phones, smartphones, tablets, automotive electronics, smart household appliances, and so on. By the way, the platform will be updated about once every six months and version 1.1 should be expected later this October.
