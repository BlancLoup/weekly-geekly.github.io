Pochta Rossii invites private carriers and implements GLONASS



The deplorable situation with the delivery of goods and correspondence through the "Post of Russia" is well known to everyone. Almost everyone had to wait for a parcel from an online store for 2-3 months instead of 2-3 weeks.

The new management of Post of Russia has resorted to an extraordinary way to unload warehouses and speed up the delivery of correspondence. They decided to outsource some directions, that is, to hire outside carriers. Perhaps the work of private companies will help correct the situation.

The company will hold a special contest in which 77 trunk and 122 intrasite routes will be played, Deputy Director General of Russian Post Alexey Skatin said today in an interview with RBC Daily, which now coordinates the work of FSUE in the field of logistics. Prior to this, the manager has been managing logistics for five years at Baltika. Judging by the assortment of stores in every Russian city, Alexey did an excellent job. It remains to wish him success in the new position.
