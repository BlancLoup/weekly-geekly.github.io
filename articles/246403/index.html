I did not manage to find in the European and American markets systems similar to those discussed in the previous parts. Of course, there are similar systems, but: either they do not provide free access, or they have much poorer functionality in comparison with the above ones.

The systems reviewed here are oriented and provide maximum efficiency when working with them on smartphones.

Family Locator http://life360.com/ (USA)




The company was founded in San Francisco (California) in 2009.

There are free apps for iPhone, Android and Windows Phone.

Application downloads on Google Play from 10 to 50 million.

The application has a Russian interface.

The system can be used to monitor the safety of family members and loved ones.

Limitations of the free version : you can create 2 geofences ("Places"); relocation history is available only for the last 3 days (in the paid version - for 30 days); There are no non-smartphone location and search functions for lost (stolen) smartphones (in the paid version, these features are available for the United States and Canada for certain AT & T, Verizon, TMobile and Sprint providers).

  • The account holder can fill out his profile (which may be required in emergency situations), indicating in it information about his state of health, allergies, blood type and other information.

Glympse http://glympse.com (USA)




The company was founded on February 22, 2008. The main office is located in Seattle (WA).

Free apps designed for iPhone, Android and Windows Phone.

Application downloads on Google Play from 1 to 5 million.

The application has a Russian interface.

The system can be used for a simple and secure way to share your location in real time with family and friends.

There are no restrictions on free use.

The company has developed and provides an SDK that allows you to quickly and easily add Glympse features to existing IOS and Android applications. Excellent descriptions, tutorials and instructions for all supported platforms are available.

Recently, the company has been actively cooperating with major auto manufacturers and suppliers of navigation services to integrate their developments into joint products.

Useful features of the system




  • Mapping: Google; road traffic (google).
  • The application does not require registration. Just install it on your smartphone.
  • To transfer your location, a special link to glympse.com is generated (no registration is required to access this link).
  • Information about the current location of the link creator is displayed in real time.
  • The link can be sent to someone via SMS, email or using any other service installed on the smartphone.
  • You can configure the time interval during which the link is valid.
  • The sender can specify the destination of his route on the map.
  • Access to the link may be terminated (temporarily or completely) by the sender at any time.
  • Access to the link can be automatically terminated upon reaching the final destination of the route.
  • You can set the option to transfer speed data.
  • You can set the battery saving mode - location data is transmitted only when someone watches the movement.
  • Good integration with social networks (Facebook, Twitter, Evernote). You can set your social network account as a recipient.
  • You can see those Glympse users who are watching your current movement.
  • There is a special mode that facilitates work with the application while driving.
  • You can request information about the current location of users (according to the address book of the smartphone) that have the Glympse application installed.
  • You can create open groups. All who know the name of the group can follow the objects that publish information about their location in this group.
