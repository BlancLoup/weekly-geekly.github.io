Motorola introduced a new secure Android-smartphone Motorola Defy +

As the name implies, Motorola Defy + is similar to its predecessor Motorola Defy.

But, compared to its predecessor, Defy + has increased the degree of protection - now it conforms to the IP67 standard, which means that it is resistant to scratches, bumps, dust and can be in the water.

The smartphone runs on Android 2.3 (Gingerbread) with the MOTOBLUR shell, a 3.7 inch screen covered in scratch-resistant Gorilla Glass.

The device has inside:
  • 1GHz processor;
  • 2GB of internal memory;
  • 5MP camera;
  • 1700mAH battery, which should provide up to 7.1 hours of talk time and up to 16 days in standby mode.
