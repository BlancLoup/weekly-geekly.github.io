Backbone.js for web application development, Speakers' Corner in Odessa with Artem Trityak

For those IT people who this week decided to enjoy the sea and the sun of Odessa , great news: Friday night can be spent with benefits! In the Odessa office of Siklum, another event from the Speakers' Corner series will be held, this time dedicated to the development of web applications using Backbone.js from Artem Trityak .

The program includes a discussion of AMD, Backbone.js, RESTful architecture, working with templates. Together with the listeners we will work with the model .destroy () model and also consider the need to use Backbone.js {silent: true}. Especially for this event, Artem prepared an application on Backbone.js, in order to demonstrate his work with real examples. The source code will be uploaded to Github and is available to everyone.

About speaker:
Artem Trityak is a representative of connex.io, he has been working with JavaScript, Python and PHP for more than 6 years both in the framework of project outsourcing and in the product team based on the experience of the employees. Created software for embedded devices. He is ready to tell all this and many other things to the audience on the next Friday!

Date: Friday, August 10
Time: registration of participants at 18.30, beginning of the event at 19.00
Location: Siklum office in Odessa, Uspenskaya 39/1, 8th floor - 33.
The event is, as always, free of charge, subject to mandatory registration ->
