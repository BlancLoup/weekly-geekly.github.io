The thinnest Toshiba Excite X10 tablet and 4 brothers under glass. Video

It seems the company Toshiba decided to seriously engage in tablets. More than 5 new products are inserted at the stand, although most of them are under glass. The company showed prototypes with diagonal screens of 5, 7.7 and even 13 inches. Well, the main tablet on the stand is a 10-inch Toshiba Excite X10 (AT200), its first samples were shown at the IFA-2011 exhibition.



This slim, stylish tablet runs on a 1.2-GHz dual-core Texas Instruments OMAP4430 processor.

The weight of the device is 558 grams. and the thickness of the case is only 7.7 mm. All ports microUSB, microHDMI, 3,5 jack, microSD are located on the left side of the tablet.
