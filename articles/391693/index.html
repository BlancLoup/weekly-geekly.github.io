Rise of the Machines: Amazon Echo began to perform commands heard on the radio



After the transfer to the US National Public Radio about the Amazon Echo digital assistant, activated by voice commands, listeners began to complain about the sudden activation of their devices. The devices recognized words coming from the radios and executed the recognized commands.

One listener complained that his Amazon Echo set the thermostat to 70 ¬∫F (21 ¬∫C). Another wrote that he could not listen to the story about the assistant to the end, because when his assistant heard his name, he began to play the audio summary of the latest news.
