Year of high traffic



This year we are 20 years old. We started with five people in knitted sweaters, reverse engineering of American modules , an antenna on the building of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, phones the size of a couple of books - and if someone then called us the amount of traffic that went through the network last year, the engineers would simply twist finger to the temple. Yes, 2012 is a year of big traffic. For example, according to the results of the 3rd quarter of this year, the data traffic in the network grew by 78% over the year, and this growth will continue.

It took a lot of changes in the network. A year before the main transmission nodes in the packet network (more precisely, the traffic processing nodes) became a bottleneck, we made a fairly large replacement for new hardware: this is LTE-ready, scalable architecture, which means lower electricity costs per traffic unit.

In addition, over the year, our engineers have done a lot in technical terms to upgrade and expand the network. Plus, at the end of April we launched HSPA + in the center of Moscow (this is a technology that allows us to achieve data transfer rates of up to 21 Mbit / s) and continue to develop this technology. In November, HSPA + worked in St. Petersburg, Arkhangelsk, Severodvinsk, Vologda, Cherepovets, Kaliningrad, Murmansk, Veliky Novgorod, Petrozavodsk, Pskov and in more than 100 locations in the North-West. Now our network supports this technology in all regions of Russia for about 40% of the 3G network. In Moscow, where traffic requirements are highest, the network capacity is also increased by 40%.

Below - more details about the different changes in the network for the year.

Mobile Internet


We are systematically preparing the network for LTE launches. As we said earlier, the first fragment of the LTE network is under construction in Moscow, we have already received the appropriate frequency permits, and we hope that the official launch will take place early next year after clearing the frequency range. We also filed applications with the FSUE HRCC to conduct radio frequency examinations in the regions where we plan to launch LTE networks in 2013.

This year we did a lot to promote mobile Internet: on the one hand, we launched a line of interesting tariffs, and on the other, we launched a campaign to cancel Internet roaming in the country. Internet roaming throughout Russia is canceled for Internet users via a USB modem, 3G-Wi-Fi router or any other devices that work with a SIM card. Users take part in the action automatically, without additional payments. Since February 2012, for users of the Unlimited Internet, Unlimited Internet + WiFi, Unlimited Internet + WiFi for iPad services, Internet roaming throughout Russia has been canceled indefinitely. Thus, for all tariff plans, mobile Internet operates throughout Russia, under the same conditions as in the home region - without roaming.

Now this action with the abolition of Internet roaming extended until the end of March 2013. Plus there were packaged offers that would greatly interest those who often use the mobile Internet or travel frequently.

Another nice thing is the continuation of the support of "zero zones" - mobile versions of sites, for example, 0.vkontakte.ru, access to which via the mobile Internet Beeline is free. Also this year, traffic to Facebook has become free, and not only to the mobile version.

fault tolerance


This year, we were the first in Russia to receive an international certificate for ensuring business continuity BS 25999. A certification audit was conducted by LRQA. Back in 2004, VimpelCom began a program to back up the most critical information systems and services and to implement a business continuity process. Now there is a backup computing center, geographically distributed backup systems for all data have been introduced, synchronous replication of critical data has been developed, backup solutions have been developed for all the most important centralized systems aimed at providing services to subscribers and ensuring the functioning of the company itself. There are internal procedures that minimize response time and recovery after emergency situations, and minimize the time to resolve incidents that occur. All implemented solutions are regularly tested to meet the requirements of the recovery business in case of an emergency.
