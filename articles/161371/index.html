Action for startups from ServerClub!

Dear Habrazhiteli!

More than one startup came out of the hands of the creators of ServerClub , so we are well aware of how complex this path is. Every extra dollar for a project in its early stages can be fatal. And monthly payments for hosting are the main burden of garage startups. With this problem we decided to help the habrakomuniti on the way to a bright Internet future.
We really do not know the demand for this offer, but for the beginning we are ready to allocate 5 Dell R210 IntelXeon X3430 / 8GB / 2x1000GB SATA (or 2x 300GB SAS) RAID 1 serer with gigabit channels for 12 months. This is about $ 12,000 of hosting at market prices - our modest contribution to habrastastaps. Depending on the specific requirements of the projects, we can issue the server entirely or cut it into several VPS.
At the first stage, we will make the share available only for active users - write the application in the comments. The application must contain a link to the site, a brief description of the project and a contact email. Also indicate the required power and current attendance (zero attendance is normal :)). We reserve the right to refuse projects at any stage of cooperation. The project is required to be at the stage at least alpha - a site where you can understand what the project is about and something is already working there, i.e. at the stage when it can already be shown to friends.
Instead, please write to the main Hosted by ServerClub Dedicated Servers. An example is Nekaka.com .
If applications will be brute force, then add servers. If there is a shortage, then we will expand the list of participants to the readers or, perhaps, even wider. If there is no activity at all, we promise to think about how to help startups in a different way :).
We can also advise on the business model and, in general, assess the prospects for a startup and act as mentors - we have many years of experience in the Internet business. The most interesting startups have a chance to receive investments from us, but this is already a result of communication.
Content on sites must comply with the laws of Holland or the United States (depending on where you prefer to place the project). We will summarize the preliminary result of the action on December 14 for the collection of applications 10 days.
New Zuckerberg - Viva!

Regards, ServerClub Team.
