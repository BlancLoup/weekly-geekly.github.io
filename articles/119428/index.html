Team Russia - the number 1 overclocking team in the world!

Dear friends, I want to tell you the "most pleasant" news. Team Russia - the largest team in Russia and the CIS in overclocking and benchmarking became number 1 in the world ranking of overclocking teams according to the website hwbot.org



Look for details and the path to the first place under the cat.



Team Russia was created less than six months ago by combining two of Russia's strongest overclocking teams - OCClub.ru and Russian Overs Team. A series of disagreements and squabbles was replaced by a universal understanding of the need to create a single professional team to achieve a common goal - leadership in the world rankings.



Throughout its existence, the team ranked second, trying to catch up with German overclockers from PC Games Hardware. The team was created on the basis of the German computer journal of the same name with a circulation of more than 100,000 copies. And on a specific page of each number you can find a call for joining an overclocker team. As the statistics show, this method is very effective and today there are already 474 people in the camp of the German team.
