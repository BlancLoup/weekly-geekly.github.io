Internet2 celebrates a decade

The Internet2 project is celebrating its tenth anniversary this week. In honor of this event, a conference is held in Chicago, where speakers talk about achievements and share plans for the future.

In particular, the creators of Internet2 are going to hold the second major upgrade of the core network. Engineers run a special blog, Internet2 Network Upgrade , where they report various updates and changes. For example, it has become known that the Ciena CoreDirector platform will be implemented to help existing technologies.
