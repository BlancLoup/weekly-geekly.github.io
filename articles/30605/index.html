Orwell diaries in blog form

From August 9, 2008, the Orwell Prizes will be published on The Orwell Prize website. Every day, new posts will appear in a specially created retrospective blog. Thus, The Orwell Prize will publish all the notes from the diary of the British writer, who led him for 4 years from 1938 to 1942.

In the new blog of the old diaries of Orwell, readers will be able to read notes on the writer's life in Morocco, where he went to recover from the wound received during the Spanish Civil War, as well as his thoughts and views on politics and journalism. Anyway, Orwell wrote about everything. Unemployment, gossip, war, fascism, chicken breeding ...

It is hardly worth arguing with the statement that the diaries of one of the most talented writers of the 20th century, published in the popular blog form, are a great way to celebrate their 70th anniversary.
