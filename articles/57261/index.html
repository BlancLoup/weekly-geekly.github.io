Cheloveche.ru social network for fun



On Monday, Alpha's new social network version of Cheloveche.ru went into closed testing .

he - in translation from Mexican "friend". In the title CheLoveChe we carry the ideology of real friends, friends by interests. That is, we propose to build relationships with people not on the basis of events that happened to you (which you often want to forget), but on the basis of common hobbies, tastes, preferences, styles, and general lifestyle. Looking for like-minded people - isn't it the most important thing in our life?

Cheloveche is a social network of a new generation. It has nothing to do with "biographical" systems like Odnoklassniki. The goal of Man is to help people find "brothers in mind" - according to their interests, hobbies, style, way of life and thought.



As an entertainment and gaming element, users are invited to create their own avatar - Man. The character constructor allows you to create a cartoon image that talks a lot about its owner. At present, the communicative part of the Human is represented by personal messages, personal blogs and interest clubs.
