Yandex 23 minutes was blocked by Rostelecom

Today, Rostelecom has blocked Yandex for its subscribers - from about 14:04 to 14:27 Moscow time. Our users were greeted by a stub page on access restriction to the site.



Rostelecom as a reason for blocking refers to "a court order to limit access to the site, whose IP coincided with some resources ." No notifications were received on Yandex. We consider what happened to be a misunderstanding.

For our part, we have taken some technical measures to minimize the consequences for users.

The official request to Rostelecom has already been sent, we are waiting for details. Be sure to share them with you when we have them.

If suddenly you still can not go to Yandex, please email us at support@search.yandex.ru .
