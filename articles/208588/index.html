This time we came up with this ...


Now all over the world it is fashionable to make startups. And a bad start-up that does not expect that it will be favored by investors, will thrive and become a new Google, Facebook or Cursor. Well, or Yandex, VKontakte and Lectorium, respectively.

In order to support startups a little in their difficult struggle for survival, JetBrains opens a program to support startups: if you are a startup and buy licenses for our products, then you will get a 50% discount! The discount is given on commercial licenses, personal licenses for the organization cannot be bought. At the same time, we remind that our YouTrack issue-tracker is free for 10 users, i.e. By purchasing a license for any of our products under the startup support program, you will be able to immediately use YouTrack.

How do we determine if you are a startup? Simply:
1) the company is not an open joint-stock company (i.e., JSC, IP, LLC is suitable)
2) the company has existed for no more than 3 years (the date of creation of the company is what you have written on the certificate of registration of legal entities)
3) it employs no more than 10 people
4) the company develops software or provides services related to software (the development of a web platform for ordering pizza is good, the production and delivery of pizza is not)
5) the company already has a working business, as well as a web site or a mention of this business on publicly accessible pages on the Internet (i.e., a group of like-minded people who are thinking for the second month whether or not to make a web platform for ordering pizza all over the world is not yet a startup ).
