10,000 virtual servers on RU VDS



Our server RUVDS created the 10000th server. A little more than half a year has passed since we started working, and already more than 8,000 people work with us.

In honor of this event, we give gifts to all customers who have paid for VPS for the year. Throughout June, customers who have paid for the virtual server for the year will receive an additional 30% discount, as well as the first 30 people will receive an additional gift - a company anniversary mug from RUVDS.

Fail safety of the service is ensured by full compliance with the company's own data center TIER III standard with a UPTIME level of 99.98% and round-the-clock technical support. From the moment of opening to the present day, the service has been actively developing, stably keeping the lowest prices on the market and does not depend on the economic situation.

The stability of our virtual servers is provided by:

1. Three independent channels of connection to the trunk communication centers MMTS-9 and MMTS-10, which allows to provide highly reliable high-speed Internet access with an average ping in Moscow up to 3 ms and a bandwidth of up to 30 Gb / s and a DDOS protection system based on the solution CISCO Guard

2. Two independent power lines of 0.5 MW, which corresponds to the power supply category 1 and allows switching between inputs in less than one second

3. Uninterrupted power supply system based on APC solution

4. Two backup diesel generators and automatic start-up systems, allowing to ensure the autonomous operation of the data center for 24 hours. A contract has been concluded for the prompt delivery of diesel fuel in emergency situations.

5. Three independent cooling systems (water, air and draw ventilation with an air-cleaning system) and a humidity control system at a level of 40-50%

6. Fire extinguishing system based on gas extinguishing agent (freon) and fire alarm system (early response and temperature sensors)

7. Four perimeters of access control to equipment, as well as a video surveillance system along all access perimeters:
  • The first one is a closed, round-the-clock, guarded federal state unitary enterprise of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia and a territory controlled by access, where the RUVDS data center is located.
  • The second is the territory of the data center RUVDS controlled by access by the Private Security Service
  • Third - access control to the data center
  • Fourth - access controlled entrance to each tight area with a magnetic card

8. Round-the-clock monitoring of all systems and technical support directly at the equipment location
