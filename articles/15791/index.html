LG Touch - a new concept phone

Korean designer Kim Do Yeop has developed a concept model for the new LG Touch mobile phone.

LG Touch introduces a completely new user interface, based on a revolutionary multi-touch display and the latest software that allows users to control the phone not only with the touch screen - a small QWERTY keyboard is built into the cradle for charging, making typing much easier. The device is equipped with two screens at once - one touchscreen on the front panel and an auxiliary one located on the side.
Also original accessories for this phone are headphones with luminous wires in the style of techno. In fact, the phone combines 4 products in one small phone: a revolutionary mobile phone, a dual LCD screen, Internet applications for viewing mail and the Internet, as well as a QWERTY keyboard. The phone is like nothing else, and at the same time it could compete with the iPhone in style and functionality. Could ... if it were produced. So far this is only a concept.
