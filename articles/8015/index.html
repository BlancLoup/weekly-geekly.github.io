Yahoo! decided to close my photo service

Yahoo! administration decided to close Yahoo Photos photo service due to the growing popularity of another similar project, also owned by this company, called Flickr. Yahoo Photos users are invited to use exactly Flickr, reports Reuters.

The number of registered Yahoo Photos users is estimated at 30 million. In June 2007, they will be informed about the possibilities of transferring content to Flickr or other photo hosting sites, said one of the founders of the popular photo service, Stewart Butterfield.

Today, Photobucket is considered to be the largest online photo service. The number of registered users from 2005 to 2006 increased from 12 to 32 million people, and by the end of 2007, according to experts, it will exceed 62 million people.
