CLRium: 10 days before the mini .Net interior conference

What is .Net? Java? Automagic control system for your code. These are whole reserves with their own rules, laws that cannot be violated and which are designed to make your work easier by adding automatism to routine tasks. Tasks for managing memory, resources, security, threads. In those tasks that you are distracted by creating your own code. I like to compare platforms like .Net and Java with Matrix. You are given an idyll in which you live without knowing anything about the real world. The programmer, having received new knowledge, leaves the reality and begins to lose knowledge of threads, processor registers and in general, completely goes into the terminology of frameworks. But, although the real world is less practical, knowledge of reality makes adjustments to the understanding of the rules of the Matrix and adds those features that were previously out of the question.
