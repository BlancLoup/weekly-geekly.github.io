WPP Group acquires 24/7 Real Media for $ 649 million

The 24/7 Real Media online advertising campaign will be purchased by the WPP Group for $ 649 million.

The old Internet advertising company, which provided its services to Yahoo and Netscape back in 1994, was founded in the same year under the name Real Media. After a huge number of purchases, she changed her name to the one by which she is now known, and has grown to 20 offices in 12 countries of the world, processing more than 200 billion advertising impressions monthly.
