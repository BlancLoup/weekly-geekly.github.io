Take off only the cream and hire the best in your business.



Of course, talented and efficient professionals are the basis of any successful business. And we, creating the franchise of the urban portals Gorodor, understood this very well.

Therefore, when in Gorodor we faced the fact that it was expensive to spend our own resources on personal selection and interviewing of new specialists, from the point of view of resource allocation, we began to look for a way out of this situation. And found!

Now, when we need a super professional in our field, we do not conduct hundreds of interviews and do not spend a single minute of valuable time searching for employees on our own. We found our perfect recruiter. And recommend to you.

Let's go for the points.

Why is the recruiting process so important for a successful business?

  • After optimizing the search and recruitment process, recruiting immediately leads to an increase in revenues and a decrease in the company's operating expenses.
  • As soon as you hire a recruiter or recruiting agency, attracting the most talented specialists becomes their task, not yours, allowing you to devote more time to other important issues.
  • Of course, hiring the right and smart specialists will help move your business forward.


Need to increase revenue? Expand your sales department, but not by anyone, but by your own specialists with experience and a solid base. And again, a great way to hire the best sellers is not to waste your time, but to contact a recruiter.

Remember that hiring the right people, especially in departments that interact with your customers, is the future of your company or project.
