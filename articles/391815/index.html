Hardware potential and software



The hardware platform, without exaggeration, is good ... It is a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 chipset, 3 or 4 gigabytes of RAM, 32/64 or 128 gigabytes of main flash memory. And the Adreno 530 chip is responsible for the graphics ... and this is where the most interesting begins.



It turns out that there will be three versions of the smartphone. The first version of Mi5 basic is 32 GB of main memory of the UFS 2.0 standard, 3 GB of dual-channel operational LPDDR4 memory, which operates at a frequency of 1333 MHz. A Snapdragon 820 processor with cores up to 1.8 GHz, and Adreno 530 graphics running at 510 MHz.

The second version, the so-called advanced - here already 64 gigabytes of flash memory of the same standard UFS 2.0, together with the same three gigabytes of RAM, but already operating at 1866 MHz. The four cores of Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 overclocked to a maximum clock frequency of 2.15 GHz, and the Adreno graphics 530 to 624 MHz.

And the last, the most "top" version, which, by the way, comes with a ceramic back cover and exclusively in black. It contains: four Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 cores overclocked to 2.15 GHz and unaltered Adreno 530 graphics with a clock frequency of up to 624 MHz. 128 gigabytes of the main UFS 2.0 flash drive, and 4 GB of RAM LPDDR4, which operates at a frequency of 1866 MHz.
