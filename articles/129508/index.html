Movie Loop - an easy way to find an interesting movie.



Have you ever tried to find a good movie? I am sure that yes :) Hours spent at the computer, tons of read film descriptions, dozens of trailers viewed, but not all?

I understand you perfectly, which is why I bring to your attention an application that will fundamentally change the situation and save you a lot of time.

Movie Loop is a handy application for finding interesting movies. The principle of operation is based on the fact that you know the movies that you like, so most likely you will also like similar movies.



Just try it and you will feel the difference;)

Functional:
- Each launch offers movies (based on your favorites)
- View the list of top rated movies (with the ability to filter by genre)
- Powerful search (including search for all similar movies)
- View trailer
- Add movies to your favorites
- When viewing the description of the film, all similar movies are also displayed.



Your opinion about the design and functionality of the application is very interesting :)
