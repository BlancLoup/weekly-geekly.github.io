The largest events of 2018: design and front-end

At the beginning of last year, we made a selection of interesting events that designers should mark on their calendar, and today it is time to continue this undertaking. This time we decided to focus on the most significant events of the world for specialists of different profiles. The material under the cut covers the design and front-end directions; The second part of the article is devoted to development and testing. The collection includes mostly large conferences, for which it makes sense to prepare, tune in and save up in advance, as well as some interesting educational events. At the end of the article you will find links to resources where it is convenient to track the full range of meetings, courses and local initiatives. Additions and suggestions, as always, are welcome.

Design Weekend

Where and when:
Ulyanovsk - January 20-21
Address: st. Iron Division, 5B, coworking "Boiling Point"
Smolensk - February 23-25
Perm - March 24-25
Kirov - April 28-29
Samara - April-May
(exact addresses will be announced in the Vkontakte group )
Conditions of participation: Free, registration is required

A design weekend is a public educational, fundamentally non-commercial, project of the Russian design community. This year a series of meetings will be held for the sixth time. Participants aim to share practical knowledge and experience between the capital and the regions and improve the quality of design in Russia. The event takes place in different parts of the country in the format of two-day seminars, initiated by local design communities. The program of each particular exit conference is formed freely and flexibly, depending on the interests of the community. This year Design Weekend announced in five cities and in each case will cover a special range of topics relevant to local professionals. In Ulyanovsk, the talk will be about design, product design, mobile applications, augmented reality, design thinking, creative practices. In Smolensk, the attention of speakers will be focused on territorial branding, urban environment, urban planning, graphic design, illustration. Perm designers will discuss: big data visualization, services for the city, neural networks and artificial intelligence in design.
