Space data center. Video report from the launch

As you remember, on April 12, on the Cosmonautics Day, our small server successfully flew into the stratosphere! During the flight, the server aboard the stratostat distributed the Internet, filmed and transmitted video and telemetry data to the ground.


In an hour or so, our web server flew to an altitude of 22,700 meters, flew 70 km, experienced a temperature drop from +25 0 to -60 0 and received 125 messages from habrazhiteley. Different messages came to the stratosphere: romantics confessed in love, offended co-investors asked for their protection, animal lovers were looking for cats and dolphins at an altitude of 22 kilometers, there was advertising, and without it. Greetings and congratulations on cosmonautics day came from Belarus, Ukraine, the Urals, Siberia, Sakhalin, Novorossiysk, Yaroslavl, Gomel, Bryansk.

In the last publication, we summarized the project, showed a small online recording from the probe and promised to show a full video of the flight. We present videos from two onboard cameras with all messages received from you, the current height and coordinates on the monitors.
