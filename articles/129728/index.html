On October 3, at 0015 hours Moscow time, the successful launch of the Soyuz-2.1b space rocket with the Fregat upper stage and navigation satellite was performed with the participation of specialists from the rocket and space industry at the 43 launch site of the Plesetsk cosmodrome at the Plesetsk space center. Glonass-M.
