Drunk data center worker brought down American sites

God, as we know, keeps drunks. But they themselves drunk while not able to save something, but on the other hand to break - easily.

According to ValleyWag.com, a massive power outage occurred in the 365 Main data center, as a result of which sites such as Craigslist , TypePad and LiveJournal , as well as a number of other large resources, temporarily stopped working.

A source close to 365 Main reported that the reason for this was a drunk employee, in a state close to the insane, managed to de-energize about forty server racks.

The authors of the notes on ValleyWag.com are confident that in official statements 365 Main will deny this information.
