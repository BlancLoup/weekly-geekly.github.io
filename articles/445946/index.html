Top Trends


There were a lot of announcements, but the trends are obvious. We will tell about the main ones.

  1. Folding smartphones: coming reincarnation clamshell phones!   2. The new generation of mobile communications 5G: the epic continues from 2016, when it was first talked about. Finally, the fifth generation of wireless communication is brought to life. Mobile World Congress Guide


The more you ride, the more experience. Of course, in reviews of past years we have already shared some of the life hacks, but this time we have collected a whole collection of tips, which is especially useful for companies intending to take part in the congress for the first time.

Forward intelligence


If you seriously think of participating in the MWC with a stand, then it is better to go there for the first time to explore as visitors. Explore the stands of other companies, mark the places with the best traffic. So you can choose the perfect place and apply cool life hacking from experienced exhibitors. Work out next year at all 200%!

Before the trip to the exhibition, carry out the preparatory work: the registered contacts are available in the official MWC application , you can understand with whom it is interesting to talk and make an appointment. Most managers have a planned schedule, and simply finding them on the stand is problematic - they are usually busy.

Budget


Be prepared for the fact that your initial budget will almost double, because even if you choose an area with buildings, TV and electricity, a lot of other expenses arise in the process.

Stand


Fine! You are ready to go to the MWC with a booth. Make its design as simple as possible, but bright: a large brand name (to make it easier to notice) and a short, clear description (to see if your product falls within the scope of a visitor‚Äôs interests).

The corner stand gives more visibility, and the space next to the bar or a very popular brand - more traffic. Put on the stand large monitors with spectacular demonstration videos that in a split second can catch the attention of those passing. Add flowers to the stand, they create a feeling of comfort.

Grab your own MWC badge ribbons so you don‚Äôt advertise other brands on your neck.


Handouts


Prepare branded materials that your visitors want to use later. Cool stickers that look good on a laptop or cards with promo codes on the application. Do not print a lot of leaflets with product information. Many people today are trying to be eco-friendly and avoid unnecessary paper, preferring to receive your materials by email.

Badge Scanner Application


The organizers are selling licenses for the Lead Retrieval badge scanning application. Buy at least one license. A person came to your booth, you scanned his badge, immediately received all of his data - email, area of ‚Äã‚Äãinterest, company. You can use tags and notes to add information about him after communication. For one click directly from the application, you can send him your marketing materials.

But if additional expenses are not foreseen, then you can scan the business card using the Business Card Reader and also send your intro to email and then make the analytics in Excel .

Demonstration zones and devices


One of the main tasks of MWC visitors is to find out, test or see something new. Therefore, the demonstration zone, clearly marked with the words DEMO AREA, can become the main instrument for attracting an audience. Take a lot of gadgets with you where you‚Äôll show your products. The devices are constantly working and rather quickly discharged. Every smartphone or tablet should have at least one backup - one sat down, another took.


Tours


Since the scale of the exhibition is huge and choosing the most interesting on it is difficult, from this year the organizers offer visitors to take advantage of thematic tours Topi Tour . If your technology is suitable for excursions, we recommend to declare yourself in one of them: a group of visitors (an average of 20-30 people) will be brought to your stand twice a day, which you can present your new products in 10 minutes.

Team


We do not advise you to go to the MWC with a team of two people: either the stand will be empty most of the time (why did you book it at all?), Or you will not be able to work effectively at the exhibition. Gather a team of three to four people (sales, marketing, event-manager) so that each one performs their own role: presentations at the stand, study of the exhibition and meetings with potential customers.

Informal communication


During the MWC, there is a huge amount of informal "parties" that are worth going to (Google to help, and to start the Eventbrite app with events in Barcelona). Sometimes in a more casual atmosphere you can find very interesting acquaintances.

Hashtag for social networks


During the congress, the hashtag #MWC (or # mwc19 this year) is very popular. Add it to all your publications in social networks, many follow it, which means another chance to shine!

At last


Prepare your stay in Barcelona in advance. Not only buy tickets and book a hotel as early as possible, but also download everything on your smartphone that will make your life easier. Here is our helpful list:

  • Download the map of Barcelona in Google Maps on your smartphone to calmly navigate in the city without the Internet.
  • Stock up on Drimsim to avoid buying a local SIM card and not spending a lot of money on roaming. In Barcelona, ‚Äã‚ÄãDrimsim has excellent communication and internet, even within the MWC.
  • To navigate the subway, download TMBAPP .
  • For taxi rides - Mytaxi ( iOS , Android ). Uber is banned in Spain this year.
  • We recommend Reby service for renting electric scooters . Expensive, but accessible to foreigners, and riding around the city on a scooter is much more fun than walking.
  • In order not to drag a bunch of paper from the exhibition, scan all the interesting materials in FineScanner .
  • Traditionally Tripadvisor to find the best places in the city.
  • For recognition and translation of any printed texts, for example, a menu in a restaurant, please be TextGrabber .
