We distribute servers for interesting projects.

We decided to conduct an experiment and support young interesting projects by the fact that we are best able to provide servers. Aged, but still interesting, too, can take part. In short - this month we are ready to provide 5 Intel Xeon E3-1230 / 8GB DDR3 / 4x1TB SATA2 / 100Mbps Unmetered servers in DC EvoSwitch (Netherlands) to the most interesting ideas: 3 according to the voting community and 2 in its sole discretion. Read more - under the cut.



Both young projects and long-existing ones, both those planning commercialization and obviously non-commercial ones are invited to participate in the competition. But it is necessary to fulfill three conditions: a working prototype must be ready, the project must be of something useful to others, and also must have a goal and a clear development plan (and follow it).
