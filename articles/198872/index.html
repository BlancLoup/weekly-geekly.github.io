Concept: how to improve iMessage


I figured out how to improve the connection between imessagege and instagram, twitter and other social app: highlight inscriptions like @username as a link and let them choose in which app to open it.

Now, to add a friend who has sent me my username, I have to manually copy the username from the message; If there is something extra in the message, delete it (yes, editing the texts on the smartphone is very convenient). Then I need to open the desired app, go to the "find by user" button and paste the text. It turns out quite a long route. In my concept, it is reduced to two tapas.

It's clear that the idea easily spreads to any app that has @ text - for example, I write comments on instagram and write my friend's twitter username - and it is perfectly transferred to all mobile platforms.

If you like the idea, please support the shot on the dribble . I would like to draw the attention of companies that develop popular appy to this concept. They may also be interested in this, and they will think about creating an API that will allow applications to exchange links of this kind.
