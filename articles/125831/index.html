A new interface has appeared in Google Docs.



Google continues to change the design of its products in the style of Google+, and this time introduced a new interface for Google Docs . To look at it, you need to click on the link "New Interface" in the upper right corner. Through the same menu, you can return to the classic interface.

If, like me, it seems to you that initially the new interface takes up too much space, then in the settings you can choose a denser design option (Use denser view) .

The new interface has hot keys that allow you to navigate the list of documents without a mouse.
