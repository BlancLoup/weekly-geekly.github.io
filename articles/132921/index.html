Desura: distribution platform for games under Linux / Windows

After several months of development , the release of the Desura client for Linux is finally out . Now this platform for the distribution of games works on both Windows and Linux.

Desura specializes in independent game developers. The catalog has 253 games, including 65 for Linux, of which 16 are free. On the occasion of the release of the Linux-client, the sale of several toys has been announced, which can now be bought for the price of one or two euros. Among them - a futuristic hacker simulator Hacker Evolution: Untold .

After installing the client and registering, you get access to dozens of new games, free mods and add-ons, and you can participate in communication with other Linux gamers, give ratings and view general ratings.

In the near future, the Linux client developer Desura promises to lay out the source code of the client program under a free license. Pre- planned to choose a model of Chrome / Chromium, that is, to support two versions: one part open, and the other part with corporate trademarks and the like - under a commercial license.
