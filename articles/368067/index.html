How to create an entertainment system in your car is not worse than in representative limousines, but cheaper by an order of magnitude (or even two), know advanced users of modern electronics. Good component speakers, high-quality wires, amplifier plus your tablet or smartphone. Design

The main advantage of Radiomize is its versatility. After all, constructively, this is a common, but well-made, cover on the steering wheel with a touchpad mounted in it:



Therefore, putting it on the steering wheel of a passenger car is not a problem. The process takes 60 seconds, as demonstrated by the creators on the project page.



Stitched case from synthetic, reminiscent of natural leather. Contrast stitching, antibacterial treatment, perforation.

The touchpad with filling works from a lithium battery, a charge which lasts for a year of active use.

How does it work

Radiomize functions in conjunction with an application that is installed on Android or iOS. Now many people use tablets as a navigator, which are also synchronized with their own mailbox, with accounts in social networks, and downloading music is available without problems. Connect via AUX-connector to the onboard acoustics, and forward. Absolutely all the incoming information plus entertainment in front of your eyes.

Install the Radiomize application and control the modes using the touchpad without taking your eyes off the road.

There are several modes of operation:

  • right button - go to the next track, receive and read messages, select and reply to messages (only when stopped)



  • the left button is all the same, only the transition is made to the previous track



  • pressing and holding the buttons - change the volume:



  • double-click - depending on the button: skip tracks and messages; turn on play mode or pause



  • slip - swipe function



The device is also equipped with an SOS-sensor, which sends a traffic incident message to five selected addresses.

Radiomize communication with your devices via Bluetooth Low Energy.
