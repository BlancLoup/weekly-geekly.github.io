Regional operators oppose the abolition of roaming



Roaming cancellation causes positive emotions among subscribers of telecom operators of federal importance and negative ones among the operators themselves. Recently it became known that regional mobile operators, which serve approximately 3.5 million subscribers, are also against roaming cancellation. This writes the "Kommersant".

Regional companies are trying to convince the government that they will suffer continuous losses, since the charge for incoming calls in roaming will be canceled. Operators believe that it will not be possible to compensate for the losses, because in regions where there is no own network, you will still have to pay for the services of ‚Äúcolleagues‚Äù whose network the customers of the operator will use. Experts believe that in such a situation it is possible that the market will either leave some companies or increase tariffs sharply. Or maybe both will happen.

Regional companies do not just express an opinion, but try to change the situation. In particular, several operators at once sent a letter to the government of the Russian Federation describing the negative consequences of canceling billing of incoming calls in national roaming. This issue was discussed at a private meeting with Vladislav Fedulov, Director of the Information Technology and Communications Department of the Government, on October 1.

The meeting was attended by representatives of the "big four", as well as regional companies - "Motive" (he works in the Sverdlovsk and Kurgan regions, the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug and the Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Region), and Tattelecom (Tatarstan). ‚ÄúIt is expected that following the results they will make a decision that will be sent to the State Duma to finalize the draft law on the abolition of roaming,‚Äù one of the meeting participants said.

The bill on the abolition of roaming within the Russian Federation was adopted by the State Duma in the first reading in July. The document states that payment for connections between subscribers within the state should be made at a single rate to the operator. In this case, the location of subscribers, both the caller and the receiver does not matter.

After that, telecommunications companies have equated the cost of outgoing calls, Internet traffic and SMS on trips around the country to the tariffs of the home region. In addition, around the end of August and the beginning of September, representatives of the Big Four canceled the roaming charges.
