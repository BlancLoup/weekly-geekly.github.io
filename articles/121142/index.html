How I discovered GUNNAR


I wear glasses from childhood, and whoever talks about the effects of monitors and visual impairment - I never believed in it, I did not intend to part with my profession and understood that my whole life would be connected with computers. The vision did not deteriorate, but the older I became and the more I worked, the less time I could spend in front of the monitor without experiencing discomfort, burning eyes and fatigue. Working for different home / work / customers monitors, by the end of the day, the eyes began to organize a real riot, began to close by themselves, and when they tried to fight them, they began to water from any light source. And a few years ago there was a serious question about reducing the load on the eyes.



The first thoughts were the need to have an operation a la Lasic to return the standard 100% vision, but such an operation costs about 100,000 r. on both eyes + probability of re-conducting. To collect such an amount, it takes time, you need to continue working, which means again the day in front of the monitor, again fatigue, again drops and a limited work day. And then I began to look for a more affordable, cheap and practical solution. That decision turned out to be Gunnar Optiks glasses.

I stumbled upon them by luck, roaming in the expanses of the foreign Internet for eye strain ("eye fatigue") during prolonged work. Manufacturer and technology


GUNNAR Optiks is a technology company that seeks to enhance the ability of the human visual system. GUNNAR glasses are specially designed to provide a person with optical and visual advantages in work, games and everyday life in the shortest possible time.

What functions do points


  • Increase visual resolution.
  • Increase contrast.
  • Increase clarity and clarity
  • Neutralize bright and reflected light.
  • Relieve eye fatigue
  • Maintain a moist microclimate around the eyes.
  • Neutralize the feeling of dry eyes
  • Increase efficiency, productivity. and visual acuity.


Technology




i-AMP technology is the result of intensive research into the human optical system and its interaction with digital devices. According to the company improves the properties of the human eye. The lenses consist of a patented material, have an advanced geometry and are equipped with a special coating and shade.

The I-AMP technology includes:
1. Lens material - diAMIX
2. Geometry of the lens - fRACTYL : Designed using computer algorithms, it creates a microclimate around the eyes while keeping moisture and prevents evaporation by air currents.
  • Neoscopic fit for comfortable viewing at close range.
  • Transcopic fit for comfortable viewing over any distance.
3. Multi-level i-FI lens coating : Filters the transmitted light and protects the lenses:
  • Anti-glare reduces glare and distracting stray light.
  • Hydrophobic reduces the number of drops from water to the lenses to a minimum.
  • Oleophobic protects against smudging and fingerprints.
Secured with a hard coating that reduces wear and minimizes scratches.

4. Shades of lenses - iONIC : GUNNAR laboratory using spectrophotometry studied the visible and invisible parts of the light spectrum in different lighting conditions. And to balance the load on the eyes, combinations of iONik shades and i-FI coatings were used in the lenses. Which, depending on the wavelength, light conditions, strengthen or weaken the amount of energy that passes into a person‚Äôs eyes, while increasing their overall performance
