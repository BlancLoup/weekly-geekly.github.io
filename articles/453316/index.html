British chip maker ARM ceased cooperation with Huawei

The problems of the Chinese company Huawei, associated with the decree of US President Trump, continue to multiply. Recently it became known that the British chip maker ARM decided to suspend all existing contracts with the former partner . Huawei uses ARM products to design processors for their smartphones.

The fact is that the development of ARM contain American technology, and Trump's decree prohibits cooperation with Huawei to all companies who have access to American technology. The ARM has already stated that the company complies with all the latest rules established by the United States.

In turn, the Chinese company stated that it is well aware of the pressure being put on the company's partners in accordance with the actions of the US authorities. However, the priority of the company remains the same - the supply of technology and products to its customers.

At the end of May, the Chinese manufacturer announced that the company is now developing its own operating system , which will replace Android from Google - its services from August this year will become unavailable to users of Huawei mobile devices.

The operating system is developed, first of all, for the Chinese market, it will be presented at the beginning or in the middle of 2020. The head of the division added that the launch of the OS will occur if it is forbidden to cooperate with Android and Windows - products of two US corporations.

The new operating system will be called Hongmeng. It is based on the Linux operating system, and its name is rooted in Chinese mythology.

At the moment, cooperation with the Chinese suspended such companies as Google, Qualcomm, Intel, Broadcom. Japanese companies also began to stop working with Huawei. Panasonic has also joined the list of companies that have listened to Trump.
