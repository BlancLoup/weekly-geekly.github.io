Want to make friends with LINUX? This is easiest to do in Kiev from June 6 to June 10 at the MUK training center.

The first part of the LINUX curriculum, which will be read at TC MUK in the second week of June, is formally included in the HP training course catalog. But in fact, this is the development of the company GuruLabs - one of the most reputable training centers for training LINUX, and was licensed by HP after a very careful selection.

An interesting feature of the course is the possibility of its tuning to the requirements of students. That is, you can read it on RedHat, you can on SuSe, but you can consider both of these distributions, highlighting the features of each. The group, starting to study on June 6th, chose to study RedHat, and there are still a couple of vacancies. The instructor who conducts the lesson has unique experience in both practical application and reading courses in FreeBSD, Sun (now Oracle) Solaris, HP UX, and a dozen of LINUX distributions. Current instructor statuses: HP / Oracle / Cisco. So formulate your problems - there is a real chance to find their solution right in the classroom - www.muk.ua/training/courses/chp/h8
