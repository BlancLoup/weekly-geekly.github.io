In the motherboard bribed low (at that time) price, the presence of built-in bluetooth (like and not needed, but the presence still warms the soul ...), well, HDMI with support for sound transmission. The body, it would seem too big, but in fact fits on the rack with the equipment is quite elegant. Of course, the less, the better and my choice was not bad.
