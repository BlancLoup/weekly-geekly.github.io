Results of the survey on the functions of the browser Vivaldi


Hello! Three weeks ago, we began a short survey on Vivaldi functions , the purpose of which was to find out which browser features are popular with users. Let me remind you that the survey participants were offered a list of already existing Vivaldi functions, from which you could choose no more than five of the most popular in the user's opinion. Naturally, it was about the functions that the survey participant actually uses in their daily work. The survey process did not go very smoothly for technical reasons - some users could not complete the vote, but, anyway, the majority managed to do it and today we can talk about the results.

First of all - some statistics. Actually, the survey lasted 10 days - from September 21 to September 30, and during this time more than 1600 users were able to express their opinions. In the table below you can see the full list of functions sorted by popularity among users (in brackets beside the name of the function is its number in the survey list):


As you can see, no special surprises happened: users still prefer the functional side of working with the browser, and all the decorative delights and amenities are overshadowed.
