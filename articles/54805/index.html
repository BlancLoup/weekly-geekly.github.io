Dell Adamo officially introduced



The acclaimed Dell Adamo laptop is finally officially presented.
Under the cut of TTX and video from the presentation.

Specifications:
  • The body of a single piece of aluminum;
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo U9300;
  • Graphics Intel X4500;
  • RAM: 2 GB DDR3;
  • 128 GB solid state drive;
  • Display with a diagonal of 13.4 inches with an aspect ratio of 16: 9 (1366 x 768);
  • Bluetooth 2.1 wireless modules, Wi-Fi, 3G (optional);
  • Ports DisplayPort, HDMI, DVI, VGA, 2 USB;
  • Battery life - 4-5 hours;
  • Weight - 1.8 kg;
  • OS Windows Vista Home Premium Edition SP1, 64-bit;




The cost of a laptop - from $ 1999
