Camera Review Samsung Galaxy K Zoom

Hi, Habr!

Since the release of the first camera phone, 14 years have passed. During this time, the quality of pictures taken on a mobile, has become superior to photos taken on some cameras. But manufacturers do not want to stop there, so they began to make camera phones - devices that are even closer to decent cameras. Samsung tried to do this with the SCH-W880 model in 2009, then with the Galaxy S4 Zoom , and now with the new K Zoom line of phones. About Samsung Galaxy K Zoom for more details - under the cut.

Design


What exactly do we have - a camera with a phone function or a phone with a camera function? On the display side, we have a 4.8-inch smartphone, reminiscent of the Samsung Galaxy S5, and on the side of a 20-megapixel camera, this is ... still a phone, albeit with a huge traveling lens. In contrast, by the way, from the S4 Zoom, which is more like a camera.

And now let's take a quick look at the history of camera phones from Samsung. It's nice that there are advertising posters for these smartphones, they will be useful for continuing the History of mobile phones and smartphones in advertising .

One of the first camera phones Samsung came out in 2009. This model SCH-W880 has not reached the Russian market. Why is the phone interesting? It was also equipped with a triple zoom exit lens. Camera resolution - 12 megapixels. The display is only some 3.3 inches and 480x800 pixels. It is very similar to an ordinary camera.



Galaxy S4 Zoom came out last year. This is a modification of the S4 Mini with additional features for photographing. But at the same time, the body has undergone significant changes: a moving lens, a ring for focusing, and also a projection for the right hand make this smartphone similar to a camera.



But the K Zoom is exactly what a smartphone with a huge lens. The company decided not to follow in the footsteps of modifying smartphones, but to single out a separate line of camera phones.



The smartphone is available in several colors: blue, white and black.

In general, it is worthwhile to talk about Samsung‚Äôs work on the bugs after last year‚Äôs S4 Zoom.

First, I have the same look. The new smartphone is not like the Chinese "soap box". It looks exactly like a smartphone, but with a huge lens. There is no protrusion for a comfortable fit of the camera in your hand.

Secondly, the S4 Zoom lens had a ring that made it possible to adjust the focus. Just like on a regular camera. This ring made the smartphone too cumbersome - it just won't fit in your pocket. In the case of K, the dimensions, though not the smallest, are not at all repulsive. And the lens does not cling to the details of clothing.

Amazing. but at the same time, the dimensions of the new smartphone are much larger than the S4. After all, the display at K - 4.8 inches, and not 4.3. Thickness - 16.6 against 15.4 for its predecessor. Save the smooth shape of the model.

From other external differences: the novelty does not have a mount for a tripod and an infrared port. Tripod still was a useful thing.

The front panel is a classic Samsung button, speaker, 2 megapixel camera and light and distance sensors. To the right and left of the main button two more touch buttons are hidden, highlighted by necessity.



At the bottom of the smartphone is an unprotected microUSB port and a microphone.



On the right side there are several physical buttons at once: volume control, shutdown and shutter button. Conveniently, the volume control in camera mode controls the zoom.



On the top is another microphone and a headset port.



On the left side there is a port for MicroSD and ... a hole for a cord. For some reason I have not seen this in smartphones for a long time. Although in the case of the camera phone this probably has some meaning.



Port for microSD is protected. Hot-swapping is possible, which is just necessary in the case of a camera phone equipped with only 8 GB of memory.



The back cover is the same as in the Galaxy Tab S tablets and the Galaxy S5 smartphone. Only the hole for the camera is much larger.







The cover is removable. Under it - the battery. Again, in the case of a camera phone in any case, the ability to replace the battery is necessary, since the flash can gobble up the battery while shooting is active.





Under the battery - a slot for a SIM-card and information about the smartphone.





Iron


Inside the smartphone after the S4 Zoom, everything changed dramatically, but it also affected the price. The starting price of the K Zoom is 24,990 rubles, and the S4 Zoom started selling 24,990 last year (0 quickly reduced to 19,990, now 11,990).

The smartphone is based on the Samsung Exynos 5260 processor, where two cores operate at 1.7 GHz, and the remaining four cores operate at 1.3 GHz. The Mali-T624 processor is responsible for the video.

For taking photos and videos 20.7 megapixel camera with 10x optical zoom, arched with a xenon flash.

It's nice that the smartphone battery can be replaced by opening the back cover. Also, support for micro SD is a positive moment, allowing you to smooth out such a disadvantage as 8 GB of internal memory.

Autonomous work


The battery at 2430 mAh, in my opinion, is too small for such a device. So for active work with him, and especially for photographing during a hike or journey, I would advise you to take replacement batteries or an external battery for charging. The same Galaxy S5 has a 2800 mAh battery, although the S4 Zoom has even less, only 2330 mAh.

Camera


Immediately it should be noted that no other camera phone now has an objective with a 10-fold optical zoom. The clear advantage of a smartphone for big photo lovers. Samsung also tried to make the shooting as convenient as possible. The camera is built on a BSI-sensor, there is an optical image stabilization. Focal length is 4.4-44 mm, aperture f / 3.1-f / 6.3.

Unlike the S4 Zoom, there is no lens ring. You can zoom in and out with the volume keys while the camera is running. You can also do this with your fingers on the display, but this method is terribly inconvenient.

Grieved by the lack of technology ISOCELL, which is described in this habraposte . Especially because in S5 it is used. Also mount for a tripod, in my opinion, should be left.

As for the video, the maximum shooting resolution is 1920 x 1080 30 fps.



As for the software component for the convenience of shooting - there are many modes with which you can easily cope with different tasks. For example, the mode "Shooting children": it is that the phone takes, firstly, five pictures, and, secondly, it meows, gurgles and makes other funny sounds to attract attention.
