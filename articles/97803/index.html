Seagate introduced an external hard drive with a volume of 3 terabytes

As promised in May, Seagate Technology, the largest hard drive maker, announced the launch of an external hard drive of 3 terabytes. As stated in the company's press release, it will be possible to simultaneously store 120 high-definition films, 1,500 games, thousands of photos and an infinite number of hours of music on this disc.

The height of the disk is 158 millimeters, the width is 124 millimeters, the length is 44 millimeters, and it weighs 1080 grams.

A three-byte GoFlex Desk can be purchased at Seagate.com or through retailers for $ 249.99. It comes with a USB 2.0 adapter that can be upgraded to USB 3.0 or Firewire 800.
