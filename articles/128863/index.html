TeraPay e-commerce forum

Usually novice entrepreneurs lack professional advice on starting and developing their own business. You can, of course, contact a lawyer or any special office where you have to pay a considerable amount for consultation. But now such assistance can be obtained free of charge.

Not so long ago, Tera Holding launched a new project - a forum on e-commerce TeraPay. It works on the popular phpBB engine.
A forum is intended for anyone who wants to engage in or is already engaged in their business on the Internet or offline.

When developing the forum, we understood that we need to seriously interest visitors, which is why we decided to help businessmen with expert advice.

So, the main advantage of our forum are free consultations of experts:
- on legal issues
- on accounting issues
- on marketing part
- to create a website for business
- on automation of business processes and management.

In addition, the forum provides sections in which partners and investors can be found both in the regions and internationally.

And in the "Thematic Articles" section you can find a lot of information useful for individual entrepreneurs and organizations (reference books, legislative base, lectures on accounting and 1C: accounting).

If you have questions on the designated topics, come to us. We think that this resource will be really useful to many. We will welcome guests and new members of the forum.
