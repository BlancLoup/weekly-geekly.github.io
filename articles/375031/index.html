From February 1, Microsoft raises prices for software and hardware in Russia by 15-30%



Rising software prices range from 15% for private clients and up to 30% for corporate, depending on the licensing plan.

Whether the price adjustment of the Lumia line will be affected is not yet clear, but the XBox consoles will jump in price by a third, XBox Live subscriptions - by 20%. The games released until February, however, will continue to be sold at the old price.

It is important to note that until February, existing customers can renew license agreements and purchase new products at current prices.

Russia is one of the few countries in which Microsoft makes payments not in dollars, but in local currency. According to Vladimir Levakov, the genius director of one of the large distribution networks, this fact allowed many partner companies to avoid bankruptcy.

If a distributor buys a product in dollars from a vendor and sells in rubles, explains Mr. Levakov, then in case of fluctuations in the financial market, he may be on the verge of ruin - this has already happened on the domestic software market in 2008-2009. Now Microsoft, selling its products to a distributor at a fixed ruble price, takes on these risks.

[according to CNews and RIA Novosti ]
