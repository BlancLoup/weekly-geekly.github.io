As you probably know, in April 2014, Microsoft stops supporting Windows XP. If you continue to use this OS, users may encounter security threats and application compatibility issues. In a programme
  • Windows 8.1 Professional and the advantages of Windows devices. It will focus on the key benefits of Windows 8.1 Professional and how devices on the Windows platform may be of interest to business users. Marat Khairulin, trainer and content manager, CCG department, Microsoft Russia

If this is your first Dell webinar, then you need to go through a simple registration procedure (from the link above) and wait for the email with the details to an email.
