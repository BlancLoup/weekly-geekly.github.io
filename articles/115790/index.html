Delicious for sale at a minimal price.



If you have a million US dollars, then you can become a happy owner of the social bookmarking service Delicious. In any case, just such information is provided by "sources close to the leadership of Yahoo." Yes, Yahoo is seeking to get rid of this social bookmarking service, and is ready to sell it for $ 1-2 million (the exact amount is not yet known). It is worth noting that the corporation itself bought this service for $ 15 million.

The buyer, by the way, may be another service, StumbleUpon. This service has just received $ 17 million, so it can easily buy Delicious from Yahoo. So far, everything is in a state of negotiation, so it is impossible to say for sure whether one service will buy another or not.

Let me remind you that Yahoo was going to close Delicious at all (the reason for this was never explained). Surprise is Yahoo's insistent desire to get rid of Delicious, since the content of such a service costs a couple of tens of thousands of US dollars.
