Mongo Moscow


On May 16, Mongo Moscow, a conference dedicated to the non-relational database management system with open source MongoDB, will be held at the Digital October center.

The conference will feature reports from specialists from 10gen, CROC and Yandex; It will focus on the development of database schemas, indexing, administration, replication, sharding and much more.

The event will be held at the Digital October center with the support of 10gen, the owner and developer of MongoDB.

The live broadcast can be viewed on the website: www.digitaloctober.ru

UPDATE : Live broadcast has started.
