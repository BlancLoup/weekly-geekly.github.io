And this is Chelyabinsk ... UWDC venue!

Chelyabinsk Internet users are so harsh that they organize a conference among the week.
On February 20 and 21, the next Ural Web Developer Conference, UWDC, will be held in the most ‚Äúharsh‚Äù city of the country.



Four years ago, several Ural guys wanted to organize their online conference with blackjack, solitaire and poetess. So began the history of UWDC. From the Ural sidewalk, the Ural Web Developer Conference quickly turned into a federal event. After all, it is not often possible to arrange such a development outside the capitals.

This year the conference will be held in a completely new format, on a new platform. A year ago, some sections were so popular that they hardly fit everyone. Therefore, UWDC'13 will be held in the entertainment complex MEGAPOLIS , which not only comfortably accommodates a larger number of participants, but also helps culturally relax after a busy day of reports.



What's on the agenda?

Web development
The excitement around cloud technologies has subsided, and frontend-development comes to the first lines of the news, driven by the growing popularity of mobile platforms - we will pay close attention to it at UWDC'13. Server programming also did not stand aside, and among other famous speakers, we traditionally expect Alexander Makarov to arrive from Yii.
Full list of web development reports

Design
The main message of this year: more practice, experience, stories about experiences with morality, and less transcendental chatter. Master classes and workshops are planned.
Our speakers will share their experience in the development of metro schemes, the use of infographics on the web, and we will also talk about the interfaces and analyze the Illustrator's tools when working with them.
Specifically, the design section will be held in a small nightclub, it is very comfortable, the interior is excellent, and even a bar with a working bar will be located right behind the listeners;)
Full list of design reports



Internet Marketing
Traditionally, this is one of the most intense and popular sections at the conference. Roman Rybalchenko will come from Kiev to tell how web analytics can help save the budget and increase conversion, and he will also hold a visual workshop on web analytics. Shabalin Sergey and Yevgeny Letov will tell how to make a website for SEO right from the start. Daniel Silantiev will share his experience in creating an e-mail newsletter for an online store and information project.
Full list of internet marketing reports

Mobile technology
The mobile section will collect reports from leading IT companies, including JetStyle, Microsoft, Bitrix, Mail.Ru, etc. This year we will talk about the development of new mobile devices, such as phones, tablets and even TVs! Also pay attention to the new trend - the mobile web.
Full list of mobile development reports

State and business
The section will serve as a discussion platform for representatives of Internet business and the state. Also, a number of expert practitioners will share their experience in building a business in the Internet sphere.
Full list of reports of the section "State and Business"



How to get?

You can get to the conference in any accessible way: by plane, train, car, snowmobile, reindeer sleigh - it all depends on the degree of your distance and the desire to share these days with like-minded people.



Like last year, participation is paid . The cost of 1500 rubles. for 2 days - democratic and at the same time protects the conference microclimate from random people. There will be only our people , and it will be interesting ;-)

A ticket to the conference can be purchased right now on the website UWDC.ru. The good news is that the ticket can be won in contests held on Twitter and on the official Vkontakte group .
For guests of the city, the Malakhit Hotel (located 200 meters from the venue) provides conference participants with a 30% discount on the room rate.

PS Do not forget to warn the management about their participation in the conference, since It takes place on weekdays, before the holiday weekend. And on weekends you will have the opportunity to recuperate or go skiing among the Ural Mountains;)

See you on February 20 and 21 in Chelyabinsk! Follow the conference news on Twitter by hashing #uwdc .
