Stem cells and Oriental cloning



Immediately, two genetic news came from Japan Square.
The first, interesting from the point of view of the future person, is the successful experience of obtaining functioning brain tissue from stem cells. Initially, the purpose of the experiment was to recreate the tissues of the cerebral cortex (which, as IPPavlov bequeathed: "The supreme controller and distributor of animal and human organism functions"), but in the end, the researchers managed to obtain cells of various tissues.
