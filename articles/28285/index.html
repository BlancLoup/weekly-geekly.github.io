Telehouse Caravan: hosting for the rich

Telecommunication company Karavan opened a high-tech platform in Moscow to provide Telehouse Caravan IT infrastructure services . This is a new project for the market, implemented and put into operation taking into account the increased requirements for the reliability of the server platform, power supply and quality of service.

The new site is focused on large companies whose need for physical hosting services has gone beyond the existing offers on the market. These are mainly corporate users, the financial and banking sector, Internet service providers, IT companies, telecom operators, and government agencies. The most demanding customers can even rent a dedicated data center (a separate room) at the Telehouse Caravan site.

Telehouse Caravan is a unified system of independent protected data centers. The engineering infrastructure is implemented using innovative design principles: the power allocated to the rack exceeds by more than five times the standard offers of existing data centers and can reach 30 kW.

From June 5, 2008, sales in the following areas of physical hosting are open:

rental of seats in the rack (1945 rubles per month);
Colocation Standart and Colocation Unlimited (2975 rubles and 5034 rubles per month);
rental racks;
rental of dedicated data centers.
For customers who rent a data center, it is possible to configure the existing parameters of the Telehouse in accordance with the requirements of the hosted IT infrastructure. Unlimited tariffs and turnkey solutions are available to subscribers of colocation services.
