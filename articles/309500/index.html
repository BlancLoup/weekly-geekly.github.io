We invite developers to Droidcon Moscow 2016

In London, San Francisco, Amsterdam, Montreal, Berlin, Paris and finally in Moscow! September 22, 2016 will be the largest Eurasian Android conference - Droidcon Moscow 2016 .

After 2 weeks, Russian and foreign Google Developer Experts will make presentations and share their insights on new technologies and tools. The conference program is divided into four main areas: Android, Firebase, VR and IoT.



Android


On the Android section, important topics will be raised, including we will talk about architecture: Daniil Serdyukov will tell us about MVVM in the realities of modern projects with good test coverage and pinch of reactivity.
Ex-googler Android developer Lisa Wray will arrive to us from New York with a report on advanced use of RecyclerView. And it will not be the only report about UI components, there will also be a report on their correct writing and subtleties of use.

Denis Neklyudov will share the experience of adapting his application to the new version of Android Nougat. We will also talk about Material Design in modern applications. Consider the details of sun.misc.Unsafe to learn Zen performance, without writing C ++ code. And the guys from Spb Software will share their development experience for Android TV and Google Cast.

Firebase


One of the main themes of the Google I / O conference in May was the release of Firebase, a set of tools designed for the development of mobile applications. Within the section, the speakers tried to cover all the components of the presented system.

The first topic will be devoted to the key tool introduced in Firebase and which favorably distinguishes it from its competitors - Analytics. Timur Akhmetgareyev, Senior Android developer App in the Air, will talk about how Analytics integrates with other components of the system, about each of these components separately and, of course, shares real cases and ways to implement them. Then Alexey Mileev will tell about the features of migration of projects created on the basis of Parse on Firebase. The third report will be presented by Sergey Smetanin to the CTO of the Rubeacon company. He will talk about solving problems from the company's practice, demonstrate the use of such tools as RDB, Remote Config and FCM. Here the participants are expected not only by the description of the implementation, but also by the performance evaluation and much more. Reports are based on real tasks: the speakers promise that they will be lively and interesting.

VR


The VR section contains reports on the latest trends in VR, on what has not yet sounded at other conferences. To start Android Team Leader from MERA, and part-time leader of GDG Nizhny Novgorod, Alexander Korshak will perform the Daydream API preparation in a report on the same technology presented on Google I / O 2016. Participants will see technical aspects only, and live examples on Android and Unity.

Next, Mikhail Vaisman, CEO of Trinity Digital, will tell you how to make a great application for Google Cardboard. An example is the AirPano Virtual Travel application, developed by Trinity Digital and awarded the Google Material Design Award. You will learn how to choose the best technology to work with the display of 360-panoramas. Completes the section of Android Team Leader of the company Trinity Digital, the leader of GDG Petrozavodsk, Andrei Khitry with the analysis of the demo with sound in VR (spatial audio).

Iot


This year, the IoT section is held for the first time on the Droidcon. Immediately, two Google Developer Expert on the Internet of Things - Zviad Kardava and Sasha Voltaire, who is developing SmartHome solutions at Deutsche Telekom AG, will talk about what awaits developers due to the growing popularity of IoT. Sasha will present a report on how to prototype IoT solutions using different IDEs, visual languages ‚Äã‚Äãand even Minecraft. Voltaire proves that this approach can be applied to real projects using IoT platforms and SmartHome solutions, such as Google Weave, Eddystone Beacons, Eclipse SmartHome or openHAB. And Zviad Kardava will show how to assemble the magic mirror himself.

You can get acquainted with the final program of the conference and purchase tickets on the official website .

See you at Droidcon Moscow 2016!
