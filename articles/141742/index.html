Startup WebcamTelecom. A look at the communications of the future

Is it possible to noticeably press the world market of expensive telephone communication operators, having slightly changed the way people organize communications on the Internet? Now it‚Äôs hard to even imagine, in spite of the development of VoIP, the growth in the number of services that allow you to call phones from a browser, make free video calls, etc.
We make our modest contribution and offer to test our vision of the development of human voice communications.

Based on the fact that the schemes and principles of building telephone networks are morally outdated, we see a future in which a cloud is replaced by an expensive complex infrastructure and strict regulations that form high prices for telephone services, within which all outgoing and SMS are free. A computer, tablet, smartphone, TV and other digital devices connected to the Internet will enable a person to use all the individual services of the ‚Äútelephone‚Äù cloud, often without using expensive voice communication channels. The concepts of long-distance, international communication and roaming in the cloud will lose their meaning, and the simplicity, clarity and familiarity of client applications will allow any user who does not have any knowledge in the field of telecommunications and communications to organize, for example, a virtual PBX or Call Center for their needs.

By sending the necessary applications to FIPS (Russian Patent Office), we present to your attention a prototype of the cloud project WebcamTelecom , which, through a browser, video-audio-chat communication, is intended to replace telephony usage, preserving the usual concepts, functionality and capabilities of landline and mobile communications, making with new user options.

Basic principles. Webcam subscribers.


Each registered user of WebcamTelecom receives a unique nine-digit digital number. With this number, he can become a Webcam subscriber and receive incoming calls. Any user who follows the link like http://webcamtelecom.com/100000001 from any site on the network (or directly), in one click calls the Webcam subscriber with the number 10,000,0001. The user who knows the Webcam subscriber's e-mail address can initiate the call by clicking on the link, specifying the email address of the subscriber instead.

The calling user may, before making the call, formulate the topic of the forthcoming conversation (or ask a question). This information will be visible to the Webcam subscriber and will help him process the incoming call correctly.
Outgoing calls from users who are not registered on WebcamTelecom (or who have not performed authentication) are admissible, but have functional limitations. For example, an anonymous user cannot enable live streaming from his webcam during a video conversation.

In order to receive calls online, the subscriber needs to be authenticated and located on WebcamTelecom. Incoming calls during the absence of the Webcam subscriber on the site can be transferred to offline (as an SMS with the specified topic of conversation) for further processing or recorded as missed. Re-entering the site, the Webcam subscriber will see information on incoming SMS and missed calls, can call back in response (reset the reply SMS), provided that the author of the SMS or missed call is also a Webcam subscriber.

Incoming calls can form a queue. For each incoming online call to a queue, a Webcam subscriber can write a response or refuse to receive a call without going into video conversation mode. You can only go to video talk with the first caller in the queue. A written response, a rejection of the conversation, or a video call session ends the call.

Virtual office PBX or Webcam-services.


For collective / corporate reception and processing of incoming calls using Webcam-services. They can be compared with the work of virtual office PBXs and even Call-centers (simultaneous reception of a large number of incoming to a single "multi-channel" number by an arbitrary number of employees of the organization, transfer of calls between employees of the organization, etc.).
Webcam services have text identifiers. For example, clicking the link http://webcamtelecom.com/Name initiates a call to the virtual PBX "Name". Any Webcam operator of the Name Webcam service can accept such a call.

The owner / customer of the Webcam service can be a legal entity or an arbitrary community of individuals who have an account in the system.
Any user of WebcamTelecom can independently register an arbitrary number of Customers, create and configure an arbitrary number of Webcam services for them. Each Webcam service should have one Customer, but each Customer can have an arbitrary number of Webcam services.

By entering e-mail addresses (as account identifiers), you can create an individual group of Webcam operators for each Webcam service.

From users who tried to call during off-hours or during the absence of Webcam operators in the network, Webcam services also allow you to receive SMS messages. Any webcam operator for a missed call will be able to call back only a Webcam subscriber or a Webcam operator from whom a call was missed or an SMS arrived.

Any registered user of WebcamTelecom can be a Webcam operator of an arbitrary number of Webcam services. Thus, a Webcam user can receive incoming calls on his digital device at the same time: personal, work, home, related to hobbies, part-time work, social activities of the real or virtual world, etc.
When a Webcam user is working at once in several Webcam services as a Webcam operator, his queue of incoming calls is formed by calls from all the virtual PBXs to which he is currently connected.
With simultaneous work of several Webcam operators of a single Webcam service, each operator sees a single formed service queue and can process incoming calls.

Monetization


For Webcam subscribers, all available functionality is provided free of charge. Webcam-services (virtual office PBX) have advanced functionality and are available for a fee, according to the tariff plans.

Tariff plans are developed separately for collective and corporate use.
In case of collective use by individuals, the service can be paid by the user who is part of the management team of the Customer. At the time of publication, electronic money and the Robokassa payment acceptance system are supported for payment.
The provision of services to legal entities by bank transfer is carried out so far only in the territory of the Russian Federation, with the execution of the necessary documentation, invoicing, etc.

All outgoing calls and SMS within WebcamTelecom service are always free. Roaming, long-distance and international calls within WebcamTelecom are free. Inbox free only for webcam subscribers.
Tariff plans for Webcam-services are organized on the principle of a monthly fee, without taking into account the number and actual total duration of incoming calls. Tariffs are formed depending on the mode of operation of the Webcam-service, the maximum number of online operators and the selected maximum duration of a video call.
For Webcam services, there is a trial period, during which you can test WebcamTelecom functionality for free by implementing it on collective / corporate Internet resources.
