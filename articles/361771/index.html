Stand for practical shooting in the country with their own hands

The complexity and cost of licensing a shooting gallery is so high that even million cities cannot always boast of having at least one shooting gallery suitable for training.
But even if you are lucky, and there is a shooting range in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãyour access, the cost of shooting in it is prohibitively high - even for amateur trainings you will have to give 8-10 thousand rubles a month, not to mention professional sports. Who am I and why am I writing this text

I am a programmer who has been developing software for an interactive shooting gallery in Elitcom LLC for about 5 years.

In 2008 I was invited to this project by a programmer. My work is the core and graphics engine.
Six months ago, tired of the project, I left Elitcom. Now the project is developing without me.
I did not spoil relations with Elitcom, and from time to time they share successes in the development of the project.
A month ago, the owners of ‚ÄúElitcom‚Äù told that they were developing a laser pistol with a complete imitation of the work of a conventional firearm, with the exception of the departure of a striking element.
I am not aware of the implementation details, as this is a commercial secret. This post is an attempt to think about how I would make this gun so that it would be as effective as possible in terms of replacing real weapons and at the same time being as accessible as possible.

The main goal of the work is to create such a gun so that training with its use does not differ from training with the use of a real weapon. But it is important to comply with the following requirements:
  • You do not need a special room for shooting.
  • The gun is available for free purchase and use.
  • Cartridges are available for free purchase.
  • The price of the cartridge is minimal
  • Completely safe for others.


The mechanism of the gun that meets these requirements is extremely simple:
We insert a laser and a piston into the barrel of a real pistol, on which, when fired, press the generated gases.
The cartridge is an empty sleeve with a cap. Capsule power is enough to press the piston and, at the same time, fully interact with the gate.

It is worth discussing implementation details separately.

Ammunition equipment

Perhaps this is the easiest part of the whole project.

The cartridge in our case consists of only two parts:
  • Sleeve
  • Capsule

The sleeve of a suitable caliber can be shot. The main thing that she was not bloated and freely entered the chamber.
Caps are sold freely and cost from 1 to 5 rubles.
The liner is reusable and the cost of the shot is reduced to the cost of replacing the capsule.

Pistol

Legally difficult part of the project.

Fortunately, there is such a thing as MMG (Layout Mass-Dimensional). In fact, it is an ordinary gun with changes that resulted in it ceasing to be a firearm and became a decorative item.
There are many options for the conversion of firearms. For example, filling the barrel with metal, which guarantees the unsuitability of the use of weapons for firing. For obvious reasons, this conversion option is not suitable for our task.
Fortunately, there is another version of the conversion: holes are drilled at regular intervals in the barrel of the weapon.
The gun remains fully functional, but when you try to shoot a live cartridge, the barrel is torn.
The gun after such a conversion is fully suitable for our task, but it is freely sold and does not require any documents either for purchase or for wearing.
The best option for an ordinary athlete is to buy a cheap used traumatic pistol for a low-power cartridge, for example, Walther P22T and then convert it to MMG.

Laser block

Structurally difficult part of the project.
The laser unit, in theory, looks very simple: the laser itself, the battery, the button and the piston that presses the button.
The main difficulty lies in the fact that all this we need to cram into the barrel of a gun, which is not characterized by the presence of a large free space.
Trunk incision with laser block inside:

List of individual parts:

  1. Insert into the barrel with partitions that will not allow the piston to leave the working area
  2. Laser assembly. Inside the laser itself, batteries, and behind the button sticks out, when pressed, the laser turns on.
  3. External powerful spring designed to dampen the blow to the piston
  4. Inner weak spring pushing button
  5. The piston, under the influence of gases moving in the working area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe barrel
  6. Cartridge without powder and striking element

The distance between the cartridge and the piston is of fundamental importance.
If the distance is too large, then the cap power may not be enough for all reloading mechanisms to work properly.
If the distance is too small, the recharging will be initiated ahead of time and at the time of ejection of the liner the burning reaction will not end. So, this reaction will occur already in the open air, which will lead to a flash from the side of the ejector. Not deadly, but loud, bright and not pleasant.
The question of laser alignment in the barrel remains unsolved.
Do you have any idea how to adjust the laser so that it is convenient, not too difficult and reliable enough?

Target construction

Obviously, the pistol of the discussed design is not capable of hitting targets.
So, the question arises of detecting hits.
The gun can be used with systems designed to work with conventional laser guns. For example, Striker, about which I spoke in a spoiler, can handle hitting a laser beam on a screen.
But Striker is worth the money and considerable. At the same time, its capabilities are redundant for regular shooting exercises.
Therefore, I propose to discuss a simple and cheap version of targets suitable for use.

The target is a sheet of plywood with an image of the target pasted on top.
Nearby is a camera aimed at the target.
The camera connects to the controller (for example, to a smartphone).
Next we have two options:
  1. Simplest

    Turn on the controller permanent record.
    A conventional camera with FPS 20 is quite enough to capture the laser dot.
  2. Difficult, but better

    We program the controller so that it saves only those frames on which the laser beam is present and automatically calculated the point of impact on the target. Plus, of course, the transfer of data to a common server in order to track the shooting results in real time.

But the second option has the problem of highlighting the laser beam.
In the case of unstable street lighting, interference with the camera is quite possible. Often changing the level of illumination is difficult to adjust.

Of course, at home or in the yard you should not shoot from it. Although it is not dangerous, but noisy, and the neighbors will be unhappy.
But to arrange training in the near career or at the cottage, no one will interfere.
The cost of the shot is 10 times lower than the present! Not everyone can afford to throw out 8,000 per workout every month. A completely different thing - 800 rubles. This can afford literally everyone.
At the same time, there are no losses in the quality of training. All the same, only there is no striking element.
The absence of a striking element is also very important when learning how to shoot adolescents.
Many of us remember the horrible story about a boy who died in archery training.
