NEX-3 and NEX-5 - a couple of interesting cameras from Sony

A couple of interesting cameras announced today Japanese Sony. New items belong to the Alpha series and are sort of compacts with the possibility of changing optics. Their dimensions are obliged to the developers of Sony, who decided to abandon the use of a system of mirrors, making the device more compact. The NEX-3 and NEX-5 models are 24.2 and 25.4 mm thick, respectively.




Both cameras are equipped with a 14.2-megapixel Exmor APS HD CMOS sensor and a BIONZ image processor. ISO sensitivity reaches values ‚Äã‚Äãof 12800. The cameras have 3-inch pivoting screens (921 thousand points), which are also used as a viewfinder. In addition to body size and colors, the NEX-3 and NEX-5 differ in terms of shooting video. NEX-3 is capable of shooting 720p video, and NEX-5 Full HD.
