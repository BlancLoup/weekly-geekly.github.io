Startup for foreign language learners

Hello!
I recently started learning English.
And of course, when learning a language, it is necessary to learn new words, expressions, idioms.
While on the road and at work, it is not very convenient to carry a notebook with you to learn words or just repeat them.
That was the idea to create a startup.

The basic idea is a personal dictionary of foreign words to which the user himself would add words, idioms, and of course their translation.
It seems to me - it would be very convenient to log in to your account through the phone, and repeat new words)
