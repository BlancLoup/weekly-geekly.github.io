And a little more about the Fujitsu World Tour

Hi Habr! In our previous post, we already talked about Russian projects that will be presented at the Fujitsu World Tour 2016 : creating the largest storage system in Russia (in terms of memory) for the petrochemical cluster of the Republic of Bashkortostan and building an integrated infrastructure for the Samara Medical Center Heart Clinic. In addition, we look forward to the presentation of international expert Daniel Thorniley, president of DT-Global Business Consulting GmbH. In this article, we will touch on other projects that Fujitsu customers will tell you about - the Safe City in Chita, the modernization of the IT infrastructure of Yulmart, and also talk about what Fujitsu GDC Russia is doing and what Retail solutions Fujitsu produces . All interested please under the cat. Registration for the conference is open until September 16!



Let's start with the projects. Most recently, a joint project was completed with Rostelecom PJSC (with the participation of a partner, AT Consulting) - building an IT infrastructure to ensure the operation of the Safe City system. It includes video surveillance in public places and outdoor cameras, as well as an analytics system based on the captured video of incidents in the city (accidents, fires, etc.), which in real time processes a huge stream of data from the cameras and provides an analytical A report with recommendations for preventing emergencies. To provide systems with computing resources in Chita, 2 geographically distributed data centers were built based on Fujitsu servers and data storage systems: several dozen servers and 2 ETERNUS storage systems per site. Storage capacity for video storage - about 2 petabytes each. All applications that record, edit video and analytics are in a virtual environment, the video is processed in an automated mode, and an analytical report is created. The project in Chita was launched at the end of last year, and at the beginning of 2016 it was fully commissioned.

Another major project that we will discuss at the conference is the creation of an integrated IT infrastructure for Yulmart, the leader of Russian e-commerce. The project of migration to PRIMERGY servers and Fujitsu ETERNUS storage systems began in 2010, and by now the vast majority of systems - ERP, 1C SCP, website, and so on. operates on Fujitsu hardware. Now there are 3 independent data centers and dozens of stores throughout Russia. The peculiarity of the project is that initially Yulmart purchased Fujitsu equipment for the implementation of the SAP ERP system, but after evaluating the quality and usability of working with Fujitsu, he decided to switch completely to systems from one vendor, which gives serious advantages. When switching to a new generation of Fujitsu servers and storage systems, the continuity of the architecture is preserved and, having learned to work with this equipment once, there are almost no further problems. In addition, since Ulmart has an SAP software infrastructure, an additional advantage is the expertise of the company. Fujitsu works with SAP directly - one of the first SAP installations occurred on Fujitsu servers.
