Live Earth Live Broadcasting will be available online.

On July 7, the MSN portal, including its Russian version, will start the live broadcast of Live Earth concerts held in the USA, UK, Australia, Japan, China, South Africa, Brazil, Germany, and Turkey. Madonna, Beastie Boys, Black Eyed Peas, Red Hot Chili Peppers, The Smashing Pumpkins, Duran Duran, Foo Fighters, Genesis, Metallica, Pussycat Dolls, Kelly Clarkson, The Police, Alicia Keys, Bon Jovi, Snoop Dogg, Enrique Iglesias, Shakira, Linkin Park, Lenny Kravitz and many other artists.
