New look at the comments. Hypercomments.com - commenting on text fragments

about the project


What is a standard blog or post? Comments are usually detached from the text and users have to quote text snippets to comment on them. And if the dispute between readers is tied around a certain statement in an article, as is often the case in Habr√©, then a reader who has just entered the post finds it difficult to grasp this interesting part of the article. How can you make comments more contextual, interactive, eye-catching? I want to share with Habravchanami project that develops one of our teams. It will be useful for bloggers, online media, and just sites with great text content. Project Hypercomments offers a new look at the comments. It is possible to comment both fragments of the text, and separate words, pictures This allows the author to revive the article, to see the most discussed, hot parts of the text, to make the content of the article more saturated by the activity of readers.
