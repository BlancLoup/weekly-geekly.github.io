But are social networks unprofitable? My humble income count

I do not pretend to any truth, just want to share my thoughts.

If you hope that it will begin to generate income, then another question arises - when? Enough time has passed to start generating this income. And if you hope to sell, so who needs a loss-making network that does not generate income with the current monetization scheme? But this is all a saying.
