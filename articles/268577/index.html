Today we will talk about robotics, or rather about the event, whose name is Robonoch!
Recently, Skolkovo approved the date for holding the Robonoch this year, this is October 13, i.e. in less than a week. I did not find any interesting articles about this and decided to write it myself!
I will try to tell you quite briefly and without much water, what was there and what plans the organizers have for this year.



For me, this is a new format of educational and entertainment events for Russia. There is something in common with Comic Con, robotics fans are gathering in one form or another, people in science fiction costumes, in general, the atmosphere is cool.




To begin with, it is worth noting the venue, and this is the Central Telegraph, I have never been there, I think, like many, so it‚Äôs interesting to look at it from the inside.

But the main thing is, of course, various art installations, stands of the partners of the event, and among them are companies such as Autodesk, Microsoft and National Instruments.

National Instruments brought robo-chess - this complex allows not virtually, but physically playing chess with a robot, as the company representatives said, under the figures there are reed magnets, the drive controls the movement of the figures, the robot analyzes the move and moves the figures. Fun stuff really.
