Created a browser for anonymous surfing

The official release of the free Browzar web browser took place , which itself deletes cookies, temporary files cache, cleans the surfing history and tidies up other traces of its owner.

Most browsers, such as Internet Explorer and Mozilla, do not consider the anonymity of surfing to be the main and absolute priority. On the contrary, they help to identify the user through cookies and save the history of surfing. Naturally, this is done solely for the convenience of users, because most people have nothing to hide (at least they are sure of it), so users very rarely think about their anonymity.
