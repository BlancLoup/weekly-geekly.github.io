Want to know how to get to FRIA?



The Internet Initiatives Development Fund is a state venture fund established on the initiative of V.V. Putin, which poured 6 billion rubles for 3 years. This fall was the first selection of projects that will undergo acceleration within the framework of IIDF for 3 months and upon leaving the accelerator can qualify for investments of up to $ 450,000.

From more than 900 applications for acceleration selected 100 projects. Many startups who have not been selected are probably wondering: what should be done to get into the IIDF accelerator, what should the application look like?

Our project entered the TOP-100, and we decided on an extremely brave step - to share with Habr our application, its full text on the link . Perhaps the text of our successful bid will help other startups to understand how to write, and what the Foundation expected from them.
