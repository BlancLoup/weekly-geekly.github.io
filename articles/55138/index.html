Technology of creating stop motion video from photos

Introduction


Not so long ago, I mounted an amateur video clip based on photos taken in photo session mode. Now I learned that the video was made in line with the modern trend - stop motion video. Therefore, talk about how it was done.

Video clip




Technology creation

  1. I uploaded all photos in Picasa on my local computer (over 1000 photos).
  2. In Picasa scattered albums homogeneous photos. Uniform - these are photos that were taken from a single angle. Changes in the model‚Äôs posture are minor. In one album there were from 4 to 10 photos.
  3. Launched the HyperCam program. This program saves everything that happens on the monitor screen to a video file (you can use any such program). At the same time launched the musical composition, which is chosen for the clip. Immediately after that, I chose one of the albums in Picasa, and I scrolled to the music with my mouse (back and forth and back and forth). Together with the scrolling photos change - it turns out like a movie.
  4. Then SonyVegas has compiled the received video clips into one video clip.
In total, Picasa made about 25 albums with homogeneous photos.
Respectively received from 25 to 30 video clips.
The clip uses from 150 to 200 photos.
