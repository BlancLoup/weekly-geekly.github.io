Imagine that you have 1 minute while the CEO of our company is waiting for your drink at the bar. You are not familiar, but you saw him and you need to not miss the chance. Try to interest him so that he will offer you to join his company at the table.

This question was once asked me, and, to be honest, he confused me. This is quite a provocative question and I asked him, as a rule, senior to candidates for managerial positions.
