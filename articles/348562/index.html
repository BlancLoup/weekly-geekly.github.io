Case with milk


Using a third-party solution, the company's management has the ability to automate the warehouse. Namely, monitor the supply, the costs of ingredients and their write-off.

Case from the work of our experimental cafe - for 6 months we observe a situation with milk overuse. At the same time, a trigger is set in the settings of the automated warehouse - to receive an SMS / email message if milk remains in the warehouse are less than 5 liters. The problem we are seeing is that the actual milk residues are always less than the residues that are in stock. The management learns that the milk ends up from a barista, and not from a system based on configured triggers.



At first glance, it may seem that the voiced problem is related to the problem of informing the management (or the institution manager). And of course, the problem of informing is solved quite simply. But this case is about something else. Case about milk overuse.

Here are just a few reasons why overruns are possible:

1. Barista spilled and hid this fact.
2. Barista uses more milk than is prescribed in the flow chart.
3. Milk consumption according to the technological card does not coincide with the consumption prescribed in the POS system.
4. Personnel steal milk or use for personal purposes (for example, drinking tea with milk at work).

Is this trifle so important? Well, lost 5-10 liters of milk per month - in cash equivalent to 500 rubles per month. In a year - 6000 rubles per institution. 60000 rubles for a network of 100 institutions. And this is just milk. And, of course, in the framework of one institution you can close your eyes to this fact. As a last resort, write off all the problems on the barista and subtract from the sn.

The whole catering is built exactly on the little things. . And the profitability of the catering business is very dependent on how to work with these trifles. Our task, as people who have come to this area from different areas, with different knowledge, is to do so in order to reduce the error in the influence of trifles on business profits.

As an IT person, I see one of the possible solutions to this problem. It is necessary to analyze the sale of drinks with milk, enter a daily inventory of residues of the problem ingredient.

So what should I write in SIMT?

Based on the data of my analysis (for a month) of the sale of beverages containing milk, I suggest introducing a daily inventory of milk. This will reveal the fact of the influence of human factors on the overrun or inappropriate consumption of milk. Daily inventory will provide an opportunity to conduct additional analysis.

How difficult is it to implement this proposal? In one particular institution, this can be implemented the very next day. And in a few days, the author of the idea will already have reliable data for analysis, recorded in the blockchain. These data will help to identify the problem, or suggest a thought about the next experiment.

The solution to a problem, found locally in an experimental institution, can be easily transmitted to the establishments of the network.
