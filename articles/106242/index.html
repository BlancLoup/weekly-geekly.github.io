For some, such a situation can happen during outdoor activities in nature, and for someone - during no less active work, forcing system administrators to descend into underground utilities, and design engineers to stand in the pouring rain on the roof of a high-rise building.

At such times, you often realize that in your work, reasonable minimalism and a high degree of reliability are the most important characteristics of devices.
