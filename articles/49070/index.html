Robot Strenco ST-2 - a real steampunk



The German robot Strenco ST-2 is not very similar to the representative of the steampunk direction. Not a brilliant brass parts, not a dozen dials ... Nevertheless - this is a real steampunk. The toy was invented by the Germans, and released in the amount of only 300 pieces. Inside the robot is a miniature steam engine. The scheme of work is simple: push the pallet out of the left side, put a dry fuel pill into it, ignite it and send it to the firebox, while a flame is seen through the holes in the chest. The fire heats the boiler, steam enters the chassis and, voila - the robot has gone. The height of the android is 35 cm, width is 20, weight is 1 kg. The toy is sold here for 399 euros.
