1024-bit RSA cipher hacked in five years

Within five to ten years, the 1024-bit RSA cipher will be cracked. With such a forecast made Arjen Lenstra (Arjen Lenstra), a well-known cryptologist. More recently, the equivalent of a 700-bit RSA key has been cracked under his leadership. Arjen Lenstra believes that in the field of distributed computing in the coming years we can expect significant progress. First, the processors become more powerful. Secondly, mathematical algorithms for finding primes are improving.

Arjen Lenstra told about a successful experiment in which two factors of a 307-digit number were found.
