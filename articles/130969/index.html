Housing and IT - myth or reality?

Hello, dear Habrazhiteli.


There is a great desire to bring to the community our prototype of the future service on housing and utilities (not cut, but sorry), and also if possible to find supporters and / or investors.

I will not describe in great detail here, if I am interested, I will tell everything as it is in a separate correspondence.

It all started with the fact that I was introduced to the head of our city. He asked him to develop a small portal for daily consumption of energy resources for the needs of the administration (UEC - a single settlement energy center). Data had to be entered by each organization in manual mode. In DES, there are servers for which these data are collected automatically, and accordingly, these data were planned to be further bolted to the portal being developed.
For a bottle of beer with a friend, we decided to take on this project. Work began on April 15, 2011 (yes - yes, we are half a year).
Our administration is poor, did not want to pay money (and does not want to), it was planned to collect a subscription fee from organizations that will keep records in the EHEC, and also in the future for automating the process of water data in this very journal. The plans did not come true (for many reasons).
At the moment, 170 organizations, more than 100 users, are registered with the UREC, to date, more than 53,000 readings from general metering devices have been kept.
A system of reports with an arbitrary choice of periods has been developed, a comparative report allows you to select two periods and compare data on them.
