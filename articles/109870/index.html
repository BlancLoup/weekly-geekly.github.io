How to open a department of an online store in a mall or why we are now standing next to the department of PocketBook

In the past, I talked about how to choose a place for an online store in real life. There were basic theoretical calculations in it, after which many requested continuations with specific numbers. In this topic - specific tips on opening a shopping center on the example of one of our points.

The topic will be useful to those who:
  • Has its own online store and wants a real one;
  • He does not know how to open a point in the shopping center;
  • Chooses between pickup point and different store options.

Squeeze:
  • A shopping center is good for the audience, but is bad for its harsh conditions and high starting costs;
  • In the shopping center is difficult, but really stand up;
  • How to present;
  • Staff hired before opening;
  • What are the bases;
  • How we made the discovery and how it should have been done.


Why open at a mall?
  • The shopping center provides excellent attendance;
  • A shopping center is an advertisement, because a significant part of the population will very quickly find out about you;
  • TC is a convenient pickup point, because it is usually conveniently located.

Short checklist:


  • Find a place by the correct calculation
  • Send a presentation and initiate negotiations to convince the mall that you are suitable.
  • Sign the contract and pay
  • Make a place and catch up on the guidelines of the shopping center
  • Find Sellers
  • Make an opening advertisement
  • Bring the product and post it
  • Expand infrastructure (we have an Internet, 1C-terminal, etc.)
  • Change advertisements ‚Äúopening soon‚Äù to normal
  • Make fun on the opening day (for example, promoters with leaflets or advertising outside the shopping center)
  • Test display in combat conditions and revalue


Looking for a place


When a place is made available in a shopping center, then something like a tender with the participation of several companies takes place. The fact is that the place in the shopping center is usually ‚Äútasty‚Äù, so there are several companies (if this is not the case, then most likely you don‚Äôt need a place). The shopping center receives the same amount from each company, so the task is to show the intangible benefits in the formation of your particular department.

How to explain the shopping center that your point is happiness


Send a presentation and try to get into the negotiations. The following should be clearly seen in your materials:
  • Prove the seriousness of the company, that is, the stability of payments TC . We list our stores, call the turnover point and show the geography. Sometimes we put a short success story. Startup is logical to call the investor, if known.
  • Show that you have the right assortment . If you sell tires in a shopping center, where the audience is mostly women and children, then you will be less happy than the lingerie department. In our case, board games are a great gift that fits well with the entertainment concept of Dreamtown.
  • What you bring the right atmosphere, that is, create and maintain interest for customers of the shopping center . Assortment + general sales style with high service gives such a guarantee. It must be emphasized that the customer comes out of your store a little happier than he was before him.
  • What do you meet the requirements . As formals, such as clean and good-smelling sellers are usually put forward, adequate unobtrusive service, compliance with a bunch of design parameters, working hours, lighting, availability and design of price tags, etc. Often, three sellers in the heap, the seller with the included cellular or ice cream - this is also a joint. If interested, I can send in a personal example of strict requirements.


Then there is a personal conversation with the person responsible for your ‚Äúsettling‚Äù. As practice shows, abstruse do not like: try to behave openly and positively, this is half the success. If you arrange each other, then you will be given a contract (fortunately, he is always on hand at the shopping center). Important items such as deposit, cost, order of payment and other payments are fixed for all.

After that, you make out a department, put an ad on it ‚Äúsoon opening‚Äù. By the way, when drawing up a work plan, you need to clearly understand that deadlines are always broken for 1-2 days. After you have to make some noise at the opening, test the display again and start everything up.

The staff is better to start recruiting before opening, but not too early. We try to have one or two sellers (even from other points) at the time of launch, which will quickly be trained by new ones. The newcomers will be interviewed and called for a field test on the very first day, where they work under the auspices of older comrades.

Another frequent ‚Äúrake‚Äù is the additional conditions that are inconsistent with the management from the employees of the shopping center itself. For example, you may want money for wiring, finishing or something else. In this case, you need to go to the leadership and explain that you have already agreed on everything, but here and here conditions have arisen that prevent us from doing business: let's solve this common problem. The question of interest for 90 is removed literally in minutes, and the remaining 10% is actually the right expenses, which are more profitable to pay than delay the opening.

Concrete


So, at the beginning of November in Kiev, we had a pick-up point, located two blocks from the metro station Kontraktova Square.



With the growth of our interest in the region and the understanding that the market is already ready for production, it was decided to open a store in a place with a lot of natural traffic. To begin with, we took measurements from our closest competitors (retail chains with board games in stock, departments similar to Detsky mir in shopping centers, etc.), which confirmed the conclusions.

Base for calculations


Data on the average check, the average number of games in the check, the average conversion of a passerby / client we had both in Kiev and in other regions, that is, the base for calculations was good. A week before the first departure, the head of the Kiev office began to select places for us. He walked around the areas where there is a high demand for games with delivery and in the city in general, and then picked up ads on the network.

After the selection of options as described in the first material , there are three left (one hryvnia is about 4 rubles) :
  • Two blocks from the metro in a residential area of ‚Äã‚Äã25 square meters, advertising was planned for the next house (the beginning of the last century). Clearance is low. There are no large stores and other objects nearby. 16 thousand hryvnia per month (60 thousand rubles). It was found after the bypass area "feet".
  • Excellent street in the center with a cafe, a university and a bunch of other objects, with high natural traffic, but 10 square meters (the former kiosk with flowers). 14 thousand hryvnia per month (53 thousand rubles).
  • Another option for 20 meters, near the subway, but in the yard. Low traffic. 12 thousand hryvnia per month (45 thousand rubles).


The third option did not suit us (we already had something similar), the second was good for everyone, except for a small area, the first did not fit the calculations. We called the owner of the second, but he did not want to meet at all: it meant an instant rejection of the option.

At this time we managed to find options in the malls. A trip to the first showed that the attendance was not the real, and the departure of the second left only positive impressions. So we decided to open up in Dreamtown. This is a new shopping center a stone's throw from the metro, which potentially has the potential to become one of the best in Kiev. The only thing that strained me personally was the huge ass of the dinosaur, which greets guests at the middle entrance.

"Dreamtown" is very good in its atmosphere: it is beautiful, it is pleasant to walk on it, there are many different wonderful places to see. If we chose the place ourselves, we would stand on the second floor (where is the children's store now). Unfortunately, the place was too big for us and already occupied. We got up on the top floor (usually it‚Äôs definitely not worth doing this), but near the restaurant block, which gives us a high cross. In addition, when taking measurements, we took into account that the customer on the street and the customer in the shopping center are not the same thing: in the shopping center, the conversion is still much higher.

"Dreamtown" is actively unwound, designed for entertainment (that is, for frequent returns of the public core) is located near the metro and allows you to beautifully present our products. In general, this is not only and not so much a point of direct sales, but also an ideal pick-up point for an online store.
