Tagline ranking by real turnover


Hi, Habr!

The site 2008.tagline.ru has added an additional rating of companies by real turnover in the development of sites for 2007.

The publication of the rating is an important step to increase the transparency of the web development market, as well as an additional tool for market analysis. The desire to open their financial indicators shows the willingness of companies to work openly on the still non-transparent market.

Tagline Group has prepared the first in Runet ranking for real turnover in the field of web development. In total, more than 60 questionnaires were submitted, of which 42 studios passed the bar for a turnover of 500 thousand rubles in 2007.

The last stage of the comprehensive market research of website development in RuNet is the publication of an analytical report on the state of the market and development trends on June 26, 2008.

* Tagline Group is not responsible for the accuracy of data provided by players.
