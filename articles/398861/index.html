Xiaomi Mi VR - an unexpected guest on this holiday


Yes, not only the frameless smartphone was able to surprise Xiaomi on October 25th. Although many thought that the Chinese company would decide to stop at its first experience in the field of virtual reality, on the contrary, it went further, making VR both stylish and affordable.

Imagine that someone would think of connecting Oculus Rift and Google DayDream View, and selling the resulting device for $ 29. No, one cannot say that Mi VR is an exact copy of the first or second one: this gadget looks stylish and unique and wears the famous Mi logo for a good reason.



The virtual reality helmet from Xiaomi is equipped with a motion sensor and a touch controller. Control is also carried out using two physical buttons. Mi VR not only tracks the position of the user's head, but also recognizes the movements and turns of his hands, and in all directions. The gadget is presented only in white color, which, perhaps, makes it even more futuristic.

Xiaomi's previous attempt to enter the virtual reality device market was much simpler. Mi VR Play, although it looked stylish, and made of pleasant materials, in fact was a tidier version of Google CardBoard, so they asked for more. But the helmet, if you can call it that, was universal and supported smartphones with a screen diagonal from 4.7 inches to 5.7 inches.



Now we have a complete mince: a helmet, like a robocop (well, almost), software optimizations to prevent unpleasant sensations in the process of using the device (nausea, dizziness), its own section in the app store, where more than 500 panoramic videos and 30 apps are already available. The number of programs will grow as 200 registered developers create them, the company said. Xiaomi claims that Mi VR can be used even for a long time without experiencing any discomfort. Well, this has yet to be verified by users.



Mi VR has advantages even in comparison with the Gear VR VR from Samsung. For example, the delay when watching a video using the Xiaomi gadget is only 16 milliseconds, while the Gear VR has a figure of 20 milliseconds. The viewing angle of the Gear VR 2016 (updated version) is 101 degrees, while the first copy had 96 degrees. Mi VR was able to albeit a little, but bypass the competitor - with a viewing angle of 103 degrees. And this is despite the fact that Gear VR is twice as expensive.

Conveniently, Xiaomi has not forgotten about people with poor eyesight - Mi VR has the ability to adjust the focus for users suffering from myopia or farsightedness. Most of the analogs in the same price segment of this function are deprived, which discourages the lion‚Äôs share of customers.

Only the fact that the new product is compatible with only some of the flagship Xiaomi - Mi5 , Mi 5s and Mi 5s Plus, presented at the end of September this year, as well as Mi Note 2 , with which Mi VR was announced, adds a spoonful of tar in the barrel of honey. Yes, frameless Mi Mix put in this helmet does not work, but sorry.



By the way, since October 26, GearBest has opened a pre-order for the new Mi VR virtual reality helmet from Xiaomi.

It's amazing how quickly Xiaomi managed to turn from a catch-up to a manufacturer who is not shy about setting trends to more serious competitors who have been present on the market for more than a dozen years.
