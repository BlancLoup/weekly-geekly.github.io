Partisans

A piece of the boring online interview with Mail.Ru CEO Dmitry Grishin on the Expert website:

26. What is the atmosphere inside your company? Do you have rules and corporate standards?
- Yes, we have corporate rules, but I would rank our company as one of the most democratic and unregulated . From the rules: our employees must be active users of our products, and usually, before launching a project, it is run in on our employees.

It is good that the Mail is a democratic company inside, but outside it is very closed. How many I remember myself during the days of the head office on Webplanet, Grishin never spoke personally on the phone, always turned the arrows on Artamonov. You could call Yandex, and immediately talk to Volozh, in Rambler, you could also talk to management, and in Mail ... No, I really think Mail is the most closed company. No one knows what the name of Mail.Ru shareholders is, how much their turnover, income, etc. They say nothing, such democratic partisans.
