Mixcloud. Five good news

Four months ago, I wrote about Mixcloud and since then they have some good news.

Download unlimited mixes

In the comments in the previous post, line_out complained about the 100 MB limit on loading. I contacted Richard Morgan from the Mixcloud team and he promised that they would soon get rid of this limit. And so it happened! Now no restrictions!


New player

There is no more flash player in the pop-up. Now there is a super player on HTML and on a separate page. Also appeared a queue and recommendations relevant to the current mix.

Groups

Now you can join groups in directions, interests and even programs for creating music. Basically, in groups, they will promote and discuss mixes.

2 years

Yes, only two years have passed, and Mixcloud is already so big!
The event was marked online and in a London club.
Holiday mixes and special T-shirts were also released.

Awards

Mixcloud was awarded two awards in three categories.

TechCrunch Europas
Best Music Startup

BT Digital Music Awards
The best place to study music
The best place to listen to music

I would like to wish the service to continue developing so rapidly.
