Lombards appeared in Entropia Universe

In the virtual world of Entropia Universe , virtual pawnshops appeared. In them, players will be able to take loans secured by various game artifacts. There are five pawnshops altogether - that is the number of licenses issued by MindArk (by the way, this brought about $ 400 thousand).

One of the licenses went to the Russian processing company Moneta.ru, the others to two users (one of them is Anshe Chun ), a German Internet bank and a private investor who wished to remain anonymous.
