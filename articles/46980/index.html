How to build an interesting startup?

What it is?


A new, but already very popular word ‚Äústartup‚Äù appeared just a few years ago and is most often used in relation to new and / or promising web projects. It is rather difficult to assess the prospects of the project, as well as the commercial or social attractiveness of the concept. The term of launching a web resource is much easier to learn.

Is every new resource worth calling a startup? I think that this title should be distributed very carefully: for an interesting idea, original implementation, compliance with the requirements of the market and the audience, as well as some other parameters that will allow the web project to exist for at least 2-3 years. Moreover, the existence of a startup should be dynamic - the audience grows with time, information is updated, services run smoothly. A good indicator of success is the appearance of clones, imitators of the startup idea.

For example, Google exactly 10 years ago was a startup whose founders had an original idea, experience of successful implementation and getting into a wave of market demand for new search engines. Now Google is one of the largest players on the Internet, creating its own start-ups and buying successful ideas for business development. The successful example of Google and other projects became the basis for the massive launch of tens of thousands of web resources around the world that tried to repeat their success ... but most of these startups have sunk into oblivion.

The presence or absence of startups reflects the level of development of the Internet industry in a particular country, region or segment of the Internet. Behind each newly created startup, there is at least one person or team who has the necessary skills, ideas and faith in success. The more talents, the more ideas, respectively, the number of "live" projects, useful to people, attracting investment, the audience and opening up great opportunities.

Are there any startups on the Kazakhstani Internet? Are there any interesting ideas, developers and audience in Kazakhstan that would like to use Kazakhstan services? The answer to all questions is positive, the only problem is one - the lack of investors and developers of a vision about the opportunities and development potential of KazNet.

With a group of international startups, everything is much more complicated, because the geographical breadth of the audience implies a different level of management organization, other means of brand promotion and popularization of services, as well as constant competition with the world's leading developers and popular international projects with a good history and multimillion-dollar audiences.

Let's try to formulate some principles, in the presence of which a startup can claim success on an international scale:
1. Analysis and monitoring of the target market - you need to know the direct competitors, to understand their strengths and weaknesses.
2. Idea or competitive advantages - the resource being developed should attract an audience of competitors or new visitors with greater convenience, speed, adaptability or a new solution.
3. Timing - it is necessary to clearly understand the relevance of the capabilities or ideas of a startup by Internet users.
4. Technological and human resources - a startup should have a long-term technical base, experienced developers and management to implement tactical and strategic steps to develop the project.
5. Possibilities of advertising and attracting an audience - financial resources and / or channels of communication with a potential audience are needed to draw attention to a startup. In the first case - investors, in the second - partner or own popular resources with a target audience.

If all the listed requirements coincide, we can say that a startup can get a chance for life, but does not guarantee its long-term or mandatory popularity. Each of the principles should be maintained and changed in accordance with the requirements of the situation and the market - new technologies, additional resources, staff turnover, planning deficiencies and much more should be corrected and changed as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Online and offline ...


Continuing the example of analyzing the success of a startup in the conditions of Kazakhstan and the claim for international recognition, it is necessary to dwell on the additional, often ignored potential of off-line capabilities.

It is not a secret that most startups in the world are completely focused on Internet and Internet users, all operations, communication and service occur online, rarely which web resources have advertisements in non-specialized off-line printed publications, outdoor advertising and printed products. Only large and successful projects begin to carry out traditional promotional activities, having at that time the necessary budget for their implementation. So what do startups do at the launch stage, not having extra funds for off-line promotion, but understanding the need to attract an additional audience by traditional means?

There are a lot of options, as always, and the limitation is only in the imagination of the startup management. Here are some of them:
1. Organization of partnerships with off-line companies offering online opportunities on a startup.
2. Barter relationship.
3. Friendly relations or support targeted offline events as an informational sponsor.

Practically for any high-quality startup, you can find interested off-line partners without additional financial costs.
