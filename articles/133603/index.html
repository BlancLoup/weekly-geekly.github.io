Announced the starting lineup of games for PS Vita in Europe

Sony has called the starting lineup of games for the PS Vita console in Europe. The list includes 12 projects developed by the company's internal studios. The European release of the new pocket console will take place on February 22, 2012. Uncharted: Golden Abyss (exclusive Uncharted prequel, events unfolding before the first part of the game - Uncharted: Drake's Fortune), WipEout 2048 (racing on anti-gravity speeders), Reality Fighters (fighting game, whose actions, thanks to the possibility of scanning real locations, can turn right in your home), Little Deviants (many killer mini-games in one game), ModNation Racers: Road Trip (another mega-race with its own track editor), Everybody's Golf, Escape Plan (puzzle platformer) , Gravity Rush (adventure action with quest elements), Hus tle Kings (billiards), MotorStorm RC (race on radio-controlled cars), Top Darts and Super StarDust Delta (arcade space simulator).




All console owners will be able to download for free from PlayStation Store three games created using the technology of "augmented reality", more about which can be found in the demo video



In addition, Sony has published a list of games for Vita, created by third-party developers. It includes Army Corps of Hell from Square Enix, FIFA Football from EA, F1 2011 from Codemasters, Rayman Origins and Assassin's Creed from Ubisoft; Silent Hill: Konami's Ultimate Marvel vs. Book of Memories Capcom from Capcom, Mortal Kombat from WB Games, Bioshock from Take Two, Call of Duty from Activision Blizzard and other projects.

The new pocket console from Sony, equipped with a five-inch touchscreen, rear touch panel, gyroscope and two joysticks, will be available on the market in two versions: with 3G and Wi-Fi and only with Wi-Fi. The prefix in the basic configuration will cost European gamers 249.99 euros, and a PS Vita with 3G will cost 50 euros more. If, before February 2012, there are no noticeable fluctuations in the euro, Russian gamers will have a new pocket console at 11 and 13 thousand rubles, respectively (we would recommend the 3G version: the difference from 50 euros is absolutely not important, but the numerous bonuses are obvious).
