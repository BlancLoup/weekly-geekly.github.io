Radio VS Player

Now I want to compare listening to music on the player and radio. By player we mean both a portable device and a player in a PC, laptop, PDA, etc. Radio - both FM and online radio stations. The player has a number of advantages: there is no advertising, it has a considerable amount of songs, plays only what they like. Why do people keep listening to the radio? Why so happy when your favorite song sounds on the radio? The secret is that any pleasure that is not the result of your actions is regarded as luck. And any luck is nice. There are also informative and entertaining programs on the radio, but this is more of a bonus than no point.

FM VS Online.

Well here it seems clear. FM care is just a matter of time and technology. Therefore, Internet radio will dominate your ears. And most likely the future is in socially-adaptive services like Last.fm. I am sure that many users with a wide channel prefer it to him, since this is a radio with its charms, and music to your taste.
