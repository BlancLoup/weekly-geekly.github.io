Flash GAMM! - meeting of flash game developers

Hello, dear flash Habra community :)
I do not know how relevant this news will be for you, but still dare to post it here.

It is no secret that today flash-games are a large industry that has not yet gained its own space for meetings, discussions and exchange of experience. Conferences dedicated to casual games do not satisfy the need for communication of flash-developers who are present at them. This information vacuum must be promptly filled with high-quality content. Therefore, the idea of Flash GAMM was born !



Flash GAMM! or Flash Game Meeting is a meeting of game developers based on the Flash platform, which will be held in Kiev on October 25-26 this year. This is not a conservative conference, but a meeting that has a more ‚Äúrelaxed‚Äù format.

This meeting is being done from scratch with the full enthusiasm of the organizers. But we have already found several sponsors, which will allow us to make the meeting more intense. We plan to take 100-150 people. Already registered more than 80 participants from 4 countries.

The meeting will feature a series of reports on various aspects of the development of flash games - from game design, art, architecture and programming to the promotion and monetization of game projects. Already, you can explore the topics of available reports , but we want to inform you that this is not all, several topics are in the process of registration. And the pros of the flash-igrostroy will be discussed by professionals in their field, including Ada Chen from Mochi Media and Anton Volkov from Alternativa Platform

It can be noted that the meeting will take place once after Casual Connect.
Participation in the meeting is absolutely free!
And for those who can not come will be held online broadcast meeting via Adobe Connect. As well as video recording and presentations will be posted on the site after the meeting.

For more information about the meeting, reports, participants and venue you can find on the official meeting site http://flashgamm.com

Respectfully,
organizers of Flash GAMM!
