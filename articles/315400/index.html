Constantly invest in your education

For almost the next two years I did not see any signs of desired personal, spiritual, intellectual or financial growth. When I studied, my earnings grew, I had more time, and my successes were more noticeable. Refusing education, I got the opposite result.

The fact is that education and knowledge are transient. They are erased not only because of non-use. If you do not raise your level, then just start to forget. Once acquired knowledge can not be stored in my head forever.

I perceive education as food, water, air, physical education - that means continuous replenishment of stocks.

Our world is getting harder. And any mistakes can lead to major consequences.

Being engaged in self-education, you can think about those things that have never even crossed your mind. And get under the influence of those ideas that have not been heard before .

By combining all this, you get the concepts and opportunities that you have enough for a lifetime.
