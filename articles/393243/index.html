Conspiracy therapists rejoice: after the appearance of a UFO, the NASA broadcast from the ISS was cut off



An online broadcast from the International Space Station was cut short soon after a strange horseshoe-shaped object appeared in the frame . Lovers of conspiracy theories have accused NASA of hiding information about aliens.


"UFO" in the form of a horseshoe next to the ISS

Conspiracy theorists claim that NASA interrupted the broadcast when the object was too close to the ISS. Not surprisingly, this phenomenon was seen by one of the UFO lovers, Scott Waring, who watched the live broadcast.

Scott runs the UFO Sightings Daily website, which publishes various "discoveries" of persons on the Moon and mysterious figures on Mars, found in the broadcast of the Mars rovers. A video of the phenomenon of an incomprehensible horseshoe, he gave a colleague, Tyler Glockner [Tyler Glockner], leading youtube-channel SecureTeam.

Tyler claims that people from NASA monitor the video and turn it off when things appear in the frame that should not be seen by the general public. According to him, the last time the broadcast was interrupted after the appearance of a massive yellow disc in the frame in 2014.

A week ago, the SecureTeam channel, also filled with videos dedicated to aliens, published a similar video, where a broadcast-like object appeared in the frame from the ISS.
