3. How was the site created in a technical sense? What difficulties did you encounter, were there any non-standard moves?

One of the main reasons for creating the site was the need to discuss the parables directly next to the parable itself (on my website the parables were in the same section, and their discussion took place on the forum). I have long dreamed of making a forum in a hierarchical form (I somehow saw one like this at the ‚ÄúOrthodox Conversation‚Äù - I liked it).
