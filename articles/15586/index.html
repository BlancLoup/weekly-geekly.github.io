DDoS attack is conducted on Bash.org.ru.

An official announcement about this was given in the official community bash.org.ru on the pages of LiveJournal:

Dear visitors bash.org.ru.

We regret to inform you that from 3:45 am on October 29 to the present moment the server is under a serious distributed attack.
We carry out all necessary actions to neutralize this attack and prevent its recurrence in the future. With this help us .masterhost employees.
There is no loss of data, but the entire channel is clogged with garbage traffic - success in combating it is variable.

At least until tomorrow the server will be unavailable. Thank you for understanding.

Sincerely, the bash.org.ru team.

Please spread this information! Permanent link to the post: community.livejournal.com/russian_bashorg/178511.html
In addition, we will be grateful for reliable information about customers and / or the organizers of the attack.


Before the official confirmation of the DDoS attack by resource administrators, a fake spread on the Internet :
