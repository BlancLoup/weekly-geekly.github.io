Technological gifts for girls



December is in full swing, and time to buy gifts is less and less. Do not have time to look back, as the last week of December comes, and the pre-New Year rush casts panic. It's time to think about what you would like to give to your second half or mothers, sisters, girlfriends. We have collected for you an overview of interesting beauty and not only gadgets that will help you make an unforgettable gift.

Useful gadgets


The best gift is a useful gift, and the fitness tracker is quite consistent with this. Usually they are available in a unisex style, without unnecessary problems. For example, the second Mi Band can be a great gift for both women and men. A neutral gamut of four colors is perfect for both genders. A simple and necessary set of characteristics: sleep monitoring, alarm clock, pedometer, calorie counting. You can set up an alert for calls, SMS and alerts from applications. What is important is compatible with Android and iOS and holds a charge of up to 20 days.



If you decide to shift the focus from universality to femininity - get ready for a long search. Very few companies make exclusively female trackers. But if you want something more elegant, you can pay attention to the Samsung Smart Charm bracelet, presented in three colors: gold, black and pink. No charge will last about two weeks. Unlike Mi Band 2, it does not have a screen, but you can set up different color alerts for incoming calls, SMS, alerts from social networks, battery level and more. The design is beautiful, but the tracker is not without significant drawbacks: it is compatible only with Android, there is no vibration.



Externally, this fitness tracker is very similar to Xiaomi AmazFit . Elegant and elegant, without display, but protected from dust and moisture. Sleep monitoring, activity, calorie counting, call alerts and SMS, wireless charging. It works without recharging for about ten days, compatible with Android and iOS. The ceramic body is not only very beautiful, it is also durable. The bracelet is not afraid of blows and scratches, which can not be said about other fitness trackers with screens or metal cases. And the most interesting: the tracker can be worn not only as a bracelet, but also as a pendant. For the white and black round case, straps of various colors and chains are selected.



Posture Corrector Lumo Lift


Direct posture is a guarantee of health and beauty. A very simple gadget magnet is easily attached to a blouse or jacket and vibrates when you start to slouch. Lumo BodyTech has seriously addressed the problem of spinal curvature. Their first product, a belt for straightening their posture, received a lot of positive feedback and raised the right amount of money for Kickstarter to launch.

Externally, the gadget is very minimalistic: there is a button on the front, a translucent diode, rear contacts for charging and mounting on clothes. Three body colors: white, black and gold. The device is attached to the clothes from the inside, so from the outside you can see only a magnet. This magnet has many different colors. The application displays the time, how many hours a day you slouch, how much keep your back straight.



OMBRA Smart Bra


A very smart and technological solution may seem like an OMBra smart bra for sports. It adapts to each workout and to a particular body, and the special design and material of the bra reduces the load on the back and shoulders. OMBra sensors record heart rate, heart rate and respiratory rate data. The charge lasts for a whole day of continuous use. The collected biometric information is automatically sent to the connected iOS device, where, based on it, personal statistics and calories are calculated. This allows bra owners to plan workouts with high precision in the future.



Teeth whitening system GLO Brilliant Personal Teeth Whitening Device


Teeth whitening at home is a popular phenomenon, so you can find a lot of gadgets and tools on the market that delicately fulfill this function. A very interesting novelty of the year - the device from GLO. It works by simultaneously heating and ultraviolet radiation. Comes with a special whitening gel.



Philips Sonicare Electric Sonic Toothbrushes


It has long been known that electric brushes are much more useful than ordinary ones. Philips Sonicare toothbrushes remove up to 90% of the darkening of the enamel surface and gently whiten your teeth. In two minutes, an electric brush makes as many motions as a normal one - in half an hour. The InterCare nozzle easily removes plaque from interdental spaces and hard-to-reach places. In addition, the brush itself controls brushing. It turns off at the end of "work", informs by vibration about the need for charging or "hints" that it is time to move on to cleaning another area.



Masseur for eyes Iris Illuminating Eye Massager from FOREO


FOREO company produces a lot of different beauty gadgets. They can find a device for cleansing the skin Luna and unusual toothbrushes Issa. It is very good in itself, but if you use it after applying facial care products, the gadget will enhance their effect.



NuFace Trinity Facial Toning Device Face Sculpting Device

Face Sculpting Device Jart + Liftra Contour Shaper


If the previous gadget seemed expensive, there is a cheap equivalent: a metal device Liftra Contour Shaper. He does a face massage and outlines his contour. Great for daily use and very quickly gives the result: the skin becomes taut, healthy, and cheekbones - more expressive.



Clarisonic Smart Profile face care device


Facial care devices simplify daily skin care and increase its effectiveness. They clean the skin well. Several modes allow you to care for the skin of various types. A nozzle Smart puts the device in the intelligent mode and starts automatically adjusting the time and intensity of exposure depending on the sensitivity of the area. A variety of nozzles Clarisonic apparatus allows you to care for the skin and face, and the whole body.



MAPO sensor mask


The startup of the Paris-based company Wired Beauty has created a sensory mask that allows you to create an individual home care program no worse than a professional dermatologist. The mask measures the temperature, counts the amount of moisture in the epidermis, carefully analyzes the characteristics of the skin, makes a detailed report and displays it on the screen of the smartphone. Thus, she will be able to advise the tools that your skin needs at the moment. The mask does not only analyze the condition of the skin. When heated to forty degrees, it greatly enhances the effectiveness of the pre-applied agent, whether it be a moisturizer or a revitalizing serum.



Priscilla Sonic Brushball to cleanse the face and eliminate dark circles under the eyes


Bruises and bags under the eyes - a sore subject for many women. The skin under the eyes requires special care, and it requires special creams and devices. This gadget includes a face brush and a steel massage roller for the care of fine eyelid skin. Massage should be carried out in the morning and evening, after applying a special cream on the eyelids. The massage ball is made of stainless steel. The accessory is powered by a single AAA battery.



Color Me Automatic Foundation Applicator Foundation Gadget
Oddly enough, there are beauty gadgets for decorative cosmetics. The company Color Me has introduced a device that is ready to replace sponges and brushes for applying foundation. A tool with a nozzle in the form of a sponge pours foundation in the skin with the help of mechanical pulsation. As a result of this procedure, you can get a smooth skin tone and resistant coating.

Diorshow Heat Curl

Home photoepilator Philips Lumea BRI863

Philips Lumea BRI863 - home photoepilator that can replace cosmetic procedures. Thanks to a special screen, a smart device is able to determine the type of skin and independently adjust the settings.
