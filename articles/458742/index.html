Kirill Tolkachev and Maxim Gorelikov about Spring Boot on jug.msk.ru

Kirill Tolkachev and Maxim Gorelikov presented their report on Spring Boot at the meeting of the Moscow Java developers community jug.msk.ru , which took place on June 28, 2019, traditionally in the office of CROC : what tasks can be solved with it, what difficulties may arise and how to deal with them.



About speakers


Kirill and Maxim, until recently, worked at the Alpha Laboratory , now they represent TsIAN . Both participate in various conferences as speakers. Kirill speaks for the fourth time at jug.msk.ru. In previous times he gave reports twice with Alexander Tarasov ( once and twice ) and once with Baruch Sadogursky .

Andrei Kogun precedes the meeting with announcements of upcoming conferences ( TechTrain 2019 , Joker 2019 ) and presents speakers.



In the first part, the criteria for selecting tools for writing services were reviewed, a change was made in the perception of Spring Boot by Java developers, and an overview of the structure and content of a typical Spring Boot application was made. Mentioned about the problems with different versions of the API services and explained the reasons for the possible appearance of their own starters.
