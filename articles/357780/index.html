LG Cloud stops working in Russia

Post on LG devices since early June 2015.



At the end of 2012, LG announced the imminent launch of the cloud-based multimedia service ‚ÄúLG Cloud‚Äù in Russia. In mid-May, the service was introduced globally in 40 countries. The service focused on instant synchronization with the cloud of all multimedia devices, which allowed, for example, to view SmartTV images and videos taken on a smartphone, without the need for any additional synchronization actions.

Unfortunately, continuing the current trend, at the beginning of June, following the sudden " LG ELECTRONICS STOPPED CABLE ACTIVITY " for Russia, the LG Cloud service is also unavailable.

To the dumb question, the answers are:
- No, the service will stop working only in Russia.
- Service LG Cloud stops working due to changes in the policy of our activities.
- ALL applications (related to LG Cloud) on devices (TV, phone, tablet, PC), as well as the service site, will stop working.
- Not. your LG account will not affect this. Only the LG Cloud service will stop working.
- If you have any questions, please contact support email at lgcloud@lge.com.

Excerpt from the official site:
[LG Cloud] LG Cloud service termination notice
Dear LG Cloud users.
Let me express sincere gratitude for the support of the LG Cloud service, which you provided to us right up to today. Due to changes in the policy of our business, all LG Cloud services in Russia, available on TVs, smartphones, tablets, PCs, as well as on the service website, will cease to work from July 15, 2015 . Carefully read the information below to avoid any inconvenience caused by the termination of the LG Cloud service.

- After the termination of the service, all files stored in the LG Cloud will be deleted. Do not forget to upload your files before stopping the service in order to avoid any problems or inconveniences.

- After the termination of the service, users will not be able to use the automatic download feature provided on smartphones / tablets / PCs. Check that the feature is activated and start downloading the files saved in the folder for synchronization.
- After the termination of the service, the user's personal information collected to provide access to the service will be destroyed. However, if, in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, the information is required to be retained, the company will retain it for a certain period of time established by these laws and regulations.
- Images, video, audio and other files stored in the LG Cloud are more convenient to download via the LG Cloud website or a PC application.

More details are available on the official website in the End of Service Questions and Answers section.

If someone included synchronization with the LG Cloud, but stopped using the service, and did not receive a notification to this effect, now is the time to check for copies of all the necessary information. A little more than two weeks remained until the hour X.
