Geoinformation ideas

At the university at the thesis, I was engaged in what I did some kind of analogue of Internet maps. The most interesting thing was working with cartographic information. When the cards in the network became more common, I was delighted, because You can do very, very interesting things. Having spent some time with the Yandex.Maps API, I realized that there was a tool.


The technical part of the idea.
Yandex.Maps allow you to put points, lines, polygons over maps. Calculate where to draw polygons, lines and points will help PostGis for PostgreSQL. It is mastered by PostGis quite easily. At least for the first result, it is enough to read the manual fluently.

The necessary information.
