Amazon Kindle Fire Review


The other day I finally got the Kindle Fire. This is the oldest and quite serious model of readers from Amazon. I think the release of color kindle is a very logical step for Amazon. It works under the control of android 2.3 gingerbread, but a pretty and nimble skin from Amazon is stretched over it.

Pre-order and delivery

I pre-ordered Kindle Fire the next day after its announcement, there were no problems with the order, Amazon accepted my card and promised to deliver it until 11/21/11, the money was not immediately written off, but warned that by the time of shipment my account should have the required amount. A pleasant surprise was that my Kindle was delivered to an address in the USA on 11/15/11, while thanking for the pre-order. So ordering this device is not a problem. And on November 25th I already had a book.

We open the box.

Kindle Fire lies in a small box made entirely of paper. The box with the book opens effectively, by tearing off the perforated strip. The kit includes the Kindle itself and a charger (micro USB). When enabled, we are asked to register and upgrade.
