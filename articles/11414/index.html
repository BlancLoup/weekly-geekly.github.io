Soundpedia - replacing the inaccessible "Pandora"

Soundpedia is a legal social music sharing network that provides streaming audio service. In other words, it is the replacement of Pandora for those who do not have the opportunity to use it (Pandora now only works in the US ).

Soundpedia helps users find new ringtones and share them with their friends. The service supports custom playlists. Users can also exchange instant messages with friends, send recommendations, comment on artists, maintain their own audio / video blog.

The project differs from Last.fm in that, in addition to recommendations, it allows you to play tracks by specific genres, artists and albums. Base performers all the time replenished.
