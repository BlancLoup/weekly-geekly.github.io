Today, an interview with the man who, along with Yukihiro Matsumoto, creates Ruby. Meet Koichi Sasad - a programmer whose main project is the Ruby interpreter (CRuby / MRI). In 2007, he received a Ph.D at the University of Tokyo and began teaching. After 13 years at the university, Koichi joined the Matsa team at Heroku. In addition, he is the director of the Ruby Association .
