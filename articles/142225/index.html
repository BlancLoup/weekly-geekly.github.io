The launch of Google Drive will take place in a week. All for 5 GB for free

According to Thenextweb.com , Google will launch its cloud storage service Google Drive next Tuesday or Wednesday. Each user will be allocated 5 GB of free space - two and a half times more than Dropbox, but twice less than Yandex Disk. The Google Drive client will run on Windows, Mac, iOS and Android.

One of the editors of TechCrunch.com, John Biggs, even managed to get the Google Drive client for Mac and install it. The version of the application is 1.0.2891, it starts up and connects to the Google account, but gives the error "Google Drive is not yet enabled for your account."
