A drop of tar

New and attractive opportunities for themselves in connection with the announcement of Pokemon Go considered and attackers. The police department in the small town of O'Fallon, Missouri, published a report on four robbers organizing armed attacks in St. Louis and St. Charles.

According to the police, the criminals searched for their victims among the participants in the Pokemon Go game, after which they lured them to a convenient place for the crime and committed an attack. In the list of affected teenagers are 16-18 years old, living in the above cities. According to the official statement of law enforcement officers, in order to lure the victim to the right place, the robbers marked Pokestop (the real place on the city map where you can pick up in-game content) and today this scheme has been used for robbery 11 times.

Obviously, the developer Niantic Labs and the publisher Pokemon Go were not ready for such a scenario, which does not mean that the search for a solution to an unexpected problem can be postponed for an indefinite future. The global international release of the game has been postponed, because interest in Pokemon Go turned out to be higher than its creators had expected and, as a result, the servers could not bear the load. It remains to hope that by the time of the global launch this trouble will be eliminated and fans of the cult universe will no longer suffer at the hands of lovers of easy money.
