How to write a presentation to attach to the letter



Hello! I continue the cycle (on the blog of a wonderful person Michael, the owner of Textbroker) about the letters, and what can be done with them.

Most likely, the presentation nafig not need anyone except you . Behind the presentation are hiding employees who do not know how to write what they need in words. In most cases, this is just a crutch, just not to blurt out anything extra.

There are two important exceptions. The first is when you are talking to big business. There, the presentation will be carefully printed out and laid on the table to the head. The leader and recipient of your letter are different faces, so you need an essence that will reach him. The second exception is when you need to pack a lot of pictures, graphs and other pieces, that is, in fact, attach either a catalog of goods, or a project outline.

The main rule of the presentation is very simple: if the first and second frame is about you, and not about the recipient, it immediately flies to the basket without any questions.

The structure of the presentation of the smoker is approximately the same (frames 20, each item - 2-3 episodes):
  1. We are such a company.
  2. We are able to do this.

Presentation of a healthy person


On the other hand, normal working investments (which are needed in practice) look completely different. This is a PDF that opens beautifully on any machines and phones. It is typed on a light background, because it implies further printing - it is necessary to take into account the life of the carrier. There from 1-2 pages (if it is A4 PDF) to 5-6 frames (if it is made under the screen). Volume - a maximum of 2 megabytes, more often - 500-700 Kb.

Approximate structure:
  1. Hi, you have this situation
  2. We will do this for you. Photo or scheme.
  3. You get this: numbers.
  4. My name is Ivan, here is my phone.

Again, the structure corresponds to the optimal structure of the product offer - the simplified steps are the same. Problem statement, solution, details, contact.

There are very few words in such a presentation, usually around 50. As a rule, these are headings and captions for pictures. Superfluous nothing. The task of the presentation at this stage is to set an impression, and not to substantiate in detail. It is still necessary to substantiate in detail at the meeting.
