Broadcasts of master classes from Mail.Ru Group on Tekhnostrim channel



Hi, Habr! Today we want to tell you about another activity of ours - online master classes on Tekhnostrim channel. In them we invite experts from the company who are giving a lecture or discuss some topic from IT - from a career path to narrow professional issues. The master class is held in the format of a live broadcast, that is, you can not only watch and listen to the speakers, but also ask them questions in the YouTube broadcast chat and almost immediately get an answer from the speakers.

In April, we have already held several master classes, for example, Natural Language Processing . Dmitry Bugaychenko, an analyst at Mail.Ru Group, talked about the processing of natural languages. Of course, it is impossible to embrace the immense within the framework of one lecture, so we concentrated on the issue of recommending content and examined the required stack of tasks: tokenization, vectorization, semantic and emotional analysis. More master class entries are here .

For May, we also have a program of master classes, which we want to talk about in advance, so that you have the opportunity to choose an event that is interesting to you and free time for it.

May 19 - Path from "Hello, World!" To high-load systems

Speaker: Dmitry Arkhangelsky
We hold this master class for those who have not yet decided on a profession or are looking for something new for themselves. Dmitry will tell about the profession of a software engineer. Answer the question of what he is - a good engineer. You will also learn where to look for a dream job.

Register on IT.Mail.Ru for reminders and links to broadcast on the day of the event.

May 24 - Using OAuth 2 in Django Applications

Speaker: Ilya Stytsenko
The master class will be associated with the implementation of authorization in applications on Django. Ilya Stytsenko will tell you the theoretical basis of the OAuth2 protocol. Together with the speaker, you will learn how to implement an OAuth2 client with python-social-auth, as well as an OAuth2 provider with django-oauth-toolkit in Django projects.

Registration on IT.Mail.Ru.

May 26 - Habits: techie and humanities about useful and harmful habits for a career

Speakers: Denis Elkin, Irina Tripapina
The master class will be held in the format of a discussion between colleagues from different directions, with a completely opposite career history - a techie and humanities. We will talk about what kind of good habits we developed naturally and which ones we had to inoculate by artificial means. Share stories about the habits that radically changed the career path and helped the professional growth.

Sign up at IT.Mail.Ru.

May 30 - Tarantool. Tarantula Training

Speaker: Vasily Soshnikov
In this master class, Vasily will tell you what Tarantool is, how to start it, and what you can do with it. You will also find out which libraries are used with Tarantool and why they are needed. Finally, we will talk about production user cases using Tarantool and present several architectural solutions.

Registration on IT.Mail.Ru.
