Android 2.1 is finally available for the LG GT540

After long promises and several postponements of the release (first until September 15, then until the 21st), Android 2.1 became available for the LG GT540.
Officially, the release of 2.1 for the GT540 has not yet been announced, but the firmware is already available.

Details for GT540 owners


You install the firmware at your own risk! If you want to be sure of the result - contact the official LG service center.

For those who are not yet in the know, but are thinking about reflashing it is worth noting that the process is different for everyone. Someone is easy and calm, and sometimes it happens with dances with a tambourine and with problems. This situation was with all previous firmware updates on Android 1.6, and this is now with 2.1. Unfortunately, the LG service software leaves much to be desired.
More information about the firmware and problems can be found at w3bsit3-dns.com: w3bsit3-dns.com/forum/index.php?showtopic=172890
