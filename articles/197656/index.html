Meeting of IT managers in Kazan: combining business with pleasure

Despite the active conference autumn 2013, we could not ignore the conference, which will be held
December 6 in Kazan - Software Project Manager Conference .



The main focus of this conference is on the quality of reports, a wide scope of topics, and the ability of speakers to present information is interesting.
It is no secret that many participants come to the conference "to the speakers." This year, the program is designed in such a way that it will be desirable if many, if not all, want to visit :) Understanding that this is a difficult task, we will organize video filming of the reports and will be happy to share the recordings.

About what kind of conference and how it was held last time, see the following video:


At the conference, 4 large blocks were allocated for discussion:
- Staff
- Projects
- Products
- Processes

Competent and modern opinions of domestic and foreign experts are presented on each of the topics. Attending the conference, you will have the opportunity in 1 day to obtain the necessary extract of knowledge without having to look for solutions scattered randomly on the Internet. And most importantly, you will have the opportunity to talk with colleagues from numerous companies, and share experiences with them, make useful contacts.

SPM Conf is a conference where IT projects are primarily discussed. However, it will be useful to all specialists from related fields, because many approaches work equally effectively in IT, and in the banking sector, in government projects. Therefore, we invite both IT specialists and anyone interested in project management issues.

The conference will be held in Kazan, in the city where a lot of innovations are born, which are then systematically implemented in all regions. You will have the opportunity to talk with the pioneers of these innovations and learn from them, no doubt, useful experience.
You will learn how the Innopolis project is being implemented to create a new city that will bring together young highly qualified specialists from all over the country, as well as how IT specialists are trained at Innopolis University.

In addition, Kazan is a city with a rich cultural heritage, in which the traditions of Christianity and Islam peacefully intertwine.
