The largest domain registration.RU this year

It looks like the domain zone .RU was left without free three-character (CCC.RU) domains.
Cosmonautics Day gave us a new player in the cybersquatting market. It acts mercilessly and mercilessly - in total more than 15 thousand domains are registered. At the same time, according to the source 3character.com, which monitors the secondary speculative market in TLD domain zones, three-character names are still not fully registered in domains such as .INFO, .BIZ, .MOBI - that is, areas that carry global significance. Against this background, the complete eradication of CCC.RU domains looks even more surprising, but this is already a harsh reality.
