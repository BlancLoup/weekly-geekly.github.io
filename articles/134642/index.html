Greetings to all!

Last month, in November, we successfully launched our new Q & A website for CUBRID users , so that they can ask their questions and receive specific answers.

In honor of the discovery, we announced a Q & A contest in which the most active users who asked the most questions or provided more answers would get an Amazon Kindle.

November was great! It was really fun to have a Q & A contest! We have already identified the winners and prepared a little news.

Briefly about the competition


The Q & A event took place from November 2nd to December 2nd of this year. The rules of the competition, which are given in detail here [in English], invited participants to ask their questions and help other users find answers to their questions. For good questions and accurate answers, all users, except the authors themselves, could vote, which increased the reputation of the authors.

Result


As a result of this contest, which took place one month, users asked 71 questions , and also provided 74 answers . 10 questions are still open , which we will answer soon.

Winners


Firstly, we want to thank the following users who have made a great contribution to the creation of our first Question and Answer site. Each user's reputation is listed in the second column of the table. Full statistics of all users can be found here .

Basically, CUBRID developers, unselected in bold type, have published the most questions and answers. Non-CUBRID users are bold.

In conclusion, based on the rules of the competition, users who have scored the maximum reputation of more than 100 points can receive a prize.

Thus, the winner of this contest is the user under the nickname nicola_magdalena , who will receive the popular Amazon Kindle reader with integrated Wi-Fi. We will contact nicola_magdalena via e-mail to clarify the delivery options for the prize.
