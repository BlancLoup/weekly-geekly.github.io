AdSense for feeds: start testing

This week, Google is launching a closed testing of the AdSense for Feeds (AdSense RSS) advertising service, through which contextual AdSense advertising can be implemented directly into RSS feeds. Testing is conducted jointly with the largest feed aggregator FeedBurner. Already, ads from Google have appeared in some selected feeds FeedBurner in the form of 468 x 30 banners, it looks like this :



For feeds that are already registered in the FeedBurner ad network, an additional advertising channel will appear. Google contextual advertising will be added to the remaining advertising slots in their streams. Since this ad will be sold through the AdSense ad network, bloggers will definitely increase their income.

After testing is over, AdSense can be advertised in feeds by any site that adheres to the standard AdSense requirements .
