Summer internship at InterSystems


Dear participants of the program InterSystems Campus!
InterSystems opens an internship program for university students participating in the InterSystems Campus program.



What to do?


Learn and work with InterSystems engineers. The internship focuses on programming training and solution development using InterSystems technology.
Each intern will have a mentor - engineer. You will be involved in real internal projects of the company.


Where?


Training will be held in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. The company will provide a round-trip flight for students and a US visa (for 1 year).
You can live during an internship at any of the campuses of the many Cambridge or Boston institutes that are empty in summer: MIT, Harvard, Boston University.


When?


The first set will take place this year until the end of February. The internship will be held 2-2.5 months from mid-June until the end of August 2012.


And the salary?


Interns will be officially employed at InterSystems for the internship period and they will be paid a decent salary for young professionals.


How to become an intern?


You must be a student and your university must be a member of the InterSystems Campus program.
You should know English well: fluent level is a necessary condition. The presence of certificates TOEFL, IELTS is a big plus.

The procedure for selecting candidates will be held in two stages: first in the Russian branch of the company, then at headquarters.
The criteria are internal tests, participation in programming contests of InterSystems, and experience in working with InterSystems technologies.
