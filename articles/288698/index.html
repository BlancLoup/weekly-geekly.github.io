Tumblr.com

Tumblr.com - in fact, the first project of mass miniblogging. As mentioned above, a huge number of users are not blogs in their current understanding, not personal blogs, but what was originally called a weblog ‚Äî a directory of links and some notes about this and that. The Tumblr.com project brings us back exactly to this concept of blogging ‚Äúfound, saw - post‚Äù. No comments , no headaches with the design. Everything is extremely simple. You can choose several themes from those offered by the creators of the service, or customize the color scheme for you. You can add messages using the form on the website or from a mobile phone by sending messages to a specific mailing address. You can attach to the post photos. The only inconvenience is that you must keep the address given to you in complete secret, otherwise some hooligan will be able to use it and clutter up your message line. The process of writing in such a mini-blog is quite simple: choose a category and add what you want. The following options are currently available:
  • Text : regular entry.
  • Photo : You can specify a link to a photo online or upload your own from a local computer in jpeg, gif, png or bmp format up to 10MB in size.
  • Quote : quotation indicating the source, which is accordingly formed in large quotes.
  • Link : specify the name of the link and the URL itself.
  • Chat : inserts a conversation from IM or another chat, which is highlighted accordingly.
  • Video : insert video (allowed direct link from YouTube or Google Video , or embed code from other services)
Commenting is not provided, however, according to recent developer statements, feedback will sooner or later appear on the site, the only question is what form. Very interesting is the import of feeds from other network services. This is exactly what, I am sure, many of you have sometimes thought about. Now everything can be collected in one place: Flickr , Twitter , Last.fm , Livejournal , del.icio.us and all-all-all ... Thus, we can conclude that this service is useful not only for those who do not want burden yourself with too much knowledge about how it all works there, but also for those who actively use various Internet services.

Twitter.com

Twitter.com is a microblogging service that allows you to keep your own news feed. And also be aware of events happening with your friends. The appearance of the service is minimal, but you can customize the look of your ribbon by setting (or removing) the background image and determining the colors of some elements of the page. You can add messages using the form on the website, via SMS and through any client that works via the Jabber protocol. Message length is limited to 140 characters, formatting is not supported. You can send messages arbitrarily, or configure the system so that it periodically asks you "What are you doing now?" To send messages to your tape on Twitter using the phone, you need to confirm it by sending a message to the number +447781488126 with the code word indicated on the site. Your new messages on Twitter will not appear as in your own feed, forming a certain microblogging, sent by SMS and Jabber to those who added you to ‚Äúfriends‚Äù. You can make a user a friend, remove from the list of friends, start or stop watching him on the user's personal page or using special control commands. Thus, you can manage the distribution of your own news, and subscriptions to other people's feeds. It is necessary to take into account that when a user is added to the list of friends, observation of him is automatically included. When you delete - turns off. At the same time, you can turn off watching a friend and vice versa - watch a person without calling him a friend. I would say that it is not very convenient, because I, frankly, do not really like the idea that strangers are watching me. The way out of this situation to some extent can serve as private message statuses. You can send a message that is visible to everyone or only to those you have added as a friend. The system also provides for the possibility of direct communication to someone, turning into a kind of SMS-chat, replacing the possibility of commenting records. Authors and fans of the service have done everything to promote it to the masses: from special informers that display your tape or the tape of your friends, to integration into other services and platforms.

Jaiku.com

Jaiku is a Finnish service that occupies an intermediate position at the border of mini- and microblogging. This is no longer an ascetic Twitter , but also not Tumblr.com . The appearance of the service is more loaded, compared to Twitter , the settings are reduced to changing the background of your page. You can add messages using the form on the website, via SMS, as well as by broadcasting feeds (which are divided into categories: Photo, Blog, Bookmarks, Music, Places, Event, Video, Other), which makes Jaiku related to Tumblr.com . Message length is limited to 140 characters. When writing a message, you can also assign it a certain icon to choose from. For example, all messages from a mobile phone are marked with the appropriate sign. To send a message to your tape on Jaiku using the phone, you need to send a message to the number +46 737 494 0501 . To activate the number, you must specify it on the site, and then enter the confirmation code that will come to you via SMS. Your new posts on Jaiku will appear both in your own feed, forming a kind of microblogging, and sent by e-mail to those who added you to ‚Äúfriends‚Äù and agreed to receive these notifications. Unlike Twitter, no SMS or IM notification is provided. But it is possible to comment on messages (minimal formatting is supported - _ italic _, * bold *, [link text] (http://jaiku.com/)).
