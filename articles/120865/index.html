Keyboard (and not only) for star warriors from Razer

To release the MMORPG "Star Wars: The Old Republic" (tentative date - September 2011), Razer has prepared a special set consisting of a keyboard, mouse, mouse carpet and headset.


Gaming keyboard




The greatest interest from the whole set is the keyboard due to some design features.
The right side of the keyboard has a color LCD touch screen with multitouch support.


Above the screen in 2 rows of 5 keys, there are screen keys (reminded by Optimus ), on which icons for skills / skills / etc are displayed.


"Low-profile" keys with golden backlight. Give the alphabet "Aurebesh" instead of Russian!


Soft keys and macro recording, switching between 10 profiles without having to go into the driver control panel.


Color customizable lights on the keyboard contour (for the Galactic Republic, blue is offered, for the Sith Empire - red, for those who will mow under the Jedi lightsaber - green, etc.).


The length of the USB cable is ~ 2 meters.
The price is $ 199.99.

Now briefly about the other devices from the set.

Gaming mouse



Mouse with 17 programmable buttons (including 12 buttons in the form of a grid 3 * 4 under the thumb), support for both wired and wireless connections. Included is a dock for charging (in combination a wireless receiver).
A 3.5G laser sensor with a resolution of 5600 dpi, a sampling frequency of 1000 Hz and a response time of 1 ms is used.
Without color lights and interchangeable fractions of fractions, nowhere, so it is also available.
The price is $ 129.99.

Gaming mouse mat



Double-sided carpet (do not forget about fractions), non-slip, wear-resistant, with anti-reflective coating.
The price is $ 49.99.

Gaming headset



The headset delivers 5.1 sound to your ears , has a flexible microphone, adjustable headband, volume control buttons and a microphone.
Color lights and replaceable panels of fractions - of course.
The price is $ 129.99.

In the sale of the device will go simultaneously with the release of the game.
