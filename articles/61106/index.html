Natal Project

At the opening of the E3 conference, Microsoft introduced the Natal project for the X-Box 360. This project will allow you to interact with the console without manipulators. With a simple wave of your hand or voice, you can manage a collection of films. Players will play games just by standing in front of the camera without the need to hold any manipulators in their hands.



Under the cut is an amazing project by Peter Moulinier based on Natal.


Peter Moulinier presented his project, which supports the technology Natal. In this project, you can make yourself a virtual friend with whom you will communicate and interact and literally touch the virtual world, seeing, for example, your reflection in the water.
