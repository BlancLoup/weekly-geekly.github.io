I2Competition - competition of ideas for web-projects and mobile applications

Friends, we know you have fresh and brilliant ideas of projects that you want to bring to life. Do not hesitate, participate in the competition "I2Competition" and implement your ideas today.

From December 12, 2010 to March 12, 2011, the Digital Bridge Management Company holds an all-Russian competition of projects at the ‚Äúseed‚Äù and ‚Äúpre-seed‚Äù stages in the field of Internet technologies and mobile applications. The main prize in the competition will be an exclusive offer from the investment company Digital Bridge on the conditions, volume and terms of investment for the winning project. Also, all participants have the opportunity to receive valuable prizes and gifts from sponsors and partners of the competition. In order to submit your own ideas for the competition, it is necessary to register and fill in an electronic form on the i2com.ru website from December 12, 2010 to March 12, 2011. Competitive selection will be carried out in five categories:
  • Best monetization
  • The best traffic generator
  • Selection of participants
  • Best Mobile App
  • Fantastic idea

  • Evaluation of ideas by an expert commission (IT and Internet experts, professionals and practitioners from leading Russian companies)
  • Evaluation by the investment board of the company Digital Bridge.
The i2competition competition is aimed at attracting public attention to the development of innovative technologies in Russia. The organizer of the competition, the company Digital Bridge, is engaged in venture investment and management, has all the necessary production facilities and expertise. The competition partners will be the largest Russian universities, government agencies, well-known Internet sites, youth organizations, investment funds and leading IT companies.
