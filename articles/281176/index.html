Why an analytical bot?


Ease of teamwork. Any working dialogue about statistics or behavioral metrics requires unloading data from analytical tools. Moreover, the live presence of several people in front of one screen is often necessary. Statsbot will allow the entire command to be aware of the various project indicators at a time.

Analytics in your smartphone. There are no Yandex.Metrics or Google Analytics applications for mobile phones where all the functionality familiar to us from desktop versions is implemented. Statsbot, or rather the conversational interface, is able to provide a cross-platform solution (from hours to TVs) without detracting from the potential of analytical services.

Geeks love text interface. Many developers use different console utilities daily. Package managers are much more comfortable than application stores, working with Git or Mercurial is more comfortable in the console, the habit of Vim, and so on.

Apotheosis


Statsbot allows you to take a fresh look at how to interact with analytical tools. A bot changes the concept of automation and literally becomes a member of the team: he can independently monitor the metrics and notify him of any results when needed.

Chat bots have a different level of interactivity. The main task of Statsbot is to become a full-fledged assistant who will help a business to focus on the most necessary indicators and emerging trends. We are actively working on the development of artificial intelligence and understanding of spoken language.
