Manufacturers of electric cars offer used batteries from cars to use for home.



Now more and more electric cars are appearing on the market, and automakers who previously did not pay attention to electric cars, nevertheless, start producing them - after all, such cars find their consumers. And with cheaper electric cars, more and more people are buying them. But here there is one problem - recycling of waste batteries, which can no longer be used in electric vehicles due to reduced capacity.
