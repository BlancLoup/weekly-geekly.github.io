Dell Mini 5 Internet Tablet



Judging by this device, which Dell has demonstrated at the last CES, the company is preparing to continue to master the microcomputer segment further. The Dell Mini 3 smartphone was already introduced last fall, and the gadget of the next weight category, the Dell Mini 5 (nee Streak), was shown at the Las Vegas show. Not a communicator, not an Internet tablet. About the characteristics so far nothing is known. It is reported that the device has Wi-Fi and 3G modules, as well as a slot for SIM-cards. The display, as the name implies, is a five-inch touchscreen with multitouch support. There are two cameras: one of which is five-megapixel. Works machine running Android 1.6.
