Welcome to Christmas Agile MeetUp

December 25, we invite you to the Christmas Agile mitap in Raiffeisenbank. To the sounds of Jingle Bells and in the pre-New Year mood we will talk about how many programmers are needed to evaluate the feature, which pitfalls in transformation and how agile helps in working in various industries - from eSports to service migration. We will discuss these and many other questions together with Mail.Ru Group, Unusual Consepts and Riot Games.

Under the cut details.




18:00 Riot Games
We learn how the guys apply flexible methodologies in their diverse projects (eSports, service migrations, high-tech), and the consolidated work of the whole team.
