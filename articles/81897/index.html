German aerial photographs of Leningrad 1942

I recently stumbled upon one wonderful website www.wwii-photos-maps.com , where aerial photographs of the Second World War from the German aircraft were posted. The bulk of the images laid out falls on 3 cities (and their environs): Leningrad, Stalingrad and Warsaw.


Aerial view of Leningrad, May 1, 1942.

At the same time, in the photographs indicated as Leningrad, I found several photographs in the Kirov area. The pictures differ in resolution, while for Warsaw and Leningrad the resolution is up to 2 meters per pixel, then for Stalingrad a maximum of about 10.


Aerial view of Stalingrad, Mamaev Kurgan, September 17, 1942.

Seeing all this splendor, I decided to do the binding and gluing of images, especially since the 65th anniversary of the victory is on my nose.
For starters, I am posting a mosaic of pictures of Kronstadt and Leningrad. All pictures are from 1942, from May to September. Most are covered with ~ 2.5 and ~ 4 m / n images.
