The iTunes Store is now in Russia - long live the ruble!

Hello! And again we write about Apple - this time the reason is very important, since everything described below is a serious step towards the development of high-quality services in Russia.



Apple today announced the opening of the iTunes Store for Russian users. Russia is not the only country where the iTunes Store was officially opened today, only 56 new countries gained access to a music and film store.

A closed iTunes Event will also be held tonight in Moscow, Apple is expected to officially announce the opening of the first and long-awaited Apple Store and, possibly, the Apple Online Store.

Naturally, the digital store opened for a reason - now Russian-speaking consumers of products provided by Apple and its partners can be closer to their favorite brand, because now we all have the opportunity to buy not only apps and games from the App Store, but also music and films. In the Russian segment of the iTunes Store, prices are very affordable (about 2 times cheaper than in foreign sections of the store) - I am glad that Apple is on the right and very pleasant path for us.
