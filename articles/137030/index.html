Bruce Schneier at Positive Hack Days!



A cryptographic guru, world-renowned information security expert Bruce Schneier is coming to Moscow for the first time to speak at the Positive Hack Days forum organized by Positive Technologies.

Bruce Schneier is the legend of the IB-world, his name means a lot to everyone who is concerned with information security. On Bruce's book "Applied Cryptography" more than one generation of cryptographers, professionals and hackers has grown.

Another Schneier's bestseller was the book Secrets and Lies. He is the author and co-author of more than a dozen cryptographic algorithms such as Blowfish, Twofish and Threefish. His newsletter Crypto-Gram and the blog Schneier on Security read more than 150,000 professionals worldwide. There are legends and jokes about him, his phrases become aphorisms and slogans of the information security industry.

We have not yet disclosed the topic of Bruce's speeches, but we are already recording in a queue for those who want to receive an autograph. Follow the news !
