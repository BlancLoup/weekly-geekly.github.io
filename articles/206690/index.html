Internal R & D Lab


In our view, Acronis Labs will be a symbiosis of the four internal units that closely interact with each other:

Performance Lab : a unified methodology for measuring the performance of Acronis products and approaches to improving their effectiveness will be developed here. Product Lab : the team of this division will create high-tech solutions with the latest technologies for a small group of advanced users. This will allow to run in new technologies before turning them into products for the mass market.
