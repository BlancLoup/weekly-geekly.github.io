Drag and drop images from the browser to a folder on the disk

The message from the area "learned and decided to share." Picking around on other people's pictures on other sites and picking up a collection for yours, I thought it would be great if pictures from the browser could be dragged with a mouse to a folder on a disk.

Something like this ..., took the picture from the browser and dragged it to my folder and ... it was a drag about the miracle, I can say taken aback and did not believe my luck, it is much faster than I could have done before. Maybe I'm the only one, and everyone else is aware of this, because it is so convenient, but still I think someone might not know. It seems to be a trifle, but extremely convenient while saving a lot of different pictures.

A small experiment showed that the feature works fine in recent versions of Chrome, FF, Safari and does not work in Opera and IE. Windows 7 operating system. Some complex logged or scripted pictures are copied as a link, but there are not many such pictures.
