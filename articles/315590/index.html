Hello! Earlier this week, I propose to think about the weekend! This coming Saturday I invite you to an informal meeting of participants of the Russian virtual reality market, where you can combine pleasant sittings in a bar with obtaining useful knowledge and business contacts. This is the second such Moscow meet-up devoted to the development, launch, positioning and monetization of products in the field of virtual reality. It will be held on December 10, 2016 (almost New Year's Eve) from 16:00 in the bar BarDisco90 , located near the Moscow metro station Tverskaya. The main purpose of the event is to popularize technologies of augmented and virtual reality in Russia and to establish informal ties among those who are engaged in these technologies. And also just to drink tea together and something stronger, listen to interesting reports, talk. Login to VR-Today is free .

More about the program of the event and the address of the venue under the cut.
