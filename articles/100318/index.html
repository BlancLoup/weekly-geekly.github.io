July 30 will be the fourth meeting of the Ukrainian community of information security specialists

On July 30, at 10:00, the Ukrainian Information Security Group will meet in the Daily Kiev Bar (HudGraf gallery) on T.Shevchenko Boulevard, 33.

Some of the active members of the community have agreed to make setup reports and set topics for communication:
- Gleb Pakharenko (Infopulse) and Maria Sidorova (Security Code) will tell you about the experience of information security communities in Ukraine and Russia.
- Vladimir Ilibman (Cisco) will talk about the IT paradigm shift towards blurring of borders, which occurs due to cloud services and mobile employees.
- Andrey Lysyuk (Ernst & Young) and Alexander Dmitriev (Das magazine) will share their experience in CISM / CISA and ISO 27001 certification

Also, it is planned to discuss the creation of the Ukrainian CERT (Computer Emergency Response Team) and the specific information threats of virtual infrastructures.
