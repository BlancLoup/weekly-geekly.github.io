Tool stand ver. 2

That's actually made back in the New Year holidays. And recently, a little remade to the second version.



In my opinion, quite a geeky homemade product, which allowed defeating the mess, freeing up space on the table, and streamlining tools.

The stand is made of aluminum corners and pipes. All knots fastened zaklepochnik. Sidewalls from a corner of 40 mm. The length of the stand 51 cm, height 24 cm. It is attached to the table with a pair of self-tapping screws.



The upper carrier tube with a diameter of 40 mm, there are holes in it F 14 mm. The average tube is 20 mm outer diameter, f 6 mm hole, and several 9 mm each for thicker instruments.



For pliers, pliers, calipers and similar tools in the bottom of a special shelf where they are easy to install and remove. It is made of nested parts of each other at 40 and 25 mm.



Rear view of the stand design:



In the first version, this stand was not distinguished by a tube in the middle, but instead was a square profile. But the vertical arrangement of small tools was not very convenient, and aesthetically disliked:



Therefore, the square was dismantled by drilling rivets, and its place was taken by the tube with the location of screwdrivers under the slope.
