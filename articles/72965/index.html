ITrack has completed a new collection of CMS data on sites in the .ru zone

The company iTrack www.itrack.ru , which in June 2009 announced a unique method of building an independent rating of site management systems (CMS), presents the updated data.


The initial selection of participants for the CMS rating was carried out using data provided by the analytical portal CMS Magazine . The number of sites working on this CMS, their TCI, the number of implementing partners were taken into account. During the study, more than 2 million second-level domains in the .ru zone were analyzed. On each domain, the robot program tried to detect the CMS from the list of participants.

The algorithm is based on finding obvious signs of CMS installations and so-called fingerprints are used to identify CMS. As a result, a rating was compiled based on information about real installations on sites.

This system and the results were received with interest by the market of CMS-developers. At the moment, the application of this data has already found a subsidiary project of the analytical portal CMS Magazine - WebIndicator . Relying on statistical data, the resource provides a realistic picture of the distribution to interested users:
  • commercial and free "boxed" site management systems;
  • web servers;
  • counters (share of the total number of sites);
  • domain registrars.

The algorithm is based on finding obvious signs of CMS installations and so-called fingerprints are used to identify CMS.

The imprint is a specific line of text placed at a specific address (URL).
For example, the presence on the /wp-login.php page of a form with the line wp-login clearly indicates the installation of the WordPress system.
