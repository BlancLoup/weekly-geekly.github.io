European science projects

We often read in the news that scientists have made another discovery in another industry, and this will make another revolution in it. But this is only part of the research in a large number of projects. About American studies have heard almost everything, turning to European projects over the past few years.

There is a scientific network called Future and Emerging Technologies ( FET ), which is part of the Community Research & Development Information Sevice ( CORDIS ), organized by the European Union. In this network, scientists of the European Union are developing new computer architectures, low-cost and high-performance processors, scalable network systems, new operating systems, robots, new tools for man-machine interaction. These inventions are already being implemented or will be implemented for about 15 years.

The content of some projects in recent years

  • BEYOND-THE-HORIZON - coordination of work and strategically important areas of research throughout this network.
  • EASYCOMP is the development of autonomous active components from which programs will be made, that is, programming must become a kind of design. Immediately the task of effectively connecting components, developing specifications, building data meta-models.
  • POP - parallel systems based on OpenMP, not only on SMP architectures for which it was originally intended, but also on any cluster systems.
  • SECSAFE is a static analysis of the source texts of programs designed to improve the reliability and quality of the program code.
  • DEGAS - development of new UML-models for large-scale network solutions.
  • DART - large software and hardware systems for banking structures that facilitate setting up applications and optimizing them for specific tasks.
  • ALCOM-FT - development of multi-criteria decision-making systems, in contrast to today's, the future must operate with thousands of criteria to find the optimal solution.
  • COCONUT is an attempt to approximate the solution of NP-complete problems using constraint programming in constraint programming technology.
  • TYPES - software training project.
  • EVERGROW - attempts to predict the behavior of the Internet, its complexity and direction of development.
  • WONDERWEB - works in the field of text semantics, description languages, tools for creating applications and interfaces.
  • MOWGLI is the same as WONDERWEB, but in the field of mathematics.
  • DELIS - works in the field of classical search engines and their transformation.
  • PANDA - development of a DBMS based on templates that can efficiently process large data arrays, automatically taking into account the diversity and complexity of the stored information. This is a project in defiance of American DBMS.
  • CINQ - knowledge base, automation of OLAP-tasks at a new level.
  • INFOMIX - development of universal data access interfaces. The concept of self-structured information.
  • DBGLOBE - similar to INFOMIX, includes models for describing and storing data and means for organizing queries to them.
  • PROFI - automatic recognition of multimedia, video, audio, graphics.
