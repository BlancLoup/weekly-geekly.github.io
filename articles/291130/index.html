Business angels have invested more than $ 2 million in AltaIR PandaDoc portfolio portfolio company

The Belarusian company PandaDoc raised more than $ 2 million in a new round of financing. She is a portfolio company of AltaIR Capital . PandaDoc is a service for working with digital signatures and document management.

A group of business angels invested in the project, Firrma reports citing a source in AltaIR. However, AltaIR "has retained a significant stake in the company," according to fund representatives.

Last July, $ 400,000 TMT Investments invested in the company. The investment was carried out on the terms of a convertible loan.

PandaDoc is located in California. The company was founded by Belarusian entrepreneurs Sergey Barisyuk and Mikita Mikado in June 2013. A little later, they attracted $ 655 thousand from Kima Ventures .

Since its inception, the project has attracted a total of about $ 3.1 million from various investors.
