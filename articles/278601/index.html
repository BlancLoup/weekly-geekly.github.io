WG Labs Announces Developer Contest

On March 3, a competition for developers from WG Labs was launched, designed to identify new talents in the gaming industry.



What for?


Any great idea needs support. The WG Labs Developer Contest will provide young and ambitious studios with an excellent chance to make themselves known, to receive funds for the development of the project and to implement their innovative ideas.

Who is involved?


Any studio from the CIS, EU, Asia, North or South America can fight for the prize fund of the competition. Applications for participation are accepted from March 3 to April 30. The team that created the best game project will be announced on May 30‚Äì31. The winner will receive not only the jury's complimentary assessment, but also a reward of $ 10,000 for the further development of his project.

Any game that meets the following requirements can participate in the competition:

  • stage: alpha and above (prototypes and projects that have been released, are not allowed to participate);
  • mode: multiplayer
  • monetization model: free-to-play, pay-to-play;
  • platforms: PC (Windows, Mac OS, Linux), mobile (iOS, Android, Windows 10).

You can learn more about the rules of participation and send your game to the competition at WG Labs .

What is WG Labs?


WG Labs is a Wargaming laboratory that aims to cultivate innovative solutions for the video game industry and implement original projects by collaborating with external teams.

The lab serves as a springboard for young and ambitious developers. Here, talent and enthusiasm are combined with Wargaming's many years of expertise in creating and distributing video games. Original and bold ideas, combined with the company's experience, a fine knowledge of monetization mechanisms and a multimillion-dollar audience of Wargaming, offer great promise to third-party developers and employees.

WG Labs brings together specialists whose projects are able to set the pace of development of the game industry, and provides company employees and third-party teams with the conditions and expert experience for prototyping, testing and implementing high-tech products, developing non-standard game mechanics and business models.

You can learn more about WG Labs and submit your project to www.wglabs.ru.
