Project Animusic 3 asks for help

Probably, many of you are already familiar with the work of Animusic, amazing musical animations. On Habr√© about them already wrote , and if someone has not yet encountered these works, I strongly recommend to fill this gap. Unfortunately, there is every chance that the next album may not be released, and in this regard, the company asks the community to help with financial investments through the Kickstarter project.

During the existence of the company, two discs with seven and eight tracks, respectively, were released, as well as a Blu-Ray disc with some of these tracks in high quality. (By the way, on the official site you can download two of them in the 480p and 720p versions.) Since then, Animusic fans have been looking forward to the release of the third album, news of the work on which periodically fueled interest. Unfortunately, the course chosen by the company did not meet the expectations of the developers. Active work on updating and improving their own software, which is used to create animations, took away all the strength and resources, so that in the end, halfway to the finish, the company was left without finance.

In this regard, Animusic opened a project on Kickstarter to raise funds for the continuation of work on Animusic 3. Within a month, it is necessary to collect $ 200,000. So far, half a month has passed and a little more than 100 thousand have been collected, about 80 of which were accumulated in the very first 4-5 days. This shows that the pace has greatly decreased, and there is every reason to fear that the required amount will not be reached. Therefore, I decided to write this note with a request to the habrasoobshchestvo to help this project and spread information among friends and acquaintances.
