The Ministry of Communications and Mass Media developed a draft law on the full regulation of the Russian Internet


E. Reasonable / Vedomosti

The draft law is only at the initial stage of development, it has not yet been discussed with the RF Minister of Communications Nikolai Nikiforov.

The document states the need to regulate the infrastructure of the Russian segment of the Internet. This term means a set of lines and communication centers located in Russia, as well as data centers that are responsible for the operation of the RuNet. There is a new concept in the document ‚Äúautonomous system‚Äù. The authors of the bill explain the term as ‚Äúcommunications and other technical means with a unique number, the traffic passing between them is carried out according to the same rules‚Äù.

The draft law provides for the introduction of a new clause in the legislation - ‚ÄúRegulation of the Russian part of the Internet‚Äù. We are talking about the regulation of the main elements of the infrastructure, including the national .ru domains of the Russian Federation, traffic exchange points, the state system for ensuring the sustainability of the network, ‚Äúthe infrastructure of the autonomous systems of Russian natural and legal persons‚Äù (all communication networks of the country).
