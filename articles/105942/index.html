HTC HD7 on Windows Phone 7 officially presented


In anticipation of the launch of the platform Windows Phone 7, HTC officially presented the whole family of phones , as well as the new flagship HTC HD7. HD7 is nothing more than a perfect incarnation of HTC HD2 with Windows Phone 7 on board. The novelty is intended for sale in Europe and the USA exclusively for the T-Mobile operator.

Characteristics of new items, in general, do not differ from HD2. Available HD7 4.3 "SLCD display with a resolution of 800x480, 5 megapixel camera with dual LED flash, 576 MB RAM, 512 MB ROM, 8/16 GB of internal memory. The same System-On-Chip from Qualcomm QSD8250 remains the heart of the smartphone.

A distinctive feature of the HD7 is support for Dolby Mobile technology and SRS-Surround Sound effects. According to the manufacturer, the smartphone is capable of playing fairly high-quality sound thanks to two good speakers.

Sales of T-Mobile version of the communicator will start on October 21.
