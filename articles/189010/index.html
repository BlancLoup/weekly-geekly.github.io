Xerox scanners and copiers can change the numbers in documents when copying

The Xerox WorkCentre copiers and scanners showed an interesting glitch : in some cases they can change small numbers when scanning / copying documents. This is an unpleasant effect, especially when copying financial documents.

Most likely, the bug is associated with the peculiarities of the JBIG2 algorithm for compressing binary images. The algorithm uses a "dictionary" of characters and substitutes them in case of similarity.
