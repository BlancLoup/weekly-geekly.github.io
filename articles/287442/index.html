New Blogger features are being tested.

Not so long ago, the Blogger development team launched Blogger In Draft , where new features of this blog hosting are being tested in a mode open to all registered users.

The first such function was posting videos in posts. True, I mean not integration with YouTube, as it would be more logical, but downloading a file from the local computer. When posting a video, you must agree to the terms of service: a link to them leads to Google Video, which, although it becomes a video search engine, continues to receive videos from users. An example of a posted video can be seen here .



A new option appeared on Blogger In Draft yesterday - voting, which the user can place in the sidebar of his blog.



In addition, you can now embed links to media files in your blog's RSS feeds so that users of iTunes and other programs used to listen to or view podcasts can subscribe to the RSS blog and download podcasts to their computer or player.

Naturally, all these features are available so far only through the Blogger In Draft interface.
