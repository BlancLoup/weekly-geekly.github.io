Whether to limit users on resources?

How much I do IT - so much I hear from admins "it will be painfully fat for users, we will cut off the traffic / mailbox volume / file ball / block the site / substitute to taste". And exactly the same I have a question: what is your business?

Let's forget that we are IT managers and manage cool storage systems, server farms, mail servers and look at all this confusion. Consider the commercial structure.

1. What does your company do?

Forget the production of toilet paper, pants or even aircraft engines.

The correct answer is: it produces money. And so that the money produced was obtained more than the spent money.


2. Who makes money?

And they are produced by the very "stupid users" who are asked to press any key of the great and powerful technomage. In fact, a technomagn is so large that it cannot read instructions in English (the working language of IT), and for Russian it is already too large.


3. With what help do they make money?

Money is also generated using IT services, including mail servers for communication with customers, the Internet, accounting systems, bookkeeping, etc. In the process of making money, IT services consume IT resources ‚Äî raw power (processors / RAM / storage), licenses, channels.

4. Who owns the IT infrastructure and resources?

Here, time after time, I stumble upon the fact that administrators are beginning to consider servers, storage systems and switches - "their own." At least a little, but they begin to treat them accordingly.

The correct answer is: they belong to the company (the owner of the company) and must fulfill their task of generating money.

5. And now - who should decide how much resources a business unit employee can consume?

As it seems to me, this absolutely should not be an IT admin. Just because he has no idea how these resources bring in money.

Resource consumption should be unrestricted by IT, with virtually zero IT weight in making decisions about allocating resources to IT infrastructure. Except maybe moments when a sudden increase in consumption can put the entire infrastructure in its entirety.

An internal billing system is needed, which instead of restricting consumption simply sends an invoice to the head of department and fin at the end of the month. Director of who and how many resources consumed. In rubles. Just because it is these two people who understand what share of the profit the employee generates and how much resources he needs to generate this profit.
