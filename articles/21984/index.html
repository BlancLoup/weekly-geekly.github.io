About transparent SaaS - platforms
Further, we will discuss here only the most characteristic subset of transparent platforms belonging to the class of multi-purpose operating platforms *. The named platform class combines the instrumental functions (development environment) and functions of the working environment (run-time environment). In this case, the instrumental service is provided to the customer only in the ‚Äúon demand‚Äù mode. In other words, we will talk about transparent social SaaS - platforms *, bearing in mind, above all, the instrumental component of their services.

Transparent platforms open too
I would also like to add on my own that, besides internal or own openness at the level of programmer interfaces and user information inherent in many platforms discussed in the article, the general trends of the social web put forward the task of external openness in relation to other social resources.

The first in this direction began the Ning movement. I have already mentioned that the company that owns these resources has joined the ‚Äúclosed partner club‚Äù Open Social.

Even today, Ning as an external container (in Open Social terminology) allows each application (apps) compatible with Open Social to connect to any resource created on the basis of the Ning platform. After that, any user of the resource to which some such application is connected can become a user of this application.

Even earlier, Ning included an instrumental service as part of its platform, using which any customer can easily build an application to FaceBook and connect it to the latter. True, this application will have quite limited capabilities, but literally, in a few minutes, a person who is completely unfamiliar with programming can create it.

Ning is currently working on a similar mechanism, but for Open Social. At a minimum, it can be expected that any target Ning resource will be easily connected as an attachment to Orkut, and then to other containers from members of the ‚Äúclosed club‚Äù. At the same time, which is very important, the hosting of such an application is also fully supported by Ning servers.

There is no doubt that other transparent social SaaS platforms will move along the way of Ning today. The interesting thing then appears, as the hero of one of the last TV shows loved to repeat, an oil painting. But I plan to talk about this in my next article.
