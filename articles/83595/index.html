Conference Cloudconf-2010. Let's talk about the "clouds"?



From March 24 to March 25, 2010 in Moscow we will hold Cloudconf-2010 , a professional conference for developers and providers of SaaS solutions in Russia.

First of all, Cloudconf-2010 is designed for developers (Internet Service Vendor) and providers of SaaS solutions (Internet Service Provider) involved in the creation and sales of such services in Russia.

We called the leading companies in the field of software, hardware and SaaS - Microsoft, Google, IBM, Juniper Networks, VMWare, Parallels, TrendMicro, ITooLabs and other vendors, in order for them to share their experience in SaaS development, implementation and sales. At the conference, participants will be able to talk with manufacturers of the largest SaaS platforms, discuss the technical aspects of developing cloud applications.

The first day will be entirely devoted to the discussion of existing cloud platforms and the construction of SaaS infrastructure. The second day - services, promotion, development and ways to monetize SaaS solutions. A detailed program of Cloudconf-2010 is available at cloudconf.ru/program.php , a list of speakers can be found on the page - http://cloudconf.ru/speakers.php . The conference program and the number of speakers is constantly growing and updated, stay tuned.



The conference program includes presentations by participants, a discussion panel, and sections on various topics related to the development and sales of SaaS solutions. Particular attention will also be paid to the discussion of data security in the "clouds".

Registration and payment of Cloudconf-2010 are open until March 20, 2010 on the conference website www.cloudconf.ru .
