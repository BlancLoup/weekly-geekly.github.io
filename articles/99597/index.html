ITrack's CMS Rating - Results of Distribution of Market Shares for the II Quarter of 2010

Published July rating of site management systems (CMS), compiled on the information about the real installations on the sites.

- In total in July 2010, 2,842,405 domains were surveyed.
- The share of domains that responded within 20 seconds was 62.3%.
- CMS detected in 12.4% of domains.

The rating for July 2010 for the first time provides information on which CMS are used on the most popular websites of the Runet. Popular online projects require a special approach in design and implementation. It is often justified to use your own site management system, written to order. This is especially true for those areas where the business risks associated with the reliability of the site are quite high. However, the cost of owning your own system is still higher, so a gradual decrease in the number of custom-made CMS is forecasted due to the transfer of sites from the TOP-100 to the circulation systems in most topics.

In the ranking of paid circulation CMS, gaining 49.18%, 1C-Bitrix leads. With a result of 26.10%, it is followed by NetCat. The third and fourth positions are HostCMS and UMI.CMS, respectively.


In the ranking of free CMS, the most common system is WordPress (36.64%). Popularity gains Joomla 30.57%. It is followed by the Datalife Engine with a result of 14.73%.


There is a clear trend towards the conversion of sites from studio, custom-made systems to production products. This is clearly seen in the example of online stores. An online store cannot work without a CMS; therefore, the share of uncertain systems consists mainly of custom-made engines, own or studio systems. Samopisnye management systems use 64.3% of stores. This is followed by Shop-Script (10.7%), 1C-Bitrix (4.9%), Joomla (3%). Other CMS use less than 1.5% of online stores. Compared to the April rating, the share of uncertain CMS decreased by 5.3%, mainly due to the transition to Shop-script, OsCommerce and PHPShop.

On the distribution of the shares of the other participants in the rating, statistics on the use of CMS in online stores, the dynamics of market leaders as compared to April 2010 - read on www.itrack.ru/cmsrate .
