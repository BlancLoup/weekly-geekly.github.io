Content marketing

The concept of a ‚Äústartup‚Äù has a lot of definitions, I like it owned by Eric Reese: ‚ÄúA startup is an organization that creates a new product or service in conditions of high uncertainty.‚Äù Usually, we have a new product on the Internet, high risks and an incomprehensible market. Content marketing is the creation and distribution of content with independent value for the desired audience. In order to use something new and incomprehensible (from the point of view of the consumer, this is any startup), you need:

  • Take an interest in this;
  • Know how to use it.


The first item refers to the birth of a marketing funnel (increased knowledge of the product), which gradually takes your consumer to the shopping mall. The second point is educational materials that are packaged in the most interesting and unobtrusive form for the audience.

Both requests are met through content marketing - the most accurate solution for promoting new or complex products.

In a broad sense, the scope of content marketing includes many independent promotional tools, including email marketing. The automatic series of training letters that a new user receives after registering with the service or trigger mailings is also content marketing, which can be called a mandatory program of any Internet service. There are many companies on the Internet that communicate very effectively and beautifully with the consumer through mailings.

Publications

Of course, publications on platforms with a predominance of the target audience, training videos and other materials that will make life easier for future customers at different stages of the sales funnel are important.

Publications attract attention, increase awareness, provide the necessary level of communication with the market and the consumer, are an essential element of SEO, create an expert level for a brand, etc. The list of advantages of placing publications is very large, and the disadvantage is one. You need a very good quality of work. This is not so much about the perfect text in terms of grammar, spelling and even style. A good publication in the framework of content marketing is the ability to write without advertising, to understand the common task and to be well versed in the specifics of the product.

In this sense, you can understand those founders of complex services who do not dare to work with freelancers or content agencies. Blog and social networks

I intend to unite these two channels here, because in a startup, as they say in romantic films, it is better for them to be together.

The company's blog is another opportunity to be interesting for its audience, which means attracting it and building loyalty. At the same time, a blog allows you to use technical marketing opportunities, such as remarketing lists, promotion of newsletter subscriptions, content optimization for search engines, etc.

A bunch of blog and social networks works, for example, like this: you publish a post on a topic that is interesting to your consumer and is related to the product. Link to the post is placed in social media, and, in different iterations (text on Facebook, infographics in interest, photography in instagram ...). The same link is sent to the weekly newsletter. During the entire time, the remarketing code collects data from the blog about visitors who you are using closer and closer to your product with the help of targeted advertising in social networks.

As a result, you are growing traffic from social networks and search, conversion of readers into subscribers, the credibility of the brand as an expert.
