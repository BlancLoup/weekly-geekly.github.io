Windows 8 Summit - the first partner conference on Windows 8

I think it‚Äôs not a secret to anyone that very soon Windows 8 will hit the stores along with new devices (the official launch is scheduled for October 26). It would seem that even more than a month ... however, as you may have noticed, a lot of preparatory work is already underway in the software development industry and many companies and individual developers are actively working on their solutions and suggestions for Windows 8.



We decided that it would be very useful to collect them all in one place on the eve of the launch, so that they could share their experience, plans and vision of the market development in the near future. To make a wish become a reality, at the end of October, a few days before the launch of the G8, we are conducting a Windows 8 Summit.

Windows 8 Summit is the first partner conference on Windows 8, in which, as the name suggests, the main role will be given not to Microsoft, but to our favorite partners, who from their experience will tell not only about how great everything is, but also about the rake , pitfalls and other difficulties of our imperfect world :). The conference is focused on the business audience, entrepreneurs, startups and professional developers.

The conference will include two separate tracks: a business track covering all stages of creating applications - market analysis, design, development, monetization and promotion; technical track for developers who already know the basics of developing under Windows 8. At the exhibition of partner solutions you will be able to informally communicate with experts and ask all your questions.

Conference site and registration - windows8summit.ru
Date - October 23
Venue - Moscow, Holiday Inn Sokolniki
