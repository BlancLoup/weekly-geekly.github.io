Visa will release a program for Android

In the depths of the Visa company, an Android program is being developed that will help Visa cardholders to find the nearest ATM or bank branch where they can withdraw or transfer funds, although this functionality may not appear right away. The first will be implemented account management and reporting on transactions with the account. The first to get access to these services will be Chase Visa cardholders.
