DevCon Information
DevCon is a series of annual large-scale Microsoft software development conferences. The conference is attended by leading industry experts and developers of food teams from Redmond. The two-day DevCon'12 program includes a plenary session, 50 technical reports, workshops, round tables, the Ask an Expert zone, a partner exhibition, and various interactive events. The main topics of the conference will be:
  • Customer development;
  • Mobile development;
  • Web development;
  • Cloud computing;
  • Development tools and software lifecycle management;
  • Development technologies and programming languages;
  • Corporate development;
  • Interaction with other platforms and technologies.
DevCon'11 conference, which took place in May 2011, gathered over 650 professionals in the field of software development for 2 days of its work, and the number of online listeners about the present and future development of the Microsoft platform was about 30 thousand people.
