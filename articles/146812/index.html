Selling photos from Instagram



Instagram is said to be a service for hipsters who use only a couple of filters. Indeed, many people have enough of it, but even a monkey can get one for a thousand shots by chance, from which you can not take your eyes off. Such a picture that you want to hang on the wall in the room. And you can even make money if other people like this work and they will order it on a real canvas.



The idea was clearly in the air, because at least two services appeared on the Western Internet that offer this service - printing photos from Instagram on canvas. Both services pay fees to photographers whose work has been printed.



Instacanv.as prints paintings in three sizes: 50x50 cm ($ 80), 40x40 cm ($ 60) and 30x30 cm ($ 40). Shipping to the US and Western Europe is free, shipping to the rest of the world costs $ 49 for the first picture and $ 25 for the next. Photographers are encouraged to create their own galleries on the site with a personal URL, they receive a reward of 20% of the order value (via Paypal). This service specializes only on Instagram photos.

Another Instaprints site is actually a mirror of Fine Art America - these guys print anything on any materials: canvas, cardboard, acrylic, cards, posters, and so on. The cost of each work is set by the author himself: the company charges a certain base price for its services (for example, for a 20x20 cm square canvas it is $ 25), and the author receives the rest. Artists' fees are paid on the 15th of each month through Paypal.
