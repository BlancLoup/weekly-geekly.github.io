Detailed review of the service: "Rent online store"

Rent online stores. What it is? Advantages over CMS. Contract-offer to use the service. What you need to know. Customer support. Additional services. Possible problems. Company overview (, )

The service of renting online stores in the CIS countries is just beginning to develop. As representatives of the service, most companies offer a boxed version with the possibility of renting for any period, plus additional services. In the west, this service has grown into a more advanced offer called Software as a Service * - software as a service, which implies the outsourcing of the technical component of the store (an article on the topic of SaaS will be written soon).

Rent a store - one of the branches of the evolution of online commerce. I believe that now any company entering the e-commerce market should begin its journey by renting an online store. For small businessmen (a shop in a city, or a beginner) and an average (several shops with a good turnover), access to the Internet is accompanied by certain risks: the budget has a certain framework, so it can be tough to compete with market leaders. In this case, there is always the likelihood that the business will be unprofitable (or the profitability will be too small).

According to tenant companies, businessmen with offline business become customers, aspiring entrepreneurs, and there are more cases when businessmen cut down or turn off offline activities and go online.



Wanting to open an online store, businessmen often have no idea about the technical component, design, promotion. They know their product, how to sell it and how to keep accounts. Such clients want to give technical issues in one professional hands. The output for them can be two options:

1. Find a web agency that, for a certain amount, will make an online store with an original design, develop a corporate identity and promote the store. Often the amount ranges from 2000 to 5000 USD

2. Rent a shop. In this case, the technical component, design and promotion fall on the shoulders of the provider. At the same time, the cost of rental services and additional services is reduced significantly.

Buying a CMS and searching for artists on programming, design and promotion is suitable only for experienced online commerce owners.

Renting an online store, a businessman has the opportunity to test future business and virtually eliminates large material losses in case of failure.
‚ÄúThe most important thing is that the buyer of the store doesn‚Äôt care whether the rented solution is from the seller or not. Outwardly, this distinction cannot be noticed, "- says Pavel Korotov, chief editor of Turnover

What are the advantages of the store rental service:
1. Payment is extended in time and does not require large material investments at the start. It is like a loan: it is often more pleasant to pay a loan more, but gradually, than a large amount at once.

2. Carry out all the necessary technical measures, as well as help with the content and advertising of the store can the support service of the tenant for a fee. Some technical activities are included in the rental price. This solves the problem of finding other contractors.

3. ‚ÄúIn addition, the rented store is almost immediately ready for work, you just need to add the product and call the wallet number. While buying a boxed CMS, it must also be installed, set up and screwed in the design, ‚Äùadds Alexey Leonov, executive director of Shop-rent.ru

Alexandra advises to clarify in advance the availability of methods for unloading goods and transactions either into another system or in the .csv format.

3. The dependence of the success of your business on the competence of the service provider. The provider may not master your dynamic development, which may require changing it or hiring third parties. Change is accompanied by certain risks, sm. 1 and 2.

4. Contractors can be changed only in advertising and design. The search for new can take a long time and there is a risk that the people found will be irresponsible. But providers often limit third-party access to the store, which can result in numerous problems.

Need to know

Even in spite of the fact that at the end of the article almost all rental conditions were laid out with which tenant‚Äôs websites were supplied, it is necessary to check the information personally and ask questions to the support service:
