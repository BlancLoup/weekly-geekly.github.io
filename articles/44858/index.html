In Runet came the fashion for non-click sites?

During the autumn, two projects for large brands, made with no-click technology, were developed at once in RuNet. Navigation on them does not require a click - the elements are adjusted to the movement of the cursor.

1. VIERA (Panasonic TVs) from ADV / web-engineering



2. Lexus RX350 from Brand Studio



Let's tell a little about the technology itself. It appeared quite a long time ago, and its ‚Äúideological base‚Äù was the http://dontclick.it/ resource, which details the essence of technology, examples, and the emotional background.


Technology, or rather, the principle, don`tClick, seems to be becoming "fashionable" in RuNet, and adopted by leading designers. Let's try to analyze the main advantages and disadvantages:

Benefits:

- Nonclick sites look unusual, therefore they attract attention (both fans and opponents).
- Due to the "slip" on the site without clicks, you can achieve a sense of dynamics and continuous interaction with the site.
- Due to the constant ‚Äúconfrontation with myself‚Äù, with the desire to press somewhere, a great memorability of the project and its contents is achieved.
- Fan. Everything unusual is funny, and if it is properly used, the user will receive positive emotions.

Disadvantages:

- Of course, the main drawback - the inconvenience (unusual) navigation, which many complain. Habit is second nature, and many users are not able to restrain negative emotions when the interface reacts differently than they expect.
- Restrictions. Still, with a click you can do more than without it. Pretty obvious fact))
- Melteshenie and fuzzy definition of "hot spot". When clicking a user can gently move the cursor, "aim" - and click. And when responding to onMouseOver, the interface may begin to react before you are going to do anything.

Summarizing these brief thoughts, we can say that technology has the right to life, but given the limitations of its application. At the moment, it is quite difficult to imagine a functional mass service made with its use. However, on promotional sites containing a large amount of presentation media content, don`tClick can be a good highlight, forming the necessary emotional coloring.
