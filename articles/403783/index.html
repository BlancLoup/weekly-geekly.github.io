Instrument Settings



The functionality of the imager is very different from previous models, greatly expanding the scope of application.

The most important setting is the emission factor. Only predefined options are available, the ability to specify an arbitrary value was not available. Perhaps this will be fixed in the future in the new application.

Possible options:

1. matte - 0.97;
2. semi-gloss - 0.80;
3. semi-gloss - 0.60;
4. glossy - 0.30.

Depending on the choice will vary. By lowering the emission factor, you can increase the maximum measured heat. For example, setting 0.3 instead of 0.9, you can get a maximum of 990 ¬∞ instead of 330 ¬∞. Then calculate the real value.
