Veeam Academy - practical classes for beginners C # developers


Registration for the course is open until August 31.

In the fall of 2017, Veeam Software launches its first educational project: a course for C # developers.

Let's not hide the fact that, like many companies that conduct their own training programs, we do this in order to attract talented developers to our team that are still at the beginning of their careers. Focusing on the dynamics with which the development of the career of developers Veeam, we select specialists with great potential for professional growth.

We offer not just the opportunity to get free training - after the end of the course, successful participants will have a real chance to get a job in the company and become part of the Veeam Research & Development team.

For whom the course


In short: The course is aimed at young professionals with basic knowledge of C #.

Many young professionals are familiar with the situation: companies do not consider candidates without development experience, but this experience can be obtained only by finding a job and having worked in real projects. To get out of the "vicious circle" we have to settle down on the principle of "where will they take", and not "where it is interesting."

And if it is not yet clear what is interesting? In this case, you should try a little bit of everything. We suggest gaining experience in team development of a project using modern Microsoft technologies (these are Entity Framework, WCF, WPF, etc.).

As they say, the appetite comes with eating. Acquainted with various techniques, you can decide what you like more. In addition, after training, all developments remain in the listener's personal portfolio (which is extremely useful in finding employment).
In our course, we focus mainly on the development of practical skills of developing industrial software by students, and also pay attention to teamwork.

Program and format of classes


In short: For 2 months of teamwork on the principles of Agile, participants will develop their own project using modern programming technologies. Full-time classes, 3 times a week in the evening. Training is free for all participants.

We will build the entire course around the development of a pilot project.
The pilot project does not imply further use of the turnkey solution in Veeam products. Therefore, his topic is chosen by the course participants collectively at the beginning of the course. This could be, for example, a social network, a media server, a version control system or something else.

Course duration is two months. Classes will be held weekly (3 times a week) for 4 hours in the evening at one of the Veeam offices in St. Petersburg, near the Ploshchad Lenina metro station. The group will train no more than 15 people. The course is free for all participants.

Classes are held in the form of lectures and practical work. The course instructor - Dmitry Shalymov, Ph.D. in Physics and Mathematics, MCSD, has more than 10 years experience in software development.


Dmitry Shalymov, leading the course.

What awaits listeners Veeam Academy:

- obtaining practical skills of programming and industrial development of boxed software products,
- the development of modern programming technologies, the study of trends in the language C # (C # 7 and. Net Framework 4.7),
- the practice of writing fault-tolerant program code,
- study of the development of multi-tier architecture of high-load distributed software systems,
- mentoring guest development experts from the Veeam team,
- obtaining skills in accordance with the methodology of Agile,
- personal effectiveness training, dedicated to the development of Soft Skills,
- Certificate of completion for all course participants.

Among other things, we are ready to consider graduates of the Academy as candidates for Veeam Software vacancies.
