Virtual Conference VORT-EX 08

On November 18 and 19, from 16.00 to 21.00 Moscow time, the virtual Microsoft conference for web developers VORT-EX 08 will take place.

The conference is broadcast on the Internet and in order to participate in it is enough to register at www.vort-ex.com , after which you can listen to reports of Microsoft experts and invited experts from other companies on the subject of web development, web design and business in The Internet.

The conference is held in English, so you can not only get new knowledge, but also practice your understanding of English.

In addition, to add joy to the event, participants will have the opportunity to download free Windows Web Server 2008, as well as all registered participants automatically participate in the gift raffle (mobile phones, webcams, books from Microsoft Press, Zune players). So there is a chance not only to learn about something new, but also to get a useful souvenir in memory.

Register, watch the online conference and write reviews - it is important for us to know whether you like these events, what format suits you best and what topics to discuss in such virtual conferences.
