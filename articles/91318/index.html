Russian overclockers first in 3DMark03

Yesterday, the OCClub.ru team consisting of Smoke , slamms , DeDaL and Lex_ set an absolute world record in the popular 3DMark03 graphics benchmark.



For this we have assembled the following test bench:

- EVGA X58 Classified 762 4-Way SLI motherboard;
- Intel Core i7 980X processor (Gulftown Q3FE ES);
- 4 MSI 5870 Lightning bios Modded video cards;
- RAM DDR3 3x2Gb Corsair GTX2 2250MHz CL8 1.65V;
- Nitrogen cup on the processor Kingpin F1EE;
- 4 nitrogen glasses on the video card Tek9 LN2 slim 5.0;
- power supply Antec TPQ-1200W and Enermax Revolution 1250W;
- cooling 160 l of liquid nitrogen;
- Windows 7 operating system.

The result was 206455 3DMarks . Hall of Fame Futuremark 3DMark03



This is the first experiment in Russia, when overclockers put four video cards on nitrogen at once, and taking into account the cooling of the processor with liquid nitrogen, five glasses were put on the system at once.



It is very difficult to monitor the temperature, as it constantly grows on each of the five glasses. But the hands in such a nitrogen pair are very cold.



The temperature on the processor was -192 degrees Celsius, on the video cards -130 degrees Celsius.



The processor worked stably at 6100 MHz, and 4 video cards were overclocked to 1350/1280 MHz.



The system is frozen very quickly, and there are literally a couple of hours to get a result. After that, the stand turns into an icing.



Even wrapped all the towels, we could not oppose the cold.













From left to right: Lex_ , DeDaL , slamms , Smoke .
