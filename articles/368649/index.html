In the US, quadcopters are registered by the owners themselves. Russia

According to the Air Code of Russia (VC RF), a quadrocopter is an aircraft, like a Boeing or Superjet. Flight authorization issued RosAvatsiya. From March 30, the applicant is required to submit a flight plan and a pilot's license. That is, to launch any radio-controlled toy weighing 250 grams will entrust only a person with a touch of at least 50 hours, including night landings and instrument control.

Work drone pilot in the United States. Prospects and realities


After the UAVs were integrated into the US airspace system, in just three years, 70,000 new jobs were created, directly or indirectly related to the UAV industry. According to estimates of specialists, after a year or two, the active use of drones for commercial purposes will make it possible to create an additional more than 100,000 new jobs. In this fairly commercial niche, professional drone pilots today receive a salary of up to $ 50 an hour or more. Therefore, the United States Federal Aviation Administration considers in detail all possible commercial ways to use them.

The number of areas where UAV capabilities can be used is growing in proportion to the technical development of the industry itself. For the assembly of drones, training of operators, directly for piloting for industrial purposes and further maintenance of the park requires a sufficiently high level of qualification. One of the most promising markets, where the capabilities of UAVs can be increasingly used in the very near future - precision farming. The ability to obtain operational information from the sky will allow farmers to optimize the process of growing crops and spraying methods, reducing the amount of fertilizer used.

The economic effect from the use of drones in agriculture in the United States at the end of 2015 amounted to $ 2 billion. Other promising areas where the copters could be successfully used are weather monitoring, cartography, oil and gas exploration, telecommunications, monitoring of habitats of wild animals, disaster relief, mountain aviation, cinema, sports broadcasting, the advertising industry and, of course, cargo delivery. Given the fact that today there are specialties that 25 years ago no one suspected, the possibilities listed are only the tip of the iceberg.

At the moment, American universities and colleges are already offering their first UAVs - educational elective programs, which differ in cost and direction in a wide range.

The starting salary of the drone pilot in the United States, according to data provided by the official website of the school of pilots , ranges from $ 50 to $ 60 thousand per year. The average is approaching $ 100,000. At the same time, it is highly likely that, operating outside the US, the drone operator will be able to receive significantly higher salaries.
