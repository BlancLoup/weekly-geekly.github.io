RCC 2014 Warmup: combat test for sports programmers



IT world froze in anticipation. From day to day, the regular season of the largest Russian Olympiad on sports programming Russian Code Cup starts. Ahead of the new interesting and non-trivial tasks, uncompromising struggle and great prizes.

On April 17, at 19:30 Moscow time, a training round of the Olympiad will be held at the site forces.ru with a fresh batch of tasks from the RCC organizers.

RCC 2014 Warmup is a test that will give beginners the opportunity to try their hand and understand what to prepare for during the championship. And for "experienced" participants this is an ideal opportunity to practice and warm up before the first battles of the RCC 2014!

The first qualifying round will take place on April 19, the second on May 18, the third on May 24. Moreover, programmers who did not pass the qualification from the first time will be able to try their hand in the next rounds.

In order to take part in the Russian Code Cup, you need to register on the site http://russiancodecup.ru/ (registration will be open before the start of the third qualifying round).

You can familiarize yourself with examples of tasks, learn detailed rules for holding the Russian Code Cup, and also see photo reports from past Olympiads here .
