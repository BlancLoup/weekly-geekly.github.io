Updated Raspberry Pi with 512 MB of RAM at the same price



Now on the market for computer devices represented a lot of mini-PCs. But the most famous mini-computer is without a doubt the Raspberry Pi. As you know, the cost of this device is 35 cu, and this is a full-fledged PC that is capable of performing a wide range of tasks. If you were planning to purchase a Raspberry Pi, but have not yet done so, it's time to order an updated model. The developers now offer a model with 512 MB of RAM instead of 256, at the same price of $ 35.

Recently, the founder of the Raspberry Pi Foundation, Eben Upton, said in an interview that there is no decent competition for his computer. Yes, now there are a lot of minicomputers that are similar to the Raspberry Pi. But among them there are practically no models whose price is 25-35 US dollars. Most of the similar models have a price of up to $ 200, and this allows us not to consider such devices as competitive Raspberry Pi.

Now the Raspberry Pi Foundation plans to advertise its device in developing countries, not as an auxiliary device, but as a main PC. And yes, now Raspberry Pi is used to solve a wide range of tasks, it is even more than the developers had planned. Both ordinary users and large companies pay close attention to the Raspberry Pi.

I would like to think that in the future developers will update the device configuration without increasing the price. Now the Model B Raspberry Pi with 512 MB will become a standard model, the cost of which, as mentioned above, is 35 USD And Model A, as before, will be sold at $ 25, because there is no possibility of connecting to the Internet.
