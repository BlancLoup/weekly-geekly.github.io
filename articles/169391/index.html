Sony Xperia Z sales start

As announced today, Sony Xperia Z will go on sale in the 20th of February at a price of 29.990 rubles.



Sony Xperia Z Specs

Display: 5-inch screen 1080 x 1920 Full HD Reality Display with Mobile BRAVIA Engine 2 technology
Processor: asynchronous quad-core Snapdragon S4 Qualcomm APQ8064 frequency of 1.5 GHz
OS: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) with the subsequent upgrade to 4.2.
Memory: 2 GB RAM, internal flash memory - up to 16 GB, microSD support up to 32 GB
Camera: 13 MP with Exmor RS matrix for mobile devices, support for HDR video, Superior Auto mode and noise reduction, thanks to which it is possible to obtain crystal clear images and video in any conditions
Front camera: 2 MP with Exmor R matrix
Battery: 2330 mAh, Battery STAMINA mode, increasing the standby time at least 4 times
