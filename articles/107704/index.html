Bada Developer Challenge: Russia

So, today is November 8, which means that tomorrow the contestants of the Russian version of the Samsung bada Developer Challenge , with a prize fund of $ 255,000, have the last day to hand over their applications ported to the phone for their final evaluation by the panel of judges. Under Habrakat - a selection of videos with some applications from this competition (Caution, 16 + 1 video).

Space hell




The game combines elements characteristic of both 3D and 2D games. According to the author, a high level of physics modeling allowed us to create an interesting, interactive environment. The protagonist of the game can move and destroy items with which he encounters on his way. This can lead for example to the fact that the hero can destroy the bridge under which there are enemies - falling, the bridge will destroy them. Much attention in the game was paid to the details of the design level and the character itself.

Little sailor




The game "Little Sailor" - a fully three-dimensional simulator of navigation.

Deaf translator




The program is also an assistant in the study of sign language, and a tool to help communicate with each other deaf or people with impaired hearing.

Photopickup




This program is an alternative to the standard manager for viewing photos and drawings on the bada platform. It has the ability to upload images to facebook, as well as the ability to provide information about the image for almost every taste.

badaSports




As the name implies, this program is intended for athletes. The functionality is huge - the program allows you to track your movements using a GPS navigator, as well as to register almost any other indicators of sports activities. Naturally, all this can be saved for future detailed study, as well as for posting on facebook or twitter.

Insect collecting




Augmented reality game using a camera and a geomagnetic sensor. Look around and catch insects with your phone.

sMOVE




The sMOVE app is another version of a personal companion for people leading an active lifestyle.

TouchRetouch




Extremely utilitarian and at the same time extremely necessary application for anyone who prefers the camera of his smartphone to an ordinary camera. With it, you can remove unwanted objects from any photo, like the Clone Stamp in Photoshop, with just a few movements. The user is only required to mark the necessary areas in the photo, the program will do the rest for him.

Handypaint




In fact, this is a normal, but high-quality Paint. All the necessary tools and even a little more in stock.

Beatplayer




BeatPlayer is a music player with musical instruments that includes:
  • Music player
  • Piano
  • Drums
  • Harmonica
  • Different funny sounds

Thumpies




Thumpies is a rhythm based music game. for any ages. By correctly pointing the rhythm of the jumping beaters, the player moves through the song. If a player presses a target on time, the points meter rises a little, but if the player misses or doesn‚Äôt hit on time, the mark drops. Unlike other similar games, Thumpies uses the jumping-object mechanism instead of the usual series of notes.

Feeling Music Player




Feeling Music Player is a new service for recommending music based on the user's emotional state. The application analyzes the music in real time through the lyrics of the song and the factors of sound resources - tempo and rhythm. Subsequently, the user will be offered a musical composition corresponding to his mood.

RaggedEdge




RaggedEdge is a futuristic racing game that takes place in space. Competitions are held at extremely high speeds from 600 to 800 km / h.

Trip journal




Trip Journal is an application for tourists who plan their trips in advance. In addition to the planning itself, the application allows you to keep a trip diary with notes and photos.

ForMyBabyApp




The ForMyBabyApp application consists of five utilities that are designed for children: Flash Cards, Dial a Phone, Letter-board, Color Piano and Quizzing.

Wamo camera




Wamo Camera is the most powerful and professional camera utility for the Samsung Wave smartphone. This application will be able to expand the functionality of the camera, compared to the available default. Wamo Camera contains 5 modes and 10 effects, supports 4x zoom, auto-white balance, flash, autofocus and photo album.
