HTC One max officially presented

The One smartphone lineup officially added a new representative - 5.9 "HTC One max! HTC One max inherited the original design of the HTC One, as well as the Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 hardware platform with a quad-core 1.7 GHz processor. Super LCD3 technology coated with impact resistant Gorilla Glass 3.

In addition to 2 GB of RAM, the device comes with 16 GB of internal memory and a slot for microSD memory cards. In addition, all owners of One max will be provided 50 GB in the online service Google Drive for a period of two years. It is also worth noting the battery capacity of the device - 3300 mAh, which can be expanded with a removable cover for another 1150 mAh with almost no increase in the volume of the device.




The HTC One max has an ultra-pixel camera with a BSI-sensor and a flash with five levels of intensity. The front 2.1 MPks camera device is equipped with a wide-angle lens and is able to shoot HDR. In combination with a large display, the multimedia capabilities of the smartphone complement the front stereo speakers with support for HTC BoomSound technology. Wireless interfaces, in addition to the infrared port at the top of the device, are NFC, Bluetooth 4.0 and WiFi 802.11 ac / n / g, as well as LTE.

Specifications




Perhaps one of the most interesting innovations of the smartphone - a fingerprint scanner, which is located on the back of the device. The smartphone software allows you to customize the definition of any three fingerprints and assign one of the actions as a reaction, whether it is unlocking the phone or launching the camera. To activate the scanner, you need to make the swipe from top to bottom with your desired finger.

Positive changes occurred with the ergonomics of the smartphone: the Power button, as one of the frequently used ones, moved to the right edge under the volume rocker. The back cover is removable and can be opened with a button on the left side of the device, but the battery is not available. Dimensions, as befits a smartphone of this class, are not modest: 164.5 x 82.5 x 10.29 mm and weighs 217 grams.

The case of Power Flip Case can not only serve as a kind of stand, but also provides additional capacity to the smartphone battery. Moreover, it practically does not add volume, since The battery is located in the front cover of the case and is flexible. The case charges along with the smartphone while it is being charged and charges the device during everyday work.



The smartphone runs on Android 4.3 and HTC Sense 5.5. Start of sales is expected in mid-October, the price will be announced later.
