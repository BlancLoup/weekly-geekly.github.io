Social service Katushkin.ru - find a company for skiing

The whole May is in good weather, which means that it‚Äôs time to get the bikes or rollers out of the garage and open the summer season, if, of course, you have not done it yet.

I want to tell you about our project - Katushkin.ru , a social service that allows you to find a company to ride on something, be it a bicycle in summer, snowboard in winter or skates all year round.




How Katushkin helps to find a company


After registering, where you always indicate what you are riding and where (in big cities - up to the district), you get to your profile. Here are your stats and merit, vehicles, photos and interests.



If you pull the card from the top, you can see who is around, what events are planned, where you can ride around. These are the three main components of the site - katalza, coils and places. They are presented as sections in the menu on the left.



It is very important that all information on the site is displayed in connection with your interests and geolocation. If you ride only a bicycle, then you will only see cyclists, bicycle reels and bicycle riding places, if you are in Kostroma, then only Kostroma.



Preferences can be easily changed if you suddenly become interested in the rollers and stop cycling, and next to adjust the display area, narrowing down to the area, if you prefer not to ride long distances; or expanding to the whole area, if you do not care.



In the section Catalza you see those who are online or have recently visited. You can select people by age or search by nickname.



Coils - a key section of the site. Here they arrange meetings, exchange impressions and photos. To join any coil, simply turn on the switch "I will!".



Coils can be sorted, for example, by popularity, to learn about the most significant upcoming events:



By clicking on the coil you can see the route and details, discuss the nuances and, upon completion, download the photo report.



You can join someone else's coils, and you can create your own - be it a regular walk around the area or a weekend hike. It is very simple:



About this coil catalans are notified of the areas through which its route is laid. This happens through email and mobile applications for smartphones.



The places that are used to build the coil route are in the section of the same name. If you often ride in any park, it makes sense to subscribe to it and be aware of new coils or catalans in this park.



On the main page summarizes all the events for which you are subscribed and displayed in one tape.



So Katushkin helps to find a company for skiing.
