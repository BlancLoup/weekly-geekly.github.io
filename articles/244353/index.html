Every week on Mondays and Wednesdays on iTunes U (for iOS devices and for Mac) lectures of the main course were laid out, and every Friday - an additional lecture on the topic related to iOS devices (they are optional, but strongly recommended for viewing). Every Wednesday - homework (Assignment), which was assigned a week (until the next Wednesday).
