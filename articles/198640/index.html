Yate5 released

Congratulations to everyone who uses YATE as their IP telephony!

On October 21, I received a letter from the newsletter that an updated version of YATE was released.

There is no list of changes yet, but the letter says that IPv6 support was added for sip (which they were very pleased with) and JavaScript support was improved. Diameter support has been added to the corporate (private) version to further develop VoLTE (Voice over LTE) support.
