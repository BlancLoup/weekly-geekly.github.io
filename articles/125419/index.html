Nokia is changing the phone designation system



Finnish mobile phone maker Nokia decided to change the identification system of their devices. Now the main rule will be the following: the model will receive only a numeric identifier in the range from 100 to 999, with the first digit indicating the device class - the smaller the number, the smaller the class, respectively, and vice versa; the second two digits will indicate the model number. Thus, if you read and hear about the Nokia 100 phone, now it will be possible to form an opinion on the level of this model without considering the device itself.

The reasons for the change in the naming principle are confusion - Nokia believes that the comparison of the Nokia C7 and Nokia X7 is difficult for users, as well as the fact that people tend to evaluate the advantages of the phone model by the number in its naming, I will get more. ‚Äù

Previously, Nokia has already used only the digital name for its products, which consisted of four digits.
