Evernote surpasses 100 million users



We are pleased to announce that Evernote now has over 100 million users.





One hundred million pioneers


When we launched the Evernote service in 2008, our intuition told us that the workflow of people around the world would soon change significantly. At that time, there were already a lot of tools for creating notes and productivity tools, but they all seemed outdated. They had less and less attitude to the real life of people.

We decided to rethink the concept of productivity for modern business people, and we want Evernote to become a workspace that is not limited to a specific place, device or time. And our users are also not geographically limited. This is where the first hundred million people came to us:



One of the most interesting moments in the first stage of Evernote growth is that our first one hundred million users overwhelmingly discovered our products for themselves on their own, without pushing from the outside. We spent a very small amount of money on the promotion of our products. In fact, total advertising costs amounted to less than one cent per user.



We are extremely grateful to everyone who helped us go so far. Thank you for being and stay with us!
