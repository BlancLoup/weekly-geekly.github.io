Online Hackathon: creating apps for SharePoint Online



Hi, Habr! I want to share information about the online hackathon for specialists in automating business processes.

Who organizes the competition


The competition is held on the new specialized platform Contestars.com, and it was organized by the IzhDevCom developer community, whose main goal is to improve the skills of IT specialists and provide opportunities for the development of technology start-ups.

The terms of participation


To participate in the competition, it is necessary to develop an application for the SharePoint Online system from April 16 to 30. The main condition is that the project should solve real business problems. In the context of this competition, document management automation or database search optimization is good, weather widgets are bad.

To enter the competition, you need to develop an application for SharePoint Online, and tell the details about it using the form on this page . You can also send to the contest those applications that were developed to you earlier, but have not yet been used ‚Äúin production‚Äù. At the first stage of the competition, an expert jury will select the most interesting projects - the short-list will be published no later than May 10.

Among the jury members are appraisers, SharePoint experts, professional engineers, IT marketers, and representatives of the Habrahabr community.

What will the finalists get


The creators of truly useful applications will have the opportunity to be interviewed in companies whose representatives are members of the IzhDevCom community. The finalists, whose works will be selected by the jury, will receive detailed comments on the pros and cons of their projects, as well as recommendations for further professional development.

Participation in the online hackathon is free; all you need to do is send the contest information using the form on this page .

Looking forward to your work!
