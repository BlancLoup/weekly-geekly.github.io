GLONASS will be made as accurate as the GPS navigation system.



The President of the Russian Federation instructed Roskomos to make the GLONASS navigation satellite system as accurate as the American GPS. In order to achieve this result, the network operator will have to reduce the error in determining the coordinates three times. And for this, a new generation of Glonass-K2 spacecraft (SC) must be launched into orbit. These systems are able to take into account the interference that occurs when a signal passes through the upper atmosphere.

The order for GLONASS designers, President Vladimir Putin was given in September 2017, at a meeting of the Military-Industrial Commission of the Russian Federation. All this time, the designers have been working on the creation of a new generation of spacecraft. The first of them will be Glonass-K2, which is being developed at the ISS.
