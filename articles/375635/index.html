Sid Meier's Starships: a step-by-step interstellar strategy from the author of "Civilization"



If you like strategies and endless space, the new game from the veteran igrostroy, called " Sid Meier's Starships ", is just for you. The other day the toy was announced by the developers. The player gets the opportunity to explore the entire universe.

Gamers will be able to create planetary federations, explore the most remote corners of the galaxy, gradually expand their influence by developing new technologies. There is also an opportunity to conduct step-by-step space battles - the influence of a certain clan or civilization will depend on the outcome of such battles.



The user can control the fleet of space ships, directing them to different locations to complete missions. In particular, it will be necessary to protect the planets and the inhabitants of these planets, to rebuild a planetary federation. Ships and their arsenal can be customized, and the appearance of game objects can be seen in the screenshots below.

According to Meyer, the new game is like the next head of Civilization: Beyond Earth, the future inhabitants of the latest version of Civilization. What happens after the new house is mastered, and when will the new spacecraft be built to explore the Universe? What happens to lost brothers and sisters on Earth? "

The goal of the development, according to Meyers, was to create a game space where space battles can be held, full of adventure, diplomacy and space exploration.

A new game should appear in the spring, versions for PC, Mac, iPad will be available. On the availability of a version for Android devices, while nothing is said.
