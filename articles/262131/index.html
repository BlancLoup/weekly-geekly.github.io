How to pay for training with VMware PSO credits



Not everyone knows, but many have heard about VMware training points (PSO credits). In essence, this is an alternative way to pay for VMware training. Points provide an opportunity to plan training throughout the year, which contributes to the rational management of costs.

How to get?

As a rule, at the conclusion of a transaction for the purchase of licenses (ELA) it is imperative to purchase PSO credits. The amount depends on the size of your transaction.

PSO credits are valid for 1 year. 1 PSO credit = $ 100. The greater the number of PSO credits, the greater the size of the discount provided.

After registration is completed, a system message will appear indicating that your application has been sent to the training center that has been selected. Once your participation has been confirmed, you can attend the course.
