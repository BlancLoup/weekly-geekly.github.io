Zillion adventures (mini-review)

I already wrote about how easy it is to develop your own game using Zillions of Games , but I did not mention one important consequence of this fact. The set of applications for ZoG is not limited to 375 games and puzzles that are included in the distribution kit. There are really a lot of games developed for Zillions of Games. Dozens of enthusiasts from around the world add to this list using both ZRF and native extensions. To date, its size has already exceeded 2000.

Of course, I can not tell you about everything that was implemented in the framework of the ZoG project. Today, I will try to tell about the applications that seemed to me especially interesting.

There are many variants of Japanese Shogi , good and different. The Japanese, perhaps, were the most inventive in the development of a variety of chess games. Of course, Shogi did not remain deprived of the attention of the ZoG community. Beginners can be advised a set of options for the game with different types of "odds", traditionally provided to a weaker player. There are also options for playing mini-boards.
