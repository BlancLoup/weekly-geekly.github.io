Google glasses are banned in Ukraine

The Security Service of Ukraine (SBU) banned the use of Google glasses. Official explanations on this issue were published earlier by the Segodnya newspaper. Buyers of glasses with a built-in video camera and other spyware devices will be held criminally liable .

UPD: In the comments they write that the ban applies to all members of the Customs Union.
