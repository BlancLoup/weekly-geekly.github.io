Useful materials on SAN

The purpose of writing the article is to help in finding the necessary materials for learning storage networks, and is aimed at beginners. Each of us is faced with the fact that in mastering a new area, it is necessary to form a basis, a foundation on which subsequent knowledge will be based. It is often not clear where and how to start, what is necessary and what is not. It takes a lot of time to search, selection of materials that satisfy our needs.
I bring to your attention a selection of useful and accessible materials on data storage networks.



  1. The book "The Basics of SAN Design" by Josh Judd.
  2. Presentations of the seminar "Brocade SAN for beginners - just about the complicated":
    Gregory Nikonov. Fundamentals of the theory of Brocade SAN. part 1
    Gregory Nikonov. Fundamentals of the theory of Brocade SAN. part 2
  3. SAN Design and Best Practices Guide
  4. Course Brocade FC-101. FC Introduction and Basics (Available after registering with Brocade.com)
  5. Small informational materials about Brocade technologies and products:
    Brocade SAN 2014 , Brocade SAN 2015 , Brocade SAN 2016 .
  6. Fabric OS manuals.
  7. Introduction to Storage Area Networks
  8. IBM SAN Survival Guide
  9. IBM / Cisco Multiprotocol Routing: An Introduction and Implementation
  10. NETAPP TECHNICAL REPORT. Best practices for building FC SAN
  11. EMC. Fiber Channel SAN Topologies.
  12. EMC. Networked Storage Concepts and Protocols
  13. Storage Area Networks For Dummies, 2nd Edition. Christopher Poelker and Alex Nikitin.
  14. Data storage systems in Windows. Server storage technologies in Windows 2000 and Windows Server 2003. Dilip Nike.
  15. Storage Networks. Mark Farley.
  16. Brocade service engineer Grigory Nikonov's page on youtube .
  17. KorP`s blog - conveniently organized materials on SAN .
