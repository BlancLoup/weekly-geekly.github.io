Warning Search: Spacecraft DX1

Finally, our long-awaited event is close to accomplishment - the launch of the DX1 spacecraft on Tuesday, July 8th. We would like to ask for help from all radio amateurs who are interested in receiving signals from spacecraft. We suggest you to take part in the "catching" of our satellite immediately after the start.



Start will be at 19:58 Moscow time from Baikonur. Based on the parameters of the orbit, the first successful communication session is expected after 3 hours over Eastern Europe, and then the satellite will return to Russia only after a few turns in a few hours from the Far East. Therefore, our MCC in Moscow will be able to hold the first communication session only the next day. The satellite beacon broadcasts in amateur radio frequency, so everyone will have the opportunity to hear it before us. Moreover, such assistance is necessary for us to clarify its orbit and confirm its efficiency. Therefore, our gratitude will be supported by memorable souvenirs for lucky catchers from around the world.
