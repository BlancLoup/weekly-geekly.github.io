Title


Already a lot of jokes were born, about the changes in the name of the composition. If you try to download a song with modified or erased metadata , then the download window will report an error. I dare to suggest that even if you change the name of a song on VKontakte, it will still be blocked. So, it makes no sense to change the name of the song.

Content


Now it is obvious that the content of the audio files is checked. There are many mixes of different tracks, which, unlike the original ones, are not blocked. For happiness, it remains only to learn the algorithm for checking the songs for originality. The first thing that came to mind was to cut the silence at the end and try to load it. This, in my opinion, is the most harmless thing that can be done with an audio file. Oddly enough - it did not help. It is understandable that in many audio files there is a long and unnecessary silence at the end. And it would be foolish to leave the opportunity to simply cut it. Just in case, I tried to cut the silence in the beginning, which also did not bring success.
It became known that the file is checked only in the main part - without silence at the beginning and at the end. It is very sad to cut a piece from your favorite song. Therefore, you need to know the minimum cut off. In this case, you can change the record so that the human ear and not notice. For example, select two vertices and remove the gap between them.



The program on the screen.
Sony Sound Forge 10 was used.

After a series of experiments with cutting and loading songs, it was found that for profit it is enough to cut out about 3-5 milliseconds from the main part of the music. Most likely, the audio file is greatly ‚Äúsimplified‚Äù and compared with the same patterns of original songs.

Quality


It turned out that you can get around the error in another way, without cutting anything. It is enough to slightly lower the quality of the recording.
To solve this problem, I identified 2 approaches:
  • Convert an audio file a couple of times with the same quality parameters as the current ones.
  • To drastically reduce the quality of the recording, setting the parameters in the converter is worse than the current ones.

As a result, it is possible to pass the test and at the same time, the quality of the recording, for the human ear, will not differ. For some files that you have downloaded somewhere, it will be enough to convert them once into the same format.

Conclusion


I prefer the method of cutting out, as it spoils the quality of the track less.
There is a big chance that the algorithm for checking audio records will change after a while.
I think that the music is not deleted, since some scripts still display information about the bit-rate and the size of the song file. If this is the case, then it will probably be possible to play the locked recordings through the script.
