  • High power consumption of smart things. The more complex the functionality of devices entering the Internet of things, the higher their power consumption. And many gadgets and sensors will have to work autonomously. In addition, with the increasing connectivity of the infrastructure and the growing number of devices in the global IoT park, the number and frequency of m2m connections will increase, which will also require additional power consumption. Solar panels and other Energy Harvesting technologies only partially solve the problem. All the same, "smart" connected gadgets and sensors will need much more capacious batteries.
