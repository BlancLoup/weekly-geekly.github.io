May 15, 2011 in St. Petersburg will be ZFConf 2011



May 15, 2011 in St. Petersburg will be held ZFConf 2011 - the second Russian conference on the Zend Framework


At the moment, a list of reports is being formed. A number of specialists from well-known companies have already confirmed their participation as speakers.

If you have something to say to the ZF community, write to us via the feedback form about the proposed report and on May 15, it is possible that you will broadcast from the stage to hundreds of colleagues.

Information and financial sponsors are also invited to participate.

This year the conference will be organized at a fundamentally higher level. All your suggestions and comments will be taken into account. The event will be held in a modern, comfortable conference hall of the Holiday Inn hotel. Meals will be provided (lunch according to the buffet system).

Pre-registration for the conference is now available! The final registration will be opened after the formation of the final list of reports. Pre-registered participants will receive priority notification of this.

Organizer: Wizartech
Partners: zendframework.ru community and ZFConf Ukraine conference

Be sure to follow the latest news:
RSS feed: feeds.feedburner.com/zfconf
Twitter: @zfconf
Group VKontakte: vkontakte.ru/club14951507

It is noteworthy that on May 13 and 14 (that is, immediately before our conference), CodeCamp 2011 will be held in St. Petersburg. Therefore, you can arrange a real conference weekend.
