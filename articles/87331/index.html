Power to the upper unit is supplied through a current collector (large cylinder in the photo). The image is transmitted through an optical transmitter (small cylinder below the current collector).

Since the upper module weighs about 30 kilograms and rotates at a speed of 800 rpm , unbalance or external influence can ruin the entire structure. To prevent this, there is a vibration sensor. As soon as the vibration exceeds the set value, the motor is turned off.
