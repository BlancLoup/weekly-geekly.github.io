Forbes updated billionaire rating

Forbes magazine has updated its own billionaire rating .

In 2015, the richest man on the planet is still Bill Gates, with a fortune of $ 79.2 billion.

It is followed by Mexican billionaire Carlos Slim ($ 77.1 billion) and Warren Buffet ($ 72.7 billion).

In fifth place is Oracle founder Larry Ellison with $ 54.3 billion.

Jeff Bezos (Amazon) and Mark Zuckerberg (Facebook) occupy the 15th and 16th lines of the ranking with $ 34.8 billion and $ 33.4 billion, respectively.

Larry Page and Sergey Brin (Google) in 19th and 20th place with $ 29.7 billion and $ 29.2 billion, respectively.
