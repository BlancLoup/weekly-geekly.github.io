New drag and drop in Google Maps

Google once again pleases with its innovations. At this time, the functionality has been extended from Google Maps - the possibility of dragging intermediate points of the route.

You can add additional scheduled stops and detours manually (by typing the address of the intermediate waypoint), but it is much more convenient to do this by dragging.
