Google nexus 7



Hello everyone, dear gadget lovers. Nothing new today, most likely, we will not tell you. Announcement for those who follow the news from our blog.

Google Nexus 7 is one of the hottest new summer / fall products. You know that the Nexus 7 is the first tablet PC in the Google Nexus line of devices, the first Android 4.1 Jelly Bean device and the first 7-inch tablet with a quad-core (4 + 1) processor. Many of you have already had the opportunity to personally meet with the tablet or buy it. Therefore, do without repetitions.

The ASUS company announces the availability of the Nexus 7 tablet computer with 16 GB of memory in all major retail networks of household appliances and electronics in Moscow, St. Petersburg and the Russian regions at the recommended price of 9,990 rubles.



We know that the tablet could already be bought from the so-called gray supplies. However, it is only now with the device that you can receive the official warranty valid in Russia. In addition, a new batch of devices is on sale, in which the errors of the samples of the first deliveries to Europe and America were corrected.

A version with 8 GB is not yet planned for sale. The appearance of the 32 GB version is not yet known. ASUS did not announce the release of additional accessories, versions with an SD card slot or with 3G. And you can definitely say that Google Nexus 7 will remain a 7-inch tablet. The 10 inch version is a violation of the concept of an incredibly light and affordable device. The thickness of the case is only 10.5 mm, and the weight is 340 g. With such dimensions you can put the tablet not only in your bag, but even in your pocket, and you can use the multi-touch screen with one hand.
