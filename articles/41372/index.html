Targus Mac Accessory Kit

The company Targus, known primarily for bags for laptops and photo equipment, presented the first in its history line of accessories for the Mac. The set consists of a wireless laser mouse for $ 70, working via Bluetooth, a conventional wireless mouse for $ 50, pointers for presentations (laser pointer + remote control + mouse) for $ 80. In addition, there is a USB cable for transferring files from PC to Mac ($ 50), a four-port USB hub ($ 30), as well as a cooling part for a laptop with two coolers ($ 50) and security screens (from 50 to 80 $).



Apparently, Targus is confident that at the sight of a piece of white glossy plastic Apple loses its will. Prices definitely look somewhat overpriced (and the set is not made by Apple, but only in the style of Apple). For comparison, similar Bluetooth mice from Logitech and Sony can be bought for 40 and 61 dollars.

However, in her quest to make money on Mac fans, Targus is not alone. A set of plywood coasters in the form of iPhone-icons Brazilian studio Meninos offers for $ 60 . Probably tortured to cut.
