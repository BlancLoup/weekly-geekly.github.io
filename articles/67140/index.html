We carry games into reality

RPGRL




Innerscope is a more mature implementation of the 3rdEye game on Android, which was briefly discussed here . The development of the direction of RPG in Real Life has progressed further: you not only see monsters in the space around you and move around the GPS crushing everyone and everything with the blows of your fist, but you can also search for various objects using the device, use them in the fight against evil forces, Of course, enjoy multiplayer!

This time in more detail and with screenshots.


Subjects


Item is a kind of mysterious thing, an artifact if you want, which can be used in several ways, bringing harm or benefit to the owner. The properties and capabilities of objects are not known in advance and you have to act at your own peril and risk, applying them, but experimentation often pays off by discovering abilities.
To search for items there is a special device, similar in action to the Geiger counter: the closer the object, the more clicks you hear. The whole matter is turned on / off by the tumler in the upper right corner of the screen. The search itself looks like this: go on the map and suddenly you hear a signal, then try to take several steps in different directions, trying to determine the direction to the object, and then go straight to it. When the goal is reached, the desired will appear in your pocket - the panel on the left, or, if it is already complete, at the bottom of the screen, from where you can drag it into your pocket, replacing the less important item.



How to use


To trigger an artifact, you need to hold it with your thumb and make several circular movements with your hand, as if you are casting a spell, and something happens. The panel is located on the left in order to conveniently clamp an object while holding the phone in your right hand, but if it is more convenient in the left, you can move the panel to the right side of the screen through the menu. Casting is not so difficult - it is only important to start touching on the icon of the item, where it will end is not so important, i.e. you can not be afraid that the thumb during the spell will slide on the screen.

Here caste caused an aura in which the player is invisible to monsters.



Another item summoned a friendly creature.



Multiplayer


The map displays not only you and the monsters, but also other participants of this lawlessness. Only the player's name is displayed, without his characteristics, so if you need to know about someone in more detail, go and ask in person. When you are with someone nearby, the possibility of exchanging items opens up - just drag the desired item from your pocket into the slot that appears with the player's name.



Players can not be beaten, PvP for reasons of possible negative consequences of the scrum from the loser is disabled and is being tested in a closed circle)

The game is available in the Android Market, called Innerscope (Games> Arcade & Action). If for some reason it is not available from there, but there is where to run, you can download it here .
