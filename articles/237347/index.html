A verbatim quote from Vedomosti:
Exercises conducted by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media showed that the Russian Internet is vulnerable, and measures to minimize risks, including the possibility of temporarily disconnecting the runet from the outside world, are being discussed, the special services officer said.
