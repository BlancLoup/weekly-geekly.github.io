The helicopter weighing 16 grams entered service with the British army.

The tiny PD-100 PRS Black Hornet drone, created by the Norwegian company Prox Dynamics, is designed for live video surveillance and intelligence both indoors and outdoors. With it, you can look around the corner or behind a suspicious bush, look out for shelters and advance ways on an unfamiliar battlefield. The device weighs 16 grams and has a rotor diameter of only 120 millimeters, but its characteristics and functionality do not correspond to toy sizes at all.




Black Hornet is equipped with a PTZ video camera, the picture of which is transmitted over a distance of a kilometer under the condition of direct visibility. The helicopter is capable of operating at wind speeds of up to 10 knots (about 5 m / s), and its own maximum speed is 10 m / s. On one battery charge, it can fly up to 30 minutes.
