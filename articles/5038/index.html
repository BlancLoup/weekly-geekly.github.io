The fashion for group suicide came to Runet

A few years ago, fashion group suicides appeared in Japan and became a real nightmare for the local police. Every month in the newspapers appear regular notes about new deaths. To go out of their lives together or three of them has already become commonplace - this does not even happen to the press. Another thing, together with five or six.

The record case of group suicide happened three years ago in the suburbs of Tokyo, when the police immediately found nine corpses . Six teenagers and a woman were found in a minibus outside the city - they were poisoned by carbon monoxide after taking sleeping pills. That same evening, two more girls were found near them, who died in the same way.

The police reported that nine suicides, apparently, had agreed to meet through one of the websites. A typical case in Japan, where with the help of the Internet more than a hundred people kill themselves a year. In the Japanese Internet, special suicide sites have been operating for a long time. People discuss the advantages and disadvantages of each method. You can also buy special medicines and kits for painless care of life. The most important thing is that there you can always find a partner and like-minded person with whom it will be easier to take the last step.

No need to think that group suicides are exclusively Japanese fashion. The same begins in Russia. It would be strange to assume otherwise, because Russia ranks second in the world in the number of suicides per capita.
