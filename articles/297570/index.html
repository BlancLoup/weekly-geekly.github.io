With public procurement, Russian IT companies will receive 15% price preferences.

IT products, services and equipment of domestic origin can receive price preferences of 15%. The Ministry of Communications has prepared a corresponding project .

The ministry proposes a mechanism of 15 percent preferences for procurement (competitions, auctions, quotations requests) of state-owned companies, state corporations and natural monopolies, the document says.

The exemption, as conceived by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media, should extend to the purchase of 22 product items. Among them are electronic computers and communication equipment, monitors and projectors, automatic data processing devices and application and system software (software), IT services, as well as services for the production of computers and so on.
