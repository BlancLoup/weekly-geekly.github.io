14 Growth Hacking Business Strategies for B2B

Growth Hacking is not a technique or a way to attract customers.
First of all, this is a way of thinking and a real growth hacker will be able to apply it in any sphere.

The person who introduced this term in 2010 is called Sean Ellis, CEO of Qualaroo.
And that's how it was:



Sean helped many Internet companies and startups to achieve incredible growth.
When he decided to retire, naturally, it was necessary to find a replacement for him.
Traditional marketers came to posted vacancies who did market analysis, made plans
development, improved positioning, but it was not that ...

"Standard" marketers have a wide level of capabilities, but startups
At the beginning of your journey you need only one thing - GROWTH and a full stop.

Sean asked the marketer and got it, so an idea came to mind.
He decided to change the name and on July 26, 2010 the following heading appeared on his blog:



Growth Hacker is not a substitute for the marketer. Growth Hacker is no better or cooler marketer.
These are slightly different directions:
The first - are obsessed with growth and nothing more.
The second - perform a wider range of tasks.

It is best to quote Sean to answer the question ‚ÄúWho is a Growth hacker?‚Äù:
"A growth hacker is a person of true north is growth"

Translation: ‚ÄúGrowth hacker is a person whose sole purpose is growth‚Äù

It is impossible to study growth hacking, because technologies on the Internet are developing so fast that the information that worked today may be irrelevant tomorrow. In addition, if you can massively train grocery hacking techniques, then this will no longer be a creative way of growth, but the most template one. You can get explosive growth where no one is looking for it.

Therefore, there will be no theory. Only examples and tips from practice.
I have compiled 14 Growth Hacking strategies for the B2B market. It is for b2b, since I myself work in this segment and, in my opinion, getting clients in B2C is easier.

B2B Content Marketing on "steroids"


1. Create entertainment content for your target audience, without direct advertising.


All tired of direct advertising. Our internal filter simply blocks it and does not notice.
Create something new for your target audience. Attract attention.
A sense of humor is a great way to evoke emotions, and therefore remain in the memory.

For example, we recently did a test for success PravdaMatka , after passing which, each participant receives personal advice on business.
The test is based on the advice of Oleg Tinkov, he acts as the main character, we put him in the center of the screen and in a comic form showed what the main secret of success is.

During the first 3 days, we received more than 300 reposts and thousands of clicks to our site. Let's see what will happen next.



Another example from the west. Checkmarx attracted 65,000 security experts on the Internet through the ‚ÄúHacker Game‚Äù .
This is a developer quiz consisting of security questions. You can test your knowledge in programming and add your own questions to other participants. What is the result? 7,500 visitors and 200 responses.

11. Create a separate product.
