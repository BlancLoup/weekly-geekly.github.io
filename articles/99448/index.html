Virtual trips to famous places of the world in 3D

Quite by chance I came across an interesting and previously unlit project on Habr- Habr√© - Vizerra.com . Having downloaded and installed a small client, it is possible to travel around 3D models of famous places in the world.



The free movement mode is available, as well as the interactive guide mode (with Russian or English voice acting).


First of all, you need to download a small client from the site and install it. When the client starts, it will check the necessary components on the computer and, if necessary, update them (DirectX, PhysX, Visual Studio Runtime, etc ...)



Next, go to the locations section, where we select the location of interest and download it.



Locations are downloaded once, and take from 10 to ~ 300 Mb, depending on the number of models and textures, as well as the duration of the sound guide.
