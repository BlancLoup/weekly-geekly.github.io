Shab-Niggurath is described as a hazy, cloud-like mass with a multitude of long, black tentacles, mouths dropping mucus and short goat legs. She is accompanied by many small monstrous creatures generated by her, which she constantly ejaculates from herself, and then devours and digests again.

Of all the creatures of the mythical pantheon of Myths Cthulhu, the cult of Shab-Niggurath is described as the most common. She is even worshiped by extraterrestrial Mi-go.

Having properly performed the rite, Shab-Niggurath can be summoned in any forest on the new moon.
