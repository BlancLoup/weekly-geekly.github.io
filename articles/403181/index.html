Italian pizzeria warmly supported cryptocurrency

In May 2010, a legendary event occurred for the cryptocurrency world. The young Bitcoin-Miner Laszlo Khanesh then went down in history as the first person to pay for the purchase of real goods using Bitcoins (BTC).



Laszlo was one of the pioneers of GPU mining, which allowed him to accumulate a large supply of cryptocurrency. He paid Jeremy Sturdivant 10 thousand BTC for the purchase of two pizzas for home delivery. At that time, such an impressive amount of koin was equivalent to just a few dollars, but today you can get a fortune on the open market - $ 20 million.

The celebration of this first real bitcoin transaction is held annually on May 22. At that time, you could not buy anything for bitcoins directly: Laszlo had to convince another enthusiast from the BitcoinTalk forum to pay for pizza with his credit card in exchange for Lasco's Bitcoins. Today, however, cryptocurrency payments are gaining momentum, and not only in Bitcoins.

Today, when it was 2017 in the yard, there was news that the Italian restaurant and pizzeria called Gusto DiVino announced the start of accepting tokens of our blockchain platform Waves as the preferred currency for paying orders.
