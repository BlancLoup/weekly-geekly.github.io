Ruby Shift Conference in Kiev on May 21



May 21 in Kiev will host a conference Ruby Shift

Features Opened by Ruby Shift


  • Ukrainian Ruby Community Support
  • New acquaintances in IT
  • Experience exchange
  • New ideas, informal communication with colleagues

Among the speakers


  • Yehuda Katz : Rails 3 Choices: The Devil Is In The Details
  • Ivan Samsonov : Rubinius - Ruby, written in Ruby

Details - under the cut!

Program



Metaprogramming in Ruby ‚Äî The Ruby object model is very rich and allows you to make many things easier than in other languages. You will learn how to use its capabilities at 100%. Subtleties and implementation details.
Rails at first hand is a powerful, gaining momentum framework for creating web applications, how-tos, the reasons for switching to the 3rd version.
Rails in Cloud - hosting Rails in the clouds. Production applications, savings on hosting, scalability in real time and the use of resources required in a specific period of time.
Scaling Rails - principles for expanding Rails applications, optimizing High-load applications, using various technologies, examples and practices.
Rubinius, MRI alternatives - JRuby, Rubinius, REE, use cases, features, support, performance.


A special guest at the event - Yehuda Katz (one of the main developers of Rails, Merb, jQuery, Contributor DataMapper Rubinius, Johnson, the creator of Thor), will make a presentation on " Real Reasons to Switch to Rails 3 " and why Rails 3 development decisions were made.
In the report " Rubinius - Ruby, written in Ruby ", Ivan Samsonov , the contributor to Rubinius, tells in detail about the history of creation, the interpreter device, the virtual machine architecture, the advantages in terms of performance, and development plans for the near future.

Workshop


In addition to reports on Ruby Shift, there will also be a Workshop (master class) for speakers and conference participants who have registered for the Workshop. Hurry up! The number of participants is limited to 30 people. Workshop will be conducted in English.
There are no passive participants in the Workshop. All are actively involved in action, and thus receive maximum benefit.
All participants will be divided into teams, and will take part in the development and Show-real project!

The program of the event is actively being formed. Those who wish to speak can submit an application on the website until May 8 inclusive.

The organizers are the initiative group coffee'n'code.

You can register for participation and send a request for a report on the event website http://coffee-n-code.org.ua/ . You can follow the progress of events on our twitter account and on the hashtag #rubybarcamp


See you at RubyShift!
