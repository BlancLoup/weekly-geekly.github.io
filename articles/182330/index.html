You would not chase, priest, for cheapness



The reason for writing this note was the discussion, which flared up on the article about travel sites, the specifics of their creation and pricing. A quote that has become an epigraph for a note belongs to the top manager of one of the agencies that participated in the discussion and developed travel sites for just 8,000 rubles and even 4,000 rubles.

Where is the firewood from?

Honestly, I was intrigued. How does a metropolitan agency, selling websites at that price, manage to exist? And in order to understand where such amounts came from, we decided to count a little and dragged our data on labor costs to create a simple website such as business cards from the storerooms. Calculations on the tourist site postponed until later.

So, according to the calculations of labor costs of the Internet-Client studio, a standard small website with a unique design requires about 20 hours of work of several employees: a manager, a programmer, a designer, a layout designer, a content maker and a tester. 20 studio hours, estimated in salaries, cost of jobs and depreciation of equipment, mandatory insurance and tax payments from salaries - this is about 17,000 rubles, or 850 rubles per hour of work. This is the cost price and the price of a standard simple site without a license, in case of choosing a paid CMS. And now we add profits here (it somehow should be), we take into account the need to invest a certain amount in the development fund (software updates, equipment, etc.), personnel training costs (not enough), taxes (to sleep well). In order for the company to at least keep afloat, it will take at least 10,000 rubles to build in excess of the cost price. The result is at least 27,000 for a site with a unique design on a free engine. But this is a site with an individual design. We took it for zeroing, and the results of such a comparison would not be representative.

We try to reduce the price, and we refuse unique design. By the way, for many representatives of small businesses and small travel agencies, the rejection of the unique design is the only right decision, because to make the design unique and high-quality within the minimum budget is extremely difficult. Eliminating the unique design, they also built a complete boxed solution to minimize the participation of expensive specialists in the project creation. The break-even cost of a boxed standard website such as a business card was reduced to 10,000 rubles. Excluding payment license Bitrix. With a business card, everything is clear, take up the tourist site.
