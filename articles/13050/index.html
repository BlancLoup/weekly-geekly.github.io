Google will pay to fill the directory of companies

Google has announced the launch of the Local Business Referral Program to create a global map of US firms. Anyone can earn: just enter the coordinates of a store, restaurant, cinema or other company in the database, specify exact hours of operation, contact numbers and other information, as well as take photos of the institution - and you can take your honestly earned ten dollars from Google after checking information. The check is that Google sends the company all the information collected about it and asks to confirm the facts with a postcard, and at the same time subscribe to the contextual Adwords ad, if such a desire arises.

A disciplined employee who will walk around the city with a digital camera and pocket computer can easily earn up to $ 30 per hour. Only one question remains: when will Google launch the same program with us? Thousands of our students will map the CIS in a matter of days.
