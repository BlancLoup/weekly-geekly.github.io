ZX Spectrum Memories - homemade digital camera from RAM chip



We listen to tracker and 8-bit music with a friend, podcasts with music from Spectrum times, reflections on the historical patterns of the development of the demoscene. The fact is that on the modest hardware zx spectrum you can now create complex special effects, windows like interfaces, but this does not mean that it was possible then. It was necessary to develop to the current technology and with the help of "downshifting" to adapt them for a limited iron. Now, with the support of a modern PC, it has become much easier to create something under the zx spectrum.
