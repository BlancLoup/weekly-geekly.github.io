P2P hardware access

Someone is suing trackers - and someone is trying to make money from them.
In the vastness of our country, as you know, chaos is created , everything is possible.
So, the company VVK, which recently released the notorious Popcorn Hour to our market, could, it turns out, agree with the owners of the largest Russian tracker, Torrents.ru .
According to the website of Computerra magazine , at the HDi Show exhibition, direct access to the tracker for searching and downloading content was demonstrated. No registration is required and the rating is not taken into account. The terms of the agreement is not known.

Such a service, of course, will increase the popularity of mercilessly cursed product IHC. Moreover, the tracker has a large amount of high-quality content. But cooperation can also be a cause of headaches for Torrents.ru administrators due to the influx of new visitors or close attention from competitors of the IHC. And the attention of competitors can pay attention to the left of law enforcement, as is often the case.
