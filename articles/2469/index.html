Big win in the casino called the software "glitch"

Breaking the bank in a casino is good luck. To make it pay - even more. This is what two lovers of gambling games from Manitoba will have to do, to whom the gaming machine has reported an unprecedented gain - almost $ 210 thousand.

But the administration of the casino refuses to pay this amount to the players, referring to the fact that the winnings were the result of a program error. The lawyers of the plaintiffs disagree with this (the case has already gone to court), who claims that there are no independent testing results, and clients are not obliged to pay their time for the mistakes of others.

According to the representative of Manitoba Lotteries, the gaming machine, as a rule, does not pay customers more than $ 3 thousand. That is why the random winnings are associated with an error in the program, reports Newsrush .
