Chrome for a Cause

Google offers you to support one of the five charities by counting the tabs you open in Chrome.



The total number of tabs opened by users will determine the amount of charitable donations (up to one million dollars) to be made on behalf of the Chrome community.
  • 10 tabs = 1 tree planted
  • 10 tabs = 1 published book
  • 25 tabs = 1 vaccine
  • 100 tabs = 1 square foot of built housing
  • 200 tabs = 1 annual supply of water for one person

How to participate?

  • Install the Chrome for a Cause extension ;
  • View web pages in Chrome from December 15 to December 19;
  • at the end of each day you will be asked to manage your tabs;
  • choose which charitable institution you would like to support with the number of tabs opened that day.
