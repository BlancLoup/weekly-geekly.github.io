HIRO III - 3D Display and Tactile Interface

Joint development of Kawasaki and Mouri Laboratory in Japan presented to the general public the touch interface, which, together with 3D displays, gives a new round of development of augmented reality.



A small video review:




HIRO III is a tactile interface of the robot, which provides realistic kinesthetic sensations in the hands of the user and fingers, and the 3D display helps even more plunge into virtual reality.

Estimated scope of application - medicine. But we are already guessing which industry can take note of the development.
