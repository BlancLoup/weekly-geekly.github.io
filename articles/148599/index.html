Handwritten Google search for smartphones and tablets

Rui Weyama, Engineer

Search on mobile devices is different from searching on a stationary computer. For example, sometimes it is not very convenient to enter a request on a small smartphone screen. Thanks to the handwriting input, you can write a query directly with your finger, not only in the search line, but also on the entire display.
