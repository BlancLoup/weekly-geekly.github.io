Mapping


  • Joseph Eisenberg again started the approval process for the camp_site=camp_pitch . In fact, this tag has already been used more than 6.8 thousand times in the area of tourism=camp_site to designate a place to set up a tent or a tourism=camp_site home.
  • Joseph Eisenberg also suggested replacing the existing track tagging scheme used by golfcars ( highway=path or highway=service + golf_cart=designated ) to use one golf=cartpath .
  • Approved tag shop=fashion_accessories , proposed by SelfishSeahorse to refer to stores specializing in the sale of fashion accessories.
  • Currently, the place=locality tag has been used almost 1.3 million times. The scope of its application is quite wide: they designate both abandoned cities and railway junctions in the USA, and territorial division in Ireland ( locality=townland and locality=subtownland ). Therefore, Joseph Eisenberg suggests using the key locality=* to indicate the type of terrain.
  • Since March 15, almost all addresses in Germany, which were implemented in the form of relations (associatedStreet), were redone: they were put on the buildings in the form of tags. The share of redundant relationships has decreased from 58% to 7%. User dktue posted on the forum simple statistics about this (automatic translation ).
