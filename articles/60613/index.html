Quick acquaintances at the 10th Startup Point mini-conference in Moscow (May 31, 2:00 pm)

Next Sunday (May 31), at 10:00 pm, the 10th Startupers Meeting (mini-conference) Startup Point will begin.
This time, the meeting point is the ArteFAQ club, which is located very close to the Pushkinskaya / Chekhovskaya / Tverskaya metro station ( map )
The program of the mini-conference, along with the already traditional formats of acquaintance and communication of startups and investors, added new interesting ideas.
First of all, these are two new communication formats: SpeedNetworking and UnConference.



Since new terms are often understood differently, it is worth explaining once more exactly what they mean on Startup Point.

‚Äú SpeedNetworking ‚Äù - (in the translation ‚ÄúFast networking‚Äù) is such a special group communication format that allows participants to quickly get to know each other. Each participant has the opportunity within 1 minute to talk about themselves, their projects and interests of the interlocutor and then listen to him within a minute. The format is designed in such a way that it gives participants an interesting opportunity to quickly introduce themselves and exchange information about their companies or projects. At first glance it seems that repeating the same information about yourself several times is quite tiring and boring, but in practice, having the opportunity to get a lot of useful contacts in one evening, meet interesting people and come up with many new ideas and suggestions, such a format dating in the states is popular.
Advantages of SpeedNetworking:
- Get in one evening more business contacts than a month of normal work
- Improve and polish your short story about the project (elevator pitch)
- Communicate face to face with interesting people.
- The fascination of the event and the fast pace make the event fun.
- Excellent prospects for young entrepreneurs :)

" UnConference " is a unique format for mutual communication, exchange of ideas and learning. This is an event whose fate is completely shaped and determined by the participants of the ‚ÄúNon-Conference‚Äù themselves, since there is not and cannot be any pre-established program of the section, no reports, master classes, plenary sessions, and so on, until the participants themselves create one of these for themselves. In the Startup Point format a few minutes before the beginning of the ‚ÄúNon-conference‚Äù section, the meeting participants can write an interesting topic for them to discuss-report-report. The best topics will be distributed among the available sites, and then each UnConference participant chooses which author of the topic he would like to join and participate in the discussion. The care of attracting participants on an interesting topic lies entirely on the author of this topic.

If you want to take part in the Startup Point mini-conference as a thematic speaker, expert, present your startup or idea, then send your suggestion to habrapost or to mimic [at] startuppoint.ru. There is very little time and opportunity for thematic reports in the program of the mini-conference.

You can register for the 10th meeting of startups here (registration for the meeting is obligatory, entry by lists). The number of participants, unfortunately, is limited to the capacity of the club and is 100-120 people.
