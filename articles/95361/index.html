Economic rationale for the use of PoE (Power over Ethernet)

Searching through habraprostor, I realized that almost nobody wrote about the use of PoE technology, so I decided to share my experience in plowing this field.

WIKI (recall what PoE is)

Power over Ethernet, or PoE, is a technology that allows electric power to be transmitted to a remote device along with data through a standard twisted pair over an Ethernet network. This technology is intended for IP-telephony, access points of wireless networks, Web-cameras, network hubs and other devices to which it is undesirable or impossible to conduct a separate electrical cable.

Before the purchase, there was an entertainment facility in the building, and, in general, the networks, telephones, and video surveillance in the building were not very good. Ambula

In GOST 34 there is a very interesting part - the feasibility study of the project - in terms of economy in the future, I decided to build a rationale for the use of PoE.

So, given - tel.stantsiya Alcatel OMNI PCX Ent (IP) - this bastard was purchased before the Kryzys, 220 phones Alcatel 4018 + 5 tel. Alcatel with secretarial functions, I do not remember the model, in general, and it does not matter - 225 phones. Alcatel 4018 has 2 ports - through the phone you can connect the computer to the SCS for 100 Mbps.

To connect all this stuff - 5 switches X 48 ports - there is still 15 cameras (Axis, ACTi) in place. RoE switches are expensive (by the way, in the HP line, they are indicated by the addition of the PWR name). We start to count on what we can save.

225 Alcatel phones come without power supplies - this is the policy. Separately, the power supply unit (PSU) GPSU15B-8 costs at least 800 rubles, all units - 180,000 rubles. Already not bad, since with PoE we do not need blocks.

We pick the blocks, and at the same time switches.

1) The GPSU15B-8 block is an ordinary transformer block, 15W. The efficiency of such a unit is ~ 60%
But to transfer power from the switch to the phone, a pulse power supply unit is used, its efficiency is ~ 98%. The switches themselves are powered by UPS, there are also pulsed power supplies, the overall efficiency of the switch + UPS pair is 96%.

2) A telephone with a power supply is always connected to the network, acc. constantly consuming their 15 W - 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Switches with PoE function allow you to manage power. This means that a phone connected using PoE will consume 15 W only at the time of direct use of the device (receiving / transmitting a call, working with a menu), the rest of the time the phone will be in standby mode and consume a few tenths of a watt.

We take out the calculator, we start counting - 15W * 24hours * 365days / 0.6 Efficiency = 219 kW * hour per year - according to the scheme ‚Äútelephone + BP‚Äù one workplace eats 219 kilowatt-hours per year.
And all 225 places - 49.3 megawatt-hours per year.

When switched on according to the ‚ÄúPoE phone‚Äù scheme, I assumed conditionally that the switch consumes energy, but not phones - the switch consumes about 80 W (the working computer without frills consumes about 60 W, measured it itself), and then the switch controls the distribution of energy to the phones - total 80W * 24hours * 365 days / 0.96 (KPD) = 730 kW * h / year. 5 switches, sootv. - 3.65 MW * h / year

The difference in power consumption is 45.65 MW * h. At the then cost of lipidism, savings of ~ 170,000 rubles a year came out.

Total - 350 000 r - for the first year this model pays off.

This is in addition to the fact that it is almost impossible to connect additional electrical power to the building in defaultCity even for a bribe physically carried by one person, and the problem of power shortage is quite acute and frequent.
