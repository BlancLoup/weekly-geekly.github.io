Goodyear has shown the concept of spherical tires on magnetic levitation

Thanks to its material, the Eagle-360 will provide a stable tire behavior in various weather conditions - in a dry climate it will behave like a more rigid structure, and with a lot of moisture, on the contrary, it will get a softer texture.
