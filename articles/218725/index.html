Problems with ishop.qiwi.ru

Hello!

For several weeks now the ishop.qiwi.ru service has not been working as expected.
In the beginning there were difficulties with the withdrawal of funds, although we always list them on a kiwi wallet.
A week ago, the automatic invoicing service stopped working, only in manual mode on the site ishop.qiwi.ru.
They do not respond to mail, phone calls are dropped.

This is the end?

PS: Thank you all, indeed, if you change the domain to .com, then the output works (at least today, I could not log in yesterday). It remains to check the billing for the REST protocol.
