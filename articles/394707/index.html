GOG.com has the opportunity to transfer games from Steam for free.

The other day in the service of digital distribution of computer games and movies without DRM GOG.com there was an opportunity to transfer the games purchased on Steam to the GOG.com library. After adding the game does not disappear from Steam, and become available in two libraries at once.


The new service is called GOG Connect and to activate it, just connect your Steam profile. A couple of important points: first, the Steam profile at the time of import must be made "Open." And secondly, you can connect only one profile and change it later will be impossible.

There are currently arrangements with Deep Silver, Harebrained Schemes, Jonathan Blow's Number None and TaleWorlds. 26 games are available for transfer:
  • The witness
