The history of the creation of "Mobile Advertising"

As the Steel Was Tempered.


The topic was published at the request and support of the residents of Habr.

I'll start with the results.

Advertising network now:


- 20 000 000 ad views per day
- 3000 advertising campaigns
- 150 advertising campaigns in rotation simultaneously
- 84 countries of the world and 326 mobile operators in targeting settings
- 100% mirror statistics with a detailed log for both sides of the mobile advertising market

For me, this is 3 years of work, with no days off and vacations, access to publishers outside of Russia and offers of integration with other systems, dozens of problem partners and everyday stress.

For all the time I worked on the project, I got a lot of experience and met interesting people.
All the experience gained is considered positive for conclusions and further work. I consider any situation, overcome by myself, an adventure and a plot of tales for friends.
At one time, the mobile Internet was for me a hobby and a game, but the hobby grew into a business, and the game became part of the business process.

I will try to do without addresses and names. Get ready, read a lot.

The story begins in 2005, when I made my first mobile site (websites never interested me). It was a catalog of nightclubs in Russia with contacts and addresses, in addition contained several links to content and had its own chat for regular visitors.

After a short period of time, I thought, as it is said now, about the monetization of the project. The purpose of monetization was self-sustaining, that is, not to invest in the project, and the project could provide for itself with attendance of about 600-800 people daily. Now this project is a tape of musical news, without catalogs and other information.

2006

At that time I did not see advertising networks for mobile sites, I knew only affiliate programs that redeem traffic to third-party sites for a penny with my own accounting algorithms. I was not happy about selling traffic to such PPs, I was constantly looking for more acceptable solutions, the requirements for which I already started to formulate for myself.

2007

One of their first experiences of placing quality targeted advertising on a mobile project is of course Google AdSense.
It seemed that it was very difficult to withdraw money from it, but fantastic prices for the cost of the transition seemed like a fairy tale. Three months or more was a block.
As many already know, my Google AdSense account was banned after filling out the address form to send a check. The reason, as I recall, was that the sites I referred to contained links that violate the rules and regulations.

The idea was in the air.

I searched, continued to put links from PP and pseudo-advertising tools, tried many, but stopped at one, which for the first time in two long years of searching paid for advertising more than the cost of hosting a project.
Advantages of the network: the output of ads under the visitor, more expensive ads were shown more often, each visitor had his own link.
I will not give names and links, but I‚Äôll say that at that time I was amazed at the possibilities and accuracy of this advertising tool, I was satisfied with the price for the transfer, every month, but ...
The cons were definitely present:
- absence of the transition log in free access
- inaccuracy in the accounting of views ads
- lack of analytical information
- dull advertisers
- calculation and pricing in WMZ Webmoney (and I would like rubles)

But all this overlapped the regular income to the account without delay and claims.

After that, it became clear to me that in Russia there is no universally uniform way of advertising on mobile sites, calculated in rubles. The niche is completely free, and PP for reselling clicks are not related to advertising and, accordingly, are not competitors in principle. So far I have not fully understood how to do this, what investments are needed and what will be required for maintenance. But he looked for people, watched, studied how the emerging advertising tools worked.

2008

Good mobile advertising tools began to appear. He began to use some to advertise his project. Acquaintance with the managers / administration of networks gave an additional reason to think about increasing advertising momentum. Claims and requirements for advertising tools were formed by themselves with increasing speed and needs. I wanted one tool, where all the minuses were removed, all the necessary chips were implemented, and there would be an opportunity to monetize not only one project, but to give everyone who wants to transparently use the advertising opportunities of their site.

Having knowledge of all (maybe almost all) traffic buying / selling systems that existed at that time, their subtleties and payment terms, I tried to organize a steady stream of transitions from one PP to another. A first project appeared, taking into account transitions, a multi-user structure and a buy / sell credit system, where the exchange rate for loans (credit is the unit of calculation) was formed by the internal exchange. Starting turnovers and price differences were insignificant, but understanding the logic of the processes can be successfully resold, remaining in a big plus. It was also on hand to new networks, which could in the shortest possible time give their advertisers a large amount of traffic and work out the resulting budgets. I myself organized advertising streams several times for advertising campaigns of famous brands, but I received only a percentage of the budget without direct contact with advertisers.

Further, my knowledge of programming was not enough. To optimize and improve the system of buying / selling needed good hands of a programmer.

At that time, I already had a substantial balance on advertising, contacts with managers of well-known networks and agreements on large volumes of traffic.
The advertising campaigns that drove the ad networks were large, the advertising budgets exceeded my income dozens of times. All this has become for me to turn into gameplay. I started looking for a programmer to improve my tools and logging system. Money and turnover allowed to hire an employee.

Many of my friends did not believe that they could work with advertising, especially in such a narrow area as the mobile Internet.

The following experience with a remote developer was not long in coming.

The task was simple:

Work on the project went very quickly, I myself did not believe that everything was realizable and customizable, although I did not take into account much in the first version of the network. Three months after setting the task, the advertising network was already working, but at least it had to have its own server, and the existing hosting did not allow either external or incoming requests, which made the work very difficult, until the project was stopped during the first stages of work.

With the appearance of good incomes, there was a desire to go into a legal existence, to establish an organization, which would give direct access to good budgets of large advertisers.
All the available experience and ready-made tools allowed us to save up on initial investments in the business. If I knew where to look for investors, how to get start-up capital and the support of specialists, then everything would be faster.
