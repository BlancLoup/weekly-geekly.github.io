Raise3D 3D Printer Review



What are we talking about


Raise3D is one of the fastest growing Chinese companies producing 3D printers, we recently wrote about going to their factory. The company produces three models of 3D printers of different sizes, in versions with one and two extruders, as well as software for them.



In this article, we will look at the Raise3D model range and share our printing experience on these devices.



Models


Raise3D N1





Construction area: 205x205x205 mm
Platform heating: up to 110 ¬∞ C
Extrusion temperature: up to 300 ¬∞ C
Double extruder: optional
Materials: most plastics used in 3D printing, including PLA, ABS, PC, PETG, HIPS, NinjiaFlex and others.
Price: from 165 000 rubles

All printers of a model range print with a standard plastic filament with a section of 1,75 mm. You can use any plastic with a print temperature of up to 300 ¬∞ C.



The device is based on a durable aluminum frame with a durable coating and steel guides.



4-point spring platform position adjustment greatly simplifies calibration.



Uploading files is possible via WIFI, LAN, USB and SD-flash. Management and indication is carried out through the full-color seven-inch touch display.



The printer is optionally completed with a double extruder for two-material printing - you can print plastic models of two colors or with soluble supports.



Print resolution is up to 10 microns in the Z axis and up to 12.5 in the X and Y axes.



It is possible to continue printing interrupted due to a power outage or for other reasons - the model will not be damaged.



Raise3D N2



Construction area: 305x305x305 mm
Platform heating: up to 110 ¬∞ C
Extrusion temperature: up to 300 ¬∞ C
Double extruder: optional
Materials: most plastics used in 3D printing, including PLA, ABS, PC, PETG, HIPS, NinjiaFlex and others.
Price: from 225 000 rubles

The main differences from the first model: the increased volume of the camera - the ability to print products up to 30.5 centimeters in size on each side.



Factory pre-calibrated platform. More convenient location of the control touch panel.



The remaining characteristics are similar to the N1, including a printing chamber that is completely closed to drafts and temperature drops, and an on-board computer convenient for a future upgrade ‚Äî everything is the same.
