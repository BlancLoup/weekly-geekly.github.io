Mobile library

The mobile operator Vodafone Romania and the publishing house Humanitas have come up with another way to increase interest in reading among residents of Bucharest, who, according to research, left much to be desired (52% of Romanians do not read books at all). Library of electronic and audiobooks opened ... in the subway . The walls of one of the central stations were covered with book covers with QR codes. The passenger must read the code and go to the page of the work on the electronic library website and upload an excerpt of the book he likes - just as much as you can read for the trip. If you like the beginning - the book can be purchased entirely, for money. The project was designed for 3 months, but it turned out to be so successful, and it was decided to repeat it several months later.
