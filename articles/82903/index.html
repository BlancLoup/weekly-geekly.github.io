Ethernet clocks always show the exact time



A new invention of Computerwise.com, a digital clock with LED-display connected to the Internet via Ethernet and, in addition to specifying the time, receive power through an Ethernet cable.

Watches are different. There is a clock built into the operating system. They are tied to the monitor of your computer. There are clockwork. But they constantly have to correct and start. There is a clock with satellite connection. But today they are very buggy (for example, they refuse to transfer time from summer to winter).
An Ethernet clock (the full product name of the ED212 Ethernet Time Display) is periodically connected to an NTP server for time synchronization.

The size of the device is 6.9 x 23.5 x 3.3 inches, the characters on the display are said to be visible at a distance of 100 feet (read 30 meters).

The device (especially in the picture) seems to be extremely simple, but in fact it is a functional thing . In her arsenal:
- Display time in the format of 12 and 24 hours
- Timers with direct and countdown
- Up to 32 set alarms at the same time
- Support for the software interface (read, you can nakodit something of their own, for example, displayed messages)
- Wireless Support (802.11b / g)

Recommended for use in offices and large rooms.
