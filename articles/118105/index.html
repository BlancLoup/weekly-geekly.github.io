Unusual manhole covers may appear in Samara

Not so often given the opportunity to change something around them or do something for the city. And here we had a chance: Volga TGC announced a competition for the design of the hatch cover. Usually we draw icons and other graphics for interfaces, and here a huge heavy cast iron hatch! In case of victory in Samara, such hatches will appear, plus, the authors will be presented with one cover.

The prospect of getting a trophy in the form of a hatch cover of our own design seemed very tempting to us. It's almost industrial design! But first I needed some kind of idea. What is usually depicted on the hatches? Some kind of incomprehensible letters and nondescript pictures.

We decided that we need to make the design of the hatch interesting for citizens and guests of the city. To hatch was about Samara and its attractions. To be sure, we drew two options:



Vector layouts are not very visual, so it was decided to render in 3D to understand how the covers will look. At the same time, I will tell in more detail about each hatch.
