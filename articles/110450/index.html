What is SkyDNS?


In a nutshell - this is a free internet filter. In the database of the service is information about nearly four million domains, grouped into several dozen categories.

SkyDNS allows you to block viral and phishing sites, banners, pornography, and other resources. No censorship! Everything is determined by your personal preferences.

Some facts


The network of DNS servers consists of four machines spread across the country (in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg and Novosibirsk).

Every day the service processes from 30 to 50 million DNS requests (the load floats, on weekends people surf much less than on working days).

The base of sites is growing at a speed of about two thousand new domains per day.
