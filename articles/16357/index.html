VoIP in Russia - Service FlashPhone.ru

In the beginning of autumn, I wrote about the fact that Flex will receive phone calls . But our comrades, do not sit still. The FlashPhone.ru service appeared (by the way, Flash Ripper once wrote about it).

I am very interested in such services, because, at the moment, I myself am working on VoIP-projects and therefore a little bit more about the service.



One of the key features of the project is that the service allows you to call directly from the browser. You do not need to install anything (the main thing that was Flash Player 9 and higher). All that is needed for calls is a headset and sufficient bandwidth to the Internet.
You can also call via FlashPhone and from Pocket PC devices. To do this, you need to download and install Flash Player 7 for Pocket PC .

At the moment, the service is in beta testing, but at this stage you can
make 3 test calls per day (talk time is limited to 3 minutes).

The only thing that seemed uncomfortable to me was registration. I was able to register only from the 5th attempt. I do not know, maybe I had no luck that day, but I didn‚Äôt like it very much.

This unpleasant impression immediately smoothed out, as I made three test calls to Moscow, to Kazan and Tashkent. The quality was just great. By the way, I called both landline and mobile phones.

So one can only wish good luck to a really interesting project ...
