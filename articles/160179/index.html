Minecraft Reality lets you see your virtual creations in the real world.



For many Minecraft players, the most beautiful thing in the game is the construction of facilities, and the new iOS application allows you to see how these creations will look in the real world. An augmented reality application called Minecraft Reality allows you to upload your creations to the site and then post them in the real world so that you can view them through the camera of your device, writes The Verge. In the application there is a social element: you can fix your Minecraft-model in a certain place where other users can stumble upon it.

The application was created by the 13th Lab with the blessing of the developer Minecraft - studio Mojang. Minecraft Reality can be bought for $ 1.99 on the App Store.
