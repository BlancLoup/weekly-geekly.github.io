6th International StartupPoint Meeting

On January 25, the 6th International StartupPoint Meeting of Startupers was held at the Celt Club with the support of the innovation-educational company RMA and the company Careelab. Each time, these meetings bring together more and more specialists in the field of creating and promoting Internet projects, forming around them a professional community of startups. This time about 120 participants, 12 projects and representatives of three investment companies were invited to the meeting: Addventure, eVenture Capital Partners and AVK-Media. Each time the quality of the organization of the event also increases - the growth of the experience of the organizational headquarters affects.

The format of the minibar is somewhat modified in details from meeting to meeting. The way of presenting projects and their subsequent discussion remains unchanged. Representatives of each project are given 2 minutes to describe the essence of a startup (this presentation is called elevator pitch).
