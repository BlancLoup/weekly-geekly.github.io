How we built a new School of Managers Stratoplan: an invitation to a presentation

15 months of large construction behind.

At the end of 2013, we decided to rebuild the School and sold all the accumulated materials for 2008-2013.

In 2014, we developed, conducted, recorded, edited, and produced 4 main modules of the future annual program of the Stratoplan School of Managers: 4 formulas - project management, work with people, negotiation and professional growth. We have strengthened the program with 2 large directories: analysis of negotiation and management situations. This was our main product - an annual transformation program for our students.



We met the design and production at exactly 1 year to launch a large annual training program at the end of 2014 and make it open to new students year-round.

In early 2015, we completed and launched two more services: professional orientation and case club .

And all this - without stopping the main business, without stopping the constant distribution of training programs, continuing to experiment with marketing and advertising, launching a new website. We did not stop work, hired and parted with people, participated in conferences and exhibitions, conducted corporate trainings and conferences. We are sure that the crisis is in our heads. You just need to do your own thing.

We invite you to evaluate the result.

On Friday, March 27, at 11:00 Moscow time (10 am Kiev time), we will hold an online presentation of the new School of Managers Stratoplan.

Come to find out what services we offer our students, what and how we teach, how much time you need to devote to study, how much it costs to study and who is currently studying at our School.
