Functional


In addition to the standard functions of social networks, that is, all kinds of relationships between students / teachers / parents, and electronic document systems, there are several elements that I would like to highlight.

The first, (screenshot from khanacademy), is the tree of knowledge. The advantages of having such an organization for all courses are obvious. How and for what reasons to build it is a separate story.


The second important point I would highlight a section for parents. In which, apart from assessments and the progress of the child, there would be more materials for classes along with the child, accompanied with the advice of teachers and psychologists. This is a certain feature of our country - it is customary to teach children by ourselves (in some families)

Thirdly, it is anonymity. For example, in the same 100ege.ru suggest to enter the name of one of the parents. Naturally, this is not checked, but the possibility of quietly entering and looking around seems to me important.

I ran over the tops. There are still such things as curricula, class statistics, virtual boards for communicating with a tutor, and so on. These are all fairly obvious things and all services will implement them as needed.

A little about the features of each system.
Dnevnik.ru - a step towards e-schools. This giant has been developing a fully electronic system since 2007 and already has more than a hundred people in its staff. At the moment, the concept of the site implies the creation of content (lessons, tasks) by resource users (teachers for students), has not yet had its own lessons and lectures.
Eftsh.ru - offers video lectures recorded with the participation of school lecturers at MIPT. The set of proposed items is still small, but the approach to students is more individual. This service provides the ability to verify written work.
100ege.ru - perhaps stands out online lecture broadcasts and the ability to ask questions to the lecturer in the air. She has materials in mathematics, Russian language, physics, social science and computer science.
Interneturok.ru is a huge archive of all kinds of lectures in all subjects (unlike eftsh.ru and 100ege.ru, which are focused on physical science), but without any interaction with the student.
