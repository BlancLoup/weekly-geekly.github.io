Russian Android Robots

I read a user aidsoid note yesterday, which impressed me to the depths of my soul, and decided that it would not be superfluous to reveal the topic of Russian robotics a bit more.
The flagship of the Russian robot is the company Android Robots , the company conducts applied research to expand the capabilities of android robots with the help of additional equipment: navigation systems, hardware vision, hearing, telemetry.
In addition to its own developments, the company cooperates with world-renowned global manufacturers of android robots, promoting their products to the Russian market. A more detailed study turned out that the company has quite interesting own developments, for example, the model Robovie M3 , AR-100

Domestic developers, unlike foreign ones, do not position their creations as toys, these developments should demonstrate the capabilities of androids, attract attention to them, rather than prepare the market for perceiving the full-size (human-sized) Android AP400 model with a power of 400 W, which will be capable of practice doing physical work. In terms of labor productivity, one such robot, according to the creators, is approximately equal to four people. The design of the AP400 will require 4.5 - 5.5 million rubles. It is assumed that the main consumers of the AP400 will be production companies.
