Samsung W7100 - children's mobile phone with siren



What is a children's mobile phone? Minimum of functions, even lower prices, but bright colors and loud melodies. However, the melodies can be too loud.

The Samsung W7100 is such a loud phone. In this clamshell there is a GPS-module, but children do not offer a program for navigation. Satellite coordinates are sent to parents in case of alarm. And the alarm can give this phone. When you press a special button, the siren turns on, which yells at a volume of 100 decibels. , at the same time interested persons sends a message with the coordinates of the child. The built-in flashlight and the function that provides for sending a message to parents when they try to turn off the device also increase the safety of the precious child.

In general, the main thing that children do not listen to this siren on the bus.

It is assumed that juvenile users will like the special illumination of the clamshell cover, which has 36 modes. The device can glow cats, hearts and other garbage. Let, as they say, kids indulge.

Sales of the device will begin on March 13 in Korea, and then in North America, Electronista does not report anything about the price.
