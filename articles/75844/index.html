Some tips for a successful person

The idea has long been in the air, I want to tell the community about the conclusions I made from my life experience ...

Welcome to my world.

It's no secret that one of the main things in a person's life is learning. This is the first tip:
  • Study% username%!

Learn yourself, teach others, let others teach themselves, the main thing here is that all this happens with desire.
The second most important thing is communication. Generally speaking it is included in the first point, but I would like to pay attention once again:
  • Communicate!

Communicate with interesting people, people who can help you in your development, but also help yourself.
Moving on, maybe a slightly uncommon point:
  • Create!

From time to time, each person has ideas, especially if this person is connected to IT and reads Habr: D All of them, without exception, are very important and this leads us to the following point:
  • Write down!

No matter where the paper, the Internet, the main thing is to take them beyond the limits of your mind.
Do not stop there:
  • Unite!

Ideas are like atoms, if we merge them correctly, we will get a living cell, if we continue to do this ... we are already playing God.
Another important point in my opinion:
  • Think!

Every person always has something to think about - do not postpone.
And one more little touch:
  • Remember!

Everything new is well forgotten old.

And now a little magic :) The above tips are to varying degrees related to each other and you need to remember that. We will make of them an infinite cycle and the possibility of applying to any kind of creative activity, and we will get infinite space for our own, and not only our own, very productive activity.

Thanks for attention!
