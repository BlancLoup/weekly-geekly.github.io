Review of the smartphone ASUS ZenFone Max



Once the trees were big, the grass is greener, the sky is bluer, and mobile phones have been working on one charge for a week. And today it is impossible to imagine a day without using a charger. For modern cell phones, the battery is never too big, and that is why so much popular are pocket power banks. Against this background, curiosity is a new smartphone from ASUS - ZenFone Max, equipped with a capacious battery and able to charge other devices.

Appearance


Included with the ZenFone Max comes charging and headphones with two pairs of extra ear cushions.



The smartphone is available in two versions: with a black or white case. The design of the front side is typical for the Zen line: clean lines, rounded corners and a decorative panel at the bottom with a distinctive texture pattern.







The case cover is made of high quality plastic with a texture "under the skin." The tactile sensations are really very similar, and thanks to this, the smartphone is well kept in the palm of your hand. There is no hidden fear of dropping it, although the ZenFone Max is quite large.

At the bottom is a large mesh external speaker, and on top - the main camera lens, two-color flash, laser autofocus and one of two microphones.





Unlike many modern smartphones that look quite monotonous, the exterior of the ZenFone Max not only refreshes the textured back panel, but also the decorative frame that encloses the case around the perimeter. It is made of plastic color "bronze metallic", and made slightly convex, so the ribs of the smartphone do not cut into the palm. In addition, thanks to the frame, the substantial width of the side faces is not so noticeable - 6 mm.

Display


In its latest smartphone models, ASUS is no longer chasing high resolutions, limited to HD and Full HD. In my opinion, this is the best option for diagonals 5-6 inches. On the one hand, the image is absolutely smooth, the pixels are already indistinguishable, and on the other hand, the processor and the graphics chip do not have to strain too much.

The ZenFone Max used an IPS matrix with a diagonal of 5.5 inches and a resolution of 1280x720 (267 ppi). The display is covered with an oleophobic coated Gorilla Glass 4. The maximum level of brightness is not very high, but not too low - the average level. Color quality is good, balanced.

Photography


One of the features of ZenFone Max is the presence of laser autofocus for the main camera. The front camera also has a 5 megapixel matrix, so selfies should be better-quality.

The main camera is equipped with a f / 2.0 aperture lens, a 13 megapixel matrix and a two-color flash that optimizes color temperature when taking portraits. The same laser focusing allowed to reduce the reaction time. Traditionally for ASUS mobile gadgets, there are a lot of various shooting modes available, including fully manual, in which you can set:

  • white balance
  • exposure correction,
  • ISO,
  • excerpt
  • focusing distance.

The histogram allows you to more accurately assess the overall illumination and the difference in brightness.

Performance and work experience


The quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 , which operates at a frequency of up to 1.2 GHz and is equipped with the Adreno 306 graphics chip, is responsible for the operation of the smartphone. In terms of its performance, it refers rather to the middle segment.

The amount of RAM - 2 GB, this is more than enough for mobile tasks. Of the 16 GB of internal memory available to the user about 11. If desired, you can add another 64 GB as a microSD memory card.

Conclusion


This smartphone can be recommended to those, then often remains without communication due to too quickly - and at the wrong time - a shrunken smartphone. And at hand, unfortunately, there is no charging, or you're on the road. If you travel / fly a lot, you will certainly appreciate the ability of ZenFone Max to hold a charge.
