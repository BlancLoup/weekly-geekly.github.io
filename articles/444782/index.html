IT Global Meetup # 14 Petersburg

March 23, 2019 will be the fourteenth meeting of the IT-communities of St. Petersburg IT Global Meetup 2019.

Spring gathering of St. Petersburg IT-communities starts on Saturday! On the islets of the communities it will be possible to become familiar with their activities and take part in activities. ITGM is not a forum, not a conference. ITGM is a meeting created by the communities themselves with freedom of action, reports and activities.



Program


Over twenty IT communities of St. Petersburg will take part in the meeting, the program is represented by more than 60 reports on all the topics with which the IT community lives and breathes. This year we paid special attention to the English-language hall where we are waiting for interesting reports. Serious reports will be replaced by remarkable thematic quizzes and physical activities. Islands of communities are happy to receive guests and answer all questions.

The novelty in the spirit of informal mitap is HR confessional, describe all the sins in the previous work and all the fantasies about the future, and the information will never go beyond the walls of the confessional, and experienced HR will bless your resume.



And also in hall F, for workshops:

  • SPM Club - Documentation in IT Project, Development and Testing: Pros and Cons
  • SPM Club - Workshop: Such different identical teams
  • SPM Club - Round table: How to find and hold a joon?
  • Embedded Group - Tell me about the device made by yourself!
  • Embedded Group - Embedded freelance, how to live in Thailand and work with an oscilloscope remotely.

ITGM objectives


It is important for us to meet and communicate with each other, understand and study parallel directions, create joint projects, it is important to attract new people to the IT industry. Our motto is learning through communication!
