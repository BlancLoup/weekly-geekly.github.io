DevConf 2014: Development of a large scalable web 2.0 project from scratch (June 15, full day master class)

DevConf 2014 master class is dedicated to the development of the architecture of any typical large project. The main surprise is that for a very wide range of projects one architecture can be applied. It is 100% suitable for social networks, web stores, online games, media, blogs, Wikipedia analogues, entertainment portals and similar projects.

We took an interview from the leading master-class Dmitry Borodin:

Tell a few words about yourself?
I am engaged in programming more than 20 years. He has devoted the last 5 years not so much to programming, but to designing large scalable systems and data storage / processing methods. In addition to Topface, he worked and is still engaged in a number of projects, such as a team-leader and software architect. Always open to cooperation with new projects.

Can you say a few words about your master class besides what is in the description?

The conference presented many thematic reports or master classes. My training is to try to convey to all the full and proven algorithm for creating any major project. The key word is an integrated approach on all topics at once necessary for developing and launching a potentially large startup.
