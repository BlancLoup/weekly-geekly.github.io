New information technologies in the field of management


The increase in the volume of information and large changes in the demand for information have begun to place new demands on the organization of information and documentation services in the organization.
Requirements for information and document support services began to change. Abroad, they were called the service of management (or management) information and documentation resources, in our country, the service of documentation management of management (DOW).
The main direction of improving the DOE, adapting it to modern conditions was the use of the latest computer and telecommunications technology, the formation on its basis of highly efficient information and management technologies in working with documents.

One of the main factors influencing the scientific and technical progress on all spheres of human activity is the widespread use of new information technologies. Among the most important and massive areas in which information technology plays a crucial role, a special place is occupied by the sphere of management. Under the influence of new information technologies, fundamental changes are taking place in management technology (processes of substantiation and decision-making are being automated, organization of their implementation), and the skills and professionalism of specialists engaged in management activities are increasing.
The scope of application of new information technologies based on personal computers and developed means of communication is very extensive. It includes various aspects, from ensuring the simplest functions of official correspondence to system analysis and support for complex decision-making tasks. Personal computers, laser and optical equipment, media and various types of communication (including satellite communications) allow institutions, enterprises, companies, organizations, work teams and individual specialists to receive the necessary information for the implementation of professional, educational, cultural and similar purposes.
The meaning of the information provision is the organic combination of scientific knowledge, scientific methodology and methodology with the latest technical tools in all manifestations of information work.
In automatic systems, the information process can be implemented without human intervention. However, in automated systems, the mandatory collaborator of the information process is a person.
