Win-win contest testing. Show yourself a connoisseur - win laptop

A joint action of Intel, ASUS and Trendclub.ru is taking place in Moscow NOTIC shops. This is a win-win competition testing knowledge of Intel and ASUS products. Each participant receives a gift discount on the purchase of ASUS products based on Intel processor technology in the shops of the NOTIK network and, depending on the results of the competition, may win a stylish ASUS netbook and other prizes .

The competition, in accordance with the Rules , is available to participants who filled out the registration form prior to testing. If the participant is a member of TrendClub.ru, just enter your details. The competition consists in consecutive answers of the player to 12 closed questions of the test task during 3 game rounds. Questions on game tours are not duplicated. For correct answers, depending on the complexity of the questions, game points are awarded.

Those who get up to 600 points will earn 3% discounts, from 600 to 1300 points - 5%, from 1300 to 4000 - 10%. The discount is represented by a promotional code sent to the player to the postal address. You can take part in the contest until June 18. Buyers will be able to buy a laptop or ASUS laptop based on Intel at a discount in the NOTIK stores until September 1, 2010.
