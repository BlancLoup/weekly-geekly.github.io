Geek house



On the pages of this post we want to tell and show where the real geeks live ... when they have a desire to visit a place where you can not only sleep and work. The troll lives under the bridge, the gnome lives in a cave, a bear is sleeping in a den, and the geek prefers the Internet, i.e. we are talking about almost any place on the planet where there is a network. But Geek House cannot be a typical parallelepiped ferroconcrete monster, around which there are asphalt and concrete fields.

Going on a trip, you often lose the opportunity to choose housing according to your own ideas about the spirit of comfort. But everything in another house, starting with the doors and ending with telephones, a computer and a stove, can become as cozy as a favorite blog. To achieve the appropriate level in ordinary hotels is difficult. It is easier to find someone who shares your world views (at least in part) and is ready to rent out the relevant housing.

Castle, tree house, school building - strange, eccentric, intriguing ads appear on the Airbnb. Most of the beautiful, geeky houses in a good sense can only be seen in the pictures on the Internet. Therefore, we have collected a small selection of announcements of places that actually exist and are currently offered for rent. Not every day you see this.

By plane




Aircraft! Yes, this is a real, blue-white jet plane, which crossed the globe 3675 times, and is now temporarily available for housing. Located next to the airstrip of the lively Amsterdam Airport, the aircraft is provided with all amenities. That's how you can get in your own use 366 square meters of space, where you can find a living room, a bedroom, two children's beds, two kitchens and eight small bathrooms. There is Wi-Fi, a toaster, a coffee maker, a TV and a game console. And here are 116 windows! From the cockpit it is convenient to watch other planes that take off and land at the airport. For complete happiness on board there is a selection of aerokinoklassiki (including the film "Snake Flight").



We recognize: housing, which at any time can fly away, is not the most suitable choice for a traveler. An alternative "apartment", located in France, will never rise to the sky, but is still ready to receive passengers. By train




The bedroom in the 1935 carriage is not surprising, but there is also a bathroom, a full kitchen and a real stove. A rare place that looks nicer inside than outside (fans of trains may not agree with us). Electricity, sockets and other benefits of civilization in stock.

Those who have a railway in childhood caused indescribable delight, do not need additional explanations.
