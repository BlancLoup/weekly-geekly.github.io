Rating task managers used in web studios and online agencies of Russia

Hello to all!

We decided to suddenly release the results of a survey of leading Internet agencies in Russia on the subject of the solutions they use to automate process management and collaboration.

Here are the top 7 of this rating:

1. Megaplan 23.8%
2. 1C-Bitrix: Corporate portal 14.6%
3. Basecamp 11.6%
4. Redmine 8.5%
5. Worksection 7.3%
6. JIRA 4.9%
7. Google Docs 3.0%
7. Mantis Bug Tracker 3.0%

Under the cut - a little analytics.



By the end of 2011, it can be stated that in the Russian web development environment a market for systems for managing tasks and organizing collaboration began to take shape:
- automated task management is used in most medium and large Internet agencies (Top-500);
- on the Russian market there are clear leaders among the solutions of this class, with noticeable marketing activity and reliable technical support;
- The problem of task management techniques and methodologies is becoming an increasingly popular topic at industry conferences.

It is very pleasant that Russian companies managed to so quickly become leaders of the market, besides - some companies are seriously engaged in expansion into neighboring countries, as well as the USA and Western Europe. We wish them success.

In 2012, we will pay a lot of attention to various technical and managerial solutions that are used by Russian Internet agencies and Internet projects. If you are interested in specific tools, write to the comments, discuss, and be sure to take into account your opinion.
