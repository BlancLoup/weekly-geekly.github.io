10 secrets of Del.icio.us

Further I will give 10 "usefulness" simplifying work with the above site.


  • 1 . How to make friends with Del.icio.us?


    Browser add-ins allow you to save links with descriptions and tags, without leaving the page that you want to mark. Addons for Firefox and Internet Explorer are located on the Del.icio.us website in the Help -> Bokmarking section . The Flock browser can work with Del.icio.us without any add-ons.
    By the way, no one will tell you how to import bookmarks from del.icio.us to Firefox?

  • 2 How to set your bookmarks as a file?


    On the Del.icio.us website, all your bookmarks can be saved as an HTML file. The Snipr.com/delicious_xml resource allows you to export bookmarks in XML format.

  • 3 Del.icio.us as a piggy of wishes?


    Start the keyword WANTED or something like that and mark them with links to the things you are interested in online stores. A list of your desires will appear at del.icio.us/ your_nik / wanted. You will only have to share the link with your rich uncle on the eve of your birthday.

  • 4 How to share links with others?


    When saving, add a for tag : username and your link will appear in his Link for you list.

  • 5 Secrets of subscribing via RSS?


    Almost every page can be read via RSS.
    All links contain the tag - del.icio.us/rss/tag/ tag
    All links contain the tag1 and tag2 - del.icio.us/rss/tag/ tag1 +
    tag2
    All popular links - del.icio.us/rss/popular
    All popular links containing tag - del.icio.us/popular/ tag
    All user links - del.icio.us/rss/ username

  • 6 How to make Del.icio.us keep a diary for you?


    In the Settings -> Blogging section there are several widgets for bloggers: from the icon I use ‚ÄúDel.icio.us‚Äù to the widget with fresh bookmarks. The best friend of the lazy bloggers will be the Daily blog posting tool, which will collect all the links you have saved during the day and post them on your blog yourself.

  • 7 How to help site visitors add it to del.icio.us?


    In the Help -> "Save to del.icio.us" buttons there is a code that can be inserted into a web page or blog template. Owners of blogs on the WordPress engine will find many other plug-ins at Codex.wordpress.org/Plugins/Delicious .

  • 8 Search by del.icio.us?


    Del.icio.us search supports additional operators
    tag: - Restricts searching with tags ignores descriptions and headings
    system: media: video - Restricts searches to video files with the extensions * .MPG, * .AVI, * .WMV and * .MOV
    systemmedia: image - Restricts searching to links to * .MP3 and * .WAV format audio files.
    system: media: document - Restricts searches to documents in * .PDF and * .DOC format

  • 9 How to find sites like my favorite?


    The Similicio.us project uses del.icio.us user preferences database to search for sites of similar subjects. Enter the address of the site you are interested in and the system prompts potentially interesting links.

  • 10 How to make the titles, descriptions and tags added to my links by others?


    The customizable Lazy Sheep bookmark saves you from having to manually provide links with titles, descriptions and tags. Part of the information "lazy sheep" will take from the descriptions made by users Del.icio.us, part - from the text, which you highlight on the saved page.
