Freelancers note

Freelancers often recommend their customers to pay for work through the terminals.

This is convenient for customers who do not always know what e-money is, but use terminals to pay for everyday services. And for the payment you need only 2 steps :
  • Write the wallet number, usually Yandex.Money or WebMoney
  • In the terminal, enter the purse number and insert the agreed amount into the bill acceptor.
It's simple, but there are several inconveniences when paying in terminals:
  • The freelancer cannot determine that the work has been paid for, since payments come without comment (the customer has to pull the TP to find out the transaction number). This is especially true if the freelancer has many customers and payments.
  • There is a commission in the terminals and the freelancer risks getting a smaller amount on his wallet, and the client has to run to the terminal again to report 10-50 rubles. Even despite the presence in some terminals calculator commissions.
