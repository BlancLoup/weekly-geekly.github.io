Wii U - a new hybrid from Nintendo


In fact, I consider this megacorporation one of the most significant driving forces of the video game industry. Suffice it to recall how much dirt was first poured on the Nintendo DS and Wii. Actually, the follower of the latter will be discussed under the cut. So, welcome - Nintendo Wii U:


How will the Japanese delegation surprise us this time? * drum roll * New revolutionary controller. Not original? Come on!
