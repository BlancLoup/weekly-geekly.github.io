Top 10 torrent trackers over the hill

Below is a list of the most visited torrent sites for 2016. In early 2016, KickassTorrents was in the lead, followed by The Pirate Bay torrent tracker. Much has happened lately, some torrent sites have disappeared, others have taken their place "under the sun."



Users of course get used to this or that site, but in case of its disappearance / blocking, they gladly, without disdain, jump over to others. This trend is also traced according to the data of the new list of top torrents. The first positions are held by well-known old players, but a couple of torrent newbies also appeared. After 10 years of work, the popular torrent resource EZTV was closed in May last year. Back in early spring 2015, EZTV lost its domain name due to incorrect information in the Whois section. The domain name eztv.it was put up for sale, the scammers immediately successfully bought it. Therefore, we note this torrent resource as newly minted.



Something similar happened with the YTS / YIFY torrent tracker site. YTS / YIFY is a well-known release group and a popular torrent resource, appeared on the market in 2010, at first the release group uploaded torrents to PublicHD, KickassTorrents, 1337x, The Pirate Bay and ExtraTorrent. The first release of the group was the DVD-rip cartoon "Toy Story" (1, 2 part). The year the group worked on reputation, after which it launched its website YTS. YIFY used to be called the unsinkable submarine. Starting in 2012, he confidently gained search traffic. In October 2015, without explanation or reason, he went to the offline site YTS, behind which stood the well-known release group YIFY. MPAA (American Cinema Rating System) reached the YIFI YTS group, went out to its operator, who lived in New Zealand, was threatened with a multi-million claim before it, if it does not cooperate with MPAA. And so, it only cost the site to "sink into oblivion," as the unofficial site YTS.ag immediately rebelled, which in a couple of months had already managed to build a user base.

Apparently, the majority of users of torrent resources are happy as long as they can get what they want, no matter what source they distribute the most desired)

KickassTorrents (CAT) remains the most visited torrent resource and continues to grow. The Pirate Bay came in second place, despite the suspension of work for two months, during which the annual turnover was lost. Of course, Rutracker would make some torrents by the number of visitors ... but, the authors of the article decided to make only representatives of the anglonet in the top.

Below is a complete list of the top 10 most visited torrent sites at the beginning of the new 2016. Some sites will be rated according to the Alexa Rank rating system. The system itself is based on counting the total number of page views and the frequency of visits to a particular resource. The Alexa Rank is the ratio of the attendance of one resource and the attendance of other Internet portals; therefore, the lower the Alexa Rank number, the more popular the resource.

The resource appeared in 2009 and last year surpassed The Pirate Bay in traffic. Recently, often jumped from one domain zone to another. For the history of its existence, there were not a few complaints, then about the plagiarism of the design, then about copyright infringement, was excluded from Google search (MPAA tried).
