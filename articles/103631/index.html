Thoughts about IFA: 3D, tablets and other imposed crap

Everything here is clear: when the production of televisions became less profitable, brands needed a new driver, because the HD-picture with all the special effects did not give a wow effect. We got cardboard glasses with multicolored glass pieces from the mezzanine, shook off the dust from them, made a rebranding and - voila! - Already that year the television industry is rolling in a 3D direction.
