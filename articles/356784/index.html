The company's servers were removed, and about a dozen of them, including the domains of subsidiary services, were hung on the Megaupload domains.

However, some time ago it turned out that the stub disappeared, and the domains now lead to advertisements of various kinds of fake antiviruses and other types of malware. As far as you can understand, the US Department of Justice lost control of its former name servers, which allowed attackers to use their own goals.
