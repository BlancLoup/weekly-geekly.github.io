Web development issues in the middle lane

Is it easy to have a complicated website not in a big city like Moscow or St. Petersburg, but in a province like Tula? We talked about this with Evgeny Ryzhkov, the CEO of OOO Program Verification.

- Hi Zhenya! Tell me what you and your company do in a nutshell?
- We are developing, promoting and selling our own software product PVS-Studio . Since we are in a Russian province, and more than 50% of sales are in the US, it is clear that we attach great importance to the website. And during our life I have accumulated a lot of things about the problems of the web industry. So now I will express it all :-).

- What state do you think the web development market is today: clean, rolling, incomprehensible, professional, or vice versa?
- Unfortunately, we have not grown to the Moscow web development market, for sure Moscow web studios are cool. But as for the provincial market - it's terrible. If you need a site a little more complicated than a site of a travel agency with 10 pages, then you have a problem. For example, we have about a thousand pages on viva64.com with unique (really unique) text - it seems that no one is developing such sites in the city. All the developers with whom we spoke, said: "It is very difficult, they do not do that." Rather, everyone gladly took it, but then it turned out that nothing worked.
In the end, we had to start a full-time web developer and make (and then maintain) a website on our own.

- Proposals to make a "rollback" or enter into informal relations from customers come?
- As I said, most of our clients are in the US, and this is not accepted there, or it is inconvenient to discuss slippery topics via the Internet. But we have never heard anything from Russian clients about kickbacks. If people need a product, then they come and just buy. So it is quite possible to live without kickbacks. As for kickbacks, this is Navalny, he is a specialist.

- Zhenya, I wanted to talk to you about the staff. How is your Tula with frames? How do you find employees in your company?
- I think the situation with personnel is like everywhere else. Adequate people are always few. And not only in Tula, but also in Moscow. And even the cool name of the company (I'm certainly not talking about myself, but about ‚ÄúLebedev Studio,‚Äù for example) does not save the situation. So when choosing employees for ourselves, we, first of all, try to look at the overall adequacy, and not at the knowledge of any specific keywords.
