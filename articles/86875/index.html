"Three Bananas" or notes on the go in Android


I decided to unload my memory by making all sorts of notes, thoughts, etc. in the communicator HTC Hero, the happy owner of which I am. I wanted something more than just notes:
  1. The ability to attach photos
  2. The ability to assign tags / tags
  3. Synchronization with online service and access via web or PC client
  4. Ability to work in the absence of a network connection (for example, in the metro)
  5. Search by notes

What did I choose? The answer is under the cut.

The first thing that came to mind was the Evernote client for Android, however, it fell off on step 4. At point 1, such programs as Astrid and others like him were dropped out. They are designed for text notes.
And I came across the program "3banana" . She provided everything I needed, and even more:
  1. There is a client for Android, iPhone and Web
  2. Synchronization with the snaptic.com service using a free account or your google account
  3. Attaching photos
  4. Organizing notes with hashtags
  5. Export to SD card
  6. Integration into the Android system - the ability to attach photos from the gallery to notes, and from the browser links.
