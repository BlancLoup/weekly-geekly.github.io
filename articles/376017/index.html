Luna mattress to turn an ordinary bed into a "smart"

The Luna mattress pad controls the temperature of the bed, monitors the quality of sleep, acts as an alarm clock, integrates into Beep, Lockitron, Emberlight and Nest smart home systems and connects to devices from other manufacturers via Wi-Fi. The device has two temperature zones, the control is carried out using a smartphone.

The project in six hours collected at Indiegogo the necessary $ 100,000, for three days the amount exceeded $ 370,000. The campaign will last another 27 days.




One third of life a person sleeps. The quality of sleep affects the health and efficiency of work. The developers of the project Luna talk about the importance of temperature for a comfortable sleep: it depends on how long a person falls asleep and how he sleeps. The mattress pad has two temperature zones, providing a comfortable sleep for two users at the same time. Luna remembers the usual time of your sleep and adjusts the comfortable temperature for fast sleep.







The device tracks the sleep phase and has the function of "smart" alarm. Luna can be controlled using a smartphone. The mattress pad is equipped with sensors of body temperature, environment, respiratory rhythm, intensity, heart rate monitor.





The device is put on the mattress and is connected to the mains. The developers claim that it has passed all the necessary tests, it is completely safe and can be washed in a typewriter. Four types of sizes are available.



Luna works with systems designed for smart homes. At the moment, there are four main partners - Lockitron , Emberlight , Beep and Nest. Developers report connectivity via Wi-Fi to devices from manufacturers Philips Hue, WeMo, Lifx, Honeywell, Dropcam. Integration with third-party devices will allow you to automatically close the lock-equipped door before bedtime, set the desired temperature using the Nest thermostat and adjust the light thanks to Emberlight.



For the first 6 hours since the launch on Indiegogo, the project collected the necessary $ 100,000, for three days the amount exceeded $ 370,000. There are still 27 days left until the end of the campaign.
