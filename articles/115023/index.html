Google, unite!

It is easy to see that Google has all the components of a social network, only separated by different sites:

personal business card page (Google Profiles);
search for people by their personal data (Google Profiles search);
messaging system (Gmail);
social graph (Gmail contacts);
Activity Ribbon (Google Buzz, also known as Buzz);
quick messages (GTalk);
photographs (Picasa);
location (google maps).

If you take all these mosaic elements and combine them into one whole, put them on one platform, comb them into a single design and show an intelligible way of working with all this, we will get a full-fledged social network and a competitor of all the world facebook. I really hope that this goes, because Google services are very impressive to me personally. Yes, and I have an order of magnitude more friends in Gmail than in all social networks combined. Google, unite finally!

PS Yes, Google, when will you fix a bug with disappearing or not appearing comments in a live tape? Already a year this bug, got it, honestly.
