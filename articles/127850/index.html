Increase the number of new computers with Ubuntu

The UALinux company and the COMFY and City.com shopping centers (Kiev) are launching a joint program - Ubuntu operating system will be installed on new computers (laptops) that are delivered to the shopping centers without an operating system.

Now, not only individual models, but also a wide range of other models from well-known manufacturers such as Toshiba , Lenovo , Acer, and others, standing on the shelves of stores, can be purchased with Ubuntu pre-installed.

The advantages of this program - the user, without additional time and money to buy the necessary software, acquiring a new computer, gets a full-fledged computer, where you can immediately get to work.
