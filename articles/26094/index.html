Free hosting for students!

.masterhost, in collaboration with Microsoft, offers free hosting services for university students for educational and research purposes, as well as for non-commercial projects.

According to the "Student" tariff, a virtual platform is provided free of charge with a free third-level domain in the zone studenthost.ru and the ability to attach second-level domain names to it as synonyms.

In order to take advantage of the offer and order a virtual platform at the rate of "Student", you must register at masterhost.ru
After registration, you should submit a "request for a free topic" from the control panel, section Applications and orders.
