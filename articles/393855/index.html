The notification "upgrade to Windows 10 or die" will stop showing users from July 30

As already reported on Geektimes , for free, up to Windows 10, current users of Windows 7 or Windows 8.1 can only upgrade to July 29 . After this date you will have to pay for the update. But what happens with the notification of the application "Get Windows 10", which is so tired of those who do not want to be updated?

Microsoft reports that after July 29th, Get Windows 10 will be removed from users' PCs around the world. Accordingly, the notification will disappear. True, it may take some time until the application is removed from the computers of all Windows users: from July 30, Microsoft will gradually remove its application from the PC to Windows 7 and Windows 8.1.

Probably, this news will please those users who are not going to be updated. Of course, nothing prevents the corporation from changing its decision, and leaving the offer to upgrade to Windows 10 in force even after the expiration of the free application. However, so far the plan is exactly like this: after July 29, there are no free updates, but the invitation to update also disappears.

After July 29, buying a license for Windows 10 Home will cost the user $ 119, and Windows 10 Pro will cost $ 199. As for those users who have already updated for free, they will be able to work with their version of the operating system until the PC is changed.
