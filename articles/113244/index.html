The film, shot on Canon 7D, bought for $ 4 million

It seems that cameras are very close to the cinematic quality of shooting. At the last independent film festival, Sundance, a landmark event took place: the Like Crazy film not only took the main festival award , but was also bought by Paramount Pictures for $ 4 million . The most amazing thing about this story is that the film was completely shot with a Canon 7D camera (more precisely, with a modified version of the Hot Rod 7D-PL with a film lens).



The film does not have its own website, there is only a small commercial .



By the way, a month ago a wonderful selection of music videos shot on iPhone was published on the Internet. They look surprisingly professional.

True, this does not mean at all that any iPhone owner can do something similar, just as any camera owner can make a movie and sell it for millions of dollars.
