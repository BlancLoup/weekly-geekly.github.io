YouTube shows Adblock Plus users an error instead of advertising ... and the video itself.


YouTube Office in Mumbai (photo: YouTube)

Some users instead of advertising and part of the video now see this nice message: ‚ÄúAn error occurred. After a series of tests, online media journalists Venturebeat claim that the problem is relevant only to Chrome with Adblock Plus, and the Firefox browser extensions work just fine.
