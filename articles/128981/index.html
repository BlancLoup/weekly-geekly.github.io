The implementation turned out to be impossible and very effective.

A little bit about the purpose of the program:

  • Connection of several sound programs in a chain so that each successive program receives sound directly from the previous one, without any intermediate devices or operations.
  • Transmit digital audio in unchanged form, without any loss of sound quality.
  • Preservation in an invariable digital form of the sound signal created by the programs, allowing only to play a signal in real time on the sound adapter.
  • Mixing sound signals from various programs connected to one end of the cable.

The multiplication of the audio signal transmitted over the cable for transmission to several programs simultaneously.
