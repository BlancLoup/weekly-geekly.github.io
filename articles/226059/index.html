Samsung announces Russia Tizen App Challenge contest for web developers from Russia

Good day and good weekend, Habr!

While some of you are impressed by the Samsung Gear 2 smart watches, others are completely involved in the Gear App Challenge competition , while others are following the news from the Tizen Developer Conference in San Francisco, Samsung announces a competition for web developers in Russia Tizen App Challenge with a total prize pool of 7 million rubles!


Participants are nominated in the following categories:

1. Non-game application
a. First prize - 1 200 000 rubles.
b. Second prize - 900,000 rubles.
c. The third prize is 700,000 rubles.

2. Game application
a. First prize - 1 400 000 rubles.
b. Second prize - 1 000 000 rubles.
c. The third prize is 800,000 rubles.

Also, awards will be awarded 5 applications for a smartphone running Tizen with Samsung Gear 2 support. Each winner will receive 200,000 rubles each.

The competition is divided into the following stages:

To participate you must:
1. Register for the Russia Tizen App Challenge contest on the official contest page .
2. Create an app using Tizen SDK .
3. Get certified in the Tizen Store .
4. Complete the registration on the official page of the contest and submit an application for participation.

Also remember the following:
1. Only applications in wgt format are accepted for the competition.
2. Type of apps with Samsung Gear 2 support - Linked or Integrated .
3. Tizen Gear SDK, which allows you to develop an application with Samsung Gear 2 support using an emulator, will be available on June 27, 2014.

Full competition rules are presented on the official Russia Tizen App Challenge website. Hurry up to participate!
