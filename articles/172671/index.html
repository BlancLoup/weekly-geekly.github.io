Google arranges spring cleaning and bans AdBlock in Android

Google in one day managed to accumulate a lot of news: and assign Pichai a new Android boss, and arrange spring cleaning services, and clean up Google Play from applications that block ads. Everything in order.

The search giant has taken the annual tradition of closing services in batches. Often these were services that you only learn from news and closing, and often useful services like Google Code Search were also closed . Today, Google, unfortunately, chose the latter. So, the most important and sad:
  • Google Reader - more in the post rapida
  • Google Cloud Connect - plug-in for MS Office, which allows you to save documents to the Google cloud, closes in favor of Google Drive. Date of execution - April 30 of this year.
  • CalDAV API - from September 16 it will be available only for developers from the white list. Everyone else will have to use the Calendar API . Want to get on the white list? Write here .
  • Google Building Maker will no longer be available on June 1, as Google believes that it will cope with 3D-models of buildings, and for other needs go Google Map Maker
  • GUI Builder and UiApp widgets will be closed in favor of HTML Service

In addition, the Google Voice for Blackberry application, the Search API for the Shopping project and Snapseed for Windows and Mac OS X fell under the distribution.

Blocking anti-advertising applications in Android


Google today delighted all AdBlock-type application developers with the news that all their applications are blocked in accordance with clause 4.4 of the Developer Distribution Agreement , which states:
Prohibited actions
You agree not to take actions (including the development and distribution of Products) that disrupt or interrupt the operation of devices, servers, networks and other objects and services of third parties, including Android users, Google and mobile operators, as well as other damage. You may not use customer information obtained on the Android Market to sell or distribute Products outside of the Android Market.


So, AdBlock Plus (and Habr was warmly met by him ), AdFree , AdBlocker and others got under the distribution. It is clear that, formally, these applications do harm the tools for monetizing applications through advertising, but the step itself is not very beautiful. AdBlock for Firefox seems to work as it should, so you can save yourself from advertising on the Internet.

PS Nevertheless, you can download ABP directly from the official site and install it on your Android device through sideloader tools.
