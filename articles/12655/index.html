Online poker becomes social

To the delight of poker fans, a new site K9Poker.com has appeared on the Internet, combining a gaming service where you can fight with partners, and a social network.

Gamblers can create their own profiles, blog, participate in discussions, share information and, of course, play. The new site may become a competitor to Partypoker.com, which, according to ComScore Media Metrix, is the most popular in this area, gathering more than 29 million visitors a month.

It should be noted that online poker enjoys great interest from American Internet users, despite the ban on online gambling.
