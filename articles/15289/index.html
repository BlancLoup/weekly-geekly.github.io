After a whois check, a free domain lives for two minutes.

Many of us faced an unpleasant situation: you invent an original domain name for your project, check it with a registrar and make sure that the name is free. When you decide to register it the next day or in a couple of days, it suddenly turns out to be busy! Intuition tells you that you have been deceived.
