Collecting funds in iTunes: support for victims in Nepal

A page appeared in the Russian iTunes through which funds are raised to help earthquake victims in Nepal. 100% of all donations will be transferred to the Red Cross Charitable Foundation, which provides first aid, search and rescue of victims. Anyone wishing to participate in the collection of financial assistance can transfer funds through iTunes or the official Red Cross app .
