Open Source Networking meet-up - now in Yandex.Cloud # 3.2019



On May 20, we invite everyone who is interested in the subject of Open Source Networking, to the third event of the OSN Meetup series this year. Event organizers: Yandex.Oblako and the Russian community of Open Source Networking.

About Open Source Networking User Group Moscow


The Open Source Networking User Group (OSN User Group Moscow) is a community of enthusiastic people who discuss ways to change network infrastructure using open source solutions such as: DPDK, FD.io, ONAP, OpenDaylight, OPNFV, PNDA, and SNAS other solutions. The group discusses face-to-face issues, shares ideas, and works together to overcome software-defined network (SDN) problems, network function virtualization (NFV), management and orchestration (MANO), cloud technologies, data analysis and improving the basic network infrastructure.

Records of previous events can be found on the YouTube channel .

To stay up to date, join our chat in the Telegram and Meetup group .
