On March 16-17, the All-Russian selection round of the web-designers competition was held in Obninsk, Kaluga region, which is held as part of the Webdesign Interactive Festival.

In total, 13 teams from Obninsk, Kaluga, Dimitrovgrad and Saratov took part in the All-Russian qualifying round.

On April 2, 2012, according to the results of the All-Russian qualifying round of the web designers competition, the winners in the Professionals group were the team from the Internet agency ProjeX from Obninsk, and the RUSSIAN ROULETTE team from Dimitrovgrad was recognized in the Students group.
