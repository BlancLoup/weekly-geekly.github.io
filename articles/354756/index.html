DevConf 2018 - vote for reports

May 18, 2018 DevConf will be held in Digital October (Moscow) - a large annual conference for web developers, where they discuss about the newest in the world of web programming. The program is determined by voting for the reports .


In the BackEnd section: the future of PHP, thinking with aggregates, service-oriented architecture, a little about blockchains, difficulties in rewriting a project, tracing, profiling, etc.

In the FrontEnd section: React, GraphQL, Node.JS, WebRTC video calls, TVs, tests and developer PhantomJs.

In the Storage section: PostgreSQL, MySQL, ProxySQL, SphinxSearch, ClickHouse and a little (a lot) about data compression.

In the DevOps section: Service mesh, Kubernetes, and also about static code analysis.

In the Management section: introducing testing, introducing Agile, how to pay employees, grow them from scratch, and protect intellectual property.

Workshops scheduled for May 19: architecture of highload projects, writing supported code on the example of a highload project, Apollo GraphQL and MySQL in the clouds.

Registration at a discount for habrayuzerov.
