New Valve Steam Machine Details


The day before yesterday, some new information about Valve's Steam Machine game console concerning the filling of promising devices became known. It is reported that the Steam Machine from the inside will be similar to a gaming PC for Windows with an Intel Core processor of various modifications, 16 GB DDR3 memory and a hybrid disk from 1 to 8 TB. As a video card, Nvidia GeForce GTX 660 will be used in low-end models and GTX 760/780 in more expensive ones.
Valve representatives emphasize that the Steam Machine will be assembled from standard components, and users will be able to upgrade it. Finally, the latest news from Valve, while in the form of a hint: for those who are not going to buy a separate PC for games, the company is preparing a solution for broadcasting Steam games from a computer in another room to HD TV.
