For DDoS attacks in Sweden will give real time

Swedish hackers planning DDoS attacks should hurry to do it before June 1 - it is from this day that criminal liability will be introduced in the country for this type of computer crime, The Local website writes.

According to the amendments, which are planned to be introduced into already existing laws (which do not envisage penalties for a computer attack aimed at denial of service), attackers who implemented a DDoS attack will be able to sit behind the grid for up to two years.
