PhpBB modification of authorization through Vkontakte, Yandex, Google, OpenID and etc.

3 months have passed since the announcement and launch of the Loginza project by our company to the masses. During this time, a lot of work has been done: we optimized the speed of loading and working of the widget, small cosmetic edits, but we also expanded the list of authorization providers supported in the widget.

In the widget added such monsters as Vkontakte, Facebook, Twitter and Last.fm. The partnership with mail.ru helped us to integrate with api.mail.ru more flexible and with a larger set of profile data than the previous authorization through openid.mail.ru.

But the most important thing is the completion of a number of plug-ins for CMS such as Joomla , vBulletin , Drupal , the Wordpress engine blog, PrestaShop online store engine, but also for the popular phpBB forum engine . Actually about him and speech.

PhpBB was chosen by us not by chance, but because it is the most common forum engine, it is free and quite functional.

PhpBB modification (MOD) Loginza - allows you to use for authorization / registration accounts of popular portals, social networks, as well as OpenID identifiers in the phpBB forum. And these are 14 authorization providers : Yandex, In contact with, Facebook, Twitter, Loginza, Myopenid Google
Flickr Last.fm, Mail.Ru, Rambler, Webmoney, AOL and VeriSign.
