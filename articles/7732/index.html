ICQ will be able to communicate with Gtalk users

07/18/2006 21:07, Text - Yuri Synodov

As it became known to Vebplanet, over the next 6-12 months, the ICQ client will support interaction with Gtalk users. Galia Resnick, regional business manager of ICQ, reported this to the Vebplaneta correspondent at the celebration of the 10th anniversary of ICQ.
