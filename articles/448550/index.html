The competition of reports on #PAYMENTSECURITY 2019 is open

For many representatives of the payment industry of the countries of the European Union and the CIS over the past three years, the #PAYMENTSECURITY conference has become a comfortable platform for sharing experiences and gaining new knowledge in the field of financial technology and information security.

July 4 and 5, 2019. FINTECH and INFOBEZ. We again mix and shake up two elements in the Pavlovsky conference hall of the Crowne Plaza St. Petersburg Airport hotel.

#PAYMENTSECURITY 2019 in St. Petersburg. Registration is open ! White nights included!


The call for papers will run until June 15, 2019. Traditionally, the criteria for selecting reports for #PAYMENTSECURITY are the novelty and usefulness of content for the audience. No sponsorship package includes the right to report. That is why we are interested.

If you have something to say on the security of payment and financial technologies, smart contracts, retail, e-commerce or fraud, if you know how to write secure code, use artificial intelligence to protect your assets, and you want to share it - wait you on CFP . A professional, highly motivated audience of the conference #PAYMENTSECURITY 2019, which includes the creators and owners of payment services and other FINTECH companies, industry regulators, information security specialists, IT managers, developers and system administrators, and penetration testing and security experts smart contracts and many more interesting people.

For anyone who wants to assist in organizing an event, we offer options for partner participation , giving the opportunity to participate in the exhibition part of the conference hall, as well as other options to declare ourselves. In 2018, we were visited by about two hundred delegates. How it was, you can read here .

For representatives of the payment industry: banks, payment services, shops, restaurants, hotels, e-commerce enterprises, industry regulators, as well as payment solutions manufacturers and NP ABIS members, participation is free of charge.

For speakers whose reports successfully pass the competition in the program committee, participation is always free, regardless of which company they represent.
