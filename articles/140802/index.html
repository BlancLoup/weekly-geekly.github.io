- Career without experience and, having completed a training course with final testing, get down to work.



To master the profession allows the program of study, which includes information about package tours, the rules of paperwork, customs clearance, stay in tourist countries, etc. For the convenience of users, a terminological dictionary in Russian and English, an interactive map of the world and other services have been created.

After passing the introductory course, in addition to the nominal certificate confirming the qualification, the user gets access to testing in 17 countries.



Access to booking tours in each of the 17 countries is opened after passing testing for one selected country. The beginner is provided with the same tools for work as a professional, namely:

- System for searching and booking tours through the Internet office
- A mechanism for comparing tours of several parameters
- Client's data transmission system to the tour operator
- The mechanism of informing the client about booking the tour

The main task of an independent tourism consultant is to build a long-term relationship with the client, helping to find the most attractive offers from tour operators. The duties of the user who chose the profession "Tourism" include: studying the products of leading tour operators, finding and attracting clients, choosing the best travel option, consulting and booking tours through the tour operator database.
