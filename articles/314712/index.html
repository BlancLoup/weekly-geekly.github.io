Recall everything: how to quickly recover data that was mistakenly changed by the user.



Often there are situations when company employees come to a system administrator or a technical support service and say that they have changed some data in 1C or operating system settings ... and forgot what they are.

There are two standard solutions to this situation:

  1. Analysis of journals (if any), to understand what has been changed.
  2. Restore data from backup.

The first option may take a lot of time, and not all changes are placed in the logs. The second option to realize even longer, besides the restoration may be lost fresh changes.

Therefore, I would like to propose a simple and effective way using an additional tool.

The Kickidler Employee Monitoring System records all activities of all employees of the organization on video. So this video is enough to view to understand what has been changed.

So let's imagine that Kickidler is already implemented in your company. If this is not the case, the installation will take less than an hour.
