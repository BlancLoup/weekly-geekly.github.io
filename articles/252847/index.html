Apple's disappointment, Unity and Epic Games generosity, Paris Hilton game - and other news of the week for a mobile developer



The long-awaited Apple Watch


On an Apple presentation last day, Apple showed its watch to the world. Disappointment - this is how one can say the reaction of users to a new representative of the wearable device market First of all, the price is disappointing, the battery life (the battery lasts no more than a day) and the need for an iPhone.

Not only users, but also investors are disappointed: in the morning, the company's shares fell by more than 2%. Analysts from Pacific Crest Securities believe that Apple will be able to sell the first few batches of watches to fans of the brand, but there is no great success in the market. And in the investment company Piper Jaffray, they suggested that the Apple Watch, given the design and brand, would rather compete with regular watches than wearable devices.

The situation was aggravated by the immediate and accurate response of competitors, who immediately offered several alternative smart watches. Google responded with a 15-second video, hinting at the variety of forms and capabilities of wearable devices for Android.



Apple Watch will be available for purchase in the US and Europe, starting from April 10, the release date in Russia is still unknown. The watches will be produced in three lines:
-Watch Sport for $ 350-400;
- base model for $ 550-1100;
- premium model Watch Edition for $ 10,000-17,000.

In the meantime, the whole world, with bated breath, waiting for the release of smart watches, we offer you to get acquainted with collections of what can be bought instead of the Apple Watch and Apple Watch Edition .


Unity 5 is not for sale!




Under this heading, two news at once: Unity 5 Personal Edition is available for free , although under certain conditions, and Unity has officially denied rumors about its sale.

The rumors appeared after it became aware of a serious expansion of the list of free features that will be available in the new version of the engine.

Although the rumors were partially true, or rather, Unity will provide almost the entire package of new engine features for developers for free, but only if the investment in the company or its earnings were less than $ 100,000 in the previous year. Otherwise, you will have to buy a license, for which you must pay either $ 1,500 or $ 75 per month.

‚ÄúThis version offers all the powerful functionality of the Unity engine and editor, including profiler, Occlusion Culling, render textures, full-screen special effects, physically correct shading, powerful graphics with a new set of tools for creating realistic lighting and stunning visual effects, a new system for working with audio "- stated in the official press release of the company.

As for the features that are available only in the paid version:
  • Unity Cloud Build Pro;
  • Team License Team License;
  • service for monitoring the game performance Game Performance Reporting;
  • access to Asset Store Level 11;
  • Unity Analytics Pro analytics service;
  • access to previews and beta versions of the engine.

It is important to note that in both the paid and free versions, no interest on sales will be charged to companies!

Anonymous Login from Facebook




Facebook announced the possibility of anonymous authorization in applications. Developers can now leave a request to participate in beta testing features on a special page . The new functionality, according to the social network, will increase conversions and help people start using the application faster, while maintaining their anonymity.

Whether Facebook will give any data about the user in this mode is still unknown. But such authorization through Facebook will allow users to log in to applications through different devices. In addition, users will have the opportunity to quite simply replace ‚ÄúAnonymous Login‚Äù with a regular ‚ÄúFacebook Login‚Äù.
