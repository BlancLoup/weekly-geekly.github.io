New payment methods and a 20% discount on a premium subscription in April



On the occasion of the anniversary of the launch of Evernote in Russia, we have prepared a surprise for our users.

We made friends with a large online software store Softkey , and now you can buy a premium subscription Evernote. This means that now the premium can be paid using a variety of payment systems and methods. Here are some of them:

  • Yandex.Money ;
  • Personal Account QIWI ;
  • WebMoney ;
  • RBK Money ;
  • Stream.ru ;
  • Dom.ru ;
  • Telepay ;
  • HandyBank ;
  • MoneyMail ;
  • Platezh.Ru ;
  • cash and cashless payments.

Here is such an impressive list. We also recall that Evernote still supports payment by bank cards, that is, we finally covered the entire list of popular payment instruments and, we hope, made the life of our Russian users as easy as possible.

On the occasion of the announced partnership and the already mentioned round date, we decided to give our users a 20% discount when they buy an annual Evernote premium subscription via softkey. In order to take advantage of the discount, you can simply follow this link and pay for a one-year subscription.

Please note that the discount will be valid only until the end of April!

After a year, to renew your Evernote subscription, simply go to your Evernote account settings and you will see the soft key in the list of available payment methods.

I would also like to remind you that the Evernote service completely free of charge provides users with all the basic functionality necessary for comfortable work with notes, and we are constantly expanding its capabilities. At the same time, more and more people who actively use the service in work and life are acquiring a premium subscription in order to increase the amount of created notes from 40 to 500 megabytes per month and ensure access to an additional set of functions.

In particular, the following options are available for premium users:
  • add and synchronize files of any type;
  • offline notebooks on the iPhone and iPod Touch;
  • indexing text in images inside a pdf file;
  • functions of joint editing of notes in notebooks;
  • the lack of ad units in client applications for Windows, Mac and the web interface;
  • answer from technical support within 1 business day;
  • priority in the queue to the servers for text recognition in images;
  • increased security due to SSL encryption of all data.

More information about the benefits of a premium account can be on our website .
