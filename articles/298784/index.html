What mistakes we made on Product Hunt and how to avoid them

On August 26, 2015, we (Rubrain.com - the catalog of the best Russian freelancers. The new project of the former top managers on Fl.ru) was published on Product Hunt . And although we scored a sufficient number of votes to enter the TOP-10, we took the 15th place. In this article, I want to talk about what rake we stepped on, how to get the maximum effect from the site and what to expect from Product Hunt in general.



Product Hunt is a community of people interested in new products and technologies. According to the legend, entering the TOP-10 (even better in the TOP-3 days or TOP-10 weeks) at the end of the voting day allows you to get into the e-mail newsletter of the next day and get press attention, investment proposals and world-wide fame. Voting takes place after 0:00 and before 23:59 Pacific time (US West Coast: San Francisco, Seattle, Los Angeles, etc .; -11 hours difference with Moscow). To participate and vote, you need a Twitter account.

Before you begin work on preparing your project for publication on Product Hunt, analyze which services receive the maximum number of votes and attention. You may find that every kind of aggregator and catalog is a success, as well as really cool products like Tesla, Nebia Shower, HyperLapse, etc. The first place in the list of " 50 Most Upvoted Products " is the Startup Stash project. It is his story promotion on Product Hunt, you will find when searching for success stories. Enter * in the search field on the Product Hunt website and you will see the list of products with the most votes.


What projects are published on Product Hunt:
  • Services: Uber, AirBnb and their ilk. You must have a landing page describing this particular service. The company's page with a description of all its products and services will not work.
  • Mobile applications: iOS, Android and Windows Phone. The link can lead to both the download page and the landing page.
  • Computer programs
  • Active campaigns (Pre-Launch) to raise funds: your project on Kickstarter, Indiegogo or Tilt.com
  • Games
  • Podcasts
  • Books: UX-design, entrepreneurship, startups. Since these books have a rather narrow target audience, publishing on PH helps them to gain fame among Central Asia.


What projects are NOT published on Product Hunt:
  • T-shirts and other small items
  • Non-community links
  • Services


How is the vote
The site administration does not disclose the list of criteria. According to our observations, the position in the rating is affected by:
  • Number of votes. No comments.
  • Who votes. A voice from a regular community member is valued above the novice voice. Moreover, a voice from a newly created Twitter account will lower your rating.
  • From where come. In the rules of Product Hunt it is strictly stated that ‚ÄúYou cannot vote on a project‚Äù. Obviously, it is impossible to track the implementation of this rule in all languages ‚Äã‚Äãin real time. In this regard, Product Hunt keeps track of whether the voter has come to your project page via a direct link or from the main page of Product Hunt. Voting by direct link will lower your rating.
  • Number of comments. In the description of your project, ask community members to ask you questions. Comment on all the answers, without exception, and ask counter-questions. Offer prizes for testing your product.
  • Exclusivity. Projects with asterisks attract more attention. Product Hunt Exclusives .
  • The dynamics of voting. Controversial moment. Hunter, who published our project, recommended 10 am San Francisco (Pacific time. -11 hours difference with Moscow) as the most successful time for publication. We published at 0:15 (11 am Moscow time) in order to reach users in Russia as much as possible. Most of the projects included in the TOP-10, also published after midnight.


Keep in mind that the site is constantly evolving and the rules are changing. If you read this article a year after its publication, it is probably outdated. Spend a couple of weeks on the site and explore how everything works at the moment.

Our mistakes:
1. Rubrain.com, in the form in which it was published, is not very suitable for Product Hunt. "More of a service and not a product" - Eric Willis (moderator of Product Hunt). It was clear in advance that we definitely would not get into the TOP-3.
2. We did not know that you can not publish anywhere a direct link to the page of our project on Product Hunt. Not a single article had a word about it. Rather, the authors urged to publish direct links and not to force users to look for the service themselves in the list on the main page. The same is said in the official rules: ‚ÄúYou can‚Äôt ask for upvotes. We strongly recommend not to do this.
3. We did not devote a sufficient amount of time to preparing for publication and did not participate in the life of start-up communities.

And now about what to do.

Step-by-step instruction

  1. Sign up for Twitter if you don't have an account.
  2. Sign up for Product Hunt.
  3. Participate in the life of the community for several months. After registration, you will only have the opportunity to vote for projects (you cannot comment and publish anything). Therefore, comment on the tweets of hunters, moderators and project creators, thank for the good findings, retweet @ProductHunt and founder of the @rrhoover project, download and test products as often as possible, go to offline meetings , subscribe to LIVE Chat and ask questions, create your collections ( a list of products you like and recommend). One day, your collection can get into the Product Hunt newsletter and you will get recognition among the hunters. In this way, you will attract attention to yourself and to your project of the active participants of Product Hunt, whose voices are valued above the votes of novices. However, remember, someone might have the idea to publish your project without your knowledge. We recommend on the site and in all social services. networks add the inscription "Please don't hunt us yet".
