Interview with Steve Jobs Walt Mossberg

Today Steve Jobs answered many questions (hooray!):

1. After switching to Intel, Apple increased its share of the computer market 3 times worldwide and 5 times in the USA.
2. Rename the company to Apple Inc. does not mean exit from the computer market, Leopard confirms this.
3. Telephone networks are invested in the development of 3G networks, but there are no devices for them normal. iPhone should turn the "children's Internet" in the "adult" on the phone.
4. Despite the words of Jobs that the real Mac Axis and the real Safari runs on the iPhone, other applications do not run on it, since they were not made for such use.
5. In June there will be an Apple TV firmware update and it will allow you to watch YouTube videos on the TV screen, but the quality of the videos themselves will remain the same. Apple TV is a DVD era internet player.
6. The number of copies of iTunes exceeds the number of iPods and this means that Apple is one of the leading manufacturers of PC software.
7. Jobs was skeptical at the time about video on portable devices, but the reaction of the public changed his mind. The video will continue to be portable.
8. Why is the iPhone not 3G? Because Wi-Fi is thriving and the speed of the iPhone is much better than 3G.
9. Will the iPhone remain a closed system? Apple is working on the ability to access other manufacturers of software for this platform, as they do not want the phone to die because of some other program. As soon as it becomes possible others will gain access. You just have to wait.
