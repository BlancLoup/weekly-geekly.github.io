Courier services with cash services

Greetings.

As I am planning to open my online store with not very large items, I decided to puzzle myself with a search for a courier service (CU) with cash services.

Many people know what to open an online store and how to make this whole mechanism work properly. Let's consider one of the components of a successfully operating small online store, the owner of which can no longer cope with the delivery of orders, but is also not ready to create its own courier service.

In this article we will look at the pros and cons of courier services , as well as go over their tariffs and working conditions.
