New items from Sony coming soon!

Hooray! The long-awaited flagship Sony Xperia Z2, the Xperia M2 dual-sense smart phone, as well as the SmartBand lifelogging bracelet, are entering the Russian market.





On April 22, a Russian presentation of the new Sony Mobile product line took place in Moscow, where the new flagship model Xperia Z2 was presented, with a detailed hand-on preview of which you can find here . We will not, once again, list the technical specifications, let us say only that the Z2 goes on sale after the May holidays at a recommended price of 2,9990 rubles (and for pre-ordering the novelty is available now - in the Sony online store .

In addition to the new flagship, a middle class Xperia M2 Dual will be on sale, which, despite the familiar letter in the name, will not be a replacement for the budget model Xperia M (although it has two slots for SIM cards), but will be a powerful device with 4-core processor and 8 megapixel Exmor RS camera. The recommended price will be 13,490 rubles, and sales will begin in late April. Well, the SmartBand bracelet, which is a mixture of a fitness tracker and smart watches, will cost 3,990 rubles and appear in the store by mid-May. Of course, soon we will provide you with reviews of all these new products, so stay tuned!
