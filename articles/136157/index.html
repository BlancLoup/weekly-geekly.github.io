In any case, the Bing team can celebrate, as the share of this search service, although a little, continues to grow. By the way, recently Bing is 3 years old. It is worth noting that the search service from Microsoft is a platform for Yahoo search. At about the same time, the search service of the "Corporation of Good" became as famous as Yahoo was known.
