MEMO ON PAYMENT OF STATE DUTY,
paid when applying to the authorized federal executive body for intellectual property (the Federal Service for Intellectual Property - Rospatent) for taking actions on the state registration of a computer program (PC), a database (DB) and topology IC (TIMS), as well as for registration agreement on the alienation of rights exclusively and the transfer of an exclusive right without an agreement on the specified results of intellectual activity (for the TIMS - an additional licensing agreement and an agreement for ord).
For the state registration of the computer, database, TIMS and other actions related to the state registration of the computer, database and TIMS, as well as registration of the agreement on alienation of the exclusive right and transfer of the exclusive right without the agreement (transfer of the right), and for TIMS - an additional license agreement and pledge agreement , a state fee (state duty) is charged, which is paid only by the applicant himself in the amount and manner established by the Tax Code of the Russian Federation (NK RF).
