Facebook spends millions of dollars on offline advertising

The social network Facebook has started to spend significant funds on its offline advertising. As an example, the amount of Facebook costs for outdoor advertising in the UK. So, last year the company spent just ¬£ 16,000 pounds sterling on this type of advertising, and this year in just a few months ¬£ 6 million ($ 8.7 million) has already been spent, RBC writes .

Of these funds, social network spent about ¬£ 3 million on television advertising, ¬£ 1.5 million on billboards, ¬£ 600,000 on advertising in print media, plus ¬£ 900,000 on television commercials.
