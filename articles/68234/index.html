Overview of nettop platform nVidia ION



Having considered the various options for cases and ION-based motherboards, it remains only to assess the feasibility of self-assembly system. Many leading (and not so) global manufacturers offer ready-made stylish nettops on the nVidia ION platform. At the moment, the Intel G45 could be considered as a competing platform, and only Atom + G45 bundles were not available to the general public. The main task of modern HTPC is one - to play FullHD media content via HDMI, and the GF9400 graphics core is the best way to cope with this.



In fact, I still could not decide for myself to choose a particular solution.
