Sports headphones Koss The Clef



It is not the first time that Koss is pleased with the adherents of high-quality sound with sports headphones. But The Clef is not only valuable headphones, but also 3-4 kilograms ... accessory.

Even the photo shows that the headphones do not look quite usual. This is just a blister (box), which is their "shell", and also contains some chips, which later will tell and show.

Let's start in order. Packaging does not contain anything remarkable. Rather ascetic black box, in which only headphones and brief instructions.



Therefore, without dwelling on it, we go straight to the headphones, which I personally immediately impressed with its unusual design.

But first, a few dry facts, that is, specifications:

The headphones themselves are white. A bit small, although rather, simply unusual, since they sit comfortably in the ear, do not fall out and do not interfere. The wire is flat because the headphones fold like a snail into its case. If someone used small mice for laptops, they probably saw about the same design. You pull the wires in different directions and the coil spins up, then you pull a little bit and the wire rolls back. Also arranged here.



As for the speakers themselves, their design is open, and I think this is very good, because during active sports you will still hear what is happening around and will be able to avoid various excesses.
