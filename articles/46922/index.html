The first application from Microsoft in the App Store !!!

No matter how incredible the news seemed, but it is a fact. Microsoft's first iPhone app appeared in the App Store.

Seadragon Mobile is an application that shows you thousands of images from the database in Photosynth.com , which is part of Microsoft Live Labs.



Regarding the appearance of Seadragon Mobile in the App Store, a source at Microsoft said that there is a group studying the possibilities of the iPhone platform. You may wonder why Microsoft is ready to help its competitors. Actually, Microsoft has its own MacBU team, ported MSN and Office for Mac users. And in principle, it is normal that Microsoft would like to expand its market share.

Let's hope that Microsoft can make Office for iPhone. But, if they do, then it makes sense to use Windows Mobile phones?
