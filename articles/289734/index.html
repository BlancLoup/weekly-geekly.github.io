How I became an investor

Prehistory


In this story there will be no expensive cars, glossy blondes on ocean-going yachts and generally a lot of money. The topic is not directly related to IT, except that the author is an ingrained IT person who has traveled his career from a junior green goblin junior tester, then a programmer, to a manager and a department and a project, and then an investor.

Story


One day, sitting in a nightly summer cafe on the seashore with my wife, she asked her who would you like to be in ten years, I answered, without too much hesitation, as an investor. But as they say, if God wants to laugh at us, he fulfills our desires.

So, having crossed the Rubicon and the crisis of 30 years, realizing the aimlessness of the then existence, being in a state of free flight, rested and specifically tired of almost idleness, I became an investor. After the accumulation of the "parachute" and the subsequent dismissal, some free money remained. And at that moment She appeared on the horizon! A girl from a rather specific consulting area, with immense ambitions, steam locomotive effort and inexhaustible energy. The girl was strongly pressed at work and she was carrying ideas of dismissal. A very important moment, which later played a key role, that neither I nor my wife were going to work in this company, i.e. it was a pure investment project. The sphere, intersecting with the competencies of my wife, was for me a dark forest. And nevertheless, I knew that Anya would succeed (and was not mistaken in that).

At the start


Immediately a beautiful picture was drawn in my head, how I invest money, the company grows and works for me. And I rightly leave most of the profit for the development and promotion of the best employees, and in little things for myself: for a car, a house, and other nice things. Oh, how hard I was wrong ... Later I learned that such investments are called investments in people. And the risks are associated with these people. The risk that something will happen to them. I understood this risk perfectly. And, running far ahead, it was this risk that materialized. But then it could not be a reason not to start this business. And we started.

They invited Anya, voiced the alignment: your 40% of business, the position of general, fixed 100K and premiums for the results. With us - guarantees of payment of salary and money for other operating expenses. For a long time did not have to persuade. Then went the exciting and pleasant process of registering and creating an LLC, choosing an office, buying office equipment, inventing a name, and choosing a logo.
