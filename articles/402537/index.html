New iPad, red iPhone. Is it time to update?

Yesterday, Apple released its most affordable iPad (from $ 329 in the US) and a red iPhone 7. The new line of tablets replaced the Air 2, squeezing out by passing the younger iPad Mini models. Banderolka examines the characteristics of the new iPad and shares other news from Apple ‚ûú




iPad 2017 model can not be called a cardinal upgrade. Something he looks like the old, something different from him. On the one hand, the screen is the same 9.7 inches, slightly brighter, but still IPS, Retina 2048 √ó 1536. No 3D touch, thin frames or curved edges of the display, which were rumored. Modules LTE, GPS & GLONASS, Bluetooth - all the same.

On the other hand, they updated the heart of the tablet - the processor. Now it is A9, migrated here from the iPhone 6S and 6S Plus. In addition, the case became thicker - 7.5 mm instead of 6.1mm for the Air 2 , and the tablet itself gained weight, though only 32 grams. They removed the 16Gb model from the line, leaving 32 Gb and 128 Gb, equipped the device with an 8-megapixel main and 1.2-megapixel front-facing cameras, as well as a 32.4Wh battery, allowing the iPad to work from one charge to 10 hours.

Substitute




The new tablet, in fact, has become a compromise model. It is not available in Rose Gold, there is no Apple Pencil support, as well as new technologies that can surprise a sophisticated user. Apple's idea is different. In addition to the presentation of the new tablet and a number of changes, the technology giant from Cupertino removed all iPad Mini models from sale, leaving only 128-gigabytes worth $ 399. Thus, the new iPad has become the most affordable tablet from Apple. The price is a key update, and this is what Vice President of Marketing Phil Schiller spoke about at the iPad presentation. He also mentioned that there are more than 1.3 million apps created specifically for the iPad in the AppStore. Apple's calculation that consumers will update tablets as often as smartphones has failed. The latter, by the way, played almost the main role in this, growing from year to year with screens. Therefore, in the advertising teasers iPad is often shown in conjunction with the iPhone. Such a tablet is needed by Apple in order, on the one hand, to attract those who do not have a tablet at all, on the other hand, to force the users of old models like the iPad 2 to update. As the results of last year showed, powerful and expensive Pros do not solve this problem. But a simple, clear and, most importantly, cheap iPad has to revive the direction.
