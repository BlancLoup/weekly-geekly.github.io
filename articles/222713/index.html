IStick - compact flash drive for IOS devices

Not so much time has passed since my use of the Samsung Galaxy Tab 2. It was the only purchase when I moved from ios to android. Now I have a modest Iphone5c of the same budget category. I still remember my "Vedroid" which allowed me to connect not only one single flash drive, but also a whole card reader. It was very comfortable. When you constantly work with customer projects, you constantly have to download information somewhere. The "pill" served as a very good assistant in work affairs.
Finally, something similar will appear in the "apple technology." I want to present you an overview of the IStick flash drive.


At the moment, the device is available in versions with 8, 16, 32, 64 and 128 GB of internal memory, which will be more than enough to pump your smartphone or tablet. When interacting with a computer, the speed of reading data from iStick is 12 MB / s, and the write speed is 7.5 MB / s. When interacting with an iOS device, the read speed is 2.5 MB / s, and the write speed is 1.9 MB / s. The iStick weighs only 10 grams, and its dimensions are 51 x 28 x 9 mm, which will make it easy to carry it with you even in the smallest pocket.


Right now, the device makers are raising funds at the Kickstarter crowdfunding platform to launch it for sale as quickly as possible. The required amount of $ 100,000, by the way, was collected quickly and the amount of funds raised at the time of writing the material has already reached almost $ 300,000. Working prototypes iStick passed all the necessary tests and are ready for mass production. You can order one of the devices on the official page . An 8 GB IStick will cost you $ 65, and a device with 128 GB of memory on board will cost you $ 199. But these are exclusive prices for those who will be one of the first to respond to the campaign, so hurry! In the near future, prices for the device will increase.
