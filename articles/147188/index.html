Presentations from Nokia Developer Days

Friends, we are pleased to present all the presentations from the conference for Nokia Developer Days mobile developers, held exactly one week ago.

The event covered such important topics as the development and design of applications for the Windows Phone platform, the capabilities of smart (but inexpensive) Nokia Asha phones and ways of earning applications for the Series 40 platform on which Nokia Asha works.



Roughly speaking, the entire Nokia Developer Days conference was reduced to discussing two important topics for developers: application development under all Nokia platforms, as well as earning opportunities on the applications created. Fortunately, the speakers managed to show the promise of developing for all key Nokia platforms, and if you were not at Nokia Developer Days, but are looking at developing applications for Nokia smartphones, be sure to look at all the presentations from the event under the screen.
