Competition for designers from Mail.Ru Mail



Today, the problem of searching and organizing useful content in electronic correspondence is relevant for all email applications. The mail contains a wealth of information - photographs, documents, invoices, booking confirmations, confirmations of orders in online stores and much more. But looking for them in the general list of letters is not very convenient. Therefore, postal services offer various solutions designed to simplify work with such information - for example, searching and sorting according to some criteria, threads, a summary section with all attachments from letters. However, these tricks do not completely solve the problem.

Mail Mail.Ru launches a competition for the design of a mobile interface concept. The task of the competition is to propose an approach for convenient and efficient work with the information accumulated in the e-mail.

You need to design and draw key application screens for Android or iOS. Based on existing applications is optional, the design concept may differ from them - fresh visual solutions will get more points.

The competition is held at the site of the international design community Dribbble. Applications are accepted until August 16, 2015, 23:59 Moscow time. On September 4, three winners will be announced, which will be determined by experts from the Mail.Ru Mail team. The works will be evaluated according to criteria such as the freshness of the visual solution, the quality of the interface and the originality of the idea. Work needs to be published on Dribbble through Rebound to the main shot . UPD : If you do not have an invite to Dribbble, you can post your work in any convenient place and leave a link to it with a comment to this post. We will help the best authors to get an invite and publish the work according to all the rules.

As prizes, the winners will receive interesting gadgets presented on Kickstarter, which are currently in the final stage of obtaining funding. The winners will receive a login and password from the account to which they are recorded:
