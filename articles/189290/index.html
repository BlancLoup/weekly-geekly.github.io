Review Supra M127G - middling with an unusual design



Good afternoon, dear residents of Habr. In this review, I would like to tell you about the Supra tablet, which bears, as happens in such cases, the intricate name - M127G. This is a fairly average device in terms of characteristics, but this is the case when a not-so-outstanding company takes steps to success in terms of design.



The appearance of the tablet is its main feature. He is rather unusual, he is rather large and he is rather masculine. I do not think that a woman would use the Supra M127G, which weighs about 750 grams.



The design is really good and the materials to the touch cause only positive feedback, but it gives a button off. It looks like a 90's tape recorder button ready. (" So, now press Play and then abruptly Rec !")



Tablet filling:

- Android 4.1
- Processor: RockChip RK3066 1500 MHz (2 cores)
- RAM: 1 GB DDR3
- Built-in memory: 16 GB
- Screen: 10.1 ", 1280x800 pixels, TFT IPS
- Video Core: Mali-400 MP4
- Bluetooth 2.1 EDR
- Slot for SIM (3G)
- Rear camera 5 MP
- Front camera 2 MP
- Battery capacity: 6800 mAh
- Dimensions (LxWxD): 252x178x10 mm
- Weight: 750 g

To be honest, I have not found Supra in the network to which country, but we will assume that this is China-made. For a cheap device, and the M127G costs from 9 thousand rubles on Yandex.Market, this is quite a good decision.

The overall work of the system can not be compared, for example, with the Nexus 10, but as a "workhorse" Supra is suitable. In addition, the tablet shows quite good results in AnTuTu: 11102 points .



According to my tests, the M127G holds a little more than 9 hours when playing movies at a brightness of 75% and the same volume.
