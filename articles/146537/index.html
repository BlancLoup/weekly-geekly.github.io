Code Review Open Source Projects

I announce my own experiment - a collection of source code reviews of Open Source projects. As a first approximation, projects are limited to the JVM platform. The review format is a series of dry articles combined by the project under consideration. Architectural features, developer errors and other interesting implementation details are included in the review.

The draft of the first cycle of articles - Mockito . Under its hood are many interesting libraries, the interaction with which will be covered in detail.

Articles are constantly updated, and you can influence the course of the review, leaving comments or sending emails to the project .

Everything related to the project, but not an overview of the features of the implementation of any Open Source project, can be read on twitter @ e Queuepy . It is also recommended to leave feedback and suggestions.
