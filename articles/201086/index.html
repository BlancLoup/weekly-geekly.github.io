Anti-piracy manifesto on the concept of a new social contract of copyright in the digital age



Noting the year since the introduction of censorship in RuNet , the Pirate Party of Russia held a press conference on a number of topical issues, including summing up the application of new laws on blocking websites on the Internet, and determining the future way of reforming copyright.

temych presented the results of the annual monitoring of the application of the law No. 139-FZ (the law on black lists) for the entire existence of the registry, as well as No. 187-FZ (the anti-piracy law), and also spoke about what RosKomSvoboda was doing all this year, and what results were achieved.

In addition, the Anti-Piracy Manifesto on the concept of a new social contract on copyright in the digital age was presented.


Quite a few network users today, it is obvious that copyright is in deep crisis due to a serious technological leap in the development of file-sharing technologies. In accordance with the PPR manifest, proposed its vision of how to pardon millions of potential offenders and criminals, and set the course for non-commercial file sharing between users not to be considered piracy, as this is now regarded by the official doctrine adopted by the Kremlin and the copyrights.



The manifesto states that it is necessary to recognize legal private non-commercial file sharing on the Web and expand the concept of free use of works for personal non-commercial purposes (fair use) , legally enshrine free licenses in Russia (Creative Commons and General Public License), shorten the term of copyright: the minimum program is up to 50 years from the date of the author‚Äôs death, the maximum program is up to 15-20 years from the moment of publication of the work , to prohibit the use of DRM , and also to put up for public discussion a number of other equally important suggestions zheniy. In the future, based on it, a more comprehensive concept of reforming copyright in the digital age will be presented.

The discussion of the manifesto and the concept is conducted on the PPR forum , where everyone is invited to offer their edits, share opinions or make criticism. Some theses of the manifesto will form the basis of the video created by the IPA and CPD .



Anyone who wishes to actively participate in the work of the online working group on the creation of a new concept on the reform of copyright - Wellcome!

The concept of its completion will be transferred through the friendly deputies of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation and the pirate lobby to the State Duma, the Presidential Administration and relevant departments with the aim of further implementation in the bill.
