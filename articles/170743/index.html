Controller for smart greenhouses


For a smart home there are a lot of solutions, I think it's time to expand into the garden.
I propose to discuss the project controller for a smart greenhouse.

I will add that all of the following are separate devices that are not always compatible with each other, but we need a single system that does not require the fitting of components.

Here is a sample list of what you want to see in the final device:

Stage I - basic functionality
At least 8 channels for sensors (air humidity inside, air temperature inside and outside, soil temperature inside and outside, air temperature in the ventilation channel, illumination, 1 reserve);
Display of sensor readings on a large LCD display (LCD and touchscreen are also possible, but this seems to be much more expensive);
Control of actuators:
-Opening / closing of watering cranes and bay barrels according to a given logic and sensor readings;
- switching on / off the fan (both in one and in the other direction) according to sensor readings and logic;
-the inclusion at night of two channels of LED-lights of plants (blue and red spectrum) in a different ratio, according to a given calendar.

Stage II - possibly in the form of a separate plug-in module, because not everyone needs
Work with a SIM card:
-the receipt of current information about the state of affairs (sensor readings) by SMS;
- notifications about critical situations for several numbers;
- device control commands via SMS;
- transfer of data via GPRS / 3G (for the next stage).

Stage III - buns
Web interface or application for Android:
-the receipt of current information about the state of affairs (sensor readings) by SMS;
- archive of indications, schedules;
-control devices through the interface;
-change settings and logic;
-web camera or snapshot;
-the weather forecast for the coming days (widget).

Initial power: 12V (car battery, charged by solar battery by an independent controller).
The platform is the Arduino / RoMeo / Rapsberry Pi / something similar , because the market is full of more or less standardized sensors and actuators ...
Climatic conditions: from -25 to +60, from humidity can be hidden in a sealed industrial box.

How much can development of such a controller cost (in fact, more programming, because there are a lot of standardized sensors and actuators now)? If without greed?
