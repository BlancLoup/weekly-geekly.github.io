Limbo similar puzzle platformer - Monochroma

Hello,

Turkish developers make Limbo a similar puzzle platform game with an interesting plot and a great atmosphere.







Limbo of such games on the market as a whole is quite small, if we consider games with a similar atmosphere. At the same time, Limbo set the vector for a similar genre in the noir style.
On the kickstarter, Nowhere Studios developers talk in detail about the creation of the game, its plot and features.

In short about the plot: an alternative universe. 1950s. A large corporation withdraws all the money from the public in order to build robots. Two brothers fall into the mess and they need to survive in this harsh world.
Of the interesting: In Limbo, the character's possibilities are quite extensive - he jumps high and runs fast. In Monochrome, developers focus on the lorian component - the main character carries his younger brother on the shoulders and therefore cannot climb high or jump far. Also, many quests with a brother on their shoulders are not passed - it is necessary to choose a place where to leave it, because a brother, like many small children, is afraid of the dark.
