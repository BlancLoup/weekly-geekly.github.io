Chinese touchscreen phone for 70 USD - what to expect?

It just so happened that one beautiful day fell into my hands a Chinese touchscreen phone - a fake analog of the iPhone. More precisely, from the iPhone there is only a part of the menu design and appearance.

His name is SciPhone i9 +++. The device was ordered by my friend directly from Chinatown. Cost - 71 USD together with the shipment. On the customs label the shipping cost is 25 USD.



In general, I begged this child to play around a bit. What I want to tell now.

Perhaps I'll start with the general characteristics.

The device is designed as a monoblock with a built-in antenna, apparently it is almost a copy of the iPhone 3G. True screen a little less. And the positions of the Home button and the dynamics are similar, although there are differences in a couple of millimeters.



The assembly is pretty good, there are no slots, the case does not creak. And, as practice has shown, pretty durable. As it turned out later, there is a steel insert-rim between the back cover and the front panel, most likely it is covered with nickel.

Judging by some reports, the percentage works at a frequency of about 60 MHz, which is not so much. The amount of working memory barely reaches 2 MB. However, it is possible to install microSD / TF cards up to 8 GB, although I set up 2 GB for the sake of experiment - it all worked.

This child weighs about 135 grams, which is basically a lot, although the device is confidently held in his hand. Dimensions are 116 x 62 x 13 mm. The back cover is made of plastic. With a hole under the camera at 0.3 megapixels, which in turn removes frankly just terrible.

The phone is able to work simultaneously with two SIM-cards in GSM 850/900/1800/1900 networks. I tried to put 2 cards at the same time - no matter how strange everything works. I was surprised and at the same time pleased that it is possible to flexibly configure many parameters for each card separately.



Information output is carried out on a 3.2 inch screen with a resolution of 240 x 320 pixels with the ability to display 256 thousand colors. The touch screen and in addition to the standard "fingers" and responds to the stylus, which comes as part of the package.





To my great surprise, even a Bluetooth 2.0 module was present in this phone, which at the same time worked correctly.

The phone came with two Li-Ion batteries for as much as 1800 mAh. Is it possible to believe this figure I do not know, but with quite active calls, the device lives for 3 days.

In the phone box, we managed to detect as many as 6 protective films for the front panel. The films are not of the first quality, but they perform their function well, I immediately applied one to the device.

Unlocking is done by moving the slider, like on the iPhone. Further we get to the six-page menu. 2 pages is a useful menu so to speak, the remaining 4 are any Chinese garbage, some java applications.
