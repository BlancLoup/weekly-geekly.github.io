V8 out of competition

The editors of Computerworld published the results of their own testing of browsers under Windows XP using the SunSpider JavaScript test. With the V8 script engine, Chrome 3.0 ran tests two and a half times faster than Firefox, five times faster than Opera 10 and nine times faster than IE8. Honestly, such a huge difference in speed is shocking.

Compared with Chrome 3.0 can only Safari 4.0, which gave him only 30%, as well as the second version of "Chrome" (-25%). The rest is far behind. Even Firefox 3.5 showed a two and a half times worse result.
