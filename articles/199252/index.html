Attention! Competition! We develop instant messenger Vkontakte for Tizen

Good afternoon, Habr!

We are pleased to announce the start of a competition for mobile application developers. Participants will need to develop a messenger application based on the VK API on the Tizen SDK 2.2 platform.



When developing a new messenger, contestants will have to perform the proposed task. The competition will be held from October 28 to December 18. On December 2, the Samsung jury will select up to 20 finalists, and on December 25, three winners will be named who will share a prize pool of 3 million rubles among themselves and will be able to download the application to the Tizen Store on seller.tizenstore.com .

Detailed terms and conditions of the competition are published in the official group of the competition: vk.com/tizen_contest .

Dare, ladies and gentlemen!
