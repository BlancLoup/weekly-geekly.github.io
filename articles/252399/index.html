Do it with love - an application that teaches you to love

Make it out of love - a conceptual application for android that helps you learn to love and popularizes such a phenomenon as the language of life circumstances.

We propose to conduct a personal experiment and pump in the activities of love.



The work of the application is based on the statistical probability of giving out at the right moment advice to commit an act with love, which will pull you out of patterned behavior and, perhaps, will be the language of life circumstances.


The language of life circumstances is always individual, someone will be notified during a quarrel, someone near a vagabond, in need of food, someone at a meeting with the second half to which he is afraid to come to meet ...
