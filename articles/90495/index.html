Jump in! Developer camp. Microsoft and the Open Source Community - a course for convergence

The event takes place these days, April 6-9, 2010 in Zurich, Switzerland. Although the organizer was Microsoft, it was not fans of this company who were invited to it, but 25 developers from the open source camp from all over the world, including from Russia.
