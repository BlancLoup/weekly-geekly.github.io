Guglofon Motorola FlipOut officially announced



Motorola officially announced the youth googlofon with an unusual form factor and MotoBlur user interface , integrated access to social networks Facebook , MySpace , Twitter and full support for Google services.



Motorola FlipOut is a very compact device. Its dimensions are only 67 x 67 x 17. The upper part of the device with a touch screen (2.8 inches with a resolution of 320 x 240 pixels) rotates around one of the corners, revealing a QWERTY keyboard.



FlipOut is controlled by Android 2.1, equipped with Wi-Fi and A-GPS modules, an electronic compass, a 3.5mm headphone jack, a 3 megapixel camera with Kodak Perfect Touch technology, a CrystalTalk PLUS noise cancellation system, 512MB of internal memory and a 2GB memory card complete set.



The device operates in quad-band GSM / EDGE and supports third-generation communications - HSPA 3G. About the price of the gadget is not yet known.
