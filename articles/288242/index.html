OpenLeaks will not yet publish any secret documents. Instead, the creators of the resource will help journalists and various non-profit organizations to obtain secret information of various kinds, for publication in the media. In other words, OpenLeaks will receive information in the same way as Wikileaks, but will not publish anything on its own. It turns out something like an information mediator. This scheme of work has many positive aspects - for example, the site and its creators will not harass the authorities, because no one will know how and how secret information leaked to any media.

As a result of the implementation of the new scheme of work, OpenLeaks will be able to act without problems for a long time, without persecutions and lawsuits. So far, OpenLeaks has been launched in alpha version, and developers recognize that there may be some problems with the work of the resource. There is a debugging of the whole scheme of work, plus the solution of some technical issues.
