Most earned by developers for the BlackBerry OS

Research company Vision Mobile has published a large study ‚ÄúThe new mobile app economy‚Äù, which is devoted to various aspects of the behavior of the market for the development and sale of mobile applications in the world. In particular, the statistics used for the development of mobile platforms was carried out, a review of the salaries of programmers was made, characteristic problems were analyzed (segmentation of Android devices), etc.

The research methodology consisted of a standard survey of mobile developers around the world who anonymously agreed to provide information about their work and its profitability. In total, more than 1,500 respondents were surveyed worldwide from both Americas, Europe, Africa and even Oceania, specializing in the most common mobile platforms - iOS, Android, Windows Phone, Blackberry and JavaME (Symbian did not get into this number).

The following conclusions were made:
  • The average income from a single application, depending on the platform, is in the range of $ 1,200- $ 3,900
  • Most developers (57%), regardless of what area they are working now, plan to add Windows Phone to their area of ‚Äã‚Äãinterest.
  • Also, the majority of programmers (more than 50%) do not lose sight of the fashion trend of tablets and release, in addition to the usual mobile applications, an option for tablets; among iOS developers, the majority is 74%

The study also provides an answer to the question - which mobile platform is most beneficial for developers in terms of generating income.
