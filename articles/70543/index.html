Disclosed prototypes of Microsoft phones

Gizmodo continues to open the veil of mystery over Microsoft projects. At this time, a popular resource showed two images of future phones.

Phones are called Turtle and Pure. They will be manufactured by Sharp and sold under a common brand with Microsoft. Phones will have onboard software to access Zune services and their own application platform. Most likely, the devices will work on Windows Mobile 7.

UPD : some details / rumors :
* Turtle is equipped with a 5 Mpx camera, the camera in Pure - 8 Mpx;
* phones will include integration with the X-Box;
* UI is written in Silverlight (!) Using SeaDragon (!!!);
* all phone data can be synchronized with the cloud;
* phones will be launched not at CES 2010, but at their own special event;
* more by reference.
