Periodic system of game controllers



Do you remember what Wavebird joystick worked with, with Nintendo64 or GameCube? Where are more buttons, Colecovision or Atari Jaguar? Now you can answer these and many other questions related to game joysticks thanks to the periodic system of console controllers.

Made by Pixel Fantasy, a flickr user, this unfinished job offers diagrams for many joysticks, consoles, and other input devices for consoles. As an added bonus, the table includes many portable gaming systems, as well as the names of their creators, and information about the processor in many cases.
