Flying cars and passenger drones, 11 modern projects

Flying cars are a must-have for a bright future. All promise that they will appear, but few really try to build them. All existing projects for the creation of flying cars, as well as their close relatives - passenger drone in this review.

Samson



Startup Samsonmotorworks trying to build a flying car. The final version of the car is not yet, but, unlike other abandoned projects, the site is regularly updated with reports of new successes. And besides, there are already 45 pre-orders and the manager is optimistic about building a prototype by the end of 2016.

Passenger drone is almost like a flying car, but without wheels. They began to try to build only at the end of zero with the increasing popularity of drones.
