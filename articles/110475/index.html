Report on the second meeting of the Apple Developers Community

On December 16, the second meeting of the Apple Developers Community was held at the Ingria Business Incubator.



The second meeting was different from the first in its format: newcomers who decided to start developing applications for mobile devices could attend the free master classes of Alexander Cherny ( iPhone development ), Anton Maximov ( Windows 7 phone development ) and Ivan Chashkin ( iPhone 5 development) ) for practical knowledge and recommendations.

Also for all those present, Yuri Shkatula and Grigory Buteyko from the DataArt studio during the report compared the iPhone and Windows 7 mobile platforms. The guys discussed the prospects for the development of mobile operating systems of IT giants, simultaneously catching on Android, with its significant drawbacks in the fight for a place under the sun.

About this and much more under the cut.

We are very pleased that the meeting was attended by many people from Habr . Young guys do not sit back and want to develop, which is good news. For our part, we will try to help this in every way by organizing such meetings on a regular basis.



Let's go back to the meeting. Yury Shkatula and Grigory Buteyko were the first to reach the barrier with a report on the opposition to the iPhone and Windows 7 phone .



In fact, it all boiled down to the fact that a completely fresh brainchild of Microsoft on all sides was compared to iPhone, revealing its pros and cons. As it turned out, W7P has very few advantages, especially as it is now (quite raw, many promises in the next versions).



Against the background of two giants, Android revealed several significant drawbacks. The lack of uniform standards for devices becomes a real thorn for developers. Different screen resolutions, different specifications, etc. For the buyer, this is undoubtedly good in terms of budget, the price starts from $ 250, which allows you to buy a good machine for a little money. But for developers, such a wealth of various characteristics leads to the fact that writing an application for one smartphone is far from a fact that it will run on another, even taking into account the same operating system.



Thus, ensuring the stable operation of the application on all available mobile devices, in half a year another ten devices will be released, on which the application will work crookedly or not run at all.

By the way, iOS and W7P will not have such problems. With "iPhone" everything is clear, and "Microsoft" for partners producing mobile phones with its operating system, imposes certain minimum system requirements and uniform standards for screen resolution and the number of buttons, thereby greatly facilitating the life of developers.

When the iPhone ceases to be the main luxury for the overwhelming number of citizens, other flagships of its operating systems will stand next to it, which will contribute both to their development and to the development of the iPhone itself.
