Early to dump

Hi, Habraludy!

I did not even have time to understand what had happened - and at the door of the guy the guy who was next was already catching, twisting his arms, knocking him to the floor and weighing a couple of good cracks. I felt a sense of pride in our country. It means that it is too early to fall into depression and dump - since we still have such conscious people as this man.
