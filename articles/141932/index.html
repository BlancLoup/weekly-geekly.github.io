Robot Petman learned to climb the stairs

Compared to other Boston Dynamics robots, such as Big dog , Sand flea or Cheetah , Petman is perhaps the least suited to the definition of a combat robot - it is intended for testing chemical protection suits. However, this is the official version, who knows what the military has in mind ...
