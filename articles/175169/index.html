Dell Inspiron 7720 Laptop Video Review



The Dell Inspiron 7720 is a low-cost multimedia notebook designed to replace a desktop computer. It is equipped with a matte display with a diagonal of 17.3 inches and a resolution of 1920x1080. The model can not boast of small dimensions and weight, and you will not find any designer frills here either. But at its low cost, it offers a fairly productive stuffing that can cope with all modern video games.


The third-generation Intel Core processor is paired with an Nvidia Geforce GT650M video card, which is quite enough for most games, except super-demanding, such as Crysis 3. In the latter case, the graphics settings have to be set to the minimum. It should also be noted the speaker system with built-in subwoofer from the company SkullCandy, which will please with rich sound with decent bass.

The laptop is equipped with two compartments for 2.5-inch drives and one mSATA slot. In the review model, one of the compartments is occupied by a 1 TB hard drive. The user has two slots for RAM, in the review model both of them are occupied by 4 GB slats.
