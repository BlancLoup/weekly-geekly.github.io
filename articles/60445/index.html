Conference "Development of youth IT-entrepreneurship in Russia"

What: Conference "Development of youth entrepreneurship in the field of IT"

When: May 26 from 4 pm

Where: BC "Vector", Moscow, Shmitovsky passage, 11A
What will be there: Conference of specialists and entrepreneurs in the IT field. The conference is moderated by Vladimir Ryazansky, Director of Pirbyte System Development Agency (PRbyte SDA).
The purpose of the conference is the exchange of experience, implementation of solutions related to the activities of youth entrepreneurship in the field of IT, elaboration of a style of interaction with customers and investors.

The target audience is young entrepreneurs in the field of IT-services and computer equipment, IT-specialists, university students with an IT specialization.

A video is being made for further publication on the Internet and the organization of an Internet conference for regional entrepreneurs.

The event is supported by the "Federal Headquarters for the organization of the Year of Youth in the Russian Federation."

Conference plan :

1. Market situation
2. Prospects for direction
3. What and where to study?
4. Legal registration of the IT-company and the pitfalls associated with it
5. Dealership and direct deliveries.
6. IT-service (outsourcing)
7. Design and installation of local networks
8. Licensed software
9. Pricing
10. Sales Technique
11. Advertising IT companies
12. Work with state orders and educational projects
13. Technical support - work style, pitfalls.

How to get:

1. Fill out an application for participation and send it to conf@prbyte.net:

* Name,
* Last Name,
* Nickname
* Age,
* Contact number,
* E-mail

2. Wait for the call or letter confirming your participation.

Attention! Application for the conference can be sent until 15.00 on May 26!
