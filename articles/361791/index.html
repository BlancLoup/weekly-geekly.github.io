Alae CO2 Scrubber




Alae CO2 Scrubber is a cheap air purifier. The basic equipment of this device cleans the atmosphere from 24 pounds of carbon dioxide per year. CO2 Scrubber uses an air pump to pump air. Charged by a simple solar cell.

Credit Card Size Controller




A very interesting device, namely - the controller is reduced to the size of a credit card. The controller can be connected to any system via Bluetooth, be it a smartphone, tablet, game system or even a computer. 12 buttons and a complete imitation of all game controllers from Nintendo to Gameboy. Operating time is 2 hours, then it is possible to connect to a computer via USB.

Motion tracking system




Quite interesting, Arduino compatible device that allows you to track movement. With the help of 4 sensors on the panel, it detects movement, it can turn on the siren in the presence of movement in the room. The same device can be programmed for various actions. Comes complete with a wireless keyring. Charged by 5 volt battery.

Ultrasonic Levitation




Device for levitation. Uses sound waves forcing objects to levitate. This is a really working device, perhaps on the basis of this device, we will soon see a new engine or at least a board from back to the future. How does she work? Ultrasound levitation is a fascinating phenomenon that is interesting and educational to begin exploring. An ultrasonic beam directed at the reflector creates a sound wave forcing objects to levitate.

V-Desktop




Forget about the mouse, keyboard, remote control and even touchscreen. Now you can wave your hands to control your desktop, laptop or TV. In general, it is a device that tracks the movement of each of your fingers in space, thereby replacing both the keyboard and the mouse. The device connects to USB and supports the function of programming applications for it, that is, the source code is open. Naturally, you can play games on the device and use it instead of the controller.
