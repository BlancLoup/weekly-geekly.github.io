Habr it right!

Good evening, anxiously following the correctness of the posts of habra people.

Who does what Sunday afternoon, and I decided to write a small extension for Google Chrome, which may make your life a little easier.

In the comments to the posts, we often see how some people tell the author about typos in the text, while others tell them something like "write like that in a personal." But indeed, to write about a typo personally to the author, you need to do a lot of actions (open his page, press the mail button, write the text ...), and this is often just too lazy to do, it's easier to write a comment. Expansion is designed to help us in this particular situation.

It works quite familiar, thanks to the orphus system, in the following way: select a word that has a typo, press the cherished Ctrl + Enter, a window for sending a message to the author opens, where most of the text is already written automatically, we can only tell the author the correct version. In the extension settings you can set a template for the subject and text of the message.

Actually, the link to the extension in the gallery is tyts .
