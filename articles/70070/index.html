main idea


There is a certain site - for example rashodniki.ru .
Several databases are stored on it - the base of printers, the base of cartridges for printers, the base of shops, these cartridges are selling, the base of properties of cartridges, and of course, in trivia.

Accordingly, the user who has come on a site, chooses the printer, enters how many people will use it (from 1 to, for example, 10), how many pages per month will be printed. If the printer is color, how many pages are printed per month in color, how many black and white.

After he entered this data, a standard process is launched - searching cartridges in online stores, comparing their cost, calculating how many of these cartridges are enough, outputting information like

1. Shop A - Cartridge (model 1) - cost / month
2. Shop A - Cartridge (model 2) - cost / month

Sort by each field or any other parameters.
