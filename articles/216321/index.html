To health! Ten most interesting new products of 2014

Last month I wrote about wearables in sports and medicine . The health trend continues to evolve, and now manufacturers offer more and more solutions - both external and embedded in smartphones and other familiar devices.

One of the first innovations presented this year is the Intel headphones, which are capable of selecting music depending on the heart rate of its owner. Moreover, they will set the owner to workout, choosing the best music for the target pulse.



Samsung Galaxy S5 and Gear Fit


One of the main events of the year - Mobile World Congress - also pleased Samsung fans and a healthy lifestyle: the Galaxy S5 will come with S Health 3.0 pre-installed.
