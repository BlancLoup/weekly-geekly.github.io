Chi.mp: Streaming + OpenID with a free second-level domain.

Now this service is in a closed beta, but, if you wish, you can get an invite very quickly. The main feature is that they give you a domain in the .mp zone absolutely free of charge (this is the zone for the Northern Mariana Islands)
