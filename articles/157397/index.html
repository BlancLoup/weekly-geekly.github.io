[LAVKA] Love shop at the Ostankino television tower

At the end of September, the Ostankino Tower development and organization service for commercial projects presented a new project to journalists and bloggers - LAVKA.

It is believed that a huge area at the foot of the TV tower is not used effectively enough. Now you can arrange corporate parties and other entertainment using the tower itself as a screen ( video on youtube ).

But a group of talented young architects was allowed to realize their wildest fantasies in this place.

Therefore, after some time we (residents and guests of the capital) will get a new modern park and a new, possibly the longest shop in the world - the Shop of Love.



The whole territory is divided into functional zones of 35x35 meters, familiar tiles for each habraiser. And these tiles will be alive.

Further briefly about each zone of the future park.



Trading area



Here, visitors will be offered to look into the trade pavilions and buy everything necessary for a good time.

Installation and Sport


Here, the Shop turns into intricate sculpture - an art object, in the center of which is provided an area for temporary exhibitions and lectures on contemporary art.

The sports part is a hybrid of a basketball and a volleyball field. There will be an opportunity to play table tennis and ... chess.

Skate park



In this zone, skaters will hone their skills and take part in competitions.
The rest will admire, passing by to the next zone.

Ball



In a balloon, you can safely climb to a height and view the park from a bird's-eye view. It will not be superfluous to glimpse skaters from a height. But the next zone should be more pleasing to the eye.

Beach


Do you know many good beaches in the city of Moscow? Well, here's another one.
A great place to sunbathe and leave the ball towards each other.

The beach will be equipped not only with showers and changing rooms, but also a bar. Hooray.

A restaurant


The restaurant will specialize in fruits and vegetables.
Vegetables will be harvested from the garden right in front of the amazed visitors.

Hopefully, despite everything, the restaurant will serve both fish and meat.

Dance floor



And on the dance floor will be dancing. All styles and trends. As well as yoga, from which we have already lost the habit.

Platform for weddings in the European model (without the toastmaster and fights?).

Shop in winter


Now, when the first snow has already fallen in Moscow, if anyone thinks about the beach, then about the beach in warm lands.

But the Store as a park and, in fact, the shop is a stationary object, once deployed, will remain in Moscow.

What will happen to her in the winter?



First, calm down, the shop will live and work.

The focus of attention from the installation area will move to a 10-meter tree.

This absolutely necessary New Year's attribute will be complemented by a hockey box, a skating rink, a slide for ice floes, as well as warm houses for tea drinking.

The rinks will be in the central part - large and small, for children. Big will have a bar.

The restaurant and the wedding area will continue to work.

In winter, the Shop will be transformed, but it will continue to delight and surprise the residents and guests of the capital.
