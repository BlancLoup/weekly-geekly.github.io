Merge IT and Design



We are glad to share good news! In early August 2011, two leading companies in the field of Russian IT and UX design merged : Turbomilk and Parcsis . The companies merged under the common name Parcsis.

Two leading Russian players in the field of IT, interface design and web design: Turbomilk and Parcsis, announced the merger of their assets, resulting in a company that brings together the best Russian specialists in software development and design with a huge portfolio implemented as well as planned projects.

According to Aleksey Pelevin , designer and partner of Parcsis, thanks to this merger, a new and very interesting company has appeared in Russia, which combines all the qualities of a system integrator while possessing one of the strongest development teams. More importantly, the best team gathered in the field of creating interfaces and designing user interaction.
