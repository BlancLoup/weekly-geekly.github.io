Opera in Russia and Ukraine

Why is Opera so popular in Russia and Ukraine?



According to statistics (Bigmir, LiveInternet, links in the basement), Opera (including all versions) in Russia and Ukraine is, if not the leader, then very close to it. At the same time, in the world as a whole, Opera is considerably (by an order of magnitude!) Less popular. The data on the percentages in the table below:



The amount for Russia and Ukraine is less than 100% since other browsers were not taken into account.
The amount for the world is more than 100%, because Wikipedia uses averages from several sources.

How can this phenomenon be explained? Why such mad popularity? I will say right away, I do not know the exact answer, I myself am very interested, hence the publication of this topic.
