TEDxDonetsk - Ideas whose time has come. The first conference in eastern Ukraine. April 16, 2011, Donetsk

TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is an international project whose main goal is the global dissemination of ideas that can change the lives of specific people and the world as a whole.
TEDx - conferences that meet the global TED format and implemented by independent organizations worldwide under a US license. TED is a modern trend in the development of public opinion. This is a new format for an intellectual show that can spur curiosity even on sophisticated minds. The mission of TEDxDonetsk is the formation of an intellectual society in which people, constantly exchanging ideas, create new solutions that improve life.



On April 16, 2011, a local project TEDxDonetsk will start in Donetsk, which will bring together representatives of the fields of management, culture and art, business, design, education, social innovation, ecology, energy, who will share their new experience and ideas.
