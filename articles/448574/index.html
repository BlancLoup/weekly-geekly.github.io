One challenge from the SEO routine: the solution in 3 steps

In truth, this is not a ‚Äúbombing‚Äù case, but a standard situation in SEO and its usual solution. Of these situations is the bulk of the work. In this case, the article will be of interest to owners of online stores.



Client site at one moment lost much in positions. As a result - less traffic and calls. A story about a typical problem and its solution.

Stage 1 - Problem Identification


1. We reveal the sagging semantics cluster. In fact, it was immediately clear that the most basic inquiries had sunk: ‚Äúbuy car trailers‚Äù, ‚Äúcar trailer‚Äù, etc.



2. We study page optimization. We see an obvious pinch of keywords. And not in the texts, but in the functional blocks of the site (a frequent mistake of the owners of online stores). For example, the frequent occurrence of keywords was in the filter (towbars, trailers, etc.).

3. On the product page was a block "similar products". He was absolutely not needed, in the context of choosing a trailer. But he added spam.

4. On the pages of articles stood pass-through (displayed on all pages of articles) block "12 months warranty on light trailers". This is another +1 key "light trailers" on each page of the site.


5. In the header of the site there were several links to manufacturers.



Pretty aggressive, "through" perelinkovka.

Conclusion - a clear re-spam keywords, the presence of unnecessary relink.

Stage 2 - problem solving


We form tasks:

  1. Disable all filters from the left column. The site has a total of 30 trailers. Such a large filter has nothing. An alternative solution to this problem is to put a filter in the noindex tag. Then the search engines will not see it;
  2. Instead of links in the header - write the type of activity - "light trailers and accessories";
  3. Turn off the block "similar products";
  4. We remove key entries from pass-through blocks in articles.

Stage 3 - result monitoring, adjustment


The overall result is this (screenshot topvisor.ru). Every month we observed changes in a positive way. In the end, the position managed to return.
