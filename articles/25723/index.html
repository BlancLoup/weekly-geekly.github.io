A couple of utilities for processing photos

Like almost all of us I use digital photos for a long time. And now it is already just for everyone, moreover, now each phone takes off with quite decent quality and the number of pictures from the phones does not increase by the day, but by the hour.

All this prompted me to write a pair of bodies that may be useful to someone else besides me. Actually for this and laid them in open access.

People still like to go to different places in companies and everyone takes foti, in the end when you get all these pictures you want to put them in one folder and sort them by date (it is taken from exif) - I hope I'm not alone in this rush: ) BUT there is a problem, the time is set for everyone differently and the pictures piled in one folder and sorted by date look pretty ridiculous. First, the man was in the water, then the frame as he is going to jump, you know :)

How to make the time go on all fotikas the same? Setting the same time to the nearest second before shooting on all fotikah is a little real task. And the second option is to use my tool. See www.photo-toolz.com . You just need to take a clock from the site and tell Tul that the pictures in this folder need to be processed, the picture with the clock is this picture, and the time shown on it is. As a result, the tool will shift the time on all the photos so that it becomes like the time on the site. Not sure what will be clear from the first reading, but for explanations we have comments :)

And the second one (again on www.photo-toolz.com ) serves to trivially decompose photos into folders with dates. Because often phones dump everything in one place. Or when importing, you forget to say that the pictures are arranged according to dates. As a result, after launching the tula, the pictures are neatly arranged in folders of the form 2008 / 19_05, etc.
