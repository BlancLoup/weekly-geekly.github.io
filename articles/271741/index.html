Welcome to GDG DevFest Omsk 2015

Hi, Habr! For several years in a row all over the world, and in particular in Omsk, there have been DevFest - holidays for fans of Google-technologies and open technologies in general. Already this Sunday, November 29, IT students, beginners and experienced developers, testers, designers, and anyone interested in the IT industry will gather at DevFest 2015, which is organized by enthusiasts from the GDG (Google Developers Group) Omsk.



This year, the organizers have combined technical workshops and barcamps with the themes of marketing and promotion. Barbara Semenikhina, mobile marketing manager for Zvooq, has been invited for keyout . In his report "Promotion of mobile applications: from development to business." She will talk about the features of the App Store and Google Play, about the paid and free promotion of applications and features of monetization: how and what you can earn.

At the "Dive in virtual reality with Google Cardboard" workshop, a graduate student of SibADI and a big fan of Cardboard Sergey Baranov will be immersed in the world of virtual and augmented reality. Also, participants will try the best applications for the cardboard helmet and feel themselves as developers for Google Cardboard.

In the framework of GDG DevFest Omsk 2015, barcamps are also planned - reports, which anyone can become speakers or moderators, proposing a topic for discussion.
