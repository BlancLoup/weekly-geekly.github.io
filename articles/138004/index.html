Future

At some point, and I hope, very soon, the way the standards for the web are adopted has to change. A decade lag due to disputes and bureaucracy is unacceptable for every official W3C standard. Browser developers, frozen in anticipation of progress on standards, have to act independently. This will become a serious problem.

The creation of the web is not yet complete. The network needs a fast evolution for self-determination. The first step on this path is to create a standards organization that can provide the fast innovations we need.
