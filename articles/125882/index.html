Ten of the most sought after specializations according to Indeed.com

Although indeed.com (one of the world's largest job search services) is not limited to IT only, its ten trends in the labor market now include only skills related to the Internet and computers.

The ten most rapidly developing technologies can be divided into four groups - cloud infrastructure, sociality, mobile and HTML5 applications.

In the tenth and ninth places - virtualization and cloud computing . The growth in demand for specialists in these areas continues, the benefits of cloud infrastructure are attracting more and more companies, but the pace of this growth is inferior to newcomers, such as mobile applications.
