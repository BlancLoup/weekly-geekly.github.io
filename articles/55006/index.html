Downloading reports from MIX09

On the MIX09 conference site , more and more reports are becoming available for viewing. And what is very important - they can be downloaded.

There will be a lot of clips on the site, and therefore I advise you to subscribe to the RSS-channel of the Mix Videos 09 site.

Enjoy watching!
