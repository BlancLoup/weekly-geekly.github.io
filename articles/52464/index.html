Salesforce.com CRM in Russia

The theme of SaaS and CRM Salesforce.com has repeatedly been raised on Habrahabr. Unfortunately, on-demand service in Russia is not yet as popular as in the whole world, but life does not stand still and the Internet is no longer a luxury, but one of the first necessities.
More recently, only the most advanced Russian users or those who worked in foreign companies knew about Salesforce.com. Most Russian companies seeking to buy Salesforce directly experienced difficulties associated with the language barrier, as well as with the payment.
In May 2008, the first Salesforce.com conference in Russia was held, at which the first official partner in Russia, CT Consulting, was presented to the European leadership of Salesforce.com. And in February we received the status of a reseller and now we will be able to provide Russian users with licenses for rubles, support and implementation.
Salesforce.com is developing at a gigantic high speed, and we will inform you about new updates and new features of Salesforce.com
