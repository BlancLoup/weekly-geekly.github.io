Intel RealSense MeetUp. All about RealSense for Developers - Quickly and Businessly



What is Intel RealSense ? For the technologically savvy, a new way of human interaction with a computer or tablet, based on gestures, video analysis and voice commands. But that's not all. RealSense can be a good practice in programming, because there are a lot of new and interesting things there. Or maybe a profitable business, since the rapprochement between a person and a computer is a general trend in the development of digital technology. The main thing is interest. And to be interested - you need to see with your own eyes. So we invite you to a meeting with RealSense.

What do you need to apply RealSense technology?

  • Application created using RealSense SDK
  • A device equipped with an Intel RealSense 3D camera
  • Finally, programmer skills

What comes out of this as a result, you can, for example, read in an article written by specialists from Cappasity - using RealSense, they constructed a 3D system for scanning real objects.



But another story : how two people literally within a few hours created a prototype of a system based on RealSense that analyzes driver's behavior while driving and helps him avoid tragedy. And not just created, and thereby won the Intel Hackaton - they are held constantly and everywhere. And not only hackathons, but also global application contests, such as the Intel RealSense App Challenge with a good prize program and a bunch of buns for participants.
