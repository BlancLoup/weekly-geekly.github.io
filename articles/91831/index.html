Video interview at RIF with Maxim Spiridonov (podfm.ru) about podcasting

The interview took place a few hours before the speech of Max on RIF



Fans of podcasting will surely understand everything here, but the general public may have questions, in that case - Wellcomes under the cat, where I briefly tell about the project of Maxim Spiridonov and my personal experience in podcasting.


The resource podfm.ru has been around for quite a while; it is still the only podcast directory in Russia, alternative rpod.ru, and is now being repositioned into a podcast magazine. And the new concept of podfm.ru is very similar to the one that I proposed several years ago.
In an interview I say that it was seven years ago - I want to get better right away, seven years ago I wrote the concept of the PodcastRecords project, and I launched it directly two years later with almost no changes on the Samsung website (now it functions here podcast.samsungfunclub.ru , though no one has been involved in it for several years)

The nuances between PodcastRecords and PodFM.ru are very insignificant. The top podcasts are selected, for which the podcast studio built specifically for this is directly responsible. The studio produces podcasts that together represent an audio journal. In addition, in PodcastRecords, every podcaster could use for himself the capabilities of the studio absolutely free: podcast editors, sound engineers, etc., were at his disposal. The studio had its own production schedule (about 50 of its own podcasts per month), plus beginner podcasters would come in, get an educational program. I also conducted podcasting workshops, negotiated with manufacturers of mobile devices and publishing houses, and quite successfully.
