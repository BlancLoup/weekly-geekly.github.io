QutIM 0.3 beta released

Hello dear habrayuzer! After a long break after Alpha, we are happy to present you the beta version of qutIM, codenamed SciFi. Over the year, a lot of work has been done and it will be described below what has appeared new.



  • The Jabber plugin was rewritten from Gloox to Jreen : Due to the fact that the development of gloox was stopped, it was decided to change the library from Gloox to Jreen, written and currently being actively developed by the qutIM team. Unlike the first, Jreen is completely asynchronous, which has a positive effect on responsiveness.
  • New file transfer engine, in which there are several options for file transfer: Directly from client to client and through narod.ru. Currently, the engine is in active development and therefore may be unstable.
  • Ports on Symbian and Maemo 5 : For this year, qutIM has become much more mobile, ports for Symbian and Maemo 5 have appeared . One Pole is developing the qutIM port for Android, which unfortunately is delayed due to the terrible lack of hands. Documentation on the creation of chat topics can be found on our forum .
  • Contact list : Now with the contact list, you can do whatever your heart desires. You can make a list, and you can split the protocols.
