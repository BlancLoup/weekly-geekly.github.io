Started futurological survey

On February 8, the Futurological Survey, dedicated to the Day of Russian Science, started on the website of the Club of Scientific Journalists. The purpose of the survey is to find out what expectations people associate with long-term prospects for the development of science and technology, that is, figuratively speaking, to study the projection of the technological future into the present.

Habralyudi can check in and take part in the survey by clicking on the link .

The code = habr parameter (at the end of the address) will allow the survey organizers to get to know the Habrahabra audience and make a summary of it. The survey is planned to be held until the end of the month, but if the rate of data collection will remain as high as it is at present, then the period may be reduced.
