Call me back on ... iTouch!



The British company Truphone, specializing in Internet telephony, today laid out for downloading a special program for iPod Touch owners with the latest firmware. Softina successfully turns the Touch into a mobile phone (provided that there is a WiFi point nearby).

Not only is the program distributed free of charge , the owners also have free calls to other truffle owners, as well as to Google Talk users and Truphone internet telephony subscribers. In the future - calls to fixed lines, Skype, msn, as well as integration with Facebook and Twitter.

In order to make a call, you need an iPhone headset, or an adapter. However, Truphone also plans to sell special headsets for communication.
