ICQ Mobile for Symbian - you heard right, for Symbian



We have released a new version of ICQ Mobile under the Symbian platform. The main innovation of the release is multilingual support, in addition to Russian, English, German, Czech and Bulgarian languages ‚Äã‚Äãhave appeared.

Maybe someone had a question - why do we even deal with Symbian clients in the era of Android, iPhone and Nokia alliance with Microsoft? The answer is very simple - Symbian is still alive.

A few numbers:
  • Symbian is the most popular mobile OS in Runet . Moreover, the fall in its share has not yet begun, and growth, if it has stopped, is only recently.
  • Symbian is the most popular platform among smartphones in Europe. The fall in the share is catastrophic, but even if it continues at the same pace, in a year Symbian will be only the second.
Another possible question is: why do we need a separate client for Symbian, if there is a client for Java that works on any Symbian device? Here the answer is even simpler - it is simply better. J2ME has a number of extremely significant limitations or, more precisely, the Symbian API provides many additional features, life with which both the developer and the user becomes much more convenient. Its decisive advantages are two, but each of them is worth a separate development:
  • The Symbian client supports multitasking much better, including sending notifications from the background.
  • Symbian client is significantly faster.
Well, in addition, translucent icons, file transfer and other pleasant things.
