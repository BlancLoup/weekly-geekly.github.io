Review (video review) notebook Asus N55SL

Battlefield 3 and Star Wars: The Old Republic were chosen to demonstrate gaming performance.



The Asus N55SL is a 15.6-inch universal multimedia notebook designed for people who need a powerful configuration laptop for everyday use. At the heart of the hardware stuffing of this model is a quad-core Intel Core i7 processor, working with the support of 8 GB DDR3 RAM.

Graphics performance is provided by the use of a discrete graphics card NVIDIA GeForce GT 635M, which has now replaced the GeForce GT 555M.

The audio system created as a result of the cooperation of ASUS and Bang & Olufsen ICEpower (supported by SonicMaster technology) should not disappoint most consumers, and the external subwoofer will help to fill all rooms with sound, immersing the user completely into the world of multimedia.
