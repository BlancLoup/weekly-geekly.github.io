Sometimes it is so difficult to find an ICQ client that would suit the user in all respects ... Some work not as stable as we would like, the other lacks functionality, and the third does not suit the interface. And all this even with the current diversity of the relevant programs! The situation is even more aggravated when it comes to the ICQ-client on a mobile device, for example, one of the many Nokia smartphones running Symbian S60 3rd Edition. Yes, a considerable number of various instant messengers were made under this platform, but for me personally, each of them had significant shortcomings, forcing to try more and more new clients in the pursuit of perfection.
So, in this article I will talk about a relatively new ICQ-client for Symbian 9, which, in my opinion, is closer to the ideal than the rest - this is BayanICQ.
