iPhone unlocked?

Participants in one of the forums claim that they were able to completely unlock the iPhone for use with any mobile operator. According to hackers, their solution allows you to make and receive calls, send SMS messages and use Internet access through EDGE. The only non-working feature is Visual Voicemail, but its work requires operator support.

The process of unlocking the iPhone is quite difficult. In addition, to perform the operations described by hackers, additional equipment is required, including a device for reading / writing SIM cards, and the SIM card itself, which will be used in the unlocked iPhone, must have version V1, as newer, V2 and V3 , have more resistant protection.

Hackers have tried to unlock the iPhone since the new Apple phone went on sale. Such operations are not considered illegal in the United States and most European countries, but it should be remembered that such actions often lead to a loss of warranty.
