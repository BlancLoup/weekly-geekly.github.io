Apple thursday

On Thursday, February 3, the regular monthly game development seminar for iOs was held in Moscow in the Hurry Tower on Pokrovsky Boulevard in Moscow. Led the banquet AlexGoncharenko . To his question - how did you hear about the event, 298 people out of 300 who came answered - From the announcement on Habrahabr.

The audience was friendly, intelligent and educated, which surprised me and resurrected faith in the bright future of humanity. Almost every listener had an iPad in his right hand, an iPhone in his left, and a macAir girl on his lap.

A total of 4 reports were heard - 4 completely different approaches to developing applications for the iPhone and getting money from the appStore.



First report

At 18-45 the show began. With delight, the audience met the first two speakers - the legendary Voynov brothers (Zeptolab), the creators of the hit 2010 - Cut the Rope. Unlike the application, the report was not the most brilliant, although it is immediately obvious that people are correct, they smile well, in the 1st act they showed the public a trick with a rope.
Cut the Rope is their second child, the first in the appStore was called PARASHYUTIK and brought the authors 300,000 paid downloads plus an understanding of the earning strategy. They created Cut the Rope on the same principles that Disney created cartoons. This is a great animation, professional sound engineer, heavy thread movement model algorithm (who has taken the most development time), a boring script, a team of testers friends and the choice of a correct and fat publisher (the target audience of the application and the publisher coincided).


The guys called this publisher Publisher and, due to my stupidity, I decided that it was a last name, like Abramovich. I was mistaken, nevertheless it was Publisher who did the main work of promoting the game. The application has been sold 5 million times. Brilliantly. How much money did the publisher take? It is not known, but the guys left immediately after the report to the railway station, and not to the airport, which is alarming and hints at a careful approach to choosing a publisher.
The brothers answered questions from the audience in the case, they did not lift their nose, thanked fate and good luck. As I understand it, now they want to cut down a little babosikov on the release of toys, T-shirts, etc., with the character of their game. When asked whether they were participating with their toad in the Sochi 2014 emblem competition, the younger brother laughed and applauded, the older one became thoughtful.

Second report

The second report was voiced by two charming girls from Game Insight. The company owns online games like Island and something else that millions of foreigners play. These citizens buy all sorts of goodies for virtual fun for real money. Game Insight ordered the iPhone version of the game to some programmers, put it into the store, received 600,000 downloads from the appStore from its users, and claims that it has profit from this event. And it took only 2 months. Normal result, yes?


The monthly income from the games they have more than 100 000 US dollars. Russian citizens, according to the girls, do not play their games and do not pay money. The quality of the report was breathtaking. The slang of DOMA-2 heroes was greeted by the kindly neighing of three hundred Spartans of iPhone developers. The girls were asked many questions, but the most important was not asked what they were doing tonight.

Third report

The third report was read by Ilya Kurylev, Flexis. He clearly and sensibly told about the iPhone Game Center, about its advantages and numerous shortcomings and about the fact that they themselves have not created a single game application for the iPhone.


Unfortunately, the public did not ask the speaker a single question, thanking him only with applause. And I would ask Illya where he got the numbers from, that 92% of iDos have 4.0 onboard iOs. The data collected by my applications speak of less than 50%. From this point of view, today there is little point in using Game Center for gaming applications. There are a lot of holes on the server side and few Russian devices with the 4th generation of iOS.
