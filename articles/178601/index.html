The Russian Indie Bundle # 1 launched


Yesterday the distribution of the first free indie games bundle from Russian developers started.
The list includes:
  • Flyborg
  • Shine
  • Kite
  • Dungelot
  • Astrodude
  • Transition

All games are absolutely free and available for download on the website, some are also available for download on the digital distribution service Desura .
Link: www.russianbundle.ru
