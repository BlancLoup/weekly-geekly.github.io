In Irkutsk, launched a non-cash payment of travel in public transport


In the public transport of the city of Irkutsk launched a cashless fare. In this article we will try to understand how this system works, and whether it has prospects for the future.

On April 5, 16 vehicles were equipped with cashless fare devices: 12 buses 37 and 67 routes, 2 second trams, and two trolley buses 1 and 7k. This is a joint project of Sberbank and the MasterCard payment system. On vehicles with this method of payment, you can see a special sticker:


The design of the sticker is not the most suitable, for my friends it was associated with free WIFI on board the bus.

Going into the bus, you can see detailed instructions for payment of travel.

Photography Instructions


You need to do the following:

1. Click on the green button.
2. Attach a contactless payment device to the screen, or insert a card with a chip into the card reader.
3. Take a check.

From the following it is clear that:

  • Sberbank actively advertises payment with MasterCard cards (in the comments they write that Visa also works).
  • Payment is made by any contactless accessory, or a credit card with a chip.
  • The payment process is simple, and it is fully produced by the passenger, not the driver. This payment method should unload the queue.

At the end of the poster there is a note: in case an error ‚ÄúYour card in the stop list‚Äù appears, go here: link (for some unknown reason, the QR code leads to the page for the city of Voronezh).

From all the information it is clear that payments are made in the offline mode, i.e. the terminal accumulates payments, and at a certain moment it is connected to the network, and the data is sent to the bank. Thanks to this payment method, payments are made quickly (the terminal does not connect to the bank‚Äôs servers). The rate of payment increases, due to the lack of need to enter a PIN code (with contactless payment).

Why did I decide that payments are made offline? It's simple. In the poster and on the website mentioned about the stop list. The stop-list includes cards with a negative balance, as well as blocked cards. The terminal loads the stop list during the transfer of payments to the bank. Therefore, you should be careful! At the time of payment, the terminal is unable to check your balance. Do not pick up debt. Different banks apply different sanctions in this case.

From theory to practice. We sit in the 67 bus, drive to the desired stop, transfer the card to the driver, the driver is confused. He does not know what to do with the card.
