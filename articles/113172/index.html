Toshiba Dynabook Qosmio T750

In the Toshiba Qosmio line there are a lot of bright, interesting models for which the latest achievements of science are realized. In its new Dynabook Qosmio T750 laptop (codename Picasus), Toshiba has added a bit of brightness to technology. As a result, the original notebook with a chameleon lid turned out. The model has been announced so far only in Japan.

The case of the novelty is covered with several layers of polyester, so that it can change color depending on the viewing angle. If you look at the cover of the laptop at right angles, it seems blue.



When you rotate the laptop in your hands, the color of the case changes from blue to purple. To this we can add that the special coating prevents the appearance of fingerprints on the glossy cover. This technology, called Picasus, was developed jointly with the Japanese company Tour√© Meihan.
