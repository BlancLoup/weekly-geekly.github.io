Google News refused the news of the Associated Press

The website of the news aggregator Google News has ceased to appear news Associated Press. The latest content from the largest informational content dates back to December 23: pruflink .

According to analysts, at the end of January, the term of the next license agreement between AP and Google ends, and now the parties cannot reach an agreement on the new terms of the deal. Apparently, AP wants more money.

The first agreement between AP and Google was concluded in August 2007 . Since then, Google News has received the right to post news AP directly from the source for a period of 30 days, after which they should be removed from the Google News index. So the moment of truth will come on January 22, 2010.

Terms of the agreement between AP and Google have never been disclosed. It is no secret that AP is one of the few content generators, who nevertheless achieved payment from the largest search engine for placing its content. This is not surprising, because the main business of AP is the sale of paid access to news, and not income from advertising, so that the additional traffic from Google News does not bring them any dividends.

Similar negotiations on a new agreement are currently underway between AP and Yahoo . All this is part of the AP's new licensing strategy , which provides for the allocation of a number of exclusive customers who receive some of the most important news 20-30 minutes ahead of the rest.
