Galaxy Note 8.0 Announced


There was a rumor on the internet that Koreans wanted to present an 8-inch tablet, and before the release, the photos of this tablet went online. Today, Samsung has published a press release for the 8-inch tablet.

The tablet resembles a stretched series of Galaxy SIII and Galaxy Note II smartphones, in addition to the S-Pen.
The company introduced a 4-core Exynos 4 processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz, a fresh version of Android 4.1.2 Jelly Bean (up to 4.2 will update quickly), an 8-inch mini tablet with a resolution of 1280x800 and 189 ppi. The main camera is equipped with five megapixels, and the front one is only 1.3. The tablet's internal memory is 2 GB, internal 16, or 32 GB (with MicroSD you can lift up to 64 GB), the tablet has a non-removable 4.600 mAh battery, support for the accelerometer. The weight of the tablet is 338 grams (plastic case).

The most interesting thing is that it will be possible to call from this box directly from this box.

Samsung says that it will be possible to buy a tablet in the second quarter of 2013, in any country.

The price is not announced.
