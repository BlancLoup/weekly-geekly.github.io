Yota gives a month of free internet when you buy a 4G laptop

A month of free internet will be received by everyone who buys a 4G laptop in the Yota sales network. Today the network includes an online store on the Yota website, mini-sales offices in shopping centers in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Ufa, and Sales and Customer Service Centers in Moscow and St. Petersburg. In each of these points, users are offered different types of devices for connecting to Yota, among which everyone can choose what suits him. For example, in the online store Yota now represented more than three dozen laptops from leading manufacturers.

If you purchase any of them from November 24, 2009 to January 15, 2010, the buyer will receive a month of free 4G Internet.

To use the gift, it is enough to go online through the Yota network, register on the company's website and activate the special gift card that comes with the laptop. After that, it will be possible to use high-speed wireless Internet 4G for exactly 30 days.
