Apple plans to order a record number of new iPhones

The company's entry into the market of large smartphones was a little slow, but this year sales of new iPhones are going very well. So, in the first half of the fiscal year, the corporation sold 135.6 million iPhones, which is 43% more than in the same period of the previous year.
