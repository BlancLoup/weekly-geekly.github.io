Exxon Mobil accused of manipulating data on the impact of the company on the environment to deceive investors

Investors, in fact, would be afraid in this case not of the harm to the environment itself, but of penalties imposed by regulators related to the tightening of international legislation on the protection of nature. It is worth noting that the investigation did not start suddenly, the Exxon Mobil case is being considered since November 2015.
