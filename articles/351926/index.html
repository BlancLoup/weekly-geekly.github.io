Welcome to Front-end MeetUp in Raiffeisenbank. UPD: Broadcast Mitap

Hello,

We invite you to the first open Front-end MeetUp on March 28 , organized by the internal community of developers of Raiffeisenbank.



Our internal Front-end community in Raiffeisenbank is actively developing. We know how important it is to communicate with people from other teams and projects, to have the opportunity to ask for advice, discuss just emerging technologies and share experiences. That is why we open the doors and invite you to visit us.

The event will take place on March 28 at the Moscow office of Raiffeisenbank in Nagatino, starting at 18:00.

The program of the mitap has 4 reports scheduled:

18:00 Grigorov Dmitry Raiffeisenbank

Higher Order Components with Functional Patterns Using Recompose

I'll tell you about some of the techniques for using higher order components (High order components) inside the Recompose library. Writing code with Recompose looks a little different from the native syntax of React, but it's not useless and my goal is to show in live examples how to use HOC.

React, Mobx and ES7

  • React library for creating client GUIs.

Building Docker + Nginx + CRA + Koajs + MongoDB in 10 minutes
