Left-handed mouse DeathAdder

The company Razer has released a "left-sided" version of the popular mouse DeathAdder. According to the manufacturer, DeathAdder Left Hand Edition is the first truly ergonomic manipulator for left-handed users.



In DeathAdder, only the design has changed. The technical specifications remained the same: the resolution of the Razer Precision 3.5G sensor is 3500 dpi, and the sampling frequency is 1000 Hz. The minimum response time of the sensor is 1 ms.

DeathAdder has five programmable Hyperesponse buttons. Teflon feet are provided for silent sliding on the surface. Dimensions of the device are 128x70x42.5 mm.

DeathAdder Left Hand Edition will be available just the other day. It will cost $ 60.
