After a fairly successful launch on the mini-computers market, the Chinese company Tronsmart is mastering a new kind of media players, which are designed to broadcast an image from a smartphone / tablet / computer screen to a TV screen. These devices are easy to use and very affordable price. In today's review, we will introduce the model called Tronsmart T1000.

‚ñ∫ Work with devices on Anroid OS

Tronsmart T1000 can communicate with various Android and Windows devices through the Ezcast application, and also supports Miracast and DLNA.
Consider the work in each mode separately.
First of all, we connect the Tronsmart T1000 to a TV or monitor and turn on the power. Switching on takes place automatically, the download is fast - about 4-5 seconds and information about connection methods already appears on the screen.
To manage, you need to download the Ezcast application and install it. In order not to bother with downloading the application from the browser - you can scan the QR code directly from the TV screen:

First, let's check the capabilities of working with Android devices. After installing Ezcast on your smartphone, connect to the Tronsmart Wifi access point, the connection password is displayed on the TV screen.
General view of the Ezcast application on the smartphone screen:

Go to the settings Tronsmart T1000 by clicking the Settings button.


You can adjust the screen resolution, Wifi access password, select an access point for connecting to the Internet, select a menu language, and also update the firmware. Firmware is updated quite often and downloaded by air (OTA update).
Navigation in the settings on the smartphone is carried out using a virtual control panel:


First of all, go to the Internet section and from the list select your router:

Now Tronsmart T1000 is connected to your smartphone and simultaneously has access to the Internet.

Let us consider the features of the Ezcast application:
Photo - display photos from the gallery of your smartphone on TV. You can edit the photo and then save it.

‚ñ∫ Miracast

Tronsmart T1000 supports Miracast mode. In this mode, the screen of your device is completely duplicated on the TV screen. This function works quite well, but playing games on the big screen will not work - a rather noticeable lag of about 1 second makes itself felt. But if you need to show something on the big screen, then Miracast is exactly what you need.
To switch to this mode of operation, you need to click on EzMirror in the Ezcast application, go to the screen settings of your smartphone and turn on the wireless projector.
