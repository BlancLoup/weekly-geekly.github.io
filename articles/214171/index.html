At one of my first trainings, one of the managers present asked a question:

- Alexander, but this is the situation. I return from vacation and find that, while I was away, my boss fired my key employee. You, as an expert, think that this is normal or not?


When they call me an expert, you need to understand the effect. I feel very pleased, I get off the ground, the brain turns off and then the flow of words begins. (If you want to set up an experiment, go to the speaker at any conference after the report and say something like: ‚ÄúThank you, I liked your report very much. - Of course, not right. This is micro-management, and in general ...

In short, over the years, I had to be very careful. And now, before answering the question, you have to ask about 10 clarifying questions.

In the end, we all clothed the intellect with a map. And they painted there not only the mechanisms for solving situations with people and changing their behavior patterns, but also:
  • Daily work with people to be done
  • Regular work with the management and the customer, which should be done

  • ...
