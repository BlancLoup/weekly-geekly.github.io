Customer Blog - I want to buy!

I decided to create a new blog ( I want to buy! ), Where I could learn the nuances of acquiring and using various IT things - hardware, gadgets and software. According to the idea, if you decide to purchase this or that thing, but still doubt and want to get feedback from other habrazhiteli, publish the topic and describe what you decided to buy, about where and for how much, as well as your need or task, to solve which product is bought . I hope that among the participants there are quite a lot of users with various gadgets or hardware, so in the comments you can read reviews about their work, maybe someone will tell you where to buy cheaper or easier, and maybe you should do with a completely different device or program.



For convenience, I would suggest the following format of posts:
- Name : the full name of the device and the manufacturer, for example, Asus X80L mini-laptop
- Where and how much : if you have noticed a purchase in an online store or an off-line outlet, you can specify the address, for example, Moscow, GigaMarket salon, the price is 28,000 rubles .
- Link to the technical description: http://market.yandex.ru/model.xml?hid=91013&modelid=1630446
- What is it for me ? Describe, if possible, your task or problem. For example: I want a convenient small laptop on the Wintel platform for use on trips and rarely, in nature. Basically, office applications and multimedia, battery life is critical, weight and size - I do not want to carry something big and heavy :)

Here is such an idea. I hope this is useful to habrazhiteli.
