Social behavior statistics on news sites: how can it help with traffic and monetization issues?

Today, when many media outlets are completely switching over to electronic versions, and the audience is devoting more and more time to their friend tapes, integration with social networks and a continuous increase in citation are very important for a news site. Social and behavioral metrics of users acquire an enormous role, about which you need to know as much as possible in order to analyze and stimulate social activity. According to Awareness, Inc., about 96% of companies measure the number of fans and followers, 89% - traffic from social networks, 55% - mention of the brand on the Internet, 55% - balls and 51% - the tone of statements.

The Media Briefing did a little research to find out which news media is best integrated with social networks and get the most tweets and balls. 6 popular titles were reviewed and the number of balloons and tweets of 50 publications of each of them was calculated. What exactly will help statistics of social behavior of users of news sites?

Growth of traffic from social networks
According to a Reuters Institute study, the news site receives an average of 7.5% of referral traffic from Facebook. At the same time, in general, traffic from social networks, according to various estimates, can be up to 30%, and first of all due to sharing. Statistics of social activity on the site will help to understand which pages have the greatest social potential and that will allow increasing the citation.

Organic traffic growth
What is fundamentally important in the first place for news portals, which are just beginning to win their share of readers. Both Yandex and Google pay increased attention to social factors when ranking resources. At the same time, the number of likes, balls, friends on social networks, non-search traffic, how much time people spend on the site, etc. is important. Many people believe that in the future social signals may even crowd out links that have long been considered the main factor in ranking. Today, sites integrated with Google+ are ranked higher in the SERP than other resources.

Monetization
Another advantage follows from the previous point. Since social and behavioral factors are gaining more and more weight when ranking sites, they are also important when building a reference mass. Sites integrated with social systems and receiving a consistently large number of likes and shores will take priority from companies that are looking for donor resources for posting links or articles.

Hanging content quality and relevance
Knowledge of social and behavioral metrics will make the content as relevant as possible to the audience. Comparing the statistics of likes, balls, the number and quality of comments, the traffic received by the pages, you can understand which sections, topics, content formats, headings work best. And then adjust the content, increasing the number of shores and satisfied readers. For example, you can easily check whether the reader really so powerfully clings to headlines like ‚Äú7 ways to change life for the better‚Äù, ‚Äú33 tips for hamster owners‚Äù, etc.

Increase the conversion of individual elements of the page
The title, the length of the text, the presence of an illustration, the formatting of the text - all these details affect not only whether the material is read, but also what is the probability that he will get a like or a ball. According to The Media Briefing, in the Guardian, texts with a length of more than 800 words receive an average of more than 100 tweets, while texts with a length of up to 500 words - no more than 10. Interestingly, the length of articles in the Telegraph, Daily Mail, The Times does not affect number of tweets.

The optimal location of social buttons on the site
The appearance of social buttons and their location affects the number of likes and shores they can get. Of course, each site is individual, and what works on one resource may not work on another. This is where social activity statistics will help. Many say, for example, that the dynamic panel on the left side receives much more likes and sherov than other variations. In the case of Quick Sprout - 226% more. Also on the news portals usually work well medium size in the field of view and easily distinguishable buttons on the page (which will not always be effective for an online store).

In increasing the number of regular readers
According to statistics, the person who put the likes or the ball is likely to return to the site. And the more balls from one person, the greater the likelihood that he signs up for updates and will regularly look at the site.
