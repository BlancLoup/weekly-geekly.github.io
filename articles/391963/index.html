"Laser remover" will destroy rust in seconds



Any of us know what rust is. It is rather difficult to fight with it, and if rust has already covered a metal product, a lot of time and effort must be spent to eliminate the rust layer. Mechanical tools (for example, a rigid wire brush) and some chemical substances will have to be used.

But after all, we live in a new century, where very often there are technologies, devices that were considered science fiction a few years ago. Flights to other planets, attempts to create a nuclear engine for rockets, lasers are all realities of today. As for lasers, they are just proposed to be used to eliminate rust. This is a "manual remover", whose work was shown on video.



The video is unusual, some YouTube users believe that this is a fake. But there is a kind of real company that developed this product. This is the German company CleanLaser . It offers laser systems of various form factors, including portable lasers, as well as mobile workstations. The site states that "Powerful, very short and fast pulsations of a laser beam lead to the emergence of micro-plasma flashes, which contribute to the sublimation and release of the target substance." In general, it seems to be true.

The capacity of the plants on the site is different - from 12 W to 1 KW, which is quite a lot. At the same time, almost nothing is said about the safety of installations. True, the operators only work in protective glasses, as seen in the photo from the site.
