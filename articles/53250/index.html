Google translator

Recently, I wrote about a class that implements text translation via Google Translator .
After some thought, I decided to go further. Why do people need a class? They want to see how everything works.

So what is it about him?
Well, firstly, it is completely open source and is distributed only in it and in no way compiled. That will allow many of the habra people to learn the basics of QT.
