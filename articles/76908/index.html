Xbox 360 hacked

So, finally opened the Xbox 360, and run a third-party code without a signature.


What does this give us?

Homebrew , games without a disk directly from the hard drive \ usb, freedom from Live, we score on the regions, and firmware drives! In general, all that and still a lot of tasty that made the first box.

In 2 words how it works. A card reader is soldered, and a switch is placed between the standard Nand (chip where the dashboard is stored) and the card reader, where we already put our version of freeBOOT, and the software we need.

Here we change the flash drive region

And now about the sad:
The mod can be made only by those who have a 7xxx version of dashboard or have a CPU Key, all others suck a paw and wait for a jtag (Pull CPU Key) on an 8xxx firmware.
And the mod itself is very laborious, requiring very straight hands, and the ability to handle a soldering iron. So in the coming months, I think there will be quite a bit of movement in this direction.
