How is electronic document management taught in a technical college?

I want to talk about the seven-year experience of conducting the course Enterprise content management (ECM) in St. Petersburg Electrotechnical University.

Subject: EDS and ECM


Recent years have been marked by a very intensive development of the ECM market. Many government and commercial organizations are switching to electronic document management and electronic document storage. In this regard, the need for specialists with relevant knowledge and skills in the field of ECM.
Engineers and programmers should be able to provide the technical side of the issue. Doktorovedy should know the specifics of electronic document management. Financiers and economists need to know the subtleties of working with electronic financial documents. The transition to electronic document management entails changes in legal practice - therefore, there is a need to provide relevant knowledge to future lawyers.
The tasks of teaching ECM course at a higher educational institution can be formulated as follows: first, it is to form students' understanding of the theoretical and methodological foundations of information management, and secondly, to study the specifics of using modern tools and information management methods in specific subject areas.
