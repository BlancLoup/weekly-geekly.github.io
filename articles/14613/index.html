Gyration M2000 Wireless Mouse with UltraSense Technology

The company Gyration , a manufacturer of accessories for desktop and mobile PCs and remote controls for digital devices, has introduced a new product - the M2000 Travel Air-Mouse wireless mouse. When creating the device, the UltraSense technology is used, which allows you to move the cursor around the computer screen, manipulating the mouse in space.

The M2000 Travel Air-Mouse is cute and comfortable by itself (the shape is symmetrical, the left-handers are not offended). But its relative uniqueness (relative, since Logitech, for example, already has a similar product - MX Air) lies precisely in the fact that cursor control with its help consists in moving the device in space. Thanks to the technology used UltraSense and the presence of several sensors, Travel Air-Mouse is highly accurate tracking movements. A wireless connection (at 2.4 GHz) provides PC control at a distance of up to 30 meters, which allows you to easily make presentations. It supports the mouse and some simple commands with gestures that allow you to adjust the volume to play music or switch between tracks in a media player.

According to Gyration, the M2000 Travel Air-Mouse will be available in the first quarter of 2008. The novelty will cost $ 70.
