Alltunes.com was left without Visa and MasterCard

The online music store Alltunes.com repeats the fate of its long-suffering "brother" in the business Allofmp3.com - it was also disconnected from the Visa and MasterCard (MS) payment systems. The store owners do not grumble and have already filed a lawsuit against Rosbank and its subsidiary United Card Services to the Moscow Arbitration Court - these structures were directly involved in disconnecting sites from payment systems. According to Vedomosti , the plaintiff considers it illegal to disconnect Alltunes.com from Visa and MC, since payment systems can only disconnect customers who do not comply with the laws of their countries. The owners of Alltunes.com from the Internet-Audit company claim that they work exclusively within the framework of the law, and through the court they demand from Rosbank to fulfill the conditions of the contract.

This conflict seems to be indicative of the confrontation of Western rightholders and Russian sellers of mp3-music. The parties are holding their positions with all their might - the first claim that Allofmp3.com and the company are grossly violating all sorts of laws, the second - that, according to Russian norms, they are definitely fine. And no one has yet judged them objectively.

For example, now representatives of Visa, explaining the disconnection of Alltunes.com, say that the decision of their company fully complies with the laws of Russia, as well as international copyright and intellectual property standards.
