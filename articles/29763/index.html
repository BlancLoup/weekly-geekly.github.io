Montenegrin domains - this is popular!


Go Daddy, the official domain name registrar in the .me zone, announced it has taken a new record. During the day, more than 20,000 new addresses appeared in the Montenegrin domain zone, which is a record number of registrations per day.
However, the huge popularity of the .me zone was seen from the first day of open registration, when Go Daddy reported that due to the large number of requests, the main servers were disabled for a while.
Let me remind you that you can register a domain in the .me zone at Domain.me at a price of $ 19.99 (the minimum registration period is 2 years) .
