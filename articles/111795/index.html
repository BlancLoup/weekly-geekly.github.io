Cyclemeter - GPS tracker for iPhone

In the iTunes App Store, there is a novelty for adherents of an active lifestyle, who can‚Äôt imagine a holiday without movement and communication - the Cyclemeter application ( link to the application page ).

Designed specifically for the iPhone, the application is purchased and installed once, not annoying advertising, for use does not require a password and monthly payments. Cyclemeter is a real find for those whose sporting preferences suggest a constant change of location. If you are skiing, cycling, or hiking, and you own an iPhone 3G, 3GS or 4, Cyclemeter is designed for you.

Cyclemeter captures a wide range of data on user movements - time, coordinates, distance covered from a given point, height of stay and speed of movement. All this data is archived, and take up very little memory. The most convenient thing is that you can set any data in the Cyclemeter not only in touch mode, but also using voice control. In the same way, in the voice mode through headphones, the user can receive data about his records in more than 20 different parameters.

Cyclemeter small video presentation:



Cyclemeter offers visualization on maps, charts, calendars, which can be organized in accordance with the individual plan and route of travel. For example, you can see what distance you covered in a day, week, or month of skiing, and then spread out this total distance on tracks of varying difficulty, or other parameters. In addition, data can be visualized relative to previously recorded, and see - on a map or graph - how your skill grows. But that's not all! Cyclemeter is able, on the basis of the entered user data, sports equipment and route, to give advice on how to improve the result, in what mode it is more efficient to follow a particular route. Also, the application can report how many calories are spent with benefit, what is the load on the heart.

Finally, Cyclemeter updates the user's location data every 5 minutes, sending this data to Facebook, Twitter and Google Maps. You can play messages from your friends tape on social networks and send e-mail in text-to-speech mode without stopping the route.
