Gramafon: a wifi router and music player in one

The company Fon Wireless, which specializes in the production of wireless routers, has begun a fundraising for a mini-router with the function of a cloud music player on Kickstarter .



From the characteristics it is known that the device will work on the Qualcomm Atheros AR9341 chipset.

In total, for the project, the company plans to raise $ 250,000 by May 15. If funding is found, then the first batch of routers from 6500 pcs. will be released in July.
The cost of a Kickstarter router ranges from $ 30 to $ 60.



The device is a compact router with the function of streaming songs from popular audio services (Spotify, Google Play, Pandora, Grooveshark, Soundcloud, Rdio, Songza).

To use the device, you need to connect it to the Internet (Ethernet cable or other Wi-Fi router) and to an audio system or speakers (3.5mm audio output). Then you need to connect your smartphone to the Wi-Fi signal of a music router, after logging in via Facebook, to play songs through it through any streaming service channel or the built-in free online service WahWah online radio.



Another feature of the device is the ability to control playback by several users. Gramofon is powered by the Qualcomm Atheros AR9341 chipset.
