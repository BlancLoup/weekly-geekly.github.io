BlackBerry 10 Jam World Tour in Moscow June 26

As an introduction, I will tell you that in one of the previous posts I mentioned that last week we interviewed a representative of RIM in Russia. For more than an hour we talked about the new platform of the company and the prospects for its development, felt new devices.

I BB10 very pleased. QNX provides truly unique opportunities for users and based on it RIM creates one of the most impressive platforms in the mobile market. If all that is said, will be brought to mind, then BB10 is quite compete with Android and iOS.

It is clear that the success of the mobile platform is applications. And RIM also performed well here. For developers, RIM offers a lot of goodies. For example - simple porting applications from Android. For example, a simple porting of Qt applications. Creating a beautiful and functional interface in a few lines, better support for HTML5, creating native applications in Visual Studio and Eclipse, etc.



And one of the important steps to improve interaction with mobile developers is the BlackBerry 10 Jam - the RIM conference, which will take place in Moscow very soon and where the new platform will be brought. The conference has already been very successful in America and is now coming to Russia. In our conversation, we also touched upon the upcoming tour, and they say that this will be not just the performances of evangelists, but also good workshops with good examples of creating different applications for BB10. Well, traditionally they play alpha devices among the participants.

Under the cut, the official announcement of the BlackBerry 10 Jam and the link to the registration.

On June 26, BlackBerry invites Russian mobile application developers to take part in the Russian part of the BlackBerry 10 Jam world tour, where they can get detailed information about the BlackBerry 10 and the benefits of developing software for the new platform.

The BlackBerry World Tour 10 Jam is a one-day series of events designed to help developers around the world learn more about the BlackBerry 10 platform. In the first part of the conference, which starts at 9-30, developers will become familiar with the user interface and BB10 architecture. Next, during the day, BlackBerry experts will talk in more detail about the technological features of the process of creating applications for BlackBerry 10, using both the original development environment and using HTML5.

The conference will also be attended by representatives of RIM, responsible for relations with developers, who will be able to personally answer all the questions asked by guests and tell in more detail about the advantages of the BB10 platform, using which you can create unique applications.

Participants in the BlackBerry 10 Jam Moscow Conference will be able to:

  • One of the first to engage in the development of applications for the BlackBerry platform 10
  • Explore the details of creating applications for the BlackBerry platform using HTML5 + BlackBerry WebWorks or the original C / C ++ based environment
  • Talk with experts who will share tips on development and provide the information you need to get into the process of creating applications for BlackBerry 10
  • Get a limited edition test device running BlackBerry 10
