It took about a year to cut in the process and coding in the evenings. During this time I became so attached to this business that when the choice arose - either I quit and finish the project in the foreseeable time, or I write it in the evenings for many more years, I decided to quit. Accumulations were, therefore, nothing held.

Three more months of daily work, and I released a couple of definition applications for iOS. One for indoor plants , the second for tropical fruits .



I must say that they did not justify any particular hopes for monetization. Yes, there are flaws with the interface. But the main reason is that it is very expensive to fill content with determinants. And the market is now a very tough place. And its rigidity, I clearly underestimated. But he turned his attention to the system for a smart home. It is close to cell phones. This is something that many do not yet have, but that everyone will definitely have. And if you take the most stereotypical and routine household chores, then watering the plants is far from the last place. And unlike robots, vacuum cleaners, almost no one has a home sprayer. Well, since I'm a nerd in the shower, the choice was predetermined. It was decided to make a system for watering plants! This decision occurred to me in the fall of 2013.

Idea


I have a lot of flowers at home. Mostly orchids, but there are others. And the constant problem with the fact that you need to water on time. And I have something to do, then leave, then something else. I forget this thing, unfortunately. So I came up with a project for people like me - an automatic watering system for plants.

It would be unfair to say that once and invented. The appearance of the system was born for several months as sketches were drawn and the first prototypes were created. Here is what happened in the end:

The watering device looks like a pot stand. Watering is done with a pump, and the remaining water flows back into the tank. Everything is controlled via the Internet, from a smartphone (iOS, Android), you only need a home WiFi network. The device remembers the frequency of watering even if the connection to the Internet is cut off. Pushes notifications to the phone if there is not enough water left. Reliably and does not leak, because in principle there are no wet connections. Holds so much water so that it is not necessary to add a month or two.

The price was conceived so that I could provide all my 20 pots with irrigation and not go broke. The use of a stamped plastic case and a reduction in the number of electronics immediately follow from this point. Long thought how to do it and came to modularity. There are ‚Äúsmart‚Äù head devices, and there are ‚Äústupid‚Äù subsidiaries, which can only be watered when the head ones order them. There is generally only a pump and stamping, you can do it very cheaply.



Why such a device? Honestly, I'm tired of the efforts of designers in this area. From those who have never grown flowers, but really want to make some nonsense. Type, hydroponic system with replaceable cartridges for growing vegetables. Well, nonsense, really. This is a toy, not an assistant to plant lovers. I want to make exactly the most useful device.

The same goes for some Swiss knives that shine and water and do anything. But everything is bad. No, let my device only water. But it makes it as simple, convenient and cheap as possible. And then expand if the demand is.

Prototypes


I did not run on investors, PR or do something else like that. Just started working on materials for the project.

Drawings in OpenSCAD (I liked OpenJSCAD more , it supports an honest object model), 3d printing of prototypes, initial electronics.
Since I have never worked in 3D packages, but I had to do a 3d drawing, I decided to go through everything on the market. Frankly, for the programmer, it is a complete horror, except for the listed open box and its branches. Well, maybe not for any programmer, but just for me. But this is some kind of hell. Blender, Sketchup - heartbreaking interfaces and stupor, even when trying to draw a cube. But parametric modeling in the scud - great and nice, what you need. Especially if you have a penchant for drafting.



After the parts were ready and fitted in 3D, it is time to look for printing services. The unpleasant discovery was that none of the foreign grandees of this business delivered to Russia. Refer to mail problems.

But, to be honest, and their price did not suit me. My product weighs 200 grams, they had it under 10 thousand. Very expensive!
Started looking for us. It turned out that in the Russian market the average price of ABS or PLA printing on amateur printers is about 20 rubles per gram. On roboforum.ru I found a companion who prints ten. Two thousand for a prototype is already acceptable. And the fact that you have to pick up at the subway station - so we have bootstrapped or how :)?

As an electronics, I used the MSP430 + CC3000 kit . Very good chip and convenient board, smart config is able (this means, there you can transfer login and password from a closed wifi network by pressing a button). He himself figured out only the basic things, the program and shild made to order. 5000 rubles, quite normal, I would have killed it for two weeks. As a result, the board accesses a specific host via DNS and takes from there the status whether to enable it or not. If the signal is on, the motor pump pumps water. Off - stops pumping.



Here I will also say a few words on the design. If you look closely, you will see that the lid is concave. So excess water flows into the tank, without getting past the device. At the bottom there are connectors for power and electrical connection of daughter modules. Holes on the sides of the top cover - the grooves for the capillary. This is a small part in the form of a plastic bracket with a capillary cord, which allows you to keep the same water level in adjacent devices.

Power in the basic version of the network through the adapter, but really want to make solar panels with intermediate charging batteries. Calculations say that this is real if you do not keep a permanent WiFi connection. But for now the power is mains, as it should be for the simplest first version.

The prototype of the mobile application design ordered for two thousand. The application itself was made in two evenings, after all a prototype, no intricacies. I could not resist making a dynamic control of the calendar instead of a picture, that's why two evenings and not one.



The prototype was ready. Works, water at the touch of a button. To fasten a schedule to it and to add the application is very simple. It is clear how to translate this into serial rails.

I will not say now who I work with. I will say about the conditions of work. I understand that they are generally common.

Video


Almost all the pictures on the product I had taken during the creation of the site. So I focused on the video. This is probably the hardest part in all the preparations for the campaign.



What should be in the video:
  • You and your team.
  • Product.
  • Story.
  • Audio.

Do not worry, from the first time you still will not remove normally.

Many people spend thousands of dollars on video. But it can be done independently and it is absolutely real. You will need a camera, a tripod, a light. Very desirable helper.

I can give the following tips:

  • If you want to speak to the camera, do not look away and use an improvised prompter - the text of speech printed on A4 sheets in a very large font. To look at the center, I just made a hole in the center of the sheet and put it on the camera.
