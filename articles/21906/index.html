MagStay Pro - Ski Mount for MagSafe

More than a year ago, Apple improved the power supply for its line of laptops, endowing it with a MagSafe plug. Personally, I have been enjoying this little trick with my ears for more than a year, especially in moments of stumbling over a white wire going from a white small box to a silver box a little more :) Actually, MagSafe doesn't give me a trip, since its magnetic attachment doesn‚Äôt release a small amount of force, saves both my foot and my laptop, acquired by overwork. This main concern for the user's health and wallet is the main function of MagSafe. But it turns out that someone needs something completely different, which gave birth to MagStay Pro .


MagStay Pro is a small lotion (or as they say - a gadget), the main function of which is to keep the power plug in the corresponding slot. It uses a USB input located in a MacBook Pro next door. We can say that MagStay Pro is a USB plug with one additional function :)

Honestly, for myself, I see the point in using this gadget, which is designed not to "firmly" fasten MagSafe to the case, but rather to increase the threshold of force leading to the disconnection of the power cord. Often when working with a laptop on the couch and in unnatural postures (for example, when writing posts for Makovod ), my plug goes unnoticed, staying at the same time as if in place. And the computer, of course, switches to the battery.

Volunteer Jordan Satok tested MagStay Pro on his own skin, more precisely on his own MacBook Pro. In the end, he expressed the hope that Apple will turn its attention to such a useful gadget and include something similar in the standard package.
