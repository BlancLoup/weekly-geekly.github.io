Overview of the inverter APSX-1250

The APSX 1250 inverter fell into the hands. Full name: APSX 1250 PowerVerter.



Converter - PowerPSerter APS X series charger for an output load of 1250 watts with automatic switching of power modes: Basic-Autonomous.

Where it can be applied:

Small power backup system.
This is not an uninterruptible power supply in the classic sense of the term - UPS.
This is a variant of long battery life, up to several tens of hours of load, instead of using a portable power station.
The system is quite autonomous and does not require user intervention to change the modes of operation. Everything happens automatically. There is a network, the load is running from the network.
When the external voltage of 220 volts disappears, the inverter automatically switches to battery mode.
In the mode of operation from the mains, the inverter operates as a charger, ensuring the charge of the connected batteries.

Reliability of any other type of connection

Bare conductors, without terminations with a sleeve and a tip for connecting to a battery.

Solder wire without sleeve.

All this is a big question. In other words, there is no guarantee of reliable operation for many years.

Why? The answer to this question is very simple. During operation, the current in the battery circuit can reach high values. It is necessary to take this into account when applying the appropriate cross-section of cable connections to the battery circuit.

In this case, the presence of a reliable contact on the terminals of the battery terminals and in the cable connection terminal is mandatory. The operation of the logic and control and management of the inverter is provided by the internal node, which operates on battery voltage. The slightest violation of the battery circuit, the loss of contact for a fraction of seconds and the inverter will turn off, and the load will be de-energized. The system will become completely inoperable.

In addition, most cases of failure of batteries and inverters - this is not the right connection.
When using open, non-sealed or automotive batteries, the inverter must be isolated from the batteries and never install the inverter in close proximity to such batteries.

Configuration and configuration

Depending on the use of the type of battery, their number, it is necessary to configure the inverter.
For this is responsible two groups of switches next to the LEDs that display the mode of operation and the battery level.
All switching and settings must be made before connecting the network and batteries, and only then make the connection.

Very important note: In most cases, the factory settings, the position of the switches does not match the actual system you are installing and plan to operate.

The switch configuration is not correct - and battery failure is possible, the inverter does not work reliably.

Recommendations for use as a backup power supply system for heating systems, automatic control of boilers for heating systems

One of the applications of these inverters is back-up power supply of circulation pumps and automation of heating systems.
In such an application, ground and neutral control is important. Otherwise, automatic heating systems do not work.
When connecting the inverter, it is necessary to take into account the control of these circuits and to ensure the presence of a neutral when the inverter is connected.

In most cases, automatic control of the boilers does not work if the connection scheme is not correct, not only for inverters, but for connecting portable backup power plants.
