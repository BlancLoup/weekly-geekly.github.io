Hackers presented "Kraken" to break the encryption algorithm in GSM-networks



As we all know, the overwhelming majority of cellular work with GSM networks, this situation is observed in most countries. Mobile phones operating in GSM-networks, now there are about 3.5 billion worldwide. One can imagine what a tasty morsel of interception and wiretapping of cellular conversations is for attackers of all kinds. True, all the data transmitted over GSM networks is encoded using the A5 / 1 algorithm (in most networks), and it is not so easy to decode the transmitted data (at least for ordinary hackers, it is clear that the special services have long used excellent equipment that allows all do it).
