Google Technology User Group St. Petersburg, Open Air

Hello!

I think that you know about the opening of the Google Technology User Group in Moscow!
During the first meeting, discussions were held on the most interesting projects in the Google office in Moscow, as well as communication with Google developers and marketers in Russia.
A little more about the Moscow meeting:
http://arksoftblog.blogspot.com/2010/07/gtug-moscow-google.html

The next event we decided to organize Open Air in St. Petersburg.
It would be very nice if ideas appeared in our native St. Petersburg worthy of discussion and consideration on Google!



Therefore, the main purpose of Open Air will be inventing, discussing and presenting your projects!

For the most part, the focus of the event will not be on the technical features of the development (that is, not the programmer one), but on the fresh ideas of new projects that will be interesting in the GTUG environment.

At this event, programmers will act more like valuable participants in terms of technical advice and recommendations on the implementation of the projects under discussion.

We invite you to participate in the discussion of interesting projects proposed by you and colleagues!
We propose to get together, combining communication, acquaintance and networking to
come up with technological projects and outline the next steps needed in their implementation.
The best projects will be presented to Google!

The meeting will be held in Yusupov Garden (metro station Sadovaya, 10 minutes walk) - this coming Saturday approximately from 16.00 to 19.00 (July 17)
