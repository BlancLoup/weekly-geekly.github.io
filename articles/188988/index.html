During this time, more than 500 students from various universities and cities of the Russian Federation passed through her classes: St. Petersburg, Volgograd, Ufa and many other cities, including, of course, Nizhny Novgorod. As a supplement to the report, I offer you two interviews with students of the summer school - present and past; how the school can affect the career of a specialist and the expectations associated with it.

Maxim Krivov, student of the summer school of 2009

Maxim, how did you find out about the summer school of Intel?
As a student at Moscow State University, I constantly monitored the Intel site and participated in their contests. For example, we twice reached the final of the Intel Game Demo Contest. There I saw an article about summer school. And then I filled out an application, well, they accepted me.

What, in your opinion, is the main thing that a summer school can give to a programmer?
The most important thing, in my opinion, is experience in a large company, when you manage to participate in the software development process in person. And the process of quality and debugged, this is Intel. Find out what code review is, how to properly conduct refactoring, learn the full power of C ++ templates. More importantly, the school lasts for almost two months, and it is possible not just to look at all this from the outside, but to do the specific task yourself under the supervision of a mentor.

How did the school influence your professional activities?
In the most direct way. I was engaged in parallel programming before her, but after participating in school I realized that this was not just one of theoretical directions, but a demanded work that few people can do normally and are ready to pay for. In addition, here I met a student from Krasnoyarsk (Sergey Grisan), with whom we worked together on one task. Immediately after school, we decided to develop our own library for heterogeneous systems, and then it started spinning and spinning. They founded their company ttgLabs, customers and money appeared, the library was brought to the product. Now we begin sales. In November, our company, by the way, will be three years old.

According to your information, does participation in a school help employers at Intel?
It all depends on personal motivation. If you set a goal to get to Intel, then the school is ideal. Not in the sense that after it is guaranteed to take on the job, but that there will be an opportunity from the inside to look at the company and choose an interesting project. Get acquainted with the leader, find out what exactly they are doing, what kind of team is there. Well, to prove yourself, of course, without this, nothing happens.

Irina Soboleva, student of the summer school 2013

What can you say about the scientific work that you do here?
It feels the high professional level of those people with whom I communicate at Intel. I like the style of communication very much: you can ask any question and they will explain everything calmly and intelligibly. A good addition to learning is the opportunity to get a certificate in programming. I tried to get certified in our city, but here everything is done much more competently and sensibly.

What is the main outcome of the school for you?
