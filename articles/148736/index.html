First set in the Interface Development School

Most recently, we wrote about the birthday of the School of Data Analysis . And today we have another birthday: we announce the first set of the Interface Development School.

SRI is primarily a course of lectures on industrial front-end development. In the classroom we will talk about the developer‚Äôs tools, how to work in a team in a better and more efficient way, about time-saving techniques, a little about design.

The second part of the School will be practical. In the process of working on a large project, the leading front-end developers of Yandex will share with the students all the accumulated knowledge.

We invite in SRI primarily senior students or recent university graduates.

Classes will begin on September 15 and will go for a month in the Moscow office of Yandex . Students will have to do their homework, and at the end of the course pass the exam.
