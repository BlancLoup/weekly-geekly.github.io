Uruguay: each student - a laptop


Uruguay was the first country to provide a laptop for each student. Over the past two years, 362,000 students and 18,000 teachers have been involved in this program. However, this is only part of the OLPC system, in which the cost of a laptop is $ 100. Linux laptops are installed with a Suar user interface.
The cost of maintenance was $ 260 per child, including maintenance, repairs, training, and the Internet. This amounted to slightly less than 5% of the education budget.
