Virtuilka - the current state of affairs

We were silent for a long time after the last publication about pre-order, since we didn't want to write any insignificant news. Now here came the news that, I think, many were waiting.

KDPV


So:



We have finalized the design documentation for the circulation version of Virturilki

The circuit diagram of the device was partially redone, Wi-Fi was removed from the main board to an additional module, which would allow further use of various communication chipsets, added previously unused outputs for special devices (for example, to connect an LCD) and much more.

They also abandoned the use of a standard Wi-Fi module of the HDG104 chip, replaced it with a more pleasant Marvell 88W8686.

It was possible to finally determine the chain of procurement, production and delivery

We will produce in China / Hong Kong. For help in the organization of production, a special thank you to the habrauer dzhe .

The production of the first part of the circulation of 100 copies

We expect the completion of production and complete testing of samples by the end of May and the beginning of June. If everything goes well, we launch the main circulation. Under the optimistic scenario, the first Virturilks will get to the happy owners in June.
