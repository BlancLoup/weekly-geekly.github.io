RedHat Acquires Ansible

As follows from the official press release on redhat.com , the Ansible and Ansible Tower systems now belong to Red Hat Inc. For Red Hat, this is clearly a move towards closer integration of Ansible and Open Stack, for the community, I think this news is also positive - this is recognition of the project by large commercial companies, and confidence in its further development.

The amount of the transaction was not disclosed, but according to various sources ( 1 , 2 ) it is about $ 100 million.
