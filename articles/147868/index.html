How is UniSender better than MailChimp?

In the end, both UniSender and Mailchimp have thousands of users - who, I think, know what they are paying for. Let's see: so, what can offer a domestic service in comparison with the world's largest service?

Functional


Let's start the main thing: UniSender allows you to do both email and SMS distribution. MailChimp can not send SMS. Rather, there is a special additional system that, with skill, can be used. But sending SMS on the same database, with the same ease as the mailing lists - in MailChimp will not work.

Both systems allow you to set up a chain of letters, embed social network buttons in the newsletter. As for the functionality of the split-test - of course, it is also implemented in both systems. Segmentation

Both services allow the segmentation of the mailing list. Segmentation in UniSender is possible in two ways: using tags and the recently introduced mechanism with advanced segmentation capabilities. The second method is a universal method that allows segmenting for almost any task.

MailChimp allows for segmentation by how often your customer wants to receive emails. However, with us you can do it too: select the desired segment into a separate list and assign different frequency to these lists.

Corporate solutions

We position ourselves as a solution for the corporate segment. It turns out that this is really the case: you can set up access for different employees with different levels of rights within the same account. MailChimp does not allow this, which means that it will not be so convenient for a large company to use the service.

Spam reports

Both MailChimp and UniSender take on contact with the postal services to determine the causes of your mailing in spam, if that happens. However, UniSender has a natural advantage in terms of working with Russian postal services due to its long-established working contacts.

Subscribe form on your site

Services are similar in that they provide the function of creating a registration form that can be placed on your website for collecting leads.

Free services

Both services provide full free accounts, so you can register and start working right away.
