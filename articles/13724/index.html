iCar: Apple will produce cars

Apple and Volkswagen are negotiating the joint production of a car called iCar. It is planned to create a compact small car for young people, in which the various ideas of both companies would be realized.

Apple CEO Steve Jobs (Steve Jobs) is negotiating with the head of the Volkswagen car concern Martin Winterkorn (Martin Winterkorn) about the joint production of the car, which would have implemented the most advanced ideas. It is known that the first meeting of two top managers took place a few days ago in California, reports the German edition of Capital.

It is planned that the development of Apple and VW will be related to compact small car and will be called iCar by analogy with the cult Apple products - iPod and iPhone. According to experts, such a machine can be most popular with young people. Both companies will certainly try to implement their best ideas in the product. The German giant to its advanced automotive concepts will get the perfect technical "stuffing" from the company of Steve Jobs.



Apple has already entered into contracts with a number of automakers, such as BMW, integrating their products into their models. It is rumored that the company recently merged with Mersedes-Benz to create a fundamentally new dashboard navigation system, which could be introduced in 2008.
