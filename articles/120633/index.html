Salaries and demand for Runet specialists in spring (March 1 - June 1, 2011)

The Internet recruitment agency PRUFFI presents the third study of the level of salaries of specialists on the Russian Internet technologies market. The study covers the period from March 1, 2011 to June 1, 2011 and is based on open vacancies in this segment from the sites HH.ru, Moy Krug, Rabota.ru, Rabota.mail.ru, LinkedIn, SuperJob and PRUFFI vacancies.

2. Maximal salary growth for PM, marketers, SEO and content specialists was given by e-commerce projects. They significantly overheat the payroll market, outbidding specialists from competitors and large Internet resources, with salary increases of up to 50 percent.
