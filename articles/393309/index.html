Tesla autopilot reduces crash rate by 50%



The autopilot system in the Tesla car has already saved one driver from the accident in the Tesla Model S when it was clipped by a truck from a nearby lane. The computer system managed to slow down and dodge. This is not the only case. There are several videos from car recorders of happy Tesla owners on YouTube that were lucky to avoid accidents.
