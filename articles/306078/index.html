ViaLatM - posting to Facebook



The article describes the updates in the service VIALATM , allowing you to quickly publish your favorite tracks on the social network Facebook.

Preliminary remarks:

In addition to the constant expansion of applications and devices supported by the service (devices that support the MiniFinder, Zenda, Sho-Me TR09 protocols are added to the list), new functionality is added - the ability to export tracks in GPX format has been added.

Create Favorite Tracks


You can add a track to your favorites either directly from the list of recent tracks, or from the selected track. Unlike getting a track, where you choose a track for a given interval, only those sections of the track where there was movement appear in the list of the last tracks (no stops or stops in this list).

Going to the list of favorite tracks, you can edit the information on the selected track. In the updated version of the service, a password for public access is not required. It is generated automatically and attached to the link to the track. It is recommended to provide the track with a title and comment. When accessing a track by public link, this information is displayed on the screen (the title is in the top menu area, and the community is in the status bar at the bottom of the screen).



Facebook posting


When you use the context menu to make a track accessible to observers, a mini-image is created for it. To fill the post on Facebook you just need to copy the public link to the track. The mini image of the track, the title and comment fields are automatically generated. This is shown in the title picture of the article. Automating the process of publishing tracks will be expanded for other social networks, and automatic generation of html code will be made that can be integrated into your websites and Internet resources.
