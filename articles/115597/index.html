Why don't "pirates" pay?

The market lacks the ability to quickly find and download movies, and any other content. Where does a person get a movie? There is an opportunity to watch movies online, there is an iTunes Store. But these offers suffer from limited choice or high prices, and low download speeds.
It is because of these reasons that people follow the movies for torrents. Because everything is there and it loads quickly.

Only 10% of torrent users are pirates by conviction (supporters of free content). The rest are willing to pay, but there is no convenient solution.

Tell us more about your "preventive" solution?

The solution consists of two parts. The system of search and blocking of unlicensed content and the system of paid distribution of content in torrents.

Initially, we wanted to block content on the side of the Internet provider. But understand that this is a difficult option.

Now we have a completely different solution. We operate in the cloud and catch files by attributes, namely, by a label that is written in all files, info-hash, individual coded label.
