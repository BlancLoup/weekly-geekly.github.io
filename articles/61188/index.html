First impressions of using HTC MAX 4G

Yesterday, I bought myself an HTC MAX 4G and decided to share first impressions of use. Suddenly someone will help determine the choice.

First, the main thing - I liked the communicator. He has a big screen, almost all possible interfaces for data transmission, a considerable time of work. If you compare with the previous ASUS P535, then HTC seems to be even more enduring. For 2 days of operation, I lost only one stick of charge. At the same time, I spent some time on the tail, when I synchronized with him. Reading books from a PDA has become more convenient. Nice theme design. The stylus on the magnet is generally considered a brilliant find. With this decision, he will not fall out of the nest.

One of the most convenient on-screen keyboard keyboards. It is curious that each key has 2 meanings, depending on whether there was a short press or a long press. Very original decision.

But the devil, as always lies in the details. And here are the little things that prevent me from living:
1. It is difficult to find a case. I went around 3 cell phone salons - the communicator refuses to enter the existing range of cases. Practice shows that without a case the communicator is scratched rather quickly. The overall functionality is not affected, but unpleasant.
2. It's hard to get used to the lack of a navigation key. For example, in the contact list it is difficult to scroll down one contact. It is necessary to drive in a name, not a flipping.
3. strange algorithm of the message block. Visible history of communication with the contact, but you can not send a message from the history. It is not immediately clear that if you poke in an empty space, then there you can write the answer.
4. There are no hot buttons to change the orientation of the screen and launch important applications. The screen for me does not fully replace them ... in the car I used to work by touch.

I would be grateful if more experienced owners tell me how to get around these details.
