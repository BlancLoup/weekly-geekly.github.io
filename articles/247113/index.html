Under the cut a brief summary of the year in InfoboxCloud.

Let's sum up 2014


Regions InfoboxCloud

In the past year, in addition to our own data center in St. Petersburg, we launched new regions in Krasnoyarsk , Amsterdam , and Moscow .

The data center in Moscow received the Tier III Operational reliability certificate in addition to Tier III Design and Facility, becoming the most reliable data center in continental Europe and the only one in Russia with all Tier III certificates. This allowed the use of InfoboxCloud for the most critical corporate projects and at the same time posting data on the territory of Russia.

The European InfoboxCloud region in Amsterdam allowed to host international projects targeted at users from all over the world thanks to the excellent connectivity of the data center network with other regions.

Reliability and performance

In InfoboxCloud in Moscow and Amsterdam, we switched to using the fault-tolerant distributed storage system Cloud Storage, thanks to which we were able to activate multiple replication of virtual machines. Failure of a physical cloud server does not lead to data loss or service stop. This has become a significant advantage of the cloud over dedicated servers (dediki) and other clouds.



For all users, we activated Enterprise SSD ‚Äì caching in the cloud based on PCI ‚Äì Express SSD and SSD DataCenter, which allowed us to obtain the highest performance of the cloud subsystem of cloud servers and successfully compete with SSD servers of leading global cloud providers, while maintaining affordable prices.

In St. Petersburg, we updated the core of the network, which significantly increased the availability of all services and the speed of the network. This had a positive effect on hosting and VPS . We continue to improve the main services, increase their performance and reliability of key Infobox services.

Be simpler, focus on the main thing

This year, we have significantly simplified the control panel of our services. This is just the beginning.

We abandoned non-core services and focused on the main thing.
