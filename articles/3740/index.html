$ 20 million for one photo?

Not every photo can cost tens of millions of dollars. But Yahoo will probably soon have to pay this amount to Shannon Stovall from Ohio, USA, who filed a lawsuit in the state. She claims $ 20 million for illegally using her photo in Yahoo Mail.

The fact that Yahoo uses her photo in her email service was discovered by the woman when she started an account there, namely, in a welcome letter that automatically arrives to all new Yahoo Mail users. Now it requires $ 20 million in compensation for moral damage, part of the proceeds from the use of its image and compensation for legal costs, reports Google Watch .
