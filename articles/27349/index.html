Google refuses to support Google Browser Sync in Firefox 3

Today, one of the activists of the movement for support in Firefox 3 of this extension has published a letter from Google. Here is a free translation:

Thanks for trying Google Browser Sync and for your feedback. It was not easy, but we decided to remove support for Browser Sync. Since the team has been moved to other projects in which they are constantly busy, we don‚Äôt have time to upgrade the extension or to continue supporting it.

For those who decide to continue to use Firefox 2, we will continue to support throughout 2008. Also, we can recommend several other products that solve the same problem. We hope that one of them will work for you:

Mozilla Weave [labs.mozilla.com] by Mozilla Labs - Offers bookmark synchronization and history across multiple computers.
Google Toolbar for Firefox [toolbar.google.com] - Stores your bookmarks online and provides access to them from any computer on the network.
Foxmarks Bookmark Synchronizer [addons.mozilla.org] - Synchronizes all your bookmarks between computers where it is installed.

Respectfully,
Google team.
