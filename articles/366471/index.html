Tomorrow the Moscow Application & Technology Expo 2015 conference will begin

From March 12 to 14, one of the most significant events in our country related to the world of IT technologies will take place in Moscow at the Sokolniki Exhibition and Convention Center (Pavilion 2) - Moscow Application & Technology Expo 2015, or simply MATE-2015 .



By visiting this event, you can familiarize yourself with all the advanced technologies that will shape the IT market in the coming years. The exhibition will present the latest technologies in the fields of augmented reality, 3D printing and scanning, applications for sports and robotics, as well as automotive and gaming applications.



At the forum it will be possible to evaluate in the work all the innovations in the field of mobile medicine, in particular, modern wearable gadgets.



Dear and professional speakers will talk about new trends in information technology, such as mobile TV, Internet of things and mobile commerce.



Also at the exhibition, Smiles.Education will present the latest Shkolnik mobile app, which is an advanced tool in the field of mobile education.



Smiles.Education Company will be represented at the booth (A 14), and on March 13 our Marketing Director will give a report at the conference , tell in detail about all the features of the application developed by us, as well as answer all your questions.

We are looking forward to see you !!!
