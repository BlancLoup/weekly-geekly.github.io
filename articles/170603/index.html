Practical necromancy on the example of the game Gulf War: Operation Desert Hammer (1999)

Good to everyone!
It all started with a closet with old games. About 10 years ago he took a game from a friend, put it in the nightstand and remembered it 10 years later. I wondered what kind of game this was, unknown to me. It turned out that this is a tank arcade of 1999, from the firm 3DO, about the adventures of a super-American tank during the Gulf War.



Googling and YouTube showed that little is known about the game. One review on our site and one on foreign.

Windows 95/98, Pentium 166 / 32M Ram / 150M Hard Drive space / 4x CD-ROM / Direct X 6.1 / Microsoft Compatible Mouse
