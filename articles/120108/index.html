Device in operation



When you turn on the book, the menu appears in front of us as in the picture above on the left. At the top, we see a list of the last three books being opened. In the center of the pictogram to select the mode of operation with the book. And below the clock and calendar, which by the way is much more convenient than watching this information using a mobile phone.
Navigation through the list of books and menu items is performed by a joystick. To browse there is a convenient key on the center of the right edge of the book.

Library


This mode allows you to select a book to read. You can choose from a file on a flash drive, select by genre in the library or download from bookland.

  • On the "files" tab, something like a file manager opens. The book allows you to climb in directories up to 6 levels of nesting.
  • Tab "library" allows you to select a book from the library by genre, type or naming.
  • If you go to the ‚Äúbookland‚Äù tab, then the piece of hardware will connect via wi-fi and give you the opportunity to search for the desired book and download from this site. The feature resembles the similar in the Amazon Kindle. What is funny, the book caught a wi-fi network while traveling in a bus from a house 30 meters from the road, which impressed me =).


Photo

This piece of iron can watch photos. More precisely, this function is available in the booklet, but it is not very comfortable to use it due to the low resolution of the screen and the small number of displayed colors. Apparently it was added to keep up with other manufacturers.

Vocabulary


When you call a dictionary on a marked or current word, a window appears in the universal dictionary ABBYY LINGVO, in which the corresponding interpretations are given. Both directions are supported from Russian and back in seven languages. In the dictionary, in addition to translation, you can call a list of similar words, edit the search word, change the language.

Applications

In my version there were only Tetris and Sudoku. But they can be added, as stated in the instructions. The games are not particularly heavy, but the battery is planted not weakly. The whole problem is in the E-Ink screen, which uses energy to redraw the picture. And the speed leaves much to be desired.

Music

I never understood why a player is needed in an e-book. However, my girlfriend likes to read to the music. So on the fan. The battery almost does not plant.

My impressions

Although this is my first e-book, but I have a very good impression of using it. For all the two weeks of use, the battery has not sat down even by half. At the same time I read at least 5 hours a day.
The cover well protects the book from scratches, but at the same time, it does not lose its presentation at all. Every time it seems that you are picking up a completely new book.
Of course, before choosing, you need to try a few more devices, but I would completely stop at this one.
