Microsoft is going to buy a manufacturer of business smartphones BlackBerry



Canadian telecommunications company BlackBerry reappeared in the news feed. At this time, the reason for this were rumors that the company is going to acquire Microsoft, according to PC Tablet . Sources claim that not only Microsoft is interested in buying BlackBerry. Such Asian giants as Xiaomi, Lenovo and Huawei are eyeing the company, and they all have good chances for success.

According to sources, Microsoft is working with several investment firms in order to assess their chances of buying a Canadian company. The software giant plans to increase its presence in the mobile segment and expand its patent portfolio in the field of data transfer (IOV), mobile platforms and the communications sector as a whole through the development of BlackBerry.

Chinese manufacturers also aim at investing in a company that, in their opinion, could improve BlackBerry‚Äôs position in the European and American business smartphone market.

So far no specific information has been provided regarding the transaction. BlackBerry has long been ready to buy another company, but so far this has not happened. Sources claim that the Canadian manufacturer is attractive to Microsoft by the fact that business software developed by BlackBerry perfectly complements the products of the giant in this segment.

The financial statements indicate that the Canadian company is rolling out its mobile division, but at the same time is actively engaged in hiring in the field of sales and marketing. The BlackBerry website has more than one hundred vacancies: 69 positions in North America and 31 in the company's office in Waterloo.
