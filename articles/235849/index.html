Tracks Flow finally closed

On a wonderful Saturday morning, a letter with the closing of Tracks Flow came to the post office:

Good afternoon, dear% username%!

Unfortunately, the Tracks Flow service is closed.
We want to thank you for being with us! You made us feel that we are not working in vain and are making an interesting product. Since the opening of the service on Google Analytics charts, we have seen growth, but it was not fast enough, we were pulling down problems with content. About a year ago, we realized that the game was not worth the trouble and froze the project. He began to die slowly. Now there is no point in supporting the project.
But know - we did not give up! We have a lot of interesting ideas, a team and an invaluable experience. Very soon we will be able to please you with new projects.

Of course, we could not leave you without your music. In the attachment you will find the data of your music profile. If any information is not in the archive - write and we will send everything.

With optimism
Tracks Flow Team
