Happy programmer!



Hello, habra developers and not only!

The ecosystem of developers is very important for the IT-industry, because it is the developers who determine what programs and applications will be, which means they determine our future.

The technical audience team, on behalf of Microsoft, congratulates all of you on Programmer's Day and wishes you an inexhaustible source of inspiration for your endeavors.

Under the cut a small contest for developers with gifts.

Leave on the wall of one of our groups ( facebook or VKontakte ) a story about how you became a developer. Story authors with the most votes (I like) will receive one of the following prizes:

1st Place - MSDN Ultimate Subscription
2nd place - MSDN Premium Subscription
3rd and 4th place - 250 GB hard disk
5th place - Mouse

Results will be announced on September 23

(Note: delivery of prizes (except subscriptions) is carried out only in Russia)

Ps as always you can use the hash tag #msdevday to congratulate colleagues and friends through our resources.
