Video review of monoblock (tablet) Sony VAIO Tap 20



The Sony VAIO Tap 20 is a tablet hybrid and, strange as it may sound, a candy bar. This all-in-one computer is equipped with a 20-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1600x900. Its main feature is the presence of a built-in battery and a folding leg, which makes it possible to use it as a portable tablet.

VAIO Tap 20 is available in two versions - based on low-voltage processors Intel Core i5 or Core i3. The amount of RAM is from 4 to 6 gigabytes, and 750 GB or 1 TB hard drives are used for data storage.

In addition to Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, the monoblock is also equipped with an NFC chip, for fast data exchange with other devices that support this technology.
