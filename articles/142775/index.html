Evernote Devcup Second Annual Developer Contest



We are pleased to announce the second annual developer competition - Evernote Devcup . As part of Devcup, software developers and designers are invited to come up with and implement interesting applications and services that will allow 25 million Evernote users to expand the capabilities of their accounts. The prize fund of the competition is more than $ 100,000, and the finalists will get a chance to present their work at the Evernote Trunk Conference event this year in San Francisco.

We have prepared the following prizes:
  • Gold - $ 20,000
  • Silver - $ 15,000
  • Bronze - $ 5,000
  • 6 finalists - $ 10,000 each, as well as flights and accommodation for the duration of ETC 2012

What would you like to see?


Successful application combines tempting functionality with a beautiful and intuitive design. We expect the finals to be used daily by tens of thousands of people, allowing them to preserve and recall important things in their lives.   • Evernote Food and Evernote Hello are mobile apps that offer users additional Evernote features for use in selected areas of life. If you divide the world of Evernote notes into these categories, you can think about the orientation of the future application to work with one of these types of notes.

We are going to talk about this (and the competition in general) in a meeting with the developers of Evernote Dev Meetup in Moscow on Friday April 27th.

Last year's experience



Last year, about 1,000 developers from around the world took part in our competition. A lot of people were from the USA, about a quarter from Asia, as many from Europe, and there was also a noticeable presence of developers from South and Central America. All six finalist teams visited ETC and presented their work. You can view their presentations here - evernote.com/about/etc . Last year, the top prize went to Touchanote . This is an Android application that allows you to link real-world objects with notes in Evernote using NFC tags. Another application finalist - Notable Meals - formed the basis of Evernote Food. The third - Colorstache - allows you to search in Evernote notes on the presence of a given color.

Details


Any software that is integrated with the Evernote API can be submitted to participate in the Devcup. You can develop an application for computer platforms, mobile devices or the Web.

Key dates:
  • From today and until June 25 - the formation of the team, ideas, creating an application.
  • June 25 is the last day for receiving applications.
  • July 23 - the announcement of 6 finalists.
  • The end of summer - the presentation of works-finalists at the Evernote Trunk Conference, the definition of the winner of the Grand Prix and the winners.



As part of the launch of this competition, we have several offline meetings around the world. You have a great opportunity to discuss the potential and feasibility of an idea with our employees, chat with other developers and learn more about Evernote. As mentioned above, in Moscow, Evernote Dev Meetup will be held on Friday, April 27 . More information about the meeting we wrote here . We also planned to hold webinars, if suddenly you can not attend in person. For more information about the developer competition, see Evernote Devcup - devcup.evernote.com .
