Life is like an 8-bit game: Manual


Life is a game that literally everyone plays. This article is your guide.

The basics

You may not even guess, but real life is a strategic game. It has fun mini-games ‚Äî for example, dancing, driving a car, running, and sex ‚Äî but the key to winning is simply managing your resources.

Most importantly, successful players spend their time on the right things. As the game progresses, money appears in it, but the correct use of time should always be your main priority.

How to obey your own commands

Many players notice that when they are going to do something ‚Äî for example, ‚Äúgo to the gym‚Äù ‚Äîthe body completely ignores them.

This is not a bug. Each player has a condition that cannot be seen directly, but it looks like this:


If any indicator of a condition falls, your body will ignore orders until you figure out its needs . Try to learn when you are tired and hungry - you will not have time to blink an eye when your attention switches to Twitter.

Especially important indicator will. Willpower melts during the day, replenishes slightly after eating, and is fully restored after a good sleep. When will power is low, you can only do what you are really interested in.

Every decision that is made requires willpower, and decisions in which you suppress an attractive option for the less attractive (for example, to study instead of watching TV) require a lot of effort of will.

There are a few tricks to control yourself:
  1. Maintain good condition. If you are tired, hungry, or you lack a lot of entertainment, your will will go to zero. Constantly monitor the status of the character
  2. Do not require too much will from one day. Distribute the most difficult tasks for several days and alternate them with less difficult ones.
  3. Start with the most important tasks. This will make other tasks more difficult, but will increase the chances of solving the most important task.
  4. Reduce willpower costs by limiting choices. If you are trying to work at a computer where you have access to Facebook, you will need more willpower, because you are constantly choosing a more difficult task (work) instead of an easy one (Facebook). Eliminate such distractions.

The key task of the game is to maintain a balance between your various priorities and the state of your body. The main thing - do not turn on the autopilot, otherwise you will never do anything.

Location

Your environment constantly influences your progress, condition, and chances of passing to the next level.

Yes, the game can play well almost anywhere, but in some places it is much easier to do it. For example, if you are a woman and play in the wrong country, many achievements will not be available to you.

Each player has almost zero chance of being born in the optimal place, so study your options and think about moving as early as possible. Environment is a multiplier of all your skills and states.

Partner search

Attractiveness is in itself a challenging mini-game, but basically it is a by-product of how you play. If you have a great condition and high skills, you are already very attractive. A tired, irritable, stupid player is unpleasant and, perhaps, should not look for a relationship.



Already in the early stages of the game, participants often reject themselves and are rejected by other players. This is normal, but, unfortunately, can deplete your condition, since most players are experiencing rejection. You will have to use willpower to continue the game, and willpower replenishes with sleep - so time will cure you.

80% of the search for a partner comes down to showing off their best sides, which - like so much else in life - means using your time on the right things .
