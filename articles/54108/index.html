RollerCoaster Tycoon 2


The second part was released in October 2002 by Infogrames.

The continuation is distinguished by new attractions, scenarios, locations and graphics since the year 99 almost did not change, except that the developers slightly sanded the corners. :)

Those who are not scared away by the graphics go to play the scenarios, and during the game they discover that the gameplay has not changed in any way conceptually! It is exactly the same as in the first part of the RCT! We build rides, ennoble the park, hire staff, develop new merry-go-rounds, pay loans and earn money. RollerCoaster Tycoon 3


Continuation of the game released Atari in 2004.

In the new part there was a 3D engine. Significantly improved gameplay. More intelligent visitors (pips), an improved mechanism for building slides. By the way, the slides that you built in the first two parts can be imported into the third. The game has 18 scenarios and a sandbox. You can easily make sports predictions and watch their results.

The main improvement over the previous parts is a fully three-dimensional engine, which makes it possible not only to drive independently on the built attractions, but also just to wander through the park as an ordinary visitor.
