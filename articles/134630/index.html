Video review of entertaining iPad apps through the eyes of a simple parent

If an editorial fly-through or a regular field trip to a photo review, for example, is delayed (and the baby accompanies the mother everywhere) - the iPad becomes especially useful, it allows you to distract the faded child for a while. However, finding good applications that can take children from year to year is not so easy.
