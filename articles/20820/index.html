Comparison of popular video hosting sites

I bring to your attention a comparison of video hosting sites such as Google Video , YouTube , RuTube , Video.Mail.ru and the video service of the social network Vkontakte.ru (due to its unhealthy popularity).



Immediately make a reservation about some things. Comparison made by me, a couple of days ago. The criteria that participated in the comparison are important, first of all, for me. With you, I just share, in my opinion, interesting information.

For me, the criteria are important, first of all, the speed of video download, the speed of video processing and the maximum amount of video uploaded through the web interface (Google Video allows you to download unimaginable volumes through the software installed on your computer).

More secondary is the ability to download videos via a link from the network and the ability to download videos directly from the webcam.

I was also interested in the quality of the video, but it is so little different that, as a comparison criterion, it is untenable. However, the results (subjective), I also show.

Compared with an interval of 12 hours, three nights and 15 hours of the day. My internet provider Corbina Telecom gives me 6.6 MB speed both ways (incoming / outgoing), and she keeps her speed. Moscow city.
