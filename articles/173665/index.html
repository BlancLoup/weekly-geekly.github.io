Google Keep is available. Again

If you follow the news related to the search giant, you probably read about the short (and probably random) accessibility of the service for working with notes on St. Patrick's Day.



Now, Google Keep is officially submitted - an article on the Android blog .

In short, it is a synchronized service that includes:
  • Text notes
  • Lists
  • A photo
  • Audio notes
