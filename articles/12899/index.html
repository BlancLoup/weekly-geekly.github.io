Mangrove invests in Jamendo music portal

Jamendo (www.jamendo.com) announced it has received significant funds in the first round of financing from the investment fund Mangrove Capital Partners, known for its investments in Skype and Quintura .

Jamendo allows users to listen and download for free over 40,000 audio recordings without copy protection under a Creative Commons license. Already 3 million albums have been legally downloaded from Jamendo with monthly traffic of 500,000 unique visitors. Since January 2007, the service has been offering its performers a program for splitting advertising revenue, half of which goes to them.

It will also increase the cultural diversity of both music and user response.
