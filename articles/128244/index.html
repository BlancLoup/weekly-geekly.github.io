ScalaDay Spb - Scala Developers Conference

Having a decent experience in organizing meetings ADCSpb , we decided not to stop there and to involve as many developers from different areas as possible in the development of professional skills.

Our next educational project will be ScalaDay Spb - the first conference of Scala-developers in St. Petersburg.

October 1 at 16:00 we are waiting for all Scala fans in the Ingria business incubator. At the meeting, you will learn about the real experience of using this language and talk personally with representatives of companies from St. Petersburg and other cities that use Scala in their projects.
  • Pavel Fatin and Alexander Podhalyuzin (both from jetBrains ) will tell about the Scala plugin for IDEA.
  • Alexei Zlobin ( e-Legion ) will talk about the development of a scalable backend for the Goozy service using Scala and NoSQL.
  • Yuri Buyanov ( e-Legion ) will talk about some aspects of the use of Scala in real projects.
  • Eduard Klementyev (Iamscientist) will show how to use Scala for backend-tasks.
The list is updated. If you have something to tell - write in a personal.

As always, you can attend the meeting completely free of charge, you only need to register on the event page .
