icq2twitter.ru - ICQ-gateway for Twitter


Most recently, I had an idea - to make a Twitter gateway via icq.

Immediately the idea moved to the development stage. And the result was the icq2twitter.ru service, which I want to submit for your consideration.

Features

A feature of the service can be called the fact that the service works through the Twitter API , and at the same time uses OAuth authentication. This allows you to send and read posts via the messenger (any icq client), while not specifying your Twitter password. It is enough to add our application to the trust list on Twitter.
