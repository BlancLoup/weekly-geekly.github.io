Let's talk! DevExpress Hot Spring


While the whole country is playing April Fools' jokes, we want to share with you our serious news.

Very soon in Moscow there will be two major conferences for .NET developers, and there you will be able to meet with us and ask all your questions in real life!

Below you will find out where and when you can find us:



CLRium, 3-4 April, Moscow


clrium.ru

Already at the end of this week, the second CLRium seminar will take place in Moscow, where Pavel Avsenin from the DevExpress CodeRush team will speak from us on the Code Coverage in .NET .

Together with Paul, we learn:

  • what kind of code coverage is, how it counts and how the tools that consider it work;
  • write from scratch your simple tool that will be able to read code coverage by methods;
  • in the process of writing, we will get to know the Profiling API and the amazing things that it allows us to do with applications running under the CLR control;
  • let's touch the COM and ATL technologies necessary for working with the Profiling API;
  • rummage through the insides of the method's body, learn in detail how it looks, including the analysis of the header and the SEH tables;
  • learn how to replace the body of the method on the fly, during the execution of the program!

Come, it will be interesting! For example, among other things, the organizers promise a story from Andrei Akinshin, who in the process of preparing for the seminar actively creates and seeks to close Pull Requests directly in the CLR core repository, simultaneously running examples on Ubuntu Linux.

You can read more about the seminar program here , and register here .

Contact: sidristij


Go #, April 11, Moscow


www.gosharp.ru/DESKTOP2015

A week later, a major event for all developers of Desktop UI & Business Applications - Go # Moscow will take place in the new building of the Faculty of Economics of Moscow State University.

At GoSharp, you can meet two leading DevExpress developers:
  • Anton Abanin will talk about useful techniques and tools needed for the successful development of MVVM business applications on the WPF platform . The report will consider typical tasks arising in the development of business applications on the WPF platform, and offer effective reusable solutions to these problems, as embodied in the DevExpress Scaffolding Wizard, a rapid development tool for CRUD and LOB applications.
  • Dmitry Garavsky will share our experience in using the MVVM pattern for developing Windows Forms applications . The common problems of this pattern and the solutions we offer in our cross-platform MVVM framework will be considered. Emphasis will be placed on practical aspects and techniques in terms of using the WinForms platform and controls from DevExpress.

The reports will have a lot of practice, hardcore and all that, without which the life of a real .NET programmer is impossible!
