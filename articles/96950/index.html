Say a word about Chinese phones

On duty, now I can be aware of the latest works of Chinese engineers. I know that on this topic there has recently been a steady ever-increasing interest, I decided not to keep in myself what might be interesting and useful to others. Immediately, I note that I treat those who believe that interest in Chinese phones is growing deservedly. And because some of my conclusions may be slightly biased.

There are a lot of electronics manufacturers of various complexity in China (how does this country differ from, for example, Ukraine or even Russia). Therefore, it is not surprising that in China there are many popular brands of Chinese manufacturers, and they successfully compete with foreign ones. Again, this is strikingly different from the situation with countries like Ukraine and Russia - we only recognize all foreign, and manufacturers by hook or by crook tend to give out their products for non-China goods, while Consumers are happy to be deceived and believe it. At this time they are busy with important business: they protect each of their beloved with foaming at the mouth, but from this no less a foreign brand and actively stamp their feet on any sodding of Ukrainian production.

One way or another, and completely irrespective of what is happening here, in China there are a huge number of local brands. Although I do not know how fair it is to consider the brands of a country with a population of 1.34 billion people local. And the general population of the planet has never heard such names as Changhong, TCL, Hasee . I decided to gradually try to correct this situation and gradually talk about what is interesting in such a distant country.

Communication with the engineers of one of the largest developers for the production of mobile phones finally helped to decompose my knowledge on the shelves and I came to the conclusion that all of the manufacturers of mobile phones from China can be divided into 4 groups:
1. Brands that orient their activities to domestic consumption. Most of them grew out of state-owned enterprises, founded in the 50-80s and therefore have an impressive history, which they are proud of. Products have a high level of quality (not lower than that of global manufacturers), development departments have impressive budgets. Originality is also to be found here. Products (unfortunately) are mostly unknown outside the country - manufacturers simply have enough for a comfortable existence of the domestic market. Although recently, due to favorable circumstances, these manufacturers have been slowly exploring access to other countries.
