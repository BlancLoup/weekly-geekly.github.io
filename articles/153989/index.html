Sophiecam







In Nokia, Symbian and MeeGo smartphones do not have a full-fledged Instagram application, although they say it will appear for WP8. However, programs that are similar in functionality, that is, allowing one touch to process a photo, are mass. Personally, I use SophieCam because it does not have any web service, and you can post the processed photo on Facebook or Twitter. With regards to filters, there are both bad and good ones, like on Instagram itself. Photos can be used as already taken with the help of a normal camera (they will have to be cropped under the square), and you can use the camera of the SophieCam application itself. The program creates your album in the photos of your Facebook account, however you can change the location of the captured photo during the sending process. In general, a very good program. True, paid. It costs 35 rubles. These are the photos I took with her.


QNeptunea




Even somehow, the hand does not rise to call this application a twitter client. This is more of a twitter combine, because I really haven‚Äôt met any of the operating systems for any of the clients for Twitter!



In QNeptunea, you can not just view the Twi-tape, reply to messages, retweet them or open links in the browser. The settings of the program itself are also striking in their advancement. You can change the font size in twi-tape, increase user avatars, assign certain color schemes and themes to display the program, choose its language, etc.



The program is paid and not cheap. In ovi.store it costs 350 rubles. But for those who actively use Twitter, it is definitely worth it! The author of the application, - Japanese Tasuku Suzuki, - it will not be superfluous to know the names of the heroes!

Nokia City Lens




Few people know that this city map service recently presented for Windows Phone is also available for MeeGo and Symbian. There is no application in ovi.store, but you can download it from the Nokia BetaLabs website . The application works quite correctly, and the degree of its convenience when navigating the terrain, when you need to find a specific institution or office of a company, is difficult to overestimate. By the way, the companies themselves can put themselves on the map for display in Nokia City Lens, - so, I think, after the official release of the application on Lumia smartphones, this map service will become very popular. And the owners of the N9 will not be deprived.

Blob




Blob is a client for posting to Blogger and Wordpress blog hosting sites. The functionality is absolutely full. You can embed in the post photos and video links. There is an integration with Picasa. All text formatting options are supported. You can make tags, manage the time and date of publication. You can save drafts and continue to work in them later. Moreover, it is possible to work in this application offline at all.

In a word, an absolutely indispensable application for bloggers, which is especially nice, is free and can be installed through a regular application store.







Meedocs





Those who use Google‚Äôs online document service will surely love this app. Thanks to him, you can at any time quickly and correctly find, read, send or download your online document. If your
materials are arranged in folders, then you can also open these folders, and even be able to create new ones, in which you suppose you need to download new text. In addition, you can also create a document on your smartphone, and then upload it to Google documents. It is also possible to share documents on Facebook or Twitter, or send a link to a particular document via email.



In short, a fully-featured application that makes life easier for users of the popular and convenient Google service.

gNewsReader





Application to another mega-popular service Googe, - RSS feed reader. The application is advanced and easy to use. Messages can be read both in the application itself, and open their full version in a browser window. You can also copy the link to the message page for its further insertion anywhere else, you can shuffle the link on Facebook, Twitter, Getpocket, Instapaper, and perform all the supposed operations of GReader with the message: tag, set +, share in G +, etc. .



In short, a very convenient application for those who use the Google RSS subscription service and at the same time is lazy to transfer all subscriptions to a regular RSS reader pre-installed in MeeGo Harmattan.



The application is free, after closing Google Reader supports the Feedly protocol.

Fbreader





This reader of books in the FB2 format, I think, is known to all. So, perhaps it was not worth writing about her. Nevertheless, I decided to mention it in my review, because it is one of the applications I use most often.



The application supports opening files from the phone‚Äôs memory, contains integration with electronic online libraries. In the settings you can adjust the font size. Naturally there is support for horizontal scanning, as well as night mode, when the text is displayed on a black background, which, by the way, saves battery power. In a word, if you read books, then this is FBreader. If the book you want to read is in a different reader format, then there are enough resources online that carry out a completely correct conversion from another format to FB2.

Recorder





A simple but quite functional voice recorder that allows you to record audio tracks, pause recording and continue it later in the same file. All recordings are stored in a separate tab, where you can listen to them, delete them or share them in Dropbox, send them by mail or to another device via Bluetooth or NFC.

File manager





For MeeGo Harmattan, there are several file managers, but as a result I stopped at this. In many ways, because its interface corresponds to the style that is gradually crystallized from third-party applications for N9. But the rest, its functionality is consistent with the name. The application allows you to perform all necessary operations with files, up to sending them to Dropbox, by mail via bluetooth or NFC. There is support for two colors, black and white. Naturally, supported and horizontal display mode.



If your N9 is in developer mode, the application allows you to see the entire file system. You can also customize and display hidden files. If your device is in a ‚Äúboxed‚Äù version, then the file manager will display only that part of the file system, access to which you may need as an ordinary user.

Dropian





This application is a file manager for the Dropbox file storage. The application allows you to upload and download files, supports displaying thumbnails of photos stored in your file storage. Directly from the application, you can create folders on Dropbox. In addition, there is a built-in text editor with which you can create text files that will immediately be saved to Dropbox. Also, of course, the application allows you to receive links to files and folders located in the file storage.



In short, a full-fledged application for the most popular, and probably the best file storage. By the way, several clients are available for Dropbox on the N9, but through natural selection, I still chose this.

Skyfiles





If we are talking about file managers, here‚Äôs another one - this time for the SkyDrive file storage. All the same functionality, with the exception of a text editor. There are free and paid versions, I recommend to choose the paid one, since the free version, among other things, does not save the login password, which is very unpleasant.

TranslateAll





Translator based on Google Translate. It only supports vertical display mode, which is not very convenient, but for some reason I haven‚Äôt changed it to another, although there are several similar translators in ovi.store.

MeePaper





Getpocket service is becoming increasingly popular. Its integration into QNeptunea, as well as the fact that I personally actively use it in Chrome, prompted me to install the application client of this service for MeeGo. Everything is very simple and convenient here: you synchronize the application with the service and all those links and notes that you made, whether from a browser or from a QNeptunea twitter client, appear in it. Links can be read, deleted, shared, opened in the browser or simply copied to the clipboard.



PureLight





This is the simplest flashlight, but I could not mention it, because I have never met a simpler and more universal solution. In all other cases, you need to open the program, and only then turn on the flashlight. Here the button of the flashlight is located right on your desktop, among the shortcuts of other programs. And you just need to click on it and the flash will turn on in flashlight mode, and the shortcut will also shine, indicating that the flashlight is on. You can turn off the flashlight only after 5 seconds after switching on. This restriction is apparently due to the need to curb those who want to play with such a convenient button.

VideoEditor





This simple but functional video editor will allow you to merge several videos you shot on N9 into one file. Each of the videos can be cut at the beginning and at the end (however, such functionality is also available in the Gallery interface). The scroll bar is scaled by multi-touch gestures, so the installation process does not cause any trouble. Expel mounted film can be with different resolutions, the maximum of which 720r. This application does not contain any special transitions from roller to roller, so when connecting, the simplest gluing of one roller to another is used.



Vimeo





By name, it is clear that this is a client of the video hosting of the same name, different from the omnipresent YouTube, which is more sophisticated and artistic video content. The client, admittedly, is great, and supports almost all the functionality of video hosting. A similar Android client, for example, is much leaner and weaker. The client has its own built-in player, which is no worse than a video player built into MeeGo.



With this client, you can not only view your and other people's video content on Vimeo and comment on it, but also upload video files to a video hosting site.



cuteTube





There are several YouTube customers for MeeGo, but cuteTube is clearly the best. Firstly, because it supports the Notification Screen and new videos from your subscriptions appear on the Notification Screen, among other posts from social networks and RSS subscriptions.



Secondly, cuteTube refers to the new generation of applications for MeeGo, which includes all the same QNeptunea, for example, that is, it is not just an application, but a full-featured combine that includes functionality that can be useful to the user, but is not directly implemented even in browser version of a service.



With this application you can fully monitor not only the videos on your YouTube-account, but also in your account on Dailymotion, as well as on Facebook and Twitter. You can comment on videos, respond to other comments, download videos, share them on the social network, add to favorites, playlist for later viewing, etc. Also the application, like the browser version of Youtube, shows similar videos.



The program has advanced interface settings and functionality. You can choose a color scheme, you can set the resolution settings of the downloaded file, search options, etc.



In short, an example to follow.







The program is not free, it costs 140 rubles. But it is obviously worth it, if you use YouTube and generally like to watch network video.

Musikloud





The client for the most popular music resource SoundCloud from the same manufacturer as the previous application. Englishman Stuart Hovart - I think it will not be superfluous to remember this name.



The application is built on the same principle as cuteTube and provides absolutely full functionality of the SoundCloud service. You can listen and download tracks, leave comments to them, view user profiles. So, wandering from one user to another and listening to track by track, in this application you can spend a single hour.







The application is paid. It is worth 105 rubles.

FM Radio





Simple but functional radio. 6 radio stations you can save in memory and switch between them with one click. Navigating the frequency scale is a simple scrolling.

Flickrup





The client is the most popular photo hosting on the Internet. The application provides full functionality for navigating the resource. You can view your friends' updates. When you open a separate image in the interface, scaling is supported through multi-touch gestures. Through the application, you can upload your own photos to the photo hosting site, although there is no need to do this through the interface of this application, since the Flickr protocol is sewn from the box in MeeGo, so you can upload photos to this photo resource directly from the Gallery.



But here's the functionality of downloading images from Flickr in the FlickrUp application frankly pleases - you can choose the size and resolution of the downloaded image.







Naturally, the application also supports horizontal scanning.



Shotmee





Beautiful in its simplicity application for taking screenshots. You simply launch this application, transfer it to the background mode, and then tap on the body of the smartphone, when the display shows the interface from which you want to take a screenshot. Files are saved with the extension .png and in the resolution of 480x854.

Bambuser





Online video client service. Through this application, you can shoot a video and broadcast it directly to your account on bambuser.com as you are shooting. Further, with certain settings that need to be set up in your account, video broadcasting can automatically stream into your accounts in social networks. Naturally, this online broadcasting is possible with a wi-fi or 3G internet connection. If there is none, then the video recorded by you will wait for the connection in the program specially reserved for it. In the settings of the application, you can also set the resolution of the video broadcast, which you think is optimal.

Mp3 cut





Audio editor that allows you to cut out separate fragments from mp3 files and save them again in mp3 format. In principle, a convenient program, when you suppose you need to cut a small excerpt from a dictaphone recording in order not to store a huge interview file on your smartphone.

Mixxmachine





Music sampler that allows you to write electronic music. For me personally, as a person who does not like computer games, this is the best option for a toy. Sometimes it is very pleasant to sit and poke in such a program making fancy rhythms and stringing musical phrases on them. The program contains a good database of all kinds of sounds for the smartphone version.



The program also announced the ability to record the resulting track in an mp3 file, but this feature is not currently implemented. It will be available in future versions of the program. However, if it turned out really something masterpiece, then you can still record it, you just need to turn on the speaker louder, and in the background to put on the recorder.

Instago





Unofficial Instagram client for MeeGo. Its informality affects the functionality because the Instagram API is closed. Nevertheless, in this application, you can view the tape of your friends, put likes, view your photos, as well as the most popular photos of the entire instagram.



In principle, it is an acceptable replacement for a popular service. Although, it is hoped that over time, thanks to the appearance of an unofficial client, and maybe on its basis, an official one will appear.



Sharetus





Free and absolutely necessary application that expands the sharing functionality in MeeGo. The application is installed in the system and appears only when you want to transfer a particular link, for example, on a social network, or send it by mail. The application does not work in the case of sharing of photos, it works only with text, and with links, however, the fact that it can be used to send the entire browser page to transfer to the Google Translator is already pleasing. Support for other resources that are not supported by the boxed version of MeeGo is also very enjoyable. Separately, the application allows tagging messages and links sent.

Titanim





Client for Vkontakte social network. For MeeGo Harmattan, there are several Vkontakte clients, there is an official one, but TitanIM, having undergone natural selection, became the main thing for me.There are both paid and free versions. Paid it seems to support video viewing, and in free it is only available for listening to music. The rest of the functionality: chats, walls, posting, comments, etc., is also available in the free version.


It is also nice that the application supports the Notification screen and status updates of your friends appear on it, and from there they open directly in the TitanIM application.



Internet radio





Client for listening to Internet radio stations with a built-in equalizer, as well as with a database of radio stations, broken down by music genre. Also supported the ability to add to the list of radio stations.



You can add individual radio stations to your favorites list, as well as view those radio stations that you listened to recently. In a word, nothing supernatural, but on the other hand, it is quite a high-quality client for those who like to listen to radio via wi-fi.



However, I still prefer to use the MusiKloud application for these purposes.

Codecam





An application for reading all kinds of barcode and QR codes. It is noteworthy that it is not necessary to make money on any buttons. You simply open the application and point to the code. The program itself recognizes it and scans. If necessary, you can turn on the flash in flashlight mode. Having scanned the code, the program suggests you to follow a coded link.

Shakelock





Convenient program that allows you to customize the lock screen of your smartphone by shaking it. In its concept is very consistent with the MeeGo besknopochnuyu interface. Personally, I use it with great pleasure.

Call recorder





For some reason, it is considered that recording telephone conversations is sinful, if not dangerous. But on the other hand, perhaps it depends on who is talking about what. For me personally, a program that automatically records all conversations is a convenient way not to write down the address or not to ask again twenty times some exact information that you will have to use later. It is always possible to listen to the recording later.

Notes exporter





This is not so much a program as a plugin to the Notes application preinstalled in MeeGo. The plugin allows you to save notes in a text file and, accordingly, send them by mail. A very convenient and simple program, although, of course, I would like the standard application of notes to have such functionality, and even better, it would allow you to synchronize content through one of the cloud services. Although when synchronization is very important to me, I prefer to use the above-described Dropian application.
