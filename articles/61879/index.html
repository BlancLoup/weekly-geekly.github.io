Meeting of the System Administrators 2009. PING!



From July 31 to August 2, 2009 in the Kaluga region near the village of Kolyupanovo (coordinates: 54Àö 25 14.55Àù N, 36Àö, 13, 5.65Àù E) the Fourth All-Russian System Administrators Meeting will take place. According to preliminary estimates, more than 3000 representatives of this profession will fly to the event from all over Russia and nearby countries. The information sponsor of the event is traditionally Habrahabr .

The All-Russian Meeting is dedicated to the International Day of the system administrator and is held annually on the last weekend of July. This is a great opportunity to gather in tents in nature and talk with colleagues, as well as participate in sports tournaments and traditional professional contests: throwing mice at accuracy, keyboards at a distance, laying a net in field conditions, a competition for the best stuffed "lamer". The winners are given souvenirs and prizes from the organizers and partners of the Meeting.

Participation is free, preliminary registration has begun on the newly opened official website . There is also a program of events, a location map, answers to organizational questions.
