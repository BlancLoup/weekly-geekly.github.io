According to rumors, Google may submit a Nexus smart phone worth $ 100.

I read the article "It is likely that Google will stop producing technology under the brand Nexus" and was upset.

After all, recently there have been rumors that Google, in collaboration with MediaTek, can present a budget smartphone of the Nexus line with a price of just $ 100. Thanks to MediaTek's cheap quad-core processors, many Chinese manufacturers have already released Android devices, with an approximate cost of $ 130. For example, the Xiaomi Hongmi smartphone, also known as Red Rice, has a MediaTek MT-6589 quad-core processor. The cost of production of the device for the company is $ 85, while its cost in China does not exceed $ 130.

Considering the pricing policy of Google, the release of a $ 100 smartphone looks very real. It is reported that the development of the budget model will not affect the announcement of the flagship Nexus 6.

Couldn't the old dream come true to have a cheap Nexus?
