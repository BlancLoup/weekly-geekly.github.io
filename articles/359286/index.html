Closed Q & A sessions with Giorgio Sardo

Hi, Habr! As you know, a couple of weeks ago we had one of the most significant Microsoft conferences for developers - Build. But that is not all. On June 4, the Insider Dev Tour arrives in Moscow for the first time, as part of which MskDotNet Community and AVRA will hold closed Q & A thematic sessions. Login will be only by invite. A couple of days ago, participants in the Windows Pre-Evaluation program received an invitation to the first Insider Dev Tour conference to be held in Moscow. We also decided to keep up with and share with you the details!

Insider Dev Tour: what is it?


The international conference Insider Dev Tour arrives on June 4 in Moscow. It is focused on developers who are engaged in the creation of intelligent interfaces based on the latest Microsoft technologies, as well as on all who want to look into the future. If you can read the code, then it doesn‚Äôt matter whether you are a beginner, an experienced expert, a student or just a keen enthusiast - you will be interested!

Speakers:
  • Giorgio Sardo is the senior director of the Partner App Development group at Microsoft.

  • Pete Brown (Pete Brown) - Principal Program Manager Windows at Microsoft Corporation, definitely worth talking to him if you want to develop an application or technology for musicians.
  • Killian McCoy - Program Manager in the Partner App Development Team at Microsoft.

  • Justin Liu - Senior Manager in the Partner App Development team at Microsoft.

  • Jeremiah Marble (Jeremiah Marble) - Director of Marketing at Windows Insider Program, as well as a creative developer at Microsoft.

  • Alexander Lozhechkin (Alexander Lozhechkin) - Head of Microsoft Strategic Technologies in Central and Eastern Europe, a member of the board of directors of the company.

In one day you:
  • Get a general understanding of the new tools for developers under Windows, Azure, Office, AI and so on.
  • Go deep into coding and learn how to create Win32 / XAML applications and Progressive Web Apps, as well as use new Windows 10 features. You will learn how to add AI elements to your projects, how to work with Microsoft Graph, and how to create Mixed Reality applications.

You can find the program and details about the event on the event website . Please note that the entire program will be in English, but you will be able to use simultaneous translation into Russian.
