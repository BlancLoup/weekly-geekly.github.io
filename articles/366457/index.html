April 9 FriendFeed will be closed

All site content will disappear in four weeks.


The official blog FriendFeed published a post , which says that the service will cease to exist April 9, 2015. The reason for this is simple - almost no one uses the site. Since the post was published (March 9), registration has been closed on the site. While users can view their messages, correspondence and photos, but after April 9, the site will become unavailable.

Five years ago, Facebook was bought by FriendFeed, and since then the number of users has gradually decreased. The size of the community and its activity have decreased only to a part of the past, and the validity of the continued functioning of the site has disappeared.
