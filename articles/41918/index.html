Energetic USB gadget - do it like a dog!


Presented USB gadget in the form of a dog, probably the most energetic of all USB gadgets. When connected to a computer, the dog starts rhythmic movements in the direction of the USB port. The dog continues its actions until you remove the object of its desires from it.

If the child asks you what the little dog does, just say "She is trying to jump over the computer, but she is a little stuck ..."

Such a gadget can be a great gift, and can entertain not only the owner, but also his friends and acquaintances.

It looks especially impressive when connected to a laptop.
