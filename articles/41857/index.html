Digital Lunches at Inamo London



Dear London restaurant and bar Inamo is going to spice up the pleasure of excellent cuisine with a digital peppercorn: Microsoft Surface touch-panels installed on worktops. Color interactive menus, aesthetic patterns, smack of innovation - all this, according to Inamo managers, should appeal to the fastidious English public.

When viewing the menu items, you can even look at the dish as if it were lying on a plate in front of you (only slightly more than two-dimensional).
