E-tune-up from gray merchants

According to the observations, there is a rather noticeable group of buyers, formed even before the stagnation of the economy, who are dissatisfied with the quality of work of our online stores (Belarus; a typical and store here is something like that ) and agree on a difference of 10-15% prices subject to "white" goods, normal warranty and well-functioning delivery system.
Suppose someone (and even if I - why not) creates an i-shop that meets these characteristics. But how, taking into account that the absolute majority of users come from the search and do the viewing of a product / store no more than a second before making an impression - how, by what means, have time to convey to them the difference between your store and the "others"?
The first moves, in principle, have already been found: the delivery time is indicated for each item (1 day, 2 days, 3-5 days). This demonstrates that the store at least has real information about its warehouse logistics. The dialogue with the seller-consultant, initiated by the seller, should also work well. But these are just a couple of ideas ... how else can you immediately show the visitor that this store is working for him?
