"Cryptocurrency" is rapidly becoming cheaper



Despite the fact that one time for one Bitcoin was given $ 33, now this online currency is falling in price, and its fall is happening at a rather rapid pace. Currently, for 1 Bitcoin give 2.8 US dollars, but, probably, this rate will hold for a short time. Already, mining becomes not very profitable, and if the rate of virtual currency in relation to real will continue to fall, then the miners will stop working altogether.

Some of the users of the system are confident that already this week the course will be 1: 1, in which case mining will certainly be a disadvantage. Bitcoin users are even planning to popularize cryptocurrency, in order to attract "fresh blood" to the system. Perhaps, in this case, the situation will be able to improve a little. On the other hand, how will new users help the system? Mining? Not so many people are engaged in this, and beginners may simply not want to understand all the technical details of cryptocurrency generation.

With the fall of Bitcoin, some of the resources involved in currency exchange and conducting operations with Bitcoin of a different kind began to be sold. In particular, Forbitcoin ( www.forbitcoin.com ) and FreeBitcoins.org ( www.freebitcoins.org ) are sold. Many users of the system began to gradually realize that the high rate of this online currency was due solely to speculative operations, and not the real price of virtual money.

As mentioned above, the price of one bitcoin somehow reached $ 33 per unit, but the price of $ 3 per unit can be considered quite normal. Some users even thought about whether this virtual currency is needed. After all, now what you can buy for Bitcoin can be purchased for regular dollars.
