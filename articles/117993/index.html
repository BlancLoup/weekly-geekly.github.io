LinguaLeo update: more games in learning English!


Virtual games occupy an increasingly important place in the life of modern people. The founder of SCVNGR says that games everywhere will become the next phenomenon that will affect life in the same way as the social revolution on the Internet did. We want to make mastering foreign languages ‚Äã‚Äãan effective and exciting game. Today released a new release LinguaLeo.ru . The long-awaited update has affected most of the service, and most importantly - it has become more game dynamics, which are so fond of users. Learning foreign languages ‚Äã‚Äãshould be entertaining, and you will not be bored! Playing English, earn experience points, complete quests, quests, get game ranks and increase the level of development of your pets.



Quest quests and hints



Quests - an important game component, immersing you in the atmosphere of the jungle. From the first day on the service, you can (optionally) complete quest tasks that tell about LinguaLeo's capabilities and lead the right course to success in knowing English. Your path is accompanied by dynamic hints from Leo, who consistently explains the service interface and learning methods.

Experience monitor



Why do we need experience? Experience shows the progress of knowledge and skills. It is charged for various useful actions. For example, adding a new word gives you +1 experience point, and mastering the page already gives +10. Completing quests and training is also rewarded.

Very soon you will be able to share your successes with friends and measure your progress with other users of the service.

Game titles and Leo level


The more experience you accumulate, the higher the level of development of your pet. For every 5th level passed, Leo receives game titles (for example, "Jungle Ranger"). Some titles include exclusive features within the service.

Thematic Glossaries



Each of us has our own interests, and this is great! Someone likes sports, some cars; some like to travel or do business. Therefore, we have added a separate section of thematic glossaries so that you can easily learn words in areas of interest to you. You can also learn thematic materials in the Jungle, so that the study of words takes place in the context of a living language.

Now there are about 50 glossaries in the catalog, divided into 9 groups: science and technology, education and work, economics and business, and others. Among the glossaries: IT and programming, cycling, journalism, cinema and theater, tourism and others.

You can also suggest adding a new word to any existing glossary or suggest creating a new glossary.

New profile with Leo badge



We have expanded the personal pages of users. You can specify additional data: gender, date of birth, place of residence and links to your Facebook, vKontakte, Twitter and Skype.
