How to pump the brain

In this topic, I want to share my knowledge about the device of the brain and how I apply it in practice.



So, as you know, the brain is the most important organ of our body. Someone says that he is the most difficult thing in the universe. And ignorance of some of its features can be very pitiable for a person‚Äôs life.

Understanding the role of the brain


There is no ignorance worse than ignorance about yourself.

The brain controls almost everything in our body - from breathing, the work of the senses to complex thought processes and imagination. There are many methods and development tips, however, as I have seen, few emphasize the role of the brain in the process.

The brain is a well-studied organ, and, having knowledge of the brain, one can understand how certain techniques affect the brain and whether to use them. In addition, you can develop your own approaches based not on theoretical reasoning, but on scientific knowledge.

Nutrition


The brain is an extremely energy-demanding organ. Therefore, food should be uniform throughout the day, and include very special substances. There are many of them and they are different, but on the whole, proteins, complex carbohydrates, and omega-three fats are recommended. For example, my breakfast usually consists of apple and scrambled eggs (sometimes from pieces of fish), lunch - from vegetables / fruits and meat, dinner - also from vegetables and meat. I also drink vitamins every day, drink plenty of water (about 2-3 liters) and 2-4 cups of green tea.

Here it must be said that the power and work of the brain are directly related. If you deal with other things while eating, the food will not be absorbed, since for full digestion you need to perceive all the senses, and allow the brain to focus on this complex process.

Also, if you eat a lot at once, the brain will not receive energy properly and run smoothly. You can compare with fire - or you keep the fire uniform, throwing a little firewood over a certain amount of time; or wait until everything burns down, then again throw a heap over the coals and rekindle the fire again, wasting too much time and having an unevenly distributed flame.

In addition, as I will write later, a healthy diet makes it easy to perform physical activities, and it is very useful for the brain.

Therefore, food should be taken very carefully - it is necessary that the volume of food at a time is small, the food itself should be filled with energy evenly throughout the day.

Environment


First of all, air. In addition to food, which we deal with every day (remember the Parerto rule, that 20% of the causes give 80% of the result?), It is very important that the air we breathe is full of oxygen. Therefore, it is necessary to ventilate the room, if possible, to sleep with the window open, and periodically do gymnastics with deep breathing.

Secondly, the order. Oddly enough, it is the order outside that allows you to put things in order inside. In this order, I call this state of the surrounding objects and people when the situation is not annoying. Sometimes, however, aggression is hidden - let's say, the presence of order is determined by whether you want to be in a certain situation or not.

Further, this is the number of distracting and annoying factors, and the number of relaxing and inspiring. They each have their own, but usually the first group includes extra noise, unpleasant odors, uncomfortable furniture, randomly arranged objects in the field of view, too bright or dim light, too bright or faded colors around, and so on. It should be noted that the brain is able to adapt and ignore them to constantly available factors - like noise from a window. And for others, such as repairs beyond the wall, it is very difficult for him to adapt.
And as for the inspiring factors - they each have their own.
In general, you need to keep this in mind and strive to create such an environment around yourself that it was easy to concentrate

Sleep


Sleep is one of the most important factors for healthy brain function. The following points can be highlighted:
  • Sleep should be enough. On average, it is 7-8 hours of sleep without waking up
  • Sleeping should be such that you can fall asleep after 15 minutes in bed.
  • Sleep should be in a decent setting. Fresh air, darkness, silence is desirable.
  • Sleep should coincide with the internal clock of the person (usually starts before 12 at night and ends at 6-10 am).


I try to sleep for 8-10 hours and get up early in the morning.

Physical exercise


They say harmony is the lack of imbalance. In this case, talking about the fact that for the brain the best rest is a change of activity, for example, a change of mental activity to physical. I have a setting in Ubuntu that blocks the computer for ten minutes after every fifty, and I have to go and take a walk. (the ratio in the region of 10-50 is established by science and recommended for those working at a computer). Every evening I try to take a walk in the fresh air for at least half an hour, or take a bike ride (1-1.5 hours).
I also like push-ups in between activities, work with dumbbells.

In addition, exercise during the day plays the following roles. First, as a means to maintain the body in an active state - physical activity increases the temperature of the body, and the brain works better. Secondly, it relieves psychological stress - it is known that muffled emotions turn into clips in the body, physical loads and sports remove it along with stress, and so on. Thirdly, regular exercise leads to the production of endorphins - the hormones of joy and happiness - and thus the person engaged during the day improves his mood. Therefore, by the way, charging so invigorates well - accelerates blood, as they say.

A lot more there. For example, switching and going for a walk, you give the task to your subconscious, which will solve it better than you. In addition, regular exercise keeps the body in good shape and allows you to look as slim as you decide.

Thus, a regular combination of the usual activities for the computer with different physical culture dramatically increases the efficiency of work and life in general.

On my own - skating regularly for at least 5 hours a week, doing different physical activities, and quitting drinking alcohol in general, I was sick for only six weeks during these six months. Against a month at least earlier.

Training


Everyone probably read the articles when long-livers are less likely to lose their mind if they work and are engaged in intellectual work. And this is true, and at any age - if the brain is not developed, then it begins to grow stale, figuratively speaking. The brain is our most important talent, and we must be sensitive to it.

For a good state of his brain, it is advisable to regularly teach any new activities in various fields, mental and physical. In this case, over a long period - from a month to a year. A very good influence on the brain: the development of new complex physical actions (dance, martial art, a new sport), music, the development of new languages.
