Apoi.ru application for Android

We are pleased to present for discussion an updated version of the application Apoi.ru for Android .

The clear advantage of this application is not only the ability to search for interesting places based on the user's location, but also the unique convergence of the application and the resource of the same name www.apoi.ru

What is the convergence? In a simple and logical system for transferring data from the pages of the site to the mobile application. We did not bother with the use of ultra-modern methods of data transmission via Bluetooth, recognition of the device by the NFC chip and other tricky technologies. We simply placed QR codes on our website. And the information encrypted in each of them is quite enough to determine the resource and the page being viewed. The application has a built-in system for reading such codes, which allows you to instantly display the information presented on the website on the device screen. As one of our users wrote: ‚ÄúImmediately I remembered the Avatar movie :-) (plot in the laboratory). Understand why we need these strange pictures with dots. Well done! Very comfortably!"

Naturally, QR code can be read not only from our application, but also with the help of dozens of others. In this case, the page from which the code is read will open in a normal, mobile browser.

What else is convenient in our application is to leave feedback. Suppose you came to a restaurant, ordered a perfectly smelling and perfectly roasted steak or any other edible food. And suddenly you find that there is an extra ingredient in the side dish, for example a piece of colored plastic, a waiter's tooth, a dragon's eye, or other troubles. All that you need to do in order to warn the rest of the visitors of this institution, it is emotionally convey your impressions of the event. How can I do that? It's very simple: in the application, you can leave not only written reviews, typing them using the software keyboard, but also dictating a review with instant text display (the function is protected in the keyboard from Google). Or just record your emotional experiences in a sound file, make a confirming photo and send feedback to the server. As a result, the site will contain not only the text of the review, a photo, but also a sound file with sincere feelings that accompanied you at that moment.
