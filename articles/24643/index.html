Daily portion of knowledge. I want more.

Please tolerate and read to the end. Maybe this is not quite a suitable blog, and in general it would be better to create a separate one (which is not enough karma yet).

I work as a project manager in a company that makes websites. I work with my head: I design, follow work and communicate with clients.

I need to be aware of the possibilities of modern technologies and trends in order to know what can be done, what is impossible, what is good, what is bad. Therefore, I get a portion of knowledge every day. Many of the sources we have with you, the reader, of course coincide. But, of course, someone will find something new here. These are the most authoritative, strong sources. I give a guarantee that if you are not familiar with them, having become acquainted you will grow into several levels. From them, second-rate streams, pupils, already diverge. So, sources (the order means nothing):

www.artlebedev.ru (http://www.artlebedev.ru/kovodstvo) - you know yourself

www.useit.com - usability guru shares some knowledge

webdesignfromscratch.com - British developer and designer tells how to make practical, user-friendly sites that work

www.habrahabr.ru is a site where there is a lot of everything, and in order to catch what you need for yourself, you have to sift a lot of information

blog.mann-ivanov-ferber.ru - blog of business literature publishers. Not the web, but very interesting and useful information (like their books) for business. Podcasts regularly appear with excerpts from books, or, for example, interviews with Igor Ashmanov and Gleb Arkhangelsky (author of the most influential Russian book on time management, Time Drive).

websitesthatsuck.com - we learn good design by looking for errors. The site is very useful for a one-time reading (especially Does Your Web Site Suck ). Then you can read a few notes about sucking sites, and you will clearly see what you can not do. Strong thing, thoroughly brainwashed.

These sources are united by the fact that they provide food for the mind, generalized ideas.

What is this topic for:
Those who did not know something, rejoice, go to the sites and learn. And those who know everything, I want to ask: what other good sources do you know from which you can learn? After all, I want more, more thoughts. And newer.

By the way, you can tell not only about regularly updated sites.

And please write comments to the resources. Thank.
