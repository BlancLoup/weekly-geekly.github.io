Stir on Flickr

Firstly, all old users (who were registered on Flickr before the arrival of Yahoo!) will have to get a Yahoo! ID until March 15.
Secondly, a limit of 3,000 contacts per user will now appear.
Thirdly, there will be a limit on the number of tags that can be hung on one photo. Now their maximum number is 75.

Service regulars outraged. Some believe that this will worsen Flickr qualitatively.

There is an opinion that the limit on the number of tags and contacts is made in order to prevent spammers from the service.

And yet, it is believed that the whole scandal was inflated by people from Zoomr - a direct competitor to Flickr.
