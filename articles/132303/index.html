Google stops supporting Gmail for BlackBerry


Google is going to stop supporting the Gmail application for the BlackBerry platform from November 22.

"This year we have focused on building Gmail for mobile browsers, and we are going to continue working in this direction further," the report says.
Users who have already installed the application will be able to continue using it, however, it will not be supported and will not be available for download.
However, last week Google released the Gmail application for the iPhone / iPad, but almost immediately removed it from the AppStore due to several errors found in it.
