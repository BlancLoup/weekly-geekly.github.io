About ImageCMS release 3.4.7.84 and news

Helmet hello from the forge ImageCMS!
For a whole month we worked in a fairly saturated mode, so this publication is dedicated to the latest news. We consider the source of inspiration tangible support from the community and customers (guys, we work for you). So, velvet August was completely spent in the next release of the system - ImageCMS 3.4.7.84 - ImageCMS and ImageCMS Shop.

The main assets are:


  • added site integration in social. Vkontakte and Facebook networks;
  • launched the possibility of placing the map of the XML online store on ImageCMS;
  • updated functionality: now it is possible to monitor the price of the goods and receive notifications about its changes;
  • entered the English version of the template;
  • made caching support for memcached;
  • developed the ability to launch a mobile version of the site;
  • the ability to add goods in different currencies;
  • developed a subscription module for news, promotions, discounts;
  • updated the way products are displayed - now this is done on the paid version of Fancybox;
  • in the administrative panel they made a conclusion about the registered users and added the export to mailchimp;
  • optimized the speed of work with a large number of products in one category;
  • improved the filter and the generator of additional fields of the store.


As before, we actively recycle bugs. In this release, this concerns errors related to:


  • properties of goods;
  • multilingual administrative panel;
  • login and password during installation;
  • the withdrawal of the old price;
  • user registration;
  • transition after saving the goods;
  • the imposition of a watermark;
  • comparison list;
  • module share - the generator of social widgets;
  • comments;
  • mapping sets of goods.
