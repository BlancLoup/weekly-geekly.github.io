Three HD webcams from Microsoft

Microsoft has not only released a lot of new software products and tools over the past few days, but also a number of interesting web-cameras. Three models: LifeCam HD-5000, LifeCam HD-5001, LifeCam HD-6000 (according to the order in the figure) have autofocus and the ability to record HD video at 720p. The first two models are identical and differ only in design (the second option is exclusive for Best Buy), the third camera has the ability to rotate the lens 360 degrees.

The price of HD-5000 and HD-5001 cameras is only $ 49.95, the LifeCam HD-6000 is $ 59.95. This is probably the lowest price for this kind of device.



In addition, the cameras for the first time used a new development by Microsoft - TrueColor, which, according to the developers, can significantly improve the quality of video in a number of the most frequent cases of using such cameras.
