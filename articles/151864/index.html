Express office at BitByte 2012

The Express Office will take part in the event held by itmozg - the BitByte fair. For us, this is a good way to tell the IT community about its existence and the advantages of such an office format. At the same time, for visitors to the event, this is a great chance to get a 10% discount on services for renting workplaces and meeting rooms in October of this year.

Moreover, this discount will apply not only to one-time visits, but also to long-term contracts for these services, which will be concluded before the end of October 2012!



Well, a few news from us in pursuit.

First of all, we are very happy to announce that there was a startup who decided to take advantage of our offer of a two-month free use of workplaces at the Express Office. Unfortunately, we cannot yet name it and tell about the essence of the project, but we hope that our modest contribution will help to give birth to a new strong business. If everything goes well, then in October we will be able to reveal the cards and jointly open the idea to the general public.

By the way, it is twice pleasant that Habr helped this team to see our offer!

Secondly, despite the end of the huge summer discounts, we decided not to chop off the shoulder and, despite the general autumn price increase, set the cost of the Express Office services lower than planned. Renting a workplace costs only 400 rubles per day , a large negotiation is 300 rubles per hour , and a small negotiation room will cost as much as in the summer only 200 rubles per hour .
