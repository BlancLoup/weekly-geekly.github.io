The first thing you need is a currency settlement account at any bank.

Before opening a currency account, it is very important to talk with a representative of the currency control group - and not by phone, but in person. If it is prohibited by the rules of the bank - look for another bank.

You will open two accounts - current and transit.

Transit account - serves for receipt of money. After the money is received in the transit account, you must transfer it to the current currency account within seven days, or sell the currency to a ruble settlement account.
