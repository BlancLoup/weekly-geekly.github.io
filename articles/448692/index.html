Three non-obvious features of Zimbra that can improve the efficiency of employees

We have prepared for you three useful features of Zimbra, which will certainly be useful to you.



Calendar card

One of the most important functions of the collaboration system is a synchronized diary, which allows managers to efficiently manage the time of their subordinates, set tasks, and set up meetings and conferences at a convenient time for all. That is why quick access to the diary is one of those things that can significantly increase employee productivity.



The ability to quickly see your schedule is available in Zimbra. It is implemented as a built-in function ‚ÄúCalendar‚Äù - a small window with the days of the current month, which allows you to view your schedule for a particular day or add a new event to your diary directly from the main window of the Zimbra web client. Forced spell checker

Business correspondence must be competent. When communicating with people who do not see you, it is literate written speech that allows you to make a pleasant first impression. In addition to typing errors, errors can creep into an e-mail when copying and pasting ready-made pieces of text that rarely get into when sending. Zimbra can help you avoid errors in emails.



In the Zimbra web client settings, you can find a feature called ‚ÄúBe sure to check the spelling before sending a message‚Äù, which works exactly as you thought. Practically in any enterprise there is a special mailbox for receiving letters with appeals, offers of cooperation, and so on. The specifics of the operation of this mailbox implies that the filtering of incoming mail must either be minimal or be completely absent. At the same time, someone should look through all received letters, and, punching through tons of spam, search for letters from living people. And the more letters on such a box, the more precious working time the employee responsible for it spends on viewing such a box. In addition, the cleaning of mailboxes from all sorts of mailings and spam can be claimed by ordinary workers. Is it possible to somehow optimize this torturous process in Zimbra?
