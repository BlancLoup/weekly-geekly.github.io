So, the conclusions:



Before us are two ‚Äúentry-level‚Äù readers, with advanced multimedia features, targeted at users with needs not only in reading, but also in other multimedia features.

5-inch model can be used by girls, it will fit even in the smallest handbag, and the complete cover will protect you from keys and nail files. Guys may not take a bag at all, but put a book in a jeans pocket, for example.

The 7-inch book is already a standard-size book; you can see one on the metro every day and not just one. It has a more capacious battery, but the power consumption of the screen is also higher.

On average, a full charge is enough for 2 days of not very active use. You can charge both from USB and from the network through the charger.

The book is probably not suitable for younger children, because It does not have built-in games and there is no possibility to install them. In any case, it is quite a decent gift option for little money, but with great potential. Packaging can be called a gift without stretch, and a set - redundant.
