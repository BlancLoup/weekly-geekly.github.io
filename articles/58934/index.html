Gradation of productivity and comments to the days

It was proposed to make several gradations of productivity, for example, from -5 to +5. There were also suggestions to add the ability to comment on the days: to know, not only whether the day was productive or not, but why. The beauty of Pinarica is its simplicity. This is also its advantage. In order to describe every day what has been done, it is necessary, in my opinion, much more effort from myself than just to note whether the day was productive or not. The same about gradations. In my opinion, gradation will complicate perception, so I will not implement it yet. Regarding the comments on the days - I think about how to make it more convenient, but have not yet thought of it :)
