Two-meter Optimus Prime from scrap metal


To be honest, this is not a robot. It is simply a big toy made of scrap metal (in particular from a huge number of parts of old cars). But this is Optimus Prime. And this is definitely the most spectacular way to process "waste".

220-centimeter, 220-kilogram transformer looks just amazing. And it costs accordingly: more than $ 4,800. Interestingly, the copy is not a single one. On the site Robot-Models.com such giants can be ordered as much as you need. By the way, the assortment of Robot-Models.com is full of various optimuses.
