What people hate on websites

Aggressive advertising, links to "trust" and flash screensavers - this is an incomplete list of things that irritate visitors to any site. For the average user, the convenience of navigation means much more than a stylish design, sometimes incomprehensible. However, there are still sites on the Web where convenience has been sacrificed for beauty. Teresa Cannigton (Theresa Cunnington), a usability consultant at iFocus , gives a list of the most hated design delights from the perspective of the average user.
