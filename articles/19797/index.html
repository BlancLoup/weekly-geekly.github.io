Sape: now with contextual links

Sape recently announced the launch of testing contextual links:
We have completed alpha testing of the mechanism for buying and selling links directly from existing text pages (synonyms: contextual links, hypercontext), and we invite everyone to take part in the beta testing of the product.

Placing links has become more natural, now they are placed directly in the content. Only the content that the webmaster took in the <sape_index> tags is indexed, and when the page is loaded, these tags are automatically deleted. For the sale of contextual links are allowed only pages with permanent content. Prices for contextual links are set from the price management page by setting a multiplying factor from the regular price (1-2).

Webmasters will be offered pages for buying links and text snippets with the found keyword. Selecting the desired text fragment with the mouse and clicking on the check mark in front of the page, you can specify the text of the link for purchase in this text. In this case, the text of the link can include not only the keyword, but also the text surrounding it. If a keyword appears several times on a page, the system will prompt you to scroll through all the fragments and select the one you want.
