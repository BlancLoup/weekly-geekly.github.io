Gartner: IT departments can't handle leaks

According to a recent study by Gartner, IT departments are not able to effectively counteract leaks. Therefore, in recent years there has been a significant increase in the number of internal security incidents. However, the problem lies not in the lack of technological solutions, but in the lack of material resources. InfoWatch experts say that a similar situation is observed in Russia. There are already solutions, but management still does not allocate sufficient resources for internal security.
