Conference "Platform 2009"

December 4-5, 2008, Moscow will host the largest Microsoft technical conference in Russia - Platform 2009 . The conference will have a lot of interesting things: a plenary report by Mark Russinovich , an internationally known specialist in the internal structure of the Microsoft Windows operating system, a plenary report, 60 technical reports, round tables, laboratory work and much more.

For those who want to learn about the latest Microsoft technologies, look at the implementation experience and listen to tips on the use of technologies, the possibility of virtual participation is provided - Platform Online. The Online Platform is a broadcast of videos of all reports in real time, as well as recordings of all reports available after the conference.

To participate in the Platform Online, simply register on the conference website .

Unlike the recent virtual conference Vort-Ex , users of almost any browser and OS will be able to view the conference broadcast, since there are several options for viewing: a player built into the Silverlight page, or a link to a video stream that most modern video players can play eg Windows Media Player.

Detailed information on the conference reports can be found in the conference participant's brochure (PDF, 7 MB). The schedule of conference reports is available at http://platforma2009.ru/agenda.aspx .
