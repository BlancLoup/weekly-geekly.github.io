UA Webstar 2007 - Ukrainian Internet Award


On September 5, a trial version of the Ukrainian Internet Award UA Webstar 2007 was launched. The main task - no more, no less - to achieve success Webby Awards. Why Ukrainian sites can not have their own Internet award and award the best sites?
By the way, this year there are only five nominations - Best Personal Blog, Best Thematic Blog, Best Social Network, Best Search Service, Best Online Edition.
The best sites will be determined by Internet voting, which will begin on October 8, and will end just before the New Year - December 29. Then the winners will be announced. The rules are such that everyone can take part if he wants to apply for himself or for his favorite resource. The main thing is that the site / blog / network is indirectly related to Ukraine. All applications are accepted until October 5.
So hurry, maybe your site can be the best.
All applications write to uawebstar@gmail.com
Rules, terms - blogoreader.org.ua/webstar
