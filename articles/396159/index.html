Expansion of business to cover the main forms of road transport


Tesla Motors currently operates in two relatively small markets for premium sedans and electric SUVs. With the release of the Model 3, the future compact SUV and the new pickup, Tesla plans to cover all major sectors of the consumer market.

To achieve success, mass production needs to be scaled as quickly as possible, writes Ilon Musk. He says that the real product is not the Model 3 car, but the factory producing these cars.

Ideally, this factory is like a machine that produces cars - with the iterative release of cars of all the best quality. The car for the production of cars Model 3 can be considered as version 0.5, and version 1.0 will be formed by about 2018. "The first analysis of the principles of automotive production physics indicates that an improvement of about 5-10 times is possible with version 3.0 on a roughly two-year production cycle," the entrepreneur and inventor explained in a flattering manner. Commentators are now discussing how to understand these words in light of the plans for mass production of Tesla Model 3 and the possible need for additional funding.

Having covered all sectors of the consumer market, what will Tesla continue to do?

Ilon Musk answers this question directly. There are two more types of electric transport: these are heavy vehicles and urban passenger transport. Both of these types of transport are in early development by Tesla engineers and should be introduced next year.

Electric truck


Apparently, the Tesla electric saddle truck will be called Tesla Semi. This truck should significantly reduce the cost of transportation, it will be safer and more enjoyable to drive.

As an example, you can look at the Nikola One truck tractor from Nikola One . The 2000 hp heavy load is equipped with a set of 32,000 individual lithium-ion batteries with an aggregate capacity of 320 kWh. Pre-orders are now accepted for it, and the official presentation of Nikola One is scheduled for December 2, 2016.

At full charge of batteries with an additional cylinder of natural gas, the tractor can travel 1,200 miles (1,931 km), this is the declared range from a single charge.

Electric bus


As for buses, taking into account the increased autonomy (see below), it makes sense to reduce the size of buses that can ply the route more often. The driver will be more likely to "control the flight."

In such buses you do not need to do several entrances / exits, because public transport stops with crowds of passengers will be a thing of the past. Such stops with the bus call buttons will remain only for those who do not have a smartphone. Autonomous bus will pick up passengers along the route. The absence of several water / exits allows to increase the number of seats in the cabin, and the movement of the autopilot will unload traffic jams in the city, which are often created by slow and heavy public transport.

The Tesla bus project provides a place for wheelchair-bound passengers and passengers with bicycles.

Autopilot


As technology develops, all Tesla cars will receive the function of full automatic piloting, as well as full fault tolerance.

Full fault tolerance means that the autopilot will be able to safely move on if any part of the vehicle is broken.

Ilon Musk emphasizes that the most difficult thing in this task is software development. All the necessary equipment already, in principle, is available: cameras, sonars, radars and computers of sufficient power.

To allow such autopilot on public roads, regulators will probably require extensive testing from approximately 10 billion kilometers in test mode. At the moment, Tesla autopilots pass a little more than 5 million km per day.

Ilon Mask also explained why the company is already experiencing an unfinished autopilot in real conditions. The answer is simple: even in its current state, the autopilot improves safety on the road and reduces the risk of an accident, as can be clearly seen from the mortality statistics . The autopilot‚Äôs ‚Äúbeta‚Äù is now rather symbolic. It will be charged when the safety of the autopilot is 10 times greater than the safety of driving in manual mode (now the safety is about 2 times higher, based on the mortality statistics of the National Transportation Safety Management of the USA).

Sharing


As soon as the regulators approve the use of autopilot, drivers will have free time to do personal things in the car: read books, sleep or play computer games. In this case, it will be very convenient to add your car to the pool of sharing Tesla machines (like Uber Pool). The nearest car is called by pressing a button in the mobile application.

Why does the owner need it? Mostly for the money. The machine will generate profits for its owner, while he is doing his own business or is working, or if he went on vacation. According to the calculations of Ilona Mask, this way the car will be able to easily cover the monthly installment for its purchase, and will quickly pay for itself in full. If a person went on vacation, then, in principle, may not return: the car will generate enough money for him to live in a not very expensive corner of the world.

In a fundamental sense, car sharing is important for the entire economy, because at the moment cars are used only 5-10% of their time, and in the rest of the hours they stand idle.

In cities where demand for taxis exceeds supply, Tesla intends to open its own unmanned vehicle fleets.
