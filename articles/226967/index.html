On the prerequisites of the socialist revolution in copyright

The discovery of Tesla Motors patents has given rise to an incredible discussion about the role and significance of this act. Some people convict Ilona Mask of excessive enthusiasm for public relations, others, on the contrary, consider him a guru and benefactor of society. The second group is more numerous, because those who know about Tesla Motors are mainly its supporters. Retrogrades automotive simply not drawn into it. In my humble opinion, the truth in this matter lies not even in the middle, but in general, to the side of the discussion about the moral character of Mask.

If you look at the trends in patenting over the past ten years, you can see that patents are going through a gradual phase of reform from the proprietary model to the prevalence of public interest.

If you look at patents and copyright as a whole as a means of production, you will notice that their main resources are concentrated in the hands of a relatively small number of corporations. In each of the industries can be distinguished small oligopolies within the framework of the world, which is the main race. Apple, Google, Microsoft - in the software; Pfizer, Novatis, Merck & Co - in pharmaceuticals. Often there are more than three, but, usually, a dozen names occupy the lion‚Äôs share of the market.

It is not worth explaining once again that if the head of one of the most influential confessions in the world speaks of a problem, then it has long gone beyond the bounds of judicial warrior.

The answer to such a usurpation of the means of production is the fierce Makhnovshchina industrial espionage and electronic piracy. It is extremely difficult to calculate unequivocally its volumes, however it is worth mentioning that the accusations of industrial espionage did not bypass the largest companies in the world.

In the unfolding conflict over the establishment of new rules on the use of the means of production of the state and national laws often become a tool, not an arbitrator. There is no single decision-making center here, so one should not expect spectacular building seizures, equestrian attacks, or loud overthrows from this process. The struggle of patents will follow the path of the market and will be conducted in the plane of their price and connection with the price of the goods.
