R-Words.com - Memorize Foreign Words



It has long been fascinated by the study of foreign languages, looking for a program that would help more effectively memorize new foreign words. On the Internet there are many programs for this purpose, but not all of them are pleased with the great functionality. The most suitable in my opinion was the program LearnWords. But it was a commercial product, and I had to purchase a license key in the online store. When purchasing on the product page there was not any note that this key is valid only for 1 year. And after this period, you must purchase a new license. I learned this feature of the program only a year later, when the program stopped working. This fact made me very angry, because I did not understand why a program in which only cosmetic changes are made a couple of times a year must be paid annually. Therefore, there was an idea to make an analogue similar in functionality and at the same time free for the user.

So the idea arose to make the Internet service R-Words (Remember Words) for learning foreign words. After a short work (about 1 month), an alpha version with basic functionality was ready.
