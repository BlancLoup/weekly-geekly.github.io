Ebay.Com again in the lead.

According to Nielsen / NetRatings, Ebay.Com remains the most popular site in the e-commerce category. In August, the site of the largest auction of all times and peoples was visited 89.4 million times. The second line of the ranking is Amazon.Com with an indicator of 59.5 million visitors. Dell Inc. settled in third place . which was visited 21.7 million times in August.
