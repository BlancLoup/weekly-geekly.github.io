Independent network channel width in the Scalax cloud

Great news! The company Oversan does not stand still, and today we are announcing the next release, labeled as 0.41, the Scalaxie clouds .

This time a complex function has been implemented, which will potentially serve well all our customers. Namely - independent scaling.

One can beat around the bush, but the main meaning is transferred to one sentence - from now on, each virtual machine has the resource to scale the width of the external network channel, regardless of RAM, in the range from 5 Mbps to 300 Mbps, 5 Mbps

Accordingly, the pricing of resources used has changed. Previously, a solid slot consisting of 512 MB of RAM and 5 Mbit / s bandwidth cost 65 kopecks per hour. Now we are charging separately megabytes of RAM (40 kopecks at 512 MB per hour) and network bandwidth (5 kopecks at 1 Mbit / s per hour).



The API allows you to customize the scaling of the network channel for your own needs in such a way as to save resources when they are not required, and to provide power on demand in the event of an increase in load. This is especially useful for owners of many projects, as it will allow even more subtle adjustment of the behavior of each machine in the cloud, for specific requests of the project or its users.

Today, tests are coming to an end, and in the near future each client of Scalaxi will be able to try out new opportunities in action. If you have any questions, difficulties, or even problems - do not hesitate to express them to us on the forum . We are famous for lightning-fast technical support, which is ready to respond to your call, not only by phone, but also on Twitter , which brings important updates.

Well, each newly registered customer is a small gift.
