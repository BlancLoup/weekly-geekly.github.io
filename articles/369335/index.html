iBottle - a modern bottle for geek athletes



When someone tries to rethink something well-established, long familiar, he (or she) is usually told that you should not reinvent the wheel. Nevertheless, there are a great number of bicycles now, and some models have not so much in common with their forerunners.

The same can be said about water bottles. For a long time these vessels have already left their monofunctional prototype. This is especially true for bottles iBottle. The manufacturing company has released several models, each of which is something more than just a water tank. It may look a bit strange, but it‚Äôs still worth assessing the idea. Let's do this and do it.

Let's start with the model iBottle YH-228



Probably, this is the strangest bottle from the whole lineup (to be honest, it even sounds weird - the lineup of bottles for water). It can perform several functions at once.

The first is the storage of a certain amount of water. In general, there is nothing to describe here - water is stored in a special compartment, for the convenience of the user there is a special tube. Very convenient, the water is almost not bottled.



The second is the storage of the phone. Yes, instead of wearing your iPhone 6 (or a phone of a comparable size) in a pocket of sweatpants or in a case on your hand, you can put the phone in a bottle. Naturally, in a different compartment, and not in the one where water is already splashing.



The developers made the iBottle YH-228 compartments tight, so there is no danger.

The third is the storage of several small things in the third compartment, at the very bottom. Here we are talking about little things like headphones, spare parts (sorry for the tautology) and other necessary things of small size.

iWater YH-229




This bottle is different from the previous model. Inside, the smartphone no longer fits (and there is no airtight compartment). But outside the bottle there is a special connector where you can place a mobile device.

This bottle is an excellent stand that can be used at your desk. By installing a smartphone, you can listen to music or chat with friends, colleagues, partners.

By the way, besides the YH-229 there is another similar model, YH-239. It also has a built-in speaker. It is wireless, works via Bluetooth, so here is a stand and a mobile media station.

iBottle YH-240




Another similar bottle that also allows you to store a mobile phone inside. All the same pleasures as in previous models, but instead of a compartment for storing small things here - built-in battery. It allows you to charge the battery at the wrong time of the phone.

Yes, and the phone is stored inside the bottle, so everything you need is nearby. The main thing is not to forget the bottle in the same gym or on the street.

Here they are, modern water bottles. Unusual and modern.
