crisisonline.ru - a project in two days.

How and who can make money on a crisis? A question that I am sure many ask themselves ...
This story began by chance. According to the tasks of the e-Shtab.ru project, we with a film crew were at a seminar on the financial crisis in Russia. Our goal was to make a story about the seminar, interview some speakers, well, and, of course, networking.

After a two-hour shooting, we realized that the video content received was not fully consistent with the goals of the e-staff, and we decided to do a separate project.

You need to run fast. It was obvious to us. To make a cool portal, comfortable, beautiful and functional - there is no time. There are enough smart people in runet who want to get ahead of us. The goal of the project is to create a platform where people can communicate about the crisis. The crisis is discussed on all popular resources of the runet, which means that a special resource dedicated to the crisis may be in demand. In addition, the video content, which we will collect for the e-headquarters anyway, can be ‚Äúdiluted‚Äù with questions of the crisis, and individual topics can be edited.

Monetization ... We want to try to make a platform for anti-crisis advertising. Most companies in crisis suffer from distrust, due to the loss of customer loyalty, and therefore channels will be demanded to inform consumers about the real state of affairs.

Do not judge strictly - the project is raised in two days. We were able to use something from the e-headquarters, but it must be admitted that when we wrote e-headquarters, there was no time to think much about re-using the code.
