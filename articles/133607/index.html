Indie Royale: The really big bundle



A few minutes ago began selling Indie Royale: The Really Big Bundle. Really Big Bundle includes the following games:
  • Really big sky
  • Runespell: Overture
  • Cthulhu Saves the World
  • Eufloria
  • Bonus: Breath of Death VII


Indie Royale is a joint project of Desura and the site IndieGames.com in order to sell games of independent game devs, which were deprived of the attention of the public. This project is to some extent a competitor to The Humble Indie Bundle. As in the HIB, you are offered to pay as much as you like, but the minimum price is constantly increasing. You can pay for games with Paypal or a credit card, you can activate them via Steam, Desura or simply download it.
All games will work under Windows, there are also toys under Mac and Linux. When a certain amount is reached, all customers usually receive additional buns in the form of DLC and soundtracks. This is the third sale of Idie Royale and at least three more are planned.

PS Added bonus: The Really Big Sky and Eufloria + Text Adventures Soundtracks to Zeboyd Games
