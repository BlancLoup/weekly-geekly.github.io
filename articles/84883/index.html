ITrack CMS Rating

ITrack www.itrack.ru has completed the January collection of data from an independent site management system (CMS) rating, compiled from information about real installations on sites.

Specially trained "spider" (robot program) regularly walks around all sites in the .ru zone and collects data for analysis.

- A total of 2,547,784 domains were polled in January 2010.
- The share of domains that responded within 20 seconds was 65.68%.
- CMS detected on 13.4% of domains.

The new study differs from the previous ones for the better in that the timeout for the server response was increased from 5 to 20 seconds. As a result, a greater number of installations were found, but the share of CMS grew unevenly - the share of more demanding server-side CMS grew more than the share of faster systems. For this reason, the dynamics of changes in shares are not published at this stage. Innovation is information on the statistics of the speed of loading HTML-code of the main page of sites with paid and free boxed CMS.






The speed is shown in relative units on a 10-point scale.
For 10 points, the results of CMS are accepted, for which the shortest loading time of the HTML code of the main page of the site was obtained.
The capacity of the channel during the study remained unchanged.

Read more about the data of the independent CMS rating, as well as about the method of collection and analysis - http://itrack.ru/cmsrate/ .

Follow the results of the new research by iTrack, where it is planned to disclose the dynamics, as well as information on the CMS, which are placed on new domains.
