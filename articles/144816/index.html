Facebook buys Opera?



Being under the scrutiny of Wall Street and its main investors, Facebook is trying to solve its problems with monetization, absorbing various companies that will help it increase its presence on smartphones.

If the data of the last report is accurate, then Opera Software may become the next acquisition.

The site pocket-lint reports : from reliable sources it became known that Facebook is interested in buying Opera to create its own browser.

Opera has more than 200,000,000 users (desktops, tablets, smartphones, mobile phones, and even the Nintendo Wii).

The Next Web portal, in turn, reported that from an anonymous source they learned that Opera is looking for potential buyers. The source could not one hundred percent confirm that the buyer is Facebook, but said that such a deal would definitely have some sense.

A Facebook spokeswoman said the company is not going to comment on this.

And the press secret of the Opera said that they are not inclined to react to market gossip.
