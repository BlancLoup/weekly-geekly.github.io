Benefits of Navitel Navigator for iPhone:
- The most detailed maps of Russia, Ukraine and the Republic of Belarus are available with pass-through routing (the route from Moscow to Kiev via Minsk or Bobruisk is not a problem).
- Free Navitel service. Traffic jams on the territory of the entire cartographic coverage, and not only in major cities.

- Ability to free download unofficial (amateur) maps of any city or country.
