Samsung's smart home unveiled at CES 2014

So Samsung got to this type of systems and at CES 2014 in Las Vegas, they demonstrated the Samsung Smart Home platform.
With this platform, the user will be able to integrate smart TVs, smartphones, as well as various household and digital appliances (even a refrigerator and a washing machine) into a home network. The system is a centralized version of various individual applications, with some additional Galaxy Gear integration.




The three main features of Samsung Smart Home: Device Management, Home View and Smart Customer Service allow you to easily connect to devices anytime, anywhere. Device management will help change the settings of the equipment, regardless of whether the user is at home or outside. You can turn on or turn off air conditioning or lighting by simply clicking on the Smart Home application icon on the screen of your mobile device, combined with home appliances into one network. The possibility of voice control is also provided.
