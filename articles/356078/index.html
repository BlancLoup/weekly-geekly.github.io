After blocking by Roskomnadzor, PornHub launched a special offer for residents of Russia

On September 13, Roskomnadzor, by a court decision, added PornHub and YouPorn resources to the registry of prohibited sites . The administration of PornHub did not ignore the fact of blocking and used it to advertise the resource.

Now PornHub offers all visitors from the territory of the Russian Federation a two week free premium account. The corresponding entry was posted by the administration of the resource on its official twitter account:

Get excited Russia !!! Starting today were not offering premium porn for 2 weeks because..IT AINT BAND YET. pic.twitter.com/6kTAnNKoie

- Pornhub ARIA (@Pornhub) September 29, 2016

When you go to the promotion page, the user is prompted to go through a simple registration procedure using e-mail and indicating the user's nickname.



Some kind of "pitfalls" in the form of "bind a credit card to activate" or "subscribe" no. Immediately after registration, the account receives a free premium access for two weeks.

The only point is: to get to the share page, you must have a Russian IP address. When using other addresses, the person who wants to get premium access encounters this stub:



Blocking the main address of PornHub does not interfere with getting into the pornhubpremium domain and registering an account that you can later access through a proxy or VPN. The problem occurs with account activation via email. By the proposed link, the user redirects to the main domain, which cannot be accessed from the Russian IP due to blocking. But even without activation, the account gets access to the premium section.
