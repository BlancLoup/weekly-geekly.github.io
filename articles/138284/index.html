Chirpify startup lets you buy products via Twitter

The microblogging service Twitter is very popular not only among users who just communicate, but also among brands, many large and small companies have got themselves registered in the service. And of course, the multi-million crowd of potential buyers does not give rest to any of the brands. Until now, the service has served only as a tool for public relations. But now, with the advent of startup Chirpify, here you can buy all sorts of goods and services. True, it is necessary that both the seller and the buyer work with Chirpify.

This startup has joined forces with PayPal in order to give buyers and sellers the opportunity to conduct transactions via Twitter. If everything goes smoothly, many other companies will connect to the service.
