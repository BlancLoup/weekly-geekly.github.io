The laboratory is a fully private educational institution and has the necessary licenses. The teaching staff consists mostly of candidates and doctors of science. As mentioned earlier, the project will be launched on the basis of the existing Sokatovo production site. Now we are ready to launch two different educational programs, for each of which we plan to invite 10 people.

The first program is correspondence course. It consists of two parts: an introductory course in Sokatovo from 02.11.2013 to 09.11.2013 (autumn school holidays), and a correspondence course for self-study based on materials issued. All the necessary questions can be asked remotely. The second visit is from 04/01/2013 to 01/11/2013 (winter holidays). Terms - approximate, exact dates will be known later.

The second program is full-time study. Dates of arrival in Sokatovo will depend on the course. In parallel with the math courses, programming courses will be available. The exact list will be announced later.
