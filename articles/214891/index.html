Olympic traffic: how and how much watched the Olympics on the Internet

During the Winter Olympics in Sochi, CDNvideo was a partner of the All-Russian State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company. All official broadcasts of Olympic events channel VGTRK led using our servers. And we counted all the spectators and received interesting statistics.

How many people work CDN. Our client publishes a broadcast and places a player on its website. All connections are processed by our servers, and the intelligent balancer determines from which node it is best to send the video stream for each specific site visitor. This node will be closest to the visitor or least loaded.

Thus, we could capture all the Olympic traffic up to the user, and we know how many people watched the Olympics, when, from where and on which devices.

Immediately make a reservation, we know nothing about those who watched the Olympics on regular televisions. We have data only on views through computers, tablets, phones. Through the TV too - but only if it is a Smart TV. It is clear that the overwhelming majority of the population of the country watched the Olympics on TV, and we can judge the popularity of broadcasts from a sample of a few percent.

Let's start with what devices Internet users use to watch sports events.
