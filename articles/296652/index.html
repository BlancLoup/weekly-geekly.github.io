Ministry of Communications wants to create a competitive market for copyright management

In August, the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media prepared the first version of the draft law on the complete abolition in Russia of the non-contractual collective management of copyright and related rights. According to the developers of the draft law, the current system is non-transparent, and rightholders have claims to it. Deputy Minister of Communications Alexey Volin believes that the author should independently negotiate with a collective management organization and stipulate conditions. The Ministry of Communications and Mass Media also hoped for the emergence of many competing collective management societies in the country.

The new version was finalized by the Expert Council under the government, it was coordinated by representatives of operators, copyright holders and Internet companies.
