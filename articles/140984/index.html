Two-week joint tests of Tele2 and Scientific Research Institute of LTE and GSM collaboration in Omsk and Pskov ended successfully

Tele2 's press release yesterday announced that Tele2 and Radio Research Institute have successfully completed the testing of the first LTE 1800 network in Russia (based on Nokia Siemens Networks equipment) in the test zones located in Omsk and Pskov. The test results confirm that the quality of services in the GSM network has not decreased, and the data transfer rate in the LTE mobile network has reached 75 megabits per second.

(These are the very tests that I mentioned at the beginning at Habrahabr in mid-March.)

  • The experience of frequency conversion is an argument in favor of technological neutrality. Sergey Fedosenkov, Deputy Director of NSN in Russia. Omsk, March 29.
