Review of the "sleeping" laptop Samsung 350U2B

Two months ago, there were only two laptops with a display diagonal of 12.5 inches in the world: Lenovo X220i and HP 2560p. A third representative of this rare species came to our editorial office.



350U2B - representative of the new concept of Samsung notebook lines. Now Koreans have 9-, 7-, 5- and 3 series.

Appearance


The laptop has a beautiful body: strong, lightweight, with pleasant rounded shapes and recognizable features for Samsung laptops. Not a single glossy part and luxurious - only seven millimeters thick - matte edging of the display.



Edging, by the way, rubberized. Due to this, the laptop slams down with a very gentle and pleasant sound.



Almost no complaints about the keyboard and touchpad: the keys are island-like and very soft, and the touchpad is almost the size of an adult‚Äôs palm. The touchpad buttons also definitely worked out.

Filling


The Samsung 350U2B is, albeit the youngest, but fully charged: the second generation Core i5 processor, 4 GB of RAM and half a terabyte on the hard disk. Although, given the compactness of the laptop, the 3G module would not be superfluous here. There is also a version with a Core i3 processor and a 320 GB hard drive - it will cost three thousand rubles less.

Double respect to Samsung engineers for connectors: I have never met a better arrangement in my life. On the right side are USB and HDMI ports, a power jack and a network connector. On the left - a headphone jack, a card reader, another USB and a connector for connecting an adapter to VGA.



It would seem that the budget version and all that, but even the little things in the Samsung 350U2B are five points. The laptop has an unusual cooling system: one of the holes is displayed at the junction of the bottom panel and the screen. Practice has shown that the solution is very good: the laptop almost does not heat up and does not make noise.

On the bottom of the 350U2B are four large rubber legs: to consciously move the laptop away, you need to make an effort.

The battery can be removed! But in order to pull it out, you need to twist nine screws and remove the cover completely. By the way, in the mode of average power consumption, but with Wi-Fi on and maximum brightness, the battery lasts for 4.5 hours.
