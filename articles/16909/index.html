Strictly No Photography: show all that is hidden

There are a great many places in the world that you want to visit, but this is not always possible and in principle impossible. With the development of the Web and the emergence of photo hosting sites, this problem was practically solved - one could look at many things right from home, of course, without getting all the sensations.

However, photo hosting sites rarely find photos from museums, exhibitions, theaters, government facilities. The fact is that photography in such places is strictly prohibited and, therefore, the number of photos is small. In order not to look for rare photos for hours, a hosting of not quite legally made photos with the name Strictly No Photography was created .

The photos on the site are divided into categories, including all kinds of museums, theaters, art galleries, live concerts, government facilities, etc. The site itself has a completely untypical design by today's standards - I would say it looks more like an old school site one author, not a collective photo hosting. But the main thing, as you know, is not the design, but all the same content. Therefore, we forget about the design and just go to see what, in theory, we should not see :)
