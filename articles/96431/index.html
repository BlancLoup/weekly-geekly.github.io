Trac 0.12 released

With a delay of six months from the plan came Trac 0.12 .

Trac is an integrated project management system that includes an error tracking system, a wiki, a planning tool, and a very powerful version control repository viewer.

In the new version:
  • Localization of the interface;
  • Multiple storage support in one project;
  • Expansion wiki-syntax, support tables, rename pages;
  • Editing mode for wiki pages, in which the preview area is located side by side with the editor and is updated automatically;
  • Editing comments with automatic preview as you type;
  • Ability to delete cards and comments (separately included in the settings);
  • OR in requests;
  • And many other interesting changes .

Also pleased with the plugin support for Mercurial .
