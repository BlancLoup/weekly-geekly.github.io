Rhinoceros, python and shot through foot at the conference SMARTRHINO-2018

On April 26, a conference SMARTRHINO-2018 was held at the REC Information Center of the Moscow State Technical University named after Bauman, where lectures and seminars were presented on four topics:


  • reverse engineering,
  • Python / ML,
  • Best Practices
  • Kotlin.


Students were able to explore the winking rhinoceros, exorcise demons in Latin with a python and understand how you can shoot yourself in any programming language and in particular on Kotlin.



In addition, they slightly broke their brains over the problems from the speakers, for whose solution they received valuable prizes from the conference organizer, INFORION .


Congratulations to the winners of the competition :


  • Ekaterina Evdokimova, who took first place, and
  • Popov Denis, Novikov Ilya, Terekhov Vladimir, Kudashov Vladislav, Gavrilov Nikita, who divided the second place!
