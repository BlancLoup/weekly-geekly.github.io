New set on Information Technology Courses

Today, for the third time, we are opening the recruitment of students and young IT specialists at the Information Technology Courses in Yandex. Courses, as before, are free.

What awaits our listeners:
  • a review of professions related to information technology;
  • familiarity with the software tools of the system engineer;
  • meeting and communicating with people ensuring the operation of Yandex services.

The theoretical course consists of seven lectures, which will be held starting from October 18, on Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 19 to 21 hours in the Moscow office of Yandex . For those who can not get to the lectures, we will try to provide a video broadcast and the ability to write questions in writing.

After the end of the lectures an exam will be held. To the students who have shown the best results, we will offer to undergo the practice of system administration in Yandex. Successful practice, as experience shows, seriously increases the chances of getting a permanent job at Yandex.

Graduates of the courses will be able to successfully work as system administrators, network engineers or information security specialists.

To get to the Courses or become an online listener, fill out the form . We are waiting for students and young professionals who are ready to attend classes in the Moscow office. Lecture broadcasts will also be available - you can become an online listener if you are unable to attend classes.

Applications are accepted until October 12.
