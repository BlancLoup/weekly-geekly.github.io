Facebook overtook Twitter in terms of audience growth. Service microblogging published statistics on income for 2014

The microbog service Twitter posted a report on user growth and income statistics for the fourth quarter of 2014. The number of Twitter users in the last three months of last year increased by 1.4%, which is the first time in history less than the increase in Facebook - 3% joined this social network in the same period.

According to the report, Twitter in the fourth quarter of 2014 earned 479 million dollars, two times more than last year. 90% of the company's revenue comes from advertising. The net profit of the service is 78 million.

For the entire 2014, the service earned $ 1.4 billion, more than double the 2013 results.
