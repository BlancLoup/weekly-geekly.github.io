Black monday


This morning, and judging by the forums and last night, we felt the effect of the new update of Kaspersky Anti-Virus.

This morning (or rather, they all died yesterday) the servers began to die. 100% CPU usage and leaking memory.

Every 3 minutes, an event occurs:
An unhandled exception has occurred.
Process: kavfswp.exe [4508].
Address: 0x04E2A571.
Exception code: 0xc0000005.

All servers with KAVFSEE 6.0.2.555 on board are crashing.

Servers with less than 2GB of RAM fell immediately. The rest were fastened to the last.

So let's say THANK YOU to Kaspersky for our hard working days.
