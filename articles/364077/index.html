For making snowflakes you need:
1. File with the scheme.
2. Printer (I think laser will be preferable).
3. Scissors.
4. The scalpel.
5. Paper.
5.1. My personal opinion is that when printing on standard office paper with a density of 80g / m 2, it is not very convenient to cut it out (after all, when you fold the paper, you get a thick layer). Therefore, I personally use thinner paper. And the snowflakes turn out "airy."
