10 crisis phones up to 1000 rubles



In the conditions of the currency rodeo, retail prices for all kinds of electronics have risen simply indecent. The double fall of the ruble exchange rate has led to the fact that we still have a long time to get used to the new value of such a unit, like $ 100. If earlier it was a psychologically acceptable amount of about 3,200-3,400 rubles (it took a couple of years to get used to it at the time), now one hundred bucks have jumped far from the comfort zone. However, it is not a disaster, life goes on. As the old Russian proverb says, there will be a celebrity on our street.

In the meantime, users have to postpone the acquisition of new gadgets or pay attention to more budget models. In light of this trend, we have made for all comers a selection of the most attractive mobile phones costing less than 1000 rubles (the average price on the market based on the offers of stores). Almost sold by weight.

The phone is equipped with a display with a stunning imagination of 1.44 inches diagonal, with a resolution of 128 x 128. As a nice bonus, there is a radio and a built-in flashlight. A 800 mAh battery allows the phone to work up to 840 hours in standby mode or up to 12.5 hours in talk mode. The device is available in black or blue case.
