Ciklum Java Subbotnik in Minsk - as it were

On Saturday, October 15, our colleagues from the Minsk office of Ciklum successfully held a mini-conference on Java technologies - Ciklum Java Saturday , which took place in an unusual place for our Ciklum Subbotniki - Photo Studio PhotoGraph .

With the support of Exigen Services, as on the previous Ciklum Sabbotnik , we once again gathered speakers and enthusiastic developers who are not indifferent to Java and flash-development.


During coffee breaks and delicious lunch, the guys had the opportunity to both meet and talk with each other, discuss the reports with the authors on the sidelines, and talk with representatives of Ciklum and Exigen.

The company TUT.by kindly agreed to conduct a live broadcast, and also posted a video conference materials .
