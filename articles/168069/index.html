I2P network has accelerated



Recently, various laws have been introduced in Russia to restrict a person‚Äôs freedom of speech, including on the Internet.

Unfortunately, there are no methods of fighting such arbitrariness (more precisely, there is, but no one wants to go to prison?) , But in our world there are ways to circumvent certain rules.

One good example of such a workaround is the anonymous I2P network.
Many have heard about it, and some may have tried it themselves, but unfortunately, previous versions of the official network client worked very slowly .

This prompted to abandon the use of this network.
But in the latest version 0.9.4, (released 2012-12-17) there have been dramatic changes in the speed of the anonymous network.

Due to the fact that many people are too busy to install and configure the client for the I2P network, I decided to make a small video that shows the speed of the network after the update.

PS For access to the network, Dual Access is used (access to the normal Internet on a direct line and to i2p sites - through the client) this is realized through Privoxy, if someone is interested in it, I can tell you in the next article.
