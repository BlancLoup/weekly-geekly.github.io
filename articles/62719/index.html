Photosynth update

Did you notice how the Photosynth site changed ?



In addition to the overall design change, aimed at improving the perception of synths, there are noticeable changes in the details.

So, on the main page displays the latest popular synth immediately with the player so that you can immediately assess what it is.



Cards of synths got comment counters and a geotag icon - now you can immediately understand which synth is actively discussed, in which you can leave a comment first, which is geographically linked:



Also improved the search. Now search results can be sorted not only by the best match of keywords, but also by the quality of synths, authors, popularity, date.
