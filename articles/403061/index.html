Often, three parameters indicate the height of the building: the level of the last inhabited space, the highest point where the flagpole can enter and the height according to the structure. The lower mark is from the floor of the main public entrance to the building. Piles, technical floors in the foundation, underground parking lots and deep technical inputs are not counted.
