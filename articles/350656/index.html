ASC scenario as the basis of timeliness and harmony in business

In the previous article we talked about the market as a stereotype, with respect to a segment, need, and proposal. An example in this article was the actions of various organizations leading them to the top of the business. We will return to this conversation, and today we will talk about the timeliness of certain actions.

According to the theory of social design, absolutely all social communities (whole industries, business organizations, political parties and even family) that can be presented systemically, pass at least once during their life, through three phases (see fig.) Conventionally called: Morphogenesis, Action, Behavior. In terms of this process (cycles) is called the ASC scenario ( Anthropo-socio-cultural scenario ). Why ASK? Here we rely on the concepts and works of the American sociologist, Pitirim Sorokin , of Russian origin, the founder of the sociological department of Harvard. He argued that whenever we are dealing with a social object, we need to think about three subsystems: man, culture and structures e. They, interacting in a complex way, generate a completely natural course of events. The understanding of these cycles, the correct diagnosis of the current situation on this ‚Äúloop of life‚Äù, allows us to apply (develop) timely and adequate steps and strategies.


Anthropo-socio-cultural scenario of life cycles of organizations

The ASC scenario has three phases: Morphogenesis, Action, Behavior. In turn, each phase has its own dynamic expression in the form of several stages.

Phase morphogenesis

The phase is characterized by two stages:

Stage 1. Transcendental destruction of the social form . The emergence of new social forms, or the transcendental degeneration of old (degrading) forms and the prospect of the degeneration of the social system.
