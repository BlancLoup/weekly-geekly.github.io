Dr. Dre has actually confirmed the sale of Apple's Beats



Yesterday, a number of sources said that Apple is close to concluding a deal to buy for $ 3.2 billion Beats Electronics - a company whose co-founder is an American rapper and producer Dr. Dre. At this time, Dr. Dre, apparently, celebrated the sale in his studio, as evidenced by the video from the actor Tireez Gibson's Facebook page, writes The Verge.

Gibson confirmed the amount of $ 3.2 billion and called on Forbes to revise the list of the richest people in the world because Dr. Dre has now joined the billionaires club. In the video itself Dr. Dre calls himself "the first billionaire in hip-hop." Although the rapper does not directly confirm the deal with Apple, everything indicates that the deal has already been concluded.

According to yesterday's reports , the deal includes both the online streaming music service Beats Music, and the part of the company that produces headphones and speakers. The largest acquisition in the history of Apple was to be officially announced as early as next week.
