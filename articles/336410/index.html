Nontrivial techniques for finding business ideas

Treasure hunters in fact and reformers in dreams, businessmen do not like to calculate the "fish places" by analyzing boring statistics. They prefer ready-made recipes and short cuts. But in the days of scientific and technological revolutions, going to fortune-tellers was not fashionable. Therefore, in their place adapted methods of managing creativity.


Designing Ideas as an Innovative Magic Crystal

Known techniques of creative management


Technical progress requires the flow of new solutions. Professional inventors have developed many methods for constructing ideas, and entrepreneurs have adopted them.

Brainstorming (conference of ideas) is based on group infection. Driven by an experienced facilitator, participants fly out of the inertia of thinking. And they begin to drive the snowstorm to generate free associations. Then the skeptics put on headphones, and a couple of three pearls are scooping from the records of the bird market. Or do not rake, as lucky. The method is random.

Syintestics and the method of focal objects have stricter rules. Synectics instructs the group to use analogies. Straight lines from adjacent areas, empathic according to Stanislavsky (I am the site, brew me). And symbolic (semantic) based on games with language constructs. The method of focal objects crosses the subject of study with anything. As a matrix of ideas studio Lebedev.


Matrix of Lebedev's ideas. 10 years unchanged

Unlike brainstorming and its derivatives, the morphological analysis is individual. It does not need assistants, meeting rooms, voice recorders. Even the computer is not needed. Draw a tablet by hand. In the line, we write the parameter A, in the column the parameter B, the combinations at the intersections of "we will marry". The morphological analysis is so obvious that it was first discovered in the XII century, and since then it has been rediscovered every year. So, in February 2017, the former owner of the Oyster book service, Eric Stromberg, described it on the Medium blog as a method for searching ideas for startups.

Tips for resolving contradictions


The algorithm for solving the inventive problems of Heinrich Altshuller (ARIZ), safely banned by the world behind the scenes, as far back as 1973, classified the intellectual methods of creating new ideas. Of the 39 described techniques, 29 were purely engineering, suitable only for solving design problems. But the remaining 10 principles of Altshuller are universal and applicable to any field of creativity. Here they are:

  1. "Merge";
  2. "Increase decrease";
  3. "Go to another dimension";
  4. "Go to the supersystem or subsystem";
  5. "Add periodic action";
  6. "Add feedback";
  7. "Add self-service";
  8. ‚ÄúDo harm in favor‚Äù;
  9. "To increase the intensity of the processes";
  10. "Do the opposite."
