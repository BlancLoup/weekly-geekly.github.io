Sony has released a 20-inch tablet with Windows 8



Monstrous 20-inch tablet / tabletop Vaio Tap 20 (SVJ20215CXW) weighs 5 kilograms.

Specifications:
Size: 504x304x45 mm
Processor: Intel Core i5-3317U 1.7 GHz
Screen resolution: 1600x900
Graphics Card: Intel HD Graphics 4000
Memory: 4 GB
HDD: 750 GB, 5400 rpm
Interfaces: 802.11b / g / n, Gigabit Ethernet, 2xUSB 3.0, SD, NFC
Touchscreen All-In-One (multitouch up to 10 taps at the same time)
Price: $ 880



If you put it on your desktop, it looks almost like a normal desktop. By the way, a wireless mouse and keyboard are included.





The device also comes with several programs optimized for such a huge touchscreen, including the Fingertapps calendar and the ArtRage Studio graphic editor.

Sales of Vaio Tap 20 begin on October 26, when the official premiere of Windows 8 will take place. Nevertheless, reviews of this unusual device have already been published in several publications: [ 1 ], [ 2 ], [ 3 ].

Of the shortcomings, observers note a small screen resolution, as well as battery life: about two hours.
