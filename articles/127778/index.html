Bill payment in KillFish bars

We have long planned to go offline and now, it happened. Now the bill in the KillFish bar (Peter) can be paid using QIWI Wallet . The mechanics are very simple: call the bartender your mobile phone number, after a moment a USSD request comes with a bill amount. By pressing 1, you pay the bill: the money is debited from your QIWI Wallet.



Also from the QIWI Wallet account you can replenish the KillFish internal payment card. In the future, this card can be paid at any bar network.



In addition to the new payment method, KillFish offers guests a temporary promotion: until September 15, when paying a bill through QIWI Wallet, everyone receives a bonus of 100% of the bill amount to the guest card. We paid the bill of 300 rubles - 300 rubles was written off from your wallet, but the same amount fell on the KillFish card. For the convenience of replenishing the Wallet, a QIWI terminal is installed in every KillFish bar.

It is just elementary to get a card: you need to fill out a short questionnaire (full name - phone) on the bar, you will receive an SMS with a verification code on your phone:



Tell him to the bartender, and in a minute he will give you a personal guest card. In addition to a large but temporary bonus when paying via QIWI Wallet, a permanent bonus is added to this card for each friend brought to the bar. In general, KillFish has created a very interesting and popular system, the bars are super democratic, not only at prices, but also according to the general mood.
