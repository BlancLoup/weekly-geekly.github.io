Bloi@Mail.Ru overtaken Livejournal by quantity, but not by activity

The number of users of online diaries in the Russian service of Blogi@Mail.Ru has reached 600 thousand. This is almost two times more than the Russian audience of the well-known blog hosting service LiveJournal.com (LJ), according to PR managers from Mail.Ru.

Now the attendance of the project Blogi@Mail.Ru is about 100 thousand people per day and more than 1.6 million per month, according to company own data. Since the start of the project, users have made over 2 million entries in their diaries.
