Conference on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence AI & BigData Lab



The GeeksLab project invites everyone on March 5 to Odessa to the AI & BigData Lab conference, which will be dedicated to one of the most popular and discussed IT topics - big data and artificial intelligence.

The conference will be held in two streams, within which developers will discuss technical issues of implementation and application in real projects of various algorithms, tools and new technologies for working with big data and artificial intelligence. The projects implemented will be presented, their functionality will be described, and also how they work inside will be shown.

Among the already confirmed reports are the following:
  • Natural Language Processing in practice (Vsevolod Demkin, Grammarly).
  • Data mining and information search: problems, algorithms, solutions (Alexander Krakovetsky, DevRain Solutions).
  • DeepLearning: complex data analysis in simple words (Sergey Shelpuk, SoftServe).
  • Parallelization methods and efficient computations in the R language (Vladislav Kolbasin, Aginity).
  • Structural Equation Modeling in the R Environment (Alexey Gaevsky).

Two two-hour workshops will be held:
  • Nikolay Pavlov on the recognition of human movement using R
  • Igor Chubin about MapReduce, big data processing in the cloud using a no-SQL database and the Hadoop platform.


Venue: HUB Odessa (ul. Grecheskaya 1a).
Time: from 9:00 to 18:30.

Participation fee: 250 UAH., With registration until February 22 - 200 UAH. For Habra members, a 10% discount code: habrahabr.

You can register and get all the details about the reports and the event on the website at: geekslab.co/events/bigdatalab
