Analysis of publications on Habrahabr for 2017. Statistics, useful finds and ratings



By the end of 2017. It's time to sum up some of the results. How was this year on Habrahabr? To answer this question, we, at Cloud4Y , decided to collect statistics on all publications over the period since the beginning of the year. In this article we will talk about what seemed to us the most interesting.

From January 1 to 18:00 on December 22, 2017, 10,684 posts were posted on Habrahabr, which have been viewed about 123 million times! We did not include into account 46 megaposts and publications that were hidden or transferred to Geektimes. On average, about 900 posts were published per month, and March was a record one.



The ratio of publications between corporate and personal blogs habrovchan for the analyzed period amounted to 51.7% to 48.3%. The share of publications in companies' blogs was the lowest in January. This is due to the long New Year holidays. The most "corporate" was May.



The average publication in 2017 gained 11,500 views and a rating of +20. At one "average" post there were 70 users who added it to the "Favorites", and 30 comments to discuss it. Posts from personal blogs lead in the number of views (+ 10%), adding to Favorites (+ 22%) and the number of comments (+ 48%).



Median values: views 7400, rating 14, add ‚ÄúTo favorites‚Äù 45, comments 12.

The graph below shows the number of views for posts that are sorted by publication date. That is, for example, from 0 to 500 is the very beginning of the year or 500 first publications. As you can see, the publications consistently gain "their" views and systematically appear "abnormally" interesting publications with 50000+ views.



The density of distribution of publications for the year fits into a log-normal distribution, which is explained by the Habrahabr voting system. We wrote more about this here and here .

It was also curious to know whether the number of selected hubs has an impact on the popularity of the article. As companies additionally add their publications to the hub ‚ÄúCompany Blog XXX‚Äù (there were 444 different in 2017), we decided to compare only articles in the personal blogs of Habrovchan. As we can see - since 2 hubs, the increase in views is very weak. Although at the level of statistical error, the situation is somewhat different for corporate blogs.
