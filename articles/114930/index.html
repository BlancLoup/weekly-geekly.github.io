LinkCharge - new service from PaySto

PaySto has launched a new financial service for freelancers and private entrepreneurs. Everyone knows that one of the main problems for a freelancer working remotely with a large company is the execution of a contract and the acceptance of non-cash money. Not every developer is capable of organizing and managing his own enterprise - and clients, as a rule, prefer to receive an account with bank details from the executor.
LinkCharge service solves exactly this problem. To create an account in LinkCharge, it is enough to know the payer's e-mail, the amount and the basis of the payment. The system automatically generates all the documents necessary for a transaction: contract, invoice, act, invoice. And the performer remains to focus on work.
LinkCharge is simple and does not require unnecessary bureaucratic procedures that make it so difficult to work with some other payment systems. Registration in the system is free, there are no hidden fees.
We hope that for many freelancers from Habr our service will be useful. Please let us know about all the comments and suggestions that you have - we will try to respond as quickly as possible.
