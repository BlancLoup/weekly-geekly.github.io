The German authorities did it again: they bought from an anonymous source a CD with a dump of a database of German citizens who have accounts in a Swiss bank. Since information is a banking secret of another state, by definition it could not be obtained legally. The source is either a hacker or a bank employee who broke the law and stole information from his employer.

The story is exactly the same as two years ago . Disk purchased from an unknown person. The cost of the disk is estimated in millions (3.5 million euros), and the affected citizens have already begun to admit to evading taxes.
