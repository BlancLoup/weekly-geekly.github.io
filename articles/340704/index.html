Artificial Intelligence Nemesida WAF



It is believed that artificial intelligence is nothing more than a marketing ploy. In the article, we will show how the artificial intelligence of Nemesida WAF can detect and block attacks on web applications with amazing accuracy of 99.96%.

Theory: the principle of work of Nemesida AI


Machine learning (pattern recognition, or, as it is now fashionable to say, artificial intelligence) is used in areas where data analysis performed by a person becomes impossible due to large amounts of information or incomplete information. In general, when we have accumulated more than a million requests to various web applications in our arsenal, the decision on the need to use machine learning has matured by itself.

Based on the analysis of scientific research and existing prototypes, the scheme of work of Nemeisda AI was constructed, and after analyzing the data, a characteristic space of elements was formed. Since most of the signs are textual, they were vectorized for further use in the recognition algorithm. Since the query fields are not separate words, and often consist of sequences of characters, it was decided to use an approach based on the n-gram frequency analysis (TF-IDF).

Analysis of the available data allowed us to form the feature space, on the basis of which the classifier was built. The task of detecting attacks from a mathematical point of view was formalized as a classical classification problem (two classes: legitimate and illegitimate traffic). The choice of algorithms was made according to the criterion of the availability of implementation and the possibility of testing. The gradient booster algorithm (AdaBoost) showed itself in the best way, which allowed for 100 million requests to provide 99.96% accuracy with a level of false positives less than 0.04%. Thus, after training, the Nemesida WAF decision is made based on the statistical properties of the analyzed data, and not on the basis of deterministic signs (signatures) of attacks.

The lack of a signature attack detection method compared to AI is obvious - a high probability of bypassing WAF with a large number of false positives (from 5% to 15%). However, signature analysis is the main source of training and provides basic protection for a web application for the period of training Nemesida AI.
