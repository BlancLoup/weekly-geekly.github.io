Solutions "Penguin Software" recommended as the basis for the creation of GMP

The Act on acceptance of work performed by Penguin Software and other participants in the project to create prototypes of the basic components of the National Software Platform (NPP) was signed. According to the technical requirements of the competition, the customer, represented by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, demanded to conduct analytical studies, prepare a financial and economic justification and project documentation for the next stages of the project, as well as develop prototypes of key program components of the future platform.

On November 2, 2011, the Ministry received 5 analytical reports, the draft design of the NPP, the terms of reference for the further development of the NPP, the report on patent research, 14 software prototypes with accompanying technical documentation of more than 5000 pages. Their evaluation and testing took the customer almost two months. The members of the competition commission were assisted in this process by industry experts, whose opinions were taken into account when rendering a positive verdict.
