Alexander Alexandrovich Rozov. Cycle "Meganesia"

Awesome young author. Strongly recommend to all lovers of fiction, utopia, alternative history (possible). This is such a fantasy that creates the future. His ideas are changing the world. Books are fascinating, exciting. This is a new wind in fiction, futurology. You will be unusual, but very interesting. I will not spoil the pleasure and retell books - read it yourself and you will understand everything.

Let me just say in a nutshell. He developed a consistent system of building a happy society for normal people. The state is abolished. The management is carried out by a contract government on the basis of a tender won from the public. All scientific technologies are allowed, including nuclear, space, and genetic. It is forbidden to prohibit under penalty of execution. For example, all aggressive religions are banned, protests after having paraded modifications of the human genome, as well as all other attempts to impose the will of limited aggressive groups on other people. Public background is summarized in the Charter. Technical, including a detailed description of all technical solutions - aviation, space, floating - are described in the novels themselves. I think it will be very interesting to everyone who is interested in small aircraft, including folding ones.

It should be noted that all this is a fantasy of the near range. Although the action takes place in the near future, all the described technologies (afaik except, perhaps, pure thermonuclear fusion in the L-bomb) already exist, and everything described can be made and used today.
