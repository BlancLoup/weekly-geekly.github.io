Report on the excursion to the largest commercial data center in Russia. Built by Megaphone (this data center will soon also become a hosting site)

In early November, Megafon-Volga region invited 15 bloggers from Samara to tour their data center (it is currently the largest among all commercial data centers in Russia). I became one of those 15. I do not work in a megaphone.

The data center was opened on October 8, 2010. The investment in the project is 900 million rubles. There is a press release , there are a number of technical characteristics. Built this data center for 2 years.

On the territory of the data center with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã6,912 square meters. m. there are 6 auto-halls with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã400 m2, electric power plants and a control center. There are 6 floors in the building (though only the 1st and 5th are working so far, plus there are no autochambers on the 5th - only meeting rooms and the Monitoring Control Center).

The exterior of this 6-story building is futuristic:


The data center is located 20 km from the city of Samara, there used to be forests there, now IKEA has been built nearby (as much as 3 years ago, but it will not be put into operation). Megaphone bought 3-4 hectares of land, and if you need to expand even more, you can attach another building next to the data center in this area.


In the data center now work 60 hours (but of course at one point in time all the 60s are not there, because someone has a day off, someone has a different shift).

The data center was built in accordance with the international standard EIA / TIA-492 "Standard for the telecommunications infrastructure of data centers." The project meets all requirements of the standard. This detached building, remote from residential buildings, is located in a non-flooded area. It provides 100% redundancy in energy supply (2 independent inputs of electricity), provides a high load-carrying capacity of floors - up to 5,000 kg / m2., A modern fire extinguishing system is in place, safe for client equipment, 24-hour security is provided. On the territory there are 2 backup diesel generators (in the form of vans). If the electricity in both channels disappears, within 90 seconds everything is powered from the batteries, and during the same 90 seconds the diesel generators go to the required power and the power supply starts to flow from these diesel generators. In general, there will be no problem with electricity anyway.

The preliminary conclusion of the Uptime Institute on the compliance of the data center with the TIER III reliability level has been received. In early 2011, the corresponding certificate will be received. Experts in the stories said that if it were not for the airport at 25 km from the data center, he would receive a fourth level of reliability, and not the third.

Center network bandwidth is up to 200 Gbit / s.

With its own large-scale transport network in the Volga region and throughout Russia, MegaFon guarantees the most reliable FOCL transmission and additional reservation of channels. Fiber-optic lines are connected by different routes, which provides additional channel redundancy in the event of a break.

The data center even has a pair of its wells for water extraction.

In general, a great place for cloud hosting.

MegaFon plans to provide collocation services (host third-party servers) in this data center. But only volumes from one rack and above (that is, one server will not be put in there). This data center is the first of the network of data centers that Megfon plans to build throughout the country.
