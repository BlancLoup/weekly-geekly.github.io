A pirate needs to score 200,000 hits on YouTube, or he faces a huge fine.

The 30-year-old Czech pirate Jakub F., who was accused by the representative of the copyright holders of the Business Software Alliance (BSA) of illegally copying and distributing software, including Microsoft Windows, got into trouble . For eight years, Yakub uploaded to various forums links to download unlicensed programs from file hosting sites.

Punishment was inevitable. BSA experts have calculated his personality - and reported to the police. After the search, they confiscated the computer, external HDD and DVDs. The case was taken to court, the hearing took place in September. The court sentenced the accident to three years conditional and confiscation of equipment. But the story did not end there. Various corporations affected by the actions of Yakub, including Microsoft, HBO, Sony Music and Twentieth Century Fox, have estimated the damage in the aggregate at $ 373,000, with Microsoft alone billing at 223,000.

Apparently, the judge was shocked by such a large amount of compensation, or he understood the obvious fact that the guy would pay with corporations for the rest of his life. One way or another, but the parties eventually concluded an agreement on alternative punishment.
