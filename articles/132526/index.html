Competition from the company Luxoft

Hello! On November 1, a contest started on the Luxoft Facebook page, the winners of which will receive a certificate for 48 hours of free training at Luxoft Training Center. The certificate is valid for six months, in any city where Luxoft Training is represented, there are no restrictions on the choice of an interesting course.

The conditions of the competition are very simple: you need to fill in an online short registration form consisting of 5 points and send a binary picture, like what was already posted on the album Upgrade your career @ Luxoft.

By the way, there is an excellent online converter that you can use, you just need to choose a picture that is more original and more interesting.

And as the audience award, the author of the picture that won the most votes will receive a closed, dynamic type headset for professional use. Sennheiser HD 380 Pro. Mega-thing, even if you're not a professional sound engineer. Participate and win!
