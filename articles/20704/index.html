Battle City: nostalgia online



This project is nothing but a manifestation of nostalgia - a kind of excursion into a distant childhood. You can play as a set of classic levels, and levels in a random order from among those that were created by you (I advise you to skip those that contain the reserved headquarters - otherwise what interest?).

Pay attention to the construction section, where you can draw your own level and place it in your blog or page. The stages section contains a couple of hundred of the most popular levels for every taste.

Have a nice game! >>
