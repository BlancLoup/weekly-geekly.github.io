Created the world's first "odor card"

Scientists from the Weizmann Institute of Israel created the world's first map of smells, which determines the place of each of them relative to the others, just as the notes are arranged in a musical scale.
Researchers expect odors to help digitize flavors in the future and even transfer them via email or mobile phone.
