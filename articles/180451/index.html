Arduino Robot: an open platform for building robots



The company Arduino announced a new family of engineering boards and released a ready-made designer Arduino Robot to assemble robots. As usual, the designer does not require soldering, is assembled without special tools. The entire system is open, easily programmable via USB and supports the connection of various peripheral devices. The central display connects to the upper control board.

The round Arduino Robot, about 10 cm high and about 19 cm in diameter, looks like a robot vacuum cleaner, but in reality these are two motherboards (control and motor), each of which runs on an ATmega32u4 microcontroller and supports the connection of various equipment.
