New Xiaomi at CES 2017

Hi, Geektimes! Behind the first half of January, and thus the New Year holidays, and CES 2017. This year, many device manufacturers have participated in the famous exhibition, and Xiaomi, which for obvious reasons deserves our special attention, was no exception. But it all happened so fast (why, Vegas is Las Vegas!), And you probably missed something. Therefore, we decided to put all the new items together and find out what really deserves attention and what can be missed.



Although Xiaomi was founded in 2010, however, until this year, it had not yet held its press conferences at CES. This time, the Chinese giant decided to take part in the event, and, as is typical of him, on a grand scale, presenting several new products at once.

Naturally, we also did not forget about CES, and even dedicated a special sale to it, you can buy dozens of products at tasty prices - from quadcopters and virtual reality helmets to laptops, routers and headphones. The exhibition is over, and the sale continues!

Another reason to buy Xiaomi Mi Mix


Let's start with not the most significant, but still interesting for many buyers - the long-awaited Xiaomi Mi Mix in white color debuted at CES 2017. His black counterpart, which, by the way, can be bought now , was introduced last fall and immediately attracted attention - still, not all manufacturers (for example, Sharp) are now trying to experiment with frameless devices. White Mi Mix has completely inherited the features of the black model - the ceramic case, the huge screen almost the entire front panel, and looks no less stylish.



The screen size of the Mi Mix is ‚Äã‚Äã6.4 inches, while the top and side frames, despite all the assurances of marketers, it still has, albeit very thin. Anyway, the screen occupies more than 90% of the front panel: as a result, the proximity sensor was replaced with ultrasonic technology, and the speaker with a special piezoelectric speaker system. Tiny by modern standards, the front camera (5 megapixels, by the way) is located under the screen, and the fingerprint scanner is on the back panel. The rear camera is 16 megapixel, has a dual LED flash and phase autofocus.



The Mi Mix display has a resolution of 2040 x 1080 pixels, a Qualcomm Snapdragon 821 is used as a processor in the smartphone, versions with 4 GB and 6 GB of RAM and 128 GB or 256 GB of internal flash memory are available to choose from. The device boasts a 4400 mAh battery and support for Quick Charge 3.0.

In general, the smartphone is interesting in every sense. Sales of Mi Mix in white should begin in the first half of 2017.



Mi Router HD - now you don't need a NAS?


Xiaomi decided not to leave without an update and Mi WiFi Router 2 - in fact, it is its improved version that is Mi Router HD. A four-antenna MIMO router with a black anodized aluminum case and a built-in 1 TB or 8 TB hard drive looks like a real beast.



Yes, and inside is not "angel." Powerful antennas with high signal range and maximum transmission speeds up to 2600 Mbps. The router allows you to set up automatic backup from connected devices (not only a computer, but also a phone), has support for cloud services, Sonos audio systems and even Time Machine - a nice bonus for owners of Mac computers. The device works with the help of the Qualcomm IPQ8064 processor.

The new router from Xiaomi will be available in the first quarter of this year at a price ranging from $ 200 to $ 500. Mi TV 4 - what was it all about?


Xiaomi, it seems, is very fascinated by the ‚Äúframelessness‚Äù - the company introduced a new representative of its line of smart TVs - Mi TV 4. The thickness of the new product is incredibly small - only 4.9 mm, and the frames around the screen are so thin that it seems that they don‚Äôt exist at all.



What is the secret? No, nothing supernatural - just resourceful Chinese took and put all the main TV hardware into a separate soundbar. And what, the last many people buy separately, why not combine business with pleasure? Moreover, the approach was already run-in on Mi TV 3, and customers liked it.


Unfortunately, the lower part of the TV turned out to be slightly thicker than 4.9 mm, otherwise it would be inconvenient to mount it to the wall. To the soundbar, which provides high-quality sound using ten built-in speakers, the Mi TV 4 connects using the Mi Port connector: the soundbar itself has three HDMI ports, 2 USB ports, Ethernet, audio system connectors (Xiaomi offers for an additional $ 500), RJ45 and , of course, a connector for the antenna.



The TV is running Android, but it has special software Xiaomi Patch Wall. It works on the principle of machine learning - it analyzes which channels and series the user watches, and she herself draws up a list of recommended programs. You no longer have to scroll through a few dozen channels from cable in search of something interesting.

The manufacturer plans to sell the TV in several versions - with a screen diagonal of 49 inches, 55 inches and 65 inches. The older version in the Chinese market will cost $ 1,500, as the younger ones will not cost. Xiaomi noted that the novelty is intended only for the domestic market.
