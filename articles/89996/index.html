Restaurant 2.0

Welcome to the restaurant 2.0!



Choose a table and make yourself comfortable, now the waiter will bring you a menu.

A waiter approaches you, greets and puts a tablet computer on the table.



You scroll through the pages, mark the dishes you like, see what you order most often, find out that Chardonnay or Beaujolais are recommended with salmon steak.

You can even watch a video of how to prepare your dish, and your girlfriend can calculate the calorie content of this dinner with one touch on the menu screen.

Yes, I forgot to say, the waiter recognized you, and entered your name in the menu. You are logged in. Now this is your personal menu, which remembers that you never order a sweet, but you really like it spicy.
