What is Workle


As mentioned above, Workle is a service for online work. By creating remote jobs, the project allows Internet users to become online employees of various Russian companies. We are talking about a variety of positions: from real estate expert to technical support employee and hr-specialist. At the initial stage, the professions of an insurance expert and a financial expert are available.

Through the Internet passes both the work itself and the receipt of rewards (on a bank card). Each Workle user can regularly attend free online training courses, develop as a professional and increase their income through career growth. Also, as the project develops, users will have the opportunity to create leagues (in fact, mini-companies) and join together to work together.

Read more on the project website .
