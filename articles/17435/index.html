Common polemics

Last time you start to think about Habrahabr, about its development, the next life. Perhaps such thoughts are inspired by the expectation of the next version of the resource, perhaps the fact that the quality of materials is steadily falling, in my opinion.

Apparently, not one I think about the fate of the resource: CurlyBrace created a theme for friends , in which he shared his thoughts on the latest changes within the community. It should be noted that, unlike all the public topics created on the resource, this one was distinguished by a favorable atmosphere: no fierce disputes, wars for pros / cons / karma - only a healthy polemics of the participants. Surely I would like to see such an atmosphere in public topics of the community. I want to say thanks to all the participants in the discussion of Habrahabr and ideas for its development or transformation.

It is also worth noting that such a discussion led to the creation of a powerman on the topic of distributed authoring mash-up .

Your interesting thoughts and observations on the indicated events are welcomed.
