Sudoku is a game familiar to almost everyone. To some extent, even already hackneyed, podnadoevshaya. I want to refresh it a little, add new emotions to the process of solving it. But can this be done? Is it possible to pour a fresh stream into the familiarly measured flow of the Japanese puzzle without changing the rules?
Well, let me try ...

Some numbers seem to be asking for their place, some are very difficult to pick up, and you will think for a long time what to do next. The case moves, each new figure brings you closer to a clue.
