Borton DSC-MP-03 - First Ukrainian telephone

In Ukraine, the issue of the appearance of the Borton DSC-MP-03 phone, which is assembled directly in Kharkov by Ukrainian engineers, is being actively discussed.

Today, there are no domestically produced mobile phones in the mass sale, and the appearance of devices, the creation of which is in any way connected with Ukrainian specialists, is of great interest to users. Kharkiv company BORTON is targeting the demanded segment of DualSIM devices and, admittedly, its first product really deserves attention.

The first review of the Borton DSC-MP-03 apparatus has already appeared. The author expresses his positive attitude to this model, and calls it very successful. Let's see what kind of demand Borton will receive in retail sales.
