Exactly one month before the IT festival Tabtabus, are you with us?



On June 18-19, programmers, designers, technologists, managers, businessmen and other IT-representatives from Russia and the near abroad will gather on the banks of Sapsho.

By tradition, the third summer IT festival Tabtabus Summer Fest will be held on the banks of Lake Sapsho in the Smolensk Lakeland National Park. Guests and participants of the open-air will be able to get a powerful charge of vivacity and new ideas, relax in nature, share experiences of interesting projects and cook the most delicious fish soup.

What awaits the participants of the festival?


  • TED-format speeches and reports on trends in the IT-sphere . We will talk about how the desktop of our computer will be in five years, learn about gamification at work and in life, talk about uberization, virtual reality and much more. Speakers include: Andrei Sharkov, a successful entrepreneur, founder of the Shokoboks company, author and presenter of the popular podcast ‚ÄúFight and Do‚Äù; Aleksey Yozhikov is an expert, business consultant in the field of Internet marketing, director for development of a group of RUWARD projects and others.
  • Corporate sport and recreation on the Sapsho shore . In addition to the business part, you will find a large entertainment and sports program. In addition, not far from the festival site is the beach of the beautiful Lake Sapsho, so do not forget the swimsuits and towels!
  • Live music from the best IT teams from Smolensk and St. Petersburg . And if you yourself love and know how to play the guitar, take it with you - after completing the music program, you can sit around the fire with friends and colleagues, remember your favorite songs and melodies.
  • Overnight in tents in the national park . What could be better? Clean air, pine forest, beautiful scenery! Not far from the scene Tabtabus, you can set up a campground and come up with an original design.
  • Ear - to all participants . By tradition, everyone who comes to the festival will receive a delicious fish soup cooked by Uncle Misha according to a special recipe.

How to become a member of Tabtabus Summer Fest?


From Smolensk to the venue of the festival will be organized transfer (there and back). All guests of the summer open-air will be provided with a place for tents, fire, firewood, business, sports and music program. For participants from the cities of Russia and neighboring countries will be held a free tour of the city.

You can register for the Tabtabus Summer Fest on fest.tabtabus.ru . Come and share your ear with friends!

Under the Tabtabus brand, there are two of the largest IT events in the Smolensk region - the autumn conference and the summer festival, which unite representatives of small business and developers of the region.
