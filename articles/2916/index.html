Web standards: WaSP announced the creation of an International Contact Group

The oldest and most respected web standardist project ( WaSP ), bringing together prominent web developers, launched an initiative to create the WaSP International Contact Group (WaSP International Liaison Group - ILG).


ILG mission

The international contact group at WaSP (WaSP International Liaison Group - ILG) is an international team of web professionals who promote the widespread use of standards to build a fair Web.
