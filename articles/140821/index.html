Part 4. Line. First impressions of listening


Special mention deserves the first impression of listening to live calls.
In short, it looks like this. There is an employee of the company who at this moment simply works in the line in the contact center. Calls, in my opinion, did not represent anything special. People call in fact with trivial problems. The most interesting for me was a call from Novosibirsk, where LTE test operation was already underway. At that time it was the only city in Russia where this technology worked.

I can not say what the impression will be of those who have never worked in technical support. I already had 11 years of experience in administering Windows-based networks on workstations and servers.

Part 5. Equipment and programs you work with as a home agent


The technical part is implemented quite skillfully according to the following scheme:

In the office there is a server to which the SIP phone (Yealink) is connected via a channel provided by the home provider. A headset is issued to the phone. From your computer you connect to a remote desktop implemented on Citrix and XenDesktop.
Databases are implemented on Oracle, MS Sharepoint and own internal development CRM. Mail - on Exchange.
