At the party in the club will call Mixd from Yahoo

Yahoo has opened a new interactive service Mixd . It represents something like a mobile social network focused on young people, actively using the multimedia capabilities of their mobile phones, reports Kompyulenta .

However, unlike such classical representatives of the genre as MySpace , the Mixd service does not offer the user to create their own profile and friendliness. According to the creators, he should become an indispensable assistant in the mobile organization of various parties, trips to clubs, to study, football and other meetings with friends. Using the service, you can form groups of users, exchange text messages and pictures with each other, both from a computer and from a mobile phone. Log of correspondence of all participants is displayed on the corresponding web page. For these messages, there is no additional charge that is already set by the operator.
