On March 15, a webinar will be held on the peculiarities of the payment business and Internet acquiring in Europe

On Wednesday, March 15, at 16:00 Moscow time, an international webinar will be held, dedicated to the peculiarities of creating a payment business based on a European licensed company and Internet acquiring features. The team of our payment provider PayOnline will act as a co-organizer of the event.

A little background. In recent years, the creation of a licensed Payment institution and E-money institution in Europe is acquiring increasing interest. Why in Europe? Back in 2009, a directive regulating the activities of payment operators PSD, 2007/64 / EC came into force.

Over these 8 years, European regulators have accumulated sufficient experience with licensed companies Payment institution and E-money institution, and banks and international payment systems have understood the specifics of such clients.

A company that has one of the above types of licenses can provide the following services to customers:

  • Open and maintain electronic money accounts / payment accounts of their clients - legal entities and individuals (including with the assignment of IBAN);
  • To issue financial instruments, including prepaid and debit bank cards;
  • Make money transfers without opening an account (remittance);
  • Accept payments for the benefit of your customers in various ways, including payments with bank cards, transfers, SWIFT / SEPA payments, etc.

The webinar will also consider the features of the functioning of Internet acquiring in Europe, how pricing takes place, work with banks and much more.

All performances are based on the real practice of Russian and international companies. These cases can serve as an example for the development and your business.
