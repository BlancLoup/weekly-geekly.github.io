HTC's dream



According to " sources close to the company ", on September 17 of this year, HTC plans to release the first production smartphone based on the Google Android operating system. The device will receive the name HTC Dream (codename G1).

The smartphone will be able to work in third generation networks (UMTS, HSDPA). In addition, it will be equipped with a sliding qwerty-keyboard and a camera of 3 megapixels, as well as, perhaps, the module A-GPS. The display of the smartphone is touch, with a diagonal of 3.5 ".

It is assumed that the smartphone will be sold with a two-year T-Mobile contract for $ 150, or without it, but for $ 399. Representatives of T-Mobile, Google and HTC did not comment on this information. However, earlier one of the leaders of HTC said that the device is running the mobile operating system Google Android will go on sale before the end of this year.

In a word, another iPhone killer may appear.
