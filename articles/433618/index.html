What is common between the lamp and art?


Lamp technology is a very unusual direction for electronics today. Circuit design of tube amplifiers is much simpler than transistor, but this apparent simplicity. A feature of tube amplifiers is to work with a path with a minimum level of feedback. On the one hand, this reduces the distortion characteristic of transistor technology caused by the presence of deep environmental protection (more details can be found in my article two years ago ), which manufacturers of semiconductor ULF now skillfully camouflage with a well-chosen set of parameters that look beautifully in the operating instructions, but are not always strongly correlated with fidelity real sound signal. On the other hand, tube amplifiers are more prone to self-excitation, and a lot depends on the characteristics of the components used, the method of their installation, the relative position of parts and connecting wires.

Two lamps with the same marking and even of the same manufacturer may have significantly different characteristics. As a result, to achieve stable quality work, careful selection of the values ‚Äã‚Äãof passive components and individual setting of the operating modes of each amplification stage are required.

In addition, the ‚Äúsound‚Äù of the amplifier can strongly depend on the type of passive elements used - capacitors, resistors, winding products, since parasitic elements play a large role. Very significant and contradictory requirements are imposed on matching transformers, especially output ones. Under the conditions of high-resistance and high-voltage circuits, noise noise, parasitic capacitances of the lamps themselves, passive components and installation become a serious factor. As a result, without understanding the essence of the processes taking place in the amplifier, it is very difficult to hope for success even repeating a successful scheme that has been proven over the years. All this is an ideal environment for the emergence and spread of numerous myths on which fraudsters of different levels and parasites successfully parasitize.

The process of setting up the amplifier sometimes acquires magical features. In many respects thanks to this, not only the nostalgic ‚Äúold men‚Äù were drawn to the ‚Äúwarm tube sound,‚Äù but also the younger generation, tired of virtuality and numbers. Everyone wants to feel like a little wizard.

What now?


So, we found out that there is a demand in the market for lamp-based ULFs, and there is a growing one. However, the market itself is already divided into segments, in each of which there is a rather intense competition.

Salons for the sale of expensive devices HiFi level of factory production. In the best of them, selling becomes a glamorous process. You can listen to the sound of the device you are going to buy. Get advice from the seller on the connection and the required additional accessories. Finally, take the chosen piece home to audition with the guarantee that if the sound is not liked, the product can be returned without explanation. The level of service is the highest, as well as the prices. The profit margin is also great. However, the opening and promotion of such a salon requires huge investments, and the demand may go down, following the leaching of the middle class.

The cheap segment of ready-made devices is represented mainly by Aliexpress. However, quite cheap it already does not turn to call the language. Buying a finished device in this market is fraught with great risks. The sound of tube amplifiers very much depends on the quality of the assembly and tuning, in which there are a lot of manual operations, and this is just what the Chinese manage to do the worst. In addition, they simply have blood in their desire to replace components with cheaper ones. As a result, having saved a couple of dollars on the resistances, they can easily ditch the sound of the ULF for a few hundred dollars, that the purchase will lose all meaning.

In addition, lamp devices are distinguished by a solid weight. As a result, their delivery costs a lot of money, and special requirements are imposed on the quality of packaging. Return such a product is almost impossible. To understand for what reason your hopes were not justified, and all the more so it is impossible to correct mistakes without rich experience.

Those who want to save even more and / and do something with their own hands designers are buying. The easiest option for involving newbies in the design of lamp technology is, of course, the sale of simple starting designers. First you need to select some of the most easily reproducible models. Main concept


Base room - workshop. It contains workplaces equipped with soldering equipment, devices for resistance welding, measuring equipment, a winding machine, etc.



I would especially like to note the need to access a set of expensive active and passive components and accessories. One of the serious problems of beginner designers of amplifiers is the selection of components that are optimal for each specific case, for example, the price of capacitors of the same capacity may differ by orders of magnitude, depending on the material of the dielectric and the quality of performance. Buying expensive components in the store, and especially on the Internet, a person takes great risks. There is no guarantee that by spending 10 times more money he can significantly improve the sound quality. Due to the wide choice of components for testing, club members can choose the appropriate option for them.

Club members are not obliged to buy the available designers, they can bring to work out their own amplifier or a kit to buy in another place and build it to perfection. The most experienced members using the facilities and equipment of the club can do the development and assembly of their own models from scratch.

In the second room is the basic equipment for machining and painting. Cheap, but decent quality models with manual control. Drilling and milling machines with coordinate tables, napped, hand tools. As for more complex operations that require expensive equipment and high qualification for handling it (for example, manufacturing a chassis), it is easier to outsource them, establishing relationships with long-term partners with selected performers. Club members should provide support in the development of documentation for their production and place orders on a centralized basis.
If the dimensions allow, a separate room can be allocated for the assembly and refinement of acoustic systems.

A storage room is also needed for equipment storage, where each club member can rent his own cell in which his child will be stored.

A separate room must be allocated to listen to the collected equipment, it can also be used as a place for holding master classes.
