I want a video on my site!


Already, according to CISCO, video traffic accounts for more than 50% of all Internet traffic. Users love to watch videos: trailers, product reviews, news, etc ... This is understood by the owners of commercial sites. To increase customer loyalty and increase sales, they often order videos on their website.

The trouble is that creating a video is very difficult. You need to write a script, find an artist, coordinate graphics and animation, make voice acting and many other things. This is a very, very difficult and painstaking work for the customer. But there is a brilliantly simple solution that owners of a huge number of sites will appreciate. And it can make every one of you ...

Promotional video by template. Brilliant and easy!


Making unique videos for each client is long and difficult. It is difficult for both the client and the performer. But if you make a template solution that can only be slightly modified for a specific client, you can save up to 90% of the time and effort.

How does it look in practice? A full-fledged video or its template elements (background, screensaver, etc.) are created in advance. And then, under each client, the logo of his company is inserted in the right place, the necessary inscriptions are made or the right voice is made. If the video assumes the presence of the narrator in the frame, then each time the narrator is simply re-recorded and inserted into the finished template background.
As a result, the production of the video takes 10-20 minutes.
