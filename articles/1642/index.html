"MegaFon-Moscow" calculated and awarded "traffic eaters"

The cellular operator Sonic Duo ( MegaFon-Moscow ) presented valuable prizes to record holders in terms of service consumption.

The most sociable subscriber was a Muscovite named Olga, who in January-July of this year spoke over 25 thousand minutes on her mobile phone. At the same time, according to Prime-Tass , the average length of a call to one subscriber per month (MOU) is about 400 minutes for the MegaFon-Moscow network. In the record holder, this value is about 3.5 thousand minutes.

A record was also recorded in the volume of mobile calls in roaming, which belongs to a manager named Andrew - the total duration of his calls in other cities and countries for 7 months of 2006 exceeded 6.5 thousand minutes.

The record holder by SMS sent about 15 thousand short messages in January-July of this year.
