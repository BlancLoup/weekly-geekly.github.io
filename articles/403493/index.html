This company has developed a smart mini-machine for throwing balls to dogs. An excellent device, especially suitable for busy owners, who hardly have free time.

There are three models:

iFetch - suitable for little four-legged buddies. This model throws miniature balls (1.5 "). You can adjust the distance yourself - 3, 6 or 9 meters.

iFetch Too is a larger starting machine designed for medium to large dogs. In addition to the size of the balls (2.5 "), the launch distance also increases - you can set 3.7.12 and up meters, depending on the size of the pet.

The device, which was invented by Ukrainian entrepreneurs and pet lovers. It is like a lifeline for the owner, who very rarely happens next to his little friend. The company produces several models, each with its own purpose.
