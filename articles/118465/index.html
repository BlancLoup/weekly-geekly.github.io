Presented by the official LiveJournal application for Android



"Soup" introduced the LiveJournal application for Android, allowing you to:

  • post entries in your own journal and communities;
  • publish images;
  • create and publish polls;
  • edit your last entry;
  • create and edit drafts;
  • manage the settings for creating records.

When publishing records, you can determine your location and specify the coordinates.

It is noteworthy that through this application you can not even read the tape of friends. This opportunity is promised to be added in the next version.

The application is available in the Android Market for users of Android 1.6 and higher.
