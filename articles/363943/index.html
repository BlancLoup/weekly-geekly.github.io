Wire: messenger from developers from Skype, Microsoft and Apple

What happens if the developers of Skype, Microsoft, Apple and Nokia get together? As it turned out, nothing special. They gathered and created another messenger.



Mobile industry and techno-media today shook the news about the release of a new program called Wire. The application, which is a cross-platform messenger, appeared immediately on all major platforms - in the form of applications for iOS, Mac OS and Android. And, perhaps, few people would notice its appearance (think, one more, one less) - there are a great many diverse messengers now, if it were not for the team of its developers, which without exaggeration can be called stellar.



It is headed by Janus Friis, who stood at the origins of the popular communication platform, which is used today by millions. Another co-founder, Jonathan Christensen, developed MSN Messenger at one time, and CTO Alan Durik is known for his work at Camino Networks and Telio.
