Game developers will gather in Dnepropetrovsk on Ciklum Game Developers Saturday

In our offices throughout Ukraine and in Belarus , Ciklum Saturdays is constantly being held, gathering developers for various technologies.

We are glad to announce that the next Ciklum Saturday will be devoted to the development of games and everything connected with it!

Ciklum Game Dev Saturday will be held in Dnepropetrovsk and promises to be colorful, versatile and interesting, and the topics are fascinating even to those who, by the nature of their activities, have not yet come across the subject of conversation.

The list of reports and the schedule is currently being formed. So, the following speakers announced their participation in Ciklum Game Dev Saturday :
  • Oleg Akimov (Lugansk) - “2D effects. Quick and easy. ”
  • Denis Romanko (Kharkiv) - “What makes players replay games several times?”
  • Valeria Mallaeva (Donetsk) - "Features and differences of flash-animation for games and cartoons"
  • Pavel Yuriychuk (Kiev) - “SVG in Game Development: To Be or Not To Be”
  • Igor Khotin (Kiev) - “The Dark Side of the Force - Backstage MMO Games”
  • Ivan Bely (Dnepropetrovsk) - "Development of cross-platform games for mobile devices"
  • Vladimir Kozhaev (Kiev) - “ Do-it-yourself isometry”
  • Vitaliy Khit (Dnepropetrovsk) - “Creating a game from 'A' to 'Z'”

Stay tuned to our blog to keep up to date.

So, we invite all game development enthusiasts to our office in Dnepropetrovsk on Ciklum Game Dev Dev Saturday :
When : Saturday, September 24, from 11:00 to 18:00
Where : Dnipropetrovsk, st. Barrikadnaya, 15-A (business center "Steps"), of. ciklum

No more than a day on Ciklum GamDev Saturday gathered more than a hundred people interested in the development of games! We are grateful to all registered members. Since the number of seats is limited, we were forced to close the registration . A few days before the event, you will receive information on the time, place and schedule of the event via e-mail.

It is very easy to become another speaker - write to this address , tell a little about yourself and suggest your topic.

UPD:

Hooray! This happened!
On Saturday, September 24, GameDevSaturday was held at Ciklum in Dnepropetrovsk
We can say that the event was held under the motto: "Many speakers are good and different!".

In the final agenda were 9 reports for every taste and color:

1. Valeria {Lerika} Mallaeva: “Specifics of flash animation: Games vs Cartoons”;
2. Vitaliy Khit: “ABC of Game Development”;
3. Denis Romanko with his report “What increases game replay”;
4. Pavel Yuriychuk: “SVG in game development: to be or not to be”;
5. Oleg Akimov: “2D effects. It's smooth and easy;
6. Igor Hotin: “Dark side of the Force - server backstage of MMO games”;
7. Vladimir Kozhayev revealed secrets in his report “Hand-made isometry”;
8. Andrei Murashko, who shared his experience on the topic “TV Games: Swap your keyboard to remote”;
9. Sam Glegg with the report “Marmalade: Cross-platform game development for mobile devices”.

The variety of topics attracted about 60 participants, completely different in specialization, but equally indifferent to the development of games: programmers, modelers, animators, designers and producers.

Reports, passing into the discussion over a cup of coffee, lasted almost until 7 pm, and after the guys moved to an even more unresponsive environment, to get to know each other, talk or just pass the time to the train.

And after the event, it remains for the small, post a video, share the photo and ... Prepare for the next work day!
