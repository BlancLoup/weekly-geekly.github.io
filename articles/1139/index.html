Currently, VimpelCom is one of the largest providers of cellular communication services for state authorities and government agencies of the Russian Federation. The company serves the presidential administration and the government of the Russian Federation, as well as special services, including the Federal Guard Service. "VimpelCom" serves its subscribers even on the territory of the country residence of the President of the Russian Federation "Rus" in Zavidovo, the newspaper writes.
