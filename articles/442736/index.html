6 board games for upgrading English



Gamification of training is a great way to increase student motivation and improve the results.

The reason is simple - games help to maintain a stable and high interest in the subject. And there are included the mechanisms of memorization and associations. Did you ever notice that the lyrics of a song that you liked do not even need to be taught? He is remembered as if by himself after a few auditions.

If you delve into psychology, then interesting information immediately creates a stable chain of associations with already existing knowledge. And the more such associations, the faster the information will go to the long-term memory.

Today we will talk about the whole section of games that are undeservedly forgotten - board games. And tell you what nastolki help you to improve your English skills.

All these desktops can be purchased in online stores without any problems. Ready? Go!

Scrabble




It would be very strange to talk about the desktops for pumping the English language and not to mention Scrabble.

Scrabble is a legend of board games, which combines extremely simple rules and fascination. Naturally, we need the English version of the game.

The complexity of the rules. Low
Level strategy Average
Trains skills: Mindfulness, vocabulary, anagram compilation, search for the optimal strategy.

rules

Players are issued with 7 chips with letters. Players must place their chips on a 15x15 field in such a way that words are formed in the manner of a crossword puzzle. That is, you can only read words vertically and horizontally.

Each letter has its own value. And the less often it is used in words, the more points you get when using it. For example, "A" or "C" are only 1 point, "F" - 4 points, and "Q" - as many as 10 points. Points from all used letters are counted and recorded.

The game is up to a certain number of points. Who first achieved - and he won.

Benefit

Scrabble is great for working out vocabulary in English. Actually, the more words you know, the higher your chances of winning. You can check the presence of a word in any English dictionary. It is better, of course, to use online dictionaries - for example, Oxford Dictionaries . Total allowed words about 260,000 pieces.

If you do not know the word that your opponent posted, take a few minutes and see its meaning. So you can during the game to replenish your vocabulary.

Once upon time




In Russian, she came out under the title "In a certain kingdom," which seemed to hint at the meaning of entertainment. The task of the players is to tell a fairy tale or a fantastic story with the help of special cards.

The complexity of the rules: low
Strategy level: low
He trains skills: listening comprehension, sentence formation, language coherence, storytelling, attentiveness, logic.

rules

Each player has a set of cards with characters, locations, properties and actions, as well as one card ending.

Players need to tell a fairy tale using their own cards. In this case, it is imperative that each card is important for the advancement of the plot.

If the opponent in front of you becomes silent, gets lost or contradicts the story already told, you can intercept the move and start telling yourself.

The game depends on the "talkative" players. It is clear that in English it will be difficult at first to compose a fairy tale, but these are only the first few games.

To draw time and come up with the next plot twist, you can add colorful descriptions, dialogues, or any interesting trivia to the tale. The tale will only benefit from this, and you will win even more from such a training.

It is important not only to tell the story yourself, but also to listen to others. Therefore, ‚ÄúOnce Upon A Time‚Äù is great for people with approximately the same level of language proficiency.

Benefit

Creating a story on the go allows you to develop the beauty, coherence and logic of speech. You need not only to use your vocabulary to create a story, but also to link the story with the cards that you have in your hands.

Here you need to talk as much as possible. After all, too much pause will lead to the interception of the course. And over time, the quantity goes into quality, telling the story becomes easier. And you can speak fluently in English about anything and for as long as you want.

A great game in which you need to explain your team a word, without naming himself or any other words that are directly related to him.

The complexity of the rules: low
Strategy level: low

Everything is extremely simple. You need to explain as many words as possible in one minute. The word itself, of course, can not be called. But you cannot also call other words that are directly related to it - they are written in the ‚ÄúTaboo‚Äù block.

Mad gab




Extremely interesting phonetic game in which you need to guess the phrase by sound.

The complexity of the rules: low
Strategy level: low
Trains skills: listening comprehension, vocabulary usage, phonetic associations.

rules

Before the start of the round, the referee chooses several cards. They are bilateral. On one side is written a meaningless set of words, and on the other - a popular phrase that phonetically resembles a set of words on the first side.
For example: ‚ÄúHeaven Iced Hey‚Äù - ‚ÄúHave a nice day‚Äù.
The task of the first player is to speak a set of words in such a way as to guess the phrase-answer.

The second player knows the answer and tells the first exactly how to say the phrase. He can give hints about speed, intonation, accents, but he cannot suggest the meaning of the phrase in any other way.

It is necessary to guess as many phrases as possible in the allotted amount of time. Most often in 3 minutes.

Benefit

‚ÄúMad Gab‚Äù is just a great game to practice listening to English. If you learn to play it well, then even the accent of the Indians or the Chinese will not be difficult.

The game also includes an associative array well, because the sounds of the riddle and the phrase-response are very similar. Often the answers are daily phrases that are used often - the more interesting their search will be.

Apples to Apples




A simple card game for a company whose goal is to choose the word that best matches the main card. A game of association and communication between rivals.

The complexity of the rules: low
Strategy level: low
Trains skills: vocabulary, associative, speed of thinking.

rules

The rules are a bit like ‚ÄúSounds like a plan‚Äù, but you need to select a description for a specific word or description.

Each player has a certain number of cards from the red deck. The leader of the round chooses one card with the word from the green deck - it becomes the main one.

The task of the players is to select a card with the word from their hands that will most closely match the main card.
For example, the green card on the table is ‚Äúcute.‚Äù In your hand cards: "lawn mower", "clown", "bottle", "sport" and "kitten". Naturally, the most appropriate word would be "kitten".
After all the players have put their cards, the presenter chooses the most appropriate one. The choice is always subjective, so players must also take into account the possible reaction of the lead.

The player who chooses the right word picks up the green card itself. The one who first collects a certain number of green cards wins the game.

Benefit

Apples to Apples coaches vocabulary well. Most of the words written on the cards, quite common. And if you consider that the number of cards in the game is from 200 to 400 (depending on the version), then this is a very good way to learn a few hundred new words or to fix already learned ones.
