e-gold raids users

The founder of the Gold & Silver Reserve Company (G & SR), which owns the electronic payment system e-gold , is tired of the constant accusations by the authorities, who claim that the resource is a refuge for thieves, terrorists and other villains.

Wanting to prove to himself and those around him that everything is actually not so bad, Douglas Jackson (Douglas Jackson) decided to organize a raid on e-gold users and was rather surprised to learn about a lot of new things. As a result of this action, about 2000 accounts were blocked and the president of G & SR is not going to stop there. It is worth noting that it will take a long time to look for villains, since every day about a thousand users register in the e-gold system, and the number of transactions per day ranges from 50 to 100 thousand. This is reported by Wired.com .
