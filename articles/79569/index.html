MyShows.ru - TV shows that I watch



Hello, dear habravchane! Because of us, there has recently been one more site on the Internet.

MyShows.ru is a new service for fans of Dexter, House, The Big Bang Theories and other equally interesting TV shows. Now every serialomaniac will be able to mark those series that have already looked, as well as receive timely information about the latest releases.

At the moment, we are 440 users. Together we killed 39 years and 3 years on TV shows 3 months 24 days 15 hours 57 minutes . And we are not going to stop! :-)

The service allows you to:
  • Make your list of TV shows, put a rating;
  • Mark scanned series;
  • Learn about the release date of the next series (original);
  • Find out what you have left to see;
  • View time spent statistics;
  • Get a link to a public profile or userbar (link in the time spent);
  • View friends' news (what they recently saw);
  • See the total statistics and rating of TV shows ;
  • See the link in the profile to the torrent (some TV series) of the new series.
We offer you to register and see everything with your own eyes.
