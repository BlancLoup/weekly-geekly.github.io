Paradise for the gamer - a game room for 30 thousand



There are a lot of gamers among representatives of the habrasoobshchestvo, and there are probably plenty of hardcore gamers too. At one time I spent a lot of hours on computer and video games, and of course, I wanted to play comfortably. Ordinary people buy a comfortable chair, a large TV (in the case of video games) or a powerful PC (for computer games) and play. Well, geeks who play for hours go on. They buy all sorts of bells and whistles for a more convenient and comfortable game (such as gloves, special mice and other things). The gamer, which will be discussed below, went even further - he set up a game room for himself, spending 30 thousand dollars on it.

Three gaming PCs are installed in this room (the configuration of each of them includes Intel Core i5 processors and an AMD Radeon HD 7770 graphics adapter). Also installed Synology NAS with 30 terabytes of file space (10 disks of 3 TB each). Also for each PC purchased its own gaming keyboard, Logitech G9, G13 and G9x. A 42-inch TV set is installed, as well as a water-cooled system unit equipped with a 6-core processor with an EVGA GeForce GTX 580 Hydro Copper Tri SLI graphics system.

And yes, this is only one side of the room. In another street, you can see the Fanatec Racing simulator with three displays (30 inches each), with the GTX 690 graphics system and various add-ons.

In general, the room is really a paradise for a gamer. It would be interesting to know how much this person spends time on the game.
