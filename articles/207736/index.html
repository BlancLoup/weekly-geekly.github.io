Apple patents a computer with wireless charging and a projector instead of a monitor

As Mashable writes , it will be something like a Mac mini, with only a projector on one side and completely wireless: induction charging will be responsible for the power supply, wireless protocols will be responsible for connecting to the periphery.



A projector instead of a physical screen gives more freedom and flexibility in work: the image can be output in the size that the user needs, and not limited to the screen. Mashable suggests that this may be possible for future MacBooks, but it seems to me that for a mobile device, dependence on surfaces for screen projection is too inconvenient, so this is more of a concept of a more flexible home / work computer.
