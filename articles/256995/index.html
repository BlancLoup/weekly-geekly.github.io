Vivaldi Technologies - Straight Talk

Hello!

Yesterday we released the next Technical Preview version of our browser , after which we received many technical questions. In order not to be dispersed by the comments, we decided to offer you a bit of direct communication: you are our users, and we are the developers of the Vivaldi browser . Communicating with your users is a good tradition, which since the days of Opera has helped us better understand each other. In the comments to this post, you can leave your questions that interest you, and in a week or two a new post will be published on Habr√© - already with the answers directly from the developers. Perhaps, one person will be able to answer all the questions, or there will be several respondents depending on the specifics of the questions - this will be decided along the way. The main thing is that we learn a little more about each other and about the browser that we create together with you.

We would also be happy to offer you a lively dialogue online, but here you need to take into account that you will have to communicate in English. If most of you have this option, we will be happy to organize such a chat, and if you like this way of communication, we will hold such events on a regular basis, for example, once a month. Attached to this post is a poll where you can vote for or against the chat. According to the survey results, we will build further plans.

For now - write your questions in the comments, we will be happy to answer them.

Vivaldi Team
