Skype launches video calls on iPhone

Skype for iOS has been updated to version 3.0 , in which video calls have appeared. The iOS client supports video communication with Windows and Linux clients, so you can also make calls on a PC. Versions for Android and other mobile platforms Skype promises to tighten in 2011, this is a priority. There is even information that at CES there will be some good news in a few days.

The service is free and works via WiFi or 3G. Given the popularity of Skype, you can almost certainly bury Apple's FaceTime.
