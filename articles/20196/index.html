Check the strength of your friendship

We all know the proverb "Do not have a hundred rubles, but have a hundred friends," but few have tried to justify it mathematically. It would seem that there is to prove - each of the friends just has to give at least 1p. But here the laws of social relations and interpersonal relations come into effect: some may not have such a sum on hand, others will be inaccessible, and still others, on the contrary, will be able to give as many as 5.

More than one social network has not tried to turn strong or weak ties between its members into some kind of monetary equivalent, or at least try to evaluate them. But ... meet: the first financial social network: Kazulya .
