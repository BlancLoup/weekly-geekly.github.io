Don't panic! The main thing to take a towel.

Today, May 25th is the international day of towels, celebrated each as a memorial day for the famous British writer, the author of comic fantasy works, Douglas Adams . This day was first celebrated in 2001, two weeks after his death on 11 May. On this day, fans of his work carry a towel. ( wiki )

Leaving today from home, on the way to work or just for a walk - do not forget the towel, it will probably come in handy for you, who knows, the ships of the Galactic Department of Hypercosmic Planning may already be nearby and you may have to travel around the worlds. Well, if not, then today it will probably rain again, and a towel is great to dry your head :)
