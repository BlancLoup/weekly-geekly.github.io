Characteristics of tablets-2011

The CES-2011 (Consumer Electronic Show) exhibition will officially begin only on January 6, but the authors of Engadget have already learned a lot of details about the devices that will be presented there. Among them are 3D-TVs, android phones, laptops with Sandy Bridge processors - but most of all tablet computers, and it looks like 2011 will be the year of their final victory. Under the cut - a consolidated text with information published by Engadget about tablets, which will go on sale this year.
