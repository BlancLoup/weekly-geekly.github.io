Broadcaster for MacBook. After f8

The recent Facebook F8 conference has made some adjustments to our development plan. The opening of the Live API excited the mind, did not give rest, and we decided to act ...


Our system already has the ability to stream stream to YouTube. This is when with a couple of clicks your live stream from our servers is duplicated to another site. The idea is simple: one stream - many sites.
Next in line was the Periscope. For him, at least, there is an unofficial reverse API, we thought, and began to experiment.
But, as you probably heard, the Live API for Facebook was open on the F8, and so of course we immediately rushed to implement the re-stream to it.
