Simplicio icon set

Smashing Magazine, together with the designer Neurovit, offer a free set of Simplicio icons for download:



All icons are available in 32 * 32, 64 * 64, 128 * 128, 16 * 16 (ICO) formats. Also included is the source (.ai) of all icons.

By the way, the model of providing such high-quality icons for free is interesting. It's simple: Smashing Magazine announced that it promotes (100,000 subscribers, regular outputs to those on digg.com) anyone who sends them enough high-quality free material. As you understand, such a person will not remain without work for the next year. The scheme works, and everyone is the most satisfied - most of all, we consumers :)
