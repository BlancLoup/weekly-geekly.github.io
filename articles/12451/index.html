Electronic clock with OLED display

The Chinese company " Shenzhen Cailiang Electronics " has released a "fully electronic" watch ADX18-A


It would seem that nothing unusual - typical quartz watches, and even watches with flash players have already appeared quite a lot . But the main difference of this device is that it uses an OLED display as a dial.


The gadget can play music in several formats and display images (including video).

Specifications:
Display: 1.5 ", 260K OLED true color, resolution 128 * 128 pixels.
Time: 12 and 24-hour format, dates in formats from M / D / Y to Y / M / D
Flash memory: built-in, 1 or 2Gb
Supported media formats: MP3, WMA, MP4 SMV
Image viewing options: JPEG support, icon viewing mode, slide show, manually.
Recording: Digital sound recording, the format varies within 256-320 Kps, WMA: 32Kbps - 380Kbps.
Output frequencies: 20Hz - 20KHz, SNR:> = 88dB
5 equalizer modes, super bass 3D sound effect
