Census of Kiev's potential coworkers


In connection with the growing hype around coworking as such and the imminent appearance of the first coworking centers in Kiev, it would be interesting to meet your potential neighbors in the workplace in absentia. For this reason, before organizing the first informal meeting offline, in my opinion, it would not be bad to estimate the required number of seats :)
I propose the following format for the so-called census of the future "odnstolchan":
1. Me: designer / programmer / SEO-person / owner of a startup /, etc.
2. I plan to work in the coworking center: at the VIP-table / at the table for one / at the common table / on the couch / standing / etc.
3. I plan to pay no more: 50/100/200/250/300 / etc. USD / Uzbek tons ...
4. I plan to visit the workplace: 1 time to see / at least 3 times a week / don't throw me out! / ...
5. For me, the priority in the coworking center: the presence of the park / minibus / metro / atmosphere / table for ping-pong / ...
6. I can allocate 24/48/64 minutes in the morning / evening / night to drink coffee / tea :)
I ask those interested to unsubscribe in the comments.
