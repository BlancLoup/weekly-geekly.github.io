ItamItut.ru new photo synchronization service in social networks

Social networks, there are many of them, they are diverse and each attracts users with its own something.

And when you want to be everywhere, you have to spend a lot of time synchronizing your accounts and photographs. Someone simply starts an account in a new social network and you can download all your photos and sort them by albums for a very long time.

The idea was born in our country, when representatives of the beautiful half of humanity after the journey downloaded long and dreary pictures of VKontakte, and then performed a similar operation with Facebook. It was impossible to flood the archive and unzip it through the console here, and I wanted to create something more universal for everyone.

We decided to simplify this task and created the itamitut.ru service, it will help you to cope with the task in a simple and interactive way.

So far, we have implemented synchronization only between two networks, this is facebook and vkontakte, but other Russian (and later foreign) popular networks are also on the way. Implementation was carried out on Django, Python, JavaScript, using the open API for developers provided by sites.

It seems to me that we got a rather elegant and beautiful solution, which allows us to perform actions that would take much more time in one mouse movement.

The service is completely free.

Please leave any comments on usability improvement and report all glitches, we will fix it. Thank.
