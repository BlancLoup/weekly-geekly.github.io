LifeTrak: budget sports watch + fitness tracker



Wearable electronics is now becoming (or rather, has already become) one of the most popular trends in the development of gadgets. The function of tracking some vital signs of the human body is included in very many gadgets of this kind, and there are specialized devices, such as sports watches and fitness trackers (often the same).

Of course, there are very expensive devices of this kind, and there are budget options that allow you to monitor calories, sleep quality, and the number of kilometers traveled per day.

LifeTrak sports watches / fitness trackers are just low-end devices with good functionality. LifeTrak Move C300 and LifeTrak ZoneC410 are two models from LifeTrak that you should pay attention to.

LifeTrak Move C300





This device has two main functions: an activity tracker (i.e. a fitness tracker) and a sports watch (moisture / shock).

What can a gadget?

To fix the heart rate (HR)
  • Determination of heart rate by pressing a single button;
  • Logging;
  • Display ECG waves;


Track calories
  • Own calorie counting algorithm combines heart rate measurements and activity data to accurately track calories burned all day long


Measuring distances and filtering false steps
  • Adjusting the length and pace of the step, automatic detection of walking and jogging;
  • Filtering "false" steps from tracked steps;




Automatic reporting
  • Burn calories burned all day;
  • Pedometer - data for the whole day;
  • Automatic distance tracking;
  • Displaying results for the week, maintaining a 24-hour schedule;
  • Synchronization via Bluetooth with smartphones on iOS and Android .


Security
  • Reliable, anti-shock (8 kV Contact and 15 kV Air (IEC6100-4-2));
  • FCC, CE, ROHS compatibility;
  • The ability to dive under water to a depth of 30 meters;
  • Durable polyurethane strap;
  • Strong fastener, blocking mechanism;
  • High impact polycarbonate lenses;




In terms of energy, the device is very economical, so changing the battery (CR2032) is very rare (about once a year).

Among other things, there is also the possibility of setting goals. For example, to go so many kilometers in a certain time. The gadget will show progress on the daily chart, as mentioned above.





You can order this device here (79.99 USD) right here (2790 rubles).
