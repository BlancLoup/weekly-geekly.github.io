Pogodav.ru - Minimalism for life

The first steps


It is time to introduce the first draft pogodav.ru Welcome.

Please do not be surprised. Yes, this is a weather site, but the site is not normal, but, as it is fashionable to express it lately, the web-dancer. Without the classic "pillar" layout, simple and clearly performing its immediate function - to give the user information about the weather in 2 clicks. In the direction of convenience and functionality, many ‚Äúmodern‚Äù site builders are moving, and we also decided to keep up with this positive trend.

Why weather site?


Well-known weather sites are usually overloaded with side information and advertising, which is why they are very inconvenient. In order to simply find out the forecast for today, one needs to wade his eyes through all this diversity. Here the idea was born to create a simple and convenient solution, both for yourself and others.

So.


Work with the site is very simple. Come on in. Choose the city you are interested in and voila !!! You can also see the forecast for the next week. If we have offended someone, you can always tell us about it through the section "Add your city". Leave a request and, if it is technically possible (if the weather information is available), then in the near future you will be able to see the forecasts in a digestible form.

What's next


In principle, the initial idea is practically implemented - the service performs the task. But simplicity does not mean primitiveness, so we are going to develop the service further. As an experiment, a simple widget for iGoogle has been created; if the idea with mobile applications is perceived well, then an application for the social network Vkontakte is in the plans. You can go further ...

In general, in which direction we will move, will largely depend on your reaction. On this site, you can leave your proposal or vote for the existing one using the Reformal system. Waiting for your feedback and suggestions. Thank.
