MacBook Pro Updates


  • New Intel Core i5 and Core i7 processors (promise a 1.5x performance boost, both are 2 core processors)
  • The amount of RAM now in all models 4 GB
  • New NVIDIA GeForce GT 320M / 330M graphics cards and integrated Intel HD Graphics will increase performance by up to 80% (finally implemented on-the-fly switching)
  • The new battery works up to 10 hours without recharging in the MacBook Pro 13 and up to 8-9 hours in the MacBook Pro 15 and 17
  • In the configurator, we added a 512 GB SSD with a space value of $ 1,300
  • In the configurator, you can add 1680x1050 matte display instead of the standard glossy 1440x900
  • With the help of the Mini DisplayPort - HDMI adapter , you can finally transfer sound
