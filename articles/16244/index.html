Sony-Ericsson: Stereo Headset with GPS

Judging by the newsletter received today from Sony-Ericsson, there is a very interesting device for a fairly wide range of users. This is a GPS-receiver, headset and remote control for the player in one bottle.



Given the support of GPS devices in Yandex mobile maps , the headset already allows you to turn a regular phone into a city navigator. Almost all modern Sony-Ericsson models that have J2ME are supported.

The device is called HGE-100, it weighs 52 grams, is powered by a phone, and also has a standard 3.5 mm headphone jack, which comes in the box. However, nothing prevents you from using your favorite Sennheiser and listening to music in the best quality.
