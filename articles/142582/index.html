Navigating the future tomorrow


Navigation is not only and not so much a map. It is also the situation on the road: traffic jams, accidents, traffic police posts, cameras, etc.

How does it work now? Most of the participants in the movement have a variety of devices on which a variety of programs are installed. The essence of these programs in one.
