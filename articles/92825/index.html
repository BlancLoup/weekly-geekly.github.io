DevPoint IT conference to be held in Novosibirsk

May, and indeed the whole year, is traditionally rich in various conferences. Including for developers. However, almost all of them are held in Moscow or St. Petersburg, and the get-together does not reach the regions.

This year we decided to rectify the situation and organized our own IT conference with chess and poetess.

DevPoint is a web developers conference, although various programmers, testers, team leaders and project managers have already shown interest in it .

With their reports at the conference will perform:
  1. Application development for Facebook , Anton Rusakov ( badoo.com , Moscow)
  2. What and why to write on Erlang? , Maxim Lapshin (company "Evil Martians", Moscow)
  3. Application development for smartphones , Igor Shcherbakov (Shamrock Mobile, Novosibirsk)
  4. Test automation. Sharing experience , Vitaly Kulpin (DoubleGIS, Novosibirsk)
  5. Distributed version control systems , Vladislav Semenov (Taba.ru developer, Novosibirsk)
  6. Dispatching system on the example of alawar.ru , Petr Zagvazdin (Alawar Entertainment, Novosibirsk)
  7. Human problems of IT managers , Alexander Orlov (founder of happy-pm.com , St. Petersburg)
  8. Modern solutions for building high-performance web systems , Andrey Shetuhin (Rambler, Moscow, head of mail)
  9. NoSQL storage, general overview with theory , Kirill Korinsky (research engineer at Yota, St. Petersburg)
For Novosibirsk, this is the first (after the Siberian Internet week , of course) specialized conference, and we hope that it will become traditional and next year will not be held in one day, but in several.

Date: May 22, 2010.
Venue: Novosibirsk, conference room at Lenin, 52 , from 10:00.
