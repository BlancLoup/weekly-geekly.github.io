Moscow Atlassian Meetup in Moscow on April 21



On Tuesday, April 21, Mail.Ru Group will organize the first Moscow Atlassian Meetup. In the office of the company will gather experts in the field of organizing communication with users and project management. The program of the event includes reports on Atlassian products (JIRA, Confluence and others), and afterwards their discussion and communication of like-minded people in a free environment.

Andrei Molchanov , Mail.Ru Group, tells the story of how, with the help of JIRA, his team automated the work of the company's content projects, such as Auto Mail.Ru, Lady Mail.Ru, Real Estate Mail.Ru, etc. It will be about analyzing the statistics of visitors, SMM and SEO in sections by authors, categories and periods, automatic payment of materials and much more.

Anton Kolin from the company Teamlead makes a review of the Atlassian Service Desk and will show its pros and cons, which had to be faced in real projects. In addition, Anton will present an alternative solution to problems related to the Service Desk.

Denis Kulyabin , General Director of IT Care, will make a report on JIRA Telephony Integration. The topic will be of interest to those who have the need to register user requests by phone. You will learn about the experience of creating a universal solution that allows you to integrate JIRA with telephony based on Cisco, Avaya, Asterisk.

What if JIRA is not fast enough? Have you ever encountered the problem of falling speed? Andrei Larionov from ALMWorks will talk about the universal toolkit for tackling and preventing these difficulties.

The meeting starts at 19:00.
