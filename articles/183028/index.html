How to quickly make a cyborg cockroach

Since yesterday Kickstarter has opened a fundraiser for the curious RoboRoach project from a small startup Backyard Brains: everyone who would like to use domestic cockroaches with benefit can get an electronics kit from the authors, which can be glued onto the back of an insect and controlled from its smartphone.

The creators of the project offer everyone a package weighing 4.5 grams from a control board, three electrodes and a lithium battery. Having a cockroach, electrodes should be strengthened on his antennae, the board should be glued to the back of the insect; The battery provides energy for wireless communication and electrical impulses to the antennae for 12 hours. After that, for some time (adaptation will occur) the cockroach can be controlled from the phone running Android or iOS
