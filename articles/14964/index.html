Blogcamp: Event Oriented

It's time for Blogcamp! Two days have arrived, in which everything that you do in the social web, web 2.0, will be concentrated. Time for a lot of new contacts and virtualizations, the birth of new projects and concepts, the exchange of creativity and experience.

BlogCamp will be held at the University of Physical Education, known as the "Institute of Physical Culture." Its exact address is Fizkultury Street, 1. It is a hundred meters from the Metro Republican Stadium, the neighboring buildings are the Planetarium and the Sport Hotel.
