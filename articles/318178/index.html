17 predictions for 2017: Microsoft researchers - what to expect in 2017 and in ten years

We decided to dream and look into the future for 1 year, for 10 and for 69 years ahead. Under the cut, you will find 17 predictions from female researchers from Microsoft Research for 2017 and 2027, as well as a greeting card that will take you to 2086.




Kalika Bali , Researcher, Research Lab in India

What are the most important improvements in speech processing and natural language in 2017?

In 2017, our language and speech applications will become more multilingual. But this means not only expanding the list of languages ‚Äã‚Äãsupported by the system. Of course, we will add new languages. However, besides this, we will create systems that can understand, process and use a language that is a link between two languages ‚Äã‚Äãin bilinguals (for example, people who speak English and Spanish, French and Arabic, Hindi and English), when during a conversation, and sometimes and in the course of one sentence, they effortlessly move from one language to another.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in the field of speech processing and natural language in 2027?

Language models will be deeply embedded in cognitive models, which will enable artificial intelligence systems (AI) to justify their decisions and communicate with people without special efforts, adapt to different social situations, negotiate, argue and persuade people. Computational sociolinguistics and pragmatic models will play an important role in creating AI, taking into account sociocultural factors.


Jennifer Chace , scientist, managing director, research laboratories in New England and New York

What are the most important machine learning improvements to be expected in 2017?

Deep learning transforms a multitude of technologies. However, most of the existing algorithms remain heuristic, they rely on the experience and intuition of managers. In 2017, we will come to a more structured understanding of deep learning and, therefore, we will be able to develop more powerful algorithms. This area is developing thanks to developments in various disciplines, including statistical physics and computer science.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in the field of machine learning algorithms in 2027?

Artificial intelligence and machine learning systems will greatly facilitate our lives. The existing algorithms are often biased with respect to data, they can be manipulated by entering obviously wrong information. One of the major improvements over the next ten years will be the development of objective algorithms that are resistant to data manipulation attempts.


Susan Dume , Scientist and Deputy Director, Research Laboratory in Redmond, Washington, USA

What are the most important improvements in search and information to expect in 2017?

In the field of searching and receiving information, the possibilities of deep learning will be fully involved. Over the past few years, we have witnessed breakthroughs in speech recognition, image recognition and natural language processing. These new features are due to the emergence of new deep learning architectures combined with an increase in data volume and an increase in computing power. Next year, deep learning models will improve the quality of search on the Internet; they will learn how to better understand documents and formulate queries using a machine.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in the field of searching and receiving information in 2027?

The search field that we are used to will disappear. Instead, the ubiquitous built-in context-sensitive search functionality will appear. The beginning of this transformation can be considered voice requests, especially when using mobile devices and ‚Äúsmart home‚Äù systems. This trend will evolve; it will be possible to issue search queries consisting of sound, images, and video, using the context for proactively obtaining information related to the current location, materials, objects, or actions without sending the requests explicitly.


Sarah Jane Dunn , scientist, research laboratory in Cambridge, UK

What are the most important improvements in biological computing expected in 2017?

Despite the widespread use of computer and technical analogies for the "explanation" of biology, the calculations performed by cells are carried out in a completely different way than calculations in microprocessors. In the short term, an important achievement will be a theoretical justification for understanding the processing of biological information: this is very important because our goal is to model, change and reprogram the behavior of cells.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in the field of biological computing in 2027?

If we talk about the development of programming based on biological models, in ten years completely new industries and solutions may arise in various fields, from agriculture and medicine to energy, materials and computing systems. Over the past 50 years, people's lives have radically changed thanks to the creation of programs for silicon microprocessors. But now we are waiting for a new revolution: the era of live programs.


Mar Gonzalez Franco , Researcher, MSR NExT, Redmond, Washington, USA

What are the most important improvements in virtual reality to be expected in 2017?

In 2017, virtual reality devices will appear with an improved body tracking function. Due to this, it will be possible to create virtual "avatars" and manage them, looking at the virtual world from the first person.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in the field of virtual reality in 2027?

By 2027, virtual reality systems will be used everywhere, they will use several senses at once, and are able to mix perceived reality with virtual reality or to change it. With this technology, people will be able to restore, change or improve their perception of reality. Unlike existing virtual reality systems that model only image and sound, in the future it will be possible to use other modes, such as tactile impact.

Nicole Immorlika , Senior Researcher, New England Research Lab

What are the most important breakthroughs in economics and game theory to be expected in 2017?

In the era of big data, people have to make increasingly complex decisions, put more effort into making the right choice. In the future, economists will develop new theories about non-optimal behavior due to excessive complexity, and IT specialists will create automated machine learning tools that can help people in such situations.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies in economics and game theory in 2027?

By 2027, automation will lead to the creation of a new economy in which the social contribution of most people will be determined by the data they create, rather than the work they do. Economists will look for ways to equitably reward people for this contribution. This will most likely require a larger-scale redistribution of funds through mechanisms such as taxes and social programs.

Karin Strauss , Senior Researcher, Research Laboratory in Redmond, Washington, USA

What are the most important improvements in equipment and devices to expect in 2017?

Moore's Law is slowly slowing down. Maintaining the pace of development of general-purpose silicon-based processors and with capacitive memory is too expensive. Therefore, in 2017, a number of new specialized hardware accelerators will appear, mainly in the form of FPGA processors. They will be used in the cloud to improve performance while reducing costs (instead of linear capacity increase according to Moore's law). Of course, general-purpose processors will continue to improve, although not as rapidly as before. As a result, we will get more interesting, faster and safer cloud based services. New devices and accessories for virtual and augmented reality, both cheap and expensive, will also appear on the market. As a result, new applications will appear on these platforms, as well as interesting developments in the field of creating content for virtual and augmented reality, including 360-degree video filming and similar solutions.

What will be the main achievements or the most discussed technologies for equipment and devices in 2027?

By 2027, we can expect the development of a number of new technologies that are not associated with a linear increase in the power of silicon microprocessors. Carbon nanotubes and other molecular production techniques, new architectures, in particular, computational approaches closer to the data, new computational and storage paradigms of data, such as quantum computers and DNA-based storage devices, may be developed to become available for commercial use. There will be a jump in the development of AI technologies with reduced energy consumption, as well as screens located near the user's eyes, due to which we will get more intelligent devices and enhanced capabilities of augmented and virtual reality.
