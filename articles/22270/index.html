Electronic Component Search System

This is my first post on Habr√© in it, I will talk about the search system for electronic components , the development of which I am currently engaged in.

March 18, 2008 launched an independent search engine of electronic components zfind.ru. The system allows you to easily find electronic components, send direct requests to sellers and agree on the terms of the transaction.

The system participants are given the opportunity to place in the database a price list of unlimited size. To date, participation in the search is free.

Features zfind.ru
- Intelligent search for electronic components. Search filters.
- View all currently available offers. Obtaining information about the cost of electronic components, availability and information about the companies offering them.
- Saving found electronic components in the "basket". View and edit the "basket". - Directing requests to sellers to confirm price and availability information.
- The system of tree discussion "buyer - sellers", "seller - buyers". Maximum convenience when discussing the details of a possible deal.
- Instant notification of the response of the seller or buyer to e-mail.
- Placing in the database zfind.ru price list of unlimited size. Download in Excel format through the website or with the help of a project manager.
- Saving and viewing the history of applications made.
- Saving and viewing history of the received applications.
- Preservation of the discussion history of the discussion of transactions.
- View the "cards" of the participants of the system.
- Convenient management of portal services through the "Cabinet".
- Providing statistics of search queries and the most interesting analytical reports on the work of the system.

The zfind.ru project was created by a group of developers independent of the vendor companies. Many programmers involved in the work on the project have previously participated in the creation of large web-based electronic component search services in Russia.

The purpose of creating the project zfind.ru is to improve the quality of the search service for electronic components in the Russian-speaking segment of the Internet and to destroy the monopoly of old search web services. The tasks are ambitious! Already this year, the developers of the system intend to achieve leadership zfind.ru among the search engines of electronic components in Russia.
