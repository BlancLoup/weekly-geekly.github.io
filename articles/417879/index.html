Official exam page


Here is this page. Exam 70-532 allows you to get the status of Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP). It can be said that this is the initial stage for obtaining ‚Äúmore complex‚Äù certifications. It is sometimes asked whether the MCP has a duration or an expiration date. Perhaps you can say that the status of the MCP does not expire as such. The exam itself (but not your MCP status) may cease to be available for passing (or replaced by another, updated exam) or technologies or products may become irrelevant over time, but the acquired status of MCP for a particular technology or product will remain in your "Piggy bank".



I note that, unlike the MCP, exams of the following stages, for example, Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE) or Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MSCD), for which the MCP is a necessary component, have the status of active only for a year. In order to maintain active MCSE and MCSD statuses, it is necessary to take another new exam once a year. More details on this topic are provided in the Do Microsoft Certifications Expire articles ? and Microsoft Certification Changes and Goodbye to MCSD Azure Solutions Architect .
