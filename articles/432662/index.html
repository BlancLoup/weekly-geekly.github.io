Expert Council under the government proposes to introduce the punishment for a fake IMEI

In Russia, there may be a punishment for faking IMEI mobile devices. This proposal was made by the Expert Council under the Government, submitting amendments to the Code of Administrative Offenses (CAO). The amendments provide for liability for forging IMEI (International Mobile Equipment Identity, International Mobile Equipment Identity) mobile gadgets, as reported by Vedomosti.

Individuals for fake IMEI are invited to be fined for 3000-4000 rubles., Officials - 15000-30000, legal - 60000-100000. The chairman of the commission, Lyudmila Bokova, said that the mobile phone buyer will be able to check whether IMEI is forged on a special portal. At the same resource, the phone can be registered, which means the need to create an IMEI database. It is proposed to form it by 2023, which follows from the amendments to the Law "On Communications" from the expert council and the Federation Council. The fee for registering a phone in the database may be about 100 rubles - but this is relevant only for devices imported from abroad. The same users who purchased the phone in the Russian retail will not pay for registration, since the device will be registered on the fact of legal importation.
