Dogs will help Malaysians fight pirates

Fight with pirated discs, whether music, movies or games, the Malaysian police will be assisted by specially trained dogs. Two Labradors, Flo and Lucky, have already demonstrated their skills by finding the one that contained counterfeit among the pile of boxes.



Of course, dogs do not distinguish legal discs from pirated ones - this task is entrusted to people. But finding the container where the data carriers are located, animals are quite capable, as they sense the substances used in the production of disks. The cost of training dogs was $ 17 thousand, and the police are confident that the money was well spent.
