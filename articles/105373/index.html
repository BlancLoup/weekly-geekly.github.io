LG Spy Watch Review

Do you dream of becoming James Bond, but do not have martial arts and espionage skills? No problem! New watches from LG will help you to feel like an agent 007. Besides the fact that the impenetrable screen can display several types of watches: from constant classics to modern watch movements, you can connect with people and transfer secret (or not) data to them.



UPD: This is the watch of our president - Dmitry Anatolyevich Medvedev. Photo at the end of the review.


To begin, I will give you the opportunity to get acquainted with dry technical details, and only then I will tell you about the impressions.



Appearance





As for the size and design of the watch, they are quite large and no less beautiful. The physical dimensions of the clock are 61x39x13.9 mm, weight - about 90 grams. Metal quality, glass completely covers the display, does not scratch.



The TFT display is made using capacitive technology, it responds only to fingers, the resolution is 128x160 pixels, bright even in the sun, the diagonal is 1.43 inches.



Black ribbed strap and rectangular shape will decorate any male hand.



On the left side there are three buttons: "green", "red" and the cancel button.



Where the usual clock has a battery, these have a sim card compartment. Unfortunately, just the same battery here can not be replaced.



In addition to SIM cards, nothing more can be inserted. That is, no interchangeable microSD-cards. Which, unfortunately, is a big disadvantage.



By the way, these contacts, which can be seen on the inside of the watch, are contacts for charging (after all, it‚Äôs not for the eternal engine to work with). Interface





I am sure that the handling of the clock will not be complicated by anything. Especially if you have ever had experience with touch screens of mobile phones. After all, in fact, this is the same phone, only on your hand.

From the main menu are available several basic functions. The first is the challenges. Here they have voice and video. After all, the LG GD910i is the first watch that supports 3G connection.



You can talk through the speakerphone of the clock, and a special "trick" is that in noisy place you are, the clock will transmit only the voice. Just remember that it will look rather strange, as if you are a spy. And you can talk through the headset.



In addition to calls, there is a page with quick access to sound and communication management.



As with any phone - there is a calendar.
