Egghead.io - Video Course on AngularJS

Egghead.io is a project created by John Lindquist, whose goal is to create video courses. According to the author, he was never satisfied with the existing implementations of sites with video lessons. Therefore, his main task was to apply the best practices for organizing and submitting training video materials in the network.
While the resource itself is being finalized and in need of high-quality design. But now the first course of video tutorials on AngularJS is available.
The project received a lot of good feedback from the community, it was also mentioned in the javascript ezine weekly and on the javascript google + page.
