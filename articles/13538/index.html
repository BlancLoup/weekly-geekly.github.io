Dopplr - Web 2.0 Service for travelers - Doppler

Dopplr - New closed Web 2.0 Beta Service for traveling around the globe. Immediately among the main features worth mentioning linking your account to your OpenId. This is definitely a plus of this service. Now I will torture you a little, forcing you to read a small FAQ from below.

Dopplr

What is dopplr?
Dopplr is a web service for frequent travelers. It allows you to share your future travel plans with a group of tourists you choose and trust. It also reminds you of friends and colleagues who live in cities that you plan to visit. You can use the service from your personal computer and mobile phone.

Dopplr. What does this name mean?
The service is named after Christian Doppler, a 19th-century Austrian scientist who came up with the hypothesis of what is now known as the Doppler effect. This is what we experienced when an ambulance with a siren roars passes by us.

Why using this can make my life better?
The main goal, as noted above, is to improve contact with friends and colleagues by telling them where we travel in the world. If there are better services - write in the comments. And I will explain the essence of this service.
