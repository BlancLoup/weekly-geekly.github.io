New mono-brand HTC salons in Moscow and St. Petersburg

Just two good news pleased owners of HTC smartphones last week. First, a second wave of devices was announced, which will receive updates to Ice Cream Sandwich later this year. It includes such popular models as: HTC Incredible S, HTC Desire S and HTC Desire HD.

Well, the second news is the opening of two new HTC brand stores in Moscow and St. Petersburg! The interior of the shops is made in the classic style for HTC: in a relaxed atmosphere, you can get acquainted with all the current innovations of the company.


A new salon in Moscow opened on Krasnokholmskaya Embankment (d.1 / 3) .


In the salon, you can not only try up-to-date smartphones of the company in action, but also get expert advice from the store employees.



The salon in St. Petersburg is open in the very center of the city on Nevsky Prospect (d. 57) .




The salons provide a full range of support and customer service. Moreover, these services can be provided directly in the presence of the client.
