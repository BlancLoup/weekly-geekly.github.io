History of creation


We are a young team that develops applications for mobile platforms and with us, like all developers, there came a time when the application code was already fully written and tested, which means it was time to publish the application in the store and start promoting it. And then the question arose - where to start? After all, we practically did not have an advertising budget, which means the chances of getting into the TOP applications were close to zero, so we had to carefully study how and where to spend the advertising budget. It often happened that the money was invested in sources that in the end had no effect on the promotion of the application.

But the more applications we got, the easier it was to promote them in the TOP of its category. We used to catch up with old applications on old applications, and this gave and gives good results - our applications began to rise in the TOP of their category on their own with little or no cash costs (now only on Android, but we are striving for similar on iOS, and in the future on other platforms). Of course, the TOP category is not a general TOP, but, in our opinion, this is also very good.

At one fine moment, the thought came to us that there are many people like us - each mobile application developer faces the problems of promoting their creations. We thought about it, but what if we share our traffic with other developers and develop a new advertising service for free promotion of applications in central stores? After all, by joint efforts we will be able to build a platform that will help good and high-quality applications to achieve success.

No sooner said than done. Service StartAD.mobi is the embodiment of our idea.

So what is this service and what are our goals?


The goal of our service is to help independent developers to facilitate the promotion of their applications and reduce the advertising budget, which is usually not enough.

StartAD.mobi is built on the principle of banner-exchange network. Developers integrate code into their applications and thus advertise applications from each other, which helps them to increase the number of app installations and thereby raise them in the ratings of centralized stores.
