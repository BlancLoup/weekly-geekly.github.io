How to implement?


My experience shows that at the initial stage, the income from the sale of links can be very good and can greatly exceed the income from contextual advertising. For example, my humble blog on investment and finance is now only through links brings about $ 60 per month. Of course, this is a bit - but not a lot of kopecks =). Moreover, the links do not strain users, unlike intrusive ads.
Now question number two. It is clear why someone may need links from our site. The question is - to whom and how to sell them? I use two different link exchanges - Sape and Xap .
The first one ( Sape ) is focused on the sale of expensive links from large pages (main and secondary pages). In Sape, you can specify the price for each link sold, manually enter into sales transactions, and so on.
Xap is sharpened by the batch sale of hundreds of links from "small" pages - with a high level of nesting and low PR. In Xap, fixed prices for links (depend on CY and PR). Another difference Xap - for the sale and purchase of links inside it uses the local currency, xapa. However, this currency is easily converted into Webmoney.

Summarizing - the recipe is simple!


So, in steps:

  • We place their code on the site.
  • We are waiting for the moderation of our project.
  • On the main page and on high PR pages, we sell links with Sape. On all others - with the help of Xap.

It should be borne in mind that the external links from our site leak some of its weight - PR. Therefore, borscht with corrupt links is not worth it.
