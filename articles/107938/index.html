5 million users: we will need more servers

Twenty-two thousand one hundred thirty people joined Evernote just yesterday. Seriously: 22,130. This is a record in itself, but beyond that we overcame the mark of 5,000,000 users!

Over the past few weeks, we actually beat record after record:
  • highest daily income from premium subscribers ;
  • the largest daily audience of Windows users (thanks to our recent update Evernote 4.0 for Windows );
  • the largest daily audience of Android users (thanks to our great new Evernote 2.0 for Android );
  • Finally, the largest daily audience of BlackBerry users who also received an Evernote client update.
Do not worry, soon we are planning large-scale updates for both Mac and iPhone / iPad!

Only 83 days have passed since the four-millionth user registered, which is about 12,000 new users every day. Here's what it looks like:





And the fact that I have said this four times does not make my words less sincere: thank you very much to all our wonderful users, partners, employees and investors! Together we made a product that affected the lives of over five million people. Is not that great?

Phil libin
CEO Evernote Corp.

UPD: and one more news for today: we opened the possibility of a quarterly subscription to a premium service.
