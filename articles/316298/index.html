Do not judge strictly. I'm just an office plush cat and my notes do not pretend to be scientific papers. I just wanted to share my observations, mistakes and blunders made during the development of the game, in the hope that someone could save their time, nerves and become a bit kinder.



Programmers - lovers of difficult puzzles with easy otgadki, probably therefore very often, deep in the soul, we remain children until the deepest old age. What do children like? And kids love toys! And the idea is born to write your toy. Naturally, it will be the most interesting and beautiful among all existing ones, because we have a lot of ideas and we can write good code. Or is it not so simple?


In this article I will try to tell you about the stages and mistakes made during the creation of the game under the iOS platform.


So, let's begin. Creating something new starts with an idea. Our case was no exception. As is the case, an idea is born under the impression of something. Describe the essence of the idea with words. Write them down so that at any moment you can skim through your future masterpiece with your eyes and enjoy it. Avoid the words: somehow, anyone, anything.
