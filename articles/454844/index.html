A great essay on design ethics and an ethical design framework written by UX Collective curators Fabricio Teixeira and Cayo Braga. In the newsletter, I unwrapped the essay, I will not do it here.



Printing: Scottish Police Federation


The design of the book about the Scottish police union, issued in honor of the 100th anniversary of the organization.



Identity and packaging design of the Bryansk dairy plant


In Moscow, the Depot agency made a style for a dairy plant that wants to look simple and honest.

Clean, systematic, logical. Very noticeable on the shelves.



Retro: Airline Logos


A collection of airline logos from the middle of the last century.



Lecture: Code LOT2046


Lecture by Vadik Marmeladov, one of the coolest Russian designers, creator of the LOT2046 clothing subscription service and gadgets. He spoke about the history and structure of the service, shared his view of the future of companies and products.

Very inspiring lecture, look!



Tool: Adaptiff


A collection of generative algorithms for creating backgrounds and patterns, with a friendly interface. You can change the input parameters, customizing the algorithm for themselves. Made our compatriot (Jura, hello).



Tool: PixelSnap 2 for macOS


Measuring screen distances with a very cool interface. You move the cursor, and the program itself determines the objects on the screen and shows the distance to them. Watch the video. The program is paid, costs $ 25 for one computer.



Free: https://useanimations.com/


Library of animated icons for interfaces. They are made on the basis of a set of free icons Feather and perfectly complement each other.
