Review of gaming laptops ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II and ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II



The ASUS ROG Strix GL504 SCAR II and ASUS ROG Strix GL504 HERO II gaming laptops replaced the first versions of the ASUS ROG Strix GL503 SCAR Edition and the ASUS ROG Strix GL503 HERO Edition, which were presented a year ago, at the IFA 2017 exhibition. The SCAR Edition was created for fans of shooters and other action games, and HERO Edition - for fans of quieter games, such as strategies, MOBA and RPG. Externally, new laptops are significantly different from the first HERO and SCAR. But the differences between the new and old models do not end in appearance. Rather, the exterior is only a small part of all differences.

ASUS ROG Strix GL503 SCAR Edition, released in 2017, is made in a body of light metal color, resembling a gun metal. But the case ASUS ROG Strix GL503 Hero Edition got a pattern that repeats the dragon's scales. The [WASD] keys were also selected for the Skara keyboard, and the [QWER] buttons for the Hiro keys. They are additionally highlighted on the contour. In general, complete harmony and stylization for shooters and MOBA games was achieved.

The new ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II and ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II continue to move in the direction given by the first Skar and Hiro. Both laptops are stylized for the same types of games, only their updated versions are better, thinner and lighter.



External differences ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II and ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II


The main visual differences between the new ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II and the ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II remain the same as in the first versions. Laptops have different case and keyboard design. However, external changes more affected ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II. This notebook has received a usual, dark metal cover, without a pattern in the form of dragon scales. And the ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II cover remained light, the color of the weapon metal. Only its texture has changed, and the diagonal strip crossing the lid is no longer so prominent.



The selection of the main gaming keys has changed. If the first versions of ASUS ROG Strix SCAR Edition and ROG Strix HERO Edition highlighted the edges of the [WASD] and [QWER] buttons, now the caps of these keys have become transparent. But the touchpads of two laptops now look the same. Recall that on the touch panel of the first "Hiro" were pointed pointed teeth, while the "Skara" - aiming grid.

Cooling system ASUS ROG Strix GL504GS SCAR II and ASUS ROG Strix GL504GM HERO II


The new notebooks have evolved a cooling system. Fans are powered by 12 volts, instead of the standard five. As a result, the new turntables rotate 20% faster, the air flow became stronger by 42.5%, and the air pressure created increased by 92%. In addition, the material of the blades has changed, so the latter have become thinner. Now 71 blades fit on one fan, which is 20% more than in previous models.



Together with the "turntables" the radiator ribs have also changed. They lost up to 0.1 mm, so more ribs fit in the same area of ‚Äã‚Äãradiators. As a result, the heat dissipation area increased by 10%. Also, the laptop ‚Äúexhaust‚Äù had a place for special channels for dust removal. The GL504 also received a thoughtful arrangement of heat pipes that are in contact with the CPU, GPU, power system and other hot components. To evenly dissipate heat, the tubes are connected to a copper plate, which acts as a radiator. In addition, the tubes themselves are correctly located inside the laptops.



The last thing you want to tell in the section of the cooling system is about the possibility of adjusting its work. In the ROG Gaming Center utility, you can set one of three levels of fan operation: quiet, balanced and maximum (fan overboost). As a result, the frequency of iron will adapt to the cooling system. A laptop can become quieter due to a small reduction in clock frequencies, and, conversely, can work without unnecessary restrictions. By the way, the maximum noise of the cooling system even in the most productive mode of operation will be 50 dB.

All of this in aggregate has changed the productivity of CO in a comprehensive manner. As a result, the laptop will not overheat and make a lot of noise, even with long games. An exception is the mode of increased performance of the cooling system, which is activated manually. In this mode, the rotational speed of the fans reaches full capacity and will be very noticeable.
