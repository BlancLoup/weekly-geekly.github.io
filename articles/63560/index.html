IT Report - the morning IT news

Hi, habra people.

I decided to show a small site, or rather, a page - no frills and vebdvanuley. This is a clone of Drudgereport , just not about politics, but about IT news.

Meet - IT Report .

I will explain what, how and why.

On duty, I am in the early morning (usually before 9-00 Moscow time) looking through articles of fifty IT publications - mostly foreign ones. Looking through, I select the best of them - later make news from them. Thus, every day I get the squeeze of the most interesting IT-news. Often - a few hours before they appear in RuNet.

At first I published them in my LiveJournal, then I created a special one ‚Äî itreport . It publishes the same morning collection. Recently, I have transferred my microblogging to IT news rails, now only headers + link are published there. Nothing personal.

IT Report - an attempt to make friends these two sources, giving a couple of dozen interesting links per day. RSS of them is reduced to one page - the idea is to be able to cover the news picture of the day both in the morning and in daily issues for the last three days (updated on weekdays mostly).

Of course, all comments and suggestions are accepted with gratitude. I hope IT Report will be useful - I still need to update it, and you may be pleased. I emphasize that the time of publication and the subject matter are directly related to the work, therefore, it is more difficult to change them than it seems.
