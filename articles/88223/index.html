FingersTune.com - musical instruments for iPhone, Mac and even iPad

Hello.

Well, actually, I already wrote a little about FingersTune.com here on Habr√© , but that was before the start.

And now start!



Today, finally, a whole collection of my FingersTune iPhone apps has come out of the vigilant censorship of the Apple App Store Review Team .




FingersTune is a viral tool, such as Okarin . When the user presses four sound keys in different combinations, the game makes different notes.

But FingersTune can sound not only as an ocarina, but also like any other instrument from the multitude of tools built into the library of game sounds. For example, as a piano, pipe, guitar, trombone , or even as the cry of a sick woman ;)

Another FingersTune has a built-in ringtone editor. The user can share his melodies on the FingersTune.com portal, as well as download tunes from this site to your iPhone.

Also, FingersTune is able to train a person to play the selected melody.





In addition to the full version for iPhone, four free versions have been released , each of which does not have a built-in editor and is limited to one of the following instruments: Ocarina, Piano, Accordion, Sax.

Also released a free version for Mac , which has a built-in editor and can upload and download melodies to the server, but does not have a player. By the way, the player will soon add. It will work through Magic Mouse and multitouch trackpads Mac Book and Mac Book Pro .

A special version for the iPad is about to be released.
