Bookmarklamp - concept lamp-bookmarks

Always and everywhere with a book? You read on the train, on the plane, in the hotel room ... And you like to read before going to bed. Everyone wants to sleep, and you want to read. You are interfering with a bright light in the middle of the night, a quarrel, a constant conflict ... Agree that the only problem is that you have a common light. I wish you had your little personal light.

And imagine - a small, light bulb, also a bookmark.



Bookmarklamp is a bookmark lamp. When the book is closed - the lamp is turned off and serves as a bookmark. When you want to read - open the book, place the lamp between the last pages and the cover. Turn it on and bend it, as you are comfortable and bright light illuminates only the pages of your book. Have you finished reading? Turn off the lamp with one touch of a button on the end, easily unbend it and place it on the place where you finished reading.
