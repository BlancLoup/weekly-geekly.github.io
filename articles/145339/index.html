Ruby Open Air (06/16/2012) Minsk

On June 16, with the support of Belarus Ruby on Rails User Group, the first in Belarus Ruby Open Air will be held - an informal conference (unconference), which will bring together Ruby and Ruby on Rails-developers from Belarus, the near and far abroad. Headliners will be Lee Hambley (maintainer of Capistrano, Germany), Denis Gorin (netzke.org, the Netherlands), Vladislav Gorodetsky (polyglot programming evangelist, RubyShift, Ukraine). Ruby Open Air will be a platform for discussing the latest trends and trends in the development of Ruby and Ruby on Rails, modern solutions, current issues and the exchange of experience. Various formats of speeches will help to get the maximum benefit: technical reports and mini-reports, discussions and networking.





The venue of the Ruby Open Air is the Terraski Manor . Participants will enjoy a rich program both at the unconference itself and after it, including the Beer & BBQ Party, sauna, Open Space concert and extreme entertainment for real Ruby developers.

Program
10.45 - Collection of participants at the Kamennaya Gorka metro station, delivery to the estate by bus
12.00 - Greeting
12.15 - 13.15 Presentations by invited guests (Lee Hambley, Denis Gorin)
13.15 - 14.45 Mini-reports (Lightning Talks) *
14.45 - 15.30 Lunch
15.30 - 17.00 Informal discussion (Unpanel)
17.00 - 17.30 Summing up, completion of the official part
17.30 - 21.00 BBQ Party, beer and informal communication.
From 21.00 to 23.00 delivery to Minsk to the metro station "Stone Hill" will be organized.

Participation in Ruby Open Air is paid - 200.000 BYR. The price includes round trip delivery, meals and a full entertainment program. The number of places - both paid and free - is limited (no more than 100 participants).

All speakers in the Lightning Talks section can participate for free. Detailed information on the website belarusrubyonrails.org

To participate in Ruby Open Air, you need to do just two steps:
1. Register for the event on the website belarusrubyonrails.org .
2. Purchase a ticket through kvitki.by (a direct link will be available upon registration) or receive confirmation from the organizers about your free participation as a speaker.
Organizers: Belarus Ruby on Rails User Group , Altoros
