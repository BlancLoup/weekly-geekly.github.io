Come back times

Repeatedly, when buying devices in stores, I encountered problems, large and small - the declared functions do not work, the formats are not readable, and in certain conditions the machines hang.
Once we could have been expanded with the words "a complex technical device cannot be returned." Now there are other times. The funny thing is that in many stores they pretend that nothing has happened and in some places there are still ads with the old wording "Return are not subject to ...". The last hike in Gorbushka showed that the sellers still do not know.

It's time to teach sellers to the new rules. Yes, and you have to get used to it (today, for example, I hand over a buggy flash drive).
Perhaps from the shelves finally disappear numerous buggy crafts Krivorukov, for which sellers will now return the money :)

This is not news, but it is important.
