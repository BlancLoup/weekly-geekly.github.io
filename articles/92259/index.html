The next version of Android will support Adobe Flash.

Andy Rubin (Andy Rubin), Google's vice president of Android development, in an interview for the New York Times said that Android 2.2, ( aka Froyo ), will support Adobe Flash, although he did not specify whether this support will be pre-installed on the OS.
