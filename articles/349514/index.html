Announcement of Serverless Moscow Meetup # 1


Hello! The first mitap in Russia on Serverless technology will be held on March 1st at Rambler & Co. We will talk about what Serverless is and why this approach appeared, and also consider two practical cases: building an analytical system for a mobile application and collecting data from smart home systems.

1. The history of serverless - Vitaly Davydov, co-founder of FUN Devs
The report will look at the history and causes of serverless.

2. Building analytics service and serverless best practices on AWS - Kirill Potekhin, co-founder of FUN Devs
The report will discuss the technical side of the serverless architecture on AWS.

3. Practical serverless experience for telemetry aggregation of the smart home - Alexander Hayorov, Unit manager at Ingram Micro
The report will describe the practical experience of using a serverless framework to collect various indicators of heterogeneous smart home systems with a Telegram interface.
