SuperDuino: Arduino compatible miniature board with color display and accelerometer



Developers of various kinds of electronic devices continue to delight the release of new models of boards that can be used by both professionals and beginners in the electronics world who want to get acquainted with this world as quickly as possible.

One of the most interesting recent projects is SuperDuino. This is a miniature board (really miniature) that comes with a color display and an accelerometer. Recently, developers have launched a campaign on Kickstarter, to raise funds for the mass production of SuperDuino, and by the time the campaign was completed, they raised 12 times more money than planned.



SuperDuino features



The fee can be used for a large number of projects, including the creation of your own smart watches, a miniature oscilloscope, an electronic calendar, and much more.



Pinout board



The developers have already created several variations of different devices based on SuperDuino, and promise that more can be done:

1. Temperature and humidity controller;
2. Voltmeter and ammeter;
3. Frequency indicator;
4. Oscilloscope;
5. Digital logic analyzer;
6. Pedometer;
7. Smart watch;
8. Talking hours;
9. Portable game console (including launch of Flappybird);
10. Smart hub to connect all sorts of devices with a smartphone.



C SuperDuino comes with several manuals that help you quickly understand various issues, including device management via a PC, setting up a LED led, creating a simple traffic light, and more.

Device Features:



  • USB Programmable (Micro USB Connector)
  • 16Mhz / 32Khz System clock
  • Two 8-bit Timer / Counters with Separate Prescaler and Compare Mode
  • One 16-bit Timer / Counter with Separate Prescaler, Compare Mode, and Capture Mode
  • Real Time Counter with Separate Oscillator
  • Six PWM Channels8-channel 10-bit ADC USARTMaster / Slave SPI
  • Byte-oriented 2-wire Serial Interface (I2C) On-chip Analog Comparator
  • Interrupt and Pin23 Programmable I / O Lines
  • 1.7 "128X160 16bit color TFT LCD
  • USB / CR2032 coin cell (Can charge rechargeable cell) / 3-12V Supply operated
  • 3 axes Accelrometer (can detect single click, double click, up, down, left, right tilt)
  • 2.54mm I / O pin Header
  • Speaker Option
  • Bluetooth add-on
  • WIFI Add-on
  • Microsd add-on
