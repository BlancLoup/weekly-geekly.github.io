With a flick of the wrist Hotmail turns into ... turns into Newmail



Apparently, Microsoft decided to adopt the experience of Apple, which called its tablet New Ipad. The developers of Hotmail decided to update their mail service quite significantly by rolling out several updates. New features have not yet been described, but the new name of the service is already known. This, as you might guess from the header, is Newmail.

Screenshots of the updated service were shown the other day, and it seems that the developers really reworked everything they could. Some users see in the new interface something in common with Gmail, others simply say that Newmail has received a minimalist interface. The developers themselves say that they have tried to update the interface in accordance with the Metro interface.

In addition to the interface, the social functions of the service received an update - so, now everything is integrated with Twitter and Facebook. Now inside your account you can get additional information about your contacts from the above social services. In general, Nemail is something like a hybrid of social mail and a social networking aggregator.

Perhaps Newmail, including the interface and new features, will be able to attract new users with its newness. In any case, the chances are there, and they are quite large.
