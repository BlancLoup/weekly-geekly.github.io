Mail.ru Group has become a leader in the non-gaming segment of the mobile apps market in Russia

According to the analytic service App Annie for March-May 2015, Mail.Ru Group has become the leader among publishers of mobile non-game applications in Russia in terms of downloads and revenues.

Last year, the holding was also the leader in the number of downloads, but was in third place in terms of income from its applications in this category. The study does not take into account the company's revenue from advertising.
