Conversations'19: conversational AI for those who develop and who still doubt

Conversations Conference, the only event in Russia dedicated to the technologies of conversational artificial intelligence, will take place on June 27-28 in St. Petersburg. How do developers make money on conversational AI? What are the pros, cons and hidden features of different dialogue platforms and methodologies? How to repeat the success of someone else's voice skills and chatbots with AI, but not to repeat someone else's epic file? Within two days, Conversations participants and experts from Google, Yandex, MTS, Just AI, Megafon, BRLab, Ozon Travel, DPD, EORA, Aeroclub IT, Ready for Sky will analyze figures, forecasts, fresh case studies and technological trends of conversational AI.



The Adobe Analytics team recently surveyed more than 400 major brands in various industries: 91% are making significant investments in voice right now, 71% believe that the voice will undoubtedly improve UX in the future. Here's another figure: by 2023, 8 billion voice assistants will be used in the world, Juniper Research predicts , there will be two or three assistants per person, but even with this scenario, the number of active users will reach 3-4 billion. So what content should developers and businesses create to win these users? The participants of Conversations'19 will have two days to deal with the questions that the spoken AI sets for us.
