HTML basics video course - to help informatics teacher

We shot the video course "Basics of HTML" . It is simple and fascinating, includes a section of the theory and a rather fun practice (about this below).

Before explaining the subtleties of the hypertext markup language , beginners should first explain what hypertext is and how it differs from ordinary text. In general, hypertext was thought up as an idea in the 1960s, and today even three-year-old children do not think about clicking on the links on the iPad. It is safe to say that hypertext is the future today.

Here is one of the first HTML course videos:



The course consists of 2 large sections - the first one describes what tags are and what they generally are: text formatting, pictures, tables and forms. And also, what is the "validity" of the document, how to check it and the main thing why. Even on HTML-entities one lesson is reserved.

In the second half of the course there is practice:

  • Create subpages;
  • View password under asterisks;
  • Insert HTML-code video from YouTube to your site;
  • Insert Mailchimp forms to collect contact future customers.

The course is actively used by computer science teachers to save time on explanations - then you can focus on the lessons / pairs on practical exercises.
