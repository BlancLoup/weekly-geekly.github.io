Unbound jailbreak for iOS 5.1.1 Absinthe 2.0.4 released

Hello to all!

Literally half an hour ago, the utility for unattached jailbreak iOS 5.1.1 came out.


Supported devices:
iPad:
- iPad (iPad1,1);
- iPad 2 Wi-Fi (iPad 2.1);
- iPad 2 Wi-Fi + GSM (iPad 2,2);
- iPad 2 Wi-Fi + CDMA (iPad 2,3);
- The New iPad Wi-Fi (iPad3,1);
- The New iPad Wi-Fi + GSM (iPad3,2);
- The New iPad Wi-Fi + CDMA (iPad3,3).
iPhone:
- iPhone 3GS (iPhone2,1);
- iPhone 4 GSM (iPhone3,1);
- iPhone 4 CDMA (iPhone3,3);
- iPhone 4S (iPhone4,1).
iPod touch:
- iPod touch 3 gen (iPod3,1);
- iPod touch 4 gen (iPod4,1).
(IPad2.4 support will be added later) Links updated, now supported!

How to use (free translation from the site):
1. Make a backup in iTunes. To do this, select your device and click "Backup".
2. Once the backup is created, go back to your device and select Settings - General - Reset - Delete content and settings. Thanks to this, the jailbreak process will go faster.
3. Open Absinthe and make sure the device is connected to the computer via USB.
4. Click 'Jailbreak' and wait. Do not unplug the device before completing the procedure.
5. Once the process is complete, return to iTunes and restore the device from the previously created backup. Just in case.
6. Thanks for using Absinthe!
