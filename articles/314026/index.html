Print support promised a year ago appeared in ReactOS

Remember this video ? We promised print support "very soon." Well, very soon ... in the end, a year passed. But the promised three years are waiting, and we managed in just one! There were two reasons for this delay:

1) the main and only developer of the print stack, Colin Fink, went for almost a year to the internship (manufacturing practice) in the USA;
2) for almost a year we waited until the Wine project team accepted our fixes to the RPC service, so that we could correctly backport / synchronize them.
