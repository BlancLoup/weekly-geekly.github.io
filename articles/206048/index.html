Shine - fitness button

Previously, it was called a pedometer. Now the pedometers have grown up, have grown up Bluetooth (it's better with Bluetooth!), Learned to count not only steps, but also calories and began to show results on the smartphone screen. Well, the name has changed backwards - now they are called proudly, fitness trackers.

We meet the star of this season - fitness tracker Shine, whose fame is running far ahead, and whose appearance in Russia is shrouded in an aura of mystery and bewilderment. However, hello.


As I said, Shine is a tracker whose creators decided not to balance on the narrow path of informativeness-energy consumption, and just went into the woods and had a picnic.
Shine is not able to show on the screen neither steps, nor calories, nor kilometers, he has no screen either. Pulse and fullness of blood with oxygen, he also can not measure. He does not know how to constantly keep in touch with the smartphone in real time so that your friends can see how fast you are walking. It even has a miniUSB port. It has no buttons!

But do not rush to close the page, my friends, hooked on the needle of the constant receipt of information. For Shine, as I see it, this is the first swallow, designed to remove us from another needle, the electric one. For all its minimalism, Shine has an undeniable advantage. He works. And it works up to six months from one CR2032 battery, the same as in your wristwatch (if they still have someone left).

‚ñ∫ Software

Shine only works with the official program, which, however, is already available for iOS and for Android. But their functionality is slightly different. If iOS users can see both the achievements, the duration of each activity, and the sleep schedule, then Android users are content with modest statistics. However, since the program for Android was posted quite recently, and is updated quite often - most likely, all these functions will be gradually added to it.

To begin with, as usual, you need to register and enter your data into the program. In the settings you can choose in what units the distance and weight will be shown, you can turn on the slip tracker function, you can adjust the display of the clock by double clicking on the tracker. You can set a goal - the number of points you want to earn in a day.


These screens are about the same for both platforms. And there, and there you can specify the type of activity and the place of wearing the gadget.


You can see how many steps and kilometers are covered on each day of activity and how many calories are spent.


These indicators can be shared on Facebook - such a ‚Äúsocial component‚Äù, which allows you to bring an element of competition, even in the counting of steps per day.

But then the differences begin. On iOS with a sweep up, you can see the statistics for the day in more detail - the number of activities, activity time and points earned. The green cup shows the place where you earned as many points as planned.
