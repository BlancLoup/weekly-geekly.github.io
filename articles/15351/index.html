Opera Software opens office in Silicon Valley

Opera Software , the only company that provides web browsers for all devices, is going to settle in Silicon Valley - the center of the technology industry.

The central office of Opera Software is located in the capital of Norway - Oslo. In addition, the company has nine branches in Sweden, Poland, China, Korea, Japan and the United States. In the United States, Opera Software opened its first office in 2005 in San Diego, California. Now two American offices of the company should be engaged both in improving relations with old business partners and in finding new ones. Now partners and customers include Google, Yahoo, Adobe, Motorola, Palm, Nintendo, Qualcomm and other companies that use Opera browsers in their products.

To advertise their arrival in Silicon Valley, Opera Software chose the Rickshaw Stop in San Francisco. The doors to the event called Rock Opera will open on October 25 at 5:00 pm PST. Among those invited will be partners, customers and members of the Opera community, as well as the press.

The new Opera Software office is located at 700 E. El Camino Real, Suite 310, Mountain View, CA 94040.
