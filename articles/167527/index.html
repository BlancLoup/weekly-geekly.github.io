Launched in September 2011, the petitioning system allows users to create petitions about everything that comes to their mind (it is quite obvious that petitions on the site still undergo a tough moderation). Now there is already a petition to cancel this decision ( at the time of writing the translation there were 6,732 signatures ), someone else asked the administration to stop this whole circus with the project ( at the time of writing the translation there were 1,000 signatures ), another asks the White House to take petitions more serious ( Signatures: 37000+. Since September 2011 , about 10 million signatures have been put under more than 141,000 petitions created. In the last two months of 2011, 73,000 new petitions have been created and 2.4 million people have registered in the system.
