Who will drive more on one liter of gasoline? Competitions "Honda ekosila"



Honda has announced the start of preparations for the next All-Japan competition under the concept of "who will travel more on one liter of gasoline."

The national Honda Ecosila 2007 competition will be held from October 6 to 7 on one of the tracks in Tochigi Prefecture near Tokyo. Participation in the competition will be cars with an engine capacity of about 50 cc. The novelty of these competitions will be a new class of cars with an engine capacity of up to 150 cc.

The competition is attended by teams of Japanese schoolchildren, students and amateur designers. The only condition for participation is still the use of Honda engines as the main engines.

The record of past competitions is 37.87 kilometers per liter of gasoline.
