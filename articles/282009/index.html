RUVDS will provide BCS clients with virtual servers in the heart of the Moscow Exchange



Hosting provider RUVDS and BCS Company have launched a joint project that will allow customers of the largest broker to purchase virtual VPS servers on a provider‚Äôs equipment located in the M1 data center exchange collocation zone.

According to Nikita Tsaplin, managing partner and founder of RUVDS, a feature of this placement is the possibility for BCS clients to place their trading systems and specialized software at affordable prices without the need to purchase their own expensive equipment.

This service is provided for the first time in Russia at such a low price - a virtual server will cost users no more than 5,000 rubles per month. By the way, the owners of their own servers pay about such a cost weekly for their placement within the walls of the exchange.

Representatives of the companies say that such services will make modern stock trading technologies more accessible and now any BCS client will receive the technological capabilities of large dealers and funds.

RUVDS is a dynamically developing hosting provider specializing in providing corporate class IAAS services. Among the company's partners are the largest financial institutions: Finam JSC, BCS Company, a number of commercial banks and social projects, including the recently opened largest Coworking 14 in Russia, for which RUVDS provided a private cloud for customers. In March of this year, HUAWEI became a partner of RUVDS, equipping the company's data center with hardware infrastructure and a virtualization platform.
