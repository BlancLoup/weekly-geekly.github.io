"Rescue" container PowerCube



The US company Ecosphere Technologies has developed a device to help people in the disaster area. In the event of such a disaster, the victims drop the PowerCube container, or better, a lot of containers. They should be put into operation by extending the solar panels.

After that, PowerCube provides electricity to the local population, and also purifies drinking water and distributes WiFi (everything works on solar energy).

Ecosphere Technologies has developed three models of containers of different sizes. The largest of them generates up to 15 kW of electricity. She spends some of this on water purification and WiFi distribution, and the rest goes to the local power grid.



If a large territory has suffered, then several containers can be thrown there at once.

PowerCube is also suitable for the supply of mobile hospitals, tent cities and other temporary settlements.



In principle, instead of solar panels, you can use a conventional diesel generator, which produces ten times more power. But it will be necessary for him to periodically throw in fuel, which is not always possible (for example, the territory of the camp is located in the area prohibited for flights).
