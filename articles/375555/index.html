Virtual reality simulator Toyota teaches not to write SMS while driving.

While some are trying to deal with sms behind the wheel using an application that disables the ability to communicate while driving, others solve this problem at the training stage. Toyota has developed a driving simulator with the Oculus Rift, which will teach teens how to focus on the road.



You can tell people as much as you like that dangerous driving can lead to tragedy, you can legislate on this topic, but this will not stop many people. Toyota decided that it was necessary to influence people with the help of vivid impressions, and it was necessary to start with young people. The TeenDrive365 training program using virtual reality glasses Oculus Rift will show teens and their parents how important it is to focus on the road, without being distracted by text messages and talking friends in the backseat.
