LinguaLeo button in Yandex.Bar for easy translation and development of unfamiliar English words on the Internet

LinguaLeo and the Yandex.Bar team present a new application - the LinguaLeo browser button .



Profit from the application - you can click on unfamiliar words on the English-language Internet and unwittingly expand the vocabulary.

The LinguaLeo button is a convenient browser extension for translating unfamiliar words and phrases and adding them to your personal dictionary on LinguaLeo.ru (a web service for learning English). The button can be installed with Yandex.Bar on Mozilla Firefox and Internet Explorer browsers. For Chrome, there is a separate application from LinguaLeo which we described in a previous post. Application for Opera is expected in the near future.

Allows you to listen, translate and instantly add English words and phrases from any web pages to your personal dictionary LinguaLeo.ru



The added words are saved with the usage context, the selected translation and a link to the source of the addition.



Inside the service LinguaLeo.ru you can take interactive exercises to consolidate the understanding of the added words.

How it works


Double clicking on the English word will show the list of popular translations for the given word.

You can choose one of them, look in the online dictionaries or enter your own translation.
Next to the added word is a voice button and a picture of the word.
