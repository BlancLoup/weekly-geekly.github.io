Eviterra.com - smart booking form and pleasant things.

Hi, Habr!

News is certainly not right here for breaking, such as our price map or a new interface , but also interesting.

We recently changed the booking form on Eviterra - now the number of passengers can be determined not at the search stage (like everyone else), but also at the stage of booking and payment (almost no one else has this).



(The ability to specify the number of passengers before the search naturally has not gone away.)

The form itself determines the age of the passenger and recalculates the total cost of the ticket depending on that. Understandably, it will not let you add more passengers than there are available seats at the rate. In general, you can add, delete and have fun.

We also screwed in the memorization of passenger data. If you entered them once, and then decided to buy tickets for another flight, you do not need to re-enter everything.

By the way, we still have cheap tickets to Hong Kong and back on a direct flight to September 23 - if you want to buy the fifth iPhone one of the first. And other announcements of cheap offers are on Facebook .
