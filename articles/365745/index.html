3.2. Quanta know the future.

The magic of quanta includes such a thing as "entanglement." A multitude of particles can be in an entangled state, at each moment of time ‚Äúfeeling‚Äù each other. Such particles in a certain sense represent a single inseparable whole that most fully meets the criteria that consciousness possesses. Ok, so what gives us the assumption that our consciousness is based on quantum phenomena? What does the free will? Recall another magical quantum effect.



Many people remember the experiment that is demonstrated in schools. If between the light source and the screen put a barrier with one slit, then on the screen we will see a strip of light. However, if you put a barrier with two slits, then on the screen we will see not two strips of light, but a multitude of strips, which are called a diffraction pattern. Such a picture happens when waves pass through a barrier with two slits. However, light consists of photons, each of which is a particle, that is, when it hits the screen, it leaves one single point. If the light source emits only one photon at a time, then after a million such emanations, we will see the entire grid of stripes. But how so? Indeed, many stripes are formed only when the light passes through two slits at the same time. If the photon passes through the first or second slit, then only two strips will be obtained. Scientists have put sensors that record exactly what slit a single photon passes and a miracle happened - as soon as they put such a sensor, only two bars appear on the screen! When the sensor is removed, then again a lot of strips. Then they set an even more cunning experiment. The photon flies though quickly, but not instantly. A certain time before getting to the screen, the particle is on the way. But what if the sensors are put at the moment when the photon has already taken off from the light source? Indeed, in this case, the photon can no longer change its trajectory. So it was found that even when the sensors are set or removed after the photon has been emitted, it still turns out to be two or a lot of strips. That is, it turns out that quantum particles know the future! At the moment of photon departure, when it is UNKNOWN to anyone whether the sensor will be installed, the photon already knows the answer!



So, if consciousness is built on quanta, and quanta know the future, then ... consciousness is able to know the future!
