Nixp.conf / 2011



One of my comrades ( shurup ) holds a small conference, but due to certain circumstances and disagreements with the administration of the great and mighty he, due to "religious convictions", decided not to publish an announcement of the event here. Correct this unfortunate misunderstanding.

On May 25, 2011, the first free software conference will be held in Moscow, organized by the Russian web portal nixp.ru, - nixp.conf / 2011.

The key task of the nixp.conf conference is to demonstrate the success stories of the use of free software in Russian realities. Representatives of well-known domestic companies specializing in the development and implementation of free software will share their experience.
