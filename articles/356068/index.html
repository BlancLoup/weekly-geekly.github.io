Roskomnadzor blocked PornHub and YouPorn on the territory of the Russian Federation

The government regulator has recently blacklisted two major foreign resources for adults - Pornhub and Youporn. According to the Roskomsvoboda organization, Pornhub was added to the register of prohibited sites on September 13 , and Youporn - a day later, on September 14 . Consequently, today all telecom operators in the Russian Federation must finally restrict access to the above resources for their subscribers.

Regulator's actions did not go unnoticed, not only among subscribers, but also among companies and the administration of blocked resources.

Thus, the blocking caused mass complaints on Twitter from users on the inaccessibility of Pornhub in the territory of the Russian Federation.
