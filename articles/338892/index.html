Moscow Exchange will hold InvestTECH 2017 conference on September 29

September 29, 2017, the annual InvestTECH 2017 conference will be held on the current technological trends in the Russian financial market. The event will be attended by representatives of the regulator, heads of banks, brokerage and investment companies, as well as leaders in the field of investment technologies.

The conference will be opened by the discussion panel ‚ÄúInvestment fintech in Russia: results of the year, future prospects‚Äù, which will be devoted to global and Russian technological trends, current regulatory issues of fintech projects, as well as industry prospects for the coming years.

Participants in the final discussion panel will discuss the possibilities and prospects for raising capital in the cryptocurrency market - ICO vs IPO.

Event speakers: Bank of Russia, Sberbank, QIWI, MTS, BCS, Finam, Alpha Capital, FinEx, ARQA, Target Global, Fintech Lab, Invest-Idei.ru, StartTrack, VentureClub, Banki.ru, AI-Community, Conomy, Waves Platform and project kript.io.
