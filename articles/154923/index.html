New HabrAjax Theme

HabAjax - a script that has been around for about a year, performs a lot of small and large work on the design of the site pages. Starting from about 20 page processing functions, it developed and gained new features. It is supported by 3 main browsers - Firefox, Opera, Chrome and a few moved away from the support of the 4th browser - Safari. Its main goal is to make the pages more compact and with a smaller amount of graphic garbage - elements that you can do without.
