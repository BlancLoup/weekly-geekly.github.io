Startup v-teme.com is open

I propose to Hrabo people to evaluate this startup; functionally services that were planned to be launched at the first stage, work, a week for beta testing and improvements, then the beginning of an advertising campaign.
Briefly about services:
Website Builder is a service that allows you to create a website in half an hour even for novice Internet users. Examples of sites that portal visitors have already created with the help of Designer: lady.v-teme.com zemlya.v-teme.com
Its competitive advantage over similar services is ease of use with a rather extensive functionality.
Resume Service - allows you to create a resume on an existing template, take a test on a psychologist questionnaire, place a portfolio, place a video summary. Competitive advantage - the possibility of a video interview of the employer and the applicant.
Video library - a service for posting and viewing videos. Competitive advantage - we pay from 5 to 10 dollars per 1000 impressions depending on the subject of the video.
Quizzes and contests for cash prizes are also provided (for the most visited site in the system, for the most interactive resource, for the best photo, video, etc.)
