I think that very few people now know that right after the birthday of the leader of the world proletariat is an equally important date. After all, April 23 is a symbolic day for world literature - World Book and Copyright Day . The date was marked mainly by not too joyful events for world literature, since in 1616 W. Shakespeare, M. Cervantes, Inca Garcilaso de la Vega died on this day. It is also the birthday or death of such famous authors as Maurice Druon, X. Laxness and Manuel Mejia Vallejo.

In 1995, in Paris, the UNESCO General Conference decided to pay tribute to books and authors on this day, urging everyone, especially young people, to enjoy reading and respect the indispensable contribution of those who contributed to the social and cultural progress of mankind.
