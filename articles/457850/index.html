Where does ABBYY Road lead


What summer is waiting for a man who works at ABBYY? Bright and filled with new experiences. To do this, back in 2008, we came up with ‚ÄúSummer with ABBYY‚Äù - a series of various trips, excursions and hikes. The first ABBYY Road was held in 2010. Then 32 travelers from ABBYY on 10 cars went for two days on the route Moscow - Sergiev Posad - Kalyazin - Uglich - Myshkin - Moscow, and the vice-president of ABBYY Aram Pakhchanyan (in the photo - from the right edge) led the rally. Since then, the routes have changed every year, but the essence of the journey remains the same. Meet, talk and make friends with colleagues, discover new places, wander around small Russian cities.

The auto race always takes two days. On Saturday morning we leave the city to look at interesting places, spend the night in a hotel at the most distant point of the route and return to Moscow on Sunday evening.

Few calculations


And now it's time to talk about several important features of ABBYY Road.

  • As experience of past years has shown, a group of 11-13 crews can be the most mobile and organized. When there are more cars, it is difficult to keep up with all the excursions and activities in time.
  • It is comfortable to participate in one rally 40-50 people. All will fit into one hotel, without problems will be divided into a couple of excursion groups (20-25 people each), all together will be able to eat in one cafe. If more people go, you will have to separate the participants, but you do not want to do this.
  • In order for as many interested persons as possible to participate in ABBYY Road, the check-in always takes place twice, on the same route. This year we have chosen for travel on May 18-19 and June 8-9.
  • "Horseless" participants will always find a place in one of the machines.
  • Only one guest can be taken on a trip (a friend, girlfriend, relative. If you have several children, you can take them all).
  • We always ask participants to count on their strength and sign up for the mileage, only if they are ready to drive or in a car for 5-6 hours. In the case of a trip to the Lipetsk region - 6-8 hours.
  • We usually leave early - about 6-7 hours. Passengers agree in advance with the drivers how they will get to the venue.

This time about 30 people signed up for the first two minutes. And in the next couple of days - another 39.

Direction : Kudykina Mountain - Galichya Mountain

The next day we went to see Galichia mountain - one of the smallest reserves in the world. He even got into the Guinness Book of Records. Here is a nursery of rare birds of prey. Falcon falcons and other endangered species are bred in it. And the birds from birth do not contact with people: for them create conditions as close as possible to the wild nature. Every year on the Galicia mountain several dozen falcons-falcons are released. Another part is sold to Arab countries, where the tradition of falconry has long existed. In addition, the falcons, born on Galich mountain, serve in Moscow airports: birds of prey scare migratory birds, which can interfere with the take-off and landing of aircraft.

In addition to falcons, owls, splushki, owl, goshawk, eagles and other birds live in the park. Some of them can be seen without cells at arm's length, but still it‚Äôs not worth pulling hands to them.
