ESports competitions online



Being, like most Habr users, partly a gamer, I want to tell you about a thing called WGS (World Gaming System) .

This is a site that takes all the risks and the accompanying hemorrhoids associated with the sensible organization of game competitions online. The WGS shares this niche in the domestic market together in the ESL (Electronic Sports League). Characteristically, only the WGS administration can create tournaments, players are not given this opportunity. But I must say that the list of events already running looks good.

The project itself is primarily focused on professional cybersportsmen, but for beginners there is something to do in the vast WGS.
