Registration for the only international conference in Russia dedicated to the development and promotion of games for mobile platforms has opened. It will take place on February 8-9, 2013 in St. Petersburg.



Winter Nights is the winter version of our White Nights: Mobile Games Conference , which was first held in June of this year. It gathered about 400 developers and publishers of mobile games. For two days, speakers from Rovio, Google, Microsoft, Flurry Analytics, Big Fish, Nevosoft, and others spoke, round tables and business meetings were held.

The initial idea was to hold such a conference once a year, but we collected so many positive reviews and saw such interest from the participants that we decided to increase the pace. And the mobile gaming industry itself is changing so rapidly that a year for it is a very long time.
