3CX and Patton become strategic partners

The strategic partnership of 3CX and Patton guarantees the full compatibility of SmartNode and 3CX Phone System gateways. Before the official release, all of the Patton SmartNode gateways were fully tested for compatibility with the 3CX Phone System . Also an important consequence of cooperation is that customers and partners can now count on full technical support from both companies.



Why do businesses need VoIP gateways?


Despite the popularity of VoIP, companies insist on using their existing telephone lines, as they still have unprecedented reliability. At the same time, some feel the need to integrate their existing telephone equipment and faxes into a VoIP system. For these reasons, companies now need VoIP-gateways.

Patton gateways are excellent expansion options because they are more reliable and easy to maintain. They also allow you to make 3CXPhoneSystem virtualization using Hyper-V or VMware. Having a reputation for quality equipment, Patton gateways provide the stability that PBXs need.
