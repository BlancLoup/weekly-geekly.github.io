Global Accessibility Awareness Day

May 9 is celebrated not only Victory Day. A year ago, Jennison Asuncion and Joe Devon organized the first Global Accessibility Awareness Day, which was dedicated to digital accessibility, because people with heightened needs are also active users of the Internet. However, creating a website or online service is not all thinking about it. In order to touch the accessibility of the web, it is proposed to drop the mouse for several hours, use screen readers, VoiceOver and special features of mobile devices and OS for surfing the web.
