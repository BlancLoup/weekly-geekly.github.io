Smartphone from Railways: dot the i

My story about the smartphone-mobile terminal of the Russian Railways ( http://habrahabr.ru/post/201422 ) caused a huge resonance. There are dozens of publications on the Internet that used my photos.

Today I will talk about everything that I managed to learn about the smartphone from different sources - from Russian Railways to the contract developer and manufacturer.





Development

Many have thought that at the heart of the Russian Railways smartphone is some kind of Chinese smartphone and this is almost the OEM model of an unknown Chinese manufacturer. In fact, everything is not so. The housing, the circuit and the smartphone board were designed specifically for this project. Circuit design is based on the reference design of the manufacturer of the processor - the company Quallcom.


Photo stuffing a smartphone from here .

All major components of the smartphone are made to order. This is a battery, camera, touchscreen, not to mention all the contacts, antennas, moisture protection membranes, various gaskets and stickers.

Here, for example, a 5-megapixel camera module with autofocus and flash. PM450 is the engineering number of the Russian Railways smartphone.



Production

R-Style acted as a system integrator. The contract developer and manufacturer is a Russian company whose name I was asked not to name. The smartphone was manufactured in China to order.

Differences from the consumer phone

You should not compare the Russian Railways mobile terminal with conventional smartphones. It has another purpose and another design: it is an industrial protected device that can withstand harsh operating conditions (frost, snow, rain, shock). It is certified IP54, but it is argued that it fully complies with IP67, it‚Äôs just not certified for this protection class yet. In the smartphone, a resistive touchscreen is used specially (and not at all from the economy), which allows you to press the screen with any objects and gloved hands. The smartphone has a laser barcode scanner installed (yes, in living conditions barcodes can be scanned with the camera, but the laser is faster and more reliable). The karterder allows to identify the user and work with the electronic signature. To charge the battery using a charging cup.
