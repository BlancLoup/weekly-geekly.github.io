Meet Magento Russia - Magento Conference in Russia (October 30, 2015, Moscow)



We invite all concerned and interested in attending the Meet Magento Russia conference, which we have been organizing for 3 years in a row.

When: October 30, 2015
Where: Digital October (Bersenevskaya nab., 6, p. 3)



The headliner of the event will be Ben Marks - a representative of the head office of Magento Inc. in America. and the worldwide Magento Evangelist. He will visit Russia for the first time and will talk in detail about the release and features of Magento 2, the output of which is scheduled for the beginning of November.

Foreign guests will also come from Vilnius (OpsWay) with a story about how ecommerce business will take Kaizen and Minsk (Amasty) along with a report on technical support work. Analytical reports from My Warehouse, Data Insight and Yandex.Market have already been confirmed in the grid.

In general, all the performances of Meet Magento Russia will be conditionally divided into technical reports and management reports. They can be found in the program section.

Why you should visit Meet Magento Russia 2015:
  • Comprehensive information about the capabilities of the Magento platform;
  • News from the world of Magento 2;
  • Information about current trends in online sales first hand;
  • Secrets of the creation and development of online stores;
  • Communication with experts Magento - development;
  • Useful links and new acquaintances in the field of e-commerce;
  • Community certified Russian developers at Magento.

It will be interesting:
  • Company owners;
  • Internet business;
  • Certified Magento Developers;
  • Magento Specialists;
  • Payment systems;
  • Logistics companies;
  • Services for online commerce and online promotion.

Ticket price is 2500 rubles.

A little help:
Meet Magento Russia is a key event from the world of Magento in the CIS and the only official event in Russia dedicated to CMS Magento Commerce, on the basis of which stores of such world brands as Gant, Paul Smith, 20th Century Fox, Mothercare, Harper's BAZAAR, Nike, Philips, Auchan, Olympus, Angry Birds, Men's Health and so on.

In Russia, the conference will be held for the third time. Throughout the history of Meet Magento, representatives of various countries, including guests from Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Latvia, Israel, the Czech Republic, the USA and Germany, visited the event.
