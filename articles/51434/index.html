Online sale of things


Do you have unnecessary things?

Perhaps there is, it can be anything from small digital technology and clothing to old furniture or a refrigerator. And all this quite often just gathering dust in a corner and does not bring you any good.
The question arises: "And what to do with this?"
A fairly common way to get rid of an old or useless thing is to throw it in the trash, but it can still be good for you and other people. For example, the story of a single red clip that happened to Kyle Macdonald.

Actually, it is necessary that other people know that you have an unnecessary thing and under what conditions you are ready to part with it. For this purpose, the project My-sale.ru was created.

I bring to your attention the beta version of the site My-sale.ru .
My-sale.ru - a place where you can arrange your own sale.
Sell, exchange or give away a thing you do not need.

Adding items for sale
So you have an unnecessary thing, for the beginning you need to register on the site, specifying the region and city of residence. Then you add the item to the site through a three-step form (Item Description, Choosing Sale Conditions, Uploading Photos).

1. In the first step, the item description fields are filled.

2. On the second, you must specify the type of placement for sale and the conditions on which you are willing to part with the thing.

3. Upload from 1 to 3 photos

After filling out all the steps of the form, your item automatically goes on sale (to the catalog of things) for one week . During this time, you will not be able to edit information about things.

If no one is interested in your item during the sale, after its completion, you can put the item on sale with new conditions.

While the item is on sale, you can offer it to be exchanged for other users' items on sale with the condition of ‚Äúexchange‚Äù.

Types of sales on sale
Now two types are available:

- Announcement
It is suitable if you want to quickly sell or give away your item, with minimal time costs.
Your contacts will be available to all visitors to the site during the sale.
The thing can be sold or given away for free .

- Deal through offer
Suitable if you want to exchange your item, or if you want to show your contacts only
those users whose offers you accepted.
Your contacts are hidden from all visitors and users of the site.
Registered users send you their offers, after accepting the offer
from the user his contacts are available to you, and your contacts to him.
A thing can be exchanged , sold or given away for free .

In plans to add an auction type.

Site sections

SALE - Catalog of things
In the section "SALE" presents things on sale at the moment.
Things are categorized, filtering by City and Transaction conditions is possible.



MY ACCOUNT - your personal account
Your personal section on the site allows you to:
- Add and edit your stuff.
- Edit personal information and contacts.
- Work with offers



USERS - Rating and user reviews



All the logic of the service is the fruit of fantasy and thoughts of the author of the site, of course this is not ideal, so any criticism and suggestions are welcome!
