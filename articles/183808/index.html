Comparison of Intel Core 3rd and 4th generation processors (Ivy Bridge and Haswell) and NVIDIA GeForce GTX 675MX and GeForce GTX 770M video cards



In this video, we show the results of comparative tests of third-generation and fourth-generation Intel Core processors, as well as a comparison of Nvidia GeForce GTX 675MX and GTX 770M video cards. In addition to tests of synthetic performance tests, we made measurements in several modern video games, and also checked the power consumption and heat dissipation of test systems.

MSI GT70 notebooks with different processors and graphics cards were used for the tests:

1) Intel Core i7-3630QM (Ivy Bridge) 2400 MHz, 6 Mb L3 cache, TDP 45W, HD4000
Nvidia GeForce GTX 675M, 4Gb GDDR5, GK104, 960 cores @ 600 MHz, 256 Bit @ 900 MHz

2) Intel Core i7-4700HQ (Haswell) 2400 MHz, 6 Mb L3 cache, TDP 47W, HD4600
Nvidia GeForce GTX 770M, 3Gb GDDR5, GK106, 960 cores @ 811 MHz, 192 Bit @ 1000 MHz
