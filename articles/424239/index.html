IBM's Autumn Seminars - Containers, Computer Vision, Digital Transformation

  1. Workshop on the use of containerization technologies in IBM Power Systems solutions
  2. IBM Cognitive Technologies Seminar, organized by IBM Systems Lab Services in conjunction with the IBM Client Center.

Workshop on the use of containerization technologies in IBM Power Systems solutions


Place, date and time


IBM office (Moscow, Presnenskaya naberezhnaya, house 10, block B, floor 6), September 27, 2018 at 10:00.

To participate in the event registration is required: http://ibm.biz/BdYGkY


About the seminar


The level of immersion in the topic is average. Focused on support staff, architects, developers, devOps specialists.


We will discuss the management of containers based on the Kubernetes platform in the implementation of IBM Cloud Private, working with containers in cross-platform environments, as well as the performance of containerized loads on the IBM Power Systems platform. The experience gained will also be useful for personal development in the field of cloud solutions.


Seminar program:


  1. Containers: where to start, or a brief guide to action. Albert Khaliulov, leading technical expert, IBM Cloud
  2. Containers on x86 and IBM Power. Alexey Simonovsky, IBM Power Systems expert
  3. The advantages of the IBM Power platform when using containerization. Alexey Perevozchikov, chief ideologist of IBM Systems
  4. Discussion on containerization and IBM Power

Indicative discussion questions are listed below:


  • Have you tried using container-based cloud environments and what is your experience?
  • Containerization and platforms? What does a business ask for and what do we think about it?
  • Management and maintenance of microservice solutions?
  • And etc.
