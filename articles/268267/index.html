Why was Google originally?


At the very beginning, the project was created as an Android application with an API for accessing the sending of notifications to itself. The Google account is already embedded in Android, authorization through it allows you to enter in one click, without entering a login or password.

Next, we made support for the Google Chrome browser. It also uses cloud sync and is embedded in many Google accounts.

Then we began to expand in the direction of the telegraph and the web-push, which, in general, can work in other browsers. We also have an iOS application, why is Google authorization there?

Easy to sign in and subscribe


We try to optimize the conversion to input. You have probably heard that we are reaching 80% conversion for a certain audience. Entry with one button without registration and additional actions allows you to achieve these numbers.
