Meet HP IT Outsourcing

It is unlikely that our audience needs to explain what IT outsourcing is. In the period of growth of the Russian IT market, this direction has been developing at a faster pace, and with the popularization of cloud services, it has acquired qualitatively new forms. But how much do we know about the leaders of the global IT outsourcing market and their activities in Russia, such as Hewlett-Packard?


Interestingly, back in 2007, with the assistance of HP, the book ‚Äú IT Outsourcing: Building Mutually Beneficial Cooperation ‚Äù (Managing Successful IT Outsourcing Relationships) by renowned European authors and IT market professionals Peter Gottschalk and Hans Solly-Setera entered the Russian market. Since then, important changes have occurred in the industry. HP's business is changing. But the outsourcing direction continues to evolve.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise - this is how the new company will be called from November 1, 2015, which will offer corporate systems (servers, storage systems, network equipment), as well as software to ensure their operation. The company will provide various implementation and support services (including financial services) for such systems with customers and outsourcing services that were previously the responsibility of the HP Enterprise Group.

The task of Hewlett Packard Enterprise is not only to ensure the development and updating of all product families in order to offer more advanced solutions in the context of significant changes in the IT market: application transformations, jobs, data growth, but also to provide modern services to customers, helping them reduce costs and increase profits.


In our country, being one of the leading players in the IT market, HP (for the time being we will call it by this familiar name) is familiar to many of its IT products - servers, storage systems, printers and PCs. Much less, she is known for her services, in particular, for IT outsourcing services, although in the last decade the Russian industry press has periodically introduced us to its success stories in this area. After all, ‚ÄúHP Russia‚Äù is also outsourcing, consulting and integration. Here we will talk about this in more detail. Despite the trend of "import substitution", the Russian business landscape is already difficult to imagine without the world's leading corporations offering outsourcing services. And HP has the widest range of offers - from print outsourcing (HP MPS) to business process outsourcing (BPO).

Back in trend


It is characteristic that IT outsourcing "comes into vogue" along with another economic downturn, forcing companies to think about turning fixed costs into variables - to shift CAPEX to OPEX. The desire to reduce costs and improve business efficiency, increasing with each crisis, spurs demand for outsourcing. Moreover, IT companies often position this type of service as a way to reduce costs and the ability to concentrate on their core business. Against the background of a widespread reduction in IT outsourcing budgets, there are chances of becoming one of the few growing types of services. However, according to analysts, in the world about half of IT outsourcing projects are unsuccessful, since their implementation does not bring the planned results.


Priorities in IT strategies of Russian companies, according to IDC . This survey was conducted before the crisis, when the task of cost optimization was not yet as urgent as it is today.

In fact, the crisis plays both positive and negative. On the one hand, contracts are being reduced. On the other hand, companies are trying to solve their problems by shifting them to a service provider. And this opens up a potentially new market, gives a chance to strong outsourcers.

In the first positions


HP has long been one of the recognized world leaders in the IT outsourcing market, offering its customers a wide range of services, starting with outsourcing support for user workplaces and ending with a global strategic partnership for maintaining information systems. By outsourcing core IT services, customers can free up additional resources and focus on core business. The results are sometimes impressive indeed.
Thanks to outsourcing, customers gain competitive advantages - HP has gained considerable experience in the global outsourcing market as a provider of solutions and services for various types of enterprises and their tasks. The company's global outsourcing resources bring in specialists from various countries along with consultants from HP Competence Centers. Moreover, HP has the ability to provide remote control based on its own data centers and remote support centers. These services allow you to quickly assess risks and prevent their occurrence.

According to Gartner ( 2015 Magic Quadrant for Data Center Outsourcing in North America ), HP is one of the leaders in the North America data center outsourcing market. Its strengths are that analysts attribute the scale of the infrastructure - about 80 thousand physical servers in 30 data centers allow us to provide a wide variety of outsourcing services.

The volume of cloud services based on HP Helion in the global market shows a two-digit growth.

In Russia, HP is actively working on outsourcing with manufacturing enterprises, retail chains, and the energy industry.

From private to public. The concept of HP involves various options for implementing a cloud model , depending on the needs of customers.

The data center hosts both IT infrastructure and customer applications. Moreover, customers are provided with a dedicated infrastructure for specific tasks. The desire of customers to reduce IT costs, cut budgets for the purchase of equipment makes them pay attention to cloud services. In many cases, monthly payments are more profitable than one-time large investments.

In the process of assessing and designing the IT infrastructure for the customer, he is offered various options. The customer receives transparent information about the formation of its service payments - the cost of equipment and the cost of service for the term of the contract.

Definitely, the service is not the cheapest in the Russian market, although it competes in prices with the offers of local outsourcers. However, in the service of HP, the main focus is on quality and long-term cooperation, and not on price. Nevertheless, HP prices are quite comparable with Russian companies, and for international companies they may even be lower.

In 2008, HP, in partnership with CROC, won a tender for the provision of IT infrastructure support services to British American Tobacco (BAT Russia). As part of the contract, HP provided the customer with contact center services, equipment and software lifecycle management, including IT procurement, IT warehouse management, and technological support for mobile communication services, starting from the contract negotiation process and ending with processing mobile operator bills and equipment replacement as well as SAP system support.
