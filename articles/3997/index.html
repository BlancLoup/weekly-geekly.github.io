You can book a ticket to the 2008 Olympics on the Internet

On the Internet you can book tickets for the 2008 Olympics, which will be held in Beijing. For this purpose, an open site is organized by the Games Organizing Committee. Adult residents of mainland China can create an account on it to book and pay for tickets after. Receipt of applications will begin next month, and you can pay the fee in July through this website or at Bank of China branches.

Already known pricing for tickets. The most expensive of them, ranging from 150 to 5,000 yuan ($ 20-646), are tickets for the opening and closing ceremonies of the Games. Tickets for athletes, swimmers, semifinals and finals in basketball, volleyball and football will cost from 50 to 1000 yuan ($ 7-132). On the site they can be purchased and foreigners, the period of stay in the PRC of which is not less than six months.

It is expected that about 60% of the tickets will cost about $ 13 - the Chinese authorities have promised to make the Olympic Games available to most of their citizens.
