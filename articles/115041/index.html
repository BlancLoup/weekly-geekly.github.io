Publish a variety of content for a couple of clicks

Let's agree at once not to write that there are already hundreds of similar services? I know it, but everything is a little wrong, and not so. I am sure that many people will like my implementation.

So, Ybex Clipboard is a Windows softphone and free content sharing service ybex.com . The name does not mean anything, but short :)




The main purpose:

  • Removal and publication in the network of the screen and drawing on top of it (annotations). This is how this function works.
  • Copy and publish selected text. For example, here is the copied result of the tracert command.
  • Get an Internet link to the image in the clipboard, too, can be a couple of seconds.
  • Copy selected data from one computer with installed Ybex Clipboard to another (shared clipboard).

I tried to make all the functionality as convenient as possible:

  • Registration on the service is done ‚Äúon the fly‚Äù, right from the program: when you first start, simply enter your email and create a password. No spam will be guaranteed. On your other computers enter the same email and password.

  • The application lives in the tray, and the main menu is called by the combination Ctrl + Shift + Y (can be changed). Press this combination, and then - hot key of the desired operation. For example, Ctrl + Shift + Y + H, and the link to the page with the screenshot of the active window will be on your clipboard. It should be noted that all links are private, i.e. if you do not publish the link on the forum or anywhere else - no one will see your screenshot.

  • All published data can be viewed through the item ‚ÄúManage published‚Äù:


  • When publishing pictures, the program chooses which format to save better in png or jpeg.

  • On the page with the published picture, you can get the thumbnail code for pasting to forums, blogs, etc.

  • All data transmitted via the shared clipboard is compressed and encrypted.

  • Now it's all completely free, with no restrictions on flooded and no ads. Although I reserve the right to kill porn and content not opened for months.
