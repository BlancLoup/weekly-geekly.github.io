4G is the standard of the future, which will give users wireless communication of 100 Mb / s, regardless of where you are. This is at least 30 times faster than the best DSL 2017 connection at the moment: the graphics in your own eyeball, the end of game consoles.

Size will no longer matter



Every two years, computers double their speed. If this trend continues to grow, by 2018 the whole system will be placed on an incredibly small device. This is a gaming device that is similar in size to a mobile phone.



However, if the size of the device itself decreases, the screen size will also decrease. Can we see all the unreal graphics on such a small screen? The answer to the question - of course, we can. The human eye becomes a monitor. Virtual indicator on the retina (virtual retinal display, VRD) is a system that is being developed at the University of Washington. Images will be projected directly to your eyes.



In 2018, this device will immerse you entirely in the virtual world and allow you to see everything differently. The graphics will be different from the real, but this will not prevent the idea to become incredibly popular.
