New form of payment for purchases RBK Money

On December 22, a new form of payment for an account appeared in the RBK Money electronic payment system. The main purpose of this innovation is to make the process of payment for goods and services in online stores even easier and more convenient for users.

Advantages of the new system:

  • Convenient, intuitive interface. All the necessary information is on one page.
  • Instructions for payment and receipt of the invoice will be clear even for a beginner.
  • The possibility of choosing the most suitable method of payment and receiving all the necessary information about it (terms of enrollment, commission, location of payment acceptance points).
  • 24-hour online support - without leaving the form of payment, you can contact customer support and get expert advice.
