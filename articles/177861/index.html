Upgrade to Android 4.1 on Xperia P, Xperia go and Xperia E dual



Starting today, a phased upgrade of the OS Xperia P , Xperia go and Xperia E dual to Android 4.1 begins. Notification that the smartphone is ready for upgrade, the owners of these models will receive during April-May.

In addition to the general innovations and improvements from Google in Android 4.1 for Xperia will be:

  • updated applications Walkman, Album and Movies;
  • power saving mode STAMINA;
  • New convenient search system for installed applications;
  • updated home screen and new features for working with widgets on the desktop.
  • deeper integration into the Google Now service interface

Upgrading to Android 4.1 for Xperia go smartphones is only possible through the PC Companion app.

An update for Xperia S, Xperia acro S and Xperia ion will appear at the end of May, which we will announce in advance.
