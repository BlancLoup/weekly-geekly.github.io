Updated mobile poster for children and parents, reference book with geolocation for iOS

KidsReview.ru has released a new version of its free mobile application for iOS - the only specialized children's billboard and reference book in Russian. To 18 cities of Russia and Ukraine (St. Petersburg, Moscow, Vladivostok, Yekaterinburg, Kazan, Kaliningrad, Kiev, Krasnodar Territory, Nizhny Novgorod, Novosibirsk, Odessa, Rostov-on-Don, Perm, Petrozavodsk, Samara, Tomsk, Khabarovsk, Chelyabinsk ) now added the region " World ".



The application allows you to determine which kids-friendly places and events are close to the current position of the user. You can immediately see information about the company, as well as see its location on the map (now Google). Events can be filtered not only by distance from the current location and by calendar, but also by time and also by age of children. For those events for which you can buy a ticket (no extra charge) on the website KidsReview.ru, the "Buy a ticket" button in the application will allow you to go directly to the online ordering page.



To determine the quality of events and the work of companies by users, the application (and the site) introduced a ten-point rating scale based on eight criteria, which allows you to quickly leave informative reviews that are useful to other users. For clarity, to review, you can attach a photo taken on the device.

In the spring of 2014, KidsReview.ru plans to release a version of the Android application and it will be very useful for us to hear the opinion of Habr's parents about what should be fixed or what new functionality should be planned in the future application.
