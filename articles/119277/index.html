Smallest HD display: just 4.8 inches



The company Ortus Technology recently introduced the world's smallest HD-display with a diagonal of only 4.8 inches. This company believes that mobile displays should be of the same quality as displays of large devices, including televisions. The resolution of such a display is 1920 * 1080, i.e. This is full HD, with 1080p.

Currently, viewing HD video as 1080p is almost impossible on mobile devices without connecting to a TV, except in some cases. The manufacturer believes that such HD-displays is the time to enter for mobile devices, tablets, communicators, phones. Earlier, Hitachi released a 4.5-inch display with a resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels, but the Ortus development is even more interesting.

With such displays, the image viewed on a mobile device will look extraordinarily clear, as, indeed, it is. In addition, developers believe, it will be easier to use 3D technology for mobile devices, so that users can watch high-quality video on high-quality displays.

Generally speaking, lately displays really are getting smaller, and the quality of such displays is getting higher. It is possible that in new models of mobile devices that will go on the market in a few months, such development will already be used.
