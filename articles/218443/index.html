Epson on Droidcon Moscow

Dear habravchane,

We invite you to visit our booth at the first DroidCon Moscow conference in Russia, which will be held in Moscow on April 11-12 at the Digital October center!

At the conference, for the first time, we will give everyone the opportunity to meet personally the latest Epson Moverio BT-200 video glasses. In addition to video glasses, interactive ultrashort-focus projectors will be demonstrated.

Briefly about the products that will be presented:

Epson Moverio BT-200




Transparent binocular video glasses Epson Moverio BT-200 is a unique gadget for entertainment and augmented reality. Thanks to the experience of developing a previous version of the product, Epson engineers managed to reduce the weight of the new version of the glasses to 88 grams! At the moment, there is simply no equivalent to the Epson Moverio BT-200 on the market, primarily due to the fact that they are transparent. This means that the picture is projected onto transparent lenses and superimposed on the surrounding world, allowing you to create and use the most diverse applications of augmented reality.



In addition, thanks to two displays (for each eye), you can watch 3D movies and content, as well as play games in 3D! Connecting to networks via Wi-Fi, as well as connecting to other devices is carried out thanks to the support of Miracast, Bluetooth 3.0, DLNA. The video glasses are running Android 4.0.4, which makes it possible to create applications using standard development tools, as well as to use the special features of the video glasses, ‚Äúcomplementing‚Äù the reality with virtual images and hints. All this greatly expands the scope of the Epson Moverio BT-200 - from the end user to the corporate sector.

Epson Ultrashort-Focus Interactive Projectors


The stand will also feature two models of Epson interactive ultrashort -focus projectors ( Epson EB-1410Wi and Epson EB-485Wi ), which not only project a large image from the minimum distance from the wall, but also make it possible to interact with it!

See you at the Epson booth!
