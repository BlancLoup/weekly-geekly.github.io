Download Master 5.5.2 (1117) - with support for video services

WestByte Company announces a significant update of the popular Download Master download manager. The new version introduced support for downloading video from video services. The following services are supported: YouTube; Google Video; RuTube; Video@mail.ru and Rambler Vision. To download a video, simply add the address of the page on which it is located, and the video will be uploaded.

One of the best download managers. It will not allow vile communication breaks to break the file download, and more importantly, it will help to plan the download in advance (to queue a large number of files). Convenient interface, stable operation, constant updates.
