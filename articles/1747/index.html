The Russian government launched a modernized version of the website

The Russian government has launched today an upgraded version of the website . According to the press service of the Cabinet of Ministers, the updated site will provide quick access to information on the activities of the Russian government.

In particular, according to the developers, orders and resolutions of the government will be published online. In addition, the site will be available to see photos informing about meetings and visits of the Prime Minister and other government leaders. Visitors to the new site will also be able to familiarize themselves with video materials about the most important and interesting events held within the walls of the Cabinet of Ministers, Prime-TASS reported.
