It all started with the fact that I was asked to pick up a "normal smartphone at an affordable price," sold in the shops of my city. My choice fell on Xiaomi Redmi Note 3 Pro. And somehow I twisted it in my hands and decided to replace my old Lenovo P780 too. Especially found cheaper in the online store. I will not advise the store, about it further
