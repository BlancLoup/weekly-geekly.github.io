Work more efficiently



Increasing the productivity of work, you can do much more. Below are some personal productivity tips.

1. Unsubscribe from all mailings that do not need to respond immediately. The smaller your mailbox, the less time you will spend on parsing mail.

2. If the letter does not require a response or some kind of reaction, send it to the archive. An empty inbox is great. Use inbox as a To Do list.

3. Do not use multiple programs / applications at the same time. Finished with social networks - close the window. Checked mail - close it. No need to surf the Internet - close the browser. Do not even leave anything behind. Several tasks simultaneously distract and prevent you from concentrating on one thing.

4. Disable all notifications. About new letters, likes, mentions. Disable everything while you work.

5. Work a little bit. For 30-45 minutes. Then take a break for a couple of minutes, stretch, drink some water, look at the window, check social networks, etc. And get back to work.

6. If upset, take your time off. Walk, eat, meditate for 10 minutes. Return to work with a clear mind.

7. Give someone a TV. It's funny how much you can do if you do not spend time watching movies while sitting on the couch.

8. Keep a log of everything you ate in a day and how you felt afterwards. You will be surprised how food affects your mood. Lunch can make you tired, hyperactive, or unable to concentrate. You will also notice how well-being depends on fresh, healthy food.

9. Read fewer people in social networks. Remove people who are not interested in you. Nothing personal, so you can pay more attention to your real friends and close people.

10. Work at the best time for you. Each person has time in days when he is most productive. Turn off the phone and email, and do not make appointments at this time.

11. Disable email notifications from social networks. Do you really need to know who your friend is? Mentioned? Suggested to read a book? Invited to the event? Come on the social network when there is free time, do not let the garbage in the mailbox distract you.

No need to work more, you need to work more efficiently. Because big employment is not the same as high productivity. Asli you will cope with tasks faster, there will be more time for something else, no less useful.

These tips do not apply to everything. Do not be so effective with your loved ones, with friends, while walking in the fresh air. No need to artificially limit the beautiful moments of life.

PS Add your own ways to increase productivity in the comments.
