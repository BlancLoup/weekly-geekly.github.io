Yandex.Bar for Safari 1.1 - no longer beta

In September, we announced the beta version of the Yandex. Bar panel for the Safari browser. We have taken into account your feedback and comments and have changed something.

There are two main differences from the beta version: the new, more familiar to Mac OS X users, design and the ability to customize the list of buttons displayed on the Yandex Bar.

Here is the new panel design:


There is a new version and other improvements. There are a lot of them, so we will list only a few - search for selected text, a letter counter, which instantly reports a new letter, and reduced traffic consumption. And in general, the Bar began to work more reliably.

Recall that Yandex. Bar for Safari is developed based on the Safari Extensions Development Guide and works in Safari version 5.0.1 and higher. The panel is not tied to the operating system, you can use it both in Mac OS X and in Windows.
