Skype for Android - available in the market!

The official Skype client for Android appeared in the market:


Skype continues to increase the number of supported devices and launches a client to one of the most popular platforms on the market today - Android.
  • Skype-to-Skype calls
  • SkypeOut calls
  • Chats
  • Phonebook sync
  • and many other utilities



Download:


Official site

Blog post



You can download Skype from the Android Market, as well as skype.com/m from your phone. The minimum platform requirement is Android OS 2.1.

Skype is not available in China or Japan.
Skype has been tested on HTC and Motorola devices with 2.1 or higher. It is also quite likely to work on other Android devices, but Skype does not guarantee full functionality or compatibility.
Problems with the Samsung Galaxy S are known and Skype is working on a solution to this problem.
