Logeek Night meeting for Java specialists from Kazan

We have great news for the developers of Tatarstan: already on April 7 we are waiting for everyone on LoGeek Night in Kazan ! This is an informal meeting of Java-specialists with the opportunity to communicate with like-minded people, share experiences and gain new knowledge.

This time we have prepared for you two amazing speakers with two interesting topics. We offer to get acquainted with the first of them:

Vladimir Sonkin has been working for Luxoft since 2006 and currently holds the position of Java and Web Development Expert in our Training Center. His topic is "Asynchronous Development in Java 8", which means that the report will consider the possibilities of Java 8 for writing asynchronous programs. You will learn how asynchrony allows you to increase productivity and parallelize the execution of tasks, and how to use it. Particular attention will be paid to the CompletableFuture class, which allows writing asynchronous code in Java.

The second speaker will be our colleague from Luxoft Poland - Jakub Dudek , who has already participated in LoGeek Night as a speaker in Poland. He will reveal the secrets of how to survive the project and what to do if everything went wrong. The report will be in English.

Participation in the event is free, but registration is required: http://www.luxoft.ru/lts/logeek/russia/
