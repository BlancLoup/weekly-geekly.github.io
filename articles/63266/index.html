AdLabs / SEOmarket beta testing - the most transparent search engine promotion system with pay per click

AdLabs / SEOmarket is a new development of our company, designed to make it as easy as possible for the client to order the promotion of their site.






The philosophy of the system AdLabs / SEOmarket - we do not earn on the client, but together with the client!
In our system, the client pays only for the final result - real transitions from search engines to the search queries chosen by him. On the one hand, this minimizes the risks of the client, and on the other hand, it stimulates us to provide the client with the service of promotion with the highest quality possible. After all, in order to earn, we must provide the client site first place in the search engines. Only this will allow the client to get the maximum number of visitors / orders, and us - the maximum earnings.

The main feature of this system is full transparency of pricing and payment and the absolute absence of risks for the client! We do not take money from the client for site optimization, we do not impose any additional paid services on him. Payment is made only for the final result - transitions from search engines to search queries that are selected by the client. The cost of one transition for each promoted request ranges from 10 to 50% of the cost of the transition for this request from the first place of guaranteed impressions in the Yandex.Direct contextual advertising system without additional settings for time and geo-targeting.

Beta testing of the AdLabs / SEOmarket system started on June 8, 2009.

You can register in the AdLabs / SEOmarket system , estimate the cost of promotion according to the queries you are interested in and start cooperation with us without the risk of losing your money!

Special offer for sites that came into the system at the beta testing stage - the cost of one transition to the site is equal to 20% of the cost of switching to Yandex.Direct from the first place of guaranteed hits !
