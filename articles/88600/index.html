Bamboo flash drive



IT products with an emphasis on ecology are now in vogue. Almost every company tries to have in its range a couple of devices with a body made of recycled plastic, wood or grass, as is the case with this flash drive.

There is little sense from such eco-insanity, since the main damage to nature does to the hull and the stuffing, but, you see, gadgets sometimes get very nice.

The flash drive weighs 10 grams, has dimensions of 56x24x12 mm and is sold for $ 33 per version with a capacity of 8 GB.
