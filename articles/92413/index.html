The third part of the review is devoted to our Affiliate program.
In our opinion, it is much more interesting to promote any products or services through an affiliate program. Firstly, there is an opportunity to communicate and share experience with partners, secondly, you do not have to pay for the services of intermediaries, as in traditional business schemes, but to pay the same money to your partners. Thirdly, people interested in the project will not only be able to use their favorite service, but also make their own small business with it.

Taxi affiliate program ... It sounds a bit unusual, because similar programs never existed. Let's look at this model and find out that it can provide interesting and profitable.

The most compelling argument is that almost all use taxi services very often. Therefore, in each city you can count dozens of taxi services, each of which has a solid profit.
And why not earn some money on it? The traditional taxi business involves large investments, quite a few employees, an office, equipment, good organization of all activities, thoughtful work tactics, etc.

The essence of our affiliate program is very simple - a partner communicates information about Taksolete to the people in accessible and authorized ways by the company; from each trip of a client involved in a taxi, the partner receives money. The partner also receives a profit from each application that the partner attracted by the partner has executed. And the last type of earnings is the percentage of similar requests from attracted partners. Yet the technical and organizational aspects of the company takes over.

In order not to turn this scheme into mlm, we limited ourselves to the second level of partners so that people could really train each other, help and assist, and not engage in pyramid construction.

Pleasant bonuses for a partner will be an extensive selection of ready-made advertising materials, a personal promotional website of a partner with his address ( example ), a multifunctional personal account, and of course, free registration as a partner, which also distinguishes us from mlm, where you must will take money for the entry.
