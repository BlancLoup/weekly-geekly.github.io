Developers, Developers, Developers! Direct broadcast of PDC 2010 in Moscow pub

If you want to watch the broadcast of the main Microsoft conference for developers in the company of your colleagues in one of Moscow pubs, you will find details under the cut

Since 1991, the most significant announcements of Microsoft on the development of the platform have been made at the PDC. This year will not be an exception either. The most advanced developers and architects will gather in Redmond to hear first-hand about the technologies of a new generation: cloud computing, mobile devices, development tools, the Internet and games. All sessions will be exclusively technical.

Join us for sharing online viewing at the "home setting" of the PDC plenary, which we will organize for you in one of Moscow pubs. The event is free, you must register to participate. After registration you will be sent a letter with details about the place and time of the meeting.

For those of you who are not able to take part in our small event, do not despair, the live broadcast of the conference will begin on October 28 at 20:00 Moscow time on the site .
