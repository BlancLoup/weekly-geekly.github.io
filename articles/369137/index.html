24-ply cardboard house is assembled in 1 day, withstand at least 50 years



The housing problem has been spoiled by more than one generation of our compatriots. Fortunately, thanks to the latest technologies and modern materials, this problem can be solved in just 1 day.

Unusual modular designs for houses Wikkelhouse was developed by the Dutch company Fiction Factory. Each house consists of segments of 4.5 x 3.5 x1, 2 meters, weighing 500 kg, which are easily interconnected. The owner chooses the necessary number of segments, designing the size of the room.

The blocks themselves are made of 24 layers of paperboard. The main advantage of a modular home is assembly speed. The house is going to any specified place in just one day.



In addition, this design is particularly resistant and durable. The developers say that the house is designed for a lifetime of at least 50 years.



To protect against rain, the material is covered with a water-repellent film. In general, the creators of this project are a group of authors who specialize in interior design, so that they have thought through the internal structure of the house very carefully.





Inside the house provides a kitchen, shower and bath, there are options to make a glazed or opaque facade.

The house is designed for seasonal or permanent residence. The cost of the minimum configuration of the three segments starts at $ 29,000.



Sales have already started in the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, the UK and Denmark, but will have to wait. The demand for particularly durable eco-friendly houses is so great that now they only accept pre-orders.
