Nexus 5 is now available!

Good time of the day, habrazhiteli!

On a holiday, on Halloween, it became available for purchase of the LG Nexus 5 on the Google Play Store.
Many characteristics of the phone were known in advance, but some I still repeat:
Price: $ 349 for 16GB and $ 399 for 32GB.
OS: Android 4.4 KitKat
Display: 5 inches IPS + with a resolution of 1920x1080. Corning Gorilla Glass 3 protected
Processor: 2.3GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 800
Weight: 130 grams.
Network support: 4G / LTE
RAM: 2GB
Battery: 2300 mAh, with the possibility of wireless charging according to the Qi standard
Rear camera: 8 MPiks. Front camera with 1.3 MPi resolution

Available accessories, bumper: gray, yellow, black, red and white.


The remaining characteristics did not appear in my browser.
Nexus 5 Advertising

It also became known that Android 4.4 KitKat will support phones with 512MB of RAM, which will allow installing this OS on less weak phones. Google services can be activated already by many familiar phrase: "'Ok, Google". Also in Android 4.4 KitKat there will be an updated Hangouts application, which will now contain not only video calls and chats, but also sms, mms and allow you to send GIF files. Added the ability to print documents, photos, web pages from a phone or tablet. Link to the description of Android 4.4 KitKat (in English).
