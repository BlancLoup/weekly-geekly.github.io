Odessa Open Class Programming Competition 2015

Our team FlyElephant actively supports various events and today we want to invite everyone to the annual competition of programmers - the Open Class Programming Competition , which will be held on May 31 at the Odessa I. Mechnikov National University.

This year the competition is dedicated to the celebration of the 150th anniversary of the founding of the university.

The main feature of the competition is the most democratic conditions for participation and holding. Schoolchildren, students, teachers, professional programmers and talented amateurs can participate in the competition. You can even compete anonymously.

The competition will be held simultaneously in two modes - full-time and online. To participate in the competition, you must register on the site http://codelands.com .

In addition to souvenirs and T-shirts with the logo of the competition for the participants in a full-time tour, there is a prize fund that is distributed depending on the number of solved tasks and the space occupied. Additionally, for the first three places there are grants in the equivalent of $ 1,000.

The general sponsors of the competition are Looksery and Kyivstar. This year the competition is supported by DataArt, CSOdessa, FlyElephant, GeeksLab. Odessa National Polytechnic University also takes part in the competition.
