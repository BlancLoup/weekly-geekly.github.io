Cooking New Year's Company in AdWords

Google has begun to actively educate webmasters on the subject of preparing AdWords ad campaigns for the New Year.

For starters, I strongly advise you to read the post on the AdWords blog .

Here are some excerpts:

A study of the UK retail market showed that last year the peak of buying activity was in January 2009, while sales increased by 36% compared to August *.

- Create new ad groups advertising popular Christmas products.
