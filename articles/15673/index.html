Today the keyboard for blondes is entering the market


Today, November 1, an unusual pink keyboard for blondes, created by the specialists of the Russian company Oldi, for the price of 600 rubles went into the open market.

The keyboard for blondes is a standard input device, which is made of pink plastic and differs from standard keyboards only in unusual keyboard inscriptions.

For example, instead of the Escape button on the pink keyboard there is a No! Button, a space is designated as the longest key, and Control and Alt are designated as the Unnecessary button and I cannot understand Vasche.

The first keyboards for blondes are created in the version for the PS / 2 port, but very soon the developers will create a batch of USB devices.

As noted by Oldi, due to the limited supply of the Keyboard for blondes, it was decided to sell no more than two devices in one hand. "We can say that we have the principle Ofiget, give two!", - said one of the creators of the keyboard.
