Most Popular Torrents 2007

Despite all attempts by right holders to combat piracy, activity in P2P networks is only growing every year. Moreover, the statistics of downloading torrents has now become the most reliable way to determine the true popularity of movies, music and software.

Analytical services like BigChampagne Online Media Measurement , which measure the popularity of torrents, scan both legal and illegal file distribution channels in the most popular P2P networks, including BitTorrent, Gnutella and eDonkey.

Among other things, statistics show that screen copies of films are more popular in torrents than DVD rips.
