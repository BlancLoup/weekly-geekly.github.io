Gum for hands


Handgamy is a pretty popular thing. For those who still have not heard anything about them - there is Wikipedia . In short, a plastic material is gently creased when gently pressed, when strong it hardens, and at rest it spreads like liquid.

The habit of crushing various items with me since my childhood - only gadgets change. I have already lost the habit of turning mobiles in my hands: if you drop them, you will not collect them later. But in this case, the handgam fell into my hands for the sole purpose: to find out whether such a thing can be useful to someone who has a lot of time knocking on the keyboard and tearing at the mouse. For example, for the prevention of tunnel syndrome.

But let's leave the debate about the healing effect of a piece of plastic by a British scientist. Handgams are not appreciated for that. Sometimes you just want to wrinkle something in your hands ...


And under the cut - a review of this thing in the combat conditions of the home workplace.

First impressions

Together with the capsule with handgam in the package - two cards with instructions. On the first - a list of contraindications. The second card describes the main properties of the handgam (photo above).
