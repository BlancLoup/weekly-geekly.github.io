New Year's gift - a paid annual subscription to Evernote


I want to join the New Year distribution of elephants (pun intended).

I have three annual gift subscriptions for the Evernote extended service (usually a yearly subscription costs $ 45).

An extended subscription offers the following benefits:
  • the ability to store and synchronize absolutely any files;
  • 500 MB of new notes per month instead of free 40;
  • Secure transfer of all data during synchronization via SSL;
  • Priority text recognition in photos;
  • Priority technical support.

In general, quite naive.

The rules for receiving a gift are simple - write here in the comments how you use Evernote and why you need a paid subscription ( you can write not about yourself, but about your friends or about your beloved grandmother, if she needs a paid account). At the end of the day, I will select the three most liked comments (original / touching / detailed / unusual / etc.) And write to the authors of these comments with a private message in order to find out the e-mail address to which to send a gift certificate.

Holiday greetings!

UPD: Distribution of elephants completed successfully.
