How convenient to read book prints

I printed a book on the printer; It turned out a stack of a hundred sheets. And, since the sheets are filled on all sides, when reading, I have to decide too often: turn the sheet over or put it off for good. Inconvenient.

The algorithm for calculating for each page I have about this:
- find and count the number at the bottom of the page,
- understand it even or not,
- remember that the odd corresponds to the first side of the sheet, even to the second,
- remember that if now the second side, then the third will not be and the page should be postponed; otherwise, flip.

At the same time, the cache for me does not work - during the fascinating (yes any) reading of the page it erases completely. At least there is no certainty, and without it the cache is not a cache.

As a result, I get periodic and frequent:

- interrupts the flow of perception
- and the frustrations of it all.
