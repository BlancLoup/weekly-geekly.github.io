sNews is the smallest CMS

sNews is one of the most compact CMS size of just 100 KB of php code. Its compactness did not go to the detriment of its functionality and is one of the most optimal for creating a simple personal page or a little blogging.

In the development of this CMS, the most necessary needs were taken into account, including the built-in mini page editor, optimized links for search queries, such as CNC, RSS feed, archive of the site, output of the latest news and comments, a site map as well as a contact form.

The minimum configuration consists of only 5 files:
.htaccess - apache configuration file
index.php - website template
readme.html - installation and configuration instructions
snews.php - CMS itself
style.css - stylish cascading shits :)

For especially lazy, 24 quite good CSS templates are provided.
