Broadcast of the final cup of innovative projects

Today, December 5, the final of the Cup of innovative and technological projects . The competition was held for two months and today the winner will be determined - the participants will defend their start-up projects before the jury.

The event is organized by the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology together with the Russian Venture Company, the Russian Corporation of Nanotechnologies (RUSNANO), the companies Troika Dialog, NVisionGroup and Autodesk. The participants are young scientists, students and graduate students of Moscow universities. The prize fund of the competition is 325 thousand rubles.

The final will be held in Moscow at Novokuznetskaya metro station, per. Klimentovskiy d. 1/18

The broadcast of this event will be available around 2 pm Moscow time. For viewing you can, for example, use VLC Player .

Enjoy watching ;)
