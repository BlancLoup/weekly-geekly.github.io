Correct bids with examples, getting started


This is perhaps the main part of my story. How you can make and throw bids will determine the profitability of your work.
  • Immediately introduce yourself as an individual, no need to invent any nonsense and write in the style of "we are a talented young company / web studio that satisfies everyone left and right." In the bidet you can briefly list the advantages of working with an individual.
  • Do not write generic bids (I have super development experience, I know all the technologies, I will do your project at the highest level). Laziness is a good thing, but it is here that you need to overcome it.
  • Insert 3-4 links to specific thematic screenshots. Links to the portfolio are very long so use tinyurl.com
  • Insert a short plan with the stages of work (deadlines and prices)
  • Leave in the text and the price the possibility of maneuver for the customer

Financial issues, conflicting prices, payments, withdrawals


Cost of participation for the event

The cost of participation - approximately 250-400r per month (depends on the number of bids, they can be re-purchased)
Payment to Elansu, percentage of the transaction - 10%

Payment - by plastic card or from an internal account, there are no problems.

Payments, conflicts

Use escrow - the designer is much easier to throw than the programmer (personally, I can not stand pinching the gips and setting up the watermark), so use a secure transaction. This is generally accepted. In general, the choice of payment model determines the customer when placing the project, you can only confirm.

In the event of a conflict with the customer, we can refer to elance arbitration to resolve the issue. Arbitrage works only with money deposited into an escrow account.

The essence of arbitration is elance trying to act as a regulating party and resolve the issue with the world (to draw up a new work plan, etc.), if attempts to reach an agreement did not lead to anything, then elance itself then decides to release the money from the account.

Arbitration is conducted on the basis of facts obtained from the buisness terms, internal correspondence and the result of the work sent.

They say that decisions are often made in favor of the performer, but I can neither confirm nor deny.
