Statistics of salaries and its changes in the IT sector in Ukraine

On Work.com.ua , in addition to salary statistics, a schedule has opened for changing salaries over time. Since reports are customized by industry and by city, you can visually track how the average salary in the IT sector in Ukraine changes, or, for example, in a single Kiev . Statistics are based on vacancies posted on the site and on the weighted average arithmetic method summary.

The most interesting, in my opinion, is the fact that the salary summaries are almost twice as low as the vacancies . That is, employers are willing to pay more than potential employees want to earn (although, it would seem, everything should be the other way around). Whether the applicants have lowered self-conceit, or their real level is so low that they are not needed for nothing.

To be fair, it should be noted that such a report, although it is a reflection of trends, is in many ways "the average temperature in the hospital." More detailed, but not changeable in time, data is on the site developers.org.ua, for example, for 2007 . Here everything is divided as you please, and although the sample of 20-30 people is not very representative, you can still say that Java is valued more than PHP, and software development is higher than web development.

In addition to the above, it is possible to add with optimism that both sources testify to the ever-growing demand for IT specialists.
