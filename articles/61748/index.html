Statistics and passive advancement

In November 2008, I launched my website www.denkononov.ru . Since then, every day I have been watching the statistics of everything that happens on it. Very interesting.

Interestingly, in six months he reached the attendance of 100-120 people / day. And it continues to grow.



This is the speed of the Estonian snail. However, the site does not advance in any way - I just write unique posts, and that is not very regular.

A very pronounced increase in attendance of 5-20 people / day after each more or less informative post. Immediately after indexing by search engines, that is, the next day, blogs are indexed much more often and faster than websites. And then after each update, if the posts move to the top.

So advice to all customers who are thinking to order a website promotion. The most reliable and durable way is to write every day on the subject of your business. Himself, or hire an expert who will do it. For money, it will probably be cheaper than ordering a seo promotion. In any case, safer for sure. And even better when users generate content on your site.
