Yandex.Pro 2.0

The team of Yandex mobile services and the team of cards launched a long-awaited piece that should really please motorists: Cork 2.0 on the mobile . These are Yandex.Maps for a mobile phone (or smartphone), which, along with the usual traffic jams according to SMIlink, also show ‚Äúgreen‚Äù sections - those places where other users of the program have recently passed, and they passed quickly.

It goes without saying that to report data on whether you are going to Yandex or not is your decision, the corresponding option is in the program. But I think this is very cool - to feel that you are helping other drivers simply because you are driving with the program turned on.

All you need is a phone with J2ME support or a smartphone, GPRS connection and GPS. If you want to save GPRS-traffic, you can download the cache card (60Mb).

All this is only for Moscow so far, but drivers from other cities can also join - as soon as a sufficient picture accumulates around the city, there are a lot of drivers with the application turned on, this city will be connected to Jams 2.0.
