9 secrets of online payments. Part 6: one click payment

Payment systems do not stand still, inventing more and more new features that make the payment process fast and convenient. One of these "chips" is a one-click payment. About this - in the new issue of a series of copyright articles "9 secrets of online payments" from PayOnline .

One-click payments are a modern payment tool that allows buyers to pay for goods using a bank card without entering full payment details. One-click payment is available only to authorized users of the online store - the client registers on the site and enters the full details of the bank card when making the first purchase. To make subsequent purchases, he will need to enter only the CVC / CVV code - the last three digits on the back of the bank card, click the "Pay" button and go through 3-D Secure authorization. In some cases, it is possible to implement without entering the CVC / CVV code, but with the help of only one payment button.
