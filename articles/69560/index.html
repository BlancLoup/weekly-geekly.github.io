Bluetooth headset for fashionistas

Designers from any thing strive to make a fashion accessory. And sometimes they do it well. Like Fandi Meng, whose concept of Bluetooth headsets would certainly be appreciated if not by ordinary women of fashion, then certainly designers of any futuristic collections of clothes.


The Bluetooth headset, according to Fandi Meng, is rather an earring (or a clip), rather than a traditional earpiece. Even despite the fact that it is inserted, as usual, into the ear (I wonder if it holds tight?).


The direction of development of thought, in my opinion, sound. The fashion industry is still a giant, fully capable of swallowing one more additional element and turning it into a stylish accessory. Well, strazikov on a button to stick, add glamorchika, Prada inscription or something in this spirit and sell in expensive boutiques.
