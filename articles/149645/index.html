Routes in Yandex.Maps will be taken from the city to the suburbs

Until recently, public transport routes in Yandex.Maps helped Muscovites and Petersburgers to move only within the city. However, modern megacities are difficult to imagine without the suburbs - many people live here, people come here to relax, see their relatives or on business. And not everyone knows how best to get from the city to the suburbs and vice versa.

Today we have added electric trains and suburban buses to the routes of the two capitals. Thanks to this, it will be easier for you to understand how to get, for example, from Tverskaya Street in Moscow to Zelenograd or from Nevsky Prospect in St. Petersburg to Sestroretsk .

With the schedule of electric trains can be found by following the link in the route list.



We collect information on routes, stops and timetables, both independently and with the help of carriers, therefore our data is complete, fresh and up-to-date.
