UNIGINE Open Air 2013: about game development and 3D technology in the open air (Tomsk, July 20-21)

Suddenly, last year we decided to conduct open-air development of games in Tomsk. Of course, we suspected that the topic would be very popular, but the result exceeded our expectations: despite the late announcement, almost 200 people from various cities gathered, even there was a developer from Ubisoft's Kiev branch.



In general, this year we decided to repeat, and indeed make the event annual. Listen to interesting and talk about the technology of 3D-graphics and game development will be July 20-21 in a sunny glade near Tomsk: 2013.openair.unigine.com

On UNIGINE Open Air, developers of games and 3D applications from Tomsk, Novosibirsk, Krasnoyarsk, Omsk, Barnaul, Biysk and other Siberian cities will gather. By the way, the authors of the article about procedural generation ( part 1 , part 2 ) already familiar to the readers of our blog, the technical artists of UNIGINE, will also share their experience at the event.
