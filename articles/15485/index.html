Correctly made questionnaire - an important step to the successful implementation of the order for the development of the site.

Hello dear habrakrady!

I think those of you who were involved in the development of websites to order, often had the need to compose a questionnaire to start working with a particular client. Of course, this question may seem to other developers a purely personal one that bears an intimate shade and is covered with a touch of corporate secrecy. However, I still think this topic is worthy of discussion, I propose my own version of such a questionnaire. And I also expect comments and constructive comments from you.

Questionnaire
  1. Company name (full).
  2. Industry / field of activity.
  3. The official name of the site (which will appear in the headlines and used in the design of the site).
  4. Domain address (URL) if you already have a website or a domain is registered.
  5. Which hosting company provides hosting services (indicate the tariff plan)? If there is no hosting yet, indicate which monthly budget will be allocated to support the project.
  6. The main contact person from the organization (full name, tel., E-mail).
  7. The desired start date of the project. Are there any external circumstances that may affect the timing: PR campaign, exhibition, annual report.
  8. The reasons that prompted you to think about the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating or improving your site right now.
  9. The purpose of the site:
    1. looking for new clients
    2. product advertising
    3. customer information support
    4. getting customer feedback
    5. other

  10. What audience is the site targeted at?
    Describe age, professional, geographic, social, or other important categories of visitors.
  11. Design Type:
    1. Image - considerable attention is paid to the graphic content of the site
    2. Functional (informational). The main task is the information content of the site, i.e. information should be easily and conveniently perceived. (minimum graphics, maximum text). Analog: newspapers and magazines.
    3. Other (describe in detail your vision)

  12. Is there a corporate style of the company, the intended color of the site.
  13. Words that best describe the style of your site.
    Example: corporate, strict, aggressive, academic, youth, avant-garde, soft, warm, etc.
  14. Sites whose design you like (ours and not ours) + sites of competitors. What exactly did you like (catalog, menu, animation, fonts, colors)?
  15. Is multilingual project support necessary?
  16. Site content.
    Specify the approximate amount of content on your site (in pages).
    Indicate the number of items of products (services).
  17. Do you plan to fill in your website yourself or do you want the content design work to be performed by our specialists?
  18. Do you have an idea about the structure of your future site? List the items that will contain the main menu, draw an approximate site map.
  19. Do I need a website promotion? Indicate the main key phrases that will attract the target audience.
