RSS Plugin Overview



Hello!
I often stumbled upon a selection of plugins for google chrome, but did not notice the thematic ones, so I decided to write a collection of plugins for working with RSS, which for unclear reasons does not support chrome as a matter of principle.


I decided to group some plug-ins into third-party readers and embedded ones. I wondered for a long time from which plug-ins to start, and decided that for the seed I would start with an extension that does not fit into one of the groups.

Revolver RSS



I think everybody had such a moment when there was absolutely nothing to do, and you surf the Internet in the hope of accidentally finding something interesting. So, what this plugin does, it simply simply opens a random entry from the RSS feed you specified by clicking a button. For example, you can score the RSS of new articles on Habr√© and you will definitely find something to read ;-)
Now there will be extensions to work with third-party services.
Revolver RSS Page


Google reader RSS Subscriber



I think many people use RSS and use Google Reader, and if they don‚Äôt use it they will use it, so this plugin is simply indispensable for quickly adding a feed (one click) to your Google Reader.
Google reader page RSS Subscriber


Google Reader Notifier



A very handy plugin that shows the number of unread entries in your reader.
Google Reader Notifier Page


Read More! for Google Reader



Again, a convenient plugin for Google Reader, which allows you to customize your feeds to read full records instead of announcements, is very convenient for those who like to receive all the information from one service.
The plugin should be configured to parse full records using XPath or CSS Selectors, but I think it is not very difficult.
Read More! for Google Reader


Page2RSS



Although the absence of an RSS feed on any site is already a rarity, it still happens, this plugin handles this perfectly, it tracks changes on the page and creates RSS to which you can subscribe in many ways.
For example, changing the google homepage .
