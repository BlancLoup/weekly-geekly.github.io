Smart TV: Intel is preparing a "smart TV"

Soon, the methods of human and television interaction will change dramatically - Intel is sure of this, which in early July presented to the public its new Smart TV technology. Televisions controlled by the power of thought. Computers that dutifully respond to user voice commands. Manage gestures, order goods at home, without getting up from the couch. TV integration with social networks. All this - not ideas from fantastic books. This is the future of television. And some of these ideas are already available under the Smart TV model. As Intel says, in the future, televisions, computers and mobile devices will find a very strong connection with each other.
