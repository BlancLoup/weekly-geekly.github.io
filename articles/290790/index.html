Bank Tinkoff launched an Internet acquiring system

Online stores will be able to accept bank card payments using the new Tinkoff Bank service. Connecting to the system will take up to seven days, and the decision about the possibility of connecting the security service will take the next day after registration.



Internet acquiring is the acceptance of payment cards for payment via the Internet using a web interface. The system from Tinkoff supports regular payments, linking cards and creating a payment form with a company logo. The bank promises integration with 1C-Bitrix, OpenCart, MODX, NetCat, UMI, Amiro and other popular CMS.

In the personal account, users can see transaction statistics, conversion data: transitions to the payment method, authorization, and general statistics on payments on the site. The service will allow you to make returns and cancellations of operations, add employees and adjust the level of access for them.

The commission depends on the type of business and turnover and ranges from 2% to 3.5%. The money will go to the specified current account in a Russian bank the next day.
