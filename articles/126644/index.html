Competition among Flash developers



SumIT and AvtoFlirt announce a contest among Flash developers!

For 64 hours you need to create a game for one of their social networks on VKontakte, Facebook, Odnoklassniki, Google+ on the topic of cars or dating.

The main prize is iPad2 32 Gb.
Encouragement prize - Powerball.

Competition time: From August 19, 21:00 to August 22, 13:00 . Summing up - August 22 at 20.30. The winners will be chosen by an authoritative jury from the SumIT team and the AvtoFlirt project.

Selection criteria for winners :
1. Originality of the game / application idea
2. Quality of implementation (stable work, minimum of bugs, level of design, etc.)

Both individual developers and a team of programmers are allowed to participate in the competition.
