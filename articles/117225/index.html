Ticket for RIT ++ is being played!

Habrovchane, welcome!

We prepared another surprise from the company REG.RU - drawing a ticket to RIT ++ - the main professional conference of the year. Be warned: this time we came up with a difficult task!

Contest participants need to offer the best option to optimize the loading / display time of the home page of www.reg.ru. Show ingenuity - after all, of course, the developers of REG.RU have tried a lot, and the company managed to find effective solutions - both among standard and non-standard optimization methods.

To participate, send from April 11 to April 17, 2011 the solution of the problem to the e-mail box at present@reg.ru, indicating the name of your REG.RU account for identification. Before you go into battle, do not forget to familiarize yourself with the technical requirements for completing the task:

- the solution should include one or more HTML / CSS / JS code optimizations, images or other recommendations that reduce the time the page is displayed since the start of the page loading; decisions are made to reduce the page display time from the beginning of its loading by at least 10%;

- all proposed solutions should be checked by the authors in practice for the contents of the REG.RU main page;

We draw your attention to the fact that only an active client (having at least one active service) of REG.RU company with a valid account can become a participant in the competition. The prize can not be replaced by a cash equivalent, can not be returned and exchanged.
