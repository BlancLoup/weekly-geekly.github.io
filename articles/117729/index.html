Bryansk Web Day 2011


Hello, dear habrayuzer.
It has been almost a year since our first conference, immediately some mistakes in the organization became apparent. So this year we tried to improve some points, in particular, we decided to move from the museum to the university, now there is enough space for everyone. There was also an idea to divide the whole event into two days: technical and artistic-design. This is in case there will be a large number of people willing to speak. On the second day, presentations on the development of interfaces and usability will probably be made.
Everything else we have made a new site, simplified the registration of participants.

At the moment, there is no definite list of speeches, it will be published on the site a little later. There is still unoccupied time, so we will be glad to all who wish to participate as a speaker. The range of possible directions is as wide as last year: design, algorithms, testing, architecture, databases, support, system administration, programming, management, interfaces.

So, Bryansk Web Day 2011 will be!
We start all the action on May 28 at BSTU (Bryansk State Technical University). Registration of participants starts this time early, from 9:30 in the morning. The first performance will begin at 10:30.

Detailed information on the website webday.info .
And on Twitter

We will be glad to see you!
