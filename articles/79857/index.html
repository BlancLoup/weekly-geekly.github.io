LG XG1 Chic External Drive


LG has released an XG1 Chic external drive. The novelty is made in a plastic case of white or black color and is equipped with a special stand that allows you to install the device in a horizontal or vertical position

Inside the case is a 3.5-inch hard drive with a SATA2 connection interface with a capacity of 1, 1.5 or 2 TB. According to the manufacturer, the device is equipped with a quiet but quite effective cooling system. Connecting to a computer using the interface USB 2.0. It is reported that the presented novelty fully complies with the requirements of the RoHS directive.

It is expected that external storage devices LG XG1 Chic with a capacity of 1 TB will go on sale in South Korea by the end of this year at a price of about $ 130, and models with a capacity of 1.5 and 2 TB will appear on the market in January 2010, but their price reportedly.
