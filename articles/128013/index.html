Free fall iOS developer courses in St. Petersburg

If you remember, in the summer we gathered a group in the summer school of iOS developers. They scored 10 people, and from July 9 to September 1, they were taught to program for iPhone and iPad once a week for free.

On September 2, they had a release that we combined with the ADCSpb # 8 meeting. There is no report on the meeting yet, as the video of the report is not ready, but there is good news for those who did not make it to the first group.

From October 1 to November 1, the second wave of iOS school will be held. Courses have become more intense: classes will now take place 2 times a week - Attendance classes are on Wednesdays from 8:00 pm to 11:00 pm , and on Saturdays from 4:00 pm to 7:00 pm Participants are required to:
  • Programming experience in any object-oriented language.
  • Reading technical literature in English.
  • MacBook / Hackintosh (having a configured and working Hackintosh).
To get to the courses, you must:
  • Perform a test task ;
  • Fill out the form ;
  • Send two documents to ios@e-legion.com by September 20th .
Participation in the courses is completely free .

The school program and teacher remained the same.
The teacher remained the same - Stanislav Krasnoyarov , a system architect and lead programmer who develops applications for iPhone in his spare time. Stanislav is known for his performances on ADCSpb, where he talked about Cocos2D and errors when working with memory .



Course program:
  • Objective c
  • Foundation and Memory Management
  • Views and View Controllers
  • Interface creation in Interface builder
  • Table view
  • Interface Development Principles (HIG)
  • Interaction with Web services server, JSON
  • Image view, Web view
  • Application Data Storage
  • Multithreading
  • Useful Utilities Instruments
