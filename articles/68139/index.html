CFI Group CFI-A8989





Very nice case for HTPC from the company CFI Group. In principle, quite normal Taiwanese PC case manufacturers. The segment mini-ITX cases they have represented only two models, one of which we are currently considering.

The case is available in four colors - black, white, red and gray. On the front panel is a standard set of two USB 2.0 ports and audio connectors. Large blue power button and a connector under the drive.
The steel case assembly and without filling weighs 2.6 kg. Inside they hid: 1 compartment on 3.5 ", 1 compartment 5.25" and power supply unit on 150W. As an additional cooling fan 80x80mm is used, which, according to the X-memory review, makes quite a lot of noise. The case also has space for an expansion card (TV tuner, Wi-Fi, sound or video card). General dimensions of the case (WxHxD) - 200x166x303mm



The case may not be suitable for those who strive for minimalism ... and those who do not like the bright blue LEDs on the front panel. The case quite comfortably fits a full-size BD-DVD-CD drive, although I would, on the manufacturer's site, choose a slim solution.

The average retail price for the device - 1700r . In my opinion - very worthy for such a pretty body.

SilverStone LC12B





The second body of the company SilverStone looks almost like the first, only smaller in size and made of aluminum. The purpose of the LC12B is a compact media center, without any frills. The input for the power cord is a compact jack, allowing you not to spoil the aesthetics with a thick power cable.



Unlike competitors, it also has a FireWire connector on its front panel, in case there is a mini-ITX board, with its support. Traditionally, inside you can put a thin optical drive and a 3.5 ‚Äùhard disk.
