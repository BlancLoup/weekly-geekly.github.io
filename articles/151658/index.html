HTC Desire V - more than two in one

Without a mobile phone now as without hands. Many respondents participating in public opinion polls confessed that they would rather leave the house without a wallet than without a telephone. However, with the popularization of contactless payment technologies, the smartphone will be quite capable of replacing the contents of the purse with you.



With the development of mobile communications, the requirements for it from the end users have multiplied. The penetration of cellular networks has also steadily increased, and it is much easier to meet people with several phones in large cities than they do without them.

If you have a personal number where conversations are carried out with relatives and friends, friends and relatives, you would hardly want to change it. However, many companies resort to corporate services to optimize and control communication costs, and this automatically means receiving another SIM card.



Let two phones may seem safer from the standpoint of probability theory, but in terms of convenience and practicality, this is far from being the case. It is necessary to monitor the status of data synchronization on both devices, to charge them in time and so on. In the end, one device can be accidentally forgotten or lost, which will result in a waste of time, possible fines and unplanned expenses.

Of course, manufacturers could not respond to the situation and demonstrated a number of solutions that can work with two SIM-cards. Over time, these devices occupied their niche, but, as a rule, they never went beyond the budget segment.

With the release of HTC Desire V, the alignment of forces has changed. The device, capable of working with two SIM-cards, has never before been presented to the market in such an elegant guise. Equipped with support for all modern cellular bands, the Desire V is only 9 mm thick, and its dimensions are 118.5 mm long and 62 mm wide, respectively. The device weighs 114 g, leaving a pleasant feeling of quality materials.



The smartphone runs on Android 4.0.3 Ice Cream Sandwich, and the convenience of working with it provides a proprietary shell HTC Sense 4.0. Responsiveness of the device provides a combination of components such as the Qualcomm MSM7227A chipset, supporting ARM v7 instruction set, as well as 512 MB of RAM. Built-in at the same time provided 4 GB, and another 25 GB you get in the form of cloud storage, represented by the popular service Dropbox. The period of free use of this space is 2 years, but after the expiration of the time the files will not be deleted, it will not be possible to download new ones.



Bright and high-quality TFT-display with a diagonal of 4 "and excellent viewing angles built on technology SuperLCD, which guarantees realistic color reproduction and decent behavior in the sun. The screen resolution is standard for these sizes and is 800x480 pixels, which allows you to run any popular games and applications without worrying about possible compatibility issues.

The five-megapixel camera is equipped with autofocus and LED flash, and its interface is as convenient as in models of the One series. Autonomy provides a battery capacity of 1650 mAh, so you can safely count on a full day with active use and up to two days in case of moderate use.
