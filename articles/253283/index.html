Microsoft Technology Expedition Starts March 18 in St. Petersburg

March 18 in St. Petersburg will host the first conference of the technological expedition of Microsoft in 13 cities in Russia and the CIS. The electronic payment system PayOnline , the global partner of the expedition, invites developers using Microsoft products to follow the expedition, participate in contests and register for events at http://msdevtour.ru .




On the eve of the launch of Windows 10, the release of Visual Studio 2015 and the upgrade of Azure and Office 365 cloud services, the Microsoft team decided to talk to the developers in person. To make the trip as interactive and memorable as possible, Microsoft decided not just to hold events in various cities, but to set off on a real technological expedition on a specially equipped trailer!

From mid-March to late April, the Microsoft trailer will travel to major cities, where 13 conferences will be held, in which more than 3,500 participants and 25,000 viewers of two online broadcasts are expected.

The first cultural expedition will be taken by the cultural capital of Russia - St. Petersburg. The conference will be held on Wednesday, March 18.
At the event, guests will receive presentations on Windows, Web and Cloud from leading experts in the field of application development, web development and cloud development.



Also, guests of the event will be able to visit the exhibition of the latest models of devices under Windows, get acquainted with partner offers and chat with experts in an informal setting. And selected participants will receive an exclusive opportunity to get into the trailer, where they organize special zones dedicated to Xbox, Windows and Visual Studio.

In addition to the main program of events, in four cities, Microsoft will hold night hackathons:
  • March 21-22, Minsk - Game Hackathon
  • April 4-5, Nizhny Novgorod - Hakaton IoT
  • April 11-12, Perm - Apps Hackathon
  • April 25-26, Tomsk - Apps Hackathon

In addition, everyone has the opportunity to get unique souvenirs from the Microsoft Developer Tour! You just need to have time to make a creative photo on the background of the trailer and put it on social networks with the hashtag #msdevtour. Details of the competition - http://events.techdays.ru/msdevtour/contest

The PayOnline electronic payment system reminds you that you can follow the expedition as well as register for an event or hackathons in your city on the website - http://msdevtour.ru .
