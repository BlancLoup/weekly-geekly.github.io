Voxelstein 3D / Voxelstein 3D, Happy New Year



Stumbled upon an amusing project - a remake of the classic Wolfenstein, made on a voxel engine. Due to the use of voxels, the hardware requirements of the demo are huge, but everything looks just plain :) You can destroy everything. With patience, you can unzip the whole level with a knife. The game even has the beginnings of physics.

The current version is 0.101, it contains quite playable one level.
