1C-Bitrix: Site Management 5.0 ASP.NET - Evolution

Habrovchane, I want to report the latest release of 1C-Bitrix: Site Management ASP.NET 5.0 . Over the time that has elapsed since the release of the previous release, CMS made a number of changes, which I will briefly describe below.

Well, everyone who is used to checking everything on personal experience can test it for free for 30 days, click here or watch the blog that Sergey Ryzhikov started as a test based on one of the templates.

Perhaps I want to start with the fact that it is hidden from the eyes, this is an increased speed of work , due to new caching mechanisms. Also, this version has acquired a new installation wizard, pre-installed site templates and new modules. I want to mention a little about the tariff developed by Pakring.ru for 1C-Bitrix .NET, it was chosen to withstand serious loads, which resulted in the removal of limits on the use of processor and RAM .

So the new version of 1C-Bitrix .NET 5.0 now works, not only quickly, but also withstands a serious load in the case of creating productive solutions.

So, what appeared interesting:

1. New product installer.


The first thing the user faces is the installation and configuration of the CMS, the new version pleases with its light design and new features, now you can immediately choose one of the site template types: personal site, company site (services or products) and community site.



It also became possible to choose a site without a template at all and set it up yourself. I especially liked that for each template there are several color combinations.
