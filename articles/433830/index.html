New LED Lamp Diall

Recently, I tested 46 Diall lamps and wrote a review , but almost immediately after this, the range of lamps of this brand completely changed. I bought 19 new lamps and tested them.


The new lamps have changed packaging, some models are now in blisters. A large number of filament lamps, including matte and dimmable, appeared. Models with two brightness levels appeared - 30/100% (they are switched by a conventional switch, to change the brightness, you must quickly turn off and turn on the light).

The luminous flux, color temperature and color rendering index were measured using a two-meter integrating sphere and a Instrument Systems CAS 140 CT spectrometer , the power consumption of the GW Instek GPM-8212 instrument, the pulsation level of the Uprtek MK350D instrument . The minimum operating voltage, at which the luminous flux decreased by no more than 5% of the nominal, was measured using a Lamptest-1 device, Stable Stabil Insta 500 and LATP Suntek TDGC2-0.5. Before measurements to stabilize the parameters of the lamp heated for half an hour.

There are no model names or part numbers on the boxes of lamps, so I‚Äôm listing articles printed on the lamps themselves and barcodes.


All lamps use good LEDs with a CRI (Ra) color rendering index above 80 (for many filament lamps it exceeds 85), which means they can be used in residential premises.

Most of the lamps have no pulsation of light, two filament candles have a pulsation coefficient of less than 3%. Excellent result!

Unlike many Russian manufacturers who overestimate the parameters, Diall is honest: the luminous flux of most lamps is even slightly higher than the declared one, the color temperature corresponds to the declared one (honest 2700K for warm lamps).

The equivalent of the power of the incandescent lamp is specified according to the European standard. In our reality, the equivalents are significantly higher (when compared with real incandescent lamps operating from 220 volts).

Relatively inexpensive matte filament "candle" with the support of brightness control (dimming). It is quite bright (630 lm, equivalent to 65 W), has a high color rendering index CRI (Ra) = 86.6, a low ripple coefficient (2.7%), using a good dimmer can reduce the brightness to almost zero. Diall lamps are excellent - cheap, with honest characteristics, without pulsation, with a good color rendering index. Now they are probably the best in the Russian market in terms of price-quality ratio. I recommend them for purchase.
