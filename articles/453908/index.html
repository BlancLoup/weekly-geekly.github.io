RTOS Neutrino in Industrial Computers

At the seminar, representatives of the industry spoke about the development trends of protected RTOS of the KPDA family (Neutrino, Neutrino-E, QNX) and tools for building automated systems based on AORV, etc. The seminar consisted of three sections. In total, he was visited by about 180 people. More information about the Neutrino RTOS is available on the developer‚Äôs website and on Wikipedia .
In addition to the seminar, a small demo zone was organized, where companies that somehow came into contact with the CPDA family of CPDA showed their solutions.
