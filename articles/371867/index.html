In Steam - more than 2000 games for Linux



Today, Linux users can circle the date in the calendar, making it memorable: Steam has more than 2,000 games for Linux. Only a week ago there were a hundred and fifty fewer games. The developers added several dozens of toys at once, including the ports of the most famous ones, which helped to overcome the mark of 2000 hosted games with Linux support.

One of the most interesting ports in the past month, for example, is SNOW and CoH2 The Western Front Armies . Unlike dozens of incarnations Tux Racer, SNOW is one of the first games with Linux support where you can drive along the winter ski slopes.

Currently, more than 2000 games are available for Linux, about 3000 for MacOS, and almost 8000 for Windows. The number of Linux games on Steam is constantly growing. At the end of last summer, their number was 1500, in early February of this year - 1900. And now, just a month later, there are already more than 2000 games.

However, not too happy to have. The fact is that only about 1% of the Steam platform actively use Linux games. These are statistics for January, for February data are not yet available.

In the near future, Valve plans to add a number of Linux games, so we will wait. Find and play games for Linux here .
