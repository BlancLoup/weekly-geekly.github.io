Coffee maker TimeCup CM-620




First we propose to evaluate the high-tech Turk (or cezve - depending on who prefers the name). She herself is made of high-temperature ceramics. This material does not enter into any chemical interaction with water or other substances and does not emit harmful substances.

You do not need to put a Turk on the fire. Instead, it is necessary to use a film-type heating element. It is located inside the case.

Coffee simply cannot escape from the Turks because of the abundance of various kinds of sensors and sensors. As soon as some detector detects an increase in the water level to a certain point, the power supply to the heater immediately stops. There are really a lot of sensors, and there is even a volume foam sensor.

On the case of the device there is a control panel with touch buttons and LED indicators. In the plastic handle is placed a digital indicator that performs the function of a timer. It also displays the temperature of the drink.

In order to start making coffee, you just need to press the "Start" button and then you can continue to go about your business. Is coffee ready? Hi-tech Turk will report this with a sound signal.

Philips Saeco Granbaristo




This is a complete coffee machine with a variety of functions. It was developed by Philips, and it seems that the company's engineers did not work in vain. With this device, coffee lovers can make almost any coffee drink. There are 5 adjustable grind settings at once. Ceramic millstones will not allow overheating of the beans, so that coffee will not have a burnt taste, which often upsets gourmets.

In this coffee maker is also a powerful boiler, it works according to the Thermospeed technology. All this together represents a system that allows you to quickly prepare a coffee drink.

And, by the way, the system is compatible with iOS and Android devices. Download the program on a tablet or phone and now - we can already prepare one of the many coffee drinks remotely. When you wake up, you can order yourself a cup of delicious cappuccino, and only get up when the drink is ready.

Favorite coffee recipe can be stored in the memory of the machine and cook at any time.

Portable coffee machine Wacaco MiniPresso GR




And this is a portable coffee maker, which is designed so that any tourist or traveler, no matter where he is, could drink coffee in the morning (or at any other time of day). The coffee maker will fit in any backpack or bag - there are no problems with that. The height of the capsule pill thermos is only 17.5 centimeters. For this, a cup of coffee is not needed; instead, the owner can use the lid of the flask itself. Inside the device - a container for falling asleep ground coffee.

An interesting point: this system simply cannot heat the water, it is necessary to pour boiling water into it. It is poured into the lower flask, the coffee is poured into the container, the machine is assembled and ... Yes, I almost forgot, to make coffee, you need to turn the device and press the pump button - thanks to it, the pressure rises to 9 atmospheres.

Different number of clicks on the pump allows you to get different coffee drinks. Clicked 18 times? Got an espresso. 28 times? Double espresso. 13 clicks? Try this delicious ristretto.

Bonaverde - a coffee maker that promises to make a revolution




A startup from Berlin, Bonaverde, for three years, promises to revolutionize the world of the coffee industry. As a tool for the implementation of such a revolution, the startup offers its own coffee maker, which knows how to fry grains, grind them and make coffee.


According to the developers, this device significantly reduces the path that coffee travels from the manufacturer to the buyer. As a result, the grain can be purchased directly from the farmer, and not to go through the whole chain of intermediaries and transportation. Coffee lovers with the help of Bonaverde can independently choose suppliers.

The developers of the device state that it takes only 12 minutes from the moment of filling the green grains until the exit of the cup of flavored drink.

The project team works with a large number of coffee producers around the world, as well as with potential investors who donate funds for the project. Coffee lovers with help

In order to make the coffee more delicious , we recommend using mineral water (without gas), or filtered water. In addition, in order to get a richer taste of the drink, we recommend buying coffee beans, plus a coffee grinder. Ground coffee from the store is good, but a more delicious drink will come out if you grind the coffee just before making it.
