Two startups from the new recruitment of the IIDF accelerator abandoned initial fund investments

In May, the Internet Initiatives Development Foundation ( IIDF ) opened the seventh set of startups in the acceleration program. As a result, 24 projects were selected.

Startups have been working at the IIDF site for a week now. In 22 companies, the fund will invest 800 thousand rubles each, and will also finance participation in the acceleration program in the amount of 600 thousand rubles. For this, startups must give 7% of their business to the fund.

The projects SweetCard and Convead decided to participate in the fund's program for their money, without attracting additional investment from IIDF, writes Firrma.
