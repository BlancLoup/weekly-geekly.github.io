GoTech.vc: Last week

The last day for accepting applications for GoTech is September 11th. Do not miss the opportunity to become a new hero of Russia's largest IT project competition!



24 selected projects will perform on October 6 at the GoTech forum in Moscow. Based on the results of the presentations, the Jury members will determine the winners in the Successful Business and Breakthrough Technology nominations. 8 finalists will have the opportunity to present their projects at such events as Web Summit (Lisbon), Slush (Helsinki), Pirate Summit (Cologne), Tech Open Air (Berlin), Seedstars Summit (Lausanne), Wolves Summit (Warsaw) and others.

Finalists interested in entering the US market will additionally receive opportunities for free participation in the Hack Temple program in San Francisco and participation in Starta Accelerator in New York (including investments of $ 130 thousand for a share in the company under the terms of the program).



For mentor support, GoTech finalists will be able to contact the contest jury members: Dmitry Chikhachev (Runa Capital), Pavel Bogdanov and Alexander Andreyev (both from Almaz Capital), Alexander Egorov (Reksoft), Pavel Cherkashin (Startup Temple), Alexander Turkot (Maxfield Capital) , Konstantin Sinyushin (the Untitled), Vyacheslav Nesterov (EMC), business angel Igor Kaloshin. In total, about 30 famous investors and entrepreneurs will take part in the work of the Jury.
