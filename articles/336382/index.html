The book "Introduction to Drupal 8"

New book "Introduction to Drupal 8": aspero.pro/drupal.pdf

The book can be used by beginners to learn Drupal. Gives all the necessary concepts of Drupal: Back-office, Front-office, Content type, node, taxonomy, block, WYSIWYG, navigation chain, etc. Also describes some additional buns: hooks, Views, Drush.

The advantages of this book in comparison with analogues:

- contains the maximum necessary information - about Drupal;
- much shorter than analogues having 400 - 700 pages (here 25);
- the most recent version is taken as a basis: Drupal 8 (except for this book, there are still no books on Drupal 8 in Russian).

I would be happy reviews and sensible criticism.
