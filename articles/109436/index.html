We launched our startup, which was fed for almost half a year by our nerves, sleepless nights, knowledge, and of course inspiration and thoughts about a bright and bright future. Post about the project has already been on lifehacker.ru . I offer you an extended review.
Podorozhniki.com is a global and convenient carpool (carsharing) service that allows you to efficiently and wisely use transport, bringing together drivers and passengers along the way. The service is perfect for trips around the city, as well as for trips between cities and countries. Works wherever there is a Google Maps coverage (in the future there will be Yandex maps).

Service tasks are simple and clear:
1. reduce the load on the urban transport systems - combating traffic jams,
2. promote the reduction of CO2 emissions into the atmosphere,
3. to reduce the cost of drivers for fuel (fuel costs may be partially or fully covered by passengers-companions),
4. allow passengers to transfer from crowded public transport to a comfortable car at almost the same cost.
In general, we hope podorozhniki.com sooner or later will help each of you make your life a little easier and more pleasant. Drivers who drive the same routes every day can also alternate, acting as a driver in turn.
In the megacities of China, by the way, carpool is already one of the main ways to combat traffic. In the US, separate lanes for drivers with travel companions. In Europe, a car-sharing boom also begins, which has been stimulated, including the famous Eyyafyatlayokudl. Then the Italian car sharing service registered up to 17,000 users per day!
But in truth, very many would agree with the fact that in most cases it is simply not reasonable to drive more than a hundred horsepower for one person over and over again. And, in general, in the world it is already a trend - to use everything rationally and rationally, including transport.

In general, everything is simple and convenient:

signed up, created my own routes and clicked search. If companions are not immediately found, then as soon as they appear, the system will notify you by email, and the companions themselves will get to your section ‚ÄúWe are on the way‚Äù. You can also contact and arrange joint travel.
Creating routes is easy. All the user needs is to go to the ‚ÄúCreate + Find‚Äù section:
1. Indicate who you are - the driver or passenger
2. Indicate the beginning and end of the route or mark it on the map
3. Specify details of the route (days of the week, time, comments)

Next, the system will analyze the route and display a list of the most suitable companions, or, as I have already mentioned, notify by email as soon as they appear.
The feature of the service is that everything works on the basis of the Google Maps API (in the near future there will be a choice between Google and Yandex maps), and the routes are analyzed precisely by the coordinates of the curves.
