New ideas in form design from Smashingmagazine.com

Bright, fresh and interesting ideas on the design of forms on the site.

Translation part:

A web form is often the main channel of communication between visitors and site owners. Feedback is always important, which is why you need to make sure that web forms are easy to understand and convenient to use. However, even in the form of a design, you can afford a little creativity.

Web forms should not be boring, and with CSS or Flash you can easily make them attractive and effective. To get noticed, you need to do something unique and interesting - symbols, icons, colors, location and size of a web form are often used to achieve interesting design solutions. We looked for a few examples, and we found them - creative, original and unusual web forms.

Below we present over 40 (very) beautiful examples of web forms, as well as modern solutions and creative ideas related to web forms. Some of the examples are Flash-based, however, in most cases you can easily create similar projects with pure CSS and (X) HTML.
