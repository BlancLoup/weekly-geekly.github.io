Time management on a personal example: 10 theses

Considering that a person spends a third of his time on sleeping, and most of the readers of this article have spent a full third of this resource, there is not much time left for them to throw around. There is no excess and lack of time - it only needs to be appreciated and be able to use its flow, like a stream of rivers - thanks to which a person moves on ships and uses the power of water in the mills. This should also be done correctly, so as not to row against the current. Even the smallest streams sharpen rocks, and seconds can take shape in years of useful work and success.



I myself in the process of thinking and writing this article often complained about the lack of time. From idea to execution, it took more than one month, and so that this article did not seem like a rewriting of the next abstracts from time management, I spent not so few minutes, hours, days and tried various ideas that I want to share. For a start it is worth understanding what we ourselves want. This is a matter of principle. Not only in work, but also in life. Each of us has a role in life.
