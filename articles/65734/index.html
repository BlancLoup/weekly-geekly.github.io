d3o - elastic material that absorbs physical effects


d3o ( di-tri-o ) is a new material, soft and elastic, until a sharp strong impact is applied to it.
It is soft as a chewing gum, but if you strike a lump of this material with a hammer, the lump hardly deforms.

Due to this property, the material is already used for the production of equipment for skiers: knee pads, elbow pads.
