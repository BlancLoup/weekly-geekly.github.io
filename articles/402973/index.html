Google Fact Check: Censorship, Manipulation or Protection from Lies?

In its blog, the search giant Google announced the global launch of a mechanism for checking news on fakes and stuffing, so the output of the search results will immediately be supplied with an assessment, which can vary from True to False with intermediate options.



In the explanation to this innovation, the corporation indicates that the reason for its appearance was the presidential campaign in the USA in 2016, during which the network was flooded with fakes of all colors, shapes and colors.

The media giant said it would not check the news itself, giving the case to outsource to a certain ‚Äúinternational reporter community‚Äù, which includes 115 active organizations around the world (and not one in the territory of the Russian Federation). This means that these organizations will evaluate the veracity of the search results displayed to users and inform about this directly in the list of search results, without bothering the user with independent checks, searching for sources and comparing opinions.

This is a very dangerous practice, which in the future will make it possible to more effectively manipulate public opinion through one-sided assessments of complex events.
