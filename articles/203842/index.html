Started pre-orders for YotaPhone for 20 000 rubles

Yota Devices, a communications equipment development company separated from the Yota communications provider, today announced the start of collecting applications for the pre-order of the YotaPhone, an original Android smartphone with LTE support and two screens, one of which uses e-ink technology.

The cost of the device at the stage of pre-order is 20 000 rubles. Smartphone promise to deliver immediately, as it goes to the warehouse in December 2013. The preliminary order is still open only in Russia. You can pay for your order with a credit card or cash to the courier. Possible purchase on credit.

According to the list, the number of phones available for pre-order is limited. At the moment, only black phones are available for pre-ordering.
