Scream On Net - New Free Online Broadcasting Service

Scream On Net is a new service that offers anyone who wants to, by connecting the camera and microphone to the computer to start broadcasting. The service is absolutely free and at the moment does not even require registration!

Thanks to the use of modern codecs, the quality of the image and sound is quite good. For comfortable broadcasting and viewing it is necessary to have access to the Internet at a speed of 160 kilobits per second, broadcasting and viewing are at this speed. If you have dial-up, then you can also use the service - the system itself will test the speed of your connection and reduce the quality. The advantage of the service is that you do not need to download and install additional software, worrying that you will get spyware as a bonus. The basis of the service - flash-applications that have a very strict security system. You can communicate with the person broadcasting via chat.




As follows from the announcements posted on the main page of the site, it will soon be possible to broadcast privately and record podcasts. It will also be possible to charge for viewing, which will allow, for example, teachers to train people remotely, earning on it.
