What if 2gis.ru is an additional step in choosing a company?

The task of 2GIS is to make the choice of firms in cities fast, convenient and enjoyable. But, admittedly, we are not able to solve 100% of life situations. But they are able to help a little in solving them. How? With browser extensions .



On company websites


Every day, each of us visits many sites, but not all of them have good navigation and full information. Let's not wriggle: for many, the devil's leg will break. Imagine that you are studying the site of a restaurant to reserve a table for the evening - the menu is well decorated, prices are indicated, there are photos and a panorama of the place. But, quite suddenly, there is no work time.

2GIS for browsers in one click will show you the missing information:



And not only work time. On the sites of all organizations that are in 2GIS, you can find out their phone numbers, addresses, location on the map, build a route and read reviews.

Reviews


Let's say you need custom furniture. You study the prices and available materials on the sites, most of which say that "Our priority is a high level of service!" Sounds OK, but it's better to check, especially since it is also one click.



Reviews help to know the real advantages and disadvantages of companies. Perhaps you are not critical deadlines, but quality is important. But in the general case, a rating of 2.5 points is a recommendation to consider other firms.

On any pages


Another example is any site with telephone numbers. 2GIS for browsers will highlight each.



This will allow, first, to call in one click, sending a push notification to your smartphone instead of typing the number. More information about this function in a large 2GIS we wrote .

And secondly, to get additional information about the company, which we find by number. If it is closed, then it is hardly worth calling it. And if you cannot reach the specified number, there will often be additional numbers in 2GIS.

Knowing what the company is behind the number can help in a variety of situations. Our favorite example is apartment rental announcements. They are often published under the guise of private, but in fact you have to pay the agency commission.

Install 2GIS for your browser : Chrome, Firefox, Safari or Yandex-browser.
