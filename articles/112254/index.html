Google (GOOG): fourth quarter 2010 - new high before castling on top

Disclaimer: This post was written by me as a private person. This post is part of a series of articles on IT company reporting.

Today, Google has summed up its 2010 fiscal year, which ended December 31. The list of participants was larger than ever - as is already well known, on the eve of this, rearrangements between the three leaders were announced.

New CEO (head of the company) becomes Larry Page and will carry out the daily management of Google.

Sergey Brin will work on strategic projects, the details of which are not yet known, his official position is co-founder (co-founder). Sergey noted that he is happy to do what is interesting to him. It is also known that this is due to the social direction.

The company's revenue grew over the year by 26% to $ 8.44 billion , net profit to $ 2.54 billion . You can compare it with the results of the previous quarter.
