Merchium + Yandex.Money = Free and powerful online shopping

Hi, Habr!

My name is Konstantin, and I will tell you about the Merchium project that our team launched together with the Yandex.Money team .

Merchium is a cloud service that allows you to quickly create a free online store and integrate it into an existing website, blog or social network page.

The key feature of the Merchium is that our service provides free sellers with the opportunities that others give for big money, if they give it at all.



Under the cut, I will tell you more about the history of the project, its current state and our plans.

Technically, Merchium relies on the CS-Cart platform we have created - a boxed e-commerce solution that has been successfully sold on the international market for nine years. Over the years, CS-Cart has accumulated extensive functionality, and we - its developers - a great experience.

Now we use this experience to create the most convenient, affordable and profitable solution for trading on the Internet.



Today Merchium is:

  • Free solution with no restrictions on usage and functionality.
  • Service, specially sharpened for Runet: integration with 1C, Russian Post, QIWI and other Russian services
  • Integration with Yandex services: Money, Metric, Maps and Market (including the ability to purchase goods directly on the Market)
  • Widget for embedding in sites, blogs and Facebook
  • Adaptive showcase with a visual design editor
  • The ability to link your own domain + ready showcase on * .mymerchium.ru
  • Adult platform with a huge number of chips and thousands of satisfied users around the world


You can see all this with your own eyes by opening a store on Merchium on our website . Creating a store takes only about 10 seconds.


An example of a store on Merchium

Merchium is actively developing: in the near future we will add the ability to edit product cards and all texts right on the storefront, goals for Yandex.Metrica and flexible settings for the order page. All of these features will also be free.

Why free?


We want the e-commerce market in Russia to grow actively, because we believe that a developed ecosystem will benefit all market participants - both sellers, developers, and buyers. The more profitable stores on the market, the higher the competition (customer benefits) and the higher the demand for various customizations (developer benefits).

Free of charge Merchium in combination with his power removes all obstacles to create profitable online stores.


Admin panel

We plan to earn money through partnership contributions in our Topics Market and applications. With each sale of your application, the developer will receive 80%, and themes - 70%. (If you want to work with us on this scheme, send an application to partners@merchium.ru or write to me in PM.)

In addition, in the future we plan to release an extended paid version for professional fast-growing stores. I draw your attention that we do not limit the free version, but we expand the paid version.

By the way, if you are a developer, then you can earn money with the help of Merchium right now, not only on themes and applications. We invite everyone to use Merchium for free in their own projects, as well as to provide services for the creation and maintenance of stores. No special permission is required for this, as long as the end user knows which platform his shop is running on.
