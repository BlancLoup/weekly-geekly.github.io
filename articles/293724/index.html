Unjustly dismissed employee brought St. Petersburg "factory trolls" to clean water

An ex-employee of the Internet Troll Factory of Internet Research LLC Lyudmila Savchuk yesterday won the case against her former employers in the Petrograd District Court of St. Petersburg. According to the lawsuit, she got there on January 2, 2015, on an ad on the Internet. The ad offered to write comments and posts on the Internet on a given topic. The salary for this job was 40-50 thousand rubles a month, but the applicant received it only in January, and in March she was fired for allegedly disclosing information and did not issue a calculation.

During the process, the company formalized a contract with the former employee and paid her wage arrears. The main victory of the plaintiffs is considered the company's official file, publicity of its activities and recognition of moral damage to Mrs. Savchuk. In compensation for moral damage, the victim demanded a token amount - 1 ruble, Kommersant reports
