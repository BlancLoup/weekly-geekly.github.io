Zabbix at RootConf 2015, as well as an informal meeting in Moscow


Hello!

Zabbix invites all those interested in monitoring systems to the RootConf 2015 conference in Moscow, which takes place during the Russian Internet Technologies festival on May 21-22. A meetup is also scheduled on the 20th. Details under the cut:

On RootConf, alexvl , the creator of Zabbix, will give an interesting talk, give him my word:

The main task of monitoring is to alert about problems. I will tell you how to make all the messages from Zabbix filled with meaning. I will answer the main practical issues related to the detection of problems. How to get rid of false positives and flapping? How to learn to formulate problem and regular situations in the native language, and not to be content with the framework of the model of simple boundary values? How to detect anomalies?

How to understand that the problem is no more? Often it is not easy at all!

I really hope that my report will allow for a qualitative improvement in monitoring, which will have a good effect on your entire team.

And, of course, I will be happy to share my thoughts on Zabbix 3.0 and the future direction of product development.
