Winners of WEB READY 2013

Today, November 29, the Web Ready2013 Final took place in the Skolkovo Hypercube!
Within the framework of the final, an exhibition of 45 best competitive projects was selected, selected by the expert council from 715 applications.
For the first time awards were awarded immediately in 9 nominations. The main nominations of Seed, Startup and Growth differed in the degree of maturity of the projects: from the beta version to the scalable success story.

Additional nominations were established by the partners of the competition and differed in the focus of the projects. So, special prizes for IT startups with a strong technological component were presented by Intel and EMC, the Playa cluster of Finnish gaming companies awarded the best game project in the Game Ready nomination, MegaLabs determined the best mobile solution, Google - the best application developer, Life venture fund .
