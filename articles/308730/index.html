Digital Help: 14 useful business bots for Telegram

The messenger that Pavel Durov invented is becoming more and more popular this year. In addition to being a convenient, reliably protected cross-platform program, the API technology allows you to create bots for an application: bots are accounts managed by programs that respond to certain commands.

Some are created to help users, some make for fun. At the same time, the number of robot assistants is constantly increasing, which can be used to solve various business problems. I prepared a selection of 14 such tools.

@ExpenseBot



Of the minuses: the bot understands only English

A simple bot to track finances. Each time a purchase is made, the amount spent is reported to the bot. He will sort the purchases by type and at the end of the month will calculate the final amount. This bot works with any amount, so it can be used both to control personal finances and to manage the budget of the whole company.

@PollBot



Creates a poll. If you need to urgently decide something in a group conversation, this bot will be an indispensable assistant.

@EGRUL_bot



Allows you to request statements about legal entities and information about their founders.

@ContentRobinBot



Bot-marketer who can assist in the creation of business texts. The program is able to evaluate the text, give advice on how to improve it, and with its help, you can organize a consultation with a "live" marketing expert.

@TransferRobot



Cloud storage for small files <20 megabytes.

@filesbot



A tool for the rapid exchange of information - to share the file, the user needs to send it to the bot, in return, a link will come to access.

@cbr_bot



Exchange rates, oil quotes, bitcoin and gold. Shows current courses at the moment.

@DoToBot



Allows you to set tasks for the team and yourself. A handy tool to monitor current affairs.

@delorean_bot



A bot that can send messages to the future - that is, reminders. Just a couple of clicks, you can create an alert for work or not to forget to do any household chores (for example, turn off the oven).

@mnp_bot



Able to determine the caller's region by phone number. It will be useful, for example, to companies at the federal level to track activity by region.

@Pravorubot



"Personal lawyer", which allows you to receive advice from experts in the field of law and jurisprudence online.
