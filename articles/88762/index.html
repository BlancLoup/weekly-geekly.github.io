Dating service for gamers

Admit it honestly, when you once again launch multiplayer in Modern Warfare or Gears of War, then your game partner is a friend or co-worker, or some other habitually dull character. Rarely manage to play a round against a young attractive girl, right? Yesterday, the GameCrush social network started working , which will give such an experience to everyone who wants it for a reasonable fee. User must be over 18 years old.



In fact, this is a dating service through computer games. In the terminology of the site, ordinary players are Players, and girls are PlayDates, choose to your taste from the catalog, there are already more than 1200 profiles with photos and videos.

So far, only Xbox 360 games (Modern Warfare 2, Gears of War 2, Grand Theft Auto IV and Halo 3) and several casual games like Tetris sharing right on the site are supported. In the future, will add Wii, World of Warcraft and other games.

For $ 8.25, they give you 500 credits, a game round with a girl costs 400 credits (plus you can give her then 100 tips credits). According to the creators of the site, they calculated the price model, based on the average price of a stranger's treat of a drink in a bar.

Girls receive 60% of the money earned. Although, someone can work for free - anyone is pleased when they give her such attention and are even willing to pay for it.

After the gaming session, you can rate your friend for the games according to various parameters: beauty, gaming ability, flirting quality. So girls will try.

UPD. Now the site is temporarily closed due to the influx of users.
