2buylist - smart shopping list!

Friends! I invite you to get acquainted with our new project 2buylist.com . The essence of the project is to structure and facilitate the process of shopping. If you have at least once been in a store, on the market, or in a hypermarket with a long and uncomfortable list of products - this resource is for you.

2buylist - service:

  • To which you can quickly and easily create a convenient list of products, structured by store departments (dairy, meat, etc.), in order to be in a certain department to see an exhaustive list of products
  • Which can be viewed from any mobile phone with internet access.
  • Convenient printed (pdf) version

How it works?

  • You create a list of products that you have to buy. In an amicable way, the list of products is compiled by you once (in the future, you nullify the purchased goods and you just have to adjust it to suit your current needs).
  • Having created the list, you view it on your phone in a convenient pda version (2buylist.com/pda), where you can easily mark each new purchase. Or print the list through visual pdf version.
To systematize the products, at your service a rubricator for the departments of the store, which will tell you in which department what to buy, so as not to return to the same department several times. Has your spouse or mother forgot to indicate something in the list of products and send you endless text messages or constantly call back? Forget it once and for all! 2buylist allows you to adjust the list online, which you will immediately see on your phone.
Now the beta version is ready, we tested it. We hope there will not be any strong jambs. Project free :) www.2buylist.com
