Gecko Surfboard - hide a computer in the keyboard

The idea to combine a computer and a keyboard is not at all new, at the dawn of the 80s of the last century, this form factor was very popular: ZX81, ZX Spectrum, Acorn Electron, MSX and many many others. In consequence, the fashion has changed, and for several decades the computing power has been separated from the periphery.

And as the story develops in a spiral, it is not surprising that various monoblocks began to appear again. So, everyone has already heard about the computer-keyboard Asus Eee Keyboard, which, like, should be released in early 2010.
