The Loc Kit conference will be free.



We decided to make the Loc Kit 2014 localization conference free for all participants. The goal of the Loc Kit conference is to increase the competence of Russian localizers, and we hope that the format of free online participation will allow us to reach the interested audience to the maximum.

The conference will be held on October 10 on the basis of the webex platform, the start of the program is scheduled for 11:00 Moscow time.
