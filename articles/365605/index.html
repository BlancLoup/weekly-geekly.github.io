Video camera review "Videomaster PRO" or how to look into a dark hard to reach place

I would like to talk about a rather unusual device. It's about the flexible video camera "Videomaster PRO". About the unusual method of using the device is written here , and I will describe how the camera can be used to solve fairly common household problems and problems.



I often have to deal with situations where, in order to see an unhappy couple of characters that designate a model number, you have to climb into places where it‚Äôs not unpleasant to touch your head, and you don‚Äôt even want to touch your hands. Here for such situations there is a flexible video camera. The length of the flexible cord is 100 centimeters, and it can be extended. To transmit the signal using the usual S-Video. But the manufacturer warns that by increasing the length of the cable picture quality may become worse.

Assembly


The device is assembled on a solid top five. With a strong twist, you can hear the crackle of plastic, but with normal use, nothing crunches, does not crack and does not look flimsy.
The connection of the flexible cord with the main unit is very reliable, metal, fixed tightly. So you can not be afraid, the port will not loosen with time.

Thought out and such a trifle as a convenient removal of the batteries.



Accessories


At the end of the cord in addition to the camera itself, there is a simple mount for several accessories. Fastening is simple and reliable, put the piece of iron, clamped the piece of metal and it will not go anywhere.
Three nozzles are available from the box: a mirror, a hook and a magnet.



The mirror allows you to bend the camera less and thus allows you to look into even more difficult corners, for example on the wall on the other hand, crawling through the ceiling. Personally, it came in handy for me in the very first time when I simply couldn‚Äôt ‚Äúaim‚Äù with a clever cord, but as I straightened and planted a mirror, I immediately saw everything that was needed.

Also among other things in the bag was a special hook. It allows you to hook the camera over a small ledge in concrete or trite it into a piece of wood. This frees your hands and you can start working looking at the display of the device. Conveniently.

The last accessory that was included is a magnet. It is rather weak, but when working in a metallic environment, it can make life a little easier. However, the camera for him to hang will not work. After some reflection, I came to the conclusion that a strong magnet would be more of a hindrance than a help as I pulled the camera cord in different directions before reaching the target. By the way for getting any fallen screws, this magnet fits great.



All accessories can be bent based on needs.

First of all I want to say about the display. It is cheap, primitive, but does not invert the colors and allows everything to be clearly seen at its 2.7 inches. Its resolution is 960 * 480. There is no special protection for the display, ordinary plastic, but there is a small visor to reduce the amount of glare (the screen is glossy). The touch controls were also not delivered, however, I would look at those craftsmen who would like to see touch controls in such a device.

Power supply


From the cheapest batteries that I came across in Auchan (at the price of 30 rubles for 4 pieces), the device worked for about 20 minutes. For a one-time look, this was enough, but for more serious work, when I had to crawl to the touch and really only focus on the picture from the camera, Duracell batteries were inserted. From them the camera worked the whole working day (9 hours), although it was stated 12-13 hours. By the way, the moisture protection and the shape of the video module allows it to be easily and efficiently cleaned of the foam, so you can not be afraid even if it drowns in it.

A separate line should be noted the absence of a built-in battery. I think this is the right decision as the battery degrades over time, and the device is clearly designed for many years of work and at the same time most of the time it will lie in the closet and wait in the wings. And if you want to use the device often and for a long time, standard-size AA batteries will help out. To work, the device needs four such pieces. On the other hand, it would be nice in the kit to see if not the best, but still batteries, and a charger for them. In this case, you could use the device immediately after purchase and think less about where to buy batteries. I am sure that a set with batteries in the kit may be considered more interesting, especially if it will be cheaper than buying everything separately.

Ports


The device has one memory expansion port that understands microSD cards up to 32 gigabytes, mini-USB with which you can connect to a computer and a two-pin minijack that allows you to display an image on a television screen through a tulip. All of them are hidden behind a rubber cap that is easily hooked.



It's nice that the bundle includes cords for connecting to a computer and displaying the image on the screen. However, the memory card is not included in the kit.
