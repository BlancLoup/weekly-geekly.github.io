HRC Roketdog: Start



Community, I welcome you. Today is significant by the fact that the Rocketdog production center has appeared on the market of Internet projects. A series of small discoveries on the one hand and sharp disappointments on the other caused the need to reconsider the view on the market. While some are busy warming up their own vocal cords, others are declaring the intellectual delusions that have engulfed them, and still others are predicting a crisis, we are ready to help good people to make outstanding Internet projects, and other good people to own them as a profitable asset.

Rocketdog for project owners:
- investments in the project at the stage of a common prototype;
- advertising campaign of the project;
- preparation of the project for sale and search for a buyer;
- advising on development, management, business;
- team selection.

Rocketdog for investors and companies:
- selection or creation of an Internet project under the stated criteria;
- carrying out a transaction of buying or selling an Internet project;
- consulting, expert opinion, project performance analysis;
- supervision and project management by the investor.

The main focus will be on the promotion of existing and new services using non-standard for the Russian Internet formats. Our goal is to deeply integrate online with offline.

For investors who want to invest in a successful and interesting service, a pool of projects was created, allowing to select a project under the necessary criteria. In the absence of the above, the project is created from scratch or a further search is performed.

We are not ready to engage in social projects and projects whose material value is zero (an exception may be services that serve social activities).

It will be fun - we promise.
