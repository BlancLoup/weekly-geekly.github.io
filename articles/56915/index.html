Meet: Minoru - the first 3D webcam



Online video chat finally got a third dimension.
Presented at CES 2009, the new Minoru webcam with two cameras planted by analogy with human eyes sends two shifted images to a computer, where they are processed in real time by the software shell.
The camera comes with 5 sets of red and blue 3-D glasses for use with regular monitors, but the software also allows you to display the image in the format used by new 3-D monitors.
The camera is fully compatible with Skype, AOL Messenger and YouTube (!).
