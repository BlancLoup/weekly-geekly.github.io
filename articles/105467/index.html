Free ticket to Platform 2011 for a short story



Many developers and IT professionals already know that on November 17-18, 2010, the World Trade Center will host one of the largest technology conferences - Platform 2011 . By tradition, the Platform gathers the best professionals from all over the country and discusses the pressing problems of the industry.

It so happened that participation in the conference is paid . Usually the cost of participation is paid by employers, but not everyone has the opportunity to do so. Therefore, in order to give the opportunity to participate in the conference for free, we will hold a small competition.

What you need to do: write a small essay about what the past Platforms were like (if you attended them) or which one you would like to see the conference this time (if you were not able to attend earlier). It is important to talk about the benefits you have gained from attending the event, perhaps you have somehow applied the knowledge gained, etc. Also, you may want to make some suggestions for improving the event - this is also welcome. So there is freedom for fantasy - dare! :)

When: the competition is held from 4/10/2010 to 15/10/2010, the results will be announced after viewing all the works.

Where to post: the best place to post your story is your personal blog. In addition, it can be various sites, for example, GotDotNet , Habrahabr and other thematic resources. If you have absolutely no place to state your thoughts, you can leave a comment to this message.

Prize: the main prize of the competition is a free ticket to attend the conference Platform 2011. The prize will receive the most vivid and creative presentation. Will also be evaluated content of the story.

Important: a link to this message and to the conference site, msplatforma.ru, must be present in your story.

The task for the contest is quite simple and interesting. Therefore, it is possible to participate in it, even for those who can not go to the event and just maintain the collective spirit! :) Let me remind you that at the time of the event an online broadcast of everything that is happening will be available, and after the event all the reports will be posted.
