What is Graph Search?

Over the past month, we have managed to communicate with more than a hundred people who run their business on Facebook and create advertising campaigns to increase their business income and reach the audience. It was surprising to know that none of them know about Graph Search and its capabilities.





Graph Search is a smart search on Facebook.



When a friend of your friend likes your favorite artist, comments on the post, adds to the group, plays the game, then all such actions are recorded in the Social Graph library and are associated with the activity of all other users on Facebook. Marketers use Graph Search as a tool for finding potential buyers and targeting advertising companies or finding useful links that are in the Social Graph library.

What can you do with Graph Search?



  • Identify your potential fans
  • Learn about the favorite interests and hobbies of potential fans.
  • Learn the likes and interests of your business colleagues
  • Steal fans from their competitors
  • Find potential business partners
  • Improve the quality and effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
