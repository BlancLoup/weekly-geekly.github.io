IXCoin - a freshly created analogue of Bitcoin


IXCoin is a fork (or clone) Bitcoin created the other day, but with some differences.

According to the official website , IXCoin started with a new block chain (without using the Bitcoin chain), in which 96 IXC (coins) are generated for each block.
The total number of coins, like that of Bitcoin, is limited to 21 million, however, generation is expected to be completed in 2017 (Bitcoin - in 2033).

Here is a diagram illustrating the emission of both currencies:

Due to the fact that the currency is not very different from Bitcoin, you can use the same miner programs, you just need to change the settings.

Brief information on miners and their settings for connecting to the pool here .

This message will first of all be interesting to those who regret the lost moment at the beginning of the formation of Bitcoin, when generation gave much more profit than now. The new currency makes it possible to correct the mistakes of youth and start forging your bright future right from today, for, as they say, strike while the iron is hot .
But for this you need to believe in the currency. Do you believe?
