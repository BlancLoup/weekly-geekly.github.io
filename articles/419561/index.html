As far as can be understood, the operators decided to cancel roaming payments against their will - they were forced by FAS. In July last year, the regulator indicated that

The Big Three operators have changed the tariffs for intranet roaming, equating the cost of outgoing calls within one region to the cost of local calls. It is worth recalling that the deputies also voted for the abolition of intranet and national roaming in Russia in the first reading in June of this year.
