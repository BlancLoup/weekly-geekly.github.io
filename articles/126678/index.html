Digest of office life in Novosibirsk

Hello Habrayuser, all with Friday!
We continue our digest of working conditions and photographs of offices, today we will show you the offices of IT companies in Novosibirsk.
Chose 3 interesting companies Parallels, 2GIS, Vicman Development.




2 GIS
They make maps.2gis.ru They have two development centers - on the Student and Academgorodok. The office for Novosibirsk is unusual: there are pear chairs, a terrace with lounge chairs, table tennis, darts and walls on which to draw. Flexible schedule - each works as it is more convenient and more productive for him. The company also organizes CodeFest. In the kitchen, team members are served breakfast every day, and during the day - fresh fruit. Each employee can optionally go to any professional conference. The most popular among employees is RHS ++, HighLoad, AgileDays.









Parallels
They develop software and make special software magic: thanks to them, Mac owners can work with Windows applications. They are among the five largest IT-companies in Russia. They sell software all over the world, but they are developing it in Russia and are looking for developers only here.
Of course a bunch of extra "buns":
VHI + Dentistry, corporate transport, own canteens, free coffee breaks, gym, track rental in swimming pools, etc.
