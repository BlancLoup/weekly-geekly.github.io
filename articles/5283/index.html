Blogs@Mail.Ru again ahead of LJ

Mail.ru company reported that the millionth online diary was created on the project Blogi@Mail.Ru . According to the developers, the number of users Blogi@Mail.Ru exceeds the number of registered Russian users of the popular blog-hosting LiveJournal.com twice.
The Mail.ru blog service has already set records in its work: in May 2006, the millionth anniversary record was recorded, and in February 2007 - a five millionth record. Recall also that last year the company reported higher rates than LJ .

During a telephone interview, Anna Artamonova ( antigona ), Vice-President and Director of Marketing and PR Mail.Ru, noted that at the moment, according to the official statistics of the company, about 20,000 entries are published daily on the Bloi@Mail.ru, of which 17,000 are in public access and 3,000 hidden ("under lock and key"). Thus, it is somewhat surprising statistics published on blogs.yandex.ru , which, apparently, does not take into account all entries.
