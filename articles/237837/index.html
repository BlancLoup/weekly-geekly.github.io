Google Play Store will show the value of in-game items in freemium games



Google Play Market users know how many free programs are in the application directory. At the same time, only a small fraction of such applications are really free, which is especially important for games. Among free games, very few toys are really free. The rest belong to the class freemium.

Downloading such a game is free, but in the gaming environment, the user is offered a mass of goods and services, including increased lives, accelerations, bonuses, etc. Often, without all this, it is simply impossible to go beyond a certain level, and willy-nilly, a gamer keen on a game has to buy these things and bonuses. Even adults are not always adequate in the matter of such purchases, but what about children?

Google is trying to fix the problem - they have previously entered a permanent request for a payment password, now the company plans to show the cost of in-game purchases already in the catalog for all freemium games.

This will be done from September 30, and for each application with the ability to buy something inside the game, a detailed price list will be displayed in the game description in the catalog.
