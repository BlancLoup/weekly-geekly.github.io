DNA
DNA does nothing but replicate its genetic code from cell to cell. A multicellular organism is a monstrous accumulation of DNA backups. By the way, this is why unicellular organisms are united in multicellular organisms - this improved their disaster recovery plan. A plan that allows a person for five years to fully update all his cells and perfectly stay himself - the carrier of the same genetic code and configured neural network, which also stubbornly replicates itself.

Ancient Egyptian
Well, the most egregious example of the use of a copy is the ancient Egyptian language. Data and technology

Data has been copied and will always be copied; only the form, volume and means of their copying change. If in the Middle Ages, copying a book was a week of a copyist, nowadays a whole electronic library can be copied in minutes.
In 2010, the amount of data created worldwide amounted to 1 zettabyte - about a billion full of 1 TB hard drives, which exceeds all the available storage space today. According to EMC, over the next 10 years, data volumes will increase another 44 times, creating a shortage of storage space of 60%.
This ever-accelerating data stream introduces new requirements and technologies. Deduplication, compression, encryption, snapshots, replication, cloud storage, virtualization, increments - this is just the tip of the iceberg of the technologies that Acronis deals with and will reveal in this blog.
