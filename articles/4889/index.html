"Big brother is watching you". Almost 70 years have passed since George Orwell wrote his cult novel "1984", and now the all-seeing dictatorship is finally beginning to be translated into reality. Nowadays, the privacy of citizens is carefully monitored as never before. This trend is particularly pronounced in the UK and the USA.
