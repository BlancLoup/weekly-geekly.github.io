One-handed cook? Easily!



Spread butter on bread, cut an apple, open a can of sour milk - all this is very simple if you have two grasping limbs? And if less? The One-Hand Kitchen Equipment is a set of kitchen appliances designed to help people with disabilities cope with food as cleverly as their pair-armed fellows.
