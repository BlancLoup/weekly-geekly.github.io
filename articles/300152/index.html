Online interview with Sergey Yurlov ended

Another online interview took place on the Advapay information portal, which aroused great interest among participants of the Russian market of payment systems and services. Such projects provide an opportunity to ask your professional question to the experts of the payment market and the first persons of Russian and international companies.




The guest of the April 13 online interview was Sergey Yurlov, President of the Association of Payment Companies of the Czech Republic. Representing the Association, Sergey Petrovich answered questions regarding the procedures for opening a payment business in the Czech Republic, spoke about the benefits of a payment institution in the Czech Republic. The participants also asked questions about personnel requirements, legal and economic aspects of doing business in the Czech Republic. Possessing high competence, Sergey Petrovich gave detailed answers to all the questions asked, which we hope will help the representatives of the payment business to think seriously about developing their business both in the Czech Republic and in Europe.

You can get acquainted with the full version of the online interview on Advapay website.
