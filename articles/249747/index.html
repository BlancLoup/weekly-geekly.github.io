Yandex.Metro is watching you



Every few minutes Yandex.Metro for Android sends such requests to a Yandex server.



The body of the request shall include:

  • 3 different identifiers: clid, uuid and andoid_id;
  • List of mac addresses of surrounding wi-fi access points and signal strength;
  • The identifier of the base station opsos indicating the signal strength.

The Yandex service for collecting ‚Äústatistics‚Äù runs in the background when the phone boots and does not require the launch of the application itself to work. The data is sent even if there is a ban on location in the phone settings.

  • How is the constant collection of information about my location related to the operation of your subway map?
  • Why do you need around the clock information about my location?
