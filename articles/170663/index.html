Digest of upcoming IT events for March 2013

Portal " IT-event. " Presents to your attention the "Digest of upcoming IT-events for March 2013."



For ease of navigation, we divided all events into 5 categories:
  • I. Developer
  • Ii. Testers
  • Iii. Junior
  • Iv. Startups
  • V. Designer



I. Developer



JavaScript Conference Frameworks Day 2013


Kiev / 2.03.2013 / Cost 150 - 250 UAH.
International conference on popular JavaScript frameworks. Audience: developers, architects, project managers from Ukraine, Russia and nearby CIS countries.

Meet DrupalCafe # 3


St. Petersburg / 2.03.2013 / Participation is free.
Session list: Roman Lapin (Evercode Lab) - Symfony2 Components. How not to reinvent the wheel; Konstantin Komelin - Security PHP / Drupal applications and sites.

Android and iOS Workshop : Mobile Application Security


Moscow / 7.03.2013 / Participation is free.
Mobile devices are no longer just a means of communication. Applications in smartphones and tablets are suitable for almost all occasions: from making a shopping list to building graphs and charts. How to protect the user and make the mobile application secure? You will learn the answers to these questions at a scientific and technical seminar.

Delphi Developer Days Seminar


Kazan / 12.03.2013 / Participation is free.
You will learn how to: develop multi-platform applications for Windows and Mac OS X; provide interface matching Windows 8; use Fire Monkey 2 - a new platform for building modern business applications; apply dynamic binding based on LiveBindings technology; start using the latest mobile and web technologies. The event will be useful: developers and team leaders; CIO and development tools analysts; IT-specialists of a broad profile, whose task is to develop and maintain software.

Conference HTML5 Camp 2013


Moscow / 03/15/2013 / Cost is not known.
Key topics of the conference: the present and future of web standards (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and related technologies and standards); modern trends in the development of websites and web applications; client web development: practice, libraries, tools; development of applications for Windows 8 on HTML and JavaScript.

Festival Mobilefest 2013


Moscow / 03.22.2013 / Cost 5 000 - 30 000 rub.
A popular annual festival that gathers about 300 mobile application developers and mobile Internet specialists. The concept of the event involves a close combination of the business section and the conference for software developers, individual master classes, active sports and evening entertainment.

Conference European Conference on Information Retrieval

Scientists from leading universities in Europe, USA, South America, Asia, as well as from companies and industrial laboratories such as Twitter, Yahoo! Festival CodeFest


Novosibirsk / 03.30.2013 / 4,800 rubles.
CodeFest is a developer conference dedicated to topical issues of development, project management and testing.

AgileDays Conference


Moscow / 03/29/2013 / Cost 7,400 - 26,500 rubles.
ScrumTrek and the AgileRussia community are pleased to invite you to take part in the 7th international Russian-language conference on the latest approaches in software development. Conference for developers, testers, managers, all those who work in the software development industry.



Ii. Testers



Conference Selenium Camp 2013


Kiev / 1.03.2013 / Cost 1 100 -1800 UAH.
The conference is entirely devoted to the product for testing web-applications Selenium. Selenium Camp is a conference whose goal is to bring together everyone who in one way or another uses Selenium.

QAClubConf 1.2 Conference . Security Testing


Kharkiv / 03/23/2013 / Cost 350 - 600 UAH.
Security testing is an unexplored area. Who will be interested? In addition to testers, PMs, admins, and programmers are also invited.



Iii. Junior



Android SDK course


St. Petersburg / 1.03.2013 / Cost 15 000 - 18 000 rub.
This intensive course will help you master the necessary knowledge and skills for developing software for the Android platform. The purpose of the course: to obtain the necessary basic skills for creating and testing mobile applications for the Android platform.

1st Open Robotics Championship


Naberezhnye Chelny / 2.03.2013 / Participation is free.
This competition is a unique opportunity for students to demonstrate their knowledge and skills in the field of robotics using LEGO, a chance to prove themselves and win valuable prizes. Teams of educational institutions in the city of Naberezhnye Chelny are allowed to participate in the championship, using LEGO and other designers to study robotics. The age of participants should not exceed 17 years.

Course School System Administrator


St. Petersburg / 5.03.2013 / Participation is free.
A special training unit created by Microsoft and Digital Design for the training of elite personnel of advanced IT departments. Being accepted into the School System Administrator is not so easy. This includes only the best of the best, withstood the most difficult individual selection. But it is even more difficult to complete studies at the School and become a real systems engineer.

Course How to create an Android application and promote to the masses?


St. Petersburg / 5.03.2013 / Cost 3 000 - 15 000 rub.
On coaching to promote Android applications, you will learn: what factors when creating an application will affect the user rating ?; what is the first thing to consider when starting the application ?; How to make an application to Google put it in the first position ?; How to promote the application so that it becomes known to the masses of people? ..

Olympics Networking Basics


Ekaterinburg / 03.22.2013 / Cost 0 - 500 rub.
Conducted to develop students 'creative abilities, improve their educational level in the field of network information technologies, identify students' level of preparation, consolidate and deepen knowledge and skills gained in the process of theoretical, practical and vocational training, stimulate creative growth, enhance the prestige of educational institutions, identify gifted youth and the formation of human resources for research and production activities of Russia.



Iv. Startups



Hackathon Garage48


Minsk / 1.03.2013 / Cost of 20 euros
The event, designed for 48 hours on weekends, set aside for participants so that they can present business ideas, get expert mentors and hear feedback from investors, form a team - and as a result present a prototype of their project.

International startup school SUMIT


St. Petersburg / 03/10/2013 / Cost 0 - 30 000 rub.
Educational program on technology entrepreneurship, which will be attended by 20 projects in the early stages of development from Russia, France, Finland, USA. SUMIT will provide teams for six weeks with equipped workplaces, support for mentors and experts, English language training from Hop & Scotch and an intensive course of lectures and workshops on entrepreneurship developed by experts from RIS Ventures. At the end of the school, the best projects will receive venture financing and places in the iDealMachine business accelerator.
