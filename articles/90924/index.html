HTC Desire vs HTC Legend - visual perception of screens

Hello fans of the gadgets in the title!
Being a potential buyer of an Android device and trying to decide from reviews published on the Internet, which of the new products is more suitable for me, at some point I realized that reviews, including comparative ones, miss one important aspect - comparative visual perception of screen sizes. After all, with similar dimensions of devices, the actual size of the screen can be significantly different even for screens with the same resolution. Perhaps this is not so important to those who buy an Android device primarily for voice communication. But if the ability to comfortably browse sites is important for you as well as for me, then looking at the picture under the cut, you will surely feel which of the screens is more comfortable for reading sites created for browsing from desktops and laptops.



Personally, I was guided by HTC Legend until I prepared this picture, which displays devices on the right scale. The result I was struck by a serious win on the HTC Desire screen in front of the HTC Legend, while the Desire dimensions are only 4mm larger in width and 7mm in height!

Now, perhaps, even the all-aluminum body of the Legend will not help her in my eyes to be more attractive Desire. My choice is HTC Desire!
