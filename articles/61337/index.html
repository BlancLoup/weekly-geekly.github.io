Crumpled paper paintings

It's amazing how beautiful pictures can be made without using anything other than plain paper and proper lighting.



This original art form is handled by 33-year-old German artist Simon Schubert, here is the online gallery of his paper handicrafts.

More pictures under Habrakat.

Interestingly, and with the help of this technique, you can make embossed walls, for example, in buildings or in living rooms. Imagine, as the sun rises, a picture gradually appears on your wall! This is only theoretically, because nobody really is able to realize such a thing so far.













Creating each painting takes about a week, and they are sold in the Schubert Gallery at a price of up to $ 6,000 apiece.
