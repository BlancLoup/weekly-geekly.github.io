Communications Minister pledged grants to transfer Russians to Tizen and Sailfish mobile OS


Nikiforov is still enjoying iOS

In the summer of 2014, Nikiforov was already concerned about the closeness of iOS - he invited Apple and SAP to disclose their source codes to Russian specialists. Apparently, without waiting for this, the minister decided to act in a different way, and generally refuse the products of the American giants.

Despite the statements of the Minister, the Android system remains an open source operating system, which anyone can read . According to ArsTechnica , Tizen is a kind of Android clone for the poor. The Z1 smartphone is weak in performance, while OSes and applications have fewer features than their Android versions. It remains to hope that this will not mean a ban on sales of smartphones on iOS or Android. It is not yet known whether the statements of the minister are consistent with the official policy of the ministry, about the extent and timing of the grants, and what development they will be allocated to.
