ITMO University Digest: Residents of our Accelerators



Since 2012, one of the first startup accelerators, iDealMachine, has been operating in Russia, and currently there are 23 companies in its portfolio. Since 2015, the accelerator for startups in the field of optics and robotics Future Technologies has also been working in our country. Moreover, the SUMIT intensive educational program has been conducted for more than 5 years.

All these projects are united by the fact that they were created at the ITMO University . The goal of accelerators is to help talented young professionals to build a viable technology business. The teams are provided with premises for work, expert and mentor support is provided, moreover, the most promising projects receive financial assistance.

You can not ignore the ITMO University Technopark , whose mission is to develop promising areas of activity for residents and partners through the integration of education, science and business.
