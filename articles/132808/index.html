Savings: how to save money without compromising the quality of life?

1. Love nice clothes? Want to buy it at a bargain price? Follow the example of Warren Buffet: buy things on sales! Seasonal discounts can save significant amounts.

2. Refuse purchases of semi-finished and packaged products. They are more expensive than natural and high-quality products, which are sold in large supermarkets and markets.

3. Do you know that the store space is organized so that the visitor buys more goods? With each visit to the supermarket, a person spends 10-20% more if he arrives without a shopping list. Have you already got a convenient notepad and pen?

8. Do you know how much dentist services cost? This is a good reason to visit your doctor every six months. Old problems with teeth sooner or later make you pay bills without looking at the total amount.
