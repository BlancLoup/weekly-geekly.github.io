LEO fitness system will become a personal trainer for those who do not have it



Nowadays, the number of fans of a healthy lifestyle is increasing, as the number of various kinds of fitness gadgets is increasing. Fitness bracelets, smart heart rate monitors, bicycle radar and everything else - to compile a list of such devices you need a thick ledger, and not one.

It would seem that with such competition, new devices no longer break through to the market. But this is not the case - some manufacturers manage to find an idea that makes the gadget popular. Probably, the LEO system, which is all-in-one, should be attributed to this category of devices. This is a fitness tracker, heart rate monitor, and GPS and much more.

What can LEO do?



For an athlete, professional or amateur, such a system can replace (for the time being, the truth) a personal trainer. LEO monitors the performance of the human body during exercise, and gives advice: somewhere it is necessary to reduce the momentum, somewhere in reverse, to increase the activity of the exercise.



The LEO functions include everything familiar to, for example:

  • Determination of heart rate with high accuracy;
  • Positioning during exercise;
  • Tracking calories burned;
  • Determining the time spent on training, as well as the distance traveled.

  • Determining the concentration of lactic acid in the body;
  • Determining the level of stress on the muscles;
  • Tracking the body's moisture level;
  • Evaluation of the effectiveness of training;
  • Evaluation of training techniques;
  • Warning system in case of too intense training.
  • Track the weather conditions in the workout area.




To work on the creation of such a system, engineers, designers, doctors, fitness trainers were involved. The team was (and is) big, but it was worth it: the system really does what the developers promise.
