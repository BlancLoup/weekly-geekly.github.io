CocoaHeads July 24th at Rambler & Co



The next CocoaHeads meeting will be held on July 24 at 19:00 in the office of Rambler & Co , and you will hear four reports on it.


Work with the request to access private data in iOS
Alexander Zimin

Many developers request access to geolocation, photos and other private user data too harshly and aggressively, and distrustful users, in turn, often deny this same access, which limits the performance of the application and leads to loss of audience. How to deal with this and how to increase the acceptance rate will tell Alexander Zimin.



ARM64 on iOS
Oleg Ovechkin

A report on the essence and differences of various processor architectures on iOS devices. Devices with arm64, FAT binary, NSInteger better than int, why do we need NS_ENUM and much more, including App Thinning.



Blur on iOS
Vladislav Dugnist

Blur features in iOS. Vladislav will talk about existing libraries, blur cells in the table and the implementation of the dynamic radius of the blur when scrolling.



Working with iOS in iOS
Irina Dyagileva , Rambler & Co

The combination of the theoretical part about the basis of fonts with an interactive demo, in which Irina will show where and why knowledge about curling and various indents can be useful. Your designer will be happy.



Mandatory registration on Timepad is required for participation. There you can find more detailed information about the passage.

Do not forget to take your passport with you - you will need it in order to pass through security.
