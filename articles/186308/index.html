Conference E-payments Russia

On September 3, Moscow will host a conference of electronic payment systems and services E-payments Russia .



Invited speakers will discuss ways to protect applications from cyber attacks, online banking, storing and protecting customer personal data, the introduction of contactless payment systems in Russia, microfinance on the Internet, and much more.

Among the speakers are representatives of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation, Yandex.Money, Kaspersky Lab, VimpelCom, Tele2 Russia, OZON.travel, RBK Money and many others.

Detailed information about the event and the program can be found on the official website .
