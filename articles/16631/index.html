Let's play in the association

And at the same time we rummage in your heads.

HumanBrainCloud is a multiplayer word game. First you are shown a word or phrase, and then you have to write another word or phrase using your associative thinking. If you have nothing to say, you can skip the word and get the next option. The process unexpectedly delays. For each answer, the game informs how many people still have a head working in the same direction. You can check your "non-standard" or just knowledge of the language. Most of the words and phrases are English, but for the sake of jokes I introduced Russian words and associations, the game doesn‚Äôt matter, and someone might be pleased.




If you are tired of typing words, you can see what happened. Probably for the sake of this game was made. All words are shown in the form of balls and links between them. Poking a word, you show all the associations associated with the word, then you can click on the association, etc. The process itself is beautifully animated and also delays. It's like slamming plastic balls, only more interesting and informative.






And, of course, as soon as the game became popular and gained about 800 thousand associations, the authors conducted a little research on how the head of the average Internet user is arranged. No surprises detected. Here is a list of the most frequently entered words:

Sex, me, money, car, water, dog, food, game, man, music.

Some people are very addicted. For example, a certain girl in one go scored 1000 words. In the dictionary of the game very quickly all the slang and curses appeared. Also, the authors chose a few just cool associations:
