Scrape the bottom of the barrel!



First of all, thank you for your congratulations on the anniversary of the first Intel processor! Over 40 years, a lot of events have happened, technologies have been developed, thanks to which the semiconductor industry remains today at the forefront of progress.

In order to visually demonstrate the path that processor technologies have done during this time, and at the same time to give everyone the opportunity to feel nostalgic, a competition has started in the IT-Galaxy community, the goal of which is to search for the oldest Intel processor lying around or, on the shelf, proudly collector. It's time to finally find out who owns the most rare CPU produced by our company.

All you need to do is take a picture of the oldest microprocessor so that you can find out the age of the exhibit and make sure that it belongs to you. Photos are published in the comments , indicating the name of the processor and its age. The contest will run until December 7, inclusive, the winner will receive fame and 1000 points from the Intel IT Galaxy program, all other participants will get 300 points each.

And in the breaks between the opening of the old system blocks and the inventory of the table drawers, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the exact opposite of the ancient technologies - the latest development from Intel: Core i7 3960X Extreme Edition, a detailed review of which was written just for the i4004 anniversary.
