Garland 8r8s

I wanted to bring some New Year's mood to the house. And to be sure with a computer touch :)
And it came out of this desire garland



To begin with, I will immediately say that I cheated a little - and did not make the garland itself. Fortunately, they are now for every taste and color. You can label me with shame, but I insist that this is about the same as using a third-party library in your software :)

We now turn to the manufacture - take one jack and go shopping in search of a suitable garland. We have the following requirements -
1. That there was an LED, and not a light bulb (less heated). You can and light bulbs, but then ask to turn on for about 5 minutes and check that it is not too hot. Although it seems to me that if the garland is designed for use with Christmas tree and tinsel, then nothing terrible should happen.
2. So that this LED is in jack.
I was lucky and already in the second kiosk I got a garland for 100 light bulbs, the light bulbs of which were included in the jack with a slight pressure


Now prepare some jacks


In principle, you can only use transparent, garland bulbs are still colored, so that it will be colored in any case. But the color jacks were and it was decided that they would go into action.

We start to connect


Through 100 connected jacks we have a garland ready!


Well, with the upcoming Habrahabr! :)


PS: Habrayuzer remember about fire safety! Do not leave garlands without your close monitoring.
