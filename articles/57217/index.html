Come to the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2009 competition and win a smartphone!

On April 18, 2009, the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2009 competition , one of the brightest student events of this year, will take place in the Cultural Center of the State University Higher School of Economics ( Vorontsovo Field Street, 5a - metro Kurskaya). The 7 best student teams from all over Russia, the winners of the regional finals , will meet on the same stage to compete for the right to represent Russia at the international final of the competition in Egypt in the summer of 2009. These are the teams of MIPT, MEPI, RSU them. Kant, UNN, SUSU, VolgGTU and DvGU.

SIGN UP FOR PARTICIPATION IN THE FINAL

Watch the video from last year's final

At this event, not only participants but also spectators will receive prizes and gifts . Winners of exciting raffles and quizzes will be awarded with valuable prizes, including the main prize - a Windows Mobile-based smartphone . No one will leave without a souvenir!

The event will be broadcast with photos on Twitter @imcup , while online there will be a reception of questions and impressions from the audience with the broadcast to the stage.



In the program of the event:
  • Multimedia performances of the best student teams
  • Exciting contests and quizzes with prizes
  • New opportunities for students - for example, you will learn how to pass an exam for a certified Microsoft specialist for free and how to prepare for it
  • Xbox Play Area
  • Lunch , coffee breaks
  • Round table on the topic of student entrepreneurship with successful young startups
  • Pepsi Party
  • Interview for Channel8 with viewers and participants, audience voting and much more!

The winners of the competition will take a trip to the international finals in Egypt in the summer of 2009, the best teams will receive valuable prizes and the opportunity to receive entrepreneurial training and support for the further development of the project.

Come yourself, invite your friends - it's worth seeing! The event starts at 10:00 . You must register in advance so that we add your name to the lists for passage to the cultural center. You should have an identity document with you.
We also ask you to bring information about the competition to other students of your university - print this poster and hang it in a prominent place, invite your friends!
