The future is behind modular electronics: Mighty Cast NEX Band

More and more wearable gadgets are appearing on the market, helping to monitor health, activity and sleep. The more difficult it is to choose them - in order to overpay for a minimum for unnecessary functions, you will have to sort out a decent number of devices.

Canadians have offered their own solution to the problem: the bracelet from Mighty Cast must be collected independently. This is something like a Pandora bracelet (girls and men who lined up on February 14 and March 8 will understand).

Pre-order is already available on the site , and a little more detail - under habrakatom.



The bracelet is compatible with devices running Android and iOS - information transfer takes place via Bluetooth 4.0. He signals the necessary events by vibration - for example, in this way he can wake you up at the right time. At the moment, the API and SDK are already available to third-party developers , so you can start writing applications.



The most important thing that this bracelet offers is the possibility of full adjustment of it for yourself. This should allow users to save. The cost of the device on pre-order - only fifty dollars, and additional "fashion" for him are planning to sell ten dollars. Unfortunately, with the purchase only one functional mod is included in the kit, the rest will serve as decoration.

It is supposed to be used not only as a fitness tracker, but also for games - for more complete immersion in them. While it is not clear exactly how it will look, because I have not enough imagination.
