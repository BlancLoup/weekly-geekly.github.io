Google I / O Extended Developer Events

Arkady Khachkovanyan, Marketing Manager, Google Russia

On June 27-29, San Francisco will host one of the main annual developer conferences - Google I / O. And around the world, with the support of GTUG-communities of developers using Google technologies, a series of Google I / O Extended events will be organized, where participants will be able to get acquainted with the latest Google products, discuss pressing issues and watch the live broadcast of the conference.

Regional events in the framework of I / O Extended will be held in Moscow , St. Petersburg , Voronezh , Omsk , Ufa , as well as Lviv , Yerevan and Bishkek .

On June 28, in Moscow and St. Petersburg, there will be exhibitions of Android apps Android Expo, where everyone will be able to showcase their applications and communicate with both visitors and participants of the Hangout live on the page on Google+ MosGTUG . Independent developers and large publishers are invited to participate in the exhibition - just fill out the form .
