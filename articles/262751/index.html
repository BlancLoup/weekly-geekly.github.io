The fifth annual IT conference dedicated to a wide range of topics. Ulcamp attracts programmers, designers, system administrators, testers, entrepreneurs, investors and opinion leaders in the IT industry. The conference is held in an unusual format of the tent-beach camp.

C ++ Siberia


This specialized conference is devoted exclusively to the features of C ++ development, testing and assembling large projects, asynchrony and competitiveness, libraries, etc. Among the speakers will be representatives of companies Yandex, 2GIS, Parallels, Kaspersky Lab.

Pycon Russia 2015




This annual conference for Python developers will be held this year for the third time. For two days, it will be possible to listen to the reports of specialists, participate in round tables and less formal conversations, visit workshops. And all this in nature, outside the city.

Dates and venue: September 18-19, Ivolga country training center, 30 km from Ekaterinburg.
Website: pycon.ru/2015

Rails Club Moscow 2015




Rails Club conferences have been held annually in various cities since 2009. This is where Ruby experts gather and share experiences. It discusses the pressing issues of designing, developing, testing, debugging, optimizing, scaling and operating products developed on Ruby on Rails.
