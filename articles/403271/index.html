Let's go back to the statistics.



Judging by the forecast of analysts, we are expected to increasingly fill the flight zone with various types of unmanned aerial vehicles. And although the military budget for UAVs changes very slowly (based on US statistics), the commercial and civilian use of drones will continue to evolve.

And this is not so much about the protection of private territory, when some citizens do not want to make public the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe storage facilities and the location of the objects in the territory, but rather about restricting flights over a particular zone.

From the above, there are only two ways out: the physical destruction of the drone or the disabling of the drone control systems with its immediate landing or expulsion from the protected territory.

Then it's time to talk about the possibilities and principles of the gun. And the principle of operation is simple and effective: break the connection with the control unit and drown out the satellite navigation signal.


The gun suppresses three bands at once: GNSS (GPS / GLONASS / Galileo L1 / BeiDou B1), 2.4G, 5.8G. Confident defeat distance reaches 1200 meters. Power consumption in active mode - up to 100 watts. Signal power at frequencies: 39.5dBm (1.5 GHz) 50.5dBm (2.4 GHz) 48.5dBm (5.8 GHz). The question arises: what will happen to the navigator of the aircraft if it gets into the sight of such a gun? And there will be nothing: the civilian L1 band operates at a frequency of 1575.42 MHz, and the planes receive a navigation signal from satellites at a frequency of L2 of 1227.6 MHz. The antenna's directivity angle is 29 degrees diagonally and 25 degrees vertically, so you should not aim very carefully.

How it works?

If you just need to drive off the drone, then it is enough to silence the signal from the operator‚Äôs console. Almost all drones are able to return to the starting point on the GPS coordinates with the loss of communication with the remote control.

If it is necessary to force the drone to be forced, then it is necessary to jam not only the signal from the console, but also the satellite navigation signal. With the loss of all types of communication, drones are programmed to land. In this case, the UAV will remain on foreign territory if the operator has decided to fly over a closed object.

Now consider what the whole system consists of.

  2. Control block

  5. Trigger - activator of the radio emitter
  6. Adjustable butt
  7. Indicators: battery charge / status, active mode, radiation mode; frequency power controllers
  8. 9x optical scope

Please note that the rifle mounted two Pikatini trims, which means that you can change the sight or add a body kit. And although the angle of the gun is quite wide, the 9-fold sight will not be superfluous, especially with a shooting distance of a kilometer or more.
