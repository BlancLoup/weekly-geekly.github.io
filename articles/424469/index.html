The government is exploring the possibility of legislating in the Russian Federation the principle of network neutrality

The Council proposes to legislate in Russia the principle of network neutrality - that is, equal speed of access to online services for all.

As for telecom operators, they oppose this proposal, believing that in current conditions one can speak only of limited network neutrality. Dmitry Medvedev insisted that the council carefully consider the pros and cons, as reported by Kommersant.

On September 22, Deputy Prime Minister Maxim Akimov instructed the Ministry of Communications, the Ministry of Economy and the Federal Antimonopoly Service (FAS) to consider the issue and submit agreed proposals for a meeting in the government.
