Windows Phone 7 client for Formatic



Make the words cramped and your thoughts will not be good ... (c)

I am sure some Habraludi are already familiar with the service of cruise sayings - Forismatic. And someone already uses his mobile clients for iPhone and Android . The benefit of the API is open and allows you to quickly create clients for different platforms.

I also really like this service, so I prompted the guys from our company to make an MS SWIT client for the Windows Phone 7 platform. Now the application is officially on the Marketplace, and we are ready to share it with the Habrasoobschestvom for free.

The application is executed in the minimalist style inherent in Forismatic and can do the following:
  • Display quotes in Russian and English;
  • Send SMS with a winged statement;
  • Show information about the author of the quote in Wikipedia.
