Injector RMIP48-8LP with integrated lightning protection, replenishment in the family of multiport PoE



Continuing the line of multiport injectors , our company presents another device, RMIP48-8LP.

The injector is designed to simultaneously provide PoE power to up to 8 devices, primarily such as IP video cameras and radio routers.

Unlike his fellow - the previously released model MIP-8LP - the device provides 48V to each of the 8 built-in ports. At the same time, the power of the built-in power source allows to provide up to 25W for each port. Detailed characteristics of the device are described on our website .

Like the previous model, we supplied each PoE-port with a separate lightning protection and provided protection both from high-voltage pickups (lightning discharges) and static voltage.

The device has successfully passed tests both with industrial radio routers and with the commercial line of our production routers .
