10k Apart - Responsive Edition Contest Results



So, the contest 10k Apart - Responsive Edition is over .
Let me remind you that under the terms of the competition, the created applications should work and look equally good on various devices - from desktops to mobile phones and tablets. Requires support for the IE10 Platform Preview, newer versions of Firefox and a WebKit browser of choice (Chrome or Safari). The permissible size of the application is up to 10 Kb in a zip-archive (not counting some popular libraries).

This time, 128 applications struggled for prizes ( last year - 367). Like last year, one main, three technical nominations and nine Honorable Mentions were provided for the winners. In contrast to the previous time, no open vote was taken for applications (with ratings), and instead, the ubiquitous Facebook Like was used.

So, we meet the winners.


Grand Prize

BytesJack by L & L

Nice app for fans of blackjack (without whores :)).

Nomination for technical performance

Space Mahjong by Toby Yun & Kyoungwoo Ham

Mahjong Solitaire for puzzle fans.

Nomination for the best design solution

Sproutable by Kevin Thompson

The application for easy search in the Yelp catalog (unfortunately, Yelp does not know anything about Ukraine and Russia).

Public choice

PHRASE by Andy Gott

For fans of statistics and infographics.

Congratulations to the winners!
