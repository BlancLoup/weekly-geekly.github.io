Research of virtual servers with SSD disks



Recently, SSD drives have become noticeably cheaper, and increasingly they can be seen as an advantage of a virtual server. For example, tariffs with SSD drives now - 370 pieces from 65 hosters . Naturally, SSD drives are much more expensive than other types of drives, so to keep costs low, hosters offer less space at a similar price compared to SATA or SAS tariffs.

Me and my friend amoskvin from https://theideahosting.com decided to conduct a small study of cheap rates for virtual servers with SSD drives. I would like to thank all the hosters who provided virtual servers for free for testing.

For testing, the maximum price bracket was chosen - $ 5 per month. The only thing I immediately want to note is that testing was done slowly and because of currency fluctuations at the time of publication of this article the $ 5 bar may be slightly shifted, but the general idea remains the same: - to test hosters and cheap rates with SSD disks.


DigitalOcean is a fairly well-known foreign hoster that positions itself as a cloud provider. It offers 8 different fares starting at $ 5 to $ 640. SSD drives are used everywhere, a large number of data centers are available in different countries (UK, Netherlands, USA, Singapore).
At a minimum rate of $ 5, a 20 GB hard drive is provided, which is quite a lot for such a price. All data centers use an IntelXeon E5-2630L processor with a core frequency of 2400 MHz.
Quite often, you can find promotional codes to get $ 10 on the account, which is enough for two months of service at the minimum rate. KVM virtualization is used, so the list of operating systems that can be installed is quite large.
