HP gives a chance to get webOS developers a tablet for $ 150



Although the future of the webOS operating system and HP TouchPad tablets is questionable, HP is trying to keep its operating system afloat. Now this company plans to interest webOS developers in such a simple way as providing a tablet from HP for only $ 150. This refers to the 32-GB version of this device.

So, each registered webOS developer can really get a tablet. All this applies to developers from the United States, Canada and European countries. True, in Europe, the cost of the tablet for some reason will be 150 euros, and not 150 dollars. But the tradition of companies to equate dollars to euros is not surprising.

As far as one can understand, each registered developer gets the right to get not one such tablet, but two at once. The negative point is that the exact number of tablets available for developers is also unknown. To be able to purchase the $ 150 HP TouchPad, you need to register before November 18th of this year. If the application is accepted, then the developer receives a coupon. You need to repay it until November 27th.
