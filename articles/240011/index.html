Read Habr in the mail. results

On October 1, an article was written about the service that was made to work with Habr's posts. The service downloaded new post titles and, upon request, sent a ready downloaded post to the post.
Today I stopped the service. All active subscribers turned off.
I share information in figures and service results.

Honestly the results of the service is not satisfied.
First of all, there were a lot of mistakes, which, although corrected, did not correct until the end.
Secondly, did not wait for habraeffekt, though I hoped.

I will simply list the errors that were and which have either been corrected or not corrected so far:
