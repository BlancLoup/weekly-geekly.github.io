Performancing -> ScribeFire

The popular Performancing plugin, which allows you to write posts to your blog directly from the browser, has been renamed ScribeFire. As you know, this plugin was not included in the transaction for the sale of the Performancing service to PayPerPost .

The plugin has a separate site , which will officially open after the New Year holidays. In the meantime, the old version of the plugin can be downloaded from the add- on site for Firefox .
