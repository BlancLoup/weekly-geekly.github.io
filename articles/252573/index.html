Conference. NEXT returns to Peter

Last year we held two .NEXT conferences: in April in St. Petersburg and in December in Moscow. Since this year April is already overloaded with events, we decided to postpone St. Petersburg .NEXT to the beginning of June.

So, on Friday, June 5, in St. Petersburg, the Pulkovskaya Hotel will host the third independent conference for .NET programmers .NEXT. The conference will bring together more than 400 people directly on the site and more than 100 people online.



Traditionally, among our speakers will be:
  • leaders .NET-hubs habrabra
  • leading engineers JetBrains
  • Microsoft evangelists

and other great specialists. No cost and no speakers, surprises. However, about this in one of the following announcements.



Registration for the conference will open in April, but for now we are starting to accept applications for reports. If you want to make a presentation on .NEXT 2015 Piter, just fill out the application .

The program committee of the conference this time consists of three people.

Dmitry mezastel Nesteruk, JetBrains
Developer, podcaster and frequent speaker at programmer events. He is interested in modern approaches in the development and effective use of software and hardware resources for solving complex problems. Microsoft MVP C # 2009 c. He works as a technical evangelist in JetBrains.

Andrey DreamWalker Akinshin, Enterra
Lead .NET Developer, MCP, Ph.D., silver winner of ACM ICPC, postdoc at the Weizmann Institute of Science. In her free time, she loves to understand the insides of .NET and share interesting findings with the community.

Andrew real_ales Dmitriev, NetCracker
Experienced program director, behind which a dozen major conferences for programmers. The organizer of the meetings JUG.ru and CodeFreeze community in St. Petersburg and Moscow. For several years he has been developing a graphical stack of JDK libraries in Sun and Oracle, and currently works as a load testing group manager at NetCracker.

Conference site: http://dotnext.ru

Ticket sales will open in early April. Follow the announcements!
