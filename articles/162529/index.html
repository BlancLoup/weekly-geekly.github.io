Creating the perfect player



We all love music.
With the advent of smartphones, portable players have become less in demand - no one wants to be hung with appliances from head to toe. Music mobile applications are now more popular than ever.
Thousands of applications claim to be the # 1 player in our devices. But what do we get? See how many players are installed on your music device? One for podcasts, another for playing music from a phone, a separate application for searching texts and clips, and, most likely, a Vkontakte client for listening to their audio recordings (where can it be without it?). Counted? Well, as you say to deal with all this mess? After all, everything can be much more convenient and better!

Let me introduce you to a truly convenient player that will combine all your music from various sources into a single playlist! Why exactly Boomfox will become your player number 1?

All on the shelves


A convenient interface is like a clean room: and it‚Äôs nicer to stay, and you always know where things are. We are sure that this is very important for music lovers, in whose library there are several hundreds or even thousands of compositions. When designing Boomfox, we relied on three main criteria for an ideal music player:

- Convenient interface. The player should be as simple as possible and yet functional. And, besides, mobile - so that it is convenient to manage it on the road.
- Not just a big, but a huge music library. The player must have access to all major music resources. Including the resources that provide near-music information - lyrics, videos, biographies of the performers.
- Autonomy or, in other words, independence from the Internet connection. We want to listen to music without dividing the space on online and offline!

Without all this, the player becomes unnecessary, ordinary and inconspicuous. So why save in your phone unnecessary and useless applications?

Play queue


It often happens that you find a track and you want to listen to it as follows, but creating a separate playlist for this is too lazy. It does not matter - it is for this that we made a play queue - a dynamic playlist, accessible from any screen, in which you can always add new tracks or change their order.

Search


We are looking for everything, we are always looking, we are looking everywhere. We are looking for thoroughly. Boomfox can search simultaneously on all connected sources. Smart search will first give the best matches for tracks already on the device, and then attach results from the connected resources to them.
