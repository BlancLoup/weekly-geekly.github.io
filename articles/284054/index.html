The best job in the USA: the most attractive specialties part 2

In the first part of the review , several interesting professions were listed, whose representatives in the USA (and not only in this country) live very well. Of course, the list is not limited to five positions, there are much more interesting works than five.

Today the second part of the review is published, and it promises to be no less interesting than the first.

Innovation / Improvement Manager




  • Average salary (per year): 96600 US dollars;
  • Maximum salary: 139 thousand;
  • Growth in 10 years: 12%.


Innovation and improvement managers are optimizing the company's workflow so that all departments work smoothly, employees know their responsibilities, and perform them well. In addition, such managers are engaged in the development of corporate culture, the atmosphere in the company - after all, all this can also positively influence the work of the structure.

Advantages of the profession: a good manager can pay a very large premium. Companies are very different, so the work is very interesting.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to society: V; Low stress: A.

Database developer




  • The average salary (per year): 88200 US dollars;
  • Maximum salary: 126 thousand;
  • Growth in 10 years: 23%.


Database developers make sure that the companies in which they work, can always get access to all the necessary information at the moment. Storage and access to information, the development of new software products - all this is important for almost all companies.

Advantages of the profession: technologies change very quickly, and the developer has to constantly learn something new. And this is a great tool for developing skills and self-development.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: In; Contribution to society: C; Remote work: A; Low stress: A.

Information Security Analyst




  • Average salary (per year): 96400 US dollars;
  • Maximum salary: 126 thousand;
  • Growth in 10 years: 37%.

Nowadays, personal information, everything that a user on the Web keeps about himself, can become accessible to one and all, if not take care of data protection. The information security systems analyst conducts an audit of such systems in order to identify weaknesses in software products and services of various companies.

Advantages of the profession: the need for specialists of this kind is constantly growing - many companies need information security specialists, because some critical problems can lead to multimillion-dollar losses and a decline in the company's authority among users.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: In; Contribution to the community: V; Low stress: A.

  • Average salary (per year): 62400 US dollars;
  • Maximum salary: 119 thousand;

Surprised that such a profession is on this list? Meanwhile, yoga instructors earn very well. They are engaged in helping their wards to achieve a balance between spirit and body, so to speak. Relaxation, peace and tranquility - for this, many are willing to pay.

Advantages of the profession: the instructor can plan both his own schedule and the schedule of classes of his student. Constant communication with interesting people, the development of your own body is a big plus of the profession.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to the community: V; Low stress: A.
