3GPP LTE: 100 Mbit / s over cellular networks - reality

Nokia said that it had conducted successful technical tests of its next cellular data network, which provided download speeds of 100 Mbit / s for the user and 50 Mbit / s for the user.

The initiative to create a new data network 3GPP LTE (3GPP Long Term Evolution) belonged to the leading European telecommunications companies Alcatel-Lucent, Ericsson, France Telecom / Orange, Nokia, Nokia Siemens Networks, Nortel, T-Mobile and Vodafone, but recently joined by Asian China Mobile, Huawei, LG Electronics, NTT DoCoMo, Samsung ...

The LTE test consists of three main stages: the already successfully passed concept test, network co-operation and final tests. Joint testing of networks will be completed at the end of 2009, and the initial deployment of the network is scheduled for 2010.
