Technology has come to the level where the home multimedia center has become not a luxury, but an affordable way for a wide range of users to get high-quality digital home entertainment at a more than affordable price. This is confirmed by the output of a large number of compact multi-format specialized HDMI devices, and the appearance of external drives with the function of broadcasting multimedia content, such as the popular WD TV Live , but a full-fledged computer still gives much more universal possibilities of its use and almost complete freedom to update the used software. The only thing that until recently was restrained by the rapid growth of ITX-systems - this is very low performance. With the release of the Intel Atom processor family, this problem was solved, and being in a very advantageous position before a competitor who could not use the "blue" processors, NVIDIA took the only right step - combined them with its effective mobile graphics and got a "bomb" that blew up market.
