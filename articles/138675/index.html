Rating increase in wages in the IT-sphere



HeadHunter researchers have compiled a list of specialties whose representatives in 2012 will be able to count on salary increases. In the process of analyzing the labor market, about 150,000 vacancies were posted by companies located in all regions of the Russian Federation. The experts conducted a comparative analysis and found out how the wages of domestic workers changed over the next five years. It turned out that the most intensive rates of salaries are rising in the information technology sector.


Service engineer

The duties of the service engineer include adjustment, repair and maintenance of equipment, and, in addition, the implementation of technical and scheduled inspections. A person who, above all, can professionally handle mechanical and electronic equipment can apply for such a position. One of the advantages of such specialists is the maintenance skills of climate and medical equipment. A person who wants to get a job must have a university degree and know the rules of technical support. Salaries of such specialists range from 20 thousand to 40 thousand rubles. The estimated growth in 2012 is 36 percent.

Web Designer

The web designer is obliged to understand what the customer wants to get from the design of the website. In addition, such a specialist should be able to create design templates, typeset new layouts and existing ones. This specialist is supposed to know such programs as 3DMax, Adobe Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator and, of course, be well versed in HTML. It would not be superfluous to have experience in creating websites and general awareness of the technologies used in the global computer network. Individual employers require candidates to show examples of successful projects. By the way, working as a web designer, it is not necessary to come to the office every day. Currently, the salary is up to 35 thousand rubles. Estimated growth - 32 percent.

CIO

The main responsibilities of such an employee - the management and control of the IT-department. An applicant for a position is obliged to take an active part in the process of developing a corporate strategy in the part of information technology. Employers require candidates to have experience in implementing information technology solutions in companies. It is also worth noting that the position of CIO is mainly taken by people with special higher education. Wages are up to 100 thousand rubles with the prospect of growth of up to 25 percent.

Site editor

Web editor or site editor is obliged to develop a strategy for filling the site with content. His job is to constantly monitor the content of the site. He must edit the posted materials, adapt them for the convenience of users, analyze and filter the necessary information. Anyone who claims to this position, in addition to having the experience and diploma of the university, must have knowledge in HTML and in writing texts. You can work both at home and in the office. Salary reaches 30 thousand rubles. The projected increase in 2012 is 22 percent.
