More Free Feedback on Freelance - More Money

At the end of last year, we implemented three major changes in the service at Freelance: we made the placement of orders free, introduced mandatory verification of customers and changed the subscription tariffing for freelancers. This led to an increase and improvement in the quality of orders placed, as well as to the fact that freelancers began to think more before leaving a response. On the one hand, an expensive subscription helps the customer not to dig in irrelevant responses. On the other hand, it can cut off really cool specialists. Therefore, we made a compromise decision - to introduce a limited number of free responses per day.



How to get


The response limit is 5 pieces. In order for them to be credited to your account, go through verification and make sure that all the required fields in the profile are filled. If any information is missing, you will see a stub on the order page. Clicking on ‚ÄúFill profile‚Äù will lead to the page, the empty fields on which will be highlighted with an error.



Paranoids and sympathizers may not fill in the data and be not verified by the mobile phone number, but pay for the subscription in the old manner. The prices remained the same: purchase-by-purchase responses ($ 99) or a day subscription ($ 270), a week ($ 490), a month ($ 1200) and, according to numerous requests, a year ($ 7,200). Yes, yes, a little more than if we left a subscription of 500 ‚ÇΩ / month.

How to spend


Each freelancer gets the opportunity to respond free of charge to 5 orders. Daily account is replenished with up to 5 responses (they do not accumulate). If you have previously purchased a response (or several), then these five are plus to those already purchased. Next to the "Respond" button on the order pages we put a counter, so you will always know how many responses you can still leave.

When spending responses, first spent those that are credited for free, then those that are bought. If you bought a response, then you were credited with 5 responses, then spent 4 responses, then the next day, you will again have 6 responses.

After the account response limit is over, you can continue to buy responses one by one or purchase a subscription.



Freelancers who have already purchased a subscription continue to respond as usual. They will have access to a new function after it expires.

What else


For new users, we have added onboarding. After confirming the mail and logging in, the user enters the personal data filling page (including the mobile phone number). You can leave this page at any time and continue filling in another time.

In addition, we reworked the menu. Added PRO sections for freelancer and customer . The first includes the purchase of responses, subscription, raising and highlighting the questionnaire. In the second - the purchase of a subscription that allows you to view contact information of other users (it is several times cheaper than that of freelancers and does not allow to respond), as well as raising and highlighting the order.

A little later, we will present the Freelancers Club - a place for communication between service users, where it will be possible to discuss issues of concern to freelancers with colleagues. And even later we will present a completely redesigned version of the project (new design, backend and frontend), which will be even more functional and convenient.
