In this regard, an illustrative situation has developed with Highscreen Black Box Connect, which reveals the curious aspects of the interaction of local B-brands and their Asian partners.

Naturally, the Chinese electronics manufacturers have their own catalogs of finished devices. And these same manufacturers, of which there are a lot in the Celestial Empire, regularly send spam e-mail boxes, including Russian companies. The essence of the proposals is obvious - branding models. Immediately, I note that this is not the only way that the second echelon fills up its range. So, in one of these letters to Highscreen, among others, there was an IP camera, let's call it conditionally No. 1. The access point in the camera is activated automatically when it is turned on. In my opinion, this automatically solves the important task of quick access to the video to view it "on site". To access the settings and other management points, first download the Unieye program, which is easily found on Google Play or the App Store. I do not think that the main dialogue requires detailed explanations. I note that in the upper left corner the device identifier is displayed, which can be changed. If you rotate the smartphone in landscape orientation, the full-screen mode of viewing the picture from the camera automatically turns on. The image is transmitted in degraded quality, apparently with the aim of preventing problems in low-power devices.
