Contacting Google Code Search Team

Preface of the translator: actually, as I said in this topic, writing in Google Groups turned out to be more efficient. The team still answered. I provide my knee-length translation, please do not throw slippers.

Hi guys.

We are very sorry about the upcoming closure of the project, but unfortunately, the matter has already been done. You may have noticed that we have been rather passive lately - this is because we have been busy justifying attracting investment in the project. Inside the company, people are also not happy, but the decision was made at the highest level.
But there is a ray of hope in the kingdom of darkness. googlecode.com will take over the search for their projects, as well as hosting the Chromium search page. (If ChromiumOS or Android is important to you - please notify all contacts that you have on Google, let them know!). Not all details were finally resolved, and that is why they were not in the official report.
We apologize to all of you for leaving you, and thanks for all the appreciation you showed.

PS As an alternative, you can use http://koders.com http://opensearch.krugle.org http://zgrepcode.com and http://www.antepedia.com , they were mentioned by many people. We do not express approval or disapproval, we simply mention them so that you can try them. And if there are others, please post them on this thread so that people can easily find them.
