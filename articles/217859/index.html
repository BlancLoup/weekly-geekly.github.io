This information resource was developed as part of the implementation of the Resolution of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Belarus of September 26, 2013 No. 843 ‚ÄúOn Amendments and Modifications to the Rules for the Provision of Telecommunication Services‚Äù and is intended to provide technical capabilities to subscribers and users of data transmission services provided through wired technology ( stationary), to assess the current level of quality of data services for their compliance with the parameters of the quality of data services, in the contract for their provision, as well as the requirements of regulatory and technical regulatory legal acts.

The evaluation results obtained using the information resource are informational in nature and allow the subscriber to obtain the necessary reference information on the current parameters characterizing the quality of data transmission services.

Test operation is carried out to assess the effectiveness of the functioning of the basic information resource subsystems, to analyze the effectiveness of the evaluation of the quality parameters of data transmission services using the load of subscribers of existing telecommunication networks.

During the test operation, the information resource will be supplemented with practical information related to its operation, as well as the procedure for assessing the quality parameters of data transmission services, possible problem issues, reference and other information about the functioning of its main subsystems.

Reference: QoS - (Eng. Quality of Service - the quality of service (service) is a term used in ITU-T Recommendation E.800 and defines the set of characteristics of a telecommunications service related to the ability to meet established and perceived needs of a service user.

The formation and operation of the information resource, as well as the implementation of other organizational and administrative actions related to its operation, is provided by Giprosvyaz OJSC.

At the time of the test operation, individual resource subsystems may not be available due to debugging activities, including taking into account the design and actual load.
