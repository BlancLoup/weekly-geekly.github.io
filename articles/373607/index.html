From today in Russia drones heavier than 250 grams need to register, but for now you can not do


Source: PixAero

Today in Russia, changes to the Air Code have come into force, according to which unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) heavier than 250 grams must be registered, TASS reports . The registration of UAVs will be handled by FSUE ZashchitaInfoTrans, reporting to the Ministry of Transport. The same company has previously developed a technology to identify drones.

The draft law on the mandatory registration of all UAVs weighing more than 250 grams was adopted at the end of 2015. Then the document was supported by an absolute majority of deputies - 438 people. There were neither abstentions nor those who voted against.
