Honeycomb in 7 inches. Samsung Galaxy Tab 7.0 Plus Tablet Preview

Not so long ago, Samsung announced a new version of the 7-inch tablet Galaxy Tab 7.0 Plus . Unlike last year's model, the gadget runs on Android Honeycomb and has improved hardware features. One of the test samples fell into the hands of Droider , and we are pleased to share with you the first impressions of the device.



The tablet has a very early version of the firmware installed, so do not pay attention to the emerging brakes and oddities. As always with Samsung, the device has excellent overall dimensions : thickness 9.96 mm, weight 345 g. Of the other characteristics worth noting: a 1.2 GHz dual-core processor, a 7-inch display with a resolution of 1024x600, 1 GB of RAM, two cameras (3 and 2 MP), as well as a 4000 mAh battery .

In general, the device has a number of serious advantages, such as compactness, as well as a good screen and processor . At the same time, the speed of operation of the final firmware, with which in Samsung tablets the last time things are not very good, will play a significant role in the success of the product. Russian retail price of the gadget is not yet known.
