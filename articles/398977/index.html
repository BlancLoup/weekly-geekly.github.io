GoPro recalls Karma quadcopters



GoPro recalls 2500 sold Karma quadcopters due to unreasonable loss of power during operation and then disconnected from some copies. "A small number of Karma owners have reported cases of shutdown of the device during operation," said GoPro CEO Nick Woodman.

It should be noted that the 2500 is all Karma devices sold, the sales of which began on October 23 of this year at a price of $ 799.

GoPro Karma - quadcopter production company GoPro, specializing in action cameras. The device is compatible with GoPro cameras, is designed for aerial photography and is equipped with special equipment to produce the best image quality. One of the features of Karma is its own camera stabilizer, bundled with a quadcopter and compatible with GoPro HERO5 and HERO4 cameras.

The official website of the manufacturer has already posted instructions for direct return of the Karma GoPro, or return through the seller.
