SAP became interested in the market for medium-sized enterprises

The world famous company SAP decided to interest and medium business. A novelty is the provision of a package of hosted versions of CRM and ERP systems. Services will be configured so that it will be convenient to work with them remotely, the system will be ready for numerous daily operations and updates.

Potential subscribers will have the opportunity to test services in advance before purchasing.

SAP believes that now there is nothing of the kind in this area in the market, and therefore the company does not want to miss its chance to occupy a certain market share. By 2010, it is planned to attract about 10 thousand new customers annually.

It seems that SAP is seriously interested in the market of medium-sized enterprises, which is confirmed by the planned additional expenses of the company in the amount of 300-400 million euros for the development of the market in the next two years. Most of these funds will go to testing, developing new software, which will be distributed and will begin to bring profit by 2008, reports Infoworld.com .

The company does not deny the fact that the new services will be of interest to companies that want to use the software provided without much customization. If the company needs a fine and special setting of the proposed programs, then it can purchase the SAP All-In-One system.

Today, SAP has only described a strategy; for the service has not even come up with a name.
