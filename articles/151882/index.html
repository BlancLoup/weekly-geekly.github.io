RIM and Marmalade joint action for game developers

RIM and Marmalade announced the launch of the promotion, which allows developers to get a free PlayBook tablet and a limited-duration multiplatform SDK license. The only condition is to release your gaming application for the tablet of a Canadian company.



The developer is required to register first on the Marmalade website. After that, over the next seven days, you must provide registration information at the BlackBerry App World store, which can be done here . As a result, you will receive a special copy of the Marmalade SDK for the PlayBook, and the tablet itself will be sent to you for debugging and testing for a period of six months. After the successful publication of the game in App World, Marmalade will additionally provide a multi-platform indie license for your SDK for a period of six months, and you can keep the received PlayBook. If the game is not published within six months, the device will have to return back.
