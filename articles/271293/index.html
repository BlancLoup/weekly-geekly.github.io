Drupal 8 released



Today, after almost 5 years of development , Drupal 8, a new version of the popular content management system, has been released. This is the biggest update ever made in Drupal. Here is a list of several changes from nearly a hundred improvements in Drupal 8:

  • Editing and previewing materials directly on the site using the WYSIWYG editor (CKEditor)
  • Integrated out-of-box materials management with entities, fields and views (views)
  • Customizing the content of pages and even forms and admin pages using the administrative interface
  • Full support for translatability and localization "out of the box"
  • Reliable configuration management for secure and easy transfer of changes between development environments
  • HTML5, responsive and mobile-oriented markup
  • REST services support
  • Improved availability and support for WAI-ARIA
  • Supports modern PHP standards and practices with the integration of popular libraries such as Composer, Symfony2, Guzzle, and Twig
  • Significantly improved out-of-box performance
  • Improved caching and best of its kind support for CDN and reverse proxy servers
  • Full support for PHP7, PostgreSQL and SQLite
