May Day discounts on Amazon

Not everyone knows that May 11 in the United States is Mother's Day. Various sales are usually timed to this date, including on Amazon. It would seem, where do geeks and Mother's Day? And the thing is that they sell at tasty prices and electronics too. Most sales have been around for a long time , such as traditional Kindle discounts. But the most delicious postponed for last.

Start your alarm clocks, because only on May 1, within 24 hours, Motorola will sell a limited batch of unlocked Moto X 16GB ( Habra-review , Wikipedia ) at a price of $ 299 without a contract, which on average will be cheaper by 50 bucks from the current price. In Russia at the moment, the minimum price for this unit is 17,400 rubles according to Yandex.Market
