The first experiment based on the largest laser facility European XFEL

The European XFEL is a three-kilometer-long laser setup, with which you can solve a number of practical tasks: from developing new data transfer methods to nanomaterial research. University experts conducted a ten-day experiment based on XFEL. We will tell about the results, features of work with nanocrystals and impressions from the process.


Simple and fast perovskite nanolaser technology

Our scientists have found a new way to create miniature lasers based on perovskite . Unlike existing methods, this process takes 5-7 minutes. We will talk about the principles of the technology and its applications.


Scientists have proposed a simplified way of working with nanostructures

At the University of ITMO will be engaged in nanomachines for the diagnosis of oncology

The ITMO University team together with foreign colleagues developed a nanomachine capable of recognizing DNA tumor markers. The basis of the new machine is a mechanism that uses deoxyribozymes to interact with target RNAs. On the results of the first cellular experiments and the future plans of our specialists.

Crohn's disease is a serious illness of the gastrointestinal tract, which is accompanied by acute pain. The causes of this disease are still unknown. To understand how the disease appears and develops, the staff of the ITMO University began to explore the genetic material of the intestinal microflora.
