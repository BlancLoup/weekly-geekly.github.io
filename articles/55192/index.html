The Battle For Wesnoth Version 1.6 Released

Battle for Spring is a cute and exciting fantasy free turn-based strategy game, the best GNU / Linux open source game of 2008, according to the guys from LinuxQuestions.org .

More than a year has passed since the release of version 1.4, during this time 3,000,000 copies of the game were downloaded from sourceforge.net. And so, version 1.6 was released a few hours ago.


What's new?

The press release (in Russian) states:
- new campaign
- the ability to give orders to the allies
- A new system for transferring gold from scenario to scenario
- old campaigns recycled
- The history and geography of Spring has been worked out much deeper.
- changes in multiplayer
- the graphics have been enriched, the fighters have much more animations, there is support for team colors
- new type of terrain - coastal reef
- five new tunes
- new translations
- map editor has been significantly improved
- new fighters
- improved AI

We download and collect or wait for the appearance in the repositories.

Cheers, comrades.
