Review of trackers of Ukraine

Looking at this post , I decided to make in the image and likeness for UkrNet.
Immediately I want to note the specifics of the Ukrainian file sharing due to the presence of UA-IX, many providers share the speed, as a rule, basically, the speed in UA-IX = 100Mb / s. while the speed of the rest of the Internet access is from <1 to 10 Mb / s.

This review is also not complete, as not all Ukrainian trackers are known to me, not everyone has public info, etc., many trackers are only additions to portals and therefore the situation with trackers can only be assessed by the number of peers.

UkrNet users also use Runet and World trackers, so this is not a P2P review of UkrNet, but only a review of trackers in Ukraine.

Looking at btlist.info and similar ratings, I realized that this is all complete fiction, for example powertracker.org (4th place, 79141 votes) 25,000 peers, did not even enter the 5 described by me, but torrents.net.ua (42nd place, 5302 votes)> 400,000 peers.

1. torrents.net.ua - August 2005 start as UA-IX Only, since September 2008 open for everyone, pre-moderated registration.
2. 0day.kiev.ua - 2007, as an addition to the portal 0day.kiev.ua, registration by invite
3. hurtom.com - September 2007 as a supplement to the hurtom.com portal, registration is open
4. berloga.net - November 2008 as a supplement to the portal berloga.net, registration is open
5. hdclub.com.ua - March 2008, registration is open
