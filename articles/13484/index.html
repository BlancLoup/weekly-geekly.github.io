Search video on Mail.Ru

On August 23, Mail.ru users have the opportunity of Russian-language video content search on popular Russian and foreign video hosting sites. Currently, video search is in beta testing mode.

Currently, the system indexes the contents of nine video hosting sites: Video@Mail.Ru , RuTube , Loadup, LiveInternet, Rambler Vision, MyVi, Video.I.UA, as well as some of the videos of Russian users on YouTube and Metacafe.

The work of the service is carried out by the search engine GoGo.Ru , the first to offer users of Runet search video. The number of indexed clips in the database of the service currently stands at about 835,000 files, and the value of this indicator is constantly growing.
