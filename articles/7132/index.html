5min - Video Wikipedia

5min - this is another attempt to collect video, but with a specific purpose. 5min collects informational video that provides answers to questions of interest to people.

The task of the site is to connect experts to the project who would be able to share their knowledge in 5 minutes.

A great part of the site is the Smart Player, which allows you to magnify a video or play it in full screen mode, and also supports frame-by-frame playback.

To implement his idea, the startup has invested $ 300 thousand, according to alarm: clock .
