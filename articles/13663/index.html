Sleek Audio SA6 - revolutionary, fully customizable headphones


Sleek Audio developed the revolutionary SA6 in- ear headphones. The highlight of the novelty is that these headphones can be customized in full accordance with your preferences in sound.

The ‚Äúears‚Äù consist of five parts: a replaceable silicone liner, a special nozzle for improving high frequencies (a transparent tube on which a silicone liner is attached), a main unit with a speaker, a detachable cord and a special bass port insert that provides decent low frequencies.

New technology called Variable Equalization System and it is based on the fact that the high-frequency liner and bass port exist in three different ways, allowing, in fact, the hardware to adjust the equalizer.
In addition, a replaceable headphone cable allows you to: replace a damaged cable, change its color to match, for example, the color of the case of a new player, and also contains some potential for the development of technology that the company reports, but does not specify what the potential is. Cables for SA6 exist in various lengths. Again for your convenience.

Headphones allow you to place both the cable down and the cable behind the ear (cable up) - just as convenient for you.

Headphone specifications:
Complex resistance: 50 Ohm
DC resistance: 24 ohm
Sensitivity: 110 dB

The headset package includes:
headphones themselves
standard 1.5 meter cable
set of ear plugs of different sizes
three high frequency liner
three bass ports
special cleaning system
box for storage and transportation
