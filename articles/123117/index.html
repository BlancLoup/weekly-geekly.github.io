AWS cancels incoming traffic charges

From July 1, all incoming Amazon Web Services traffic becomes free (previously it was 0.10 per GB). Although for most sites this does not play a significant role, but for some applications (crawlers and other bots, backup systems, Dropbox) is just a gift. By the way, by a strange coincidence, last week Windows Azure also canceled the fee for incoming traffic .

New AWS tariffs for outgoing traffic in the USA and Europe:

The first 1 GB / month is free (it was)
Up to 10 TB / month - $ 0.12 per gigabyte (it was $ 0.15)
The following 40 TB / month - $ 0.09 per gigabyte ($ 0.11)
The next 100 TB / month - $ 0.07 per gigabyte ($ 0.09)
The next 350 TB / month - $ 0.05 per gigabyte ($ 0.08)
