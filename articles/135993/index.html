Die, agent, die!

Every year, for the first time in their lives, thousands of people are faced with the need to rent an apartment. Honestly, the business of realtor services for renting out apartments is still shit, especially in such a large city as Moscow. For this reason, every year there is another young man with "burning eyes", which gives another vyser super- project assistance in finding a rental.

These projects are very similar to butterflies. They appear in the hot summer during the high season, and die in the freezing cold . Just like the butterflies flapping their wings beautifully and uselessly and disappearing without leaving a trace. Why does this happen? Some thoughts are set forth.



Introduction


To begin with, it should be said that due to certain circumstances for more than 10 years, I occasionally have to both rent and rent apartments. I do it both for myself and help my acquaintances. Unlike, perhaps, 90% of the audience of Habr, who observes the process only on one side, I see it from both. But in this post I will not talk about the intricacies of the process of hiring or surrendering, but will talk about points that the authors of such startups do not take into account.

Typical startup


So, what does the author of the next "killer agents" think? Faced with this particular business and sipping the original portion of shit (sometimes losing a certain amount of money), our enthusiast comes to the following conclusions:

  • Most open source offers are crap
  • Sometimes there are bits of happiness
  • Most live deals come through agents who withdraw money for nothing.
  • Sometimes there are grains of living sentences without agents.
