The Internet in Moscow can become free in 2030, the authorities are preparing to use cryptocurrency



It is likely that in Moscow and other metropolitan areas Internet access will be free for all residents. This will happen around 2030, says Alexei Chukarin, head of the analytical division of the Moscow IT department. He announced the forecast during a speech at the round table "Smart Home: Architecture of the New Time".

Instead of a connection fee and traffic, content monetization will come, Chukarin explained. Internet providers will be transformed into multiservice companies or sell, lease the infrastructure they have created.

As of 2015, about 75% of residents are Internet users. 89% use cellular communication. In Moscow, organized "universal access via Wi-Fi, including transport and public areas."

The Internet has become the most significant segment of the ICT industry (about 1.1 trillion rubles), and by 2030 the Internet economy will reach new frontiers. All Moscow residents will be connected to the Network, and users will be identified in real time.
