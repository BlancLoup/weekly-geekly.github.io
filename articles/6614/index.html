Google figured out with the search engine Jooble

Search engine vacancies and resume Jooble fulfilled the requirements of Google to change the logo and interface of the resource.

In October 2006, a search engine for vacancies and resumes jooble opened on the Ukrainian Internet, whose interface resembled the popular search engine google.com. After the correspondence with the central office of Google Inc. and negotiations with the representative of Google in Ukraine Dmitry Sholomko, the parties agreed. As a result, on April 5, 2007, the resource jooble presented a new design, reported HiTech.Expert Roman Prokofiev, partner manager at Jooble.

As before, when creating a new design, the main emphasis was placed on the simplicity and usability of the resource. The resource logo has become a smiling rabbit.
