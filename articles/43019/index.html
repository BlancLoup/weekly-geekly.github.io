PlayProject.ru - live, listen, play! Music portal

I would like to introduce you to our new site. We sincerely hope that he will interest you.

PlayProject.ru is a project entirely devoted to musical maniacs, people who simply cannot exist without music, people who live music!

Our main goal is to bring to the user the most accurate and reliable information about the performers and all musical styles and directions, to create conditions in which you can freely communicate with your peers and find new friends.
This is achieved by the fact that any added information is strictly controlled by the moderators and repeatedly checked and corrected and only then approved.

Our collection, in which there is the most complete description of music bands, their albums, covers, tracklists, lyrics, photos and logos of groups, is growing every day. Any user can contribute and tell everyone about the new group that he has discovered!

A database of musical trends, with more than 350 descriptions of styles and sub-styles, is also available for editing and making adjustments to any of the users.
