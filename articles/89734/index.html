Fur audio headphones

Concerned about the safety of my favorite ears, I was going to buy ordinary fur headphones, but discussing a gift with Kujallah , I had the idea to embed the speakers in them.

And indeed, the shape of the fur "ears" is very similar to the usual. And why should the place be idle?



Under the cut there is a small story about how I turned ordinary Koss into a warm headdress.

In general, the idea itself seemed so obvious to me that at first I was going to find such ears in the store. Honestly rummaged through the ruins, and found only Ritmix RH-509.



They looked terribly scary, and they were afraid of pink fur and plastic in the picture.

In general, having scratched my head, I decided to make a gift myself. Here you need to immediately make a reservation - the world is full of good audiophile headphones that completely cover the ears. But I wanted it to be first of all a headdress, and the music became a pleasant addition.

In general, it is said - done, especially since there were no particular problems foreseen. It was necessary only to take suitable headphones and sheathe them with fur.

The most obvious was to get a bluetooth headset.
Pros:
- can be used as a telephone headset
- You can switch tracks directly from the headphones.
- There are no wires again.
Minuses:
- in the cold batteries quickly sit down
- it is necessary to provide a hole in the fur for charging
- The sound quality of wireless headphones is inferior to traditional
- for the same money in fur, you can roll up headphones of a higher class

Having weighed everything, I decided to make fur ‚Äúears‚Äù from traditional headphones. Naturally, the question immediately arose of cutting the wires. I speak naturally, because, I repeat, I wanted to have a hat.

The headphone cable was cut off and mini-soldered soldered. The result is autonomous headphones and a removable cord. The socket on the headphones is stuck on a double-sided tape and placed above one of the speakers so that the wire is as inconspicuous as possible. So, I present the faithful's bundle. Ears are unpacked, put on the head. While my wife is spinning in front of the mirror, I quietly plug in the sound ... Oh, how much enthusiasm!
