Google Street View shows interiors



The ubiquitous Google Street View has already filmed all the streets in the US - and began to penetrate inside buildings. At the request of the owner of a public institution (cafes, restaurants, shops), experts from Google go inside with the equipment and take panoramic pictures for 20-30 minutes .

Montage of digital photos is carried out on site, so if something does not work out, you can immediately re-shoot.
