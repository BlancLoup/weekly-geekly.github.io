Imeem.com Statistics

I recently wrote about www.imeem.com . Now I want to draw attention to their service statistics views.

Each user has the opportunity to get detailed information on viewing the content of his journal.

The start page of statistics looks like this:


On the graph you can see the total number of hits across the user's UGC (playlists, photos, music, video) by week. Below shows the amount of content in groups and the total number of hits, also broken down by groups.

Further, the user can get a detailed report on visiting any of his music files, videos, playlists or photos.

First, the user is shown a label, for example, with all mp3s and general statistics on them.


Further, by clicking on the file, the user is provided with detailed statistics for each file by day.


The trick here is that the user can see how many hits came from other resources, where the mini player with his composition was placed (remember, in imeem.com you can copy the embed code and post it to your website or magazine).

It is clear that, for example, on MySpace you can find more detailed statistics, but among the recently formed services, such detailed and visual statistics are very rare.
