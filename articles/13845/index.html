Google Reader Update

So, one of the most popular today rss-aggregators finally got new interesting features.
1. The search has finally appeared! It seems that it is no longer necessary to use third-party scripts to create a search window. Google's solution has an undeniable advantage - the search can be conducted among all subscriptions, and in folders, and directly inside the feed.
2. The counter of new messages has learned to count to at least 1000 and began to show the exact amount of the unread, instead of the mysterious inscription ‚Äú100+‚Äù.
3. There is an arrow on the side panel, clicking on which you can hide it. Goodbye, another script! By the way, this possibility was before, in order to hide the side panel, you had to press the u key. It seems that the developers gradually visualize the functionality due to the increasing popularity of their offspring - and few ordinary users like to memorize new keyboard shortcuts.
4. Removed the Google Labs logo, because Reader Now responsible for almost everything related to feeds in Google services.
5. According to subjective sensations, the speed of work has increased.

In general, Google Reader once again made a powerful leap. It looks like the new design of bloglines will not save!

PS There was support for Russian tags (thanks, mgarkunov ). In addition - Google Reader now looks like a regular web page, i.e. can be used to navigate browser buttons forward / backward.
