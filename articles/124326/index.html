GScreen company has released a laptop with two displays



Today, the company GScreen announced a model of a laptop with two displays, long awaited by some gadget lovers. This miracle of the gScreen SpaceBook technique is called, and information about it appeared a couple of years ago . Then the company reported that the laptop should appear soon, but everything turned out to be a little different, and the gScreen SpaceBook became available for pre-order just now.

Interestingly, the company has released two models of a two-display laptop. The difference between the two models is small, basically, this is the processor and the amount of RAM. So, the younger model is equipped with an Intel Core i5-560M processor with a frequency of 2.66 GHz. Also this laptop has 4 gigabytes of DDR3 memory, a graphics accelerator NVIDIA GeForce GTS 250M with 1 GB of RAM. There is also a 500 gigabyte hard drive, plus an optical drive. The cost of this model is $ 2395.



As far as can be judged now, the second model differs only in that the system is equipped with a different processor, Intel Core i7-740QM (1.73 GHz), and a large amount of RAM, 8 GB of RAM is installed here. The cost of the older model is $ 2795.



By the way, both the display - with a diagonal of 17 inches and a resolution of 1920 * 1080 pixels.

In general, a good device, but it is unlikely to be convenient to take with you on a business trip - a rather dimensional device turned out. Apparently, it is best to use it as a mobile desktop PC, the benefit, the configuration is quite nothing.
