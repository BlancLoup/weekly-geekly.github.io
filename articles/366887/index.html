Evapolar - the first effective mini-conditioner to create an individual comfort zone

Welcome to the pages of the blog iCover ! Today we would like to invite you to discuss the novelty from Russian developers Vladimir Levitin and Evgeny Dubovoy - the Evapolar mini air conditioner, which, according to the authors of the invention, is capable of creating an effective individual comfort zone within a radius of 2-3 meters. The project was announced at IndieGoGo, where it shows impressive growth dynamics.



Russian projects on crowdfunding sites can be found quite rarely. The more attractive is the story of the startup Evapolar, who managed to collect almost S300,000 dollars on the IndieGoGo platform since September 2015. The authors argue that the level of energy efficiency of their mini-conditioner exceeds all existing counterparts that work on the same principle and is not inferior to wall, floor and window climate cooling systems.

Why do we need mini-conditioners when there are multifunctional full-fledged systems?


The urgency of the lack of local air-conditioning was confirmed by preliminary surveys conducted by the authors. They note that about 30% of users of standard climate systems are not satisfied with the capabilities of equipment purchased by them or installed in offices. In large business centers, multi-zone equipment turns on at the beginning of the working day from 9 am and turns off at 6 pm, when part of the staff is still sitting at work, the opinion of each individual employee is usually interested in the administration moderately. The problem of individual climate comfort is quite acute in domestic life, especially where there are children, weak and sick people.

On the other hand, the market today is not ready to offer a truly efficient and cost-effective alternative to existing household air conditioning systems for general use. "... On the one hand there are many manufacturers offering full-featured systems that control the climate of the entire room or building, on the other - fans and humidifiers that do not support the cooling function, or cope with the air-conditioning function , but at the most mediocre level." Evapolar - Pros


The weight of the device, capable not only to cool the air, but also to work as a humidifier, is only 1.68 kg. The evaporation rate, which determines the air temperature, is regulated automatically. Efficient distribution of moisture over the entire surface of the cooling cartridge allows for intensive local cooling and humidification with minimal power consumption, 12 times less than those that occur in average wall and floor air conditioners. At the same time, the probability of a cold is high for traditional large-sized cooling systems due to the creation of directed cooled air flows in the case of Evapolar tends to zero.

Cooled to a comfortable temperature, evaporation is not only not harmful, but beneficial to the hair, skin and respiratory system. Restrictions not reported by the authors


Speaking about the advantages of the Evopolar mini air-conditioning system, which works on the classical principle of evaporation, it is necessary to note that the efficiency of the device largely depends on the humidity of the surrounding air. In hot climates and relative humidity up to just over 50%, the system works flawlessly. The efficiency of the installation is reduced in proportion to the increase in air humidity, so even if the temperature outside the window has exceeded 40C, with a relative humidity of 80%, the effect obtained will noticeably decrease. That is why the development of Leonid and Vladimir has brilliant prospects, for example, in the countries of the Middle East, in Mexico and in the south of the USA or in the arid regions of Asia, where the Evopolar can provide high efficiency with a minimum of energy costs.
