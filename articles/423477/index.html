Be a security ninja: start your way to the top of IB



Information security is one of the disciplines gaining widespread popularity in recent years. Meanwhile, many are frightened by the threshold of entry, the fragmentation of knowledge or the lack of an academic base. We decided to help everyone and organized a cycle of free webinars on information security.

For better understanding, we broke the cycle into five webinars on the main areas of information security. Now more about each event.


Basic terms of information security


Quick Start Guide on basic definitions, concepts and approaches to ensuring information security

We suggest starting with basic terms and definitions, basic concepts and approaches to providing information security. IS standards


Rapid immersion in best practices of information security

We review standards from international information security consortia, such as NIST, OWASP, ISACA, SANS, ISO 27XXX. You will get an excellent technical and methodological base if you plan to protect the infrastructure or intend to use your strength in pentest.



Webinar "Standards IB" will be held October 9 from 16.00 to 18.00.
Register for an event: jetinfosystems.timepad.ru/event/811833

IS requirements


Current requirements of the Russian legislation of the Russian Federation on information security

In this webinar we will examine several types of regulatory documents:
- defining requirements and approaches to ensuring the security of information that may have value for the company;
- defining requirements and approaches to the use of cryptographic information security tools.
In addition, we will consider areas of responsibility and requirements of regulators to ensure information security.



The IB Requirements Webinar will be held on October 11 from 4 pm to 6 pm

Overview of the main technical means of ensuring information security and principles of their work

From the webinar you will learn about the main technical tools that are used to comply with the principles of information security: antivirus, sandboxes, firewalls, IDS / IPS, honeypot, vulnerability scanners, DAM, DLP, PKI, IAM, MDM, SIEM and many others. Additionally, we will consider the main elements of building a safe ecosystem of the company's information structure.
