Beltelecom announced expansion of the external channel to 12 Gbit / s

Since May 19, the data transmission channel in the direction of Russia has increased from 6 Gbps to 10 Gbps. Bandwidth to the west has not changed. This was reported by the press service of the Belarusian monopolist .

Now the total external Internet gateway Beltelecom is equal to 12 Gbps .

According to unverified information , this time Rostelecom was a partner of the Belarusian monopolist, and the purchase price of the external channel was $ 18.5 per 1 Mbit / s. Let me remind you that the previous expansion in the direction of the Russian Federation happened quite recently - on March 19, 2009 - from 4 Gbit / s to 6 Gbit / s, and the external Internet gateway was 8 Gbit / s.

Now Beltelecom resells the channel to providers 50 times more expensive than it buys (compare $ 18.5 for 1 Mbit / s, purchased from Rostelecom, and $ 932 (excluding VAT) for 1 Mbit / s for national Internet service providers).
