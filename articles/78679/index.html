Chalk

I see this game as a shooter, although in fact you will not shoot anything here. By controlling a piece of chalk with a mouse, you destroy enemies by crossing their weak points with drawn lines. This game is a great example of a good implementation of a talented idea.

Free
Windows

Another innovative shooter, Clean Asia, allows you to control either Michael R. Dola on an attractive ship, or Miki I. Dola on a reflector ship. Your goal? The destruction of the evil eyeballs that stupefied the people of Asia (hence the name). But in fact, this strange plot is invented only for the wrapper of resourceful game mechanics, which forces you to smash enemies into pieces, and then use these pieces as bullets.

Kent Cho, revered in the TIGSource community as if he were a Chaldean god, created some really excellent, albeit somewhat abstract, scrolling shooters. A tale in the desert

A fascinating looped MMORPG, in which the main focus is on the economy, politics and socialization in contrast to the classic battles and quests. Despite the small number of players, ATITD stands out for its friendly and strong community, which makes a considerable contribution to the development of social orientation and group dynamics of the game. Users have a real opportunity to moderate the game, are able to write and pass new laws that entail wide gaming opportunities, including bans of some individuals (PG (N), hello!).

The chzo mythos

A cycle of four adventure games from Ben Kroshaw aka Yahtzee, the plot of which is twisted around the mysterious evil spirit of John Defoe. Starting from "5 Days a Stranger", where you play a thief-fortochnik imprisoned in the house with John's ghost, and ending with "6 Days a Sacrifice", which takes place at the headquarters of the fancy cult, the Chzo series of games spans hundreds of years and life of the three main characters. Each game is unique and filled with terrible secrets, but you have to play them in order in order to get real pleasure from this excellent game series.
Play this way: 5 Days A Stranger -> 7 Days A Skeptic -> Trilby's Notes -> 6 Days A Sacrifice.

Free
Windows
