With the help of promotions domestic tablets Oysters took first place in the Russian market

According to the results of the first quarter of this year, the domestic B-brand Oysters became the leader in the Russian tablet market. The company occupied 17% of the market in real terms, overtaking Samsung from 12.1%, according to MTS .

Svyaznoy specialists estimate the share of Oysters at 20% in units and at 7% in monetary terms. Last year, this figure did not exceed 2%. And only by the IV quarter rose to 8-9%.

For the first time, a domestic manufacturer has become the leader in sales in the Russian market. Samsung kept the lead in terms of revenue, but for the first time in several years, lost the lead in the number of sold tablets. In MTS stores, the share of Oysters tablet sales reached 43.8% in the first quarter, MTS representative Dmitry Solodovnikov said.

During this time, about 300 thousand Oysters tablets were sold, the head of the company's marketing department, Mikhail Turkov, estimated. Sales growth was provided by a diversified network of sales of devices, increased interest in budget tablets caused by the crisis, as well as marketing campaigns of partners.
