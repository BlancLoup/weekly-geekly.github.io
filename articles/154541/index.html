Google released a new colorful and educational project - Cultural Institute



The search giant launched the Cultural Institute multimedia encyclopedia, which is a collection of historical documents such as photographs, videotapes, manuscripts, letters related to important events that occurred in the last century from 1905 and in the first decade of the 21st century - until 2008.

The creators of the project say:

By creating this site, we wanted to provide you with new interactive opportunities to touch the culture. Attend online exhibitions, view artifacts, photos, read manuscripts, watch videos and more.

Although the choice of historical events from the point of view of Google may raise some questions (for example, the Second World War is considered only indirectly), the quality of performance of the site itself and its materials is a matter of respect.
