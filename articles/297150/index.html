Let's help a friend

Tuesday will make the day of help for the unemployed and distressed friends. In fact, today we offer you to apply all your skills in the field of networking and try to help a friend to find a good job (and among friends and loved ones there is always someone who is looking for it). It is especially useful to turn all this to those who are not yet ready to give up work, but want to understand what is happening in the market.
Firstly, it is good for your reputation as a person who constantly strives to help and support his neighbor. Characterizes you as a professional and personality. If in a circle of people who are interested in you from a career point of view, there are those who will be ready to help your friend, then you will probably be ready to turn to him at some point, and most likely he will come to you. // Wednesday
Watching ourselves

On Wednesday, you need to do this. Work all day as usual. But try to pay more attention to how you perform your duties, what brings you pleasure, how much time you spend, when your qualities show up as a leader or a responsible employee. Maybe you ingeniously cope with stress? All this must be recorded as soon as you notice something special behind you that may take place in your resume or cover letter. It will also be just great if you recall any story that will personify all your qualities as an employee and write it down. Storytelling in the summary, letters, and during the interview, has not been canceled.
