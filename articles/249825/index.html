The legal side of the issue
The use of technical means of suppressing signals of mobile phones and other radio communications refers to the sphere regulated by the state. For legal use of cell phone blockers, these devices must be certified for use in the Russian Federation and properly registered. The manufacturer or the seller (importer) is responsible for device certification. The owner registers such equipment, and a separate certificate must be obtained for each device used, indicating the identification data of this device.
