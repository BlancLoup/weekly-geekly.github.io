Revelation from the "Promotion"

He offered, no less, how to give them his clients on promotion and receive commissions for it. And they, allegedly with the help of some super-secret technologies, will bring sites to the very top. And before that I was curious - what are the latest developments that I decided to study their site. Something, and I discovered there new products with interest.
