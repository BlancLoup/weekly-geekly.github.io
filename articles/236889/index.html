Opera 25 Beta: Bookmarks and New Express Panel



Today we want to show you the beta of Opera 25. Starting from this version, the test builds, which are about to become final, are again called beta, and not Opera Next. It turned out that the word "beta" is much clearer to the average user and there is no doubt about the name of the browser itself.

Bookmarks


I managed to grab a lot of drawbacks for this idea, but we have been saying for a long time what to do as we were not interested in, we are interested in doing better. Therefore, the new bookmarks in Opera 25 beta are not just an endless and useless array of links. The bookmarks interface is more reminiscent of the old express panel (yes, it has also been updated, see below).

It helps to add bookmark recognition of the picture selection dialog to the bookmark. Following the example of social networks that generate an extended snippet from any link, we analyze the page and suggest you choose the right picture for it:

Express panel




The new express panel now resembles the bookmarks bar in mobile browsers and also seeks to show the site icon, rather than an inaccessible snapshot.
