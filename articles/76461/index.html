Testers for your iPhone application



Hello!
Recently, the network came across a useful resource - ibetatest.com .
The essence of this service is to provide testers developers for their applications. The idea is simple, there are developers, and there are testers. The developer post his project, makes a description of it and tells the testers what he is ready to please them if they come down to the test. Those and others in the win. Developers do not need to hire their testers, the ability to test the application on different iPhone OS and devices. Testers can earn a little or a lot :) or get the application for free, which is also nice :) The service started recently, but already has a large community, so take it on board, if that ...
