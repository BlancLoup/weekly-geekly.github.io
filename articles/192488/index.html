Smart photocopying with Kyocera FS-1135 MFP

Copy the four passport reversal on one A4 without dancing, but with the help of Kyocera FS-1135 multifunction device

About a year ago, we got a Kyocera FS-1135 MFP in our office. And I liked it so much, both to the admin and users, like a printer-scanner, that now I have a dozen of these little animals. Among the buttons on the printer panel, attention was drawn to the four buttons on the right that were signed with the word ‚ÄúProgram‚Äù.
In fact, this is not a program at all; it is a set of device presets so as not to clog them before each copying / scanning. A preset, it turns out, really, a lot.
A particularly sophisticated and beloved by the personnel department method of using this multifunction printer is copying workbooks. And here's why: they put the workbook on glass, press the green button, turn the spread of the book, press the green, [cycle repeat the necessary number of times], and the MFP gives them an A4 sheet, on which there are four reversal labor, two turns on each side A4 . Save time and paper at the same time.

The program "four A5 on one A4 from two sides for kyocera fs-1135"


Remove old program. To do this, press the hold button with the program number.
Switch to copy mode ("copy" button on the left)
click "function menu"
Next, change the parameters to
paragraph1 Paper selection -> paragraph 3 (A4 portrait)
n2 leave as is
P3 Duplex -> P2 (1 side >> 2 sides)
P4 Scale -> P2 (Auto)
P5 Combine -> P2 (2 on 1)
P6 Format of the original -> P2 (A5 landscape)
continue to change nothing
exit function menu

Save program: press hold button with program number

Is done

Using:
1 press the program button
2 confirm its execution
3 lift cover
4 put the next passport turn
5 lower the lid
6 push the green puck
7 repeat pp3-4-5-6 the required number of times
8 under the screen, click "complete scan"
9 pick up copies and original
