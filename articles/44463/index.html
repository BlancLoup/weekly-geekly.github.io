Korean netbook weighing 315 grams

UMID Company from South Korea introduced a new netbook weighing only 315 grams. It will be equipped with a 4.8 inch touch screen with a resolution of 1024 * 600, the dimensions of the device have not yet been reported.



Netbook UMID is based on the Intel Atom processor with a frequency of 1.33 GHz. The amount of RAM is 1 GB, SSD-drive - 32 GB. Wireless communication is represented by HSDPA, WiMAX, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules. Also, the netbook has a slot for a microSD memory card and a miniUSB connector. Built-in webcam resolution of 1.3 megapixels.

Of the possible pre-installed operating systems, Windows XP, Windows Vista and any operating system based on the Linux kernel are currently announced.
