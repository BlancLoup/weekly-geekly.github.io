Semi-compact GTK theme.

Many, including myself, believe that standard GTK themes are a bit gigantic. As a result, compact modifications of standard themes began to appear. I also used the Human compact for quite a long time, but it always seemed to me that it is different from the standard one, it also rushes to the extreme, this time minimalism. In this connection, I decided to make my semi-compact theme based on Human and, in the tradition of oss, share the result with everyone:

narod.ru/disk/2543607000/Human%20Middle%20v2.tar.gz.html

Now I will allow myself a few remarks:
In addition to the reduced size of elements, the theme is no different from the standard one. Unfortunately, the possibilities of gtk are not limitless and the result does not suit me 100%. I also want to note that the purpose of creating the theme was not only compactness, but also the preservation of aesthetics. Who is interested in maximum compactness - google: human compact. In the topic file, I allowed myself to make comments in our sovereign language in order to distinguish them from those already in the file.
