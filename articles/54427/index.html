AlertThingy shows updates from Basecamp and other social applications on the desktop

AlertThingy v3 shows updates from social networks right on your desktop. Able to send Tweets to Twitter, update Facebook status, upload photos to Flickr, post to Tumblr and something else. The third version of AlertThingy makes it easier and faster to manage your online life. Also there is a nice RSS reader built in.

For business users, there are modules to work with Yammer, Basecamp and Huddle. In AlertFingi you can combine work and leisure. Version 3 helps to control the flow of information, which is increasing every day. It also has a filtering system that allows you to visually highlight the most interesting information for you. It will help you quickly see All your friends from different social networks in Contact List.
