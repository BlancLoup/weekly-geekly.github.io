IBM Watson helps Chinese oncologists treat their patients



The IBM Watson cognitive system has been helping physicians for a long time. One of the tasks of Watson is to help doctors all over the world to research and treat cancer. Now our company has entered into an agreement with the company Hangzhou Cognitive Care from Beijing. The essence of the contract is the implementation of the Watson for Oncology program in 21 hospitals in China.

In this country, the level of cancer of the population is quite high. Every day, 12,000 patients are diagnosed with cancer. In addition, 2.8 million people die in China every year with such a diagnosis . IBM Watson will now study the problem. ‚ÄúThe number of oncological diseases continues to increase. 21 hospitals in China will use IBM Watson resources to develop individualized treatment for each patient. Individual approach always gives the best results. But in ordinary hospitals, doctors simply do not have time to search for an individual method of treating patients, therefore, methods generally accepted in medical practice are used. IBM Watson, after analyzing the genetic material of patients, can quickly find a course for any person. For this purpose, a database containing about 15 million medical text pages is used.
