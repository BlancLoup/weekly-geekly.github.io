Zemanta - service for bloggers

I came across an interesting service - Zemanta . The service allows you to receive tags, images, links on the entered text. It works like this - you enter text (so far only English is supported, but they promise to add more), the system recognizes it and gives pictures. tags and links on this topic.
Of course, the service does not give out 100% correct collections, but most of it goes on the topic.

For example, I tried to enter the text about Juno Reactor from Wikipedia . He gave me pictures about cyberpunk, the matrix, the group members. Not bad, he gave me a big picture just for some reason with Billy Idol, but then I saw one of the Juno Reactor band members standing nearby and understood everything.

Zemanta works like a plugin in Firefox, it does not support other browsers. It is installed as an extension and can then be used in Wordpress, Blogger, Typepad engines. I have not tried it myself, but I will take a word.
