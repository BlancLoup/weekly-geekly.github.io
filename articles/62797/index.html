More eSATA flash drives

At this time, in the field of hybrid USB-flash drives, the company Walton Chaintech distinguished itself, announcing the imminent appearance on the market of a series of devices called APOGEE Pronto .


The new flash drives are equipped with eSATA and USB 2.0 interfaces, weigh only 18 grams (size 79.5x30x10.3 mm) and have a capacity of 32 and 64 GB. They are compatible with all current operating systems (Windows 98 / SE / 2000 / ME / XP / Vista, Mac OS 9.x / 10.0.x / 10.1.x / 10.2.x / 10.3.x and Linux 2.4.x).

The data transfer rate via USB is 32 MB per second (read) and 25 MB per second (write). For eSATA, this figure is much higher: 93 MB per second and 52 MB per second for reading and writing, respectively.

But on the timing of the appearance on sale and price of the Walton Chaintech APOGEE Pronto is not yet known.
