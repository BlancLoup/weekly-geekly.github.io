Free music on youtube

There is no denying the power of music. The right song can create a magical mood . At the same time, the wrong one will ruin it in an instant . No matter what state you want to dive into, the soundtrack is crucial.

When you upload videos to YouTube, understanding where to look for the right melody can be quite difficult. We do our best to provide authors with the tools necessary to fill the content and simplify this process. But we also want to simplify the search for great music for your videos.

That is why today we are launching the YouTube Audio Library . Any YouTube author now has access to more than 150 free instrumental compositions that can be used for free, at any time, for any creative purposes (not just for YouTube videos). You will find a link to the library in your video editor and will be able to filter tracks by key, genre, instrument and duration. Tracks can be downloaded as MP3-files (320 KB).

If you are creating a melodrama, listen to The Engagement


Guitarist Oscar Rodriguez (Oscar Rodriguez) Records a Track for the YouTube Audio Library

As you can see, this project was full of fun. We have long and diligently searched for musicians to create tracks in our library, and ultimately found like-minded people in various places: an acquaintance in Los Angeles, music studios across the country and a famous music producer in Brooklyn who produced Phish and Shawn Lennon albums ( Sean Lennon). Thus, we became practically rock stars (at least, we felt like them for a while).

If you are shooting a trip, pay attention to the Hot Heat

We would like to know your opinion so that we can continue to improve the contents of the library and its viewing capabilities. And if you know any of the musicians who might be interested in contributing to the library, let us know .
