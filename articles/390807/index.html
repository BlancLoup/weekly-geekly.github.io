How to travel using virtual reality technology



Teleport to any corner of the planet? Why not. Today, VR-helmet is enough for that.
Just a couple of movements, and you hear the distant noise of city streets and have already made the journey to London, physically being in your city.
This opportunity today provides a virtual reality headset Oculus Rift. The device can "teleport" a person to the most exotic virtual environment, no matter where he is in the world.

"Teleportation" to your favorite city


Such an experiment is actively pursued by the global Marriott hotel chain. Its guests have the opportunity to virtually visit tourist places, using the Oculus Rift with high resolution and three-dimensional screen.



True, virtual travel is still a rare pleasure, but in the very near future they can become massive. Representatives of the tourism industry see great potential in virtual reality.

This experience goes beyond the boundaries of Google Street View or VR tours of hotels and resorts. As planned, virtual reality can replace the real journey. Perhaps not complete, but testing VR fragments such as traveling through the Grand Canyon or walking along the Wall of China will allow customers to choose their place for future rest.

Innovative technologies make it possible to go into outer space. Recently, Kickstarter presented the Overview One project, through which everyone will be able to feel like astronauts.


Space adventure


In theory, it will be possible to send passengers to the International Space Station (ISS) using a panoramic camera, the viewing angle of which is 360 degrees.
