Roman Osipov ‚ÄúA detailed review of Mathematica 10 and Wolfram Cloud‚Äù



The presentation is available here .

The presentation and the video talk about both the Mathematica system and the Wolfram Language programming language in general, as well as many innovations that appeared in the recently released 10th version of Wolfram Language ( Mathematica ):

- the fundamental structures of the language - associative arrays, databases, effective methods of "freezing" the code, time series, templates, HTML, lines, documents;
- Integration with the Wolfram Cloud;
- built-in geometric sublanguage;
- built-in sublanguage of geographical calculations;
- means of centralized access to the Wolfram | Alpha databases and new supervised databases;
- embedded parsers and means of semantic data interpretation;
- Functions for the numerical and analytical solution of differential equations (analytical solution of differential equations with a retarded argument, finite element method for solving PDE for regions of arbitrary shape, etc.);
- machine learning functions;
- date and time functions;
- overview of other built-in functions of the most diverse orientation: from generating and recognizing QR codes to shortening a URL and building Mandelbrot sets.
