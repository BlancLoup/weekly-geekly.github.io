Will the regional BZH domain appear?

Brittany is an old French province with rich traditions and a special language. This inspired many other independent regions that are part of large states, including Brittany. Then a group of independent users made a petition and placed it on PointBZH.com. More than 15 thousand people have subscribed to the online application, and as a result, a project committee was created.

The preparation of documents for the committee uses the experience of similar projects in the GAL (Galicia - Autonomous Region in Spain) and CYM (Wales) domains. A presentation report campaigning for the creation of the BZH domain should be submitted by the end of December this year. Then the preparation of the official ICANN application will begin.
