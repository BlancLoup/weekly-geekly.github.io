Yandex.Disk congratulates the new year


In addition to donated 200 gigabytes of free space in the cloud as an apology for the failure, Yandex.disk presented me today 2014MB for using Yandex.disk in 2013.

Having logged in to Windows virtual system, I saw that the familiar Yandex.disk icon in the tray has changed a bit, a Christmas tree has been added to it. At first, I thought that the Christmas tree is just an addition to the icon to set the mood and remind that the new year is rushing to us, soon, they say, everything will happen. But when I clicked on the icon with the Christmas tree, I saw this message with an offer to receive a gift. And after clicking on the button Get a gift, a message in the tray notified me that the volume of my Yandex disk has grown by 2014MB


Thanks to Yandex for a nice New Year gift.
