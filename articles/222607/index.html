Happy freelancer!



Today is a common holiday for all kinds of people who are not united by a profession or gender. It is a celebration of all those who have chosen restless freedom and independence in the difficult task of earning their daily bread. This is a celebration of free shooters, labor book ronin. Today is the day of the freelancer!

For our country, the phenomenon of freelance if not new, it is certainly still unusual and somewhat alien. Although everything is new - well forgotten old. In Russia, there has long been a stratum of people, as they would say today, who are not in the staff of any organization. Instead, they were hired for various temporary, piece-work. Of course, not all of these workers chose this way of life of their own free will, in part this was dictated by unemployment. That is, being a freelancer means a priori being a good specialist and being able to sell yourself.
