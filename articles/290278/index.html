There are two main forums where SEOs hang out: searchengines.guru and maultalk.com. At the same time, the attendance of the first forum is higher and amounts to 35-40 thousand users per day.

Calculations, logic and combinatorics


Many people in childhood probably watched the program ‚ÄúRussian Lotto‚Äù, we decided to take just such a principle as a basis for the draw. Each member receives a ticket.

Then the barrels of numbers are pulled out of the bag in the air, the participants cross out the numbers in their lottery ticket, the one who has the ‚Äúcombination‚Äù has won.

Such a scheme perfectly fit into the format of the draw on the forum. After all, in the comments, users will ask for tickets, and we will issue these tickets in the comments. Thus, the topic will always rise in the ranking.

In a traditional lotto there can be several winners, and the prize fund is divided into all. We played prizes, not money. Therefore, each ticket had to be unique.

Besides, I really did not want to win a ticket that was not distributed.

So, we solve a simple math problem


It is given: in the ticket 5 numbers. The combination of numbers in each ticket is unique. The order does not matter. In the bag 90 barrels.
Question: how many tickets?

Decision.
The rules of combinatorics tell us that the number of combinations without repeating n different elements with respect to m is calculated using the formula:



Those. the number of possible combinations is 90! / (5! 85!) = 86 * 87 * 88 * 89 * 90/120.
Too much! Accordingly, in order to reduce the number of tickets, you must either reduce the number of numbers in the ticket, or the number of barrels.

Of course, we hoped for the maximum effect, but we understood that it would hardly be possible to distribute more than 500 tickets. Therefore, we decided to play it safe and add barrels as needed. Those. First, we distribute all the tickets that combine 10 numbers of 5. Then add the 11th keg. When all possible combinations of 11 numbers are distributed, add the 12th barrel and so on.

At the same time, we announced to users that no more than 3003 tickets would be distributed. Those. the maximum number of barrels would be 15. The limit on the number of tickets pushed users to be more active and hurry to pick up their ticket.

As it turned out, the scheme was chosen correctly. In total, we distributed 316 tickets, so there were 11 barrels in the bag. In this case, for 13 days the message on the forum was viewed almost 20,000 times. Seoshniki learned about Energloslon, which means that everything went according to plan.

How to organize an online broadcast


The next issue that needed to be resolved was to conduct an online broadcast. This is where Google and its Hangouts helped us. Hangouts has the ability to broadcast live on youtube. Moreover, the number of simultaneous viewers broadcast is not limited. This is exactly what we needed.

Video filming needed to be organized in such a way that viewers could see:

  • first leading;
  • secondly, the bag, which should always be in the frame;
  • thirdly, the numbers on the barrels.

It became clear that you need to shoot with two cameras. In Hangouts, we decided to organize a meeting between two users, and switch cameras at the right time. We also needed a microphone to be heard well. And a bluetooth headset to let me know in my ear the names of the winners and other information.

So, 4 laptops, two cameras, two microphones, a bluetooth headset were simultaneously involved in the online broadcast. None of us had any experience in holding such events, but it seems to me that we did very well.
