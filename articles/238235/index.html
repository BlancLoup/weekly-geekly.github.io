Police drones are looking for undeclared mansions in Argentina

Hi, Habr!

Many people fear the invasion of drones in privacy. And no wonder they are afraid. But drones can help a person in many ways: take breathtaking photos from the air, control events and even search for illegally built mansions. And, of course, to look for routes of drug traffickers, what they successfully do in Argentina. The cost of drones used by special services is about $ 10,000.




Law enforcement agencies in South America are already using drones to search for drug smuggling routes. So now, if they suspect that an undeclared mansion is located somewhere, whose owner is hiding from taxes, they are sending drones equipped with cameras there. This approach is already bearing fruit: instead of photos of desert areas and bushes, drones brought pictures of huge mansions with pools. Their owners are faced with serious fines, which, according to government estimates, will bring about $ 2 million to the country's budget.

Already 200 buildings and 100 pools have been discovered.
