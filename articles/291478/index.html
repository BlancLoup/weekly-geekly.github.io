Human Resource Management - Planning Loss of Working Time

I lead a small team of 40 people who work in the 24/7/365 mode. All the tasks assigned to me would have been impossible without a well-balanced team. The loss of working time by employees plays an important role in finding balance in the team, and the fight against the loss of time is one of the tasks of the manager, since there is nothing more expensive than time.

I would like to tell you how I struggle with the loss of time and learn how you do it. I ask under the cat :)

In my opinion, the loss of time can be divided into two groups, managed and unmanaged.

Managed loss of time:
1. Vacation - we are all human;
2. Study (Trainings, seminars, universities, etc.), business trips - a team of professionals;
3. Hospital - they can also be controlled!

Unmanaged loss of time:
1. Hospital - black box;
2. Dismissal - a new person;

I consider the most interesting losses of time to be hospital ones; they can and should be managed by me, they make up 3-4% or 1 person per year!
