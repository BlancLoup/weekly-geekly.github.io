DevCon 2013: conference program announcement



Dear software developers and testers, participants of DevCon 2013 conference. On behalf of the organizers, I am pleased to present you the program of the two days of the conference, which included reports in six parallel tracks, six exclusive workshops and an evening program with round tables and the Night of Coding hackathon.

At http://www.msdevcon.ru/schedule you can find the current conference schedule.

Below I will talk about some details of the program DevCon 2013.

Main program


The main program of the conference includes a plenary report, keynotes and mini-sessions of both days. In total, conference participants and online viewers will be able to choose from 67 very different presentations .

Note! The entire main conference program will be recorded and broadcast online in real time! If you are not lucky and you can not take part in DevCon 2013 in person, then visit the official website http://msdevcon.ru on May 29 and 30 to view the sessions online.

This year we have decided to make the conference program and reports more in-depth. Therefore, more than a third (closer to half) of DevCon 2013 reports will be level 300 or higher.

The first day of DevCon 2013

The conference will be opened by a two-hour plenary report , within which, according to tradition, a general overview of all company news for developers over the last year since the last conference will be given. Participate in the plenary report will be: Jonas Persson , Jason Zander and Alexander Lozhechkin , as well as other colleagues from Microsoft.

Note! On the first day of the conference, there will be a question and answer session with Jason Zander , corporate vice president of Microsoft, who previously led the entire development of Visual Studio tools, and today is responsible for the entire Windows Azure platform. For DevCon 2013 participants, meeting with Jason Zander is a unique opportunity to meet with the highest level Microsoft director and ask him all the questions.

On the first day, along with the plenary report, participants and online conference viewers will be able to attend 25 sessions on all software development topics: client and mobile application development, web and cloud application development and development, effective development tools and building a development process using ALM, corporate application development.

The second day of DevCon 2013

The main program of the second day of the conference will open with 30 minute interesting mini-reports. You can learn about many interesting things about software development tools from our partners and guest speakers.

The entire second day will be filled with technical reports. During 30 hour presentations, conference participants and live viewers will be able to learn a lot of interesting things about the most modern software development approaches and the latest tools.

Note! One of the sessions, labeled NDA (What is Hekaton ...) , due to exclusivity and secrecy will not be broadcast online and recorded, and its participants will have to sign a non-disclosure agreement on the spot.

The final of the conference will be the official closing ceremony, during which the results will be summed up, the winners of the competitions will be announced and memorable gifts will be given out.

The first day of DevCon 2013

On the first day of the conference, parallel to the main program, three simultaneous master classes will be held:

  • Master class by Gaidar Magdanurov "From Idea to Success"
  • School of Functional Programming with Dmitry Soshnikov, Roman Dushkin and Andrey Laptev
  • Intensive training on software testing from Dmitry Andreev

In the evening of the first day, the participants will have an entertaining evening program with a surprise, which we secretly prepare and round tables of the additional program:

  • Roundtable JetBrains. Tool support for mobile and web application development
  • Round Table Clouds: A Second Breath for Your Decisions
  • Roundtable Startup in Russia: Myths and Reality

Interesting participants are expected at round tables, for example, Ilya Ryzhenkov, the head of .NET development at JetBrains.

Another great activity of the DevCon 2013 conference will be Hackaton ‚ÄúCoding Night‚Äù , where conference participants will be able to apply the knowledge gained during the day into practice, coming together for coding. The hackathon will be divided into two parts: practical for writing code and theoretical with lectures. In addition, as part of the hackathon will be laboratory work Windows Store App Lab . Hackathon will last from nine in the evening to three in the morning.

The second day of DevCon 2013

The following workshops will be held on the second day of the conference as part of the additional program:

  • Master Class. Scrum: Theory and Practice in Visual Studio 2012 by Askhat Urazbayev and Viktor Strelkov;
  • Design School " Interface Design for Non-Designers " by Denis Kortunov and Yegor Gilev;
  • Master class by Paolo Salvatori. Building a cloud backend for mobile applications using Windows Azure

The second day will end with the closing ceremony of the conference.
