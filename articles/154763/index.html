For Blackberry OS 10 open reception applications. The action with a refund of $ 10,000 continues

Today, I received a notice in the mail that for the long-suffering Blackberry OS 10, which could not be released on time, they began accepting applications for certification to the requirements of the new system.

It is worth recalling that RIM is trying hard to draw the attention of developers on its platform, whose forces are occupied by Android and iOS. So, at the beginning of this year, the company offered a free PlayBook tablet of 16 GB for porting its applications to the BlackBerry OS; then, a little later, announced the promotion, according to which, the developer of the application that has passed certification in the AppWorld application store and who cannot earn $ 10,000 over the year, is guaranteed to receive the difference to his account (although there is also a condition written in regular font : the application still has to earn at least $ 1000.
