Swift Telematics


I know personally the history of this company, from which WavIoT was subsequently separated. He is well acquainted with Evgeny Akhmadishin, who was the co-founder of the company and the technical director in the initial stage of development of ‚ÄúStryzh‚Äù.

Sources of information about the company in Wikipedia .



Methods of receiving a signal in the radio have long been well studied, everything is done close to optimality. But at a certain point in time there were 3 factors that allowed to create something new in this industry:

  • the accuracy of quartz oscillators gradually increased and increased, making it possible to increase the duration of signal coherence;
  • the power of the emitters (namely, power, and not energy per bit of information) is so limited by state laws that there were problems with signal transmission over distances of more than 100 meters for existing radio protocols;
  • there was a market for IoT devices.

In "Strizh" developed a technology for receiving and processing signals, in which 1 bit of information could be transmitted up to 100ms. The probability of receiving a bit of information is determined by the signal-to-noise ratio, in the numerator of which is the signal energy (or power per band, which is equivalent). By improving the quartz oscillators, it was possible to narrow the transmission band under the previous limitation on the signal power, extending the coherence duration of the radio signal.

In addition, the signal processing bandwidth due to the use of special calculators and the original processing method was about 1 MHz. One receiver made it possible to receive signals from thousands and tens of thousands of sensors (it depended on the transmission intensity, but here it was even better than in LoRa because of the separation in frequency). Low sensor consumption made it possible to create sufficiently compact devices that have been operating for years from autonomous power sources. The image is an example of a water sensor.

In general, this is a great example of an innovative company that could be created in Russia. Here it is possible to find qualifications in the field of radio and mathematics, and this qualification was absolutely necessary to create a competitive technology. Personally, I was also amazed that, in addition to creating the technology, they were able to make a grocery solution, which included everything from direct sales by the housing manager to the front-end client applications.

Then the company was divided into 3 businesses (Swift Telematics, WavIoT, NWave), I can not imagine how things are going for each of them, but as far as I heard, sales for all are growing and reaching billions in rubles in one of the companies . NWave does business successfully and attracts investments based in London.

In the meantime, the investigation opened a case because of parking sensors for Moscow parking lots, which were designed and manufactured in Strizhe. The company received a contract from FSUE FSO Sveko, which in turn participated in the state contract. The investigation formulated the criminal community, which included the technical director and co-founder of Strizh, Yevgeny Akhmadishin.

Today, Yevgeny Akhmadishin is in the SIZO for more than 1.5 years, losing everything he built.
