IT Championship: first 10 days - normal flight!

On www.it.campus.ru published assignments for the participants of the First All-Russian Student IT-Championship. Those who have not yet had time to become a participant can still register on the campus of the IT championship and choose one or several tasks from partner companies to solve.

The legend of the Russian Internet and one of the most famous programmers in the country, Anatoly Wasserman, supported the IT Championship . He also expressed his readiness to join the expert council of the competition and stressed the importance of this event for young professionals and for the industry as a whole.

The first All-Russian IT-Championship opens a cycle of professional competitions for young people, which are launched by the career-educational social network Campus.ru. The championship is focused on students and graduates who specialize in programming and information technology. They will solve the tasks of the most famous companies in the industry, getting acquainted with the real needs of the business. The winners will receive valuable prizes, as well as the possibility of internship and subsequent employment in one of the companies.

It is no secret that because of the crisis, many young people are in the most vulnerable position, especially those who do not have work experience. Campus.ru aims to help young people not only improve their educational level, but also find a job in their field.

To take part in the championship, you need to enter its campus. 2,000 people have already done this! Join now!
