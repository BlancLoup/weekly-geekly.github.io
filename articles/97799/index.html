Live Wallpaper with Mario for Android (2.1 and higher)



Cvetko Pirsch introduced Mario Live Wallpaper for Android 2.1 and higher. This is a live wallpaper, on which the notorious Italian plumber passes randomly generated levels on the street, underground and in the castle.

How it looks, you can see on these videos:





Although this is not an interactive game, the plot is not set, but is created in real time. This can greatly affect the battery charge level, but in the settings you can turn off the background of the route and reduce the frame frequency.

To download Mario Live Wallpaper, login from your Android device via this link or use the QR code. The app is free, you can donate something via PayPal here .
