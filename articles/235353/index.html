Smartphone Meizu MX4 officially presented

Today in Beijing, a presentation was held at which the Chinese company Meizu presented its new flagship - Meizu MX4.
Details under the cut.


The smartphone received a 5.36 ‚Äùscreen, made by Sharp New Mode 2 technology, with an aspect ratio of 15: 9. The screen has a resolution of 1920x1152 pixels (418 ppi) and is covered with a protective glass Gorilla Glass 3. The device has a body made of aviation aluminum and an even thinner frame, due to which its dimensions are comparable with many flagships with a smaller diagonal. The dimensions of the Meizu MX4 were 144 x 75.2 x 8.9 mm, with a weight of 147 grams.



As for the technical specifications, this year, Meizu decided to abandon Samsung's Exynos processors by switching to an MediaTek 8-core chip: MT6595, made using big.LITTLE technology (4 Cortex-A17 cores with a frequency of 2.2 GHz and 4 Cortex-A7 cores at 1.7 GHz, each group operates alternately, depending on the complexity of the tasks, with the ability to use all cores simultaneously. This chip is gaining more than 47,000 points in AnTuTu. The new quad-core PowerVR G6200MP4 chip is responsible for the graphics. Meizu MX4 is equipped with 2GB of RAM and 16/32/64 GB of internal memory (128GB version, as in MX3, no). The battery from Sony has a capacity of 3100 mAh.



The smartphone is equipped with a 20.7-megapixel Sony IMX220 Exmor RS main camera module with focus for 0.3 seconds and a protective Gorilla Glass 3 glass on the lens. The camera can shoot video in 4K format, as well as Slow Motion 120fps in 720p mode. The device also has a dual LED flash of different tones. The front camera has a Sony IMX208 module on 2Mp.
