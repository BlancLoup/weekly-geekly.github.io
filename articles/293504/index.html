This story began in June of this year in Silicon Valley and led us to the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating a Hub for programmers and entrepreneurs in Mexico in the wonderful city of San Jose Del Cabo, which is only 3 hours from San Francisco. Under the cut the details, a lot of pictures and a killer video.

IT technologies are developing by leaps and bounds, and soon there will not be a single area that would not be affected by IT. A programmer may consider himself to be the creator of a new matter out of nothing and all he needs is a computer with Internet access and comfortable environmental conditions. That is why big companies such as Google and Facebook are investing a lot of money in coffee machines, game rooms, sleep capsules and thousands of other devices that are intended for people of the creative profession. The output is a high-quality product, which sometimes grows into a billionth history.

Already, there are people who travel freely, while working in a place that they like, surf, while solving very important and innovative tasks on the Internet. With this post we want to tell about another such place where it is comfortable to work, while receiving all the preferences from life.

Meet CaboHub - a hub for programmers and entrepreneurs in Mexico.

Advantages of the hub:
1. Close to Silicon Valley
Only 2.5 hours of summer will finish CaboHub from San Francisco. This is very important because it allows you to be present in the Valley at least every week, searching for investments, partnerships, marketing, and all that Silicon Valley is so helpful.



2. One hour zone with Silicon Valley
+1 hour from San Francisco to San Jose Del Cabo, this means that you can work comfortably with your colleagues from San Francisco and give up tedious work in the evenings and nights, which is inherent in distributed teams.

3. Facilitated visa regime
You can enter Mexico for up to 90 days on a US visa, and Russian citizens do not need a visa to enter Mexico. It is also very easy for citizens of Belarus and Ukraine to get a Mexican visa. You can also get a long-term visa for 4 years, showing an income of $ 1,750 per month.

4. Cheap accommodation
$ 200 - $ 300 per month for one-room, $ 500 - $ 900 for a three-room apartment, 2500 - $ 3500 per month for a villa. These prices are cheaper from 2 to 5 times what Silicon Valley offers.


5. Warm weather all year round and, unlike Silicon Valley, a warm ocean.

For whom is this:
1. For companies that have received investments in Silicon Valley and would like to optimize their fixed costs. California is very expensive and many people move to Texas and even to the other coast, solving this problem, but losing the difference in time.

2. For distributed teams located on different continents, working with Silicon Valley.

3. For entrepreneurs creating a business in Silicon Valley, or just planning to create it.

4. For programmers looking for work in Silicon Valley. In America, you can stay a maximum of 6 months at a time on a guest visa, but the more you are there, the higher the risk that you will not be allowed next time. While in Mexico, you can fly to California quite often, while saving on accommodation and reducing risks at customs.

5. For colleagues from California, who would like to escape from the bustling everyday life and dive into the atmosphere of sunny Mexico, surfing, and get acquainted with the CaboHub community.
