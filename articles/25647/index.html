Virgin Group launches WiMax network in Russia


The company Virgin Group , owned by Richard Branson, launched a WiMAX network in Russia covering 20 cities, based on an agreement with Swiss tele -investor Trivon . The network is called Virgin Connect .

Telecom Trivon has established its network in 32 regions of the Russian Federation, including the Moscow, Leningrad region and 20 largest cities, where it has the appropriate licenses and frequencies to provide WiMAX service.
Connection services are already available in the Moscow region and Belgorod, Orenburg and Ufa.
