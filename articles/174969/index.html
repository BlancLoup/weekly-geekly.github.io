Nokia Lumia 920 against the spring sun

We have already talked about the PureView technology used in creating the Nokia Lumia 920 , and about the capabilities of the camera of the smartphone, which allows you to make great shots in low light conditions.

Now we decided to check how high-quality images can be obtained in bright daylight, in the warm rays of the spring sun that has already appeared.





Moscow

So, we went on a photo walk through Moscow streets to share spring mood with you. For the purity of the experiment, we filmed similar shots simultaneously on our two smartphones - the Nokia N8 and Lumia 920.

All pictures were taken with the standard settings of the camera, large versions of images are available by clicking.

New York

However, Moscow does not indulge us with an abundance of various spring plots. Our colleague came to the rescue, who returned from New York and brought with her a large collection of pictures taken on the Lumia 920.

Judge for yourself: optical stabilization allows you to take high-quality photos on the go, and the supersensitive screen responds well to pressing with gloves (the air temperature in New York at the beginning of March was slightly above zero).





In addition, the preparation for photographing takes a few moments: get the smartphone out of your pocket, hold down the camera's hardware key for a few seconds to immediately pass the unlock process, switch to shooting, and then, without removing gloves, tap on the screen where it should be focus.
