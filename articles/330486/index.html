As the "Pilot" upgraded the fiscal registrar in Fujitsu CCT

Surely, you read a lot about the landmark amendments to the Law 54-FZ "On the use of cash registers", obliging domestic stores to report in real time about the sales, transferring data on settlements with customers via the Internet to the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation. The changes affected almost all retailers (except for a small list of exceptions specified in the text of the amendments), even those who had previously worked under the simplified tax system.
In detail about the essence of the law and its impact both on stores and on ordinary customers, the Pilot group of companies wrote in its blog . Now we will clearly demonstrate how the cash registers (CCP) need to be upgraded according to the requirements of 54-. Interested please under the cat.


Let's show it on the example of cash registers (CCP) POSprint FP510-F . The FP510 printer from Fujitsu produced in Japan was taken as the basis for the printing device.
