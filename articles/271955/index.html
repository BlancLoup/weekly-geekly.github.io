We invite you to the December meetings of the IT community in Samara, Novosibirsk and Krasnoyarsk

As promised in his previous post about meetings - new meetings of the IT community in Samara, Novosibirsk and Krasnoyarsk. This time, the theme of the meetings is Windows 10. Windows 10 operating system came out about 4 months ago and on the eve of next year, you can calmly meet with colleagues and discuss what's new in Windows 10, what new implementation scenarios it supports, how it can be managed in a corporate and not-so environment, how to manage Windows 10 updates and many other issues that arise with any IT person when he learns about the new OS.

We invite you to the meetings of the IT community in Samara, Novosibirsk and Krasnoyarsk, organized by the IT community and MCP clubs with the support of the Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals community!
