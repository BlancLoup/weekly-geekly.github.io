Humane and effective mousetrap

Blogger Chris "MacGyver" Glass, in his blog published a very simple and effective way of catching mice, below is a free translation, although it is clear from the pictures how it all works:



  • We take a tube (base) of toilet paper, or make any other of your choice, from not too light or too heavy cardboard.
  • In the case of a tub of toilet paper, we cut it on one side of the circle and glue the two halves so that the design could lie flat on the surface of the table or on any other platform, do not stagger and roll.
  • We put a small piece of cheese or cookies on the edge of the mousetrap, and put the design itself on the edge of any surface you choose so that the bait is on the opposite side (see the figure).
  • Now it is necessary to put an empty open bin under the constructed structure. so that the mouse does not get out.
