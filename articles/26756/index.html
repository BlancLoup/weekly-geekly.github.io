AdFun is preparing a new network

It is rumored that the AdFun affiliate program, popular among entertainment sites, is preparing a new version of the network. The new engine will significantly increase the efficiency of the network and will be a surprise not only for advertisers and partners, but also for competitors working in the market of monetization of entertainment traffic.

Currently, this affiliate program occupies a leading position in terms of the volume of redeemed traffic on the entertainment sites of Runet and enjoys great success among network partners with current efficiency. However, the creators of the affiliate unofficially declare that the new version of the network will more than double the efficiency, thereby doubling the supply of traffic to advertisers and funds by a partner with a similar volume of promotional materials in the current version of the network.

How this will be achieved is still a secret, however, there is every reason to believe that the stated indices of entrainment response will be achieved.
