ProductCamp Minsk 2015 - video of performances

In October, ProductCamp took place in Minsk - the annual BarCamp for experts in the field of product management. The event covered various thematic areas of product management: from marketing research and development to market launch and operation. All camp participants are professionals in product management and marketing, which promoted active informal communication and the exchange of knowledge and experience.
On the camp, experts from Wargaming, Yandex, Localway / Anywayanyday, Dnevnik.ru, Ecwid, SEMRush, Netology-groups, Skyeng, Tutu.ru presented their reports.

Under the cat you will find recordings of speeches:

  • Free products. Where is everything going? / Gleb Kudryavtsev, Dnevnik.ru
