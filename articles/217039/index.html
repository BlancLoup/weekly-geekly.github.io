All on Strike! The largest conference in Ulyanovsk will be held April 11-12



Dear colleagues, on April 11-12, in Ulyanovsk, for the third time, an IT conference STACHKA will take place and this time there will be a lot of surprises for participants.

In 2013, STACHKA gathered more than 3,000 participants and became the largest conference in the regions, at the same time the event hit the digital-market infrastructure map as one of the most significant festivals in the IT sphere. The organizers did not stop there. This year, STACHKA goes to the international level, you will hear speeches by foreign speakers.

We, at Microsoft, together with our friends, the conference organizers, are planning to take part in the conference and offer you several activities. First, we took part in organizing the conference and preparing the cloud section of the reports , which will be held on the first day of the Strikes:

  • Ivan Mosiev, AltexSoft, Ukraine . Comparison of the largest cloud providers Amazon, Rackspace and Windows Azure on personal experience
  • Kirill Smorodinnikov, Yandex, Russia . Distributed Storage Systems
  • Vladimir Yunev. Microsoft, Russia. From PHP to Java and C # via Python and Node.js. How to write cloud projects in your favorite language?



Secondly, especially for the conference participants, we will organize a series of practical training workshops for developers with the following topics:

1. Infrastructure in the cloud . Virtual machines and networks. VPN between the cloud and the workplace. Clusters and HPC and Big Data tasks on demand;

2. Single cloud backend for all mobile platforms : iOS, Android, Windows, Windows Phone, Xamarin, PhoneGap, HMTL5 / JS, etc. The solution to the task of sending push notifications to different groups, in different languages, to millions of users on different platforms.

3. Quick Startup in the cloud . How to get and start using cloud power for nothing. Dev & Test scripts in the cloud

4. Internet commerce in the cloud . Accommodation online store. Fast start. Scaling. (Representative AdvantShop.net)

Come to our master classes on the second day of the conference!

And thirdly, together with colleagues, we will organize a booth of the Windows Azure cloud platform, where we will be happy to meet and talk with the conference participants, answer questions, distribute souvenirs and prizes.
