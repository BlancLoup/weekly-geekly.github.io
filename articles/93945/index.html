Amazon Web Services provided less redundancy storage.

Reduced Redundancy Storage (storage with less redundancy) is a new service Amazon S3 , which allows users to reduce the cost of storing non-critical, easily reproducible data using less redundancy.



RRS is a cost-effective solution for storing content that can be replicated from other sources. Standard Amazon S3 storage and storage with less redundancy - both store data across multiple sites. Standard Amazon S3 storage is designed to provide 99.999999999% reliability and data loss protection at two sites, while the RRS guarantees 99.99% at one site. Both stores provide a high level of data access and are under the Amazon S3's Service Level Agreement . More information is available at aws.amazon.com/s3.
