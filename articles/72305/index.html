Wii bike

Information about the new amazing add-on for the Wii console has appeared online. Fans of yoga, fitness, bowling and golf have long received their accessories for their favorite console. Now it is the turn of bike enthusiasts!
Meet: Cyberbike!

The device is expected to be available very soon, in January. Time selected successfully. In winter, even in relatively warm countries, you will not ride a bike on the street, so a new virtual exercise bike will come to the rescue.
Naturally, the device itself will not work, so in addition to it, a game for the Wii has already been prepared. It is possible that over time there will be more of them, the sport is quite popular.


The game allows you to control not only the bike, but also very futuristic devices, the analogues of which have not even been created in the real world. For example, a submarine on the pedal.


The game has three modes: fitness, story and multiplayer for up to four people. Although I personally can hardly imagine multiplayer on a stationary bike. Most likely it will be just the race in turn.
