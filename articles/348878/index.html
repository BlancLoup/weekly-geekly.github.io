New online software for developers

This spring, CS Center and Academic University launch three free semi-annual online programs on the Stepik.org platform:

  1. "Algorithms and effective calculations",
  2. "Math for Developers",
  3. "Development in C ++, Java and Haskell".



Let's not be modest and tell you what these programs are good for:

  1. Lecturers. Courses are read by experienced developers and scientists.
  2. A lot of practice. To consolidate the material studied, students have to solve more than 100 problems.
  3. Personal support. If difficulties arise, the assistants and teachers will help to deal with the material.

Programs consist of courses that are already freely available on Stepik. To enter the program, however, is worth it, because students will be able to practice solving additional tasks, as well as ask questions in a separate tracker to assistants and teachers who can help at any stage of the course.

Education in the programs is free, but based on the results of the online entrance exam. We propose to solve several problems and write a short but capacious essay about why you plan to study on the program.

Start of programs on March 1, you can pass the entrance tests until February 26. All the details on code.stepik.org .
