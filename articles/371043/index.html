Fuse 1 video review - the first affordable SLS 3D printer



Watch the video review of the 3D printer Formlabs Fuse 1, which we translated into Russian for you.

This is the first personal SLS 3D printer - previously this technology was used only in industrial printers for hundreds of thousands of dollars.

The original video released by Adam Savage's YouTube channel Adam Savage's TESTED.

What kind of printer


Formlabs Fuse 1


  • Technology: SLS
  • Printing area: 165 x 165 x 320 mm
  • Printing material: nylon
  • Producer price, in the US from: 9,999 usd

What is interesting Fuse 1




The technology of 3D printing SLS (selective laser sintering) was used earlier only in industrial 3D printers. Such printers took up much more space and cost from a few hundred thousand dollars to a million. Fuse 1 is the first personal SLS printer, the price of which is ten times less, and the print quality is not inferior to expensive colleagues.

What prints


Fuse 1 prints with nylon - a material that favorably differs from common plastics for FDM printing by its mechanical properties - bending strength and impact loads, wear resistance, resistance to heat and ultraviolet radiation.
