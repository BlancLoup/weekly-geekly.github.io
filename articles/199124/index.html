iversity - online courses from the European platform MOOC



Massive open online courses (MOOC - Massive Open Online Course) have become a real alternative to other forms of distance learning. Over the past two years, along with the open educational resources of the leading universities of the world, independent specialized sites have appeared on the Web - MOOC aggregators, such as Udemy , Coursera . Another example is iversity.org .
What is MOOC? This is a course of study, all materials and tasks of which are available online to any inhabitant of the planet. MOOC brings together teachers and students interested in a particular topic. The more participants and open materials, the more effective the training will be.

These are the courses that iversity offers to everyone for free. Here you need to perform only three simple steps to start learning today: choose the course you like, enroll in it and start learning. I also want to draw your attention to the fact that the platform has German roots and therefore some courses are presented in German.

Among the "first swallows" that became the prototype of the MOOC were 11 places where you can take university courses for free. Then, continuing the topic, MIT's online resource at MIT OpenCourseWare further announced the deployment of new free educational materials for self-study. And very soon his example was followed by Yale University, posting 35 free courses available through the Open Yale Courses.
