ScrumTrek and AgileRussia: Requirements Management in Agile Workshop | December 2nd

ScrumTrek and the AgileRussia community, with the support of CustIS, hold monthly, free-of-charge seminars on various aspects of Agile software development and web services.

Agile project / product management is primarily concerned with effective requirements management. Good requirements = customer value. For any product manager, there are two main obstacles to product implementation:
  • What to do first? How to manage priorities?
  • How to integrate requirements gathering into an iterative development process.

At the next meeting of AgileRussia, which will be held on December 2, we will discuss:
  • What is the difference between the collection of requirements in Agile from the classical approaches
  • What are the practices of working with requirements
  • Let's discuss what Product Owner should be responsible for.
  • We will touch on the topic Usability, as an integral part of the requirements development process.
  • Let's talk about what incremental design is when developing desktop and web application interfaces

Location and time: the center of Moscow, the company's office Custom InformSystems

Conditions of participation: Free . Registration is required. Just fill in the registration form or write a letter to askhat@agilerussia.ru .
