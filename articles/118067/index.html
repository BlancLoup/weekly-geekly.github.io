Criteria Description

Here are some of the criteria listed in the document.

Import and export data
All the formats found in the table are quite common.
CSV (comma separated values) - here and so everything is clear. Data is listed in rows. Inside a line, items are separated by a comma (or other character). This format is read by Excel and many other programs.
OFX (open financial exchange) is the most common format for exchanging financial information. The majority of Internet banking can export data to it. Alpha Bank and Citibank too.
QFX is a proprietary variant of OFX. Used exclusively by Intuit in their Quicken financial collection application.
QIF (quicken interchange format) is an open format that appeared before OFX and is not supported since 2006.
MT940 (message type) is a message format introduced by SWIFT for exchanging information about accounts. Used by banks around the world.

Server installation
Item for megaparanoikov. If you do not want to share your financial data with service staff, and you need access from all devices at least in the apartment, then install the service on your server.

Account Support
Ability to create many, many accounts (> 1). Under the account refers to all sorts of options for storing money: bank cards, savings accounts, cash ...

Types of accounts
Cash, current accounts, credit accounts / cards, debts. I was especially interested in the ‚Äúdebts‚Äù clause, since not everyone has implemented it, and the clause is very important in our harsh reality.

Recipients
The ability to enter payment recipients: companies or people. It can be used in the formation of statistics, such as "how much I spent on the purchase of pencils in ikei."

Ability to recognize checks
Personally, this function would be very useful to me. Those expenses that we pay in cash, sometimes just too lazy to make manually. This function was not detected in any application.

The ability to attach files
As a replacement for the previous function: made the costs, attached scan scan. You can save the invoice in the mail.

Currency support
The ability to specify for each account and transaction its own currency.

Profile Support
The ability to specify who owns the account: me or my dog. It is necessary if the budget is conducted for several people.

Invoices
Invoice - a document provided by the seller to the buyer, which specifies the characteristics of the goods, as well as the delivery time and amount to be paid. According to Wikipedia, an analogue in the Russian Federation is an invoice. Obviously, this is only necessary for people who are going to trade something.

Budget planning
Setting a limit for expenses, and receiving messages when this limit is reached.

Nested transactions
For a nested transaction, you can break the amount into categories or simply by name (as in a regular store receipt).

Rules for automatic transaction processing
This feature resembles the filters in the mailbox. For example, if the name contains ‚ÄúInternet‚Äù, then the recipient must be set to ‚ÄúMegatelecom‚Äù.

Filters for statistics
Receipt of a report filtered by various criteria: time, categories of payments, etc.
