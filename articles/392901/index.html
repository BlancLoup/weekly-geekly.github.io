Meet the Honor 4C Pro. Real marathon runner

To the question "What kind of smartphone to take in the region of 10 thousand?" You can safely answer: Honor 4C. But if this is not enough and there is some more money, you should take a closer look at the 4C Pro - they changed the case, and specifically pumped the battery and changed the processor.



Appearance


Five-inch smart five-inch - strife. There are comfortable models, and there are real bricks. 4C Pro from those that are conveniently arranged in the palm of your hand: the back cover is convex, the faces are not sharp, and the plastic is quite pleasant to the touch. Some charm adds a distinctly tangible texture pattern from the back - small bumps and do not irritate, and at the same time make the gadget more catchy.











The only thing that strains - frank zakos under the metal frame. In actual fact, the case is entirely plastic (which is not even bad), you just do not need to hide it, because everything secret becomes apparent.







The back panel picks up without problems, do not worry about the nails. Inside is the main feature of the entire device - a non-removable 4000 mAh battery. How it turned out to fit him in a not-so-large building is a mystery. But somehow nice. Here, under the lid, slots were placed for two micro-SIM cards and under microSD up to 128 GB.













Battery


The battery in this smartphone is the one for which it will be bought at all. Not a budget gadget can work two (that's for sure), or even three days from a single battery charge. The low display resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels and an economical processor will not land at 4000 mAh faster than this monster. But, if you buy an OTG cable, you can charge other devices with it. The Honor 4C Pro itself with the help of a standard adapter for 2 amps has time to charge up to 100% in a little less than a couple of hours.



Display


The five-inch display of the Honor 4C Pro can be called bright while you are indoors. If you go outside, you have to unscrew the settings to the maximum - the light sensor works, to put it mildly, slowly. And yet, it is worth turning the gadget at least a small angle, the picture immediately becomes noticeably darker. In general, with the display in this model is not particularly wondered, and, perhaps, as a compensation, the manufacturer put in a set of protective film. Before you glue it, you should make sure that the screen is clean, and to squeeze air bubbles with your finger, push it to the screen. The reward will be an additional protective coating. It does not affect the sensitivity of pressing, but the brightness seems to decrease a little more.





Iron


Chipset MT6735 can not be called particularly powerful. The four cores of the processor run at 1.3 GHz and it‚Äôs not worthwhile to count on the fact that it, combined with 2 GB of RAM, will vigorously pull heavy games. Basic applications work, and thanks for that. A firmware in Huawei optimized so as not to load the already weak iron - removed from there all the optional beauty and made the animation easier.



In general, everything depends on the price of components and battery consumption. Put the manufacturer in this model some Kirin 620 with eight cores, and the device would be discharged much faster and warmed up more. Snapdragon would be much more expensive, so the choice fell on an inexpensive Mediatek solution.





The memory of the smartphone is 16 GB, while the user is traditionally less available - 10.55 GB. In principle, for the first time this should be enough, and then you can get hold of an SD card. At 128 GB, however, such a card will cost about half of a smartphone, but the prices for 64-gigabyte microSDs do not bite anymore. If you choose not the fastest, you can easily find a couple of thousand rubles.

Soft


The optimized EMUI 3.1 shell is one of the best modifications of the basic Android 5.1 that can be found on the market of low-cost smartphones. A pleasant "European" interface design (yes, the main design bureau at Huawei in Paris) and a lot of fine settings look just fine against the background of frankly Chinese graphic solutions for most competitors. Here, something similar to iOS: svaypas are supported almost everywhere for switching between different screens and there is no separate application menu - all the shortcuts are located right on the desktops. A special feature of any smartphone with EMUI (even if the Lite version) is the original notification curtain, logically divided into two screens: with the quick menu buttons and the notifications themselves.







Camera


It would seem that there is a 13 megapixel sensor! But, as you know, the point is not in the resolution itself, but in optics, the sensitivity of the matrix and the data processing algorithm. The resolution of photos obtained with Honor 4C Pro is large, but on the computer screen it‚Äôs better not to watch them - you will immediately notice artifacts of noise reduction and compression in JPEG. In the dark, the noise level immediately rises. Well at least the camera application starts quickly and generally does not slow down. In short, for Instagram it will come down, but it's better not to count on more.

















Total


Finding a worthy competitor Honor 4C Pro for 13 thousand rubles is not so easy. P1m comes to mind from Lenovo for about 14 thousand, well, or look in the direction of these "Chinese", but then you have to order through AliExpress. In general, Huawei has turned out quite a good machine for those who do not need superpower, but the long autonomous work - on the contrary. The main thing is not to take the Pro prefix seriously and not to expect a strong performance increase relative to the base model. Well, the original appearance, too, should not be written off.
