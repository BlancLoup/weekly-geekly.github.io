IT digest of November events (part two)

The forecast for the end of November in the IT community differs little from the forecast for its beginning: a steady blockchain, local hackathons and a pleasant abundance of chamber and large-scale initiatives in the regions. However, there is a slightly increased concentration of distributed systems and web development.



ZeroNights 2018

When: November 20-21
Where: St. Petersburg, Med. Ave., 3, A2 Green Concert
Conditions of participation: 10 000 rubles.

The organizers have spared no effort to create the most useful program of reports: the selection of those who want to speak was held on a competitive basis. The finalists were five speakers - high-level experts from different countries. With their help, participants will learn how the update mechanism can become a gap in BIOS protection and what to do about it; what are the specifics of working with domestic manufacturers of components of industrial control systems; what vulnerabilities are typical for compilers; how the race condition is exploited and how to protect the app on Node.js.

Blockchain Conference Moscow

When: November 20
Where: Moscow, st. Tverskaya, 22, InterContinental
Participation conditions: 15 000 rub.

The largest blockchain and cryptocurrency conference brings together all involved - developers, financiers, investors and just enthusiasts - to enjoy insights from experts from two dozen countries and discuss all pressing issues with representatives of the global community at a high level. Among the speakers are presidents of large formations, representatives of the public sector, authors of famous works, evangelists and the founders of initiatives. Two streams of reports, Fintech Hall and Blockchain Hall, will provide a reasonable division of the audience by interest.

CryptoEvent RIW

When: November 21-23
Where: Moscow, VDNH, Prospekt Mira 119, Pavilion 75
Participation conditions: from 2900 rub.

This year, within the framework of Russian Internet Week, a large crypt conference will be organized for the first time with an integrated exhibition where participants will demonstrate how the blockchain is used in various fields, from FinTech to medicine and art. The program includes the results of 2018, research from rating agencies, financial indicators of the largest blockchain companies, and trends in all major areas. Within 3 days, more than 90 speakers from Russia and the CIS will perform on the platform: from blockchain developers to top managers of reputable IT companies. The exhibition will feature more than 50 products: platforms, ICO projects, cryptobirds, cryptocurrency exchange services, mining equipment. In special zones, participants will be able to discuss opportunities for cooperation and partnership.
