ITMO University Digest: 12 thematic conferences, hackathons and MOOC courses for IT students

This is a compilation for specialists and technical students. In this digest we will tell about the nearest full-time and distance thematic events.

Conferences, forums, festivals


1. BreakPoint Forum

When: March 16 - 17
Where: St. Petersburg, the place is specified ( group in VK )

This is an event for students and graduates of technical specialties. The organizer is the International Youth Organization AIESEC. The forum will work two streams: "Career" and "First Step". "Career" is suitable for those who seek professional development in an IT-company. The ‚Äúfirst step‚Äù will be useful for people with an idea for a startup, but without a clear implementation plan. For two days of the forum, it will be possible to discuss trends, attend master classes on the topic of career development, participate in the discussion of business cases and pump soft and hard skills at trainings. To register you need to fill out the form .


2. Digital Spring 2019

When: March 28
What time: from 10:00 to 19:00
Where: Bolshoy Prospect of the Petrograd Side, 37, Vvedensky Hotel

Here they will share the experience of creating VR, AR and MR content, but without long lectures and monologues. Gadgets can be tested and participate in master classes. For example, Matvey Kardash, amoCRM development director, will talk about the intricacies of web analytics.


3. International Youth Scientific Conference "Gagarin Readings"

When: April 16 - 19
What time: from 9:00 to 18:00
Where: Moscow, st. Dubosekovskaya, 4, the main building of the Moscow Aviation Institute

Annual event for students, graduate students and young scientists dedicated to the aerospace industry and developments in the field of robotics, IT and power engineering. Deadline for applications - February 23 (application requirements ).


4. II Technology Festival MY.TECH

When: June 8
Where: Medikov Ave., 3, Technopark of St. Petersburg

The program will be four tracks: science, development, industry and business. The organizers offer various forms of participation: from presentations and reports to workshops and networking. Among the speakers: representatives of large businesses and experts from innovation support funds.

Contests


5. Competition of students "ITMO.Online"

When: May 28, 2018 - May 27, 2019
Where: online

This is an opportunity to offset the costs of paid online courses on open education platforms . Students can take undergraduate, graduate, postgraduate and postgraduate studies at ITMO University. To win the competition you need to apply through the university information system, complete the course and receive a certificate.


6. International competition of light installations and video mapping

When: August 17 - 18, applications are accepted until March 29
Where: Gatchina Park

Here you can submit your work in two categories: light installations and video mapping. In the first can participate static or dynamic art objects, where the main role is played by light. In the second nomination, audiovisual works will be exhibited in 2D and 3D animation formats, as well as other video art techniques.

The themes of the competition are water and reflections. The final will be held as part of the St. Petersburg International Festival "Night of Light . " The winner of the first nomination will receive one hundred thousand rubles, and the second - fifty thousand rubles.


7. XVI All-Russian competition ‚ÄúMy country is my Russia‚Äù

When: accepting applications until April 10
Where: the first stage is online; the places of the regional and internal stages are specified

The purpose of the competition is to involve schoolchildren, students and young teachers in the development of Russia and the regions. To participate, you need to submit an author's project in one of nineteen nominations related to various aspects of people's lives: from transport and production technologies to health and education. In addition to the competition there will be trainings and seminars. Among the prizes are internships, additional points for entering a university, grants and awards.

Competitions and hackathons


8. 2019 ICPC World Finals

When: March 31 - April 5
Where: Online and in Porto

Finalists are already defined. It remains only to support your favorite team. This year's ICPC final will be held in Portugal. The broadcast will be here - ICPCLive .


9. HACKUNIVERSITY 2019

When: March 22 - 24
What time: from 17:00 to 19:00
Where: st. Generala Khruleva, d. 9A, building St. Petersburg Film Studios

Here, teams of four will have to develop a prototype in 48 hours in one of nine areas: Big Data, VR / AR, blockchain, IoT and robotics, Linux and open source software, audio, social services, logistics, information security. The program has workshops, presentations and workshops on various technologies and topics, including the application of the theory of solving inventive problems. The winners will receive certificates and cash prizes, as well as the opportunity to attract investments to develop their project. More organizers promise to help with the search for temporary housing. To register you need to fill out a form .

Where: Moscow, participants from other cities will be paid for travel expenses

Courses

When: March 4 - May 26, recording is open
Where: online
