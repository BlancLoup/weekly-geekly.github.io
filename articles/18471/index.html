MacHeist II - time to buy

Today began selling a set of programs MacHeist II . Those who have not yet understood the essence of this action, refer to the post " MacHeist - season two ." The ten programs included in the bundle (aka the bundle ) MacHeist II are sold for $ 49, and if you bought them in the normal time, they would cost more than 300. No one, except the managers of this promotion, knew what exactly Programs will be included. But I already have operational information, which I hasten to share with you.
