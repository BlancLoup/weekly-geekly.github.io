Taiwan: iPad - Free, Cover - $ 1000

The sale of Apple tablets in Taiwan is illegal (the authorities of the Republic of China have not yet issued an Apple license). Therefore, businessmen have to look for workarounds and apply unusual marketing schemes.

For example, on the Yahoo Auctions website you can find an iPad case with a price of 32,000 new Taiwan dollars (approximately $ 1,042).

Although in normal online stores the same case is 30 times cheaper, here it is among the most popular products. The fact is that a 64GB iPad with WiFi and 3G is included with the case for free.

Officials have already commented that selling an iPad in this way still remains a violation of the law. However, they can not make a complaint to the site Yahoo Auctions and remove the goods from the sale, because formally the auction service does not sell Apple products.
