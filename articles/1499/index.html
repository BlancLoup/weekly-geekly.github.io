Putin will hold an Internet conference at Yandex

June 30 at the "Yandex" begins the collection and publication of questions for the Internet conference of the President of Russia. The most popular questions will be handed over to Vladimir Putin during an Internet conference to be held on July 6, 2006 on the website president.yandex.ru .

On the conference website you can not only offer new questions, but also vote for the questions already asked. The more votes a question has collected, the more likely it is to get an answer from the head of state. Users can raise the importance of the topic, calling to vote for her friends and acquaintances. For the first time in Russia, the president will answer questions selected during a popular Internet voting.
