SeeMap.ru - Public transport routes. Continuation


 In December, the website of the Routes of St. Petersburg urban transport was presented - SeeMap.ru (see Kartu.ru). Since then, many shortcomings have been corrected, comments and suggestions have been taken into account. This time we will try to show the web 2.0 in its own beauty, but will it be possible ...?



First of all - the most interesting! Now, any visitor to the site can edit all the information about the route, including the trajectory, and, if the route is not in the database, easily add it by typing the names and tracing the trajectory. Of course, the addition is semi-automatic. Before updating the route on the site, it is necessary, at a minimum, to check it for grammatical errors, i.e. the route is pre-tested. How to be otherwise? (question number 1)



Secondly , you can vote for the route. If the information is correct, put a plus sign, no - route to ignore. Therefore, it is already possible to judge the relevance of the data by rating. All actions are possible without registration in the system (and adding routes too)!
