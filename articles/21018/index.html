Gossip (2.0)

Most willingly, it seems to me, people will go to a resource that relies on one of the sins (from cook sites personifying gluttony, to those indicated by three x-ones personifying lust), because sin has always been more attractive.

Now in the period when all classmates and friends are cataloged and listed as friends, everyone is fighting over the question - what's next?


Gossip is certainly not a mortal sin, but who does not like to discuss someone or something in a narrow circle. Fortunately, information is spread via the network instantly and the same news-gossip is retold a couple of times. So why not make it easy for gossipers to speed up the process and give birth to new gossips?

Imagine that there is a resource, a person is registered, a certain id is assigned to him, no names, no emails, no asek called. Everything is anonymous. The person creates an exciting topic, makes it either closed or open to prying eyes, and sends invites to friends, with a brief description of gossip. And then - enjoy the discussion and washing the bones of all, from Kolya from 11B, to the future president of the Russian Federation.
