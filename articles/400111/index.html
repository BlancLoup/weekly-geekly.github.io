California authorities officially banned Uber from testing unmanned vehicles



Uber has received an official ban from the California authorities to test their own unmanned passenger cars, reports Reuters and a number of major English-language publications.

Testing began last Wednesday and after a few hours, Uber management received a formal notice demanding to stop work under the threat of a lawsuit from the state administration. Negotiations that took place last week did not lead to anything. Now Uber will have to look for a new place for testing.

The ban came due to the fact that Uber, according to the authorities, did not properly register its test vehicles as autonomous vehicles. Representatives of Uber, for their part, argue that they do not need such registration, as their development cannot yet move independently without human participation throughout the route.
