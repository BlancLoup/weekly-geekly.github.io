Smartphones are coming



We stand on the threshold of the greatest era of smartphones.

This is the conclusion of the new ZenithOptimedia report. It says that by the end of 2018, 66.5% of people in key digital markets will have smartphones . In parallel, the growth of Internet users is slowing.

The study analyzed the current position of digital marketing in 47 countries. Such indicators as Internet access, media consumption and features of user behavior, as well as the projected increase in the quality of Internet connections and the improvement of smartphone and tablet technologies were considered.

Curious facts came to light. For example, the fact that on a global background, the introduction of smartphones is higher in the Asia-Pacific region and Western Europe. Singapore has the highest level of smartphone penetration in everyday life - starting at 89% in 2014 and ending with growth prospects up to 97% by 2018 (based on government plans to ensure ubiquitous access to free wi-fi).
