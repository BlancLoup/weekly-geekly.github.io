Dates of the competition

The competition is held in two stages.

The first stage is the acceptance of entries.

Acceptance of entries will last until December 20, 2011 inclusive.
According to its results, the finalists will be selected - 10 teams (participants) who have scored the maximum number of points on the rating scale (about her slightly below).

The second stage is the final.

It takes place from December 20, 2011 to January 15, 2012.
The 10 finalists who scored the most points should upgrade their applications to the state of a commercial launch with the help of LG engineers. After that, the jury team will re-evaluate among 10 applications of finalists (again, according to the rating scale).

Announcement of the winners - January 30, 2012.

Winners Awarding Ceremony - February 10, 2012.
