The hunt for licensed content: we produce music

Hi, Geektimes. Since May 1, amendments to the anti-piracy law No. 187-FZ came into force in the Russian Federation. Now the court has the right to block not only resources with illegal video production, but also with music, lyrics and software - after the appeal of the copyright holder, of course. That is, now almost any type of content (except photos) falls under the law. And besides - and this change is the most important - if the same copyright site again violates the same plaintiff, the resource in punishment will be blocked indefinitely . As you can see, everything is really serious.



As an educational program - this is how this law is being implemented in practice now:

Hidden text
After the right holder appeals to the court and confirms the fact of the placement of illegal content, Roskomnadzor immediately contacts the site owner with a request to remove it; if after that within three days the content is not removed, the authorities will contact the hoster. If the hoster does not take action, the appeal will follow the provider (which is unlikely to go against the court order). Blocking a page with content (or the entire site, depending on the situation) will last 15 days, during which the resource owner may file a counterclaim. And yes, it will still have to remove the content.

Not only trackers and versioners, but also music search aggregators, as well as the Vkontakte people love, can be under the impact of a toughened law. Something will go to foreign servers (which, however, does not hurt to block access to them from the Russian Federation), something will go where the ICQ is now and the modem, something will be redeployed or (as is now Odnoklassniki ) license posted content. And, most likely, the last way will go a few.

Thus, the relevance of the issue, where you can get the content legally, gets up to its full height. In addition, the mass use of pirated content in our country is developed not so much because of greed or lack of funds, but because of the unwillingness to waste time and nerves to get licensed games, music and movies. If users are available convenient platforms with legal content, then without any anti-piracy law they will be in demand. And fortunately, there are such platforms in Runet.

In our article we will look at where, at a distance of a couple of clicks and without unnecessary trouble, you can listen to copyrighted music for free or for reasonable money. At once we will make a reservation - it will be a question only of the largest resources having Russian-language interfaces.

The first, perhaps, is to consider the good old LastFM .



Yes, yes, LastFM. Now it is certainly not as popular as it used to be, and it seems to be used more as a database of information about groups and performers and as a community of music lovers than as a radio station and content storage. Nevertheless, there are really a lot of free tracks loaded by the musicians themselves. Yandex.Music




Yandex.Music is not a search engine aggregator (which may be under the impact of an updated anti-piracy law), but a storehouse of music sorted by artist and album. Completely licensed, just in case, we note. At the moment there are about 18 million tracks here - more than enough, if you are, of course, not a fan of some, for example, very little-known dark folk like Werra or there Rose Rovine E Amanti. There is an opportunity to collect personal playlists, listen to mixes-radio, there is a system of recommendations (based on the listened tracks and your profiles on Facebook and VK - however, it is not clear how NM manages to detect them). In short, there are abundant functions. There is no only possibility to download tracks to your hard drive - however, with such a completely free set of functions, there is nothing surprising. By the way, keep in mind that the mobile application rips off a monthly fee of 169 rubles per month, but it can cache tracks for listening in offline mode. You can also listen to your music, but for this you will need to install Yandex.Disk.

Google Play Music




It's all the same - millions of licensed tracks (including a lot of free ones), radio, recommendations. But there are also quite significant differences. First of all, you can upload your own tracks (up to 50 thousand) to the cloud and listen to them without restrictions on your smartphone or via the web interface. And no one asks where you originally took them :) In addition, the music represented in the system can already be downloaded to your own media. However, for this you need to buy it separately as Google Play products. But still there is such an opportunity for those who prefer to keep the music in full offline. On top of that, you can transfer the library from iTunes to Google Music for free (let's talk about iTunes itself later).

Of the minuses, there is a completely dull system of music breakdown by genres (and also often a strange genre definition - for example, the latest album In Flames is designated here as progressive metal), a banal selection of recommendations, and a shortage of Russian-language tracks (compared to Yandex). The next object of our attention is Deezer , an analogue of the Spotify service (not available in Russia).

Deezer




The main advantage of Deezer is that it is easy to handle like a hammer. Top random radio based on the tracks you listened to (Flow), bottom individual recommendations, on the side of a fixed panel the search form, profile data and player. Really comfortable and nothing extra. And it looks stylish.

The amount of music here is almost two times higher than in the same Yandex.Music - after all, foreign origin has an effect - 35 million tracks. In addition, there is an extensive database of lyrics.

Deezer is free for computers and tablets, but a paid subscription (199 rubles per month + free trial period for 30 days) gives many good features - the ability to cache tracks to listen offline, import MP3s from your device, and remove ads and improve sound quality. And yes, it allows you to enjoy all this splendor on your phone - without a paid subscription, only Flow and mixes will be available to you. By the way, there are virtual gift cards here - they are useful if you want to drag your friends to Deezer.
