New legal reference system "Prav.ru"



Any field of human activity is governed by regulations and legislative acts. And the better prepared and informed you are, the easier it is for you to live in a highly bureaucratic system. The reference and legal system Pravo.ru is interesting primarily because it gives anyone full access to the regulatory documents of any constituent entities of the Russian Federation, as well as to the judicial practice of arbitration courts, and in the near future, the opinions of leading experts of any legal fields.



Online access


Access to the system is carried out from any place: from work, from home, in a car - the only condition is an Internet connection. At the same time, optimization of the system allowed not only to ensure high speed of work with a slow connection, but also to significantly reduce incoming traffic.



Document update


For any help system, the frequency of updating the database is important. Only in this case can we guarantee that the user will receive relevant information. Often it is up to it to make specific important decisions. All documents in our database appear and become available after their official publication in open official sources. The user does not need to update or reinstall something - the documents are available immediately online.

List of codes with all revisions available for viewing



To date, the total number of documents in the system is 359,185 . Every day we fill the database with new documents and archives.

Single document base


All documents are immediately accessible to our users - from federal legislation to normative acts of individual constituent entities of the Russian Federation. For convenience, the user can limit the search to their region.

Restriction of regions to view



Quality and search results


The accuracy of search results is ensured by a unique algorithm that takes into account not only the degree of compliance with the request, but also legal force, as well as document popularity and other factors. The search result takes into account the interests of users and displays exactly the documents that are most in demand by them at the moment.

Standard output of the search result , with the ability to filter by the main types of documents



Display of search results in a structured form , with the ability to sample by various parameters



Interface and Functionality


Even the most unprepared visitors will easily get used to the new environment. The main approach used to create the interface was taken from popular search engines - Google, Yandex, Bing. Any element of the system has interactive prompts and a description in the detailed instructions. At the same time, the appearance and capabilities are constantly being improved in accordance with the wishes of real users.

Document display and context menu



Already, visitors are available: saving a document, leaving notes, interactive navigation through the document, a mechanism for comparing revisions, searching for specific text. And in the near future it will be possible to send the text of the document to another user, various options for sorting search results, as well as interactive services and calculators of state fees. All features are described in more detail in the system help .

System help window



Social network


The exchange of knowledge between users is one of the most significant features of the system. Already, visitors can leave comments and discuss regulatory documents, and in the near future will be able to unite in interest groups for the exchange of experience in all legal areas.

User comment for a specific paragraph of the regulation



Mobility


In addition to standard browser access, you can always use the mobile version of the system if you have an urgent need to study a specific article of the law or regulatory document. An iPhone version is already ready, and applications for other platforms are expected in the near future.



Soon we want to tell you more about how the system was created, what opportunities it has, and what's new will appear soon. We are happy to accept criticism and want to make a truly worthwhile product.

Please note that the full set of documents and features is available only to registered users.
