Interactive Mood Clips

Why is it interactive? The answer is simple, the clip starts to play, if you hover it with a mouse. Examples of the use of music can be found in your blog. Basically it can be used to show your mood, opinion about something, share a cool gif image with friends.

To create a muzpic, you need to download an animated gif, select a style of sounds, then a sound and click the create button.
