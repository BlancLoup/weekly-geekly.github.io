Implementation. These were the main elements of the project:


Large video wall

Video wall from MicroTiles projection cubes, made according to a unique design project for a non-standard screen shape. Consists of 38 MicroTiles projection cubes and a Radar Touch device. The content was developed according to a separate project by the partner company ENGAGE. Radar Touch works very spectacularly: the device tracks the touch around the perimeter of the wall, and in accordance with this touch, it works out some kind of activity. It turns out something like a big touch-screen. People can watch precious metals rates, use exchange calculators, climb historical data of exchange rates, and so on. Static content was simply poured onto the server and updated by hand, dynamic was constantly updated from various sources, prepared for the wall and was taken from the server by the controller.



This thing is one of the most expensive solutions that we deployed. She stands at the entrance to the office. Instead of making a boring wall of rectangular or square shape, you can scatter the video cubes as you want. The picture, of course, must be written under the wall. On tests, she aroused the admiration of all the people who simply approached her. What is important - you could immediately get the necessary information from the wall, for example, at the rate of gold.

The wall was hung on the mount, which was prepared for individual orders. It was welded to the bearing steel beams, on which the foundation of the entire building is built.
