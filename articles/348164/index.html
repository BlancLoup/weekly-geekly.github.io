Great developer, true engineer and real leader - RIP Shawn O. Pearce


January 29, 2018, died Sean Pierce, a well-known programmer, author, committer and founder of many projects, including Git, Jgit, libgit and Gerrit Code Review.


An outstanding developer, a real engineer and a great manager died, leaving wonderful boys, a wife and ... all the open source community.


Almost a year ago, when he was diagnosed with lung cancer, he had only one request, do everything possible, look after the boys and Sarah to help them during the transition period.


He joined the Git-project in 2006 and has since been one of the most productive project developers. Its merits include many basic functions such as the Git-over-HTTP protocol, the concept of fast-import (which many other version control systems later adopted), the concept of reflogs.
