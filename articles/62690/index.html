VIDEO medicine library

Unfortunately, the educational segment of Runet is still far behind the West and the United States, especially when it comes to video.

However, educational video portals of a good level are beginning to appear in our country. Let's take a look at the informative video about great doctors, and also find out why and for whom the medical video portal Med-edu.ru was created and what materials can be seen on it!



All videos created with the participation of reputable specialists in the field of postgraduate education and are available for free online viewing. You can watch the operation with the surgeon's comments, view videotapes of lectures and reports, and ask the lecturer a question.

I understand that for the main audience of the beloved Habr most of the material may not be particularly interesting (although some lectures on general subjects, such as biochemistry or psychology , will certainly be interesting to many), therefore, if among your relatives and friends there are doctors or medical universities - send them a reference.
