The shape of the device is dictated by the smartphone. Unfortunately, HTC placed a microUSB port on a smartphone on the left side of the case, closer to the bottom. In the case of the ‚Äúcradle‚Äù a corresponding ‚Äúbed‚Äù is made, repeating the bends of the body of the smartphone. It highlights the connector connection. Sync connector

Operation of the smartphone in the "cradle"



Smartphone in the "cradle"

The smartphone is placed in the stand at an acute angle to the surface of the table.
Install the device in the stand is not so easy. It is necessary to accurately observe the angle of inclination and horizontal bottom edge of the smartphone. Otherwise, the protruding socket of the stand will rest against the body of the smartphone without hitting the microUSB hole. After proper positioning, the smartphone enters the stand. The device is placed very tightly, forming almost a single unit with the stand. This is facilitated by the combination of the gloss of the stand and the shiny surface of the screen of the device itself. There are practically no gaps between them. It looks beautiful, but I'm not sure that such beauty will be for the "Sensation" with a white body.


Side view

When connecting the cable to the computer, the influence of the stand is imperceptible. The smartphone with the same success synchronizes with the PC and charges. The speed of the charge from the PC is the same as directly, within the margin of error.
It should be noted the chosen slope of the smartphone placed in the "cradle". This is exactly the angle at which the screen is located almost perpendicular to the view, and the fingers comfortably touch its surface. I appreciated this using my smartphone for application development, when the device should always be turned on next to the PC keyboard, and not hang around the table.
