Notion Ink Adam can be ordered again



Notion Ink has opened the free pre-order Adam android tablet. The first pre-order was in December, then all available copies of Pixel Qi screens snapped up almost instantly, and many interested users did not have time. Now comes the second: first, the invitation links were sent to those registered on the fan sites Adam, but now it has reached everyone else, and on a special page they collect addresses, which are then sent to passwords for the order. The expected shipment period of the ordered tablets is from February 25 to March 10, but it can still change. The price, as before, ranges from $ 375 to $ 550, depending on the model and $ 50 for shipping to anywhere in the world. You can only pay with Visa and AmEx cards, so far it has not been possible to agree with Mastercard. If you have any questions about the tablet and its acquisition, ask, I actively read the fan forum , so that I can answer some of them. Update: useful text in the comments .

So far, the tablets come to those who participated in the first pre-order. Judging by their reviews, everything is far from being as beautiful as it seemed before the release , but the problems are mostly soft and can be solved by updates (some of them were fixed a few days ago). Notion Ink also said they are already working on upgrading Adam to Android 3.0.
