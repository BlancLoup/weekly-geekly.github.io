How is the replenishment


  1. A client comes to a convenient VTB Bank office ;
  2. Reports the Yandex.Koshelka number and the amount of replenishment (no more than 15 tr.);
  3. Makes the sum (in rubles) and the commission for the transfer of funds (in UAH 1.5% of the amount of payment at the rate of NBU);
  4. Receives a document confirming payment acceptance;
  5. Funds are credited immediately after making the payment;
  6. Saves the document (confirming payment).

How to calculate the cost of replenishment


First, the fee for sending is not paid in rubles, but in UAH at the NBU rate. Secondly, it is possible to pay the replenishment amount in cash or hryvnia at the bank's selling rate (in fact, to buy rubles at the box office). Usually the ruble exchange rate at VTB Bank is adequate, but those who want to save money should look at courses at other banks . Say, with a replenishment amount of 15,000 rubles (this is the maximum) and a difference of 0.2 kopecks, it is worth buying cash rubles in another bank. The win will be 30 UAH.
