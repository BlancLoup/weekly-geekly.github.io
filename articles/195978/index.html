Ciklum Android Saturday gathers all interested in Android development in Vinnitsa

We hurry to please all adepts of the Android development of Vinnitsa.
On October 12, Ciklum Vinnitsa Android Saturday will be held at Ciklum for everyone!
Regular events called Ciklum Saturdays bring together specialists from various fields to share knowledge of ideas and professional networking in Kiev, Kharkov, Dnepropetrovsk, Vinnitsa, Donetsk, Odessa and Minsk.



As always, we have interesting speakers who will share their experiences with the participants:

1) Andrey Stakhov , coach, author and host of the blog android-helper.com.ua, Ciklum (Vinnitsa).
Topic: Where is the money in Android?
The report will be about where the money is in Android. Model building programs for successful earnings.

2) Kuzmik Artem , an active traveler who has been working on mobile development for about 4 years, Ciklum Android Team Leader (Kharkov).

Topic: Simplifying life with Gradle
Gradle is a build system that allows you to create and customize Android projects. Artem will tell you how to configure Gradle for your project in the IDE, on the CI server, how to solve issues of various assemblies with a few lines of code.

3) Andrei Kozak , developer Xamarin, Ciklum (Vinnitsa)
Topic: Automatic build projects in Ant and Xamarin development
Andrei will talk about Hamarin because he considers it a very convenient tool that helps us develop our applications quickly and efficiently. The report will present the Xamarin framework and demonstrate how to quickly develop multiplatform applications in C #.

Participation is free by prior registration goo.gl/XGU4m1
Start at 11:00
Venue: Vinnitsa, st. Cathedral 24, Sky Park, shopping center, 3rd floor, Ciklum office

We are waiting for you on Saturday!
