Popular sites Runet received special versions of Opera 10



November 5, 2009 - Oslo, Norway - Opera Software Company announced special versions of the Opera 10 desktop browser for a number of popular Russian Internet resources. Now users and readers of Auto.ru, ComputerBild, Habrahabra, KinoPoisk, Molotok.ru, OZON.ru and SuperJob got quick access to the most important sections of their favorite websites using the modern and powerful platform for working on the Internet - the browser Opera 10.

Each of the announced versions of Opera 10 provides users with one-click access to the most important and popular sections of websites with the help of predefined links in the Express panel, decorated in branding of the web project, as well as using bookmarks installed in a special version on default In addition, versions for some portals have a proprietary custom shell and pre-configured RSS feeds, as well as search resources added to the Quick Search list.

The development of special versions is a non-commercial project, the main purpose of which is to provide the latest Opera 10 browser for groups and user communities of interest. In the future, more in-depth detailing of special versions is planned in accordance with the feedback and wishes of users of Russian web resources.

Opera Software is very attentive to the requests of its users. The release of special versions was another step of the company in the direction of creating software products that best meet the needs and preferences of users. Worldwide, the desktop version of Opera is used by more than 45 million people, and Russia leads in this indicator. According to various independent estimates, the share of Opera desktop browsers in Russia is 23-27%.
