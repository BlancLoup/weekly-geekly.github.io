What mistakes you should try to avoid at the beginning

When I started this business quite by accident in April 2006 (by chance, because I saw the start of selling domains in the .eu zone and registered one for an offline business. Before that, I bought domains for 1 per year for my projects, not more) and I registered about 200 .eu domains, and after that another 300-400 .com during the week, I thought I had made millions automatically. And that my domains bestshoes.eu or youcars.com cost thousands and tens of thousands of dollars. As it turned out, only I thought so. :-) Subsequently, I simply did not extend most of these domains. But I learned my lesson.
  • At first, do not register many domains. No matter how confident you are in their value, in 99.9% of cases, they will cost you only for you, and those who are engaged in this business will not give for your domains and as much as you paid.
  • Register on specialized forums and actively consult with those who understand this.
  • Be sure to enter a budget for registration. (Expensive domains are not worth buying for the first six months - a year without the help of investment specialists in this area).

What should be done:
  1. Read a lot
  2. Study the market
  3. Think outside the box
  4. Search for generic domains (generic domains that make sense in sequential spelling, the domain must exactly repeat the word pronunciation sequence) for example: cars.com, exoticcars.com, usedcarsonline.com
  5. Get acquainted with people who have already reached the heights in this matter, and unobtrusively consult from time to time
