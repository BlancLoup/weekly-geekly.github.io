On games in alternate reality

ARG, or Alternative Reality Games - one of the surest ways to kill time and have fun, almost without getting up from the couch. Those who are tired of boring and uninteresting wallpaper games, are immersed in an alternate reality in search of untold treasures - by the way, really existing. Such games are a great advertising move to promote a movie, game or some boring math software.

Under the cut - the history of games in an alternate reality, their present and future. In addition, you expect a very small how-to, which will save you about ten minutes if you decide to promote your product in this way.

Where have you gone ...


The genre itself ARG was born only about ten years ago. The father of the industry can be considered the game The Beast, spinning around the movie Artificial Intelligence (AI, Artifical Intellegence).

... Microsoft's creative director Jordan Weisman and game designer Elan Lee came up with a detective story that was going to happen on hundreds of websites, using email, mobile communications, voice mail and fictitious advertisements.

What about the Americans?


The Americans in this regard, of course, were ahead - to take at least The Beast and three million members of Cloudmakers. Most of the popular games - Vanishing Point, Art of the Heist, I love bees - were quite successful and gathered a lot of people under their flags. So, in the above-mentioned game Vanishing Point, organized by Microsoft Corporation and dedicated to the release of Windows Vista, was attended by more than 70 thousand people. The vanishingpointgame.com page was viewed more than 20 million times, and the first person to solve all 48 puzzles went to space on a personal rocket.

Forward to the future


Of course, such games are an excellent advertising tool. A person who accidentally noticed something unusual, unwittingly becomes a participant in the game - and it is no longer so important whether he has experience of participation in such projects or not. Such games are becoming more and more inventive, the number of participants is increasing each time, and the effect after the completed puzzle is indescribable.

In addition, recently a person is increasingly using the Internet outside the home or office. All sorts of variations of portable communicators may well contribute to an increase in the number of games in an alternate reality. And the operational space is really extensive, especially in Russia.

Instead of conclusion


ARG is a new, powerful tool for social interaction. With proper distribution of funds you can get incredible results. Millions of people around the world solve such riddles, continually doubting the unreality of what is happening.

Finally, I note that ARG is not a game. Here, everything is for real - sites, phone numbers, places and sometimes even people. Participants do not know the rules - they are based on their own mistakes.
