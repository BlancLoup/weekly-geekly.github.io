Dropbox: auto upload photos and videos for everyone



The ability to automatically import images and videos from external media to Dropbox appeared a few weeks ago in beta. Today public version 1.4 has been released, including a new feature for everyone. Now Dropbox can automatically download content from all cameras, smartphones, tablets and SD cards.

In addition, this is a new opportunity to upgrade your account for another 3 GB! But more on that below.


The web version has a more convenient page for viewing photos - a gallery of large thumbnails, grouped by months:



When you first upload to Dropbox automatically, you will receive +500 MB of free space.
And even up to + 2.5 GB for downloading every 500 MB.

Download Dropbox 1.4

It is not yet known whether those who have already used the beta version will receive a bonus.
