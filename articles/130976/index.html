Skolkovo for a startup. Who can help and how to get?

We have drawn up a small mindmap about the procedure for obtaining the status of a participant in the Skolkovo project for IT projects.

I can not see anything here, so I give a link to the pdf and the mindmap version.
As well as links to additional materials:
Foresight on IT (Foresight in this case is like an order of the state for accelerated development of individual IT areas)
Investment Policy Skolkovo
Regulations on the receipt of the Participant of the Skolkovo project (it is better not to even open it - a boring document)

To collect these materials in the mindmap made the fact that everything on the Skolkovo website is too detailed and therefore nothing is clear from the first, and mindmap allows you to quickly get an understanding of the whole process.
For us, mindmap is still a working tool and there seems to be no errors in it =)

Now we are helping all the projects of the incubator , which have a scientific component, to prepare documents in Skolkovo and see that in general it is not difficult. Now we even help projects that are not in the incubator.

Key factors of getting into Skolkovo


  • the availability of the scientific component of the project (the task of Skolkovo is the commercialization of innovations)
  • potential success in overseas markets

The key that gives Skolkovo


  • tax reduction: no income tax, social taxes are reduced to 14%
  • there are no customs duties (if you need to bring servers for the project)
  • financing (grant or co-financing in grant form). Skolkovo does not climb into the capital of the company (!)

If the project has everything you need to get into Skolkovo and the project needs what Skolkovo gives, then getting status is not difficult. Success story . And according to our feelings, projects in Skolkovo are fair.
If there is absolutely no time for the preparation of applications, you can always find several professional teams that, for money / share, will help prepare and submit documents (without a guarantee of the result, of course).

I am publishing this material right now, because I heard that the main distribution of money in Skolkovo takes place at the end of the year, and the two remaining months are just the time period for which the whole procedure can be completed.
