We are pumping a DropBox account by mail in the .EDU zone in 15 minutes



Many of us have an account in the Dropbox service, many articles and life hacks have been written about this service, but today I want to show you how you can get the coveted e-mail in the .edu zone in 15 minutes.

In the first step, we will need a random data generator, since we will register with an educational institution so as not to arouse strong suspicions.

1) Go to the ru.fakenamegenerator.com page and click the Advanced link.


2) Set: Name of the set - American, Country - USA


3) Click to generate.
4) And we get our generated data.


Step 2. The received data will contain the SSN (Social Security Number) number, in order for the number to be correct, go to www.ssnregistry.org/validate and insert the number. After receiving a positive response, move on.



Step 3. Here we come to the organization that allows you to register email in the .edu zone.

Go to eid.k-state.edu and see the link Register now !, click, and go to the page where you need the same data from the first step.



Enter the data from the generator, and your e-mail, which will receive a service message. We continue.



We confirm.



Enter all other data. And we press to continue.



At this stage we are offered to choose your login. Choose from the presented, or enter your. Further.



Enter your password. OK.



We write for every fireman a secret question and answer for a password retry, in case of forgetfulness. Mobile is not needed. Further.



We confirm registration.



Is done. Again, go to eid.k-state.edu and enter the received login and password. Click Sign In.



Now we see the created profile, we need the link Set up e-mail forwarding.



Click and go to the page where you need to enter your working e-mail for forwarding university mail to yours. We write mail, we press add forward, and we see your created box in the edu zone, and that your mail has become attached.



We are asked to wait 15 minutes for the changes to take effect. We are waiting for 1-5-10 minutes. After that, copy the mailbox address, and go to the coveted www.dropbox.com/edu, enter the received e-mail, and wait for your letter to drop with a link to confirm.



We get. We turn.



We are happy !!!



In such a non-trivial way, it is possible to increase your place on DropBox. This box is no longer needed. You can forget it with calm. And most importantly, everything is legal. This topic presents one of the many sites that highlight an educational e-mail, just look.
