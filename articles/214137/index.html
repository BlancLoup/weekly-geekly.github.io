ThL T100S - smartphone based on 8-core processor MT6592

In November 2013, MediaTek officially unveiled the true eight-core processor MT6592, in which, according to the company, a balance was found between performance and power consumption. Most Octa Core devices use the big.LITTLE architecture, in which at any given moment either a block of productive or a block of energy efficient cores functions. MT6592, by contrast, can work in any configuration, including all eight simultaneously.



Devices operating on the MT6592, massively went on sale by the end of 2013. Now there are already enough smartphones on the market with this processor, but we decided to review the most popular and stylish among them. Meet the "iron man" - ThL T100s.
His first batch was only 2000 devices! Exit T100 in China caused such a huge stir, that the entire batch was bought out in just 9 minutes! The Ironman version already has our communication standards, GSM and WCDMA. New T100S - really except as a flagship, can not be called. This is one of the most productive Chinese devices to date. But first things first!

At the heart of the ThL T100S is an 8-core MT6592 processor with a frequency of 1.7 GHz, while the amount of RAM is 2 GB, and the internal one is 32 GB, and there is a slot for memory cards. The display has FHD resolution and is protected by third-generation Corning Gorilla Glass. In addition, the smartphone was equipped with 13 megapixel cameras from Sony, two SIM slots, a 2300 mAh battery, OTG and NFC functions, and Android 4.2.2 is responsible for the work.

‚ñå DESIGN, DIMENSIONS, CONTROL ELEMENTS


The body is made of polycarbonate, which is now popular. Such material allows to achieve maximum strength and solidity, but it is not prone to collecting scratches and prints. Monolithic glass is made flush with the other elements. Metal inserts are located above and below.



The front camera and voice speaker are traditionally located at the top of the front panel. In addition, there are placed sensors of lighting and distance. There is also an indicator that only flashes red when the battery is low and constantly on when charging. About calls and SMS alerts do not show.



Touch buttons without illumination are combined with the color of the smartphone. With excellent lighting, such buttons are easy to notice, but in low light only memory or a spear method will save. There is a backlight, vibration feedback too.



On the back are the camera's peephole, LED flash, speaker, and optional noise canceling microphone. The surface of the cover is slightly rough and velvety. The body is pleasant to the touch and almost does not get dirty fingerprints.



Just below there is a modest company logo.



External speaker at the bottom. He is one and small. The sound is loud and medium quality.



The upper end with a rough groove is occupied by a 3.5 mm headphone jack and a microUSB connector. The smartphone supports work with Flash-drives via USB-interface (OTG).



On the right is a lock button on the background of glossy plastic. To unlock, you do not need to shift the phone or drag it with your second hand - everything is clearly under your thumb.



If you look at the gadget in profile, you can see how its back case is gently bent. Accordingly, the smartphone lies in the hand, like a glove and does not even think to slip. At the bottom there is a recess for removing the back panel and the microphone hole.



It is worth noting the small thickness of the body and weight. And oddly enough, but I still consider design one of the main advantages of the device: it has already become almost at the level of well-known manufacturers, and even surpasses them somewhere.

‚ñå CAMERAS


The description states that the ThL T100S is equipped with a 13 megapixel rear camera and a front one also with 13 megapixels. The main camera is capable of taking pictures with a resolution of up to 4096x3072 pixels.



The autofocus system responds quickly to a change in the situation in the frame and allows you to shoot nearby objects. The focus area can be specified manually.



HDR support allows you to take pictures with an extended light range.



It is equipped with an LED flash, which copes well with its responsibilities in the evening, autofocus, has an aperture of f / 2.4, there is a face recognition function, as well as the ability to record video in FullHD format.



The front camera has similar characteristics, but as a rule it is used only for making video calls and communicating via Skype.





The cameras are pretty good quality. If you try and successfully pick up the angle and light, then it is quite possible to make a good shot.

‚ñå SOFTWARE PLATFORM


Smartphone ThL T100S running the operating system Android 4.2.2.



The appearance of the system is almost unchanged. Perhaps the only decoration is a translucent curtain of notifications and colored icons in the settings.



A set of applications can be called standard, there are main Google services, including the Play Store and other standard Android stock applications (audio and video players, calculator, clock, etc.). Of the extras added: a file manager, notes and a flashlight.



One of the main features is the availability of an NFC module and firmware for working with it.



As you can see, the interface here is naked without a shell from the manufacturer. Added some add-ons related to improving the sound (BesAudEnh) in the headphones and increasing the volume of the speakers (BesLoudness). ClearMotion allows you to make the image on the TV more smooth and clear. You can also calibrate the accelerometer and proximity sensor.

‚ñå WIRELESS NETWORKS


The smartphone supports dual SIM cards. Interface and SIM-card settings are standard for MTK chipsets. In the settings you can choose the main card for calls, SMS and mobile Internet. Also the color of the honeycomb is adjusted there. Formally, 3G supports two slots, but at the same time it can work only in one, the second one will always work in 2G only mode. To change the 3G-card, you do not need to change its position with a 2G-card, just change the item in the settings of the mobile network.



In addition to voice communications, the ThL T100S received support for wireless technologies such as Wi-Fi b / g / n, Bluetooth 4.0 and GPS. All communication technologies work well, at the level of analogs.



Unfortunately, there is no support for LTE, but not for long. MediaTek has already introduced its first 64-bit processor with an integrated LTE module - a single-chip system MT6732. Just when the coverage of 4G networks is already large enough, the processor will arrive in time.

‚ñå PRODUCTIVITY


The MT6592 has a 4-core Mali-450MP4 video accelerator with a frequency of up to 700 MHz, which is about 2 times more powerful than the Mali-400MP2 installed in MT6582. MT6592 is made in a modern 28nm process technology, integrated 8 core Cortex-A7 with a frequency of 1.7 GHz. Second-level L2 cache is increased to 1Mb, LDDR2 533MHz / DDR3 666MHz and 13 megapixel cameras are supported. Video recording can be made in 1080P 30fp formats, 1080P 30fps H.264 decoding, as well as in 2 new HEVC / H.265 and VP9 formats.



2 GB of RAM and 32 GB internal (about 22 GB available to the user).



As a result, the device turned out really the most powerful among fellow Chinese and some smartphones from A-brands. See for yourself by viewing the test results below.



On such hardware, the smartphone easily copes with high-quality 1080 / 50p video, including such popular formats as avi, mpeg and even mkv. In addition, most modern 3D games can do it, for example: GTA: San Andreas, Dead Trigger 2, Colin McRae Rally, Deus Ex: The Fall and LEGO City My City.











In normal operation, the device is smart, the interface works smoothly without any hangs. Also, there are no problems with work on the Internet, everything is fast and stable. If you need a fairly high performance and an inexpensive price tag for it, then the MTK6582 platform and its capabilities will suit you completely.
