Ainol Novo 7 Paladin - MIPS-tablet on Android 4 ICS

The ainol company made an interesting move towards getting rid of the domination of the ARM architecture in mobile devices - it began mass production of tablets using a MIPS processor.
So, the hero of our today's review, Ainol Novo 7 Paladin


And its characteristics:
Processor: Ingenic JZ4770 XBurst, 1 GHz; GPU: GC860
Operating system: Android 4.0.3
Memory: 512 MB / 8 GB
Screen: 7 inches, capacitive multitouch on 5 touches, 800 x 480
GPS: No
No camera
Bluetooth: No
WIFI: 802.11 b / g / n
HDMI: No
Battery: 4000mAh

This tablet is remarkable primarily for its price, which varies around $ 100. And of course, decent performance for the price.

For the sake of such a tasty price, the company decided to make the device as light as possible, which ended up left without BT, GPS, HDMI and a second USB. Well at least a slot for cards and WiFi left.
However, you can pay tribute to the engineers of the company - they did not save on the screen and the processor, having put, of course, not the perfect screen, but quite good - bright and with a capacitive sensor.
There is no Russian language in the official firmware, and there will not be - someone from the Russian market players signed an agreement with the manufacturer, so in the near future we can expect the appearance of this tablet in our stores under the brand, for example, Explay. Or DNS. :)

Specifications
Processor: Ingenic JZ4770 XBurst, 1 GHz; GPU: GC860

MIPS32 processor from the company Ingenic Semiconductor. Made on 65-nm technology, it includes one core with a frequency of 1 GHz, hardware decoder 1080P video in H.264, VC-1 and VP8 formats, and the Vivante GC860 graphics core operating at 440 MHz.
The processor is very energy efficient - only 0.25 W with 100% processor and video block load (1080p decoding)
Interestingly, the processor of the same series (JZ47xx), was used in the much-loved habravchanami Video-in-Print module in Vogue and Playboy.

Programs and work
A spoon of tar. Firstly, there is not 2.x, and not even 3.x. Some programs do not work well on ICS, for example, JustReader, I could not run the latest version of JustReader, it works fine.
Secondly, the modified architecture also imposes its limitations - only some applications can run on it, for others it is necessary to install MagicCode - a program that allows you to run applications written for ARM. Unfortunately, some programs can slow down and / or fail. In particular, Swype tossed over quite sluggishly, I had to return to the stock keyboard.
Also, normal applications for Root will not be able to help either, you will first need to install ClockworkMod Recovery , and then the Root_Fix package (you can get the Paladin_ROM_ROOT_Fix.zip file from here ). Russification is also easy - just follow the instructions .
W3bsit3-dns.com the user Teoret1k wrote a small list of programs and games that exactly work on the Russian firmware 4.0.3 with MagicCode.
In general, not everything is so scary. The device has attracted users, supplements are constantly being added to it, the list of applications that correctly work on ICS is constantly expanding.
