Will Google make cell phones free?

Google CEO Eric Schmidt (Eric Schmidt) is confident that many consumers will agree to get a mobile phone for free and receive ads in return.

For many users, this form of mutually beneficial cooperation with advertisers would be a way to save on the purchase of a mobile phone. According to Schmidt, phones are becoming more and more like handheld computers, and many users spend 8 to 10 hours a day talking, typing SMS, and mobile web surfing.

Now Google is experimenting with the delivery of text, image and video messages to mobile phones, according to CNN .
