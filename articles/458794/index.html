Mitap Business Analysts at Redmadrobot July 18



July 18 at 19:00 in the office of robots will be held the first mitap of business analysts. The speakers from Redmadrobot, DM-Solutions, SuperJob and KODE will talk about the subtleties of communicating with the customer, the real grocery work and creating your own product in an outsourcing company - all with real-life cases and examples. "The origin of its own product in an outsourcing company"
Dmitry Marchikhin, KODE


Everyone in the outsourcing development thought about creating their own product, but coordinating and selling the idea to the management, the internal customer is not an easy task.
A year and a half ago, we decided to take this step and were able to create Tortu.io , a voice interface design tool that is now used by VUI designers around the world.

In our example, I will tell:

  • how the idea to make your product appeared and how we substantiated the idea;
  • how the relationship with the internal customer was formed;
  • how to validate the idea with minimal resources;
  • and how our approaches can be used in working with external customers.
