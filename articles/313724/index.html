15 best Telegram bots for business



Telegram bots are increasingly entering our daily lives, their abilities are becoming diverse, and the functions they perform replace the usual "heavy" services. We have prepared a list of bots that we use ourselves - a lawyer bot, a HR bot, a call bot, and literally a few little helpers to Telegram. Full list below:

1) telegram.me/SkillangeBot is an indispensable assistant for the HR department or business owner. Helps to find a person with certain skills: programmers, designers, teachers, administrators, etc.

2) telegram.me/tviggo_bot - are you still paying for roaming? Then this bot is for you. He will teach your telegram to call and even give you a couple of interesting pieces: call anywhere in the world without roaming charges, a fixed fee per month, and even have the opportunity to call between messengers.

3) telegram.me/pravorubot - legal assistance in minutes. The bot will help to get answers to questions, and to find a specialist versed in jurisprudence.

4) telegram.me/cbr_bot - keeping track of the exchange rate is very difficult. This bot will help you to be always aware of the price of oil, gold and even Bitcoin.

5) telegram.me/egrul_bot - an extract from the Unified State Register of Legal Entities. Just send the bot INN or OGRN. Please note that the physical person (and IP) bot is not able to check yet.

6) telegram.me/mnp_bot - allows you to determine the mobile operator and region by phone number. Enter the number you need without any extra characters and spaces.

7) telegram.me/transferrobot - nowadays almost no one uses flash drives, everyone uses cloud storage. This bot is a confirmation. Post your file size <20mb or just add a bot to the group to upload all files to the cloud.

8) telegram.me/evernoterobot - Evernote presented its bot. Just connect your Evernote account with Telegram and send messages to the bot. All messages you send will be added to your notes.

9) telegram.me/topdf_bot - pdf - a universal format for storing information. This bot will help translate your text documents and photos into pdf.

10) telegram.me/multitran_bot - even in Africa, with this translator bot, you can feel relaxed, as the bot contains several dictionaries.

11) telegram.me/GetStatsBot is an equally important bot for those who conduct business on the Internet. This bot is always up to date with data from Google Analytics for your site. The bot will show the number of users, sessions for today, previous or for a week. Available as a digital display, and in the form of a graph.

12) telegram.me/expensebot - you can control what you can measure. That is why this bot will help you manage and track your daily expenses.

13) telegram.me/orfobot - business correspondence with errors is impossible. Therefore, if you doubt the writing, it is better to give this bot the text for review. And yes, this bot will not only not give you a deuce, but also fix all the errors for you.

14) telegram.me/do_itBot - it‚Äôs impossible to keep everything in memory. The Do it bot comes to the rescue, in which you can create a reminder, and the bot will notify you via a telegram client.

15) telegram.me/RubleRobot - can not make an important decision? And about the method of "flip a coin" heard? Add this bot and you will not face the problem of decision making.
