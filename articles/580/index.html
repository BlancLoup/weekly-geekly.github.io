Meebo prettier

Meebo is the best web-based instant messaging system. With it, you can go to all popular instant messaging networks without installing individual programs.

A couple of days ago, Meebo was seriously updated . The design was improved, it became possible to display chat windows in separate browser windows, it became possible to drag your contacts through the list, and other minor changes were made .

Meebo, first of all, cares about the convenience of users. There is no advertising in it, it can be used without registration, not to mention the fact that the system is absolutely free. In addition, thanks to the volunteers, Meebo is translated into many languages. And most importantly, the Meebo interface is very simple and convenient.
