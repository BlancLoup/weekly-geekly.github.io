Competition for web developers and web designers

In October 2011, VKontakte holds 2 competitions: a contest for web developers and a contest for web designers. Competition dates: from the 14th to the 28th of October. The announcement date is October 31st.

The prizes in both competitions are the same: for the first place - Macbook Air 13; for second and third place - iPhone 4S. Also, participants who took the first three places will receive an invitation to work in the VKontakte team.
