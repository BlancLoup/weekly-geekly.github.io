The line of "smart" watches Samsung Gear Live will be released on the platform Android Wear

Good afternoon, Habr!

Samsung has recently introduced Samsung Gear Live smart watches, which will go on sale this month and will be based on the Android Wear platform.

The Samsung Gear Live watch is a new gadget in the Samsung family of wearable devices, equipped with a 1.63-inch Super AMOLED display (320x320), a Snapdragon 400 quad-core processor (1.2 GHz), and 4 GB of internal memory + 512 MB of RAM.

The device is distinguished by easy setting of the dial design and simple activation mode: one touch of the screen - and the device is ready for operation. Samsung Gear Live have an IP67 protection level - they are not afraid of any dust or immersion in water. Included with the watch is a 22-mm bracelet, which the user, if desired, can be replaced by any other in Samsung branded stores. Finally, the novelty will allow consumers to monitor their health during fitness classes thanks to the built-in heart rate sensor, which is synchronized with various fitness applications.



Watch Samsung Gear Live on the Android Wear platform will allow on the go to follow the news and messages in real time. A wide selection of Android applications compatible with the device make it possible to synchronize and receive fresh notifications from social networks, news with photos, information about new collections and sales in the city‚Äôs stores. The new gadget also responds to voice commands, for example, by saying the intro phrase ‚ÄúOk, Google‚Äù, you can ask the question: what time will the next flight of the aircraft or who won the last World Cup match in football? In addition, you can set your watch task: call a taxi, send a message, book a table in a restaurant, set an alarm clock.



Samsung Gear Live watches can be paired with any Android gadgets equipped with Android 4.3 or higher. They will be available in Russia in July in all Samsung brand stores in black. On pricing information yet.
