MBLTdev 2017: amazing iOS track



We remind you that on October 27, the 4th annual conference of mobile developers MBLTdev 2017 will take place in the Digital October space.

Under the cut: descriptions of the reports of the iOS track, CodeLabs, a small survey and gifts from JetBrains, Ray Wenderlich and Skyeng.



Reports


Luke Parham, Fyusion
"Accelerating the work of iOS applications"
You will learn how to track the causes of the fall in the speed of the application and the mechanisms for its increase. In addition to working at Fyusion, Luke writes tutorials for raywenderlich.com .

Alex Kurailo, Agoda.com
"AR-reality"
Alex will demonstrate using Core Location to create AR interactions tied to real objects. We also learn about the use of technology to improve various areas of life: from retail to navigation in museums.

Makar Stetsenko, Silicon Valley Insight
"Jedi Path or REST to GraphQL"
The report contains the necessary minimum for working with GraphQL. Disassemble the myths associated with it and see how to start using it in native applications written in Swift and Kotlin.

Victor Starikov, VoltMobi
"Using Audio Unit Extension"
Audio Unit Extension is a technology that was introduced in iOS 9. It creates DSP modules that convert audio signals supported by various applications. For example, you can make a synthesizer, drum machine, or sound effect, and plug it into the Garage Band. At MBLTdev 2017, we will look at the capabilities of audio extensions, the way they are created and used.

Ilya Kulebyakin, e-Legion
"The Legacy Cookbook"
Let's talk about working with large mobile projects. What turns them into the so-called Legacy and what to do so that the development of such products brings pleasure.

Cyril Bessonov, Tinkoff
"The architecture of the mobile operator application"
Approaches to the formation of a team for new projects and features of the development of mobile only product. Kirill will talk about the difficulties of front interaction with backend systems and SIM cards for the Apple Watch, in wearable devices and for IoT.

Timur Nurov, Delivery Club, Mail.Ru
"70% of orders, but not Mobile-First"
iOS developer Timur Nurov and his colleague Nikita Kleschin, Android developer, will tell you how the development of a constantly evolving product for a mobile audience at Food Tech No. 1 in Russia is arranged. Scandals, intrigues, investigations.

Codelabs




For the first time this year at MBLTdev will be Codelabs! CocoaHeads team will conduct a training session for iOS developers. You will meet and integrate the best approaches of Swift, as well as take part in the Challenge to detect errors in the security system of the finished application. The fastest will receive prizes.

Tickets


Registration continues, but prices are rising. Now the ticket price is 7500 rubles. Already on October 2, the ticket price will be 9000 rubles. We also remind that the number of tickets this year is limited - only 600 pcs.

Presents


At MBLTdev 2017 you will find many surprises and activities. For the coolest questions, speakers will present books from Ray Wenderlich , annual subscriptions to JetBrains products, Skyeng lessons , cool T-shirts and many other gifts from conference partners.
