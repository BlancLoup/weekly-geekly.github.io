Relevance of education


I worked as a teacher, teacher, organizer, head of the scientific and technical department, and acting. Director in the House of Children's Art Oktyabrsky district of Yekaterinburg. I created a 3D modeling and 3D printing studio for children and teenagers, which was extremely high in demand, especially against the background of other associations - macrame, aeromodelling, etc. The video can be viewed below. In the course of the work, we had to face colossal difficulties of an administrative-bureaucratic nature. After analyzing them, I realized that the school system and the system of additional education are not just overloaded with bureaucracy, it is in a systemic crisis, which is caused by:

1. The isolation of teachers from the modern post-industrial, market and industrial economy. Teachers and teachers of schools are state employees who simply do not see, do not feel and do not know what kind of world they live in. These are people of a long gone socialist era who simply cannot teach children to live in the modern world.

2. The institute of education is turned off from the production process. Pupils today are simply overloaded with knowledge that is divorced from reality, which are not applicable and even harmful. The education system does not have the opportunity to get involved in the production economy - there is no legal basis for this, and if at least one school starts producing something, then the director will be charged with a criminal case. Today's obsession with the USE kills everything creative in children, or kills their future, if the USE does not pass.

3. There are no strong players interested in the education system. Earlier, graduates went to industrial enterprises, but now they are simply scattered in small businesses, serving nature, with fully atrophied brains. At best, they can open their own business. Only the most talented can rebel against their education and defeat him. Neither business nor state are interested in high-quality school education. Well, and parents? 4. Failure of continuity of generations. The last strongholds of engineering knowledge - Soviet engineers are aging and dying. Kalashnikov died today. Economic relevance

The world is now being completed, and according to some data, the process of globalization of the economy has completed. The old economic system and economic growth was based on the expansion of markets; there was struggle and war for these markets. Now that the world is global and markets can no longer expand, the economy is changing. Until now, demand has exceeded supply, and now supply exceeds demand in the global market. This means that the global market economy has come to an end. There are many implications of this. Let us dwell on those relating to Russia.
Russia sells oil and buys products, things, goods. As a result of falling oil prices, Russia will not be able to purchase these products. In this case, there are 2 ways - either die, or develop your own production and create your own domestic market. I see that a customs union is being created for this. So, Russia chooses the path of its own market. A huge advantage of Russia in relation to the developed countries is that the domestic markets in Russia are free for their own producer, and in developed countries they are already occupied, so they are waiting for a depression, but Russia is not. Russia is such a country, which seems to be technically developed, but somehow it does not want to, the crisis will enter an acute phase, oil will fall, and will immediately want.

The optimal scheme of work TsMIT, business plan

The business of creating a CMIT is a very bright, interesting thing. This business of the future is the production of innovation and work with young people. But even the brightest business stops or develops slowly, if interest is not supported financially. The question arises: how to make your TsMIT profitable business?
I developed and tested the organizational model of the work of the TsMIT. When using this model, it is guaranteed that you can reach a positive turnovers in the first 2-3 months of the center.
As an organization, TsMIT is a united center of three directions:
1. Execution of orders for 3D printing, robotics, automation and other applied engineering surveys;
2. Engineering school for children, adolescents and adults;
3. Mini-production, producing small series of products.

Education in TsMIT - subject. Children at the training stage are attached to engineering, learn to develop and design equipment, acquire entrepreneurial skills, thereby learning and preparing for future adulthood. But from the school of engineering a great benefit for other areas - the flow of ideas and the enthusiasm of children enormously helps in creating innovation.
Direction 1 (Prototyping Center) is the initial base of the TsMIT - it brings a small stable income. Direction 2 (School of Engineering) - generates income, but is mainly a factory of ideas for the production of innovations. Direction 3 - (Mini-production) is the main organizational and financial part. All 3 directions are divorced in time and space so that the safe operation of the center is ensured.
For those who create TsMIT according to such a model, or in some other way, there is always the possibility of receiving grants and other fundraising activities. In some cases, when the focus is on education, grants and fundraising can be a major source of income.

A sketch of a business plan might be:
1. Buying an average 3D printer
Costs -100 000 rubles

2. Implementation of orders for 3D printing (prototyping studio) and the sale of plastic
Costs: Advertising -1000 - 3000 rubles / month Materials -3000 12000 rubles / month Revenues: 15000 - 100 000 rubles / month

3. Conducting 3D modeling classes for children + teacher training and teacher employment
Costs: - 5 000 - 25 000 rubles / month (rent, salary) Income: 5 000 - 84 000 rubles / month

4. For participants, teachers, children, teenagers, there is an opportunity to produce small series of products - an innovative business.
No cost Profit unpredictable

My business went on payback, and then in a small plus already in 2-3 months of existence, there was no significant rent and advertising expenses, because the business is very mobile - I took the printer in my arms and went. The class room is taken by the hourly rental. Printing can be done right at home. Who cares, he can conduct a study of the demand for 3D printing in his city and district through Yandex. You can also see the market for additional commercial education in your city. If there is demand, go ahead.
