[HighLoad] Alexey Rybak: master class - Basics of building scalable high-load web projects June 10, 2012


Interview with a leading MC on DevConf2012 - Alexey Rybak (Badoo.com)
devconf.ru/offers/31

This is my hobby project somewhere since 2006, and I constantly complement it.
This is a very interesting experience, it is very different from what I acquire at work, so I will read until I get bored.

The basic approaches to the development of scalable applications have just been settled, some 10 years ago our industry did not really exist at all! We are still far from any development environments or frameworks that would give us all the necessary solutions out of the box. A number of technologies and tools are beginning to emerge, which are advancing precisely as a universal solution to scaling problems.

I consider it extremely important not only to discuss specific tools, but to form fundamental ideas about problems, genuine, not stereotyped knowledge, which would help engineers to have a more balanced approach to development and make decisions.

My master class is aimed at all developers who want to learn how large projects are made (Badoo now has more than 147 million users), what architectural techniques are used, what can be done and to be ready to be screwed to your projects.

I would like to wish the fighting mood that you would learn more not only interesting, but also useful, so that new contacts would be found, in general, that time would not be in vain.

And friends, remember that usually all the most interesting things are on the sidelines. Do not hesitate, catch the speakers of your interest and ask them more actively.

Good luck and see you at the conference!
