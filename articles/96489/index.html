Fable 3 - exact release date and new video


Microsoft announced the exact release date for Fable 3. The next creation by Peter Molyneux will be released on October 26, 2010. This was announced by Mr. Mulina himself at the Microsoft press conference devoted to the launch of the E3 exhibition.

With the advent of the new series, Fable will no longer be exclusive to the Xbox 360 console. Despite the fact that during the announcement last August last year, nothing like this was out of the question, in May 2010, the game developer Lionhead confirmed that that Fable 3 will be released on personal computers.

It is already known that the actions in Fable 3 will take place 50 years after the events of the second part. Players will manage the offspring of the former protagonist. Peter Mulina talked a little about the gameplay of the future hit: the system of experience accumulation and the usual graphical interface (health bar for example) will disappear from the game. The project manager also warned that Fable 3 would be rife with cruelty, violence and blood. However, you probably already heard about everything.

Note that the PC version of the new product is planned to be distributed through the Games for Windows Live digital distribution system. On store shelves, it will appear later.
