Seventh annual Microsoft Research Summer School. This time about machine learning and intelligence

Hello!

July 29, once again, for the seventh time, the annual Microsoft Research Summer School will open in St. Petersburg. This time the school‚Äôs theme is machine learning and intelligence . The school program includes lectures and seminars by world-class scientists from leading universities from around the world, including from Russia, as well as Microsoft Research researchers. The head of the school is Evelyne Viegas, director of the semantic computing department at Microsoft Research Redmond. Details under the cut.



Applications from students, graduate students and young scientists are accepted until March 31. You can submit them in the registration system .
Schooling is free, but you need to try to pass the selection, for foreign participants also organized free food and accommodation.

Microsoft summer schools have been held for more than 7 years in different cities of Russia. Schools have always been devoted to the key topics of Computer Science: HPC, computer vision, verification, large data processing algorithms, parallel computing, clouds. In 2014, more than 600 applications were received, of which 62 young scientists were selected (40% were students and 60% were graduate students). By the way, the fifth part - the representatives of the weaker sex. In general, more than 400 young scientists from around the world took part in Microsoft Summer Schools in Russia.

For all questions, you can write to Pavlova Elena , Microsoft Research Program Coordinator in Russia.
