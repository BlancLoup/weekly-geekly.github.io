Zonu.SU again want to "painlessly remove"

At the ICANN conference in Brazil in Sao Paulo, representatives of this corporation, as well as the largest providers and registrars, discussed ways to remove the best and most painless removal of the domain zones.

According to Cybersecurity.ru , domain zones, where there is not a single website, are theoretically subject to deletion , and there are also zones belonging to countries that no longer exist on a modern political map.

The first candidate for deletion was the .SU domain, in which open registration continues, and there are about 7,500 domains in operation. The question for ICANN representatives why such a decision was made remains unanswered.

Perhaps the Russian colleagues will be able to "pay off" and save the zone for some time. As you know, the clear leader in the number of registrations in the SU zone remains RU-CENTER , which accounts for over 87% of registrations .
