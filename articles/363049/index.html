Time to boycott Ubisoft games?


One of the smallest bugs of Assassin's Creed Unity (PC version): the main character has lost his face somewhere

In the ranks of gamers brewing discontent with Ubisoft. It is said that the level of discontent rolls over to such an extent that soon Ubisoft will be hated almost as much as EA, which twice won the competition for the title of America's worst company.

Last year was extremely unfortunate for Ubisoft. The company entered the shit at every opportunity : these are the "embellished" Watch Dogs demos , the rejection of female characters in Assassin's Creed Unity , the locking of Assassin's Creed Unity on frame 30 FPS allegedly for "cinematic effect" (!), The ban on publishing (negative) game reviews on the first day of sales, demonstrations of greed: selling in-game objects in Assassin's Creed Unity for up to $ 99 , etc. This is even if you do not remember the scandals of previous years .

Degree of criticism reached the boiling point with the release of Assassin's Creed Unity. A lot of technical problems, glitches and poor performance have outraged millions of players. The PC version is almost impossible to play .

According to the PC Gamer test results , even on advanced video cards like the Nvidia 670, the game does not always reach 30 fps (with the High graphics settings), and in some places it drops to 10 fps. They say that even the double Nvidia GTX 980 SLI game slows down and constantly bugs .
