Digest: ReactOS 4 months later [after release 0.3.16]

Good afternoon, Habrazhiteli!
4 months have passed since the release date of the latest release 0.3.16 and, it seems to me, it's time to share the current state of affairs in the project.

  • Now the last hours of the new attempt at crowdfunding - ReactOS Community Edition - are going . Collected 23 thousand 25 141 dollars or 873 277.66 rubles at the current rate , the money in any case go to the cashier of the project. According to the results of voting among the Beckers, it will be determined what they should be spent on. Links do not give the topic did not get into the hub "I am promoting"

  • ReactOS has its own full-fledged DOS emulator - NTVDM . The emulator can be run both in 32-bit and 64-bit environments and is based on the new implementation of the abandoned softx86 , as well as on the fast486 processor's own emulation library. Components were developed by TheFlash developer for about a year and were recently poured from a separate branch into a trunk. Video demonstration of work under the cut. Several harmful bugs, in particular, working with the palette has not yet been eliminated . Palette fixed.
