Mobile torrents: hit 2008

Download torrents on smartphones and PDAs can be one of the main trends in 2008, writes ZDNet . Indeed, the signs of an upcoming boom are already visible.

According to analysts, support for the BitTorrent protocol will almost certainly be built into the Google Android platform from the very beginning, and if not, independent developers will fill the gap very quickly.

The program WinMobile Torrent has already grown to the second version. A similar program Symbian Torrent is distributed in version 1.3. In this case, large peer-to-peer networks will be able to automatically form at train stations, universities, and other places where people gather.
