Idea: I write correctly

Hello!

A couple of days ago, a colleague marshalkin offered to share ideas of web services (I think that the word startup can be pronounced when a couple of investment rounds have passed, and not even when there is no working prototype) with which you can earn and set an example for us.

I will support his introduction and here is my idea:
Web service for checking and correcting texts: spelling, punctuation and style

Whence it went : in Russian in school 3- . I understand that this "not kosher", but it's too late. Sometimes I resort to the help of competent friends. Those. I throw off a piece of text on ICQ with the note "check pliz, but it is embarrassing to send :-)".

What I want : I have my ID, which I throw $$$. I load the client (Adobe AIR, Exe, Web-client, plugin for Miranda and Qip), in short, something like ICQ. There I throw a piece of text, after a while (depending on the volume) I get the correct text.

How it works : There are girls-proofreaders, 5-6 pieces (to cover all the time zones of the Motherland), who have an advanced client. the text comes to them, they correct it and send it back. for the amount of text they receive their $$$, which respectively will be deducted from your account. Because the style needs longer to be corrected, up to a complete correspondence of the entire text, then the price is 3-5 times more than for simple spelling and punctuation.

What are the competitors : Now most programs have spellcheckers (spelling and punctuation). MS Word, FireFox. In MacOS, it will soon be right out of the box to work in all programs. But the Russian language is so powerful that I am not sure that they put the commas correctly. Not to mention the style.

Add-ons : connect translators. Those. when you need to competently (stylistically) translate.

Audience : illiterate population + doubters.

Anonymity ensures that only the user ID is used in the system and the corrector operator only sees it.
