Mobile gaming market: $ 28.9 billion in 2016


Juniper Research has prepared a report with forecasts of the development of the mobile market, and a squeeze of the most interesting is offered to us by venturebeat.com.

According to the report, the mobile gaming market will grow to USD 28.9 billion in 2016, which is 38% more than the industry should bring this year (USD 20.9 billion). Thus, the gigantic mobile gaming market is not going to slow down, and by 2019, the speakers promise an increase to USD 39.6 billion. The main drivers of growth will be the countries of the Far East and China, reaching a total share of 57%.

Major players in the mobile market, such as King and Supercell, rely on attracting players to their products to maintain their income. This strategy is very wasteful, and Juniper assumes that there has been a general trend in the strategy, where the analysis of product performance is at the forefront, which in turn allows you to raise LTV and thereby significantly increase your ROI.

Juniper also points out that tablet users spend more money on games, justifying this by saying that there are many serious games on tablets that attract hardcore users from other platforms, but Juniper notices that this will not prevent the mobile console from having niches and tablets.
