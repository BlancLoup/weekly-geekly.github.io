The fact is that individual solar cells convert light into electricity slightly better than others, absorbing photons in a wider range of the spectrum. This is due to pseudo-random patterns in the surface texture of ‚Äúultra-efficient‚Äù cells, as compared to completely random patterns in other cells (while cells with random patterns work better than photo cells with a periodic clear texture).
