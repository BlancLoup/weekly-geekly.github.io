Perhaps the strangest accessory. HTC launches bluetooth smartphone phone

Suppose you have a wonderful Android smartphone. Powerful, brick with a large display, functional.
And surely you use it as a navigator, player, device for accessing the Internet, a video player, a device for checking mail, a coffee grinder, a flashlight ... But what else can there be today a smartphone, and even more so on a flexible open source platform and the possibility of writing applications.
But a smartphone is primarily a telephone. And to see, in fact, the phone in these monsters every month becomes more difficult. In the NTS on this, too, thought. Thought hard. And they showed the world the HTC Mini (not to be confused with the HTC HD Mini) - in fact, a wireless headset, made in a simple mobile phone form factor.


The functionality, as expected, is simple: the device has a monochrome display, and communicates with the base phone via bluetooth or NFC and can do the following:
- Directly, dialing numbers (including from the phone book) and receiving calls
-Write and receive text messages
- Call log
-The calendar
-Display notifications
-And can also be used as a remote control when a smartphone is connected to a TV
Announced the device along with the five-inch HTC Butterfly, and apparently designed on the basis of its specifics, although it can be compatible with other smartphones.
At first, the device will be available exclusively in China, the price is not called
