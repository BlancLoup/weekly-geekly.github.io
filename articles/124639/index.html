Invitation to Chaos Constructions 2011

On the last weekend of summer, on August 27 and 28, 2011, the computer art festival Chaos Constructions 2011 will be held in St. Petersburg

There has already been a competition for the best invitation to the festival.
The prize was won by the Quite group with chaos constructions 2011 invitation .

Invitation takes only 64 kb, you can download from the site Pouet


The text of the official announcement

The festival will reopen its doors to programmers, administrators, artists, designers, musicians, hackers, and anyone interested in computer technology. Every year the scope of the festival expands. To date, the program Chaos Constructions has several dozen disciplines for a variety of platforms. The main areas of competition: demo, intro, tracker music, drawn and rendered graphics and more. In addition to traditional disciplines, the works for which are prepared in advance, the festival hosts many Realtime contests that are held directly on the territory of the event. Subjects of competitions: graphic design, music and the creation of special effects.

Also in the festival program: interactive Hackquest - competition for security systems hackers, seminars, an exhibition of computer equipment and electronics of the past and the present, an exciting game zone and much more.

The festival originates from the distant 1995, when in Russia only events of the ‚Äúdemoparty‚Äù format began to appear. The team of organizers of Chaos Constructions consists of experienced and well-known representatives of the Russian demoscene, who previously participated in the ENLiGHT festival.

The organizers set themselves the task of giving Chaos Constructions visitors and participants a unique atmosphere of informal communication, new discoveries, vivid impressions and amazing images created by talented digital artists.
