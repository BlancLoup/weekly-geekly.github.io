11. Work with hotmail account only.

When I first started, Windows asked me for a Live ID account and happily consumed my home MS account on gmail's mail. But the happiness was not long, when trying to download something from the store, they demanded that I log in again. And here came a surprise - it turns out that the windshield does not allow working with the marketplace with a mail-based account different from hotmail.

And the second surprise was that it was no longer possible to re-register from another account. By this time, the device has already been configured - and the only way out is a complete reset of the shovel.

But I struggled, I went (from the computer) to the settings of the Live ID account and created an alternative name for my ID using Hotmail. Then I tried authorization again. Nifiga

As a result, if you want to use the Live ID account, which is sent to a mail other than hotmail, follow the following instructions:
  1. Go to the Live ID settings from your computer
  2. Associate with your hotmail account mail
  3. And only after that start the first launch of the device.

It works only in this order. 3,1,2,3 - does not work, you have to do a full reset.
