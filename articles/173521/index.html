New design Python.org

The beta version of the new site Python.org - preview.python.org was presented at PyCon 2013 in Santa Clara (USA).
The new adaptive version of the site looks nicer and more modern, navigation through the site has become more convenient and understandable.

The last time the site design was updated in 2006. Although, if you look deeper , the site structure has not changed since the late 90s.

The new site is made on the Django 1.5 platform (Python 3.3)

The administration asks those who wish to participate in the beta testing of the site and report problems or constructive suggestions via the back link form .
