Automatic feeder Maitre D'og



If you spend days and nights at work, then your cat is most likely unhappy. Moreover, it is not so much your own absence that afflicts him (cats are creatures independent), as well as the irregularity of feeding. To restore the spiritual balance of the caudate, the Maitre D'og automatic feeding trough designed by Andrew Au has been designed. The device can be programmed and then at a certain time it gives a sound signal (for example, the sound of an opening refrigerator), after which a strictly measured portion of food falls into the bowl. The animal is full, and the owner, having left the office, can without remorse of conscience afford a couple of glasses of beer.
