From hyperconvergence to properly prepared Wi-Fi: the summer series of HPE webinars

On the first day of summer, we are starting a new series of our free webinars. In June and July, we offer you a detailed overview of several technologies and solutions: hyperconvergence (including the new HPE SimpliVity 380 platform), data center infrastructure components, updates in our storage portfolio, non-volatile memory (NVMe), and HPE Aruba network solutions. Registration for webinars - under the cut.



1. Containers, virtualization, servers - how to manage it? Webinar June 1.
Today, more and more companies are switching to the use of a mixed infrastructure, in which some applications run on physical servers, some in virtual machines, and some use containers. How to manage all this diversity, to get real benefits and not dig in various technologies? In this webinar, we will talk about HPE's experience and expertise in this area.

2. Racks and power infrastructure from HPE - where the IT infrastructure begins. Webinar June 7th.
Racks with the ability to install access control systems, "smart" power distribution modules, uninterruptible power supply devices, in which the batteries are replaced for free during the entire period of support. All this is an updated portfolio of equipment for the data center engineering infrastructure from HPE. We will tell on the webinar about safe, practical and effective updates.

3. HPE SimpliVity 380 is the most complete hyperconvergent platform on the market. Webinar June 8th.
If you are looking for a simple solution that will cover your IT infrastructure requirements in computing resources, fault-tolerant storage, data backup, optimization of the data transfer channel between sites, then HPE SimpliVity 380 is the solution for you. At the webinar we will examine the technical capabilities and applicability of these hyperconvergent solutions, which, according to some analysts, are the most advanced and complete on the market at the moment.

4. Anatomy of HPE Hyperconvergence: Embedding in PMS, Branches, VDI and Design. Webinar June 14th.
We will continue the topic of hyperconvergent solutions at the webinar on specific applications of HPE HyperConverged and SimpliVity systems based on practical experience gained with our partners and customers in Russia and the world. Among the examples we will examine: complete migration of the customer's IT environment to the hyperconvergent environment; equipping the branch network of a large federal enterprise with standard scalable systems; linking the distributed branch network with slow data transmission channels in a single disaster-resistant system; solution for the organization of virtual workplace infrastructure (VDI) "out of the box."

5. The fastest entry level array gets even faster! Webinar June 15th.
Entry-level does not necessarily mean "slow" or "inefficient." At the webinar, we will look at the possibilities of the new generation of the most successful HPE data storage system for small and medium-sized businesses - HPE MSA .

6. How to extend the life of everything (not) necessary with the HPE Archive Manager. Webinar June 20th.
At the webinar, you will learn how to use traditional LTO tapes to create convenient, capacious and easy-to-use archives, how transparently for users to transfer files from expensive disk storage systems to them and thereby reduce storage costs.

9. Non-volatile memory as the next stage in the development of data centers. Webinar July 5th.
NVMe over Fiber Channel offers the performance and reliability of Fiber Channel transport, as well as the ability to simultaneously run FCP and FC-NVMe protocols in the same infrastructure.
