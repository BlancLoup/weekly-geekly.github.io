June 30th is the last day of HP ProductBulletin

Dear friends!

Everyone who is connected with information technology, in one way or another, came across HP equipment that raised technical issues. HP ProductBulletin, an electronic technical reference for all Hewlett-Packard hardware, helped you with this and the other.

I inform you that Hewlett-Packard is no longer supporting HP ProductBulletin and the last day of the tool‚Äôs work on June 30, it will be replaced by a new, more modern and simple HP QuickSpecs tool.





Download the latest version of the HP QuickSpecs Offline application, you can follow the link - http://h71069.www7.hp.com/quickspecs-offline/setup.exe

When you first start HP, QuickSpecs will ask you to choose which directory to download. You only need to select Worldwide Quickspecs, there is no additional information in EMEA. After that, HP QuickSpecs will independently download from the cloud all current information to date.

Everyone enjoy using the utility, personally I have already installed and enjoy the new pleasant interface of the program. I found a lot of information in it on server hardware hp and workstations hp
