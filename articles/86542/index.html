NVIDIA drivers disable video cards

The release of the new version of drivers for NVIDIA video adapters (version 196.75) may lead to damage to the video card. Reports about it ZDNet with reference to the manufacturer. It turns out that the drivers released on March 2 lead to unstable operation of the cooling system, which is expressed in slowing down the speed of rotation of the cooler or stopping it completely. Vendor recommends uninstalling the driver and rolling back to the previous version 196.21.
