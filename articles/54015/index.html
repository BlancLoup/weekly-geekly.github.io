"Keys" on 8 GB

LaCie showed off several new storage devices developed by 5.5 Designers. Among them are iamaKey , itsaKey and PassKey . The names are dumb (at least the first two), but interesting devices hide under them.



iamaKey and itsaKey are USB-flash drives, made in the form of ordinary keys: metal, waterproof and scratch-resistant. Volume - from 4 to 8 GB. Data transfer rate is up to 30 MB per second. The write speed is 10 MB per second.


Another device - PassKey - is not a flash drive, but a microSD / HC card reader.


The cost of 4-gigabyte iamaKey will be $ 18. 8-gigabyte - $ 28. ItsaKey of the same volume will cost $ 15 and $ 24, respectively. You can buy a stylish PassKey card reader for 10-ku.
