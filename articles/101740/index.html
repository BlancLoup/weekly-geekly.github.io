Contest of articles on computer (overclocking) topics from Gigabyte and XtremeLabs.org

I think most of us have a very positive attitude to the possibility of freezing or for small services to receive money, a valuable prize, encouragement, etc. The desire to work on an interesting matter a day or two is mainly among the young (soul) and enterprising people. I once belonged to such people, taking part in various article contests on overclockers.ru, 3dnews.ru, modlabs.net. Today we have to play for the other side - for the organizers of copyright competitions.
All advanced PC users with literary talent, overclockers, enthusiasts who use their own computers outside the box are dedicated to: Gigabyte and XtremeLabs.org articles competition.

Gigabyte Technology Co. Ltd and the XtremeLabs.org Extreme Labs project announce a competition of articles on Gigabyte products that will run from August to November 2010. The purpose of the meeting is to increase the awareness of Gigabyte products, to reveal the nuances of its use. Welcome materials on overclocking, modifications, extreme boost of PC components.
Anyone can take part in the event, regardless of the country of residence - it is enough to write material about the motherboard or video card produced by the organizer and send it via special address. If you want to share thematic information, but are not ready for the painful author's attempts - post a short report on the pages of Extreme forums!

The first stage of the competition will last from August 13 to September 12, 2010, the second - from September 13 to October 12, 2010.



The winners of each stage, authors of materials published on the site, authors of the best forum entries will receive valuable prizes: Gigabyte GA-X58A-UD3R (LGA1366) board, Gigabyte GV-R567OC-1GI video card (Radeon HD 5670 1 GB), backpacks, numerous souvenirs.

I hope that many of the local inhabitants will be interested in the described event, and some will also be encouraged to write an article and win a valuable prize. Good luck!
