New update for Nokia X2 has been released

Good news for the owners of Nokia X2 - last week began its distribution next update under the number 2.1.0.11.



There are a lot of pleasant changes: a new camera application, an updated app store, new personalization options and ... wait for it ... even support for Gmail (mail and contacts). But about everything in order.
