Vibrating Insoles - wearable technology that you did not expect. (concept)

How do you get the idea: make insoles that vibrate to tell you where you should go?

This is exactly the idea that came up with the employee Dari Dent ( Dhairya Dand ).
SuperShoes are insoles that connect to your smartphone, and unobtrusive vibrations tell you where to turn. For GPS navigation, this is a worthy substitute for voice alerts, or constant glancing at the phone screen.


The device is terribly simple, several components are built into the silicone insole:
  • Capacitive plate - so that the gadget knew when you take a step
  • Three vibrator motors - so that you will be informative and pleasant
  • Battery
  • Bluetooth module
  • Microcontroller



All this happiness through a mobile phone connects to the cloud service ShoeCentral. The cloud can no longer simply indicate your direction, but advise you to go where you have not been before, based on your preferences.

But, unfortunately, this is only a concept, but for now we will wait for the gloves, which will tell you when to hug the girl better .-)
