EeePC is the perfect gadget for bloggers

Hardly anyone from my audience has heard of the new device from Asus - EeePC. For technical specifications please come here , but for now we will talk to whom and why Eee may be needed. The main complaints that I heard in his address are a rather weak processor (by the standards of 2008), a small RAM, and just a ridiculous 800x480 screen resolution. IMHO all those who say this simply do not correctly position the new device. No need to treat it like a laptop and everything will fall into place. It's just a gadget for networking and with documents whose dimensions allow you to carry it with you just everywhere.
