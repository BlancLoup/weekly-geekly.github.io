Bookmix: New Book Mix

Today I learned about the new book network - Bookmix .

I decided to try, everything new attracts me, and besides, I have not yet found a service that would satisfy me completely. I have tried many, but something is missing everywhere ... ( In my blog, I have already written about LiveLib book networks, My Library, Books in my hands, and Bookmate search engine. )


The main page briefly tells about the project, here the latest reviews, popular books, new arrivals, latest news and categories with genres are neatly displayed. On the right - personal account, search, rating of participants, tags and sales hits.

Registration is standard - we enter the required e-mail (it will be a login), first name, last name, gender, date of birth and password; Those who wish can immediately specify contact information (icq, skype and phone), upload an avatar, briefly tell about themselves.
