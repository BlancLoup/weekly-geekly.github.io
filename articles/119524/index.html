OrenConf Developer Conference

Dear Colleagues!

We invite everyone to attend the OrenConf developers conference in Orenburg, organized by Softline.

Participation in the conference is free! It is necessary to go through the registration procedure on the site, as the number of participants is limited.

The conference will be held on May 28 at 12:00 in the building number 3 of Orenburg State University .

Details of the venue will be communicated a few days before the conference after the selection of the premises according to the number of registered participants.

Reports are planned in three sections:
- php
- java
- Layout, testing, management

The list of reports and speakers is still being formed. Do you have a desire to speak? Write me a message or leave contacts on our site.

Detailed program and registration: orenconf.ru
The official hashtag in twitter: #OrenConf

See you soon at the conference!
