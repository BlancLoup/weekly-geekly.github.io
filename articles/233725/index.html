Strange, but the accelerometer does not work in the tablet from birth. Despite the fact that the auto-rotate screen works, some (all?) Applications do not see the accelerometer. I read somewhere that for an auto-turn, eastern friends sometimes put something like a pendulum. I do not know the reason or not, do not want to dig. Because of this, and also because I already dealt with FreeTrack , I used it. Also a nice bonus is that this software is natively supported in my car simulator.
FreeTrack determines the position of the head by three points. To work, you need a web camera aimed at three contrasting markers: for example, LEDs nailed to the head. As a more humane option - to the cap. In the normal (average) position of the head markers should not be on the same vertical plane (in this place in this implementation of points I sfilil). It is better to use LEDs as markers, but you can also use reflectors with external illumination. I used infrared LEDs. To power the LEDs, we had to modify the microUSB-OTG cable. For this, two additional power wires were additionally removed from the cable, to which three LEDs are connected in series through a current-limiting resistor. According to the idea, the cable functionality should have been saved.
We also had to modify the camera by removing the IR filter from it. After that, it is desirable to use a visible spectrum filter on the camera. In this capacity, the fully illuminated color works remarkably well (I did not get the trick from a black-and-white one) film if someone else remembers how it looks.
