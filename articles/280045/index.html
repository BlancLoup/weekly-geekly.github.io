Yota application for Windows Phone - from Novosibirsk to St. Petersburg



As it is known to owners of smartphones on Windows Phone, until recently Yota did not have its own client based on this OS. We will not hide, initially we planned to launch a mobile application on three platforms. This turned out to be quite laborious, and in the process, unfortunately, I had to sacrifice one of the versions. As you might guess, this was a mobile client under WP. And since for the owners of SIM cards Yota the application is the main tool for managing services, now it's a holiday on the street for the owners of Windows Phone - our official application has been released. And the most surprising thing is that two programmers from Novosibirsk, who once released an unofficial client for Yota subscribers, became its authors.

Although the proportion of Windows Phone users is relatively small, including in absolute terms, since the launch we have constantly received many questions why we do not support this system. We have really been carrying out plans to develop a client for this platform for a long time, since it is important for us to provide the opportunity to conveniently manage our services to all or almost all owners of smartphones. In addition, the availability of a Windows Phone application would allow our customers to freely migrate from one OS to another.

For a long time, for various reasons, we did not have the opportunity to make an application for Windows Phone on our own. At some point, the developers from Novosibirsk contacted us with the story that they won the hackathon with the application prototype, the guys asked for our API and data access parameters. Initially, we regarded this as an experiment - thanks to young talents, an unofficial client could appear in the market, and our users with Windows Phone-smartphones would be able to see information about the balance, the balance of minutes, etc. In the end, we offered the guys cooperation. And you know what? They agreed, and now they are in our team.

And now let us give the floor to them.

Yota on Windows Phone. Now - officially


Our new mobile application - already official - was created from scratch. When developing it, we, of course, focused on the functionality of versions for Android and iOS. In the official version of the application for Windows Phone, you can contact support, change the number, bind a bank card. New customers will also be able to take advantage of our current promotions and get the first month for free .



However, the novelty can boast of innovations that are not yet available on other platforms. This is primarily about the component, which made it possible to transfer most of the logic of interaction with the Genesys support chat server into a separate SDK. The component is cross-platform and will be used on iOS and Android in the future. The second important innovation is the transition to a new concept of WebAPI, which allows to simplify the logic on the client and reduce the cost of testing. I would also like to note that the mobile application uses the ‚Äútrick‚Äù of the Windows platform - live tile, which we hope will settle on the desktops of all users of the application :) When creating the interface, we focused not only on corporate identity, but also on Microsoft recommendations.
