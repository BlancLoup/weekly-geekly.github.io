Wireless USB. Four hundred for convenience

USB - a thing, no matter how cool, useful and convenient. One only upsets - you have to constantly deal with the wires. Everyone, most likely, from the port on the back wall of the system unit (if it is not placed on the front panel) is the cord with the adapter pulled?

The current state of affairs can be corrected with the help of the Wireless USB 2.0 Extender device developed by Gefen .


Wireless USB 2.0 Extender consists of two modules. A transmitter that connects to the PC's USB port, and a receiver for connecting to an external USB device. Data transfer between modules at a speed of 70 Mbps is carried out over a wireless connection standard UWB (Ultra Wide Band). The zone of reliable signal reception is 18 meters, which is even better than with most Bluetooth transmitters. The device weighs a little (680 grams for two modules), and the dimensions are not bulky at all (transmitter - 3.55 √ó 1.27 √ó 8.63 cm; receiver - 11.68 √ó 2.54 √ó 10.66 cm).
