12 best overseas developer conferences in 2016

New Year is near and it's time to schedule participation in interesting conferences. Colleagues from Mail.ru have been sharing a review of Russian events for several years in a row (here is a list for 2015 , waiting for a similar review for 2016), and I decided to go overseas.



Why import conferences? The Russian ones are also very interesting, but sometimes you want to combine business with pleasure: not only to listen to smart people and establish new connections in the US and Western European markets that are relevant for us, but also to see some pleasant city.

Criterias of choice:

  • Conference language: English.
  • Location: Europe and the USA.
  • We celebrate local events that only local students attend.
  • Subjects: Development, testing, management, Web, .NET, Java, LAMP, mobile development.
  • Speech: if there is a real opportunity to speak at the conference - this is a big plus. If the conference is held in a non-English speaking country (say, in Sweden or the Netherlands), this is a plus +, because the roughness of Russian English will be less prevalent over the speaker.
