The terms of the MSDN Academic Alliance program have changed since January 16

Since January 16, the terms of the MSDN Academic Alliance program have changed.

Now the subscription will be called DreamSpark and will exist in two editions:
- DreamSpark (analog MSDN AA Designer);
- DreamSpark Premium (similar to MSDN AA Developer).


Full comparison: https://www.dreamspark.com/Institution/CompareSubscription.aspx

Purchasing options in physical form (DVD) or electronic (Electronic), for 1 year or 3 years, are also saved:
  • DreamSpark Electronic Software Delivery (1 year)
  • DreamSpark Electronic Software Delivery (3 years)
  • DreamSpark Electronic Software Delivery + DVD (1 year)
  • DreamSpark Electronic Software Delivery + DVD (3 years)
  • DreamSpark Premium Electronic Software Delivery (1 year)
  • DreamSpark Premium Electronic Software Delivery (3 years)
  • DreamSpark Premium Electronic Software Delivery + DVD (1 year)
  • DreamSpark Premium Electronic Software Delivery + DVD (3 years)
