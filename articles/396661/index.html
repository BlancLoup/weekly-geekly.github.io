Overview of the USB-Lightning Transcend JetDrive Go 300K flash drive

Hi, GeekTimes! How many times has the question of the need for memory expansion for Apple devices? External hard drives helped more than one thousand iMac and MacBook users, and iPhone and iPad users continue their ordeals. Traditionally, Transcend showed interest in this niche, and once again pleased with the new product - JetDrive Go 300K. Lightning + USB 3.1 - nowhere fresh that in performance and ease of use? At the same time, I already had a flash drive from Leef - Bridge for 16 gigabytes - was it not wasted money? Read under the cut.



For the millionth time I will write a commonplace: ‚Äúthe theater begins with a hanger, and the product begins with the packaging‚Äù. The product from Transcend stands out from the series of simple plastic blisters - cardboard packaging with a transparent window, in which the flash drive is visible. I will not paint what exactly is put on the cardboard - buy and read it yourself. On the back of the box there is a QR code - scanned - a page was opened in the AppStore - downloaded proprietary software. Without it, nothing will work. However, more on that below.



The main thing that interests the buyer is the volume and speed (it is declared to be read at 130 MB / s).

You can not just take and start using the drive JetDrive. The official desktop utility was downloaded from the official site (in my case, MacBook Pro Retina 15 mid2012 with installed for tests via BootCamp Windows 10 Pro).

Welcome window. No functionality, just information.



The second tab - Backup - you can see what backups are on the flash drive (I did not have time to do any more).



Recover - bookmark for data recovery from backups. Camera archive is perhaps the most popular type of content that passes through these items.



Formatting (under the mysterious point Optimized Format hides the prosaic FAT32, which with the same agility will correctly perceive both Mac OS X and Windows). I chose the disk, set the volume label, pressed Format - the output drive is ready for use.



Cognitive information: inside the controller SiliconMotion SM3269, which can work with Lightning, by the way, his brother SM3268, is used with might and main in double-sided flash drives with USB Type-C connector. Samsung's memory is also the freshest, made on 16 nm process technology.



Standby comparison reference test - Crystal Disk Mark. When reading a record of small blocks, sadness-sadness occurs in both drives, but linear read / write is much better given to a new toy.



What is used flash drive in the first place? Correct for copying files. Therefore, the main test will be copying multiple movies to a USB flash drive. It does not make sense to use a large BDRip for verification purposes, because the purpose of the Transcend JetDrive Go 300 (as well as any other flash drive) is to bring along more content.

To measure the time, the utility TeraCopy was used, it was used to measure the time of copying from the working SSD to the cinema drive about robots (part 4) in fairly good quality. File size - 3 117 881 344 bytes, about 2.9 GB.


For a clear advantage Transcend won (now it became clear why I bought a new flash drive).

Mobile app


To use the drive on your phone, player or tablet, you need to install the Transcend JetDrive Go utility. This can be done either by scanning the QR code on the package, I mentioned it at the beginning, or by finding the application in the AppStore.



The functionality is quite diverse: you can open files from a flash drive, including playing video. Here it should be noted that the 2.9 gig rip was read without any problems, but there were brakes with the BDRip. On the other hand, why on the BDRip phone 12 or more gigs?
