Olympic widgets for your site: live broadcast from social media

Follow in real time the development of events anywhere in the world? Really using themed social media feeds. For the upcoming "event of the year" - the Olympics in Sochi - we have prepared a special widget - photos, videos and text messages from social network users about the Olympic Games in real time in one tape.



The tape contains the most interesting, relevant (real time) and popular content of social networks - posts, photos, videos about the 2014 Olympics. Share with your visitors the most recent and commented posts, keep up to date and support ours!

What's in the widget?

Widget reflects 3 types of social media content:
  • Posts from Twitter, Vkontakte, Facebook, LJ and other social networks
  • YouTube videos
  • Instagram photo

The tape is updated in real time. Messages, photos and video content of social media users about the Games can be sorted in the widget by popularity (number of comments) or date. For each message, the source, author, time of publication and number of comments are indicated.

The flow of public Russian-language content in social media has already exceeded 20 million messages per day, hundreds of thousands of them are devoted to the upcoming Olympics. How is the widget tape formed?

The tape is formed using full-text search for publications of social media users:
  • from 20 million daily published messages, by keywords (Olympic Games, Sochi 2014, hashtags, URLs of sites, etc.) there are reports of events related to the Olympic Games;
  • in just one day, several tens of thousands of Russian-language messages pass per day on the topic of Sochi 2014,
  • spam bots are automatically detected and filtered,
  • Every day the flow is growing: the day before yesterday 33 thousand, yesterday 43 thousand, today 58 thousand,
  • since there are so many messages so that the tape is readable, no more than 15 messages per minute are randomly selected for the widget,
  • implemented mat filter and obscene expressions

Widget Settings

The design and size of the widget is customizable: 4 design templates are available, and the size varies from 240-450 for width and 300-800 for height. You can also set the name of the widget and the background color of the header.

These settings allow you to use the widget in the composition of any site, with almost no restrictions.
Implementing the widget code via the iframe tag ensures the security of user data and the peace of mind of the site owners.
Obviously, the widget increases the time spent by users on the site. One of our partners has already managed to evaluate the "Olympic effect" and share our emotions with us - the average time spent by users on the site has increased to 2.5 - 5 minutes!
