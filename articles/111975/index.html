January Startup Crash Test - in search of projects

On January 21, a new crash test season will open on DigitalOctober. WiFixer and Cafenote startups will perform at the SCT in January. Recruitment is still open - offer your projects.

Venue: Moscow, Center for DigitalOctober, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6.
Registration for the event on the website www.greenfield-project.ru is required.

Startup Crash Test's traditionally attract the attention of start-up industry experts, specialists who can strengthen the project team, as well as private and venture capital investors. For entrepreneurs, this is an opportunity to test the business model of their project, to learn valuable lessons from the experience of colleagues and to make business contacts.

The organizers invite startups who are ready to declare themselves to the professional community.
  • 5-7 young projects will be able to perform as part of the pitches - two-minute speeches. This is an opportunity to practice a short presentation in public and interest potential colleagues and partners in your startup. Those who wish to speak - write to sctmoscow@gmail.com .
  • Half-hour "crash tests" are designed for 3-4 projects that are already working, have first sales or a prototype. The leader or participant of the project makes a 10-minute presentation, after which they answer the questions of the ‚Äúguru‚Äù and the audience, receive expertise, development tips, and points of concern. If you want to express yourself, write to the organizers at sctmoscow@gmail.com .

Together with you they will tell about themselves:

WiFixer ( wifixer.ru , ‚Äúwireless networks of all countries, connect!‚Äù ) Is an ambitious project to create a free global WiFi-network. With it, some will be able to access high-speed wireless Internet not only in cafes and business centers, while others will offer people their own access point and make money on it. You can connect to someone's network for virtual money. Owners of access points ("agents") themselves set the price and can compete with each other.

To start using WiFixer, just register and install software for Windows 7, Vista or XP. After that, you will see a list of wireless networks registered in the system and you can choose the one that is currently suitable for price and conditions. Payment for using someone's network goes either by the minute or by traffic volume. When you register, you automatically receive 10,000 virtual money, in the future you can replenish the balance through electronic payments or bank transfers. The service already has the first users and the first ten registered access points.
