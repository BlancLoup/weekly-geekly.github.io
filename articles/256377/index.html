Hacken und minen

Logs and mines - an ancient German game. The game's plot resembles chess etudes.
Interested in the puzzle, I checked the Appstore. Search failed.
And I filled the world gap.

Rules of the game


I remind the rules of the game - given a chess field of 8 by 8 cells. On it are thrown N min and N logs. N in the range from 2 to 6.
Logs can be moved according to the rule of a chess rook. Jump over other logs can not. When crossing mines and logs - both items are removed from the board. The goal is to eat all the mines and all the logs.

If the log does not encounter any obstacles while moving, it flies off the board, the etude is considered unresolved.

I dare to offer you three etudes, the solution of which will bring mathematical pleasure.
