Synchronization

Smart Pen 2 has a single button that turns on, syncs, and turns off the device. It is located almost at the opposite end of the rod, next to the output for charging, which is very thoughtful and will not allow you to click on it, accidentally disconnecting the device during operation.



Synchronization takes place at the prompts in a few simple steps, after which you can immediately proceed to work with notes. To activate the sheet, you just need to click the rod on the "envelope" icon in the corner of the page, after which a blank sheet of paper will open in the application. Synchronization occurs in real time, and you can watch the characters appear on the screen. The color and size of the lines can be selected in the special menu, which is located in the corner of the page.
