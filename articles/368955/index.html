Google and the Ministry of Education of Ukraine opened a resource for inventors



There are a lot of inventors in the world, but only a small fraction brings their ideas to practical implementation. To help such people, sites like Kickstarter, Indiegogo and others are created. Recently, a similarly focused resource appeared in the Ukrainian segment of the Network. The project was named " Ideas that change the world ." Anyone can offer his idea to the world through this site and find like-minded people to implement the idea.

According to the developers, the site was created for Ukrainian creative people who can now get together, implementing joint projects. Now the site already has a section with a number of interesting projects.

In addition, the portal tells about the inventions made by Ukrainians in the past. The primacy of some inventors can be challenged, but the main task of the portal is to collect good scientific and technical (and not only) ideas, as well as help the author of the idea to realize it.



In order to offer your own idea, you need to click on the home page of the site, and fill out the form All offers are moderated, so not all will be included in the catalog. Portal developers recommend before adding ideas to get acquainted with the conditions of placing information on the resource. Unfortunately, so far all the site information is presented only in Ukrainian.
