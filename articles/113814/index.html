How we build Russian Y Combinator

People with burning eyes dedicated.

We received about 160 applications at the 4th Moscow Startup Weekend , 78 projects were announced at Friday's presentations, 67 were made. The main selection criterion was the adequacy of the author. There is a huge gap between the initial idea and the final product, and it depends on the author of the project whether he can overcome this gap. Often, from weak or insane, at first glance, ideas, sensible projects grow, the main thing is to discern healthy grain in time and develop it in the right direction.
The output turned 39 projects . There is nothing unusual in such a sharp decrease in the number of participants: half of the projects do not live to see the final presentations. Web studios
Experiment with web studios can be considered as accomplished. Half of the representatives of the studios "fell off" after Friday's presentations, before the Sunday meeting with the projects got 9 studios, including ADV. After talking with the teams, the studios admitted that there were few interesting projects for cooperation - one or two. Reason: Too raw ideas. We have taken note of this and will continue to cross web studios and projects without development forces in a different format, most likely already beyond the Weekend.
