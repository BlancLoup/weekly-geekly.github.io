Introduction


Hello!

Recently, I wrote here http://habrahabr.ru/post/183794/ about the tests that I am going to conduct on computer hardware of different degrees of old age.

I combined this with the review I was going to do on my own, and this is what came of it. The sequence of actions may be broken, but the essence remains the same.

Part 1: Celeron 300A and 640 MB, which is enough for everyone

But, quite naturally, only the 9th opera was more or less tolerable. It loaded less than 20 seconds even, and the tabs opened better. Then I put a 12.15 opera, and my computer liked it a lot less. A modern browser requires more resources, of course.



When scrolling through the page load on the processor increases thorough. Now you usually do not notice this, but 300 MHz is not very good at this. In the background is loaded QT Creator, through the opera.



If you close all unnecessary and restart the browser, then the memory memory will decrease significantly, you can work with this more or less, but still, you want to sit down again on some Celeron D.

Part 2: Celeron 1100 and RAM


I initially took the most powerful of what I had to see how it would affect. Of course, at 640 mb it ran and worked fairly well, and it was not interesting. I armed myself with a memory stick of 16 mb and tried to start the car. Could not, the computer cursed at the memory and said he did not want, I had to put 32 MB of pc66 memory. VinHR booted and cut in 15-17 minutes, by eye. I'm tired of waiting for everything else to load, and I slammed the dropbox, Java and stuff.





And it looks unappetizing. At 32 MB of memory it is impossible to work in principle! It all turns so slowly that it took me almost titanic patience to at least cut down the computer and not go to the kitchen for a beer. He cut down another five minutes.

The next memory level is 64 mb. Download without all the excess.




And then with everything included.



Here, if you look at it this way, the performance was similar to the 300th Celeron, only the system was loading slower and it worked faster. About 5 minutes of downloading all together, counting dropbox, java and google update. Browsers are loaded as slowly as on the 300th, but they work faster. Loading time of the opera and chrome with a page with a habr - about 1 minute completely. Mozilla again ate a lot of memory and booted in three minutes.
Skype earned. He liked the computer with a low memory size and the brighter process.



And he even let me have a test call. It worked much better and faster than 300A, and we understood this a long time ago.

The next step is 128 mb.

The system was loaded for half a minute, apparently, 128 MB of RAM - this is the necessary minimum for working on XP. Browsers run for 20-30 seconds before the end, Mozilla started in one minute. QT Creator still crashes and seems to be crashing. Next stage - 256 mb

The system boot time is about 10-15 seconds, i.e. with memory doubled, the system booted twice as fast as possible. True, we have here only dropbox, java and google update, nothing criminal. Browsers, all as one, are loaded quickly (as far as possible on the 1100th Celeron), and I did not even notice much of a difference in speed, except that an opera with chrome can argue. Mozilla started up as always slower, but not as noticeable as before.
Departing QT Creator crashes. Apparently, it is something else.



Skype still says that the computer is slow, but it also starts up significantly quickly. If the system is not dirtied, then it is even more or less tolerable to work at 256 mb of RAM and winXR. Music and a browser with a bunch of tabs do not particularly slow down, although this does not mean that it works perfectly. After two cores, two gigabytes, a gaming video card seems unpardonably slow. And what to say, 256 MB of memory is still actively found at work. Feels like Celeron 1700 works much worse than the 1100th, there is no longer a matter of gigahertz, you see.
Skype works well, the voice test turned on very quickly and spoke cheerfully.



In minecraft still can not play.



He has little memory, you must have at least 512 mb, and still it will be impossible to play.

Something like a conclusion


Actually, 256 MB of RAM can be considered as the amount of memory with which some kind of comfort begins when working, you do not have to wait long for downloads and endure constant suspensions. Of course, for serious work this is not enough, especially in our time, when programs are eating their gigabytes. And for the office and the Internet with music, and this will be enough.
