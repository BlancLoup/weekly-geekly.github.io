Dell Latitude 5491: a rigorous corporate laptop with a powerful processor

Finding a compact notebook with a powerful processor is not easy. In the vast majority of models with diagonals of screens less than 15 inches, low-voltage Intel Core U processors are installed. What if you need a significantly higher CPU performance? For such cases, there is a Dell Latitude 5491 . Strict and fairly compact 14-inch corporate-class laptop with six-core processors and discrete graphics inside. Today we talk about it in detail - welcome under cat.



Appearance and ergonomics


Dell Latitude 5491 - a strict laptop, and even a little harsh. The design is extremely restrained and minimalistic. Users of classic or, especially, gaming laptops may even say that it looks boring. But such a machine should not be fun and attractive at all - it is a ‚Äúworkhorse‚Äù, the main thing in which is reliability, convenience and practicality. Still, Latitude is a business series of Dell laptops designed for corporate needs.



The whole case of the notebook is completely made of fairly thick plastic. The cover and the working panel at the pressings bend only slightly. On the whole, questions about the strength of the structure are unlikely to arise - very close attention was paid to the build quality here.

Separately, we note that there is no glossy element on the body of the Dell Latitude 5491 - all surfaces are matte. Elements with soft-touch coating in the design is also not provided. As a result, the fingerprints on the computer case are almost invisible and can be easily removed with a regular dry microfiber cloth, even without special cleaning products.



The cover of the laptop can be completely tilted back 180 degrees, which is very useful when several people are watching the screen at the same time, bending over the table, say, a meeting room. Quite a frequent scenario, it is typical for the offices of large companies. In all other positions, the screen is also securely held by metal hinges. The width of the frames around the display is not a record one, but it is also difficult to call them overly thick.



The thickness of the body of the 14-inch laptop is 20.3 mm, the computer weighs 1.65 kilograms. Again, this is also not a record in class performance, but with them the Latitude 5491 remains quite a portable solution that you can take with you from the office to your home, to carry on meetings and on business trips.

Interfaces and communications


Ports on the body of the Dell Latitude 5491 are located on three sides: both on the sides and back. On the back side are the 8P8C connector for connecting to the Internet using a wire, the input for the power adapter, the ‚Äúbig‚Äù USB 3.1, HDMI and a sliding tray for the SIM card. It is in all versions of the laptop, but the laptop can only connect to the mobile Internet in those configurations where the WWAN module is present. The good news is that if you choose a configuration without it, and then decide that you still need the mobile Internet in the PC, then it will not be a big problem. The slot for the appropriate adapter and antenna for it is in all versions of the Latitude 5491. So you can simply purchase it and install it yourself.



In practice, the location of these particular interfaces on the back of the laptop is quite convenient. Neither the network cable nor the thick cord from the monitor or projector will interfere with the operation, since they are far away from the user's hands.

On the left side of the Latitude 5491 is a card reader for SD cards (it is made in the form of a tray, where you need to install a storage device, and it is very fast!), Another USB 3.1 Type-A and USB Type-C, which in basic versions supports interface DisplayPort, and in more advanced - Thunderbolt 3. In our test sample, which we took for the review on Habr√©, just the second case. In addition, it has an optional smart card reader.



On the right side of the notebook we see the combined audio jack, the third USB 3.1 Type-A and the VGA port. Note that here and on the opposite side all interfaces are offset to the back of the laptop case, i.e. Connected devices will not interfere with the work either.



An Intel Wireless-AC 9560 adapter with Bluetooth 5.0 and both Wi-Fi ranges is responsible for the wireless internet connection. The solution is good and has long been well recommended. Neither the stability of the connection, nor the speed of data transmission will not have to complain.

On the part of the interaction with the system, users of the Latitude 5491 have a lot of different options available. Calculate? The keyboard and touchpad are classics, one and two. There is no full-fledged digital unit in this laptop, but there is a tricky feature - the ability to type them using the Fn button and standard letter keys. Very useful, given that the laptop is corporate, and constant reports, often filled with a lot of data, are an integral part of corporate life.



The touchpad is relatively small, located exactly in the center of the bottom of the working panel. The surface is slightly rough, the finger slides with little effort. With the accuracy of tracking movements, there are no problems, multi-touch gestures are supported. Just below the touch pad are two physical buttons with a deep stroke. In general, this control replaces the mouse quite successfully in cases where you need to work behind a laptop somewhere outside the office.

In the test with continuous playback of Full HD video to full discharge at maximum brightness, the laptop lasted exactly 8 hours and 20 minutes.
