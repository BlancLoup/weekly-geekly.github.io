Client for HabraHabr for Android

Hi Habr.

After reading the old posts (of those times when the mobile version of Habra was not created), I decided to create a small mobile client for this site for Android - Habra Droid . The client allows you to read the last 20 posts of the site and learn the karma of any Habr user. If the application is successful, I am ready to develop it and add new functions!


For users with not fast and expensive Internet, the function of displaying posts without images has been introduced, which will save traffic.
