Google has improved its toolbar

Google introduced a new version of the toolbar Google Toolbar 4 for Internet Explorer. According to the company, the service has become personalized. Among the new features - bookmarks, improved search, as well as opportunities for organizing the sharing of information.

Bookmarks, says Google, are good because they are stored in a user account. If a person, for example, creates a bookmark while sitting at a working computer, he can turn to it from home.

When searching from the toolbar, prompting words are offered to the user while entering a query. A new opportunity for organizing information sharing is that you can send the contents of web pages by mail Gmail.
