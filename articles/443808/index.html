Analytics of girls with low social responsibility (Power BI, Qlik Sense, Tableau)

Who are we and what were the prerequisites of the project?


Good afternoon, my name is Vladimir Lazarev, I am the head of the BI integrator Analytics Group . We make for the business visual reports on marketing, sales, finance, logistics based on the leading analytical platforms Qlik Sense, Power BI, Tableau.

In BI platforms, the visual component is very important. If you have watched dozens of demo reports of BI systems and you don‚Äôt like how this or that platform looks like, then most likely you will not implement it, even if you are satisfied with the price and technical specifications. Based on this, it is necessary to see the same data in different analytical platforms, so that you can compare.

And it is desirable that the data were interesting ... :-)

Where did the idea to make this report come from?


Several years ago, the Higher School of Economics published an article about pricing the services of girls of low social responsibility in Moscow. These were aggregated analysis data of 1,800 questionnaires. We found the data that is behind these findings of HSE sociologists interesting. And we decided to work on this topic.

Key ideas:

  • Show not only the conclusions that lie on the surface, but the data on which they were built;
  • To enable those who wish to independently analyze the patterns, put forward a hypothesis and test it.
  • Elaboration should include a large sample of questionnaires to increase accuracy;
  • Show not only the "market offer", but also the demand based on statistical data in search engines;

Data sources:


  • SQL database of questionnaires *, formed on the basis of one site, included in the TOP 3 rankings in Yandex and Google search engines.
  • Excel-file with relevant ** queries on the subject area of ‚Äã‚Äãanalysis.
  • Excel file with the historical frequency of queries in the Yandex search engine.
  • Excel file with the frequency of queries in the Google search engine.

* A parser was used to collect information. Data parsing was done once.
** The tool for the selection of relevant queries was wordstat.yandex.ru

Business roles & USER-story


In normal projects, we always specify who will use the analytics and which questions (direct or indirect) a person wants to find answers.

But that's bad luck - this project has no customer. Therefore, we had to invent three roles and their questions.

So the roles we have the following:

  1. Low social responsibility girl
  2. Site administrator with profiles
  3. Property owner

The girl with low social responsibility


"Indie" Elvira is 25 years old. Looks good, familiar men estimate her age in the region of 19-20 years. Goes in for sports, attends a solarium, a healthy diet, a good manicure and everything. She used to live well, not really deny something. But such a lifestyle requires a lot of money. Office work, she believes, is not for her. She loves sex and does not see something shameful in this activity. Economic education and analytical mind pushed her to approach the process of determining pricing, the list of services provided with a pragmatic approach. Elvira decided to analyze the data using the BI platform.

Objective: to identify key points to be competitive and earn more.

She is interested in the following questions:
  • For what value do ‚Äúcolleagues‚Äù with similar parameters (height, weight, chest, age) sell their time in the Elvira residential area? Does it make sense to find an apartment option in another Moscow District for the same money, but with a higher average check or less competition?
  • Should I overestimate or underestimate my age in the questionnaire?
  • Does it make economic sense to increase the 2nd breast size to 4th and how quickly will the investment in the operation pay off?

Main questions:

He also drew attention to the significant predominance in searches of requests from mobile phones - almost three times. Therefore, instructed the programmer-maker of his site to check the quality display on the mobile phone.
