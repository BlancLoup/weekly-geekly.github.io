Windows 7 Ultimate Hacked

Actually copy-paste

A few days after Windows 7 RTM was made public, craftsmen from hackers-hackers found a way to hack and activate Windows 7 Ultimate using the OEM key. Activation is achieved via offline method.
As previously reported on the site, last Friday, the main partners received distribution boxes for installing and configuring the system on their new computers. These include eminent firms such as HP, Toshiba, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, Dell, Sony and Fujitsu. According to the information, the leak occurred at Lenovo. Further, hackers had everything just a matter of technology, I will not describe the details of their work, but at the moment there is a working crack on Windows 7 Ultimate.
It must be recalled that this crack only works on Windows 7 Ultimate, but supports both editions: both 32-bit and 64-bit systems. As for other versions of Windows 7, at the moment the work does not stop and hackers are looking for ways to hack licenses and on them.
From experience we all know that various hacks, activations or key generators for popular software come out very soon. Sooner or later the crack for Windows 7 would come out, it's just a matter of time. The speed with which the solution was found for the "free" use of the system should have a noisy effect at the Redmond headquarters. Although, such fast leaks are haunting the Seven throughout the entire development phase, as they say, Microsoft is no stranger to it.
Let me remind you that the release date of Windows 7 RTM is July 22, and the wide distribution of Windows 7 through retail chains is scheduled for October 22, 2009.
