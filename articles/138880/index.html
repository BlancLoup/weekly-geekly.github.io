Now most cellular networks use audio coding in the 3.5 kHz band, while human hearing is capable of perceiving the 20 kHz band. HD Voice codecs transmit in the 7 kHz band, and Full-HD Voice encodes and broadcasts the entire audible range, so the sound quality is now limited only by the microphone and speaker / headphone capabilities.
