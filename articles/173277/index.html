Tetris first person



This classic game will soon turn 30 years old, and during this time it was managed not only to be implemented on any devices that have at least some processor and display, but also to come up with a huge variety of options - from the already banal color Tetris to 3, 4 x and even n-dimensional variants, triangular and hexagonal tetris, multiplayer tetris and tetris with realistic physics (there is a very interesting article by Colin Fei about history and different variations of the game).

On the site firstpersontetris.com you can play the first-person Tetris version - the falling figure is fixed in the center of the screen, like hands or a character‚Äôs weapon in 3D shooters, and the playing field moves relative to it. The page of the game is framed in the form of an old TV with a VCR, to which the Nintendo game console is connected. And this is done not just for beauty - a realistic picture allows you to experience almost as much dizziness as you turn the figurine from another classic game - Descent , so be careful!
