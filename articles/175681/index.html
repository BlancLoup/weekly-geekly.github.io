The future of mobile cameras

The race for megapixels actually lost its meaning a couple of years ago and now engineers are more and more paying attention to quality rather than size. Someone invents ultra-pixels, someone makes a Binary Pixel sensor, and someone focuses on the processing power of the processor and graphics chip for processing information from the sensor. Than this race would not end, in any case, we will win - the end users.
