Video podcast installation in Sony Vegas

It's time to talk a bit about the practical part of video podcasting. In my work I use Sony Vegas, it's just a matter of habit, for those purposes that we face in this article, almost any video editor will do.

In order not to go far, let's take a look at the list of such programs, not the most complete, but the choice, you see, is quite broad:

Here ... :) And now let's finally get to our today's topic - simple video editing in Sony Vegas. You can argue that all this can be done in a much easier and more accessible Movie Maker program, and you will be right, but the essence of the article is to BEGIN to use Sony Vegas, gradually moving to such things that Movie Maker never dreamed of.

Open Sony Vegas (in my case - Sony Vegas Pro 8.0). Its interface does not look very complicated, it is not. At the bottom of a large field, there will be located audio and video tracks. There may be a lot of them, that is, one video can be played against the background of another, all of which is framed by background music and sound effects. Now there is not a single track, so let's make a new video track. Video - because the picture or the inscription acts as a video in this program.

New track created and it is empty. It's time to insert ... I think you need to start with the title. There are several options, in the end you can insert a picture (just drag the file from anywhere in sony vegas into this track), but this only matters in some cases. If you want to insert text and just text, then I suggest the following.

Select the place where the title will be on the track, right-click on the newly selected one and select Insert Generated Media. Next, choose Sony ProType Tittler - this is a convenient way to create all sorts of inscriptions. Double click on the large black field - and now we enter the text:

Entering the text, you need to press 'Esc' and we will be able to move and resize it as we want. On the left - the parameters, I think they are familiar to you, even if not - just move the sliders and you will immediately understand their purpose. By the way, the text can be entered in several lines and change the parameters of each segment separately, that is, we can easily do this:

As soon as you have finished inserting the text (let's talk about any effects and additional settings next time), just close this window. Now on our video track there is the first video - a few seconds of text. It would be great if this title slowly appeared from the darkness to the music and disappeared just as slowly. Make it easy.

This video segment has blue triangles in the upper corners. If you hover the mouse cursor over them, it (the cursor) will turn into ... Ahem ... I can hardly explain the resulting geometric object, but you will immediately understand - drag this blue triangle to the side and get something like this:

It turned out smooth lines. These lines show the appearance and disappearance of the clip. It is very easy to regulate them - you can make another speed or another place for the end / beginning of such an effect.

Well, let's finally get to the video series itself! Drag the video file to the same track where we have the title. The audio part of it will fall on the lower audio track, but we do not need such a layering, so take the audio from the freshly-laid clip and drag it a little lower - the new track will automatically appear.
