PopCornUA v2.0.0: more, better, faster!

Hello to all! I am glad to announce that after 3 weeks of work my application for Android was successfully reworked. PopCornUA is a movie poster for Ukrainian cinemas.

What was done? First of all, this is of course a change in design - almost everything has been reworked. Also added search, transfer to the SD card (for this it was necessary to remove the widget), improved work with the map and stability. The application is free and without ads. A designer and a great man Timur Prokopiev helped me with the design and video.
