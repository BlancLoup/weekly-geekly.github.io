1. Contextual advertising (Google AdSense)


The most obvious way to think about in the first place. An advertising code is set on the website pages and each visitor of the website displays individually selected ads. Pay per click. The cost of a click can be increased. Google and Yandex give too general recommendations about this (‚ÄúMake high-quality websites‚Äù), so you have to rely on the observations of site owners:

- Choose the most profitable topics: banks (loans, mortgages), insurance, legal services, forex, stock market, airline tickets, hosting, medicine.
- Increase the attendance and credibility (PR, TCI) of the site, pick up (if only you are launching the site) a domain with a large age and already available indicators of TCI and PR.
- Use all ad formats (media, text, graphics).
- Change from time to time design ads.
- Use a filter against advertisers with cheap clicks (you can find ready).
- Keep only quality sites on your AdSense / YAN account. According to the observations of some webmasters poor-quality projects can pull the average cost of a click down for all sites on the account.
- Do not overload the site ads (4-5 blocks per page, no more).
- Use advertiser‚Äôs most popular ad formats. Google calls the most effective formats 336x280, 300x250, 728x90, 300x600 and a large mobile banner 320x100. Many site owners confirm this, but not all.

How much can you earn on contextual advertising? It depends on the total amount of traffic, its quality and the theme of the site. The owner of BigPicture.ru in January 2015 gave figures: with traffic of 80-100 thousand unique per day, the site earns 150-220 thousand rubles with YAN. On average, 5.5 rubles per click, CTR 0.16%. True, Yandex advertising is placed on BigPicture as stubs when there is no display advertising.

In the same interview, an estimate was given that "it is unlikely that there will be at least 500 sites in the entire YAN network earning $ 1,000 a month."

CPA is an advertisement with pay-per-action: registration on the site, entry into the game, paid order, etc. The model is not new, but it has gained a lot of popularity in RuNet over the past few years. CPA can be used if you have a well-targeted audience and you will purposefully still process it. For example, if you have a website with promotional codes online stores. Or a site with reviews of mobile phones and applications. Or a gaming site. In general, you can earn serious money on the CPA, but you have to work. These are not ad networks that work according to the ‚Äúset and forget‚Äù scheme.

When registering with CPA affiliates, there is a formal filter: you need to show your earnings experience on online advertising. But in practice, it is easy to get around: you can find on the forums ( Maultalk , Webmasters , kote.ws ) other people's screens and show them to the support during registration.
