Google postpones the launch of Nexus Q. All pre-order will receive the device for free

Google Nexus Q - the media center presented at the Google I / O conference, was received quite cold. Everyone admired the appearance, but no one could really think up a usage scenario. Nevertheless, despite the high price of $ 300 geeks, they pretty quickly dared the first devices from the Google Play Store. Today, Google has released an official disclaimer on starting sales now:

We have important news about your pre-order Nexus Q. When we announced the Nexus Q on Google I / O, we distributed the devices to the test. Industrial design and hardware were met with enthusiasm. We also received the first reviews that users want more opportunities than they have now implemented. In response, we decided to postpone product launches until we make it better. To thank you for your early interest in the new product, we want to expand the number of early testers to those who pre-order - Nexus Q will be sent to you for free. If you had other devices in the basket, we will only pay for them. Your Nexus Q will be sent to you shortly, you will receive an alert and tracking number from Google Play when the device starts shipping.
Team Nexus Q.
