Skype 3.2 for Windows has been released

The beta version of Skype 3.2 for Windows has been released today and can be downloaded here .


Here are the most important changes in this version:
Sending money via PayPal

Everything is very simple - the sender and recipient must have a PayPal account. If you send money to someone who does not have a PayPal account, they will be able to receive it after registration. This feature depends on where you live, since PayPal does not work in some countries. If it turned out that PayPal does not work in the recipient's country, you will not lose anything - the money will go back to your card.
Webcam Snapshot
Do you have a webcam and you would like to use it to make a photo in profile? Thus, if you do not use video, people will still be able to see you. Previously, for this, you would have to use other third-party programs to take a picture, change its size and crop unwanted portions, convert to the desired format and only then place the picture in the Skype profile.
