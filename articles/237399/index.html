Mobile application "Guess the game"


Not so long ago, we wrote about our first attempt to test ourselves as developers / publishers of a mobile application . Being in the backstage of the publishing business, we had to not only use the skills acquired over the years in the field of localization, but also actively explore the features of monetization and promotion of applications.

Initially, the project was aimed only at participation in the GDC Europe exhibition, however, we are planning to release an application on Google Play in the near future. Everyone will be able to install "Guess the game" on a device with Android system and test your level of game knowledge on several levels: "survival" (play before the first mistake), play up to three mistakes (more gentle mode), and for a while (you need to get maximum number of responses in a short period of time). There will also be an opportunity to test yourself in various game genres and categories.

Currently, the application is in the final stages of development, and in a month and a half we are planning to launch beta testing. If you are interested in joining the project, we will be glad to cooperate! All you need to do is write to us at ebokhan@allcorrectgames.com and declare your initiative. When the project is ready for testing, we will send a download link to your email. Thus, you will have the opportunity to go through all the levels first and not only evaluate the application, but also influence its development!
