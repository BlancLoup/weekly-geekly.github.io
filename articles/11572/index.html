Affiliate Program Adsense Referrals 2.0 expands on all products

The Adsense Referrals 2.0 affiliate program is out of a long beta test and is now available to all publishers. The main difference of the second version of the program is that now the deductions rely not only for distributing several programs like Google Desktop, but for promoting thousands of other products from Google advertisers.

On your website you can place affiliate links and receive a percentage of the sale of these products. Since the assortment is very wide, advertising can be placed by keywords. The site owner may limit advertising to certain categories of products and certain brands. The settings window is shown in the screenshot.
