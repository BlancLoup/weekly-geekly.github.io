Macheist nanoBundle

Today, around 3 nights in Moscow, MacHeist presented a new set of programs to everyone.



Currently 6 programs are available in it:
- ShoveBox - notes organizer
- WriteRoom - text editor, especially for writers
- Twitterific is a stylish Twitter client.
- TinyGrab - utility for taking screenshots and uploading them to the site
- Hordes of Orcs - a game in the genre of Tower Defense (the site has fallen so far)
- Mariner Write is a powerful word processor that will be available when (and if) there are 500,000 participants.

The creators of MacHeist say that they bundled this bundle free to introduce as many Mac users as possible to good Indie programs.

6 days left until the end of the distribution.
