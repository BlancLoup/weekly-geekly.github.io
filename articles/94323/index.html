Yandex.Probki widget

Good afternoon, dear habrovchane.

My next attempt to master the Android platform from the point of view of a developer this time was a success. Its fruit has become quite simple, but at the same time, a useful widget: the Yandex.Probka widget.

If one of its names is enough for you - you can immediately proceed to the Market and download it. Or use the convenient picture on the left.

And for inquisitive minds I will attach a short description.

Widget can:
  • choose a city:
    • Moscow
    • St. Petersburg
    • Yekaterinburg
    • Kiev
  • display traffic points for the selected city and the corresponding traffic light color
  • display the time of the sample data
  • adjust the frequency of your update
  • run the Yandex Maps app for tapu at traffic lights, if installed


Suggestions / bug reports / comments are welcome.

UPDATE: An updated version is available.
Corrections:
  • incorrect display at high resolutions
  • minimum SDK version downgraded to 3 (Android 1.5)

Known issues:
  • On Android 2.1 and higher, the launch of Yandex Maps works only after the phone is rebooted (the devil knows why)
