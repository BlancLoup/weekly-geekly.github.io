Kinect prices announced

Today, Microsoft has finally approved the price of a new Kinect game controller, which is a "controller without a controller." It allows the user to interact with the Xbox 360 using voice controls, gestures and objects displayed.

The price will be $ 149.99 . Kinect is fully compatible with any Xbox 360 ever released. Anyone who preordered Kinect will be able to download three unique levels for the Kinect Adventures game that comes with the controller. At the start of sales, which will begin in November 2010, 15 games supporting Kinect technology will be available, 3 of which from Microsoft, Kinectimals, Kinect Sports and Kinect Joy Ride, will cost $ 49 each in preorder.
