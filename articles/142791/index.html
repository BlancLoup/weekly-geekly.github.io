Google is building a new data center in the US



Google Inc. is investing $ 300 million to build a new data center in the United States. Construction region - Iowa, USA. According to the corporation, Google has already invested about 900 million dollars in the construction of various kinds of objects in Iowa. About 50 people will work in the data center, including such posts as air conditioning, logistics, and computer repair specialists.

Construction takes about 1000 acres. Unfortunately, there is no information on how big the new data center will be. In addition, it is not known when this data center will be ready. A large data center company, which was opened in 2009, is already operating in Iowa. This data center services such services as Google Search, Gmail and Google Maps. The new facility will provide additional capacity for the specified services.

For this data center, a special tax model was created, thanks to which the company would pay less than it would have been supposed if it were working according to a standard scheme. Because of this, the corporation decided to build a data center in Iowa, and not in any other state.

Previously, the corporation was going to build a data center in Nebraska, but due to the creation of an attractive tax model in Iowa, it was decided to build a new data center here. A little earlier, the company has already begun construction of an additional data center in Oklahoma.
