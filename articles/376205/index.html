AutoFon - the last chance to find a stolen car



Most modern car owners are content with anti-theft systems that are pre-installed by the car manufacturer. If there is none, standard protection systems are used, mainly GSM alarms. But neither conventional alarms with a remote control or GSM systems are a panacea. What to do - technical progress makes moving forward not only car manufacturers, but also hijackers. And the preset "signaling" by many experts break into one or two.

I once looked at what professionals of the security systems market advise motorists, and the main advice is to use several anti-theft systems at once, and, especially, they are pressing, such systems should be non-standard. If ‚ÄúAvtoFon‚Äù is activated, then as soon as the car starts moving, the owner receives an SMS. In some cases, this makes it possible to quickly respond and prevent theft. But the main purpose of the device is still different - not to prevent theft, but to find a car.

How to find out where the car is?


This device is the size of a matchbox that can be hidden anywhere. True, experts recommend hiding yet not anywhere, but choosing certain places that are difficult to reach. In general, the hijackers are also far from fools, but you can hide a small sensor very well, and even a good specialist will find it difficult. And no one bothers to hide not one module, but two or even three - the more difficult it will be to find and eliminate them all.



Size, of course, is not the main advantage of the system. The main advantage is that the device does not appear on the radio at all, for the time being. If the intruder uses the GSM jammer, then sooner or later it will be turned off, and then the Avtofon case will enter. The system determines the location of the car by satellite (inside - GSM and GLONASS modules) and GSM-cells, and at irregular intervals (so that they are not detected by the interceptor) sends an SMS and / or sends a message to the server, from where it already hits the owner. The device also works as a GPS tracker, and as a GPS passive beacon. Coordinates of cars can be obtained by SMS-request.
One battery lasts for two years in passive mode.
