All-Russian Mourning: Beginning of the End (Part 1)

Now the whole country is in mourning for the death of miners, residents of the nursing home and passengers of the TU-134 airliner.

In fact, this is the moment when the three accidents due to dilapidated conditions coincided in time, which is probably not accidental, but speaks of the impending wave of mass accidents and emergency situations.

It is no secret that in our country there is the Ministry of Emergency Situations - the Ministry of Emergency Situations. It was in our country that state of emergency became so normal that it took a special ministry.

Started a wave of accidents. Bridges will fall, train derailment, buildings collapse, weapons depots, etc., explode. The safety margin specified in the USSR ends.

In Moscow, it is not as noticeable as in provincial cities. Yes, they are building new shops, houses for the rich, but bridges, railways (many branches are simply dismantled for scrap), ordinary houses - everything is becoming unusable.

And provincial hospitals are terrible! I wish you never get there. If you had an accident and you have no relatives to buy blood, plasma and drugs, then you are a corpse. I saw it with my own eyes.
