A little about the conference Microsoft TechEd 2012

In this article, I would like to share my impressions of the Microsoft TechEd technology conference on November 27-28. Just want to say that the reports on Lync, Exchange, Dynamics and InfoSec were not considered in this article.
The plenary speech was opened by Nikolai Pryanishnikov, President of Microsoft Rus, who in his report focused on the arrival of a new era of cloud technologies and cloud services. In turn, Steve Balmer spoke on the arrival of this new golden era of cloud solutions for companies, developers and customers. Next was the speech of Kirill Tatarinov, President of Business Solutions, he highlighted the universal interfaces that allow the same type of control of various devices on Microsoft platforms that do not require additional training. After the demonstration of Dynamics CRM, Scott Guthrie, corporate vice president of Microsoft Azure, appears on the scene, emphasizing the main advantage of Windows 8, which is integrated with work in the cloud. The demonstration of Windows 8 was held by Mikhail Cheronomordikov - the head of the group of experts.
On the features of software development was the report of Stas Pavlov - an expert on strategic technologies.
