Baltimore is completely covered by a WiMax network

Baltimore's WiMax network data transfer rate ranges from 2 to 4 Mbps.



Tariff plans for users:
- $ 10 / day (apparently for city guests).
- $ 25 / month for stationary PC.
- $ 30 / month for mobile internet.
