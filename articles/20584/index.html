Witcher: Improved Edition

The Polish office of CD Projekt RED has already announced that in the future it plans to release the continuation of its successful role-playing game The Witcher. But the sequel is only the verbal promises of the Poles. In the meantime, they set out to please fans of the game with a new patch. Even, rather, add-on.

The main problems of the game "Witcher" were and remain a long time to load locations and save (in the later stages), far from optimized engine, issuing a low number of fps on "not high-end" computers. This is, first of all, and overcame the Polish igrodely in a special version of The Witcher: Enhanced Edition. According to CD Projekt RED, the download speed of locations will be reduced by 80%, and the game itself will work stably in almost all configurations.

Another reason for the release of the add-on was the addition to the game of more than 50 models for minor characters (in the Witcher's world, many residents of cities and villages were the same person) and new animation for all the characters. In addition, an editor will also be included to create fan-made modifications and two storyline modes made by CD Projekt RED itself, with a total duration of about five hours.

Perhaps one of you will say that the Poles, taking advantage of the popularity of the game, simply set out to cut down a little money by selling additional content. In some ways it is. The Witcher: Enhanced Edition will be sold with a bunch of bonus materials: soundtrack, developers' guide, etc. But in fact, you should pay tribute to CD Projekt RED. In order to get the promised fixes of the engine, the editor and new mods will be enough just to download a large patch. This can be done on May 16th.
