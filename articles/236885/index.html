The first intermediate results of the competition Android-developers Moverio Apps Market

Let's sum up the short intermediate results of the Epson Moverio BT-200 video application developer contest :


  • We have just started receiving applications from participants and testing the first applications.
  • Among the applications there are very non-trivial and interesting solutions, for example, related to navigation and geolocation using voice!
  • In the process of communicating with developers, we received many useful comments regarding the process of coordinating and downloading applications to the store, which were handed over to the team responsible for developing Moverio Apps Market.
  • If you have any comments or questions regarding the process of downloading applications to the store, write them here or a personal message. We will consider all messages and answer all the questions.

Still, two weeks is not enough to collect data for the interim report, so the next report will be posted in a month.

Dear developers! We are waiting for your applications!
Do not miss the chance to win one of the valuable prizes !

UPDATE (September 18, 2014): As our Japanese colleagues told us, it is not necessary to have a PayPal Business account in order to receive funds from application sales at Moverio Market. You can also use a PayPal Premier account, provided that it is owned and authorized in the name of the developer who uploaded the application to the store.
