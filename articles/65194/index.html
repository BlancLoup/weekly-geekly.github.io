Synaptic ClearPad 3000 - 10-finger touchscreen display



Today, Synaptics touchscreens are used in more than 550 million devices worldwide. These include the Samsug YP-P2 player, Sony Vaio L laptop, Dell monitors, Logitech consoles, NTT Docomo phones and many, many others.

The other day, Americans showed their new development: the ClearPad 3000 touchscreen display. In fact, according to the logic of previous generations, the novelty should be called ClearPad 10000. The fact is that the first generation - ClearPad 1000 supported pressing one finger, ClearPad 2000 - two, but ClearPad 3000 allows you to tap the screen immediately with ten fingers.

The display is able to distinguish one finger from another in shape and size and, moreover, has an enviably low power consumption. According to the developer, the use of ClearPad 3000 technology even in an 8-inch screen makes it comfortable to work with the ten-finger method without being afraid that the battery will sit in fifteen minutes.

Um, there is a suspicion that in the near future we are waiting for a revolution in the field of entertainment.
