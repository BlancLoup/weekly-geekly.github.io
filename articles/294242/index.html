What to do when you're in your early twenties



If you are under thirty, most likely your parents, like mine, were brought up in the USSR. They studied hard to find a better job and get decent money. And you and I were prepared to become a good worker, although the world around was already different. The Internet took a monopoly on knowledge from teachers, made it possible to get a profession without leaving home and showed us the first young millionaires. We were taught to efficiently exchange our time for money, at the moment when we had other opportunities and other ambitions.

This series of articles should not be taken as a recipe for success, although I give very specific advice. This is more like a message to yourself - seven years ago such an article would change my life. Then I read Kiyosaki and Branson - their example is inspiring, but it is difficult to use the experience in practice. Real estate and multimillion-dollar deals are impressive, but if you want to make hundreds of thousands for a start, I think my experience can help you

Since childhood I have been a fan of graphics, so by the time I graduated from school I was already working as a designer. There was no clear plan for the future - I just wanted to get better and better. Revenues grew, but to make good money, you need to spend a lot of time. I think everybody has such thoughts - the salary has a ceiling, time is also limited, and I want to be rich and independent. Where to begin


Anyone who plans his business, the question arises - where to start? Can make a social network of a new generation? On the Internet, all the money. Or a coffee franchise? My mother's friend has access to suppliers. Or start a business with a friend programmer? Limitless possibilities make it difficult to move towards the goal.

First of all, give yourself the right to make a mistake . It‚Äôs difficult to choose a lifetime business, but you can create a small project and gain experience in entrepreneurship even now. As in an RPG - before fighting a dragon, kill ten rats in the basement. In any case, with the experience of an unsuccessful business, you will become a hundred times cooler than someone who has not even tried.

Evaluate ideas helps elementary planning, on the fingers. While you are just fantasizing, you do not need to download business plan templates and calculate machine depreciation for 10 years ahead. Work in large strokes - for a start, think about profits and the number of sales. By profit, I mean a mark-up on a product or service - relatively speaking, margin. You can often sell with a small markup or less often, but with a big one. And if you can sell a lot with a large margin, you can swim in money like Scrooge McDuck. Do not be wise - rate your ideas as a first approximation, ARPPU or APC will appear later.

Sales


Before estimating the number of sales per month, think once and for all about coverage. How big is the market you are going to enter? No need to examine the reports of leading analysts and count the market to a cent. Just think about who you will sell. Each word in the project description reduces the chances of success - if the service to increase sales interests every entrepreneur, then ‚Äúan increase in sales of trousers in Omsk at night‚Äù will interest only a couple of trouser magnates. At the same time appreciate the possibility of scaling abroad.

From personal experience: Now Log machine makes more than all logos in Russia, although initially the idea seemed too simple to me. But this was her strength - there are no problems with work in Russia and abroad, the service is necessary and understandable. I myself have often become my own client. It is not necessary to have a revolutionary idea - the absence of competitors is more often a minus than a plus.
