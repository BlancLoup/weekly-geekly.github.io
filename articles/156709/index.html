How to create a working landing pages


Landing - landing. Landing pages - landing pages.

You and without us know for a long time that landing pages are the basis of any advertising, including the mailing list . These are the pages that a user gets from any advertisement - and how they are made depends on the effectiveness of this advertisement in the end. Speech in this article will go about how to eat fish, and not to choke with a bone, namely: to improve the conversion due to grounding pages, and at the same time improve the search performance of your site.

Do not be greedy to create new landing pages


The main rule of grounding pages: they need to produce and multiply. They should appear easily and quickly, in large quantities.

The first reason for this rush is the unspoken rule to create a separate grounding page for each distribution. Below you will read tips on how this can be avoided in case the resources for some reason are running out. But it is better to plan the creation of pages in this way. You can create a grounding page template and then simply fill it with content. It can save time.

The essence of such an action is that the page that is more carefully adjusted to the CTA (call to action, urge) mailing gives a greater conversion. As a rule, the difference between a conversion with a well-thought-out grounding page and a similar page taken ‚Äúat random‚Äù (read - not thought out) is 30-50%. Since the goal of your mailing is a conversion, grounding pages are an important tool for achieving the goal.

Click on the button - you will get the result


Everything is simple with grounding pages: they must be associated with the distribution. This is the same principle as when buying advertising, for example, Yandex Direct or Google AdWords. If you offer a person sandals, and brought him to the page with rubber boots - you have lost it. Unfortunately, this error occurs very often. In about 40% of cases, advertisements do not lead there, and 0.5% fall on a link that comes "nowhere" to 404 errors. The result is a low or zero conversion and an empty marketing budget spent. Therefore, creating a mailing list, immediately create the text of the grounding page. Start with CTA and format the content of the newsletter and the page content so that the customer walks smoothly through them, as if in a sales funnel. After all, the distribution with the grounding page is the entrance to this funnel.

Higher and higher (in the top)


Another beautiful thing in numerous grounding pages is that they can also be used for SEO and search engine optimization. This can be done quite simply as follows:

  1. Prior to the creation of an ezine, the SEO specialist expresses suggestions on which keywords are now particularly relevant. This point needs to be coordinated with what the distribution will be devoted to. What will be prioritized is decided by negotiation. Sometimes certain keywords can help come up with a specific storyline for mailing. More often - initially there is a mailing topic, and from your set of relevant keywords you need to select the appropriate ones.
  2. After the grounding page is created, attract an SEO specialist to review it and, possibly, condense the number of references to keywords on the page. Of course, be careful and do not make machine text out of the page - remember that you first need a cover, so do not overdo it with optimization. Use it sparingly.
