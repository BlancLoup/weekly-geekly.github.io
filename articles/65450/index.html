Android will get a Mozilla browser



Mozilla plans to put its mobile browser Fennec on the Android platform.

Last week, Google announced the Android Native Development Kit version 1.0, which will allow developers to create applications for Linux-based operating systems.
In this regard, Mozilla Vice President Jay Sullivan said that Android users have a chance to get Fennec browsers to their smartphones, as the development team has already begun to check whether NDK has the appropriate capabilities.
