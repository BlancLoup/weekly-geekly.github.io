Crossover holds the Hiring challenge competition and hires hundreds of Java and Ruby on Rails developers.

Team Crossover announces a competition among the leading developers of Java and Ruby for the position of Chief Software Architect with a salary of 300.000-470.000 rubles per month (after tax), which will allow you to work from anywhere in the world. Anyone can go through the competition on February 18 in Moscow.



Crossover is an intermediary platform between employers and remote workers. Cloud Wages does not depend on the region of residence, therefore, working through Crossover, you can poke your finger anywhere in the world where reasonably fast internet is available, and immediately book one-way tickets.

In the position of Java Chief Architect, you will be responsible for solving complex architectural problems, designing and developing and deploying enterprise-class software products for customers, and will be the technical leader for highly qualified colleagues. As the owner of the architecture and technical side of providing an enterprise product, you will need to work as a chief technical manager for this product, playing the role of an architect, a developer of the most complex elements of a product, and a technical team leader.

The Crossover hiring tournament will be held February 18 in Moscow at the site # tceh / IIDF. As part of this event, all participants will have the opportunity to pass a competitive task, consisting of 4 stages:

Automated test - 2 hours
The first task for encoding is 1.5 hours
The second task related to coding is 1.5 hours
Interview in English - 0.5 hours

The prize fund for winners is $ 10,000

With you will need to bring a laptop to perform test tasks. Meals are provided for all participants.
