The event digest for HR-specialists in the field of IT for July 2019



July is the time to plan a trip, write a vacation application and forget about working for a couple of weeks. But how to have fun for those who, for whatever reason, could not go on vacation this month and spend it at the computer in the office? The answer is simple: make your work as close to vacation as possible!
