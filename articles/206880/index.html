TV on the Internet

If we talk about the current state of the telecommunications market, we can note the obvious transformation of telecommunications in the field of content services in recent years. And this transformation, I think, is associated, in the first place, with IP-TV.



What can the Internet give us?


Understanding this fact and all the possibilities that it gives IPTV, and pushed me to write this article. IPTV gives the user such opportunities that simple television, for example, adaptive broadcasting, high quality video and sound (up to HD resolution), interactivity (the ability to watch TV programs at a convenient time, and on any device), pause live . I will begin my review with a popular program for watching TV channels on mobile devices - SPB TV . It immediately suggests itself, to note one drawback - viewing only on mobile devices. But first, let's talk about the pros. The main plus, of course, is multiplatform. The player works on all platforms, among them: iOS, Android, Windows Phone, Symbian or Blackberry. The player is also suitable for Smart TV - this is quite relevant today. If we talk about the proposed content, there are about 200 channels on SPB TV, while there are both free and paid subscriptions, while the creators claim that their prices are lower than the prices for ordinary home television.



SPB TV in all its glory

The offered channels are both Russian and foreign. And all these advantages come to the user for free. The listed advantages of the player are noted by the creators on their site, what can be said about the disadvantages. First, there are significant problems with updating the program, understanding the user's needs. After the latest program updates, there are significant problems with playing channels, many channels have disappeared, many do not work at all. Not very user-friendly interface, no brightness adjustment. Of course, after user complaints, developers should correct these shortcomings, but as long as it is, this is a significant drawback of the program. Often there are problems with viewing Russian channels abroad.
