The history of my company: from a hobby project to an Internet company

In this article I will tell how my business started and what it is now. Now it is an Internet company in Nizhny Novgorod .

Briefly about the article: how it all began, the first unsuccessful experience of joint business (if the division of business already reached the police), the creation of the company double 2, the development of the project, the development of new activities, the creation of new projects.

This is not a simple matter - own business :)


It all started with the fact that I got married (then I was 19 years old, 3.5 years ago). During the preparation for the wedding, I realized that in our city there is a clear lack of a local wedding site. At that time, I was working as a site editor at a local television station (TK Volga). Riveted some small saytik, some were quite popular, but could not effectively monetize.

I thought - did. Launched the forum, began to promote the resource. Within a month or two, the first core was formed, many of which still communicate on the site.

In such a "frivolous" state, the site lived for about a year. During this time, the resource has evolved, has become quite active. But not about any advertisers, I have not thought. Somewhere in a year I went to work in an Internet company. After working there for half a year I met a person who was interested in my project.
