"Popular tags" authors and readers - different things

Greetings, habra people!
I am now busy writing a city social network (this phrase creaks in my teeth, right? :)), and at this particular moment I am writing the tag cloud mechanism.

If I'm not mistaken, tags are displayed in the Cloud, which are most often used when writing a new text (news, survey, etc.) on the site as a whole. On awareness of this moment, I pereklinilo - "Something here is not a tag" ...

It seems to me that this logic is somewhat, to put it mildly, incorrect. More precisely, on the one hand, of course yes, but on the other - no. I will try to explain it. First, let's take as an example the Cloud on Habrahabr.

Here I come on Habr. I look at the Cloud, which performs the function of the main navigation on the site. What I see? With the most popular news and blogging topic - old google. Please note that this is a popular topic for AUTHORS texts. And for READERS? Not a fact, not a fact - about Google and so they write everywhere and everything. The great popularity of the tag among AUTHORS does not mean that behind it hides interesting news to the reader .
