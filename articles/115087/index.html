WAVI Xtion



Another new product that ASUS introduced at CeBIT is a motion recognition device. The functionality of the system is similar to Microsoft Kinect for Xbox 360, but now it acts on a PC. A practical implementation is a peripheral device that allows you to control a computer with simple gestures and movements.

WAVI is a wireless media stream transmitter in HD quality. Xtion - a sensor that tracks movement. The device operates as follows: while you are, for example, in front of a large TV, WAVI transmits media data from a computer to the screen. The device uses the WHDI wireless video transmission standard, which has a range of 25 meters.

WAVI Xtion is equipped with HDMI, has 2 USB ports, can transmit eight-channel audio and video stream with a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. Xtion works if the player is from the device at a distance of 80 cm to 3.5 m.
