DonorSearch's experience in attracting young people to donate blood through geo-targeting VKontakte

On New Year's Eve, it is especially important to replenish the supply of donated blood and blood components. Because after December 15 and until mid-January the number of donors in blood centers is greatly reduced. And the need for hospitals, on the contrary, is increasing. Therefore, the Kazan Blood Center turned to DonorSearch so that we could help attract donors in early December.




I will describe under the cut how we doubled the number of donations in the blood center with the help of advertising on VKontakte for geotargeting. Our experience is useful to those who are important to reach an audience for an offline event.



Just in case, let's clarify right away that blood is not directly transfused. And blood centers precisely fill up those stocks that will leave during the New Year holidays.


Background


Thanks to the support of the Charity Department of VKontakte , we received a coupon for 10,000 rubles at the VK for Good conference in November in an advertising office. Previously, we had joint promotions with VKontakte, such as Donor Day , VK Fest , and also won an advertising grant for the promotion of social advertising - the finalist of the social video contest. Our scandalous socialist advertisement ‚Äú Being a Man ‚Äù received, according to the results, coverage of over 500 thousand views.



Screenshot from the advertising office for the Donor Day. 10 out of 10 quality indicator.

Of course, such high rates are not taken immediately. At least 10 ad variations are tested on a limited budget. Varies text, picture, audience. The experience of past advertising campaigns has shown that in addition to such settings, it is possible to vary the user's geo-position with an accuracy of up to 500 meters.


That is why we decided to test on the budget of 10 thousand rubles the opportunity to invite VKontakte users to a specific blood center, given the convenience of getting to this center. Our hypothesis:


Young people are sometimes just too lazy to get to the blood center to donate blood. So, our task is to take care and call on young people from those areas where it is convenient to get to the location for blood donation.


So we did in our example. In Kazan, getting to the blood center is most convenient by tram. And because they put in the settings of geo-location several points with a radius of 1 km along the tram line. So, to get from home to the place took no more than half an hour.

In the best case, we obtained a performance indicator of 9.4 with a CTR higher than 6%. With a total result - 1890 transitions and an average cost of 4.85 rubles per transition. This is a great indicator.



Screenshot from personal account. The less efficient were shut down earlier.

As a result of the campaigns, it also turned out to attract 174 new participants to the project community on VKontakte (instead of the average value of 30). This is a small indicator, but the task was not joining the group.


Major successes


During the week, 130-158 people came to the blood center per day (instead of the average value of 80-90). Also, usually 10-15% of primary donors are on average, this week - 20-25%. Advertising was conducted on November 27-29. Data on donation metrics is provided by the blood center (December 3-7).


It was possible to find the best examples of advertising posts with a price below 5 rubles for the transition. This value can be reduced in the future due to further testing of other text, image, and targeting options.


It is important that such high rates turned out as for a heated audience - through retargeting. So with the new one - the youth of Kazan is 18-25 years old within a radius of 3 km from the blood center. At the same time, targeting just Kazan as a whole did not give such a conversion effect.


That is, the key task is to find an audience that would be convenient to come to the event (in our case, to the blood center). Many are ready for a good deed. But it is more effective to bring the call to that part of your audience, which will be easy to get to the location and do a meaningful job.


For greater efficiency, of course, it is better to use the audience of retargeting and the look-a-like format of the audience. This allows you to increase the reach of your audience, similar to the audience that you already have.



Our audience for retargeting
