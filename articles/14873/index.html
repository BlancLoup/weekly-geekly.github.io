Google launched youtube advertisements in adsense

Google has begun to show YouTube videos with advertisements attached to thousands of sites participating in the AdSense program. Thus, the Internet company hopes to accelerate the process of return on investment in YouTube.

Google has announced the expansion of its AdSense advertising platform with a new content distribution channel. Now on thousands of websites on the Internet you can see YouTube videos, accompanied by advertising. Advertisements do not use video format, but are displayed in the form of a banner and text string associated with the movie. Previously, the company has already broadcast YouTube videos with ads on a limited number of sites, but now this feature is included in a significant AdSense network. So far, the offer applies only to English-language sites located in the United States, but over time it will become available to everyone.

Now participating in AdSense sites have the opportunity to specify the type of video they want to show on their pages. Not all YouTube videos will be broadcasted via AdSense along with ads. For these purposes, only the content whose provider has signed the corresponding agreement will be used. About 100 providers have already expressed their consent to provide content.

The goal of this step by Google is to make money from showing advertisements and speed up the process of returning to investment in YouTube. The company will share the revenues received from the new distribution channel of advertising with content providers and the site where ads are shown.
