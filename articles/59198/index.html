DE: CODED '09 - All-Ukrainian IT Festival

DE: CODED is a unique, unique computer festival in Ukraine.

Why the festival? Because this is not an ordinary programming contest or a course of lectures on the basics of computer networks. This is an interactive event that lasts 3 days (!) And includes various and non-standard contests for both IT pros and ordinary amateurs, seminars and master classes from leading companies in Ukraine and the world, an extremely fun entertainment program for participants and spectators festival.



But the true essence of the DE: CODED festival is a meeting of like-minded people, people who are not indifferent to the field of information technology, real professionals of the computer business and ordinary IT fans. A unique opportunity to exchange views and ideas, meet interesting people from all over Ukraine. We create a platform for your communication!
