How to prepare a unique sales proposal in 30 minutes using MindMap



If you are tired of "puzzling" how to build up from competitors, this article is for you. Intellect cards are not a panacea, while they are great for facilitating the structuring of information when creating a UTP. The whole process takes an average of 30 minutes. In the article you will see how and what to do with examples of physical goods and educational services.


MindMap does not replace brainstorming, customer surveys, web analytics. This is a way of thinking, finding links between 4 marketing parameters:

- Properties - the characteristics of the product (what it does, how it works, what it consists of);
- Benefits - the properties of the product are useful for buyers;
- Audience - whose life the product makes better;
- Situation - when the product can make the life of the buyer better.

The result is:

Value - exactly the characteristic feature of the product will make the life of the buyer better. This is what you create the whole scheme for. The essence of a unique sales offer.

Ideally, UTP should give potential customers answers to 3 questions:

1) Why do I need it, what need does it cover?
2) Why is this product, and not other solutions?
3) Why should I buy from you?

Value can be used:

- In the landing page header;
- In the ads contextual, targeted advertising;
- In the selling texts on the site, promotional materials;
- In product information articles.

Almost every product or service has several target groups and applications. Intellect card will help to quickly establish the relationship of properties - consumer benefits and prepare proposals for each audience.

MindMap Tools

The easiest option is to draw a map by hand. Yes, yes, take a sheet of paper with a pen and draw "how God puts it on the soul." Let it be crooked, with a bunch of handwriting, while drawing hands free, removes the fear of doing something wrong.

For fans of gadgets, MyPaint is a free program that allows you to make digital images of acceptable quality. Not Photoshop, but the bells and whistles in this case are not needed. Mindmap will be able to draw a person with any level of proficiency in graphics. The most convenient way to work on the tablet.

When you need to "pack" the card beautifully, we take special services. We used XMind - an easy-to-use cross-platform software (works in Windows, Linux, Mac OS).

To begin, "we outline" an approximate scheme. In the center - the product or service. The first "orbit" - all the significant properties of the product, both positive and negative. The inclusion of negative properties is important for practicing objections. The last circle is the target groups + for what and when they use the product (fields of application and situations).



From properties we stretch connections to situations and target groups. The element to which several arrows lead is the key to creating the UTP.
