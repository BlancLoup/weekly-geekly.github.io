My Mir@Mail.Ru is looking for talented application developers

Our social network has launched a contest for the best application. There will be three winners (according to the number of nominations): according to the jury, according to the results of the user vote, My World@Mail.Ru will award the author of the best tutorial on developing applications for this site.

What will give you participation in the competition?
1. The chance to get into the field of view of the "sharks" of the business of creating applications. The jury includes the head of My World Platform. Ru. Alexey Terekhov, Alexander Vaschenko from IT-Territory, Nikita Sherman from drimmi, and Alexey Kostarev from i-Jet.
2. The ability to get a free advertising package for 25 million impressions
3. ... and a pleasant "trifle" in the form of a laptop with a three-dimensional image of Acer;)

To take part in the competition, you must send an application to the address api_contest@corp.mail.ru and indicate in the letter the following information:

* - Link to the application in my world@mail.ru.Ru
* - A small (2-3 sentences) description of your application
* - Author of the application
* - Contact email, phone number and your mailing address (for the delivery of the prize)

The contest lasts until June 14, 2010, the results will be announced on June 21.

Details are here . And also we will be happy to answer all your questions in the comments.

We are waiting for your creations!
