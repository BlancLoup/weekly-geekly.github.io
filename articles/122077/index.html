We assort on screws: iOs and Android, AppStore and Android Market



On June 28, the first in-depth master class for mobile application developers will take place in Nizhny Novgorod, opening a series of dedicated Developer Garage conferences.

The peculiarity of the Garage is that each speaker‚Äôs speech is a detailed analysis of the case study on development or promotion, with examples and advice. Garages will be held in different regions.

The theme of the first Garage is the development of applications for iPhone and Android. Participation in the workshop is free. To participate are invited programmers, developers of mobile applications, both beginners and have experience, entrepreneurs and project managers in the field of IT.

As part of this mini-conference, participants will be able to gain practical experience in creating applications for the iPhone and Android, tips on marketing, promotion in the AppStore and Android Market, monetization of the products created.

Developer Garage is a local event in a series of major developer conferences: Mobile Developer Day ( www.mdday.ru ) and the Mobilefest annual mobile application developers festival ( www.mobilefest.ru ).

PROGRAM


Pavel Salnikov, MERA
IPhone Application Development
Workshop for developers at a basic level.
The main stages of the development process, the Objective-C language, as well as all the main libraries of the SDK and examples of their use. Design and creation of user interface elements.

Pavel Sharkin, MERA
Android Application Development
Basics of developing applications for the Android platform on the example of cases.
SDK: tools, samples and code samples.
Practice: collecting and reviewing applications on Android

Vsevolod Novikov, CIO, DorogaTV
Cross-platform development on Airplay SDK
The DorogaTV project case is a cross-platform development for iPhone and Android based on the Airplay SDK.

Denis Tsarev, CMG Group
App Store Promotion
How to publish and how to promote applications? Whether to enter into a contract with the publisher? Tools to attract attention and traffic.
Cases for successful promotion of applications in the AppStore.
