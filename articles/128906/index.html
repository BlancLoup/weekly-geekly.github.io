Where is the money, Zin? ..

Surely, many will be interested in the question of profitability. Here, I will probably be forced to disappoint - the task of obtaining the maximum profit (at the time of the experiment) did not stand, therefore only ordinary contextual advertising was set.

Initially, advertisements were made as simple as possible - they installed two Google AdSense banners. A few days everything went well, but suddenly on most pages the ads stopped showing. After several hours of active googling and attempts to find technical support contacts, it became clear to have no one to talk to, and Google has such problems when their robot cannot find an advertisement for a particular page, or for some reason found this page to be inappropriate There is only one way out - do not put ads on these pages, or change advertiser. Chose the last option.

Yandex.Direct quickly came to replace AdSense. At Yandex, I was very surprised by the need to register and spend all the data and money through CSCs (Partner Service Center), which are completely unknown offices that have no relation to Yandex, but for some reason you can withdraw money only through them. Why I cannot receive money from Yandex myself without employing third parties, I did not understand. Another point that confused in Yandex is a strange CPC, which ranged from almost zero values ‚Äã‚Äãto several rubles. The nature of such fluctuations remained unsolved.

In any case, Yandex showed approximately the same performance, and its advertising was also sometimes not visible to users. Now we use the following solution - in Yandex.Direct (and in AdSense) there is an option of ‚Äúbackup advertising‚Äù - if Yandex could not show the user an advertisement, then it tries to show Google ads. In this scheme, in almost all cases, advertising is shown as necessary.

In the middle of the month I came across the site Nolix.ru, and decided to try their system - I really liked the concept. I can say that Nolix gives a much greater return than contextual advertising. However, recently they have become more technical problems - advertising is sometimes not shown, the site has nginx errors. Contact the support is impossible, because while the site is lying - the mandatory captcha doesn't work for them :).
