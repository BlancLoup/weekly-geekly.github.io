Another acquisition - Panoramio

Sergey Burkov, director of the Moscow Google Development Center, at the Google Developer Day that is being held today, announced the company's next acquisition. The next asset was the company Panoramio.com, which owns the eponymous site .

Panoramio is a site integrated with Google Earth: it allows users to publish photos of localities on the site and link them to specific coordinates on the satellite map of the Earth, accessible via Google Maps and Google Earth. After moderation (usually takes about a month), one or another published photo becomes available to all users of Google maps.
