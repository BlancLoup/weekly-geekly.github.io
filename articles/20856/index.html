Does it make sense to hire a Hindu php programmer?

Many businessmen notice now high market prices for salaries of programmers. What to do if there is no such money? I decided to analyze the Indian labor market in the direction of "PHP-programmer" and compare it with Russian prices.

Several Indian sites were examined for salaries.
