Some facts about Avito Android development


  • We at Avito pay a lot of attention to mobile applications. And we plan to maintain this trend.
  • The Android team uses Kotlin, which gradually replaces Java in our products.
  • We invest in the development and promotion of engineering practices - CI, CD, Code Review, test automation.
  • An important feature of Android development in Avito is a loosely coupled, scalable architecture that allows several development teams to develop a large project without collisions and respond quickly to the needs of users.
