Snom A150, Snom A100M and D Headset Overview


In continuation of the series of reviews of Snom products, today we will introduce you to three headsets at once: Snom A150, Snom A100M and D.

Snom a150


This wireless DECT headset, like any Handsfree device, is designed so that you can talk on the phone without having to hold it in your hands. This may be necessary during long telephone conversations or conference calls, when constantly keeping the phone near your ear is very tiring. When talking through the headset, you can simultaneously write, enter information into a file on your computer and do any other necessary work. For example, you can warm up, walk, walk to the cooler, coffee machine, etc., the benefit is that the range of the DECT communication inside the premises reaches 25 meters. And to complete the current conversation, answer the next call, decrease or increase the volume - there is no need to go to the base or phone.

From the end of the headset are convenient buttons that allow easy pressing to answer calls and end the conversation, as well as adjust the volume in the headset. In addition to the buttons, there is an LED at the end that signals an incoming call and a low battery charge.


The headset is worn on the rim of the ear, and the earpiece is equipped with an anatomically shaped insert. The package includes several additional ear inserts of different shapes.



The rim-holder configuration is well thought out, and the headset itself is so light (only 25 grams) that the ear does not get tired, even after wearing for hours. When testing, we often had cases when a headset was simply forgotten.


The Snom A150 has several degrees of freedom: first, the ear bezel rotates around its axis:



Secondly, the bar with the microphone rotates on the big round hinge, if desired, it can be turned at least to the back of the head:



Finally, the bar is deflected in a small range closer to or away from the cheek.



In other words, Snom A150 can be customized to suit any structure of the ears and face.


The headset together with the charging station has small dimensions and takes up very little space on the table. And thanks to its shape and high-quality materials, the headset on the docking station also looks pretty.

The microphone at Snom A150 is highly sensitive, equipped with a noise cancellation function. Included is a USB dongle A230 , through which the headset establishes a DECT connection with the sound source. This can be any Snom 3xx or 7xx series telephone, a C520 conference phone or a personal computer.



Snom A100M and A100D


The following headset, which we consider - wired Snom A100 two versions, M and D.


The Snom A100M is a mono wired headset with one earphone. Her weight is only 56 grams. excluding wire. Despite the unpretentious appearance of the headset, the A100M is equipped with a high-quality speaker that delivers high-definition broadband sound.


Comfortable ear cushions of high-quality artificial leather allow you to use the headset throughout the day and not experience discomfort.



The flexible bar rotates 300 degrees, and the position of the microphone can be conveniently and easily customized. The microphone with passive noise reduction provides high-quality transmission of your voice, without interference and extraneous noise.



Included with the headset comes a wire with a Quick Release (QR) 4P4C connector, as well as several QR adapters (for USB, as well as 3.5 and 2.5 mm audiojack), so you are unlikely to have difficulty connecting the headset to phones or conferencing devices.



The Snom 100D differs from the Snom 100M only in the presence of a second speaker and a stereo sound. Yes, the headset occupies both ears, but it is more comfortable at work.
