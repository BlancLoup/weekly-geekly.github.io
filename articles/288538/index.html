The first snow fell and bloggers were the first to report this to the world. How can snow fall and not get on the pages of several million blogs? Unthinkable situation! Snow-covered blogs sparkle from the screens of monitors and dazzle Internet users. So, winter is coming, the New Year, Christmas decorations, the smell of tangerine, confetti litter.

Perhaps the main event of the past week was the launch of Six Apart, a new platform for bloggers called Vox . Let me remind you that Six Apart is the owner of the most popular among Russian-speaking users of the livejournal.com service. There is a logical question: then why another platform? The fact is that, first and foremost, Vox is designed for people aged 25 years and older, because teenagers mostly use the livejournal in America, and only here it has become a kind of cult among the Internet intelligentsia. Therefore, with the launch of the new service, Six Apart is planning to conquer a more adult audience. In fact, there are no fundamental differences from the livejournal. A more advanced security system, you can publish not only text, but also video / audio data. A powerful keyword search as well as convenient import tools from other web services, such as YouTube, have been created. Let's see what will happen next.
