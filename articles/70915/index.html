Usability Marathon 2 - now free and online



Last year, we, UIDesign Group, conducted an educational event for Russian usability specialists . Basically, conferences and educational programs on our discipline are held abroad, and not everyone has the opportunity to attend such events. Therefore, we invited several eminent Western specialists to Moscow, and they held seminars on usability research and interface design . The experience was successful, and we decided to organize the Marathon again.

Unlike last year, this year it will be held in an online format , in the form of a series of webinars. We hope that this format will make the marathon more accessible and open to all interested usability. We invited well-known Western experts with extensive practical experience. In particular, they will talk about usability metrics, how to best prototype in Axure, how to build a strategy to promote usability, how to correctly use statistical methods, will always reveal the actual topic of justification and sale of usability services. In addition, we received confirmation from Kim Goodwin that she will hold her seminar within our Marathon.

Despite the fact that we are organizing a usability marathon primarily for Russian participants, the online format of the event will allow specialists from all over the world to participate in it.

The Marathon itself will be free in English . To participate in the Marathon, you will need to install the appropriate software and register for the selected webinars.

Registration will soon open on the official website of the event . There you can also get acquainted with the program of the marathon and with its participants. On the beginning of the registration report separately.

We are waiting for you at the Marathon!
