Today starts the conference Google I / O

May 19, at 20:00 Moscow time, the next annual conference for developers will open in San Francisco


On it, the Internet giant is expected to make a number of important announcements of new products and services.

The themes of the Android conference, App Engine, Chrome, Google Web Toolkit, OpenSocial, Google AJAX APIs and of course Google Wave. The main theme of the conference will undoubtedly become the operating system for Android mobile devices. It is expected that developers will finally be shown in action the next version of the system - 2.2 (Froyo). Froyo is a dessert made from frozen yogurt. It is rumored that Sony is going to show TVs with support for a specialized version of Android, codenamed Dragonpoint, controlled by a special Atom processor prepared by Intel for teleproblems.

Google has taken care of those who wish to virtually participate in this event. They will be able to do this during the opening hours of the first and second day of the conference. Video broadcast of these two speeches will be held on www.youtube.com/GoogleDevelopers in the public domain for everyone.

First day - May 19, 20: 00-21: 30 (Moscow time)
The second day is May 20, 19: 30-21: 00.

For more information about the conference, please visit: http://code.google.com/intl/ru-RU/events/io/2010/
