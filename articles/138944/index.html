The line of smartphones HTC One officially presented

The night before in Barcelona on the eve of the opening of the MWC 2012, HTC introduced a new product line HTC One. Particular attention was paid to the technologies that formed the basis of the new version of HTC Sense. It is no secret that one of the most frequent actions that mobile phone users perform is listening to music and taking photos. With the help of Beats Audio and HTC ImageSense technologies, consumer characteristics of new smartphones have reached a whole new level.


HTC ImageSense


The heart of the HTC ImageSense technology is the HTC ImageChip, a special image processor. Thanks to ultra-fast autofocus, HTC One smartphones allow you to take a snapshot in just 0.7 seconds and continue to take an unlimited number of photos without releasing the shutter button.

The CMOS sensor of the camera is made using backlight technology (BSI), through which you can create high-quality images in the dark. The HTC One X and HTC One S cameras use an optical system with an F / 2.0 aperture, which allows you to capture 40% more light than standard F / 2.4. Also, smartphones support HDR technology.

The VideoPic function will allow you to take photos directly during video recording, and integration with Dropbox will help you synchronize photos from 25GB in the cloud right there.

HTC One X


The HTC One X smartphone combines recognizable details of the classic HTC design. The case of the device is made of a single piece of polycarbonate, which reduced the weight of the smartphone to 130 grams (against 162 for Sensation XL). The heart of the phone is System-On-Chip nVidia Tegra 3 with a quad core processor architecture Cortex A9 1.5 GHz. The fifth core of Tegra 3 is used to perform low-priority tasks and improve the power consumption of the device.


720P HD-display device is made by technology Super LCD2 and protected by glass 2.5D Corning Gorilla Glass. The internal memory of the smartphone is expanded to 32GB (26GB available to the user), RAM - 1GB.



HTC One X is equipped with wireless NFC, Bluetooth 4.0 and Wireless HDMI. The 8 megapixel camera of the device is capable of recording video in 1080P resolution, the front camera is 1.3MPx. Battery capacity - 1800 mAh. Sim card format - microSIM.

HTC One S


HTC One S is made of a single piece of metal and is the thinnest smartphone of the company - only 7.9 mm. The device works on the basis of the new System-On-Chip Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 with a dual-core 1.5GHz processor.

The 4.3 "qHD-display of the smartphone is made using Super AMOLED technology and is protected by Gorilla Glass. The internal memory of the device is 16GB (12GB is available to the user), RAM is 1GB.



HTC One S is equipped with Bluetooth 4.0 wireless interfaces and Wireless HDMI. The 8 megapixel camera of the device is capable of recording video in 1080P resolution, the front camera is VGA. Battery capacity - 1650 mAh. Sim card format - microSIM.

HTC One V


HTC One V will appeal to fans of the HTC Legend. The smartphone runs on a 1GHz single-core processor and includes a 3.7 "WVGA display. The device's built-in memory is 4GB (1GB available to the user), RAM is 512MB. Among other things, HTC One S and HTC One V support GLONASS.



HTC One smartphones will appear in the Russian retail in April. Price will be announced later. HTC One X will be available in a gray and white case. HTC One S - dark metallic, gray, black. HTC One V - gray and black.

Full specifications are available on the official website .
