Brainstorming on marker boards

When a whole group of people is working on one project, various forms of organizing a collective search for a solution are used. We prefer brainstorming on marker boards. The usual composition of the working group in Nimax: manager, art director, designer. If necessary, other employees are connected: web-technologist, illustrator, photographer, etc. The boards after the discussions are a pretty funny sight - we have long wanted to show them, which we are doing.




In general, any employee can participate in such an interesting undertaking, the only condition is that you have all the necessary information on the project. According to the rules of brainstorming, participants can express any ideas, including obviously absurd ones - the task is to move their heads. The key to a successful search is a tough selection of ideas for compliance with the goals and objectives of the project.

Everything happens in several stages: for a start, the team is loaded with information, then the first meeting of the group is held, at which all the questions raised are asked, then assaults are arranged, usually in several stages.
