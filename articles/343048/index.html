Free PBX for 1 year, overview webinars, training course in Moscow and completion of 3CX v14 support

Friends, this week will not be a purely technical topic. Instead, we will make some important announcements that we hope many of you will find helpful.

Free PBX 3CX with Google Cloud hosting for 1 year


We have prepared for customers and partners a unique offer on the market - a free PBX with the same free hosting on Google Cloud for a period of 1 year.

This offer will allow you to launch and use the 3CX PBX without any obligations very quickly. And then, when you see that the system suits you and works smoothly - you can expand the free license key to a commercial one and continue to use the PBX further. It is recommended to transfer the configuration to your own cloud (or your own cloud account in Google), a local server, or a mini-PC (if you have a relatively small organization).

If you are a 3CX partner, activate the system by entering your partner ID. Thus, at the installation stage, this installation (potential buyer) will be formally assigned to your partner account. After some time you will be able to offer the client the transition to the commercial version.

What does free PBX from 3CX include?


  • Unlimited users (extensions)
  • 1 year of free hosting on Google Cloud (provided by Google cloud for 3CX customers)
  • One year of free system updates, including upgrading to a new version.
  • 5 3CX WebMeeting Video Conferencing Participants
  • Connecting your SIP trunks and gateways, i.e. phone numbers in the organization
  • 3CX software clients for iOS, Android, Windows and MacOS
  • Presence indication, user status, chat, web and video-on-demand conferences and other UC functions
  • Advanced system security features, blocking all known SIP attacks
  • Simple and fast installation through an online wizard in 5 steps.

Terms of use of free PBX 3CX


If you do not use a PBX for 30 days or make less than 5 calls per month, we reserve the right to schedule your system to save resources. Also, this proposal is relevant only for new users of 3CX. Survey webinars 3CX in Russian


In November, we begin a series of webinars in Russian for users interested in upgrading their telecommunications system to a modern IP telephony system. On small 30-minute webinars, 3CX Zero Admin semi-automatic administration technology, connection of IP-phones, external lines and other basic information, which helps to orient in the product, are considered. At the end of the webinar will be a live demonstration of the system, where you can appreciate the ease of management. At the demonstration you will see:

  • Create user or extension
  • Adding a SIP trunk (your phone number)
  • Installing 3CX software clients, including clients for smartphones
  • 3CX control interface
  • New 3CX web client - the main tool of the user
  • Quickly create a web conference

At the end of the webinar, you can ask questions and get answers directly from a 3CX employee. Webinars are held on Thursdays.

Completion of the 3CX v14 life cycle


If you are still using 3CX v14, we recommend switching to the new version of 3CX v15.5 as soon as possible. Currently, v14 support has already been discontinued and Webmeeting video conferencing servers for this version are disabled. And from January 1, 2018, support for 3CX v14 software clients for PCs and smartphones will be discontinued. Note that the validity of SSL certificates for the work of the technology Push in iOS has already expired.

We remind you that if you have a current subscription for updates, you can upgrade to the latest version for free. And if your subscription is already expired, you can upgrade to the 3CX annual license of the latest version of the same edition and capacity that you had.
