Neofab Legion II - the world's brightest flashlight with 1 LED



Neofab Legion II - the brightest flashlight with one LED in the world. Of course, one cannot say that the 742 lumens given to them are shocking. For comparison, an incandescent lamp with a power of 60 watts emits 680-730 lumens, and the usual high-quality LED flashlights give about 550 lumens. But the trick is that it uses only one LED, which will last at least fifty times longer than this light bulb (guaranteed 40 thousand hours versus 750).

At maximum brightness (and it is, of course, regulated) the battery charge lasts for 135 minutes. The flashlight weighs 500 grams (you can actually light someone up if you wish).

The full test report of the lamp is here (eng), and the device itself is sold here for $ 179 (aluminum case).
