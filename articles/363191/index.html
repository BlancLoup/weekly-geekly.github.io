Sony SmartWatch 3 in stores soon

We're glad to announce that the new generation of smartwatch SmartWatch 3 will be on sale in the coming days! The device is running the latest Android Wear operating system and works closely with the LifeLog application, which allows you to keep detailed statistics of your actions, whether it be sports, an evening walk or watching a movie on a tablet screen.





The watch is equipped with a bright 1.6-inch transflective display with a resolution of 320x240, a gyroscope, an accelerometer, a GPS module, a compass and a microphone, thanks to which you can control SmartWatch 3 using voice commands. Thanks to the built-in memory, which amounts to 4 GB, the device is quite autonomous - you can use it separately from your smartphone, for example, while jogging, listening to music via bluetooth headphones and storing all the necessary statistics for further synchronization with the LifeLog application. In addition, as with the previous models, SmartWatch 3 supports third-party applications that can be found on Google Play. The device is protected from water and dust in accordance with IP68.

Recommended price SmartWatch 3 - 8990 rubles. With a review of the gadget you can find out soon.
