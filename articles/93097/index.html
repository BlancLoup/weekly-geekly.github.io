All Points Bulletin - key distribution for beta testing



Realtime Worlds hands out 7,000 keys to closed beta testing.
Link

APB (All Points Bulletin) is a massively multiplayer online game in the action genre for Windows. The plot of the game is based on the confrontation of two parties: the police and the criminals. The player can choose any of the parties. The development is headed by David Jones, the creator of the original GTA. The project is being developed by Realtime Worlds.

Good-natured Electronic Arts has become the publisher of the MMO-action APB (PC, Xbox 360), the creation of which is not the first year the studio Realtime Worlds, noted in the past not bad game Crackdown. Together with this solemn news, APB quite unexpectedly had an approximate release date - hard workers from Realtime Worlds plan to send APB to store shelves early next year. One moment - this only applies to the PC version of the game. Unfortunately, the 360-adaptation of APB was not mentioned by developers, which means that we only dream about the synchronous release of the game on computers and consoles.
