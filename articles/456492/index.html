Troll Army



There are ordinary trolls. They have fun in the comments to the news and articles, entertain people and enrich the discussion with emotions, often sweet and harmless. They are on their own and act in their own interests. And there are others who stand under the flags of unknown forces, they are attracted by the brilliance of gold, they are merciless and ready to destroy everything in their path. Their whole horde ... element controlled by other people's interests.

Paid trolling (wiki)
Paid trolling - actions of organized groups of Internet users, for a monetary reward involved in the formation of public opinion in a certain direction and the manipulation of public opinion on the Internet. They are usually used for political purposes, often by governments, and have different names depending on the country or organization that they represent.

The following discussion focuses on the trolls acting in the interests of others, and most often in the interests of states. Their goal is to throw dust in the eyes and form a false public opinion on acute events. Such trolling is developing in many countries. In the article Bloomberg Russia is called the master of sports in this business, the key exporter of this tactic and the birthplace of trolling.
