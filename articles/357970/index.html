Second GIS-hackathon "Guessing on the Cards"

On May 19‚Äì20, developers will gather at Innopolis University to create working prototypes of technologies for processing Earth remote sensing data in the field of machine learning and photogrammetry. The prize fund is 400,000 rubles. Applications for participation are accepted until May 18.




For their project, participants will choose one of 6 areas of competition:

  • Agriculture: yield forecasting and vegetation monitoring;
  • Monitoring of protected zones: oil pipelines, power lines - search for violations and prevention of accidents;
  • Oil production: field modeling;
  • Navigation and maps: using machine learning to identify new building sites, determining the type of road marking coverage;
  • City and environment: functional zoning and defining the boundaries of residential and land;
  • Photogrammetric data processing: processing of raw space or aerial photographs to obtain final products, such as terrain models, terrain models, vector 3D models of cities, orthophotoplans.

The authors of the best creative project will receive 200,000 rubles, for the solution of cases from Exact Farming and Gazpromneft companies there are special prizes - 100,000 rubles each. NVIDIA will provide winners with access to the NVIDIA DLI Deep Learning Institute educational platform. Also, following the results of the competition, promising teams will be offered a partnership with Innopolis University.

Applications are accepted from teams of 3-5 people and individual developers.
