The first results of the championship developer Devcup 2013: the winners in nominations



With Evernote, you can do a million things, but Evernote itself is only one application and cannot cope with everything. Fortunately, our active community of developers and designers constantly generates ideas, applications and services that extend the capabilities of Evernote for users.

Every year we can see a surge of creative activity of this community when we start the competition for developers - Evernote Devcup . This year, the teams presented over 180 applications and services.

To mark the best works presented at the Devcup, we have provided awards in several nominations corresponding to the popular scenarios for using Evernote, as well as several awards for technical and design achievements. These apps have been awarded for great new features for Evernote users. They serve as good examples of what the Evernote platform is capable of.



Each of the teams that have won any of the nominations will receive invitations to our third annual EC3 conference . There, teams will have the opportunity to talk with our developers and designers and present their projects to journalists and investors. In addition, each nomination winner will receive a $ 1,000 loan from Amazon Web Services to provide hosting for their growing projects.

We are pleased to present you the winners in all nominations of Devcup 2013:

Better integration with Evernote Business: SmartTM




SmartTM , Poland - Watch the video

SmartTM is a task organizer built according to the GTD methodology. SmartTM is tightly integrated with Evernote, including reminders and business notebooks. SmartTM is a powerful and feature-rich solution designed specifically for business users.

Design Achievements: Bubble Browser




Bubble Browser , Poland - Watch the video

Bubble Browser is an application that allows you to visualize your Evernote notebook. The solution offers a beautiful visual search for your account, offering an original way to find information and links between your data in Evernote, which you could not find without the application.

Better technical integration: Hop.in




Hop.in , Chile - Watch the video

Hop.in is a mobile browser and web clipper that allows you to copy your favorite content on the Internet and synchronize it with Evernote. At the same time, Hop.in allows users to determine exactly what information on the page they want to copy, and select individual multimedia elements to be sent to Evernote. Hop.in's smart copy technology has made the product a winner in the nomination for the best technical integration of this year.

Education and Research: biscuit

Family: BabyWaa




BabyWaa , South Korea - Watch Video

BabyWaa is a multiplatform application that allows parents to record moments in the life of newborns. The functionality of the application allows you to share photos and information on social networks, and save selected points in Evernote for safe storage for the future.

Food and cooking: Grocerytrip




Grocerytrip , USA - Watch the video

Grocerytrip is a smart mobile organizer for shopping lists. When copying recipes and other information from the Internet, Grocerytrip automatically determines the ingredients you need to buy and generates a shopping list that is sniffing from Evernote. So, by the next trip to the store or to the market you will have everything at hand.

Paperless Future: Lightly




Lightly , Hong Kong - Watch Video

Lightly is a mobile web clipper and text extractor application that allows users to save interested information from the Internet to Evernote. Convenient copying and the ability to mark important points of the text in Lightly provide an extra reason to refuse to print interesting articles to read and mark with a marker.

Photography: Cooliris




Cooliris , USA - Watch the video

Cooliris is a photo browser with a web version and an application for mobile devices. It syncs with Evernote and other cloud platforms, allowing users to easily share photos using the built-in chat.

Productivity: Context Booster




Context Booster , Germany - Watch the video

Context Booster is a smart helper when working in Evernote. The application searches the Internet for information related to your Evernote notes. With it, you can fill in your notebooks with additional data related to the notes that you create, which makes the application an excellent assistant for research and writing.

It is integrated with Evernote and allows you to synchronize notebooks dedicated to various trips. What's next?
