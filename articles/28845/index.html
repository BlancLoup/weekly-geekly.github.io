AppStore opened

Today, Apple has updated iTunes to version 7.7 in which access to the AppStore has appeared . But the best news is that Russia is on the official list of countries.

The AppStore now has a huge selection of programs and games for the iPhone and iPodTouch. As you can see, for the time being, all this diversity cannot be established, but tomorrow, with the output of the new firmware, everything will change.

All programs in the App Store are neatly sorted into categories, which greatly simplifies navigation.

On the AppStore main page lists of the most popular programs, the new software and the most actively gaining popularity software are displayed.

PS Meanwhile, the iPhone 3G is already filling the store shelves .
