GTA fans shot a GTA-like video using a drone



Grand Theft Auto series has long been a super-popular series. Millions of players do mafia tasks every day, enjoying the gameplay. Now, with the release of the fifth part, the online world has appeared in the toy, GTA online. The graphics here are more and more perfect, and the gameplay is more and more realistic.

However, there are still fans of the previous versions of the game, which were developed in 2D. For fans of the previous parts of the day, the video was shot by the same fans. The video was shot with the help of a drone, the authors of the video tried to realize the camera angle, similar to what we see in 2D versions of the toy.



Once about the same thing was realized by director David Fincher, in the movie "Zodiac". But there was a big budget and a lot of contract specialists, a helicopter and everything else.

The guys who shot the GTA 2 Live video had nothing but friends, a drone and a desire to do something similar. And after all, the video turned out to be very similar to what we saw in GTA 2 (those who saw can appreciate it).
