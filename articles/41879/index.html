Saga of high technologies in Russian public education

Recently my mother called me (she is a teacher in a regular secondary school) and started crying to explain that again she cannot fill out a control report on algebra for 8th grade. What I saw on her computer, upon arrival, so shocked me that I could not resist writing a malicious post about our education.

Then a lot of screenshots and quotes.

All this is called the StatGrad Educational Statistics Processing System . You can read more in black and lilac on the official website .

In short, to send data on a test, the teacher should do the following:
  1. Enter the site and enter the login and password (which is constantly changing)
  2. Download the .xls file to your computer (for some reason it is saved in a poppy with a double .xls.xla extension)
  3. Score all students and grades for k / p (grading system is a separate topic)
  4. Enter into the special field the additional ‚Äúlogin in the StatGrad system‚Äù
  5. Export data to .csv format
  6. Go to another page StatGrad-a. Enter login / password again and send .csv file
