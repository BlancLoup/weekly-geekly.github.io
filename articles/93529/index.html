Wow64 and Silverlight Dump Analysis

I wrote a complicated form on Silverlight and when I finished writing, I was horrified to find that I had a memory leak somewhere. I didn‚Äôt worry, but took a few 3.5 gigabyte dumps and wanted to find out in WinDbg what was really the case, but SOS persistently sent me to any of the teams.

The fact is that Silverlight is x86, so as soon as we load the dump into WinDbg x86, we must clearly say that we will work in 32-bit mode.

Magic word:
! wow64exts.sw

Example:
.loadby sos coreclr
.chain
! wow64exts.sw

We continue to work as usual.
