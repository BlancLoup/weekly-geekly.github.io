Secrets of self-development, or How to become a happy victim of success

Success does not need to chase - paradoxically, but he himself comes to those who deserve it. So how to become the person who attracts success? This is a good question, and the answer is not as obvious as it may seem.



The right bait for goldfish


Many would immediately advise you to work hard, plan your next steps, and persevere toward your goal. Yes, of course, one cannot do without it, but let's remember how many of us are really hard at work, dream and struggle to make our dreams come true, but the cherished luck bird does not hurry to appear on the horizon. Something is missing, something we have forgotten ... Missed factors do not allow all our efforts to develop into a path that will lead us to success.

And it always seemed to me that we are pursuing success like prehistoric hunters pursuing a mammoth. But it turned out that in the pursuit of success, homo sapiens cannot even think that, if we are worthy of success, he himself will accompany us, being a logical consequence of all our actions. So everything is just the opposite - this success should haunt us. After all, promising prospects and opportunities are what you attract to yourself. We just need to work on making the bait worthwhile.



People are our everything


In order for Mrs. Luck to become interested in you, you cannot leave anything to the good of chance. It is necessary to cultivate, and this should be done in the right direction. Alas, as practice shows, it is not enough to be a class specialist, you need to be an attractive person who, like a magnet, attracts others to him, and with them the cherished keys to achieving goals. No matter how trite it sounds, success is people. It is they who create opportunities, they can be the key to victory or defeat.

In today's world, information and connections are valued most. Yes, it is communication that should never be neglected. And so you need to work on yourself, develop all the skills that you already have, and acquire new ones, tirelessly polish every facet of your character, learn to communicate with people, look after your physical health, and mental harmony - then you will become a person who will attract others. Your reputation will speak for itself - you will find people who are happy to want to build a business with you.
