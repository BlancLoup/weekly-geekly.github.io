Specialties, the study of which almost guarantees you a job in the United States in the future

Hello friends. Today, I would like to post an article translated from US College News and Rankings about which specialties will be promising for the next nn years in the United States and which are guaranteed to give you employment opportunities in the country - due to the fact that most specialties are on the STEM list .

1. Biomedical Engineering / Bioengineering.

People working at the intersection of life science, engineering and medicine can be confident in a stable employment outlook, as well as enjoying all the pleasures of work. The US Bureau of Labor Statistics gave 62% of the increase in student employment between 2010 and 2020, who study at Biomedical Engineering at American universities.

2. Biometrics / Biometrics.

This specialty teaches students how to build automated identification devices, such as face recognition systems. The very scope of the biometric industry is expected to grow to $ 363 million by 2018, according to market research.

3. Forensic Science / Forensics.

Forensic students learn how to use technology to analyze evidence of crime. As technology develops much faster than universities prepare graduates, the profession of a criminalist is becoming more and more needed.

4. Computer Game Design.

The global market for video and online games is expected to reach $ 82 billion by 2017, according to DFC Intelligence research. To succeed, the student needs to learn animation, audio design, programming, and production management skills.

6. Data Science / Data processing and analysis.

Science draws data from data in various fields, including mathematics and information technology. 7. Business Analytics / Business Analytics.
