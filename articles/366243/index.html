Selected 100 candidates for the colonizers of Mars

Selected 100 candidates for a flight to Mars within the framework of Mars One. Of these, select 40 travelers. The colonists will live without sex, alcohol, food, relatives and family, and return a ticket to Earth.



Mars One is a private project involving a flight to Mars and the establishment of a colony on the "red planet" with the broadcast of what is happening on television. The flight will take seven months.

Astronaut selection started in 2013. 202586 people applied for participation, out of which 700 were selected in the first round, and after the second round only 50 men and 50 women left. A list of candidates is available on the project website. Of these, make groups to give the opportunity to demonstrate the ability to work in a team. As a result of the selection, sixty more participants will be eliminated from the race.

All candidates will take a ten-year training program to identify their abilities to act in emergency situations, versatility and the ability to work together with colleagues. Only after that they will be allowed to fly, from which there is no turning back. Flight start is scheduled for 2024.
