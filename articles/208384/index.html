The official openSUSE forum is hacked


Again VBulletin, again data leakage, again deface. This time, the hacker with the pseudonym " H4x0r HuSsY " faced a Linux distribution forum , namely the official openSUSE forum .

In recent months, owners of forums based on the VBulletin engine are indignant, hacking is carried out one by one. It all started with MacRumors, which was cracked in November 2013, then vbulletin.com itself was hacked , and now the openSUSE user forum.
Reportedly, there may be a data breach of 79,500 accounts. The page with the deface is still alive, the feeling that the owners are not aware of the things going on with their website: forums.opensuse.org/vbseo_sitemap
If you do not have time, you can always look here .

Let me remind you that the forum is built on version 4.2.1 , as evidenced by the copyright in the CSS file, which is vulnerable.
If you have an account on this forum and the password is used elsewhere, change it .
