ILook platform


In the previous post, we noted that the iLook Media Center allows you to read not only news from the usual RSS feeds, but also create your own thematic streams (by search words and / or sources), add existing streams and customize the reading of social media tapes.
This functionality was made possible thanks to the existing technology platform iLook, using which the media center receives information feeds.
The platform API we have created will be open to developers of third-party applications, so a couple of words about the platform.

The iLook platform in real time collects, stores and multifactor analysis of large amounts of social media data: Twitter, VKontakte, LiveJournal, LiveInternet, YouTube, thousands of blogs, forums, online media. iLook stores an archive of over 3 billion messages, daily receipts - about 10 million messages per day.

The core of the system is a set of tools and technologies that allow solving a large-scale spectrum of modern tasks of the Online Big Data & Analytics class based on information provided in social media and publicly accessible digital data storages.

In addition to collecting and storing social media publications, the iLook platform performs statistical processing of incoming information and builds ratings by the number of comments (for news, blog posts and videos on YouTube) and by the number of retweets and hashtags for Russian-language Twitter.

A complete list of sources, as well as statistics on the number and dynamics of incoming messages is available on the site .

Based on the platform, it is possible to create recommendation and information services, such as user ratings of films or books, and such large-scale ones as our Media Center iLook;)
