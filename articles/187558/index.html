Why not stop suffering on trifles?


Second time rule


If the annoying trifle happened to you for the first time - this is an annoying trifle. You will forget about her in a minute, she no longer affects your life. If the annoying trifle happened to you a second time - this means that your work process somewhere is built incorrectly. From this point on, you will be annoyed that you have not dealt with the trifle last time, there is a fear that the trifle will hinder you again (and it most likely will really hurt). In your life there is another task that puts pressure on your shoulders, distracts your thoughts, interferes. The second occurrence of a minor problem is the moment when it is necessary to begin to deal with it.

Two minutes rule


The well-known rule of GTD - if you are 100% sure that the problem can be solved in no more than 2 minutes - do it right now. Even if there are more urgent matters, even if the task is a trifle, even if it can be done later. Even once you postpone this task will cost you more - you need to remember / write it down, then remember, and most likely it will remind you of yourself at the most inopportune moment, nerves again, again the decision what to do - damn it all! Less than two minutes - doing now.
