Kryptonomicon


Publishers: AST, Keeper, 2007
By Neil Stephenson
Hardcover, 912 pp.
Book on ozon.ru , books.ru

The book is a classic cyberpunk, written in accessible language, covering the events of the Second World War and the Internet boom - the mid-nineties.
Only in this book you can read about Turing's youth, see the war with your own eyes, read about Van Eyck's interception and learn about cryptography.
