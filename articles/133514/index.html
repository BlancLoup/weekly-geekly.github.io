Yandex acquired SPB Software, a mobile product development company.

Moscow, November 28, 2011. Yandex (NASDAQ: YNDX) acquired SPB Software, a leading developer of software for mobile device manufacturers and mobile operators. SPB Software offers a wide range of mobile products, including a three-dimensional interface for smartphones and tablet computers.

Thus, Yandex begins to provide vendors with an important component of any mobile platform - an interface that includes access to key services. This will allow Yandex to work more closely with many companies in the mobile industry.

The system shell SPB Shell 3D, developed by SPB Software, will be integrated with cloud services and Yandex technologies. In addition, Yandex will be able to offer users convenient access to its services, including search, through mobile devices from various manufacturers.
