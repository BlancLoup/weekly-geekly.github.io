Gratitude works wonders, or how to love your work.

Sometimes a person can be brought to such a state when the only way to avoid a nervous breakdown is a change of activity to the completely opposite. And this story is about how I could find peace in madness. Although I was fond of computers since childhood, but having a talent in another field, I took the path: languages, sales, VIP service.

Having received a diploma from a VIP-service humanities specialist, I did not find anything else but how to get a job at a local wholesale company as a regional sales manager. The company is good, but taking into account the specifics of the goods, we had to communicate with a large number of people who considered it their duty to cheat, to be rude.

Every day began with about 50-60 cold calls, then meetings followed, and then the customers themselves called, dissatisfied with the state in which the products were sent to them. Claims were to me, but in fact these were the problems of our warehouse and transport company.

Exit problem

Having worked for six months in this company and accumulated savings for the purchase of a car, I raised enough money to realize my dream: to open my content project, which was supposed to give me financial freedom and happiness.

As many people have, I overestimated growth and scaling opportunities too much and struggled for a whole year to reach a level of self-sufficiency. Then, due to the nature of the project, I came up against the level of profit, and now I knew exactly what I needed for further development and income growth. But it all came down to a significant amount of money that was needed for development.
