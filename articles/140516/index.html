New Generation SIM Cards

The German company Giesecke & Devrient (G & D) last year introduced a new version of the SIM card, which is called nano-SIM . The size of nano-SIM cards is approximately 12x9 mm. And this is about 15% less than microSIM, which are already used in the Apple iPhone and Nokia Lumia smartphones.




The European Institute for Standardization in Telecommunications ( ETSI ) will vote next week on the choice of a new standard for SIM-cards. The meeting will consider proposals from two major manufacturers of smartphones - Apple and Nokia, media reported.

In the past, Apple advocated the abolition of sim-cards as such, but was forced to retreat under the pressure of cellular operators receiving part of the proceeds from their sale. However, a new generation of sims forced the company to register six of its European divisions in Cannes for ETSI membership on Tuesday. Any unit with a revenue of more than 8 billion euros can count on 45 or less votes.

Apple is struggling hard with its competitors - Motorola Mobility, RIM and Nokia - in order to adopt even more miniature nano-sim sim cards as an industry standard.
