Top three car racing

How to occupy hands away from home or work if they are already busy with an iPhone. The answer is simple download and play games. I love racing. Honestly, I was quite skeptical about the first races for iPhone, because for good races you need at least buttons, and better a steering wheel with pedals. The iPhone itself is a steering wheel, my constant hero will object - an attentive reader. Now imagine that you have to hold the steering wheel in your car, not attached to anything, and twist it like that. Strange! So the game developers and Apple itself offer to do just that.

Need for speed undercover




EA just couldn't do badly. EA did well, only very arcade. The good old days of racing are back again: the car is driving along a track, the track is only slightly changing direction, and it is completely impossible to turn onto an adjoining street. But you can go into drift, slow down the course of time, buy new cars, do their tuning. Dynamic and exciting gameplay provided. The main advantage of NFSU is amazing graphics. Against the background of other games, it seems that EA just stepped over the edge of the possible. Before NFSU, no one had such graphics.



Bottom line: exciting arcade racing for 4-5 days.

Fastlane street racing




This is the first normal race appeared in the AppStore. All previous had a complete bullshit. Very difficult, but no less fascinating race. A lot of cars, though completely unreal, though remotely resembling the appearance of some very expensive sports cars. On the other hand, where will independent developers get the money for licensing real models? However, all machines are significantly different from each other.

In the main game mode - arcade, as usual you are chasing opponents along various tracks that open as you progress through the game. In the second mode, you yourself become an opponent: you have to drive the route without touching the fences, have time to drive the required number of laps and catch up with your opponent. All for a while.

At the very beginning it is necessary to suffer literally a couple of hours, get used to the management, get into the spirit of the game and provide you with several weeks of exciting races. For those who want to feel the game for free, there is a light version .



Bottom line: cool race with interesting physics for a couple of weeks.
