Forbes rating is now on the "Rambler"

On August 1, the Russian edition of Forbes and Rambler presented a joint project - an online version of the rating of Russian celebrities. On the rating pages, you can vote for a particular star.

But the voting results do not greatly affect the place of celebrity in the ranking. Each of the positions is determined by more significant factors: revenues (total amount of honorarium payments), press attention (number of mentions in publications), public interest (number of Internet searches). In the current year, in compiling the ranking, requests made by users of the Rambler were taken into account.
