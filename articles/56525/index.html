Abaco Primo: nettop for only 99 euros!

The Italian company Abaco, announced the release of a new nettop Abaco Primo. According to the characteristics, this computer does not stand out among other computers of this class. But the price makes it very attractive - only 99 euros (including VAT).


For these relatively little money, the user has at their disposal a system with a 1.6-GHz Intel Atom 230 processor, Intel GMA 950 graphics, 512 MB of DDR2-667 memory, expandable up to 2 GB, 4 GB flash drive, sound class subsystem HD Audio based on Realtek ALC662 controller, Fast Ethernet controller. To fit into a tight price framework, the manufacturer decided to use the Linux XUbuntu distribution instead of Windows.



To get Abaco Primo at a price of 99 euros, the user will have to assemble a PC with his own hands. Ready assembled computer will cost more by 12.5 euros. Also offers a configurator with which you can select additional options and change the configuration.
