The head of Google commented on the launch of a search engine censored in China



The management of Google Corporation for the first time commented on the situation with the launch of a search service with censorship in China. A statement regarding the new project of the company was made by Google CEO Sundar Pichai. He said that the corporation is trying to establish long-term relations with this country.

The project itself was named Project Dragonfly, for the first time it became known about it in August, after The Intercept online resource published information about a certain search service from Google with censorship.
