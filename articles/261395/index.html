Oracle invites information technology professionals to the free webinar "Choosing a Transportation Management Systems class information system"

Today, customers of information solutions ask especially many questions about how to choose a TMS system, who should choose it, what functionality should be paid attention to in the selection process and can transport logistics management be transferred to the cloud?
