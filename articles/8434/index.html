Social networks - the fattest chronophagus

Watching TV too much? This is because you haven't tried social networks yet. Here is the fattest chronophagus, which is best able to devour the time of a person on the Internet. It is social networks that pose the greatest threat to television. This conclusion was made by researchers from the company eMarketer. They analyzed how the habits of people who first entered the social network have changed. The results are shown in the diagram.



As you can see, 32% of users who get into the social network increase the time spent on the Internet. In addition, people are beginning to increasingly use e-mail, IM and mobile phones. Naturally, time is not taken from anywhere. Peculiar "donors" are television and video games.

The American, who is online social networks, watches TV less on average two hours a week.
