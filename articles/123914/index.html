The fight for air conditioning!


You know, in my department all non-smokers and hard workers. Therefore, we usually do not go to smoke, and very rarely spend time on empty conversations. In a word - conscientious employees. But with the onset of summer heat, and with it the stifling nature, it became impossible to work normally in rooms without air conditioning and airing. I think many will agree with me. On my own well-being and well-being of my colleagues, I notice that after 2 hours, thoughts start to get confused, and the stuffiness to take away attention. You have to go out once an hour for 10 minutes to get some air. But putting a hand on my heart, I will say that, it does not help: (Yes, and does not allow to concentrate on returning to complex tasks.

More than once I tried to defend the purchase of air conditioning from investors, but to no avail. Maybe because I acted emotionally.
But tomorrow, and each subsequent day, I decided to continue to defend the acquisition of a normal air conditioner.
And I will do it like this:

New Day = New Argument

and until they buy!

Help me collect arguments for every day. I want to try the remaining 20 days of this month to persistently and convincingly seek victory and achieve it in the end!

For example, for tomorrow I prepared the following argument:

if all employees of the department (6) go out 6 times for 10 minutes, then the company will lose 180 hours a month. Considering that the average earnings of a programmer in our company is 40,000, we get a loss of this amount.


I beg you to help me in this quest and write your comments with possible arguments that I can apply. I promise not to compromise and not to take enemies into captivity :)
