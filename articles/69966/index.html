Russian supercar "Marussia"


Since June, a new Russian supercar Marussia has been developed and is being produced. The project manager is Nikolai Fomenko and Efim Ostrovsky.
According to the official website, the main innovation of this car is a universal chassis, which is designed in such a way that you can put on it several completely different body styles.
As the power unit is now used V-shaped 3.5-liter "six" Cosworth (245 horsepower and 330 newton meters of torque), working in tandem with a 5-speed manual.
Also worth noting is the fact that quite a lot of computer technology is integrated into the interior of the car. In particular, the on-board computer will work under Linux-based OS, and passengers will be able to use the following functions: WI-FI, WiMax (Yota), Bluetooth, GPS navigation, TV, Radio, DVD, iPod, Skype.
It is also reported that the car can be serviced at any Renault or Nissan service center.
