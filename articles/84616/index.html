Award for 10 billion downloads from iTunes

Apple is preparing to celebrate the 10 billionth anniversary of the number of downloads from iTunes. The user who is lucky enough to initiate the anniversary transaction will receive an Apple gift certificate in the amount of $ 10,000.

For this amount, you can buy goods in Apple stores or sell a certificate on eBay. Perhaps he will be considered a rarity and will be bought even more expensive than the nominal.

A 17-year-old teenager from Michigan won a similar prize on February 23, 2006 for his billionth download from iTunes. In addition to the $ 10,000 certificate, he received ten iPods, an iMac and a phone call from Steve Jobs.

To qualify for a prize, you do not have to buy something, you can simply fill out a special form on the site (this can be done up to 25 times a day).
