Online conference "Growth of the mobile applications market and its development prospects"

Want to develop mobile apps? Think on which platform it is better to do? What directions are the most promising? How much can you earn and how to enter the foreign market? Without leaving your home, join an online conference. Mobile technology experts will answer these and many other questions.

On June 13, 2012, the Ingria Business Incubator, in conjunction with the Web Ready competition, will hold an online conference on the topic: Growth of the mobile application market and its development prospects.

We invite all those involved in the development of mobile applications on different platforms, the leaders of companies in the field of mobile technologies and all who are interested in this topic to participate.

Format: Video conference is an event where several speakers can discuss issues and problems together, focusing on a group of up to several thousand participants on the Internet. The service runs from a regular Internet browser, does not require installation and IT staff.

Online conferences will be held with the support of RIA Novosti, on the site - spb.ria.ru

Questions:
- The market of mobile applications in Russia, numbers, facts, prospects;
- The choice of mobile application development platform; cross-platform development;
- Mobile applications for media and magazines, games and useful utilities. What direction is most promising?
- Mobile commerce. Applications as a tool to increase sales. Russian and foreign cases;
- Publishing successful applications of Russian developers in foreign markets.

These and other questions will be answered by participants in Moscow:
- Leonid Bugaev, creative director of Nordic Agency AB
- Anna Grigorieva, Marketing Director, Directorate of Electronic Media, Seven Days Publishing House
- Andrei Moroz, AdWired Director
- Representative Digit.ru

Participants in Petersburg:
- Evgenia Barchenko, project consultant, Business Incubator Ingria
- Andrey Verbin, CEO of Studio Mobile
- Kirill Petrov, one of the company's founders, head of i-Free Innovations
- Anton Rybakov, CEO of JoyBits Mobile

Conference time: 17: 00-19: 00

Anyone can ask their question by leaving a comment below, or right now send by mail i.kuznetsova@ingria-park.ru

Prepare your questions, the discussion promises to be interesting!
