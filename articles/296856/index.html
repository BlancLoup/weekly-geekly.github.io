How to achieve this effect? Here are 5 cool life hacking.


1. The human factor
Write like a friend. That is, a friend who you want to tell you about what a really cool candidate you are. Maybe he does not know about this story. And she doesn't know why you want to get into the company. It is not necessary to imagine Vasya, with whom you sat at the same desk and then grew up together - let it be just a man on the other side of the screen who does not want to read the same letters (like you), tired of working (like you), wants to be a little happier and more successful (like you).

2. Company knowledge, position
We have probably talked about this a hundred times, but still. If you want to immediately declare yourself as a professional and a person who does not have a superficial attitude to finding a job and a specific position in the company, then a certain research is simply necessary.

3. Details
Because they decide and make your "image" a little more complete and humane.
