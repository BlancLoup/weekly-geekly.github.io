TV Episodes ten months later

Hello!

It's been 10 months since the launch of TV Episodes . In honor of this event we are launching a new version of the project, which contains interesting innovations.
The essence of the project is simple - you choose the series and get notifications about the release of new translated series.


TV series filtering


At the moment we are tracking the release of new episodes of 86 series, and it becomes difficult to find the necessary ones among them. Therefore, we added the ability to filter TV shows by title - just enter the name or part of it, in Russian or English, and all the extra TV shows disappear from view.


Watched series


Forget which series you left off? You are not alone :)
We have added functionality to solve this problem. We looked at the series - marked it as viewed, and you will no longer receive notifications for this series.

Login to the site with a password


We have replaced the previous version with the transition to editing the account from the links in the letters. Now we have full authentication with login and password. In fact, it differs from the previous version only in the ability to remember your password.


Genres, TV series rating


Based on the preferences of our subscribers, we have built a rating of TV shows :

The rating was quite interesting. "Bison" like Heroes and Dexter give up positions gaining popularity of the Big Bang Theory and Theory of Lie . Of course, Dr. House without competition - the gap from the rest more than one and a half times.

Wiki system


Adding links takes us a lot of time and effort, sometimes the speed of mailing suffers. We decided to ask our users for help with this. Now subscribers can add descriptions of the series and download links themselves, without our help. Links are pre-moderated, descriptions appear immediately after editing.
We are counting on your help.
