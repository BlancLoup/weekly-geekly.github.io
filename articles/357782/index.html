YouTube is asked to remove video stolen from other people's webcams



At the Black Hat hacker conference, the creation of a new public organization Digital Citizens Alliance (Digital Civil Alliance), which aims to fight with the "rats" - RAT'terov, was announced. In their forums, the rats share the most interesting videos with the sexiest victims, preferably in the nude. Something spread on YouTube.
