Westone W80: eight components of good sound

At the end of 2016, the Westone company pleased the audience with W80 headphones. The manufacturer does not stint on the assurances of the quality and uniqueness of the new product by integrating several decades of experience into a small plastic case. No doubt - Westone makes good and high-quality headphones, this has been proven more than once or twice. But is there a limit to perfection? I propose to get acquainted with the flagship closer.

A box of thick cardboard is strict and beautiful:

Under the two black covers, the inner box made of orange cardboard looks out over the wishes and autograph of designer Karl Cartwright (Karl Cartwright):

Inside it is a foam insert, giving the package strength. All useful content is packaged in a small hard zippered case:


Its dimensions are small (210 x 140 x 70 mm), the quality of performance is good.


Inside there are several compartments for storing the headphones, wires, attachments and care products themselves:


All trifle easily fits on a microfiber rectangle. These are three pairs of removable decorative parts on the W80 case, a tool for replacement and cleaning, ten pairs of soft nozzles of various shapes, sizes and materials. Half is made of Comply high viscosity memory foam (True-Fit), and the rest is made of silicone (see the first photo).

Someone like this variety may seem redundant, but do not make premature judgments. As practice has shown, pick up the tips should be not only the size of the ear canal, but also the desired shade of sound.

For carrying in a pocket inside a large case is another, smaller, with a mesh bag for the wire. In any case, it was stored there at the time of unpacking.

Nothing prevents you from shuffling the contents to your liking.



The W80 comes with two cords - one so-called ALO Audio Reference 8, braided with translucent rigid insulation and a three-prong (TRS) 3.5 mm jack for connection to an audio source:



The conductors in it are made of highly refined silver plated copper, which should have a positive effect on the sound quality.

The second is the usual black, with a four-pin (TRRS) 3.5-mm jack. The fourth contact is needed for the operation of the buttons on the remote and the microphone when connected to a smartphone. Given the frequent damage to the cord in wired headphones in the field, as well as the considerable price of the W80 themselves, such care of the user is more than appropriate.

Headphones and cables easily fit into one case:



By the way, covers, like cable, have their own names: Premium Small and Premium Deluxe. The first, judging by the size, is intended only for the headphones themselves, and the second for the joint transfer of the entire portable audio system: a player, amplifier, headphones, and a hiking set to care for them.

W80 - channel reinforcement earphones with eight (!) Drivers. With the help of the crossover, tasks are distributed between the emitters: two work out low frequencies, two - medium and four are responsible for reproducing high frequencies.



Also, the manufacturer claims a noise reduction system that can reduce their level by 35 dB. A good role in this is played by the correct selection of nozzles for the ear canal - in the case of the W80, they are very soft and elastic. This allows you to securely fix the headphones in the ear and to ensure a snug fit of the nozzle to the skin of the passage, sealing the connection.



W80 sound good and always different. Much depends on the sound source and the ‚Äúseals‚Äù used.
