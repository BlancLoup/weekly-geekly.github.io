Idea


The concept of escape-room is based on the following ideas:
  • A team of players, usually 2-4 people;
  • For a limited period of time, usually 60 minutes;
  • Tries to get out of a locked room;
  • Solving puzzles, solving ciphers and getting quest items.

The room in which the action takes place, is framed in accordance with the plot and creates an atmosphere of immersion in the quest.

Advantages:
  1. High atmospheric.
  2. The work of the head is combined with the movement of arms and legs (climb on / under the table, feel the walls, floor and ceiling).
  3. Team work can be used for team building.

Disadvantages:
  1. Zero replaisability. Re-pass the same room does not make sense.
  2. A time limit means a significant probability of loss. It can upset someone.
