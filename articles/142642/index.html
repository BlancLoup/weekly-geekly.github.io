Free Mobile Monetization Webinar for Nokia Series 40

Hi, Habr.

We invite you to register for the free webinar " Revenue Models and Developer Monetisation Tools ", where we will talk about the monetization of free and freemium applications and games for Nokia Series 40 phones. The webinar will be held tomorrow at noon (MSK) in Russian. Registration is required .

The first part of the webinar will be devoted to using the In-App Purchase API for Java , which allows users to make a quick purchase of additional content (or unlock unavailable functionality in the basic version) in games and applications.

Using the In-App Purchase API, you can implement, for example, the acquisition of additional levels in the game or a subscription to any service within the application, even without leaving its interface. At the same time, an in-app Purchase API allows you to pay using a bank card or mobile account balance.

The second part of the webinar will focus on the monetization of free applications through advertising. Keynote speakers are an expert of inneractive , a company specializing in monetization of applications for all popular mobile platforms using its own advertising network, and a representative of HeroCraft , one of the leading Russian companies in the field of mobile game development.

Register for the webinar.
