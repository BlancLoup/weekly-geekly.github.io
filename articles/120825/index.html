Rambler Cashier. Movie tickets

On the night of June 6-7, Rambler Internet Holding launched the Rambler ‚Äì Cashier service, through which you can buy a ticket to your favorite cinema by paying with a credit card, WebMoney or Yandex.Money.



Availability information is updated in real time. After payment, the user receives a digital code of the ticket in the form of an sms message or by email. The code must be dialed in the terminal at the cinema, which will print the ticket or call the cashier and get a ticket from him.

A network of cinemas Karo Film, Kronverk Cinema, Kinomax and others are already connected to the Rambler-Cashier system. In the near future, the Cinema Park network will be connected.

It is worth noting that the sale of tickets is made through the websites of partners: "KinoPoisk" , "Film.ru" , "Poster" .


Checked through kinopoisk, at the moment only credit card payment is available. I hope that in the near future to connect the network of cinemas "Luxor". Still popcorn and cola could be paid in advance :)
