Epic Growth Conference 2018 - grocery marketing conference in Moscow

On March 29, Mobio, Getloyal and Appsflyer, with the support of myTarget, Appodeal, Appnext, Gett, are organizing the second international conference on product marketing Epic Growth Conference 2018 .

More than 600 representatives of the largest food companies will gather in Moscow to exchange experience and listen to guest speakers from SkyScanner, Yandex.Taxi, PandaDoc, Intercom, Aviasales, etc.



Representatives of product companies can apply for a free ticket on the website egconf.ru . The number of places is limited, manual selection of applications is conducted.

EGC 2018 speakers:

  • Anna Buldakova (Product Manager, Intercom);
  • Bayram Annakov (CEO, App in the Air);
  • Daniel Shuleiko (Managing Director, Yandex.Taxi);
  • Anton Baitsur (CPO, Aviasales);
  • Mikita Mikado (CEO, PandaDoc);
  • Alexey Pisarevsky (CEO, Getloyal);
  • Caterina Toniolo (Principal Growth Manager, Skyscanner);
  • Ferdinand Getzen (Head of Growth, Recruitee);


Stay tuned to the site, read news on product marketing in the telegram channel @epicgrowth .
