XFX Charity Event

The XFX company returns to customers who bought GeForce GTX 280 or GTX 260 video cards for the period from June 16 to July 11 at $ 120.

It is clear that such an action, aimed at increasing customer loyalty, is caused by the rapid decline in the cost of NVIDIA accelerators in an attempt to equalize them at least somehow with the price of the Radeon HD 4850/4870.

The action, unfortunately, applies only to the United States and Canada. We have no "blood" will not return.

By the way, EVGA also joined XFX, which also returns GTX 280 $ 125 to buyers and $ 60 to those who bought GTX 260.
