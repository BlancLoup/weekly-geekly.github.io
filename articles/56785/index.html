New iPhone 3G Ads: Itchy, Office and Student

Today, Apple has released three new commercials for the iPhone 3G. I publish them with the translation.

Itchy




One of the conveniences of the iPhone is that even in order to find out which direction to go on a hike, there is a special program.

Or if you want to find out whether the bird you are looking at is really a red-faced warbler (a kind of bird), that is, a program for that.

And if you want to find out if you will itch tomorrow because of the bush in which you are standing now, there is also a separate program for this.

Yes, there are programs for any situation. Only on iPhone 3G.

Office




Imagine that you are a small business owner and you need to knock out a check to the buyer. Well, for this there is a special program.

And you need to print a shortcut for delivery. For this, too, there is a program.

Then you need to see if your customer received your purchase. And for this purpose there is an application.

Yes, there are programs for any situation. Only on iPhone 3G.

Student



Imagine that you are studying at a university and you need to rent an apartment closer to the campus. Well, for this there is a program.

Or you need to find a store with the lowest price on a biology book. There is a program for this.

Or you need to find at least one photo that you can show your parents. For this, too, there is a program.

Yes, there are programs for any situation. Only on iPhone 3G.
