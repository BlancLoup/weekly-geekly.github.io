3D camera with 158 lenses

Professor Isino Youzirou and his students from the Nagoya University of Technology built a camera for three-dimensional photography, which fell into the Guinness book of records. On an aluminum arc frame with a diameter of half a meter, 158 lenses are placed each at a cost of $ 2.



Creating a design took six months. The camera is designed to capture the flame from the maximum number of angles, although in reality it is unlikely that it has any special scientific value. Nevertheless, the professor received the official certificate from the Guinness Book of Records in November.
