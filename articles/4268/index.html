Optimizer "Vebalte" to help

Recently , Vebalta began testing a pilot version of an anti-doorway program. The goal of the project is to improve the quality of issuing the search service of the same name, which is needed to facilitate the search for information relevant to the request.

The program is free, to participate in it, the optimizer is invited to contact the administration of the system - in response, they will send a special address where all the rules and wishes for working with it will be written. Further, when a project participant finds out in his extradition on his subject doorways, he informs the editors about this. You cannot directly remove spam - at first, all actions and suggestions of expert optimizers are moderated. However, this happens quite quickly - within a few minutes after processing the information, the unwanted site is removed from the issue.

It is reported that optimizers with a good reputation over time will be able to remove spam on their own. When conferring such powers, preference will be given to participants registered on authoritative optimizing forums Mastertalk.Ru and Searchengines.Ru . The best of them will be offered a job in the company.

Vebalta also reports that now the company's specialists are finishing testing a comprehensive system for dealing with doorways - internal (cutting spam at the time of issuance) and external (when the site appeared in the search results). The initiative of the company associated with the involvement of optimizers, of course, refers to the second part of the comprehensive program.
