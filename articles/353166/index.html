And we will have a real cosmonaut! On Imagine Cup 2018

Dear friends! Agree, being a student is one of the craziest moments in the life of each of us. It was then that we meet the most loyal friends, believe in incredible ideas and are confident that to become the second Bill Gates is just to spit. Every year we support all these ideas and hold one of the brightest student events around the world - the Imagine Cup !



Under the cut, you will be able to recharge ideas and learn about the projects that are presented this year, as well as about the very astronaut who makes incredibly beautiful pictures from the ISS. In addition to student teams, the event will feature many interesting people, one way or another connected with technology. To participate, do not forget to register (the number of places is limited), and if you can not come - see online translation .


Small preface


Almost every year, our teams win prizes in various categories at the international finals, and, importantly, continue to develop their projects after participating in the competition. Among the participants of the past years, you will find successful businessmen who open accelerators and enter the Chinese market, create their own game development studios, help the visually impaired to navigate in space, get into Forbes and tops of mobile app stores (like OVIVO , recently released on mobile platforms, winner of the Imagine Cup 2015.


What will be interesting?


  • Presentations of the 16 best student projects, including homemade HoloLens, made by students from Nizhny Novgorod, a virtual physics laboratory from Moscow State University, music in VR and much more.
  • Fascinating inspirational lectures from people like Sergey Ryazantsev (hero of Russia, pilot-cosmonaut), Dmitry Shostko (MTS), Alexander Lukichev (DVR), Alexander Baulin (Forbes), Maxim Karakulov (Habrahabr), Sergey Tsyss (Mechanium) and many others.
  • The exhibition, in which, for example, a non-anthropomorphic robot "astronaut pillow" from the Mechanium company will be presented for the first time.
  • Free Azure Certification
