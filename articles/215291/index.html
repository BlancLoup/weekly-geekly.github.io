2048

19-year-old Italian developer Gabriele Cirulli created an extremely exciting game in 2048 , crossing tetris and tag.



At each round in the game, two tiles with the number "2" appear. Pressing the arrows, you need to reset them to the side, while the tiles of one "denomination" are added. Winnings count when the result is 2048.

For a successful addition points are added, in accordance with the nominal value of the tile. Despite the apparent simplicity, to win in 2048 is very difficult. The author says that he has the best result - about 6000, despite the fact that he himself has been playing all day without a break. However, several still managed to win .



The program code is open . By the way, there are similar games Threes and 1024! for iOS.
