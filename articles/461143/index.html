To begin with , for those who are suddenly not in the know, briefly analyze what this game design is, what the English-language Wikipedia will help us with:

Game design is the art of applying design and aesthetics to create a game for entertainment, for educational, physical or experimental purposes.

And let's go counterclockwise - mechanics :

  1. Do not disdain mechanics from other genres, this is your originality. As examples, let them be Path of exile, with its incredible even for role-playing games skill system and sane plot and Times of Discord, which I am not afraid to call one of the first kki in the role-playing game.
