R'ha is a short animation film by a German student that opened the door to Hollywood.

Surprisingly, in RuNet there is almost no information about this bright event, but I will correct it now. No joke, a first-year student of the High School of Media Design from Berlin, thanks to his short animated film called R`ha, got a contract in Hollywood!



... Machines, rebelled against their creators, want to destroy the alien race. They torture one of the aliens about the coordinates of the meeting with the escaped ships. But he is silent. He thinks cars are soulless nonentities ...

"In his work, there are shots worthy of a film with a budget of $ 150 million," writes the producer of the film Hurricane Season.

But in order.




Kalebu Lekhovski is only 22 years old and he finished only the first semester at the High School of Media and Design in Berlin. Twenty-two is a perfectly normal age to start studying in Germany. Usually, the gymnasium ends by the age of 19, the army or alternative service follows it, and only then does the study begin.



During the first semester with the help of computer graphics, he created a short film with a duration of almost 6.5 minutes. The film received 600 thousand views in the first five days and was so impressed with producer Scott Glezgold that he offered the student a contract.
