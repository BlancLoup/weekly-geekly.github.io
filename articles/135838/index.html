Let's play snowballs?

Accidentally discovered that the site http://www.android.com/ embedded game. Go to the page, scroll down to the end and move the cursor on the green man (next to copyright). At the same time in his hands you can see a snowball. We click and we start the game!

The game is simple. You take turns throwing snowballs - the snowman is in you, and you are in it. You need to calculate the flight path to accurately hit the enemy. Management is simple - just click the mouse.



For three hits in a snowman give a gold medal.


Enjoy!
