How to register for the Third Minsk Startup Weekend?

In April 2010, the third Minsk Startup Uikend will take place, which, unlike the two last ones, will be entirely devoted to business projects in the field of IT technologies. About 10 investors and approximately the same number of business consultants are expected to participate.

The event is non-commercial and represents a one and a half day training marathon, a business run-in and launch of new business ideas - start-ups and attracting investment in them.

Algorithm of registration for the Third Minsk SpartUp Weekend:

1) Pre-registration is carried out by calling (+375 17) 288-14-54, (+375 17) 288 14 91 and includes receiving an application for participation. You can also send a request for participation by e-mail - startup2010 (a) tut.by

The application for participation must specify:
- your last name and first name
- Scope of activity / profession
- Contact mobile phone
- As someone you want to take part in the training (participant, author of the project).

Preference is given to people with business experience of at least 2 years. Students are registered, but you must prove your interest in the project (why do you want to participate?).
If you are participating as an author of the project, then you need to send your project to the address: SpartUp2010 (a) tut.by.
The deadline for submission of the project - is specified. A preliminary analysis of the projects is being specified.

2) Participation in the event is conditional-paid both for the authors of the projects and for the participants.
