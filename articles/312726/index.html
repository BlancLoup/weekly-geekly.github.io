At the end of the last two years, I made a list of books that somehow influenced my life. I made it for myself, but later decided that it would be useful to you too.

A public promise and a group of like-minded people with whom we shared the results helped me to achieve an ambitious goal. Then we did it on the walls of soc. networks, which was not very convenient. Now all these functions are assumed by the SmartProgress target achievement service.
