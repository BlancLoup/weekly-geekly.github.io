More than 1 billion active Apple devices worldwide



Apple said it has 1 billion active iPhone, iPad, Mac, iPod touch, Apple TV and Apple Watch devices. These data were obtained by tracking devices that used the company's services in the past 90 days.

Compared to last year, the number of active gadgets increased by 25%.

During the first quarter of 2016 fiscal year, Apple sold a record 74.8 million iPhones, 5.3 million Mac computers and 16.1 million iPads. The company also earned a little more than $ 6 billion in revenue from its services, including the App Store and iTunes, AppleCare, Apple Music, Apple Pay, licenses, and many other services.

Earlier in January, the company announced the 2015 New Year holidays as the most successful for the App Store in the company's history. For 2 weeks (until January 3rd), customers spent a record 1.1 billion dollars on apps and purchases.

During the week after the grand Christmas sales, on the day of the New Year, users spent $ 144 million, which is also a record figure.

The latest Apple data shows that 75% of active devices are running iOS 9, 19% are still using iOS 8. The remaining 7% of devices are running on earlier versions of iOS. Earlier, the company reported that iOS 9 may be the most downloaded version of the Apple operating system for the entire history of the company.
