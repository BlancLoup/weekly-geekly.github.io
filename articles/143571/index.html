How was the Moscow Facebook Garage

We have a short video from the second Russian Facebook Garage, which took place on April 12 in Moscow, so if you were there - look for yourself, if you weren't, listen to what they say.

Let me remind you that in order to tell about Facebook as a platform, an entire landing force has landed in Moscow, consisting of both company executives represented by Vice President Joana Shields, and Julien Codornier, program manager for working with partners, and Tom Elliot engineers (pictured) and Bruce Hazan.




The main topics of the meeting were the use of a social graph, the creation of mobile applications and a story about the opportunities that Facebook offers for novice developers.


The hall was full (more than 300 participants) and attentively listened to both the slides of the presentations and the screens with the source code, when the speakers demonstrated how this or that problem was being solved in Facebook.
