Yandex.Money cannot be mapped to a non-Russian bank

Please note: the recipient must have a Russian bank card - Visa (including Electron) or MasterCard (Maestro). Transfers to foreign cards are not accepted now.

Such news now flaunts on the page of withdrawal of funds in the system of poison . If you are not from Russia - be careful, use other payment systems. Webmoney, for example, can again be displayed in Ukraine.

Withdrawal to foreign bank accounts is possible.

This restriction concerns the withdrawal of money from the wallet to the card, and not the replenishment of the wallet!

UPD Commander from Yandex :
Indeed, transfers from a Yandex.Money wallet to cards issued by _lyuly_ non-Russian banks are temporarily restricted to Ukraine, this is not related to the territory of Ukraine. The problem is technical, it is solved.
We hope by the end of March to return transfers to all cards. While alternatives - transfer to a bank account (including the card account) or Contact.

Transfer from a Yandex.Money virtual card to other cards is possible with the help of third-party services that support MasterCard MoneySend (for example, Alfa Bank's Opp). The transfer from the wallet to the cards of foreign banks through the Yandex.Money interface (this one ) is limited. At the same time, you can transfer from wallets to accounts in foreign banks ( here ).
