Smart speakers with "Alice" on a low start


Yandex.Station

In July 2018, Yandex began selling the first smart speaker in Russia, Yandex. A station with a voice assistant, Alice, and sold the first batch in one day (see the line at the store ). Now the company first revealed the approximate number of devices sold for all the time.

During the period from February 1 to March 10, 2019, buyers purchased approximately 5,000 devices. Approximately the same number of Irbis A and Dexp Smartbox speakers with Alice voice assistant were sold.

For all the sales since July 2018 sold "tens of thousands of devices," said the company. That is, at least 20,000 pieces. This is significantly more than analysts had expected for such an innovative device. For example, Eldar Murtazin, an analyst at Mobile Research Group, calculated that in all, from the start of sales in July 2018, Yandex could sell no more than 5 thousand "Stations".
