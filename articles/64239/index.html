Question Win7 connoisseurs

In connection with the release of the 7600 build, I remembered about the win7 on my computer and the problems that prevent me from finally switching to it.

The first question.

Somewhat unexpected, but what to do with it - I'll never know. The fact is that my seven breaks network connections. It happens every two or three hours, but maybe three times in a row for half an hour. It is expressed in the fact that the ping to the gateway and vpn-server suddenly disappears, and, as a result, to the rest. Attempts to disconnect a LAN connection or a VPN connection cause a complete hang of the network service in general. It is treated only by rebooting.

Tested on three builds, 7000, 7077 and 7100. Russification is not worth it, I tried it on an absolutely empty Windows, without any prog - the same garbage. On XP, which set in parallel - everything is ok.

It resembles the effect of a virus that caused the Generic Host error, but there are no viruses. Definitely not.

The only thing - I sin on the built-in VIA network card.

The second question.

As soon as you perform any actions that require CPU load, you quickly switch tabs in Mozilla, you simply load a page with a large number of small elements, drag a scroller in the explorer with thumbnails of images, even just in Photoshop you apply the sound. Stuttering is like slowing down a frequency. That is, such "stretching" notes appear. Although in Winamp, even in a flash player, at least in anything else.

On other axes and processors, this has not been observed since the time of the Pentium 60.

Prots - Pentium D 925, built-in zvukovuha. The wood for sound was updated to my native Realtek old and new ones - it did not help.

I have been suffering with these troubles for about half a year now, jumping now on XP, then back to the seven, and right now, I feel that right now with the help of you, habra people, I will find a solution to these problems))

UPD: hmm ... a miracle happened? Not loaded in the seven seven months. Now uploaded - problems with sound disappeared somewhere. No stuttering at all. Drivers standard microsoft from 04/21/2009.
We are waiting for the miraculous healing of network problems))
