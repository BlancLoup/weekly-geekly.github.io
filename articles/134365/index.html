Jobs in new projects - we need you tomorrow!

Today was the first day of Harvest - working days off, during which IT-entrepreneurs create future star projects. We will prepare a detailed report a little later, but for now the operational news.

On Saturday morning, nearly 30 project ideas were presented. Many of them require specialists. Programmers, marketers, designers ... Find your future work here and come back for Harvest tomorrow!



Here, who needs projects:


EYC
Mobile loyalty system for cafes and restaurants. Establishments are provided with a cloud-based CRM-service, which allows increasing customer return.
  • Developers: front end (PHP, Ruby), back end (CRM development)
  • Developer of mobile applications for iOS and Android
  • Sales Manager
Verdict.me
Users will learn about how they are seen by those around them: you can leave an opinion (including anonymously) about each person on the service, including an assessment, characterization.
  • Designer and usability specialist (better in one person)
  • Web developer
Yoopus
Philosophical search engine: unlike Google, it does not provide information on popularity, but allows you to find out the opinion of famous people on difficult life issues. What did your problem with Nietzsche, Turing or Stanislav Lemm think?
  • designer and usability specialist
  • developers: front end (HTML5, Java Script), back end (PHP, C)
  • Unix admin
  • Promoter (PR, marketing, advertising)
No depression
The site that will help cure the biggest Russian problem after roads and fools is depression.
  • Information, advice, advice. Monetization through advertising and paid services for treatment and counseling.
  • developers: HTML5, Java
  • specialist in finance and accounting (perhaps an invitation to the role of co-owner)
Bookpoisk
As a film search, only book search: complete and structured information about books, where you can find all the necessary information, from the output data to high-quality feedback from readers. Specialization in books about business and personal growth.
  • Web developer (Java, PHP)
  • Marketer
  • Negotiation manager (negotiate cooperation with publishers)
Design cast
A site with high-quality and conveniently classified tutorial videos for Photoshop and other graphic editors. Quick lessons without voice acting, which clearly show what and how to do and allow you to learn, saving time.
  • Web developer
Crocodile
A service that allows you to play Crocodile using a webcam, no matter where you are. Crocodile using a webcam will make this cool game accessible to everyone.
  • A developer capable of working with video technologies (Flash Media Server and analogues)
  • Designer
  • We are waiting for people who can sing (need 8 [!] People) - for tomorrow's presentation!
Game diary
Online diary in the form of an RPG game: cute, fun and motivating.
  • Web developer: HTML5, Java Script, PHP
  • Mobile developer, all platforms
  • Usability Specialist
  • Product Marketer and PR Specialist
  • Administrator (Unix and Windows)
Musicant.biz
The service meets the interests of all three sides of the music business: listeners will learn about concerts, concert organizers will download artists, and musicians will receive offers from venues and promotional groups.
  • Web designer to visualize the site
  • PHP developer
Button Exchange
A resource dedicated to the old tradition of cutting off and storing buttons from old, but favorite things. Housewives can share photos of their collections, change, chat, buy new buttons.
  • Designer, and even more likely an artist with good taste.
  • Mobile developer universal
ADR
A platform that simplifies the work of shops with suppliers. Automatically sends a request to suppliers about the amount of goods left, finds photos of goods, checks the availability of goods in the warehouse of the store. Significantly simplifies the life of the merchandiser.
  • Marketer
  • Web Designer

  • Consultants and salespeople in different business areas for cooperation and partnership. Priority sectors: construction, insurance, medicine
