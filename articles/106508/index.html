Russian Flash Awards Winners Award Ceremony


On October 22, 2010, from 18.00 to 20.00, the fifth Awards Ceremony of the Russian Flash Awards will be held as part of Russian Internet Week-2010 .

Venue : Expocentre on Krasnaya Presnya - Pavilion 3
Address: 14 Krasnopresnenskaya Embankment, Moscow, recommended entrances: the Northern Entrance and the Western Entrance.

The award will be held in 9 nominations:
  • Art;
  • Commerce;
  • AIR / Flex;
  • Animation;
  • Animation;
  • Technical merit;
  • A game;
  • Video;
  • 3D.
