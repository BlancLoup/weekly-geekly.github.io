Not exactly a facebook phone

INQ today showed the public two models of phones based on Android, which were created in collaboration with Facebook.
The INQ Cloud Touch and INQ Cloud Q phones are both intended for those to whom Facebook has become one of the main ways to communicate. This is expressed in the deep integration of Facebook functions into the phone shell: the home screen is the news feed of your Facebook account, access to all the main Facebook applications: chat and messages, Places, friends list and notifications. The phones also actively use the Facebook Single Sign On system and the Social Graph API. The latter helps, for example, to follow the news of exactly those friends with whom you communicate most. Not forgotten, and "native" for Android services - for example, events from Facebook Events are synchronized with Google Calendar.
