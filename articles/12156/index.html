Nokia N98: 7.2 MP camera, S70, 3.5 touchscreen display, GPS

This Nokia N98 mobile phone, very reminiscent of the Apple iPhone, is just a beautiful concept.

Specifications Nokia N98 seem pretty fantastic today. According to the author, the device should work under the operating system Symbian S70 3rd Edition, and support Bluetooth technology, Wi-Fi (802.11 b / g / n), HSDPA. For photo and video, the concept has a 7.2 megapixel camera with Carl Zeiss optics, 5x optical zoom and 20x digital zoom. The display of the Nokia N98 has a diagonal of 3.5 inches, it is touch-sensitive and works with a VGA resolution (800 x 600 pixels). There is a GPS module and a graphics chip.

The concept is certainly great, but Nokia is unlikely to present something similar over the next year or two.
