- Is there a black list on hh.ru of candidates and employers?

- How many responses with my company will receive a vacancy programmer?
A medium-sized company that offers a salary slightly above market level and interesting tasks receives about 5-7 responses from applicants per week. Of these, suitable (those that can be invited for an interview) will be half. But in general, developers prefer that companies look for them on their own. This means that sometimes it is worthwhile to respond if the job / vacancy has attracted - the competition will be small.

It so happens that the right employee has already been found, and the vacancy has not yet been closed - that same human factor. Therefore, some recruiters refuse, without looking at a resume.

- If I have a lot of resumes on hh.ru for different tasks, will the employer be able to see them all?
It can, but not always.
When viewing resumes on hh.ru, the employer sees the item ‚ÄúThere are still so many resumes of this candidate.‚Äù This amount will depend on what visibility settings you have set on your resume. If they are open to all clients of hh.ru, then the employer will see each. If the settings are more subtle, then only those that are available to him, based on these settings, will be seen. Therefore, if it is important for you to have different summaries for different cases and that they do not overlap, turn on the visibility settings.
