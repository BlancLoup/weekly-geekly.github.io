TOP 5 tools for the selection and analysis of hash tags



Hashtags have long and firmly become the tool that entered our life and received the status of irreplaceable. Someone uses it to find the right information, someone - to directly communicate with specific people on the network. And someone - as a tool for self-expression and promotion "to the masses" of their ideas.

For the latter, choosing the right tags has become a vital issue, since the slightest mistake can turn into the most unpredictable consequences for the promoted product or service. I bring to your attention the TOP 5 on-line tools that will help you choose the right tags for your idea .




1. Ritetag

Interface language: English.
Cost: free.
Comment: one of the strengths of this resource is not only a vast tag database with very detailed statistics, but the ability to install the plugin in the browser. During the selection of the tag, the Ritetag plugin shows its popularity and reach, allowing you to select the most optimal coverage words.



2. Tagdef

Interface language: English.
Cost: free.
Comment: the resource allows not only to search and analyze tags, but also to view the popularity of tags (always, constantly or only this week are popular) and the most active users.



3. Trendsmap

Interface language: English.
Cost: from $ 9, there is a free plan with limited functionality.
Comment: was it ever interesting for you to ask exactly in Moscow or Paris? Trendsmap allows you to view the most popular tags in relation to a specific geographical object. ‚ÄúMust Have‚Äù tool for everyone who works with small local markets.



4. hashtags

Interface language: English.
Cost: from $ 49.
Comment: a very powerful platform that allows you not only to analyze data by tags, but also to receive information on those of them who are gaining popularity or are in the top. The system contains a database of explanations on the tags and a large amount of additional information.



5. Tagboard

Interface language: English.
Cost: free.
Comment: a platform that works with social networks: Twitter, facebook, Instagram, Google+, Vimeo and Flickr. It allows you not only to analyze the popularity of a particular tag, but also to see where, who and what published with it.
