MagSpoof Portable Credit Card Swan

Ten years ago, Samy Kamkar created a worm that added a million people to his friends on MySpace (the Samy worm). Since then, the Pole has continually pleased us with new finds: these are the non-removable EverCookies cookies; SkyJack drone, automatically hacking other drones and giving the operator on the ground access to control and video cameras, and much more . In general, in the world of hacking, Sami Kamkar is like the legendary Fabrice Bellar in the world of programming.

Another ingenious gadget from Sami is the portable MagSpoof credit card spoofer , whose work is generally shown on video.




* Ability to save copies of magnetic stripes from all of their bank cards in one device

* Wireless operation with all common magnetic strip readers (NFC / RFID not required)

* Ability to disable Chip-and-PIN protection (source code is not published)

* Correct prediction of Amex card numbers and expiration dates on the previous number (source code is not published)

* Support for all three tracks from the magnetic strip, as well as simultaneous support for Track 1 and Track 2

* Simple device design, easy to assemble on the Arduino board
