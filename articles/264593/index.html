Modding air conditioner Stulz CyberAir

Under the heading "First, it's beautiful": in our largest data center, NORD 4, you can now see a unique glass air conditioner.



Experimented with the latest model of precision air conditioners Stulz - CyberAir. NORD 4 cool 112 air conditioners of this brand paired with CyberCool chillers.
CyberAir is interesting because it works on warm water (18-24 C) - that is, they are able to cool data centers by means of free cooling for much longer than conventional air conditioners.

Air conditioner Stulz CyberAir ordinary


But our happiness would be incomplete if the functional thing did not become in addition also beautiful. So, we replaced the standard doors and side panels of the air conditioner with tempered glass panels, plus we put the same sheets instead of a couple of raised floor tiles (for we have nothing to hide! :).

Air conditioner Stulz CyberAir tuning


Heat exchangers close up


To make the air conditioner play with all the colors (like the stands in the dark ), they added a LED backlight. Even the console is attached to it, so you can adjust the mode, like on a Christmas garland :).
