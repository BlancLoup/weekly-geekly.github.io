Digest of June IT events



After a short break, we return with another banner of events for developers for the next month. This time we have a little bit of everything: a couple of hackathons, some specialized events, something for startups and a good portion.

Creating a development environment for C ++. Inside view

When: June 1
Where: Veliky Novgorod, st. Student, 2a, Park Inn
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

Meeting of the Novgorod integrated community without professional division: juniors and seniors writing in C ++ can discuss software development problems together. The event is focused primarily on applied benefits, analysis of specific tasks and assistance "from the developer to the developer." In the official part - performances by experienced programmers from the company MIR with stories about their own experiences in overcoming particular problems.

Loginom Hackathon 2019

When: June 4-5
Where: Moscow, Ryazansky Prospect, 99, State University of Management
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

It is already too late to join the student teams battle, but it is quite possible to attend the venue as a spectator. The participants who passed to the final following the results of numerous selection stages will demonstrate their knowledge of business intelligence and data science, as well as present projects created using the Loginom component libraries with various purposes - client analytics, logistics, cleaning and data enrichment.

ok.tech: Frontend meeetup

When: June 4
Where: St. Petersburg, st. Kherson, 12-14
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

Discussion for front-end developers under the strict guidance of employees from OK.ru, Yandex and mail.ru will cover both the latest news and eternal questions like testing and texts. Four reports from company representatives are being prepared: the benefits of testing based on properties over the classic (with life examples), a review of the new EndorphinJS library performed by the author, approaches to and plugins for working with texts and, finally, a case from Yandex about transferring search technologies to React .js.

QuizIT second season! First game

When: June 5
Where: Novosibirsk, st. Tereshkova 12a, 2nd floor
Conditions of participation: 2000 rubles. from team

The Siberian event of an unusual format for those who missed the opportunity to shine with knowledge last year. In the quiz will compete teams of up to six people (only representatives of IT-companies are allowed); they will be offered three sets of questions on various topics (both related to development and from other areas) and formats - text, audio, multimedia. At the end of the evening - prizes to the winners and a photo session for everyone.

II Technology Festival MY.TECH

When: June 8
Where: St. Petersburg, st. Medics, 3
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

OS DAY 2019

When: June 10-11
