Rating of hosting panels used in online agencies of Russia

Tagline Agency published the first rating of the leaders of the hosting panels market according to the results of a study conducted among about 300 web studios and Internet agencies of Russia at the end of 2011.

The shares of the leading hosting control panels were distributed as follows: *



  1. ISPmanager - 49.1%
  2. cPanel - 46.8%
  3. Parallels Plesk Panel - 21.5%
  4. Direct Admin - 15.1%

The leader of the rating is the Russian company ISPsystem with the product ISPmanager - a multifunctional control panel for the Internet server. Almost half of the survey respondents chose this decision (49.1%).

The resulting rating generally reflects the alignment of forces in the market, experts believe the rating, if you do not take into account the segment of small companies that, rather, choose non-commercial panels such as OpenPanel and ZPanel, self-hosted panels hosters or configure the server manually. It is also noted that the situation is determined not so much by web studios participating as respondents in the survey, but by large Russian hosting providers who install this or that control panel to themselves.

Details and detailed comments of experts rating on the trends in the market of hosting control panels can be viewed on the rating page .

* During the survey, users had the opportunity to choose more than one option.
