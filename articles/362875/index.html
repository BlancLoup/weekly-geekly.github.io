LG G3 first to get Android Lollipop

Do you already want to update Android on your smartphone? You are lucky if you are the proud owner of LG G3 and live in Poland. LG officially announces updates. But the raw version has already begun to be tested on the owners of Moto X.



It would seem, why not start with smartphones Google Nexus? So no, we decided to start by installing a new version of Android 5.0 Lollipop on LG smartphones. Already this week, owners of smartphones from LG in Poland will be able to upgrade the operating system.

Since LG has too little time to change the interface, there will be little external improvements. Most of them will be inside or will be connected, for example, with new notifications or the ability to unlock the phone next to other devices like smart watches.

As mentioned above, the new Android Lollipop can already be put on Moto X and Moto G if you want to be a volunteer and test the raw version of the system on your smartphone.
