RoboHunter nominated for Runet Award 2015



We are glad to announce that RoboHunter was nominated for Runet's main award!

Runet Award 2015 is awarded to the leaders in the field of high technology and the Internet. The solemn meeting will be held for the 12th time, and the winners will be announced on November 10, 2015.

The Runet Prize encourages companies that have demonstrated significant achievements in the field of information and electronic technologies, business structures or business leaders who have contributed to the formation of the domestic Internet segment.

Preparation and organization of this event takes almost a year. The organizers are the Russian Association of Electronic Communications and the National Domain Coordination Center , which are seeking to bring information and communication technologies to a new level. Runet Prize is designed to facilitate the implementation of projects and their dissemination.

The organizers are actively working to create a unified information society. Today, more than ever, it needs experienced high-tech experts. Also one of the main tasks of the Runet Prize is to increase the level of trust in the Internet environment.

RoboHunter, which is nominated in the category "Technology and Innovation", shares the values ‚Äã‚Äãof the project. Information development should be actively developed. Electronic media have long been one of the significant areas of the economy.

Projects that take part are nominated from July to October, so that later in October and November, experts could vote and select industry leaders. Winners are determined by the Expert Club and the Council, formed on the eve of voting. The prize also holds a popular vote.

The awards ceremony will be held at one of the large-scale sites in Moscow. The exact place will be announced on the eve of the event. Only 6 laureates in the main categories will receive their statuette, 3 special nominations are also appointed.

RoboHunter wishes all participants victory!
