We invite you to participate in Security Meet Up December 4



We invite experienced and novice bug-hunters, as well as experts in the field of Internet security to take part in the Security Meet Up, which will be held on December 4, 2014 at the Mail.Ru Group office with the information support of Hacker magazine and SecurityLab.Ru portal. The theme of the meeting will be the practice of conducting bug bounties programs.

To date, programs for the payment of awards for the detection of problems in the safety of projects there are many IT-companies. ‚ÄúHunting for mistakes‚Äù is a truly effective protection mechanism when the users themselves are involved in the company's security process, and the company demonstrates its readiness to ‚Äúrespond with money‚Äù.

How to understand if you are ready to launch bug bounty? And to participate in it as a supervisor? How to organize the process of paying remuneration, collecting and evaluating applications, interacting with participants? What to expect and what to prepare? How to submit a good report and by what criteria is it different from bad? We will try to find answers with these and other common questions related to bug bounties with you.


A representative of one of the world's most respected white hacker communities, HackerOne , who will join us via video link, will touch on various organizational subtleties, as well as share his experience of working with international companies.


Vladimir Dubrovin , head of the Mail.Ru Group testing group, Taras Ivashchenko , head of the Yandex product safety group, and Ilya Ageev , QA Lead, Badoo, will share their experiences with the bug bounty program, talk about what bumps they ran when they started and what they achieved in result, as well as disassemble a couple of the most interesting vulnerabilities.


Dmitry Bo0oM Boomov , a member of many bug-searching programs and the author of several interesting reports in the Mail.Ru Group program, will tell why hackers participate in bug bounties and share live hacking.

Security Meet Up will be held at the Mail.Ru Group office at the address: Leningradsky Prospect 39, Building 79. The beginning of the gathering of guests is at 19.00. The first performance (HackerOne) at 19.30. Do not forget to register and bring your passport or driver's license! The event will be broadcast on our website .
