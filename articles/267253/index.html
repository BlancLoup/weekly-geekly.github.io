In the Chinese AppStore, 39 infected applications were detected due to a modified version of Xcode.



Palo Alto Networks detected malware in iOs. He got through a modified version of Apple's Xcode tool - XcodeGhost.


An unofficial version of the program was found on one of the most popular sites for developers. In the AppStore got 39 applications with malicious functions, namely with the functions of phishing. The programs were compiled using XcodeGhost, which was posted on a popular Chinese website and was loaded by developers apparently due to the small speed of the official Apple server in this country.

Infected 39 applications from different categories, mobile banking, cards, and games. The most famous infected applications are WeChat and Didi Chuxing. See the full list of apps here .
