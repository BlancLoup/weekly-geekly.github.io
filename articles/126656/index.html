Energizer company has released a universal charger



Each of us now has a lot of gadgets. It used to be a mobile phone, at best, that's all. Now, many have phones, communicators, tablets, portable gaming devices, readers and more. All this needs to be charged, and manufacturers, as if mockingly, produce unique chargers for each of their gadgets. Even different phone models from the same manufacturer may have several different charger connectors. As a result, on a business trip or on vacation you have to take with you a whole tangle of wires and chargers.

While manufacturers are talking about the standardization of chargers (these conversations have been going on for more than a year), different companies are trying to make the life of an average geek easier by releasing universal charging stations. In particular, the company Energizer decided to release a device that is compatible with Apple products, and with other devices.

So, at the top of the iSurge Travel Charging Station, as the charger is called, there is an iPod or iPhone docking station. On one of the faces of the device there are two USB ports. Plus, there are 3 AC-connectors (US) for connecting any other devices.

There is also a nightlight-LED, which can be used if necessary. In general, the device is something like a Swiss knife for charging gadgets. True, for the CIS countries and for domestic "plugs" this device is not quite suitable, but with the use of adapters it can be used. The cost of this device is $ 60, and on store shelves it should appear in October of this year.
