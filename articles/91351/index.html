Please love and favor, TeamViewer now also under Linux

Good day, Habr!


Not noticeable for Habr, on April 15, the development team introduced the beta version of TeamViewer for Linux.
Builds available under:
  • Red Hat, Fedora, Suse, Mandriva (32/64-Bit)
  • Debian, Ubuntu (32/64-Bit)
  • and .tar.gz

Under the cut video preview and download link.

Download page:


I think this will make life easier not only for beginner Linux users, but also for those. personnel serving Linux machines.
I hope that not everyone will forget about such a thing as VNC. In Linux, for users it becomes less and less command line. I think every Linux beginner should go through the trivial setting of the VNC server.

But I already see a huge plus of TimVivera in front of the standard VNC, this is an easy way to log in to the current session compared to the VNC, Live Session.
That is, not a separate session is opened, but it will be possible to work directly with the working environment of the logged in user and show him all the manipulations!
