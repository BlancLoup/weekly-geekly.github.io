Change in privacy policy

Today we are pleased to present an important change in the policy of storing sensitive data obtained through Bing . We will delete the IP address data associated with the search query after 6 months, instead of the current 18. This change will be included in the current privacy policy, which now provides reliable protection for Bing users.

This change is the result of a number of factors, including the satisfaction of business needs, the current competitive environment and our ongoing dialogue with human rights defenders, consumers, as well as EU regulations related to data protection.

In accordance with the current policy, when Bing processes a search query, Microsoft immediately takes steps to de-identify the data, separating the request data from the account data, information about which can be used to determine the identity.
