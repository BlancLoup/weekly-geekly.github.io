Professional growth of employees - what it is and why it is needed: we communicate with Dodo Pizza, Icons8 and Evil Martians



Last year, we launched an assessment of employers at My Circle, where employees can evaluate companies by 12 qualities - from comfortable working conditions to the company's contribution to improving the world, and based on the results of evaluations of companies, we published the IT employers rating a little later.

Now we wanted to find out, and how do companies understand the qualities for which their employees value them? Therefore, we decided to make a series of publications on the My Circle blog about the main criteria for evaluating employers. To do this, we will select the companies that received the highest score for each of the criteria, and ask them to answer a number of our questions that will help us reveal how companies see this criterion.

In the first issue, we discussed Professional Growth with Dodo Pizza, Icons8 and Evil Martians and tried to find out how they understand it, how they support it and why employees value it for this quality.


Icons8 develops software for designers: designer of realistic photos Moose, graphic editor Lunacy and, of course, design icons.

Evaluation of the company on "My Circle".



Ivan Boyko (Founder Icons8)

We have an employee who came as an icon designer, but besides that she organized the shooting process, had a hand in collages and tagging, and in working with partners. Now she is assembling the designer of illustrations, and I'll smash myself into a cake so that she will not be bored anymore.

I ask the candidates how the company can help them, and I seek the maximum number of common interests. If interests are, you need to pick a person team: accomplices from related specializations, mentor-old man, wards-new, in which the blood boils.

We all need to surround the person with touching care and wait patiently for the result. Maintain when experiments break down. Rejoice small steps. Introduce to other people within the company. And all the time to give feedback.

Professional growth is not required. It may be personal, it may not be for the company, but simply for itself or even as a recipe for boredom.

Unlike professional growth, challenge is always needed, even in ordinary things. For example, the photo team has a monotonous task of tagging photos. But we noticed that taggers like to make headlines. We specifically made it so that you can add a few headers. If the photos have 4 or 7 captions, it is not because it is necessary for business. This is simply because taggers are carried away. And it is beautiful.

Sergey Chekmenev (Support Specialist in Icons8):

I worked in various teams both abroad and in Russia. The trouble of modern business is that he no longer sees the value in man, as such. A person is an impersonal appendage that is driven and intimidated by rules, superiors, regulations, KPI, etc. Working in one of the IT companies in Dallas, I acutely felt this spirit of modernity. The project constantly recruited 30% more employees than required. People were constantly hired and constantly fired, not because they could not cope, but only because a new employee appeared who showed N percent more KPIs. Such an approach does not develop, but suppresses. Both professionally and humanly.

And in Icons8, the atmosphere is unique because of Ivan‚Äôs sincere desire to reveal each employee, help everyone find and realize himself, and ultimately become a happy, harmonious person. This I have never seen. Ivan does not put employees in a rigid framework, but gives an opportunity to try himself, he is ready to help. He is experiencing ups and downs, joys and sorrows, in each employee emphasizes human dignity. Minimum formalism and maximum confidence. And the whole team functions as one well-adjusted organism: cohesively and harmoniously.


Dodo Pizza is the largest chain of pizzerias in Russia, which already has more than 460 pizzerias in 12 countries, and combines the classic pizza business with IT-technologies.

Evaluation of the company on "My Circle".



Vitaly Pomozov (developer at Dodo Pizza):

In my understanding, professional growth is primarily associated with the growth of competencies. Growth can be both in depth, sharpening their skills to the maximum, and in breadth, studying different technologies and ways to solve problems within their position.

The IT world is developing rapidly, and if you do not grow at a time when there are so many changes around, you experience stress. The company is growing rapidly, new countries are opening up, orders are growing, and we need to develop the system according to the needs of the business and not forget about the growing load. The lack of professional growth contributes to high staff turnover, which degrades the quality and sustainability of the system.
