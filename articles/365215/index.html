In San Francisco, noticed driving a drone Mercedes-Benz



Reddit user sfchillin posted a link to a photo of the futuristic concept car Mercedez-Benz, which he noticed at lunchtime in San Francisco. After some time, together figured out that this is an unmanned F015 Luxury In Motion . The concept car was first shown to the public at an electronics show in Las Vegas earlier this year.

The appearance of a fantastic car noted by local media. Later on, other photos of the car began to appear on Reddite and Twitter (the second photo was taken from the press release of the manufacturer).


In an interview with Huffington Post, Mercedes speaker Deirdra Wiley said that the F015 was taken to the San Francisco area for an advertising photo shoot. Pedestrians, noticing a very unusual car, did not fail to share what they saw on social networks. They saw that the car moved on its own, an indirect confirmation of which can serve as photos. However, it remained unclear whether the car was driving on its own or whether the driver was driving after all.
