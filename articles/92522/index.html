As I already wrote, NVIDIA Optimus technology found use in this notebook - moreover, ASUS managed to get the right to use this technology first in the world on its laptops. The essence of the technology is that a laptop can independently decide for each application when and which video system to use. If greater performance is not required, then only integrated graphics work - in this case, this is a good Intel HD Graphics; otherwise, the laptop switches to a discrete graphics card without restarting the system, providing more options. This approach allows you to significantly save battery life of the device, reduce the level of heat and noise from work.
And the indicators on the sides of the speaker grille, it turns out, show which video card is being used. The blue color of the integrated graphics indicator changes to white when switching - simply and clearly!
