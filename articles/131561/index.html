So, based on the main comments to the previous article, and comments received from a few, but already the owners of the table, the following changes are foreseen:

1. The table in the first modification had a narrow tabletop and a sliding shelf:
Worktop with dimensions of 130 cm (length) to 60 cm (width)
The extendable shelf is 70 cm long and 30 cm wide.
Against the retractable shelf, many commented.

Design change:
- eliminated sliding shelf under the keyboard,
- the width of the table top is increased by 10 cm to 70 cm (width).
- the height of the box was reduced, under the table where the shelf went, (it is impossible to completely remove the box - it gives the rigidity and strength of the structure, and it is also possible to hide the wires from the equipment on the countertop in the box).

2. The stand under the monitor is fixed - a constant height of 40 cm (from the table top to the center of the monitor). Convenient for standing, unusual for sitting.

2.V. Design change:
Rack with height adjustment of the monitor (height only), i.e. the monitor goes up and down in the range of 10-15 cm.
