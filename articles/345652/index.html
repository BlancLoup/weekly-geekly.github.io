PostgreSQL News Digest



Friends! We decided to launch a digest of fresh news, articles, releases and events from the world of PostgreSQL, which will be published every two weeks. In the collection you will find links to the most interesting materials on PostgreSQL, published for the period. If we missed something important for you - write in the comments!

Releases


  • Postgres Pro Standard 10.1.1 released . All key improvements and new features of Postgres Pro Standard 9.6 DBMS are transferred to this version, some of the errors found are fixed. PostgreSQL 10.1 build for Windows has also been released.
  • PgBouncer version 1.8.1 released. Fixed a bug in 1.8: the missing file was added, now PgBouncer is collected from tarball without any problems.
  • The driver version psqlODBC 10.01.0000 has appeared . Some corrections and improvements compared to version 10.00.0000. For example, memory leaks are eliminated.

Articles


  • In the article Jsonb:
    Dmitry Dolgov ( Zalando ) announced PostgreSQL , MySQL and MongoDB performance on YCSB tests. The processing performance of binary JSON s (JSONB and BSON) was compared. The testing methodology (in the cloud) is detailed, there are conclusions and recommendations.
    Prior to this, the topic was discussed at PGConf.EU 2017 in Warsaw and at other conferences. For example, in the presentation of Oleg Bartunov on the results of YCSB testing in Postgres Professional (slide 81 and on). In these tests, only MongoDB and PostgreSQL were compared on dedicated powerful servers, and the emphasis was placed on high load (thousands of clients at the same time).
  • Comparison of the quality of the Firebird , MySQL and PostgreSQL code.
    As experts, authors of the study are the developers of PVS-Studio
  • Part 8 of the series Egor Rogov (Postgres Professional): PostgreSQL Indices .

  • In the article What's new in DataGrip 2017.3
    Maxim Sobolevsky from JetBrains talks about a new version of their DataGrip development environment : In this version, there is already support for key PostgreSQL 10 innovations, such as declarative sectioning .
