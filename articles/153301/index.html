What is Ivideon?



Ivideon has no ads. At all. Even despite the fact that a significant part of our users are connected for free.
We launched the video surveillance service almost a year ago and it was free without any restrictions for everyone. Three months ago we launched commercial rates and imposed restrictions. Two cameras are free of charge, including the possibility of broadcasting them on your own site, and additional cameras for a small monthly fee. Most non-corporate users of the two cameras are more than enough.

But most importantly, we left the service free for almost 100,000 accounts that were registered before entering the tariffs, regardless of the number of connected cameras. We did not want to lose our customers because of the desire to earn more.

Still, we have no advertising. Because we do not want our service to be associated with pads, chewing gum or laundry detergent. Even now, when we are negotiating to attract a new round of investment, one of the conditions of entry, we put the lack of advertising in the video stream. At least so clumsy and senseless, which is used today popular video hosting. Our financial model already looks quite attractive without this component of income.

If it is simple, then Ivideon gives a convenient opportunity to combine an unlimited number of cameras scattered geographically in one personal account interface. They can be located in the same office or on different continents.

Viewing live video from cameras as well as archived recordings is possible on a computer via a browser or using the Ivideon Client application, on mobile devices using applications for iPad, iPhone, Android.



Today Ivideon is already a rather serious international project. And while none of our competitors have the opportunity to provide such a service around the world due to the lack of a scaling mechanism, which was laid by us initially in the development of software for the organization of the service.

What features does Ivideon provide as a service?



  • Transparent and easy connection of cameras with the ability to view via the Internet. No external IP address is required.
  • Full encryption of traffic over HTTPS / SSL, including video.
  • Convenient viewing via browser, PC application or using mobile apps for iOS and Android .
  • Save archived video in AVI format from any camera without going to the camera installation site.
  • Video archive recording service in our cloud . If something happens on the site, your notes will not go anywhere.
  • Want to embed video from your camera in your blog or on your website? Do it in 3 clicks . The rest of the work will be done by our CDN. The service is available even at a free rate.
  • There is no site, but you want to share video from your camera with friends? Post to social network? No problem .
  • Need to be notified that your video server has disconnected? Or is the camera turned off? There is such a function.
  • Build a corporate network of cameras with access rights differentiation between different people? The function of transferring rights to view video and archive to other personal accounts is also implemented.

How to connect cameras


To connect cameras to the Ivideon cloud, you do not need an external IP address, knowledge of network settings or expensive equipment. We tried to make everything as simple and affordable as possible.

There are two options for connecting cameras to our service.

First option. Ordinary webcams, IP cameras or analog cameras. A free Ivideon Server application is installed on a computer running Windows, Linux or Mac OS X, and cameras are connected to it.



It has all the basic features of the modern IP video surveillance system and supports virtually all IP cameras (including those with sound) using the RTSP and HTTP protocol, PTZ cameras (a number of models), and allows you to record on the disk locally by motion detection, scheduled or constantly. When recording by motion detector, a prerecord mechanism is supported, when you see not only the fact of an event, but also what happened a few seconds before it.
All these functions can be used for free even without connecting to Ivideon and without limiting the number of video channels, as is done in other surveillance systems.
But, the main advantage of using Ivideon Server is the ability to connect to our cloud.
To connect analog cameras to Ivideon Server, they need to be digitized. This can be done using any IP server or DVR that supports RTSP.

The second connection option is IP cameras with Ivideon support right on board. These are completely ordinary IP cameras from well-known manufacturers who supported the work with our service directly in the firmware of the camera itself as an add-on. It is noteworthy that one of these manufacturers was Axis , which was the first in the world to produce such a device as an IP camera and today is the market leader in IP video surveillance. In addition, the Korean company Microdigital, the Russian brand Sarmatt and Taiwanese 3S work with us.



We are actively expanding the line of such cameras and plan that soon most manufacturers will support Ivideon. And by the end of the year, integration with 4 more brands will be completed.

To connect the camera with the service Ivideon does not need a computer. It independently establishes a connection with our cloud (although I prefer the name data center), depending on the geographic location.

Some of the cameras support the ability to record an archive on a flash card, and some allow you to connect a Yota or 3G modem and monitor where the wired Internet is not available.

We do not seek to earn money on the sale of equipment. Therefore, all our developments, including ready-to-use firmware, as well as detailed instructions for installing them are presented on our website. This is primarily necessary for those who, for one reason or another, have already become the owner of cameras supported by us, but without our service on board. Nevertheless, for those who prefer to buy cameras that are already ready for use and tested by our specialists, we have opened our small online store . All prices listed on the cameras are on the same level as in other companies, and somewhere even lower.
