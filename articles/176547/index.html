Resource ROI was launched in test mode on April 2, April 15, we transfer it to the industrial mode of operation.
For two weeks, large statistics was collected on visits, load, requests to servers, etc. On the basis of it, on the 12th and 13th of the day, planned work was carried out to optimize the work of memcached server (caching algorithms).
