Eco-friendly server box made of cardboard

As is known to experts, in a modern server, more than 10% of the cost is the body. The cost of the package ultimately affects the cost of services for data center customers. Previously, our company has developed cost-effective 2U-cases, which have reduced the cost of entry-level servers, which could not fail to please our customers. We decided to develop this idea, and considered further ways to reduce the cost of our services. And since our company is environmentally conscious, we have successfully and fully implemented a new production project - server housings made of cardboard:
