The experience of organizing WiFi at the conference

It so happened that I was able to participate in the organization of an international conference (medium-scale) ISFD-11th-RCBJSF. The conference was held in the framework of the Ural state. University. A.M. Gorky.
So, the input data: 5 days of the conference, more than 250 participants from 25 countries. The venue was chosen World Trade Center in Yekaterinburg. There were more than enough tasks, but I want to tell you about the organization of a wireless network.

In the exhibition center of the World Trade Center there are no fixed WiFi equipment, therefore, all those who rent space are forced to somehow solve this issue themselves.

The story began when, in the network department at the university, they said ~ ‚Äúlet's do it yourself somehow‚Äù. It seems like there is equipment, and a controller for controlling points, but it did not work out. After an unexpected outcome for us, we began to find out among providers and companies in Yekaterinburg, who can organize a wireless network at the conference. Having in stock more than a month, due to the specifics of the work of state. institutions (office services, signatures, approvals, suppliers, again signatures, and so around), had to act very actively.
The requirements were quite simple:
  • coverage throughout the conference area;
  • smooth operation;
  • maximum ease of use for conference participants;
  • some malomalsky roaming;

Two weeks later, after endless correspondences and repeated raids on the object, we had several offers from providers and companies who really wanted to help us. It is understandable, the amounts in these proposals (in my opinion) were above average.
Not to be unfounded, let's see the offer of the Firm Z:
  • The cost of organizing a wireless network 36000 p.
  • Wireless network maintenance for 5 days 17000 p.
