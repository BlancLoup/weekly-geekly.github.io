Last days of discounts for the conference # MBLT13

Two weeks later, on May 15, an international mobile conference # MBLT13 will be held in Moscow.



This year the conference will be attended by more than 30 invited speakers, including Kate Teare ( TechCrunch ), Charles Birnbaum ( Foursquare ), Harry Koponen ( Rovio ), Matteo Penzo ( Frod Design ), Rory Kenney ( TripAdvisor ), Ciara Byrne ( VentureBeat ) , Christian Geissndoferfer ( YOOSE ), Boris Kim ( QIWI ), Svyatoslav Ostrovsky ( Sberbank ), Yevgeny Kozlov ( uBank ).

Alexey Volin , Deputy Minister of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, will be a special guest.

From May 1, the price of a ticket will increase from 9,000 rubles to 11,000 rubles. Therefore, those who are going to go - do not delay and register . Subscribers to our blog can write to the mail and get a special discount for users of Habr.
