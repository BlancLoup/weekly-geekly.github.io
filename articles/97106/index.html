In short, if you measure yourself correctly, then you are guaranteed to be able to put on the purchased item (unless, of course, you have time to get fat during its delivery). For example, it is enough to attach your foot to the ceiling of a sheet of paper, outline it and measure the distance from the heel to the thumb. Then on the plate to find the desired size.

With electrical equipment is not so simple. In the US, another current flows through the outlets (the current of freedom, so to speak). The voltage in the American sockets is 110 volts, and the frequency is 60 hertz (we have 50) and they look completely different.
