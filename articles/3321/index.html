Internet cafes of Belarus will be heavily controlled

According to the resolution approved by the government of Belarus, the control over access to the Internet is increasing in the republic. In the Belarusian Internet cafes, all connections must now be recorded and recorded without fail.

The owner of an Internet cafe or an authorized person must keep an electronic log of the domain names of the sites to which users have access. At the same time, in certain cases defined by law, the state security officers, law enforcement agencies and state control authorities may familiarize themselves with the contents of this magazine, ITAR-TASS reports.
