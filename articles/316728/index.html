Review ACS with free software

One of the interesting trends in the Russian market of access control is the emergence of manufacturing companies representing their full-featured software absolutely free.

We will clear it of marketing layering, and tell you how not to get hooked by marketers.



It is clear that companies must earn money on something, it would be logical to assume that the costs of developing software are simply included in the cost of controllers. Therefore, with the controllers and begin, consider the characteristics and cost, and then the functionality of the software.

Moreover, the software of each manufacturer works only with its own controllers.

The company was founded in May 2010 in Moscow. Offers the widest range of equipment under the RusGuard brand, including equipment unique to the Russian market, for example, the ACS-102-CE-B (POE) controller with a built-in POE splitter, which allows powering not only the controller with readers, but also locks and latches etc.

Types of databases


The type of database will directly affect the cost and more importantly, in my opinion, the speed

Microsoft SQL Server
RusGuard Soft and AC Tech use Microsoft SQL Server, which is free in the Express edition, which has certain limitations.

The main limitation of SQL Server Express is the maximum database size is 10 GB.

Is it possible to quickly fill the access control database up to 10 GB? The average figure obtained by expert is 3 - 4 years.
Of course, it strongly depends on the number of employees and the intensity of use, the main volume will be given by photos and scans of passports, when using such functionality.

It is also important to understand that 10 GB is a point of no return, that is, if the base is filled up to 10 GB, it can only be updated to the paid version or thrown out.

If you categorically do not want to do this, then you need to carefully monitor the size of the database, and start cleaning it before it reaches 10 GB.

findings


The question of the cost of software acquires importance when a large number of client licenses are needed, this is mainly required for large companies with a wide geographically distributed structure.

But for such companies, the issue of free software is unlikely to be the main one, for them the cost of owning a system is much more important, which in turn depends on reliability, security, software performance, quality of technical support, quality of hardware, availability of hardware.

And taking into account the fact that many ACS producers with paid software use a licensing scheme in which only server software is paid, and client work places are provided free of charge, the benefits of companies with free software are minimized.

For medium and small companies with a small number of staff, and they are unlikely to be able to experience the problems of speed and security of the database, and the problem of overflow of the database they may face for the first time in 5-7 years.

For them, the initial cost of access control, cost in general, with controllers, readers, installation services, etc. will be important.

And given the fact that the most popular, it is also the cheapest controller for access control in Russia, the Z-5R costs only 590 rubles , the same conclusion suggests that the issue of free software will not be the main one.

If you sum up all of the above, it turns out that the free software is more a marketing tool than a serious criterion for choosing an ACS.

This conclusion is confirmed by the leader identified by us - the company Rusgard, the leader in software, in hardware, and the leader in terms of the number and cost of paid modules.
