A medical marijuana startup has raised $ 10 million in a round with Snoop Doga

Eaze, a service for delivering medical marijuana to US residents, raised $ 10 million from DCM Ventures , Fresh VC , 500 Startups and Casa Verde Capital funds, which is owned by rapper Snoop Dog. This was reported by the publication of the New York Post.

Eaze was founded by Keith McCarthy, an ex-employee of the Yammer social network. The service is a mobile application. With it, users leave their orders.
