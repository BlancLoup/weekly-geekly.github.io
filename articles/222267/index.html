Higher School of Economics opens the program "Management of online gaming projects"



The Higher School of Economics (HSE) together with the Higher School of Business Informatics (VSBI) launched a professional retraining program ‚ÄúGaming Internet Projects Management‚Äù - Russia's first professional retraining program in the field of computer games development in the development of computer games created by expert practitioners.

Our company took part in the development of the training program in the section "Localization".
Demid Tishin, Natalya Novikova , Marina Ilinykh and Nadezhda Lynova will perform as teachers from our company.

Among the teachers of the course:
  • Alexey Filatov - Head of the Mail.Ru group monetization;
  • Dmitry Tabakov - senior game designer of the Allods Online team;
  • Konstantin Sakhnov - game designer studio Panzar and others.

General information about the course:
Form of study: part-time (evening)
Duration of the program: 9 months
Lesson schedule: 2 weekdays from 18:45 to 21:35 and Saturday from 10:30 to 16:30
Final examination: final certification work on the development of a business plan for an online gaming project
Issued by: a diploma of the established model of the Higher School of Economics on vocational retraining; the diploma gives the right to conduct professional activities in the management of computer game projects
Start of classes: October 13
Reception of documents: March - September
Cost of 210 000 rubles

Graduates of the course will be able to work as game designers, producers (operating online games), project managers, product managers and localization managers.

VSBI also promises to help graduates with employment.

June 4 at 19:00 will be the open day of the program, where you can ask your questions.

By the way, we are very interested in what you would like to see in the localization course. We will be glad to your wishes and comments.
