Functionality and features

Dropcam works with HD-video and writes a pretty high-quality color picture (you can see it in the review). I did not understand with what codec the device interacts, but the super-fast Internet is not required. In this case, the camera writes and sound, and video and delays occur only in rare cases. In general, the manufacturer recommends 50 Mbit speed.

The camera has a speaker and a microphone. Both are sensitive and sound quality, both in transmission and in receiving - at the highest level.



Of course, it was not without a motion sensor. It activates the recording while moving, while saving space on the server (although, I already wrote that it is not limited. Only storage time is limited). But this can help in the case when the connection speed is very weak or when traffic is limited.
