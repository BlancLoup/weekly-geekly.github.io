We are glad to share two good news at once.

After a rather long period of time, the ReactOS project again had its own official channel on Youtube. And as a premiere video, we will show you the first "official" laptop project. Why is the laptop "official" and what is the point of the idea?
We wanted, on the one hand, to prove the obvious fact that ReactOS can already work on real hardware, and on the other hand, to look at the situation from potential users and eliminate a number of existing bugs. This could be achieved only if key developers had an absolutely identical set of hardware at their disposal, which would have made it easier to identify systematically arising problems.

In fact, this laptop model came to us a long time ago, more than a year ago, in the amount of ten copies, which the employer of one of the ReactOS developers donated to us, but we did not want to talk about it ahead of time.
