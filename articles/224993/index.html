AudioOrbs, known as the developer of the world's largest audio system for the iPhone and iPod, did not stop there and developed the first spherical giant speaker. This unusual audio system designed for a smartphone, made in the form of a huge transparent ball-capsule. Inside The Orb, the listener is almost completely protected from external noises and sounds, which allows you to immerse yourself in the atmosphere of the music that flows through the built-in speakers.
