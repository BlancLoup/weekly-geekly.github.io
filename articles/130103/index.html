Seminar "What is the use of metrics?" In St. Petersburg

October 13 (Thursday) from 18:30 to 20:30 will be a seminar "What is the use of metrics?". The event will be held at the premises of the Yota company, Sergey Povolyashko (Kharkov), the organizers - the independent community of testers of St. Petersburg.

Participation is free, registration is required.
Seats are limited, please register only if you are really going to come.

Short description :
Everyone has heard about measurements in software development. But what is the use of their introduction? And what are the necessary conditions for implementation?

You will be presented with various ways to measure the quality of a product, how they can be used to improve workflows, identify problems, support contractual obligations, assess the achievement of goals of an individual, department or company. You will also learn how to select and implement the necessary metrics.

Attention! There will be a prize! For the most curious story from the audience, either the incorrect use of metrics or making judgments, making decisions based on sensations (without facts and figures). Come with stories, we will be happy to listen and vote!

Full announcement of the event can be found on the community site .

See you!
