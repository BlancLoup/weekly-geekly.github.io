Humble Indie Bundle 7

Launched seventh in a row Humble Indie Bundle . This time, it includes six great indie games. And also, one incredible film about indie developers - Indie Game: The Movie !



List of available games:
  • Dungeon Defenders + all DLC (Trendy Entertainment) *
  • Legend of Grimrock (Almost Human) *
  • The Binding of Isaac + DLC Wrath of the Lamb (Edmund McMillen and Florian Himsl)
  • Closure (Eyebrow Interactive)
  • Shank 2 (Klei Entertainment) - Mac version of the game is in beta testing
  • Snapshot (Retro Affect)

List of available soundtracks:
  • Dungeon Defenders: OST (FLAC and MP3) *
  • Legend of Grimrock Soundtrack (FLAC and MP3) *
  • Snapshot Soundtrack (FLAC and MP3)
  • Closure: Original Soundtrack (FLAC and MP3) - winner of the Independent Games Festival
  • Indie Game: The Movie (Soundtrack) (FLAC and MP3)
  • Shank 2 Official Soundtrack (FLAC and MP3)

* - for those who pay more than average

All games are cross-platform and DRM-free. System requirements can be found here .

Charities such as the Child's Play Charity and the Electronic Frontier Foundation are supported.
