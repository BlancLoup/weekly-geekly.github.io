The lack of video cards due to hobbies mining even affected SETI

The SETI project envisages a number of projects and methods for searching for extraterrestrial civilizations and the possible entry into contact with them. Some experts believe that in our galaxy - a huge number of planets, some of which can be inhabited, and the inhabitants - reasonable.

That is why in the last century it was decided to start a large-scale project to find brothers by reason. The program is gradually expanding, and scientists and ordinary people continue to believe in its effectiveness.
