Bank card binding
In order to verify your new account, as well as to cover the shortage of funds in your PayPal account when making purchases, you need to link your real card to it. The procedure is performed absolutely ordinary. Cards of banks operating on the territory of Ukraine will be suitable, with a high probability, any of them. We can say for sure that the cards of the banks Erste (recently - Fido) and Alfa-Bank are suitable. As already mentioned, the discrepancy between the first name and the last name, and the more so the addresses specified during the registration of the account, will not interfere with the success of transactions. The 16-digit card number, its expiry date and CVV code will be enough so that you can pay with PayPal with money from your card, which means it can be verified, since the procedure is based on withdrawing a small amount of cents from your card and then returning it.

Annual limit on incoming funds
By opening and verifying a new account, PayPal sets an annual limit on receiving funds in PLN 9,600, which is approximately equal to $ 2,900. Receiving funds here means only transferring money from other PayPal accounts to yours; If the money gets into the account from your card (for example, when you buy with payment through PayPal and the lack of funds on it), this amount will not bring you closer to reaching the mentioned limit. If the opportunity to receive $ 2,900 a year is enough for you, you can skip the next paragraph. Remember, however, that if it still happens to exceed it, the account will be immediately and without warning limited until you raise the limit. He will be able to accept payments, but not send them. If this has already happened, (or if not already, but you have a little limit), the next paragraph about it.

Open horizons


It is obvious that now it will be possible to transfer money from other PayPal accounts to your account (including accepting payment for items sold on eBay) and, of course, pay for purchases with funds from a linked card. What it is worth to dwell upon is the possibility of using PayPal funds for their intended purpose - as payment for purchases. The fact is that you will still not be able to withdraw them without significant commissions from the system, which means that the disposal of money with the highest efficiency - the purchase.
1. You can buy goods with physical delivery directly to your real address for this money only on eBay. The address you specified in your eBay account will be higher priority than the address associated with PayPal, so sellers will send you where to write in your buyer profile on eBay.
2. Outside of eBay, you can still buy products that require physical delivery, but you have to use Mail Forwarding to do this. This does not at all obligate your long-suffering Polish address to be a warehouse address.
