Each corporate culture has its own rules. Somewhere you will be answered at least on the same day or in the morning of the next day, while in others you can wait for an answer for weeks. If you, of course, do not put extra effort.

Use two days rule


It is important that you do not forget about. And you yourself did not forget that you need something from someone. There are comrades who write that they need an answer very urgently and disappear for a month. Funny, yes? To avoid becoming a sleeping beauty yourself, set reminders and let it become a habit.

Become a cyborg reminder


Each time to write "Unfortunately I did not receive an answer from you and bla-bla-bla" frankly zadolbalo. Why not automate this routine operation? It turns out that in each operating system there is an autochange or you can put a specialized Alfred .

I am working on correspondence with two large groups of recipients: colleagues and customers. I prepared the text for autochange for each group. And for each iteration I make a reminder in which I write the date of the previous iteration. As the number of iterations grows, I increase the degree of the text. Let's start with colleagues.

The time between iterations can be varied. For example, it may take three days before the first iteration, two days before the second, and one before the third. Everything is also very dependent on the situation and the choice of intervals for each person is better to set your own.

Do not allow raids in reminder letters. It is not necessary to write "This is already my second reminder, but there is still no answer from you." It is better to write how important it is for you to get an answer and how it will help your addressee.

Increase the addressee's motivation to answer you


I have many times encountered the lack of an answer due to two reasons:
- the addressee did not understand why he needed this letter, what it has to do with it and how it can help;
- in the letter there is no explicit question.

These moments are clarified in the process of phoning a few days after the first letter. Time is an irreplaceable resource, so always check the clarity of your syllable and the presence of a targeted action for the addressee at the end of the letter.
