In April of this year, the first model of the third generation of tablets from Samsung - GALAXY Tab 3 7.0 went on sale.



The tablet is equipped with a TFT-screen with a resolution of 1024 x 600 pixels, cameras - the main one with 3 megapixels and the front one with 1.3 megapixels. Responsible for the performance of dual-core ARM-processor with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz and 1 GB of RAM. There are options with 8 and 16 GB of internal flash memory and a slot for expanding the memory with microSD cards (up to 64 GB). The battery capacity is 4000 mAh. The edges on the sides of the screen have become thinner, making the tablet smaller than its predecessor. Its dimensions are 188 x 111.1 x 9.9 mm versus 193.7 x 122.4 x 10.5 mm for the GALAXY Tab 2 7.0. Weight also decreased by 40 grams: the version with support for Wi-Fi weighs 302 grams, and the version with 3G - 306 grams.
