April 24 in Moscow will host the first seminar on Drupal

This is the first seminar in Russia devoted exclusively to the topic of working with CMS Drupal .

The purpose of the seminar is to familiarize with the possibilities and introduction to the architecture of CMS Drupal.

Target audience - any interested persons choosing CMS / CMF for their projects. The theoretical presentation is complemented by short master classes on creating popular varieties of sites.

The seminar speakers are members of the drupal.ru community.

Seminar program:

  1. Beginning at 14:00 (MSK).
    Introduction to CMS, opensource and Drupal project.
    Drupal features out of the box; useful add-ons; Demonstration of the creation of a business card site.
    Drupal Basics: The Concept of Extensible Document Nods and Taxonomy.
    Solving typical problems with examples: personal blog, online business card, social networking site.
    Break - 30 min.
    Hosting features. Masterhost for Drupal.
    SEO in Drupal.
    Immersion in architecture - working with nodes and building forms, programming without programming under CCK + Views.
    Answers to questions received during registration.
    Ending at 19:00.

    The seminar is free, but pre-registration is required . The space provided for the seminar can accommodate up to 50 people, so we simply can not accept everyone. To register, you must fill out a form . After taking the questionnaire, the manager will contact you.

    Upd: registration for the seminar is terminated.
