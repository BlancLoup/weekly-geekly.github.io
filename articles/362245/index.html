An eye for an eye: Rutube began to cut the quality of video to users who cut ads

For users of banner cutters like AdBlock, video on Rutube is now forcibly displayed in the minimum quality and with the appropriate notification:



The option was probably turned on on Friday (at least on Thursday I did not notice this warning). The same video without ads looks like this:


The difference in quality is obvious
