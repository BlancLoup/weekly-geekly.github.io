In trend: Mail.ru Group has allocated work with big data into an independent unit

The new division will provide its clients with services for the creation of predictive mathematical models, marketing research, consulting in the field of infrastructure development and big data methodology. The unit was headed by Roman Statiugin.

The pattern thus obtained may serve as a model for predicting the occurrence of an event or help in decision-making.

Examples include models of forecasting customer churn, response to proposals, and prediction of response to a call through a certain communication channel.
