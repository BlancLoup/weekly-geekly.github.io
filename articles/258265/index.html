Live broadcast of DevCon conference on May 20 and 21

May 20-21, 2015 Microsoft will host the fifth conference dedicated to the development and testing of software DevCon 2015 .



DevCon 2015 is the first Live Code conference, which will feature more than 50 reports on current topics - from development tools, mobile platforms, to corporate practices, web and cloud technologies.

For those who did not manage to register or cannot attend the venue in person, we will organize an online broadcast of all 5 tracks of the conference within 2 days. You are also expected to:
  • live broadcasts from the site of the online studio;
  • communication with experts and speakers online;
  • an opportunity to ask a question to the speaker and find out the answer live - we will organize a Q & A session with experts online, follow the schedule!
  • And much more!


Watch the online broadcast on May 20 from 10:30 am and May 21 from 09:00 (MSK).

The main topics of the conference:
  • Client development - the creation and design of modern applications for Windows 8 and Windows Phone 8 using the unique capabilities of the platform, as well as their monetization and promotion for application stores.
  • Cloud and Web development services for Windows Azure, as well as the use of the cloud platform as a backend for mobile and other applications.
  • Tools and languages ‚Äã‚Äã- the methodology and use of Visual Studio development tools and software lifecycle management. Use current features and discuss the future of the .NET platform, C #, JavaScript, XAML, building technologies.
  • Corporate development - work with data, business analysis tools and Microsoft office platform. Creating enterprise applications on SharePoint, Office and .NET.
  • Game development - for the first time on DevCon tools, approaches and experience in developing gaming applications on popular tools: DirectX, Unity, and others.
