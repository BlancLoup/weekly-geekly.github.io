From Russian Roulette to secure LOTO: how to protect data center personnel


Safety first is not an abstract call, but a very concrete plan of action at enterprises with industrial safety risks. Data centers are related to such facilities, and, therefore, should have developed rules for labor protection. Today I will tell you how the LOTO system works on the Linxdatacenter site in St. Petersburg, which increases the security of data center operation.

Analysis of industrial accidents, injuries, accidents and occupational diseases shows that their main cause is non-observance of safety requirements, ignorance of the nature of man-made threats and methods of protection against them. According to Rostrud, from 30 to 40% of industrial injuries with serious consequences for health in Russia are caused by human factors.

At the same time 15-20% of all accidents are associated with incomplete disconnection of equipment from energy sources during the repair and maintenance of equipment. Workers are most often injured due to the release of residual energy, as well as due to erroneous switching on or violation of equipment shutdown procedures.



Only one way out: to comply with safety regulations. Including to choose the most safe technological processes, organizational and technical measures and the rules of personal behavior.

What does the data center?


Even taking into account the fundamental difference between the data center and the plant or NPP, a potential danger exists when operating the engineering systems of the data center. Still, the data center is a few MW of electrical power, diesel generators, cooling systems, ventilation, no reinsurance in the direction of industrial safety can not be superfluous here.



In the process of preparing for the attestation of Linxdatacenter's operational efficiency according to the standard of Uptime Institute Management & Operations , we decided to put in order this direction within the framework of work processes in the data center.

The task was as follows: to develop and implement a unified procedure for reliable blocking of sections of the engineering networks of the data center and to create an understandable system for designating types of work and performers. We conducted a study of the available solutions and made a choice in favor of a complete LOTO system, as the most adequate and simple. Let's look at how this system works.

Block, mark!

In the Russian language, the names ‚Äúsystems of protective blocking‚Äù and ‚Äúblocking systems‚Äù were fixed. The common English abbreviation "LOTO" is also used. Its main purpose is to protect people from interacting with energy sources at industrial facilities, where electric, gravitational (gravity), hydraulic, pneumatic, thermal and other forms of energy with an uncontrolled release can entail a risk of injury to humans.

  • Lockout . The first part of LOTO consists of Lockout procedures, which consist in installing special blockers and locks on a site of the engineering network - potentially dangerous due to the possible energy release. However, simply blocking the site is not enough, it is necessary to inform people about the potential danger and exactly what kind of work and for how long led to the withdrawal of this part of the network from normal operation.
  • TagOut . For this, there is the second part of LOTO - TagOut. A potentially dangerous part of the network where work is being done and in connection with which it is disabled or blocked is indicated by a special warning tag. Birka informs the rest of the staff about the reason for the shutdown, from what moment, for how long and by whom. All information is confirmed by the signature of the responsible person.

Formally speaking, using LOTO allows you to:

  • reduce the number of accidents

  • Reduce downtime and increase productivity.

In sum, this reduces the indirect costs of operating the engineering systems of the data center.
