Service Face.com learned to recognize the emotions of people in the photo



Face.com is known as a software developer used to recognize faces in various photos. Quite a long time ago, the company added a smile recognition function, and now other emotions have been added: surprise, anger, happiness (actually, the same smile), sadness and a neutral facial expression.

The technology developed by Face.com can be used for websites or regular applications. The range of uses for such a technology is quite wide - from games to user identification systems by facial expression (yes, you can come up with one).

At the same time, the determination of the emotional state of a person, whose photo is uploaded to a website using Face.com technology, is carried out by the service itself, which processes the photos. Yes, not so many users send banal smiles, the most part - all sorts of "curse words". But, actually, this was to be expected.
