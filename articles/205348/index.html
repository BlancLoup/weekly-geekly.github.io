Call-to-action in e-mail newsletter

Why are you doing e-mail newsletters?

Probably in order to bring subscribers to the site or sell some product ... In this case, it is very important to focus the attention of the person who will receive the letter on what he should do after receiving your letter.

For example, today a letter from a fairly well-known brand got into my mail.
