Subtle Patterns: background textures for your site

If you are launching a new site and completing its design, then it makes sense to look at the Subtle Patterns collection: a perfectly designed collection of background textures (patterns) under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported license. They can be used without restrictions.



The site attracts with a convenient preview: each texture can be instantly applied to the entire page as a background. Now there are 93 works there, the collection is updated weekly with great new items.

All the textures on the Subtle Patterns website are repetitive, i.e. glued together in a seamless fill.

By the way, for fans of "infinity": not so long ago, a small gallery with background textures, made on the basis of the cicada principle , a new web design technique, opened on the Design Festival website.
