How does work stress arise?


It can occur in a team of any size, no one is protected from it. This happens in large companies and indie studios, with newbies and veterans. The difference between "bad" and "good"


When industry leaders talk about "good" stress, they usually mean cases of voluntary participation - the team is highly motivated, confident that it is working on an outstanding project, and unanimously wants to invest as much as possible in creating an even more amazing product. Recycling for compensation
