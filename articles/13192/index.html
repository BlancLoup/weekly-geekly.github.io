From the beginning of July 2006, RTO subscribers using broadband access became almost three times more, and from January to June - by 60%, exceeding 1 million. NWT subscribers were the fastest recruited (increase from the beginning of the year - 78%), Uralsvyazinform "(67%) and Sibirtelecom (65%).
