New security measure for Payoneer cards

Dear users of "Habra", we are pleased to announce the introduction of a new service that will help improve the security of your Payoneer account. The new service will allow Payoneer cardholders to receive email alerts for every transaction that meets certain criteria.

Among the criteria by which a transaction is evaluated are the country in which the purchase or issue of cash was made, the amount, the number of previous transactions, and much more. The cardholder, who has received such a notice, may check the transaction to make sure it is correct, or immediately contact Payoneer's security department if there are any questions. If the transaction is completed correctly, you can ignore the notification, if you do not recognize the transaction or are not sure, please reply to the letter or call a phone to block the cards.

Currently, this service is launched in a closed test mode, that is, it is available only to certain cardholders. We will gradually add other cardholders, and announce as more updates are made.
If you have any comments or suggestions about the new service, please let us know. You can contact our support service in Russian by mail at support (a) payoneer.ru; The department for working with Russian partners is available at partners (a) payoneer.ru, keep up to date with Payoneer news, follow us on Twitter and VKontakte
