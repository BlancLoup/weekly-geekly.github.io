ZX-Spectrum Games

There was such a very addictive game for the Spectrum ... I remember that Bomber was written on a diskette with it (if, of course, my memory does not let me down :))

The essence of the game is as follows - 2D side view, a small airplane flies (a small airplane is in place, and the earth moves under it), there are houses, tanks shooting at the plane below, sometimes oncoming planes fly ...

The goal - to bomb everything that hinders and fly to the end of the level.
All this action was accompanied by a completely nasty whistle of the speaker, depicting the sound of falling bombs)

Who will remember? :)
