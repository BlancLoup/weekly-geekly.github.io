New 2GIS

Today we showed a new 2GIS and a couple of new products. The first to update the online version is beta.2gis.ru. Other versions will gradually change - offline version for Windows, Linux, Mac OS and mobile versions for iOS, Android, Windows Phone, BlackBerry.

New interface, new philosophy, new search, new information both on the map and in the directory, new mobile product and product for browsers.



Lyrics


The new 2GIS is not just an improved version of the previous one. We have created a new product from scratch. Work on it began when we realized that with all the ability to collect a lot of useful information about the city, we are limited by the ways of its presentation. We wanted to create something that will help our users get only the information that they need, and only when it is required.

We took as a basis the observation of our audience and our understanding of how the information should be presented. The interface is as small as possible. Maximum space given to the information. The map fills the entire screen, and each object that we see above it is filled with meaning and performs a specific task. If desired, the entire interface can be reduced to a single search line.

But search is not just a search box. No less important is the map. For cases when it is necessary to understand at a glance where in a particular area a pharmacy or restaurant, we have drawn onto the map the familiar to many POIs - points of interest. These are the very small icons of hotels, museums, gas stations and other things that help you quickly navigate "on the ground."



For 2GIS, any click on the map is also a search query. And each next click will give you more and more detailed information. Clicking on the house will show a list of companies located in it and all information about the building - for example, a zip code.

Selection


Search results are now adjusted to your request. If you are looking for a gas station, then immediately on their list you will see the brands of gasoline being sold, the hotels - the number of stars and the cost of rooms, online stores - website addresses. This rule works in the opposite direction: when searching for food delivery or a taxi, you will not be shown physical addresses.



The next user step is the company cards. We added 350 thousand reviews from Flamp.ru and 200 thousand photos from Foursquare.

The companies themselves fill the booklets, and we also tried to make them pleasant for them. An updated Personal Account allows you to create a new building booklet in less than 10 minutes.
