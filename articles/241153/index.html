Portal to search for like-minded people when creating projects

Some time ago we noticed that there is not yet a single database of student projects and students who wish to participate in such projects.

Then we made it. Called talentkit.net . You can register here .



What will * be possible in it?

If you want to participate in something and develop skills - you can find a good project. The project can be any - a technology startup, music band, sports team, strange people making a film in the dark, etc.

If you have a project idea, you can, respectively, find like-minded people, experts in the right areas, people with and without experience, and other friends of interest.

If you are a university, find out what students actually do and help / not interfere.



How it works

Everyone who has registered has a profile (settings). It has some general information and a skills section. Skills is what you can do.

If the skill is active, you will be invited to projects where you need a participant with this skill. If not, it will just be visible. To participate in projects it is necessary to have one active skill and it is desirable to indicate the city. All projects in which you participated, appear in the profile.

Your project is also created in the settings. In addition to general information, there is a ‚Äúvacancy‚Äù block, which indicates with what skills you need people. Here is a search in the database.

To participate it is not necessary to be a student, the main thing is the desire to do something.

* Why "will"?

We just launched the project . Register, add projects and the future will come swiftly. =)

We are very grateful to you. There are three ways to help.

1) Invite friends. The more participants, the better.

2) The system has a list of project types and skills. It reveals only those areas in which we understand. If you can paint any area - we will be very grateful. Here is a list.
