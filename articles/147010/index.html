Public transport routes for Minsk and 7 new cities

From now on , residents of eight more cities can plan trips on public transport on Yandex.Maps . These are Cheboksary , Khabarovsk , Penza , Kherson , Simferopol , Sevastopol , Yalta and the Belarusian capital Minsk .

The service is able to build routes taking into account traffic jams and offers from one to four travel options. Users choose which way is best to go and how much time to spend on the road. For example, if in Minsk you need to get from Pobediteley Avenue to King‚Äôs Street , the service will offer to get to your destination in two different ways, with or without a transfer.



When building a route, the service takes into account various factors - the type of transport, the speed of its movement, the time for transfer and the way to the stop. If you find an inaccuracy, let us know via the feedback form - and we will promptly make changes.

Not only a web service, but also mobile Yandex.Maps for iOS and Android platforms can build public transport routes. Now routing is available for more than 45 cities.
