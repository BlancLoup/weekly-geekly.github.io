Microsoft Soapbox back in service

The beta version of amateur video hosting from Microsoft, MSN Soapbox , after closing, became available to users again. Recall that the project was closed because of the fear of becoming accused of piracy, when with similar claims attacked on YouTube.

The main reason for re-opening was the introduction of Audible Magic technology, which should stop the appearance of illegal content on the site.

Now you can see a reworked, improved interface for both the site itself and the video player.


Of the minuses, I noticed the lack of support for the Opera browser - in version 9.21, I displayed some individual elements of the page that do not carry any functionality. However, I'm already used to the fact that Microsoft services are not in a hurry to work with browsers other than IE ...
