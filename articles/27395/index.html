ICQ on iPhone

About a week ago I bought myself an iPhone. Naturally, I immediately wanted to download ICQ. I did not care what, if only to work. In the repository, I found only Apollo, downloaded. Entered Win and password, tried to connect. But he said that the server is wrong, and for some reason I could not change this field. Then I remembered that there is a thing called Fring, and that they, besides VoIP, also keep ICQ. Added the fring.com/iphone.xml repository and downloaded. Everything worked, I was pleased. But by the evening of the next day all contacts on my iPhone were gone! I thought what a glitch, came home, synchronized with tuna, everything is in place. But the next day they disappeared again = (. After searching the forum on iphones.ru, I realized that it was all because of the fringing and demolished it. And I started thinking about how to deal with ICQ. I remembered meebo.com, but after 5 minutes, I realized that this is not the case. Since I am a web programmer, it was easier for me to write some kind of web ICQ, but first I looked on the net that already exists:
