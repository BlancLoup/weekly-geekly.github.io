REG.ru has divided part of domains

A few hours ago, REG.ru registrar domains were delineated, with their own domain names listed as ns servers. In technical support reg.ru I was told that the problem is massive and is now being solved by specialists of the company. 

domain: SETNS.RU state: REGISTERED, NOT DELEGATED, VERIFIED person: Private Person registrar: REGRU-RU admin-contact: http://www.reg.ru/whois/admin_contact created: 2011.06.06 paid-till: 2015.06.06 free-date: 2015.07.07 source: TCI


I checked selectively several domains on the basis of statonline.ru/domains?tld=ru&registrar=REGRU-RU and indeed - domains under similar conditions have the status NOT DELEGATED. And, accordingly, are not available.

Write in the comments who affected this problem.

PS Apparently, reg.ru solved the problem and returned DELEGATED status to the affected domains. However, the complete lack of reaction from the company is sad. I did not find any official statements explaining the reasons for what is happening.
