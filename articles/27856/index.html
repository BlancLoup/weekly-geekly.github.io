Real turnover rating



Somehow, the new rating from Tegline was imperceptibly past .
I present the first five to public attention:

1 Actis Systems 253 000 000 rub.
2 Promo Interactive 100 000 000 rub.
3 Red Keds 87,500,000.
4 Atilek 42 000 000r.
5 QSOFT 41 000 000 rub.

And now the background. After the original rating for 2007 was subjected to a wave of criticism, the expert group clearly had a conscience and within a couple of weeks a letter came to the corporate box of our studio as follows:

We invite you to take part in another study of the expert group.
The next official rating of the TEGLINE group will be built on the basis of real turnover for 2007, declared by the representatives of the companies.
To participate in the rating you need to log in to the site and fill out a form in which to answer a series of questions about your company. The deadline for the survey: May 28 - June 4.


After this period, I vainly updated the news line on the Tegline website, and today, unexpectedly, among the links to our website, I found the following: tagline.web.integrate.ru

And then the real-world rating was discovered. The results are quite amusing, especially given the fact that many large players did not give out their data, including the ADV, which was repeatedly ranked among the organizers of the rating.
