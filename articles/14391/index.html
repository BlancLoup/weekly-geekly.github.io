In Germany, prohibit copying discs

The upper house of the German parliament approved a bill according to which the Germans are forbidden to make copies of any discs, CDs and DVDs, even for home use. The new law will enter into force in 2008, according to Variety.com

In addition to the ban on copies of CDs and DVDs, recording and storage of broadcasts from paid and public TV channels will be prohibited in Germany. About what sanctions threaten violators of the new law, not yet reported ...
