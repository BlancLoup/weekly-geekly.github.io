Features of launching the Jelastic cloud platform on the Infobox

On September 12, we launched a new cloud service based on the Jelastic solution.

The question arises: why does Infobox need another cloud? After all, less than a year has passed since the launch of the Parallels Automation for Cloud Infrastructure (PACI) cloud platform, which solves similar problems.

And why in Russia one more installation of Jelastic? Before that, two other hosters Rusonyx and Reg.Ru have already launched a service based on Jelastic.
What special will we offer on the Jelastic platform?

All answers are under the cut.


A couple of words about Jelastic


Jelastic is a worldwide known platform for running Java and PHP applications. Jelastic has gained popularity due to the simplicity of deploying applications without having to make complex server environment settings (which is especially important when working with whimsical Java applications for settings).

The platform provides images that are already configured and ready to go. That is, you do not need to install Apache, NGINX, MySQL, PHP and other necessary services. Moreover, the functionality of the platform allows you to install popular CMS with an optimized web environment in one click.

The second advantage is the presence of autoscaling, which allows you to optimize costs at the time of small loads and calmly experience various habra effects. At the same time you can set the minimum and maximum limits of resources that can be allocated to the application. Payment is made only for actually consumed resources, which, however, is typical for the cloud platform.
Allocated resources are measured in cloudlets, which are containers (virtual servers). One cloudlet includes a 200 MHz processor and 128 MB of RAM. Disk space is charged separately upon use. Tariffication of all resources hourly.

That's all the basic information about Jelastic.

Questions to yourself


Now back to the questions that we raised at the beginning of the post.
Why does Infobox need another cloud? After all, we already have a Parallels Automation for Cloud Infrastructure cloud platform .
And why is our Jelastic installation more interesting than other hosters?
We answer in order.

PACI is mostly an IaaS platform focused on corporate Clients who need to place part of the IT infrastructure in the cloud. It is this target audience that uses our service at the current moment. But since a significant part of the Infobox Customers are developers, webmasters and site owners, it was necessary to offer a cloud solution for them too.

We have been familiar with the Jelastic platform team for several years, but the focus of the Java solution has seen a somewhat narrow segment. But as soon as the platform began to support PHP applications, we began launch negotiations. And he took place!
Thus, adding a Jelastic-based solution to our service line is a deliberate, long-planned, logical and surely the right step.

Then another important question arose before us: how to offer potential Clients something that would encourage them to choose our service, and not the service of competitors who already sell Jelastic?

We could only experiment with the automatic installation of applications in 1 click and try to surprise with interesting rates. Not the cheapest, and not like everyone else.

About tariffs


We were always confused by the rather large sheet with the prices for the clouds from other Jelastic providers. This is usually eight gradations for the cost of the cloudlet, depending on how much they are used at a time. On the one hand, it looks profitable: if you use more, you pay less. After all, the maximum discount for providers reaches 55% of the base price. But in reality, this means a significantly higher price for a small number of cloudlet, which is often what the Client needs. Judging by the comments on Habr√©, it is this pricing policy of providers (and as a result, the high cost of resources) has caused the most criticism among users.

We decided to simplify the tariff grid as much as possible and offer only two types of prices - up to 100 klaudlets and from 100. At the same time creating our tariffs, we decided not to enter into price wars - this is the path to nowhere. We have offered a price for a cloudlet, which, for example, for the entire range from 1 to 100, corresponds to a price from the range of 50-60 from other providers.
This means that closer to 100 cloudsets, our price may even be slightly higher than the others (or maybe not), but with a small number of cloudsets, it is definitely more profitable. Much more profitable. This makes the entry bar for using the cloud platform much lower.

In honor of the launch, we decided to offer another surprise to our Clients. To everyone who starts using our platform by December 31, 2013, we have made the first three cloudlettes free for the whole period of using the service .
And the three clouds are as much as 600 MHz of the processor and 384 MB of RAM. You save at least 540 rubles per month.



Thus, if your application needs a maximum of 5 cloudlets, then you will only pay two. And when the load is minimal, for example, at night or during application testing, you may not have to pay for resources at all! Such is the bonus to all customers from Infobox.
