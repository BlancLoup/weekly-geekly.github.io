New product Rambler - Virtual Communication Center Virtus

Rambler launches an alpha version of its new product - Virtus , a super-biz among communicators!

Virtus is a universal communication platform that allows you to comfortably communicate with friends on the Internet, regardless of what type of instant messenger, blog or social network they like more. Rambler - open portal! Jealousy is not our method, Shurik!

Virtus runs on Windows, MacOS and Linux, allows you to correspond with users of Mail.ru Agent, Ya.Online, Google Talk, QIP. It also allows you to send emails and receive replies to them.

Already at the alpha stage, in addition to the traditional messaging function, Virtus will help the user to follow the blog posts of his friends, sending SMS messages to friends for free (OMG!). In addition, the companion-translator is installed by default in the contact list of Virtus, which will help to quickly translate from English and German into Russian and vice versa.

Virtus is still far from perfect - we are waiting for good and evil comments and comments, and urge you to create a new generation communication platform with us. Feel free to use the feedback form: scold, suggest, praise and feel yourself as a constructive participant in the development, and not a dull commentator in the bushes!
