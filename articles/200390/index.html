How the world's first Bitcoin exchanger works



The Bitcoin exchanger that opened in Vancouver this Tuesday (as promised earlier) turned out to be quite popular: on the very first day, the terminal performed 81 operations with a total cash flow of $ 10,000. At the same time, it is curious that about a third of these eighty people were dealing with cryptocurrency for the first time: so to speak, they came to see what it is.

The operating conditions of the exchanger are such that privacy can potentially suffer: to perform operations, the device requires a palm scan; He also can scan the QR code of the wallet from the smartphone screen. The daily limit is 3,000 Canadian dollars - the limit is due to local anti-money laundering legislation. The exchange of the deposited amounts in Bitcoins is performed on the Canadian stock exchange VirtEx, and you can also receive a check if you wish.
