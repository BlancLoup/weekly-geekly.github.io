DJI Phantom quadcopter review for use with GoPro

Technical characteristics of the device:

Thoughts about ...

DJI itself is on sale in RTF (Ready-to-Fly) state, but, unfortunately, I had to calibrate the compass through a PC before launching it. The control itself was not given immediately, and the first launch was hit hard by the ceiling, but when you figure it out, it remains to enjoy. By the way, DJI is quite a strong device, during experiments and takeoffs, he once fell from 20 meters with screws down and nothing happened to him, oddly enough.

In general, the Phantom is not a toy and you shouldn‚Äôt compare it, for example, with ARDrone. With the help of DJI you can carry out complex technical work. Recently, we ordered a movie and it was necessary to shoot a car from the air. We rented a helicopter and fixed GoPro to it. I think that the rental price is roughly comparable to the purchase of a DJI, so this is an indispensable thing in such work. It's a pity that the helicopter is only 15 minutes long, but I think more capacious batteries are being sold.

Another example of use is the osmatr of the state of cultural monuments. Why order a crane for this if you can run a similar quadrocopter in summer windless weather?

In general, a powerful and cool thing and I liked it a lot more than ARDrone 2.0: it is more maneuverable and faster, and you don‚Äôt need to buy an iPhone for that. This is a professional device designed for work in industry, agriculture, aerial photography, etc. Such drones as DJI are very different from amateur toys like ARDrone. They work in the pouring rain, and in the cold, and in the heat. In addition, such an important function as a return to the home point on GPS is unlikely to allow losing the device.

The price of DJI Phantom in Russia is about 30,000 rubles (in the USA it is $ 679). A lot of it? I think so, but on the basis of the functionality, it is quite a normal and reasonable price, and if you take into account that this thing will not be your toy, it will help you in your work very cheaply.
