Be a man, open ip

The main thing that I want to convey in this post is that when opening the company, you do not evade taxes (do tax officers read Giktaits?), But in fact you want to become an individual entrepreneur or not, with all the advantages and disadvantages of this concept.

Perhaps I'll start with the minuses:

  1. The SP responds with all of its assets. Yes, this is true, and in this place we can return your head to the warm sand, or understand that you will set the framework for this responsibility as an IP.

    Let me explain - let's say you as IP Ivanov I.I., enter into an agreement with Romashka LLC, under which you work for them, write the software module, and if the agreement says that the planned module availability date is 12/31/2016, and for each day delay you pay a fine in the amount of 30% of the contract value, and the maximum amount of the fine is not limited, then yes, you hit, and maybe your apartment, if it is 100% owned by you, and it does not contain minor children, will go to LLC ‚ÄúChamomile‚Äù by the court, but if you read the contact before signing it, and correct whether this clause is at a penalty of 0.1% per day, and the maximum amount of the fine cannot exceed 10% of the contract value, then everything starts to look different.

    In fact, contracts with SP are signed by others. As a rule, the head of Romashka LLC understands that you are not ready to work without money until the end of the year, and you agree (you are an entrepreneur, and negotiations are now part of your work) that you provide your company with programming advice, on a chargeable basis, for 5 rubles a day. There are practically no fines in such contracts; think further yourself.

    There may be fines from tax (for not reporting, or for late payments), but based on such considerations, it is better not to sign anything at all except an employment contract in life - this is dangerous! For example, if you sell your car and don‚Äôt pay taxes, the tax may issue you a fine, and you will be surprised, but such a fine is unlimited!   2. I will not have a hospital / vacation / seniority / thermal slippers / wheels for hamsters. Yes, it will not. You are no longer an employee, but an individual entrepreneur, have you forgotten? So what should I do?

    Vacation - calculate your annual income based on the fact that you will work not 12, but 11 months. I will give an example at the end of the article.

    Insurance, business phone, slippers and all that you lose in comparison with the current place of work - include in the amount of annual income compensation. At the same time I urge you to do it honestly - if you do not have an official car now, then it is not nice to include compensation for it. I can say simply about insurance - if you are under 30, there are no children and chronic diseases, then it will not do for you. In other cases, it must be considered.
