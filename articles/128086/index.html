Conference Symfony Camp UA 2011



The Symfony Camp UA 2011 conference is dedicated to developing web applications using the PHP Framework Symfony . The main theme of the conference is the new version of the Symfony 2 framework. This is the third conference to be held on October 29, 2011 in Kiev.

To participate, you must register . Conference Free!


The conference program is still being formed. Anyone wishing to make a presentation at Symfony Camp UA can apply at the site 2011.symfonycamp.org.ua .


You can choose one of the topics suggested below or suggest your own:

- Doctrine 2 (ORM, ODM, MongoDB) / Propel 1.6
- Twig
- Form + Validation
- Symfony2 Security
- symfony CMF
- Silex
- Dependency Injection and Symfony2 Service Container
- Accelerate development with symfony2
- Development of high-load sites with symfony2
- Optimization frontend (Caching, Assetic)
- Admin for symfony2
- Unit and Functional Testing with Symfony2
- Transfer project from 1.x to 2
