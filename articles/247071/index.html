Features and configuration of 3CX WebMeeting

3CX WebMeeting Features

3CX WebMeeting is a plugin for IP-PBX 3CX Phone System that allows you to organize video conferences in a browser via Web-RTC. On the client side, Google Chrome v.35 or higher must be installed. Let's look at how to configure and the basis of the functionality of 3CX WebMeeting.




Go!..

Enable WebMeeting on Side 3 of CX Phone System

For any commercial version of 3CX Phone System, conferences for 3 participants are available for free. With a demo key, this functionality is closed.

1. Activate the license

2. Assign a portal administrator from PBX subscribers. Webmeeting portal will be created automatically as a subdomain 3CX.EU




3. Indicate the subscribers to whom the functionality of video conferencing will be available.

3CX WebMeeting Interface
The 3CX WebMeeting interface is divided into 4 parts:
Top Menu




Members




Chat



Video with other members





Share Screen
With this item you can publish the screen completely or a separate window (application).
Co Browse
This feature allows you to send a link from Google Chrome to other members.
1. Click "Co Browse" and select the page you want to share.
2. Locate the 3CX logo on the right side of the address bar.

3. Click on the 3CX logo and the link will be sent to all participants, the link will open in a new tab.
Attention: When scrolling a page or clicking on links, this is not transmitted to other participants. To send a new link, click on the 3CX logo again.
React
In the React menu, each of the participants can state their reaction towards the conference organizer.
