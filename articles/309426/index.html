The role of content in website promotion and customer acquisition

What determines the content of the site and promotes its promotion, affects the attraction of customers and increasing traffic. What type of content to give preference and where to order it.



The question of creating a website every day is given to thousands of Internet users who are interested in promoting a business, expanding the client base of firms and companies, in disseminating interesting and informative information, news, etc. Creating websites independently or with the help of specialists, IT companies is only the first technical step. Further success of the project directly depends on the content, in other words - on the content. Content is a powerful lever of influence on potential customers, buyers, readers and subscribers.

Content feature in site promotion


Filling the site with content allows you, first of all, to provide guests of your resource with complete information about the project, products and services. "Dumb" resources that do not have a semantic content, will cause only bewilderment for the visitor who has come, who cannot become your client.

How to fill the site


There is an erroneous opinion that the content is a purely textual content of the web pages. However, this concept is much wider and multifaceted.

At the moment, content marketing is represented by such commonly used categories as:

  • Text content;
  • Media content.

Briefly about them ...



Text content


This category is best known and familiar to Internet users. Without such content, virtually no resource will manage. Even one that specializes in video or audio content. After all, the text allows you to describe the meaning and purpose of the project, briefly describe the section, product. In other words, the text allows you to awaken the primary interest of visitors to the resource.

Media content


This category is represented by audio, video, graphic content. These include pictures, photos, videos, audio recordings that allow you to sell information, goods or services in a bright visual form. Such content is particularly cost-effective to use for online stores, reviews, otzovikov.

This type of content is in demand primarily because of human nature - reading huge amounts of text to many is elementary laziness, but to see a few vivid pictures, or fascinating video recordings - this is more interesting.
