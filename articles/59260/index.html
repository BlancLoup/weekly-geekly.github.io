Useful video

The online video has a reputation for something frivolous and entertaining. However, in reality, this is just a format, not guilty of the fact that it is used to distribute "Yozhin s Bazhin" or "Guitar". There are several video resources that store a collection of files that may be useful for self-education. My colleagues at ‚ÄúRuformator‚Äù found several such services.


1. Youtube Edu is not an independent service, but a channel on Youtube. The content is very colorful - from university lectures on matan to a video about teenage pregnancy. It is important that almost all videos are somehow generated by either universities or companies with a strong research spirit (such as Wolfram). Now I myself am in the process of studying a course in solid state chemistry . The materials of the channel have become useful for me not only in terms of knowledge, but also in terms of the ability to express my thoughts. Courses with subtitles help those who wish to pump not only the subject, but also English.
