In one old post I mentioned components, their versions and product lines . As I wrote the material based on the experience of working as an integrator in Motorola, the problematic was taken from there - from the engineering of industrial-level products.

And the speakers relied on the rich work of another telecom giant, Sony-Ericsson and other similar companies. For cell phone manufacturers it is very important to be able to combine various features of their products depending on the operator, price segment, form factor, country of sale and many other things. Hence, the long-standing problem is how to ensure, with the lowest possible cost, the best opportunity to adapt products to these conditions.

The code of all complex systems is somehow divided into modules, i.e. the components . So, at the product level it is more logical to operate with components and their variations - versions and branches . In this direction, work and led - have developed a model that takes into account the components and the rules by which the components are assembled. The concept of a base of components is introduced, from which with the help of a configurator a whole variety of products necessary for the end user is obtained. They describe the developed model. And the work goes on the junction of SCM, design and project management.

Unfortunately, it is not indicated at what stage of readiness and practical application this model is located. Knowing that she is engaged in a man from a commercial company, we can assume that the work is being done in a practical way.
