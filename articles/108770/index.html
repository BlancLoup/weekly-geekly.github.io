TeamViewer for Android

Today, on the official utility website, an announcement about the release of the TeamViewer beta for Android has appeared. The version is operational until January 31, 2011 and supports connections to teamviewer hosts of versions 6 and above.

The mobile version has all the features of an older brother and even has a Russian-language interface. The limited period of validity is most likely associated with testing the utility, and later we are waiting for the division into pro and free versions (as is the case with previously released iPhone versions).

Download TeamViewer beta for Android here .


PS For those who do not know what TeamViewer is - a utility for remotely controlling a computer. Optimized to provide remote assistance and technical support. For personal use, it is free, and for commercial it provides for different licensing options.
