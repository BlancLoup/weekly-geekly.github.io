A study of the American gaming market. Demographics, sales and other interesting facts

Association Entertainment Software Association has published a recent study of the American market of computer and video games. According to the report, the average age of a gamer is 34 years!





Demography



  • The average age of the buyer of games is 40 years . 54% of them are men, and 46% are women
  • In 2010, the number of players over 50 years old will reach 26%
  • The average number of years devoted to computer or video games is 12 . At the same time, in men this period reaches 13 , and women do not exceed 10
  • In 2010, 64% of players prefer to play with someone (increased compared to 2009 - 62%, 2008- 59%)

Parental involvement


  • 93% of parents are present when buying games or renting
  • 64% of parents believe that games have a positive impact on the lives of their children.
  • In 86% of cases, children ask for parental permission before they buy games or rent them.
  • 48% of parents play with their children at least once a week
  • 76% of parents find the parental control tools present on all modern consoles useful. In addition, parents assign time limits for games more often than for other types of entertainment:
    • 83% of parents set time limits for video games.
    • 78% limit TV viewing
    • 75% restrict the use of the Internet
    • 66% limit movie viewing


4 most popular reasons why parents play with their children:
  1. It is fun for the whole family - 87%
  2. Because children asked them - 83%
  3. This is a good opportunity to be closer to your children - 75%
  4. This is a good opportunity to check the content of games - 60%
