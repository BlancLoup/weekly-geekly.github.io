The embodiment of the transportation system of the future

What the Communist science-fiction writers have been talking about for so long has finally come to pass. Long live the future!



Recently, this blog has described what we have already seen many times - what the future transportation system should be. And today you can already look at how it works.
Heathrow launched, promised two years ago , an automated road. Personal unmanned vehicle modules move at a speed of 40 km / h on a special road network, and are able to transport 4 passengers and their luggage between the 5th airport terminal and a commercial vehicle fleet.

To be more precise, in theory, they are able to relocate them from any point (stop) of this transport network to another point (there are many of them in the network).


Battery powered cars. Be sure to watch the video. It shows the prevalence of the network, describes parking and more.
