Announcement of the master class of Gaidar Magdanurov, director of investment at Runa Capital, in the framework of DevCon 2013



Dear software developers and developers! On behalf of the DevCon 2013 conference team, I am pleased to announce, within the framework of the conference, a master class ‚Äú From Idea to Success ‚Äù, which will be held by Gaidar Magdanurov , Investment Director at Runa Capital .

Want to learn more about the successful implementation of ideas in IT? Have an idea, a team of developers and are interested in how to succeed in a competitive market? Want to know how to attract an investor and become a leader? Interested in examples of success, investment experience and major startup mistakes? Then this master class is for you!

The purpose of the master class


To acquaint participants with the main stages of creating a technology startup. Share the skills and competencies required from the entrepreneur and the project team to achieve results. Show examples of successful business models and discuss key success criteria.

Listeners will receive the decals about the end of the master class and ask questions to a well-known expert of the venture industry.

About speaker


Gaidar Magdanurov is an investment director at venture capital fund Runa Capital. He is engaged in investment, development of the technological entrepreneurship ecosystem, entrepreneurs training and the introduction of modern technologies. Before joining Runa Capital, he worked at Microsoft Russia as an evangelist, head of web technologies, head of technology entrepreneurship, and managing director of the Microsoft Seed Fund. Prior to Microsoft and already in Microsoft, he participated in the launch of more than fifty web projects as a developer, architect, project manager or mentor.

Runa Capital is an investment fund investing in early-stage high-tech companies. Directions of investments - software, mobile and cloud services.

School program


Beginning after lunch - block duration 1 hour 15 minutes

  • Business models of Internet projects and mobile startups
  • Monetization mechanisms
  • Creating a product concept
  • Business model development

Break - after a break the block lasting 1 hour and 15 minutes

  • Formation of the project team
  • Project management and product creation
  • Product launch
  • Marketing, attracting and retaining audience
  • Investment attraction

About DevCon 2013


DevCon conference is not the first year and has already managed to catch the fancy of Russian developers and guests from other countries. A key feature of this series of conferences is its country format, the venue is carefully chosen from the best guesthouses near Moscow. At the same time, all conference participants drove into a country holiday home and lived there for two days of the conference, receiving all the pleasant advantages of these places.

  • Modern client applications
  • Mobile development
  • Cloud computing
  • Software Development and Lifecycle Management Tools
  • Development Technologies and Programming Languages
  • Development, monetization and promotion of applications for app stores
  • Modern Design Microsoft
  • Corporate development, data processing
