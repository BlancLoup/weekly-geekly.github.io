ImageCMS Patch 4.1


Repentance - the path to perfection! And, we admit, after the last release , we had good reasons to sprinkle ashes on our heads, and dancing melancholically. BUT! Not forgetting the beloved tradition of working and trying to produce a high-quality product, we preferred active actions. As a result, we collected all the updates and fixes in a single patch for the system.

Actually, we dedicate this blitz report to this.


Thanks to our more than energetic users, we quickly fixed all the detected bugs and unaccounted moments in the stable version. They, as you already know, turned out to be quite a few, but we all cleaned it to shine.

Another item in the list of elaborated - the system of import-export. In addition to eliminating all sorts of problems (we will not publish any sneeze;), we corrected errors related to the import of photos.

So, stabilized. Now we are working with a clear conscience to fulfill the wishes of our users. Of course, not looking up from the bulk of sales and further planning. The bright heads of our developers on the eve of the end of the world are replete with ideas for improving the system, so our users will find several useful innovations.
