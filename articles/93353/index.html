IPhone 4G on the radar again


This time in Vietnam. Few details inside.
UPD: under the cut added video and some technical specs

The guys from the Vietnamese site Taoviet outdid themselves. As far as can be seen from the photos, they managed to get a real iPhone 4G 16GB. Judging by the numbers of the form XXXX on the back of the black cover, this is really a model that goes on sale. No OS is installed on the device except for the Bonfire test utility. Despite this, the phone looks very neat, which can be expected that the guys from Apple will hunt it for a long time so that the current owner doesn‚Äôt reveal the truth of the iPhone 4G stuff to us :)

There are some differences between this pattern and the one that popped up earlier on the gizmodo website. In particular, two bolts at the bottom of the case disappeared, which may indicate that this prototype is closer to the serial launch (its design has become more like the design of the current 3G and 3Gs). The differences are visible in the photo.

As it became known later, the guys from Apple did not have time for that guy :) There were long-awaited photos showing us the stuffing of the iPhone.
