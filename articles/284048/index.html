Roskomnadzor will explain to Google and Yandex the law on data storage

Roskomnadzor will hold ten explanatory meetings with representatives of various industries, including Internet companies and e-commerce companies, banks, insurance companies, sellers of air and railway tickets, RBC reports .



On September 1, 2015, Law No. 242 on the storage of personal data in Russia will enter into force . Roskomnadzor will be responsible for monitoring and supervising the processing of personal data. Until mid-March, Roskomnadzor will hold meetings with Internet companies, including Google, Yandex, Mail.ru Group and Rambler & Co. Then there are meetings with PayPal, eBay and other e-commerce companies, as well as with banks, insurance companies and sellers of air and railway tickets. Vadim Ampelonskiy, a representative of Roskomnadzor, noted that at the end of this session there could be proposals for clarifying the law.

The industry needs to understand how the law will work. For example, is it possible to store copies of personal data in Russia, but not to transfer the server.
