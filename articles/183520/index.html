Why Hong Kong is an Excellent Option for Snowden



Many at first did not understand why the NSA informant fled to Hong Kong. But knowledgeable people immediately saw all the genius of his decision. There are at least six reasons for choosing this country.

1. The convoluted extradition procedure is shown in the diagram . The average time between the beginning of the process and the receipt of the case in the court of appeal is one year. Until the last court of appeal - another three years. In difficult cases, the extradition process may be delayed for a much longer period.

Hong Kong's legal system is based on British law, in which compliance with the procedures is mandatory and immutable. Any error in the document or procedure will send the case for review, so the show with lawyers and judges begins anew.
