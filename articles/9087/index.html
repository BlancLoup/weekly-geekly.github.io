The domain administrator .travel is about to go bankrupt

Domain zone .travel is endangered. The reason is that the domain administrator has serious financial problems. This is not at all surprising, because since the beginning of registration in this strange zone with a long suffix and a high price, only 25,000 domains have been registered.

The .travel administrator is Tralliance Corporation, a subsidiary of TheGlobe.com. So, recently the parent company has published financial statements ( Form 10-Q ), judging by which, it has been left to live for a very short time. The company runs out of cash reserves, but there is no profit. In March 2007, the corporation was forced to close its unprofitable divisions of computer games and VoIP-telephony, so the closure of the "domain" division is also quite likely.
