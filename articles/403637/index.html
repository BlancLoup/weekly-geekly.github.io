One of the very first online courses on robotics concerned the topic of "Artificial Intelligence" and was released on the Udacity platform. Now you can choose several courses on this topic. For example, one of them is Artificial Intelligence Enginner. An unusually paid course, which consists of two sections, costing $ 800 each, but you can win a grant and study for free. By the way, the feature of the Udacity platform is that students who have successfully completed online courses can even help with employment. True, I have not heard a single such example in Russia.
