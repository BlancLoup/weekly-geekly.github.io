Russia and the United States will jointly fight free file sharing

On December 21, US Trade Representative Ron Kirk announced that Russia and the United States had agreed on an action plan to combat intellectual property violations, according to which Russia pledges to actively pursue pirates in all areas - to close websites illegally distributing copyrighted materials, to conduct raids, seize and destroy products and equipment used to violate copyright, to seek more severe punishments for pirates.

In addition, Russia will have to change the legislation in such a way as to strengthen administrative responsibility for copyright infringements and impose on Internet providers part of their efforts to combat piracy.

It is possible that we can talk about the introduction of an analogue of the "six warnings" system ("six strikes"), which is now being introduced in the United States . The provider will be obliged to monitor what the user is downloading, and if he has downloaded the "pirate", make him a warning.
