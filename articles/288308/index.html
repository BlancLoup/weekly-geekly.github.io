16-bit screensaver for "The Simpsons" from the fans of the animated series



"The Simpsons" - the most popular animated series of all times and peoples. The popularity of the cartoon was won due to the original plot, the drawing of characters and a huge amount of humor, which helps to better perceive the problems that are considered in the cartoon.

The army of admirers of The Simpsons numbers millions of people, and among them there are also multipliers. A team of experts who are not related to the cartoon, recently presented its own version of the screensaver for the cartoon, made in the style of "pixel art." The graphics here are 16-bit, the soundtrack is appropriate.

The authors of the screensaver are Paul Robertson (the author of the toy Scott Pilgrim), Ivan Dixon (Rubber House) + music from Jeremy Dower. It is worth noting that earlier producers of the animated series invited third-party authors, including Banksy, to cooperate. Perhaps soon we will see a similar screensaver officially, in the next series of the cartoon.
