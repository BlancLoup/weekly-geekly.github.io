Presented by Verizon iPhone 4



Verizon Wireless and Apple announced today that the CDMA version of the iPhone 4 will be available on the Verizon Wireless network starting February 10. Verizon Wireless customers will have the opportunity to pre-order February 3.

Outside of Verizon, the phone has basically remained the same, although Verizon's CDMA network does not support simultaneous voice and data transmissions like the GSM version. It has a new antenna design due to the fact that the CMDA requires a different antenna configuration.

The device uses iOS version 4.2.5, which includes new Personal Hotspot features that allow you to use your iPhone to connect to up to five Wi-Fi devices.

The suggested retail price for the Verizon iPhone 4 with a new two-year contract is $ 199.99 for 16 GB and $ 299.99 for 32 GB.
