HP Pavilion tx2550er Notebook Review

Not so long ago, I became the proud owner of this wonderful laptop. I bought it second-hand, so that the full configuration can only guess. In my version it was the laptop itself, charging, a stylus and two batteries (regular and high capacity).





Specifications


  • Processor : Turion X2 Ultra 2100Mhz
  • RAM : 3Gb DDR2 800Mhz RAM
  • Hard Drive : 320Gb 5400rpm SATA
  • Optical drive : DVD-RW, internal, removable
  • Video Card : ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3200
  • Screen : 12.1 inches, 1280x800, widescreen, touch (wacom)
  • Network card : 10/100/1000 Mbit
  • Modem : yes
  • Wireless interfaces : Bluetooth, Wi-Fi 802.11g
  • Connectors on the case : 3xUSB 2.0, S-Video, VGA, S / PDIF, LAN, Modem, Expansion Port 3 (dock connector), microphone, 2 headphones jacks.
  • Expansion Slot : ExpressCard / 34
  • Card reader : MS, SD, xD-Picture Card
  • IR receiver : yes
  • Webcam : 1.5MP
  • Optional : remote control, fingerprint scanner, built-in stereo microphone, speakers.
  • Weight : 1.9kg.
  • Price : ~ 38t.r. (~ $ 1250)


Durability and Reliability


Probably everyone has already noticed from the picture that the laptop screen has another degree of freedom. This is done so that you can expand the screen and lay it on the keyboard so that it is convenient to draw. The swivel mechanism is gently fixed in the extreme positions. The rotation is carried out only in one direction - in order not to get confused on the body an arrow is drawn.
Initially, looking at this device, it seems that the loop is very weak and the screen will soon fall off. This is not the case at the same time. On the one hand, deliberately breaking off the screen is not so difficult, on the other hand, the loop is strong enough to withstand the screen turns back and forth without any consequences. In addition, in order not to create an excessive load on it, there are protrusions on the sides of the keyboard that fit into the slots on the cover when it is lowered (there are slots on both sides). This creates additional stability of the cover and, accordingly, the strength of the whole structure.



Convenience


Despite the size of the laptop, his keyboard is full-sized. Moving from a 15-inch to 12-inch laptop was almost imperceptible.
The screen, although small, allows you to see everything I need. In extreme cases, the font can always be increased. Yet it is much better than any kind of netbook.
Since the extra battery allows you to work with a laptop for up to 4 hours without recharging, it is quite convenient to watch movies on the bus on it. Turn on the movie, insert the headphones, turn and lay the screen on the keyboard. The result is a fairly convenient player =).



In addition, the touch screen allows you to draw in real time. We lead with a stylus and see what we have drawn. This favorably distinguishes it from a computer with a portable tablet, since it allows you not to turn your head and immediately see your result. A keyboard gives an advantage over all sorts of tablet PCs.



Special thanks I would say to the developers for the touchpad. Firstly, the scroll bar is taken out a little separately, which is quite convenient. Secondly, right above the touchpad there is a button to turn it off. No function keys ... simple and convenient. In addition, it is made so that it is almost impossible to accidentally press it.



Compatibility


All hardware is supported in both Windows and Linux distributions. The only thing I had to tinker with was the video card driver. He did not want to wake up the video card after exiting the standby or hibernation mode.
Numerous buttons on the lid of the laptop are defined as keyboard keys and video and audio players react to them without any extra settings.
The control panel, oddly enough, also begins to work without installing additional drivers. The functions of its buttons are similar to those of the buttons on the cover.

What they can do at all


The field of activity for this device is enormous. I will give a few simple examples:
1. Workplace of the programmer, sysadmin or any other who is engaged in mental work. Access to the workplace is securely protected by fingerprint authorization.
2. Multimedia station for communication via the Internet, as well as watching videos through a large portable monitor and speakers. Watching videos in nature or on the bus.
3. There are craftsmen who connect to the beech GPS module and make it awesome navigator.
4. Workplace designer or artist. The touch screen allows you to make thumbnails easily and quickly.

Shortcomings


The most, perhaps, strong flaw, is that the laptop is very hot. In winter and spring, it is almost not felt, but in summer, the problem will probably manifest itself. Also, for the disadvantage, I consider a lot of weight with an extra battery, but for long work I have to sacrifice weight.
