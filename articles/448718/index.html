Creation of a metal farm in SPDS Metal structures

We invite you to take part in the free webinar "Creating a metal farm in SPDS Metal structures."

REGISTER



The webinar will be held on April 23 at 11:00 Moscow time. The webinar program includes a demonstration of the new features of the SPDS Metalware software product designed to automate the development of design documentation of the KM and AS brands.

The purpose of the webinar is to demonstrate to CAD users how the efficiency of the work of design engineers is increased when using the specialized software for the DPS Metalwork. Within the framework of the webinar, a new program functionality will be demonstrated, namely, a new team for the creation of piles for various purposes.

The program implements new parametric objects: reinforced concrete piles, steel piles of pipes intended for the construction of supports for oil pipelines, bridge supports, high-voltage power lines for antenna, mast structures, open switchgears, machines and mechanisms with dynamic loads, communication lines and other structures, according to project documentation in thawed, with seasonal freezing and permafrost soils. The functionality of the program performs automatic numbering of piles and automatically generates a specification for the pile and a group specification for the pile field.

What tasks solves the SPDS Metal?

  • drawing up plans for plans, cuts, metal structures;
  • the ability to create various structural solutions of metal structures;
  • automatic formation of the list of elements and specifications of metal-roll, as well as specifications of elements for the EXPERT section;
  • drawing up drawings in accordance with GOST 21.502-2016 ‚ÄúRules for the execution of working documentation for metal structures‚Äù.

The webinar is focused on specialists of design departments, employees of IT-services, as well as all those interested in the capabilities of the SPDS Metalware program.
