Samsung announces Chromebook 2 release in May

Good Friday, Habr!

The other day, Samsung announced the launch of the second generation of Chromebook notebooks, which, by itself, have become more productive, long-playing and interesting in their capabilities. New Chromebooks will be available in screen configurations of 11.6 and 13.3 inches. Pay attention to the beautiful styling of the body under the skin.


Both Chromebook 2 models feature Exynos 5 Octa branded single-chip systems. The processors are manufactured using the 28-nm SLP process technology (Super Low Power 28-nm High-K Metal Gate) and consist of eight cores integrated by the ARM architecture big.LITTLE.



The 13.3-inch Chromebook 2 boasts Full HD (1920x1080) screen with a 250 nits brightness and 4 GB RAM. The younger model has a 11.6-inch display with a resolution of 1366x768 and 2 GB of RAM. Both versions of the Chromebook 2 are equipped with 16 GB eMMC iNAND drives, HDMI and USB 2.0 / 3.0 connectors, a microSD slot, two 2W speakers and a webcam with HD shooting.

Chromebook 2 is lightning-fast: a cold start lasts no more than 10 seconds, and the device leaves sleep in less than a second. From one charge, the laptop can work for a full working or school day - up to 8.5 hours offline.



Of the undoubted advantages of devices based on Google‚Äôs Chrome OS, regular automatic updates of software and functions, simplicity of work with operating systems can be distinguished (all you need is a Google account to synchronize and configure cloud storage). In addition, the Samsung Chromebook 2 series comes with a pre-installed software package worth more than $ 100. It includes a smartphone access app, Air Droid Premium for cloud data management, a Wunderlist Pro task management and collaboration app, and LittleBridge.com English learning app.



Samsung Chromebook 2 will be available in the US in May, priced at $ 400 for the older model and $ 320 for the 11.6-inch device. Prices in the domestic market have not yet been officially announced.

  • Battery - declared operating time up to 8.5 hours;
