Terminals of registration of arrival and departure


Terminals for time recording do not control any actuators (turnstiles, electric locks, etc.). This is due to their low price - from 9 670 rubles . Software almost always comes bundled.

The logic of work: the first identification - the arrival, the second - care. Will work on condition that the manager decides to pay for the work day only if the employee has been authenticated twice, when he came and when he left.

Most of the terminals use a human biometric attribute as an identifier. Most often it is the imprint of a skin pattern of a finger, the shape of a face, a venous finger or hand pattern.

Biometric terminals are the most convenient. ID can not be lost, forgotten, broken, in short, he is always with you. In the case of a fingerprint, the main limitation is the purity of the hands, because The sensor glass can easily be stained, and the identification error will be higher.
