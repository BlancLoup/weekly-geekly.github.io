Distributed computing Would you share your computing power?

I have a fairly powerful personal station. Powerful graphics card with the possibility of parallel computing.

And it so happens that the PC can stand 8-10 hours without any load. Turn off laziness. And the electricity is still being spent.

I would be happy to share my capacities for large-scale calculations, design, modeling, etc. at no cost. Somehow I personally have an interesting team, projects, institutes, researchers.




Can anyone know if there are such voluntary computing distributed networks?
It would be interesting to create a project where people could share their capacities, thereby increasing the efficiency of work and their machines and various studies. With the guarantee that they are not part of the botnet.

And the question to the experts, is it real? I, obviously, is not the very first to think about it, but I have not heard about the implementation of such projects.
