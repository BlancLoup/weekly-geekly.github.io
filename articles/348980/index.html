About CRM


CRM for dating investors and startups


Venture Fund Dorm Room Fund released VCWiz , a tool with which startups and business owners can find an investor who is ready to invest in a company. In fact, this is a kind of online CRM-system in which you can enter information about a startup, register your industry and even specify some wishes for an investor. There you can also find a project for the investor himself or spy on what competitors you have to compete with, read the reviews. Information about recent investments, co-investors, deals, etc. is available to certain user groups. The mechanism of this CRM suggests the possibility of moving through the stages of the transaction: from negotiations to closure. The system works with external sources, social networks and uses machine learning to recommend start-ups to investors. By the way, this system was developed as part of the MIT master's thesis.

Our comment: The idea is successful, implementation at height. Such bases make the relations between the parties open and transparent, eliminate the influence of the human factor and accelerate business processes within the investment order. In addition, such interactivity gives additional contact in a relaxed atmosphere outside of calls and correspondence, which means everyone has time to think and analyze.

Of course, this is not a CRM system in the sense in which Russian business has become accustomed to seeing it, but it is CRM in terms of managing customer relationships. It would be nice if similar solutions were implemented for the B2C sector, for example, in insurance.

Based on the reasoning of Manash Chaudhuri, ConvergeHub about blockchain in CRM


Have you gotten used to backend-as-a-service (BaaS)? BaaS is now a blockchain-as-a-service. Sophisticated guys have already noticed this Microsoft page (someone has already used similar in work, share?), But other corporations will soon offer BaaS. And by the way, it is expected that the blockchain will find its place in CRM systems. One of the biggest problems with CRM systems is incorrect or duplicate customer data. The blockchain will allow to realize the principle ‚Äúone client - one block‚Äù, and thus the information will be unified and accurate, and all transactions will also relate to this block. Thus, sales, marketing and support will get rid of the hated duplicates in the systems. And of course, advanced customers and business customers will more trust, for example, purchases in your online store, if the blockchain is used in CRM. Plus, the blockchain will help to make exclusively personalized offers and unique loyalty programs.

Our comment: Unfortunately, while the blockchain is associated (and sometimes confused) with the bitcoin among the majority of the population. Meanwhile, this is a promising technology for situations where potentially unsafe transactions are made. Blockchain in CRM-systems is still difficult to imagine, but in banking and online retail - quite. The main thing is that the use was not nominal - we have already seen several examples of how software vendors declared neural networks onboard their development, and in fact, all this turned out to be not a particularly sought-after business. So far, the Russian small and medium businesses do not have a very high culture of automation, the words ‚Äúblockchain, bigdata, neural network‚Äù will only suggest that he buys an expensive solution and overpays for unnecessary functionality. So the technical implementation of the blockchain in CRM is far away, and the transformation of technology into a marketing piece without real implementation is digging the grave for ourselves. However, we do not exclude experiments with the same Salesforce.

Amazon presses


Walmart cuts to 1,000 jobs at headquarters. Optimization caused by the growing threat from Amazon. The first wave of reductions at the beginning of 2018 amounted to 500 people, and marketers, HR specialists, merchandisers, real estate managers and others fell into it. The second 500 people out of the expected 1000 will be cut in 2019. At the same time, operations will be carried out to reduce the real estate pool to form a new headquarters.

Our comment: Amazon is destined to crush more than one giant and radically change more than one rule of business and technology. To confront him in the American and European markets is extremely difficult. Literally in mid-January, at CES-2018, it was possible to observe how Amazon Alexa was introduced in almost every iron and refrigerator, how hard she struggled with Google's voice assistant. And Amazon will determine how retail will become for a few more years.

Regarding the structure of reductions at Walmart, it reflects the market as a whole: managers, marketers and HR are in many ways able to replace automation, companies are better focused on investing in IT infrastructure.
