The test time is 105 minutes, or 1 hour, 45 minutes. In normal mode, the battery was not enough for me for one day - it was fully charged in the morning (10-11 am), it sat down completely at 6-7 pm, just when I wanted to read a book.

Extended Battery:

The test time is 157 minutes, or 2 hours, 37 minutes. One and a half times more.
