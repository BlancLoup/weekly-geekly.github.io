ShopConf 2016 - June 17 in Skolkovo - the program is formed

There are already quite a few e-commerce conferences in Russia. All of them are interesting in their own way. However, there has not yet been a single conference at the junction of the world of programmers and the world of online store owners.

In an attempt to correct this situation, the organizing committee of the fairly well-known DevConf conference programmers in the world created a new event - ShopConf - a sort of fusion of the programmer and trading community.
We deliberately abandoned the PR reports in the direction filled with interesting speeches. We focused not only on the successful sales orchestra, but also bring to your attention reports at the interface of the programmer platform, engine and successful sales experience .

We are trying to lead to understandable simple dialogue between the customer and the programmer.

Kuzma Feskov in his report will tell you how to build work with programmers correctly, if you don‚Äôt understand anything in programming. Simple and clear ways to control will allow you to always keep your finger on the pulse. Save your money, as well as to protect your online store from accidents. He will share with you ways to protect the personal data of your customers, as well as tell you how CRM differs from CMS and why it is important to understand these differences. Ponders on the choice of engine for your online store and the pros and cons of boxed and custom-made solutions.

Natalia Karpenko will touch on a very important topic of working with Google Merchant. At that time, as more and more entrepreneurs are unhappy with Yandex, this platform is gaining momentum and allows you to advertise your products at least effectively. We will talk about differences in the Yandex and Google services, find out the subtleties of placement and settings, examine the rate management system and tell you about the pitfalls of this system. In general, at the end of the report you will be able to work with this tool, if not as a professional, then as a trained entry-level specialist!

Email marketing is one of the most important sales tools today. In his report, Alexey Rakhmanin will focus on automating email marketing. That will significantly reduce costs and improve the efficiency of mailings. From the report you will learn what a transaction is, how to properly and effectively build a chain of letters and learn how to create effective trigger mailings. Abandoned shopping cart and customer base reactivation are one of the most important and pressing problems of online stores and will not be left without attention!

Contextual advertising can be both an effective sales tool in any business, and a bottomless pit devouring your money. Elena Firsova - a serious expert in the field of contextual advertising - will not allow the loss of your money! Her report will tell in detail how to create advertising campaigns correctly and effectively, how to keep your ads up to date, what categorical ads are, and how to stop and start companies correctly and on time. Income optimization and conversion - also will not go unheeded!

Dmitry Popov will tell about the strategy of a full expansion that is gaining momentum . How to reduce your costs by transferring the warehouse and packing the goods in complete center? What is a complete filment and how it works. Why and to whom it can be profitable. In general, a comprehensive detailed story awaits you in this report!

It is no secret that many stores live, in fact, on random orders. Alexey Kravets, in his report, will put an end to this waste! We will talk about repeat sales, about how important and necessary it is to keep your customer and turn him into a permanent one. This will allow you to reduce advertising costs, create your customer base and sell, sell, sell ... The customer base is the key to the stability of your business.
