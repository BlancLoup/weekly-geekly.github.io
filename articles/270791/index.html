DataTalks # 4: Predictive Analytics

The fourth DataTalks took place on October 10th. This time the topic of the meeting was the predictive analytics, and we would like to share the video recordings of speeches with the community.

Why predictive analytics?
It allows, based on historical data, to predict various events in the future, such as customer behavior or the results of actions taken. Thanks to her, a business can make optimal decisions and take into account forecasts about future actions and wishes of their clients.
