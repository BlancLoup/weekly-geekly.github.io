Plugin for Habr - the best topics, quick access to sections, statistics

Today is Friday and this is an excellent occasion to improve the life of the habrasoobshchestvu. I suggest to get acquainted with the plugin that will help you quickly browse the best topics, go to the main sections and find out what sky you are on.

A bit of background


Recently I began to notice that, using daily habrom, I lose a lot of time on fairly standard actions:

  • see if there is anything new and interesting in the posts,
  • and what can be read today among the best topics?
  • go quickly to your favorites, where a few days ago I noted a useful article for myself.

This list can still be continued, but I think you understood the point. I wanted to use Habrom quickly and conveniently. I went to google and habra something suitable, because surely I am not the only one, but, unfortunately, I did not find anything suitable. This fact itself became decisive for writing your own plugin.

I just want to say to all fans of FF, Opera, IE, Safari that the plugin was written under Chrome because for the author the method proposed by Google was the easiest and fastest, this is a purely subjective opinion, nothing else should be sought here.

Plugin


The plugin allows you to quickly jump to personal sections:
  • Posts
  • Q & A
  • Comments
  • Notes
  • Favorites
  • Personal mail
  • Settings

And also to the general sections. In my opinion (purely subjective), such an organization is more convenient, all sections are immediately visible, and in any of them you can go with one click.
And, of course, you always, without looking up from the main occupation, you can view the best posts in 24 hours.
