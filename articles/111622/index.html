Video Resident Evil 2 on PlayStation Phone is online



Do you still think that PlayStation Phone is a fake? There is less chance of it, although, of course, there is such a possibility. A couple of days ago, another series of photos of the PlayStation Phone and a description of the device hit the Web after testing PlayStation Phone by Chinese bloggers . Now the network has "flowed away" video tests Resident Evil 2 and Rage Racer on PlayStation Phone. Let me remind you that until now no one has seen how the gameplay goes on this device. It is worth noting that the device that we see in the video is no different from the photos - the same D-pad, buttons from the PlayStation, and more. There is also the notorious touchscreen, which is designed to improve the functionality of the device when playing games.

Unfortunately, the format of the games for this device remains unclear, but the video clearly shows us that to launch the game in this case, a third-party ROM Buddy application is used, which is available for Android devices.
