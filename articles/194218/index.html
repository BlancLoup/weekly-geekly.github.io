Ramos i9 - 8.9-inch tablet with Full HD screen on the Intel Atom


By the end of the year, tablets on an Intel Atom Clover Trail + x86 chip began to appear on the Chinese market. If the 7.9 "model on this chip is not so interesting because of the low resolution of the screen, the Ramos i9 tablet impresses with its 8.9-inch screen with a resolution of 1920x1200 pixels.
The cost of the tablet is also very nice - about $ 220 including shipping.

Camera: front 2 megapixel, rear - 5 megapixel
