Nokia Belle Refresh



Finally, Nokia owners of smartphones on the street is a holiday. The Nokia Support blog tells us about the Nokia Belle Refresh update for Nokia N8, Nokia E7, Nokia C7, Nokia C6-01, Nokia X7 and Nokia Oro devices. The update carries version 111.040.1511, and for Nokia E6 - 111.140.0058. For the aforementioned devices, the update is already starting to come everywhere. For the Nokia 500, the new firmware will come in a few weeks. Wait. And now about the changes of the new update.

  • Browser Update to Improve HTML 5 Compatibility
  • Map updates, mainly improvements were made to voice search and image linking to a geographic point.
  • Update Nokia Social 1.5, the widget has been completely redesigned, and reminders have appeared from Facebook
  • The owners of the N8 have updated the Big Screen, Colorize, PlayTo, Gallery, Qt 4.8 applications, re-branded Ovi services and updated Nokia Store
  • Updating the music player, especially I want to note the new widget, which is almost half the size of the old one and most importantly, by clicking on it, the transition is not going to the general list, as it was before, but to the current track, and also pleased with the interface with more convenient gradation
  • Added about a dozen new widgets, for myself, especially noted the widget transition to offline mode and the updated widget notes



On the N8, I have already received an update, judging by the messages on the forums, on other models, this update may still be unavailable.
