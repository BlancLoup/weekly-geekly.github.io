Skype Video Cards

Jim Carrey's hero in Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind complained that St. Valentine's Day was invented by companies that print postcards. If you do not want to help the pulp and paper industry and the publishing business, try sending a video card this year.

Skypevideocards.com is a cool service that will help you send a live video card for February 14 (or any other holiday).


You will need:
1. Computer
2. Webcam
3. Microphone
4. Internet (fast)
5. Fantasy
Well, someone who would be happy to receive a video card from you.

It's very simple: you need to go to the site www.skypevideocards.com , choose one of the cover options:

Then allow the Flash application to use the webcam and microphone on your computer, and when you see yourself in the postcard window, click the Record button and actually record your message.
When everything is ready (you can check whether everything turned out as you intended), send a postcard by email or use the code to insert it into the blog.
And if you think that just reading a greeting is boring, you can try:
- sing
- Dance
- Show focus
- Provide a performance of congratulations to the puppet doll, painted hand, shadows, etc.
Or think of something complicated and amazing (remember - you will have several duplicates). Here you can get a little inspiration.
