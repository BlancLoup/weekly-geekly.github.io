What is Smart Social Media Analytics?

In the Merriam-Webster dictionary, the term ‚ÄúIntelligence‚Äù is defined as: The ability to recognize or absorb information.

Based on this definition, it can be said that simple data collection or monitoring of social media is not yet Analytics. Intelligence (Smart Analytics) process becomes only when certain conclusions have been made from the data, or the data have been used to adjust the course of action.



For example, you can simply see on the graph an increase in the number of subscribers, say that there has been a sharp change in the rate of inflow. Difference between Monitoring and Social Media Analytics

Social media and their users generate huge amounts of information every day. And nowadays, most companies naturally search for information that affects them on the Internet - references and user opinions about their brand, products and services. Social media monitoring is absolutely necessary for brands of all sizes to know what is being said about them, for which they are praised and scolded.

But simple Monitoring of social media, as mentioned above, cannot be considered as Analytics by itself. If the brand receives 8,000 positive mentions during the week, then this is certainly very happy information, but it is not clear, what were the specific references that were caused? If you do not analyze the data, then simply collecting data from several sources does not imply Analytics of the dynamics of the process.

Difference between Reporting and Social Media Analytics

Social media reporting is a pre (a) presentation of events that have already occurred, but in order for it to become an Analyst, you must ask the question WHY. Returning to our previous example - a graph showing a sharp increase in the growth of subscribers is usually the result of some existing factors. When reporting social media, you need to think about ‚ÄúAnd what follows from this?‚Äù To reflect the answer to this question in the report.

For even more in-depth understanding, you should find out, but what was so interesting about this particular message? Was this a picture or video that the public was actively sharing? How exactly is your brand used in this message? Was it advertising in its essence, or was it a congratulation on a seasonal holiday? In order to spread among the broad masses, brand content is being promoted in a variety of ways, and therefore it is useful to understand which methods of promotion have led to positive results.

By itself, reporting on data collected from social media is not a particularly difficult task (if you have access to a high-quality system for collecting data from social media - Translator's note), so if you want to add importance to your report by adding analytic information , you should think about the causes of the incident.

Data Analytics on YouTube

Every minute more than 300 hours of videos are uploaded to YouTube. With more than a billion users and a wide range of advertising opportunities, this channel is an ideal intermediary on the way to the consumer.
