Concepts, concepts, ideas and products (part 1)

Introduction

When I was working at some jobs, but due to an engineering and creative mindset, I still opened my company a couple of years ago to more freely engage in the nonsense of interest to me. The company is of course in quotes the company is an orchestra of one person. It is created much more than the actual sale.
And in the breaks between attempts to earn a lot of money for food and family, I create different ideas and concepts. Some are just on occasion, some based on some kind of order or as a base for a new product.
Next will be some of these ideas - maybe someone will come in handy.


USB-to-network adapter


Category: ancient idea, device



I forgot the whole point, apparently the USD data should have been transmitted through the internal network of the house or apartment.
Those. on the one hand, a computer-USB-adapter-network, and the other: a network-adapter-USB-device.
In general, it is useful (connect a printer, webcam, speaker) and probably there is something on the market.

Automatic Presenter


Category: ancient idea, device



A small device: a rotatable platform, 4 diode lights, a camera with backlight and zoom, pico-projector (+ video output).
We place an object on the platform (for example, a telephone), it rotates according to a given pattern and check-points with the necessary information are put on the screen.
Now almost the same device is already there to shoot 3D models.

Motion tracker


Category: ancient idea, device



This was before the days of Kinekt and Leap Motion. It was proposed to use a marker and for some reason, IR illumination.
The chip is a two-color marker for a finger-type gesture.

Compact Automatic Parking


Category: ancient idea, automation



In an Asian country where I managed to live, there is a problem with parking, but at the same time there are quite a lot of empty small sections of shop-house houses.
And such a solution - drive up, and your machine takes the machine. In general, from the new - only compactness and modularity.

Car Camera Assistant


Category: ancient idea, cars



A small camera on a folding (so as not to fall off, if that) and telescopic (to raise higher) mount.
Turns in any direction, well, in the cockpit, of course, the screen and control. Thought scenarios buttons: turn, traffic jam, parking.
I would have bought it myself now, but nobody offers something.
