All customers have gone letter starting with the words "Hello, Evgraf Shmatkunos!". In those days it did not seem like a big tragedy. Chukhnuly, neighing and gathered to forget. Fire in the office - 2003

It is now we occupy three floors, one of which was built by ourselves. And at first they were sitting only in the second, in two cabinets.

And the neighbors on the ground floor caught fire. But it managed - they quickly extinguished and there was no real threat (I checked it myself). However, it was normal - it was impossible to work, but it was necessary. I made a willful decision and dismissed everyone home. Work. He closed all the doors and left. I come back for tomorrow, and the front door is almost at the jamb. She staggered and collapsed under her feet - literally waiting for me, holding out the last effort.

It turned out to be her firemen. We decided to check whether everything is in order on the second floor, collided with a closed door and, without thinking twice, opened it with an ax. Thank God, during the absence of us and the door is not lost.

Marketing Director from 2012 to 2017, Tatyana Grebenyukova


How I mined Sheremetyevo - 2016

At one of the RIWs, we took with us a model of Atlanta - our failover cluster. They printed it on a 3D printer, glued it together, soldered multi-colored light bulbs and powered them with batteries. Atlanta blinked, beamed and drew general attention to our booth. People approached, asked - in general, everything, as we wanted.

After the exhibition, I lagged behind my colleagues - I was late for checking in baggage. I changed the ticket and waited for my flight in the cafe. Layout Atlanta with all the wires and light bulbs lying next to the box from under the PS3. There he stayed.

I realized it just before going on board. I called the cafe - I ask about my box and I understand that I am describing something that looks like a bomb. So it is, our Atlanta was at first frightened by visitors, then the cafe staff, finally the police and the sappers. They were frightened by passengers of terminal D. And the whole story frightened me a little.

The cafe staff carefully advised not to return for the box. I wanted to flee the country, but decided that it was quite enough to go to my native Siberia.

Head of Sales, Natalia Duzenko
