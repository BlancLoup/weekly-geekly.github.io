Google showed the dynamics of the popularity of music trends over the past 64 years



Google has many interesting divisions and projects that lead these divisions. For example, Google Research recently presented the results of a study of the dynamics of the popularity of various areas of music over the past 64 years. The project itself was called Music Timeline .

The company processed information not from the top charts of the Billboard service, but the data obtained from its own Google Play Music service (information on artists and albums from this service). When you click on the main directions, musical currents appear inside such a direction, and at the bottom of the graph, the most popular music groups and performers that created the music of the current displayed on the graph are displayed.
