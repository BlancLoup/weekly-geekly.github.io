Yandex joined the W3C

We will participate in the consortium in discussions and development of those of them that seem to us the most important and influencing what will be not only the Runet, but also the Internet as a whole. The Yandex representative at the W3C will be Charles McCathie Nevile .



Yandex has previously participated in the work of some W3C working groups. But it was difficult to call it full-fledged cooperation. Now we will act together with other big market players and will be able to directly influence the development of standards that affect the lives of our direct users.
