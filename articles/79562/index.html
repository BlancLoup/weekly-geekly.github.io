Razer arctosa



My acquaintance with the Razer Arctosa began with an online store. Just one evening I was bored with my keyboard, and I decided to change it. The services were as follows: the backlight is not needed, because I type blindly, necessarily black, a full set of keys, a low enter, to which I was used, separated by a small gap of caps lok, a pleasant appearance, a rather modest keystroke (because I type quickly enough). Well, I did not want the keyboard to rumble when working. The contestants fell off one by one, until there was only one - Razer Arctosa. Just a couple of clicks - the order is issued, tomorrow it will be possible to take.

I got the product in perfect condition, no bruising or damage to the package. The box is strong enough, framed in Razer brand colors:
