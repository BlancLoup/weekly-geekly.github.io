Electrochromic film




The electrochromic film consists of two layers of a film with an internal conductive layer and a liquid crystal base. In the off state, the crystals are arranged randomly, forming an opaque white and matte surface, scattering the transmitted light. When voltage is applied, the crystals instantly stabilize, the film becomes transparent and transmits light.

Technical characteristics of the film: power supply 90-110V, current 0.1A / m2, power - 7 W / m2, switching speed - less than 1 sec., Transparency 7-75%, switching resource more than 100 thousand times, operating temperature range from - 40 ¬∞ C to + 75 ¬∞ C.
