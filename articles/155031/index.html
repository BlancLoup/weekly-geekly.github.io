Robofollower - Instagram promotion automation

Greetings I want to bring to your attention my small startup - Robofollower

Robofollower is a project that allows you to promote your Instagram account without investing any funds. We provide two main functions: Exchange of followers, exchange of likes. Details under the cut.


Scheme of work


Each user is given the opportunity to like (or follow) through the panel on the site. Instead, the user gets one point for like, or two points for following. Points are removed when you like or follow.

Robofollower is my debut project written in ASP.NET MVC 4, spinning on Azure WebSites.
