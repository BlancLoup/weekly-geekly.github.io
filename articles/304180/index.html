PyCon Russia 2016: latest news and final program

Hello!

Remained a week before the fourth Russian PyConRu . The conference will be held on July 3-4, 95 km from Moscow (there will be a transfer to the venue and back).

Ben Nuttall from Raspberry Pi will perform at PyConRu, we made a telegram bot, the final grid with the schedule is ready. Under the cut - detail about all the latest news. This post will be useful for those who still think whether to go to the conference or not.


More pythons on laptops! One of the PyConRu Stickers


Ben Nuttall from Raspberry Pi to perform at PyConRu

The program has another speaker, a foreigner. Ben Nuttall, manager of the Raspberry Pi Community at the Raspberry Pi Foundation in Cambridge (United Kingdom), author of the GPIO Zero python library, will come to the conference. Ben will give a talk on "Physical computing with Python and Raspberry Pi".

Connect to our telegram-bot @PyconRu_bot . Bot shows the schedule and abstracts. With it, you can evaluate the speaker and express your opinion in the general chat.

And the bot will help us, the organizers, to always be in touch and help you with any questions.

Once again about the place

The conference will be held in the country hotel "Yahonty Tarusa" . The hotel has a swimming pool, fitness center, bowling alley, billiards, beach, rental bicycles and rollers. For children there is a playground, an amusement room and a trampoline.

Prices are affordable. Roller and bicycle rental - 200 p / h, bowling - 300 p / lane, fitness center - 300 p. From 15.00 to 21.00 swimming pool for conference participants will be free.

A ticket for those traveling with you costs 5,000 rubles. It includes everything that a member's ticket, except for visiting reports.

In the evening of July 3rd, we are waiting for all the participants at the afterparty with a bonfire, songs and nastolki. Bring your guitars!


During breaks you can swim in the fresh air.

Student discount

For students, we have a special fixed price - 8000 rubles. To buy a ticket at a special price, send a student scan to om@it-people.ru, in response, we will send a promotional code.
