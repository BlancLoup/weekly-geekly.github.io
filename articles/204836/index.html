Valve joined the Linux Foundation

Valve, developer of the gaming platform Steam, developer and publisher of computer games, joined the Linux Foundation , which promotes the free GNU / Linux system and open source software.

Such a move should not surprise anyone, because recently Valve is actively using Linux. For example, the Linux kernel runs the Steam OS operating system, which is planned to be used in the Steam Machine gaming consoles (expected to be released in January 2014 ). Co-founder and CEO Gabe Newell said that the future of the gaming industry is for Linux . The official Linux client for the Steam platform was released in February of this year.

Gabe Newell last year called Windows 8 "a disaster for the personal computer industry . " And although most Steam users work under Windows, Valve hopes that over time, more and more of them will switch to a free OS.
