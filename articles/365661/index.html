Mirai is the future. The world's first hydrogen-powered production car. If you believe Toyota. Miniphoto report



Here it is, our beauty. Or not? Well, as they say, the taste and the color ... But what a modern one!

What will make us happy with this technological novelty? Comfort inside the cabin, quiet ride, futuristic design of dashboards.



Thanks to the onboard computer, you can not worry about the fact that the fuel will end unexpectedly.



The undoubted advantage over its environmental counterparts is a long distance run on one gas station. About three hundred kilometers.



Refueling is quick and easy. Just two minutes and you can go further. The truth behind the seeming simplicity hides huge technological problems. Liquid hydrogen, which is the fuel for this machine, is difficult to produce, but even more difficult to deliver to the refueling site and store.



Auto, first of all, should please the Arab sheikhs. First, in the minimum configuration, it costs more than $ 60,000, and secondly, water pours from the exhaust pipe - you will not die of thirst in the desert!



Well, let's drink from the exhaust pipe for the latest high-tech innovations to help improve the environment, and not to turn the world into a "radioactive ash." For the future, for Mirai!
