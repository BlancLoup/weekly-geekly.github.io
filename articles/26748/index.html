Microblogging in a new way


In connection with the frequent fall of Twitter, many Twitter users began to look at other services. Not so long ago, another microblogging platform appeared - Plurk .
Today, Twitter has a huge number of competitors, such as Jaiku or Pownce . And to stand out from the faceless crowd, you need to offer something new. Such an enticing feature in Plyuk was the presentation of posts in the form of a timeline.

Another interesting trick is the original addendum to the post sent like a phrase ‚ÄúKenobi thinks ...‚Äù or ‚ÄúKenobi loves ...‚Äù. This gives an emotional coloring to the post. In addition, on the tape you can select all the posts, the authors of which love or hate something.

Many newbies on Twitter scared off how to display answers to messages from other users. It was difficult to understand all this jumble with @ characters. Plurk also provides this in a more decent form - in the form of regular comments under the text of the message.

The service also has excellent support for YouTube, Flickr and other multimedia exchangers, providing a link to them in the form of a small preview.

The platform is built using Ajax so that it is easy and fast to work with it. Supported work with popular IM, such as GTalk or Jabber. For some, it will be interesting to use the karma that we need in order to open an additional package of smiles.
