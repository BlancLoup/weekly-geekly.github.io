Answers to Habra from Skolkovo

Last Wednesday, an agreement was signed in Rosstandart on cooperation and collaboration between the Federal Agency for Technical Regulation and Metrology (Rosstandart) and the Development Fund of the Center for the Development and Commercialization of Skolkovo Technologies.



The agreement provides for cooperation in ensuring the uniformity of measurements, standardization and conformity assessment within the framework of the Skolkovo Foundation activities, active involvement of Rosstandart specialists in consulting work on all existing clusters, creation of expert working groups and organization of joint scientific and practical conferences parties.

Commenting on the signed agreement, the President of the Skolkovo Foundation, Viktor Vekselberg, noted that this agreement will be one of the starting points for the further development of high-tech and high-tech industries in Russia and bringing them to an international level. Standardization of innovative products will make it possible over time to make such industries export-oriented and competitive.

At the end of the post are the answers to the questions posed by the Habrawers.



The head of Rosstandart, Grigory Elkin, in turn told reporters: ‚ÄúThe signed agreement will allow for adjusting the work to technical committees that will be able to develop standards that promote the commercialization of Skolkovo products for the domestic and international markets. In addition, cooperation with Skolkovo will make it possible to move from national standardization focused on the domestic market to advanced international standardization. ‚Äù

Working with specialists of Rosstandart within the framework of the agreement will allow speeding up the procedures for bringing the results of the research activities of the Skolkovo project participants to the market. In addition, we can now safely speak about the possibility of standardizing Skolkovo products under a simplified scheme, including using the mechanism of prestandards. This means that participants in the Skolkovo project will be able to initiate improvements in the Russian standards base and form requirements for high-tech products, which, in turn, will make individual projects more attractive from the point of view of future profitability.



At the press conference, journalists raised several interesting topics:

What does cooperation with Rosstandart mean to you?

First of all, this will speed up the procedures for bringing the results of the research activities of our participants to the market. We are talking about the standardization of Skolkovo products under a simplified scheme, including using the mechanism of prestandards.

This means that Skolkovo project participants will be able to initiate the improvement of the standardization base of the Russian Federation and independently formulate requirements for high-tech products, which, in turn, will increase the commercialization component of individual projects.

Since we are talking about positioning in global markets, we focus on the experience of Rosstandart and the Ministry of Industry and Trade of Russia in harmonizing existing requirements with international standards, including within the framework of the Customs Union and the Russia-EU dialogue.

Perhaps, thanks to such cooperation, in a few years a new quality guarantee will emerge in Russia - the Skolkovo international standard. "

Do you plan to sign such an agreement, for example, with the International Organization for Standardization (ISO)?

‚ÄúToday, there is already a practice of participation in such international organizations of representatives from specific states. And in this case, these representatives are just employees of Rosstandart, whose task is to take part in the development of international standards together with representatives of other states. And the role of Skolkovo in this case is to help Rosstandart expand the range of issues that should be brought to the international level in order to prepare the ground for those companies that have participant status to enter international markets.

What standards will be applied on the territory of Skolkovo?

- In accordance with the Skolkovo law, we have the right to apply both Russian standards and standards that operate within other countries, for example, standards of EU countries. The decision on the use of a standard is made by the management company during the project. Moreover, if the decision is in the direction of European standards, we are obliged to inform the agency that we are applying the standard not from the Russian Federation. Moreover, such a decision must be well substantiated.
