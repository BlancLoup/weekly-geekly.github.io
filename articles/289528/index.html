Samsung began to study Bitcoin

The research center of the Korean giant Samsung began to study Bitcoin cryptocurrency technology, reports the Kommersant publication.

Even despite the unclear perspective of digital currencies in the long term, Samsung, following organizations such as IBM, the US Federal Reserve and the Bank of England, supports the development of cryptocurrency.

Samsung Research America, located in Silicon Valley, in particular, stated that it is separately studying the technology of the block chain , which may be interesting not only from the point of view of electronic currency. First of all, this technology can be applied in the field of data storage, Samsung hopes to simplify the processing and storage of data from all types of online transactions with its help.
