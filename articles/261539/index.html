Alt key for mouse gestures, as well as various important fixes in Vivaldi 1.0.212.3

Hello!

Today we present the next weekly assembly. This time we focused on correcting errors and polishing existing functions.

Since last weekly assembly, we have been doing mouse gestures, correcting shortcomings and improving the work of this function. Now you can use to activate this function not only the right mouse button, but also the Alt key.

We also fixed a bug with a blank page of extensions, and also fixed a few errors in working with tabs. Plus, they corrected the work of the search field and corrected several ugly places in the interface, including a bug with dark fonts on the bookmarks page with the selected dark window design in Linux.

For lovers of extensions, too, there is good news - now installing Adblock Plus does not lead to a crash of the browser. But I remind you that officially extensions are not yet supported in Vivaldi.

That's all for now. Download the new assembly can be on the links below:

  • Mac

  • Linux RPM 64-bit

  • Linux DEB 64-bit
