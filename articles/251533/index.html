Growth of the Russian smartphone market


In 2014, according to IDC Worldwide Mobile Phone Tracker, the Russian smartphone market experienced a rapid growth: sales grew by 46% compared with 2013.

The balance of power in the market at the end of 2014 is as follows:

By sales:

1.Samsung (6.05 million)
2.Apple (3.25 million)
3.Lenovo (2.22 million)
4.Nokia (1.79 million)
5.Sony (1.59 million)
6.LG (1.06 million)
7.HTC (852,000)

By profit:

1.Apple ($ 2.14 billion)
2.Samsung ($ 1.45 billion)

The Sun will create a mobile platform with its games and payments.


Sun Play - this will be the name of the new mobile project from Nektan for the tabloid newspaper The Sun. From the press release of the project, it became known that a number of games will be released in which it will be possible to play for real pounds - how this will be implemented is not yet revealed.

On the one hand, it may seem rather strange and desperate for the newspaper, especially at the height of the media crisis, when everyone is trying to somehow stand out in the market or, on the contrary, save and reduce 90% of all employees. But it is worth paying attention to the fact that 7 million people read the The Sun newspaper every day, and the target audience has already been studied and there are ideas about its interests - if not to make mistakes with the games, then the publication is clearly not deprived of traffic.


Localytics on application activity peaks


Localytics conducted a study that reveals activity peaks throughout the day in various types of applications. This information can help you decide on the timing of your next promotion or announcement, holding a contest or update. As a result, all the information can be divided into 4 points with their own charts:

Application start at 7am and peak at 8pm


news, travel and weather are used more in the morning from 7 to 8 o'clock, and the second peak is at 5 pm for travel and closer to 10 for news and weather;


business, finance and music are used equally throughout the day and evening;


entertainment and social applications are more used late in the evening and at night, although users of media applications are active even at night.



New game from Zeptolab




Just recently, Zeptolab released their new game King of Thieves , and discussions have already erupted around those who liked it, and those who did not like it. I rather belong to the second, so be careful, do not take my words for granted :)

Probably, this is a sign of a good game, that users did not remain indifferent and actively discuss. In my opinion, the game turned out to be a niche, for those who love platformers who do not forgive mistakes. You need to be very patient, dexterous and attentive. I do not think that the modern casual player has these qualities. Not without an evil random, which can suddenly interrupt a gaming session, forcing either to watch ads or pay. But, in general, the game deserves at least that it was set, watched and formed an opinion.

Statistics of the most popular categories in the App Store. Games they rule!
