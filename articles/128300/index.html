Video Card Repair

After reading a recent note about the repair of processors , I decided to share the method with which I repaired my video card.

Somewhere about a month ago in the course of work, I received artifacts on the screen. Since the video card is hot GeForce 9800GTX + AMP Edition, and it's summer outside, I was not surprised. I thought that just a video card in a closed case warmed to the state of artifacts. I note that because of its overclocking past, which is mainly focused on video cards overclocking, artifacts do not lead to panic. Turned off the computer, let the gland cool down a little, opened the case cover, the cooler turned it on at full speed, rebooted the computer. But it was not there, the artifacts met me right from the boot screen. Then I appreciated the scale of the tragedy. Such problems are usually not treated and it is easier to buy a new video card.

I had to switch to an integrated video card and started to prepare for the purchase of a new video card. For the right choice looked at the forum overclocker.ru. Once there, I decided to ask the community if there are ways to cure such problems and I was not mistaken - there were ways .

The fact is that in the process of work they can warm up to a temperature at which the solder softens, the contacts gradually recede and as a result at some point the contact disappears completely - artifacts appear. Contacts disappear either on the GPU or on the memory chips. I apologize for such an explanation on the fingers.

What is the repair with such problems? The point is the same heat and return the contact in place. So how to find out what kind of contact went problematic, we decided to heat all the contacts. In order to save everything that may suffer from heating, we wrap the entire board with foil, except for memory and GPU chips, and leave ‚Äúunscrewed‚Äù and place under the chips on the back of the board.   • I expose 100 degrees, I wait for heating and 4 minutes I hold at this temperature
  • I set 150 degrees, wait for heating and keep it at this temperature for 4 minutes
  • I set 200 degrees, wait for heating and keep it at this temperature for 2 minutes
