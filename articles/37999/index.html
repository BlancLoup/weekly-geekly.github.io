The importance of factors affecting the ranking of a site in Yandex

I organized a survey among experienced SEOers on a subject. I hope - appreciate.

Factors with importance on a 10-point scale in pictures (the more - the more important):



The rest of the graphics hid under cat.











I note only that there can be no question of any accuracy. The survey participants for the most part responded without much thought, and in some cases they could imagine another side of the same factor.

An example is the page nesting level. The HC itself (the one on the exchanges) does not affect the ranking. But if the page is in 4 clicks from the main one, then in the end it will get very little weight, if it is indexed at all. Another example is the importance of availability in the Yandex Directory. In my opinion, the importance of this factor is noticeably different for newly created sites and sites that are already the n-th number of years old.

And as Denis Ivanov correctly noted in a personal conversation - the percentages of nausea are a very strong simplification of reality. In general, these results do not need to be taken as something objective or as a guide to perfect optimization. Their task is simple - to visually show a very approximate temperature at the hospital - nothing more.
