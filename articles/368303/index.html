The secret of successful New Year's decisions - in passing through the door



The beginning of the year is the right time to formulate plans and goals for the future. And this does not interfere with advice from recognized experts. We have previously published translations of Oliver Amberton's articles , which can be used to increase personal effectiveness . And now - to your attention one more material, written by the author especially for those who in 2015 decided to radically change their lives! The secret of making decisions that are actually made is also the secret of making a hit movie. So, let Oliver teach you how to create a hit movie (well, or turn your life into an excitingly successful blockbuster).

First, our hero lives his usual life. We would not look at this for too long, but, fortunately, in all the good stories the hero has to go through the door.

Passing through this door guarantees the final, whatever it may be. Our hero must defeat his sworn enemy, or to catch up with his love at the airport, or to defend his desire to dance in spite of the disapproval of his parents.



This is the formula for almost every story ever told, because it works. Once you enter the door, you cannot go back.
