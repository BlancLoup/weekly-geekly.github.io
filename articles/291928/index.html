Slack audience reaches 1.1 million and continues to grow rapidly

Yesterday, June 24, it became known that the daily audience of Slack corporate messenger reached 1.1 million. In April, this figure did not exceed 750 thousand. In the same month, company representatives reported that Slack raised a $ 160 million investment in a round involving the DST Global fund Yuri Milner.

For three months, the number of paid accounts increased by 100 thousand and reached 300 thousand, reports The Verge. Annual revenue (ARR) Slack this year exceeded $ 25 million.

Slack - a tool for organizing corporate chat. Last October, Slack raised $ 120 million with a valuation of $ 1.12 billion. The number of users then was about 250 thousand. In 2015, it doubled and exceeded 500 thousand.

Slack was launched in February 2014 - almost a year and a half ago. In total, Slack raised $ 340 million. The company's valuation for April 2015 was $ 2.8 billion.

The ratio of company valuation to the number of users of Slack is greater than that of large instant messengers and social networks.


Source: Quartz

Today, the service is integrated with more than 900 thousand applications. Slack supports such well-known services as GitHub , Google Drive , Trello , Twitter , Asana . For example, it allows you to set up alerts about how many people mentioned your brand on Twitter.
