Ya.Subbotnik: BEM in the development of interfaces

On September 8, 2012, in Moscow, the first Y.Subbotnik will be held this fall.

It will be devoted to the BEM ecosystem, which appeared and develops in Yandex.

Employees of the Moscow and Simferopol offices of Yandex will talk in detail about the existing components and share their vision of the future of BEM.

Come, listen, ask questions and chat on professional topics over a cup of coffee.

On our Ya.Subbotnik you learn about:
  • the history of the BEM;
  • template engines in general and BEMHTML in particular;
  • introducing BEM into existing systems;
  • mixes;
  • I-bem.js and why it is needed;
  • unit testing;
  • automatic build and bem-tools.

In addition, interactive stands will be waiting for you, which we will discuss in addition.

Program and theses of J. Subbotnik

Participation, as always, is free, but you need to register .

Registration for the event ends on September 7 at 18:00 Moscow time.

Limited number of seats.

For those who do not fall into the number of participants or will not be able to personally attend Y.Subbotnik, an online broadcast will be organized.
