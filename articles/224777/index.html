Sublimation of iodine or sublimation




Sublimation (or sublimation) is the transition of a substance from a solid to a gaseous state, bypassing the liquid phase. Iodine cannot be in a liquid state at normal atmospheric pressure. That is why when heated it turns from solid peas into gas. But after again settles on the cold flask as a solid. The reverse process is called desublimation.
