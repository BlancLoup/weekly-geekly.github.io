Data Fest is the largest free conference for researchers, engineers, and developers involved in machine learning, data analysis and processing, and what the press likes to call AI.

About Data Fest Moscow 2018

Data Fest 2018 - more than 30 different sections in 7 rooms, where you will plunge into the world of Data Science and find out how the community of specialists in this rapidly developing area lives.

Special master classes by leading Russian research groups:
