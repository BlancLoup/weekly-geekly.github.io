New google.com interface

As I recently learned, google.com is preparing to redesign its custom search.


And now we can look at it with our own eyes.


To do this, follow the manual:

1) Since this feature is not available in Russian Google, we go to the American one: http://google.com/ncr

2) When the search engine loads, we enter the following code into the address bar:
