October 25, 2018 Atlassian User Group meeting in Raiffeisenbank

Hello!

The Atlassian User Group (AUG) is a community of Atlassian users. The community holds regular meetings where we share our experience on using Atlassian products. On October 25, AUG will meet in the office of Raiffeisenbank.

Meeting schedule


18:30 We start registration and get acquainted


19:00 Migration of configurations or automation of the delivery of changes to the product.


Danila Koshel, IPONWEB, Atlassian Product Manager

I'll tell you about the experience of using Adaptavist products to automate the process of configuration releases

19:30 Autumn migration of cranes and projects


Stanislav Ryzhov, MailRu Group, developer

We will touch the basics of ornithology and find out why I have not had a weekend for 2 weeks and I sleep for 6 hours.

20:00 Not profile use of Jira Service Desk


Ivan Tovbin, Raiffeisenbank, developer, atlassian community champion

I'll tell you how you transferred work with applications from mail to JIRA, which rakes were collected and how you managed to solve them.

Site Address

Prospect Andropova, 18, Bldg. 2. A few minutes walk from the subway Technopark

Entry will be organized by lists. Therefore, in order to avoid problems with the passage to the event, we ask you to register under real name and indicate your company. Please register before October 23. After that, the lists will already be transferred to issue passes.
