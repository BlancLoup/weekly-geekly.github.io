Flexible, glowing, waterproof Brando keyboard

In the online store Brando , on the "shelves" of which you can often find interesting high-tech devices, a product called the Flexible Illuminated Full Sized Keyboard appeared . This is a flexible, full-format backlit keyboard. Plus, it is also waterproof.

In general, keyboards are flexible and backlit keyboards are no longer a novelty. But so that and that, and another at once - this is already quite interesting. Made of a Flexible Illuminated Full Sized Keyboard made of silicone, it can be folded or collapsed into a tube in any way and without any problems spill coffee on it. And due to the low weight (335 grams), the keyboard is also quite convenient for transportation. And it's easy to wash it: put it under the tap, rubbed it with a sponge and that's it. The backlight (available in four colors - blue, white, pink and green) can be turned off as unnecessary.
