A1 Pay ceases to provide services to individuals

Before each developer (and even a regular webmaster) at least once in his life, the question arises: ‚ÄúHow to monetize your project?‚Äù And each of us plunges headlong into the search engines in search of a quality operator that provides such services.

As an SMS billing, many opt for A1 Pay. The choice was obvious. 12 years experience of stable work, rich API, easy connection, a very wide choice of short numbers, work with bank cards and the most popular payment systems ...

Today, A1Pay conducted a sad email newsletter among its subscribers.

From September 1, 2013, the A1Pay payment system stops providing individuals with services for processing SMS payments and subscriptions. Partners interested in further cooperation on these sales channels can contact partners@alt1.ru.

From September 16, 2013, a1pay payment system stops providing individuals with payment acceptance services via mobile commerce, plastic cards and e-wallets. You can continue to cooperate with us if you enter into a contract as a legal entity. To do this, send a request to e-mail sales@mainpay.ru indicating your username in the system.

The corresponding news is published on the A1Pay site in the news archive a1pay.ru/news/index

I recall that today A1Pay allows you to receive money via SMS (short numbers, MT-billing), bank cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.), mobile commerce programs, terminals, Personal Account Qiwi, Yandex.Money payment systems , WebMoney, RBK Money, W1 and other electronic currency.
