HP Pavilion g6 laptop video review



HP Pavilion g6 is a line of inexpensive multimedia laptops. The series has many different models, of which we have chosen two for the review. Both modifications are identical in characteristics with the exception of the processor. One model works on Intel Core i3, and the other on AMD A6. In this review, we made a comparison of the performance of these laptops and made a kind of competition: Intel Core i3 vs. AMD A6.

In both models installed: 4 GB of RAM, 500 GB hard drives with a rotational speed of 5400 rev / min and a discrete graphics card AMD Radeon HD7670M. The screen diagonal is 15.6 inches, and the resolution is 1366x768 (gloss, TN).
