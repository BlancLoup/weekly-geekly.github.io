The second project of Glavstarta - "Sea Salons"

Glavstart presents its second project from St. Petersburg - Sea Salon .
The Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãSalons is an online recording system that starts working on the beauty salon market, but the platform is designed so that it can be expanded to other market segments: restaurants, car washes, medical clinics, fitness clubs, notaries and many, many others. .

In addition to the online recording, the service itself offers two more possibilities:
  1. A group recording system linked to predetermined recording times. By registering for a specific unpopular time, users get discounts, growing with the number of those who signed up at the same time. This scheme is similar to the model of coupon services, but at the expense of timing it ensures uniform loading of the passenger compartment.
  2. The system of discounts for suddenly released time, which allows you to enroll in a popular master in a popular salon. The same principle lies in the model of the lastminute.com successful service.


Distinctive features of the Sea Salons service


For users:
  • clients no longer need to personally communicate with administrations; You can enroll in the salon on the service site itself, at any time of the day, it is just as easy to cancel this entry
  • automation and convenience of recording for customers: integration with electronic calendars; SMS reminders about the recording; no need to store discount cards; search for salons on a convenient filter system; search specialists for ratings and reviews
For salons:
  • using coupon codes to increase the flow of visitors in unpopular times: there are unpopular hours and days in beauty salons; in order to increase the load on the salon‚Äôs resources at this particular time, the administration can place discount coupons tied to unpopular hours and days in the service,
  • the use of last minute deals to attract customers in case of urgent cancellations: in addition to unpopular times, there are still problems for visitors to cancel their entries; in these cases, the salons can post hot offers to fill the vacated time slots



While the Sea Salons project is actively developing in St. Petersburg, Moscow is in the plans.
