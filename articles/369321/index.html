What is Moodle


Moodle's capabilities are difficult to block even for very serious corporate solutions. The platform is a course management system designed for cloud-based learning. This virtual learning environment can be customized from scratch directly to customer requirements. It has a wide toolset of plug-ins, which allows the user to customize their own e-learning space for the specifics of future courses .

Despite the fact that the system has a 14-year history, it has not taken root in the corporate segment. Alternative solutions


Teachers keep up with progress, even if they do not use Moodle. Skype classes are becoming increasingly common, as well as courses from teachers on Youtube, which often gather a wide audience due to the dynamism and quality of presentation.

And here comes Teachbase. Immediately make a reservation that we will not compare ourselves with Moodle - this is not quite correct. Moodle has more features and more flexible customization options - in case you are ready to spend time and budget on the web developer who will build the system specifically for you. In turn, Teachbase is a light cloud commercial solution with a simple interface and one of the most affordable price tags.



Teachbase allows you to organize online courses from and to. All you need to start is to register in the system. The functionality of the system makes it possible, firstly, to properly organize training and testing material, testing, and secondly, to organize the workplace of students with the ability to monitor the performance of each individual student and the group as a whole.

The platform allows you to conduct live courses through webinars, add pre-prepared courses to them and, thus, combine synchronous and asynchronous training. Everything is in an easy and convenient interface where everything is ready for the work of a teacher, which significantly reduces the implementation time - from the idea to the finished course.

The simplicity of the courses was already appreciated by teachers from various schools and training centers, who, within the online conference of Intel and Educational Galaxy, created their first courses from scratch to ready state in just 10 days. Many courses now live their lives and up to 200 students attend them.
