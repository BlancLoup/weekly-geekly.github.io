Comfortable laptop table

I was here, I was already writing about the Laptop Cool Table laptop furniture . About easy and kind of like a comfortable stand under the laptop. In the comments on the topic, there were a few interesting links to similar products. The topic turns out to be relevant. So here's another interesting furniture gadget. From Logitech, which decided to pamper users with their vision of such a stand.


They called it Logitech Comfort Lapdesk . It is intended for those who work with a laptop at home. According to the manufacturer, the stand provides a stable placement of the computer and helps to eliminate the problem of heat dissipation, if the user works while sitting on the couch, lying on the bed or on the floor.

Unlike the aforementioned Laptop Cool Table, there are no coolers in the Comfort Lapdesk. And there is a heat shield and air duct between the base of the stand and its upper plane. In addition, the laptop adjoins the stand not with its entire bottom plane, but only in four places. What, in theory, should contribute to "cooling", i.e. more or less efficiently dissipate heat and improve airflow.
