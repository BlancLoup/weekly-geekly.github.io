I use IE

Active fighters are already a bit tired.
I use IE.
Violet to me what others think about IE.
This is my will and desire. I tried both opera and firefox.


I use the software that I like. If someone does not like this software, then this someone can ignore this software, but why fight this software too?

There is no perfect software. And it is unlikely that such will ever appear.
And I have the right in this world of non-ideal software to choose the non-ideal software that suits me. What I am doing.

When I say that I use IE. I do not mean that everyone should use IE. I do not mean that other browsers are worse than IE. I do not campaign for IE, I just use it. Because it is just a program. She helps me get the information I need. And it suits me.

When I call myself an IE user, I don't mean that users of other browsers are more stupid than me. I believe that users of other browsers are smart enough to decide for themselves which browser suits them. And reasonable enough to understand that everyone chooses their own browser.

IE is a browser. He allows me to view hypertext.
It is not an object of worship and hatred. IE is just a browser.
Any site is part of hypertext. Therefore, it is available to any browser by definition.

Any site is a source of information. If the site is not reflected in any browser, then the creators of the site did not consider their information important enough to convey it to all users of all browsers.

Discrimination of users on a browser-based basis is disrespect for the user's right (namely, any site is done for him) to choose their own browser.

If the user does not know about the existence of other browsers, then it is rather a lack of work of the browser creators in the promotion of their offspring. And not a consequence of the stupidity and limitations of the user. MS successfully popularized IE. Simply implementing it as a free part of your operating system. And so IE is used by most. Many people associate Internet only with IE. This is a very good and strong marketing move. If you are unable to outrun IE in popularity, then it‚Äôs more your problem than IE.

Any software product with careful analysis will certainly allow to detect vulnerabilities. Therefore, any browser can be the target of intruders.

I use IE.
