Novice Project Manager - Design

Little preface. We have a need to make a website. And not just a business card, but an online store with a non-standard ordering system. More precisely - to alter the existing one, but since the existing one in terms of safety and ease of administration (as well as design and quality of the version) was not suitable, it was necessary to do it anew.
Having talked with a number of programmers (I myself am such), we realized that CMS (even modified) does not suit us, self-written code disappears for a number of reasons (there is no guarantee of security first of all), we need a website on the framework.
Why it was not to turn to the site for a ready-made team, where there is a manager and trusted employees?

Firstly , there was already experience in freelance exchanges, experience of 2 years, an employer account with 20+ reviews ... everything seemed real, given that I myself can impose (really something quite simple), set to hosting - in short, I have basic knowledge and experience in dealing with specialists.

Everything turned out to be not so obvious at the check, but there appeared basic principles that I would like to state in the article, as well as to get additions - perhaps the person who was or is the project manager also got them together - thus the collective mind forms an interesting topic and, perhaps discussions in which, as you know, truth is born.

Everything written is a personal opinion, it is definitely worth considering.
