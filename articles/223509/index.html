And the reaction, again, according to the developers, will be controlled. As far as possible - I propose to discuss in the comments to this material.

The project itself was named Focus Fusion. Its capabilities are based on what is considered a disadvantage of modern thermonuclear reactors ‚Äî plasma instability, the inability to keep an extremely hot plasma for any length of time to conduct a controlled thermonuclear reaction and receive energy.

The Focus Fusion project has been around for a long time, in particular, Lerner talked about it in 2007 on Google Tech Talk.
