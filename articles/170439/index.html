About procrastination popular, or Hollywood vs. laziness



Someone calls procrastination "the plague of the 21st century", but surely it is not. This psychological state is characteristic not only of us, the people of the "new time", many of our ancestors probably suffered from it. I propose to get acquainted with its types and ways of dealing with it with vivid and recognizable examples.



In short, procrastination is a phenomenon due to which we cannot achieve what we are striving for. Instead of doing important things, a person focuses on trifles, and, therefore, the result is obvious: guilt, low self-esteem, and other people's discontent. The consequences of the procrastination demonstrated by us can provoke further procrastination ... As you can see, it turns out a vicious circle, from which it is not so easy to get out.
What is the reason for such unproductive behavior? We can not appreciate the importance of the case? No, we are well aware of this. We do not have the strength and desire for the necessary action? On the contrary, everything is in abundance.
In my opinion, there are two main obstacles to the successful implementation of the plan.
