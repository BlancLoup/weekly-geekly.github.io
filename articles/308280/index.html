Regional stage TopCoder Open 2016 at ITMO

September 3, 2016, one of the largest competitions in sports programming TopCoder Open arrives in St. Petersburg! This year, in addition to the online competition and the finals in Washington, TopCoder holds a series of regional stages in China, USA, India, Indonesia and now in Russia. In the program of the event :


  • Onsight-round Algorithm Competition: Single Round Match competition (3 tasks for 75 minutes + 15 minutes of challenge), from which the top 10 participants will go to the online Wild Card Round, from which, in turn, two winners will go to the TCO finals in Washington DC ( rules );
  • (Discussed) Mini-marathon (competition for the best solution of one complex task), with prizes for the winners;
  • T-shirts for all participants.

If you want to participate - register , there are still places.
