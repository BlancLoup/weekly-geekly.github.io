Nabaztag - The First Smart Bunny



Nabaztag - in Armenian rabbit. It was created by the Parisian company Violet. Nabaztag is a fairly simple, but very nice gadget. It can connect via Wi-Fi to a computer, play podcasts and speak weather forecasts out loud. It can be programmed so that it will wake you up in the morning with a gentle female voice and encourage you while you are going to work.

The cost of the device is not very high and is about $ 180 without delivery.

According to Forbes magazine, the company has already sold 62,000 of these gadgets, with half of the buyers being women.
