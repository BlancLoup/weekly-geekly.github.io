Fragmentation?

When I read about the fragmentation of devices on Android, I mentally nod, agreeing with the author of the article. When I see the thesis that device fragmentation interferes with software development, I raise my eyebrows. But when they try to prove to me that the fragmentation of Android is the main problem of this platform and the main disadvantage for mobile software developers, I, frankly, start laughing. Sorry, but this is either purely marketing deliberately nonsense, or a complete lack of understanding of the situation.

Each of these price segments has its own competition. You can make cheap furniture, sell it in cheap stores, do not even know about the existence of the Caroline Islands, and at the same time do not overlap with manufacturers of elite furniture, who also will not know about the existence of you.
