A game (idea and alpha version) for those who played the war game as a child

How many of you in childhood played this outdoor game? The two teams disperse, and then begin to look for each other and ‚Äúshoot‚Äù with toy weapons or just sticks :) Super game, the forerunner of the paintball. One problem - the controversy who got into whom initially.

Then later shooters appeared on the computer and they also really liked a lot. How much time is spent for Wolf, and then for Doom, the Heretic, and so on. And now Battlefield with its super graphics.

But no matter how hard the manufacturers of graphic engines with their super realistic shadows and soft light, they can not keep up with the usual picture of the window.

Again, Nerf makes a lot of toy weapons. So it seems there is a demand.

So why not combine: a network shooter and a toy machine with a mobile game on the street?


Therefore, the idea is for an app:
  • Take the background c camera
  • We draw on top of sight
  • We lose the sounds of shots at the moment of the shot
  • We determine the coordinates with GPS and the direction where the camera looks from the compass
  • regularly helmet our coordinates to the server
  • at the moment of the shot we send the coordinates, direction, caliber, Id arrow to the server
  • The server receives shots and calculates whether there are hits or really slightly missed
  • The server informs shooters about the hit in the form of a decrease in health and playing a sound from a bullet hit (or only the sound of a rebound)
  • when a player is killed - you can show killcam - google street with a place from where they shot
  • Naturally, we keep statistics and enter achievements.
  • if the weather is very bad, then just play on the map where the players will be drawn. So you can shoot at least 10 000 km


Those. You can make a natural network shooter that will be super healthy for you - you have to run along the street, which is more useful than just sitting at the computer.

Ideally, you also need a holder for a mobile phone so that you can insert the phone and shoot with the trigger and also provide a shutter for recharging.
