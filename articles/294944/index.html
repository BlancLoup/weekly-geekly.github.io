Warner and Universal companies failed to get compensation for digital piracy from Vkontakte



The well-known companies Warner and Universal, which are among the most active fighters against digital piracy in the world, could not get monetary compensation from Vkontakte. As part of the lawsuit, both companies demanded cash compensation of 50 million rubles from the social network, Kommersant writes . At the same time, the court still obliged the social network to make it impossible for users to re-download deleted songs using software methods.
