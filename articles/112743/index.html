Restore order in the wires

Probably everyone is familiar with the situation when a shapeless web of wires is formed under a computer desk and it is possible to find the necessary wire in it with great difficulty (not to mention the nagging spouse who is trying to clean the floor under it). Another example: I have a special box in my closet for storing chargers from numerous gadgets in my family (mobile phones, players, navigators, etc.), and no matter how carefully I put them in the box, the wires are tangled. It seems to me that at night, when we sleep, they come to life in a drawer and crawl away, taking on the most bizarre forms.

Once I decided to end this mess and came up with a wonderful device for arranging wires of any length and size. At once I will say that I spied the idea from some kind of branded device, my only implementation.

The device is very budget, the average cost is around 3 p. on one wire.

All that we need for manufacturing is shown in the photo:


Fig. 1. Required components
  1. Velcro ordinary. Sold in any store with sewing accessories, I buy at a price of 30 p. per meter.
  2. Stapler. Or option for married - spouse with a needle and thread.

So, first of all, cut off a piece of velcro (shaggy half). Its length is selected depending on the thickness of the target coil of wire. On average, for simple charges, I cut off 10-12 cm. And also cut off a piece 2 cm long from the other half, the one with hooks (see Fig. 1).

Next, we attach another, small piece to one of the edges of a long piece of velcro using a stapler (or the hands of a loving wife and a needle and thread). They should be located to each other non-working surfaces.


Fig. 2. Fastening two pieces of velcro stapler


Fig. 3. Ready connection

That's all! The fixture is ready. Now, any dangling end of the wire is twisted into a skein and fixed with just made yoke. This solution is suitable for multiple chargers, as well as for computer wires and network equipment.


Fig. 4. Examples of use
