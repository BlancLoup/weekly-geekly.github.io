Linux Install Fest - Side View

A few days ago, in Nizhny Novgorod, a classic event of the ‚Äúlimit Internet‚Äù times was held - Linux Install Fest 05.19 .



This format has long been supported (~ 2005) by NNLUG (the regional Linux user group).

Today it is not customary to copy "from screw to screw" and distribute blanks with fresh distributions. The Internet is available to everyone and literally shines from every teapot.
At the same time, the educational component remains relevant. The festival has confirmed its importance this time.

The organizers invited the speakers to talk about any interesting topic from the field of Linux and free software. As a result, the final list covered serious "admin" tasks, graphics, game sector and audio music applications.

While the speakers registered topics on the NNLUG site , the organizers made announcements, including on Habr√© . Immediately, a task list in GD was compiled, open to all who would like to join the training.

What did the organizers plan?


8 reports, use-demo fresh Linux-distributions, gaming stands of different caliber and, as it turned out - a music session in the final.

All this is in the spacious assembly hall of the NRTK with decent acoustics and a tea table in the corner.

Below are pictures!

And Saturday came. In the air was a terrible holiday spirit (C)

Alexey was the first to find more empty tables and set up a gaming stand.


Neon glow is not weak iron and gamers are right there.

The gamer series was enhanced by RetroPie with joysticks (the setup was assembled and tested by Yegor).

The problem of starting the SEGA-emulator was not solved.

  • Ubuntu 04.04.2

Ubuntu MATE 04/18/14 on RPi 3 is located just to the side.

One of the participants rightly asked:
Why so many different Linux?
The question is correct, I did not find what to answer. I was running Debian Lenny on my home machine with KDE3 and it was enough for typical office and multimedia tasks.

It seems that in addition to disparate desktops - different distributions may still have their own, unique philosophy, approaches, configuration and security nuances. Many of the distributions recommended in the NNLUG community had to be set aside for further study.

A few photo-screenshots and superficial impressions of the distributions - under the spoiler:


Ubuntu 04.04.2. The unsuccessfully taken snapshot basically reflects my initial impressions of Gnome: a tablet. In principle, not bad if you have the habit of "scattering eyes" on a bunch of icons.



Lubuntu 19.04. Kouteshno and succinctly. Perhaps my number 1 choice of the candidates.



Solus 4.0 Budgie. Certainly beautiful: translucent windows, grouping by running applications, but a little something.



Astra Linux CE (2.12). 2nd place in the personal rating. Installed the longest since it is expected (due to the size and announcements on the site) has established a lot of things. During the installation, I asked for a complex password and a bit later I displayed a list of checkboxes defining a more formidable level of security. Given the approach to the creation - a candidate for more detailed study in the future.

Alt-linux recalled a distant KDE3. Very simple with specialized for learning software.

With a slight delay, the event officially began. Next will be a subjective point of view on the reports. Fully familiar with them can be in the record 6-hour stream .



Denis talks about an interesting Meshroom package. In short, on the basis of 50-100 photographs of an object from different angles, the program builds a 3D model with a formed texture. Were demonstrated nuances of the settings and the results obtained.



Vladimir added virtual gravity, mentioning Proxmox VE: mostly user cases and general impressions. The release of this debian-based tool is not frequent, but for its support and updates, more special conditions are required.



About a good gaming developing package for children GCompris told Innocent. The software is capable of small people from ~ 3 years old and contains different types and types of small games. The game form can additionally stimulate the development of children: expanding horizons, the development of logic, the approach to children with autism.

The new version of Blender 2.8, according to Denis (his second report), is more stable than some analogues. Adjustment of functionality. Changes in the interface.



Artem Kashkanov ( radiolok ) praises Nextcloud. He says that he has been using it for a long time and very well. A good option to have a local "analog DropBox."

Artyom Poptsov ( avvvp ) mentioned the great Audacity, making a philosophical basis by demonstrating the correct sine wave. Good package, good compressor, de facto standard for initial sound processing under Linux (my personal opinion).

In more detail about audio processing was told and demonstrated in the next report by Ilya. He, as a professional musician and composer, has successfully used Ubunty Studio in his work. The story touched upon the basics of the Linux audio system and the more specific Supercollider and Pure Data packages.
