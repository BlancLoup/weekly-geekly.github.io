Illegal number

09 F9 11 02 9D 74 E3 5B D8 41 56 C5 63 56 88 C0

You have just witnessed how I violated the American DMCA law.
If Habrahabr and / or I were in the jurisdiction of the United States, the company AACS LA could seriously sue me. They could hardly have won, but ... the point is not that.

The fact is that this set of innocuous tsiferok is an AACS encryption key - one of the cryptographic keys used to encrypt HD-DVD and Blu-ray disc content. As you probably already guessed, using this key you can bypass the protection against copying these discs, which is illegal in the United States. The fact that not only copying, distribution and public display is considered illegal, but only the fact of circumventing protection is a serious, but a separate topic and I would not like to be distracted by it now.

I am far from being a lawyer, although I do not understand much in the laws, so I will be glad if lawyers correct me in the comments.
