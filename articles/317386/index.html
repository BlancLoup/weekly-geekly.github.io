C ++ Russia 2017



Good news, everyone! I hope you have no plans for the February holidays, because in 2017 the C ++ Russia conference will be held February 24-25.

Briefly about the main thing.

What: pancakes, nesting dolls, C ++!
When: main program 24-25 February, the day of master classes on 23 February.
Where: Moscow, Borodino Hotel (Moscow, Rusakovskaya st., Building 13, Building 5)
Ticket price: 9500 rubles main program, 4000 rubles master class, 2000 rubles online ticket.
Buy tickets here

Keynotes


The advent of Core Guidelines and Guideline Support Library is an important event in the recent history of C ++. So far there are more questions than answers. Therefore, we decided to invite people to talk about GSL and the Core Guidelines first hand. The conference will be opened by Neil MacIntosh: principal software engineer at Microsoft, the Meintener of Microsoft's GSL implementation. He will tell you what GSL is, why it is needed, how it can be used, whether there are already projects that use it, what state different GSL implementations are, and so on. About Core Guidelines tell Sergey Zubkov: a developer from the company Morgan Stanley, who was directly involved in their creation.

Reports


At the conference, presentations on a variety of topics will be presented: boost, Qt, functional programming in C ++, coroutines, C ++ 17, multithreading, specifics of different compilers, changing the code of a large project and much more. If you also want to share your pain with your experience, send a request to talks@cpp-russia.ru . Requirements for the report: no more than 45 minutes, a technical report on C ++ and everything nearby. We will be happy to discuss any topic.

Online tickets


When you buy an online ticket, you get an individual link and you can watch any report in real time. After the conference, this link will be available for two months video recording of reports.

To students


Like last year, there are discount tickets for students. In order to get a discount you need to send a scan or a photo of a student / graduate student to the address students@cpp-russia.ru. In response, we will send you a promotional code and you can buy a ticket for 3,500 rubles.
