The unique IaaS solution from the company "Microtest"

Migration of individual business applications and the entire IT infrastructure to the cloud is becoming an increasingly popular model for the development of corporate IT. More and more Russian companies are actively implementing cloud solutions into their processes and are virtualizing their IT infrastructure in order to achieve greater flexibility and resiliency, as well as to achieve a significant reduction in operating and capital costs for the acquisition, maintenance and support of corporate IT.

Today we consider one of the most relevant services for large and medium-sized businesses. Leasing a virtual computing infrastructure (IaaS) is the first step to the diversity of cloud services: it allows you to get the necessary computing, disk and network capacity and all the necessary capabilities for optimal management of the leased infrastructure with minimal costs.

Cisco Unified Computing System (UCS) IaaS
Cisco today is one of the leading vendors in the development of solutions for the organization of corporate cloud services, in particular IaaS. The Cisco approach to the implementation of commercial IaaS services is based on the Cisco Unified Computing System (UCS) solution, which is characterized by a high level of security and serves as the basis for creating a unified infrastructure to reduce costs, optimize resource use and provide the most secure virtual access to all offered services .

On the territory of Russia, the vendor‚Äôs first solution was used by the Microtest company to organize its services, and in the beginning of October the project is scheduled to be launched into commercial operation. The choice in favor of the approach and Cisco solutions was due to the fact that at the moment it is this company that offers the most modern scalable approach to virtualization solutions. In particular, in Cisco solutions, the blade server is divided into two components: these are the chassis on which the blades are directly installed, and the so-called factory is the switch to which the chassis are connected.

Mikrotest positions its solution as unique on the Russian cloud services market, since the entire cloud infrastructure of the provider is implemented on the basis of high-tech and high-quality equipment from leading world vendors. The cloud is built on the FlexPod reference architecture, based on products from Cisco, Microsoft and NetApp.

FlexPod architecture is the best system for flexible management of your service.
FlexPod is a set of configurations of server and network equipment, data center software, offered by Cisco and NetApp and based on Cisco UCS servers, Cisco Nexus switches, and NetApp FAS storage systems. This solution provides ready-made, unified and tested combinations of equipment that provide all the necessary tools for networking, computing and data storage facilities, the organization of cloud services, the operation of any standard set of technological or applied software.
The benefits of FlexPod are in reducing the cost of architecture development, design, deployment, and testing. The infrastructure deployment cycle is almost halved. When using this solution, resource pools are managed, rather than individual systems.
This is the industry's only solution for creating end-to-end secure multi-user environments. It provides a consistent quality of service at every level and provides independent management of each resource pool. Reducing risks and costs while increasing the flexibility of the IT infrastructure, as well as the proven Cisco architecture, has become one of the key components for us when choosing a solution.
