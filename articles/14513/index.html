"Poster" buys NightParty.ru

The Afisha Industries company is negotiating the purchase of the NightParty.ru portal, one of the most popular club communities in Runet. Negotiations should be completed within the next two weeks. Experts believe that the deal will be useful to both parties. The cost of NightParty.ru is estimated at $ 250- $ 350 thousand.

The Afisha Industries company, part of the Prof-Media holding, is negotiating the purchase of the NightParty.ru portal. As the owner of the portal Arkady Kulev told CNews, they should be completed within the next two weeks. Mr. Kulev declined further comment.

According to experts, the deal will be beneficial to both parties: Afisha will help to enter the well-developing market of nightclubs, which grows in Moscow by an average of 20% per year, and in Russia - by 30%, and bring the project to a qualitatively new level of development and popularity, taking into account the existing experience of the company. NightParty.ru is quite an attractive asset with good attendance (about 200 thousand monthly visitors - approx. CNews.). Prof-Media Holding acquired the Afisha publishing house from the Sputnik group in January 2006. The transaction amount was about $ 18 million. In addition to Afisha, Prof-Media owns controlling stakes in Rambler Media, several newspapers, radio stations, as well as in the Cinema Park network and the Central Partnership company.
