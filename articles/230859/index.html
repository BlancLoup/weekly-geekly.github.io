Head-up Display for any car - how to drive safer in poor visibility

The principle of the application is so simple that it is not clear how nobody did it before. In general, any car owner ( read: the one who has an iPhone and a car ) gets the opportunity to use Head-Up Display-technologies without extra costs.

This is almost space - a drawing of the road, with all its turns, is projected in real time onto the windshield. The idea is to make driving in case of poor visibility more secure - the driver has a picture of the road right in front of the face and in such a way that it is perceived very easily. Well, to focus from the projection on the road and back is just as easy as from the speedometer - and I look at it very often. Yes, for the top car segment this is not new - BMW, Audi and a number of other automakers are already offering the Head-Up display as a built-in option. But for all other HUD-software, which does not require a separate hardware - this is a very cool idea.
