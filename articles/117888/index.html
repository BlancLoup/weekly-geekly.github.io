Webvisor in Yandex. Metric. Look at the site through the eyes of visitors

At the end of 2010, Yandex acquired WebVisor, a technology that displays the actions of website visitors in videos. Since then, users have never tired of asking us when they can use the capabilities of the Webvisor on their sites.

This day is today. The WebVizor technology is integrated into the Yandex.Metrica system.
With the help of a web browser you can study the behavior of site visitors. It records all visitor actions: mouse movements, clicks, page scrolling, keystrokes and form filling, selection and copying of text.

The site owner will see the recording of visits in the video, which will exactly repeat all the actions of the visitor. You can see everything that happened on the monitor of a person while working with the site, as if sitting next to him. At the same time, the tool does not remember passwords, and only the site owner can view the video - and no one else.

The web visitor provides data for a complete behavioral analysis. This will help to identify weaknesses of the site, interface problems and usability.

The report of the Webvisor in the Metric displays a list of all visits with detailed information about each of them. You can view information on each visit and build a report on a group of visitors. Visits are filtered by entry and exit pages, by referral sources, by goal achievement, and other parameters.

There are innovations in the WebVizor technology itself. Now WebVizor support websites created using AJAX, as well as multi-domain websites linked to one counter.
A webvisor is available to Yandex.Direct and Yandex.Market advertisers, who, on average, in the last three months have allocated 6,000 rubles per month to advertise on Yandex.
