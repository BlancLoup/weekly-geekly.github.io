How to become a speaker at PHDays V



December 3 in the program of the fifth international forum on practical security Positive Hack Days opens Call for Papers . If you want to share the results of your research, if you have something to tell the community, then we are waiting for you on May 26 and 27, 2015 as a speaker.

Over the years, forum speakers were cryptographers Bruce Schneier (pictured) and Carsten Nol, security experts Travis Goodspid and Mark Hoise (van Hauser), artificial intelligence expert Igor Ashmanov, State Duma deputies Ruslan Gattarov and Vladimir Zhirinovsky, chairman of the International Partnership for Countermeasures cyber threats (IMPACT) with the UN, Datuk Mohd Nur Amin, and many other famous figures.

Permanent participants of PHDays include top officials, CIO and CISO of the largest Russian and foreign companies, leading international experts in the field of information security, hacker elite from around the world, representatives of the Skolkovo Foundation, government agencies and departments.

The first phase of the CFP will last until January 30. Do not delay with the application - the number of reports that fall into the final program is limited.

Recall that in spring 2014 more than 2500 specialists from 18 countries of the world attended the PHDays IV conference, and in 2015 more than 3000 participants are expected. We advise you to read the reports of last year‚Äôs forum.

We are not so important to your regalia and certificates, as a fresh look at any of the actual problems of information security. The program committee reviews applications from both recognized experts in the field of information security and from beginning researchers.



All details about the format and rules of participation, as well as instructions for submitting an application, can be found on the PHDays website at: www.phdays.ru/program/call_for_papers

We are waiting for you on PHDays V. See you soon!
