Discussion areas




For those who have been on the last Mobius, there will be no surprise in this, but everyone else should know. In contrast to the usual approach, when the speaker after the speech has time for only a few questions from the audience, we make it so that everyone can ask their questions.

How it works: each speaker immediately after his speech moves into the discussion area - a specially designed place with an illustration board, seating and the opportunity to grab a cup of coffee. And everyone with a report that has raised questions can ask them there with feeling, sensibly, with an arrangement: delving into the nuances and clarifying everything incomprehensible.

In general, it becomes clear ‚Äúwhy go to conferences when it is possible to watch and read something on the Internet‚Äù: it‚Äôs not just a one-way flow of information from experts that pours out, but there are detailed dialogues with them.

Online streaming




All conference reports can be viewed online by purchasing the appropriate ticket . Such a broadcast has even a slight advantage over the live presence: if at the beginning of the report you suddenly realize that for some reason it does not fit, it is easier to switch to another room than to run between them.

We are making a broadcast not for the first time, but from year to year it becomes only more beautiful. Here are some recent improvements worth telling:

  • Video in 1440p - thanks to this, you can simultaneously display the presentation of the speaker in FullHD, and the speaker himself;
  • Although the broadcast does not have access to the discussion areas, this does not mean that the audience does not ask any questions to the speakers at all. They can be written in the Telegram-chat of the conference - and if there is enough time for them at the end of the report, the trackers will set them;
  • And in the intervals between presentations, when the speakers and participants on the site are removed to the discussion zones, the audience of the online broadcast does not have to be bored, especially for them we make separate content: interviews with speakers and sponsors. If you wish, you can get an idea about them by recording the broadcast of Mobius 2017 Moscow;
  • The sound quality has been improved, the recording system has been seriously reworked;
  • Many minor technical problems were solved, which most did not even notice, but they were there. In general, the feeling of watching the broadcast should be better.
