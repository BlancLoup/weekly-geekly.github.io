Microsoft Corporation and Softline announce the launch of a special program for students and university staff: "Accessible Microsoft Office 2007 for student and teacher." Students of any form of study (full-time and part-time) over the age of 18, as well as teachers and other employees of those universities and Associations of universities that have signed the Academic Select Student agreement with Microsoft, can participate in the program.

Under the program "Accessible Microsoft Office 2007 student and teacher" you can purchase a license for Microsoft Office 2007 "Standard 2007" or Microsoft Office 2007 "Professional Plus" at a special discount price: the cost of Microsoft Office 2007 "Standard 2007" is 1500 rub., Microsoft Office 2007 Professional Plus - 1900 rubles. Along with the license comes a disc with an activation key. The certificate confirming the license will be the Academic Select Student License Agreement, which indicates the number of the agreement with the University or with the Association of Universities, which includes the university, product, version number and full terms of the agreement. (The agreement will be sent electronically by e-mail after payment of the order.) Detailed information about the program is available at www.studentoffice.ru .
