Google gadgets can now be published on any site.

The official Google blog announces a new gift to all webmasters: they can now place on their sites any of the gadgets offered in the catalog !

If before they could only be used on a personalized Google page , now anyone can get the html-code to insert it into the pages of their sites. Among those proposed are virtually all of the most popular gadgets, such as date and time , a calculator , a currency converter , moon phases , bushism, and even a pony .

Of course, most of them are aimed at residents of the United States, but there is also some Russian-language content (for example, 24news ).
