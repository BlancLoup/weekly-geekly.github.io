Comparison of tariffs for mobile Internet in different regions

Mobile operators have a strikingly different tariff scale on the Internet in different regions, but many of these tariffs either work all over Russia right away or require an additional option to be connected, which, nevertheless, can be cheaper than using the tariffs of your region.

It happens, sometimes I have to use only mobile Internet for a week, and even in another region. Traffic for this week is spent about 3 gigabytes, although I usually have enough gigabytes per month.

I would like to have some kind of SIM for travel and everyday use, where would be the cheapest traffic, but which operator and which tariff / package to choose? This is what I tried to find out. As you understand, SIM will be used all the time outside the home region, so this comparison does not claim to be complete, because I considered only those options that interested me. It should be noted that the calls and SMS did not interest me at all - I do not call and do not call me.
