What's next for Delicious?

Many of you have recently read the news about Delicious. We are sincerely sorry that there was so little information in this news that interested our regular users. We cannot answer each of your questions separately, but we would like to explain a few points that we can touch on at this stage, and we promise to keep you updated on developments in the future.

Delicious closes? Should I worry about my data?

- No, we do not close Delicious. However, we have determined that the service is not strategically important for Yahoo, and we believe that the ideal home for Delicious is outside the company where the service can be provided with resources at the level at which it can be competitive.

What are Yahoo going to do with Delicious?

- We thought a lot about the future of Delicious, and we believe that there is a home for service outside the company, which will be better for it and for users. We are in the process of exploring various options and talking to companies right now. And we will tell you about our plans as quickly as possible.

What if I want to pick up my bookmarks from Delicious right now?

- As noted above, there is no reason to panic. We support Delicious and encourage you to continue using it. However, we have the ability to export. In addition, many services provide the ability to import links and tags from Delicious.

We can only guess how frustrating the latest news was for many of you. As for us, we were very disappointed with the way everything was covered in the news. We will let you know more as events unfold.
