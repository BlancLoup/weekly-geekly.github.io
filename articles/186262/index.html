Mail.Ru run mail for personal domains



Today I learned about a new service from Mail.Ru for business - biz.mail.ru. It is in beta and is a mail organization service in its own domain. Direct competitor for services such as: pdd.yandex.ru , google.com/a or domains.live.com .

At the moment it is free (and probably will remain so) and moreover, it gives the opportunity to register a domain in the RU zone for free :

nserver: ns1.reg.ru.
nserver: ns2.reg.ru.
state: REGISTERED, DELEGATED, UNVERIFIED
org: Mail.Ru, LLC
registrar: REGRU-REG-RIPN
admin-contact: www.reg.ru/whois/admin_contact
created: 2013.07.10
paid-till: 2014.07.10
free-date: 2014.08.10
source: TCI

From Whois, we see that the domain is registered to Mail.Ru data, and the registrar and DNS host is REGRU. It is strange that they did not verify (UNVERIFIED) their contact. Also in the panel on biz.mail.ru it is possible to register your A-record in DNS. Of the main features:

  • Unlimited Mailbox Size
  • Online viewing of office documents
  • Mobile Applications
  • The calendar
  • Integration with Mail.Ru Agent
  • 5000 boxes on the domain

It has long been thought about why Mail.Ru does not have such a service. They waited :)
