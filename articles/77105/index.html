Tightening rules freelance-exchange Weblancer.net

One of the largest freelance exchanges among the CIS countries has published planned innovations. In general, they are aimed at tightening the rules for working with the stock exchange, and it is freelancers, not employers, who are limited in their rights. Now you need to confirm your passport data and pay a monthly fee for access to the service.

1. Requirement of mandatory provision of personal data

Mandatory provision of full name, date of birth and location.
This restriction was introduced in the service more than 10 days ago, but users who filled out the profile earlier will need to make the appropriate adjustments.

The correspondence of the user's IP address and the specified location will be checked in a test mode using specialized services.

As an option:

To confirm the accuracy of the name and dates of birth freelancers will need to provide a scanned image of the first page of the passport.
Passport series and number must be hidden before shipping. The image is not saved and is deleted immediately after checking.
