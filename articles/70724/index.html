Site structure scanner

GmadS - Google madnet Scaner

www.madnet.name


This service is designed to determine the structure of the site. Often, we have to use various scanners to determine the structure of the site, but we forget that in 99% of cases, the best network service, great and powerful GOOGLE, did it to us, and often he remembers something that a mortal cannot get to. This system is trying to extract the maximum information about the site from Google and build on the basis of its site tree, the utility will also be useful for web programmers to analyze Google site indexing.

Scanner features:
1) Search for PHPINFO information available for viewing.
2) Displaying directories that are prohibited for indexing
3) Display a list of Sites on aka server (ReverseIP)
4) Server Utility Information



A useful thing.
