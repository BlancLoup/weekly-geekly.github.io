IT Jam 2013

September 7-8, near Kiev, IT Jam 2013 will be held.
This year, the organizers will be pleasantly surprised by the completely changed format of the event.

With the assistance of professional moderators of the WikiCityNomiki team, the first Foresight of the leaders of Ukrainian IT companies will occur.


Foresight will focus on modeling the future and developing optimal strategies for the development of Ukrainian IT. For 2 days, IT Jam 2013 will become a creative space in which participants will be able to learn and work with innovative tools to achieve their goals.

Forsyth is designed for leaders by nature, since only they can create fundamentally new things, set boundaries of the desired future and combine the energy of many for the benefit of everyone.

At IT Jam 2013, active participants of Ukrainian IT will gather, who contribute to the development of the industry, giving reports on various topics, speaking at various events and organizing conferences and meetings of IT communities.

IT Jam 2013 promises to be the most unusual IT event of the year as it will bring together the brightest and most enterprising people to create something unique together.

If you are an active speaker or organizer of Ukrainian IT conferences and communities, then you are the person who would be interested in becoming a member of IT Jam 2013, submit your application to Alexandra cha@ciklum.com, skype Emfimbria or +380504000270. Limited number of seats.
