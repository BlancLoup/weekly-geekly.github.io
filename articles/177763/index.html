About cloud and not only technologies of Yandex in Kiev this Saturday

This Saturday in Kiev it will be possible to hear firsthand about how we develop our services, what from our experience will help you in your work and about many other interesting things.


In particular, you will learn in detail:
  • how the Yandex.Disk data warehouse is organized (and these are billions of files and folders, tens of millions of users, high availability and fault tolerance);
  • what APIs Yandex has and how they can be useful;
  • what Yandex.Predctor is and how you can use it;
  • how to use BEM in your work (you can ask any questions to its developers).

Come necessarily - it will be very interesting.
