Kane & Lynch: Dead Men went to print

Another potential hit, the game Kane & Lynch: Dead Men went to print. In Russia, both stamping discs with the English version, and localization of the creation of Eidos and IO Interactive studio deals with 1C. According to the publisher, the English version of the game will appear "on the shelves" on November 30. The translation will be released a little later: the date has not yet been named, but work is already underway.

Kane & Lynch: Dead Men - team fighter with a few non-standard gameplay. The player will have to manage two "bad guys" - a mercenary and a psychopath, getting involved in fraud, robbery, murder and read nasty things. Knowing the capabilities of IO Interactive Studio (and these are the guys who made the unforgettable Hitman), we can assume that Kane & Lynch: Dead Men turned out to be a very interesting product.
