RailsClub 2016: October 22 in Moscow will be the creator of Ruby Yukihiro Matsumoto and not only

Hi Habr! Three months left before the main Ruby event of the year in Russia. The RailsClub 2016 Conference will be held this year on October 22 in Moscow, in the Technopolis Congress Center. An excellent list of speakers, the hottest topics, one day, two streams, 500 participants. Begin to reveal the details!



This year's conference is especially important for us. First of all, because after long years of persuasion, Ruby creator Yukihiro Matsumoto himself will come to us to speak! We have been preparing for this event for a very long time, we recorded an invitation video for Matsumoto-san with the help of the entire community. If you have not seen, then see:



Everything worked out!

Yukihiro Matsumoto will give a talk. And if you have questions about why Ruby is so and not otherwise, be sure to come and ask the person who started it all. Buy a ticket , and we are already doing for Matsumoto-san a visa to Russia.


Another guest from Japan is Akira Matsuda . A person who does a lot of things in the open source world, a committer in Rails, Ruby, Haml, the creator of a heap of popular plugins and libraries: Kaminari, activedecorator, actionargs, i18ngenerators, html5validators, motorhead, erd, database_rewinder, etc. And the creator of the Ruby community in Japan, Asakusa.rb.


The third speaker does not come to us for the first time, and we are glad to welcome him again to the conference. Steve Klabnik , Rails and Ruby committer, recently has been mainly working on Rust.
