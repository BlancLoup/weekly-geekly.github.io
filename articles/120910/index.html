Jizo Hotel Search Service


We want to tell you about the new useful feature of our project Jizo - hotel search service hotels.jizo.ru
Now, within the resource, you can plan your own travel: find a convenient flight and find the most suitable hotel.

Since the end of May, Jizo provides site visitors with the opportunity to select hotels from more than 30 of the world's largest hotel suppliers, including such well-known brands as Expedia.com, Booking.com, Hotels.com and others. As a result, visitors can access hundreds of thousands of hotels around the world!

How it works:
Jizo.ru meta search engine in real time collects offers from all possible sources: from airlines and travel agencies. This data is compared and presented to users in a convenient form. The user is able to evaluate the convenience and cost in a few seconds and understand their dependence. As a result, the system redirects the user to the website of the supplier who has found the best offer.

To search for a hotel, the user only needs to specify the city and travel dates so that the system offers hundreds of available hotel options.

The Jizo.ru service is free for users. Try and express your opinion - perhaps it will make the service even more convenient.
