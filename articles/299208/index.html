The right holder of the trademark requests to change the name of the series House of Cards

Excerpt holder of the trademark House of Cards can be envied.
He tolerated before the start of the 4th season and only then filed a lawsuit about the violation of his rights.





Background


Trademark company D2 HOLDINGS LLC registered in 2009 for services
41 classes
There is a lot of fun in the world of music and television; satellite, audio, and video; Music, variety, news and comedy shows; There are various types of transmission media; Entertainment services, namely, providing an on-going radio program.


During the validity of the TK, the company granted the right to it to only one company - Granary Way Media, which uses it for the radio show about the gaming industry of the same name.

The series itself started on February 1, 2013. There is no reason to talk about the bad faith of the copyright holder. There is more likely an oversight of the producers and the risk when choosing the name is clearly not taken into account.

In defense of the creators of the series, it should be noted that they themselves tried to register the name, but received several refusals of 41 classes (just for the TV show).
