Portable subwoofer for Macbook



Twelve South has released a subwoofer designed to turn your Macbook into a portable sound system.

The BassJump subwoofer is designed specifically for the Macbook, in a proprietary design, one of a kind, convenient to transport and powered via USB. To connect the device, only one USB cable is used, through which power and sound are transmitted.







The software acts as a crossover, equalizer and filter, dividing the sound of the Macbook's built-in speakers and the BassJump subwoofer, expanding the sound range. When the BassJump is connected, the built-in speakers automatically start working as tweeters (tweeters). After that, listening to music from iTunes is a pleasure!









At your disposal are various frequency adjustments and several popular presets - rock, pop, R & B, etc ... It‚Äôs really convenient to use BassJump - one program, one cable, one volume control.

To assess how much the system improves the standard Macbook sound, developers recommend using the large on / off button.









Like the Macbook, the BassJump subwoofer is completely portable. It is easy to hire, visit or travel. There is a convenient neoprene case for this.
