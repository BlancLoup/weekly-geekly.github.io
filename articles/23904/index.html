AnjLab.SyncIT - sync your tasks with Microsoft Outlook

Teamwork is unthinkable without the means to manage tasks, distribute them between team members, track changes. For this purpose, task tracking modules of such popular teamwork systems as Trac, Team Foundation Server, Salesforce, etc. are used. At the same time, it is more convenient for a person to store all his tasks, both personal and official, in a personal organizer on his computer or PDA. MS Outlook 2007 is well suited for this purpose.

There is no contradiction between these approaches; the task is to synchronize the ‚Äúcollective‚Äù and ‚Äúpersonal‚Äù repositories of tasks. This problem can be solved with the help of our development SyncIT.
