Hyperloop - the transport of the future



In 2012, Musk announced another project - the high-speed vacuum train Hyperloop (Hyperloop). He presented his idea as the fifth mode of transport, the fastest and cheapest.

Hyperloop was conceived as an overhead pipeline located on supports, inside of which single transport capsules move at a speed of 480 to 1,220 km / h with an interval of 30 seconds in one direction. Two options were assumed: a passenger with an internal diameter of 2.23 m, a capsule section of 1.35 mx 1.1 m, which can accommodate 2 rows of seats with 14 seats each; passenger and cargo with an internal pipe diameter of 3.3 m.



Hyperloop project

The capsule had to move with the help of a linear electric motor. To this end, it was envisaged to obtain energy using solar panels. They must generate 57 MW with the system need only 21 MW. Musk promises passengers an unprecedented level of security. In case of emergencies in the nose of the capsule behind the fan there was an electric compressor, which was supposed to accumulate compressed air on board during depressurization. Also in the stern placed 1.5 tons of batteries, which will last for 45 minutes. This time is enough to get to the nearest station during a power outage.

In January 2015, Musk announced his intention to build a special track for testing Hyperloop. According to preliminary information, the project will be implemented in Texas.
