Hewlett-Packard buys Tabblo, a photo startup

A photographic startup Tabblo , repeatedly sung in various reviews, will soon become the property of Hewlett-Packard . The latter is largely interested in technology that facilitates the process of printing photos over the Internet.

Financial details of the transaction have not been disclosed. The purpose of the purchase is quite obvious - firstly, to provide users with new services and thus increase the audience, and secondly, to earn extra money. The contract is scheduled to be signed as soon as possible.
