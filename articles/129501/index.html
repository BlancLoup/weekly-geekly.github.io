Freedom Tablet from Packard Bell. Video review

Something for a long time we did not post any video reviews. Today is the time for the Packard Bell Liberty Tab G100 10-inch tablet. This device is strongly reminiscent of Acer Iconia Tab A500 and in terms of characteristics and appearance, which is not surprising, because Packard Bell is fully owned by Acer.



At the heart of the gadget is the Nvidia Tegra 2 chip, and the screen resolution is 1280x800. The tablet has a rather stylish red case. However, the dimensions are not very pleasing: 760 grams, 177x260x13 mm.



The device works on Android 3.0 Honeycomb , although in some stores firmware version 3.2 is already listed. In this case, there are no significant changes to the shell, and the operating system works smartly. In general, the gadget is focused on a business audience and is a strong representative of tablets on Android.
