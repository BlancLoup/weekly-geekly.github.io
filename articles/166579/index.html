Yes, first of all - stylish. And then everything else.
Black glossy plastic, only sharp corners, chopped forms. The designer took and drew his monitor on the line. And it turned out very tricky. The stand is asymmetrical, thanks to which the bulkiness of the 27 inch display is not felt. It looks like a 20-22 inch.
As it usually happens with designers, they disagree with technologists. Design business - small. Drew and all. And the technologist must shove the components so that it still works, but does not break during operation. However, here the designer and technologist apparently worked in the neighboring premises and listened to one another. It turned out that the monitor is stylish, while comfortable.
The delivery package is very simple. The monitor itself, stand, D-Sub cable, external power supply, audio cable (jack-jack), brochures and manuals - everything.
The power supply is a classic notebook.

For the gloss, there is not enough rags, well, I would like at least one digital cable.
However, the monitor has digital only HDMI ports. On the one hand, this is bad, your DVI-D cable will have to be connected via an adapter, and on the other hand, there are two HDMI ports, so your periphery (smartphone, tablet) can be connected with a standard, short wire. All ports are on the stand (in the leg). Also located there is a Kensington lock.

In addition to connectors, on the stand are also control keys. Fortunately, they are mechanical. Also, on the stand, there is a work indicator (blue).

When connecting all the wires comes out very carefully - everything remains at the level of the table, nothing hangs down from the display.

The display itself is FullHD IPS matrix, glossy protective plastic. Again, dual impressions.

Gloss is the enemy of office work. Everything is reflected, eyes get tired. To somehow help yourself - you need to unscrew the brightness to the full, which means that the eyes will tire even more. But if you use the monitor at home, or just in a not very lighted place, then all its potential is revealed.

Very good color rendition, excellent (even outrageous) brightness, well, good response time (6 ms). Yes, as usual - maximum viewing angles.

Resolution - FullHD 1920 * 1080 pixels. Yes that's right. FullHD resolution at 27 inches. This is the same resolution as on a 42 inch LCD TV, or the same as Sony Xperia Z (and all trend smartphones in 2013).
But this resolution has an indisputable advantage: if your smartphone or laptop has FullHD resolution - you can use the Acer S275 as an external monitor without changing anything. It is very convenient! Pixel to pixel.
