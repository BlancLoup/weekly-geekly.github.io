Mail.ru overtakes Rambler in profitability

The owners of Mail.ru portal yesterday reported on their financial results for the first half of the year (April-September 2009). Oddly enough, the results were pretty successful ( PDF ). Moreover, in terms of revenue, Mail.ru came in second place among all RuNet portals, pushing out Rambler.

For six months, Mail.ru earned 1.028 billion rubles. (+ 30.7% over the same period last year), while the Rambler revenues decreased to 1.019 billion (-17.5%).
