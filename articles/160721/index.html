AppClub Kyiv {build, monetize} - a club for mobile enthusiasts and professionals

Recently in Kiev, we organized a club for mobile enthusiasts and professionals called AppClub Kyiv . The first meeting was devoted to the global launch of Windows 8 in the world and gathered about 25 participants. Before that, we organized User Group meetings for several years, so the idea was not born from scratch.



The goals of the club are: professional communication on the subject of mobile development, promotion and monetization, as well as providing an opportunity for mobile start-ups to tell about themselves and get live feedback. Platforms do not matter much.

We plan to discuss topics such as:

  • development and promotion
  • monetization schemes
  • how to work directly with various App Store
  • legal issues for mobile developers
  • trends and prospects of mobile
  • crowdfunding and crowdsourcing

Deep Dive into Windows Phone and Windows 8 development


On December 20, we will hold the second AppClub Kyiv meeting, where practical aspects of development for Windows Phone 8 and Windows 8 will be considered. We will share only practical experience in developing successful projects.

Planned reports:

  1. Migrating WP7 applications on Windows Phone 8.
  2. Improve the performance of your Windows Phone applications.
  3. Development of Windows 8 applications: real experience.

Speakers:

  1. Krakovetsky Alexander (DevRain Solutions). Microsoft Regional Director, Microsoft MVP, by wp7rocks.com;
  2. Galushko Michael (DevRain Solutions). MCPD, by wp7rocks.com.

The program may change a little, but only for the better.

Participation is paid, but within reasonable limits. You can register via the appclubkyiv.eventbrite.com link.

You can follow the events of AppClub Kyiv in the Facebook group , twitter and on the website .

For speakers and partners


So, if you -

  • a speaker with practical experience in the development, promotion and monetization of mobile applications;
  • a startup that uses mobile platforms;
  • a company willing to support the AppClub initiative;
  • enthusiast and want to become an AppClub representative in your city,

then email us at info@devrain.com and we will discuss all the details of our cooperation.

Windows Phone 8 Camp


Also on December 3, in the Microsoft Ukraine office, Windows Phone 8 Camp will be held - a one-day event dedicated to the development of applications for Windows Phone 8.

Reports:

  1. Sergey Baydachny (Microsoft Ukraine). Windows Phone 8: a general overview of the platform.
  2. Sergey Lutai (DCT). Model of Windows Phone 8 applications.
  3. Alexander Krakovetsky (DevRain Solutions). Windows Phone 8: Best Practices for Developing High-End Applications
  4. Sergey Baydachny (Microsoft Ukraine). C ++ when developing Windows Phone 8 applications.

Participation is free, registration by reference .
