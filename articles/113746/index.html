ZX Spectrum - my first computer

Acquaintance


My first computer appeared more than ten years ago. He was brought by the godfather from Moscow. By the time I was in elementary school. Strictly speaking, this device even, perhaps, could not be called a computer, rather a prefix. Because, in my understanding, the computer had to consist of a screen, a system unit and a keyboard. Here was one keyboard, a bunch of wires and a blue power supply with a big white button on the side.





However, it did not bother me. Of course, the most worried about connecting the keyboard to the display. We did not have a monitor at home, but there were two televisions of the Foton brand. One black and white, the other - color. It never occurred to me that they could be combined, and get a full-fledged computer. However, it was possible.

A friend of my dad showed a diagram of how you can connect a keyboard to a TV - this is how a switch-lever appeared between the computer mode and the TV mode itself on the TV. Communication with the TV has been established. There were no problems with the power supply. It remains only to attach the keyboard to the recorder "Spring". Information was on a magnetic tape. On the tape were recorded or programs, or games. I inserted a cassette, entered the necessary command on the keyboard, and - let's go. The boot process was long, tedious and unnecessarily noisy. While the program or game was loading, unusual sounds were heard from the speakers of the tape recorder. In addition, sometimes at some stage in the boot process on the TV screen could appear some image. It took 30 minutes or more to download one program.
