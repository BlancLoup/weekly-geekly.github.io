Optical box for work in Spartan conditions



We will gradually begin to acquaint the audience of Geektimes with the products of our telecom supermarket . Today, we will talk about the new product, which will be of interest primarily to those who work ‚Äúin the field‚Äù, providing access to the network of the end user.

The SNR-FTTH-FDB-24A distribution box to 24 subscriber ports is designed to accommodate optical connectors and terminate a linear cable with patchcords (pigtails). The design of the box provides for the removal of the subscriber cable. The box can be used in fiber-optic networks with deep optical penetration (FTTH, PON, etc.).


Application area:

  • FTTH
  • Pon
  • GPON
  • Telecommunication networks
  • Local Area Networks

The design of the box allows you to enter two linear cable for mounting which uses special clamps. The power elements of the cable are fastened with special clamps.



The case of the box is made of a special UV-resistant plastic that allows it to be operated in a wide range of temperatures (from -40 ¬∞ C to +85 ¬∞ C).


To protect against vandals lid box is equipped with a lock. A rubber sealing gasket located along the entire perimeter of the optical cross between the housing and the cover ensures complete tightness and protects the cable from dust and moisture. Therefore, SNR-FTTH-FDB-24A is an ideal solution for the implementation of input-distribution devices in places with adverse environmental effects, be it a street or dusty rooms.


Inside the box is a flip panel, which on the one hand has a panel for installing 24 optical adapters of the SC type, and on the other hand is a large splice cassette in which there is a place to accommodate 24 welded fiber joints. Thus, in addition to the 24 connections for the bends, you can place more connections transit fibers. Inside the box there is a place to accommodate the optical dividers of the PLC.


Boxing is mounted on poles, walls and another surface. For mounting the box on the pillars, you can use mounting tape, clamps, metal plates or tension pliers.
