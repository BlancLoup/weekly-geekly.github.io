Megafon, MTS and Beeline launch 3G in Moscow. And what about the tariffs?

It is finished.

Actually you can read about the news itself, for example, by searching Google News .

Megafon-Moscow has a news-announcement of the launch of 3g in Moscow :
December 14, 2009. Today, MegaFon has received permission from Roskomnadzor to use the frequencies necessary for the third generation network to work outside the premises in Moscow. <...>

‚ÄúMegaFon is leading in terms of 3G deployment in the metropolitan region, we intend to be the first to serve 3G subscribers on the streets of Moscow. If all the documents required for a commercial launch are processed in an expedited manner, MegaFon will be able to start phasing in 3G base stations in Moscow for servicing outside subscribers even before the New Year . <...>

At Beeline and MTS, there was no news about the launch of 3G at the time of this writing.

The fact that Megafon is going to install several hundred towers in a couple of weeks, of course, gives us confidence in our bright future.

However, 3G, in the first place, means an increase in the data transfer rate to 7.2 Mbps, i.e. and a half orders of magnitude above EDGE. This means that with the current average price of 6 rubles per megabyte, you can very quickly destroy your balance. And the STOP button is unlikely to save here.

So, what did the operators offer?

Megaphone


3G services are available to all MegaFon subscribers who have phones or modems that support this standard. Switching to the 3G network from the GSM network occurs automatically, the current tariff of the subscriber is saved. For active users, a special tariff offer "Unlimited Internet" has been created .

In short: anlim package for 1,100 rubles per month (+150 for connection)

This ‚Äúwonderful‚Äù fare has a lot of reservations:
The package is not available in roaming.
The package is available only to subscribers of the tariff plans of the Modem and Communicator series.
When using a packet from 01:00 to 08:00, the volume of transmitted and received packet traffic is not taken into account.
When using a packet from 08:00 to 01:00, if the transmitted and received packet traffic exceeds 5 GB per month, the data transfer rate is limited to 128 kbps.
The offer is valid from 10/05/2009 until 01/31/2010.
