Buick Riviera is one of the bright novelties of the Shanghai Auto Show.



At the Shanghai Auto Show Buick presented the 2 + 2 concept Riviera. The car is the result of a joint venture between GM and the Chinese company SAIC. In addition to the stunning design, the novelty promises many surprises. While the specifications of the car remain secret, but GM representatives announced the release of Rivera in 2008. He will be associated with the opening of the Beijing Olympic Games.

At the moment, it is known that the car will be equipped with 21-inch wheels, special panels of carbon fiber and chrome-plated elements. In addition, blue lamps are installed in a large number on it, which, as seen in the photos, look amazing on a silver case.

It is also worth paying attention to the implementation of the door, which is called the "wings of the seagull." In terms of convenience, such a design decision may raise questions, but only positive emotions remain in aesthetic terms.

As for the Rivera model, about 1.1 million of these cars have been sold in the United States since their introduction from 1963 to 1999. What awaits the new model is difficult to say. But, given the current trends in sales of Buick, the main number of customers will be in China.
