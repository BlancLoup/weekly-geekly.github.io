Review of the budget 8 "Chuwi Vi8 Plus tablet on Windows 10 for $ 88

In December 2015, a fresh Chuwi Vi8 Plus tablet with a full-fledged Windows 10, 4000 mAh battery, Intel Cherry Trail X5 Z8300 processor with a frequency of 1.84GHz, 2 GB of RAM and 32 GB of internal memory went on sale. On this tablet, you can play in some Bioshock, and at the same time there is a gadget less than $ 100.



Design


Chuwi Vi8 Plus * - the usual sound tablet with steel stripes on the right and left and the Windows button in the center. At the same time, there are no complaints about the build quality at all - no backlash or squeaks. It seems as if done for ages. But let's see how long he will live with the active use of my two-year-old daughter.



Of the benefits - the presence of the micro HDMI port in the upper end of the tablet, next to the USB Type-C port and headphone input. In general, it was strange to see in the budget Type-C device instead of the standard micro USB, but there were no wires like that at home.



From the right end of the tablet (or from above, when it is placed in the cover with the keyboard) - the power button, the volume rocker and just below - the port for the micro SD card. In the eMMC 32GB tablet, the port supporting the 64 GB card is very useful.





The manufacturer has thought over the design of the back cover of the tablet - it is plastic and has a pattern on which no scratches will be noticeable. And she is unmarked when it comes to black.

The tablet is equipped with two cameras of 2 megapixels.





In any case, I advise when buying a tablet, which now costs $ 88.46 per share, to purchase a cover with a keyboard. The keyboard is charged using microUSB, you can not call it very convenient - but this is out of habit, and because of the size. Play some Bioshock quite real. The cover itself looks solid, the magnets are strong - the keyboard just won't fall off.

I did not expect anything special from a tablet cheaper than hundreds of dollars. Therefore, I was very surprised when I could run Bioshock on it without any problems. Of course, the game is old - as much as 2007, and even at that time is not the most demanding, but still the fact that it goes on this tablet without brakes and at maximum settings is a good sign.





The tablet is based on a quad-core Intel Cherry Trail X5 Z8300 processor with a frequency of 1.84GHz. This processor is sharpened to work with no more than 2 GB of RAM. Responsible for the graphics mobile graphics accelerator Intel HD Graphics, supports DirectX 12.

Screen - IPS with a resolution of 1280 by 800 pixels. Everything can be seen perfectly at home, but I have not tried it on the street - after all, it is not summer.



The tablet supports Bluetooth 4.0. It has HDMI V1.4 output, supporting 1080p HD video. For charging and data transfer uses USB Type-C.



The tablet has 32 GB of memory, of which 28 GB is available to the user. Most of this space I successfully occupied Bioshock. Lack of memory will be a problem for you if you want to install several games at once. True, playing at eight inches is not very convenient - but it will be possible to connect the gadget to the TV.
