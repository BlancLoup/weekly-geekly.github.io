As the Prophotos team launched this week the First Open National Photo Awards "Best Photographer - 2009" (then it will become ... -2010, ... -2011, etc.), opening the site BestPhotographer.ru .

As you remember, in general we already had a portal about contests . So why another one, you ask? We answer. The first competitive site, Photoawards , was like the first stage of preparation for the project. We got acquainted with our audience even closer - if users only read Prophotos.ru magazine , now they have shown on Photoawards.ru that they are shooting themselves. We recognized their hopes, their aspirations. During the work with this project, we met many good photographers who, having downloaded literally a few photos, acquired the highest - platinum - status. But then they are masters - each photo turned out to be a prize in its competition. It was these users that we were the first to invite to participate in the Award.

Unlike the Photoawards.ru contests, the First Opened National Photographic Awards "Best Photographer", although it has a website on the Internet, is primarily an offline project. Works are submitted via the Network, but their further life - exhibitions, books and, of course, the award ceremony takes place offline. In the same place, offline, master classes of the award participants are held. Unlike our past projects, here the site is just an auxiliary tool.

During the preparation for the project, we managed to communicate with a bunch of various very important people from the world of photography, who were called to the jury. In general, the work with the Prize is interesting - the Prize will be developed together with us, and we along with it.
