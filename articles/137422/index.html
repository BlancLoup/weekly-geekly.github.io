Courses for upgrading SharePoint developers



Hello friends!

I am announcing a good educational topic: ADV together with IT Academy and Microsoft created a training program for SharePoint specialists.

The program will be useful to good dot-developers who want to become SharePoint developers, as well as beginner sharpenters who want to upgrade and systematize their skills.

The first set will be free for students. There are only 15 places in the group, so if there is a wish, we must act quickly.



The course is based on combat missions that arise in real work. There will be a lot of practice and certified trainers. Start - March 5, the duration - 1 month.

Following the results of training, anyone can pass an interview for a SharePoint developer job at ADV. If suddenly there are 15 volunteers and everyone will be wonderful people with good skills and great potential, then we will send their resumes to other HR services - there is hardly a good shareholder in Moscow who is left without work.
