Sony Xperia SmartWatch - where are the arrows?

Earlier this week, the network has pictures of the new accessory Sony Ericsson LiveView 2 . It turned out that this gadget now bears a slightly different name, the company Sony called it a smart wrist watch. At the stand of the Japanese manufacturer, we managed to learn a little more about it.



The case of the Sony Xperia SmartWatch differs from its predecessor in a more stylish design with a metal frame and a strap mechanism. This time the device has a touch screen and just one button.



To display the time, it is enough to shake a hand. All the rest is done by tapping the screen. For the command back, tap is used simultaneously with two fingers. All smart watches are controlled from an Android smartphone using the LiveWare application. Android version requirements are 2.1 or higher. You can install various applications, widgets on the watch, and even send pre-prepared SMS messages from them.
