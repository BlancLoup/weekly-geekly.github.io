UserLook - Find out what's happening on your site now.

UserLook - provides the ability to monitor the situation on the site in real time.



Below is a cat interesting facts about a startup.


Why did we decide to launch this project?


For about a year, we used a similar American service, but in it we lacked some goodies. In particular, we monitored a multi-domain site. In their service, all subdomains were equal to the one in the first request. And all such pages were highlighted under this subdomain. This greatly distorted the real picture of the visitors. We among many made a request for this chip to them, but a year has passed and the situation has not changed. Then we decided - why not try to make the service even better?

How long did it take to implement?


The service was implemented in three approaches 35 + 7 + 14 days of coding and testing evenings
Initially, there was a plan to launch everything in 35 days, but we successfully failed it, there was an interval of about 3 months between the first and second approaches when the project was upholding.

How much spent finance?


Everything was done on its own and at its own expense, without attracting investment from outside. Basic costs: server fees, domains, costs covered by profit from other projects.

What is the service implemented on?


Service implemented on a bunch of Django + Nginx + MongoDb + Postgresql

How much is?


Until the service of accepting payment is connected, the service will be free for all.

Current features:


  • View statistics in real time
  • The ability to view the situation that was on the site at any time, during the saved period
  • Track project references by selected keywords on Twitter
  • Ability to view from which countries your visitors come
  • It is also possible to see how many visitors and on which page are located, the module ‚ÄúPopular pages‚Äù
  • Tracking referrer allows you to identify sources of referrals to your site
  • Quick registration through popular services or OpenId does not take precious time
  • Fully automatic data update process, no need to refresh the page.
  • Setting the time zone allows you to display data in your local time
  • Support for monitoring multi-domain sites
  • Interface without using ActiveX, Flash, support iPad


In the near future we will develop the service in the following direction:


  • Have an idea to enable live chat with users.
  • The module that will show the average loading time of pages on the site
  • The system of notifications about events occurring on the site such as peak attendance or site downtime


We will be glad to have constructive comments and ideas on development from Habrupt users, we are waiting for you on our feedback.userlook.com support forum
