New experience


1. New monetization model


We decided that it is worthwhile to open only a few courses at once, and everything else is revealed for coins, which can be obtained in open courses, answering correctly or buying for real money. The more questions in the course, the more expensive it is.

Screenshots



For all the time of 64,000 (15,000 currently active) installations, as many as 9 purchases were made!



2. Repeating unsuccessful monetization model in iOS version of the application


We did not manage to feel the difference between income from the Android version and iOS.

"Android installs apps more actively, but iOS users spend a lot more money."

But alas and ah. The version for the Apple Store, we released November 16, 2016.
For all the time, with 10,000 users there were absolutely 5 purchases!

3. Little time was spent on content.


The questions in the application are not organized in an appropriate way, sometimes they resemble ‚Äúpasta‚Äù, and it is very difficult to find something concrete without searching. Our courses are located in a list with three levels of nesting with advertising banners between the elements. And to remove from the list of courses such names as: ‚ÄúFinal training 1‚Äù, ‚ÄúFinal training 2‚Äù did not reach the hands.

4. Spied on solutions from popular applications, but did not use


When it comes to such content as ‚ÄúThe Russian Language‚Äù, there are a lot of opinions, both erroneous and true, in which some argue that the question is incomprehensible, the answers are incorrect and they all write in reviews, underestimating the rating of the application. One of the successful solutions I saw in Duolingo, where you can see the discussion of the issue with other users, and the answers of experts, as well as leave your comment.

Everyone benefits from this.

Duolingo example


5. If you have the opportunity to make the application working without the Internet - better do it.


We were limited in time, so the simplest option seemed to be a completely online application. All user statistics are stored on the server, questions and even purchases. This solution accelerated the development time, but it was not without jambs when the server suddenly went to bed. The number of bad comments on this issue has increased, many have expressed displeasure on this score. Of the benefits is the constant synchronization with the server, which allowed us to quickly correct errors in questions, solve problems with coin purchases

Conclusion: The CIS market is huge, as is the competition in Google play. We did not conduct global research on what users want to see in such an application, what courses they would like to repeat, and perhaps it was worth doing in advance. Judging by such applications, the most simple content is popular. We tried to make our questions as simple and friendly as possible, but we still have to work on this. As for the monetization model, it became clear that for this type of application it does not suit us, so it was decided to make all courses completely free and open for use, and leave only advertising inside the application.
