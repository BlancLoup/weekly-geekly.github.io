First Introductory Session of the Association of Internet Developers (AID)

On February 1, 2010, the Association of Internet Developers (AID) will hold the first event to receive new members, during which market participants will be able to get acquainted with the goals and objectives of AIR, various aspects of its activities, and if desired, fill out an application for membership. The event will be held in the office of Microsoft. All players of the Internet development market are invited to participate.

The Internet development market in Russia is developing rapidly: the number of players and the complexity of the work performed are constantly growing, as well as the needs of customers. However, often the customer has no guarantee that the services offered to him are performed qualitatively and meet modern standards, but if the contractor is in the official professional community of all-Russian scale, the advantages of working with him are obvious.

The activities of the Association of Internet developers are aimed at increasing the attractiveness of developing Internet projects, both by the members of the association and by investing in Internet projects in general. For a customer, an AIR company is a company that seeks to maximize customer satisfaction, regularly improving the professional level of its employees by participating in AIR educational events. The Association already holds such events regularly, including in the regions. Thus, in the near future, the very fact of membership in the AIR will become for any customer a kind of ‚Äúquality mark‚Äù.

Members of the Association of Internet Developers will also have a number of attractive opportunities: from participating in tenders and partner programs, redistributing work within the AIP, and working together on open solutions, to being able to certify a company according to international standards.

The AIDA opens the reception of new members, potential participants need to confirm the high level of their company's activities, have an impeccable reputation and ‚Äúkeep up‚Äù with the development of modern Internet technologies.

The association has a lot of work to do. The meeting will talk about the Association itself, its plans and the work already done. All players of the Internet development market are invited to participate.

We invite all Internet developers to this meeting!
