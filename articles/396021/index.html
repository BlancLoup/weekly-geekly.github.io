The fact is that only one increase in communication tariffs, due to which operators will compensate for their costs, all this will not do. The services of the Post of Russia will rise in price, and the parcels may begin to be taken in the clear


Experts of the National Association of Distance Trade have calculated that the expenses of Russian companies delivering packages and letters may amount to 180 billion rubles.
