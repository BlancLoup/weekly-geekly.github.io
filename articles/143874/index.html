The way to the Syndicate is through Startup Weekend

Friends, we have great news for Startup Weekend participants in Moscow, which will be held on May 25-26 : Weekend finalist projects, selected by Arkady Moreynis, will be presented to the Investment Syndicate for consideration.

Startup Weekend is a 3-day work program from the Glavstart investment factory , which will allow Internet entrepreneurs to consult with experts, check in advance the viability of their project and get initial investments.

A syndicate is a group of people, professional seed investors and business angels who voluntarily agreed to consider Internet projects for joint investment in them. The syndicate considers projects at the seed and post-seed stages. The volume of syndicated investment of the Syndicate ranges from $ 100,000 to $ 1,000,000.

The best projects that have passed through Startup Weekend, Arkady Moreynis, as a member of the Syndicate, will recommend for co-investment to the other participants of the Syndicate.

The remaining investors of the Syndicate:




Objectives of the Syndicate participants:


  • diversify your risks by investing in more projects
  • give projects a new opportunity to receive syndicated post-sowing investments
  • use the competencies of the members of the Syndicate for qualified mentoring and project assistance.

How to become a member of Startup Weekend and submit your project to the Syndicate


To get to Startup Weekend with your project, send a request to idea@glavstart.ru
