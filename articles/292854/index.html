8 things that everyone should do before 8 am

We know that morning is the time when you are trying to open your eyes, make yourself rise from the bed. In general, it should look wrong. Do not let your laziness rule you. Start the morning tomorrow with another. We offer you.



1. Sleep well for 7+ hours


Yes, it sounds utopian, but it is really very important. It is also important how to drink a glass of water or eat something in the morning. Sleep can not be neglected and deplete your body too.

In the US, there is an organization responsible for sleep issues (it exists!) And it is called The National Sleep Foundation (NSF). So the researchers found that 40 million Americans suffer from 70 different types of sleep disorders. In general, about 60% of adults and 69% of children have trouble sleeping at least once a week.

Now we will try to convince you that you need to sleep and what the lack of sleep affects:
- Memory deteriorates,
- Your life is shortened,
- You become less creative,
- You can't concentrate,
- Resist the stress,
- Increases the risk of getting into any kind of unpleasant situations,
- You are prone to depression,
- Google to help you!

Do not read further this article and do not try to introduce something else into your life until you improve your relationship with sleep. Just stop pretending that it is not very important. It is important. HIGHLY.

2. To achieve harmony


After you slept, you need to set yourself up for a good day, positive. Everyone does it differently. You can, for example, meditate or ... pray.

Cleanse yourself and your head of all horrible thoughts, tune in to the good. Man is a magnet. It attracts good to itself, if it radiates itself.

3. Do sports


Exercise is the key to well-being. Man is a creature that requires movement, and nowadays we hardly get it. In the morning, go for a run, turn on your yoga lesson for 30 minutes and you will feel better.

Force yourself to move in the morning. Move!
