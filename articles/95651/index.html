Why you should choose to rent a server, and not to buy it

It is worth reading some forums, as you will surely come across similar questions. Say, I am going to launch an online store, tell me, how much money do you have to spend to buy your own server? And although answering a question with a question, to put it mildly, is not very justified, I still want to ask all these sufferers: why? And, really, gentlemen, why do you need your own server, and even at the start of this or that project? Have you considered the economic feasibility of such financial investments?

Maybe for a start to consider the option of renting a server? By the way, such a decision will save about 5-10 thousand American money (otherwise it will be necessary to exchange this amount for your own server!), Which can be invested in advertising your own goods and services.
Now, quite briefly, what is server rental. Server rental - computer rental (system unit) that is connected to the Internet around the clock. By the way, when connecting the port you choose. The minimum bandwidth is 100 mb / s.

You simply use the server, not forgetting to pay the rent, and the data center ensures its uninterrupted internet connection, provide additional services (albeit for a fee), software licenses, and so on.

By the way, DC NetNetirekt, the official representative of which is SarTelecom, offers its customers a server rental with instant activation. As part of this special offer, you can order server rental online with a ready-made configuration. Access to the server in this case, you will receive within 15-30 minutes from the date of payment. Geographically, the servers of the data center Netdirekt are located in Frankfurt in Germany. However, where exactly to order the server rental, of course, you choose, just let me give a little advice in the final of this short note. To get started, carefully review the proposals of several companies, evaluating them by a combination of parameters such as price / quality. We would put 90 against 10, which as a result you still use the services of SarTelecom. You can also choose the most attractive prices yourself, but we will give the most important argument in our favor. Technical support on all issues of iron and network operation is provided by SarTelecom around the clock, just as it installs servers.
