Identification in Yandex.Money through Alfa-Bank

A few days ago, Alfa-Bank made everyone happy that now it is possible to go through Yandex.Money identification via Alfa-Click.

Attention! The offer is available only until March 1 and for wallets linked to an account with Alfa-Bank until January 25, 2011.

An identified user has more opportunities to work in Yandex.Money than an anonymous one. After identification you can:
  • withdraw up to 300,000 rubles a month from each Yandex.Money account to a pegged account at Alfa-Bank;
  • Pay for Yandex.Money goods and services in the amount of more than 15,000 rubles;
  • use the Yandex.Money account to collect donations;
  • in case of loss of access to the account, it is easy and quick to restore it.

So, what to do:
  1. Log in to the Alfa-Click system. Go to messages , find a letter with the subject "Identification of users of the Yandex.Money system."
  2. Click "Reply" and enter a text like "Yes. I agree to the transfer of personal information. ‚Äù
  3. Within 5 business days, you will see a message on the Yandex.Money main page (or in the Internet.Wallet program) asking you to confirm your identification data. Follow the link from this message and carefully check the information received from Alfa-Bank.
  4. If everything is correct, click "Yes, this is the correct data." Shortly thereafter, the identification will be completed, and you will become an identified Yandex.Money user.
