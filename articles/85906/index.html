What else does the company produce? And there are few who can answer the question. In a nutshell, the French company Parrot is one of the most profitable, intensively developing companies. About functionality

Parrot Grande Specchio has a number of functional features that significantly distinguish it from the variety of such devices.

Bluetooth support

Everything is standard here: device name, enable / disable bluetooth module, visibility, advanced settings. To send photos from the phone, select the image and transmit. A new photo is immediately displayed on the frame screen.

The remarkable thing about the device is that Specchio is the first frame in the world that supports NFC technology. Quote: "Near Field Communication (NFC) - a combination of contactless identification and wireless communication of mobile devices, custom electronics, computers and other devices." What does this give in practice? If your phone supports this technology, then select the image to send, bring it to the hidden frame sensor, click Yes - that's it! Simply and technologically! NFC technology is supported by a small range of mobile phone models and is only gaining momentum.



Wi-Fi support

Parrot Grande Specchio has at its disposal a built-in Wi-Fi module that allows you to connect to any computer or laptop using a router and even access the Internet. If the router supports WPS technology, the connection process will take no more than a minute. Otherwise, manual configuration may be required. The test was used wi-fi router Yota. The setup process took no more than a minute, all you had to enter was the access key to the network. Connection is configured. Then we launch the browser, or use one of the pre-installed applications for working with the Internet.



The frame boasts a full-featured browser with support for Java-scripts, Macromedia Flash, bookmarks, history preservation, downloads and many settings.

Some difficulties are caused by typing. To do this, you must type the site address or the desired word using the virtual keyboard and trackball. Page size can be customized to fit the screen, or leave the original. These parameters are configured through the settings.

Summing up

Style, functionality, chic - the first words that come to mind when seeing this photo frame. In the off state, it is a golden mirror.
