ReactOS - No.2 in the plans of the Russian government for import substitution software

The Russian Federation, together with the BRICS countries, is exploring the possibility of abandoning strategically important foreign proprietary software and replacing it with free / open source software.



Since this initiative can be very useful for the further development of the project, Alexey Bragin decided to take part in it on behalf of the Reactos Foundation. Since October 2014, he has been a member of the working group on operating systems, organized by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media (Ministry of Communications and Mass Media), and actively participates in discussions within its framework.

In April, the Ministry of Communications developed the issue of supporting the development of independent technologies in various categories (client and mobile operating systems, server operating systems, database management systems, custom office software, cloud infrastructure and virtualization management tools). Reactos Foundation presented two projects: ReactOS user operating system and RosServer - ReactOS server based OS.

Many other great projects could not even fall into this and other categories, but the Reactos Foundation would like to help them by concluding as many cooperation agreements as possible, thus creating and strengthening links with them.
