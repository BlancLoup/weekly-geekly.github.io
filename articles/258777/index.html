Interesting modules

  2. Dynamic widget for commerce line item field
    In a good way , it is incorrect to store only references to existing products in the archives of orders from online stores. Product properties may change over time, so it‚Äôs best to keep all product data in the line item of an order. The module does just that.

  11. Safeword
