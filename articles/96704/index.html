Transformice: Lemmings with Real Physics Online

Transformice is actually the reincarnation of the famous lemmings online.
UPD: Now RU server!



Briefly about this wonderful browser game:
Everything, as in the good old lemmings, only this time in the main role of a mouse, one of which will be controlled by you. One of the mice is special - the shaman, it is he who is responsible for delivering the rest of the mice safely and securely to their holes. For this, the shaman has a whole set of items: from boards and boxes a la Crayon Physics Deluxe to exploding balls and "motivators."
A small difference from the lemmings - on the way to the hole it is necessary to tear off a piece of cheese. The one who made it earlier and managed to get the cheese into the hole will get more points.

The levels are very diverse, although there are not so many of them - around a dozen. In addition to solving puzzles (especially in the role of a shaman), it is also very entertaining to watch the collective mind, because each mouse is controlled by a real player. To coordinate actions there is a chat, and coordination itself is sometimes very, very important, for example, at a level where it is necessary to balance a huge board with a mouse mass.

Finally a couple of tips:
1) Create an account for chatting and saving achievements.
2) When playing the role of a shaman, select the desired item on the right side of the screen, place it near your alter-ego and hold LMB for a few seconds.
3) Do not be greedy, enough cheese for everyone!
4) To play with friends, you can create your own room: / room% roomname% .
5) Glasses are given to ordinary mice for cheese delivered (the first three are more than the others). Shamans are given points for each mouse that brought cheese. If there are two shamans and there are holes of only one color - each of them will receive for each mouse. If there are holes of different colors, the shaman will receive points only for the mouse that brought the cheese into his hole.
