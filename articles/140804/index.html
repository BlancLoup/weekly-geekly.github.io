Ciklum Mobile Saturday / iPhoneDevCamp in Kiev Odessa and Vinnitsa!

Only lazy is not interested in development for iOS now. And often the most interesting events are held in Kiev - and it is not always possible to visit them personally. Due to numerous requests, we again go to please the minds and minds, who are eager to find out the freshest and most useful in iPhone development, iPhoneDevCamp will be held on Saturday in Kiev , and we organize the broadcast of the sensational event for the residents of Odessa and Vinnitsa !

Celebrate March 31 in your calendar - and come to the iPhoneDevCamp broadcast in your city!

For you will perform:

Vladimir Kirillov with the report of Introspecting Mach.
Vladimir will continue the story of tinkering in the nodules of the Darwin operating system, begun at the previous DevCamp, and share his research on the Mach subsystem using the DTrace dynamic tracing framework.
Vladimir loves to explore various operating system models and their implementation. This report is one of the fruits of his research.

Vladimir Puzanov with the presentation of CF is fun.
Vladimir will talk about the interrelation of CoreFoundation and Foundation, will show some methods of debugging and introspection of the internal behavior of Cocoa classes. Fun GDB street magic, processor registers content analysis and good mood provided!
Who from mobile developers does not know Vladimir? He drank all the cocoa a long time ago and gnawed all the apples, so he loves hazelnuts in caramel. Works in Sikloum as an apple application architect.

Anton Pavlyuk explains why Why You'll Love a CFRunLoop: what input / timer sources are, how to use RunLoop observers for your own benefit, why you need RunLoop modes and what to do with them.
Anton loves apples since 2005 and has been nibbling them actively in Siklum for 1.5 years now.
