Against the background of the growing popularity of various kinds of gadgets and devices for geeks, the world famous brand decided to launch a very specific product on the market - watch phones (although it is worth noting that the device is not unique). The device received a fairly high price, but the build quality and reasonableness of management, in contrast to the much cheaper Chinese counterparts, partially justify the money that will have to pay for it. Analogs from famous brands simply do not exist. And I will try to paint the pros and cons of the miracle machine.





Standard equipment includes the watch itself, a bluetooth headset, a charger that allows you to simultaneously charge both, and, respectively, a software CD and a book. For a headset, there are 3 pairs of interchangeable ear hooks of different sizes. Leather strap with a secure buckle and conveniently adjustable length.
The charger is cradle, because There are no direct connectors on the watch itself. In any case, no one will charge the watch without removing it from the hand, therefore it is rather a convenience. Full charge takes a little less than 2 hours. After that, the clock confidently works 1-2 days depending on the intensity of use.



The headset is configured once, then instantly picked up when an incoming or outgoing call. The only thing that could have been done differently is to slightly bend the ‚Äúleg‚Äù of the microphone. The headset has a sufficiently high-quality microphone system - extraneous noises are well muffled, including wind noise.

The first and main screen is the clock. You can choose from several designs. It is possible to display the time in two time zones. Not to say that LG worked hard on elegance or originality. Hopefully, firmware updates will appear periodically with new design options.



Behind him comes the call screen: voice and video call, for which the GD910 is equipped with a VGA camera at the end and support for 3G networks. The second screen - settings "Contacts", "Messages", "Utilities", "Settings".



Contacts allocated 1000 memory cells. You can set groups, speed dials. The following fields are available for each contact: name, phone numbers, e-mail, birthday, anniversary, home address, company address, web address, melody, image and group.
