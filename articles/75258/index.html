Aeroflot. Transportation rules


I want to talk about how I recently found myself in a fairly normal situation for me faced a rather unusual position on the part of the Russian airline Aeroflot.

Having agreed on two vacation days, I received weekends from 4 to 8 November and decided to fly home to the Siberian city of Tyumen. Having a bad experience with Sky Express, this time I decided to use the largest airline in Russia, Aeroflot, imagining it to be a solid and reliable office.

I ordered round-trip tickets to St. Petersburg-Moscow-Tyumen for a modest 8 thousand rubles. For comparison, I can say that this is the same amount for which I could fly SkyExpress and a third cheaper than the flight airline S7.

As always, nothing foreshadowed trouble, the check-in took place on time and I went to the gym to wait for boarding. Twenty minutes before departure, landing did not begin.
