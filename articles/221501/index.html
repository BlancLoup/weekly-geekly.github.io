Free software for design - to authors of innovative and useful projects.



We are glad to share great news: Autodesk launches a program to support innovative startups in Russia. The authors of promising ideas will be able to access software products for design from Autodesk for free, that is, in vain.

The main conditions are as follows:

but. To participate in the program, you must have a project in the field of producing something (it can be practically anything that exists in physical space);
b. Your product should be beneficial - for example, to resist harmful environmental changes, solve any humanitarian problems, etc.
at. Apply for and get approval.
Design your product and tell the world what you got!

For example, Tesla Motors electric vehicle manufacturers, QBotix, the company that develops the latest building insolation systems, APWaters, whose achievements are related to water treatment and reuse processes, are partners in programs around the world. In Russia, we cooperate with young companies, leading developments in the field of bioprinting, producing low-cost prostheses for children, designing innovative building safety systems.

So, if you have a useful innovative project - write to us, and we will support you with free design software for up to $ 150,000. Program participants will be provided with a wide range of software, including the Product Design Suite Ultimate software package and Autodesk Simulation products for analyzing, calculating and modeling a product based on a digital prototype.

You can apply for participation in the program by writing a personal message, as well as on the Autodesk website here (note, in this case, further communication will be in English).

For residents of Skolkovo there is also a separate program. Program conditions are available here .

All success, inspiration and interesting projects!

Autodesk Team
