We have already written about our other sites more than once. Today we will tell more about the Frankfurt data center.

Telehouse Frankfurt is connected to Europe's second largest Internet Exchange Point, the DE-CIX , which provides premium services and is the world's leading interconnection platform, providing peak traffic at speeds of more than six terabits per second. Cooperation with several hundreds of international Internet providers makes it an ideal tool for collaboration of fast-growing transnational corporations, small and medium enterprises that need a reliable access point in the most secure place near the largest center of the European economy.
