Conference in Sofia

Yesterday the first international conference of administrators and registrars of national top-level domains of the CIS countries , Central and Eastern Europe began its work. About 100 people from 25 countries of the world, as well as representatives of various international organizations: ICANN, IANA, WIPO, CENTR, take part in it. List of participants.

During the conference, many issues will be addressed, in particular, the policy of resolving domain disputes, the problems of identifying users, modern threats and security on the Internet, the introduction of technologies in DNSSEC, IPv6 in the RU zone, etc. Particular attention is paid to the topic of accelerated receipt (special fast track fast track procedure) of Cyrillic top-level domains.

It is noteworthy that the representatives of Bulgaria expressed great interest in this issue. He is told about this in the message of the president of this country to the participants of the conference, and in the speeches of the officials of the Bulgarian administration who have already entered. Let's hope that joint efforts will bear fruit and will accelerate the emergence of Cyrillic top-level domains.
