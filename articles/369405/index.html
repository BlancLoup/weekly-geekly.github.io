Pure.Charger - safe charging your mobile devices



At least once in our life, each of us had to ‚Äúsave ourselves‚Äù by charging our mobile device in public places using charging stations or using someone else's computer. Charging the device in this way, is it possible to be confident in the safety of your data and that they will remain only with you, or rather with your mobile. In addition, there is a risk that such public charging stations may collect information from devices and, at the same time, install malware on them.



Kaspersky Lab engineers, as part of the Future Crew Team, presented the Pure.Charger device. Pure.Charger - a special device, similar to a USB flash drive, is an adapter and allows you to protect personal data while charging mobile devices. Pure.Charger, automatically blocking the transfer of data, transmits only an electric charge when a ‚Äúneeding‚Äù recharging object is connected to a power source.



Pure.Charger:



The developers released three versions of Pure.Charger,

Pure.Charger - basic version, Pure.Charger Pro - with built-in flash-memory on 16 GB, Pure.Charger Executive - with built-in flash-memory on 32 GB.



In addition, engineers have developed a special mobile application. The device remembers data on the USB port to which the smartphone is connected, and via Bluetooth transfers them to the mobile application.



Using this application, you can view the history of where and when you charged your mobile device, and also find out which charging stations tried to access data and information.
