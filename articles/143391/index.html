Domestic Qt-projects

As you know, with the use of the Qt framework, many programs have been written, and there are more and more domestic ones among them lately. So, what was written in Russia using this framework?

Kaspersky Internet Security
Kaspersky Internet Security 2012 is a solution for ensuring an optimal level of security. Innovative hybrid protection instantly eliminates malware, spam and other Internet threats, saving computer resources through a combination of cloud and antivirus technologies.
In the program folder I found the following modules: qtcore , qtdeclarative , qtgui , qtnetwork , qtscript , qtsql , qtwebkit .

2GIS for iOS and Android
2GIS is a free electronic directory of organizations with a city map. 11,550,000 PC users in more than 150 cities of Russia and Ukraine regularly use 2GIS for personal purposes and for solving business problems.

qutIM (by EuroElessar , Gorthauer87 , torkve , Nico_izo )
qutIM is a free multiprotocol (ICQ, Jabber, Mail.Ru, IRC, VKontakte) instant messaging client for Windows, Linux, MacOS X, OS / 2, Symbian, Haiku, Solaris, Maemo / MeeGo and * BSD.

Rambler-Contacts (developers: silvansky , Lion34 ) (added bagzilla )
Communicator Rambler-Contacts saves your time and makes communication on the Internet easier and more convenient. No matter which social network or messenger your friends use, you can always keep in touch with them using one application.

Ya.Online (added by silvansky )
Ya.Online is a free program for those who want to be always in touch.
Communicate with colleagues, friends and acquaintances on the Internet, instantly learn about new letters with the help of Ya.Online.

Vacuum-IM (by Lion34 ) (added bagzilla )
Vacuum IM is a free cross-platform Jabber client written in Qt4. The principal difference from other cross-platform clients lies in the open modular architecture that allows you to flexibly customize the functionality for specific needs, as well as use the capabilities of existing modules when developing your own.

Simple instant messenger
Simple Instant Messenger (SIM) is an open source multi-protocol messaging client that runs on platforms supported by the Qt library it uses, for example, Microsoft Windows, Linux, FreeBSD, Mac OS X, and OS / 2. [from wikipedia ]

Juffed (by Mezomish )
Juffed is a free, cross-platform text editor for programmers and advanced users. The highlighting of many programming languages, the addition of code, the search and replacement of regular expressions, the support of many encodings is a list of the standard features of this Qt program. [ link ]

LightLang (by Liksys )
LightLang is a modern system of electronic dictionaries for Linux, combining convenience and ease of use. Having collected all the best, eliminating flaws and adding a lot of our own ideas, we created the most perfect program of this kind. Its design and architecture are fundamentally different from all analogues, the interfaces are clear and convenient, and the search capabilities exceed all expectations. Using LightLang is convenient and pleasant, whether you are learning a language, working as a professional translator, or you just need a program that helps you understand foreign texts - it will suit everyone. LeechCraft (added by @FakeFactFelis)
LeechCraft is a free, open source, cross-platform, modular Internet client. LeechCraft allows you to browse the web, chat on instant messaging, read news feeds, download files via BitTorrent, Direct Connect, FTP, HTTP, automatically download and play podcasts (and other media files) or listen to them directly from the Internet, as well as much more.
