Little bit about Last.fm

Last.fm is a musical social network created in 2003.

The service provides visitors with an opportunity not only to listen to various compositions, but also to share their musical preferences with other users and discover new music for themselves. Thousands of personal radio stations are available on the site, a large amount of information about more than 7 million different artists and groups, as well as at least 65 million songs of various genres and styles. The free Last.fm program, which, having registered, can be downloaded directly on the site, helps to create a music profile for each individual user and select interesting songs, concerts, as well as people with similar musical tastes.

Last.fm rewards:
- BT Digital Music Award 2006 - the best music community
- Winner of Seomoz Web 2.0 Music Award 2006
- Social Networking Awards 2006 - the best musical social network.
