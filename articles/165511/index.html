What is the UEC and what it eat

What it is?
A universal electronic card is a card that contains information about a user in a visual and electronic form that provides access to this information to confirm the user's rights to receive state, municipal and other services. In cases provided for by federal laws, a universal electronic card can be a document proving the identity of a citizen.
UEC will replace the social map of Muscovite. The main difference is that the universal card is issued to all citizens, and it will be possible to use it in any region of Russia. During the transition period, the Muscovite‚Äôs social card services will be provided in the same amount.

Continued - under the cut.

For what?
With the help of a universal electronic card, you can receive and pay for state, municipal and commercial services in electronic form at any place convenient for you and at any time without queues. A universal electronic card can be used when personally contacting government and other organizations to speed up service to citizens.

What does it include?
The universal electronic card combines the functions of mandatory medical and state pension insurance policies, travel documents for transport, and a bank card.
It is designed to simplify bureaucratic procedures, improve the quality of public services and raise citizens' awareness of their rights.
For children there is a preferential fare for public transport, subsidies for food in children's educational institutions and a number of other services that can be obtained with the help of UEC.

What gives?
In state structures
You can use a universal electronic card for submitting applications, registering documents, obtaining references, statements in state and municipal structures. The need to collect additional references and documents will disappear. Many services can be obtained remotely. Using the banking application, you can use the UEC to pay for services, taxes, government fees and make other necessary payments.

In transport
Universal electronic card - a universal ticket for all types of transport. Rights to free or reduced fare will be confirmed by a card.

Payment for commercial services
Using a universal electronic card, you can pay for commercial services (including utility bills, tickets, communications, in-store purchases) as with a regular bank card.

Online payment *
It can be used on the Internet, ATM, bank payment terminal, public transport to pay for travel, in fast-service networks, in state, municipal and commercial access control systems, as well as in a regular store to pay for purchases

In the clinic
In order to avoid the queue at the registry, it is possible to make an appointment with a doctor through an infomat located in a medical institution. The possibility of remote recording to a doctor through self-service terminals (ATMs, info kiosks, infomats) is planned throughout Moscow.

At school
The main purpose of using a universal electronic card in the field of education is to ensure the safety of students, the elimination of cash payments on school grounds, the organization of payment of preferential food on the fact of using the card, informing parents about attendance and progress of students.
Universal electronic card can be used as a pass to the school. In this case, parents will be able to control the time of arrival of the child to school and the time of the end of classes.
With the help of UEC, free and preferential meals will be provided, it will be possible to pay for the child‚Äôs meals at school and to check whether the child received it.

* in the presence of the reader (card reader). As I understand it, it is purchased separately and while it is not in free circulation.

The practical result of how to get UEC

In order to get the UEC, you need to have with you:
1) Passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation
2) SNILS (insurance number of an individual personal account)
3) Come up with a code word in advance (you can use Russian), as well as remember your e-mail and phone number (you can be mobile).
4) If you live in Moscow, then drive up to the branch of Sberbank at the address Greater Georgian, 37 and apply for UEC. In this case, if you believe the official site, then you can apply in other places, namely:

a) To issue a universal electronic card for newborns - parents or legal representatives, you must contact the district registry office. Address to find the point of receipt of applications for receipt of UEC. Search for places of application for UEC on the website of the Government of Moscow
b) Privileged categories of citizens (pensioners, disabled people, persons receiving social support, etc.) - to the district department of social protection of the population. Address to find the point of receipt of applications for receipt of UEC. Search for places of application for UEC on the website of the Government of Moscow . You need to choose the category of citizens "beneficiaries"
