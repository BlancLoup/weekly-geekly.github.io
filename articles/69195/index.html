Apple introduced new iPods



Today Apple introduced iPod updates.



iPod Touch:

  • There was a model with a memory capacity of 64 GB
  • Added support for OpenGL ES 2.0 (only in new models with a capacity of 32 and 64 GB)
  • Got faster
  • I got a new firmware version 3.1 ( what's new? )
  • Positioned as a gaming platform, for which the Sony PSP and Nindendo DS have already been left far behind in the number of games, continues to be supported by the monsters of the gaming industry (Gameloft, EA, etc.)


iPod Classic:

  • New model with a memory capacity of 160 GB


iPod Shuffle:

  • Now there are models of 5 different colors.
  • There is a special metal model


iPod nano is in this model most innovations:

  • The new models have added a camera and a microphone with the ability to shoot video (video resolution is 640x480, photo can not shoot)
  • Captured video can be synchronized with your PC or Mac, viewed directly on your iPod or exported to YouTube
  • For the first time in the history of the iPod, a radio was added to it, as well as the Live Pause feature.
  • Added pedometer
  • Added voice recorder
  • The screen has become larger - 2.2 inches
  • Aluminum housing now


Thus, the long-running rumors that Apple is going to equip their iPods with cameras were confirmed, but only partially, only iPod nano was equipped with a camera. But the fact that the iPod Touch will remain without a camera, few people have expected.
