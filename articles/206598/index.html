What is better for the student of the training center: online profile or certificate?

What does the listener dream about when he comes to Java, testing or IT marketing courses? Of course, to gain knowledge and skills, apply them at the current place of work or successfully get a new place. Will the certificate obtained at the exit, reliable evidence of the full development of the material? Is the future employer sufficient name of the course, training center and the date of training?

Our answer: ‚ÄúNO! Paper sax! We need to break stereotypes. ‚Äù
For a year now, at the Uransoft training center, instead of the traditional certificates and certificates of attendance, we have launched online profiles for students. How we came to this and what results were obtained - read under the cut.

The last straw that prompted us to write this article was the resume that the applicant sent us. It consisted of scans of various diplomas (about 20) collected in a ppt-presentation.

Assessing the ‚Äúbeauty and informational content‚Äù of the solution, we understood: we are on the right path with the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãonline profiles.

‚ÄúPerfect‚Äù profile and requirements for it


From the title it is clear that this is some information about the student of the course. Let's imagine the perfect online profile. To do this, take into account the views of three parties: the training center, the listener and the future employer.

Training Center Requirements

The profile of the listener is a great opportunity to show your product face. In this case, the goods can be understood as a training course, as well as the listener, who at the end of the course acquired certain skills and knowledge. What does the training center want to tell? Of course, first of all - about your educational content:

  • Detailed information about the course: program, lecture topics and their theses, video with the teacher and course;
  • The most interesting: information about practical classes - what task was put before the listener and how he performed it, assessments, attendance;

And also about the listener:

  • Brief information - who he is, what he specializes in, why he went to courses and what plans he has for the future (for example, he wants to do an internship in an IT company);
  • General feedback from the teacher about the student - assessment of interest in the topic / subject, work in a group and at seminars, homework assignments and final works, communication skills, personal qualities, and so on;
  • Links to the listener's resume and his contacts in social networks;
  • Video of the listener himself, where he talks about why he came to the course, whether his expectations were met, what was good and what needs to be worked on.


HR / employer requirements

The profile will solve the main task here - without inviting the applicant for an interview, it will allow you to get an idea about the future employee. Therefore:

  • The profile must be available, open and distorted through search engines;
  • Information must be authentic (authentic and reliable);
  • Must be the identification of the applicant (uniquely identify - name, photo, age, education);
  • Correct information on the course that the applicant has listened to must be given (since the courses are constantly being improved and changed, the information is relevant at the time of listening to the course);
  • The ability to check the information provided by the listener (for this, there are public contacts of the teacher);
  • ‚ÄúVideo business card‚Äù - an employer will immediately be able to evaluate how a potential applicant expresses his thoughts.


Requirements of the listener as a potential applicant:

In addition to all of the above, the course listener needs the ability to:
  • provide informative and objective evidence of the fact of training (not only in its presentation, but in the opinion of an independent competent party);
  • demonstrate the desire for professional development, loyalty to the profession (spent money and time).
  • self-promotion and additional information about yourself.


What we designed


After analyzing all the requirements, we formulated the main idea.
So, the online profile of the listener should be a page on the site of our training center Uransoft. It should be:

  • personal information about the listener: his photo, full name, experience, IT skills and knowledge, links to personal profiles in social networks, links to sites with a summary)
  • information about the course / courses with teacher feedback (detailed program and content, theory and practical tasks of the course, feedback from the teacher about the listener himself, as well as about his work and tasks performed during the course)
  • feedback from the listener about the organization of courses in general, separately - about the course heard and feedback about the teacher.

Also, the online profile should carry, in addition to confirming the knowledge of the listener, the function of direct contact of the employer with the teacher or the course founders. Naturally, the listener can place a link to his profile in his resume on any headhunting site.

That video is well indexed in search engines and appears in the issuance of thematic and personalized requests.
