Intro


Today we would like to tell you about an ambitious project. Namely, about the calendar for ITshnikov and all who just like it. Why such an idea arose, what motivated us and what is the result, will be discussed in this article.


When programming various applications for different platforms, the thought often arises: Did my program help someone? Or maybe nobody needs it? After all, when you do something that someone needs ... that solves someone's problems, and why the person using the program has stopped lingering at work, because some process has become automated ... it always gives the development much more pleasure than when no one is trying to use your application. There are many reasons to enjoy software development.



Take an example: let you write software for a large bank. In addition to your program, this bank has about 350 projects, each of which can be divided into 5-10 subprojects. And each of these subprojects due to the use of other projects from other development teams (some of which are located in India, and some in England, for example) is written, to put it mildly, distorted. Well, your program is not an exception to this rule. And let your program copy files from point A to point B. Bored, you say, a little motivation? But in fact, this program not only automates a process, it acts as a very powerful helper to the support team. After all, the human factor is now excluded, because of which the wrong versions of files in the wrong folders were constantly laid out. You helped an uncountable number of employees who were raised at night due to an incident caused by another sleepy employee who manually using the remote desktop through the SSH tunnel in some kind of internal security protocol, logged in tokens, copied this file and made a mistake in its name on symbol.

Take a different situation ... They came up to you and asked you to write a contact manager for the Android OS. Is it boring Not at all! Unlike games and other applications, the contact manager is an application that will never be deleted, and which will live while the phone itself is alive. Consider how responsibly you need to approach the development of this application. It should be comfortable and thoughtful.




We decided to make this calendar so that people can feel that IT is not just something new in our life. IT is firmly established in our lives. IT is everywhere, from your pockets, where plastic cards work from Java, and phones are more like laptops than just communications. And the downloads of space stations and air defense equipment. Music, art, planning, finance ... Yes, almost everything is now controlled by computer systems. While you are currently reading this article, there are so many computers in the world that their computing power is enough for the Institute of Nuclear Physics to calculate the physical processes that occur when an atomic bomb explodes.

They are at a great distance from each other and live in different countries. When dawn breaks out with one interlocutor behind the window, the other has night and lights come on ... These two have completely different faith, different values, different language ... But using their laptops, they are together. They can communicate on those topics that they are interested in. They can be inspired by each other's work, the results of each other ... They can fall in love with each other, and dream of being together. These are truly amazing features that computer technology gives us. Have you ever lay on the couch, remembering the last conversation with your loved one?
