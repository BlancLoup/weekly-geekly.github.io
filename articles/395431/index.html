June free webinars from MediaTek Labs

June 21 and 23, 2016, MediaTek Labs invites everyone to traditional free webinars that will be of interest to those for whom the Internet of Things is not an empty sound or a fashionable term with a vague meaning, but a job or a hobby!






On June 21, 2016, a webinar will take place on getting started with the LinkIt 7687 HDK breadboard and developing an effective and secure connection for Internet of Things devices using Wi-Fi.


The new MediaTek LinkIt breadboard for real-time operating system (RTOS) is based on the ARM Cortex-M4 processor and provides excellent opportunities for developing various network devices and smart devices, home and office automation elements, control modules for the Internet of things.

The MediaTek LinkIt 7687 HDK development board (hardware development tools) was originally developed by Silicon Application Corp. Group (SAC) for supporting the LinkIt platform for RTOS and based on MediaTek's reference design. This breadboard with Wi-Fi module can be used to quickly connect devices in a smart home or office.

During a 30-minute webinar, MediaTek Labs technical consultant Philip Handshin will show you how to get started with LinkIt 7687 HDK, which allows you to develop a quick prototype of connecting devices for the Internet of things using a secure Wi-Fi connection. Then Philip will answer the questions of those present at the webinar.
