Now Bitcoin in Ukraine can be bought for cash at any of the 4000 terminals

A group of enthusiasts from Ukraine has made a real breakthrough in integrating Bitcoin with the usual payment systems. The Btcu.biz service allows you to buy crypto coins for cash through any of the huge variety of terminals located throughout Ukraine ( location map ). Terminals operating with Btcu are owned by National Credit Bank. In large cities, you can find them on almost every corner, which significantly reduces the time from the moment you decide to buy a cryptocurrency to the moment it appeared on your wallet.

At the time of publication of this post, 1 Bitcoin will have to be paid into the terminal at 8327 hryvnia, which at the rate of PrivatBank is $ 693. The price of Bitcoin on exchanges according to Coinmarketcap is $ 648. $ 45 - is it a big difference? Somehow I helped to buy 2 Bitcoin-coins to a friend who had cash for this purpose. So, Privat24 replenishment, then transfer to PerfectMoney, Btc-e replenishment (exchange commission for deposit and withdrawal + hold of funds for 72 hours), then forced us to spend on converting and commissions no less than the above. It is a pity that then we did not know about Btcu.

But to buy coins through the terminals you need to do the following:

  1. Find a replenishment terminal nearest you.
  2. Choose replenishment service btcu.biz.
  3. Enter the amount of 50 hryvnia (terminal commission of 1%, but at least 2 UAH).
  4. If you want to receive an activation code on the phone number, enter your mobile.
  5. Get and save a receipt with an activation code.
  6. Enter the activation code on the website of the btcu.biz service, specify the required address in the bitcoin system. Coins will be sent to you automatically.

The main advantages that you get working with btcu.biz:

  • Anonymity. Making transactions with btcu.biz, you should not disclose your personal data, all operations within the system are anonymous.
  • Efficiency. After payment in one of the terminals, which will surely be found nearby from your home or office and entering the received code on the website, Bitcoins will be automatically sent to you.
  • High security.
  • Work in compliance with the laws of Ukraine.
  • Guarantee the safety of users.
  • The interest of the company in the development of the field of cryptocurrency in Ukraine.
