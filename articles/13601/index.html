Table lamps mopeds


The design of the Vespa mopeds has long been recognized as classic, and the mopeds themselves (both modern and vintage) are very popular all over the world.
What do those who like the appearance of Vespa mopeds do, but there is no desire or opportunity to purchase a two-wheeled vehicle itself?
The company Lamponi's Lamp answered this question by releasing a collection of unique table lamps, made by hand and repeating the look of the front of a moped.
Handmade and beautiful design has always been worth the money. That is why the prices for masterpieces of Lamponi's Lamp start at $ 1,772.
