Two-day conference-workshop on design and usability - D-Camp

Hello, dear readers Habr :)
We are happy to present you an event that will be held September 24-25 in Ukraine, in the city of Zaporozhye - D-Camp .



This is a practical workshop conference that focuses on wise interface design for any sites and any programs - for desktop computers, mobile, tablet and web applications.


The event itself will take place right on the bank of the Dnieper , in the recreation center ‚ÄúJazz‚Äù, which will provide us with comfortable accommodation for all participants and the entire infrastructure. Around - a very pleasant atmosphere, a huge area (7 hectares) with cozy gazebos and a huge lake with swans right in the middle of the park in front of the hotel.


How much is?


Until September 1, the cost of participation - 800 hryvnia ($ 100).
The price includes food, accommodation and transportation from the station to the conference venue.



Who is this camp for?


For designers, practitioners who seek to improve their knowledge in the field of interface design and information architecture.
As well as sympathizers with the designers themselves and this topic :)


What is the format of the event?


The participants will be engaged in practical work on interface sketches for applications under the guidance of usability experts.
All conference participants will be divided into groups (maximum 5 people). Each group is a separate combat unit that will compete for very weighty and enjoyable prizes.

There will also be several introductory theoretical reports from experts in the field of interface design that will help to make high-quality sketches.

Every evening there will be small parties with drinks and games :)

Sign up right now , the number of participants is very limited.

Looking forward to seeing you at D-Camp.
