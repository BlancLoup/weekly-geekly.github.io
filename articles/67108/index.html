Create and publish your presentation.

Hello.
I decided to share with community members who do not know or simply did not notice the useful feature of Google Docs.

It turns out that you can create full-fledged presentations in Google Docs :) And also, open them for collective use and viewing, and also integrate them into the pages of your blog or site.

This is done simply:

1. Go to docs.google.com and create File -> New-> Presentation
2. You do the same thing as in Power point.
3. The most interesting thing is that you can open access to your presentation to everyone or take a piece of code (like from YouTube) for integration into the pages of your website / blog. To do this, you need the Share button (located in the upper right corner). Then I think everything is clear. If not, ask - I will help.

Here is my example docs.google.com/present/view?id=0AWd6yQ6LVGK2ZGNzY2QzM2JfMTBmdzZkdnJmcg&hl=en
