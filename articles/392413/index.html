HyperX Introduces Cloud Revolver Gaming Headset

Hi, Giktayms! HyperX is Cloud Revolver - the latest device in the famous line of Cloud headsets. Pre-order for a new product can be issued in the 4Frag store from April 1 to May 9, 2016. Anyone who preorders during this period will receive a gift from Kingston. In the free sale of the headset will go from 10 May. Under the cut more details about the new product.



The luxurious HyperX Cloud Revolver gaming headset has the widest audio range and provides the most accurate sound reproduction. Thanks to the 50 mm directional speakers, the headset creates a studio-class sound scene with depth and volume that allows you to more accurately determine the sources of sound in first-person shooters and games with large open cards. Speaker settings, front speaker design, enlarged cups with air holes allow you to hear the enemy from afar and gain an important advantage in battle. Music lovers will also appreciate the sound quality close to the acoustics of the concert hall.



The HyperX Cloud Revolver headband and ear cushions use branded polyurethane foam with memory effect and premium artificial leather: they will give players comfort and convenience during long gaming sessions. The steel frame in the headband gives the design strength and reliability. The HyperX Cloud Revolver noise canceling microphone provides excellent voice quality and reduces background noise during a call. The device supports work with various platforms (PCs, game consoles and mobile devices), is compatible with Skype, Ventrilo, Mumble and RaidCall, as well as certified by TeamSpeak and Discord services. HyperX Cloud Revolver comes with a two-meter extension cable with a sound control unit and plugs for connecting to the audio output and microphone jack.



Visitors to the MLG CS: GO Championship in Columbus, Ohio, who will be held April 1-3, will be the first to try out and purchase the HyperX Cloud Revolver. HyperX is the official sponsor of the event with a prize fund of $ 1 million: the brand provides branded headsets, RAM modules and SSD drives.

Features and benefits of HyperX Cloud Revolver:


  • Studio grade sound stage: in HyperX Cloud Revolver, a wide audio range that gives the sound a sense of volume and depth, as well as gives users an advantage in games.
