Hood.e - a new way to listen to music on the go



This concept from Tim Dubovitsky (Tim Dubitsky) called Hood.e. It looks like this device can be an excellent replacement for headphones. In any case, this is something radically new.

Hood.e is a device built right into the hood. On the right and on the left side there is something like a mini-speaker, from which the music is played. Hood.e may be necessary if, for example, it is dangerous to walk in the headphones in the street (there are no sounds around).

As reported on the official website, which by the way is very unusual, this gadget will be compatible with any MP3 players. This is still only a concept, but certainly worthy of being translated into reality.
