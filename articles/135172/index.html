Forismatic 3.0 - quotes on iPad and iPhone



Everyone has a favorite app. It should not just like more than others, but be in the forefront among the most used applications on your device. It should inspire.

Our favorite application is Forismatic . The first version came out a couple of years ago and within 2 weeks it took the honorable first place among free apps in the AppStore Russia. Then, in December 2009, I helped the post in "I am promoting" . Now this review and screenshots of version 1.0 cause a slight smile. Since then, the number of fans has been growing every month. Now let's talk about the present.

Today we released Forismatic 3.0. This version contains everything you asked for:
  1. iPad application - Forismatic for iPhone has become a universal application and now fully works on both devices.
  2. More quotes - for a nominal fee, you can order the entire collection of quotes that we collected during 2011. The collected funds will be spent on the development of the project and your inspiration.
  3. Synchronization of bookmarks - we keep up with the times and have implemented iCloud technology. Now your favorite quotes are always relevant on all your devices.
  4. Of course, we remember your friends and improved integration with VKontakte, Facebook and Twitter. For business people - LinkedIn, and for collectors - Evernote.
  5. Moreover, we remember our old friends and try to keep support for most of the functions for older versions of iOS 3.x.

IOS client usage statistics:
  • About 3,000,000 quotes are read each month.
  • the application runs almost 200,000 times a month
  • average reading time about 14 minutes
  • 95% are old friends, 5% are new
  • Constant audience approaches 70.000 users
  • Demography: 75% - Russia, 9% - Ukraine. Further the USA, other Russian-speaking countries.

Public API usage statistics:
  • Approximately 2,500.000 statements are read every day
  • that's about 30 utterances per second
  • or about 50 sayings per second by day, since 80% read by day

Looking forward to your feedback and advice.

We also actively run the page on VKontakte , Facebook and Twitter .
