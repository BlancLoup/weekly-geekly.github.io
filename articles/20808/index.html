Yahoo! Go 3.0beta



Yahoo! Mobile offers a single solution that allows you to enjoy the best of the Internet on your mobile phone: send mail, upload photos, look at the map, search for answers to questions, check prices on the stock exchange, or get the latest news - all this and much more in Yahoo! Go.

The new widget also according to Yahoo! Go will help make life easier by combining all the possibilities into one. It offers a selection of widgets of various currently popular brands that can be added and removed at any time, which allows the use of mobile as needed.

Yahoo! Go 3.0 is in beta version, to see this thing, go to the link and get a link to your mobile.
