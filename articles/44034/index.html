Chinese fake Nokia N95


Yesterday, 2 of my acquaintances immediately acquired a Chinese fake on the N95 from their hands. One of them bought it for 6000 rubles, the other for 2000. This phone has the following characteristics different from the original:
- Memory 1 Gb (in original 8Gb)
- No GPS (in the original there is)
- No Wi-Fi (in the original there is)
- Camera 0.3 megapixels (in the original 5 megapixels with CZ optics)
- There is no possibility to install additional programs (there is an original one)
- No light sensor (in the original there is)
- No flash (original is)
The list can be continued indefinitely ... This phone is not stitched, not compatible with Nokia accessories. The easiest way to distinguish a fake from the original is in the quality of the camera.

Beware of fakes.
