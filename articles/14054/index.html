Nissan will release cars specifically for geeks

Just imagine - modern teenagers do not like cars! They consider them a foul-smelling and noisy anachronism, which came from the past century, which pollutes the environment and is a completely useless thing. What do they like? Computers, games and the Internet. And this is not a joke - the researchers from Nissan, who interviewed modern youth in the USA, Europe and Japan, came to such results.

Therefore, the car turned out as if it had just come off the computer screen - aggressive, high-tech and ... social.

There are three seats - one in front in the center and two in the back. This interior architecture was not chosen by chance. This is also a "curtsy" in the direction of computer games. The interior itself, like the design of the car as a whole, is made in the style of fantastic cartoons popular in Japan - with many sharp edges and unusual materials of decoration.
