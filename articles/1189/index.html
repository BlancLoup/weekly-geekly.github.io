Google AdSense "come out" on the streets

Google has submitted to the US Patent Office (USPTO) an application for patenting the technology of advertising on street billboards connected to the Internet. A new type of advertising will be available through the Google AdSense service.

The system will allow stores to manage ads depending on the availability of goods on sale. Communication with the warehouse database will allow to timely exclude goods from the set of ads as they are fully sold and, conversely, to include as they are received.

In my opinion just a great idea! I would like to see this.
