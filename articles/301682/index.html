The Nutanix OS Xpress edition is a special edition of the software, which, on the one hand, includes some features from the "top" versions, on the other hand, features that are irrelevant for small companies and small clusters where Xpress / SX is aimed are thrown out.
The restriction of 4 nodes per cluster and a maximum of 2 clusters in the company I have already mentioned.
This also implies some additional limitations.
  • Only 1-to-1 DR replication (since Xpress is only two clusters, it is no longer necessary).
  • ‚ÄúHeterogeneous‚Äù clusters are not supported, that is, from different models (because in Xpress there is only one model - SX-1065, and adding NX is not supported).

  • No Availability Domain. Again, this is more of an enterprise feature for multi-mode clusters.
  • There is no support for drives with built-in encryption (-S models from Nutanix, not supplied to Russia) and AllFlash.
  • There is no support for Prism Pro, our ‚Äúadvanced‚Äù Prism, with advanced analytics, recommendations on the dynamics of storage volumes, customizable dashboards and management of multi-cluster infrastructures, this is for enterprise. The familiar Prism built into CVM and now called Prism Elemental has remained as before.
