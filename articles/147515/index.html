CMS among the winners of the contest "Rating Runet-2011"

At the end of June, the Runet Rating portal announced the entire list, congratulated and awarded the winners of the contest of sites-2011, which started in January. All this time, first users, and then experts voted for the work they liked. At the awards ceremony, the organizers voiced the top three in each of the 27 nominations. The final list can be viewed at the link if one of you missed this event: http://www.ratingruneta.ru/awards/

But I want to tell you not quite about it. The other day, for the sake of interest, my colleagues and I considered what engines were in demand among the top three winners of the 2011 site contest. The results and observations that we made after today I submit to your judgment.
