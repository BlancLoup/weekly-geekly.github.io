Published professional standards of the Russian Federation for programmers, database administrators and other professions

In the framework of the federal program initiated by the decree of the President of the Russian Federation No. 597 of May 7, 2012, the development of new professional standards in the field of IT is underway. The other day drafts of standards for the database administrator, programmer, information technology project manager, software development manager, etc. were published .

September 11-13, 2013, open webinars will be held with a detailed discussion of the first version of each of the developed professional standards.
