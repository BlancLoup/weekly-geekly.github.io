HTC Vive Virtual Reality Helmet Review

Hello!



We have already written our review on NVR's domestic virtual reality glasses in our blog . This time we decided to publish a review on the virtual reality helmet HTC Vive, prepared jointly with GeekToTheFuture. HTC Vive is a joint product of HTC and Valve, making it one of the most progressively developing products on the VR market.



Specifications:

Screen resolution: 2 displays with 1200x1080 pixels each
Matrix update rate: 90 Hz (frames per second)
Interfaces: 3.5 mini jack, USB 3.0, USB 2.0, HDMI and a power jack
Sensors: gyroscope, accelerometer and laser positioning for head rotation. More than 70 sensors in total
