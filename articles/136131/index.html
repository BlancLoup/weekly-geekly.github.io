Therefore, I do not know to which section it is better to refer this topic - to tablets, to Internet commerce or to humor, citizens are moderators, if necessary, please move to the appropriate place.

What came of it: read below.

It all started just fine :)
When there were 10 minutes left until the end of the action, and there were only 3 devices available, I frantically checked the availability of money on the card for Chinese purchases, quickly registered on this site, found a coupon that allows you to pay another $ 10 and in disbelief of my luck I successfully paid for the purchase Paypal.
