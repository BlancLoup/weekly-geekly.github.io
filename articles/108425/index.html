Online sandbox with tabs and files

To publish examples and tasks in the English version of javascript.ru, a sandbox was needed a little more powerful than jsfiddle / jsbin.

I wrote and posted a beta on javascript.info/play/rus (the site itself is still half closed there).

Additional features include:
  • Files
  • Tabs
  • Hotkeys (for now except Opera)
  • Export to zip
  • Saving with the ability to scroll through previous revisions
It was conceived for a simple purpose - the publication of examples, tasks, solutions in a convenient form for viewing online and editing. I would be glad if amiss on something else.
