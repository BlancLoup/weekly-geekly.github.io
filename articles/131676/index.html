Results Opera Startup Awards



So, the Opera Startup Awards competition that lasted for more than six months was successfully completed. Let me remind you that one of the main tasks of the competition was to help young talented developers to create real projects with good commercial prospects based on Opera Software technologies. As a result, Opera Startup Awards fully met expectations: the teams showed good results, some projects launched their services into real work, while one of the participating teams was even able to make a small profit from the work of their service in test mode before the final.
