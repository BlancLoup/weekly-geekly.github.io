KeepMeOut: Saves time spent on the Internet

Spending too much time on the Internet can negatively affect your daily life, your studies, and your waiting job. If you are addicted to any website like Facebook or Twitter, etc., then this will lead to a loss of focus on your studies, your goals or work. If you are looking for a way to reduce the time spent on various websites, such as Facebook, Twitter or any other website, we have found excellent service KeepMeOut , which will help to overcome this problem.

Foreword


KeepMeOut is an excellent online service that prevents a user from visiting a particular website according to a specific time interval. Using KeepMeOut , you can specify the website that you want to limit and bookmark it. Using the bookmark, it will prevent you from visiting the specified website too often for a given time. If you visit the website too often you will receive a warning.

Using


KeepMeOut is really easy and convenient to use. All you have to do is select a website, determine the time (after which you will receive a warning), and click the Submit button.



You can click Advanced Settings to further customize preferences: certain days and time in order to impose restrictions. Set a warning for each day or weekday and determine the time period in which restrictions will be imposed.



After you click Submit, you will be redirected to the page where you will be given a link to add to your bookmarks. You must save the link in the browser to save your time.



Now, open Facebook or any other website specified in KeepMeOut and it will show you a warning that you have already visited the site during the specified period of time. It will also show you the time after which you can re-open the website along with the time, general statistics of average daily visits and visits of past months.



If you want to change the settings, click on the Change Settings button.



Conclusion



KeepMeOut is a service to try, especially for those who want to limit the amount of time spent on a particular website. However, there is one drawback, this will only work with the KeepMeOut link. Otherwise, if you go directly to the website, it will not show warnings. But nevertheless, this service can help you become more organized and focus on your tasks instead of wasting time on various websites.
