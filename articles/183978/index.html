Video of DevCon 2013 reports and interviews of participants are published on TechDays.ru



On May 29-30, in the Moscow region, in a traditional country format, a large conference for software developers and testers, DevCon 2013, was held. You can watch the reporting video of the conference on TechDays.ru and once again dive into the memories of how it was, perhaps , you will even find there yourself or your friends.
Within two days of the conference, each participant had the opportunity to:
  • Choose to visit reports from six parallel tracks (more than 60 technology sessions)
  • To study technologies in practice, taking part in various master classes, thematic round tables
  • Directly on DevCon, write your application under the Windows platform on "Night of Coding" (Night Coding)
  • Visit expert zones and Microsoft Partner Solutions Shows
  • In an informal setting, discuss with colleagues and experts questions about the future of technology development.
  • Those who could not attend the conference in person had the opportunity to see all the major technological reports of the program online.

If you could not attend the conference in person and missed the broadcast of the reports, now you have the opportunity not only to watch all the reports online , but also to download them along with presentations of the speakers on the video portal TechDays.ru.
The plenary report , which was conducted by Jonas Persson, Jason Zander and Alexander Lozhechkin, and the video of the reports on eight main areas of the conference - Visual Studio, Windows Azure, Windows 8, Windows Phone, corporate development, effective application development, team development and ALM. In addition, recordings of all interviews with participants of DevCon 2013 are also available there.
