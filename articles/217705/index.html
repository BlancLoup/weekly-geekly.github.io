Fujitsu Webinar "A New Level of Reliability for x86 Servers"

April 16, 2014, we invite you to participate in the Fujitsu webinar: "A new level of reliability for x86 servers."

Combining the power of Intel Xeon E7 processors, standard features of Microsoft Windows and Linux operating systems, and other advanced technologies that provide high reliability, availability and business continuity, Fujitsu PRIMEQUEST servers ensure operational efficiency in meeting critical business needs -that open standards.

Fujitsu PRIMEQUEST 2400E and 2800E servers have self-healing features and unique features such as dynamic configuration that provide the highest platform reliability, better accessibility and serviceability. The dynamic configuration feature allows you to adjust the allocation of resources during the operation of systems. Isolated physical partitions are the basis of high availability, since equipment malfunctions in one section do not affect the performance of other sections. In the event of a system failure, which is unlikely, the faulty motherboard is automatically replaced with the system board of the backup system, allowing you to instantly restore the normal operation of applications and services.

We invite to participate: IT professionals interested in obtaining information on solutions for critical applications.
Date: April 16, 2014
Time: from 13:00 to 14:00 (Moscow time).
Our expert: Alexander Yakovlev, Fujitsu product marketing manager in Russia and the CIS.
Registration link: fujitsu-ts.webex.com/fujitsu-ts/onstage/g.php?t=a&d=958370784
