The launch of the module "Science" to the ISS may cancel due to problems



Sending the Russian multifunctional laboratory module (MLM) "Science" to the ISS can be canceled, TASS reports . This publication said an unnamed source in the rocket and space industry. The launch of this module has been postponed several times already, the last one - in March of this year. "Science" left with the manufacturer, at the Khrunichev Center . Specialists of the Center found contamination in the fuel tanks of the module.
