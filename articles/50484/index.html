Scandinavian auction

Heard probably ( http://tvoypriz.ru/ )?

We read the rules:
The price step is 25 kopeks, the cost of the bet is 10 rubles.
And now we will take some prize-winner and we will look at how much earned on it.

http://tvoypriz.ru/finished/218
Lot sold for 622.5 rubles alleged savings 8297.5 pub. (93%)

Calculate:
1. Bids made: 622.5 / 0.25 = 2490 bids (by all participants)
2. Earned at all rates and payment for goods 2490 * 10 + 622.5 = 25522.5 rubles
3. Overpayment 25522.5 * 100% / 8920 = 286.13%

Conclusion: before you buy something you will spend 3 times more on deaf auctions (someone still raises the rate)
