About magnitudes

A few ideas and thoughts about how and how to show the numbers and other information of this kind, which I gathered, playing in Need For Speed: Most Wanted 5-1-0 (edition for PSP, there is a sin) - I play);)

While playing, I constantly saw a lot of numbers, for example - the time during which I traveled the circle or the whole race. Looking at this number, for example 02:18:37, I often thought - so what? What does 2 minutes 18 seconds 27 milliseconds mean? Is it a lot or a little?
It is not so difficult to understand that this information in isolation from other data of the same type loses any meaning at all. The same can be said, for example, of the condition of 18,000,000 CFA francs - I have no idea how much it is in rubles and I do not know how much money other Africans have. And if they tell me that someone has set a new world record in swimming for some distance - 21 seconds, then I would not be surprised either. Because I do not know how much cooler it is. ;)
But if they tell me about the person that he is in 8th place in the Forbes rating, or about the fact that the new record in swimming is 2 seconds ahead of the old one - then yes, it is easier for me to imagine what the trick is.
Never write anything that cannot be compared without an extra search (yes, I could find the CFA franc rate - but do I need it? I'm lazy).
For example, on the main Habr:
Rating 
  	 I'm smart 10194.04

  	 User Interface Design and Usability 6587.54 
  	 ...

As you can see, quite informative. It is immediately evident that being clever is more popular than web development [almost] as much as this web development is more popular than design.
But imagine if the main page would be only: "The most popular blog is I am smart, with a rating of 10194.04." The phrase carries useful information, but in isolation from other meanings, it is equivalent to the inscription "The most popular blog - I am smart." Just like Lebedev's - divide by 2;)
