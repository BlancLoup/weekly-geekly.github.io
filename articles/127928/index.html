Work without borders - we investigate the Yaware online service for time tracking

The relevance of taking into account the personal effectiveness and timing of your working day has already been proven, the methods and tools are described - take it and use it. However, the Internet has long changed not only the personal lives of billions of people, but also forced us to move on to completely new business principles. To all this, he changed the internal structure of companies, scattering employees in different parts of the world, gave the opportunity to work remotely to thousands of specialists.

If you worked with US companies through the Odesk system as an hourly-time freelancer, then you know that the worker‚Äôs in-line control of working time is imperative. In Odesk, you install the application on your computer, which automatically takes screenshots of the desktop computer every 10 minutes and calculates the amount of time spent on the task.
Many freelancers agree that this style of work helps to be calm for the fact that all working time will be paid in full.

An online service, Yaware , appeared in RuNet, which makes it possible to keep track of the work of freelancers, as well as company employees working remotely. The service automatically collects detailed information about the work of the employee: what time he started work, where he finished, when and for what time he was distracted, what programs and resources he used, what websites he visited, and so on.




In the paid business version there is the possibility of taking screenshots, Kemshots ((from the word CamShot) webcam images). You can also keep a record of working time is not on the computer. Paid account allows you to monitor the work of an unlimited number of users. Thus, if you work with various contractors, you can simply send them an invitation email with a link to download Yaware and then, if the cooperation has ceased, simply deactivate it.

Fixing the work of the performer / employee, Yaware analyzes his work in accordance with previously defined categories - productively, unproductively, neutrally:



If watching a video on Youtube for an employee is productively used by time, it will be reckoned to him. Otherwise, time will be classified as wasted. Productivity of resources can be customized in accordance with the specifics of the user.

For convenience of perception, Yaware forms the collected information in the form of compact but capacious diagrams.
