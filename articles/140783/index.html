Rent exists rather optional. The main snag is that rental offers are changing daily, so they are very difficult to call and moderate. What do we have in the end?


- raw authorization system
- limited search capabilities
+ marketable application
+ convenient work with the found options

A great idea from the category of "always lacking": in the process of finding housing for rent, I was personally most annoyed by the calling process - you find the number on the site, dial, then leave somewhere in the third place notes ... Uncomfortable. Here all the main functionality in one program: found, saved, rang, after the conversation - added a note. Very convenient, a decent saving of time is obtained, and most importantly - all in one place.

Supplementing, perhaps, the implementation of the registration process - however, it pleases that the process is one-time and will not interfere with the use of the application in the future.

What is missing?


Perhaps, the advanced search capabilities - the choice of several metro stations, the choice of the search area on the map, etc., and more subtle filtering results in the configuration.
