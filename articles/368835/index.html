Yamaha introduced the Motobot robot capable of driving a motorcycle



At the Tokyo Motor Show, which is taking place these days in Tokyo Auto Show, the company Yamaha was surprised by the introduction of a humanoid robot capable of driving a motorcycle. At the same time, a robot called Motobot controls not only the usual stock bike, but also a fairly powerful model - the 2015 Yamaha YZF-R1M sports liter model.

The company has been working for several years to create a robot capable of driving stock bikes on the track. Unfortunately, little is known about Motobot - it is mentioned only that driving a motorcycle at high speeds requires the coordinated work of many complex and high-precision systems.



Naturally, the goal of the research is not to create robots that will drive motorcycles instead of people, but to develop a pilot assistance system, like the common auxiliary systems that are being introduced into all modern cars.
