Synergy as a way to drive traffic and improve core metrics

Marketers are always arguing about what is more effective to use to attract traffic: social networks or have a newsletter? In order to make a choice, you must first decide on the advantages of these two tools. In social networks, company employees can communicate with customers in real time. Customers, in turn, are engaged in "advertising" by sharing information about the company's products or services on their walls. With the help of email newsletter you can personalize the offer as much as possible and send only what the subscriber is interested in. But they also have similarities: communication with customers, increasing loyalty, increasing traffic, collecting leads.
There is such a statement that 40% of users intersect on the social network and have a mailing list. You start to communicate with the same subscribers only with the help of different tools, but there are still 60% of the remaining customers with whom you need to work.
Then why divide social networks and email marketing if, by combining them, we get a mega powerful tool? There is synergy for this. With it, you can get more opportunities to attract new customers, track their actions, better segmentation, purchase motivation, etc.

How to convince community members or friends to subscribe to your newsletter

1. Place on the site a form for collecting addresses. Make it simple by requiring you to fill in only the name and have an address. Offer a free gift or bonus in exchange for data, this will increase your chances to collect as many contacts as possible.

How to work simultaneously with two tools?

Despite the fact that social networks and email distribution work in synergy, these are still two different tools. Part of your audience will overlap, so the content should not be duplicated. You can repeat only the most important, for example, announcements of events, contests, discounts and promotions.
You can use comments from social networks as motivators in the newsletter or to work with abandoned baskets to motivate the customer to complete the purchase.
If the results of testing your email list showed good results - place it in social networks for discussion.
