UHoo smart analyzer will tell you all about the quality of air in your home or office



The air we breathe can lead to various health problems if you do not try to make the air cleaner. Of course, on the streets of the cities we can do nothing on our own, except perhaps to wear a gas mask.

But what can be done at home, in the office? A lot of things, if you know what to do. And for this we need data on the composition of such air, monitoring is needed. And such an opportunity is provided by the uHoo smart air analyzer.



UHoo features


This gadget can perform several types of air analysis, including such as:
  • Suspended particles: uHoo sensors can detect the presence of pollen from various plants, as well as dust in the air. All this is almost impossible to see, but people with various respiratory illnesses or allergies like these impurities have a very negative effect. uHoo will report if there are more particles in the air than allowed by the standards;
  • Harmful chemicals. uHoo is able to detect harmful substances, the source of which may be poor-quality paint, solvents, glue in furniture, etc. Unfortunately, the developers do not indicate which substances are detected by the device;
  • Carbon dioxide. If there is too much carbon dioxide in the room, this will adversely affect the performance and general well-being. uHoo will tell you when to ventilate the room.


In addition, uHoo developers have provided for the presence of a temperature sensor and a sensor for relative humidity of air. Humidity and temperature are very important parameters, and they should also be monitored.

The device can adapt to the user's preferences, and take into account the habits of the owner. However, if the user's preferences for humidity / temperature are not very healthy, uHoo will report this.

How it works?


The device has a wireless module for connecting to a smartphone. Of course, the developers have released a corresponding application for the mobile device, which serves as both a control panel and an information panel.



Design






In size uHoo no more cans of coke. The gadget can be installed on a table in the living room, in the kitchen - in any place where you spend a lot of time.
