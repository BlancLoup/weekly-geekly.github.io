The digest of interesting materials for the mobile developer # 96 (on March 23-29)

This week's main feature was the F8 conference from Facebook, where the social network presented many interesting tools and solutions for developers. But besides its premieres, there are many other exciting things: the online school of Android developers, the large investments of Cyanogen, an interesting article about the era of phablets, prototyping in Sketch, a review of the Russian mobile market, and a few dozen materials in the new digest.



Free online school of Android developers

The new school course will consist of 13 lectures on Android-development. Representatives of such companies as Tinkoff, Aviasales, JetBrains, OK.RU, Kaspersky Lab, Parallels, LinguaLeo, Mail.ru, ABBYY took part in the lecture notes. During the course, experts will tell not only the theory of development for Android, but also show various chips, as well as share original ideas that appeared in the process of working on their own applications.
