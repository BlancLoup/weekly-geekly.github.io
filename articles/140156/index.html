War is war and dinner is on schedule. Samsung iPad 3 display

It would seem that Apple and Samsung are bogged down in violent patent wars in many countries. Apple even refused to produce processors from Samsung ...

Workers iFixit dismantled the new iPad. And they found that Apple‚Äôs pride in the new iPad 3 - a touchscreen display with a huge resolution - turned out to be made by Samsung.

Also, the battery has grown significantly, its size has increased by 70% compared with the iPad 2. And the capacity has increased from 25 Wh to 42,5 Wh h.

The Apple A5X 2-core processor with frequent 1 GHz and 4-core video processor increased in size by 36%. In addition, the new iPad uses 1 GB of Elpida LP DDR2 memory, permanent memory made on Toshiba 24-nm MLC Flash.



The main camera uses the same sensor as the iPhone 4 - 5 megapixel CMOS Omnivision OV5650. The Qualcomm RTR8600 transceiver is responsible for radio communication, and the Qualcomm MDM9600 acts as a 3G / 4G modem. The modem is essentially an all-band, but the transceiver limits LTE operation on American frequencies, apparently in European models there will be another transceiver.



GLONASS support has not yet been announced, but judging by the Qualcomm radio chipsets, there is support at the hardware level.



For the difficulty of repairing the iPad 3 received 2 points out of 10 (where 10 is the easiest to repair), iFixit noted the following advantages and disadvantages during the repair.

Pros:
  • The LCD display is easily removed after the front panel is removed.
  • The battery is not soldered to the motherboard, which slightly simplifies its replacement.

Minuses:
  • Like the iPad 2, the front panel is glued, which greatly increases the chances of damaging the glass when it is removed.
  • All parts are glued.
  • The screen is glued to the front panel with foam tape.
  • You cannot access the front panel connector without removing the screen.
