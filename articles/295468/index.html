As CaboHub increased its activity and began to attract residents to the Hub for programmers and entrepreneurs in Mexico, we began to receive questions from readers.

If you wish, you can fly to Mexico City and for $ 60 fly to SJD by local airline Volaris - flies every 4 hours. 3. What about transport? I understand there without a car in any way? I think you have to go 2-3 times a week to the city? How much is a rental car for a month?

Travel around the city and near the city costs $ 0.8. In another city - San Jose or Todos Santos - $ 2. Airport - $ 5. The house is 7 minutes walk to the bus stop. Without a car is quite possible - I live without a car.


Chevolet Aveo rental costs $ 35 per day.
We will have a car! We buy a jeep for our hub. With a jeep this question disappears by itself.

4. Is there any difficulty with buying a local sim card? what tariffs?

Buy a local SIM-card is not a question - a passport and a prepayment of $ 7. The connection here is prepaid, as in Russia and Belarus. Calls to the US are the same as calls to Mexico, there is no roaming between the states of Mexico. A minute of conversation costs $ 0.12; SMS is $ 0.06. 3G / LTE Internet Limited - you need to buy packages: from 10 MB for $ 0.3 to 2 GB for $ 25. Starbucks / McDonalds / Burger King type cafes always have free WiFi.

Tariffs for cellular communication can be found here . Prices in pesos (MXN), which are also denoted by the dollar symbol $. Divide by 16 to get the price in dollars.


5. Language. How hard is it to communicate without knowing Spanish? Is there a language school nearby to learn Spanish?

Most locals speak English, or understand the language. Not fluent english, but you can communicate. For 1.5 months here I had language difficulties only with gas tankers in the house - they did not speak English at all.
There are 3 Spanish language schools:
Spanish in Cabo - www.spanishincabo.com
Columbus School - www.columbus-school.com
Sham Rock - www.shamrock-school.blogspot.com
At Tijuana University, I was recommended the last one - Sham Rock. I haven't checked it yet, I don't know.
We also have Minerva Ballesteros - she can be your Spanish tutor!
