Enertia Elektromoped will be available in early 2008

Brammo Motorsports has announced the creation of a motorcycle running on electricity. An electric motorcycle (or rather, an electromoped) called Enertia Bike runs on rechargeable batteries, which have a charge of 70 km and can reach speeds of up to 80 km / h.


It is noteworthy that the Enertia Bike is not a hybrid two-wheeled vehicle, but a fully electric motorcycle. This means that it operates exclusively on electricity supplied by six lithium-phosphate batteries placed on the sides and bottom of the frame. Battery charging time is only three hours.
