About the VeeamON First-Hand Conference - Not Everything That Was in Vegas Remains in Vegas



From 6 to 8 October, the first international conference on data backup and data protection in virtual environments - VeeamON ( Habr√© announcement ) was held in Las Vegas (USA), which gathered about 1,200 guests from more than 50 countries.

Looking ahead, I will say that the first experience was very successful and it is already 100% known that VeeamON will become an annual event.

This year, the conference announced the release of two new products, one of which was already known to the audience of GT and Habr Veeam Availability Suite v8 . The second novelty is "explosive" in nature - this is Veeam Endpoint Backup FREE , a free tool for backing up data from physical machines - laptops and desktops - based on Windows.

What else will be remembered for the first conference for administrators of virtual infrastructures? While the memories are fresh, and the jet-lag does not let go (the 11-hour time difference is still not a joke), I will give you a reportage from the scene and try to convey the atmosphere of the event.
