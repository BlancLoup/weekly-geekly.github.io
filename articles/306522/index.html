Now two important terms in the technology of creating media "viruses"


  1. In any viral PR campaign, there is an overriding stage called ‚Äúsowing‚Äù. Sowing is a paid (or barter, or by personal acquaintance) placement of a news story in the ‚Äúseed media‚Äù, which people and other media must pick up for further distribution. Such ‚Äúseeding‚Äù is usually called ‚ÄúPaid media‚Äù; placement in own info-channels is called ‚ÄúOwned media‚Äù (in possession); and received for free - Earned media (earned).
  2. As well as the second term - "virus coefficient". This ratio indicates how much we get free views on the number of purchased ones. For example, if we bought 1 million views on Youtube, and the final number of views after a certain time is 2 million, then the coefficient of virality is two. This ratio applies absolutely to any information .


Signs of "seeding" are


  1. Instant response . Those. information usually appears immediately on many resources in the shortest time from the date of the posting of the viral video (for example) to which all this news refers.
  2. The media that hosts the information . Ideally, it is a reputable media outlet that does not post unverified and commercial information.
  3. Headline It contains the name of the product being promoted and the exact information message that other media should pick up.
  4. Content and design news . "Sowing news" is usually made out of high quality, it is inserted into it with pictures with logos and video.


On the other hand, the usual journalistic note (in Earned media) is much more concise, contains facts, different photos (and not one that is actively promoted, or video), in the usual news, mention of the brand and description of the facts with enthusiasm is avoided.

Further dissemination of information (through Earned media) is characterized by the fact that the reaction is delayed, the headlines are simpler, the news is short and dry, and journalists try to look at the informational occasion from the other side. This second wave of news, which follows the "sowing", is very easy to recognize by their "poor" branded content. But this is of no interest to anyone, since it's free.
