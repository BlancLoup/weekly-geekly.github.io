You can already use SIMs of other operators on iPhone

Since the advent of the iPhone on the market, hackers of all countries have worked hard to enable mobile operators of all operators, not just AT & T, to make calls and send SMS from this smartphone. And now, finally came true!

As it turned out in the process of hacking, the iPhone has a much better protection than all the other mobile phones of our time. At first, hackers could not offer anything better than a program to activate only non-phone functions of the device.

Now the Macedonian hacker and the team Hackint0sh report on the successful completion of the "hacking" of the iPhone. According to their statement, from now on, having done with your device those operations that are required by their hacking algorithm, you will be able to make calls from your iPhone using a SIM card of any operator.
