Google+ traffic grew by 1269% in one week

New statistics from Experian Hitwise for September 17-24 showed a huge jump in the attendance of the social network Google+, immediately by 1269%. It is easy to guess that the reason for this was the cancellation of invites. Immediately after this, Google+'s American audience has grown from 1.1 million to 15 million people.



Of course, these figures do not include foreign users, as well as visitors who came from mobile devices.

According to Paul Allen , which is based on comparing the frequency of occurrence of rare surnames on Google+ and in the results of the general census of the United States and other countries, the audience of the service is about 43 million people.
