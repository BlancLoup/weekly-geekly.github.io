Coworking office in Yekaterinburg

The first coworking office opens in Yekaterinburg. We selected five options for the office - so that we could choose the premises according to heart and money. Photos and descriptions can be seen here .

We expect that the result is not exactly an office: the business style, although it is concentrating attention, still causes a certain rejection. Something like that:

It will probably be the best club-office option, like the creative antiformalism of Lebedev Studio. But this is for my taste :) You will probably choose something else.

Estimated cost - $ 150-200 per month. Rather, 200, than 150. The number of places - for 10-15 people, for a start. Expected: ITshniki, designers, copywriters, writers. Weekly seminars? Common projects? Probably yes. I see no reason to limit myself in this.

And also: if there is a desire, let's play with the interior designers. How would you arrange functional areas (kitchen, conference room, rooms), where to put the tables, where the sofas, and so on, down to the smallest detail. We'll do the office ourselves, so that work was nice :)
