From point A to point B

What happens when you mail something? What happens between sending and receiving a package? How many people and processes are involved and how many stages does this process take?
I wanted to get an answer to these questions. So instead of sitting on the pope, I made a simple project to see everything myself. I put a small camera in the box, made a timer on the Arduino and sent it by mail.

It's pretty simple. The timer was made to make a short 3-second video clips every minute and record longer ones at the time of the package advance (so as not to miss the most interesting moments).
