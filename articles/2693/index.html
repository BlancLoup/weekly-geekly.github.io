RBC wants a lot of love

According to the Finam investment company, the project Loveplanet.ru, which is actively advertised in the open spaces of Runet, belongs to the RBC holding.

Loveplanet is a dating service that has been in existence for a year and a half, but many Internet users have learned about it recently, thanks to an active advertising campaign in which all contextual advertising services are involved. TV viewers also learned about it: unexpectedly, project commercials appeared on the Muz-TV, Ren-TV and TNT channels.

According to the top manager of one of the Russian dating services, RBC plans to spend $ 10 million on the Loveplanet advertising campaign.

The company RBC declined to comment on this information in any way, according to Vedomosti .
