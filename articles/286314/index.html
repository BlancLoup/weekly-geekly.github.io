Wired Edition Redesigned Site

The English-language publication WIRED presented a new design based on Wordpress.

The restart of the publication took place for the tenth time since the existence of the site since 1994.

This time, publishers and developers focused on the speed of downloading files and the readability of content on high-resolution devices.

Also the headings were reformatted, now there are only six: Business, Design, Entertainment, Gear, Science, Security.

It took more than two years to work on a new design.
