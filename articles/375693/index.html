The European company offers cardboard houses for cats



The company, successfully producing disposable cat litter made of cardboard PoopyCat , decided to expand its range . So a prefabricated cardboard game house for pets appeared. At Kickstarter, their campaign to raise money for a project called Blocks was successfully completed, which exceeded expectations by almost 5 times - 23.8 thousand euros instead of 5.5 thousand.



Happy owners of furry animals know how they adore cardboard boxes. It is easy to find photos in which the cat sits in a box from under the purchased game house, instead of developing the house itself. A cardboard houses and cheap, and disposable, and even completely environmentally friendly.



The basic set for 40 euros (not including shipping) consists of two "beams", two pipes, a tunnel, a bridge and connectors. The creators claim that despite the cardboard base, the house turns out very strong. Thanks to the many ways of attaching parts to each other, the final version of the house is limited only by your imagination. And from several sets you can build a whole fortress, which certainly will serve as entertainment for the whole family.
