How to present a startup investor at a distance?

Hi, Habradruzya!

Often there is a desire to attend an event, but it just does not work. Especially if it takes place in another city. June 29 in Moscow will host a major startup conference BizSpark Camp . And we want everyone to have the opportunity to present their project on it and watch the broadcast ( register for an online broadcast ).

How to do it?


At the last KIBORIFE, we tried a new project presentation format - poster presentations. As a result, visitors to our section then saw more than 50 posters from various projects from across the country.
At BizSpark Camp, we will again present startups in this way.
If you, habrachelovek, have an idea that you want to share with the world, but you cannot come and present it yourself - send us information about your project on an A3 poster, and we will hang it in the hall where the conference will be held.

What you need to attract the attention of investors and experts with your project?


  • Download poster layout
  • Fill it with your information, guided by your ideas about the beautiful. The template is placed to make it clear what information can be conveyed. The more creative the design will be, the more chances you have to interest visitors to BizSpark. Here is an example of a cool poster design .
  • Send it to us at support@startuppoint.ru


Recall that experts will come:
  • Igor Ashmanov - Managing Partner of Ashmanov and Partners
  • German Klimenko - the owner of "LiveInternet"
  • Dmitry Stepanov - executive director of the publishing house "Playbill"
  • Askar Tuganbaev - Director for Development of Internet Projects, CTC Media
  • Petr Didenko - Chief Specialist for Strategic Development of SKB Kontur
  • Arkady Moreynis - Head of GlavStart Holding

Those who are not able to come to BizSpark in Moscow will gather together in their hometown to watch the online broadcast ( register for an online broadcast ), discuss what they have heard, and also have fun. For them, we have prepared our unique content and small bonuses.
At the same time, our partners in the regions (Yekaterinburg, Chelyabinsk , Perm , Kazan, Penza , Voronezh, your city) will be able to tell you about the solutions that will be offered at the conference, to answer your questions. To participate, you need to register for the online broadcast, contact them and come to the general viewing of the event.
  • The conference will be held several special sessions of questions with experts for the regions. Questions will be accepted only through our partners in your region.
  • You will be able to discuss on the spot new opportunities presented at the conference.
