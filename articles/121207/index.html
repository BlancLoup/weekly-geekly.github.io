X5 Retail Group - thieves and rogue?

Company X5, which owns the grocery stores "Crossroads" and "Pyaterochka", also has in the asset online stores Bolero.ru and 003.ru

003.ru, by the way, a few years ago was a pretty decent home appliance store, I did orders from them and everything was fine.
But then the quality of the service began to deteriorate, then they started selling everything they could think of, apparently things went really bad.
And in May 2011, the news slipped through that X5 is putting these online stores for sale.
But I did not see the information that she lent them to someone.

And yesterday, at our online store, we uploaded photos of new products to the site, and accidentally, in Google search, we found out that 003 shop happily steal our old photos and uses them to sell similar products on its site.

We made these photos on our own. We negotiated with suppliers, went to their office, organized a photo session there, then these pictures were processed and uploaded.
Who knows what hard work it is, he will understand.

Well, now 003 shop decided that of course it is easier to steal photos from another store and quietly make money on it without spending a dime.

I would calmly react to the use of any of our materials, if it was not related to commercial activities.

But if people want to hurt money on all the money, then I become a champion of copyright.

I will not refuse an apology from this company, confirmed by modest compensation for the use of our materials for commercial purposes. Although I doubt it.

Well, we wrote them a letter through the feedback form. If something is answered, I will inform you.

By the way, a similar case, but with other products, occurred with the store ozon.ru
To be honest, they responded rather quickly, explained that the photos were laid out by their business partner, and these photos were removed.
