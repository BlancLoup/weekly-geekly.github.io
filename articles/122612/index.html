Photocell bikini



A limited production has received a bikini containing 40 thin and flexible photovoltaic panels connected by conductive threads. Through the USB cable, a new swimsuit is ready to recharge the phone or player, while the owner of the mini-masterpiece is basking on the beach.

Solar Bikini invented the New York designer Andrew Schneider (Andrew Schneider). He once joked among colleagues that he would like to create a swimsuit that can cool beer on the beach.

With the current development of photocells, the power supply to the fridge was not enough (at least with the bust sizes acceptable by nature), but for a cellar or player it is quite enough. By the way, the designer does not despair. He is currently working on a prototype of men's solar shorts that could generate enough energy to operate a tiny cooler of beverage cans. They are named in accordance with the fashion - iDrink.

Let's hope that he will be able to realize his idea and he will have enough money from the swimsuits (you can order the swimsuits on a special website )
