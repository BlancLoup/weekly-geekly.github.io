Charles Stross on the Girls Around Me app



English science fiction writer Charles Stross expressed his opinion on the free Girls Around Me app, which recently appeared on iTunes. This very simple program by pressing the "radar" button shows the photos and profiles of all the girls who are currently nearby. Information is pulled from Facebook and Foursquare.

Charles Stross does not agree with the opinion of observers that this program from the Russian company SMS Services should be simply banned. According to him, the problem is actually deeper - in the wrong model of social relationships that Facebook promotes, and in fact, people who consider it necessary to disseminate private information about themselves as widely as possible.
