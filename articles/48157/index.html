Carlson's propeller for the New Year

Happy New Year! This time we decided to make a Carlson costume out of scrap materials for the holiday;)



Devices and materials


To make a miracle propeller, we need:

  • Cooler
  • Old braces
  • 4 punch cards
  • Stationery knife
  • Needle threads
  • Posting
  • Insulating tape



Training


At the beginning, it is required to remove the case from the cooler, this is done very simply with a snap;)



Next, cut the blades from punched cards



And of course you can drink a couple of bottles of beer in between;)

Construction assembly


We sew a cooler to old braces



And glue the blades of punched cards, then paste all the blades on the cooler. We solder to the contacts of the cooler or connect them by twisting the wiring to the switch. The power supply is suitable for it 9 V, we received it from the old charger and batteries tied with adhesive tape (which we hide in our pants)

Result


The result is a propeller Carlson
