IT in the region. Ulyanovsk. We leave from the twilight

Ulyanovsk is my hometown, but I started my development career in Moscow and went from a PHP developer in a web studio to a Ruby On Rails programmer on Skype. Zelenograd, in which the Skype office is located, turned out to be a very unusual city. He is really very green, well maintained and pleasant to live. It was there that the thought came that the light did not converge on Moscow. After some time, I quit, and, having no plan or savings, I went home to Ulyanovsk to create the company of my dreams.

What was my surprise when I learned how developed the IT movement in the city.

Educational movement



IT master

Open regional competition of computer creativity among schoolchildren, organized on the initiative of Pavel Makarov from Ulyanovsk State Technical University and Elena Bukina from Lyceum at UlSTU. Every year hundreds of schoolchildren take part in this event, who present their works in 17 nominations. Representatives of IT companies in the competition conduct master classes. Master IT has been held for 10 years and is the largest school competition in Ulyanovsk.

Volga IT

Volga IT Olympiad among students and postgraduates Volga IT annually gathers participants from all over the Volga region. This year the Olympiad has already 9 nominations. In 2011, about 400 people took part in the first round, over 100 competed in the final. The Olympiad is held on the basis of the leading universities of Ulyanovsk, and this year the secondary specialized educational institutions joined. Founded by Creative Development, in recent years the Olympiad has grown into a major regional event, organized and sponsored jointly by key local IT companies and the government of the Ulyanovsk Region.

Professional movement



ULCAMP

In the summer of 2011, on the banks of the Volga, the Script Company held for the first time ULCAMP, an informal gathering of IT specialists from Ulyanovsk and invited guests. ULCAMP'2011 was the largest event of its kind in the region and served as a powerful incentive to strengthen ties within the IT community of Ulyanovsk.



In July 2012, ULCAMP turned into an international festival that received a considerable response in Russia and without a doubt became the most notable IT event of the past summer. That there is only a list of speakers: Sergey Belousov (Runa Capital), Peter Didenko (SKB Kontur), Sergey Zefirov, Sergey Kotyrev (UMI), Andrey Aksenov (Sphinx) and even Monty, the legendary creator of MySQL.

Future



Recently, we often hear from our colleagues from other regions that Ulyanovsk sounds more and more often and louder. This is due to the openness of companies and the expressed desire to establish horizontal links. Literally 4 years ago, the situation was not much different from our neighbors - everyone sat at their own ‚Äúiron curtain‚Äù and saw only attempts to divert employees around. In 2010, it became clear that this was the way to a dead end.

Despite the existing differences between the companies, we are actively interacting and looking for common ground. For example, everyone is worried about the outflow of specialists to Moscow, and we are learning from each other to create more favorable conditions for employees. Largely due to this, the ‚Äúbrain drain‚Äù in the capital is indeed decreasing. Attracting investment and the emergence of new companies in Ulyanovsk are also in our area of ‚Äã‚Äãinterest. We see how the exchange of experience and technological diversity leads to an increase in the overall technical and organizational level of local companies.

And, although many issues are still being resolved without proper focus and professionalism, there is a chance to correct the situation and to the fact that our example of interaction between private and public initiatives will prove to be a worthy example for replication in other regions of Russia.
