Future of Photoshop: Content-aware fill

What Photoshop is coming in prepares us - most likely in CS5, but maybe a little later. The video is in English, but, in principle, everything should be clear. Do you want to erase a tree so that nobody will notice No problem, a couple of clicks. No more clone tool. Want to draw a panorama to the corners? There are no problems, the same couple of clicks and empty places were drawn by themselves. Actually, the continuation of the development of ideas PatchMatch .

The most impressive things after 2:50 and until the end of the video. For the ignorant in English - the work goes with one layer and the objects (tree, road, empty pieces in the panorama) are deleted (painted over and drawn) by Photoshop in automatic mode.



More examples and about technology - under the cut

If, however, it is not clear in English, then the essence is quite simple. PatchMatch searches randomly in the photo for pieces that could be inserted into the ‚Äúhole‚Äù, so that their edges match the texture with what needs to be filled in - and inserts them. Then the edges turned out to be new - the algorithm again looks for a piece of the photo that could be glued to the edge. Plus, all this is added color correction, most likely. And so on until the whole "hole" will not be sketched.
