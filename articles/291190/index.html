Graduate Y Combinator drew $ 1.5 million in a round with the participation of Parse founder Ilya Sukhar

The founder of Parse and the partner of the Y Combinator foundation, Ilya Sukhar, invested in Yhat . She is developing data science technologies in data science.

Tikhon Bernstein, Justin Kahn, KEC Ventures and some other investors also participated in the round. At the end of the round, Yhat received $ 1.5 million, reports Firrma.

Yhat is a graduate site for startups Y Combinator. Today, the company has two applications - ScienceOps and ScienceBox . ScienceOps is the flagship product of Yhat. This is an expert system that analyzes large amounts of data. It allows you to make decisions and move forward in creating a product based on the analysis.

In May, Ilya Sukhar invested in the French startup Algolia . The company is engaged in optimization of search engines of sites. The total volume of the round was $ 18.3 million.

Parse is a cloud platform for developing games and applications. Ilya Sukhar launched it in 2011. At the initial stage, he managed to raise $ 7 million. After that, he received offers from companies such as Dropbox , Google and Yahoo! However, in 2013, a startup company bought Facebook for $ 85 million.
