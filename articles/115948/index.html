Release Opera Mini 6, Opera Mini 4.3 and Opera Mobile 11

Opera Mini was updated to version 6.0, and Opera Mobile - to 11.0. The release took place at CTIA, March 22, in Orlando, USA.

Opera Mini 6 is available for Android, BlackBerry and Symbian, as well as all mobiles with a J2ME platform. Opera Mobile 11 is released on Android, Symbian, Windows 7, Maemo and MeeGo.

What's new?



  • Opera Mini 6: Pinch-To-Zoom (zoom with two fingers);
  • Opera Mini 4.3, 6, Opera Mobile 11: the "Sharing" button (" Share "), which allows you to share the open page with friends, including on the social network "VKontakte";
  • Opera Mini 6: a separate version for tablets based on Google Android, MeeGo and iOS;
  • Opera Mobile 11: optional support for Adobe Flash 10.2 for Google Android (‚ÄúSettings‚Äù -> ‚ÄúAdvanced‚Äù -> ‚ÄúAdobe Flash‚Äù: ‚ÄúDisabled‚Äù, ‚ÄúOn Request‚Äù, ‚ÄúEnabled‚Äù);
  • Opera Mini 4.3, 6: YouTube video support for mobile (playing through the player built into the phone);
  • Interface improvements.



Opera Mini 6 on the Android Market (1.3 MB)


Opera Mobile 11 on the Android Market (20.72 MB)

Opera Mobile 11 beta 1.7 at Maemo.org (7 MB)

Opera Mobile 11 on Opera.com - versions for Windows 7, Android 1.6+, S60 and MeeGo.

There are no Opera Mini 6 versions for iOS yet, it will be released for approval in the AppStore only in April 2011 and will be available at the end of the censorship period.
Versions of Opera Mobile 11 for iOS are not foreseen, as in the case of Opera Mobile, Apple sees a direct violation of licensing policy and duplication of Safari functionality. Opera Mini 6 does not violate these conditions, as it does not use the alternative WebKit engine, but only delivers compressed content on Opera servers.

Versions of Opera Mobile 11 and Opera Mini 6 for Windows Mobile will not. Opera decided to stop developing browsers for this OS.

To install Opera Mini 6, Opera Mini 4.3 or Opera Mobile 11 for any platform, visit m.opera.com from the appropriate device.
