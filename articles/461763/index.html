Can advertising agencies kill business at the start of the journey

There are a large number of advertisements from SMM agencies that promise a flow of customers for a small budget with minimal risk. And you thought about ordering the services of an advertising agency to increase the number of customers.

What's better? Set up advertising yourself or outsource the agency? Now we will figure it out together.

Let's meet first!

I am a guy and I am 23 years old, I have been friends with advertising for more than 2 years. He was one of the founders of the agency for the provision of advertising services in social networks. Already set up more than 75 advertising campaigns for small and medium-sized businesses. At the moment I do not work with the agency, I have a start-up startup in another area. I will prove that advertising agencies at the start of the way only kill the business.

"Pseudo-Individuality"

Specialists, when they set up an advertising campaign for your product, have other products and tasks that need to be solved, plus personal problems.
This greatly affects the result.

As a result, specialists spend the minimum amount of time when setting up advertising on the principle of a conveyor.
Number of customers = amount of money.
