garage48 for the first time in Kiev!

This weekend in Kiev will be a very cool event - Garage48 - the idea is to create a startup in two days. This event was invented in Estonia and has already managed to visit many countries around the world. Finally, Garage 48 reached Ukraine: from May 11 to 13 we will occupy the KPI Library , which is located on Victory Avenue , 37, and will enjoy the company of foreign mentors abroad (they will guide you in the right direction when creating a startup and guide you on the way , if you go into the jungle), as well as the company of its colleagues and organizers of the event - representatives of the Garage48 Foundation, DOU and IT activists Vika Zhurbas.

On Friday we will get acquainted with the ideas of the participants, select the most interesting ideas and form teams. On Saturday morning, we will check which of the teams survived and did not disintegrate overnight, and during the day each team will be lucky enough to talk with our mentors and get a good portion of advice. At Sunday lunch, we will begin active preparation of the participants for the pitches. In the evening we will see the results of the work of young businessmen and the jury will choose the winner. Detailed schedule can be found here.
