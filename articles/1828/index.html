"Enforta" went to the capital

Regional wireless broadband access operator Enforta acquired 51% of shares of ArtCommunications Ltd , a similar operator operating in Moscow and St. Petersburg. Upon completion of the transaction, both parties expanded their business opportunities: Enforta will now begin to connect large corporations in Moscow and St. Petersburg, ArtCommunications will work with clients from regions of the Russian Federation.

According to Lee Sparkman, president of Enforta, the company's initial strategy was to focus on regional markets where competition is relatively low. The expansion of the service to Moscow and St. Petersburg in the plans was listed, but it took place earlier - due to the expanded client base.
