Elastix 5 beta

A very unexpected news came from sunny Spain, where they are developing a popular add-on called Elastix for managing primarily Asterisk, and secondly with a large number of additional services conveniently assembled in one distribution. It is possible to increase the functionality with the help of modules. Distribution kit with a large community around the world. The first version was introduced in 2006, more than 3 million downloads of the distribution today.

And today there was news from the developers about Elastix 5.

The new version will be based on 3CX! Especially for Elastix there will be a free version with 25 accounts and 8 simultaneous calls. Accompanying, selling licenses, teaching systems on Elastix 5 will be handled by 3CX itself. The distribution is based on the latest release of Debian (Jessie).
