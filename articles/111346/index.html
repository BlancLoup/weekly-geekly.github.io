Apple launches Mac App Store

Today, January 6 at 5:00 pm, Apple launched a new service for its customers - the App Store for Mac. The main advantage of the program is the ability to install any applications with one click - the downloaded Mac App Store is automatically installed in the Dock.




In fact, the new service is the development of the familiar App Store for the iPhone, iPod touch and iPad. In the desktop store appeared sections: office applications, games, music, etc. The purchase is carried out in the same way as in the iPhone: with one touch. Thus, the time spent on installation is several times less than when downloading programs from the Internet or ‚Äúthe long way‚Äù - moving from iTunes to the web version of the App Store.



Also, the Mac App Store allows you to constantly be up to date with the latest updates used programs. You can update them one by one or all at once. Another convenience - when buying a new Mac, you can install all previously purchased applications on it



To install the App Store, just download the latest Mac OS X update. Unfortunately, despite the possibility to select the country of use in the settings - Russia, the App Store is not yet available in Russian.
