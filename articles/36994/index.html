parking.nic.ru: participation in closed testing

At the end of March, there was a rumor that Ru-Center was preparing to launch a domain parking service. This was confirmed in the form of a commented out link on the new site of the Ru-Center to the corresponding domain. And so, quite recently, customers of the existing service Parked.ru , and now a partner of the Ru-Center, received a letter proposing to test the new parking.



The functionality, as it seemed to me, is mostly inherited from parked.ru, but wrapped in a very nice interface and made more clearly and efficiently. Bugs come across quite often, but mainly concern auxiliary functions and are quickly corrected. I think we should expect the opening in September.

Next come screenshots and invitations. If you do not like the beta version and jpeg, you can not watch;)







The most impatient domainers have the opportunity to join the testing, but, as far as I understand, only by referral link. If you have idle domains and you want to see the service before anyone else, you can use it . Additional login and password for access to the site and the forum - parking: iwillbeveryrich.
