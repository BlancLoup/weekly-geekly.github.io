Boom for health electronics



According to Accenture, a growing number of people in the US are using wearable devices and mobile applications in the health category .

In fact, their number doubled in just two years. Thus, the number of consumers using these applications has increased from 16% in 2014 to 33% today, and the number of consumers using wearable electronics (for example, Apple Watch) increased from 9 to 21% over the same period.

The data was obtained in a survey of about 8,000 respondents from 7 countries - including 2,225 respondents from the United States. The obtained data were compared with the results of opinion polls among doctors.
