Google Maps API: Now For Money

Greetings. Today changed the terms of use of the Google Maps API. In case the load coming from the site to the API is more than 25,000 hits per day (for using stylized cards - 2500 hits) - every next thousand hits will now cost $ 4.

Google Geo Developers Blog says that for very popular sites, it is likely that a Premier license will be beneficial. Google also says that non-commercial applications and applications that are considered useful to the public (according to Google) are not subject to these restrictions on use.

Details in the FAQ service (the Russian version has not yet been updated).
