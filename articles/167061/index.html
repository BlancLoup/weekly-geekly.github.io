Hello, I want to talk about the problem that our team recently faced and how we had to participate in the fraud of the lottery results.

So, in the very first week of the new year, we were given a simple but urgent task. Buyers must enter them on a special page of the site (along with a zip code to send a person to the nearest cinema, if he won).
