Traditionally, parks are occupied by two warring factions. The first is mothers with children from 0 to 5 years. They are usually based on playgrounds and violently hate the second group - dog lovers. Dog lovers, unlike mothers, are often quiet, docile and realize that not everyone is delighted with their little Bobby.
Both mothers and dog lovers are herd creatures. They, like their pets, are bound by the desire to rally their efforts and get the most out of their daily runs. The technology of mothers is quite simple: to find suitable connections for your child and, having acquired the telephone of the mother, to go for a walk in the future, having previously coordinated the time and place.
Dogs are more difficult: they walk at different times and with different regularity, their children are very different in size and character, and most importantly - they tend to move much more actively during the walk than the contingent of playgrounds. Just for them was invented the service "Acquaintance for dogs." +++
