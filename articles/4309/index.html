SmartMarketing on the PDA market in the II quarter of 2006

Growth in sales of PDAs, smartphones and communicators grew by 271.6% over the year, according to the SmartMarketing report. A total of 220,000 units were sold over the period, against 81,000 in the second quarter of 2005.

In general, the market showed positive growth and significantly exceeded the results of the same period last year, the researchers say. The semiannual dynamics is slightly more significant: the growth in sales in the first half of 2006 relative to the corresponding period last year amounted to 274.2% - 329,000 units against 120,000. Experts believe that high market growth rates will continue in the next quarter, and even until the end of the year .
