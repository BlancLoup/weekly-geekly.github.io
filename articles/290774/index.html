Anthropomorphism - a new method of managing the chaos of thoughts of designers and designers

How to start designing a complex information system? In this article, I propose to look at an example of an interesting method of projecting a human person onto a website or application being created, a kind of analogue for creating an identikit for the sought concept.



The last five years, the design technique, repelled by fictional characters, causes me more and more mistrust. It cannot be said that this approach is outdated - no, it also helps to structure the chaos of the thoughts of the development team and tune everyone into a single mental wave, allowing you to generate hypotheses and build predictions in a single vector. This is all true, but here are the guarantees of success ...

Increasingly, the feeling that a system designed for 2, 3, 5 fictional people does not leave me - as a result, will work only with these few invented characters. My own design methodology proposes to leave imaginary users alone and concentrate on the character that will be our information project.
