Chobots.com - Virtual online world for children and parents



Today, the opening of a public beta test of the virtual online world for children and their parents - Chobots.com from domestic developers of VayerSoft LLC .

Chobots.com is an innovative project that opens up many new opportunities for the children's Internet audience. Only the purpose of the project - for children , makes you look at this virtual world, because the number of projects that target children are children in our country has only a few, and such quality projects as Chobots can hardly be found at all.

The online family game Chobots develops the most important skills of children: for example, various team games, inside the virtual world, will teach a child logical and creative thinking, achieving goals and simply knowing how to work in a team.

The virtual world itself contributes to the development of sociable qualities, and helps children learn English and many other languages! Just when communicating with their foreign peers, the kids will learn practical English from them.
