Google launched domains to quickly create documents

A few days ago, Google published a small life hack for those using Google Docs. Now you can instantly create documents, tables, slides, etc. The user only needs to type the keyword in the browser line and add the domain ".new" to it.



For example, doc.new will create a new document, and sheet.new will create a table.

Full list of available domains:

  • Documents - doc.new , docs.new , document.new
  • Tables - sheet.new , sheets.new , spreadsheet.new
  • Slides - slide.new , slides.new , deck.new , presentation.new
  • Forms - form.new , forms.new
  • Sites - site.new , sites.new , website.new
