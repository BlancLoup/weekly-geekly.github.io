Shooting Full HD video is now possible on the Canon 50d



Introduction About Magic Lantern


Magic Lantern is an open source development environment for the Canon 5D Mark II / 500d / 550D / 600D / 60D / 50D digital mirror. In fact, this is not a cracked or modified firmware, but an add-on that starts up with the original firmware, booting from the memory card each time the camera is turned on. The executable file is written to the flash card and automatically loaded into memory when the camera is turned on. The only change to the original firmware is the ability to download this very add-on from the memory card. Basically, the firmware is sharpened for shooting video (after installing it, Mark II gets the functionality comparable to professional video cameras RED ONE and Phantom 65, which are in completely different price categories), but there are quite a few new features for photos:
  • Bracketing - up to 9 frames with a shift from 0.5 to 5 EV / Ie. reportable HDR can be done without thinking at all.
  • Intervalometer - automatic shooting with a specified time interval.
  • Spotmeter - estimates the brightness of a microscopic object in the center of the frame.
  • LCD Remote Shot - replacement of the remote control. You close and open the sensor under the viewfinder - the camera takes a shot. And no shaking at high shutter speeds because you touched the shutter button with your hands. Because of this, I always pull the remote control with me, and if I forget, I use the descent delay.
  • Audio RemoteShot - the same thing, just by the sound (click of a finger, clap, etc.)
  • Trap Focus - release the shutter when something sharp appears in the focus area. Convenient when shooting animals, birds, butterflies. Developers write that you can shoot even a zipper.
  • Magic Zoom - automatic zoom in the window for the central part of the frame. It can be activated, for example, by rotating the focus ring. Those. exactly when it is necessary. No need to press any buttons.
  • Bulb timer - manual shutter speed from 1 second to 8 hours
  • Stack focus - focusing bracketing, you can infinitely increase the depth of field.
  • Displays the current focusing distance and the GRIP calculator - shows the hyperfocal (for the currently selected aperture) and the depth of field depth (for the selected aperture and focusing distance).
  • White balance in Kelvin
  • Performance test flash drives, showing the number of actuations of the shutter and much more.

The remaining features can be found in the official manual .

Installing firmware


Consider the process of installing the Magic Lantern firmware using the example of a Canon 50D camera. The function of shooting video was present in it initially, but was not available. Although implemented previously using image capture through the use of LiveView. The parameter for LiveView has three values: 0 - off, 1 - on, 2 - on. with the video. The standard menu allows you to change the parameter from 0 to 1 and vice versa, ML also switches between 1 and 2. It also removes the limit on recording time.

Conclusion



The maximum duration of one video with a standard bitrate is up to 14 minutes. When bitrate below the standard - up to 29 minutes 59 seconds.

As you know, in 50d there is no sound recording capability. But you can use an external recording device ( Zoom H1 , H2 or H4n ) and subsequently synchronize the audio track with the video.

If you decide to remove Magic Lantern, do the following:
- In the ML settings, disable video shooting
- Uncheck the boxes in EOScard
- Format the memory card

By the way, if you do not turn off the video recording function, then even after removing Magic Lantern and flashing, the ability to record video will remain with you.

If any errors occur, simply remove and reinsert the battery.

Congratulations! You have updated your camera and breathed a second life into it, thus discovering new horizons for creativity! I wish you success in sampling the pen with shooting video.
