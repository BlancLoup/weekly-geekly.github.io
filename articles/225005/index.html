Let's start from the beginning, with the box and configuration. To leave no doubt that the bracelet can work with any heart rate monitor with Bluetooth, but with a brand and even more so.



The bracelet itself looks quite stylish. Black plastic, shiny metal inserts, this accessory will look good on the hand of a businessman and on the wrist of an athlete. Alas, there is no straps of another color, and in principle it is impossible to change them. Therefore, if you do not like black color, you will not like the bracelet. By the way, about the strap. The thing is, the Polar has an original concept. On the inside of the bracelet there is an unusual connector that performs two functions. One - the obvious - is to connect the charger bracelet to charge the battery and synchronize data. The bracelet is charged for 30-60 minutes, but it can work for a week. However, about such indicators are now in all modern fitness trackers.
