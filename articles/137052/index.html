LitTime.ru - ribbon of book novelties

Hello, friends. It seems to be worth taking a pretentious note and talk about your mega-project, but it does not work. I just want to tell you about my project, which I worked for almost 3 years (I will leave the story for later). More precisely, about his first finished stage - the tape of books published in various publishing houses. I am writing because it may already be useful to some of you. So, LitTime.ru




Functional


At the present time on the site you will find news feed on books published in the largest publishing houses (AST, Eksmo, Alpha-book, Alphabet, etc.). Books carry the maximum amount of information a publisher gives (not all printers indicate an ISBN, for example) and can be found. For ease of viewing, we created a filter by genre, with the ability to cut off books-reissues (for example, if you read only in electronic form, then reprints may not be interesting for you). For registered users, the filter is saved; when you come to the site, you can only see what is interesting for you.

Also, so that you do not forget the book that interests you, we have created a list of ‚ÄúI will read‚Äù (located in your personal account). For example, I read fiction and non-fiction in turn, so it can sometimes take a month or more when I get to the book I am interested in. And if there are several? A list can help.

On the main page there are buttons "I will read."



When you click on the button, the book will be entered in the list we need. Logging in to your personal account, you will see it.



Clicking on the button "Read", you remove the book from the list.

Also, for the convenience of those who order books via the Internet, we display links to description and order pages in paper and digital book stores: Ozon.ru, My-shop.ru, Read.ru and LitRes.ru. You can find them by going to the page describing the book.

That's all that our site offers today. LitTime will evolve unceasingly, and I hope that the current functionality could be useful to someone, it will increase very soon.

Where we will develop


The next step in our development will be the bibliographies of the authors. Everyone is interested in knowing everything that the interested author wrote, in what order (mandatory or not) to read his cycles, to see the most popular and highly-rated works. This is the next step of our project.
And then ... Plans are great, but we will begin to implement them as much as we can. Now there are three of us - a programmer, a marketer and a content manager.
