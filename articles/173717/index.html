Launched the first two domains of the dot.UkR level

On March 19, 2013, the procedure for the delegation of the national Cyrillic top-level domain for Ukraine, the dot.UKR (.xn - j1amh) was successfully completed. The corresponding entry appeared in the root domain name system.

The first domains were test.ukr (xn - e1aybc.xn - j1amh) and mind.ukr (xn - l1ank7d.xn - j1amh).

The decision to delegate the dot. UKR to Ukraine was made by ICANN in early March. Thus, Ukraine has become the tenth country that received its non-Latin domain. In total, there are 12 such domains today .
