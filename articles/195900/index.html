DropBox now supports screenshots



You must upgrade the client to version 2.4.0 . in order to appear in the settings option screenshots.

Now by pressing printscreen , alt + printscreen , ctrl + alt + printscreen , ctrl + printscreen , a screenshot will be taken and saved to the DropBox folder. Combinations with ctrl allow you to immediately copy the screenshot link to the clipboard.

On Mac works with Makovsky hotkeys for screenshots.

upd: As the Firz habraiser suggests , this feature is already present in version 2.3.31
