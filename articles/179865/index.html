Big update of google maps



But the Google I / O conference hosted the presentation of a fully updated Google Maps service. So far, it can be viewed only by invites , but in the future this beauty will become publicly available.

What's new:

1. Google Earth is finally integrated with Google Maps, so now you do not need to install a separate program for viewing 3D Earth or the surface of Mars. Flight simulators and other games on the Google Earth API will probably work without installing the Google Earth plugin, as it is now.

2. In the Street View added uploading custom photos for the area that you are currently watching (carousel). A tape of photos is displayed at the bottom of the screen.



3. Individual map for each user : Google Maps analyzes the history of surfing, place of residence and place of work of the user (calculated automatically on the coordinates of the Android device), ratings and reviews, and then builds an individual map with recommendations.



While you have not sent an invite to the new Google Maps, you can watch a brief video demonstration.
