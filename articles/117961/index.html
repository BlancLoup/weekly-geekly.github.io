Examination of the FGU FIPS website

A week ago, a topic was published inviting people to examine the site of the Federal Institute of Industrial Property (FGU FIPS).

Perhaps Friday is not the best day to start working together, perhaps then it was just the first warm day, but the topic did not appear on the main one and none of the users took an active part in the examination. Please offer another day and / or time in the week when it is better to begin the examination of the next site (if there is still powder in the factory). While I offer the following examination to begin on Monday.

I, as the initiator, should set an example, so I carried out the examination myself, as I could. As a result, in about 3-4 hours of work I managed to find 11 advantages, 19 shortcomings, to express 11 wishes and note 7 interesting facts about the site under study.

I will tell you about the merits of the site and interesting facts here, and more detailed results with my recommendations can be found in a document posted on Google Docs (another link: source file in .docx format on ge.tt ).

Site advantages
  • A large amount of information is structured so that you can quickly find the right.
  • Very, very much information. Visible large, carefully executed work.
  • For each page shows the date of the update information.
  • Convenient contrast lighting highlighted menu item.
  • The texts of the pages themselves are pretty clean from the HTML / CSS point of view: no Word frills, unnecessary tags and attributes are noticed.
  • In the Inventions and utility models section, both application and application templates and examples of their completion for various cases are well cited. The same applies to the section Samples of applications, applications and petitions and examples of their filling .
  • Everywhere in all texts there are many references to other relevant documents, where you can find out the details. Email addresses are also valid links. Phone numbers are listed with the area code.
  • On all pages at the top of an adequate navigation chain.
  • A good search on the site, taking into account the morphology (such as).
  • Excellent detailed search with the ability to set a variety of criteria - did not see analogues on other similar sites.
  • Organized access to specialized databases (registries) at www1.fips.ru/wps/portal/Registers (sorry, there is no full-text search for them).
