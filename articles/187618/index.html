The first porn shot on Google Glass



The inevitable happened: porn stars James Dean and Andy San Dimas made a video on Google Glass, writes The Verge. In the detailed trailer (NSFW), the pair used Google Glass to record video and watch each other's eyes. The trailer is a parody of everything that you can imagine about futuristic glasses: X-ray vision, random queries to Google and the use of a face recognition database to search for anatomical details about a random walker.

In fact, given that Glass has been quite active in distributing a decent amount of time already, it is likely that this is not the first porn recorded using glasses. However, this is the first professional video with the participation of stars. Points, apparently, were provided by the author of Mother's Motherboard, Arikya Milliken, who was present during the shooting, and the company MiKandi, who developed the Tits and Glass application for glasses, which Google immediately deleted.

This video presents an interesting look into a possible future, in which directors abandon traditional cameras and simply put glasses on the heads of actors. If Glass becomes as popular as Google counts, then the person in them will not look like a fantastic character from William Gibson; it will be just a man with glasses.

Dean and San Dimas, apparently, were not fully convinced. According to Dean, he had long wanted to play with Google Glass, but during the shooting his glasses quickly fell off him and fell into San Dimas hair. In addition, in the process of shooting glasses began to heat up. (Not from the scene being shot - this happens if you record a video for too long.)
