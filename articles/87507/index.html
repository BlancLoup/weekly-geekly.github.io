First look at Xigmatek Achilles

Xigmatek has introduced a CPU cooler with an interesting name - Achilles to Russian users. Achilles - one of the main characters of the Trojan War. The perfect choice of name for the cooler, because the manufacturer is not the first cooler with the name in the style of "Military"



Zoom

The special features of the cooler include a quiet and sufficiently productive 120mm fan at 1500 rev / min, a comfortable mount and a well-thought-out design. Four copper heat pipes pass through thin aluminum fins and, thanks to the technology of direct contact with the processor heat diffuser lid, provide the best heat output.



Zoom

The cooler is compatible with all modern processor connectors, which makes it simply versatile. And the mount itself will not cause difficulties even for those users who first encountered installing a cooler on the processor.

Galaxy GTS250 with two graphics processors



Galaxy has introduced a graphics card based on the GPU on the basis of the GTS250. Galaxy has developed its own board design, on which the company's engineers managed to locate two such graphics cores. This approach to the structure of the video card has allowed to achieve the performance level of top-level video cards, such as GeForce GTX285.



Information about the cost of new items yet. And in general, the release of such a product is not completely clear, since the dates for the Fermi announcement are already close. I am afraid that with the release of Fermi, relevance to the hybrids of the past generation will be minimized.

G.Skill 2000 MHz CL6

A unique set of DDR3 RAM for the Intel P55 platform was introduced to G.Skill.



Zoom

The four-gigabyte kit impresses with its uniqueness. This is the first and so far the only RAM set at 2000 MHz capable of working with the regular timings of CL6 9-6-24 at a voltage of 1.65V.



Zoom

The memory is completed with a proprietary cooling system of the original design. A blue LED with a combination of a 3500 rpm fan makes the cooling system not only attractive to the human eye, but also quite effective. You can read the detailed test kit on OCClub.ru

ZBox from Zotac

At CeBIT 2010, at its booth, Zotac presented an interesting box called ZBox.



The basis for the new product based on Nvidia ION. At the heart of the box is a dual-core Intel Atom D510 processor - the second-generation Atom. More performance should add and the new Intel chipset - NM10. The fact that the nettop generation is developing by leaps and bounds, and the company's Zotac products are leading the way in ion-building, can not but rejoice.
