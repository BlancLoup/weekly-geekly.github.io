The twelfth Moscow habrakstrecha

Hello! New Year's holidays are over, everything is back to business, which means you can return to the tradition of regular habravstrech . I propose to meet tomorrow, February 2, at 20 o'clock in the Eastern Hall of the cafe-club "Marseille" on Novoslobodskaya.



By the way, it will be the 12th Moscow meeting in a row, not counting the separate ones in chronology of St. Petersburg, Kiev and Arkhangelsk. There is an idea to add some content to the meetings this time, so if anyone has ideas for a small presentation / report for a maximum of 3-5 minutes (about your work, project, technology) - write in the comments or email me .

Where: the eastern hall of the cafe-club "Marseille" , metro Novoslobodskaya, Moscow, st. Krasnoproletarskaya 16/1. How this hall looks like, you can look at the photo from the previous one there .

When: Thursday, February 2, 20 hours.

Mark your intention to come in the comments or on the event's facebook page so that you can see how many we will be. Do not write "I will be sure" and then do not come. But to play silence to the last is also not worth it.
