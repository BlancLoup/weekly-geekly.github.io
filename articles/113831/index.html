Review of HTC smartphones just shown at MWC 2011 - Wildfire S, Desire S and Incredible S

HTC decided to roll out a lot of devices this year on a mobile button! For Europe, prepared two new smartphones focused on social networks - Chacha and Salsa. And in our country three updated communicators will fall: Wildfire S, Desire S and Incredible S. What the letter at the end of the name hints at, I really did not understand, but there are suspicions that S is a derivative of superior or sensetive, since They are significantly different from their predecessors and have become entirely touch-sensitive, having lost not only all the mechanical buttons, but even the optical trackballs. But the star of the program was the seven-inch Flyer tablet, which, after the recent announcements by other manufacturers, now faces a difficult struggle for a place in the sun.





Wildfire s


The baby has become even more compact, although the screen diagonal has not changed - the same 3.2 ‚Äù, but already with a resolution of 320 x 480 pixels, which will allow you to run any applications from the Android Market without any problems. The filling, by the way, is also insignificant, but has improved: the amount of RAM has increased to 512 MB, and the processor has also changed - now it is Qualcom MSM 7227, operating at 600 MHz. Plus a new version of the Sense 2.1a obolchki, which hides Android 2.3 Gingerbread. HTC plans to sell its smallest smart in June at a price of 14-15 thousand rubles.





Desire s


The bestseller and the best smartphone of 2010 according to NOMOBILE.RU version also slightly changed, although it remained recognizable. Behind the letter S there is an unibody aluminum case, thanks to which those squeaks disappeared, which in the previous model were slightly annoying. In addition, the mechanical buttons, much loved by many, disappeared, in exchange for which sensory buttons came. The resolution and diagonal of the S-LCD screen remained the same - these are already familiar 480 x 800 pixels, placed in 3.7 ". But the updated iron. Now, in the depths of Desire S, the Qualcomm MSM 8255 gigahertz processor is paired with 768 MB of RAM. Yes, and the built-in flash array has also increased significantly - the user has 4 GB available for storing files and installing programs, which, however, everything can also be transferred to an SD card. It works all under the control of the Android OS 2.3, naturally with the proprietary shell of HTC Sense 2.1. The price of the issue is 23-24 thousand rubles, which, if desired, can be taken to the HTC Care store in April.







Incredible S


And this model before we have not sold, and was also known abroad as Verizon. It is assumed that the back cover of an unusual shape will be the main enticing factor (maybe, even, the distemper touch buttons, which, like the screen, rotate depending on the position of the smart). However, there will be something to drool and in addition to this. Stuffing Incredible S is also what you need! The four-inch display, made with S-LCD technology, has a resolution of 480 x 800 pixels, but otherwise it is not inferior to the updated Desire, and even surpasses it somewhere. Camera, for example, eight-megapixel with dual LED-flash and autofocus. But for some reason Sense is in it version 2.0, and the gadget runs on Android 2.2 Froyo.





Flyer


HTC understood that it was necessary to work hard on the tablet so that it could compete with other similar devices on an equal footing. And they tried! In the unibody package, there is a Qualcomm MSM 8255 processor running at 1.4 (!) GHz, 1 GB RAM, and 32 GB of main memory, which can be increased by as much by inserting a microSD card. All this suggests that the picture displayed on a seven-inch display (1024 x 600 pixels) will not slow down. That's just the question: for how much all this wonderful power is capable of sucking the energy out of a 4000 mAh battery? Hopefully, Android Honeycomb has moderate appetites. However, at first the tablet will run under Gingerbread, and only in the middle of summer it will be updated to version 3.0. Nevertheless, both OSes will be hidden under a special version of Sense Tablet. These high-tech 420 grams of aluminum will go on sale in late April at a price of about 30 thousand rubles.
