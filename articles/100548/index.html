10K Apart contest launched - create a 10 Kb web application and win $ 3000



The Mix Online team launches a competition for web developers called 10K Apart .

Under the terms of the contest, you must develop a website application with a total size of 10K, which includes all the resources: markup, scripts, styles, images and so on. However, you can use one of the following libraries: jQuery, Prototype and Typekit with CDN and their size will not be counted in the total size of the application site .

The work should be an HTML5 compliant website that will work in IE9 Preview 3, Firefox, and Webkit- based browsers.

Among the jury of the contest are famous people: Jeffrey Zeldman , Eric Meyer , Nicole Sullivan , Whitney Hess , Jeremy Keith .
