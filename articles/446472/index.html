Global update of the results display Lamptest.ru

Today I am pleased to present to you a completely redesigned page displaying the results of testing LED lamps at Lamptest.ru . Dynamic table update, smart search, tooltips, column management, visual display of the lamp type - these are not all changes.


The display of the results table can now be customized to your liking. You can turn on or off any column, select the number of rows displayed on the page, turn on or off the color highlighting the difference between the tested parameters and the declared ones. There are three presets, including a minimal display of information, a standard display, and a display of all columns. There is a tooltip for each column header.



Tooltips work both in the table and in the filter window. For the cap field, a hint was made in the form of a picture with photos of all found caps (I hope we will do the same for different lamp shapes).


The color illumination allows you to make the perception of the declared parameters more comfortable: the color temperature is highlighted in blue, gray or yellow (cold-neutral-warm light), non-standard lamp supply voltages (12 and 24 V) are highlighted in red, gray is a sign that the lamp is matte , mustard - filament lamps. Each of these elements has a separate tooltip.


When you hover over the photo icon, a small pop-up photo of the lamp is shown.


Of course, when you click on this icon, a large photo opens.
As before, the table can be sorted by any parameter in the forward and reverse order by clicking on the header of the corresponding parameter.

The shape of the filters has also undergone great changes. All parameters are now located vertically. For each parameter there is a tooltip. Brands are shown in a long list with scrolling, and the list is divided into two parts: at the beginning of the 15 brands whose lamps have been tested the most, then all other brands.


Intelligent search supports enumerations, keywords, denials, masks, and even queries in ordinary language, such as ‚Äúa good dimming candle‚Äù.


In addition to all the display updates, two new columns appeared in the table: Rf and Rg are the Fidelity and Gamut color rendering indices of the new standard for measuring the color rendering of lighting TM-30-15. They are not yet in the cards of the lamps and they are indicated only for the last hundred tested lamps, but soon they will appear in the cards and will be added for all the lamps tested in the last year.

A new system for displaying results can be compared with the old one, still available at lamptest.ru/results .
