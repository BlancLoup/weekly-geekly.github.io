Archos Attached a Photo of Lenovo Yoga to the Transformer Laptop Press Release

French electronics maker Archos has published a press release about the new Flip hybrid laptop with Windows 10, using a photo of a Lenovo Yoga 11S laptop with Windows 8 on the screen.


[ Screenshot ]

Archos produces low-cost smartphones and tablets, but wants to enter a new segment - the laptop market. The company announced a budget hybrid laptop Archos Flip for two hundred and fifty euros. It runs on a quad-core Intel Cherry Trail 1.4 GHz processor, has 32 gigabytes of memory and 2 gigabytes of RAM. The display is 11.6 inches with a resolution of 1366x768. Battery - 8000 mAh. The operating system of the future gadget is Windows 10.

How the new laptop will look like - we will see at IFA 2015 in Berlin. While the company is replicating information about the new product, using someone else's photo - Lenovo Yoga 11S. In Yoga 11S use Intel Core i3, i5 or i7 - up to 1.5 GHz, from 4 to 8 gigabytes of RAM and from 128 to 259 gigabytes of SSD. The cost of the device at the time of launch in 2013 started from 799 dollars.

The photo shows Lenovo Yoga 11S with Windows 8, and Archos plans to start selling Flip with Windows 10 out of the box.
