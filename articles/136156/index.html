Technical review of the smartphone Samsung S5830 Galaxy Ace

Samsung S5830 Galaxy Ace can be called a bestseller among Samsung smartphones worth up to 10 thousand rubles, in which the ratio of price, quality and functionality just can not but rejoice.



The key features of the gadget are a 3.5-inch HVGA screen with a resolution of 320x480 pixels (alas, only the viewing angles leave much to be desired), a decent quality camera and Wi-Fi with DLNA support.

It is impossible not to notice that the appeal of the Galaxy Ace appearance is due to the sensational Apple iPhone 4: similarities are visible to the naked eye. The dimensions of the smartphone from Samsung are: 112.4x59.9x11.5 mm and weight of 113 g. The dimensions are quite ergonomic: the phone is equally comfortable to hold in hand and carry in pockets of clothing. The assembly of the device is of impeccable quality.



Another positive detail is that the device is equipped with an additional back panel, so that the user can choose between a glossy white and a rough black back surface.

The device has the following stuffing: a Qualcomm MSM7227 processor with a frequency of 800 MHz and 256 MB of RAM, a graphics accelerator Adreno 200, Google's Android operating system, built-in 160 MB for free filling and the ability to expand the volume with memory cards. We have to admit that this is not the most advanced functionality, but the Samsung S5830 Galaxy Ace works flawlessly.

Detailed instructions for parsing



We begin to disassemble

Analysis of the phone starts with a simple one: remove the back cover, made of a fairly flexible plastic, and take out the battery. The capacity of the Li-Ion battery is 1350 mAh and is typical for smartphones. On average, the battery lasts a day for normal use: as usual, of course, but I would like more.



Rear panel

Then unscrew the 7 fixing screws around the perimeter of the device, then hook on all sides and remove the back panel (the part looks pretty durable), then proceed to the system board.





Motherboard

You must first disconnect the cable and all side buttons. Then we lift and unfold the board, which can be fully removed by clicking the display module cable. We disconnect the camera from the board (good quality for the Samsung device, by the way: 5 MP, flash, digital Zoom and autofocus are available), then a polyphonic module with a headset connector (by the way, the volume of the speaker on the phone is enough for the room, which is not possible say about a noisy street).



The front side of the motherboard holds loops with buttons, a microSD memory card reader (TransFlash, up to 32 GB), a processor, flash, a radio unit, a power controller, and a membrane with a Home button.



The reverse side of the board contains a system connector, a SIM card reader, a battery connector and a microphone.



We finish the internal review

The display module in this model is nonseparable. Therefore, if the owner of Galaxy Ace is not lucky enough to break the screen, then it will have to be changed along with the module, which is obviously not cheap.



Summary

Samsung S5830 Galaxy Ace is an affordable, high-quality and functional solution with a universal design and minor flaws.

Main advantages:
  • price;
  • quality;
  • performance (within this segment of smartphones);
  • ergonomics;
  • camera.


The main disadvantages:
  • screen quality;
  • No camera button.
