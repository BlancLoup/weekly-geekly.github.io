4. Europe-Asia

In Yekaterinburg there is a unique opportunity to visit simultaneously in two parts of the world: one foot in Europe, the other in Asia. 5. Constructivism is our all

In Yekaterinburg, about 140 constructivist buildings, sometimes occupying entire neighborhoods. Such concentration of monuments of Soviet constructivism, perhaps, you will not see anywhere else: the complex of the town of the Chekists, the First House of the City Council, the General Post Office, the house of Uralsnabtorg on Lenin Avenue, the sports complex Dynamo.
