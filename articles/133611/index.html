In order to disassemble the iPad tablet you will need:
- A special hair dryer, to soften the plastic and weaken the action of the adhesive.
- Plastic tool for separating the front panel (sold in spare parts stores for phones).
- Plastic mediators, for the convenience of separating the panel (you can use everything that is at hand and has similar qualities).
- Special screwdrivers of small size (can be ordered online, or use kits for mobile phones).

Getting Started:
At the initial stage, you will need to separate the front panel of the device, for this you need to heat the area to the right (home button below) of the home button with a hairdryer for easy separation. Be careful when directing a stream of hot air, the button may be damaged if it is very hot.

Next, you need to separate the front panel using mediators, for more details on how to do this, see the photos.









After separating the front panel, it is necessary to unscrew the 4 screws securing the device screen. For this, you will need a small Phillips screwdriver, better if it comes with a magnetic tip. Also, it is better to think in advance where you will put the screws, so as not to lose them, and then anything can happen.

Next, re-use the plastic tool to separate the plumes of the screen, touch-screen and all the rest. Again, look at the photos to understand which cables need to be disconnected.














Next on the photo, red, showing the bolts that need to be unscrewed, look carefully







In order to remove the battery it is better to heat the tablet behind the hair dryer.







Assembling the tablet is made in the reverse order. To glue, warm the tablet again at the edges. If you accidentally burned glue, you should use a special double-sided tape, you can buy it in the store of radio components.
