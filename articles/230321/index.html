Startup Day at Odessa Innovation Week



July 27, the logical conclusion of Odessa Innovation Week will be StartUp Day . We will summarize the hackathon GeeksLab: E-government / City Projects and talk about the future development of e-government in Ukraine. Moreover, it is not too late to assemble a team and register for a hackathon!

Visit Odessa StartUp Day is a must if you:
  • Interested in novelties of engineering and love to design and invent.
    You will have a unique opportunity to test innovative solutions in hardware startups and the latest gadgets. On the eve of the opening of the first hackerspace in Odessa, we will present HubLab and talk about the future of 3D printing, as well as dive into the virtual reality world of Oculus Rift games.
  • You are thinking about a new IT specialization and, as we believe, IT is the future of our economy.
    We are waiting for you at the presentation of the IT education development fund of the BrainBasket Foundation, which will be conducted by board member Roman Khmil. The main goal of the fund is to focus on the development of the IT industry, which will make it possible to become the largest exporter in 10 years, generating more than $ 20 billion in export earnings, which will make Ukraine a ‚Äúcountry of IT people‚Äù.
  • Looking for funding and investors for your project.
    Let's talk about alternative investment - crowdfunding with a new platform for raising funds to support creative solutions and startups - Na-Starte with the project's CEO Igor Bazarsky. Na-Starte specialists will also advise you on how to make your project exactly successful, on what to pay attention and to put accents.
  • Want to learn more about startup incubators, accelerators and how they can help in the development of the project.
    Catch the opportunity to talk with industry leaders from GrowthUp, Wannabiz, Tolstoy Camp.
  • Want to test the business model of the project for strength.
    Register as a participant in the Startup Alley and the most interesting projects will be selected for presentation on the alleys and participation in the StartUp Crash Test. This is a great opportunity to get practical advice and criticism from the audience, to find partners and like-minded people, to get a media coverage. In addition, SCT is a platform for the selection of local angel and venture capital investors, thereby acting as a platform for informal communication for the investment and business community.
  • Need people in the team, new acquaintances and effective networking?
    July 27 - we invite everyone to afterparty Odessa Innovation Week! And a lot of bonuses from partners.


All details and registration on the event website at: geekslab.co/events/odessa-startupday .

Students and startups who wish to participate in StartUp Day are given a 50% discount . In order to take advantage of the discount, enter the promo code: 972343453 in the registration form.
