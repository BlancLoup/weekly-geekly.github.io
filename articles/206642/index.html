New Year's quest Radmin

New Year's quest Radmin continues!

Each participant who successfully passes the quest will receive a license for Radmin 3 as a gift! The main prize - the Seagate external hard disk with a capacity of 4 TB - will go to the one who will complete all the quests of the quest first. Another 4Tb external hard drive will be played randomly among all participants who successfully completed the quest.

To participate, you must go through three stages of the quest, connect via Radmin to a remote computer of the company Famatech and find out the secret key.

There are 3 days left until the end of receiving applications for participation in the Radmin New Year's quest. We remind you that you can complete the quest and apply for prizes until December 26 inclusive. Due to numerous requests from the participants, we decided to give a small hint: the password should be meaningful.

Immediately after the announcement of the start of the contest, thousands of participants began simultaneously trying to establish a connection with a remote computer via Radmin. Frankly, we did not expect such interest from the participants, but now we rented a more powerful web-server so that the participants did not have any difficulty in connecting.

You can take part in the competition and learn more about the conditions on the special page: www.radmin.ru/newyear2014.php .

Do not give up, keep looking for the key, and the victory will be yours! Looking forward to your responses!
