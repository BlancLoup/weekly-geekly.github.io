Where do special offers come from



Every day we publish interesting announcements about cheap flights to Germany, Poland, the Czech Republic, Holland, Austria, France, Italy, Spain, Croatia, the UK, the USA and even to Africa, India and various islands on our twitter. Where do such prices come from? How it works?

The fact is that the airlines for which Eviterra is looking for often carry out various actions to reduce prices for their flights, for example, during the holiday season or on holidays, which is used by our employee Ivan, who regularly looks at the table of flights and looks for attractive offers. The system takes into account all the flights ever shown and accumulates statistics to calculate the average price in any direction. The next time a new airline takes effect from any airline, the site will immediately mark a cheap offer and show how much cheaper it is in this direction.
