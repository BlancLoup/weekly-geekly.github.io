Samsung revealed the secret SGS3 processor


Samsung today managed to "boast" of its new processor for the next generation of smartphones "Galaxy". The processor will be called Exynos 4 Quad and will have 4 cores, with a clock frequency of 1.4GHz. Exynos 4 Quad is built on the ARM Cortex A9 architecture. It is produced by 32-nanometer process technology.
The company claims that the performance of the "new" processor is twice ahead of the Samsung Galaxy SII. It also consumes 20 percent less energy than SGSII. The smartphone with the new processor will be able to record video in 1080p resolution with a frequency of up to 30 frames / sec.
Samsung Galaxy SIII will be presented in London on May 3, and apparently, we will be something to watch. It is also quite possible that Samsung will present its cloud service called S-Cloud at this conference.
"New" smartphone may well become the main competitor of the iPhone 5.
