Rest can not work

New year has passed. And Christmas. And even the old New Year. Beloved by us " from 1 to 13th ", no matter how desirable they are, still ended with a well-deserved rest. Someone had a great time to rest, and someone used long holidays to work on his startup. And again, the week began on Monday, which means hello - weekdays, gray sky over Moscow, mud under your feet, pain in the eyes of the monitor and waiting for the weekend / holiday / vacation / vacation (to be underlined).


And what if you combine rest and work?
For example, to work somewhere on the seashore or ocean in a country where the number of sunny days approaches 350? Say in Cyprus or in Spain: working in comfortable conditions, the opportunity to bathe instead of contemplating the corner of a neighboring house during breaks, the use of international relations and a significant reduction in costs - this is not a complete list of possible advantages.
For example, go to Thailand, as the LinguaLeo project team did. The guys rested there for half a year, and returned with ready service, which they wrote about more than once (I highly recommend reading their story). For themselves, they have chosen the path of self-organization of space and infrastructure. The disadvantage of this option is its duration: the guys spent a few weeks on this. There is another option for California, but here you need to remember the possible difficulties in obtaining a visa.
Or you can go to work on a project in Spain, as was done by the participants of the Spanish Field Village first priority business accelerator, Priority Project, for example, the Queryhunter project, who shared his impressions of staying there. Most recently, we have summed up the results of the 1st exit in Moscow. View information about the event and presented projects here .
