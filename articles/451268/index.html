Victor Gamov about Kafka Streams IQ on jug.msk.ru

May 8, 2019 at a meeting of the Moscow community of Java-developers jug.msk.ru Victor Gamow told about Kafka Streams IQ ( Interactive Queries ).



About speaker


Victor speaks for the fourth time at jug.msk.ru , his previous reports were about Nashorn ( November 13, 2014 ), Hazelcast ( May 4, 2016 ), Apache Kafka ( April 23, 2018 ). Like a year ago, currently working at Confluent , but now as a Developer Advocate .

Co-founder and host of the popular podcast Debriefing , co-author of the book Enterprise Web Development , speaker of many conferences (including those organized by JUG.ru Group ). Victor's site (with a technical blog in the same place), video reports on YouTube , presentation files on Speaker Deck and Notist , Habr , Twitter and GitHub .
