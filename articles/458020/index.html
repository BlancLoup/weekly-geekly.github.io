Digest of July IT events



Summer is coming to the middle, there is a long-awaited heat and brings consequences: community activity decreases slightly, and conferences start to be held somewhere closer to greenery and water. Nevertheless, we have gathered another portion of events, where there are hackathons, and international congresses, and festivals, and, of course, small meetings on interests.

Go meetup

When: June 29
Where: Kazan, st. Aivazovsky, 3a
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

Opportunity for developers on Go to spend another evening in good company with communication, sweepstakes, souvenirs, and most importantly - reports. The speakers will share the experience of organizing the routine, developing the request of the validator to the logical finale, accurate error handling in microservices, and at the same time they will tell you why templates are needed for them and how they are done.

PhotoHack NSK

When: June 29-30
Where: Novosibirsk, st. Deputy, 46, BC "Cititsentr"
Conditions of participation: free of charge, registration is required

Novosibirsk developers can still make it to the last car of the initial phase of the hackathon to develop an image generator. The task for teams is to create a prototype of a viral web service that creates posts for Facebook and descriptions of them. The toolkit can be used any, but the organizers provide an API for photo processing and software for creating photo masks. Each team should have one computer with Adobe Photoshop installed. The prize fund is 600 000 rubles.

When: June 30
Where: Moscow, st. 2nd Chernogryazskaya, 6, k.2

Pilot meeting of Moscow experts in cryptography, the opportunity to meet with the organizers and colleagues in the shop, as well as participate in shaping the agenda for future meetings. The program is supposed to have elements of improvisation: the organizer will prepare an introductory presentation, after which the conversation will switch to areas interesting to the audience. Reports from the participants are welcome.

Open Source Database Meetup

When: July 1
Where: Moscow, Leningrad Prospect., 39, p. 79
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Like the Moscow MySQL User Group mitapas, only more information and a pronounced learning orientation. Three-hour high-level practitioners will talk about cases that have been resolved when working with open DBMSs, from MySQL reconciliation with high loads to SWIM protocol features.

Luxoft Open House

When: July 3
Where: Moscow, 1st Volokolamsky passage, 10, p. 3
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Luxoft invites you to see where the developers of the team work and how, to ask them about the projects and just walk around the office. Throughout the evening, staff will make presentations (topics are varied - building a trading platform, horizontal scaling without changing the architecture, interaction of services within Spring Cloud, etc.), so guests can choose and listen to their presentations.

IT CONNECT Tver

When: July 4
Where: Tver, st. Soviet, 7
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Network and network security problems with examples from Fortinet, Juniper Networks, Extreme Networks and other global manufacturers. Analysis of useful cases and a little less formal communication for beer and snacks. Registration closes eight hours before the event (18:00 local time), you can come at any time.

Geek picnic

When: July 6-7
Where: St. Petersburg, Primorsky Victory Park
Participation conditions: from 1300 rub.

A large open-air festival for anyone interested in technology and innovation. The theme of the year this time sounds tempting - "To live forever." Developers will be especially interested in the Campus section, where the educational content of a scientific and technical nature is presented. The section includes the Gamedev, AI, Hard IT platforms, as well as a number of sites with projects and lectures on different branches of science - from space to the very technologies of immortality. You can take a break from self-education in the Picnic section with different types of entertainment (book zone, meetings with authors, desktops, retrogaming, cinema), and get a sharp impression in the WOW !, sections where everything is collected - world-famous celebrities, four-legged robots and races on thrash cars.

iOS Party

When: July 9
Where: Ekaterinburg, st. Khokhryakova, 10
Participation conditions: free, registration required

The traditional summer meeting of iOS-developers in the office of Yandex. This time, the community has much to discuss: after WWDC 2019, it became clear that with the introduction of the new framework, SwiftUI, serious changes are coming for all the processes and development tools. About these changes and plans for the coming year and talk. Also, the speakers, who attended the conference in person, will share their impressions and tell you more about the announcements.

Mitap for Java development

When: July 9
Where: Moscow, Middle Kalitnikovskaya, 28, p.4
Participation conditions: free, registration required

X5 Retail Group developers are looking for colleagues who also write in Java to exchange life stories and learn something new. How did the Optional class evolve from version to version? Is Kotlin so good, as is commonly believed? How many abstractions do I need to plan a car for a flight? These and other questions will be answered at a meeting at the company's office.

Hydra 2019

When: July 11-12
Where: St. Petersburg, Victory Square, 1
Conditions of participation: from 215 euros

International event about distributed systems. Among the participants are experts from Russian and foreign universities and market leaders. Reports are divided into two streams and are marked in the program by markers corresponding to different directions and levels of complexity. At the moment, about twenty presentations have already been announced: data synchronization, reduced waiting time, testing, and best data storage methods.

PeerLab Kirov Meetup

When: July 13-14
Where: Kirov, Slobodskoy district, Settlement of programmers
Participation conditions: from 1250 rub.

Conference on the interaction between IT specialists in different scenarios: teamwork, communication at interviews, speaking to an audience at community meetings or at internal events. Speakers (developers in leadership positions, product managers, psychologists) will help students understand how and why develop the ability to communicate thought and make contact. The conference will be held outside the city - tent camp, volleyball, beach, barbeque attached.

404 CAMP

When: July 19-21
Where: Samara, camping-park "Volga Discovery" (transfer from the stop Barboshina Polyana)
Participation conditions: from 1200 rub.

A traditional summer outdoor conference, where the balance is maintained between good rest in nature and deep immersion in the problems of development. More than five hundred experts will gather to, first of all, discuss the transition to the new Angular, TensorFlow Lite on Swift, neural networks, the advantage of open source, Vue.js 3, and so on, but in the meantime participate in visas and sports competitions, learn to suberfighting, arm wrestling and cooking shawarma, go kayaking and listen to DJs.

Meet & Hack 2019

When: July 22-26
Where: Innopolis, st. University, 1
Participation conditions: free on the basis of selection

Complex workshop for students, graduate students and novice developers who want to improve their level. The first two days of the event will be given to a series of workshops from employees of the Open Mobile Platform, where students will get acquainted with various development tools and basic APIs, as well as receive recommendations on the design of interfaces. Then the practical part will follow - hakaton, where students will be able to apply new knowledge, united in teams. To participate, you must pass a qualifying task, which will be sent to the post after registration.
