For antivirus, you can pay "SMS"

The Allsoft online store has expanded the range of possible ways to pay for purchases - a mobile payment system (PayCash) is connected, allowing you to pay for the Dr.Web anti- virus license in any part of Russia where there is a cellular connection.

According to CyberSecurity.ru , to make a payment, no computer or Internet access is required. It is enough to send an SMS with the necessary team, and the payment will be made in real time. Payment is possible from any city where operators provide roaming service.
