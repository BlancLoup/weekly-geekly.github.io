How to check the site for free design errors that prevent sell



Last time, I wrote about site usability errors that repel visitors and reduce conversion. Today I will tell how to find these errors.

Difficult way:
  1. Take in one hand the site, in another directory of common errors .
  2. Carefully scroll through the pages, noting problems from the catalog.
  3. For each problem to figure out how to do better.
  4. Give the job to a freelancer or employee to fix it.

And now simple:

  1. Write in the comments to this article the address of your site with the mark "full lynch".
  2. Get a free review from a regular visitor and professional designer .
  3. To give a review to a freelancer or employee, to correct the comments.

We take into work only one free application, so if there are several interested people, we will choose the site randomly.

If you want to take advantage of this offer, make sure that you:
  1. You have the desire and authority to actually change the site (and not just read the review and put it on the shelf).
  2. We agree that the review will be published on the main Design Lincher.
  3. We are ready to leave a public review: how useful the review was, how changes affected the parameters of visits and conversion.

If you are not ready for such serious work - it does not matter: there are 5 more places for free checking in the error catalog (without a review of the visitor and designer). The only condition is to leave a comment with the site address. If there are more than five volunteers, we will choose the sites by lot.

Comments are accepted until 12:00 on Tuesday, we will prepare reviews by the end of the week.

UPD. Thank you for the application! Choose sites for full lynch and check the catalog until the end of Tuesday.
