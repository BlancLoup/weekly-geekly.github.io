The right IT conference. Ural version: DUMP

What makes a good IT conference?

Working Wi-Fi + interesting practical cases + a lot of professional communication and coffee.

The recipe is simple, but for some reason a tasty dish is rarely obtained. Another attempt to reach the ideal will be made on May 30 in Yekaterinburg. Yandex, SKB Kontur, IT-People.ru, NAUMEN, E1.RU, Jetstyle, Artsofte participate in the preparation. Places still have.

Why this time should work?

Ekaterinburg is the third most developed city in the country (we know that Novosibirsk and Nizhny have a different opinion on this score :).

Ural developers have accumulated vast experience, which is not often presented in the capitals, while local conferences simply have not been. There are interesting developments in the field of highly loaded systems (up to 1 million business users) and usability of complex services (including for Yandex), in the field of flexible telephony management systems and cartographic services, etc. In the end, just wondering how to solve their problems in terms of organizing the development of "the same guys from the Urals", and not just Muscovites with their high loads and budgets.

The whole program is divided into 4 sections: "Software Architecture", "Web Development", "Management" and "Interface Development". Each has its own feature and its own fan. There will be round tables on the development and design of API, practical Agile, interfaces of the future, master classes on methods of prototyping, combating packaging and material incentives for programmers. And much more interesting.
