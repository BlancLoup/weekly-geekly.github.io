Pick Up and Return - a new service from ASUS

Failure of equipment is always stress, and a trip to a service center is a double amount of stress, and even a waste of time, effort and money. The new service ASUS - Pick Up and Return will help to avoid all this. If problems arise, owners of ASUS laptops will not have to go to a service center - the laptop will be taken away, repaired and returned to the owner in the shortest possible time.



Call the ASUS Technical Support Center or the regional service center and report a breakdown. The courier will arrive and pick up the laptop for repair no later than the next working day, and will pre-arrange the arrival time by phone.

After the repair is over, they will call you and at a convenient time for you the courier will deliver the repaired laptop directly to your home. If the case is recognized as non-warranty, here, alas, you will need to personally visit the service center to pick up a laptop or agree on the conditions of non-warranty repair.

Convenient, right?

At the moment, the program applies to models of laptops ASUS NX90J / U33J / U53J / U43J and operates in the following cities: Moscow, Volgograd, Voronezh, Yekaterinburg, Kazan, Krasnodar, Krasnoyarsk, Nizhny Novgorod, Novosibirsk, Omsk, Perm, Rostov-on-Don , St. Petersburg, Samara, Saratov, Ufa, Chelyabinsk. Hopefully both of these lists will expand soon.

The contact phone number of the service center of your city is listed on the ASUS Technical Support website .
