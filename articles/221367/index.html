Many have already read about Netsukuku and believed that the project was dead.
Yes, the story of this project is complex - created in C, was rewritten in Python and, after the refusal of development by the maintainers, was abandoned in 2009.
In 2011, one unknown developer announced that he would rewrite everything to Vala.

And now, on March 5, 2014, exactly three years later, the porting was completed .
At the moment, some features are not implemented, but packages for OpenWRT are already available ( instruction ).

And now, the fate of Netsukuku is once again under threat!


According to a report of April 14, the developer is experiencing regular financial difficulties.
