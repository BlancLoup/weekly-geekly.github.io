Total "Gadzhetizatsiya" population

Hi, Habr!

We have collected here such juicy material that we cannot but share it with you. Inside there are a lot of numbers describing the situation with users of different gadgets!



In the modern world, the number of personal electronic devices is constantly growing. In daily communication, an active person is not limited to just one device. According to a global study by Kaspersky Lab 1 , an average of 3.3 personal electronic devices with Internet access and the ability to store large amounts of information per family in Russia .

The highest rate of the countries studied was found in Saudi Arabia, where 4.3 households per household. The least amount of gadgets in the house turned out to be in Romania - only 2.6 . Russia in terms of security devices was on the same positions with Mexico and China.

pros

From the modern set of personal electronic devices, the most popular in Russia is the desktop PC. Almost 66% of respondents confirmed its presence in the house. And 18.8% of respondents indicated that they have two or more desktops.

Laptops also do not lose their relevance due to the combination of high power and mobility: every second family uses them - 53.2% . 16.2% of respondents have two or more such devices in the house.

More than half of the respondents were owners of ‚Äúsmart phones‚Äù - 52.5% , while 24.5% of respondents reported that there are two or more smartphones in their families.

As for such a new-fashioned gadget, like a tablet PC, there are not as many of them as it seems! Only every tenth respondent, namely 10.7% , has already acquired it, and almost as many research participants ( 11.3% ) are going to acquire them in the next six months.

Already, in Russia, almost 6.5% of families in big cities are happy owners of a full set of modern gadgets (home PC, laptop, smartphone, tablet).

Minuses

Meanwhile, such popularity of personal electronic devices opens up more and more opportunities for cybercriminals. As you know firsthand, every day a user makes purchases online, communicates in social networks and chat rooms, checks email and surfs on the Internet. To do this, both home PC or laptop, and a smartphone or tablet on different operating systems can be used equally effectively.

In 2011, Kaspersky Lab experts observed an intensive growth of malware for most platforms. Thus, the Windows operating system that dominates among PC users is equally popular among virus writers: the malware for it has increased by 80% .

Malware for Mac OS also showed a significant increase of 35% .

Among mobile operating systems, the most increased number of malicious software for 4 platforms. Closes the rating of Windows Mobile with 13% . Bronze receives Symbian OS with 16% . The Java2ME environment is still a tasty morsel for virus writers, because most simple mobile phones work on its base. The amount of malware for it has increased by as much as 160% .
