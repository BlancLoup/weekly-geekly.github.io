Hello! Everyone who heard or did not hear about MongoDB and NoSQL and wants to know when to use them and how to do it correctly. I will also talk about a different type of thinking that is needed in order to rebuild the data into more appropriate "documents", compared to relational databases.


Are you one of the PHP team? Could you tell us a little about yourself? How did you do PHP?
