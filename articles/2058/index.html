Explosion at PayPal Headquarters

On Tuesday evening, an unidentified explosive device worked at PayPal headquarters in San Jose, California, USA. According to CBS , at the time of the explosion, 26 employees were in the office. According to their stories, several windows were broken out as a result of the explosion.

An explosive device was laid near the exit from the building. The guards say that they heard several explosions. San Jose authorities report that there was only one explosion, which damaged the glass and the frame of one of the windows.
