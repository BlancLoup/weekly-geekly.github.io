Second issue of StartupIndex Review

On May 20, the second issue of StartupIndex Review, a quarterly analytical collection of innovative projects in the field of Internet and New Media, will be released. In addition to the description of the projects, the magazine will, as always, include reviews of promising segments and interviews with one of the industry people.

Those who hear about the publication for the first time, can learn the details on the company's website StartupIndex or from the announcement of the first number on HabraHabre .

This time, we focused our attention on two markets that are actively developing today: web applications based on the SaaS model and children's entertainment and game projects. On the trends and prospects of these segments - two articles with an overview of key players and an analysis of investment-attractive areas. Among the projects that are included in the second number are Smesharikov Country , Megaplan , MoySklad , Risovas'ka , Flaphone , Gooroo , Vstrecher and others. Here, for each project, a description of its concept and development plan, market analysis, financial indicators and information about team members are given.
