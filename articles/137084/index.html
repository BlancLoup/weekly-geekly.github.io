Not a computer mouse and high technology

In ancient times, the word "mouse" was interpreted unequivocally, then came the new year, other manners and now have to mark - the article is not about a computer mouse.

Sometimes people encounter mice. Well, if this meeting is random and fleeting, but it happens that mice like your accommodation and they start to consider it theirs, and this is not very good for both. We have to reinvent the wheel ...

Option 1. MehMag (mechanical, magnetic).
While serving as an officer, he rented a room in the attic in the private sector with one very good grandmother. Then the officers did not live very well, I hope that now they are provided with housing and high salaries!
Once, with the onset of cold weather, a mouse family settled in the attic. Would live quietly, so no - got into the habit of dragging our cereals.
We are not greedy people, but because they spoil the products!
I'm tired ... I treat mice with disgust, and maybe I just can't cook them?
He put on his brains ... the young one was still an officer, not numb. How to catch and not touch these creatures?
I took a tin container from the VN-6 vacuum oil, roughly speaking - a high square bucket and cut off the upper part. Next put the box. On the edge of the box set the fixed bar, secured it with a cunning plate with an axis of rotation. To keep the plate in a horizontal position, it made a magnetic lock that adjusted a couple of grams of weight at the end. The design is clear from the figure.


The magnet holds the bar hard, but at one point (fine for us, this moment is so-so), when the mouse reached the bait and dreamed of eating it, he was no longer able to keep the mass multiplied by the length of the lever and ...
The earth leaves the ground, the mouse experiences a short-term state of weightlessness and, like in an aquapark, flops into a pool of water. Pour there is not sour cream, beat your paws, do not hit - you will not get butter. Cruel of course ... but what to do?
In the morning he set a trap and left for the service, in the evening he came back from the service - the caught mouse to the street, the water in the tank, the bar to the platoon, the seed to the edge, he went to sleep. In the morning, another swam!
Once there was an interesting case ... I come, and then there are two of them - ran to the race for seed? Or the second in the MOE worked?
In a very short period I caught 13 pieces, saved a supply of cereals, and saved the family from starvation!
