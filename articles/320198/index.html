Welcome to Moscow CocoaHeads January 27



On the last Friday of January, the Moscow CocoaHeads iOS developer will meet. We will gather in our Moscow office to listen to experts and talk on professional topics. In the program of the meeting three reports, read the details under the cut.

Meeting program:

- Quick start iOS-applications on the example of iOS Mail Mail.Ru
Nikolay Morev, Mail.Ru Group

Nikolay will share his own experience about the techniques and tricks of speeding up apps for iOS, specific recipes. What can and should be optimized? How to reduce the time from clicking on the icon to display the launch screen? What are performance analysis tools (not just Time Profiler)?

- How to make friends with the robot
Andrey Volodin, Cocos2d-ObjC Lead Developer

In the report, Andrew will tell you how to release the application on Swift in the Google Play Market.

- Mobile interfaces of the future
Alexander Zimin, Uberchord Engineering

Alexander will talk about his presentation of the future mobile interfaces. For technical clarity, abstract code examples will be shown.

Also at the meeting you will find a quiz, and AppCode licenses will be drawn. The event starts at 19:00. Address: Moscow, m. Airport, Leningradsky Prospect, 39, p.79. Pre-registration is required. For those who will not be able to attend the meeting, an online broadcast will be organized. Waiting for you!
