SMM metrics


A very important point is the definition of the success metrics of your activity. Any action can be estimated and counted. How effective is engaging in groups, statistics of advertising campaigns will show. How good and useful the content is group growth. To assess the work of a community manager will help the level of involvement in the group. How many transitions to the site, Google Analytics or Yandex.Metrica will show with properly configured targets and marked links. Make a list in advance of the metrics to be measured, and watch them from the very first day.

Direct and indirect social performance indicators

To direct we will assign:
  • monthly increase, which shows the number of new members of the group or new subscribers, as well as their degree of interest in the brand and content;
  • involvement, which can be calculated as the percentage of users participating in the life of the page, to the total number of subscribers;
  • The number of unique group visitors shows the number of group views by new users who have not previously interacted with the pages of the brand. The higher the number, the higher the quality of engagement and the quality of the content.

By indirect usually include the number of users who came to the site from the social network. These indicators characterize the social network as a source of attracting users to the site. This source should be assessed by the following parameters:
  • the total number of users who came to the site from this channel;
  • time spent on the site by such user;
  • number of pages viewed;
  • the duration of the visit;
  • bounce rate;
  • percentage of new users.

The time on the site, the number of pages viewed, the duration of the visit should not be less than the average indicators for the site.
  • The number of users who turned to you in the offline representation of the action held on the social network. It is necessary to take into account the absolute indicators of such actions, for example, with the help of promotional codes.
  • The growth of positive references on the Internet about the brand, the reduction of negative references, the overall increase in the number of references to the brand name on the Internet. This indicator should be assessed using regular Internet monitoring and tracking absolute indicators for reporting periods.

Control measurements of all meters should be carried out once a month, preferably for the month.
