Hackathon APPS4ALL in Moscow

Good afternoon, Habr!



We are glad to inform you that on December 13 and 14 Moscow will host the largest in Russia and the CIS hackathon APPS4ALL!

For two days and one night in Coworking Nagatino, an exciting programming marathon to create a mobile or web application will take place, in which more than 70 teams will take part. Advising participants (programmers, designers and entrepreneurs) will be invited experts on open data, representatives of partner companies, mentors, investors and market experts.

Hackathon APPS4ALL will be held in 2 stages. At the start of the participants expects online registration on the site hackathon.apps4all.ru , which will end with the formation of teams. At the second stage, the formed teams, as well as the participants, who will find the team directly on the platform of the hackathon, will take part in the marathon, where they will be able to implement any of their ideas.

Participation in the hackathon is absolutely free - just register on the event website.

Hackathon partners presented 5 nominations:

1. "Mobile application created using the Samsung Mobile SDK ." When registering for a hackathon, you need to write what you would like to use from this SDK when writing an application.

2. "Mobile application on the Tizen platform " from Samsung. By the way, all participants of the hackathon will be able to get the Tizen device! In addition, there will be an opportunity to talk with Samsung evangelists and learn a lot of new things.

3. "Mobile application with backend in Windows Azure" from Microsoft.

4. "Application for Androidx86" from Intel.
