Ultra slim smartphone Huawei Assend P1 S and compact Mediapad to boot

At the booth of the Chinese company Huawei presented a large number of devices on Android , many of them have already been shown earlier. Our attention was attracted by a new smartphone on Android Ice Cream Sandwich , with a 6.68mm record-breaking case thickness and a 7-inch Android Honeycomb tablet with a screen resolution of 1280x720.



The smartphone runs on a dual-core 1.5GHz-s processor from Texas Instruments OMAP 4460 with a PoverVR SGX540 graphics accelerator. Exactly the same set in the Google Galaxy Nexus .

The device has 4GB of internal memory and a slot for microSD cards. Screen size is 4.3 inches, Super AMOLED matrix with a resolution of 960x540. Huawei Assend P1 S is one of the first smartphones on Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich . Chinese manufacturers slightly changed the system interface. We managed to notice a new unlock screen and our own camera app.



Tablet Huawei MediaPad running Android 3.2 Honeycomb and has a quality 7-inch IPS screen with a matrix with a resolution of 1280x720 pixels. A Qualcomm Snapdragon dual-core processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz is used.



The case of the tablet is made of plastic, the back cover is ribbed, and fits well in the hand. The device is equipped with a battery with a capacity of 4100 mAh, which allows you to work continuously for 6 hours. Also MediaPad has 2 cameras on 5 and 1.3Mp.
