Conference "UniversITy 3: IT-Startups"

February 20, 2010 in the educational center "Master Class" thanks to the fruitful work of the organizers AIESEC and i-Klass will begin a two-day conference "UniversITy 3: IT-Startups", which focuses on analyzing the current situation of the IT market in Ukraine and the nuances of start-ups ideas before creating and selling a product.
The main goal is to stimulate the realization of talented young people of their own creative developments. For the sake of achieving the most fruitful dialogue between the participants and speakers, the format "conference" is merged with "camp". Together with the classic presentations, informal seminars will be held, aimed at the practical consolidation of the knowledge gained.
Master classes will be conducted by representatives of Bay View Innovations consulting company and the founders of the most successful Ukrainian startups such as InvisibleCRM and Viewdle.
