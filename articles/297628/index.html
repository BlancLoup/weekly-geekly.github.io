Rambler & Co becomes shareholder of UCS



A post was posted on the main site of the group of companies Rambler & Co, in which it is reported that the holding acquires 50% of the share capital of UCS, a leading developer of software for entertainment and hotel businesses. One of the most popular products released by UCS is the UCS Premiere cinema ticketing system, the UCS-R-Keeper restaurant business system and the Shelter hotel automation system, which simplifies and speeds up customer registration in hotels.

This acquisition will give Rambler & Co the ability to integrate UCS software into the company's consumer services, which will provide a new service consisting in integrated automation of multifunctional entertainment centers operating under the unified management. The UCS-R-Keeper system will be integrated with the Afisha.Restorany restaurant selection and table booking service, the UCS-Premiere program will be integrated with the booking service for theaters, cinema and sports events Rambler.Cassa, and the Shelter system with service for the sale of tours "Rambler.Travel".
