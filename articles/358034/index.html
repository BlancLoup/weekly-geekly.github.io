The lock is directed against the Zello service (simulates a push-to-talk walkie-talkie), which is very popular among truckers. In March 2017, protests against the Platon system were coordinated through Zello, and in April of the same year, Roskomnadzor initiated a restriction of access to IP addresses used by the service. The official reason for blocking is the refusal of Zello to store the correspondence and personal data of users, as well as to provide this information to law enforcement agencies.

Now Roskomnadzor decided to "hit the squares."
