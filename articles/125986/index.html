OGS Mahjong Mahjong Solitaire Up to Version 0.8

It has become a good tradition to write here about the development of the OGS project and the OGS Mahjong game in particular.
Constantly, two people give their free time to the project: I and kornerr , but two more people helped us in the work on this version, which will be discussed below.

Even a leisurely job brings its results. Day ago came version 0.8.


We worked mostly on fixing problems, however there are several new features.

  • Improved work of the camera, changed rotation limits, added the ability to disable restrictions.
  • Improved interface. Added preview of layouts and saved games.
  • Added the ability to highlight even layers.
  • Fixed closing the game by pressing Q.
  • Fixed problems with installing in / usr / games in Linux systems.
  • Deb-packs for Debian are collected (compatible with Ubuntu and Mint).
  • A second alpha version of the layout editor has been prepared ( here ).
  • Improved translation support. Fixed the German version, thanks to Dirk Pervolts for reading the text and sending its corrections.
  • Connected new models of chips. Highly detailed and medium models were changed. For them, we are grateful to Timur Malikin. We hope for his further participation in the project.
  • Three music tracks from Kevin MacLeod and Dan-O were also added (thanks to them too, although they didn‚Äôt try for us).
