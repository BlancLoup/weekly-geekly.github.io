What will the work bring you to?

Online recruitment agency Career Building has placed on its website an amusing flash-program that will allow you to see now how you will look in old age if you stay at your current place or finally get a job in a dream company.

You feed your photo to the program, adjust your portrait to an uncomplicated template, answer three questions and that's it ‚Äî Age-O-Matic looks into the future and shows you your own reflection, dated two thousand-year old.

Actually the questions that you have to answer by moving the slider on the scale from one answer to another will not be very serious, and it will be difficult to answer them correctly. But fun. The truth for this is to know at least a little English.

You can also record an audio message through the phone or dial it from the keyboard and voice the built-in voice engine. The truth will have to tinker with the writing of the phrase, because the program, alas, does not understand the Cyrillic alphabet.
