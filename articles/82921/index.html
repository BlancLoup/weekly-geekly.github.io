All-Russian Olympiad in Applied Informatics

This article will be interesting to those who are not averse to spending a little time to read the story of one victory, who is interested in how such events take place in our country and, in my opinion, is indispensable for those who want to take part.


The event is organized by MESI and is held every spring in April-May.
The Olympiad is held in three rounds: city, regional, all-Russian.
Participation in it can take students 3-5 courses of universities, specializing in applied computer science in various fields. The team consists of 3 students. It is fair to note that the majority of students study in the field of applied informatics in economics, where I also study. The essence of the Olympiad is to create a project to automate some business process of a particular organization.

My acquaintance with this Olympiad began in the fall of 2007, we prepared for a long time and we were sent to Ufa in April 2008, where we took second place.   3. The last part, performance and presentation of the project in the form of Power Point slides, strain and read how to properly present information using slides.
