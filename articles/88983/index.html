DevConf :: ASP.NET () - an event that should not be missed



The developers of .NET and ASP.NET, soon, namely on May 17, 2010, in the hero city of Moscow, a very large DevConf conference will be held. DevConf is a professional conference dedicated to leading programming and web development technologies. The uniqueness of DevConf is that it will unite the participating developers for a large number of platforms. On the day of the conference it is planned to organize streams of reports: DevConf :: PHPConf (), DevConf :: Perl (), DevConf :: Python (), DevConf :: Ruby (), DevConf :: ASP.NET (). Separately from this, on May 18, there will be a stream of reports on RIA-technologies: Flash, Silverlight and others.

You, as developers, have two options for participating in a conference: as a spectator and as a dadac. It is interesting to look at other people's reports, but I invite all of you to join the speakers and apply for your own reports on DevConf :: ASP.NET (). At the moment, 4 applications for reports have been registered, including mine, in which I will talk about the MEF framework.

Participation in conferences is not only new knowledge. These are new acquaintances, connections, communication. Come to DevConf :: ASP.NET (). Participate in DevConf :: ASP.NET () as a speaker. Share experiences or gain knowledge.
