Google removes links to competitors

Yesterday, January 16, a message appeared on the Search Engine Journal website that Google had excluded links to Yahoo! Maps and MapQuest from your local search results. As a proof, you can see any search query .

The step, in general, is quite understandable: than to refer to competitors, it is better to promote your own products. But we have to admit that it is not quite beautiful.
