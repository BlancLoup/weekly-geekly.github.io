Roskomnadzor blocked 127.0.0.1



Finally it happened, the address 127.0.0.1 was entered in the registry of prohibited resources.

Roskomnadzor tried to block himself from the Internet? Hmm, but a good undertaking! ))

The corresponding registry entry indicates (or rather, there are two of them ) that the supervisory authority blocked this IP by the decision of the Moscow City Court No. 3-1115 / 2016 of October 19, 2016. But in this court decision, only the domain name itself (karaoke-besplatno.ru), which needs to be blocked, is listed, and IP addresses are not mentioned there. It follows from this that the introduction of IP localhost into the register of prohibited sites is an initiative of Roskomnadzor employees themselves who are responsible for processing court decisions.

Currently, this IP is already promptly excluded from the registry , but its very entry there is remarkable.
