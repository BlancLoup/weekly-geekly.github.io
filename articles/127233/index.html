3rd .NET Developer Conference

The 3rd .NET Developer Conference will be held on September 25 in Chelyabinsk. The official address on the network http://www.dotnetconf.ru , meeting Vkontakte and #dotnetconf + @dotnetconf . Registration for the conference is open at http://www.dotnetconf.ru/Registration .



Why are we going?



We are interested in practical experience . You can not leave with a report about the fact that a new version of ASP.NET MVC or SharePoint has been released. You can read about it on the Internet faster than the speaker will tell about it. The experience of our speakers should be related to the use of a library or development environment in 1-2 commercial projects so that you can go out and say ‚Äúthis is really useful, but you should take a closer look at this part, I got these problems with it, I suggest solving them like this".

Program



Scheduled 16 reports, which will go in parallel in 2 tracks. The time of the presentations and description can be found on the conference website in the Program section

I am glad that so many professional developers have joined the team of speakers. Rinat Abdullin , Arsen Fazylzyanov , Alexey Zakharov , Alexander Karpich , Dima Salakhov , Lyudmila Utkina , Sergey Zvezdin , Timur Rakhmatillaev , Alexander Mezhov , Alexander Bindyu , Pavel Tsytovich , Vladimir Yunev , Dmitry Khryukin will perform before us.

Trainings


The conference will be 4 training . Two training on project management: Agile Development with Scrum and Agile Requirements Analysis. Two programming trainings: Design Principles (SOLID) and Test Driven Development (TDD). Description of trainings can be found on the conference website in the Trainings section. Training for programmers I will hold before the conference.

Prizes and promotions


JetBrains has provided 3 licenses for ReSharper and one for dotCover and dotTrace Performance .

Microsoft has provided mice, keyboards and tambourines.

Prizes from JetBrains and Microsoft will be raffled during the conference.

REG.RU will hold a special promotion and give two months of free hosting at any rate and a 50% discount on domain registration in .RU and . zones! A promo code for a discount and free hosting can be found on REG.RU flyers, which can be obtained directly at the conference.

Sponsors and organizers



Thanks to them, the conference will take place! We are pleased that so many companies and information portals have agreed to assist in the holding of .NET developers. A list of all sponsors and partners can be found on the conference website in the Sponsors section.
