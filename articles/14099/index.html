Rutube clips will be shown on television

Earlier this week, a joint project of ATV (Author's Television) and Rutube.ru started on the DTV channel. Spectators of the Extra program, which is released on weekdays at 20:30, have been asked to post their video stories on the website Rutube.ru, the best of which every day, according to the results of the rating selection, are broadcast. This is the first implementation of the TV 2.0 concept in Russia.

Rutube.ru here serves as an aggregator of user-generated video content, and in the future, partners plan to make live-air television broadcasts from different parts of the world. Including the planned organization of video feedback with the viewers. A little-known channel DTV can now claim to be the most innovative in Russia.
