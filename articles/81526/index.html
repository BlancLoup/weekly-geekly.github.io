Mobile Office - OfficePOD



European countries have long noticed a tendency to increase work efficiency at the expense of employees working at a distance. Modern technology allows you to always be in touch, and working at home does not need to spend time and money on the way to the office and back.

Comfortable home conditions, with good organization, increase the efficiency of labor, and for the employer this is an excellent opportunity to reduce the rent.

As part of this economic trend, the British company OfficePOD Limited has developed an unusual mobile office project - OfficePOD, representing a personal office set in a garden near the house. OfficePOD is a small cubic room, a little over two meters high, with a door and two transparent walls. Being inside OfficePOD, you get into the atmosphere of a comfortable office with a computer, air conditioning, Internet access, telephone and office furniture.

OfficePOD Limited is positioning its product primarily on corporate customers, offering rental modules for its employees. For 5000 pounds a year, the company will install an office and provide round-the-clock technical support. OfficePOD is easy to install and is mounted in just one day. Naturally, this project pays great attention to ecology - low energy consumption and use of materials suitable for recycling. Thanks to a good insulation and ventilation system, OfficePOD significantly reduces CO2 emissions by 47-67%.

OfficePOD project should be an excellent option for remote employees who have their own home and the need for special conditions for concentration at work.
