Specter is one of ten winners of student IGF 2010



I would like to bring to your attention one entertaining game, the participant of the student IGF 2010.


In addition to the main IGF competition, there is also a separate nomination for the 2010 IGF Student Showcase , in which 10 winners are selected .

One of them was a peculiar game Specter . The main character in it is 73-year-old Joseph Wheeler (Joseph Wheeler), during the game he tries to tell us about his past years, starting with 7 years.



At its core, the game is a platformer. We need to select exactly 9 of his memories in such a way that at the end we get a coherent story. Traveling through the levels, we catch the "memories" and listen to pieces of history. But the platform gameplay here plays a secondary role, and most importantly lies in a monologue, the voice of which took so much space.



The game will require a good knowledge of English and a special mood. The old man Joseph has 52 stories in store for us, but it will not be so easy to collect them.
