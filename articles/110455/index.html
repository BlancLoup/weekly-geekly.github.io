With the world-by-thread - the results of the first month

At first, many predicted that the idea would not last a week, then no one would come, then they said that there would be no projects, then they said that no one would donate money ... everything turned out to be wrong. Skeptics are confounded again.

Already as a month the project is getting up on its feet, growing stronger, overgrown with new convenient features and making friends.
