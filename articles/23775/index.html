Children in networks


In June-July of this year, RBC Holding together with the Israeli company Tweegee plan to launch a children's social network . The Israeli side will provide the platform, which is used by 80% of Israelis aged 8 to 14 years, and RBC, in turn, will receive $ 6 million investment over three years.
Experts note that children's social networks are a fashionable trend in the West, but in Russia they do not earn money on them yet.
