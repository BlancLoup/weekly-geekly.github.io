LauncherPro Plus - HTC Sense replacement

On Habr√© there was already a small review of LauncherPro , but in this topic I would like to tell about its new milestone of development, namely about LauncherPro Plus.
LauncherPro Plus is a paid version of LauncherPro, which costs only $ 2.99, the main distinguishing feature is the presence of widgets like HTC Sense.

Translation news from the developer:
Last week I promised some interesting news about LauncherPro, but I was a little late, I think it's worth it.

One of the features that you requested is support for HTC Sense widgets. Everyone seems to want them. Due to the features of HTC Sense, widgets are built into the launcher itself, so it's impossible to get them. And since not every user has a phone with HTC Sense, respectively, these widgets are not available to all users.

Today I am pleased to announce a new milestone in the history of LauncherPro. Starting today, there is a new version called LauncherPro Plus, which will consist of several widgets exclusively for LauncherPro Plus users.

Widgets that will be included:
Contact widget - To quickly access all contacts or selected or specific group. (Included in this release)
Bookmark Widget - Quick access to browser bookmarks.
Friends Widget - Lists of status updates of your friends from Facebook and Twitter, as well as the ability to update you your status too.
Calendar widget \ Agenda - Shows the calendar, you can scroll through it to scroll through the months, and you can also see the agenda.
Messages - Scroll through the list of SMS messages or one by one. Quick links to delete messages, reply, etc.

I will be adding these widgets in the next two weeks. Contact widget can be seen right now. Other features will be announced in the future only for LauncherPro Plus users.
