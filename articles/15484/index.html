Crysis demo

A demo version of the most anticipated recent project, first-person shooter Crysis, has been released.

First-person shooter from the company Crytek famous release superhit FarCry. The main trump cards of the new project are an unprecedented level of graphic design and a fully interactive world. As expected, the system requirements are at the highest level: dual-core processor, 2 GB of RAM, and the more powerful video card. DX9 and DX10 modes are supported. If your computer has the above requirements, welcome to the world of Crysis. Gamers playing the demo version are unanimous - the game was a success.

The demo version includes the first level of the full version of the game and the Sandbox 2 level editor.
