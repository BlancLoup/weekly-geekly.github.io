Adobe in Russia grew by 260%

Adobe summed up the first year of work in Russia, calling the country one of the priorities. In the third quarter of 2007 fiscal year, Adobe sales in Russia grew by 260%. The local representative office of the company received its development plan for the first time.



Adobe Corporation reported on the results of the annual stay in the Russian market. The best-selling Adobe product in the country today is Creative Suite 3, followed by Adobe Photoshop and Acrobat. The head of the company's representative office in Russia and the CIS, Pavel Cherkashin, said that the sales priority is just product packages, and not individual programs.

Following the results of the last III quarter of the 2007 fiscal year, sales of Adobe products in Russia grew by 260%, while the company's similar indicator throughout the world grew by about 40%. Absolute sales figures, as well as the share of Russia in the general sales pie of Adobe, the company does not disclose.

Mr. Cherkashin told CNews that Russia, the CIS countries and Eastern Europe are priority markets for the company. In the near future, the company plans to localize several more of its key products to these languages.
