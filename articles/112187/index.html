Tomsk StartPoint Announcement

January 28 (Friday) in Tomsk will be an open StartupPoint meeting for IT startups and those who want to build their business on the Internet. We promise - presentations of Tomsk projects, expert evaluation, reports and new acquaintances. We are waiting for teams, projects, and just everyone interested.

Dates: January 28, 2011, 6:00 pm (Friday)
Address: Open Youth University, Tomsk, ul. Mokrushina 9 p.1


Why is this event and why attend?


First of all, the Startup Point community meeting is a communication platform where you can present your project, get expertise completely free, find out the opinion of the audience about your startup, find useful contacts and, of course, light up before investors. Moreover, the performance on Pointe guarantees you getting into the qualifying list for distribution via our database, which includes more than 100 private, three strategic (Yandex, Head Hunter and Muz-TV) investors. And also 20 funds.

In order to better understand how the event takes place, we recommend that you look at the report on the 3rd Startup Point meeting in Yekaterinburg - startuppoint.ru/blog/public/33669.html

If I don't have a project?


Then come look at those who have already implemented their own startup to understand what to do and how. And what not to do. Chat with experts, investors. Find partners, make new acquaintances. After all, as you know, communication is everything.

Attention! Registration for the event is required.
You just need to press the button "I will go" (available for registered on the site users) - startuppoint.ru/event/32606
