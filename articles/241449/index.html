Mathematical drawings

In this post I will give a few drawings, drawn with the help of mathematical formulas. The purpose of these drawings is not just to draw something on the screen (there is computer graphics for this), but to offer a simple formula that defines the drawing.




The first picture shows a lotus. Figure built in the program Wolfram Mathematica.
