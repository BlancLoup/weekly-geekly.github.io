Keep track of prohibited sites will be a third party, not Roskomnadzor

Sites whose content does not comply with Russian legislation (first of all, the notorious child pornography, plus some other types of content) will be added to this registry. It became known about the creation of the registry for a long time, but that's who will lead it, so far no one has decided.
