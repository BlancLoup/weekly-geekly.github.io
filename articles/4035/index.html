San Francisco Zero - Another Alternative Reality Game

An original game called San Francisco Zero (SF0) is gaining popularity in and around San Francisco . People scamper around the city and perform tasks that appear on the web.

Game tasks are sometimes completely absurd. For example, one of the most amusing is checking at passers-by passes to the passage along the street . This task requires two people. The first gives citizens free pass passes (sample pass in PDF format), and the second participant stands around the corner and checks them.

Assignments can be very diverse. Actually, they come up with not the organizers of the game, but the participants themselves. The organizers believe that this collaboration model is similar to the wiki.

The game involves several teams . For completing assignments, each of them gets prize points and increases his rating. The number of players has already exceeded two hundred people, and there are only five teams so far. But the number of participants began to increase rapidly when the press mentioned this game.
