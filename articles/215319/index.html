Payler - go ahead!



Friends,

What we have been waiting for has finally happened! After several months of working on our product, we announce the launch of Payler . We will tell briefly what functions of the system are available today.

So, Payler provides customers with the opportunity to organize on the website payment acceptance by Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Diners Club, JCB credit cards. One-step and two-step payment authorizations, 3DS transactions, refund and unlock funds are available. Antifraud module can be used as a separate service. Statistics on payments is displayed in the administrative office.


Payler services and additional features also include:

- Development of a payment business model and a legitimate legal scheme for trade and service enterprises with a non-standard approach and start-ups.
- Regulation of the preparation of the site to receive payments;
- Solution for sales analytics and transaction status management on the Internet;
- Integration with any of the acquiring banks (direct agreement with the Bank);
- Development of mobile applications from the creators of the gateway (http://poloniumarts.com) and their integration with the payment gateway Payler.

That is, we provide a full range of related services for connecting to the system and preparing to work with it. To date, acquirers are the Bank of Moscow and Russian Standard. Soon there will be new banks, we are at the negotiation stage.

The commission for bank cards Visa and MasterCard is 3%. We decided not to do complex calculations with a multitude of categories, but established a single tariff for all our clients.
A detailed description of the Payler system and the services provided can be found in our presentation: www.payler.com/Payler.pdf .

We are currently working on the next version of Payler. In addition to the updated interface, the main in the second version will be iOS \ Android mobile applications, which will include:

- graph of cash flow;
- Financial payment analytics;
- Ability to customize the antifraud;
- Terminal for moto transactions;
- Merchants payers;
- 2-stage payment authorization (d / s blocking), refund;
- Providing SDK for quick integration with the gateway.

Also in the near future, an integrated SMS gateway (based on billingrad.com ) will become operational for Payler users. We will report this separately.

The second version will also include chat support, the ability to make recurrent payments, as well as the SDK for Ruby, Python, C #, Android and iOS. In future versions there will also be an SDK for PHP, Java and Erlang, and much more, such as integration with a QIWI wallet.

Follow Payler news on our blog! We promise to add something new, useful and interesting with each version. Join us.

Best wishes,
