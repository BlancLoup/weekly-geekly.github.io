ThL W100 - a small but powerful smartphone

Before some, the question arises regarding a cell phone: which smartphone to choose according to the optimal ratio of price and quality. Many, when answering this question, try to study the characteristics of devices, their results in tests and other parameters. But a small number of people pay attention to the devices released by Chinese brands.



And in vain! One of these solutions is the THL W100 - a smartphone, which is one of the low-cost and high-quality devices. While the mobile giants are competing in the display diagonals, ThL has released a model with a screen of ‚Äúonly‚Äù 4.5 inches. The device has a rather nice design and belongs to the class of budget 4-core smartphones. It is about this model that will be discussed in the review.

The smartphone uses a four-core Mediatek MT6589 processor, made on the 28-nm process technology, flavored with 1 GB of RAM and Android 4.2.1. For a smartphone of the price category to which the ThL W100 belongs, the stuffing is quite high. The model has two cameras, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.0, 3G and GPS - in general, everything you need for happiness.

The device comes in a standard carton. It shows a photograph of the model in white and some features.

‚ñå DESIGN, DIMENSIONS, CONTROL ELEMENTS


Due to the aspect ratio of the screen, the smartphone has a slightly elongated body shape. Overall dimensions and weight are well matched, the device fits well in your hand if it is larger than your average size. The shape of the smartphone does not differ originality, but still looks strictly and universally, without a hint of "glossy plastic soap dish."



On the front of the front panel there are light and proximity sensors, a front camera and a speaker, covered with a thin metal mesh. The interlocutor is heard clearly and legibly, mainly dominated by medium and high frequencies, extraneous noises and echoes are absent. There is a light indicator of events. Only, unfortunately, he has one color - red. Sometimes it works incorrectly.



"Back", "Home" and "Menu" are under the screen. Touch buttons, there is a white backlight, high sensitivity. Just below is a microphone.



I liked the thickness - not to say that the smartphone is thin, but it is a convenient solution, it will be difficult to drop the device out of hand. A standard 3.5 mm audio jack with a microUSB and power button shared the upper end.



On the left side modestly located volume rocker. There are no keys on the right.



The material of the back cover is plastic. A part of the frame between the screen and the silver inset, which follows the contour of the device, is made from it. Fingerprints on the case are not noticeable, thanks to the white and pretty color of the device. On the back side there is a small flash and a camera module, bordered with a metal ring.



Below are the manufacturer's slogan and speaker hole. The volume of the external speaker on the built-in ring tones is at an average level. In this case, the sound picture essentially depends on the placement of the device. If it is a speaker up, then it is heard much better.



To open the case, you need to pry the cover on the bottom right. Above the battery there are two connectors for installing SIM cards: SIM1 (2G / 3G) - on the left, SIM2 (only 2G) - on the right. In the middle - the microSD connector. To replace the SIM card, you will need to remove the battery. But the memory card can be installed and so.



In general, the device produces a good impression for a budget device. The materials were chosen successfully, the design is traditionally universal, the combination of size and weight is correct, there are no comments on the quality of the workmanship.

‚ñå DISPLAY


ThL W100 has got a quite popular screen diagonal of 4.5 inches with a typical resolution of 540x960 pixels today. Of course, it uses a rather high-quality IPS-matrix. The density is 240 pixels per inch.



The touch layer is made by capacitive technology, the sensitivity is excellent. Supports "multitouch" up to 5 simultaneous touches.



Maximum viewing angles. The color rendition is natural, it can be clearly seen on the street. The display fades slightly in the sun.



High brightness, automatic brightness adjustment does not always work adequately, but is tolerable. At night, the smartphone tries not to lower it much, which is why the eyes get tired and are not very comfortable to use. And the extra battery consumption. And quite a good screen option.

‚ñå SOFTWARE PLATFORM


At the heart of ThL W100 is Android 4.2.1 Jelly Bean. A stock JB is selected as an interface with a small amount of changes from ThL. In the notification menu are various switches. Holding the home key brings up a list of recently opened applications. The company has pre-installed its useful applications: Flash Player, Flashlight, Task Manager, Note Book, File Manager, APK installer, Task, Backup. Most of them can be safely removed from the standard Android application management menu. This will free up some memory in the device.



The creators of the firmware were not too lazy and slightly changed the interface. The icons are now colored, it looks pretty nice, and the status bar conveniently implemented the management of the main modules of the smartphone. And it differs from stock Android'a. So, for example, to turn off Wi-Fi you need to tap on the icon and not hold (as it is implemented in version 4.3)



Do not go unheeded and lock screen. It allows you to get quick access to the camera, messages or phone. And of course you can unlock the smartphone. Change applications for their own, alas, is impossible.



The organization of management of SIM-cards is implemented by the same method as that of other models on MTK processors. In the settings there is a separate menu called ‚ÄúSIM Management‚Äù, where the user selects which SIM to use for. Since the priority of SIM-cards can not be changed, work only in the 3G network that is installed in the first slot. Regardless of whether the data transmission is working or not, two SIMs are available for communication, but if the call arrives on the second card (SIM slot 2), Internet access will be suspended. If the call is received on the first one (SIM 1, 3G), you can simultaneously talk and surf. You can also link a call to a particular SIM card.



The flash can be used as a flashlight, for this there is a separate utility. The file manager allows you to install applications if there is no access to the Internet. Well, the compass will help you navigate the terrain.



In the interface of this model, a user previously familiar with Chinese smartphones will not discover anything new. Except maybe a modified lock screen. Otherwise, everything remains unchanged - no proprietary interfaces, but there are a number of pre-installed applications that will be useful to many. I liked that there are no Chinese programs that can not be removed without the help of Root-rights.

‚ñå WIRELESS NETWORKS


The phone works in 2G cellular networks (GSM / GPRS / EDGE, 850/900/1800/1900 MHz) and 3G (900/2100 MHz). In this case, one SIM card functions only in 2G, the other in 2G or 3G. Internet access speeds are normal for this type of network - up to several megabits per second for reception and several hundred kilobytes per second for transmission.



The platform supports Bluetooth version 4.0 (EDR / A2DP) for file and speech transfer. There is a wireless connection Wi-Fi IEEE 802.11 b / g / n. The device, of course, can be used as an access point (Wi-Fi Hotspot) or modem. There were no problems with the connection, the Wi-Fi sensitivity is high: for example, at a distance of 7 meters (behind the wall) from the ThL W100 router, it confidently showed 3 divisions out of 4.



The smartphone is equipped with a GPS receiver with support for A-GPS. In operation, the navigation service showed itself well - the satellites were caught quickly, the positioning accuracy is good, the receiver sensitivity is normal.



ThL tried to do the work of the modules without any complaints.

‚ñå PRODUCTIVITY


ThL W100 has an MTK6589 processor, and also includes four Cortex-A7 processor cores with a clock speed of 1.2 GHz, a PowerVR SGX544 graphics core and 1 GB of RAM. ROM capacity is 4 GB, of which 1.9 GB is available.



In performance tests, the ThL W100 showed expectedly good results. They are certainly not flagship by the standards of 2013, but they correspond to what we saw last year in the LG Optimus 4X HD and HTC One X. In AnTuTu, the smartphone scored 13527 points, in Quadrant - 4011 points. PassMark test gave 2084 points.



The smartphone allows you to play modern shooters and races, there are no incompatibility problems with the device in the Play Store.



Power, of course, enough for medium-gravity games.



In the arcade toy, everything is very beautiful and smooth.





In general, ThL W100 is a convenient and fast device, in addition to dynamic games like Real Racing or GTA Vice City, its stuffing works well with video playback, surfing and simple everyday tasks.
