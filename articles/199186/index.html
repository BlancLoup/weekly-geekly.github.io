How we made a free Balda for iOS and how we plan to make money


Since the beginning of this year, my friends and I have been making a well-known multiplayer word game for iOS - Balda Online . For those who have not played Baldu: the game takes place on a field of 5x5 cells. There is a starting word of 5 letters. Players take turns adding one letter at a time until the cells run out. The course is given 2 minutes. The longer the words, the more points. In the game such qualities as erudition, quickness of mind, memory, composure and calculation are important.

We have set ourselves the task of making the best in Baldu world and we are trying to consistently move towards this goal, constantly bringing in new and new "chips". Interest in the game is also quite large: 12 thousand people play every day in Baldu Online (up to 1000 players at a time at peak time), spending more than 25 thousand games every day. We are confident that we will be able to further increase these figures at times.

By the way, information about the current number of players is displayed directly on the main page of the game. Download the game and check how many people are playing right now.

Despite the fact that the Game Center in theory should connect between more or less equal players (based on its own rating), it does not do very well. In each league, players play among themselves. Players can move between leagues when their place changes. The division into leagues helps to hold matches between more or less equal rivals.

Some statistics


The game is implemented using the Apple Game Center for multiplayer (which makes it impossible to cross-platform at this stage), plus its own server for storing the user profile, calculating the rating, location and statistics. The most resource-intensive operations on the server are made in cron, which allows you to service a large number of players without delay. Currently there is an iPhone only version.

Interesting distribution of players by time of day. The minimum point is 2-3 in the morning.
The number of players. X axis - seconds since the beginning of the measurement.


The maximum number of people playing Baldu from 22 to 23 pm. Then after 0 o'clock there is a sharp decline. On average, one player plays 2 full games per day (more than 1 turn), i.e. spends about 30 minutes in the game.

Promotion


The game did not immediately come to such good performance. This was facilitated by 2 paid reviews with a total cost of 14 thousand rubles.

First review

October 8 at 19:00 a paid review was published on the website appleinsder.ru worth 10 thousand rubles. That day the game was set by 1400 people. (+1100 people). Within 1 week, the game was released on 3,600 daily setups.

Second review

On October 14, the second paid review in the VKontakte group , worth 4 thousand rubles.


Obviously, the first review was for the game "kick ass." Further growth was due to an increasingly high place on the App Store charts. Not the last role was played by the worked out gameplay and game design, confirmed by high user ratings (4.5 stars).
As a result, almost 2 weeks after the start of the promotion, the game ranked 28th in all free applications in the Russian App Store, 10th place in games, 2nd in puzzles and 1th in verbal! Now Balda has rolled back a bit from the maximum positions.
