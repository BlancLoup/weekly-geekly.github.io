IBM's Will Strike in Second Life

Dissatisfaction of IBM employees in IBM with low wages and the refusal of management to increase it in due amount can result in a protest. True, according to the plan of the Italian trade union called Rappresentanza Sindacale Unitaria, the strike will take place not in reality, but in the online world of Second Life.

At the moment, the administration of the trade union on its website actively encourages IBM employees to join the protest. Moreover, on the same site provides detailed explanations for beginners Second Life on how to start the game and how to create your character.

Meanwhile, according to IBM's internal corporate policy, employees are not allowed to speak negatively about the company on the Internet. Therefore, it is not yet known what the initiative may lead to and what the consequences will be for the participants in the strike. But the idea itself is interesting. You can go to work and strike at your pleasure.
