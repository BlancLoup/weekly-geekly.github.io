Flow


It's no secret that now all the information, or rather, the content is arranged in streams. With the development of the Internet, it would not have worked otherwise. There is so much content being created that it is impossible to control its elements - only streams, as an entity of a higher order.
Just look at any popular website or service that provides textual or video content, and you will see these streams, whatever they are called. Hubs, channels, categories, categories, trends, playlists, groups, tapes, series, etc.


It is increasingly common to manage flows using artificial intelligence or machine learning in order to make it as easy as possible for the consumer to search for suitable content and keep his attention on the resource for as long as possible, because attention is converted in time, and time is monetized.
