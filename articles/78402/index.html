Help desk Beeline - short sister of talent

After a recent topic about managing Beeline services via the Internet, I decided to climb and see what it is like. The overall impression was a bit more positive than the author of the last topic, but the interesting was ahead. Among the services I found an unfamiliar and non-deactivated service Notify which I definitely did not connect. I decided to search the site for information about her, but even there it turned out to be empty, not a hint of the existence of it. Since I now decided to limit myself to a letter to the support service and not to call with questions.

The letter was as follows:

Hello, can you tell me what the Notify service is? I found it in the list of services in my account, but I did not find information about it on the site.
Thank.


Beeline's answer came the next day and struck him with its simplicity and genius.

Thank you for taking the time to write to us.

Answering your question, we want to inform you that at the moment there is no information on this service. With all the changes you can find on our website:

mobile.beeline.ru

We wish you all the best!

Your "Beeline".


Here is such a wonderful service from Beeline.
I have been using Beeline for about six years, but lately there have been more and more disappointments.
