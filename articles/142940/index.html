From Twitter - to search results

A couple of months ago, we agreed with Twitter - the microblogging service gave Yandex full access to the data (aka firehose). At the same time, twitter.yandex.ru appeared, a bit later we started using information from Twitter for fresh answers in the search . Today we want to share with you another news on the same topic.

In the search results there are special snippets for responses from Twitter. For example, you are looking for [ Twitter Lebedeva ]. And here it is - the first answer. And along with a link to the account itself, there is a brief description of it: the number of readers, the userpic, and the three most recent tweets of Artemiy. Hover over the snippet - the bird will fly. Yes, you can follow the fold directly from the search results page.



And directly from the results page, you can reply to the found message, cancel it or add to favorites. Suppose you are very interested in fresh messages about the May holidays. We, by the way, understand you very much. Whether you ask with a hashtag or simply [ May holidays on Twitter ], Yandex will understand and show you the latest tweets. For the rest, welcome to Blog Search.



And about friends. In the search results you can see what of the results you found, they shared their microblog. If you are registered on Yandex and your Twitter account is listed in the profile , there will be a corresponding mark next to such answers.
