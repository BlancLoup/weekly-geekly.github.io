nanoCAD 3.0: some statistics for the first day


So, under the slogan "nanoCAD 03: first aid in design" we launched the first release of the domestic CAD platform nanoCAD 3.0 . The day has passed and you can sum up the interim results.

Of course, the product release was accompanied by massive visits to the site - mail.ru statistics is available to everyone: http://top.mail.ru/rating?id=1460284 .

On the day of launch, a little more than 3 thousand people came to us according to mail.ru, the next day (after being sent to the user base) - 4300 people. If you look at the overall visits for June and compare with this period over the past year, you get the following graphs:

Fig. 1 Visits site nanocad.ru in the period from June 1 to June 16 for 2009, 2010, 2011 (according to mail.ru)


Fig. 2 Comparative schedule of visiting the site nanocad.ru in the period from June 1 to June 16 for 2009, 2010, 2011 (according to mail.ru)

Those. visits grow, more and more people learn about us - which is actually very good!

Since the product was launched on June 15 at 17:00, we can now analyze the issuance of licenses for the product a little more than a day. We issued the following number of licenses and jobs:
Licenses Work places
Private individuals 1300 1300
Organizations 300 11,000

It turns out that in general, during this time, we have licensed 12 thousand jobs with the new version of the nanoCAD 3.0, the native CAD system. And that means 500 jobs every hour! Wow ...
