MobileFest-2011 - Mobile Software Developer's Festival - Held March 18-19



Winter MobileFest Russia '2011 - All-Russian festival of software developers for mobile phones and new projects in the field of mobile Internet will be held March 18-19 , 2011 in the sports park "Volen" (a ski resort in the Moscow region, 46 km from Moscow Ring Road).

MobileFest Russia is a conference-festival that for the third year in a row gathers representatives of development companies, mobile operators and vendors (Microsoft, Intel, Nokia, Sun Microsystems, Mera Networks), as well as venture capital managers and investors in the field of mobile development (Softline). Venture Partners).

The program of the festival Winter MobileFest Russia '2011:
- new trends, new technologies and features of mobile software development from market leaders based on Android, iPhone, Windows Mobile, Symbian, Java platforms;
- invaluable experience in attracting investments, secrets to overcome the ‚Äúdeath valley‚Äù of the start-up;
- informal communication with potential investors;
- rich cultural program;
- Completion of the ski season '2011 in a trendy park of extreme entertainment.

The peculiarity of the festival is in the free form of communication between speakers, participants and investors - everyone who is interested in the field of content production, software and applications for mobile phones. This is a unique platform where developers and users of mobile software exchange experience for the third year in a row.

The festival takes place out of format, outside the framework of strict conferences. The atmosphere of freedom and creativity, work for their own pleasure and for the delight of users of the product being created is what attracts its participants in the environment of new technologies and developments. Seminars and project presentations are mixed at MobileFest Russia with performances by musicians and extreme types of recreation.

The collection of applications continues - representatives of large corporations - Nokia, Intel and Mail.Ru, as well as groups of independent developers, who will present their own mobile content for iPhone and Android, etc., have indicated their participation.

Registration is already open on the website www.mobilefest.ru .
Registration fee for participants - 1500 rubles. Travel and accommodation - at your own expense (if you are going to stay overnight, we recommend taking care of the room in advance). From Moscow (m. Altufyevo) will run the bus to the ski resort Volen and back www.mobilefest.ru/avtobus/

If you want to make a presentation at the Winter MobileFest Russia '2011, then you can send the topic of your speech at md@mdday.ru.

Information about how the Mobilefest Russia festival took place in previous years can be found here 2010.mobilefest.ru
