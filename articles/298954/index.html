10 amusing internet services

We have selected ten effective resources that enable us to quickly check the semantics of texts, exchange messages, update and replace programs, store data online and edit them on the pages of sites.

1) Iqbuzz

Iqbuzz is a professional management tool that analyzes online reputation through monitoring social online media.
Advantages of the applied technology:
  • optimization of the work of marketing, PR;
  • reduction of time for analysis;
  • an increase in the number of information media sources (more than 10,000) for data collection.

On the site, the user can make analytical reports, identify a claim to the brand, analyze references to competitors.

Rush Analytics automation tools will take care of instant selection of any number of keywords on the subject of the resource.
