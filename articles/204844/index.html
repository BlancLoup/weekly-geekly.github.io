Last Saturday of the month: Linux



Every last Saturday of the month in Minsk is Linuksovka - a kind of light version of LVEE . There are no rules here: the speakers are not limited by anything (including making a statement for a presentation with a report directly during the Linux program) - everything passes as freely as possible. At the meeting in October, several reports were filmed:
  • The Best of LinuxCon Europe 2013 (imho);
  • Measuring the convenience of modern windowing interfaces;
  • Libusb vs linux kernel - compare userspace & kernelspace API;
  • Overview of basic free software licenses;
  • Implementing the open-source TCP / IP LwIP stack in an Android application in the name of Satan.

All videos are available under the cat, as well as links to the presentations themselves.
