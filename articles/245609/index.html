Improving mobile sites with Google Developers in Russian

Hi, Habr!

In mid-November, we were telling Google to mark pages optimized for smartphones and tablets in search results . In the results of our search in Russian, these marks can be seen starting from this week.

The network may come across sites that are inconvenient, and sometimes simply impossible to view on a mobile device due to the lack of appropriate optimization. To help site owners avoid user dissatisfaction when browsing their resources on the Internet, we have prepared a guide in Russian with recommendations for creating websites optimized for mobile.



How to make a mobile version of the site better?

Our guide is divided into seven sections, covering the main problems in content indexing with Google crawler and its display on mobile devices. Each section contains a very short description of the problems and possible solutions to them, where required - the corresponding illustrations are given. Of course, for many developers, English is the second native language, and they will understand the materials in the original, but this guide is designed not only for experienced webmasters, but also for novice users who have created personal pages and sites without the help of professionals, besides it is always easier for the Russian-speaking guide to discuss the problem with the ever-busy superiors.

Guide to action

Not sure where to start? Especially for you, we have prepared an excellent guide on how to test your site for compatibility with mobile devices , what to do to improve and how to monitor the work of Google search engine with the mobile version of your site.

Modern WEB

A year ago, about 66% of the adult and solvent population of the global network used both desktops / laptops and mobile devices to access it. Over 17% of total Internet traffic is generated only by smartphones and tablets. Modern technologies have made the Internet closer, simplified the development of websites, allowed to carry eight-core processors and QuadHD screens in their pockets, work on the road and receive HD video at a speed of 100 Km / H, but, unfortunately, they cannot fix for us the mistakes that we have made when creating sites in the past. Most of the recommendations and advice require a minimum of time to implement and greatly facilitate the lives of users of smartphones and tablets. Let's make mobile browsing better together.
