Story
3M was founded in the town of Two Harbors, Minnesota in 1902, initially as a mining company, and later created processing plants. Currently, 3M has grown into a diversified tech company with a wide geography of activity, with headquarters in the USA in Saint Paul, Minnesota

Interesting Facts
At the dawn of activity, attempts to create sandpaper with anorthosite mineral were not successful, so it was decided to purchase another mineral, the Spanish garnet, after which the sales of sandpaper went uphill.
However, not everything was so smooth with sandpaper. After a while, a complaint came from the customer, saying that spraying is pouring from your sandpaper! As a result of the investigation, it turned out that he had been soaked with oil when importing the mineral, so he did not stick on paper. In order not to cancel the order and not to lose money, it was decided to fry the mineral so that the oil evaporated, which was successfully done.
This fact is considered the starting point in the development of 3M R & D directions.

3M Russia
The beginning of 3M activities in Russia dates back to the 70s of the last century. A 3M representative office was opened in 1991, and a few months later the 3M Russia company was formed. Currently, 3M provides a wide range of high-quality products and services to Russian customers.

Currently, the company's operations in Russia are carried out through the central office in Moscow and the Client Center in St. Petersburg.
