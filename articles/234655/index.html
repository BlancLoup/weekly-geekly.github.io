Exit control


At the exit from the assembly area from 1% to 10% of the product is selectively opened and checked from beginning to end. If everything is normal, a go-ahead is sent to the warehouse.

Transportation


When we reduced production faults, the number of returns fell, but not as much as we would like. I remind you that we reduced the number of marriages to approximately 0.01% (counting by discovered: some may not return even when in rare cases someone decides not to return the box).

In this case, most of the returns were related to transportation. Here is an example of a recent return to the store (the box is not our production):



It could be someone's gift, but did not - thanks to the loaders. Therefore, we went a little further for our production and decided that it was useless to swear with loaders (more precisely, other people are engaged in this). And for a particular warehouse, harsh Russia is just a surrounding reality that needs to be accepted with all the features.

Therefore, the next step after getting ready-made games is to assemble special transport boxes . Each game is carefully measured before printing and a box is made exactly millimeter-to-millimeter for the batch. Usually it is 8-10 games for a transport corrugated container for standard boxes, 12 for cubic ones, 24 for small ones, 5 for extra large ones. The result - there are fewer returns, but there is much to grow in this plan.

General procedure


If a person has a defective copy in his hands, he can call the color of the cup from under the lid - this is necessary not for production, but for us, retailers. We can track where this party went, and open 10 more boxes to figure out if this is a one-time marriage or serial. Not everyone is able to recognize the color exactly, so we ask you to send a photo or focus on the box brought. At the same time, similar colors lie far apart in circulation (pink and purple will go into circulation with a minimum difference of six months), so it usually turns out to solve everything by phone.

Production sends a replacement through us (Mosigra retail) or by itself. Somewhere a couple of times a month, additional sending of cards is done in registered letters, a couple of times - whole boxes are sent.

In parallel, when we have the debug box code, the number of the collector and the data about the lot from which the assembly was made, we can understand what exactly the error was. An additional advantage of the system - each collector has a bonus for the lack of marriage. If his codes are not returned to production, he earns more.
