Download and convert video in MediaPipe.ru

The task of downloading videos from the servers of popular video hosting sites, such as YouTube or Video@Mail.ru as a whole, is not so difficult. Fortunately for this, there are a number of offline programs and online services. However, the issue of convenience of this process is still relevant. And the online service MediaPipe is just trying to answer this question. It differs from other similar services in that it allows not only to merge video from one or other video portals, but also to do it in a user-friendly format, giving the possibility of partial adjustment of the conversion.

Download as you can even without registration. To access the form of converting clips will have to register. The conversion process is as simple as downloading: after specifying the link to the video in MediaPipe, the service automatically, according to the parameters set by users, converts the video and provides a link to download the converted file.

You can convert videos to AVI, MP3, FLV, 3GP, MOV, MP4 formats. Or, you can also make an MP3 file by cutting an audio track from the specified video file and then downloading it on a PC or saving it on a MediaPipe server.

The toolkit MediaPipe provides the ability to convert videos into a format suitable for viewing on mobile phones, media players and other portable devices. To do this, in the form of converting it is enough to specify a link to the video, select the desired video format, resolution, video and audio codecs in the settings.


You can use the preset conversion settings for popular phones, players and gadgets, which greatly speeds up and facilitates the process of setting parameters. To date, preset settings are available for several dozen devices by Nokia, Sony Eriksson, Samsung and LG.
