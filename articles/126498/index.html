The essence of the project is as follows.

The control system I proposed is an electronic circuit that is controlled by the PIC16F877 microcontroller.

The rotation of the solar battery in two planes is carried out with the help of electric motors with gearboxes.


For a more vivid demonstration of automation, I needed to make a model of a real diesel generator. To do this, I connected 2 electric motors of different power with a belt drive, thereby obtaining a generator simulator.
