Telebit - a service for exchanging bitcoins via Telegram



At the forum of bitcoin enthusiasts there was a message about the new project Telebit . This is a built-in bitcoin wallet for users of the Telegram messaging service. The service allows you to store and transfer funds to each other without the need for additional registration anywhere.

To work with the service, you need to add a telebit to your contact list. Management is carried out using text commands. BALANCE will tell you the amount of money in the account, WALLET will show you your bitcoin address to replenish the balance, HELP will give a list of all commands. All participants of the project are promised several satoshi for experiments.

In fact, when transferring money within the transaction system in the block chain, there will be no money - the funds are stored on the Telebit server. Therefore, the transfer of funds is carried out quickly and the fee for these operations is not charged.

The creators in the future plan to add gambling with bitcoin, pay users for viewing ads and the ability to transfer funds in traditional currencies.
