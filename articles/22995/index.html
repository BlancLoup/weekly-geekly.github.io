Well-known trademarks

Well-known trademarks are such trademarks that, due to their wide popularity, are additionally protected: they cannot be registered even in other classes. For example, the mark Yandex is not registered in the class of "coffee", but is well-known in the Russian Federation - thus, Yandex coffee is not registered as a trademark. (Of course, the owner of the mark himself can give such a right or independently produce coffee.)

In Russia, there were only 58 such signs.

- Of the 58 characters, 16 are symbols of alcoholic beverages;
- the most "ancient" well-known sign - "Uralmash";
- The only well-known medicine in our country is Pentalgin;
