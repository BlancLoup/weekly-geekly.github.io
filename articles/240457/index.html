The second wave of speakers at the mobile developers conference #MBTLDev

In two weeks, on October 28, the first conference of mobile developers # MBLTDev will be held in Moscow. Experts from world IT companies such as PayPal, VKontakte, Artsy, Parallels, TCS Bank, Typhoon, Soundcloud, Intel, ViaForensics, Odnoklassniki, Aviasales, Realm, will tell about the features of using and synchronization in iCloud, cross-platform development, programming on Kotlin and Swift , trilateration with iBeacon and more.



In the last post we told you about the first three speakers of the conference and their reports. Today we will introduce four more experts and tell you more about their reports.



Tim Messerschmidt - Head of EMEA PayPal - in his report will talk in detail about the two-step authorization and how to treat the use of wearable devices as a method of authorization.


Ilya Slavutin - Head of Mobile Software Development at Intel - will talk about the creation and use of the Intel INDE library, which expands the media capabilities of the Android SDK, iOS and WinRT. The library allows you to edit video, capture it from the screen in games, stream to the network, and so on.


Ruslan Shevchuk - iOS developer Aviasales - tells about the features of using and synchronizing iCloud. Namely, the tasks that are solved in the application using Core Data, features of working with large amounts of data, performance, multithreading and synchronization of Core Data using iCloud.


Alexey Panfilov - Parallels Access product program manager, Parallels - will look at three areas of problems that each cross-platform mobile developer faces: technology, business logic design, and work with various application stores.

In addition, visitors to the conference will receive a Parallels Access raffle, prizes from the organizers and two beautiful lounge areas from TKS and Odnoklassniki.

The final schedule will be formed next week. Follow our news on Twitter and Facebook , we have a few more surprises for you :-)
