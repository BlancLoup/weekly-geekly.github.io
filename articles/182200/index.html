Mobile browser game developer Playtox attracts $ 3 million from Runa Capital

Investment will accelerate the international expansion of free (to-play) massively multiplayer online games (MMO) for mobile phone browsers and the development of upcoming new products adapted for HTML5.

Palo Alto, pieces California, USA, and Novosibirsk, Russia - June 4, 2013 - Playtox, a leading developer of free mobile browser games, announced today that it has closed a deal to raise funding in the amount of $ 3 million from the venture fund Runa Capital. The funds raised will be used to distribute Playtox mobile browser-based MMO games worldwide, including the US and emerging markets, as well as the development of HTML5 technology in the company's current product line.

For over 2 years, Playtox has been successfully developing massively multiplayer online games for mobile phones that run on all phone models and operating systems. All games are free and work in the Internet browser of a mobile phone. The product line has 10 MMO games.
