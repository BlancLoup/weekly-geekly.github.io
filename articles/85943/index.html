Competition for SaaS Solutions Developers


Microsoft and Parking.ru announce a competition among SaaS solutions developers - Spring aggravation!

The main prize of the competition is a Sony Vaio VGN NW2Z laptop , for the second and third places participants will receive a Windows Phone HTC HD 2 and an Acer neoTouch S200 communicator.

The competition runs from March 1 to May 31, 2010 . In order to take part in the competition, you need to place your solution, built on the Microsoft platform in SaaSCatalog! To win, you need to attract visitors to the page of your decision in SaaSCatalog and actively :) rent it. The more you earn, the higher your chances of winning :)

Detailed conditions for participation can be found on the contest page .

ps Since the competition is going on for a long time, the prizes can be replaced by more relevant models that will appear by the summer :)
