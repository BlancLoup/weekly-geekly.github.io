New budget 3D printer


There are all new 3D printers in the family RepRap . Printrbot was designed to make the printer cheap, as simple as possible to assemble, and therefore accessible to wide circles. The project motto is " Printrbot - your first 3D printer." Looking ahead - the planned price for the base model is $ 549.

The project aroused keen interest on the kickstarter and 50% of the required $ 25,000 was raised in less than a day and a half. By the end of the month, by the end of the month, 830 thousand projects were collected. $ (3 323%)
