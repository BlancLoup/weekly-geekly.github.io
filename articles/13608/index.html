RORER is a new format of advertising with pay per result

A gift to all its regular and future customers was prepared by RORER for its third birthday. The unique T-RORER advertising format was proudly presented to the public. With its help, it is planned to build an advertisement with payment for the result on the place it relies on in the vast Runet.

Now advertisers can, along with the usual contextual ad, also post a picture of 100x100 in size, which will add an image effect to contextual advertising.

Only transitions to the advertiser's site will be paid, while the system provides advertising to the target audience.

In the T-RORER system, there was such a plus as the absence of an auction, that is, fixing the price of a click on any subject and keywords, which allows you to accurately plan your advertising budget.

For the sake of saving time and customer nerves, the system specialist will set up and launch an advertising campaign himself, and absolutely free of charge. Clients are also offered help with text and ad pictures.

The reputation of the company provides a careful choice of partners, so that among the advertising sites there are no any questionable sites.

Despite the fact that announcements are broadcast on the leading sites of Runet, the demonstration of illustrated ads with their image impact is made free of charge.
