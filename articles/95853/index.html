Paying with Visa and MasterCard on the Internet is Safer Now?

Visa and MasterCard simultaneously released a novelty - bank cards equipped with a display. This type of card was created to increase the security of payments made without a reader, since the main reason why payment by cards on the Internet is now a concern is simply to know the data on the card in order to commit fraudulent transactions.

The new Visa card is equipped with a keyboard and an 8-bit display. The display shows one-time passwords: it must be entered with each purchase in addition to the standard details. A touchscreen display is installed on the MasterCard card , where you can view information on the account balance, limit spent, etc.

From the point of view of enhancing security, of course, these cards are interesting and will help increase user confidence in online payments, compared to "traditional" plastic cards. Although the question of their cost remains open - it is not known how many customers will want to purchase such a card specifically for online payment.
