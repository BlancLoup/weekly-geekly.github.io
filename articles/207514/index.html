Sale of e-books and video courses from Packt Publishing

Electronic books are available in ePub, PDF, mobi formats as well as for reading online in a browser. Video - in mp4 format. All DRM-free.
When you buy an e-book, you also get a 50% discount on a paper copy.

All books are publishers . The list contains the price of the paper edition. The price of shares on the web page of the book.
