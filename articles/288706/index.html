You work primarily for yourself

I walked in the gardens for a long time, tried to hint and write between the lines, but I think that once you need to express your thoughts in ordinary words without hints or hidden text. The text is dedicated to ordinary employees of ordinary organizations.

So, comrades. Once and for all you must forget
  • about corporate ethics and standards;
  • about the goals and mission of the company;
  • about minimizing company expenses;
  • about the future profits of the company;
  • about problems that may become in front of the company in the future;
  • that the company is your second family.
You should remember only about yourself, about your real family and have a conscience when communicating with exactly the same people who just think about themselves and their family.   • reduced costs - received a portion of the money saved in his pocket;
  • optimized the business process - went on vacation with his wife at the expense of the company.
