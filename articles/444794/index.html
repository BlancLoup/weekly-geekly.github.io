How does it work

Tractive has a built-in GPS sensor that works almost worldwide , having agreements with mobile operators. This is a universal solution to not bother with SIM cards, do not open the device to replace them, breaking the seal. As a partner in Russia - the company MTS.

This decision seems to be justified: firstly, the integrity of the structure is indeed preserved, and the "Tractive" has protection against water, which is correct! Plus, it saves you from thinking about which operator to choose, and what to do if you leave, and what about roaming, etc.

Like any GPS tracker, Tractive provides precise control only in open space. In conditions with a weak signal, there will be an error in the room. Another thing is that it is necessary to control the animal in the apartment? - The question. But if the cat went off and huddled somewhere in the attic, then it would be possible to understand this only approximately. As soon as he runs out into the street, everything will resume again with high accuracy.
