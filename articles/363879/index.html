In the Google Inbox will be the ability to cancel sending letters

The word is still not a sparrow, but it will be possible to catch it in Google Inbox: in the comments on Reddith, the developers of the new Google email service revealed some of the secrets of the project, including the possibility of canceling the sending of letters .

I must say that in the list of experimental capabilities of the late Google Labs Bose (Google Labs) there was a delay in sending a letter: after sending, the letter leaves only 30 seconds, during which you can cancel it and continue editing or delete the letter altogether. For those who once turned on this function, it still works.

Google Inbox is a new vision of mail as a task manager , in which each letter is considered as a task. The service is still available only by invites, and the developers intend to implement a number of other functions before making it available to everyone. Among them, in addition to the cancellation of sending - cross-browser support and support for tablets. However, in the same place, in the comments, they hinted that they would keep all the most interesting features secret until they were ready.
