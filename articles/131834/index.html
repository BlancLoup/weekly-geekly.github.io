Vote for the best Mango app for Windows Phone

For 2 months, we held a competition Mango applications for Windows Phone. There are only a few days left until the end of the competition: the results will be announced on November 6th.

We have another prize nomination, namely the audience award.

Voting will take place as follows:
  • by means of voting on the site - for this you need to vote in the right panel for the application you like;
  • via social networks - you need to like or tweet a page with an overview of the application you like.
The application that collects the most votes in total will receive a prize - a phone running Windows Phone. Voting will end on the night of November 5th.

A list of all applications with links to reviews can be found on the contest page, more details about voting can be found here .
