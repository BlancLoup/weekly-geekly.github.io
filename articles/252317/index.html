Parallels Labs Labs, Summer and Winter Schools for Students


Who: Parallels and MIPT, MSU, NSU and SPbAU RAS
Free, possible scholarship following a good project
Pros: work on real projects in groups of 3-5 people, even from the first course. Sometimes they accept students from other universities.

The main admission criteria: the presence of at least some programming experience. Plus the absence of threes. Therefore, for the duration of the sessions, they give out sessions. To get to the laboratories from other call, you must separately contact representatives of the program .
