Presentation of the team "AngioScan"

The company "AngioScan" develops and introduces innovative non-invasive methods of early diagnosis and monitoring the effectiveness of the treatment of socially significant diseases into the practice of healthcare. The greatest attention is paid to cardiovascular diseases associated with the development of vascular atherosclerosis. Over the past year, AngioScan has developed and introduced 6 new mobile medical devices. The line of diagnostic instruments ‚ÄúAngioScan‚Äù is serially produced on the basis of a unique method for assessing arterial function developed by Doctor of Medical Sciences, Professor Parfenov Alexander Sergeevich.



The team conducts independent development of the mobile diagnostics device throughout the year As part of the Telemedicine project, a single diagnostic platform with a biofeedback based on a smartphone and a series of individual wearable modules are being developed. The developed MDU will allow for self-diagnosis of health status, analysis and registration of basic medical indicators, as well as receive recommendations on a healthy lifestyle, including normalizing health conditions with the help of Biological Feedback (BFR).

The first-generation mobile diagnostic device ‚ÄúAngioskan-01P‚Äù has already been introduced to the market. The concept of a unified diagnostic platform of the MDU has been developed. Development work is underway on several new biosensors.

The purpose of creating an MDL is to adapt existing breakthrough non-invasive approaches in occupational medicine for individual use - this is the main difficulty, with much attention paid to the simplicity of testing and visualization of diagnostic results.

MDU is a software and hardware complex, which consists of software and hardware components that are divided into 4 groups according to their functional purpose:
  1. Biosensors - represent a whole group of various sensors using different physical principles of measurement. The focus is on sensors embedded in the earset (iUh).
  2. Compact docking station.
  3. Software for smartphones with operating systems iOs, Android.
  4. Cloud storage.

The team concept assumes that there are 2 categories of measurements performed by the MRLs - continuous measurements of a person‚Äôs movement indicators and measurements performed at rest, sitting or lying down. In the quiescent state, measurements are made using biosensors located in the docking station. The docking station also serves to recharge the iUh module. You can download, store and analyze measurement results in the cloud data storage.

The developed MDU-iUh is designed for the widest range of consumers and will allow them to independently and at home in a timely manner and objectively learn about the risks and the presence of various diseases. The planned retail price of the complex is from 6-7 thousand rubles.
