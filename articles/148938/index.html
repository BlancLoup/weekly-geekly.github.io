Simple as java

Technological migraine leaves developers forever - hosting Java projects will now be easier. Cloud platform Jelastic has received $ 2 million investment. The money will be spent on business expansion: the United States and the countries of the Eurozone have already shown their interest in Jelastic .



Probably every Java developer faced with the problem of choosing a hosting. At the same time, the cloud services market has been stubbornly silent for 15 years. Actually, Jelastic also grew from this problem. Having created a large Java project, Ruslan Sinitsky, Konstantin Alexandrov and Alexey Skutin for a long time could not find the platform on which it would be located. They were faced with a dilemma: take advantage of a ready-made platform and rewrite the entire application code or take on the troublesome administration process. Both paths looked unattractive. And then the founding fathers of Jelastic chose the third option - they created their own platform.

For programmed machines, people came up with almost 3,000 languages. Some of them are used only by a narrow group of initiates, and some are so universal that they belong to peoples. More than 9 million developers use the Java language. Yes, indeed, having appeared in the mid-90s, Java has become the most popular programming language. Today, more than 4 billion devices work on it. It has a very wide range of applications: computers, telephones, medical equipment, household appliances, navigation systems, smart cards ...

Jelastic platform reiterated the democratic nature of Java. Having created the environment using a set of standard software stacks, it remains only to click the mouse several times to load and deploy the application. Such a routine as rewriting code, changing settings and selecting configurations is in the past. By its simplicity and functionality, Jelastic competes with the development of Google, Microsoft and Amazon.

It is quite understandable that the authors of the project are overwhelmed with optimism. Agreements with such hosters as HostEurope, Dogado, ServInt, Tsukaeru, Layershift, Rusonyx testify to the investment appeal of Jelastic. It is no coincidence that the venture funds Almaz Capital Partners and Foresight Ventures have already invested millions in the project.
For two years of work, the creators of Jelastic brought to the logical conclusion the main principle of Java - universality. And until the last moment, Java technologies did not have a decent hosting. It is even strange that for 15 years no one tried to solve this problem. Now you can try to launch your first application on Jelastic completely free of charge, and share your impressions in the comments to this post.
