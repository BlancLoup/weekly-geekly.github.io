Escene ES292 IP Phone Review

Today we look at the new IP phone Escene ES292 with support for three lines. In the line of corporate models of the company, the phone ranked between the Escene ES220 model and the older Escene ES320 model, each of which was covered by separate reviews published earlier. By its characteristics and functional features, this IP phone is closer to the older model.

Escene ES292 is the elder brother of the Escene ES290 model, which supports two lines. Each of the three lines Escene ES292 is completely independent and can be configured for one or three different IP-PBX, and the phone can handle up to three simultaneous calls.

When developing the devices, the engineers and designers of Escene used a unified approach to design, functional characteristics, as well as to control interfaces. The line includes models from basic to advanced phones, but they all share similar features - they meet the high requirements and have all the necessary functions of corporate IP-phones.

The phone is made in a strict and elegant style and is made of high quality materials, has a large bright screen, three independent lines, an intuitive interface in Russian, a huge set of functions, supports HD audio quality, two Ethernet ports and PoE power (optional) .


The device is represented by two models: Escene ES292-PN with PoE support (powered over Ethernet) and Escene ES292-N without PoE support (equipped with an Escene AD200 power supply unit).

For a model with a PoE power supply unit is not included in the kit, but if necessary it can be purchased separately.

At the same time, the retail cost of a model with PoE is 3,400 rubles , without PoE 3,200 rubles , given the quality and capabilities of the phones, this is one of the most profitable offers on the market.

VPN settings tab



If you need to connect your phone via a secure VPN channel, this can be done directly from the phone, without buying additional equipment (for example, a VPN router), the phone supports L2TP and SSL VPN. This is a very useful feature for several reasons.

Firstly, if you have several phones that need to be delivered to a remote office, there is no need to buy a VPN hub at each remote location, you just need to configure the VPN client built into the phone. Further, through the tunnel to register his phone on the IP PBX in the central office.

Using a VPN client solves both of these problems, so this useful feature will become increasingly popular.
