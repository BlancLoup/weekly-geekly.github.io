Whoer.net - service for checking your anonymity

We present to your attention a new service for checking information transmitted by your computer to the Internet - Whoer.net .

It is ideal for checking Proxy and Socks servers, tells you about your VPN server, checks the IP address for being in the blacklists, indicates whether ActiveX and Java are enabled on your computer, what its language and system settings are, what OS and browser are installed will determine the DNS, etc.

For your convenience, we have prepared an easy and advanced version of the resource, which displays a large amount of additional information. Also, there is an opportunity to choose the interface language and SSL-connection, according to your desire. Light design, simple address, great functionality and fast speed, this is Whoer.net .
