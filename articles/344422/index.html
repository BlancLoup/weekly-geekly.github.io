Targeting advertising budget in Ya.Direct

Advertising budget is a tricky business. That he is, then it is no longer there. And this can happen, not because your ad, in Yandex search, worked really well and many interested clients passed on it, but also because your competitors decided to spend some of their money so that you could lose yours.

Is it legal? Is it possible to? Yes, but first things first.

At the end of the article I will tell you why you should not engage in such activities and what this may entail for you.

When you realize that the advertising budget has merged and gave a zero result


Recently, I began to notice that more and more robots began to move onto the site for advertising. They sit on a page for 2 to 5 seconds, it happens that even 10. Refusals increase, money per click is written off, viewing depth decreases, behavioral drops.

I used to think that it was just a coincidence, nobody purposefully deals with this, who generally needs to bother. But how was I was wrong.

At one fine moment, the manager of the company, which deals with skipping the advertising budget in Ya.Direct, called me. Moreover, according to them, for this purpose they developed a special robot that fully emulates user behavior and is not recognized by Yandex as a bot. It scans the search, follows different links, but the main task is to follow exactly the first three links of the advertisement, under the search bar. And if you are a client of this company, then the address of your site is entered in the white list and no one will follow your links.

At first I decided that this is another razvodily. Give us the money, we will remove the competitors and other noodles. He began to be interested in this company on the forums and directly at Yandek himself.

On the forums, of course there was a mass of incomprehensible reviews and comments. Angry, laudatory and other, nothing worth attention. But the answer to Yandex puzzled me.



It turns out, Yandex is well aware of these guys. But nothing else, "We try, money is not written off for bots, let them exist for themselves," they do not want to undertake. Yes, the price tag for these guys is quite substantial, on average, 45-50 thousand prepayment. But then there are a lot of people who are willing to spend such a sum. In some niches of business, such an amount is spent on advertising for a couple of weeks or even days.

After a little more search in the forums, I found some interesting reviews and comments, sort of like an employee of Yandex.

As for ordinary users, whose budgets may suffer. Agree to the services of these guys or not, your business. But you need to remember one truth:

Even if you have paid for competitor clicks, he can also pay for clicks on your ad. No whitelists can help. After all, if in the end everyone will cooperate, then whom should they link then?

Still decided to order a competitor?


Then consider the following:

  • If the transitions are really carried out and are defined as real people, not bots, then you merge the budget to the competitor, there is no doubt. But did you consider that the CTR of a competitor can grow in proportion to the number of clicks?
  • As we know, the higher the CTR, the cheaper the clicks in the future. You can spend your money trying to knock out a competitor's ad, drain its budget. At first, you may even succeed, but when his CPC starts to fall, and your costs for bots grow, because you have to click more, then you will realize that you have spent both budget and time in vain.
  • The amount spent on sklikivaniya, you could throw in your advertising campaign and get good customers. But alas, you donated the money to someone, it is not clear why, and the competitor also began to spend less on advertising.
