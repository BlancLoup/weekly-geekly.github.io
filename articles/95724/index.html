Announcement of the WWDC 2010 audio broadcast



Caution! If you do not like Apple products, I advise you not to read Habrahabr, Twitter and news blogs in the next few days. Tonight at 21:00 Moscow time and at 20:00 Kiev and Minsk the Worldwide Developer Conference or WWDC'10 starts, which traditionally begins with the presentation of Steve Jobs. Check the time here .

To listen


According to the good tradition, Dean , Ponomar , Arina and I decided to hold an audio broadcast of the event. We do not pretend to an expert opinion, but we will cover this event from the standpoint of ordinary makovodov. Come, it will be very interesting. For those who do not have time to broadcast, we are going to record the broadcast.

It is worth saying in advance that none of us are present at the event itself. We will translate live what foreign journalists write, who are directly in the building of the Moscone Center and cover the event, and add their comments.

Audio broadcast will begin 15-20 minutes before the official event.
