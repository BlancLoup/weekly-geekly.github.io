Google is preparing a competitor Evernote

It seems that Google will soon start Google Keep - a service for creating text notes. The Android Police website reports that on Sunday, March 17, the service was available for a while online on a tip from a Google Plus user with the nickname 10 100 , but now the https://drive.google.com/keep/ page gives a 404 error.

Android Police journalists managed to save some screenshots and work a little with the application. Keep allows you to create text notes and to-do lists, attach photos to them.
