ZTE V881 smartphone review

Hi Habr!
In continuation of the review of our smartphones, today we want to tell you about the heir of the once popular smartphone ZTE Blade V880 - the smartphone ZTE V881, which received an improved technical stuffing and a new design.


ZTE V881

Design


Appearance of the smartphone: touch monoblock rectangular shape of glossy plastic. The bottom edges of the smartphone have a rounding from bottom to top, and the top and bottom of the smartphone have a semicircular shape. At the bottom is a microphone and speaker for playing tunes.



On the upper part on the right side there is a semicircular key of silver color for switching on / off the phone. There is also a standard headphone jack on the left side.



At the top of the back cover is a camera (5 MP) and a flash below it, and a second microphone for noise cancellation near the camera eye.



On the left side - the rocker volume control sound and under it - a connector for charging. On the front side of the phone in the upper part is the speaker, and on the left side of the speaker there is a light and proximity sensor.



On the front side at the bottom there are three mechanical keys, a context menu, ‚Äúhome‚Äù and cancellation of the last action. When the charger is connected, a red LED lights up under the mechanical menu key. The front panel is two-tone, with black plastic around the screen and white trim.



There is nothing on the right side of the phone.



Equipment


The phone is packed in a black box, the box is small, almost square.



In the box itself is located the smartphone itself, the battery, the brand warranty card, a quick start guide, a microUSB cable, a charger, and a headset / headset.

Screen


Screen with a resolution of 480x800 pix. great for watching videos or photos, and for surfing the Internet.
