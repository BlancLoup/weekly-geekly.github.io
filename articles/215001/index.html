All the problems of Sony Xperia Z1 Compact in one post

Intro


Hello to all.
For about three weeks now I have been going with the Sony Xperia Z1 Compact as the sole and main smartphone, and I want to tell you about all its negative features. I want to immediately warn that the article contains only constructive criticism of software and hardware, as well as ways to solve most problems. So the drama and tragedy in the spirit of "aah! all is lost, return the money "do not wait.



To begin, praise Sony. The release of the Android smartphone on a compact size with top-end filling has become, for many, a breath of fresh air in the world of spades. Now Xperia Z1 Compact is the most desirable device on version w3bsit3-dns.com.



The post is designed for people who are still going to purchase the Z1 Compact. All information is reliable at 146%, confirmed by personal experience, as well as _multiple_ reviews on large resources w3bsit3-dns.com and xda.

The film on the screen by default


The smartphone is equipped with a tempered, scratch-resistant glass (and not Gorilla Glass, but something of its own), sealed with protective factory film. In fact, this is already an old story for Sony, they have been doing it for a long time. At first I thought it was a big plus. Plenochki people we love to glue. And, for a start, it must be purchased, and then it must be installed evenly and without bubbles.

In reality, it turned out:
1) Sony film attracts dust in his pocket. I do not know what laws of physics explain it, but it is so. My last Motorola Droid Razr M phone always had a clean screen.
2) Factory film scratches pretty well.
3) If you remove the factory film, then you lose the logo (also an old story).

The solutions are obvious:
1) to score (like me)
2) Glue on top of another

Lottery with screens


Also an old story. Sony puts in one model screens of different manufacturers. These are JDI (Japan Display Inc) and AOU (AU Optronics Corp). JDI is considered better. Airplane mode


I was surprised to find that starting from version Android 4.2 for some reason, they prohibited third-party applications from switching the phone to the ‚ÄúAirplane‚Äù mode. I'm very used to the fact that the device automatically switches to this mode at night. First, no one will disturb the sweet dream, and secondly, the battery is not wasted at all (Former Motorola did not lose a single percent in 7 hours). Curve scrolling


Hold the phone with one hand and thumb down the page. Instead of scrolling, we get an increase in scale (pinch to zoom is activated). It happens very often. Annoying. Hopefully they will fix it in the next update.

Delay on the display from the proximity sensor


Standard functionality of any smartphone. Make a call, bring the z1 compact in the face, the proximity sensor turns off the screen so that it is not pressed with a cheek. When the phone is removed from the face, the screen remains off for another 1-2 seconds. At first I thought it was nonsense. But in life is very uncomfortable. And if you communicate with the robot, wandering around the menu, it turns into horror.

Solution: wait for the update or get root and edit ro.telephony.call_ring.delay in build.prop

Problem with launching the camera and unlocking in Stamina mode


If you use the corporate energy-saving mode of Stamina, then be prepared for the fact that pressing the photo call button will work through time. Also, the device may not respond to the power button (very rarely, but it happens).

Solution: do not use Stamina and wait for the fix in the update
