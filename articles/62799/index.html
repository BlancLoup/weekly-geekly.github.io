HP Mini 5101: 10-inch Business Netbook



Hewlett-Packard announced the Mini 5101 netbook. The ten-inch machine is aimed at business users. Depending on the version, the model is equipped with a screen with a resolution of 1024x600 or 1366x768 pixels, a 160 or 320 GB hard drive (optional 80 or 128 GB SSD), an integrated EV-DO / HSPA modem and an almost full-size keyboard (95% of the norm). At the same time, the prices for the mini-laptop start from $ 449. Sales begin at the end of July.

Among other features, you can specify a magnesium alloy body and an acrylic panel protecting the display.

Characteristics of HP Mini 5101:
OS: Windows XP Home, SUSE Linux Enterprise 11, FreeDOS
Processor: Intel Atom N280 with a clock speed of 1.66 GHz
Chipset: Intel 945GSE Express
Graphics: Intel GMA 950
Memory: up to 2 GB DDR2 SDRAM 533 MHz
Display: 10.1-inch, with a resolution of 1024x600 or 1366x768 pixels
Winchester: 160/250/320 GB (7200 rpm) or 80/128 GB SSD
Sound: HD audio (stereo speakers)
Camera: 2 MP
Communications: Ethernet 10/100/1000, Wi-Fi 802.11 a / b / g / draft-n, Bluetooth 2.0, EV-DO / HSPA
Card reader: SD / MMC
Connectors: 3xUSB 2.0, VGA, RJ-45, microphone, headphones
Dimensions: 262x180x23 mm
Weight: 1.2 kg
