Clipardo.com - search by outline

We want to offer the attention of a respected start-up community, which rekub and I have been doing over the past few months during our free time from study / work.
Clipardo.com is an online service that searches for raster and vector graphic design elements: clip art, logos, icons, etc. - on a rough outline by hand. The images among which the search is carried out are posted on various Internet sites, in particular, in paid and free clip-art collections.

The user interaction with the service is simple:
1. The user on the service page draws an approximate black-and-white (monochrome) outline of the object that he needs to find with the help of a mouse, stylus or other graphic index.
2. As a result, he is given links to color images from the source sites, as well as small copies of these images for preliminary familiarization.
Here is a screenshot showing how the service works:


An example of a more complex search, a screenshot was sent to me by one friend:


As you can see, the service finds a lot of not very relevant pictures, but similar ones are also found.
In general, the service aims to create a sketch for searching as fast as possible (in a few strokes): there is no need to specify either the color gamut or the relative size and location of the object on the sheet (figure). Service, unlike many analogs like Gazopa , TinEye , Retrievr, is not designed to search for photographs, but to help create illustrations, diagrams, mind maps, presentations, posters, etc. It is possible in some approximation to consider service as a way to quickly translate the general outlines of an object into its detailed image.
