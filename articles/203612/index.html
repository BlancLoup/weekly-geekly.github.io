Turn any webcam into a powerful microscope

Hello, habra users! This post will show you how to make a high - quality microscope from an old webcam . Make it really easy. If interested - continued under habrakatom.




Step 1: Required Materials


  • Actually, the webcam itself
  • Screwdriver
  • Super glue
  • Empty box
  • Brain and some free time


Step 2: Opening the webcam


To begin, open your camera. But be careful not to damage the CMOS sensor.


You need to extend the wires of the capture button to get still images. I also got wires on / off the LEDs. They were gray and yellow (you may differ).


Step 3: Using the Lens


Now we need to flip the lens over the CMOS sensor. Place it in 2-3 mm from this sensor and fix it (for example, with superglue).



Step 4: Assemble the camera


After turning the lens over, assemble the camera back. Now it is ready for use as a microscope.


Step 5: Final Stage


Now you need to fix the camera on the box, as shown in the photo. Now she is ready to receive images!
