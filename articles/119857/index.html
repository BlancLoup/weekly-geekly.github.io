The bottomles
Ukrainian application developer, Pavel Alexandrovich, in his service The Bottles focuses on a wide audience - lonely and curious people.

The Bottles is an anonymous messaging service with game functionality. Now The Bottles exists as an application for the AppStore (7000 downloads). When launching the application, the user sees floating bottles with notes, which can be opened, read, leave a comment or start a correspondence without registering.

Among the competitors of The Bottles are Distant Shore and Messege in a bottle applications. Pavel considers bright design and the flow of bottles, which ensures the constant appearance of new messages and undying user interest, as his main advantage. +++

Viptalon
Viptalon service is designed specifically for lovers of discounts and sales. Users (and there are already about 40,000) are given the opportunity to receive a variety of discounts on purchases in stores.

Visitors simply choose one of the offers presented on the site, pay for a discount vip card, print it out and present it at the checkout in the store.

Discounts are available for various types of products - clothes, shoes, household goods, etc. However, the creators of the project basically do not offer discounts on services - in their opinion, this is their main competitive advantage over the few competitors. +++
