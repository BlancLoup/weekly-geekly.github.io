Tesla Model S vs Lamborghini Aventador: the results of an experimental race



Tesla Model S P85D - a great electric car. But can he get on the race track headlamp to the headlamp with the Lamborghini Aventador? Allen Wong, a software developer, and in combination, a millionaire, decided to check who is faster. The results of this test Wong posted on YouTube.



The fact that the Lamborghini Aventador was faster, there is nothing surprising - it is still a supercar, which was created for speed lovers. The boundary speed indicator for this car is 320 kilometers per hour. As for the Tesla Model S, here the indicator is 250 kilometers per hour. So the winner was known in advance.

Nevertheless, Tesla had an advantage in the early stages of the race, when the cars accelerated. From 0 to 80 kilometers, Tesla accelerates faster than the Lamborghini Aventador.

Then, from 80 to 130 kilometers per hour, cars go "headlamp to headlamp" (2nd gear on the Lamborghini Aventador). But after 130 kilometers per hour, when the Lamborghini Aventador moves to 3rd gear, Tesla is left behind.

It should be noted that already now Tesla launches an experimental station for changing batteries for their cars. The cost of changing the battery (while the execution time of this procedure is about 3 minutes) will be less than the cost of refueling a gas tank of a regular car with good gasoline.

And the network of elektroAZS is constantly expanding, so that soon new electric refueling will appear throughout Europe. Moldova, Russia, Ukraine will receive their points of electric stations.
