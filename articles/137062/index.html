Secure Payments with ActivateTo Codes (Part I)

Online sale of goods and distant work, when the customer and the contractor do not meet in person, imply a certain risk. Even under the condition that the payment is made and the product is sent to the consumer, online fraudsters can intercept or simply steal funds or sensitive confidential data. Safe and guaranteed payments in a closed environment are necessary, and ActivateTo from PaySto will help entrepreneurs and consumers of their products.


The consumer of the service can be both legal and physical person. With the help of ActivateTo, you pay for digital goods and services. Among them are activation or upgrade of accounts in various systems, activation of services from various providers, payment of license keys for software, replenishment of wallets in social networks and online games, various digital (information) products and, finally, gift certificates and coupons.

Of course, if an entrepreneur uses ActivateTo , it means that he has an account, Personal Account in the PaySto system. Here, in his account, funds are collected on a personal account that can be withdrawn at any time and in any convenient way for the user, be it transfer of funds to the bank account of an entrepreneur or transfer to a convenient electronic payment system.

Among the advantages of ActivateTo , first of all, the guarantee of receipt of payment for the product provided.
