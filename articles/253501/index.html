The domain zone .com is 30 years old

The era of domains began back in 1985. The first registered domain became symbolics.com, the record for which was created on March 15, 1985 and has not been deleted since. It is this date that is considered the birthday of the .com domain. To commemorate this event, we offer you a light Friday post about various interesting and strange things associated with domain names.


The first domain zones were .com, .net, .org, .edu, .mil, .gov, delegated as early as January 1, 1985, and in the same 1985, in February, the first geographical domain zones began to appear. Domain registrations in those times were rare. In 1985, only 6 .com, 1 .net and 1 .org domains were registered.
