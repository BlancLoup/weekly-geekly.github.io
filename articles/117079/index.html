The results of the auction for the sale of domains in the zone. RF revise

The results of the auction for the sale of domain names in the zone. RF will be revised. According to the agency, the decision was made on April 7 by the Coordination Center of the national domain and the new management of RU-CENTER, the largest domain registrar.

The RU-CENTER management changed, and the new manager, Alexander Panov, offered the Coordination Center a list of measures to resolve the situation: allow domain names to be entered into the unified register in accordance with the auction results, then collect complaints from dissatisfied RU-CENTER customers and consider claims in individual order. In addition, RU-CENTER will return money to the winners of the auctions, who will give up the acquired names, and put the names themselves into the so-called quarantine, that is, temporarily make them inaccessible for purchase.
