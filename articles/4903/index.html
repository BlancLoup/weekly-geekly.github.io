Q & A systems compared

Q & A sites have become very fashionable lately. The undisputed leader in this genre is Yahoo! Answers, which leaves in the shadow of other similar projects, and in fact they are many.

Which of these sites actually answer the questions better? Where are the real experts? To find out, the MIT Technology Review journalist spent several days scrutinizing six sites: Yahoo Answers , Microsoft Live QnA , Answer Bag , Yedda , Wondir and Askville . The results are published in the journal .
