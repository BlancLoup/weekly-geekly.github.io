Testing


Testing was conducted in 3 areas:
  1. load test from Antutu Tester - measured in "parrots"
  2. Battery charging test from 1% to 100%
  3. The test of maximum endurance in time.
    The same conditions were created for all the batteries: Wi-Fi and 3G were turned on all the time, the phone was used both for surfing and for reading for the same time, directly proportional to the capacity of the battery

findings


This post was created not to prove the last quotation from Captain Obviousness, but to check: are the batteries sent almost from the same city really, but at different prices (sample No. 1 approximately 8 times more expensive than sample 2) can compete together?
My answer is no:
  • irregular shape of the cover, which cannot be fixed without a file;
  • slightly smaller capacity and strikingly different amounts that did not pay off;
  • the lack of confidence that this miracle of Chinese thought will not blow your telephone;

You can buy 8 of these batteries and believe that one of them will suit your phone, but I would not risk it, all the same phone is more important. That is why the battery from the Chinese noname I put on the shelf, as a last resort, when you just need it, but there will be no alternative. But the chance that this happens seems so scanty, I take into account that my total capacity already exceeds 10 Ah.
