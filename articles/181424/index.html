Solid price


Sly Koreans obviously took into account the lack of serious competitors on Android in this size and set a seemingly indecently high price level: 17 990 rubles for a tablet with Wi-Fi and 21 990 with a built-in 3G modem. The amount of memory offered is the same - 16 GB. Supporters of the "apples" immediately giggled - they say, and our mini is cheaper (ten rubles, 13 and 19 thousand)!
