Samsung Galaxy S III - three on the top five

Display


The screen has a diagonal of 4.8 inches and a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels. The matrix here is Super AMOLED, which makes the screen very bright and sharp. Some colleagues even asked: Is this a sticker on the screen?

However, there are those who find flaws in this matrix. The fact is that PenTile is an array consisting of non-honest RGB pixels (red, blue and green subpixels in one cell). Instead, the pixels are staggered, and several neighboring points use a common blue subpixel. There are people who see this unusual lattice, and it annoys them. And if you are one of them, the company has something to say about this. According to Samsung marketing manager Philip Bern, the blue subpixels in the AMOLED matrices "die" faster than others, and if there are fewer of them in the new matrix, it will generally last longer. Thus, the company takes care of that the buyer does not have to change this wonder-smart smartly to something else too quickly.
