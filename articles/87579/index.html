Wortmann Terra Mobile 1745 - German 17-inches for 499 euros



The German manufacturer Wortmann AG has announced the start of sales of the 17-inch Terra Mobile 1745 laptop. The model is targeted at corporate customers and in the basic version is offered for 499 euros. Low price due to low performance. However, in an office typewriter, the main thing is the screen size.

The machine is built on an Athlon 64 TF-20 processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz, equipped with 2 GB of RAM, an integrated ATI Radeon X1200 video card and a 250 GB hard drive.

The display at the laptop is 17-inch with a standard resolution of 1440 √ó 900 pixels.

Among other things, you can mention the modules Wi-Fi and Bluetooth (optional), DVD burner, 1.3 megapixel camera and 7-in-1 card reader.
