Digium launches its own IP phones

Digium introduces a new family of high-definition (HD) IP phones designed exclusively for use with the Asterisk computer telephony system.


The official release of these phones to the market and the start of sales will be held in April 2012.



So what is the peculiarity of Digium's new IP phones?

These are the first phones designed to maximize the power and capabilities of Asterisk, which is the most widely used and open source open-source communication software in the world, as well as the award-winning Switchvox unified communications system from Digium.


Thanks to Digium's technology, which applies to both the server and the phone, you will benefit and benefit from maximum performance and unprecedented integration of the telephone system, as well as the unique ability to customize it to suit your needs.


And all this at a very attractive competitive price.
