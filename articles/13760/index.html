Lobster Modular Multimedia Gadget


The Yanko Design project never ceases to delight with unique, amazing or simply beautiful concepts.

Another new site is the multimedia device from Danilo Mangini's Lobster, which is able to increase its potential with the help of additional modules.

The developers believe that the device is like a tail of cancer, and therefore gave the device such a name.

At the heart of the device is an LCD display, which in combination is the base for plug-ins, which can be: mp3 player, GPS navigator, removable storage device, camera, and even a cardiometer. It seems that the modules will be cheaper than a specially-oriented product, therefore, you can assemble a device that meets only your requirements and costs less than a set of high-grade similar devices.
