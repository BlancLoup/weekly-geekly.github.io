Remote registration of SPD (FOP) in Ukraine

Hi habrayuzer.

I decided to share my experience and leave the information in the form of a small article-hint on SPD electronic registration (FOP) in Ukraine. Since at the time of my registration I was the first to do this with the help of new technologies, I decided to take this responsibility upon myself.

It was in August 2011, and I needed to register SPD. One of my colleagues remembered the law of the ‚ÄúElectronic state registration of enterprises‚Äù that had come into force, and since I did not like the prospect of registering it by mail or going to the place of registration to my bureaucrats, I decided to try.

  • Passport
  • TIN
  • Receipt of payment "For state registration"
