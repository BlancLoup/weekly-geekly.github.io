Published APS-package MODx Revolution



The previously announced APS auto-installation package MODx Revolution 2.0.0, successfully passed the certification and published in the public domain on apsstandard.org with Gold Application status.
The information primarily concerns hosting companies and owners of servers with installed host panels that support the APS standard (Parallels Automation, Plesk, Small Business Panel or SysCP).
You can download and use the APS package.
