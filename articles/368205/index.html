Tesla Roadster can now travel twice the distance without recharging

This is even more than the flagship model of an electric vehicle, the Tesla Model S, can travel without recharging.

Tesla Roadster was announced in 2008, and since then it has been somewhat eclipsed by the new model, the Model S.
