e-Registration in Russian

Recently, President Medvedev talked about how easily he managed to register a company in Singapore without leaving his computer. And I must say that we are approaching Singapore standards with large steps.

In April, the Federal Tax Service Inspectorate launched an e-registration experiment in Moscow. Due to this, notaries began to file businessmen documents in tax form in electronic form. This saved businessmen from many hours standing in line at the 46th IFTS.

So far in Moscow there are only five notaries who work according to this scheme. In order to file these documents, you need:
  1. Bring a notary fully formed, signed, but not stitched set of documents, including paid bank receipts for payment of state duty.
  2. The notary certifies the applicant's signature and scans these documents with you, and takes the originals to his archive.
  3. A container will be formed from the scanned documents, which the notary will sign with his EDS and send to the IFTS.
  4. After that, during the day, the notary will issue you a receipt, and on the appointed day you will come to collect your documents from the tax inspectorate.

The second option is possible when you yourself scan documents and create a package for sending, but for this you need a special electronic signature from the tax inspection.

To be honest, we are not satisfied with either the first or the second option. Therefore, the Dokumentook team has begun to develop additional functionality for electronic registration. But, since in this process a large number of nuances and much depends not on us, we managed to realize only a part of the conceived, yet too much manual labor. Now with the service, the registration process is as follows:
  1. For 15 minutes you prepare the documents in the "Documentation". Thus, you no longer need to go to a lawyer and pay from 3 to 5 thousand rubles. Tell us a convenient time for which we will write you to the notary.
  2. Pay receipts in the bank.
  3. At the appointed time you give the notary the prepared documents.
  4. Visit the tax and take the certificate.

The list of notaries is constantly updated. It can be viewed on the tax website http://www.r77.nalog.ru .
