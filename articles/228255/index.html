Rostec will open an office in Innopolis

This is a big event in the sphere of Russian IT, which usually does not come into the attention zone of Habr, so at first a small educational program: Rostec (former Russian Technologies) is a state-owned corporation, which includes a significant part of the technological production in Russia: from optics to concern "Kalashnikov", from VSMPO "AVISMA" to AvtoVAZ, from "Aviation equipment" to the concern "Constellation". A total of 663 companies , which are grouped into 14 holdings - nine in the defense industry, five in civilian industries.

The choice of Innopolis in the corporation is explained by the desire to decentralize the IT-environment in Russia, which is now concentrated mainly in the capitals. For the time being, Rostec is planning to settle RT-Inform, a Rostec subsidiary responsible for the procurement of equipment and software for the entire state corporation, in Innopolis . Due to the consolidation of supply volumes Rostec expects to optimize budgets for IT procurement by 30%. Hopefully, not to the detriment of the interests of individual companies belonging to the corporation. In total, Rostec allocated about 50 billion rubles for the purchase of IT equipment and software in 2014-2015. In addition to RT-Inform, Rostec plans to deploy its IT expertise and IT design center in Innopolis, but this will not be limited to: Rostec has plenty of plans: due to circumstances of international politics, the state corporation needs to expand the list of projects related to development and the introduction of Russian IT solutions.
