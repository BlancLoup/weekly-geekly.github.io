Photography: Convenience

Preamble

I want to immediately say that this is just a note and is designed to show you how to work with photos. In my description, I rely on the fact that a person has a camera that allows you to take pictures in RAW format. For those who do not have such an opportunity in the cell - just skip all the references to this and that's it.

Transfer to computer

Before copying directly, I create a new folder with the name of the place or event where the shooting took place in the shared folder for all the photos. Sometimes, in case of coincidence I add the date to the title.

Further, without any third-party programs, I copy photos directly from the card into the folder created for them. After that, immediately in the folder with photos I create two more subfolders: Edited and JPEG. The Edited folder is intended for photos processed from the Raw format that are saved there in TIFF format without compression (yes, the size of such a snapshot is about 30 MB, or even more, but for post-processing just in case you need the least compressed snapshot). The same frames fall into the JPEG folder, but in JPEG format. Why double? Everything is simple - for the Internet.

In the program, I mark the photos (asterisk), and she stores them in a separate collection.
