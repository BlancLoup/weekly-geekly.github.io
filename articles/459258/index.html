About rally

The rally lasts 36 days, in total, participants drive through 13 countries, the initial list of participants is 105 crews, the length of the route is almost 15,000 km.

Retro rally "Beijing - Paris" takes place every 3 years. It was first held in 1907, but there was a break and the rally of 2019 turned out to be the seventh in history and fourth in the history of Nizhny Novgorod (1907, 2007, 2016, 2019). In general, any rally changes the route and rarely passes through the same city, especially in the middle of the road, but we are lucky :-)

The contribution of participants is about $ 65,000 (or to be exact, then ¬£ 53,500), and this is without taking into account gasoline and travel expenses. Cars are delivered to the launch site by ships, and one, late, flew by plane.
But both we and the assembled citizens of Nizhny Novgorod were waiting for the crew going to number 1. The Contal Mototri Tricycle is a car that participates in the ‚Äúretro‚Äù class, produced in 1907. Car era Brass Era, the very dawn of the automotive industry. These French cars were produced in France from 1907 to 1908 by a company that made tricycles for delivery and mail. Compared with his other auto-contemporaries, he was very complex. The same tricycle took part in the first rally of Beijing-Paris in 1907, but failed in the Gobi Desert, and the crew barely saved their lives. They were forced to drink water from the radiator in order to survive, and the car itself remained not found in the sands of the desert. And now, after 112 years, the tricycle participates in the same rally, wanting to continue and finish the journey of 13,840 km, literally through centuries. Behind the wheel - insanely beautiful and courageous Anton Gonnissen, a real adventurer. It sounds romantic, but in fact, the car is dangerous and uncomfortable and the navigator allowed some indulgence, including a Harley Davidson saddle.

105 cars reached the finish line. July 7, the rally finished in Paris. Let's see who became the winner in the Vintage class and in the Classic class.

Chrysler CM 6 (8) - 2 place Vintage


The Chrysler CM 6 model combined a lightweight body, a powerful six-cylinder engine and a reliable chassis, so immediately fell in love with motorists of its time. In addition to interesting running characteristics, this car differed from its predecessors by a very comfortable cabin. In 1931 alone, 65,000 cars were sold, thanks to which the manufacturer was able to survive the height of the Great Depression that hit the United States in 1929. There is a version that in order to get the drawings for the future GAZ M11 and GAZ-51, it was necessary to connect the intelligence forces and the NKVD, with which they adequately coped.





Bentley 4 1/2 Le Mans (17) - 3 place Vintage


The Bentley 4 1/2 was originally an elegant and massive British car with a 4.4-liter engine, but 720 Bentley 4 1/2 Le Mans cars were also produced. As the name implies, the model was created for the prestigious 24 Hours of Le Mans race, which was the best advertisement one could think of. The goal was achieved in 1928. The steepness of the car does not end there: in 1932, it was just such a model, but with supercharging, set a speed record of 222 km / h.
