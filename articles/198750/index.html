Why do startups need a good "felling"?

This year, for the first time, a new startup competition Startup Competition will take place in Kiev. The battle of projects will be held at the Startup Addventure (Europe Venture Summit) conference. The peculiarity of this startup fair will be one prize place and an unprecedentedly large cash prize for Ukraine. In this post, we have pointed out several main reasons why startups from Europe and the CIS should take part in the competition.

Startup Competition will be held in Kiev in the NSC "Olympic" on December 4-5 of this year. Participants who pass the qualifying stage will have the opportunity to write their projects to venture capital investors from around the world. The conference will be attended by real rock stars of the industry. In particular, the founder and managing partner of the largest venture seed fund and one of the largest startup accelerators in the US is 500 Startups Dave McClure (Dave McClure) , whose assets include investments in more than 500 companies. As well as the founder of the world's largest networked startup incubator The Founder Institute Adeo Ressi , the creator of startups worth $ 2 billion.

At the moment, the organizers of the Europe Venture Summit - the American-Ukrainian full-cycle incubator Happy Farm and the American venture fund 500 Startups - are conducting a set of projects that will take part in the competition. At the moment, 280 teams have already submitted to it. Of these, 41% represent the United States, 40% - Europe, and the remaining 19% - the CIS.

Applications are accepted only from projects, the volume of attracted investments in which does not exceed $ 2 million. You can apply for participation until November 8 through the social network for startups and venture investors Angel List . Startup AddVenture (Europe Venture Summit) is the largest specialized conference that will gather more than 3,000 IT entrepreneurs, venture investors, business angels and incubators from Ukraine, the CIS, Europe and the USA. The event will be held December 4-5 in Kiev in the NSC "Olympic".
