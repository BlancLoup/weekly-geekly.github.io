uSpeech - the story of a startup

Hello! I would like to share with you thoughts about your startup.

For a start, what is it all about:

The service solves the problem of developing vocabulary and cleaning up the Russian language with our users.

After authorization in the application (only VKontakte is available now) you will receive ratings of your words, and then you will receive tasks. Each task contains 2 points:

1. A word that is best not to use at all
2. The word that you should try to replace synonyms

After sending a certain number of messages, your task is checked and depending on the success of the execution your rating changes.

How things developed:

Initially, for fun, there was a service WeSpeakIt, which gave users only the statistics of words that they and their interlocutors use most often. People used it, it was fun (without any investment in the project in a couple of weeks I had about a hundred people who are interested in it).
