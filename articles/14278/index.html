The smallest watch with a touch screen


IMOBILE company presented its new wristwatch - C1000. Their main distinguishing feature is the smallest size among the watches with a touch screen. Their size is slightly larger than the average men's watch. It is worth saying that a wristwatch with a built-in touch screen can be counted on one's fingers, and this one is undoubtedly the smallest.

In addition, the watch has a rather impressive functionality. In addition to supporting communications in GSM format, which will allow you to use them as a mobile phone, the watch supports Bluetooth, has a 1.5-inch LCD display with color reproduction up to 262,144 colors and a resolution of 176x220 pixels, built-in audio and video player and 60 MB of onboard memory .
Unfortunately, while the clock supports only Chinese. However, nothing is known about the price and timing of the release.
