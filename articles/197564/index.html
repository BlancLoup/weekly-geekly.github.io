Domestic projects on Kickstarter / part 2

From Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, 6 games, 1 gadget and 1 wallet are now launched on Kickstarter.

I want to talk a little about the four new projects on Kickstarter from Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, and another one to which definitely the Russians are related. And also I will remind you those projects about which I already wrote earlier in this habraposte and which increased their fees.



1. Universum: War Front - a game that combines many genres: RPG, shooter, real-time strategy, MOBA and more and more.
Interestingly, the game is positioned as developed in just a year and a half by just one person! This was an excellent informational occasion, and all the top American publications wrote about the project. Although both article readers and backers still hardly believe that one person could do all this =)

The project has already raised $ 37,687 from $ 20,000 and will last 17 days.



2. Skyjacker - a space shooter with the ability to break everything and everyone, and even shoot the necessary part from an enemy ship, then to fix it and fasten it to your own. This is a very colorful game project with a huge cooperative universe. This project is being launched for the third time, before this the team has already raised funds for the production of the designer for their space ships, now it‚Äôs time to populate the universe!)

The project has already collected $ 15,918 of $ 150,000 and will last 38 days.



3. The Mandate - here in these guys I doubt if they can be called domestic, but they have a Russian-language website and a Russian-voiced Kikstarter movie. The game itself is a real-time space tactical strategy with RPG elements in the universe of the Great Cosmic Russian Empire. Everything is pathetic, imperial and pathetic again, I like it.

The project has already collected $ 94,932 of $ 500,000 and will last 47 days.



4. Lords of Discord - a project from the Russian studio Herocraft from Kaliningrad. Very beautiful art, not quite neat 3D, but the project looks very nice in general. The game itself is more than fully reminiscent of Heroes and Disaplays.

The project has already collected ¬£ 3,623 from ¬£ 60,000 and will last 24 days.



5. Oceania is an online MMO RPG and its second attempt to enter Kickstarter. The game makes us happy with a knight in full armor against the background of the beach, the sun and palm trees =) This is a game with an open world, active interaction of players who have a great influence on the development of the world itself. It sounds interesting, but, lately, a lot of MMOs with sandboxes have appeared, in my opinion.

The project has just started and has already raised $ 661 from $ 50,000 and will last 29 days.

And now I will remind you of some projects from my last post that have not yet ended, but have significantly increased their fees!


1. Petcube is a home gadget that allows you to watch and play with your pet using a smartphone, even when you are far away from it, and a mobile application with video broadcasts and the ability to play with friends' pets.

The project has already collected $ 160,001 out of $ 100,000 and will last 21 days.



2. Steam squad - turn-based tactical wargame, which takes place during the First World War in an alternative universe.
And the authors of the project are guys from St. Petersburg!

The project has already collected $ 50,138 of $ 50,000 and will last 2 days.



4. Victoria Wallet - another thin wallet.

The project has already collected $ 10,257 from $ 800 and will last another 20 days.



We wish the guys good luck!
