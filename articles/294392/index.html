Apple investors did not appreciate the presentation: corporate quotes are falling



According to the results of the trading on September 9, Apple shares fell immediately by 1.9%. besides, after the session was closed, Apple‚Äôs quotes fell a little more - this time by 0.2%, writes Slon.ru. It is worth noting that at the beginning of the presentation there was an increase in stocks, but this trend was short-lived. Perhaps investors were affected by the fact that nothing unexpected was announced at the presentation.

Recall that this time Apple showed a new iPad Pro tablet with a stylus, Apple TV, plus two new smartphones - 6S and 6S Plus with 3D Touch technology.

Analytical company BTIG Research presented statistics on the dynamics of the value of Apple shares on presentation days. As it turned out, over the past three years, Apple quotes during the presentation and after it fell, the average level of decline is 0.4%. According to experts, this situation is due to the fact that users expect very much from new Apple products, and, as a rule, these expectations are not met.

Over the past three months, Apple quotes have decreased by 14%. Nevertheless, the corporation remains the world's largest capitalization, with $ 628 billion.
