Guest lecture by Ruslan Tagiyev at the Higher School of Economics

Announcement:

On November 18, at 18.10, a guest lecture by Ruslan Tagiyev (General Director of TNS Gallup Media) will be held on the Internet measurement methodology, as well as on the results of the Web Index survey. The lecture will be held in the framework of the educational program " Management in the field of e-business and Internet projects ", as a guest lecture. The entrance to the lecture is free, but by appointment, as the entrance to the HSE itself by passes.

TNS today is the main meter of the Runet (sites) in humans. Measures with the help of surveys and a panel (continuous research) and gives out regular statistics on the largest sites (about 2 hundred sites) of the Runet. Statistics is available and free for advertisers. Most likely, if you attend conferences, you have already seen excerpts from these studies regularly. The study itself has existed for more than two years and now since spring of this year it covers not only Moscow, but all the major cities of the country.

The lecture will be held at the Higher School of Economics in the building on Kirpichnaya (Semenovskaya), where the faculties of management and business informatics, as well as the VSBI are located. Duration - 3 hours (until 21.00 approximately, but as luck would have it, it happened that at 11 it was all over).

You can sign up for the guest lecture in my blog or here (it's better on the blog, but I will try to sign up here as well not to lose). If you have a pass to the HSE (or student), it is not necessary to register.
