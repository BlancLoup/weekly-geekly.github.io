I want to tell you about readyto.travel - a special ticket search engine developed by rtt .
Its distinguishing feature is its own route search algorithm, which allows you to find unobvious cheap and fast flight options.



How is this achieved?

Selection of transplants


Barcelona - Los Angeles, September 17 : $ 341 against the option for just over $ 900, which everyone has. By the time the combined version is even slightly faster.


Of course, there is some inconvenience to this option. However, I think the difference in price of 2 and a half times is an essential argument to think about this variant of the route.

You can give another example of the connection within the country: Novosibirsk - Budapest, August 30


The routes that competitors have in this area are at least one-third more expensive, and even thus often several times longer.

Departure from another city


When selecting transplants, our algorithm also takes into account that for greater savings, you can offer a flight from another city, which can be cheaply reached by land.
