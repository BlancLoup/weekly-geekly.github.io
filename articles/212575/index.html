DevCon 2014: the most resort conference



Hi, dear software developers and testers. The preparation of DevCon 2014 - our largest conference specially arranged for you is in full swing! Today we are ready to share with you the description of the venue - the natural resort of Yahonty .

The DevCon 2014 conference is a unique event that will be held outside the city for the fourth year in the picturesque natural resort of Yakhonta. Let's get acquainted with the features of the venue or, if you have already been to our conference, remember this place again.

Recently, our team of organizers visited Yakhonty and took a series of photos, which we gladly share with you. Some of the photos taken from the official website of the resort.





In the center of the resort is a large, very beautiful lake.





Today the lake is covered with snow, but by the end of May it will be pleasing to the eye.







The resort itself consists of several low-rise buildings with rooms and a large number of small individual cottages. Neat paths are laid between the buildings, and the buildings themselves are surrounded by greenery.









Our visitors, participants of the DevCon 2014 conference, will live in such nice cottages. Their interior decoration can be viewed on the official site .





And this is the main building and the main conference area. Today there is a lot of snow in this place, but by the summer this place will be greatly transformed.







Recall or learn how it was last year at DevCon 2013 will help you this post with a large number of photos .

See you soon at the conference! I remind you that the tickets for the last DevCon 2013 ended three months before the conference. Hurry up to take your place at DevCon 2014!
