I played on Topol for almost half a day, and I can highlight the following points. The first is that the tablet showed a very good level of autonomy in the "game console" mode. For the first time, the battery with a capacity of 4,000 mAh was asked to charge about five hours after the pilot launch of Asphalt 8. The screen brightness was set to 80%, and all wireless modules were turned off. I think if you activate 3G or Wi-Fi, then we get about more than four hours of autonomy. This is a very good indicator: usually tablets with 4-core processors, which are not able to provide the same high performance and level of graphics in 3D games, work for a comparable time without recharging.

The second important point - bb-mobile Topol 'practically does not heat up. That is, of course, during the "sessions" of active 3D gaming, it becomes warm, but certainly not hot. Apparently, there are two reasons for this. The first one is the well-chosen material of the back panel: the metal (and the ‚Äúback‚Äù of the Topol is made of just a thick but pleasant to the touch piece of metal) dissipates heat well.





The second reason is precisely in the features of the MediaTek MT8392 chipset. Like most MediaTek chipsets from 2013-2014, CorePilot technology is implemented in this model ( details ). Its essence is in the selection of the optimal configuration of the processor cores to perform a specific task. Roughly speaking, if for ‚Äúsmooth‚Äù gameplay it is necessary to activate only 3 (4, 5, 6 ...) cores out of 8, then the system will do just that. Plus, they will work at the lowest possible frequency, so that the toy goes without slowing down, and there was no heating of the case, and no extra energy was spent. That is why the "Topol" very decent indicators of autonomy.
