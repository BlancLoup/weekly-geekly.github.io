The Russian version of Visual Studio 2010 is already here.


On May 21, at the ReMIX conference, Microsoft introduced the Russian version of Visual Studio 2010 , which was announced on April 12, 2010 at the launch of Visual Studio 2010. This is the first release that took place simultaneously with the release of other localizations. Visual Studio 2010 is the only fully localized development environment. All its components are presented in Russian, including an updated interface, IntelliSense autocomplete technology, a debugger, and pop-up hints. Documentation for the entire Microsoft development platform - the MSDN library , is also fully localized and distributed for free. MSDN newsletter readers can be the first to evaluate localization, visit the MSDN online library right now . For MSDN subscribers, the Russian version of Visual Studio 2010 will be available on May 21 from 10:00 AM (Moscow time), while trial and Express versions will be available for download . In the near future, Russian versions of Visual Studio 2010 will appear in the programs WebsiteSpark , DreamSpark and BizSpark .
