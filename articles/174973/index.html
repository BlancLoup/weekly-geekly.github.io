New version of Yandex.Navigator: Expanded coverage of 3D map mode



Today a new version of the popular Yandex.Navigator application has become available, the main change is a small redesign and update of the 3D road map of Russia .
Now your summer house is also in 3D

Yandex.Navigator is one of the best applications that turns a phone or tablet into a full-fledged navigator that knows about cameras and traffic accidents, and even knows how to speak Russian and understands voice commands.


Official changes in the new version:
- updated map of roads of Russia;
- Improved application design;
- Improved display of traffic jams at an overview scale;
- Improved consideration of traffic jams and maneuvers when building a route;
- fixed bugs



Please note that now the current version of the map of Moscow and Moscow Region is January 2013, I think that it is not necessary to transfer the map to get the actual data, the application itself does not offer this, but just in case, you can also transfer ~ 250MB not such a big figure.
(~ 250MB - weight of the map of Moscow and Moscow Region)

At the same time, the size of this map has changed, downwards; previously, the size of the map of Moscow and Moscow Region was ~ 261MB

And the most interesting is that Yandex decided to expand support for the 3D map, a random village near Sergiev-Posad was chosen for the test, and in it, country houses are drawn in 3D (!).



No, this is certainly not Street View from Google and Apple's 3D map mode, but damn it with such a pace of development, I think, Yandex will soon present something similar.
