Creating a web studio: it's easy

After reading the topic The history of my company: from a hobby project to an Internet company, I decided to talk about how we opened a web studio in Volgograd in March of this year.

The studio has three founders, we all worked in the same Internet agency (programmer, project manager and myself, an Internet advertising specialist). At a certain moment, we realized that it became close to us in the existing conditions, it‚Äôs time to change the situation. Considering that in Volgograd, in our sphere, career growth is almost impossible (it was not possible to change the awl of one studio for soap of the same reason) we decided to play our own business. No sooner said than done.

First you need to decide on the name. During the brainstorming, we took into account three conditions: 1. Companies with this name should not exist. Or in extreme cases there should not be many of them in the Russian Federation. 2. Free domain name in the zone ru.
In the end, after a few days of searching, we had a whole bunch of weird and not-so-so-many names like: Muraka-Baraka, Nick Cave, Ogenry, Keitblansh, Magwai, Donnie Darko.

We chose Magway. At that moment I was unaware of the existence of the Gremlins film , the other two simply forgot about him. This is how the Magwei Web Studio - the creation of sites in Volgograd .
Our main activities: website development, advertising (seo, contextual, etc.), corporate identity + further creation and development of our own projects.

Office search
We were looking for an unsprained room up to 25 meters, with a reasonable price and not too far from the city center. Some tips for those who are currently searching for what you should pay attention to:
1. Price, layout, state of the office, the presence or absence of air conditioning, from which side the sun rises, where the blinds go - all you need to know first.


2. Does the price include utility payments and electricity? Sometimes include, sometimes not. Does the amount include cleaning the room or the cleaner will have to pay separately? What other services are included in the amount?
3. Phones. Usually connect for money. If the phone number belongs to the landlord, then he will most likely take money to connect them to your office. Sometimes a more expensive option - the number will have to buy from the provider, in Volgograd, it costs 6000-7000 rubles. for every.
4. Infrastructure. Is there parking, tasty and for what money they feed in the nearest cafe and is there any at all? Elevators or should I take the stairs? In our building the elevator is turned off at 18-00, it‚Äôs good that we didn‚Äôt take an office on the 7th floor. Is electricity cut off at night? Toilets can also be checked. The toilet is the face of an office building.
5. Mode of operation. Many office buildings do not work on weekends - for some it may be inconvenient.
6. Internet. What providers are available. See their rates.
7. Do they take a deposit? Many landlords practice a mortgage in the amount of the monthly rental price. In case you leave and leave the skinned wallpaper behind you or don‚Äôt want to pay for the phone. If all is well, the money will be returned to you when you want to leave. If you plan an office for a long time - in fact, this money is lost.

Bank, accountant, hosting
The next step was to search for a bank. Here, three factors were important for us: the location of the bank (not too far from the office), the cost of maintenance and the availability of the Internet bank. Our choice fell on the bank Avangard. After 3.5 months I will say that I am not disappointed. We perform all operations with the help of Internet banking, I appear in the branch only to withdraw money for salary.
After opening an account, do not forget to submit information about it to the tax office within ten days. Otherwise, the fine - 5,000 rubles. We, by the way, have forgotten.

Hire a remote accountant immediately. It is quite inexpensive (we pay 1500 per month), but it will save you from talking to the tax and you will always be with whom to consult. This can be either a freelance accountant or a company employee.

Hosting Do you need or do not need your own web server? Everyone decides for himself. A license is required to provide hosting services, so for clients you only provide assistance in hosting, which should be noted in the contract.
But my opinion is better to have than not to have. Pros: all in one place, more individual approach, do not depend on server neighbors, reliability is often higher. Our server in Holland, while uptime 100%.
