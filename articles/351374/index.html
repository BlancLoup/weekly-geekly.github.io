Barcode scanners for USAIS: myths and reality, selection criteria

In the last article, we wrote about the new version of the Unified State Automated Information System 3.0 and the introduction of mandatory mark accounting of alcohol products in Russia. Now entrepreneurs are seriously puzzled by the problem of automation in accordance with the law.

There are a lot of myths about what reading equipment is suitable for working with the Unified State Automated Information System, from specific mistakes in recommendations on the websites of regulatory bodies, to marketing manipulations of the mind, which are used by equipment suppliers. In this article we will describe in detail what the equipment for the Unified State Automated Information System actually is.



Let's start with barcode scanners


Hand on heart, excitement and lack of awareness of entrepreneurs we, as a supplier of equipment, only on hand. Nalepil red bun with the inscription "for the Unified State Automated Information System" on the appropriate scanner in the catalog and catch the frightened customers in these five magic letters. What to believe


In the equipment catalog on our website , there are subsections in the corresponding sections, for example, ‚ÄúScanners for EGAIS‚Äù . When automating the work on EGAIS using a scanner, you need to know only one rule: the scanner for the EGAIS is a 2D scanner (that is, a PDF417 reading code). There are no other rules, all other characteristics, form factor, connection interfaces, etc. - all this refers exclusively to the requirements of the business processes of the enterprise and its internal commodity accounting system.

And if you see somewhere some special recommendations for the Unified State Automated Information System on any other grounds, except for the possibility of reading a 2D code - do not be fooled.

Recommended scanners for USAIS


If someone is not too lazy to plunge deeper into the choice of a scanner for USAIS from dozens of 2D scanner models, he will notice that two-dimensional scanners of the same manufacturer with almost identical characteristics may have a double or triple difference in price. Let's see why.

As soon as the EGAIS bill went through the rustle and it became clear what technical means it would need to be automated, the leading scanner manufacturers quickly put two plus two together. The main task of the manufacturer was to provide the Russian market with a suitable competitive 2D scanner model and eat off the maximum market share. And while our entrepreneurs here complained about the law, manufacturers of equipment over the hill already fought the idea is not childish. They developed new products and started to release optimized scanner models. We needed scanners with excellent 2D optics, while the cost price had to be cheaper. And the size of the Russian alcohol market required well-thought-out production plans and logistics.



Leading scanner manufacturers (Honeywell, Datalogic, Zebra, and others) have released scanner models, focusing on this Russian project, and positioning them as scanners for USAIS. In these scanners, software-optimized barcode processing is optimized, the device better recognizes damaged, worn excise or federal stamps, thanks to special algorithms and a matrix of increased resolution. But not without victims. To reduce the cost, manufacturers sacrificed multi-interface, bundling or packaging costs.

How to choose a scanner for USAIS


The choice is wide, but the rule is only one - the scanner should read PDF417. Manufacturers have taken care to make such models with optimally low price - they should be paid attention first of all.

When selecting equipment, we recommend to give preference to reliable manufacturers who offer models with decent technical specifications to the market. If you buy more than one scanner, and automate the retail network, equipment vendors may suggest that you test the scanner in action (evaluate ergonomics, read quality and speed, etc.).

When choosing a scanner for USAIS, pay attention to the warranty conditions from the manufacturer, they may be different (1 year, 3 years or 5 years). And also, we recommend paying attention to the quality of the scanner and its design.
