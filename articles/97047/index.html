New NOOK


That updated NOOK .
Now it will be without 3G and with a white cover (otherwise everything seems to be the same). Oh yes, even a little less weight: 329 grams instead of 343 grams before. Apparently, the 3G module at 14 grams pulls.
BUT! The most important thing is the price. Only $ 149 , now it will be a real killer Kindle.
