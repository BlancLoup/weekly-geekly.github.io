Speakers Corner: Meet Symfony2 in Kiev and Odessa with guru Lukas Kahwe Smith

Siklum company never ceases to please a variety of conferences and seminars with the participation of international experts. Speakers Corner is a format of short events-reports on interesting topics already loved by many, which have been hotly discussed after a long time.

This time, we invite PHP and Web specialists to our offices in Odessa on July 6 and in Kiev on July 10 to meet with experienced web developer Lucas Smith. Topic of discussion: Exploring Symfony2 by Code . Attention! The report and discussion will be held in English without translation.

About the report:

As part of the report, you will not see standard slides and presentations. We will study together the code elements in the editor, call commands in the CLI and look at the result in the browser, discussing in detail everything that happens. We will see how Symfony2 handles the request from start to finish. During the discussion, it will become clear how Symfony2 works, what are the advantages and main disadvantages of the approaches used. Some examples of open source solutions that extend the standard framework capabilities will also be considered. At the end of the event, everyone will take out for themselves the essence of the philosophy of Symbony2, the pros and cons of using and common confessions. You can view the files in advance at g ithub ->



About speaker:

Lukas Kahwe Smith

Engaged in the development of web applications in PHP since the beginning of the century! It was part of the PEAR team supporting some of the most popular components. Was co-release manager for PHP 5.3. Now he is one of the developers of Symfony2, PHPCR / Jackalope and also a contributor to Doctrine and other open source projects. Ultimate Frisbee is a hobby.
