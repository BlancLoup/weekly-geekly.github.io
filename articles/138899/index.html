Samsung Galaxy Beam - a new smartphone with a projector



Until now, there seems to be no smartphone models with built-in projectors. And if they were, then they were piece models that never became popular. Now, it seems, everything can change, since Samsung is going to release to the masses a smartphone with a built-in projector. This model will be presented at Mobile World Congress. And this is not a concept, as usual, but a real device with real characteristics.

The device configuration includes a 2-core processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, plus a 4-inch display with a resolution of 800 * 480 pixels. There is a 5 megapixel rear camera with a front VGA camera. The operating system will be installed Android 2.3. In general, the configuration is a mid-sized smartphone, which can hardly surprise the average user. The amount of RAM will be 768 MB.



But there is a zest, which just distinguishes the device from a number of similar models. This highlight - a projector (15 lumens). The projector, of course, can not show the image on the entire wall, but the projector is quite enough to show the presentation, though not in a particularly large format. The diagonal of the projection will be 1.27 meters, which is enough to demonstrate all that is necessary.

In general, quite an interesting project. And the phone is not bad, and there is a projector in it. Probably, if this phone becomes popular, projectors will be embedded in other phones. Maybe in a year or two no one will be surprised to see a projector on the phone being sold.
