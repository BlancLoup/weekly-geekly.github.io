Final selection in Plug and Play Russia

Hello!

We have already told that the well-known American business incubator of the Plug and Play Tech Center is coming to Russia. Global TechInnovations help make the first selection of semi-finalists.
Actually, the selection is in full swing, the teams slowly pull up to the GTI office for interviews. According to the results 10 teams will be determined, which will get to the final selection. Of these 10, 5 finalists will be determined, and this will happen this Thursday, October 13, at the Premier Plaza (this is where the Russian Venture Company is located). The event is open, to everyone - well. Just register in advance.

Those who arrived by 9:00 will take part in the presentations of Said Amadi (main of the original Plug and Play Tech Center), Sergey Belousov (founder of Parallels, senior partner of the venture fund Runa Capital), David Yana (founder of ABBYY), Andrey Romanenko (president of Qiwi group) and Ivan Nechaeva (Gene Director, Russian Navigation Technologies).

And the actual project performance (in English) will begin at 11:00. Format - 10 minutes to speak, then a discussion of the project by experts. The experts are Said Amadi, Sergey Belousov, David Yan, Andrei Romanenko, Ivan Nechaev, as well as Graham Kruk (general partner of Helix Ventures), Jan Ryazantsev (director of investment and expertise department of RVC) and Murad Sofizade (director of Finam Capital). After all the projects come out, the experts will confer for half an hour separately and determine the 5 finalists who will be the first residents of Plug and Play Russia.

Come, we will be glad to see you.
