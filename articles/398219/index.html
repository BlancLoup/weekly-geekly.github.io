"Avtofon" - a real way to find a stolen car

Cars are becoming more and more, and many of them are of particular interest to the hijackers. Despite the fact that anti-theft systems are being improved, the teams of intruders also do not stand still, and are developing more and more new ways to bypass the protection.



The easiest way to hijacking consists of several stages. The first is the inclusion of a jammer (or noise generator). Thanks to this device, a built-in alarm system of a certain type is deactivated (there are many of them, and far from all are eliminated in this way, there is also a variant with an algorithmic or replacement code grabber). Then a special carbide ingot is inserted into the larva of the door and lock, a key with a head, which is then turned.

A special electronic device is connected to the standard connector in the car, which allows you to register your chip in the memory of the immobilizer. After that, if there are no other protection systems, the car starts and the hijacker takes him to a special place. This may be the place where the car is taken apart. And maybe the next yard, where the car is left to "sucks". During the forced idle time, the auto batteries of the protective systems are gradually discharged (the standard battery is disconnected) and the vehicle becomes transportable.

What is AvtoFon?


This is one of the smallest GPS beacons on the market, which operate according to the principle described above.





The device is already out of the box. In the package - the device itself, a 2-sided tape for attaching the system and a cover (the beacon can be used not only for cars, but also for people). With such a beacon, you can, for example, send a child to school or track the movement of an important load.





To get started, you just need to get the device out of the package, turn it on and hide it in the car or give it to the person who will wear it. The batteries included in the package during basic operation (sleep and periodic awakening) last for 2-3 years of the device. In active mode, the charge will last for several days.



It is worth noting that the GPS chip is located in the case from the side of the logo on the case. Therefore, it is desirable to place this side up. And it is highly recommended to place the device so that there is not much metal above that will screen the signal.
