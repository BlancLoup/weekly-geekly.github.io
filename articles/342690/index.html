How to limitlessly * surf ** on a smartphone for 20 *** rubles a month

Good Friday everyone!

If the student's spirit (or the spirit of the miser) is alive in you, you want to experiment or you just wonder how to get around the restrictions, then I will tell you how to do it. The idea was lit in my head like a light bulb when I read the description of the new tariff plans. And she lit up at the moment when I read that the operator provides unlimited traffic to a particular social network for an amount from 10 to 30 rubles per month. How to use these options for unlimited surfing? Read under the cut!

In Vkontakte I mainly listen to music, because I connected this option for 20 rubles. Also, I have applications, games, including mine in Vkontakte. My game is a browser and in Vkontakte it is inserted via an iframe ... And it worked. No, she does not eat a lot of traffic, but what if instead of my toy to insert something more useful? Wrapped in iframe yandex.ru, google.com (ru) I received errors that you can not use these sites through iframe. Long or short, but I was satisfied with the option with the iframe search engine Bing. But not until the end)



Further, already through a search engine, we can open a significant number of sites. Including YouTube via the iframe already Bing'a and drive a bunch of traffic. But I was satisfied with this half-way version a little - I wanted accessibility to all sites. Further, having placed on a shareware hosting page that can bypass the same-origin property (which blocks the use of sites via iframe), I got an option that was a little closer to the real no-limit * for surfing ** for 20 *** rubles a month .
