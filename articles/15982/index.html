HP does not want to release cameras

According to the information that appeared on the respected dpreview.com website, HP intends to cease self-development, production and distribution of digital cameras in order to direct all its forces to a more profitable business: producing home photo printers and providing photo services online.

However, all this does not mean that cameras with HP nameplates will completely disappear from the shelves. The fact is that the leadership of Hewlett-Packard intends to find an OEM-partner who will be engaged in the production and distribution of digital cameras under the HP brand. The company plans to sign the corresponding contract in the first half of next year. Until then, HP will sell its cameras on its own.
