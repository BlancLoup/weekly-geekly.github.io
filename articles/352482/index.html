Prehistory


Mobile Dimension is a mobile application developer working in foreign markets, and, in general, we found it logical to participate in the MWC Shanghai last year, and in the MWC Barcelona this year.

Last year we went to Shanghai as participants in the Russian pavilion organized by the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media of the Russian Federation. We went to Barcelona with the Russian Export Center and this time we had our own stand at the pavilion.

I'll tell you how it can be done significantly cheaper


The Russian Export Center is an organization that is engaged in all kinds of support for Russian exports (including non-raw materials) abroad. The support program also includes co-financing (partial funding), i.e. indirect material support for SMEs. I translate into Russian - if you are a small and medium business, you can get co-financing for your participation in a major international event, both as part of a Russian delegation, and separately. Under indirect support, it should be understood that no one pays you money directly, but takes on a fairly large percentage of your costs. The total amount is different each time depending on the specific event, but from myself I can say that it very significantly saves you the marketing budget + gives you a lot of interesting opportunities, such as an extra badge or other benefits from being on a national stand.

For reference: At major exhibitions such as CES, CEBIT or MWC, there are almost always national peacock exhibitions. They represent themselves young companies, united in a national pavilion.

What is the advantage of participating in the national pavilion?


A very simple. If you are an abstract startup HighTech Mobile Innovation Inc, then the maximum that you can count on MWC is a stand of 6 squares for big money with a simple building. And, what is the most annoying, such giants as Nokia, Samsung, ZTE, Huawei and others, redeem the area for the years before the exhibition and take precedence over all others in choosing a favorable place. As a result, small stands are often stuffed into the far corner so as not to spoil the plan and development engineering. This year places were sold even in the corridors between the pavilions. It is expensive, meaning - mercilessly little. There is gigantic traffic in the corridors, and on the one hand it is good, but on the other hand, in order to use it, you have to make a huge amount of effort to get their attention.


In general, what is called, taste and color ... But from simple logic, participation on the stand of the national delegation of 100 squares is more profitable than on the stand of 6 squares in the corridor. A large stand is more noticeable, higher, attracts more attention.

What else does participation on the national stand give?


Of course, you will be printed in the pavilion catalog, as well as various business programs and business events, meetings with business missions from other pavilions and other cult mass events. Otherwise, the main meaning is everywhere - participation.


What is required and what awaits you?


First of all, to be morally prepared for collecting, filling out and sending a large number of documents, incl. in the originals. With this sign, everyone who has ever prepared a tender, the list of documents is approximately the same. You will need an extract from the SME and from the Unified State Register. If you have a special person in the team who can be entrusted with this - consider yourself lucky. And remember that even if at first no one warns about this, then at the end you will surely request originals by mail, be prepared for this and keep each piece of paper as the first spring flower. To begin with, you can make an application for registration in the system (ie, become an exporter) and, having passed this stage, assess whether you are ready to continue collecting papers. In the comments I will be ready to share links if someone needs advice on the process. Get ready for the fact that the whole procedure takes a lot of time. Pavilion 8.1 was fully dedicated to mobile applications and development, promotion and mobile advertising. If you are a mobile developer and are planning to participate next year - write to the organizers and ask where they plan to host the developers room. Why did this turn out to be important? Because many who come to the MWC, come immediately with specific tasks, to specific companies and with pre-arranged meetings and do not go through random halls to click the beak. The result is that if you are a developer, but you are not in the hall you need (in our case, not at 8.1), your audience may simply not know about your stand. Our booth was located in Pavilion 6 and it turned out that this was not entirely successful. In Shanghai, there was no such problem, as there were almost no mobile developers there, and the exhibition itself was less.

Who knows, perhaps the organizers will start splitting the rooms into Apps, Health & Wearable, IoT and other categories to make it easier for visitors to navigate. But so far such a clear boundary was only in hall 8.1. - mobile applications.

Think over the system of contests on the stand, do not load anyone with a business program, hire promo girls, put a mustang on the stand and pour beer, give a smart watch and invest in your stand promo. So, we got to the conclusions.

It makes sense to participate if you:

  • Mobile developer or sell service for developers
  • Make anything wearable - gadgets, medical devices, fitness trackers, headphones
  • Make software with versions for mobile devices
  • You have implemented mobile technology in your company and want to tell the whole world about it - the Mercedes Benz stand presented the Smart concept car and the new A-classe stuffed with electronics and mobility
  • In general, anything related to mobility, even if you produce batteries


Participating for sure does NOT make sense only in one case : if you produce something that is not translated (not localized) at least in English. Going to the exhibition is advisable only with a fully translated and finished product.

Should I participate without a stand, walk and hand out flyers, get acquainted?
Yes, but it is not accurate. The entry ticket of the simplest level costs one thousand euros + travel expenses. He gives only passages to the exhibition. To get to the additional lectures, you need to get a silver pass, it is immediately much more expensive. If there is no opportunity to put up a stand, then surely it is better to go just as a visitor than by any means. But at the same time, you need to understand that there will be a million people like you, mixed with a million promotional personnel with leaflets.

It will be difficult, but fun.
