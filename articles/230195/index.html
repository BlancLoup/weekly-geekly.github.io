Pivothead main features



The device, as already mentioned, has a built-in HD-camera. The camera allows you to write everything that occurs on the way, as well as family parties, meetings, hiking and everything else.

Of course, you can get photos, not just video. The resolution of the camera matrix - 8 MP.

All data is downloaded to an internal 8 GB flash drive. For their points, developers sell various add-ons, including, for example, an external AIR SYNC drive, where you can store up to 6 hours of video (data is downloaded via USB cable).

AIR SYNC is also equipped with a WiFi module, thanks to which the device can be connected to a home or work network, as well as to form a Wifi bridge with the ability to simultaneously connect 6 people to the Network.

Weigh glasses very little - only 50 grams, so it is unlikely to interfere with the owner. Thanks to high-quality lenses, the presence of glasses can be forgotten.

Lenses, by the way, have several types of coating:

  • Scratch coating;
  • Revo Coverage;
  • Hydrophobic coating;
  • Anti-glare;
  • Gold and silver plating / light filters.



The material from which the frame is made is hypoallergenic.

The manufacturer offers several models of glasses Pivoted, but in this case they differ only in design, all characteristics of different models are similar.
