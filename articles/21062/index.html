Blogger.com Call and Voice Blog Posts

With the help of GrandCentral , a free service from Google, you can receive phone calls and voice messages right on your blog. Although the system is currently undergoing a period of private beta testing, you can not wait for the final ending, but register right now.

Web Call Button

After you add a special button from GrandCentral to your blog, readers can easily call you or leave your voice message without seeing the real phone number.
You can view calls by accepting them or sending them to voice mail. In addition, it is possible to block unwanted callers altogether. You can learn how to add a call button to your blog here (eng). As far as I understand the service (calls) is available so far only in America.
