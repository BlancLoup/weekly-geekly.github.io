Ivan Ponomarev and Nikolai Potashnikov on the display of tabular data, Celesta and Flute on jug.msk.ru

September 21, 2017 at the meeting of the Moscow community of Java-developers were made by Ivan Ponomarev and Nikolai Potashnikov. Ivan - with the report "The hidden complexity of everyday tasks: the display of tabular data," a joint story of both speakers - about software products Celesta and Flute open platform for creating business logic in the Java ecosystem.



About speakers


Ivan is the project manager for the development of the KURS company and the leading developer of Course Orchestra platform. In addition, she teaches at MIPT and participates as a speaker in JUG.ru conferences.

About reports


Andrei Kogun opens the meeting, recalling further current events from the world of Java . The main event of this day is the long-awaited release of Java 9 .



In the first of three reports, Ivan talked about non-trivial aspects of the implementation of the presentation of data in a table form. Under the seeming simplicity of the task was hidden many pitfalls. The report was a modified version of the previously presented at the JPoint 2017 conference.



The second and third reports dealt with software products Celesta and Flute . Nikolai began by making a general overview of the platform.



Ivan continued, focusing on the technical details. The presentation was complemented by a demonstration of the code and architectural features.
