What is JUG.EKB
JUG.EKB meetings are not just new and relevant information for participants. This communication with non-indifferent to Java and new technologies, this is an easy dialogue with speakers and useful acquaintances!

Here everyone can find a favor. Experienced developers will be able to ask difficult questions to speakers, take part in discussions, solve problems of their own projects, consult or, conversely, share their experience with colleagues "in the shop". Or even prepare your report and get a new experience! Beginner developers at JUG.EKB meetings will be able to find like-minded people, learn a lot of new things, understand what else they need to learn in order to grow as a developer.

Alexey Stukalov and Yury Artamonov (HAULMONT, Samara)

Do you know the feeling when an update of a framework on a long-standing and stable project explodes the code and turns it into a red-crossed mess? As developers of the CUBA platform, we are painfully familiar. So, during the existence of the platform, she changed 3 ORMs, 3 major versions of Vaadin, not to mention the Spring Framework.
