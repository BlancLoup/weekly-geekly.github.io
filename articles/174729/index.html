Ukrainian laws in the new ipLex application for Windows Phone 8

A new free mobile application ipLex has been released. Laws for mobile devices on Windows Phone 8 OS .

ipLex. Laws are a collection of all applicable codes and fundamental laws of Ukraine.
It is installed completely on the device and works offline without access to the Internet.
To update the revisions and the composition of the documents, a fresh version of the application is released monthly.
Documents can be searched by words in the text and details, as well as by thematic rubricator.
Inside the text, the search by context works.
Favorite documents, for quick access, can be placed in the folder "My Documents".

ipLex. Laws are a mobile "legal first-aid kit" . The application will help in court, at the exam, on the road and in other similar situations when you need to refer to the laws, but there is no computer and the Internet at hand.

You can download the application from the Windows Phone Store

For reference.
Earlier versions of ipLex were released. Laws for Adroid, iOS (iPhone, iPad)
The developments were made as part of the startup ipLex - the leader in the field of mobile legal systems of Ukraine .
In addition to free versions, ipLex provides Ukrainian lawyers with a commercial version of ipLex.Profi .
This is the only complete mobile legal system in Ukraine. It works both on desktop platforms Windows, Linux, Mac OS, and on mobile - Adroid and iOS (iPhone, iPad). In the near future, plans to develop a version for Windows Phone 8.
