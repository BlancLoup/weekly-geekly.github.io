QIWI and Payoneer collaboration

Good day to all, I want to tell you about a new opportunity for debit card holders from Payoneer . What kind of cards are these and who will be issued will not be distributed, the company is quite well-known and tens of thousands of webmasters from Russia have these cards in their hands and actively use them. The essence of the novelty is that now having a Payoneer card and a wallet in the QIWI system you can tie your card to the wallet and completely officially and without intermediaries replenish the balance of your wallet.

Why do you need it? Unfortunately, a huge number of Russian services: payment of utility bills, payment of mobile, Internet and television providers, payment in Russian online stores, payment of fines for traffic safety is not available with credit cards in principle, or there is no payment from a card issued by a non-Russian bank with this twice in the last week, even the payment for the RIF could not be done on the one hand. On the other hand, Russian webmasters hold a huge number of Payoneer cards for which they receive payments from Western affiliate programs, freelancers exchanges, etc. Until recently, in order to pay for example utility services with the money on the card, you had to withdraw money from the card at an ATM, then either go to the post office (to the bank), or go to the nearest payment terminal in order to replenish your wallet in QIWI, now everything has become It is much easier to simply bind your card to your wallet in a couple of clicks.
I hope that this information will simplify the lives of many Payoneer cardholders.
