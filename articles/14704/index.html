Frisling, sport for singles



It is known that Shaquille O'Neill is so fast that he can pass the ball to himself. But even this brilliant quality will not make the game of basketball with itself an exciting occupation. Group sport with yourself is generally a problem. But, as usual, but not for Japanese scientists .

Tireless experimenters from Tokyo have developed a "frisbee for the lonely." The novelty combines a flying saucer of a frisbee , and a toy yoyo . A simple mechanism returns the plate directly to the thrower. Almost eliminated the risk of losing the plate, or throw it somewhere, without hope to get back.

Frisling (that's what this funny thing is called) has one more attractive feature: practicing with it in a public park you will surely attract the attention of others. Who knows, maybe this attention will be the first step to chatting and making friends with whom friesling is not needed.
