Smarthalo - a system that will turn the life of a cyclist into a paradise



The Smarthalo system is intended, first of all, for cyclists who ride in the city. Here and a reliable security system, and navigation system, and a fitness tracker for cyclists. In addition, the system also includes lighting that works exactly when it is needed. If the cyclist departed from his ‚Äúiron horse‚Äù, Smarthalo will reliably guard the bicycle.

In general, all this sounds interesting, but is everything really good? Let's look at Smarthalo for details.

Pivoting navigation


Cyclists often need a navigation system that is not inferior to car devices. Plus, a cyclist usually doesn‚Äôt have much time to navigate, to look at the map in more detail. Smarthalo developers claim that there will be no problems with this system. You just need to connect Smarthalo to your mobile device, set a point to which you need to get, and start pedaling.

Smarthalo will show whether the cyclist is going in the right direction. If not, the indicator will show it. If all is well, the green LED will shine soothingly and evenly.



As it approaches, more and more green LEDs will light up, and the circle should become completely green at the target.

Fitness Tracking


Naturally, almost every bike owner would like to track his progress - how much distance is traveled, how fast, how many calories the cyclist burned. Plus, the total arrival time and everything else - that you usually expect to see in such an application.



There is also the ability to set goals, as well as track their achievement.

Illuminate the road


Smarthalo is equipped with a light sensor. If it becomes dark, the LED light turns on. If the cyclist stopped and moved away from the bike - the lighting immediately goes out. If you need to go again, the lantern turns on. Everything is organized quite reasonably and rationally.



Notifications and weather


Cyclists, like no one else, will come from the weather. If it starts to rain, and even a hurricane - the race becomes often impossible, and you have to look for shelter. In order not to get wet in vain, Smarthalo warns the owner if the weather starts to deteriorate - the forecast is taken from the relevant services, using the Internet channel of the phone.



If someone calls, sends SMS or e-mail - Smarthalo will notify about it.

Safety system
