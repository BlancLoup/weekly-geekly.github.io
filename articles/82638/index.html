Exwork.Ru - become our sucker!

Do you think free is only cheese in a mousetrap? And no. There are also the creators of the site Exwork.Ru, who are looking for suckers partners and pay them for viewing ads. This is in theory, but in practice everything is quite the opposite. I registered and immediately after registering about the miracle - 32 rubles as a gift to the account. It remains to earn another 418 rubles, and then you can withdraw money through WebMoney, Yandex, Visa or MasterCard. 4 days I went to the service and spent 1-2 minutes to see promotional articles, websites, banners. And when I earned 160 rubles, the service got worried, God forbid someone steals them from me. Yes, to stop this terrible attempt to ruin my account, the service says that you need to go through 3 steps to confirm your account. We choose from the list the country in which we live.
