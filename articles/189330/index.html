BitByte Festival is a meeting place for the IT community

On September 21 , with the active support of the Aichi-Event.rf portal , Digital October will host BitByte , the annual festival of professional development in the IT sphere, on its platform. The festival will bring together gurus and newcomers to the IT sector, giving them all the conditions for a technical and career "upgrade."


In a programme:

Parallel sections of speeches of technical and career orientation. Visitors will receive answers to their questions from experienced technical experts, will hear career development tips from HR directors. Everyone will be able to practice at master classes and get feedback from market experts on them.
The main topics of the presentations: trends and the future of the IT market, programming languages, mobile development, business reputation, career planning. Speakers will share their own successful cases, technologies and tools.
