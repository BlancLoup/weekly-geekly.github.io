Overview of the auto.ru mobile application for the Android platform

We are continuing a series of reviews of the Android application interfaces, prepared jointly with UsabilityLab . Read the previous review of the series here .

About the application


The auto.ru application allows you to view materials from the portal of the same name from your smartphone. So, using the application, you can search and view ads for the sale of cars and motorcycles, as well as add individual ads or search queries to Favorites. In addition, the application has the ability to read articles about test drives, reviews about certain models, news of the automotive world and leave your comments.

Users generally positively appreciate the functionality of the application, but at the same time pay attention in their comments to the lack of some features. For example, Anton Kirillov , commenting on an application on Google Play, notes the most popular of these functions:
‚ÄúThe application is very good and convenient, but: 1. Give the opportunity to choose the price of a car manually or reduce the range, 2. Make sure that the cars sold are removed from the favorites automatically, 3. You can‚Äôt see the number of hosts on the TCP. Thank!".
Average user rating of the application - 4.0. Apparently, it turned out to be lowered due to a number of problems that had arisen in previous versions - the application does not detect all the ads posted on the site, and constantly ‚Äúcrashes‚Äù. However, when testing the current version, these problems did not manifest themselves.



Briefly about the main thing


The application is executed very succinctly both in terms of structure and content. It contains only a few sections that are not overloaded with functionality.

However, when working with the application, there are several main problems:
  • lack of ad filtering options: there are not enough criteria for ‚Äúcolor‚Äù, ‚Äúnumber of owners on TCP‚Äù and options related to bundling;
  • lack of filtering by make and model in the section with test drives;
  • the absence of any indicators in the "Favorite" ads that have lost relevance.

In addition, users familiar with the capabilities of the site, make the following suggestions to the functionality of the application:
  • Add additional options for finding ads (in response to an already indicated problem);
  • add the ability to publish ads for the sale of the vehicle;
  • put in the application a separate section for commercial vehicles, as is done on the site.

Turning to the application for the first time, the user sees the splash screen with the auto.ru site logo. This screen is replaced by the main, where all sections of the application - ‚ÄúCars‚Äù and ‚ÄúMotorcycles‚Äù (announcements for sale), ‚ÄúFavorites‚Äù, ‚ÄúReviews‚Äù, ‚ÄúTest Drives‚Äù, and ‚ÄúNews‚Äù are placed.

"Reviews" and "Test Drives" contain articles about specific car models. The last section contains the latest news on automotive topics.



Search ads


The main task of the auto.ru application is to search for ads. At the moment, it implemented a search for cars and motorcycles.

Accordingly, when going to the ‚ÄúCars‚Äù section, the user immediately goes to the filter, where he can set such parameters for the search for ads as ‚Äúcity of placement‚Äù, ‚Äúbrand‚Äù and ‚Äúcar model‚Äù, ‚Äúprice‚Äù, ‚Äúyear of production‚Äù, ‚Äúmileage "," State "," customs ", select the type of gearbox, steering, body, engine characteristics, as well as select the seller, the time of ad placement and adjust the display of ads with photos. Users themselves note that for a full-fledged selection of a car, they lack some options for selecting a complete set, which, for example, are in the filter on the site auto.ru. The most critical of them - the color of the car and the number of owners on the vehicle passport - it would be logical to put in the application. The second option is to take out all the parameters of a complete set, hiding them under the button ‚ÄúAdditional options‚Äù, as it is implemented when selecting motorcycles.

It is worth noting that the filter contains quite relevant drop-down lists for most fields (‚Äúmake and model‚Äù, ‚Äústate‚Äù, ‚Äúcustoms‚Äù, etc.). Another problem that a user may encounter when setting parameters for searching for ads is that you cannot include in the filter, for example, several cities, makes or models of cars, and body types. Referring to the individual filtering options, we will focus on choosing the region and brand of car. So, the regions are not sorted in the most obvious way - alphabetically within federal districts. It is more convenient to place the administrative units in the list strictly alphabetically.

Favorites


Users of the auto.ru application in their reviews say that in cases of price changes or loss of relevance of an ad added to favorites, this is not reflected in the system. Because of this, the following situation may arise. The user liked the car, and he added her card to the "Favorites". After some time, the owner sold the car, and the ad disappeared from the site, but remained in the "Chosen" in the application.



If such a problem really exists (it was not revealed during testing), then announcements that have lost their relevance should be noted. It is not recommended to delete them automatically from the ‚ÄúFavorites‚Äù, because the user will not understand exactly what happened to them and why they disappeared.

Oh good


The developers of auto.ru presented quite a high-quality and convenient application that allows you to search for cars and read about cars. Simple control allows you to quickly get comfortable, and extensive content attracts not only potential buyers, but also to motorists in general.

Design


Without regard to the functional, we can note the strong similarity of the application under consideration in the style of design with the application for iOS. This concerns the appearance of input fields and action buttons, range selection tools, as well as the location of some buttons in the interface. Ideally, all interface elements should follow the Android style guide .



Total


In general, the application makes a good impression, and the elimination of minor shortcomings in the short term and the expansion of functionality in the future will help make it even more popular with users.
