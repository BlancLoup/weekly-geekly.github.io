myOpenID closes February 1, 2014

Hello.

I would like to contact you personally and inform you that we have decided to close the service myOpenID. It will be turned off on February 1, 2014.

In 2006, Janrain created myOpenID to make online registration easier. Since then, social networks and email providers. emails such as Facebook, Google, Twitter, LinkedIn and Yahoo !, have adopted open identification standards. Now the billions of people who have created accounts through these services can use them to conveniently register on sites across the Internet as myOpenID intended.

By 2009, it became obvious that the vast majority of customers preferred to use an existing account from a well-known provider rather than create a separate myOpenID. As a result, our core business has changed to achieve the desired, and we have introduced social authorization technology.
