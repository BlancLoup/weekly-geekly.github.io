In the United States begins the action for the preservation of network neutrality


Photo: Chip Somodevilla / Getty

US users have 4 days, 11 hours and 50 minutes to save network neutrality in their Internet segment and to protest the procedure for canceling network neutrality , which was approved by the Open Internet Order of 2015, agreed in February 2015 by the US Federal Communications Commission.
