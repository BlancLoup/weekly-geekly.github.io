Results TopCoder Open 2009

The finals of the TopCoder Open championship in Algorithms and Marathon Match ended in Las Vegas.

Algorithms results:
1. crazyb0y - Bin Jin, China
2. UdH-WiNGeR - Vladislav Isenbaev, Russia, SPSU ITMO
3. marek.cygan - Marek Cygan, Poland
4. Petr - Peter Mitrichev, Russia, Moscow State University
5. Im2Good - Rune Fevang, Norway
6. lucasr - Lucas Agustin Rearte, Argentina
6. SnapDragon - Derek Kisman, Canada
6. syg96 - Stepan Gatilov, Russia, NSU

I recall that Vladislav Isenbaev also recently became the champion of ACM ICPC.
Task conditions can be read here (registration is required).

Marathon Match Results:
1. KOTEHOK - Andrey Lopatin, Russia, SPSU
2. Psyho - Przemyslaw Debiak, Poland
3. nhzp339 - Xiaoshi Lu, China

For the first time the schoolboy won the TCO-2009 Algorithm. Interestingly, he is also the current winner of TopCoder High School. Another interesting fact is that the second and third winners of the TCO-2009 Algorithm became the winners of the ACM ICPC in 2009 and 2007, respectively, and the winner of the TCO-2009 Marathon Match in 2000 and 2001.
