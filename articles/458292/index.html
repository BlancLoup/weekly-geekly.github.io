  • [RFC] Strict operators directive - PHP produces implicit type conversion for most operators. And since the conversion rules are rather complicated, this can often lead to unpredictable results. This RFC proposes to introduce a new directive, strict_operators , which will restrict type conversions and allow you to throw a TypeError error for incompatible operands. The document is very detailed and describes many examples, I recommend to see the whole. Also, the directive changes the behavior of the switch construction.
