Yandex. Search by comments

Yesterday, Yandex launched a search for comments on blog hosting sites. While this is LiveJournal and Lira But this is more than enough.
In every discussion, all remarks are important, and questions are usually asked for answers. Comments left on blog posts are often of no less (and sometimes more) interest than the posts themselves.

Starting today, Blog Search is not only looking for blog entries. We announce the start of beta testing by commenting.

You can try it yourself now: blogs.yandex.ru/search_comments.xml?text = happiness

Further read here .

Separately, I will highlight the ability to search for comments of a specific person: blogs.yandex.ru/search_comments.xml?text=author%3D%22dolboeb%22

At first glance, a useful feature, you say. But if you think about it, then this is all, you know, a pretty strong blow to the privacy of each user. Of course, the developers offer and assure that we can hide our comments and posts from being indexed, but yesterday the search engine indexed everything, including hidden discussions.

And this is not the case (by now they have promised to fix it). The fact is that it has now become possible without interference and sophistication, having entered a nickname, to see absolutes all the activity of a particular user - this can be said, akin to what a hidden camera takes you ...

The Internet is getting deeper into our life ... To be continued ...
