iceflow 1.0 beta 3 - extension for Opera browser

I invite all habra users of Opera browser to participate in the beta testing of my iceflow extension. This extension intercepts links on pages to YouTube-type video services and, upon click, opens the player over the page. Download the beta version here . When clicking on a link in Opera, a window will immediately open with the installation of the extension.

Currently the most frequently used services are supported: YouTube, Vimeo, mpora and PinkBike. As required and user requests will be added support for other services. Also, the extension has a settings panel where you can specify when and how to open the player, and there is also a list of exceptions where you can safely enter any sites and pages.

In addition, in the form of a small bonus iceflow "clicks" flash drives on those sites where the authors were too lazy to use swfobject and similar solutions. For example, on all the same YouTube.
