Amazon question and answer

Amazon launched the service Askville - an analogue of Yahoo! Answers and Google Answers . It is assumed that with the help of Askville, anyone can get an answer to any question from other users, and ask questions, unlike Google Answers, will be free of charge.

At the moment, the service is in the testing stage, to participate in which is possible only by invitation from the current user. A chat has already appeared on the Internet in which you can request an "invite".

Recall that not so long ago, a similar Russian-language service was opened - Answers@Mail.Ru .

Askville is another Amazon project that is not directly related to sales of goods on the Internet.
