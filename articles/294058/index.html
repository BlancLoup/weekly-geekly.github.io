According to SimilarWeb , headHunter has become the third most popular job site in the world. In this case, the first place is taken by the American resource Indeed.com, and the second - the French site Pole-Emploi.fr.



Among other domestic sites, SuperJob.ru (12th place), rabota.ru (39th place) and job.ru (50th place) were noted.
