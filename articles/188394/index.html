Nexus 7. What's new?



Today, the thirtieth of July 2013, the day of the official start of sales of the new modification of the famous Google-tablet Nexus 7 from Asus. However, the holiday atmosphere is not particularly felt, and all because this device is already being sold and bought in America. Having decided not to wait for the official release of the tablet, such large retailers as Amazon or, for example, BestBuy started selling Nexus 7 2013 four days ago - 07/26/13.

Is this release interesting? Definitely, especially given the fact that Google managed very well to tweak the combination of "price / quality" and made the tablet much more attractive to buy.

Another good news is that you can buy a new Nexus 7 on Amazon , and at the lowest price available today, which means that you will not need to fuss with the deception of the Good Corporation.

Characteristics of the device



And now a little about the hero of the occasion! However, just presenting the characteristics of the new Nexus 7 is not very interesting. It is much more interesting to compare them with what was before and what became after:



Well noticeable, the new Nexus is significantly superior to its analogue last year. And it can be seen in almost everything, but what really catches your eye is the magnificent screen resolution, which provides a decent pixel density of 323 ppi.

Battery

It would seem - why in the new model, at a time when power growth is obvious, did you install a smaller battery?
