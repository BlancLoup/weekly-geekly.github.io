Qt 5.0.1 and Qt Creator 2.6.2 released

It turns out that the Qt 5.0.1 update was released on January 31, with more than 400 improvements ( reportedly ) compared to Qt 5.0.0. The new version does not bring new functionality, only fixes and improvements.

You can view the full list of changes here: changes to qtbase , changes to qtdeclarative and qtmultimedia .

Binary compatibility with applications compiled in Qt 5.0.0 could not be fully supported due to bug fixes in the qtmultimedia module. Therefore, if your application used QtMultimedia plugins, recompilation is necessary. He promises not to let this happen anymore.

In Qt Creator 2.6.2 there are also numerous fixes, the full list is available here.
