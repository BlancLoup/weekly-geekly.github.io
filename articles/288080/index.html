How to get into the Technorati Top 100 rating in 24 hours

Technorati Rank depends on the number of blogs you cited over the past six months. From one blog is determined only one link, no matter how many times she met there.

To get into the Technorati Top 100 , you need to have about 4820 blogs that you cite. Blogs are in the Top 100 for several years, while it can be done in 24 hours! Well, let's start ...

The page shows the number of blogs referenced by you. On me (translation on behalf of the author) there were 3 links from a separate independent blog, displayed with different authority. Obviously, Technorati recognized them as 3 different blogs, although they were parts of the same blog. An investigation into the issue revealed that the links to each other inside the pages of this blog were also counted as ‚ÄúAuthority‚Äù to others. Somewhere there was a Technorati error? Let's get to her ...


Suppose in this example that you use Wordpress as a blogging platform and have Apache with mod-rewrite activated.


Suppose you have a blog www.exampleblog.com , then the pages should look like this:
  • www.exampleblog.com (Home)
  • www.exampleblog.com/2007/05/05/a-typical-post (Standard Post)
  • www.exampleblog.com/2007/05/05 (All entries for one day)
  • www.exampleblog.com/2007/05 (Archive records for the month)
  • www.exampleblog.com/tag/a-category (All entries of a selected category)
  • www.exampleblog.com/a-typical-page (Standard Page)



Technorati is able to find that all the above links correspond to one blog except for the last case - the standard page. If from this page there is a link to your blog or post in it, then this page will add "credibility" to your blog. Unless, of course, Technorati is aware of the existence of this page.


Confused, but we go further!


When you publish a new entry, the Technorati server is pinged by calling the rpc.technorati.com/rpc/ping page, after which Technorati retrieves the URL from the Referrer and navigates through it, finds all the links on the page and writes them to the database. However, this will not happen if you publish the page. The workaround is to manually ping from technorati.com/ping , this should increase your blog's credibility by one.


So, add the Begin With Me page to your blog with a link to a blog entry, it will be published at www.exampleblog.com/begin-with-me . Open the Technorati Ping page and add the URL of the new page (http://www.exampleblog.com/begin-with-me/). Within a few minutes, your page will be discovered by Technorati and will be displayed on the technorati.com/search/www.exampleblog.com page. The rating, however, can be added only after 24 hours, as it is not calculated on the fly, although it is determined at regular intervals - once or twice a day.


Pages that you ping do not have to be shown on the blog pages and they can be hidden for visitors / search agents.


And after all this, can I get into the Top 100?


In fact, it all depends on your quickness (the speed of creating new pages and their further pinging), as well as how quickly Technorati closes this bug. They can limit manual pinging by IP-address, or temporarily close the form of ping until they correct the error. Although, in the worst case, your website may simply be banned.


Now you have seen how this is done - you can test your strength.
