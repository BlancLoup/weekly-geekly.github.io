We launched a new startup: Pinme.ru
Last week, a social service for storing and sharing images Pinme.ru was launched on Wednesday. This is a new investment project of the company Fast Lane Ventures , which was created on the basis of the startup model Pinterest.com , which, according to Alexa.com, occupies the 327th place in the world in attendance and the 55th in the United States.

The concept of the project is a virtual corkboard to which you can pin a picture or photo with a pin, which is reflected in the logo. In the case of pinme.ru, these are pictures from an Internet that you can save in your profile on the site. To do this, you just need to run the script saved in the browser bookmarks. From each image you can go to its original source in the network, which provides these resources with a large reverse traffic, and for the user turns the service into a convenient storage of graphic information without departing from the context in which it was copied. Why this may be needed? An interesting case (using pinterest.com as an example) is described in the LJ community art_of_shopping - you can save pictures from the product catalog in an online store, and later return to it to make a decision - unlike the ‚Äúbasket‚Äù in the store itself, your saved goods will not go anywhere if you reboot or go online from another computer. In fact, this is such a notebook, like Evernote, only in pictures.

The creators of the project themselves are focusing on the social component, the ability to share and comment on the photo, calling it "Twitter for photos."
