Of course, the capabilities of this calculator are somewhat different from the usual functions of ordinary calculators, but the news is still interesting. By the way, the device itself is one of the models of the new line of graphic calculators manufactured by Texas Instruments. The calculator has a USB connector designed for data exchange with a personal computer. In addition, there is a flash memory of 27.8 megabytes. The calculator has a sufficiently high resolution and can even provide a separate window for each task.

Here are the characteristics of a calculator:

- Display size: 240 x 320 pixels (3.5 inches) - twice the size of the models TI-83 Plus and TI-84 Plus
- Screen resolution: ~ 115 DPI; 16 grayscale - five times higher than the resolution of the models TI-83 Plus and TI-84 Plus
- Size: 19.8 cm x 10 cm x 2.8 cm
- Weight: 290 g
- 4 AAA batteries
- Memory: 20 MB flash memory / 16MB RAM
- USB port for connecting to a computer, for exchanging information with other TI-Nspire handheld computers and much more.
