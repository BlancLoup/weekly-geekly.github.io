Apple Notebook Update

They waited! Today, February 26, Apple finally presented the public with a new line of MacBook Pros , in which we are waiting for new processors, the ability to install an LED display in the Seminashka, and the long-awaited touchpad with Multi-Touch support. Not without loss ...


So, the new machines are based on Intel Core 2 Duo Penryn processors with clock speeds of 2.5GHz and 2.6GHz. Made on the 45-nanometer technology, they consume less energy, which should most favorably affect the battery life.
