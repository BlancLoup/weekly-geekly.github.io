For 10 euros, everyone can be on-line homeless

In the German Internet is gaining popularity "game of the homeless." This is how Pennergame is translated - an on-line game, which was developed by two young men from Hamburg.

The number of registered members of Pennergame has already exceeded one million.
You can start the game by paying 10 euros. At the beginning of a virtual player - Hamburg unemployed, who can neither read nor write. His goal - to get rich. The instruction tells you where to start your journey to wealth. First you need to learn to read and write a poster to attract the attention of others. Then you should get yourself a few dogs - it makes people feel pity.

The game provides other opportunities to achieve financial well-being. In particular, you can steal watches, wallets and jewelry from passersby. You can collect and donate bottles, break a chewing gum vending machine or a sausage kiosk. Each "raid" brings a small profit.

As a result, the unemployed can become the owner of a chic castle in one of the fashionable regions of Germany.
