HYDROP: a reliable way to protect your shoes and clothes from dirt without galoshes and rubber boots



It is spring now, the signs of which are the same in many regions - these are puddles, dirt, soiled and drenched clothes and shoes. You leave the apartment clean, ironed and smelling pleasantly, and you come to work or meeting friends with dirty shoes and soiled clothes. In the spring, neither personal transport nor taxi helps. Sometimes a few meters of muddy sidewalk are enough to get dirty before the very purpose of your journey.

Exit here - either to monitor the cleanliness of the sidewalks and roads, or to somehow protect their clothes and shoes. In some regions, they are trying with varying success to fulfill the first point, but it‚Äôs not good at all and not always. In this case, go to step two. But what kind of protection can be used? Galoshes or rubber boots? It is possible and so, but there are more modern ways. In particular, the tool HYDROP.

What is HYDROP?


This is a highly effective water-repellent compound that protects the surface of clothing and shoes from getting wet. Accordingly, if the fabric or skin is dry, in most cases nothing can stick to it. This tool is produced by the domestic company Nanobarrier, which spent about two years and 8.5 million rubles on the development of the composition. As a result, the desired composition still managed to get. It has a strong hydrophobic effect. 93% consists of water, 6% - these are additional components plus another 1% - miniature particles of titanium and silicon dioxide. After the composition was received, the creators demonstrated its work in a simple but effective way: they dipped the tie of one of the meeting participants soaked in HYDROP in a smoothie (yes, without a smoothie - nowhere). As a result, there was not the slightest dirty or wet spot on the tie.

Now the company produces four different products with a completely different principle of operation. For example, the protection of clothing and footwear from moisture and liquid contamination, which works for suede, textiles and nubuck. This is HYDROP Textile.



There is also a tool that can remove even ingrained dirt of various types. It does not require the use of water. This tool is called HYDROP Clean.

Another product - HYDROP Fresh. This substance has deodorizing, antistatic and antibacterial properties. That is, the T-shirt will protect from smell, it will be useful for shoes.

The last thing worth telling is about the Phantom Reflective Spray. This tool will be useful for cyclists and motorcyclists. The spray is completely invisible on clothes during the day, but perfectly reflects the light of headlights and flash units at night.
