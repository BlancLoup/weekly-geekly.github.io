Linux USB stick + TFT

In the continuation of a series of Linux news in USB-key + VGA and Computer with Linux in USB-key .

Calao plans to launch a series of daughter cards that significantly expand the functionality of USB dongles. For example, for A9263 series trinkets, Calao will offer a DAB-USB-A9263-AVI board with audio input / output and a 4.3 "TFT touchscreen display with a resolution of 800x600.

A suitable designer for those who want to build a PDA on their own :)

Full list of products:
** DAB-USB-A9260-GPI **

* Daughter Board for USB-A9260 with 1x MicroSD, 1x IrDA, 1x I2C, 1x SPI, 1x OneWire, 1x Serial Port (RS232) & 1x Serial Port (TTL). Prototype made quick and easy (sensors, i / o exp., ADC, DAC, displays, keyboard ...)

** DAB-USB-A9263-AVI **

* Daughter Board for USB-A9263 with Audio in / out, 4.3 "TFT display interface with touchscreen (TDO43MTEA2 / 800 x 600)

** DAB-USB-S8815-AVI **

* Daughter Board for USB-S8815 with Audio in / out, VGA output, 4.3 "TFT display interface with touchscreen (TDO43MTEA2 / 800 x 600)

****** USB-S103V ******

* Usb key form factor based on STMicroelectronics Cortex M3 processor with 1x MicroSD, 1x IrDA, 1x USB device, 1x I2C, 1x SPI, 1x OneWire, 1x Serial Port (RSL232), 1x Serial Port (TTL) & 1x CAN bus. Prototype made quick and easy (sensors, i / o exp., ADC, DAC, displays, keyboard ...)

Since the products are still under development, the release date has not yet been determined.
