SimCity 2000 became free



It has been 20 years since the release of the legendary game SimCity 2000 , and now we can play it completely legally and for free. EA has laid out this game for free .

True, the publication of the source code speech is not going, but the game will be available for download for some limited time. EA advises to "act quickly."

SimCity 2000: Special Edition contains all the extensions and additional packages, including one that brings down more than a dozen of every disaster on a carefully constructed city.

This version has several qualitative changes compared to the original version, including different levels of land elevation, new buildings like schools, hospitals and prisons.

Well, good news for those who want to remember the past or make children go through the games of their childhood in the same order in which we ourselves passed them. This is what independent game enthusiast Andy Baio did with his son. There is a certain logic here: in a few years the child will study the entire history of video games.
