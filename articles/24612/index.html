Business Plan - Description of the notebook market

Continuing to write a business plan, publish a third note with a description of the market. According to the content of the business plan that I sketched from the very beginning, descriptions of the e-commerce market and the laptop market should be separate. This time I combined these points, because It turns out not so much information can be found that would make an analysis of the market. And all ready-made marketing research costs enough money.

It turns out that what everyone constantly writes and says is not so easy to find in numbers. Eventually I focused on the following sources of information:
  1. Association of Information Technology Enterprises of Ukraine
  2. State Statistics Committee of Ukraine - http://www.ukrstat.gov.ua/
  3. Gemius Ukraine ‚ÄúElectronic Commerce in Central and Eastern Europe: Ukraine - Free Version of the Report‚Äù
  4. Site http://kontrakty.ua/
  5. Site http://itnews.com.ua/

I recommend the first three to everyone. A lot of useful information. And now about the main thing:

The online trading market shows high growth rates. According to various sources, the growth of the e-commerce market in 2007. amounted to 60-80% (volume up to $ 250 million.). In 2008, an increase of 70-90% is expected [4]. This is due to the increase in network users in the country. The capital is a leader in online commerce, taking from 60% to 80% of all sales. At the moment, purely online stores are more popular, more offline shops with offices in the Internet [3]. The total number of online stores is 3549 [according to the rating bigmir.net]. The boom of Internet commerce is expected in 2009/2010.

The main problems in Internet trading are the fear of paying for goods online. A greater number of purchases are paid to the courier upon delivery (62%). [3]

The Ukrainian market presents the following options for paying for goods in online stores:
  • cash payment to the courier
  • payment by bank transfer through the bank
  • payment via WebMoney
  • Payment via Partmone.com by Visa and Mastercard
  • payment through IBOX terminals (UkrBuy.com)
  • payment through the payment system INTERKASSA (Webmoney, Rupay, E-Gold, Ukrmoney, Liberty Reserve, Pecunix, Limonex and others)

The following brands are on the market:
Acer (22%), Apple, Asus, Dell, Fujitsu-Siemens, Hewlett Packard, LG, MSI, NEC, Panasonic, Samsung, Sony, Toshiba.
