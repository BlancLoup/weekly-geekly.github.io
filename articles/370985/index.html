IoT - Philosophy of Education

The development of digital society is inextricably linked with the level of implementation of new technologies in the educational process. The synergy of innovative solutions and new philosophy in the field of education form the basis of "smart" education (Smart Education). Using the concept of the Internet of things allows you to make the learning process more interactive, and therefore diverse and interesting.



Assistant Sensors


Those who follow the development of the Internet of Things (Internet of Things, IoT) - one of the breakthrough trends in IT technology in recent years, no longer perceive the components of its "smart devices" as fashionable toys. An army of smartphones, sensors, webcams, GPS-navigators and other equipment connected to the network can solve problems in various areas - from production and agriculture, to medicine and everyday life.

And if the more well-known "smart" devices such as Smart TV, fitness trackers, or "UAVs" are very far from schools or universities, then there are a number of other, specific IoT devices. They make the learning process more flexible and lively, allowing the teacher or teacher to receive feedback from each student in a different than traditional form of two-way communication. With the advent of wireless communications and the IoT concept, it has become possible to transform the familiar attributes of the educational process, such as desks, blackboards and even classes or audiences, into virtual assistants for teachers and students. Using built-in IoT devices, they are able to capture attention in training and cut off distractions.

The capabilities of the IoT concept allow you to program various devices and applications included in the educational IoT platform for specific tasks. At the same time, they free teachers and teachers from many unusual functions, especially administrative ones, which allows them to devote more time directly to the educational process.

Of course, the massive use of IoT devices in the educational sphere is still far away, and even developed countries cannot yet boast of a total digitization of educational institutions. However, the growing number of devices connected to the Internet, their diversity, as well as the improvement of wireless communications, will certainly contribute to a gradual change in the concept of education, both higher and initial. And this process is actively going today.

Learn to survive


Of course, the full potential of IoT today can be used by educational institutions in countries with modern wireless infrastructure and appropriate financing. But gradually this tendency will prevail and dictate the demand for a new philosophy of education.

As economic processes in developed industrial countries show, there is always a demand for new knowledge and forms to get it. Today's business can be done by people with high creativity, quick decision-making, able to work in a team, and, naturally, technically prepared. However, they must constantly learn in accordance with the changing range of tasks. Thus, modern education is already considered as an investment, an asset, the formation and capitalization of which must be managed.



It is quite possible to achieve this by using solutions provided by the concept of the Internet of Things. Social learning


Yet, the technical component of Smart Education itself, despite all its importance, will not bring the expected breakthrough. The new philosophy of "digital learning" will involve many of its forms. As mentioned above, to be in demand, a person must constantly improve their professional skills and knowledge after graduation. And in this context, one of the solutions is the formation of communities in the form of social platforms, where people will be united by common interests, in particular, the desire to self-educate and share experience.



Of course, there are still many such professional resources. As you can see, Smart Education on the SIoT platform is a kind of fantastic space where the technical, educational and social aspects of modern human life will harmoniously connect.
