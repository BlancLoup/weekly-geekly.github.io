Ciklum supported the meeting of the Kiev Python-club

Simple truths have long been known: you need to meet. With like-minded people like you, and sharing your thoughts, experiences and the more often you do this, the more experience you will have - personal and professional.

So, for example, this Saturday, September 17, our office with a picturesque view will host the fourth open meeting of the Kiev Python Club.

This time the meeting will be friendly and preparatory for the conference PyCon Ukraine 2011, which will be held on October 22-23. The purpose of the meeting is to listen to speakers who want to speak at PyCon Ukraine 2011, if necessary, adjust their speeches, push them in the right direction and give them the necessary experience of speaking to the public.

The organizers promise speeches by the following speakers:
  • Igor Davydenko (oDesk) with the topic of testing django applications
  • Igor Pochechuev (DjangoStars) with the theme of Python GIS projects
  • Vasily Dyzhaka (DjangoStarts)


We, together with the organizers, invite all Python specialists to this meeting - to share professional hints and just chat. If you also have a desire to speak at PyCon - come to the meeting of the Python-club this Saturday, September 17 at 11:00 at the address Kiev, st. N. Amosova, 12, Horizon-Park Business Center, Ciklum company, 20th floor (SkyPoint) , or contact the organizers of Pycon Ukraine 2011

About Ciklum:
Ciklum very often supports various professional development communities in various cities, for example, in Ukraine (for example, in Kiev , Kharkov , Dnepropetrovsk ) and other cities, as well as in Belarus (for example, in Minsk ). Recently, our office hosted Chrome Hackathon & Contest , which brought together more than 30 Ukrainian developers.

Other details about our events can always be found on our website , facebook page , and twitter.
