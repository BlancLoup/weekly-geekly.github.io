  • I. Developer
  • Ii. Startups
  • Iii. Marketing
  • Iv. miscellanea

/ Moscow / 08/24/2013 / Cost 1 000 - 1 400 rubles.

The official international conference dedicated to the Magento platform, the development of online stores and e-commerce in general. Participation in it will undoubtedly be interesting for both Internet entrepreneurs and web developers.

Ii. Startups


Hackaton Hackaphone in Odessa
/ Odessa / 08.08.2013 / Participation is free
Competition teams, which for a limited time (about 40 hours) turn a bare idea into a working product. The presentation of application ideas will take place during Mobile Developer Day UA. We will select the 7 best ideas that teams will work on on August 8 and 9. The final will be held on August 10th.

Hackathon Garage48
/ Kaliningrad / 08/09/2013 / Cost 15 EUR
The essence of ‚ÄúGarage 48‚Äù is that after the presentation of ideas and the formation of teams, the participants try to create a minimally working product from Friday evening to Sunday evening, ready to present it to a potential investor already at the closing ceremony of the event.

/ Zaporizhia / 08/17/2013 / Participation is free
Participants will have 24 hours to write any application. The choice of technology used is yours. All participants are guaranteed to have lots of fun and pleasant communication, and the winners will get an interesting prize.

Iii. Marketing


Conference Mobile Developer Day UA 2013
/ Odessa / 08.08.2013 / Cost of 700 - 1200 UAH.
Developer Day for mobile platforms. This is a business part in the form of a conference for those whose business is related to the development of mobile applications, special workshops for programmers on development technologies, Developer Alley - an exhibition-presentation of studios and mobile projects, a space for communication with customers and, for the first time this year, Hackaphone - hackathon for mobile application developers.

Conference AppClub {build, monetize} Revolution
/ Odessa / 08/10/2013 / Cost 49 - $ 119
Much attention is paid to the design, development and testing of software products for various mobile systems, but the main focus is on promotion and monetization methods. At AppClub meetings, there are both recognized industry experts and young startups who have the opportunity to talk about their product and get feedback in the early stages of development.

Conference Apps4All SUMMER FEST
/ Moscow / 08/17/2013 / The cost of 10 000 rubles.
Leading developers, experts, representatives of world-renowned manufacturers of mobile devices and companies involved in the development of advanced mobile technologies will gather onboard the Gzhel motor ship to discuss the achievements and current problems of the mobile market during a walk along the Moscow River, and, of course, nice spend time in a great company!

Master class Secrets of selling texts + E-mail marketing: sales through mail
/ Ekaterinburg / 08.22.2013 / Cost 9,500 - 13,000 rubles.
The organizers combined two topics that are directly related to the sales of your business. For selling texts you: study a universal algorithm for creating selling texts; Create a set of marketing titles and text for your business. By email marketing you will learn: how to attract subscribers; how to write email sales; how to improve the effectiveness of mailings and much more.

Conference OdessaCamp 2013
/ Odessa / 08/23/2013 / The cost is 200 UAH.
This year OdessaCamp will open an exhibition of works by the best vector artists of the world - OdessaDraw. The conference will feature presentations on the following topics: communications: logistics, business processes on the verge; social media and marketing: SMM, SMO, social networking applications; startups: business planning, investor search, presentations, teamwork training, etc.

Iv. miscellanea


IT Battle Battle'13 Tournament
/ Minsk / 3.08.2013 / Participation is free
Sports and entertainment tournament, a mini-festival of colors and emotions, which will bring together the best IT companies, or rather their best fighters from all over Belarus. Participation in the tournament will be able to take everyone who is related to computer technology - programmers and designers, managers and accountants.

Festival Geek Picnic 2013
/ St. Petersburg / August 3, 2013 / Cost 300 - 6 000 rubles.
Russia's largest open-air, dedicated to modern technology, science and art. 20,000 participants, 100 lecturers and speakers, more than 50 robots, interactive museums, augmented reality, installations, open laboratories, lectures and master classes by world scientists, music and outdoor recreation.
