Dream Coders Team programmers talk about creating MPack

Russian programmers from DTC (Dream Coders Team) say they have nothing to do with the criminal world that uses their program MPack, which appeared a year ago on the Internet. Now this set of PHP scripts and exploits has become extremely popular for automating computer infection and botnet management. However, programmers say that heightened attention from the press may force them to stop selling the product.

About this in an ICQ-interview online edition of SecurityFocus said one of the representatives of the team, who wished to remain anonymous. According to him, three programmers from Russia work in DCT, as well as several freelancers from other countries who are just doing business: they buy fresh exploits at a price of up to $ 10,000, they update their program. In order to convince readers that Dream Coders Team is not involved in the criminal world, a mysterious interlocutor noted that the release of malicious programs is prohibited by Russian law.
