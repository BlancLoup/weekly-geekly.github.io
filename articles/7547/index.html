Today is 21 years from the day of the Chernobyl accident

I hope the habra people have not forgotten that today is 21 years from the date of the accident at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant. The task of all mankind - to make sure that the disaster does not happen again. This is not only a terrible tragedy, but also an eternal reminder of how fragile this world is. How dangerous can be the influence of man on the environment. The consequence of this accident is not only radioactive contamination, but also a drastic change in attitude towards this method of producing energy.
Unfortunately, for the time being there is no other equally effective way of obtaining energy, so that nuclear power plants will be operated for a long time. In the future, mankind may move on to the extraction of energy through thermonuclear fusion, as hydrocarbons will be exhausted, and alternative ways will not be able to provide energy needs.

Eternal memory to the liquidators of the Chernobyl accident.
