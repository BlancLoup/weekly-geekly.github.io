International Conference of online game developers

International conference of online game developers in Ukraine. This is the first national conference dedicated to browser games and is being held internationally.

The conference will discuss current problems of the gaming industry and identify ways of its successful development. The organizers expect that the event will become the center of business communication between domestic and foreign publishers and developers of online games, which will allow them to exchange experience and join forces for effective business interaction.

The main objectives of this forum are to assess the development trends of the online games market in Ukraine and abroad, present the experience of successful Ukrainian and foreign projects, demonstrate advanced software, services and platforms for developers.

The conference will be attended by developers of online games from Ukraine, Russia, Poland, Germany and other countries, game publishers, investors, companies working in related fields (hosting, payment systems, advertising, outsourcing).

The event is organized by the International Forum " Internet-Ukraine '2009 " and the Company Art4Art Entertainment .

Partners of the event are the Game Developers Conference (KRI), the Ukrainian community of programmers DEVELOPERS and the Ukrainian Adobe Flash Platform User Group (UAFPUG).

Similar events were held in Moscow and were a great success. Therefore, to expect that the conference in Ukraine will pass unnoticed correctly.
