UNIBOTY and their combat "Fang"

Fighting robots "Bronebot-2016" in the Olympic after two days ! The first fights will begin this Sunday.

12 teams announced competitions from all over the country. We have already visited half of them - we have seen both real nerds and engineers of the Moscow Aviation Institute and harsh men from Big Brother , but UNIBOT makes us really surprised.



This team of participants is definitely different from all the others in that it is not just schoolchildren. Three of them are girls! The team applied for participation in the battles and they could not refuse - all the application requirements were met. All this was possible thanks to the school of robotics, where they are engaged.



In those days, when the sky was blue, and the orcs - greener, 4 young Padawans decided that they would create a great machine, which was destined to conquer the world. They wandered around the world trying to find teachers who could pass on their innermost knowledge to them. In 2015, they found them in the company " Unium ". So began this great story.


behind all - Elshan Bagirov
next row - Alexey Slonov, Mark Bochkov
And the team: Vladislav, Alexey, Adeline, Leila

The Unibot team was organized by Unium as a training project for schoolchildren attending robotics courses at the company's educational centers. The team consists of students of grades 7-11, and their teachers from the students of the best technical universities of the country help and guide them. We believe that the best way to show children what robotics is is to give them the opportunity to build a robot and put it against another robot. These guys just seem like cute schoolchildren - they are smart, cunning and extremely ambitious.



50 kilograms of young robotics ambitions expressed in austere and angular forms. Do not be confused by his seeming harmlessness - his canine is able to tear apart the most durable materials. According to unconfirmed information, the genuine NOKIA 3310 was destroyed during the tests, but eyewitnesses to this event do not give any comments on this matter. This machine is the result of a symbiosis of the imagination of young and ambitious students and the knowledge of not such young, but experienced teachers. Separately, we would like to mention the help of our general partner - the store RCMOTORS.RU. This robot is created to show children that robotics can be spectacular!



Name: Fang
Year of creation: 2016
Weight: 50 kg
Speed: 1 m / s
Armor: 3 mm composite steel armor with a reinforced special compound layer
Power: 1200 W
Weapon: Squeezing Fang
Strengths: Ability to overcome, body strength, fang strength
Weaknesses: The complete absence of self-preservation instinct, the absence of reverse



The children themselves solder the microcircuits and program the controllers - the teachers only help order the case welding on an outsourcing. The main thing is that they themselves are confident in their victory. Let's wish them victory in their weight category, the car they got beautiful. But even more surprising was the team.
