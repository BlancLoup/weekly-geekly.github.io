What is Microsoft Office 365 and how can it help the company?

Many Office users find it hard to understand what Microsoft Office 365 really is. He is often associated with the "classic" Office suite. Therefore, users believe that they just get Word, Excel, PowerPoint and other applications "from the cloud." But it is precisely this aspect that has new additional advantages. Anton Naydel, a specialist company COMPAREX technology Microsoft, will tell you what they are.

1.1. What is the difference?


Although Office 365 provides the actual Office suite, it is only part of the proposed components. The real advantage is often underestimated or even omitted. My personal opinion is that Office 365 creates an integrated and strategic direction within the enterprise. In addition to features such as Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, or Skype for Business Online, Office 365 provides security features, data analysis, project work, online communication, social networks, and more.

1.2. What are the benefits of subscribing to a cloud service such as Office 365?


There are a number of reasons. Here are some examples:

  • The company wants to focus on core business, which makes a profit. For most enterprises, this will require the creation and maintenance of a very complex IT infrastructure;
  • Ensuring the availability and uninterrupted operation of the IT infrastructure will be too expensive for the company;
  • The company does not have the human resources necessary to operate a complex IT infrastructure;
  • The company wants to reduce costs, for example, for software licenses , the amount of memory for countless mailboxes and corresponding archives;
  • The company decided to always use the latest software versions, but avoids any action to always check for the latest updates in its IT infrastructure;
  • The company plans to work with third parties without incurring additional costs for licenses or security.

This is just a small selection of standard situations. A much larger number of scenarios is possible if we consider the advanced features offered by Office 365. You can only answer the question of whether Office 365 meets the needs of a company by carefully examining the available functionality, possible restrictions, necessary efforts, integration complexity and a preliminary estimate of costs.

1.3. Why do companies use Office 365?


We come back to the question of why we need to implement Office 365. Within the framework of this publication, it is impossible to give any definite answer to this question. On this topic you need to write a whole book! Therefore, we consider one example.

Let's look at a medium-sized enterprise in the automotive industry. Of course, the company must have a reliable IT infrastructure. Therefore, to create a suitable IT environment, resources, technology, and budget are allocated. In addition, in order to be reliable, up-to-date and always accessible, it must also be easy to maintain. As our examples, we will look at maintenance and relevance. Indicators in these two areas may be as follows:

Service:

  • to provide proactive protection against downtime;
  • to ensure effective work;
  • to ensure their safety.

How about relevance?


It is not only update management or security patches. This is a question of creating a modern workplace.

This is only one side. The other side sets an even more difficult task for the company. How can I introduce the concept of using employees' own devices (BOYD) without accepting it forever? How soon can I deploy a new Office, and will it compare with my server? What resources and budget do I need to manage such projects?

There are many arguments in favor of new software. Consider a couple of examples.
