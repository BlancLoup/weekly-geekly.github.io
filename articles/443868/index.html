One of the exercises with physical exercises
Because of the sedentary work, some muscles of our body are shortened, others lengthen. And the muscles are in this position for a very long time. This subsequently affects everything: posture, gait, muscle tone, even the work of the internal organs and, consequently, on overall health. Therefore, it is recommended to periodically stretch muscle groups. For example, for the neck:

Starting position - standing, arms along the body, legs on the width of the pelvis, pelvis in a neutral position (there is no excessive sagging in the lower back - for this we direct the tailbone to the floor). Muscles of the press are tinted, we look straight ahead.

  1. We make a smooth tilt of the head to the left, while trying to lower the right shoulder down. The left shoulder is in a neutral position and does not rise up.
  2. As your head gently drops to the left, feel the tension of the neck muscles, trapezius muscles. Make sure that you do not feel any discomfort in the cervical spine.
  3. In the lower position of the head, when the muscles are maximally stretched, you can gently , with a very small amplitude, gently move your head slightly forward and backward, thereby changing the tension vector and stretching the nearby neck muscles on the right side. Hold the position of the neck as you feel comfortable - 10-30 seconds.
  4. We return the head to its original position, then do the same in the other direction.
