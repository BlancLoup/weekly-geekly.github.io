It is clear that once in this area there is money, but there are people who earn money on domains. And one of these people is Gary Millin and his company, World Accelerator. Gary and Co. have been working for 21 years in the domain sphere, during which time they managed to earn millions of dollars and accumulate domains for tens of millions.

The biggest deals for Millin are selling or exchanging sound domains like world.com, usa.com, doctor.com, lawyer.com, comic.com, email.com, cyberservices.com. Now it has domains that any domain manager wants. How did Gary begin his journey?

It turns out that he began working with the Internet in the mid-90s , in partnership with investment specialist Gerald Gromen. Partners founded the company mail.com (it still works, this is a mailer), while still studying at Harvard Business School. The idea behind mail.com is to provide a person with a lifetime e-mail address that the owner could work with for a very long time. The idea really worked, and at one time mail.com was a very popular service - and this company is still making a profit.

When the dotcom bubble burst, Gary had a pretty hard time, but then he took up another business ‚Äî buying and selling domain names. He entered this business with his former partner.



After names like Kosher.com, London.com, and England.com were sold for a couple of million dollars, Millin realized that you can earn more on domains, and not only with the help of buying and selling domains. In 2007, he founded the company World Accelerator, which became involved in domains. Only here the idea was no longer just for selling domains.

Instead, World Accelerator gives a person the desired name, in exchange for a share in a startup that will work on a domain. The rights to the domain still belong to Millin. Now Millin practically does not sell domains, but is engaged in work according to the above scheme.



In principle, each of the doctor.com domain names can be sold for millions of dollars. But Gary believes that making money by leasing or leasing domains can be much higher than with direct sales. Like it or not, it is not clear now, but on some domains there are already working services.
