Accreditation of freelancers


Remote collaboration is becoming more and more popular: recently, an increasing number of different specialists have been coming to freelance, respectively, and the level of competition between freelancers has also increased. On the remote exchanges, the number of accounts is growing, among which it is becoming increasingly difficult to navigate, especially to an inexperienced employer. But the ultimate goal of any customer is to attract an experienced and qualified specialist to the project. Experts, in turn, try to allocate their page in various ways and attract the attention of employers. Of course, today many freelance exchanges offer their users to highlight a questionnaire using special accounts and rating systems, but often such assessment methods are not objective, since there is a possibility of receiving them for money. As a result, such situations arise when the customer receives poor-quality work even from a highly rated specialist.

PROhq skills accreditation system
The remote work exchange PROhq offers its users a system of accreditation of skills that will allow an objective assessment of the knowledge and experience of a freelancer in their field. An accredited freelancer receives a special badge in his public profile that allows an employer to learn about the level of a specialist. Accreditation at the PROhq exchange can be carried out in two ways: using documents and using special tests.
  1. Paid accreditation of freelancers on the basis of documents. This type of accreditation is carried out on the basis of documents submitted by the freelancer. These may be diplomas, certificates, certificates, certificates of completion, etc. To pass this type of accreditation, the freelancer sends the exchange copies of documents in electronic form (scanned), and service specialists make requests to educational institutions to confirm the information. Features of accredited freelancers
The PROhq accreditation system expands the possibilities of cooperation between freelancers and employers: a remote employee gets the opportunity to add information about himself, drawing the attention of a potential customer to his professional level, and the customer gets the opportunity to choose a specialist among remote employees whose qualification is confirmed officially and is suitable for performing one or another project. Thus, PROhq accreditation significantly improves the quality level of remote collaboration. Benefits of accredited freelancers
