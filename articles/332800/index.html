In short: work, motivation and value system


We pronounce and hear from colleagues and friends: Friday, hell, hard labor, torture, work for paying thanks, etc. We are all clever - this is a subjective, and sometimes a superficial relationship. In addition to the needs and desires, there are many factors that determine a person's behavior when choosing a job, and subsequently at work.
