Skype will start video calls on iOS devices

Today it became known that skype is preparing to acquire the possibility of video calls for mobile devices on the iOS platform (in particular, iPhone 4), equipped with a front-facing camera.

About the possible start of the service it became known from the help-document. The client version itself will most likely be presented at the upcoming CES 2011, which will take place on January 6th. As part of the presentation, the company also plans to come up with several new services.



It is not yet known whether this service is being prepared for launch for Android, Symbian, Wndows Phone and other platforms, but this could be a great gift for many users. Previously, only Nokia N900 had such an opportunity, of course, among mobile devices.
