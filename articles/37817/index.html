Opera-9.60 - first public build

This is a surprise: instead of the expected weekly test assembly Opera-9.52 expected on Fridays, the developers pleased with the release of the public test assembly Opera-9.60. Well, we will try and evaluate innovations, among which are:

Opera Link
Now the list of search engines and the history of entered addresses have been added to the system for synchronization of bookmarks, Quick Start, personal panel and notes.

Preview feeds
Before subscribing to receive RSS / Atom news feeds, you can familiarize yourself with their content.

Opera Mail: Traffic Minimization Mode (Low Bandwidth Mode)
Traffic minimization mode allows Opera (M2) email client to minimize the amount of downloaded data. In the case of IMAP, this means that M2 will only synchronize new messages and will not download the attached files until the corresponding command comes from the user. For standard POP mail, the email client will download no more than a hundred lines of message ‚Äî the rest of the content will be loaded only on an individual request.

Opera Mail: Track / Ignore emails and contacts (Follow / Ignore threads and contacts)
This is a new option designed to make life easier for users who receive many messages. It allows for an easier way to get rid of unimportant messages, and for important topics, on the contrary, it facilitates their processing.

Opera Mail: Go to Chat (Go To Thread)
Now you can use the ‚ÄúGo to correspondence‚Äù option, thanks to which only messages related to the specified correspondence will be displayed in the mail window. Particularly appreciate this option, those who are accustomed to view messages in the usual way according to the date of receipt, and not in the form of correspondence.

As you can see, most of the innovations relate to the mail client, which indicates a significant change in M2. You can also note the increased speed of loading pages (it would seem - much faster!), The remaining moments take time to test. What, in fact, do.
