Sharp 3D phone review compared to HTC Evo 3D and LG Optimus 3D

I offer you a video review of this miracle device in comparison with HTC Evo 3D and LG Optimus 3D. Prospects for such devices and personal considerations under the cut.

Is there a future for 3D phones in general? Of course, humanity is currently on the verge of another technical revolution: a 3D structure is gradually being introduced into everything and everyone. First there was Avatar and movie theaters, later TVs and computers, and now phones.

At the moment, only 3 devices are massively sold around the world, which, by the way, appear in our review: HTC Evo 3D, LG Optimus 3D and Sharp Aquos Phone SH-12C. The first two appeared on the market in the summer of this year; Sharp - even earlier, but only in Japan.
