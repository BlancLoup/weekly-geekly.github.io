Russian software exporters benefit from a weaker ruble

Exports of Russian software in 2014 grew by 10% to $ 5.7 billion. Software exporting companies have benefited from the strengthening of the dollar: their expenses have remained in rubles, and their income is pegged to the dollar, Vedomosti writes . During 2014, the dollar strengthened by 73% against the ruble - it rose from 32.6 to 56.6 rubles.
