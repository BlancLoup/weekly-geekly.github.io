Rumors: the new Mac Pro and Mac Mini are already in August


One of the editors of CNet, Brian Tong (Brian Tong), said on his Twitter account that Apple is preparing to introduce the new Mac Pro and Mac Mini in August of this year. Tong also said that the new models will have Thunderbolt and Sandy Bridge processors, forcing many potential buyers to slow down with the purchase.

Do not forget that the stocks of Apple Pro are already running out of Mac Pro and Mac Mini, which gives grounds to make such guesses. If Macbook Air and AirPort / Time Capsule are updated (as many expect), then Apple will probably hold a special meeting to showcase new products in August.
