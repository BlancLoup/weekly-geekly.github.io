AWS: Marketplace became available to IAM users

Hello community!

From today, AWS Marketplace supports IAM users. IAM users also have rights that allow you to buy software in the Marketplace:


Let me remind you that Marketplace is an online store where you can easily and quickly purchase software that can be run on AWS. Big names like CA , Canonical , Couchbase , IBM , Microsoft , SUSE , Red Hat , Riverbed , SAP , and Zend are on a par with open source solutions ( Word , Wordpress , Drupal and MediaWiki) .

I personally do not find this option very cool and useful, because I rarely use the Marketplace. But for those who still use it, this is another closed hole, so as not to give root email and password to purchase the software that is needed.

In general, AWS, as always, does not stand still, and when the development of new features does not bring results yet, they tighten up the security keys, giving users more convenient tools for managing the cloud.
