Big Collection of Ubuntu DVD Games - Ubuntu GamePack

Available for download The largest collection of games for Ubuntu - Ubuntu GamePack.

More than two hundred fascinating games were collected on 4 DVDs: strategies, shooters, simulators, fantasy, puzzles, educational games for children and many other genres.

You will play classic chess, solve riddles, perform aerobatics at the helm of an airplane over a metropolis, feel like a racer, fight space battles in the vast expanses of space, build a city and shoot at enemies from a machine gun in a modern shooter.
The disks have colorful educational games for little heroes from 3 years old.

Most games can be downloaded from the official Ubuntu repository or from the additional www.playdeb.net .
But the advantage of this collection is that having it on hand and not having a FAST Internet (some game installations take up more than 100M, and you can hardly download such a volume quickly via a modem), you can easily install the game on a computer that does not have a connection to the Internet.
All games on the disc are ordered by genre.

The disks contain only games and do not contain an operating system, therefore, require a pre-installed system Ubuntu Desktop 10.04 or newer.
All games presented on disks have installation packages for 32-bit and for 64-bit systems.
