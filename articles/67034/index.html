Google gives Russia "Googles"

Google has announced the launch of the "Russia in Google search" project. Weekly, Google will present cartoons of the most popular searches.

Note:
In the company itself, these caricatures are labeled "googles".





These funny pictures are images of requests that gained popularity during the week. Everything is very simple: a noticeable, or not very noticeable, but interesting and unexpected event occurs, users begin to search for information about it in a Google search engine. Then the company employees look at the most popular 10 requests per week, choose one of them and draw cartoons.

Note:
The author of the drawings is Sergey Yolkin, a cartoonist.

Google also offers to follow the trends of the week by yourself .
