New details GAMER LIVE 2010

Today, the organizers share the secrets and details of the event, and if some of you still have doubts, then this news will certainly dispel them.

The festival will be held under the motto of a healthy lifestyle, which means that guests will enjoy a rich sports program: football, basketball, volleyball, swimming in the pool, horseback riding and morning fitness classes from the organizers.
