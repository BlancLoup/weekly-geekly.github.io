Developers who want to increase their value in the labor market

The project University Innopolis announces the extension of the grant competition for training young software developers in Software Engineering for the 2014-2015 academic year in connection with the launch of the program of the President of the Russian Federation - Global Education.

To participate in the competition, you must submit an application and submit all the required documents until the end of February 2014!


Purpose of the competition


The purpose of the competition is to select talented students and specialists in software development to fill research and teaching positions at Innopolis University, as well as to implement joint projects with partner companies (Parallels, Acronis, Mail.Ru Group, etc.).

In order to improve their professional skills and study international experience, future employees will be trained in the Master of Information Technology - Software Development Management / Software Engineering, MSIT-SE program with partner Carnegie University. Mellon, USA Two semesters of students will be trained in Pittsburgh, and the last semester - in Kazan.
