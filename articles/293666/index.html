Google will pay for the domain for Alphabet just $ 8 per year



The recently created Alphabet holding, which also includes Google, has registered the domain abc.xyz. The administrator of the .xyz domain zone is Daniel Nagari, who paid $ 186,000 last year for the administration right. Domain registration and renewal in this zone costs the registrant only $ 8, Fusion writes . Thus, the abc.xyz extension will cost the holding a similar amount. True, the registration of the premium domain "abc", most likely, cost the Alphabet holding more than $ 8. Unfortunately, neither the zone administrator nor the domain owner discloses the amount of the transaction.
