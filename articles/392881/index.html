The introduction and spread of online betting coupled with Russia's current fiscal problems makes the development of online poker regulatory systems extremely favorable. It is expected that after the legalization of online poker players will also support the introduction of the TsUPIS system.

In July 2015, Vladimir Putin initiated a study on the possibilities of taxing online poker. The collected funds should go to sponsor other sports, in particular - the Russian Federation Chess. According to Ilyumzhinov, "there is no reason for poker to be considered an illegal game." The status of online poker is still under big question, but is already being actively discussed. It is possible that online poker will soon be recognized as a legal intellectual sport.
