We get exchange rates before anyone else

Every day, around 12 o'clock in the afternoon, the Central Bank of the Russian Federation announces exchange rates the next day. Sometimes it happens a little earlier, sometimes much later.

For those who work with currency and for whom it is important to receive information about the next day before all others, there is a service on sms.ru that monitors the Central Bank website for the emergence of a new course and sends it to subscribers, at the time of its appearance, as SMS messages.

Your choice of currency rates are available in 36 countries:



Just mark those that you are interested in , and they will come to you every day with one compact SMS.
