Now a few words about what happened.




Tutumba is a service of user reviews about regional companies (shops, restaurants, beauty salons, etc.). The inspiration behind the project was the insanely popular in the West, Yelp.com , which we, of course, thought about and creatively reworked.

What Tutumba can be useful to users? Here you can find and choose the best fitness club, cafe or auto repair in the desired area. Therefore, an important component of the project is a search with convenient filtering by a variety of parameters. Well, after the user has selected the right company and used its services, you can return to Tutumba and make the most important thing - leave your review. Since most of all on such projects is annoying registration, and no one believes anonymous reviews, we decided to make a quick authorization through all popular services.



Those who write often, interesting and useful receive virtual rewards. And in January, we plan to hold an action for review writers with quite real rewards.



The monetization of the project is related to the provision of premium services to regional business owners. Tutumba can be another platform for them to communicate with consumers. Access to the account and basic functionality is provided free of charge, and we are now working hard on additional functionality.
