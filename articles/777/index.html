Distribution of visitors by Google resources

Bill Tenser, head of global research for HitWise, published in his blog statistics on the distribution of traffic among the 33 resources owned by Google. In the first place is the most standard search on the Internet , which accounts for almost 80% of all visits. Next comes the search for pictures , then Gmail , Google Video and Google News . Only these resources were able to overcome the barrier of 1%.
