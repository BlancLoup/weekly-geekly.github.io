Copiny
Dmitry Chistov from Bryansk came up with the Internet service Copiny, which allows customer support to work three times more efficiently. These are not empty words, but an assessment of Evernote, which uses Copiny. The opinion of the managers of Evernote is expensive: in Russia, the Evernote program has 180 thousand subscribers, and in the world it is 8.5 million, and you need to keep in touch with everyone. Copiny is created just for effective interaction with customers. +++

Your second me
Programmers from Peter Stepan Mamontov and Andrei Alikov came up with the Internet radio "Your Second Self". This application is for the Vkontakte network, which will compete with Radio Kassandra. It was installed by 300 thousand users. But St. Petersburg programmers are hoping that 2 million people will install their radio.

The radio competitor works according to this principle: the user marks the songs like or dislike in his music library, and the player selects songs similar in style throughout the Vkontakte network and compiles them on air. Radio "Your Second Self" will guess not only those songs that you like, but also those that correspond to your mood, state of mind or occupation at a given time. +++
