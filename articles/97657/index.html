Laptop and nature



I want to share practical observations of working on a laptop in nature. I will not discover anything new for those who have already traveled to nature with a laptop. They can skip this note. The post is dedicated to those who are just planning to go on nature, and represents how they will sit at the laptop in their free time and do something useful.


Figure 1 - Laptop on the beach and joy

The Internet and the magazine are full of photos of happy people who sit with laptops on the benches in the parks and do something on them. And there is advertising in the spirit of the article " Comfortable work in nature with a laptop for rent ." And everything is so beautiful and tempting.


Figure 2 - Happiness from nature and a laptop

Of course, I understand that these are staged photos. However, I so want to believe advertising that the laptop will allow you to immerse yourself in the world of information, wherever you are. Now I, in general, except with a smile, cannot relate to these photos.

Having gathered for a week in a sanatorium to be in silence and sunbathe, I think for a long time whether to take a laptop or not. I decided to take it all the same. I have long been tormented by the desire to work at times somewhere in the resort area, which would dilute the programming and writing of articles, rest, picking mushrooms and fresh air. And although this time I did not plan to create something ambitious in the process of rest, I took the laptop to check its applicability in the field in advance. Namely, I planned to try a little refactoring of PVS-Studio and continue writing one of the articles. Plus, I also wanted to check e-mail and answer when necessary when the Internet was available. In general, quite modest desires. And then I will share my observations on myself.

1. On the laptop screen on the street almost nothing is visible

2. It is scary to leave the laptop in the room / house for a long time, if there is no safe


I personally do not worry about this issue and leave it. But for many it can be a very unpleasant moment, to entrust a laptop with a flimsy door with a flimsy lock, or an alternative to this - to carry extra weight all the time. After all, not all hotels have safes.

3. It turns out you quickly get used to the good Internet


And asceticism in the form of GPRS (Beeline USB modem) turned out to be very inconvenient. No 3G course, and there was no mention. In addition, please note that in the field, the Internet can work very unstable, as it was in my case. And the reception / sending of a letter in 2-3 attempts is annoying and clearly does not contribute to a holiday mood. And in order to be able to read Habr, so incredible fortitude and perseverance are generally needed.

5. Overheating


My laptop has a fan at the bottom and if you put it on a towel or bed, then after an hour of relatively active use (watching a movie, a game), it will turn off abnormally.

Total


I do not discourage taking a laptop on vacation. For example, I sometimes enjoyed listening to audio books and successfully communicated with a colleague on some issues, and even programmed a little. However, I have listed the disadvantages, and you decide. Who knows, that is armed. I hope this helps someone to decide.
