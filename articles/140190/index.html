Monthly boycott by pirates of media corporations

While copywriters are lobbying for tougher laws, their opponents are inventing new ways of fighting: from March 1, citizens around the world began a boycott of media corporations in the framework of the Black March action. Apparently, the main coordinator of this action in Russia is the Communist Party of Russia (Pirate Party of Russia). In addition, the Progress Revolutionary Directorate declares March as the month of the legitimate DDOS of state bodies.

The Pirate Party of Russia calls on its supporters to support this action and for a month to refrain from buying games, films, music, books, magazines, as well as going to the movies.
