Asgardia: the first nation to live in space



Not everyone lives in peace in one place. The desire to go somewhere, no matter where - a completely normal desire for a person. Traction to the unknown, the desire to visit other lands accompanied mankind throughout history. That is why many people want to fly to distant lands.

Someone moves to another street, someone dares to go to another city. Even emigrate to another country. Travelers, scientists and trackers explored the Earth far and wide, climbed into the deepest depths and rose high into the atmosphere. 546 people from 37 countries visited Earth orbit. Where should we go next? What to research?

Everyone is talking about a colony on Mars. Of course, it will inevitably appear in a few decades. But the mass resettlement of colonists to the Red Planet will happen, nevertheless, in a more distant future. Probably, this possibility will appear in our children and grandchildren (or in the children of our friends, if we do not leave offspring).

Where to go adventurers now? Is there a chance for a simple guy from the backwoods to fly away from the planet Earth? Now it is.

Welcome to the space country Asgardia ! This is the first nation in the world, which is going to establish an independent state in space - in Earth orbit.

As is known from myths, after the fall of Asgard, Tony Stark proposed to rebuild the city with the help of a new repulsor-technology based on anti-gravity. The reborn and modernized city was called Asgardia . A real symbiosis of technology and magic existed under the wise rule of the goddess Mother of everything , in the presence of the Father of all, Odin , in self-imposed exile.


Mythological Asgardia

Asgardia hovered above the Earth in the same place where Asgard was located when he was brought to our planet, but later Mother of all decided to leave Earth in order to better control the inhabitants of Asgardia. Then the city flew into lunar orbit, and then completely moved to the orbit of Saturn.

Hopefully, the fate of the mythical city will not befall the real Asgardy - a small colony of earthlings, which is planned to be based on a satellite in near-Earth space.

The idea belongs to the well-known scientist, scientific director of KB-1, Chairman of the Presidium of the Independent Expert Council on the problems of aerospace defense of the Russian Federation, a member of the Academy of Military Sciences of the Russian Federation, Doctor of Technical Sciences Igor Raufovich Ashurbeyli . Now Igor Raufovich has a new, no less interesting task.

The first official press conference of Asgardia was held yesterday in Paris. The launch of the inhabited satellite Asgardia was supported by scientists and experts from the USA, Canada and Romania. The project is supported by David Alexander , Director of the Rice Space Institute , Dean of the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Rice University; Professor Ram Yakhuh , Director of the Center for the Study of Air and Space Law at McGill University (Canada); Joseph Pelton , Director of the Space and Advanced Communications Research Institute (SACRI) at George Washington University (USA) and the Romanian cosmonaut Dumitru-Dorin Prunariu .

Registration in citizens of Asgardia has already begun on the official website. By this time, 69,576 people have applied for registration - and this is just a day after the press conference.
