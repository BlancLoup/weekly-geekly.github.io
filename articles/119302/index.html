From June 2 to 5, 2011, with the support of the Russian Venture Company, we will organize the Founders BootCamp in the MSU Science Park

From June 2 to June 5, 2011, the American company Global Innovation Labs www.innovationlabs.net with the support of the Russian Venture Company will organize Founders BootCamp in the MSU Science Park.

The main task of Founders BootCamp www.innovationlabs.net/founders-bootcamp is to bring companies to the Russian and international venture capital markets. American investors and business angels who are members of the American associations of business angels Hacker Angels, Mass Medical Angels, and the Seraph Group are invited to Founders BootCamp. These are the very people who really know the market and for many years successfully invest in projects with a high degree of risk.

The entire Founders BootCamp program aims to prepare companies for investment. For four days, under the guidance of mentors and with the active participation of American business angels, practical training will be held on the preparation and optimization of presentations and business models. At the same time, special attention will be paid to the protection of intellectual property, the conditions for obtaining investments, and the demystification of financing conditions. Experts, investors and businessmen who have their own experience in transforming ideas into a successful business have kindly agreed to act as teachers and mentors.

All finalists will be given the opportunity to make a detailed presentation to American and Russian investors at the same time, as well as answer their questions.

For more details on the conditions for competitive selection of participants and the Founders BootCamp program, you can find out on the Global Innovation Labs website www.innovationlabs.net/founders-bootcamp

Applications are accepted until May 25, 2011.
