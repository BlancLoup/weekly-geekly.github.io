This time we have on the wire Yuri Gorbachev , the chief architect of Intel OpenVINO Toolkit - a set of libraries, optimization tools and information resources for developing software using machine vision and Deep Learning.
Yes, it is about Deep Learning and the OpenVINO Toolkit that we will have a conversation. And to make it interesting and informative, send your questions - all that you want to know. First, a few autobiographical words about our expert:

After the acquisition of Itseez by Intel, he was engaged in the optimization of computer vision algorithms and deep learning. Currently he leads the architectural direction in the OpenVINO product and, in particular, the development of the Intel Deep Learning Inference Engine.
