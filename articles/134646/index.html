ROCCAT Isku - smart keyboard for gamers



The company ROCCAT is known for its solutions for gamers. This time, the engineers did not disappoint, and a new keyboard for hardcore gamers was born. The keyboard is called ROCCAT Isku, and is completely "sharpened" for the game. Of course, it is possible to work with office applications with it, but not for this purpose it was created.

According to the developers, a technology was applied here that allows assigning an additional function to each of the keys. In addition, there is a separate button that controls the recording of macros. The macro contains a maximum of 500 individual actions. Settings can be saved by creating an individual profile for this.

There can be five such profiles, no more. But this is clearly enough for most gamers. There are lights, as without it. Thanks to this feature, you can play without light at all. The backlight has only six levels of luminosity. There are also mouse and headphone jacks on the keyboard. Well, how this device works can be seen on the video.
