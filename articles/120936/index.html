Acer Aspire Ethos 8951G and removable touchpad

Acer Aspire Ethos 8951G and 5951G are aimed at multimedia and entertainment, the touchpads of these laptops are removable and can be used as a remote control. In addition, the distinctive features of these laptops (with 18.4 and 15.6 screens, respectively) are: high-definition displays, Blu-ray recorder, 5.1-channel surround sound and keyboard backlight.






In the press release of these machines, the removable touchpad is mentioned in between times, although this is actually one of the key features. Acer simply adds that the second function of the touchpad is to turn it into a remote control. The picture shows that the touchpad in this form can be used to control the playback of music and video. Also, this function will be convenient if you make a report and you need to switch slides.



The Ethos series uses second-generation Intel Core processors and NVIDIA GeForce GT500 discrete graphics. Model 8951G boasts a resolution of 1920 * 1080 pixels, and 5951G - typical 1366 * 768. Both displays are made using Corning Gorilla Glass protection technology to protect the glass from scratches.

Blu-ray or DVD burners are optionally available, use of HDD in combination with SSD, card reader also understands SDXC format.



Acer focuses on InstantOn's fast-launch technology and on its clear.fi service.

The approximate cost is $ 1,634 USD. If you are satisfied, then wait until the middle of June - it is then that laptops will go on sale.
