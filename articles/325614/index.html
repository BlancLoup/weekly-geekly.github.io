MBLT17 preliminary program

Less than a month is left before the start of the International Mobile Conference MBLT17 . A preliminary program has been published on the site, in which representatives of Google, Facebook, Shazam, Tinkoff Bank, Mail.ru, Uber, Sports.ru, App Annie and others will take part.



The business program of the conference will be held in the format of reports, discussion platforms and open interviews - MBLT Talks.

The topics of the reports have already been approved, and together with the speakers we will discuss:

- the present and future of the mobile industry,
- the latest fintech trends,
- new VR and AI,
- the use of chat bots for various business areas,
- transformation of the approach to the development of UI due to the development of AI technologies,
- strategies for monetization of mobile applications,
- mobile marketing.

Fintech


Fintech will be one of the main topics of the conference. As experts, we invited Klemen Chang from Spiking, Anna Mikhina and Maxim Evdokimov from Tinkoff Bank, Lukasz Leonieuski from Facebook, Igor Kostylev from FutureBanking, Alexey Blagirev from Otkritie Bank, Arseniy Kosenko from Lifepay, etc. The topics of the reports will be devoted to: trends of Asian financial technology, UX for daily banking, the impact of mobile on the financial industry and the prospects for the development of financial mobile solutions.

How to overcome the stagnation in the development of banking applications in connection with the glut of functions? Is there a future for atomic applications? Is UX important in mobile banking? What is the role of AI in banking applications? To answer these questions, representatives of large Russian banks will gather at a round table to discuss all possible points of growth for mobile banking.



Mobile strategy


Josh Partridge from Shazam, Andy Zmolek from Google and Matt Jones from Manning Gottlieb OMD will present the topic of strategic development of mobile business. From them we learn how to attract and retain the user and develop a mobile-first strategy when launching your own product.

Mobile marketing


Reports on mobile marketing tools will be covered by representatives from Branch and Qlean. What is the difference between the approaches to display and mobile marketing and how to increase the conversion, Emre Aksoy and Roman Kumar Vias will be shown on real cases.
