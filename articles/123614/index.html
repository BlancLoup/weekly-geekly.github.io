July contest. City of the future

For a long time we have not held contests on the Trendclub pages. But here it is one more hot summer and we decided. By 2050, 6 billion people will live in the city.



The cities of the future have many problems: overpopulation, global warming, depletion of resources, environmental pollution. On the human side - new technologies, already existing and only being developed. How can the latest architectural, managerial and digital solutions help?
