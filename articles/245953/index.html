Typography basics

We are more and more immersed in questions of usability, interaction with users, trying to find the very optimal picture that will allow us to achieve the necessary actions from the client / user. One of the most important elements / channels of perception of information is the text. And typography is the art of typing. Below is a list of books that will help you learn the basics of typography.



Wiley - Adobe InDesign CC
The Classroom in a Book series (in English), in this world-famous line of textbooks, a book has been released, distinguished by the interests of both beginners and professionals. This tutorial will allow you to take the first steps in Adobe InDesign CC. He will also help a professional designer to master the tricks of a powerful package, which he previously did not pay attention to. The material is presented in the form of practical step by step instructions. From simple to complex they will guide you along the way of studying the program.

Jan Chihold - The Look of a Book
The articles of Jan Chihold of different years, which, in fact, made this book, belong to a different era. The author lived and worked at another time, when there was no such rapid development of electronic publishing. But his advice on this does not lose relevance. Jan Chihold - Font Samples
Those who underestimate or do not understand the meaning of fonts and the complexity of their creation are very wrong. Jan Chihold in his work "Font Samples" will help to understand this issue. This tutorial is designed for those who want to quickly and fully master the work with such a multi-faceted design tool like Adobe InDesign. It uses an effective teaching methodology, which is used in the training of designers in various educational institutions.
