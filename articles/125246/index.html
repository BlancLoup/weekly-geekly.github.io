IT Jam 2011 Meet & Mix - very soon in Odessa!

The most driving and the biggest all-Ukrainian event for IT-specialists of IT Jam Meet & Mix this year will be held on August 20, 2011 at the Odessa Maritime Station .



IT Jam Meet & Mix - an annual large-scale event for professionals working in the field of IT technologies has been held for 4 years in a row and gathers more than 1,500 developers and dozens of speakers. This year, 49 (!) Speakers have already registered, who will share their experience for free and ‚Äúpump up‚Äù knowledge on a wide variety of topics: from technology to IT management.

This year, the IT Jam Meet & Mix program has not changed much compared to previous events - it is also full and interesting:
  • Reports in 5 parallel sessions (PHP, .NET, Java, Quality Assurance and Mobile development) and 1 mixed stream (IT trends, team management, success stories, etc.)
  • At the end of the reports, a panel discussion will be held on the main stage with the directors of IT companies and IT Jam Pecha-Kucha, organized by Timofey Yevgrashin
  • Also at IT Jam 2011 will be organized "Territory Gadget" , where representatives of Samsung, PocketBook and other companies will present a new gadget: computers, smartphones, tablets
  • and the day will end under the explosive chords of the groups in which IT shniki play at the IT Jam Music Session

We are pleased that this year even more advanced IT companies have become partners of IT Jam Meet & Mix : EnvisioNext, Exigen Services, Sigma Ukraine, ZoralLabs, Magento, Lohika, GlobalLogic, NetCracker, Cogniance!

Informal communication, a lot of new knowledge and impressions, meetings with old acquaintances, good mood - guaranteed! Come, it will be driving, interesting and fun!

The event is free. Register here: www.it-jam.com.ua/register

About IT Jam Meet & Mix
IT Jam Meet & Mix is a non-profit project designed to promote the development of expertise in software development, to highlight the latest trends in information technology. The event is held annually in an informal setting, where IT specialists (about 1,500 developers in 2010) meet to exchange experience, knowledge, and simply to communicate with colleagues and gurus of the IT world.

For more information about speakers, venue, registration and scale of IT-Jam Meet & Mix, as well as more detailed information on past events, please visit www.it-jam.com.ua

We look forward to seeing you on August 20 in sunny Odessa - right by the sea - at the seaport ! :)
