JXD S7100 tablet release on Android 2.2 platform



A few days ago on the official website of the brand JXD (JinXing Digital) a new gadget appeared - the JXD S7100 tablet on the Android 2.2 platform.
This news has clearly aroused the World Wide Web ...



The uniqueness of this device is that it is both a gaming console and a tablet PC with a 7-inch touchscreen that supports 800 x 480px resolution. And unlike the usual tablet PCs, on the front surface of the device there are buttons for hardware control, which organically fit into the overall design.



Thus, besides applications from the Android market, the portable tablet JXD S7100 allows you to run retro games from Nintendo, Sony (Play Station 1), Neo-Geo, etc. Thanks to the embedded 1 GHz ARM Cortex A9 processor and the Mali 400 graphics accelerator good performance for both games and work.



The undoubted advantage are the other characteristics of the JXD S7100:
  • HDMI port for playing games and videos on the big screen
  • Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g / n and USB 2.0 support.
  • built-in memory from 2 to 16 Gb
  • reasonable price (retail price in China from 899 yuan without delivery, which is about $ 142)


Dimensions JXD S7100 - 238.5 x 121.7 x 12.35 mm, the duration of work when viewing web-pages up to 4 hours, and when playing music - up to 10 hours.

Waiting for PS Vita and Wii U to be released, in my opinion, the JXD S 7100 deserves attention - why is it not a gift for gamers for the new year? :)
