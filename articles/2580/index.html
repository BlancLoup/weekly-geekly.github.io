Google plugin gives passwords

The anti-phishing extension for the Firefox browser , developed by Google, turned out to be "full of holes" - with its help, you can easily find out your email addresses and passwords. The illustration (rather large JPEG file ) is published on the Finjan website.

The company Finjan, which discovered the vulnerability, signaled this to Google (before giving information to the public), and now an updated version of the plug-in is available for download, writes Ars Technica .
