M-commerce grows 300% faster than E-commerce

Mobile devices set a clear trend in the modern world. More and more Internet users make purchases through smartphones and tablets, using them both to select a product or service, and directly for payment. At the same time, the growth of the global mobile commerce market is far ahead of the growth of the e-commerce market - 42% against 13% according to forecasts at the end of 2016.

By 2018, mobile user spending will grow to $ 600 billion - an increase of 200% compared to 2014, which resulted in mobile spending of $ 200 billion. The most attractive countries for mCommerce are China, the UAE and Turkey - these are the countries with the largest number of mobile buyers. The leaders in the growth rate of M-commerce are the countries of Europe and China. Other interesting facts about the global mobile commerce market can be found in the infographics, translated by the PayOnline team, the company that processes payments on websites and mobile apps.
