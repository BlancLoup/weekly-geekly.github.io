About the Startup of the Year Award


This competition was organized by the HSE Business Incubator. The history of the event begins in 2008 and has been going on for 7 years. The Startup of the Year award is presented annually to the most outstanding projects in the business community. This year, the Startup of the Year 2014 award was organized with the support of RVC and Sberbank of Russia.

The main task of the Prize is to encourage the most successful and interesting entrepreneurial undertakings. Mark a professional approach to creating a new business, evaluate the success of bright startups and motivate young entrepreneurs to conquer new business tops.

‚ÄúStartup of the Year‚Äù is a kind of Oscar for startups, a spectacular and interesting show. At the awards ceremony, viewers will not only be able to read the success stories of young entrepreneurs and watch videos about nominee projects, but also vote for their favorite startups.
