Start pre-order Meizu MX2!

Welcome, Habraludi! Today we have another important reason for the news, although we were late for a day, but that is not a problem. On December 10, we launched a pre-order for the new Meizu MX2 smartphone, on the same day as the launch of pre-orders for a new product in China.




Three options are available for pre-order:

  • Meizu MX2 16 GB - 17,997 rubles
  • Meizu MX2 32 GB - 19,997 rubles
  • Meizu MX2 64 GB - 21 997 rubles

Each pre-order will receive a Meizu EP20S branded audio headset as a gift! You can pre-order in the official online store Meizu in Russia - mymeizu.ru .

The main features of the Meizu MX2:


  • Stainless steel frame at the base of the housing, which provides high structural strength
  • The minimum thickness of the frame between the display and the edge of the case (only 3.15 mm), due to which the smartphone is relatively compact with a large diagonal display
  • High-quality screen Sharp New Mode 2, a diagonal of 4.4 inches, a resolution of 1280x800 pixels, pixel density of 347 ppi
  • Durable Corning Gorilla Glass protects your screen from scratches and other damage.
  • CABC (Content Adaptive Brightness Control) technology automatically changes the screen brightness level depending on the type of information displayed to save battery power
  • Quad-core processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz and 2 GB of dual-channel RAM
  • Ability to choose one of three versions by built-in memory: 16/32/64 GB
  • Instant activation of the camera with a left or right swipe from the lock screen and continuous shooting at 9 frames per second
  • Video processor Mali-400MP4 with a frequency of 533 MHz
  • Wolfson Microelectronics Sound Chip
  • Support for standard audio and video players in most formats, including uncompressed audio and video
  • Automatically record all phone conversations
  • Two microphones: main for voice transmission and additional for suppressing external noise
  • Flyme 2.0 corporate interface on the current version of Android 4.1.1, with many useful programs and settings
  • Superuser rights (root) "out of the box"
