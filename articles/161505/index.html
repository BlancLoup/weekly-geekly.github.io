Express schools and webinars on Imagine Cup



Friends,

We are continuing the series of express schools and webinars, where we will tell you more about the online competitions of the Imagine Cup and about the competition of games and answer your questions.

Each of the webinars is not only a story about the competition, but also an opportunity for you to share the topic of the proposed project, get feedback from the competition curator at first hand, useful tips on participating in the competition and on improving specifically your idea. Do not miss the opportunity to talk with experts directly!


In the next week we will hold several webinars:

Windows Azure online contest

December 6, 17.00 Moscow time
Host: Natalia Efimtseva ( inatale )
Connection link: join.microsoft.com/meet/natale/NTSLNH13

Windows 8 online contest

December 7, 17.00 Moscow time
Host: Konstantin Kichinsky ( kichik )
Connection link: join.microsoft.com/meet/v-nikit/HNL3YZLW

Games Competition Imagine Cup

December 10, 17.00 Moscow time
Host: Dmitry Andreev ( dmandreev )
Connection link: join.microsoft.com/meet/v-nikit/86QDND2J

Windows Phone online contest

December 13, 17.00 Moscow time
Host: Stas Pavlov ( stasus )
Connection link: join.microsoft.com/meet/stanisp/P4Y649L6

To connect, you must first install Lync Attendee, and when connecting, be sure to select the appropriate option (do not use a web connection via a browser - this way you cannot hear the speaker).

Express Schools

We are also continuing a series of express schools for developing Windows 8. The express school is a unique workshop where, in one day, a team of like-minded people, together with experts from the Microsoft community and ready-made ideas, create prototypes of applications for the Windows platform.

The December schedule is available here: www.microsoft.com/rus/imaginecup/#camps

I also remind you that on December 14 we will hold the Imagine Cup school in St. Petersburg , with which an online broadcast will be organized, and during which you can, for example, learn how to effectively present your project.
