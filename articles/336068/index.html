4. Choco: balance the mark


Choco is a chocolate workshop that makes handmade sweets. The logo of the workshop shows a piece of chocolate, we gave some tips on how to enhance the logo.

The first thing you can advise is to balance the sign and the font by making the sign smaller. In the first version, the sign interrupts the font, it is too big compared to the font.

The second tip: make a smaller angle of the chocolate bar and remove the strokes around it. They make the logo "dirty" and too loaded. You can add bevels at the corners and make the color more "chocolate". So the sign will appear the desired shape and volume.
