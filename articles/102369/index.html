Booot CMS is a free store engine.



We wrote our CMS for a long time, ran it on work projects and added different functions and it seemed to work out pretty well! We cannot hide it anymore and give it to everyone.

This edition includes all standard functions: editing content, blocks, distribution of access rights in the administration system, editing templates and css from the admin area.
As well as two simple and very useful modules: a catalog of goods and a store.

It turns out very simple and convenient online store, although it does not have super functions for the selection and comparison of goods, but it is expanding, which we do. The engine has documentation (in the form of a wiki), a demo and of course you can download it from here - booot.ru .
