How to become a member of the electronic bidding state order

In many business sectors, obtaining a state order is a guarantee of the demand for services and providing employees with work, stable profits and advancement in ratings, a kind of airbag.

Among a large number of Russian companies there is an opinion that after receiving a certificate of electronic digital signature in one of the many certifying centers, you can immediately participate in electronic auctions, for example, a state order.

As a rule, the day before the start of trading, quite unexpectedly, it turns out that you need to do a lot of things that take a lot of time.


FROM ACCEPTING A DECISION ON OBTAINING A CERTIFICATE OF EDS TO A CONCLUSION OF A STATE CONTRACT WILL TAKE A MORE AT LEAST MONTH


So, what to do, how long it will take and how much it costs.

Step 1. Obtaining an EDS


Duration: 1 to 5 days
Actions: selection of certification center, preparation of documents, application, payment, visit to the office of certification center for obtaining digital signature

It is necessary to receive the certificate of the EDS in the authorized certifying center. The lists of authorized certification centers are published on each federal electronic trading platform. Any certification center from these lists is authorized on all five federal sites. The cost of the EDS certificate and the necessary software is about ten thousand rubles. It is necessary to fill out an application in the form of the certifying center and submit a fresh notarized extract from the Register, as well as a copy of the document confirming the authority of the manager or power of attorney confirming the authority of the owner of the EDS certificate. The SP is sufficient to submit a notarized copy of the extract from the EGRIP and the passport. Sometimes the certification center may require other documents, such as a copy of the statute.

Step 2. Install the necessary software


Duration: from 30 minutes to several hours
Installation can be performed by certification center specialists.
There is a free software (crypto-provider) that you can download.

Step 3. Accreditation at federal electronic trading platforms


Duration: from 5 working days
A unique case - 60 (!) Working days in connection with the appeal against the denial of accreditation

For potential state order suppliers this service is free if there is a certificate of EDS obtained in an accredited certification center and a properly configured computer.

Official government procurement portal:
zakupki.gov.ru

Official sites of federal trading platforms:
Sberbank-AST instruction
RTS-Tender manual

Roseltorg - EETP instruction
MICEX instruction

Here begins the first difficulties that need to be addressed in a short time.
The sites require strict observance of their regulations, and the forms for accreditation and peculiarities of software customization at each site are different. Information with varying degrees of detail and readability is published on official websites. There are often discrepancies in the 94-FZ law and the requirements of the site. Terms in which sites consider applications also vary. But the most paradoxical is that the documents that meet the requirements of some sites are rejected by others. All documents are submitted in electronic form, scans are best done in pdf so that the file size does not exceed 5 MB.

You can quickly resolve issues in case of refusal of accreditation by e-mail and through feedback forms, and some sites even provide the opportunity to communicate with operators by phone.

DO NOT CALCULATE FOR PASSING ACCREDITATION FIRST TIME


Passage of accreditation sometimes lasts longer than 5 working days, according to the regulations. Companies that clearly understand the timing of the competition should be taken into account. It often makes sense to entrust the passage of accreditation to a third-party organization that deals with this professionally. These are acceptable amounts, as a rule, not exceeding several thousand rubles, given that accreditation is given for 3 years.

Step 4. Selection of the competition, study of the tender documentation


In addition to time-consuming, a certain skill is required. Recently, the government procurement portal, which publishes information on all state order tenders, works quite well. Thoughtful analysis of the tender documentation will reduce the risk of participation in the competition, which will cause you losses.

There are a sufficient number of examples of obtaining negative experience of participating in obviously unprofitable projects. In addition, there are cases of improper reading of the requirements of the tender documentation, and as a result - the fulfillment of unfavorable conditions, therefore, the recommendation of a professional, his study of the tender documentation of the auction you are interested in is the key to the company's success.

Step 5. Transfer of financial support of the application to the electronic trading platform account


Duration: from 1 to 2 banking days
Difficulties: finding financial support for an application or a company that is ready to provide it

Participation in the competition is possible only if there is money in the special account of the bidder to secure an application for participation in the competition. Usually this amount ranges from 2 to 5% of the initial contract price. When applying, this money is blocked on the account and is subject to mandatory return at the end of the competition. The winner of the competition shall be charged an amount covering the cost of the site services (up to several thousand rubles). It should be borne in mind that at some sites money appears on the account only after 2 days (banking day + business day) and nothing can be done about it.

While there is no money in the special account of the electronic trading platform, it is impossible to submit a bid. The site will block the submission of documents.

Step 6. Preparation of the first and second parts of applications and submission for participation in the competition


Duration: 1 to 6 hours
Difficulties: compliance with all requirements for the design of applications

So, the money on a special account, you can proceed to the application. This action is significantly different from submitting an application for a paper competition. The application consists of two parts, but they will be available to the customer and the competition commission at different times.

Step 7. Consideration of the first parts of applications by the commission


Duration: from 3 to 5 days

The first part of the application is considered by the competition committee prior to the beginning of the competition, after which a decision is made on the admission of the company to participate in the competition.

Step 8. Electronic bidding procedure


Duration: 10 minutes to 48 hours
Difficulties: difficult to sit at the computer for 48 hours

One should not mention the heat of passions and tension - everything is clear, as sometimes the cost of the competition can be equal to the annual turnover of the company. Of course, each participant knows in advance the approximate cost of their services or goods, to which he is ready to bargain. But if the participant made a mistake at the crucial moment, the cancellation of the bet will not work. It is very convenient when the contest is conducted by an performer from the side who understands well the principle of the auction and knows the extreme price.

Step 9. Consideration of the second part of applications by the commission


Duration: 4 to 6 days depending on the amount of the contract

The second part of the application becomes available for consideration by the commission after the electronic bidding, on the basis of which the final decision is made about which of the participants will be contracted.

LITTLE TO WIN A COMPETITION OFFERING THE BEST PRICE. MUST CONFIRM YOUR OPPORTUNITIES TO PERFORM A CONTRACT


If the technical winner, the company that offered the best price, will be recognized, upon consideration of the second part of the applications, unable to fulfill the order, then the contract will be concluded with the participant whose second part of the application fully complies with the requirements of the tender documentation.
