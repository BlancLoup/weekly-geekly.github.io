Comparison of the cost of using cartridges for laser printers (top models in 2014)



We welcome the habrasoobschestvo again!
Recall that the previous post ( Laser printers and consumables: a memo for choosing and extending the life ), we devoted to the description of the basic principles of the choice and extending the life of laser printers and consumables for them.

In the comments to the material, a curious suggestion arose: to compare the cost of using consumables for different groups of printing equipment. The proposal is really quite interesting, and since we can give a detailed (more than) answer, we hope that all this information will be useful to both private users and corporations. In general, let's get started.


There are 4 main groups of laser printers:
- personal equipment;
- small equipment;
- medium technology;
- large equipment.

We also identified 3 main groups of consumables for laser printers:
- original cartridges;
- refilled and reconditioned cartridges;
- compatible cartridges.

Choosing 2-3 top models in each of the groups of printers, we compared them according to the ‚Äúcopy price‚Äù criterion, where the copy price is the cost of the printed document, which includes only the cost of consumables (cartridges) without taking into account the maintenance of the printer.

Comparing the cost of using cartridges for laser printers

The purpose of the comparison is to give the most objective assessment of the cost of using various consumables.
We made the choice of TOP cartridge models on the basis of data from the Business-Inform Information and Marketing Agency (http://sforp.ru/docs/marketing/index.htm), as well as on the basis of statistics unloaded from our company's information system.
Cartridge prices are an arithmetic average of prices for several leading suppliers of original, compatible and refillable / remanufactured cartridges.
