Protect user passwords on your site with Yandex

Immediately make a reservation that this message will be primarily interesting to developers of CMS, online stores, portals, forums and blog hosting sites. It will be a question of safe and convenient authorization on your sites. The topic has always been important, and recent industry events have once again emphasized this.

We want to remind you about the possibility to do authorization through Yandex . It is much easier, faster and safer than creating your own authorization system. You do not need to typeset anything, think about captcha , take care of storing passwords - there is a ready-made, easy-to-implement, free technology that can be connected to almost any site.

In addition, it simplifies access to your site for approximately 40 million users registered on Yandex. They do not need to invent and remember another login and password. And if the user is already authorized on Yandex at the time of entering the site, then he only needs to make one click to authorize on the site.

Instruction:
1. Register your site on the service OAuth ;
2. Implement on-site receipt and processing of OAuth tokens
3. Provide users with a login interface through Yandex - add an authorization button to the site and realize the receipt of user data.
