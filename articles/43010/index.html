Samsung Epix - QWERTY-smartphone with a "touchscreen" and "optical mouse"

Hello!

Samsung, together with the mobile operator AT & T, officially announced the release of the new Samsung SGH-i907 Epix smartphone (Windows Mobile Professional 6.1.).




The device is in many ways similar to the model Samsung SGH-i780. It is equipped with a QWERTY-keyboard and is equipped with a miniature touch panel, called the developers "optical mouse" (you can control the device like a regular laptop). As for the technical characteristics of the product, they are:

  • The ability to work in networks GSM / GPRS / EDGE (850/900/1800/1900 MHz) and UMTS / HSDPA (850/900/1900/2100 MHz);
  • 256 MB of ROM and 150 MB of RAM;
  • Slot for connecting microSDHC-cards up to 32 GB;
  • Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g and Bluetooth 2.0 (A2DP);
  • USB interface;
  • Support aGPS and AT & T Navigator;
  • Integrated 2 megapixel digital camera;
  • Built-in audio player, operating formats MP3, WAV, AAC, AAC +, eAAC + and WAV;
  • 1800 mAh rechargeable battery, which lasts for 7 hours of continuous negotiations or 14 days of autonomous work in standby mode;
  • Overall dimensions are 117 x 61 x 13 mm;
  • Weight is 129 g.


Buy Samsung SGH-i907 Epix, taking into account the design of a two-year contract and discounts, you can now at a price of $ 200.
