The Ministry of Finance and Communications of the Russian Federation and the Accounts Chamber of the Russian Federation signed an agreement on cooperation in the field of IT

The Ministry of Finance and Communications of the Russian Federation and the Accounts Chamber of the Russian Federation signed an agreement on cooperation in the field of information technology. Under the terms of the agreement, cooperation will be organized in two main areas: the creation of a unified information analytical system of financial control authorities in Russia and the development of an information-analytical system of financial control over the implementation of priority national projects, Interfax reported.
