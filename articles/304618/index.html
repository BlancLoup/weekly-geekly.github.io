OctoberCMS Stable Release

It is finished! After more than two years of development, the first stable release of October CMS , which is based on the Laravel 5.1 LTS framework, was released. During these two years, the system has been installed more than 100 thousand times.



October - CMS, based on the Laravel 5.1 LTS PHP framework. This means that from the box we have access to the artisan , Eloquent ORM command line tools, migration, which greatly simplifies development.

October has a built-in admin panel that allows you to fully manage the site, edit templates and more. Also out of the box has a built-in file manager that supports CDN, can work with images and manage files directly from the web interface.


Also, this great system has its own store where developers can sell their plugins and themes. In honor of the release, the creators of OctoberCMS give us a discount coupon for 15% discount for plug-ins and those that cost more than $ 10.

Coupon is valid until July 9
