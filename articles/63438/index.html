Closed beta testing of Majesty 2

On the official website of the game there was news that the acceptance of applications for participation in the closed beta testing of Majesty 2 is starting. Since July 9, players aged 18 and over who have Russian citizenship will be able to try themselves as the ruler of a fairytale state.

In order to become a beta test participant, it is necessary to send an application to the e-mail box beta test@ino-co.com by July 8th. In the subject of the letter, you must specify the "Beta test", and in the text - your name or nickname, age, nationality, configuration of the existing computer and Internet access parameters. The number of participants in the beta, of course, is limited.

And this means that there is very little waiting for the game.

Who for some reason is not familiar with Majesty, I tell you: this is a cult real-time fantasy strategy, where the system of indirect control of units is implemented. The player rebuilds a small settlement around his castle, builds guilds and temples, where he hires heroes. Heroes of the player can not control, only to motivate. For this, he has at his disposal two flags (and there will be four of them in the second part) ‚Äîthe attack flag and the research flag.
