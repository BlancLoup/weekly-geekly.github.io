As for the storage systems, they differ depending on the category of disk resources. For the vStorage Silver category, this is, for example, the IBM DCS3700 and Hitachi. For the Gold category - IBM XIV Storage System , which I want to talk about in more detail.

With its innovative grid architecture, the IBM XIV Storage System provides unmatched performance and scalability. Grid-architecture consists of a set of similar components, united by a common interconnect. The disks in IBM XIV behave like ‚Äúcommunicating vessels‚Äù, due to which their uniform load is achieved. All IBM XIV components are reserved under the Active-Active N + 1 scheme, ensuring a high level of reliability and availability. In case of failure of any component, the fastest recovery is ensured.

Variety of resource accounting

TelecityGroup always for an individual approach to the client. We are pleased to offer various options for accounting for virtual resources, and always pre-conduct an audit of the current IT infrastructure of the customer to select the most optimal option.

Option 1: the upper and lower limits of the allocation of computing resources are individually negotiated. The lower limit is guaranteed and the resources between the upper and lower limits are allocated on demand and are paid upon consumption. This ensures payment flexibility and scalability. Disk resources are paid on the fact of consumption.

Option 2: a pool of resources with a fixed monthly fee for customers who can predict the amount of virtual capacity they need.

Option 3: Full-scale Pay-as-you-Go model with hourly billing.

Billing is automated, and in a short time it will be possible to view the statistics of spending funds directly on the self-service portal. Now you can get information from our support service, which serves your interests around the clock, seven days a week. Incidentally, regardless of the option prepayment is not required.
