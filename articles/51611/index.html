Squeeze on mobile

One of the main problems for a mobile phone user is a long url. To open a picture or go to an article of interest, you had to rewrite the text on a piece of paper, then write it neatly in the address input string in the browser. Agree, a difficult and tedious task. And by the way, the iPhone also does not know how to copy anything, which makes life very difficult for its owner.
Fresh and interesting service delivr.com should come to the rescue.
Why is he so good?
Firstly, it makes easy to remember and quickly typed links.
For example, the link to Habrahabr is http://delivr.com/102lh .
Secondly, the service offers many additional features. A user who has followed a short link will not immediately get to the site. At first, a light pda page will open on which you will be offered to choose in which form it is convenient for you to view the site of interest: mobile or original. Also, the resulting page can be translated into any language, just by selecting from the drop-down list, what you need.
Perhaps the most important thing on the Internet is the opportunity to exchange interesting material. Delivr.com and then become an indispensable assistant. All from the same page, you can send a link to Twitter, Jaiku or send an e-mail. Below you will find a link to the QR code, sending sms (alas, it works only for the USA so far), and in the upper part there will be a small translation of the site.
For advanced users, a bookmarklet is offered (just drag the link to your bookmarks), which will allow you not to constantly go to the site of the service, but gives you the opportunity to simply click on the tab and get ready-made legal entities like hot cakes.

Be more mobile, and this will help you a new service!
