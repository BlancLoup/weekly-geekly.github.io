Xcellery startup for sale on eBay

The creator of the site Xcellery, the online viewer and editor of MS Excel spreadsheets, put his brainchild on sale in the online auction site eBay . The lowest starting price of the lot is $ 10 thousand , which is approximately $ 1 per user.

The sale of the project does not mean its closure, however, as stated , the Xcellery team does not have time for this site and it is going to start work on new projects. Founder of the site Reto Lammler (Reto Lammler) said that he and his co-founder Heng Yi (Heng-Yi) hope to stay in the team.

This is not the first startup put up for sale in eBay. So, at one time, the meta search engine jux2.com , another search engine Digforlt.com , the online calendar SynapseLife and something else that passed my ears went under the hammer :)
