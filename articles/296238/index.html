eToro and Sberbank will talk about the modern realities of the financial world

On November 11, the CEO of eToro Yoni Assia in Moscow will present the report Money Management 2.0, reflecting the modern realities of the financial world. Together with the Vice-President of Sberbank Mikhail Ehrenburg and the general director of the Venture Fund of Sberbank Viktor Orlovsky, the company will present the Russian version of the social trading platform eToro.

Today I will be at this event. Write in the comments you have questions - I will try to ask them or pay attention to the necessary information during the presentations.



Founded in 2007, eToro is the largest social network for traders in the world. In November 2014, the Venture Fund of Sberbank SBT Venture Capital, together with the Chinese corporation Ping An, invested $ 27 million in the company . Investments have helped eToro accelerate development in Russia. Pavel Salas, head of eToro in Russia, then confirmed the launch of a full cycle of services in Russian.

Now the eToro platform is used by 4.5 million people from 140 countries. Its competitors include Alpari , Plus500 , Forex Club , and Finam .
