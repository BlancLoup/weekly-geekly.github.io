We pay money for useful reviews.

January 31 at the project "Tutumba" starts the action "Valuable Opinion" . As part of the promotion, any user who leaves 50 useful reviews about companies in his city on a project receives a reward of 1,000 rubles!

All users registered on the Tutumba project automatically take part in the Valued Opinion action. More information about the conditions of the action can be found here http://www.tutumba.ru/reward.html#top .

And for those who already write about restaurants, shops and clubs in their city in their blog, we offer to become an expert on the Tutumba project. Experts receive the appropriate distinguishing mark and have additional opportunities for earnings.

Tutumba is a city guide that allows consumers to share their impressions of stores, restaurants and service businesses in their hometown. And business owners communicate directly with consumers and tell them about their opportunities and offers.

The project was launched in December 2010 and is currently operating in the 36 largest cities of Russia. The project has more than 700,000 regional companies registered.
