Nokia launches new loyalty program for developers DVLUP

Hi, Habr!

We are happy to share great news for application developers!

Every day, your applications entertain millions of users around the world. We really appreciate your choice of our platform and prepared a special offer.




DVLUP gives you the opportunity to receive awards for developing applications for Windows Phone. As soon as you send the application to participate in the contest, you get experience points, which you can then use to get prizes from the DVLUP award catalog. The awards catalog is quite varied: from gift cards from leading retailers and the possibilities of advertising applications in the Windows Phone App Store to the latest Nokia Lumia models and much more.

Join the project right now and take part in any of the large number of DVLUP contests: publish your applications for Windows Phone and Asha and get points for new applications, updates of old, high rating applications, use of music, cartographic APIs, application of NFC technology and many others the tasks that can be found in the tab Challenges and which await you in the near future.

In order to start using the new service, you need to register with DVLUP and go through the competition in the "Applications", "Games" or "Updates" sections, sending the application for publication in the Windows Phone App Store.

DVLUP is directly linked to the Windows Phone App Store and Nokia Store. When publishing applications for Windows Phone and Asha, the system will automatically recognize all published applications and award points to you.

And remember: in DVLUP, everyone who completed the task gets points, and not just the first ones who have done it!

You can get acquainted with all the details of participation by watching the official DVLUP video program .
