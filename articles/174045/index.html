HTC Myst is still real and will be presented very soon.

Myst is a code name received by a smartphone from HTC which will be closely integrated with the social network of Mark Zuckerberg. Soon, Facebook-Phone will be officially presented.



The rest of the information under the cut.

This communicator can be a joint product of three companies at once: HTC, Facebook and the mobile operator AT & T, which will sell the device, but nothing has been said about whether the new product will appear in the future on the markets of other countries, including Europe.

Let me remind you what we expect from the HTC Myst (also known as HTC Opera UL) 4.3-inch HD display, Snapdragon MSM8960 dual-core processor with a clock frequency of 1.5 GHz, 1 GB of RAM, 16 GB of internal memory, 5-megapixel main camera, 1.6 megapixel front camera, support for 4 generation LTE networks and Android 4.1.2 Jelly Bean as an operating system.

The presentation of this unit may take place this spring.
