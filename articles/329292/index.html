Videos: CocoaHeads meetup at Badoo office


On Friday, May 19, CocoaHeads-mitap for iOS developers was held in our Moscow office. Diverse in content: two presentations by speakers, a quiz on knowledge testing and a panel discussion with experts from well-known IT companies on the market. Video report - under the cut!


Speakers


Katerina Trofimenko, Badoo


Topic: Development of features: from flags to a / b-tests


Katya told us what feature flags are, how they help us in Badoo to develop large features iteratively, by several developers, and not to worry about the code going into releases. She told about how the targeted layout of features has grown into a / b-testing system and how it all looks from the side of the iOS client



Slides

Alexander Zimin, iOS developer


Theme: Swift Magic


Sasha spoke about non-standard language features for real projects. About why complicate your life and what advantages it can give.


  • Protocol-Oriented Programming and its dilemmas
  • When and why use generalizations and nested types
  • Swift present and future


Slides

Quiz


CocoaHeads for the first time publish the traditional quiz, which is held for the sixth time. We gave the winners cool prizes: Lego-set and Malin from Ampereka - to make it even more interesting to play.



Round table


We invited some of the most active guys in the iOS community to ask how large IT companies conduct interviews, luring programmers how to build DevPR.
