eTraxis - SaaS Tracker hosted in the cloud

The kindest.

My time is following the development of the open source project eTraxis , this is a change requests management system. Most of these systems are known as bugtrakers. I happened to write for her a little code and participate in its implementation for the needs of a large team (several dozen people). Its distinctive feature is a great flexibility in customizing workflow templates, their fields, user groups and access rights - up to a separate field in an arbitrary state.

In general, the project is interesting to me, I follow its development. Well, not so long ago, the creator of the system decided to reach a new level - and that is, to create an online service (what is now called SaaS). No sooner said than done, https://www.etraxis.com/ was launched.

After registration, a separate 3rd level domain becomes available to the user (it is specified during registration), you can create your own templates and your user base. Know yourself, read the docks and do what you want.

For the launch, virtual hosting in the cloud of one young cloud provider, Clodo, was purchased . This allowed not to waste time on trifles and create subdomains for created projects. And at the same time to make any server settings, including screwing SSL.

The service is available for free, but with a limit of 2 workflow templates and 5 users for the project.

In general, it turned out well, in my opinion. Who is looking for a public tracker - I recommend.
