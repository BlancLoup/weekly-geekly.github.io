New features of LifePay: everything for the convenience of customers

Today we want to talk about two new features: cache and tip.

The cache function is designed to account for cash payments in your account. The client on the spot, as before, selects a payment method. Only now, in the application, and accordingly in the personal account, all the money received is recorded - both via a card and cash, which gives additional control and accounting for the actions of couriers, employees, etc.

Tip. It is logical that when you pay with a card, you need to climb for cash, to leave it to tea, if you liked everything, the client does not want to, and it is uncomfortable. Therefore, we have introduced in the application the opportunity to receive tips when paying with a card - a good incentive for your staff to work better.

In connection with the foregoing, the procedure for making a payment has changed. The first step remains the same - enter the payment amount and its purpose and click Next.

A screen appears with a choice of payment method - cash or card.
