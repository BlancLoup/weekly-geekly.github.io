Favourite buisness

It has long been noted one truth that a person achieves success in the fact that brings him the greatest pleasure. In life you can meet many examples, I will give you one of my personal life, which I remembered the other day while watching the movie ‚ÄúThank You For Smoking‚Äù.

This story happened a few years ago, and then I did not attach much importance to it, but now I understood the meaning of that lesson ...
I played a role-playing game in one of the Internet forums, the idea was not original, but I came up with the plot and characters myself. I created a game that I myself would be interested to play. I will say more that I created a game in which it was interesting to be the lead :). On the other hand, I did not strain at all physically or morally, I did everything relaxed and with pleasure, perhaps even with passion. Time I spent a few hours, and then enjoyed the game. More precisely, at first I enjoyed creating the game, and then playing it.

What was my surprise when all the participants, even the losers, expressed great enthusiasm for the game. It is worth noting that the majority of the participants were people I did not know very well, besides, for them it was not the first role-playing game in this forum, but I saw such positive feedback from them for the first time. At that time I was not engaged in conducting trainings and sincerely amazed how such insignificant efforts on my part can cause such a large positive effect.

This case and many examples of other people make it clear that when a person is engaged in a business that he likes and enjoys, he does it many times better than a trained professional who does not experience such emotions at work.
