annotation
Have you ever wondered why it makes sense for Russian engineers to work abroad? Have you heard of Silicon Valley?

The book introduces the reader to the life of Russian engineers, working in the US, contains specific recommendations on how to get to work in Silicon Valley, tells about what to expect working in the US.

The author of the book is an insider who has traveled from an institute in St. Petersburg to a software development manager at a growing Silicon Valley company. He looks at the problem both from the point of view of a Russian engineer who is trying to get a job in the USA, and from the point of view of an employer who hires such engineers from time to time.

The book is intended for a wide readership: engineers, programmers, students, graduates, high school students, as well as for all active people who are striving for success.
