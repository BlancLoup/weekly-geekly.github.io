MySpace fell out with Photobucket

The administration of the social resource MySpace has blocked the display on its pages of videos and slide shows hosted on the Photobucket website.

The reason for this was (as stated by MySpace representatives) violations of the rules by Photobucket, which encouraged users to place on their pages advertising content on the film "Spider-Man 3."

An appeal to users who are invited to "vote with their feet and keyboard" is published on the Photobucket website. It is interesting that with the photos posted on Photobucket, no problems arise.
