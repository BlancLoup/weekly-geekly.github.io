Welcome to the MIXAR conference on September 24



We invite you on September 24 to the ZIL Cultural Center, where the non-classical conference on the latest technologies MIXAR2016 will be held, where we are the general partner. The event will combine art, science and business in the areas of 3D, augmented and virtual reality, interactive technologies. This is a full day conference with immersion in all sorts of realities. Offers:

  • technology exhibition;
  • reports and open discussions;
  • shopping area;
  • installations of digital art.

Read more under the cat.

Conference program:

10:00 Start registration.
11:00 - 11:30 Opening of the conference.
11:30 - 20:30 Lectures in three lecture halls: art, science and business.
11:30 - 21:00 MIXAR VR FILMFEST.
20:30 - 21:30 Award Ceremony MIXAR AWARDS.
21:30 - 23:30 A party.

The conference will have several zones.

EXPO . An exhibition of Russian and foreign companies will be held in the column hall of the ZIL Cultural Center. In this area, you can get acquainted with representatives of companies, learn how to apply technology in your business or how to start your career in innovative areas. This year, guests will have the opportunity to visit a real VR film festival in the conference expo area, where they will watch films of many festivals in the format of 360 degrees in the format of a mass immersion.

LECTURE . The lecture zone consists of three sectors: art, science and business. The conference will be collected truly unique content. All participants undergo compulsory moderation, among the speakers will be our employee Maxim Pestun. See the lecture schedule on the conference website .

ART . Art permeates our whole life! That is what inspires scientists to discover in their fields. At the conference, the art zone will slightly open the veil of a stunning new phygital exhibition, which has no analogues all over the world.

LOUNGE . A small area in the courtyard of the Cultural Center ZIL for shopping, catering and lounge at the event.

KIDS . Area with educational tech entertainment for children.

PARTY . By tradition, a busy day of knowledge and impressions on MIXAR will be replaced by an atmospheric party soaked in the soft sound of techno and deep house, talk of other realities and a variety of drinks and snacks. The conference space will change and give the mood of comfort and good mood! But that is not all. This year, a special surprise was prepared for afterparty guests, do not even try to guess, you will see for yourself.

The conference will provide an opportunity to find potential customers and performers, recruit a dream team, attract young professionals, acquire new ideas, knowledge, contacts, partners and showcase their achievements to investors.

Participation is free, but you must register . Address: Moscow, st. East, 4, Bldg. 1, m. Avtozavodskaya.
