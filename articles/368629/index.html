Modern Music: Headphones Overview Sennheiser Momentum 2 on-ear

Frankly, there are many reasons why I like Sennheiser products. Legendary brand, attention to detail in the design and production, sound quality, extended warranty on all their products. Below a certain bar, the legendary German concern does not allow itself to descend not only in the top lines, but also in a completely affordable price segment. Recall at least HD 598 or super-popular CX 300 II - the level of sound quality and assembly for their price I once just hit. It was all the more interesting for me to take in my hands or, if you like, in my ears, the representative of the new generation Sennheiser - the model Momentum M2 OE .



As you already understood from the name - headphones are classified as overhead in their form factor. Probably, now I risk to hear the deaf murmur of experienced audiophiles, who will tell me that in this form factor there can be no good sound in principle, because the size of the drivers will not allow to get a truly ultimatum sound out of them. But let me, gentlemen ... aren't the big, full-size headphones designed for quiet listening to music at home, along with a glass of good brandy and a Cuban cigar? And this is a sacred process, which you obviously will not be able to repeat, while on the road or sitting at your office laptop.

In-ear'y? Yes, I agree, the sound of good in-ear headphones allows you to enjoy music regardless of your location, but, firstly, not everyone can do it without a certain discomfort in the ears, and secondly, let's be frank, this sound will cost you much more expensive. That is why the invoice headphone form factor (on-ear) has become that very trade-off option, which will allow not to lose much in the sound, and not to part with your favorite music.

In general, as you have probably understood, I will tell you today about this very interesting representative of the Senment of Sennheiser Momentum - Momentum 2 On-Ear models.
