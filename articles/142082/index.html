Microsoft Open Technologies Inc

Microsoft has surprised many by announcing the formation of a subsidiary of Microsoft Open Technologies Inc., which will be engaged in Open Source projects, communication with open standards groups and cross-platform initiatives. The new company will be formed on the basis of the Interoperability Strategy division, which recently worked on such projects as HTML5, HTTP 2.0, cloud standards in DMTF and OASIS, Node.js, MongoDB, Phonegap / Cordova, etc.
