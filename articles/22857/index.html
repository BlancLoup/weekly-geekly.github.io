Password Bird Password Generator

Password Bird - service for automatic generation of passwords based on some source data. In this case, you can enter a name, phrase and date. Based on them, a password will be issued. I will not say that such an algorithm is ideal, but it is quite suitable for fast generation. Usually the result is easy to remember. The creators claim that they do not save the results of the generator. That is nothing that you have received, no one will see.
