Stop analytics

Some kind of madness is going on around, everyone is analyzing something.

I'll tell you by example. I am promoting websites. As a creative person, I can be distracted, go headlong into new ideas, and so on. For example, now began to keep your blog . But like every person, I have some basic activity that feeds me.

Today I decided to find out what my employees are doing and I realized that they are all sitting and analyzing something.

Example 1

The client's site fell out of the index, because he was attacked, then he returned to the index, but to bad places. I wrote a letter in Yandex, I was told that on the site "re-optimized texts", they say correct, make the texts readable for people and we will return you to the top.
I called the assistant, she instructed the optimizer to do an analysis of the top, how many entries we have, how many competitors have. He made this report, he took him 8 hours. The optimizer receives 200 rubles per hour, so this report cost me 1600 rubles, not including electricity, rent, taxes and depreciation of equipment.

The question is, who needs this score, who will read it? I will not, I already see that the texts on this site are ala "Somehow SEO-specialist comes to a bar, restaurant, buy alcoholic beverages, clubs, the best bars in Moscow, order a banquet in a restaurant," I understand reports Not needed. The report will be read by the assistant, will still climb the site, look at everything and also spend a couple of hours on it.

And that in the end, in the end there is a report, but nothing has changed on the site. And it was possible without a report, just spend these one and a half days to redo all the texts on the site.

Example 2

Conclusion

Today, when sorting out google docs, I realized that most of the reports are not read by anyone. Moreover, the part is made in such a form that it is impossible to read it at all.

When an employee sits VKontakte, plays games at work, you can fight it.
