Why a StackOverflow profile is a good resume


What are the main questions asked at interviews in most companies?

  1. Tell us about your education.
  2. Tell us about your work experience.
  3. What technologies did you work with?
  4. What projects did they take part in, what was their level of complexity?
  5. Pass the test and show examples of written code.

Resume and LinekdIn profile can formally answer all these questions, but they will not answer exactly the other, more important ones:

  1. How did the applicant go into this or that technology or subject area?
  2. How long has he used this or that technology, skill, etc.?
  3. Is he a real ‚Äúseignor developer‚Äù or just decided to call himself that?

Imagine, for example, such a situation. The applicant indicated the technologies with which he worked: ASP.NET, WPF, SharePoint, JavaScript, Java.
