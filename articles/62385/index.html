Layar - interactive reality

Yesterday (06/17/09) a unique Dutch service was launched http://layar.eu/ .
With an Android phone, you can access real-time information about the world around you through his camera.
It can be information about cafes, restaurants, hotels and other social places.




At the top of the camera image, Layar adds layers with content. Layers are equivalent to pages in regular browsers. You can easily switch between layers by selecting them using the menu buttons or by swiping your finger across the screen.

This thing works through the API, which provides Layar to create a separate layer on your site. Those. information is displayed only for those objects that participate in the project.
