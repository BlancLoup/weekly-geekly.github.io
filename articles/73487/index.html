iPhone as remote control for car



Berlin techies have written an application for the iPhone with which you can remotely control the car. Naturally, the machine must be pre-prepared. The car was equipped with a video camera with a remote control system, Wi-Fi, satellite navigation, a three-dimensional laser sensor. But the iPhone has not undergone any modification.

Using the application, a person can control the clutch, brakes, gas, shift gears, and, of course, turn the steering wheel.

The project was implemented by Professor Raul Rojas as part of the study on the possibility of creating a taxi service without a driver.
