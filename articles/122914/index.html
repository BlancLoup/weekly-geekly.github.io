Reviews of web studios on WebProfessionals.ru

On WebProfessionals.ru launched a new service "Feedback on the work of web studios." Customers are given the opportunity to evaluate the work of the web studio and leave a comment.

Criteria for evaluation:

1. Satisfaction with the result
2. Punctuality in meeting deadlines
3. Mutual understanding, professionalism of employees
4. Probability of recommending a company to colleagues

Each criterion is rated on a scale of 1 to 5 points. Answers are accepted only upon confirmation of a corporate e-mail and if you have the necessary contact information. Of all the estimates, the total score is formed.
