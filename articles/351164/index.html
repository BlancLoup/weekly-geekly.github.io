Welcome to the Whatever Hack hackathon March 16


In our Moscow office on March 16, a 48-hour machine learning marathon will begin, during which you can demonstrate your skills to create a solution for generating memes, distinguish a wardrobe from a carpet, or make the best MVP (minimum viable product) within a free theme.


We invite everyone to solve any of the three tasks and fight for a prize fund of 1.5 million rubles (or not rubles).


Further - about the tasks and details.


The rules are very simple. Each team can have up to 4 people, with the in-person presence of all team members. Participants are invited to decide on the choice of one of three problems:
