Amazon, through Holidays Deals, sells its branded electronic products at a reduced price. For about another week, you can save money on a tablet, reader, smartphone, or set-top box from Amazon.




The price reduction has affected three out of five tablets in the newest Amazon lineup.
Kindle Fire HD 7 is temporarily available for $ 129 (instead of 154).
Kindle Fire HD Kids Edition can be bought for $ 159 (the regular price is $ 30 higher).
Kindle Fire HDX is priced at $ 199 - you can save 40 dollars.
The Kindle Fire HD 6 still sells for $ 99, and the largest in the line, the Kindle Fire HDX 8.9, as before, will cost $ 379.

At a lower price, you can also purchase two of the three available electronic ink readers. Fresh budget Amazon Kindle sells for $ 59, and the backlit version of the Kindle Paperwhite can be grabbed for $ 99 (instead of the usual 79 and 119 earlier). The price change did not affect the Kindle Voyage, which continues to be sold in limited quantities at a price of $ 199.

The revolutionary (but not demanded) unlocked Amazon Fire Phone is now available for $ 229, which is half the price.

The Amazon Fire TV set-top box is temporarily discounted by $ 20, you can buy it for $ 79.
