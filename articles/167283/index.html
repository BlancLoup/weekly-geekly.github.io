Chinese gamer killed two and burned the house because of the Internet shutdown


changed the picture at the request of workers

The incident occurred in an Internet cafe, when a quarrel broke out between the client and the owner of the establishment because of the disconnection. The infuriated client stabbed the owner with scissors and hammered his wife to death, and then set fire to the building.

The police in Renqiu City (Hebei Province) arrested the killer only at the beginning of this month, although the incident itself happened in December. Then a gamer named Zhao came to the Internet cafe, where he played online games from 6 pm. In the region of 10 hours, the Internet in the cabin suddenly disconnected. Zhao called the owner of the house, last name Ren, to find out the reasons for the gap. Ren explained the reasons that Zhao had downloaded a virus that disrupted the network.

Then the two began to quarrel, Zhao hit Zhen with his fist, in response to which the owner of the institution took out a hammer and began to defend himself. Then Zhao took the scissors and cut Zhen several times, took away the hammer and beat the owner of the cafe with blows to the head. When the master's wife came running to the noise, Zhao struck her several times with a hammer, scissors and a kitchen knife. After which the offender set fire to the building to get rid of evidence. The bodies were found only the next day.
