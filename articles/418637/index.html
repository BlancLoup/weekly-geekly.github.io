About Slurm and plans for Slurm-2


We planned Slurm for 30 places. Then they added another 20, and still, after 10 days, the places ran out. Once the topic is so popular, in the fall we will have Slurm-2, where there will be a repetition of the Slurm-1 program and an advanced course. There will be no more seats, so there is a chance that they will quickly run out again. If you leave a request on the site , you will receive a newsletter when we open the sale of tickets.


What will be on Slurm


The list of lectures you see in the schedule of broadcasts.
In addition to them, there will be practice and 5 supporting webinars:


- Docker / Ansible
- Kubernetes
- Helm
- Monitoring and logging
- CI / CD


The practice takes place on servers that are allocated by Southbridge partner and sponsor Slurm: Selectel. No minicubes, we raise, configure and check the failover of clusters on live servers.
