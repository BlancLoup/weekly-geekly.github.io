30 Apple Apple Design Concepts

Apple designs excite the minds of millions. This article contains 30 design concepts for Apple-style products. I will allow myself a somewhat loose translation of the article, rather even a retelling.

iPhones



Iphone elite

iPhone Video
This concept is designed for video. The author refined the design of the button, added a couple of features specific to the TV - mounting additional effects on the wall (Ambilight) and here it is - the new iPhone from Chromatix.



iEye
In the concept of the Eye, the Home button mutates into a button from the end like on the N95 camera. The device should be designed to shoot HD 720p without tension, be armed with a 5.1 mega-camera, 12 Gb flash memory and a high-capacity thermonuclear lithium-ion battery. A 30 pin connector (!) iPhone Nano
What will happen if you cross the iPhone and Nano? iPhone Nano! Excellent concept bundles of iPhone 3G and Nano G4 - style and elegance. I conjured FreshAndContent.



iPhone Pro
An iPhone for those stylish guys - straight lines and austere design. Supplied in 2 versions: cast iron, aluminum and glass.



Desktops and Laptops


Mac Mini (Slim)
Mac-mini in a super thin design - it has all the advantages of the original car, but in a slim case. We connect to the LED-display from Apple - and we get a powerful computer. Designed by Isamu Sanada.





Apple iView
IView - a concept with an oval display - more convenient for human perception. On the back side there is a smaller display - for which no one knows, but it's cool. There is also a neon light 2 cameras on the sides of the display - their purpose is kept by the author in secret. Created Nuno Teixeira.




Apple tribook

Tribook - what you see in the picture - i.e. a troecran. In the center - the usual screen. On the sides of the same additional screens that can be folded to save space. For their production it was proposed to use transparent screens - the patent technology of apple. Adam Benton dabbled with photoshop.



Transparent iMac
Real concept with unrealistically transparent screen. Why are there a screen - the keyboard and mouse are projected on the table! Especially for studio apartments at 32 meters your Adam Benton.



Macbook mini

Make your MacBook even smaller. A retractable trackpad panel over the keyboard is a concept for the future. I folded my MacBook 3 times Isamu Sanada.




Mac air

Now this is something purely innovative. Hybrid Apple Tablet and Mac Mini. 5.2-inch Led-display, wireless keyboard, mouse, Super Drive and Time Machine - we meet a full-featured wireless desktop. By Isamu Sanada.




Mac mini

The concept of avoiding flaws that infuriate the Mac Mini owners. Easy access to the port panel and V-shaped case for better cooling - this is the new Mac Mini. Designed by Sait Alanyali.



Mac tab

MacTab can be used as a tablet, and as a desktop computer with a keyboard. The keyboard is very thin and can be fixed on the back of the tablet for transport using the frame, which also serves to ensure a stable position on the table. Fantasized Yann Le Corroler.



Tablets



Mac Tablet

Macbook touch

Another concept from Isamu Sanada. The MacBook Touch is a large iPhone in a slightly modified package with additional ports.

MacTab Apple concept tablet with keyboard slider. Sculpted Creative Maverick18x.



Apple TouchBook

iPods



iPod Chimera
This concept is definitely one of the best and most complex iPod concepts. Along with the sleek and slim body, it is also equipped with wireless headphones. Titanium iPod chimera has a scratch and dirt resistant display capable of playing 720p HD video.



iPod Nano 4G

Not very creative, but pretty good representation of the iPod Nano 4G. The concept has smoother curves and a great combination of colors. The design is higher than the functional, but the accelerometer is still worth adding. Created Chromatix.



ipod shuffle

This concept will appeal to lovers of running. Ipod Shuffle in the form of a bracelet with wireless headphones. Isamu Sanada believes that the army of girls with this device will look very cute.



hiPod Concept

hiPod - iPod in the form of a wristwatch. It has a folding display, which when closed becomes a digital clock. Also standard iPod dial-navigation and a pair of wireless headphones included. By Isamu Sanada.



Miscellaneous Apple Concept Gadgets



Apple iRing

With this iRing, you can control your iPod via Bluetooth. You can control playback and volume on any of your Apple media devices using touch strips. Built-in OLED-display in the form of an apple displays the status. Posted by Gandalf Victor Soto.




iVault

iVault attracts an affordable price of about $ 499. The concept will store hundreds and thousands of songs, videos and images. You can synchronize it with a laptop or desktop computer like a Time Capsule using a wireless connection.



iCal Watch

Wonder concept in the form of hours. It syncs with iCal via Bluetooth, so as not to miss a single meeting. In addition, a watch with a touch screen - which gives space for ideas of use.



iSticks

iStick is compatible with all Apple products. Well, by itself, better and more reliable acoustics from Harman Kardon.



iLight

Flashlight has a touchscreen control panel. Once poked - turned on, the second - turned off. Swipe your fingers forward and backwards and adjust the light level, and double-tapping blocks the touchscreen. Miyoshimasato fought against darkness.
