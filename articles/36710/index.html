Idea for a new startup

Hello! In the course of the latest military-political events, I again thought about how difficult it is to filter really high-quality information from the stream of informational lies. In this regard, the idea was born, inspired by campaigns on the links that Irina Serbina published in her blog . I got to the pages of Expert, where I found more or less real information (at least, very close to the truth in my opinion).

In general, the idea is as follows. We need a recommendatory network, which would allow to distinguish among the mass of publications the best quality (read - true) materials. It is built on personal dating. For example, I am a journalist / blogger, I know another journalist / blogger well and I can say with confidence that he writes not by order and by request. Ie, to hear the truth from his mouth is much more likely than, for example, from the mouth of another journalist / blogger who has established himself with not the best side. Thus, thanks to the recommendatory network, we obtain a list of the most reliable sources based on the honesty of the authors who write there. As a side effect, there is an incentive for the writing community to create the most honest materials so as not to lose its status in this recommendation network. To indicate the status, you can use the rating system.

I understand that even in honest publications it may not be true because of the juggling of facts in official sources, but here you have to find a middle ground and separate the wheat from the chaff based on the publications of other trusted authors. In any case, such a referral network can help get rid of 80-90% of deliberately false or biased scribble.
