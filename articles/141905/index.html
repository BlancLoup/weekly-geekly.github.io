Webinar: How to make presentations, or why not everything takes off?



Continuing the tradition of holding regular webinars on technical and near-technical topics, we want to remind you that tomorrow there will be another webinar dedicated to the preparation and creation of presentations.

As part of the webinar, Konstantin Kichinsky (this is me), an expert from the Microsoft Strategic Technologies Department, will share his experience in planning and preparing presentations. In the course of the report, the issues of choosing the appropriate presentation format, elaboration of its structure and overall narrative composition will be considered. Special attention will be paid to the tasks of visual design and subtleties that are specific to technical presentations on the development.
