Adobe offers to solve the space quest

On October 31, 2398, the IXS FarStar spacecraft was supposed to enter Earth's atmosphere, but at 16:47 GMT, the ship's commander Nova Ryder reported that pilot Meledi Harbinger had become ill with an unknown disease. It is necessary to discover the cause of the pilot's illness and cure it.

Such is the start of the #PsMystery quest from Adobe, which the company timed for Halloween. The player, during the week starting from October 26, will need to follow the blog posts . It will be published daily tips, which are Photoshop files with some images, studying which is required to answer quest questions. The results of their research will need to be shared on the official Photoshop page on Facebook.
