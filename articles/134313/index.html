HP will sell Touchpad at $ 99 December 11 on eBay



It seems that lovers of acquiring good things at a not very high price have every chance to do so. HP has announced it will sell its TouchPads with webOS on eBay on December 11th. Both 16 GB and 32 GB models will be on sale. Their cost is 99 and 149 dollars, respectively. One can only imagine what kind of excitement will arise at the ‚Äúrelease‚Äù of a batch of these tablets.

Yes, the important point is that sales will begin at 6 pm (time is not Moscow, of course).
