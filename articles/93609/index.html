Launching Twitter Business Center - Monetizing High

The company " Twitter " confirmed that testing of new services focused on business accounts has already begun. One of them, known as the Twitter Business Center , has already been successfully launched and is being tested by a limited number of selected companies.

As far as is known, the service has been in beta test mode since December of last year. To test the service invited single accounts, the responsibility of the owners of which include the preparation of reports on the subject of testing. Naturally, the service will become public as soon as the results and reviews satisfy the company.

Several business users received an e-mail with a notification about a certain " Twitter Toolkit ". To start testing it, you are invited to go to your account settings, then enter a new section under the tab " Business ", where you need to provide the company with information about the company. This information will be required to confirm the status of a business account.

Unlike existing accounts - as soon as a new business account is activated through a link in an activation letter - it automatically receives confirmed status and the owner can immediately begin setting up his business functions. Users of business accounts have the opportunity to provide contact information, as well as information about the size of the company.

Confirmed business accounts receive a logo that proves their truth ( like celebrities or major players - note the author of the translation). The benefits of verification are obvious - the trust of followers and visitors, the impossibility of phishing.

Confirmed accounts in the business center have only four tabs:
  • Overview - general information about a business account
  • Business Info - here you can change the information about the company, which was entered in the settings when you activate a business account
  • Verification - account verification status information
  • Contributors - members of a business account, user group
