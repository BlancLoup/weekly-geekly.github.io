Lenovo has updated the Yoga line with two new transformers with 11- and 14-inch displays with a resolution of 1920x1080. The maximum amount of RAM - 8 GB, SSD - up to 256 GB.

The older model is equipped with the sixth generation Intel Core i7 - the first laptops on this processor appeared in the fall of 2015 and were presented at the IFA in Berlin. The device weighs 1.65 kilograms, body thickness - 17.3 mm. Intel Graphics is responsible for the graphics.

The version with the 11-inch display in the maximum configuration comes with the Intel Core M5, weighs 1.1 kg and has a body thickness of 14.9 mm. Responsible for the video graphics processor NVIDIA GeForce.

Laptops are running Windows 10. They will be available in May 2016 at a price of 50,000 rubles.
