Digest of IT events for July

Vacation season is not an obstacle to self-development. Programmers gradually move from incubators and offices to tents and stands in the open air, but persistently continue to discuss professional topics. We publish another list of events for the IT community throughout Russia for those who have not yet decided on plans for July.


User Requirements

When: July 1
Where: St. Petersburg, Krasnogvardeysky per., 23, letter E, office of SC ‚ÄúSCOUT‚Äù
Participation conditions: from 1 500 rub.

The dream of most programmers is to forget about the existence of customers altogether, but alas, their role in shaping system requirements is too great for such an idyll. This training is designed for those who have accepted this reality and want to learn how to translate what customers say into a more familiar, technical language for productive and painless communication. The presenters promise to reveal such topics as the differences between the requirements and expectations of users, the classification of requirements by user roles, the specifics of the statement of requirements in the form of use cases for coordination with the customer and the team and the use of illustrative materials in this difficult task.

Angelhack

When: July 1 - 2
Where: Moscow, Russia, st. Krasnoproletarskaya, 4, Beeline Headquarters

PG Day Russia

When: July 5 - 7
Where: St. Petersburg, st. Starting, 6a, Crowne Plaza Airport
Conditions of participation: 16 200 rubles.

The annual conference on PostgreSQL this time pleases the community with its breadth of topics. The sections will be divided into five areas: PostgreSQL, commercial MySQL / NoSQL databases, system administration and data storage, databases and computer science, Greenplum Day Keynote. About 50 speakers have been invited to speak, among them are experts in computer science and world-class data storage technologies. In addition, participants will have the opportunity to attend specialized workshops with various topics (PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle, MS SQL Server, popular NoSQL solutions), which will be devoted to the first day of the conference.

Intellectual property protection of an IT company. Russia or offshore?

When: July 12
Where: Moscow, st. Antonov-Ovseenko, 15, p. 2
Conditions of participation: 2000 - 5000 rubles.

Legal security is a problem that is worse than information security. The organizers are calling on business owners and startups not to regret a few hours in order to find out in detail what nonetheless such intellectual property is and what the Russian legislation says about it. The focus, of course, will be on those actions that should be taken to avoid complications. In particular: what to consider when concluding contracts with employees, when to register a trademark, how to protect a logo and other intangible assets, according to which scheme to cooperate with foreign companies. The speakers are professional lawyers who are familiar with the problems firsthand.
