Sony Ericsson has announced a new generation of Xperia mini

Today in London, Sony Ericsson has introduced a new generation of compact smartphones Xperia mini and Xperia mini pro to the world. It is expected that the model will be available in the third quarter of this year.



The last generation of mini has gained popularity due to the fact that they provided the functionality of full-fledged smartphones in a very compact package. New "minks" adhere to the same concept, only now at the modern level:
  • 1GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon processor
  • Android Gingerbread 2.3
  • and other features, including multitouch, Mobile BRAVIA Engine‚Äôs best-in-class imaging technology, and HD 720p video recording.

Camera - 5 MP with autofocus, the function of "catching" the moment of a smile and a full gentlemanly set of options (8x digital zoom, autofocus, face detection, flash, geo-tags, image stabilization and red-eye removal).



A nice bonus: the screen diagonal increased from 2.6 "(which was still not enough) to 3", and the resolution increased to 320 x 480 against 240 x 320 pixels earlier. So now this is really a full-fledged, only compact, smartphone.

Dimensions at the same time increased insignificantly: 88x52x16 mm for mini and 92x53x18 mm for mini pro versus 83x50x16 mm and 90x52x17 mm for old mini and mini pro, respectively. Weight - 94 and 136 grams.
