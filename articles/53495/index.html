Hewlett, Packard, California!

An endless stream of events dedicated to the glorious 40th anniversary of HP work in Russia continues. On the website hp40.ru , which is entirely devoted to our anniversary, we launched contests with interesting prizes. And the main one is the search for the doubles of HP founders William Hewlett and David Packard.


Look at their faces in these photos ... If you see one of them every day in the mirror, then you have the chance to win our main prize without any effort - a trip to sunny California. To do this, you just need to upload your photo portrait to the page of the twins contest on hp40.ru and ... call all your friends to vote for you on the site!

Two voting leaders, most similar to Bill and Dave and selected by a commission of HP employees in Russia, will first receive an invitation from us to one of the September events, where we surround them with honor and glory. And then visit all the places that have grown around the famous Garage in the vicinity of San Francisco. In this garage, now recognized as the base of the Silicon Valley, Hewlett and Packard started their business in 1939. And look at what it all poured out ..!

In addition, the site holds weekly quizzes on the knowledge of history. The history of HP, in fact, has long been part of the history of the whole world. Therefore, a little bit of erudition and quickness, and you will become the owner of the last squeak of fashion - photo books, this return of old family photo albums to our digital age. The certificate giving the right to order and receive it free of charge will be given every Monday to the first person who answered correctly the question of the week.

We are waiting for you on the site hp40.ru !
