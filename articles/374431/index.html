Hyperloop plans to build the first branch in Abu Dhabi in 2019



Hyperloop Transportation Technologies announced the signing of a contract that allows starting work on laying the first line of high-speed transport as early as 2019. Hyperloop TT will work with a partner, Aldar Properties PJSC, in Abu Dhabi. The first line will be very short and close to the border between Abu Dhabi and Dubai. If all goes well, then Hyperloop will develop and grow, spreading like a cobweb across the UAE.

The length of the first tunnel will be only about ten kilometers. Next, a line of high-speed transport will be laid in the direction of Al-Maktoum International Airport and even further - to the Dubai Expo (the venue of the World Exhibition in 2020). So far, the exact configuration of the path is unknown, but it can be assumed that the Hyperloop will run parallel to the E311 or E611 highways.



By the way, Aldar Properties PJSC - the developer. The company Hyperloop TT has previously entered into contracts with developers. One of them was signed by a participant in the implementation of the eco-city Quay Valley, which is located between Los Angeles and San Francisco. This city will get its own highway Hyperloop, which will be used by residents of the village. But if the Arabs invest a significant amount of money in the project, they can be the first to create Hyperloop.

True, Abu Dhabi is not the only location where high-speed transport will soon appear. Already, Hyperloop Transportation Technologies is building in France, and not just one branch, but two at once. The first elements of the tunnels arrived at the test site in Toulouse. The first section of the network will not be too big - just 320 meters. And it is clear that no one will roll passengers on it. This is still an experiment that should show how quickly a safe and functional tunnel can be built.

As soon as the first tests are carried out, and they show the reliability of the entire system, the length will be increased to a kilometer.
