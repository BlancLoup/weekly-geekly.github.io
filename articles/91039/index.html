In early April, there were significant changes in the payment system.
The first and most important change: the faceless Personal Cabinet has become a QIWI Wallet , the site address has changed, now it is: https://w.qiwi.ru/

There have been other significant changes. I will dwell a little more on some of them.

Officially launched transfers between users

All you need to know to transfer is to know the recipient's Russian cell number:

Transfers go through the bank, as only banks can send money on the instructions of one individual to another individual. Violation of this principle threatens to be accused of illegal banking activities and serious consequences - up to the closure of the enterprise.

Statistics accumulated over the week already allows us to draw some conclusions. The following categories of our users actively use this function:
  • Freelancers accept payment for work
  • Various services are paid for: from Internet access to buying game currency
  • Debts are returned and private loans are repaid.
  • Money is transferred to friends and relatives for expenses.
Commission - 1%. Translations are online . The size of the commission may seem high when compared with the poison (0.5%) and Webmoney (traditional 0.8%). However, the convenience and ease of translation justify this unit. In addition, most of the commission goes to the bank.
