Moto Hint: Motorola's Smart Portable Headset



Google Motorola, in addition to the widely replicated new products, like the Moto 360, has released another device, and very remarkable. We are talking about the Moto Hint smart wireless headset with which you can control the connected smartphone.

The headset itself is really very small, but its possibilities are vast. The functionality of the device is revealed after the owner connects the earphone to the smartphone, via Bluetooth, and inserts it into the ear. The headset automatically turns on (it is equipped with an infrared sensor), and begins to "listen" to the user, as well as receive incoming calls.


Moto Hint Control


There are no function buttons, only the touch pad of the device is used. As already mentioned, the earpiece turns on immediately after the owner inserts it into the ear, and turns off after the Moto Hint is removed.



It is worth noting that the developers have provided the possibility of automatically forwarding the conversation from the headset (if removed) to the smartphone, in order to avoid interrupting the conversation. If the user listened to music (although listening to music with one earphone is doubtfully a pleasure), playback will be paused.

Smartphone control with Moto Hint


This is what concerns the control of the headset itself. But, as mentioned above, with the help of this headset, you can control the smartphone itself. At the same time, working with a smartphone begins with the phrase ‚ÄúOK, Moto‚Äù (the analogue immediately comes to mind, right?).

After this phrase, you can begin work - for example, dictate a message or ask the phone to call a specific person. In general, there is no particular novelty in this kind of control, because almost any modern phone can perform voice commands.

But the developers claim that their system is the most-most: the most sensitive, the most accurate, etc. Well, let's take a word.

Compatibility


The headset Moto Hint is compatible with any device, and not just with Motorola phones, this is good news. When connected to the iPhone, Siri will already help the user.

Design


Actually, everything can be clearly seen in the photo - the drop-shaped form of the device is not bad for inserting into the human ear, and the device is unlikely to interfere.



For fans of non-standard things, the company releases six types of Moto Hint at once - with wooden, leather and metal inserts. Stylish and beautiful, yes.



The headset is supplied in a case, which is also a charging station.



No wired charging connection is required, a pad is provided in the case, which is combined with the earphone's contacts, after which the battery is charged.
