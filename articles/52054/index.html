Startup Crash Test v 2.0

On Friday, the second Startup Crash Test will be held in Kiev. The event was invented by a group of activists to help young startups. Helping is done by sorting out the strategy and business model of a startup by experts and viewers, which does not always go without a trace for the psyche of entrepreneurs.
Two startups, which will be presented at this time, are working in the social networking segment. One project is focused globally, the second - on the Internet.
This time, presentations and discussion of startups will be preceded by a round table on the topic of M & A, which will be attended by companies that last year actively engaged in shopping on the Ukrainian Internet market.
Anyone who is interested - welcome! More about the last event and the program for Friday - you can see here.

UPD1: Participation is free
UPD2: Meeting place by letter after registration
