iPhone application LinguaLeo published in the AppStore


The long-awaited release of the LinguaLeo iPhone application happened. Now happy owners of iPhones will be able to appreciate the convenience of mobile English language improvement by installing the application on the official page in the AppStore . Attention to Android owners: the LinguaLeo application for Android will be released in the next 2-3 months, please, do not switch to the iPhone for now!

The fact that the application can and how to use it, so that it is pleasant and useful to develop skills in English, read on! ..


How to use the iPhone version of LinguaLeo?

If you already have an account on LinguaLeo - install the application from the AppStore and log in with your login and password. New users can create an account right inside the application. After logging in, your personal dictionary is synchronized with the site LinguaLeo.ru, so you can not worry about losing any word. If you do not have a LinguaLeo account yet, create it directly in the application:



Leo will meet you and help. Leo will offer new users to add 10 first words from thematic glossaries to their personal dictionary:



The words that you mark ‚Äúto study‚Äù will fall into your personal dictionary. In it you can see the word, its translations, transcriptions and visual associations. You can also add your own text association to the word. All words added to the personal dictionary will go to workouts. After adding the first 10 words to your personal dictionary, Leo will offer you a workout.



At your disposal are 4 types of interactive workouts for fixing added words: word-translation, translation-word, word constructor and listening:



Get training on fixing words everywhere: whether it is public transport or a boring lecture at the university, a long lunch at work or an endless queue to the doctor. You can learn new English words at any time!

Based on the results of the training sessions, experience points are awarded. If the training is completed correctly, 10 experience points are awarded. And the daily satiety of Leo is growing!



All the way, Leo will give optimal recommendations, depending on your activity, the number of words in your personal dictionary and in training.

How will the application evolve?

This is the first version of the iPhone application . It was developed by the magic ink team in close collaboration with LinguaLeo. Please write your feedback, suggestions and ideas for further development in the comments, to the address iphone@lingualeo.ru or to a special group of iPhone applications on Facebook . We will surely take everything into account!

Learn English on LinguaLeo.ru in your iPhone and have fun!
