Results of SAP Coder 2017: smart refrigerator, recruiter virtual assistant and much more


Recall, SAP Koder 2017 is our first CIS online developer competition on the SAP open cloud platform. Among the top ten winners there were many interesting projects, including ambulance dispatching systems, collection management for an ATM network and optimization of retail prices. And the jury was so interested in the monitoring service for shipping goods that the phrase ‚Äúalmost bought‚Äù sounded in the hall.

The competition was held from April 3 to June 28, the participants had about a month to create a prototype business application based on the SAP Cloud Platform cloud platform. You could offer your task or take one of the three proposed areas - mass recruitment, sales promotion or management of refrigeration equipment.

In the final, for the victory in each of the two nominations (for independent developers and for development companies), 3 participants competed. The event took place on the territory of the Lexus Dome, located in Moscow City.

Rolf Schumann, our vice president of platform and innovation, came to the finals. He not only headed the jury, but also spoke about the changes taking place in our daily life thanks to high technology. So, Rolf came to the final in "smart" sneakers. Using a mobile application, he was able to find out the distance, the number of steps and other parameters of his movements. The Internet of Things is closer than we think.


Mr. Schumann also demonstrated the SAP Sports One platform - a solution based on the SAP Cloud Platform that allows you to effectively manage sports teams and players. He showed how, based on an array of data on penalties, the system allows to predict the goal sector, which will be hit, and its result depending on external conditions. And when everyone practically believed in the power of predictive analysis, he demonstrated in practice that unpredictability is still a strong side of humanity.


Natalia Parmenova, Executive Director of SAP CIS, spoke about the work on launching the SAP Cloud Platform in the Russian data center. The launch is scheduled for autumn 2017.

The company has created for its customers a convenient application for monitoring the status and accounting of surgical instruments, the formation of sets for operations.


Participants listened with interest about new technologies, but the tension did not subside - everyone was waiting for a denouement.
