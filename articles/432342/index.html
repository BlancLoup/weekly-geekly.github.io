Camunda BPM Meetup Raiffeisenbank UPD Broadcast Mitap

On December 18, we invite you to the first open meeting of the Camunda BPM , which will be held at the Raiffeisenbank site in Nagatino.

It is very important for us to form communities and share knowledge and experience both within the company and outside. That is why on a regular basis we conduct open meetings in different directions. This time we open the doors and invite you to the first mitap of Camunda BPM. Within the framework of the mitap, we also want to create the Camunda BPM User Group chat, it is much easier to live with the chat, because then there is an opportunity to quickly ask colleagues something or just throw off an interesting article or meme. Want to chat? Then you here

And now about the mitap. The program of the event includes four reports:


Artyom Kuraev, Raiffeisenbank


JUnit process testing

How to organize BPM testing in Camunda using JUnit

Ruslan Gainutdinov, Futurice


Microservice Orchestra with Zeebe and Kafka

Using Zeebe from Camunda and Apache Kafka for organizing microservice workflows in a hybrid API / event-based architecture.

Alexander Trekhlebov, Promsvyazbank


Launch of the Camunda BPM system on the microservice architecture

How to get away from the monolith when building industrial BPM systems. Distribution of processes on microservices, organization of interaction between processes

Sergey Novikov, BPMteam


CMMN. What case management is and how it is implemented in BPM Camunda

What case management is and how to use it in Camunda
