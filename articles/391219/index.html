Olsson block - practical synergy
Olsson block is a hot end with interchangeable nozzles of various diameters. Allows you to quickly change the nozzle. There are 4 nozzles in total - 0.13 mm, 0.4 mm, 0.6 mm and 0.8 mm. By changing the nozzles you can achieve the best print result for you. The company Ultimaker officially placed Olsson block in the list of its accessories.
