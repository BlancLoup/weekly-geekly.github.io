Some chips for English learners

1. Anonymous chats : omegle.com, chatroulette.com
2. Help Sites : kwiz.me, kimir.org, englishtips.org
3. Podcasting : eslpod.com, effortlessenglish.com, npr.podcast.com, businessenglishpod.com,
4. Download movies / series, then subtitles to them. You can view with slowdown in the VLC player to better digest. I still recommend the comedian of the show, for example, Important Things with Demetri Martin - you will not regret.
5. In Skype, we are looking for some kind of Indians with SkypeMe status and are not sinking.
6. When reading a book or watching a movie, it would be nice to prepare this way: take the text, set the word length and frequency of use in the text and run, for example, this script (PHP): pastebin.com/m7672c2a9
As a result, we have a digest of words, most of which you already know, but the rest should be learned. And when you watch a movie or read a book you will understand this word automatically.
7. Pronunciation of words - we put the Russian-> English mode in Google.translate and write an English word in the text field - it will appear without translation along with the flash drive that says the word. Also forvo.com, howjsay.com
8. Social response services :
answers.yahoo.com, vark.com
9. Social networks for learning the language :
livemocha.com, lingq.com, italki.com
