PlayStation Phone fell into the hands of Chinese bloggers



In principle, no one doubts the reality of the imminent appearance of the PlayStation Phone, which is a hybrid of a portable game console and communicator, on the stores. But less than a month ago, photos of the PlayStation Phone were called fake, fake. Various photos of the device appeared on various resources. Now the device has fallen into the hands of the authors of the site IT168. Those did not fail to clarify the configuration of the device.

In general, the PlayStation Phone is really equipped with a Snapdragon QSD8255 gigahertz processor, plus the Qualcomm Adreno 205 graphics subsystem. By the way, the same processor is installed on HTC Desire HD. By the way, in early December, the device performance was tested using the Neocore application, then the average was 24.4 fps. Perhaps the developers turned something up there, because the Chinese showed the same test 59.1 fps. Agree, quite an interesting achievement.

In addition, the device configuration includes a 1500 mAh rechargeable battery, 4-inch display, 5-megapixel camera capable of recording video with 720p quality. The amount of internal memory is 512 MB, the amount of RAM - the same 512 MB. There is a slot for memory cards format microSD, a slot for SIM, port micro USB, plus an additional microphone to reduce the level of third-party noise.

The intriguing point is that the PlayStation Pocket application, which launches games, still does not launch anything. In other words, there are no games in the memory of the device yet, and experts do not presume to say which games will be launched on this device. Will it be games with PS1? Either these are games with PSP, or in general games of a special format ... The fact is that PSP Go has a special architecture that is incompatible with the architecture of the PlayStation Phone. The PSP Go has a 333 MHz processor, 64 MB RAM, and a 3.8-inch display with a resolution of 480 x 272 pixels.

Whatever it was, but the device is really interesting, it remains only to wait for the official statement from the manufacturer + demonstration of games that will work on the device. Well, we are waiting.
