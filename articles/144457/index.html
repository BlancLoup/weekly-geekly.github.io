YOTA has included LTE network in Samara

In Samara, the main network construction stage has been completed, 150 base stations have been installed.
This amount is sufficient to provide coverage for Samara and parts of the suburbs, including Novokuybyshevsk. Access speed is up to 20 Mbps. Internet for all YOTA users is unlimited.


The network operates in the range of 2.5-2.7 GHz. This means that devices released for the Russian and European markets will be able to connect to LTE in Samara. Devices released for other markets, such as the current version of the iPad 3, will not work in our frequency range.

From May 23, 2012, the YOTA Modem became available at YOTA points of sale and in partner networks at a price of 2,900 rubles. In a couple of months, the Mobile Router and the Internet Center will go on sale.

YOTA Ready devices will be on sale: ZyXEL Keenetic and ZyXEL Keenetic Giga routers. To access the LTE network via a router via Wi-Fi, you just need to connect the ‚ÄúYOTA Modem‚Äù to the routers.

Each user can choose the optimal connection conditions and change them at any time at no additional charge, depending on the needs. The price depends on the connection speed - from 250 to 900 rubles per month. For users who have connected YOTA immediately for a year, the month of the Internet 4G at the maximum speed will cost 750 rubles.

For those who do not use the Internet constantly, YOTA offers access at no additional charge at speeds up to 128 Kbps. If necessary, the maximum speed can be turned on for 24 hours for 100 rubles.

Every person who buys a device to access the YOTA network can test 4G internet for free during the week. If the quality of the service does not suit the user, YOTA will return the money for the device.
