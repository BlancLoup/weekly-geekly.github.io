How following the rules get banned Vkontakte

In this article, I want to warn those people who still have profiles in the above-mentioned social network and who, like me, value my rights and time.

It all started with the fact that, being in the first courses of the institute, my friend and I did not know what to do and decided to make some application for Vkontakte. At that time, the first applications appeared and when we made our New Year game in November and invited a couple of friends - we quickly received the first 10 thousand installations and in a month grew to 300 thousand without any efforts.

So my friend and I had a new hobby. It is not known why, but we spent all our free time on it, despite the fact that the profit from all this was minimal.


This year it all happened again. There is a ready application, there are a couple of hours a day and you need to add new features and launch it again. Not for the money - for the usual pleasure. So did. This time there was a lot more profit and fewer people. But still, 250,000 installations and 40 thousand hosts per day we had.

Everyone knows about how much the developer will receive, withdrawing a small number of votes from the network, and for those who do not know, 2.4-2.6 rubles for one vote.

And so, having 800 votes in hand, it was decided not to withdraw these funds, but to spend them here. Without thinking twice, we announced a small contest on our blog, according to the rules of which people who send us interesting ideas for social applications get from 50 to 100 votes sent by Vkontakte using the official method. In a few days, we received more than 40 letters. Having selected the most interesting among them, we contacted the authors and each day transferred them the promised votes during the week, since the translation of more than 100 votes is prohibited.

This is where the most interesting thing begins and you will never believe what happened :) Our account was simply banned! Yes, yes, that's right.

I think the moderators telepathically catch the sale of votes and immediately take action without explanation. So what? Remember where we live.



If 90% of the proceeds go somewhere, then definitely not for development. Development is when there is at least those. support.
