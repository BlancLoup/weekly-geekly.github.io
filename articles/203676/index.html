Welcome to ALM Summit Russia 2014


If you are a software project manager, a leading developer, a person who is interested in software engineering, be sure to register at Digital October in ALM Summit Russia , which will be held on February 6, 2014 in Moscow. At this conference, you will be able to gain new knowledge and information on how to improve your software development processes.


You will also learn about the best industrial practices that affect existing difficulties, and how Microsoft's solutions in the field of ALM allow you to solve problems related to the automation of the software development cycle. You will have the opportunity to talk directly with Microsoft employees who are developing Visual Studio products, engineers from other Microsoft departments using these tools, and industrial experts from other companies. By visiting ALM Summit Russia, you will become familiar with new tools that make it easier to create software and support processes, and therefore prepare for making decisions on choosing such a toolkit.

This year, the ALM Summit will consist of a plenary session and four tracks:
  • ALM track in which issues of the entire life cycle of software products will be covered.
  • Quality Assurance track in which the issues of ensuring the quality of software products throughout the entire life cycle, including operation, will be covered.
  • Track Agile in which it will be told about the process approaches designed to optimize the work of the team and get the desired results.
  • Affiliate track in which will be told about the experience of operating various solutions in the field of ALM

Who conducts reports


Managers, architects and managers from Microsoft, as well as recognized industrial experts in the field of ALM, will hold reports covering all aspects of ensuring the life cycle of software products at all its stages. You will have the opportunity to ask questions and find out details on many issues related to the implementation of ALM in organizations, as well as the best practices for such an implementation.
This year, a special guest from Microsoft - Brian Keller will arrive at the ALM Summit:

Brian Keller is a technical evangelist for Visual Studio Application Lifecycle Management products. Brian has a very deep theoretical knowledge of the organization of ALM processes, as well as practical experience in creating ALM infrastructure solutions. He is the author of several books on this topic as well as virtual machines and laboratory work .

Industrial experts will talk about the most advanced experience in the implementation of ALM tools that will interest a wide audience of practicing managers, leading developers, and ALM consultants.
The conference program committee includes:

Askhat Urazbaev - The main ideologist and specialist in Scrum of ScrumTrack . Most of all he likes to speak at conferences and give clever advice. Askhat has long been engaged in flexible methodologies, worked as a manager in Agile-projects, coached and coached many teams in Russia and the CIS. Prior to Agile, he led the creation and implementation of the Luxoft Standard Process. And before that, he worked in different companies, where he passed the glorious battle path from a junior developer to a project manager.


Vladislav Orlikov - General Director, Founder of Testing Laboratory (SQALab) , Moscow, Russia.
Master of Business Administration in IT Management.
Organizer and co-organizer of a number of international IT conferences, including SQA Days , Application Developer Days , Analyst Days , SPM Conf .

Who recommended the conference


Practicing software project managers, ALM practitioners, leading developers. The ALM Summit Russia annual conference will be held in Russia for the third time, and this is a unique opportunity not only to talk with Microsoft industry experts, but also to find like-minded people interested in ALM issues.

See you at the conference!
