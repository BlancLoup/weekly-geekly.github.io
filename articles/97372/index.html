Verizon and Motorola introduced the new Droid

Verizon Wireless and Motorola introduced a new smartphone, the Droid X. It is manufactured by Motorola and runs on Android 2.1.

Droid X has a processor with a clock speed of 1 gigahertz and is equipped with a 4.3-inch display.

The device will run on Android 2.1, but by the end of the summer, users will be able to upgrade to Android 2.2. Also, users can install Adobe Flash Player 10.1.

Droid X will go on sale July 15 at verizonwireless.com and in Verizon Wireless Communications stores. Taking into account the discount of $ 100 for a two-year contract, its cost will be $ 199.99.

UPD: an infographic is published on Mashable comparing the characteristics of the Droid X and the iPhone 4.
