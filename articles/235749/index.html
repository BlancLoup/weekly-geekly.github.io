Smart watches from Lively service: personal rescue service that is always in touch



Not so long ago, the Medgadgets blog has already published news about the interesting Lively system, which lets you know if your grandmother has health problems. The system is installed, for example, in the home of an elderly person (several sensors are installed), and at first it adapts to the personal mode of this person.

Sensors (no cameras, privacy remains private) can track how many times a person has left the house, how often he took medicine or opened the door of the refrigerator. And if the system notices a deviation from the usual mode, then it sends a message to the relatives / personal rescue service, in which quality the Lively service works. If a person really has problems (heart attack, fell and can not rise), all this is solved very quickly.

Now the service has released and an addition to the system: a smart watch with a panic button.

Watch features



Security

First of all, the watch serves to communicate with the personal security service from Lively: just press the button, hold it for a while, and a call to the Lively service will be initiated.

The button is pretty well protected from accidental clicks, but anything can happen. In order to make sure of the reality of the call, the manager tries to contact the owner of the watch by phone, and if it turns out, and the owner requires assistance, the manager stays in touch until the arrival of the emergency room.

If it is impossible to contact by phone, the connection goes with the relatives, and the ambulance is sent again.



Smart features

Already in the second place, the watch from Lively works like a smart watch. Moreover, most of their functions are combined with the functions of the main system.



Thus, the display shows, for example, the mention of the need to take a certain medicine. Of course, displays information about the current time, date, battery charge. At night, the display is illuminated by LEDs, so that all the information is clearly visible.

The watch case is waterproof, so they can not be removed while taking a shower.



In addition, the calculation of the steps traveled per day and the like information is carried out.

Soon it is planned to add the function of determining the fall of a person, so that help can only go on this signal, without making calls (although it is not entirely clear how they will determine whether the watch itself fell or its owner).



Price



The system itself costs $ 39, hours - 49.95 (they can be bought separately), +24 dollars must be paid monthly as a monthly fee for the service Lively.
