As the store grows, the store owner faces a serious task - how to automate the process of synchronization of goods with the warehouse and order processing by managers, because you will agree that in manual mode all these processes begin to seriously hinder the growth of the company. A good solution would be to transfer all logic to 1C products. The question of recommendations for choosing an accounting system, we have already considered in his article .
