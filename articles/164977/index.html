Read UPD7, Adobe posted an official response on its blog.

Share unprecedented generosity from Adobe. They decided to distribute, though outdated, but still full Creative Suite of the second version. Versions are available for both Mac (PowerPC) and Windows.

Included:
  • Creative Suite 2
  • Acrobat 3D 1.0 for Windows
  • Acrobat Standard 7.0
  • Acrobat Pro 8.0

  • GoLive CS2
