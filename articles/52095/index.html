Solar Phone

LG has announced the creation of a "green" mobile phone with built-in solar battery. The prototype of the device will be demonstrated at the international congress MWC 2009 in Barcelona. In the meantime, you can only get acquainted with not too detailed information "on the subject."


So, LG claims that the phone case is made from recycled materials. Yes, and the device will be sold in environmentally friendly packaging. Even the user manual will be printed with soy ink on recycled paper.

But the main thing, of course, is the solar battery. The manufacturer claims that 10 minutes of being in the sun will last for 3 minutes.

What is the name of the "green" LG mobile phone, how much it will cost and when it will be on sale is also not known yet.

via electronista

Ps. LG, by the way, is not the first company this year to introduce an environmentally friendly phone. Earlier, Samsung was noted for its Blue Earth, which is also capable of charging from solar energy. About him almost nothing is known. In addition, it will be available in the second half of 2009.
