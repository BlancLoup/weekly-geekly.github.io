Microsoft Edge on Chromium officially available for open testing.

Yesterday we began a public testing of the Edge browser on Chromium. For now there are 2 preview versions available for Windows 10: Dev and Canary. Dev builds contain the best additions of the past week. They have been tested by the Microsoft Edge team and are generally more stable than Canary. Canary is updated every day and it is there that you can follow the daily progress in the development of the browser.

Download, try and leave feedback here . Waiting for your feedback!



By the way, we have the @MSEdgeDev community on twitter. There you can find out all the latest news and be involved in the development of the browser. Join now!
