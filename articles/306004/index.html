How I Received a Code Signing Certificate from Comodo

I decided to share my difficult experience of obtaining a certificate for signing applications on Windows.

Once I wondered about this issue in order to increase the confidence of potential customers in their programs. I rummaged through the Internet and found Comodo, a more or less affordable company, whose certificates can be bought for only $ 75 a year. I read casually the pitfalls that it is necessary to submit an application and receive a certificate on the same computer, on the same browser, and so on, connected with the installation and use of the certificate. For some reason, I forgot about reading the process itself.

The price of $ 75 I found on Tucows , while on the Russian and Kazakhstan sites offered about $ 110 a year. As always, without having read what is written in small print, he cheerfully pressed the buy button, entered the card details and paid for the order. After that, I receive a letter in the mail that the purchase is made, we congratulate you, you are so well done, but we will not refund you the money. That is, if anything, then you will remain without your blood. Well, well, I thought that this could happen.

On the same day, I received a letter from Comodo - the order was confirmed, the process of checking my company began, which may take two working days. I sent a scanned passport, ID and certificate of IP. To which I receive an answer (again, the next day) - you have an individual order, so you must pass the so-called face-to-face check.
