Smartphone, laptop and tablet controls the light in the apartment

Smartphones have grown firmly in our hands. We sleep, eat, and, to be honest, we go to the toilet with our favorite gadgets. Well, what about without a favorite tweeter? How can you skip an important Facebook post? And almost everyone in the house has a Wi-Fi access point, to which all home smartphones and tablets are connected.
How to turn this bad habit into a useful one? Do not just waste your time on messengers, but return to reality a little? To adapt the smartphone to perform the necessary functions, and not just consume content and play. How?
It is known that today's smartphones can control various household appliances through applications, can be a universal remote control for anything from TV to microwave.
A small lyrical digression.
For nine years I managed the electricity of the capital of Belarus, when I was on a shift in the control room of electrical networks. Could remotely enable and disable Minsk housing estates, factories, substations, electric transport, pumping stations and other objects. Subsequently, my line of business changed, and I began to engage in information work in the energy system, but the craving for electricity management remained.

If you think about it, the smartphone can control and electricity, at least in his apartment. It is enough to purchase a special Ethernet gateway , connect it to the router via Wi-Fi, configure its presence on the network, install the application on the smartphone - and here it is! You are the energy master of your home power grid and control electricity remotely!
