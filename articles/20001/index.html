Mobile Egg Gadget


Designer Michiel van der Kley created one of the most unusual mobile offices in the form of a ball, more precisely in the form of an egg. The gadget moves easily in any direction thanks to inconspicuous wheels. When not in use, it does not take up much space and can be used as an interior item. Disassembled wheels of the device are blocked, so you can count on good "stability" of your workplace. At the bottom of the gadget is a good soft seat, in the upper half there is a stand for a laptop.


In general, this is quite an innovative device that will interest a fairly large part of buyers. Many will like the original design, compactness and mobility of the gadget.
