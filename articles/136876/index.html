KISS principle in action

I will try to describe his positive qualities. There are technologies of time management, in which literally everything is painted to the smallest detail. But how many people can follow them? What should be the warehouse of character for this? Maybe I'm too lazy, but after a day or two I get tired of using them. Few things were better with ToDo lists. You can plan for as long as you wish, arranging the chains of necessary affairs. And what to do, if tomorrow morning there is no desire to do it at all? Where to get the motivation?
Quite differently, I treated simple monosyllabic motivators. A well-known tomato method, time for fun, an hour for fun or just do it. When an idea is expressed in one sentence, it comes better to the soul or to the mind.
Another advantage is that this approach forms the habit of doing useful and important actions. Gradually, the brain gets used to look for outstanding tasks. Immediately after the morning check of mail, an automatic list of important cases is formed.
