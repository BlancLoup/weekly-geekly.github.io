1,000,000 passwords from Yandex mailboxes leaked to the network

Today, on one fairly well-known resource they placed a database of email addresses with passwords from Yandex mailboxes. The base is a text document in which 1 million positions are declared.

I was surprised to find one of my own, and although the password was not of cosmic complexity, certainly not for brutus and dictionaries.

When exactly, and for what reason, this database leaked to the network, remains unknown. In the comments, the people wrote that at least 8 of the first 10 that came across were currently valid.

In general, we change passwords together, while representatives of Yandex are looking for a mole.
