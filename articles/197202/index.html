In bright sunshine more than once there was a feeling that a certain halo arose around the device, a very unusual feeling, I want to tell you. The main working area is located in a slightly raised bath. Inside it is a screen, under it are three touch control buttons (menu, home, back), and on the top: a front camera (1.9 megapixel) and a speaker. All this is covered with a protective glass Asahi Glass Dragontrail, recommended as an excellent alternative to the same Corning Glass.



The side faces of the smartphone are slightly sloping from the front to the back, as a result, the smartphone has the shape of a rounded inverted truncated pyramid and quite successfully, because of this, lies in the hand. Screen.

The display of the smartphone is made using IPS technology. When creating the screen, the air gap is minimized, the borders of the screen can be seen, but only slightly tilting the device. All this, of course, had a positive effect both on the viewing angles and on the richness and brightness of the transmitted image. In the sun, the picture fades, but not much, taking into account the use of mostly light design, there are no problems with reading information under direct light. The screen has a diagonal of 4.3 inches as well as a resolution of 800 by 480 pixels. The last numbers are likely to be surprising, because the high-resolution trend imposes an imprint on the younger models. But I can say that with normal use, there is no shortage of pixels in the smartphone, but this has reduced the load on the battery well. In addition, you can not imagine how nice after the small buttons on FullHD smartphones go to a larger interface. CPU, memory.

The smartphone, despite its simple positioning, has excellent characteristics. The main use is the proven MediaTek MT6589, which operates at 1.2 GHz. The use of 28 nanometer tech-process in the production of single-chip quad-core processor allowed to achieve an excellent ratio of performance / heating / power consumption. MediaTek is our official partner, so this is not only cooperation in terms of sales / purchases, but also work to improve the performance of each smartphone.



The PowerVR SGX544MP is a video chip, a pretty good solution, giving you the opportunity to enjoy the latest games. Launched Real Racing 3, for example, works without problems, with maximum graphics settings (rear-view mirrors), but Asphalt 8 has already shown teeth and excellent FPS can be achieved only with medium settings. Also in the smartphone is installed 1 gigabyte of RAM and 2.5 gigabytes of available internal memory, of course, it can be expanded with a microSD card. Stable work is guaranteed at 32 GB, but our first users point out excellent work when installing the 64 GB option. With these features and individual software optimization work with the support of MediaTek, the smartphone has a smooth and fast interface, especially for its price segment.
