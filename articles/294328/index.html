How we participated in the hackathon



My colleagues and Microsoft are preparing to implement one very interesting project for the educational organization of the city of Moscow. Therefore, we actively study and engage in educational initiatives.

And now, within the framework of the search for new progressive ideas for the educational system of the Russian Federation, on September 5-6, an international IT marathon was held to create innovative educational products called HAKATON EDHACKOUT 2015 . The task of the event is to create a creative concept of the National Open Educational Platform, to try oneself in building the future of our national education. Young and creative were invited to participate.

Today, hackathons no longer belong to hacking, they are just programming marathons. Usually hackathons last from one day to a week.

Since the essence of the event boiled down to brainstorming in the field of online learning, the term hackathon is quite appropriate. And when the whole of Moscow celebrated the day of the city, we worked, created, presented, created, in general, hackathones and got every kind of fan. About twenty-five teams were gathered, mostly from students from various institutes. The first day was devoted to the generation of new ideas in teams, the second - their correct presentation for the jury.

Fourth . Corporate social network is a good platform for hackathon. I remember the story that occurred in the company Grundfos . As part of the development program for talented employees, an event was scheduled well in advance for six months to meet with management. But due to the high cost of the event, the event was canceled and the organizers were asked to find a cheap virtual alternative. As a result, a global event within 48 hours was held in a closed group on the corporate social network Yammer. The teams received instructions, solved tasks, held meetings, meetings with managers, held a question and answer session. In total, the event was attended by employees from 55 countries. As a result, Grundfos executives rated the event as extremely effective and offered to disclose the results for the entire company, which was done.
