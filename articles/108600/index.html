What startups are interesting to strategic investors? On the example of HeadHunter (November 26)

Within the framework of cooperation between StartupPoint and HeadHunter, a thematic Point on Internet recruiting will be held on November 26th, where the best projects in the field of Internet recruiting will be presented.

This Point will be remarkable in that we will try with examples to show which projects and for what reasons strategic investors may or may not be interested.
Top managers of such companies as HeadHunter, Yandex.Rabota, JobList.ru, TALANTING, 100rabot.ru, free-lance.ru, job.ru and others are invited to the event.

The event will include 3 questions and answers for startups, experts and investors, respectively, where we will talk about the work plan of the Glavstart holding with strategic and private investors, the mentoring system and how to send a resume to 100 runet investors.

We found more than 90% of all recruiting projects in runet, but if your project has not yet contacted us, this is your last chance to jump onto the outgoing train and be presented to a strategic investor, HeadHunter. We are waiting for applications from recruiting startups on hh [dog] startuppoint.ru

Recall that this is the second presentation session conducted by StartupPoint for a strategic investor. The first was held at the Yandex office in September of this year, where 6 projects were presented. Read more here.

Registration for the event is required.
