ViewSonic VNB132 - DVD drive optional



Together with the netbook, ViewSonic announced a full-fledged 13.3-inch laptop.

The model is called ViewSonic VNB132, belongs to the budget class and comes with a Core 2 Duo SU7300 with a frequency of 1.3 GHz, 2 GB RAM and a 320 GB hard drive. The display resolution is 1280x800 pixels.

Among other things, we note Windows 7 as an OS, HDMI and D-Sub interfaces, a 7-in-1 card reader and for some reason only two USB ports (a clear puncture).

At the request of the user, the laptop can be equipped with a DVD-drive and cost $ 900 or not be completed and cost $ 800.

On one battery charge, the laptop runs up to 8 hours.
