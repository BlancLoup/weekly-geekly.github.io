New LTE network in Krasnodar

Dear friends,

To launch the new LTE network in Krasnodar, several of our base stations were actually dismantled in order to retrofit equipment from WiMAX to LTE.

Unfortunately, for technical reasons, at some sites it is impossible to simultaneously place the stations of two standards at once.

All users who will not have a network during this period will be refunded the money spent on the Internet from the moment of dismantling the station until the date of switching to LTE, as well as 1700 rubles are credited to the account (the amount required to purchase the Internet for a month).

Compensation will be charged on appeal to the YOTA Contact Center. Contact phones: 8-861-299-29-99, 8-800-700-55-00.
