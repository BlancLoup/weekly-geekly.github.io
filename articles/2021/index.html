Six Apart Launches Vox Platform

The company Six Apart , which owns LiveJournal, announced the launch of the free blogger platform of the new generation Vox , which we described in detail during its testing. This development has absorbed all the best from MovableType and LiveJournal and is a mixture of blogs and social networks.

According to Anil Dash, vice president of the company, the Vox platform is designed to communicate with close people and provides a variety of options and tools for this communication. Each account owner gets the opportunity to create their own multimedia blog and fill it with content: text, photos, audio and video files, and even books from Amazon.com .
