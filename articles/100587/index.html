Velcom announces contest for Android developers

Today velcom operator announced that from Monday it will begin selling Android-smartphones at attractive prices. Initially, users will be offered devices Sony Ericsson Xperia X10 and Xperia X10 mini and Vivaz Pro, a little later - the device HTC.
The cost of the Xperia X10 will be about 1 million 900 thousand Belarusian rubles ($ 638.65), which is lower than the market value.




Let me remind you that Life already has a similar campaign :) Belarus, they offer the Android smartphone Huawei U8230. Its price is 899,000 Belarusian rubles ($ 302.2), taking into account the monthly traffic of 750 MB during the year.

Velcom also announced the launch of an open competition of mobile application developers with the Android operating system.

The following prizes await the winners of the velcom Android Belarus contest:

1st place - 21,000,000 Belarusian rubles ($ 7058.8) (tax included)

2nd place - 15,000,000 Belarusian rubles ($ 5042) (tax included)

3rd place - 9,000,000 Belarusian rubles ($ 3025.2) (tax included)

4th place - 5 smartphones (Sony Ericsson XPERIA X10 mini and HTC Hero)

To participate, you should register on the site android.velcom.by and within a specified period develop an Android application according to the rules of the contest.

The competition starts on August 1, and the results will be announced on November 20.
