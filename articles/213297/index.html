Kegbot: Beer Bottling System for Arduino and Android

Kegbot is a unique technological upgrade for the kegerator, a keg beer filling system. It consists of a flow sensor, an Arduino microcontroller and an Android tablet as a control system.



Turning on the tablet actually opens up opportunities unprecedented for users. The system can control how much beer is drunk and how much is left, who drank it. She can tweet when someone pours a beer, check consumers foam, automatically take their pictures, authorize through NFC.

The authors of the project have already received more than $ 37,000 on Kickstarter with the initial 15,000, while the campaign will last another 4 days, so that you can have time to connect.
