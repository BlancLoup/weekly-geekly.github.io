Conference PM-Labs 2009. July 25, Kiev

Summer Conference for Project Managers in Kiev!

All aspects of project management and project programs, organization of teamwork and building effective project communications.



Colleagues, at the PM Labs 2009 conference , the best practitioners will share their experiences and approaches to the practical application of software development project management techniques.

The conference program presents reports of two main topics: "Project Management" and "Human Factor" .

Reports will be held in two streams, and during breaks over a cup of coffee you can get new acquaintances in the format of a kind of networking session. I have long wanted to get acquainted with people who manage the development of entire offices and make projects, services and software, without which you can‚Äôt imagine modern life? The conference is the time and place to expand the circle of dating!

We invited people who make the most notable and complex projects:
  • Internet portals with a million audience;
  • mail services used by hundreds of thousands of users every day;
  • solutions that allow you to make phone calls every day, watch TV and pay bills.


We will try to talk and discuss not only project management issues, but also related areas where technology intersects with people: for this conference will have two tracks of reports and mandatory flip-chart sessions , where - as our experience of other conferences has shown - serious battles unfold and tied interesting acquaintances.
