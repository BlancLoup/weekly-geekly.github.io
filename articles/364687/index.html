Julian Treasure and the art of listening



All that you did not know about the sound and its influence on us. How to make you listen and hear? All this and more in five performances by Julian Treasure on TED

1. How to speak so that others would like to listen?

Have you ever felt what you say and nobody listens? Julian Trezher to the rescue. In a rewarding performance, the sound expert demonstrates powerful speech tools: from practical vocal exercises to tips, how to speak with empathy. This is a performance that can help the world sound more beautiful.


Original video


2. Shhh! Good health in 8 steps

Julian Treasurer says that our world that is increasing in volume eats away at our mental health and even costs us our lives. He makes a plan of eight steps to soften the acoustic assault, starting with cheap tablet headphones, and restore our connection with sound.


Original video


3. 4 ways that sound affects us

The sound can make both pleasant and unpleasant impression. Julian Treasurer shows 4 ways that sound can affect us. Listen carefully, especially about the shocking truth about the noise in open-plan offices.


Original video


4. 5 ways to listen better

"In our increasingly loud world," says sound expert Julian Treasure, "we lose the ability to listen." In this short, fascinating report Trezher shares 5 ways to reconfigure our ears for conscious listening to the people around us and the world around us.


Original video


5. Why do architects need to use their ears?

Due to poor acoustics, students in the classroom do not hear 50% of what the teachers say. Patients in hospitals do not sleep well due to the constant feeling of stress. Julian Treasure calls for action and asks designers to pay attention to the "invisible architecture" of sound.
