Russian javascript reference

There was an idea to write a Russian-language reference for modern living script. As a platform I chose Sphinx. A couple of days I dig in to assess the labor costs and decide finally.

Actually there are two main goals: up-to-date information on compatibility and convenient for editing and updating the format of source texts of documentation. Side (but also important) goals: the stylistic uniformity of all texts, correct and numerous examples of use, separately highlighted various subtleties and possible rakes.

A preliminary sketch can be found here:

morg.regolit.com/js-ref/core/array.html

Well, the most important goal is to work together on the material. Hosting is, mercurial is already configured. However, the amount of work is titanic, one of the most hemorrhoid moments is the definition of inter-browser compatibility. Is the game worth the candle?

P.S.
For some completely mysterious reason, the actual reference is not even in English. Numerous books do not count, because you need a reference, not a flat file.
