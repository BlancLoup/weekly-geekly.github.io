Presented the first phones WP7. All phones in one place, plus a little about the press conference



A press conference was held dedicated to the launch of devices based on Windows Phone 7. The event was held by Microsoft and At & T is a partner in launching devices in the USA.

During the event, it was announced the launch of nine different phone models at once. Below are the details about each of them. The devices will be on sale in Europe, Asia and Australia on October 21, in the USA and Canada - on November 8.

In addition, AT & T made a statement about the launch of its popular video content delivery service U-verse for Windows Phone 7 and xBox 360 platforms.

During the press conference, a long presentation of the capabilities of the new platform was held. Here are some of them: instant start of photo mode for quick shooting from a sleeping phone, sending pictures to social services with one button, saving mode of all pictures in the cloud storage. Applications for eBay, IMDB and standard WP7 components were shown, including OneNote, whose data can be automatically synchronized with the web storage, and through it immediately with a PC.

Together with the launch of WP7, Microsoft will launch the free Find My Phone service with advanced capabilities for remote control of your phone, including complete cleaning of the contents.

A lot of things were shown, the full video of the press conference can be viewed at this link (when it appears). Do not miss: a huge amount of exclusive material is posted on Engadget for review. Under the cut description of the announced devices.

HTC 7 Surround

The first phone with a sliding speaker and integrated technologies Dolby Mobile and SRS Surround Sound.



Display: 3.8 inches, 1Ghz Snapdragon, 576 Mb RAM, camera 5 Mpx 720p. For sale in the US and Canada, the price is $ 199.

HTC HD7

Essentially a redesigned HD2 model, HTC's flagship.



Display: 4.3 inches, 1Ghz Snapdragon, 576 Mb RAM, 16Gb flash memory, 5 Mpx camera with dual-LED flash, WiFi 802 b / g / n, Dolby Mobile and SRS Surround Sound.

HTC 7 Mozart, HTC 7 Trophy, HTC 7 Pro

Phones for Europe, and HTC 7 Pro will be released next year for Sprint networks (CDMA)



Trophy and Mozart. Display: WVGA LCD, 1Ghz Snapdragon, 576 Mb RAM, 8Gb flash memory, WiFi 802 b / g / n, Dolby Mobile and SRS Surround Sound. The Mozart has an aluminum case, a 3.7 inch screen and an 8 megapixel camera. At Trophy 5 Mpks-camera and the display in 3.8 inches, the company promises to make this model one of the cheapest in the model range.
