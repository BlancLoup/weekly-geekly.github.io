Myths and reality: How to make audio equipment an element of the interior

Last week we began a series of publications , in which we will examine the most frequently asked questions and myths from the field of audio. / An example of our installation in a country house


A tangle of wires and bulky home acoustics cabinets - what to do with all this?


Hi-Fi class speakers have long ceased to be a luxury item and have become an indispensable attribute of a modern home. Being a full-fledged element of interior design, acoustics can become its harmonious continuation or completely destroy its perception.

Take care of this preferably at an early stage of repair. To do this, even there are specially trained people who can give advice on the installation of equipment, based on information about the size, shape of the room, finishing materials and interior.

One of the most common problems that worries our customers is wires. The fact is that the windows of most stores do not show all the technical nuances that can arise with the direct use of audio systems.

The buyer sees beautiful photographic materials and simply forgets about such trifles as cable management. Of course, there are wireless systems on the Hi-Fi and Hi-End equipment markets today, but they also work from the network, so there is no way to get rid of all the wires.

There are many solutions to this problem. For example, there is specialized furniture, which provides a convenient location of "iron" and the possibility of laying the cable in the hidden walls of furniture. In this case, the manufacturer of such furniture takes into account all the nuances, up to the possible heating of the equipment.

In most situations, this approach allows you to reduce the time for preparatory work, not to make walls, and the most important thing is to arrange the audio system so that it not only sounds good, but also looks decent.

Today it is not customary to show off bulky equipment as it was fashionable in the 80s. People think primarily about ergonomics and efficient use of space. Some solutions can be easily ordered and built into the interior, but in certain situations it is worth consulting with a specialist.


/ Ready projects of Audiomania

If we continue the topic of convenience, it is worth noting a huge number of hardware management solutions using smartphones and tablets. We in Audiomania use a system called Bitvise, which solves the necessary tasks and does not cause inconvenience in customization.

Such systems provide enough flexibility to adapt the controls to your own design. The system works via Wi-Fi, and we can turn on music from anywhere in the house.

Thus you can work with the management of the entire home theater system, and if you do not want to buy specialized furniture, you can move all the equipment to another room. Such a room can be quite compact, the only condition is sufficient ventilation.
