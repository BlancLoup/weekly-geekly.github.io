The first ever ReactOS Hackfest

We are glad to share important information. The first ever ReactOS hackfest to be! The event will be held from 7 to 12 August 2015 in the city of Aachen (Germany). All are welcome.

All information about the event can be obtained on a special wiki page .


Photo from a similar event GNOME WebKitGtk + Hackfest

The city of Aachen is located in the place where Germany merges with Belgium and the Netherlands, 4-5 km from the borders with these countries. To the south of the city begins the Eifel National Park. Discover the most western city of Germany. In the historic center of the city, Aachen offers at the same time to taste the spirit of the scientific environment with the opportunity to appreciate the huge variety of pubs. Let's catch this atmosphere and we will code the whole week in order to achieve decent results in the team!

Developers such as Alexey Bragin , Eric Kohl, Thomas Faber, Victor Martinez, Amine Khaldi, Daniel Reimer, Stefan Ginsberg have already confirmed their participation.

The list of participants and ideas for the hackfest is constantly updated on the second wiki page .

Participants pay for their own travel and accommodation. However, in exceptional cases that are not directly related to a trip to the hackfest, the Fund may partially or fully cover the costs of the participant, which is subject to individual discussion.

If a potential participant applies in advance, that is, right now, the Foundation can provide reference and information support on a visa, organize a joint transfer from the airport and stay at the hostel together with all participants, which will significantly reduce costs.


We are already preparing to meet you!
