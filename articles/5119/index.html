Habrarevolution 2.0

While everyone was asleep, we once again rebuilt the structure of the site.

Now all content is linked to blogs and is divided into several types: habratopik, translation , interview , podcast , question . There will be no more texts. They were decided to refuse them, because they ceased to be in demand - in the total share of requests on the site.
As before, blogs remain collective and personal, that is, podcasts can now be collective or personal, interviews, translations, and other types of blog entries can be the same.

In connection with all these innovations, the filter thresholds have changed for the title page. For collective blogs, they look like this (for personal blogs, the odds are twice as large):

- Khabratopiki - 10;
- Interview - 3;
- Translations - 5;
- Podcasts - 3;
- Questions - 3.

Today during the day, mio will make changes to the reference section and explain what is happening (and only then will go to bed). While he is doing this, I ask all those previously published in the texts to put a habracut in their notes, if it is needed there, and everyone and just curious to report errors on the site in the comments to this habratopiku. We will rule and repair.
