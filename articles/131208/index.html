CRM Idol 2011: the video will determine the winner

Went to the finish straight international competition CRM Idol 2011 . Organized by Paul Greenberg , the guru of the global CRM market, the CRM Idol 2011 competition is held for the first time, but has already become the main event of the western CRM market.

The selection of finalists was not easy - out of 60 participating companies, judges identified 7 finalists (3 from the EMEA region and 4 from the Americas).

Among the finalists, the domestic SaaS CRM system BPMonline CRM (manufactured by Terrasoft) is fighting for winning the competition.

In determining the winner of CRM Idol, the votes of viewers of video clips submitted by the finalists will be counted.
