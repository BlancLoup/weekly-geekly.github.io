Second training marathon dedicated to training in software development

June 27, 2009 in Moscow, the Training Labs 2009 conference dedicated to training in software development will take place. The event is organized by Luxoft Training Center.
At this conference, participants in the Software Engineering education market present their best workshops and consultations.
According to the tradition of Training Labs, mini-training covers the main disciplines of Software Engineering - from requirements analysis to load testing, from designing web-sites to managing a team of programmers. Separately, you can say about the section on consulting in software development. Consultants with examples will show what is behind the fashionable word consulting , will give recommendations on the involvement of external consultants - how it works, and when it makes sense to order.

The conference will be a one-day training marathon: 5 sections, each with several mini-trainings / workshops of 1.5 hours duration. The conference program can be viewed on the event website.

Sections Training Labs 2009:
  • Project Management and Communication
  • Requirements Analysis and Management
  • Software Testing
  • Software Design, Architecture
  • Consulting Software Engineering
