Why you need a smartwatch: ten options for using Pebble

At present, technologies have become so firmly established in our lives that most people who have forgotten their phones at home feel at least uncomfortable, and at most, this forgetfulness causes panic. "And if I urgently need to call?". Agreed to meet, but the person is not ... "And if he called to postpone the meeting?". And what our relatives and friends sometimes think, if you don‚Äôt pick up the phone or sit down on a battery - such horrors will never come to your own mind.

If you already have a Pebble, or you are just eyeing them, or you are just choosing a smart watch for yourself - I ask you for a cat. I will try to talk about the most interesting features of watches at the moment and how to simplify your life with smart watches.

What is a pebble?

For those who still do not know what a pebble is, I recommend reading the review from grokinn , the article on developing applications from vvzvlad and about the release of the Pebble AppStore . Much time has passed since the first article was published, and the situation has changed radically, since Third-party developers have updated and released a large number of applications.

In short, Pebble is a smart watch that is connected to your phone and acts as a kind of companion for it. They show incoming notifications and messages from programs - sms, mail, comments, messages, and so on. To look at the watch is much faster and more convenient than getting the phone out of your pocket or bag.

Since on the watch case it says ‚ÄúMade for iPhone. Works with Android ‚Äù, then the developers of the official firmware do the main emphasis on the iPhone. The situation is aggravated by the fact that iOS has a limited number of devices, while Android produces phones for everybody with a huge number of options for internal stuffing, and developers have to spend a lot of effort implementing compatibility with all (mostly) Android phones. As the developers themselves write in their blog, several people work with android and they simply do not have enough time. That is why we still have not seen the official release of the application with the built-in AppStore, which is available only to developers. But in order to become a developer, you just need to register on the forum and download the latest version of the application . At the moment, not everything works so smoothly, the application is frankly tupit, and many people periodically eat battery badly. It can work at idle all night, and can drop the battery to zero per night from 40%. Again, it also depends on the applications that are installed on the watch. Maybe there is a strong weather update from some watchface.

Installing firmware

So, choosing the version of hardware and ticking the options that interest us, press the Create Firmware button.
Thinking, the site will give us a direct link to download and QR-code. It is more convenient for me to collect the firmware from the computer and then download it with the help of Barcode Scanner. In the Android file manager (for iOS, you will have to install the firmware via Dropbox), we click on the firmware, and the phone offers to choose a program to handle the action. Select Pebble, in the program itself, select Load and wait for the end of the firmware.


At the same time, if you already have an updated application - all previously installed applications can be downloaded from Locker, a kind of repository of installed applications that are stored on the phone and do not require an Internet connection during installation. With this, you can also ‚Äúcircumvent‚Äù the restriction on 8 applications by simply unloading the currently unnecessary applications and loading them back with all the settings when they are needed.

What is Watchface and Watchapp

Everything that is installed on the watch is divided into 2 categories: watchface - in fact, it is a watch in different variations with additional functions such as calendar display, weather, clock and phone battery level, connection status, and so on. Watchapp - programs that perform a variety of actions and have their own interface, run from the menu.
I don‚Äôt like standard watches, so I noted in the generator that they are not needed and removed the item in the watchfaces menu as well. Moreover, some faces are forced to update the weather when flipping, and when there are several of them, it‚Äôs not very comfortable to flip through the ships there. And so, when you press up or down, the weather is updated on the same face in one motion.
Having tried many different skins, I settled on Modern. It shows the analog dial (seconds are removed so that it doesn‚Äôt eat the battery), shows the day of the week, the day and the weather. In my opinion, the most convenient and minimalistic option.

Glance for Pebble can make your own customized versions of watchfaces, you can download from several thousand ready-made direct from the official application or the unofficial Pebble apps , or do it yourself online at Watchface Generator or in the Canvas for Pebble program.

First application. Manage calls with hands: Pebble Dialer

The main application that is used on the watch and greatly expands the capabilities of the standard application. For correct operation, you must disable the call notification in the standard notifier. Allows you to accept / reject the call, put on the speakerphone, turn off the microphone when making a call, informs about the duration of the call. By default, the incoming name will be truncated, because Russian language occupies twice the volume when sending characters. But the craftsmen from the well-known site translated the interface, increased the transmitted number of bytes from 16 to 32, broke the caller's name into 2 lines (though the second character is truncated and if the last name contains 8 characters, you will see 5 characters and a three-dot). Allows you to view call history, make calls. Works great with a headset. You can never remove the phone from your pocket.


The second application. Manage music: Music Boss

The application extends the capabilities of the standard, with the ability to perform actions by double and long pressing the buttons. You can adjust the volume, switch tracks, switch the application for music, which will respond to buttons, launch the active application on the phone. There is integration with tasker and can intercept commands from the standard music application.


Third application: Receive notifications without getting the phone out of pocket

There are about 10 notifiers at the moment. But they can do dismiss - that is, an analogue of the svayp in the notification panel of the phone, only 3 pieces. Notification Center for Pebble , a mod on its basis and the YaNC application - Yet another Notification Center from the same developer, but with a different working principle. For myself, I chose the latter, since It generates a notification picture and sends it to the clock. Someone annoyed that the drawing of the picture is slow, but the clock vibrates only upon completion of the drawing of the picture and it does not bother me at all. You can customize the filter of applications from which you want to receive notifications and due to the fact that the processing takes place on the phone, allows you to send any characters, including emoticons from the Hangouts. There is an integration with the tasker, you can add 4 actions from the list on the phone (if the tasker is installed on the phone and configured).


Fourth application. Wrist Navigator


Pebble Adventure Navigation
The application works through Google maps, sends the next hint on the route to the clock. If you still translate the team into Russian, and make it possible to talk about maneuvers in advance, then the price would not have been. At the moment, the new instruction comes during the passage of the checkpoint, and at the prompt ‚Äúgo straight for 5 km, then turn right‚Äù, here it is to the right and it will buzz. It is not suitable for navigation in the stream on machines - you do not have time to rebuild. For pedestrian navigation is a good application.


OnkoCycle
The application is still in beta, so you need to join the G + community to access the download in the market. It works on the basis of Google maps, but some cut-down version. Houses are kind of shown on the map, but unclear and there are no numbers. Although if you poke into the house, the address will show and you can make a route. Originally intended for cyclists, but allows you to choose a hiking route and a car. Rebuilds the route when leaving the target, there is voice navigation, and for cyclists additional useful settings like "do not make the route up, down or just over the hills." Whether due to the fact that the application is in beta, I have conflicts with something, but for 10 minutes the way home hung for 2 times for 2 minutes. When reaching an intermediate point, it vibrates and gives a new hint. The clock displays the current, average speed, distance to the intermediate point and the next maneuver. If you finish and integrate with google now for normal search of routes and display of maps, I will always use it on trips.


Fifth application. We read books: Pebble Reader


A handy application for reading documents in txt format. It has a file manager with which you can select a file without getting the phone. For some, this is the perfect option for cheat sheets with tickets divided into folders and files. When you press the button back, it starts the clock, when it is restarted, it opens the file at the point where the viewing stopped. It has font size settings, display options are white on black / black on white. It remembers the last directory, there is a manual and auto-selection of the encoding, the settings for the line break, the indent value, the line spacing. Can hide infobar, which displays% of read and number of pages read / total pages. When you start the application, you can forcibly turn on the backlight during operation.


The sixth application. Sticky Notes: PebMsg

A simple application to send a small text to the clock. The text is shown as a notification and therefore it does not occupy a slot in the clock. I use it every day, sending my shopping list to the watch, which my wife takes off in the hangouts. It is much more convenient than walking with a leaflet or constantly holding / taking out a phone with a list.


Seventh application. Camera control


Watch Trigger for Pebble
The program allows you to take a picture from the phone or start / stop video recording while running a third-party application on the phone. With integrated camera does not work. It is convenient to take photos for example of yourself and the girls, setting the phone so as to get into the frame. You can turn off the sound of photographing, set the timer, control the flash.


Pebblecam
It looks like the previous application, but in addition it sends a picture from the viewfinder to the clock. The quality is not very, transmitted and updated slowly enough, but to understand what exactly is in the frame can be. You can also turn off the screen in the program on your phone so as not to give yourself away.


Eighth application. The mood of the action itself: PebbleTasker


The application allows you to attach Tasker events to your buttons using the application on your phone. A single press on the 3 buttons, a long press on the upper and lower buttons is still an event and with a long press on the middle button, a list of 20 tasks opens. Total, you can use 24 commands for the phone.


Ninth application. Full Messenger: Pebble Messenger

Shows the last 10 sms or whatsapp messages. Allows you to respond to these messages 10 pre-prepared templates. It is very convenient to send an answer if you are driving or under the rain, given that the watch is waterproof. You can also write messages by letter. Only English characters are available (I hope the craftsmen will think of something). The way is vaguely reminiscent of T9 on the phone. For buttons, 3 groups of characters are defined. The upper is from a to i , the middle is from j to q and the lower is from r to z . If you need to insert the letter m, then you need to press the middle button, then the choice will be given by the following option: upper jkl button, middle mn , lower opq . Accordingly, for the letter m, you need to press the middle one, then the last choice will remain: at the top of m , at the bottom of n . Being accustomed, you can quickly type the text without getting the phone out of your pocket, which can be useful in the cold. For messages sent from the clock you can add a signature.
