Choosing a certification authority

Prepared and decided to take. It is necessary to go through a simple procedure. Register on the VUE website. ‚ÄúWhat kind of VUE, but I‚Äôll pass the Oracle certificate?‚Äù Many might think. Calm Oracle itself develops certification, but trusts its implementation to a certified center. He, in turn, has an agreement with the centers on the ground. When registering on their website ( http://vue.com/ ) you need to choose the scope of certification. Further, it is enough to choose a certification center, choose a convenient time, write down their address and appear at the appointed time.

Payment for the exam passes by bank transfer (ie. Card). Cost - 150 USD

There are about five centers in Kiev. After a certain search, I stopped at http://www.flane.com.ua/ . And did not regret. Their office provided the greatest possible advice on the process of passing. Geographically, they are located on Pozniaky (5 minutes from the metro).

Certification

Appears for certification is necessary for 15-20 minutes before the specified time. So that you do not have a chance to write off, all personal belongings are locked in the closet, the key of which is given to you personally. At the exam you are given the correct leaflet and the correct pen. Before the very beginning of the exam you are photographed and take a copy of your signature.

Important: you need to have two documents with you with your last name and first name. On one of the documents they must be written in Latin (these data will be indicated in your certificate). I used my foreign passport and Ukrainian passport.

The exam gives 90 questions and two and a half hours of time. Plus, after the exam, two questions about the quality of the exam and its usefulness. All questions in style: choose option two or three. Those. Always know the number of correct answers. 2 and a half hours is more than enough for delivery. I managed one and a half (and this is in view of the fact that I revised my answers).

In the process of passing the tests, you can mark questions and return to them later. This is very useful and gives you the opportunity at the beginning to answer all that you know, and then everything that you doubt. The test result is reported immediately and a printout is made.

Motivation (well, or conclusions about the usefulness of this adventure)

Much has been said about this. But I still add my couple of kopecks. Initial certificates are not often needed, but can play a crucial role! I have been in the shoes of a man who takes on a robot of people. And by itself, if several candidates are about the same and go to the initial positions, then the person with the certificate is much more preferable and we will choose it. And if you think that you are the only one so unique, well, then you only need the initial certificate in order to go further because such is the requirements of Oracle.

As for higher certificates, then everything is more interesting. In the CIS, for example, not many have the highest level of business architect. This does not mean that very few people with such knowledge, just many of those who possess them are not interested in certification. However, on the other hand, if you are at the initial stage, then you will have to prove that you deserve the title of architect for a long time, and such a system of attestation of a specialist allows you to at least formally receive confirmation that you are already at this level, which can later become decisive.

In short, this piece of paper will bring exactly as much benefit as you can squeeze out of it (and therefore may not bring it at all). Moreover, I also have this study motivator, since having paid for the exam in advance, whether you want to or not, you will learn =)
