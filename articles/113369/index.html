In January 2010, as part of the Runet Rating project, a new service was launched that allows you to organize a tender for website creation or promotion: www.ratingruneta.ru/tenders

During this time, 36 tenders have already been held. Of these, 15 are for search engine website promotion and 21 for website development.



The principle of the service is simple. The portal offers a form in which the customer of the site makes a request: a description of the site that he needs, a price corridor and the location of the contractor. Based on these criteria, suitable companies are filtered from the database. In addition, the tender can be organized directly from the ratings of web studios or SEO companies. The user forms a short list and fills in a simple questionnaire, and the service sends this data to web studios or SEO companies, which must respond with commercial offers by a certain date.

The service is absolutely free for both customers and performers.
