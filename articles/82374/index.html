reference

Lookalike is a service based on facial recognition technology designed to find and find people like each other by simply uploading photos (yours, friends, etc.). People around the world can find their counterparts and communicate with them. The service will be of interest to girls and guys who miss their ex.

In the face recognition program, elastic bunch graph mapping is used - the points responsible for the main parts of the face (nose, eyes, etc., about 50 pieces) are automatically located on the face. The similarity of persons is determined from the sum of the similarities of the neighborhoods of the corresponding points; these similarities are calculated using special formulas. It is based on the implementation of the algorithm from the Colorado State University - Face Identification Evaluation System, which we can use and improve, thanks to the CSU license. To search for faces in the photo, the openCV library is used.
