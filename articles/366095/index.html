[Video] Journey from the Sun to Jupiter at the speed of light

In our usual understanding, light moves very quickly. However, in comparison with the huge distances in the universe, it is very slow. This video demonstrates the travel of a photon emitted by the sun through a part of the solar system.

The author allowed himself some freedom in leveling the planets and asteroids, but in general he tried to display everything as accurately as possible. He also decided to finish the video immediately after Jupiter, so that the duration of the video was less than an hour.
