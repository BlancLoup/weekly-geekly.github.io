iWeekend Eastern Europe in Kiev

On November 19-22, 2009, iWeekend will be held in Kiev - an event that is designed to catalyze the creation of startups and the financing of their early stages. Last week we agreed on everything. Investors and experts from Europe promised to come to Kiev. Most likely, participation will be free of charge and young entrepreneurs from Eastern Europe will be invited to participate, well, it is understood that first of all, apart from Ukrainians, Russians and Belarusians.
Unfortunately, this year's Seedcamp in Eastern Europe will be held only in Warsaw, which is far away. And November 19-22 falls on the dates of the REDD.

For Kiev entrepreneurs looking for knowledge and investment, in the spring there are practically no major events specialized, with the possible exception of a few speakers at the Internet events at the bottom of the list.

But autumn will be hot

Seedcamp (London, UK) - end of September - beginning of October
TechCrunch50 (San Francisco, CA, US) - September
Microsoft VC Summit (Mountain View, CA, US) - September
Investor Day @ Blogcamp CEE (Kiev, Ukraine) - October
