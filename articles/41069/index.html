Apple time machine



Apple Time Capsule and Apple Time Machine are the so-called data backup device and the program for it, respectively. Equipped with a hard disk of 500 gigabytes or 1 terabyte, the device connects via wi-fi to all macbooks within reach and allows you to share or save user files. In addition, Time Capsule works as a hub for the printer. The user-friendly time machine interface consists of a single On-Off switch. The price of the issue is $ 299 or $ 499.

TiVo Video Recorder



The TiVo digital video recorder can record video from any television source - antenna, cable or satellite. When viewing such a recorded program, you can return to the missed episodes, skip ads, stop viewing. The latest generation of devices download videos and movies directly from the web, synchronize the program guide, record regular programs and TV shows.

Stiletto - digital radio



Zing's Stiletto is a whole line of players, both personal and car, downloading music via Wi-fi, satellite and Sirius Internet radio and storing about 100 hours of content. In 2007, Zing was purchased by Dell. Currently, the service has about 18 million subscribers.

Sony LocationFree TV




Sony LocationFree TV connects to the Playstation 3 and broadcasts content from the console's hard disk via the Internet. With the help of a PSP or a mobile phone, you can connect to your own access point, to watch TV channels (via another Sony device) and movies, and to listen to music.
