SHARED.menu - search virtual hosting

Not so long ago, the development of an analogue of VDS.menu was completed , for virtual hosting - SHARED.menu .



A very large list of parameters is available for users to search for the ideal rate of virtual hosting.

  1. Disk capacity
  2. The number of sites that can be hosted on a shared hosting
  3. Number of databases you can create
  4. Number of mailboxes
  5. Availability of PHP support. Support for this programming language is almost all hosts, but sometimes there are cheap rates, in which this parameter is missing. Especially for such cases, the absence of PHP is highlighted in red, in order not to accidentally buy a tariff that may not be suitable for the characteristics.
  6. The amount of traffic that is included in the tariff
  7. Price. Just as on VDS.menu, you can search by price per month, 3 months, six months and a year. Also, you can always choose one of 10 currencies and the site will calculate the server cost at the current exchange rate.
  8. The control panel that is used on the server. Most often, those who are accustomed to a panel do not want to switch to another, so you can always do a search for tariffs with the desired panel.
  9. Country of placement of equipment. When choosing a country, the emphasis is on ensuring that the site opens to visitors as quickly as possible. If the target audience is in the USA, then it is more reasonable to take hosting in the USA or Canada, if the target audience is from Russia, then in this case the best option would be Russia or the nearest European countries.
  10. Payment method
  11. SSL support. A secure connection is needed, for example, online stores that have a personal account and online payment.
  12. Access to the server via SSH. Sometimes it is much easier and faster to do any operation through the console than through the control panel, so you can quickly find hosts that allow you to connect via SSH to your servers.
  13. IPv6 availability
  14. Minimum payment period. Sometimes it happens that hosters require you to pay a tariff for a period longer than a month. This is usually at cheap rates. Thus, it will be easy to find tariffs that can be bought for 1 month, for example, when you do not want to pay a large amount and just try the services of a new hosting provider.

Tariffs are now in the process of adding, so there are not so many of them, but I hope that, by analogy with VDS.menu, hosters will be able to fill in a special file with the characteristics of their tariffs and send it to info@shared.menu. I add any tariffs with the price of 10 rubles or more and not unlimited disk.

On both projects, the menu of currency selection and the number of tariffs on the page is now duplicated in the top menu, I hope, it will now be easier to switch the currency or the number of tariffs.

In the near future I want to greatly increase the number of hosters in the directory and make the English versions of these two projects. I will be glad to hear feedback.
