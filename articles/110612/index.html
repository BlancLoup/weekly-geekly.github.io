Xmas Time: nice girl draws time

Hello! Merry Christmas and Happy New Year! We did not come empty-handed and want to tell you about our new project.

Xmas Time is a video clock in which a cute girl in a Santa suit draws a new arrow every minute, erasing the old one, and in the breaks from this hard work trying to get your attention.
