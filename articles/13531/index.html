Page Promoter 7.6: effective technology to attract customers

Recently, NetPromoter has released a new version of the program for integrated analysis, promotion and management of Page Promoter - 7.6.

The new module "Placement of articles" has been added, allowing to place articles on the pages of thematic resources, even on those that require entering a security code from an image. This module provides features such as preparing articles for posting and analyzing the report generated by the program after the posting process is completed. The report will provide information on whether the attempt to post an article was successful, and if not, for what reason.

These additions concern the Page Promoter edition of the Expert. The transition to the new version of the Page Promoter program for registered users of this edition of the program is free. You can purchase the installation package that is the same for all editions of Page Promoter here , and the functions of a specific edition become available after purchasing and entering the corresponding license key.

About the new features of the program will be available on September 20 at a free seminar on analysis, promotion and resource management on the Internet, for this you just need to fill out the questionnaire of the participant .
