WikiLeaks Wins Trial against Visa and MasterCard in Iceland



WikiLeaks won a lawsuit against Visa and MasterCard in Iceland. The court ordered the local payment systems partner to resume processing donations to the WikiLeaks project.

An Icelandic court ruled that Valitor - a local partner of Visa and MasterCard - violated the contract law when it stopped processing donations for WikiLeaks , Mashable writes .

Visa and MasterCard , as well as other US financial companies, such as PayPal , blocked WikiLeaks operations in 2010 after the site published more than 250,000 US secret diplomatic telegrams.
