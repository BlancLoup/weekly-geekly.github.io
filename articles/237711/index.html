I need help: Alexey Chervonenkis disappeared

Alexey Yakovlevich Chervonenkis , a professor at the Yandex Data Analysis School, a professor at the University of London, disappeared near the Losinoostrovskaya platform yesterday.



Alexey Chervonenkis is the author of fundamental works in machine learning theory, in particular, the concepts of VC-dimensionality and VC-entropy, one of the key people in the emergence of statistical machine learning.

The guys from Liza Alert are engaged in his search (this is a volunteer organization that searches for missing people). Need help: with the dissemination of information from Lisa Alert, and with searches on the spot. Here is a post on their forum: lizaalert.org/forum/viewtopic.php?f=207&t=8429

UPD: LisaAlert reported that a body was found from a helicopter, and that this is supposedly the body of Alexei Yakovlevich. Relatives are now reaching the place of detection to identify and say for sure.

UPD2: Unfortunately, Alexei Yakovlevich could not be saved: on Tuesday at 12 pm he was found from a helicopter in an inaccessible area, and an identification was held late in the evening. We thank everyone who responded to our call for help in the search. We grieve over the loss and express our deepest condolences to relatives and friends.


09/21 he went for a walk from the Losinoostrovskaya platform towards Mytishchi, the last time he got in touch on the night of September 22. Said he got lost. Since then there is no information about his whereabouts.

If you are ready and can physically assist in the search - call the curator Ana Sovunye by phone +7 (916) 995-51-36.
