February 11: The Techleads meetup. How to create and maintain dynamic development


The time has come to announce our second Techleads-metap for leads, department heads and SRT, created to combine technology, development and management on one platform. It will be held on February 11 in the office of Badoo in Moscow. Beginning: at 12:00.


At the last meeting we discussed the topics of the mobile web, testing, technology and communications. This time all the speeches at the mitap are united by one common theme: how to create and maintain dynamic development in the context of constant growth of the team and changes in the company.


The topic is so interesting that we could not stop while choosing the speakers at the meeting. You will like it: Max Lapshin (Erlyvideo), Evgeny Potapov (ITSumma) will perform with their stories, Badoo - Alexei Rybak, Ilya Pyatin (CarPrice) and Andrei Shelekhin (Tinkoff.ru). Under the cut - the schedule of performances.


Speakers and Topics


Max Lapshin, Erlyvideo



Topic: How to create a small and profitable software company, if you are a programmer?


In the report, I will talk about a fairly successful history of the calm development of a company engaged in a difficult thing: we make niche software, which is the technological basis for foreign businesses.


I will talk about:


  • how we plan the development of software, which is then sold in 100 countries around the world. What we are doing now, what then and what we are doing with what is impossible to do.
  • What are we doing to not make ourselves a daily stress? The specificity of product development, which is very far from the end users and is not fundamentally tested in-house in full.
  • how we simplify our work to move forward while keeping stress levels low.

What turns out, and with what bad cope.

The processes associated with supporting the project have always stood apart in the planning and development of the company. How can you save benchmarks in a sharp change of priorities? How can you keep and develop a team in this constant stress? Evgeny Potapov will tell about his development history of support in conditions when it is necessary to support not one project, but 200. The history of the company's growth from 3 to 60 people.


  1. Staff issues:
    - how to find and train the right people

    - organization and automation of "back office".

Topic: Vzhuh, and you have Agile!


You want to build a quality development process. It seems that around the mass of sources that will help. For example, books on Agile and Scrum, explaining what is actually written in the 17-page Scrum Guide; trainings that will finally explain to your inflexible developers about the healing properties of the board with stickers, that story points are better than ideal hours, and how to punish those who are late for the Daily Meeting.


All these are ready-made approaches and practices. Start applying ready-made solutions without understanding what is hidden behind them, and they will be not only useless, but also dangerous for your process.


There is another kind of help - an external consultant. He is ready to work with the team every day until each of the participants learns to explain the difference between Agile and Scrum, fit the User Story on the sticker and tell how the day went. But the external consultant does not guarantee you success. Contacting the wrong one will only make it worse and just lose time and money.


The solution is to independently and consciously build development processes based on common sense and the surrounding reality.
How can I do this, I will tell in the report. Using the example of the internal development department of Tinkoff, we analyze our checklist of the correct development process and the questions:


  • What do development managers forget?
  • Can processes solve problems in development without interfering with business?
  • How to break the rules described in the books about Scrum, and not blame yourself for it?
