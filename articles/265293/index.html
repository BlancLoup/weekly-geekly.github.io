Frontend Union Conf conference this Saturday

Just ten years ago, a significant part of the frontend-development was a layout of photoshop and the fight against cross-browser incompatibility. It seems that it was yesterday. And today, the frontend is one of the most dynamically developing areas, in which tools and practices change every six months, and developers still have much to bring about successful approaches and solutions from other areas. As I have repeatedly written , conferences are not a way to learn something new. This is a way to talk to interesting people on interesting topics. This Saturday, the FUNC organizers provide a truly unique opportunity to talk with frontend community leaders from different countries. The conference will be held within the walls of a large event site in the Mail.Ru office, with huge rooms and delicious coffee. The event plan is under the cut, participation is free, but there are not many registrations left. Join now!
