  • Noteworthy is the work with the DWG-format. This is one of the platform chips. The technology has acquired useful and pleasant functions in the section of fonts, cleaning drawings, significantly improved and simplified save and transfer operations.

He did not adapt to them in the course of market promotion, as it happens with Western systems, but tune in to them in its root, at the core.
