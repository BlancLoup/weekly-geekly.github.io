Antigravity. Magnetic levitation



It was this mouse that first experienced its effect.

Scientists from NASA have created a magnetic field of sufficient strength to force a laboratory mouse to levitate above the surface. This is as big a breakthrough as a levitating board from the movie Back to the Future. The only problem was that the mouse was drunk on the board.



Scientists from the Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California, have created a superconducting magnet that can generate enough energy to tear a rat-sized animal from the floor. Prior to this, experiments were conducted with smaller animals: frogs and beetles, this is the first time such an approach has forced such a large animal to be torn off the ground.
