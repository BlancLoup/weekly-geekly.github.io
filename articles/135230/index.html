Apple is working on a fuel cell laptop project



It is very similar to the fact that Apple is currently working on a fuel cell laptop project. In any case, a patent for this technology has already been filed. In principle, if the "apple" company did it, and the laptop with the fuel cell would go on sale, one would expect an incredible rush. For geeks, so surely it would be a gift.

Experts from Apple argue that the fuel cells in netbooks and laptops can become a reality in the next few years. If we are talking about fuel cells on hydrogen, then the laptop can work on such batteries for several days or even weeks in auto mode. In this case, such a battery may be significantly less than the current batteries.

Unfortunately, so far all this is only a theory - in the real world there is not yet fuel hydrogen cells that can really become part of an ordinary laptop and be, among other things, beneficial in commercial use. But if Apple can create something like this, success is guaranteed for something. Well, provided that such a laptop will not cost many thousands of dollars.

A little more information on this issue (including the diagrams from patents filed by Apple) can be found on appleinsider . It would, of course, be very happy to get technology that would allow laptops to work for many days on a single refueling of the fuel cell. We will wait for the appearance of all this on sale and hope that this will happen in the next couple of years, and not decades.
