A world scale experiment, or as we called Kickstarter to Russia

Exactly a month ago, two intimate friends met and talked about the fate of crowdfunding in Russia, about projects leading in this direction, about their prospects, and in general, about the future of this social phenomenon.

In the course of the conversation, as befits in business circles boldly looking into the eyes of a terrible tyrnet, the question arose-the idea ‚Äúwouldn‚Äôt we make a successful Western project clone‚Äù? And we have gas in the apartment, and gasoline ends ... (joke, but it seems like a joke already). So, a cursory analysis revealed the presence of at least 4 crowdfunding projects in RuNet + the newly emerged factory of startups from Yandex. The situation is almost a stalemate, a new KickStarter clone in Russia is not needed.

What about bringing KickStarter itself to Russia?
This is where the fun began!



We used Kickstarter, started studying it from the inside, tested sending platage (donates) to good projects, tried to create your project, started an account on Amazon, reached the sending request for the project, learned that Kickstarter was two years old at the end of April , we learned that the project was born happened between April 27 and 28 ... And they decided to act.

- What do we have that can help this project to come to our country?
- Does this project want to expand its presence?
- How to draw attention to yourself?
- What do we want to get?

Answering these questions, we drew an algorithm of actions.

1. we have people, opportunities, understanding and desire;
2. they have a desire to expand, but there is no clear understanding of the terms (as it is written on the website);
3. To attract the attention of the project to yourself in different ways, but the most logical is to place your idea on it and try to work out the whole scheme using your own example;
4. we want to become a part of Kickstarter, and are ready to solve serious problems - maximum. Minimum - we want to create a serious informational occasion, with the mention in the news feeds not only in Russia, but also in America.
