SteamBoy - a portable console for running video games from Steam (and not only)



And not only from Steam. SteamBoy, in fact, is a portable PC, enclosed in a case with controls, like on a Steam controller. It is worth noting that the delivery of Steam Machine, game consoles from Steam, is postponed to 2015, and then you can expect the appearance of a portable console.

By the way, a third-party development team is responsible for creating SteamBoy, who recently released the first video for their project.

Unfortunately, not much is known about the configuration of the device. In addition to its appearance, information is available about the size of the display - 5 inches, as well as that the system has a 4-core processor, 4 GB of RAM, 32 GB of internal memory, plus wireless communication modules and a 3G module.
