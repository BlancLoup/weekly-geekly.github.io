For video VKontakte - 5 days of administrative arrest


I think people who have pages in social networks will be interested in this precedent.

Briefly:
On September 27, a young man from Naberezhnye Chelny was imprisoned for 5 days of administrative arrest for materials posted in 2012. In fact, these materials are called "photos of Nazi symbols, which are open and accessible to an unlimited number of users." Although in reality it was a documentary about Kolovrat, and screenshots from this video were attached to the case. The case was considered so quickly that they were taken directly from the prosecutor's office of Alexander to the court, and from there to immediately serve the sentence.
