First of all I want to greet all the necrohardophiles!

From scraps of information from the Internet, I learned the following about the insides:

  • Video : 16 Kb

Power Supply


The power supply case is made of good thick metal, but it is not remarkable for this. Firstly, the connector for the power cord is classic, like on any monitor or desktop today, thanks to this, there were no problems connecting the laptop to electricity, thanks to the developers (nowadays, by the way, not every laptop can boast such versatility ). Secondly, from the power supply to the laptop go two wires: for power and for charging. And if my memory does not lie to me, then for a long time I read somewhere that they need to be connected in some particular order, otherwise the collapse of the universe.




Moreover, the wires for power and charging have jacks of different thickness: charging will fit into the power supply, but vice versa. Well, the jacks are painted in different colors so as not to confuse. Thought about the little things.

Keyboard


The keyboard is wonderful, mechanical, but on it a strange layout. When clicked, it clicks nicely (you can listen to it in the video), but strange with shifta: there is one full-size shift on the right, a small one on the left, some edit shift in place of the current win and a cap on the right in the menu. In capslock and numlock embedded LEDs. At the same time, edit shift works as a capslock, but in a mechanical mode: the button remains pressed until the next pressing. What is the meaning of duplication is incomprehensible to me, maybe it works like fn, but I still do not understand.




And the keyboard goes. This compensates for the insufficiently large opening angle of the screen: you can put the laptop on the stand and put the keyboard on the table. In a high-quality connector, a regular telephone connector is used, and at the end, under the thermocallic, there is probably a magnetic ring to eliminate interference. Whether the interference was earlier than it is now, or the paranoiac was developing this laptop. Inside the laptop there are special grooves for the wire where it is inserted when removing the keyboard. And in this picture you can estimate the layout.
