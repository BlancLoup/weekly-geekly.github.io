> Interface and software


The camera interface is simplified to minimalism, by pressing only two buttons (switching - button on; selection - recording button), you can change the current modes of operation: video, photo, serial photo, time-lapse, settings. Moving through the menu is only in one direction and information about the settings in the current mode is displayed on the screen.

The camera does not have a built-in viewfinder; this problem is corrected by an external LCD touchscreen from commercially available accessories. Also, the viewfinder can be a smartphone or tablet running iOS or Android. To use this feature, you must activate the Wi-Fi module built into the camera. To connect the mobile device and GoPro, you need to select the appropriate mode on the camera. To work with the remote control, you need to select the desired item in the camera menu. Simultaneous operation from a smartphone and remote control is not possible. After pressing the button on the left side of the camera, the local wireless network with integrated security system is activated (the name of the access point and password can be changed). To fully use the viewfinder, control the settings of the camera and access point, you must install the GoPro App for iOS or Android .

The camera uses a new Wi-Fi chip. The delay time when viewed in the application for mobile devices is 1 sec. Video on your phone or tablet is displayed in almost real time.




> Shooting in various modes


A fisheye lens with a fixed focal length has a viewing angle of 170 degrees. The software of the camera allows using several modes in viewing angles: Wide, Medium, Narrow. The new Super Wide mode gives additional spatial distortion when shooting.
A wide range of resolutions is available for shooting, up to 4K, but in my subjective opinion, the most optimal and universal mode can be considered ‚Äú1080 Super View‚Äù (1920 x 1080 at 48/50 frames per second).
The camera completely lacks interlaced scan modes, i.e. There is no interlace when viewing. In general, the colors are very bright, the picture is characterized by sharpness and saturation.
With this camera you can take slow-motion video in the modes: 1280 x 960 with a frequency of up to 100 frames per second, 1280 x 720 with a frequency of up to 120 frames per second and 848 x 480 - up to 240 frames per second.
I would like to separately mention the Protune mode, this mode does not particularly stress the built-in processor, but it allows you to adjust the white balance and improve the picture quality.

GoPro Hero 3+ Black Edition can take photos with a resolution of 12 megapixels. Pictures are obtained with some spatial distortion and sharpness reduction at the edges. I would especially like to mention the photo-shooting mode called ‚Äútime-lapse‚Äù, in it you can set the interval between frames (from 0.5 seconds to one minute) and manually control the number of frames in the interval.

> Conclusion:


Hero 3+ Black Edition made a positive impression on me, it meets all the requirements for modern portable action cameras. It should be noted a number of factors that allow GoPro cameras to be popular and in demand: build reliability and utmost compactness; a large number of recording formats; wide-angle optics, giving excellent sharpness and excellent resolution of the matrix. The camera is also popular due to the large variety of mountings. They make it possible to fix the camera anywhere, which allows you to get unusual shots from different angles. This is especially true for shooting outdoor activities and practicing extreme sports, when shooting on a regular camera is simply impossible.

Pros:
1. Shooting FullHD video with high frame rate (50-60fps)
2. High-speed Wi-Fi with a minimum delay time for broadcasting a video stream (1s)
3. Micro HDMI interface for output video without delays
4. A serious reserve of autonomy, the battery at 1180 mAh (2 hours of continuous shooting)
5. Compact size and light weight (75 g)
6. Waterproof protective box and other accessories included.
7. Good software optimization for mobile devices (GoPro App)
8. Lack of interlaced shooting modes (no interlaced scanning during playback)
9. LED indicator for charging, shooting mode and Wi-Fi operation

Minuses:
1. Mechanical buttons (the only possible option for normal camera control in gloves)
2. Archaic monochrome LCD display, left by the manufacturer due to the preservation of the compact size of the case. If desired, you can connect a full - fledged color touch screen from the available accessories.
