Event for university teachers in St. Petersburg on programming training



Dear friends!

So we are ripe for an event for education in St. Petersburg.

September 22 at 14.00 we are waiting for university professors at the seminar: "Training in programming and project training at the university: environment and technology."

We will discuss the problems of education in teaching programming, we will consider the issues of applied development in a multi-platform environment. Let's talk about new standards, and how to combine them with the rapid development of technology.
The event is supported by our friend on educational projects, Samsung, represented by Vsevolod Leonov, who has experience in teaching programming at the Bauman University.

In a programme:
  • Teaching programming in the program of training applied specialists in higher education institutions;
  • Training in mobile development in high school: features, approaches;
  • Learning to create cross-platform applications: how to optimize the teaching process;
  • Project training is a new vector in new educational standards;
  • How to keep up with the emergence of new technologies: Internet of things, robots, mobile development, web 3.0;
  • Training specialists demanded by the labor market


Venue: ITMO University, Exchange Line V.O., 4

Registration is required. Participation in the event is free.
edu.delphitour.ru - event website with registration and program.
