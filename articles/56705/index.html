  • Cheap life. The same standard of living can be saved at a much lower cash cost. For those who like to stick this word in each article - ok, call it an anti-crisis proposal. For those who do not like - the costs are so small that it was more convenient to live here before the crisis.
  • Easy life. There is a lot of infrastructure, it is near, it is cheap. A bunch of food establishments, cafes, markets, entertainment of various kinds. All in a minute ride a motorcycle.
