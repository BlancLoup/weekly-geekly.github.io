HoloLens Augmented Reality Glasses are available for pre-order.


RoboRaid game included

Starting today, HoloLens (dev-kit) augmented reality glasses are available for pre-order . Deliveries start from March 30 (USA and Canada). True, Microsoft has set a fairly high price of $ 3000 on glasses, but they warned about this. The gadget is really unique.

Imagine that monsters are flying around your room, then virtual characters jump over your furniture, and a beautiful girl sits next to you on the sofa. Such games are offered by Microsoft to develop using Visual Studio 2015 under Windows 10 (these are mandatory requirements for PCs).



Inside the device, there is a 32-bit Intel processor, 2 GB of RAM, a 2 megapixel camera, 64 GB of file storage, support for Bluetooth, 802.11ac, and USB 2.0.

The set includes a clicker (pictured).



Three games are recorded in points: Fragments, Young Conker and RoboRaid.



HoloLens also contain a special version of Skype in which you can communicate using holograms.



Two more programs have been installed: HoloTour for virtual tours in various cities and HoloStudio for 3D modeling.



The battery in glasses lasts for two or three hours of "active use", but the gadget can be used simultaneously with charging via USB.

HoloLens Weight - 579 g.
