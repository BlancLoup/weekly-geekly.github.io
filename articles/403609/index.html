Functionality

Everything that a bidet can do, and this - several modes of operation - is made on clear buttons, which will be to the right of the person sitting.


Oh, those sweet graphics =)

Our smart toilet seat recognizes with a motion sensor whether someone is currently on top or not. This is also done to avoid misunderstandings if someone decides to play around and just press a button. In this case, nothing will happen.

There are three basic modes:

  • Washing
  • Bidet
  • Turbo

Moreover, the use of the latter in the "lite" mode "Children" is impossible. Also available "Drying", "Massage", "Pulse".

For example, in the ‚ÄúMassage‚Äù mode the water jet will move back and forth, and during the pulsation the pressure will automatically change. At the same time, the head power can be adjusted independently by means of the corresponding control buttons.

Mode "Drying". In this mode, the ‚Äúwindow‚Äù opens to the right of the shower.

The stop button turns off both the water supply and the device as a whole. In order to turn off the toilet seat, the button must be held for three seconds.

Also, in addition to adjusting the water intensity and modes, the user can control the temperature of the jet using the appropriate button and heated seats. The latter possibility, by the way, does not seem so funny. Who is not annoying to sit on the cold ?! Br-rr-r.

The function will start automatically, but already the blowing intensity can be adjusted independently.

ECO mode

Separately, let's say a few words about power saving modes. A single press activates the automatic mode, which provides for maintaining the temperature of the seat and water at 32 degrees. At the same time at the time of use, you can manually adjust the temperature of the water.

When you double-click on the "ECO" button, the green light is activated - the mode is off, and the heating will be carried out manually during use.

Triple pressing will allow you to independently adjust the constant water temperature.

The water in the device accumulates constantly! The back, where the hose is connected, is the reservoir.



You need to know this also because when you need to remove the device, the tank needs to be emptied. This is done by pressing the universal button, which is easy to grope from the side of the wire.
