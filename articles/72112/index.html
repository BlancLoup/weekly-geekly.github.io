A new tool for travel agents based on Microsoft Surface

You came to a travel agency to choose a tour. The manager politely invites you to the Microsoft Surface table, where you view the coast of Majorca using the Google Earth interface. You choose a convenient hotel for yourself - away from the noisy center and closer to the beach. Watch a video presentation of the hotel, photos of attractions and excursions. At this time, the travel agency manager, looking at his computer, tells you prices and makes reservations.


Choose a hotel and book a tour using the new Microsoft Surface for travel agents will be available in February next year. Many are waiting for a coup in retail sales of tour packages with the advent of this technology.



The developer of the application is TravelTainment, which is owned by one of the leaders of the tourism industry, Amadeus. TravelTainment is well known for its advanced research and new developments. The developer has not yet disclosed which countries will be the first to apply advanced technologies. It is known that the application was developed in English, French and German.
