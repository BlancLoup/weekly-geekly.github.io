Yandex plugs for color blind

Actually, I already wrote about this issue in their support.
I belong to the unlucky 10% of the set of men who do not distinguish shades of red and green colors.
On the big screen at the computer, I can somehow distinguish the red and green lines of traffic jams and free roads, but on the communicator on the screen 3 "for me this turns into torture.
I understand that this color is associated with traffic light signals, which I, by the way, perfectly distinguish on the road. It is intuitive to the rest of 90%, but still, I believe that 10% is a market for which you can fight.

So, my suggestion is to make a setting for people with limited color perception in the functionality, and if there is a green line, do not display it at all. Those. if the road is clear, then nothing is drawn. When traffic jams red line is displayed, when movement is difficult, yellow.
I hope the community will support me in this difficult endeavor. There are a lot of us. Let's claim our rights. :)
