Lock iOS: do not fall for blackmail!

By the noisy scandals with leaks of intimate photos of stellar users of Apple, it seems soon will add another one. In recent days, many Apple phone and tablet owners have been faced with blocking iCloud credentials. Accounts are blocked by Apple itself, responding to mass attempts to find passwords. Experts at Positive Technologies warned about the attempts of attackers to use the lock on iCloud for the purpose of extortion and urge to ignore suspicious emails.



Such letters have already begun to arrive, and the criminals are sending them on behalf of the largest Russian portal in the field of information security, SecurityLab.ru .

How to restore your account


You can restore your account on iforgot.apple.com - using your email address, answering a security question or other methods offered by Apple (you can save the old password or set a new one).



Experts of the company Positive Technologies informed Apple specialists about the current situation. All iCloud users who have their account blocked (most likely, this happened as a result of insufficient opposition to the automation on the iCloud side), we recommend that you go to the official Apple recovery procedure and not fall for the tricks of fraudsters.
