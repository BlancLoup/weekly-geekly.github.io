The concept of the project as an engineering document - the basis of a successful project

The day before yesterday, a PMDays 2008 conference was held in Moscow. I decided to put the text of the presentation of my speech here, I hope that he will help you in your current work, and I am also ready to answer any questions. After some time, videos of performances will appear on RuTube.

  • Presentation
    Concept example

    Story plan



    • What is the concept
    • How does it work


    Concept informally



    1. Who wants what and why
    2. Why now it does not work
    3. How it should be arranged for everyone to like
    4. What is the most important thing to do to achieve this?


    Purpose of the concept



    1. Tool for developing and harmonizing understanding:
    2. Communication tool providing:
    3. Basis for TK and decision making

    4. Filter for project initiation (litmus test)


    What is the concept different from TK



    • Focus on the causes, objectives of the project and the key properties of the product (rather than detailed descriptions of the properties)
    • Brevity (3-10 pages, against 30-80)
    • Does not change during the project


    Why it is often not written

    1. " And so everything is clear ":
      the value of fixing and communication is incomprehensible

    2. ‚Äú But the concept is in the letter of Fyodor Ivanovich! ":
      incomprehensible value of formalization

    3. ‚Äú What to write, work is necessary! ":
      procrastination
      (preference to do interesting, but not necessary).


    When to make a concept?



    IS ALWAYS

    If the project is small - half a page A4

    The risks of working without a concept



    • Goal Creep + Scope Creep
    • Loss of motivation
    • The groundlessness of TK
    • Conflicts
    • The failure to achieve the goals of the project!


    Concept creation process


    1. Who wants what?
    2. What now? Description of the current situation:
    3. What then?
    4. How to achieve?


    Artistic text properties


    Communication task - the formation, transmission of the image and sensations:
    • Freedom of presentation (unstructured)
    • Fascination
    • Emotional coloring


    Engineering Document Properties


    Communication task - the creation of a clear sound picture:
    • Structuredness
    • Interconnectedness
    • Sequence (from the general to the particular)
    • Traceability (traceability) statements
