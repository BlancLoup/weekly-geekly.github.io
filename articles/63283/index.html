Ukrainian football top resource started its own video section


I place at the request of bezi - the person responsible for creating several video services on the porstory of the CIS.
At the weekend, the launch of its own video section of the Ukrainian football site UA-Football.com. Now all football fans will be happy to review the most pressing football moments, reviews and curiosities directly on the site, as well as leave your opinion in the comments to the video, share a link to the video with friends or insert the video you like into your blog. The video section is available at ua-football.com

UA-Soccer is one of the leaders among Ukrainian online sports publications and is not going to stop there.

UA-Football.com site is one of the oldest Ukrainian football sites, it has been operating since 2002. Many fans know it not only as a news resource, but also as an active project that supports many Ukrainian sporting events, such as the qualifying round of the 2009 European Rugby Championship.

Until now, resource editors had to use third-party video resources, looking for suitable video fragments there or specifically post selected football videos, but now the video section has been fully available to UA-Football sports correspondents and they can select, store and upload videos to printed materials or current football video.

In addition, the administration of the resource believes that it will be much more convenient for the users themselves, who will now watch videos not only in the context of articles and news, but will also be able to find the video they are interested in without reference to a specific news, match name, team or player .

In the near future, we provide the opportunity for UA-Football.com users to watch online football matches, especially for matches that are not broadcast on national TV channels, as well as work towards the socialization of the resource.

The most difficult thing was to integrate with the already working system of authentication of football users and to give registered users the opportunity to add videos and post comments, but this difficulty was successfully overcome. Now football fans can contribute to the UA-Football.com video library.
