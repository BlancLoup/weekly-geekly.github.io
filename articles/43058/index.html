One-time mail

Sometimes you do not want to shine your e-mail to be registered on any resource. How to be? Use one-time mail. The bottom line is - click on the button "get an account" and voila, a valid e-mail for 10 minutes. If mail is needed for more than the allotted time, then it can be extended without problems.

www.10minutemail.com - pretty good service, and without too much hemorrhoids. Everything is simple and understandable, but there were some problems with receiving attachments (although why are they needed in the mail for 10 minutes?)

www.mytrashmail.com - here you can set the user name yourself, and not get the generated one.

www.gapmail.ru - the interface is minimal, which attracted me, but it was not possible to test it. The letter sent to the temporary box did not come as long as I did not press the update button.

Yes, and you should not forget about security. Good luck)
