Look instead of a mouse

Swedish engineer Martin Tall suggests using Eye-Tracking technology (an infrared camera that monitors the user's pupils) to interact with screen objects. This is called the NeoVisus gaze interaction interface .

Despite the fact that everything is smooth in the video, there are certain doubts about the practicality of such a solution: those who have seen the randomness of eye movements of users will surely understand what this is about.
