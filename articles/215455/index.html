The first Samsung U28 monitor U28D590 is already on sale

Good afternoon, Habr!

Samsung Electronics announced the start of sales in Russia of the first in the 28-inch UHD monitor product line - U28D590. The main feature of the novelty is 4K (3840x2160) screen resolution.



Natural color reproduction with smooth color transitions is achieved due to the fact that the monitor is capable of displaying more than 1 billion colors. For connecting all kinds of media players, game consoles, PCs or laptops, there are two HDMI ports and one Display Port (DP) interface connector. The maximum frame rate at a resolution of 3840 x 2160 pixels will be 60 Hz in the case of connection via the DisplayPort interface and 30 Hz via the HDMI interface. You can connect two PCs to the monitor at once and simultaneously play back content from both devices without loss of quality: the image resolution from each PC will be 1920 x 2160.

Specifically, Samsung's new display will become a desirable object for gamers: the matrix response time is 1 ms, the viewing angles are 170¬∫ horizontally and 160¬∫ vertically, the maximum brightness is 300 cd / m2, the static contrast ratio is 1000: 1. The special Game Mode mode automatically adjusts and optimizes the color parameters of the monitor directly under the content that is displayed on it.



Also a useful feature of Samsung U28D590 can be attributed to the presence of the updated function ‚ÄúPicture in Picture‚Äù (PIP 2.0). With its help you can reproduce the image of a small window in the original resolution up to FullHD (1920 x 1080) while the main picture will be reproduced in the UHD. In addition, when playing low-resolution content, the new feature UHD Upscale will improve the clarity and image quality to the highest possible resolution of 4K.

The Samsung U28D590 UHD monitor went on sale at all Samsung branded stores yesterday and is available at a suggested retail price of 25,000 rubles.
