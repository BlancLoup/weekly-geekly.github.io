Iran has created a special unit that carries out attacks against "enemy sites"

Probably, many countries have specialists in stock who, if something happens, will be able to create a lot of problems for a resource indicated by the government. Many suspect that China has a whole division of hackers who work secretly on the tasks assigned to them by the Chinese government. In the US, there is a structure that is engaged in the elimination of threats to the American segment of the Network from the part of intruders. But all this is more or less secret organizations. But Iran, without secretly presenting it, presented a special unit that would impede the work of "enemy sites."

Iran motivates the creation of such units by the constant "raids" of foreign hackers on Iranian government sites. Now the government of this country cooperates with volunteers who voluntarily expressed a desire to work for Iran in the field of protecting the state interests of this country on the Web.

It is clear that there are a lot of resources in the world, which show dissatisfaction with the regime in Iran. However, if other countries (for example, the same China) simply block access to such a site for their own citizens, Iran plans to destroy such resources. So, recently it was stated that "the enemies of Iran" created "874 sites" to destabilize the situation of the Iranian government.
