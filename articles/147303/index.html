The Fat Thumb - pinch-to-zoom one-finger replacement

We all loved pinch-to-zoom (this is when the content on the screen can be scaled with two fingers by dragging or pinching them).

But for sure, many of you are faced with a situation where the phone is used with one hand, and the second is busy. To dodge and make a multitouch gesture in this case is extremely problematic.

Canadian scientists from the University of Calgary offer to solve this problem using the proprietary method " Fat Thumb ", the essence of which lies in the fact that for zooming, you use your finger on the screen with the entire phalanx, and not just the tip of the pad.
