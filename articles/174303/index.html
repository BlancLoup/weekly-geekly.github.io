AppClub {build, monetize} # 5 will gather mobile experts and startups again



The last meeting of the club of mobile enthusiasts and startups AppClub {build, monetize} was a record one, bringing together about 40 people - developers, entrepreneurs, startups, even media producers and journalists were noticed :-) I am glad to announce the fifth AppClub meeting, which will be held on April 4 in Kiev and will bring together excellent experts and startups under one roof.

What awaits the participants this time? Welcome under cat.

How to get 2 million downloads of Windows Phone applications?


Applications for Windows Phone, published by DevRain Solutions , have already downloaded more than two million times. And this is over a period of just over a year. Which applications download more, how they use, how loyal users are, why some applications are more successful and others less, how difficult is it to earn the first million downloads, and the second? You can get answers to these and other questions.

How I was looking for the right developers - middle-aged starter start-up impressions


After nearly 20 years of work at Mars and Danone, Eugene Golub left his career as a top manager and started his own project: creating a mobile application - a recommendation exchange service. What made Eugene take the path of entrepreneurship? How does the former director of logistics and supply find a common language with ambitious young programmers? What does a corporate manager learn from yesterday's students and what could he teach? What does a customer without IT experience and knowledge expect from developers and how can developers successfully cooperate with such clients?



These and other questions will be the subject of our conversation in this part of the program.
