"Beeline" fined 100,000 rubles for sending advertising messages

The ninth paragraph of the Agreement on the provision of communication services "Beeline" forced all the subscribers of the operator to "agree" to receive advertising.

This state of affairs, as I believe many other subscribers, did not suit me. Initially, I called the CPC at 0611 , wrote emails to info@beeline.ru , to refuse to receive SMS spam. After that, I visited the office of the operator, where I wrote a statement refusing to receive advertising. But, unfortunately, it did not help get rid of annoying ads.

Then I turned to Rospotrebnadzor, where my complaint did not find understanding, and then to the Stavropol OFAS (Federal Antimonopoly Service Directorate), whose competence includes the consideration of cases on violation of advertising legislation.
