Here is a piece of insider information that has not been aired anywhere yet.



As you can see, the barrel broke to the devil. True, it took too long to wait. But it was worth it! ;)
The essence of the experience is as follows:
  • pour liquid nitrogen into a plastic bottle;
  • we twist a cover and we place a bottle in a barrel;
  • we fall asleep with ping-pong balls;
  • a bottle explodes;
  • the barrel jumps up and all the balls fly up, then fall to the floor. We get an orchestra of falling tennis balls.

When liquid nitrogen is heated, it begins to boil, changing from a liquid to a gaseous state. Its volume changes accordingly (gas takes up more space than liquid). But since the bottle is closed, there is no place for gas to go out and the pressure in the bottle begins to grow. This can not happen forever and the bottle, unable to withstand, burst, releasing the gas out. It turns out the blast wave, which hits the bottom and walls of the barrel and, having met resistance, rushes into the only free space - that is, up. And we get rain from the balls.

They began to analyze why the barrel could not stand, and it turned out that it was made of fragile plastic. And the waiting time can be reduced by adding a bag of hot water to the place of the barrel where the bottle of nitrogen is placed. This will speed up the process of gas formation in the bottle.

We bought a barrel of softer plastic and decided to repeat the experiment, but without the balls. And, you know, the barrel really survived! And the waiting time was reduced from 15 to 5 minutes. Now we are ready for a full-scale experiment :). This moment:
