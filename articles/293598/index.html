Experts talked about effective tools and the latest trends in SEO optimization in the Russian market

The publication Sostav.ru discussed the latest trends in SEO optimization with Russian specialists.

SEO specialists solve quite a wide range of tasks, but they have very specific goals: increasing traffic, internal website optimization and error correction, analyzing competitor websites (including in social media), improving the website in search results, optimizing the semantic core. However, to achieve them, you will need a diverse SEO toolkit. Experts commented on the means to achieve these goals and offered appropriate tools.

Increase traffic


According to experts, Key Collector is suitable for this purpose. The basic function of Key Collector is the collection of requests and tips from search engines for areas of interest.

Just-Magic will enhance the result - a professional service for the selection of semantics, which will divide into groups the core of requests collected in Key Collector and make a calculation on each page on a separate page.

Oleg Smagin, Kokoc.com optimizer (Kokoc Group):
If the client chooses to promote the site by action, for example, for a percentage of sales, the optimizer is faced with the task of not just bringing traffic to the site, but converting it as much as possible. In this case, you need to start with the correction of errors, for example, using the Screaming Frog tool. For carrying out A / B testing of changes on the site, the Google Content Experiments service will be useful.

Competitor Analysis


The most commonly used tools are FastTrust and the ahrefs service. Optimism.ru CEO Roman Klevtsov adds to this list a tool for collecting and analyzing incoming links to the websites of Majestic.com competitors, services for analyzing and selecting semantics for Spywords.ru and Prodvigator.ru sites, and an automatic SEO audit and site diagnostics service. Improving the position of the site in search results


Wizard.Sape allows you to create the optimal text for each group of requests. The optimizer introduces the query of interest, and the tool analyzes the TOP-10 sites based on the results of search engines. Based on the data obtained Wizard.Sape makes recommendations.
