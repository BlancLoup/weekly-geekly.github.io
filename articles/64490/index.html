Started porting Grim Fandango under the Nixes


You can run Grim Fandango under Gnu / Linux, Mac and other Nixes using Residual , i.e. Vine does not smell here

Residual - a project from the authors of the notorious ScummVM , for those who do not know, they do not make new games, they adapt them to other platforms. As always, the theme touched on quests from LucasArts, yes, yes, you guessed it - LUA quests made on the engine GrimE - Grim Fandango and Escape from Monkey Island . The first game, judging by the statistics, works at 50%, when Escape from Monkey Island works only theoretically. Unfortunately, there are also a lot of bugs in Grim Fandango. Apparently, it is very difficult to complete the game so far , so at this stage of development on Residual you can see from interest - no more.
The development is at a very early stage, it is not even about alpha yet, and the bug reports are not accepted yet - see the TODO list and so full, so far we are only happy about SVN. In general, we wait and hope. =)
