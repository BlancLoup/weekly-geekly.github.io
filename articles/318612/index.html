Ten IT jobs that are hardest to find people



The dizzying race for technological innovations in the era of digital transformation makes it difficult for companies to search for talented people with the right mix of great experience and skills. But, according to Matt Sigelman, director of the labor market research firm Burning Glass , it is even more difficult to find a person whose personal qualities and leadership skills correspond to his technical skills.

‚ÄúIn IT, most people at the level of middle and top management have achieved their place because of their technical skills, and not because of personal qualities or managerial talent. This means that some vacancies are very difficult to fill, since they require technical ingenuity, special experience and managerial skills. If you require a candidate to sit in two chairs at once - technology and management - you complicate the task of filling vacancies, since the number of potential candidates decreases immediately, ‚Äùsays Sigelman.

Based on a study of Burning Glass 40,000 vacancies posted on sites from August 2015 to September 2016, a list of the most difficult to fill vacancies was built, and the average number of days required to search for the right employee was given.

1. Technical Manager / Director


Time to fill out: 107 days

The Technical Manager is responsible for the management and technical processes in the IT department, as well as for personnel management and for interviewing candidates.

2. SAP Manager


Time to complete: 64 days

Responsible for the implementation and integration of SAP solutions in the organization. He, among other things, must have experience with the SAP Solution Manager software in order to be confident in the effective and efficient operation of SAP solutions in the organization.

3. System analyst


Time to complete: 63 days

Specializes in the analysis, development and implementation of information systems and IT solutions for business clients. Sets up new computer systems, both hardware and software, and can also implement new applications to increase productivity.

4. Java architect


Time to complete: 63 days

Identifies, integrates, updates, and maintains the infrastructure and applications in a company that are required to support Java-based applications. This includes everything from online shopping software to financial management programs and web applications.

5. Developer Salesforce CRM


Time to complete: 54 days

According to Sigelman, the Salesforce CRM platform is becoming a staple of today's digital business, and such vacancies require a good knowledge of the fundamentals of the business behind this system and experience in its integration and use. ‚ÄúIn many industries there is a great demand for experience with Salesforce, which speaks of the universality of the platform,‚Äù says Siegelman.

6. Database Architect


Time to complete: 54 days

Engaged in the development, creation, implementation and management of the organization's data architecture. It requires an assessment of the company's data sources, both internal and external, and the development, creation, and management of the data infrastructure that stores and analyzes this data.
