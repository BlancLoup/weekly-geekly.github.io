DevCon 2015: announcement of the second wave of speakers and conference reports

Dear friends! We are pleased to present you the second wave of the speakers of the DevCon 2015 conference and open to you some topics and further details of the technical program of the conference. We have previously announced more than ten speakers of DevCon 2015, a description and detailed information about their reports can be found in this article .



At the very beginning, I would like to share with you information about foreign guests who will visit our country and, especially for the participants of the DevCon 2015 conference, will talk about the most interesting and share their experiences.



Jon Galloway, web development specialist, ASP.NET and Azure from the Microsoft team of experts in the United States will share the latest news from the world of web development on the Microsoft platform. John will give two presentations that will be devoted to the most current innovations - the new version of the ASP.NET MVC 6 framework and the internal structure of the ASP.NET 5 platform. We look forward to John in Russia at DevCon 2015! You can follow his activity on twitter @jongalloway and his blog .

DevCon 2015 welcomes Dmitry Nikonov , vice president of innovative technologies for VIAcode from the United States, who specifically agreed to come visit us and give a presentation on the deep and important topic of DevOps, release management and organization of the ALM closed loop.

We are again happy to see Evgeny Chigirinsky , the head of the development team and the most experienced veteran from Microsoft, at the conference. This year, Eugene will share with DevCon 2015 audience his rich experience in developing the largest cloud projects, including MSN.com, the largest portal on the Internet. Another topic that Eugene will speak about will be the deployment of cloud applications in the Azure cloud, including using the popular Chef technology.



We are very pleased to welcome colleagues Vladimir Kolesnikov and Nicolas Metulev from the group of Microsoft experts from the USA, who, together with the general speaker of the plenary report Giorgio Sardo, will come to us to tell us about the latest updates from Microsoft. Colleagues will certainly speak at the opening of the conference, as well as make a number of reports in the conference program on the development of newest applications for Windows.

In addition to colleagues from abroad, experts from Russia, representing technical companies and communities of our country, are invited to the DevCon 2015 conference. Among them:



Alexey Losev - the head of the development team from FG Life, who at the conference will talk about the technology of statistical analysis of processes using a real example of analyzing team bugs for two years with Shewhart control card.

We are pleased to once again welcome to Stanislaw Vyschepan, DevCon 2015 - a consultant for the development of business applications and Microsoft MVP, who will talk about the most popular TypeScript technology and share his experience of effectively using TypeScript in practice.

Denis Kretov , technical director of the Russian company Notissimus, will share another real life example. He will talk about iBeacon technology in payment solutions and a cross-platform solution created using Azure and Xamarin for iOS, Google Android and Windows Phone.



We are very pleased to present Igor Shcheglovitov , a senior engineer from Kaspersky Lab, who, in his report on DevCon 2015, will tell you about the organization of testing processes and building quality infrastructure on the Microsoft platform.
