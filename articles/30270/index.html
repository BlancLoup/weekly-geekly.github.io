Link Exchange - an overview

Recently I compiled a review of reference exchanges and decided to compile information for an article on Habr√©. Such a brief reference material on the subject of "who is who in the market."



Link exchange is a service that allows you to buy and sell links (traffic).
Someone may call this ‚Äúpaid advertising‚Äù and he will be right, because this definition is the main essence of the service.

Using link exchanges, you are exempt from a very important part of the work - you do not need to look for advertisers on your own (if you have your own website, and you want to make money by placing advertising links on it) or advertising platforms (if you have any or a project requiring promotion on the Internet).

There are two main types of reference exchanges.
In the first type of payment occurs for the number of unique clicks (with a unique IP once every 24 hours). The cost per click in this case is the difference between the price of the advertiser and the exchange commission for the provision of services. As practice shows, on average, for one click it is about 50 kopecks (prices range mainly from 0.1 to 1.2 rubles).
In the second type of payment occurs during the placement of advertisements on your resource.
