Become a speaker and win

Recently announced its opening service Dubbee , which translates materials from news sites and blogs into an audio recording format, where all texts are voiced by professional announcers. Audio, which is published on the service, you can download and listen in any convenient form: from a computer, mp3 player or mobile phone.
