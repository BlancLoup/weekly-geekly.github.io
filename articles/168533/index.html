Railway - reminiscence of the old game ShortLine

Once upon a time, in the days of DOS, there was such a wonderful game ShortLine. The essence of the game is to support the uninterrupted delivery of goods between cities until the year 2000. Now you can play it in a new arrangement - on your Android device. We present you the game Railway.




You are the manager of the railway. Your goal is to carry as much cargo and passengers as possible until 2000 New Year. Let the beginning not seem to you to be simple - the whole game depends on the successful or unsuccessful beginning. Let's see what gift you can afford in the next century.
For each next planet, the critical date is increased by 100 years.





Building the right branches of railway tracks is not an easy task. But if you managed to do this, then you can easily handle the most incredible situations and make a lot of money. Remember, the more money you earn at each level, the easier it will be to play the next one. After all, money is a safety cushion that will allow you not to go bankrupt when accidents do happen.





Each new level has its own surprises - flying saucers, which seize sections of roads and steal trains, meteorites, mercilessly destroying rails, winged pigs, which dump heaps of gold into various places, crazy uncontrollable trains and, of course, more and more heated atmosphere of the game .
We wish you good luck! We hope our game will take you minutes of tension and pleasure :)

Google Play link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.o0.game.railroad.app
