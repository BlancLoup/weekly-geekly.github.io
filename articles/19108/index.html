Total Commander Knowledge Base

I think everyone knows the file manager Total Commander (a few years ago called Windows Commander). The popularity and convenience of this tool under Windows is such that many users simply do not know how to use a computer without TC. I - including.
But also, unfortunately, many users have various difficulties when working with TC. This is normal - after fifteen years of its existence, TC has become a rather complex program. Of course, reading the documentation (Russian reference for TC is even better than the original) helps, but not always. And not all users guess to read it ...

For many years now, along with the official TC forum, there is a Russian-language support forum Total Commander . The community helps solve problems, gives advice, does translations, etc. The amount of information accumulated over the years is truly huge. And this creates a new problem - the problem of finding the right information.

Although porting a lot of articles to a wiki engine took quite a long time, I hope that now it will go faster: all members of the community will be able to edit articles, not just me.

What information can be found on RU.TCKB?
A large number of articles, both for beginners and experienced users.
Answers to questions that are not designed as a simple FAQ, but also as some kind of textbook.

Descriptions of existing plug-ins and utilities (currently a complete catalog of plug-ins is being compiled).
