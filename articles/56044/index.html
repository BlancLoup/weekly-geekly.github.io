25-year-old Englishman died playing a "jogging run" on the Wii

A healthy 25-year-old man collapsed and died in front of his girlfriend and best friend while playing Wii Fit.


Nothing foreshadowed tragedy. Tim returned from Portugal, where he celebrated his mother's 50th birthday. Shortly before the game, he talked to her on the phone - he told her that he ordered a kebab and was sitting with a glass of port.


An autopsy was performed, but the cause of death remains to be determined. The family was told that he died from Sudden Adult Death Syndrome, also known as Sudden Death Syndrome from Arrhythmia, which is a violation of the electrical system of the heart.

Conditions for the manifestation of this syndrome is an abnormal heart rhythm. The heart can stop pumping blood, causing the brain to be deprived of blood and sudden death occurs.
