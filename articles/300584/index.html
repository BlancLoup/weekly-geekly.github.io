Skating and picnic with the team Withme | # Sports the first to meet


The Withme team invites everyone to PicnicWithme 2016!

The event will take place on May 7 at the Exhibition of Economic Achievements at 13:00 Moscow time. The cultural program will include:

- The opportunity to meet new interesting people
- Riding on everything that has wheels
- Communication with developers on the development of Withme
- Picnic on a soft plaid in a cage
- Good mood and positive emotions

To get new sensations it is not necessary to go on a world tour on a sailing yacht, sometimes it is enough to spend time with new people. This allows you to move away from the pressing problems and just have a hearty fun in an interesting company. New jokes, new buzzwords that we often hear from strangers - all this is a great way to forget this gray, terrible March winter soon and enjoy the early May spring!

Contact with users.

We have a certain opinion about communication with developers. We are sure that the technical part of the project and the user's opinion occupy one step on the ladder of importance. While working on the service, it is necessary to take into account the opinion of the people who will use it. Here, a good example is the reaction of the Melkomyagky company to the request of downloaded customers to return the Start menu in one of the Windows updates to 8.1 and later in the version with index 10. Yes, the innovative metro-interface, yes, amazingly comfortable on tablet computers However, to please the users, the good old Start was back.

Many companies forget about users, like James Maskell, for example, being fascinated by the technical component of his service, he did not take into account the opinions of those for whom, in fact, he created the service. The result was not long in coming.

At a picnic, we will be able to learn about your wishes and ideas, communicate and get the maximum feedback in order to make our service better, more convenient and more useful.

All detailed information is contained in the event description in our application. The event is assigned a gold status - you definitely will not miss it. Questions about the organization you can ask here in the comments, in the discussion of the event itself, as well as in social networks.

Come, we are all looking forward to!
