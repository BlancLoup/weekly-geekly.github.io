New Yandex.Webmaster



Today at the Fourth Webmasters , Yandex representatives told the new Yandex.Webmaster - it is already available in beta status. What is interesting is the long-awaited update?

Following Google, Yandex begins to take into account the convenience of viewing the site on mobile devices. This will directly affect the position of the site in the search results. It will start working not today and not tomorrow, but in a few months, and only in the issue from mobile devices. Sites adapted for viewing from mobile phones will be labeled "mobile version". In this regard, the new webmaster has a site checking tool - ‚ÄúChecking mobile pages‚Äù.



Now it is far from the first time to receive a verification report - the service is in beta, and the burden appears to be very high so far. You can check only the site that is registered with the webmaster, in contrast to a similar Google service . But the better ‚Äúcheck‚Äù from Yandex is that it gives information about the problems and the conditions fulfilled.
