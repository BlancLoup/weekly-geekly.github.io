iPhone - teaches foreign languages

Apple iPhone gave an impetus to the development of language learning tools. The Hong Kong company Chris Lonsdale and Partners is now mastering the fullest potential of this smartphone. In the list of recent developments - the Chinese course ‚ÄúThird Ear‚Äù, which in two weeks allows you to master the language at the initial level. By the way, Chris Lonsdale himself perfectly learned Chinese in 6 months.

The general idea is that words are better remembered when they are associated with melodies. Each word from the "Third Ear" corresponds to a sample. Having mastered some vocabulary, the user proceeds to the next stage, while on the phone, videos are played in which native speakers show how to pronounce words and phrases correctly. The program is divided into many stages and you can switch from one to another only by completing a series of actions - correctly pronounce the phrase, translate, etc.
