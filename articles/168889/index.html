What is different iPad 4 128 GB?



Friends, hello. You are probably already aware that Apple has released a 4th generation iPad with a 128 GB memory module. I had an iPad almost immediately after the release and, after conducting some tests, I wanted to make a small review of this device.

The tests themselves showed the following results:

1. The user has 115 GB of memory.

2. Downloading content from The Bard's Tale, NFS: Most Wanted, Modern Combat 4 (with a total weight of about 7 GB):

iPad 4 64 GB - 7 minutes 10 seconds;
iPad 4 128 GB - 5 minutes 46 seconds;
_

iPad 2 (2012) 16GB - 07 minutes 34 seconds;
iPhone 4S 16GB - 07 minutes 02 seconds;
iPhone 5 16GB - 6 minutes 20 seconds.

Apparently, the novelty has a new memory module, which is slightly more productive than modules in other Apple devices.

3. Also managed to weigh the iPad:

iPad 4 64GB 4G - 663 grams;
iPad 4 128GB 4G - 666 grams;

iPad 2 and almost 100 grams less: iPad 2 16GB Wi-Fi - 586 grams.

4. iPad 4 128 GB comes with iOS 6.1 and lends itself perfectly to the jailbreak.
