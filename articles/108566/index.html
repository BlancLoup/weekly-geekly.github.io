Tango - Operating System from the Future


Hello. I have long thought about the topic, but what if we assume that all modern operating systems could be much better. How would that be? What would they look like and be arranged inside?
Approximately, in the same spirit reflects a huge number of people about the future structure of our planet. For example, what will happen in 10 years, will we use flying machines, wireless energy and will we give up paper money. I decided that it was possible in the same way to reflect on the device of operating systems, to dream about a better life. The result, the reflection was the book - "Tango - Operating system from the future." I would be glad if you read it, it is very interesting to know your opinion.
