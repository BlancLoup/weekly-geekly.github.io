Listen to our music while you read.


Generally, if you are an independent musician, then placing on iTunes will cost you money. For example, on Tunecore, the price for placing an album is $ 30 for the first year and $ 50 for each subsequent one. But at the same time, the musician takes 100% of the profits for himself. Just put your catalog written in for many years comes out very expensive.
Therefore, as soon as we found people who organized a publication for us for 30% of royalties, we immediately opened the label and offered all of our musicians friends to upload the contents of the archives to the network. Simple mathematics suggests that this option is more profitable if the income from the album does not exceed $ 160 per year.

A terrible secret is this: in the four years of work of the label with aytyuns, we only bred four thousand rubles once. In the rest of the releases earn around five hundred rubles a year. Venues like Bandcamp give significantly more serious sums! And, looking at all these reports, I want to state with confidence that the only benefit of aytyuns for an independent musician is to show her friends at the bar that you really are a musician. Let's look at the activities of indie group, as a startup. You release the goods - musical compositions. And some of you, on the basis of this product, provide the service ‚Äúlive music program‚Äù. How do we ensure a steady increase in sales? I am sure that the basic strategy of an indie musician should be to collect a base of subscribers . The subscriber base is a key resource, and it is the main indicator of group success.

What is the subscriber base? This is a list of any contacts of people who like your music, and having received a message from you, they will not throw it into spam. We collect the following info: name, phone, email, address in social networks and city.

How to use the subscriber base?
When you come out new music - you tell it to the entire base. It would be nice to be able to verify that the message was delivered.
When you have a concert announced - you choose from the subscriber database from the desired city and inform them.
If you have a good collection of contacts, you will be able to provide a certain amount of ticket sales, and orgs will adore you for it. In small clubs, orgi themselves do not have a base of their regular visitors!

How to build a subscriber database online? I will write our ideas:
1. The obvious way is the social network. We get acquainted through drugs with people who have joined your public / meeting or like music, and if they go to contact, we add them to the list.
2. Give music to download for free if you leave your email. On bandcamp this functionality is available by default!
3. We organize online contests in which you can participate by leaving your contacts.

How to build a subscriber database offline?
In fact, none of my familiar musicians are not collecting subscriber lists offline, but only filtering those who joined the meetings. Meanwhile, the people who raised their fifth point from the sofa and came to your concert is the most loyal audience. If a concert has been rolled in, they are completely yours. First of all, they should form the basis of your base. Look for ways to collect their contacts! Here are a few ideas:
1. Just hand out a questionnaire at the entrance, which will include questions like "which song you liked more," and of course the contact details. Then do not forget to send them a letter with gratitude!
2. Give a discount on merchandise if you fill in the form
3. Play prizes among those who filled out the questionnaire
4. Collect contacts under the pretext that you will send pictures from the concert
5. If you are recording a concert, offer to leave contacts to those who want to get the record before anyone else.

Why are aytyuns evil?
Because you do not know who is listening to your music. Some anonymous people listened to your tracks for half a beat, and from this neither money nor pleasure. You are lying there on the far shelf, and only those people whom you sent there yourself will listen to you. At the same time, aytyuns takes a lot of money for yourself!
Now compare with bandcamp : you know the mail of everyone who downloaded your music. You immediately get the grandmother with each sale, and the proportion there is very honest. And you can sell merch through the same platform.
