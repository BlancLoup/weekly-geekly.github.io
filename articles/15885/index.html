In Windows Live they gave the opportunity to register ID in the zone @ live.ru

Today it is possible to register Windows Live ID in the zone live .ru. To register a new Live ID, go to: get.live.com/getlive/overview and select the "Registration" section. The domain is lukewarm, lots of good names are still loose. You can use your Live ID login to log into all Microsoft Live services (mail, calendar, blogs, MSN, etc.).

As Anton Danilov notes in his blog, there is an opportunity to move from the old Live ID @ hotmail.com to a new one.

"In accordance with the published strategy for developing online business, by 2010 Microsoft will be among the top three leaders of the Russian Internet market, and Russia will be among the top four countries in terms of revenue from online business for the corporation," the Internet tweet reported .
Therefore, if you are not so much attracted by Microsoft on-line services, do not rush to give up, think about the future. ;)
