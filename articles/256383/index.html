Get a grant for network security training in the Netherlands



Innopolis University collaborates with the University of Amsterdam (Netherlands) in the implementation of the Master's Program in Systems and Networks Engineering ( FEC ) with a focus on information security. It is recognized as the best master program in Holland in the field of IT (Keuzegids Masters2013) and the best educational program (Keuzegids Masters 2015). Innopolis University invites you to the magistracy in network security (site in Amsterdam).

In 2015/2016 , this program will be taught at the University of Amsterdam, which is in the top 50 universities in the world. The duration of the program is 12 months.

Applications for training under the System and Network Engineering program are accepted until the end of April.
How to apply?
For this you need:
  • Fill in your personal data in your account.
  • Attach the following documents:

  1. One letter of recommendation;
  2. Translation of the diploma and its annex in English. lang (certified by the university dean);
  3. Certificate confirming the level of English proficiency (if you already have it).

If you have not passed the TOEFL / IELTS exam, then:
  1. register for the next dates (you can consider exam options in another city);
  2. get ready for the exam;
  3. get tested.

For questions about the application process and details of the program, you can consult with Elmira Halitova (e.khalitova@innopolis.ru).
Those interested in obtaining a grant for training and reimbursement of related expenses in the form of scholarships should contact Amal Ahmadullina (a.akhmadullina@innopolis.ru).
