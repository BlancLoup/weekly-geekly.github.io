Phone with big buttons - it exists!

Went into the Ion store today by chance and saw there what I was looking for literally like air some time ago. This is a mobile phone with BIG BUTTONS!
In general, the situation, I think, is familiar to many: I have a grandmother, and a grandfather, who spend every summer in the country, and I, as a caring grandson, want them to always be in touch, you never know ... Pah-pah-pah ... Therefore, I needed a mobile phone: without any polyphony there, but simply with a loud call, without a color display, but a regular one and with large numbers so that it could be clearly seen, and of course with large buttons. How many did not look for - nothing like this has been done for a long time: (Alas. And the good old Nokia 3310 has all died.
So today I saw it :)



Poryskav on an Internet, found that it turns out that the comrades from Megafon launched such a thing a couple of months ago.
Here is what they write about this:
This phone has large buttons, bright and contrast monochrome display, which greatly facilitates the use of mobile communications for the elderly and visually impaired. A durable case and several variants of its color design will make a new phone optimal as the first mobile device for children, fans of extreme sports and lovers of fashionable, original accessories.

The mobile phone provides support for basic mobile network services. Due to the reduced level of radio emission, it is compatible with pacemakers and hearing aids. On the back of the device there is an SOS button that can be programmed to dial four specific phone numbers. The phone has a high-capacity battery. There are flashlight and FM radio as additional functions, and no antenna is required to listen to the radio.
It is such a phone 3500 p.

In general, I was glad, though I did not buy it, because I found my old sister some kind of old one, with quite decent buttons - my grandmother does not miss :)
But the future can be borne in mind.
