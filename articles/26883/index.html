"Domain Wars." Episode Two.

I have previously promised to repeat the analysis as the full owner of the registered domains, I solemnly confirm. My all-consuming interest in domestic registrar companies is growing exponentially. Choose the best of the best, you see, the task is not so easy. First of all, each of the 5 registrars who took leading positions in the previous rating are professionals in their field. Naturally, you can delve and find in each their own some minor flaws, but it is unlikely to spoil or distort the general idea about them.
But still, I am driven by the desire to help Ukrainian users find for themselves the right registrar in order to avoid any problems in the future. I REPEAT, for those who have not read the past rating: I HAVE NO RELATIONSHIP TO ONE OF THE COMPANIES PARTICIPATING IN THE EXPERIMENT!

Five leader registrars are brought to the public for trial, in which I recently had the opportunity to register one domain name (exclusively for experimental purposes). I note that all domain names were registered in the com.ua zone.

1. TanHost - tanhost.com
2. Imena - imena.ua
3. Redo - redo.com.ua
4. Adamant - adamant.net
5. UkrHosting - ukrhosting.com

It is in this sequence that these recorders are located in the ranking. I'll warn you right away that, according to the results of this analysis, the place of the leaders may change.

It all started one fine morning exactly 10 days from the date of the publication of this article, when the plan to register domain names originated in my head. I decided to start my hard business with TanHost. The first pancake was almost no lump. Registered on the site without any problems, made an order. The domain name ordering system is very convenient, understandable even to a pioneer. The payment method was chosen by bank transfer. To all my further questions on service and transfer, technical support answered with lightning speed and diligence, whether because of a sense of guilt in front of me, or because of general professionalism, it remains a mystery. But, despite a slight hitch in the domain name registration, the general impression of the registrar is quite positive.
