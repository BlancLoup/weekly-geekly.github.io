Guided tour of the data centers of Scandinavia, 12-15 November



From November 12 to November 15, 2014, with the support of Media Grus and the telecommunications provider Mastertel, the second field event for data center managers, the DCExpedition Scandinavia Tour, will be held. This time we offer to go on a trip to Finland and Sweden to explore the most famous Scandinavian data centers. The expedition participants are expected to spend four days with a rich excursion program in data centers and business parks, as well as the opportunity to get acquainted with the culture and traditions of Finland and Sweden. And of course, the DCExpedition Scandinavia Tour is a great opportunity to strengthen business ties between data center industry professionals.

We will visit:


1. Data Center Bahnhof - Lajka (Space Station)
2. Data Center Pionen
3. DataCenter Finland
4. Data Center CSC - IT Center for Science
5. Data center "Herman IT"
6. Renforsin Ranta Business Park
