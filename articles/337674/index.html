Kindergarten, pants with straps: where do programmers come from


There was a boy. Became a programmer. Something like this can begin and end with a short biographical note on any developer. At the same time, it is obvious that not everyone in childhood and even in his youth planned to link his fate with high technology. It was curious to delve into children's diapers and learn about IT dreams for children from different countries. Enjoy!
ZY Taking this opportunity, we congratulate all those involved on the Programmer's Day!

Marco Kalasan


My jaw hit the floor when I learned that this Macedonian boy, at the age of eight, had become the youngest Microsoft-certified Microsoft system administrator in the world, having received a Microsoft Certified Professional certificate. At the age of nine, he also successfully passed the certification exam for a system engineer from Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer. By the way, Marco is now 17 years old. By this time, he managed to write a book on Windows 7 and continues to code.



Mark Zuckerberg


Did you know that the founder of Facebook studied at the Harvard School of Psychology? Mark Zuckerberg was born in the state of New York in the family of a dentist and psychiatrist. In addition to his parents have three daughters. From an early age, Mark showed an interest in programming. At school, he distinguished himself by creating a fairly popular networked computer strategy game. Despite the fact that already in their youth world corporations noticed a talented programmer and offered him a job, the young man chose the psychology department at Harvard University. As a student, Mark created Facebook and in the beginning abandoned his studies. But quite recently, Zuckerberg returned to Harvard and received an honorary doctorate from Harvard University. By the way, there he voiced a very encouraging speech .



Linus torvalds


It seems that dad Linux in quiet Finland since childhood wanted to be a programmer. Considering that the boy touched the first computer at the age of 12. In 1981, Leo, Linus' grandfather, a mathematician, introduced his grandson to the Commodore VIC-20 computer used by him for mathematical calculations. Linus became interested in programming and read the manuals for the machine. Then he began to read computer magazines and write his own programs, first in BASIC and then in Assembler. From school, Linus received scholarships for mathematics success. The first computer they bought was Sinclair QL, which then cost almost $ 2,000.

Sergey Brin


Today, few people need to explain who Sergei Mikhailovich Brin is. One of the people changing our world and other epithets related to Google. And forty-four years ago, the little one who appeared in the family of Soviet mathematicians was pulling at the owner of the digital world. Meanwhile, considering that both the grandfather and the father were prominent mathematicians, it was impossible to think that the son would start growing flowers in the future.



At the age of six, Bryn and her parents moved from the USSR to the USA. There he went to school. With the school program boy coped easily. Sergey never had any problems in studying the exact sciences, moreover, he simply fell in love with mathematics. The parents were confident that the child would follow in their footsteps, become an engineer, a researcher or a teacher. Sergey justified the expectations of parents. Mathematics and computer science became his deep passion. At school, Sergey regularly argued with teachers, putting their knowledge and competence under serious doubt. In mathematics, young Brin was a cut above his teachers.



My father told me about Sergey, that he grew up as an ordinary boy, but he always tried to be closer to the computer. It all started with games and the old Commodore 64s, one of the first personal computers. It was a birthday present for a nine year old child. True, the grandmother was unhappy. What grows out of this child, she grumbled, if he didn‚Äôt spend hours with the computer. At the very beginning of the 80s, when even in the USA computers remained technical gadgets, Brin mastered programming and chose the main road of his life. His interests focused on mathematics, in relation to the latest computer technology. On this path and went his self-development.



In 1993, Bryn entered Stanford University, California. It was there that he met his current friend and ally, Larry Page. There are legends about the birth of Google, so we will not dwell on this story for long.

Vitalik Buterin


Kolomna kid Vitalik Buterin is 23 years old. Most of the life of a crypto guru lives in Canada, but has not lost contact with the Motherland. About himself Vitalik spreads a little. Says only that since childhood, fond of mathematics, programming and computer games. For several years, Buterin was playing World of Warcraft. Nikolai Dobrovolsky


The co-founder and vice-president of Parallels, before he successfully crossed the grass and launched a hedgehog with a team of like-minded Windows on the Mac, successfully gnawed the granite of science in a linguistic school. There were a lot of hobbies, but already in 10 years an inquisitive mind and tireless feet brought Nikolai to the Moscow House of Pioneers. The local programming circle has inculcated code-building.
