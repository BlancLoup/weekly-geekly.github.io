Mail.Ru will release a phone under its own brand

Mail.Ru portal has announced plans to release a branded mobile phone with pre-installed software from Mail.Ru. The project was called the working name of Meilofon. The appearance of a new model on the market is planned in the first quarter of 2009.

The mailofon will combine a wide range of communication capabilities and high-speed mobile Internet access. The device is equipped with two cameras, one of which is designed for video conferencing, GPS-navigator and WiFi-adapter, supports 3G networks, and is also equipped with a special software module that provides a continuous Internet connection in GSM networks.

The name of the partner company Mail.Ru, which took over the creation of the hardware, is still kept secret, but it is known that it is one of the world's leading manufacturers of mobile phones. The first 3D models and even prototypes of the future apparatus have already been created. Currently, about $ 15 million has been invested in the project.
