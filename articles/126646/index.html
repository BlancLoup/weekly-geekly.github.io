Americans only need iPad

According to a report published on August 17, 95% of tablet computer owners and those who want to buy it, dream of an Apple iPad.




The report, which was published by analyst agency Robert W. Baird & Co , was compiled from a survey of 1,114 people, 98% of whom live in the United States.

More than 50% of respondents aged from 22 to 60 said they had already purchased a tablet or were interested in purchasing it, however, 95% of respondents chose Apple iPad as a priority.

Although survey participants could choose several different options, only 10% chose a Hewlett-Packard TouchPad tablet, and no more than 4% chose a Playberry tablet from Blackberry. Iconia from Acer, which uses the Android system, gained no more than 1% among the respondents.
