Welcome to the pages of the blog iCover . The pursuit of ultraportability. It is this that in many ways changes the priorities of the set of users who are switching from tablets to smartphones with large diagonals. But, the rejection of the tablet in favor of a mobile phone inevitably brings its owner new problems associated with a low level of comfort when working with many familiar applications. And about the possibility of full-fledged viewing of films or, for example, video games on screens of 5.5 ", it is better not to talk in principle.

According to the information provided on the project site, the cost of such a folding monitor, depending on the screen resolution, will be from $ 300 to $ 500.



Arovia is a projection display with a screen of translucent plastic material. SPUD Arovia is positioned as a backup monitor for compact PCs, laptops, tablets or mainstream smartphones. In the latter case, the possibilities of such a solution look especially attractive, since they allow to solve the problem of work and rest behind a full screen wherever there is such a need and there is a smartphone with content of interest. This can be really convenient, and sometimes it is necessary for mobile specialists who spend up to 10% or more of their time outside the home or company - on the road, in airport lounges, in a hotel, etc. With Arovia, they will be able to comfortably move between windows , viewing and / or comparing documents without scrolling, maintaining full-fledged exchange trades with technical analysis of multiple graphs at the same time, working with some graphical applications, etc.
