The device "InSight" sent a second selfie from Mars


His first self-photograph, the descent scientific apparatus "InSight" made December 6, 2018, ten days after landing on the surface of Mars on November 26, 2018.

Five months of InSight operation on Mars have passed, and now it is possible to evaluate how its appearance changed during this time.

Self-photographs of the InSight machine are obtained at NASA by collecting a mosaic of 14 photographs, which the apparatus itself makes with its camera using a robotic arm 1.8 meters long.

Parameters of the device "InSight":

- length of 6 meters (with unfolded solar panels);
- width 1.56 meters (equal to the diameter of the central deck)
- deck length from 83 to 108 centimeters;
- length of a robotic arm is 1.8 meters;
- weight 360 kg;
- two solar panels (diameter of 2.2 meters each).
