Indifferent to Habr

On all known tracker rutracker.org there is one, probably the most useless section - Business Forum. There are no distributions - this is a common forum for users to communicate on business topics. The purpose of the resource for such purposes, of course, is not quite suitable, the capabilities of the engine, too ...
And yet, there is one interesting feature - the audience.

Not all, but the part that does not read this post and others like it: not looking for communication on the Internet, not in network communities, are not geeks. Indifferent people, including Habr.

They know how to use the Internet, mastered torrents. They are interested in all sorts of things: rare films, art house, audio books, various directions in music and the rest of art. Perhaps this is almost everything they need the Internet for. The rest gives them offline.

On the tracker, the line between them and us (interactive users) is running out. The leechers get into the Business Forum: entangled in the search for the desired distributions, simply looking at the usual resource - here for the first time they begin to pay serious attention to the comments, and then write themselves. New individuals are attached to us, opening their inner world, that is, more specifically, their business practice.

I am the moderator of this forum. My goal, one of them, is the virtual socialization of such users, the creation of new communities with them, the improvement of business communications and the development of new partnership opportunities.

Business reluctantly masters the Internet. We model this process to know how it will proceed tomorrow.

If you have your own ideas in the field of Business 2.0, even the most risky ones that can not be made commercially successful soon, for this reason, at least nobody will lead them away - join us, try to ride together.
