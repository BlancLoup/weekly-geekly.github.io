Prerequisites

Apple has announced last year two new versions of the iPhone - 6S and 6S Plus. It is a traditional move for the team from Kuppertino. New models visually have not changed, having received a more powerful stuffing and a new feature - 3D Touch. This technology allows you to determine the force of pressing on the screen, opening up new possibilities for working with the interface. As a person directly connected with the development, I was rather skeptical about using 3D Touch in the interface. At first. Why? The answer is simple. All 3D Touch capabilities are Hidden features, that is, a person can only empirically know if this or that interface element has support for this technology. Perhaps in the future, designers will learn to visually mark the interface elements with support for 3D Touch.



On the other hand, this is a good start for pro-users. An interface that works well on the stage of onboarding and learning how to use a product may not be so good for people who actively use it. I3D Touch will come to mobile phones, like short / hot keys on desktops.



Something I got carried away, back to our wallpaper. Another innovation was Live Photos. After taking a photo, the user can play a short video with a strong push, sort of like animating a frozen image. I played with Live Photos for a couple of days and realized that as a result I get so-so pictures and shitty videos, with a low frame rate, a total of 2 times more than a regular photo.



But! Together, these two technologies have opened a wonderful world of animated wallpaper. And not just animated wallpapers, as it was in iOS 7, when the user was available 8 pre-installed screen savers, similar to the colorful snot (remember these circles floating on the screen?). The key difference of this type of personalization is not immediately obvious, but it is vital - to play the animation, the user must interact with the animated cover! (Press and hold!)

Do you understand how cool it is? A person must perform a physical action! It is the need for physical action that opens up the possibility for the user to develop his habits (I, at least, I hope for this). If you look at the stages that the user goes through using the product, then the covers available before have no important element - Action:



In the case of 3D Touch, we have a very specific action required from the user. With the right product and positioning, we can create a real need for new and new animations for the phone.
