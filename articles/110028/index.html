IBM computer will play against two champions Jeopardy!

The quiz will be held according to standard rules. Three participants compete among themselves in the struggle for the right to be the first to answer the question asked. Questions are formulated, as a rule, in the form of statements where the search word is replaced by a pronoun. Players must guess what is being said and give an answer.



Jeopardy! - The most popular quiz in America, so the victory of artificial intelligence here will make even more impression on the audience than the IBM Deep Blue victory over Garry Kasparov in 1997.
