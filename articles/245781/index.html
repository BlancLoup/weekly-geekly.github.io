Develop a mobile app and earn money

Monetization of mobile applications
You all know that you can make money on mobile applications in several ways, such as:
  • Primary sale application
  • Domestic purchases
  • Advertising
  • Etc.

In this article I want to tell in more detail about advertising in the form of goods on the example of our project Shmother and how you can make money on it if you are ready to develop your mobile application and promote it.

Why is it convenient for the user ?:
  • Banners are not imposed on him, we show only what the user is looking for. For example, he is looking for Nike sneakers, we give him this, in the form of specific products from more than 100 stores.
  • We have many categories and various filters - these are brands, composition, details, and so on - so that users can conveniently find what they need.


This model also has disadvantages , but concerns the costs of business, not of users:
  • We have 4 people checking selected items from stores so that there are no errors in the catalog.
  • It takes a lot of effort to create special filters for different categories.
  • Through the CPA model, we / you get a deferred profit, unlike other forms of advertising - where they pay almost immediately.


How it works?:
  • You are developing the application and place it in the product
  • Using your application people make orders in stores
  • And, only after the store earned money, he lists them to you.

There are several sites that offer this type of advertising - these are Affiliate networks , from there you can take goods directly. Examples of parter networks:
  • Actionpay: 150t products
  • Admitad: 200t goods
  • CityAds: 50t products
  • Western networks: cj, link share, etc.


Why do we offer ourselves? Why it is better to work with us than with partner networks:
  • We have united all the partner networks on our topics (clothes, shoes, accessories, cosmetics, perfumes, for sports and at home)
  • Plus we have several direct stores.
  • We now have more than 1.5 million items.
  • We carefully select things.
  • We have made many categories and filters for more accurate search of things by users.
  • We know which stores and brands sell best and share this with you (partner networks will not give you this information)


There is a minus: Our commission is 30%
