Ubuntu is 10 years old! The new release of Utopic Unicorn is available for download.



Canonical has posted the final release of the new version of Ubuntu, Ubuntu Unicorn under version 14.10. As far as can be judged, the new version of Canonical decided to mark a round date. The fact is that Ubuntu is already 10 years old. The first release appeared just in October, 2004. Over the last two years, more than 30 million PCs with a pre-installed Ubuntu distribution kit have been sold. Over the past few years, the number of PCs and laptops with installed Ubuntu has increased several times.

It is worth noting that in version 14.10 there is nothing revolutionary new. The developers say that all updates in Ubuntu Unicorn, or rather, the majority, will be of interest to developers in the first place. In addition, many developers have paid attention to the security of the OS.

As new items presented Ubuntu Developer Tools Center, simplifies the task of developing for Ubuntu. There are all Android toolkits with all dependent libraries. In addition, the familiar to many applications LibreOffice, Firefox, Chromium and Rhythmbox updated.
