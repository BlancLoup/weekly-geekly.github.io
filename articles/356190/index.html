Now providers: Rostelecom imposes limits on "unlimited" tariffs


It seems that the introduction of restrictions on "unlimited" tariffs is becoming a new trend. On June 20, 2017, the following news was published on the Rostelecom portal:
Dear subscribers!

We draw your attention to the fact that from 01.07.2017 changes to the contract for the provision of communication services come into force.

Namely: when the threshold of consumption of incoming Internet traffic is reached in the amount of 1 Tbyte (1TB = 1,048,576 Mbytes), a limit on the data transfer rate of up to 64 Kbps is introduced on unlimited data plans for telematic services during the billing period (one calendar month) .

Please read the text of the Public Offer to change the data transfer rate.

Sample news , you can check for your area and city. web.archive.org

The offer includes the Macro-regional branch "Center" of PJSC Rostelecom and regional branches, which are in its administrative and functional subordination. Unfortunately, it is not strong in the administrative division of the Republic of Tajikistan, but I suppose this means that it will affect all subscribers in the central region.

About any additional options like "Prolong Speed" (additional package for money at the speed of the main tariff) are not reported in the news.
