Sony chose Opera browser for LCD-TVs and Blu-ray-players

Thanks to the Opera Devices Software Developer Kit, an open platform that allows you to adapt the browser to a wide variety of devices from pocket readers to, as we see, TVs, the developers managed to provide happy owners of Sony‚Äôs devices to use the Internet without even thinking about the correctness of displaying pages, since The browser, thanks to the new Presto engine, provides support for many HTML5 specifications (and in WHATWG terminology is just HTML), HbbTV and OIPF .
