How professionals work. Pia Wemmelund, CEO of Momondo


The heading "How Professionals Work" continues to acquaint the readers of "Megamind" with the working habits of various specialists in the IT market.

Recently, we are increasingly able to communicate with foreign professionals - and it pleases! Indeed, throughout the world a huge number of enthusiastic people, not only in Russia. So today, a native of Denmark, the executive director of Momondo , Pia Wemmelund, will tell us about his working habits.

Ms. Wemmelund has been working at Momondo since 2007, taking the position of commercial director, where, as expected, she was engaged in sales, internet marketing, finance and administrative matters. Already in 2012, Pia is appointed managing director, since 2013 she has been on the board of directors of the company.

Prior to joining Momondo, Pia Wemmelund worked for more than 19 years in the Scandinavian airline SAS, rising from one of the ordinary positions to the commercial director of the holding. Continuing to work in the tourism industry for over 25 years, Pia notes that she still likes it.
