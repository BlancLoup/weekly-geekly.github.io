Homemade Cards Worldwide - Touchnote.com

Touchnote.com is a British website that allows you to send postcards, the most real paper postcards around the world. You do not just choose postcards, but create them yourself. You can upload your photo, picture or choose from those offered on the site, add an inscription on the card itself and much more. If you do not have photos on your computer, but there is a webcam, it‚Äôs provided on the site and that is you can take a picture with the camera.


Based on the format of a traditional postcard, here you can also write from two sides - front and inside.

The only negative is that there is no support for the Russian language. Perhaps in the future this will be corrected, but so far all the inscriptions have to be written only in English.

As already mentioned, delivery takes place around the world. Because the service is British, then delivery will be made in Britain the next day; across Europe - within 3 days; to any other part of the world within 5 days.

Postcard size - 10x10 cm.

Postcard prices are as follows:

from 1 to 4 cards - $ 3.98

from 5 to 9 cards - $ 3.5

25 or more cards - $ 1.98

Shipping Costs:

in the UK - $ 0.84

to other countries - $ 1.18

Payment is made through PayPal
