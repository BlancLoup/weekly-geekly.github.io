January 29, 2011 , on Saturday, at the Microsoft office in Krylatskoye, an #imcupmeet event will be held for students who are potential participants in the Imagine Cup software project competition. Beginning - at 11:00, the end - according to circumstances (but we expect around 20:00). Sign up if you want to participate!


At the training camp you can learn more about how to effectively participate in the competition and win. This event is for ideas that have already come up with, and for all interested undergraduate and graduate students. Among other things, it is planned:
  • Round table on the topic " Why the Imagine Cup is needed " - with the participation of participants of past years, judges of the international final, Microsoft employees
  • Brainstorming and the generation of brilliant ideas for those who have not yet invented their own
  • Formation of teams , the ability to find the missing link in the team, to meet and talk
  • Opportunity to talk with experts and jury members, improve project positioning
  • Competition of mini-projects
  • The opportunity to communicate with people from the business environment and understand how to turn an idea into a product

For those who already have their own ideas and commands , this is a great opportunity:
  • talk about your idea and get critical feedback, comments and tips for improvement

  • understand how best to present a project at a competition
  • find out what may be the future development of the project to turn it into a business
For students who have not yet come up with ideas and have not decided whether they want to participate:
  • chat with interesting young people who are in love with information technology
  • learn more about the competition and ask questions to the organizers, former participants and jury members
  • right at the event to come up with and develop an idea, make a presentation and / or prototype and get feedback
