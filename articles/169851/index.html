Intel has cooperated with faculty members in laboratories at Nizhny Novgorod, St. Petersburg, and Novosibirsk State Universities and has developed several online courses for distance learning. In Russian. Is free. With the issuance of certificates of completion of courses. But nobody knows about it. Except me. And I'll tell you about it a little bit.

The courses are created in three different levels of complexity, so the Hello world will not end there, but you will have to start with this hell. Now you can get two types of certificates: Intel Parallel Programming Professional (IPPP) and Intel Mobile Programming Professional (IMPP).

It is nice to look at the course about compilers, and at the same time understand that this is a modern course about modern compilers for modern processors and the course was written with the help of the company that develops these processors and the same compilers. First-hand in Russian and for free. There is also a Great Purpose - a certificate. Well, anyone can sign up for a short course: feel like a student again and start attending lectures, and do practical tasks at night in order to surely get everything done by morning.
