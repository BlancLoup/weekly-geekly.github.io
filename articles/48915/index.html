On the magazines and printed materials.

In response to the topic . Just my opinion.

Six months ago, I would say that the magazines will not die and will continue to be issued for many more years. And even now I understand that if this happens, then not in a short time. But it is becoming more and more profitable for companies dealing with issues of publication to produce Internet magazines. In Russia, while such journals are few, and if they do, they are based on foreign platforms.

One of these platforms is issuu . Based on this platform, you can publish your journals in PDF format and they will be divided into pages to create a page turning effect. Also there are a number of functions to make it easier to read. Each page is loaded as a separate element, so that the journal as a whole loads faster. In general, to publish a journal there, this platform will come down, but in it, although it is developed on flash, no interactivity is used inside the journals.

The creators of the Ceros platform went further. They have all the same as in the issuu , but interactive has been added to the pages of the journals. All pages are made with different animations. Some pages have audio accompaniment. On the other video added. It is possible to go from the page with the announcement immediately to the necessary page, there is navigation through the magazine. There are forms to send e-mail letters immediately from the magazine. Even somewhere there was a magazine shop where flipping through it could throw the goods in the basket and the end of the magazine to make a purchase. In general, almost everything that can be done using flash can be implemented in your journal based on this platform.

I considered only two examples of platforms on the basis of which you can create your own journals. Looking at what has been done, it is much easier for me to watch such a magazine on the Internet while listening to music and watching videos parallel to my reading. I agree with the fact that printed materials will always exist, for example books. Or such brochures that are distributed before the election for mass campaigning. Also, as long as there will be magazines like avon, where you can rub your hand and smell the scent of perfume or cream. But some products such as newspapers and magazines will gradually give way to technology.
