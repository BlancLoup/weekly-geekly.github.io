New Android Market

Within a few weeks, according to a post in the official blog of Android Developers , users of (exclusively) phones running Google Android 2.2 and higher will be available FOTA-update Android Market. Among the new features:
  • Buying and renting movies (you can watch in the Market itself in streaming playback mode or without connecting to the network): currently available only in the US, but soon will be available in other countries;
  • Buying books for Google Books: currently available only in the US, but soon will be open in other countries;
  • New design, more compatible with the current guides on building the interface of applications for Google Android (like Google Docs );
  • New home page;
  • Redesigned cataloging, allowing (developers) to promote and (users) to find more relevant content.
