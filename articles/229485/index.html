Naumen became a partner of the conference PyCon Russia 2014

In the first working days of the summer, representatives of the IT community who are keen on developing in Python, met at the conference PyCon Russia 2014. Instead of a frosty Ural winter, this time an unpredictable Ural summer. The event was held at the recreation center "Oriole" on June 2-3, near the city of Yekaterinburg.

The conference was attended not only by our compatriots, but also by foreign guests. The reports were in both Russian and English, workshops, round tables, and lightning talks, already loved by everyone at this conference, were also held.
