Sites for online training of web developers and web designers

The note contains sites with training materials for web designers and web developers. I tried not to repeat with the post 27+ resources for online learning habraser nicolausYes .

Academic Earth


Many free lectures, including from well-known universities (Harvard, MIT, Stanford, etc.).



Lynda.com


One of the largest video tutorial sites with nearly 2000 video courses. Subscription costs $ 25 per month, there is a weekly trial period.



Google Developers University Consortium


The site contains a huge number of courses and other useful information for working with Google products: Android, Google Maps, Google App Engine, etc. There is also educational material on more general topics.



Tuts + premium


Probably the most famous and large site with lessons, covers many areas of the Internet and not only. Some lessons are organized in the form of courses. Subscription costs $ 19 per month, it is possible to take free courses for a test period.



iTunes U


Excellent educational service from Apple for designers and developers.



WebDesign.com


Training for web designers and web developers in the form of live webinars. Cost $ 197 per year.



Dev.Opera


Opera's website with web-based educational materials: CSS3, HTML5, SVG, Javascript.



P2PU's School of Webcraft


Free gaming environment supported by Mozilla. Looks like the famous Codecademy . There are courses for both beginners and more experienced developers.



Why's (Poignant) Guide to Ruby


A fun and simple Ruby course with great illustrations.



Web Standards Curriculum


Guide to web standards, there are training materials.



Openlearn


Courses from Open University cover a lot of technical and not only areas.



Skillfeed


New training site from Shutterstock for web design, photography, web development and much more. Subscription $ 19 per month, there is a weekly trial period.



KillerPHP.com


Selection of PHP-lessons for beginners and experienced developers. Subscription $ 29 for three months or $ 99 per year.



Timothy Training


Despite the poor appearance, the site contains a lot of interesting courses on basic web technologies and programs.



Don't Fear the Internet


This site is designed for beginners, the basics of HTML and CSS are presented in the form of interesting video tutorials.
