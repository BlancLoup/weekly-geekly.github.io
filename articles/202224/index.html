Video review tablet Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 (2014)



Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 (2014) - the latest generation of Samsung flagship tablets, replacing last year‚Äôs Galaxy Note 10.1. The device boasts a PLS-display with a diagonal of 10.1 inches and a resolution of 2560x1600 (299 ppi). The device is based on the eight-core Exynos 5 Octa processor (including 4 Cortex A15 cores with a frequency of 1.9 GHz and 4 Cortex A7 cores with a frequency of 1.3 GHz). The amount of RAM is 3 GB, and the internal storage is presented in three versions - 16/32/64 GB, depending on the model. One of the key features of the Galaxy Note is the use of a special S-Pen stylus (recognizing gradations of pressing force) for handwriting input or drawing. It should also be noted support for LTE (but without the support of Russian networks, which is sad), Micro-USB with support for OTG, DLNA and MHL 2.0.
