Electronic books in education and the future

Electronic books, reading rooms, book readers - as they are just not called. When you take an e-book in your hands and you begin to realize it, it is easy to get confused, whether it is a book (because it is real) or a device for reading books.

Phenomenon


The e-book is not a virtue, not a threat, it is a symmetrical response to the era, the rapid obsolescence of information. Can we say that e-books have become a phenomenon, not a toy for geeks? In my opinion, you can. December 26 last year, the online store "Amazon" said that on Catholic Christmas, for the first time in its work history, sales of electronic books exceeded sales of paper . Book readers, in Osvnovnoye, are divided into two classes: for reading fiction and reading newspapers and scientific literature. So far, these classes can be divided by price and screen size. So, the latter include Amazon Kindle DX (9.7-inch display), Sony Reader Daily Edition (PRS-900) (7.1-inch display) and Apple iPad (9.7-inch display). For everyday reading and carrying with them, more compact readers on 5-6 inch displays are more convenient. One of the most interesting applications, in my opinion, could be the use of electronic books in education.

In education


Two (known to me) programs of e-books in education should start this year approximately simultaneously in Donetsk, Ukraine and in the state of California, USA.

The basis of the Ukrainian program will be the Pocketbook 901 - the brainchild of the Ukrainian-Taiwan company PocketBook Global. It is his governor who is trying to defeat him, reducing the state budget deficit. Between two books


I tried to imagine the difference between the e-book and the usual textbook today .

The benefits of the textbook:
  • tactile;
  • durable;
  • the possibility of marks in the text;
  • full color.

Parity:
  • reading due to reflected light;
  • there is a table of contents;
  • do not know how to play video;
  • no interactivity;
  • You can hit a classmate.

The advantages of e-books:
  • search by text;
  • stores many texts;
  • changes the font size;
  • weighs little;
  • plays audio;
  • promptly updated.

From this small comparison, it can be seen that there is nothing left for e-books to complete the replacement of traditional textbooks. Moreover, if you take a device like an iPad as a textbook, then ‚Äúvideo and interactivity‚Äù are taken into account for an electronic device (but on an iPad, an IPS screen, which is a controversial issue for children to constantly watch).

Unsuccessful experience


While in Ukraine and in California they are making plans for electronization of school textbooks, at the University of Texas they conducted an experiment on engineering students, mathematicians, and others, entrusting them with 1000 devices. 200 000 USD was spent on this program, however, 42% of students were dissatisfied with electronic books, 23% were undecided, and 35% supported the initiative. "About half of the students bought printed versions of books, confirming that the Internet generation also likes to rustle pages."

Development


E-books have where to develop? Of course! Let's rely on the current functionality of computers, think about where the reader can grow further.

Font size
The function of changing the font size is an amazing and magical property that is not subject to printed books. If you remember, we all start with books with children's large print. For the poorly seeing, this ‚Äúchildhood‚Äù may not end.

Speech synthesizer
Sadly, someone with a vision is not lucky at all. And then the e-book can read the text. Probably even simple command book management will be understood "by ear".

Boring pictures
Not all pictures are boring, old illustrations, for example, in biology or anatomy, were made intelligently and carefully. But as they say, ‚Äúit‚Äôs better to see once than to hear a hundred times.‚Äù It is better to watch the video once, as the mycelium on the roots of trees form mycorrhiza and work in symbiosis, than a hundred times to look for an air-boletus in the field.

Notes
From childhood I was taught to handle books carefully. Therefore, only recently I began to take notes in the margins. Electronic books allow you to leave a note to any letter of the text, to link them together, to shuffle them - to analyze the book.

Magical transformations
What is easier to build a graph of a function or calculate a function by points of a graph. Or, as it will be visually pull the equilateral triangle, turning it into an isosceles or rectangular. It is easier to understand the sine and cosine on it alive ( an example of problems of understanding ).

Social graphs
Who said that a social network can only be modern? No, by correspondence, places of visits, meetings documented in the literature, you can build a social network of any historical segment (the newer, the more accurate, of course). For example, dating social group "Decembrists".

Geology on the fingers
There are no problems to impose historical maps on modern ones, to scale back and forth, to turn drawings into a quasi-volume map. Try what a crustal shift means, what consequences (earthquake, tsunami, volcanic eruption) can cause an impact in one place or another.

One can fantasize further almost indefinitely, but the general idea should already be clear: what we used to comprehend from pictures and slides, tried to imagine, much can be visualized, shown visually and interactively.
