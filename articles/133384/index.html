Google added 4shared, The Pirate Bay and others to the list of pirated words

This initiative of the search giant provoked disapproval in the Internet community, because such methods smack of censorship, and unauthorized, because Google did not agree with the list of prohibited words with anyone. It includes terms such as [torrent], [BitTorrent], [RapidShare] and others.
