Registration for the DevCon 2015 conference is open

Friends, software developers and testers!

We are pleased to inform you that registration to the main technology conference Microsoft DevCon 2015 is open!

The fifth anniversary conference will be held May 20-21, 2015 .



The format of the conference remains unchanged - this is a country-day two-day event at a natural resort in the near Moscow region, and everything is included in the ticket price for the conference:

  • Food and hotel accommodation;
  • Participation in the main program;
  • Attend master classes;
  • Participation in round tables and focus groups;
  • Transfer from Moscow and back;
  • Entertaining and sports program;
  • Evening program.

Sign up now! From the experience of holding conferences, we know that tickets run out a few months before. Hurry up to take your place.

We pay a lot of attention to the quality of the program and what you can learn. You will have a unique opportunity to talk with Microsoft representatives directly involved in the development and creation of products, as well as with employees of other companies who have deep practical experience in using our technologies. Windows


Development of modern applications for all platforms with Windows - from tiny chips, wearable devices, phones to PCs, game consoles and large screens. Development, monetization and promotion of applications for app stores.

Azure & Web


A detailed introduction to the new version of the ASP.NET 5 web development platform and all the tools added to the web development tools.
