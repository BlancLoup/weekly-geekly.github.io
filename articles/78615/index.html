One of the most popular capabilities of media monitoring of the blogosphere is rightly considered the analysis of sentiments, or rather its automation.

What is Automated Sentiment technology?

This piece is designed to automatically determine the mood of a sentence or phrase. Radian6 analyzes feedback on the topics of messages as they arrive, identifies and shares positive, negative or neutral values ‚Äã‚Äãbased on specified keywords and phrases, moods. How can this technology help?
