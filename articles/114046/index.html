Conference "Online stores of Ukraine 2011"



March 25, 2011, Kharkiv meets specialists in the field of e-commerce and everyone who sells through the Internet at the annual conference "Online Stores of Ukraine 2011". The event is devoted to topical issues of Internet business development and key issues in this area. The conference will be useful both for online store owners and e-commerce specialists, as well as for those who are just going to open and expand their business on the Internet. The conference program includes reports from experts in all areas of e-commerce.



Here are a few topics already announced reports:


- New niches and rapidly developing segments of online trading.
- The activities of online stores under the new rules of the Tax Code.
- New online shopping. How to overtake the leaders?
- Merchandising and usability in Ukrainian online stores.
- Target users - where to get them and how to track their actions.
