Microsoft Research Conference - Cloud Computing for Microsoft Azure, May 19 at MSU

Hello!

May is getting hotter - we already have Microsoft DevCon 2015 in Yakhontah - the main conference for Microsoft developers in Russia, Embedded Day - the conference on embedded technologies and the Internet of Things is a school for machine learning that is Microsoft Research .

However, we have prepared another real present - a whole conference from Microsoft Research on how to do research in the cloud. Every day the amount of resources needed for scientific computing is growing, and it has long been impossible to cope locally. With the advent of the cloud and application tools, as well as the efforts of Microsoft Research, we have much to offer the scientific community. On May 19, at the Moscow State University, a mini-conference will be held, where we will discuss how the cloud helps in applied and theoretical research of truth. With us - leading experts, doctors of science from Microsoft Research.



In a programme:

  • Joined-up Planetary Information Drew Purves, Head of Computational Ecology and Environmental Science Group, Microsoft Research
  • Accelerating your Research with Cloud Computing, Dr. Kenji Takeda, Solutions Architect and Technical Manager, Microsoft Research
  • Microsoft Azure for research projects at University
  • Big data, HPC and Internet of Things live demonstrations using Microsoft Azure, Alexander Belotserkovsky, expert on strategic technologies, Cloud, Internet of Things & HPC, Microsoft Russia
  • Panel discussion - cloud research


Here we will also launch a special nomination for Russia for a grant from Azure for Research (cloud resources for the year).

We show how Microsoft Azure is used by researchers around the world in various areas, including Computer Science, Environmental Science, Social Science and others. We will communicate with you.

The conference is designed for researchers, representatives of the educational community, experts from various fields, and everyone who wants to understand how to use the cloud.
