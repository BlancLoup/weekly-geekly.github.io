Watch Nike Amp +

Many people probably remember Apple's iRing concept ring .
It remains a concept. I want to tell you about the Nike Amp + watch, which performs a similar function, i.e. perform the role of a wireless remote for iPod.

The chip of this gadget is that it belongs to a series of opposite Nike + accessories, which means it supports work with Apple iPod nano and special Nike sneakers.

With Nike Amp +, you can switch music tracks and select a workout mode from the menu. While running, the music is periodically interrupted by a voice that indicates the current time, the distance covered, and the number of calories burned.



Instead of the LCD display and the dial, bright orange LEDs are used - the time is clearly visible both on a sunny day and at night. The design is a waterproof bracelet. The clasp is made of stainless steel and the belt is made of soft rubber. The control buttons are easy to find by touch, so you can switch music tracks without looking, add volume and find out the time.

The approximate price of the gadget is 2000 rubles.
