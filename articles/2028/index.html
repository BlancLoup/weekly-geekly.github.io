Danish Internet providers block access to Allofmp3.com

Copenhagen City Court (Denmark) ordered the Internet provider Tele2 to block access to the Allofmp3.com site for all its users.

According to one of the representatives of the Danish division of the International Federation of the Producers of Phonograms (IFPI), the site Allofmp3.com deceives the performers and steals millions of crowns from them every year. However, the Internet provider Tele2 intends to file an appeal.

This is not the first measure that Western media corporations initiate in order to hinder the work of the "pirated" service. Earlier payment systems Visa and MasterCard announced the termination of work with the resource .
