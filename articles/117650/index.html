Foreword

Few people know that mobile operators receive a huge percentage of income from those who once bought a SIM card-inserted and calling. This is because buying a tariff with an enticing inscription or on the advice of a consultant, most often we do not think about the passage of time, operators have more and more new offers. And at the old rates the price rises.
Operators specifically raise prices at old rates. Motivating it so that users switch to new tariffs and do not linger on old ones. But in reality, few people follow the changes in tariffs. Information from the developer Tarifer.ru Ulex

On behalf of the tarifer.ru developers, I would like to clarify something. We are currently testing a new scheme of the service, so some users will not be able to directly download the details to the site. Instead, there will be a field for entering the phone number and password to the personal account (and brief instructions for obtaining a password if it is not already there).

Why is that? I plan to write on Habr a separate article on this subject, but briefly I will tell here:
First of all, most people have difficulties with obtaining details, including quite objective ones (for example, the Beeline server periodically lies and cannot fulfill requests).
Secondly, and this is important - the call detailing does not give a complete picture. In most cases, it does not contain information about the connected services, it most often does not contain information about periodic and one-time charges (subscription fees for the tariff and additional services, some content services).
Thirdly, we have expanded the list of features of our service. It is now not just the selection of the tariff.

What the service does:
After receiving the number and password, the robot enters the user's personal account, orders the call detail for the full calendar month, the bill for the same period, analyzes the list of connected services, receives data about the current tariff and the list of tariffs that are available for the transition. After receiving and analyzing data, several levels of recommendations are formed:
- A list of ‚Äúsuspicious‚Äù services and paid services that could be connected implicitly (operators and content providers have a lot of ways to do this) plus information on how to disable these services.
- Recommendations to reduce costs without changing the tariff - which options, packages, favorite numbers to connect to the current tariff and what will be the benefit of it.
- Recommendations on changing the tariff - which tariff to switch to and which services to connect, while maintaining the current number.
- Recommendations for changing the tariff with changing numbers.

In other words, we are developing a service that will allow you to do a full audit of mobile costs, which will check if everything is in order and what ways are there to optimize, as well as protect you from fraudsters and imposed services.

Today it works in test mode for Moscow subscribers of MTS, Beeline, MegaFon. For the rest of the regions, so far everything is unchanged - it is possible to upload a detail file directly to the site and select tariffs. By the way, today we support 15 regions and about 70 detail formats.
