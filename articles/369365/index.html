Results of the Microsoft presentation at E3

The new Xbox One S and Project Scorpio, Minecraft cross-platform servers, Witcher card game, indie platformer INSIDE and more




The standoff between Sony and Microsoft at E3 has already become traditional. Both companies are giants of the gaming industry, primarily as the creators of the two main gaming consoles of the latest generation - Xbox One and PlayStation 4, and during each exhibition a huge part of the attention is focused on their stands. Bethesda studio has already made a significant contribution the day before , but their performance was far from being the main thing at this event.

So let's see what Microsoft prepared for us this year.

Xbox one s

Project scorpio


Project Scorpio is a 4K console developed by Microsoft with support for VR technologies. So far, little is known about the console. For example, Scorpio performance will reach 6 TFLOPS against 1.32 TFLOPS on the Xbox One.

At the same time, Project Scorpio creates a precedent when, in one generation, they will exist with fundamentally different performance.

The novelty will be on sale only in 2017 .

Xbox Play Anywhere, Xbox Design Lab, Cortana and the rejection of regions


Xbox Play Anywhere is a new service from Microsoft, which has been requested for a long time. XPA will allow users not to pay for the same game twice: if you already have a copy of a cross-platform (xbox-PC) title on one of the platforms, then on the second you can play it for free. Quite an obvious step since the launch of the Xbox One, which, however, Microsoft did just now.

Xbox Design Lab is another new service, this time for customizing gamepads.



Judging by the description, with its help any person will be able to choose the configuration of the original gamepad that he needs to be made for the player to order. Thus, Microsoft offer "modding gamepads out of the box."


Presentation video

The Xbox One also now has a Cortana voice assistant. You can control the console with a voice, for example, "Hey, Cortana, Xbox on".

Another good news for gamers - Microsoft has taken the path of liberalizing its policy and is refusing regional restrictions. Now you can connect to Xbox Live from anywhere in the world and play in any available language.

And of course, besides services and new modifications of the Xbox, Microsoft brought a lot of games to the exhibition.

Minecraft


At the last presentation MS announced quite significant changes in Minecraft. The technology giant has decided once again to experiment on the crossing of ecosystems: in the future, single servers will be open, where console gamers can play with players on the PC platform.

A good move or not, time will tell. In any case, Microsoft is far from the first: earlier, the creators of CoD and Battlefield tried to bring PC gamers and console gamers together, which the latter did not like. Perhaps the problem was in the specifics of the game (shooter) and the experiment with Minecraft will be more successful.

During the presentation, a free version of Minecraft was announced.

Halo wars 2


Microsoft also presented a continuation of the strategy of Halo Wars - Halo Wars 2.


Halo Wars 2 Trailer

The original Halo Wars - a real-time strategy for the Xbox-exclusive Halo universe. The game was designed by Ensemble Studios, sponsored by Age Of Empires, and released in 2009.

The developers of the second part were Creative Assembly, the authors of the Total War series.

Yesterday, after the announcement at the presentation, beta testing was launched, which will last a week - from 13 to 20 June.


Xbox One Multiplayer Trailer

The game will be available on Xbox One and Windows 10, the release is scheduled for February 21, 2017.

Forza Horizon 3


Players waiting for the release of new races from the Forza series.


Forza Horizon 3 trailer

In addition to the beautiful picture, which is not so surprising gamers, the developers promise a huge number of modes, network mode, as well as a cooperative for four. The game will be available not only supercars, but also mass-produced sports cars, buggies and even SUVs.

The game will be released on September 27, 2016 , and for pre-order the ultimate edition, you can get access to the game earlier, on September 23 (Friday), 2016. Available Forza Horizon 3 will be for both the Xbox One and Windows 10.

INSIDE


Surprisingly, the presentation of such a giant as Microsoft got an indie project from the studio Limbo - the game INSIDE. Her trailer was shown back in 2014, and since then work has been done on creating the game. And now, at E3 2016, another trailer was shown and the release date was announced.



INSIDE is a typical indie platformer with riddles, puzzles. The overall setting of the game is rather gloomy saved on drawing the background : the main character is a little boy who is trying to confront an unknown evil and save humanity.

The game will be released soon enough - June 29, available on Xbox One and PC.

Gears of war 4


Of course, Microsoft can't give up its other high-profile Xbox exclusive after Halo - Gears of War. At E3, the company announced the release of the fourth part of the popular shooter - Gears of War 4.


Video from the presentation

Multiplayer is being developed by the British from the studio Splash Damage, whose record of service includes cooperation with Id Software and work on the legendary Return to Castle Wolfenstein , Doom 3 and Batman: Arkham Origins.

Tekken 7
