Chords for stringed plucked instruments

Inspired by http://chordfind.com , as well as an example of r3code , I decided to bring my own version of tablature to mind.

The idea was to make a universal thing, with which one could schematically show the neck of a string instrument. What is not important. If only the strings in the provided schemes were enough for display.

Pros:
- since the ‚Äústrings‚Äù of different width, you can clearly see where the top and where the bottom;

- no graphics are used;
- only one css-file;
