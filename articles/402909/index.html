For those who love it: advanced lenses for Canon, Nikon and Sony cameras

No matter how great the pictures do smartphones, physics can not be fooled. This means that the photos taken on a normal camera will exceed the shots taken on the camera phone. Especially if you use the right lens.



As the statistics show, a significant number of newbies who bought the first camera with interchangeable lenses, despite the extensive photo plans, go with the complete ‚Äúglass‚Äù. And it's not even that the quality of the received frames completely suits them - there are a lot of lenses, the prices for them differ many times, but it is not easy to figure out all this diversity, and it takes a lot of time.

In this post we want to recommend lenses that are best suited for a particular genre and are in a reasonable price range.

Remark one: in the statement that the shots taken on the camera, surpass similar pictures on the smartphone, there is a little slyness. In terms of a high-quality camera in a smartphone, a sunny day, and viewing photos on a smartphone screen, the difference in any general picture may be minimal. But once we dive into the genres (for example, portrait) or try shooting at dusk, and then display the photo on a laptop screen, the laws of physics will heartlessly remind of themselves.

The second remark: why we limit ourselves to reviewing only Canon, Nikon and Sony. The first two companies need no introduction, and the lion's part of the DSLRs is produced under these labels. Well, Sony makes just amazing mirrorless cameras, for example, the Sony Alpha A5100 is considered one of the best entry-level mirrorless cameras. Well, the Sony Alpha A6000 has long been firmly established in all sales charts.

Initial lenses


As a rule, the first lenses that are worth trying out in business are the so-called fixes, i.e. lenses with fixed focal length. The absence of moving parts (and, as a result, the need to solve a bunch of engineering problems) reduces the price of the lens and allows you to achieve the highest quality images at a single focal length.

Wide angle lenses


As a rule, right after the zoom, photographers start to get involved in wide-angle lenses. Because it becomes much more interesting not to remove a distant object, but to convey the panorama opened to the eye as widely as possible. Especially such a lens is in demand during city shooting - dense building does not allow for a sufficient distance to move away, allowing to fit all the most necessary things in the frame. Or while photographing parties.

Remark five: wide-angle lenses are very much loved by realtors and hotel owners. Thanks to this lens, the rooms look bigger than they are.

The most interesting lenses in this category:

  • For Canon: Canon EF-S 10-18mm f / 4.5-5.6 IS STM (17 990 rubles). A great option in its price range. Most reviewers of this lens say that for such money this is a very, very good offer.

  • For Nikon: A bit unexpected, but the Sigma 10-20mm f / 3.5 EX DC HSM (31 990) has the best price / quality combination. Why precisely he? The fact is that most wide-angle lenses have a standard problem caused by the design itself - frame distortion. This is quite easily corrected by software when processing photos. But in the case of Sigma, these distortions are minimal. It means that it is easier to process photos and better result. Experts from the reputable project DXOmark.com have tested the lens and agree that it is not inferior to the brand Nikon AF-S DX NIKKOR 10-24mm F / 3.5-4.5G ED.



  • For Sony: very good Sony 10-18mm f / 4 (62 990 rubles). We pay a lot - but we also get high luminosity, optical stabilizer and automatic autofocus. In principle, in the case of a wide-angle lens, autofocus is just not so important. If you remove it, the stabilizer and the label of Sony itself, you get a good Samyang 12mm F2.0 NCS CS. But it can be very difficult to find on sale.
