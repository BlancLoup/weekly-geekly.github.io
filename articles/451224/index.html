Collectors are requesting access to the debtors' phones




The National Association of Collection Agencies (SRO NAPCA) sent an appeal to the State Duma with a request to provide collectors with the telephone numbers of debtors specified in the Unified Identification and Authentication System (ESIA). According to collectors, the provision of access will allow to solve the problem of calls to citizens whose numbers are indicated in the questionnaires erroneously.

Experts disagree with this opinion, stating that the problem of calling third parties exists, but here is the way to solve it, which are offered by collectors, carry additional risks. In particular, this will entail a weakening of the protection of personal data.
