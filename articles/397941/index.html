"Maestro, music!": How to get a job of a sound designer in the game room

The gaming industry for many years is considered the same art as cinema or music. At the same time on large-scale marketing campaigns, the amount of money invested in promo, and sales revenue, some publishers can even with the coolest movie bestsellers. For example, in just three days after the release of GTA V from the Rockstar Games studio, it earned $ 1 billion, whereas, for example, Avatar, the highest-grossing Hollywood blockbuster, collected a little less than three billion over the entire rental period.

A good game should have a thoughtful plot, nice graphics (avid gamers here can disagree and will be right), high optimization indicators. However, it consolidates the success of the digital product and praises it to the top of the sales chart as nothing more than musical accompaniment ( music from Undertale would be a good example).

Most recently, we figured out how to create sound for cinema , and in this article we decided to touch on the theme of musical design in games and job search for a novice sound designer in the game industry.

Experts like Will, in their own experience, prove the fact that working in the field of music design is not only profitable, but also very interesting. The game market is constantly growing, and there are much more opportunities to write music. And it is not surprising that some companies offer a salary of $ 30 thousand per 30 minutes of the soundtrack.

However, it should be remembered that this is not the easiest career path. With the development of the industry, the requirements for sound designers are also increasing. According to Will, not everyone can become a sought-after professional, since customer requirements can be very different, for example, a person needs a specialist who will write mariachi music to play.

The work of the sound designer


Requirements for sound designers and working conditions in different companies, of course, vary. Firstly, you can work as a freelancer: take on one project, execute it and get a reward (thus, you can work with several projects or companies).

Secondly, there is the option of working in the office: standard working day from 9 am to 6 pm from Monday to Friday. However, it is necessary to undertake not only for projects that you like, but also for those that are given by the employer. Of course, there is a compromise option - a temporary contract, when the designer works as part of a team at the company's headquarters, but only for a certain amount of time, for example, a year.

Most often, companies are looking for a person for the position of an audio programmer or audio designer. The work of an audio programmer (audio programmer) is associated with the performance of several tasks: audio coding, working with DSP, creating utilities for audio designers (post audio tools programmer), etc. These functions can be performed by one person or the whole team.

At the same time, some audio programmers are engaged in introducing music into the game or solving questions of the playback logic. Now many audio engines, such as FMOD and Wwise, simplify the execution of these tasks. It turns out that if you are applying for a position called an audio programmer or a sound programmer, then you should be ready to write code.

As for the audio designer (audio designer), he performs a large number of tasks: he creates sounds, records them, mixes, masters, edits dialogs, and he also injects and tests sounds. And writing music for the game.

This is very often given to freelancers who like to sit in a cozy studio and create ‚Äúcool sound sets‚Äù or search for new sounds. Moreover, large companies often hire a specialist to create a specific sound, for example, to work on the sounds of an explosion.

An office audio designer is a bit different. As a rule, in this case you will have to do several tasks at the same time. For example, Will, while working on GTA Vice City, edited and mixed dialogues and created some sound effects. Therefore, before agreeing to a job offer, it is better to read the requirements for the applicant and find out what will be included in the terms of reference.
