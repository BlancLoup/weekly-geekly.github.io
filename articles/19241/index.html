Rumor: IBM will unite with AMD


There are active rumors that we can expect the unification of two major players in the IT market: IBM and AMD. Against the background of these rumors, the shares of both companies grew in price.
Literally, the message, published by JMP Securities analyst, was: Advanced Micro Devices and IBM can establish a more formal alliance in the field of semiconductor manufacturing than their current partnership. Such an assumption arose during the dialogue between industry participants, analysts and MergerMarket. The deal could look like a strategic infusion of funds or even a merger of the relevant IBM division and AMD. According to information sources, this event may occur in the short term.
