RIF-Voronezh will be held November 10-11

RIF-Voronezh 2011 - the main IT event in the region!
Russian Internet Forum, an all-Russian event dedicated to the Internet and near-Internet industry.



The forum will include exhibitions, workshops, seminars, round tables on the program of the event.
Opportunity to communicate in an informal setting with an IT guru and get to know all your questions and gain knowledge.
The event will be a large number of reports on topics:
SEO, Analytics, Security, Web Development, Government Initiatives, Marketing, Mobile Applications, Advertising, Media, Social Networks, Startups, Studios and Customers, Management and Personnel, E-Commerce, Usability. (At the moment, there are 23 reports, but this figure is growing).
And there will also be user reports that anyone can read (at the moment 7 pieces).

In addition, there will be an exhibition, which will display stands of various companies and much more.


Registration on the RIF website is required. Until November 2, registration is free. Attendance of workshops, seminars will be on badges. From 3 to 9 November - registration 300 rubles. Registration during the event - 500 rubles.

Registration entitles you to:
- attend the exhibition, any master classes, seminars, round tables on the program of the event;
- make appointments to participants, experts (the function is being tested, it will be launched later);
- receive gifts from partners of the event;
- get access to forum materials (video from sections, presentations of speakers);
and just enter the history of RIF-Voronezh 2011!

It is also a chance to communicate in an informal setting with professionals in the IT field and find out everything that interests you, as well as gain new knowledge.
