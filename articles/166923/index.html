How to know the color of borscht? Colorchuzer will help



Colorchuzer is a simple application that will help you find out the name of the color instantly! Just point the camera and you will see a hexadecimal color code and its full name.

The application will be useful not only to designers and people associated with graphics, but also helps to resolve the argument with the girl about the color of her blouse, formulate the name of the color that you thought you could not convey in words and just laugh at the different shades that occur in everyday life.



Some features of the application:

- The base contains 995 names of colors and their shades
- Simple interface
- Fixing the color with one click
- Ability to copy color name to clipboard
- The ability to share the resulting color via Twitter

Of course, the application is free and available for both the iPhone and iPad.
