Do you want to be a YouTube star?

Kinsey Kramer (Kinzie Kramer), marketing, YouTube partnerships

Thanks to our NextUp program this year, we were able to find some fantastically talented video creators. Today, we are pleased to announce that this project is opening its doors to European partners YouTube again.

NextUp is a development program created specifically for YouTube Partners with great creative potential. We want more content creators to use the site as a launching pad for their future successful career. To do this, we created NextUp. For beginners in YouTube, we develop special educational programs, provide financial support and help with promotion. Such assistance, for example, funds for new equipment and training seminars, allows novice partners to create unique online content for a global audience. For example, did you know that you could iron during a skydive ?

How to get involved
And now - the details! If you are an official YouTube partner in the UK, Germany, Spain, Italy, the Netherlands, Poland, Russia or France, and you are over 18 years old on the day you submit your application, you can participate in the program. As of August 17, 2012, your channel should have from 5,000 to 10,000 subscribers, and over the past 6 months (that is, from February 17, 2012 to August 17, 2012) at least 6 videos with access parameters should be uploaded for all". If all this is about you, you can apply here .

To do this, you will need to send one pilot video and description of your channel:
  • The pilot video should tell about the project that you want to create for your channel, and demonstrate your creative abilities, narrative talent and the ability to shoot video (video duration should not exceed 3 minutes).
  • A written description of your YouTube project and additional information to your pilot video. The text should contain no more than 400 words.

You can submit applications until September 23, 2012. When the submission is closed, the YouTube jury and the 2011 NextUp winners will select 25 winners from across Europe.

Prizes
The winners will be fully paid for a trip to London, where they will attend a week-long workshop and a course of lectures. They will be held in our creative space, equipped with the latest technology. Among other things, there are studios and hardware for video editing. Moreover, the winners will receive a certificate for 4,000 euros for the purchase of video equipment and 3,000 euros in cash to improve the quality of the content and promote the channel on YouTube. And of course, in order to win a new audience!

We look forward to your incredible ideas!
