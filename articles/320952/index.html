How were you looking for an investment? What is the size of the team at the moment? What were the difficulties in product development? Are you developing or outsourcing?

Team - 6 people at full employment. Part-time from 5 to 10 people. My partner and I started it all with our own money, having invested literally 20 thousand rubles.

What is the main lesson you have learned from your personal experience related to the business up to now?

There are several:

  • I was convinced from my experience that you should never stop learning.
  • We need to find time for something other than business. Not to devote all his time to him, but to develop in some other directions.
  • Need to trust people more.

What is the most difficult task you had to solve?

This is an emotional burnout that I have been experiencing for a year.

How to cope?

First, do not give up what you are doing, no matter how much you would like. Secondly, to distract from the business, to develop in some other areas, while not letting the business take its course.

If five years ago I told you where you are now, would you do this?

I think I would. This type of activity - entrepreneurship - develops a person very well as a person.

Our service is an example of such an idea. Another example is that publishers of mobile games lose a lot of money on piracy.

What advice can you give to more experienced people?

Creating a company, you need to look for partners who can close those blocks that you can not close yourself. Now I will tell you how to search ...

If you are looking for a technical director, you can publish a task somewhere on the stock exchange. Offer to make it, for example, 20 candidates. If there is a person who makes the best of all, for an adequate price, with high quality and on time, then you need to communicate with him in more detail. It turns out such a microtender.

I also wanted to say about investments.

Do not take them. In my opinion, you need to do a project for your money. Investments discourage, and people very often make mistakes in this state. And when you have every month as the last, you are very collected and make only the right decisions or ... close.

As for the workflow ...

You must immediately try to automate repetitive work or delegate it. Do not overwork: it is better to work in standard mode 5/2.

Another tip is to keep an eye on finances.

I, for example, the first year or two, when the money was running out, sat down in the middle of the month and watched the pace of income. By the end of the middle of the month, I predicted whether I would have money to pay my employees and myself. If I understood that there was not enough money, I began to quickly look for ways to earn them.

In our case, we did custom development, advertising, promotion - and survived. And it seems to me that this is more correct than taking investments.

We also tested various promotion methods, and it helped us.

And which method was the most effective for you?

Article advertising on various Internet resources. In addition, integration with popular CMS (Bitrix, inSales) worked well.

How do you see the future of the project?

We have two options: either sell it and go somewhere else, or continue to develop. In the latter case, it is of interest to me to enter the international market. I see prospects there: there we have only one noticeable competitor, and we are no worse, and in many respects even better than him. So we have the opportunity to take some share there.

Imagine such a situation: the Post of Russia comes and offers you twice as much money as you expected in your wildest dreams. Will you sell the company?

I think yes. But she will not come and will not offer (smiles).
