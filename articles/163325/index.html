MTS launches branded smartphone with support for NFC-payments

MTS released the first in its line of branded gadgets smartphone with NFC support and contactless payments.



This model was the first carrier NFC-smartphone that can be used for contactless payments without thinking about additional devices. At the same time, MTS 965 is not among the most advanced smartphones in terms of technical characteristics:

- Android 2.3
- touch screen, 3.5 "diagonal, 320x480 resolution
- camera 5 MP
- built-in memory 512 MB, memory cards microSD (TransFlash)
- support Bluetooth, NFC, Wi-Fi, 3G, GPS
- weight 125 g, battery 1250 mAh

It will cost about 5 thousand rubles: it is significantly cheaper than other models with NFC support on the domestic market.

The launch of a carrier phone with NFC support in an actively growing segment of budget smartphones will contribute to the popularization of NFC services. In the coming months, MTS plans to increase the use of contactless payments among its subscribers by at least a third by selling authorized brand-name Mastercard by Mastercard
- told RIA Novosti MTS press secretary Valeria Kuzmenko.

To use NFC technology, the owner is offered to change his SIM card to a special one from MTS, which will ensure the passage of NFC payments. This SIM-card will be emulated MasterCard MTS-Bank credit card. Payment using such a card will be free of charge worldwide. The bank does not plan to take payments for card servicing either. If you have a compatible with the wireless payment technology Master Card Pay Pass payment terminal, you will only need to bring a smartphone in order to pay. If the transaction amount is up to 1 thousand rubles, authorization of the bank card holder will not be required; for more expensive purchases, you will need to enter the PIN code issued by the bank.

According to MTS, contactless payment services have a good potential, and for the next five years, the currently absent domestic market for NFC payments may increase to 50 billion rubles a year.
