December 14, Beeline will present its tablet

During the telecommunications exhibition in Beijing, the Chinese manufacturer introduced the ZTE Light tablet. The first mobile operator to sell it will be the Russian VimpelCom.

It is expected that it will be a device with a 7-inch display, memory, expanding to 32 GB, and working under Android. The price of M2 set by VimpelCom, according to sources, is 13,000 rubles.



ZTE Light tablet on Android 2.1. Equipped with 512Mb RAM + 512ROM, 3G, wifi, bluetooth, GPS, 3-mp camera and an SD expansion slot supporting cards up to 32Gb. Resistive screen with a resolution of 800x480px. Weight - 400 grams. The battery declared by the manufacturer works up to 10 hours.
