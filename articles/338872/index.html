" Optimal movement "


What it is: automation in the pharmaceutical industry - quality control systems for the execution of tasks, automatic calculation of the necessary parameters based on an innovative system of algorithms, automatic recording of solutions, applied volumetric flasks, and data collection from equipment in the control laboratories of pharmaceutical industries.

What is the point: the company's solutions help unite a large number of monitoring tools: behind the premises (for example, monitoring and evaluating ventilation parameters in clean and conditionally clean rooms), employees (monitoring operations using NFC tags and QR codes), documentation , equipment maintenance and other production parameters.

The project has no competitors in Russia and offers solutions several times cheaper than foreign analogues. At the same time, the company's developments comply with GMP standards (international system of norms, rules and guidelines regarding production in the field of medicine and food industry), thanks to which they can be brought to markets throughout the world.

In the future: the company plans to enter the European market and is negotiating with Western partners to launch testing of the company's products at European manufactures.


Stress sensor


What it is: a complex of monitoring the state of the human cardiovascular system with a prognosis depth of 2-3 days. The device performs the functions of a pulse oximeter and an electrocardiograph and provides an assessment of the state of human health, and in a form that is understandable to a layman. Data received by the device can be accumulated and transferred to a PC / smartphone with specialized software.

What is the trick: the main innovative component of the project is software, whose task is to predict changes in the state of the human cardiovascular system. In particular, the device may respond to changes in fitness, increase / decrease in stress resistance, and may also note changes in the state of the body, not associated with an increase in load - this can be regarded as an alarm signal. In addition, the gadget can collect information necessary for remote diagnostics of the user's health status - data can be sent for analysis to the attending physician.

FactoryFinder


What it is: an online service for the effective work of the purchasing department, which allows you to automate the search and analysis of suppliers' offers, compare prices, delivery terms and conditions. As a result, the company receives savings of up to 45% when choosing the best deals.

What is the point: this resident of Technopark works as a global marketplace and this is different from the self-written solutions that companies develop to interact with their own suppliers. Now the service brings together more than ten thousand suppliers, engineers and purchasers. For each of them, the system conducts an audit of reliability - and does not allow to get into the "top" of profitable offers to suppliers who (by accident or intentionally) set incredibly low prices.

This approach helps to deal with kickbacks within companies and to check procurement managers - whether or not they choose the most profitable offer from all possible. By the way, sorting in this case can be conducted not only by price, but also by supplier reliability, as well as other conditions - for example, you can compare different suppliers taking into account different logistics costs. The team continues to work on the project and make improvements to it: the current version of the system is the fourth.

In the future: the project team continues to optimize the service and enter new product and geographical markets (to Europe and the USA).

ATG Service Gene


What it is: a company that performs custom molecular biological experiments (oligonucleotide synthesis, DNA sequencing, gene synthesis, peptide synthesis, protein expression, and others). Works with biological and pharmaceutical companies, state scientific organizations of Russia and the CIS.

What is the trick: this company-resident Technopark ITMO has developed technology for the gene encoding of proteins responsible for therapeutic processes in the body. With its help, the company creates gene therapy drugs to speed up regeneration, prolong wakefulness, slow down aging (human genes are injected that encode certain proteins necessary for various therapeutic purposes).

One of their developments is a gene therapy analgesic, one dose of which is able to have an effect on the body for seven days (the term was chosen taking into account the need for prolonged anesthesia and possible tasks for subsequent treatment).

In humans, there are proteins that are responsible for blocking pain, for example, some types of endorphins. When the body is weakened, it cannot produce this protein in sufficient quantity. If you enter into the body the DNA that encodes endorphin, then its production will increase, and the person will no longer feel pain. Thus, we do not introduce a chemical substance into the body, especially from the morphine or opioid series.

- Ilya Dukhovlinov, founder of ATG Service Gene [ source ]

The drug is injected by intramuscular injection - the DNA encoding endorphin enters the muscle tissue, resulting in a new focus of endorphins production in the body, which is active for a week.

In the future: the company plans to patent the development at the international level. To date, patents have been received in Russia, USA, Japan and Brazil.


Parseq lab


What it is: a company developing solutions for molecular genetic diagnostics (for example, for the detection of hereditary diseases).

What is the trick: at first the company was engaged exclusively in the creation of reagents for biochemical studies. According to the results of such studies, the specialist received data that require further careful processing and decoding. As a result, the company came to create a comprehensive product that allows you to conduct research and correctly interpret its results.

For example, a test system for detecting genetic diseases in newborns is used both in Russia and abroad, and turns out to be cheaper than alternative methods.

In this case, the child takes a blood sample, on the basis of which the genome is sequenced (decoding the sequence of the DNA molecule within the gene that determines the formation of the disease). The analysis allows to determine the clinically significant mutations: you can identify about 500 different options for deviation from the norm.

In the future: this project has passed clinical trials in European laboratories and can be officially used as a diagnostic solution not only in Russia, but also in the EU. At the moment, the company is preparing new developments that are in the process of designing and testing.
