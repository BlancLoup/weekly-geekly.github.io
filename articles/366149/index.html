Moscow plans to create a network of space food vending machines



In the next couple of years, Moscow may become a place where even a hungry astronaut will not disappear. JSC Space Lab is preparing a new project for the placement of machines selling "space food" - tubes and other packages with food, which cosmonauts usually eat in flight. This was told by the general director of JSC Andrei Vedernikov.

The initiators of the project are counting on interest in the project from Muscovites and guests of the capital. If there really is interest, the company plans to supply up to 500 such machines in various places of the capital, including schools.
