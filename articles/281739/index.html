Competition for the best publication about the development, design or testing of a mobile application



We, Appodeal, together with Habrahabrom decided to hold a creative (in the community concept) contest for all those users, readers and, of course, experts in the field of mobile development, for the best publication on the topic with the tag "#Appodeal".

What for? It's simple - good content takes effort. And good content requires high-quality experience - otherwise, what to describe? With this, everything is clear - truism.

Therefore, we decided to award 30, 20 and 10 thousand rubles to the authors of the three best publications on Habrahabr in the subject of mobile development. All that fits thematically under the following hubs:

  • Mobile Application Development
  • Android development
  • IOS development
  • Mobile App Testing
  • Mobile Application Design



As you can see, we have tried to interest not only Objective C, Swift and Java specialists, but also testers and, of course, talented designers. Because the world is not locked in on some developers, and, as we know, quite a large number of a wide variety of professionals always take part in creating high-quality and popular mobile applications.

Naturally, the seats are distributed to the usual habrew user voting for the publication - we did not change the usual scheme. But, in addition to this, the competitive jury, consisting of the company's employees, will monitor the absence of markups and the full disclosure of the topic by the author.
