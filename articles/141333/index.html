Conference "Online stores of Ukraine 2012"



In Kharkiv, on April 20, 2012, the conference "Online stores of Ukraine 2012" will take place. The event is focused on representatives of online stores - owners, top managers, internet marketers, as well as all those who are engaged in e-commerce.

The aim of the Conference organizers - Shoplists and WEB4PRO online stores catalog - is to provide the maximum number of practical examples of the correct construction and maintenance of online business. Therefore, the program includes reports from professionals, working cases, master classes and high-quality expert consultations.



A small list of reports that you hear at the conference:

  • Legal issues on the organization of online stores. (Galina Bakum)
  • 10 simple steps to attract visitors to the online store. (Elena Gunina)
  • Proper logistics = increasing your income. (Dmitry Seleznev)
  • How do you lose customers and how to deal with it. (Galina Vityuk)
  • 10 profitable ideas of 2012. (Evgeny Musienko)
  • Checks can not be avoided but the consequences - you can. (Vitaly Makhno)
  • Why is there no effect from the promotion of the store? (Yuri Kushnerov)
  • Security online store: problems and solutions. (Dmitry Suslov)
  • Marketing design online store. (Roman Rybalchenko)
  • Copyright protection for photos on the Internet. (Ilya Kryuchkov)
  • Promotion of online stores - what entrepreneurs forget. (Oleg Gavrilyuk)
  • Why buy from competitors? (Stanislav Matyushenko)
