Mail.Ru will be registered in Yekaterinburg

Mail.Ru will open a branch in Yekaterinburg, reports Delovoy Kvartal.



On one of the leading Internet services related to work, a vacancy was posted for the head of the Ekaterinburg office of Mail.Ru, whose tasks include not only opening a branch, but also organizing sales, ensuring profitability, and attracting large customers. Thus, the goal of creating a branch, apparently, the development of regional sales. Representatives Mail.Ru officially refused to disclose details, citing the fact that the corporation does not comment on their plans.
