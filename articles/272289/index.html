Construct your reality: hackathon on virtual reality will be held December 19-20, 2015 in Hypercube

What to do if you are not satisfied with the world around us? Of course, create your own, virtual! You can have a great weekend with pizza, cookies and virtual reality on a specialized hackathon, which will be held December 19-20, 2015 in Skolkovo Hypercube.



To participate in the hackathon , you must register , but you can also register your idea in advance and try to find like-minded people .

The hackathon is organized by Microsoft and the Skolkovo Mobile Technology Center , the general partner is mixAR's VR & AR community . The main companies involved in the topic of VR in Russia (and not only) are VRARLab , Fibrum , App in the Air , VISR VR , PinkApp Games as partners of the hackathon.



Why is this hackathon completely unmissable ...


  • As many as three nominations (games, educational projects, corporate VR) - and valuable prizes in each of them;
  • A huge number of people pursuing the same goal - to make cool VR-based applications and games;
  • Educational lectures - if you didn‚Äôt know anything about VR development, then feel free to come: we‚Äôll tell and show everything in real time. Although if you prepare in advance, then there will be more time for development on the hackathon;
  • Extra bonus: the ability to scan yourself with kinekt and use the model in the application;
  • Hakaton in the heart of technology and innovation - Skolkovo hypercube. This is a weekend in the country, which is useful not only for the mind, but also for health!


What will happen?



It will be an exciting night of coding, a lot of drive and communication! A detailed program of the event will soon appear on the site . Most importantly, in addition to the traditional for hackathon events (exchange of ideas, team building, coding, lunch / dinner / breakfast, performances of teams) on our hackathon will also be:
  • Opening plenary report on the future of virtual reality
  • VR / AR round table on which it will be possible to discuss on the applicability of virtual reality technologies in business, in education, in the entertainment industry
  • Educational program in which we will teach you to develop VR applications on Unity from scratch


What to take with you


  • The most important thing is friends and good mood!
  • Computer with Unity 5 , Visual Studio 2015 Community Edition and the installed package for developing universal Windows applications or Windows Phone 8.1 SDK
  • A smartphone on Windows Phone 8.1 or Windows Phone 10 - you can insert it into the cardboard issued on the spot. Although if you have a phone on a different platform, or no phone at all, or it is not smart enough, come anyway!
  • Optionally, bring your team and idea with you - but even if they are not there, register and come, you will not regret!
  • If you have never developed a VR application, you can preview a course on creating virtual and augmented reality applications on the Windows platform.
  • We will have food, we will have pizza, we will have internet - but as practice shows, this is always not enough on hackathons! Bring with you chocolates and alternative ways to access the Internet - just in case. And also a toothbrush, a favorite pillow and a book to read at night.
