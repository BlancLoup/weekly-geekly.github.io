Huawei Tecal RH2288H V2 - First Impressions

Perhaps one of the advantages of working in an IT trading company is the opportunity to work with various equipment. Constantly expanding the list of our partners, we also receive from them equipment for testing and just for dating. At the end of 2013, another player appeared on the server hardware and storage systems market of Russia - Huawei . The main focus of Huawei is telecommunications equipment, the DPRK sales market. But in recent years, the company is actively developing new areas and expanding its market. Thanks to our acquaintance with representatives of the company, one of the last guests of our test lab was the server Tecal RH2288H V2 . As the name implies - this is the second generation of this 2U server.





When we received a box with a server - the level of our interest was quite high, because the company will have to enter a fairly developed and already occupied market.
But we will start from the beginning and tell you about the technical characteristics. The server supports the installation of one / two Intel Xeon E5-2600 / E5-2600V2 series processors, 24 memory modules, with a total capacity of up to 768Gb and from 8 to 26 2.5 "(or 12 3.5") sizes, including 2 drives installed with back of the server.



The RAID controller (LSI SAS2208) supports levels 0, 1, 10, 5, 50, 6, 60 and carries the cache in 512Mb or 1Gb, but the controller's performance is similar to the blade mezzanine.



The server is also equipped with 2 or 4 1Gb ports or 2 10Gb ports. Up to 7 PCI-e slots are available for server expansion: 6 PCI-e x8 and one PCI-e x16. But with USB ports, they were a little greedy - only 2 ports are available on the front and 2 on the rear panel. For cooling, 4 coolers with the possibility of hot replacement are used



Power supply units (manufactured by LiteON) is 460W or 800W, just as naturally with hot swapping.



Remote server management is performed using BMC, which, in general, is not functionally different from competing products - iDrag or iLo.



In general, the server left a pretty good impression of itself. Well-made body, good assembly.



There are some remarks on the design features, for example, an attached battery with a cable attached to it slightly hinders the removal of the duct cover - it is impossible to remove it without removing the riser, and the riser, in turn, is easier to unscrew using a screwdriver.



And now I propose to consider what advantages and disadvantages this Huawei company has in comparison with other market players - HP , DELL , IBM , Supermicro , Fujitsu .

  1. Huawei server hardware and storage systems are aimed at medium and large businesses. This is evident from the equipment line, i.e. Huawei has no models in the Tower form factor and no servers on the lower-end Intel Xeon E3 and AMD processors. Also in the line there are no simple shelf expansion servers.

  3. And lastly, let's look at what is included in the standard warranty that comes with Huawei servers and storage systems. So, the warranty period is 3 years, with 24x7 support, but the claimed recovery time is 60 calendar days. The replacement process for failed components is as follows:
    • The application is started by calling the Huawei SC.
    • you contact those. a specialist to determine the cause of a breakdown; if necessary, he will be sent the system logs.
    • according to the results a malfunction report is sent to you. After that you need to come to the SC (you need to have an act and faulty equipment with you), where you will be replaced with equipment.

    Needless to say for Business servers, such equipment downtime is not very acceptable. There are 2 ways out of this situation:
    • Store spare parts for the most frequently failing equipment (RAM, Hard drives, Power supplies)
    • Purchase an extended warranty package. Approximate cost extra. guarantees range from 2% to 10% of the cost of the server being purchased depending on the level of support.
