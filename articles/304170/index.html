Admission to the "free" magistracy in the United States. Part one

Despite the fairly large amount of information on this topic in the open spaces of the network, I want to share my experience - maybe someone will find something new and useful for themselves.

So, my experience in applying for a magistracy with full funding consists of four main stages:

  • Search / analysis / comparison / selection of programs;
  • Preparation and passing the necessary exams;
  • Preparation and sending of all necessary documents (especially the letter of motivation and letters of recommendation);
  • Getting the results of the competition and the next steps for enrollment in the university

1. Search / analysis / comparison / selection of programs


After making a decision, you must determine who you want to do. In the states, future undergraduates are free to choose a specialty, regardless of the bachelor's degree . However, do not be in a hurry to rejoice: in this case it is almost impossible to obtain financing. You can save only experience in the related field.

So, who should the 4th year student of the VTIPO specialty do for two years of working as a programmer? Of course continue your favorite business in the field of computer science!

Despite the fact that the United States is the epicenter of the IT world, there are many other countries where computer and engineering sciences are taught at a level no worse than Ivy League universities, and maybe even better. I also considered Germany, namely DAAD, however I did not have time for the deadlines (if memory does not refuse, the deadline for submission of documents is November 5). This is probably for the best.

Today, the Internet provides all the knowledge of humanity, the main thing is to be able to focus on the subject of the search and sort unnecessary / incorrect information.

"Top USA Classes Computer Science" - my first search query in Google, which immediately put me on the link . Using this link, I looked at all 50 university sites, and found only a few educational institutions that provide Full Tuition Scholarship by MS in Computer Science (MIT, Cornell, Duke, etc). As it turned out the deadline at their December 15, and I did not have time. Here I also found for myself such concepts as Master of Engineering and Master of Science (MEng & MS).

In short, these two concepts are different in that MEng will not be able to receive a PhD in the future , and he does not need to defend Thesis Project (diploma). In principle, MS will not be able to get a PhD either, if instead of Thesis Project it chooses regular subjects in the last semester. As you probably understood by the name, MS begins its training on becoming a scientist (but this does not mean that you will be obliged to enter the PhD).
