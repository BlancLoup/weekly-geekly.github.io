"Norilsk Nickel" and "Gazinformservice" completed the 1st stage of building the protection of corporate ERP-system

In January 2019, replication of the unified enterprise management system was completed, which ensured coverage of the entire production chain of the company in a single operating model based on a centralized ERP platform.

According to the head of the IT infrastructure protection department, Andrey Kulpin, such a large-scale digital transformation required the development and implementation of a reliable, modern SAP protection system.
