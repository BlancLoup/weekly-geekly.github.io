Jaman is a video shaman.

The problem of the availability of good films to the American audience was interested in the creators of Jaman (http: // jaman .com /) in January of this year. Ten months later, the guys were able to improve the interface, thereby hitting the top ten most famous video startups in America.

There are two options for the distribution of world cinema. The first is an innovative way to transfer images (according to the statement of Jeff Berg, the executive director, the quality of the picture is sometimes higher than on DVD) to the monitor using native software. In this case, you are just a TV viewer, since the film is broadcast in non-stop mode (four pictures available to the Jaman user every day). The second method is more humane, because it consists in transferring the file to your computer thanks to the P2P network. After registration, three pictures become available, after which you can think about the need to pay for such a service.

In addition to delivering unique films, on top of this channel, the company also creates a social network - on the right side of the movie viewing window there is an interactive control panel, with which you can include comments from other viewers, as well as comment on one or another fragment of the film. ‚ÄúWith the latest interface updates, watching movies and chatting with friends has become synonymous,‚Äù says Guro Dylan, owner of NASDAQ: INFA, which is developing Jaman.com.

In any case, more useful features were added: so finally a good search engine appeared on the database and the ability to tag files, so that after a Bollywood query, you can get a list of all available Indian movies on Jaman servers.

Despite the fact that the fathers of the Internet shout in all voices about the impossibility of completely replacing TV technologies due to channel congestion, more optimistic forecasts repeat the likely improvement of already existing networks by more than 1000 times. This means that the time Jaman.com will come in the near future.
