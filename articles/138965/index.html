Preview of 2 smartphones of a new line - Sony Xperia P and Sony Xperia U

As you probably remember, at the CES conference, Sony showed off its first smartphone without an Ericsson console - the Sony Xperia S. Yesterday in Barcelona were presented two new items of this line, which was called the NXT .



The Sony Xperia P and Sony Xperia U have an almost identical case design, just like the Xperia S , with a thin transparent strip at the bottom of the case.

Sony Xperia P has a 4-inch Reality Display screen with support for the new White Magic technology. With the help of it, we managed to achieve perfect white color, for this each pixel was supplemented with an additional white subpixel. Due to this, the smartphone screen was made even brighter.



Sony Xperia P Technical Summary:
  • Processor : dual-core 1GHz (NovaThor U8500 chipset)
  • Screen : 4 inches, Bravia Engine, resolution 960x540 pixels
  • Memory : 16GB internal, 1GB of RAM
  • OS : Android 2.3.7 Gingerbread (update to Android 4.0 ICS in the second quarter of 2012)
  • Camera : main 8MP, panorama, HD Video, front VGA
  • Dimensions : 122 x 59.5 x 10.5 mm weight 120 g
  • Optional : accelerometer, compass, NFC chip
  • Data transfer : GSM, HSDPA (3g), microUSB, HDMI
  • Battery : 1300mAh

The smartphone case is made of aluminum using the Unibody technology; it will be sold in three colors: silver, black, and red.



The smartphone Sony Xperia U is smaller in size, the diagonal of its screen is 3.5 inches. He has the same characteristics, but is more focused on the female audience. The transparent strip is highlighted in different colors, depending on the chosen theme or the image viewed on the screen.

He uses a simpler 5MP camera. Battery capacity 1320mAh. The choice will be presented four colors of the case black, white, pink or yellow. In addition, the kit will be replaceable panels for the bottom.
