Tutoronline.ru - how it all began

It all started in 2010. It was then that two friends - mathematicians Alexander Pashkov and Valery Solovyov, who together for 6 years participated in the creation and promotion of Internet projects aimed at a student audience - had the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating their own business, in which they could combine the Internet, services and education. As a result, a service was developed and launched that provides instant help to schoolchildren in case of difficulties with homework. The idea was to respond to the first request of a specialized teacher and conduct classes in the virtual Classroom.

In Russia, at that time, such a service as skype tutoring was actively used, but the market of remote tutoring services itself was just beginning to take shape. Having studied the demand, using personal experience (Alexandra‚Äôs wife, a Russian teacher, worked on Skype for a long time with the children, and also worked as tutors in mathematics during their student years), Alexander and Valery decided to create a company tutoronline.ru that could type of service to a new level.

Business model
The main issue to be resolved was to choose the right business model. As a result of studying foreign experience, primarily American, it became clear that there are several models of organizing such a business on the market:
  • companies providing access to a tutor base and a platform for conducting classes. Such companies see their main task in performing intermediary functions and in developing or supporting a platform;
  • companies specializing exclusively in software and "renting out" their "classroom";
  • companies that provide services and coordinate the interaction of the student and the teacher, and the selection of the teacher is carried out automatically by the system.

It took several months to test foreign services and their models. As a result, it was decided that the service model has the greatest potential. It is more difficult to start (due to the construction of a system of interaction with teachers, supervision, schedules, etc.), but it is more responsive to the trend ‚Äúget everything in one place‚Äù or ‚Äúthrough one click‚Äù. The founders were assigned 2 serious tasks:
  1. make a convenient and high-quality IT platform;
  2. develop a system of interaction between the student and the teacher, so that the number of actions on the part of the child is minimal and assistance is provided as soon as possible.

At this stage, the first employees and contractors appeared in the company. Development of the platform (site, IT part, billing) was entrusted to the contractor. The curators of the pedagogical process department and the business analyst were taken to the staff, and a little later the call center operator and the sales manager joined them.

Since the formation of a clear idea until the commercial launch took about 8 months (the launch took place on 01/03/2011).
