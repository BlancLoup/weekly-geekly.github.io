Vomp

Later, at the request of a still small audience, additional functions began to be added, such as searching the database of existing films (here is a special thank you for the base for the guys from the Cinema Base), the ability to download videos, etc.
Initially, VOMP was decoded as Vkontakte Open Media Player, but based on what was planned for the future, the project will probably become just VOMP.
