Life in Belarus. Work in EPAM!

We all ever think about changing our place of residence. Someone does not see the prospects for professional development at home, someone just wants to change the situation and get to know a different culture, and someone is looking for a way to move for personal reasons. EPAM Systems provides a unique opportunity for IT-specialists from neighboring countries to learn such a close, but at the same time unknown Belarus, and build a career in one of the largest IT-companies in the CIS.



The relocation program has been in EPAM since May 2013. During this time, more than 500 employees took the opportunity to change their place of residence and continue their professional development. Now the possibility of relocation appeared among IT specialists from neighboring countries who had not previously worked for the company.

In the ranking of countries in terms of life, published in the UN Human Development Report for 2013, Belarus has become a recognized leader among all CIS countries: a high standard of living, and at the same time - housing, education and cultural expenses are lower than in most Western countries and Eastern Europe.

The mild climate, natural products, beautiful nature, a developed education and healthcare system, diverse cultural life and a dynamic IT environment make Belarus one of the most comfortable countries to live in the former USSR.

Why EPAM Belarus?

EPAM is the largest provider of project (custom) software development and solutions in Central and Eastern Europe. Today the company has offices in 19 countries. It was the office in Belarus, in Minsk, that became the first EPAM Development Center - the history of the company began with it. Today, the Belarusian office is the largest division of the company in terms of the number of specialists employed.

For each employee there is a project in accordance with his knowledge, professional and personal interests. EPAM uses a whole range of technologies - both modern and well-known: Java, .NET, ASP.NET, BI, C / C ++, JavaScript, PHP, SharePoint, Oracle and many others.

How to get to EPAM?

The path to EPAM Belarus begins with sending a resume to our recruiting service. Resume processing usually takes several days; After that, a recruiter will contact you by phone, Skype or email. The task of such communication is to clarify your professional skills, technologies and methodologies that you used at previous places of work, as well as the reasons for changing your place of work and residence. Even if you have an interesting work experience, but at the moment there is no suitable position for you, our recruiters will certainly continue the conversation in order to understand which of the projects you can apply for.

Moving to Belarus is easy

EPAM Systems provides legal, logistic and material assistance when moving. If an employee needs visa support, it will be provided. Transportation costs, including payment of baggage, are fully covered, both for the new employee and his family members. In the first month of stay in the country, the company provides free living space. The new employee and his family can enjoy all the bonuses from the company's social package, including free medical care at all medical centers working with EPAM.
