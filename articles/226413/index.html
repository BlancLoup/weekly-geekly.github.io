The sum of opinions: what users say in the test drive Nokia Lumia 1520

Hello friends.
As you may have noticed, test-drives of our devices have been held at Habrahabr for quite a long time. We were the first to speak in such a format since the appearance of the Nokia Lumia 920 on the Russian market. Now, a whole range of Nokia smartphones, including the flagship device with a large screen Lumia 1520, is available for tearing apart by hackers and any other Internet users.


Photo: Kirill Kotlyarenko

To date, the project has accumulated quite a lot of reviews of this device, and we decided to make a brief squeeze of opinions. In order to avoid accusations of the advertising nature of these posts, we remind you that everyone can take a device for a test drive and, having made up their own opinion, can tell about personal experience on the project pages.

To begin with, it is worth noting that after reading the texts, we made two important conclusions for ourselves. The first is that although users expressed a number of complaints about the device (however, most often of an individual nature), Lumia 1520 was generally liked by everyone. The second point is that the Windows Phone 8 platform has ceased to be something outlandish for users compared to last year. This time, users talked more about the characteristics and parameters of the smartphone, but did not describe the experience of the first acquaintance with the smartphone on WP and practically did not compare the OS with other mobile platforms.


Photo: victorr

Having become accustomed to the size of the device, users began to evaluate the technical characteristics of the Lumia 1520, starting, obviously, from the display:

Ilya Lyakh
In this model, a six-inch IPS-matrix of 1920x1080 pixels is installed. As a protective coating for the display, Gorilla Glass 2 with an anti-reflective layer and an oleophobic coating is used. You can adjust the readability, saturation and color temperature using the detailed menu. Thus, the display of this model can be called one of the best among Full HD-screens.


Dmitry Obydenkov
The first time when I got the phone on the street, I could not understand what was happening: the picture was not displayed as usual. Inquiring on the Internet, I found out that it was Assertive Display technology, which allows to increase the contrast of the picture in bright sunshine. And this technology really works, readability in sunlight 1520 is much better than that of the Korean and American flagships!

Dmitry Obydenkov
It is worth paying attention to the video ability, more precisely to the Nokia Rich Recording technology. One can argue for a long time that these are only advertising tricks, but having recently visited a concert from my 925 I received a recording with a very good sound, most phones simply cannot adequately record a loud sound. To record sound in the 1520 already provides four microphones, marketers call it directional stereo sound. Unfortunately, I did not have time to go to any concert in five days, but judging by the records on youtube.com, the quality has become even better.


Dmitry also spoke in detail about Windows Phone and favorite applications:

One of the advantages of Windows Phone is its speed, it turns against expensive devices: on $ 600 smartphones, the system works as fast as on $ 100 smartphones. Perhaps there are differences in the speed of work, but they are minimal. The only thing I was able to point out is almost instant application launch, while less powerful smartphones are a bit more thoughtful in this regard.
One of my favorite apps from Nokia is Projector . It works very simply: open beam.nokia.com in the browser, launch the application, show the QR from the browser to the phone - that's all, you can broadcast the image from your smartphone directly to the browser via the Internet. Personally, this allows me to show Power Point presentations right from the phone, saving me from having to take a laptop with me for fifteen minutes.



Photo: Dmitry Obydenkov

In the home environment, you can use the Media Center application. Just a DLNA player. The device, by the way, supports Wi-Fi ac.



Photo: Dmitry Obydenkov
