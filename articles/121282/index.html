Samsung Chromebook: to be or not to be? We need your voice

Good Tuesday, Habra!

As you know, just the other day we introduced our new laptop on the Chrome operating system - Samsung Chromebook 5 series. At the moment we are faced with a difficult choice: to start its deliveries to the domestic market in the near future or to wait. There are supporters of the speedy launch of the Hrombuk in Russia, while, on the contrary, someone urges not to rush, arguing that the gadget is useless if not connected to the network, because its offline capabilities are very limited, and so on.

That is why we decided to turn to the Habra community for advice. Please take part in the survey and help us decide whether to launch the Samsung Chromebook 5 series laptop on the Russian market or wait for the Chrome OS functionality to properly finish? Choose one of the answers below, which is closest to you. And, of course, informative comments on the topic are welcome. Thank you in advance! You can read more about Chrombuki here .


UPD. : friends, we realized that we are more interested in your opinion and comments than any standard answers, so we decided not to drive you into the framework of the survey. Thank you for understanding!
