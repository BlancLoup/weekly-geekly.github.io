Secrets of emergency recovery of self-control



You need to start an important conversation, make a presentation or take a new project: this is the culmination of a long stage of preparation and hard work. But suddenly a hard-won belief in oneself evaporates. In this article, we will tell you how to return confidence in a matter of minutes before the start of a presentation or before an exceptionally important event .

‚ÄúIf you are in a place where you can do deep breathing exercises or stretch yourself without drawing too much attention, do it,‚Äù he says. ‚ÄúSometimes we wind ourselves up badly because we focus on a high result, which is simply beyond our control. You cannot predict how people will respond to your message, whether they like your work, ‚Äùsays Aziz Gazipura.
