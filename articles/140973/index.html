Top Favorite Media Articles - Editor's Choice in Google News

Anna Eremenko, Partner Relationship Manager

Today, Google News has a new feature - Editor's Choice. This is a special section on the main page of Google News, where the 5 best materials of a particular publication are presented - according to its editorial.

The Editor's Choice window is to the right of the news feed on news.google.com. Under their logo, partner publications place links to materials - whether they are articles, interviews, infographics, or collections of photographs. Using the arrows, you select the sources of interest, and if you are logged in, you can mark which editions you want to read more often and which ones you can skip with a special cursor in the lower part of the window. If you liked the article, click +1 and share it with your friends on Google+.

In Editors' Choice, you can read the world's largest media outlets - Reuters, Time, The Washington Post, The New Yorker, Los Angeles Times and The Wall Street Journal. Now they have been joined by leading Russian publications - for example, RIA Novosti, Vedomosti, Kommersant, Rossiyskaya Gazeta, Gazeta.ru, Lenta.ru and many others. The list of publications is constantly updated - scroll through the tape to the end.

Happy reading and good news!
