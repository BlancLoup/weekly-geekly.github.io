HP SAS License Key for B320i + HP DL360 Gen8 (non-standard SAS drives)


Recently, our old HP DL 360 G5 server with the b200i controller began to die. The 2 sas 146Gb screws were in the mirror and at some point the second slot stopped working properly and the new backup screws could not be raised.
It was decided to update the server, but not so expensive and angry.
I took an inexpensive DL360e Gen8 + power supply to it + hard SAS Article 507127-TV1.
The seller offered precisely these hard ones, since they are cheaper, and why cheaper, no one could really explain, the description says - analog 507127-B21 . Well, I think they are tough and they are tough in Africa, I did not go into details.
Well, the server flew to me, unpacked, inserted the screws, but somehow strangely they entered there:



Well, the screws are cheap, not new, let them stick. Not critical.

I started to understand that yes, as I found out the following, that only the screws that are designed for it, i.e., work with the new Gen8 servers. those that are published in the specification to the server. This screw is listed there (original), but instead of it I took an analogue 507127-TV1.
