Blogs




We think that all marketers who specialize in the promotion of mobile applications, know that most of the fresh ideas and chips are drawn from English-language articles. Therefore, we paid attention to the selection of foreign resources of various directions.

1) MobileMarketer
Without exaggeration, the leading publication on the news of the world of mobile marketing, media and commerce. The purpose of the resource is to help marketers understand how to use a mobile channel or combinations of channels for branding and attracting customers. This resource is worth reading regularly: as a rule, there are subject case studies, a lot of specifics and useful data. Despite the fact that most of these studies concern large corporations, you will also find information about smaller companies.

2) Apptamin
A blog about various aspects of mobile marketing, starting with the promotion of applications for Apple TV and ending with the optimization of search queries on Google Play. Among the many topics you will surely find one that will be interesting for you.

3) Localytics
The blog is run by a group of experts who share weekly current trends in the mobile marketing world with readers. If you are looking for a reliable source of useful information, then Localytics is what you need. This blog offers a variety of topics with consistently high quality articles.

The blog is a little more than completely devoted to content marketing: you will find all the latest trends, techniques and features in this area here. There are also real cases with explanations of how to achieve the desired results with the help of content.
