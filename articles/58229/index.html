Toshiba NB200 Netbook Presented

The first NB100 netbook from the well-known company Toshiba was much less in demand than the competitors from Asus and Acer. The company could not put up with this state of affairs and released a completely new model, much more sophisticated and convenient, having many differences not only from its predecessor, but also from its competitors.



Presentation



The netbook was presented to the public at a presentation on April 21-22 in Hamburg. The event took place in two stages. The first is a small theatrical performance that demonstrates how a new netbook could change the life of famous literary heroes. ‚ÄúGot‚Äù to Hamlet, Faust, Romeo and Juliet and others. According to the stories of visitors, the organizers did their best - it turned out great and funny.

The second part began with the results of surveys of users of netbooks conducted by the company. A total of 2,500 respondents were surveyed from five European countries - Germany, Great Britain, France, Poland and Spain. These were people between the ages of 20 and 55 who already have a netbook or are about to purchase it within six months.

98.9% of respondents have a desktop computer or laptop. A netbook is not the first, but the second or third device. A Toshiba spokesman argued to the viewers that a netbook is not a gadget. Not a new electronic toy that will soon go out of fashion. A new independent class of devices that have their own advantages, such as increased battery life and modest weight and size characteristics. By the way, according to the survey, only 9.8% of users bought a netbook because of low prices, the rest made this choice because of its autonomy (33%) and modest size / weight (32%).

Specifications



  • 10.1-inch widescreen (1024 x 600 WVGA 16: 9) with LED backlight
  • processor - Intel Atom N270 (1.6 GHz) or N280 (1.66 GHz)
  • Graphics - Intel Graphics Media Accelerator (GMA) 950
  • RAM - DDR2-667 (PC2-5400) from 1 GB
  • hard drive - from 160 GB (HDD)
  • WEB-camera
  • card reader - SD; MMC; MS; MS Pro
  • network interfaces - HSDPA, HSUPA / WCDMA, Wi-Fi (802.11 b / g), Bluetooth, connection via a dedicated 10 / 100Mbps line;
  • three USB ports, one of which supports Sleep-and-Charge technology
  • weight of models - from 1.18 kg (with a 3-cell battery)
  • declared battery life - from 3 to 9 hours




Toshiba NB200 is equipped with the function of protecting the hard drive from damage during shaking. A special sensor monitors the position of the body and reports its abrupt change. The hard drive heads are instantly parked.

The cover of the Toshiba mini NB200 screen has a pleasant to the touch matte textured finish. The model will be available in four colors: black, brown, pink and white.
Modifications NB200 - two, with a different type of keyboard. Someone would prefer a keyboard with gaps between the keys, like on Sony and Apple laptops (photo below), someone will like another version of its performance.



Will be delivered with OS Microsoft Windows XP Home Edition. Price is not reported.
