Google Searchology: Search Options, Squared, Skymap, Wonderwheel

Recently, the Searchology conference held by Google for announcements of new search technologies, company products, trends and so on has ended. On Habr√© and on Twitter, we broadcasted the entire conference.



Conference slides are here.
The Google channel on Youtube has also been updated, where demonstrations of Google have been posted (Search Options, Wonderwheel, Skymap).

So, today's announcements : Google Search Options, Android SkyMap, Google Squared, Wonder Wheel

Under the cut a lot of graphics from the conference


The first significant announcement - Google Search Options - now you can manually filter search results by a variety of criteria, including temporary, trending, you can include user comments, and so on.



The second announcement - Google Wonderwheel - visualization of the search using a fractal map - round (optionally, square) images. The wheel is a "web" of keywords, between which you can switch by clicking on the "map".



Google Labs Announcement - Squared .

Squared returns a search query in the form of tables, organizing information from chaotic web pages. For example, when requesting a ‚Äúdog‚Äù Google, gathering the search criteria will compile a table by breed, weight, color, nickname and so on.



Rich Snippets Announcement - the technology adds information from third-party sites to search results - user reviews, for example. A search for people will show the details of a person with LinkedIn - his place of work, contact details, etc. Microformats are supported.

All innovations promise to activate as soon as possible.



The Skyapp application for Android is shown - using GPS and a compass, the application shows the location of the stellar bodies, the constellations (with the ability to zoom in) with names above you; It is necessary to change the angle and the map starts to turn. Impressive!
