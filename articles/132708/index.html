XP Days in Ukraine

Consider yourself a XP guru practitioner? Or just recently heard about them? Want to know more about TDD and how to use it correctly? All this and much more can be found at the XP Days Ukraine conference, which is entirely dedicated to Agile engineering practices. The first two days of the conference ( December 15-16 ) will be filled with a variety of training , master classes and meetings. The third day (December 17) will be reserved for reports , open discussions and other presentations in the format of the conference.

The conference was organized by the XP Injection Training Center in the person of Nikolai Alimenkov, Anna Alimenkova and Alexey Solntsev.
This event will highlight the main engineering practices: Unit Testing, TDD, Continuous Integration, BDD, Code Review, Refactoring, Acceptance Testing and others. Architectural issues in Agile projects, Technical Debt, developers and testers relationships, as well as many other problems of modern development will also be discussed.
