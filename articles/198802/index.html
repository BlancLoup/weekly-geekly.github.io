New Developer Economics survey

VisionMobile has launched a new survey of mobile developers . The results of the study will be published free of charge in January as part of the world's largest report on Developer Economics (including in Russian, we are translating to Apps4All). Which platforms are more profitable to use, which tools are most popular, how best to monetize applications, what trends in the market? All these questions are answered VM research.



Why is it important

In the framework of the previous study, the Russians again became the third in the number of respondents, after the Chinese and Americans, which is very nice. I hope that in this study we will show decent results. This is important in order to understand how Russian development is moving and whether there are global differences from the world situation.

Prizes

Traditionally, among all the participants are prizes: iPhone 5c, Samsung Galaxy S4, Lumia 925, AR Drone 2.0 and other gadgets. Also, everyone has traditionally been offered a month of free work at Bugsense.
