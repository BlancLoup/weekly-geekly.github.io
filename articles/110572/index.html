Domayner, screenshot and champagne!

Today, the Ticno guessing contest for two soft-gifts, announced together with seven new programs in the last post , has ended.

We got a lot of assumptions (funny and not very). One habrazhitel sent at once 20 different options with the widest possible interpretation (such as addition to browsers), but did not guess ... No one named both programs, but we have a winner who guesses one program - a screenshot.

He gets a set of champagne from Asti Martini. Details about the programs under the cut!





So the program Domainer (Ticno Domainer) - stores all your domains and information on them (databases, ftp, admin) in one place.

Itself many times came across that text files do not save. Some little things are lost, everything is ruined and not structured. It happened that I forgot to extend the domain. Enough! Domainer will put things in order: he will remind you of the extension, he will give you all the access to the domain, the databases, the ftp and the admins, on which aipishs the domain hangs and much more.

All this is local, password-protected and haughty (portable).

Link to the program - Domayner . The beta version of the program will be available for download next week, so by the New Year you will have time to clean up the mess :)



The second program - Screenshot. Everything is quite banal - several options for removing the screen, different formats for saving and uploading to the web with getting links. All this in a simple and convenient interface with a minimum of problems.

Link to the program - Screenshot . A beta version of the program will also appear next week.



Programs are completely free. I hope that they will be useful to the respected haberdressers!
