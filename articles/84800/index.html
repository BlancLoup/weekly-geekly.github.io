Who keeps the sniper rifle in the office? (curious survey by February 23)

Every man is a boy at heart, and all the boys love to play war.
That is why on the eve of the men's holiday, the research service hh.ru decided to find out what role each of our users assigns to themselves in this ‚Äúwar game‚Äù.

It all began with the question: what would you serve if you had to defend the Motherland.
And even there it was not without a surprise - along with such professions that are consistently attractive for men, like a sniper or a pilot, the military engineer and partisans made their way into the top three!
And if the appearance of the first can be explained by the desire of potential defenders of the homeland to realize the military occupational specialty obtained in military departments, we could only justify the craving for the partisan underground with a new passion of books and cinema for future apocalypses, in which the person is a lone stalker.



It is also curious that the dream of becoming an astronaut still remained on the list of leaders, even if he surrendered the position of the first Soviet dream.

As for women, the answers to the question ‚ÄúWho would you like to see your man?‚Äù Did not surprise us. The beautiful half still prefers pilots and special units soldiers. An exception was made only for military engineers who took an honorable third place.


Then we wondered how men's ideas about the ideal military specialty change depending on the real profession of the respondents.
The absolute record-holder was also the specialty of the sniper. Employees of the media, sales, IT, marketing and advertising, banks and many other industries dream of a rifle with a telescopic sight.

But this is not the most interesting.
Curious was the fact that many modern men in the event of war are ready for the benefit of the armed forces to use their existing skills and make their current military specialty .

For example, educators and trainings are not against becoming intelligence officers, and representatives of the logistics sphere are ready to go to the partisans.
So there is a reason to take a fresh look at your colleague in the office.
