1C products - service, sale from Infors

"1C: Accounting 8 for Ukraine" provides a solution to all the tasks facing the accounting department of an enterprise if the accounting department is fully responsible for accounting in the enterprise, including, for example, extracting primary documents, accounting for sales, etc. This application solution can also be used solely for accounting and tax accounting, and the tasks of automating other services, for example, a sales department, are to be solved with specialized configurations or other systems.



The structure of "1C: Accounting 8" includes a chart of accounts of accounting, corresponding to the Order of the Ministry of Finance of Ukraine "On approval of the chart of accounts of accounting and instructions for its use." The composition of accounts, the organization of analytical, currency, and quantitative accounting in accounts comply with the requirements of the legislation on accounting and reporting data in the financial statements.
