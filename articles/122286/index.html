Skype 5.5 beta - with Facebook chat

Skype has released a new beta for Windows . The most noticeable innovation is the ability to chat with friends on Facebook right in the Skype program.

Now you can stay in touch with your Facebook chat circle without leaving Skype. To do this, a new tab has appeared in the Skype contacts window, in which you will find your friends on Facebook, you can see when they are online and send them a message directly from Skype.



Total, in the new beta version you will find:
- Facebook chat - chat with your Facebook friends directly from Skype
- Contacts Facebook - a new tab in Skype containing a list of your friends from Facebook
- The ability to "like" and comment on the posts of your friends on Facebook.
- New call control panel with updated icons
- Improved mechanism for storing phone numbers in the section "Calls to phones"
- Visual improvements to the call hold mode

Download Skype 5.5 Beta and chat with your friends on Facebook and Skype. And do not forget to tell you what you think about the new beta, using the menu Skype -> Leave a review. Or follow the news about the release of the final version with these innovations.
