Jay Leno - from successful TV host to bloggers



Blog Jay Leno (Jay Leno) one of the most famous English-language TV presenters. And in the past, stand-up comedian. In the pauses of a career on TV, he devoted himself entirely to his beloved hobby. And if only you thought, they say "bye", so what, a site about a hobby of one person. And not everything is so simple. The fact is that Jay is one of the richest people in the show business. And his hobby is collecting and repairing the rarest cars and motorcycles in the world. Today he has one of the most ambitious collections of cars in the world. Many museums go out and die in the background of his garages.



What is dedicated to his site. On the site you will see the discussion and unpretentious tests of both new and old rarities remaining in a pair of copies. Often, Jay becomes just the first to know and talk about auto-premieres, which no one else knows. And, of course, if there is a queue of people willing to buy at some exotic supercar, Jay will always be the first.

Today there are more than 200 vehicles in his collection with a range of up to one hundred years old. And you guessed it is not cheap mass cars. In his collection are Ferrari, Lamborghini, Corvettes, Bentley and more. Among the car-antiques stand out, almost the only car in the world with a steam engine on the go and the equally rare Hespano Swayze (Hispano-Suiza) of 1915 with an 18-liter aircraft engine.
