How Adobe Upgrade Flash to Version 10

Recently I was interested in the question - ‚Äúhow many users do we have who already have the 10th flash installed?‚Äù. We collect statistics on flash versions on one of our projects. As of yesterday - the 10th flash is installed in almost 30% of users.
However, this is not the most interesting thing. Interesting in how this version of the flash reached such a value.
