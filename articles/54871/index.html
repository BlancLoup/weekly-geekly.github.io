The Microsoft Business Start program. 3rd year. Or "41 start-up companies have just received a grant!"

Greetings to all!

The moment has come when I can share with the community the results of the 3rd round of the Microsoft Business Start program, which allows an IT startup to receive a government grant to develop or refine a product prototype. 1M rubles for the first year, only 6M rubles. for 3 years (under certain conditions). Plus support from Microsoft.
So - the result - 41 winners!

Behind a month and a half of project examination, 128 applications, 4 jury in Moscow, 4 jury in the regions (St. Petersburg, Tomsk, Nizhny, Yekaterinburg), 80 experts working, a total of about 1,100 examination hours, listening to the jury for 20 projects a day. In general, it was not easy.


The results are published on the website of the Foundation for Assistance www.fasie.ru in the section where Microsoft-Business-Start was announced. I would venture to give a link: www.fasie.ru/anonses/anons_start09/Maikrosoft -BiznesStart-09.aspx , although, I confess, I am afraid that the site may not withstand the interest :) Immediately I warn you that the results are only the numbers of winning applications , so that those who did not participate, they will not say anything.

The jury gathered the most objective, attracting representatives of universities, IT companies and really existing investment structures. Taking into account the fact that 7-10 people participated at the same time, in fact, none of the experts was not something that did not have a decisive vote, but could not even significantly affect the total assessment of the jury. Received very positive feedback from external contributors. Thus, we were able to realize what it was all about - an objective selection of IT projects in which the state has a reason to invest.
