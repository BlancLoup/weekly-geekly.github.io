All in One Schema.org Rich Snippets






As the name implies, the All in One Schema.org Rich Snippets plugin helps you quickly and conveniently create advanced snippets (information about the website and business in a concise form that search engines display in the results). Reduce Bounce Rate








Reduce Bounce Rate is a plugin that allows you to obtain more accurate data on the percentage of failures than in Google Analytic and other well-known analytical tools. Example: one of the visitors spent 3 minutes on the site, reading the article, but without clicking links, without going to other sections of the menu, without putting likes. The user just read the article, added it to the bookmarks and left the site. For Google Analytics, such a scenario is likely to serve as an indicator of failure, and the time on the site will be fixed as 0. Reduce Bounce Rate helps to get rid of these errors. The plugin every 10 seconds tells Google Analytics that the user is still on the site. Thus, the percentage of failures in reports looks more plausible, which allows us to more objectively analyze the behavior of users on the site and find out which content works best. So we will increase the usability of the resource and the overall satisfaction of visitors from viewing the site.
