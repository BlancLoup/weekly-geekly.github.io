5 million security startup

Apply for iSecurity project competition!

The Skolkovo Foundation invites to participate in the competition projects related to such technological areas as improving the security of the Internet, preventing cyber threats and applications for cyber-investigations, as well as data protection in cloud and distributed computing environments.

The jury of the competition will evaluate the applications of the projects according to the following criteria: scientific and technical innovation of the project, consumer properties of the product, the likelihood of achieving the indicators stated in the project, as well as the prospects for commercialization.



The winner of the competition is promised financial support from the Skolkovo Foundation in the amount of 5,000,000 rubles, mentoring support from leading companies of the network security industry, as well as special prizes from the Competition Partners.

Applications will be accepted until September 30 inclusive.

Details and registration:
