Amateur Radio Exam

First of all, it was necessary to prepare for the exam. On the website of the Union of Radio Amateurs of Russia (SRR), I found questions for the exam and the program for self-testing. For dummies there is also a manual . It became clear that it is better to take the exam at the RFTC of the Central Federal District, because CPP requires the exam to enter into and pay a fee.

It turned out that there are as many as four ranks of radio amateurs, just like in sports.

The fourth category is for beginners. Radio amateurs of this category are allowed to work on all VHF bands with a maximum power of 5 watts. Requires minimal knowledge of the rules for the use of radio stations, the rules of radio communications.

The third category is also for beginners, but the possibilities are much greater. It is allowed to use not only all the VHF - ranges, but also specially designated areas on all HF (except 30, 20, 17, 12 meters). The device can have a power of 10 watts.

The second category is the most widespread; it is much more difficult to pass the exam than on the 3rd and 4th. To the knowledge of technology are much higher requirements. Received a certificate for 2 cat. can use VHF and HF bands with a capacity of 100 VA.

The first category is the most serious of all. Surrenders will be asked to accept Morse code at a speed of 60 characters per minute. But they also have more rights than others on VHF - the power range can be 500 W, and on the HF - the range is 1000 W.

Registration of RES

After a couple of days I received a certificate of call sign education through the office in an electronic form, it only remains for me to register the distribution zone via the website of public services . Immediately make a reservation, without a digital signature, you can not submit an online application.

To make life easier for beginners, telling how to do it. You need to go through the following tabs on the site:

All services ‚Üí Government authorities ‚Üí Roskomnadzor ‚Üí Registration of radio-electronic means and high-frequency devices for civilian use ‚Üí Registration of a certificate of registration of radio-electronic means ‚Üí Tab to receive a service .


This is how the form of filling looks, which must be carefully filled.

It is necessary to carefully fill in all the fields with an asterisk in exact accordance with the passport of the radio (information about the call sign, the emission class at VHF frequencies F3E, transmitter power, information about the antenna and the category of radio amateur). The applicant may even choose the method of receiving the application: in person or electronically through public services.

After filling out the application, you will receive an electronic copy of the certificate of registration of RES in your personal account on state services.
