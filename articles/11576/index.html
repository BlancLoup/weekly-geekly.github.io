Want R2D2? - Choose!

Back in early 2007, NIKKO Home Electronics announced the release of multifunctional robots, executed in the form of the legendary R2D2.


R2-D2 DVD Projector is equipped with a card reader and a DVD / CD / MP3 player. It also has FM radio, 20W speakers, an iPod connector, a variety of different outputs for connecting to an external TV, LCD panel, speakers, etc. The growth of this robot is 20 inches.

The eye of the robot performs the functions of a projector. He still does not know how to display holographic images, but he does a good job on an 80-inch screen with a resolution of 800x600 pixels.

The R2D2 robot is able to perform not only the functions necessary for the media center, but also to move, deftly avoiding obstacles, and even speak a little.

Controlling this robot is another miracle - a remote in the form of the Millennium Falcon. In addition to the design, this remote is remarkable in that it makes sounds of the starship's engine.
