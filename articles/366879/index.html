Disadvantages:

In order to avoid unnecessary resentment, I write right from the door about the shortcomings. NationStates does not suit you if ...

  • You do not know English well or you do not have enough patience to constantly use the dictionary.   • You want a game for which you need a lot of free time. NationStates is most suitable for busy people who can not spend hours chopping into WoW.
  • You want a lot of beautiful graphics, and in 3D. NationStates does not shine with beautiful graphics, and there is no 3D at all.
  • You like games with a rich plot. NationStates has no plot. Totally.
  • If you are too serious and expect hyper-realistic. Humor is often found in NationStates (although not too often), and even before the completely realistic political simulator of NationStates as to the stars.




History and Business Model of NationStates

Creating your country

This process is simple, so I will not describe it in detail, but some nuances need to be considered.

First, you MUST provide your email address, otherwise you risk permanently losing your country if you forget the password. The pace of development of countries is very slow, so it will be very sad to lose a country that has been pumped for several months.

Secondly, in NationStates all countries are divided into 27 categories :


A country can move from one category to another, but it can be a long and difficult process. If you want your country to be in a certain category from its very foundation, then you should best use the ideological test guide .

Thirdly, if you intend to create several countries that will be compared with each other, I advise you to create them in one category in advance, so that you can see how much their paths diverge.

Fourthly, if you are a supporter of the principle ‚Äúeverything that is not directly prohibited - is permitted‚Äù, then I suggest starting with Anarchy, as with the system that is as free as possible from any restrictions. Thus, all restrictions that will only be in the country will be a direct result of your legislation. That is, you will avoid the fact that things that you did not explicitly prohibit will be prohibited.

If you are a supporter of the principle that ‚Äúeverything that is not directly permitted is prohibited‚Äù, then start with Psychotic Dictatorship as from the ‚Äúsqueezed‚Äù system. All subsequent freedoms will be a direct result of your actions.
