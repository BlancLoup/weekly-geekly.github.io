bb-mobile Topol 'LTE (TQ863Q): 8 cores from MediaTek for account games


The model with the loud name bb-mobile Topol '(TM859AC) went on sale in 2014. Let us explain: this tablet was named in honor of the world-famous Russian missile system. The "tablet" was based on an 8-core MediaTek MT8392 processor. And, by the way, it became the first tablet in Russia with 8 cores. The test results were impressive: 30 points in AnTuTu and trouble-free work with the most "heavy" toys of the time.


bb-mobile Topol 'LTE (TQ863Q)

This is already 37,000 points in AnTuTu, which is abundantly enough to run 3D games with maximum graphics settings. Bb-mobile Topol 'LTE (TQ863Q) is the only tablet model on this platform.

Among other advantages, bb-mobile Topol 'LTE (TQ863Q) is a metal case that not only protects the tablet from overheating, dissipating heat, but also an additional stiffening rib. When struck and dropped metal protects the inside of the device from damage. On the whole, the metal case looks more solid than the plastic one and is much more pleasant to the touch.
