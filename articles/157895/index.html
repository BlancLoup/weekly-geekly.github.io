Domain me.ga no longer belongs to Kim Dotky



For a long time, Kim Dotcom, the creator of the temporarily (?) Deceased Megaupload file sharing service, promises to show the world the updated resource. It is clear that the previous domain can no longer be used, it is blocked. It was decided to name the new resource Mega, and the domain me.ga was registered for it. Ga is Gabon, a state in Central Africa. For some time the domain worked, there was a redirect to the personal site of the Dotcom on it. But the authorities of Gabon decided to withdraw the domain.

On November 6, that is, two days ago, the authorities in Gabon reported that the domain will be blocked, since Gabon does not support the work of resources that violate international copyright laws. The decision to block the domain was made by the Minister of Communications of this country, who gave the order to the relevant services.
