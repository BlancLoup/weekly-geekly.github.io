Come the developer's ammunition! # 3

This morning came another package with the Russian MSDN missing in the package with books.
Well, they sent everything they had promised, I look forward to the next distribution of the freebie stock.




Thanks to the organizers of the action for the gifts for the new year :)

ps For those who do not know or have already forgotten: those who received books did not have a disk with msdn, which they promised to send along with their ammunition, and those who received an electronic version had this disk right away. In one of the comments was born the assumption that msdn is sent only with the electronic version of the ammunition, as if compensation :)
