Deus Ex will come to iOS

To the delight of all fans of the cyberpunk franchise Deus Ex, the Japanese developer and publisher of games Square Enix announced a mobile version of the game which will be called Deus Ex: The Fall. Developed by the American N-Fusion Interactive. The game will be available in c for iPhone and iPad at an estimated price of $ 6.99, which is quite a lot, but this is unlikely to scare fans of the Deus Ex universe.

The release date of the game is not called, but judging by the video, the development has been going on for quite a while and a significant part of the game is already ready, so it‚Äôs not a very long time to wait.

It is worth noting that the game is quite similar to its "big brother", the computer version of the game Deus Ex: Human Revolution. The actions of the game will be based on the novel Deus Ex: The Icarus effect, published by Random House, and develop in the same time period as the Human Revolution but with other actors.
The developers did not come up with a new simplified gameplay for mobile devices, as is often the case with other games, which, in my opinion, is an undoubted advantage.
