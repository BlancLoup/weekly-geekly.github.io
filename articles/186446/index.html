Amazon, Reddit and Yandex also pay Adblock Plus developers

Recently, the creators of the most popular browser extension for blocking Adblock Plus ad are accused of all mortal sins: they reported at the beginning of the year that they allegedly offered to include sites on the white list in exchange for a full third of the company's advertising budget, and in June the German blogger said Adblock Plus has strategic partners in the advertising industry, calling ABP the advertising mafia.

Recently it became known that the developers of Adblock Plus receive money from Google for the fact that advertising from the search company is not blocked by the extension.
