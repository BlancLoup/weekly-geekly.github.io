Vector graphics for free - selection of sites

Vector graphics have many advantages. Unlike raster images, vector images are more flexible, easily scaled, maintain quality, etc. Below is a selection of web resources with free vector icons, symbols and images.

1. Freepik


www.freepik.com

One of the largest websites that offers hundreds of new vector images for personal and commercial use.



2. All Free Download


all-free-download.com/free-vectors

Approximately 190,000 samples of vector graphics are available for free download (for non-commercial use). You can search for 15 available signs or the most popular, new.



3. Vecteezy


www.vecteezy.com

A huge "home" of vector graphics, images and all sorts of patterns. In addition to free, there are available kits only to premium users.



4. Free Vector


www.freevector.com

Site for searching company logos, brands, emblems in vector. Images can be used under a Creative Commons license , which represents the right to change them and share them on other resources (but with a link to the source).



5. 1001 Free Downloads


www.1001freedownloads.com/free-vectors

A collection of free vector graphics in 35 different categories: food, sports, technology, business, science, etc. Images can be used for personal and commercial use, but with a link to the site.



6. Free Design File


freedesignfile.com/category/free-vector

The site has 30 categories of various vector images. All materials are free, but you must stick to the same Creative Commons license.



7. Free Vectors


www.free-vectors.com

There are more than 30 categories of vector images on this website that can be downloaded for free.



8. FVector 4 Free


vector4free.com

Vector4Free.com offers free vector graphics in the following formats: Illustrator AI, EPS, PDF, SVG and Corel Draw CDR.



9. Cool Vectors


coolvectors.com

This website distributes vector graphics from other sources. Users can rate the quality of the images.



10. Vectorportal


www.vectorportal.com

This portal has 25 categories of vector graphics, which can be freely downloaded, but indicating the source. There is a rather interesting and unusual category, where the arms in the vector are collected.



11. Pixeden


www.pixeden.com/free-vectors

This site contains high-quality, free vector kits / packages.
