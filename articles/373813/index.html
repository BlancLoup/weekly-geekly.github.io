Telegraph


Yes, there is no typo in the headline. In 2012, both brothers began work on creating a secure messenger. Initially, the project had the code name Telegra.ph or simply Telegraph. To work on it, they founded almost the same name LLC Telegraph (registration - August 7, 2012). The messenger was decided to develop as a private communication channel, access to the correspondence of users of which will not be anyone but the users themselves.

A year later, on August 14, 2014, the first version of Telegram messenger for iPhone appeared. It was necessary to rename it to Telegram because of the interest in the project of auditors from the UCP fund, which bought 48% of Vkontakte shares.

As a result, all the developers of VK engines went to the Telegraph office, plus Nikolay Durov himself went there. After he finally left Vkontakte, Rosenberg became the technical director of the social network.

After some time, Pavel Durov began to think about leaving Vkontakte. That very statement of the creator of the social network about his dismissal, posted on April 1 , was made on the humorous advice of Rosenberg. Then a refutation was made, but on April 21 Durov was fired for real.
