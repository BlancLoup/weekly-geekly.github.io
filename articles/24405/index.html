SQA Days 2008 May 27 - Conference for Quality Assurance Engineers (QA)

On May 27, 2008, the third international conference SQA Days 2008 will be held in Moscow, to which experts in testing and quality assurance of software systems, as well as other interested parties, are invited. The conference is devoted to issues related to testing and quality assurance of web applications.

For testers

Now the number of people in Russia and the near abroad engaged in the field of testing and ensuring the quality of software systems is growing rapidly. With the rapid growth of the industry, it is conferences that provide the opportunity to organize the most dynamic dissemination of information and effective exchange of experience. The SQA Days 2008 Conference is the successor to the SQA-2 conference, which received a great response among the testers. We hope that in the new format the conference will become even more interesting and informative.

For developers

The fact that the conference on testing and quality assurance of web applications is combined with a conference for web application developers is far from random. Testing and development are two sides of the same coin. To ensure high quality software systems can only be through joint efforts, and for successful communication, developers and testers need to understand what problems worry their teammates. Therefore, we invite developers to our conference, and also strongly recommend testers to attend conferences for developers.

For their bosses

Want to organize a testing department? Want to know which tools are worth buying and which ones are not? Want to know which testing methods are currently the most popular? Want to increase the professionalism of your testers? Do you want to find a contractor to perform independent testing or, on the contrary, a customer for your testing services?

We invite speakers!

We invite experts in testing and quality management, WEB-project managers and IT directors to participate!

We invite sponsors and partners!
