What I learned from the 90s games


Recently, I began to think often about why, all other things being equal, starting conditions in childhood, we become so different when we grow up. I mean the question of how our perception of the world is developing. We studied according to one textbook, we walked in the same clothes. Our parents were average physicians, teachers, and vendors. School, courtyard, sports sections and various clubs and ... In this publication I will try to tell about one huge layer of my childhood - the game Dendy. To tell about the role that this miracle of the 90s had on me.

Dizzy - project management


We took cartridges with games for rent in salons, leaving the parents passport as a deposit. Chip and Dale - Teamwork



This game was a great way to learn how to work in a team. In childhood, I already learned to divide people according to various characteristics / abilities. In this game, companions for me were divided into three categories. They play better than me, worse than me and like me.

Better than me . You play the game, you are constantly at the left end of the screen. Do not have time for the leader. He forwards you to collect all lives and does more damage to the main boss. You start to notice his moves, jumps. Constantly learn from him. And be sure to look for an opportunity to sit down with him for a nearby joystick.

Worse than me You leave him a life, because you and so, with one you can pass the whole game. Grab it, carry it and jump over difficult places. You show him safe places in the battle with the boss.

Like me . This is the most interesting. Here we come up with a wonderful option. We need to go to the end of the game, while messing with each other. Grab each other and throw at enemies, when moving up the map, do not wait for your partner and leave him in the screen abyss. It developed maximum agility.

It was then that I realized that all people are different and the approach to them must be individualized, since you happened to play in a team. A lot of time has passed, now working in a team and managing people, I always select individual methods of working with a person and never row with the same comb. And now, what do you think, with whom is it most interesting to cooperate / work in business, who is worse than you, is better or like you?

Race America - self control



Waiting for my turn at the joystick, I, in order to pass the time, watched not only the TV, but also the players. You, probably, also noticed that many players, playing various races, moved with their whole body behind a virtual machine / motorcycle. You can even compare with playing pianists. You look at them and it seems that they live life together with the performed work. But I had a different approach. I began to control every move. Comfortable posture and no swinging. The buildup. In athletics, this rule was hammered into us. There should be no movement from side to side when running - a waste of energy and the wrong flow of thoughts. I accepted the pose of a monk whenever I wanted to be a better player.

Have you heard about non-verbal communication? People constantly read you and give you signs themselves. In any stressful situation, take my favorite pose - "racing monk." And then you will often come out the winner after any business negotiations. Ask for wage increases, get a job, quit, listen to criticism or criticize yourself - no swinging.
