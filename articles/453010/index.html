Description of measuring points




To compare different light sources, GOST is used, which regulates the main points of measurement of illumination according to a table placed on the wall. The number in the designation of a point is equal to its distance from the car. The letters L and R denote the location, left and right. The main measuring points according to GOST: 75R and 50R - illumination of the right shoulder at a distance of 75 and 50 meters; B50L - is responsible for the level of blinding oncoming car driver; far is measured at the center of the table and is located at the center of the lane; axial luminous intensity determines the level of illumination in front of the car.

The Japanese Fukurou F1 halogen bulbs became the leader in light and illumination testing on the road.
