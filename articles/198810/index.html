Now universities - Positive Education participants will be able to use MaxPatrol for training.

It is not a secret for anyone that traditional university educational programs do not always have the necessary flexibility to comply with the development of technologies and the situation on the labor market. To fill this gap and to help young people master interesting and sought-after specialties in the field of information security, we launched the Positive Education program , which has been joined by dozens of universities over the year. Now the participants of this program will be able to use the control system of security and compliance with standards MaxPatrol in the learning process.



Educational institutions that have joined the program will be able to demonstrate to their listeners the full range of features of MaxPatrol: scanning in the PenTest, Audit, Compliance and Forensic modes, inventory, searching for vulnerabilities, analyzing the configuration of various operating systems, telecommunications equipment, DBMS, ERP systems, components of the process automation system.

In addition to software and documentation, educational institutions will receive methodological assistance in the implementation of the MaxPatrol system in the educational process and building on its basis laboratory workshops on security analysis, vulnerability management and monitoring compliance with regulatory requirements. These workshops include work on the areas of web security (XSS, SQL injection, Remote Code Execution, WAF circumvention techniques) and VoIP security (detection of VoIP devices, attacks on RTP and SIP). Competitive materials of the international competition on practical security Positive Hac Days III Capture The Flag , which took place in May of this year and gathered more than 100 best specialists from Russia, USA, Korea, Spain, the Netherlands and Tunisia, as well as materials from Hack Quest and other competitions, are also used. conducted by Positive Technologies.

To join the Positive Education program, you must send a request to: edu@ptsecurity.com .
