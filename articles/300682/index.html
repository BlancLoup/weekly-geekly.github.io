Scientists declare: willpower is strongest in the mornings, so get down to business actively


Probably, you have heard such advice: first of all, there must be something significant and significant in a day - a task that requires high concentration, will and determination. The reason is simple: our composure is limited.

This concept is visually represented using a force model. Self-control is formed on the basis of shared resources, which are depleted over time. Willpower can be considered as a muscle in which the load causes fatigue.

At Nottingham University and the National Institute of Education of Singapore, scientists analyzed 83 studies on self-control, and came to this conclusion:
The results revealed a significant effect of the weakening of internal forces on performance in the performance of tasks related to self-control. The depletion of resources had the strongest effect on the efforts made, the perception of the complexity of the task, negative emotions, the subjective feeling of fatigue and the level of blood glucose

For those who work at home, both psychological and physiological factors affect the ability to perform work.

The closer to the evening, the stronger the "fatigue", weakening willpower . So, the more important it is to benefit from the early morning.
