What is Invoice Mall and who is it for?


InvoiceMall is a SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) model-based service that allows you to manage clients, projects, documents, invoices and other business processes online. If even shorter - this is your workplace.

IM - for those who are tired of keeping information on their projects in their heads, look for it among thousands of letters and hundreds of ICQ history files, store data about their customers and contractors on paper or in scattered electronic documents, issue invoices in excel, losing their precious time to fill in constantly repetitive information.

IM - for those who are tired of synchronization and data integration between systems:
  • CRM (Highrise, KarmaCRM, ...)
  • Project Management (Basecamp, Lighthouse, ...)
  • Collaboration (Drop.io, Behance, ...)
  • Support (Tender, Zoho, ...)
  • Todo (RemeberTheMilk)
  • Accounting, invoicing and others.
Do you work with electronic money? Pay taxes? Need statistics? Do you have a small hosting company or an online store? You will enjoy working with us.



In our opinion, IM, first of all, is interesting to freelancers, small businesses, all those who do not want to wallow in the confusion of incoming and outgoing information.
