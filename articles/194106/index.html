How to find out all payments in the Yandex.Money system?



Is the Yandex.Money service safe?
Yes. We do everything to ensure that the service is reliable and safe.
Yandex money

The answer to the question in the title under the cut.


1. Become a store.
2. Get access to statistics and install self-signed Yandex certificate.
3. Go to the statistics and select "ALL" in the list of stores.
4. PROFIT.



In addition to the data in the screenshot, the amounts and payment details of the number of wallets and phones of ALL payers to ALL stores are available. In the screenshot - payments for today. Is Yandex.Money here? Can you answer in public faster than by mail or phone?

Important UPD:
Several merchants (less than five) received for several hours the opportunity to view information not intended for them due to an error in the new release of statistics from 09/17/2013. But they did not have time to use it, because the access was promptly closed - the security service made sure that there was no data upload. Measures taken to restrict unloading and exclude further access to statistics with a certificate that does not have a correct restriction of rights were not allowed to divulge by the security service in order to avoid abuse.
