Glam Shuttle: XPC Barebone SG33G6 Deluxe

The company Shuttle announced the release of a new computer in a small form factor. The representative of the Glamor series, XPC Barebone SG33G6 Deluxe, made in a black aluminum case, is positioned as a high-performance solution with advanced multimedia and communication capabilities. Another interesting feature of the SG33G6 is the user data protection implemented in it, by identifying fingerprints using an integrated biometric sensor.


Intel G33 Express + ICH9DH, supporting Intel Core 2 Quad processors, Intel Core 2 Duo E6000 / E4000, Intel Pentium Dual-Core E2000 and Intel Celeron 400 Series with Socket 775, is selected as the chipset for XPC Barebone SG33G6 Deluxe. - Intel GMA 3100 with HDMI support (HDCP). The ability to install dual-channel DDR2 800 MHz RAM up to 4 GB is supported. PC Sound - 8-channel HD with Dolby Digital Live! and DTS Connect.

As for communication capabilities, XPC Barebone SG33G6 Deluxe implements Gigabit LAN, FireWire, Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR and Wi-Fi 802.11b / g interfaces, as well as six USB 2.0 ports.

Cost Shuttle XPC Barebone SG33G6 Deluxe without VAT will be? 366, and on sale will appear in the near future.
