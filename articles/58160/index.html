Pirates: to be or not to be

Brought to you by The Pirate Bay.

Now more and more often from owners of rights threats in the direction of torrent trackers in particular and pirates in general are heard. Their position is clear: they lose profits. On the one hand, users are good, they need to spend less money, on the other hand, they get a lower-quality product, but many people do not care about that. But my subjective opinion.

I'll start with the products of Microsoft, namely, Microsoft Office. Of the entire office suite, I actively use Word, Excel, Outlook, and sometimes OneNote and PowerPoint. But! Next come the game. Honestly, I do not understand people who use pirated versions. The only advantage is that they are much cheaper. But is the money saved on launching such games worth the time and nerves worth it? I think not. Again, if it does not take off every 15-30 minutes due to the next crack. Games are fun, and for him, as you know, you have to pay. Only, for some reason, few people understand this.

Music and movies. With this category of products, everything is much worse. The quality of even licensed discs often leaves much to be desired, printing also does not please the eye. What do we get in the end? Consumers simply can not buy everything they need because of the same rightholders. And also ...
