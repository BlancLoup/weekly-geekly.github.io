Online courses for project managers

Hi, I have a note in Evernote in which I collected courses on project management, which I have passed or plan to take in the near future. I am happy to share information with the community. If any educational project management resources are hidden from me, write in the comments - your knowledge will be useful to everyone.

What managers can learn from philosophy. Centrale supelec
Start: Anytime
The courses draw an interesting parallel between philosophy and management. The course does not give practical skills, but it forms the correct model of thinking.
www.coursera.org/learn/management-philosophy
www.coursera.org/course/businessandphilo2

Critical perspectives on management. IE Business school
Start: January 2016
A look at management in terms of business and innovation. At the end of the course will be issued Statement of Accomplishment
www.coursera.org/course/criticalmanagement

Introduction to Project Management Principles and Practices Specialization. University of California, Irvine
Start: Anytime
Specialization is a set of three courses, each of which is devoted to a particular project management issue. During the course, the user will learn the basic approaches that are used in project management based on PMBoK. The information is given in more textual form and not in the form of lectures, as is usually the case on Coursera. Unfortunately, without a confirmed certificate, it is impossible to fully complete three courses - in the audit version it is impossible to pass tests.
www.coursera.org/learn/project-planning Project initiation
www.coursera.org/learn/project-risk-management Risk Management
www.coursera.org/learn/schedule-projects Project planning and budgeting

Introduction to Public speaking. University of washington
Course Start: Anytime
A huge and long course on public speaking. Considered one of the best materials on Coursera
www.coursera.org/learn/public-speaking

Career Success Specialization University of California, Irvine
Course Start: Anytime
Specialization in basic management skills. Project management, business correspondence, business communication, time management, introduction to finance. In the end, you can get a certificate of specialization Career Success
www.coursera.org/learn/fundamentals-of-management Basics of Management
www.coursera.org/learn/problem-solving Effective problem solving and decision making
www.coursera.org/learn/work-smarter-not-harder Time Management Course
www.coursera.org/learn/project-management-basics Fundamentals of Project Management
www.coursera.org/learn/negotiation-skills Basics of Business Negotiation
www.coursera.org/learn/art-of-negotiation Business negotiations Part 2
www.coursera.org/learn/business-writing Business Correspondence
www.coursera.org/learn/finance-for-non-finance-managers Introduction to Finance
www.coursera.org/learn/communication-in-the-workplace Communication in the workplace
www.coursera.org/learn/entrepreneurial-thinking Entrepreneurial Thinking

Economy for non-economists. HSE
Course Start: Anytime
For those who are tired of studying in English, a very interesting and capacious course on the basics of economics from HSE.
www.coursera.org/course/ecnonec

Basics of Project Management
Start: When 3000 people are typed
A huge course with lots of project management information on PMBoK processes and related project management areas. A lot of information, voluminous homework and complex homework that require reading additional literature. Personally, in my opinion - mast.
universarium.org/courses/main/392

Management Training Tutorials at Lynda.com
A lot of specific information for advanced users. Unfortunately, Lynda is a paid platform.
www.lynda.com/Management-training-tutorials/1713-0.html?previousCategory=2
