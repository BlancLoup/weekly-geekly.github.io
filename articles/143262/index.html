They waited! Samsung GALAXY S III Premiere

Good night, Habra!

Many of us have been waiting for this moment, building various hypotheses about the appearance and filling, eagerly studying the next, supposedly ‚Äúofficial‚Äù concept of a smartphone designed to become the brightest novelty of this year ... Yes, we are talking about the triquel of the acclaimed blockbuster called GALAXY S! The veil of secrecy has finally opened, revealing to the world the splendor of the new flagship Samsung. Your attention is the first ‚Äúhot‚Äù announcement of Samsung GALAXY S III from the presentation of Samsung Mobile Unpacked 2012.



Unfortunately, no details about the processor, graphics accelerator and RAM have yet been disclosed: apparently, they are left for dessert. We will definitely highlight this point in a full review of the device. What did you learn from the stated specifications:

Screen.
HD Super AMOLED with a diagonal of 4.8 inches and a resolution of full 1280x780 pixels. High clarity and 16: 9 aspect ratio.



Camera.
The gadget boasts an 8 megapixel main camera and an extra 1.9 megapixel front camera. Autofocus, flash and zero delay included. Shooting and playing video - only Full HD hardcore at a frequency of 30 frames per second. There are a couple of nice shooting features: Burst Shot gives you the opportunity to shoot a series of 20 frames, and the Best photo function selects the best of eight photos taken for the user.



Operating system.
Android Ice Cream Sandwich 4.0, of course.

Memory.
The smartphone will be delivered with 16 or 32 GB on board (in the near future, the version with 64 GB is promised). There is an opportunity to further expand its volume through the microSD slot (up to 64 GB).

Dimensions.
136.6 x 70.6 x 8.6 mm, 133 g.

Battery.
Impressive indicator of 2100 mAh.

In addition to the above technical characteristics, great attention was paid to the presentation of the functional device, namely - the interaction with the user, which was brought to a new level. The smartphone is charged with advanced technologies for recognizing the face, voice and gestures of its owner.



Among them is a curious technology of Smart stay: using the front camera, it captures the movements of the wearer's eyes and determines when the user is using a smartphone ‚Äî for example, reading an e-book or browsing the web. Based on its observations, the device automatically maintains the display brightness at the same level for convenient reading or browsing the Internet.



Samsung GALAXY S III is also equipped with a voice user interface S Voice, which allows the smartphone to recognize the voice and respond to the words of the owner. Now you can turn on music with your voice, control the volume of your smartphone, send text messages and emails, schedule appointments or automatically turn on the camera and take pictures.



An enhanced version of the Android Beam technology - S Beam - allows you to transfer various files just by touching your Samsung GALAXY S III with your smartphone. At the same time, neither Wi-Fi connection nor GPRS is required. The transfer speed is impressive: 1 GB will be delivered to another device in three minutes, and an audio file of 10 MB in just two seconds.

The AllShare Cast feature will allow you to connect Samsung GALAXY S III to your TV and display content from your smartphone on the big screen. For instant file sharing between the Samsung GALAXY S III and other devices, be it a tablet, PC or TV, the AllShare Play service is available to users. In this case, the distance between the devices does not play any role.



There is another useful feature of Group Cast : thanks to it, you can watch any content from the smartphone screen on several devices that are on the same Wi-Fi network.



At the time of launch, the smartphone will be available in two colors: blue (Pebble Blue) and white (Marble White). In the future, Samsung will release models with different body colors.
Samsung GALAXY S III will appear on the European market at the end of May. The date of commencement of sales of the smartphone in Russia will be announced later.
