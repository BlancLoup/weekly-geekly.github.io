News sites are not ready for high traffic.

Two hours ago, explosions in the Metro began (hotline: 637-22-22 , 622-14-30 and 624-34-40, list of victims , hospitals , where to donate blood , where to report danger ).
I watch news sites. Practically all of them (from those that I usually read) do not cope with the load and "lie", namely:
  • lenta.ru - opens 1 time out of 5, in typos headers.
  • gazeta.ru - opens 1 time out of 10.
  • interfax.ru - initially opened with ASP errors and broken links, is now completely unavailable.
  • mosmetro.ru - does not work at all

They prudently arrived at rian.ru - if I understood correctly, they transferred the whole site to a mobile (lightweight) mode, so they give the page without delay.

News aggregators ( news.yandex.ru , news.google.ru ) work well with the load, but they give out information with a noticeable delay of about 10-15 minutes. Yandex initially confused the materials and included in the summary of this news information about some other terrorist act in another part of the world.

I believe that our electronic media are generally not well prepared technically to adequately fulfill their mission at critical moments.

It is better not to look into the blogosphere - there is complete discord and wavering from tears and hysterics to a political mock in the spirit of "we said."

I express my condolences to the dead and injured.

PS: I myself and a girl drove along the red line past Lubyanka 10 minutes before the first terrorist attack, so that both are still in some shock.
PPS: I believe that normal resistance to terror acts is a normal life without fear. We must not allow terrorists to pursue their own. You can not sow panic and escalate the situation, brainwashing others. Be calm while remaining vigilant. Take care of the safety of their loved ones and should always and everywhere.
