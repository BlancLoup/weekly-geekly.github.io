History reference


For a start, a little excursion into history. On June 20, 2011, at the ICANN conference (International Corporation that Manages the Internet Address Space) in Singapore, the program for creating new domain zones NewGtld was approved. From now on, any organization could apply for the creation of a top-level domain, paying only for its consideration, regardless of the outcome, $ 185,000, but even this price did not deter those who wanted to. Upon completion of the collection of applications for NewGtld, .app (13 applications) took the lead; .home and .inc (11 applications); .art (10 applications) and other zones, among which .shop, .news, were also applied for the Russian zones Moscow,. Children, .rus and, in fact, for the domain .moscow. The Foundation for the Advancement of Internet Technology and Infrastructure Development (FAITID) became the initiator of the creation of the zones Moscow and. Moscow. In April 2014, the domains were delegated, i.e. available online, and already on June 10 the first stage of the registration of names began.

By the way, about the registration periods. As you already know, when starting a new domain zone, there are three key stages of registration in the following order:

the stage of priority registration for trademark holders, the so-called Sunrise stage;

the stage of premium registration, when a domain name can be registered by anyone, but at a price higher than the standard price;

the stage of free registration, when everyone can register a domain at the standard price of the selected registrar.

For the month of priority registration from June 10 to July 10, 2014, in order to protect trademarks, they hurried to acquire their domains Apple inc., McDonalds. On the same dates, FAITID registered strategic metro.moscow, kremlin.moscow and others. True, none of the domains is not used for its intended purpose.
