Visit 4 data centers per day and do not regret the time spent

For the third time in St. Petersburg, under the auspices of the provider ‚ÄúComfortel‚Äù, a tour of Data Center Expedition PRO is held . The essence of the event - in one day to get the widest possible view of the market of data centers in St. Petersburg. This time, we, the Linkmeup fighters, also managed to infiltrate the tour .

But just spending the whole day on contemplation of server fields is boring, so this time the trip was more thematic. Four data centers, each was created to solve specific problems, is aimed at a specific audience of customers and is endowed with its own specific engineering solutions.



Previous races were organized in the format of ‚ÄúBrilliance and poverty of data centers in St. Petersburg‚Äù and showed everything: from the very thrash data centers to the ‚Äúluxury‚Äù segment. But so far they have decided against this format, for it is too fast and superficial.
Data Center Expedition PRO aims to show the city‚Äôs data center market in one day, as not many people know it. Therefore, it was decided that less is better, but better.
And during the excursion tour, entertained by the fascinating stories the honored and legendary telecom operator of St. Petersburg, Dmitry Petrov, part-time director of Comfortel.

Few common words


Let's first agree on what a data center is in order to speak the same language. So, the data center is not a room where the racks are standing, but an engineering facility in which conditions are created for the uninterrupted power supply of a given quality, at least two power inputs, a set of UPS and a diesel generator with auto start. Also, strict temperature conditions are ensured and there is a fire extinguishing system, the operation of which does not stop the operation of the placed equipment. And it is quite good when a vigilant server security guard sits at the entrance, and there are at least two engineers and a power engineer in the monitoring around the clock.
The choice of options for the implementation of these requirements lies solely on the shoulders of the owner and depends on the goals that they set for themselves at the design stage.

All the data centers in the review are claimed as Tier 3, but there is a nuance - in St. Petersburg there is not a single certified Tier 3 DC. Not at all. As soon as you ask to show a certificate, they all go to the unconscious, mumbling something about the high cost of certification, empty formalities, but full compliance with the requirements. That is, they, as it were, are ready, but no.

And finally: the area, power and other fascinating figures, this is very important and interesting, but you can look at them at any time on the site, so this article will be just personal impressions of each object, with a certain claim to objectivity.
So not all the data center that was called the data center and not all that data center where Tier 3 was written.

Linxdatacenter


So, the first in our path was the Linxdatacenter data center. Representation of the distinguished Dutch company Linxtelecom with data centers and communication channels around the world. At the opening of the data center came already the Prime Minister of Holland himself. They claim that they work without failures from the moment of opening, i.e. already 5 years. Known for coming to the data center market when no one else in the country knew what it was.
Of interesting technical solutions: its own gas piston substation and, as a result, independence from urban electricity. Naturally there is a backup input from the city, batteries on the pickup and diesel in case of gas interruptions. Cooled by freecooling. Post ready 1000 racks.


The prize for the most beautiful building, clearly, at Linxdatacenter

The data center was built inside the Sky Trade Technopark building, i.e. there are offices on the right, on the left, on the bottom and in general everywhere. But linx fenced off from all the thick walls. By the way, the entrance to the technopark for an unknown reason is located exactly on the opposite side from the entrance, i.e. as if behind the building.
The main impression: expensive, rich and very corporate. It is really big, beautiful, it works only with legal entities and evaluates its services in euros. Although the crisis does not spare anyone and prices have been lowered, but still remain above average in the market.

Another feature is remoteness. Technically, this is a city, even though its very outskirts. And the industrial zone, surrounded by residential areas, and this traffic jams every morning and evening. But there are only two large data centers that can accommodate a really large customer. These are Linx and SDN, both of which are in the north, only SDN is located directly on the Ring Road. In short, the choice is not particularly necessary.
The status of the institution is maintained here from the doorstep: you will be greeted by beautiful girls, referred to here as commercial representatives, taken to an excellent meeting room, filled with delicious coffee and fed with excellent cookies. While we were busy with food, the girls organized a survey presentation on the topic of where and how many data centers were already built, what are the communication channels between them, where there are points for connection and other marketing things with high numbers. The network is really big, and there are a lot of points of presence. If you have a business in several countries - you are here.
Do not forget the list of the most famous clients. The list is very solid. I think that 90% of companies are familiar to any man in the street in one form or another.

Miran


Another well-deserved player in the St. Petersburg data center market, launched a little earlier than Linxdatacenter. Located near the city center, consists of two parts. The first part is a classic data center, and we will not dwell on it for long.
The second part is a modular data center, it is being actively built, and the first module was relatively recently commissioned.



The data center actively plays both its location and its history. By the way, the number of telecom operators here is 4 times more than in Linxdatacenter, and even the purely visual location of the communications center looks extremely solid. Operators are happy about this state of affairs, so feel free to install powerful core-level iron and aggregation. There is even a trunk hub in place, and a lot of cables are wound up by different routes, i.e. We can safely say that from the very beginning Miran was oriented towards friendship with the operators, and clearly did not lose. For many current customers, the ability to connect to a diverse operator has become the most important argument in choosing a location.

But back to the structure of this data center. In fact, these are two different data centers located in the same place and in the same hands. The second part is much more interesting: it is created by progressive modular technology. In place of this, it means that in a huge factory hangar, specially designed module modules are installed, each of which is engaged in its own business. In some there are racks, in others cooling, etc. At the moment, only one module is fully launched, the second one is being actively mounted, and there should be four of them altogether. All development from and to custom.


Lawlessness racks. But they are clearly struggling with him - before it was much worse

Modules are specially welded double-decker containers, similar to standard sea containers. On the first floor there are racks, air conditioning units and a fire extinguishing system, on the second floor - a power unit, UPS, and so on. Everything is controlled by clever automatics, which, for example, can supply cold air to specific heating points, rather than simply increasing the air flow throughout the module.
The hangar in which they are located, naturally underwent special training. Ceremonial works were carried out, powerful exhaust fans and passive coarse filters were installed under the ceiling, which change regularly. Thus, the air in the room gets relatively clean and at the entrance to the system of free cooling passes only fine cleaning. It is argued that such a modular solution is unique to St. Petersburg.

IMAQLIQ


After a delicious lunch, we went to the IMAQLIQ data center. This data center has the strangest history of appearance and development, despite the fact that it is not inferior in age to the previous two.
For all its existence, it was known as Arroba, CubioRus, IQdata, General Datacomm, and only relatively recently focused on the current version of IMAQLIQ.


Modestly, very modestly ...

It all started with the company General Datacomm, which is engaged in the development of network equipment. Especially leaned on the radio relay for mobile operators. Then they bought the operator Kubio, who tried to impersonate Finnish, which is particularly beneficial in St. Petersburg, because All communications to Europe go through Finland.


All engineering here is done somehow very beautifully and with a soul.

This is how the company engaged in the creation of equipment for telecoms, quite unexpectedly for all, entered the market of these telecoms. And in order to completely surprise everyone, they also decided to build a data center, which was alternately named by all names.
Geographically, the General Datacomm assembly workshops are located on the territory of the turbine blade factory. What does it mean to be located on the territory of the former Soviet factory? Right! Huge territory and almost unlimited electricity. Therefore, the data center is built under the same roof with a warehouse and to say that there is less space there, it is just brazenly lying. Previously, they knew how to build from the heart and did not hold back on a scale.


Free space inside - at least play football

But back to the history of the data center. He did not receive a strong impetus to development, and, apparently, the secondary importance of this business line for the founders of the parent company is to blame. Therefore, its development until recently was extremely non-linear and in fits and starts. But now, having passed the way from just a large server to a complete engineering object, this data center is clearly beginning to gain momentum.
In places this means the following: the data center is now almost empty, and there is an active phase of finding customers, which means more than reasonable prices and the willingness to make concessions, which you will never agree to elsewhere. All major operators have already logged in, so there is no problem with communication.
