Teradata CTO Road Show - May 28, Moscow

Dear habravchane, we invite you to participate in the technical seminar Teradata CTO Road Show , which will be held in Moscow on May 28, 2013 . The seminar is organized in the framework of the European Road Show of Teradata, dedicated to the main technological trends in the field of data warehousing, big data analytics and BI.

Sterad Brobst, the Teradata CTO , will talk in detail about technological innovations that make Teradata a leader in the market, prove the importance of using semantic modeling to successfully build an integrated data warehouse by reducing data redundancy, improving data quality and reducing TCO.

Other speakers : Hermann Woestefeld (Hermann Westfield), Director Architecture Consulting Teradata International and Mikhail Ganyushkin, Lead Architect Teradata Russia.

The following topics will be covered during the seminar:
  • Analytics graphs as the most important component of big data analytics
  • New functionality Teradata 14.10
  • When and under what circumstances it is advisable to use database indexes


The Teradata CTO Road Show Workshop focuses on technical experts in data architecture, analytical solutions and data warehousing.

Registration and program on the website : www.teradata-cto.ru

Venue : Hotel Radisson Blu Belarusian.
