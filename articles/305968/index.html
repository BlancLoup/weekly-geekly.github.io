.NET conference DotNext 2016 Moscow, December 9

On December 9, the sixth DotNext .NET Developer Conference will take place - it will be called DotNext 2016 Moscow and, as you probably guessed, in Moscow (Slavyanskaya Hotel). By the way, DotNext is the only .NET-conference in Russia that gathers under 600 Senior / Middle .NET-developers.



What will be new this time? I ask under the cat.

Speakers


With each DotNext, and this, I remind you, twice a year, the list of speakers grows stronger - speakers with strong and rating reports perfect their performances and prepare new brain-blasting hits, and the number of foreign experts willing to share their experience with their Russian colleagues is steadily growing. Just estimate the dry numbers: if in 2014 there were only two reports in English, then in June 2016 there were already six.


So who has already confirmed their participation? Of course, the authors of the best reports of past DotNext:

  • Sasha Goldshtein , CTO at Sela Group, Microsoft C # MVP, Azure MRS, author of Pluralsight, author of Pro .NET Performance, and one of the best .NET performance experts in the world. Best speaker DotNext 2016 Piter;
  • Dino Esposito , Legendary author of a series of books on ASP.NET, an expert on CQRS and BDD;
  • Andrey DreamWalker Akinshin , .NET MVP, Ph.D., silver winner of ACM ICPC, one of the Rider developers at JetBrains. He is fond of micro-optimizations. Maintainer of the BenchmarkDotNet project;
  • Dmitry Soshnikov , Ph.D., Technological Evangelist of Microsoft, is engaged in the popularization of modern software development technologies;
  • Adam Sitnik , developer, specializing in performance and fault tolerance, Open Source contributor to BenchmarkDotNet and corefxlab projects;
  • Sergey Shkredov , Head of .NET Products at JetBrains, directly involved in the development of ReSharper and Project Rider.
