Digest of IT events for May

We continue the review of metropolitan and regional events for developers. May brings with it the atmosphere of Saturdays and, accordingly, a wave of various hakatons and intensives. In addition, the community continues to develop the topic of cryptocurrency and information security, collectively awaits Google I / O Keynote and makes short excursions into unexpected areas - say, mobile forensics.



Digis

When: April 29
Where: Krasnodar, st. Severnaya, 319, Europe Business Center
Participation conditions: free of charge, registration required

Krasnodar developers can still catch the closing event of April - a conference dedicated to mobile applications. The organizers invite developers, IT-students and IT-business owners to participate - everyone who wants to join the professional community and share their insights. Reports from local and visiting specialists (Badoo, HeadHunter, Labyrinth) are announced: Instant Apps, teamwork, trends in iOS and Android development.

View I / O Extended 2018 Moscow

When: May 8
Where: Moscow, st. Lesnaya, 7, Business Center "White Gardens", 15th floor
Participation conditions: free of charge, registration required

Google I / O is an important enough event to spawn child sessions. Avito and GDG Moscow invite all Moscow developers to watch the broadcast together. Find out what innovative technologies and services the giant company is preparing and hot on the trails discuss the prospects among like-minded people.

Mitap BEM for beginners

When: May 11
Where: Simferopol, st. Kazan, 1a, 4th floor
Participation conditions: free, registration required

Do you know anything about the BEM web development methodology? Then you will be welcome at the upcoming master class from Yandex. Here beginners who are ready to learn its principles and capabilities from scratch will gather. Mitap will have a purely practical orientation: the speaker will demonstrate the capabilities of the BEM, perpendicular layout, and then review the tools for the development and testing of its ecosystem with concrete examples.

iS THiS DESiGN

When: May 17-18
Where: Moscow, 4th Syromyatnichesky lane, 1 / 8s8
Participation conditions: from 2000 rub.

Hackathon for IT engineers who professionally use the 1C platform. First, participants will listen to the theory performed by experts from large domestic companies: microservices, event integration, smoke testing, deployment, multichannel, and everything else. Drinking information, participants will begin work on solving actual problems on the second day. Hackathon includes three areas: 1C + Algorithms, 1C + Infrastructure and 1C + Integration.

HolyJS 2018 Piter

When: May 19-20
Where: St. Petersburg, pl. Victory, 1
Conditions of participation: 29 900 rubles.

Traditionally, there are three things you need to know about HolyJS: this is only JavaScript, this is an immersion in technical problems without interruptions in management reports, and this is an international-level event. The actors of the May conference are Douglas Crockford, Lea Verou, Azat Mardan, Gerard Sans, Minko Gechev, Vitaly Friedman and other experts from Google, EPAM, JetBrains, VK, Yandex. In their reports, speakers will talk about the architecture of corporate networks, reveal the secrets of crypto-mining through a browser, review debugging tools, review performance and memory leaks, compare React and Vue - in general, they won't let the audience get bored.

#WBCSUMMIT 2018

When: May 19-20
Where: Moscow, Lower Susalny lane. 5, p. 4, 1st floor
Participation conditions: 36 250 rub.

At the summit of the International Decentralized Association of Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain, the entire color of the crypto community from several continents will be gathered. The first day will be held to discuss decentralization in various areas (medicine, education, law, logistics, industry) and the latest trends - a total of 12 thematic sections. On the second day, the creators of the leading blockchain platforms will talk about the application side of the process: what constitutes the success of ICO, how to implement blockchain projects and what risks are associated with creating their own cryptocurrency. Also at the summit will be held World ICO Show - a competition with a solid prize fund. Applications for participation are still being accepted.



Card reading

Where: Innopolis, st. University, 1
Participation conditions: free of charge, registration required

Innopolis University in conjunction with the company Exact Farming poses a difficult task for developers on the weekend - the introduction of geo-information technologies in products of different directions. Harvest

TechRec 2018

When: May 25
Where: Moscow, Lower Susalny lane. 5, p. 4, 1st floor
Conditions of participation: 10 000 rubles.

The conference is entirely devoted to recruiting in the IT field - an extensive and very painful topic. The list of participants promises a variety of perspectives: among the speakers are representatives of global corporations with worked out schemes, and small startups, where each person is a separate case. How does this labor market differ from all others, why many generally accepted schemes do not work, how to carry out selection in specific industries and countries, what are the consequences of unbalanced supply and demand, and how, eventually, to find good specialists in a team - all this will be covered in the stated the program.
