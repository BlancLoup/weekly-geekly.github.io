Usability Bulletin. Issue number 11

World Usability Day 2007
The Usability Professionals' Association (UPA) reports that World Usability Day 2007 will be celebrated on November 8, 2007.
The history of World Usability Day
World Usability Day was founded in 2005 to show people around the world the importance of simple and convenient design. The initiator of the celebration was the ex-president of the UPA (Usability Professionals' Association) - Elizabeth Rosenzweig.
Usability Stars in Moscow
On November 8-10, as part of User Experience Russia 2007, five usability gurus will immediately visit Moscow. Elena Bocharova, one of the organizers of the conference, interviewed them for readers of Bulletin Usability.
Video interview with Elizabeth Rosenzweig
World Usability Day, never heard of it before. You ask "What is it?" This is the first event that promotes user-friendly devices and helps companies create more convenient and simple products. Visiting CNN Usability Specialist - Elizabeth Rosenzweig, from the Association of Usability Professionals (UPA). She will tell us about it.
