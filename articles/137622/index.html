Smartphones recently and now

Recently I switched to the next smartphone, putting the Nokia N900 in the archive of old bricks and thought about the trend, as it was before on the shelves of stores, and as it is now:



At first it smiled at me. But then I decided to look through the archives on Habre - the way it turns out to be.

I remember that at the beginning of the 2000s, the Swedish Ericsson (then without Sony) gave way to the Finnish Nokia, since the first believed that mobile phones were for businessmen. The Swedes refused to launch removable panels, especially pink. The Finns understood the market differently, and at the peak they won over 40% of the market.

Today, however, a reverse trend is clearly observed, at least in the external design of the phones. Maybe already ate? Maybe everyone is on top of the leaders? What do you think?
