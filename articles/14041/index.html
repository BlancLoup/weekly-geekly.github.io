Russian link traders enter the international market with TNX.net service

An international version of the well-known system for mass purchase and sale of links, aimed at promotion in search engines - TNX.net, was launched . The service is an analogue of XAP.ru , however it is suitable not only for Russian webmasters, but also for western ones.

The advantages of the system:

For the webmaster:
- Ability to earn income from all internal pages of the site, regardless of their distance from the main page: by default, 1 link per page, and the number of links the webmaster can increase to a maximum of 4, provided they are installed in different places on the page (do not arrange linkwashing)
- Massiveness: the system can work well in automatic mode with tens of millions of pages and links - the most difficult thing we have been working on for xap.ru for many months. The system sells a million links per day (for a month), maybe 2-3 million with an increase in supply and demand.
- Manual system moderation of all links uploaded by advertisers = high quality links, no links to doorways, link relevance, very strict requirements for advertisers
- Cost coefficient x2 for eng / de sites, which are still few in the system compared to thousands of Russian-speaking
- Protection against the appearance on Cyrillic links on eng / de sites at the stage of creating campaigns
- Funds for links are charged in advance a month in advance, quick withdrawal of funds in Webmoney or PayPal, you can directly make transactions on the exchange (not yet automated) with other participants without the mediation of the system, prices are constantly increasing by 2% per week
- Security and confidentiality: the absence of lists of participants, open to write folders, tags. Easy installation of php, perl or asp-code using the ‚Äúcopy-paste‚Äù method.
- Natural links for Google: links for many months are on the same page, do not blink every day and do not change places on the page.
- The price is adjusted automatically depending on the parameters of each page (PR, yahoo backlinks, subject, soon (as we launch the search engine): the number of external links on the page, the weight of each page
- Good prices for pages with non-zero Google PR. The presence on the site of pages with PR> 0 MANDATORY for admission to the system.
- Calculator of your potential income from the site in the system on the main page of TNX.net

For advertiser:
- The system is designed for fast, convenient and most economical promotion of a large number of low- and mid-frequency
- More than 30 million pages available (including Russian-language pages that work perfectly for google.com)
- Links are placed only automatically on the pages of sites selected topics (PR, Yahoo backlinks / TIC to choose from)
- Ability to promote thousands of pages of one site, and each URL is not manually required (batch download)
- Many links are placed in a prominent place on the pages = visitors not only from search engines, but also from sites
- Favorable prices for links: from $ 10 for 10,000 direct links per month
- Convenient ajax interface
- Optimizers and advertising agencies receive a discount of up to 50%, the ability to pay via Paypal or Webmoney
