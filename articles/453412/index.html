Top 3D Shop became the exclusive distributor of UFactory



Top 3D Shop has become an authorized exclusive distributor of UFactory products in Russia and other EAEU countries, ready for sales and reduced prices. Deliveries to the indicated territories through other channels were stopped.



UFactory is a manufacturer of educational collaborative robots from the center of Chinese industry - the city of Shenzhen. In the product line of the company - manipulators uArm Swift Pro (57 000 p.) - the closest and more accessible competitor Dobot Magician Basic (156 000 p.), Which has the same advantages: both devices - manipulators for education, working with the software environment Blocky / Python / C ++, both can be controlled from a PC or smartphone and both are available with optional nozzles and accessories, both raise up to 500 grams and have a positioning accuracy of 0.2 mm. The main difference is in cost. Apart from, of course, different manufacturers, design and design nuances. The functionality is similar.

The company also manufactures accessories and components for uArm, such as grippers, nozzles for laser engraving and 3D printing, cameras for precise positioning and feedback, stands and conveyor belts.







Robots are easily trained and programmed and are suitable for use in a wide range of educational and entertainment purposes.







Top 3D Shop is pleased to present such an important product for education in Russia and the CIS.



You can buy robot manipulators and UFactory accessories in Top 3D Shop .
