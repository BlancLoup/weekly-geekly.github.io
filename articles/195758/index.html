Sony Xperia Z Ultra: personal impressions and the opening of one of the biggest smart cards

To be honest, I have never used smart cards in everyday life (they are also ceiling lamps, they are fablets). There is a smartphone, there is a tablet. And yet, the number of people around with different versions of the Galaxy Note hinted that there is a demand for such devices, which means it is at least strange to ignore smartphones. Therefore, use - I do not use, but I write about them quite regularly. And now one of the most gigantic smartphones has fallen into the hands - Sony Xperia Z Ultra. I tried it in and, according to tradition, looked inside to learn the details of the design.



On Habr√© recently published a review of this device , and I will try not to repeat. I will focus on personal impressions and internal structure.

I'll start with an unexpected moment for many. When thinking about buying a smart card, be sure to check if there are suitable covers for it. After all, in the pocket of your jeans you most likely will not be able to wear it - it hurts healthy. We'll have to put it in a jacket pocket or even in a bag, and there are coins, keys, flash drives ... A colleague recently bought a not the most famous brand smart card and at first was very happy about the machine itself and notable savings. But it was not possible to pick up a normal case due to non-standard dimensions, and somehow I didn‚Äôt want to stick a smart card into a sock like. As a result, in just a month of operation, the gadget was pretty much torn off.





Xperia Z Ultra - the device (for now?) Is not the most common, but there are enough covers for it. At the time of my acquaintance, I took Krusell from white leatherette. Issue price of 1300 rubles. You can find cheaper, but I was bribed by a strict appearance and a built-in stand, thanks to which the smart card can be conveniently positioned on the table or on your lap when watching movies.



Included with the Xperia Z Ultra comes a compact docking station. This is, in fact, great news, because the smartphone, like all Sony's flagship mobile devices, is waterproof and dustproof. You can take it with you to the shower and write piercing tweets about reptiles who have turned off the hot water. Or, sowing in the bathroom, you can look at a rather large screen, the latest series of "Dexter". But there is a moisture resistance and a downside: all connectors, including micro USB, are closed with special plugs. Accordingly, if you charge a smart card in the usual way, the latter will have to be pulled back and forth, and one day something bad will happen to it. The docking station solves the problem: just enough to put on it a smart card and the process will go. Unfortunately, for this you have to remove it from the case.




Please note that the dock connector has changed (on top is the Xperia Z smartphone). Hopefully, the new one will remain relevant for more than a year.

The SmartSet itself makes an impressive impression. The screen diagonal is already 6.4 inches, and this is really a lot. The length of the device is only slightly inferior to the iPad mini. Probably the best place for such a giant is a man purse or pocket bag with a laptop.



The screen resolution is standard for top devices - 1920x1080 pixels. Part of it eats off for the panel with control buttons. All of them, according to the tradition of Sony, are virtual.

The quality of the picture suited me perfectly. The screen is clear, with a good color reproduction. Black is similar to black (although in fact, of course, slightly grayish). The backlight is uniform, without annoying spots in the corners. Web pages, photos, movies - everything looks great. Unfortunately, the Z Ultra, unlike the Sony Tablet Z, is devoid of a magnetic latch that allows it to turn on when you open the front cover of the case. You have to press a button. Well, nothing, we are familiar guys.


After Z Ultra, even quite big Z and Z1 (above) seem very compact.

The basis of the smart card is the Qualcomm MSM8974 chipset with a quad-core 2.2 GHz processor belonging to the Snapdragon 800 line. This is the best that Qualcomm has to offer today. It is also used in the recently released Xperia Z1 and surprisingly decent LG G2 (I also plan to write about them soon). Interestingly, most of the time, the processor operates at a frequency of 300 to 650 MHz, and to a peak (it is 2150 MHz in practice) it accelerates only under load.


The indicators in AnTuTu Benchmark fascinate ...

RAM 2 gigabytes. That is quite enough. The onboard flash memory of 16 gigabytes is a slot for microSD. I inserted a 64 gigabyte flash drive, it worked without problems.

No complaints about performance. Very, very fast machine. Probably, over time, as it is usual for guglofonov, small slowdowns still appear, but for now - a delight.

The Wi-Fi module is dual-band, so if you have the correct router, you can go to the unspent 5 GHz band. His sensitivity is very decent: in the secluded corners of the apartment, where the Xperia Z smartphone usually loses the signal, the Z Ultra clings to it rather tenaciously.

As for mobile communication, here the ‚Äútenacity‚Äù is quite ordinary. No worse and no better than Xperia Z. Works great in our LTE networks. I tried with sims of MTS and MegaFon, the flight is normal, no dancing with a tambourine is required.

Do not forget about the support of NFC. Not long ago, I was indifferent to this technology, but after the appearance of the LG mobile printer and the Sony DSC-QX10 smart-graph in the household , I appreciated the convenience and began to pay attention. Especially since the wireless headphones with NFC went.

Speaking of headphones. Some review authors were outraged that the connector for them is located on the side. Of course, if the smart card got into his pocket, it could be annoying. But, as we have said, wearing it is better in something more spacious, which means that half a centimeter on the side of the weather will not do. Moreover, with a horizontal arrangement, the connector on top is more than appropriate. And thanks to Sony for finally learning how to make moisture-proof headphone jacks that do not require annoying plugs.



The volume of the Z Ultra is decent, headphones are well pumped even with a resistance of 64 ohms. But the very sound quality inspired medium. You can listen, but the elaboration of the details leaves much to be desired. Xperia Z sounds more interesting. And nothing of the LG G2 with its separate Wolfson audio chip.

The camera in Z Ultra is average. Feels like it migrated to this device from the Xperia V, the smartphone of the last generation. Megapixels is enough, as many as eight pieces. But the quality of shooting is very ordinary. In the bright sunshine it is still here and there, but it is worth the sun to hide, as the noise crawls, the detail falls ... I was particularly alarmed by the work in the panoramic shooting mode: even on the street, the camera clearly ‚Äúsmears‚Äù it. I do not know why they put such a module. Taking into account the price (which is lower) and the capabilities of Sony (its photo modules are used in almost all top products of competitors), it was possible to choose a more vigorous option. Inside

And now it's time for my favorite item in the program, the autopsy. It is worth noting that the Xperia Z Ultra at first managed to perplex even such a seasoned guy, like master Alexander Levchenko. Out of habit, he was looking for bolts or latches, but all of them were not and were not. As a result, the guess came that the whole body was assembled on glue. An autopsy showed that two-thirds of the space inside the case is occupied by a 3000 mAh battery. This in itself is quite a lot, and smartphones with a smaller screen (for example, Sony Xperia Z1 or LG G2) live from it all day long and the next one remains pretty decent. Unfortunately, the huge screen area increases the power consumption of the backlight, so the Z Ultra does not demonstrate any wonders of longevity. Enough for a working day, and in the evening welcome to the docking station for charging. It's sad of course. A pair of autonomous days would be a powerful competitive advantage.



The main load on the battery is exactly the screen. Total
