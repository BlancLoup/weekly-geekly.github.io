OS Tizen from the inside: Meet-Up in the Samsung office on March 27

Hi, Habr!

During our workshops and hackathons, we repeatedly demonstrated and helped other developers to see for themselves how quickly and easily you could master the development of applications for Tizen. We will definitely continue our educational activities: 2014 will be very, very productive in this regard.

But what about the development of the Tizen platform itself?



We thought and decided that now is the time for closer communication with the community on this topic. And in this regard, we invite technical specialists, who are interested in how the Tizen OS is built from the inside, to our office at the address: Moscow, ul. Vozdvizhenka, 10 on the next Thursday, 03/27/2014 at 16:45. The entrance to the business center is from Bolshoy Kislovsky Lane, the Samsung signboard will be above the entrance. Collection of participants in the hall of the first floor in front of the reception. You must have a passport with you!

For communication, you can use our corporate email: dev.cis@partner.samsung.com, as well as VKontakte page: vk.com/tizen_russia and on Facebook: www.facebook.com/groups/rtdug

Come, it will be interesting!
