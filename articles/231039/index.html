And the evolution of Cisco router flash cards

Cisco AS 5350 , a memorable piece of iron. December 22, 2006 ceased to be sold, and in December 2011 - supported. Under it is the Cisco VPN 3020 firewall, the sale ended in 2007, support two years ago. It is still used by some telecoms, but is already outdated -
The performance of data encryption (using the AES / 3DES algorithm) is 50 Mb / s, while modern counterparts show a performance of several hundred Mb / s. This piece of hardware was sharpened only for receiving users, the new hardware also protects network assets from attacks, prevents intrusions (IPS), provides content security services, including URL filtering, protection from phishing, spam, spyware, antivirus software and content filtering. .



Series 1700 , Cisco 1711 and 1751 - went off sale in 2007, with support - in the middle of 2012. This router turned out to be very convenient for ATMs, just like the native size. Simple, reliable, quality. Would put up to 2020, if not for the termination of support.





Cisco 2501 , left the sale in 2002, with support in 2008. Ten years have not been used.



It is stored in the warehouse as a donor of spare parts: what if someone else needs to be replaced in some old system. Another couple of years - and go to the museum, I guess.
