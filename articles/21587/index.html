15 million for lost time (Gazprom and Rutyube)

Gazprom buying Rutyub is most welcome: GazpromMedia not only "pounds" a place in the growing Internet TV market, but, most importantly, disguises its extremely late, fussy activity in the return of lost opportunities. It is not a pity for this, and 15 million, especially since everyone understands that this is a payment for lost time.

About time lost

We look at the Internet projects of GP-Media's subsidiaries: ntv.ru , tnt.ru , results.Ru , 7 days , the sites of radio stations (perhaps, except EchoMoscow ) - everyone looks extremely depressing. It is obvious that the management of companies has focused on the off-line business, and the Internet perceives it as a channel for informing the audience about the products of traditional media, and nothing more. However, they can be understood - the Internet in the total share of the media market is still microscopic.

And then for the GP there is the possibility of revenge in the form of such a tasty and fashionable piece.
