Three convenient programs for working with todo lists

I bring to your attention a small review of programs for Mac OS X, designed to simplify the work with task lists as much as possible.

Check off



Features:
  • Easy and convenient access to tasks through the menu

  • Each task can have a note with additional information.
  • Tasks can be assigned colored labels, as in the Finder
  • IPod Sync

Compatibility: Universal Binary
Status: open source, licensed under the GPL
