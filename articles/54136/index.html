In March 2008, we had a team of four people: two programmers, a designer and a project manager. And we sat down to create Scribbler'a.
All initial ideas were based on what we ourselves lacked during our studies at the institutes. The main needs were the ability to continuously monitor changes in the schedule, as well as work collectively on documents - whether it be group cases or joint preparation of answers to exam questions. And yet I would like to change the scans of lectures, spurs, well, yes, everyone knows, who ever studied at any institute
Further in the development process, we realized that in addition to the basic study there is also an additional study: meetings with interesting people, conferences, master classes and so on. I remember that I tracked such events on the website of my university, which was convenient. But these were the events of only one university. It was necessary to create a place where all the events would be collected, visiting which you can get interesting information and knowledge. We decided that the Scribbler is the most suitable place for this. This is how the Event service appeared.



Next we have created a service of the Communities, which are organized in different directions.
