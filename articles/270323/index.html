Microsoft cloud-based prescription services: the practice of Russian service providers

On November 10, at 12:00 (Moscow time), connect to the next online Jump-Start event to find out how Microsoft builds national cloud services with Russian service providers as part of the Cloud OS Network Russia initiative.



During the event, our partners, participants of Cloud OS Network Russia, will talk about their experience in deploying and operating cloud services based on Microsoft technologies, we will discuss the possibilities of implementing hybrid scripts with Microsoft Azure, and also find out how one of the largest data centers in Russia works.

Broadcast does not require registration, connect and ask questions to the speakers online.
