Porn company sues Tumblr for copyright infringement



If you wondered how sites like Tumblr continue to work, when copywriters of all stripes are activated from all sides, you will be interested - the largest web players (representatives of the porn industry) are suing Tumblr because of copyright infringement.

The publisher of adult products, Perfect 10, filed a lawsuit for $ 5,000,000 for the distribution of photos, the rights to which belong to its regular photographers.

In his lawsuit, Perfect 10 notes that Tumblr indulges violators - all you need to do to distribute photos is a click of a button.

The plaintiff sent six DMCA notices to the former head of Tumblr, John Maloney, with the requirement to remove over 200 photos. So far, only a few photos have been deleted.
