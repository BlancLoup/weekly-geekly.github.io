Touchphone for the blind


Designer Zhenwei You has developed a mobile phone concept called B-touch for blind people, which will help them communicate on a mobile phone like everyone else does. Using this phone is aided by the fact that its screen is based on touch technology for the blind (Braille). Also in the phone there is a voice system and software for its work as a regular phone, a navigation system, a device for reading books and recognizing objects.
