Russian tourist online service OneTwoTrip changed the CEO and development strategy

Russian tourist online service OneTwoTrip announced a change of strategy. This happened after the appointment of a new CEO. The position was taken by the former managing director of the company, Mikhail Sokolov. The new strategy involves the intensification of work on mobile applications. In addition, the new manager plans to enter foreign markets.

Former CEO Petr Kutis will lead the board of directors and will also take the place of product director.

In addition to hotel reservations, ticket sales and train tickets, a car rental service will be launched in October. The company plans to add new services further.
