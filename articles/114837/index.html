I was fortunate to not only watch the live video broadcast of the event, sitting in the London BBC Television Center, but also to touch the novelty with my own hands. It is only 8.8 mm, which is 33% less than the tablet of the first generation and even less than the thickness of the iPhone 4 (9.3 mm). The trend towards sharpening continued, and therefore the iPad 2 looked like a much enlarged iPod touch 4G.   • Improved streaming audio, video and photo content on Apple TV - AirPlay. The main innovation - support for third-party applications for data transfer.
  • Well, the most pleasant, in my opinion, function is the ability to turn the iPhone 4 into a hotspot for other iGadgets.

Free upgrade iOS to version 4.3 will not be next week - March 11th. But only owners of iPad, iPad 2, iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, and also iPod touch 3G and 4G can do it.
