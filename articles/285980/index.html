Sberbank Fund and other investors have invested $ 6.5 million in financial startup Mobeewave

The venture fund of Sberbank, together with other investors, has invested 6.5 million dollars in the Canadian company Mobeewave, RBC reports . Mobeewave technology turns a smartphone with NFC into a payment terminal.



The first round of investment in Mobeewave has been completed. The largest investor was the Ford STB - Venture Fund of Sberbank of Russia. The managing partner of the fund Mircea Mihaescu explained that in the USA the ratio of contactless cards and terminals is 600 to 1 - this explains the interest in the Mobeewave technology.

In the world at the moment there are 8.7 million payment terminals with the possibility of contactless payment from the card. In this case, cards with this technology by the end of 2018 will be released 4.2 billion. Mobeewave technology allows using an application to use a smartphone with NFC as a terminal for receiving payments. NFC, as of February 17, 2015, supports about 300 smartphones.

The venture fund SBT in the amount of $ 100 million was established by Sberbank of Russia in 2012. In July 2013, the fund made a first investment , investing $ 12 million in Sequent Software.
