Large retail network Overstock began to accept Bitcoin



It seems that over time, the Bitcoin cryptocurrency still emerges from the status of a purely speculative unit, and will become a real currency for which you can buy anything without any problems. Yes, now Bitcoin accepts some services, such as Reddit and OKCupid, in Canada you can even pay for a taxi cryptocurrency, and in the US you can buy a pizza. But still, until now, it was quite difficult to buy any real goods for cryptocurrency.

Now this is quite realistic, since the Overstock.com network has begun to accept Bitcoin.

Now you can buy a lot of interesting things for cryptocurrency, from washing machines to coffee makers, and in this case no percentage is withdrawn from a transaction, as it happens when you pay for goods with a credit card. However, for the time being, payment by cryptocurrency is possible only for goods delivered in the USA. After some time, the company promises to expand this opportunity for buyers around the world.



Perhaps, in the near future, other retailers will start accepting a cryptocurrency, in this case, probably, the Bitcoin rate will rise a little more. Although, how to know.
