Do not miss the largest Google event for developers in Europe


We remind you that on September 5 and 6, in the Polish city of Krakow, one of the largest events for European developers - Google Developer Days - will take place. Beginners and experienced professionals will be able to participate in training seminars and master classes, learn about the latest trends in the world of technology, as well as communicate with developers and experts from different parts of Europe.


In Central and Eastern Europe, more than a million highly skilled developers. Every year, more IT specialists graduate in total in Poland, Ukraine, the Czech Republic, Slovakia and Russia from universities than in the USA. That is why, it seems to us that Krakow is an ideal city for European Google Developer Days.


The website has an event schedule available in English. If you are going to come to Krakow or connect to our live broadcasts, you can choose the most interesting sessions in advance.


More than 40 seminars about technology. Most of them are live.


Google Developer Days will begin on Tuesday, September 5 at 10:00 local time (11:00 Moscow time). At the very beginning of the conference, we will introduce you to all the Google innovations for developers. Within two days, participants will be able to participate in seminars and trainings, as well as learn about current events in the industry. For example, we will talk about TensorFlow machine learning, Progressive Web App technologies, development for Google Assistant, using Android for the Internet of things, and much more. Full list of topics - on this page .


Those who will be able to attend GDD personally, will be appreciated by our Codelabs, will be able to attend Ask X seminars (conversations with Google developers) or decide on Review Clinics (view and check their sites and mobile applications).


We invite all female developers in Europe



Technology gives everyone the opportunity, including women, to become new leaders in the digital sphere. Technologies change the world in which we live, and girls and women change technologies, make the industry versatile and free it from prejudice.


One of the main tasks of Google is to make the technology industry as diverse as possible. We want women to have more opportunities to join Google Developer Days. If you are a female developer and we can somehow help you attend our conference, write to gdd-europe@google.com.


And further

Do not forget to register . If you are unable to attend in person, watch the live event broadcast on Google Developers YouTube channel and follow the news on the #GDDEurope tag on Twitter , Facebook and Google+ .
