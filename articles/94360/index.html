x86-pc in the car

Greetings to the habrasoobshchestvo. Today I would like to touch on the topic of PC-x86 in the car. This area is very vaguely displayed in this blog, except for car racks for laptops.
Next under the cut are a dozen photos with peppered text. Geek motorists, you will be interested.





Car





Ford Transit is a pre-latest model produced from 2000 to 2006 inclusive. Medium base and height, 2 sliding doors on the sides. Initially, 3 seater (ala Gazelle?))), But has been redone. Half of the truck part of the car, it was decided to fence in favor of increasing the cabin. Thus, it was possible to install another row of seats. (Ford‚Äôs native ‚Äústools‚Äù were also removed, all the BMW heated seats) The cabin is spacious, even if you are standing on your head, the trunk is very roomy at the same time. :)



Without going into details, the entire cabin is treated with thermo-sound insulation, upholstered with thick plywood, and then covered with a Carpet.



PC


The selection of components was not a difficult task. Of course, this is a fashionable Intel Atom + i945 bundle now. The reasons for this choice are many. The ability to install in a small case, despite this, eliminates the problem of cooling, a small price and, at the same time, almost all components are included in the $ 50-100. Worried about performance ... and in vain, everything loads quickly and runs, watching FullHD in traffic was not in the plans. ;)

  • HDD - by itself, only notebook 2.5 ', about SSD - is still too expensive.
  • RAM - 1GB, enough. More - too long inclusion after hibernate. Less - obviously not enough for a full-fledged comfortable work CarPC.
  • The DVD drive is a regular notebook (NEC).


All components are neatly installed in the Mini-ITX case Modecom FEEL 102 , screwed 1 small cooler, run in half-power.





This small box is located under the navigator's seat. :)



* Soon the case is to be replaced, I want 2 HDD, drive in the "glove box", close up all the openings with dust filters and other small treats.

Power



Power is provided by a regular power supply inside the case, the allowable voltage at the input is 7-14 volts. It feeds directly from the battery through the diode (-1 volt). On - not a fixed button, off. - either by the same button, or the ACC of a car with a delay of two minutes. (any time relay)

Control



To control the touch-screen is quite convenient, but not when you're sitting behind the wheel. Came to the aid of a 3d-joystick from Logitech . As usual, this brand pleases. Very high quality, pleasant materials. Despite the very unusual use of the joystick, it turned out to be very convenient to use. No false positives, easy to calibrate and adjust.
