New Fidel.ru

Fidel Solutions just recently launched a radically updated site Fidel.ru for pilot-combat operation. As before, we concentrate on music and video, but in a new form and quality. In fact, this is no longer a store, but something more, which the project team would like to tell you in the next few paragraphs. We highlight in our opinion the most significant options that affect the user experience and, we hope, a stable desire to maintain contact with the project for long months.
