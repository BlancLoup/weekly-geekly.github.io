Toshiba Satellite U920t Ultrabook Transformer Video Review



Toshiba Satellite U920t is an ultra-transformer, equipped with a 12.5-inch touchscreen IPS display with a resolution of 1366x768. Thanks to a special slider mechanism, the ultrabook can turn into a full-fledged tablet. The device works on the basis of low-voltage Intel Core i5-3317U processors, have onboard 4 GB of RAM and 128 GB solid-state drives.

The thickness of the device is 20 mm, and the weight is about one and a half kilograms. The transformer is equipped with two USB 3.0 ports, an HDMI port, an SD card reader and a combined audio input for connecting headphones or a headset. The battery life is about 5-6 hours of Internet surfing over WiFi.
