Buyers create fake Amazon pages to bring prices down

Large US stores promise prices no higher than Amazon ones than enterprising citizens took advantage of.

Black Friday's big stores open very early: at six in the morning and even earlier. Since 2012, those Walmart stores that do not work around the clock open at 8 pm on the day before the sale.

On November 13, Walmart announced a pricing policy at the time of sales prices of some online sites. This is a common practice ; many other stores do the same. On Black Friday, some retailers hold prices at Amazon, and for the rest of the year their price tags correspond to Best Buy and Target.

The policy says literally that if a customer finds a product cheaper in one of the online stores from the list, then Walmart will sell the product at the same price. This was taken advantage of by some enterprising buyers.
