Base GeoIP - countries and cities, January 2011

A new version of the Russian-language database of countries and cities has been published. Major changes:
- The Google Maps API was used to translate city names ( chernikov idea)
- geobaza.ru database (idea derketzer ) is connected to search in the CIS countries
The result of this work is available for free download.

The database is distributed in two versions: " Countries and cities " (22Mb, after installing ~ 123Mb) and " Only countries " (1Mb, after installing ~ 6Mb). Also in the archives is a small example of using the database in php.

The accuracy of determining the country ~ 99%, the accuracy of determining the cities on average ~ 95%, Russia ~ 98%, Ukraine ~ 95%. Whether it is a lot or a little depends on the tasks that you are going to solve with the help of this base.

Test demo - determine your country and city by IP-address

Work demo - determining your city by IP in the form of a flight search
