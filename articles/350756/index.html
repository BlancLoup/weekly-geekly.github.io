AI.Hack - a series of hackathons in Moscow and St. Petersburg
Hi, Habr! Very soon AI and hackathons will be held in Moscow and St. Petersburg.

It is not the first time when artificial intelligence becomes the subject of a hackathon, because solutions that can be built with its help are essentially limited only by the imagination and skill of teams.

This time the tasks are set:

  • Gazprom Neft
  • Speech Technology Center
  • Leaked
  • BestFit.ai
  • Robomed

But first things first.

Gazprom Neft


From 2016, the company has been conducting detailed logging of customers' transactions - payment for gasoline cards and goods sold in small stores at the gas station territory.

Participants for work will be available about 1.5 GB of data.

But what you need to do:

Predicting customer churn


St. Petersburg

Using the data provided, create an algorithm that will be able to identify among customers those who are planning to stop refueling at the Gazprom Neft filling stations. That is, it is necessary to create a predictive user model from the horizons of 1 month. From the options presented by the teams, the best one will be selected.

Clustering customers based on the purchase of petroleum products and the search for patterns of consumption of related goods and services


St. Petersburg

It is necessary to analyze and create the maximum possible number of customer segments based on the purchase of petroleum products.

For each of the selected segments to find the specific patterns of consumption of related goods and services.

Dataset is the transaction flow of the Gazprom Neft filling station network sales that you use to solve a case study.

  • NP sales
  • Sales STIU combined in nomenclature groups

The task consists of two parts:

  1. Find the maximum number of patterns in the purchase of petroleum products and divide customers into segments based on each pattern
