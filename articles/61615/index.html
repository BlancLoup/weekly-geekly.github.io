The first Japanese netbook with WiMAX



Japanese Onkyo in the lineup of 10-inch netbook Sotec C204 announced the model A5 - the first Japanese netbook in which there are WiMAX and Wi-Fi modules.

In addition to the simultaneous presence of WiMAX and Wi-Fi, the netbook does not represent anything remarkable. The machine is built on a standard Intel Atom N270 processor (1.6 GHz) and Intel 945GSE Express chipset. The netbook is equipped with 1 GB DDR2 533 MHz RAM, a 32 GB SSD hard drive, a 1.3 megapixel webcam, a 4 in 1 card reader (SD / SDHC / MMC / MS Pro) and two USB ports.
