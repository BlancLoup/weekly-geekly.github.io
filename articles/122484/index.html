Opera Startup Awards - Semifinal


So, the experimental project of Opera Software, the Department of Technological Entrepreneurship and Innovation Management at St. Petersburg State University of Information Technologies, Mechanics and Optics (ITMO) and the MTE Master Program (ITMO) , which began a few months ago , came to an intermediate finish: on Saturday, June 25 , will be the semi-final of this unusual event. Details can be found on the project page . I am pleased to announce that the organizers of the competition have prepared an additional bonus - all finalists also become winners of the competition of the Master of Technology Entrepreneurship Master Program portfolio and are accepted to the MTE magistracy on preferential terms without an entrance exam.

For those who, for various reasons, could not attend the seminars held on the days of the competition, we prepared a full set of videos with recordings of the presentations of the event organizers, including the presentations of Charles McKatiNeville, the main specialist in web standards at Opera Software. The publication and video recording of a master class on web development, which was held in real time under the guidance of Charles in the second part of the seminar, also began.

We invite all those who have not yet had time to register their idea for participation in the Opera Startup Awards to do this through the form on the website before 6 pm on June 24 (Friday). All the details are on the project website .
