I immediately tuned in to create a hardware console. Why do we need it? The hardware console, firstly, immediately gives us a full root without dancing with a tambourine, and secondly (which is more important) gives us access to the u-boot bootloader (analog of the comp BIOS), and so we can change the firmware, restore linux even if we we will lock it in with our malicious experiments, etc. In comparison with which the access that was obtained here is a warm-up, and is suitable for those who are not friends with the solder.

We open the card. It is convenient to begin to open it from the side opposite to the contacts. There is a knife, and allows you to push the edges.
