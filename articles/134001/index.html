The conditions of the financial market in Ukraine are slightly more flexible than in Russia. At least they were until the moment when the NBU forced Fin. institutions in case of currency exchange require identity documents from clients. Banks and exchange offices (which after the well-known actions of the NBU diminished) are trying to attract a client to themselves with a close location, a convenient course. For the sake of these needs, the FINANCE.UA portal has a long and successful opportunity for fin. institutions publish their exchange rates. These rates can be presented in the form of a table (for finding the best course), graphs (for analyzing the dynamics of a course change) and in the form of marked marks on the map (for finding a convenient exchange rate nearby).
