META company released an updated MetaMarket

Today, the Ukrainian company META has published a new version of the MetaMarket price aggregator.



This service not only gained a fresh design, but also enriched with a number of user-friendly features and interface elements.



The interface of MetaMarket has been completely updated, it has become more modern and intuitive.
Serious functional changes, by and large, have undergone 3 pages: a catalog section with a list of products, a product card and a search in the catalog.

1. Directory section

Example: metamarket.ua/ru/telefony-i-svyaz/mobilnye-telefony

The sections of the catalog on the new MetaMarket have acquired a complete form of selection, with the help of which one can easily restrict many goods of the section both by manufacturer and price, and by detailed technical characteristics.

Each item in the catalog section has got the option ‚ÄúAdd to comparison‚Äù, with which you can add products to the comparison list and analyze their differences in technical parameters.

2. Item Card

Example: metamarket.ua/ru/mobilnye-telefony/0-500036123/Apple-iPhone-4-32Gb

Tabs appeared in the item cards, containing:
  • store offers;
  • specifications;
  • customer reviews;
  • accessories suitable for the selected product;
  • graphs of price, supply and demand of the product.
A user rating system has also been added, which allows you to vote for useful and high-quality products.
