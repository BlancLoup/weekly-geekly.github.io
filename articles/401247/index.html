Olimex TERES I Modular DIY Laptop



Olimex‚Äôs Bulgarian hackers have been working on the open source design of the laptop on the Allwinner A64 processor for over a year - and finally completed this unique project . From February 2017, the company begins to take orders for sets to build a laptop for 225 euros .

The open design allows you to add the necessary modules to your laptop: GPS, GSM or something more exotic, change the design to your taste. The case is included in the package, although it can be printed out on a 3D printer using a published CAD model.

The user manual explains that the laptop is not sold ready-made. Send only assembly kits.



The list of parts (with the name of the part on the item) see below. Details are listed in the order in which they appear in the assembly instructions:

  • TERES-006-Keyboard - QWERTY keyboard.

  • TERES-023-Touch - touchpad with cover TERES-022-Touch-Cover and buttons TERES-010-Touch-Btns. The touchpad should be attached very carefully, connector up, that is, so that he looked in the opposite direction from the touchpad board.

  • TERES-PCB3-Touch - touchpad board.

  • TERES-PCB2-IO is a board with a headphone jack, microSD and USB connectors.

  • TERES-PCB4-Btn is the board for the power button along with a plastic button.

  • TERES-009-Pwr-Btn and shrink sink TERES-013-LED-pipe.

  • TERES-PCB1-A64 is a motherboard on the Allwinner A64 processor. It should be handled with extreme care, not bending, not dropping. It is very sensitive to static electricity. The TERES-PCB2-IO I / O board is attached to TERES-PCB1-A64 with a TERES-027-FPC-IO-Main cable. The 40-pin cable is exactly sized for the distance between the boards. It extends through the entire body of the laptop, almost without bending and not taking up extra space. To connect the other boards are more miniature cables TERES-028-FPC-Pwr-Main and TERES-029-FPC-Touch-Btn. It will not be so easy to connect the touchscreen fee - the cable will need to be bent at a right angle twice, and then it will definitely fit along a predetermined trajectory.

  • TERES-PCB5-KEYBOARD - keyboard control board (bevelled board in the center in the photo above) is connected to the touchpad board with the TERES-031-FPC-Kbd-Btn cable.
