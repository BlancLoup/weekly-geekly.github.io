The little redis book



Yesterday Karl Seguin , the author of The Little MongoDB Book , which is often recommended for a quick start with MongoDB, published a similar guide for Redis called The Little Redis Book (with the participation of Perry Neal ). The book consists of 29 pages written in 2 short days using Markdown . The original markup of the book is on github . The book itself is distributed freely under the Attribution-NonCommercial 3.0 Unported license .
