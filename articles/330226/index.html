Global DevOps Bootcamp


Before kat we invited you to the event and of course we will tell about it in more detail.

Global DevOps Bootcamp is a one-day developer event held by local DevOps communities around the world on June 17th.

The holding initiative is owned by Xpirit and Solidify. In Moscow, it is organized by the Microsoft Most Valuable Professional community and will be held in Digital October.

Global DevOps Bootcamp is fully dedicated to the practices and tools of DevOps , built on the basis of Microsoft technologies. Learn all about the latest trends of DevOps, get into the essence of new technologies and share your experience with the rest of the community, and also try your hand at Global DevOps hackathon.

Leading DevOps experts will be the curators of your team and help in project development.

The event will be opened by Donovan Brown (Donovan Brown Program Manager) at Microsoft , a world-renowned DevOps specialist.

On the jury: Microsoft, Yandex, Sberbank Technologies, Kaspersky Lab, ABBYY
