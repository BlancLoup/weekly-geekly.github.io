Published records of the conference reports ALM Summit 2016

On February 18 in Moscow, we traditionally held our annual conference on Microsoft solutions in the field of software development lifecycle management. This year, the ALM Summit consisted of a main track and round tables on which the following thematic areas were covered:

  • ALM infrastructure solutions in the cloud that allow you to quickly deploy a set of ALM tools and launch the project in a matter of hours.
  • Methodological recommendations on project management, SCRUM, Kanban, Agile, collecting information on the progress of the project, its analysis and reporting.
  • Quality assurance of developed systems using testing, how to build an effective testing environment using the tools of Team Foundation Foundation Server and Team Foundation Services in the cloud.
  • Operation of developed systems, providing feedback to improve quality.

As usual, we broadcast and record the conference.
