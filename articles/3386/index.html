Word is vulnerable again

A day after the release of the package "Tuesday's patches" in MS Office found another vulnerability .

This time it is necessary to update Word and other programs in Office versions 2000 and XP. The vulnerability is related to the possible execution of remote program code. Microsoft has already received reports of the attacks, but does not report when the security update will be released. By tradition, users are asked not to open Word documents received from unknown senders.

In recent months, this is the ninth identified vulnerability in office programs developed by Microsoft .
