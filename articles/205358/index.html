British startup PlayCanvas became the best in Europe

Ukrainian project LeadScanner ranked second

On December 5 in Kiev, during the Startup AddVenture conference, the best startup in Europe was identified. They became the project PlayCanvas (UK) - a cloud platform for developing 3D games. The winners received an investment of ‚Ç¨ 25,000 and a real boxing champion belt.



A total of 350 teams have applied for participation in Startup Competition. Applications were accepted and reviewed through AngelList . The jury consisted of 20 investors, business angels, representatives of venture funds from the USA, Europe and the CIS. The jury was headed by Dave McClure - the founder of the largest venture seed fund in the world, 500 Startups (USA) and Kausal Chokshi - President of the Cross Border Angels Foundation (USA). The finalists of the competition passed an intensive three-day pitching course based on the Happy Farm business incubator .



Ukrainian startup LeadScanner took second place and received $ 25,000. LeadScanner offers a new generation of tools that helps small and medium businesses to increase sales by finding customers in social networks. With the help of special algorithms, LeadScanner finds social network users who want to buy certain products or services, and then provides their contacts in social networks to interested companies. Service developed by YouScan team under the direction of Alexey Orap.

About the Startup AddVenture Conference
Startup AddVenture (Europe Venture Summit) is the largest international startup conference that took place on December 4-5 in Kiev, at the NSC Olimpiysky, with the participation of 50 stars of the startup industry and venture business in the US and Europe. The event gathered more than 1500 IT entrepreneurs, venture investors, business angels and incubators from Ukraine, the CIS, Europe and the USA. The conference program consisted of panel discussions, interactive workshops, Expo and Startup Competition. The conference is organized by Happy Farm Business Incubator . The next Startup AddVenture conference will take place on December 3-4, 2014.
