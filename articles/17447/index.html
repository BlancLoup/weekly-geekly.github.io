Google Docs are ten times more popular than Office Live

The Google Docs and Spreadsheets office software suite that has not yet been out of beta has already occupied the first place on the Internet in terms of attendance, an order of magnitude ahead of all its competitors.

This is seven times more than the year before in the same month when Google Docs was first born.
