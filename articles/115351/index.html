Art directorate and design


Illustration by Kevin Cornell

The imminent appearance of art directorship on the Web is one of the latest trends in interactive design. Currently, several sites are dedicated to this event. There are even plug-ins for these sites. Sadly, however, many designers do not understand the difference between design and art directorship ; even more sad is that many art directors don't see her either. But art directorship creates the basis for design, it adds humanity to design.

Art directorate is not blogazine


A popular article, The Death of the Blog Post, talks about a ‚Äúblog journal,‚Äù a mix of journal articles and blog posts. The article states: outstanding designers said a new word and began to make the concept of " art-directorship " on the Internet. The article states that the art direction in the "blog-magazine" is much more than the unique design of each blog post. The term "blog journal" is often confused by art directors . It is like saying, ‚ÄúLook! This blog is like a magazine, because every post is different! ‚ÄùOften,‚Äú blog magazines ‚Äùare just‚Äú dressed up ‚Äùblog posts.

Magazines are not just created for the individual design of articles. Their goal is to combine the visual image and text to enhance the expressiveness of the narrative. A variety of designs are the result of this aspiration, and not just diversity for the sake of diversity. In the staff of the magazine, art directors and copywriters spend a lot of time on brainstorming in search of various ways to enhance the story ‚Äî from choosing a design style and relevant content features to honing the key of the story.

To transfer this process to the practice of web design, we need other structures that would give flexibility within a given format. Their germ can be considered unique fields for user styles within content management systems at the level of a separate blog post. However, the ability to automatically create unique custom CSS files does not mean that this publication has passed the stage of artistic control (control of the art director ). Art direction goes beyond user blog posts. This is something more. Art direction raises and enhances expressiveness.

What is what


Artistic leadership brings clarity and certainty; it helps our work to transmit a certain signal to a specific group of people. Art directorship combines art and design in order to create a cultural and emotional response. It has an impact on movies, music, websites, magazines - almost everything that causes us to respond. Without artistic supervision, we are left with dry, sterile episodes that are quickly forgotten. Can an ad in the New York subway about homeless people make you want to donate money? Why do you want to pray Clarice Starling to turn around , despite the fact that you know that she can not hear you? Why do candles turn ordinary food into a romantic evening? Art directorship refers to the challenge of corresponding emotions, to creating an emotional connection with what you see and experience.

By contrast, design is the technical realization of this connection. Are these colors combined? Is the length of the text line convenient for long reading? Is the photo cutting? Does typographical hierarchy work? How well balanced is this composition?

If I tell my wife that I love her, but I do it with a frowning face, her impression will be ambiguous. If I say this indifferently, looking at the TV, she just won't believe me. But when I say this with a sincere smile and a bouquet of flowers - my thought is clear. In this example, my love is art-directorship , whereas my smile and the deep red color of roses are design. They work hand in hand to convey a thought emotionally and physically. Design is the perfection of technology; Art directorship refers to the important, but sometimes not quite palpable emotions that inspire the design.

Regarding art directors


Large differences in the role of art director reinforce the misunderstanding of the difference between art direction and design. Following one extreme, some agencies hire art directors who do not know how to design, but who understand it enough to give instructions to other designers. On the other hand, in some agencies, the position of art director is the next logical step towards paying for an experienced designer. Most jobs are somewhere in between.

Many small agencies for various reasons do not hire art directors . This fact gives us the wrong idea that art directorship is an optional part of the creative process. However, the opposite is also true. Art directorship is so important that it is never missed, it is simply unintentionally and subconsciously performed by designers who are not ready to perform such duties.

I had a system on my site ‚Äî hard stencils that did not give much flexibility, so I worked within these limits. I did not want to change the design for the design itself. I wanted my readers to understand how special my grandfather was to me. I wanted to convey my thoughts and feelings as much as possible and thereby at least slightly change their attitude to reality. I wanted them to empathize with me, that, reading my text, they were with me. Art directorship , and not just design, allowed to achieve this effect.
