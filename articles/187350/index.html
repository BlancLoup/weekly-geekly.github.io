Sattelite Reign - the spiritual heir of the Syndicate

About three weeks ago, Kickstarter launched a campaign to raise funds for the development and launch of the Satellite Reign game, the spiritual heir to the isometric action / strategy games Syndicate and Syndicate Wars .

In 2012, the Syndicate series was rebooted in the form of a first-person shooter, but it didn‚Äôt get particularly popular, even though the multiplayer mode was tried with maximum attention to the original series.

Realizing that the fans did not get what they wanted, Mike Diskett (Mike Diskett), the author of the original Syndicate Wars decided to assemble a team of five people to create the correct continuer of the series. The team includes people who worked on such games as GTA IV, Darksiders II, Star Wars, LA Noire, Syndicate and Syndicate Wars, as well as many others.
