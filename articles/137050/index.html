Glove overview for iGlover Classic touch screens

All modern smartphones and tablets are equipped with capacitive touch screens. They only respond to the touch of fingers or special stylus. So, in the cold, which is now only getting stronger, using a smartphone with bare hands becomes uncomfortable. Some got the hang of answering calls with their noses, but with SMS they can cause problems. Special gloves for similar screens have come to our editorial office, more details in the video review:



IGlover classic gloves consist of acrylic with a small addition of six. Due to the use of a special conductive fiber at the fingertips, they are able to interact with capacitive screens. iGlover is compatible with Android devices, as well as with iPhone , iPod touch , iPad and other gadgets.

This model is presented in several colors, while the size is universal, and is suitable for most users. Inside there is a layer of insulation, which is largely absent from counterparts on the market. Due to this, they can withstand more severe frosts.
