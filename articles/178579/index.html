Open source phone "Gongkai" for $ 12

For the price of a couple of cups of coffee, you get a full-fledged phone with a two-color OLED screen, Bluetooth and the ability to play MP3 music. All this is in a transparent green case.

As bloggers write, the estimated cost of a Gongkai phone is about $ 10, that is, it is also sold with a good margin of 20%. The package includes a charger, USB cable and a protective silicone case.

It is interesting that in the assembly of the device no screws or screws are used, parts of the case are simply latched using a mechanism. Fastening internal components is also likely to reduce the cost of production - the screen, a lithium-polymer battery and other parts are simply soldered to the motherboard.
