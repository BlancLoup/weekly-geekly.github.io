Hone Bluetooth Dongle helps keep your keys safe.



The remote controls and keys are lost so often that jokes are drawn up, comics are drawn, and they are just joking. Pul d / y is still fine - it is usually easy to find, and the loss of the console for some time is not very critical. But the keys have an interesting feature - they are always in sight when they are needed, and immediately disappear as soon as you need to leave the apartment, and go to an important meeting, to which you are already late. And in fact, if the phone is lost, you can call it from another phone. And you will not call the keys. But now the situation has changed.

Hone Bluetooth Dongle, a miniature signaling device that syncs with a Bluetooth wireless phone, is now available. Unfortunately, the developers have so far created software only for Apple devices, depriving all other platforms of attention. Presumably, soon this defect will be corrected.



By itself, the key fob serves as an indicator of the location of keys. If you lose them, you need to take the phone with the installed software, and run the program. When you click on the "Find" button, the key fob starts to vibrate and beep, so finding the keys becomes possible in a few seconds. It works all at a distance of up to 45 meters.



At the same time, the keychain's battery lasts for about half a year of work, so that every two to three days it will not have to be charged. And this device is not so much - $ 49. Of course, not everyone needs this device, but who needs it will pay a large amount, so long as the keys are no longer lost.
