Mixlr - service for music broadcasts

It so happened that I wanted to arrange a small concert for my distant friends, friends and just interested people. Since most of them live two, four or more time zones away from me, I started thinking about audio broadcasting, good from an iron point of view, everything is easily realizable (Alpha-Alpha, my dear ...) But the problem is my 44kb recoil is clearly not pulled on the audio server. Of course, there is Skype, but there are limits on the number of conference participants - this time, and not everyone has it - these are two. And we need to add users ... Horror, in short. And he takes only the left channel - it creates additional torment.
But after some time, I found Him, My Dream Service, such a hefty Mixlr ! Mixlr ! I will also tell about it under a cat. And a little bit about how I process the sound before sending.
So, I ask to the table under the cat! (But be careful! Traffic!)





The title screen is unlikely to appeal to feminists, but it allows you to quickly select a category of broadcasts or radio, go there and hear everything your heart desires. In addition, users can click the ‚ÄúBroadcast now‚Äù button to go to the broadcast start window, where ...

In addition to the avatar, name and sharing buttons, there is a button that will show us how many users are listening to the broadcast, along with their avatars, if any. By the way, about the connection. In addition to Soundcloud, you can connect twitter / facebook accounts to automatically send messages about the start of the broadcast and mixcloud, audioboo and dropbox. Why do we need the first two, alas, I do not know, but the connected dropbox allows you to export recordings of your broadcasts to it. Yes, they are recorded. There are no limits. Anyway, you can connect dropbox and throw everything there, without straining the server Mixlr.
In addition, you can get the player code for your broadcast for a website or blog, Wordpress or Facebook.


In case you decide to make a mini-radio, you can schedule the start time of the broadcast. It will end, obviously, when the tracks run out. But if you decide to broadcast live, then go to the desktop application.
