Microsoft won the trial against the site that distributes certification training

A federal court in Connecticut decided to prohibit the site preparing for the network certification tests to distribute materials related to Microsoft after the software giant filed a lawsuit against the company, claiming that they are selling real exam tasks.

A preliminary conclusion signed last week by District Judge Warren Eginton (Warren Eginton), Pass4sure.com and his parent company Freetech Services were prohibited from distributing these materials.
