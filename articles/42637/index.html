Site Idea - Free Domain Name Dictionary

Today, it is often possible to find a situation where entrepreneurs create a company and choose a name for it based not on their desires, but from a free domain name for this business.

The idea that came to my mind is:
  1. We take the dictionary of nouns of the Russian language (nominative, singular) and translating them into translit, we check if they are free in the .RU zone (possibly .COM) I am sure that there are such words and there are a lot of them.
  2. We form the basis of these words, where visitors, developers and other people interested in this information will be able to choose an interesting name for themselves.

In addition, it is impossible not to take into account that English is a fairly strong part of our life, and even more so in the business environment, so the same can be done with the dictionary of English words by applying them to .RU (and to a lesser extent to .COM )

To understand, it makes sense to do this, or it is not worth the effort, you need to consider all aspects of the case, but I am sure that the idea has the right to exist.

PS: In addition to domain names, you can consider a similar service for the names of postal addresses of free mail services, although this is hardly promising due to the absence of even a hint of commercial activity in this regard.
