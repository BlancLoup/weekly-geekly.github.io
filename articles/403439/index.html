The developers of the Red Dead Redemption mod for GTA V have stopped working on the project



Rockstar Games makes great games, nobody doubts it. And this concerns not only GTA, the developer and the publisher have other quite popular games. For example, Red Dead Redemption . This is a third-person action-adventure western with an open world and RPG elements.

Game Western has become so popular that there are those who want to connect the world of this game with GTA. On March 18, fans of both games announced the start of work on a mod, which was to be transferred to GTA V locations from Red Dead Redemption. The announcement of the start of development was accompanied by a demonstration of screenshots of the mod. Tens of thousands of gamers eagerly awaited the completion of work, but, unfortunately, these expectations were in vain.

Today, the same developers that announced the new mod in March, announced the termination of work on the project. The disappointment is even stronger because shortly before this, the mods posted a video demonstrating the capabilities of their mod. Now even access to this video is closed. The reason - copyright infringement, in this case - the rights of the company Rockstar. Obviously, the project attracted the attention of not only gamers, but also copyright holders.
