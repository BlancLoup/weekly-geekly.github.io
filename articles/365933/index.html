About Kingston


The American company Kingston was founded in 1987. Currently, the company has more than 4,000 employees in divisions around the world. Priority Kingston - the highest quality products, which is achieved through rigorous testing. Support for consistently high standards is the key to a successful partnership with many partners over the years of the company's work. In addition, an important place is occupied by investing in the development of its own employees.
