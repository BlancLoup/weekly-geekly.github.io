In the rhythm of modern life

It would seem that technical progress has led to saving time and manpower. But do people free up their time on themselves, or use it to become even more busy?

Here, for example, that the president of Microsoft in Russia advises to combat the lack of time:

  • Plan your schedule in such a way as to solve as few secondary urgent tasks as possible, and important ones - to solve before they become urgent;
  • Do what you love with maximum engagement;
  • Consider the principle of life balance as an important personal and managerial task.

Yes, we can not pause the world. But we can change the approach to business and attitude towards our own lives.

About a dream


The best rest is not a change of activity at all. The best (and necessary) rest is a dream.

Working to the detriment of sleep is not correct. Try to sleep for 7-8 hours a day, adhering to a constant schedule. Do not miss the opportunity to sleep at lunchtime for 20-30 minutes.

You can only occasionally allow yourself to work day and night. Regular lack of sleep will cause a number of problems (from the aforementioned book ‚ÄúRework‚Äù):

  • Loss of ingenuity. People who achieve a result many times more effective than the majority are distinguished from this majority not by greater diligence, but by ingenuity, which allows them to find a solution that requires one tenth of the effort compared to the others. When you do not sleep, you lose such an important advantage as ingenuity;
  • Lack of drive. You will be doing what is really not important, for example, reading another non-meaningful article. When tired, you lack the motivation to do big things;
  • Aggression. Feeling tired, you cease to be patient and tolerant. The likelihood that others will become victims of your insomnia increases;
  • Bad decisions. It is bad decisions you are destined to take in a state of fatigue;
  • Stubbornness: in a state of fatigue, it always seems easier for a person to continue to work in the chosen direction, no matter how bad it is, instead of revising its correctness. The finish line seems like a constant mirage, and everything ends up going too far into the desert.

About goals


Any action will be much more effective if you break it into goals, tasks. Tasks are the same goals, only on a smaller scale.

If the goal is not recorded, then it does not exist. You can write it anywhere: in the diary, on a sticker, on a piece of paper. Many people prefer to use special services, for example, the service of achieving the goals of Smartprogress . This is one of the best Russian-language services, which allows you to record goals, dividing them into stages.

By registering on the site, you formulate your goal, break it into stages, and keep a kind of diary, commenting on the progress of each stage and the progress towards the final goal. The service also allows you to discuss your own and other people's goals with other users, consult, comment, etc. The result of all this is a visual representation of the progress in the process of accomplishing its goal. Some consider these services to be something of a narrowly focused social network, and doubt their usefulness. But for many, such a public statement of their goals really helps in achieving them. Among the English-speaking public, 43things.com is a very popular service.


About time management



The fulfillment of our duties does not always cause us spiritual enthusiasm and enthusiasm. Instead of working, we read articles that are supposedly useful to us, we put things in order on the desktop, etc. In other words, we do anything to delay the start of work on an uninteresting or routine task.

The most effective way to solve the above problem is the correct distribution of working time, time management.

A large number of books, articles and manuals have been written on how to organize time. You can easily find a lot of materials on the topic, for example, on the website improvement.ru , on thematic forums and in blogs.

Getting acquainted with the methods of proper organization of working time, do not forget about the main thing:
Time management for you is not a theoretical discipline, but an applied method.

If instead of working you will only study the methods of its organization, this is unlikely to contribute to success.
We are all different, so maybe the individual way will be better than recommended in any new book? All this you do to make it more convenient.

Total


In order for life and work to turn from a race and stress into an enjoyable, calm process, it is necessary not so much:

  • Clear formulation of strategic objectives and tactical objectives;
  • The correct distribution of working time;
  • Full rest and sleep.

Indeed, a little, and even something similar to the advice of Captain Obvious. But how many of us follow these simple rules?
