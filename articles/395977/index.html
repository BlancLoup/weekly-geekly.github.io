The semi-annual AnTuTu rating has redeployed places in the 10th leading smartphone

The developers of the popular mobile benchmark AnTuTu presented to the attention of the interested public the results of the updated ranking of the top 10 most productive smartphones of the first half of 2016. The rating was compiled based on the results of testing devices using the sixth version of the Antutu Benchmark 6.0 application for the time interval from January 1 to June 30, 2016.



The first five positions in the rating of July 1, 2016 were the models of Chinese smartphones, and the absolute leader was the OnePlus 3 , announced on June 14 of this year, with more than 140,000 points. Behind him downstream are the Vivo Xplay 5 Elite, LeEco Le Max 2, ZUK Z2 Pro and Xiaomi Mi5. Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge with a result of 134 599 got only the sixth position, and Apple iPhone 6s uncompromising competitors ousted to the seventh line. The four-inch iPhone SE turned out to be the ninth, skipping ahead one position ZTE Axon 7. Closes the top ten leaders ZUK Z2.

The rankings took into account the results obtained using Antutu Benchmark 6.0. The quality of testing and the objectivity of the results are the business card of the company, and here everything is really very serious. Phishing paid tests are excluded by definition. To be able to get into the list, each smartphone had to be tested at least 500 times, and only the arithmetic mean value obtained by processing the results of at least 2000 tests of the same models with the same components was taken into account in comparison.



As demonstrated by the test results, some of the tested devices did not show any significant advantages due to 6 GB of RAM. The delta with the models of 4 GB of RAM was only a few percent. At the same time, as we know, the specificity of AnTuTu tests is in their focus on evaluating performance, first of all, on the CPU, GPU and drive.

Thus, seven smartphones from Chinese manufacturers immediately determined their place in the top of the AnTuTu rating, which makes it possible to speak about a quite clearly formed trend.
