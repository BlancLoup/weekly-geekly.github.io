Nintendo will offer their games (3DS and Wii U) for download



The company Nintendo, despite the fact that its products remain popular, recently announced significant losses. The company management, of course, has long been aware of this state of affairs, and is trying to take appropriate measures. One of the steps to normalize the business is to open your own online game catalog for 3Ds and Wii U platforms. According to the developers, games for the 3DS portable console and the next generation of the Wii U consoles can be bought in this catalog immediately after the finished version of the toy appears.

As for losses, they, according to the management, are due to a change in priority among gamers. Many of them buy powerful smartphones instead of portable (and also usual) gaming consoles. As a result, the console business of Nintendo is not in the best condition. True, soon there will be a console and a new generation of Wii U, which can change the situation.

In the online catalog it will be possible to buy games for Wii U, and not just for 3DS. This may increase the company's revenue. Other steps are now being taken to normalize Nintendo‚Äôs business. At the end of this year, fans of the Super Mario series will already be able to buy a new version of the toy, and this game can be purchased in the Nintendo eShop catalog.

The appearance of the online catalog of games from Nintendo did not become such a big surprise - after all, most game companies have such resources, and they work very well. In the case of Nintendo, we can say that the company was somehow late with its own catalog. The company's management believes that the new resource will help Nintendo compete successfully with Microsoft and Sony.
