New netbook from ASUS

ASUS is preparing to launch its new netbook. The model, called the N10 (what about the Eee?), Will be equipped with a 10.2-inch display and will work under Vista or Linux.


At the expense of processor power (and this, perhaps, will be the Intel Atom) and the amount of RAM data is not yet available. We can assume that since ASUS is going to deliver a netbook with Vista, the machine will be more powerful than its previous colleagues in the Eee PC series. At least the HDD in the N10 is exactly larger - 320 GB.

Of the required and useful appendages in the N10 available HDMI output, SDHC card reader, USB 2.0 ports, Bluetooth 2.0 and built-in 1.3-megapixel camera.

As for the price, it is defined in the range from 330 to 430 euros. Still relatively cheap. Although it is possible to accurately estimate the adequacy of pricing policy later, when ASUS publishes the full specifications of the product.
