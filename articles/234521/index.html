Korean 3D-printer 3Dison. We print with plastic, metal, chocolate, and then we engrave with a laser

- Young man, what do you do?
- I am a 3d printer journalist.
- And what are these?
- From now on there. I am the first.

Recently, the Koreans announced a 3d printer with incredible possibilities, namely: accuracy of 25 microns, printing 50 materials (plastic, nylon, bronze, chocolate, silver, ceramics, wood, etc.), printing at a speed of 1000 mm per second, laser engraver.



The first printer flew from Vladivostok to Moscow by air delivery and I managed to take a few photos of the appearance of the device. I did not have time to test for speed and various materials. I hope that in the next review I will provide a report on the speed of printing and how the printing takes place with various materials in my hands, and not Korean ones.
