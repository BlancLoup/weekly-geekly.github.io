5 days before the MBLTdev mobile developers conference

Within a few days, MBLTDev , the second international conference of mobile developers, will be held in Digital October in Moscow. The conference program will be divided into three streams: technical, business track and track with seminars.



The reports of the technical track will cover various aspects of iOS, Android and Windows development. Innovative methods for testing applications, the magic of fast data synchronization between various platforms, the solution to using VIPER, the evolution of GPS in mobile products, automotive information security, development on Swift and for AppleTV - this is what speakers from Wunderlist, Runkeeper, Spotify, Tinkoff, and Rambler & Co, Parallels, Microsoft and other companies.

The business track will be dedicated to those companies that have managed to build a whole ecosystem around their products. Speakers from such companies as Kaspersky Lab, Aviasales, Meduza, Sports.ru, Tinkoff Bank, STS Media and others will share their experience.


The third track will host seminars for developers on virtual reality and creating cross-platform applications from leading Microsoft experts. Pre-registration is required, details will be sent to all participants!

In addition to reports in the halls of Digital Ocotober, all participants are waiting for activity on Microsoft stands, reLeadgion, Kaspersky - the world of virtual reality, room-quest, light installations, IoT puzzles. Also on the stage of the Main Hall, our partner Avito will arrange a raffle of valuable prizes for iOS and Android developers.

The full program is published on the official conference website . Ticket price - 7500 rubles. When buying 2 or more tickets, a 30% discount is valid.

See you on November 17th!

Conference organizers: e-Legion and RAEC
General Partner: Microsoft Russia
Partners: Kaspersky Lab, Avito.ru, Rambler & Co, reLeadgion
