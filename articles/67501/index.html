Naga: 17 buttons on one mouse

Razer has released a 17-button mouse under the "talking" name Naga , intended (so decided in the marketing service of the manufacturer) for MMO games.


In addition to the already familiar 5 buttons, the device is equipped with a 12-button module on the left side. Actually, this is the whole point: a lot of buttons that can be reassigned as you like. Fans of WoW and other online entertainment should be satisfied.

Naga will cost $ 80. According to the manufacturer, the mouse will be on sale soon.
