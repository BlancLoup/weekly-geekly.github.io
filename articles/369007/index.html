Don't worry, BitHappy - talk about the first bitcoin browser

The main objective of the world's first browser for Bitcoin is to simplify the process of online trading in Bitcoins as much as possible and differs significantly from the existing trading platforms with a friendly and intuitive interface.


BitHappy combined online trading zone and payment bitcoin service on one platform

The new e-commerce platform, BitHappy , will allow online merchants and retailers to easily sell, and users - to buy goods and services for bitcoins - with the help of an application that the developers themselves have dubbed "the first Bitcoin browser."

For sellers, BitHappy will become a marketplace where you can place an ad for the sale of goods - with a photo, description and price in local currency. The platform automatically converts the specified amount to a bitcoin equivalent. BitHappy can also be used by sellers of offline stores who agree to receive payment in cryptocurrency. To sell goods for Bitcoins, the seller will simply need to scan his barcode. The system independently converts the price into bitcoins, publishes the goods on the site, and also generates an identification number of the goods and sends the invoice to the buyer to pay for the purchase in bitcoins.

BitHappy buyers will be able to easily search for nearby stores that accept cryptocurrency for payment. BitHappy is directly connected with Mycelium e-wallet service, so that buyers can pay for purchases in Bitcoins in one click.

The capabilities of BitHappy can also be realized for non-commercial purposes: using this service, you can collect Bitcoin donations, or organize fund-raising bitcoin campaigns.

According to the creators of the service, the BitHappy platform is aimed at people who are not covered by banking services - especially individual entrepreneurs who would like to trade online, but not spend time and money on searching for premises and issuing banking documents. With BitHappy, anyone can post a photo on the site, describe the product and name its price.

The creators of Bitcoin applications do not intend to charge a commission for purchases made via BitHappy.

BitHappy application will appear on the market in December 2015.
