Athlon II X4: the cheapest quad

AMD has already celebrated interesting low-end products this year. For example, an inexpensive and high-tech dual-core Athlon II X2. Now we are talking about the four cores and the launch of the Athlon II X4 series.

At first glance, there is nothing special about the Athlon II X2. It is not hidden from close attention that the novelty is the same Phenom II, but without a third-level cache. Nevertheless, the 45-nm process technology, compatibility with the new AMD 785G chipset and support for the 4000 MHz HyperTransport interface are on the standings.

The Athlon II X4 line includes two chips so far - AMD Athlon II X4 620 and 630 with a frequency of 2.6 and 2.8 GHz, respectively. The first is less than $ 100, the second is a bit more expensive - $ 112.
