2014 through the eyes of Isaac Asimov: predictions from 1964

However, at that time there lived and worked a great (not afraid of this word) science fiction writer, thinker, scientist, popularizer of science and just a man Isaac Asimov. In 1964, being impressed by the World's Fair, held in New York, he decided to imagine what the world would look like in half a century. This year, 2014, it is exactly 50 years of this prediction, therefore, I propose to recall those Azimov predictions that came true.
