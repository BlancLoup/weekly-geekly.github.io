Spam faces

Some time ago there was a wave of specific spam: they offered to buy extremely cheap (up to 0.50?) Shares of obscure companies with eloquent descriptions of activities and assurances that we must take and the course will clearly jump and everyone will be happy. Filters this spam on the trash can not be allowed. According to unverified data, 1 out of 100 spam recipients use the received commercial offer. Logically, the shares really grew up for a short time, then to fall again, bringing money to the organizers of the event. After some time, this type of spam has exhausted itself, despite the fact (or due to the fact) that the media have focused on a brief increase in stocks. It was about a year ago and the topic was forgotten. Here comes the fresh Spiegel (German non-yellow gloss). I open it on the page I have found - an ad to the whole page: shares of an obscure Turkish holding at 0.30? ..
Deja Vu!
