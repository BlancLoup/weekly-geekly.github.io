What are ePayService payment cards?

EPayService cards are registered debit dollar cards of MasterCard class Standard. This means that this card is suitable for paying for purchases in offline stores, as well as on the Internet. Do not be confused by the fact that the card is a dollar card - you can withdraw funds from the card worldwide in any national currency loaded into an ATM. And you can be sure that the losses during the conversion will be minimal, and the exchange rate is as close as possible to the MasterCard rate. .

1. The maximum withdrawal limit is up to 10,000 USD per day and up to 300,000 USD per month.

How to recharge ePayService card?

The ePayService card is replenished directly from a USD account in our payment system. Replenishment occurs instantly in automatic mode. There is a counter question: how to replenish the USD account in ePayService? Our system is used by professionals, not housewives. These are mainly: international IT companies, gamedev studios, affiliate programs and freelancers. Accordingly, the methods of account replenishment in ePayService are mostly highly professional:
1. Cashing a bank check in your name (in USD) or in the name of our company (in EUR, CAD, GBP).
2. Acceptance of international wire transfer Wire Transfer to the details of our company with the data of your account number in ePayService, including from third parties.
3. Acceptance of ACH / DirectDeposit from payers from the USA (Hi, Amazon!).
4. Instant replenishment of a USD-account with a WMZ-transfer through a merchant from the WebMoney Transfer system.
