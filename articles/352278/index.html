Cisco Connect

When: April 3-4
Where: Moscow, Krasnopresnenskaya emb., 12,

M-Health Congress

When: April 3
Where: Moscow, st. Poklonnaya, d. 3 A
Conditions of participation: 10 000 rubles.

Annual gathering of specialists at the intersection of two areas - medicine and IT - to discuss new products in mobile technologies and the potential of their use in the health sector.
