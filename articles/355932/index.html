Roskomnadzor began to block sites selling jamon, prosciutto and other sanctions products


Prohibited products: if you really want something ... you still can not

This month, Roskomnadzor blacklisted the site of a supplier of products for catering enterprises, hotels, and restaurants. The reason is the presence of sanctions products in the list of goods on the site It is about the company "East-West", whose case was considered in court, writes Cnews. The decision to block was taken by the Belorechensky District Court of the Krasnodar Territory.

The company itself, whose site was blocked, did not participate in the proceedings. The lawsuit was filed by the local prosecutor, who found sanctions food products on the company's website, the importation of which into the territory of Russia was banned in the summer of 2014. In particular, we are talking about meat products.
