Welcome to the second Camunda BPM Meetup Raiffeisenbank

We invite you to the second in Russia open mitap Camunda BPM , which will be held on May 30, 2019 at the Raiffeisenbank site in Nagatino.

How was the first mitap of the community Camunda BPM can be viewed in this post .

It is very important for us to form communities and share knowledge and experience both within the company and outside. That is why on a regular basis we hold open meetings in different directions.

A strong community is a cool platform for development, so we not only invite you to the meeting, but also actively invite everyone to join the Camunda BPM User Group chat. With the support of the community, it is easier and more fun to live, because then there is an opportunity to quickly ask colleagues something or just throw off an interesting article or meme.
