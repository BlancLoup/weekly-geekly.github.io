Begin courses "Universarium"

I would like to remind everyone who is interested in the development of Internet education that today, January 20, the Universarium project courses begin: Search and selection of business ideas , Chemistry is useful and useless , Graphs and combinatorics: from pearls of theory to modern applications .

On Habr√© already discussed this project. Universarium, along with the Hexlet project, is one of the first Russian MOOC platforms. Here is what the authors say about themselves:



Basic principles of the project
  • An open educational platform and the involvement of leading universities and the best teachers in the country in the project
  • Affordable and free training within the project
  • Active educational environment and the use of modern technologies and methods - video lectures, automated knowledge control, interactive homework
  • Orientation of the part of courses to specific employers with the possibility of further employment

All training will be built on the principle of passing successive modules of the educational course. The total duration of the course (study time) is 7-10 weeks depending on the intensity and complexity of the program. Each module includes video lectures, independent work, homework and testing.
