OWASP Russia Meetup - recording speeches



On April 3, with the support of Jet Infosystems, a regular meeting of the Russian branch of the OWASP community was held, at which information security experts gathered. You can watch the recordings of the speakers in the video below.

The program of performances:
Cloud Security , Evgeny Sidorov, Yandex
Advanced methods of operation XXE , Ivan Bulavin and Alexey Haidin, Informzaschita
Advanced Web Fuzzing with BurpSuite: Arkady Litvinenko, BI.ZONE



The OWASP community is actively developing and expanding its ranks. If you want to speak at the nearest meeting and share an interesting report - fill out the CFP .
