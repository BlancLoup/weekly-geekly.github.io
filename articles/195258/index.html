Humble Mobile Bundle 2



September 25, at 22:00, the second compilation of mobile games Humble Mobile Bundle was released .

All games after purchase will be available for download on Android directly from the Humble Bundle website and are DRM-free. This time the maximum savings can be up to 300 rubles.

This time it includes 4 + 2 games for the Android platform:
  • Star Command + Soundtrack
  • Time Surfer + Soundtrack
  • Punch Quest + Soundtrack (30.53 rubles on Google Play )
  • Bloons TD 5 + Soundtrack (94.26 rubles on Google Play )

For beat-the-average, which at the time of publication was equal to $ 4.14, you will receive:
  • Ravensword: Shadowlands + Soundtrack (233.87 rubles on Google Play )
  • Carmageddon (66 rubles on Google Play )
