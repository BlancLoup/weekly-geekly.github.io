3D television broadcast of the UEFA Champions League final

On May 22, at 22.45 Moscow time, the television channel NTV-Plus will host a 3D-broadcast of the UEFA Champions League Final . This is the first in the history of Russian television program in three-dimensional format, which will be transmitted to the whole of Russia, except for the April 3D ballet broadcast on an exotic satellite channel.

In April 2010, a television broadcast of the ballet of the Mariinsky Theater (organized by Platform HD, Tricolor TV, General Satellite and Samsung) was carried out in 3D, but then the signal could only be received in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Paris. In the case of "NTV-plus" technology goes to the masses.

You can watch football if you have a special 3D TV at home and active glasses with polarizing filters. These TVs are sold in stores for about 90,000 rubles.

Perhaps there is some way to make a homemade 3D-system, taking a digital signal through a TV tuner and performing polarization of the picture using software methods. Will anyone have time before Saturday? If not, then by the end of the year the problem urgently needs to be addressed.

By the end of the year, a full-fledged 3D channel of its own production may appear in the NTV-plus package, the Vedomosti newspaper reports with reference to the company's management. Similar plans were also announced by the Akado company (3D Demo Channel from Eutelsat Communications).
