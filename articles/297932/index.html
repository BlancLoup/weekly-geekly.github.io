According to the established scheme: users will have to pay for free listening to Apple iTunes Radio

Apple iTunes Radio will be paid from January 28th. This company reported to users by e-mail. After January 28, only those users who have subscribed to Apple Music can access iTunes Radio . The subscription price for this streaming service in the US is $ 9.99 per month. The cost of an individual subscription in Russia costs 169 rubles, and a family subscription costs 269 rubles.

But Apple still has one more free radio - Beats 1. This is a 24-hour music radio station with DJs and exclusive premieres.

Since 2013, iTunes Radio has been offering free access to two hundred radio stations and a catalog of iTunes music tracks. The lack of a subscription was compensated by the presence of embedded advertising in the service. iTunes Radio allows iPhone, iPad and Mac owners to listen to radio stations broadcasting music of various genres, add new stations and so on. The user can purchase a song being played in the iTunes Store by pressing a special button.

After the merger of iTunes Radio with Apple Music, the online radio Pandora will be one less competitor. But the competition with Pandora has probably ceased to have any meaning for Apple.
