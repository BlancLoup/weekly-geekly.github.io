From October 22, the operator Tele2 launches communication services for residents of Moscow

In addition to the "big three" of operators in Moscow on October 22 will be the fourth mobile operator Tele2 .
The Federal Communications Agency ( Rossvyaz ) allocated Tele2 7.5 million numbers in the 977 code (in the range from 250-00-00 to 999-99-99).

A month before the start of sales, the certification program starts: special paper cards that the operator will hand out give the right to connect to Tele2 on preferential terms. Each month for a certain time, the subscriber will be charged the amount that he can spend on the payment of communication services.

In the second quarter of 2015, the revenue of T2 RTK Holding increased by 7.1% year-on-year and amounted to 23.6 billion rubles. EBITDA reached 7.3 billion rubles. A year earlier, this figure was 6.7 billion rubles.

To enter the Moscow market, Tele2 in February 2015 received from one of its shareholders, VTB , a preferential loan of 16 billion rubles. In the development of 3G / 4G networks in Moscow, the operator invests its own funds.
