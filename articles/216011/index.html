Screen and control

Touch screen Below it are three touch buttons in the android style - Back, Home and Menu.
On the side - a round silver button, similar to the ones that Sony uses in its smartphones. The first press turns on the backlight, the second one goes to the program list, the third one returns to the standby mode. In the list of programs, the movement occurs with right-to-left swipe.


In standby mode, the backlight is turned off, the selected dial is displayed, the screen does not react to anything.
The screen at the watch is transflective, therefore, with the backlight turned off with external illumination, the image is perfectly visible. And the brighter it is, the better it can be seen. The disadvantage of this screen is the graininess of the image.

Working hours

With my usual mode of use, the clock works for about five to six days, if not turned off for the night. Of course, not two years, but once a week I am ready to charge my watch. Although, I think it can be averaged that the clock works for four days.
But on the phone work with the clock affects more seriously. If I usually had a battery for 10-15 percent per day, then with the connected clock, 30-35% of the charge flies away per day. Not deadly, I still put the phone in the cradle every evening, but I must take it into account. Although the external battery is still in your pocket anyway, it will feed the phone and the watch - the benefit of the charging connector is the same.
