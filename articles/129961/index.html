Ciklum Java Saturday (15.10) - Informal mini-conference of Java developers in Minsk

In our offices, both throughout Ukraine and in Belarus , Ciklum Saturdays is constantly being held, gathering developers for various technologies.

October 15, we, together with Exigen Services, invite developers to our Minsk office at Ciklum Saturday, dedicated to Java development . This Ciklum Subbotnik , like the previous ones , will be held in the format of an informal mini-conference.


According to the tradition of our Subbotnik, participants of the event will be able to informally communicate, exchange knowledge and share experience not only during presentations on technology topics on software development, but also throughout the event. Coffee breaks with treats, as well as a photo session in the new photo studio are attached :)

Preliminary list of reports :
  • Vladimir Gorshunov will give a talk on "Introduction to Google App Engine"
  • Andrei Marmysh will talk about the experience of developing Java applications on GWT2.3
  • Alex Kruk will share the features of development on the VMForce platform
  • Igor Hotin (a special guest from Kiev) will talk about Gradle - a new generation java-tool
  • Andrey Lumyansky will highlight business processes in java-projects in the context of BPM

And, as always, at the end of all we invite you to join the after-party in the beer restaurant ‚ÄúThe Old Channel‚Äù, where the most persistent will be able to spend a Saturday evening together for heated discussions.

A direct online broadcast is planned, so guests from other cities, as well as the laziest Minsk residents, will be able to attend the event virtually and ask questions via social networks, follow the updates on twitter via hash tag #CiklumJava

Participation in Ciklum Subbotnik as always free of charge, subject to prior registration .

Attention! Registration is open only until October 13 - hurry, the number of places is limited!

PS
A detailed schedule of reports, as well as the final list of speakers, we will send closer to the event in a personal e-mail newsletter for registered participants.

We promise that the subbotnik will be a drive, interesting and useful for all participants who not only listen to the reports, but also take an active part in the discussion. And the most active ones will leave not only with gifts from the companies Ciklum and Exigen Services, but also get their own portfolio!
