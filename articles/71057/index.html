Webbook: Chinese DVD DVD

Imore introduced a 10.1-inch Webbook, whose main feature is the presence of a DVD-drive, despite its size.


The webbook is built on the Intel platform (945GSE logic with integrated graphics controller, Atom N270 1.6 GHz processor). Onboard 1 GB of the RAM, 160 GB HDD. The display resolution is 1024x600 pixels. There is a Wi-Fi controller, a card reader, a webcam with 1.3 MP, two USB 2.0 ports. Webbook dimensions - 260x193x31 mm and weight 1.5 kg. The whole thing is running Linux.

How much the device will cost is not yet known. But I think the Chinese will not break too much for him.
