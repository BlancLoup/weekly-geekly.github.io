Bring robots to YaC 2012

This year, an exhibition of robotics will be held at the Yet Another Conference in the interactive lobby area.

We invite everyone to take part in it!

At this time, you can not only see other robots, but also your own show.

We welcome any manifestation of your imagination:

  • unusual materials
  • specific abilities
  • transformation capabilities.

Applications are accepted until September 20 at ya-events@yandex.ru.

Please indicate in the application:
  • Name (of course);
  • a brief description of the robot - the size of what can, what is their name, are there any special features;
  • Do you need a socket?

Invited inventors will show their work at the conference, as well as receive a small gift from Yandex!

Team Yandex.Events,
