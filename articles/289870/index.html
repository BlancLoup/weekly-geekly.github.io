Etsy announces support for Apple Pay

After a recent IPO , Etsy announces Apple Pay support in its application for US users.

The company claims that more than half of its visits to the store come from mobile devices. Thus, the company plans to significantly improve the usability of the platform, expanding the possibilities of payment.

This, insignificant at first glance, addition, for reasons of the company's specialists, can seriously affect sales growth.

Etsy successfully debuted at Nasdaq last week. The initial $ 14 per share public offering quickly increased to $ 30, and now the shares are trading at $ 25.75 as of today's closing market.
