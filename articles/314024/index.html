Huawei Hosting Platform

In 2016, the Russian hosting provider RUVDS , which provides virtual server services ( VPS / VDS ), began cooperation with Huawei, a major supplier of communication solutions and hardware infrastructure for modern data centers. In particular, as part of a joint project, Huawei equips the data center RUVDS, located in the Moscow region, with its equipment. This guarantees a faster and more stable operation of virtual servers, allows us to offer our customers even better and more modern services. In addition, these reliable and cost-effective server platforms allow you to optimize the cost of services provided to customers.


Huawei is more widely known in our country as a supplier of network and telecommunications equipment. Solutions from Huawei are used by companies working with telecommunications and Internet technologies in more than 150 countries around the world. However, it is also a major manufacturer of servers that it has been developing for over 14 years. All Huawei Enterprise server solutions are the company's own development.

The distinguishing features of Huawei Enterprise servers are high performance, ease of management and operation, as well as high reliability required for the telecommunications industry. This powerful and inexpensive equipment aimed at real-world tasks. Huawei Enterprise servers are popular with service providers (ISP). And this is not surprising, because the servers of this manufacturer have already proven themselves well in the process of operation in the data center of the Chinese search engine Baidu. The company is one of the top 5 x86 server providers in China.


Currently, Huawei offers a full line of servers on Intel x86 processors.

RH-series servers are standard rack-mount servers in a 1U \ 2U \ 4U \ 8U package with support from 2 to 8 Intel Xeon processors. This equipment helps customers get high performance while reducing costs in any area - from business-critical applications, virtualization and cloud services to high-performance computing. These servers provide high storage density, high performance, reliability and scalability.

Huawei RH2288 servers are among the first systems in the industry with SSD accelerators certified by the Microsoft SQL Server 2012 Fast Track Data Warehouse program. A similar hardware solution is used as a platform for Microsoft Exchange.

Obviously, Huawei will try to actively promote its products to the "external" market, that it is ready for favorable price terms. The quality of the products is at a fairly high level, and the equipment boasts an excellent price / quality ratio.

Taking into account discounts, Huawei Enterprise servers are on average cheaper than those of competitors, and in addition, the vendor invests impressive funds in research and development. The company pays great attention to the development of new technologies. This makes Huawei products increasingly recognizable in the server market.


The lineup of Huawei rack servers.

It is not by chance that Huawei RH1288 V3 servers were one of the company's solutions chosen for equipping the RUVDS data center. We will get acquainted with them in this short review.

Workhorse: RH1288 V3


Huawei RH1288 V3 is an inexpensive two-socket server in a 1U chassis for rack mounting. The developers have tried to achieve in it a balance between performance and density of the layout due to the optimal design. It is a good choice for enterprise computing-intensive applications, workloads that require both computing power and data storage capacity, virtualization systems, and cloud applications.


Servers Huawei RH1288 V3 - the right solution for applications with high demands on computing resources.

These servers support up to two Intel Xeon E5-2600 v3 processors (up to 14 cores each, 35 MB L3 cache and QuickPath Interconnect (QPI) 9.6 Gt / s), up to 16 DDR4 DIMM 2133 MHz allow you to get the maximum operational capacity memory up to 1 TB. Using Power Supplies (PSU) 80 Plus Platinum provides efficient power consumption. Moreover, depending on the server equipment, PSUs with 460, 750, 800 and 1200 W are supported. For reliability, PSUs are reserved (1 + 1).


Typical use of Huawei FusionServer RH1288 V3: database server (50-100 users), terminal server, application server, cluster computing node. Among the interesting features worth noting is the system of quality monitoring of energy consumption and rapid management of the system.


The Huawei RH1288 V3 server is a worthy representative among competitors, to which you can also add the Lenovo X3550.

The server provides for dynamic energy management. For temperature monitoring in real time, temperature sensors are embedded in the key components of the server. And the use of cooling modules with feedback increases the efficiency of energy consumption and cooling of the system.

Remote server management is performed using the iBMC module, which, in general, is not functionally different from competing products - iDrag or iLo. Independent iBMC control module implements the functions Serial over LAN (SOL), remote KVM and server remote on / off functions. This server also provides a wide range of management interfaces, including IPMI, CLI, HTTPS, SNMP and WSMAN, which facilitates integration with systems from other manufacturers.

An error code is displayed on the front panel for accurate localization of problems in the event of a server failure.

The logical architecture of the server Huawei RH1288 V3.

Servers Huawei RH1288 V3 can be equipped with solid-state drives. Currently, the vast majority of hosting providers in the world use servers with HDD, since this is the cheapest option, disk drives have a large capacity, are easily configured to work with servers, do not require special hardware.

However, modern SSD SSDs provide a good combination of low cost and high performance for data centers and provide significant gains in speed and reduced delays compared to traditional HDD drives used in the data center. Equipped with flash drives, servers and storage systems today can solve the most demanding tasks, such as searching in large databases, online transaction processing, business analytics and large-scale virtualization in cloud computing.

In the configuration of the Huawei RH1288 V3 with SSD for connecting solid-state drives is not using PCIe, and the NVMe interface. Interestingly, the model with NVMe supports several types of drives: up to four HDD SAS or SATA and up to four NVMe SSD, instead of which can be used the same HDD SAS or SATA. There is a lot of room for different variants of tiring - multilevel storage of hot / cold data - or caching.
