Android Market Update

The blog of the developers of the Android platform has a message about updating the Android Market.

The update is as logical as expected - finally, application developers will be allowed to show screenshots of their applications. Let's hope also increase the number of characters in the description of the application. Also added four new categories - sports, health, themes, and comics.

And probably guided by the saying "it is better to see once than hear a hundred times" posted a video.



Which, among other things, pleases with a more obvious separation of paid applications and free ones. At the current moment, in order to divide the output, select the Change View item in the menu and then select one of the modes there. In my opinion it is not very obvious and even difficult action. Moreover, it has to be done every time when the Android Market is loaded into memory.

Along the way, they also reported that the placement of paid applications is available to developers from Italy. The turn of Russia I hope not far off.
