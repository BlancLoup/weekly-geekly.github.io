Samorost and all-all-all



Who knows and loves the games of the Samorost series, I mentally shake my hand. Wonderful atmosphere Flash-toys, stunning in design and implementation. If anyone has not heard of these Flash quests , I strongly recommend playing both the first Samorost and its continuation - Samorost 2 .

Not long ago, I wondered if the third installment of this wonderful series would come soon. It turned out that not yet - the authors of the game, the studio Amanita Design , are now working on a full-fledged, as they put it, adventure game Machinarium , which is scheduled for release in the first half of 2009. Judging by the screenshots, this toy should be a great example of drawn quests, which, due to the massive 3D-site, almost disappeared into oblivion (honor the memory of two-dimensional Petka and Vasily Ivanovich saving the Galaxy and the painted Pilot brothers).
