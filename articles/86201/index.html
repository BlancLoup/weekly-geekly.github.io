Mozilla firefox


According to market analysts, today Firefox is the most popular machine in the world. It is economical, relatively safe and has good speed characteristics. However, all these advantages are peculiar only to the basic configuration of Firefox.

Unfortunately, it only includes the chassis, body, engine and wheels. The steering wheel, seats, wind, front and side windows, as well as headlights and gearbox must be purchased separately - all this comes in the form of extensions. If you need them, choose them carefully: some parts may not be compatible with different basic versions of the car.

It should be borne in mind that after installing the extensions, Firefox will become more comfortable and spectacular, but it will add significantly in weight, which means you can forget about economy and speed. 80% of Firefox users use the basic version without extensions.

Microsoft Internet Explorer


This car just until recently was the most popular. Why did he surrender to Firefox? Because it is difficult and unsafe to drive a two-wheeled car with an engine in the cabin, seats on the roof and a wheel from the bike. Especially without a seat belt. Especially for older people. However, they remain the main users of the IE car. Just because they do not know about alternatives.

Some IE drivers fainted when they learned that there were 4-wheel alternative cars in the world with comfortable salons and powerful engines, with a more friendly round steering wheel. Despite the continuous improvement of the system of protection against hacking, IE remains the most hijacked car according to traffic police statistics. Today, most of these cars are sold as part of social packages for corporate employees who cannot change them for anything else.

A few months ago, the manufacturer publicly announced the release of a new, perfect and unique car - now with three wheels. In addition, thanks to the leak, it became known that in future versions of IE, the bicycle handlebar may be replaced by a helicopter arm!

Apple safari


Comfortable car with a beautiful design, high-speed performance and opportunities for driving over rough terrain. In order to experience the benefits of this car, you also need a home, a garage, clothes and pets from the same manufacturer. Well, if you are a perfectionist and want to get the best result, you will have to move to the Apple zone.

This is a region with an administrative center in Cupertino, with streets and avenues, parking lots and traffic lights specially designed for the needs of residents, which are significantly different from those that are accepted in the rest of the world and are more convenient.

Also keep in mind: if you decide to repaint your Safari-car to a new color without permission from the authorities of Apple zone, tune in to a radio station broadcasting from a neighboring area, or put on unapproved bus administration, you will have to pay a substantial fine and possibly leave the zone.

Opera


The only representative of the European auto business in the top five largest automakers.

The latest version of Auto Opera is declared as the fastest on the market, and indeed in most tests it is ahead of even Google Chrome race cars.

However, builders of highways, intersections, bridges and tunnels sometimes experience problems with the compatibility of Opera cars - periodically their dimensions go beyond the pavement (however, the manufacturer himself declares that he manufactures his cars according to all auto standards).

Opera machines are supplied only in the maximum configuration. Even the basic model is equipped with heated seats, power accessories, GPS-navigator, barbecue, a small fridge with Norwegian beer in the trunk and salty crackers in the glove compartment.

However, you will not be able to independently purchase additional options, such as a jacuzzi with built-in girls or billiards. You need to send a wish to the manufacturer and expect a result. Perhaps the manufacturer sells what you need in the next versions of the car and maybe not.
