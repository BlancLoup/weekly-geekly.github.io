Seminar "Clouds and reality: cases, rakes, good news", July 13, St. Petersburg



Hi, Habr! We are preparing to launch a new course for DataLine Universities. This time, about our knowledge of cloud affairs. The first seminar will be held in St. Petersburg on July 13 - for us this is a double premiere)

At the introductory seminar of the course we will tell about the IaaS practice in Russia and in the world, share life hacking in the field of migration and talk about the cloud of the future.

We have only a few places left, and we will be happy to see CIOs, engineers, and all those involved in servicing the IT infrastructure.

What shall we talk about:

  • To whom it is good to live in the clouds, and to whom it is not very: Russian and global IaaS practice in the context of locating business-critical systems and IT services.
  • Cloud migration: common mistakes and lifehacks.
  • Cloud of tomorrow: our view on key trends in the development of technology and service in general.

After the seminar for all participants a delicious buffet.
