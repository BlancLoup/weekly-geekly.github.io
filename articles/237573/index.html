Model S cars can now be given personal names.



The company Tesla Motors has released another update of the software Model S. In the coming days, Software v6.0 will go to electric sedans.

One of the new features: now the owner of the car Model S can assign its official name to your pet. This name will be displayed in the mobile application on the smartphone and on the screen of the car computer, in the section ‚ÄúAbout Your Tesla‚Äù.

Since 2012, Tesla Motors has repeatedly upgraded the car. Among other things, added a mode of "smart" suspension, help system when moving uphill, sleep mode and much more.

Here are some new features in firmware 6.0.

Tips for navigating with traffic and detours. Tesla Model S begins to monitor the situation on the roads even before the driver got behind the wheel. And if he drives along the traditional route, which is suddenly closed today for repair or because of traffic jams, then immediately after switching on the car will advise an alternative route - a pop-up window will appear on the 17-inch panel. Accordingly, laying routes to unfamiliar places now also takes into account information about traffic jams.

Night power saving mode and the ability to remotely connect from a smartphone so that the car does not come out of sleep mode.

Start the car from the smartphone. If the driver has forgotten the car keychain ("ignition key"), then he can now turn on the car by entering the password in the mobile application.

Diary: scheduled events from the diary on the smartphone are displayed on the car screen. If you specify the place of the event on the smartphone, then you do not need to re-enter the coordinates into the navigation system.

Adjusting the air suspension according to the memory of the past actions of the driver on a particular section of the road (by coordinates). The function is relevant for those cars that are equipped, in fact, with this mechanism. For example, if the driver increased the clearance of the car every time he approaches the house in order to overcome the obstacle, the Model S will now do it automatically.

PS It's nice that modern cars are updated as programs on the computer. A car with some basic firmware for a few years upgrade can be gradually upgraded to a business class car (just kidding).
