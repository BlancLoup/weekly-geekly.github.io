“Read”, “Looked”, “Listened” and “Want” - new Facebook buttons

On Thursday, September 22, there will be a f8 developer conference, where Facebook is going to present some important news. One of the leading developers said that this would be “the most important or second most important f8 for the entire duration of such conferences.” It is not yet known exactly what will be presented, but TechCrunch ’all-knowing journalists already have suggestions .

Informed sources say that the concept of the Like button will be announced at f8. The well-known Facebook symbol will be added / replaced with more neutral Read , Listened and Watched buttons, which will correspond to the type of user content (text, audio, video), and in the future they will join the ( Want ) button for social commerce.

The “Want” button will open up new monetization opportunities in front of Facebook, and give users a convenient way to shop.

Thus, the interface of social networks continues to degrade and improve, in order to better fit the tastes of users, who on Facebook have almost a billion people (this is an active audience).
