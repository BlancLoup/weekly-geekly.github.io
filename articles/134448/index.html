Imagine Cup Meeting in Tomsk



Microsoft's partner students invite all undergraduate and graduate students in Tomsk to take part in the Imagine Cup Meeting, an event dedicated to preparing for the global Imagine Cup competition.

We are waiting for students of all courses, boys and girls, technicians and humanities. If you have an active life position, and you want:
  • broaden your horizons;
  • chat with interesting people;
  • do something meaningful for our planet;
  • go to Moscow, and then immediately to Australia;

and you
  • I have never heard of the Imagine Cup, but there is a desire to do something global;
  • you know about the Imagine Cup for a long time, but you are not confident in your abilities;
  • You already have a project, but you do not know how to develop it;
  • you have a komad, but no idea;
  • You know how to save the world, but you need people to help;

then come to the Imagine Cup Meeting !

What's waiting for you


  • we will share with you the experience of participating in the competition;
  • show you how to program under Windows, Web, Cloud and Windows Phone 7;
  • let's tell what the judges pay attention to;
  • show the reasons for the success of some projects and the failure of others;
  • We will show you how to improve your project and what to do next.


Especially for you, we invited the winners of the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2011 and the participants of the international final in New York - the Oricrafter team. They will share their experience with you. Also at the event will be present the finalists of the regional stages of the past years.

Preliminary registration on a site is necessary (you do not want, that you did not get buns?).

Venue: Tomsk, Lenin Ave., 30, aud. 234 (Main building of Tomsk Polytechnic University).

Time of the meeting: December 16, 2011, 16: 00-20: 00.

The event is supported by the Microsoft Innovation Center at TPU.
