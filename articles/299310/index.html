Startup conference and networking in Silicon Valley

A large number of start-up conferences and other events take place every day in Silicon Valley. It is useful to visit any startup manager. The main problem is the right choice of visiting startup events in Silicon Valley and the San Francisco Bay area.

We visited, took part and sometimes even helped to co-organize many start-up events, and we want to tell about some of them. Networking plays a huge role in the startup culture of Silicon Valley - it is communication and familiarity with the circle of people who can help you quickly and efficiently solve complex problems that are often found in the startup world. In Silicon Valley, it is at conferences that it is customary to meet and network; therefore, many entrepreneurs come to the Valley for networking.

We have reviewed the main startup events in Silicon Valley:

  • Lean Startup Conference
  • Tech crunch
  • Collision Conference
  • Silicon Valley Innovation Entrepreneurship Forum (SVIEF)
  • Global Mobile Internet Conference (GMIC)
  • Silicon Valley Open Doors (SVOD)
  • Launch festival
  • Startups Showcase
  • Startup Weekend
