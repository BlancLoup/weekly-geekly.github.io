New generation iphone


Now you are looking at a new generation iPhone. He was found in a bar in Redwood City in Silicon Valley, disguised as an iPhone 3GS.

As long as Apple thinks about the final version, we can see what is new for sure:
  • Front camera
  • Improved main camera
  • Flash
  • Micro-SIM instead of standard
  • Improved display
  • Additional microphone to eliminate interference
  • Separate keys to adjust the sound
  • The power and sound keys are now metallic


What changed:
  • The back cover is flat and made of plastic or ceramic.
  • There was an aluminum side running around the phone
  • Forms have become more square
  • Weight increased by 3 grams
  • 16% more battery
  • All internal components are compactly integrated to provide a larger battery area.
