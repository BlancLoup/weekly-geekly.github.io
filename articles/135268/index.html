GoDaddy director refuses to revoke signature in support of SOPA

GoDaddy this week has been boycotted by the Internet community for supporting the SOPA bill. Site owners bulk transfer domains to other registrars.

According to the DailyChanges monitoring service, the number of domains on GoDaddy servers (ns.domaincontrol.com) has decreased by a few thousand each day in the last week. With a total of more than 32 million, this is not very irrelevant, but the management is still alarmed.
