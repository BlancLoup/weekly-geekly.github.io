Zip-quest about space admin Vasily Romanov



Hello!
I bungled a zip quest for the sysadmin day. Here take the archive, and forward.

What you need to know:
  • You play as a space administrator, who for the first time departed beyond the limits of the Earth's orbit. He is you, only smaller and different.
  • Each level is a gift, a password can be obtained from it, using information from previous levels.
  • Since we have Russian space, passwords can contain Russian characters, even code snippets with comments, every trash, and be case-sensitive.
  • Even on the Russian space station, traditional keyboards, mice, screens, etc. remained about the same as in the late 90s, that is, in English. The same applies to some other household items.
  • There are no tasks for pure bruteforce, but there are a couple of times (and this is almost clearly indicated) for selection according to limited sets of dictionaries, which we have to go through for a long time.
  • In addition, at the lower levels there are a couple of tasks that will require the collective work of habra users. Quest takes into account that you can change the information in the comments here.
  • If you opened part of the plot and want to put in the comments - under the spoiler, please. Do not forget that the spoiler is not available for any value of karma, so be sure to make a preview.
  • The game may end abruptly. I warned.
