IBM poll: analytics for managing business processes in your company



IBM has repeatedly published articles on the management of business processes and tools for working with analytical tools that provide an objective picture of the development of the company.

Now IBM is working on collecting information for a full-fledged research (annual) IBM Institute for Business Value. This year, the corporation pays special attention to the tools needed to convert big data (Big Data) into structured information, giving any company a competitive advantage. Who owns the information - he owns the world. Well, or at least your business sector.

The study is designed to understand how various organizations and their departments use data and analytics to manage business processes. To this end, IBM conducts a survey among representatives of various companies and organizations around the world, and invites members of the community at Habrahabr to express their opinion by taking part in an online survey that takes no more than 20 minutes.

A study will prepare a 2014 IBM Institute for Business Value Survey report. All survey participants, if desired, may be the first to receive a copy of the IBM report.
