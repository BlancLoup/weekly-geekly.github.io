A project is launched for iOS developers Digest MBLTdev

e-Legion is the organizer of various industry events. A large niche is occupied by educational programs for mobile developers: the MBLTdev international conference, the free iOS, Android and WP developers' school, the Apple Developers Community meetings.

Today our new project for iOS developers Digest MBLTdev is launched .
During the week, our top developers Sasha Cherny and Ruslan Humenny will collect the most interesting and useful links to the latest articles and blog entries from the world Internet. On Friday, all members who subscribe to the site are sent an email with the collected information. All information is also published on the project website . The first release is already there!


We were inspired by the success of a similar project abroad and decided that there must be the same in Russia. We will try to cover all useful materials as much as possible and we will be happy if you take an active part in the formation of the newsletter! You can send links to interesting pages at digest@mbltdev.ru. After moderation, they will be included in the weekly newsletter.
