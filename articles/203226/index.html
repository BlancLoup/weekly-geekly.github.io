Nimbus Clipper - Android version

Not so long ago, we presented our clippers for Chrome and Firefox desktop browsers (those who have not read, can read this link ). In order not to pull, I will immediately describe the main features of the new application. So, Nimbus Clipper for Android can:

  • Save as entire web pages, as well as individual fragments
  • Save only articles without ads and unnecessary links (ala Instapaper or Readabily)
  • Create screenshots of web pages
  • Edit photos or screenshots at your discretion, including cropping, blocking and supplying various graphic elements.
  • Quickly send all of the above to Nimbus and receive a link for sharing




If details are interesting to you, then I ask under kat.



Save the necessary information

Nowadays, a smartphone or tablet is the most faithful companion and first assistant, because it helps to be in touch and work 24 hours a day. Our new application will help not to waste a single minute of free time. While on the road, you can easily search and save any information from sites without any extra advertising and graphics, including the necessary fragments of text or article.

To do this, open the Web Clipper item in the application and open the desired site. Then you can save the entire page (Save Full Page) or only the article (Save Article). If you need a specific fragment, then make a long tap on the desired fragment and use the + buttons to adjust its size. Before sending you can see what happened in the preview window. After the note is created, you can get its direct URL and share with whom you think is necessary :)



Sometimes it's easier to draw than to explain in words ...

With all the variety of words in Russian, it is sometimes difficult to explain fairly simple things. Trying to describe your wishes to the designer, programmer or builder, you can lose a lot of time. There is no guarantee that they will understand you correctly. It is much easier to make comments and notes directly in the picture. This will also help you our application.
Having made a photo or a screenshot, you can quickly make the necessary improvements in a convenient editor, where there are not many functions, but they are all necessary. This and various elements, such as: arrow, pencil, line and text. There are two types of blur (for example, to hide a certain element or, on the contrary, to hide everything except a certain piece of the picture). If you need to crop the image, then you can do it with the help of cropping.



The finished image can be saved to an SD card or sent to Nimbus, after which you can get a direct link to the finished note.
