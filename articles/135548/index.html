New Year Gift from DevExpress

The New Year is coming and it seems that everyone already has a pre-holiday or holiday mood in anticipation of the upcoming fun and receiving gifts :-)

For those programmers who use the .NET platform, DevExpress together with Microsoft also prepared a small gift - a free Map Control for working with maps in WPF applications.

Here is a small video demonstrating this control in action:



Among the features of our free WPF Map Control, I would like to note the following:
- work with the main providers of maps, such as Bing Maps and OpenStreetMap;
- built-in navigation elements for end users;
- support for loading shapes from the Shapefile format, as well as the ability to draw your own shapes;
- drawing several layers on top of each other;
- tooltips and much more ...
