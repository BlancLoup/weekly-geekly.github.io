The winner of the competition "100 000 for a T-shirt" becomes ...

A little more than a month ago, we launched the Evernote T-shirt Design Contest . In a month, we published 730 papers (they sent us even more papers, but not all met the formal requirements of the competition). You can imagine how difficult it was for us to decide on the winner.

With the vote, we decided to do this: first, several employees from our company chose the three works he liked. As a result, the first round of candidates for victory was determined - 20 works .

Then we gave each Evernote employee the opportunity to vote for one job from this list.

As a result, the winner was determined, it was the work of EverSphinx :
