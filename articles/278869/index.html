We are looking for you to help you earn $ 5000 this summer

We want to share the good news with everyone!

5 years after the last time, the ReactOS project was again accepted into the Google Summer of Code summer internship program . And that means what? This means that we are looking for all those who with our help will be able to earn 5 thousand dollars this summer.



So. We are looking for programmers (undergraduate and graduate students, full-time and part-time students, as well as recent graduates) for the summer internship program Google Summer Code 2016.
The internship is paid in the amount of US $ 5,000 to each participant upon successful implementation of the plan. Participation is remote, schedule is free, can be combined with anything, subject to the implementation of the plan.

We are extremely interested in improving the material situation of the maximum possible number of participants.

Requirements:
  • Programming experience in C or C ++ or assembler from 1 year
  • The presence of at least one work in the portfolio (even a course project or a thesis project may be appropriate)
  • Knowledge of English at a level not lower than intermediate (as an alternative, suitable French , German or Spanish at an advanced level)
  • Willingness to work on the result.


Deadline application - March 24, 2016. But at the last moment it will be too late. Get in touch right now!
