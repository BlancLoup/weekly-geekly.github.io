Gimao.com - general view of the service

I would like to present to the general public a wonderful service, to the creation of which I have a relationship.

We provide a Group Information Management (GIM) service.
The company is located in Israel, the board of directors includes some famous people from the IT industry.
The meaning of the service is very simple in Russian: you download a ICQ-like client on your computer, through it you create a group / community, in which there is not just a messenger and further blog, but also a convenient common PIM Database with a tree structure.
In this database, you can store absolutely any data, and for this you do not need to be any programmer - the interface is extremely user friendly and does not assume any tables, keys, and so on.
All members of your group can, together with their permissions, jointly manage this Community PIM Database.
