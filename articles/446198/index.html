It's no secret that to completely and permanently delete information from digital media, simply select a file and press the del key or use the rm command is not enough. "Traditional" way. Coercive force


This method is simple as an ax, and also reliable. To completely and permanently erase all data, it is necessary to create a magnetic field of such intensity at which the ferromagnet will simply demagnetize.

The value of magnetic field strength necessary for complete demagnetization of a ferromagnet is called the coercive force.

But this is for the most inquisitive, and we will continue.

So, what do we need to use this method?

We already have a ferromagnet - this is your favorite disk with very important data, or rather a thin layer of chromium dioxide deposited on pancake plates. It is this thin layer that is responsible for storing information that should not fall into the hands of rogues and enemies of the people.

It remains the case for small - to get a magnetic field of the desired intensity.

The magnetic field created by the wire with current is directly proportional to the current flowing in the conductor. Therefore, in order to get more current, demagnetizing a hard disk will require more voltage and a wire with a larger section.

Although the domains of the ferromagnet are relatively lazy, a short current pulse should be enough to demagnetize the disk. Therefore, for the accumulation of the required amount of energy is quite suitable battery capacitors. Capacitors are connected in parallel to increase capacity. And for simplicity of construction, we include them directly into the network through a diode bridge. Perhaps not the most elegant solution, but very simple. On the other hand to the capacitors solder wire, entwined around the hard drive. And do not forget about the power button.
