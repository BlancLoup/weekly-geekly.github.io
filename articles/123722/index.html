Black BlackBerry Bold 9900 can be bought from September 15, white - in a month

The company Research In Motion (RIM) decided to postpone until September the release of the BlackBerry Bold Touch 9900 smartphone, announced in May, writes UnwiredView.com .



The new date is not official, but is simply listed on the site of the British online store Amazon: BlackBerry Bold Touch 9900 in black case will begin to be sold on September 15, and in the white case - on October 15, that is, a month later. The cost of both versions will be 549.99 pounds (about $ 880).

The BlackBerry Bold 9900 is a candy bar with a 2.8-inch touchscreen display and a QWERTY keyboard. Built on a 1.2 GHz processor and running the BlackBerry OS 7 operating system. Of the other features of the device, 5 MP camera with flash and 720p video, 8 GB of internal memory, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC and GPS are worth mentioning.
