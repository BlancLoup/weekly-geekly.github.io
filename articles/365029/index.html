The winner of the competition "Composites for life" will take a two-day practical course "Making a prototype of carbon fiber"

The Russian company Composite is holding a competition called Composites for Life , whose members must offer new options for using carbon fiber to improve the product used in everyday life. The main prize is a two-day practical course "Making a prototype of carbon fiber" at the production site in the Technopolis "Moscow". The winners of the second and third places will receive sets for the realization of the idea.



Holding company "Composite" holds a competition "Composites for life." The company offers to share ideas for creating new products used in everyday life from carbon fiber -based materials like tow, fabric or prepreg. The winner will take part in a two-day practical course "Making a prototype of carbon fiber" at the production base of the holding company "Composite" in the Technopolis "Moscow" .

Composite material - a heterogeneous solid material of several components with a clear difference between them. Such materials have been used for several thousand years; today they are common in the aircraft industry, automotive industry, medicine - including prosthetics and dentistry .
