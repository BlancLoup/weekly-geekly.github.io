About KDE 4.2

KDE 4.1.4 itself and the KDE 4.2 release candidate are available for download.


The release of KDE 4.1.4 has been released in which the accumulated errors have been fixed. Especially a lot of bugs fixed in KMail, including the elimination of about a dozen causes of crashes and several problems leading to the loss of letters.

KDE 4.1.4 - the latest release of the KDE 4.1 series, KDE 4.2.0 release will be released on January 27, and KDE 4.3 will be released for the summer. Binary packages with KDE 4.1.4 are currently available only for the Linux Kubuntu distribution. In the near future, packages will be compiled for openSUSE , Debian (unstable), Fedora and Mandriva, and a special LiveCD will be prepared for a visual assessment.

At the same time, a release candidate for KDE 4.2 has been released .

Among the innovations we can note the appearance of vim-like navigation in the Okular document viewer via the H, J, K, L keys, Okular also improved the means of printing documents, added the ability to print to PDF, DVI and DjVu files, KAlarm implemented color differentiation of active and inactive entries in the list.
