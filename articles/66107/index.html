Working for three months today, and we finally updated the site design. The project has updated the logo, and the interface has also undergone cosmetic improvements.

Logo


The green cube has finally found its finished form in the form of a gear with a clock, which symbolizes work and time.

It was: It became:

Design


The main page of the site is decorated with a comic strip, briefly explaining to visitors how the worktrack can help them in their work :)
We divided the menu, placing the main functions (calendar, planning, statistics, magazine) up, and the rest of the links are removed down. In general, the design has become neater and more pleasant for the eyes.

Interface


Calendar, scheduling and to-do list are finally connected. In the calendar, when you hover on the cell, you will see a list of tasks and all events for the day. In planning, when you click on a date, a form similar to that in the calendar will be released. And the red button will alert you to the availability of tasks. Categories that previously not all users could find are now placed to the right of the temporary tape.

When designing the interface, we tried to take into account the wishes of users. Therefore, if you have any comments or comments - write, we will try to take everything into account.

In our future plans: mobile version and integration with third-party services (including plug-ins for browsers).
