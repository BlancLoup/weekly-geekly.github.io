If you want to attract investment

In February last year, a venture fund was opened in the MSU Science Park, which invests in Internet start-ups. I want to tell you about how we worked and what experience we learned.

First, as we were looking for projects. At the very beginning, it was decided not to promote and work non-publicly (in fact, this is how many investment funds work). This was done in order to avoid the influx of inadequate people. We independently searched for projects that are interesting for us, head-handedness and motivated people who want to leave a large company and raise their project.

The conditions on which the investment was provided were different and depended on the specific situation, namely, on such factors:

In general, in order to attract investment, you need to consider a few things. First, investment funds do not steal ideas. It is much more important to find a team that can raise the project. The success of the project is 90% dependent on the management of the project, the remaining 10% - good luck, an idea and so on.
For success, it is not enough to make a technically good service, you need to understand how you will promote it until attendance starts to grow.
