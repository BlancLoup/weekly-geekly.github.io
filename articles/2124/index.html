Ashmanov is preparing the largest search exhibition marketing event

Simultaneously with the fifth conference on search engine optimization, on November 16-17, the exhibition "Search Engine Optimization and Website Promotion on the Internet" will be held at the Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel. According to the organizers (the company "Ashmanov and Partners" ) it will be the largest exhibition event in Russian search marketing. At one site will bring together the largest industry leaders involved in search engine optimization, contextual advertising, online consulting, hosting and website development.
In addition, visitors to the exhibition will have the opportunity to get acquainted with the latest developments in the Internet technology industry, advertising on the Internet and select partners for business development on the Internet.
