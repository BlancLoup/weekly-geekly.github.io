Idea Presentation: Mobile Office



Again this stuffy office! How sick! How can? Do I really spend my whole life here so here?

And life, the one that is real for itself, begins on Friday evening and ends on Monday morning. Yes, twice a year is a vacation. Bad weather included. Where without it? No, sometimes you get lucky and you manage to catch sunny days. Just like everyone else.

So the understanding came to me that it was time to change something in my life. No sooner said than done. Left work. Became a freelancer. His own master. Furnished his office, everything is convenient, no one bothers. The cat is kicked out of the door, supplied with food and valuable instructions for its use - now you can focus on work. But, all the same four walls. Still tied to the place.

Again, work ... Life passes by. I decided to change the situation. I went to the resort. Road luggage. I managed to work on the road for half an hour. I will work a couple of days late - I'll make it up.

A week has passed. Liked. Why stop there? Road to the next travel point. The train, the laptop in the evening of the first day of the villages ... Goodbye a couple of days of productive work. Eh ... Okay. It's worth it. We live once.

That's how I started spending most of the time on the road. Suitcases, trains, buses, search for a place to sleep and rest. There are cockroaches, there are noisy neighbors in the room. Got sick.
