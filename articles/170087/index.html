PlayStation 4 Presentation?

February 1, 2013 appeared information on the official website of Sony about the presentation called Play Station Meeting 2013 , which will be held in New York.



There are suspicions that NextGen will be presented with a serial number 4 console. At the moment, there is a lot of information about the future console in the network - starting with specifications , photos of the new gamepad , integration with social networks and ending with an estimated price of $ 429 per model with a minimum configuration . It also became known that some functions of the console can be controlled using a smartphone or tablet, for example, the purchase of games that will automatically be tied to the player's account. The question of supporting games with PS3 may well be resolved with the help of the Gaikai cloud service, which the Japanese company bought last year for 380 million dollars. In addition, streaming games will cost less than the "boxed" versions.
In general, there is a lot of information about the new Playstation on the network, it remains only to wait for its confirmations.

The event is scheduled to start at 3:00 Moscow time.
