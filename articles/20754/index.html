Small solar-powered car

I would like to talk about a high-tech toy. This time USB has nothing to do with it. A toy is not quite for children - I am sure it will be interesting to many.



The Mini Solar-Powered Car is the world's smallest solar-powered vehicle. If you still do not want to buy a large, real solar-powered car, so why not start with such a small one?

She can ride as long as the sun shines on her top panel. On this panel, the energy is converted into electricity and the engines are driven. There are no batteries and batteries, it works only from solar energy. The dimensions of the Mini Solar-Powered Car are just 3.3 cm. X 2.2 cm. X 1.4 cm.

This gadget can be perceived as just a demonstration of the work of cars from solar energy, and you can play with it somewhere on the street during the day. It costs $ 20, you can buy in the store ThinkGeek.
