Speech on the acoustics of JBL Arena 180, which were first introduced at CES 2015 and today are one of the sales hits not only in our network. It should be noted that JBL deliberately created a product with high power, accentuated low frequencies, high accuracy of sound and low price. Such acoustics have long been waiting for the market and, in my opinion, among devices with similar positioning, Arena 180 has become the best.


Marketing risk and rejection of the concept of prestigious prices


JBL has long been considered a company with a predominantly concert-studio reputation. In spite of the fact that they have had a consumer electronics division for a long time, their products in the mass consciousness are associated as acoustics for professionals.

A few years ago, the company‚Äôs management apparently decided to change this view and prove that JBL can easily compete in the consumer sound segment along with market leaders. It was then that they began to develop cheap headphones for mere mortals, portable BT-speakers and other consumer goods, which the company had practically never been engaged in before.

Today, it is widely believed that modern everyday acoustics are made more technological, but at the same time, less attention is given to details and quality naturally suffers. Or on the contrary, any more or less decent speakers is like an airplane, and everything that is not worth it does not even make sense to evaluate. By increasing their activity in the consumer market, JBL engineers tried to destroy these perceptions.

After examining the aspirations of consumers, management has set a difficult and ambitious task for engineers to create a powerful outdoor speaker system, costing $ 400-600, surpassing existing price analogues in terms of significant reproduction fidelity characteristics.

This is how JBL Arena 180 appeared. The new acoustics were presented at CES 2015 and almost immediately became a hit. The Arena series (largely due to Arena 180) was recognized as the best product by the European Image and Sound Association (European Imaging and Sound Association), receiving the title of the best product at the EISA Award for 2 years in a row (in 2015 and 2016).

Design


With the design everything is trivial, among the advantages one can note a rather large volume, which obviously reflects the continuity with the company's concert acoustics. Classic bass reflex acoustic design, providing low accented. Relatively rare for a two-way acoustics floor design, used to increase the volume of the speakers.



A relative design flaw is the phase inverter on the back of the case. This arrangement does not allow placing the speaker close to the wall.



The system is two-way, each column was equipped at once with two MF / LF drivers and one dome twitter. The use of 2 speakers allowed to increase the sound density, sound pressure and eliminate work in peak modes when using the working power range. The speakers are tapered, pulp-polyester, with no frills, but with a powerful bottom and expressive averages.



Inside the case is qualitatively damped, which is relatively rare with budget acoustics.



The system is lightweight, which is very surprising, given its size, each column weighs about 15 kg. For the case used chipboard, which, in my opinion, is not a drawback. The vast majority of modern speakers, even in more ‚Äúheavy‚Äù price categories, are made of this material. Moreover, in a number of acoustic properties, for example, sound absorption, chipboard exceeds plywood.
