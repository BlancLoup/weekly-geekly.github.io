UFO on the desktop

The Scandinavian designer Lasse Klein has invented and created the concept of an excellent lamp, behind which a line of Sci-Fi fans will surely line up - it is made in the form of a flying saucer, as it is usually depicted in the corresponding illustrations.

The plate will be made of metal, inside will be placed a light bulb illuminating the windows and the beam illuminating your desktop. To turn it on, you will need to click on the antenna placed on the roof.

Most likely, this lamp will be released in several versions, which are presented on the official website - in standard form, with an abducted cow and with a man.

Pre-orders are not accepted yet :)
