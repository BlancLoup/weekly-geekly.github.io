Konstantin Krivopustov and Alexey Stukalov about the CUBA Platform on jug.msk.ru

At a meeting of the Moscow community of Java developers jug.msk.ru in the office of CROC on June 29, 2017, Konstantin Krivopustov and Alexey Stukalov from Haulmont talked about the CUBA Platform , a platform for creating corporate systems.



Haulmont is the developer of the CUBA Platform and several products based on it, the largest of which are Sherlock and THESIS . A year ago, the CUBA Platform core code was made available on GitHub .

The company was a silver sponsor of JUG.ru conferences: JPoint 2014 , JPoint 2017 and Joker 2016 . Also, its employees participated in JavaOne 2015 conferences (with a report and being a silver sponsor) and JavaOne 2016 (with master classes).

Arguments are given why EclipseLink is used as an ORM . The story about Polymer UI and the mention about CUBA Studio seemed interesting.
