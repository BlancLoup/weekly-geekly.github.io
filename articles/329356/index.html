We invite to the meeting of Unity developers in Moscow

On June 7, Wednesday night, we will host Unity Moscow Meetup at the HSE Higher School of Business Informatics . This is the third meeting of Unity developers in Moscow. We invite you to share experiences using Unity, ideas and modern approaches, as well as make new contacts. And of course listen to interesting reports.

Participation in the event is free, but admission through the lists - therefore, prior registration is required.

Under the cut details about the event:



Event program


Optimization of graphics in the mode of intensive growth of a mobile project , Svyatoslav Gampel, Art Director of CarX Technologies



Svyatoslav will share his experience in graphics development and its optimization in the framework of the mobile project CarX Drift Racing, which has evolved from a technical demo version into the game with millions of players. He will tell you what difficulties the team solved in the conditions of regular scaling and updating the game. Such aspects as rendering optimization, writing shaders, memory usage, expansion of the editor's functionality, features of mobile devices, as well as some aspects of the workflow that allowed to output 24fps to iphone 4s will be covered.

VR (Game) Design , Alexander Bragin, CEO & Creative Director @ VR2GO



Alexander will tell about that:

  • How to choose the technology for your content.
  • What are the basic principles that should be the basis of each VR experience.
  • How to solve the problem of moving in VR.
  • How to achieve the effect of immersion.
  • Basic techniques of storytelling in VR.
  • What game mechanics work in virtual reality.
  • Fundamentals of user interfaces in VR.
  • Paper prototyping VR experiences.
  • VR2GO's experience in creating gaming marketing VR experiences.
  • What features should be foreseen when creating VR-content that will be used in offline locations.

Gathering guests: 18:30
Event starts: 19:00

Address: st. Trifonovskaya, d.57, p. 1 (metro station Rizhskaya).

Please bring an identity document with you.
And do not forget to go ONLINE REGISTRATION !

Waiting for you! Such meetings allow you to get interesting new information, make useful business contacts, collect feedback on your projects from the professional and of course just relax and hang out in good company.
