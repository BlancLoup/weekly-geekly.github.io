FAS has accused the "big three" operators in violation of the law "On Protection of Competition"

The Big Three will pay for too high tariffs if the Federal Anti-Monopoly Service proves its guilt. FAS opened a case against MTS , Megafon and VimpelCom (Beeline brand) in connection with the violation of the Law on Protection of Competition.

Operators have introduced po-client SMS traffic accounting for SMS aggregators. This innovation has led to an increase in mailing rates. FAS interprets this behavior of operators as a violation of Part 1 of Article 10 of the Law on Protection of Competition.

The case was initiated by the appeal of the companies National Processing Center LLC and SOGAZ-MED Insurance Company OJSC , which used the services of sending through SMS aggregators.

Earlier, there was a system for calculating the services rendered for the total volume of SMS traffic between the SMS aggregator and the telecom operator. The traffic coming from all customers of mailings to subscribers of a telecom operator through SMS aggregator equipment was taken into account. The FAS Russia has established that as a result of the introduction of a poker client accounting system, the SMS aggregator pays the telecom operator a monthly amount of SMS traffic from each final distribution customer, the service says. This change in billing procedures resulted in higher tariffs for end customers.

However, the Big Three mobile operators disagree with the FAS accusations.
