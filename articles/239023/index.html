The FLIR One: What can a thermal imager for a phone?



The infrared camera for the phone, mounted in the case - a good idea, which was implemented by the creators of The FLIR One. One of the copies of the device fell into the hands of Wired journalists , who made up a rather detailed overview of the device's capabilities.

Immediately it should be said that FLIR One has so far been released only for Apple smartphones, iPhone 5 and iPhone 5S. Already there are analogues that are suitable for many other models of smartphones, including Android-smarts. One of the following articles will be published on such analogues, and now let's take a look at the capabilities of FLIR One.

The gadget itself is monofunctional, its only task is to make the thermal radiation visible on the display of the smartphone. At the same time the image can be customized.

Battery



FLIR One has its own battery, the gadget does not consume the battery life of the smartphone, this is a plus. But you need to understand that FLIR One will have to be charged separately from the device, so instead of one charging gadget we get two.

The negative point is that to charge the imager will have to disconnect it from the phone.

Does the FLIR One case make Apple's smartphone too bulky?



In general, the thickness of the FLIR One + iPhone exceeds the thickness of the smartphone itself (Captain Obvious has worked on this statement). But the system still enters the pocket easily, and all this does not look repulsively thick, the iPhone with such a case looks normal.

Yes, you need to download the FLIR One app for the iPhone. The application allows you to use the camera, measures the temperature and allows you to receive photos and videos. A total of 4 applications have been released for FLIR One.
