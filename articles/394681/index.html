Display


ASUS GL502VT is equipped with a 15.6-inch display. IPS-matrix with a resolution of 1920x1080, anti-reflective coating. There are two types of matrices: with NTSC color gamut of 72% and 94%. You can also buy a model with a resolution of 3840x2160 and NTSC 72%. In all cases, the use of LED RGB-lights.

I didn‚Äôt have the equipment for measuring the monitor's color span, so I can only give a subjective assessment. I have no complaints about the quality of the color rendition, the image looks balanced, the colors are moderately saturated, I did not find any noticeable color distortions. The laptop has large viewing angles, and even at an angle of 150 degrees, the picture almost does not lose in brightness and saturation.

Performance and work experience


The laptop is based on the sixth generation Intel Core i7-6700HQ quad-core processor. The maximum clock frequency is 3.5 GHz, the base clock is 2.6 GHz. The amount of RAM - 16 GB, for sale is an option with 8 GB.

HWiNFO Specs



There are two graphics cards: integrated Intel HD Graphics 530 and discrete Nvidia GeForce GTX 970M with 6 GB of memory. Accordingly, the first is used at low load levels - during surfing, in office applications, etc. A 970M is involved in games and other applications demanding graphics.



There are two drives: a 1 TB hard drive and a 256 GB SSD connected via NVMe interface. Winchester used quite slow, at 5200 revolutions / min. But, thanks to the high-speed SSD, the system loads very quickly.

In games, the laptop showed its best. Battlefield 4, Mass Effect 3, Tom Clancy's The Division - in all three cases, I did not have the slightest lack of FPS. Everything was loaded very quickly, since the size of the SSD made it possible to install games on a high-speed drive. So, it was always possible to occupy the equipment on the respawns one of the first.

The GL502VT uses independent cooling for the processor and video card. This made it possible to slightly improve the efficiency and reduce the noise level: the manufacturer claims that it is easier to cool two separate circuits than one common. At high load, the cooling system is quite noisy. It is understandable - its dimensions are relatively small, the laptop turned out to be rather thin for a gaming machine, only 23 mm. The noise is dominated by mids, rather than high, so it is perceived more comfortable. And if you wear a good headset, so little is heard at all.

The battery capacity is 62 Wh. Not too much, especially considering the powerful stuffing. Probably, developers had to reduce the size of the battery to fit it in a relatively compact case with a powerful cooling system. In games and benchmarks, the laptop lasted about 2.5 hours, in surfing mode it lasted about 7 hours.
