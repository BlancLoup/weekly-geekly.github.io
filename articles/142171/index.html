StartUp Sabantuy! - new IT park project

Kazan IT Park with the participation of the investment fund Runa Capital announces the launch of a new project - StartUp Sabantuy! From mid-May to mid-June this year, a team of experts from Kazan IT-Park will travel through all the capitals of the Volga Federal District (13 cities - Izhevsk, Yoshkar-Olu, Cheboksary, Nizhny Novgorod, Saransk, Penza, Samara, Ulyanovsk, Kirov, Perm, Ufa, Orenburg, Saratov) in search of promising IT startups.



Elevator pitches will be held in each city, following the project the winners will receive invitations to the final DemoDay in Kazan IT Park , where they will be evaluated by specially invited experts, they are also potential investors: representatives of large investment funds are numerous friends and partners of IT Park First of all, experts of the fund Runa Capital.
