Russian documentation on mootools 1.2

According to the summer survey on habrahabr , about 8% of developers program on mootools. It is clear that the more popular the framework, the faster and more efficiently it develops, the more plug-ins under it appear in the network. Can we influence this figure in any way? I think we can!

The basis of any good framework is of course its functionality. But there are other important factors affecting popularity: the size of the code, the speed of the framework and, paradoxically, the documentation! If the functionality is not described, only a few will be able to dig into the source and find the coveted method or plugin.

The MooTools developers did a good job on the documentation, but on the web it is available only in English. Well, I decided to correct the situation. Now only the Core section is fully translated, the remaining sections are still in the process of translation. So I decided to contact you, dear developers, to take part in the translation (anyone interested in my proposal write me at oleg dot cherniy at Gmail.com).
