Buying process


When the console entered the stores in Europe, something about 60 euros was drawn on the price tag. Add assortment


Last year I wrote that the craftsmen learned to flash the console by adding new games. Therefore, I decided to see what tools there are to add games. On Habr√© found a great article from the developer of a special utility for adding resources to the NMC (Nintendo Classic Mini). His nickname is on Habr√© @ClusterM .

He wrote a utility that allows you to add games without dancing with a tambourine. We connect the cable to the laptop and fill the games (you can also change the background music and something else). The device itself is based on the processor from Allwinner, which opens up all these possibilities.

By the way, Nintendo itself uses the pirated iNES format, the games on the console are no different from those ROMs that can be found on the net in a multitude. It turns out that Nintendo delivers pirated copies of its own games. So if you want to add titles, we download ROMs on the network and load consoles into memory.





What games to flood? On one of the sites I found a pack that includes the most popular games (according to the authors of this collection, of course). But, in general, there really turned out to be good and familiar titles, which it was decided to pour onto the console.

By the way, the program from ClusterM allows you to flood hundreds of games; in this case, they are broken down into daddies. But hundreds - what to do with them? Therefore, it was decided to add an additional about 30 games, leaving those that already exist.

The program is not just functional, but very functional. When a ROM is loaded, information about them (which will be displayed in the NES interface) can be changed. Moreover, to facilitate the work of the user, the author has provided the ability to download the cover from Google images, right in the program interface. Perfect.
