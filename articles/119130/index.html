Instant deposit (withdrawal) to QIWI Wallet via Visa Money Transfer

Here is what Visa writes on its website about the VMT service:
no matter where your recipient is: You can transfer money to his Visa card in any corner of Russia, as well as in other countries.

Translations were in demand. This shows the dynamics of our turnovers for this product:

But these transfers had one major drawback - the terms of transfers from several hours to several days depending on the bank. Until the end of 2011, hundreds of banks will offer instant transfers, but the first 8 banks took the lead by offering Fast Funds to their users right now:
  1. QIWI Bank
  2. Moscomprivatbank
  3. Commercial Bank "Privatbank"
  4. Gazprombank
  5. Russian Standard Bank"
  6. Rosselkhozbank
  7. TransCreditBank
  8. Bank24.RU
For QIWI Wallet users, this means that you can replenish the Wallet online using the QIWI Visa Card number in the ATM network (Internet banking) of the above banks.

It works in the opposite direction: you can also withdraw funds from the QIWI Wallet to Visa cards of the same 8 banks instantly!

Commissions: partner banks and payment terminals set the amount of the commission for the provision of services at their discretion. Usually, everything fits within 2%, often less.

Important : the commission is taken from the one who makes the transfer - the recipient does not pay anything. A good reason to look at such transfers as a way to get remuneration for the work of the same freelasers.
