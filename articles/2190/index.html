US Department of Justice tested Windows Vista

The new Microsoft operating system does not raise questions about antitrust laws with the US Department of Justice, reports CNET News.

According to the documentation of the Ministry of Justice, Windows Vista will not be the reason for filing new antitrust lawsuits against the corporation. When testing the OS and Internet Explorer 7, no gross violations of the law were revealed. On the contrary, the corporation offered dozens of third-party companies that produce competing software products to take part in joint work to optimize their work.
