VKontakte blocked BestPersons.ru

Recently, the social network Vkontakte.ru blocked any interactions with BestPersons.ru .

In particular, the ability to send VKontakte notes from BestPersons and add images to users' walls was blocked.

Now even any mention of bestpersons.ru on VK pages is replaced with SPAM DETECTED.

BestPersons is the first service in Runet, designed to unite social networks, which interacts with more than 25 social networks.

The situation is a bit like the one when Facebook banned Google Friend Connect in May.

Sadly, one of the largest social networks in the Russian segment of the Internet is hindering the integration and integration of services. Ultimately, everyone benefits from this: both users and services. We hope that the situation will change in the future.
