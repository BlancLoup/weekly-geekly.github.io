Our body must maintain a delicate balance between acid and alkali, which is vital for maintaining health. The main task is to normalize the biochemistry of the body and preserve the right balance between acid and alkaline products. As our body must maintain a certain temperature, so our blood must maintain a constant pH level. The pH measurement scale (a measure of the concentration of hydrogen ions) has 15 divisions, where 0 is pure acid and 14 is high alkalinity. Neutral environment corresponds to 7. Since each tissue of the body requires its own pH level, our blood is forced to maintain this figure at the level of 7.365. A weak deviation from this figure in any direction will lead to death, and therefore our body will do everything to ensure this balance. He even borrows minerals from bones and connective tissue to stabilize the pH and neutralize acids.

Most people are sure that if you are ‚Äúfit,‚Äù then you are healthy, and this is fundamentally wrong. There are many people who are in excellent shape and have a gorgeous body, but they are terribly unhealthy. What is the difference between fitness and health?

Physical fitness is the physical ability to exercise athletic activity.

The goal is to be healthy, not just in good shape. I definitely sin by putting physical fitness above health, because I participate in fitness competitions. The term "aerobic" means "with oxygen" and indicates what level of exercise you want to maintain for a certain time. The term "anaerobic" means "without oxygen" and refers to exercises that give a short release of power, such as a sprint or weightlifting. Anaerobic exercise burns glycogen and carbohydrates, while forcing the body to accumulate fat.

Often, after a sprint or pushing a barbell, you experience FATIGUE throughout the day. The fact is that such a load creates acid in your body, and it is forced to expend energy to neutralize it. Personally, I use both approaches. Right now, I am training mainly in an anaerobic mode, which helps me to "shape" the body that I need, and I like it. But at the same time, I do aerobic exercises that give energy and allow you to achieve goals related to health. I'm sure you need to keep a balance. If you are going to engage in anaerobic style, you should definitely neutralize the acid formed in your body.

To determine which group - aerobic or anaerobic - is one or another exercise, you can, focusing on the intensity of the load - the heart rate. The general rule is this: if during classes you are able to maintain a dialogue, then this is aerobics. And if you do not have such an opportunity, and you do not have enough air, then we are talking about anaerobic training. Personally, I use the heart rate sensor during my runs to monitor the load and to be sure: I am at the optimum pace.
