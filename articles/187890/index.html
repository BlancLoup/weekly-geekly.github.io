Podcast: 10 formulas about power, motivation, influence and freedom from Mikhail Zavileisky



I bring to your attention the speech of Mikhail Zavileysky, DataArt CEO, on IT-talk. Write down some of his thoughts that I would like to share with you.

Soundcloud Link : snd.sc/15O2ItR

ATTENTION: the recording was made on the MP3 player from the last row. The quality is very average. For lovers of Dolby 3D and Kvadrasonika, it is not recommended to listen!

What was the conversation (officially announced):
1 Are company work and freedom compatible?
2 How to influence, but not to manipulate?
3 Is it possible to manage your career?
4 How to make decisions with a lack of information?
5 Do you need power to control?

What was interesting :

1) Analysis of the concept of loyalty in the IT field. Loyalty - predisposition, the quality of a person, not an acquired property. HR / supervisor / team cannot expect loyalty from a person if he was not inclined towards it at the previous place of work.
2) The problem of fear as motivation of employees. Any imbalance is a source of threats and fears. Fear is the most poor-quality motivator, because at a certain stage of work it has to be overcome in order to accomplish the task. From a stimulus, it turns into a blocker.
3) The quality of being is the result of work. Even if it seems that everything in the project is perfect - the result may not be predictable.
4) The problem of responsibility. Responsibility is not the result of the implementation of a specific plan in software development, but the fulfillment of the expectations of the people who have assigned you the task. Waiting and plan - not always the same.

The rest is in the record.
