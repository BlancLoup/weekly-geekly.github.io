Welcome to TechDay in Moscow on December 10

December 10, 2015, we invite you to TechDay , an IT community conference held in Moscow.



We continue the tradition of holding technology conferences by the IT community. Without exception, the speakers of the next Microsoft TechDay event are technical experts, experts external to Microsoft, MVP. They will state their point of view regarding the possibilities and scenarios of using various Microsoft technologies, share their experience gained in real projects, talk about the most interesting technological aspects of a particular solution. Therefore, the list of topics considered is quite wide - from PowerShell and management of client workstations to private clouds and features of Windows Server 2016.

You can get acquainted with the program of the event and register on the conference website . All reports will be broadcast online, recordings will be available on Channel9 and Microsoft Virtual Academy.
