Kinect 2.0 specification has leaked to the network

The other day, the Kinect 2.0 specification leaked to the Internet . Apparently, they will be equipped with the new generation XBox, codenamed Durango.



Boring numbers look like this:



As you can see, the new Kinect will be equipped with USB 3.0.
The FOV angles are increased, which in practice means a larger playing space, there is also no need for a drive that changes the angle of the sensor.
The new sensor will be able to return a full FullHD image with a frame rate of 30 fps.
The resolving ability of the data stream about the depth is increased, which means it will be possible to recognize smaller objects.
An improved infrared sensor will increase recognition accuracy in low light conditions.

Also, improvements are stated in the body position tracking system:

- Tracking players growth from 1 meter.
- Tracking the condition of the palms. For example, they are open or clenched into a fist.
-One game mode for seated and standing players.
-Ability to simultaneously play up to 6 players.
-Tracking the position of the joints.
- Tracking side poses.
