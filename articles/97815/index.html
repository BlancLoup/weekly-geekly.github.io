Know. Here are simple and convenient lists of interesting places on the map.


We always wanted to mark interesting places on the map and share them with others, and when Google and Yandex added this feature to their maps, we gladly started doing it. But they soon realized that their systems lacked flexibility and simplicity. Therefore, immediately there was a desire to make everything easier and more convenient. We realized that we were so accustomed to online bookmarking services that we didn‚Äôt imagine such a system without tagging. Therefore, it was decided to base the navigation system on the basis of tags and a convenient minimalistic interface.


The result was a service Know. De (‚ÄúKnow Where‚Äù in Ukrainian). Know. Here is an easy way to keep lists of interesting places on the map. In order to quickly look at how it all looks, we created a demo user. So , for example, looks like a list of the beaches of Dnepropetrovsk. And so - the details of a particular place.

Pilot version features:
  • Adding places to the map (you can set detailed information - phone, time, etc.)
  • Easy navigation with tags
  • Ability to view and copy other people's places
  • Text search of their places



Of course, we understand that this is only the beginning, and we already have a whole bunch of ideas for development - a social component, a mobile version, integration with other systems, etc. And we are full of strength and desire to realize it all. But before moving on, we would really like to hear what exactly you lack in this kind of services? Perhaps for your project or business need some kind of geo-functional? Express any wishes and ideas! We will gladly take all this into account in the next versions, in order to make the service as convenient as possible for you.

PS This pilot version is something like delicious.com or memori.ru only for places on the map. There are no restrictions on cities and countries.
