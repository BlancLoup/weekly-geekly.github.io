Ultimate WALL-E - cartoon robot character

About 3 weeks ago, a full-length cartoon from Pixar and Disney called WALL-E started in cinemas with great success. The main character of the cartoon - the robot WALL-E - simply fascinated viewers of all ages and both sexes. The producers of the film, of course, knew that this would be the case and conceived a lot of things dedicated to this hero. And among this is a robot toy Ultimate WALL-E.



Ultimate WALL-E - is almost an exact copy of the main character, made of plastic with a remote control. A robot can speak its ‚Äútrademark‚Äù phrases in the same voice as it was in the film. He can also dance, play music from an MP3 player (connects with the help of special wires), ride and turn. With the help of the control panel you can give him up to 1000 different commands.



Ultimate WALL-E can turn its head 360 degrees towards the human voice. Built-in sensors help to find out about the obstacles ahead, so as not to bump during movement. In addition, the robot is very curious and can move somewhere and follow the movements of people.
