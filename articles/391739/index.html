Payment by cards is not news. On the agenda of the car payment

At the end of February, Visa introduced an unusual payment solution - an application that will help drivers pay for petrol at a gas station without leaving their car.

Visa and Honda have teamed up to implement a new car payment application, which was presented at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona. It allows you to pay for parking and refueling the car without leaving it. On plans to develop such an application for cars Visa said a year ago.

Visa and Honda have created an application that can be used directly from the control panel of the car. It informs drivers of low fuel levels and builds a route to the nearest gas station. When the car is parked near the fuel pump, the application calculates the cost of refueling and offers to pay directly through the control panel.

Visa suggests that this technology has enormous potential, and within four years, about a billion cars will be connected to it.
