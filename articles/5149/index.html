Nokia 8800 in Russian

The Russian company " ISSE " presented a new collection of jewelry mobile phones Monaco .

All the phones in the collection are extremely expensive modifications of the Nokia 8800. What are the materials of which the phone is made: the front panel is made either from gold or silver and encrusted with diamonds, rubies, garnet; the case is made of platinum, silver or gold, and the screen is protected by sapphire crystal.


As for the technical side, today this phone can not boast of something outstanding. This is the usual tri-band GSM mobile phone. The screen resolution is not great - only 208 x 208 pixels, but it is worth noting that the quality of the screen deserves all praise - 262 thousand colors are displayed, and the picture is very bright and juicy.

If anyone is thinking about purchasing a phone in the near future, then Monaco will appear in Russia on March 19 ...
