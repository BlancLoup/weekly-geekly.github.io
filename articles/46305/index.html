PESRUKOT (simple and effective way to radically reduce the number of errors in the text)

Re-read written before sending.



I apologize for the evil banter in the title - could not resist.

I believe that a person who claims attention, interest and respect for his statement should read it at least once. This is an elementary politeness in relation to tens, hundreds and thousands of potential readers.

This method not only drastically reduces the number of errors, typos, random repetitions and other annoying misunderstandings, but also helps to more clearly express thoughts; provides an opportunity to think about whether to speak at all.

No, the message cannot be so urgent that it has no time to re-read it.

No, the message can not be so important that it will come down.

No, the author can not be so busy that he has no time to re-read.

No, the author can not have many other advantages that compensate for such blatant disrespect for the reader.

Even if you yourself do not want to read your writings - do not publish.
