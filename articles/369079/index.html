Choosing gifts for the New Year on the GearBest

The research company GfK, together with Google, conducted an interesting survey, the purpose of which was to find out how Russians approach the issue of choosing gifts for the New Year to their loved ones. So, it was found out that about half of the respondents, instead of going to the store, would turn to Internet sites, and 35% of respondents decided to choose a smartphone as a gift.



Why did we remember this study? Yes, because winter is near, and if you are from that half of people who prefer to shop online, then the question of buying gifts can begin to be addressed now. Especially for those who are not indifferent to interesting technical things, we have chosen 10 unusual gifts for the New Year, which are likely to attract both geeks and their loved ones.
