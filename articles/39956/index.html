CodeWeavers has released Google Chrome for Linux and Mac OS X

Download it here - http://www.codeweavers.com/services/ports/chromium/

First, a little about this company. She created a commercial branch of Wine (CrossOver Office) with a convenient configuration, support for popular programs (yes, there are uncles who pay salary for MS Internet Explorer to work under Wine without problems) and build for MacOS X. 98% of its developments are gives to the project Wine.

Now she has released a native version of Google Chrome under Linux and MacOS X, compiled using winelib . It does not need to shamanize with the installation and it works with HTTPS.

Of the shortcomings - it's terrible brakes and some glitches with the address bar ... but CodeWeavers promises to fix everything in the near future.

PS: And this is also a good advertisement for Wine, which was able to launch such a complex product so quickly under a non-native platform.
