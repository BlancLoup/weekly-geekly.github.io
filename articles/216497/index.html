Evolution of flexible frameworks: Scrum-And in project planning and monitoring, March 26 (Minsk)

In the martial art of owning a blade, there is the concept of the three steps of developing the skill of Shu Ha Ri. Shu - follow the rule, Ha - destroy the rule, Ri - be the rule. Improving our professional activities, we also go through these three steps.
On March 26, at the office of Ciklum we are pleased to introduce you to the new speaker Ciklum Practice Leaders: Management and communications Nadezhda Svirnovskaya.


For several years, Nadezhda has been actively looking for answers to questions about how to plan a project in a flexible world, whether you can take on Fixed Price contracts, relying only on the Scrum arsenal, how to track progress during the release and not to miss the moment of decision.
Recently, the Scrum-And framework has become increasingly popular. Adjilists dilute Scram XP and Lean with practitioners, creating new artifacts and rituals.

During the meeting, Nadezhda will talk about her Scrum-And framework in the field of project planning and monitoring , as well as share interesting ideas and useful tips on how to enrich and diversify your Scrum practices.

Nadezhda Svirnovskaya, product / project manager (Exigen Services), certified by Scrum-professional (Scrum Alliance).
Since 2008, Nadezhda has been engaged in business analysis and project management, helping development teams competently build processes, conducts training and consulting on the use of agile practices. Hope is a regular participant and speaker of professional meetings, conferences and forums on agile topics.
