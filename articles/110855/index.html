CakePHP Community Launches CakePHP TV Project



CakePHP TV is a video site dedicated to CakePHP and everything related to it. The CakeDC team is pleased to announce the release of the project CakePHP TV 1.0! We are developing apps to bring more attention to the CakePHP community. This application will allow you to collect and share access to all videos created by the CakePHP community.


Already, you can see the reports that have been made at several CakeFest meetings.

Currently available such interesting features as:
  • Voting and commenting videos
  • Offering new ideas to the community
  • Voting and commenting on proposed ideas
  • Anyone can join the implementation of the idea.

CakePHP TV is designed for developers of any level. You can contribute to the community, even if you are new. You can suggest new ideas that will be analyzed by the community.

If you are an experienced CakePHP developer, then you can post a video. To do this, you need to take the idea and upload the video. This way you will help the community grow and develop.

We hope you like CakePHP TV.

This is the third project made by our team this year. We also reworked CakePHP Bakery , and also implemented the project Questions about CakePHP

In the near future, CakePHP TV will be translated into several languages, including Russian.
