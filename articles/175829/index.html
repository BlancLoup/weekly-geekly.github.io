Exhibitions in China - 48 hours from idea to launch

Service " Exhibitions in China " is an online calendar of the main industry and international exhibitions in China, with the ability to synchronize with mobile devices via Google Calendar.





By the nature of my work, I often visit specialized exhibitions in China, which are held annually here in huge numbers. The popularity of these events, as well as the number of people who come to these regions with working visits from around the world, is growing year by year.

For some, the exhibition is an opportunity to find suppliers and manufacturers in China, meet potential partners, test product samples and conclude cooperation agreements on the spot. For others - a convenient way to enter international markets and present their own products.

The main centers of the Chinese Expo are Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen and Hong Kong.
