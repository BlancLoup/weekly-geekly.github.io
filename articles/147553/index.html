Kickstarter is gradually becoming international - England is the first

So, the well-known site for mass financing kickstarter yesterday announced on his twitter that by the autumn they plan to launch in the UK.



I think this is good news for us. This means that Kickstarter is slowly becoming international. As I understand it, this became possible after the aggregator of payments that they use (amazon payments) has expanded to the UK.

Let me remind you that before this, the rules of this service allowed to create projects only for US residents.
