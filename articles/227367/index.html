Satellite router Globalstar Sat-Fi distributes the Internet to ordinary gadgets

Now a satellite phone is not necessarily a prehistorically looking handset with a huge antenna - the Globalstar Sat-Fi router allows you to satellite any smartphone or tablet on iOS or Android. It supports connecting up to eight devices simultaneously, voice calls, mail and posting photos on Facebook.



Works Sat-Fi over Wi-Fi protocol through a special application. The user has exactly the options that are supported by him: mail, calls, social networks. This is probably due to the extremely low speed available to the user - 9.6 Kbit / s according to Engadget. A little later, there will be support for SMS and emergency calls, applications for Windows and Mac.



Buy Sat-Fi while you can only in the US for $ 999. Cost of services - from $ 1 / minute to no limit for $ 150. The Glbalstar low-orbit constellation has 40 satellites.
