In Begins, an open beta of the Neighbors Online project has begun . This is an interesting project that combines the functionality of a social network and an information portal with geo-targeting.

The goal of the project is the same as in the recently opened MirTesen.ru project , that is, to give users the opportunity to find out what is happening in their area, what useful places and interesting people are nearby. To help find friends in your home or porch, find like-minded people and provide an opportunity to exchange views.

An important feature of the new service is the Google Maps, which displays interesting places, upcoming events and people. On the map you can, for example, display all roller skating enthusiasts in your area. In other sections of the map you can see the locations of the upcoming events, information about which is published immediately. And of course, on the map are those places that the project users found interesting, to them, visitors to the resource add their reviews.

It is obvious that the developers of "Neighbors Online" did not copy the development of competitors "MirTesen", but simply did not have time to open before them.
