BucketVAC simple vacuum cleaner and catchment

BucketVAC is a very simple concept that will give a second life to old buckets of paint and glue, will significantly save money on the purchase of an industrial vacuum cleaner (the price is not specified yet) and will speed up the time to clean up the area as it is very easy to attach to any 5-gallon bucket of 18.9 liters .



BucketVac is mounted in such a way that it does not block the handle and thus allows you to easily move the container with the garbage.
It goes on sale in the fourth quarter of 2009.
