IPhone 5se smartphone and iPad Air 3 tablet will go on sale March 18



Apple is going to change the strategy - now sales of gadgets will start a couple of days after the presentation, and not in a few weeks or months, as before.

In particular, the presentation of the 4-inch iPhone 5se smartphone and the new iPad Air tablet will take place on Tuesday, March 15, and on Friday, March 18, they will go on sale, writes the 9to5Mac edition, which exclusively dug up this information. Apple has not officially confirmed the fact of the start of sales on March 18, but if this is true, then they should announce this at the presentation.

It is reported that the small iPhone 5se smartphone with a diagonal of 10 centimeters will combine the shape of the iPhone 5 model of 2013 and the stuffing of more modern 6 and 6Plus: A9 and M9 processors, NFC chip for Apple Pay payment service, ongoing support for Siri assistant, camera from iPhone 6 with the possibility of animated images of Live Photos.

The iPad Air 3 tablet with an A9 processor is equipped with a new smart connector and is adapted to work with new accessories - the Smart Keyboard keyboard and the Apple Pencil stylus.
