Startups in the Smart City area. Is that all you can do?



"Sadly." This is the word that came to my mind while browsing startups in the Smart City niche. A lot of variations on the topic of parking, a dozen concepts of data exchange from the field of internet of things and social networks, and everything else is some unintelligible nonsense. Are you guys serious?

In a nutshell, how this article appeared. I am interested in new technologies, inventions that change our daily lives. Here's the news on string theory, with all due respect to Hawking - not interesting. And, for example, the sensors that will turn on the lights on the road only when it is moving on it - very. To see what is so curious inventing at the moment, I decided to look through the projects on angel.co - a platform where every self-respecting start-up should publish information about himself (well, maybe not everyone, but there is the largest directory of startups I know - 16 thousand projects).

In the Smart City section under two hundred projects. Judging by the concentration of parking solutions - this is the only thing that prevents people in a modern city. Here we will park all the cars - here all happiness will come.
