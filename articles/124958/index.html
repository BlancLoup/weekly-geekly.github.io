CapyBar - plugin showing visitors on the site and provides an opportunity to chat with them.


Good day!

CapyBar.com is a userjs script (plugin) that shows who is currently on the site with you. Gives the opportunity to chat, meet.
And also allows you to share any page in the social network. Even those pages that do not have these functions.
View the demo can be on the site.

Now it is at the stage of open testing. Anyone can open and use.
Registration was normal, but then I thought it was the 20th century. on this now you can go using the social network.

I would be happy to receive feedback and parting comments. There are also very few specialists in the field of website promotion. If there are any readers who want to participate in the project - write.

Recommend.

UPD : Yes, I agree that this does not seem to be safe. This plugin is disabled on all https sites, but there is no information to collect. In DB everything is cached and only the host is sent (for chat). In other matters, you can only trust in my words. Decision is on you.
