Roskoschestvo presented a rating of wired and wireless headphones available in Russia


Wireless Headphone Ranking: Sony WH-1000XM2

ROSkachestvo together with the International Assembly of Consumer Testing Organizations (ICRT) conducted a detailed study of different models of headphones from a variety of price categories. According to the results of the research, a rating of the best devices that are available for Russian buyers was compiled

In total, experts studied 93 pairs of wired and 84 pairs of wireless headphones of different brands (at the same time professional studio models were not tested). All models were tested for parameters such as the quality of the sound transmission system, the strength of the headphones, functionality, sound quality and ease of use.

The testing itself was conducted at a leading international laboratory that operates according to the ISO 19025 standard (quality standard adopted by the International Organization for Standardization).

To assess such parameters as the quality of the sound transmission system, the strength of the headphones and their functionality, specialized equipment was used. The sound quality and convenience of the device was tested by experts. The technique is not capable of such an assessment.

Interestingly, some manufacturers of non-professional headphones indicate a very wide range of reproducible frequencies, which, first, does not always make sense, and second, often does not correspond to reality.

Creators of music, performers, music teachers were invited as specialists. All invited - of different ages and with different musical preferences. The tests were performed by listening to seven music sets in each pair of headphones: classical, jazz, pop, rock, electronic music, as well as speech and pink noise (the spectral density of such a signal is inversely proportional to the frequency, it can be detected, for example, in heart rhythms, in almost any electronic devices, as well as in most genres of music).

As for checking various characteristics, a special device was used to assess the quality of sound transmission for measuring amplitude-frequency characteristics and sensitivity in electroacoustics, audiometry, and other similar areas. This device is often called an artificial ear. With it, experts estimate the level of acoustic leakage. This indicator helps to understand whether the device ‚Äúholds‚Äù the sound well. For example, with a large leak, the music played in the headphones is audible to others, plus the bass is distorted.

And such an indicator as the functionality included checking for ease of use - for example, whether headphones are easy to fold, how easy or difficult it is to determine where the earphone is for the left ear, and where for the right ear, whether a case or a case is included in the package, whether there are headphones built-in buttons for receiving calls and controlling music playback, etc.

Another important parameter is the safety of using headphones. At the same time, experts warn that the number of people who suffer from sensorineural hearing loss has now dramatically increased. One of the reasons for the violation - listening to loud music in the headphones.
