Map of Kiev (Metro) in the Internet

From the new stations one can mention - Boryspilska, Virlitza, Krasny Khutor on the Siretsk-Pechersk line. Opening of the stations is planned soon: Goloseevskaya, Demiyevsk, Vasilkovskaya on the Kurenevsko-Red Army line.
