Japanese designer Enoch Morishima believes that the advent of the era of robots will coincide with the revival of the traditional Asian taxi rickshaws, which taxi drivers have completely crushed in recent decades by car drivers and scooters.

According to the artist, the robot rickshaw could find its niche in the urban transportation system. Moreover, such machines do not necessarily have to carry people. A free rickshaw can be unhooked from the passenger carriage and sent to deliver pizza, mail or junk.
