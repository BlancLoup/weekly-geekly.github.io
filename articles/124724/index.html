Innovative laboratory extends the competition!

Good news for those who did not have time to collect their thoughts. The competition of ideas held by the Innovation Laboratory in July continues. The Expert Council took into account the wishes of the participants to extend the tight deadlines of the competition, in which the PlayBook would be the prize for the best idea, and the winners - cooperation with iLab. Therefore, it was decided to extend the acceptance of applications until August 20, 2011.

We remind you that the competition of ideas concerns tablets, not smartphones. It should also be remembered that the degree and depth of elaboration of ideas at this stage are not as important as their innovativeness and commercial appeal.

You can not worry - all previously submitted ideas are filed, taken into account and accepted for consideration.

All applications are also accepted at: konkurs@innovationlab.ru

The Expert Council of the Innovative Laboratory will identify the contestants and put them to a vote, which will be held on our Facebook page .
