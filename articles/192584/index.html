Samsung introduced new devices at IFA 2013

Today in Berlin at the international exhibition IFA 2013, in which many manufacturers show their new products and hotly anticipated devices. Samsung has not bypassed this event side and presented interesting devices.



I will try to briefly introduce the devices that were shown at the exhibition.

Rumors about Samsung‚Äôs smart watches have been around for a long time. Various screenshots of the device appeared, but now the characteristics and features are known. So, Samsung GALAXY Gear - this is the name of the miracle watch. The connection is via Bluetooth 4.0 LE, the device is able to notify the user about incoming messages, calls, mail and various reminders. On the screen you can see the name of the caller, reset the call or read any message. If you need more detailed information, you will need to unlock the smartphone and read already directly on it. The device has a 1.9-megapixel camera with BSI-sensor and autofocus, record 10-second videos as 720p.

‚ñå GALAXY NOTE 3


Another long-awaited new shovel is the Galaxy Note 3. The Galaxy Note III demonstrates a display with a slightly larger 5.7-inch diagonal compared to its predecessor, replacing 5.5, but the resolution also increased, switching to FullHD, the matrix type remained unchanged (Super AMOLED). Dimensions decreased, the device is more compact than its predecessor: 151.2x79.1x8.25 mm and 168 g against 151.1x80.5x9.4 mm and 182.5 g (GALAXY Note II). But the battery capacity has increased from 3100 to 3200 mAh. Available in black and white versions of GALAXY Note 3, as well as the pink version.



To decorate the device, you can buy Flip Wallet covers and new S View covers with colors for every taste.



The smartphone will be available in LTE and 3G versions. The LTE version will receive a Qualcomm Snapdragon 800 quad-core processor (MSM8974) with a clock frequency of 2.3 GHz and Adreno 330 graphics, the 3G version is the latest eight-core Samsung Exynos Octa 5420 chip (four cores with 1.9 GHz, four - with 1.3 GHz ) and graphics ARM Mali-T628 MP6. One of the innovations was the use of 3 GB of RAM. The minimum amount of internal memory has grown from the usual up to 32 GB, there is an option with a 64 GB drive. There is the possibility of using memory cards (up to 64 GB).
