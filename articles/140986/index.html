Open Day: E-Commerce from EPAM Systems, Kharkov

One of the most anticipated events of the IT crowd: Open Day from Epam Systems will take place very soon, on April 8th . The event will be held in the institution with the symbolic name of IT-cafe . The main goal of Open Day is the desire of Epam to share its experience in working with e-commerce from various points of view. The speakers of the event prepared the most informative and interesting reports on the following topics: Challenges in E-Commerce Projects; Oracle Way to Conquer the E-Commerce World; Best practices to implement E-Commerce projects. Developers to developers.
Open Day is a great opportunity to get the necessary knowledge and experience from a leading company in the field of software development.

I. Problems of e-commerce projects
Development of e-commerce systems is quite a challenge, and the use of simple approaches leads to numerous problems that lead to ignoring the basic principles of e-commerce. The presentation will tell about the strategies and priorities of e-commerce, the main features and problems of this area.

Ii. E-Commerce: Oracle Solution
Oracle, which has proven itself as a supplier of various solutions for business development, has recently become a major player in the e-commerce market. In the report we will consider typical tasks and problems that arise both for small companies and for the giants of this industry. E-Commerce - the platform from Oracle provides the ability to solve them without making significant efforts to refine. Ready modules, clustering, search - all this facilitates the implementation of this platform, leaving customers more time for business.

Iii. Best practices for the development of E-Commerce projects. Programmers are programmers.
Low stability, unacceptable response time, performance problems are key points for improving any E-Commerce solution.
The presentation describes approaches - how to overcome problems and achieve a fast and scalable application (based on a series of detailed examples from real projects).

Date and time: April 8, 12:00
