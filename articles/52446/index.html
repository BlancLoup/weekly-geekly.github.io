I recently revised my views on doing business. I had previously sought to create an atypical company, in which the central role was played by creating good working conditions. Making a profit with this approach is simply a logical result. Work should bring not only material, but also spiritual income - this is how my basic principle can be formulated.

My name is Maxim Saveliev, I am the head of Twin px and today I begin to create a fundamentally new organization, based on the experience of leading companies in various fields, discussing and analyzing this experience with you, modifying and applying it in my practice. If I succeed or not, time will show, in any case, it will be useful for me and very interesting.

So, where do I start. I have a spacious office located on the attic floor near the center of Moscow.
