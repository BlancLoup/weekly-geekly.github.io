Site: time frame


Site creation - the process is quite long. The time of its creation is not limited, since this work is creative, depends on the requirements of the customer. There are 5 main stages:

  2. Content preparation - 5 - 15 days.
  3. Site design - 4-18 days.
  4. Website layout - 7-14 days.
  5. Programming - 5-21 days.



We took the average time spent on each step. Of course, time essentially depends on the level of the developer, the number of people in the team, and also on the customer.
