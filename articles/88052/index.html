April 1 is a sad day for payment terminal owners.

And for those who are used to constantly using terminals - to replenish the balance of a mobile phone, to pay for utility bills, freeing themselves from the weary staying in queues, or to pay for the Internet, satellite television and much more.

And the thing is that since April 1, a new law comes into force, according to which all terminals must be equipped with a cash register equipment (CCP), and be registered with the tax authorities. At the same time, only 40% of all registered devices in Russia have KCP installed, and only about 1% of them are registered with the tax inspectorate.
