We receive a personal certificate WebMoney

It all started with the fact that the WebMoney service has blocked two of my account. I blocked it in a very interesting way - you can enter money, and answer it with an error :) After contacting the support service, they explained to me the reason for the blockage and the procedure for obtaining a certificate. In fact, everything is simple.

To begin, we make out an application for a certificate through passport.webmoney.ru. Then we print and fill in the application form of the applicant, make a photocopy of the passport and come to the registrar (in Belarus it is Tekhnobank). We pay the application (12.000 BYR = ~ 5.5 USD) and talk to pleasant registrars :) After 20-30 minutes, wait for a message to the internal WebMoney mail. If everything went smoothly, a gold certificate icon will appear in the terminal to the left of your last name. Now you are an authorized WebMoney user and all transactions that will be made from your account are considered to be completed by you and there are relevant documents for this.
