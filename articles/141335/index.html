Mobile First! Conference: creating mobile applications



The main objective of the conference is to identify the most effective practices that make it possible to earn on the development of mobile applications and services in both grocery and outsourcing companies, as well as to provide participants with the opportunity to discuss the creation and promotion of mobile software.

Among the subjects of discussion there are both success stories of specific developers, and questions of the difference between outsourcing and product companies. Despite the fact that none of the topics touches upon the direct programming of mobile applications, developers will be interested in familiarizing themselves with other aspects of product creation - choosing the subject of applications, promoting, defining the target audience, interacting with mobile software customers, marketing and so on.

Conference program:

  1. Alexander Krakovetsky, DevRain Solutions. Experience creating Undead Carnage games for WP7.
  2. Dmitry Ryzhkov, Paragon Software. Digital mobile publishing: from platforms to media-neutral content.
  3. Alexey Fear and Alexander Sorokoletov. Experience creating a popular utility gMaps.
  4. Pavel Bashmakov, Stanfy. From mobile outsourcing to grocery.
  5. Vlad Davidov, IKON. Using Mobile as a new media in the distribution of content.
  6. Oleg Vakhromeev, Redmadrobot. The whole truth about Mobile Klondike.
  7. Roman Beloded, e-Legion. Experience of interaction with customers of mobile applications in the outsourcing company.
  8. Dmitry Tarasov, Tarasov Mobile. The main trends in the development of the mobile application development market.

Event Information

The event is free, but pre-registration is required. Event hash tag: #MFConf, twitter: @MFConf .

When: April 26, 09:30 - 18:00
Address: Kiev, st. Zhylyanskaya 75, conference room in the office of Microsoft Ukraine.

The event is organized by Tarasov Mobile , Microsoft and Nokia. Information partners - WP7ROCKS.com , DevRain Solutions , e-legion .
