Programming Olympiad of the MIPT Summer School in Applied Mathematics and Physics

At present, a school in applied mathematics and physics is being held for MIPT applicants (for more details, see the official website ). In its framework on the site http://judge.mipt.ru/index_school.html passes the correspondence programming contest. It is held on the Kirov system (that is, points brings
even a solution that passes only part of the tests) on Ejudge. The results of this Olympiad will be taken into account at the interview at MIPT and the distribution of freshmen into groups in computer science. There will be tasks of different levels from the simplest to the most complex, so it will be interesting for everyone, and the winners will receive prizes and souvenirs from sponsors. Part of the tasks is available now, the rest will be added as the School works. Winners will be awarded on July 9th, along with the winners of the Summer School Physics and Mathematics Olympiad winners.

UPD.
Upon request, I add to the post a link to the instruction on the checking system. If you have any questions, ask them in the comments, I will answer with pleasure.

Tasks are added to the Olympiad daily. All tasks are simple and are aimed more at ensuring that applicants once again realize their knowledge of languages, algorithms, and computer architecture, as well as become familiar with the testing system, with which very many of them will have to communicate during the entire first course.
