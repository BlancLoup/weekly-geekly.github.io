Universal e-book reader from computer screen and mobile devices

Good day!
I propose to the public a new service, e-book reader enreader.com

1. Load the book to the server (support TXT | RTF | DOC | PDF | EPUB | LIT | FB2)
2. Read a book on the desktop, or on the iPhone-iPad-iPod, or on the android device.
