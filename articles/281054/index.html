QA: Conference. Certificates and reporting tape in the broadcast



We in the past post announced online tickets for our QA: Conference conference . Now I would like to reveal a little map regarding the online format. If you look at the broadcast page, you will see that there are three zones on it: The flow itself (you can choose among 3 halls), comments - from the bottom and to the right - a reporting tape. We also introduce certificates of attendance at the event. What is all this for?

Reporting tape


During the conference, in addition to video operators and photographers, several professional testers will report. This is usually done by Microsoft and Apple during their events. One of them is Alexei Vinogradov , one of the leaders at Radio QA , who has already conducted more than 20 issues of his podcast and is ready to report directly from the conference. For you, this means that the presenters will comment on both the event itself and the reports (and in different rooms, enabling you to switch to those reports that you like more), highlighting the main ideas from them.

The reporting summer, in addition to giving an understanding of what is happening at the conference, allows you to connect to the reports at a time that is convenient for you. Did you see that the presenter wrote something interesting? And on the current report is still introductory? Switched, looked, switched back. You are afraid that you need to move away and miss the most interesting? broadcast can be paused. Coupled with reportage tape in my opinion it becomes a very convenient tool.

As for comments, everything is very clear: this is an opportunity to ask questions, chat with colleagues and discuss the report.

Certificates of attendance


We noticed that many needed certificates of attendance at the conference. First of all it concerns young professionals who need experience and a way to talk about this experience. It is quite possible that when you get a job, having such a certificate will help your employer make a positive decision in your favor.
