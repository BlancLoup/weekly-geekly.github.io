Music with you: wearable sound

The number of gadgets in the farm from year to year is constantly growing. And now my grandmother has mastered the tablet and is watching TV programs not on a TV, but on a compact and light mobile computer. In traffic jams, it is increasingly possible to see drivers who watch movies from tablets or phones. And in all these cases, if the volume is not enough, you have to use headphones or connect a mobile device to any amplifier. The solution was found - Mobisound.





The device itself is a wireless headset and comes in a small box.


The package itself includes the headset itself, a jack-jack cable for connecting to a sound source and a USB-microUSB cable for charging the built-in battery.


It should be noted that the headset is made collapsible to reduce overall dimensions during transportation. And for normal use, the headset unfolds in one movement. If necessary, the scope of the headset can be increased. This is required for tablets larger than 7 inches. It should be noted that the headset comes in three colors: white, blue and yellow.


Interfaces and controls are located on one of the sides of the headset: a power button, aux connector and microUSB charging port. It is noteworthy that there are no volume control buttons: the device is passive and only produces sound. At the same time, the volume of the sound source is controlled - a tablet or a smartphone. Synchronization with gadgets occurs via Bluetooth, and to facilitate the connection, the headset supports NFC technology.
Not for nothing this device is called a headset, because it is equipped with a microphone. It is worth noting that his sensitivity is low and that the interlocutor can hear well will have to speak much louder than when talking on the phone.


The headset can be worn around the neck for wearing and listening to music, or used as a stand for a smartphone / tablet.


Special notches will not let the gadget fall out of the garnish capture.


As for wearing it, it's pretty simple, although the device weighs 380 g. The bezel just hangs on the neck, and the sound is directed forward and down, so even loud music will not stun.



We now turn to volume and sound quality. In the form of external speakers for a smartphone and tablet, this headset can be recommended safely. The sound is clear, without wheezing and imbalance in the high and low frequencies. It is pleasant to listen to music, and it is convenient to watch movies even to several people. The location of the speakers and the propagation of sound is such that there is no pronounced direction in a closed space, like a car, so there is decent audibility.

The screws are located under the silver and rubber pads, so you need to gently pry them.
