Viliv X70

Viliv company released information about the new device - X70 Atom Communication MID. This machine is a compact and powerful enough UMPC with an Atom Z520 processor at 1.33 GHz, 1 GB of RAM, a 60-gigabyte hard drive and a 7-inch touchscreen WSVGA display.


The X70 Atom Communication MID is equipped with WiFi and Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR modules, an SD / SDHC card slot, a USB port and, optionally, a WiMAX / HSDPA module. There are stereo speakers and a microphone. The hard drive can be changed to an 8 or 16 GB SSD. There is also a GPS receiver on the SiRF Star III chip on board.

X70 dimensions are 210x117x22.5 mm, weight - 660 grams.

The device will work under Windows XP or Linux.

Information about when the X70 Atom Communication MID will be available, and how much it will cost, not yet. But the manufacturer claims that the device is already ready to enter the market.
