Personnel changes in the largest Russian IT and media companies this week.

The first full-fledged working week (January 11-15) of 2016 began with noticeable personnel changes in the largest Russian IT companies and media resources.

Someone went to "nowhere." Someone quit on their own, and someone received an offer that can not be refused. And some people even took a non-existent, "virtual" post.

"Megamind" invites readers to recall and make a "commit" of the changes.

1. On January 11, Elena Sukhovey became the executive director of the Price.ru marketplace (part of the Rambler & Co holding). It will serve as the general director of Price.ru/Begun, Alexei Romanenkov, who left in October, is specified in the holding. Earlier, Sukhovey held the post of marketing director of Wikimart marketplace.

2. Marketing Director of Pronto Media Holding (owns Hand-to-Hand and Job.ru sites ) Georgy Chibisov left his post. Chibisov said that he was going to return to e-commerce, he had previously worked in the Wikimart marketplace. According to him, he will not return to Wikimart, but did not tell where exactly to go.

3. On January 14, it became known that the former head of Yota, Anatoly Smorgonsky, went to work at Tele2 . In Tele2, Smorgonsky will take the post of "director of development for the segment of virtual operators and partnerships".

4. Nikolai Uskov, who previously led the Snob project , replaced Elmar Murtazaev as the chief editor of the Forbes business publication in Russia. About this edition of Forbes reported on January 14. Murtazaev will leave the edition of his own accord. Uskov will develop Forbes magazine, Forbes Woman, Forbes Life applications, forbes.ru website and Forbes closed club.

5. Michael Touch, president of the retailer of digital communications for Svyaznoy, will leave the company, reported the Secret of the Firm publication, citing company representatives. Sources of publications say that the Touch goes to the "M.Video" for the position of CEO. Post Tacha take Evgeny Davydovich. Up to this point, Davydovich was the chairman of the company.

And for dessert


The President of the Russian Federation signed a decree appointing the founder of Liveinternet.ru , Mediametrix and other Internet and offline projects, German Klimenko, as his advisor. Putin offered him to take this position on December 22, 2015, at the Internet Economy forum. Then Klimenko served as Chairman of the Council of the Institute for Internet Development ( IRI ).
