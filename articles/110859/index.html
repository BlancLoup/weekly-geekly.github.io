New Year's release Hedgewars 0.9.15!


The development team of the open clone of famous worms congratulates everyone on the upcoming! But what a New Year without gifts? We present you a new version of the wonderful game Hedgewars!

Innovations:

  • New Christmas theme levels Christmas;
  • Snow settles on the ground. It can both interfere and help the player;
  • New weapons Drill Strike for the destruction of enemies who are hiding under the ground;
  • New weapon Mud Ball for colliding enemies into the abyss;
  • New game mode - capture the enemy flag and protect your own;
  • The possibility of random distribution of weapons between teams;
  • Several new missions to pass;
  • New hedgehog hats: beaver, porkey and sheep;
  • Ability to turn off the wind;
  • The ability to choose the size of the rope;
  • Improved interface for easier game settings;
  • More new effects (dust when drilling, snow settling on the ground ...);
  • Improved Lua support for writing scripts and level scripts;
  • And finally, the most important thing is the level editor! Now everyone can quickly create levels before the game.

You can go to the game page , or download the game .
Congratulations to all the upcoming New Year!
