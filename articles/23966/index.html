Some clarifications on the RJ45 connector

For public enlightenment, I would like to give some clarification on the RJ45 type connector.

Many people mistakenly believe that Ethernet uses a connector of this type. But this is not true.



In fact, Ethernet uses an 8P8C type connector .

You can find out here and here .
It is also interesting to go here: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RJ45 (pay attention to the title of the article).
