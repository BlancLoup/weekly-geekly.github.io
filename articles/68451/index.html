Business 160 bytes. Part one, introductory

SMS messages are not only convenient and simple means of communication, but also open up extensive business opportunities. Only in Russia the market of SMS payments is 300 million dollars, and this is at a maximum message size of 160 bytes.

If you have not seen the proposal to send SMS on TV, on the Internet or at least in spam on ICQ, then most likely you live on a desert island without all the pleasures of modern life. I suppose few of you were lucky to solve the housing problem on a similar scale, doing away with the useful fruits of civilization and not always. Nevertheless, human knowledge of the additional features of SMS is quite limited, and according to this, let us try to dispel some myths by drawing a lot of interesting information at the same time.

Let's start with the simple. What is SMS is described by Wikipedia article , there is a history and technical details, so we will not repeat. From this article, however, it is important for us to remember that SMS can be of two types - sent from the phone (MO - Mobile Originated) and received on it (MT - Mobile Terminated), then we will meet these terms in other meanings.

With the help of SMS-protocol we can:
1. send messages to each other from a mobile phone to a mobile phone;
2. send messages from a mobile phone to e-mail or instant messengers like ICQ, and vice versa - from instant messengers to a mobile phone.

Where and how the SMS will take place is decided by the SMS center - a gateway that processes SMS going in different directions. The cost price of an SMS message is actually zero, calculate for yourself how much they cost 160 bytes of traffic, but this does not prevent the operators to charge a lot of money from users for them. Of course, with mass mailings, the cost can drop below one cent (millions of SMS), but as you understand, with such volumes, every fraction of a cent matters. Typically, such volumes are used for mass SMS mailings during advertising campaigns or in SMS notification services. It is worth noting that in Russia, SMS sending by decision of the Supreme Arbitration Court is not considered spam , but in other countries the situation may differ.
