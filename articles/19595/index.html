V-IM [Vkontakte Instant Messanger]

What is VIM? This program is a client for convenient communication on the well-known vkontakte.ru portal. The first worthy project for this portal. Support many functions. The rest will be added as users propose. The goal of the project is to cooperate with the administration of VKontakte and combine the capabilities of both companies.

So, now cry all the executives who have closed access to the site.





Key features:
* Sending / Receiving messages to friends VKontakte
* View friend's info
* Support avatars of friends
* Visual alerts about new events (friends, photos, groups, meetings, videos)
* Quick access to the site's nodal pages (my settings, photos, videos, friends, notes, groups, news)
* Message History
* Sound alerts
* Many skins (you can customize the look to your taste)
* Site Status
* Translation of the names of Winamp tracks in the status of the site
* Groups in the Contact List
* Secure encryption of stored password

Website: vim.lavente.ru
