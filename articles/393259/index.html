In Moscow, opened the first in Russia electric gas stations Tesla Supercharger

In 40 minutes, such a station can charge the Tesla electric vehicle battery by 80%



In the Moscow Region Golf Club, owned by Roman Abramovich, the first charging station in the Russian Federation for the accelerated charging of Tesla electric vehicles appeared, writes Meduza. At the same time in the official list of stations Supercharger new facility yet.

Nevertheless, according to the previously announced plans of Tesla Motors, in the Russian Federation several charging stations should appear already this year. More specifically, there should be five stations. One of them was planned to be built in the Moscow region. The rest should open in Tver, St. Petersburg, on the highways M-9 and M-10.



As mentioned above, the Supercharger can charge an electric car battery at 90 kWh by 80% in just 40 minutes. For 1 hour and 15 minutes, charging will be 100% complete.


The station looks good at night.

If the plans are implemented, and the Supercharger will actually appear in the Russian Federation in the amount of 5 pieces, then about 30 electric vehicles will be accounted for one station: only on January 1, 2016, 152 cars from Tesla Motors were registered in Russia.
