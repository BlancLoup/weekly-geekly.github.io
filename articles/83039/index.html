Rest in BarCamp format

Cheap rest on the sea and unwind can be, for example, in Egypt. But having been there once, it probably becomes boring if lying on the beach doesn‚Äôt attract you much and you are not a fan of drinking alcohol at night. My personal experience was such that half the day is spent on the sea, and the second half of the day you do not know what to do if you have already been on excursions, but you do not want a bustling market and a disco.

For those who do not like to sit in their place, I propose a different concept. Why not make a holiday in BarCamp format? At the same time, the essence is not in the organization of serious seminars and workshops.

A group is going, for example, consisting of:
  • cyclist
  • scooter
  • swimmer or diver
  • yogi or martial artist
  • leadership trainer
  • craftsman
  • a cook who can make sushi
  • gamer or ithnik
  • bartender
  • master of anything interesting


As a result, for a 7-day rest, you can offer this program:

The first day
The first half of the day - arrival, settlement
The second half of the day - acquaintance with the sea, acclimatization, evening walk along the shore.

Second day
Morning - Sea / Sleep / Free Program
The second half of the day - acquaintance, fun games, a la team building, to learn more about each other.

Third day
Afternoon - diving to corals, water polo / beach volleyball
Afternoon - workshop for the preparation of sushi and other culinary delights

Day four
Morning - cycling
Afternoon - sea, diving, beach volleyball

Fifth day
Morning - Rollers
Afternoon - Counter-Strike Championship

Sixth day
Morning - Waterpark
Afternoon - Yoga, relaxation training.

Seventh day
Morning - the sea
Afternoon - Cocktail Master Class

Eighth day
The first half of the day - the sea / departing from the previous master class
Afternoon - it's time to go home.

In other words, each person from the company comes up with some kind of master class or other activity for 2-3 hours, and the organizer builds a schedule so that everyone can share their skills and, on the other hand, the program promotes relaxation. You can easily include a guided tour or quad biking.

Why Egypt? This may be Turkey or another country where you like. ) Just Egypt, in my opinion, is still inexpensive, but a different level of service.
But what about the Crimea and the mountains, you ask? But this is a completely different story, and if you want to relieve tension in a week, lie in the sun and do not become stupid, then this format is just fine. Do you agree?
