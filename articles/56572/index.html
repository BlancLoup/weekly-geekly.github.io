Version 1.20 of the free real-time meditative strategy game Dyson has been released

Dyson is an original multiplatform (GNU / Linux, Windows) real-time abstract graphics strategy developed by two enthusiasts, Alex May and Rudolf Cremers.

The game was nominated for the Grand Prix of Schemus McNally at the 2009 IGF (Independent Games Festival) competition.

In version 1.20, the game became even more pleasant in appearance (the roots of trees flowing smoothly to the cores, the glow of asteroid cores, grass on the surface of asteroids) and the rules were slightly changed.
