Apple may trade Google for Bing

The collaboration between Apple and Microsoft is infrequent, but nevertheless it has taken place repeatedly. For example, Apple optimized iTunes for the Windows operating system, and Microsoft released versions of its Office suite that are compatible with OS X.
And now these two companies are once again negotiating, this time about the possibility of installing Bing.com as a main search engine on the Apple iPhone. Now the default search on the iPhone is Google. The search giant pays Apple $ 100 million a year for this.
Apparently, Microsoft have the opportunity to offer a larger amount.



But it's not just about the money. The iPhone-Google contract was made even before the device itself was released in 2007, while Google and Apple operated in complementary areas. Also, both companies worked together to improve the services of Google Maps and Youtube, so that they work better on the then new iPhone.
