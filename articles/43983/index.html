Four thirds of E-30

The company Olympus announced the release of a new E-30 Micro Four Thirds camera with a 12.3-megapixel matrix and a flip-down and rotating 2.7-inch display.


The light sensitivity of the E-30 matrix ranges from 100 to 3200 ISO. The novelty is implemented 11-point autofocus system, image stabilization based on the shift matrix. There are already familiar "add-ons" like Face Detection Technology and Shadow Adjustment Technology.

The camera image is processed by the TruePix III + processor. In addition, it has an Art Filters image processing system, which allows one of six filters to be superimposed on a photo without a computer. Supported photo formats - RAW and JPEG. To store the footage used memory card format CompactFlash or xD-Picture.

Camera Olympus E-30 will be available in early 2009. It will cost $ 1,300.

Detailed information on the specifications of the Olympus E-30 can be viewed on the official product page .
