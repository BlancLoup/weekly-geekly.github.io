J & P: Digital content in Russia in 2006-2010.

The volume of the legal digital content market in Russia was $ 1.7 billion in 2006, according to researchers at J'son & Partners . By 2010, this market can grow more than 3.5 times to $ 5 billion.

This positive trend will determine a number of factors. Second, retail chains selling legal products will grow. This segment of J & P promises 50% of the total market by 2010. Finally, the demand for consumer electronics and digital devices will increase - competition in the market will grow, and it will become more fragmented (there will be a mass of niche offers).
