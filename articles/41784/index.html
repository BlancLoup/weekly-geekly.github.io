A runaway alarm clock is one way to quickly wake up.


Is it difficult for you to wake up in the morning? Do not want to get up and move? Do you turn off the alarm and go on sleeping? This gadget will not let you fall asleep again!

The alarm clock starts ringing at the appointed time, jumps off the table, the bed on the floor and, winking with light bulbs, runs off! Chasing the apartment for screaming alarm make you wake up!

The alarm clock has dimensions of about 13 centimeters in length with a wheel diameter of about 7 centimeters, and operates on 4 AAA batteries. You can buy a gadget for about $ 15 at eBay auction.
