Game programming in Light Bot

Light Bot is a fun flash browser game where you need to program your bot to perform a simple task: reach the goal without any obstacles.

As a person practically unfamiliar with the principles of programming, I tried to achieve the goal in an aggressive way, ignoring meaningless (for me) terminology like "Main Method" and "Function 1". But having reached the end of level 1, you begin to exude rays of joy and pride, struggling with the desire to send a resume to the largest companies in the world for the post of programmer, attaching only a screenshot of your result.

"Great little game" - so called Light Bot in Boing Boing .
