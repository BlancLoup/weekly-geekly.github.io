The book "Freelancers and Shabashniki"



Two years ago, my wife and I moved to live outside the city. And from time to time I have to be in the shoes of the customer. So it turns out that the responsibilities of the owner of a private house, which in the city is usually performed utilities. Therefore, so that the roof was repaired, the sewage system was made, the courtyard was refined - you need to hire people. Only instead of freelancers they are called "shabashniki." Due to the fact that for most, the provision of such services is an additional income.

During the short time that we live outside the city, I have already managed to fill the bumps in working with builders and "shabashniki." As it turned out, these bumps are exactly the same as those of clients who work with freelancers and design studios. Namely:

  1. Poor quality of work that makes itself felt through time, at the most inopportune moment. Moreover, the services of the contractor may not be the cheapest.
  2. Breaking deadlines. Believe me, to sit an additional week and a half without water is no less painful, as without design a week before the launch of an advertising campaign.
  3. Performers on the recommendation, even from the best friends, can screw up a lot more.
  4. If you do not properly control the process, then you will steal time, money and resources. When buying construction materials myself, I learned a few methods of how this is done.
  5. If you do not understand the question, it is very easy for you to hang noodles on your ears.




The root of my problems when working with builders lay in the fact that I do not understand electrician, plumbing, metalworking, etc. I naively thought that I earned good enough so that other people could not do what I can do. . I thought that there was no need to go into all the details, because there are experts who know how to do this. That was probably my biggest mistake, which cost me a lot of money.

Most likely, this is not what you wanted to hear. Of course, I want to find a specialist who will take your "headache" with the repair (website) on yourself. Plus the fact that there are such specialists (I speak from my own experience). Minus - to find them you must, at least minimally, understand the topic. This raises the question:
