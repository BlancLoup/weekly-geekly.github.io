SMELLIT odor synchronization system

There is another truly amazing device in the world that will allow you, literally to sniff what you are watching or what you are playing.

And when you consider the fact that the company is already making the synchronization system of odors - Odoravision, for cinemas. There is a chance that in the end a real product will be created, which can be purchased at any store of digital equipment. Since SMELLIT is still a prototype.
