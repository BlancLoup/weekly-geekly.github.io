YiiConf 2012, final registration is open.



The final registration for the first annual professional conference YiiConf is open.

  • Conference date: May 19
  • Participation fee: $ 50, 1500r. or 400 hryvnia
  • Number of seats: 200
  • Venue: st. Mikhailovskaya, 1/3 (Independence Square) Kiev, 01001, Ukraine




Speakers



Alexander Makarov


An experienced engineer, one of the main developers of Yii, the creator of yiiframework.ru and rmcreative.ru , the author of the book Yii 1.1 Application Development Cookbook. He will talk about how Yii is being developed, tell the facts about what will happen in Yii2, answer questions, teach you how to help the project properly.

Alexey Spiridonov


The head of the development team and the architect of the project api.2gis.ru. Talk about how Yii is used in 2GIS.

Grigory Kochanov


Timlid in the company sotmarket.ru (a large Russian online store, affiliate network), has been outsourcing for 8 years, in the past - a financial engineer at UkrSibbank. Explains how to cook MANY_MANY correctly.

Andrey Geonya


Developer 2GIS, will continue the story about how they work in the company. His report is called "Theory of Constraints in Agile Team".

Artyom Demchenkov


The head of the PHP department of Ontiko will talk about the problem of content personalization and its solution using Yii and Sphinx.

Sergey Bezborodov


Developer trud.com , will tell about the problems of growth and complexity of the project.

Roman Gomolko

Shows how to simplify your life with KnockoutJS when building a complex interface.

We remind you that a visa from Russia is not required.

You can follow the news on Twitter and social networks.

For all questions you can write to org@yiiconf.ru.

See you at yiiconf!
