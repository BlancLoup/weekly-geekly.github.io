The first international forum of investors and Internet projects "Elba"

Hi, we are in a hurry to present an extraordinary event in the Internet industry.

Company K2Knews decided to hold the first international forum of Internet projects and investors " Elba " which will be held in the boarding house "Bor" from 27 to 29 November 2009.

On behalf of the organizing committee , which includes Internet browser and radio presenter Alexander Plyuschev, Internet expert Sergey Belousov and Badri Gobechiya, a financier and entrepreneur, I want to invite everyone who cares about the topic of investments in the Internet, everyone who has startups, ideas, ready working projects or, for example, money.

We hope that " Elba " will become a permanent platform for meetings of investors and project owners. A place where you can get expert advice, see what's going on in the start-up market and, possibly, make a good deal.

But that is not all.
Within the framework of the forum "Elba" there will be a competition and the presentation of the " Ivy Prize ". This is a real opportunity to get a fair and objective reward in one of these nominations, to tell about yourself to a wide and at the same time target audience, and in the end to receive recognition of your own merits.

Register , meeting on the Elbe will not disappoint you.

If you have any questions, wishes or suggestions, do not hesitate to contact us!
We wait!

Update:
For owners of Internet projects and sympathizers - participation is free!
