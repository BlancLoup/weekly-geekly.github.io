An inside look at a modern domestic plant for the production of microelectronics and not only

In February, a new plant for the production of sensors and systems opened in Kursk. They opened pompously: the red ribbon, the first persons, all things ... After a couple of weeks after opening, I was sent there for work, and I took this opportunity to take what was inside. I will share my impressions.
Details below (many photos).

The plant is located on the outskirts of the city, consists of seven huge red hangars, you can't even tell what is inside the production.

There are not so many people, apparently, due to the fact that most of the processes are automated. It is interesting, of course, to see what will be in the remaining buildings.
