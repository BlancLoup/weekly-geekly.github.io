Okie Dokie - the first Android client for YouTrack

Hi, Habr! In today's post, we would like to give the floor to a small Strintec development team from St. Petersburg, which developed an Android client for one of our products, the issue tracker YouTrack .

Let's give the word to the guys themselves:
To begin, we would like to thank the company JetBrains , which allowed us to publish this short review on our blog.

The challenge that our Okie Dokie application solves is to provide you with convenient access to YouTrack when you are far from a desktop computer / laptop. In addition, we are exploring the possibilities that a phone or tablet could still provide for working with an issue tracker.

The application is still in development, and so far it has the following features:

  • support for multiple accounts;
  • filtering the task list for a project and / or filter;
  • editing issue fields and adding comments;
  • Attaching images from the camera and images from the memory to the tasks;
  • interception of links to YouTrack in the application.
