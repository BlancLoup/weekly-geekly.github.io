Thousands of Samsung D6x00 Series TVs are defective - cannot display 3D in Full HD

3D SOS: Samsung's 3D shame



As the name implies, this is a very big mistake in the model range of Samsung D6xxx LED TVs.

We checked all possible signal sources: Playstation 3, Samsung BDP D6500, USB SBS MKV, USB MPO-file - and each time the result was the same! The TV does not want to show FULL HD 3D images!

Samsung D6xxx owners can check their TV for the presence of this defect. Here we have placed for you a few photos for the tests.

Problem



The guys from HDTVTEST.co.uk found in independent tests that there is a very unlikely loss of resolution in 3D mode.
Therefore, participants in the German Hi-Fi Forum began the discussion. At first, no one wanted to believe in it, and everyone thought that maybe this was just a mistake or they checked the defective TV. However, as it turned out, ANY of the tested D6x00 reduces the resolution very much, regardless of the size of the matrix, no matter 32 or 50 inches. Therefore, we decided to create this Wiki page so that you can check your TV for the presence of this defect.

What we were supposed to see turned out to be a shocking loss of quality when you turn on 3D.
You can see this in the photo on the side of this page.

If the TV can show this picture in Full HD 3D format, then there should be no difference between the 2D and 3D versions of the images.

We found that the D7x00 and D8x000 series do not have this defect.

SamyGO participants understand that the TV turns on the Low Quality Frame Buffer Resolution option when the 3D mode is turned on. At the same time, even content such as menus, text, and graphic icons decreases quality. We don‚Äôt know why Samsung needs switching to Low Quality Frame Buffer when 3D is turned on. I have doubts that this is an unconscious low-level error in the D6x00 series.
Some users have reported that this defect is not visible on the 60-inch TV series D6x00 , which is quite expensive.


The TV compresses the original frame of 1920x1080 (up to 960x1080 or 1920x540 pixels per eye), then increases it, therefore the HALF resolution is lost.
But this is not the end of quality. In the ‚ÄúVertical stereo pair‚Äù mode (‚ÄúTop-and-bottom‚Äù), the bottom frame (1920x540) is enlarged to the size of 1920x1080 and the vertical Odd lines are repeated and superimposed over the EVEN lines. This means that the bottom image (for the right eye) has only 25% (or 960x540) of the quality of the original left (1920x1080).

What can you do?


Check the TV for the presence of the defect described - FULL HD 3D image quality loss in 3D mode. And if your TV also has this defect, please call Samsung or email them, ask why the TV does not show 3D in FULL HD resolution.

Also read the Consumer Protection Act in your country ... In most modern countries, the buyer may be able to choose from the following options in case of a TV malfunction:
  • You can return the device and return the money in full.
  • You can get a free upgrade to the D7000 series, which has Full HD 3D.
  • You may request a reduction in price due to a defect. As if you wanted to return the price difference between the D5000 / D6000.

In Russia

Televisions are on the list of technically complex products .
Under the Consumer Protection Act (art. 18) :
In the case of a technically complex product, the consumer, in the event of deficiencies in it, has the right to refuse to execute the contract of sale and to demand the return of the amount paid for such goods or to demand to replace it with a product of the same brand (model, article) or another brand (model, article) with a corresponding recalculation of the purchase price within fifteen days from the date of transfer of such goods to the consumer. After the expiration of this period, these requirements shall be satisfied in one of the following cases:
  • detection of a significant shortage of goods;
  • violation of the deadlines for elimination of defects in the goods established by this Law;
  • the impossibility of using the goods during each year of the warranty period in the aggregate of more than thirty days due to the repeated elimination of its various shortcomings .
