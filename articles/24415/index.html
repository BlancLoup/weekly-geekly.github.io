Register a trademark for protection?

I want to ask your advice or thoughts about whether to register a trademark for an Internet project in order to protect?

The first (which is what interests you now) for which it is possible to cling, they can register our name as a trademark and then ask, so to speak. We are beginners, of course, the money is "very limited" compared to them. The question is whether to register the brand itself, and in what country or is it possible to do without this hemorrhoids? Guys of some significant internationally rendered services for some reason do not always bother about this ...

We are from Austria, the online service is designed, in principle, for the whole world ... To register in Austria, they can do it in the US, what does it threaten us with, and then in Austria it is not necessary, it turns out? Or to do both in Austria and in the USA? What do you think, maybe someone has a similar experience?
