HTC Wildfire went on sale in Russia

According to the official Twitter of the company HTC, the first shipments of the budget smartphone Wild Wildfire HTC are already on their way to online shopping in the country. The recommended price for the device will be 12,990 rubles, and may vary up or down depending on the retailer.

This smartphone is notable for being in fact the first full-fledged budget Android-communicator from HTC. Available a trusted HTC Sense interface, a capacitive touchscreen display with a QVGA resolution and a diagonal of 3.2 ", WiFi, GPS, GSM / WCDMA support, a microUSB port, and support for microSD memory cards up to 32 GB. The HTC Wildfire is based on the Qualcomm hardware platform MSM7225 processor with a frequency of 528 MHz. Also Wildfire is equipped with 384 MB of RAM, which will allow you to run a wide variety of widgets, and 512 MB of ROM.

Wildfire will run on Android 2.1, and will also receive an upgrade to Android 2.2 this year. Thus, HTC Wildfire is a good purchase option if you are satisfied with the budget QVGA resolution.
