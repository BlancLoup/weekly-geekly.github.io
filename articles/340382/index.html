Russian Design Cup 2017

It is time for sleepless nights in the name of leveling up design skills and mining award iPhones - we are launching the sixth contest Russian Design Cup 2017 .



The schedule of the tournament is a little easier than usual, but the tasks have become as before (with the hill, in the sense):

  • Qualifying round: October 17 - November 19 (1 challenge)
  • Round 1: December 4-17 (3 tasks)
  • Round 2: January 22 - February 4 (3 tasks)
  • Round 3: February 12-28 (3 tasks)

In short, now we are almost like the Russian Premier League - let's see how convenient is the scheme for you with the division of the tournament into autumn and winter.

As always, in order to separate the best works from timidly promising, we called upon the help of bright local designers: Max Tenths (Redmadrobot), Max Orlov ( ONY), Ivan Vasilyev (Alfa-Bank), Leonid Ivakhov (Odnoklassniki) and Yuri Vetrov (Mail.Ru Group).
