Exchange for cash in one of the certified exchange points


I changed. Damn, I have been changing it this way for two years ... This is probably the most inconvenient and unprofitable way. It is justified only when you really need to exchange WMZ for USD. Among its shortcomings:
  • High commission of exchange points - from 1.5% to 5%. In this case, in some exchangers, the percentage depends on the amount (the more money you withdraw, the less the commission will be), while in others it does not.
  • Undervalued dollar exchange rate in exchange for UAH. Here is a real example. At one Lviv cantor (the cantor of the Beavers brothers :)) WMZ was bought at the rate of 5.5 UAH / WMZ, and sold at 7 UAH / WMZ. In currency exchange offices, the dollar buying rate was around 6.5 UAH / USD. Cool?
  • Not reliable. Constantly there is a risk that you will be divorced. One guy, who took 2.5% for the exchange and from whom Igor Reiter and I took the money several times, once gave me a fake 100 UAH. I did not understand intentionally or accidentally - I just stopped working with him. In addition, contact with him was problematic.
  • Not stable. Recently, I began to face the fact that there is a lot of WMZ in exchange offices and vice versa - very little cash. Those. they stupidly do not buy anything - they only sell. So I waited for three weeks to change money at one of the previously checked points.


We consider. The minimum commission of the exchange office is 1.5% (surely you will find such a hell by the way) + commission webmoney 0.8% = 2.3% .

Transfer through Aval-Express and SOFT (Ukraine)


I use this method now. The only inconvenience is changing only WMU and only for UAH. And since the USD rate is now unstable and mostly growing, I only change the WMZ when I really need cash.
You can change WMZ to WMU directly in the program itself (I once did not know and changed through ROBOX). The course is more or less decent. For example, when in Privat the purchase rate was 7.0 UAH / USD, and in other banks 7.1 - 7.2 UAH / USD, then webmoney rate was 7.23 WMU / WMZ. The disadvantage of this gimp - you have to pay an additional 0.8% webmoney.

Now on systems:
  • SOFT for some reason did not work when I changed. His commission is lower and is only 1%.
  • Aval-Express works - commission 1.5%. After submitting the application you receive an invoice that is desirable to pay. Two days later, you can take a passport and walk to the nearest Raifazen office for money.

We consider. 0.8% webmoney commission when exchanging WMZ for WMU + 0.8% one more webmoney commission + 1.0% SOFT or 1.5% Aval-Express = 2.6% -3.1% .
A bit more than in the previous version, but much easier, faster and more reliable.

Transfer to a bank card


You can choose from several types of maps. I will talk about Payoneer, because I know for sure that they work in Ukraine.

Here you can see the tariffs of the system when working with Webmoney Payoneer. As you can see, a lot depends on the certificate. So for the Initial Passport, the commission for the accrual of funds is 1.5%, and for the Personal Passport - 0.5%. Also, the limits on the card replenishment are also different. I think in this case obtaining a Personal Passport will be a justified action.

We consider. 0.8% commission webmoney + 0.5% Payoneer + 1% for cash withdrawals = 2.3% .
As you can see, the total commission is the same as the minimum commission for withdrawing funds through the exchange office. But:
  • No hemorrhoids with the search for the exchanger, which is ready to change money. Independence yopt!
  • No risk.
  • The USD cards come on the card, which can be changed at any time to UAH at any ATM or bank.
  • The card can be used for payments over the Internet and abroad.

It seems to me that the advantages of this withdrawal method are obvious.
