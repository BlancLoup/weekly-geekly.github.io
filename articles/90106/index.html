Call for applications for LVEE 2010 opened

The sixth international conference of developers and users of free software Linux Vacation / Eastern Europe will be held from July 1 to July 4 at the Khimik camp site in the Grodno region. Open acceptance of reports and applications for participation, sponsors are invited to cooperate. Details are on lvee.org

The annual conference of free software developers and users in Belarus invariably gathers specialists in this field from Belarus, Russia, Ukraine, the European Union countries, etc., near Grodno.

Not least, the popularity of Linux Vacation / Eastern Europe is provided by its format - professional exchange of experience is combined with three-day informal communication in nature. The organizing committee, which works on a volunteer basis, selects reports and forms the conference program, ensuring its high professional level. The format includes reports, short presentations, workshops, round tables. The range of topics presented at the conference is traditionally wide - from a story about new free technologies, software products and their implementation experience to an analysis of the legal and economic aspects of free licensing.

To participate in LVEE 2010, you must register on the conference website lvee.org . More information about the conference is available at the same address: project history, abstract design requirements, information on the delivery and accommodation of participants, materials from past conferences.

Applications for participation are accepted until June 12, abstracts of reports - until May 24.

The organizing committee also invites sponsors to participate. The event is traditionally supported by IT companies interested in working with qualified free software specialists, as well as companies interested in supporting free software as a socially significant phenomenon. Participation in LVEE-2010 gives them an exclusive opportunity to work directly with an audience consisting of the most active developers and users of free software.

To date, Sam solutions, Promwad, Local Networks and IT magazine Bel (IT Director) have already announced support for LVEE-2010.
