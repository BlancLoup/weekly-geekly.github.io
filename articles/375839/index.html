Congratulations to all the students on their professional holiday!

Dear Giktaimer students!

On behalf of our academic Microsoft team and on my own behalf, I congratulate you on your professional holiday - student's day! In my understanding, even those who have already graduated from the university, but at the same time continue to learn, learn something new - they may consider themselves involved in this holiday! In the professions related to information technology, you can not stop learning at any age!



On this day, I want to wish you that ‚Äúthe exam was always a holiday for you‚Äù. In the broad sense of the word - an exam can be understood as a difficult task at work, and the need to understand a new technology. After all, any test makes us stronger, better, more experienced.

Student Day is a good reason to remember what good we do for students. For many of you, the most important thing is to find yourself in life, to discover something interesting for yourself that can later become a source of happiness and income. Therefore, we are trying to help you find your way in the world of information technology!


The main stages of this path are:
  • Have access to free software . All students can get the software necessary for the developer according to the DreamSpark program , either directly, having passed verification on the site through a VKontakte account or student card, or through their own institution for DreamSpark Premium - in this case the list of available software is wider and includes the latest versions of Windows.
  • Have the opportunity to easily learn the necessary technology. This can be done online either on the Microsoft Virtual Academy portal or the MVA Club , or in person, by taking part in our events at universities or student hackathons . We have a lot of interesting courses: from an exciting introduction to C # for beginners to courses on creating mobile applications . Also a good way to improve your knowledge and skills, as well as to check your level is to perform a number of exercises tasks in the C0deStars project and become a code star .
  • Become active and interested . This is the most important and difficult point - to start doing something at some point, although no one forces you. We offer students to participate in the best international competition of projects of the Imagine Cup , in order to get a lot of pleasure, experience in developing applications in a team, and perhaps start their own startup (in more detail about why to participate ). And the most active students become Microsoft partner students and help other students become more active and technically educated.
  • Build your career by finding a job in a successful IT company, or by setting up your startup . After the Imagine Cup and the MSP program, students are open all the way; many find work at Microsoft or other top Russian IT companies. And to support your startup, you can get free software and cloud resources on the BizSpark program.



Once again, we wish all students an interesting study, and those who have already graduated from a higher educational institution - always maintain an interest in knowledge and a desire to learn new things throughout their lives! Be happy!
