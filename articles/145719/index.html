The second meeting of the Microsoft .NET User Group in Gomel

The second meeting of the Microsoft .NET User Group in Gomel will take place on June 16 at 10:00. Meeting place: GSTU. BY. Sukhoi , 2nd Corps, Room 306, October 48 Ave., Gomel, Belarus.

Awaiting reports:
  • Access to data in ASP.NET. Speaker: Morozko Alexander
  • Code First in the Entity Framework. Speaker: Dmitry Lopunov
  • XAML in WPF and Silverlight. Speaker: Ezhov Sergey
  • Development of add-ins for Microsoft Office. Speaker: Terekhov Dmitry


A coffee break will be organized during the breaks.
The meeting will be held with the support of Gomel IT-companies - Rus Wizards and EPAM.

To participate you must register:
  • Official conference page
  • Vkontakte community
