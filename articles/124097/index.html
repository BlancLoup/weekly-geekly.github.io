Simple SDK to embed chat in iOS app

Good day, Habrasoobschestvo!
In May, we wrote about our iPhone app Squeek. In short - Squeek allows you to communicate with those nearby.
Today we offer you an exclusive version (only for habrayuzerov) Squeek SDK beta .

- Why is he to me?
- Squeek is a good way for you to get feedback from users of your application, as well as create a community around it. We offer you a free tool that increases the time spent by users in your application.


- What exactly is Squeek SDK beta?
- The library allows you to easily integrate chat into your application.

- How to embed chat in my app?
- You only need to write to us at team@squeek.ru about your application and the room (name + geographical location) to which you want to attach it, which will allow Squeek users to communicate with those who are chatting in your application.

Where to get?
Download the SDK here - instruction inside.

We will be very grateful for your feedback and suggestions! :)
