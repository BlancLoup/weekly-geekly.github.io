RBC Information Systems - Bankrupt?

A copy of a document (PDF) on arbitration court proceedings regarding RBC Information Systems for bankruptcy has been published on the RBC website.

Judging by the text, the lower court has already declared the plaintiff bankrupt. At least, the date of adoption of the judicial act on declaring the debtor insolvent is indicated: 25/12/2009 and case number: 40-170473 / 09-44-929 B.

Now the arbitral tribunal will consider the case and, in turn, may reconsider the decision of the lower court.

The full text of the document under habrakatom.
