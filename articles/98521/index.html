HTC Incredible (Verizon)

Well, I will try again to write a review of a toy I recently accidentally got. The first review was written more on emotions, which eventually resulted in a well-deserved merger. I'll try to fix it. :)

Over the past 8 months I have had the opportunity to use three phone models: HTC Hero, HTC Droid Eris and Motorola Droid, so I will compare with them.

Experimental - HTC Incredible (Verizon).




Price:

On ebay: $ 475-510
Retail in Ukraine: estimated cost of $ 570 and above

Specifications

Dimensions: 117.5 x 58.5 x 11.9mm
Weight: 130 grams
Processor: Qualcomm QSD8650 Snapdragon with a frequency of 1GHz
Base operating system: Android OS 2.1 with HTC Sense
RAM: 512MB
Internal memory: 8GB
Display: AMOLED 3.7 "with 480x800 WVGA resolution
Network: CDMA2000, 1xRTT / 1xEVDO / 1xEVDO rev. A
Peripherals: GPS, Bluetooth 2.1 with FTP / OPP, Wi-Fi 802.11b / g / (n?), 3.5mm stereo jack, micro-USB
Camera: 8 megapixels, autofocus, flash
Memory cards : microSD with format support (SD2.0), cards up to 32GB are supported

Equipment.
As far as I understand, the times of cool bundles for CDMA phones are over on the HTC Touch Pro. In a very small box they showed up: the device itself, usb-cord, battery, charging and 4 books of "waste paper". There was not even a memory card for at least 2GB, which was in the HTC Hero, not to mention the 16GB card that came with the Motorola Droid. Although, in fact, this is offset by 8GB of internal memory. After a while it was found out on the phone screen that there is also a glued-in protective film, very convenient, thanks.

General view and first impressions.


After the Moto Droid, the phone was very nice in the hand. The absence of 40 extra grams in weight is very sensitive. The position in the hand feels as if the phone is slightly arched. The body is very well fitted, no shifts, gaps, etc.

To the touch, the material of the back cover is like rubberized plastic, very pleasant. The front part looks like a very hard plastic, but I will not say for sure, not an expert, so if anyone knows, please correct.

Removing the back cover does not want to return it to its place. The inner part is painted in a very bright red color. To change the memory card, you do not need to turn off the phone and remove the battery, and I consider this practice of hiding a memory card to be flawed, although I understand that this is required for hamsters.



A little tar. No matter how beautiful it all looked, the case seems to be unreliable. Very much, I'm not sure that when you first fall light on a hard surface, the back end will scatter, too thin and delicate. Check the time. The second spoon is the location of the microUSB. When the device is on recharging, the cord interferes with the normal position in the hand.


Display.

Just indescribably. 3.7 inches fit in a compact case with thin sides. Color rendering on a great level. No words only emotions. Just great playback of high-resolution videos from Youtube; viewing is a pleasure. Someone will say - "Fuu, again gloss!", But I can not imagine a matte display on such devices. The prints, of course, remain, but they are also easily erased. By the way, HTC, well, at least they would have put a rag for the screen, taking care of the user and all that!

1x network and EVDO:

One of the reasons for parting with the Moto Droid is a very poor-quality microphone and proximity sensor to the ear. After ear dialing the phone number during the conversation the tenth time - begins to bother. In HTC Incredible - as in the HTC Droid Eris - this problem is not present. The microphone is just great, the sound of the speaker is clear, but the volume is still not enough (tested when driving at 110-130 on the highway with an open window, as well as when talking in a cafe during a football match).
I don‚Äôt know how-where, but in our Odessa and Odessa region I could not find an area without EVDO coverage from Intertelecom. Data transfer rate from 1.5 to 2.1 Mbit. Always in touch, always access to mail and twitter. Actually, maybe I was just lucky with my place of residence. :)



Battery:

The eternal problem is the battery. This time a pleasant surprise. With infrequent conversations (about 30-35 minutes a day), with EVDO turned on, updates every five minutes (mail, twitter, checking programs), the battery lasted two days (day, night, day). But this is rarely the case, so we check under load. A toy with good graphics (iCommando) put the battery in just ... 4 hours! This was even more surprising, since the Moto Droid is already in 1 hour 20 minutes under the same conditions. In general, I liked the battery, but there are more on ebay and advanced ones.
Note: it is suggested that it is due to the lower consumption of AMOLED displays and an increased operating time is achieved. If anyone understands this more, I will be glad to comment.

Constantly enabled GPS and using it in conjunction with Google Latitude reduces the possibility of using the phone to 1 daylight day, which is basically enough, considering that on Moto Droid the estimated time was no more than 5-6 hours with GPS turned on.
