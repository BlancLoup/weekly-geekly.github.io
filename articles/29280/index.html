What does Media Plus offer:
At the first stage, Media Plus invites its customers to take advantage of the following advantages of advertising on the VKontakte.ru site:

* to improve the quality of contact - the ability to control coverage and frequency (capturing), allowing to achieve the most effective distribution of the frequency of contact with the advertising message in the target audience;
