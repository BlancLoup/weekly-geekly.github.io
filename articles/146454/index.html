Management of a network of online stores in 1C 8.2 through PHPShop "1C-Monitor"

For large projects with a multitude of stores, PHPShop offers a unique utility ‚Äú1C-Monitor‚Äù, which allows, in a single information field 1C, to organize and control work with many online stores.

Some users have already appreciated the merits of working with an online store based on PHPShop Pro 1C in conjunction with 1C 8.2, which provides almost 100% data synchronization between 1C and an online store: goods, orders, users, documents, etc. Now, more and more often, shop owners are connecting several online stores to 1C, and until recently, this has complicated their work. PHPShop proposes to use "1C-Monitor", which provides a single picture of all the stores in the network. "1C-Monitor" consists of a desktop and connected widgets:

Widgets "New Orders" and "Sync Log"

Both of these widgets are designed to process orders and study the progress of automatic operations (logs) on 1C synchronization with several online stores at once, which makes it much easier to manage them.



Widgets "Visitor Statistics" and "Active Users"

They draw data from the statistics module of the online store ‚ÄúStat‚Äù and display detailed information about who is currently present on the site and which pages are being viewed. Modules draw dynamic graphs of visits right on the 1C desktop. The ‚ÄúStatistics of Visitors‚Äù widget is able to identify authorized users online and will be useful for analyzing site traffic, it will show how busy the IM is and where visitors come from.



Widget "Order Basket"

Allows you to see the preferences of customers, showing information from the module of the online store "Visual Cart": what they have now in the baskets, and what it was before. The widget helps to pre-reserve goods in stock, with the obvious interests of the user for certain products or to clarify with the user why he did not complete the ordering process, for example, using an online chat.



Information in the 1C-Monitor is updated in real time, to save resources, you can configure the update time and time shift when the difference is local time and north. "1C-Monitor" is now available only for 1C 8.2 "Managed Application".

To test the capabilities of 1C Monitor, you need to download the PHPShop EasyControl utility package.
