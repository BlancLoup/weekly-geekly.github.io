Microsoft Expression and Silverlight. (Conference in San Francisco)

Today in San Francisco at the InterContinental Hotel there was a Microsoft presentation on Silverlight 3 and the Expression 3 product line. If you have a good communication channel, then the HD version of the video will be immediately available for you, and if not, Silverlight will perform the calculations and after the server and client communicate, depending on the speed of the Internet and the CPU, the required video viewing quality will be selected independently. so as not to skip viewing video upload.
