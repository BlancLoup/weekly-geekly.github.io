Live Rup #RuPostgres: questions and answers with Avito experts

We are actively using PostgreSQL in our work, we participate in thematic conferences a lot, hold competitions and write about administration and development. And today I want to invite you to take part in another meeting dedicated to Postgres - a live meeting on #RuPostgres Live. In the live broadcast, which will be conducted and moderated by Nicholas Postgresmen Samokhvalov, my colleagues and I will answer your questions. Details under the cut.



Experts


Your questions during the broadcast will be answered:


I, Konstantin Evteev - Leader of the AvTP OLTP-unit


Sergey Burladyan - Database Architect


Dmitry Vagin - DBA Team Lead


Possible topics


  • Dev & test environment;
  • code deployment (versioning of stored procedures) and migrations;
  • PostgreSQL major versions;
  • organization of failover in a distributed data processing system and data reconciliation after an accident;
  • data delivery to analytical subsystems;
  • organization of search results (data delivery to an external index) and issue;
  • PGHACK ;
  • loads and data volumes in Avito;
  • non-blocking counters and matvue;
  • PostgreSQL monitoring;
  • logical replication.

However, I urge you not to be limited to these topics. Questions can be sent in advance to the form , or during the broadcast on the youtube channel #RuPostgres this Thursday, October 26, starting at 18:00 Moscow time. Here is the event page at meetup.com .
