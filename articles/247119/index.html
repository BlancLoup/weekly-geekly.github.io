Top 5 best Windows Phone Store apps:


1. Minecraft: Pocket Edition

On the Windows Phone platform, the first line is taken by the mobile version of the legendary game Minecraft, which is loved by users all over the world. Minecraft Pocket Edition you need to create your own world from cubes "from scratch", and the game retains all the favorite game modes of the "big brother" - in the "Survival" mode you will need to get comfortable in an unfriendly world, find the right resources and change your environment in according to your vision, and in the ‚ÄúCreativity‚Äù mode the imagination will be your only obstacle - endless resources and the ability to fly will take care of it.



Weaves of hidden caves and mysterious jungles, small villages and dazzling megacities, endless fields and inaccessible mountain peaks - you can create anything you want in Minecraft Pocket Edition. And if you are bored with creating and exploring your own world, there is a multiplayer mode at your service that will allow you to evaluate the creations of other players and share your creative results with them.




2. Age of Empires Castle Siege

Age of Empires: Castle Siege invites you to lead your empire through the Middle Ages. Choose a civilization to your liking: among the available options are the states of Britons, Teutons, Byzantines, and Kievan Rus. Upgrade your castle, strengthen the walls and place troops to protect your empire from unwanted guests. Having strengthened, build up forces to attack neighboring cities and throw troops into battle to crush the defenses of opponents.



While sailing along the river of time, you will explore new technologies, take part in famous battles and attract famous medieval characters such as Saladin, Richard the Lionheart and Joan of Arc to your banner. Replay strong opponents and defeat the weak, earning achievements and rising up the leaderboard.



3. Cloud Raiders

In the Cloud Raiders, you will find yourself in a distant, cloudy region, where life is not nearly as serene as is commonly believed. There are thousands of flying islands in the sky, and your task is to conquer them all! But first you need to turn your personal island into an impregnable fortress - to build fortifications, equip them with powerful guns, clever traps and magical artifacts. As soon as you are sure of your invulnerability, collect a formidable army of heavenly warriors and fly to the attack!



You are waiting for grand battles with countless hordes of enemies, and insidious pirates - the most harmless of them. Carefully think over strategy and tactics to crush the enemy's army and get their untold treasures. Unite with your friends and achieve joint victories! Grab the undivided power in the heavens!



4. Afterlight

Afterlight is the perfect application for quick and easy image editing.



The combination of simple design with powerful and accurate tools will allow you to achieve the desired image in just a few seconds: you have 8 correction tools, 57 filters, 66 textures and numerous tools for working on the image.



5. Candy crush saga

Candy Crush Saga immerses players in the fabulous world of sweets. Bright design, charming characters, as well as many interesting tasks and game levels will win the sympathy of all fans of puzzles and quizzes. Your task is to put together suitable crystals and go to the next level! In Candy Crush Saga, you can play alone, or in the company of friends and loved ones, moreover - the game is free.



Candy Crush Saga consists of hundreds of game levels, each of which invites users to solve an exciting puzzle game, new ones are added weekly. And even if at first glance the tasks do not seem very difficult - it will not be so easy to move to the next level! You can follow your results and success of your friends on a special interactive scoreboard.
