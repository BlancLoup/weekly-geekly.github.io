Ideal Technopark model

In St. Petersburg this year begins the active construction of the Ingria Technopark , the first high-tech park in the city. Since some still consider Technoparks to be something like a tram park, we will try to reveal in more detail what it is and why they are being built.


We will understand the concepts
To refer to high-tech parks, there are several similar terms: science park, technology park, research park. Wikipedia leads them as equals. Their use depends, rather, on the region: in Germany - Technology park, in England - Science Park, in the USA - Research park, in France - Technopole, etc.

Are technoparks spread around the world?
According to WAINOVA Atlas of Innovation, in 1,500 countries of the world there are 1,500 technology parks and about 1,000 more innovation centers. About 350,000 companies are their residents.

The role and purpose of Technoparks
Technoparks are, above all, a locomotive, the core of the region's innovative development. The main goal of Technoparks is to increase the welfare of the region by creating the conditions for the commercialization of knowledge and technology.
Technoparks unite large technology companies and start-ups, universities and investment companies in one territory, form an active business environment and provide platforms and infrastructure for innovation activities.
Thus, Technoparks contribute to technology transfer, the creation of highly paid jobs, develop a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship.

To achieve these goals, according to the International Association of Scientific Parks IASP, Technopark:

‚Ä¢ Facilitates the creation of innovative companies through the incubation process (start-ups, spinoffs, etc.)
‚Ä¢ Provides all necessary services , including quality premises and work spaces.

Technopark Models
Technoparks today are very diverse: in size, and by type of ownership, and by specialization.

II - 80-90 years. Market Push. Companies began to think about the development of technology from universities and began to make industry technoparks. Provision of rental space and complex support services for innovative businesses.
III - 2000. Interactive "local flows" Technoparks become organizational structures.
