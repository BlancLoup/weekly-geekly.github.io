Since childhood, we are taught, who works more - he earns more. Many believe that in order to double the income, you need to get a second job. In life, everything is exactly the opposite.



When I first started my own business, I worked alone. I was looking for orders myself, I executed them myself. My earnings depended directly on the time spent in the office at work. But while executing orders, time was missed for which new customers could be found, which means money was lost. At that time, a friend joined me and the first thing we hired was a sales manager. We started with a customer search site. Finding someone who can talk on the phone and write emails is easiest. More time was freed up and the flow of orders increased at the same time.
