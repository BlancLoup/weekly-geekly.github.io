Video review tablet (ultrabook) Acer Aspire P3-171



Acer Aspire P3-171 - ultra-transformer, combining the capabilities of a tablet and an 11.6-inch laptop. It is equipped with an IPS display with a resolution of 1366x768. Devices are based on low-voltage processors Intel Core i3 or i5. The amount of RAM is from 2 to 4 GB, and for data storage drives are used with a capacity of 60 or 120 GB.

Included with the tablet is a special case with a keyboard (connecting to the tablet via Bluetooth), which allows you to use the device as an ultrabook.
