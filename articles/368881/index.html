Every seventh person on earth today will go on Facebook: the number of daily users has reached a billion

On Wednesday, November 4, Facebook published a financial report for the third quarter of 2015. Every day, more than a billion people use the social network. Mobile devices continue to be an audience growth driver.


Photo: Fool.com
