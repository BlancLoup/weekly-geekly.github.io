Siberian technology in Abu Dhabi

I once wrote here how I started the production of one relatively simple electronic toy in the USA - the story began 5 years ago. The device is a remote control with digital mirrors. Here was the original topic about the production. And today I was told that our console was used in the production of a video about Abu Dhabi. According to the author, this video collected 300,000 views on YouTube, provided him with an interview on Yahoo and a bunch of Arab TV channels, and led orders from the Discovery Channel and the BBC.



Here it is, in English. Just for the sake of everything holy, look in full screen, and with normal sound (at least external speakers, or headphones). The music was written by Vladimir Persan, a Russian from Azerbaijan.

ABU DHABI 2011 - DEFINITIVE VERSION, English intro from Beno Saradzic on Vimeo .

You know, after watching, I once again understood why we work. I can not describe this feeling, but it seems to me that helping to make the world even more beautiful gives more than all the money of this world.
