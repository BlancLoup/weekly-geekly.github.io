Google programmer made Facebook Disconnect extension

Developer Brian Kennish (Brian Kennish) from Google has released a Facebook Disconnect extension for the Chrome browser that blocks the Facebook Connect module while visiting third-party resources. This module is installed on more than a million sites .



This is especially true in connection with the recent leaks of data on users of the social network. A reasonable alternative for those who want to delete a Facebook account, but can not decide.

Brian Kennish says he made the expansion on his own initiative without any requests from the employer.
