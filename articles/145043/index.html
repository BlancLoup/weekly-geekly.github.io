Google World Wonders Project brought together wonders of the world



More than seven wonders of the world, or eight. In every corner of our planet there is something that can be considered a miracle. These are buildings, cities, and whole regions. Google has decided to bring all these wonders of the world together so that anyone can admire them. Of course, the effect of a virtual visit to the same Tower of Pisa is less than from a direct trip, but still it is interesting. The new project Google World Wonders Project will bring together (or rather, has already collected) 132 different objects and regions, from Yosemite National Park to the same Leaning Tower of Pisa.

All objects and places represented in the project are available both in the plane and in the form of a 3D model. In addition, video materials are presented, plus literally tons of information from UNESCO and other organizations involved in the preservation of territories and architectural objects.
