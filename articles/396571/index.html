Wrist airfield for drones and skirt-infinity: new work SexyCyborg



A user of Reddit with the nickname SexyCyborg recently published a message describing their new project, called the "skirt-infinity". SexyCyborg is a girl from China who likes to develop unusual electronic devices. She is also interested in the field of information security. On Geektimes already wrote about the development of the young talent: shoes with a cache , printed on a 3D printer and " Shining Skirt " with a ribbon of LEDs.

The Chinese woman has also created a wrist station for miniature drones, which allows you to carry these devices anywhere and everywhere without fear of damaging them.



The drones docking station is mounted on the wrist, and accommodates two small devices (Nano Drone). The controller for controlling the drones is mounted on the belt buckle, so that the whole system for launching the devices is always with you. The docking station is printed on the printer, as is the buckle.
