Yandex.Kit: new firmware for smartphones

And we, as a company that answers more than 250 million inquiries from Russian users a day, know a lot about such features and needs.

The built-in keyboard of Keith knows the cases of the Russian language and prompts words when entering with their account. Searching the address book understands that Alexander, Sasha and Shura are one and the same. Thanks to data from the Yandex.Reference Guide, you can search for the phones of the nearest pharmacies and cafes right in the dealer interface. This also makes it possible to determine an incoming call from an organization unknown to you, the data about which will be loaded from the cloud. And this is not all that Yandex has learned for all those years, which makes search and mobile applications in Russia.



Individual elements of China will be presented in February at the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona. In March, we will be ready to share with you the firmware that you can try on your devices.

We want to warn that it will be installed on top of the system that already exists in your smartphone without the ability to save data. Yandex.Kit is a ready-made software solution that is installed on phones or tablets during production. Therefore, we will provide the firmware only to those for whom it is important to understand how Yandex.Kit works and who are ready for this to run an experiment on their device.
