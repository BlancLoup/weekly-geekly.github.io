Rosnano will make RFID for Russia

As you know, Russian passports are equipped with radio-frequency tags (RFID-chips), as well as cards in the Moscow metro. The widespread introduction of RFID chips in retail and transport logistics systems begins. Such chips are needed by all. Today there is information that nanotechnology specialists from Rosnano are expanding production within the framework of this highly profitable project, which has already been launched at the Mikron plant. The equipment will again be borrowed from the European manufacturer of microchips STMicroelectronics.

Chip production with a 90 nm process technology on foreign equipment is what Russia considers to be nanotechnology.
