Google is starting to open up access to a new blogger design



As already reported in March, the Blogger team is now actively working on a fundamental change in the design of the service.

Recently, a new design began to gradually appear among users in the test version of Blogger - Blogger in Draft . All Draft users will have their designs updated in the next few days and weeks.

The update is not only in fresh graphics and new page design - the developers claim that they have completely rewritten the service from scratch using the latest web technologies.

And this is only the beginning of the many updates planned for Blogger this year.
