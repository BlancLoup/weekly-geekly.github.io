I did not think that a strange thing would be born in my head! But...
Everyone is familiar with the situation when in a transport or a queue you become an unwitting listener of someone else's conversation. Some even like it, some involuntarily think about the background, represent the actors ... And, of course, if there are voyeurs, there will be exhibitionists. About the strange thing : the minimum client on a PDA or smartphone, broadcasting the conversation (namely!) To the server. Participants (or, what is more natural, few people aware of recording) mark the conversation with tags (you can add in the process, but not delete it). Conversations are anonymous and unmoderated, the name sharek'a (here the term has come!) Is a randomly generated string of characters at its beginning (so as not to turn into a strange podcast).
With the help of the same minimalist client, you can listen to conversations, switching, if something does not suit you, either completely randomly or pseudo-randomly, taking into account tags.
