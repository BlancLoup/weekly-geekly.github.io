Conference "8P: Internet Marketing in Ukraine"


July 16, 2011 in Odessa, near the very blue sea, the conference " 8: Internet marketing in Ukraine " will be held. 8P - this is not a man in sunglasses, showing you the language lying on the couch. 8P is a new internet marketing formula: product, place, price, promotion, people, purchase, probe, public relations. The event will be held in the conference hall of the hotel "Odessa" (sea port). Reports will be held in three streams - the program will be announced soon. Some of the speakers are already known , and some - go through the voting phase . Speakers geography: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Ryazan, Kiev, Kharkov, Dnepropetrovsk, Odessa.

The conference is aimed at owners of small and medium-sized businesses, marketers of large companies and owners of online stores. In addition, a number of reports will be designed for industry-specific specialists.

Reports will be held in a classic format: 25 minutes for a speaker and 5 minutes for questions from the audience. In addition, a round table will be held, during which representatives of leading studios will discuss current trends in the SEO market in Ukraine.

We invite each and every habravchanin to visit us. You can not only have a great summer Saturday, but also listen to real Internet marketing gurus. A short list of speakers: Damir Khalilov, Dmitry Satin, Leonid Grokhovsky, Elizaveta Tribunskaya, Konstantin Leonovich, Sergey Bakhar, Elenka Kamskaya, Devak, Terehof. The special guest of the conference is the great and terrible Anatoly Wasserman .

The cost of tickets at the moment is 800 UAH and it will grow to 1400. To order a ticket, you must register on the site and after authorization make an order of the ticket.

Organized by 8P is Netpeak Internet Marketing Studio. The general sponsor is Promodo.
