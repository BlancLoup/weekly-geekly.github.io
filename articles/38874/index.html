BlueStone Supertooth 3 (orig. BlueAnt Supertooth 3)

Now your attention is represented by a car ( CarKit ) headset designed to make it easier for you to combine telephone conversations and control your vehicle with minimal trauma and without violating existing legislation. This miracle is fastened on a curtain from the sun, which is not far from your head and therefore provides a good sound.


So nice when you have someone to talk to ...


Like the headset described earlier, this representative of the family of blue ants loves to talk. Himself. Those. For example, when you first pair your phone with this device, a pleasant female voice will give a short introductory lecture on using the device. Everything is simple and clear.

After the first connection of the headset with your phone, the entire phone book will gradually be pumped into the memory of the headset. If the caller is in the list of downloaded contacts, the device will notify you who exactly is calling. If there is no match, simply announce the number.

As you already hope, you understand, the device does not have a screen, which adds security, because You do not need to be distracted from the road to read the information about the call. Supertooth 3 supports 6 languages ‚Äã‚Äã- among them are American English, Spanish, French, Italian, German and British English.


This device is REALLY handsfree


The only thing that can be noted about most handsfree headsets is that they do not completely free their hands . As an example - in order to answer you must click on the button. In the case of Supertooth 3, this is not the case. After the call is received and the number of the subscriber is called, you simply say "OK" and answer the call. So you do not need to take your hands off the wheel.

Easy installation



Installing a headset in the car is really indescribably simple. To attach the headset to the visor, a metal clip is used, to which, in turn, the device is attached using built-in magnets. This allows you to later easily remove the device and hoist it into place without any extra effort. The built-in battery allows the SuperZub to work up to 15 hours in talk mode and up to 800 hours in standby mode. And most importantly, really no wires! True, in case of need, not only home charging is supplied, but also a car charger.


Total


By all measures, BlueAnt Supertooth 3 can be called one of the best car headsets. Its slim sleek design, high battery life, and most importantly, full voice control makes it the undisputed leader.
