The state of e-commerce in RuNet

Aleksey Petrovsky, a senior analyst at the Begun contextual advertising service at Price.Ru (Rambler), published several slides from a report on the state of the Russian e-commerce market.



Judging by the published figures, the absolute leader in e-commerce in RuNet is the Chinese Aliexpress store. His traffic (number of visitors) exceeds the traffic of several pursuers combined.

The second and third places are followed by eBay and Ozon.
