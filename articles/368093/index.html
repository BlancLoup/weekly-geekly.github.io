Domestic igrostroy in business: arcade Creepy Road came out on Kickstarter



Russian developers make quite a few great applications, including games. There are enough examples, and even listing them all is not worth it - each of us knows at least one purely Russian toy, it does not matter for a PC, console or mobile device.

Now there is another game, called Creepy Road. Announced it last year, at the exhibition "IgroMir 2015". They wrote about it on 3dnews, Gamedis and other resources (on Geektimes they were only mentioned a couple of times in the comments). The plot of the toy is that the main character goes to his girlfriend, but in the end he finds himself in the thick of problems - there are enemies around him, a lot of angry animals (even birds look like fiend) and a rather strange environment.

What's inside?


Oh yeah, the main character's name is Flint Tracker. Well, it is immediately clear that he is a trucker, right? He is available 10 types of various weapons that need to destroy about 30 types of enemies. As usual in arcades, there are bosses at the end of each level. A total of 15 levels.



What is it like?


Developers, in their own words, have played and played quite a lot, and the first gaming experience began to accumulate since the days of Dandy and Sega. Especially memorable to the authors of Metal Slug, Battletoads, Comix Zone, 2D toys with the need to both fight and solve various tasks, all on the go, in active mode.



How was the toy made?

Gameplay


The game uses the techniques of many classic arcade toys - here you need to run, shoot, cut out, jump (including on platforms), plus look for Easter eggs - there are a lot of them.

Additional data


The toy itself is prepared for Unity, the work began almost a year ago - in April 2015. In order to accelerate the development, it was decided to go on Kickstarter , whose users supported a lot of games.

Now a tenth of the required amount is collected, which, in principle, is not bad. Work is in full swing, agreements are made with Microsoft, Sony (first Creepy Road is scheduled for release for gaming consoles), passed Steam Greenlight (back in November last year). The release of the toy is scheduled for the summer of 2016, we are waiting, sir.
