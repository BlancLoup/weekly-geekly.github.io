Yandex.Lingvo is 5 years old

On July 10, the Yandex.Lingvo project is 5 years old, according to the Yandex blog .

From July 2001 to June 2005, the project evolved from a Russian-English dictionary to a five-language one and survived even one design change, and after combining Lingvo and Encyclopedias into Yandex.Slovari, it retained its face - lingvo.yandex.ru.
