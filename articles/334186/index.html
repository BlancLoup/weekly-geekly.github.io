Programming Contest: JSDash (Intermediate Results 2)

Announcement: the term for making decisions is extended until August 17.

Thanks to everyone who has already taken part in our programming contest ! We received 39 solutions from 26 unique participants. We publish new intermediate results.

So far, instead of the names of the participants - solutions IDs. Your ID is in the automatic email you received after sending the decision. It is not forbidden in the comments to this post to disclose that such and such a decision is yours.

This time, we took the years of the appearance of Halley's comet in the last thousand years as seeds. In the final testing, the results of which will determine the winners, the numbers will be different, so it makes no sense to manually sharpen the solutions specifically for these levels.

The results of intermediate testing published on GitHub . The tables show the total results, as well as individual indicators for each of the levels. For the sake of curiosity, we also prepared tables with the number of diamonds collected, killed butterflies, chains (streaks) and the maximum length of the chain. All these indicators will not go to credit - the winner will be determined solely by the sum of points scored.

There is still time to get around the current leaders. Send your solutions!
