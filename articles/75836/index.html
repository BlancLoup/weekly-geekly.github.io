HDMI cables and other products received updated logos



Updated HDMI Adopted Trademark and Logo Usage Guidelines documents. In particular, there was a labeling of HDMI cables and other products corresponding to their tasks and capabilities.

According to the new documents, manufacturers of HDMI products will no longer be able to operate with HDMI version numbers, either in packaging or in advertising. For cables, this rule is introduced from the present time, the rest of the products should be transferred to the new labeling by 2012.
