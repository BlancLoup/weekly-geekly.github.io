New device helps to detect the cause of sleep disorders



Some people have nervous system disorders that lead to sleep disorders. In addition, third-party factors can sometimes interfere, which are not always definable. The result - fatigue in the morning, constant lack of sleep and other problems associated with the main one. Recently, the company Zeo introduced an unusual device, which, according to company representatives, should help identify sleep disorders, and establish a strong, healthy sleep.

The device was called Zeo Mobile, and is priced at $ 99. When using this device, it is necessary to put it on the head, before going to bed, by itself, and not after. During sleep, the device will read the necessary information to determine the "quality" of sleep, including the different stages of the sleep phase. The developers have provided special applications for processing the data received by the device. The data is transmitted to a mobile device (iOS or Android), and the user can clearly see what and how.



Data transmission is via wireless (Bluetooth). The idea, as you can understand, is not a bad one.
