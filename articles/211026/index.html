You like to read exciting books - love and wear a vest ...

The process of reading in the near future can be even more exciting than ever before and it is not about online books. Reading will be accompanied by brand new sensations with a vest on, which creates physical sensations based on the words read, reports 8banks portal.





We, as an electronic library , could not resist and did not write about the book project MIT'a. The project, called Sensory Literature, was created by the media laboratory at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. The reader puts on a vest-like tricky device that regulates the lighting effects, vibration, temperature and compression force to create physical sensations that reflect the environment and feelings described in the book.



Pre-programmed actions are triggered through sensors and actuators when the reader reaches a certain page. If the text describes love feelings, the waistcoat vibrates to increase the reader's heartbeat; If the text describes a scene where it is cold, the vest may, for example, lower the body temperature. The equipment includes a body compression system, a heart beat and shudder simulator, and a reader's temperature control system ‚Äî all these aspects are responsible for physical effects. 150 LEDs embedded in the cover of the book , light up depending on the action in the plot.

"This is an artifact that will undoubtedly cause a lot of discussion."
