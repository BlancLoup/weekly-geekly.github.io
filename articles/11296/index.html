Wikipedia logo has errors

Attentive visitors to Wikipedia saw two errors in the logo of the free encyclopedia.

The gaze of Japanese language experts and Devanagari (the script used in Sanskrit and some modern Indian languages) was not escaped by small inaccuracies in writing the corresponding symbols in a puzzle puzzle.

The creator of Wikipedia believes that there is nothing particularly noticeable in the errors that have been noticed, because the resource is made by ordinary users who are mistaken. By the way, the attentive Japanese reader suggests not to correct incorrect characters at all, because for the majority of the audience they have no meaning.
