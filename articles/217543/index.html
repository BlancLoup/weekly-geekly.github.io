Artem Gavrichenkov, Qrator - DDoS-attacks [Video lecture]

A couple of weeks ago, we managed to pull out on CodeFreeze with the report of Artem Gavrichenkov aka ximaera , CTO at Qrator Labs - a company that is one of the world leaders in dealing with DDoS attacks.



Actually, I called Artem to give us an overview lecture on what DDoS attacks are, what they are and how to deal with them.

More specifically:

  • History of the term. What is the difference between DoS and DDoS attacks?
  • DDoS attacks from the point of view of information security experts, risk assessment;
  • Types of attacks and their classification;
  • Counteraction for each type of attack;
  • DDoS attacks on the Internet of the future: how will IPv6, IoT and the growth of the number of network participants affect its network, security?


The lecture turned out great - more than two and a half hours. Therefore, we did not do the decoding. Before watching, stock up on popcorn and beer.



The CodeFreeze team wishes you a pleasant viewing!
