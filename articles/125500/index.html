Review of the salaries of "mobile" developers

Our company is engaged in the selection of IT-staff in many large cities of Russia and Europe. Twice a year we conduct research on the salaries of employees of IT companies in order to be aware of the slightest market changes.



In this post , a small part of the research was touched upon. In the comments, the people demanded more specific data and bread for many positions, and I promised that we would try to regularly publish data on wages in various specialties in the field of IT.

No sooner said than done! The first post is devoted to a detailed analysis of the salaries of experts in the field of mobile development. We will be guided by the data obtained during the study in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Nizhny Novgorod.

Labor market situation


After analyzing vacancies and resumes on various job portals (hh.ru, superjob.ru, itmozg.ru) in Russia on July 27, it can be concluded that demand exceeds supply:

Between the junior and the lead developer is usually the so-called "middle developer". This is a programmer with experience of more than 1.5 years. It does not require a detailed formulation of the problem, is able to independently find solutions and, accordingly, is not so attached to the lead developer.

Tips for novice applicants


We would like to give some advice to novice programmers for mobile devices who want to choose the companies in which they would like to work:
  1. Have 2-3 ready applications in the App Store or Android Market. The finished products in the portfolio will allow the employer to assess your qualifications.
  2. Participate in programming contests and developer competitions (ACM, i-Free Innovations, HTC Android Developers Contest, iAppContest, HackDay IT, etc.). This will determine you as an enterprising, ambitious and goal-oriented person.
  3. Participate in the development of commercial applications.
  4. Have a high GPA in school.
