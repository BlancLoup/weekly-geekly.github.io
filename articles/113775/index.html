3D-smartphone, tablet and other news from LG in the video

As the name implies, in 2011, the company makes a big emphasis on 3D technology. On the back of the smartphone there are two cameras for recording 3D video. But that is not all. The screen of the device allows you to play 3D-content without special glasses. And it works! True, the viewing angles when viewing these clips are very limited, and it is not known how your eyes will react to the daily use of this technology.

In addition, the phone, unexpectedly working on the not so fresh Android 2.2, is pre-installed with several adapted for 3D games. Now look at the tablet.

The LG Optimus Pad , like the Optimus 3D, has a dual-core processor and two cameras for recording 3D video. But the tablet cannot play it, but Android 3.0 Honeycomb interfaces are pleasing to the eye, which is becoming one of the main trends of the exhibition.



The internal memory of the device is as much as 32 GB, and the screen resolution of 1280x768 with a diagonal of 8.9 inches makes the image very clear.

Another interesting example of the LG stand was the Optimus Black smartphone. Unlike its counterparts, it has a simpler hardware component (if one considers a simple 1 GHz single-core processor), since the emphasis here is placed on low weight and size. The mass of the device - 109 grams, thickness - 9.2 mm. At the same time, the screen diagonal is 4 inches.



In addition, the smartphone is equipped with a couple of interesting features. The first is the Gesture Button . With it, you can navigate through the desktops, gallery or page in the browser, tilting the phone in the right direction. Another feature is the WiFi Direct technology, which allows you to combine several smartphones into one network and transfer data between them.
