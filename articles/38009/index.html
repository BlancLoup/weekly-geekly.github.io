Wireless VGA to USB connection


The gradual deliverance from wires seems to be becoming a fetish of modern civilization. Even wire-free transmission systems are being developed. But in the PC area, almost everything except this electricity can be connected without wires.


Wireless mice and keyboards are commonplace. Wireless monitors? Also already there. And what if the consumer wants to connect an additional projector or HD TV to a PC? Yes, even through the USB port? Please, this is not a problem. IOGEAR has already developed a similar system.

Supports image transfer with resolutions up to 720p. The range is up to 10 meters. Perhaps the only drawback is that only Windows XP SP2 (32 bits) and Windows Vista (32 and 64 bits) are supported. Owners of other operating systems until this luxury is not available.

The gadget will go on sale in September for $ 229.95.
