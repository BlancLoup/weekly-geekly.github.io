Megaphone - new Russian cloud operator!

Megaphone began to provide cloud servers for rent, i.e. essentially work on the IaaS model.

In my opinion this is great news for the domestic market of cloud services. This will give a significant impetus to its development.
By the way, the prices are quite affordable: the rent per hour of 100 MHz of processor time will cost customers 0.1 rubles., The use of disk space is 0.03 rubles. for 1 GB of RAM - 0.01 rubles. for 32 MB.
Servers are located in the Tier 3 data center in Samara.
