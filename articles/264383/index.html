Tox developers disassociate themselves from the Tox Foundation

Habr for more than a year forgot about the free and protected Tox messenger, they say, everything works - and okay.

Now not everything works, more precisely, everything, but somewhat differently.



It turns out that a terrible thing happened a few months ago - a certain Shaun Qureshi (also known by many pseudonyms), the head of the Tox Foundation, appropriated the foundation's money and fled to warm countries and refused to admit his guilt. With this domains 
  tox.im, toxme.se and libtoxcore.so
remain at his disposal. For a long time, the developers tried to force him to return the money and solve the matter in peace, but in mid-July they were forced to make their blog statement and transfer resources to the new domain - https://tox.chat/ - and start promotion from the beginning.

Developers apologize and promise to avoid repeating this situation in the future.

PS Do not forget to change the Tox repositories to actual ones .
