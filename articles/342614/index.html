MEGA Accelerator Finalists: Five New Ideas for Shopping Malls



Following the results of the pre-accelerator, nine projects made their way into the final part. On the example of some of them we will tell about new technologies and solutions for shopping centers.

700 people showed up for the qualifying round of MEGA Accellerator 2, which united in 275 teams. The leading cities are Moscow and St. Petersburg. We took projects in three directions: MEGA-space for meetings, MEGA - interaction with visitors and MEGA - an automated back-office, and the second was the most popular. Nine finalists showed promising projects and received admission to the internal accelerator: these are three weeks of intensive refinement and testing of the solution under the guidance of mentors and experts and, as a final, launching a pilot in MEGA and the main prize of 2 million rubles.

The projects of the finalists fit into the concept of MEGA shopping centers - a space for meetings and socializing, a place where you can get out with your whole family, for a whole day, with colleagues and friends, to relax and to have a good time. We want to introduce you to five of them.

VR in the mall: Hexa 3D content workshop


Hexa is a solution for creating 3D content using artificial intelligence algorithms. With regard to trade, it allows you to make 3D-models for various e-commerce sites.


Hexa service developed by the same company. Due to the introduction of AI algorithms, creators managed to automate and personalize content creation:

  • Increase the value of each 3D model, customizing it for each user and improving monetization.
  • Automate the creation of 3D content, replacing people with machines and reducing the amount of time, money and skill sets needed to create each 3D model.

In some subcategories, we only need 2 photos to make a 3D model and reduce the cost of creating content by 99%.

Why is it MEGA


Hexa gives you the ability to create, scale and integrate 3D content, both online and offline, on all platforms. This will potentially increase sales and attract new customers to the physical and digital space of shopping centers.

MEGA Accelerator is an opportunity for our company to gain recognition in the Russian market and to interest new online trading sites in the possibility of a qualitative improvement in the visualization of their content.

Hexa is no stranger to e-commerce. Its customers include major industry players, including Alibaba, Walmart, JD, Rakuten.

Now the company is developing at the expense of its own funds. During the existence of the business, the owners have attracted investors several times: in April 2016 - Samurai Incubate (Israel) (amount - 100 thousand dollars), in March 2017 - HTC (amount 50 thousand dollars), in August 2017 - Seed UK angel ($ 1 million).

Effective and inexpensive training: chat bot Beesender


Beesender is a program for automating communication with employees and customers using instant messengers, social networks and chatbots. The program has three packages: Customer Center, Marketing, HR Bot.

The author of the product is the Belarusian IT company Altaras, which specializes in automating the processes of working with clients in large companies.



Beesender embeds communications (for example, work with appeals, lead generation, conducting micro-trainings, polls or advertising games) into the company's business processes. The built-in designer allows you to create bots without programming, so any company employee can make a chat bot.

Why is it MEGA


At the pilot project stage, startups will implement Beesender HR Bot. This product is designed to configure chat bots for training, testing and employee surveys.

In each company there are trainings on which everyone wants to learn, and there are trainings for which people have to literally drive people. The latter are usually associated with safety issues, internal regulations and regulations. One of these MEGA trainings will be transferred to instant messengers.

Chat bot will benefit both employees and business. The first will be able to get new knowledge and take tests in a convenient format for them - in messengers, which are already used dozens of times a day. The company will be able to effectively manage the educational processes of thousands of employees: their own and tenants.

Participation in programs like MEGA Accelerator, on the one hand, helps to better understand how to develop and promote your product. The organizer of the program, the company GVA, gives cool mentors and experts, to get to which is just so almost unreal. On the other hand, this is a great opportunity to enter the Russian market. By and large, both the MEGA shopping centers themselves and their tenants are our target customers.

Beesender sales have just begun. Among potential clients of a startup are big financial, retail and telecom companies. The target region for 2017-2018 is Russian-speaking countries. Beesender is already used by several banks, retail chains, car dealerships and an online store.

Since its opening, the project has been funded all this time at the expense of its own funds.

Automate HR: Vera Robot Recruiter


Robot Vera is an automated personnel officer. The project emerged as a result of the pivot of the first startup of the team - Stafory, which was a marketplace with a commission monetization scheme, where employer companies place vacancies, and freelance recruiters select personnel. In May 2016, one large retailer placed an order for the selection of 100 storekeepers - but no one took it up, because it looked too time-consuming. Then the founders of the startup had the idea to automate basic and routine tasks: searching for resumes and calling candidates to save recruiters time. The result was a robot Faith.



Vera handles new vacancies from the service: looking for a resume on job sites, calling the candidates and conducting an initial interview with them. If necessary - Vera can conduct a video interview. Thus, the hr-manager of the company receives feedback only from targeted and interested candidates, significantly reducing the time and costs of the company in the initial selection.

Why is it MEGA


As part of the test, Vera will search for staff on work sites with outgoing calls, as she does in the standard way. After the first test results, it will be decided what the robot will eventually do.

MEGA and IKEA are large players who have expressed the need for staff recruitment: there are about 100 current vacancies on the career portal. For Robot Vera MEGA, Accelerator is an opportunity to launch a pilot project and promptly assist the company in finding personnel. Shortage of employees is an acute problem for a business, which causes it to incur operating losses.

Now the main customers of Robot Vera are large companies that constantly need employees, such as MTS, Rostelecom, Burger King, PepsiCo and others. In addition to them, Vera successfully works on the side of medium business: she was looking for both programmers, eyelash extension specialists, and accountants.

The creators of the Robot Faith attracted third-party investments: the Internet Initiatives Development Fund invested 67 million rubles in the project.

Where to go: Altair VR virtual planetarium
