Now Xmarks and under Google Chrome



In everyday work and just browsing the vast expanses of the powerful Internet, I almost always began to use Google Chrome. Googled in full, so to speak. They bribed, like many, at least some extensions, opening speed, compact interface and, as a last resort, the synchronization of bookmarks between Chrome through a Google account.

But, anyway, Firefox is still in demand, and sometimes generally indispensable browser for many tasks. I'm not talking about testing sites in different browsers, which is also a duty to do, but I mean a lot of unique lotions, which are just not there under Chrome. And therefore, until I completely tied up with Ognelis, I looked in the direction of the Chrome Bookmarks API, so that I could create something of my own for the synchronization of bookmarks between Chrome and Faer. But, as is usually the case: laziness, lack of time, laziness again ...

How pleasantly I was surprised when I discovered that my favorite Xmarks, which I was using under Fox, is testing my own extens Xmarks for Chrome Alpha !!!



You can read and subscribe to the addon testing on the beta.xmarks.com/welcome page (entrance for Xmarks users)

I do not know why Alpha, but right after I installed and logged into my Xmarks account in Chrom, everything worked fine. Having opened two browsers at once and having made a bookmark in one, I found it automatically appearing in the other (the machine seems to not always work).

In short, my joy has no limits and now I can stretch a little more using two browsers and using the same bookmarks in both, until all the dampness and bugs in Chrome are finally fixed. What and recommend to you.

Warning: the plugin works only under Chrome development channel release version 3.0.196.0 or later.
