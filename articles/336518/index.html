What to expect from Apple: the main thing about iOS, macOS, Watch 3, iPhone 8



Apple's capitalization is approaching a trillion dollars, but not everyone approves of the path chosen by the company. Critics point to the lack of a wow-effect in recent releases, the stagnation of technology, the copying of ideas from competitors who have come a long way in the technological race. It is possible that part of the claims can be dispelled very soon, when the Yabloko employees will begin the presentation of several long-awaited products.

The main thing that Apple will enjoy this fall is the iPhone 8. After lengthening the update cycle of the iPhone and iPad, user expectations have increased. But not only the iPhone is interesting the upcoming season. Smart HomePod column, Apple Watch 3, macOS High Sierra - all these new items should appear in the very near future. The amount of information is multiplying (both reliable and in the form of rumors), but this collection contains only data confirmed from several independent sources.

New Camera, Augmented Reality and Face ID


The iPhone 8 camera is equipped with a VSCEL touch laser sensor (‚Äúsurface-emitting laser with a vertical resonator‚Äù), which allows determining the depth of a scene with high accuracy. This technology will improve the quality of the work of augmented reality systems and will allow for more accurate autofocus for photos.

Many thought that Apple would embed a fingerprint scanner into the screen, but the company decided to completely abandon the Touch ID and Home button in favor of Pearl ID (scanning the iris of the eye and face). The function will allow you to unlock the smartphone, as well as conduct transactions.

According to another leak , the iPhone 8 will shoot 4K video at 60 frames per second from both cameras. Shooting will be conducted in the new HEVC format, in which the file size will be halved compared to the files in the iPhone 7.

macOS High Sierra




macOS High Sierra has already been announced, but will be available for download in the fall. By the way, as partners with Apple, we had access to the prototype system in advance, which allowed us to incorporate High Sierra support into the latest version of Parallels Desktop 13 .

The update continues the Snow Leopard and Mountain Lion line, mainly adding performance enhancements to the system thanks to the new Apple File System (APFS) file system.
APFS is optimized to work with large-volume files, provides data preservation in case of power outages or system failures, and also has built-in encryption (it doesn‚Äôt disclose which one yet).

High Sierra will have an effective HEVC video compression format (H.265 helps compress video more than the previous codec, not inferior in quality) and a new platform for working with Metal 2 graphics, supporting interaction with external graphics cards. Metal 2 can be used for machine learning in speech recognition, computer vision and other areas of the development of artificial intelligence.

High Sierra supports the Unreal Engine, Steam VR, and VR virtual reality game engines, as well as the HTC Vive virtual reality glasses.

There are many other less noticeable changes. So, the Safari browser will automatically block auto-play videos and banner ads, and the Photos application will safely synchronize face recognition data within the Apple ecosystem using iCloud.

Apple Watch 3




Apple Watch 3 can show along with the iPhone 8, but so far it is necessary to collect the available information bit by bit. The most important of the innovations of the smart watch is the presence of an LTE modem that allows the use of a virtual card of individual operators. Thus, Apple Watch 3 works as a standalone device, without being tied to a smartphone.

A new dial with an assistant will appear in watchOS 4, with the latest information displayed on it: traffic jams, weather, tasks for the day and so on. Music from Apple Music will be automatically saved to your watch so that you can listen to it through wireless headphones.
