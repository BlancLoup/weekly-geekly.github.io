HaiPad M702T - Tablet / Media Center

good day

Today I want to describe my feelings from using the HaiPad M702T.
The main reasons why it makes sense to focus on this device are its price, aluminum housing, full-sized USB port, multi-touch screen and HDMI in combination with a remote control.

Specifications


Operating system: Android 2.1
Processor / Chipset: ARM Telechip TCC8902 720 MHz
RAM: 256 MB
Built-in memory: 4 GB
Memory card support: microSD
Wi-Fi support: Yes
Bluetooth support: No
Mobile Internet: No
Active recharging time: 3 hours
Battery Capacity: 2600 mAh
Diagonal: 7 '
Screen Resolution: 800x480
Screen Type: LED
Touchscreen: Resistive Touchscreen
Multitouch: Yes
Rear camera: No
Front camera: 0.3 MP
GPS receiver: No
G-sensor: Yes
Connecting external devices: USB
TV / Monitor connection : HDMI
Audio / headphone output: 3.5 mm
Dimensions (LxWxD): 232x160x70 mm
Weight: 644 g

Contents of delivery









The kit includes the device itself, instructions in English, charging + adapter to Orthodox sockets, USB data cable, headphones and remote control + battery for it.

Everything is neatly packed and decently looks, does not smell, does not stain hands :)

Ergonomics







In terms of ergonomics, the device was able to hold in the hands of a comfortable and pleasant. The aluminum case gives the tablet an extra charm.



Separately, I want to note the presence of hardkey buttons: back, menu and home. In my opinion this is a big plus.

Interfaces





In this regard, too, everything is OK. On the side panel are, a charging connector, a microphone hole (not everyone knows, there is a reset button in this hole), a headphone jack, mini HDMI, mini USB, a slot for microSD cards and a USB port.

USB


I connected a hub to USB and through it a mouse, keyboard, USB flash drive and external screw - everything is ok.
3G whistles are not tested, the following dongles are announced: Huawei E1750 (WCDMA), Huawei E1550 (WCDMA), Huawei E1752 (WCDMA), Huawei EC1261 (EVDO), HSDPA7.2M (WCDMA), Vodafone K3565 Rev2 (WCDMA), ZTE (TDSC) ). From the forums it is clear that people successfully use.

Monitor





The color rendition and viewing angles in my opinion are quite acceptable, they have seen and worse.

We should also focus on the sensory component of the screen. In my memory, this is the first resistive touchscreen with multitouch support, to be more precise, dualtach. To use this property you need to adjust. The person who is trying to use this multitouch for the first time has a strong desire to throw the tablet with all his strength against the wall.

In general, the responsiveness of the sensor is quite acceptable (for a resistive touch).

Software part



System


Although the Chinese wrote wherever they could Android 2.2, it did not, and in fact is Android 2.1. As I understand it 2.2 and will not.
It should be noted that firmware updates periodically come out and make the device really work better.

There is a Chinese launcher preinstalled on the device, the removal of which leads to the death of the firmware, be careful, in the application manager it looks like a yellow house.

Root on the device is easy to get using z4root.

Games


You can play on the device, but for some reason, the default firmware contains video driver curves (for example, artifacts in angry birds from there).

Media Center



HDMI


HDMI works well, the sound transmits and the picture does not cut. To activate, you need to select video out in the video and audio settings and there HDMI, after that it broadcasts everything that happens on the screen to the TV (which is very good, the owners of fytouch II should understand me).

To connect, you need a mini-HDMI to HDMI cable. It is not included.

Remote controller




On the remote there are the following buttons: home, menu, ok / play, stop / back, directional arrows and volume control buttons.

Managing the console without looking up from the cherished sofa will not work, the console does not work in all programs and not even in all interfaces of the system, which makes it useless fucking. I used a radio mouse for its intended purpose.

Video


Vidio files from microSD card plays well and smoothly. Flush on it and does not smell. Youtube is spinning well, you can watch videos from VKontakte too (VK media to help). Contrary to my expectations, SPB TV and other programs of this plan did not work.
