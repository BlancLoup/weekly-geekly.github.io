Russian AI Cup 2015: race for survival for programmers



We are pleased to announce the start of the next International Artificial Intelligence Programming Championship - Russian AI Cup . This time the championship was named CodeRacing. Braves who dare to take part will have to program artificial intelligence to control a racing car. But he will not only have to play the role of a driver, but also simultaneously shoot his rivals. In the "game" world, there are four types of units: codemobiles, shells, bonuses and fuel oil puddles. The most "dirty" techniques on the track will only be encouraged: you can push other people's cars, damage them and break.

You are free to choose any of these languages: C ++, Java, C #, Python, Ruby or Pascal.

Qualifying selection began in the Sandbox on November 16. The participants who showed the best results will be able to continue the game in the next rounds of the championship, more complex and exciting. You can polish the skill of your AI for the entire championship.

Championship winners will receive the following prizes:

  • 1st place: MacBook Pro;
  • 2-3 places: MacBook Air;
  • 4-6 places: iPad Air 2;
  • 60 participants in the final: winners sweatshirt;
  • 360 participants of the second round: branded T-shirt.

Also prizes from our partners Western Digital and Kingston will be awarded to the winners in the sandbox. 1-3 place: external hard drive My Passport Ultra 500 GB from WD, 4-6 place: SSD Now V300 120 GB from Kingston.

Last year, the participants of the Russian AI Cup "played" in hockey. The winner was Nikolai Vabishchevich from Moscow. A total of 1,584 programmers from 17 countries took part in the championship.
