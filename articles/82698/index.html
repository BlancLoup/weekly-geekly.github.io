At the end of March 2010, the first in Russia conference on the Zend Framework will be held in St. Petersburg


The reception of theses on the planned reports has recently ended. It is noteworthy that the list of speakers included the leading members of the Russian-speaking community ZF, the most experienced specialists and one of the creators of the framework personally!

Participation in the conference is planned to be made free or at a low cost. At the moment, pre-registration for the conference is already open! To solve a number of questions, it is very important for the organizers to understand now the approximate number of people who can attend the conference or in some other way take part in it.

Each forthcoming report can be discussed on the forum ZF-community. Your opinion is very important for authors!
