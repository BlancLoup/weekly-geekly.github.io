Statistics


All-Russian State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company and its controlled media in their arguments are based on the myth that VKontakte video service is the main reason for the popularity of the resource. Statistics do not confirm this. Every day VKontakte is loaded and sent:
  • 100,000,000 private messages;
  • 25,000,000 wall posts;
  • 12,000,000 new photos;
  • 100,000 videos;
  • 100,000 audio recordings.
Video pages are viewed 50 times less frequently than photo pages. It is clear that viewing one video takes more time than viewing one photo (the average length of a VC video is 4:12). However, please note that viewing an album with 200 photos and 500 comments loaded by Ajax is also considered one viewing.

In general, video pages account for about 3% of user activity.
