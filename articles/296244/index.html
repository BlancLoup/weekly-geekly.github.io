How professionals work. Anna Artamonova, Vice-President of Mail.ru Group

The heading "How Professionals Work" continues to acquaint the readers of "Megamind" with the working habits of various specialists in the IT market.

Anna Artamonova is another professional, as they say, widely known in certain circles. Throughout her adult life, Anna worked at Internet companies, in the late nineties and early zero having worked as a columnist for the online newspaper Deadline, and a little later as an editor for Webrating and Outdoor.

In 2003, Anna was appointed vice-president of Mail.ru Group, initially engaged in marketing and PR of the entire company, and now she is the head of the mail and Mail.ru portal.

Current Location:
Moscow, Mail.Ru Group office

Current place of work:
Mail.Ru Group, Vice-President, Head of UJ ( ed.: Business-unit ) E-Mail & Portal

Education:
Journalism ( ed.: Institute of Journalism and Literary Creativity )

One word that best describes how you work:
Interesting

9-10

Without any applications, programs, utilities you can not live?
I thought and decided that I can live without any programs and utilities. What I use regularly: Mail.Ru Mail and Mail.Ru Cloud, myMail, Facebook, Instagram, Google Maps, Evernote, Google Translate, Urban Dictionary, Litres Listen! and Bookmate, as well as Appstatic.

And, I remembered one utility, without which it is difficult to live - this is the creator of quick screenshots (with the addition of arrows, text, etc., and pouring into the cloud). I use daily.

Which life hack helps you save time?
You need to realize that decision-making actually takes a lot of time, if you do it at least weighed a little. The adoption of not the most important decisions can be delegated.

I did not get accustomed to specialized Tudu managers, I use Evernote and the built-in Notes in the iPhone for all occasions - both for storing information and for making plans.
