With the Internet - TROUBLE

Today, from 8 am Moscow time. NOT DELEGATED status was set for all domains in .ru, .su registered through RU-CENTER

The reason for this failure is still unknown, but this leads to the fact that about a million second-level domains were unavailable. Currently, they can still be "visible" through the browser due to the presence of the query cache in the DNS servers, but in the event of a prolonged failure, the number of inaccessible sites will only increase.

There are no reasons for panic, life goes on, but it may take up to several days for providers to re-update zones in DNS servers.
