The Linux Foundation introduced functionality for Linux.com


After the Linux.com domain was purchased in early March, The Linux Foundation finally presented a list of features that will be implemented in the new portal.

Dell's Ideastformer Response - Ideaforge

Allows users to share interesting ideas (all about / for Linux), as well as to hold their discussion and vote.

News 2.0 (Aggregation of news on the principle of Digg.com)

Regional Events Calendar

Monthly calendar of regional events such as LinuxFest, conventions, LUG meetings. It will also be possible to send notifications to parties interested in specified events.

Regional support system

Professionals can register based on their geographic location. And if someone in this region needs support, he will simply find the closest ones and arrange a meeting.

Single "application store" Linux / FOSS

On Linux.com, all kinds of applications will be offered, not just business lines. Everyone will be able to register their project with a detailed description, links to the project site, etc.

Bloggers community.

Everyone will be able to start a blog with % username% .linux.com

"The best app of the week"

According to the results of user voting. There will also be widgets for third-party sites.
