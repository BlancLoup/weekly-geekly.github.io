New line of LED monitors Samsung CA7 and CA5

At the Forum for the CIS countries held in Budapest (and a little earlier at CES) we showed one of our new products - LED monitors of the CA7 and CA5 series. This is a universal solution for those who work on a laptop, but prefers to be able to connect it to a large monitor in the workplace.

Monitors are equipped with a proprietary docking station Central Station, which serves as a universal multi-functional device for displaying information.



The algorithm of the system is extremely simple: a monitor with devices connected to it communicates with a laptop or PC via a wireless (on CA750 models) or wired (on CA550 models) connections. To automatically connect to the docking station, a user with a laptop just needs to appear in the range of the CA750 monitor, which is at least 1.5 m. In addition to this, the Auto Configuration technology automatically adjusts the optimal resolution to display information from the laptop.

Another advantage of CA7 and CA5 that you should pay attention to is the ability to almost completely rid your laptop of unnecessary wires. The docking stations of the 7th and 5th series monitors are equipped with a set of connectors, without which no serious multimedia product can do without nowadays: this is the notorious HDMI, and the latest generation USB 3.0, and, of course, Ethernet / LAN. All this allows you to easily connect a variety of devices, including joysticks, flash drives, cameras and many others. The ultra-fast USB 3.0 interface makes it possible to transfer data 10 times faster than standard USB 2.0, and also to charge mobile devices twice as fast.



LED monitors CA7 and CA5 are available in two versions - with a screen diagonal of 23 and 27 inches. A weighty argument in favor of the new product is a Full HD image with a resolution of 1920x1080 and a record low response time - just 2 milliseconds.

Well, for dessert: the proprietary Mega DCR technology boosts the contrast level to record levels - 1,000,000: 1, allowing you to fully enjoy the depth and brightness of colors.
