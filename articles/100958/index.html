Towards the end of the apple year

Against the backdrop of a stunning increase in sales of Apple iPad, the results of the next fiscal quarter of Apple do not seem too unexpected. The unprecedented demand for iPads allowed Apple to rank third in terms of sales of portable computers. Behind there are such market leaders as Dell, HP and Lenovo. The fiscal year will end only on September 1, until this point the company will still have time to sell devices for several billion more.


Last quarter, the company earned $ 15.8 billion. This is almost 2 times more than in 2009. Only Taiwanese company Acer can boast a large increase in sales - behind it is first place in the ranking. Recently launched the last fiscal quarter, during which Apple can reach the amount of $ 20 billion. In late August - early September, companies always sell more than in previous summer months.
