Black Onyx Boox A60

In April of this year, I presented to you the Onyx Boox 60 e-book reader. Half a year has passed and it is time to show you the updated version of this gadget. So, meet, Onyx Boox A60.




In fact, this model has few fundamental differences from the previous one. But the changes that were made can not but rejoice. As a person who used both devices, I can say that the A60 is more pleasant to hold. The main addition was the update menu. Now it is more structured and intuitive. Also, the design has changed somewhat (for example, the volume button has become smaller). The cover that comes in the delivery has become more pleasant to the touch because of the rough material. On top appeared rezinochka, protecting the book from falling out of the cover. And the back is now made of velvet, and not of leather, which contributes to fewer scratches on the device.

But, nevertheless, I want to make a full review, and not just to describe the differences. So, let's start in order and analyze everything on the shelves.

General view




So, before our eyes a thin black tablet. Immediately I liked the fact that matte, although it leaves imprints on itself, but still, they are less visible than on gloss. Pretty elegant and stylish. It lies comfortably in your hand, especially in a case that comes standard.

Incidentally, the kit is not a lot of accessories.
  • Onyx Boox A60
  • USB cable
  • Power adapter
  • Case




But this list is enough. The cover, as already mentioned, is leather, with a velvet insert inside (protection from scratches). It has a very comfortable velcro, which allows you to fix the cover of the cover in the closed and open state.

Appearance




This is not to say that this e-book differs much from its fellows. No, everything is basically the same. E-ink screen, about which you probably know more than me. Control buttons, connectors, and more. Perhaps one of the distinguishing features of this model is the presence of a touch screen, which can be controlled using the stylus.



As you can see, on the front of the book is a screen, a charging indicator and a control unit, which has 9 control buttons (the silver part is also a button).



On the back, perhaps, there is nothing remarkable, except for the connector for the stylus and the "hole" Reset.



On the right side, in contrast to the pristine left, conveniently located switch Wi-Fi. There is a slot in the case for it, so there won't be any difficulties.

Add. facts and results





The battery in my active use mode (reading, listening to music) sat down for about 6 days. I think this is quite normal. With Wi-Fi on, it worked for 2.5 days.

Pleased with the possibility of reading .pdf and .djvu. Often you have to read instructions or magazines in these formats.

Charges via USB from the network and from the computer.

The book has built-in dictionaries. They are not yet implemented very well, I hope it will be better. But in principle, to translate a few words in order to understand the meaning of the text is real.

I also liked the fact that firmware updates are released periodically and can be downloaded directly on the manufacturer's website .

In general, I am glad that the manufacturers took into account all the flaws and made this book better compared to the previous model . In fact, now I have nothing to complain about. From the previous version there was only one minus - this is the price. And even then, I think that for a product of this quality it is not very overpriced. In general, I was pleased. Now the case is black, not easily soiled, the book does not fall out of it. All glitches in the software removed. The product is pleasant to use, there is no inconvenience. Very much, by the way, helps the stylus when scrolling through web-pages, although I habitually flipped through books with hardkey buttons. The only thing that still strains, and it is unlikely to change in the near future - this is a long update of the E-ink screen. That is, during the transition, you lose about 0.5-1 second. Summing up, I want to say that Onyx Boox A60 is an ergonomic beautiful e-book that will save you time on the road and in your home.
