Concept of depreciation


A person, like any object of the production process, is a ‚Äúthing‚Äù that wears off. Moreover, the useful life is officially determined (by the state) - this is the retirement age. In accounting there is a concept - depreciation .
So, if to speak in the language of accounting, then time for rest and leisure is the means of depreciation of a person. To understand what will happen if you omit this factor, consider the following example from real economic practice.

You were given a machine, announcing that the guaranteed useful life will be 1 year. Then the production capacity will be reduced. Even under the condition that you properly cared for the machine, it still fails over time, it will need to be replaced with a new one or it will be necessary to make a major overhaul.

Accordingly, if you do not take into account in the pricing (cost) the amount of depreciation deductions, and you do not deduct it from the profit, then after some time you will literally find yourself at the bottom of the trough.
