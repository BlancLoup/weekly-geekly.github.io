Expression 4 will be launched on June 7th!



On Monday, June 7, Microsoft during the event, Internet Week will launch the fourth version of its package for designers and developers Expression Studio 4 .

The package includes the following components:

- Expression Web - site editor and HTML + SuperPreview for simultaneous testing of markup in different browsers;
- Expression Blend - editor of WPF and Silverlight applications + SketchFlow - a means of prototyping a new generation;
- Expression Design - editor of vector and growth graphics;
- Expression Encoder - video editor (VC-1, H.264 / MPEG-4 AVC)

The event will be hosted by Bill Buxton - the famous designer.
