New professional standards from AP KIT and the Ministry of Communications

On September 19 of this year, 4 new professional IT standards , which are being developed under the auspices of the Association of Computer and Information Technology Enterprises (AP KIT) and the Ministry of Communications and Mass Communications (Ministry of Communications), were presented for discussion.

New professional standards cover the following types of work:
  • System programmer
  • High Performance Computing Programmer
  • Distributed Computing Specialist
  • Technical Documentation Specialist (Technical Writer)

The purpose of this message is not to analyze or criticize the above standards, but to draw the attention of the habrasoobshchestva to the subject of the development of professional standards in our country. Those interested in analyzing the content of professional standards can send their comments and suggestions for their refinement through the AP KIT website until October 12. Already approved standards are available in the relevant section of the Association website.
