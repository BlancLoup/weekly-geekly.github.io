Totals Just For Fun Weekend Programming



A person always has some small ideas that would make his life more comfortable and happier. Usually there is no time for such ideas, but having gathered my strength, I created a question in qa about helping programmers and within a month three small projects were implemented that made my life better.

Everything was Just For Fun, no money, pure altruism, except that on request I gave out invites to programmers in order to improve the community of habrabra.

First idea

Write a script to monitor new work on the American freelancer exchange odesk.com with an alert on the email. Since when applying for a job it is very important to what account you submitted (and the first person who receives the order most often receives an order), the service can be very useful for freelancers from Odessa. Probably worth mentioning why just do not read the rss, unfortunately the work with a slight delay gets into the rss, which is unacceptable for a quick bid to work. Several programmers got the idea, implemented only smileonl . And over the weekend he managed to give birth to the www.odkeeper.com service, where you can subscribe to updates to sections with work on odesk.

Second idea

It was very old and its essence was in comparing boobs and a set of interesting statistics. Here a good pythonist was successfully caught, over the weekend he scribbled sravnisiski.com service on python + mysql. Enjoy everything for fun.

Third idea

Useful to those who read Habra through rss. Very often, articles are minus and the authors are afraid of karma rating hide them in drafts, but sometimes they are simply deleted. On such a case, the programmer Dmitry made a parser of all articles of the habr in a simple wordpress blog. Having lost the required article, it is enough to savepearlharbor.com to enter its title or number into the search box and you will receive the article you need. Source codes of a parcer are on githab if who wants the copy of a habr.
