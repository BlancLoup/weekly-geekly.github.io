What we dream and what we fear: the result of the analysis of 3 million publications over the past 10 years

What spheres of progress cause humanity enthusiasm, and what fears and fears? Which countries are optimistic about this or that technology, and which - with pessimism? ABBYY specialists analyzed the base of three million materials of scientific, state, public and value reflection development in the media for the period 2005 - 2015.

Mankind is waiting for a cure for cancer and is interested in space, discusses the intervention in the human genome and is not sure of the security of his mind and brain.



According to the study , humanity is consistently concerned about the technological challenges associated with health and medicine, education, the impact of technology on culture and values, the role of state regulation of science and technology. Compared to 2005, mankind has twice become more concerned about how digital technologies are changing our lives.

The main fears of mankind are the inability of science and medicine to defeat a number of diseases, including cancer and tuberculosis , and the inability to eliminate the risks of war and terrorism. If Canada, the United Kingdom and the Netherlands are focused on discussing the risks and problems related to technology, then Russia, Switzerland and Singapore, on the contrary, see prospects and innovations in technology. Russia, China and Asian countries are discussing the prospects and possibilities of robotics. The European Union is focused on threats and risks, people are worried about possible unemployment. In every fourth text, the issue of unemployment is adjacent to the development of robotics .

Experts expected to see a lot of attention to the topic of artificial intelligence , but the results surprised them: only 0.2% of the total array of publications are devoted to this topic. Most often, artificial intelligence is spoken in the USA, Japan, Korea, and Singapore.

Humanity is extremely unsure about the security of its consciousness and brain: 1.3% of the total base of articles is devoted to the risks that technological explosion carries to our consciousness.

The interest of scientists to the problem of GMOs dies away after a peak in 2012. To feed a multi-billion population is more important than doubts, now we are not afraid to create modified tomatoes. On the contrary, one of the fastest growing calls to mention is the intervention in the life of a person of technologies of genetic modification, and specifically of human genetic editing . The concern of scientists with this topic has doubled around the world in seven years.

The state and its function in the innovation environment causes irritation in a bizarre set of countries: here the United States, Great Britain, Russia, India and Iran. In China and Korea, the state as a participant in innovative development is neutral, while in Israel and Japan they are inclined to believe in its smart power in matters of progress.

The security threats associated with the risks of war and terror have both the highest rating and the steadily growing momentum of interest.

Interest in space is huge - 0.43% of all texts are devoted to it. This is 2.5 times the total number of texts about cancer drugs. The technological challenges associated with the health industry are 2% of the total amount of information analyzed.
