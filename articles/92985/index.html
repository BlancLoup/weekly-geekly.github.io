ViewSonic 3D projector, do you need it?



3D itself is not at all a terrible technology, but the prices for 3D monitors are so scary. Especially on large format. In this regard, ViewSonic proposed an alternative - an inexpensive projector that can provide 120 hertz scan.

The model bears the PJD6531w index and has non-record characteristics: light output 3000 ANSI Lumen, contrast 3000: 1, resolution 1280x720 pixels (720p).

The main plus of the projector is the price. ViewSonic offers a device for $ 700. Thus, if you need a projector in your house, and at the same time the toad is stifling (absolutely fair, I note) to overpay for a 3D monitor, you can consider it as an alternative. Although I would wait for the displays themselves to fall in price.
