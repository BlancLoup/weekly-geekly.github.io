OpenCD 12_2010

The Nizhny Novgorod Group of Linux Users in the light of the transition of the schools of the Nizhny Novgorod region to the PSPD, has released a regular release of a disk with a set of free software for Windows - OpenCD. This disk is perfect for anyone who needs after the installation of the system "charge" your computer for all occasions. The disc contains both a complete office suite supporting all modern office document formats, as well as the most popular and secure web browser, email client, raster graphics editor, vector graphics editor, publishing system and other applications for almost any kind of task.

Among other programs on the disk:

  • OpenOffice 3.2.1 (InfraResource with JRE) - a modern office suite that supports all popular office document formats
  • FireFox - a modern, fast and secure web browser.
  • ThunderBird - email client for receiving and sending messages
  • Celestia - a solar system and galaxy milky way simulator
  • Stellarium - virtual planetarium
  • SciLab - analogue of MathLab, the system of algebraic calculations
  • GIMP - raster graphics editor
  • InkScape - vector editor
  • Blender - a 3D modeling and animation environment
  • ScriBus Publishing System
  • Audacity is an audio editor that allows you to quickly process a sound product.
  • AviDemux - fast and reliable video editor that supports a large number of video formats


Also on the disc contains methodological materials on the office suite and packages, raster, vector and 3D graphics.
