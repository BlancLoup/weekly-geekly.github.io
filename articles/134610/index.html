Siclum supports DOU STARTUP MIXER

On December 16-18, DOU STARTUP MIXER will be held in our cozy office on the 20th floor with the name SkyPoint and a picturesque view of Kiev .

We decided to help conduct DOU STARTUP MIXER , not only by tradition, but also because it is an event where every start-up entrepreneur can understand the rules of a start-up business, create a team and start working on a project or idea. In addition, start-up entrepreneurs, programmers, social media specialists and ideological inspirers will be able to find potential partners for joint projects, or simply valuable experiences and acquaintances.

The participants of DOU STARTUP MIXER are faced with the task of creating a start-up in three days, while the team can be created on the spot or come with your own.

Mentors from already established entrepreneurs and a jury of professional investors will assist the participants in the work on the conceived project.

As planned by the organizers, the task of DOU STARTUP MIXER is to introduce as many people as possible who can create new cool projects.

To participate in DOU STARTUP MIXER, the organizers invite:
- programmers front-end, mobile, server-side
- interface designers and artists
- marketing and social media professionals
- people with ideas
- hustlers !

To submit your idea on DOU STARTUP MIXER you need to make a one-page presentation of the idea and send it to the organizers . This visual presentation will be shown behind the speaker during the pitch.

Other details of the program and details of the event can be found on the website of the organizers.

About Siclum Company:
We always try to support and organize meetings, camps and other events of various professional development communities. For example, Chrome Hackathon & Contest or Alt.NET meet-up in Kiev, OpenTalk seminar in Kharkov, RubyShift in Dnepropetrovsk and many other events in cities of Ukraine, as well as in Belarus (for example, in Minsk )
