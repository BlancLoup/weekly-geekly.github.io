The updated FXI Cotton Candy mini-computer will go on sale this summer.



On Habr√©, the FXI Cotton Candy mini-computer was already mentioned , which since the first February show of the audience at Mobile World Congress has received a number of improvements and new features. The Norwegian company FXI is already accepting pre-orders for Cotton Candy at a price of $ 199, promising to release the device by the end of this month, while release around the world is expected by the end of summer.

In fact, the FXI CC mini-computer is a compact (size can be estimated in the photo, 7.5 cm) device, which the manufacturing company calls as hardware characteristics:

  • 2-core ARM processor (Cortex-A9) with a frequency of 1.2 GHz
  • 4-core graphics ARM Mali-400MP
  • 1 GB DRAM
  • HDMI (up to 1080p), USB 2.0, microUSB
  • microSD cards up to 64 GB
  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth


As operating systems, Cotton Candy supports Android 4.0 and Ubuntu (after purchasing the device, you will need to register on the site and choose which system to install). Not only that, there are also built-in virtualization clients for Windows, Linux and MacOS.
