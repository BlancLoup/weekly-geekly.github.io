Cubescape - make a 3D-pixelart masterpiece with your own hands

Cubescape is a simple web application that provides tools for creating 3D pixeart works of a small area. In fact, you can only put cubes of different colors on top of one another, as well as blow them up. The whole process of building is remembered and then it can be played. In general, the thing is not weak (= there are just masterpieces, such as this Lenin or the Firefox logo.
