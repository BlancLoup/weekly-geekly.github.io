Presented by The Daily - media created for iPad


The Daily is a new periodical created by News Corporation specifically for the iPad. The new edition will contain daily news in digital format and include photos and high resolution videos, panoramas.


The first issue is devoted to Egypt in connection with all the well-known situation in this country.



In addition to multimedia content edition integrated with social networks. In addition to the already familiar share-buttons from facebook and twitter, a connection with Twitter has been made a little deeper. In some articles, in which we are talking about a person, it can be embedded in the broadcast of his messages on Twitter. The very same material is an html5-page, which can be shared via the Internet in the social. networks - by clicking on the link you can read the information through the website The Daily, but not all pages.



For sports fans, you can see not only the results of the latest game teams, but also photos from them.



Judging by the broadcast on The Daily site - the presentation itself was rather short. That part, where exactly showed how it looks on the iPad. Then there were questions, during the answers to which it turned out that the creators relied on the popularity of the publication. The cost of creating a publication was quite low due to the lack of printing and distribution of the publication.

So far this is an exclusive for Apple. As soon as other tablets and their manufacturers stand on their feet as firmly as Apple with the iPad stands on its own - the publishers plan to distribute the publication on them.

As the name implies, the publication will be daily. 1 Issue will cost 14 cents, weekly subscription - 99 cents. Today, February 2, at 20:00 Moscow time, the publication should appear on the App Store
