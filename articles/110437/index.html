Idea:

Autumn 2009, the idea to make a site for sites and their owners was born in our small company. As usual, it all started with an idea, so that the idea began to be realized, we took paper and a pencil and began to write and draw. As we know, the process of presenting an idea in writing is a sobering idea, but not in our case.

Actually the idea was to create a fabulous map on which each site owner can place their home. The house, which is a business card of the site and its owner.
