BackTrack 4 Final Released

BackTrack 4 Final is finally released . Along with the release you are waiting for great news, improvements and innovations. BackTrack 4 has come a long, thorny path from the beta version last year. We decided to postpone the release of the final version until it was brought to perfection in every aspect.


The release, based on a completely new kernel, includes a huge extended repository, many of the tools from which you can find only in BackTrack, and also, more importantly, fixes for all (well, most ...) major errors about which we knew. This release has received tremendous support from the community, and we are very grateful to everyone who contributed to its success.

We moved to a new server, and now you can constantly follow the latest news and development progress. Our goal was to finally create a security distribution with our own resources, personnel and a disk suitable for BackTrack like never before.



We hope that when you download BackTrack 4 , you will agree that this is the best release ever made by our team. The release received the code name "pwnsauce".

UPD: Distribution intended for auditing network security. Comes as a LiveCD, LiveDVD and LiveUSB.
