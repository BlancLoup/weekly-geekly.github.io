As a result, after 3.5 years, broke out and posted an article on Habr . There were hopes that after that publication the project would immediately take off, people would start using it and marketing problems would disappear. But it was not there, only a few people came. Meet the investor


Father's colleague calls, asks for advice on one IT project. Consultancy has become a cost estimate for development. On December 31, 2015, I met on Skype with the project investor. There was nothing interesting in that project, but for the sake of a chance to work with an intelligent and successful person who has a lot to learn, I agreed, and my savings were already running out. This was my first experience in life as an investor. After 2 weeks, he became interested in Dcoin (my cryptocurrency was called so) and decided to invest. We registered in the contract $ 250k, which I was given in installments on a monthly basis. I neatly delegated the creation of his website so as not to be distracted from the refinement and advancement of my brainchild.

There were no problems with the revision, but with the advancement there was a trouble. How exactly to promote, what to focus on, I could not understand. Consulted with marketers, but it was not that. Although, if I had spent the ICO, I would most likely have collected $ 5-10 million, but I didn‚Äôt need this money, as there was no product promotion strategy. I was sure that he had to move himself without any participation, all he had to do was push him a little and then leave to develop on his own.

It was necessary to do something. In mid-2016, I decided to make a new product based on the source code of Dcoin, which was very similar to Ethereum, but with a different concept and with a much lower threshold of entry. Due to the fact that gentee came to our team a few months earlier, having responded to my vacancy in this article , we were able to do the MVP in just a couple of months.

Ico


In the fall of 2016, an investor called me to his hotel to transfer money for my team‚Äôs salary. He began to demand explanations as to why the funds are spent on the 2nd project, how long it will continue and what he will get from this. It became clear that he did not want to give more money. I offered to invest another $ 30k in return for a percentage of the tokens that will go to ICO. He agreed. As a result, for all the time I received from him $ 106k. On advertising, ICO spent just about $ 5-7k, the rest went to the team‚Äôs salaries.

As a rule, the ICO on the crypt is going to a purse from which the organizers can not take money until the end of the process. To do this, use a multi-signature with escrow (some independent party). To withdraw money, we need two signatures of 3.

On ICO day, it turned out that I can only have one signature out of 3. The other two should be at the escrow. I tried to find someone who wants to be third, did not find it, I had to give two signatures to one escrow. It turned out that he had complete control over the wallet, where the bitcoins were sent. All I could do in time was to ask him for a passport scan and look at it via Skype. He turned out to be a bad German speaking English. Later I was told that it was about $ 1000 to fake such a passport.

During the ICO, the money ran out, and it was impossible to spend the money collected before the end of the process, according to the terms of the escrow. Said the team that the salary will be delayed for 2 weeks.

As a result, they collected only about $ 70k (82btc). Suddenly, the German says that it is necessary to return money to investors. I'm shocked - "Why?". He says that with this money the product is not developed and it is necessary to make a return. I explain that the product is already ready, you can download, test, here are the sources, here are the binaries. He apologized for not studying the project well and listed all bitcoins, except for his commission. I immediately felt relief, now we had another six months to come up with something.

Spring 2017 He warned the investor that in a month the team will have nothing to pay, since the money collected at the ICO is running out. Offer to invest did not receive. As it turned out, I had to make an application for money, since we had a contract in force, according to which he had to invest $ 250k. My joint, it was necessary to carefully read the paper.

New investor


In June 2017, a lawyer who designed a deal with an investor introduced me to a new investor, who offered to invest in marketing and development for a share of the money collected at the 2nd round of ICO. In this case, all the marketing he took over. Perfect for me option. Besides, there was almost no money left. I agreed.

Old investor


He invited me to his office and said that either in a week I was describing the ICO scheme in white for him, or I should pay him $ 2.5m. I could not offer a white ICO scheme (in the end, it took us almost one year to figure out how to do everything in white and get approval from the European regulator). He gave a strange paper for signature. I did not sign anything.

As it turned out later, if I had signed, the new investor could stop financing, since his interests were affected there. The next day, the old investor called, threatened, I still did not sign anything.

He offered him to invest $ 144k so that the conditions of the previous agreement could be applied. Although if he reminded me how the $ 250k figure came out, I probably would not dwell on those $ 144k and would agree to its terms.

How did $ 250k


A year and a half before. I sit in the kitchen, have breakfast with fried eggs. The investor calls.

He: Let's discuss the numbers. How much do you need?
Me: $ 50k should be enough for my calculations.
He: I can give $ 250k, 50k is hardly enough for you.
I: Ok, super.

He refused to invest $ 144k and tried to kill the project during the next week. With that, he had a stake in the future ICO, plus I planned to give him some of the company's shares. He is a good man, but apparently he was afraid that he would be thrown. Strange was the situation. As a result, consensus was found and everyone was satisfied.

They brought Chinese vodka, I stacked a whole stack under the gaze of one of the Chinese all evening, as it turned out later that he was their local KGB officer or something like that.
