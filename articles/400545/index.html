Free upgrade to Windows 10 is still possible, time limit - advertising move



As you know, Microsoft, after the release of its new operating system, Windows 10 constantly did two things: trying to convince users of previous versions of its OS to upgrade and reminded that on July 29, 2016, it would be impossible to upgrade for free. Then the company claimed that after the named period, the upgrade to the new version of the operating system will be paid for by all users.

In general, this is good, since never before has a corporation offered the possibility of a free update of the operating system. Some users suspect the Redmond company that in the future, Windows 10 will be provided as a paid service with regular updates, but so far it is not. Moreover, it seems that the time limit regarding the free update is just a publicity stunt, because you can upgrade for free now.

Probably, this fact cannot be considered a breakdown of covers, but still many users are not updated now because they do not want to pay. And do not need to do this. According to the journalist of the online publication ZDNet, the whole process of free updates is extremely simple. You need to go to the Windows 10 download page , download a copy of the latest operating system, and install it by updating the existing installation of Windows 7/8 / 8.1. Microsoft does not comment on this feature.
