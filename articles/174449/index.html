Console Command Notifications

Undistract-Me is a simple but extremely utility utility that does one thing ‚Äî displays a notification when a long command (10 seconds by default, but can be customized) has completed its execution.



Also shows the name of this team and the time it took. Should work with all desktop environments.
Especially good when you, for example, decide to compile something, and do not want to stare at a black screen all the time or check every minute it still works. Now while rebuilding the kernel, you can enjoy your favorite videos with cats!

PPA for Ubuntu:
