Electrovilch stimulates the feeling of salinity with the help of current



Japanese engineers from the University of Tokyo have created a plug that helps reduce salt intake. It's all about the electrical impulses with which the electronic cutlery stimulates the receptors of the tongue. The pulses are chosen so that it seems to a person that the food is salty.

At the special presentation, anyone could taste various classic dishes cooked completely without salt. It is argued that such a device can completely replace the salt in food, which should have a positive impact on reducing the consumption of white powder.

The regulators on the fork allow you to choose one of three levels of virtual salinity, as well as switch the effect on bitterness emulation. Naturally, the effect is felt only when the teeth of the fork touch the tongue.

The cost of the electronics used in the prototype device is only $ 18. On the possibilities of commercial distribution electrovilki not yet reported.
