Correspondence Olympiad FUPM MIPT

Like last year , in 2012-2013, the OOFM Programming Contest is held. Details on the website judge.mipt.ru

The Olympiad is held according to the Kirov system (that is, even a solution that scores only part of the tests brings points). The results will be taken into account at the interview at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology and in the distribution of first-year students in computer science groups.

There will be tasks of different levels from the simplest to the most complex, so that everyone is interested. Winners will receive prizes and souvenirs from the faculty and sponsors. The tournament is available until February 18 .

The contest is compiled by trainers and part of the teams of MIPT Waterogers, ACM ICPC gold medalists from 2011-2012 and MIPT Lambda, ACM ICPC 2012-2013 finalists. All of us are graduate students and graduates of the Faculty of Management and Applied Mathematics of MIPT.

We wish you success and hope that you will enjoy the tasks!
PS Ask questions through the testing system.
