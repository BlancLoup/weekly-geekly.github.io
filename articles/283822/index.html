2015: Student Year



Providing each student in school or university with a laptop or tablet does not qualitatively change the learning process. In some cases, such a move, on the contrary, will adversely affect the learning of many students. However, a qualitatively new education can be obtained by the student independently.

For a school or an institute, as a system, changing the learning process is quite difficult. Some teachers introduce an element of novelty, but this happens infrequently. However, in the new year, much will depend on the students themselves - on how much they themselves can change their educational process. And in this, students will be helped by various tools, products, resources.

2015 will be the year of the student.

The changing role of the student

The teacher plays a major, dominant role in the whole process.

Nowadays, on the contrary, the role of the student is changing. From a passive participant in the learning process to a student who complements and expands his or her mind and education process is one step. Now students can work on their own projects, where they need creative thinking, the ability to solve problems, the ability to work in a team. In general, all that is required in the 21st century.

The result is a quick acquaintance of the student with the real world, and this acquaintance will no longer impose a negative imprint on the former dreamer about a beautiful life, as students were 10 years ago. A modern student knows that everything is not easy, and something can be obtained only after hard work.

At the same time, the opportunities for learning in the modern world are extremely wide. Even a five-year-old child can learn to program, with proper learning. What is the result?


As a result, all these changes gradually change the educational process.
