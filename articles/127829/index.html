Competition "Transformation". Prize - Asus Transformer

Most recently, we published a review of ASUS Transformer and judging by the comments, many were interested in them. We decided to go forward and make a contest in which the prizes are Asus Transformer tablets with different configurations.

Simplicity, motivation, space for creativity. In an effort to enrich the rich creative set of contests for any parts of the brain, in September the laboratory of spatial experiments TRENDCLUB invites you, the natural scientists of the future, to the competition where non-Euclidean geometry, imagination and iron logic can coexist.

This is a Transformation contest and it is timed to enter the ASUS tablet in the Russian market.

The Transformer feature is a plug-in keyboard dock that turns a tablet computer into a full-fledged netbook. A nice feature of "2in1" can change our ideas about computer technology.

On the promo page www.ASUSTransformer.ru we have developed the concept of Transformer and offer you to choose the devices that you would like to combine into one. Transfomers are undoubtedly the form factor of the future. The time to come up with all possible combinations has come now.

To participate in the contest, send photos of two things (or one photo of both things at once) that you would like to combine. Tell us about the resulting new device with all its incredible features and specifications.

Your imagination is limited only by your own imagination. This is the first competition Trendclub, where you can show not only the knowledge of the alphabet and the ability to use the camera. We welcome the creative approach, unusual solutions and other stuff in the field of creative styling: you can tie up things with scotch tape or make something new, using carpentry or plumbing tools.

We took into account your wishes to the criteria for the evaluation of work. In addition to the usual "likes" - hearts, clicked by friends and acquaintances of participants on the page of work, an important parameter of the selection of winners will be the assessment of creativity.

This approach will allow you to choose the best of the best. Cheating the rating, if someone has a similar desire, will only lead to a legal holocaust - we will remove the work and ban the author forever in the entire Internet.

The authors of the three best works will receive ASUS Eee Pad Transformer of various modifications. The top configuration, of course, for the first place.



Deadlines : works are accepted until September 30 inclusive. Results will be announced on October 10th.
