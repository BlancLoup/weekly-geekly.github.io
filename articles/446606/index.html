Jobs of the conference participants




Looking for a job in additive technology and digital production? 3D printing, CNC machine tools, 3D modeling, VR / AR - your professional interest?



Every year, Top 3D Expo is visited by dozens of employers of relevant companies. This year we organized a stand where everyone can familiarize themselves with the vacancies offered by participating companies and pass on their resume to them.

Some of the vacancies :

Internship in the laboratory of biotechnological research , 3D Bioprinting Solutions
Design Engineer , Picaso 3D
Engineer of the Department of Research and Development , JSC "Center for Additive Technologies"
Partner Relationship Manager , RangeVision
Sales Manager 3D Services , Top 3D Shop

There will also be presented vacancies, information about which is not published in open sources.
