Mind Mapping (Intranet Design)

Intranet is a management system for a company and its projects for turnkey internal use.

One may speak about the development of a technical assignment, a well-executed contract, but without a serious approach to design, the result can be deplorable.

The order may be delayed, the amount for the development to change every day, the project is much more complicated than expected. As a rule, the client does not like such news, there are more and more disagreements between the client and the contractor, and as a result the customer refuses to cooperate. This situation may be repeated in the same project several times.
However, to avoid such a course of events, you can use the right approach to design.
