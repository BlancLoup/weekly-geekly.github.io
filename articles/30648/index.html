Design + 400 MHz + CDMA 1xEV-DO = You'll love it!

While there are annoying disputes about 3G, EDGE, HSDPA on fruit and berry devices,
I would like to draw your attention to a very successful model of a CDMA communicator
HTC Touch Vogue (PPC6900) .

This device is quite suitable for our CDMA (1xRTT and 1xEV-DO) networks.


Small, convenient, powerful enough, communicator controlled practically without stylus,
with a fast internet channel and modern software system.


A special bonus, the ability to simultaneously have a voice channel and an EV-DO data channel from different operators.







This is not a review, I just want to express my impressions, to show an alternative , you know what.

The best alternative, especially for those who know how to "modify the file" to a mirror shine, for those who want more in less.


Let's start with the fact that the communicator was announced relatively long ago, in November 2007.
However, look at the parameters:


Cellular Networks: CDMA800, CDMA1900
Cellular Data Links: cdmaOne, CDMA2000 1xRTT, CDMA2000 1xEV-DO Rel.0, CDMA2000 1xEV-DO Rev A
Screen Type: color transflective TFT, 65536 scales
Screen size: 2.8 "
Screen resolution: 240 x 320
The size: 59.6 x 101 x 13.9 mm
Weight: 112 g (with battery)
CPU: 400 Mhz, 32bit Qualcomm MSM7500
ROM: 256 MiB
RAM: 128 MiB
OS: Microsoft Windows Mobile 6.1 Professional
Expansion Slots: 1 (microSD, microSDHC, TransFlash, SDIO)
External connectors: USB 2.0 client (12Mbit / s, mini-USB)
Data transfer: 1xEV-DO Rev.A, Bluetooth 2.0


More about the device


This device can work in most of our local CDMA
networks (except CDMA450 of course). By the way, the reception quality is more than enough.


The device has 2 switchable phone profiles. Stitched. You can put on them 2 different operators and switch as needed.


However, due to the fact that the voice in CDMA goes to 1x, it is possible by a ‚Äútricky‚Äù prl-a and a small tweak of the system
configure voice from one operator, and data (EV-DO) from another, and on one, mainly, profile.
So you can keep, for example, running ICQ or softphone and call-talk as needed.


For me, this combination is convenient to have a data channel connection.
through a relatively inexpensive but high-speed EV-DO channel from People.net.ua,
a voice channel (with a direct number and minimum telecom rating),
from an older CDMA operator, but not an EV-DO, but with wild data transfer rates.

The battery , subjectively holds about 3 days, with not too intensive use.

The speed of the communicator is much faster than the 200MHz HTC Touch. Which is not surprising.

Internet speed depends on coverage and operator. In my case, real 1-1.5 megabits
were quite mundane. And ssh can work quite comfortably, the delays are incomparable with the connection via EDGE.


GPS , it is in the communicator and works quite well in modern firmware,

The camera is not bad for this type of device. 2Mpx, and the built-in software in the Touch style is quite pleasing.


Memory , its a lot. After installing and setting me

Management, convenience of work , now there are quite a few touchscreen communicators,
but not everyone can control the fingers. This can be.
In addition, the communicator is very "well" in hand.


That's what makes this device so convenient and prevents it from letting go:
special, rubber-like surface of a high-quality body
Convenience of touching the screen, even with the same hand in which you hold
screen sensitivity is just in measure
the screen is not sunk relative to the communicator surface
Good weight and size, it can be carried in a shirt pocket, but it does not seem small.
