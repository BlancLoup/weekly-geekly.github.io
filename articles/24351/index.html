ReMIX conference with Steve Ballmer to take place in Moscow

ReMIX Conference is a unique technical conference dedicated to the interaction of web technologies and Microsoft products with other companies' platforms and technologies, as well as open standards. At the conference, Microsoft CEO Steve Ballmer will share his vision of Microsoft's Internet strategy with Russian web developers.

On May 23, 2008, the first ReMIX conference (www.remix.ru) will take place in Moscow, which is the Russian continuation of the well-known technology conference MIX, the third year of Microsoft for web developers, web designers and active Internet users (www.visitmix. com).

As part of his one-day visit to Russia, Microsoft CEO Steve Ballmer will speak at the conference. He will take part in an open discussion with the famous Internet leader Anton Nosik, and will also answer questions from the representatives of the Russian Internet community.

To determine the most pressing issues from today on the site www.remix.ru starts the contest "Ask a question to Steve Ballmer." Winners will be determined by voting and invited to participate in the conference.

You can join the event webcast by registering on www.remix.ru .

Within the framework of the ReMIX conference, there will also be a round table with well-known figures of the Runet, and Microsoft experts will conduct five reports on the technologies Internet Explorer 8, SilverlightTM 2.0, Windows Server 2008, IIS7, etc. Additionally, participants will be able to visit the partners exhibition and the Xbox gaming zone
