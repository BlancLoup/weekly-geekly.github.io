Kaggle, real datasets and workshops
Each case study in our program is taught in three stages. First, students learn to see common patterns and parse well-developed tasks from Kaggle. Further carry out independent projects with real data under the guidance of tutors. For example, in the framework of the second case, students will try to optimize the algorithms for displaying advertising in the Data-Centric Alliance system - and those who achieve good results will not only get the approval of the teachers, but also be able to completely recapture the cost of education.

In the first two stages, students are given practical skills in the full cycle of working with big data. It:
  • Deploying Hadoop / HDFS / HBase
  • Pre-processing and data cleaning
  • Prediction model building
  • Selection of the optimal machine learning algorithm
  • Model calibration

The third part of the case allows you to get acquainted with ready-made tools and best practices. Speakers from Yandex, Sberteha, Spotify, MTS, IBM, Cloudera will conduct master classes and tell you how to collect, store and use big data in their companies. In this block, students will be able to assess how the specificity of data in various industries influences the choice of tools and analysis algorithms.
