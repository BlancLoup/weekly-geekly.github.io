Russian hackers are attacking Estonian sites

Russia "attacks Estonia simultaneously virtually, psychologically and realistically." So says the head of the Estonian Foreign Ministry Urmas Paet. What is psychologically and realistically, more or less can be imagined, and under the virtual attack the minister meant hacker attacks.

The attacks on the websites of the Estonian government agencies began after the authorities decided to start exhumation and identification of the remains of Soviet soldiers. According to Urmas Paet, IP addresses have already been established, through which cyber attacks are launched on the websites of Estonian government agencies and the office of the President of Estonia. Moreover, it turns out that the attacks are conducted from the IP addresses of the Kremlin administration . True, the Estonian side did not provide any evidence of its words.
