Festive menu - in Yandex. Bar

In addition to Friday, today there is another event worthy of a festive dinner. Yandex.Baru for Firefox today is exactly one year old.

In honor of the holiday, Yandex has prepared a new version of Yandex.Bar 3.0 for Firefox fans, in which several useful innovations have appeared.

The first is the counselor. It will tell you the most popular variants of your request from other users.

The second is the address line corrector. Thanks to him, you can not think about the layout and enter the address of the site even in Russian - it will still be true.

And for dessert - the ability to send your friends an excerpt of the page that interested you with a link to its full version. To do this, select the desired paragraph, right-click on it and select "Send by mail".

New Bar is already open for download .
