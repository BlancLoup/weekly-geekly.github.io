19 games at the start of sales Kinect for Xbox 360

Microsoft announced a line of games specially created for the Kinect controller and available for sale at the start of Kinect sales in November around the world.



Plus Adrenalin Misfits and CROSSBOARD 7 from Konami and Get Fit With Mel B from Black BeanGames / Lightning Fish.

The first game - Kinect Adventures will be included in each Kinect box. Most of the games are games for active family holidays, sports or fitness games. In such games, the potential of Kinect opens up - controlling the character in the game with the help of your body.

In addition to these games, soon after the launch in 2011, others announced at the TGS will follow. Let me remind you that the start of sales of Kinect is scheduled for November 4 in North America and November 10 in Europe, including in Russia.
