How we filmed a video for CMS

In 2014, we decided that we needed an advertising and image video for our DIAFAN.CMS site management system. We did not plan advertising on television, we were not going to move on YouTube, we didn‚Äôt want to animate the site, but the video turned out to be badly needed. It is necessary and that's it, you know how it happens, just "caught fire." The main need is ‚Äúso that our CMS has a beautiful video‚Äù. Familiar guys (videoproject.ru), professionally involved in video shooting, at first frightened us that it was a few days of preparation, a whole shooting day and a couple of weeks of post-production to fake a dozen seconds of a decent movie. We were convinced that for CMS it is more expedient to order graphic animation, in other words, a cartoon ( something like this ) for a couple of tens of thousands of rubles. But the flywheel of our desire was not to stop, and we decided to shoot a full-fledged video.




Scenario


First of all, we gathered to discuss what and how we will shoot. Initially, we wanted to get just a beautiful image movie within one minute, with a nice bright picture, camera bursts on laptops, with some close-ups of hands knocking on the keyboard, blurring the background, etc. People sit at home, at work, on vacation, and easily manage their sites, which can be said voiceover against the background of pleasant music. And at the end of the video to bring the logo "DIAFAN.CMS site management system." For this kind of shooting you need only rooms, people, accessories and a good camera to take it all off. However, in the process of calculating the efforts that are required to implement even such a simple scenario, it turned out that in any case the costs would be considerable. And since we decided not to skimp and shoot everything well, then why don't we still put some idea into the video. Then we began to complicate.

In order for the video to convey some idea to the viewer, the direction of the video should be more advertising than image. But how to advertise a site management system? This is not a mayonnaise, where you can show a salad and a satisfied full husband. This is not a car where you can remove the interior of the cabin and the road. And this is not washing powder with clean clothes in the frame. This specific software is quite narrow focus. Let's say right away, we discussed a lot of ‚Äúcreative‚Äù options, and site management from the inside, through the eyes of CMS, like in an electronic factory, and a mirror image of the browser, for the site visitor and administrator, and the real store versus online store, to show how to manage Internet -Shop is no more difficult than moving goods on the shelf of a real store. There were many ideas, but they all broke about the Hollywood budgets, which were required for a little bit of high-quality shooting of something large-scale. And we didn't want to make a cartoon. Therefore, we began to build on the original scenario and as a result an interesting idea was born. Different people in different situations easily and simply manage their sites, and in the end we somehow show that there is a lot of work behind a simple click of a button. After all, CMS is a management system, that is, the mechanism that serves the site. The administrator changes the price in the administrative part, and the price changes on the site, thanks to the CMS. Therefore, we decided to present the system as a super-hero who rules over the site and does everything with it that the owner tells.

Then we decided that we will shoot exactly three situations. A business woman who works in her office. The traveler, who sat down in a street cafe, put another article on his blog. And the girl at the resort, on the beach, who, from a deckchair, administers her website. A girl in the admin panel changes the price of a product, the camera pulls away to the side, the viewer sees the browser of the site, which shows the same product. The super-hero in the DIAFAN.CMS costume comes out of the frame, removes the old price from the site and puts a new one in its place. A curtain. In addition, we decided not to shoot people's faces, in order to depersonalize our heroes and additionally focus on the main thing - the computer screen and DIAFAN.CMS depicted on it.

Shooting process


The magic of television is that the leader of strict news can sit in swimming trunks and slippers, the main thing is that only a jacket gets into the camera. Also arranged many studios, emulating different locations. Often, interiors and exteriors are recreated in the same room of the studio in different corners, and shooting at the right angle will create the necessary illusion. And you can also shoot the end of the film first, and then the string, and at the end mount it in the correct order, so we started with a traveler.



The most important thing when shooting in such studio locations is light. Different spotlights, spotlights, color filters can be in a room without windows and doors to create the effect of moonlight, the evening sun, a bright day. In our first location we wanted to create the illusion of a street cafe, the morning sun and a light breeze. The sun was a 1000W source, and Dmitry created the illusion of wind and waving leaves. :)



You can see that when shooting on the laptop screen nothing is shown, but only pasted 4 points in the corners. This is done because, firstly, the flickering of the screen is poorly removed, and secondly, the interfaces themselves in the actual size of the screen in the screen look too small and incomprehensible. Most often, all interfaces are superimposed on four labels already when mounting a clip.

The next location was the office of a business woman. We shot this episode much longer. At first we drove the actress from one side of the couch to the other, then the costume designer rejected the light blouse and dressed the heroine in a dark one, then we put the table under the laptop, but in the end pushed it away.
