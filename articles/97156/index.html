Google Voice is open to anyone who lives in America.

Today, the promising Google Voice service has become available to anyone living in the United States of America. If before it was possible to register only by invitation, now there is no such barrier.

Google is in a hurry to announce its new services in order to have the necessary set of services for the convenient use of the cloud operating system by the time Chrome OS is launched.
