Opera 10 browser downloaded over 10 million times



Oslo, Norway - September 8, 2009 - Within a week after the release of the final version of Opera 10, users from around the world downloaded this latest browser more than 10 million times.

Opera remains committed to the ideology of the Unified Web, ensuring the availability of the Internet from any hardware and platforms and regardless of the location of users. The philosophy of the Unified Web is reflected in the development of Opera for all devices - from desktop computers to mobile phones, televisions and game consoles.

The number of Opera desktop browser users exceeds 40 million people. Tens of millions surf the Internet using Opera browsers for mobile phones, game consoles, TVs and other devices. The number of downloads of Opera 10 suggests that alternative browsers are recognized by an increasing number of users. It is important to note that many of them have never previously worked with Opera browsers.

The new concept of accelerated loading of web pages, implemented in Opera 10, is focused on the use of low-speed data channels. Such conditions of work on the Internet periodically occur in many of us, for example, in hotels, airports, in areas with limited access to the connection. Opera 10 with Opera Turbo technology is designed to simplify the work of users facing slow or congested access networks.
