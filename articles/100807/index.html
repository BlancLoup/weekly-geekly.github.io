Ogs Mahjong 0.6

Some time ago, I and the kornerr programmer took on an interesting and informative task: sort out existing free three-dimensional engines, select one and write a simple toy based on it. After a series of experiments, Ogre3D was chosen as the engine, and the game was solitaire-mahjong.

Yesterday we finished work on the beta version. All the originally planned functionality is ready, but you want more and better. Testing, bug reports and suggestions are welcome.
