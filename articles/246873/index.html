The release of the educational program for startups ABRT-Mangrove CEO Camp 2.0

ABRT-Mangrove CEO Camp 2.0, an educational project for the founders of promising start-ups, organized by ABRT Venture Fund, Mangrove Capital Partners, RIS Ventures and ITMO Business Incubator with expert support from Veeam Software, has recently completed. The main goal of the CEO Camp project is not to receive investment projects by the participants, but to accelerate their own revenues . Startup CEO Day was divided into two parts.

In the first part, the project teams of the previous stream (CEO Camp 1.0) performed. Each presentation began with the selection of the basic skills and tools that were acquired by the participant in the program. For example, Georgy Cheishvili from AddReality spoke about the importance of working with clients through partners. The second part of Startup CEO Day consisted of speeches of investors and graduates of the current stream.
