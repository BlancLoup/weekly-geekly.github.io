Kazan.rb # 1 - a meeting of Ruby & Rails programmers in Kazan on May 21 in It-park

Hello,

By analogy with Piter.rb & Kiev.rb, we plan to hold conferences on the topic of Ruby & Rails in Kazan .
The first meeting will be held on May 21 at 12:00 in It-Park on ul. Petersburg, 52, in the hall for press conferences.

It will feature reports in the form of 10 slides, 20 minutes of the story and 10 minutes of discussion.

Planned topics:
  • Cucumber (Leonid Dinershtein)
  • Git flow (Timur Vafin)
  • JS for Rails developers (Timur Vafin)
  • Rails Hardware (Yaroslav Dmitriev)
  • Simple restful application (Vladimir Mikhailov)
  • Mountable apps (Ruslan Gatiyatov)
In developing:
  • HAML, SASS
  • CoffeScript
  • Rails & Node.js
On the same day, the second part of Startup Weekend will be held, which is the first stage of selection in the IT-business incubator. SWK listeners will be given free time in the second part of the day to visit Kazan.rb. Place of SWK: Idea Technopark, St. Petersburg 50.

A big request to confirm your presence in advance using the form on the site , otherwise it will be difficult to get because of the super protection of It-park.
