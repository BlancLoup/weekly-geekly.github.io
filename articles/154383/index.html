Google Nexus 7 comes to Russia

Large Russian dealer networks have begun to report that the sale of the 7-inch Google Nexus 7 tablet will begin on October 12. The cost of the 16-gigabyte version is announced at 10,000 rubles, in the Yandex market the prices for gray tablets start at 11,000 rubles. In the Connected, you can already pre-order .
Nexus 7 announced in June with the launch of Jelly Bean. The cost of the device with 16 GB of memory in the US - $ 249. In addition to her, the company produces a model with 8 GB of memory. In M.Video it is not planned to sell it.




According to M.Video sales, the tablet market in 2012 continues to grow steadily - in the first half of 2012, the company sold over 80,000 tablets, which is 400% higher than in the same period last year. The emergence of new competitive devices with different functionality and a decrease in the average price for them will further increase the demand for tablets in the run-up to the pre-New Year sales season.

For the full year 2012, the company plans to sell about 350,000 tablets.
