Internet TV appeared in Bynet

The following press release came to Habraredaktsiyu. It seems that this is a really interesting project, one of the first Web 2.0 projects in Bynet (of course, after podcasting ).

A new media resource ITV.BY has been launched in Belarus .

No, we are not talking about the next file archive with predictable and standard content. The Internet begins broadcasting new media, a unique symbiosis of traditional television and interactive features of the global web.

Daily ITV.BY offers its users to get acquainted with the news of the country, the world, business, culture, science and art, sports, city life through audiovisual content. Daily the resource accumulates the best plots of terrestrial, satellite and cable TV channels from all over the world, and the user can choose with whose viewpoint on events he wants to get acquainted.

In addition to a variety of news, ITV.BY gives you the opportunity to plunge into the bright world of sports, music, entertainment and cinema. Plunge into the world of high-quality licensed video, as one of the main principles of the resource is a guarantee of respect for copyright.

ITV.BY invites visitors to choose what and in what sequence to look. And this is not just a new style of media consumption, it is a radically different principle of it. From now on, you can make up your own TV program, as well as compare, analyze, speak out, exchange views, etc. And this means you can model your own information space.

Due to the peculiarities of the content, access to the resource can be obtained only via broadband communication lines. Technically, servers with video content are located within the peering ring of Minsk, thus, the resource traffic is purely internal.

ITV.BY is a real revolution in the mind of the media consumer. This is a different consciousness of society. This is a real media revolution!

ITV.BY encourages you to look at the possibilities of the Internet in a new way. ITV.BY calls to watch the Internet.
