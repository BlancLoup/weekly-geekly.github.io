Many logo does not happen!? .. It happens!


Launched to the free swimming project MnogoLogo.ru , created specifically for designers.

About the idea
Working hours must be paid. With this you can not argue. But as often happens, 99 options for the logo (the same goes for site layouts) spent 2 weeks, and ONE option that the customer chose was done in 2 hours. And, accordingly, the customer paid ONE logo, or 2 hours of time spent on it.
What to do with the other options and time spent?
We want to offer designers to sell MANY logos, get money for the work that designers actually did, for the time they spent.

What do designers get on Monoglogo?
  • opportunity to sell your work;
  • wide audience of potential customers;
  • compensation for time spent on creating the design.
