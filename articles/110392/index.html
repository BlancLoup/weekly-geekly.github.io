In these of our Internet came across a wonderful life hacking


A fairly simple design allows you to take with you everything you need.

But for all its simplicity, you are free to place things as you like, and take what you need.


Strong rubber harnesses, for example, will hold any power supply. If you get tired of tangling wires, then there is nothing easier - just fold them, and you can be sure that they will not be lost, not be confused with each other and will always be at hand.

PS In an online store, the thing costs from 15 to 25 cu, depending on the size, but the idea is simple. IMHO, it does not cost anything to bring it to life.
