Club Innovators now in Nizhny Novgorod

Date: April 14, 2011, 18:00
Venue: Nizhny Novgorod, Oktyabrskaya St., 25. Assembly Hall of the House of Scientists.
Project specialization: IT.

April 14 in the Nizhny Novgorod House of Scientists held the first meeting of the Club of Innovators. Beginning at 18:00.

After the welcoming speech of the Nizhny Novgorod organizers, Vladislav Kudinov, chairman of the Innovators Club of St. Petersburg, will tell about the activities of the Club in the city on the Neva and in Moscow, the benefits that the past meetings brought to the participants, and plans for the development of the Club.

Vasily Kozlov, Rector of Green City Business School, Member of the Expert Council of the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs, Ilya Korshunov, Director of the Nizhny Novgorod Scientific Information Center and Kendrick White, Director General of Marchmont Capital Partners will take part as invited speakers.

The first meeting of the Club will be devoted to information technology.

After joint discussion of projects and their evaluation by invited experts, we will select the best, which will then be presented to investors. Of course, not without a coffee break - informal communication with interesting people over a cup of coffee or tea.

The meeting will be of interest to anyone interested in information technology, as well as those who would like to learn more about the activities of the Innovators Club in other regions and join the Club in Nizhny Novgorod.

Participation in the Club meeting is free, but by prior arrangement.
