iSleep

A wonderful concept of a very useful device for those who like to work at a laptop until late, caught my eye. It is called uncomplicated, in pursuit of fashion - iSleep, and it looks like this.

The device is not only convenient, but also technologically. It is able to heat up, using the heat of the computer, to launch the selected playlist when the laptop lid is closed, which will make sleep as comfortable as possible. Well, of course, the built-in alarm can wake you up at the right time. Pleasant dreams!



Unfortunately, the device has not yet entered production, but is only an interesting design and engineering solution.
