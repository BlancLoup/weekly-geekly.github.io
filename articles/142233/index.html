We are looking for talented game developers with original ideas! We are ready to invest money in standing projects for mobile platforms and help you get access to a multimillion audience. What is needed for this?

2. Alpha version of the game for the mobile platform (iOS, Android, Windows Phone 7); integration of elements of social games is welcome;

3. Holistic vision of the project, including monetization schemes.
