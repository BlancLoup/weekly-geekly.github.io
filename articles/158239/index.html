About cameras and lenses

So what is PureView? This is not the name of the 41-megapixel sensor, as many could decide after the release of the Nokia 808 PureView. PureView is a whole complex of hardware components and software solutions that allows you to make high-quality images.

"Smart shooting"


We all sometimes come across this situation: we are going to have a rest, we see an interesting and picturesque place and we want to take a group picture with our friends. And here we ask someone to take a picture of us all together, the person presses a button and we get a great photo in which one of the friends blinked, and the second decided that the picture was taken, and turned away. The second, but not the least important, function of the Smart Shoot lens is the removal of moving objects from the captured image.
