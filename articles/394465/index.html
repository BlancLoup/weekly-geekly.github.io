The Japanese want to arrange at the opening of the Olympics man-made stargazing



Japanese company ALE Co. wants to offer their services to the organizers of the Olympics 2020 , which will be held in Tokyo. The company is developing a system for creating man-made meteor showers, the first test launch of which is scheduled for the second half of next year.

In the concept of Sky Canvas - microsatellite in Earth orbit, which contains from 500 to 1000 balls. In the fall they burn in the atmosphere in different colors. According to the company's calculations, the apparent magnitude of burning balls will be about -1 (it will look a little less bright than Sirius - the brightest star in the night sky, the apparent magnitude of which is -1.46).



The company believes that even in a large city with its light pollution of the sky, their man-made meteor shower will be clearly visible to the naked eye. Also on the site, the organizers promise that their meteors will fly and burn longer than usual, so that viewers can enjoy the show longer. And in case of bad weather, the show can be delayed at the right time.



According to the company, their artificial meteors will burn at an altitude of 60 to 80 km, and therefore the approximate diameter of the area on Earth from which this stellar fall will be 200 km.
