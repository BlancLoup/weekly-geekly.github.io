Linus Torvalds was awarded the Millennium Technology Prize 2012

Today the names of the winners of the Millennium Technology Prize 2012 have become known - the world's largest award for achievements in the technological field, which is awarded every two years.

This year there are two winners: Dr. Shinya Yamanaka for a new method for producing induced stem cells by reprogramming somatic cells by epigenetic and Linus Torvalds for Linux.

The organizing committee noted the merits of Torvalds in creating a new open operating system, which led to the widespread use of the Linux kernel. Today, millions of computers, smartphones, and TiVo digital video recorders work on Linux.
