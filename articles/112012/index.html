We read content from Twitter without clicking on links.

Many of us use Twitter, which has become a means of receiving the latest news and all sorts of links.
So, to read the content without clicking on the links, LiteFeed service was created, which allows you to read the Twitter feed already with text, photos or videos that are behind the links. Pages are pre-parsed and converted to a readable form using an algorithm based on Readability, which is also used in Safari Reader.




But this is not a Twitter client, but a Twitter reader, so you cannot post new tweets here, but you can retweet or facebook like for the article you like, there is also support for lists. It happens that not all pages are displayed correctly, if you suddenly notice this, you can click on the ‚ÄúReport Problem Page‚Äù and I will know about the problem.

Do not waste time on useless clicks, but just read LiteFeed ;)
