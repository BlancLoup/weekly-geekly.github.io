Phone calls to instant messengers

Six months ago, my best friend changed jobs. In the new office, her biline phone refused to confidently catch the connection, so Lena got herself all sorts of online messengers (Skype, ICQ, msn and gtalk), in order not to experience communication problems during the working day. Well, with my mobile work, I was not ready to sit at the computer all day. In general, a break in the exchange of fresh gossip put our friendship at risk ... until I decided to use my official position at E1tele.com .

The company E1tele.com gives its subscribers the opportunity to make calls to the most popular Internet messengers. In the Russian market is a unique service. Many of our customers use this service, as it allows you to make calls at very competitive rates.

This service is also relevant for those who often travel abroad. No matter where you and your interlocutor find, the cost of connection will always be minimal. It is comparable to the price of local calls offered by mobile or city operators.

With the help of E1Tele.com, you can call subscribers of Skype, Gtalk, ICQ, MSN and Yahoo! from a mobile or landline phone. You can order a call by SMS. For this you need to send the messenger prefix and the unique name of the interlocutor to the SMS center number on the E1tele.com network.

List of prefixes:
  • Skype - 99910
  • MSN - 99911
  • GTalk - 99912
  • ICQ - 99913
  • Yahoo! - 99914
For example, to connect with a Skype user, you can send an SMS to any SMS center number on the E1tele.com network with the text: 99910SkypeUser, where SkypeUser is the login name of the called party. In a few seconds you will receive an incoming call from E1tele on your mobile phone. com. Answer this call, wait for the connection and talk.

To receive an incoming call from the E1tele.com network to a landline phone, you must also indicate the city number in the SMS message. For example: 99910SkypeUser * 74951234567. The number of the SMS center E1tele.com is + 7-926-240-09-90.

By the way! On E1tele.com, newly registered users are credited with a free 50 cents account.
