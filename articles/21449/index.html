GMail + The Bat! 4.0 = TheBat.net

Let it be a little late, but I still consider it my duty to share the news with the public. Google and RITLabs announced the launch of a joint project. Now every user The Bat! versions 4.0 and above will be able to take full advantage of GMail postal service under the domain name TheBat.net.

In The Bat! 4.0 the configuration process for TheBat.net mail service is as simple and clear as possible. However, the result of the joint work of Google and RITLabs can be used by users of the third version of The Bat! .. You can access the features of TheBat.net by registering on the RITLabs website.

First impressions: Registration is very simple and fast. However, I immediately want to upset the owners of a non-licensed version of the popular mailer: you need to enter the serial number and login on the site www.ritlabs.com . After the registration process is completed, you will have a mailbox of the form username@thebat.net.
