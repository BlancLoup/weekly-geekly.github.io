Begin.com with Google

It's no secret that to achieve the goal you need to do something. With startups, everything is exactly the same - only someone has been carrying out one idea for months, and someone is eager to fight and literally over the weekend makes his plan into reality.

If you belong to the second type of people who are not ready to sit back and wait for something, then the Begin.com event, which takes place from the evening of June 25 to the evening of June 27 in Moscow, was created especially for you. And even if your idea has not yet acquired clear outlines, you can still attend the event and help implement not your own, but someone else's other project - to gain experience creating something from scratch, learn how to work in a new team, start new ones. dating and just having fun to spend time.

Start.com will be held with the support of Google this weekend in Moscow, but everything interesting will start on Friday, when among all the participants, by voting, the best ideas will be selected, which are destined to turn into a working draft. To implement each of the ideas, various teams of developers, designers, and managers are assembled, who work hard on their projects for the remaining two days. All this time, the work of the teams is monitored by experts who give practical advice and help to bypass a number of pitfalls.

Perhaps you now (and maybe earlier) thought that this event is surprisingly similar to Startup Weekend, and in that case you were not mistaken. Start.com is a logical continuation of the well-known Startup Weekend, which this time is supported by Google. Cooperation with Google involves the use of technology search giant in the project, although it does not commit to this.
Last day Begin.com will end with a general vote, where the participants and experts themselves will choose the winners. However, in the case of Start.com, the main thing is participation, not victory, because participation is an invaluable experience.

Come to Start.com and start building your team and your exciting and interesting business!
