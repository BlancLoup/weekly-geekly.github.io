Co-Laboratory: Atlassian User Group mitap



Platforms Confluence and Jira are used in every second IT-company. And not always exclusively as an error tracking system and a collaborative environment (as the creators are positioning). We, for example, Confluence acts as a platform for corporate training, and Jira, among other things, to optimize the work of multilingual support sites. Colleagues who work with these systems decided to hold in our office a meeting of the Moscow Atlassian User Group community, to which we invite everyone to attend on October 26.

The meeting is devoted to the development of Atlassian and their practical application. Our colleagues will explain why we chose Confluence and Jira, as well as share tips & tricks, tricks of adapting this software to our needs, experience in dealing with problems in the process of setting up and extracting undoubted benefits at the output.


Brief program of the meeting:

Confluence as a corporate learning portal
Rodion Nagornov, head of the training and certification group, will tell you how to turn Confluence into a platform for organizing corporate training (in conjunction with LMS EduBrite through the Gilly plugin). This scheme was developed four years ago and during this time has undergone many changes. His story includes a description of the features used by the bundle of products and improvements implemented by the manufacturers of LMS over the period of the system.

Site management support using the Jira Service Desk
Senior technical writer Yevgeny Ozerov and translator Alexander Prudnikova will share their secrets as Kaspersky Labs were able to optimize the creation and translation of articles for the support site, simplify communication between technical writers and translators, and also save time on processing requests using the Jira Service Desk.

Analyzing Atlassian Application Data with eazyBI
Raymonds Simanovskis , the founder of eazyBI, a business intelligence analyzer, will talk about how his products analyze Atlassian application data and answer any questions. And the answer in Russian.

As usual, the mitap will be held at the Moscow office of Kaspersky Lab. We are waiting for everyone on October 26 at 19.00. You must first register on the site .
