International Conference FOSS Sea 2009

Dear Colleagues.

We invite you to take part in the second International Conference "FOSS Sea 2009" , which will be held from 9 to 12 September in Odessa. At the conference, the Mozilla community developers and users will gather for the first time in Ukraine at MozCamp Eastern Europe 2009 .

The objectives of the conference are to ensure effective communication and exchange of information, discuss the prospects for the development of free software, initiate new projects on the use of open source software in science and education.

The conference was organized by: Odessa National University. I.I. Mechnikova , UAFOSS , Odessa IT-Club and the company RootUA Media.

The organizing partner is Media Magic .

Information support - RootUA magazine, Linux Format magazine , FOSS News newspaper , Open Source electronic application , root.ua portals, osdn.org.ua , habrahabr.ru , opennet.ru , nixp.ru , developers.org.ua , linux.md .

The main areas of the sections within MozCamp Eastern Europe 2009:
  • Problems of localization and interaction with user groups.
  • Mobile technology and Web 2.0.
  • Mozilla is a development platform (XUL / JS / CSS).
  • Product Marketing Mozilla and its specifics for Eastern Europe.
The main directions of the sections at the FOSS Sea 2009 conference:
  • An introduction to free / open source software.
  • Free / open source software in education and science.
  • Free / open source solutions for government and public sectors.
  • Free / open source solutions for the business sector.
  • Free / open source hardware solutions.
  • Interaction between developers and users of free / open source software.
  • Scientific projects using free / open source software.
  • Free / open source software in web space.
  • High-performance systems and clusters.
Registration of speakers - until the 30th of August on the conference website.

Types of reports: in-person report, blitz report, master class, correspondence report.
The time limit for the report is 30 minutes.

Competition for the best report.

Live broadcast of the conference.

We invite to cooperation.

Conference site - www.foss-sea.org.ua
