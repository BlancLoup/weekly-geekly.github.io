Display


The resolution of the display is amazing. Of course, this seems like a race for the numbers, but the image quality is nonetheless truly excellent. The aspect ratio is 16:10.

In fact, the main advantage of this display is not resolution. Tab S is the first large-format tablet with a Super AMOLED screen (three years ago, Samsung already had an AMOLED Galaxy Tab 7.7 tablet, but small and relatively expensive). The use of Super AMOLED technology gives several advantages at once. First, the large color gamut - the colors are noticeably cleaner and richer than on the average LCD screen. Samsung promises a color coverage of 94% AdobeRGB, typical LCD tablets usually have slightly less coverage than sRGB ‚Äî about 70% AdobeRGB (although some LCD models go beyond sRGB, but they are literally units). Plus, AMOLED has a very high contrast, so black is really black, good economy (unlike LCDs, there is no backlight constantly consuming full power, no matter what happens on the screen), less glare - the picture is better seen in bright light , even in the sun. Finally, the AMOLED screens are thinner than the LCD, so the Tab S's 6.6 mm case was able to place a fairly large capacity battery (one third larger than the Sony Tablet Z2, which is 6.5 mm thick but made on a standard LCD -screen).

Of course, there is a problem with the fact that almost all photographs and films are now made for ordinary screens, so on AMOLED colors may look more saturated than they should be. But in the settings of the screen there are several profiles, one of which reduces the color gamut to the usual sRGB.



Autonomous work


The battery capacity is 4900 (for 8.4 inches) or 7900 mA ‚Ä¢ h (10.5 inches). This will allow to work, according to the manufacturer‚Äôs certification, for 11 hours. In addition, there is a function of "Extreme Energy Saving" - it translates the screen into black and white mode and disables all minor services and functions. The meaning of the black and white picture is that the black background on the AMOLED consumes almost no energy, so the remaining charge will give several hours of real use, and not just lying on the table. The function is activated manually even on a fully charged battery, so if you leave, for example, on a camping trip, where there will be no power outlets, you can turn it on immediately.
