Rating for each store on Yandex.Market

Recently, we changed the method of calculating the rating of stores on Yandex.Market, which was described in detail in a blog and in more detail (with formulas and graphs) in help for stores. Now, when calculating the rating, along with the user rating, the data of the Market Quality Service are used.



Thanks to the innovation, every store now has a rating, which has been posted on the Market for more than a month. And new stores without a rating are specially marked, so that the buyer is especially attentive to other indicators of their quality, for example, the convenience of the site and the politeness of the manager taking the order.

Now that almost all the shops on the Market can be compared not only in price, but also in quality, feedback is very important to us. Please tell us if you consider the ideology of the new rating and the method of its calculation to be correct? Did we make the choice of the store more convenient and understandable?

Team Market, waiting for your feedback
