Mac OS X Snow Leopard for $ 9.95



The other day I learned about a special program for mac buyers.

The bottom line: if you buy any mac from this list from the 8th of June 2009, you can participate in the program and get a dvd from the new OS for $ 9.95 instead of ~ $ 30 for the regular leopard upgrade.



To participate in the program they will need to fill out a registration form, pay for participation in the program (the same $ 9.95, I used PayPal for payment), then print a form with a bar code, attach a photocopy of a detailed computer bill to it, and send it by mail (yes, , the most real mail, most shocked) to the nearest Apple software update center. I was asked to send somewhere to England.

You need to do all this within 90 days from the moment you buy a computer. The program is valid until December 26, 2009. Those before the Catholic Christmas application must have time to get to the software update center.
