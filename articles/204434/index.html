Graph500 (supercomputers rating 2013)

IBM supercomputers ranked first in the latest (November) Graph500 rating, which was presented at the Supercomputing Conference (SC'13) in Denver.



The first three places were awarded to supercomputers Sequoia ( Livermore National Laboratory ), Mira ( Argonne National Laboratory ) and JUQUEEN ( Research Center Juelich ). All supercomputers are based on the IBM Blue Gene / Q system .

The full list of Graph500 can be found at the link .

The Graph500 project, which is updated every six months, was first presented in June 2010 at the International Supercomputing Conference . The initiators of this list were made by experts from the user community of national research laboratories headed by the Sandia laboratory.
