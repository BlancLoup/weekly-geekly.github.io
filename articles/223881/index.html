Tabtabus 2014 Summer Fest - open registration for the summer IT-open-air fest



There is a desire to escape from the stuffy cities, apartments, social networks?

Beautiful lake, pine forest, tents, ears, live music and only IT people. If these words mean something to you, then this event is for you. June 21-22, Smolensk invites IT-representatives of business - programmers, designers, technologists, managers and IT-entrepreneurs - to the shore of beautiful Lake Sapsho.

In the Smolensk Lakes National Park, we will hold a two-day summer IT-fest under the open sky. Actively take a break from working days, share the experience of interesting projects and prepare the most delicious fish soup. Especially for teams from other cities and speakers - a bonus (see below).

To the participants


It will be the weekend. Come teams, participate in our quests, share experiences. The organizers will provide you with a transfer, a place for tents, a fire, firewood and a program. For all participants the freshest ear will be organized. Tents and meals on site you organize yourself.
