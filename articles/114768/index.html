HTML5 Camp: records of events published



February 17, 2011 in Moscow, the HTML5 Camp conference was held. This is the first of a series of international Microsoft conferences devoted to the topic of modern web standards and the future of the web. The event was attended by about 400 web professionals and over 24,000 joined the online broadcast. Links to speeches and presentations are posted on the event website .
