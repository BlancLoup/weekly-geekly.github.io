Design Camp - software update and mini contest for Habr



Friends, as you probably already know, this year we decided to continue the design history of last year and to hold the second Design Camp ( report from the first on Habr√© ).

Like last year, we are holding a conference with great support from the British Higher School of Design , calling you class speakers for interesting presentations on current topics and preparing an exciting program on the spot. We hope it will turn out even better than it was last year.


If you have seen the links to the conference site earlier, then I recommend to come again and see the program update (the remaining reports will be published in the coming days, but they are already known if you are in doubt).

Design Camp program conditionally consists of four large parallel tracks:
  • Applications, games, sites
  • Graphics, concepts, infographics
  • Design Thinking and UX
  • Devices, Prototypes and Usability


Immediately after the opening, you will find a round table with a discussion of the relationship between design and art.

Also Bart Decrem from The Walt Disney Company will come to us and talk about the company's experience and strategy in the field of mobile games, in particular about such hits as Tap Tap Revenge, Where's My Water, Temple Run: Brave and Oz. Do not miss!

The conference program is currently available on the website , we also announce individual reports on the event page on Facebook .
