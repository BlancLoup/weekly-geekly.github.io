Interior Design. 12 sites to start.

To whom it may be interesting:
- designers and interested in interior design;
- those who are looking for a designer to repair a house or a country house;
- to those who are going to make repairs themselves and are looking for ideas.

Catalogs of designers and interior projects (in alphabetical order):

designstory.ru - collect information about private designers, design studios, interior design shops and design schools in St. Petersburg.

infolios.ru is the youngest, but actively growing catalog of interior designers with the ability to host projects.

ivd.ru - on-line version of the magazine "Ideas of your home." One of the largest sites dedicated to repair and interior design. Very lively forum.

kombinator.ru - a catalog of designers and architects with the possibility of placing projects.

proekty.ru - directory of finished interior and architectural projects.

salon.ru is the largest catalog in runet of luxury interior projects.

Communities in LiveJournal:

community.livejournal.com/spb_interior/ - the largest and lively community
community.livejournal.com/ru_interiors/
community.livejournal.com/interior_ru/
community.livejournal.com/ru_comfortel/

Blogs about interior and decor:

omami.ru - not only about the interior. highly recommend.
obstanovka.com
