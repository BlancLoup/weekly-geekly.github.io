Open platform for developers of open source projects



At one of the meetings of the Program Committee of the HighLoad ++ High - Load Systems Developers Conference , the following idea emerged - an open tribune on HighLoad ++ for opensource developers. We give an equipped hall, form a special program for activists who are ready to tell you about their opensource product. Engine, framework, library, technology, database. Every 30 minutes, advertise yourself, engage, look for your followers!

How do you like the idea, who would like to try? Which product?


Leave applications in the comments, there will be many applications - we will make a rostrum. In order to tell about your project, you do not need to buy a ticket - the entrance for the speakers is free.


Audience HighLoad ++ - about 2500 developers, timlidov, DBA, devops-engineers and system administrators, technical managers and directors.

UPDATE: We have decided to open the open source platform. Details on the conference website .
