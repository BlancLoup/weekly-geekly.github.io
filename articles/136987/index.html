The first thing we need is to make the program think that we are designing the electrical part of the project (and not what we want!), But at the same time getting the results we need.
Laying of conductors with different methods of fasteners, we will perform a very exotic method. The conductor needs to be hammered into the base as a "pipe", and on the basis of this pipe it is necessary to create cable-carrying systems with the appropriate fasteners. For our project, they will need three:
  • Round steel for laying on the roof with manual arrangement of fasteners
  • Round steel over the wall with automatic fastener counting
  • Flat steel in the ground with automatic counting of fasteners
No cables will go through them, but the specification will be properly filled.
Accordingly, in the base you need to score:
  • Round steel conductor
  • Flat steel conductor
Just do not forget about hardware and components, we make them in the "normal" in the "materials" section:
  • Dowel
  • Screwless holder
  • Holder support
  • Cross connector for flat conductors
