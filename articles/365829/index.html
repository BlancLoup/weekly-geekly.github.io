Stability
I think that beginners will appreciate the smoothness of his movements in the low-power mode. And those who already had experience in managing quadcopters will be able to train their skills in passing obstacles on it.



Flight Modes
If we are talking about modes, I immediately note that there are three of them here. Changing the mode is very simple, just press the left button, under the index finger, on the controller. One squeak - the first mode. Two squeaks - the second mode. Three squeaks - the third mode.



And now, in the third mode, real fun begins to tame the quadrocopter. We give full throttle and full tilt forward and the quadrocopter sweeps away at great speed. And this is where the ‚ÄúHeadless Mode‚Äù comes in handy.

Functions
Headless Mode , aka "Headless Mode". Named so not because of his lack of conscience and carelessness, but for the functional removal of the ‚Äúhead‚Äù. In short, in this mode, it does not matter which side has blue front lights or red-rear lights. Where initially turned his face - there is now "forward." That is, you can fly off for tens of meters, twist around its axis, and then push the stick back and it will obediently fly to your side. For quadcopters who do not have GPS, this is an excellent mode so as not to get lost.

Flips . Like any self-respecting quadrocopter for entertainment, Nihui can also do flips in all directions with one button. To do this, press the right button under the index finger on the controller and press the right stick in the direction in which you want to flip.

By the way, turn off the video and take photos is very convenient with the right button under the index finger.
