3 new ways to deliver to Russia from eBay

From today, eBay opens up new ways to deliver goods to Russia for its customers.



Now added three new delivery methods, which I will discuss in more detail.

Parcel can be sent in one of three delivery methods (depends on the seller, if he added these methods).



The first way is ePacket ( RU Tracked Packet from China ). Implies delivery from China with a track number. They promise about 7-15 days. You need to receive such a package in your department.



Examples of products with ePacket: www.ebay.com/rpp/GBD-DCP-Electronics-Main/ePacket

The second method is Courier Delivery (Expedited International Courier Delivery to Russia). This is an express delivery in less than two weeks, the courier will bring it directly to the door.



Examples of goods with courier delivery: www.ebay.com/rpp/gbd-dcp-electronics-main/fast-courier-delivery

And the third way is to use the Dostami intermediary (the official partner of eBay). The service allows you to register a virtual address in the United States, to which you can order delivery. Thus, the American sellers do not formally send goods abroad, and Russian buyers are able to order parcels from the United States. Products from the online auction are collected in one package, which reduces costs. Delivery is carried out using its own service Dostami Express.

These are the good news from the eBay site. Now there is hope that we will receive parcels from China and other countries much faster.
