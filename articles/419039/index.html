Sberbank introduced several innovative robotic systems that may be widely used in the future.

The first one is Nika's robot , created at the Sberbank robotics center. Reportedly, this is the "avatar for artificial intelligence of the bank." System developers from the laboratory of robotics "Sberbank". Photo: RIA "News" / Evgeny Biyatov

Robots are not the only technological innovations promoted by the largest Russian bank. A few days ago, with the help of Sberbank, the work of a non-contact beer tap was organized. Such devices can be installed in bars, on the streets, stadiums, etc. To get a drink, just press a button on the screen with a choice of volume - and bring a contactless payment card, phone or clock to the device.


The first crane was put in the Moscow bar Sean O'Neill's Irish Pub, and there it is already popular. According to the owners of the establishment, every evening up to three dozen visitors prefer to fill a glass in this system, and not at a live bartender.
