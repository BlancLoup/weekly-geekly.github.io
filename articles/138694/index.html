Google office in Switzerland

Hi, Habrovchane. I spent the new year in the city of Zurich, where I talked with several employees of the Evil Corporation, and walked around their office.

In my post I will try to describe more of the social features of working at Google in Switzerland, but I cannot hide the interior of your office from you, it is gorgeous :)

All information received from employees of the company in words, so perhaps something may not be accurate or not at all correspond to reality.

So, in Zurich is the central office of Google in Europe.



It employs about 1,500 employees, of which about 60 are Russian speakers. Hunt them both from other offices (for example, many people move from the Moscow office of Google), and directly.

A typical day for a company employee in Zurich is as follows: ascent, small fees, walk to the office (Zurich is a small city itself, so you can walk from any district. In extreme cases, by bicycle). Breakfast in the office, work, lunch in the office, work, dinner in the office.
