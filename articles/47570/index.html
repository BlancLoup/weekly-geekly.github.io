Where to sell an old laptop?



A couple of months ago, scx and I were surprised to find that there was practically no easy search on runet on websites for buying / selling used laptops. Ads are usually placed in plain text. To find something, you need to read everything.

We were so surprised that we sat down and made bubuki.ru - a platform for buying / selling used laptops.


How Bubuk differ from ordinary flea markets and auctions:
- each announcement is not just text, but a set of specific technical parameters: processor, memory, video card, Bluetooth ... It‚Äôs just convenient to read - this is the first thing;
- secondly, it allowed us to do a normal human search. You are looking for not just a piece of text in the ad, and pick up a laptop on the parameters;
- Also, when entering search parameters immediately shows the number of suitable options. You can narrow your search until you get a reasonable amount of options to view. Check it out :)
- soon the base will begin to replenish with offers of used laptops from stores. And they abound in Russia. :) As a rule, it is safer to buy a laptop in the store, although it is more expensive;
- here you can find information about firms engaged in urgent purchase of laptops;
- as well as selling used laptops on credit (under current conditions is not yet relevant :)).

From pleasant things - the ability to increase the effectiveness of your ad by inserting the ad code into a blog or forum.

The site has been around for about a month. During this time, it appeared 250 ads from 26 cities of Russia. Most of all from Moscow - 132 ads. Every day, according to Google analytics, about 300 people visit bubuki.

From interesting, we noticed that many people place ads for the sale of brand new laptops. Someone won in the drawing, someone gave a prize at work with a laptop.

We have already asked one store to post information about it. I hope after the publication of this post will be more stores.

We made the platform fairly versatile in advance. So in the near future there will be the same convenient sites for mobile phones, bicycles, snowboards, car tires, etc.
