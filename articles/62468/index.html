We register the program, we receive the certificate

I took from the Federal Institute of Industrial Property (FIPS) a certificate for the program.

We registered fairly quickly: 1.5 months had passed since the application was submitted, before the certificate was prepared. Of the financial costs - only the payment of state duty of about 1,200 rubles. The application itself consisted of 74 sheets of a fragment of the source code of the system and 5 sheets of other papers.

Briefly the registration process itself:
1) I sent a request to the email address otd69@rupto.ru of the relevant department of FIPS, stating that documents for registering the computer program are needed

Subject: Request for a package of documents for registration of a computer
Text of the letter:
Good day.
Please send a package of documents that must be prepared for the official registration of the computer.
1) the object of registration of the computer;
2) the status of the legal owner of a legal entity;
3) number of authors 4 people.
Respectfully,
Dmitry Borozdin

2) After a few days of waiting, I called them, asked me to pay attention to my request, so our state services work.
Deputy Head of the registration department of the Pr, computer, database and TIMS FGU FIPS Kulikovsky Vadim Alexandrovich tel. (499) 240-63-54
The next day, a letter arrived with documents that had to be filled out and instructions.
3) I paid the state. fee in the bank.
4) Further I prepared the application and carried it to the FIPS (reception days: Tuesday, Thursday).
5) I called the FIPS 2 months after the submission of the application, for a long time I found out the necessary phone number (+7 (499) 240-33-42 - the phone number of the expert group of the department).
6) In FIPS, I definitely needed a seal - when I arrived there for a certificate, I had to additionally sew the sheets with the source code, put a list and a seal on them.
