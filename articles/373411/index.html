A series of seminars about the new features of TrueConf Server 4.3.8



From May 16 to May 26 in the largest cities of Russia a series of seminars will be held by our beloved partner, leading vendor of video conferencing systems in the CIS, TrueConf. The seminars will be devoted to the new features of TrueConf Server 4.3.8 and the secrets of the selection of video conferencing equipment for workplaces and halls.

Seminars will be held on May 16 in Kazan, on May 18 in Yekaterinburg, on May 23 in Moscow and on May 26 in St. Petersburg. Admission is free, but you still have to register.

Speakers will include: Development Director of TrueConf - Dmitry Odintsov, along with colleagues from Unitsolutions, Tayle / Aver, Hi-Tech Media and STSS. They will talk about the latest news in the world of video conferencing and AV, help you choose the right equipment for your meeting room and share the secrets of successful integration of video conferencing systems with third-party IT resources.

In the program of seminars:

  • Training in administration and live demonstration of all the features of the new version of TrueConf Server 4.3.8.
  • Secrets of the selection of equipment for conference rooms and meeting rooms.
  • This is a workshop from developers for IT and AV professionals. Guaranteed pleasant atmosphere and a lot of useful practical information.

No one will go hungry, and at the end of the seminar all participants will be issued certificates of advanced training.
