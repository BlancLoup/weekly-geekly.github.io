Startup Digest: 10 nearest IT events in Moscow

This is a selection of events to be held in the capital in the coming days. In my list today there are mitapas devoted to software development, as well as technological hackathons.


Photo by: office hours at GLPH.media

Techstars Startup Digest is my weekly event distribution for startups, developers, designers and all those interested in IT.

I have been doing Newzletter in Moscow since 2012, and the Startup Digest itself has appeared earlier. Here I talked about how he developed and eventually teamed up with Techstars.

For mailing, we select useful (by itself) and free (or for reasonable money) events, where you can learn something new, upgrade your skills and communicate with colleagues.

1. Technopolis: Copter Hack


When: Fri, Oct. 19 - Sun, Oct. 21
What time: 19:00
Where: Technopolis "Moscow", Volgograd, etc., 42, b.5
How much is: Free

Participants will be given 30 hours to assemble a quadrocopter. Winners will receive valuable prizes from hackathon partners. Pupils of the senior classes, students, graduate students and graduates of universities are invited to participate - all who are interested in unmanned aircraft systems.


2. Boiling Point: Space Apps Challenge


When: Sat, October 20
What time: 7:00
Where: Boiling Point, Small Konyushkovsky Lane, 2
How much is: Free

On this international hackathon from NASA, a team of representatives from all professions, from developers to astrophysicists, can try their hand. The participants will have to develop a draft software product in two days that will help humanity master space or solve the long-standing problems of earthlings - the shortage of water, food and clean air.


3. RO: MosFinData


When: Sat, October 20
What time: 10:00
Where: "Red October", Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, p. 3
How much is: Free

Hackathon from the Moscow Department of Finance for developers, designers and data journalists. You can create different projects: applications, services, bots, plugins, infographics. The main thing is for the project to solve socially significant tasks in the context of finance. For example, he answered the questions of users ‚Äúwhere are our taxes going?‚Äù Or ‚Äúhow is the budget spent?‚Äù. The working material will be open data of state bodies.


4. RO: #Itsubbotnik


When: Sat, October 27
What time: 11:00
Where: "Red October", Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, p. 3
How much is: Free

Mitap was created for IT professionals to share experiences and interesting cases with each other. The program of the autumn meeting was divided into three streams: Big Data & Java, Frontend & Javascript and business analytics. The topics of the reports will be different: from cases of Kafka implementation to working with the native capabilities of the web and recipes for rescuing customer relationships.


5. DI Telegraph: Real Innovations


When: Sat, October 27
What time: 10:00
Where: DI Telegraph, Tverskaya, d.7
How much is: $ 10

Talented startups and development teams will build solutions for the city, media and retail on product concepts. Fest will be held in the interactive foresight mode with leading practitioners, experts and technologists. At the end of the event, teams will present their decisions in the format of TED-performances. A demo zone, lecture rooms will be held at the site, and master classes will be held. Any guest will be able to break his head over quests in augmented reality.


6. MSU: SINCOPA


When: Mon, Oct. 29
What time: 19:00
Where: Faculty of Economics of Moscow State University, Leninskie Gory, 1, p.46
How much is: Free

7. FRIA: Corporate Innovation Day


When: Thu, November 1st
What time: 10:00
Where: FRIA, st. Myasnitskaya, 13, p. 18
How much is: Free

Why do companies need digital transformation? How to start the introduction of new technologies? These questions will be answered by FRIA experts and invited speakers - representatives of Leroy Merlin, MTS, Kirovsky Plant, Megafon and other companies. But they will not just answer, but will talk about personal experience in introducing innovations. The event will include open presentations and panel discussions, workshops and pitch sessions, consultations and discussion tools. Recommended for representatives of large businesses.

What time: 9:00
Where: International Trade Center, Krasnopresnenskaya Embankment, 12

Voice assistants, smart chat bots, conversational interfaces: analytics, technology overview and first-hand cases. The conference workspace will be divided into four sections. When: Tue, November 20
What time: 9:00
Where: Digital October, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, p. 3

10. Opening Arena: Sport Innovations


When: Thu, November 22
What time: 8:30
Where: "Discovery Arena", Volokolamsk Highway, 69
How much is: Free

Here we will talk about how to apply digital solutions in the sports industry.
