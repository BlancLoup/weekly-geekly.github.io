Come to experience new flash storage systems for data centers

Hello!
Feel and potest iron worth a million dollars can not every day . So, we have a new Violin Memory system, and I invite you for a test drive. Yes, by the way, it was we who, at one of such test drives for a dispute, disconnected a switch from the working rack in the data center. But this time we will not do that.

What kind of iron is this?


This is a storage system that keeps the response time for reading and writing for about 1 ms under high load and at the same time gives the smallest price for an I / O operation in a known galaxy. The main vocation of life is to clean bottlenecks in a bunch of cases.

Who do I call:

  • Those who handle small amounts of data (up to 50 TB), where the response time and the number of I / O operations per second are extremely critical.
  • Those who have Oracle, 1C, SAP or similar large-scale systems, or complex analytical queries (on conventional storage systems are ‚Äúchewed‚Äù on 10-15 hours each).
  • Those to whom the decision in cost from $ 300 kilobaksov to about one million approaches. That is clearly not a small business.
  • Heads of divisions at whom system administrators complain of work of SHD.
  • Heads of data centers and similar sites to assess how you can save.


Examples from practice:


  • Suppose a request is made to perform an analytical operation, which consists of 10,000 transactions to different databases. If you place all the bases on Violin, the speed will increase 10-11 times. Because with a conventional system, each transaction takes 2-3-4 milliseconds, and here - one. One foreign bank made such requests on a conventional storage system for 300 minutes, after the introduction of Violin - for 11.
  • With massive virtualization on thousands of machines. For example, at the beginning of the working day, when all employees of the call-center come through thin clients, the load on the storage system is very large (the so-called boot-storm occurs), and Violin with a full flash architecture copes with it.


About prices


  • Yes, it is expensive when you do not have high-performance tasks.
  • This is beneficial when you need great speed.
  • With the right tasks, this is more profitable than buying storage systems with 10-15 flash drives.
  • Saves space: the Violin system - 3 units, similar to the storage system - up to 2-3 cabinets. That is, ultimately, saving money again.
  • Saves on complex software licenses: usually minus 70% of the cost.
  • It is beneficial when the speed of customer service rests on the speed of the application.
