Team LinguaLeo goes to Montenegro for 2 months. On board 5 empty seats! The mission of the team is to release the most important releases.



The history of the lingual web service LinguaLeo began with a 6-month development on this island in Thailand. It was there that a small team released a beta version of the service. Sea and Jungle helped!

More than two years have passed. Today, there are 1,300,000 registered users on LinguaLeo, the daily audience is about 80,000 people. But while LinguaLeo is available only for Russian-speaking users ... There is an iPhone application and a beta version of the WinPhone application, but there is no Android application ... There is a Jungle , a convenient personal dictionary and workout for expanding vocabulary, but there are almost no tools for learning grammar and phrases ... And all this is so close, so necessary, and so important for humanity, the qualitative study of foreign languages. In addition, a million service audience still does not have the opportunity to communicate with each other live and practice English ...

What to do?


We have a good old hack - to leave the entire development team (now ~ 20 people) to another world, free from the conventions of the metropolis. Devote a week to communication, understanding the problems of users learning languages, discussing ideas and concepts. And then - a maximum of communication, teamwork and drive to achieve the goal: the desired releases!

This time we chose Montenegro. Sea, mountains, sun, beach and friendly Serbs!







With what result do we want to return to the capital?


We want to finally make LinguaLeo multilingual! Implement the possibility of live communication between users, convenient tools for mastering grammar, launch an application for Android, iPad ... Make LinguaLeo as useful as it can be! We have a large support group on a big land! During these two months, we plan to implement most of these ideas.
And we need your support!

To implement our plans, our team lacks 5 good specialists:
- Android developer ,
- PHP developer ,
- JavaScript developer ,
- Lingvista Manager ,
- System administrator .

If you are focused on opportunities, are interested in languages, know how to play in a team and are sufficiently savvy in your business, we are waiting for you! We guarantee a lot of interesting tasks, fan, operations on the leading edge of theory and practice, dedication to the secrets and know-how of education, learning languages, motivation and game processes. Come to us and contribute to the creation of a world-class product for the study and practice of foreign languages. If you become part of our team, you will never forget these 2 months! And after that you will be able to work for the benefit of the multilingual world together with the whole LinguaLeo team in the Strogino Technopark in Moscow.

In the meantime, you are thinking, learn more about LinguaLeo, think about how you could help LinguaLeo become the most convenient and popular web service for learning and practicing foreign languages.
