RSS aggregator with VKontakte and Twitter

I am an active user of Google Reader, I find out all the news from him, and the news about its closure could not pass by. Having looked at alternative and open source projects, I could not find for myself the usual and suitable one.
Having estimated by time, I thought that I could write such an aggregator myself, leaving the main functions and usability of Google Reader.

Meet myreader.info :


Key features:
  • minimalistic interface;
  • two-pane navigation with automatic loading of publications during scrolling;
  • navigation between publications using hotkeys (k or p - previous, j or n - next, space - page forward, shift + space - page back);
  • Auto-mark publication as read;
  • selected publications;
  • automatic extraction of links to the RSS-feed by a link to the site;
  • Sign in with a Google account
  • ability to import favorites and subscriptions from the Google Reader dump via takeout.

Not without pluses, in myreader.info you can subscribe to updates of users on VKontakte and Twitter.
In the plans, the choice of the sorting method, the folder for subscriptions, the renaming of subscriptions.

PS After the baptism of fire and tests, I will download the source code on github, so that everyone can raise their own Google Reader.
