HSE Business Incubator opens the second set of participants of the FINTECH program

HSE Business Incubator launches the second set of Fintech HSE {Pro} Fintech software . The industry track partners will be a decentralized platform for crowdfunding and managing Waves communities and the National Settlement Depository (NSD). Until December 4, inclusive open applications. Start of the program is scheduled for December 12th.

The program is aimed at developing projects in the field of financial and blockchain technologies, in particular services for banks and their clients, personal finance management systems, p2p services, investment systems, payments, transfers and other fintech products.

Invited to participate technology projects in the early stages, which require industry and business expertise, as well as startups interested in attracting investors and partners.

During the four months of the intensive program, the projects will pass the required minimum during the educational course: they will become familiar with lean startup methodology, launch MVP and gain user base, and also receive first sales, which will confirm that there is a market request. Residents will be able to launch a pilot with track partner banks. Their list will be announced later.

The first flow of HSE {Pro} Fintech was held from May to September 2016 with the support of Bank Saint Petersburg, NSD, Yandex, QIWI and Smarthub. Ten projects were graduates, including Suretly, which received a development grant from HSE, and iCoinSoftware, which continues to cooperate with NSD.

Learn more and submit an application on the site fintech.inc.hse.ru
