Then the fathers of Russian democracy will save Superfreevpn.com


Superfreevpn.com is a vpn-service that does not require installation of any software, is free for everyone with almost no restrictions (only P2P does not work), in particular, it allows you to watch Youtube even in HD quality.

To work, you need to create a remote access connection with the following parameters:
VPN Type: PPTP VPN
Encryption: Enabled (or Auto)
VPN Server: superfreevpn.com
VPN Username: free
VPN Password: changes a couple of times a day, you need to look at the main page of Superfreevpn.com


We start the connection ... And we are on the Internet without borders through an encrypted connection. A portable installation of TOR or Opera (with Turbo on), launched from a USB flash drive, will complete the picture of prosperity and peace of mind.
