Aha! -Moments: how insight comes

Life can be divided into two parts: until the moment when you understand your purpose, and after. Between them there is only a moment - the moment of illumination. A single idea, through which just a person turns into a person with a purpose.

About 2,200 years ago, Archimedes climbed into the bath and exclaimed: "Eureka!"

It means "found." It was at that moment that Archimedes found a solution for a problem that had not given him peace for a long time.

It was a moment of sudden discovery. "The Effect of Eureka." A moment of deep understanding. Epiphany, which is translated from the Greek means "the manifestation of the divine." At this moment, for a previously unsolvable problem, a simple and obvious solution suddenly appears.
