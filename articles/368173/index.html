Personal gun safe with a fingerprint scanner is opened with a clip


Self safe

Safe GunVault GVB1000 is positioned as a compact and secure storage of personal firearms, and specifically - the gun. The product is made of armored steel, protected from hacking by a lock with a fingerprint scanner and costs $ 399 on the manufacturer's website .

But the video appeared in the network, in which the author reveals this ultra-modern weapon safe with the help of a usual paper clip and straight hands.

Unfortunately, neither the reservation nor the scanner protect the main (after the hull) part of the safe - the locking mechanism. You can access it through the side openings, pushing a straightened paperclip or piece of wire inside, and open the door so that the owner will never even know about it.
