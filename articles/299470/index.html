Silicon Valley Accelerators and Business Incubators

In Silicon Valley, a huge number of accelerators for startups. We passed 1 pre-accelerator and 4 accelerators. These business incubators / start-up accelerators differ in the complexity of hitting them, the terms of interaction and financing of your startup. It is about them that we tell in our video.

- Startup Next - pre-accelerator, which is easy enough to get at the very early stages, helps prepare for other accelerators. Usually takes a small amount of money from each team member. The business incubator is educational in terms of customer development ( Customer Development ), pitch, Product Market Fit , assessing the size of the market and obtaining financing.
- Forward Accelerator is a rather expensive business incubator that accepts commands that are ready to pay to work with them. They give step-by-step assignments to teams where startups work on their ideas and prepare for meetings with investors.
- Society 3 is an accelerator, positioning itself as an accelerator, helping to attract a large number of users. In fact, he teaches more to use his platform, which should help create a viral effect.
- NSF i-corps is the state educational program for the early stages of startups, where the course on the Business Model Canvas by Steve Blank is being worked out and motivating startups to personally communicate and interview at least 100 potential and actual users of their product.
- Founders Space is the best accelerator for foreign startups in Silicon Valley according to Forbes. The business program is set up to learn how to sell your idea to users and investors.
- One Traction - offers different structured programs depending on the startup stage.
