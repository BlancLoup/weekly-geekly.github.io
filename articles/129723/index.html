Competition for the new default design LiveStreet CMS

The current default design / template has served faithfully for almost 3 years, many people like it for its simplicity, ease and versatility, someone does not like it :)

And now the time has come and there is a need to update it, make restyling.
We again appeal to the community for help and announce a competition for the new default design LiveStreet !

Prizes:
  • 1st place - 20 000 p . and the inclusion of design in the distribution as a default copyright author
  • 2nd place - 15 000 p.
  • 3rd place - 10 000 p.

The terms of participation:
  • You need to design LiveStreet 0.5 functionality, but at the discretion of the participant it can change and modify the functionality for your design.
  • To participate in the competition, it is necessary to provide layouts (any of the formats: psd, png, html, ..) of the three main pages: main , topic , user profile
  • Only new designs that have not been published anywhere before are allowed to participate. One person can offer any number of options.
  • To participate, it is sufficient until November 15, 2011 to publish your version of the design on the website livestreet.ru in the blog "Participants in the competition for a new design LiveStreet"
  • The winner will be selected by voting (drawing up a short list of 10 designs) and the choice of the project administration
  • The competition may be declared not held due to the small number of participants and the low level of submitted designs.
  • To receive the prize winners must provide the source PSD or HTML layouts of their design. Designs should be distributed free of charge.
  • To receive the prize, the winner, who won the 1st place, must provide layouts of all meaningful (different) LiveStreet 0.5 pages within a month after the end of the contest
