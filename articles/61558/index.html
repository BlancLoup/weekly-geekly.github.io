I already wrote about a very interesting and promising technology called Cappuccino. Today I want to draw your attention to the second most elaborate project on this new framework - almost.at .

The debuted Cappuccino project remains 280slides from the authors of Objective-J and Cappucino himself. Almost.at shows us that it is possible to make an even more functional project on the emerging framework.

And so, what exactly is almost.at? This is a service that tracks interesting (from the point of view of the administration) topics on the network: on Twitter (in the form of tweets and pictures) and simply by keywords. It does it in a beautiful mac-like interface.
