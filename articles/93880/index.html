Svyaz-Expocomm 2010: what did the big three show?

From May 11 to May 14, Moscow hosted the twenty-second annual exhibition devoted to the field of information communications - Svyaz-Expocomm. Of course, among the companies participating in the exhibition, there were mobile operators, who demonstrated their new technologies in the allotted days.

On the stands of mobile operators, those products and technologies that now seem to be fantasy seem to be presented to us, however, they will soon become quite ordinary things.

MTS at the Svyaz-Expocomm 2010 was represented at the booth of Sistema with Comstar-UTS and MTS Strim - by the way, all these companies will be replaced by the MTS single brand by the end of 2011, - where it showed a number of unconditionally innovative solutions for both business and the consumer market.

So, MTS gave a glimpse into the future, demonstrating LTE technology , or 4G, which allows data transfer at speeds up to 326.4 Mbit / s - this is more than twice as fast as WiMAX.
