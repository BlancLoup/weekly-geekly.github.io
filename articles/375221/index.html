360 panoramic video support will appear on Youtube at the end of this quarter.

Panoramic 360 HD camera Giroptic 360cam raised $ 1.4 million on Kickstarter . The 360cam camera streams video over WiFi, records video, and takes pictures.

At the present CES 2015 exhibition, the French company Giroptic announced that its 360cam camera will be the first camera that natively will support Youtube. Giroptic also states that Google will allow users of their cameras to upload panoramic video in Street View.

At the moment, video panoramic cameras from other manufacturers can only be viewed in specialized players. With the advent of support for panoramic video on Youtube, interest in such cameras will increase.
