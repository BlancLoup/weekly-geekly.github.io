Higher education, non-representative sample

The topic of education in Russia does not give me rest. I will give a little story from personal experience. I do not pretend to objectivity, therefore I named the topic accordingly.

So, 6 years ago, I was actively interested in everything related to computers, and therefore I had only one option as a future programmer - a programmer. Finishing grade 11 in the local high school, I was faced with the problem of choosing a faculty. Yes, yes, it is the faculty, since we have one de facto university in Saransk.
