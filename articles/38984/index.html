Clock Delay - a watch for real geeks for $ 1885

If you dream of a watch for which it will be very difficult to determine the time, which will take a lot of space, and besides, and will cost incredibly expensive, then something better than Clock Delay from the developer STUDIObloomm, you are unlikely to find .



This bulky device is a system of three gears, which are responsible for displaying hours, minutes and seconds - the current time can be found in the place where the teeth of these wheels are closest. Size of construction: 35 cm. X 35 cm. X 48 cm.

The original Clock Delay, of course, attracts attention, especially when you find out that they cost 1885 dollars. And imagine how they will look at you on the floor in the room or on the table!
