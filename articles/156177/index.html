Sony Xperia TX goes on sale



Another sales start from Sony - this time in the pre-New Year race for the right to get under someone's tree, Sony Xperia TX has joined. The new flagship of the company will go on sale at a price of 24,990 p. in the beginning of December.

Lucky, who will find Xperia TX under the tree, will get:

  • 4.6-inch HD screen with a resolution of 1280x720 pixels
  • Chipset Snapdragon S4 based on dual-core processor 1,5 GHz Krait (again, the most powerful in the line of Xperia)
  • 13 megapixel camera with Fast Start Fast Capture
  • Record video in 1080p format (main camera), 720p (front camera)
  • PlayStation Certified Certification
  • Android 4.0.4 on board
  • NFC-module for quick activation of functions, data transfer and payment
  • Black, white and pink body colors to choose from
  • BA900 1750 mAh battery
  • Compact block of the memory of increased power of ER880
  • Wired stereo MH750
  • USB cable for charging and syncing

In early December, the smartphone will appear in the Connected and Sony brand stores. But now you can pre-order the Xperia TX in the Sony Store and Connected .
