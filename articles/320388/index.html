Logeek Night St. Petersburg, February 16



Petersburg, great news: February 16, we are waiting for everyone at the informal meeting of IT specialists Logeek Night!

What is Logeek Night? First of all you need to know that Logeek night is a free event from LTS. This is an informal meeting of IT-specialists with the opportunity to communicate with like-minded people, share experiences and gain new knowledge.

On February 16 we meet at Sasha's Bar, which is on Marat 5, discuss interesting topics on Java and QA, communicate and treat ourselves to pizza.

In the program of performance of our employees:

Nikita Khokhlov will introduce you to TMMi, which is a recognized standard in software testing. You can not only get acquainted with this model, but also find out how the techniques and techniques used can be applied in real life.
Kirill Shirokov will tell you about the experience of using Apache Storm, namely: channel typing, patterns, why intermediate interludes are needed, flow control: bolts against threads, bitemporality, RPC.

Come chat will be interesting!

Please note that the event is free, but registration is required here .
