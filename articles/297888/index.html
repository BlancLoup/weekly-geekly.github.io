Report on the results of "My Circle" for December 2015

Congratulations to all the last New Year holidays! Despite the longest festivities of the year, when the majority ceases to work two weeks before the New Year, the statistics of My Circle pleases.

First, we have the first small record: in December, on average, we have 8 responses per job. Secondly, this is our third in a row report. And today we will not only show what the competition was among IT-specialists in December 2015, but also show the three-month dynamics of the average response for the most popular vacancies.


In December, about 400 vacancies were posted on My Circle, which is 20% less than usual. This is due, of course, to the fact that two weeks before the New Year, many companies stopped looking for and interviewing new employees, postponing this sensitive issue until next year. At the same time, curiously, the total number of responses remained unchanged, and on average, 7.98 specialists responded to each job in December. Compared with September and October, this is an increase of 20%.
