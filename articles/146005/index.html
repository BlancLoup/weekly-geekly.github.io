New Russian applications in Google Chrome

Arkady Khachkovanyan, Google Russia Marketing Manager, Anatoly Lebedev, Google EMEA New Business Development Manager

In December 2010, we announced the launch of the Chrome Web Store (Chrome Web Store), a catalog of web applications, extensions and themes for the Google Chrome browser. Then, three years ago, there were hundreds of them, today there are already tens of thousands, and the number of installations is estimated at millions.

By themselves, web applications are interactive programs that run directly from the browser. They can have a wide range of functions or perform one specific task, such as editing photos. Each of them has its own page where you can read comments, see ratings and leave your review. You can set those that you need, for example, for work, leisure, or budget planning, and also search by category. The icon of the installed application will be displayed in the browser tabs, and you will always have quick access to it. And if you work on multiple computers, you can synchronize all applications, extensions and themes using the synchronization function of the browser.

Applications have other benefits. For example, site owners who develop applications for Google Chrome often add features not available on the site itself. Some applications may even work offline: for example, Gmail Offline will allow you to read mail and write letters even when your computer is not connected to the Internet.

The number of applications developed in Russia is growing very rapidly. Today, we are happy to share some of the updates prepared by well-known publishers specifically for Russian Google Chrome users. Now you can watch movies and TV channels, play Cut the Rope, write an online diary and even buy equipment. And all this is right in your Chrome browser window!

Cut the Rope (Zeptolab) - one of the most popular Google Play apps - now in the Chrome Web Store! The task is simple - the player must cut the rope and feed a green monster named Om Nom.

Russia TV - you can watch the latest news live online via the Chrome browser! Online broadcast of popular TV channels in Russia: Russia 1, Russia 2, Russia 24, Moscow 24, Russian Roman and others, as well as broadcasts of the leading sports and cultural events in Russia and the world, the exclusive rights to which belong to VGTRK. Already through the app, you can watch Russia 1 and Russia 2 live on the European Football Championship.

MVideo is an application from one of the largest Russian retailers that allows you to search and sort products by price, brand and popularity. Right in the Google Chrome window, you can find in which store the product you are interested in and make a purchase.

LiveInternet.Dnevniki Offline - the application is designed for users of the blog service LiveInternet.ru. In addition to the standard features presented on the site, the application for Chrome allows you to simplify the appearance of messages and diaries, automatically preview messages and even read posts and comments without access to the Internet.

Tanchiki (Alawar) is a game based on the classic arcade games Tank Force and Battle City. 55 levels of military simulator, in which you can feel like a tank commander, will not let you get bored!

Easyfinance - keep track of your expenses even easier. Thanks to this application you can get quick access to information on accounts and plan a budget.

Wunderlist is a task manager that helps you keep track of which things have already been done and what time it is to begin. Tasks are automatically synchronized with desktop and mobile applications. You can also share them with friends and colleagues.

VideoWave - a catalog of more than 7,000 legal TV shows and programs, cartoons and new movies. At the same time, more than 90% of video content is open for free viewing. If the video is closed, the system will prompt you to go to the owner's website, where it will be available after payment.

Join millions of Chrome users around the world and enjoy these and many other Chrome Web Store apps!
