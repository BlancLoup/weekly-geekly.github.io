Xbox One in Russia on September 5th. GTA V on PC, Xbox One and PS4 also fall



This week just two great news for console lovers and not only: Xbox One will be available in the fall, GTA V too.



XBOX One in Russia in September



Microsoft has announced that official sales of the Xbox One gaming console will begin in Russia on September 5th of this year. Buyers will be available as a simple version of the Xbox One (one wireless controller and a 14-day Xbox Live Gold subscription), as well as the Kinect version. The latter, among other things, allows recognizing the owner and reading QR codes, as well as participating in Skype videoconferences. The manufacturer promised to inform about the prices for Xbox One for the Russian retail later.
