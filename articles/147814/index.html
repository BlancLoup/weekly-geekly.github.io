Google Translate has started to show examples of sentences with translated words.



Examples of sentences have long been a standard feature of many electronic and paper dictionaries. Since yesterday, Google Translate also shows sentences that use translated words, according to TechCrunch .

Unlike traditional dictionaries, which carefully verify these sentences, Google uses the latest news phrases. This, according to Google , ensures that users will understand the new words, "watching them in their natural habitat."

To use this function, translate the necessary words and click on the new icon under the translation. Unfortunately, if traditional dictionaries show usage examples for each meaning of a word, then Google apparently chooses sentences randomly, and they may not display all meanings. However, Google promises to continue improving and enriching the corpus of example sentences.
