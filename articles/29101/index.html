Godville - a game without a player

Many probably remember the game Progress Quest - a unique RPG that does not require the attention of the player and thereby saving him a lot of time that can be spent more usefully. Unfortunately, in recent years it has not developed, and the need to launch a local client is tiring.

Let me introduce you to Godville - a variation on the PQ theme, adapted for online. Nothing but a browser is needed - once created, the hero will tirelessly perform feats, humbly waiting for your next visit to him.


The game is quietly written in free time by two people and so far is far from perfect. But the desired meditative effect is noted already now, so it will be glad to see new users :)
