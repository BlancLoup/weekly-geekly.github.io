New tourist service Turometr.ru

What is a product or service?


[turometr.ru] is an international travel portal offering a unique web service for tourists from all over the world - an opportunity in one place, with a minimum of time, in an accessible way, to get the necessary and high-quality information about tourist sites, routes, countries, to book hotel, book air and train tickets, pick up a tour, find a companion, on the one hand; to express oneself, to make feedback, to give an assessment through communication (a tourist social network), on the other hand.

[turometr.ru] are services for professionals (promotion of the tourist product) and for tourists (realization of the tourist product through on-line booking and development of the tourist Internet community, the global social network of users.


What problem does it solve in the market?


To book a tour, hotel, cruise or tickets, a Russian tourist now has to contact several agents (offline sales) or travel sites for information in order to receive complete, reliable and necessary information. The latter can be very difficult to do, as the offered information is sometimes no longer relevant for travelers, does not answer the frequently asked questions of tourists, does not reflect both the positive and negative aspects of recreation in the resorts of the world. Everywhere you can find thousands of resources, stuffed with outdated and duplicate content, which often contribute to the search system of tours, catalogs of hotels and resorts. Search and reservation forms for tours show users descriptions of hotels presented in one database, often showing only the hotel name and the message that "hotel information is temporarily not available." Blogs, social networks, contacts, forums, services for the storage and exchange of photos and videos - all these sites that we visit regularly. However, we use several resources instead of one full.
