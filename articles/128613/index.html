Microsoft is negotiating the purchase of Yahoo again



So far this is only preliminary data, but some sources, including BusinessInsider, claim that Microsoft is in talks with Yahoo again. The essence of the negotiations - the purchase of the entire company. Thus, the Redmond giant returns to the essence of the 2008 negotiations. It was then that Jeffrey Young, the former CEO of Yahoo, refused, and from that time the company started having problems. It is worth recalling that while Microsoft offered 44.6 billion dollars for Yahoo, this amount at that time exceeded the real value of the company. Now Yahoo's capitalization is $ 18.6 billion.

On Habr√© already published information that after Bartz left the post of CEO of the company, several parties appeared interested in buying all the assets of Yahoo or a certain part of the company. Among others was the private investment fund Silver Lake Partners. So far, the company does not deny the possibility of selling its assets, but Yahoo representatives believe that there is no hurry.

Integration with Microsoft can be beneficial for both companies. For Yahoo, this means the end of all problems, both financial and structural. Most likely, Microsoft already accurately represents how Yahoo can be used with maximum expediency. For Microsoft, such a purchase means the acquisition of several interesting projects, plus a fairly large proportion of traffic.

Generally speaking, over the past couple of years, Microsoft has received several profitable partners at once, plus bought several services that corporations need. The main purchase, of course, remains Skype. In addition, Microsoft was able to agree with Nokia on long-term strategic cooperation. If Microsoft does buy Yahoo, the corporation will be stronger than ever.
