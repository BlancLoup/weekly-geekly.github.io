Why I do not believe in the domestic space - a view from the inside

For several years I have been working as a programmer at one of the enterprises of Roscosmos - FSUE XXXXX and in this post I would like to share my impressions about this organization.

The main problem of the domestic high-tech industries of the 90s was a general lack of funds. With reference to the present, this problem is practically absent. Salaries are paid, repairs are carried out, new expensive equipment is purchased and even sometimes licensed software. At this positive points end.

Hello, we are looking for talents


It is no secret that state-owned enterprises lack young specialists, but what do enterprises do to solve this issue? It so happened that I got to work here through an acquaintance, being a graduate student. At the same time, I received quite an interesting task, the result of which is applied in practice. Was it possible to get to this place honestly? I think no. This job simply wasn't on the list. Whether the heads of departments do not send requests to the personnel department, or whether they dissolve without a trace.
The personnel department is a set of aunts who can do, for example, the following things:

1) To calculate the heat dissipation on the boards, take a specialist in building heat engineering.
2) Send in-service training information to select specific courses, get selected courses and ... PROFIT enter into contracts for other courses. Including those for which the senders did not have the necessary basic knowledge.

Also, the personnel department suspects the existence of sites for job search, but for some reason it is squeamish about using them. Therefore, vacancies can be found only on the site of the enterprise.
The second way to get fresh blood is students. Students come to practice by orders from above, and nobody cares that they may have nothing to occupy. In my university, I had to report in some detail on the work done, there was a mini-defense, but this is rather the exception to the rule. The result of the practice of many students - well-established days and all. If a student walks one or two days a week, it is considered that he cannot be charged with anything, although, given the general inhibition of all processes in the enterprise, this is the optimal number of days to visit in order not to sleep at work. Moreover, if the student is still given a task, and he kicks the bolts, then nothing can be done with it. He was sent by agreement with the Institute. Naturally, after serving the allotted time, the student dumps far and forever. Students are paid depending on their abilities, but generally not enough.
