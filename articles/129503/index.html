Brand catalog for $ 250 (with .com domains)

A vivid example for domainers how to build a business model: the new Stylate service. Here, a start-up startup can pick up a beautiful name for the site - and immediately buy the selected .com domain along with the logo in vector format. Logo comes as a bonus. Most importantly, the domain + logo packages are sold at a single price of $ 250. This is a real dumping on the market of beautiful domains, where transactions often take place in the region of $ 10 thousand.



So far, the Stylate catalog is quite modest: only 33 brands are offered for sale, but a rubricator for 31 industries is already ready. Obviously, they will replenish the range.

Stylate is also ready to buy beautiful domains from other domains, but now they have been filled up with lists of hundreds of names, so the guys are reviewing the financial conditions.
