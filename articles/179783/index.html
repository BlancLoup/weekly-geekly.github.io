PayPal received the Russian license of NPO

In continuation of the topic .

PayPal LLC, the Russian representative office of the international payment system PayPal, has received a license from a non-bank credit organization . On May 14, the Russian LLC paid up the authorized capital in the amount of 18 million rubles, after which the company received a license from the Central Bank of the Russian Federation.

Obtaining such a license will allow domestic PayPal users to withdraw money from their account in this system. Previously, the Russians could only replenish their PayPal account using credit cards. And the lack of the ability to withdraw funds from the account, according to experts, repelled many users and sellers from working with the system.
