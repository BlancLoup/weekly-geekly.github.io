WysiBB - WYSIWYG BBCode Editor

Currently WYSIWYG editors are becoming increasingly popular. They are popular because of their ease of use for ordinary users. But for the most part, most of these wave editors do a good job with creating HTML content and only a small part of them can create content with BBcode markup. And if they can, then set up these editors to fit their needs is a very, very difficult task.

Since one of the projects also used the BBCode markup format, I began my work on the convenient WYSIWYG editor BBCode . The result is a product called WysiBB .




The project is currently in the process of active beta testing and has not yet a very extensive and complete description of all its capabilities. But at once I would like to note a few moments why he deserves your attention.

  • BBCode visual input
  • Connecting only the BB codes you need
  • Adding your BB codes
  • Mobile support
  • Easy installation on any forum or site


Currently there are a huge number of forums and sites that actively use BBcode markup, but do not have a user-friendly WYSIWYG editor. It is for these resources and created WysiBB.

I am actively engaged in the development of this project and it is already working successfully, as it is being tested, on the Uagadget website. Also, the work of the editor can be viewed in the section "Demo" at the office. site.

For my part, I hope for objective criticism and ideas in the development of the project from all not indifferent people, as in my opinion, WysiBB can be useful to a large number of users and sites using BBcode. My plans are not to dwell on what is, but to develop WysiBB and make it simple and convenient.
