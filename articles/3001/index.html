Akamai will acquire Netli

Akamai , the developer of a distributed caching and content delivery system, announced the purchase of Netli , the creator of a service that accelerates Internet applications.

Akamai plans to integrate all Netli services into its service package, which is used to improve the performance of web applications (for example, in video services).

According to CNet , Akamai will buy 3.2 million shares of Netli, the amount of the purchase is not reported.
