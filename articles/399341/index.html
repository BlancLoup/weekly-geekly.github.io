Watson Element is the first IBM Watson educational app for schoolchildren.



IBM has developed and introduced the first Watson Element learning application, which is positioned as a digital assistant for schoolchildren and teachers. First, this application will be tested in the United States at the Coppell Independent School District (ISD). The authors of the project claim that the application will provide teachers with the opportunity to take their work to a new level, allowing them to conduct training with an individual approach.

ISD is one of the most successful school districts in Texas. It includes several settlements with a total of 1,200 students and 750 teachers. The need for good teachers is becoming increasingly significant in all countries of the world. In the US, for example, about a third of students need extra classes with tutors after they finish school and go to college. The quality of education received at school is not too high. One can argue about the reasons for this state of affairs, but we need to correct the situation now.

And the facts speak for themselves. Thus, the level of student achievement in the United States is now below the required national level. Accordingly, the needs of enterprises for qualified employees are only partially met.
