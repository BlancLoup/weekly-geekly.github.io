6,000,000,000 photos on Flickr



The six billionth photo came from this Montbretia flower photo, uploaded on Monday by the user eon60 .

The last 5 years, the number of added photos has increased by 20% every year, and we love these achievements. This is a great opportunity to learn tags, geographical data, camera information, groups, comments, favorites and everything that makes a huge collection of Flickr photos unique. The more photos you upload, the more we will be able to find out!

This is what eon60 said about this:
I am on Flickr for about four years. I have two cameras - Nikon D70 and Panasonic LX3, and photography has been my hobby for 40 years. As you can see from my photos, there is nothing that I would not like to photograph. I also love to see photos and other users. I photographed the Crocosmia flower (Montbretia) in my garden. Some consider it a weed because it is an aggressive plant, but I like the color and shape.

Many thanks to all who added their pictures and stories to help us reach 2 , 3 , 4 , 5 and now 6 billion photos.
