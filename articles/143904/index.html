Rusonix starts selling Jelastic platform

We are pleased to announce that since May 16, 2012, Russian developers can experience the commercial version of the cloud platform Jelastic . This service is provided by Rusonix hosting provider.
For 8 months of existence in the market, PaaS Jelastic has attracted more than 16,000 users, 12% of which are Russian developers.

Let's take a closer look at our payment model. Using Jelastic services, the user pays only for consumed resources, i.e. memory, processor power and storage. Jelastic is the first platform, the payment for which services are carried out upon the use of resources. As you know, an application does not always use the same amount of resources. Thanks to the automatic vertical scaling, changes the size of the container under the application. Accordingly, the user does not need to overpay for reserved resources that are not used, as is the case with other PaaS.
In Jelastic, memory and processor power are measured in cloudlets (1 cloudlet is the approximate equivalent of 128 MB RAM and 200 MHz CPU). The fee is charged hourly, relative to the maximum amount consumed by the cloudlet in the current hour. The price of one cloudlet for users of Rusonix is ‚Äã‚Äã70 kopecks per hour. One storage is equivalent to the capacity of the used hard disk space up to 1GB. Its cost for users of Rusonix is ‚Äã‚Äã49 rubles. per month.

Let's look at a small example that will help you figure out how to calculate the approximate cost of a service per month: suppose you created an environment with two Tomcat application servers, a nginx balancer, and a MySQL database. The Resources column shows the amount of the cloudlet and the storage consumed by the whole environment or its components separately.



The first number in the Resources column indicates the amount of storage. In our case, each container consumes 1 storage. This means that the whole environment uses 5 storage.
The second in the Resources column is the number of the cloudlet. At the same time, the first digit (4) is the number of the used claudlet, and the second (56) is the maximum possible number. Jelastic makes it possible to set the limit of scalability so as not to get the score higher than expected.

To calculate the price for the consumed claudlet for the current hour, you need to multiply the number of used cloudlet by the price for one. Thus, if the price is for 1 cloudlet, for example, 70 kopecks per hour, then we have:
4 clouds X 0.7 rub. = 2.8 rubles

If you want to calculate the price per month, then multiply the number of used cloudsets per hour by the price for one, as well as by 24 hours and 30 days. If your environment consumes 5 clouds every hour, then the monthly fee will be:
(4 clouds X 0.7 rub.) X 24h X 30d = 2016 rub.

With respect to storage, everything is simple:
4 storage X 49 rub. = 196 rub.
The total cost of services per month for the environment with this topology will be from 2,212 rubles. up to the user-set limit.
If you use an application for development or testing, for example, you can turn it off for a night or weekend, it will significantly reduce the monthly fee.

Jelastic users who used the platform in the beta version will not have to change anything and they will be able to continue working with their applications and data. At the same time, when switching to the paid version of Jelastic, all the beta restrictions on resources will be removed. Another plus: paid users will have access to features such as high availability and fixed Public IP addresses. There will also be access to the professional support service for Rusonix. In addition, all already registered and new users will be given a bonus of 1,000 rubles, which will allow them to continue or start using the platform before making a final decision on switching to the paid version. All users of the beta version, who will switch to paid terms by May 21, will receive a discount of 50% of the deposit amount. Those users who switch to the paid version before May 28 will receive a 25% discount.
