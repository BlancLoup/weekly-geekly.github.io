Center for Additive Technology: Industrial 3D Printers 3D Systems, Stratasys, SLM, EOS



Hello friends! With you Vasily Kiselev and today we are in Voronezh. Voronezh is one of the largest industrial centers in Russia. In particular, here is the "Design Bureau of Chemical Automatics", which develops rocket engines used in intercontinental ballistic missiles and astronautics.




Today we will visit JSC "Center for Technological Competence of Additive Technologies" , also known as the Center for Additive Technologies (CAT) of the Voronezhselmash group of companies, the largest manufacturer of agricultural equipment supplying it to Russian and foreign buyers. We will see how DAC helps enterprises to develop digital production. At DAC, equipment from manufacturers such as 3D Systems , Stratasys , SLM , EOS and many others is presented.



With us is the general director of TsAT Alexey Mazalov, who will tell us about the organization and activities of the center.

What is DAC


TSAT is a public-private partnership project, which began in 2013.



Now we are one of the country's largest organizations for the operation of equipment in the field of additive technologies, these are 12 industrial systems, 10 of which are 3D printers working on all sorts of 3D printing technologies with all kinds of materials.



There is also an industrial 3D scanner and a vacuum casting system.

We have two production units, the first and main one is a service delivery unit using additive technologies.



The second block develops and builds its own 3D printing desktop systems.



SLM


Our center presents 3D printers with SLM technology (selective laser fusion), such as the installation of 3D Systems ProX 100 with a construction area of ‚Äã‚Äã100x100x80 mm. These 3D printers print parts by fusing the layers of metal powder with a laser beam.



There are also two similar systems from SLM, the SLM 280HL .



Samples made of domestic materials. This is important, since domestic materials are much more affordable and possible to quickly purchase.



High-quality powders for metal printing using the SLM technology are developed and supplied by Polema JSC, VILS OJSC, VIAM Research Institute, NITU MISiS and Rusal.

The main customers of TsAT in this profile are large state-owned enterprises, enterprises from Rostec and Rosatom corporations, and in the near future we hope to start cooperation with Roskosmos.

We work with a large list of domestic steels and alloys, but also use imported, made in Germany, England and China.

From domestic now we use stainless and tool steel, two heat-resistant nickel alloys, cobalt-chromium-molybdenum alloy, there are developments in titanium and aluminum alloys.

SLM is valued by customers in cases where quickly and with a minimum of preparatory work, without accessories, products of complex configuration with high mechanical characteristics are required.

This technology is especially valuable and comes to the rescue when it is impossible to create a prototype of a project developed by the designers that corresponds to the design documentation using traditional methods.

Design and Technology Service


Here we have engineers, designers, operators of our systems.



You can observe the casting scanning process by our specialist.



This is a small-scale order of one of the industrial enterprises of Voronezh.



We deliver the casting part, we carry out its locksmithing, cleaning, then scanning and comparison with the digital model of the customer. So we check the quality of the parts. Next, we build a deviation map and provide it to the customer for improvements.

EOS polyamide machine




Machine for laser sintering of polyamides. The sintering of the layers of material is performed in the volume filled with not sintered powder, which allows us to build models of complex geometry without supports and in the entire chamber volume.



The machine allows you to work with materials such as pure PA 12 and mixed with fillers - aluminum, glass, carbon. The use of filled polyamides allows you to create parts from sintered dispersed composites, which gives enhanced physical characteristics.



Also used materials based on polystyrene, which can be printed burnable models for casting.

ProJet 660 Pro





We have two ProJet 660Pro devices printing using 3DP full-color 3D printing technology.



The technology allows you to quickly and accurately create color layouts and layout elements.

Stratasys Connex500




Industrial SLA printer. The system works with liquid UV curable photopolymers. It has two powerful UV lamps. One of the most accurate systems.



Layer thickness up to 16 microns. An extensive list of materials used, including, for example, mechanically similar rubber and ABS. In one operation, you can make a functional prototype for testing ergonomics, constructive, collectability.



It helps to determine the feasibility of ordering an expensive metal prototype. Also used for the manufacture of high-precision master models for casting in silicone.

EnvisionTec Perfactory 4 Mini




A small high-precision photopolymernik with DLP technology. Here you see the made set of burned-out master models for the subsequent creation of molds and metal casting.



EnvisionTec Ultra 3SP




Another photopolymer machine. It features a larger construction area, allowing to produce more different prototypes and models for casting. The output is a smooth soft-touch surface. Often used for the manufacture of equipment for forming aligners - corrective cap used in orthodontics to correct the bite. Also, the apparatus is used for the manufacture of master models for vacuum casting.

Vacuum casting


The TsAT equipment park contains equipment for vacuum casting into silicone. Recently we had a separate article with an interesting case of vacuum casting in industry.



The vacuum casting system does not directly relate to additive technologies, but to a large extent it works in conjunction with them, significantly expanding the capabilities of the center.



With this system, you can quickly produce small batches of polymer products for end use, including complex configuration.



Used a large number of different materials for casting.



It is possible to create very complex shapes. One of the most interesting orders is the case of an electronic device, a remote control for controlling special equipment, which was molded into this form.



Casting orders a very wide range of customers. We had to open the second shift in vacuum casting. The main customers are small and medium business, instrument makers, the automotive industry, large enterprises.

About prospects


We meet with the chairman of the board of directors of the Voronezhselmash group of companies, Egor Koblik.



We ask Egor a few questions about the near future.



Please tell us about your strategy for the near future.

In the next three years, we would like to develop the achievements that we already have. We are talking about cooperation with enterprises of the military-industrial complex, with design offices, such corporations as Rostec and Rosnano, with which we are already working. In addition to large industrial enterprises, I would like to introduce additive technologies in small and medium businesses, because not everyone uses these tools yet.

What advice would you give to businesses that are not yet applying additive technology?

Just try. Try to print with any materials, implement different ideas, give us tasks of any complexity, try to introduce new technologies in our own production.

Technologies seem to be known to everyone, but few people are ready to apply them on a daily basis, and that‚Äôs what they are intended for. There are whole branches of medium-sized businesses that order various parts with us for production in 24 hours. I would like to advise novices in additive technologies not to be afraid to delve into and order as much as possible different and interesting, because only such experience allows the whole industry to develop.
