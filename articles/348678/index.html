When: from February 13
Where: Novosibirsk, Academician Koptyug Avenue, 4, office 138, IT-Academy

DDOS Prevention

When: February 26 - March 7
Where: Moscow, Warsaw highway 9c1, Rows Soldatenkova, entrance 2
Participation conditions: 17 550 rub.

The advanced course of virtual self-defense teaches us to understand and repel DDoS attacks. Listeners will be able to look at them from the inside, having considered in detail the order of the performance, the role of botnets and backdoors, the essence of zombie networks. Teachers will touch on all popular types of attacks, focusing on flood and modern methods using cloud technologies. The second part of the course will be devoted to a variety of precautionary measures and safety standards (starting with load testing and ending with perimeter protection), with the help of which participants will be able to assess the state of their resource and build a protection strategy.
