Skype Out and other VoIP services will still block: the draft Ministry of Communications published

The Ministry of Communications and Mass Media has finally completed work on new rules for connecting telecommunication networks, which have been talked about for a long time . For several years now, there has been a problem in the industry that Internet instant messengers and VoIP services offer users cheap long-distance communication and drive voice traffic over the Internet, thereby violating the rules for connecting to Russian telecommunications networks. Instead of the standard charge for an international call ($ 0.14 per minute), Russian operators only get paid for a local call (a little more than $ 0.01). Soon, this "chaos" will stop.

The draft government decree " On Amendments to Certain Acts of the Government of the Russian Federation in Part of the Interaction of Telecommunication Networks " was published on the portal regulation.gov.ru . The new edition of the list of those who can join the local, zonal and other networks excluded data transmission operators for the purpose of transmitting voice information , that is, VoIP operators.

Until now, the activities of VoIP operators have not been described in the legislation. If the project is accepted, VoIP subscribers will not be able to call fixed and mobile phones, and vice versa.
