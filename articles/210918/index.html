DevCon 2014 Conference - What Can We Tell Now


There are still four long months to DevCon 2014 , but the planning work for the conference is in full swing. As in previous years, the organizers strive to ensure that participants receive maximum knowledge and unforgettable impressions. First of all, DevCon 2014 is an opportunity to get first-hand information on the latest Microsoft technology. Therefore, the team that is working on the substantive part of the event is already planning what will be discussed at the conference. You will learn how to use development tools effectively to make your work easier. How cloud and web technologies will help you quickly create complex information systems. We will talk about new data processing and new technologies for corporate development. Within the framework of the event, master classes, round tables, and the Night of Coding hackathon will also be held.


DevCon 2014 includes four main tracks and more than fifty reports:



  • Development of client applications for the Windows and Windows Phone platforms
  • Cloud Computing and Web
  • Tools, development methodologies and programming languages
  • Corporate development, data processing

Each of these tracks is carefully designed to include the most interesting reports. At the same time, we analyze the experience of past years, which reports were popular and attracted the attention of the audience. For DevCon 2013, for example, such topics as application code optimization, efficient work practices in Visual Studio, architectural tools, working with SQL, cloud technologies and the web. Also, great attention of the audience is attracted by reports on technological innovations. DevCon members want to know the latest news about Microsoft products first-hand, from people directly involved in the creation and promotion of new platforms, services and tools. In addition to the main program, the content of DevCon includes workshops, round tables and hackathons, which also attract deserved attention of the audience. This is an opportunity to gain new knowledge in areas not related to technology, communication and new contacts, deep practical experience.

Everything that you love DevCon for will be this year. Already today we can tell you about the part of the program, about who will be speaking at this wonderful event.

Master class "Technological business on the Internet"


Last year, one of the most popular and highly praised, was the workshop "From Idea to Success." This year we decided to continue the tradition and hold a master class on the topic in which a practicing investor and mentor will talk about the process of creating a grocery business on the Internet from the moment the idea arises to creating a product and attracting investment to the project. The master class will be of interest to those who are working on new projects within existing companies or are considering starting their own business in the field of technological entrepreneurship. You will learn about the process that current companies use in practice and will be able to decide on their future ideas or the next step in product development. Someone master class will help decide to start a technology business, and someone may abandon this desire. And we are happy to present you the leading master class:


Gaidar Magdanurov , Investment Director, Runa Capital

Gaidar Magdanurov is an investment director at venture capital fund Runa Capital.
