Jobs wanted the future campus to match the company's ideology ‚Äî embody functionality and have perfect form. Amazon Environmental Space




Among the stamped multi-storey buildings of Seattle, decorated with iron and glass, it is so pleasant to look at something that extends beyond the boundaries of global architectural thought. That is the creation of the new headquarters of Amazon, whose construction began in January 2014. The complex will include three office buildings, as well as an eco-center - a 25-meter building consisting of three glass transparent spheres.



The project designer is the famous architectural bureau NBBJ. The eco-center will be equipped with work offices, retail outlets, conference halls, recreation areas, but the main asset will undoubtedly be an atrium of rare plants and trees from around the world, creating the most comfortable microclimate for a person. Thus, the future headquarters of Amazon will be not only a place for 2 thousand employees of the company, but also a public place where everyone can have a good time. Completion of the project is scheduled for the end of 2016.

Facebook and creative atmosphere




According to Mark Zuckerberg, the creator of Facebook, innovations are born in the process of interaction of a large number of people. To implement this idea, Facebook commissioned the construction of a new headquarters to the famous architect Frank Gehry. The building will be an ideal factory of ideas: no walls and windows, offices for top management and other infrastructure. In a modest space without borders there will be many places for spontaneous intersection of employees, and consequently the birth of a new solution. The five buildings of the complex are interconnected by landscaping with a roof with walking paths. At first glance, the new campus is a rather modest work of the great architect, but, according to Zuckerberg, fully corresponds to the spirit of the company.
