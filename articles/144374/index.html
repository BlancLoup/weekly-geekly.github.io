Maximum practice, minimum formality: PHDays 2012 program published

Very soon the International Forum Positive Hack Days 2012 will take place at the Digital October technocenter in Moscow. We prepared this event for a whole year, collected all the most relevant and interesting information security issues and tried to take into account the preferences of the community representatives, so that the forum was interesting for everyone - from programmers to CIO.

Today we present to your attention the final program PHDays 2012. It consists of two large blocks connecting theory and practice - the conference and the competitive program.

The conference includes:

  • business seminars - discussions with the world's leading experts, in which the most acute problems of information security are discussed;
  • technical workshops targeted at a more specialized audience will be conducted by authoritative expert practitioners;
  • master classes : online, under the guidance of well-known Russian and foreign experts, you can gain practical experience in solving various IS tasks (read about it in our habratopic !);
