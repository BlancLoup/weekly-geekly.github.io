Telegram Messenger Inc. filed a lawsuit in the US District Court for the Northern District of California against the California cryptostartape Lantah LLC, which can issue Gram cryptocurrency earlier than Telegram itself. The plaintiff from the British Virgin Islands accuses the American respondent of violating the right to the Gram trademark, for which $ 1.7 billion of investment has already been collected from about 200 accredited investors from around the world, including from Russia.

The problem is that Lantah LLC applied for a trademark on February 25, 2018. Telegram began to collect investments at the end of January 2018, without having the rights to the trademark and not registering it properly. But the company insists that it "approved" its right to Gram earlier than Lantah applied for.

Owner Lantah LLC Daniel Jeffrey (Daniel Jeffrey) says that his company used the trademark Gram before it became aware of its use by Telegram. In favor of the defendant, Telegram may have played a closed placement, and the prospectuses for investors were not published in the public domain.
