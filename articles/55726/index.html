Move To The Music

Hello, dear habrovchane! Today I would like to tell you about an experiment that I intend to carry out in the near future.



I think many of you walk / run / walk to the music. Naturally, someone likes stepping under the accompaniment of rock musicians, and someone walks, listening exclusively to representatives of rap culture. Actually, now no one walks with a tape recorder on his shoulder (although it was quite funny). In those days, people who listened to music in this way, walked slowly, filled with chsv. And what now? What musical trends go faster, or, on the contrary, slower? What makes people speed up a step, and what - slow down?

In our experiment, we will try to find out by first going through the same distance to different music, and then, based on the stopwatch and step counter, after conducting a kind of analysis, create a comparative table (or this is not a table, not yet decided).

So, over the course of several weeks, I will walk the same distance (~ 1km) while listening to different music:
  • Rock
  • Metal
  • Alternative, Punk, Independent
  • Pop
  • Chillout / Downtempo / Trip-Hop / Ambient
  • D'n'B / Jungle / Breaks
  • House / Electro / Dance
  • Trance
  • Rap / R'n'B
  • Blues / Gospel / Jazz (thanks to pingwin for the reminder)


With the help of the Soviet stopwatch and the Nokia Step Counter program installed on my mobile, I will measure the results.

How long it will take is impossible to say. I hope that in the next month I will be able to provide the results of the experiment.

Of course, I would like to attract you, dear habrachiteli. If you are ready to take part in the experiment - write directly in this topic, or contact me by im / e-mail. Contact information is listed in the profile.
