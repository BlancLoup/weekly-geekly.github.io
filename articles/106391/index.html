Registration of IDN-domains in the .com.ua and .kiev.ua zones opens

Perhaps for someone, this is not news, but starting tomorrow (October 19, 2010), registration of Cyrillic domains in com.ua and kiev.ua zones begins. Registration will take place in 3 stages.

The first stage - from October 19 to November 17 (IDN-domains are registered only to trademark owners)

The second stage - from November 19 to December 19 (IDN domains are registered for a period of 10 years)

The third stage - from December 21 (free registration of Cyrillic domains)
