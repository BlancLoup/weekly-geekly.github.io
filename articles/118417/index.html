Symfony2: Beta 1 is available!

A month ago, I announced that the final version of Symfony2 would be delayed. Then we were looking forward to merging with the new framework of forms. And now I can tell you that we did not wait in vain. Bernhard has completed his fantastic work and I am sure that you will love the new version.

All symfony 2.0 features are now available. This means that finally we can enter the long-awaited beta / RC release cycle, which will eventually lead to the release of the brilliant version 2.0.

Beta means that not all features are stable. Beta also means that we will break backward compatibility only for important reasons and all of these violations will be explained and documented in the UPDATE file to facilitate the migration of existing applications. (Finally, it will not be necessary to track down in commits what and why it broke - approx. Transl.)

Download Symfony 2.0 Standard Edition beta1, read the quick tour and dive into the book.

Oh! And do not forget to give us feedback. Tell us about the remaining pain points, create tickets for errors , offer patches .

As with the preview releases, we will release new Beta / RC releases often, perhaps once a week. And depending on the reviews that will be, I hope that the Beta phase will end within a month or so. The first release candidate will be released when all blocking errors are closed and the documentation is complete.

(Do not kick much, first public translation - approx. Transl.)
