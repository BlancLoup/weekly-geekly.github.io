Android 2.2 AKA Froyo. First impressions

Two hours ago, I successfully updated my Nexus One to Froyo (2.2)

And here are my first impressions:
Pros:
  • Automatic recovery of programs, after downloading, the phone itself installed all the programs from the market that were previously installed.
  • Noticeable acceleration of application launch - thanks to JIT
  • Updated Market (separate tab for comments, automatic update, Update All)
  • Updated Gmail - finally the full Copy / Paste in the Gmail application
  • New Widgets: Android Recommendations, Android Market Recommendations
  • Tethering - mobile internet sharing on both usb and wi-fi (your phone as hotspot)
  • full Flash after installing it from the Android Market
  • Native Russian keyboard
