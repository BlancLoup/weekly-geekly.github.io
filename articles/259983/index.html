Google Developers Launchpad - everything you need for a successful launch

Hi, Habr! We try to support developers with tools, technologies, information and attention. One of our activities is the Google Developers Launchpad program, a support system for startups and application developers. It was recently launched in the world and almost immediately came to Russia.



Under this program, we provide various opportunities: both business services, PR promotion, and technical things. For example, access to the Firebase service, advanced access to the Google Maps API , testing tools and the like. We also enable one-on-one teams to talk with Google experts who can evaluate the design and infrastructure of the application, advise how best to implement certain functions or optimize the application.

The program is promising and a number of Russian projects have already passed or are now going through it. Together with the AppTractor.ru project , we selected six Launchpad participants and talked to them about their work.

In total, we will have six podcasts, six interviews. These are the projects Anews , Whisperarts , App in the Air , Tviz.tv , Reactive Phone and Bookmate - the largest Russian applications with millions of installations.

The first two recordings are ready and waiting for you: an interview with Vyacheslav Masenkov, the general director of Anews, and Oleg Demyanov, the head of the Whisper Arts studio, are available on the official page of the project.

We took care of all the users: those who prefer to listen to the podcasts will be able to listen to them, and those who prefer to read the interview texts will read the transcript.

Listen and join Google Launchpad!
