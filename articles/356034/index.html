"10 years for the spread of imitation and hentai" unanimously adopted in the second reading

The bill , under which imitation and hentai will be considered child pornography, was adopted unanimously in the second reading this afternoon.

438 deputies voted for, no one voted against.

Now let's see how many people per year are punished under these articles and how they are punished.

The Judicial Department has a website with remarkable statistics - www.cdep.ru/index.php?id=79 At the moment it lies, maybe it will work tomorrow. There are xls files with statistics of convicts under articles by year and with statistics of penalties under articles.

For 12 months of 2015, 165 people were convicted under Article 242 + 75 under this article as an additional one.
According to Article 242.1, 180 people were convicted + 15 had it as an additional one.
In almost all cases, the conviction was by the most serious part of the articles - with an aggravating "using the Internet" attribute.

Total convicted 435 people.

According to Article 242, 4 people received a real term, 67 - a condition and about 10 people fines and so on.
According to 242.1, 25 people (one third!) Received real terms , 54 - conditions, about 10 forced labor, and so on.
