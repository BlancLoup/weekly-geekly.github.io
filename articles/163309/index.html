There was a game about robots - Robotrok . I strongly advise, a good thing on the road or play with the child. The game is generally amazing of its kind: here is a very accurate balance, and simple rules. Children trudge from robots and their assembly, adults - from a very thoughtful and deep tactics. Half of the game you need to collect parts at the dump (according to the environmental legislation, robots can only be made from them) - this is the solution of the traveling salesman (Dijkstra) problem, taking into account the prediction of the actions of other players. The second part is pure bluff, the third, quite simple, is an assembly according to the optimal scheme.
