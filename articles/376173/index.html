PPSSPP - PlayStation Portable emulator for Android



Modern smartphones are much more productive than gaming consoles, released a few years ago. Therefore, now developers have all the opportunities to produce emulators of game consoles for smartphones. Emulators, in general, a lot. There are NES, SNES, Sega emulators, and now even the PlayStation Portable emulator has appeared.

At the same time, the program is shareware, and you can download it from the Google Play Store (to be fair, you need to point out that there is an advertisement in the application, and in order to remove it, you need to pay $ 5.99). It should be noted that the application has been developed for several years, and now the emulator works fine on smartphones, tablets or even TV-sticks. True, the device configuration must be modern, old phones or tablets will not be able to work with the emulator.

Even with the purchase of a version of the application without advertising, all this will be much cheaper than the cost of the PlayStation Portable itself. As for the games, the developers recommend using native applications and ripping their own PSP games. But it is likely that there are unofficial ways to launch games downloaded from the network. Not all games are currently supported, but normal operation has been confirmed with Wipeout, Grand Theft Auto, Soul Caliber, Crisis Core, and Final Fantasy: Type-O.

For the game, you can use the display or an additional controller (for example, a Bluetooth controller).
