Profitbid.ru site trailer

We finished the trailer of the site Profitbid.ru, when the project in the form in which it is shown in the trailer, was already dead.

Profitbid.ru itself continues to live, but in a somewhat different role: the history of the developers .

Somehow it is a little uneasy to look at a bright, positive video for a project that no longer exists.



A word to the developers:

ProfitBid is a Cash Back service from purchases at eBay and other US stores and platforms. The project began its work on June 1, 2009 and quickly gained popularity online, due to the uniqueness of the services offered and their financial benefits.

The cooperation model that is beneficial for both parties allowed the portal to generate quite a large amount of customer traffic to the eBay trading platform in a relatively short time. As a member of the eBay affiliate program - EPN (eBay Partner Network), the service paid as part of the return of the commission received from the eBay trading platform to its users, who made the purchase through the referral link posted on the ProfitBid portal.

At a certain stage, the ProfitBid team attempted to reorganize and qualitatively improve the system. On this occasion, a promotional video was created with a demonstration of the new functionality. Unfortunately, the resource development plans were violated by the eBay affiliate program (which is described in detail in the article ‚ÄúThe whole truth about eBay affiliate program‚Äù).

What today?

At the moment, the ProfitBid service only returns from purchases from Amazon.com and its branches in Europe. Users receive a Cash Back in the amount of 2-3% of the value of the goods, excluding shipping.
