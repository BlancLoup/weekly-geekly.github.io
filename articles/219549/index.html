Getting ready for Google I / O 2014

If you are a developer and use the Google Compute Engine and the Google Maps API to build an oscillating Supertraster and reduce Doug Kessel to 12 parsecs ... well, or just to create applications, then Google I / O is an event for you.

Do not miss the opportunity to attend the conference and talk about what is happening in the world of Google. What was on the last I / O? Using Google Glass and Google+, we broadcast a jump from the airship to the roof of the Moscone exhibition center (where our developer conference was held), showed new gadgets (the original Nexus 7 tablet and the first custom Chromebook), and also showed how Google services help develop applications.

Although this year we seem to do without skydiving (never say never), there will be a lot of interesting things at the conference, one way or another covering three main topics: design, development and promotion of applications . So sign up and see what we have planned for Google I / O 2014:

  • More real communication . If you come, you will have enough time to "sit in the sandbox", where our partners will show you their applications that have taken the best from Google and open source. There, you will be able to personally interact with Googleers, discuss the code, page layout patterns, business plans, and even work with them in a team. Do not come empty-handed. Take your applications with you, tell us about how you design, develop and promote them, and be prepared to listen to what others think about it.
  • Focus on core topics . Feedback from participants at the end of the last conference did not go unnoticed. We heard you: you need to focus on the main thing. In May we will publish the schedule of the event. Meetings will be organized where you can get ideas for your new application and learn about the tools that will help you create it. And we will prepare Code Labs sites for those who want to go straight to I / O.
  • After Hours with local flavor . If you talk without interruption, any conference bored. After the main events of the conference, you will be able to drink refreshing drinks, buy something to eat in the food vans that have become the hallmark of San Francisco, and, sitting comfortably on the lawn, listen to local indie music (yes, you heard that, this year after hours - open air).

We look forward to the start of the conference this year. We hope you too. Do not forget to apply for participation. Registration closes on April 19 at 01:00 (at 1:00 in the morning) Moscow time . Participants will be selected at random. The results of the draw, we will announce approximately April 21.

See you in June: at the Moscone Exhibition Center, at an I / O Extended event or during an I / O Live live broadcast.
