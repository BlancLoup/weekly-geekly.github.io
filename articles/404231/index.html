Intel Core i9 - new processors with the letter i


Historically, the top of the Intel Core line was a small, isolated series of ultra-powerful processors for enthusiasts and gamers. These processors, which usually had an i7 -____ X or i7 -____ K index, had a special case, their system logic and a decent price, which still did not scare lovers of the exclusive, but could, on the contrary, attract. And at the new stage of evolution, within the framework of the Intel Basin Falls platform, top core processors Core finally got their logical place in the line. Today we bring you the Intel Core i9-7900X - the first representative of the i9 family. And at the same time, and other processors platform.
