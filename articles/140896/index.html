GamaK - competition for game developers

Hello! We thought you would be interested to learn about the new competition for game developers. Nevosoft acts as an expert, but anyone who knows how to make games can take part. Organizers: HeyMoose advertising platform! and the IT recruitment company DigitalHR. The competition is called GamaK, and it is held with the support of the Higher School of Economics.




The contest has one goal - to arouse interest in playing, but there are several tasks for this contest.

First of all, it is, of course, the search for talented teams that are ready to offer an alternative to the dominance of "farming" and "urban" patterns in the social market.

Secondly, it helps those very young and talented developers to promote their projects, which is also important.

And, thirdly, the assessment of existing trends in the social games market. Following the results of the competition, the expert group, which, in addition to Nevosoft, also included Playrix, Progrestar and Playflock, will share their views on the current situation on the market and will reveal in detail exactly how the competition was held.

There will be three winners too. And each of them will be selected in the original way: by voting of users, experts and organizers.

To participate in the contest, it is enough to create a flash-game before April 20 and post it on any social network, without forgetting to write about this letter to elena.privalova@heymoose.com again not later than April 20th. If the application is already developed and uploaded, all that remains is to write a letter (the main thing is that the game be published online no earlier than January 1, 2012). And that's all.

The main prize - a comfortable, high-quality, personal hammock will receive the leader of the popular vote. Included with a hammock is a cash prize of $ 20,000. The prize from the organizers will be 50,000 rubles, the prize from the experts - 100,000 rubles.

All winners will receive promotion of their projects online on exclusive terms from HeyMoose!

For more information about the competition and conditions of participation - we invite you to the website of the organizing company HeyMoose!
