Why do we need priests in high school?



The reason for this post was the news that the Department of Theology was opened at the National Research Nuclear University MEPhI . I am not a militant atheist, I am quite tolerant and with understanding I treat people who are believers - this is their own business, what and how to believe. It is also not a secret that many great scholars were believers and even entered a narrow circle of people who have a little more relation to religion than ordinary worshipers (just look at the list of members of the Pontifical Academy of Sciences in the Vatican). Still, the news mentioned above leads to very sad thoughts.

In my opinion, the ever deeper penetration of religion into the life of modern Russian society is not an indication that the world is getting better, being cleared of "communist contagion." Analyzing news related to church activities, I see only more and more aggressive marketing. Yes, it is marketing: there are all signs, ranging from press releases and ending with the price lists for services in churches and temples. By the way, do you know how much a rocket consecration costs before each launch? Yes, yes, at Baikonur (where I was fortunate to serve in the then Soviet Army during the launch of Buran), this procedure became standard in 1999 (it was then built at Baikonur and the temple of the Holy Great Martyr George the Victorious), and since and his assistants - regular units of the crew for launching missiles (sic!). So, the consecration of each rocket before the start costs the treasury 170 thousand rubles. From the budget for the construction of the rocket and launch - i.e. from the state budget (from your and my pockets, in other words). At the same time, the ‚Äútoolkit‚Äù is used by the servants of the church in no way cosmic - absolutely the same as for the consecration of apartments or cars, the cost of which consecration starts from 0 rubles - according to church rules, everyone here does not give much.

But marketing is an integral part of business. And here - religion, concern for the souls of the parishioners ... However, there is no inconsistency here: religion has long become a business, and very profitable. Moreover, as in any traditional business, the cost of corporate services is much higher than the cost of similar services for individuals - and therefore the consecration of the rocket costs much more than the consecration of a car (for which people usually give 500-1000 rubles). So, after all the business. And now this business comes to universities, as one of the studied subjects. However, students will obviously not be taught the subtleties of church business - this is a forbidden topic. And will there be a divine explanation of the nature of the neutrino? And as the theory of explaining the meaning of all the fundamental physical constants, can the great divine plan come forward?
