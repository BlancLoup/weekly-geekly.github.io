SCAT DPI 7.0 Review - SORM Product Roadmap


We are talking about the functionality of SCAT 7.0. For example, the ‚Äúfocus‚Äù of this webinar was the Radius Accounting function. It allows you to use Radius to read information about how much traffic the subscriber consumes. We give a step-by-step algorithm for setting up Radius Accounting and note the limitations of using the function (for example, in which case you have to configure the network stack).

Another new feature is GRE tunneling through NAT. One of its features is setting a limit on the number of TCP and UDP connections for a pool of IP addresses. This allows the operator to save resources in the allocation of address space between corporate and private clients. We will also talk about how to activate support for IPv6, and tell you about the implementation of BRAS in VAS Experts.


What else is discussed in the presentation:

  • step by step algorithm for configuring CG-NAT;
  • CG-NAT broadcast logging (when to use);
  • SORM types and plans to develop this solution.

Preparation of the network operator for the implementation of SORM-3


In this webinar we will touch upon the main problems that the operator may encounter when introducing SORM. We will pay special attention to SORM-3 and its differences from previous versions. We will also discuss how to choose the SORM-3 solution: examine the storage times for information, the possibility of upgrading, the method of implementing the FZ-374.

Note that you need to do to meet the requirements of the RF government decree on the approval of the interaction of communication operators with government agencies (PP538).


Other webinar topics:

  • pitfalls in the automated unloading of data of legal entities in SORM;
  • changes in PP538;
  • implementation of SORM-3 (schemes "in a gap" and "on the mirror").

BRAS based on SCAT DPI: features of interaction with billing


Presentation in two parts. In the first one, we talk about the benefits of the BRAS technology with DPI: traffic policing, multi-user functions (when multiple IP addresses or subnets are hidden behind one login) and whitelists with zero balance.

The second part of the webinar reveals the issues of sharing BRAS and billing. We will talk about the advantages of such integration and the solutions, schemes and methods of authentication offered by LANBilling.


What else is in the presentation:

  • the pros and cons of IPv6;
  • how to configure BRAS;
  • L2-BRAS and L3-BRAS authentication methods from LANBilling;
  • service management through Radius attribute binding.

Changes in the method of unloading lists from Roskomnadzor


The purpose of this webinar is to consider changes in the method of uploading lists from Roskomnadzor. Let us explain the features of the previous method, we will highlight its pros and cons. Then we will discuss the new method (uploading only changes - Delta), as well as its advantages (uploading by login and password, reducing the time for preparing lists) and shortcomings (increasing the requirements for response times and saving the digital signature).
