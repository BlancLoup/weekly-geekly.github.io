In today's review - folding monitor, universal stylus, portable projector and electronic nail cutter.

The Spontaneous Pop-up Display (SPUD) - 24 "folding monitor






The original draft of the portable monitor, launched by the Arovia team. The idea is really unique - a portable monitor that can be folded and put in a backpack. What is it really?



As a basis - a projector connected to a laptop. It is inserted into the folding soft box with black walls. The image is projected on a 24-inch screen. Thus, a portable monitor is obtained.

Each device is worth $ 400 . For this money, you can buy a great projector, or an entire monitor. Can a 300 lumen projector handle a bright sun? Hardly. Is it possible to use it everywhere? Dimensions will not allow.

Flip! - universal "smart" stylus




It seems to be the dream of everyone who uses the stylus. The Lynktec development team (16 projects on Kickstarter) plans to solve all input problems on the touch screen using Flip . At the core of the work is a system of magnets, with the help of which the software accurately determines the pressing force and the position of the stylus.

Included - three different nozzles that will replace the brush, pencil, pen ... Everything you might need for handwriting or drawing on the screen.

There is no Bluetooth in the pen, no batteries. And in the list of compatible devices and applications - almost everything that can be in the hands of the user. But we know that again everything will rest on the accuracy of the screen - the quality of the sensor and its calibration. And then the most magical stylus will not save.

Saves the project low cost - $ 60 . Given that this is an Aplle accessory - you can at least try. Many people buy it just for this. If it doesn't work out, you can just forget about the money.

ODIN2 - a projector that could




Wonderful, wonderful, wonderful. The Alex Yoo of Dos Owls team with 10 projects offered their mini-projector for $ 400. And this is an excellent example of Kickstarter marketing.

ODIN2 Brains work on Android 6.0. Interfaces are enough to connect any peripherals and battery life, not to mention the connection to any sources. All perfectly.

In addition, this is a $ 400 projector with a brightness of 300 lumens. Such mass on the Chinese trading floors, dollars at 150-250. A convenient form factor and native support for streaming services (however, through official Android applications) - this led the project to success.
