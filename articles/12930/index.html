Why last.fm?

For listeners:

* If you have unlimited and fairly high-speed Internet access, you can simply forget that you need to download, search or buy music from somewhere. Many songs can be downloaded directly from last.fm, and many others you will find links there. And links to shops, including, if we are talking about the music of those groups and musicians who are still confident that they should pay for low quality music too.
* If you cannot even download every track that would be interesting to listen to, then last.fm will tell you which names and albums you should look for in your friends and in music stores. And with very high probability that you will be offered to last.fm, you will like it.
