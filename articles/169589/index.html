Surface Windows 8 Pro overview and comparison with RT



Friends Habravchane, in June 2012, Microsoft introduced a tablet of its own design - Surface RT and Surface Pro. At the end of October of that year, the RT version was released, a review of which we have already done. A week ago, to the delight of users, Pro appeared on the market (and in my hands).

And, apparently, Pro'Fessional Surface is professional only in hardware, since in many ways the novelty pumped up. Although, not everything is as bad as it seems: Surface Pro has trumps up its sleeve ...



Dimensions

For comparison: RT has an HD display, thickness - 10 mm, weight - 678 grams.

What's inside?

Ivy Bridge Intel Core i5 3317U (similar installed in the latest MacBook Air 11 ')
4 GB of RAM
Intel HD4000
64 GB or 128 GB of internal memory. Of these, the user is available 29 and 89 GB, respectively. Many Western resources scolded Microsoft, but in my opinion there is no problem, so there is a slot for a MicroSD card.

Battery performance

Surface RT works for about 7-8 hours, Pro has slightly different results ...

Playing FarCry 2 via HDMI (graphics: medium, 1080p) - 1.3 hours
Surfing the web with Internet Explorer - 4.5 hours
Work in Photoshop CS6 - 4 hours
Work in Premiere Pro CS6 - 2.5 hours
Watching a MKV movie (Remux) - 2 hours (and 30% charge left)

Screen quality

Apart from the fact that Surface Pro has an excellent screen with a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels (for RT 1366 at 768), it has twice more powerful backlighting in comparison with its counterpart.
