Zynga launched CityVille on Google+



Zynga - developer of the most popular games for Facebook - today announced the launch of CityVille for Google+. Zynga launched its first game for Google+ at the same time as the launch of the Google gaming platform itself, but CityVille is the first really popular game Zynga, it ranks first in the ranking of Facebook games.

Zynga social games have over 146 million users, but they are almost completely played inside Facebook. However, in July 2010, Google quietly invested more than $ 100 million in a gaming company, ensuring the emergence of the main Zynga games on its platform.

CityVille - a kind of crossbreed between FarmVille and Sim City - boasts over 72 million Facebook players , which is 30 million more than, according to recent estimates , Google+ users in general.
