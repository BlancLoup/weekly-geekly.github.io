How to deal with procrastination?


After all, this is really the disease of the 21st century!

There is an exit. It may be purely individual and subjective, because the root causes are different for everyone, and the result is the same. It helps me planning, planning and planning again.

The first thing to do is to throw out all the plans and thoughts from your head , and transfer it to paper or electronic form . And it works! This is similar to the situation when the hard disk is full of the necessary information, but very buggy. Which exit? Evernote helps me in this regard, but you can also be helped by a paper notebook, because to each his own!

I have two files: one common task and the second task for each day. It's simple. During the day, you write down all the tasks and thoughts in a general note, and set deadlines, and when you go to bed, you make a plan for tomorrow. You transfer to it what you are going to do and what you can do (you look into the general file and take some of the tasks from there).
