How we decided to find out why people use Raspberry



After the selection of mini-boards for developers , we seriously thought about what people are doing with such devices, and held a contest for visitors to our site!

As a result, several interesting works were received, more precisely, six, and this, frankly, is six times more than we initially expected.

The brightest show here:
  • Interactive robot with expressive eyes;
  • Radio for supermarkets;
  • Prototype remote probe;
  • Smart greenhouse for a balcony or loggia.

Online radio for malls and supermarkets RMPI (RMC + PI)





The main task is to create a distributed network of radio points for shopping centers and supermarkets, with the possibility of local broadcasting, for each of the stores of the network.

The system is managed from the central administration panel: the necessary store is selected, music records are sent to the outlet, and, if necessary, commercials. There is a possibility of setting the frequency and time of broadcasting, while playing the advertisement, the music calms down.



The project itself has existed for 4 years, created to reduce the cost of the workstation. In 2014, the project was modified, the Raspberry Pi mini-PC was chosen as the main control element.

Prototype of the remote probe (all-terrain vehicle, UAV, amphibian) based on the Raspebrry Pi B v2 with the Raspbian



According to the developers, the project was created in order to produce a universal prototype of a remote probe, such as an all-terrain vehicle with an all-terrain vehicle, an amphibious vehicle or an aircraft.

Smart greenhouse on the balcony - the prototype





The system works on the basis of Arduino UNO. Equipped with temperature sensor, humidity sensor, water pump, etc. The author has implemented automatic watering of plants. The humidity sensor determines the humidity and compares with the values ‚Äã‚Äãthat were set in advance and, depending on the values, the water pump is activated and watering is performed for a specified time.



In addition, there is a climate control system. If the sensor indicates that the temperature is too high, the servo actuates and the vent opens. If the temperature does not fall, the active hood starts working. If the temperature is lower than it should be, heating is turned on.

According to the author, the system can be scaled, used for greenhouses of any size.

Raspberry based game console





The author of the project has created a video set-up based on the Raspberry Pi. The prefix allows you not only to play 8-16 bit games, as well as doom, duke nukem, quake, minecraft, but also to broadcast any video from the Web to TV, view home media archives. In addition, you can connect a keyboard and mouse. RASBMS and RETROPIE were installed on the device.

Actually, the author just slightly modified the native case of the console, inserted a mini-PC into it and connected the necessary cables, slightly soldering everything that was needed for the ports.
