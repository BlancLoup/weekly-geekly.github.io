What colors use the most popular sites?



Color is an important factor when choosing a product. This can confirm the majority of buyers of various goods - from cars to shoes.

Color significantly affects the performance of sales on the Internet and site traffic in general. Nearly half of users form an opinion about a website based on its design. The color scheme plays a significant role here.

Designer Paul Hebert analyzed 10 popular sites. He collected statistics on the distribution of colors used on the main page of the site and in the style sheet.

‚ÄúI often have problems creating color schemes, and I was curious how other companies act in this case,‚Äù he reasoned .

For a start, Paul studied the Alexa rating and randomly chose 10 of the most visited sites - Google.com, Youtube.com, Facebook.com, Baidu.com, Yahoo.com, Wikipedia.org, Amazon.com, Google.co.in, Qq.com and Twitter.com.

Color, according to neuroscientists and psychologists, has the ability to influence certain segments of the audience.

Other findings





Each user knows that the design of the site of the popular social platform Facebook is a lot of blue. However, along with it, the site widely uses a range of other colors, which, for example, on Baidu, Google and Wikipedia sites are three times smaller.

The designer intends to re-analyze the leading web resource palette, including 100 or even 1,000 sites in the sample. Hebert expressed the conviction that the results of a larger experiment would help him find answers to several fundamental questions that had long since excited him.
