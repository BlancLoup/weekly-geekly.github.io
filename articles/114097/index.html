Maximum Protected Camera - Sealife Mini II



In general, most of the equipment that each of us has is quite fragile. Communicators, tablets, cameras - sometimes one drop from a small height leads to a fatal, if I may say so, outcome. It is clear that equipment manufacturers create and quite secure devices, for example, such a laptop . Such devices appear not so often, but they always find their customers. But if protected mobile phones and laptops are known to many, then there are not so many protected cameras, and there should always be a lot of buyers for cameras of this class. In general, not so long ago, the super-protected Sealife Mini II camera appeared on the photo equipment market. In appearance and can not be said that this camera is really so reliable.

The Sealife Mini II camera is really very well protected - thanks to this protection, the camera is not afraid of fairly deep dives, working at a depth of 40 meters. In addition, a drop from two meters to any type of surface does not cause the camera any noticeable damage. The case, as you can see, is rubberized, and the function buttons are spaced far enough apart - it is more convenient to take off with gloves.



However, thanks to this protection system, Sealife Mini II cannot compete in functionality with its fragile "colleagues" - there is no optical zoom or image storage in raw, everything is saved in .jpg. By the way, the camera is equipped with CMOS 9 MP. But you can separately purchase a wide-angle lens, which expands the capabilities of the camera. The device configuration also assumes the use of SDHC format memory cards, plus there is 28 megabytes of its own memory.

The camera was announced only recently, and it should go on sale in mid-March this year. The cost of the camera, by the way, is quite acceptable - $ 259.95.
