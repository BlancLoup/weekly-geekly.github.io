Presentation at Apple Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) 2016 [text translation]

Since 1983, Apple has held annual conferences for software developers from around the world to show them their new products. In 1997, Steve Jobs returned to the company he founded and laid the new WWDC tradition: a small speech on the first day of the conference.

This year is no exception. Not everyone can get into the tiny guest list of the conference. Not all of them can be present in the hall where Tim Cook will speak. Therefore, the performance will be broadcast on the Web - both by Apple and other sources. Inside this post is a text translation of WWDC 2016.

It became known that this year, for the first time since 2006, in addition to the Mascoon Center , which traditionally serves as the main venue for the four-day event, the opening of WWDC will take place at the Bill Graham Civic Auditorium , built in 1915 and accommodating 6000 people. The previous WWDC by the number of spectators did not exceed 5,200 people.

Event starts : 20:00 Moscow time (10 am for San Francisco). Text translation within this post is actively updated before the start of the presentation.
