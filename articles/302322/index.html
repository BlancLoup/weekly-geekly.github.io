How to promote video content? Review of the best video marketing platforms



The use of interactive content is an important part of any marketing strategy. For communication to be effective and engaging, it must affect all the senses of a person. The video, as well as possible, performs this task - we hear, see and fully immerse ourselves in the plot of an advertising or marketing video. And this combination leaves the deepest imprint in the minds of consumers.

At the recently held Hybrid Conf'16 conference, BYYD Mobile Advertising Platform CEO Ruslan Kuvaev made a presentation on mobile video, which contained interesting statistical data from reputable world sources. Here are some facts.

  • The number of clicks when using video is increased by 200-300%;
  • Using video, you can increase the conversion on the landing page by 80%;
  • The average user spends 16 minutes a month watching online video ads;
  • One minute of the video equals the effectiveness of the impact on the target audience to 1,800,000 words.


And this is only a small part of what video can be useful for.

In today's issue, we want to tell you about popular social platforms that will allow you to build a successful marketing strategy through a video content channel.

Not so long ago, these platforms were adapted for the promotion of video content. Improvements for marketers include an increase in the possible resolution of downloaded video materials, and their sizes. By uploading video content to the pages of these platforms, you automatically increase the organic reach of users, since it is usually much higher than regular text and graphic materials. By adding your content to these resources, you will gradually be able to build an extensive multimedia library and take your brand beyond the official website.

Live broadcasts on Facebook, Periscope, Meerkat

Do not want to spend valuable time creating content? Do a live broadcast in a few minutes. Share the latest news of the company, make a ‚Äústuffing‚Äù of information about the products planned for launch, create a question-answer session. The best strategy is to show the ‚Äúhuman‚Äù side of video marketing, life inside the company. You can appoint one company representative who will regularly appear in front of the camera for your social platforms. So many famous brands do. Try the technique in action. Choose a topic that is close to your business, and shoot issues with tips that will be useful and interesting to your potential consumers. The main indicator of successful content is its use for the audience. Therefore, the more important information you pass on to your viewers, the more views you can get. And this is a direct path to the popularity of the channel in particular and the brand as a whole.

Instagram and Snapchat

Not so long ago Instagram raised the allowable length of the video. On March 28, 2016, the launch of the innovation started and now it is already available to all users of the popular service. As stated on the Instagram blog, over the past six months (data for March 2016) , the time spent by users watching videos increased by more than 40% , so it was decided to provide an enhanced ability to view and download videos.

For videos to be interesting and informative, they should not be too long. In today's world, people are limited in time, so do not want to spend it on viewing content.

Video hosting Wistia , designed for business, one of the main competitors of YouTube, analyzed the dependence of audience involvement and the percentage of views on the length of the video. The result was quite expected: the shorter the video, the better . The priority was video lasting less than one minute . This study was conducted in 2012. For 4 years, the pace of life has become even faster, respectively, people began to value their time even more. So the conclusion suggests itself.

For the study several hundreds of branded channels from the fields of FMCG, cinema, cars, telecom and others were examined.

The main focus of the analysis was on the Engagement rate (engagement), which reflects the response to the video and user activity (comments, likes, reposts). The final stage of the study was monitoring the number of views, distribution and user feedback on the video.
