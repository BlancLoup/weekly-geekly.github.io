Who is the project manager according to the business owner and how to deal with it

I would like to talk about what today in Russia is meant by project management and what, according to many, is the role of the project manager.

Colleagues know that in our country there are certain discrepancies on this subject and some actively carry the right understanding to the masses, others have given up trying and pupated in their cozy little world, while others, unfortunately thinking that they are engaged in project management, will now find that it is not So.

To understand the depth of error, it is enough to open any website with vacancies and type in the search engine the phrase "project manager". What do you think will be the most vacancies? That's right - job sellers. Project managers for sales, project sales managers, project managers with customer search, cold calling and so on. And it would be nice if this is just such a specific business in which you are given a chair, table and telephone, determine the industry and offer to find a customer for the project for yourself, and then everything else, including resources for its execution. In this sense, it will, of course, be a project activity, however, more often it is just a matter of unformalized sales and order execution processes.

Perhaps these are links of one chain with well-known sales managers, which we still have in many stores instead of sellers, but in my opinion, this is the result of a deep misunderstanding on the part of managers and business owners about what projects are, who they are for and what directs what competences should be possessed and how it all differs from ordinary operations (moreover, that such operations are also often not known - for them the company just does something, people there do something and everything else buzzwords and other HYIP).
