Reports of the first meeting of Dart-developers in St. Petersburg

On November 30, the first meeting of Dart developers in Russia was held at the St. Petersburg office of Wrike.



The Dart community in Russia is not so numerous yet; nevertheless, Google language developers are ready to support our programmers using Dart in their projects to develop the ecosystem and talk about the latest changes. At the mitap, we realized that the language is of great interest not only to front-end vendors, but also to those who want to go into web development, having experience in Java, C # or PHP.

With the release of Angular 2 for Dart and several other updates, the language received a new impetus in development, so it was interesting for us to gather all interested developers to discuss news of the language, communicate directly with engineers from Google and, in particular, discuss the features of switching to Dart from typed and scripting languages .
