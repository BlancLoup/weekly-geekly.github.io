Suddenly, I had a task to choose products in the online store with my sister. It would seem simple, but my sister is 800 km away from me.

Throwing links to each other or fumbling one of the desktops, I considered ineffective. But there was a revolutionary service that is similar to Google Docs, but only for co-visiting sites - Channel.me .

Who needs Channel.me

The service is created by fervent guys from Holland and is under active development.
Channel.me is useful if you and a girl choose a tour or apartment for rent. Or just want to watch YouTube. It is free and you can start using it without registration.

There are also business solutions for online stores and online user support. Consultants will be able to guide the user through the catalog and help select suitable products.

Key features at the moment
  • You and your friends are watching the same page.
  • You can watch videos on Youtube
  • The mouse cursors are visible
  • There is a convenient mini-chat
  • You can leave stickers notes on the page.
  • There is a Russian language
Browser extensions allow you to create a channel based on the current page in one click.
