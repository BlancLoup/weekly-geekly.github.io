Sony Xperia go review



Sony continues to develop successful ideas embedded in niche models of past years. The new Sony Xperia go - a compact phone with a waterproof case replaced the familiar many Sony Ericsson Xperia active , the first Sony-protected smartphone on Android. The model is already on sale in Russia, and it is time to tell a little more in detail about what exactly Sony has prepared for its customers.

Design


Immediately striking reversal of the concept of unsinkable compact smartphone 180 degrees: replaced by a niche Xperia active came dressed in all-civilian Xperia go. The highest level of phone protection remained unchanged, but the image was completely changed: if Xperia active was bright (black and orange or black and white), with catchy detail in the form of a strap attachment, as powerful as a towing eye, then Xperia go is modest and nothing gives out his superpowers. Like James Bond, who has not had time to drink vodka with martini.

Ergonomics


The most noticeable feature of Go ergonomics is the lack of a camera key. The reasons are clear: fewer buttons - fewer slots through which water and sand can penetrate into the case. But still unusual.

Some replacement for it should be the camera key on the lock screen. This and the fast capture mode of fast capture in total give a fairly quick launch of the camera, even without a button.



The lack of a camera button made me complain about the location of the power button (also known as the screen lock) on the upper end of the case.



The traditional solution becomes not very convenient when you try to make a quick shot: by reflexively turning the phone into the "landscape" position, you find that you have to unlock the screen with your little finger - or rotate the phone again, losing precious seconds. It is no coincidence that the camera button is always located on the side, as it turns out on the most convenient place when taking a photo. The power button on the right side, like on Xperia P, for example, would save the day, but alas ... Go owners will have to develop a habit: first unlock the screen, then turn the phone. Or shoot in the "book" orientation.

The display of the smartphone is as if raised above the front surface of Go, which forms a smooth ledge under its lower part. This relief not only forms the recognizable look of the Sony Xperia, but also allows you to conveniently position your thumb in the usual position - without the risk of accidentally pressing one of the three located just above the touch keys.



It resembles the ideology of human curvature (man-pleasing bends), suggesting the sharpened design under human palms.

There is a microphone at the bottom.



The volume rocker is located on the usual place - on the right.



Connectors left only two, the most necessary - microUSB and headphones. Both are closed with tight plugs, which, however, are easily otkovyrivayutsya nail at the edges.





When removing a headphone or USB cable from the connector, a warning appears on the phone screen about the need to close the plugs back - otherwise, they say, you are waterproof.



The care is pleasant, but unnecessary - the spectacle dangling on an elastic band is not a very aesthetic spectacle, and it is also frightening - well, how it will come off. Therefore, it is unlikely that Go owners will forget to put them in their place. A screen warning can be turned off.

Despite the fact that the model is categorized as compact - you can't call it small. The dimensions of the device are 111 x 60.3 x 9.8 mm - in the breast pocket of a jacket or shirt it fits just fine, but it also lies confidently in your hand. 3.5 - 3.7 ", probably the ideal size in terms of convenience for the palms of any size.
