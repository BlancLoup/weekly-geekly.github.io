Intangible motivation - what is included there

I really, really love to read about Maslow's pyramid, intangible motivation, growth in the team, and in general reshpekt and respect, which by no means can not be counted in money.

tl / dr: a list of all kinds of intangible bonuses, and what to ask at the interview.

Most of all I love only the stories from HR at the interviews, of the form - with us, you will not believe, white! 1 single salary.

But only often the list is exhausted by this, alas. I'll start with the material

  • Salary: white, gray or both. And in what proportion. How much salary, how much bonus, is there any.

  • Reverse premium - fines. Is, for what and from where are deducted.
  • Indexation of RFP for inflation
  • Processing, work on weekends, their frequency, payment.
  • Work outside the plan - for example, the elimination of accidents. Payment, terms of payment.
  • Is there a re-certification system for and not the results, "grades" and other things, and how it all works.
