Video review Syma X8G - quadcopter with 8 MP HD camera

Today we would like to consider a Syma X8G quadrocopter, with GoPro analogue on board. Test drive and review of this model in the video below, which was specifically translated for GeekTimes. Enjoy watching.



Unlike previous versions of the Syma X8G, an 8 MP camera appeared in the kit, which is fixed using a fixed gimbal. Photos and videos are shot in HD quality. The only negative is that this action camera is powered by the quadcopter itself, which reduces its flight time.

Syma X8G is sold for $ 112.22 * with free shipping to Russia.


Specifications:
  • Battery: 7.4V 2000 mAh Li-po
  • Flight time: 10-15 minutes (you can increase by removing his legs and camera from him)
  • Power supply: 4 AAA batteries
  • Charging time: about 3 hours
  • Dimensions: 48.5x48.5x19 cm
  • Material: plastic
  • Number of channels: 4
  • Control frequency: 2.4GHz
  • Communication range: ~ 100 meters

Equipment:
  • Screws - 8 pcs.
  • Screw protection - 4 pcs.
  • Feet - 4 pcs.
  • Charger - 1 pc.
  • Battery - 1 pc.
  • Control panel - 1 pc.
  • Camera - 1 pc.
  • Memory card for 2 GB - 1 pc.
  • Card reader - 1 pc.
  • Screwdriver - 1 pc.
  • Manual in English - 1 pc.



Conclusion:
Syma X8G belongs to the "budget" class of quadcopters and is perfect as a first option for beginners to practice aerial shooting. But do not forget that the quadric is equipped with collector motors, therefore it is better not to fly it in windy weather.
