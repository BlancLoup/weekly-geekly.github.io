"Energy" points

In the future, according to designers Hyun-Joong Kim and Kwang-Seok Jeong, sunglasses will not only protect your eyes, but also provide various portable gadgets with solar energy.


An attempt to combine the incompatible - the concept of "converting" the energy of sunglasses ( Self-Energy Converting Sunglasses ) - is a combination of solar cells and unspecified nanotechnology. The latter, according to the designers' idea, should ensure not only the conversion of solar energy into electrical energy, but also leave the possibility to the person wearing these glasses to see through them. "Collected" energy will be supplied on the rim to the port, from which, by cable, is already directed to a particular portable device.
