For example, researchers at the Wright Patterson Medical Center in Dayton, Ohio examined the surface of 68 dollar bills and found 87% of banknotes a bacterium that could trigger a generalized infection in people with impaired immune systems, such as those suffering from human immunodeficiency virus or cancer .
