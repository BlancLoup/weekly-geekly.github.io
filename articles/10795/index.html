Contests Series Connected Services Sandbox Mashup Service Design and Assembly

There is a series of contests announced by Microsoft and British Telecom on software development with a total prize fund of 40,000 US dollars. A competition is now open in the field of information architecture and GUI, with a prize fund of $ 4,500. Perhaps someone from the Habr-people will be interested to try yourself in it.

This is actually an idea contest. The idea can be presented either simply as a description (in English, of course), or as a series of interface prototypes. And you can describe + prototypes, of course :) The deadline for submitting an application is June 21, 16:00 EDT. The prize for the first place 3000 USD. In general, the value of participation in the contest in the opportunity to earn extra money, to think about an interesting problem and put in the portfolio work for well-known brands.

More details about this particular competition (problem, rules, prize money, etc.) can be found here . Competition powered by TopCoder .
