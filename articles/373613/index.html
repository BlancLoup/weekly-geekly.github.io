Security officers told what messengers terrorists use in the Russian Federation


Telegram, Viber, WhatsApp and Skype are weapons of terrorists

Sources in the security forces told Izvestia what kind of messengers terrorists use to recruit Russians into their ranks. According to statistics, ISIS (banned in Russia) most often uses Telegram, Viber, WhatsApp and Skype. With the help of these messengers, thousands of Russians have already been recruited.

The interviewee revealed a specific mechanism for how recruiters work. At the first stage, they identify people most prone to information processing. According to him, the terrorist attack in the St. Petersburg metro on April 3, 2017 was coordinated through WhatsApp and Telegram, and the activities of all sabotage and terrorist groups planning to commit terrorist attacks in Russia were sent by the ISIS emissaries via Telegram and the Zello Internet radio.

Moreover, ISIS employs a security service whose specialists are knowledgeable in IT-technologies.
