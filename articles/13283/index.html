Leningrad region - the first in Yandex

The Leningrad Region is the first Russian region to receive the opportunity to present its news on the portal of the largest Russian information retrieval system Yandex .

In July, an agreement was concluded between the information department of the Government of the Leningrad Region and the company that owns the Yandex search system, according to which the main news of the regional press center is also published online in Yandex news. The portal of the Administration of the Leningrad Region was the first to attract the attention of Yandex professionals, and now it will be presented as an official source of information on Yandex news along with the websites of the President of Russia, Priority National Projects, the Ministry of Industry and Energy of the Russian Federation, the Federal Agency for Subsoil Use, Rossport and FSUE Russian Post .

The portal of the Leningrad region has existed for 10 years and is saturated with a large number of diverse information about the region. The news feed offers daily not only news, but also announcements of events, comments of specialists and at the same time, a volume that can easily compete with the sites of the largest news agencies. Also the choice was affected by the high traffic of this site.
