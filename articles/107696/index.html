Xperia x10 / x10 mini / x10 mini pro - upgrade from 1.6 to 2.1

Naturally, I became interested and happily ran to update my phone.

As usual, I launched Sony Ericsson PC companion, saw the update request, clicked on, I saw a warning that during the update all my personal data will be deleted and I need to make a backup - ok, I went to Backup & Restore , installed the proposed application, made backup.
I click further, put a checkbox that I made a backup and I understand that all my personal data will be destroyed, I wait a while and receive a message
