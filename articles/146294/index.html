The development of figurative thinking



Fiery greetings, dear habravchane!

In my debut article, I want to tell you about the games-games on the development of figurative thinking, which will be useful not only to people of creative professions: unlike the exercises on the development of imagination, in which the right hemisphere dominates, the pumping of figurative thinking actively involves analytical skills, and this is a symbiosis of the two hemispheres. Therefore, these exercises are perfect not only for obtaining any fruits for people whose work is somehow connected with figurative information, but also for any person who wants to have a sharp mind and well-coordinated interaction of both hemispheres of the brain.

Exercises should be performed in a comfortable environment: no time limit, no competitive motivation and criticism of actions, the average degree of achievement motivation. In the exercises, not only the number of responses produced, but also their originality is appreciated. You can test your imaginative thinking in one of the chapters of the wonderful training manual ‚Äú Diagnosis and Activation of Creative Abilities ‚Äù.

In the image of
In this exercise, you need to find figurative synonyms of the object as a whole or its individual parts. For example: violin ~ waist ~ bottle.

The chain can be either centered (synonyms stem from the original version), or consistent (synonyms cling to the previous version), or chaotic (some sequence is not respected).

You can complicate the exercise and make chains not only in a figurative likeness, but also in functional, applied and other. This technique is called the visual matrix .

Kaleidoscope
The essence of this game is very simple and is based on the ratio of the object and our projection on it . In this game, we simply juggle our projections onto the observable. The beaten example is a glass half empty or full.
