Genres and settings of mobile games - statistics for April 2017

Our partner, Playliner , an analytic company, has prepared a study on top genres and mobile game settings for the blog of the Management Game Internet Projects program. Under the cat, you will find information about which genres and settings are currently leading in the top of the Google Play grossing. Were analyzed 150 games on April 1, 2017.



The leaders in the distribution of genres were the battlers . 32 games of this genre are placed in the top 150.
