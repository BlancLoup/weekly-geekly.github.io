The best sites of Runet in the online game "Ru-Voice"

Dear Habravchane!

REG.RU announces universal voting for the best sites on the Runet! For this we have prepared a unique tool - the online game "Ru-Voice". No more automatic ratings. Now everything is decided by users.

How does it all start?
You just need to go to the site www.ru-golos.ru and share your favorite URL. The more votes will be given in favor of the resource, the more territory it will occupy on the playing field.
On the playing field? - Yes! The first voices formed the basis of a map of popular sites, which received a visual embodiment in the form of a tag cloud on the website www.ru-golos.ru and before the final of the game, which is scheduled for October of this year, will change right before the eyes of the voters.
Everyone can vote!

The success of a resource in a game depends not only on the number of voters, but also on their activity. Each user can vote for any number of sites, but give no more than one vote for a specific site per day.
