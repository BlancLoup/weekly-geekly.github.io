New cities with Google Street View

Four new cities have appeared in Google Maps with the support of the Street View function, recently ( announced in May ), which allows you to virtually walk, or rather take a ride, through the streets. San Diego's high resolution images from Google and images of Houston, Los Angeles and Orlando in low resolution from Immersive Media were added to previously available city photos.
