I find out dearly by the retina: modern methods of identification by biometric indicators



Dactyloscopy is the most well-known and widespread method of identifying individuals using a biometric parameter. Iris Identification




Like a fingerprint, the iris pattern is a unique characteristic of a person, and the method of identification according to this biometric parameter, according to experts, exceeds the usual fingerprinting in reliability. In order to fix the pattern on the iris, you need a camera with high resolution. The resulting image is enlarged and converted into a unique code assigned to a person.

The drawing of the iris, which is finally formed in the second year of a child‚Äôs life, remains almost unchanged during life if the person does not receive injuries and does not suffer from serious ophthalmic pathologies. The advantage of the method is the simplicity in scanning. It is not necessary for a person to look intently at one point, because spots on the retina are located directly on the surface of the eyeball and are easily read at a distance not exceeding 1 meter. It is convenient to use this method in banking organizations or public transport. Smartphone manufacturers were also interested in technology - in 2015, the first model with an iris scanner, the Fujitsu Arrows NX F-04G, went on sale in Japan. According to the developers, the introduction of identification technology on the iris will help protect the personal data of smartphone owners.

Retina Identification




Scanning the retina - the inner shell of the eyeball, which reacts to light, is more difficult: for this, low-intensity infrared light rays are sent to the blood vessels of the back wall of the eye through the pupil. This method of identification is considered highly effective and is widely used in government and military facilities.

Capillary pattern of the retina differs even in twins, which reduces the likelihood of identification errors. However, in 2012, scientists from the University of Notre Dame in the United States found errors in determining the personalities of people whose data were entered into the database before 2008, and proved that, unlike the pattern on the iris, the retinal pattern is subject to a number of age changes.

And again, manufacturers of mobile gadgets did not stand aside. A number of companies ( for example , the Chinese ZTE CORPORATION) are working on the creation of combined identification technologies on the retina and iris.
