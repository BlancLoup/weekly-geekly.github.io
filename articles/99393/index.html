PowerColor launches video card with original cooling system


I think the PowerColor brand is known to many. At one time it was one of the most popular manufacturers of video cards ATI, although then somehow they sort of died down. But now they decided to release something original and it turned out they have the following: PowerColor PCS + HD 5770 Vortex Edition.

This video card is overclocked in the factory, has support for DirectX 11, but its main feature is the cooling system.


The peculiarity of this cooling system is that if you have enough space in the case, the cooler can be raised higher from the radiator, thereby improving air circulation and, according to the developers, get an additional 15 degrees of cold.

Yes, and the characteristics of the card "not children":
  • 40-nanometer AMD Radeon HD 5770 chip
  • 1.36 teraflops
  • 800 stream processors
  • 128-bit memory bus at a frequency of 1 225 MHz (although there could have been more widely done, IMHO ...)
  • 1 GB GDDR5, 4900 MHz
  • Raster and shader domains operate at 900 MHz
  • Directx 11
  • ATI CrossFireX
  • Dual-DVI, HDMI and DisplayPort

Memory (from 4.8 to 4.9 GHz), raster and shader domains (from 800 to 900 MHz) and memory bus (from 1200 to 1225 MHz) were overclocked

Also, buyers will enjoy a pleasant surprise in the box: a coupon for downloading a licensed copy of the game Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

True, how much all this happiness will cost is not yet known.
