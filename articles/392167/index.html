5 home projectors

What could be more interesting for a child than to watch old Soviet filmstrips ? Just watch the digitized filmstrips on the wall using a projector. If you do not have a tablet with such a function, you can choose from several options for home projectors.



If your apartment has the opportunity to place the projector 3.5 meters from the wall - then the image on the wall of the UC - 40 projector will have a diagonal of 100 inches. The resolution of this projector is 480 x 320, the brightness is 400 lumens, but it will cost $ 33.89 * . For filmstrips quite fit. The device has ports AV, SD, VGA, HDMI.





The UNIC UC40 + projector allows you to broadcast an image on the wall in a resolution of 800x480 pixels, but it works with 1080p format. Brightness - 800 lumens. And the size of the picture on the wall will be from 34 to 130 inches. Now you can buy it for only $ 63.39 * .







Multifunctional home projector with a brightness of 2600 lumens at a resolution of 800x480 pixels. You can already watch it almost in the light. The device costs $ 109.76 * .



The Excelvan CL720 projector boasts a resolution of 1280x800 pixels, support for HDMI, USB, 3000 lumens of brightness and an analogue input for TV. That is, you can simply insert the antenna into it and use it in place of the TV. The contrast is 2000: 1. For sound, there are two built-in speakers. Image size - from 25 to 260 inches. It costs $ 115.99 * . You can choose from two options for body colors - black and white.



This miniature multi-colored projector on Android 4.4 and with a resolution of 854 x 480 is even more expensive. One of the functions of this gadget-like Rubik's cube is to charge mobile devices. The gadget's battery is 4800 mAh, so it will turn out at least once to charge the smartphone with 3000 mAh. With a 49% discount, the projector costs $ 164.99 * . But the brightness of the gadget - only 70 lumens, so that it can be viewed only in the dark. For hiking - the most it.





In my opinion, the best choice is Excelvan CL720 * . First, it is the brightest of the options presented, and secondly, its resolution is one of the highest. But the most important thing for me is the ability to connect an antenna and use it as a zomboyaschik.
