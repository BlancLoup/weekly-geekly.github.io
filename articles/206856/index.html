Not so long ago, IBM announced its intention to open the Watson toolkit for third-party developers. It is planned to do this in the form of the IBM Watson Developers Cloud cloud service. It is assumed that developers will interact with the "cloud" through the API, opening up access to approximately 40 different tools of the system.

At the same time, partners of the company will be able to use their own data to create applications, as well as work with the IBM Watson Content Store, using data from third-party companies. The corporation attracted to the project about 500 specialists, experts from various fields of science and technology, who will help partner companies to create applications based on Watson resources.
