You must understand that all sales channels for products can be divided into two categories:

  1. Cooperation with retailers;
  2. Own retail (wholesale) network.

Each manufacturer somehow tries both approaches. It is important to note that the larger and more popular the manufacturer is, the easier it is for him to organize his own retail sales scheme: the brand, name, and popularity among consumers work for him.
