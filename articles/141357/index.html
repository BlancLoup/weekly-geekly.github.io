In the assembly of Opera Labs (Mobile) appeared support extensions

On the blog of mobile browsers Opera, there was news about the release of an experimental laboratory build of Opera Mobile with support for extensions.


According to the developers, this is not a release, but only a laboratory version, and the design and functionality of which, of course, will undergo changes more than once.


It is proposed to test this build on Android (.apk) or in the emulator on operating systems such as Windows (.zip) Mac (.dmg) Linux (.tar.gz) and also try extensions such as Ghostery , Layers , Google Images Direct , LastPass , HTTP Header .

You can also try the work of other extensions , but so far most of those I have tested do not work or work incorrectly.
