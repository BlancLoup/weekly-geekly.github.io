MobileFest 2011 in St. Petersburg

Summer is over, but for now the weather allows you to try to spend more time outside. So the guys from i-Free and GeeksFreaks thought and decided to organize the final barcamp of the season in the open air. The event will be devoted to the development of software for mobile phones and projects in the field of mobile Internet.

In addition to reports of participants waiting for the rock music festival , more than 100 projects in the field of mobile technology, 50+ interesting topics, songs around the fire and much, much more.

MobileFest 2011 will be held from 8 to 10 September in the sports complex "Northern Slope" near St. Petersburg. To the complex of participants will be taken free of charge by bus.

The festival program will consist of speakers from leading companies in the field of mobile technologies - Alexey Kokin ( Nokia ), Olga Egorova ( Samsung ), Mikhail Chernomordikov ( Microsoft ), Andrey Kiselev and Ola Larsen ( Blackberry ), etc.
