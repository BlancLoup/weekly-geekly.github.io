Leopard, iWork, and iLife may appear in June

Today on the ThinkSecret website there was news that said that information from the release date of MacOS X 10.5 was obtained from a reliable source (which remained unnamed).

The news says that Leopard will be released in June, somewhere close to the Worldwide Developers Conference 2007, which is scheduled for June 11 (by the way, an iPhone will appear on the market this day).

It used to be said that MacOS X 10.5 Leopard will appear this spring, but informally call the time until the end of the third fiscal quarter, June 21, in the spring - so Apple in this situation fits perfectly in time.

Also (again, according to an anonymous source), iLife and iWork will be released at the end of June, which may exchange the ending of '07 for Leopard Edition.
