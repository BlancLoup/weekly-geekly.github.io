May Rambler.iOS meeting



Rambler.iOS - periodic meetings of iOS developers, conducted by Rambler & Co. In May, we are going for the third time - and we will be happy to see guests!

Initially, these meetings were strictly local in nature, and only our team took part in them. But, as you know, knowledge and experience should be shared with the rest - therefore, since last time, we began to invite guests - both colleagues from other companies and independent programmers.

We are not the first to organize Moscow meetings for iOS developers - so we try to learn from the mistakes of others. Since all the speakers are members of our large team, all performances go through a preliminary audition. This stage helps to ensure an early feedback, on the basis of which the speaker can correct his report - correct mistakes, clarify non-obvious points or add interesting content.

So, the themes of Rambler.iOS # 3 reports:
  • Dependency Injection in iOS (Tolstoy Egor - yourdestiny )
  • Test-Driven Development in iOS (Andrey Rezanov - hastewave )

Time: May 14, 19:00
Venue: Moscow, Varshavskoye highway, 9, p.1 (Business Center Danilovsky Manufactories).
