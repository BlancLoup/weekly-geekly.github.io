New layers on 2GIS map

If you have not updated 2GIS for PC to the latest version, it's time to do it. After all, something new appeared in it, namely additional layers.




Some might think, ‚ÄúWait! Add. layers were in 2GIS before! ‚Äù And it will be right, but only in part. Earlier ext. layers existed in the form of plug-ins that needed to be found somewhere, downloaded, installed, or, if you want real hardcore, done by yourself. Now everything is easier - additional layers for your city are available through one menu.

Layers are updated via the Internet every time 2GIS is launched. In this new will be highlighted in color. Without any exaggeration, the layers are included in one click - select the one you need, it will be loaded and displayed on the map.

The information displayed in layers can be practically any: webcams, points with free Wi-Fi, bicycle rentals, whatever. Therefore, if you are suddenly struck by the idea that it would be nice to create a layer, and you have all the necessary information for this, send it to us! The layers@2gis.ru box was created with this very purpose and is waiting for your letters around the clock.

To clarify: you can send information about what you want to see on the map and where it is located. We will select the best ideas and produce the appropriate layers. One thing: the additional layers do not contain ads, so we will not consider commercial data.

Wait for your offers!
