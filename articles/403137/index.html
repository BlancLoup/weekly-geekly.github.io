Prisoners in the United States collected computers and went online through the prison network


Here was placed one of the illegal servers created by prisoners.

A prison is a place where people who are dangerous to society are isolated. Here, it is believed, prisoners can be reformed by convincing them to leave the vicious path. The conditions in most prisons are not very good, and people try to improve the quality of their lives on their own. For a modern person who is imprisoned, the situation is aggravated by a ban on equipment. No phones, no familiar Internet - nothing.

Therefore, criminals (and not only) invent a variety of ways to get all this workarounds. Someone carries mobile phones, but someone creates a server and connects it to the prison network to communicate with the outside world. It was this method of improving the conditions of its existence that was chosen by prisoners in one of the American prisons located in Ohio. The reformed criminals created not even one, but two working computers, hid them in the ceilings of the ceiling and hooked them up to the prison network.

Pornographic materials and various software were downloaded to the hard drives, including a proxy server, VPN, VOIP, antivirus, Tor, hacker gadgets and the Wireshark packet analyzer. All this became known thanks to the discovery of the hi-tech hiding place by prison staff.

All elements of the created computers were received from friends and acquaintances who somehow managed to transfer the details with the parcels. Found the work of prison hackers by pure chance. Those began to load a large amount of data that was noticed by the administrator. In this prison there is a certain limit on traffic, which at some point has been exceeded for some unknown reason. Plus, once the prison network administrator received a system alert about the connected device.
