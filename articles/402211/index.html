Five ideas how to distract children from the TV and tablet

If a child spends all day sitting in front of the TV and is distracted only on the tablet, it is not at all necessary to declare war on electronics. Some children's psychologists say that the fascination with gadgets among children of different ages is a new reality, so categorically prohibiting or hiding is not the best solution. It is better to adopt other devices and show how much interesting things can be done outside the home.



Our children with incredible speed master everything new and high-tech, realizing the craving for knowledge and new discoveries. On the one hand, this is good, but when the whole range of interests comes down to the tablet and game console, many parents begin to sound the alarm. Evil technologies have locked the child at home, and he communicates with friends only through instant messengers! But in our childhood everything was the opposite, and parents spent hours searching for a child on the street.

But they are not to blame, and modern electronics manufacturers all the more. Children slide to the forefront of progress, and their parents' hobbies of yesterday do not attract them anymore. But this does not mean that you can not find a compromise between gadgets, exploring the world and active entertainment with friends or family on the street. So we made a selection of ideas how to distract a child from cartoons and games. Well, the main advice of all child psychologists: do not forget to study and test these devices with your child, because the time spent together is even more valuable than the gift itself.

Quadcopters


Having played enough in computer games, the child has already gained management skills with the help of joysticks. Great, now is the time to uncover these skills in the real world and learn how to control technology. And this is not as easy as it seems. It will not work in a couple of clicks to start the mission anew, so mastering the copter will help develop perseverance and patience, and you will have to become an example to follow and stop despair in case of failure. So that the learning process does not become too tedious, you can come up with goals that are interesting for the child - for example, learn how to land the rotor in a specific location.

Buying the most sophisticated model is not worth it, because the main goal is a training one, and you shouldn't blame it for breakdowns either. But to teach the repair technique can and should be - this experience will be useful in adult life.

This, of course, is not the club of a young aircraft designer, but much better than staying in the summer in the walls of a house.

Telescopes


Nothing motivates the study of the new, as the awareness of the scale of the world around us and the amount of the unknown. For any child, the starry sky is an absolutely mysterious space. Why not try to study it together? With the help of an ordinary amateur telescope, you can see quite a lot for a novice astronomer. The main thing is that the weather did not let down, and the sky was clear. Consider the craters and contours of the "seas" on the moon, count the Pleiads, see the satellites of Jupiter and the Ring of Saturn. With a telescope, you can see the grandiose Andromeda alive and look at the dense cluster of stars in the sleeve of our native galaxy. Do not forget to supplement this with a Hubble gallery of pictures, and as soon as the child sees Venus, show photos from its surface made by Soviet aircraft.

The main characteristic of the telescope is the maximum magnification value. In addition to it, when choosing a model, it is worth paying attention to the limiting magnitude. It shows the level of luminance of objects available for observation. The higher this value, the duller the stars and planets can be studied through the optics of the apparatus.
