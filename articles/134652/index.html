DevCon'12: registration for the conference is open!



We are pleased to announce that registration for the DevCon'12 conference is announced open! Along with the opening of registration, we updated the conference website http://www.msdevcon.ru , which now contains more information about the format of the event, key topics, payment methods, and so on.

DevCon'12 - is the main event of the spring of 2012 from Microsoft for developers. The conference is aimed at software development professionals, testers, architects, development team leaders, and IT industry experts. For two days, you will be able to take part in any of the sessions, explore technologies in practice - visit Boot Camps *, round tables and various interactive events, and discuss with colleagues and experts questions about the future of development technologies in an informal setting.

DevCon'12 conference format:
  • 2 days, country format;
  • 50 reports;
  • Boot Camps * (Learning technology in practice);
  • Master classes;
  • Night of Coding (Night coding);
  • Focus groups;
  • Round tables;
  • Evening program;
  • Outdoor activities;
  • Sports activities.
Participation in DevCon'12 conference is paid (accommodation and meals are included in the price, as well as transfer from Moscow). At the conference there is a flexible system of discounts, in a hurry to register early , having received the maximum discount! For the convenience of registration, we added the ability to log in via Twitter and Facebook. For development teams there is a group registration.

For more information on pricing and payment, please visit the conference website http://www.msdevcon.ru/conditions .

Conference DevCon'12 is an all inclusive conference, that is, the cost of participation includes everything:
  • Food and hotel accommodation;
  • Participation in the main program;
  • Attend master classes;
  • Participation in round tables and focus groups;
  • Transfer from Moscow and back;
  • Entertaining and sports program;
  • Evening program.
DevCon'12 will be rich in topics of reports on all the most relevant areas of software development. Well-known development experts will attend and make presentations at the conference. We are currently preparing a program of reports, follow the conference site and expect a number of surprises.

Share information about the conference with your friends on Twitter through the hash tag #msdevcon. Write about your wishes on the reports or the conference as a whole in the comments.



Learn about how DevCon took place last time in May 2011 via the link http://www.msdevcon.ru/devcon11/ .

Welcome to the site and registration , waiting for you at the conference!
