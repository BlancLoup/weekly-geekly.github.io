Autoportal.ua - Car portal on the Ukrainian Internet or autocadab killer :)

A new automobile portal, AvtoPortal, has launched on the Ukrainian Internet, the creators did not mess with the name.
The main focus of the project was on a high-quality catalog of new cars, with good descriptions and photos.
The list of models in the catalog is implemented with side photos, which visually helps to determine.

Also on the site implemented an interesting service for calculating distances , which allows you to calculate the route and the cost of gas from city A to city B in all CIS countries and Europe.
Of course the site is not deprived of news, bulletin board for the sale of cars, a catalog of tape recorders, acoustics and a forum.
