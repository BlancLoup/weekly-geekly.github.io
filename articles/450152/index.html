SEO optimization product card: step by step instructions + lifehacks

An important part of the online store - the goods. But most SEO specialists do not pay him enough attention. Prepared for you a mandatory list of what should be on the item card and detailed information on its optimization.




We at AlkoDesign stand for proper and high-quality SEO. Earlier we wrote about how to correctly optimize the sections of categories in the online store . Category sections are not all that an online store consists of. Its important part is the product. In this article we collected everything that should be on the product card of an online store, prepared a cheat sheet and a list of life hacks that will make your life easier.

Of course, where without the name of the goods. Still, visitors are not Vanga and they should know what they will buy.

Detailed description


This text is written with the title of the second level.

An important nuance is uniqueness.

What to do with it, if the text, for example, technical? Nothing. We do not recommend to reinvent the wheel and grind out what is not.

About clothes and household appliances you can make a unique text of +100 characters. On the screws - is unlikely. Therefore, we rely on the subject and act on the situation.

And if a lot of goods?

There is a small trick: insert the name of the product in the text. Even if the text from the supplier is repeated on all sites (yes, it happens), then the product name will be unique within the site.

Documentation


How is documentation related to search engine promotion? The answer is simple: search engines rank above those sites that have answers to a large number of possible user questions.

That is, if you have a technical online store and the user usually asks for a detailed description, characteristics and instructions - make his life easier! Post this information on the site. It is desirable in the file and with the ability to download it.

Reviews


Feedback is a magical thing.

Firstly, they show that the store is used. The user increases the credibility of the product and the site as a whole.

Secondly, reviews (if they are real) are a unique text. And it enhances the uniqueness of the product card as a whole and has a positive effect on its ranking.

Tips:

  • remove the captcha to write reviews.
  • set up adding feedback through authorization in the social. networks
  • make a rating by reviews.
  • Break reviews on cons, pros and comments.

This will help users quickly leave a comment and get the full amount of information about the product.

Lifehacks


Without life hacking on the Internet in any way. We also divided them into categories. Some of them may be familiar to you. But repetition is the mother of learning!

Products that are out of stock


Rule number 1: if the product is not available, then it can not be bought.

Rename the ‚ÄúBuy‚Äù button to ‚ÄúOut of stock‚Äù.

Further:
  • remove the product from the internal search;
  • move it down a category;
  • add a display of alternative products.

If there is no product and it is unlikely to appear, do not rush to delete the page!
Carefully read the statistics. If users are actively logging onto the item card, leave similar items on the page (but do not delete the page).
If the traffic is not going or it has dropped significantly, put a redirect to a similar product or product category.

Micromarking of goods

Remember to include microdata:

  • Currency;
  • Picture.
