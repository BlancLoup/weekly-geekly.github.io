SuperJob Meetup. System business analysis. Live broadcast

Today at 19:00 Moscow time in the SuperJob office will take place the IT-meetup "System Business Analysis". Join the live broadcast!



At the meeting, we will discuss working with business system requirements at an early stage: organization of communication, the transition from business analysis to system analysis, methods to speed up work, tools to improve the quality of analytical artifacts. We invite system, business analysts and managers of IT-projects.

What is needed to launch a new IT project or an iteration of the system development?

  • Sponsor - forecast how much the system costs, how and when the investment will pay off.
  • Technical team - the requirements for the planning and execution of works.
  • Marketers - the main chips for the "packaging" of the product.
  • End users are understandable explanations of what they will get.

All this need

  • Quickly and cheaply, as this is the stage when people especially do not like to wait.
  • Super quality, as here are the main risks of the project.
  • It is clear, since at this stage the most dizzying changes in the style of communication take place - from marketing slogans to strict TK, from backstage discussions to public presentations.
