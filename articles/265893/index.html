"The principle of the switch" and its influence on QA



The ‚ÄúSweetcard Principle‚Äù is emerging. Once in IT, ‚Äúswitches‚Äù most often have a two- or threefold increase in wages relative to their previous activities. Then a more positive scenario unfolds, and the skill level begins to grow.
