New LG monitors of Ultrawide line

Good day, Habrahabr! Recently, new models of monitors of the line of 2014 came to our office, and we will talk about the most interesting models today. Changes in them - the mass, so that there is exactly what to read about. Sit back.



We, like any other large company, receive a huge stream of feedback, reviews, opinions of both professionals and ordinary users, and we try to take into account criticism when creating the next generation of products. This year, the changes touched on many aspects, touched on both the line of smartphones and TVs (about them and their new Smart TV system - just a couple of days), and, of course, the heroes of today's material - monitors did not stand aside.

New technologies


In the entire line of monitors, which will be made in 2014, we have applied a number of technological innovations designed to save your eyes and improve the display of pictures on LCD panels. One of these technologies has become Flicker Safe - LED matrix backlight completely devoid of PWM modulation. Brightness is adjusted smoothly, within wide limits and without a perceptible effect on color rendition or image quality.

The second significant improvement was the complete unification of controls in new monitor models: the five-position D-Pad on the bottom edge of the display.



Interactive and visual menu, improved Russification and, as a result, convenient management even of those monitor functions that are needed once a year.



Reader Mode is the third technology, albeit quite modest. There is nothing supernatural in it, the only thing it does is quickly switch the color balance and white balance to warm colors, reducing the contrast of black text on a white background to comfortable values ‚Äã‚Äãand reducing eye strain. If you sit at multi-page documents, tables and charts all day long, and color accuracy is secondary for you - the function is simply must have, in the evening your eyes will thank you. The parameters for Reader Mode were selected by LG engineers in conjunction with eye care practitioners and safety professionals.

Ultra-Wide sinema screen
The line of monitors with an aspect ratio of 21: 9 came to taste to the users, so this year it has significantly expanded and replenished with several interesting models at once.



The most interesting monitor in this group is the premium LG 34UM95 , which will be available soon. 34 inches, resolution 3440x1440, support for Display Port and Thunderbolt II, support for 10-bit input mode per channel, a new backlight system and support for both hardware calibration and 4-screen split mode for the most popular OS: Windows (7, 8) and OS X.



The monitor received a thin-frame design that has become a classic for LG, a new stand with adjustable tilt angle and different razboltovkoy for two positions in height, neat speakers 2x7 watts.



The display resolution is comfortable enough to work without increasing the DPI in the system, both in OS X and WIndows 8.
