Why I won't hire you



A friend of mine recently was actively looking for work, and asked me how to apply myself to an employer. Googling, we flipped through a dozen articles on how to do it and how not to do it during the search, and I decided to translate one of them for the habrasoobshchestvo, perhaps this will help someone to avoid the mistakes that are described in it. The manner of the article is quite provocative, the language is obscene in some places, so I tried to smooth the corners. So let's go. .

If I spend more than 30 seconds to read your resume - forget about the job, you pissed me off. For some reason, since the resumes were sent electronically, people began to think that they could write 10 pages of text about how good they were for this job, expecting me to read every word. You cannot say why you love your current job.


I always ask people what they like most about their job before telling them about all the details of a job. I want to see if you can be happy at the new job. I do not mind if it does not apply to future work, although it helps. Just be prepared that I can ask such a question - and in your best interest to have a good answer.

You have no plans for the future.

If you can tell me how work for me will help you move forward in your long-term plans, I understand that you have an incentive to work productively. No relevant skills


Please do not disturb me if you do not have the necessary skills - I still find out. If you are a programmer, then you have to show skill at the interview, and show it well. If you are a project manager, you need to know how to build a project plan or project vision.
