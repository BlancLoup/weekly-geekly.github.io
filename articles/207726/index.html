RuTracker.org blocked

Everything. RuTracker.org blocked.

Added.
There is no information on the listing of the domain rutracker.org on the website of the single registry.

Added.
WildShadow suggests that the tracker is blocked by the IP address 195.82.146.114 since 12/27/2013.

Added. Official information :
From 12/27/2013 Roskomnadzor obliges providers of the Russian Federation to block the ip-address 195.82.146.114 on which the Rutreker forum is located.

In this case, the domain name in rutracker.org in the search nap.rkn.gov.ru/reestr/ does not appear.

We did not receive official information about the blocking and its reasons. Just as there were no requirements from Roskomnadzor to remove information that violates the laws of the Russian Federation.

Thus, the forum may already be unavailable with some providers.
