Analysis of the Windows VPS proposal from Russian hosters

Greetings, habrazhiteli!

Habr I read regularly and for a long time, but decided to write for the first time.
And that's why.
Not so long ago, I was given the task to study a proposal on virtual dedicated servers for Windows. This topic was not very popular in Russia and the proposal is small. I was only interested in the option of using a relatively modern and new virtualization technology from Microsoft - Hyper-V. For those unfamiliar with the features of the hypervisor and the differences from, for example, Virtuozzo, I recommend reading ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyper-V . There are many useful links. Microsoft is quite active in promoting technology, on www.techdays.ru there are also a lot of good reports. The technology is really worthy of attention and respect, but now is not about that.

I spent quite a lot of time studying the offer for renting virtual servers under Windows, and I would like to share the information received, perhaps, for someone to come in handy.
I chose 4 hosters and made a comparison of the price and technical characteristics offered by the VPS for guidance.

Summary
After collecting all this information, I personally have no doubts left. It may sound like PR Mchost, but they are really great and out of competition:
- the lowest prices for used resources
- a large number of preset templates
- do not zhlobitsya and use Enterprise Edition, not the Web or Standard
- powerful DC and unlimited traffic without any problems with the "shareware"
- of the minuses so far, I discovered only the inability to increase certain parameters of the UPU (frequency and memory) without switching to a higher tariff. Want the best options - go to a more expensive rate. And the lack of a full-fledged backup system (only snapshots).

I am not discarding infoboxes and using them too, because they are pleased with their test period of 1 month. But their prices (excluding PROMO-512) do not hold water.

I do not claim for completeness and objectivity of the description.
I hope the information will be useful.
