Webcast on NTV-PLUS



Today, I was puzzled by the question of watching the CSKA Champions League match - Sevilla. Many people know such resources as livetv.ru and myp2p.eu , but for some reason they are not suitable for me. And now, having visited the NTV + website, I learned that from February of this year they began broadcasting sports broadcasts on the Internet . The service, of course, is not free (an average of 75 rubles for one match, payment via SMS), but the quality and price of the question suited me perfectly. As for the pitfalls: the forum has messages that some subscribers have problems with getting the code to start watching the broadcast. In connection with this, a special section has been created in which conflict situations that have arisen are resolved. Administrators advise you to pay in advance for what would be time to solve the problem. Information is also available on the minimum technical requirements applied to the equipment and it is possible to assess the quality of the picture that will be on your screen. To do this, on the page with the list of available broadcasts, click on the "Buy" button and select "test" in the window that appears. I will also add that broadcasts on the NTV + website may have geographical restrictions.
