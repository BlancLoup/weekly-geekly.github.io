We invite you to the IV conference on the practical application of data science DataScience Lab May 13



The FlyElephant team invites everyone on May 13 to Odessa to the IV conference on the practical application of data science Data Lab (exAI & BigDataLab).

Data Science Lab is an annual technical conference that brings together Data Science researchers, engineers, and developers to share experiences and discuss current topics in machine learning, natural language processing, pattern recognition, and other aspects of data analysis. The topics of the reports cover issues ranging from the practical implementation of research results to the latest theoretical developments.

Among the reports, the following can be noted:

  • From Terpile Eugene / Pavel Khudan, Data Scientists / NLP Engineer at YouScan.
  • Patient similarity: cleaning up duplicates and predicting missed diagnoses (Victor Sarapin, CEO at VITech)
  • Serving big data models with Apache Spark (Stepan Pushkarev, GM (Kazan) at Provectus / CTO at Hydrosphere.io).
  • Graphic probabilistic models for decision making in project management (Olga Tatarintseva, Data Scientist at Eleks).
  • BioVec: Word2Vec in the tasks of analysis of genomic data and bioinformatics (Dmitry Novitsky, Senior Researcher at the IAMMC NASU).
  • Optimizing ML Hyper-parameters with Bayesian Optimization (Maksym Bevza, Research Engineer at Grammarly).
  • Data Sciences and Big Data in Telecom (Alexander Saenko, Software Engineer at SoftServe / CISCO).
  • Generalized preferred connection (Alexander Krot, Head of Data Science at Sberbank).
  • Monitoring fashion trends with deep learning and TensorFlow (Olga Romanyuk, Data Scientist at Eleks).
  • How to know everything about customers (or almost everything)? (Darina Peremot, ML Engineer at SynergyOne).
  • Correction of geometric distortions of optical satellite images (Alexey Kravchenko, Senior Data Scientist at Zoral Labs).

Among the blitz reports, the following can be noted:

  • Enterprise out of the box (Sergey Shelpuk, Head of Data Science Office at Eleks).
  • Recent deep learning approaches for speech generation (Dmitry Belevtsov, Techlead at IBDI).
  • Distributed computing: using BOINC in Data Science (Vitali Koshura, Software Developer at Lohika).
  • The use of machine learning in the development of HR product (Mamed Khalilov, CEO & Founder at Morbax HR).

The full program of the conference, all details and registration at: www.datascience.in.ua .
