Google Calendar is now in Russian

Today, Google has released a Russian version of Google Calendar - a service designed to help optimize personal and work time. On the website of the online calendar, you can create a personal schedule and see the calendars of their loved ones. Who and what to show in a personal calendar is, of course, decided by each individual user.

Google Calendar "can" automatically remind you of meetings by e-mail or sms. The service is open source - built on open standards iCal and XML. It is compatible with Lotus Notes Microsoft Outlook.
