RubyConfUa 2010 Conference

The first full-length two-day conference of RubyConfUa 2010 will be held in Kiev, October 16-17. The main objective of the event is to create a comfortable platform for communication and exchange of experience among Ruby developers from Ukraine, Russia, Belarus and the CIS countries.

The event will include:

* two technical sessions;
* business section;
* demonstration of Ruby projects;
* workshops for beginners;
* interviews with special guests;
* A lot of useful and interesting communication.

His enthusiasm and sense of purpose contribute to the creation of new and the development of existing open source projects. He also maintains a company blog.

We invite Ruby developers to take an active part in the formation of the program. Applications for reports are accepted until September 1. The program of the event will be published on September 14.
