Spring Law - assessments on the other hand

About the Spring Law (also known as the Federal Law of June 6, 2017 No. 374- ‚ÄúOn Amendments to the Federal Law On Countering Terrorism‚Äù) many copies were broken. Estimates by experts in the field of costs of its implementation contained amounts in the billions and trillions. 2016 ended in arguments and expectations. In the new year, accurate estimates of the costs of implementing the law have already appeared. The truth is not from the providers.

First of all, we recall that a rationale is attached to each law during its development, indicating the costs of implementing the law. The corresponding rationale for the law 374- stated that it does not require budget funding. Already at that time, it provoked healthy skepticism, since any actions entrusted to any body require funding - in particular, due to the need for on-site activities, day-to-day monitoring and so on and so forth. What actually confirmed.

Due to the fact that Public Initiative No. 36F28432 on the abolition of the law, Springov gained the required number of votes, an expert opinion was prepared in accordance with the rules for reviewing public initiatives, under which various departments expressed themselves . Including Roskomnadzor.

Roskomnadzor is an organization that is often criticized. But it was she who sounded the cost figures for the implementation of the law. And it is nice.


At the beginning of his reply, Roskomnadzor indicates that, despite the existing requirements of the law, it does not have the authority to exercise appropriate control (the answer then contains a list of laws that need to be amended). That is, at the moment there are no necessary legal acts for ministries and departments, which should ensure the implementation of the law.

Further, the response indicates the number of telecom operators that are subject to the law and the relevant checks.


The annex to the answer specifies the number of data centers.


Then we will talk about how (most likely, since there are no specific requirements - the Government of the Russian Federation should develop them) it will be necessary to make an inspection.


That is, the equipment that telecom operators will have to install will have to implement not only information preservation functions, but also periodic control functions with transmission of information on compliance or non-compliance by telecom operators with the requirements of the law.

And at the end of the answer - the amount of funding that Roskomnadzor needs to carry out the intended checks.



A rather big number - and this is only one organization of those who must incur costs in the framework of meeting the requirements of 374-.
