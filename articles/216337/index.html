Ru-Center launched VDS

In Runet remained unnoticed, but on March 4, the largest domain registrar, Ru-Center, entered the hosting market with its offer on VDS.

As the technical support service said, the service is built on the latest edition of Hyper-V, as bonuses in the tariffs are free licenses for Windows Server of any edition and unlimited traffic + the ability to connect your distribution kit. The price tag starts from 500 rubles per month for a virtual machine with 512 MB of RAM and 1 CPU core (2 GHz) .

The tariffs themselves are hidden in the Hosting section of the hosting.nic.ru/vds website with a total of 4 pieces, you can get 4 GB Ram and 4 cores of 2 GHz each .
