The first 6 lectures of the online school Android developers

A little more than a month ago, we, together with Google, started an online school of Android developers, and today we want to take intermediate results.

This month:
- 18,000 people registered at the school
- we have covered the Russian-speaking Android community from Russia, the CIS, Europe, USA, Canada, Singapore and China
- 6 lectures were issued from JetBrains, Parallels, Kaspersky Lab, LinguaLeo and Tinkoff Bank (you can see them on the school website )
