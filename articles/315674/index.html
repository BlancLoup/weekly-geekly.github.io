Competition of projects in the field of advertising technologies Go to AdTech

The Untitled ventures Foundation announced a competitive selection of projects in the field of advertising technologies and new media - Go to AdTech . The winner will receive an investment offer of $ 100,000. The competition operator is GoTech fund.



Applications for participation are accepted until December 6th. 12 selected projects will be invited to the finals in a road show format on December 16, where they will perform in front of the leaders of leading advertising agencies and media platforms, as well as profile investors. All finalists will receive direct contact with potential customers, expert and PR-support in the advertising market. The winner of the competition will receive an investment offer of $ 100,000 from the Untitled ventures fund.

Projects from the seed stage are invited to participate, creating new technologies for advertising or increasing the effectiveness of existing ones:

  • big data and analytics;
  • user identification;
  • geofencing;
  • content personalization and native advertising;
  • brand safety;
  • viewability of advertising campaigns - solutions for analytics of a new level;
  • new media;
  • new advertising channels and models of user interaction.

Konstantin Sinyushin, managing partner of the Untitled ventures fund, talks about a new competition: ‚ÄúThe mission of the seed fund is to understand the trends of the corporate market and correctly transmit them to the startup audience. Therefore, together with the GoTech Foundation, we have taken the path of thematic competitions and events. Now advertising technologies are one of such trends. After selecting the Go to AdTech contest, participants can count on industry expertise, access to pilot projects with large advertising agencies, seed investments from the fund and help in finding investors for the next round. ‚Äù

Applications for participation are accepted until December 6, 2016.
