How did LinguaLeo find $ 3,000,000 venture capital investment?


After the long-awaited launch of the service from the Thai island, we returned to Moscow. At this point, we ran out of money, because the initial monthly revenue plan was not justified. And it doesn't matter, because we were about to receive investments for further development, but our expectations hit a wall of strategic misunderstanding even after a successful release.

I had to freeze the project for 6 months. All this time, LinguaLeo lived only on the enthusiasm of users. No development. Only accounting, support service and fix critical bugs.

But in the fall of 2010, the Perm Youth Innovation Convention was held in Perm. Ainura, the founder of LinguaLeo, was invited to tell the success story of the student project ‚ÄúClub of Native Speakers‚Äù. So offers to meet us from venture investors came down to us.
The first was Runa Capital : they met at their office in Tsvetnoy, they were pleasantly surprised by the strong team and nice talk. Conclusion - they are good ... But then we did not agree on conditions.

What does the LinguaLeo team dream of?


There are 4 or 5 well-known Russian companies in the world (created by Russian teams) that have become globally competitive abroad: Abbyy, Parallels, Esset, Kaspersky, Badoo.

We dream that LinguaLeo will be on a par with these projects and help people from all over the world learn not only English, Russian and other languages, but also understand that it is possible in Russia to create a competitive technology company whose product helps millions of people from different countries of the world. learn to understand each other!

Why is it so important that our dreams come true?


Because people who speak the same language with foreigners are more likely to get to know each other, fall in love, create families, and build a business. They are less likely to fight, do not understand or blame each other. They are more mobile, successful and developed.
