MonoTouch trial version available


A trial version of the iPhone development tool is available using Mono and C #. The MonoTouch license costs $ 399, but the released trial version is free, you can download it at monotouch.net/DownloadTrial . The limitation of the trial version is that the launch of the application will be available only in the emulator. But this is quite enough to assess how MonoTouch fits the needs of the developer.
