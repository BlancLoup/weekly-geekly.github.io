Announcement of the summer school "Algorithms for analyzing, modifying and comparing 3D polygonal models"

3D models, medicine and the future: the emergence of affordable digital stereo cameras and three-dimensional reconstruction algorithms for stereo images opens up new possibilities for applying 3D modeling in medicine.
School goal:
  • Research and implement algorithms of computational geometry for analyzing surfaces and reshaping 3D models
  • Web-based platform for visualization and storage of 3D models
  • High load calculation system for building a 3D body model

Instruments:
  • C ++, OpenCV, PCL, WxWidgets, Ogre3d
  • Javascript, HTML 5.0, Chromium, WebGL, Alternativa3d / Away3d
