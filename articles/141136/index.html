StartPSD - to help website designers, automate work

Today I decided to share with you my materials on the automation of work in photoshop. Namely, this is the starting template from which I start to make any site design.
Corrected, added, designed in the form of a pleasant product called - StartPSD.
You can download, edit, use on health!

Meet







Download StartPSD v1.0

StartPSD is a set of two files.
The first one is actually a layout with a browser, a grid for convenience, the second is a nice vertical canvas to demonstrate the design to the client, which also needs to be slightly edited for you (insert your logo, change contact details if desired). In the first file, I also use my copyright blank for a dark and light background, always at hand and do not need to play with the size every time, insert your logo from another file, it is convenient.

In general, someone will like it and will help only be happy, I myself work on this principle.

Leave your comments about the impressions, possible improvements, I will read with interest.
