Connect to the online broadcast of the international final of the Imagine Cup!

For the first time in history, the international final of the Imagine Cup competition is held in Russia, in St. Petersburg! About 800 of the most talented students from around the world will share their vision of the future and show their unique developments.



If you do not have the opportunity to attend the event in person, touch the international final online.
Connect to the online broadcast of the ceremony of awarding the participants of the international final today at 17.30 (Moscow time). The host of the event is Matt Smith!




Imagine Cup is the world's largest annual technology competition held with the support of Microsoft since 2003. The competition is open to graduate students and students from around the world, in a team or alone. The task of the participants is to use their knowledge and imagination to demonstrate how IT can help solve modern global problems.

Over the 11 years of its existence, the competition has raised a whole generation of creative and active young people. 1.65 million students from around the world have already participated in the Imagine Cup. This year the competition was attended by the largest number of young people in its history - about 500 thousand students, which is 70 percent more compared to 2012. In Russia, the Imagine Cup 2013 has collected more than 3,000 applications with project ideas. By their number, our country ranked second in the world, second only to China.
