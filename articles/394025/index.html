Xiaomi introduced Xiaomi Yi 4K Action Camera 2 that allows you to shoot FullHD video at 120 fps

On May 12, Xiaomi officially unveiled the second generation of action camera, the Xiaomi Yi 4K Action Camera 2. The device is equipped with a Sony IMX377 12 megapixel sensor with a pixel size of 1.55 microns, an aperture of f / 2.8 and a viewing angle of 155 ¬∞. The manufacturer entrusted the image processing to the dual-core processor Ambarella A9SE95, which has already demonstrated its performance and energy efficiency earlier.



Recall that the announcement of the predecessor took place in 2015 and did not go unnoticed: the first generation camera Yi Action Camera managed to win an audience due to its compact size, worthy of functionality and affordable price. Winner Yi 4K Action Camera will be able to shoot videos in 4K format with a frequency of 30k / s. 1080p shooting modes at 120 fps and 720p at 240 fps are also supported.



The battery capacity of 1400 mAh allows you to shoot video in 4K resolution at 30 fps for 110 minutes. The footage is broadcast on a screen with a diagonal of 2.19 inches in a resolution of 640x360 pixels.





Xiaomi Yi 4K Action Camera 2 is equipped with dual-band Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 4.0. It is worth noting that two weeks ago, on gizmochina.com, the results of comparative tests of the transfer rate of 500 MB of data over the Wi-Fi channel between Xiaomi Yi 4K Action Camera 2 and Xiaomi Mi smartphones and iPhone 6S Plus, as well as between the same GoPro Hero 4 Black Edition smartphones and camera. And according to these results, Xiaomi's Yi 4K Action Camera 2 handled the task twice as fast as GoPro.

The novelty will be available in white, black and pink versions. As for its cost, despite the attractiveness of the opening functionality, the price order of 4K Action Camera 2 at $ 300 is somewhat uncharacteristic for Xiaomi. The contrast is especially striking if we recall that the first generation came out at a price of 400 yuan (about $ 64 at the exchange rate at the time of the announcement). In the retail sale is expected in June.
