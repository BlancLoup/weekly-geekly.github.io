Announcement: Introduction to the YotaPhone SDK for Developers

Last time we announced the upcoming YotaPhone SDK testing. We inform that the event will take place in Moscow this coming Friday - August 16, from 10:00 to 19:00. We invite all interested Android developers to register here .


We have planned a fairly rich program: in addition to explaining the principles of working with the second screen, examples of implementing applications and demonstrating YotaPhone functions, we will discuss with all the participants possible ideas for services for such devices and give the opportunity to implement them on site.
Those who will cope with the proposed tasks better than others will deserve to be rewarded and, perhaps, we will offer to cooperate further.

Unfortunately, we can not accept everyone, as the number of places is limited. After successful registration you will receive an email with further actions. Registration is open until 14:00, August 15.

Please note that you will need a passport (to get into the building) and a laptop with the Android SDK installed (to participate in the hackathon).

Food, electricity and internet provide.

See you!
