Yandex and Exchange


Yandex is the largest Russian Internet company. In May 2011, the search engine conducted an IPO on the American NASDAQ exchange, during which shares were placed for $ 1.3 billion, and the entire company was valued at $ 8 billion. The cost of class A shares was $ 25 per share. The IPO was organized by such famous companies as Morgan Stanley, Deutsche Bank and Goldman Sachs. Currently, over 70% of Yandex shares are trading on NASDAQ (244.2 million shares have been issued for free circulation), and its current capitalization is about $ 10 billion. On Friday, May 30, the company's securities closed at the US stock exchange at $ 31.14 .
