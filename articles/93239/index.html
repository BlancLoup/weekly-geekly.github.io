June 5, 2010. IT-Conference in Chernivtsi, Ukraine


We invite you to take part in the work of the 1st International IT Conference Chernivtsi In Touch of specialists in the field of software development, which will be held on June 5, 2010, in the city of Chernivtsi, Ukraine.

Chernivtsi In Touch is:
  • communication of people involved in successful commercial IT projects
  • the way from the idea to the sale of the finished product
  • Agile development methodology discussion, with real practical examples
  • attention to QA as one of the most important stages of development
  • sales experience of commercial products from effective sales managers
  • examples of the interaction of software development and sales worlds
  • introduction to Crowd-sourcing - a modern way of software development
Format :
  • Thematic reports are held in parallel with the master classes - you can choose what is interesting and useful for you!
  • Coffee breaks - you can talk in a narrower circle with people interesting for you!
  • Closing the conference - participate in the raffle of valuable prizes!
Speakers come from Ukraine, Russia and Holland

Venue :
Ukraine, Chernivtsi, st. Kotsyubinskogo, 2,
Chernivtsi National University. Yuri Fedkovich.

Cost: 100 UAH
The participation fee includes the registration fee, 2 coffee breaks, a participant's package with handouts.
