3D in the item description

Good day, ladies and gentlemen,

To write this topic in "I am promoting" is somewhat wrong, because We are not looking for customers, but partners. Therefore, I chose the hubs "Web Development" and "IT Infrastructure." If the decision is not correct, then please inform the PM.

I want to present you one of the projects that should contribute to the penetration of 3D in Online. The idea is not new, and it has been tried many times to implement, so far, as I understand it, not successfully, because everywhere it is not used. The point is that in addition to photos of the product in the store, you can add a 3D model. Quite often, this model carries more information than a huge set of photos. This happens for the simple reason that the model is perceived as one, but the photos must also be combined, which is not given to everyone.

The model can be inserted anywhere (almost). For example, you bought a cool Lego robot for your son, posted photos from your birthday on Odnoklassniki, of course, there is a gift too. But you can also add a 3D model to the photos (see, what a cool gift!), Especially since it costs nothing and is done quickly. A couple of months later, the robot got tired of his son, it is a pity to throw him out, because He is in good condition and not cheap. A contract is signed with the son: the robot is sold, three weeks of ice cream are not bought (or maybe you need 5 to bring math?), Money from the sale + the above bonus and a new toy is bought. No sooner said than done. Old robot put up for auction. A 3D model is added to the description with photos. It will look like this (real lot on E-Bay). The model is free, buyers are satisfied.



So why did we invent the bicycle again? Well, because I like the idea itself and I cannot get used to the idea that nobody needs it. I analyzed other people's implementations and selected key points that, in my opinion, prevent this idea from winning a decent place in the market.

1. Difficult. Many implementations provide for the purchase of a certain number of files that you need to upload to your server and in some place you need to insert some code. The most annoying thing here is the upload to the server. For this you need someone who understands this all. Those. an ordinary manager cannot do this. Plus it is also the load on the server (the content is heavy). Plus, you need to make sure that it all works (access rights, etc.). Or maybe you also need to change something in the data bank? Not so impossible. Our thought is simpler. You go to us, choose a product, get a link (right there, without departing from the checkout), insert the link in the desired place as a regular photo. It's all. This method is perfect for electronic auctions and social networks. And what about those who have a store? Every time to climb to our site? Well, no, this is insanity.
