About building


Technopark has two sites: one is in Kazan, the other is in Naberezhnye Chelny. The total area is no less than 53,000 m2.
Here we create a whole ecosystem that opens wide access to opportunities: from a developed infrastructure to assistance in promoting IT products in Russia and abroad. And for any IT company, first of all, we undertake data protection.
In the IT park of Naberezhnye Chelny, the infrastructure differs only in the lack of a data center.

Today we start with the data center, in the future we will talk about other units. Data Center Infrastructure


"
Raised floor between racks

The data center consists of two machine rooms, two provider rooms, various data center communications, rooms for the elements of power supply systems, cooling, ventilation, communications, and fire fighting. In total, in two engine rooms, we have 311 rack places. At the time of this writing, 26 service providers place their hardware and communication nodes in two provider rooms. It is easy for customers to choose a suitable provider, connect to communication channels and to traffic exchange points.

Briefly tell about the principle of the data center. The cooling system of the data center is designed as follows: in the machine rooms there are precision air conditioners Emerson-Liebert Hiross HPM L-14C, M77UC, which are reserved according to the N + 1 scheme. Precision air conditioners cool the air, filter, regulate humidity and supply cooled air under the raised floor, then the cooled air goes to the racks. On the roof are Emerson-Liebert CBS073 chillers. The implementation scheme is simple, common and reliable.


In the photo - one of the precision air conditioners

The power supply of the data center comes from two carts from different sides of the building: from the nearest thermal power station and Zainsk TPP. There are two battery stations with uninterruptible power supplies Emerson Highpulse, two Cummins diesel generators, and various automations. The stock of fuel in the storage is enough to work for two days. Maintenance, inspection are carried out according to the rules: diesel engines regularly start, test.

For each rack we bring two power lines, and the load is distributed equally, and inside the racks there are distribution units (PDU) with monitoring that allow them to be monitored in real time.


One of the rooms of the UPS


Standby diesel generators

Gases are used as extinguishing agents, and the automation is set up in such a way that the extinguishing takes place in an automatic mode and allows extinguishing in separate rooms.

Security



The main reception Technopark. The first circuit of the armed guard

There are a lot of specific security requirements for data centers, there are many levels of implementation: two circuits of armed guards, territory, premises, machine rooms, rows, everything is under video surveillance. Access to the data center requires gaps, and to get into the computer room is also confirmation with a fingerprint. You can get to the data center on any day and at any time of the day.
In addition to physical security, there are various IT security tools, but that's another story.

Certificates that we have: the FSTEC license to protect confidential information, the FSB license to work with cryptography - all this is necessary for working with personal data, and compliance with the requirements of PCI-DSS allows us to place the infrastructure of banks and financial institutions.

Services




The main service in data centers is usually colocation (placing your server on our technical hub), we partially fall under this, because we also have TIERIII compliance , fault-tolerant infrastructure, competent staff, many communication channels, but most of all, we like cloud computing. Cloud technologies and virtualization are what we focus on. To do this, we have a virtualization cluster running vmware vsphere 5.5 using reliable hardware: IBM Pureflex server blade, Cisco 6509 network core, IBM Storewize v7000 storage system. In addition to hosting and virtualization, we also have other services: communication channels and telecommunications, connections and cross-connects, rental of software and hardware, and integration.
