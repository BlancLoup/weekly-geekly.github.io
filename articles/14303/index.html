I am online

Yandex Blog :
The other day we offered our users to test another tool for quick (or rather, instant) notification of new letters - the Ya.Online program. A small notification with the address of the sender and the subject of the letter appears on the screen at the users of the program as soon as the message arrives in the mailbox. At the same time, there is no need to keep the browser open - one click on the notification is enough to go to the new letter.


The program has only two functions:
  1. Notify about new mail
  2. Do not notify about new mail (busy mode)


The notifications themselves appear beautifully on the left above the Start:



Unfortunately, while there is only a version for Windows, which can be downloaded on the page: http://mail.yandex.ru/notifier
