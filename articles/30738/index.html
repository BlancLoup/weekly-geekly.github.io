New keyboard for Apple from Logitech

Logitech introduced the diNovo keyboard for Macs.

Wireless diNovo Edge Mac Edition is made of translucent plexiglass and aluminum. The thickness of the keyboard is just over 11 mm, and the weight is 940 grams. Stylish little thing. Yes, and with good features. Such as PerfectStroke - technology, by which Logitech has increased the key travel from standard 2.2 mm notebooks to a more comfortable 3.2 mm, several hot keys to launch applications and the TouchDisc navigation panel to scroll through documents.

To computer diNovo Edge Mac Edition connects via Bluetooth. The batteries are charged through the included base. In Logitech claim that diNovo 10-minute charge will provide the day of work. And charging for 4 hours will allow the keyboard to work for a month.

On the shelves of stores diNovo Edge Mac Edition appears in the current month. The novelty will cost $ 160.
