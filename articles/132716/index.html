10 changes in the issue format and algorithms of Google


Continuing a series of publications on the Google Inside Search blog, revealing the features of Google's search algorithms, as well as describing in detail all the changes made to them, the chief engineer of the corporation Matt Cutts listed the 10 most significant changes in the search algorithm that came into force in the last 2 weeks:
