Virtual desktop service overview



Hello, dear habr inhabitants! It so happened that I needed a remote desktop for 5 employees of my small project and since this project practically does not bring us money at the current stage, the choice of a virtual desktop service provider was extremely important in terms of an adequate price / quality ratio. I came across an interesting review of cloud providers, but the virtual desktop service was not available in the review, so I decided to conduct my research with a summary.


As a result of my searches, 10 companies responded to the request. Each company prices varied greatly, so it took to understand why so cheap or why so expensive. The results of my analysis, tasks, requirements and evaluation methodology, see under the cut.


+ bonus: during the selection of candidates, I discovered that 2 different providers have the same calculator and billing, which suggested that it is the same company ...


Task


For our company, it is necessary to organize access to a remote desktop (DaaS - Desktop as a Service, desktop as a service) via RDP for 5 users with monthly charges.


Requirements


  1. It is important that all users have admin rights, as it is required to periodically install their software.
  2. Company candidate must be located in the data centers of the Russian Federation. I did not want to bother with compliance with the requirements of the law on personal data FZ-242.
  3. SSD Drives
  4. Disk read / write speed of at least 500 MB / s
  5. Each user has at least 10GB of space, not counting the system.
  6. Windows server of any version.
  7. Office applications Word, Excel. We plan to use Office Libre or Open Office, but optionally considered MS Office.
  8. SLA 99.95 availability guarantee. I perfectly understood that such a guarantee would not save us from possible financial losses if the service was dropped, but still left this item for the sake of possible financial compensation from the provider in case of unavailability. This is better than nothing;)
  9. 24-hour free support. This item, I hope you can not comment.
  10. Backup. At the moment, I was interested in the opportunity and cost, since at the start in the first 1-2 months this is not critical, but in the future it will matter.
  11. Any virtualization.
  12. Cpu intel. This is because of the particular software that we use. It requires Intel, not AMD.
  13. Download / download speed of at least 300Mbps, respectively.
  14. Lack of brakes when working with a virtual desktop
  15. Price up to 1500 rubles / user
  16. Free test for at least 5 days

Testing Methodology


If the provider satisfies the requirements, then put a +1 point. If they do not meet the requirements, then put a negative score of -1. If information is not provided, points are not awarded.


Start


Appeals to the company were made from my legal entity. After I sent a request to the candidates, I wondered how quickly I could get an answer. As a result, not everyone answered me, despite calls and calls to online chat rooms. Some candidates do not provide a virtual desktop with a number of users less than 20. These indicators are not included in the assessment, but it is possible that the speed of response to a request may be important for someone.
