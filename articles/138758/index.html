Refrigerator guard

This device is useful to those who work at home and often in the breaks can not resist the temptation to visit the refrigerator for a portion of something tasty.

It is a magnet on the door of the refrigerator that has a timer, a display and a speaker. This is not a siren that screams every time you open the refrigerator - you also need to prepare breakfast, lunch, etc. His task is to remind you that you open the refrigerator too often and show how much time is left until the next store opening. with goodies. "



Every time you open the door of the refrigerator, the device counts a 30 minute interval in which you can open the refrigerator any number of times (for example, you make breakfast, lunch or dinner). After this period of time has elapsed, the device goes into alarm mode, and if you open the refrigerator before 4 hours later, a siren will be heard calling out to your conscience and reminding you of an approaching summer.


In such a simple way it will help you to get rid of the constant temptation to eat during the working day. Of course, it would be cooler to block the refrigerator and prevent it from being opened, but such a solution should already be integrated into the refrigerator itself during production. :)

This is a concept, but perhaps you know where you can buy something similar or have already seen similar solutions.
