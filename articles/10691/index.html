I am a genius, or how to learn a foreign language.

The son of my acquaintance, who had left for Italy for a vacation with his grandmother, arrived the other day, and came to visit my son. I immediately pounced on him with questions about how he was in a foreign country and whether he managed to master the language during the time spent there. I was terribly interested in his story in the light of my research into the methods, techniques, methods and secrets of studying and teaching foreign languages , since his method was completely unique!
I knew him from childhood, he is the same age and friend of my son, and I remember that while he lived here, at school he studied rather mediocre and was not distinguished by any particular diligence or discipline. What was my surprise when he said that he speaks Italian fluently and has absolutely no problems with communication in a foreign country, although he lived there for less than a year. And to the question of how he managed to learn a language so quickly and simply, he told an amazing story.
It turns out that no one taught him specifically and did not study individually, he was simply sent to study ... to a school for Italian mentally retarded peers! Those. This is a school in which for 10 years they have been giving an elementary school course - reading, writing, simple mathematics, chewing everything in detail and repeating and repeating it many times. And all these children were fluent in the language!
