New MacBook with NVIDIA graphics cards just around the corner

Another piece of rumors about a fresh line of MacBook laptops. Journalists from TUAW, citing some anonymous sources, say that Apple's new laptop computers will be equipped with graphic solutions from a well-known company NVIDIA. Allegedly, such laptops have already been demonstrated to employees of the company and the official announcement will take place on October 14th.

Of course, for now this is just a rumor, but not the first. Information about these products has long been circulating on the web. As they say, there is no smoke without fire. Apple continues to remain silent. The inclusion of NVIDIA graphics cards in computers may also mean that the company will seriously take on games that are still poorly represented on this platform.

In any case, we expect the announcement of fresh, more advanced and thin MacBook laptops before the end of this year.
