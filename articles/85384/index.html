Quake Live Beta celebrates birthday

Quake Live is a free online multiplayer first-person shooter designed as a browser plug-in.

Exactly one year ago, on February 24th, 2009, Quake Live opened for beta testing for everyone. On this wonderful day, let's look back on the first year of the launch of this great game.

The number of people registering for Quake Live Beta has exceeded all expectations. More than a quarter of a million players created accounts immediately after launching the game into an open beta test! Since then, players have spent more than 8.9 million hours in more than 60 million entertaining matches, earning an impressive 8 million awards and 810 million frags.

The id software company understands that not everyone has time to reach such heights in the open spaces of this defeat from the first person, so every player who completed an online match during two holiday weeks from February 23 to March 9 will receive a special holiday award.

Developing Quake Live, the developers have made many changes and additions to the game over the past year. In particular, the introduced function 'Duel Detective', which allows players to find an opponent in accordance with their skills. The developers also expanded the platform to include game support for Mac OS X and Linux users.

Also, the game added new modes - Pro Team Deathmatch and instagib CTF .

The introduction of these features has provided Quake Live with a rapidly growing share in eSports online and LAN tournaments.
For the first time, QuakeCon 2009 combined both professional and open competitions, becoming a platform for the professional championship and the open Quake Live tournament.

Over the past year, eight new maps have been added, including several of the most requested by the community. Id software understands that there are not many good cards, and promises to keep players happy with the inclusion of both nostalgic works from the community and special new cards designed specifically for Quake Live as they develop.

For example, the Fallout Bunker CTF card has already been added in today's holiday update.
In addition, the holiday update includes the post-processing effect of Bloom , as well as the New in-game functional menu, which contains presets that allow you to switch between the global configuration and individual saved settings.
