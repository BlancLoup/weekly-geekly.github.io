Bottomless "flash drive" from Infinitec



Almost unknown in our latitudes, the American company Infinitec announced a "bottomless flash drive." The essence of the gadget is that in fact it is a Wi-Fi module in the case of a flash drive. The gadget allows you to connect via Wi-Fi things that have a USB port and do not support wireless communication.

Let's say you need to transfer information from one computer to another. Insert the "flash drive" into the USB port of one machine, and the receiver into the port of another - and that's it, you can copy. It is also possible that the TV shows the video stored on your computer and the printer prints the documents - you just need to share the folders and so on. In general, nothing revolutionary - a pure increase in comfort when transferring information from one device to another.

The device will be sold from March 1. The price is still unknown.
