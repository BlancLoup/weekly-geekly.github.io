OnVystavke.ru - the first online exhibition on IT

In April, a new online project was launched on the Vyshvke.ru ( www.navystavke.ru ),

which opened on April 20 the first in Russia virtual exhibition on information technologies IT ONLINE SHOW 2009 .



At this site, well-known Russian and foreign IT companies presented their solutions and services on online stands, as well as in reports in a virtual conference room.

After registering and receiving a badge, as at a real exhibition, you can walk through the virtual stands of Kaspersky Lab , DELL 'a, ABBYY , NT Computer , Microsoft Hardware and other companies, chat with specialists on the stands, exchange virtual business cards, put into the package of interested materials. And then you can go to watch video reports in the conference room, where by the way, after viewing, you can ask questions to the speaker online.






Online exhibition will go 2 more days.
During the first days, more than 2000 business visitors from various regions of Russia - from Kaliningrad to Vladivostok - have already managed to register and wander around the virtual exhibition stands.
The organizers note the great interest from regional visitors, who cannot attend real exhibitions, especially in times of crisis.
By the way, every day within the framework of IT ONLINE SHOW there is a drawing of valuable prizes from participating companies. On Friday, under the curtain of the event, the fortune will smile at the lucky one who will become the owner of a powerful computer of the AgeNT series from one of the leading Russian computer manufacturers of the company NT Computer.



As for the real exhibition, no badge is allowed :)

The project on the exhibition.ru plans to hold various online events in the future. It can be both industry exhibitions and online events of individual companies. The resource is designed taking into account the goals and needs of visitors and participants of real exhibitions, so that a registered user, having visited an online exhibition, could really feel himself at the Exhibition.
