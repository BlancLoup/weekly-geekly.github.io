How to find someone else's balance? To do this, you only need to know the card number - 16 digits (sometimes less) - and the date of birth.

Knowing the card number (or part thereof, further details) and the date of birth of the owner, it is enough to call from any phone on the hot line of the bank and in the IVR menu select the item ‚Äúcheck card balance in automatic mode‚Äù.
