It became interesting to me and I decided to play Sherlock Holmes.

First of all, I began to study the TM and TM ID home page - I did not find any hitch or easter eggs (including in the source pages). However, the first snag was found "on the surface": I went to the Habr group on VK.com and from there I went into the profile of the public administrator - a certain group of GeekTimes was found in his group list, which apparently (1 subscriber) was created only recently.



I decided to search this name in different domain zones. It did not take long to search - the first mention was in the domain zone .RU, and the domain was registered on Private Person and more recently - on September 9 (apparently, it was on this day at the TM office that it smelled fried).
