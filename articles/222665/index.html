Ciklum .NET Saturday in Odessa on May 31! Registration is open

Great news for all .NET developers in Odessa on May 31st Ciklum will take place. NET Subbotnik !
We are still working on the final agency and the list of participants, but now we are ready to announce the following speakers and topics:



Dmitry Efimenko, Kharkov: Fusion of Technology and Management
Everyone knows that teams and products are born, grow, age and therefore require constant rejuvenation. Dima's idea arose from more than 5 years of experience in managing the team and product. It will cover the following questions:
- signs of maturity of the product and its team
- about value systems and relationships
- about the principles of building processes and specific solutions
- about typical problems and principles of their solution
- about the obsolescence of processes and practices and methods of rejuvenation
- about the erosion of the product architecture and the principles / practices to overcome it
- about the long-run and refactoring principles / practices
- on the difference between product and customized development
- about examples from life - funny and not so
Note: the details depend on the duration.
About Dima : manager, architect, developer, tester and practicing trainer. 17 years in software development for money and not only. 6 years in product development. Current directions: payments by bank cards via the Internet and in self-service networks.

Dmitry Mindra, Odessa: Strong Foundation for Good Code
We will look at how to apply the SOLID principles in practice. I have a game written by itself. We will use the SOLID principles and unit tests to make it a candy. At the same time, make sure that adhering to principles makes it easier to write good and clean code.
About Dima : 14 years in the software industry. Dima, an expert in software development, is currently working with world gaming industry leader Unity Technologies, having come from Microsoft Ukraine. Sometimes you can find Dima at the Odessa .NET User Group meetings while he is preparing for another trip to the next conference around the world!

Ian Druhalja, Odessa: Finding Multi-threaded Problems in NET Applications.
In his speech, Jan will cover various methods of detecting multithreaded problems and show how they are used in Unity.

About Yana: 12 years of experience in the development of commercial software. Currently - SDET at Unity Technologies.

When: Saturday, May 31
Time: 12:00 - 16:00
Where: Odessa, Lech Kaczynski, 7. 7th floor, office Ciklum

Traditionally, Ciklum Subbotniki is absolutely free! If you are going to participate in the event, please register goo.gl/dluf0A .

Event confirmation and detailed adgend will be sent closer to the event date!
