Jamendo - music server "free" music.



Jamendo
www.jamendo.com

Jamendo is a french-born internationally essentially music server that distributes music under the license of creative commons (some rights reserved). Simply put, you get absolutely legal music files with the consent of the author. From today, Jamendo began sharing 50% of advertising revenue with musicians.

At the moment, the server offers more than 2000 albums. Files are distributed via BitTorrent and eMule P2P networks. Format to choose mp3 ~ 200Kbps or Ogg Vorbis ~ 300Kbps. The download takes place in whole albums.

Naturally, there are communities on the site (unfortunately the Russian segment is very small). All music is conveniently broken down by tags and ratings (Albums of the month, Recommendations, etc.). Each album can listen, download and add to favorites.

An interesting feature is the ability to translate the interface by the members of the community the other day added Polish, Portuguese and Italian.

Most of all I liked the quality of the recommended music, choosing the tags of interest (for example, funk) most likely you will not regret downloading albums from the top.
