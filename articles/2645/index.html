Exotic options

Less common is a dim lamp near the projector. Turn off the lights, dim the windows, everything will be useless. Need to change the projector.

A simple solution. What does half the organizers not do?

The solution to these problems is striking in its simplicity and efficiency. It is necessary to check in advance the operation of the laptop and the projector. Run Power Point and make sure the presentation is displayed on the screen and the picture is in order.

An ounce of prevention is a pound of cure

Problems with software are also solved. First, determine the technical requirements for the presentations and get them to comply with the speakers.

Secondly, you need to get all the files in advance and make sure that they work on the computer from which the image will go to the screen. Presenters should know: if they do not provide the files before the start of the presentation, then you will not give them any guarantees.
