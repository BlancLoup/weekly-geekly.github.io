Skolkovo Robotics visit via Webot robots

Today, our robot remote presence Webot stands at the exhibition Skolkovo Robotics, which takes place in the Hypercube. We would like to provide everyone who wants to give the opportunity to try out our service of viewing events through robots.


You can control the robot on our website https://wicron.com/ru/queue , waiting for your turn. You will be allocated up to 10 minutes. The robot is controlled only from browsers Google Chrome and Opera Next. You will be able to ride on the first floor of the Hypercube. Control the robot, as in computer games - to drive ASWD, turn your head with the help of arrows. You can record a video from the robot, it will then come to your email.

We would be grateful for any of your feedback and comments. They will help the development of our project.
