BitLit offers free e-books for paper copy

The BItLit application for iOS and Android devices allows you to receive an electronic version of the book if you already own a paper copy. For this you need to take a photo of the cover with your name written on it.



Paper books are still loved, but they sometimes lack portability. And while hardly anyone wants to pay twice for the same book. BitLit application for Android and iOS is designed to fix this problem: the owner of a paper copy of the book can get a free electronic version.

To do this, you need to take a picture of the book cover in the application - BitLit will check if there is an electronic version and whether it will be worth the money. After that, you need to confirm that this is actually your book, using a regular pen - write your name on the cover. This step is necessary so that people do not begin to photograph books in shops and libraries. As soon as you confirm your possession of the book in this way, the application allows you to send an electronic version to Kindle, Kobo, Nook, or simply read a book in PDF.

A similar service already offers Amazon - Kindle Matchbook . The service allows you to buy books at a price of 99 cents to $ 2.99 after purchasing a physical version on Amazon.
