Job search on the map appeared

A map appeared on the site of Jobs.ru, which shows the places in Moscow where they give money. As they say in the instructions , on September 5, it showed 27,164 job ads and 11,942 offices. They promise that this number should grow. The principle of operation is elementary: you call the street / subway - they show you where there is work nearby. How long it takes to get to your future office, whether there are traffic jams, etc., on the way, the card does not speak yet. Map service - eAtlas company.
