1. How and when did the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating such a device come?

At one time, our team really liked the idea of ‚Äã‚Äã3Doodler. But having burned a couple of times with the hot nozzle of this 3D pen, we decided it was time to make our own safe version.
