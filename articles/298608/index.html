As the largest online cinema in the CIS, Megogo increased its annual revenue in Russia by 86%

Online cinema Megogo earned 419.4 million rubles in 2015 in Russia. This is 86% more revenue for 2014. The bulk of the revenue (in 2015 - about 89%) was provided by advertising.

In 2015, advertising revenue increased by 66%. In terms of the share of advertising revenue in the total revenue structure, Megogo is similar to the rest of online cinema runet.

However, Megogo earns not only on advertising, but also on paid services. Thus, the cinema implements the principle of a mixed monetization model.

Paid services brought the company 11% of revenue in 2015. In 2014, this share was only 2%. Revenues from a paid online movie theater subscription increased sevenfold.

In Megogo explained the growing interest in paid products of the service by increasing the volume of the library due to the large number of new products, including films of major Hollywood studios.
