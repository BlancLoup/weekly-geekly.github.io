Superbook, which turns the smartphone into an 11 "laptop, scored $ 1.5 million on Kickstarter



At the end of last month, a campaign was launched on Kickstarter to raise funds for the Superbook, a device that allows you to turn your smartphone into an 11 "laptop. Some details of the work of such a device have already been published on Geektimes. In particular, it is known that the Superbook is a kind of disc with a battery. Keyboard and high-quality display. To get started, you need to connect the smartphone to the Superbook, and the familiar Android interface will immediately appear on the display of the device.

The data is transmitted to the screen "laptop" with the help of a special application. The developers of Superbook do not offer any limitless possibilities, but the user of such a system can work with documents, play games and even program. And all this - on the device at the price of only $ 100. In this situation, a small quasi-notebook can quite replace the usual one for those users who need only e-mail, documents, undemanding toys. This project raised 3,299% of the required amount, $ 1.6 million instead of the planned $ 50,000. The collected amount will allow developers to add a number of buns to the so good system.



These buns are:
  • Additional USB port;
  • A more capacious battery, the operating time of the device in standalone mode will now be 10 hours;
  • The display has become FullHD (1080p);




The news about updating the display will appeal to those users who need the device not only for work, but also for watching movies or games. Play will become more comfortable. In principle, the Superbook will allow you to immediately edit photos and videos taken with the phone. It is very inconvenient to do this on a mobile device, but the Superbook makes this task easier, and very significantly.



Above it was mentioned that the developers believe that programmers will feel pretty good with such a device. In particular, it will be possible to use IDE for Android for work.



The developers themselves claim that their system allows you to forget about the need to upgrade your laptop. When you buy a new phone system capabilities are improved. This is similar to updating the laptop itself, only the form factor and the size of the system, which is already used, remains the same. Superbook is compatible with all modern smartphones, including models of such manufacturers as Samsung Galaxy / Note, LG, Nexus, HTC, Sony Xperia, Blu, Huawei.



And by the way, when you connect the phone, its battery will be charged, so that by the end of work the user will remain with a fully charged smartphone.

Specifications Superbook:
  • 11.6 "LCD display with FullHD;
  • 7.6 "x 11.3" x 0.7 "(19.3 cm x 28.7 cm x 1.8cm);
  • 0.9 kg;
  • USB Type-C port;
  • Supports Type-C and Micro-B devices;
  • 10+ hour battery life;
  • Recharge smartphone when connected;
  • QWERTY (Android OS layout)


Phone system requirements:
  • Android 5.0+;
  • Dual core processor;
  • 1.5 GB of RAM or more;
  • Type-C or Micro-B port;
  • 25 MB free memory (100 MB recommended)
