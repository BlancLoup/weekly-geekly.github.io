Neato XV-21 Robot Vacuum Cleaner: Purchase in the USA, Delivery, Experience of Use

Before the appearance of the child, the wife washed the floor almost every day. However, after the birth of time, she became sorely lacking, and clearly needed my help. The only adequate way out of the situation that has come to my IT head is to entrust this business to a machine, or rather to a robot vacuum cleaner.

Only two questions bothered me:
  • Which robot to choose?
  • Where to buy it?

In this article, I would like to share with you the experience of choosing, buying and impressions of using a robot vacuum cleaner.




Selection


After reading a few reviews and comparisons, here , here , here and many more places, I looked at two popular gadgets - Neato and Roomba. It is they who divide among themselves practically the entire western market of robotic vacuum cleaners. Roomba is a much more popular robot, but if you look at the real characteristics and reviews, I liked Neato more. Plus, for the rumba would have to overpay about $ 300.

The first thing you notice in Neato is that for its parameters and functions the robot is not at all expensive. In America, this vacuum cleaner can be bought for about 10 thousand wooden, well, or $ 320 with free shipping for US. At the same time, Neato removes no worse than its more expensive counterparts - and, unlike Roomba, it is equipped with a full-fledged navigation system and does not waste a lot of time on randomly moving around the apartment. Having a toy of some kind of intelligence is captivating - you want to pay for something really relevant, supporting the development of such technology.
The same important point that bribed me when reading the reviews is that Neato works as a full vacuum cleaner, i.e. he has a good traction, of course in this regard, and more noise from him.

Neato has several major advantages:
  • High cleaning speed
  • Fewer accidents
