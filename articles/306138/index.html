Registration for MBLTdev 16 is open - International Mobile Developers Conference



The date of the third international conference for mobile developers MBLTdev is scheduled. Registration is open!

The organizers of e-Legion and RAEC, as before, will gather Russian and foreign gurus of mobile development, from Australia to the USA. The program will be divided into several streams: hardcore presentations and seminars for mid + developers.

How was it last year


  • 650 participants
  • 40 speakers from Wunderlist, Runkeeper, Spotify, Tinkoff, Rambler & Co, Parallels, Microsoft and many other companies,
  • 2 streams of reports and seminars for iOS, Android and Windows developers,
  • business flow for owners and managers.

Video recordings of reports from MBLTdev 15 are available here .

Program


A set of speakers and filling tracks in full swing. Participate in the creation of the conference program - write in the comments, reports on what topics you would like to hear.

Follow the program updates and conference news on the official pages of VK.com , Facebook and Twitter .

Application for reports


If you have an interesting experience in mobile development, about which you want to talk publicly - send your applications to np@e-legion.com. In the letter, indicate the preliminary topic and key thesis statements.

Tickets


Until September 1, you can purchase an Early Bird ticket at the lowest price - 2500 rubles. Details on the official site .

See you at MBLTdev 16!

Conference organizers: e-Legion and RAEC
