Completion of the 11th All-Russian Conference of IT & Security Forum


On May 26, the 11th All-Russian IT & Security Forum Conference was completed in Kazan. The conference was held at the Korston Hotel. The conference gathered more than 900 participants from 80 cities of Russia. At the conference 60 reports were heard, a large number of thematic stands were presented in the demo zone.


The format of the conference has already become familiar. Zero day - May 24 - the day of arrival of the guests, followed by two days of fruitful work of the conference.
