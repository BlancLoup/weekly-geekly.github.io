Oculus launches the new $ 350 DevKit Oculus Rift

Last week, Oculus lost the opportunity to order the first generation DevKit. Many waited for a better replacement for this kit, and here it came.

The order of the new version of Oculus Rift, Development Kit 2 is open. On the official website you can order this set for $ 350, let me remind you that the previous version could be ordered for $ 300. Shipments for customers of the improved version are scheduled to begin in July 2014.

The new version has improved performance, updated design, and in general it is based on the prototype Crystal Cove, which was shown at the beginning of the year at CES.

The most important innovation of Development Kit 2 is to improve the screens, now the screen resolution for each eye is 960 √ó 1080 and they are made using OLED technology.

Also added an external depth camera, which allows you to more accurately scan the picture of the real world in front of the player and track movements. The case is changed, the new helmet is equipped with one output for a combined USB / HDMI cable, which at the end diverges in two, this solution reduces the number of cables extending from the helmet.

Engadget published a video demonstrating the work of the helmet.



If developers can try out the new product very soon, then ordinary users can only recommend to be patient and wait indefinitely when the desired new product goes on sale for mere mortals ... There is still no news about the product launch date.

I want to remind that a couple of days ago, Sony introduced its virtual reality helmet, it seems that in the near future virtual reality projects will gain momentum, which is good news, the technology has been around for more than 20 years, and it will only be possible to debug for the mass user soon.
