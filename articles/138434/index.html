Screenshots of the new Google Drive service have appeared on the Internet.

In the English-language blog GeekWire appeared screenshots of the new service Google Drive, which made one of the users.



Information that Google will soon launch a competitor Dropbox called Google Drive, appeared last week. According to user expectations, the novelty will allow you to store your files in the "cloud" and access them from various devices - stationary and mobile PCs as well as smartphones. The service has been spoken for over a year.

GeekWire does not specify exactly how one of the readers received these screenshots, but they show that Google Drive has a design similar to other Google products - Calendar and Docs.

The Install Google Drive link in the left menu may indicate that a special client has been developed for the service, which, like Dropbox, will upload files to Google servers and synchronize them on different PCs.

So far on drive.google.com and google.com/drive 404 answer, but different. We are waiting for the official launch. A Google spokesman, when asked about it, declined to comment, citing company policy regarding rumors and speculation.
