3DMark Vantage: New Total Record



The famous overclocker duck set a new world record in 3DMark Vantage . The record was broken at the event, which, apparently, was held by Galaxy. The Japanese finally managed to overcome the treasured mark of 75,000 points, which two months ago almost conquered the overclocker k | ngp | n, and score 75324 parrots. To achieve this impressive result, duck used four Galaxy GeForce GTX 580 graphics cards combined in 4-Way SLI and overclocked to 1170/4500 MHz (core / memory).


Detailed composition of the test stand:
  • Processor: 2 x Intel Xeon X5680 @ 5368 MHz;
  • motherboard: EVGA Classified SR-2;
  • graphics card: 4 x Galaxy GeForce GTX 580;
  • Memory: 6 x Corsair DDR3-2000 CL7;
  • Power supply: 3 x Corsair AX1200 + SilverStone SST-ST1200G;
  • storage: SSD Intel X25-M 80 GB;
  • OS: Windows 7

The overclocker used its own production of the Water Bear Pot WB02A , the F1 and Tek-9 glasses made by Kingpin Cooling, as well as several fans and insulation as a cooling system.
