Seminar-meeting "e-Estonia - the way to Europe!"

On April 24, 2012 in St. Petersburg a seminar-meeting ‚Äúe-Estonia - the way to Europe!‚Äù Will take place.



The purpose of the event:


Introduce Russian IT / Telecom companies with the opportunities and advantages of doing business in Estonia.

Who is this event for?



  • For company executives who would like to expand / organize a company outside of Russia
  • For young companies / start-ups that already have a finished product and the need to sell it on the European market
  • For representatives of educational institutions that are interested in partnership programs with universities in Tallinn and Tartu


Event format:


In the first part of the four-hour conference, you will have the opportunity to learn how the Estonian government managed to develop and implement an e-government system in the daily life of the country and what undeniable advantages this system provides for business development in this country.

Further, the participants will have a unique opportunity to get acquainted at once with all the largest techno / science parks in Tallinn and Tartu, as well as to get full information about the range of services they provide, about partner programs and about the benefits of working with them. Presentations will be made by representatives of technoparks and technopolises: Tallinn Science Park "Tehnopol" , Technolopis Ulemiste , Tartu Science Park

At the end of the presentations, a matchmaking section will take place, where it will be possible to communicate personally with representatives of technoparks, get more detailed information, and establish useful contacts.
