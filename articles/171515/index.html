A bit of background

Technology Forum has been held since 2010. In past years, the speakers of the event were Vladimir Gabrielyan (Vice President and Technical Director of Mail.Ru Group) , Igor Ermakov (Director of Instant Messaging) , Boris Wolfson (CTO HeadHunter) . Vyacheslav Egorov (SE Google) , Sergi Mansilla (Lead Developer, Ajax.org) and other IT experts, including those from Cloudera, Puppet Labs, Codecentric, Opera Software, etc., participated as invited speakers.

Topics for presentations at Technology Forums cover various aspects of development and administration, from development and implementation practices to process management. For example, at the fourth, autumn TechForum, we talked about how machine learning works in practice, how to live, if you manage a large development team, and how the VoIP engine works, providing audio and video communication inside Mail.Ru Agent. You can read these and other reports in the archive of the forum, there are many interesting things there.
