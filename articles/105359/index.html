Open registration ". RF" - November 11, 2010

September 30, 2010 A meeting of the Council of the Coordination Center was held, at which the issue of the start of an open period on domain registration in the RF zone was decided. As a result of the meeting, it was decided to start open registration of the Krill domains at noon on November 11, 2010, but with some restrictions ...

At the meeting, various options for open registration were considered - in particular, a price increase for the registration period or the limitation of the number of domains "in one hand".

According to the voting results, the option was chosen whereby registration will be available only to citizens of the Russian Federation and legal entities registered in the Russian Federation.

Also during the year there will be a restriction under which the ban on changing the domain administrator will be prohibited.

At the Council of the Coordination Center, it was decided to create a stop list with a list of domains that can not be registered. This list will include domains that contain non-regulatory vocabulary in their names or domain names that are in court proceedings.

And finally, the most pressing issue of domain prices - domain registrars will set prices themselves, there are no restrictions on the minimum price.
