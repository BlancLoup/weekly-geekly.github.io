In Ukraine, the most popular browser is Firefox 3.x


According to the two global statistics systems Ranking.com.ua and LiveInternet in Ukraine, Firefox 3.x has become the most popular browser. The second in polarity is the ex-leader of the last 12 months, Opera 9.x.

The share of Firefox 3.x has reached 23.5-24% and continues to grow. The latest version of Firefox 3.5 includes the TraceMonkey engine for JavaScript, due to which it is more than twice as fast as Firefox 3 and ten times as fast as Firefox 2. *. In addition, such innovations and improvements have appeared: private browsing mode, smart address bar, restoring a session, and more ( http://www.mozilla-europe.org/ru/firefox/features/ ). Opera 9.x began to lose its audience due to the release of a new version of Opera 10.x. Despite this, the share of all browsers Opera has increased over the year to 32.7-33.4% and practically caught up with Internet Explorer from 37.3-37.6%.
