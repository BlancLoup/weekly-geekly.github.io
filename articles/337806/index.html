Selection: 10 useful tools for an internet marketer



In an online marketer's job, it is important to spend time efficiently and stay collected. In the new material I collected ten convenient tools that will help generate ideas, write high-quality texts, create images on their own, track brand references, improve advertising and conduct research.

DashaMail




The service for creating and sending newsletters allows you to create beautiful letters using templates in the online editor and deliver them based on local time subscribers. The tool gives analytics on discoveries, clicks, returns, unsubscribing and geography, it can be integrated with Google.Analytics and Yandex.Metrica.

DashaMail is suitable for those companies that have just started to engage in email marketing and have not yet gathered their base - the service allows you to dial subscribers using special forms.

Glavred




The tool helps to clear the text from language garbage, make it convincing and easy to read. All you need is to add text to the field and evaluate the result. The service will highlight controversial words and expressions and give advice on how to replace them.

Suitable for those who want to write convincing texts about the company and the product, but travel stories or restaurant reviews can make it dry and uninteresting.

Orthogram




Service to test text literacy. It helps to calculate spelling and punctuation errors, as well as to improve the style. For example, it offers synonyms and epithets for words.

Similar Web




The resource allows you to check any site and compare it with competitors, find out the sources of traffic, follow the trends and new players in the industry, understand consumer behavior and even find leads and partners.

It is useful for developing marketing strategies, finding a new audience and tracking your position in the market.

Notify




The service keeps track of references to the brand, competitors, events and topics on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Google+, Medium, blogs, news and forums. Convenient to quickly find and work out the negative, answer questions in social networks, etc. You can configure the service so that notifications come to Slack.

Canva




With Canva, you can quickly and easily create pictures for social networks, presentations, posters and graphics for your blog. The service has ready layouts for all these types of images or the ability to collect your own picture from templates. All images are standard sizes. With Canva, you can work with a smartphone.

Amplifr




Service for postponing and analytics in social networks. The interface and usability is similar to the well-known Buffer, but unlike the latter, it allows you to work with Russian social networks - VKontakte and Odnoklassniki, as well as Instagram and Telegram. The service determines the best time for posting, shows analytics for each of them and forms a convenient, understandable report.

K50




The tool is designed to increase the effectiveness of contextual advertising within the specified KPI. It integrates with Yandex.Direct, Yandex.Metrica, Google Analytics, Google AdWords, Competera, CRM and call tracking services. The service collects statistics from these sources and generates a report with data on transactions, expenses, income and other important indicators. It also analyzes online and offline applications, helps to optimize the advertising budget and predicts conversions.

Google Consumer Survey




It helps the marketer to conduct a quantitative study with a minimum of costs. Suitable for those who are familiar with the research methodology and are able to ask the right questions.

The service adds questions to banners, shows them to people who are ready to respond online. Instead, they get access to high-quality content or a Google Play credit, and marketers - reliable results at a low price. At the same time, the audience can be segmented, choose whom to show banners, and receive a representative sample in just 48 hours.

User Testing




Suitable for those who launch a new site or application, or improve the old one. Allows you to conduct usability testing service without a laboratory. You can give a task to users, and they will perform it (the system at this time records actions) and comment on the process.
