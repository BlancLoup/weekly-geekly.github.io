Russian cellular operators stop selling BlackBerry to corporate clients

Beeline from March 2, will not connect subscribers to the BlackBerry Enterprise Service, and the monthly cost of services for existing customers will grow from 1,000 to 1,900 rubles. MTS stopped connecting corporate customers after BlackBerry stopped selling Enterprise Server v5. Megaphone does not plan to develop relations with a Canadian company, conditions for current customers will not change yet.

The suspension of corporate clients' connection by mobile operators is associated with a possible cessation of BlackBerry business development in Russia, as well as an increase in the exchange rate.

Official sales of BlackBerry in Russia began in 2007, after the FSB agreed on the implementation scheme and its compliance with legal requirements. The Canadian manufacturer MTS and VimpelCom began selling devices in 2008, Megafon - in 2011.
