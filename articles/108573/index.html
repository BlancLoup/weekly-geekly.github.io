How to buy via twitter

So, first in Runet, we implemented the selection of goods through Twitter on the tag #wishbuy. If you are interested in how we did it technically - ask, we will try to answer.

So, we write tweet:
I want a new #iPhone as a present by 11/30/2010 , Moscow #wishbuy
In response, replies from stores selling iPhone will come - and your friends will not only know what to give you but also where to buy it on favorable terms. Please note, here in the tweet shows the date until which the desire is relevant, and the region of purchase. After this date, the wish will be removed from the database and the responses will stop coming.

Here is an example of a response from the seller (to inquiries from prompt guys):
@% username% Response to your request: sunflower seeds Tel: +7 (495) 646-8595 bit.ly/c0XxPI Price 30 rub. #wishbuy


Another variant:
I want to buy a Husqvarna chainsaw - within a week, Moscow #wishbuy .

There will be up to 10 offers from stores (phone, link, price). You will have plenty to choose from;)

If you do not want your friends (children, wife, bosses) to know what exactly you are planning to buy, then write your request in a personal message (Direct Message) to twitter user @wishbuy . No one except you and the shops will see this request, the response offers will also come in the form of personal messages. By the way, regardless of how you publish a request, stores get access only to an impersonal wish base, they don‚Äôt get your username or other details about you.
