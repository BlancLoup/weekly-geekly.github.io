Interactive screens in elevators

5 years lived in a city with a large number of skyscrapers and elevators. The buttons in the elevators are despondent.

The office district of a modern city consists of dozens of lobbies with elevators, many times more than the elevator booths themselves and hundreds of hours, totally spent in waiting for the elevator and in the cab itself. All this is accompanied by buttons, occasionally small screens with looped ads, in some cases - digital screens showing the number of the floor.

What is there to do? We put a large touch monitor with a beautiful interface, big buttons. Outside the elevator in the lobby and inside. We connect the monitor and its control PC with a classical control system.



In addition to the elevator call interface, we also get a beautiful display of floors with animation, an advertising and information space, an audio-video notification and monitoring system, an integrated access control system, a mini-directory of offices.
