Announced service Steam Music (beta)

Very interesting news appeared a few days ago from Valve.
Then my free translation of their official announcement .

In the near future, Steam Music Beta will appear in the Big Picture and SteamOS mode, followed by the announcement for the desktop client. To enroll in beta testers, just join the group Steam Music . Members of this group will be invited to test in several visits until the feature is available to all.









Steam Music allows you to listen to your music collection while playing. After you indicate to the client the path to the local music directory, your Steam library will include assembling albums and artists of your collection.







From this point on, you can view and listen to albums and tracks, manage the playback queue and open the Steam Music player using the Guide button on your controller (if you don‚Äôt have a controller, use the keyboard and mouse through the Big Picture main menu).

In the game, the player will be accessed via the Steam overlay, from where you can control the playlist, browse your collection and listen to music by mood.
