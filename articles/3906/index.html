SiSoftware Sandra XI SP1 (11.22)

One of the first of its kind programs, which clearly and clearly told users what their computer consists of, and also allowed to carry out simple tests of the processor and RAM to compare computers with each other. Without a reference in the form of SiSoftware Sandra , other diagnostic utilities like AIDA32 / Everest , HWiNFO32 , ASTRA32 and others would not have appeared.

Time passes, and the development of Sandra does not stop. Version 11.22 has just appeared with full support for Windows Vista and the ability to recognize the most advanced computer components. Download SiSoftware Sandra XI SP1 (11.22):
- san1122a.zip , mirror (12.8Mb)

Only the basic version (Lite) is available for free download, in which, however, there are almost all the necessary features. Details about other versions are painted on a special page .
