Mongo Conference in Moscow - May 16

Good day!



I would like to tell you about the upcoming Mongo DB conference at Cifriab . Since the official announcements are unlikely to interest anyone, I will write a few things that are not on the office, but which may be of interest to the public.

Where did that come from

It seemed to me that there were too many web conferences in general at Cifriab, that is, on aspects related to management, advertising and all that, so I decided to make several technology conferences. MongoDB Moscow - the first of them; I hope that it is far from the last, and they will all be kept in the same format:
  1. First-hand: inviting technology developers. On May 16, the main part of the program will be presented by Richard Kreuter and Matius Stern, the developers of Mongo from 10gen.
  2. For those who are aware: do not let reports like "why you should use Mongo". Why - it should be clear from the report itself.
  3. Less powerpoint, more console: practical reports, based on the fact that the speaker does something with his product right on the stage: on a demo stand or in some other way.
To be honest, I'm not sure that this conference will ideally follow all the listed principles. At least, I can say that I explained them to Adam Kroll from 10gen, who understood and promised to convey to the speakers.
