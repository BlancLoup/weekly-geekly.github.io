The Alcatel Plus 10 transforming tablet received a 10-inch IPS-screen with a resolution of 1280x800 pixels and a quad-core Intel Atom x5 Z8350 processor. The built-in memory in the device is 32GB, there is a slot for a microSD card. Included with the tablet is the keyboard. The two devices in conjunction capacity of the battery is 8410 mAh, this should be enough for 8 hours. The keyboard allows you to connect to the network via 4G LTE and can serve as a Wi-Fi access point for 15 users.

From the ports in the new tablet will be Micro-USB. normal USB and Micro HDMI.

Alcatel Plus 10 runs on Windows 10. The gadget with the keyboard weighs about 850 grams.



Huawei unveiled its Microsoft Surface Vision - MateBook. The first transformer on Windows 10 from this Chinese company received a 12-inch screen.

The device weighs only 635 grams - this is one of the lightest tablets with such a diagonal. Thickness - 6.9 mm. The screen resolution is 2160x1440. For comparison: Surface Pro has a resolution of 2736x1824, and iPad Pro - 2732x2048. The manufacturer promises that the battery will last for 10 hours.

The gadget is based on Intel Core M processors - there will be options with M3, M5 and M7 with a clock frequency of up to 3.1 GHz. Buyers will be able to choose versions with 4GB or 8GB of RAM, SSD will be from 128GB to 512GB of capacity.
