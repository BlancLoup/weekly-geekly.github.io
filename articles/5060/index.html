4 aspects of building a freelance team

What processes in the field of web development are easier to outsource?


Vyacheslav Terichev ( polosaty ):
In my case, almost everything is outsourced: layout, programming,
design, content. Our studio is virtual - without office and staff. It is better
all, in my opinion, to give layout, design. After all, the work of a designer or coder
is episodic (for example, the creation of a common concept design site
and technical design). To have a good designer on staff,
I need to give away $ 2500 per month (excluding taxes). Same designer
I can get for $ 400-600 as a freelancer. With the coder is even easier -
it has a clear scope of work, minimal documentation at the start.

What mistakes in the formation of freelance teams did you make?

Vyacheslav Terichev ( polosaty ):


  • Lack of a backup option. Working with freelancers,
    even with the most reliable ones (for example, I have a programmer with whom I work
    7 years) you must necessarily have an alternative specialist. To be
    ready for a situation where the performer simply disappears. No matter what happens, it will fall.
    Internet provider, fall in love, go into the lead, sick ... For each member
    teams you must have a safety option.
  • Lack of planning. Need to make a schedule
    and keep up to date with all project participants. Remember that man, for sure
    works not only for you.
  • Lack of full project documentation. Here
    everything is simple: to get exactly what you want, you need as clear as possible
    to describe.
  • Need to negotiate on the shore. Disclaim all conditions
    cooperation and the amount of work needed at the start, so that later there would be no mutual
    insults. If you can not accurately determine the amount of work, it is better to negotiate this
    at the start.
  • Require reporting. It's simple: did - write it down.
    Better initially stipulate the form of communication. For example, I prefer
    task setting and reporting by mail. In ICQ - the only solution
    operational issues.
