Testing

So, the test itself. To grow a beard for a long time, and somehow there is no desire to be painted ... so I checked not all, but some of the promised things that are not confused by the program. The program has two modes of recognition: High Convenience and High Security, respectively: it recognizes better or more security. Below is a table describing (or showing) tests and results.

The results are quite good: in the priority recognition mode, neither glasses nor a bandana became an obstacle for identification. The security priority mode was safe and missed only the face without distortion. But with the light things are bad, with a lack of light it was not possible to pass the test, even after 5 minutes of heading in front of the webcam. Moreover, the program in principle can not find a person, perhaps you need a more expensive webcam, rather than a built-in laptop.

Now, the most interesting, we realized that the program copes with its task as a whole, but will it cope with the attempt of forgery? How to fake a face? The most obvious way is photography! Here there is such a thing, the worse the webcam, the larger the photo you need, because the small one has to be brought very close, and there it is already out of focus. I took a photo from the table, without glasses and other things, brought it to the TV and brought a laptop to it ... voila ... login occurred! And in security priority mode too !!! Those. the program failed to distinguish a living person's face from a photo. Negative, crossing out everything, because the program promised some security, and so ... it only protects from those who cannot get your photo ... The program did not meet expectations and does not fulfill its task. So its only purpose is to have a little fun and surprise your friends with an unusual way of launching the laptop ... a pity, the idea was good.
