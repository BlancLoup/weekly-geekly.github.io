Deuce for quoting wikipedia

The founder of Wikipedia appealed to students not to use the free encyclopedia in term papers and dissertations, as well as for other serious scientific research as a source of academic knowledge.

Wikipedia - an inexhaustible source of information on almost any topic. Written in clear language, it is deservedly popular as one of the most convenient universal sources of information.
