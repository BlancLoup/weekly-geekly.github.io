Lumia 920 in Minecraft

Friday post.

If you have not seen this video, you should definitely watch.

The user Gokuroyu (aka Darek) created in the game Minecraft a model of our new Lumia 920 .

It took him more than 370,000 blocks and 14 days.


Here is a slideshow about how this vitrual masterpiece was created.

The picture is clickable.

Darek says that the song 'Creep You' feat. Nina Sky (Nokia 'Sped Up Mix' from Nokia Lumia 920 video ).

Darek made its construction not only similar in appearance to the Lumia 920, but also worked on the key features of the smartphone from the inside. Moving around the model, you can see the cameras - main and front, internal memory, RAM module, Snapdragon S4 Dual Core 1.5 Ghz processor. In addition, we can look at the Nokia Maps and Nokia Music applications. On the live screen there are tiles SkyDrive, Nokia Drive, City Lens and other applications.

At the moment, the player improves the phone he has created by adding Marketplace, IE10 and messages to it. When asked about the plans, he said that he wanted to add a wireless charger to the already existing work, as he was asked about in comments on Youtube.
