Chrome Extension "Habra-Karma"

I have long wanted a convenient way to add a plus to karma to a person, without looking up from reading the rest of the comments. So I wrote a small "extension" for Google Chrome, which will try to solve this problem.

After installation, immediately go to any topic that interests you and watch what happens.
And this is what should happen:

An arrow will appear next to the comment author's name (yes, the same as the link to the author of the post). We press it, and it turns into the current indicator (again, as elsewhere in Habr√©).

In order to lower the karma to a person, you need to hold down the Ctrl key and just click on the arrow (it will be shown upside down).
