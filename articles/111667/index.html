Results of the December HTML5 Games Contest

Every month we have a contest for HTML5 games ( html5contest.com ), and it's time to announce the winners of the past month.



It is very nice to see how quickly the quality of games improves. Back in September, we saw spruce-playable prototypes, and now we have full-fledged games.

Let me remind you that the essence of the competition is to make a game in HTML5 that would work without problems both on the desktop, and on the android and on iOS. Actually, this is our strategy of mobile games in Spiel Geyms - universal access to games through any mobile device.

And here are the winners:
(under the cut)
(recommended to play on the mobile device)

1. Blast Effect



Blast Effect on the concept and performance competes with the first games for the iPhone. And for HTML5 games this is a great achievement. A completely simple toy where you have to blow up the little people on the screen.

2. Second Lazors



Logical puzzle, the task in which is to arrange the nodes so that the lines do not intersect. It has the charm of classic puzzles, in the sense that the first levels begin very easily, and then the puzzles come.

3. Spy Chase



The race for HTML5, in which you need to catch spies.

As you can see, if you compare it even with the November winners , the quality of the games has greatly improved.
