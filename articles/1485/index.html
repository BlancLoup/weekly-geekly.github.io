"Yandex.News" show maps

The plots of Yandex.News , in which geographical addresses associated with the events described are mentioned, are now illustrated with relevant fragments of a city map. The search for addresses in the news and the selection of the necessary map fragment for illustration are based on the fact extraction technology developed in Yandex .

Addresses are usually mentioned in regional news: this could be, for example, the construction of a new house, a street concert or an accident. You can see how the binding of the event to the map works in the news of Moscow, Yekaterinburg, Kiev and other regions. In total, 10 cities are being processed.
