Video from the ISS in HD quality in real time: High Definition Earth Viewing



I do not know why, but the news that the ISS began broadcasting in HD quality somehow passed by Habr. But this is a new experiment, which was launched on April 30 of this year.

The project has installed several very high-quality HD video cameras aimed at the Earth. The cameras, as far as can be understood, are located outside the ISS, on the casing, but enclosed in a case with controlled pressure and temperature.

The view is constantly changing, the broadcast is conducted from different cameras in turn.
