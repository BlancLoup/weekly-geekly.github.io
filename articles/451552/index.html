We build the network channels of sales of the gadget DO-RA



In early March 2019, Intersoft Eurasia team completed work on a test batch of DO-RA gadgets - personal, cross-platform dosimeters-radiometers to monitor the radiation situation at the measurement site, compatible with smartphones and tablets for iOS and Android.

What does the user get by purchasing such a device: reliable electronics checked under radiation load in the plant's laboratory, a stylish color case in the spirit of Malevich;) for every taste, gift packaging, color insert instructions in Russian and English, a special cable for recharging the gadget through USB connector, a free updated DO-RA.Pro application from the App Store and Google Play.

The next step in the implementation of our project is to find the best sales channels for Made in Russia products in the challenging environment of stagnant purchasing power.

Domestic network sales channels


In practice, the choice of promotion of DO-RA line devices was limited by a number of factors peculiar to our investment opportunities, combined with network retail features and above all: the price of network entry, the possibility of consulting support in selling goods to the consumer, the size of the audience coverage and network commission fees, return periods money for the goods sold, advertising budget.

In the Russian Internet space, the main e-commerce market players operating non-stop were chosen: OZON, GADGET, CSN Retail and others, where the first mini batches of our goods were sent for review.

Then, representatives of the DO-RA product distributor started dialogue on product promotion in networks of cellular retailers, and in particular, negotiations with the Svyaznoy company and its network of offline stores, in aggregate consisting of more than 5,700 salons (monobrand + franchise). Svyaznoy agreed to take the first hundred DO-RA products for a sample for its C-Store.ru network.

Also, negotiations were held on the promotion and sale of DO-RA products in networks owned by other large companies, such as: Megaphone - 5350 salons (monobrand + franchise + 50% of Euroset salons), Beeline - 5000 salons (monobrand + franchise) and Tele- 2, which at the end of 2018, in aggregate, there were about 15.000 stores of communication. Negotiations began with specialized electronics stores: M-Video, Eldorado and others.

It is important to know that in addition to the arrangements for the sale of devices with the retailer, there are a number of features for the "entry" into their network, which should be considered. For example, the commission of retail chains, which can vary in the range: from 15% to 30% of the value of goods in retail, that is, on the virtual shelf of a store. By the way, virtual world networks work on the upper border of the commission: Google Play and the Apple Store, taking a fixed 30% from each purchase of users in their online markets. At the same time, the initial contributions of owners of goods on Google Play - $ 29 for registering an account, a contribution to the Apple Store $ 99 per year.
However, there are champions of commission - some Russian retailers who ask for up to 40-50% of retail, which is the edge of the profitability of sales of any product. We immediately refused such offers.

In addition, we must not forget about the refund period for the goods sold, which, as standard, in Russian retail, is from 3 to 6 months. At the same time, for some retailers, a systematic delay in the return of money can reach from 1 to 2 months in addition to the refund period specified in the supply agreement.

Foreign retailers declare a shorter return period for the goods sold. In particular, AliExpress promises 1 month for a refund for the goods sold, which also includes 14 days for possible claims or the return of defective goods.
Actually, all of the above factors determine the final retail price of a real product on the virtual shelves of Internet retail.
