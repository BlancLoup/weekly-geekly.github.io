Sennheiser HD 238 Precision Headphones Overview


I offer a small overview of the middle class headphones from one of the world leaders in the production of high-quality audio equipment, the company Sennheiser GmbH & Co.

HD 238 Precision is mainly optimized for use with portable playback devices (iPhone, iPod). An advanced transducer with highly protruding neodymium magnets ensures intelligible and accurate sound reproduction. Headphones open design strict classic design with metal elements in the style of Hi-Tech.

Description


Headphones are made of plastic, but the quality of the design is excellent. Taking them into the hands does not create the impression of fragility, they are fairly light, but when they have enough weight, that would not create an obsessive impression of the need for careful use. The headband has a small deformation radius (bend), while the ear cups fit snugly to the ears and do not create any discomfort. Headphone adjustment will satisfy any head size with a retractable classic headband that will provide the perfect fit. Fastening ear cups have several vertical and horizontal non-fixed positions that allow you to arbitrarily fit under the ears.



On the outer edge of the cup have a soft velvet finish, with the inside of vinyl. Despite being similar to the style of the 80's ear cups, they are very comfortable. Even after prolonged wear, no fatigue or discomfort was noticed. The headband has a small insert from a fabric lining with a pad that distributes the force evenly. Headphones have a cable of increased strength and a length of 1.4 meters. Also included is a case for storing and carrying headphones. Since the cups have a turn of 90 degrees, finding headphones on your neck also does not create any inconvenience during periods when you are not listening to music.

Since these are open headphones, there is a slight sound leakage. and listening to music at full volume, you can create some discomfort to others, and if you remove the headphones from your head, and also turn on the music at full volume, then this volume is enough to listen to music in a room or office without any effort .

Sennheiser HD 238 Precision offers good sound with significant sound depth and level of detail. In general, the sound is equal and clear. Low and medium frequencies are quite detailed, but when listening to music as 128-256 kb / s, the sound becomes drowned and not expressive enough. Moderately perfect and clear sound in headphones is achieved while listening at 320 kb / s. At 100% volume, the headphones do not hiss and do not distort the sound, but only slightly pleasantly vibrate on the bass. When listening to music at 100% loudness in the first minutes, the music seems to be rather loud, but over time, the eardrums get used to this level of sound, and the sensations from listening to your favorite music achieve complete satisfaction.
