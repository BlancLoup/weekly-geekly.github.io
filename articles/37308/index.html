Concept phone with two touch displays

Most recently, I talked about a new concept from James Piatta, which caused controversy about its appearance and convenience. But now, a few days later, designer Andy Kurowitz drew his concept of the same form factor. Phone with two screens.

Designer Andy Kurowitz believes that a single display is not enough for a mobile phone. In principle, phones with two displays already exist, so this is not a focal point, but the designer offers a somewhat unexpected design: the second one, equivalent to the first one in size and capabilities of the diples, will be extended from the side, i.e. This design can be called a side slider. At the same time, the phone itself has a slightly curved shape, so that the displays will not be quite even. It is also interesting that his camera will also be retractable and judging by the photo, the designer suggests making it 7 megapixel.







Something really stirs the minds of designers this form factor that does not exist in hardware ...
