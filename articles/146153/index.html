CX-01 - another mini PC



Usually, after a product or service becomes quite popular, analogues appear immediately. Modern companies are trying to keep up with each other, and produce analogues of successful gadgets, one after another. As an example, and tablets, and netbooks, and much more. This time, the concept of a mini-computer is gaining popularity, the excellent implementation of which is the Raspberry Pi. Now both large and small manufacturers are starting to produce similar mini-computers.

For example, not so long ago on Habr√© news about a mini-computer from VIA Technologies was published. Now a less well-known company has released the CX-01, a mini PC on the Android OS. This mini-PC allows you to connect the device to the TV, and work with Android applications on a large display. An HDMI adapter is provided for this, located on the other end of the device. There is a USB port for mouse and keyboard. The developers have provided and mini-USB, allowing you to connect the device to the "big brother".

Despite the fact that developers are positioning their device as Internet TV, in fact it is really a mini-PC. The device configuration includes a processor Telechips TCC8923 ARM Cortex-A5 with a frequency of 1 GHz. In addition, there is a graphics adapter Mali 400. Plus, the developers have provided the presence of a wireless communication module WiFi. The RAM here is 512 MB, plus 4 GB of user-accessible memory.

Unfortunately, the authors of the project have not provided a slot for memory cards, so you have to be content with the ability to connect a flash drive or any other type of drives. The cost of the device is not very high - $ 53.
