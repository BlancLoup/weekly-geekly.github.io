Free webinar: Personal performance for project managers. July 21, 2009

Date: July 21, 2009
Time: 17: 00-19: 00 (Kiev, GMT + 2) | 18: 00-20: 00 (Moscow, GMT + 3)
Format: online webinar
Speaker: Vyacheslav Pankratov



This training is designed for project managers and leading specialists who work in a multitasking environment and are responsible for the performance of work by other project participants.

The training provides an opportunity to implement a simple and working system of daily management of personal and work tasks and activities.

Goals:
  • Master the step by step technique of setting and categorizing tasks
  • Develop daily practices for effective work


Seminar content:
  • How does the human brain: what is the energy for the brain
  • Snowflake Manager: Debris and Delegation

  • Tools: whiteboard, markers, MS Outlook and phone
