Registration is now open for Raiffeisen Data_Cup

We invite you to take part in the Raiffeisen Data_Cup data analysis championship. The online part will be held on the platform of Boosters.pro. Participants will determine the place of residence and work of Raiffeisenbank's client using an anonymized payment and cash withdrawal history. The online part runs until March 23 , and the results will be summed up and awarded in Moscow on March 30.




The championship is aimed at showing what tasks are facing the staff of the bank. The author of an interesting and useful solution will be able to get not only a prize, but also the opportunity to join the team of bank analysts, find like-minded people and learn new things.

Task


Raiffeisenbank customers make purchases and withdraw cash from ATMs with the help of cards. Having received their transaction history, information about merchants (a place that allows to accept payments using a bank plastic card), the participants of the championship must predict two pairs of coordinates: the house and the client‚Äôs work. The assessment of the quality of the solution in the problem is the percentage of hits in a circle with a radius of 0.02 degrees relative to the real coordinates of the house and work.

Who can participate in the Data Science Contest


Citizens of the Russian Federation and the Republic of Belarus who have reached eighteen years of age are allowed to participate in the Contest. To participate in the competition are not allowed to persons directly or indirectly involved in the preparation of tasks, data, as well as the organization of the Competition.

Individual or team participation


Individual, non-public exchange of code in the competition is prohibited. You can publicly share code examples in the Telegram Championship chat.

What are the evaluation criteria


Solutions are evaluated automatically, based on a comparison with a test dataset available only to the organizers. Rating updated in real time.

How to participate in the Raiffeisen Data Cup


Register on the site Boosters.pro. Create your decision on the data and load it. Solutions can be upgraded and uploaded again, but no more than three times a day.

You can join later


Yes, but it is better to register immediately. Download solutions will be available until March 23, 23:59 inclusive (Moscow time).

You can learn more about the rules of the championship and download the solution here .
