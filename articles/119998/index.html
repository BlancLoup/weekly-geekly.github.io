Live broadcast of DevCon'11 reports. The first day



Attention, the start of the conference and the broadcast were postponed 30 minutes ahead

Today, on May 26, at 11:00 am 11:30 Moscow time, the DevCon'11 conference will be held on software development. Everyone will be able to watch the plenary report and more than 30 technical sessions of the conference live at http://event.msdevcon.ru/ .

Use the #msdevcon hashtag to share opinions and comments or find information on the conference on Twitter.

Content of the first day of DevCon:

The opening of the conference with the participation of Neil Leslie, Tim O'Brien and Alexander Lozhechkin, during which some interesting announcements will be made. Do not miss the plenary report!

Track "mobile development"
  • 15:00 - 16:00 What's new in Windows Phone for the developer: the long-awaited taste of exotic fruits (Vladimir Kolesnikov)
  • 16:15 - 17:15 Earn on Windows Phone 7 - real experience (Mikhail Chernomordikov)
  • 17:45 - 18:45 Power and beauty of corporate applications in Windows Phone
  • 21:00 - 22:00 Transferring to Metro (Dmitry Zhukov)
Cloud Computing Track
  • 15:00 - 16:00 European experience in developing solutions on Windows Azure (Yuri Zaitsev)
  • 16:15 - 17:15 Everything you wanted to know about Windows Azure (but were afraid to ask) (Dmitry Martynov)
  • 17:45 - 18:45 Optimization of applications for working with databases in cloud computing (Dmitry Artemov)
  • 21:00 - 22:00 Immersion in the cloud: a demonstration of the newest features of Windows Azure (Dmitry Martynov)
The track "corporate development"
  • 15:00 - 16:00 Applied software testing with Visual Studio 2010 (Vladimir Gusarov)
  • 16:15 - 17:15 Software quality assurance: diagnostics, optimization and other tasks with Visual Studio 2010 (Maxim Goldin)
  • 17:45 - 18:45 Guide for busy developers on version control with Team Foundation Server 2010 (Dmitry Andreev)
  • 21:00 - 22:00 Access Control Service as a powerful personal identification tool (Ilya Vorobyov)
Track "Internet business"
  • 15:00 - 16:00 Choice of startup startup steegy (Sergey Kotyrev)
  • 16:15 - 17:15 Developer's career - who will you need tomorrow and how much will you pay (Alena Vladimirskaya)
