Now smart search and in asana

In Asana, a smart search has appeared, now it's even easier to manage tasks.



Who does not know what Asana is, then there is an old overview of this task management system . Since the publication of the previous article there have been few changes, and their significance has not been great. But the new search gives a huge amount of new possible scenarios and all this in the spirit of the simplicity of the system.

Now you can do a search on virtually all available parameters. For example:
  • Find all closed tasks during the last week
  • Search for all open tasks that have the word Suggestion in the title
  • Search all open tasks created by the boss

Search results can be sorted by Last Modified , Created Date or Task Date . And those searches that you often need can be saved by marking them as Loved Ones .
