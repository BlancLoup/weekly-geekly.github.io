Antispam "send SMS"

Hello!
About a month ago I invented an anti-spam service (see this topic ). Let me remind you, it is devoted to messages like "send SMS". Essence - you need to specify what is being asked to send and where, with a large number of messages, the spammer will not receive money, since payments are delayed.

What changed?
  1. In addition to text and numbers, you can specify additional data. The full text of what they sent is used to analyze similar messages. In addition, a program is currently being developed that will draw the number and text from the full text. Screenshot - it is sometimes asked by operators to confirm the message. Accepted in JPG, GIF, PNG.
  2. To automatically send a message, now you need to specify your address and enter the number from the captcha. Address - to find out additional details (sometimes providers ask for the way in which a message is received, or ask for a screenshot). Number with captcha - protection from bots.
  3. There are feedback and bookmark buttons. I suggest all of them to use;) to express their ideas and save the bookmark.
  4. Statistics appeared. You can look at what numbers are asked to send SMS and what to write in them, as well as look at the full text of spam messages.
  5. There was a version for the PDA - an analogue of the full version, but without unnecessary references and instructions.
  6. There could be YOUR idea;)

There were no banners on the main page, there will be only a link to Yandex.Money ... If there will be an advertisement, only on additional pages (statistics, etc.)

In the plans:
  1. Automatic recognition of numbers and text, based on available texts
  2. Recommendations for those who sent SMS: what to do and how to return the money.
All existing questions can be asked here, ideas expressed here or in the feedback.
If someone from those who used the service received letters from Incore Media, please unsubscribe in the comments!
