In search of Yahoo embedded player from Rhapsody

Now you can listen to music directly from the search results page on Yahoo.com .

If you heard a song on the radio, you had to look for where to download or buy the file, just to make sure that this is the song you heard. Now, Yahoo's search pages have built a special FoxyPlayer flash plugin (technology from Rhapsody ), so you can listen to the found song at the touch of a button. Of course, it's free. Well, if you like the music, you can buy it, or even buy a whole album of a vending artist.



Currently there are about 10,000 music tracks in the search database. True, there is one limitation: you can listen to no more than 25 songs a month for free, after which you are asked to buy a paid Rhapsody account.
