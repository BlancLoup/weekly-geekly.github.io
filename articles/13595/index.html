How to start making money on a blog? You have already started writing interesting and useful articles to the blog and attracted a certain number of regular readers; now you need to start earning the first money on the blog. For this there are dozens of ways and services on the Internet, from them you need to choose the most appropriate and most effective. I can offer you the main ways to make money on a blog:

  1. Partnership programs. Some sites (site) offer you to receive rewards for customers attracted through your site. Usually after registering on their site you get a link with a unique parameter, place it on your site and get your money for those customers who have gone to siteP and ordered something there. Affiliate programs have link exchange systems Sape and Xap , many online stores.
  2. Contextual advertising. These are advertisements that appear on your site. You get rewarded for clicks from your site by the links in these ads. Earn on contextual advertising offer search engines Google, Yandex, Webalta and the system Runner.
  3. Sale of text links through automated systems. You register in Sape or Xap systems and get paid for placing sponsored links on your blog pages. The more pages on your blog and the higher its characteristics (TIC, PageRank), the more you can earn.
  4. Direct sale of advertising on the site. You can receive money for placing advertising banners and text links on your website. For this, the website usually provides information about the possibility of advertising. The advertiser contacts you and upon agreement you receive money for advertising. Usually advertising is placed on one or several months.
  5. Writing custom articles or seo-copywriting. You can start writing articles of praise about a particular service or product.
