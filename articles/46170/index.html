BikeCamp 2008 Minibar

On the eve of the upcoming Belarusian barcamp - ByCamp 2008 , the working group organizes a small minibar, the main theme of which will be the preparation for the presentations. Reading numerous blog posts and letters coming through the feedback form, we found that many do not want, do not understand, or even be afraid to speak and tell interesting things to other people! But this is so interesting and useful!

To remove many of the issues related to this (and some others, if time permits), we gather on Saturday , December 6, at 1.30 pm for 413 audiences at the Institute of Journalism of the Belarusian State University for a maximum of one and a half hours (so this will not take much of your time), where we will tell and listen about how to make presentations, how not to, why it is needed and what benefits can bring.

And of course, you can ask any questions regarding BikeCamp, offer your ideas, and even become its organizer!

If you plan to attend other events that will be held in parallel at this time in Minsk, or you are just too lazy :), do not worry - all materials and presentations will be posted on the Web and published on the website.

See you later!

UPD: for those who read the topic a couple of hours ago - time 13.30!
