Intel applications for Android. Informational resources

In our blog, we try to reflect all aspects of the versatile activities of Intel, and we generally succeed. However, one topic - Intel applications created for the Android platform, almost all the time eluded our attention, which is certainly wrong. Correcting our mistake, we immediately launch a series of posts on this issue, dividing all applications into topics; The first post will be devoted to information and reference programs.
Of course, all Intel applications for Android, unlike the processors of our company, are completely free.

As a test platform, the Orange AZ210A communicator on the Intel Atom processor will be used.

Intel ARK


The mobile equivalent of the well-known Automated Relational Knowledge Base (ARK) well-known knowledge base, containing information about Intel hardware solutions with search and comparison functionality. It has an automatic update mechanism, at the same time it is an independent offline application that does not require access to the Internet. Intel Channel Products Guide


Intel's hardware catalog contains information about boxed versions of processors for PCs, motherboards, and SSD disks, as well as instructions for their installation. It has the ability to select equipment suppliers in accordance with your location, and this functionality in Russia works correctly. Unfortunately, in operation, he showed himself not in the best way - he is prone to flying out (or rather, rather, not starting).

Intel VIP Tech Zone


Under this loud name appears a web application for gamers. Their attention is invited to the news feed, reports on events, promotions with sweepstakes and prizes from Intel. VIP Tech Zone members can get privileged access to game events sponsored by the company. I, frankly, not a big fan of games, but noted the beautiful design of the application and a large amount of material contained in it.

Jobs at intel


Well, here, as they say, everything is clear - the vacancy section is perhaps one of the most visited on the Intel site. With a proprietary application, searching for interesting offers is even easier, since there is an opportunity to save search history and its results. Well, the search itself can be carried out in the direction of activity, and by geographical location. Definitely a useful application for studying the labor market.

Android version of the Intel Free Press web portal, a well-known online publication that talks about digital technologies and everything that happens around them.
