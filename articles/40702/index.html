Who will get Schisago2016.com?

Yesterday, the administration of the city of Chicago, together with the US National Olympic Committee, filed an application at WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization). Its essence lies in the request to transfer them to the second level domain chicago2016.com . It seems that another domain scandal is being planned ...

The essence of the matter is as follows. In April 2007, the US National Olympic Committee nominated the city of Chicago as a contender for holding the 2016 Summer Olympics in it. For informational support and marketing purposes, the domain is Chicago2016.org. It is with his help that the organizers intend to ‚Äúpromote‚Äù the city and increase its chances of winning. They wanted to use the domain Schisago2016.com, but it turned out that he was already busy. Back in 2004, he was registered by an American student, Stefan Frain.

Both sides have arguments in their side. Stefan Frain says that he registered the domain before the promotion of Chicago, and therefore it cannot be blamed for cybersquatting. In addition, he claims that he is not going to extract any profit from this domain name - he plans to open a forum on it, in which Chicagoans can leave wishes and comments to their beloved city. Well, representatives of the National Olympic Committee of the United States argue that the disputed domain should belong to this organization simply because they registered the trademark Chiago-2016. In addition, it coincides in name with the main "Olympic" domain of Chicago - Shisago2016.org. Also questioned is the lack of self-interest of the student. It turns out that he registered about 40 domains with the names of different cities that are potential applicants for the 2016 Olympics. Among them are, for example, the domain Tokyo2016.com, which may also be a matter of dispute - it is claimed by Domain Trade, Inc.
