Science


In 2018, we want to consolidate the success of the Science section, which returned triumphant last year!

This time we want to expand the halls of the mind, go beyond math and computer science and try to look into the future.

We want reports:

  1. From the field of exact sciences.
  2. Understandable without special education, but not too simple. I would be guided by the level of graduates of IEN USU (or as it is now called). The more complex things can be explained in plain language, the steeper.

  4. The speaker should at least have a specialized education and / or experience in the relevant field. Personal contribution to the breakthrough is not required, we do not expect the arrival of the Nobel laureates :)

The main requirement for speakers in our section is constant: the ability to explain complex things in a clear language. We will discuss the topic of the report - the main thing is that your eyes are burning.
