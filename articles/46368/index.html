How to call Ballmer for 3 rubles

The problems that usually arise when using IP-telephony are the difficulties associated with the payment and use of services: subscription fee; prepaid cards; special equipment and various settings repel ordinary people from using this technology. I will tell you about the "One phone", which is devoid of many of these shortcomings. Forget headset, softphones, computer, and more.

The Single Telephone is a virtual telephone operator from CJSC CPI, which is the organizer of the United Wallet payment service. "Single Phone" provides technology for making long-distance and international calls at competitive rates and without special equipment.

Thus, by registering on the website www.w1.ru , you can not only make calls, but also use other services of the Single Wallet, for example, pay for cellular communication, the Internet and other services connected to the service.

The registration process is very simple - just enter your mobile phone number and an SMS message with your wallet number and password will be sent to it. You can make calls and use the wallet, as well as all its functions, via a web-based office, a wap site or a mobile application, versions of which are available for almost any phone or smartphone.

How to call?


You can order a call in any client application. To do this, you need to find the item ‚ÄúCall‚Äù in the menu ‚ÄúCalls‚Äù and enter 2 phone numbers: yours and the other person. After demonstrating the cost of the call, the United Phone will connect you to the other party.


At the end of the conversation, the cost of your wallet will be charged, and the corresponding entry will appear in the call history.

How it works?


For making a call, the Call Back technology (callback) is used, which allows you to connect two phone numbers so that the call for each of them is incoming and not charged by the service provider. Those. in fact, when ordering a call, ‚ÄúSingle Phone‚Äù first dials to the 1st subscriber, then to the 2nd, and then connects them to each other:



Thus, when making a call, two calls are made, one to each of the numbers involved in the conversation. In this case, the call for each subscriber is incoming, and the cost of the call is deducted from the wallet balance.

How much is it?


"Single Phone" is an aggregator of VoIP-traffic, which allows it to choose the most favorable tariffs for each direction. Tariffs for all destinations are published on the Single Wallet website, and the cost of the call is displayed before it is made.

Please note that the cost of calls to landline phone numbers is significantly lower than calls to mobiles. For example, connecting 2 mobile phones of one operator can cost more than a regular call between these phones, and a call between landline numbers in Russia and the United States will be very cheap. This pattern is traditional for IP-telephony, but mobile phones with local numbers are equated to landlines.

In the tariffs of the "One phone" highlighted the so-called bonus directions. These are popular destinations, for calls to which, in the presence of bonus minutes, only connection fee in the amount of 1.50 rubles is charged. The talk time is not charged. Bonus minutes are charged for each replenishment of the wallet, and the list of such areas includes fixed numbers of Russia, Italy, Canada, USA, UK, France, Germany, Japan and 38 more countries.

Thus, the cost of a call to Steve Ballmer in Redmond, Washington (USA), say from Novosibirsk can be as little as 3 rubles, regardless of the talk time, of course, if you know the local number of Steve :).
