Special cocktails for the MIX'11 conference!



The developers of the Cocktail Flow application for Windows Phone 7 presented a promotional event dedicated to the MIX'11 conference, which will be held from April 12 in Las Vegas. Cocktail Flow is an application that many, not without reason, consider to be one of the most beautiful for the Windows Phone 7 platform.

A new promotional offer offers to get the application for free, plus offers a new special set of cocktails with distinctive names and even the appearance, which guesses Microsoft products, which will be discussed at the MIX'11 conference!



Conditions for participation in the promotion are presented on a special page at http://cocktailflow.com/mix11/ . Hurry up, the offer is limited in time.
