Welcome to Moscow CocoaHeads January 29



We invite on January 29, Friday, iOS / OS X developers to meet the CocoaHeads community, which will be held in the Moscow office of Mail.Ru Group. In the program - 3 reports and, of course, informal communication. The event program is waiting for you under the cut.

Search iOS 9 , David Lashkhi, Delivery Club

David will describe 3 search technologies presented by Apple on iOS 9: searching using NSUserActivity, Core Spotlight and Web Markup. There will be shown examples of their implementation in an existing project, processing the results of the issue, the differences of the technologies used.

David Lashkhi has been working with iOS for almost 3 years, he is interested in sorting algorithms. In addition to iOS, he was also engaged in testing, a bit of design and working on the technology of virtual holograms Musion Systems.
