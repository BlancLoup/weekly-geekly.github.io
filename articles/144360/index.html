DrupalConf - June 2

Very soon, on June 2, the largest Drupal conference in Russia will take place in Moscow on Saturday. The conference audience is professional developers, SEO-specialists, system administrators, owners of web-studios, project managers from Russia, countries of near and far abroad.


We have been preparing for a long time - and we have several surprises:
  • For those who just want to get acquainted with Drupal, we will hold a 2-hour training-training
  • Florian Loretan, co-author of The Definitive Guide to Drupal 7 (English), will attend the conference with a report.
  • The report will be read by Graeme Blackwood, creative director of DrupalCon London, creative director of the Deeson Group (English)
  • Martin Mayer, leader of the User Group of Munich (English) will tell about the creation of the community.
