Developer Contest: integrate the application with Evernote and please the first users with premium subscriptions


As you may know, we have an open and free API for integrating the Evernote service with applications from other developers. Thousands of people and companies are already using it to extend the functionality of their products. We want more and more such products. Therefore, yesterday at Wowzapp 2012, we announced a new contest for developers.

As part of the competition, we offer to create an application or service for any mobile and desktop platforms of your choice. The main thing is that the product should use the Evernote Cloud API in its work.

We will consider your applications within a month - from November 11 to December 11 inclusive.

What we are ready to provide to our partners whose products will seem useful and interesting to users of Evernote:

  • access to 40+ million service users and global prospects for your product;
  • ready external information storage for your application with the ability to synchronize between all key platforms, handwriting and printed text recognition technology in photos and scans and other features;
  • active marketing and PR support for launching and further promoting the product, including an announcement on our social sites and a blog, working with journalists, in-app banners, etc .;
  • presentation in the Evernote Gallery , a catalog of selected partner solutions from around the world, which can be accessed both on the website and in Evernote desktop applications;
  • and of course the prize. Evernote will provide 100 quarterly premium Evernote subscriptions that you can use to promote your application, for example, giving them to the first hundred users or arranging a rally. For our part, we will also support this PR campaign.

What we will pay attention to when considering affiliate applications:
  • on the quality of the implementation of the application: appearance and performance;
  • on the functionality of the application, its relevance to users;
  • on the amount of integration with Evernote: the application can simply send information to our service, and can download it, create new notebooks, use recognition technology, etc.

We will try to advise you on any related issues (both technical and organizational).

Send your entries to ru_hackathon@evernote.com

We will announce the results of the developer competition after December 15th.
