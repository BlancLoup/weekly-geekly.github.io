New service from REG.RU - domain monitoring

Want to know what is happening with any domain - to be aware of what DNS is registered, has the administrator changed? Or are you a business owner who wants to receive in time news not only about all domain registrations that match the company's trademark, but also receive notifications about the imminent release of your domain?

Thanks to the new service from REG.RU, it is easy to get answers to these and other questions.
Domain monitoring allows you to track the status of any domain of interest by selected parameters:
- change of domain administrator;
- change DNS servers;
- expiration of the domain registration;
- change the date of registration;
- release of the domain;
- change of IP-addresses of the domain (domain hosting);
- change the status of the web server on the domain.



In addition, you can track not only a single domain, but also a group of domain names using a domain mask (only for .RU / . / .SU domains). Domain masks allow you to select several similar domains for monitoring at once. For example, the ‚Äúdomain *‚Äù mask will correspond to domains starting with ‚Äúdomain‚Äù. The maximum number of domains that match the same mask is 500. This feature is convenient for companies that want to protect their brand from online scammers.

After setting up the monitoring of domains according to their needs, the user will receive an email containing a report on the current situation and the changes made when changing one of the monitored parameters.

In the process of implementing this service, REG.RU specialists collected and analyzed information on the Whois data output formats of all domain zones in the world. In particular, a collection of regular expressions was created to extract information from the Whois about the domain administrator, his contact information, and the date the domain was released. This allowed us to implement and configure the system of alerts associated with changes in this data.

Use the results of the work of specialists of the company here http://www.reg.ru/domain/tracking/ .

The service is paid: the cost of monitoring 1 domain will be 5 rubles per month, monitoring 1 mask - 750 rubles per month. At the same time, for partners of REG.RU and Privilege Club card holders there are significant discounts: 30% and 15%, respectively. Monitoring each monitored domain is a separate service - a list of domains is easy to manage, removing uninteresting and adding new domains.
