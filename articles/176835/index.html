Google Glass Technical Specs



Google has published a page with some of the technical specifications of Google Glass.

SoC : unknown.
Display / projector : 640x360, "the equivalent of a 25-inch display from a distance of 2.4 meters."
Camera : 5 MP, 720p video.
Audio : Vibration Transmitter to Bone.
Connectivity : 802.11b / g WiFi, Bluetooth.
Memory : 16 GB NAND total, 12 GB free.
Battery : Capacity unknown, "1 day of typical use."
Input / output : microUSB.
System : Android 4.0.3 or higher with the My Glass app (it appeared on Google Play today).

The display / projector resolution of 640x360 with an aspect ratio of 16: 9 is known from the requirements for the user interface of applications for Google Glass.

According to rumors , the most likely SoC in the device will be OMAP4.

The lack of 802.11n specifications seems quite surprising. There is also no GPS module. All this is due to the fact that Google Glass is constantly working in conjunction with a smartphone.

Simultaneously with the technical specifications, both the documentation and the Mirror API for application developers are published.
