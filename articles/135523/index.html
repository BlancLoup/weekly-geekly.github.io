2.7 million emails between the most influential people in the world will soon be online

Hacktivists are preparing to publish in open access 200 GB of letters, obtained as a result of the hacking of the company Stratfor . The services of the private intelligence and analytical agency Strategic Forecasting are used by major US corporations and government agencies. The company itself is called the "shadow CIA" - its experts make unbiased economic and geopolitical forecasts, for example, about the collapse of Russia.

The forum reddit already make a list of keywords for which you will need to search in this e-mail archive.

It should be noted that Anonymous denied any involvement in the cracking Stratfor.

The media trumpeted that hackers obtained Stratfor credit card details. In a specially published statement, the hacktivists emphasize that their goal was not at all credit cards, but postal correspondence, so the media should not mislead the public and represent them as intruders.

What kind of information will be published soon? In addition to the mail archive, these are the names of thousands of informants who have provided information to Stratfor for more than ten years. Many of them work for American intelligence and NATO, military-industrial complex enterprises, and also government agencies. As the example of Wikileaks has shown, all these organizations, along with some others, are conducting a targeted campaign to discredit Wikileaks, the hacktivist movement and freedom of information in general.

Publication of open access to correspondence between members of this high-ranking "group" will contribute to the fight against corruption, crime and deception among representatives of the highest echelons of power. In addition, the names of the informants will be an important link for further investigations.

Most likely, files with postal correspondence will be published on the site echelon2.org .
