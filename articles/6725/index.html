Google offers to choose new Gmail features

Google offers users to choose from the list that they think they need the new features for Gmail. You can choose the five most needed features from four different categories:
PersonalizationOrganizationIntegration with other Google products. Useful gadgets.
In the first category, you can vote for adding features such as color templates for mail, signatures with complex formatting, background in the chat window, various gadgets in messages, new emoticons, creating your own shortcuts ...

In the second category, you can vote for creating filtering outgoing messages, changing the label system to the folder system, creating a hierarchical system of labels (or subfolders), sorting mail according to different parameters (size, sending date, sender, etc.), turning on or off viewing conversations ...

The third category offers integration with Google Calendar, Google Groups, Google Reader, Picasa, Google News, Blogger, orkut, Google Talk ...

In the fourth and last category, you can suggest adding To-do list, IMAP support, importing messages and contacts from other mail services, exporting messages to CDs for storage, warning of new letters via SMS, group chat ...
