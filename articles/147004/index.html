Tale of CPA-Typesquatter

That just does not make people thirst for easy money and profit.

Yesterday we discovered a number of domains with typos, registered on April 3, 2012 by one Typersquatter, which he began to use according to the following scheme:

1. takes the name of a popular resource (A) *;
2. selected with typos the names of the resource (A) and squat;
3. put a redirect (A) - (B) **, where (B) is a resource of the same subject matter;
4. selected with typos the names of the resource (B) and squat;
5. put a redirect (B) - (B), so that (A) thought it was a trick (B);
6. and then ...

We thought there would be an a la scheme:

- Hello, this is a representative of the resource (B), we here merge the traffic from your typos to yourself, if you want, we will give you the domains for 100,500 money.

But it would somehow look strange, since (B) is not quite a petty player to whom reputation is worth more than traffic fused from typos. Yes, and did not want to believe in the possibility of this.

Emotions (after all, Monday was just beginning) were written to representatives (B), without going into technical details, saying that ‚Äúthey did a good job, thank you‚Äù. And they themselves began to analyze the domain field in this context, and, I confess honestly, they even thought ‚Äúwouldn‚Äôt someone pour traffic to myself‚Äù? After all, there is someone to pick up (list for dessert)!

But, in practice it turned out like this:

- Hello, hello. This is the technical director of the resource (B), you wrote today that some domains with typos of your name will redirect to us. How do you comment on this?
- Uh ... (told their story from 1 point to 5, further clarifying whether this is not their tricks).
- No, this is not our tricks. These domains are redirected to us via the Belarusian server reg-org.com, and are registered at salaf@tut.by (Sergei V Dubin), the same person under the same nickname is registered as a webmaster in the CPA affiliate AdmitAd. Also, an impressive number of domains have been registered to this address, including GODADY.RU (Note of the author - godaddy.com is one of the largest Western domain registrars).

Here it is - the truth of life, the thirst for profit, the cunning and greed of the "partners"! AdmitAd, by the way, hinted to representatives (B) that they can disable this webmaster, but they do not advise. This is on condition that both (A) and (B) were sitting on the pipe working with AdmitAd.
