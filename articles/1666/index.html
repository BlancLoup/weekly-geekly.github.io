Leonid Reiman's forecast for the IT market volume

On Monday, the head of the Ministry of Information Technologies and Communications Leonid Reiman predicted the volume of the information technology market: in 2006 it would be $ 13, 4-14 billion. He recalled that last year the volume of the IT-market amounted to a little more than $ 11 billion, and the export volume - $ 1 billion against $ 400 million in 2004.
