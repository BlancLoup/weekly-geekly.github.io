Microsoft Xbox One Complete Parsing

So, today we continue to acquaint you with the consoles of the new generation. After a successful review of the PlayStation 4, it was the turn of the new device from Microsoft. Xbox One went on sale on November 22, and Microsoft already reports about a million consoles sold per day. The previous generation of the console came out 8 years ago, in 2005. A total of 80 million consoles were sold, Microsoft will continue to release Xbox 360 until 2015.



Specifications


  • CPU - 8-core x86-x64 CPU APU 1.75 GHz
  • GPU - AMD Radeon GPU as part of the APU, based on the AGCN-architecture with 12 compute modules, which have 768 cores; D3D 11.2 @ 1,23 TFLOPS
  • RAM ‚Äî2 memory types: DRAM and ESRAM. The volume of DDR3 is 8 GB (2133 MHz) (only 5 GB are used, since the remaining 3 GB is limited to the needs of operating systems). ESRAM capacity - 32 MB. RAM capacity is 68.3 Gb / s.
  • Hard disk - 500 GB, non-removable, it is possible to connect an external hard disk
  • Blu-ray and DVD drive
  • Kinect 2.0 controller. High definition camera - 1080p. Face Recognition. IR sensors increase the quality of shooting and allow you to shoot in the dark
  • 3 USB 3.0 connectors and two HDMI (input and output)
  • Wired Gigabit Ethernet (1 Gb / s), Wireless Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11 a / b / g / n, IEEE 802.11ac)

Xbox One supports 4K resolution and 7.1 sound.



The console package includes a power supply, a Kinect 2.0 controller and a gamepad. The lucky ones who bought the prefix from the first installment will notice the inscription "Day One 2013" on the gamepad.
