New gadget for bacillofobs

Bacillofobia (bacteriophobia) - an obsessive fear, fear of being infected by germs from infected objects. People suffering from this mental disorder are afraid to touch any things in public places. They can not take up the door handle without gloves, they are afraid to use touchscreens in ATMs, etc.

It turns out that there are quite a lot of bacillofobic sufferers, and enterprising businessmen even saw this as a business opportunity. The American company Handler specializes in the production of high-tech devices that facilitate the life of the unfortunate.

The latest Handler is a multifunction keychain with which you can open doors, manipulate the toilet paper holder and press ATM keys, avoiding direct contact with all these infectious items. A feature of the device is the built-in surface disinfection system using "silver nanocrystals". The surface covered with a special substance kills all microbes with which it comes into contact. Accordingly, the gadget constantly disinfects itself.

Moreover, the company proposes to introduce a self-disinfecting agent into clothing, medicine, soap, etc., in order to make the world around us perfectly clean.
