PYCON RUSSIA 2015: the final program

Hello!

On September 18-19, Yekaterinburg will host one of the highlights of the python community, the PYCON RUSSIA conference. 21 reports from the leading python-developers from the USA, France, Germany, the Czech Republic, Ukraine and Russia, two master classes, Lightning Talks - and this is only in the official part. You have another week to look at the program of this year, buy a ticket and come to Ekaterinburg. All details below.




The final schedule is in front of you. Abstracts of the reports can be read by clicking on the title of the report.

Cost of participation - 10 000 rubles.

The ticket price includes: participation in the conference, lunch 18.09, dinner 18.09, lunch 19.09, coffee breaks and afterparty, a bus from the center of Yekaterinburg to the venue and back. The place where you will live is paid separately (for example, on the Oriole - from 2800 for a double room per day with breakfast).

Students - 50% discount from the price. Teachers associated with the python disciplines participate in the conference for free.
