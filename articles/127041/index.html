Second Moscow JavaScript Meetup

Almost a month ago, the first meeting of JavaScript developers took place. On it, we discussed all the modern technologies and were glad for the bright future of JavaScript. Many participants of the meeting wanted to make regular communication with colleagues, so we decided to hold events about once a month. And the next meeting will be held on Thursday, September 1, at 19:00 in the Yandex office. Register for an event! All JavaScript developers and their sympathizers are invited.

The program of the first mitap was a bit spontaneous, so the second one will have a more rigid format. At first there will be reports of 15 minutes plus 5 minutes for questions, then informal communication. Anyone who wants to make a report, write!

To continue chatting online, we now have a group on Facebook, join us ! And, of course, follow us on Twitter!
