Registration for NeoQUEST-2016 is open

Great news: registration is open for the NeoQUEST annual cybersecurity competition! We traditionally see off the winter and meet the spring with the online stage of the hacker competition! The online stage NeoQUEST 2016 will take place from March 11-21.

At the end of the online stage, the best participants will be selected, who will be invited to St. Petersburg for the summer confrontation, giving them the opportunity to win cool prizes and a trip to one of the international conferences on cybersecurity! More about the topics and tasks NeoQUEST-2016 - under the cut!

For those who first hear about NeoQUEST


NeoQUEST is an annual cybersecurity competition for individual participants, conducted in two stages: the online stage and the face-to-face stage (often referred to as the ‚Äúface-to-face‚Äù).

As part of the online stage, anyone can register on the event website and complete tasks posted there, combined by an interesting legend. All tasks have a different level of complexity, some of them are able to pass even to beginners in cybersecurity. The main thing is savvy and attentiveness! In accordance with the dynamic scale, points are awarded for each task completed. The more participants passed the task, the less points will be awarded for it, and vice versa. Also, the earlier the participant passed the task - the more points he gets! All those who have completed at least one task are expected to receive small prizes from the NeoQUEST team!

And the best of the best, in addition to the prizes, also receive an invitation as a participant in the hackquest for the summer ‚Äúconfrontation‚Äù in St. Petersburg, where they will have 8 hours to meet with their rivals in the online stage in reality and fight for the main prize - a trip to one of the international cyber security conferences!

Those who are not among the best at the end of the online stage should not despair - as long as participants go through the hackquest, everyone can enjoy a vivid event, including:
  • interesting reports from information security practices;
  • exciting and fun contests;
  • online quiz on Twitter NeoQUEST "USE";
  • workshops on cybersecurity (for example, at the on-site stage NeoQUEST-2014 a cold-boot attack was demonstrated using liquid nitrogen, you can see the photos here ).

At the end of the evening, the results of the competition are summed up: the hackquest winners are awarded with gifts, the winner of the "Examination on Unified Security System" is announced.

This year, NeoQUEST-2016 is dedicated to the confrontation between the police and the mafia, and each participant can choose the side that he likes the most!
