Digg Launches American Presidential Election 2008 Social Network

The most popular social network today launched the Digg the Candidates section. The work, as can be seen from the profiles of the candidates, has been going on since spring, the starting content has been filled, and beta testing has been going on since November 17th. Diggers can now friend candidates and read all news related to them in a digglent. Rating is set by the number of friends. Even now, although it has been less than a day since the start of the service, digg spammers, overestimating the ratings of some candidates, have become more active.
The candidates have created profiles not only on digge, but also on facebook, myspace and even in flicker. For example, Rudi Giuliani on Facebook 10k friends. And we ironically, why Putin is not VKontakte.
