On-line webinar "Modern approaches to the organization of software development processes: Lean and Agile"

Summer is not the most traditional time for obtaining new knowledge (although all people are different). But in the midst of its height, on July 14, we will organize a free online webinar on modern approaches to the organization of software development processes with ScrumTrek .

The main topics of the webinar:
  • What comes to replace the old (cascading) approaches to software development?
  • What makes Agile and Lean different from traditional approaches?
  • What principles and principles are based on these approaches?

Total: July 14, 2011 (Thursday), Nikita Filippov (ScrumTrek) conducts a webinar "Modern approaches to the organization of software development processes: Lean and Agile." Beginning at 11:00 (Moscow time), duration ~ 1 hour and 15 minutes (including with the session of answers to questions).
