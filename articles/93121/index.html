1. Preface


The year was 2009, and for 3 years now I have been the happy owner of the ASUS P535 LKS. During this time, he survived the move from WM 5.0 to official WM 6.0, and then to self-made WM 6.1.21018. But as time went on, it was hopelessly outdated, which in practice was most often expressed in a catastrophic shortage of RAM for simultaneously launching at least two applications.

At the same time, on the Internet, I met information about the upcoming announcement of the M10 communicator from Garmin-Asus. His TTX and the appearance of me hooked, but knowing the time lag of the asus from the announcement to the start of sales, I just made a mark and forgot about it ...

And on March 9, 2010, the Ukrainian representative office of ASUS announced the start of sales of the M10 in Ukraine. I must say that Garmin-Asus took into account the mistakes of the previous model M20 and M10 assigned a price that is adequate to its characteristics. I decided to purchase it.

6. Camera

In addition, there is a geotagging feature. If it is enabled, then the shooting coordinates are recorded in the EXIF ‚Äã‚Äãtags of the created JPG file. The coordinates are taken from the GPS, so it automatically turns on while doing so.

The following resolutions are available for video shooting: 800x480, 640x480, 352x288, 320x240, 176x144.

Recording is done with sound.

7. Performance


The device is equipped with a Qualcomm MSM 7227 processor, which operates at 600 MHz. In the process, the device does not heat up.

Processor performance is more than enough for all tasks that can be performed on this device. There are no brakes at work, the graphical interface is very responsive, scrolling is smooth everywhere, the screen is not redrawn in pieces. There is no jerk when taking photos / videos. Playing an unconverted movie in Xvid 720x400 25.00fps format is carried out smoothly, without jerks and stutters.

Separately, it is worth noting a large amount of RAM, which already has 512 MB in this device, of which 356 MB is available for the system, and about 225 MB for the user. This is more than enough for any modern program, including running simultaneously. In the process, there were no cases that there were less than 150 MB free, even with about 10 programs running, including navigation, which is traditionally very demanding of resources.

8. Navigation


Garmin-Asus M10 has the full right to be called a navigation communicator. The device is equipped with high-quality sensitive GPS-module. The speed of searching for satellites after recent use is less than a minute, it often happens less than 10 seconds, you just have to go outside. By the way, indoors in the middle of the house at a distance of about 4 meters from the window, 4-5 satellites are caught, which is enough to determine the location with good accuracy.

When you turn on the device or when you restart it, the GPS module automatically turns on. If necessary, it can be disabled manually. GPS works independently of the GSM module or any other interfaces, it is completely independent. Also no internet connection required.
At a considerable distance from the last location with the GPS off (more than 1000 km), when it is turned on, the device offers to connect to the Internet and update the ‚Äúsatellite map‚Äù. It is optional, you can refuse.

The communicator comes with the pre-installed Garmin Mobile XT navigation software with maps of the region of purchase, in my case with a detailed map of Ukraine.

9. Software shell


The communicator runs on Windows Mobile 6.5.3. Over it, a proprietary shell from Garmin-Asus is installed, which includes both the main screen and a set of additional software to expand the capabilities of the device. Corporate software actively uses all the capabilities of the device, including an accelerometer.

In general, it is worth noting that the communicator is usable from the moment of purchase without preliminary finishing, there is enough standard software for almost all needs.

I don‚Äôt see any point in considering the standard software WM 6.5, so only additional software will be described below.

Main screen and task manager



The shell allows you to place up to 5 screens on the site, each with 9 icons. All of them can be replaced, moved.
Task Manager allows you to switch to any running application by selecting it in appearance. It is possible to close any application or all at once.

Status, GPS screen, Wireless Manager



The status screen allows you to control the battery charge level, volume, cellular signal reception level, on and GPS level, data transfer.
The GPS screen allows you to turn on and off the GPS, displays the signal level of the satellites, current coordinates, speed.
Wireless Manager allows you to turn on and off all available wireless interfaces. Each interface is autonomously controlled and independent of others.

Facebook, Music, Media Gallery



The Facebook application allows you to manage your Facebook account, track changes, write messages, etc.
The Music application allows you to play MP3 songs, you can create playlists.
Media Gallery allows you to view images and photos located on the device. When you turn the device into landscape mode, the picture effectively rotates and turns around to full screen.

Clock, weather, MSN Money



It is possible to set alarms, start the countdown timer and stopwatch.
The Weather application displays the weather in the nearest settlement (determined by GPS), the source is my.garmin.com.
MSN Money allows you to view stock quotes.

Service, Where am I, Motion counter



The Service menu provides quick access to viewing information about recent routes, viewing the map of the Garmin Mobile XT application, etc.
The Where I am screen displays the last recorded address of your location when GPS was turned on. This could be, for example, a parking place.
The movement counter displays statistical information collected while driving while GPS is on. It is noteworthy that in this case it is not necessary to use a regular navigation program, I used another one, but the statistics were collected anyway. While driving displays the current speed. It is possible to reset statistics for the last trip or for all time.

Program menu (Start) and Options





The program menu, which is displayed by a single press of one of the three Start touch buttons, contains a list of all installed programs. Here you can also see all the branded programs that are not in the WM 6.5 package.

System information and information



System information allows you to view the "iron" stuffing of the device, as well as the version of the firmware of the device itself, ExtROM and the radio module.

Memory and the contents of the onboard 4 GB flash drive



It is possible to view both available and total memory RAM, ROM and memory cards.
As you can see, the maps of the pre-installed Garmin Mobile XT are located on the onboard flash drive. There is also a detailed manual on the use of the device with 128 pages. Included with the device is only a brief printed guide to use.

Call, Search, Map Overview



Applications launched by pressing each of the 3 buttons, all the time located at the bottom of the main screen, regardless of the current page.

Virtual keyboard



View of the virtual keyboard with different layouts. When you press a key above it, a tooltip pops up with a large image of the character being entered, which is convenient.

Marketplace



The Windows Marketplace application allows you to install additional software on your device placed in the directory. As you can see, there are a lot of free applications on the list.

Newstation, Shared Internet



Newstation allows you to read news RSS feeds of various sites.
Shared Internet allows you to share the Internet, available on your device, for external devices connected via USB or Bluetooth.

Currency Converter, Backup, Adobe Reader LE



Currency Converter allows you to recalculate the required amount from one currency to another. The exchange rate is updated from the site my.garmin.com.
Backing up allows you to make a backup of your contacts, messages, documents and recorded gps-tracks of the Garmin program with the possibility of subsequent recovery at any time.
Adobe Reader LE allows you to read PDF documents, in particular, a detailed manual on the use of the device itself.

Flight status





The Flight Status application allows you to find out the schedule of departures of the nearest airport in any direction, view the flight status, find out the arrival time of a flight.

Type of installed Yandex.Maps, Navitel Navigator, Google Maps
