Before you introduce the device, I will talk about the purchase and delivery. The device comes with the prefix Google Play edition, and therefore sold in the Google Play store and only for residents of the United States.

Delivery through an intermediary

Of course, if you have a friend in the states, you can send the device to him. If there are no such friends, then you need to use the services of an intermediary. Each of them has its advantages and disadvantages. For me personally, the deciding factors when choosing a mediator were: a way around the Russian Post, the speed of delivery and a small price. By all criteria, Shopfans approached with his original Shopfans-Express delivery in less than 10 days. I want to immediately say that the delivery method so far only works for Moscow, the Moscow Region, St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region. If you do not live in these regions, I advise you to use Priority-BWW . Its advantages are that the parcels go through the "shopfans" channels, and they better watch the parcels and know where they are at each moment of time and when they will have you.

First you need to create your account on the site of the intermediary. Nothing complicated, go to the site Shopfans and select the registration . Enter your personal data.



After registration, we will be given a billing address, located under the spoiler. SHOW MY SHOPFANS ADDRESS . Delivery of the Galaxy S4 to an intermediary is free only if you use tax-free staff. In this case, you need to use the address in Delaware. It is more suitable for buying electronics.



Then in the settings you need to add your home address. You can use the worker. In general, we write down the address that is convenient for the delivery of the parcel. But that is not all! If you plan to send using the Shopfans-Express method, then you should attach scans of documents. There are not many of them: a copy of the passport , a signed contract with a customs broker , a guarantee certificate .



Why do I need to provide documents? The fact is that due to strict customs rules, Shopfans are forced to follow formalities when departing via the Shopfans-Express method. And therefore you need to prove that the goods are sent to a real person with this contract. Therefore, along with him, need a copy of the passport and signatures on the documents. All documents are loaded once, which means the following orders will be sent much easier.

Passport
We scan 2 pages of our passport (we need the first page with your photo, and the one where your registration is indicated), placing both passport pages on one A4 page.

Customs broker agreement
The contract itself is downloaded here . We print, read, sign. We scan only the signed last page.

Letter of guarantee
We print this letter of guarantee , we read, we sign. We scan the signed letter.

We should have 4 files (2 files - a passport, 1 file - a contract, 1 file - a letter). The recommended format for scanned documents and photos is jpg or pdf. We lead to a normal form and compare with the sample: https://shopfans.ru/guide/shopfans-express/examples.html . After that, enter the passport data in Russian and attach files.



In fact, the procedure takes 5 minutes - if there is a scanner at home. I did not have it, went to the neighbor, the elapsed time - 10 minutes.
