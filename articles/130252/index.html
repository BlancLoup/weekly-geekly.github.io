Android on Windows

A small company of like-minded people presented the alpha version of the software that allows you to run Android applications under Windows.





The mission of the developers from the company Bluestacks is quite well expressed in the amusing demo video that can be viewed from the company's main page: bluestacks.com/home.php . I recommend to see a fairly high-quality demo video.

A distinctive feature of this software is that it is not an emulator. According to the developers, they implemented the native execution of Android applications. That is why Android applications are very responsive and do not cause problems with delay.

By installing the software, you can:

  • Run Android applications in full screen mode, and they will work quickly;
  • Downloading your favorite Android applications from your phone to your computer;
  • Run 10 applications available out of the box;
  • Install up to 26 of your favorite apps;


Additionally, developers warn that the application may run slowly on old netbooks and "tablets".

The application is developed for Windows 7 and you can download it absolutely free.

The developer company has already attracted $ 7.6 million of initial investment, having interested large investors with its development.
