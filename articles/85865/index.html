Bookmark design for books. Progress and does not think to stop

We still read paper books. Despite the growth in sales of e-book readers and many reader programs for communicators, the paper still holds us in its arms. And this is, in general, good. But if the appearance of books remained unchanged for decades, why not pay attention to related products? I'm talking about bookmarks.



Under the cut a small number of design ideas on bookmarks.

Lyrical digression: it often happens that when working on an article, you leave some interesting and unusual materials behind. Today I would like to share a couple of finds that were not included in the main material , but seemed to me too interesting to just forget. I hope they seemed interesting not only to me.

So, dear gentlemen, bookmarks. It would seem that you can still come up with in this field? However, designers Jung-Hyun Lee, Won-Sik Chae & Rhea Jeong offer the amazing inflatable bookmark ABRACADABRA. The idea is simple to the genius - in front of us is a rectangular pad with air, half of which is flattened under the weight of the pages. It is necessary to compress the part sticking out of the book, as the air will open the book on the necessary page. Voila



The most interesting thing is that the idea is not only beautiful, but also easy to perform (I think many people remember very well how they glued the covers for textbooks with an iron).

The second tab, with the save icon, is made even simpler: we only need a printer and scissors.
