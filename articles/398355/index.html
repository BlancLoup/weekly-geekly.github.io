For those interested in financial innovation - Russian FinTech Meetup # 1

October 27 in the center of Moscow on the site Digital October will be held mitap , organized by the community Russian Fintech . The event opens a cycle of monthly meetings for all those interested in financial innovations: from project founders and company executives to financial-enthusiasts.
According to various estimates, in Russia about 100 project teams are involved in start-ups in the sphere of fintech, and their number does not cease to grow. Practices have emerged in this area that have deeply studied various areas of financial innovation. At the same time, the unrealized potential of interaction between participants in various areas of fintech is noticeable.

The goal of the Russian FinTech community is to bring together industry representatives who are ready to share their accumulated experience and knowledge. Thus, it is planned to form a community whose members are able to support and accelerate the development of the main technological areas of the financial sphere.

The themes of the first Russian FinTech Meetup # 1 meeting will be relevant for anyone who wants to keep abreast of modern trends in the development of the financial industry and its advanced technological solutions.

Event speakers:

  • Alexander Ivanov, founder and CEO of the Waves blockchain platform. Topic: "Crowdfunding VS Venture";
  • Vladimir Maslyakov, CTO, Hub KL10CH. The topic of the report: "The evolution of money: from seashells to bitcoins";
  • Marina Gureva, Head of HSE Innovation Management, Member of the Blockchain Community. Topic: "The current state of cryptoeconomics."
