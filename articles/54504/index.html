Siberian Internet Week in Novosibirsk

Tomorrow Siberian Internet Week starts in Novosibirsk, an event for Internet business professionals and their customers working in the regions of Siberia. The conference will be held from 16 to 20 March. The visitors will be offered 32 reports, some of which will be held in the format of a round table or a master class.

The whole day (Tuesday) will be devoted to what the Internet can do for traditional business and how to use it properly, so the event promises to be not just a get-together for Internet professionals. Here, by the way, I am PR: I will tell you about what benefits and how traditional companies can derive from social networks.

Of course, an important topic, as always in recent months, will be a crisis - no one is going to forget about it, on the contrary, the emphasis will be placed on ways to monetize Internet projects and increase profits from Internet activities for traditional companies.

Judging by the topics of reports and the companies represented in the list of participants, it will be interesting, so everyone who is in Novosibirsk or nearby and wants to listen and talk is welcome!
