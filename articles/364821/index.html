The first smartphone on Firefox OS is not for emerging markets - Fx0

On December 25, sales of the Fx0 smartphone with a transparent case, designed by Tokujin Yoshioka for the Japanese telecommunications company KDDI, will start in Japan. A mid-range smartphone with a 4-core Snapdragon processor, a 4.7 "inch screen and 16 GB of memory will cost 50,000 yen, or $ 420.



Specifications:
Display: 4.7
Processor: Snapdragon 400 SoC
OS: Firefox OS
Memory capacity: 16 GB
RAM: 1.5 GB
Headphone jack: 3.5 mm
Microsd
GPS
LTE

The new smartphone has received a transparent golden case, a button with the Firefox logo, an average Snapdragon 400 SoC processor, which consists of 4 Cortex-A7 cores, 16 GB of internal memory and 1.5 GB of RAM. The transparency of the case symbolizes the openness of the operating system. To look at Firefox OS 1.1, you can download its simulator for Mozilla. Produces a smartphone company LG.

Fx0 is the first smartphone on Firefox OS , not belonging to the lower price segment. Prior to this, this OS was used in smartphones worth up to $ 100-150, while Fx0 will start selling at 50,000 yen or $ 420. At the same time, this is the first smartphone on Fx0 with 4G support.
