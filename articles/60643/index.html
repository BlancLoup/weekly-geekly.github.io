Linux Mint 7 Gloria released

Today released a new release of Linux Mint 7 Gloria, a distribution based on Ubuntu 9.04 and updated user-friendly design and a modified set of programs. Distrowatch distribution page is the third most visited.



Linux Mint differs from other distributions by a low initial threshold of entry, the presence of codecs, flash and other proprietary parts in the distribution (in the normal version). The system is fully translated into Russian. It is developed by a very small number of guys in Ireland and, in fact, is Ubuntu with installed codecs, an unusual theme, and several tools specially written for it. The layout of the design elements resembles Windows, which facilitates the transition.
