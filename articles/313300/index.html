How to assess market share in the segment of mobile device users?

Many owners of online stores and other sites are faced with a choice: do an adaptive layout of the site for mobile devices or not, ask questions:

What about what numbers are we talking about?
How much extra profit will I get?
For how long will the adaptive version of the site pay off?

We also asked these same questions and could not find any open statistics on the Internet, visual indicators for which one can understand, and how many people search for my product or service through a mobile phone or tablet?

It is clear that the specificity of each direction is different, and the number of requests is not all that you need to know in order to estimate the profit from the mobile segment of users.

It would also be good for us to understand how the conversion will increase after connecting the mobile version of the site. After all, now your site is also displayed on mobiles, you can go to it and even successfully make an order, sometimes trying a lot.
