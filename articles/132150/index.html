In New York, built a 6-story Windows Phone 7.5

To support the sale of two new Windows Phone 7 Mango phones, Samsung Focus S and Samsung Focus Flash smartphones, the guys from Microsoft decided to do something original - to build a 6-story Windows Phone.

During the eight-hour show, each tile became truly alive, revealing the whole essence of the human-oriented interface of Windows Phone. So you could see the games in the Games Hub and listen to the musicians in the Music + Video Hub.

As for the phones, the Samsung Focus S and Samsung Focus Flash smartphones were the first AT & T operator devices running Windows Phone Mango. Model Samsung Focus S is equipped with a 4.3-inch touch screen Super AMOLED Plus, 1.4 GHz processor and 8 megapixel camera, and its thickness is only 8.55 mm. A Samsung Focus Flash smartphone is simpler and cheaper - it is a candybar without a keyboard with a 3.7-inch touchscreen Super AMOLED, 1.4 GHz processor and 5 megapixel. The declared value of $ 199 and $ 49, with the contract of course.





The main guest of the program was the Far East Movement group.


And for a snack, the construction of a 6-story Windows Phone in 60 seconds.
