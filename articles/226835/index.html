Gadgets that you wanted to take on vacation



June is nearing its end, which means that summer-vacation-vacation is in full swing, no matter what the thermometer shows. On this occasion, we have already published a selection of mobile applications that may be useful to travelers and just relaxing in nature. But applications are applications, and there are quite a few interesting gadgets in the world besides smartphones and tablets that can decorate a good rest. See our selection of gadgets for holidays and outdoor activities.

We will not touch smartphones and tablets. If you are reading this text, then you already belong to a layer of modern, educated people who keep up with the times. So you have a smartphone and a tablet, and certainly not one by one. We are sure that you will not forget to take them with you without our advice, otherwise what will you run applications from the above mentioned collection on ? Talk to other gadgets.

Action camera

Recently, action cameras have become very popular. These are very small compact video cameras which fasten on clothes or special fastenings and automatically remove everything that happens. This is useful if you are fond of active sports or extreme entertainment. Another possible application - as a kind of DVR, if you do not want or can not for some reason hold the camera in their hands.

For maximum coverage of space in such cameras use wide-angle lenses, therefore, closely spaced objects are greatly distorted. There are quite a lot of different accessories for such cameras: mounts on the helmet, extension handles for shooting selfies, underwater and protective boxes, etc.

The choice of models does not go far beyond all reasonable limits, but you can‚Äôt call it small either. The best samples shoot video with FullHD resolution and a frequency of 30 frames / sec. When choosing also recommend to pay attention to:
  • protection from moisture and dust;
  • fastening method;
  • the duration of the work;
  • weight;
  • availability of wireless broadcast on mobile devices;
  • proposed optional accessories.

Price range: 3000-17000 rubles.



Portable speaker

These gadgets are becoming increasingly popular. The days of the huge boombox recorders that black guys wore on the shoulder were music lovers in nature a long time ago. To provide a small company with music away from computers and music centers, today there is a pretty good portable speaker and a selection of your favorite music on your smartphone or tablet.

Some speakers compensate for their small size with built-in vibration dynamics to provide bass. It is enough to place the gadget on the table or stick it on the door, and these surfaces will perform the function of a peculiar sound amplifier. Larger portable speakers cost on their own, pretty well reproducing low frequencies.

When choosing, pay attention to:
  • the width of the reproduced range;
  • weight;
  • the size;
  • battery life;
  • dust and moisture protection.

Mobile router

If you do not spend your vacation completely separated from civilization, you have access to a cellular network and you need internet, then you may need a mobile router. A compact device that uses a SIM card and distributes traffic over Wi-Fi. The benefit here is in saving the charge of smartphones and tablets, as well as in the possibility of simultaneous connection to the Internet of several devices through a single SIM card. In addition, in the zone of unstable reception, you will not need to catch a stronger signal with your smartphone, it is enough to put or hang up a router here, and you yourself can comfortably be located nearby. But do not forget roaming does not sleep.

When choosing, pay attention to:
  • battery life;
  • the ability to use 4G networks.

Price range: 800-6000 rubles.

If you for some reason do not want to use a separate camera and prefer to shoot on a smartphone, then thanks to external optics you will be able to significantly improve the quality of photos. A great gadget not only for holidays, but also for everyday use in public transport. This does not mean that you find yourself in complete silence, but listening to music becomes much more comfortable.
