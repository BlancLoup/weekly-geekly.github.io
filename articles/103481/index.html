The world's first laptop with LTE support



Today at the IFA 2010 exhibition, Samsung showed the world new laptop models. An interesting model was N350, about the properties of work in LTE which was mentioned back in February. This netbook supports LTE and HSPA + standards. At the heart of the new product is likely to be one of the Intel Atom processor family. Monitor 10 inch.
The company also presented QX and RF series laptops, equipped with Nvidia video cards and enclosed in a metal case, at its booth. The RF series laptops use a GeForce 330 graphics card, a 640 GB hard drive, and a Blu-ray drive. In the US, prices start at $ 1,000.
