Yahoo! Net Profit decreased 2.5 times in 2006

Yahoo! Net Profit on GAAP in 2006 amounted to $ 751 million, a decrease of 2.5 times, compared with 2005 figures - 1.896 billion. In the 4th quarter of 2006, the company's net profit was $ 269 million compared to $ 683 million for the same period of 2005. .

Yahoo! revenue in 2006, it increased by 22% to $ 6.426 billion. Revenue, excluding Internet traffic costs, was $ 4.56 billion compared to $ 3.696 billion in 2005. Prime-TASS reported this with reference to the company's press service.
