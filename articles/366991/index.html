Currency and commodity markets


At the beginning of last week, the official dollar rate grew by 1.22 rubles - from April 28, the Central Bank set it at the level of 51.469 rubles (the euro rose to 55.8747 rubles). Subsequently, the exchange rate of the dollar rose above 52 rubles, and the euro exchange rate on Friday exceeded 57 rubles.

A supporting factor for the ruble was the decision of the Central Bank to lower the key rate to 12.5%. Within a minute after the appearance of this news, the dollar fell from 51.9 rubles to 51.18 rubles, and the euro - from 57.8 to 57.3 rubles.
