WebTotem or how we want to make the Internet safer

Free service to monitor and protect websites.



Idea


In 2017, our CARK team began developing a tool to monitor the entire cyber space in the national domain zone .KZ, and this was about 140,000 websites.

The task is complicated: it was necessary to quickly and very quickly check each site for the presence of traces of hacks and viruses on the site and display the status of the entire Kaznet in a convenient form of dashboards.

This is how WebTotem was born - Service for monitoring and protecting websites





We provide our solution to all website owners for free. We earn on solutions for corporate clients who need to monitor a large amount of resources (CERT, Hosting providers, etc.) and on additional services for website owners (Load testing, audits, outsourcing and help with the treatment of viruses, etc.).

WebTotem product


For 2 years, our system has grown to a full-fledged platform with 9 modules that are interesting to the ordinary site owner.

You simply register in the system and add your site for monitoring. If you want to install security modules, then download the script for the site (Currently, scripts for php sites are available in your account, a module for nginx is being tested)



Monitoring modules:

Monitoring accessibility during the day / week / month;
Checking the installed SSL certificate for validity and validity;
Collecting information about the platform on which the site works (CMS, OS version of the server, etc.);
Check the presence of deface on the site;
Site verification against reputation databases (VirusTotal, Spamhaus, etc)
Collection of information about the domain and the expiration date of its registration;
Open port scanner;

Protection Modules:

Antivirus - Search hacker shells and backdoors on the site
Web Application Firewall - a module designed to detect and block attacks on web applications
