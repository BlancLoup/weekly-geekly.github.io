Orion spacecraft successfully passed the landing test.

For example, it is on Orion that the astronauts will be delivered to an asteroid for its study. On the same ship, perhaps, people will fly to Mars (after the successful completion of the asteroid mission).

Now, the Orion prototype is undergoing a variety of tests, including testing of a parachute landing system.

The last test ended the other day, and everything went smoothly. The capsule was lifted on a C-17 military plane, to a height of 10 kilometers, and then dropped down (the test region is the Arizona desert). The free fall lasted about 10 seconds, after which the landing system was launched, using parachutes (2 auxiliary and 3 main).
