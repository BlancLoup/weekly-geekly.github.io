Vertical Video Syndrome

The massive proliferation of smartphones and tablets has led to the emergence and rapid spread throughout the world of a new disease of the 21st century.

Vertical Video Syndrome (Vertical Video Syndrome)


Every day, more and more people are shooting vertical video. This, of course, is not a crime, but such a video is actually terrible. Often, people do not think that it is one thing to take pictures in portrait orientation, which can be easily rotated as you like. Another thing video - turn which is impossible without loss. The result is a narrow strip of video with huge black bars on the sides.

The syndrome has already spread to all continents. To a greater extent, the beautiful half of humanity is subject to this disease, although it does not bypass men.
