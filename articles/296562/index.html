Cosmetic MIX from Kokoc Mobile

The ability to create your own cosmetic product gives anyone who wants an application developed by Kokoc Mobile (Kokoc Group mobile development unit) for the online laboratory MIXIT.



Thanks to a new application made in the form of a designer, the process of creating cosmetics (face, body or hair products) from several ingredients will take only a few minutes. For example, to make normal face skin cream with vitamin Shea butter, you can add vitamin E, aloe vera gel, lemon essential oil and put it in the ‚ÄúBasket‚Äù. Next, mix and shake the ingredients will be experts MIXIT.

True, you first need to pay for the order, for the convenience of users, the developers have implemented the possibility of buying with a mobile phone and credit card. You can get finished products anywhere in Russia by selecting the appropriate postmark of the Pickpoint delivery system. By the way, if your recipe is successful and your friends will definitely like it, in the near future you can share it using authorization via social networks.

Do not worry about the accuracy of proportions and the presence of ingredients: Kokoc Mobile synchronized the application with the internal server and the company's website. This allowed us to make the work of the designer as stable as possible and eliminate possible problems with the formation and ordering of products.

- Work on the application was an unusual creative experience. A peculiar challenge was the need to make it intuitive for ordinary users, given the large number of ingredients, including a description of the properties and a complex mechanism for creating a designer, - said Gregory Kharchenko, head of Kokoc Mobile (mobile division of the Kokoc Group).

The MIXIT application is available for iOS and Android users.
