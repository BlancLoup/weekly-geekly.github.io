Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet Update to Android 4.0.3

While Sony is updating its Xperia S , Lenovo decided to do the same this morning, but to version 4.0.3 with a kernel of 2.6.39 ( ThinkPadTablet_A400_03_0069_0121_ROW ).





Thus, Lenovo tablets will receive ICS earlier than Samsung tablets (July-August).
Subjectively, the animation has become smoother, work is faster and more reliable. In the market more applications became available, as well as Wi-Fi 802.11 n (!) Earned instead of 802.11 g .

So, dear owners, turn on Wi-Fi and begin to update, if you have not done so already.
