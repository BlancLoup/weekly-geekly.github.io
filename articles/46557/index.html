Morality


Going for interviews is necessary and important to know what happens to them and what to be ready for.
The environment is generally friendly. The tasks proposed at the interviews are not super complicated. It is enough to know the basic algorithms and be able to use them. Plus, try to think logically in the tasks for ingenuity.
Do not be afraid to communicate in a foreign language (I know, already tired of it).
As for offices, workplaces, working conditions, office response rate - everything is about the same everywhere. Somewhere a little better, somewhere a little worse.
There is an assumption that in the Moscow offices they are now recruiting people for specific tasks. They need ready profile specialists, but this is just my guess.
