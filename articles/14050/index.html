Alexey Gusev made a search engine for amateur photographers FotoGid.com

Khabrachelovek Alexey Gusev ( shikari ) from Novosibirsk has an unusual hobby: the creation of thematic search engines. He makes them from the Google Custom Search Engine Search Engine Designer, although alternative tools for creating personal search engines can be found on the Internet.

His new work - FotoGid.com , a specialized search engine designed for those who are either already keen on photography, or just intend to get a camera. FotoGid.com is looking for reviews, tests and detailed descriptions of various photographic equipment, camera characteristics, photography lessons, articles on processing and editing digital photos in RuNet. The search area also includes forums and blogs with reviews and discussions of photographic equipment.

The search engine searches for several dozens of sites with guaranteed high-quality content (no online stores, which are filled with Yandex and Google). Alexey Gusev personally collected these resources, and in the future the list may be updated with user suggestions.

This is the fifth thematic search engine Andrew. Here are the rest of the author.

Codavr.ru - Search Engine for Webmasters.
LJSearch.org - LiveJournal Search Engine.
Kinoved.com - Search engine for movie sites.
Articleum.com - Search engine for English-language archives of free articles.
