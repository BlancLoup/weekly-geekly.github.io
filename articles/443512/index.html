By the way, the guys from SIBUR talked about the tasks of the Hackathon in their article .

Specialists of the following profiles can take part in the hackathon:


  • Data engineer
  • Data Architect
  • Data scientist
  • Solution architect
  • Front-end developer
  • Back-end developer
  • UX / UI Designer
  • Product owner
  • Scrum master

Hackathon is divided into 4 stages:


  • Educational program
  • Mitap
  • Offline Hackathon Team Selection
  • Hackathon

In the framework of the first stage of the educational program, the participants registered for the hackathon have the opportunity to watch video tutorials and read articles specially prepared for them by the company's specialists. The lessons will help improve your skills and quickly create a convenient functional prototype on the hackathon. In addition, the participant can earn points for passing the tests after each lesson, which they can then exchange for the hackathon for various prizes. The scores of all team members are added up.


Mitap will take place on the 29th, one day before the hackathon. It will meet most of the participants, where they will be able to unite in teams, meet, ask questions to experts and begin serious preparations for work.


On the same day, the final teams of participants will be selected, who will be invited to the hackathon.


March 30-31, the hackathon will take place. By the way, about his program. Collection of participants begins at 9:00 on March 30. Already at 10:00, the event will be open and expert sessions, check points for checking decisions and other preparatory stages will begin.


The last check-point on the first day will take place at 21:00. After that, and until the morning, teams will have the right to work all night. The second day starts with charging at 8:30. At 11:00 the expert tube will be held, after which, at 14:30, the finalists will be announced - 10 teams that presented the best prototypes for the chosen one of two tasks. From 15:00 to 17:00 there will be performances of teams and summarizing the hakaton. At 17:00, guests will be waited for by the closing ceremony, awarding the winners and a buffet.
