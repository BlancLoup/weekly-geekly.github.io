Twitter blog do it yourself



Today I will describe how to make a small twitter blog from a favorite wordpress engine.
Advancing your own twitter blog:
  • The possibility of collective blogging
  • Flexibility - you are free to customize your microblog as you like
  • The ability to embed any media content, that is, you get your own full-fledged micro-blog


There is nothing special to explain, but I will add a small instruction:
  1. Install wordpress (in case you don‚Äôt have it)
  2. Download a special theme from the automattic command (I quickly localized it)
  3. Unpacks the theme to the wp-content\themes folder
  4. Activate the topic
  5. Enjoying :-)

This topic is an excellent visual aid for the capabilities of the WordPress engine. The developers even considered the ability to write to the blog from your favorite iPhone ;-)

The theme is nice and easily editable, for example, you can add a modest visual editor for posting media content.

I thought that many would be too lazy to install the theme just for the sake of seeing and I made a small test site, giving you a root :-)
