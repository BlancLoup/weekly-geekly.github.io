Become a hacker author and get an invite to the PHDays V forum



Like last year, the editorial board of Hacker invites its readers to turn into writers and to attend the Positive Hack Days conference for free. The five best materials that you send before May 15, 2015 will be published on the pages of the publication, and their authors will receive a fee, along with invitations to the forum.

In addition to the magazine‚Äôs editors, the materials will be evaluated by the program committee, which is responsible for Call for Papers on PHDays. The author of the most interesting research on the topics of computer forensics, practical hacks, attacks on web and corporate applications, new discoveries in the field of competitive intelligence, etc. will receive three invites to the forum at once, and the other participants will receive one invitation.

An article claiming to win the competition should demonstrate a practical approach to business and a truly hacker look at modern information technologies. And, of course, it must be unique. Send materials to goltsev@glc.ru . The maximum amount is 30 thousand characters with spaces.

The research of the contest winners will be published in the July and August issues of the magazine. For the fifth time, Hacker becomes the information partner of Positive Hack Days international forum on practical safety. This year the forum will be held on May 26 and 27 in the Moscow International Trade Center.
