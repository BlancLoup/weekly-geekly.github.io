The company Magma-Computer produces the program nanoCAD SPDS Construction site, allowing to develop sections POS (construction organization projects), PPR (projects of works), TC (flow charts), HDD (horizontal directional drilling). The program is constantly expanding the boundaries of its application. Currently, it works on any of the three platforms: nanoCAD, AutoCAD, ZWCad, which allows you to actively apply it throughout Russia, in Kazakhstan, Ukraine and other countries.

The program nanoCAD SPDS The construction site allows you to organize construction processes for a variety of objects: small buildings or structures; buildings designed in cramped urban environments; large-scale facilities, designed for several stages and construction phases. A tool such as a Project Manager will collect information from several dwg files and combine it into a well-defined hierarchy of works. Works are created by the user or taken from the provided bases of the GESN and ENiR, they are assigned such characteristics as completion dates, scope of work and units of measurement, number of workers required. Different tabs of the Project Manager use a single list of works, but they contain more detailed information about prices, equipment, building materials.

On the Technics tab, the machinery used is assigned to work in the Project Manager, and then placed on the drawings. Although it is possible, and vice versa, we first draw the construction plan to later assign equipment from the project to work. This relationship gives tangible advantages if necessary, for example, replace one tractor with another. The Replace Position for the entire project command reassigns this position in all works, and also replaces all graphic displays of this technique in all drawings and in all dwg-files related to this project.
