Python lessons from Google

A great way to learn Python is the Python training class at Google Code University. The lessons for this class were written by Nick Parlante from Stanford, and those who have completed the course not only celebrate the high, but the fantastically high quality of the lessons.

The course includes six video lectures on Youtube , where Nick conducts training for newcomers-googles and talks in detail about various tricks and features of Python. Some video lectures are up to 50 minutes long.

There are more downloadable puzzles . They are pretty simple and made especially for beginners. The course is ideal for learning Python by programmers who have experience with other programming languages.
