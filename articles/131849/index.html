Record of educational program on the Personal Data Protection Act

Today we held a webinar dedicated to the notorious Personal Data Protection Act (RF). The host of the webinar Yevgeny Tsarev (the author of more than 10 studies, 50 articles and 300 publications and comments in the media on information security) spoke about new issues in the legislation, and also answered many questions from participants.

Recording and presentation in the public domain are available here .

Main themes:
  • Changes in the law on personal data;
  • Principles of personal data processing;
  • The duties of the operator in the collection of personal data;
  • Measures to ensure the security of personal data during their processing;
  • Processing of personal data using automation tools and without them;
  • Measures to bring the organization in accordance with the requirements of the Federal Law-152;
  • Licensing of personal data protection activities;
  • Responsibility for the protection of personal data.
