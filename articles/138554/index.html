In part, I have already explained why I took the word autoregistrar in quotes - the device, thanks to the additional options that come with the basic package, has much more features than ordinary autoregistrars.

Now about a set of additional options that expand the capabilities of the device even more.
In my case, it was a kit of the Action Kit, which includes three options - mount on the helmet, bike carrier (it also allows you to mount the recorder to any pipe) and hermobox. Options for such additional. There are several sets for every taste.



Mount on the helmet will allow you to use the recorder when doing winter sports, you can mount it on a helmet or on a mask when playing paintball. In this way, you will be able to carry out ‚Äúoperational shooting‚Äù while taking up your hobbies, but not hold your hands, which can be very relevant.
I have been playing role-playing games for a long time, playing paintball and I have many friends who are interested in airsoft - I think that many of them would not have refused the opportunity to conveniently record games and competitions in which they participate.

The bike carrier is an adjustable clamp and fasteners for the recorder, so it can be used both on bicycles and, possibly, on motorcycles or scooters. Since driving a motorcycle is much more dangerous for a driver than driving a car, this option may not be superfluous.

The third option in the optional kit is a sealed box that allows you to shoot under water at a depth of 20 meters or simply in high humidity conditions, controlling the camera using the remote control.
Separately, the remote control is a radio control that allows you to control the recording when the device is not in direct line of sight.

In the basic package, as indicated above, there is not only a charger, but also a sync cable, as well as an AV cable for displaying images on a TV, which will allow you to share your impressions with relatives and friends anywhere, not just where there is a computer .
