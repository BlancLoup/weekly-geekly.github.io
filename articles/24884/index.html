Why use clones?

There are many successful and really necessary Internet services. There are countless different clones of them. Often the quality of clones is below the level of the leader, or at best no worse than him . But almost always clones contain nothing fundamentally new.

The question is, why do people use them?

Why clones produce is understandable - I want to bite off my piece of cake by doing what a successful neighbor does. In the real world, this is justified, since there is a geography factor, but it is not on the Internet!

I myself am an entrepreneur, so the word ‚Äústartup‚Äù is a way of life for me, but a startup is a strife. In my opinion, doing the same thing that a neighbor already has is a universal evil! This is a waste of their own and others' resources, this is the smearing of the market, this is the under-received profit for all its parties, this is chaos in the camp of users. There are a huge number of unresolved issues, why waste your energy on repeating an existing one?

Why do you need several social networks with the same functionality? Why do several dating services do the same thing? Why a whole lot of video and photohosting? It complicates life! This brings additional costs for the maintenance of several accounts for the author of the content, for the search for information (of the same information!) In several sources for the users of this content.

In my opinion, you need to think three times before doing a mass service, and at least twice to think before using clone services. Why mock listeners \ readers \ viewers, especially making it difficult to access their information?
