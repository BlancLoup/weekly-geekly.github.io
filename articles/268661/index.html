Hackathon VKontakte October 31



We invite everyone to take part in the open data hackathon provided by the VKontakte API and partner companies. It will be held on October 31 in St. Petersburg.

Team registration will begin October 31 at 9:00. At 10:30, the official opening of the event will take place, and at 11:00 you can start writing code. In a day, on November 1, at 11:00, you will need to complete the development of your ingenious prototypes. In two hours, at 13:00, the projects will be presented. Each team within 5 minutes will be able to talk about the possibilities and advantages of their development.

The hackathon jury will consist of VKontakte employees and representatives of partner companies.

During the hackathon there will be breaks for lunch, dinner, breakfast and coffee breaks. Meals will be provided by the organizers for free. The location of the hackathon will be announced later.

You can apply for participation through a message to a special bot vk.com/hackaween_reg .
