In general, the zuckerberg region works

Before the trip, we set goals to personally meet and, distracting from domestic problems, to work productively. All three goals are more than achieved.
The prototype of its own platform is undergoing refactoring, there are more than 1000 registered users on the site, excellent partners from Moldova are introducing our service, a clearer vision of its development has appeared.
The absence of the need to solve culinary, transport and domestic issues made it possible to concentrate more fully on the project. Sea, beautiful city, sunny weather - inspired.
All met and became friends.
Now even being in different cities, offices, houses, apartments, countries, our product plus Skype allows us to refine the ideas that have arisen in the joint work, to solve new problems.
We are not familiar with Zuckerberg and his methods, we have our own. But to everyone who has ever thought of getting a team somewhere in warm places to dive into the project, we advise you not to doubt. It really works.
