GDG DevFest Nizhny Novgorod 2015

Hi, Habrahabr! The DevFest conference has been held for several years in a row in different cities of Russia and gathers a large number of participants: developers, IT specialists, IT students, faculty, everyone who is interested in Google technologies.



This year we have already held DevFest in Voronezh (the report will be published soon), and the November gathering of Google technology enthusiasts will be held in Nizhny Novgorod. For the IT sphere of this city, DevFest will be a unique event. Firstly, in Nizhny Novgorod, we meet for the first time, and secondly, among the speakers are not only local speakers, but also reputable experts from other cities.


Florstein, Kornileva, NickSharov, KKotyusova, AlexTref871, Bestalex - Own work CC BY-SA 4.0

The event will be held on Friday, November 13 at the address: Nizhny Novgorod, St. George's Congress, 3, hotel complex "Alexander Garden" .



Attention: the event is free, but pre-registration is required. Limited number of seats.
