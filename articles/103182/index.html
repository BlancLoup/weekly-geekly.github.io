Microsoft introduced the new game controller for the Xbox 360



Yes, this is so, gamers really will have at their disposal a new controller for the Xbox 360 game console, however, this will happen only in November. Now the gaming public shows the controller itself, which is really changed. For example, the developers finally paid attention to the D-pad, changing it so that it became more convenient. But of course, the main change that users can see right away is the changed design. Instead of white and colored buttons, we now have a grayish joystick with buttons colored in different gradations of gray.

But most of all players should be pleased with the new D-pad, on which the developers have worked hard. By the way, "Major Nelson", the head of one of the branches of Xbox Live, first introduced this controller in his blog and posted a video. So, he believes, and rightly so, that users really dislike the D-pad, and it was on him that the developers worked the most.
