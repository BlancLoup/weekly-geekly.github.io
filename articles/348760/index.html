Shadow ban and what it eats

Many of the game developers reddit.com is known as a good means to advertise their game, but not many people know that modern reddit, as well as some other sites, can play a pretty bad joke with them, which eventually results in a lot of lost time. Even if you have never intended to use reddit, but periodically write on the forums, I advise you to read this article in order not to get into the annoying situation in which I found myself.

I created my account on reddit.com about 2 years ago, post it periodically but then for a long time I did not use it. Some time ago I wrote a few topics, after which I periodically answered all sorts of different questions in the subdits / r / gamedev / r / unity3d.

In recent days, a suspicion began to emerge in me: none of my posts received any answers. Neither the topics nor the answers on the forums caused any attention from other reddit users. In theory, it is, of course, interesting, but as shown by my own experience, it can simply make an idiot out of a man.
