The ten millionth e-book downloaded in the store Sony

Sony announced yesterday that a ten-millionth e-book was downloaded from its Reader Store. The anniversary purchase took place on May 5th, three and a half years after the store opened in September 2006. Sony decided to mark this momentous event with the distribution of $ 10 to everyone who bought a book on the day in the Reader Store. A 10 millionth purchase for Sony is a great achievement. Especially compared to the tablet Apple. Let me remind you that in the month since the iPad was released, iBookstore has downloaded one and a half million books - and this is despite the fact that Apple‚Äôs books are read only on one device and that representatives from one country, the USA, downloaded the books.
