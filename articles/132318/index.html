Facebook flight search app



The Russian search service Jizo.ru has announced the world's first Facebook application for finding airline tickets Jizo Flight Finder.

By installing the application from the Jizo.ru page , each user will be able to see where his acquaintances and friends are now and how much the air ticket will cost to them, as in the picture above. The service automatically calculates the best price for a single ticket per adult passenger.

This application is clearly worth it to install. It clearly demonstrates, firstly, where your facebook friends are - for me, for example, the location of some of my friends was a surprise, and secondly, that getting to them is not that expensive or difficult.
