[Peter] Meeting JUG.ru with Venkat Subramaniam - Design Patterns in the Light of Lambda Expressions

On Thursday, October 18, the day before Joker 2018, in the Petersburg office of Oracle, JUG.ru will meet with Venkat Subramaniam, the world-famous Java enthusiast, Java champion, famous speaker, consultant and most popular book author. on programming. Registration and information about the speaker - under the cut.




Design patterns are one of the central ideas in object-oriented programming. As soon as lambda appeared in the Java language, there was a reason to think about their influence on the design patterns. On this Juga, we will look at some sample patterns and rework them using lambdas. In addition, we will get acquainted with less popular, but no less interesting patterns, in which lambdas can be successfully applied.


Joug will be held in English.


Venkat Subramaniam is a world-renowned Java enthusiast, Java champion, well-known speaker, consultant and most popular author of books on programming and development techniques, a teacher at the University of Houston. He trained and trained thousands of developers in the USA, Canada, Europe and Asia. Welcome speaker at JUGs and conferences around the world. Author of many technical books . Twitter - @venkat_s . You can also read our interview with Venkat.


Participation is free, registration is required. You can register here .
