New Russian Internet TV has appeared in Runet - Teledu.ru

On August 2, 2007, the company SIMPALS launched Internet TV Teledu.ru .

Teledu.ru is a full-fledged television in the Internet space, which allows viewers to create their own television channels from personal video.

In addition to posting videos, you have a unique opportunity to broadcast live from home webcams and discuss live broadcasts in chat. And all this is free (only domestic Russian Internet traffic is used).

Teledu.ru is a Runet screen, on which you will see the best domestic video, TV channels, live broadcasts of interesting events.

The concept of New Internet TV provides for ease of navigation, accessibility and various themes of commercials. The main principle of Teledu.ru is that you only see what you like.

Teledu.ru is a television made by your hands.
