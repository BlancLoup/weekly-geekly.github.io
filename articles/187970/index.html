New features Nekaka, hacks and bonuses

For a long time we did not write anything about the project, waited for the completion of the new functionality. As usual, it became more interesting, in addition, a freebie was added. But first things first.

Sync with ...


We believe that it is convenient to store files in one place, but to duplicate them in several storages, while having a single interface for accessing files, is even better. Therefore, we added a completely new feature - synchronization with folders from other file storages and social networks. Of course, someone will say that it was already with Dropbox, but we redid it, improving it and adding it (there are no more file size limits). So, meet the sync with:
  • Google Drive - complete 2-way synchronization of files and folders, as well as the export of Google documents
  • Dropbox - full synchronization, as it was, but we fixed a lot and removed the limit on the size of synchronized files
  • Facebook - now you can download and upload photos and videos to this social network *
  • Vkontakte - the ability to synchronize photos and documents *

* A small note, the API of both social networks does not allow to delete photos, and Facebook also changes the description to them.


Interesting opportunities


I hope you know that you can upload and download files from nekaka.com not only through the site, but also through the Windows client , as well as using any WebDAV or FTP client. You can also upload files by sending them an email to an email address. In addition, you can download anything through the built-in torrent client at speeds up to 1 GBit / s.

And what can it give us?

And this, for example, gives you the opportunity to upload photos to Facebook and Vkontakte through the Windows client Nekaki or download all photos and videos from social networks via FTP or WebDAV .
There was no client for Google Drive, Dropbox or Facebook on your phone, but do you want to upload new photos from the holiday right now? It does not matter, send them to a pre- generated email address, and in the subject specify the name of the folder associated with one of your social networks and the photos will soon be uploaded to your photo album. This album can be shared not only with friends, but also with any person by sharing a folder and sharing a link with it.
I think you will find many more options for uploading, storing and sharing files with friends. Soon we want to add more social networks and expand the possibilities of interaction with them. Maybe you already have ideas? Write in the comments or on panic@nekaka.com and we will definitely consider, and perhaps encourage, a good idea.

Freebie



And now about the free place to store your files. We have created a simple invitation system that will double (and possibly more) increase the storage capacity. At the same time you get not a fixed increase, but 5% of the account size. Let us give an example to make it clearer: you recently purchased a premium account and got 100GB of space, invited your friends, who after registration received 5% of the free account (approximately 500MB), and you get 5% of their 100GB, that is + 5GB. Not bad, right? Just do not try to register not real accounts, most likely such cheating will lead to blocking or mutual reduction of available space.
