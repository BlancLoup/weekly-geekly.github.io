Which suitcase to take?

Choose a bag or suitcase based on the duration of the trips and the purpose for which you are traveling. There will be meetings - there will be a suit, shoes, shirts, a belt.

Collection method

In order not to forget anything that I may need, I am going like this: I imagine my day from morning to evening.
And it turns out quite a logical scheme:
  • Morning exercise puts in a suitcase pastes, brushes, combs, shampoos, underwear and more.
Do not take into the aircraft capacity larger than 250 ml. Stock up on plastic bags with a lock (ziplock bag) - in the same Germany, they can find fault with the lack of them, and there will be no place to take them.
Also, do not forget to put in a computer bag what you might suddenly need in flight - medicine, a comb, etc.
  • The working day puts suit, shirts, belts and shoes in the bag.
Take shirts and trousers with a reserve - a cup of coffee spilled over in the morning can always happen. It is better not to forget that the belt should be combined with shoes; especially abroad will pay attention to it. And yet - do not wear white socks with anything other than sportswear.
