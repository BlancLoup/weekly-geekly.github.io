LUXA2 H1 - Phone Stand

LUXA2 is a division of the well-known company Thermaltake , established in 2009. The main directions in the design of LUXA2 are simple shapes with elements of luxury. In the assortment you can find: cases for media centers, phone coasters, cooling coasters for laptops, docking stations and many other pleasant things.
It will be a question today, about one rather convenient and elegant product, a stand for a phone.



Aluminum was chosen as the material from which the accessory is made.
The system itself holding the phone is quite flexible in setting and has rubber feet.


Swarovski crystals (CRYSTALLIZEDTM Swarovski Elements) were used for greater appeal.


By the way, the stand is suitable even if you, for example, do not have an iPhone.


In general, there remains a very positive impression of the experience of communicating with the stand. It will look very aesthetically pleasing on any table.
Usually, many people pitch the field when they come to work and sitting at the table, the phone is automatically thrown wherever it is under the monitor or where it goes. And here there is a convenient stand and your phone is easily accessible and will not be scratched once again.
