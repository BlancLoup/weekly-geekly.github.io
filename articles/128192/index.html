Free book "Best of Smashing Magazine"

September 8 marks five years of Smashing Magazine, an online magazine popular among designers and developers. Its creators honestly admit that when they first started, they did not plan to turn their blog into an online publication known to the whole world. The secret of the success of their magazine, they consider the desire to do something useful not only for themselves but also for others.

In honor of its fifth anniversary, Smashing Magazine made a gift to its readers - a free anniversary book “Best of Smashing Magazine”.

The book is available in three formats: PDF, ePUB and Mobipocket.

Apple's iTunes Store
ZIP archive on the server Smashing Magazine (.zip, 55 Mb).


The book contains the best articles published on Smashing Magazine for five years, that is, the most useful, most interesting and most inspiring publications. Articles have been carefully edited for more convenient reading from book readers.

The book discusses usability issues, principles of effective design, beautiful typography and modern trends in web design. In addition, you will learn how to master the professional techniques of working with Photoshop. If you never thought that harmony is possible between developers and designers, then you definitely need to read this book, it shows how these two camps can work together productively, complement and improve each other.

The book will be of interest to both gurus and beginners in the world of design or development.
