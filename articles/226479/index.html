Blackphone, protected from listening to the smartphone, will go on sale in July



Earlier this year, Habr√© published information about the first smartphone, the most protected from listening. The device is called Blackphone, and it was developed by the cryptographic company Silent Circle in conjunction with Geeksphone.

This device has built-in cryptographic protection, the phone works under PrivatOS, a special Android fork. At the same time, the device is not tied to either a specific telecom operator or the manufacturer. True, the problem here is - in order to send someone an encrypted message, this someone must use a similar application, Silent Circle. When buying a Blackphone, the buyer gets a 2-year subscription to the application, plus the right to distribute 3 copies of the same application, with a one-year subscription, to friends or family members.

In other words, encryption can also be used when communicating with users who have different phone models.

In addition to communication, the phone allows you to perform an anonymous search on the web, as well as block connections to untrusted access points.

The device features quite interesting: NVIDIA Tegra 4i platform (4 cores, 2 GHz), 1 GB of RAM, 16 GB of internal memory, WiFI, Bleutooth, GSM, HSPA +, 4.7-inch display with a resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels, battery for 2000 mAh

The price of the device is 629 US dollars.
