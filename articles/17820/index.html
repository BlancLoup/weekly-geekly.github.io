Linux demoscene

Today I walked through my file system and went into the directory "/ mnt / one / etc / demoscene /". As a result, having looked at several works, I decided to write about them (and then more about several ^ _ ^) on Habr. Of course, there are many times more jobs that are directly related to the demoscene than this modest list. Even if you take the ones that I looked at and evaluated, the ones described here are just a small part. But nevertheless I tried to bring in the brightest works for me. I hope you will like it. :)


demo

^ _ ^
Space Delirium by Syn [Rj]
Smoother, yes, even smoother! Yes ... this demo just slips on the mind. I do not want to say that the rest slows down - it is not. It's just that in other works the movements are usually much sharper, but here they are slow and predictable.
New world by rebels
Another very nice demo with very beautiful special effects. It all reminds me of summer personally. Actually, a positive rain is included. I'm serious. ^ _ ^
cold by adhoc & kakmonsterklubben
This work is to some extent the opposite of the previous two. On the one hand, it is very cold (it is called: cold). with ice and snow - unlike New World. On the other hand, this work is distinguished from Space Delirium by abrupt, unpredictable movements. Nevertheless, I can‚Äôt say anything bad about this work - it‚Äôs done very well and, I think, I should like it, especially under the appropriate mood.
Stercus Accidit by Fit
Very nice work. There are no cool 3D effects here, but there is very beautiful heavy music (metal is very rarely found in demos - something much more electronic is usually used) and the statements in Latin, to my great regret, are incomprehensible to me, but somehow beautiful .
wedtro by Limp Ninja
What is the best wedding gift? Wedtro, yes! I would like to get such a wedding if I have one. Well, this wedtro was made for mlein and sharky. Congratulations to them! Moreover, the demo turned out very nice and positive.
8-Bit Wonderland by Kakiarts
The most ridiculous, in my opinion, demo, from me seen. Personally, I look at her with a smile from beginning to end. A bunch of hints on old games, such as Mario, Tetris, even Snake - on games, for which many spent dozens, hundreds of hours. There is a scene with advertising old computers - ZX Spectrum, Commodore and others.
XenomorphiC by RGBA
Stylish work with graphics, which in places strongly resembles some interesting computer game.
Rajut Hevit by wamma
Below is the intro, perfectly lulling people. This is quite the opposite: literally hurls the viewer's perception from side to side. As a result, it is very invigorating.

Sapph-Fire by 0x80
Beautiful special effects, various looming figures (some kind of Cthulhu, a coffee mug, a butterfly and others), synchronization with cool music ... 4 kibibytes. Even less - 4083 bytes. In general, I have no words, I can only say that the author (together with the author of the work below) is my god.
Townwoofer by lft
Quite awesome intro. From the thought of how much disk space it takes, hair all over the body stand on end. Very beautiful music and an impressive plot. In my opinion, it means the city, a sad day, bad weather, the corresponding mood. But it soon becomes clear that this cannot continue forever. And indeed, the sun comes out, a rainbow appears, the music becomes invigorating and joyful. That is, after the bad, good always follows, and that‚Äôs fine.

Zyberflux by Onetwenty
A dynamic game with good music and very beautiful graphics (except for little things like sprite trees ^ _ ^). I reminded me of Ballistics, but if you need to go there inside the tunnel, then here, on the contrary, you need to fly, "sitting" on one of the axes of a kind of light tube. You can shoot from opponents (or crash into them - this is how lucky).
Pogo Sticker by tAAt
Are you using Ion ? Long time removed the mouse to gather dust on the cabinet as useless? Get it! In Pogo Sticker, the mouse is the main and only control tool. Manage, in fact, to be one of two characters who have amazing skills in jumping. Pressing the mouse button is perceived as an increase in the force of the jump by one, and the deviation of the cursor from the character in one direction or another - as an inclination for this jump. You just have to jump all the time, but you shouldn‚Äôt hit your head - sometimes one time is enough to break.
