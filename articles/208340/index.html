CES2014: Transformer Laptop with Two OS from ASUS

In the follow up to the post about the Android desktop solution in the form of a monoblock ( Android on Android - upcoming trend? ) Another news from CES 2014. ASUS announced a laptop with two OS: Android and Windows.

Under the cut more.

Android 4.1 and Windows 8.1 are installed on the device at the same time. Switching between them is done by pressing one button, which is called Instant Switch. According to the statements, the device ‚Äútransforms‚Äù in just 4 seconds.


Specifications of ASUS Transformer Book Duet TD300:
* 13.3 "1080p IPS screen.
* Intel i7,4GB of DDR3L RAM and 128GB solid-state drive. Memory capacity can be added thanks to a docking keyboard with 1 TB HDD. In addition, the docking station contains two USB 2.0 ports, one USB 3.0 port, as well as Ethernet and HDMI connectors.
* Bluetooth 4.0 + EDR and Wi-Fi 802.11ac
* A microSD card reader, an audio jack for headphones, a front-facing camera and two speakers that ensure high sound quality thanks to the use of SonicMaster technology.
* A battery capacity of 38 Wh provides up to 5 hours of battery life in Windows 8.1 or up to 6 hours when using the Android platform.
* Weight - about 1.86 kg, which significantly exceeds the weight of most ultrabooks. The thickness of the body of the tablet is about 13 mm.

And yet, the price of the device, which is $ 599, will significantly reduce the fire of criticism of users about its some shortcomings. ASUS Transformer Book Duet TD300 will go on sale in Europe at the end of the first quarter, and in the US it should be expected a little later.
