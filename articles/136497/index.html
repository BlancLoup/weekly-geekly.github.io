Devconf 2012 is looking for speakers

DEVCONF 2012 invites speakers. To become a speaker, you must fill out the appropriate form .
A list of reports can be found here: http://devconf.ru/offers/ .
Unfortunately, I have not found direct links from the site. :(
According to the authors of the conference, participation for all speakers will be free. Moreover, nonresident rapporteurs will be compensated for travel and accommodation.
The conference will be held in June at the Izmailovo Hotel. Information for students is available here: devconf.ru/members

Disclaimer: I have no relation to the organizers of the conference, but this year I plan to make a presentation .

Ps. I'd add that the conference is still surprisingly cheap. I liked the past and the last one in quality. Its money is definitely worth it.
