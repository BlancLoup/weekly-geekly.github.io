He who seeks will always find, but not a job during a crisis :)

So it happened that at the very beginning of the crisis, I was at a fork. On the one hand, it is clear that I was mistaken with the current place of work, and that the work, though guiding, is essentially a monkey with a business card there . On the other hand, negotiations about a new interesting job - the head of the representative office of a small western IT company - come to the very finale. Well, yes, on the third hand there is a crisis. He sat, thought, remembered that there is still interest in my person from several companies.

And he came out, as it turned out, not at the door, but through the window :)



Foreigners with offer said sorry and moved aside.

Are there any vacancies?




Over the past 4 months, there was a mass of interest in my resume and in person. But mostly they are poor hungry and feral representatives of recruitment agencies, who have one open vacancy for several agencies and a passionate desire to work, as the payment is piecework.

Everything falls, maybe you need to temper ambitions?




Maybe you need to reassure your ambitions, and as a friend of HR advised me, look for a job two or two below what I did last time. It seems like the work of programmers and consultants is now there.

So does someone get a job?
