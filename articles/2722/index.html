PHPConf 2007: new topics of reports, abstracts registration extended until February 5

Preparations for the 6th conference of leading web developers PHPConf 2007 (Moscow, May 24-25, 2007) are actively continuing.

Due to the large flow of interesting reports, the reception of abstracts has been extended until February 5.

New Theses:
  • The practice of independent WEB-development (freelance)
  • Benefits of using LDAP services in the enterprise
  • Aspect-oriented programming (AOP) in PHP
  • Managing dependencies in the program code



The practice of independent WEB-development (freelance)

The report plans to summarize personal experience and conduct a survey study of the phenomenon of independent WEB-development (freelance) in Russia. The main landmark is not the combination of freelancing with the main work full time, but a completely independent development.

Key milestones of the report:
  • Attempt classification: full time full time and remotely, partial freelance, fully independent development.
