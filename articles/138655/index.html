Speech by Sergei Belousov in Chelyabinsk

Attention to Ural startups!



On February 25 and 26, the Ural Web Developer Conference (UWDC), the largest event of the year for the Ural Internet community, will be held in Chelyabinsk. On the 26th, at 5:30 pm, you can attend the performance of Sergey Belousov .

Since 2010, UWDC has been gathering web-studios, free professionals, owners of large projects and startups. This year, 50 speakers and 500 participants will discuss new Internet projects that have appeared over the past year and the latest technologies.

More information about the conference program can be found here . But that is not all. This year there will be two events at UWDC that were not there before and for which it is worth attending the conference.

First, it is StartupDay-Chelyabinsk, which will feature prominent figures from the web industry: Leonid Volkov , Alexey Kosarev , Dmitry Kalaev and Evgeny Sharovarin . Secondly, this is the performance of Sergey Belousov for the smartest startups - " Smart money for smart startups ".

Sergey Belousov is a successful entrepreneur and manager with 19 years of experience in creating, developing and managing international IT companies in North America, Europe and Asia. Sergey is known for his innovative activities in the field of virtualization, cloud computing and data center automation and often speaks at industry conferences around the world.

In 2011, Sergey entered the top 25 must know Russians according to the Financial Times version, the top 5 most successful Russian businessmen who made international businesses according to Forbes. Currently Sergey is a senior partner of Runa Capital - an early stage international venture capital fund.

Runa Capital invests in technology start-ups in the fast-growing segments of cloud computing, the Internet and mobile applications, virtualization. The main goal of the fund is to help talented teams to build large global businesses, applying the expertise of their partners in the creation and scaling of technology companies. It is not difficult to guess why Belousov comes to UWDC and StartupDay.

At the same time, Sergey is the founder, chairman of the board of directors and chief architect of Parallels (world leaders in the field of developing software solutions for virtualization and automation), founder of Acumatica, Acronis, Rolsen and more than 20 other technology companies.

Founders of startups, managers and investors are invited to the presentation of Belousov. The most important issues for entrepreneurs will be discussed:
  • What is the difference between approaches to entrepreneurship in Russia and abroad?
  • How to use global trends when creating an IT company?
  • How to bring a company and a product to the international market?
  • How to build a strong team and lead it to success?
  • How to find the right investor?
  • How to get investment venture fund Runa Capital?
