More about the first seminar


The Postgresmen company organizes a series of seminars on the use of PostgreSQL DBMS. On September 19, the first seminar of the series, ‚ÄúUsing PostgreSQL. Features of PostgreSQL in conjunction with 1C: Enterprise ". Duration: 8 hours.

The seminar will be conducted by well-known PostgreSQL specialists, members of the PostgreSQL Global Development Group, developers of a large number of popular PostgreSQL extensions (including PostgreSQL patches used in 1C: Enterprise) Oleg Bartunov and Fedor Sigaev.

Brief program of the seminar:

* Introduction to PostgreSQL, an overview of the features.
* PostgreSQL server installation and initial configuration. The basic principles of tuning the system.
* PostgreSQL and 1C: Enterprise.
* Overview of the main extensions PostgreSQL.
* Tools to work with PostgreSQL (administration, monitoring).

The seminar is designed for database administrators and 1C administrators who are beginning to use PostgreSQL or are interested in switching to Linux / PostgreSQL. In addition to these topics, students will be able to find answers to their questions and get information "first-hand."

Registration is required to attend the seminar. Participation fee: 5000 rub. until September 15, further - 6000 rubles. Email for applications: info@postgresmen.ru.
