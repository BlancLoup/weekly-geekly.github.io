Apple has become more expensive Microsoft

Today something happened that fifteen years ago it would have been difficult to even imagine - Apple overtook Microsoft in market value. The gap today was $ 2.89 billion.

Not all resources have this information still updated, but the situation can be monitored on the pages of Google Fincance or Yahoo Finance .

This is not a victory over Microsoft, of course, since the securities market is very unpredictable and after a week Microsoft can return to the position of the most expensive IT company in the world.

In addition, only market capitalization outside the context says little. It is a mistake to assume that a company is considered better or worse from this. The apple company still needs to overtake a competitor in such important indicators as net profit, the amount of finance available for costs and growth stability. And amid the appearance of news about investigations , courts and lawsuits in the direction of the company, the attractiveness of buying stocks is lower than apples would like.

From the interesting facts:
  • Microsoft spends about eight times more money on research and development than Apple - $ 8.6 billion - $ 1.1 billion.
  • Google also spends on R & D more than Apple - $ 2.8 billion.
  • Microsoft has more assets - $ 82 billion versus $ 53 from Apple.
  • If five years ago I invested $ 2,000 in Apple stock, now I would have $ 12,000 in my hands. This is $ 10,000 profit, excluding taxes and commission brokers.
  • In 1997, Microsoft gave Apple $ 150 million, now Apple has no debts.

Let's see if the championship will be fixed.

UPD 23:45 : While writing, the gap was reduced from six to five billion dollars.
