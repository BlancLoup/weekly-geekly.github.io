Top 3D Expo 2017 held



That was the second annual conference Top 3D Expo.



On April 14, 2017, in the DI Telegraph metropolitan coworking met the manufacturers and professional users of 3D equipment and consumables for it, as well as people interested in new products of technical progress in this area.



The exposition presented interesting samples of 3D printers and 3D scanners of various types and purposes, materials for 3D printing, both FDM and photopolymer, as well as milling machines and engravers with program control.



All equipment could be observed in action. For the first time, the newest Picaso 3D Designer X Pro was publicly demonstrated. You can learn about its structure and advantages from the presentation below.



The invited lecturers spoke about their work: the creation of 3D-technology, its application in dentistry and other fields, the modern community of 3D-makers and its development. Many interesting topics were touched upon and uncovered.



Here you can find some of the presentation materials that the speakers used in their speeches:

Vasily Kiselev: Key trends in the development of 3D technology



Alexey Schetkin: Affordable digital production



New approach to prototyping from Roland



PICASO 3D patents the future of FDM printing



REC - a story about the company, its materials for printing and their production



IMPRINTA - on improving the print quality of large objects



Range Vision talks in detail about using 3D scanners.



Sergey Pushkin, founder of 3DToday, an information resource and social service for 3D printers, told about the company's concept and the constant growth of the community.

A master class on 3D printing and scanning took place with a full house - not everyone could immediately find a place in the audience, they had to take a turn.



The conference was successful and extremely productive - someone found new business partners, someone decided on what kind of equipment would be suitable for achieving his goals and solving problems, others just had an interesting and useful time.



We will continue the annual conference next year.



Top 3D Expo 2018, the third annual exhibition and conference, promises to be even more interesting and meaningful. We will not yet disclose plans, but preparations for it begin now.



Thank you all for participating! And, of course, come to us again. And we will please you with the latest innovations and interesting speeches by professionals.
