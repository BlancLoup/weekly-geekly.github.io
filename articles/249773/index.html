DevCon 2015: announcement of the first speakers and conference reports

Dear friends!

We are glad to present you the first wave of the speakers of the DevCon 2015 conference and to open you some topics and details of the technical program of the conference.



First of all, let me introduce Robert Eicheneer and Mario Shpusht - our guests from Microsoft offices in Germany and the USA. Robert and Mario will bring the latest news and innovations of Microsoft for developers to Moscow, and as part of their reports, as well as deep master classes, they will share the following topics with you: Internet of Things, .NET 5 Core, ASP.NET 5, Hybrid Cloud Architecture making.

The next guest, Alan Mendelevich, is the world-famous author and director of the AdDuplex project. Alan will speak at DevCon 2015 with guidance on the promotion and monetization of mobile Windows applications.

Another expert in mobile application development at DevCon 2015 was Mikhail Samarin - our guest from Finland, who worked closely with the Nokia and Microsoft teams and has extensive experience in creating various mobile applications. Mikhail will tell the participant the most interesting details of new Windows technologies.

Another of our guests is our old friend Georgy Mogelashvili , who today works for the well-known company Booking.com. At the conference, Georgy will tell interesting details about the convenient development and A / B testing of cloud websites.

We are pleased to welcome Sergey Zvezdin , a veteran of development and one of the most experienced and well-known speakers at Microsoft conferences in Russia. Sergey will bring to the conference a number of reports on the hottest topics of web development and ASP.NET.

One of the most interesting and original reports you can expect the report of Alexander Kirsanov , who will tell about reactive programming in robotics and work in real projects with Kinect sensors.

And finally, I am very pleased to present my fellow application development experts from Russian Microsoft, who will share with you the latest announcements and innovations of the company's technology: Dmitry Soshnikov will tell you about Cortana and voice input, Dmitry Andreev - about the latest DirectX 12, Alexander Belotserkovsky - about modern technologies of the Internet of things and I, Vladimir Yunev, will tell you about the latest services and components of the Azure cloud, which will help make your applications faster, more reliable and safer.
