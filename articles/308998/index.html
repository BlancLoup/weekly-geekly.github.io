Dropbox - stop displaying HTML content in the browser

This morning (09/01/2016) I received an email with the following content: 

, username!  ,       HTML         .         Dropbox    HTML  -,       . 
    ,           3  2016.         .        Dropbox.     Dropbox.  Dropbox
This is my translation from Ukrainian. Perhaps the Russian text is slightly different, but not the essence.

Strange, at first glance, the decision. But perhaps it has something to do with the recent data leak .

Personally, I, like hundreds of other users, have long loved the simple and convenient dropbox HTML-sharing as the best tool for displaying interactive 2D / 3D demos (for the Unity Assetstore, through the Unity Web Player) or even full-fledged 2D games - on WebGL, HTML5.
Example
3D demo - dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/96409476/antique%20walk/antique%20bild.html


Interestingly, I did not find this sad news on Dropbox blogs. Well, you have to look for an alternative.
