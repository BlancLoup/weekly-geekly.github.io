How was the usability conference UXPeople 2015

On June 5, the annual UXpeople 2015 conference took place in Moscow, dedicated to design, user interface design, user experience and usability. It brought together leaders, practitioners and masters of their craft to share their knowledge of user interfaces, the results of their work, and learn something new.




This year, the conference moved from Minsk to Moscow, to the event hall ‚ÄúInforostranstvo‚Äù, and brought together more than 300 designers, interface designers, usability specialists, product managers, marketers and programmers from the CIS and foreign countries to discuss new developments in the industry Ux

Starting with the morning coffee already traditional for such events, the participants had to make a choice as to what report they would like to hear. The speakers spoke in three streams, and each of the participants could choose what was more interesting for him.



At UXpeople 2015, 26 speakers delivered their reports. Ruslan Savvotin spoke about the topic of cheap usability testing. About his advantages, benefits, and an example showed how it can be quickly organized, and what inexpensive "tools" can be applied.



Viktor Ivanov spoke about solving non-standard UX tasks, and what problems they encountered when designing the interface for the self-service cash register.



The head of the team of design and interface design from Mail.ru, Yuri Vetrov, talked about platform thinking in large projects. About how we spend a lot of energy on the formation and support of artifacts, which blurs the main goal - the launch and development of the product itself.

The main feature, from year to year, is the opportunity to communicate with their ‚Äúpartners in the workshop‚Äù in a formal and informal setting. Such free communication helps to find a solution for small everyday problems, or to be inspired by new ideas.







No one can tell about the conference as fully as its organizers.

Sergey Polazhenko :
The conference turned out to be soulful, tastefully, largely due to the openness and constructiveness of the speakers, and a convenient conference venue. Information space was the right choice for the venue - it provided space and comfort. Traditionally it was difficult to choose reports when several interesting speakers spoke at the same time. But as in all the halls there was a recording of reports, this calmed the participants. Now the site has already launched a subscription to the notification of the appearance of video reports. In the future, we expect more international speakers to expand the geography of the conference, not only at the place of its holding.


In two years, UXpeople has transformed from a small meeting of like-minded people to a grand celebration, where you can meet not only colleagues, but also old friends. See you at this holiday next year!
