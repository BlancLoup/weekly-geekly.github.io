Spotify closed $ 526 million investment round

The Swedish telecommunications holding TeliaSonera will invest $ 115 million in the music streaming service Spotify and receive 1.4% of its shares. In addition, the companies plan to develop common advertising and media projects, as well as conduct joint marketing research. Spotify received a valuation of $ 8.53 billion. The deal is reported in a press release on the holding's website .

This is not the first transaction between the two companies. More than five years ago, they had already concluded a similar agreement.
