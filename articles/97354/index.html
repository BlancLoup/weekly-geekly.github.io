Toshiba CAMILEO BW10: shooting in any weather

In the line of Toshiba camcorders, a new interesting model has appeared, designed for people involved in sports, leading an active lifestyle and travelers - CAMILEO BW10. This device with a slim body and compact size is designed for shooting in extreme conditions. Toshiba CAMILEO BW10 will appear in Russia in the second half of 2010. In the near future will be available for testing, you can contact.





The camera is equipped with a detection function of up to 12 people in the frame, allowing you to take excellent portrait and group shots, aligning the tone of human skin. Moreover, the model offers a maximum sensitivity of ISO 1600, which allows you to take good pictures even in low light conditions.



The camera can work in bad weather or even under water at a depth of 2 meters. To test the performance of the model was immersed in an aquarium.



Snow however also did not affect the quality of work.



CAMILEO BW10 is available in three colors: silver, turquoise and yellow.
The camera is capable of shooting video in Full HD (1080p / 30fps), offering a built-in video stabilization function and a 10-fold approximation for shooting distant objects.
