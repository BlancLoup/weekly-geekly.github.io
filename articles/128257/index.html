The long-awaited version of ATP "Prav.ru" for Android

Great news for practicing lawyers with phones on the Android platform!



Today for them the version of the ATP Pravo.ru mobile application has finally been released. Now all codes, federal laws, decrees and other regulations are always at hand in a beautiful application with a user-friendly interface ...

... and as always free to download !



Application Overview



Android owners have access to all the same sections as iOS users : the Constitution, codes, federal legislation, decrees of the President, Government decrees, regulatory acts of ministries and departments, as well as letters and other official documents. Navigation through document categories is available from the main page. Here is the ‚ÄúUpdate‚Äù button, which launches the update of the document database.

Document search





If you need, for example, the law " On Police " - go to the section "Federal Laws" and enter in the search bar "about the police." The application issues the desired document and other legal acts, in which the desired words are found. Reviewed documents are marked to avoid reopening the same item in the list. Having opened the text of the desired document, you can quickly go to a specific article by dialing its number, or again, use the search in the text of the document.



Work with document





In addition to convenient document navigation, users can access such features as adding a part of the document to the Favorites, bookmarks, and sending the selected paragraph to the specified email box. Recall that the "Favorites" stores the document that you most often need when working, and the "bookmarks" help to mark the document for a quick transition through its parts. Information is available for each document: regulations that have made changes to the text being viewed, the date of adoption and the body that adopted it.

Epilogue


This is the first version of the ATP "Pravo.ru" for devices on the Android platform. We continue to work on the application and we welcome your feedback and suggestions on its work. Download the application and send emails to sps@pravo.ru
