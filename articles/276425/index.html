Analysis of hosters and their tariffs for virtual servers

Most recently, the number of hosters that are represented in the HOSTING.cafe catalog has exceeded 250, so I decided to do a study about hosters and their tariffs for virtual servers , by systematizing information by various parameters. New domain zones are used by only a few hosters: sinaro.host, lite.host, relcom.host, ua-hosting.company, hosting.energy.

Most of the payment methods (43) accept 3 hosters at once: xlho.st, hosting.energy, vpsdom.ru

The average number of payment methods per hosting provider is 17.11.

Price
The minimum price is 75 rubles (1 dollar) per month, but only if the one-time payment is paid for the year, the maximum is 72,165 rubles (960 dollars) . At the time of writing this article, 1,728 rates from 193 hosters are presented in the virtual server catalog. The total cost of all tariffs is 4 300 012 rubles, the average cost of the tariff is 2488 rubles.

Disk capacity
Minimum hosters offer 2 GB of disk , maximum - 4 TB . The total value of all tariffs - 150,428 GB, the average - 87 GB. The number of tariffs is greatly reduced with increasing hard disk space.

Thus, we can conclude that SSD drives have already fully reached the first place.



RAM
The minimum RAM offered by hosters is 128 MB. That is the volume of 10 tariffs, and the maximum amount - 128 GB, presented on 5 tariffs. The total volume for all tariffs is 8,130,096 MB, the average value is 4,704 MB.

CPU
The minimum tariff includes 256 MHz of the processor, the maximum value is 32 cores at 2200 MHz (coarse multiplication gives 70,400 MHz). The cumulative frequency is 11,999,312 MHz, the average value is 6944 MHz.

Traffic
740 tariffs have traffic restrictions - from 3 GB to 40 TB per month, the remaining 988 tariffs have no restrictions.

Port speed
The minimum port speed is 5 Mbit / s, the maximum - 10,000 Mbit / s. The average value is 489 Mbit / s.
