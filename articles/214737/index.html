RussianCodeCup 2014 - very soon!



Friends, our annual sports programming contest RussianCodeCup will begin very soon. This championship is just one of the events we hold to support young and talented developers (among them, the Russian DesignCup Contest for Designers; the Russian DevelopersCup Championship for mobile applications, games and Internet services, and the Russian AI Cup , in which participants compete in the ability to write artificial intelligence on the example of game strategies).

During the RCC, programmers have the opportunity to try their hand, get a unique experience and express themselves. In addition, prize-winners are encouraged with worthy cash prizes: $ 10, 5 and 3 thousand.

This year we will continue the tradition of inviting world-renowned experts to speak at the competition participants (last year, Edward Yordon , Dmitry Sklyarov , Ken Goldberg were our guests).

Event schedule
  • Start of preparation for the qualification rounds and applications for participation - March 23, 2014
  • I qualifying round - April 19
  • II qualifying round - May 18
  • III qualifying round - May 24
  • Qualifying stage - June 8
  • The finale is October. The exact date will be announced later.


On the conduct of the qualifying stage and the final of the championship, we will tell further.
