Bmorn V99 Tablet Review



Purpose of purchase

We decided to give tablets of our son's grandmothers so that they could always be caught in Skype and that they could read our private blog easily and naturally.

Grandfathers, of course, have already mastered computers for a long time, but grandmothers are a little afraid of such complex devices, and the tablet, nevertheless, is simpler.

We decided that for our purposes any Chinese device would be suitable. Moreover, the reviews on them, in general, are not bad.


Choice and price

The choice fell on MOMO11 Bird, which was immediately ordered on aliexpress.com. The purchase cost 237USD , of which:
198,99USD - the tablet itself,
20USD - charger, protective film and simple case (for some reason, the charger is not included in the base cost of the device),
23,08USD - delivery to Pattaya (we were in Thailand at the time),
-5USD is a coupon received when you register on AliExpress.

But soon the seller unsubscribed that the manufacturer had some problems with the screens on these devices and while they stopped sales. Instead offered Bmorn V99. According to the characteristics, he arranged for us, the price was the same as that of MOMO, and we agreed to a replacement.

Delivery

The order on the website was issued on February 28, on March 4 it was transferred to EMS, on the 8th it was already in Bangkok, and only on March 15 they brought a notice that you can pick it up at the Thailand Post central office in Pattaya.

The voids of the parcel box were filled with polystyrene, the box with the device itself was wrapped with 1 layer of pimply film. Everything arrived safely and securely, only one corner of the box stuck a bit.



Appearance and equipment





In the box lay:
- the tablet,
- HDMI cable (1.5 m),
- headphones,
- micro USB cable type B -> USB type A,
- adapter micro USB type B -> USB socket type A,
- stylus
- instruction and warranty card in Chinese,
- 220V charger + adapter under the "euro" (I asked about it in the comments to the order),
- protective film,
- cover.

The last 3 positions are not included in the basic package and are selected separately.

Externally, the tablet looks quite normal.

On the left side is empty.
