Aurora watch. Now with a laser

An interesting concept of the clock was presented by the designer Jihun Yeom. The watch does not have the usual dial. Quite the contrary. In this place there is a completely through hole. The time is indicated by two laser beams of red (minutes) and blue (hours).

In addition to the images, there is no specific information on how these watches will work. Although it would be very interesting how this watch will work on a hairy male hand. There are suspicions that you will need to shave a neat little circle on your hand in order to wear this designer product.

Also, apparently, the laser will be seen poorly at a right angle and you will have to tense up a bit to see the time.

It remains to hope that the amusing concept will not suffer the implementation, if it is, naturally.
