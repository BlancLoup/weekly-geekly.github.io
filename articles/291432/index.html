Mailboxes on pochta.ru will continue to work even after transferring to the control of the Post of Russia

Thanks to an agreement between RBC, QIP buyers and Russian Post, mailboxes on the poctha.ru domain will continue to work even after the domain is transferred to the control of Russian Post. RBC is selling the pochta.ru domain to the RF Postal Service, reports ROEM.

Note that the service "Mail QIP" until recently also belonged to RBC. March 15, this service was sold to shareholders Traffic.ru and ActionPay.

Thus, a trilateral agreement between RBC, the new owners of QIP and Mail of Russia, allows users to keep pochta.ru mailboxes in working order.
