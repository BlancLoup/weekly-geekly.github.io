Let's clean up the mess!

So, we gradually move to the next stage of the development of the project Tracksflow.com .
Now that we have provided our users with access to virtually all music and almost all the necessary accompanying information, provided an opportunity to communicate and learn how to deliver it all to mobile devices, we can say that we have achieved the necessary result from the initial stage of development. The project is growing quite well (adding ¬± 12% per week), people actually use the service, spend a lot of time, browse many pages, listen to a lot of music, we get a large number of ideas, wishes and bug reports from users. In general, it seems that we are doing something necessary.

But the quality of the information that we manage to collect on the Internet suits us only partly.
It would seem strange, but we could not find in the network a single resource, where all the music is properly cataloged and stored in perfect order. In the same last.fm , in the cards of artists very often there is a mess, I'm not talking about our Russian catalog. We receive metadata from other resources, we have agreed with Discogs.com , musicbrains.com , deezer.com . But this does not solve the problem. Therefore, the task - to restore order. We decided to enable our users to administer the pages of their favorite artists. We suggest you use Tracksflow.com , including, as such a kind of Wiki, a musical encyclopedia. We hope that this will be useful and in demand.

The mechanics are very simple - any user can send a request to manage the artist's page, including all the content that is associated with this artist. After moderation, the administrator gets the ability to create / delete albums. While this is the first steps in this direction, the functionality is limited. But in the future, and if necessary, we will certainly realize other possibilities.

To make it more interesting, we decided to show the administrator on the artist's page, it can be added. And we, in turn, will try to encourage our admins as much as possible.

See how it works, and, of course, we are waiting for your comments on this idea.
Today, invites, as always, are delivered very quickly to all those who have requested (request in person to any employee of the company, do not forget to include an email address in the message)
