CTC Media is filling its price: Usmanov will have to buy a company without the promised discount


Source: Reuters

In this regard, the owners agreed with the co-owner of UTV holding Alisher Usmanov and Ivan Tavrin about the sale of 75% of the operating business of STS Media at an initial price of $ 200 million, wrote Megamind in September.

However, it became known in the fall that the amount of the transaction under a conservative forecast could be reduced to $ 185 million.

The discount could have been caused by several factors, including the indicator of the audience share, the revenue of the flagship channel of the CTC and the cash flow (in the UTV it was thought that it could be negative). To a large extent, the low results of the work of CTC Media in Kazakhstan could have affected the amount of the discount.

In November, CTC Media announced a possible reduction of the discount to several million dollars, and by the end of December it became known that there would be no discount on business in Kazakhstan. Management made efforts to collect revenues and effectively controlled costs, which made it possible to fulfill the cash flow plan, explains the Kommersant interlocutor.

At the same time, in the first nine months of 2015, the net cash flow from the company's operating activities decreased from $ 55 million to $ 0.9 million, which was influenced by the volatility of exchange rates and the fall in the advertising market. The company's revenue in rubles decreased by 22%, to $ 14.7 billion, profit increased by 9%, to $ 3.5 billion.

The implication was that the discount would be possible if in the second half of 2015 the market fell the same way as in the first, but already at the conference call for the third quarter it was clear that CTC Media will show good results, said Raiffeisenbank analyst Sergey Libin. If the transaction goes along the upper boundary, the total price of the shares will be about 15% higher than the current one ($ 2.19 versus $ 1.86 per share), this is good news for sellers, he concludes.
