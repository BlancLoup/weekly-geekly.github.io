The possibilities of communication on the Internet are quite extensive. A certain classification of Internet communications has not yet been created, so I will try to create my own classification.

In my opinion it is necessary to divide the Internet communication into 2 large sections - internal and external communications. Internal - these are the ones that occur within your website (promotional website, corporate website, industry portal or online store). External communications that occur outside the Internet space, which belongs to the company, such as: blogs, social networks, news sites, industry portals, search engines, and more.

Internal Internet communications
For successful internal communications, you initially need to have a well-prepared site. During the development of the site, you definitely need to keep abreast, you need to understand all the advantages of your competitors, just look at foreign colleagues and perhaps come up with some original idea. From the general recommendations:

- News - if the company has news every half year, then it is better not to do such a section on the site, there will be only a negative effect from news of six months ago;

- Products / Portfolio catalog - the more thoughtful the given section is, the better the content, the more product items, the more information about the product (ideally prices, but actual!) - the better will be the attitude towards your company;

- Contacts, communication is also one of the most important points. If you have an email address - the answer must be within 24 hours. If a phone number is indicated, then it must be answered. Ideally, if there are more interactive forms of communication: icq (instant messaging), Skype (Internet telephony), FAQ (question-answer, created for frequently asked questions), a forum.

Also a good communication option is a company blog. In a blog, company employees write various notes, articles on the company, there may also be unofficial information, such as corporate photos.
