FlickrSlidr - slideshow on your site (blog)



A new service has appeared that allows users to publish a beautiful slide show on their resource using the photo bank flickr .
FlickrSlidr makes the process of creating a slide show extremely simple: specify the address of the user of photos or groups you want to publish, specify the desired set of tags, the width and height of the slides, after which the service gives the code that you just need to copy and paste into your online resource.
