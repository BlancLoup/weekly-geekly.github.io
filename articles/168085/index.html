Development of GPS tracker from scratch and 512 months warranty

Greetings For more than five years we have been engaged in the development of various devices for monitoring vehicles, this article will tell about the whole process - from the idea and model board to the final production stage in serial batches. Who cares - please roll up (many pictures).


Development


We are one of those who do everything themselves. Let it be a long time, albeit expensive, but in such cases we remove all the shortcomings until the moment of release, and nobody needs to blame for the failure. The main tasks that faced the development of a new device were reliability, the presence of an accelerometer, work with any devices using rs-232, and also the convenience and ease of installation for the end user (bought - installed - used). No auto electrics and other installers.

GSM


Due to interruptions in the delivery of Telita to Ukraine, the choice was made in favor of the Quectel M12 module. The module is quite famous and replaced the module M10. It has the same form factor as Telit (SMT), it is convenient in troubleshooting, because all its outputs are bounded on the sides (no BGA). For the most part, the module has absolutely the same (or very close) characteristics to Telita. It is worth noting that in the Ukrainian market almost all modules have the same functionality with the main differences only in the development environment, and here everyone already chooses what he knows best and what is more convenient to ‚Äúwrite‚Äù. I would like to mention about the M12 chip in the form of OpenCPU , which greatly reduces the entire process of developing a ready-made device, and is extremely easy to learn.

GPS


Well, there is an interesting story. Initially, everything was developed under the chip, which was used in the past device Uranus 625r. But at the time of the order, the Chinese stated that this chip was discontinued, and they are 100% sure that the new chip (of their production) will satisfy us a little less than completely. During the negotiations, pin-to-pin compatibility was agreed (and this is rx, tx, reset (special attention will be written about this below), the ground), this point was raised several times in correspondence and oral negotiations. The presence of a new Sirf IV and support for loading ephemeris for the a-gps mode is extremely pleased. Looking ahead, I can say that if you remove a fairly large number of defects (about 10%, which thank God, you can filter out at the device assembly stage) the quality of the receiver itself is quite excellent.
