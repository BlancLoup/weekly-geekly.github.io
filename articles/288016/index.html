State Duma deputy decided to teach Internet hams

LJ-user v_alksnis , he, probably, State Duma deputy Viktor Alksnis, intends to give battle to rudeness on the Internet. The representative of the authorities is going to arrange a “demonstration process”: bring the user tarlith to liability for the insult inflicted on him, Alksnis. V_alksnis clarified that the status equates to civil servants of category “A”, and intends to receive a complete satisfaction under Article 319 of the Criminal Code “Insulting a representative of the authorities” (inflicted during the performance of his official duties).

“I have already prepared a draft appeal to the General Prosecutor’s Office of the Russian Federation on this issue,” the deputy’s blog says, “and I have no doubt that the matter will be taken.” At the same time, Alksnis does not want to confine himself to compensation for the dignified value. He is focused on restoring order on the Internet and is now looking for like-minded people who are ready to "take part in the development of a special bill."

Elite LJ-society did not ignore the incident attention. The well-known businessman Alexander Lebedev, the deputies also, did not support a colleague, saying that the prosecutor’s office was “still a bust.” “This is free space. Everyone understands and uses this freedom to the best of their corruption, ”noted Lebedev. “If we start dragging the prosecutor’s office and the courts here, this will all be over, it will be an inanimate journal.”

Anton Nosik, who holds the post of head of the blog service at Supe, believes that in this story there is “the first serious provocation to attract Russian security forces to conduct political intelligence in LJ.” He doubts that Alksnis himself writes in his diary - the PR specialists of the deputy “in a couple with colleagues from the FEP” can “indulge” in this way. “It’s much worse,” according to Nosik, “if the initiative actually comes from the authorities that want to set a precedent and test the procedure.”

However, the participation of Viktor Alksnis in the project “Internet community - against Internet hams” is so far indirectly confirmed. As an expert, the deputy was invited tomorrow to the shooting of the program “Politkokteyl with Philip Leontiev”, going on the air 02TV. His opponent is LJ-user tarlith, historian Timofey Shevyakov.
