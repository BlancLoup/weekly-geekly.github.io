How to write a cover letter when looking for work in the USA: 7 tips



For many years in the USA, the practice has been extended to require applicants for various vacancies not only a resume, but also a cover letter. In recent years, the importance of this aspect has begun to decline - in 2016, only about 30% of employers required cover letters. This is not difficult to explain - HR specialists, conducting initial screening, usually have too little time to read the letters, it takes just a few seconds to analyze the statistics summary themselves.

However, polls show that the cover letter phenomenon has not yet become a thing of the past, especially with regard to the positions associated with creativity, where writing is important. A programmer can find a job with one resume in the form of a profiled profile on GitHub, while testers, analysts, marketers should take the time to write a letter - they will not be read by ‚ÄúEychary‚Äù, but by managers who select people for their team.

I found an interesting post about how to approach the writing of a cover letter today when looking for work in the USA, and prepared its adapted translation.

Need to use a template


Usually, with an active job search and resume distribution, quite often there are announcements that, when responding to which, you need to insert or attach a covering letter. A strange fact: at least according to statistics, less than a third of employers read them, up to 90% of them require them to attach. Apparently, this is considered as an indicator of the responsible attitude of the applicant and the way to filter out the most lazy.

But even if you are not too lazy to write a cover letter, doing it from scratch dozens of times is too tiring. Therefore, you need to use a template in which only parts related to a specific position are changed. Here is what a template might look like.

Be sure to include the title


Most often, the cover letter can be attached as an attachment, so it would be good to arrange it. To do this, you can follow the rules of business correspondence, which implies the availability of the following information:

  • Name;
  • Phone number or email;
  • Who do you write to (name of the manager, if indicated in the vacancy / company name);
  • Links to your profiles in social networks / site.

Since this is a business correspondence, the style must be appropriate. If you do not have your own domain, at least use mailboxes with neutral names, various sexydude1988@mail.com will not work. You should not write from the corporate mailbox of the current employer, even if you are not working in the USA now - if your resume is studied, then this website will most likely be entered and either will not understand and be confused, or will be understood and everything will not look very correct in relation to the current employer.

Use the three paragraph rule


The main task of the cover letter is to draw attention to your resume. That is, it is an auxiliary tool, which should not delay too much attention on itself, which means that it is not necessary to make it long. Three paragraphs will be more than enough. This is what they can be about:

  • In the first paragraph it is important to try to capture the attention of the reader.
  • In the second - to describe what you offer.
  • In conclusion - to consolidate the impression.

Here are some examples of what you can write about in each section.

Introduction: an indication of suitable experience


According to various sources, recruiters spend from 6.25 seconds to 30 seconds to view a resume. It is clear that they are also not ready to spend a lot of time on the cover letter. So the first paragraph is the most important.

Try to avoid long and overly formal offers. It is important to saturate the paragraph with details that will make it clear that you are a good choice for this particular job.

Bad :
I am writing a job posting. I would like to apply this position. / I respond to your job PR-manager. I have more than seven years of experience in the field of PR, I would like to offer my candidacy.
At first glance, this example is normal. But if you read and put yourself in the place of the hiring manager, it becomes clear that the text could have been made much better. For example, there are no details at all about why this particular candidate is suitable for this particular job. Well, yes, he has more than seven years of experience, and what, to take him only because he did something, as he believes, similar to the tasks described in the vacancy?

Good :
I was excited to follow the PR Manager position. It would be a good fit. While working at the outreach

The difference is obvious. The volume of the text has increased, but the information load has also increased significantly. Showing concrete achievements in the form of numbers, one can see the desire to apply knowledge and experience to solve new problems. Any employer should appreciate this.

What next: describe the benefits of cooperation


After the initial attraction of attention, you need to develop success and give even more details - for this you need a second paragraph. In it you describe why cooperation with you will bring the company maximum benefit.

In the example above, we considered a cover letter to respond to a PR-manager job at company XYZ. An organization may need a person who:

He has extensive experience with various media, bloggers and blogs, worked with incoming requests for product reviews, etc.

He understands technologies, monitors trends in this area - after all, XYZ is a startup in the field of artificial intelligence.

Here's how to respond to these tasks in a cover letter:
...
I am working on the organization of the SuperCorp. For example, technology-related publications (TechCrunch, VentureBeat, etc.) by 20%. The number of mentions from the list has increased by more than 30%. Referral traffic now brings about 15% of overall website traffic (compared to 5% the year before).

Transfer
At my current place of work in SuperCorp, I deal with PR support for new product releases, plan campaigns, and do reporting. For example, one of the most important tasks in the current year is to increase the number of mentions in the top technological media (TechCrunch, VentureBeat, etc.) by 20%. By the end of the first quarter, the number of references in publications from the list increased by 30%, and the share of referral traffic now amounts to about 15% of the traffic to the site (a year ago the figure did not exceed 5%).
At the beginning of the paragraph, the candidate described his tasks at the current location, indicated that this work was similar to the tasks that now face the new employer, and illustrated the achievements with numbers. An important point: the whole text is built around the advantages for the company: a higher coverage of top media audience, more traffic, etc. When the hiring manager reads this, he will immediately understand what exactly the company will receive if he hires this particular specialist.

Mention any event related to the company, its product or service.

Important: everything needs to be rechecked


Once again - the cover letter should not be long. The rule of 300 words should be applied to it - everything that exceeds this limit should be reduced.

In addition, you must get rid of typos and grammatical errors. To do this, run the text through a specialized program.

Conclusion: Bugs and Tips


In conclusion, let us reiterate the mistakes in the preparation of cover letters to respond to the vacancies of American companies and ways to avoid them.

  • Focus not on yourself, but on the employer and the benefits that the company will receive if it hires you.
  • Use the three paragraph rule. Maximum you can add another line PS All text should not exceed 300 words.
  • Use the template in which you add keywords from the vacancy you are responding to, attach the description of achievements to the tasks indicated in the announcement.
  • Recheck everything - ask someone to read the text and run it through the software to search for typos and grammatical errors.
