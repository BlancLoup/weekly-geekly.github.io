Analyzer


At the moment, it is very difficult to imagine the Internet without search monsters such as Google, Yahoo or Yandex, but only the trouble is, the more the search engine becomes, the more difficult it is to find that very needle in a haystack, and in such moments there is a need defragment and analyze the search results of these monsters, and it was for this purpose that the Analyzer project was created.

At the moment, the system has three sections:
  • People - this section is designed to search for people on various social networks and services (HR'y is for you)
  • Products - consumer section - search for information on various online stores about products of interest to you
  • URL - SEO URL analyzer, section focused on SEO optimizers and site owners (XINU was taken as a prototype (http://www.viciao2k3.net/services/xinu))

Each section is associated with a specific set of plug-ins, the most complete ‚Äúcollection‚Äù of SEO URL Analyzer, plug-ins are quite autonomous and even beginners can get on with their teeth. The system uses the Zend Framework as a framework, while PHP is built with support for JSON, SimpleXML and CURL - so that information can be easily obtained using a variety of APIs, as well as with the help of banal page parsing.
