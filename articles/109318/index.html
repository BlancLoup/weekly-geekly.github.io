Moto CMS will speak in Russian?

It seems that we have good Friday news for many habrayuser, flash developers and simple Russian-speaking users. At the time of writing this post, before the launch of Moto CMS in Russia, there were only three days, nineteen hours, forty-three minutes and fifty-seven seconds.

This fact confirms the main page of the Russian site Moto CMS



The statements of the company's management in various interviews have long made it clear that they are very interested in the Russian-speaking market. But as they say, it's one thing to talk, and another thing to see. The launch of the Russian-language version will occur, according to simple mathematical calculations, in the afternoon of December 7, 2010 . This means that it took the creators less than a year to release a full-fledged adapted version.

To test the quality of the Russian-language service all habrayusers, one of the first, will be able next week.
