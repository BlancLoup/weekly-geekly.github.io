CES Results: Samsung New Laptops at the International Consumer Electronics Show

And again good afternoon, dear Habrazhiteli!

We continue to sum up the results of the international consumer electronics exhibition CES 2013, which was held January 8-11 in the city of Las Vegas, USA. Today, our focus is on the two latest models of the Samsung high-performance notebook line: the Series 7 Chronos and Series 7 Ultra.



Notebook Series 7 Chronos

The new Series 7 Chronos notebook is a powerful workhorse, suitable for both daily activities and entertainment, as well as for professional activities that require good performance. Contrary to the interpretation of the concept of "workhorse", this laptop is enclosed in a nice looking thin aluminum body - only 20.9 mm.

Inside the laptop is equipped with a 2.4 GHz Intel Core i7 3635QM processor, an AMD Radeon HD 8870M graphics adapter with 2 GB of GDDR5 video memory (PowerExpress), RAM (4 or 8 GB, but can be expanded up to 16 GB) and a hard disk with a capacity of up to 1 TB Screen size - 15.6 inches, resolution - 1920x1080 (Full HD), and its brightness is 300 nits. Touch screen, supports 10-finger multitouch.

There are 2 USB 3.0 ports, 2 USB 2.0 ports, HDMI, HP / MIC, VGA and an RJ 45 network connector (three-in-one). The laptop battery promises up to 11.5 hours of battery life. The boot speed of the laptop after switching on is twelve seconds. To get out of sleep mode, Chronos will take two seconds. Windows 8 is pre-installed on the laptop.

From small but pleasant additions, you can note the backlight of the keyboard, a spacious clickpad and a built-in HD camera above the screen.

Notebook Series 7 Ultra
