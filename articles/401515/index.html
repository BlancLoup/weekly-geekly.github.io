UP! Mini 2 - 3D printer for your kids

Good day! We are often asked about which 3D printer a child can buy, and this question has always put us at a dead end. there was not a single model that could be 100% advised, and finally such a printer appeared. At the end of last year, TierTime released an updated version of their favorite UP 3D printer! mini - UP! mini 2. This 3D printer can truly be called a homely, compact design in Apple style, no noise and smell due to the closed case and the built-in HEPA filter, automatic calibration, simple and intuitive software, high print quality and the ability to use various types of materials makes this printer unique in its own way.



The printer looks in my opinion a bit futuristic =)



For the coil has a separate container with a convenient compartment for tools. (Only a standard 0.5 kg coil fits well into the container., 0.75 kg coil. - completely flush, 1 kg. - does not fit in width)



Specifications



Beyond that UP! Mini 2 was able to resume printing after a power outage (resume printing AFTER printer initialization). A good addition to Wi-Fi was the presence of a slicer for a phone or tablet, while only ios is available.

UP cost! Mini 2 - 44 900 rub.

Slicer


Visually, the slicer remained the same.

To be able to add new print profiles, you will have to register on the site and link the printer to your profile in the slicer.





After activation, profiles will be able to create new profiles. Only two parameters - the temperature of the table and the nozzle. Although for most materials this will be enough.



The rest of the slicer remained unchanged - simple and intuitive.

Kinematics. The mechanics of the printer did without any major changes.
