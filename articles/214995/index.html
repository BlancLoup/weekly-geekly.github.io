How much can you save electricity using Flash in storage?

Many have heard that in the west, data center owners are constantly concerned about the amount of energy consumed and the fact that their maintenance is very expensive. One of the most voracious components of the data center is storage , due to the fact that the latter use a large number of mechanical hard drives. Many have also heard that electricity "over the hill" is much more expensive. And what about us?

Flash savings

As can be seen from the performance test, the FAS8020 is slightly better in terms of throughput and slightly better in response speed (latency), while the cost of electricity costs for 5 years of this system is approximately 300 thousand kW / h less and, accordingly, it is cheaper by about 30 Thousands of $ than a similar system with a large number of spindles.
