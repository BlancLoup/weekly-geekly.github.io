Big update

Hello! We hurry to inform you the joyful news. Update 14.3.A.0.681 (Android 4.4.2) is now available to owners of Xperia Z Ultra smartphones, Z1 and Z1 Compact. Moreover, closer to mid-April, it can be installed on models Z, ZL, ZR and Tablet Z. You can install the update using Sony PC Companion, Sony Update Service or Sony Bridge. The possibility of updating "by air" will appear a little later. With a list of innovations, you can find under habrakatom.




- Improved performance and optimization of UI.
- Correction of various errors, including the known problem with the proximity sensor.
- Improved performance and optimized camera settings.
- Updated Status Bar and quick settings menu, even more intuitive and customizable.
- Convenient and flexible setting of pop-up notifications.
- New interface to interact with the PlayStation 4.
- The latest versions of branded applications: Messaging, MyXperia, Smart Connect, Smart Apps, TrackID, Sony Select, Smart Social Camera and others.
- Media applications (Walkman, Album, Movies) with cloud technology support and Sony Entertainment Network service.
- Incredible customization of the interface using Xperia Themes .
