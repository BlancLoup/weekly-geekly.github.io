Forbes has published a rating of the 100 richest IT entrepreneurs in the world

American magazine Forbes presented the ranking of the richest IT entrepreneurs in 2015.

Rating headed by Bill Gates. He is the richest man on the planet. This year, Gates' fortune is estimated at $ 79.6 billion. 13% of his money is invested in Microsoft shares. Over the past 21 years, he has become 16 times the richest man in the world according to Forbes.

In second place is the founder of Oracle, Larry Ellison. His fortune is $ 50 billion. Today he is the chairman of the board of directors and the technical director of the company.

The list of the richest IT entrepreneurs included 33 representatives from the Asia-Pacific region, 51 Americans, eight Europeans, two Israelis, one Hispanic. 40 out of 100 businessmen live and work in California.

The aggregate state of the rating participants is estimated at $ 842.9 billion. The basis of their wealth are assets in hard- and software companies, social media, online casinos and high-tech manufacturing.

The average age of the list is 53 years. 94 out of 100 made their fortunes, starting a business from scratch.
