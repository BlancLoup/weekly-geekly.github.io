Announced the start date of sales of Xperia X Performance in Russia

Sony's flagship smartphone, the Xperia X Performance , will be available on July 9th. And its price when buying in the company's online store , the network of Sony Center and partner outlets will be 49,990 rubles.



Let me remind you, Sony Xperia X Performance is equipped with a five-inch screen with Full HD resolution. The back cover is metal, as befits a flagship device. On the side is the power button with a built-in fingerprint scanner - you do not need to invent a complex password to unlock and worry about the integrity of information. The smartphone is presented in four colors: graphite black, white, pink gold and gold lime.

Inside is a powerful hardware: a Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor with 64-bit architecture and 3 GB of RAM, there is also support for microSD cards up to 200 GB. The front camera has a 13 megapixel sensor, the main one - 23 megapixels. Optics allow you to take clear and sharp images without noticeable noise in low light. In addition, both cameras, the main and front, support SteadyShot stabilization technology with active mode for shooting video.



2700 mAh battery provides about two days of active use. And Qnovo's adaptive charging technology will save the battery from rapid deterioration: the battery life is extended twice.

When you buy Xperia X Performance by the end of July, you will receive a free subscription to Google Play Music, a voucher for twelve films from Privilege Plus and an extended screen warranty - you can replace the display for free within a year after purchase.
