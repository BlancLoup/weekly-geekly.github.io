Nokia prototypes on Windows Phone 7 - first impressions and the whole line at the beginning of 2012

Do not have a hundred rubles, and have a hundred friends. It is this saying that leads me through life and allows me to get acquainted with certain products at the stage of their initial development. My old acquaintance appeared in Moscow, who brought a lot of news about Nokia and Windows Phone 7, and he also had one of the prototypes of such a device. Especially for Nokia lawyers I will add that this device does not belong to me. I would venture to share with you a number of impressions, as well as additional information that I learned from our communication.

Nokia plans for WP7 - 4 devices in development
Inside MS there are specifications for WP7 devices, the so-called chassis (chassis - I'm not sure what I am writing, but the word is just that - chassis). At the moment, all the variety of devices available on WP7 is very similar to each other - they have the same functionality, thanks to WP7 and the lack of customization, the same processors, the Qualcomm platform, as a result, very similar impressions and performance. Today, there is no difference in the device of which manufacturer to buy, they all look alike, the differences are minimal (screen type, battery, case materials, and so on).

At the end of the year, a second wave of devices comes out, the so-called chassis2, there are certain indulgences in relation to the performance of the devices (the processor may not be so fast). But at the same time, chassis2 allows you to add new form factors - for example, QWERTY all-in-one PCs with touch screens, which Windows Mobile was known for at one time. All manufacturers create such models today, these are HTC, Samsung, LG and a number of other companies, even the SE conducts similar developments and studies the issue.



For Nokia, this trend is absolutely new for many reasons, but the main and main one is that the company has been suing Qualcomm for many years and is historically an antagonist. The deal with MS and the requirements of the manufacturer forced Nokia to use Qualcomm chipsets, today's prototypes are built on them, do not use any other solutions (although Nokia really wanted not to use Qualcomm, but MS‚Äôs position is tough on this issue).

Nokia was left with the freedom to choose what they would do on the chassis2 and the company very briskly began to build prototypes, and this was done before the official announcement of a possible deal with MS. Elop really wants the first products to appear by Christmas 2011, but most likely it will still be the end of January, February.

The time limit caused the need to use ready-made developments in the field of design, in particular, the prototype that was held in the hands of your humble servant, nothing more than the Nokia X7 with another screen (WVGA), the QSD8250 chipset. I have the use of the same chipset as in a pile of other products already on the market has caused many questions. Why not actually something new from Qualcomm? The answer was quite expected - it was necessary to start from something, this chipset was initially available, so we started the work, literally, from the wheels. At the moment, this model and, accordingly, the prototype are most ready, they are alive and you can see what WP7 plus Nokia is.

We even joked that the X7 was chosen simply by the numerical index, as the model that best suits the requirements of MS - we need a seven in the name. But this is only a joke.

The case from Nokia X7, the screen from the new supplier (this is not Samsung, the screen is not AMOLED, but everything can change, and the screen quality is very good, I liked it). The design of the device is memorable, so there are no complaints. I emphasize that this prototype was actually important in order to get very fast working samples and starting from them to do the rest.

There are no miracles in the world, and this model reminded me of all the impressions HTC Mozart, exactly the same functionality. The camera is installed, by the way, with 8 megapixel autofocus and there is a flash. In principle, at all WP7 from Nokia more expensive than a certain level of the camera will be with autofocus, this will be different from Symbian products and this is a conscious decision.

This model is planned to be the first to launch on the market. In fact, it is already ready in a certain condition, so that in the standard form, it can be launched even in the fall. And here comes the main question, whether MS will allow to customize the Nokia phone software or not. At the moment, the position of MS is very simple - no special preferences will be made for Nokia. If the possibility of customization appears, it will be given to all. Something like this.

Therefore, W7 is used as a test device, the company's engineers stuff their bumps on it, learn and so on. In general, according to my friend, there are many new experiences that need to be accumulated; they study many small, but time-consuming questions. Learn in one word. And studies probably better on proven things.
