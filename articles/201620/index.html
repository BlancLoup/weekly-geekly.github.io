Zoom TV: how to change the way you control your TV forever

In the last post, I insidiously mislead honest citizens and bore anti-scientific nonsense outlined an important, in my opinion, problem: the traditional method of controlling a TV gives mothballs and does not correspond at all to the current socio-technological moment. To force the user to manually search for the transmission he needs by means of a television remote in our time is the same as preferring manual human labor to robots.

Again, I firmly believe that the modern urban man has every right to furnish the space around him in full accordance with the precepts of consumerist Feng Shui: that is, receive services to the maximum, and put efforts to a minimum (ideally - do not put any effort at all). The elimination of mechanical and monotonous actions from our lives is one of the goals of technical progress. And the long-term resistance of TV (more than 50 years) here is something out of the ordinary.
