The third meeting of the Scala User Group in Moscow: the last day of registration!

Dear Scala lovers! We use the last moment to inform you about the next meeting of the Scala User Group in Moscow. It will be held this Friday, April 25 in the territory of the research institute "Sunrise". Beginning at 19:30. In order to come to the meeting, you must fill out the registration form: docs.google.com/forms/d/1tYo8unkWNpQYN-lCwoZz4mL7AcvNQGi_Zt1O7TTpknA/viewform (all fields are required, full names must be entered completely). The last day of registration is today, April 23rd .


At the meeting, Mikhail Aksarin will talk about Lifted Embedding in Slick 2 in Live Demo mode, Roman Timushev will talk about Design patterns and antipatterns in Akka, and Ilya Klyuchnikov will present an introduction to Scala Macros and use them to check Scala code for completeness. In the end, of course, there will be time to discuss the reports and chat.

Meet in mitap with details: www.meetup.com/Scala-Moscow/events/171706192
Facebook group: www.facebook.com/groups/552831821450933

Address: metro Vernadsky Avenue, st. Udaltsova, d. 85. You must have a passport with you. Unfortunately, only citizens of the Russian Federation and Belarus can enter the territory of the research institute "Voskhod".
