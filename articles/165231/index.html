Microsoft R & D office robbed, only iPad tablets stolen

The small Daily Post newspaper, which is published in Palo Alto, reported a curious case of robbery that occurred during the Christmas holidays, although the company announced it only on 2 January. The robbers broke into one of Microsoft's office buildings at 1075 La Avenida Street in Mountain View, where Microsoft R & D is located.

Thieves cleaned out three office premises and stole five Apple iPad tablets, including two iPads 2, two iPads 3 and one iPad 4.

None of the Microsoft products were stolen.

The story of a Microsoft office robbery was first considered an anecdote, but a few days later the author of the article, Angela E. Ruggiero, confirmed that the story is really true. Microsoft R & D specialists are developing mobile applications, including those for iOS, so the presence of iPads in the office can be understood.
