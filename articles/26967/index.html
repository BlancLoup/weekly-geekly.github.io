1. Hi. How did such an original idea come to mind?
Actually, this idea was in my head from the very first day of the creation of the project. I really wanted to have some very significant distinctive feature, I wanted to do something that no one else had done, at least in Ukraine.
And in Kiev, they were already actively distributing free newspapers, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich I really liked. But the usual newspaper did not want to - journalists, editors, censorship, etc. I wanted something new, light, youth. So the idea was born to combine the site and print.

2. Any specific details - when can we expect a newspaper? What will be the initial circulation? Let even approximate numbers.
We are planning to release the first issue of the newspaper on June 23/24. Circulation we think will be at least 1000 copies. Already agreed with information agencies to not violate their copyright. The number by 20 should be ready for T-shirts so that people who will distribute newspapers can be clearly seen.

3. I know that at first it will be distributed at 2 metro stations (where there are many students). What are the future plans to promote the publication to the masses? And in general, how bright is the future?
Prime plans - all Kiev metro stations. We also plan to deliver newspapers to large office centers in Kiev. And then the cities of Ukraine with the largest number of Internet users. After all, we are not an ordinary newspaper, but we can say an online newspaper.

4. Interested in the material side of the issue. Are there any sponsors, how, actually, are you planning to become self-sufficient or even profit?

Thirdly, the project will be a source of interesting information, which was published and selected by ordinary users. Neither the site nor the newspaper will not be clamped in any frame format.
