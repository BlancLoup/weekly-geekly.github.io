Open MP3 - a new resource for finding free mp3

Once I discovered that all sites offering to download / listen to mp3 do not work. Some were closed, others sold their domain, and others were stupidly broken. At that moment, I realized that I needed to close this gap :) This is how my website was born - Open MP3

I think that no one here needs to explain how such services work - the user writes a query to the search bar, and in return receives a list of direct links to downloading tracks. Better I will talk about what distinguishes my search engine from all the others, of whom a great many have proliferated on the Internet.

The main differences


First: there is no advertising on the site. No. Those. You will not see there obsessive pop anderov, which open several tabs with porn, flashing banners, pop-ups and other crap. Only the search string and a table with the results in the form of direct (almost) links to files. I do not create any restrictions - you can download several files at maximum speed at once. And finally, there is no unnecessary registration.

Secondly: you can not only download music, but also listen. Both directly on the site, using a convenient flash player, and on your computer by downloading the playlist in the M3U format.
About playlists in general is a separate topic, so read here - andmp3.ru/info.html#usepls

Thirdly: the site has a system of music categorization. True while it is in its infancy, but something is already possible to see. Rubrication is the search for music by tags, genres, performers. The more music users download, the more information gets into the database. Therefore, this feature will fully manifest itself only after a while.

And one more little bonus: user statistics. The site stores information about the tracks you have downloaded, search history, ratings and more.

Full description of all features on the help page .

Development plans


I have a lot of them :) What is now is only the germ of the service that I want to implement. You should have something in between bee.fm and last.fm, namely:
  • Recommendation system based on downloaded and evaluated compositions
  • Database for most artists with a brief annotation and album navigation
  • Building ratings and charts

Habraeffekt


The site is located on a very weak virtual hosting, so I ask you, please be careful :)
If you just want to rate the site and not download music, then click on the links in the "Recently searched here" box. These are links to the results of cached queries, and they will not take a lot of resources. I specially placed the topic at night so that the load increased smoothly. In any case, if you fail to open the site, then I am not guilty - this is a nice effect :)

Bugreport


The resource is just launched, and therefore I am sure that there are a lot of uncatch bugs. Also, I am sure that the community has a lot of ideas to improve the functionality. I ask all comments and suggestions to write or in private, or me on e-mail: admin@andmp3.ru
I will be very grateful for any feedback.

I have it all :)
