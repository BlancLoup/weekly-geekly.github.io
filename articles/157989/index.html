ZTE enters the Ukrainian smartphone market

Dear friends, we are pleased today to inform you about the release of mobile devices under its own brand ZTE in retail sales in Ukraine.



Up to this point in the Ukrainian market, ZTE mobile devices were sold only under their own brands of mobile operators.
And now, in November of this year, a whole range of ZTE products will appear on the shelves of Ukrainian stores: a powerful and modern ZTE V970 smartphone, a productive ZTE V881, an indispensable assistant to ZTE V880E Dual-SIM and a budget version of ZTE V880S with Android ICS on board.

We would like to note that since the third quarter of 2012, ZTE has entered the top four of the world's largest manufacturers of smartphones, being, according to IDC, among the TOP 5 mobile device suppliers in the world.

Until recently, the majority of ZTE smartphones covered the initial level, but with the release of the ZTE Grand line, it reaches the medium and high levels of smartphone segments.
In Ukraine, we will also cover all price categories of mobile devices.

Our smartphones have several important advantages - low prices compared to competitors' devices with similar technical characteristics, and most importantly, quality and reliability, which millions of users around the world have already made sure of.
