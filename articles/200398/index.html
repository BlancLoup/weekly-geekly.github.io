Pays.io payment service closes

Latest news. The service through which we planned to make payments - paysio.com , died suddenly.


Today we received such a letter:

From: Kirill Ponirovsky <k.poniroffsky@pay-s.ru>
Date: October 31, 2013, 14:23
Topic: Closing the pays.io service

Good afternoon, we hereby notify you of the closure of the service Pays.io.
Service will work another week. Regardless of the closure of the service, all obligations to pay you money received through us will be fulfilled by the final processing to which you were connected (you have an agreement with it).
To continue work and on support issues you need to contact the manager in the firm Platron (our partner through which we made the connection).
Mendeder contacts: e.lysova@platron.ru +7 495 983 3279
Thank you for being with us all this time.


Of course, no one reports the reasons for the closure. Open information about this, too, did not find. Here, they decided to tell you if someone thinks to connect.

Now we will look for a new partner. If you have any suggestions, we will be happy to read in the comments. We need a service that will be able to accept payments from customers' bank cards, and after a while (after the consultation), transfer this money to other bank cards to consultants.
