HackDay. Moscow. October 22-23



We invite you to take part in the largest HackDay of autumn. Participants are given the opportunity to turn their idea into a working prototype, assemble a team, have a productive time and get investments to develop their project.

The general partner of the event is Microsoft, whose experts will tell you how the company's products will help your startup, as well as conduct a separate business section.

In addition, we invited the creators of two well-known startups who are ready to share their experience, we will announce their names a week before the event.


We also want you to also take part in the formation of the HackDay program: write here in the comments or to the organizers which startups you want to see as speakers at the event.

A prerequisite for participation in major events HackDay - participation (in any role) in the development of any of the announced projects. On the solemn presentation of finished prototypes on Sunday evening, everyone is allowed as an audience. The best projects will be awarded.

Among the prizes to the winners: a certificate for training in the program for Internet entrepreneurs Start in Garage, a cash prize, a netbook.

To participate you must:

1. Register on the site;

2. Add a description of your project ; By the same link you can join the project you like, projects are constantly being added, stay tuned.

HackDay is not a conference or a meeting for discussion. This is the time for brainstorming in search of ideas and the most successful ways to implement it. You can come up with an idea, or with a project already started, and continue working, as well as contribute to the development of other projects.

We invite: programmers (from beginners to professionals), designers, managers, interface designers, marketers, PR-specialists, lawyers specializing in the field of IT, as well as all sympathizers.

Location

Moscow, Bersenevskaya Embankment 6, Digital October Center.
