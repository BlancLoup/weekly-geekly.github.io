Financial security in freelancing

If you believe the statistics, then today more and more people prefer to make money without having a supervisor. Yes, yes, I mean freelancing (and for those who do not like the newfangled buzzwords - we are talking about home-based work). And if people used to work this way predominantly from Western countries, the wave has recently reached us.

Naturally, home-based work, like all other types of employment, has both its negative and positive sides. If we consider each such moment, we get at least a treatise, and at the maximum a doctoral dissertation or a weighty book. Therefore, in this article I will touch on only one aspect - how to protect yourself as much as possible from customers who want to get the results of your work completely free of charge.
