LBook V3 Extended Review

Greetings to you, oh habraedi! I will support a wave of interest in e-books with an article and a video review of the LBook V3 . The question of the acquisition and use of such devices became interested a couple of months ago. Working for half a day at Apple Cinema Display , I began to feel that when reading bulk materials, attention was partially distracted by the colorful elements of the websites and the OS interface. Theoretical background


Electronic paper has been developed as an alternative to LCD displays. The main difference is that electronic paper forms an image in reflected light, like a regular printed sheet, without creating additional voltage for the eyes, and consumes energy only to update the image, which makes it possible to significantly increase the battery life. In recent years, technology has become widespread due to the popularization of electronic books.

Reality

The Internet is good because it allows you to quickly find and assimilate the necessary information. But there is one big ‚Äúbut‚Äù - besides the one that is necessary on the screen of our monitors, there is such a raging stream of pixels that quite a large part of the information is dissipated or is not digested to the extent that we wanted.
Figuratively speaking, we feed our [under] consciousness not just candy, but candy along with a candy wrapper.
The thirst for information leads to the fact that often, in different places a person picking up information, cannot create a complete and harmonious image of her in his mind.
The main advantage of the book is its way of perception. Reading the book, we are left tete-a-tete with it. Nothing prevents to perceive information.
I can relate the above with regard to myself, because I do not have the opportunity to enjoy reading, holding on to the rail of a bus / trolleybus / subway car with one hand. I try to read only in silence and solitude - that's the best thing.

New technologies

The paper book is good for everyone, but what if there is a need to read several books at the same time, making movements in space from time to time? Carrying at least a few books is quite expensive.
It is for these reasons that my attention turned my attention to e-books.

E-book price

The average price of such devices is about 12,000 rubles . In my opinion, it is somewhat overpriced, due to the modest size of their market.
Price is the only factor deterring me from buying an e-book for the last time.
My joy knew no bounds when, when visiting a popular virtual Petersburg " flea market ", I found an advertisement for the sale of a used Lbook V3 .
The man selling the book was very close. Doubts plagued me until he uncovered the LBook V3 . The moment when it was given to me to see the device with my own eyes - they disappeared.
The first impression was surprisingly funny. I remembered the dummies of cell phones with colored pieces of paper instead of screens that have a place to live on the shelves of provincial shops of cellular communication.

Using the LBook V3







The device got me in very good condition. All that remained to be done was to transfer everything that I wanted to read to the memory card.
Over the past year, quite a lot of electronic literature has accumulated, which I really wanted to study - books on web development, web design, web technologies, video editing and much more.
Only one fact did not allow me to do this - I work at a computer. Considerations on the subject of dispersal of attention read above. Writing code and reading articles is excellent. To perceive large amounts of information is troublesome.
There is no limit to the joy of how useful a purchase has become. I read a lot, every day, those books that I have long wanted to read - as if I pay debts to myself.
The book understands all the formats I need - PDF , fb2 , DjVU , html , chm .
And fb2 , for example, easily reads from the archives.

Design and interface


The design is simple, but at the same time delicate. Buttons exactly as many as needed. 8 items (folders and files) are simultaneously displayed on one main menu screen - they correspond to numeric keys. 9 and 10 , duplicating navigation, allow you to navigate through the menu screens.
You can select the source of information - SD card ( SD / MMC / SDHC up to 16GB ) or built-in memory ( 512MB ).
Impressions of the device and work with it are the same - simple, easy and convenient.
Different programs are responsible for different types of files. For example, fb2 reads one, and PDF - another.



When reading formats such as fb2, you can select the font size, as well as recall the contents of the book and navigate with it. There are functions to rotate the screen ( 360 degrees in increments of 90 ), go to the next / previous chapters, browse 10 pages, manage bookmarks.

IPhone comparison






The first tangible difference in the transition from the iPhone to the LBook V3 is a reduction in eye strain.
Read freely easily. I also thought about the iPhone , but, going over, I felt the difference. Whatever you say, the screen backlight strains the eye.



The surface area of 6 " ( 800x600 pixels) of the e-book screen is almost three times higher than that of the iPhone .

Autonomous work

Working with any mobile devices, there was always an edge to the question of battery life. For example, the MacBook Air lives on a single charge for about 3 hours , and with the iPhone 'and without recharging, you can read about 6 hours .
The e-book in this regard is the first mobile device that greatly pleased.
Judging by the reviews, 5,000-6,000 page turnovers , announced by the manufacturer, are overstated, but the resource of one charge is more than sufficient. If you read every day and in large quantities, the Nokia BL-5 battery life (you can buy a spare one for a couple of hundred rubles) lasts for several weeks.
If you make a simple proportion of the ratio of the information received to the battery life, then the e-book is leading with a huge margin compared with laptops, PDAs and other gadgets.

findings

Having filled the shining vacuum emptiness inside with the help of measured and calm reading, having stabilized the ratio of absorption (reading, studying) information and its compilation (work, searching for new sources), I decided to reduce the level of unproductiveness to zero.
