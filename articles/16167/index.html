Snake in the loader

The other day, I optimized Image Processing for the project by using SSE instructions. And in the evening, rather bored by the assembler and having a couple of cans of beer, threw in a simple little snake, and to make it more interesting - put it in the boot sector :)


Minimum system requirements: 8086 compatible processor and floppy.
If someone dares to take a look, the bochs config and diskette image can be downloaded here , this image can be burned to a diskette and tried directly on your computer. In case someone doubts the safety of booting from a floppy disk, they can disassemble and check :)
The snake is somewhat "unfinished" (beer tends to run out :(), but it is quite efficient.

What did you write for beer? :)
