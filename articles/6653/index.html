eBoy - pixel people

Is pixel graphics a thing of the past? Nothing like this! This technique has a lot of followers. I hope that after this small note they will become even more.

My story about the German studio pixel design eBoy.


I've been following these guys for a long time. And I like the perseverance with which the studio moves the pixel into the world, printing posters and publishing illustrations in various publications.


One of the favorite themes of the Fighters is the Apocalypse Today. Cities are dying because of the invasion of evil aliens and gigat monsters, terrorists seize buildings and shoot men, tanks plow streets, bandits attack a shop or bank. And I thought that Germany is a prosperous country.


But do not worry. Most of the pictures are still peaceful.


There is an animation.


A lot of illustrations are devoted to information technologies.


The office even released a book with their works.


And recently, eBoy graphics have been used to create cartoon ads.

The hero of the first video was cheerful Einstein.


A second video is devoted to the works of Shakespeare.
