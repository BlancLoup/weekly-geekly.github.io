Top 10 internships for IT professionals


Getting theoretical knowledge at the university is important. But no less important is the opportunity to test them in practice, especially if we are talking about such an applied area as information technology. If you are tuned to this, then an internship while studying or immediately after graduation is for you. And to facilitate your search, we, StudyQA , have prepared a selection of the top 10 internships for IT professionals. When choosing programs, we took into account the status of the company and the terms of payment.

Amazon

Amazon is a real giant not only in terms of online sales, but also in terms of career opportunities. At the moment, the company offers internships mainly for software students in Canada and the United States. Bachelors and masters can apply for participation in the competition.

Requirements for interns are the same: C / C ++ and / or Java, experience with UNIX / Linux or Windows and, of course, the ability to solve tasks.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Cisco

If you work in the field of information technology, then the name "Cisco" is familiar to you. This is an American company of international scale, dealing with network equipment. Cisco offers a comprehensive internship program focused on programmers only - IT University Program .

Internships are available in the following locations:

India;
United Kingdom;
China;
USA.

If you study in Moscow MTUCI , then you are lucky - you can take part in a special internship program from CISCO called CIIP. This internship lasts a year, and only outstanding students from certain universities from different parts of the world can participate. A complete list of universities that participate in the program can be found here .

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Hewlett packard

According to InternMatch, the internship program at Hewlett Packard is among the highest paid. There is an opportunity to go on an internship both in America and in Europe and Asia. The opportunity to listen to feedback from past interns before submitting is also available - just go to the official website and watch a couple of videos posted there.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Apple

Compiling a collection of internships, it is impossible to get around Apple. And this is impossible for one simple reason - the number of internships for IT specialists there is simply huge. As locations offer various cities of the United States, China and several European countries.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Facebook

Facebook is another giant of the information technology world and your chance to gain practical experience. At the moment, programmers are offered to go to London, Dublin, Paris and several American cities. As standard requirements, there is the ability to cope with Apache, memcached, Squid, MySQL and knowledge of at least one programming language.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Buddyguard

And this internship is for those who do not want to merge into the world of huge corporations. Buddyguard is a small Berlin security company. Their main product, FLARE, is a device that can make decisions on its own. At the moment they are looking for an intern who will work with them on machine learning algorithms. An intern should be able to program in C / C ++ or Python and work with CNN, SVM, PCA, LDA algorithms.

How to apply for participation: send your resume to hr@buddyguard.io.

Deadlines: as soon as possible

Intel

In 2017, Intel entered the list of companies with the best internships, being in the twentieth place. At the moment, she offers two options for potential programmer interns:
Standard internships;
Rotary programs for the newly released (Information Technology Rotation Opportunities);

For those who want to learn more about the internship at Intel at first hand, the company has published a special interview about the internship.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Google

According to Vault , Google is the absolute leader in terms of prestigious internships. This year, the company scored 9,528 points out of 10, ahead of Apple and Facebook. Google offers internships in various areas of IT, from a standard software specialist to a specialist in programmer relations.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Bosch

Acquaintance with the Bosch internship is better to start with the FAQ on their website - there you will immediately find out when you can apply, what is the minimum duration of the internship and what is the payment. If everything suits you, feel free to open the list of open vacancies and choose the internship you are interested in.

How to apply for participation: choose an internship on the official website and take part in the selection

Submission dates: regular set

Allpago

Our internship in Berlin closes our list - this time at Allpago. Allpago is engaged in payment systems in Latin America and right now they are looking for a student programmer to join their team. Requirements include JavaScript, UI and PowerShell, the minimum duration of the internship is 3 months, the maximum is 12.

How to apply for participation: send a motivation letter, resume and statement with estimates to careers@allpago.com

Deadlines: as soon as possible
