Nokia Booklet 3G - from $ 799, and Nokia smartbook on ARM in 2010

Nokia Booklet 3G - Intel Atom, built-in 3G, 12 hours from one battery, inside supposedly Windows 7. We already know about it . The photo shows a comparison with the Nokia E71 all-in-one communicator.



Details have become known today. Of course, unofficially.

The unpleasant news is that it will cost (in the world) from $ 799 . How many will be in Russia - consider yourself.

Any price in the purchase of such a device, this price kills. Many also believe that at this price Booklet can be considered an ultraportable laptop rather than a netbook (like the Sony Vaio). The netbook is size & price. In my opinion, a beautiful formula.

The good news is that, again according to unofficial data , Nokia in the field of netbooks and ultra-portbl is not going to stop there, and Nokia has a smartbook (do you feel like a new terminology is being instilled?) - that is, a netbook on ARM. It will be released in large quantities in mid-2010.

The release dates are strikingly the same as the release dates for Google Chrome OS, don't you think so?

In any case, wait and see. And we will know everything for sure. The price for Nokia Booklet 3G will be announced on September 2.
