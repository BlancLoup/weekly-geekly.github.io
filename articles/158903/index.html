Support the Motorola petition for opening the bootloader


Motorola behaves in a bad way. Once they announced that they were ready to open the bootloaders of their phones. But despite a sufficient amount of time since then, nothing has changed. The situation is aggravated by the fact that Motorola recently canceled many previously scheduled software upgrades. The angry buyers of their products even made up a petition in March last about this. Then gathered more than 11,240 signatures of those who supported the idea. But it seems that the matter has not moved from a dead center.

The fact is that Motorola promised to open the loaders by the end of 2011, which was safely over. Therefore, disgruntled Motorola users have launched a new petition on Change.org . If you are a developer or just a fan of picking on your phone, customizing it and trying on custom firmware, then I urge you to immediately follow the website to support the global Android community.
