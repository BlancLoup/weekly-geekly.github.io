New version of the site Mooteam.ru

Hello!
This is the first blog post of the project Mooteam.ru , which appeared in RuNet at the end of December 2007.

The developers of Mooteam.ru recently launched an updated version of the site. Anyone who went to the site earlier, just notice a big difference. Here is what we did:
- more convenient interface
- the ability to search by "Poster" and its sections
- the ability to finally use "My Billboard", that is, to receive an adequate schedule of activities for the marked interests
- clear and convenient setting personal user posters
- An updated system of events and invitations. Now she is in the testing stage: it is possible to create only her own events and invite only friends to Mooteam.ru. When the testing will be completed (literally a few days left), our invitations will pleasantly surprise you :-)

Front work is still very large! Now we are debugging a new version, working on bugs and listening to the opinions of users, for which they THANK YOU VERY MUCH!
We welcome all comments and suggestions!

PS Do not forget to meet with friends! Life after all goes and out of the Internet :-)
