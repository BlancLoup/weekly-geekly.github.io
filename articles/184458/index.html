Seminar on the improvement of mobile products using the playtest method

On July 4, Eastlabs Gaming Accelerator in Kiev together with the Playtestix research company will organize a seminar for developers and publishers of mobile games and applications, dedicated to technologies for improving mobile products and speeding up their development.



During the seminar, Playtestix specialists will talk about the method of obtaining user assessments, hold several master classes at which they will share basic skills in testing and correcting the development process, and will also share interesting real-life cases. The event will discuss:

- Definition of the target audience.
- QA and / or playtest?
- What data is needed in testing
- The art of formulating questions and compiling questionnaires.
- Who is suitable for respondents and where to get them
- Data analysis and conclusions.
- Real cases.

The event is free , you must register .

Venue: Kiev, st. Zhilyanskaya, 75 (4th floor, Eastlabs) ,
From 14:00 to 17:00.
