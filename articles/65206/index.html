The announcement of the 13-inch laptop Packard Bell Butterfly S FC



Fudzilla shared information about some of the technical characteristics of the new lightweight 13-inch Packard Bell Butterfly S FC laptop.

The machine weighs 1.8 kg and is based on the Engergoe efficient platform of the Intel CULV platform with a heart in the form of a 1.4 GHz Intel Core 2 Solo SU3500 processor. The developers claim that the power consumption level of the notebook allows it to work without problems for up to 8 hours on a single battery charge.

Among other features, you can mention 4 GB of RAM, 500 GB hard drive and a discrete graphics card - ATI Mobility Radeon HD 4330. And, yes, there is an HDMI port.

The computer will start selling next week. The European price of a laptop is 650 euros.
