The easiest way to learn how to type blindly

Transferred from a personal blog

Free advice:
If you still do not know how to type blindly - be sure to learn.

Spend a couple of days of your precious time. This is even more profitable investment than buying shares of Google in 2004. Saving time will be a lifetime, and a lot, honestly.



How to learn to type blindly
Courses stenographers and keyboard simulators to anything. On Shahidzhanyan, too, do not be fooled, psychotherapy - for self-doubt losers. There is a much simpler and faster way: boldly throw your keyboard into the trash, go to the store and buy a new one, the same one, only without Russian letters. Breaking will last one day, training will take another day. In a week you will look at the keyboard as Richter at the piano.

I bought myself a couple of years ago a laptop without a Russian keyboard. All these two years (minus the week for training) I am typing at about the same speed with which I speak. What you want.
