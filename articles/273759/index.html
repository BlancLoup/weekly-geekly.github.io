2. Equipment.


Our homemade PTS uses the following equipment:

For general plans, we will use webcams. The quality of the image of certain webcams has long been not inferior to some amateur handhelds ...

After going through different, we stopped at the Logitech cameras. Value for money we are more than satisfied. Somewhere I heard that if you use the same webcams, the virtual mixer on the computer will confuse them, the settings may fly, in general, this will complicate the work. We decided not to experiment with this and took four cameras of different models.

So, we have included the following Logitech models: C310, C510, Pro 9000 and C910 (Details in the video below). Despite the fact that these cameras are used by us in shooting at 480 points, they can all work well in 1280x720 points. model C910 even copes with Full HD (that is: 1920 to 1080 pixels). By the way, at the moment the model C920 has appeared at the firm Logitech. We checked, it worked well with us for the C910 . But colleagues said that the C910 and C920 computer confused. So we do not recommend having them both, although in the picture they are the best, in our opinion.



Further, besides 4 webcams, we use two cameras in DV format. One of them is the legendary Panasonic ‚Äúweaving‚Äù ( Panasonic DVX 100 ). The second one can be the same, and maybe the ‚Äúhandheld‚Äù (an amateur camera, which is easy to lose in hand), anyone who can give a DV signal. It may even be a professional HD camera switched to DV mode. In general, you can use any two DV cameras. But, as you understand, the quality of the picture will depend on their matrix and optics. When we use a handheld instead of one of the "weave", the picture is much worse. It seems that this is due to the smaller number of points. But there are as many of them, which once again proves that "happiness is not in pixels".

The next item in our kit is a Windows 7- based computer (before that, everything calmly worked on Windows XP , service packs 2nd and 3rd). About Windows Vista and Windows 8 I will not say anything, to try - there is no desire. I7 processor It will be more convenient if two monitors are connected.

We also use a simple indicator service connection made on our knees, which we will separately discuss in the following chapters.

Also, in our PTS audio circuit, the Behringer processor was used in order to make the sound fatter and more equal. But later we abandoned it, because the new sound console, from which we received the sound for broadcast, already knew how to compress and limit the audio signal.

In general, this is all that is required from the equipment in our case, for multi-camera shooting and live broadcast on the Internet.

Of course, for the commercial level, this survey is not suitable. But for the archive, or the report, or for the broadcast, you can shoot not only a corporate party, or a meeting, but also a completely dynamic concert, provided there are competent operators on the first and second cameras and an experienced director of the TCP, at the computer.
