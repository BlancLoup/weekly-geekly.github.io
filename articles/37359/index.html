fring began to support Symbian 9.3

Last week, support for two new smartphones ( Nokia E71 and Nokia E66 ) was announced, but now a version has been released that can work with Symbian OS 9.3 !

Thanks to this, five new smartphones are added to a number of models supported by fring, all working just under the control of Symbian 9.3:
  • Nokia N78
  • Nokia 6550
  • Nokia 6210
  • Nokia 6220
  • Nokia 5320


Traditionally, to get fring for Symbian 9.3, just go to the download page and specify your data: http://www.fring.com/download/

PS: Owners of the Sony Ericsson G900 smartphone - fring remembers you. Follow the blog posts - news about this model will appear in the very near future
