Cognitive and MISiS held a school competition

Hello, Habr !!


NUST "MISiS" together with the company Cognitive Technologies summed up the All-Russian correspondence programming contest among students in grades 9-11. The Olympiad took place on October 20 and was held according to the ACM ICPC rules. Its goal was to search for new talents for the subsequent selection in the MISiS team on sports programming. According to the organizers of the Olympiad, 1871 people took part in it, 191 of them received a participant certificate for success in solving problems. And the 33 most capable will be invited to the winter programming fees from January 3 to 10, 2014, within which an in-person round of the Olympiad will take place. It will be in one of the holiday homes in the Moscow region. Moreover, the organizers will take on the costs associated with accommodation and meals. His winners, in addition to valuable prizes and get a chance to join the new team of Cognitive Technologies and MISiS programming.

Representatives of the Organizing Committee of the Olympiad reported that the level of the tasks presented was correct, in a sense, optimal. This, by the way, is not always the case at olympiads. It happens that one - two tasks turn out to be "dead" - that is, no one could solve them. This is not good for anyone. In our case, there was not a single task that participants would not overcome. On the other hand, the tasks were not simple, because there was not a single person who would solve all the problems. The best result was seven solved problems.
As a result, Ivanov Mikhail from the Yaroslavl Region (Lyceum No. 2, Rybinsk) and Gramovich Pavel from the Samara Region (Lyceum No. 67, Togliatti) became the winners of the Olympiad.
