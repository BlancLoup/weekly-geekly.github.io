Distance


The official marathon length is 42 km and 195 meters.

The International Olympic Committee in 1896 measured the actual length of the distance from the battlefield in Marathon to Athens; it was equal to 34.5 km. The length of the race was not originally fixed, since all that was important was that all the athletes ran along the same route. The exact length of the Olympic Marathon depended on the route chosen at the respective Games. At the first Olympics, it was equal to 40 km. The starting point for the Olympics in 1908 in London was moved from a mark of 25 miles to a mark of 26 miles 385 yards (42 km 195 meters), so that the royal family could comfortably watch the mileage from the windows of Windsor Castle. Finally, the length of 42.195 km as the official marathon run length was established in 1921 by the International Amateur Athletics Federation (the current IAAF). A source

However, in practice, participants run a greater distance. This is due to suboptimal cornering, or if an athlete is distracted by running to a pit stop or if a participant is distracted too much by the audience ‚Äî he kisses, hugs, claps his hands, etc. In this regard, experienced athletes control this aspect: they try not to run in a crowd, optimally take turns on a tangent, etc.

Training


According to Herodotus in 490 BC. it was all a bit different:

  • Fidipppe was a messenger, i.e. as if by a professional athlete
  • before the battle, he was sent to run to Sparta for reinforcements and back (230 km),
  • he could participate and be injured in the battle itself,
  • besides him, other volunteers also fled to Athens, and he had to overtake them in order to bring the message first.

Pidippid's professional training completely changes everything. According to Herodotus, Fidippid is more likely the founder of a triathlon or all-around, rather than a marathon.

In general, if short distances can be overcome without any special consequences, probably, anyone. Well, the time will be better or worse, but somehow you will be feeling your 400 meters, 800 meters, 5 km or even 10 km. For example, I participated in the 10 km race in the Moscow Marathon in 2017 without any preparation for the company (result: 00: 53.35).

A marathon without training is better not to run. Good news: it turns out that preparing for a marathon is very cool and not at all difficult. All you need is a pair or two of sneakers, running clothes for summer / winter, a training plan, a sports watch, and of desire. It is even better to train with a trainer less often or more often to correct the technique of running. Only 5-6 months of training, and you - a marathon!

I already had a watch like this - Apple Watch Nike +.

It turned out that the preparation plan is also not a problem at all. Manufacturers of sportswear and equipment have already taken care of everything. There are tons of free or low cost options.


This is how a preparation plan is made through the Asics website You indicate that you want to run a marathon and when, the result of your previous race, gender, date of birth. Then you will have to specify how many days a week you can train and that's all - the system will calculate the predicted result and plan for it. The same can be done through the Runkeeper iOS application and then use it to track runs.

I used the workout plan from NikePlus and their iOS and Apple Watch apps ( Nike + RunClub ). Everything is quite convenient - I see in the application a workout plan for every day / week before the race itself, I record my runs with the Apple Watch app, and they are immediately taken into account in my plan.


Screenshots of iOS applications Nike + RunClub

The picture above shows screenshots from the Nike + RunClub iOS application. With the same success, you can use other options - Endomondo , Strava . Subscription is worth some money, but it is not very expensive.

When to find time for training? In fact, it is full! I, for example, like to run after work - this is how I let off steam after a busy day. Nutrition


‚ÄúEveryone can run 20 miles, but no one knows what awaits him after that,‚Äù wrote the 1972 Olympic champion American Frank Shorter. - Carbohydrates give the body about 1200 kcal, in addition, up to 800 kcal, it can produce from its own fat reserves. During the marathon, the body overloads and reacts specifically to food. What you can safely eat normally, you will not get into the throat after the 20th kilometer. Therefore, nutrition is also necessary to train. It is recommended that you plan your marathon meal plan in advance and train it during long runs.


My meal plan was as follows:

  • three days of rejection of vegetables (because they are rich in fiber);
  • on the eve of solid pasta with small meals;

  • 1 gel 45 minutes after the start;
  • 1 gel every 30 minutes;
  • every 3rd gel is caffeinated.
