5 cool sites for everyone to think you are working

Are your favorite sites blocked at work? Did the office firewall cover up Twitter? Here are five sites that will help solve this problem and at the same time pretend that you are working;)

1) Vanishd - we hide one web page for another

Let the staff, boss and friends think you are working. What if we could, for example, cover up any web page with a false Word document or even google.com page and look through a small window on YouTube or something else? For these purposes, a site Vanishd.com





In this case, you can move the mouse window with your favorite site and change its size as desired. If you need to quickly hide the site - just move the cursor to any edge of the window and the window will disappear. It's simple.
