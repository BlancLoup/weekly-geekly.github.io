Tag log

Often people call me IM clients for some advice. Explain something, tell something, express an opinion. When questions are asked by people whom I respect, I try to answer quite fully. The result is a good material on an interesting topic.

This is basically a standard practice, I myself appeal to people who understand something better than me to cover these issues. As a result, a good FAQ is accumulated in the logs, which would be useful for many other people. But the principles of IM clients are old and there is no tagging. And useful often gets bogged down in hundreds of lddydbr messages.

Immediately save the necessary dialogue is rarely obtained. Therefore, I propose to tag these useful messages yourself, for example, by sending the keyword "! Useful". Then the search will be easy to find.

We look wider: you can create (or rather take the already familiar and ready, let's say from a set of your tags to a blog, mail or files) a set of correct tags:! Useful,! Usability,! Ppc,! Private,! Sql,! Seo,! Design ! idea,! cyberculture,! google,! socnet, etc. There will be a small spam to the interlocutor, but I think he will also appreciate the subsequent possibility of the tags and will start using it himself.

For 8 years, I have probably had thousands of disclosures of interesting questions on my ICQ logs. But in these megabytes of logs you will dig ... I would have come up with this idea in my head earlier ...

Maybe in some IM client it is already implemented inside?
