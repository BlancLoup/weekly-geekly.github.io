Mention users


It was possible to mention the user in the post or comments using a special tag, but now this trifle has become more functional. Just in a special way we write the nickname of the right person, and he will receive a message that he is mentioned in one place or another.

Notifications will come at the mention almost everywhere:
- in the post and in the comments to it
- in the question , in the commentary to it, in the answer and in the comment to the answer
- in the comment to the events

To mention a user is easy, any of the following combinations work:
- habitual from twitter @login
- a little bit geeky <~ login>
- familiar to us <hh user = login>

Earlier this function was tested on Avtokadabra . You can practice in the comments to this post, but do not abuse - mention users only when they are in demand in the discussion.
