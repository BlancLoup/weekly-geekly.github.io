Samsung Needle Slim - 3mm thick TV



Samsung Electronics announced the 40-inch LED TV Needle Slim with a record thickness - only 3 millimeters. Until now, only Sony XEL-1 could boast of such squeakiness, but its diagonal is only 11 inches.

So, if Samsung is cunning about the thinnest TV in the world, it is definitely not lying about the world's thinnest 40-inch TV box.

Needle Slim Full supports 1080p HD resolution and boasts a contrast level of 5,000: 1, as well as a screen refresh rate of 120 Hz.

When, how much and who will be able to purchase a novelty, unfortunately, is not yet known.
