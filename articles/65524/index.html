Want to be inside a radio-controlled car?



In 2008, Nikon announced the release of a unique in its kind portable media player called Media Port UP300x. Novelty is a futuristic-looking headphones with a display for viewing video





In fact, Media Port UP is a portable mini-computer or media player with headphones that can be worn right on the head. The size of the display of the novelty is very small (0.44) inches, but, according to the company's explanation, modern optics will create the effect of viewing a 50-inch screen from a distance of 3 meters. On this screen, you can view video files in MPEG-1, MPEG-2 and WMV formats, and the resolution supported is 640 x 480 pixels. In addition, thanks to the implemented support for Wi-Fi 802.11g, the device owner can visit websites and even buy music.



The device has 4 or 8 gigabytes of internal memory for storing content. The novelty works from two standard AA batteries. It is estimated that this is enough to watch a 120-minute video or 270 minutes of listening to music.

And now I will tell you what is the meaning of the foregoing.

A miniature wireless WiFi video camera is installed on a radio-controlled car (for example, Mini-Z ), which transmits a signal to the Media Port UP300x.



The result is the effect of your full presence inside the Mini-Z , racing on the highway:



You can buy the headphones yourself in online stores in Japan ... the price of Media Port UP300x is $ 745 excluding delivery. A solution based on the Media Port UP300x for Mini-Z is not yet possible to buy - this is a concept. While it is only run in on the Mini-Z track of the Tokyo Club KYOSHO OMOTESANDO and anyone can test it in August.

On the other hand, you can not wait until such a decision will begin to sell in a single box. Video glasses and wireless miniature video cameras are sold freely in Moscow ... The solution will be much cheaper, but the effect is the same.
