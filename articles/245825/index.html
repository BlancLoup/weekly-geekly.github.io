Black Friday 2014. Results


November 27 at 19:00 Black Friday began, which lasted 3 days. Like last year, I followed the prices of goods before, during and after Black Friday. This year, 7 stores were under surveillance: sotmarket, ozon, enter, technopark, mvideo, euroset, svyaznoy. Under the cut will be posted analysis of the prices of goods for each store.

The analysis period covers prices from November 10 to December 1. Total number of products: 25,981. Number of products shopping:
  • sotmarket: 10 642
  • ozon: 2,720
  • enter: 2 405
  • technopark: 898
  • mvideo: 3 849
  • euroset: 2,257
  • svyaznoy: 3 210
