For distributing pornography through file-sharing networks, a user from Chelyabinsk received 2 years probation and a fine of 100 thousand rubles.

Traktorozavodsky district court of the city of Chelyabinsk was sentenced to 2 years probation with a probation period of 2 years and sentenced to a fine of 100 thousand rubles an Internet user who in 2007 searched and downloaded pornographic images to his computer. including pornographic images of minors. He stored the downloaded materials on the hard disk of his computer and on CDs, some of which were shared with other users of file-sharing networks. The court found these actions public distribution of pornography. During the search, 165 disks were seized from him, which, according to the expert opinion, contained pornographic materials. During the trial, the defendant fully admitted his guilt. The verdict came into force.
