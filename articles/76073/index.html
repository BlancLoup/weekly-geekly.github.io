The final version of Opera 10.10 browser with Opera Unite platform



November 23, 2009 - Oslo, Norway

The company Opera Software today announced the final version of Opera 10.10 browser with Opera Unite - technology for the personal exchange of content between different devices. Opera Unite is available as a standard browser option, which can be downloaded for free at www.opera.com

Opera invites you to join the Internet revolution. The combination of a web browser and a web server in one software package allows you to change your familiar ideas about semi-private data exchange methods between users.

The main features of Opera 10.10:
- Have you ever given access to 10 GB of photos located on the hard disk of your computer using a browser? Try the Photo Sharing application for Opera Unite.
- Have you ever listened to songs from your home music collection from a mobile phone, gaming console or office computer? We recommend using the Media Player application for Opera Unite.
- Have you ever made coffee, waiting for the web page you need to load? Opera Turbo speeds up the loading of content on low-speed channels up to 8 times compared with other browsers.

Why we believe in Opera Unite:
- With Opera Unite, we become equal users of the Internet. Our computers, phones, game consoles can not only consume, but also distribute data. Now we have a choice with whom, when and how to exchange information.
- The capabilities of personal devices are growing. From on-board computers in cars to entertainment systems on airplanes, from netbooks to telephones ‚Äî any device can not only receive, but also distribute content to other users.
- Our devices will receive such features that they did not have before. Direct communication between them will change the way we interact with applications and data. For example, a mobile phone can become a home electronics control panel, and friends and family members will have access to the data they need, stored on your computer, from anywhere in the world.

About Opera Software ASA
Opera Software ASA is an industry leader in the development of web browsers for desktops, mobile phones and other devices. Opera Browser has received worldwide recognition from users and manufacturers due to its speed, compactness and greater proximity to standards than other browsers. The headquarters of Opera Software is located in Oslo (Norway), and territorial offices are located in several countries around the world. The company's shares are traded on the Oslo Stock Exchange Stock Exchange under the ticker OPERA. More information about the company: www.opera.com
