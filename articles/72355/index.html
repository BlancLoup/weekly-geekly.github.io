If your Acer Revo does not play MKV, either it plays poorly

Hello!
After buying the Acer Revo r3600 (Atom 330/2 gig / 250 screw), I encountered the fact that the declared 1080p videos are going very badly - the full power of the video system is not used by the players.

It was mentioned about KMPlayer, but it did not help me, anyway mkv - slows down!

As a result, having studied the question, I came to this method of making MKV play on Revo without brakes at all!

OS: Windows XP SP3

So, our actions:
1. We install Windows Media Player Classic (I already had K-lite)
2. Download and install - coreAVC professional codec (from www.coreavc.com/ or some other place :) - is there)
3. Launch Media Player Classic
- go to Options
- go to Output, select EVR in DirectShow Video (only for vista / 7 users) - I did not
- click on External Filters
- click on ADD Filter
- choose in the list - CoreAVC Video Decoder
- !!! Be sure to put Prefer on CoreAVC Video Decoder (besides, I removed the daws from all the other filters)

then we climb through the start / start menu in the CoreAVC Video Decoder settings and there we put a daw on CUBA (at the very bottom right)

Everything, now it is possible to enjoy video without brakes!
