How did the new MacBook Pro

Apple has not updated its line of professional laptops for a long time. So long ago, that some foreign blogs seriously called her laptops obsolete and advised them not to buy. Yesterday all this came to an end - at the regular presentation a completely new MacBook Pro was presented.

Software


Apple understands that the target audience of the Macbook Pro works primarily for its laptop, so the presentation presented new versions of two popular professional programs: the installation Final Cut and everyone's favorite Photoshop. Both logically received full support for the Touch Bar.

In Final Cut, when you install the video, an additional panel displays the timeline video, and you can move along it with a simple swipe with your finger, like in some iMovie for iPhone. In the same place, if you moved to the section on working with sound, you can raise and lower the track volume. The same habitual movement.

In Photoshop, the new functionality seems to be implemented even better. Swipe can change the size of the brush, as well as choose the desired color from the scale. An impressive number of shortcuts will be available on the Touch Bar: for example, a hot key for adding files to a project or a separate submenu for layers. All this without the need to crawl the mouse over the mat. In addition, in one tap, you can call the history of all changes to the images and, if desired, quickly return to the desired stage. The updated version of Photoshop promise to release before the end of the year.
