Mitsubishi announced a 23-inch high-speed IPS-monitor



Mitsubishi Electric introduced the Diamondcrysta WIDE RDT231WM-X monitor in the Japanese market. The model is positioned as a competitor to TFT-displays while retaining all the advantages of the IPS-matrix.

The diagonal is 23 inches and supports the resolution of FullHD resolution - 1920x1080 pixels. The dynamic contrast of the monitor is 5,000: 1, the response time is 3.8 ms, the brightness is 300 cd / m2, and the viewing angles are 178 degrees vertically and horizontally.

Like most modern multimedia monitors, the novelty is equipped with two speakers, DVI-D and D-Sub ports. The kit also includes a remote control.
