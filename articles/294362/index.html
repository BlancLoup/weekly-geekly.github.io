The decision to create a service


On June 25, in our group on the business of VKontakte, I voted on whether they needed a callback service for calls from any form on the site (initially it was planned as the main advantage and UTP)



Voting obviously showed that such a service was needed by our audience. So, on June 25, 2015, product development was launched. No name yet. Initially, the idea was to make a product that will call from all forms on the site. Said and done, that day the development of the service began.

Immediately the requirements were set:

  • Service should be very simple;
  • Service should be as described as possible. Each element must contain a certificate to reduce the number of calls;
  • The service should be as automated as possible. What would it serve a minimum of people;
  • We do service without external investments. Only the resources of three people. Me, Igor - development guru and Diana - sales wizard.

All these requirements are derived from previous experience in developing and promoting services. Previously, I wanted to have a big team, which I achieved and there were times when 150 people worked in the company, now there are about 110.

But now I wanted to make the service as automated as possible and as autonomous as possible. What service would do as few people as possible.

During development, it was decided that a single function of the call from the forms would not be enough, and the subscribers of the group began to ask if there would be a button. It was decided to make a full service callback one of the functions of which will be a call from any form on the site.

We decided to chop cabbage from people for each lead as much as 50 rubles. But after the start they decided to revise it, but more on that later.

Launch


Somewhere from 26-27 we started the service in testing. They put the cost of the lead 50 rubles without a call time limit. We decided to sell through cold calls. We made the first installation, received the first payment. But for some reason there was no joy.

Uniqueness


On Friday, July 31, having worked for a week, we sat down and took hold of our heads. Hooked into the office hookah. From hookah smoke dizzy. The kettle did not have time to boil. A cup of a cup of tea and coffee were sent to our stomachs.

We abandon the model that is accepted on the market and turn it upside down. Leave a minute without extra charge, and we will take payment for the service. Sites are visited daily by people including entrepreneurs. Someone wants to install the same widget on your site. Follows the link and puts an affiliate link on its website in the widget. Schematically, the affiliate program can be represented as a diagram
