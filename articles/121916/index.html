Seminar "Java Card - extending the capabilities of smart cards using Java"

I invite everyone to take part in the seminar "Java Card - empowering smart cards using Java."
The seminar will be held at the office of Oracle in St. Petersburg on June 30 at 15.00 in 2202 at 10th Krasnoarmeyskaya 22a, second floor.
Traditionally, we invite outside participants to participate, registration is open to everyone.
oracle.timepad.ru/event/8066

Java Card is a Java smart card platform. At one time, she changed the entire infrastructure of smart cards. Market sectors such as telecommunications and transport, banking and ID, and many others today are actively using the Java Card.
During the presentation, the basic principle of the technology and its basic capabilities will be presented. At the same time, we will show how the Java Card platform is changing in accordance with the new requirements and what can be expected in the future. Special attention will be paid to the use of Java Card technology in practical applications. Moreover, new developments by Oracle in this area will be demonstrated.
The seminar is conducted by Alexander Glassman.
The seminar is designed for 2 hours, including questions and answers.
