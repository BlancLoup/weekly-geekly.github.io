Softline and Microsoft announce MOST-2007 Marathon

Microsoft and Softline invite high school students and students to take part in the MOST-2007 youth marathon : the path to a career .

Participants will receive various prizes, and may also become holders of the international certificate Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS).

The MOST-2007 Marathon is held from February 19 to May 30, 2007. To participate you need to be no older than 20 years old, study in Russia or Kazakhstan and be able to work with Microsoft Office 2000 or Microsoft Office 2003 - during the marathon you will need to show skills in such packages as Word, Excel and Power Point.
