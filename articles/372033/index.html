Kingston Introduces Memory Card for Industry and Extreme Conditions

Hi, Giktayms! Kingston introduces the Industrial Temperature microSD UHS-I (SDCIT) memory card for use in extreme conditions. The drive has a wide operating temperature range - from -40 ¬∞ C to 85 ¬∞ C - which ensures stable operation in situations in which an ordinary microSD card can easily fail. Under the cut there is more information about the new product, and immediately after the appearance of the sample in the Kingston blog there will be a detailed review of this memory card.



Industrial Temperature microSD UHS-I drives are used in heat and severe frost: for example, in test equipment for working with high temperatures and in equipment, which often remains for a long time in the cold. High durability helps them withstand multiple connections to various devices and shutdown after reading or writing data, and MLC NAND memory chips make it possible to record large amounts of data. The novelty is presented with versions from 8 to 64 GB.3



Kingston carefully checks all memory cards during production. Each accumulator passes the test for the effect of thermal cycles under extreme conditions, many hours of testing with changes in humidity parameters, as well as testing in a chamber with a wide range of varying temperatures. In addition, all devices are tested for water resistance, impact resistance, resistance to extreme heat and cold, vibrations and X-rays. The user can be sure that photos, videos and other important data will be reliably protected.



Support for the UHS-I Speed ‚Äã‚ÄãClass U1 interface with a read speed of up to 90 MB / s and write up to 45 MB / s allows new products to work effectively with applications that operate with large amounts of data. Consistently high write speeds improve data integrity and reduce the risk of data loss during transmission. In addition, it greatly speeds up the download of files on a PC. The miniature form factor has provided microSD cards with popularity as a standard storage for expanding memory in smartphones, tablets, navigation systems, and industrial equipment. And with an adapter for the SDHC / SDXC format, they can be used with equipment that does not support regular microSD cards.
SDCIT memory cards are covered by a five year warranty.

More detailed specifications of the new product can be found on the Kingston website.
