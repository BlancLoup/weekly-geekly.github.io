On Friday, an official warning appeared for developers that the site would cease to function on December 17th.
Thus, in less than a month, online access to distributions, codes, Symbian encyclopedias and other resources required by developers under Symbian OS will stop. However, it will be possible to order the necessary documentation by e-mail or on DVD / USB. Sending files by mail will begin on January 31, recipients will pay shipping.
