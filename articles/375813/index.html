The automatic table will change its height and tell you when to sit and when to stand.



The ‚Äúsmart‚Äù table from Stir not only tells you that the time has come to change positions (from sitting to standing or vice versa), but also thanks to the built-in motors, it will automatically rise or fall. This is the second generation of similar devices from Stir.



Miracle table called Stir Kinetic Desk M1 must be connected to the outlet, while it itself is equipped with four outlets, and besides this - a computer running Linux, a 1 GHz processor, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth (because everything becomes better if you add Bluetooth ) and a 5-inch touch screen. Thermal sensors under the table top determine your current position, write everything into the base and, based on internal algorithms, periodically make recommendations for changing positions.



Without permission, the table does not move - you need to confirm the operation by poking at the screen. After confirmation, the motors built into the legs change the height of the table top (the height range is adjustable in height). The CEO of the company, JP Labross (who participated in the development of the first iPod at the time), argues that in order to maintain health, a person needs to change his posture every hour - sitting for hours is as harmful as standing for hours. This table weighs about 70 kg, and its cost in the US is $ 2990.
