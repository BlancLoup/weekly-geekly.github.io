Caricatura.ru is looking for an investor

While we are discussing the creation of startups and the search for money to implement their own ideas, investors are looking for already successful projects, and they do not need billions of dollars.

Today, the search for resources for the development of the notorious site caricatura.ru and attended to Ruslan Kurepin .
The catalog exists for the seventh year and today has more than 13,500 copyrighted works of more than 700 cartoonists-caricaturists.

In general, an interesting period. This season we have real opportunities to invest in Russian IT companies - in Mambu , if there is a lot of money, and in Caricature , if there is a little less money.


There are two options for investing money in the project.

First: the general sponsor .
An individual or legal entity possessing, in addition to the official status of the General Sponsor, the exclusive right to place advertising and information on the site. It is possible to create special sections, change the design of the site, modify the supporting scripts for the interests of the sponsor.

Sponsorship is sold for a period of 3 months. Approximate cost: $ 5000 per month. Under certain conditions, significant discounts are possible.

Second: investor .
Individual or legal entity wishing to invest in the development of the project: promotion and popularization of the Internet component and the creation of an offline agency of cartoons, comics, collage and cartoon. Establishing an offline agency requires the usual for such cases.
infrastructure: accountant, lawyer, managers, office, etc.

To date, several dozens-hundreds of print publications use the materials of the site for free, often only because there is no streamlined administrative and technical ability to do so for a fee. Artists dream of selling their work through this agency and fulfilling orders for electronic and traditional media, they are happy to illustrate books and souvenirs.

The investor is offered a venture investment or a direct purchase of a stake in the project. The size of the share depends on the investment conditions.

You can contact Ruslan using public contacts.
