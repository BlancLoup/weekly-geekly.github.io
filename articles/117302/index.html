Home and personal robots available in Russia

Robots ...

Each of us has our own associations with this word, but mostly many people imagine something like this thing ...

And maybe even a more advanced design, reminiscent of the robot NS-5 from the movie "I am a robot."
Over the past few months, by the nature of my work, I have been studying the situation in the market of home and service robotics in Russia. What is this market? What can modern robot-toys do? What is the average cost of a robot and which robots are suitable as a gift? I will tell you about all this a little now.

A bit of theory and numbers
Currently, robots have entered human life in different areas, but there is still no clear separation among robotic devices, as well as a single software platform. Different manufacturers make different and totally incompatible hardware. Technologies and tools are sharpened every time for specific problems, it is almost impossible to reuse them. The main stumbling block when creating robots is artificial intelligence.

Many decision-making devices, based on data received from sensors, can also theoretically be considered robots. But in this case, such devices can include elevators that we use every day, and anti-lock braking systems that help avoid accidents.

Nowadays, the leader in the density of robots per capita is Japan, where there are 295 robots per 10,000 enterprise workers, the United States and Europe are far behind, the gap with other countries is even more significant. In Europe, there are 50 robots per 10,000 workers, the majority of which are engaged in heavy industrial production, pharmaceuticals and the automotive industry.

Compared with the rest of the world, Russia as a whole is scientifically in no way inferior, including robotics. However, technical implementations are far behind after the collapse of the USSR.
Robots focused on the leisure and entertainment industry, as well as helper robots (vacuum cleaners, lawn mowers, pool washers), are in the greatest demand in Russia.

Android robots (toys and semi-professional)
The most simple android robots are mainly represented by the goods of the American company WowWee . The most famous series of Robosapien is a humanoid robot that can be controlled, both from the console and programmed a sequence of movements. What can such robots? First, how strange it sounds, they can walk! As I already mentioned, mechatronics is a complex science, and to teach a robot to walk properly is not an easy task. Therefore, in low-priced products like Robosapien, naturally this is implemented relatively simply. Robots of this series can also move hands and head, and the built-in camera allows them not only to orient themselves in space, but also, for example, to memorize some objects (the Robosapien V2 remembers the color of the ball using a color sensor). The most interesting feature of Robosapien is its programming. You can easily program it to perform any movements, and this is done manually: you set one position yourself with your hands, then the next, and so on. The average cost of Sowen robots from WowWee is about 7-10,000 rubles.


A more serious model is, for example, i-Sobot from Tomy . First, it is the smallest in the world, according to the Guinness Book, a humanoid robot that is mass-produced (height 17 cm). Developed in 2007, it is still quite advanced in its class. The robot already has several modes: ordinary remote, voice, programming and special effects. Can perform up to 157 commands. 17 servos (and the same degrees of freedom) plus a gyro sensor - a good filling for a robot, costing less than 10,000 rubles.

If you decide to buy
In RuNet, there are now about 25 shops selling robots, and usually either this is a robot toy store or vacuum cleaner robots. The latter have become quite popular especially among the residents of the capital, who do not have enough time for cleaning and are ready for 20,000 rubles (the average price of a vacuum cleaner robot) to get rid of this headache. The most useful shops in RuNet:

RoboticsLab - a fairly wide range of thematic store will allow you to pick up various robots, ranging from toys, to designers and components.
RoboTronic is one of the oldest stores, in which, in addition to robots, other gadgets are represented.
NanoJam - here you can buy not only toys, but also research models and platforms.
LeFutur is a famous store, part of the range of which is also represented by WowWee toys and funny little retro robots.

At present, the commercial market for domestic robots is growing at an average of 15% per year and, according to experts, a big boom awaits us soon, as it happened with computer technologies and products.
