Gfranq.com

What is it?

Gfranq is a photo processing solution for professional filters in a few simple steps and a personalized pinacotheque with social networking integration.

The idea is the ability to instantly share the processed photo from any device: computer, webcam, phone.


Who is it for?

Sustained interest in photography brings together the generation of social networks firmly and for a long time. All around they want to have beautiful pictures that are not ashamed to show friends. Such a desire is quite understandable - who among us has not laid out his holiday adventures while waiting for a flurry of comments? And secretly upset if there were no reactions? People want to look beautiful in the photo, unusual - they want to be liked. They offer professional editors, creating the desired effect. But how many people understand them, spend time and money on studying complex programs? Units The rest are content with a couple of mobile applications or suffer about "lay out - do not lay out." For those who save time, money and effort, but at the same time want quality photos, a free online service Gfranq.com was created.



Minimalistic interface of the site is able to master even a child. All you need to do for the miraculous transformation of a standard photo in lomo or, for example, a retro picture:

  • Upload a photo from a computer or webcam by simply clicking on the button.



  • If necessary, rotate the photo and crop it.





  • Select by scrolling and apply one of the 42 current filters.



  • Now you can send the transformed photo to any social network and save it on your computer. If you want to try another filter, you can always start the process again.


The processed photo is automatically saved in a personal pinacotec - a dynamic storage of images on the user's page. Photos can be viewed in blocks, scrolling through them, or in an enlarged version by clicking on them with the mouse buttons. A kind of kaleidoscope of life, photo-story with the possibility of a census.



For fans of mobile photos, the Android application and the mobile version are already available. Applications do not litter the phone's memory - all processing occurs on the site servers. The quality is fully preserved. Soon there will be applications for iPhone, Blackberry and social networks.

PS Project Gfranq 2 months, it is growing rapidly and, of course, wants to conquer the world. Up to the ideal there is still a lot of work, but we are burning with what we are doing.
