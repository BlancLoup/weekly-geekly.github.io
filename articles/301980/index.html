Exclusive interview with PayOnline CEO



The Advapay information portal, as part of a series of online interviews with top officials of Russian and European companies in the market of payments and services, on May 26 met with PayOnline CEO Marat Abasaliyev.

From the first day of the interview, on the Advapay * forum, questions began to appear that our viewers wanted to ask Marat.

Our guest was asked about the fate of plastic cards, legal features of doing business in Europe, innovative technologies, features when working with online stores, payment terminals and competition in the payment market.

The dialogue turned out to be interesting due to the detailed and detailed answers of Marat and equally interesting questions from our viewers.
