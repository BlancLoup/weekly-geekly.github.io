Online course on iOS development for beginners from Redmadrobot and Contented



For those who have already dealt with the code, but are not yet able to write for iOS, along with Contented, we launched an application development course.

Our team leaders have included in the course what the novice developer needs to know in order to study further on their own and find their first job:
- the basics of Swift;
- mechanics of interaction with server API and backend development;
- features of working with databases and frameworks for interacting with them.

There will be homework that needs to be done, tests that need to be passed, and additional materials that are worth learning. During the course, students will write a client-server application, and teachers-robots will help to sort out all the errors and questions that have arisen.

Anton Polyakov, iOS developer Redmadrobot:

- When you start to learn a new area, the first problem is the threshold of entry. You immediately find many articles and tutorials, but they all rely on the knowledge base, which should be the default student. The meaning of the training is just to hold the student's hand on the basic concepts and tell what to read first, what's after, what topics you should not pay attention to and return to them later. Plus, since we work in this area, in addition to general knowledge, we share personal experience that is not recorded in books or articles.

Admission to the course started on April 9th. For Habr's readers, we have the promotional code WR5I37 - according to it you can sign up for the course until April 30 at a discount.

Details and recording here: ios.contented.cd
