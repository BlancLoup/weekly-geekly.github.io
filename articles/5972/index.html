Travel@Mail.Ru now with video

Users of Travel Travel @ Mail.Ru got the opportunity to post videos in the "Traveler reviews" section. Previously, one could only accompany his impressions with photographs. Now Mail.Ru plans to create a base of travel video clips as part of the service.

In April, several more services will be released on the Journey, said Anna Artamonova, marketing and PR director for Mail.Ru. One of them will be the service of "burning" tours. For travel agencies will be offered an auction system for the distribution of advertising.

According to the company, the audience of Travel@Mail.Ru is about 90 thousand people per day and more than 1 million per month. During the holiday season, a significant increase in the latter is expected - by 1.5-2 times. Now the resource contains information about 4.5 million rounds.
