They waited. Release PC version of GTA V



Rejoice, for it has happened! After years of waiting, the company RockStar still made happy PC players. Now you can play GTA V with the help of a personal computer, which stands under the table. Today, at exactly 2:00 am Moscow time, the game became available on Steam . While we were waiting for GTA V, a new generation of consoles was released! And the GTA V version for these new consoles has been released! Even Gabe admitted that there would be no Half-Life 3 , and people waited patiently! And they waited! For only $ 39.99, you can plunge into the world of violence, banditry, drama of the main storyline of GTA V!

For quite a long time on the portal rockstargame.ru system requirements for the game were published, which, by the way, coincide with information from Steam:

MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
Operating system: Windows 8.1 64 Bit, Windows 8 64 Bit, Windows 7 64 Bit Service Pack 1, Windows Vista 64 Bit Service Pack 2
Video card: NVIDIA 9800 GT 1GB / AMD HD 4870 1GB (DX 10, 10.1, 11)
Processor: Intel Core 2 Quad CPU Q6600 @ 2.40GHz (4 CPUs) / AMD Phenom 9850 Quad-Core Processor (4 CPUs) @ 2.5GHz
RAM (RAM): 4GB
Free disk space: 65GB
Sound Card: 100% DirectX 10 compatible

RECOMMENDED SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
Operating system: Windows 8.1 64 Bit, Windows 8 64 Bit, Windows 7 64 Bit Service Pack 1
Video card: NVIDIA GTX 660 2GB / AMD HD7870 2GB
Processor: Intel Core i5 3470 @ 3.2GHZ (4 CPUs) / AMD X8 FX-8350 @ 4GHZ (8 CPUs)
Random access memory (RAM): 8GB Free disk space: 65GB
Sound Card: 100% DirectX 10 compatible

Recommended requirements are not impressive, yes. But the developers say that for owners of powerful PCs will be where to turn. Including the promised lack of FPS restrictions in the game , fine-tuning the graphics, such a load as auto-traffic on the streets, etc. And yes, 4K is not the limit , they promise that GTA V will run on 8K monitors, yes, in real 8K, again, without FPS restrictions.
