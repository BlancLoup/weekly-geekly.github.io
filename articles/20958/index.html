Mouse vacuum cleaner - clean and tidy on your desk

The Japanese company Thanko came up with an interesting solution for those who are too lazy to constantly keep order on their desk. The computer mouse has turned ... into an elegant mini vacuum cleaner for your desk!

The mouse connects to a computer via a USB 1.1 port and has an optical sensor at a resolution of 800dpi - seemingly nothing unusual at first glance.
But the mini-vacuum cleaner built into it makes it simply indispensable and perhaps the most necessary thing, allowing us not to forget about cleanliness and order among the working day.

The dimensions of the mouse are 62x115x40 mm, and it can collect small debris and crumbs through a small hole in the base. It is powered by a mini-miracle from USB and does not require additional batteries.
You can buy it now only on the manufacturer's website for $ 19.
