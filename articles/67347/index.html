Thunderbird update

Yesterday, FC11 updated Thunderbird to beta3 version. Among the pleasant changes:

- A new theme with new icons, IMHO looks nice, although a bit unusual;
- stopped to fail and slow down the search folders on the imap-server;
- startup and shutdown occur very quickly;
- A new viewing mode has appeared - if you select several letters, then in the viewing area there will appear a list of initial text fragments for each of these letters, and their total number;

I especially liked the last feature - fantastically convenient, and does not slow down.

Among the shortcomings, in my opinion, it is worth noting the opening of new letters in the tabs by default. I'm not used to it - it's more convenient to work with windows, when the list of all letters is immediately visible in the taskbar. :) But, in general, everything is much better than before.
