Google will spend 200 million dollars on marketing new channels



Google Corporation, as previously reported, continues to fill YouTube with premium content, create new channels, sign contracts with various celebrities, who are leading new channels. Earlier, the corporation spent about 100 million dollars for these purposes. Now another $ 200 million will be spent on marketing - after all, new channels are good, but new users are also needed.

In order to attract new users, various kinds of advertising of new channels will be displayed, both on YouTube and the entire Google Display Network. By the way, the other day the corporation announced the signing of contracts with new celebrities, musical groups and individual performers. In order to make it easier for users to find the most interesting and relevant content for them, the developers will create a special guide / search engine.

As a result, YouTube management hopes to significantly change both the content of the service and the attitude of users to the service itself.
