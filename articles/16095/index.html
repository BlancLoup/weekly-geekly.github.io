Good News: And again about Google shares



While the majority of investors mourn the lost capital of Google shares, Bonnie Brown - the company's full-time masseuse, gradually comes to life. When she received a part of shares as a compensation package when she started working, she became a multimillionaire, selling her share at the very peak - $ 701.2 per share.

The exact amount of income, for obvious reasons, was not disclosed, but if we take into account the fact that Brown came to Google in 1999, when the staff of the future giant was less than 50 people, you can imagine how impressive it was.
