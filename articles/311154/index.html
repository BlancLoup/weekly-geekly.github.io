Smart Bunker FX - solution for Microcentre Data

Schneider Electric's Smart Bunker FX - Micro Data Center (Micro Data Center) solution (which includes power supply, cooling system, security and monitoring tools) - everything that is present in traditional information centers, but in an isolated, autonomous, secure and sturdy rack.



Smart Bunker FX is an easy-to-deploy and highly secure Micro Data Center solution, consisting of backup power, cooling and control systems mounted in a single package. It is ideal for locations where there is not enough space to accommodate IT, requiring additional levels of physical protection or security.

Smart Bunker FX data microcenters are collected and verified directly at the factory. They are delivered to the consumer completely ready, with the subsequent minimum local installation in the client's room.

The independent and compact design of the devices minimizes the time of their commissioning and the complexity associated with preparing a traditional server room. This design also allows their painless movement to another location.

Smart Bunker FX comes in versions that meet the industry standard 23U, 42U and 46U. They are designed for loads ranging from 3.5 to 30 kW, and thus correspond to a wide range of computational needs.

Advantages of the solution


Each Smart Bunker FX Series device contains an uninterruptible power supply (Uninterruptible Power Supply, UPS), power distribution system, active cooling, environmental sensors and software for remote control.

All devices of the Smart Bunker FX line are made in accordance with the ‚ÄúPlug & Play‚Äù principles, which makes it easy and safe to deploy IT equipment.

For more complete protection of critical systems, additional options are provided, such as fire suppression, cooling from an external industrial system, biometric security tools, and others (see below).

In entry-level configuration, Smart Bunker FX includes a UPS, a cooling system, a Power Distribution Unit (PDU) and remote monitoring tools. Some cooling, safety and redundancy options are available on request.

Smart Bunker FX is an ideal solution for organizations that need a fast, scalable and cost-effective information center infrastructure or want to build a distributed data center asset system (distributed data center assets).

Disaster recovery, cloud computing, Internet of Things (IoT) computing, edge computing, and all possible industrial applications that require high security are all implemented in Smart Bunker FX for those who need data storage and management with such capabilities.

Advantages of the SmartBunker FX Solution


Protect critical data . The device protects sensitive IT equipment from aggressive environments, vandalism and natural disasters. The device is thermally insulated and maintains the required temperature inside the cabinet, ensuring the durability of servers and network equipment.

Simplify deployment . The device replaces small server rooms in remote locations and directly in production workshops. They are produced in finished form and tested before delivery. Quickly and easily deployed at the customer site without the need to create a special server room and a separate air filter system.

Minimum waiting time . The data processing point is close to the production site of the main operations in order to minimize network delays. This improves the work of the enterprise in real time, which is important for research, local production and quick execution of the main work of the enterprise.

Thus, the key characteristics of Smart Bunker FX include the performance of Self-Contained, full readiness and repeatability. This allows organizations to quickly create a repeatable data center infrastructure for any application and geographic location. There are also several available extensions and add-ons that make Smart Bunker FX compliant with seismic, maritime, and military requirements, as well as shielding requirements in remote locations and aggressive environments.

Practical application of Smart Bunker FX

Enterprises and collocative solutions (Enterprise & Colo) . The device is ideal for organizations that need fast and cost-effective data center solutions and for those who want to scale their on-premise infrastructure to minimize latency and eliminate bandwidth problems for IoT and computing on the edge of the network.

Industrial production (Industrial) . An aggressive and unstable environment requires special physical protection of critical data systems, both for research and development (R & D) and for production. For example, due to strict regulatory requirements and the growing conversion of operations to digital form, oil and gas production areas need distributed computing systems that are installed in remote locations and an aggressive environment.

Health care . Spatial limitations and the growing need for digital technology today require the use of data microcenters in all departments of hospitals.

Military applications (Military) . Military operations require durable, mobile and easy-to-deploy solutions in order to provide control both at the front line and in remote locations in an aggressive environment. Smart Bunker FX is well suited for military applications as a tactical information center, since it is very easy to move and adjust to meet the requirements for use at sea, seismic and blast resistance, and shielding.

Smart Bunker FX can be equipped with the appropriate means of power supply and cooling, allowing to receive up to 30 kW of load in a separate rack. This allows enterprises to run HPC applications and create high density zones, like the development of legacy infrastructure data centers.

Private clouds . Smart Bunker FX is a great tool for implementing a private cloud. It is well suited for those enterprises that want to use hybrid cloud models, when only part of their applications are hosted in public clouds.

Disaster Recovery . Organizations that need disaster recovery of data and applications can rely on Smart Bunker FX to copy the most important information ‚Äî including in remote locations where building a large traditional data center can be difficult and time consuming.
