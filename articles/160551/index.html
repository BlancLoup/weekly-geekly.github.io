Sony Xperia V will appear in Russia in December



Sony Mobile continues to bend its line with the creation of waterproof smartphones in all segments, but Sony Xperia V is also the first Sony to work in Russian LTE networks. And you can buy it from the beginning of December on the recommended price of 24,990 rubles.

From the features of the device I also want to note the following:

  • New display production technology Sensor on lens, thanks to which the screen is brighter and has wider viewing angles
  • Advanced Mobile Bravia Engine 2 technology, which makes the image more realistic
  • Camera with a new 13MP Exmor R matrix with enhanced shooting capabilities and a graphical editor
  • The most powerful Qualcomm 8960 Snapdragon S4 chipset with a dual-core 1.5GHz Krait processor in the Xperia line
  • NFC module for quick activation of functions, data transfer and payment
  • Dust and moisture protection (IP55 / IP57)
  • Work in Russian LTE networks




The official package includes:

  • Smartphone Sony Xperia V (LT25i) (black soft-touch, white matte, pink matte)
  • Battery BA800 1700 mAh
  • EP880 Compact High Power Charging Unit
  • Wired stereo headset MH750
  • Charging and Sync Cable

From December, Sony Xperia V will go on sale immediately and will be available in various retail chains and stores, and you can pre-order it on the Sony Store website .
