Yandex banned Google?



Yandex nullified TIC at domains google.com and google.ru. The link "" Google "- search engine" from Yandex.Catalog now leads to a porn site.

search.yaca.yandex.ru/yca/cy/ch/google.ru

What's happening?

UPD. Yandex representative response :

The error is already fixed, justice is restored. Thank you for your vigilance :)
