Gamejack for Lua-developers on the Corona and Defold engines


Partners and friends Appodeal, Corona Labs, together with Defold and DevGAMM Minsk , announce the launch of the #CoronaDefoldJam game based on Lua . Jam will be held from July 28 to September 30, 2017 on the itch.io platform. The requirement for the participants is only one thing - games within the framework of the jam must be created on Corona or Defold. If you have never used these engines, not scary: both platforms are easy to learn and have very friendly communities that will always help you figure it out. In general, #CoronaDefoldJam is the perfect reason to learn new tools for creating games; especially since the jam is supported by the legendary John Romero.



Jam rules allow the use of ready-made archives (for example, this one ), but the game must be created specifically for #CoronaDefoldJam. Another good news is that the jam starts with Ludum Dare 39 and will have a common theme with it. It will appear on the page of jam on July 28-29. Itch.io will allow to promote games in a wide international circle of developers and fans of indie.
Jam - a great opportunity to finally take up the development of the game, which you have long thought. In addition, the organizers promise prizes:


  • 10,000 installations from Corona with an advertising campaign in 25,000+ applications worldwide for the best game on Corona;
  • For all winners - the opportunity to exhibit with the created game on DevGAMM Minsk (November 16-17, 2017), where the awards ceremony will take place;
  • Wacom Intuous Pro for the best art on Corona;
  • Swedish JAYS headphones hand-built for the best game on Defold;
  • Three Amazon Echo Dot for the best technology at Defold;
  • Credits for Steam or itch.io to choose from + Spine ESS license.

More information about the conditions can be found at the link .
