We work on the Federal Law-54: How to automate couriers to meet the new requirements of the law?

In Russia, the law on the use of cash registers has been developed for several years. And now, almost two months, as FZ-54 came into force.


If with the transition to new conditions of work in offline retail, everything is more or less clear, how did the adoption of the new law affect the mobile employees of delivery services? This will be discussed in this article.



How did the couriers work before the release of FZ-54 and how now?


So, what is the new algorithm for working with cash registers now waiting for couriers, as well as how to facilitate the work of already loaded employees?


Let's see.


Prior to the entry into force of the law, the courier could have arrived at the customer with a ready sum check without the name of the goods and delivered the goods. The buyer could not be sure of the legality of the delivered goods and its subsequent warranty service. There were cases when a courier could sell a ‚Äúhis‚Äù product to a client or render an unrecorded service, and the company lost not only its profit, but also its reputation.


Now the courier is obliged to punch a new sample receipt within 5 minutes after payment of the goods, which is transmitted to the cloud operator of fiscal data (CRF), which will contain all the necessary details of the seller‚Äôs company and the buyer will not have to keep a paper check to contact the warranty service, since You can always print it from the CRF data.

If earlier it was enough just to drive in the amount at an autonomous cash desk, now, at the request of Federal Law No. 54 , the check must contain a specification for each item with indication of the name and its value. To do this, the courier must have special software for printing the receipt.


Let us dwell on the main changes in the work:


  • Buying online with payment via courier

If the customer chooses the goods in the online store and plans to pay for the purchase with electronic or cash money upon receipt, the courier needs to have a portable CCP. Depending on the preferences of the client, he should be provided with an electronic or paper check.



  • Payment of goods on the seller‚Äôs website

If the goods are paid by credit card directly on the site, the situation changes. The electronic check must be generated by the site and sent to the email or mobile number of the customer within 5 minutes. The issuance of a printed check in this case is optional, so the courier delivers only the goods from the invoice.



  • Refusal of the client from a part of the delivered goods

According to the new law FZ-54 , if the customer wants to abandon all or several items from the list of delivered goods before payment, the courier is obliged to correct the check on the spot and print out to reflect these changes. If the client wants to return any items of the order after the settlement, the courier must print a new return check for these items and return the money for them. Previously, the return receipt was printed only within one shift, according to the new requirements - at any time.
