I am writing to support and get the answer ...


Hello.

In accordance with clause 4.6.2.3 of the Agreement on the use of the Yandex.Money system, expenditure transactions in your account no. Xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx are suspended to clarify the purposes for which you use the payment system.

You can view the text of the agreement on the money.yandex.ru/doc.xml?id=522764 page.
Unfortunately, we are not entitled to discuss the decision to suspend operations by e-mail or by phone.

Please note: expenditure transactions are suspended temporarily, until a positive decision on your application.
The statement must tell you what you are using the Yandex.Money system, as well as comment on the payments and credits that you made on xx.02.2010.

117393, Moscow, PO Box 273, OOO PS Yandex.Money

Applications from minors are accepted only in the presence of parents or with the provision of their notarized written consent to use the System.

Thank you for your request.
