On the hackathon of Igromir Games Coding Night "Wins Evil"!



On the evening of October 3, at 19.00, the special night hackathon Games Coding Night ended at the Aquarium Hotel (Crocus Expo).

38 registered teams competed for the right to be winners and, after 24 hours of continuous work, defended their projects before the jury.

After the final results were announced, it became known that the winners of the evil team become the winners and owners of the main prize!

On October 2, the hackathon participants listened to the reports of IT experts and set about creating their own projects.



The goal was to invent and create a prototype of the game application, which must be presented to the jury 22 hours after the start of the hackathon.

With the help of invited experts, participants managed to get acquainted with the features of game development on various gaming platforms : HTML5, Unity , XNA, Monogame , etc.

The teams paid special attention to the development tools of Visual Studio and Unity3D .
