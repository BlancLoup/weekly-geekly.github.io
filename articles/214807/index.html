Wikipedia will mark writers by request

Paid edits and their authors on Wikipedia may become more visible: a new amendment to the Terms of Use is discussed in the Meta-Wiki. Now the proposed amendment is still under discussion by the wiki community. Leave your comment on the discussion page until March 21. After this, the amendment will be submitted to the Board of Trustees of Wikimedia.

Wikipedia.org ranks among the ten most visited websites in the world (6th place according to Alexa ranking). The number of active users involved in the creation of Wikipedia, in January 2014 amounted to 76 thousand people .
