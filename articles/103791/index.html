Baseline conditions

Question: how to correctly configure and conduct a "one-cent" RK in Yandex Direct?

Step 1. We select words



Rule number 1 in the selection of words - do not chase the RF queries! By definition, any RF request carries a lot of thematic noise compared to the MF. As a result, it is much easier to keep an acceptable CTR on 10 derived phrases with a frequency of 1000 per month than to keep the same CTR on the 1st source phrase with a frequency of 10,000 per month. To show ads in the wrong place and in the wrong people is an unaffordable luxury for ours, let's be frank, a beggarly budget.

For beginners it is optimal to focus on the figure of 100-2000 hits per month. It is possible and less, but only if you have an infinite amount of free time to tune and manage all this low-frequency home gardens.

We go to wordstat.yandex.ru and poke around in queries. We are looking for banal and most incredible key phrases that will be interesting to our audience. Among them sometimes come across unthinkable words and sentences: buy in full.

Pick up the request of the top level "grain" and plow the field ...

Step 2. Sift words.



We take our footcloth out of requests, add them up and drive them into ‚Äúan estimate of the budget of an advertising campaign‚Äù (Yandex predictor). In parallel, we brush requests.
