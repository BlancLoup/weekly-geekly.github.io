HP sells top smartphone Pre 3 for EUR50


The attraction of unprecedented generosity from Hewlett Packard continues: while users are wondering if this is a marketing move or a move dictated by the real state of affairs, HP decided to sell off the top-end Pre 3 smartphone for EUR50. For greater surrealism of what is happening, here are the specs:

Display : TFT capacitive touchscreen, 16M colors; 480 x 800 pixels, 3.58 inches;
Memory : 8Gb ROM, 512Mb RAM;
Connectivity : HSDPA, 7.2 Mbps; HSUPA, 5.76 Mbps; Wi-Fi 802.11 a / b / g / n, dual-band; Bluetooth v2.1 with A2DP, EDR; microUSB v2.0;
Camera : 5 MP, 2592x1944 pixels, autofocus, LED flash; Video 720p; Secondary VGA camera;
Iron : 1.4 GHz processor, Adreno 205 GPU, Qualcomm MSM8255 Snapdragon
Battery : Li-Ion 1230 mAh
Keyboard : QWERTY, sliding out;

Unfortunately, only residents of France and the United Kingdom will be able to purchase this device without binding and a contract with an operator at that price. In Germany, as HP said, the phone is already sold out. On the date of commencement of sales HP will notify in addition.
