MDK2 returns in HD


Interplay and Beamdog have announced the imminent release of MDK2 for Wii and MDK2 HD for personal computers. It is curious that for some reason (for now?) A version for the Macintosh is not planned, although the first part of the MDK was bundled with the first iMac 1998 release.

As you probably know, the first part of the cult MDK series was created by Shiny Entertainment and released by Interplay for PC in 1997, later versions for Mac and PlayStation followed. In 2000, a sequel developed by BioWare studio was released on PC and Dreamcast, which a year later was transferred to the PlayStation 2.

Well, MDK2 HD for PC will be distributed through the online service Beamdog. Unfortunately, the release date has not yet been announced.
