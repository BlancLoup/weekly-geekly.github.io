Where is the paradise or funny cases from Apple Review Team


Photo 1-2. From the photos you can determine where the Apple Review Team is sitting with an accuracy of 500 meters.

Friday, I venture to talk about a couple of funny cases when publishing an application on the Apple store.

For air travel, I made a small application. More precisely, three applications. With the same functionality, pictures and descriptions up to a comma. Why three? Because the test of your application can take people with a different perception of the world. As for the Russian mote, the German is a log. It happens and vice versa.

So, in the application I have five main functions.

  • Display current altitude.
  • Show the history of your height.
  • Display maps offline with cities of our planet.
  • Showing 10 nearest airports in the world.
  • A word game.


It seems everything is clean. And simple, but not enough zest. Then to the 10 nearest airports I added a button - 10 closest beautiful women. Placed them in famous cities, stuck photos in clothes (quite risky, but decent) and fake names. Distances to airports and girls are calculated programmatically. By default, airports are always shown to find a list of girls - you need to figure this out and climb to a height of more than 500 meters.

In the description of the game I put a 12+ mark, that there is a photo of erotic properties.
Laid out to the store. All three applications.

First application


The first application was filed with the wording - Pornography.
