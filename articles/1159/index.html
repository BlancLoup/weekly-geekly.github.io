Wealth ratio

One said that there are already $ 8.7 million millionaires in the world, and the second that Forbes counted 793 billionaires. As a lover of mathematics, I was immediately drawn to the calculations, given the recent discussion on the computer forum in the discussion of the article 5 percent .
And I decided to calculate the wealth ratio :)
