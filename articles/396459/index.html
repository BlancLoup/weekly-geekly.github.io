Appearance and equipment


Sistemnik comes in a very large box. It does not fit in every trunk and hardly fits into the opening of the rear door of the car.

Comes with a wired keyboard and mouse. These are unpretentious models, from a delicacy only the volume control. The keyboard is not notebook type, the keys are full-sized, with a long stroke.









One of the highlights of the ASUS GT51CA is a special bracelet. It is made of soft material - either silicone, or elastomer - and if you bring it to the system unit, you can access the secret section on the disk where you can store your passwords and other important information.





The system unit is much larger than the average tower. Design aggressive, sort of transformer style. The body is steel, with high-quality coating, overhead panels of smooth plastic.





At first it seemed to me that the front panel is a door, but this is just a decorative element. The NFC receiver for working with the bracelet is located approximately midway between the logo and the top edge of the panel. This place is indicated by a small icon N.



Immediately attracts the attention of the supply fan cooling system. The color of the backlight can be changed using a proprietary application. During acceleration, the backlight pulsates, the duration of this effect can be set independently. The color of the vertical faceplates and the backlight of the motherboard is also separately regulated.



To the right of the front panel is a vertically positioned DVD drive. And on the left - the overclocking button







Above there is a series of connectors with highlighted symbols. Very correct decision, it is not necessary to peer or highlight where to insert the USB flash drive. The connectors are inclined at about 45 degrees, so they are much more convenient to use when the sistemnik is under the table.



On the upper plane of the body there is a large ventilation inlet closed by an elaborate grid. The top panel itself is raised a few centimeters above the wall of the steel case, which can be viewed through the grille.



A transparent window is embedded into the left wall of the case, through which you can enjoy the backlight of the motherboard and the luminous inscriptions on the video cards. Sound


Hardcore gamers need high-quality sound like air. In order to determine by ear, where did the rocket, projectile or boom come from? Not to mention the fact that you need to listen to your favorite music from a good recording and on good equipment. For the first, ASUS is not responsible, but as for the equipment, the GT51CA uses a digital-to-analog converter and an ESS Saber headphone amplifier. I listened to FLAC videos from my collection, and I have to say that some songs sounded new to me. I even thought about replacing the audio card. In the GT51CA games it sounds great: no noise, clear detail, good spatial positioning of sound sources, impressive bass, and not crushed mid and high frequencies.

Software


Sistemnik comes with Windows 10 Home installed, fully configured and ready to use, you just need to connect a monitor and keyboard with a mouse. Adware and trial software turned out to be a little, less will have to clean.



Corporate application Aegis II allows you to:

  • Adjust the color and brightness of the different body elements.

  • Allocate more resources for specific applications.

  • Perform fine system overclocking.

  • Control the workload.

  • Manage the bundled bracelet. To take advantage of all the amenities of this accessory, you must first create an account or log in under the existing one. After that, if you attach the bracelet to the case, the secret virtual disk automatically opens.

  • Also Aegis II allows you to instantly overclock the system without rebooting - just click on the inconspicuous inscription Turbo 2 in the upper right corner.


Another proprietary application, ASUS Command, is a set of system tools for managing computer power, firmware upgrades, security, and data recovery.

Performance and work experience


From the point of view of "iron" in the GT51CA everything is very serious, top grade.

Processor - Intel Core i7 6700K , referring to the sixth generation. According to the specification, its maximum operating frequency of 4.2 GHz. In the GT51CA, the upper limit is raised to 4.3 GHz, and with one mouse click you can overclock to 4.6 GHz. ASUS claims that every processor for the GT51CA is tested overclocked for 40 hours. The motherboard is based on the Intel Z170 chipset. The amount of RAM 64 GB DDR4 (four modules of 16 GB each).



In this model, there are three drives: a two-byte hard drive



and two 512 GB SSM-drives NVMe, mixed in RAID0. This solution made it possible to achieve a very high write / read speed.



The video subsystem consists of two Nvidia GeForce GTX Titan X video cards operating in SLI mode. Each card is equipped with 12 GB of memory.

Conclusion


ASUS GT51CA is a massive and very high-performance computer. Today it is difficult to collect something more powerful. RAID 0 of two NVMe SSDs is twice as fast as single drives and 6 times faster than conventional SSDs connected via SATA. 64 GB of RAM is enough for any game for several years to come. The processor will also be long relevant, given the slowdown in the development of new chips.

The only question is the video card: with the release of NVidia GeForce GTX 1070 and 1080 installed here Titan X are no longer top. The fact is that the GTX 1080 was announced only at the beginning of June, and the tested sample was assembled earlier. And ASUS already has plans for the release of the GT51 version with the GTX 1070 and 1080. But in any case, the GT51CA buyers won't be left behind - the Titan X pair will plug the vast majority of existing systems into the belt for a long time.

As for the design, the system unit does not exactly look dull. Four separately adjustable backlighting zones allow you to color the computer for every taste, mood, and even for a particular game. Thanks to a custom backlight, it will fit into any interior.
