Palm reborn from the ashes with a new gadget



Palm company forever inscribed in the history of modern "iron". Its development has served as an example for other companies, at one time fans of Palm products were not considered. Like many other brands, Palm still had to leave the market, for a number of reasons. But the company's devices and its software are still popular with geeks.

The company was bought by HP, in which it dissolved. After absorption, HP released several devices based on a new software platform, but, unfortunately, new items did not become particularly popular. After that, HP sold the rights to the Palm brand of the Chinese company TCL, this happened in 2014. Since then, nothing has been heard about Palm ... until now.

By the way, TCL is developing hardware for another almost disappeared brand - Blackberry, and it does it quite successfully. RIM company deals only with software, including services focused on user security.
