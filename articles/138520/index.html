New technology allows you to remove random "fellow travelers" from a photo

Scalado recently announced a new technology that will allow all of us to safely take pictures of anything, even on the busiest street in the world. After that, extra people can simply be cut from the frame. As far as you can understand, all this works so far only on a mobile device.

A company did not provide a clear description of how this software works. But the software itself exists, and it even has its own name - Scalado Remove.
