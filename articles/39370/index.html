Webcam announces Inline Player

After launching the pilot version of Aborama, we collected enough information that would be useful to our users. And I would like to start announcing key, in my opinion, innovations.



The first thing we tried to reconsider is a symbiosis of text and music. Now for the introduction of music into the text are used "widgets", which are done in flash. But widgets have a problem. They, as a rule, break the narration, thereby forcing to make out the songs as footnotes. And this is never vebdvanol. The guys from the Design Bureau of Artem Gorbunov suggested the concept of a player that solves this problem. In their vision, any link to a song on a webcam is actually a player. And you can listen to it right away, without leaving the line of the story. Being programmers to some extent, we decided to call this concept ‚ÄúInline Player‚Äù.

Before demonstrating how this works, I have to make a small lyrical digression. The fact is that the integration of such functionality requires server support. So that links turned into players you need to connect JavaScript to the site. Naturally, not every social network will decide on this.

Realizing this, we made another version of the script. This option is connected via GreaseMonkey or as a user script in Opera.

In general, download the custom script from the official site and look at the result:
The compilation includes songs not released on the albums, such as Dancing In The Streets , My Friend Jack , as well as many long versions of famous hits from singles.

As you can see, the script adapts the player to the color palette on which the text is located. The only thing that stands out is the control buttons. Made specifically to show that following a link and playing is different actions.
