Top Selling Software

The best-selling software in the United States, according to the NPD Group, is MS Office 2003 Student / Teacher Edition. Released in October 2003, this office software package was most interesting to customers in 2006; Its cost is $ 136.91.

TurboTax 2005 Deluxe With State ($ 38.41) ranked second, Norton Internet Security 2006 ($ 65.39) ranked third. Among operating systems, only MS Windows XP Home Edition Upgrade ($ 93.34) was able to get into Top10.

In general, according to the statistical report , the level of software sales fell by 2% compared to 2005 and amounted to $ 2.9 billion. Business software and system utilities showed a certain increase, but the overall statistics were more affected by the fall in sales of operating systems and financial programs . According to NPD Group representative Chris Swenson, the decline in OS sales is associated with a smaller number of users who need to switch to Windows XP (the main wave of transitions to this OS ended in 2005).

Thus, the business software sector ranked first in sales (at the expense of MS Office), and system utilities took second place (mainly due to Symantec products).
