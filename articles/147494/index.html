Special project about clouds at Finam.FM

Our joint project ( Megaplan and Finam.FM ) about cloud services for Russian business has recently been completed. We recorded 20 programs about various aspects related to the management of processes and, in fact, SaaS-services that are intended for this.

I suggest to those who did not have time to listen to broadcasts on the air, listen to the online recordings on the Finam website , there are a lot of interesting things.

Thanks to everyone who helped us to realize this project, and a separate one to our leading Elena Likhacheva, who endured us for a month in the studio!
