Check positions in search engines% username%, free

Hi, Habrazhiteli!
A few months ago, the Seumka position verification service opened.

It allows site owners to track the position of their sites in Google, Yandex and Rambler automatically.

After a long test, we are happy to put the service on display in Habrasoobshchestva.

Our service offers the sufferer the following functionality:

1. Tracking positions up to 20 sites.

2. For each site, you can track up to 150 phrases.
3. Import a list of phrases from xls / txt files.

4. The output of the report in 2 types.
5. Export report to xls.
6. Plotting.
7. To simplify the work, it is possible to create up to 3 links for quick access to the report, which allow you to view it without entering the site.
8. There is an email notification system.
9. It is also possible to track LiveInternet statistics, but it works in test mode.

During the development process, our team faced many technical difficulties, and, with success, overcame them.

PS: Now we use: PostgreSQL, Nginx, Xen and Gentoo Linux. I'd like to see how the service behaves under load.
