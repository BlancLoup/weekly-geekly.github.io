Upper middle class - a review of Sony Xperia SP

Appearance and ergonomics

Xperia SP boasts an elegant, despite the size, appearance and concise design - the number of extra buttons and holes is minimized, even the ear for hanging birds from Angry Birds has been sacrificed. The device is very comfortable in the hand, the location of the buttons does not cause any complaints, except that, in my opinion, it is a little more convenient for left-handers due to the fact that you can press them with different fingers, and not just big ones. Well, you will have to get used to the power button located in the center if you used to have an Acro S.



The 4.6-inch screen occupies about 65-70% of the ‚Äúfacade‚Äù, above it is the eye of the front camera, equipped with a grill, a speaker and a light sensor. In the lower left corner is a hole with a microphone.

On the right, there is nothing but the combo USB / HDMI connector, and on the left there is a large round button for turning on the phone, a button for activating the camera, and a volume control. 3.5 audio jack on the upper end is shifted to the left, which, in my opinion, is familiar and convenient.



The lower end is decorated with an illuminated transparent strip of polycarbonate, which deserves a separate description. Loved by many owners of last year‚Äôs models, the detail became a bit larger and acquired dynamic backlighting, which changes depending on the image on the screen, whether it is the main screen, a photo or an album cover in the player (in the latter case, the bar will also flash to the beat of the music). The back panel is slightly convex, made of a thin matte plastic, resembling soft touch, but a little different, also very pleasant to the touch, absolutely not soiled and does not slip. Here is a massive lens with a protruding aluminum edging, which, on the one hand, does not allow glass to be scratched, and on the other hand, prevents the installation of additional lenses with magnetic mount.



Below are the LED-flash, a second microphone and a speaker with the same mesh as the front one. The cover is quite easy to remove, opening access to slots for micro SIM and micro SD-cards.
