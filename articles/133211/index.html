Guideman - guide on selected male attributes

Almost three months ago, we launched a new online edition for men, similar to the western project Uncrate , in which we are trying to cover the most interesting news - gadgets, watches, cars, motorcycles, photo and video equipment, movies, games and much more. Starting with the alpha version, in which there was only a superficial functionality, we thought out further functionality, won a place in the hearts of loyal users and, finally, launched a refreshed version entitled beta.

In order to avoid an incorrect reaction, I want to add that we do not sell anything - we place links to online stores, which are either based in Russia or are engaged in international delivery.


Ideology

There are a lot of print publications in the world, including glossy ones, and web sites that are targeted at a male audience and advertise various products on their pages. Such advertising is far from always useful - not every manufacturer that has large advertising budgets creates worthwhile products. We write only about what we like visually, functionally and spiritually.

Format

We are distinguished from news sites by brevity, as well as the absence of vague wording and meaningless speech. Calling us a blog is also not a language, because all our reviews are extremely objective and unobtrusive.

Useful functionality

At the moment, we have launched the registration, commenting and likes system. By clicking on the "I like" button, the user adds a certain product to his "Favorites", which will soon be available in open profiles. Thanks to this sample, any close person can find out what you like from the freshest novelties and not guess what to present to you as a gift for the next celebration.

Little about the technical part

The project is hosted on the server of MakeIdea , located in one of the data centers of the company telefonet.ru

The site is written in Perl, the database is PostgreSQL, the frontend is Nginx, the backend is Apache + mod_perl, the template engine is Mason, the file storage is Nginx + WebDAV, the platform is our own development.
