Bio Ring: ring tracker with heart rate monitor and non-invasive calculation of calories consumed

Currently, Indiegogo continues to raise funds for the BioRing smart ring, which is an alternative solution to fitness wrist trackers. Among its most interesting features: automatic calculation of calorie intake.




The gadget has already collected more than half of the required amount, and it probably has a chance to reach the required amount of money in a month, since the goals of the project are not very ambitious: $ 50,000 . The Early Birds are now flying at a price of $ 189 and should be released by November of this year.

The ring tracker has several fitness sensors that can analyze activity, calculate steps and distance, record calorie consumption and evaluate sleep quality. Also, the ring will be equipped with a heart rate monitor, which will help in the diagnosis of stress. Will show BioRing and hydration level.

The most unique advantage of the product developers is the possibility of automatic non-invasive calorie counting using a bio-impedance sensor, which, however, is already known from the Healbe Gobe ‚Äã‚Äãgadget , which offered exactly the same features in the form of a bracelet.

The tracker ring is not addressed to athletes, but to those who want to lose weight or maintain their weight at a certain level, want to better control the functioning of the heart, avoid stress, or deal with them in time.

The ring will be paired with proprietary applications, charged from wireless charging, and sold in two colors: black and white.
