What do we offer developers under Mac OS?



Good afternoon, Megamind!

Today I would like to tell you what we offer to an ordinary Mac developer and what we think is missing in our application, I ask for cat.

Place for your applications.





Perhaps we will start with the most important for each developer - with the distribution of applications. If your application is free, we provide you with a platform for distributing your application completely free of charge. Our primary task is to help and contribute to the distribution of high-quality software that for one reason or another cannot get into the official Apple store, as well as help the development of novice developers and their rather interesting ideas. We do not require from you any money for a developer account or a fee for hosting your application.

Advertising for your applications.





We are also ready to offer you an alternative advertising platform, similar to Apple iAD, which we are developing together with DesktopAD and which you can embed in your paid or free application. According to our information, some developers earn about $ 1500 from their free applications in a few weeks with our advertising platform and distribution through our store. You can learn more by visiting our developer center at https://hack-store.com/for-developers

Advertise your apps in HackStore.





Moreover, we provide you with low-cost advertising for your applications in our store, several options are available for this, such as two types of banners, as well as paid positions in the top and Editor Choice for a certain period and news about your application in our social networks.

Placement of paid applications.





If you have a paid application, we are always ready to sell your application in our store using the FastSpring payment system. All you need to do is register as a developer at https://hack-store.com/for-developers and download your paid application through HackStore. We charge 20% of each successful purchase of your application, and at the end of the month we list your profits to you; moreover, you can always watch your sales statistics in your account. Also, our store supports the option try before you buy which allows the user to download the trial version of your product for sample and subsequent purchase of the paid version.

What we lack.



In our opinion, we really miss you fellow developers and your applications, we try to create unique conditions for you and we hope that together we can change the distribution path of third-party applications in Mac OS. And we really miss your advice on how we can become better, what to change or add to HackStore, we try to be better and we hope that you will help us with this!

We are waiting for your comments, comments as well as your applications in our store, and thank you for being with us!
