Unicorns ... Unicorns everywhere

What are we talking about?

T-shirts


Everything is very simple. Choose the unicorn you like, go to the download page. Two options are available for download:

If you plan to put the image on a white T-shirt - upload the image from the first link and carry it to the printing house. All variants of images are A4 prints, and 7 unicorns are available for download.

Mugs


With circles, too, everything is simple. The mechanism for selecting unicorns, as well as options with or without a background, is similar to T-shirts. Explain, perhaps, the size of the images.
  • 250x95mm - standard image for printing on circles. Carry to the printing house, make an order, get a mug.
  • 90x85mm - an image exclusively with a unicorn (slightly smaller in height due to the fields of the circle). It will be useful to you if you want to place a unicorn in some arbitrary place of the circle.
