Would you like to live and work in the USA?

For 2010, Russia has finally been included in the number of countries among which the Annual US Government Lottery "Green Card" is held!

1. Main page of the organization Usafis

Keep in mind : Those who are interested in this post on the possibility of emigrating to receive a green card: USAFIS is a private organization that earns money by helping to apply for the Diversity Visa lottery (winning in it allows you to get a green card - a residence permit in USA). On the US Department of Immigration website, you can find a questionnaire and submit it for free.

At DV-lottery 2010, applications were no longer accepted on December 1, Russians were allowed to participate. Call for applications for dv-2011 will be open in October 2009.
Participation in the lottery is absolutely free on the site DVLOTTERY.STATE.GOV
