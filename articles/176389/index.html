Investor base: our version

Since we communicate with startups a lot - we noticed that there is one difficulty. Namely - where to start looking for an investor for a startup. On the Internet, there are many "cemeteries of investors", where stupidly published lists of everything that is possible. Next to the investor who invests in IT projects is an investor in agriculture.

Therefore, we made a small service to facilitate the search for investments for IT startups .


1. All published funds, accelerators, incubators, etc. - really invest in IT projects.

2. We made a simple investment search form where you only need to specify the amount you are looking for.


3. We contacted the companies in our database and get additional information from them. Example:



We plan for each company to make a similar auxiliary text, we communicate with the funds.

4. We will constantly update information about companies investing in IT projects. New funds, incubators and other members of the startup industry appear. Since we are an online edition of startups , we must write about them. So do not miss anything.

We do not specifically do the service of mass sending an application to the investor, because each such investor has his own requirements for registration, indicated data, etc. We give a tool for you to immediately eliminate those funds that simply do not invest these amounts.

For example, you are looking for an investment of $ 50 thousand, and write to an investor who invests 10 times more from the amount. Your application, your work - disappear. This situation was the impetus for the creation of a small service.

Scold, praise, use.
