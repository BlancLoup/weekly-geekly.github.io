Instabank is now on Android, in St. Petersburg and with Gold cards

Instabank announces the release of the application for Russian owners of smartphones on the Android platform. This will allow the market to offer convenient mobile banking services in the context of social connections to a wider range of consumers.

The Android version of Instabank includes most of the functions previously implemented in the iPhone application. Android users will also be able to make transfers and requests on Facebook, keep track of expenses in the statement-tape of events, set limits on expenses and replenish the account from cards of other banks.

Also, starting from June 17, new customers of Instabank will receive Gold MasterCard cards. The first year of card service for all new customers will be free.
