Tizen DevLab and Hakaton in Novosibirsk



Surely one of the participants who subscribed to the Tizen hub , has already noticed a new event . In this post, I will tell you a little more about the upcoming event in the context of the past trainings and Tizen hackathons.

The FRUCT Association conducted three Hakatons for Tizen for us: in Petrozavodsk, St. Petersburg and Nizhny Novgorod. We have already planned three more, the first one will be in Novosibirsk. Participation, as before, is free, but registration is required, which can be completed on the event home page.
