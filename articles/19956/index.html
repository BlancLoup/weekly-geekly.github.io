Annual turnover of WebMoney exceeded $ 3 billion

Russia's largest electronic payment system has published statistics for 2007 .

The number of registrations in the WebMoney system since its opening in November 1998 has already exceeded 4 million and came close to 5 million. As of February 7, 2008, the number of accounts was 4,794,878. Of this number, about 1.44 million registrations were made Last year.

The turnover amounted to $ 3,337 billion. The number of transactions is 27.4 million. No electronic payment system in Russia can even come close to WebMoney by these indicators.



On July 23 last year, a fundamentally new type of WMG was introduced, that is, WebMoney Gold (secured with gold). The client interface of the WM Keeper Classic system has moved to version 3.6.0.1 with significantly changed and expanded functions . Already in the beginning of 2008, the Login.Webmoney service appeared, which provides access and authorization on any WebMoney-site without the need to use ActiveX, that is, it is not necessary to use Internet Explorer now.
