Who are Anonymous


Recently on Habr√© the name Anonymous more often slips. However, in almost all cases, there is a misunderstanding of the ideology, structure and methods of work of Anonymus. I will try to clarify some elementary things.


Organization

Anonymous is an ideology, not a group of people, as most news writers assume. Followers of this idea are fighting for the general availability of information, against the oppression of freedoms by governments and corporations, against corruption, and against any other manifestations of society that some of this society does not approve of.

There are no leaders or representatives in Anonymous, no one can speak for everyone. In Anonymous, everyone is equal. This is a kind of anarchic Web 2.0 society. Of course, sometimes there are users with elevated rights, but this is just a sign of distinction of the technical staff who are working to maintain communication sites.

Anyone can become Anonymous, regardless of their skills or experience. To do this, simply join the operation of interest to you, or create your own. Agree, to launch LOIC , or just to distribute information, everyone can.

The rules for participants are simple: stay anonymous and protect the anonymity of others.

Of course, for its recognition and implementation you need like-minded people.

There are operations that are not supported by the majority. For example, #opBART is a small group of independent Anonymus who want to hack hacked bart.gov and whether to publish personal information of citizens.

Anonymous - not LulzSec

These are completely different things: LulzSec is cracked just because they can, but Anonymous pursues some goal dictated by the needs of people. To compare these two concepts is about how to put Robin Hood and Kevin Mitnick in the same row (the latter was hacking for his own pleasure).
