The nForm guys recently started a drawing and sketching table at home, made from parts of IKEA. It provides the ability to store old sketches in a variety of compartments below, and on the surface of the table there is a wonderful Kraft roller (designed to store large canvases of related sketches).

It has two main advantages:
  1. It has become much easier to make daily changes to current projects.
