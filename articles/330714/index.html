Studies of various analytical companies and experts only support the growth forecasts of the data center. For example, Statista talk about increasing the annual traffic of data centers to 9.8 exabytes in 2018. Such loads exacerbate the tasks of efficient use of resources, increasing the flexibility of the IT infrastructure and timely response to customer requests.

The solution can be found much faster and at lower cost if the company is ready to use the tools that it offers to its customers. We apply this approach in the management of our own IaaS-provider - almost all of our employees work with the available service tools and know in practice what and how it works.

Feedback from the team allows you to sensibly assess the situation and the need for those or other opportunities that are necessary for the effective work of clients and the company as a whole. One example is the development and implementation of a load monitoring system for the infrastructure of our clients. With the help of such a service, a customer can estimate in real time what he pays for and refuse a part of unused resources.

To realize this opportunity, we didn‚Äôt need any complicated manipulations. What we did: we chose a graphical library for displaying information and configured vSphere integration using the .NET SDK to obtain load data for individual virtual machines. All this is one more step towards the pay-as-you-go model - paying only for consumed resources and entering the automatic scaling system at peak loads.

Analysis of the market and customer opinions led us to understand the need for input and other innovations. We have already talked about this more than once, but let us briefly dwell on the main points:

  • redesign of the main sections of the company's website (and one more improvement in the future)
  • development of a special calculator that allows you to quickly assess the potential costs of maintaining the infrastructure and move on to its launch
  • setting up pre-installed software (control panel and the ability to track license status) and content management tools (pre-installed CMS)
  • automation of various service tasks ( an example of our DNS manager)

Such projects do not require a global revision of the entire service, multi-year design and development. At the same time, they help to increase the level of accessibility of our services to an audience that is sufficiently far from IT specificity and does not have specialized knowledge.
