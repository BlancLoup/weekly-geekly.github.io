We are 1 year old!

Probably, someone remembers how exactly a year ago I wrote a topic in Habr√© about the launch of the project nekto.me
Conceived as a small service of anonymous opinions about famous personalities, over the past year it has evolved into a full-fledged social network, imbued with anonymity :)
200,000 users, 950,000 comments and 38,000 visitors per day is nekto.me today.



Under the cut a small article about the life of the project.

How many people, so many opinions?
Yes. In fact, since the launch date, 1 145 000 opinions have been published on the project.
Simply, many opinions are deleted, so now there are only 950 thousand of them in the database.
In the first half of the year, 300 thousand anonymous opinions were left, for the second, already 650 thousand.
Currently about 6500 opinions are published per day.
Opinions can be written without registration.
The question of spam was solved simply: set a limit of 90 seconds between comments.
Those who write on the merits do not interfere, but spammers and flooders are indignant :)
In addition, the links in the opinions are not clickable and the temptation of spammers is not enough.

About growth
3 weeks after leaving the test mode, we had 3500 users registered.
The main impetus was given to Habr :) And then everything went like a snowball - people came and come following the links of their acquaintances / friends, register and call their friends.
The site refers at least 100 thousand pages. Overwhelmingly, from social networks.
Now about 800 new users are registered per day!

There are more than 300 thousand page views daily, that is, about 9 views per visitor on average. Half a year ago, one visitor looked, on average, 4.5 pages.
The increase in the number of views associated with the emergence of blogs and friends.

By the way, on February 1, 2011, we were told about us on radio Ekho.Moscow three times all over the country, but this did not give a visible influx of visitors. More precisely, I did not notice it at all :)
