We share information about the work of your site. Service Overview HostTracker Part 5

Openness and transparency - many modern companies mention these concepts among the principles of their work. The monitoring service HostTracker provides an opportunity to transparently and openly share with the world information about the lives of their sites and services. Honestly admitting to clients when and what didn‚Äôt work - in some cases a very good idea. Unless, of course, this happens infrequently. On the other hand, what could be the best incentive to stop postponing the accumulated tasks and finally make the site work properly?




So, the monitoring service collects data about our website (I described it in detail earlier) and we can easily view all this, and even use it for hosting altercations, if you suddenly need it. How now to show their customers? There are several options.

Widgets





The most informative for the client method allows you to easily verify the presence / absence of the problem. By the way, not only for the client. Admins and other responsible persons are also helpful from time to time to see how things are going. Quickly and conveniently, you can place on any page of your site, both for users and for their employees. Widget can be edited both visually - by choosing colors to match your site - and by content. For example, to merge several sites in one diagram - then everything will be fine on it only when it is good on all sites at the same time. If you plan technical works in advance and do not forget to indicate this when setting up monitoring , this information will also appear on the widget. And the admin does not grab the heart, and the client does not run to the competitors - because he sees that this is not a breakdown, but an important and, most importantly, planned update.



You can also add your company logo to the widget, for example, if you provide someone with analytical services and use HostTracker. The diagram is also interactive - the user can see in detail what happened when, just by clicking on the incident.
Let me remind you that no applications need to be installed for all this. Sites are monitored from the cloud , and for the widget you only need to add the generated code to your page.
