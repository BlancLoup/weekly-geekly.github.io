Bill No. 305828-6: Liquidation of the Russian Academy of Sciences

RIA News:
On Thursday, June 27, at a meeting of the government of the Russian Federation, a large-scale reform of the Russian state academies of sciences was announced. It is assumed that out of three academies (RAS, RAMS - medical, RASN - agricultural) one will be formed, which will turn into a "club of scientists", free from the need to manage financial flows and property. Three more academies will be transferred to the government.

As an employee of one of the institutes of the Russian Academy of Sciences, I cannot deny that I am vitally interested in the fact that what the draft law provides for does not happen. In fact, it implies the elimination of three academies, the creation of one single RAS, deprived of property management, procurement and research costs.
