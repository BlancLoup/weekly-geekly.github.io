Sex.com - bankrupt

Sedo has announced that it will act as a broker for the sale of sex.com , which since January 2006 has been at the top of the list of sales of domain names at around $ 14 million.

The story of the abduction and return of the domain gave 1350 documents to the criminal case and material on two books by different authors. But fame did not help recoup an expensive purchase, and creditors forced the owner to sell this asset. Meanwhile, in 2001, Ron Levy, the owner of Cybererotica, offered 400 thousand dollars a month, plus 40% of the profits over this amount for the monthly rent of sex.com.
There will be no open auction, Sedo invites potential buyers to contact them to discuss the details of the transaction.
