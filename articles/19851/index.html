Yandex earned $ 145 million, and Google LLC - $ 10 million

Over the past year, iConText has been researching the contextual advertising market. Now they were able to publish some final figures.

According to analysts, the volume of the contextual advertising market in Russia more than doubled and reached $ 225 million.
