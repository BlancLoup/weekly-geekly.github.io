Ideas for the future: a wrist PC



Although technologies have not yet matured, but designers are already looking forward and making concepts of devices that may appear on the market in a few years. It is believed that after smartphones and tablets, the next breakthrough in the mobile device market will be wearable computers. For example, like this concept MIMOS WristPC with a 3.5-inch touchscreen and keyboard, which is mounted on the wrist.

According to the designers, such a computer will be convenient to use both on the arm and in the stationary state: on the table. You can even take it with both hands and play like on a small PSP game console.









Design authors: Saharudin Busri, Mohd Nizam Najmuddin, Mohd Rohaizam Mohd Tahar, Nuzairi Yasin, Malaysian Institute of Microelectronic Systems . The concept won the Red Dot Design Award 2010 ( press release ) .
