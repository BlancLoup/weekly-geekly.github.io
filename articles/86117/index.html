Apple sued HTC for patent infringement

Today, Apple has filed a lawsuit against HTC for violating about 20 patents related to the user interface, architecture, and iPhone hardware.

Quote from Steve Jobs from the press release (in free translation):
We can sit and watch how competitors steal our inventions, or we can do something about it. We decided to do something about it. We believe that competition is great, but competitors should create their own original technology, not steal ours.
Another quote:
Apple re-invented the mobile phone in 2007, introducing the revolutionary iPhone, and did it again in 2008, using the groundbreaking App Store, which now offers more than 150,000 mobile applications, in more than 90 countries. Worldwide, more than 40 million iPhones have been sold.
Read the press release here .

A natural question arises: until? How long will Apple restrain progress with its patents?
