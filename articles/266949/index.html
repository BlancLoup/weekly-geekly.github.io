Time to materialize: a contest for Material Design from Google

Hi, Habrahabr! Today we have very good news for you. As you all probably know, in Android 5.x and its follower, Android 6.0 Marshmallow uses a new design. The Material Design concept was introduced at Google I / O 2014 and received a positive response not only among designers and application developers, but also among users.



To date, more than 200,000 applications on Google Play have been reworked, taking into account material design guidelines. If you have already updated your app on Google Play to Material Design, or plan to do it in the near future, then take part in the Material Design contest, which started this week and will run until November 15th. Playing out: placing your application in the section featuring the Google Play store, tickets for the annual Google I / O conference and Nexus 9 tablets. We will select the top three applications in the three categories. Details in the contest rules .
