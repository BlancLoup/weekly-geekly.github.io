Facebook + Glavstart: April Theses

Glavstart, Russia's first official partner for start-ups on Facebook , is launching a massive program to support developers of non-game applications for social networks.

In Yekaterinburg (April 10), Moscow (April 12) and St. Petersburg (April 17) together with Facebook we will have Communication Tube for social applications , the market of which in Russia is not yet sufficiently developed, which means it is free!
