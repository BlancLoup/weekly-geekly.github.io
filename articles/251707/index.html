Our company at the largest game development conference in San Francisco - GDC

From March 2 to March 6, 2015, the Game Developers Conference (GDC) will be held in San Francisco - one of the largest and most awaited conferences for game developers, and for those who are somehow connected with the gaming industry.

GDC 2015 includes over 400 companies, 8 summits, more than 900 speakers with presentations and speeches on various topical issues: from game design and design to monetization, marketing and advocacy.

In the framework of GDC 2015, 8 summits will be held on:
  • Artificial intelligence in games
  • Community management
  • ESports
  • Game plot
  • Free-to-play games
  • Gamification education
  • Independent games
  • Games for smartphones and tablets

As part of Developers Day, speakers from leading industry companies such as Google, Amazon, Unity Technologies, NPNF and Imagination Technologies will perform. Microsoft, NVIDIA, Sony, Valve and many others will also take part in GDC2015. The conference provides an opportunity to look into the future of the gaming industry and understand how the industry itself is developing and where you need to strive for yourself.

Our company also did not stand aside.
XIM and XIMAD will occupy box number 2423 , which can be easily found by the elegant two-meter figures - the characters of our new game Star Crusade.



Star crusade
Star Crusade is a multiplayer collectible card online game inspired by games like Magic: the Gathering and Hearthstone , but with a unique theme of space fiction. We offer a unique universe, the world of the future in the style of science fiction, in which different space factions are at war with each other. At the GDC, the DEMO version of the game will be presented for the first time, and anyone can plunge into the atmosphere of our universe. Star Crusade , like all collectible card games, is as accessible and understandable. But we tried to take the best from today's popular game analogs and make the game less cartoony, with a faster change of the game situation and a dynamic plot. In the future, we plan to implement the exchange of cards between players. Say nothing more to keep the intrigue.

Magic jigsaw puzzles
In addition to new directions, the flagship of the company XIMAD - Magic Jigsaw Puzzles (Wonder Puzzles) will be presented. Puzzles have been and remain the leading application, recognizable and appreciated both abroad and in Russia. It was our first global experiment to turn an ordinary puzzle game into a full-fledged gaming service. Thus, the market simply has no analogues to our application, or rather to the range of services and benefits that users acquire thanks to it. We managed to collect more than 5,000 virtual puzzles in one application. Due to the presence of its own store, each user can choose only those packages that are really interesting to him, without being lost in a few thousand pictures. The players have personal accounts with which they can control all the actions performed within the game. At the moment we are working on improving the social functions of the application, we want to give our users the opportunity not only to create their own puzzles (since this function has already been implemented), but also to be able to collect puzzles created by other players.
