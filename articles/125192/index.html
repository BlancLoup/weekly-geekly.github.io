A large collection of games Ubuntu GamePack 11.04

Presented the largest collection of games - Ubuntu GamePack 11.04

The kit consists of 6 discs (5 DVD discs include 156 of the most popular games of various genres. Ubuntu Desktop 11.04 is the 6th disc).
Now there is no need to explore the Internet in search of games, and finding the game wondering how to install it in Ubuntu Linux.

On the discs there are brief descriptions of games and illustrations of game fragments, and the installation of any game you like will take no more than two mouse clicks.

The kit will be interesting gamers of any age. You can play classic chess, solve logical puzzles, perform aerobatic maneuvers at the helm of an airplane, feel like a racer, fight in the vast expanses of space, build a city and conquer civilization, shoot at enemies in a modern shooter and plunge into the virtual world of multiplayer online games .

These drives are not a modification of Ubuntu Linux. They only contain games, so they require a pre-installed Ubuntu Desktop Linux 11.04 system (or based on it, such as LinuxMint, SuperOS, etc.).
