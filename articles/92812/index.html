Dropbox Android App



Today Dropbox for Android has become available.
  1. Access your Dropbox on the go (streaming video and audio files are also available)
  2. Download files and synchronize with Dropbox (the ability to send a public link to the files via e-mail)
  3. Opportunity to share a link to your files


Dropbox is available right now in the Android Market!

References:

  • Dropbox for Android
  • Dropbox for other platforms
  • Dropbox blog entry

It should also be noted that today, in addition to Dropbox for Android, Dropbox for iPad has become available and Dropbox for Blackberry (coming soon) is announced, and in addition Dropbox mobile API is available for registered Dropbox Developers.
