New contest Vkontakte - arrogant deception

I myself am from Latvia, yesterday I received a letter from the administration of Vkontakte, in which they told about a new competition for foreign visitors. Within two weeks, you attract new users by your link, the top 30 invitations by region receive iPod nano as a gift, and in the overall ranking the prizes are already more impressive - laptops, smartphones, etc.

It so happened that 15,000 people visit my game portal every day and I know admins of other local resources. I wrote about the competition at my place, asked to write others, walked on Skype, Latvian social workers and gradually climbed out to first place in Latvia.

Then I decided to arrange a campaign for my visitors and offered them to invite their friends for some bonuses on the site. Invited 5 friends - a bonus.

The guys are actively connected and I got to the first place in the overall rating. The gap was quite large and, with a sense of accomplishment, I went to bed to find out that I was quietly removed from the contest this morning. Without any explanation.

Apparently they did not want to upset the other participants.

But they upset me very much.
