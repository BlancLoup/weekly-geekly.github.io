MTS and Vkontakte launch a free version of the site 0.vkontakte.ru

Following Facebook in Russia , free access to 0.vkontakte.ru appears.

The telecommunications operator MTS and the social network VKontakte have announced the free access to the mobile portal 0.vkontakte.ru for their users since September 1, which allows them to communicate with their friends on the social network VKontakte for free.

Portal 0.vkontakte.ru is optimized for fast loading of pages through a mobile connection - this is a faster and easier version of the mobile site m.vkontakte.ru.

Portal 0.vkontakte.ru allows you to use all the features of the mobile version, but it does not display graphics, photo and video files, so that the speed of access to the resource increases significantly.

Access to the content posted on the user's pages can be obtained by clicking on a special link, while the user is notified that he leaves 0.vkontakte.ru and, accordingly, a free charging zone.
