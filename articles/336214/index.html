Announcement of the second Uptime community conference in Moscow: let's talk about the worst

We decided to dedicate the second Uptime day (the first was in April) to fatal accidents in the IT infrastructure - such things happen to everyone sooner or later. It was easy to choose a date - meet ‚ÄúUptime day: Friday, the 13th‚Äù (by the way, this year there is only one such Friday).



It is not customary to talk about accidents in public. There are IT conferences about development, high loads, but nevertheless, accidents are part of the life of any business and how to eliminate them, how to avoid repeating them, how to learn to understand how to solve them faster - these are the most important issues in life any project.

October 13, 2017 in the Moscow co-working of Deworkacy, speakers from Badoo, Carprice, ITSumma, Bitrix24, Revizium and other companies will talk about what accidents happened in their lives and what regulations these companies have in order to respond to accidents and how to solve them, as well as what software the team has for the subsequent analysis of emergencies, how such analysis is conducted and what is being done so that the problems do not repeat.

The conference is organized by Uptime.community created with our participation - a community of experts who are engaged in rapid response to problems with web projects, organize monitoring of their projects and fault-tolerant architecture. The first Uptime day conference was held in April 2017 in Digital October - then we discussed monitoring and providing technical support for major web projects and services - Parallels, Badoo, 1C-Bitrix, Booking.com, ITSumma, etc.



We already laid out the video recordings and presentations of the conference speeches in a separate post .

Uptime day participation is free, but you need to register on the site - there are only 300 places. We will announce the program soon!
