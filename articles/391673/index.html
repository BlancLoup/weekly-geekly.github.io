Competition announced for the creation of weapons from improvised means


Chunotron from the game Fallout 4

The research division of DARPA is exploring unusual weapons that terrorists can use. They are weakly technically armed, but very inventive, they use improvised means for the manufacture of lethal weapons. There was already a case when a bomb was made from an ordinary double boiler, and this is not the only example. Serial drones deliver explosives and spy. Among Islamic terrorists, the most popular trigger mechanism for bombs is the Nokia 105 phone . Experts say that terrorists prefer this model because of its reliability and long battery life.

Modders and hackers who can think up the most effective weapon, spending a minimum of money and time, can count on winning the competition. That is, components should be easy to buy, and then quickly assemble the necessary device.
