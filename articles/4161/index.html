Vimpelcom announced the purchase of a 51% stake in the Georgian company Mobitel, which owns a license to provide cellular services in the GSM-1800 standard. Formally, the license covers the entire territory of Georgia, but the de facto unrecognized republic of Abkhazia finds itself outside the zone of the operator. The controlling stake in Mobitel cost VimpelCom $ 12.6 million. This stake was acquired not directly, but through an offshore Water Trail Holdings from the British Virgin Islands, while with its former owner, GMC Group , VimpelCom signed an option for repurchase of the remaining 49% of the shares of the Georgian operator.
