In Sochi and Krasnodar, anyone can now connect to Yota



Yota starts sales of devices access to Yota Internet 4G in Sochi and Krasnodar.
Sales in Krasnodar will start on November 16, in Sochi - in a week, on November 23. Thus, the open test operation of the Yota network in these two cities begins.

In Sochi and Krasnodar, the first users of the network - Yota Champions - appeared in October of this year. By the time of the start of test operation, more than half of the main territory of cities was covered. During the work of the Champions, the total amount of outgoing traffic Yota in Krasnodar was 9.46 TB, the incoming - 27 TB. In Sochi, the volume of outgoing traffic is 7.3 TB, the volume of incoming traffic is 21 TB.

During the test operation, network access to all users in Sochi and Krasnodar will be provided free of charge. Detailed information for those wishing to connect to Yota in these cities is on the site . The transition to commercial operation of the Yota network in both cities is scheduled for early 2010.
