JetBrains at Sun Tech Days 2010

This year, JetBrains takes an active part in Sun Tech Days . On April 8 and 9, you will be able to meet with leading developers of our products at the JetBrains stand and listen to their reports. Here is the schedule of our participation in the conference:

Summary of reports

IntelliJ IDEA: Life after Open-Source

In the fall of 2009, JetBrains released the Open-Source version of IntelliJ IDEA. Dmitry Zhemerov will tell about what came of it and about the company's future plans for the development of the product line based on IntelliJ Platform.

Continuous Integration with TeamCity

The report is devoted to the description of the basic features of TeamCity, the features of its architecture and use, as well as development plans.

Java extension and DSL creation using MPS

Why are DSL (domain specific languages) important, how is their implementation possible in the Open Source Language Workbench, MPS? The answers to these and other questions are during the presentation of Konstantin Solomatov, who will show the process of creating a small but useful extension for the Java language.
