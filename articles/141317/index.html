20% discount for Habrasoobshchestvo on iForum - Kiev, April 17, 2012, IEC

Probably, everyone already knows what iForum (Ukrainian Forum of Internet Actors) is and it is not necessary to talk in detail about it.

The good news is that, especially for Habrakhabr users, a promotional code was allocated, which until April 9, inclusively offers a discount on a ticket - 20%

To whom it is interesting - hurry.

The discount can be used in your account after registering on the conference website iforum.ua
Promo code for discounts: 616650 (enter it in your account after registering).

The conference will be held in four streams, which are divided into the following topics:
  • Internet business
  • Internet advertising
  • Social media
  • Internet technologies

The conference is expected the best speakers from Russia and Ukraine, among the speakers are representatives of Facebook, Google, Yandex, Microsoft, Mail.ru, Imena.UA, Microsoft, LiveJournal, Ashmanov and Parnera, and others. More detailed program can be viewed at the link iforum.ua/programma

Date of event: Kiev, April 17, 2012, IEC
