Touch candy bar Eee Top

ASUS has published on its website specifications for new members of the popular Eee PC family - all-in-one PCs with a touchscreen display.


It is already known that the Eee Top ET1603 and ET1602 are designed based on Intel Atom N270 processors with a frequency of 1.6 GHz. The motherboard chipset is also Intel - 945 GSE. Onboard for both models are 1 GB of DDR2 RAM, 160 GB SATA HDD, Ethernet adapters, Wi-Fi Draft N, card reader (SD / SDHC / MS / MS Pro), a pair of speakers of 4 W each and 1, 3 megapixel webcam. Both models are equipped with a 15.6-inch widescreen (16: 9) display. The whole thing works under the control of OS Windows XP Home Edition.

Two new Eee PC representatives differ by video cards (ET1603 has ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3450, and ET1602 uses the built-in chipset). In addition, in the more advanced ET1603 model, a Bluetooth module is available.

These desktops will enter the market in the first quarter of 2009. For the price data yet. In addition to unverified information, according to which the cost of devices will be less than $ 500.
