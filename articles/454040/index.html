Conference React Russia 2019 is June 1

Friends, spring has come again! Beautiful summer weather is getting closer. Productive weekdays brought new results, which means it's time to meet again!

This year we have prepared something special. This time we meet not just in the evening on a weekday at the mitap (mini-conference), but for the whole day and on Saturday. Yes that's right. We are starting what we promised last year. Meet

React Russia 2019 is a conference of professional developers using React.js and React Native in Russia. Being a logical continuation of all our React Moscow and React SPB mitap conferences held since 2016 with the support of the largest companies in the market, we meet to share ideas, present and tell about new solutions and technologies, share our experience and jointly do our work even more productive, pleasant and efficient.
