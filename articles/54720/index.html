Dell studio one


On March 12, in the Land of the Rising Sun, Dell unveiled one of its new developments - the Studio One candy bar. According to the Gadgetwise blog (NYT): "one of the most beautiful personal computers that this company has ever produced." Moreover, it is offered in five different color combinations: pure white, red, blue, pink and black.

But that's not the point - for a starting price of $ 699, not just a 19 "computer with a touchscreen is offered, it also comes with: special software for accessing popular resources and programs with one touch, drawing with a finger (with the artless name YouPaint) and even , photographers and designers, please do not read the proposal until the end, the ability to edit photos with your finger.

In the standard configuration of Studio One there are: 160 GB hard drive, which can be easily replaced with a version of 750 GB; quiet Intel Celeron 450 processor (2.2 GHz), which, if you are not against noise, gives up its place Core 2 Quad Q8200 (2.33 GHz), the same happens with a standard CD / DVD drive if Blu is required -Ray. What is surprising is the complete absence of a built-in Wi-Fi card as standard - this is nonsense for a computer made in 2009.

It is worth waiting for the appearance of this beauty in the shops of your city at the end of spring.
