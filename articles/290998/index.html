Researching campus security issues can make the difficult process of finding a university or college even harder. In accordance with federal law, colleges are required to establish standard safety protocols on campus.

Parents and applicants can look at the university's annual security report, which should be available on the school's website, in order to find statistics on crime, disciplinary action and campus security policies.

1. What should a university do to make it safe?

For parents and applicants, campus security should be a priority. And universities, in turn, should make campus security a # 1 issue. If you put the issue of security in the first place and bring it to public attention, then this will attract more future students to this particular university.

2. How quickly does the university respond to emergencies that occur on campus?

The university can and should use various methods of different methods of warning students in times of danger. These can be emails, warning displays, or siren sounds. Ideally, universities should be able to use modern technologies such as telephones and the Internet. Find out or have such methods of warning at the university.

4. Who investigates sexually motivated crimes at a university / university campus?

It may sound like this, but it is a harsh truth, and it happens that students become victims of sexual crimes. The main goal of the university is to prevent such incidents in every possible way. Families of students, as well as the student himself, should look first and foremost to the statute and rules of the university on this delicate, but very important topic.
