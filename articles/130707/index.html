This is not a fashionable web-based startup with a valid layout. This is an old old school site, covered in scars and crutches.

A modest community of tourists, not those from the word "tour" and "tagiil", but those with a guitar and a campfire, as well as cyclists. There are more than 10,000 of us who left several messages on the forum, and up to 10,000 people a day. The largest forum is 5,500 people. I will try to tell the story of our non-commercial cozy site.

Briefly about the concept of creation.

Initially, the site was conceived with the idea that people who prefer to spend weekends outside the home (oh, the word outdoor is not enough in Russian) is a completely separate class of people, and they can change the type of recreation, but not the lifestyle. Today, a person is fascinated by a parachute, tomorrow is diving, the day after tomorrow is a speleology and parallel fire show, the bike changes from mountain to road, the jeep changes to enduro motorcycle, but the drive to travel, extreme sports, adrenaline does not disappear anywhere. The idea was right.
