Financial technologies used by children

Banks and especially fintech companies are increasingly focusing on millennials , understanding that they will ensure the prosperity of their business in the near future. In the meantime, some players look even further and come up with solutions for children, the so-called generation Z. In this article, we will tell you what solutions the city authorities, banks, and fintech companies interact with those born in the 21st century.

In the fall of 2014, Sberbank offered its small customers to use candy . The device filled with sweets, Sberbank placed in its branch on Tverskaya. The terminal was available for visitors until April of this year. The overall concept of the project, including software and hardware, was developed by the agency Deluxe Interactive Moscow . As planned, using the device, children who came to the bank with their parents could learn the functions of an ATM at an early age.



The process of using konfomata assumed game mechanics. A bank employee issued a card to a child (an analogue of a plastic card), with the help of which one could use a candy. Having inserted the card into the card reader, the small user was asked to indicate the age and complete a set of tasks of the appropriate level of complexity. The topic of questions and problems is finance. The terminal gave out candy to children for the right decisions. Answering questions, small users interacted with the game character Drakosha.
