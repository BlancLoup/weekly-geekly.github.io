How to make money on mining with a regular home computer

If you do not have a huge farm of dozens of video cards, and there are no ASIC Miner paras as well, do not be sad - there are still ways to make money on cryptocurrencies. One of the more or less working options is in front of you.





First, choose the currency to be mine. You can pick it up, looking at the list of fresh Coin'ov on the Internet. For example, this one:
cryptocointalk.com/forum/40-new-cryptocoins
As an alternative - another list of currencies that are just preparing to launch:
cryptocointalk.com/forum/557-unlaunched-cryptocoins

The conditions are as follows:
1) The currency should have a sonorous name - this is the first thing that other users are looking at. If the name is successful - there is hope for growth in the future.
2) Not bad, if the currency has a sensible description, a beautiful logo, a working site, and normal wallet assemblies for all popular platforms. All this gives hope that the currency will not die a month after launch.
3) Zero or very low (less than 0.5%) premine. We want to make more developer.
4) Rewards for found blocks (block rewards) - large at the start and quickly decline after a few days / weeks.
5) The most important. The currency algorithm is preferably not Script and certainly not SHA-256. Thus, neither the owners of the video cards nor the owners of the ASICs will be able to start mining at the start, and the lucky one with a weak iron can manage to grab a piece of cake.

Next, we have a few days purposefully mined the selected Coin, after which we stop and wait a couple of weeks when it appears on some stock exchange (even if the Chinese one). As soon as this happened, we convert the currency into well-established Bitcoin / Litecoin. Profit! At the same time, you can choose and start to mine the next Coin in the same way. One currency will not fly up - it will surely work with another.

Under this scheme, I was closer to the end of 2013 in just a few days on an ordinary home computer I earned about 15,000 rubles on mining Applecoins (those that are APC, and not MAC).
