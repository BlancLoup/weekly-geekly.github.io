Transfer hosters to php 5.3, statistics


Surely many web developers at php are tormented by the question: use the goodies php 5.3, or still early.
This is especially true for CMS / CMF developers. So, colleagues? :)
And so I decided to do a little research in order to clarify the situation with php 5.3 support from popular hosts and sent them to the boxes. support question about support for php 5.3 on their shared hosting, as well as their plans to upgrade to this version, if there is no support at the moment.
So, let's go straight to the results:


In total, about 40 hosters were polled, responses were received from 31 (the rest perhaps still simply did not have time to answer). There was no complicated methodology in choosing hosters. These are the first in Google, the first in Yandex, the first in various hosting directories, those that I personally used, Russian and Ukrainian. I did everything solely because of personal interest in this matter, the goal to promote some kind of hoster was not and is not.
I immediately asked all hosters not to offer VPS / VDS.

Further I bring directly lists of hosters that have / no / plan to support php5.3.
