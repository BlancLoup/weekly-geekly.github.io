"Pirates" spend on music 10 times more money

Those people who download music from torrents are at the same time the most active buyers of legal content. These are the results of a study conducted by the Norwegian BI School of Management. They explored the habits of more than 1900 Internet users over 15 years of age and determined that P2P network activists buy ten times more music than ordinary users who have never visited a torrent tracker.
