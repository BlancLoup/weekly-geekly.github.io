Not using Del.icio.us yet? Now worth a try.

Michael Arrington in his article on TechCrunch If you didn‚Äôt use Del.icio.us, You Will Now, dated April 5, announces the release of the del.icio.us Bookmarks plugin with a significantly higher speed.

Social bookmarking service Del.icio.us offers one of the most popular plugins for Firefox. Some time ago, Del.icio.us released a new version of the plugin that actually takes over all the work with bookmarks from Firefox, allowing you to work with bookmarks and tags in a browser and synchronize with your Del.icio.us account. But the synchronization speed left much to be desired.

Today, Del.icio.us has released an update for this plugin, in which the speed problem has been solved. If you are a Del.icio.us user, this plugin will become your most used Firefox plugin. If you have not yet joined the two million Del.icio.us users, you have a reason to try. Download the plugin here .
My biggest claim to Del.icio.us over the past two years has been its often slow work. This prompted me to switch to his very worthy rival Bluedot.us . But Del.icio.us has significantly increased its speed lately, and this incredibly useful plugin has also appeared. Now I'm back at Del.icio.us.
Currently, Del.icio.us allows only two options for bookmarks - personal and public. I hope that they will soon add an intermediate option - the ability to provide access to some bookmarks only for friends.
