Forsta - pure bamboo sound



Designer Jocko Chan proposes not to bathe over the wheezing speakers of a home theater, but to grow a good sound on your own. Yes, right in the bucket. Rather in buckets. The peculiarity of the Forsta concept proposed by him is that live bamboo stalks are used as the body for the speakers. The more you water, the thicker the sound.
