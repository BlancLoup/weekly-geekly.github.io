Case ASUS VENTO TA-M1 - chetyrehkulerny draft

The ASUS VENTO TA-M1 case shown the other day is focused on high-performance systems. Therefore, the main task that ASUS engineers tried to solve was to ensure the most efficient heat removal.



In order to achieve this good goal, the developers have placed in the body as many as four fans: frontal, aft and two side. It is not known how it affected the noise, but this quartet of coolers should be well ventilated. The design feature of the case is that the side coolers with a diameter of 4.7 inches are mounted on a movable plate. This solution allows, if necessary, to redirect the air flow, for example from a video card to the processor and back. The side wall itself, as well as the front and top panels are made of fine-meshed mesh, which also contributes to better ventilation.

Given the size of the case, it can be argued that it is perfect for both 15-inch PCI-E VGA and 11-inch SLI / Crossfire cards. In total, the housing has seven slots for video cards. In addition, ASUS VENTO TA-M1B boasts the presence of 4 five-inch and 4 three-inch slots for drives and card readers. At the same time, the devices are fixed in the case with clips, and not with screws, which users obviously appreciate. The front panel has two audio outputs and a pair of USB 2.0 ports.

Unfortunately, the time of the appearance on sale and the cost of ASUS VENTO TA-M1 are not yet known.
