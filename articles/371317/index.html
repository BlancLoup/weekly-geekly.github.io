Alfa-Insurance Leaks Personal Data

As a client of Alfastrachow, when looking for insurance for a trip abroad, I looked at their website.
At one time, I was attracted by the possibility of making an inexpensive policy, and even through the Internet.
I logged in to my personal account, found the old policies, and, to put it mildly, was surprised to see a dozen policies issued not for me.
I covered up the policy numbers so as not to disclose other people's personal data.
For example, there is not a single policy, somehow related to me.



Here I have drawn up the three top policies.
The rest are not mine.


In addition to detailed information about the car:


You can view information about the driver and his driver's license.


And also about his passport data.
The same information is available about additional drivers included in the policy.
Scans themselves policies are also present.


All policies have only one thing in common - all owners are called just like me. The surname, first name and middle name are the same.
Unfortunately, in response to my e-mail, I received, apparently, an automatic reply that my appeal was accepted, but since February 28 nobody has contacted me, so I consider it possible to publish the information here.
One can only guess who in my personal account are attached to my policies with personal data.

UPD
Yesterday afternoon, access to all other people's data disappeared from my personal account, as well as one of my policies.
In the evening, I was contacted by the representative of the Alfa Insurance.
Apologized for such an incident. He promised that the missing policy will return to the Personal Account, and this situation will not happen again.
