We have launched subscriptions for cheap flights

Hi, Habrahabr!
Once inque already wrote about where the special offers come from , and today we will please you with a new feature - a subscription to the interesting directions.



Now you can enter your e-mail in the search results of airfares in Eviterra and as soon as an offer appears that is 20% cheaper than the average in a given direction, you will receive an email with a link to this option! For those who are worried about their mailbox: emails are sent no more than twice a day and you always have the opportunity to unsubscribe from the newsletter directly on the link from the email.

In the future, when we finish the personal account, it will be even more convenient to manage subscriptions, for example, to subscribe not to the e-mail newsletter, but to rss, and also to limit the range of dates and prices of the offer. Follow us on Twitter and Facebook to keep up to date with the news!
