Broadcast of TechNet Virtual Conference 2016 from Redmond, March 1-3
From the 1st to the 3rd of March, our colleagues at Redmond are holding a virtual conference TechNet Virtual Conference 2016, which is aimed at a wide audience of IT specialists.

On each of the three days, the broadcast starts at 8:00 pm Moscow time:
  • The first day reveals the issues of IT transformation, career development of IT specialists.
  • The second day is entirely devoted to Windows 10 - various aspects from management to security.
  • The content of the third day is focused on the IT infrastructure, both local and cloud.

Among the speakers are such well-known experts as Jeffrey Snover , Jeff Woolsey , James Whittaker , so I am sure you will not be bored.
