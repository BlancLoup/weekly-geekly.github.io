3. Work conscientiously


There are quite a few people working with a neutral attitude. And there are not so many who make even a trifle with inspiration, creative zeal, putting the soul. Here you can hear opinions from the theory of "after / if." "If they give me an interesting task," "after I have been recognized by all."

For example, after completing the task, test the bugs, identify yourself, act not formally, but conscientiously, qualitatively, write the code with comments right away, comment on all the commits - then it will be pleasant to yourself. Behind this there is no need to lose the essence - effectiveness.

4. Helping a manager is helping yourself.


The head also has questions that need to be addressed. Even the company faces such questions. The better you understand where the company is moving, the better you will know the tasks of your manager.

As a rule, the head is loaded above the roof, and there is usually no help waiting. Therefore, an initiative employee who is able to understand the problems of a manager is a very good sign of the degree of your interest in work and in the success of your department. There is no need, however, to push, and you need to be really ready to solve problems not to the detriment of your tasks.

The head, seeing it, will definitely mark it. And as he moves up, he will take with him precisely those who best understand him and worked with him.
Therefore: help to the head is investing in your future


5. Respect


A leader is a person who does a lot. In addition to his tasks, he is responsible for the work of all his subordinates together, and each one individually. This is a very important and difficult job. You need to be able to respect the work of others - today it is not so common. Respect helps to build good long-term relationships, to solve work issues fruitfully.
Therefore: respect and be respected


6. The importance of the initiative


There is a fear effect of the first step. When everyone is ready for the exam and you have to take a ticket, an awkward wait arises - everyone is afraid of being the first. Therefore, someone destroys uncertainty and takes the first step, followed by everyone else.

In the work there are often such a hitch. It must be remembered that luck loves the brave, and take responsibility for the first step. This will improve the work of the team as a whole, and get yourself a plus in karma.

In addition, there are always so-called "important, but non-urgent tasks." Knowing them, and learning to cope with your work in a shorter time, you can no longer wait for new tasks for growth from a manager, but take steps (by coordinating the initiative) for exactly these tasks. It will also move your department and the company as a whole forward, and will not slow down to reflect on your assessment as a specialist.
Therefore: grow on your own initiative


7. Teamwork


Much depends on the relationship in the team. If you learn how to build a climate in a team, build bridges between team members and even between employees from different departments - you will also take some of the work of the manager on your shoulders and make his life easier - and improve yours.
Therefore: work not in a team , but with a team
