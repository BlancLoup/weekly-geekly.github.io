The difference between the OneMarkt marketplace and its main competitors

During the existence of the project, since January 2015, we have changed the concept several times in accordance with our own observations and tests. We tried to make the product accessible and understandable to everyone, and also not like competitors. In the end, we decided to use the standard idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe marketplace, but present it a bit from the other side.



The main problem was the creation of intuitive site navigation, both for buyers and for sellers. We did not want to make thousands of pages of the site for each process. It was necessary something one-page. And that's what we did.
The main page of the project is a Ribbon with posts of stores - participants. Here they can quickly upload information about new goods in their stores, make posts about promotions and discounts, and share useful information with potential buyers.

A new user who visits the site sees all the latest news from the stores, sees the stores themselves recently registered in the system (which is an additional promo for these stores). If he needs a specific category of goods, say "Dresses", then the user can go to another page with all categories of products on the site. Moreover, it can use the search string.

If the user wants to register his store on the site, then this can be done within 5 minutes, including the first loading of goods. Everything happens as quickly and simply as possible.

Thus, OneMarkt is suitable for participating stores for daily work with the platform. Potential buyers will always see the latest items from the store, and are more likely to want to visit its storefront and make a purchase.

In the near future, we will add several features that will allow stores to simplify work with the platform even more, and customers to make the most profitable purchases.
