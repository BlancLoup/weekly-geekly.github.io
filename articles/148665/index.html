The level of projects and their evaluation
There is an opinion that it would be cool for a freelancer to grab some hellish complex order for 100.5 million money, methodically to start shoveling it and to raise its financial position with it, having broken the big sum.

The opinion is erroneous, because except heaps of hemorrhoids, it will not bring anything. Such orders, as a rule, are ambiguous, have a bunch of pitfalls, a huge burden of responsibility, and a bunch of paperwork to boot. You meet him for long months, without the possibility of escaping. To pull off this strap alone is difficult, and most importantly not. If a wolf clogs up an elephant, then it is unlikely that he will eat it, the elephant will quickly become rotten. Much more effective to catch hares and mice. And it also provides a pleasant opportunity at any time to score and relax as much as you want.

So I only took on such a project that could have done two unhurried work in a day. Such a project, the full one hundred percent implementation of which I saw in my head immediately after voicing the TK.

Usually consisted of a power supply, controller, logic, strapping, and one of the standard plastic enclosures. To speed up the development, a specialized de-payment Pinboard was developed, now it has already a second reincarnation and is being sold as an independent product. Who was on my site , he knows.

To accelerate the design, all typical nodes, such as power stabilizers, microcontrollers with strapping, key drivers, all relay switching, were already pre-set and ready. As a result, the circuit board was assembled as from a designer. I threw on the board the templates of already divorced units, and connected them with tracks, by necessity reigning small details. Thus, the development of the PCB took me from 10 minutes to several hours, no more. Programming is even easier. Because The tasks are simple, then the code is there at the level of the amateur arduinschik
