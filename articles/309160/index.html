Russian Post for Dummies

Russian Post is a large, complex and not very friendly structure. It is customary to spit on it in social networks, and going for a letter or package often turns into a small quest. They say about this: "Life did not prepare me for this."



Want to send a book as a gift to a friend. You come to the department, and a tired woman asks: "How do we ship, by registered packet with a declared value or first class?". And you understand that it will be more complicated than high-load systems, big data and neural networks.

We at Dadat are strongly associated with mail: we correct addresses and are well versed in the features of its work. We wrote a detailed guide to the services of the Russian Post, for dummies. The author of the article is Sergey Korol, editor and post boom.


What are the departures


For a start, let's look at the two basic types of shipments.

A simple shipment is sent independently, without separation - just thrown into the mailbox. These are ordinary postcards and letters.

Registered mail is sent only from the department. For him, fill out the paper, and get a track number to track. Such a shipment is received only with a passport. Additional options are ‚Äúattached‚Äù to registered items:

  • A Notice of Delivery is a legally significant confirmation that you have received a letter. He is loved by all sorts of government organizations and private companies. Any military registration or enlistment office or court will send you a letter with acknowledgment of delivery.

  • The inventory of the attachment is proof that the letter contains
    what is contained. For example, you send a claim to the contract. You make an inventory, and the post office officer stamps it - this means that he has become your witness. Now the recipient will not say that he received congratulations on the anniversary. Usually such letters are sent both with an inventory and a return receipt - a CC!

  • The declared value is, in fact, insurance. If the mail loses the package or damages it, then the value of the declared value will be returned to you. Also, part of the cost will be returned for violation of the delivery time. The higher the declared value, the more expensive the shipment. In fact, to return something difficult. This thing is better not to use.

  • Cash on delivery is the amount that the recipient of the parcel will pay. She will return to the sender by money transfer. With cash on delivery, old online stores often work (normal ones accept cards on the site or send by courier). Cash on delivery cannot be greater than the declared value.

Mail boxes and packages

Parcels


A parcel is a cool mail shortcut, something between a letter and a parcel, but only for printed products. In parcels send books, newspapers, magazines, packs of documents.

A parcel is cheaper, and it does not need to fill in forms. Just put the book in the package, write the address and give it to the operator. The main thing that weighed less than two kilograms (otherwise only the package).

Theoretically, in a parcel you can send a small thing like a T-shirt - the operators do not really watch what is inside.

Class 1 shipments


These are any registered letters, parcels and parcels with a jet attachment. They are more expensive, but reach twice as fast.

For class 1 shipments, there is also no need to fill out godless forms. It is enough to write the address on the envelope or package.

EMS


This is a superstructure above the mail, in the manner of a courier delivery service. In fact, there is nothing to write about it: long, expensive and terrible service. If you really need to send something by courier, choose a private courier service.
