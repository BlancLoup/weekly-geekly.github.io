Microsoft updated SkyDrive app for iPad


* there was a picture with iphone

Some wonder - why release an update specifically for devices with Retina? The answer is that SkyDrive provides the ability to not only store files, but also view photos and documents right in the app. Now with a resolution of 326 pixels per inch.

Another of the innovations is multi-loading of photos and videos from devices to the cloud, an improved system of sharing (either using mail or using a link that you can give to your friends).

The current version of the application - 2.1.
