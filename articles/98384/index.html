Roskomnadzor decided on the procedure for removing comments from the Internet media



It seems that the fight against extremist commentaries has now also been added to the fight against child pornography. Now unwanted servers from data centers comments can be deleted very quickly - during the working day. Today, Roskomnadzor has determined the form and timing of notifying online media about extremist user comments.

The head of this service, Sergey Sitnikov, stated that at first the notifications would be sent to representatives of online media via e-mail, and within working days after receiving the notification, the extremist comment should be removed. Note that the email will be duplicated by fax.

The same Sergey Sitnikov considers regular mail to be ineffective, since an ordinary letter can go in Moscow from a house on a nearby street for several days. That is why Roskomnadzor took as its basis faster ways to transmit messages.

Recall that rumors about the pre-moderation of comments on Internet media sites have been going since the beginning of the summer, however, after the Supreme Court reversed this ruling. However, the monitoring of comments should still be conducted, and Roskomnadzor has been given the authority to determine which of the comments are of an extremist nature, and which are left by good users.

An interesting situation arose after the adoption of such a resolution: Roskomnadzor has already appealed to the agencies "Data" and the APS with the requirement to remove certain comments.
