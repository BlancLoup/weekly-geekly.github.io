Smartpot: the easiest way to grow plants


Pictures to attract attention - smartpot grows cherry tomatoes on the table.

Smartpot or smart flower pot, with the help of this device, any person who wants to grow this or that plant can achieve success.

Inside: what it is, how it works.


What is Smartpot?





All of us, one way or another, belong to IT technologies, and this means that our work is sedentary and also in one place.
Many try to diversify their workplace with various accessories and gadgets, and some with plants.

Moreover, I assume that many people would like to have a living plant on their desktop, but the main problem is that the plant requires attention.

But I stumbled upon an interesting development - Smartpot.

And due to the fact that such a class of devices was not considered at all in the habr, I decided to introduce the habr users to them.


The smartpot is a smart pot for flowers (and other plants). It was created in order to take all the cares to take care of the plant on yourself.

What's inside the device?




A smart pot consists of 3 main parts:
1) Case
2) Logic board
3) Cartridge





We will dwell on the cartridge in more detail, because it is smart : as soon as you install it in the device, all the plant care information, the required humidity, watering frequency and other parameters are read from it.
Thus, as soon as you change the cartridge, the base itself knows how to care for the plant.

Principle of operation of the device



Smartpot works as follows:
After unpacking, you will need to install 4 AA batteries in the slot and pour about 1 liter of water, then you must install the cartridge.
After its installation, all the required parameters for the selected plant will be read.

If all is well, the device will inform you about this with a green indicator.


(A list of all possible indicators for the device)

What can you grow?


The smartpot that was reviewed can grow many plants, below I will list the most interesting plants (in my opinion):
(Balsam, cherry tomatoes , red pepper , celosia, lemon balm )



Smartpots are attractive because they can grow you lemon balm, which you can add to tea all year round, but if you don't like lemon balm, you can always try growing cherry tomatoes and other plants that are not only beautiful, but also tasty. And as I said, with minimal labor costs.
