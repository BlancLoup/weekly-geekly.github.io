Robotic Bed - Disabled Bed



The prototype of the Panasonic robokrovat will be shown on September 29 at the Tokyo-based exhibition of equipment for the disabled and the Big Sight.

The bed is equipped with a built-in TV, and has three main transformation options: for sleeping, for relaxing and for vigorous activity.

Panasonic specialist Yuka Arya believes that these beds will start to be mass-produced in 2015, although it is not yet clear at what price.
