Foundation "Evolution" instead of "Dynasty": the popularization of science in Russia continues


Photo: Stanislav Krasilnikov / TASS

October 31 (this is Saturday) the Dynasty Foundation finishes its work. As is known, this organization supported young scientists, its mission was to popularize science, with the conduct of publishing. Dynasty was founded by Dmitry Zimin, honorary president of VimpelCom, in 2002. For all its existence, the fund has spent about 2 billion rubles on the fulfillment of its goals. The organization has supported more than 2 thousand scientists and 5 thousand teachers, held more than a hundred scientific conferences.
