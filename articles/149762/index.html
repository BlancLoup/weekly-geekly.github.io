Some practical advice from a little experience that has already appeared.
1. First you need to learn how to fly in order to make a normal video, not to threaten the device or to harm others.
2. Strictly comply with safety regulations when flying near people, animals, objects, power lines.
3. One of the best vehicles for shooting long streets is a motorcycle with a sidecar or a rickshaw. Then the driver sees the flying machine well and agrees with him his speed, and the operator freely controls the flight. In our case, the car was used where the brother was the helmsman. He did not see the drone, so it was necessary to adjust the speed in words, and to control - leaning out of the window. All this is inconvenient and affects the quality of flight. It is also necessary to follow the rules of road traffic, while you have to slow down the apparatus flying in the air. This is clearly seen in the video from Builders Avenue.
4. At high altitudes and distances, as well as in conditions of radio interference, it is better to record on an onboard USB flash drive than on a tablet over a radio channel. In our shooting, this can be seen from the shooting on the lake, where the recording on the tablet was turned on and the image disappeared at high altitudes.
Also on Berezovaya Street it was impossible to control the device because of the passing high-voltage line. Therefore, the recording does not start from the beginning of the street.
5. It is advisable to adjust the setting of Dronich so that it is as ‚Äúslowed down‚Äù as possible. Then the image will change more smoothly. This is especially true of models where the camera is rigidly connected to the body, as in our case.
