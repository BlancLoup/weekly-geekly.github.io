11 new business trends for a new economy

Active business today understands the need to pay attention and invest in information technology, tools and products. The overall task is to learn correctly and work more effectively in conditions of total informatization. For this it is important to understand the features of the modern economy and the basics of professional work with information in its various manifestations.
The result and investment are gradually but surely moving from productive and financial capital to ideas, knowledge, data and events. Management is forced to focus on information and provide itself with efficient and productive information tools and technologies in order to maintain and develop competitive positions in established and emerging markets.

Informatization of the economy makes it possible to develop its efficiency due to a more accurate and reasonable formulation of alternative solutions and a conscious choice of the most effective of them. The transformation of the ‚Äúpower management‚Äù model into the ‚Äúinformation management‚Äù model is gradually taking place.

However, the fact that a direct prohibition and blocking cannot cope with this phenomenon becomes more and more distinct. The economic damage to a particular business from the uncontrolled distribution of the author's product can be significant. Copyright holders are protected by different methods, depending on the type and format of their product and potential consumers: from ‚Äúshareware‚Äù versions, to special service paid support and additional benefits (bonuses).
In any case, certain concessions to consumers have to be made and look for recipes rather than in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãprohibitions and restrictions on the dissemination of information based on the unshakable positions of the owner, but in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãflexible and compromise management of information flows.
The search for the best option for the implementation of copyrights and the products that are based on them is one of the directions for the development of modern economic relations, the task of which is to determine not only a decent remuneration, but also the best channels for providing products to consumers.

In the global information economic space, it is necessary to attack and actively defend. Public companies have to reckon with this. Business in anticipation of new technologies of mass communications, in anticipation of creative solutions and convincing for the consumer ways of transmitting information. We regulate knowledge


The flow of information contributes to its constant accumulation and the development of new practically applicable knowledge. In the sphere of economic relations, companies are no longer able to carry out lengthy evidence-based procedures for the formation of truly fundamental concepts and theories with their subsequent statistical and functional verification. Actively use new experiences and new ideas, try and make mistakes, but find advantages in completely contradictory facts and events - this is how business works more and more consciously. Theorization and proof of knowledge will go into the practical plane of their regulation for the short term. If it became clear that a certain chain of actions is successful, then there is nothing more profitable than immediately fixing it for execution. There is no need to do much research when tuning and optimization is done during regular work. For the practice of management, the value of research and consulting services in their classical sense is partially lost. Become a priority to intensively change when obtaining key evidence of the effectiveness of specific practical solutions.

An economy in which information quickly and qualitatively changes a business, it is important to have a special kind of consulting services: system management of knowledge and experience in a particular subject area.

Closely and clearly, the business saw a new technology: the effective time-based use of labor, material and financial resources to carry out specific orders for specific consumers and only on the skillful management of key information. In addition to crowdsourcing and crowdfunding, powerful crowdservice is a service based on generalized tangible, intangible and financial resources for any consumer based on various alternative business process models.

The economy of goods is being transformed in the direction of the economy of services, and producers of goods are moving into the category of service packers. How to deal with the inherent sense of ownership and ownership of exclusive things? Replace them with a sense of superb experience. In the service economy, status is participation in a flight on a suborbital spacecraft, and not the possession of a fashionable sports car.

Robotization: revolution is postponed?


The widespread seizure of jobs by robots in the near future may be at risk of banal management problems. Building a coherent production process based on robotic systems requires a bit more effort than it seems at first glance. The reason, of course, is not the complexity of the production and implementation of technological and error-free robots in the technological cycles. The reason is that the robot cannot ignore or correct the orders issued to it. To build business processes that will be executed by algorithm-dependent mechanisms requires a damn ability to model processes accurately and in detail. Note that often the robots in the enterprise are surrounded by the caring hands of engineering and technical specialists who constantly set them up, reprogram them and serve them. Such professionals act as a stabilizing link between top-management solutions and real-world capabilities of programmable hardware. The situation is similar when using information technology for automation. No business can normally use automated control systems in one way or another without recourse to the services of those professionals who are able to transform a complex reality into fixed software algorithms.
The employee in his place can not only follow the instructions of the manager for the pre-installed algorithms, but also correct his execution. He has the experience and skills to give feedback. Ignoring the inadequate decisions of a higher management can be useful for business in certain cases. A significant number of routine procedures are already performed today by robots or robotic complexes. But a complete transition to a closed robotic cycle without an understanding of all the features associated with this by the management team is impossible.

The development of a special type of production based on the processes performed by robots is effective in fairly narrow production operations. In this sense, it is advantageous to create customized production and technical platforms that are ready for specific orders to carry out special operations for the manufacture, assembly or packaging. Highly specialized, but highly efficient production services are able to concentrate and develop a resource base with a minimum composition of robots and relevant specialists.

Management of risks


Economic entities are exposed to a variety of risks in their activities. And this list is constantly expanding and will expand as a result of the increasing complexity of the technologies and technological tools used, as well as as a result of all the intensive and close interaction of the subjects. Today, every business depends not only on itself, but also on many other businesses. If a company does not want to lose everything overnight and face the insurmountable consequences of an extraordinary event, it must conceive and defend against the most probable and destructive risks. Alone, no business can handle.
Not every company can afford to have in its structure professional risk managers who could stop a business from a line beyond which the risk is unreasonably great. And if a business is built on its basis not on material assets, but on ideas and knowledge, then risk management is an important point not only for its growth, but also, first of all, for self-preservation.
