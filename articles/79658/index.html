Multimedia "sloth" with a keyboard

Lenovo has released a strange device - a remote control with a mini keyboard and a trackball.



The product has no names (not counting the same set of letters and numbers 57Y6336), it has appeared on sale only in Singapore (worldwide - at the beginning of January). However, a wireless multimedia remote control (2.4 GHz, 10 meters, the transmitter is connected to a PC via USB) can be very useful in a household device. Especially if it is the PC in the house that acts as a multimedia and entertainment center. The cost, however, scares: $ 60.
