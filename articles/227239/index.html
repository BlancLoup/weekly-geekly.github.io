3 of 130. New Android tablets at Intel Atom


In early June, Intel president Rene James, speaking at Computex, promised that this year 130 new Android and Windows tablets from leading manufacturers on the Intel platform will appear on the market. The first copies were also presented there - all on Windows. And in less than a month, the claimed ASUS models under the ASUS brand appeared on the counters: ASUS MeMO Pad 7 , ASUS MeMO Pad 8 , ASUS Transformer Pad - 7, 8 and 10-inch tablets, the latter with an attached keyboard included. Tablets come with Android OS 4.4 KitKat; their heart is the 4-core Intel Atom Z3745 processor (Bay Trail generation). The amount of internal memory - 16 GB, RAM - 1 GB. Cost on Amazon is $ 149, $ 199 and $ 299 respectively.
Well, a start. Well, with the help of simple arithmetic operations, you can find out that there are still a lot to see for the new products this year.
