Habrostatistika, part 2 - refutation with graphs

Yesterday I published an article on the topic of when it is better to publish in the Internet. Since then, my spider has collected statistics for the entire 2015, and the picture has changed a bit. I also built several graphs, and the whole picture became clearer. Caution traffic!

First, we look at the statistics separately for each day:

Designations on the graphs are as follows:
  1. Quantity - the number of articles published at this time.
  2. Comments - average number of comments left
  3. Views - the average number of views scored
  4. Rate - the average typed rating
