# MBLT14: three days left until the end of discounts



Less than a month left before the international mobile conference # MBLT14 . More than 600 participants and 30 foreign and Russian speakers will gather on May 23 in Moscow to discuss the latest mobile technology news and be inspired by new ideas for business development.

Representatives of Twitter, Facebook, Opera, Spotify, Distimo, Deutsche Telecom, Aviasales, Paypal, Line, Mail.Ru, SoundCloud, Qualcomm, Dentsu, Samsung, SPBTV and many other companies confirmed their presentation. The special guest will be the CEO of Opera Software - Lars Boylesen.

Until May 1, you can purchase a ticket at a discount. Hurry up!
