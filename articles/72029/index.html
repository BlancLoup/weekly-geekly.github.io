About what bumps I filled

Hello Habrahabr community!
I would like to share my experience with those who started, or are just thinking of starting their own business, in particular, related to web design.
A drop of history
I have been working in the field of web design for almost 5 years. I started naturally with a free-lancer, created projects, reputation, etc. Everything was good for the time being. While on the horizon, the dream of a lifetime has not loomed at that moment - own business. With a few months of deliberation, I decided on this, as I now understand the desperate step. By chance, I found investors, and they helped me with registering a legal entity, start-up capital, etc. etc. The team at that time, too, was quite worked out. Everything turned out great, the company appeared, a few months later our own office, though small, but still, the feeling of wild pathos did not leave our hearts :-).


A thrash broom start


My biggest and most stupid mistake was the selection of the team. When the team was assembled, and nothing predicted trouble, the fun began ...
More specifically, the permanent members of the team, with soul and mind, they still lived in free lance, and stubbornly did not want to understand that the level was already somewhat different. Initially, I was engaged in HTML layout, and knew a couple of engines (vBulletin, WordPress, IPB, Joomla, DLE, etc.), that is, it was, let's say, in the subject line, and it was harder for employees case jamb. Since the guys were already ‚Äútheirs,‚Äù of course, I would like to have a decent salary. But, unfortunately, there were no orders (they were not looking there), and powerful delays were obtained, which, of course, spoiled the relationship. I had no idea about business management. Mistake, was in the wrong motivation of the team, and not the ability to set goals. This is where the second so-called "FAP-FAP" appeared.

Error two - Timing.


Before creating a studio and moving to the ‚Äúofficially‚Äù level, we were famous for the terms and quality of work. What some specialists have been doing for a week could have been done in 24 hours. After registering the company, all this has gone somewhere. The problem was in me, I set the tasks for people, and, as was to be expected, they performed them incorrectly. The problem was that in the studio, almost, I was almost everything, I was a layout designer, I was an account manager, and a client relations manager, and a programmer (php know, very mediocre) and a seo-optimizer, and manager projects, in general, a person is an orchestra, and as a logical conclusion, everything was done poorly, because I simply did not have time.

Mistake the third is self motivation and time management.


Very, very painful topic. Absolutely ridiculous distribution of own working time to all garbage, as me, and the team as a whole. Hence the second problem of this point - the motivation itself, could not force itself to overcome congenital laziness in some issues. It was not possible to create motivation for oneself and the team. And I do not know why. The strongest motivation (one of) is money, but it did not work, and there was no money ... a paradox, however. Distributing your own time, and seeing the final goal, is vital. Think on 3, no, better on 4 or even 5 steps in front, calculate all situations, believe, it will be a plus.

Error Fourth - Work with Clients


I was absolutely wrong (and yes, I am ashamed) in working with some clients. I pressed on the "party line" without making the slightest concessions. So you can not do. Wrong construction of the work process, relaxation of the ‚Äúleash‚Äù.

What was after?

  • In no case, nor when, nor who do not trust in business.
  • The rule is that there are no friends in the business.
