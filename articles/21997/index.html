The most expensive Apple iPhone bought Russian


The most expensive iPhone in the world for 120,000 euros was purchased by a businessman from Russia . The name of the buyer is not disclosed in the interests of the investigation. However, according to the number of billionaires, Russia, if you believe the magazine Forbes, ranked second in the world, and it makes sense to look for an amateur of high technologies in this list.
The Apple phone, modified by Austrian jeweler Peter Alloyson , costs 120,000 euros and is called the iPhone Princess . In functionality, the phone, trimmed with 18-carat gold and 318 diamonds, does not outperform the usual "iPhones". Simple mortals who cannot afford to spend such serious sums on gadgets, Alloison offers the iPhone Brilliants - the total weight of the gems used for inlay is less, so it costs only 45 thousand euros. However, in a decent society with this cheap it is better not to appear - they will laugh.
