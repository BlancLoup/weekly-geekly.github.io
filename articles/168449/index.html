Hello!

What are the consequences may increase the loading time of the site just 1 second? Catastrophic! For example, for Amazon, this could result in a shortfall of $ 1.6 billion a year . Learn more about why it is worth being cautious with the addition of heavy chips to the site, reducing its performance, see the video from Alconost .



A bit of text and a long infographic under the cut.


According to USA Today, most Americans will not wait in the queue if it takes more than 15 minutes, half of the visitors never return to the place where they were forced to wait, and every fifth buyer can be rude if they are served ‚Äútoo slowly.‚Äù

Approximately the same picture is observed in the online world. Every fourth visitor leaves the page if it loads for more than 4 seconds. You will lose about half of your visitors if the page on their mobile device does not open in 10 seconds.
And if we are talking about e-commerce, then every second becomes important: 40% of mobile customers leave the online store, whose pages load longer than 3 seconds.

  • decrease in the number of page views by 11%;
  • decrease in customer satisfaction by 16%;
  • decrease in the rate of conversion of visitors into buyers by 7%.
