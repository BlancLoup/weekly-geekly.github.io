New on SMSter.ru: "Interesting" feed, search, MMS and actions!

Over the last month, almost 8 thousand new entries were posted on the SMSter , and we sent out 60 thousand SMS alerts. In addition, there appeared a lot of new features, the most interesting of which are below:

1. Now each SMStera user has a karma . However, unlike karma on Habrahabr, karma on the SMSter is entirely in the user's hands - the more posts and comments he publishes, the more friends he invited and the more users added to his friends or subscribed to his channels - the higher the karma. The more karma, the higher the user in the rating and the more features of the SMSter are available to him.



2. SMSter.ru has a system for encouraging interesting entries . Clicking on the "like" link, the user recommends this entry to his friends (they fall into their "Interesting" feed). The more users liked the post, the higher the karma of its author.



3. Now you can search not only other users on SMSter.ru, but also their records, even if the search word or phrase is in response to the message! It also became easier to find the channel of interest - the search works by the names and descriptions of the channels.



4. Following the placement of previews of pictures, we added the ability to receive messages with photos via MMS . Thus, on SMStere you can now keep your mobile photo blog. You can also send messages using regular e-mail.

5. And, perhaps, the most noticeable change: actions appeared in the entry field. We write in SMSter.ru about what is happening with us, what surprises us, interests us , what we think , what we observe , want , love or hate , where we were , we are or will be .
Now, in WEB- and WAP-interfaces you can start your messages with one of such actions and it will be marked with a special color tab - it will help you navigate your own and others' records, and make using SMSter more vivid and interesting :)
