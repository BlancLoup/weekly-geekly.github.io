Conference on AR Augmented Reality Technologies

Dear habravchane,

Epson is an information partner of the AR Conference, which will be held on November 14 at the Artplay design center.


This event is dedicated to the trends, development prospects and applications of Augmented Reality, which will show the new possibilities of using AR-technologies in everyday life as well as in marketing, PR, entertainment, innovative, scientific and even secret projects.

Visitors will have the opportunity to personally test the latest wearable gadgets, and experts will be able to communicate with experts and market leaders, make useful business connections, and, possibly, conclude a promising contract!

The conference will:

  • discuss trends and key aspects of the use of AR-technologies in various areas of business and science;
  • the best advertising, gaming and entertainment cases using similar technologies and a review of wearable AR-gadgets are presented;
  • demonstrated the best AR-applications, Startup-projects and much more, which, perhaps, you do not even know.

Participants of the event:

  • top managers, representatives of marketing and PR departments of advertising and media agencies, investment and financial companies, as well as various funds, corporations, technology parks;
  • investors and business angels;
  • representatives of the state segment.

Come, it will be interesting!

Date and place: November 14, 2014, Moscow, st. Lower Syromyatnicheskaya, 10, Artplay.
Website: www.ar-conf.ru
