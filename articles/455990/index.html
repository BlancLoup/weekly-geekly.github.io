Illustrated description of our cooling system. And an unexpected offer for very economical, but a little fearless owners of server hardware.


The cooling system of the server equipment based on the contour heat pipes is considered as an alternative to the fluid system. Compared to efficiency, it is cheaper to implement and operate. At the same time, even in theory, it does not allow leakage of fluid inside expensive server equipment.


Last year, our first experimental rack was mounted in the DataPro data center. It consists of forty identical Supermicro servers. The first twenty of them with a standard cooling system, the second twenty - with a modified. The purpose of the experiment is to test the applicability of our cooling system in a real data center, in a real rack, in real servers.
