BlackBerry PlayBook became the first tablet certified for the government

Interesting news in the light of the fact that Russian officials will lose their iPad and get protected tablets.

Latest news: RIM reports that the QNX-powered BlackBerry PlayBook has received a FIPS 140-2 certificate, making it the first tablet to be certified for use in US federal organizations.


According to a press release , the PlayBook is the only tablet that successfully passed FIPS (Federal Information Processing Standards, Federal Information Processing Standards) certification at NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology, National Institute of Standards and Technology), which is required by US federal law Information Security 2002 (FISMA).

Yes, I understand that there are too many abbreviations. But cool news!

The event is more fully covered by National Post , TheBerryFix and CrackBerry.com .
