Google I / O Extended in the CIS


Dear habravchane, IT-Schnick, developers and others not indifferent to technology people! As you probably know, the famous annual Google I / O conference will take place on May 10-11 in a distant California in the glorious city of San Francisco. It will bring together leading developers of Google, who will talk about the latest most delicious technologies. The conference is so popular that tickets are sold out a few months before the event. To give everyone a chance to feel at the conference, Google, in partnership with the Google Technology User Groups communities, is streaming the event, Google I / O Extended , in various cities around the world.
