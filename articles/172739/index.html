Update L-style smartphones 2013

Hello everyone, today we will tell you about the new LG L-style smartphones, which have changed the first generation L9, L7, L5 and L3.


LG L-Series 2012.

The L-device line was created as an inexpensive and practical solution for those who simply need a modern smartphone. Not a super flagship, not 8 cores, not record figures in benchmarks, but neat, convenient in everyday situations, a long-playing device with a pleasant appearance and a good screen.

The company received a lot of feedback from users and decided to update the line of smartphones, taking into account the wishes and criticism.

First, all three mainstream smartphones (L3, L5, L7) will receive both single-value and double-value execution.

Secondly, the design of dvuhsimochnikov will differ from single-device devices not only by the buttons at the bottom. Odnosimochnye devices retain the basic features of the first generation - a neat and strict design, hardware key "home", touch "back" and "menu."


LG L-Series 2013: L7, L5 and L3

Dvuhsimochniki get a different body shape, the keys below the screen will be touch, and the texture of the back panel will be different depending on the color of the device.
