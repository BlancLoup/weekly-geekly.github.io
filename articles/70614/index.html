Consumer Expert Social Network

Our mobile marketing agency (SMS distribution) constantly faces a lack of constructive communication between consumers and suppliers of goods and services. Perhaps this is some sort of regional peculiarity, but it is difficult for a very simple man in the street to make purchases without visiting the point of sale directly and further the risk of acquiring low-quality goods or services, etc. And suppliers do not see their customers. That is why I am trying to perhaps crookedly present to the court some kind of expert, consumer network where:
Users on their pages can select and record their favorite brands and preferences for:
1. Direct contact with suppliers of goods and services in the region. Meet with an assortment and review comments. Getting information from companies.
2. Receiving or generation on the e-discount web page of the barcode on the phone. (friend's story than an infinite amount of plastic discounts).
3. Communication on various topics, products, services, etc. Information exchange.
4. The ability to leave wishes in comments or blogs, wishes to purchase a service and a description of its quality.
5. Receive remuneration for sales ideas, organizing stocks and projects, attracting buyers.

Suppliers.
1. Companies in the region have pages with a brief description and a link to the main site with the possibility of placing on their page a product catalog.
2. Visitors can recommend a list of goods that are used; the name is a link to the catalogs.
3. Polls. Ratings Shares attract attention, by means of word of mouth.

Additional programs for visitors.
Economy. The counter of expenses of the means and the publication of some articles, for example "utility expenses", for discussion of variants of economy or improvement of quality.

Family. Bonus program to attract friends and relatives to actively participate in the network.
The ability to export notebooks from any phone.

Ideas. Any ideas for improving the quality of the provision of goods and services. Competitions-applications with a prize fund from companies.
We will be grateful for the comments, because all the above technologies have long existed and are working, but a huge number of non-advanced users do not even guess about them.
The question of promotion is another question ...
