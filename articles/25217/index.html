Philips Xenium 9 @ 9k: First Impressions

I always thought that Nokia is the brand of cell phones with which I will always be. However, after reading about Philips Xenium 9 @ 9k, my holy faith in Nokia has been shaken. Within a month there were hesitations and yet it broke: last night I bought Xenium 9 @ 9k in Euroset.



It is impossible to fully recognize the phone in the evening, so I will describe first first impressions.

Bought in Euroset. There was no promised 128 MB microSD. The color was different too. The price was within the stated - i.e. no more than 4000r: 3990.%)

Externally, it looks decent.

Size: two matchboxes:



I was pleased with the function of the repeating alarm clock - i.e. immediately for the whole week. A trifle, of course, but it is this triviality that is not found on most simple Nokia phones.



I also liked the fact that for all connected external devices (charging, headset, connection with a computer) one connector is used: miniUSB.



Little things that do not cause discomfort, but still present:
1. You can not bring the radio to the speaker - only in headphones.
2. Menu items that do not fit on the screen. In order to read completely you have to wait a couple of seconds, then they begin to slowly crawl to the left. Patience is not enough to read to the end.


That's all for now. I only need a phone for calls - i.e. for its main function, so all its add. opportunities are of little interest. If you are interested in any other details - ask.
