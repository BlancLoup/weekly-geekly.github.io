Flash GAMM - Flash Game Developers Conference


Prologue (or some lyrics):
The atmosphere of a decadent feedback vacuum still reigns in the already large, but so far barely noticeable eye of a potential investor in the online flash games industry. Without unnecessary noise, large and small games are created. In a calm and silent atmosphere, virtual money supply, micropayments, transactions, and numerous end-user clicks occur.

There is not enough communication: they are silent, earn, spend and dream, sometimes they want to share the joy of success, the thirst for success, unlimited experience, learn a couple of important and non-obvious secrets, games, game developers, creators of unique worlds can tell a lot to each other about the realities of the online game development May 14, two thousand and nine in a huge glorious city, in Moscow.

FlashGAMM! - This is a meeting of professional developers of flash games to share experiences, contacts and ideas. First of all, this is a meeting of producers, programmers and artists, as well as representatives of gaming portals and potential investors.

Conference objectives:
Get to know each other. Develop and cheer up.
Find out where the game money is and how to get it from there.
To voice actual problems and tasks of the industry, together to search for their solution.
To develop the flash games industry and the Flash platform as a whole.

Date and place:
The meeting will be held in Moscow on May 14, 2009 (Thursday), the day before the CWI.
The venue is the Cosmos Hotel.
