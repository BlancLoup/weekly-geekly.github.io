Robots will take away male professions, female professions - out of threat

Scientists from Oxford in 2013 analyzed the skills that are needed in more than 700 different professions, and found that 47% of these professions in the coming decades will be taken by robots. A deeper study of the data showed that typical female professions are not in danger - men will remain without work.
