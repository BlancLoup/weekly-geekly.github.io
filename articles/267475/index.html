How to bypass the lock screen in iOS without knowing the password

I was surprised to find that Habr had bypassed the news that anyone can bypass the iPhone lock screen and gain access to private data, such as photos and the phone book. Provided that the smartphone is enabled Siri.



For those who do not want to watch the video or who could not play, I provide detailed instructions.
The main thing in the second step.

1. Enter the wrong password four times.
2. For the fifth time, enter the THREE digits, hold down the Home button and immediately press the fourth button to enter the password.
3. Ask Siri what time it is.
4. Click on the appeared Clock clock.
5. In the opened application, click on the "+" button.
6. Next in the search enter any set of letters.
7. Select the previously entered set.
8. In the pop-up menu that appears, select the item "Share".
9. In the new menu, select "Messages".
! If there appears a window for entering the password to the device, then you have done something wrong in paragraph 2.

10. Well, then simple, you can create a new contact or view the entire phone book. When creating a new contact, you can view the photos by selecting the option "assign a photo to the contact."
