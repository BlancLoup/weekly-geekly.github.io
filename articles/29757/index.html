YouTube is not an argument yet

Actress and playwright Tricia Walsh-Smith decided to divorce her husband. Yes, that's bad luck, even before the wedding back in 1999, a marriage contract was signed, which turned out to be absolutely unprofitable to the mercantile American. Not finding legal loopholes, she decided to call the public for help by posting a video clip on YouTube in April of this year, in which she talks about all the weights of the female married share.


But, justice triumphed, and the court found that with this video Tricia not only humiliates her husband, but also puts pressure on the court. After that, the verdict was rendered: the marriage contract was considered valid, Tricia needed to leave her husband's apartment within 30 days, and her husband needed to pay the ex-wife compensation in the amount of $ 750,000. However, Philip Smith, the owner of Broadway theaters and part-time ex-husband of the actress was very pleased with the decision of the court.
