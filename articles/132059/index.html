Android Market will soon be able to pay with PayPal



PayPal may soon become one of the permissible means of payment for goods in the Android Market app store. Of course, rumors about this move by the search giant have been circulating for quite some time, but recently they have received more real reasons.

Recently lit and already started to appear on some devices Android Market 3.3.11 in its code contains references to PayPal. In the .apk file of the new Android Market application store, there is a number of lines pointing to the payment system. Thus, the emergence of the ability to pay with PayPal may soon become a reality.

Now, some users have the opportunity to buy Android applications using Checkout, which is considered as a competitor to PayPal. At the same time, Google enters into partnership agreements with operators on the use of their accounts in payment applications, so support for PayPal is a reasonable step, which is intended to increase the income of developers and Google itself.

Android is becoming more common, and because Google should provide users with ample opportunities to pay for goods in its app store. However, so far the Internet giant has not made any official statements about it.
