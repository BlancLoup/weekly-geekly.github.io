Zhongji Metallurgical Holding buys the creators of Family Farm for $ 960 million


China is once again blowing up the minds of ordinary people with news from the local mobile industry. Not only is the amount too high (for example, Zynga bought NaturalMotion for only $ 527 million). So also the buyer was a company that has nothing to do with the game industry. Zhongji Holding is engaged in metal trading, the sale of building materials and real estate.

FunPlus Game agreed to sell its division of DianDian Interactive Technology along with such well-known projects as Family Farm, Family Farm Seaside, Happy Acres, Royal Story and another non-announced game. Zhongji will also receive 120 employees who support these projects. The deal will be closed in 2015, and FunPlus Game will advise Zhongji for the next 3 years.

It is doubtful whether such investments will pay off Zhongji. FunPlus's monthly revenue is about $ 6 million, and profits are $ 4 million. DianDian Interactive Technology division is about half of the company, and FunPlus still has Barn Voyage strategy, as well as about five upcoming games. Guide FunPlus even decided not to conduct an initial public offering on the stock exchange right before the deal.


Gumi opens four new offices and turns into a new market giant


Japanese publisher and developer of mobile games Gumi, known for the games Brave Frontier and Puzzle Trooper, announced the opening of four new development studios. Company offices will be located in Austin, Vancouver, Stockholm and Kiev. This is despite the fact that the company is currently developing 30 new games!
The company also plans to open an office in San Francisco, which will develop business and PR. The head of the Gumi development department in the United States will be AJ Redmer, a former Xbox Studio manager.

All this is done in order to finally gain a foothold in the Western market and in the future to become one of its leaders. Let's see what happens.


Bandai Namco launches Pac-Man Friends us and Android


Good old hits continue to bring money. A new shoot on the old Pac-Man tree from the Bandai Namco gaming unit - Pac-Man Friends on iOS and Android.



New friends Kolobok, who gave the name of the application, have special abilities - invisibility for ghosts, the ability to pass where others do not pass, and the like. They are saved and join the player as they progress through the game. All the Koloboks on the screen are controlled at the same time - shifting one, you move everyone else. In fact, this is a modification of the old puzzle in the style of PacMan.

Time will tell whether to wait for Pac-Man Run and Clash of Pac-Mans, and now the game can already be purchased at app stores, all available seven episodes with 95 mazes, for $ 4.99. Wow, an unusual price from the Japanese, did someone make a mistake when choosing $ 14.99?
