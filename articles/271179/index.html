Results of the Russian Developers Cup 2015



On November 14, the final of the third international championship of the Russian Developers Cup was held , in which developers from dozens of countries gathered in teams of like-minded people and put their ideas into practice. The total duration of the championship was six months, and more than 3,500 people from 38 countries took part in it. During this time, 166 prototype projects were created.

The expert jury , consisting of leading developers, business consultants, game designers and marketers, chose 48 finalist projects in two categories: technology start-ups and game projects (24 projects each).

In the category of "technology startups" winners are:
  • RISE.ME is a service that allows you to create a company representation in social networks.
  • VR Planetarium is an application for virtual reality glasses, with which you can ‚Äúvisit‚Äù the planets of the solar system.
  • Moreven is a site that allows you to search for goods and services through communication, where you can share your need for free and anonymously.
  • One City Day - an application for building tourist routes around the city based on preferences and free time.
  • Leecero: text-to-action service - a web service to automate the interaction between companies and their customers using software bots.
  • The designer of educational quests is a technology that turns tutoring lessons into an exciting computer game.
  • The alphabet with elements of AR is a mobile application that allows you to supplement the pages of the alphabet with interactive animation.
  • Online consultations of doctors - a site where you can get qualified advice on health at any time.
  • Sport Clone is a free online service for home sports activities in conjunction with an online trainer. This project won in last year's Russian Developers Cup.

In the near future, all these projects will receive grants of 400 thousand rubles each from the Foundation for Assistance to the Development of Small Forms of Enterprises in the Scientific and Technical Sphere.
