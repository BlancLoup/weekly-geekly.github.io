Atmel optimized parametric microcontroller search for mobile phones


Atmel announced the launch of a mobile version of the parametric search for microcontrollers - Microcontroller Selector . Functionally, the search repeats a similar tool for the PC and works for all existing Atmel microcontroller families from AVR and Cortex-M0 to Cortex-M7 and -A5.

As in the full version, you can add / remove the list of displayed parameters, as well as change their relative position.


Clicking on any of the positions opens a window with brief information on the characteristics, as well as direct links to the detailed product page, datasheet and the pages of purchase and order samples.

The selection results can be sent in a few taps as a link by mail or to print.
