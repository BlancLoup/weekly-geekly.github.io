New Year! Distributing 10 Trips to New Orleans on VeeamON 2017

2017 is already very close, and on this occasion, with an influx of positive feelings, I want to share two things on behalf of Veeam:

  1. The most important thing is THANKS to you for being with us during the full different events of 2016. We wish you all good health, success and peace in the new year.

  2. In this honor, Veeam launched the already traditional holiday prank when you can win one of ten fully paid trips to New Orleans , USA at VeeamON 2017, the international conference of virtual infrastructure administrators , during the week of May 15th.



What are the prizes and how to participate?


Until the end of the year, Veeam will distribute 10 paid trips to VeeamON 2017 . Each trip includes:

  • Full conference entrance ticket including meals
  • Fly back and forth to New Orleans from the residence of the winner
  • Rent a room in a good hotel near the conference venue

To participate, fill out a short application on the draw page . Deadline - December 31 inclusive. Winners will be chosen arbitrarily.

What is VeeamON?


We wrote about it in detail here . In short, VeeamON is an international conference for administrators of virtual infrastructures and not only. In 2014 and 2015, the conference was held in Las Vegas. Below added a video review, as it was last time.


In 2017, we plan to take 2 times more participants (about 3,500 people) in New Orleans. The first day will feature such prominent speakers as Mark Russinovich , CTO, Microsoft Azure and Sanjay Poonen, Customer Operations, VMware. In total, within three days of the conference there will be more than 85 sessions on technical and business topics.

Also during the conference, we traditionally give a check for $ 10,000 USD to a person who will show the best knowledge of Veeam products.
