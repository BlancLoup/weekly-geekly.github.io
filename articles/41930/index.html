Gippos: look where your friends are

Gippos is a free program for mobile devices that allows you to determine your location, show it to your friends and see on the map of other users. In addition, you can exchange messages with users from the contact list.

To determine your coordinates using Gippos, you will need to use GPS, and in order to exchange messages and see on the map of other users - access to the Internet.

In addition, you can see where a registered Gippos user is located at www.gippos.ru . If you wish, you can hide your coordinates, then other users will not see you on the map. Details can be found in the Instructions section.

Currently available version for Windows Mobile 5.0 / 6.0, soon will be released for the iPhone and PC (win32).

The product is in beta testing, so we will be grateful for the feedback you can leave in the Email us section.
