World OpenStack Summit and other news

Author: Ilya Stechkin

Do you think that we just kept silent all this time that the spring sun has worn us out? Not at all! We chose what to tell you. After all, so much is happening that there is no time to write.
In April, we worked with the materials of the Mirantis Open Day in Russia . At the same time they experimented with A / B testing the site (thanks to Pasha Karpov).
