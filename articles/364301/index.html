YouTube has the opportunity to make GIFs from video

Movie can be cut into animated images


YouTube is slowly rolling out the ability to export the video in the format of animated GIF-images. Official statement has not yet been, the first novelty said Andy Baio.

So far, the feature is not available everywhere. For example, on the GIF, you can crush any video channel PBS Idea Channel (at least this ). Just click on the Share / Share button to see the GIF sub-item.

The function works very quickly, everything happens right on the YouTube servers.
