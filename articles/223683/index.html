How the SPY module was created

Creating such a module was a very difficult task both from the technical side and from getting iTunes connects. Several large analytical companies, having gathered a decent pool of applications, became monopolists in the market of mobile analytics. But we believed that it was possible to create a technically more advanced service, and most importantly, make it free! Developers do not have to pay for analytics, especially at $ 10,000 per month.

The team clearly understood that the module would not be the goal of making money, but the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating a truly useful service for mobile developers was very motivating.
