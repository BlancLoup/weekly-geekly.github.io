A revolution on the horizon navigation: Google Maps 5



Together with the start of sales of Google Nexus S, the search giant has pleased all owners of smartphones with the Android OS. And the joy is in a big update of Google Maps to version 5.

And the revolution is that soon you will not need any other navigation software other than Google Maps. Monopoly! Who is glad?

And now more.


What is going on?


The new version of Google Maps is available for smartphones, with the Android OS version 1.6 or higher. This is generous enough. However, not all functions are supported in older versions of the OS. You can fully appreciate all the advantages of the fifth version of Google Maps on Android 2.0+.

What changed?


Five years after the service was introduced, 3D mode was finally added. And he is unique. All objects are a mathematical code. So, for drawing an object, your smartphone considers simple equations. So mapping becomes smooth and fast.



Fully updated design of the application.
