Who does and will do online shopping in Russia?


In Russia every day there are new online stores. At the same time creating even a small online store is quite a difficult task. At the conference, held this month by the information portal Oborot.ru, it was reported that in 2011 more than $ 1 billion was invested in the Russian e-commerce market. And this investment is equal to the investment in the US e-commerce market.

So who gets these investments in Russia?

Basically, all the funds go to the maintenance and development of existing Russian projects.

Who creates new online stores?

1. Projects already working and earning money. Attract investments from Russian and Western funds for the development and maintenance of expensive backoffice. The costs of which are huge and often comparable to income. But no one is looking at the revenues, everyone is trying to grab a piece of the already growing pie.

2. Money gives teams with the experience of creating e-commerce projects, which, under various conditions, ‚Äúraise‚Äù the project and give it to the customer. At the same time, there is no founder as such in the project, and this adversely affects its results.

3. Large network companies that have free funds for new projects, as well as back-office and network infrastructure.

Here are some examples:
  • X5 RETAIL GROUP created a project. Order table E5 . This is an online store that allows you to pick up orders yourself in the X5 Retail Group retail chain. The catalog of this online store contains more than 400 thousand products.
  • HOFF is a network of hypermarkets selling home & dekor goods. Promotes its online hypermarket hoff.ru
  • M.Video - the site of this retail network has long been the largest online home appliance store.
  • MediaMarkt has also recently opened Internet sales.


4. Distributors and manufacturers open online stores in order to sell products at retail. For them it is easier than for everyone else, the goods are already in stock. Plus, there is a significant amount of resources to create an online site.

Examples of famous projects:
  • Citilink electronic discounter is a project of a major distributor of computer equipment MERLION .
  • The company Posuda.ru is the largest wholesaler of tableware with its online stores.


5. Young entrepreneurs. There is less and less chance of creating a profitable online store and having a good growth without significant investment. Previously, it was possible to find an exclusive product or a niche market. Now it is quite difficult, all the main categories of goods are sold by large online stores. They have good conditions when working with suppliers, they can buy goods at their warehouse, accordingly they can give a lower price and provide better service.

But the most interesting is the fact of almost complete indifference to e-commerce of those who have the very millions to turn them into billions.
The largest enterprises and distributors are not in a hurry to go online. The main reasons for this are the older generation of executives, who are very poorly versed in technologies and the absence of fierce competition that would force them to begin to understand.
