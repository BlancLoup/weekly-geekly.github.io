Door handle with combination lock


Another simple invention from the category of "it is strange, why no one had thought of this before." She came to the head of designer Jess Han (Jess Han), who proposed to mount a combination lock right in the door handle. Of course, we would not advise anyone to put such a lock on the front door to the house, but it would fit the storeroom or utility room in some state institutions. For protection from access of children in household chores, perhaps, too. It remains only to wait until this idea is launched into mass production. By the way, you can make another modification that has a lock on one side of the door only. The main thing is not to put such a handle on the door in the toilet.
