On a surface

For a start - about the iron component. The screen diagonal of the Dell Latitude 10 is 10 inches with a resolution of 1366x768 pixels. The system works on the Intel Atom Z2760 processor with a peak frequency of 1.8 GHz. This is the only (so far) Intel chip made specifically for tablet computers.
