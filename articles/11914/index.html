Fujitsu launches the world's largest 2.5-inch external hard drive

Reports of new, even larger hard disk drives have been constantly replacing each other lately. Only half a year ago, the release of the first hard drive of 1 terabyte was reported. I then offered my wife to celebrate this event, but did not meet with understanding. :-)

Today, Fujitsu manufactures the world's most capacious 2.5-inch external hard drive with a capacity of 300 gigabytes. The device is equipped with a USB 2.0 interface. The rotation speed of the spindle is 4200 revolutions per minute.
The package includes software to encrypt data. So if you lose a precious drive with secret data, the enemies will not be able to take advantage of your absent-mindedness.

Together with a 300-gigabyte model, a model with a capacity of 120 gigabytes and a spindle speed of 5400 rpm will be launched on the market.

The price of the older model in the US is $ 229, and the youngest is $ 129.

Expected delivery date - the third quarter of this year.
