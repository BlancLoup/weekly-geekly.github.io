Sviaz-Expocomm 2010 through our eyes: photo report

It has already happened so that we go to all more or less noticeable events with a camera, or even more than one. We cannot say that we somehow specially plan the shooting, we are preparing for this. Rather, we like the reports captured on the go. They probably will not be glossy, maybe the picture will suffer somewhere, but in our opinion, the moment is better transmitted. We bring to your attention a photo report from the annual Sviaz-Expocomm exhibition, whose history spans almost two decades. Today, you can still have time to look there to see everything yourself. The photo report does not in any way claim to be useful, a lot of interesting things remain behind the scenes. Most of the photos are provided with comments.
