A month ago, I sold my Acer e-machines d620 and bought the Asus k40ij (there was no webcam on the first one).
I installed the standard software including the Asus Life Frame program in which there is a small menu with games - but this is far from those I want to tell you about, these games with the imposition of various hats and other things pushed me to the point that there are full-fledged casual games for webcam.

This is where my story begins, I went to Google and after a short period of time I already had a few pages open with descriptions of games, about the most interesting of them - below.

1. OvO Webcam Games

This is perhaps my most successful find. The game features 11 games for the webcam, where you need to use your arms, legs, and head to control the action of the game. The program responds to any movement, but for the full sensation of the game will require a webcam at least ... with an average refresh rate. Also this game is very picky in relation to the background that is behind your back, and also flatly refuses to work without natural daylight.



And I will also inform you that the game is paid - the full version of the game costs 478 rubles, but there is an opportunity to try the demo version (I did not identify any restrictions) or to find and download the game on some portal that does not make You labor .

www.ovogame.com

2. Webcam Cyclops play

'WebCam Cyclops Play' is a collection of 5 games that, thanks to a webcam and special technologies, will take you to the monitor screen.

  • Streetball (street basketball)
  • Wasp killers

3. Motion Bubbles

Pretty simple and interesting game made on flash. Your task is to burst balls with your head or head and avoid flying prickly mines. You have 60 seconds for which you need to score the most points.



Game page:
www.extendedreality.com/webcam_game_motion_bubbles.html

4. Shouter

This is a bonus for those who do not have a webcam, but have a microphone. A flash game in which the airship is controlled through a microphone - you need to create noise to fly. To drop bombs you need to make a short, loud sound. Avoid the clouds! Be careful not to tear the vocal cords :)



Game page:
www.motiongames.net/shouter
