How a former command post becomes a data center

The use of former air raid shelters, bunkers and command posts for data centers is becoming popular in the world. Thus, these objects acquire a second life. Recently, we talked about the peaceful profession of a bomb shelter , in this article we will discuss the former command center of the Swiss Air Force (NATO), which is now used as an international data center, including by Russian providers.


Located deep in the mountains of Switzerland, the Deltalis data center is equipped with a multi-layered protection system while providing customers with a wide range of flexible and scalable services - from simple equipment placement to full managed services. This data center in Swiss Attinghausen occupies hundreds of square meters and meets the most stringent standards, it applies the solutions of leading vendors, it is possible to place powerful high-density equipment.

Deltalis is one of the most reliable data centers in the world. For large customers and customers with special security requirements, there are separate rooms and zones - in fact, a data center within the data center. The private zone can be separated by solid walls. In addition, customers can deploy a data center at their dedicated site with utilities that meets their specific requirements. For them, customized security solutions can be implemented, including badge readers, biometric and security systems, and intrusion detection systems.



The peculiarity of this data center is that it is located in a former military facility, which means it is very well protected. The use of former air raid shelters, bunkers and command posts for data centers seems to be becoming popular in Europe , in Russia , and in Ukraine . This unique data center has been accepted into the International Data Center Group (Alliance of Data Centers) and obtained the highest five-star status after passing the certification procedure of EuroCloud Europe.

Hidden deep in the Swiss Alps, the bunker was created to protect against nuclear explosions and foreign invasion, but was not useful for this purpose. Today it is used to protect digital data. The area for loading, unloading and temporary storage of equipment in the data center is separated by a grid.



This is the former command center of the Swiss Air Force (NATO). Security is the highest. The data center itself is hardly visible from the outside. Data Center protect doors weighing 3.5 tons. Moreover, the protection is complex: from radiation, electromagnetic radiation, earthquakes (there is a layer between the premises of the data center that absorbs possible shocks). Natural cooling - with water from a mountain lake, very effective. The bunker was built to use water from a mountain source and electricity from nearby hydroelectric power plants.



This bunker belonged to one of the largest command posts in Switzerland, but was decommissioned in 1950. Deltalis acquired it from the Swiss government in 2007 and opened a data center there in 2010. Massive doors protect against explosion, flooding, and radiation. Such an object, perhaps, can withstand even a nuclear strike or the use of modern electromagnetic weapons.



Labyrinths of the bunker are at a depth of hundreds of meters from the surface. This pipe is an air duct. The air conditioning system of the data center is tied to an underground lake, the pumps pump cold air and remove hot air.



Air pipes cooled by lake water. No chillers outside.



The bunker still has signs of the past days, when it belonged to the former strategic center, cells with radio signal blocking, once used for super-secret telephone calls, and faded maps of Europe and Switzerland with numerous moving flags. This control panel is the historical legacy of the NATO command center. A responsible general worked here and controlled the deployment and movement of troops.



The cooling system in the data center is dual-circuit with raised floor. The cooled air is supplied from under the raised floor to the closed cold corridor and through the perforated floor tiles and the perforated door of the racks enters the equipment, from there to the open hot corridor and is removed from the hall.



In the containment - the ranks of such closed racks. To get to the closed corridor, you need to bring a card to the scanner. On it you can go in one closed corridor, where there is a specific rack with the desired server. Everything else for access by outsiders is closed.
