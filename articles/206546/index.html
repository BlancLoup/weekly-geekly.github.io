SendVoice, or how to hear your visitor

The question of feedback for site owners is far from idle ... After all, the response from a live audience - visiting your resource (regardless of subject matter) and directly participating in its life - is worth a lot.

And now let us ask one more question: What is easier for you: WRITE or TALK?

It seems that the majority will answer it clearly: to speak! So what was the matter? Moreover, today there is a service SendVoice.ru.





The main function of the service is to send voice messages to the site owner. Messages are sent using a special widget that can be called through a plate on the site or through a normal link. If this does not fit, then embed code with the widget can be embedded in the site. The appearance of the plate and the text in the widget can be almost completely changed to suit your needs.

Messages can then be read, listened to and answered, both with ordinary text and voice mail through a convenient web-interface.



Why is this necessary if there are excellent scripts / services of various chat rooms? It is often psychologically difficult to speak with an unfamiliar interlocutor. SendVoice allows you to express your opinion without psychological stress, because an unfamiliar interlocutor just LISTEN at a distance. And the user always has a chance to listen to the recording and rewrite it, if something was not pleasant.

Who can use SendVoice?

  • The owner of the online store will be able to constantly receive feedback and have an idea about the quality of their goods, the work of the resource and the level of customer service.
  • A blogger / podcaster will receive an excellent ‚Äúlive connection‚Äù with his audience and will be able to make his resource even more relevant, taking into account all the wishes and requests of the targeted visitors.
  • The company's management will receive an additional bonus in the eyes of its potential customers, find for itself a new source of feedback on its work and will be able to objectively evaluate the work of its organization.
  • Sendvoice can be successfully used for educational purposes. For example, a teacher may both record and send his lectures to students, and receive answers from them.
