Foundation 3



The third version of the Foundation framework has been released. You probably heard about Twitter Bootsrap . After it, most frameworks look faded: the elements are smaller, the markup is even more illogical, a lot of bugs and nothing new. Foundation differs for the better:

  • The markup is more concise and logical.
  • Sharpened to work with a bunch of any devices. You can control exactly how the site will look at what screen settings. There is a very flexible adaptive mesh.
  • For everything, use `box-sizing: border-box`.
  • Good sets of buttons, molds, menus and small items.
  • Tabs, gallery and modal windows that work fine on all devices.
