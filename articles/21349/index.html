Website concept development

Project specifics


When developing a site, understanding the specifics of a project is the basis. Customers evaluate the project on the effects of the site and its service life. First of all, we try to determine the role of the site in the work of the customer.

We start with typical tasks:
  • formation of a company image;
  • attracting customers;
  • product promotion;
  • automation of business processes .

A set of tasks determines the specifics of the project. A site can be a grocery, like a promo site www.botaniq.ru , attract customers like www.meka.su , or automate processes like www.makroseuro.ru .
As a rule, sites solve several problems. For example, the site www.senat-cs.ru , supports the image of the company, attracts customers and applicants.
It is the effects of problem solving that are important to the customer.
