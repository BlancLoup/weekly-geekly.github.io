User's manual
1. Turn on Wi-Fi on your phone.
2. Connect the phone to the router (access point).
3. Download the application ‚ÄúAmaryllo‚Äù to install devices, install and run it.
4. Select the installation option (install a new camera, access from another device, install a new camera with a pre-configured network).

6. Connect the micro USB cable from the camera to the power supply. When the camera is connected, the blue LED will turn on. It is necessary to wait a little for its blinking to start the installation.
7. For models with remote control, it should take about 25 seconds before the start of the calibration process, when the camera reaches its extreme values ‚Äã‚Äãin the horizontal and vertical planes.
8. Select the required Wi-Fi network and enter the password from your Wi-Fi router.
9. The application in the device will generate a QR code.
Rotate the image of the QR code towards the camera and keep the screen parallel to the camera lens at a distance of 10 ... 30 cm (smartphone) or 20 ... 30 cm (tablet) until a short sound signal appears confirming the transfer of information between devices. The QR code contains information for connecting the camera to a Wi-Fi router.
The timer will show the time remaining to complete the installation. If the time has expired, you need to start the installation again.
If the QR scan failed, a question will appear: ‚ÄúDo you want to try again?‚Äù With the answer options:
- check information;
- repeat.
10. Two beeps will indicate that the installation of the camera is complete. At the same time, a continuously lit green LED indicates the correct connection via Wi-Fi.

Installation option with pre-configured network.
1. Connect the phone to the router.
2. Press and hold the WPS button on the router.
3. Within 60 seconds after pressing the button on the router, press and hold the WPS button on the camera for five seconds or more. When the green LED is blinking, it means that the camera is in WPS mode.
5. Then follow the instructions of the application.

Reboot
Connect the device to the network and wait until the calibration is completed.
Press and hold for at least five seconds the reset button located in the hole on the bottom of the case. All factory settings will be deleted and restored to factory settings.
Within 25 seconds, the camera will begin the calibration process, rotating around its own axis.

Temperature of use
Indoors, at temperatures from 0 to +35 degrees Celsius.

Indication



Blue LED.
Lit continuously - the camera is connected to power.
Blinks once a second - during a QR scan.
Blinks twice a second - the camera is used in viewing mode. That is, when someone is viewing the image from the camera, the blue LED starts to flash.
Flashing three times per second - the installation process was successful.

Green LED.
Lit continuously - the camera is connected to Wi-Fi and works correctly.
Blinks once a second - the camera is trying to connect to the Wi-Fi router.
