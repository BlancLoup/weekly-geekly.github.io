Bot lawyer won $ 3 million cases



18-year-old British IT-student Joshua Browder (Joshua Browder) in August last year opened the site donotpay.co.uk , which helps car owners automatically create and send an appeal to the receipt of the fine. On their own, people cannot do this, because the procedure is difficult for many, and the services of lawyers almost always exceed the amount of the fine.

In just six months, the bot lawyer helped to return fines of more than 2 million pounds ($ 3 million). The site was used by 86 thousand people, and almost 40% of the appeals were successful. The author of the project itself was very surprised that the bot is so popular.
