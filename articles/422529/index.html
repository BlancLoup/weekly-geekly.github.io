Specialists Group-IB , one of the leading international companies for the prevention and investigation of cybercrime and fraud using high technology, prepared a two-month autumn course on the vulnerability of mobile applications. Why did we decide to create this course?


Have you ever thought that the question of your privacy depends on a small startup - a mobile application developer, where quite ordinary people work who can make a mistake at any moment and all your photos of cats are (and not only they!) In the open spaces the internet?

You will learn:
