From the camp of Internet addicts made a massive escape

On Habr√©, the Chinese labor camp has already been discussed for the treatment of Internet dependent youth. Strict discipline (getting up at 5 in the morning, turning off the light at 21.30), camouflage clothing, two hours of compulsory exercise, psychological preparation, lessons of philosophy and calligraphy under the supervision of army instructors - all this effectively helps get rid of the painful computer dependence of young geeks.

However, several patients in the clinic were fed up with such a life and they escaped . 14 patients aged from 15 to 22 years tied their instructor to the bed and fled to the nearest town, where they called a taxi. However, they failed to get to their favorite computers and game consoles: according to Chinese media, they did not have money to travel and the taxi driver brought the patients to the police (in general, the attempt of 14 people to get into one car itself is suspicious) .

Now adolescents will have to be beaten by their parents, who paid 18,000 yuan (about $ 2,635) for a six-month course of treatment. And what the instructor will do with them in the camp is scary.
