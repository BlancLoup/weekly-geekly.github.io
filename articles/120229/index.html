Tariff "For Life" from Megaphone is over!

A few weeks ago, I wrote about how Megaphone in every way refused to change the tariff. I have left a lot of interesting comments. But everything turned out to be very simple. In one of the last comments, it was suggested to write a complaint appeal to the North-West branch, and from there my letter would be sent to my region. Unfortunately, my letter apparently did not forward anywhere. I found a statement on the tariff change on the site, filled it out, printed out the complaint appeal and went to the service center. I handed the girl a statement about changing the tariff with the words that since you did not follow this statement, I already wrote a complaint. The girl mumbling that this tariff does not change, in two minutes everything changed and even advised another, more favorable tariff for me.

The result is this, if you are somewhere "football" - please contact in writing.
