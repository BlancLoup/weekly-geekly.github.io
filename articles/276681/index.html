List of technical and IT conferences 2016. Part # 1



It's been almost a month and a half of 2016. I think that this time, you, just like the author of this note, spent in military labors. In spite of the enormous load, I would not like to go by Bulat Okudzhava, and once again looking away from the monitor, see on the calendar 1st January 2017. A natural question arises - how to maximize the benefit, not only work the remaining 10.5 months of the year of the Monkey , but also to learn about the latest trends to the maximum, having met interesting people at the same time.

In carrying out this task, a small study was conducted on the most interesting, in my opinion, interesting conferences that you should pay attention to and, if possible, attend from March to May 2016.
