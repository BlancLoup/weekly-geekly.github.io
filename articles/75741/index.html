PR Vkontakte or competition results

In continuation of the topic, the new VKontakte contest is a brazen deception .

As many have noticed, recently one of the largest soc. CIS networks - VKontakte - has entered the international arena .

The new owners of vk.com decided to promote this event by launching a spam competition: who will most of all invite friends who will not only register with the CC, but also attach their account to the mobile phone number. With one limitation: invited from Russia, Ukraine and Belarus are not considered - the competition is aimed at foreign audiences.

So, let's see the results a few hours before the end of the competition:



A simple calculation: the top 30 registered about 30,000. We give a discount on the unfinished registrations, invitations removed from the competition, and also that which was not included in the top 30 - a total of 35,000-40000 reg. Accordingly, for the entire time of the contest PR social. network saw about hundreds of millions of users.

Was it appropriate?

15 Sony Ericsson Aino smartphones = 15 * 16990 = 254850
Total: 869300. Taking into account discounts - 550000-600000.

Result: public relations in a global audience for 100 million people for half a million rubles. IMHO, very good.
