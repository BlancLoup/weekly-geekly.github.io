July 18 - Open Call, the first start of GDDru!


Hello!
We have great news! "Pre-start" GDD11 July 18th!

We invite you to participate in Open Call for Google Developer Day 2011!

Open Call is two mini-contests organized by Google in anticipation of 8 GDD events around the world:
1) Android ADK - July 18
2) HTML5 - 1 August.

At each competition the ten best works for the GDDru zone will be selected. The authors of these projects will receive invitations to GDDru and the opportunity to personally demonstrate their developments to GDD participants and key Google specialists.
Hurry up to learn all the basic (and most importantly - the rules of participation!) On the official website of Open Call for Google Developer Day 2011 !
