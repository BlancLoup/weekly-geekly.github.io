FAQ on the purchase and activation of the PSN Card for the American PSN Store



In this article I will talk in detail about how to purchase and activate the PSN Card.

PSN Card will allow you to pay for purchases in the American Store, regardless of your place of residence.

To purchase a PSN Card, we will need an electronic currency (any suitable, but wmz is better) or a plastic card (credit and debit cards will not matter) with the possibility of payment via the Internet.

Further points:

If you have wmz or any other electronic currency
If you have wmz, go to Google, type in the PSN Card search (or related request) and choose the online store to your liking.

If you have a credit card or debit card

After registering a credit card in paypal, go to ebay.com and buy a Psn Card
Monaule to buy PSN Card via Ebay.

Regardless of the method of purchasing a PSN Card (by a plastic card through ebay or wmz via the online store), cards are always sold at a premium, so before buying, look carefully at all the offers and choose the most profitable.

For comparison
Buying a PSN card with a nominal value of $ 20 in an online store for wmz, I gave 27.45 wmz (wmz equivalent of $)

Paying a PSN card with an ebay credit card for a $ 20 card gives away $ 23

PSN card come in different denominations. The greater the value of the card, the less we lose on the purchase due to margins.
