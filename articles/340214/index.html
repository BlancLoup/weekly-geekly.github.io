Preview RamblerFront & # 3



The third external RamblerFront & meetup will be held on October 26 at the Attic of Rambler & Co, where our employees will share their application knowledge in the field of frontend-development.

Topics for presentations:

1. How to file 80 React forms and sometimes to be at home - Ilya Sychev (senior client software engineer, Rambler & Co advertising technology team)
A report on what JsonSchema is and how to use it to validate data. Let us tell you how you can quickly and efficiently build a React form of any complexity and what alternatives to the Redux-form are.

2. Data caching using Service Worker - Artem Danshin (Senior Client Application Development Engineer, Rambler & Co, Media Project Development and Design Department)
How well do you know the Service Worker API? And how to use it for data caching? In this story we will try to understand how Service Worker can interact with requests, what and how to cache, and also how all this will help our sites with you to load much faster.

3. Designing the Rambler design system: translation difficulties - Aleksandra Yermolenko (art director for Rambler /, Rambler & Co)
Building a healthy and long-term relationship of design and development on the example of creating a design system of Rambler.
