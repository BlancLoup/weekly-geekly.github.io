Who can participate?
A student, graduate student or team from a university in Russia, the CIS and the Baltic countries - a member of InterSystems Campus. You can find your college here , add college to the program here .

Prize fund
The cash prize is paid to the three best teams of 60, 40 and 20 thousand rubles, respectively.

Project Topics
The competition offered options for those whose implementation is most interesting to the company. It is also possible to apply for a free topic.

What projects are winning?

The announced projects are evaluated by engineers at InterSystems. Criteria for evaluation:
