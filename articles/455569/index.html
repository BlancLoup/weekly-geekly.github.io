Welcome to Tarantool Conference June 17


On June 17, the second annual open Tarantool Conference or shortly T + Conf will be held at the Mail.ru Group's Moscow office - a conference not only about Tarantool itself, but generally about using in-memory computing, Tarantool, cooperative multitasking and Lua to develop high-load fault-tolerant services. Preliminary reports are sorted by topic: microservice architecture, grid architecture, tutorials, enterprise. And consequently, they will be interesting not only for experienced developers, but also, on the one hand, for beginners, and on the other hand, for architects in the enterprise sector. Details can be found under the cut.


List of reports:


  • SWIM - cluster construction protocol , Vladislav Shpileva
  • CI / CD applications on Tarantool: from an empty repository to production , Konstantin Nazarov
  • How to ensure omnicanality with ChannelControl event handling platform , Alexey Kuzin
  • How are we going to weave a relational network using Tarantool , Maxim Koshkaryov
  • Tarantool Roadmap , Konstantin Osipov
  • Why did we fork vshard , Mikhail Khruzin
  • Why Tarantool Enterprise , Oleg Ivlev
  • Tarantool in Aeroflot, or MDM on the fly , Rustam Kildiev, Nikolay Shevtsov
  • Vertical scaling of Tarantool using Intel Optane , Georgy Kirichenko
  • Tarantool caches and replication from Oracle , Alexander Deulin
  • What is useful Tarantool Enterprise , Yaroslav Dynnikov
  • Replication from Oracle to GreenPlum with a delay of less than 4 hours , Alexey Ryabov
  • How we did the core of Alfa-Bank's investment business based on Tarantool , Vladimir Drynkin
  • Approaches to building applications on Tarantool , Vladimir Perepelitsa
  • Not like everyone else: the most vicious practices of using Tarantool , Vladislav Zaitsev
  • Tarantool: now with SQL , Kirill Yukhin

Detailed abstracts of the reports can be found at the link . In addition to the speeches themselves, you will find useful handouts, delicious coffee breaks, and after the conference you will be able to watch videos of the reports you liked the most.


Like a year ago, the reports will begin at 10:00 and end at 19:00.


Address: Mail.ru Group office, Leningradsky pr., 39, p. 79.
Participation in the conference is free, but you must register for it.
