12 useful government services for entrepreneurs



In the work on the site ‚Äú I love IP ‚Äù we use various services that make life easier for entrepreneurs. In this article, we have collected the most useful public services for entrepreneurs. They will help you register a business, pay taxes and insurance premiums, submit reports or find out about possible fines.


IP registration


1. Find out TIN . With this service you can enter your passport data and find out the TIN number. If you have an TIN, then it must be indicated when registering the IP. If the TIN does not exist yet, then it is not necessary to do it in advance. You will receive it simultaneously with the IP registration documents.


2. Get a TIN certificate . In spite of the fact that for registration of an entrepreneur, the TIN number is sufficient, you will need the certificate itself to open a current account With this service, you can fill out an application for a TIN certificate and send it to the tax. The service will also automatically generate a receipt for payment of state duty for re-issuance of a certificate (300 rubles).


3. Apply for registration of IP . This service will help you register IP via the Internet. To do this, you must enter your passport data and select OKVED codes. The service will automatically generate all documents, including a receipt for payment of state duty (800 rubles). Detailed instructions on how to apply for registration of IP via the Internet, read on our website .


4. Find out the registering authority . You can register individual entrepreneurs in a special tax office, which has such functions. It may not coincide with your local tax. Find out exactly where you need to submit documents for registration of IP, you can use this service. Enter your address and click Next in the lower right corner. On the next page, find the details of the registering authority, which is entrusted with the registration of IP . It is in this tax need to contact on all issues related to registration.


5. Get an extract from the EGRIP . With this service, you can get an extract for any legal entity or individual entrepreneur in electronic form. To do this, it is enough to know its number OGRN (OGRNP) or TIN.


Insurance premiums


6. Find out the department of the FIU . After registration of the IP registration in the FIU is carried out automatically. This service will help you to know your branch of the FIU. At the top of the page, select your region. Then the Insured ‚Üí Service: Reception of policyholders for the calculation and payment of insurance premiums ‚Üí Service Territory: your city and district.


7. Find out the registration number of the FIU . After registration of the IP, you should receive a notification from the FIU with your registration number by mail. You will need this number to pay compulsory insurance premiums. Registration number can be found on the TIN site of the Pension Fund for Moscow , for St. Petersburg . If you live in another region, enter here the first three digits of your department and on the next page, click Forgot your registration number of the FIU? .


8. Find out the details of the FIU, form a receipt for the payment of insurance contributions to the FIU . The service will help you find out the details of your branch of the PFR and form a receipt for paying insurance premiums. Detailed instructions on how to use the service, read on our website .


To pay insurance premiums, you will also need the OKTMO code. How to find it, read below.


Calculate insurance premiums PI . On our website there is a calculator of insurance premiums . With the help of a calculator, it is possible to calculate the compulsory insurance contributions of an individual entrepreneur to the FIU and to the Federal Medical Insurance Fund in a fixed amount, as well as an additional 1% to the FIU.


Taxes


Find out the tax details, OKTMO code . With this service you can find out the details of your tax inspection. Enter your address and you will learn the code of the Inspection of Federal Tax Service and the code of OKTMO (municipal formation) to which it relates. To find out the details of the IFTS, click Next in the lower right corner and on the next page find the Payment Details .


9. Generate a receipt for tax payment . This service will help you generate a receipt for the payment of tax on the USN (or other taxes), as well as pay for them via the Internet. Detailed instructions on how to use the service, read on our website . It is important to remember that you can pay taxes online only with your own card.


10. Calculate the cost of the patent . Another useful service from the FTS, with which you can calculate the cost of a patent for different types of activities. Select the period of use of the patent (from 1 to 12 months within a calendar year), your region and municipality. Next, indicate the type of activity that you plan to engage in. The service will calculate the cost of a patent for a specified period, and you can compare what is more profitable for you, apply the simplified tax system or switch to a patent.


Statistics


11. Get a letter from the statistics . Registration in the statistics bodies is also carried out automatically after the registration of the IP. With the help of this service you will be able to receive an email with statistics codes, as well as a list of forms that you need to submit.


Penalties and fines


12. Learn about account blocking . This service of the Federal Tax Service of Russia is designed for banks, but it can also be useful for entrepreneurs. With it, you can find out if your current account is blocked. To do this, enter your TIN and BIC of any bank (for example, Sberbank 044525225). If you have valid decisions to suspend account transactions, information about this will be displayed in the service.


An account can be blocked if there is a delay in filing a declaration for more than 10 business days. How to fill out and submit a declaration on the USN read on our website .


( Optional ) Calculate tax and insurance premiums . This service will help you calculate penalties for delayed payment of taxes and insurance premiums. Select the type of tax (or contribution), year and period. Next, specify the date of actual payment and the amount owed.


( Optional ) Calculate the penalty for failure to submit a tax return . This service will help calculate the penalty if you do not submit your tax return in time. The minimum penalty (including for the non-delivery of a zero declaration) is 1000 rubles. To calculate the amount of the fine, select the type of report, year and period. Next, specify the actual date of delivery of the declaration and the amount of unpaid tax.
