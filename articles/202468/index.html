vbulletin.com hacked

On November 15th, a warning appeared on the vbulletin support site to change the password. This is due to the fact that the forum was subjected to a hacker attack. Personal data leakage, including passwords, is not excluded (judging by the screenshots, I can say that the database has completely leaked).



Yesterday the team Inj3ct0r claimed responsibility for this hacking. In addition, they reported that they had discovered a vulnerability that allows to execute arbitrary code on the forums running vBulletin 4.xx and vBulletin 5.xx It is also alleged that there is a ready exploit that allows you to download the web shell. The cost of this exploit is estimated at $ 7,000. Anyone with this amount can easily purchase it , which means that in the near future there will most likely be a new series of hacks.
