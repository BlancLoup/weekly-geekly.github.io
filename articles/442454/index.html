Magic Leap plans to add digital layers to the real world



Representatives of the startup Magic Leap confirmed their intention to begin the implementation of the Magicverse project, which was first announced in October last year. It represents the creation of several digital layers, which in the form of augmented reality would be added to the real world.

According to the developers , they plan to blur the boundary between the real and digital world as much as possible. Elements of the digital layers will be all real objects - from the premises in any building to the whole country.

Understanding the concept itself and its adaptation, as the authors of the idea believe, will take some time, since everything here is not easy.

Real objects will be displayed in several AR layers. One of them can show entertainment zones, the other one can be related to health care and health, the third one can show the transport infrastructure of the region.

On the basis of such a system, you can create a lot of new type of applications that will make life more comfortable, improve work efficiency, and allow you to relax better.

Well, access to the layers can be obtained using a headset - not only the one that is developed by the company itself, but many others.

‚ÄúMagicverse will accelerate and increase economic processes in communities around the world, erase the boundaries of space and time, provide communication and work using tools and methods that have not happened before,‚Äù says the release of Magic Leap.

True, the existence of such a system requires a new infrastructure, including high-speed communication lines, data warehouses, etc. Communication systems are now being improved, as 5g is gradually penetrating the real world. And the faster the fifth generation networks will spread, the more real the Magicverse idea will become.



The problem of the digital world can also be a question of personal data - because if you transfer such a large amount of data to a figure, then sooner or later there will be a conflict between the need to ensure privacy and the need for the further development of the system. However, Magic Leap is already working on both the policy and the architecture of such a system.

The company's team says it wants to create a positive, decentralized infrastructure, where people and businesses safely store their data. But for this you need to develop leverage, counteracting the possibility of abuse of personal data. Participants in the digital universe will be able to fully control their own information.

Starting this year, the company began to attract new partners to work. The team is developing tools, hardware, software, architecture, and visions for the whole concept.
