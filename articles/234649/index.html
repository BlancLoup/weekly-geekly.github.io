The deadline for applications for the Stepic Challenge contest has been extended until September 20

August 25 was the last day for accepting applications for the open online courses Stepic Challenge project competition . Since the competition for deadlines unsuccessfully coincided with the summer vacation period, in recent days we received many requests from teachers and universities to postpone the deadline a bit. We decided to extend the acceptance of applications until September 20 inclusive, but for now we can sum up the preliminary results.



94 applications were submitted to the competition, 66 of them in Russian, 28 in English. The majority of applications, about 70%, came from university professors, about 20% from applications from school teachers, and another 10% from students and independent authors. It's nice that there are applications from different regions and cities of Russia - Moscow, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Tomsk, Voronezh, Kazan, Nizhny Novgorod, Arkhangelsk, Yekaterinburg and others.

How will we build work with applications further?


Tomorrow we will start viewing the submitted applications. We will post the best on the contest website for open voting, the results of which will be taken into account by the jury in the selection in the second round. If possible, authors of applications will give personal recommendations on how to improve them. Until September 20, they will have a chance to refine the materials and go to the second round. We will work with newly submitted applications in the same way. According to the new plan, the winners of the contest will be announced on October 15.
