D59 - the first Perm conference of software developers

For the first time in many years, a conference for software developers will be held in Perm - D59 !



Only the best IT companies and the best developers, as well as the stars of the Perm IT scene, total a maximum of 100 people by pre-registration.

Bright, lively reports of "half marathon" for a maximum of 21 minutes + 9 minutes to questions and answers.

No advertising, propaganda and other nonsense, talking only about our products and projects, sharing our experience, anyone, the main thing is to be interesting and useful to a wide range, and preferably fresh, not foul.

Interesting details, technical details, the choice of frameworks, architecture and other things that developers love. Negative experience is also important as a positive one.

Backstage, informal communication. Intensive exchange of experience. Saturday, April 27, 10 reports of 30 minutes + break + final IT battle. And of course after-party in one of the bars of the city.

At the conference 4 tracks of reports will be presented: about people, about technologies, bloody enterprises and interfaces. And there will be very interesting speakers that you have not seen anywhere else. We will be glad to see participants not only from Perm, but also from other cities. The cost of participation in the conference - 1000 rubles.
