Smart home and television



The volume of the smart home market in 2014 and 2020 in billions of dollars according to Statista.com

The concept of a smart home can dramatically change our views on the organization of life in a modern apartment or cottage. To date, about a dozen home automation technologies are available, and a modern smart home integrates all engineering systems, providing control over heating, electricity, air conditioning, security systems and water supply, creating a comfortable environment for humans.

The smart home market is constantly growing and developing. According to forecasts of Statista.com, the value of the global smart home market will grow to $ 58 billion. Of this amount, slightly less than $ 10 billion will be the share of the home entertainment market. As for Russia, in 2016 the Russian market of smart home entertainment systems will be estimated at $ 16.6 million, which at an average annual growth rate of 43.41% will give $ 70.2 million in 2020.

Entertainment multimedia systems have long been part of our lives. Home theater is a common phenomenon and can be found in almost every apartment. Other home cinema includes one or more sources of sound and images, a speaker system, a plasma panel, an LCD TV or a projector with a screen.

By making the cinema system part of a smart home, you get the opportunity to control all this equipment from a single console or touch pad. As the latter can easily be a smartphone. To do this, you just need to install a special application .

In this case, the TV will act as a kind of information station.

In this regard, Smart TV technology has gained great popularity, which is a popular destination for startups in the field of TV, as it allows to implement many innovative ideas in a new large market. Often a component of a smart home is a multi-room system that allows you to transmit video or audio from a source to several TVs or speakers located in different rooms. In this case, the devices in the room are combined into a zone and get their own individual set of custom playback options. The system is controlled using the remote control or touch panels.

The system allows you to receive and distribute on-air or satellite TV throughout the premises, as well as distribute other digital data streams throughout the home. Smart home makes rational use of satellite and cable television, allowing the display of several channels on televisions located in different rooms.
