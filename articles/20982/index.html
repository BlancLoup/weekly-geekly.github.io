MTI Micro launches digital SLR fuel cell

MTI MicroFuel Cells presented a prototype power source for digital cameras to the public at the Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Expo in Tokyo. This power source is made in the form of a fuel stick module - ‚Äúappendage‚Äù, which is attached to the camera and provides additional operating time.
