Hello!
November 27 in Minsk will host the international marketing conference ROMI Con .

What is ROMI Con?

ROMI Con is an open discussion platform created for the exchange of experience among the professional community of marketers. At the conference, you will find 3 streams of reports in which experts from Wargaming, Celemi, IKRA and SplitMetrics will hold thematic lectures on PR, advertising project management, monetization, design and video creation, event marketing, and community work.
For 17 years, Wargaming has created legendary game projects known throughout the world. During this time, the company has accumulated vast experience in the field of marketing, and now it's time to share it. If you are interested in Wargaming expertise in attracting, involving and retaining customers - welcome to ROMI Con.

Details you will find under the cut.



Format and participation
The ROMI Con format involves informal dialogue and networking. We welcome the two-way exchange of ideas and believe that the discussion of professionals will help find many promising joint solutions.
Participation in the conference is free, but it requires prior registration .

Speakers
At ROMI Con, presentations will be made by leading experts from Wargaming, Celemi, IKRA and SplitMetrics. A detailed program can be found on the conference website.



When and where?
Date: November 27, 2015
Beginning: 10:00
Location: Belarus, Minsk. Hotel Renaissance, Dzerzhinsky Avenue 1E

We remind you that participation is required prior registration .

See you at ROMI Con!
