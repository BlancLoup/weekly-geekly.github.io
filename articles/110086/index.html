Thanks AdWords for the greeting

AdWords celebrates its anniversary - 10 years.
As a gift, they sent their advertisers an excellent thank-you video.
Individual for each advertiser!
Watch carefully!

If you only at the end noticed the address of the site on the moon, then you missed something.


The AdWords team has attracted the best scientists to figure out how to thank their publishers (advertisers).
But unfortunately, not everything goes smoothly.

At first, the bees on the remote control are trying to gather in the sky in the form of the address of your site, but something goes wrong and its barely noticeable.
Then a giant pyramid of 10 million domino seeds after the fall reveals the address of your site, but even here everything is not going smoothly and a helicopter blows it off.
And finally, at the end, almost a victory, for a few seconds a giant laser projects the address of your site to the moon for a few seconds.

Original is not it?
