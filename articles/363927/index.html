Lifetrak R450: What's Inside a Smart Fitness Watch?


Three options for device design

Lifetrak company produces a number of fitness trackers, some of which are, in fact, sports fitness watches. Today we will analyze the most advanced model of the device from Lifetrak - Lifetrak r450.

The device is a fitness tracker with additional functionality for displaying notifications from a paired mobile device. SMS, incoming call, e-mail - Lifetrak r450 will alert the user about the event. That is why we called the gadget smart fitness hours.

In fact, the functions here are much more than just displaying notifications:
  • Sleep quality tracking and smart alarm - Lifetrak r450 wakes the wearer during the onset of the sleep phase, when it's easiest to wake up. Yes, and determine what the problem with sleep, the device also helps;
  • High precision heart rate monitor - works during the most active workout;
  • Step counter (there is a difference between running, walking and jumping);
  • Waterproof case allows you to take a shower, swim and dive without removing the gadget;
  • Calorie counter - this will not surprise anyone, but Lifetrak r450 counts calories burned quite accurately;
  • Counting the time the person stays in the light - if you are constantly in a room without normal lighting, the clock will remind you that it's time to take a walk;
  • Plus, the shock-resistant case protects the device from damage during a very active workout.



This is what Lifetrak r450 looks like.
