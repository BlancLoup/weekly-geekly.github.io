Images with shadows


Adding an internal shadow to the image is a bit more complicated than to a normal div . For starters, here is the usual image code:

It is logical to assume that you can add a shadow like this:

But the shadow is not visible:



There are several ways to solve this problem, each of which has its pros and cons. Consider two of them. The first is to wrap the picture in a regular div :
