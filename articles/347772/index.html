MentorHack: Tinder for Mentor Search, AI Boss and a bit of HRTech



From 13 to 15 February in Moscow will be held MentorHack - hackathon to create services for mentoring in the corporate environment, entrepreneurship and education. We invite everyone with experience in development, machine learning, product management or HR and the desire to receive prizes and interesting data from partners.
The total prize fund is 1 300 000 rubles.

Under the cut - details of participation and examples of ideas for projects: from starting services for the selection of Facebook and LinkedIn mentors to AI Boss concepts.

How to participate


  • Be over 16 and have experience in development, machine learning, product management or HR.
  • Be able to describe your experiences and ideas in the application .
  • Assemble a team or be ready to find it on the first day. The application must be submitted to each member of the team.
  • Receive an invitation from the organizers and confirm participation until February 10 inclusive.
  • Add to the chat and appear on February 13 to open with your computer.

Prizes


Teams that successfully pass two checkpoints will present projects of a jury of representatives of the top management of large corporations and will be able to compete for prizes:

  • The total prize fund is 1 300 000 rubles.
  • Special partners from prizes and gifts.
  • The opportunity to conclude contracts with partner companies of the forum.

Examples of ideas for projects


Participants are encouraged to develop services for the selection of mentors, tracking efficiency and optimization of mentoring processes. A variety of ideas are welcome, below is a selection of examples.

To solve such problems, we will need methods of machine learning and digital profiles with summaries, descriptions, linking to social networks and food photos from the insta, data on the number of hours spent in Fb and Hearthstone behind a working computer - everything we find. By the way, the compilation of this profile and the aggregation of information is also a fairly large-scale field for projects that can be worked on within the framework of the hackathon.

Organizers and partners will help us with the data - corporations and well-known job search services (SuperJob, HeadHunter) and employees will set the tasks for the participants, which we will announce later. Be sure to use all sorts of open data, for example, with GitHub.
