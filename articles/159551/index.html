Himself Santa Claus


Darius Kazemi ( Darius Kazemi ), a programmer from the United States decided to diversify his life with gifts ... to himself. But since in this case the zest is lost in the form of a surprise from an unknown gift, he decided to buy presents using a handwritten script, calling it Amazon Random Shopper .
As the name implies, after launch, the program takes a random word from the Wordnik API and searches Amazon for products for this keyword. If it is a book, CD or DVD and costs less than the budget it has laid, then the program buys this product. The program works in a cycle until it spends the entire budget or does not buy a certain number of gifts. The fact that he bought the script, he finds out only after receiving the goods by mail.
