VK Mobile Challenge 2018

VK holds VK Mobile Challenge - an online contest among developers for Android and iOS platforms. Show your skills - and fight for the prize fund, which will be from 1 to 2 million rubles. Participants with the best results will get a chance to join the VKontakte Team.



The competition will begin with an online tour, which takes 90 minutes. It consists of a test of knowledge of the Android or iOS platform, several algorithmic tasks and tasks with a free form of response - you need to solve a real problem that may arise in any mobile application.


Participants who score high points will pass to the second stage. They will have to solve a grocery problem designed for several days of work.
