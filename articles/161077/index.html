Design Camp - the first Microsoft design conference in Russia + Winter Design School for Windows 8



December 13th,
Digital October, Moscow


Design Camp is a professional Microsoft conference held with the support of the British Higher School of Design and dedicated to the design and engineering of modern applications.

Registration : events.techdays.ru/descamp/2012-12


Why do we do it?


Here is a simple answer: we just looked at what was happening around and decided that it was time to hold a conference for designers.

The theme of design and, in particular, the design of applications for us is not new. In fact, for more than a year in almost all of our conferences and other events dedicated to the development of applications for Windows Phone and Windows 8, we have inserted at least one design session. Quality application design for our platforms is undoubtedly a key factor in the success of our platforms. Like all of you, we want applications to be better and more satisfied users.

Today we see that it is time to go ahead and do something special, expanding the professional audience involved and the subject of the event. By organizing Design Camp, we focus on designers, designers and usabilityists, project managers and developers who create modern applications.

What will happen?


We and the invited experts (by the way, they are in the majority) will not only talk about Microsoft platforms. We look wider and we see that today it is important not only to study specific application design cases and learn the best practices, but also to see trends, take a fresh look at the processes taking place in the design industry, not forgetting, of course, the basics - those ideas and transformations that are behind the new design of Microsoft.

Especially for you, we have invited experts from Mail.ru Group, Yandex, Usabilitylab, UIDesign, Parcsis and leading teachers of the British Higher School of Design. A special guest is Yevgeny Gavrilov from the team of Windows Phone Design (Microsoft), who will talk about how the pictures turn into life and about the experience of their design integration team.

Registration : events.techdays.ru/descamp/2012-12

And further! Training!


On the weekends after the conference, we will organize the Winter Design School for Windows 8. This is a two-day free event sponsored by ITMozg.

Not all will fall, you can apply for participation here: msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=1032537874&Culture=ru-RU&community=0
