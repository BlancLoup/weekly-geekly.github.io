CodeCamp 2009 (Kiev), March 28-29

I am pleased to announce that CodeCamp will be held in Kiev on March 28-29. The event is free, admission is free



CodeSamp 2009 is a two-day all-Ukrainian conference on the topic of modern software development technologies and information security. The event will combine both the traditional format of the conference and the ‚Äúnon-conference‚Äù. This means that part of the reports will be free for participants to speak (by prior registration). Thus, the guests of the conference will be able to hear both recognized experts and specialists in specific areas, which will cover the most topics.



During the conference, 3 sessions of sessions (reports, presentations, workshops) on the following topics will be held simultaneously:

  • software development technologies
  • project structure development process
  • use of API and services for software development
  • team projects
  • outsourcing
  • network and software security
  • corporate security
  • vulnerability risk analysis
  • security problems of new technologies
  • web project security


The conference will include 2 special events:
InnovationDay - on Day One, participants will be able to present their innovative ideas and projects. The opportunity to attract new people to your own projects, show your ideas to a wide audience and find potential investors.
Sapka Developer Competition - competition for programmers, held from March 13 to 20 from the organizational partner Stanfy . The award ceremony will be held at the end of Day Two. ( http://stanfy.com.ua/contest/ )
