In general, the body is well recharged in the morning, of course, eggs. There are few basic recipes here, but their variations do not have a number.

Eggs can be cooked

We take 1-2-3 eggs, put them in a dipper, put them on a fire, the water boils, wait 2-3 minutes (depending on the rate of water heating by your stove) - we get soft-boiled eggs when the yolk is liquid; waiting for 4-5 minutes - get the eggs "in the bag" - part of the yolk is liquid, but the bulk is already curled; wait 10 minutes - get hard boiled eggs - an ingredient in many salads and just a dish that can be eaten with mayonnaise or ketchup. Upon reaching the desired time, the scoop with the eggs - under cold water to cool down faster. And you can eat.

Here are some tips: do not trust your ability to detect time with the power of thought, buy a kitchen timer or put a reminder on the phone - it helps a lot. To speed up the process (this advice applies generally to all cooking that requires boiling water) you can boil water in an electric kettle, and then pour it into a dipper or saucepan, but it is better not to wait for boiling in the kettle, as double boiling water can already cause to the deposition in it of all filth.

By the way, you can buy an egg cooker (500-1000 rubles), but this is if you really like eggs, and it will gather dust on the shelf until the big holidays, when you need 6-8 pieces to cook right away.

By the way, you can make a ‚Äúrestaurant serving‚Äù type - poached eggs that are boiled without shell in boiling water: boil water, reduce heat, break the shell and pour the egg into a bowl, and from the bowl gently into boiling water. Very cool and tasty dish. The main difference - the eggs are whipped.
