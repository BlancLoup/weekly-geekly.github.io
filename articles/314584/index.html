Do I need book tips for aspiring entrepreneurs?


Tom Peters is one of the most famous business gurus, whose books have sold millions of copies. Peters' management theories are based on his unique consulting experience. He spawned not only many imitators, but also a new category in literature - books about ‚Äúsuccessful success‚Äù. The leitmotif of such works is the existence of special secrets / principles / rules / lifehacks of achieving success in any chosen field.

The public perceives such books with a bang, because it is so tempting to get a magic guide that is guaranteed to lead to the top. However, more serious authors, for example, professors of American universities, harshly criticized both Peters himself and his followers.

Not that success in business was unattainable, but simply the principles of its achievement, which are the same for everyone, do not exist, they say. That is why management gurus do not know how to distinguish real success from simple luck, all the more they are not able to teach him. A negative example of the results of third-party consulting can serve as Peters himself - most of his corporate clients have long since left the pedestal.


This is, so to speak, a long introduction to the fact that business books should NOT be read. Cafe / Bar / Pizzeria


This book is for those who want to open their cafe, bar or pizzeria. The catering industry is traditionally popular with start-ups, and it may seem like a fairly simple direction from the outside. Organizing the work of mass segment establishments is really easier than a restaurant, but even here there are plenty of problems.


The biggest difficulty in opening a food service point is obtaining permits from SES and fire inspection. To get the go-ahead from these services, it is necessary to prepare a project with a plan for placing equipment, linking it to water supply systems and electricity, and develop and agree on a production control program.


The selected room should not only meet the requirements of services, but also be in a place with good traffic. We told where to find such a place, how to calculate the potential number of clients and find out about the earnings of competitors.


How much and what kind of dishes you need for a cafe? Where to find inexpensive equipment? Why is it impossible to play music at a public catering point for visitors even from a purchased license disc? How to analyze the menu for profitability? What are the cafe performance indicators? How much money is needed to open the most modest catering point and when will it pay off? Learn about this in our book.


Beauty saloon


The market for hairdressing and beauty services in Russia is considered one of the fastest growing and having good potential for further growth. Moreover, its saturation is still far away. Marketers believe that in comparison with European and American countries, the competition between salons is 3-5 times lower.


Beauty salons are conventionally divided into three classes: economy, business and luxury. The more expensive the salon, the more costs will be required to open it. In the book, we calculated how much to invest in an economy class cabin and what profitability of services you can count on.


The essential income item of the salon is the sale of professional cosmetics, home care products and appliances. But here it is important to know about which taxation system you need to choose for the salon, so that the tax burden is not excessive, and you do not have to transfer substantial sums to the budget.


Often our users ask a question about licensing activities in the field of beauty services. Indeed, in the implementation of procedures that are classified as medical, a license is obligatory, but services such as haircut, manicure, makeup are not licensed. We also talked about the sources of loss of income in the salon and how automated computer systems will help organize work with clients.

How to form the price for your services and how to sell a subscription for legal services? Show the customer its benefits, and you will not have to persuade him.
