Junimotion - do it yourself robot



As you know, a man from a boy is distinguished by the value of his toys. Please: Junimotion robot. It costs four thousand dollars and is sold as a set of parts that need to be collected. The output will turn out 40-centimeter guy weighing almost three kilo. The robot, mainly consists of LEDs, audio and gyro sensors. When assembling, much less cables are used than usual, therefore, if you assemble everything correctly, you will get not a flimsy under-suit, but a hefty old one.

The best part is that it is controlled via Bluetooth. Over time, when a special program appears, it can be monitored from a mobile phone. Among the shortcomings it can be noted that the battery lasts only 30 minutes.
