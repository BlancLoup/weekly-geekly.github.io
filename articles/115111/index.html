Opera launched Opera Mobile Store



Opera announced the launch of a new store for mobile applications Opera Mobile Store .

Opera Mobile Store uses Appia technology. It presents free and paid apps for devices on Java, Symbian, BlackBerry and Android platforms.

The store is available in more than 200 countries for users of Opera and other mobile browsers. The application catalog is personalized for each user based on his operating system, local language and currency.

The company also launched a portal for developers who want to publish their applications in the store.
