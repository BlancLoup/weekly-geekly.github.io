Top 10 universities in Europe to study IT



The recruitment company PageGroup claims that the programmer is the most sought-after specialty in the world; 24 countries are waiting for professionals in this field. The competition among programmers, however, is also great, so for successful work you need constant improvement of skills. Studying at a top European university is suitable for this purpose, and it is possible to increase the level of knowledge there with the material support of the educational institution.

The StudyQA team will be happy to tell which universities are suitable for this purpose. In our today's selection of collected 10 best European universities for IT-specialists with scholarships.

The selection is made based on the Top Universities 2017 rating .

University of Cambridge (UK)

Let's start the selection with the University of Cambridge - the best educational institution for the study of information technology in Europe. By the way, last year he finished fourth in the list of the best universities in the world according to UniPage. Not bad, right?

Cambridge supports applicants and offers scholarships for undergraduate, graduate and doctoral studies. On the official website of the University operates a search engine for scholarships. Scholarships are filtered by specialty, student's country of origin and program of study.

ETH Zurich - Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (Switzerland)

ETH Zurich, also known as the Swiss Higher Technical School of Zurich, is the most prestigious university in the country. What is really there, in him, Albert Einstein himself studied. And now you can learn in it.

ETH Zurich offers material support for students of master programs - Excellence Scholarship & Opportunity Program (ESOP) and Master Scholarship Program (MSP). The first one covers all tuition fees and living expenses, the second covers only part of the school expenses.

The criterion for applying for a scholarship is standard - academic performance. You can apply only if 10% of the exams for the undergraduate degree were passed perfectly.

University of Oxford (United Kingdom)

Oxford University is listed as the best educational institution in the world in the prestigious rating of Times Higher Education. Training in information technologies in it is also at the level - it ranks second in Europe in this area. Education is worth a lot, but you can still learn there as an IT specialist if you get funding. The university offers a whole list of scholarships for different levels of study. The list of scholarships for which you can apply is available in a search engine on the official website.

Imperial College London (United Kingdom)

Another British university in our collection is the Imperial College of London. His material support applies only to doctoral studies. There are two general scholarships offered: Anne Seagrim Accommodation Scholarship and President's PhD Scholarships. The first covers only the cost of living, and the second will allow you to pay tuition and living expenses.
