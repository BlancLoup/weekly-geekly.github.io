Samsung has announced a new line of PCs based on Windows 8

Good evening, Habr!

The other day, Samsung Electronics shared details about the upcoming product line of mobile PCs and All-In-One devices based on Windows 8. The new series of computer solutions includes both portable laptops and Samsung ATIV Smart PC ‚Äútransformers‚Äù tablets, and elegant All- In-One desktop PCs.



Samsung ATIV Smart PC 500T is designed for lovers of maximum mobile devices. This device can easily change the form factor from a traditional laptop clamshell to a tablet PC and back.



Samsung ATIV Smart PC Pro 700T is a powerful premium class PC carrying an Intel Core i5 processor. Similar to the 500T, the Pro 700T can transform from a laptop to a tablet.



The Samsung Series 5 Ultra Touch is the company's first ultrabook with a 10-finger multi-touch display. The third generation of Intel Core i3 or i5 processors, premium multimedia features, high-resolution screen and ergonomic, stylish design provide the laptop with high mobility and functionality that meets the requirements of both regular customers and those who will use the device in everyday business solutions.





The Samsung Series 5 All-In-One, a powerful all-in-one PC solution, was designed primarily as an optimal solution for the kitchen: its keyboard and mouse fit comfortably under the device, saving space, as well as small screen size, which is most in demand in rooms of this type.





The Samsung Series 7 All-In-One is a premium home-class PC solution with powerful components inside an elegant and minimalist case. In addition to the 10-finger multi-touch interface, the display also recognizes gestures.







The new Samsung computers are equipped with exclusive applications that are designed to increase productivity and ensure maximum ease of use of devices. All products easily interact with other Samsung devices, such as mobile phones, tablets and televisions, through the Samsung AllShare app, allowing you to create a unified office or home ecosystem.

The devices are also equipped with the SW Update function, which allows you to update the software and drivers quickly and effortlessly. The first free update for the new Samsung line will be the Quick Starter App, a feature that adds a toolbar and a Start button to the Windows 8 interface. This will definitely help users, thanks to a recognizable navigation pattern, because A new operating system takes some time to get comfortable.

The ATIV Smart PC Pro series will come with pre-installed applications optimized for using the S Pen (such as S Note, S Memo and S Cloud). The pen itself will be built into the corresponding slot on the device and will be similar in functionality to the S Pen, which comes bundled with the new Samsung GALAXY Note II smartphone.

Officially, prices for a series of new products have not yet been published. However, during the press conference, which took place in mid-October in New York, the following figures were announced:

  • ATIV Smart PC 500T - $ 750 (with docking station) / $ 650 (without docking station)

Samsung Mobile PC (laptops)
  • Series 9 Premium Ultrabook - $ 1,400 (15 "), $ 1,300 (13")
  • Series 7 Notebook - $ 1,100 (15 ")
  • Series 5 Ultra Touch (13.3-inch laptop with touchscreen) - $ 860 (Core i5), $ 810 (Core i3)
  • Series 3 Notebook - $ 450 - $ 900 (depending on configuration)

Samsung All-in-One PC (Desktop PC)
  • Series 7 All-in-One - $ 1,700 (27 "); $ 1,100 (23.6")
  • Series 5 All-in-One - $ 800 (21.5 ")
