Post sense neurotic civilization

My dear, beloved, sokhabrovtsy! I must confess to you that I, as a religious fanatic, am a completely happy man, but of the last 16 years, out of my 32, I have been observing around me an increasingly aggravated picture of the struggle of society with the absurdity of life. The fact is that many of my friends work in large companies, where office sansara is mercilessly spreading, and I, so it happened, never had a hard schedule and an overwhelming corporate car behind my back, for which I praise Allah day and night . Being at a considerable distance from these problems and being emotionally not involved in them, doing science in my pleasure at the Scientific Research Institute of System Technologies (and earning a living from my own IT business), using miraculous system analysis, I will try to summarize everything I know about .

To begin with I will give an introduction to my incompetent research. I write something based on stories, and something from personal experience or experience deeply felt in close communication with customers.
1. I usually catch up with my familiar corporate employees at the weekend and in a state of " weekend neurosis ." In short, this is complete apathy, lethargy and indifference, which attack people after a busy week in the office. Usually, this condition is taken as a form of relaxed rest from working life, but this is a deceptive feeling. During the holidays, if you do not artificially load yourself with vigorous activity, it can last for weeks. Many IT people even come up with projects for the soul for weekends or holidays, so now the May days have come, and let's honestly raise our hands, who sat down for their favorite activity? I myself raise the first one.

Secondly, this is a terrifying senselessness , with which all business processes in 99% of companies are imbued, and in order not to lose your place, you need to vigorously pretend that projects are full of meaning. Fortunately, if there is a healthy sense of humor that does not allow believing in the reality of what is happening in the company, and if it is not there, then a serious esoteric self-embarrassment takes place, accompanied by standard phrases and clever looks without a shadow of doubt. The greater the capitalization, the greater the confusion


Analyzing what businesses are doing, I came to the conclusion that 90% of them are built on the exploitation of primitive needs, delusions and fears, it can be delusions and fears of customers (consumers of goods and services), and maybe - delusions and fears of business owners. In the first case, there is a conscious intimidation of the audience, tough propaganda and the spread of dependence on the goods and services, and in the second - a bubble of pride of the founders is inflated. The more money is invested, the harder it is to get rid of the desire to think that we are on the right path. Say something to the leadership rarely anyone can, and they will hear if they do not want to hear. How to work in such an organization? Subconsciously, every employee feels that the flow of directives is either immoral or meaningless.

Did you remember before?


It seems that they used to recall, but earlier there were ready answers. After all, there are two main approaches to the semantic fulfillment of existence: religious (mystical) and scientific (atheistic). But does everyone choose them freely? There is a certain fashion for this and the conditionality of the cultural context. Well, in order not to go to the holivars, we will assume that everyone chooses for himself. Mystical, if it is based on a young, lively and pure faith, it is beautiful, but if it came to the patterns and monotonous automatism of the old cult, then it is terrible, even atheists do not argue with that. The scientific approach, on the contrary, is terrible while fresh and young, and it becomes beautiful when science moves from the euphoria of uncontrollable progress, reversal of rivers and the preparation of all life, to mature wisdom. But in spite of their cardinal difference, they have one thing in common - both are forgotten and replaced by liquid, administered intravenous informational swill.

Moreover, these people themselves do not notice this at all and will be very surprised if they are told that someone is exploiting them. Take care of them!
