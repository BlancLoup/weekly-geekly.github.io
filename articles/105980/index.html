Announced a single subscription for Xbox Live and Windows Phone stores and a new App Hub resource

Microsoft takes another step towards developers and announces a single subscription for placing applications in the Xbox Live Marketplace and the Windows Phone Marketplace!

Now developers have the same free tools, one technology (XNA) and one subscription to create and sell applications for Windows Phone and Xbox 360. This is a significant step forward in cross-platform development, now developers of mobile xna applications can discover a huge audience of Xbox 360 and vice versa.

A single subscription costs $ 99 per year or is free for students of DreamSpark subscribers.



In addition, literally just announced a new single App Hub Developer Center, which is located at http://create.msdn.com where you can find everything you need to study technologies , create applications, and place them in stores. On the App Hub you can find the latest news, announcements and communication with the developer community.
