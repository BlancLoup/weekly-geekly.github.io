Max and the Magic Marker (Max and his magic marker)


Run, jump, think, and take the path to victory in the game Max and his magic marker.





About the game


- Support for Xbox LIVE for Windows Phones;
- 2D platformer;
- The puzzle.

Story


Max is a boy who likes to draw. One day he gets a magic orange marker in the mail. With the help of him, he painted a blue monster, and then with horror sees that the monster he painted came to life and escapes to other drawings drawn by him. Stunned, Max realizes that he must stop the monster. To do this, he draws himself in the same drawing, in which the monster ran away, and becomes involved in the game.

Additionally


- A new element in the gameplay: a marker - allows the player to draw directly inside the game world, where everything he drew becomes a physical object.

ilomilo


ilomilo is a puzzle game where the player controls two friends, whose names are Ilo and Milo. Friends are always separated in the surrealistic and constantly changing world in which they live. ilomilo is a puzzle game where the player will have to use three-dimensional thinking for success. The ilomilo world is made of cubes. Ilo and Milo can walk on either side of the cubes, as well as move the cubes around. The player controlling Ilo and Milo must help them meet each other.




About the game
