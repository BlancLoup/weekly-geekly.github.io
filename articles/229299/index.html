ZTE LivePro: mobile access point, projector and backup battery with touch screen and Android OS



Now in the market of gadgets continue to appear hybrid devices, among which come across very interesting ones. For example, ZTE presented ZTE LivePro at this year‚Äôs CES. This is a mobile access point, a projector and a backup battery in one package.

July 11, the device goes on sale in the US, and in other regions should appear.

The ZTE LivePro configuration includes a 5000 mAh battery, a 4-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 800 * 480 pixels, 1 GB of RAM, 4 GB of internal memory. In addition, a 2-core Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM8930 processor with a 1.2 GHz operating frequency is also installed.

Android 4.2 is installed as an operating system (so, among other things, you can make public demonstrations of your skills in playing Angry Birds). ZTE LivePro supports the launch of any Android applications.

As for the projector, its brightness is 100 lumens, and the life of the lamp should be enough for 20 thousand hours.



There is a built-in speaker. Plus, the developers have equipped the device with wireless communication modules WiFi, Bluetooth.

Among other things, the device can be synchronized with a mobile device, from where you can stream media files, for example. In addition, you can use a USB flash drive or SD card, all this can be connected to the device. There is also an HDMI port and a headphone port.



As a mobile access point, the device supports connecting up to 8 devices to the Network. The device itself works with 3G and 4G LTE, in the USA
ZTE LivePro works with Sprint networks.

As mentioned above, LivePro is equipped with a 5000 mAh battery and can be used as a backup power source. Simply connect your smartphone / tablet to the appropriate port, and the mobile device will charge.



In the US, Sprint is responsible for LivePro sales. The sale starts on July 11, at a price of $ 450 without a contract or 299 with a 2-year contract.
