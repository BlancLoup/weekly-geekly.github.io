Typegram - Zen blog platform

Hi, Habr. I decided to show you the engine that I am developing on the sly. This is a blogging platform, very close to the medium and similar projects.


Basic features:


Publications, comments, favorites, subscriptions, notifications, ratings, voting.


Minimalistic, text design:


Playground


There is a playground where you can touch the platform, without prejudice to civilians: test site .


Localization


The service automatically divides users by language, directing Russian-speaking users to the Russian subdomain, and English-speaking users to English. Those separation is essentially implemented at the content level. True interface is available only English. Each subdomain has its own database and its users, registration is not end-to-end (each subdomain has its own ecosystem, life did not originate everywhere, for example).



Optimization


The service is characterized by very aggressive optimization. All downloaded images are more than 100 kilobytes, they are pressed by the Atkinson method in black and white previews, with the ability to view the original, for example:


Heavy scripts and styles are not used, as well as external scripts that monitor user behavior. The site remains fast and convenient on any platform, including with javascript disabled. In the future, I want to reach the speed of loading telegram channels.


Subscriptions


The main page displays a list of authors to which the user is subscribed, and the number of new publications for each author. When you click on this button, unread publications are opened, in chronological order, as in the telegram channels:



Notifications


Also on the main page there are notifications of references in the comments, with the possibility of transition:



Editor


The editor supports both marcown and visual text formatting, with the ability to preview, expand to full screen and so on. Work is underway to ensure the availability of the site for blind and visually impaired users.
