Let me begin my story with a story about free time, family and craving for beauty. The free time I can spend with my wife is usually very little. Either I am busy, then my wife. That both of us. But sometimes an hour or two is released that can be spent together. And what can be done together? Well, besides what you thought, you can still watch the movie together. If there is a tracker on which hundreds of interesting films are constantly laid out? If you have a good TV and a sofa on which to watch movies is much nicer? No, sometimes, of course, it is pleasant to go to the cinema, or review the novelties of 2006, but in most cases we want (1) at home , (2) watch for free (3) a new movie (4) in good quality (5) without waiting for it is downloaded.

As I have. Well, more precisely, it was before. If there was free time, when both my wife and I can and want to watch a movie, I turned on the computer, went to the tracker, flipped through new items for a long time, read descriptions, chose a movie, put it into download, waited for the download to finish, connected the computer to the TV, turned happy to my wife and ... I realized that she had fallen asleep for half an hour while I was doing all this. At some point I got tired of it and I went the other way, which I will now tell you about.

For starters, I got rid of the need to connect a computer to a TV. From the old iron I assembled a computer dedicated specifically for the role of a media server. On this computer, I put the Linux assembly "mythbuntu", specially created for this purpose. I found an infrared receiver, bought 7 years ago with a Sony Ericsson mobile phone. I bought a remote in the subway for 190 rubles. I connected the assembled computer to the TV and so I got a media server with an intuitive graphical shell, controlled from the console.
