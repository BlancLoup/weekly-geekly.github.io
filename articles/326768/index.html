Instantly determine your IQ and temperament



Our rating system LevelPride has accumulated a sufficient amount of data that can be analyzed. Using this data as a training sample and links from the social network VKontakte, we can make an assumption about the temperament and IQ level of almost any user of the social network Vkontakte.

Instantly determine IQ and temperament here . We would appreciate if you unsubscribe about the accuracy of the results and take part in the survey. Below we will talk about our algorithm.

Users of LevelPride rating system conduct their portfolio by uploading information about their achievements from various spheres of life into the system. The user's portfolio, provided it is filled, allows you to learn a lot about it. Recently, we connected the Vkontakte API and asked our regular users to give us permissions to access their information and the list of friends. To information about the user stored in the system, added information from the social network.

As an experiment, we decided to try to implement a service that would determine some characteristics of a user based on his social connections. We just get user connections from the social network and based on these data (as well as age, gender, education, etc.) we are trying to determine which group of users from LevelPride will be located most closely.

While you can only define your data. In the future, it is planned to create a service that will be able to analyze the data of other users, provided that they are open. The service can be useful in evaluating staff or recruiting a freelancer.
