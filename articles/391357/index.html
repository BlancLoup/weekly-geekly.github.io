Amazon (and not only) openly sells clocks for students

In the first year of university, I wrote down notes using a ballpoint pen, while in the second year I only wrote on a laptop. But cribs had to be handwritten or cut from collections bought at the Young Guard bookstore at the Polyanka station. With the advent of mobile communications, hands-free headsets began to be brought to the exams - but universities put jammers on. Students began to upload data to smartphones - but teachers also took them away. Then students who wanted to get a good grade paid attention to other gadgets - for example, smart watches.

On Amazon and other sites, they sell devices designed specifically for students: "These watches are specially made for cheating on exams and have special software for this."



The British public (at least the BBC is pretending) is shocked by the fact that Amazon openly sells for $ 49.99 hours to cheat on exams. The seller directly declares the target audience in the description of the gadget. The device supports TXT, MP3, JPG, GIF, WAV, WMV, AVI formats and has a special "emergency" button, when you click on it, time appears on the screen.

The smart watches from China in question are equipped with a 2-inch screen, capable of showing time and, most importantly, allowing you to read e-books. For cheat sheets, they have 4 gigabytes of internal memory. In addition, they have a standard 3.5 mm headphone port, so you can listen to music and watch videos. Headphones and USB cable included. This is just a dream of my childhood for 2000 rubles * .





Of course, this is not the only option. The seller from Poland offers Draverik watches on eBay - but they already cost $ 99, that is, about 7,000 rubles * . And while they are more like a cheat sheet.



But if your teacher already takes away his smart watch, all that remains is a calculator. With a color display, 4 gigabytes of internal memory and the same functions as the clock. This calculator will cost $ 59.98 - about 4290 rubles * .



In any case, fortunately, these gadgets do not need those two suitcases.
