Where can I get nanoCAD?
Traditionally, we support three distribution protocols: HTTP, FTP and TORRENT. The first two are our resources:
  • Download nanoCAD 3.5 via HTTP (273 MB, prior registration is required on the site!);
  • Download nanoCAD 3.5 via FTP (273 MB);
  • UPG: Download nanoCAD 3.5 via FTP (69 MB, version without Microsoft .NET framework).
But these protocols are often overloaded due to the influx of visitors. Therefore, we initially decided to use the torrent protocol when creating the project. And which site is one of the most popular and related to torrents? Exactly - rutracker.org ! Welcome to the nanoCAD 3.5 page:

Our distribution on rutracker.org - connect and help spread nanoCAD!

Shared resources on nanoCAD
In addition to the official website nanocad.ru and the official forum forum.nanocad.ru , we have a new website: developer.nanocad.ru . This is the entrance to the club of developers who want to create applications under nanoCAD. While on it we accept registration. Then we approve the questionnaires and let them go to the holy-holies - access to the API, the developers forum, the API error logging system, and our collaboration begins ...

Anyone who is interested in the development of a modern CAD system, welcome!
