Will they be forced to register on social networks with a passport?


The newly created media committee, headed by deputy Alexei Mitrofanov, intends to take the initiative to expand the list of data provided by users in social networks.

The initiators of the proposal explain this proposal by the need to combat the spread of child pornography, slander, etc. Experts believe that there is no talk about tightening the screws so far, but only if such amendments to the legislation do not pull other restrictions.
