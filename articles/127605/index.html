HTC Titan, HTC Radar and HTC Mozart officially presented

The first of September 2011 was marked by the release of two great new products of our company: Titan and Radar based on Windows Phone 7.5, and today we can confidently assert that Windows Phone has officially come to Russia!

HTC Titan has a huge 4.7 "display with a resolution of 480x800 pixels, which makes this smartphone truly unique in the entire product line on Windows Phone. The iron component is also at the level: the novelty is based on the Qualcomm MSM8255 platform with a 1.5GHz processor, RAM size is 512MB, and the size of the internal memory is 16GB (12.63GB available to the user). Titan is dressed in a stylish unibody-aluminum case, and its weight is only 160 grams.



The presence of 8MPks camera with aperture F2.2 and BSI-sensor (for shooting in low light conditions) allowed us to raise the quality of mobile photography to a whole new level. HTC Titan also has a 1.3MPx front camera for making video calls via Skype. Titan will appear on store shelves this fall, the recommended price will be announced later. Detailed specifications can be found on the official website .



Radar


Titan will be a stylish HTC Radar . The monolithic aluminum case of the HTC Radar is able to naturally emphasize its premium design. It comes with Radar 3.8 "SuperLCD display with a resolution of 480x800 pixels and runs on the Qualcomm MSM8255 1GHz platform. The internal memory size of the device is 8GB (6.64GB available to the user). Front VGA-camera is also available. You can find out detailed specifications on our website .





Mozart


But that's not all! From September 16, sales of Svyaznoy and MTS will start selling acquaintances to many HTC 7 Mozart with the latest version of the Windows Phone operating system at a price of 12,990 rubles .



Windows phone


The new version of Windows Phone 7.5 will meet you with the familiar Metro UI interface, but it can surprise you with a whole set of useful innovations. For example, support for Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn is now built into the operating system, and the email client got the opportunity to work with several mailboxes at the same time, thus creating a unified inbox.

Me Hub was significantly reworked, and support for communication through social networks was built into the internal messaging system. Bing Maps have become even more functional, and Internet Explorer 9 has gained hardware support for rendering pages.

It is worth noting that Windows Phone 7.5 smartphones are simply created for the corporate environment: in addition to improved integration with Exchange, Microsoft Office Mobile is available on Windows Phone, which means that you can view, edit and even synchronize documents with Office 365 directly on your smartphone.

We are pleased to provide new features and user experience to our customers. We see great new consumer opportunities thanks to the integration of the most popular Microsoft products and services.
