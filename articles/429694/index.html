Mail.Ru called "absurd" a rating of sites from "Yandex" and demands to remove its brands from it

Yesterday, Yandex launched the Yandex.Radar project - a rating of the most popular resources in runet, which is based on a mathematical model based on aggregated data from Yandex.Browser. If desired, sites can connect data from Yandex.Metrica to add demographic data to the audience, etc.

The new service was not liked by everyone. The main competitor of Yandex in the Russian segment of Mail.Ru immediately declared that it did not trust this metric and asked them to remove their sites from the rating.

Mail.Ru believes that Yandex itself is a player in the advertising market, and therefore it is incorrect for him to maintain such a rating. In addition, it deliberately incorrectly reflects the audience of the sites, since it relies on the data provided by Yandez.Brouser. That is, the inflated figures will be at sites where the share of Yandex.Browser users is high. Obviously, these are loyal to Yandex resources.
