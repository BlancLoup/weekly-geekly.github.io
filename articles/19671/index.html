Eee PC will appear in Russia in March


PC Week reports that in March the Eee PC laptop will be on sale in Russia. Alexey Muzhzhevlev, a representative of ASUSTeK Computer, reports that a 4G model will be sold (7 ", 4 Gb SSD, 512 Mb DDR2, video camera). According to him, the retail price with Linux will be 11-12 thousand rubles, and with Windows XP - 12-13 thousand.
On this occasion, the largest forum dedicated to the Eee PC, opened a special section for Russian users .


UPD: however, only the Eee PC 2G Surf model (XP / 700X / RU) is presented on the ASUSTeK Support website
