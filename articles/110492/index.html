Shop2CD - creation of PHPShop satellite sites and catalog copies on CD

PHPShop Software, a developer of CMS for online stores, has introduced a new utility that makes it easy to create copies of an online store catalog for writing to a CD or for creating satellite sites.

The Shop2CD utility is an online store promotion tool that can be used to create satellite sites or as part of affiliate programs when a store catalog is hosted on a partner site.

The program creates html-pages with the description and image of the goods in the online store, without requiring an additional license. CD generation takes place in on-line mode and allows you to create a complete copy of your product catalog. When viewing a catalog of goods on a CD, a buyer can immediately go to the website or add goods to the cart and then place an order in the online store.
The utility supports various themes and is easily customizable for personal design.







Shop2CD is included in the PHPShop EasyControl utility package, which can be downloaded for free on the official website .
