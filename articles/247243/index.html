Conferences that are waiting for us in 2015
Professionals are primarily distinguished by the desire to learn and improve their level of knowledge and skills. We do our best to assist in this by supporting various thematic IT conferences throughout the year. As in the past year, we bring to your attention an informational leaflet on relevant conferences that may be of interest to various specialists.

Unfortunately, the organizers of many conferences have not yet announced plans for 2015, therefore only information on announced events is presented here.

Winter Nights: Mobile Games




One of the largest conferences in Europe for developers and publishers of mobile games. The conference will feature representatives from a number of international companies involved in the creation and distribution of games for various mobile platforms. Very rich two-day program.

Dates and venue : February 9-10, 2015, St. Petersburg, Victory Square, 1, Park Inn Hotel.
Website: http://www.wnconf.com

SPM Conference (Software Project Management Conference)




The fourth international conference in the field of IT-projects management. Presented here are reports of renowned experts in their fields. The topics of the reports are divided into four groups (principle 4P): personnel, processes, projects, products.

Dates and venue: February 20-21, 2015, Moscow.
Website: http://www.spmconf.ru/ru/index

AgileDays




Specialized conference for developers. This time the main topics of the conference will be the philosophy and methodology used for flexible process management, be it the development of a company or product development.

Dates and venue: March 19-20, 2015, Moscow.
Website: http://agiledays.ru

eTarget




The conference is dedicated to internet marketing, advertising and SEO. Within the program, invited experts will read reports. There will also be round tables. Anyone can visit the exhibition, which will be attended by companies specializing in advertising and promotion on the Internet.

Dates and venue: March 12-13, 2015, Moscow.
Website: http://www.etarget.ru

Analyst days




This international conference on systems and business analysis traditionally discusses various practical issues related to innovations, trends and problems in the field of design and requirements management, as well as relevant technicians, tools and technologies.

Dates and venue: April 17-18, 2015, Minsk, Belarus.
Website: http://www.analystdays.com/ru/index
