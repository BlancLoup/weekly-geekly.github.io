Meeting on cross-platform development for Windows, iOS, Android - December 18, Moscow



On December 18, we invite developers of mobile applications and those who are only interested in mobile development for the New Year community meeting.

The meeting will focus on cross-platform development for iOS, Android and Windows.

This time, we have prepared for you something special. We are planning a guest visit to the office of Tinkoff Bank, which actively uses the mobile market and mobile applications for its business.

You will have the opportunity to communicate with specialists in development , marketing and promotion , and learn "how they do it" first hand.

schedule


18.30 - 19.00 - Collection of participants
19.00 - 19.30 - Cross - platform application development in Visual Studio 2015 (Tatiana Smetanina, Evangelist, Microsoft).
19.30 - 20.00 - Mobile Applications Architecture (Kirill Bessonov, Lead Developer, Tinkoff)
20.00 - 20.30 - Application Promotion: Tinkoff Experience (Kirill Bobrov, Head of Internet Advertising, Tinkoff)
20.30 - 21.00 - Analytics systems for mobile applications (Anastasia Kataeva, mobile application development manager, Tinkoff)
21.00 - 21.30 - Dinner and free communication.

Location


Address: 39A p. 1, Leningradskoye shosse, Moscow, Olympia Park Business Center, Tinkoff Bank office.



check in


Our meetings are open and accessible to everyone . But, in order to correctly calculate the number of places, food and make the meeting more comfortable for you, we ask, if possible, to leave an application for registration.
