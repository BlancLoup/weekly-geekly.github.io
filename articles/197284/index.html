Google and NASA have teamed up to create a quantum computer.

In May, in collaboration with NASA, we announced the Quantum AI Lab - a place where researchers from around the world can experiment with incredible power and quantum computing capabilities.

We are still at a very early stage, but we think that quantum computing will help solve the most complex scientific and computational problems. In particular, we are interested in how a quantum computer can improve machine learning , which can later be applied to many areas: from the search for drugs to understanding changes in climate.

As soon as the team began working together this summer, we decided to make a video in which we would briefly tell about the "backstage" life of the project. In it, we presented a few strange, "enduring the brain" and certainly amazing concepts.

If you are interested in the project, you can subscribe to the community page on Google+ to keep abreast of all the latest research (plus of course discussions and various videos).
