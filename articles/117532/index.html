Business in Russian or again about coupons and promotions

Recently (namely over the past two months), Russian couponers have another super-idea - selling cars at a discount. I will start with a little background and tell you how it all began. It will be about BigBazi, whose action I have been following for the last few days, not only because I wanted to win a car, but because it was very interesting to learn the essence of their mesmerizing, mysterious action - audi with a discount.

It all started about a week ago, when the BigBazi website reported that very soon there would be a special offer for all registered users - a discounted car. Nothing was reported about the conditions of the action, and moreover, nothing was known about the car (it was covered with a mysterious yellow case). Here is the information that has been communicated to users:

- BigBazi has been waiting for this promotion for a whole year.
- Any registered user can participate in the promotion.
- Subscription by SMS and email is available on the start of sales
- This is not an auction and not a lottery

On the promotion page, all users had the opportunity to leave comments.

How events developed further:

Night from the 13th to the 14th of April - the first users began to monitor the situation with the action, write comments and wait for the case to be removed from the car and the veil opened. The morning of April 14 is more and more comments, the number of waiting times is growing. The action is actively discussed. Dinner - the first skeptics and assumptions that this might be a divorce began to appear. 18.00 - the number of comments exceeds 1000. Tension and excitement grows. BigBazi answers all questions that the action is about to take place. Literally - "you waited a whole week, wait a few more hours, minutes, seconds." Evening, 9 pm - many people write that they can no longer sit at the computer, that they update the site every half minute throughout the day ... 11 pm - there are more skeptics, a group of VKontakte appears - Club awaiting audi on BigBizzy.ru or Naebuzzy.ru
where communication goes into a new form and where comments are not moderated by the BigBazi administration. In 10 minutes the group grows to 100 people. At 23.00 comments on the page with the action of more than 2500.
