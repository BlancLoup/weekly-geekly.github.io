The founder of photo hosting TwitPic launched a Twitter clone



The founder of photo hosting TwitPic Noah Everett announced the launch of his startup Heello , announced exactly a year ago. Everett himself calls the new service a competitor to Twitter, but in fact it's just his clone.

It is noteworthy that the launch of Heello coincided with the launch of the official internal photo hosting site for all Twitter users. After that, Twitter users lost the need to use other auxiliary photo hosting sites, the most popular of which was TwitPic. Everett said it was a complete coincidence, but he was glad for him.
