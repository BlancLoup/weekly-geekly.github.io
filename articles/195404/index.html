Behance - a community of creative professionals from around the world

There are a lot of really talented guys among Russian designers, illustrators and photographers. Perhaps many of them are aware of the existence of Behance . I wrote this post in order to attract more of our compatriots to the resource so that on the world stage of design and creativity Russia would be presented in a bright light.

The launch of Behance took place in 2006, the project is the largest community and a source of inspiration for people of creative professions. In 2012, Adobe bought Behance and closely integrated the platform with its Creative Cloud service.



With the help of Behance, millions of designers, illustrators and photographers showcase their work, share experience, receive rave reviews and comment on the work of their colleagues.
