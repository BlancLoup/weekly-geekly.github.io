A little about media content: fees for services

After reading the record of criticism of DRM , I caught myself in one amusing thought. I will share with you, suddenly it will be interesting.

Bulk content should not be sold and bought - it should be available for free - you need to sell related services . On the example of music: it can be a convenient interface for searching and selecting albums / tracks, a good channel for downloading, many delivery options, including on various media. And the authors need to make deductions from such services, since their free (!) Works are used for making profit.

The harsh realities of the Internet and the Runet are already such that almost any music, clip, film can be found independently and without acquiring it, but time, nerves and traffic will be spent on it, plus the result will be no guarantee of the recording quality or delivery (speed downloads). Moreover, it is against the law at the moment. BUT! After all, many people do not want to have problems with searching and wasting time, but they want to have a guarantee of getting the record in good quality and in a certain period - you can and should pay for it. And they pay for it.

Is this the future?
