We collect in one place developers for iPad!

At the beginning of June in Ingria we want to organize an Enabling Meeting, a free event for startups dedicated to the development of applications for iPad ! The purpose of the meeting: to explore the opportunities, prospects and "rules of the game" in the market of iPad-applications.

We want to invite everyone interested in this topic. Now we are developing the program of this event, look at what we have already thought.

It is supposed to divide the meeting into 3 parts:
1) Description of the "rules of the game." Speech by experts about the assessment of prospects, opportunities, about the rules for creating and publishing applications, about monetization tools.
2) Investments. Presentation by a representative of an investment fund planning to invest in application development. Now such things are quite attractive for venture investors, so developers can catch a wave!
3) "Trial of the pen." Discussion of several cases from developers in the format of a round table or just a gathering, personal experience of the participants
