Issuing SSL certificates in 5 minutes

Ukrainian ICANN accredited registrar, the Center for Internet Names of Ukraine (Ukrnames) has launched an updated service for registering and renewing SSL certificates .



The new service allowed 100% automate the process of registering and renewing SSL certificates. From the moment you fill out the application to the actual issuance of the SSL certificate, it now takes no more than 5 minutes!

In addition, the range of SSL certificates has been significantly expanded - cheap certificates with domain verification, SSL certificates with SGC and SAN support, EV certificates (extended validation certificates - ‚Äúgreen line in browser‚Äù) and Code Signing certificates for software developers have been added.

SSL certificates allow you to provide a secure connection between clients and an Internet resource.

If the SSL certificate is signed by a trusted Certification Authority, it is prestigious. To a site using a trusted SSL certificate - visitor loyalty is significantly higher than to a site using a self-signed certificate. This is especially true for online stores, portals, postal services and other resources working with personal and billing customer data.

You can order the registration of a new SSL certificate, as well as extend the existing one, regardless of where it was previously ordered, on the website: http://ukrnames.com/certs/ssl_certificates.jsp
