Software Freedom Day 2010 - Saratov

In preparation for the celebration of Software Freedom Day in Saratov, to be held on September 18, 2010, the organizing committee invites all open source supporters to take part in shaping the program of the event and organizing a team of assistants.

Please read the preliminary description of the holiday (will be updated): sfd.sgu.ru/sfd2010.pdf

At present, a program of reports and master classes is being formed, as well as a competition of innovative ideas and projects in the field of free software. You can offer your project, performance and / or master class on the site sfd.sgu.ru/takepart (you need to register in the main domain of SSU or register directly on the site).

You can also offer any other help or sponsorship to sfd.sgu.ru/takepart or to the organizing committee: saratov-sfd-org@googlegroups.com

Details: sfd.sgu.ru
