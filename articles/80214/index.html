MobileJoomla! - we transform a Joomla site into a Joomla mobile site

On the issue of displaying sites on mobile devices. Today, in correspondence with a man came across the site MobileJoomla! which offers tools for converting your Joomla site to a mobile site, which will be displayed on most mobile phones and PDAs.

What do they offer?

As it says, the site will be compatible with all phones in the world, including the iPhone, smartphones, iMode and wap.


Just installed and configured. Installed, read the instructions and can be used.
A flexible system of parameters and settings will prepare your mobile site for display on any mobile device.


Modern methods of detecting devices and smart methods of adapting content provide the best look on your mobile device.


WURFL integration, support for the latest devices. Instant update options.


Well, for dessert:
  • Advanced User Agent Detaction (multiple WURFL, Compact WURFL, fixed or missing).
  • Milti site support.
  • Extended image adaptation.
  • Flexible layout settings.
  • Redirect to subdomain
  • Advanced device classification: XHTML, iPhone, and iMode WAP.
  • Special templates for each category of devices.
  • Different menus for each category of devices.
  • Fully customizable through the use of various modules and templates.
  • Compatible with modules, components and extensions Joomla.

    • But the development is still on the alpha stage. www.mobilejoomla.com
