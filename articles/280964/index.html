RUVDS thanks Habrahabr users for their choice

By independent evaluation , RUVDS AlexeyKh user is recognized as the best among the tested VDS / VPS servers on Windows.
In honor of this event, especially for readers of the blog habrahabr, the virtual hosting project RUVDS provides a unique discount on its services.

Use the promotional code on the banner and get a discount of up to 50% on any configuration of the VDS server.
The terms of the promotion apply to new servers: the basic discount on the promo code is 30%, the discount on the promo code is summed up at a discount when paying for 3, 6 or 12 months. Thus, when paying for 3 months, the total discount will be 35%, if paid for 6 months - 40%, if paid for a year - 50%.
