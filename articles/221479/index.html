Speaking of lightsabers. Here are 13 facts that even many Star Wars fans don't know about:

1) Light swords were created by adherents of the Dark Side.
The first protomeche was called forcesaber (sword of force). It was a directional flow of dark energy (not to be confused with dark matter), formed into a blade with the help of crystals and alchemy. Therefore, if the Jedi took such a sword in his hands, then there was a great risk of his immediate transition to the Dark Side. Therefore, the Jedi created their own version of the lightsaber to resist the weapons of evil.

2) Light swords use compact energy sources.
The first swords had no built-in power source. Owners were forced to carry a supply of batteries in backpacks or belts, connected by a cable with a sword. The first to create a compact embedded energy source for lightsabers were Sith.

3) At first they were called "laser swords" (lazersword).

However, during the filming, Lucas decided to make it green because during the battle in Sarlacc the blue blade was poorly visible against the sky.

In addition to the desire to destroy the Jedi, Emperor Palpatine outlawed the possession of light swords. It was even forbidden to trade in crystals for their manufacture. Darth Vader was granted special permission to own a lightsaber.
