Yandex Developer, Yandex API Bar: Yandex.Bar with your buttons

Independent developers, webmasters and owners of online resources! This version of Yandex. Bar is for you.

When developing Yandex. Bar, we always thought that the implementations are worthy of the components of all services, though not the most popular, but interesting to you and your users. That the buttons on the Yandex.Bar panel should be able to work not only with Yandex services, but also with your favorite sites outside the portal. That installing new buttons should not force you to rearrange the entire Bar.

As a result of our reflections and work on them, in the new version of Yandex. Bar 5.0 Beta there appeared a language for describing custom buttons and an API that supports it. This bundle is called "xButton".

xButton is an opportunity to place your button in millions of browsers of users of Yandex. Bar. Anyone can write their own button or a set of buttons and offer it to other users.

The xButton language is descriptive, no programming skills are required to create buttons. Not required, but will be useful when writing complex components :) xButton is relatively safe - a component written with it cannot delete files on a computer or write a virus to a hard disk.
