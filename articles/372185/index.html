Gadget Posting Tail turns a dog into a blogger

Nowadays, even pets can have their accounts in social networks. Owners diligently fill the pages with content, upload photos, change statuses and leave comments under the posts. In general, they spend some time and effort on the development of the virtual life of their pets. The new joint development of the company Pedigree and the advertising agency Saatchi & Saatchi will provide the opportunity to fill the pages directly to the pets themselves.
