Ontario conducts an experiment with an unconditional basic income

Residents of the Canadian province of Ontario will soon begin to receive monthly financial payments without any conditions - the so-called unconditional basic income . This is a pilot program, which, if successful, can be extended to the whole country.

The basic income means that each person has a share in the total national production, including the share of national natural resources. Canada replenishes a significant part of the budget by selling oil. It is logical that the income from the sale of national wealth is distributed among all citizens.

According to Canadian economists, the payment of AML not only improves the average standard of living and is consistent with social justice, but even stimulates the creation of new jobs. Oddly enough, when they get unconditional income, people continue to work almost as much as before. Citizens spend more time on education , child care, and while reducing the number of working hours , labor productivity increases .

Payments to AEDs eliminate the shortcomings of standard unemployment benefits. First, many of those who cannot register as unemployed will receive financial assistance. Secondly, the fat bureaucratic stratum, which deals with the design and distribution of benefits, is eliminated. Practice shows that the current system of unemployment benefits is ineffective and does not fulfill the tasks assigned to it. This means that in the future the society will become more healthy, the crime rate will decrease, and the children themselves are more likely to get an education and a good job. That is, AML has a long-term positive effect.
