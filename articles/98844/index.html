Now you can pay with plastic

Russia is still a country of cash, but the tendency to increase the use of bank cards for making payments is already obvious. Many of our compatriots still do not fully trust the "plastic", but those who have already appreciated all the advantages of non-cash payments, not only gladly use cards for off-line and on-line payments.

Plastic VS Cash

Abroad to the use of plastic for a long time accustomed. According to the British Payments Council, after 5 years, the amount of cash used will be halved. The forecast, of course, is impressive, but for Europe today this situation does not seem fantastic.

As for our country:
Of the total volume of operations with bank cards in 2009, only 11.2% (or 10.6 billion dollars) accounted for the operations of paying for goods and services with cards, and the remaining 88.8% (or 83.6 billion dollars) accounted for ATM cash withdrawal operations. Link

The process is slow, but it is going. This is confirmed by the active work in this direction at the state level: obviously, the desire to convince people to reduce the use of cash, because it is convenient for all participants in the payment process.
