Russian car industry will make a flying car by 2020

A grandiose document was published on the website of the Vedomosti newspaper - the final version of the development strategy of the Russian car industry until 2020 ( TIF file, 101 pages ). The strategy has already been officially adopted and approved.

Of particular interest is clause 9.1, which describes the development of the national R & D base (pages 85-88), including the creation of unmanned vehicles (flyers) for the transport of passengers, vehicles for the industrial development of the Moon, etc.

AvtoVAZ will have something to do in the near future.

1) the development of promising technologies of hydrogen energy and the creation on their basis of energy-efficient transport systems with ultra-low emissions of harmful substances;
