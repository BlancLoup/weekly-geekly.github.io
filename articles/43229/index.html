It's a question of time

Today, at 03:00 am, most of the habrauers have their time zone changed from EEST to EET.

Only one institution in Russia does not convert hours to summer time. Due to the complexity of the settings of the computer complex in the Mission Control Center, the clock is not transferred there. MCC works year-round in winter Moscow time.

Twice a year with new force renewed debate about the need to transfer hours. There are suggestions that the transfer of hours to summer time, and then the reverse transfer to winter time may adversely affect health. However, according to the director of the Serbsky Center for Forensic Psychiatry, there are no reliable data on the negative impact on a person of the transfer of hours. For the most gentle passing of the clock to summer time, it is enough to adjust your weekend mode. And on the night of the transfer of hours, go to bed half an hour or an hour earlier.
