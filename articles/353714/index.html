Announcement of AppsConf: 2 days of application, hardcore and HYIP reports



October 8-9, Moscow will host AppsConf , a conference dedicated to problems in mobile development and their solution. This will be the third AppsConf, and now we have decided to rework the format and approach the organization from a slightly different angle.

But first about the important:

  • We are looking for speakers , here is the application form.
  • Early bird tickets are available , the price will start to rise from the first of June and further down the schedule .
  • Videos from previous conferences AppsConf 2016 and 2017 .

So, what has changed ...

Structure


First, not one day, but two. Secondly, not Skolkovo, but the very center of Moscow, the Infospace site. Thirdly, several parallel tracks and about 60 reports at once. Fourth, a lot of networking and additional activities.



Positioning


We are very strict approach to the selection of reports. Alone, the promises of the most hardcore conference will no longer entice anyone, so we identified three main types of speeches that we will select into the program.

  1. Those same hardcore
    You will not receive this knowledge from anyone except the experts invited to our conference.
  2. Applied
    The knowledge that you will receive on these reports will be useful to you the very next day.
  3. HYIP
    You will want to tell your mother and all your friends about this after the first five minutes of the report.



Content


We have already begun active preparations for the conference. The program committee , consisting of developers of top domestic companies and organizers of large mobile communities, is working on the selection of speakers. And, of course, we already have something to tell about the headliners who submitted their applications for participation.

Graham lee
Legend in the world of iOS development, the author of several books about TDD and security, the main architecture for the company in Wealth Wizards. Over the past few years, Graham has been exploring the similarity of functional and object-oriented programming, contrasting the pure simplicity of one over-complexity of the other. At AppsConf, Graham is ready to share the results of his observations.


Maxim Efimov
Software engineer from Uber, with experience mainly in Android development . Most of all likes to carry out code review, and work on architecture. Maxim plans to talk about how the development processes are built in the company , where several hundreds of mobile developers work on one application.


Brandon williams
After devoting many years of mathematics to his life, Brandon moved to Kickstarter, where he was responsible for iOS and Android development for five years. One of his key achievements is the translation of all mobile development to open source on GitHub . Brandon is currently working on Point-Free , a video blog about Swift and functional programming . At the conference, Brandon is ready to talk about synchronization primitives and the use of the type system in Swift in conjunction with algebra to simplify working with some data structures.
