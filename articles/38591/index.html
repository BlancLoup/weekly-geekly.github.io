VotImenno.Ru - Russia's first risk-free naming service

I offer a respected Habrapublic for your spanking my new startup - VotImenno.Ru.
The essence of the project is the following - people who need to come up with a beautiful domain, name or slogan, place an order in the system. They receive a lot of options from which to make a choice. The person who proposed the chosen option receives 40% of the order value (the cost of selecting a domain is 600 rubles, a slogan is 1200 rubles, and the name is 1000 rubles). If none of the options do not like, then the customer gets his money back. In other words, there is no risk. Either you get what you are looking for, or the money is returned to you upon request.

We have been operating in Russia for a month and a half (initially, we started working in the west for a few years nahad under the name PickyDomains.com - and we had a great time to unwind there). So far we have VotImenno.Ru registered 100 customers and 570+ contribi. The turnover is still small - about 30 thousand rubles. Now in active work 20 orders. There are satisfied customers and copywriters (and dissatisfied too). Criticism (constructive), as well as participation in the system, both as a copywriter and as a customer, is welcome.
