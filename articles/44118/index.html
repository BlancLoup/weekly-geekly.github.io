Vkontakte under Miranda

Introduction

Like everyone else in the era of global social networking hobbies, I started a Vkontakte account. Like most normal people, with time I began to go there less and less. But my friends were people whom I had not seen for a long time, contacts with whom had already been lost. This is the last place where we can contact, make an appointment, exchange phone numbers, etc. It is not good to lose it ...

Prehistory

Since I am a distributor of portable software among friends, I gave my wife an opera without cleaning the profile. And so it was already happened that sometimes she told me: a message came to you on Vkontakte ... And I wondered how, if I often came into contact, know about messages? The answer, as always, is simple - a plugin for your favorite IM. The first link in google was correct (and how he knows what I want :)).
