Conference developers highload systems HighLoad-2007



HighLoad - 2007 will be a training conference. We assume that each report will last an hour, during which it will be possible to thoroughly and comprehensively discuss the topic concerned. All reports will be held in one stream so that participants can visit everything that interests them. As a handout we will prepare a book including the full texts of all the reports of the conference.

The audience of the conference is mid-level developers (they will have the opportunity to learn, improve their skills) and gurus (for them, this is an exchange of opinions, a brain shake, communication with their peers).



The proposed program is divided into four parts:
  • Mini workshops on scalability, on what it generally is. On the load and the main methods of its parallelization, the main approaches to the design and development of high-load projects;
  • Examples of solutions, details and details of existing systems, architectures. In this segment, we will look at examples of several large working systems;
  • High-load database tuning (from database developers themselves), operating systems (from developers of operating systems), and web servers (well, you understand;)
  • Associated moments - features of testing, layout, deployment, hosting, backup high-load systems. This important item contains many similar stones. Everyone who tried to roll out new code simultaneously to dozens of disparate servers knows about their presence. Separately, we consider the hosting of large projects, protection against DDOS and other related issues.


We are negotiating that our foreign colleagues from Yahoo, Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Livejournal and other international companies share their experience at the HighLoad - 2007 conference.

Details on the conference site!
