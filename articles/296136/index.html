13 job search hacks that may work better than a traditional resume



The job search can be ranked as one of the most stressful activities - often the standard sending of your resume to employers gives you such a devastating feeling, as if they all go into an all-consuming black hole.

Fortunately, now there are many non-standard ways to snatch a great job. Users have access to applications and online services that will help you get a free lunch with a potential employer, better show your personality or, for example, turn your track record worklist into a great infographic. In addition, it is possible to get remote work for a few hours with instant pay on completion of the project or organize an internship with reference to a specific project where you do not have to prepare coffee for colleagues.

We present you 13 "technological" ways to get a new job.

ResumUp turns your resume into a great infographic.




The application provides an easy and convenient template, thanks to which you can get an impressive infographic from your standard resume. Perhaps this tool will not be able to fully replace the CV - it is more suitable as a supplement, the second page for the traditional resume. At the same time, ResumeUp will be useful for those who cannot boast of an ideal and logical way of climbing the career ladder, but would like to show their entire working career in the best light.

Price / Platforms: free of charge, premium features are provided ( web version ).

Speedlancer gives you remote work that can be done in 4 hours




The essence of the service is to provide the user with a small freelance work, which takes 4 hours of time. This is an ideal solution for those who would like to quickly earn a certain amount, and who are not alien to such things as design, copywriting and working with data.

Such work does not require any discussion on the cost of work and interviews - you just do some work and get paid for it. And all this - remotely.

Price / Platform: free ( web version ).

Lunchcruit allows you to dine with a potential employer for free




The service gives you the opportunity to ask for lunch at the company you are interested in without any obligations. Just fill out a small application form and then schedule time for a free lunch and conversation. The application is primarily used to organize meetings in the field of modern technology in San Francisco.

Price / Platform: free ( web version ).

You can apply for many jobs by sending a link to your LinkedIn profile.




LinkedIn was previously just the place to put your resume online. Now the service has become a powerful business platform that makes it easy to connect with specialists from the field of interest to you and even publish content in accordance with the direction of your company.

Now the service provides many vacancies for which you can send your application using only your profile. In some cases, even writing a cover letter is not required.

Price / Platforms: free ( web version , iOS, Android).

Freelanship gives you the opportunity to get an internship on a specific project.




The service connects you with an employer who needs an intern for a specific work project. This is a unique opportunity to show your ability to do this or that job. It is also important that due to the service the trainee avoids such attendant duties as, for example, making coffee.

It's simple. The employer needs help on a specific work project, and you need to prove that you can help him. At the end of the project, decisions may be taken on the admission of the intern to a permanent job.

Use Twitter to show that you can remain professional without losing your individuality.




Twitter can get you into trouble, and it can also show you what a colleague you will be. You can use the service to discuss your own and other people's ideas, share funny videos, comment on articles that are interesting to you - that is, do all those things that are considered informal and at the same time far from defining ‚Äúinappropriate‚Äù. JobCV.me - an easy way to create an outstanding resume




This service is one of the most uncomplicated (and at the same time convenient) ways to create a resume that is better than others. The templates provided are modern, but devoid of the hint of madness, which often scares a potential employer. In addition, the result is easy to save as a PDF file.

Price / Platform: free ( web version ).

Wildcard was created by analogy with a typical dating application, only here we deal with business contacts.




The service resembles dating apps for building business connections. You create your personal business card (a kind of online business card) that contains information about who you are and who you are looking for. Then you look at the cards of other people and choose those you would like to meet. If both users are interested in meeting, they will receive each other‚Äôs contact information, which will be included in their virtual files.

The service uses the well-known principle of creating a resume, but teaches it from the other side. Instead of listing work achievements, About.me helps you create your personal page where you can talk about your hobbies. This page does not necessarily have to be comprehensive - just a small sketch of who you are.

Price / Platform: free ( web version ).
