HackDay: Games, Moscow, March 12-13

The third Moscow HackDay is on its way to the capital! His goal - the development of games in two days. It can be any genres and directions, browser games and games for PCs, game consoles and mobile devices. We welcome all projects: from HTML5 Tetris to Duke Nukem Forever for a calculator. You can rob cows. Also invited non-game projects.
You will be able to realize your idea and find like-minded people, or join the already existing team; Listen to our interesting workshops and chat with technical consultants. And, according to tradition, we will have wi-fi, coffee and cookies, good music and a pleasant atmosphere.
HackDay: Games held together with the movement Start! and with the support of the Russian Venture Company .

Venue: Technopark "Strogino", st. Twardowski, 8.

HackDay: Games is a great opportunity to rally a team, socialize and work in a creative atmosphere. We have no restrictions on the profession and subject area. Both developers and marketers, project management managers and representatives of other professions are invited. You will find people implementing a conceived project, get acquainted with new technologies that can be used in the game, and acquire new ideas. And your new idea is your future capital. HackDay Demophest is visited by investors, business angels and funds who select interesting projects and good teams. So we are waiting for you and good luck.

HackDay: Games will open on Saturday morning with master classes. The program includes theoretical workshops from Begin! and practical from ArDoor - they will talk about game development based on augmented reality. Other practical workshops are being specified. Active work will take place on the afternoon and Sunday.
The event will end with a demofest where the developed prototypes can be seen on the big screen. According to the voting results, the winners will receive both symbolic and valuable prizes.
