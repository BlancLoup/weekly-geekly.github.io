CryptoPati! - this is a workshop, the purpose of which is to get the participants a real experience in the organization of trust channels of communication using modern data encryption technologies and replication of this experience.



Story


The movement is based on a powerful foundation created by the 90's encryption. They, however, actively supported and promoted him.

The parent of the format is an activist under the pseudonym Escher Wolfe and his friend m1k3y . They invented to make parties where people will share with each other technologies and knowledge in the field of security and anonymity.

The movement became viral and in a year and a half more than 70 years ( 2012 , 2013 ) parties have passed around the world.

The handbook of the activist is available in English.

Who needs it?


First of all - to journalists, businessmen, political activists, as well as to anyone who just wants to calmly read what he wants to read, love the one he wants to love, say what he wants to say.

Basic set of topics for the report


  • TOR - anonymization of surfing and filter bypass
  • OTR - private communication technology
  • PGP / GPG - encrypt and sign mail
  • as well: zrtp, TrueCrypt, bitcoin ...


Principles


  • DIY - make cryptopaths as you see fit!
  • Sudo leadership - take the initiative for a while, and easily part with it.
  • Financial and political independence
  • Listen to lectures and have fun - invite DJs and have fun
