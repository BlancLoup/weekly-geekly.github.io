THL T11 - a budget version of the famous flagship T100S

More than a year has passed since I first became acquainted with THL phones, namely the THL W7s model. This model made a positive impression on me and showed itself well from an operational point of view. It became interesting what new things can offer, how to surprise and what THL has achieved in this period in the public sector.
It turned out that they have something to please. In this review, I want to talk about the budget smartphone THL T11, built on a new 8-core processor MTK6592 Octa Core with a frequency of 1.7GHz. It is known for certain that the progenitor of this model was the sensational flagship THL T100s.



In size and dimensions, the models do not differ, with the exception of some small design elements (metal frames). THL T11 is a younger and cheaper model, the screen resolution in it is reduced from 1920x1080 to 1280x720 pixels, with the same screen diagonal (5 "). Also, the main 13 megapixel camera is replaced with 8 megapixel. The review contains detailed photos, camera shooting examples, tests and my impressions of exploitation.

Today, THL is one of the largest mobile phone manufacturers in China. High-tech products manufactured by ‚ÄúTechnology Happy Life‚Äù are a great success all over China. ‚ñå DESIGN AND ERGONOMICS


The phone comes in white and black color. The back cover is made of plastic with a matte surface (does not slip in the hands, fingerprints are almost imperceptible). I did not find backlash and squeaks, even with very strong pressure on the screen and the case. Monolithic glass is made flush with the other elements.

THL T11 has an original corporate design with pronounced angular shapes and ergonomic rounding of the side faces. The black color of the case merges with the black color of the screen. The overall look and design creates a pleasant impression of the phone at the level of world brands. The dimensions of the device are: 144.3 x 70.4 x 8.4mm with a weight of 142 grams.



The front panel features a 5-inch capacitive touchscreen, a slot for the earpiece, a front-facing camera, three ‚Äúmenu‚Äù, ‚Äúhome‚Äù and ‚Äúback‚Äù touch buttons, a microphone slot, and proximity and lighting sensors. The THL T11 features a 5-inch capacitive touchscreen display with a high contrast IPS matrix. It has a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels with a density of 294 PPI. The display is made by technology GFF (analogue of OGS) - a technology that combines the display and the sensor / glass into one, which will reduce the thickness of the device, make the image clearer and increase sensitivity.
The display gives a very bright and rich picture with excellent color reproduction. The screen viewing angles are close to 180 ¬∞. Supports multi-touch up to 10 touches. Sensitivity is good, problems with touch-screen did not reveal. The screen is protected by third generation Corning Gorilla Glass tempered glass. At maximum illumination, the screen remains readable under the bright rays of the sun.

‚ñå COMMUNICATION


THL T11 supports work with two SIM-cards (one of which is micro form factor). Interface and SIM-card settings are standard for MTK chipsets. In the settings you can choose the main card for calls, SMS and mobile Internet. Also, the color of the honeycomb is adjusted there. No problems with voice communication or data transfer is not found. The smartphone holds a great signal. Remarkable by the presence of the full range of WCDMA 850/1900 / 2100MHz, in many Chinese phones 1900 is missing.



The phone has a Wi-Fi module of the standard 802.11 a / b / g / n, I did not find any glitches and dumps. The test was conducted directly near the access point, through one and through two brick walls. I also found out that the WiFi module of the phone can work in 5Ghz networks.



GPS works well, at the level of analogs: after 5 seconds at the window I found 10 satellites from 13 possible. Conclusion: the phone can be fully used as a navigator in the car.



THL T11 supports Bluetooth 4.0, with stereo headsets connects without problems and transmits the stereo signal in good quality, without interference and noise.

‚ñå INTERFACE and software


THL T11 runs on the Android operating system version 4.2.2 with full support for the Russian language. The interface flies, applications open almost instantly, turning pages without brakes.

Immediately updated the firmware (04/01/14) to the most recent (06/23/14), a small instruction with links to all the necessary software:
Download:
1) SP Flash Tool
2) MT6592 USB VCOM drivers
3) Latest firmware for your phone
Install:
1) Unpack the archives of the SP Flash Tool, MT6592 USB VCOM and the archive with the firmware
2) Turn off the smartphone. The cable is not connected to the computer.
3) Press the reset button
4) Connect the phone to the computer with a cable. The operating system will find a new device - specify the system folder with the driver MT6592 USB VCOM (for its version of the OS)
5) Disconnect the cable from the computer
6) Run the program SP Flash Tool
7) Click on the Download_Agent button and select the file MTK_AllInOne_DA.bin (it is in the program folder)
8) Click on the Scatter-loading button and select the MT6592_Android_scatter.txt file (it is in the folder with the firmware).

9) Click the Download button.
10) Connect the phone to the computer with a cable. The program should see it and start the firmware.
11) The confirmation of the successful completion of the firmware process will be a green circle with the words "OK". After the end of the firmware turn off the phone.
12) Done.



The appearance of the system is classic and strict in a beautiful black and blue gamut (OTA - wireless update is present)



The firmware contains the main Google services, including the Play Store and other standard Android stock applications (audio and video players, calculators, watches, etc.). Also added: a file manager, notes and a flashlight, and a few more applications.
