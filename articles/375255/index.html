Bitcoin rate dropped significantly at the beginning of the year.

The year 2015 started for Bitcoin is not very good - the currency suddenly fell below $ 300 and continues to fall. Together with the cost, the number of transactions falls (according to the blockchain information).

It is unclear what caused such a sudden fall, because no unpleasant events were recorded.
