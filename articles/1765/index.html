Identified the best modules for Google Desktop

Google held a contest (with cash prizes), which resulted in the identification of the best software modules for Google Desktop .

As you know, Google Desktop is a utility that installs on a personal computer under Windows XP or Windows 2000, provides full indexing of information on the hard disk, and also supports the connection of additional modules, small programs. The most popular such modules are the calendar , flower pot and weather forecast .

At the competition of modules, the main prize of $ 5000 was received by the diGGGadget program, which downloads news from the digg website. In second place is the Multiplayer Reversi game with several players connected. Quite a bit was not enough to close the top three winners, Sergey Polkovnikov from St. Petersburg, with his speech synthesizer RSS to Speech

All software modules are created using the Google Desktop SDK .
