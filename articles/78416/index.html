A little about the prices in freelance

It often happens that you need to make a website, many go to web studios, but some prefer to find a freelancer who does everything for cheap. My conclusions regarding prices for work, which, of course, may vary from country to country, city to city, but the overall picture is as follows:
  • <$ 150 is a newbie who is trying to pull out a hack. As a rule, a student, as a rule, he will not do the work. Why? Because it is not clear that the front of the upcoming work represents coordination and a bunch of nuances, that is why it will throw this thing halfway through. The pros are dubious, the downsides are the loss of time and nerves.
  • $ 300- $ 400 for an uncomplicated project - a novice is more experienced, or a semi-pro. Work is done, but languidly and slowly. Often the sign is a rather impressive work schedule. If time does not burn - the best option. Pros - in principle, cheap (compared with the studios), cons - a long time.
  • > ~ $ 1000 . Here are sitting pros intelligent people, or scammers. With such people it is necessary to conclude an agreement before you do the project, an intelligent pros - will sign without hesitation, throwing - will run away. Work is done on schedule and rarely serious delays. The TZ is compiled, the accompanying documentation and other whistles are written. As a rule, the quality is high and even better than in studios for the same money. Pros - quality, timing, responsibility. Cons - the risk of running into a scam is relatively expensive.
