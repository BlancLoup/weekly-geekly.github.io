Connect to the online translation Unified Communication User Community (UC2)

April 28, 2015 from 19:00 to 21:00 (Moscow time) the first meeting of the IT Community Unified Communication User Community (UC2) will be held.

The meeting will be held in 2 formats:
  • In-person at Microsoft Technology Center - Registration
  • Live Broadcasting with Live Meeting - Registration

Mandatory registration is required for all types of participation.
Address: Microsoft Technology Center (Moscow, Lesnaya str. 9. Belorusskaya metro station)


Program:


18:45 - 19:00 Welcome coffee

7:00 PM - 7:30 PM Optimized and compatible with Microsoft Lync phones. Comparison of models, advantages and pitfalls, examples of implementations.
Alexander Zhuravlev, Beltel Company. Comparison of models optimized (Polycom, Mitel (Aastra), HP) and compatible (AudioCodes, Snom) with Microsoft Lync phones. Demonstration of opportunities live, the nuances of settings, possible pitfalls. Giving examples from real practice.

19:30 - 19:45 Break

19:45 - 20:15 OAuth authorization protocol in Office Suite products.
Oleg Krylov, MVP, Etako company. Analysis of the work of the protocol mechanisms. Configuring Microsoft Exchange Server 2013 Interaction - Microsoft Lync Server 2013 - Microsoft SharePoint Server 2013. Basic and in-depth troubleshooting.

20:15 - 20:30 Break

20:30 - 21:00 Microsoft and IT community - what are we waiting for, what are you waiting for, what is not necessary to wait.
Alexander Shapoval, Microsoft. Prospects for the development of the IT community in Russia and its interaction with Microsoft.
