Pizza Hut will create the perfect pizza for you with eye-tracking technology.



Remember your feelings when you are asked to choose the ingredients for a subway subwoofer? Eyes scatter, I want all the ingredients and sauces at once. As a result, you will, of course, leave a very limited set, but doubts that something is missing or that it was worth something to replace will remain with you. This is normal. This is how human psychology and the body works. In Pizza Hut figured out how to facilitate the choice of the doubters.

The famous pizza chain, together with the Swedish Tobii Technology, is working on the Subconscious Menu (menu for the subconscious), which will understand what ingredients you want to add to your pizza before you understand it yourself.

The thought-reading menu uses eye-tracking technology to fix which of the 20 ingredients offered to choose from the hungry customer wants the most. For this system, only 2.5 seconds is enough. For the ingredients on which the gaze lasts the longest, a smart program will make the perfect combination by analyzing possible 4,896 combinations.
