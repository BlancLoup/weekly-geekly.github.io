Amazon is considering buying webOS



The other day Forbes published interesting information that Amazon is considering the possibility of acquiring webOS, a mobile platform, the future of which is still very vague. The likelihood that Amazon will still decide to buy is pretty high. About this, as always, said "sources close to the leadership of Amazon." In principle, now quite often there are news that some company is going to purchase webOS, but then it turns out that these are just rumors (as is the case with Samsung). However, with Amazon and webOS everything is serious.

Despite the fact that the 7-inch tablet Kindle Fire has won the approval of those who have already familiarized themselves with the device, Amazon is now working on the creation of a 10.1-inch model. It may well be that for this model or for any others that appear in the future, Amazon will decide to use the webOS platform.
