Freelance. Four success stories: left the office and life started

In the last article we talked about four heroes who decided to radically change their lives and went to work for freelancing, in fact, we did not even expect that the material would cause such a resonance and decided to continue. On freelancing, I have since 2013, changed the office dress code to slippers before the birth of a child. And as a personal leap: I have put a lot of effort into self-education, authoring projects, editorial and other diverse experiences in the field of media. I was really scared to fall out of the market when you go nowhere and do not understand what is going on around: knowledge without practice is nothing, and outdated knowledge is even worse than nothing.

You start all over again: even if not from working positions, then necessarily from internal strain and powerful stress. Here you were at home, sang children's songs, and then "hello, cruel world." Three years later, I can assure you: in the world, regardless of the market, so much has changed that to continue working on maternity leave was one of the most correct decisions, and it remains to them today.
