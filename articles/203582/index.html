Contest: Telegram for Windows Phone - 3 million rubles in the first round



Anyone who is interested in developing for Windows Phone!

The number of Telegram users and registrations in instant messengers based on the MTProto protocol is actively growing and in November reached a million people.

In honor of this, Pavel Durov announced the launch of a competition among developers under Windows Phone. Now you have a great opportunity to spend the beginning of winter and winter holidays for the benefit of yourself, your wallet and WP users.

The task is to create an instant messenger based on the open protocol MTProto under Windows Phone. Anyone can participate.
The prize fund of the first round is 3 million rubles.
The deadline is from November 25, 2013 until January 9, 2014.
