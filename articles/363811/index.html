Iphone will fall to the floor like cats

Apple's patent for changing the angle of a smartphone when it was dropped was registered by the United States Patent and Trademark Office. The cat, as you know, always falls on its paws . Apple decided to patent the application of this idea for gadgets. Sensors - gyroscopes, accelerometers and GPS - will determine the angle and speed of the fall, after which the vibration motor can reorient the smartphone so as not to break the camera and display.
