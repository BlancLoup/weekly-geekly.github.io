A shareholder or director of a company can be both a legal entity and an individual, regardless of the place of registration or citizenship.
There are no requirements for the number of shareholders or directors in Belize law.

In Belize, a closed (non-public) shareholder register of the company.

Annual fees


$ 100 - if the share capital does not exceed $ 50,000 and all shares of the company have a nominal value. (i.e., if capital is declared and not contributed);
$ 1,000 - if the share capital exceeds $ 50,000;
$ 350 - if the share capital does not exceed $ 50,000 and some or all of the company's shares do not have a nominal value; and if there is no share capital and all shares do not have a nominal value;
$ 4,000 - if the company has the status of "public investment company".

Fees are paid in the next year, after the year in which the company was registered, until July 31 .
If you have delayed the payment of the annual fee, its size increases by 10% and a new term is given - until October 31st.
If you did not pay the fee before October 31, its size will increase by 50%.

What you need to do to register a company?


Send an official registrar, who has the right to register companies in Belize, with a request to register a company. He will ask you for a copy of your passport. In return, he will provide you with an Application form for filling out (it is not very difficult to fill it in if you are not the first day in business) and will offer to sign a company registration contract with him. After all the documents are ready, will invoice.

How much is the registration of a company in Belize?


The value of a company with a standard share capital (declared but not contributed, with shares that have a nominal value) with the registered agent with which we registered the company, is formed as follows:

$ 100 - registration fee;
$ 175 - payment for providing the Registred Office + Fee of the registrar itself;
$ 75 - delivery of documents;
$ 75 - Apostille (if needed).

Nominal services (for one year):

$ 300 - legal entity, nominee shareholder or director;
$ 400 - individual, nominee shareholder or director;
$ 75 - Power of Attorney from a nominee director or shareholder.

If you want a company with a special form of authorized capital, then paragraph 1) the registration fee will change upwards. The cost of the annual fee is always equal to the cost of the registration fee. In the ‚Äúannual fees‚Äù section I wrote down the details on the amount of payment.

The main areas of application of offshore companies registered in Belize


Belize companies use in their operations some outsourcing companies, startups, call centers, owners of various services that accept small amounts of money from a large number of users, owners of games and applications, companies associated with PPC, CPA and others.

Like it or not, offshore remains a convenient tool for doing business and tax planning.

Immediately I will note that offshore is not suitable for every business, but this is not the current topic.

Such a company is created as a parent company, and with the help of it, subsidiaries are established in countries with open shareholder registers. Thus, it is in some way a holding company.

With the help of such companies, they invest in different IT projects (I have already encountered one such case in practice), or they buy real estate.
