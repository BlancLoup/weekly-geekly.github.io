InterSystems recruits interns

InterSystems opens a new intern recruitment cycle.
We are considering candidates for two sites - InterSystems Russia in Moscow and InterSystems HQ in Cambridge , Massachusetts, USA.
Below are internship conditions and details.

Moscow

In Moscow, an internship takes place for 3 months, in two sessions: winter, from January to March and summer, from June to August. Winter internship can be considered as a variant of pre-diploma internship. Summer can be shifted to July-September. For non-resident students travel to Moscow and back is paid.
Cambridge

For an internship at Cambridge, fluency in English is a prerequisite. Time - from May 29 to August 15. The company provides a US work visa and flight interns to Cambridge and back.
During the internship you will receive the necessary training and will participate in real projects of the company, under the guidance of experienced engineers.
The purpose of the internship is to gain experience in the real application of InterSystems technologies, experience in a team.
Interns are officially employed and they will be paid wages at the level of the company's junior engineers.

Interns can be students and graduate students from universities within the InterSystems Campus .
The condition for acceptance is the successful completion of online testing. Apply for testing here .
We wish you good luck, fruitful and creative work!
