Almost hi-end. High quality speaker system

To create this topic I was pushed through this project (It is of course done incorrectly, but only prof. Columnists will understand me, the filter must be calculated by measuring the frequency response and starting from the case). In general, I thought that this is not interesting, but it turns out there is interest.

Therefore, I want to share with you my design. Description of the project and a lot of photos further ...

The first thing I started with the assembly is of course the choice of speakers. The system was conceived as a 4-way, so the following dynamics were chosen:

- Tweeter (tweeter): SB Acoustics SB26STAC-C000-4



- Midrange (Midrange Speaker): Peerless Peerless 830813 (not a serial speaker, accidentally bought on ibei)


- Woofer (woofer): ScanSpeak 18W / 8535



- Subwoofer (SUBWOOFER): Peerless 830846


Next, you need to measure the parameters of Tilla-Smoll, as well as at least the frequency response of the speakers. Measurements in the construction of acoustic systems are needed as well as the correct spelling of the dealloc method. Otherwise, you will get a box with speakers a bad speaker system.

For measurements, I used the following technique written by my friend, for which a special thanks to him. The parameters of the speakers spread here I think there is no need for anyone interested to see them here (first post).

The next stage is the calculation, modeling and assembly of the hull. I will not go into technical details about calculations and measurements since it is difficult and long.

Also, the system needs amplifiers and a filter. Chose a digital DSP filter . It has a very significant advantage over passive - you can reconfigure the system online. To do this, connects the filter to the USB and through the software that comes to it we set up as you please. Settings are applied on the fly i. when music is playing!
I use channel / multiband (as he calls) amplification, so each speaker has its own separate amplifier. This gives a good increase in sound quality. For LF-MF-HF channels, I took amps on the LM3886 (30W each). For SUBnch took on the chip LME49830 (100W each)
Well, all this should be put in the case, which I bought here .

I just want to say that the most important thing that needs to be understood is that audio is a technology that is based on a solid scientific basis. Audio is not art, and audio products are not characterized by any artistic characteristics with the exception of appearance. Therefore, without measurements of the frequency response, phase response, impedance, Til-Small parameters and other "scary" to do anything worthwhile will not work! Simply take the speakers, make any body for them and put some kind of filter - this will only lead to the creation of a box with speakers ...
