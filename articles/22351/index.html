Original PC or SPECTRUM form factor returns



New computers Zero-Footprint-PC from the American company Cybernet are literally at the fingertips of the owners. At the same time, they are full-fledged workhorses.



Cybernet wanted so much to save space on the customers' desktop that it created a line of computers that do not have a system unit. The entire filling is located inside the input device, namely in the keyboard, which is almost not different from the standard in size.



Three models have been released so far - ZPC-9100, 9100GMP and GX31. GX31 specification: Intel Core 2 Quad quad-core processor with a clock speed of 2.66 GHz (or a cheaper version - Core 2 Duo), up to 4 gigabytes of RAM, SATA hard disk up to 750 gigabytes (optional - quick 2.5-inch hard drive 80 gigabytes), optical drive CDRW-DVD (or Dual-Layer DVD ¬± RW), Ethernet network card that digests 1 gigabit stream per second, DVI connector and dual monitor support, built-in speakers and more.
