How to choose topics?


Firstly, we, of course, follow the trends. And this is not only Scala and React - no one has canceled our beloved PCP.

Secondly, no matter how much we would like to think about the program down, we are doing it first of all for you, which means that we need to know everything about you. Therefore, once or twice a year we launch a survey, after which we instantly begin to understand you perfectly, recognize your professional interests and guess where you put the curly bracket immediately after or on a new line.

Thirdly, we take the formula 80/20%, we run through it the trends and sacral knowledge about you and we get an explosive mixture, which eventually becomes the CodeFest program.

How do we prepare the speakers?


We do not care how everything goes, we care about the comfort of the speaker and your expectations of beauty. Therefore, we help the speakers with the preparation for the presentation. In the compulsory program - two skype auditions, slide run, technical review and course of a young speaker, if necessary.
