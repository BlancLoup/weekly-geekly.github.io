Mappi - panoramas of the streets of Moscow, the region and major cities of Russia

Dear Habralyudi, I want to present you a new site for circular panoramas of the streets of Moscow , the Moscow region, the Golden Ring and other major cities of Russia.



You can give links to panoramas, or you can even embed them on your website!

If you are interested in the details, welcome under cat.


Mappi confirms - there is life outside the Moscow Ring Road!


In the panoramic view of the Mappi are available more than 40 cities of Russia. The most detailed photographs were taken of Moscow, the Moscow Region, the Vladimir Region, Pereslavl-Zalessky, and Kostroma.
Now our cars filmed about 6,000 km of streets. If you put them in a single line, you can get to Baikal or to Beijing.
And we are not going to stop at this, new cities constantly appear on our panoramas.

A complete list of cities: Moscow, Alexandrov, Aprelevka, Bronnitsy, Vladimir, Dedovsk, Dzerzhinsky, Dolgoprudny, Domodedovo, Zheleznodorozhny, Zvenigorod, Istra, Kirzhach, Klin, Kokoshkino, Kolomna, Korolev, Kostroma, Krasnogorsk, Kubinka, Lobnya, Lukhovitsy, Lytkarino, Lyubertsy, Mytishchi, Naro-Fominsk, Noginsk, Odintsovo, Pereslavl-Zalessky, Petushki, Podolsk, Pokrov, Pushkino, Reutov, Ruza, Sergiyev Posad, Solnechnogorsk, Troitsk, Khimki, Khotkovo, Schelkovo, Yubileyny, Yakhroma.

Opportunities for all


Viewing the panoramas can be controlled with the mouse or from the keyboard - the arrows and the keys shift and ctrl. Full screen mode allows you to see the smallest details in all pictures.
Have you seen a poorly glued or too dark panorama? Do not worry, we already know about it - after all, all our panoramas are manually checked, and all the bad ones are queued for re-shooting.


House-egg in Moscow panoramas

In addition to simply viewing the panoramas of your street, your home or just the place where you are going to go, we have prepared some more convenient features for you:

Panoramas on your site


Panoramas can be easily embedded on your website, any registered user can use this feature for free. For example, placing a panoramic picture of your office or point of sale on the website of your online store or photo studio.

Links to the panoramic picture


On the panorama, you can make links that take into account the position, approximation and viewing angle. Moreover, so that you do not have to use link shorteners, we immediately tried to shorten them as much as possible, which makes it possible to give links to panoramas on Twitter without any problems, which we regularly do by showing you interesting pictures.

We are always happy to hear your comments and comments!
