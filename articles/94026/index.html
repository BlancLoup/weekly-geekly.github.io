The integration of RuTube video content into Sony televisions was made possible through Bravia Internet Video technology. New RuTube videos are added to Bravia Internet Video in real time, so users have almost unlimited entertainment options.

By agreement of the parties, the order of watching TV shows on TV can be determined not only by the on-air channel network, but also by the user's personal preferences. Having set up an Internet connection, TV owners will be able to create their own TV program, choosing the best content of TV channels - both the newest and the one that has already been aired.

All TV models for which Bravia Internet Video is offered are equipped with an integrated Wi-Fi module or provide the ability to connect an external Wi-Fi adapter via USB. This eliminates the need to pull additional wires to connect to the Internet and allows you to enjoy Bravia Internet Video without additional settings if you moved the TV to a new location. All Sony Bravia TVs support all existing Wi-Fi signal encryption standards. Therefore, when you connect the TV to your secure home network, you will not have problems that are not uncommon when trying to establish a Wi-Fi connection on other devices.
