Search for the moon on March 8



The biggest greeting card for March 8 is the Moon. In 1971, wanting to congratulate their female colleagues, the operators of Lunokhod-1 drew "8" on the surface of the moon. The path of "Lunokhod-1" is known, and its traces can be found in pictures of NASA LRO satellite. Today, in honor of the holiday, try to find this figure eight.
