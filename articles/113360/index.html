New demopati for Easter in Germany: REVISION 2011 (ex-Breakpoint)

The tradition to make a large and real (no gamers!) Demopati on Easter in Germany continues! Breakpoint existed for 8 years, and was last held in April last year. Time to move on - REVISION 2011.

Some of the organizers of Breakpoint gained new people, really discussed everything to make this new demopati even better than the previous ones and try to incorporate the advantages of other European demoparty into it, and finally chose a new place.

How to get there? The cheapest and easiest way is via Riga, from where Ryanair airlines can fly to Frankfurt-Hahn Airport, where you and other sceners will be able to pick up a special bus going directly to Revision. There are many other ways.
