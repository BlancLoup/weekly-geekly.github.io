My Place and Twitter

The service of Internet bookmarks MyMesto.ru has a new way to add notes - now it can be done via Twitter. (Yes, we can store not only your bookmarks, but also notes and files .)

Twitter has grown in popularity, and many of our users are concurrently Twitter users. Tweeter has created many applications for a variety of platforms, including iPhone, Symbian and Windows Mobile. So, if you already use Twitter on your mobile device, adding notes to My Place will be much easier.

It remains only to link your Twitter account and MyMestO in three steps:




1. Go to twitter.com/2moemesto and ask the user 2moemesto as a friend.

2. User 2moemesto will immediately add you as a friend and send a personal message (Direct Message) with a special link to your twitter.



3. Click on this link to go to My Place and confirm the linking of your Twitter to My Place.

Everything!



Now, to add notes, you can simply send private messages (Direct Messages) to 2moemesto user via any Twitter client.
