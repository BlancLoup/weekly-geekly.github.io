USB flash with fingerprint sensor

The Princeton company introduced a USB flash stick with an interesting name XiaoClef2 . A special sensor is built into the device that allows the owner to be recognized by fingerprint.


The market will feature four models with a capacity of 512MB, 1GB, 2GB and 4GB for $ 75, $ 120, $ 225 and $ 370, respectively.

The protection is certainly interesting, but will there be such desperate buyers who are ready to give for the protection of their 4GB of porn important documents the amount equal to the cost of 3-4 regular flash drives?
