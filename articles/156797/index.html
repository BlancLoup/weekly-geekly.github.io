Why normal SEO does not spoil sites

Search engines began with a trivial search for matches between user requests and content in the index pages, then they added - and this was a Google revolution! - the reference graph of credibility, then ... And then it turned out that this is more than an effective and balanced system. It only needs protection from exploitation through weak points and fine-tuning. Now, quite sophisticated methods are used to protect users from tricks and setting up relevance coefficients ‚Äî right up to machine learning (and this is a form of weak artificial intelligence), but in general, web search continues to determine the relevance of requests to specific pages based on matches in content and link mass. What does all of this mean?

Until a strong AI has been created, the search engine most likely will not be able to understand the meaning of the text. Frequency characteristics were introduced into the ranking formula, which distinguish a text written by a human for humans from a differently varying key word confusion.
