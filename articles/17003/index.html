Ours at Stanford, the second day

During our second day at Stanford, we were able to do much more than the first day .

In the beginning, we visited the technology licensing office. The fact is that the copyright to any research that is conducted inside Stanford belongs to Stanford himself. Yes, including the rights to the Google engine. Those. Google Inc. owns only the rights to the domain name, but the technology itself is licensed from Stanford . Therefore, it was in our interest to discuss the issue in advance so that technologies developed jointly with Stanford would be available to us later. At the first meeting, we agreed with Hector Garcia-Molina to draw up a list of tasks to be developed at Stanford, and then sign an agreement fixing our rights to use these technologies.
