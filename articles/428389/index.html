We invite you to IBM seminars - chat bots, blockchain, machine learning, etc.



Hello! In November-December of this year, in our client center (Moscow, Presnenskaya Embankment, 10) we conduct a series of seminars on IBM cloud services. We invite all interested developers! Participation in the seminars is absolutely free, and coffee-tea-cakes at our expense. ) At the end of the seminar, each participant will receive a certificate from IBM. Limited number of seats.

We will cover 6 topics, including development topics in the cloud, chat bots, image recognition, blockchain, private clouds, machine learning and data analysis in the cloud.

The seminars are conducted in English by experienced instructors of the IBM European Competence Center. The necessary support in Russian will be provided by the Moscow office of IBM.

The seminar will provide an overview of key concepts related to the IBM cloud platform for developers ( IBM Cloud Platform , formerly known as IBM Bluemix).
