Flash 9r48

I came across a discussion of a Linux flash player on the Internet, in which one person said that 9r48 flash worked faster than the current one. According to my observations, this turned out to be true. Anyone who wants to try it himself can find a 100 megabyte archive on the Adobe website with old flash versions for developers: fpdownload.macromedia.com/get/flashplayer/installers/archive/fp9_archive.zip or download only this version from drop.io: drop.io / 2xv7fab and put in the folder with firefox plugins (usually /home/username/.mozilla/plugins). It is advisable to remove the remaining versions before this.
