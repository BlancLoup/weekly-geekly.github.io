Tracks Flow is open


For those who are not familiar with the service - TracksFlow.com is a single access to everything connected with music. Our sources of metadata include:

* last.fm
* Discogs
* Deezer
* Junodownload.com

Files we are looking for:

* tumblr.com
* ex.fm
* soundcloud.com
* vk.com
* promodj.ru

Often, the music that is needed right now is difficult to find. Therefore, to make it easier for you to find a new one, we have made some interesting tools:

* stream (something like a music twitter)
* friendly playlists
* trends
* track / artist playlists

Music properly discussed, so we did everything that you were comfortable to communicate.

And yet, the music should always be accessible regardless of the availability of the Internet. We have an iPhone app that has offline mode. And this means that all your music can be synchronized with the phone and the Internet will not be needed for listening.

VK users can install an extension for Chrome , Safari and Firefox browsers that allows you to add a song from VKontakte to Tracksflow in one click!
