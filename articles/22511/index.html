Top 10 concept phones!

Mobile phone concepts are the realization of the ideas of real designers. They are always on the verge of design art, they have a creative set of options, and the usability functions are transformed beyond recognition. Unfortunately, not all of these incredible ideas are realized in life. But let's see what interesting concepts of mobile phones currently exist.

The advantage of this gadget is as follows: it has a talk button on the phone, so when using it as a watch you can talk on the phone and do whatever you want, since your hands are free for any action. Such a phone can be very convenient for drivers, teenagers, while skating, skating or skiing, children who often lose the phone.

9th place . Sky Mobile "Sleak n Slim" based on the latest technology with a touch screen. What really attracts attention is its design. Its design form is really unusual, but, to be honest, I'm not sure if this form is convenient when using the phone. The manufacturability of this phone is that it has a discretely luminous pen and hidden touch keys. The gadget looks stylish, modern and fashionable.

8th place . Nokia 888 communicator takes almost any form. Its design is very modern. Speaking about the features of this gadget, it is important to say that the phone uses liquid batteries, a speech recognizer, a body touch detector and a flexible touch screen. This Nokia concept phone looks like it was brought from the 22nd century or a fictional film.

7th place . This concept of the phone will help you to always feel the incoming call, because it will be located right on your wrist. A bracelet phone with a built-in MP3 player looks like a fashion accessory, but with some extra features. Keys in the form of diamonds will allow you to work comfortably with the phone. The gadget also has a built-in MP3 player, but this is no longer an innovation these days.


6th place . The CUin5 concept of a mobile phone is definitely something unusual and fresh, because every part of the gadget has a keyboard, microphone and speaker. When using one part of the phone, the other parts are in blocking mode. It looks like its only function is to make and receive calls. The gadget does not have a display, which means that you cannot send messages. An interesting idea, and implementation, but I think it will not find its users.

5th place . The BenQSiemens phone snake looks like an improved combination of the Triple Review and the Nokia 888 Communicator. There are only a few buttons on it, but the shape makes this gadget not very convenient to use.


4th place . At first glance, Dark Label Retroxis phone does not look different from other modern mobile phones. But it has some interesting features. First, it is incredibly bright. 3rd place . The Black Box Concept phone may seem rather cumbersome. But its difference from other phones is that it changes the control panel in accordance with the necessary functions. This is a new idea, and its implementation can find its fans.
