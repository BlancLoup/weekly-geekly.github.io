Google Nexus 4 ended up in Google Play stores in the US, Europe and Australia

According to reports from news agencies and my personal observations, the Google Nexus 4 smartphone, which appeared on November 13 on sale in Google Play stores, was sold out in just a few dozen minutes. In particular, just 10 minutes after the start of sales in the UK store, both models of the communicator were already shown as sold out. In Australia, the 16GB version ended 22 minutes after the start of sales. Similar happened in the USA.

From myself I can say that on the American Google Play, I personally managed to catch an accessible 8GB version of the smartphone, but without even thinking about the possibility of such a rush, I did not order it, since I was waiting for an option with more storage. This very option at that time was (apparently already) unavailable. After 20 minutes, re-checking the offer page, I found that both options were not available.
