Mobile Developer Day: developers present startups

As we have already written , Mobile Developer Day will take place on December 9 in Moscow - a developer day for mobile platforms.

In addition to keynote speeches and speeches by such famous guests as Intel, Nokia, Mail.Ru, Google, Microsoft, Free-lance and others, ambitious Startups will present their ideas to the general public. For them, the organizers made a special offer - free participation and the opportunity to present their project to the audience.

Who already wanted to declare themselves:

1. 33 bucks - Talents Sergey
Personal finance accounting system using a mobile check scanner (a program that is installed on a mobile phone). As well as useful recommendations on savings based on the analysis of the data specified in the checks.

2. infmanagement.ru - Markov Dmitry
Improving the productivity of managers and his team (Companies of the PR / Marketing / Media sector)

3. Mobiety
Innovative survey tool - mobile technology. Possibilities of using a mobile phone for marketing and sociological research.

4. Mobiquest.ru
Online platform that allows private and corporate users to quickly and easily create mobile interactive products: games, polls, tours and more.

5. Mobile Directory
With the Mobile Directory, the mobile phone will become the universal guide to the world of social and economic information.

6. Myrange.ru
New web-based solutions to improve the quality of the organization of the event, increase the opportunities for communication and business interaction of participants, organizers and speakers.

7. Speaktoit
Creation of programs for communication using mobile devices and personal computer, using text and voice interfaces.

If you have an idea, a project, a company that is less than one year old, you only need to write about it. In the letter (md@mdday.ru) specify the date of foundation, if you have an existing project and / or briefly describe the idea and its scope.
In the letter, be sure to indicate whether you are ready to publish information about your project, or wish to maintain confidentiality.

Also at 10:30 a press conference will be held dedicated to MDD and summarizing the 1st round of the Golden Mobile Website Contest. The competition is designed to enable owners of mobile sites to announce their site and application and get support for the mobile advertising network Plus1 WapStart in its promotion.
We invite journalists to participate!

The event will take place on December 9 at the address: Moscow, Vorontsovo Pole, 5a, in the conference halls of the Business Incubator of the Higher School of Economics.

You can find detailed information about the event, the program and register on the official website www.mdday.ru. For unimpeded passage to the conference area, be sure to fill in your profile fields: full name and e-mail address.

Registration fee - 1 500 rubles. (payment halls, coffee breaks and other organizational moments.) Follow the news on the website or on Twitter using the hashtag #mddru ( @mddru )
