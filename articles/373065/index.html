January 26, the conference "Regulation of cryptocurrency in Russia: interim results"

January 26 at 19.00, the Digital October Center will host the conference "Regulation of cryptocurrency in Russia: interim results."

In recent years, statements by the authorities on the regulation of cryptocurrency have been regularly appearing in the media, which were either negative, encouraging or neutral. As a result, the legal status of decentralized digital currencies remained unexplained for many. Therefore, the main task of the conference speakers is to shed light on the legislative regulation of cryptocurrency and to answer the backlog of questions from both users and entrepreneurs on this topic.

At the event will perform:


- Artem Tolkachev, Director, Head of Legal for Tech Group at Deloitte CIS

Theme: "Blockchain: possible areas of legal regulation"

From the side of corporations:

- Konstantin Goldstein, technical evangelist at Microsoft Russia

What questions will be raised during the conference and the round table:


- What is the position of the regulator and law enforcement?
- What is international experience?
- Is there a regulatory framework and judicial precedents?
- How is cryptocurrency regulation organized in Russia today?
- What are the prospects for the development of cryptocurrency regulation in Russia?
- How do Russian cryptocurrency and blockchain projects interact with the regulator?
