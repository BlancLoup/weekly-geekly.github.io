Preloader Generator (Load Indicators)

Problem: create an animated preloader with the original design and the necessary color scheme.
Solution: there is a special resource for generating preloaders http://www.ajaxload.info/ . Choose from the list the type of load indicator (more than 20 options are available). Next - the color of the background (you can make a transparent preloader) and the color of the indicator itself. Click GENERATE. We look. If the result is suitable - click DOWNLOAD and save the resulting preloader.

You can read about other useful tools and tricks on the blog www.bontonweb.com
