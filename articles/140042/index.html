HTC announced a new technology for the manufacture of housing for the smartphone



Manufacturers of mobile devices, especially large ones, are constantly trying to find something new, something that can help make the device even better. It is clear that this is mostly due to the desire to get more money for your product, the desire to get more customers. But still, decisions are very interesting, and relevant. The other day, HTC has introduced a new technology for the manufacture of a case for a smartphone, which can become the main one for this manufacturer.

The technology is called Micro Arc Oxidation. The technology is based on a number of processes leading to the transformation of ordinary aviation aluminum into a material whose texture resembles plastic. But the strength of such a material is five times the strength of ordinary aviation aluminum. Of course, it is metal, but its properties make the device case very reliable. So reliable that HTC developers consider it possible to refuse protective phone cases.
