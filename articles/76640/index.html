The Passenger: 7 minutes in 8 years



The production of this magnificent 7-minute cartoon, went on for 8 years.
Only one person did it, in his free time as a hobby. Work on the project began in Lightwave3D 5, and the Pentium 133 was used as a workstation. A full-fledged cartoon, as well as Making Of, look under the cut.
