Opening Offshore Bank Accounts with Payweb.com



Hello, dear habropozovateli!

This post would like to draw your attention to the possibility of remote opening of full-fledged offshore bank accounts for individuals using our service.

It is necessary to clarify that our service itself is not a bank, but in this case acts as your reliable agent.

Accounts are opened at Loyal Bank Ltd. on the islands of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines.

The advantage of opening an account through Payweb is, first of all, a significantly lower cost of maintenance and commission, rather than when contacting the bank directly. For comparison:
Opening an account directly costs $ 150, through Payweb $ 50;
Monthly maintenance - $ 8 versus $ 1;
ATM cash withdrawals - $ 3.85 + 1.15% against $ 2.

More information about our conditions and commissions can be found here , as well as in the relevant section FAQ .

In addition to lucrative commissions, an important advantage is that your account will be linked to your Payweb wallet, which will allow you to make real money transfer to online and vice versa.

And the last important plus is that we will always be happy to help you interact with the bank and resolve any difficult situations!

An offshore account will allow you to:
- Receive and send bank transfers;
- Pay for goods and services or withdraw cash through a MasterCard debit card;
- Cash out virtual currency with a minimum commission;
- Use an account with your broker for operations with securities and forex;
- Secure your assets.

I will be glad to answer your questions.
