And now about the competition


Together with the Kvantorium technology parks, Epson announces an all-Russian competition for young developers of augmented reality applications.


What kind of "Kvantorium" such?


Surely, many of you remember footage from films about American schools, where children participate in science fairs and present their ideas and projects. In Russia, a similar format is provided by the Kvantorium children's technology parks. They are equipped with high-tech equipment and act as an additional education of children in the field of engineering sciences. They are preparing, so to speak, personnel reserve for high-tech and high-tech industries of the Russian economy!


But back to the competition. Only paired teams can participate: students of technical universities, as well as all students in the Kvantorium technology parks.


That is, if you are a university student and want to participate - you can write to the address below, colleagues from Kvantorium will acquaint you with young talents, and together with them you can start creating your promising, perhaps even commercially interesting project.


And although there are only three themes for applications, the field for fantasy is quite extensive:


  • Urbanism

  • Edutainment

  • Science Art

We will show the best works to enterprises and business representatives. It is possible that some of them will be interested in the commercial implementation of your project.
