Preservation of anonymity when using the Internet in the Moscow Metro

Some time thinking about whether to publish or not, but still decided to try to lay out my thoughts and ideas.
I'll tell you a little about the access points of MosMetro_Free, namely, how to remain anonymous and not provide your mobile number.

1. A bit of theory


When a person authenticates in the metro when joining the MosMetr_Free access point, an activation code and an activation link come to the specified number:
cdn.vmet.ro/auth/activate.html?code=98765&phone=79250251234&idmac=123456
where
code - device activation code;
phone - mobile phone number;
idmac is a unique database entry associated with the MAC address of the device.

You can follow this link and activate it from anywhere, just by being in the subway. This makes me happy.
