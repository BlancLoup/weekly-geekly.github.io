Full archive of Einstein's manuscripts



The Hebrew University of Jerusalem finishes scanning and online manuscripts of Albert Einstein. Now on the Einstein Archives Online website about 3000 scanned images are presented, by the end of this year their number will grow to 7000. The full archive of Einstein exceeds 80,000 records, including letters, travel notes and other personal documents, such as a school certificate .

All documents are recognized and indexed for full-text database search . Now there are about 43,000 documents in the index, by the end of the year the base will be complete.
