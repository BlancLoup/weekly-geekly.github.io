Application Developer Days - for creative programmers

December 13, 2013 in Minsk will host the fourth international conference Application Developer Days.


Application Developer Days is a conference made by programmers for programmers. For those who directly write code, thinks through the application architecture and tries to increase their productivity using new languages ‚Äã‚Äãand libraries. In addition to the programmers themselves, the conference will be of interest to all those who are involved in the process of creating software products, who want to understand what developers are doing, to look at everything from the point of view of a programmer.


A good developer knows that the set of tools that he could apply in his work is constantly expanding. New languages, libraries, products are coming out. What else did not have alternatives yesterday is obsolete today and is losing out to competing instruments. It is the technical leader who has to follow the innovations and introduce new tools in his team.

Alone it is rather difficult to cope with this task. Especially when you still have a lot of tasks on the main project, and this is how it usually happens. It would be nice to consult with someone, but for this you need to ask the right questions to the right people. Where to find these people? Of course, all people are on the Internet, and all the materials on all-all technologies, and all the news about all the updates, and another 100,500 reviews from those who have tried it, but they have failed.



The only problem is that reading the entire Internet will not work. You should either already know what to read, or you will be lucky to find useful information in the free search mode. Actually, an overabundance of information on the Internet is the main reason for the existence of conferences today (it used to be different).

The idea is to gather people who know your subject in one place and make them talk to each other. Someone talks, others listen. Then, often, change places. The conference is exactly the place where the programmer can disconnect from the daily routine and think carefully about what and how he is doing.

Within Application Developer Days-4, there will be a discussion of a whole range of issues related to software development, choice of programming languages, consideration of successful architectural solutions and recommendations for their creation, consideration of the most popular technologies, products of well-known vendors and Open Source solutions.
