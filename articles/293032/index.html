So, let us consider in practice the principles of earnings in infobusiness.


In theory, the scheme of work is quite simple. Naturally, any deviations from this system are possible, but the general idea is exactly that.
There are five key concepts: Base, Traffic, Cover, Offer and Content. They can be called anything but the meaning does not change.

Base - a way to interact with customers. For example, your website and subscription base.
Traffic - sources of customer acquisition. For example, Yandex.Direct
Cover - useful material, as a rule, free. It is necessary for the info-businessman to earn trust and prove his authority for the client.
Offer - motivates the client to pay for services and get much more useful information.
Content - information required by the client. Served in various forms: books, video courses, personal training, etc.
