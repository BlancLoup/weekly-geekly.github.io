Microsoft launched a massive ban of stitched X-Box 360

On GamePlay write: Microsoft started banning the flashed Xbox 360 in Live. I myself did not try to connect to the network, but many people have already suffered - when I try to log in to Live, I get an error and write that the console has been banned. What is strange - while the bans are selective, many are still online. Banned consoles continue to function normally and read pirated discs, but they cannot enter Live. Nobody deleted any accounts either, they work on unmodified consoles. In general, until everything finally becomes clear, I recommend not to enter Live, perhaps, new firmware versions will solve this problem.

From myself: in general, we either get a ban (seemingly on the console ID) and play pirates offline, or roll back the firmware back to our native and use the license. The first option, perhaps, will not live long - a hole will help to close a thread of the update from the game disk ... The only thing worth waiting for is the firmware update.
