ATTENTION: TONER!


I suspect that in some places, if you try to even get close to a pneumatic tube to a toner press, the unlucky cleaner will be immediately disarmed, deprived of a bottle, pay and turned out outside the perimeter. Cleaning of fine particles is a special discipline.

The first drew attention to the problems of cleaning the toner user cyberly , he also pointed to the professional class of products such as "service vacuum cleaner." For example, a 3M Service Vacuum product removes toner, but it costs like five Golden Eagles :)

But then etz et al came along and reminded us that the main components for cleaning toner are a replaceable filter cartridge and an antistatic hose, and pressure can be obtained in a cheaper way. Cartridge costs about $ 20. By the way, as well as car vacuum cleaners of the lower range, but for toner they cannot be used even as disposable ones - only fine particles will be inflated around the room. Black toners have a conditional particle size of more than 1 micron, colored toners - from 0.3 micron. Accordingly, 3M offers two types of filters: Type 2 (cheaper) for black toner with dust and Type 1 (more expensive) for any toner, including color.

In the era of 3D printing, the creation of nozzles, nozzles and adapters was easy, but I would choose conductive materials like conductive ABS : dust does not stick to them, they are antistatic. If it turns out to put this construction on the Berkut, and (most importantly!) If he has enough traction to blow the 3M filter, I will definitely share with dear readers. While I doubt it.

findings


  • Automobile generator will not give enough power to install a filter for fine dust and especially toner.
  • Fine dust easily disappears from the car, but indoors is a problem.
  • No need to try to clean the room itself with a car vacuum cleaner: the less it works, the less fine dust it raises into the air.
  • Quickly clean one or two PC enclosures with Berkut is quite possible by helping yourself with a pneumocylinder.
  • Allergy sufferers and asthmatics, who are in close proximity at this moment, are unlikely to be delighted with their sensations.
  • Cleaning toner, as well as cleaning machinery on an industrial scale in terms of life safety, requires special equipment: a service vacuum cleaner.
  • It is possible to assemble a toner (service) vacuum cleaner with your own hands, but factory components for a toner vacuum cleaner will be needed, first of all, filters and an antistatic hose.
  • For advanced DIY adherents, I recommend using electrically conductive (conductive) ABS plastic, available including. for 3D printing.
