Today Skwibl is a service for communication between a designer and a customer. All those who worked with designers know how difficult it is sometimes to explain their vision. A startup is a big responsibility and a lot of stress. A small team must solve many difficult and often not familiar tasks. Therefore, it is very important to love what you do. Love on weekends, love without money, day and night until you meet a younger startup with a bigger budget =) No, seriously, you should enjoy what you are doing.

If you fail to do this, you apparently are doing something wrong, because the accelerator with partners (and sympathizers) constantly provides an opportunity to recharge your mood and have a good rest. The main thing is not to miss such moments and to notice them in time. If you still miss the most interesting, I will tell you what to do.

1. Talk with a successful person you trust.

On the path of start-ups, you will always have doubts - are we doing this, and is it really necessary, can you try something else? Do not hesitate - ask for advice from someone who has already helped you somehow. For me, Svyatoslav Vakarchuk became such a person, it was his work on quasi-integrable systems that helped me become interested in supersymmetry and choose a topic for my dissertation. Conversation with him added confidence and helped me not to give up. By the way, Svyatoslav also sings great!

Famous physicist, author of a robot with quasi-integrable systems - Svyatoslav Vakarchuk

2. Do not forget about the sport.

Going headlong into programming, you can completely lose shape sitting in place. Therefore, it is very important to remember the sport. Boxing champion Wladimir Klitschko helped us refresh our memories.
Communicating with Vladimir replaces a good workout, he is interested, asks difficult questions, praises or doubts the answers. After this, you feel invigorated. Although you should not forget about the gym, yet these people do not come every day =)

World Boxing Champion - Wladimir Klitschko

3. Participate in contests.

A good way to understand if you are moving in the right direction is contests. It is fun, interesting and makes you think creatively.
And even if you do not win, a place in the semifinals is also very nice.

Awarding of the winners of the international IT conference IDCEE 2012

4. Learn something new.

Concentrating on a startup and its problems, you sometimes stop to find a way out even from simple situations. In such cases, do not get hung up, you need to forget about everything for a while and discover something new. Do not hesitate, education is fun, especially if you are trained in the funniest incubator in Kiev (and Kiev region = P) Happy Farm . For me, the lectures of Bob Dorf became a real rest, and besides they gave answers to many questions that tormented me.

Co-author of the best-selling book The Startup Owner's Manual, Bob Dorf, shares the experience of creating startups in the Happy Farm incubator.

5. And most importantly - always be yourself.

No matter what happens - ups, downs, successes or failures, always be yourself. Joke, enjoy life, do not forget about love and everything will be fine with you!
