Appearance and inside


The kit is small - a device, wire 3.5-3.5 15cm and waste paper. The battery was disdained, but the batteries are not a shortage.


So what is this little box?
This is a miniature parallelepiped with two entrances above and below. Both at 3.5mm. The output with a headphone icon is equipped with a red diode that comes on after plugging in the headphones. Side door for AAA battery.
No buttons, no switches - turned off by pulling the headphones.

The case is plastic, glossy. Scratched on the first day, but given the constant life in your pocket - there is not much difference.

The inside is the National Semiconductor LM4917 amplifier. Honestly, this does not tell me anything, but if someone understands, maybe it plays a role.
