In the main working time we are engaged in playing music. Completely different: from popular and easy pieces to the most complex classical works. The headquarters building has a special studio with an orchestra, where rehearsals take place.


I was not allowed to shoot the rehearsal itself, but after the end - please.

Standard rehearsal goes like this: picking up, adjusting for a general tone and several sets of rehearsals for 45 minutes with breaks for tea and rest. The work process is strictly regulated by the time of labor law and is violated only in consultation with the musicians. The directors in the musical theater in which I worked before often tried to break it. For some reason, they had the impression that we were just sitting and doing nothing. And working for an extra hour is not difficult for us.

In fact, working in an orchestra is a physically and mentally complex process. Imagine that your lungs should work like a compressor, giving an outrageous pressure for the average person that hits the head. Add to this a constant auditory control, so as not to fly out of the group and rhythm. And visual, where one of your eyes looks at the notes, and the other at the conductor. All this must be done at the same time, while pressing the right buttons. Yes, and it is advisable not to overstrain, but the sound suffers. Another hour?

Why am I doing this?

Pause. Oh, I have them! I spend almost half of my working time counting pauses. The picture is this: part of the work, I am silent. Then at a strictly allotted time I play a piece of music. I am silent again. I play again. And so to the end, just observing the temporary vertical. This is probably the whole art - to be able to count huge pauses in the background, without being distracted from the book or phone.

The pipe is a tool with a wide range of technical, dynamic and sound capabilities. In addition to the ability to count pauses as a background, you need to be able to read and write what is between the lines. A pack is in this case an ensemble chord game. Those. we in 4 pipes perform a chord sequence of 4 sounds, where everyone plays his part as a melody. And so that it sounds bright, juicy and beautiful, we have to give everything to the fullest.
It is necessary to take into account the different degree of hearing of sounds of different heights.
