Prototyping in Axure RP PRO 7

I have been doing prototyping ever since Axure became known in Russia. Since 2008. And the design is much longer. Although it is possible to call that work design a great stretch. Rather, the writing of technical specifications, and the design for the simplest sites, in functionality is inferior even to modern promo.

I had to learn everything myself, reading books, experimenting on my own projects, training on others. Changed and complicated technology, and with them, and tools. And the only program that I could not find an alternative to is Axure. She allowed all the way to quickly prototyping anything and clearly demonstrate the result to the client and the other participants in the process.

If the work of a designer can be compared with the work of an architect working with drawings, then the work of a prototyping specialist is more like a 3D artist who makes a virtual three-dimensional layout of a building according to these drawings. According to this layout, you can walk around and see how the building will actually look like before it is built. With the drawings it will not work. If we continue to draw analogies, the drawing of the building is the functional specification of the project. And all the accompanying documentation is already a complete technical task. And the more complex the project, the more complex these three components become and the less they can live without each other. However, I seem to be distracted.
