Deutsche Bank Java School

Duration: immediately indefinite employment in the Technology Center
Cost: Free
Advantages: regular work and high wages from day one, offices in Moscow and St. Petersburg

The salary of Java-developers is 30-40% higher than the average for the market, and at the Technology Center of Deutsche Bank, where they develop modern banking products in Java, they can hire even without programming experience in this language.

All the necessary knowledge will provide on a monthly intensive in Java-school. There will be many lectures and seminars by leading IT specialists of the bank, plus in-depth practice involving participation in the development of real projects.

To get to the program, you must have a completed technical education or be a last year student and be able to work with one of the object-oriented languages.

Applications are accepted here . By the way, young specialists from the regions can also submit an application - the first round of selection will be held online.

To participate, you need to know Python, Ruby, Bash, Java or C ++. And, it is important to be able to attend classes during working hours.
