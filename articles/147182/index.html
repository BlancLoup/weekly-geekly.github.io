The third free school iOS developers in St. Petersburg

Since August 1, the summer free school of developers for iPhone and iPad begins its work. The course is designed for novice programmers and will be held for the third time.



To study at school you need:

  1. fill out the participant's application form and send it to ios@e-legion.com ;
  2. receive in response to the test task, which must be completed and sent to the same address before July 23 .

Classes will be held 2 times a week in our office, on Wednesday evening and on a Saturday afternoon. Duration 2-4 hours. The lecturers will be the leading iOS developers of e-Legion. Over their shoulders - many years of experience and many implemented projects of high complexity.

All additional information is available on the website mbltdev.ru .
