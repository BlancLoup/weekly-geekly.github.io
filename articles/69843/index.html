Russian HyperVideo now works in Flash

Actio (ie, we) announces the launch of hypervideo technology on the Adobe Flash platform. A new solution to make the video interactive is presented in the form of a plug-in to any existing flash-player. The technology is easy to install, does not change the existing characteristics of the player and provides maximum opportunities for broadcasting in an interactive format. Previously, the technology was available only on the Microsoft Silverlight platform.

An example of hypervideo technology on the Flash platform can be found at www.actio.tv/flash.html , as well as on our website actio.tv

Soon, all videos will be transferred to the flash and presented for viewing, both in Silverlight and Flash.

Our plans for the future:

In the near future, a completely new system of contextual sales through video will be presented. Thanks to her, when showing the usual (non-interactive video), instead of the usual post-rolls and banners, interactive videos can be shown in which the viewer can click on the product, view it from all sides, leave a comment inside the video and immediately make a purchase in the video itself, without leaving the site. This technology is especially beneficial for online stores, as in this case every video played on the Internet becomes a potential showcase for them, through which the user can order goods.
