Scarlett Johansson starred in the film version of "Ghost in the Shell"



Recently demanded actress Scarlett Johansson has signed a contract for filming the famous anime film "Ghost in the Shell" in the film adaptation. Reports about it The Verge. The film will be shot by DreamWorks, which is now led by Steven Spielberg. Spielberg admitted that he was a fan of the work and had long wanted to make a film based on his motives. As expected, the director of the film will be directed by British director Rupert Sanders (Snow White and the Huntsman). William Wheeler will write the script. The release date of the film is not yet specified.
