For a while, Xiaomi released only logical switches of the smart button type from the kits described above, but market demands were too obvious not to make the switches at the same time as the physical contact breakers and with the ability to control through the application and configure scripts.

For those who need the same, but with mother-of-pearl two keys, take the switch Xiaomi QBKG03LM Aqara Wall Switch ZigBee Version .


Say a little about the protocol ZigBee. This is a communication protocol for devices of some integrated systems of a smart home, including Xiaomi. It differs from Wi-Fi and Bluetooth wireless technologies in several important ways. The most important of them is energy efficiency. ZigBee transmits up to 250 Kbps per second. Any reader will say that this is just turtle speed, but for components of a smart home there is enough of it - but due to this, there is a solid saving: standard cell-batteries in the components serve for a couple of years, the components themselves also have an increased service life. ZigBee can easily go into sleep mode, and due to this it becomes even more energy efficient.

The sensors of the listed devices are synchronized at a frequency of 2.4 GHz and can ‚Äúcommunicate‚Äù at a distance of 22-25 m. Thick concrete walls can become a hindrance, take this into account when selecting a full set of devices.

Developers and engineers have access to the ZigBeeClusterLibrary ZCL library, in which about 200 presets for smart home components are available in the open form. At the same time, radio modules are incredibly cheap. In general, engineers in many countries of the world appreciate ZigBee for reliability: when one or more components break, the system continues to work and coordinate within itself. Although, of course, the final ode will not work - as long as the protocol has problems with standardization. Well, this is if someone decides to start your project.
