The Moscow Government opens co-working center to help small businesses.



Good day, dear habravchane. I want to share amazing news for those who like to work outside the office and start-ups - on Monday, January 28, a new Moscow co-working center was launched in test mode. Supervises this site GBU "Small Business Moscow". The project is entirely their personal initiative, and in the future it is planned to create such centers (possibly smaller in size) in each district. The co-working center is located not far from the Pervomayskaya metro station, on Srednyaya Pervomaiskaya street, on the third floor of the house number 3 (BC Mayskiy).

Everyone can use the services of the center absolutely free of charge! But, unfortunately, only until the end of February. In March, the organizers will decide on prices, a list of all services and will begin to charge for the use of space in the center. Most likely, there will be standard tariff plans for such establishments: for a day, a week, a month + the cost of services may vary depending on the package of services used. Government co-financing of the project gives hope for a more democratic pricing policy, in contrast to private co-coaching.
