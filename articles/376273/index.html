Russian approach to vibro-dynamics Party Fon MAX

To the review of vibrodynamics , called Party Fon MAX , and provided to the test by the company Dadzhet, a number of questions were asked in the comments that are peculiar to the inquisitive mind of the Russian person, such as:

  • vlreshet : I wonder what will happen if you attach two such devices to the same surface and include different tracks?
  • vlreshet : I wonder what will happen to the sound if you attach the device to the bottom of a bucket, or a three-liter jar, for example
  • isden : It is more interesting to include the same track, but to organize a variable delay on the same dynamics.
  • hungry_ewok : It is more interesting to try to make a resonance
  • dadget : attach them to each other - who wins

You will find answers to these questions in this note, and besides, I did some really useful things, I made a real stereo vibro-system out of two such speakers. (Lieutenant, set aside!)




vlreshet : I wonder what will happen if you attach two such devices to the same surface and include different tracks?

The effect is not the same as if you include two tracks on two different audio systems. The sound does not just mix, but also distorts the other. Two different tracks at the same time and so is not the most pleasant thing, but here it turns out frankly disgusting :)

vlreshet : I wonder what will happen to the sound if you attach the device to the bottom of a bucket, or a three-liter jar, for example

At first it seemed to me promising, I tried on a can and a large saucepan - the result was disappointing, the main role was played by the size of the surface, and best of all, that it was flat. Maybe the effect of the gramophone can be achieved, but the banks and pots for this role do not fit well. If anyone has a real gramophone, we can cooperate and try to make partyfon-gramophone-MAX.

dadget : attach them to each other - who wins

To answer this question, my friend and I staged a musical battle: Infected Mushrooms vs. Aria. They included two tracks on their phones at full power, and connected the speakers with silicone pads. The result was very interesting. Both speakers are very sausage, all the vibration was transmitted to the hand. It seems - as if you are drilling a wall with a perforator. So, not only musical groups and speakers take part in the battle, but also the hands of the fighters.
My hand gave up first. But I prefer to believe that it was not youth that won, but Aria.
