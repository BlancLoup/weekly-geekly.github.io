Modules Build and Resources ##


SAP Business ONE includes a basic platform for easy production (more precisely, to say the assembly). On the basis of sales documents or MRP forecast, you can create production orders automatically. To create a production order based on the Sales document, you can use the Verification Assistant, which provides step-by-step instructions for the entire document creation process. You can start the wizard by selecting the Purchase Document check box in the Sales document, or directly from the Production module in the main menu of SAP Business ONE. In addition, in the production order you can see which Sales document is associated with the document.



The Resources module is an extension of the Build module. Unlike goods, resources have available capacity for a certain period, which can be assigned to production orders. The consumption of resources in the production process increases the total production costs, which can be divided into basic types of costs for further accounting.



The following changes occurred in the Build module:


  • In Specifications and Production Orders (besides products), you can now include resources and text strings. A new invoice NP column has also been added, in which you can assign a NP invoice by default to each product or resource. The account specified here is used in released production orders and vacations to the assembly. A new column Additional quantity allows you to enter the consumption of resources and goods, in addition to the consumption defined in the Specification. In the Specification window, you can reorder the rows using the up and down arrows. The established order is reflected in production orders. In the specification, you can add custom fields that will be reflected in production orders.


  • Component management for specification: This feature allows you to update specifications in bulk. You can add, delete, or change strings in custom specification ranges.
  • The Production data tab has been added to the Product and service guide window for entering assembly information.
  • The "By-product Handling - Manual Vacation" method is now supported for by-product components in production orders and is reflected in the receipt from the assembly. This new feature allows you to manually update the cost of by-products.

Framework agreements ##


How many people know that SAP Business ONE allows you to fix the framework contract to reflect the long-term agreements for the sale or purchase, concluded with business partners? You can create two types of framework contracts:


  • General framework agreements: for example, to receive a special premium at the end of the year or upon reaching a certain turnover of transactions with a business partner.


  • Special framework agreements: used to obtain a special discount for a separate purchase or sale transaction. They are also used to determine the delivery schedule, for example, by specifying intervals at which specified quantities of goods should be delivered.


You can associate sales documents with the framework agreements, which are used as a basis for forecasting expected revenue, reservation and volume planning.
and purchase. In addition, framework agreements are taken into account when calculating cash flow and planning material requirements (MRP, MRP).



Storage places ##


SAP Business ONE supports warehouse storage management (cellular storage). The function of storage bins can be activated for individual warehouses.



With admission, vacation and relocation, SAP Business ONE allows you to automate the process of assigning goods to storage bins. Assignment can also be done manually.


Functions available when activating storage bins:


  • view information on goods in stock, such as the quantity of goods and the number of goods;
  • limiting the destination of storage bins, for example, by a separate lot, product or group of goods;
  • restricting the use of storage bins, for example, only for goods receipt or distribution;
  • replenishment of stocks when storage levels fall below a certain minimum
  • support of group generation, updating and removal of storage bins in order to simplify the process of managing storage bins
  • management of serial numbers and batches in storage areas. When assigning serial numbers and lots from storage bins, you can choose to pick the goods by storage bins or data from the serial numbers and lots. In addition, information on storage bins can be viewed in reports on operations with serial numbers and batches.

Several units of measurement (UI) ****


SAP Business ONE provides the ability to manage products with multiple units of measure (UI). Using multiple UE allows you to sell, purchase, accept and ship products to any desired UI and determine UI by default by type of operation. All sales, procurement, and inventory documents support items with several defined UIs.



This feature includes the following features:


  • use of generic and product specific UI without limitation;
  • setting up UI groups with conversion rules from basic UI to alternative ones by groups;
  • the use of these UI groups in the directory of goods and services, marketing documents and inventory documents;
  • IT of sales, procurement and inventory by default and automatic conversion to other applicable IT during processing;
  • appropriate expansion of the packaging, EAN code and pricing functions:
    Packaging settings for several UE are included in the product and service directory and are used in sales documents to determine the recommended number of packages;
    for a product with several UE, you can define several EAN codes and different prices for each UE.
