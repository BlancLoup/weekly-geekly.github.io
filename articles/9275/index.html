Microsoft was defeated near Moscow

Victor Zudenkov, the manager of the Unitechnoplast plant accused of illegal production of disks with Microsoft software, was acquitted by the court of Lobnya. Microsoft, acting as the injured party, intends to appeal the verdict to the Moscow Regional Court.

Another court hearing took place in the court of the Lobnya town near Moscow in a criminal case against Viktor Zudenkov, general director of the Yunitekhnoplast plant in November 2005. The judge of the Lobnensky court Stanislav Rozhkov told CNews that the defendant was acquitted on procedural grounds. A representative of Themis refused further comments, confirming only that Microsoft, acting as the injured party in the case, could appeal the verdict in the established legal order.

The reason for the acquittal is the same as about six months ago, said in an interview with a CNews correspondent Alexander Fear, a lawyer at Microsoft. The judge considered that the case had been initiated in an improper way and had been investigated by an inadequate authority.
