Open Moko - Linux based smartphone

FIC, a company engaged in the production of motherboards, has released a mobile Linux-NEO1973 machine with phone functions on the Open Moko platform with all the ensuing possibilities. Compile the kernel, write your programs, install what you want - the system is completely open except for GSM / GPRS-components and AGPS (responsible for GPS). But even these proprietary components have an API, so their capabilities can be used.


Hardware

    screen: touch 2.8 "VGA (480x640) TFT
    Samsung s3c2410 SoC
    GPS: Global Locate AGPS chip
    Ti GPRS (2.5G not EDGE)
    Unpowered USB 1.1
    microSD slot
    2.5mm audio slot
    2 buttons
    Power supply: 1200 mAh battery (charged over USB)
    128 MB SDRAM
    64 MB NAND Flash
