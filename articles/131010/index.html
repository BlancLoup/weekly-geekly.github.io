Non-standard phone Pantech Pocket



Now the market has a huge number of models of smartphones and ordinary "dialers" (although there are fewer dialers), but all the phones are more or less alike. Of course, there are such devices that are very different from others, but still there are not so many such devices. But the smartphone from Pantech, called Pantech Pocket , is different from everything that is now on the market. By the way, before I did not see the cellular production of this company. But about 7 years ago I saw phones manufactured by Bosch. Let's look at some of the characteristics of new items.

So, the Pantech Pocket is a fairly productive and functional device, with a 1 GHz processor, 4-inch diagonal display and 600 MB of internal memory. But the aspect ratio of the device is very unusual - 4: 3. The size of the device is 114.7 * 78 * 11.3 mm, and the phone weighs 132 grams, not so much. Android 2.3.4 works here as an operating system. And yes, there is 5 MP camera, plus the device is capable of writing video in HD quality. The RAM here is 512 MB, also pretty well.

So far, it is planned to release Pantech Pocket only under the AT & T operator (respectively, only in the USA), but it may well be that soon there will be models that are not tied to the operator. Actually, it is not so difficult to untie the device, so on EBay and other services all this can be found soon after the release.
