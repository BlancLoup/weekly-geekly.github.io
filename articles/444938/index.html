Submission of the 3-NDFL declaration to the FTS


You can file a 3-NDFL declaration online to get a tax deduction for treatment, to buy an apartment, and for some other expenses, but then still go to the tax and stand in line, because the declaration was somehow crookedly received at the FTS. It turned out that it was a bad idea to use our own ES (issued, of course, by the accredited Ministry of Communications TC) when signing the declaration. As a result, one of my declarations with the attached files did not "pass", because The attached files were somehow crookedly signed. I had to delete my ES from the office, ‚Äúgenerate‚Äù ES from the FTS, with saving to their servers (securno, yes) and after that the declaration was sent to me without problems and registered with the FTS also without problems. But in the end, time is still lost, because the FTS, by law, reviews declarations for no more than 3 months. So with each re-sending of the corrected declaration, the term begins to be re-considered. By the way, one of my declarations just disappeared, and when I sent it again I received a message that such a declaration already exists, although it is not displayed in my account. It was necessary to indicate the correction number in order to resubmit the declaration. Getting a second passport


You can also apply online for a second passport , edit the photo for 2 hours, and then come to the MFC and find out that the required window is open until 17:00 and only on weekdays. Perhaps this is the most useful service, because allows you to get at least something tangible, while at the time of registration online you can save 1,500 rubles by spending 3,500 rubles instead of 5,000 if paid through public services.
