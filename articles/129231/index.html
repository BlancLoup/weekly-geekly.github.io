3D technologies of the future: Sony's Personal 3D Viewer HMZ-T1

Sony is finishing the development of a unique headset - Personal 3D Viewer HMZ-T1 - a futuristic visor that allows you to watch 3D movies and play 3D games at a new level. The model worn on the head in the manner of a Star Trek visor is equipped with two 0.7-inch OLED panels with a resolution of 1280 √ó 720 and headphones simulating 5.1-channel sound.
