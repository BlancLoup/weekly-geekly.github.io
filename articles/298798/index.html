FAS fined Google and Mail.ru for advertising



Today, the Moscow Department of the Federal Antimonopoly Service (FAS) in Moscow found Google and Mail.ru guilty of violating the law "On Advertising". As a result, companies were fined - Mail.ru LLC is obliged to pay 100 thousand rubles, while Google LLC is 150 thousand, as reported by the FAS representatives in an official statement.

The reason for the imposition of a fine was advertising LLC "Ruscredit" on the proposal of the contribution of consumer funds. The ad was distributed through the Google AdWords and myTarget ad networks, for which Google and Mail.ru are responsible. According to representatives of the antimonopoly service, it is companies that should be responsible for moderating the advertised ads.

In the case of Ruskredit, deposit services were actually advertised from a company that did not have a banking license.
