Timelance.ru - exchange of services and goods: the launch of the beta version

Yesterday, February 10, 2010, the official launch of Timelance.ru was announced - the All-Russian exchange of services and goods. The aim of the project is to create a single information space between the performers and customers of services in various fields of activity.

Freelancers seem to be the most accessible to understanding, because on Timelance.ru customers and performers can find each other by analogy with remote work exchanges, but the project‚Äôs feature is not only in searching for jobs in the near-computer field. The site provides the widest possible choice of service categories - from auto-service to jurisprudence (total 32 headings), including classic freelance.

With regards to goods, it should be noted that the project provides for an auction system for the sale of goods, as well as a system of transactions with a fixed price.

Judging by the first feedback received from the forums , users gave the project "Green Light", which of course can not but rejoice us as the developers of the project. However, this is only the beginning of a long, and probably somewhat thorny, but painfully interesting road for the development of the project.
