Month of use of the IBM cloud server: free opportunity to get acquainted with the Softlayer platform



Not so long ago, IBM invested more than a billion dollars in the development of the IBM Cloud cloud platform, including hosting Softlayer. IBM Cloud is a powerful enough complex of cloud technologies and services, allowing to solve a wide range of tasks for customers of the company.

Softlayer is a hosting that provides real and virtual servers for high-load sites and applications. In this case, Softlayer allows you to build your server yourself, using the appropriate designer. The amount of RAM, the number of processor cores, the amount of internal memory - all of this is set independently.

Now you can try out the cloud version of Softlayer service for free , and not a couple of days, but a whole month.

For familiarization with the service, the initial configuration of the server is issued:

CPU: 1 x 2.0 GHz Core
RAM: 1 GB
Storage: 25 GB (LOCAL)
Public Bandwidth: 5000 GB Bandwidth
Primary IP Addresses: 1 IP Address
Uplink Port Speeds: 100 Mbps Public & Private Network Uplinks

When configuring an order, you can select a suitable data center (several regions of the United States, Western Europe, Central and Southeast Asia), as well as one of several available operating systems for your server.

When ordering a trial server, you need to indicate a valid credit card, and if you do not turn off the service, but continue to use it after a month, the funds will begin to be debited from the credit card.

As mentioned above, you can order and real, and not just a virtualized server.



You can do it here , and IBM now offers a discount of $ 20 on this service (for dedicated Xpress servers based on Intel Xeon processors). In addition, the opportunity to increase resources is provided.
