The rebirth of an idea

And after the resources were released, I decided with my partner to once again risk my money and launch the project of the flash games and applications partner program - NextGame.RU . The idea was reborn in the fact that we take not just ordinary games, as well as games and applications from the social. networks. Those. And we can offer applications and games for sites suitable for their audience. For example, for dating, horoscopes, psychological tests, one-on-one games; for women's resources - arcades, quests, applications with various recipes, the calculation of critical days (joke), etc. etc.

The financial scheme has also undergone changes compared to the original ideas - these are games that work according to the Free2Play scheme, i.e. You can play for free, but for additional bonuses you need to pay. Also, this model allows you to leave the audience on the site, i.e. without dropping it to third-party sites.

Support sociality outside soc. Nets

But we are talking about social games, i.e. This support for profiles, friends, communication between them, writing on the wall, viral mechanisms, etc. The idea was that we would provide the developer with all the possibilities. This is achieved in various ways, which I will describe below.

We also developed our own API for sites, which allows us to mark our catalog of games right on the site. And there are 3 levels of integration with the site:
- The most elementary (first) - place the code of our "frame" with the game on the page of the site, like a regular counter. Those. Our platform will assume all functions, including authorization.
- The second level of integration allows you to maintain site authorization, so that the site visitor does not have to log in again in the game. Also at this level there is the possibility of integration with profiles, friends, messages, etc.
- We carry out the third level of integration with very large sites - this is when we use their billing. At previous levels, our billing is used, from our NextPay.RU payment aggregator .

Out of the vicious circle
I initially assumed that we would get into a regular vicious circle consisting of:
- owners of web resources, having come to them, they will of course be asked to show a list of games and applications (which is quite logical)
- developers, having come to them, they, of course, will ask if not the list of sites, then at least the daily audience (DAU)

We solved this problem by launching a new small team that would make games for our platform, and for classmates, VKontakte, mail.ru. Of course, the games should not have been so big. The tasks before them were quite clear: a certain number of games with simple logic, and, if possible, traffic generators. Those. making money was a secondary task for them.

The task is less fulfilled. We made eight applications, the quality and the games themselves do not always suit me, but the goal is fulfilled. Now they face a goal to achieve self-sufficiency, i.e. this is the development of better-quality games, and most importantly, income-generating games.
