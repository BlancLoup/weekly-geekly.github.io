BB-Mobile Techno 9.0 3G Tablet Review

Recently, many manufacturers have put on the flow of cloning iPads. That is why it is nice to see the devices with the original design (not from Apple). This time, the Techno 9.0 3G tablet from BB-Mobile, which had previously entered the market with gadgets like door peepholes and tablets for children, became a topic for review. Immediately, I note that the resolution of the 9 "display is 1920x1280, and the battery capacity is 7500 mAh.

Operating system: Android 4.2

Memory Card: MicroSD up to 32 GB

Connectors: MicroUSB, Jack 3.5 mm, MicroSD, SIM card, mini HDMI

Equipment


The tablet comes in a fun green box. The device lies in a cardboard lodgement surrounded by foam rubber, a lid is also lined with foam from the inside - the manufacturer clearly made sure that even the Russian Post could not kill the tablet.





Grade extremely rich. In the box with bb-mobile Techno 9.0 3G fit cable from USB to microUSB, charger, wired headset, cover and cleaning cloth, OTG-cord, instructions and warranty card.



Under the film with information about the device, a protective film is already attached at the factory - a pleasant and useful bonus.





Wired headset with headphones frankly surprised, and then disappointed with the sound quality. It is comfortable and it is enough to use the tablet as a phone or chat on Skype, but do not count on listening to music in good quality.



A rag is a good thing, but not very necessary - there are no traces on the tablet body, and the glass, as I wrote above, is already protected by a film. Another great bonus is a soft case.



Unfortunately, charging the tablet from microUSB will not work - you have to use the charger, which is included in the kit. When will the full standardization of this connector for all gadgets pass?



The tablet can be used as a phone.





It is possible to talk on it without additional devices, if you are Denis Tsyplenkov, or you can use a headset or such a mini-phone - it is not included in the package, but it is suitable for any Android devices.



Ergonomics


BB-Mobile Techno 9.0 3G is not a clone, unlike several other BB-mobile tablets, which is already pleasing. Original design, looks decent.

The back cover is made of soft-touch white plastic, perforated and does not slip out of the hands, even if held with two fingers. The weight of the device is only 497 grams.





The 1W speaker is located on the back wall. Ibid - 5-megapixel camera. At the bottom left is a hole giving access to the reset button.





The paired volume buttons are located on the upper edge of the case, next to the power button.



On the bottom are connectors for microSD, charger, 3.5 mm jack, mini HDMI, microUSB and SIM card. There is also a microphone.



The side faces are freed from any buttons and ports. The narrow frame makes the tablet more miniature.

On the front side placed the front camera and light sensor. There was no space for hardware or touch buttons.





It is worth noting the build quality - no backlash and no squeaks. Here are just a silver frame around the perimeter of the tablet can wipe.

Camera


The tablet is equipped with two cameras. The front one is 2 megapixels, which is more than enough to communicate on Skype. The five-megapixel main camera allows you to make decent for the tablet shots. Images are clickable.





Iron


Techno 9.0 3G is built on the basis of a quad-core RK3188 processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz. RAM - 2 GB, built-in - 16 with the ability to expand using a microSD card with a capacity of up to 32 gigabytes.

Built-in WiFi module with support for 802.11 b / g / n, as well as Bluetooth 4.0. There is a mini HDMI connector. Battery capacity is 7500 mAh.

Working hours


For the first battery test, the capacity of which is 7500 mAh, I used this application . For 3 hours and 50 minutes, 70% of the charge was consumed during the operation of the CPU, GPS, display, and continuous vibrating. The schedule is clickable.

Total


The tablet looks decent, is not a copy of the iPad and has a high-quality bright display. The device is suitable for those who are going to read on the road, surf the net, watch movies and play toys.
