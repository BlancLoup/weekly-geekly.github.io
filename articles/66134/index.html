Promo site - to make simple, but impressive

Hello everyone, I just want to start with the process of creating one of our promotional website compressed in a few minutes (the work, by the way, was extremely exciting):


www.pointblank.ru

And so, it all started with the fact that the task appeared to make a promotional site for the new mmo-shooter from NCsoft, which is published in Russia by us - the Innova company.

At the same time, as almost always, there was very little time, and they asked to make it ‚Äúcool‚Äù. Actually, since we believe that it came out with dignity, we want to describe further a very simple technical method, which, with its ordinariness, allows you to make quite spectacular sites.

Everything is very simple and everyone knows - you can make a video from a series of pictures. From several beautiful photos, we make using flash the effect of moving around the fighter of the game Point Blank.



I probably incorrectly put it when I said about the ‚Äúdescription of the technical method‚Äù, since everything is so obvious that it is easier to tell very briefly how the site was made:
  1. During the discussion of the problem, our designer Denis comes up with the concept of moving around the details of the fighter's ammunition, everyone likes the idea.

  3. Further, we take a lot of pictures and process them; pay attention, before you go to the studio - you need to know exactly which key frames you will use.
