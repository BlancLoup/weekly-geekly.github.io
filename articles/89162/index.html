Review 30 image hosting for quick publishing

A list of image hosting sites is presented, selected for an unusual, but in special cases important feature - the ability to publish a photo without authorization on the site. Photo hosting sites, which claim that they are ‚Äúfree‚Äù, but at the same time forget about this important degree of freedom, are ruthlessly screened out, but somewhere at the end of the review, links to the most popular ones are given. Other important conditions for entering the list are not forgotten - reliability and storage time, usability and the absence of intrusive advertising. Subsequently, it is worth choosing from the entire list of 5-7 most worthy and convenient hosting sites.



The special cases for which this review is made are the publication of a certain image (pictures, drawings, photographs, screenshots, graphs) on other (not own) resources, especially where there are no solutions for publishing photos (Habr including) or they are inconvenient . These are articles on foreign resources or in their blogs on foreign hosting (for example, blogger.com) with inconvenient or defective picture management, on online auctions, comments on blogs and forums.

Hosting for this publication provides text links in the form of HTML-, BB-codes or direct links to the image and page view, preview (they are also thumbnails) of various sizes and a number of other amenities. Consider them all and arrange the hosting found in descending order of averaged convenience. At the end, as a bonus, links to some other online image preparation resources, which also follow the optional authorization rule.


Research history


Recently, some hosting was introduced for public advertising purposes, which caused a number of unobvious doubts about its respectability. Meanwhile, ideas of using it as an image service for publishing on Habr√© were heard. There was a counter-question: which image hosting, in particular, for Habr, is better to use?

First, several hosting sites were investigated using the example of a single image. It was found that the convenience of work for different hosting sites is different, and the results (by weight of the full picture, type and weight of the preview) are different. It is not clear that in this ‚Äúindustry‚Äù there was a quality standard; everyone is striving for convenience and stop at different lines. There are few reviews of hosting sites [1] (all of them are also important as evidence of their former reputation), give an answer only to some of the questions. Therefore, it was decided to go into testing more thoroughly, to figure out what is really needed, by priorities, and what can be donated. It turned out that there are not 5-10 suitable hosting sites, but 4 times more (about 30), despite the fact that it is 2 times more screened out (40-60). The list of hosting does not claim to be complete and does not recommend anything to use - it simply cites data on the listed hosting, and the data speaks for itself. It is likely that temporary unstable services got here and more worthy ones did not get. Therefore, in the future, the table will sometimes be shuffled according to the results of checks.

For example, in the future it is not bad to carry out load testing of each of the hosting sites [4], to make download attempts several times at different rush hours, to monitor the availability of each hosting for months. All this, of course, is not done immediately. We take advantage of the fact of publication, to then monitor the services and monitor the quality of storage.

Target service niche


They are not designed to show small service drawings of website design (some allow), for this purpose, regular universal hosting is used. But they are trying to ensure the quality and reliability of displaying important for the user graphic information. On the reliability of the show, even their own development hosting will lose to them in the long-term existence of the link.

We are interested in hosting with some hard conditions. For example, there is a mandatory authorization - then you can not watch (because there are enough hosting with the possibility of anonymous publishing). Working only with the client program or inconvenience without it is the same. No preview size options - great reasons to choose other alternatives. Yes, and any restrictions of the free version of the account do not go to benefit, because they distract for unnecessary control: will my old photos not stop showing if any threshold is exceeded? Nevertheless, they are worthy of consideration and choice of tastes.

The required services are only a part of the overall image processing task. The previous parts (editing, capturing) are not deliberately considered, although some editing may end up in the hosting service. We will not look at too simplified and integrated services like gyazo.com, in which little attention is paid to the quality of details. They are good for one-time viewing of screenshots by a small group of people, but they lack tools for publishing (for thousands of views and quality requirements). Therefore, by default, we assume that we have a graphics editor and a screenshot taker, and without what they can do, we can do without.

Image hosting is divided into photo hosting and, conditionally, technical image hosting. If the first ones are focused on photographers and other ‚Äúordinary people‚Äù who may not have their own site, then the second ones suggest publishing photos (or screenshots, pictures) in blog or forum entries, and therefore do not seek to give their own gallery service for viewing. The first are fully capable of working in the niche of the second. Therefore, the strongest hosting in the world is designed for the first type - for all. As payment, they all mostly limit free accounts in the rights and duration of storage. We are interested in both reliability and non-restriction, so we will have to consider not only recognized leaders.

We list, in descending order of priority, the necessary and desirable qualities of service. At the beginning of the necessary, then under the line - desirable.
