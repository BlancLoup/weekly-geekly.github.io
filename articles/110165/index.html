LG Optimus 2X: the world's first Android-smartphone with dual-core processor

LG officially unveiled its Optimus 2X (LG Star) in South Korea


Key Features:
Android 2.2 (with the ability to upgrade to 2.3)
Dual Core 1GHz NVIDIA Tegra 2 (Cortex A9)
NVIDIA Tegra 2 is the second generation of Nvidia Tegra, combining the functions of a whole computer in a single chip (system on chip), developed by NVIDIA as a platform for the production of mobile devices.
Processor: 2-core ARM Cortex-A9 MPCore with a frequency of up to 1.0 GHz
Memory subsystem: 32-bit LP-DDR2, DDR2 memory
Graphics from NVIDIA: OpenGL ES 2.0 (2-fold increase in performance when working with 3D graphics compared to the previous generation Tegra)
Multimedia data in Full HD:
- 1080p H.264 / VC-1 / MPEG-4 video decoding
- Encoding 1080p H.264 video simple profile

A huge 4 inch display with a resolution of 800x480
Two cameras: the main 8-megapixel, shooting in FullHD and front 1.3 megapixel for video calls.
1500 mAh battery
Also HDMI output
