Hot root or how to get a full-fledged Russified HTC Hero with Google applications from the "cropped"


Many stare at the phone on the Android OS.
And, among the available options, HTC Hero is for many a cherished dream.
Unfortunately, for some reason, Google does not like Ukrainian citizens and does not allow officially selling phones from the Android Market in Ukraine - even with its free version ...
And besides, there are many phones available in the gray market from other countries in which there is no Russian or also no market.
But, the beauty of the Android OS is in its openness ... So, under the cut, I will try to tell you how you can flash the phone with full-fledged firmware with full-fledged Android Market, Russification and the possibility of buying paid programs.
This manual is particularly relevant for the official Ukrainian HTC Hero, but is suitable for owners of any Hero who want to change the firmware of their phone or open access to additional functions of their phone.

  • Everything that you do, you do at your own peril and risk and no one will bear any responsibility if you do something wrong and your Hero turns into a brick (the probability is minimal, but who has few curves?


The whole process of unlocking and flashing the phone can be divided into 2 main stages:
1. Firmware update Recovery- special menu for repairing and flashing phones, which will allow to flash third-party firmware (not from HTC)
2. Directly firmware third-party firmware.

1. Firmware Modified Recovery
You should start by checking the firmware version of your phone. It depends on how hard the process of ‚Äúhacking‚Äù the firmware will be. After the firmware version 411.5, Hero closed some holes and it became much more difficult to unlock the phone. To check the version, go to Phone Settings - About phone and look at the firmware version:
  • If you have firmware 411.5 or earlier, I congratulate you. Unlocking your phone is almost no problem - for you Option number 1 :
  • If you have a newer firmware, but the phone is Russian official (PCT sticker) - Option 2 for you

Option number 1 (the easiest) Suitable for phones with old firmware.
Installing third-party recovery - It will allow you to install updates without a digital signature. So go:
You must have Internet connection on your phone, preferably Wi-Fi, since download will have about 5 megabytes.
  • Go to the phone settings and in the menu "Applications" put a tick in front of "Unknown sources." The answer to the warning is affirmative.
  • Open the browser and in the address bar type r.androidfan.ru/flashrec.apk (~ 83 Kb). Then press Enter, download the application and install it. You can install flashrec without the Internet, then you need to throw flashrec.apk into the root of the SD card. Then open the browser and enter the following perverted address:
    content: //com.android.htmlfileprovider/sdcard/flashrec.apk
  • Launch Recovery Flasher, it should be first in the list of applications.
    In the input field there should be a link to r.androidfan.ru/recovery-hero.img (~ 3.5 MB) Click ‚ÄúDownload Recovery Image‚Äù and wait while it downloads. As an option, if there is no wi-fi at hand and it's a pity to waste precious traffic, then you can put recovery-hero.img on a USB flash drive, and in FlashRec in the address bar type /sdcard/recovery-hero.img
    We make backup of the old Recovery to the memory card, for this we click "Create Backup Recovery"
    We press "flash Recovery", if everything is successful - then the inscription "Firmware done" should appear. Congratulations, you got a pass to the world of third-party firmware

Option number 3 (the most difficult)
If you have a non-Ukrainian (Chinese, Vietnamese, etc.) phone or option number 2 issued a Wrong CID, then you will only save the creation of GoldCard , which will allow to flash your phone with official firmware from any other regions.
For this you need:
  • Computer with SD card reader + Sd-MicroSd adapter
  • HxD Hex Editor
  • MicroSD card with capacity from 128 MB to 2GB (native card from camplet is not always suitable for operation) SanDisk flash drives often do not work with this method, my method worked only with transend microSD for 2GB

Step 1. Find out the unique serial number of the card. To do this, install Terminal Emulator (http://r.androidfan.ru/terminal.apk) on the phone, launch it and type the following commands:
ls /sys/class/mmc_host/mmc1/
we get the answer like: device mmc1: XXXX (XXXX is a unique number)
after that we give the command:
cat /sys/class/mmc_host/mmc1/mmc1:XXXX/cid
For example, my number was 1b534d303030303010b1de9d11008860
upd: How correctly prompted, we turn the code: for this we download the QMAT from here. Attention-demo version only works 10 minutes. Select Cryptoanalysis Tools then Crypto Toolbox. At the bottom of the form there is a text field. Enter your sid there, click Reverse String. two characters replace 00-for example 12345678910 will be 00345678910
Step 2. Go here revskills.de/pages/goldcard.html and enter your email and the number you received, send you a file (goldcard.img) to your email that you need to write to the beginning of the flash drive.
Step # 3 Through the card reader and the adapter, insert the card into the computer, format the USB flash drive into fat32
Copy the file to it from here: files.androidfan.ru/flash/hero/RUU_Hero_HTC_RUS_2.73.411.5.rom.zip , after renaming it to heroimg.zip
Step number 4. Run HxD Hex Editor. In it, in the top bar, select Extra> Open Disk. In the Physical disk column, select Removable Disk, remove the check mark from ‚ÄúOpen as Readonly), and click OK.

Go to Extra - Open Disk Image, and select goldcard.img, which we received in the mail (when opening, select "Sector Size" 512 (Hard disks / Floppy disks), and click OK.

Then from the goldcard.img file we copy all the items with 00000000 - 00000170 and paste them into the Removable Disk (your hard disk) with the replacement. Then click on the save and vaul- Your GoldCard is ready!
