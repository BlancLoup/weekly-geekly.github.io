HDD Seagate Savvio 10K.4

Seagate has released two new Savvio 10K.4 HDDs. Drives are made in the 2.5-inch form factor and are characterized by a spindle speed of 10,000 revolutions per minute.



The lineup includes two hard drives of 450 and 600 GB. Devices withstand loads up to 40g for 11 ms in the operating mode and up to 400g for 2 ms in the off state. Interface - Serial Attached SCSI (SAS). The data transfer rate is about 140 MB per second. The stated time between failures reaches 2 million hours. The new products implemented technology Seagate PowerChoice, which allows to reduce energy consumption.

Seagate is positioning new HDDs for use in high-performance servers and storage arrays. Unfortunately, the cost of new products is not yet known. As the start of deliveries.
