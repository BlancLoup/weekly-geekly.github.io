Search LiveInternet - not for Ivy and Nosik

The LiveInternet portal, which unites the statistics collection system, the blog platform and the dating service, has updated the design of the main page, coinciding with this moment the test launch of its own search engine.

The new search engine is not going to compete with Yandex and Rambler, and even more so with Viktor Lavrenko , says the head of LiveInternet.ru, German Klimenko. At the moment, this is a kind of study of what can happen if we combine the search on sites and statistics.
