The truth about cars on Yandex. Auto

For most people, buying a car is a serious step. And they usually prepare for it thoughtfully and thoroughly: they buy automobile magazines, read safety ratings and long lists of technical characteristics, ask friends ... Many people are interested to know what car owners themselves say about the car they like, who have seen not only a beautiful advertising picture, but also everyday without embellishment . Yandex users are really interested - we receive many requests of the type every day [Ford Focus reviews].

Now thousands of interesting reviews from the sites of our partners are collected on Yandex.Avto. We show them on those pages where they are especially in demand.
