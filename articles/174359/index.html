Wolfram has his own ideas about the future of humanity and technology. One of these programs is nanoshield . It focuses on the protection of humanity from self-replicating nano-weapons. Specialists from the Lifeboat Foundation are studying various materials that could serve as the basis for the creation of such devices. Radiation and acoustic protection are now considered the most promising.

Other experts at Lifeboat focus on one of the most popular stories in Hollywood - protecting humanity from an asteroid threat. The asteroidshield program is working on preventive methods that could one day save our planet. The battered decision - the destruction of an asteroid (as in the film Armageddon ) - in reality is impossible. To test the hypothesis Lifeboat consider it necessary for one of the space or military agencies to attempt a controlled change of the asteroid's orbit by 2015.

But perhaps the most interesting project of Lifeboat is protecting us from strength, which we still do not fully understand. AIshield is a program that develops a solution to protect humanity from unfriendly-tuned artificial intelligence. According to Lifeboat, full-fledged artificial intelligence with capabilities beyond human capabilities will appear in the coming decades. Scientists believe that some of these creatures can be dangerous and lead to tangible negative consequences for the world. The result of the research is one of the most popular topics in popular science literature:

Such killer robots can be created extremely ruthless, and built for this purpose can turn against those who created them. Like the most dangerous weapons of mass destruction, be it nuclear bombs or biological weapons, robot soldiers can eventually destroy their creators.
