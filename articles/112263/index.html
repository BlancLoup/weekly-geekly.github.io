Runetologiya-TV, Issue 3. Rutube.ru: video interview about video hosting

The success of RuTube confirms the saying that everything ingenious is simple. The video service, created as a clone of YouTube, has grown into the largest resource on video hosting in RuNet and a few years ago passed into the possession of Gazprom-Media.

It is always interesting to hear how such a giant grows from a small project. Mikhail Ilyichev, the general director of RuTube, has a well-defined theory on this subject. As always, in Runetology , third-party experts express their opinion about the project - it is not always positive, but it often touches on acute questions for everyone. For example, how is the legality of content laid out on RuTube regulated? In connection with the recent lawsuits against the user "VKontakte", this question probably worries everyone. What is RuTube as a business? How is his collaboration with Google built?

In addition, you can always learn something useful for yourself. For example, we liked the theory of the finiteness of the Internet - makes you wonder.
