Case: how we returned our good name from Apple

Many people know that the iTunes Store is Apple's online store from which users download applications. However, not everyone knows that once having specified the name of their organization, the company-developer cannot independently change it in iTunes. From the word ‚Äúin general‚Äù. How did we manage to solve the problem?

For us, this was critical, because at that time we were running the latest version of the Smarty CRM service. The option ‚ÄúOblachnye Tekhnologii OOO‚Äù in the international market did not suit (the application is used in 176 countries). Therefore, for the sake of the brand, we decided to go far.

The difficulty is added by the fact that when a company name is given for the first time, no documents are required for this. But if you need to update the name, you need patience terabytes, an English-speaking employee and a lot of documents confirming that you are you.

The name change took a month and a half. How did we go this way?

To start, they sent a letter to itunesconnect@apple.com, as well as through the form on the website , where they clearly stated the request: rename ‚ÄúOblachnye Tekhnologii OOO‚Äù to ‚ÄúClouds Technologies LLC‚Äù, as this corresponds to legally valid company documents. And it is better to always have access to the iTunes-account, from there often request information in such cases.

Then we called Apple support for developers and reported the case number. The list of phones is here . Please note that you will not be consulted in Russian, communicate in a foreign language. We talked with several offices, but stopped in German.

He was chosen because we and they could communicate in both German and English. It was a great relief, as managers from other offices speak with a lot of emphasis. In total, 5-6 Apple managers spoke to us, who replaced each other. At the final stage, there was only one.

After several sessions, it turned out that they were expecting from us:

- extract from Incorporation (original and translation);
- a request from our employee, who acts as a Team Agent iTunes-account
- answers to 9 questions.

The questions are as follows:

1) The previous name of your company: Oblachnye Tekhnologii OOO
- Previous name of your company: Oblachnye Tekhnologii OOO

2) Your company: Clouds Technologies LLC
- Your new company name: Clouds Technologies LLC

3) Does the app store have any apps?
- Do you have apps on the App Store that are already there or that are in the process of checking?

4) Has the new company bought the original company?
- Has the new company bought or acquired only the applications of the original company?

5) Has the original company been dissolved in?
- Was the original company liquidated or is it still in business?

6) Has a whole business been made?
- Has the new company bought or acquired the original company entirely?

7) The legal entity has changed, or has the legal entity also changed?
- Did the original company change only the name or did it also change its legal status (form of ownership)?

8) Has the company undergone an address change? If so, please provide your new address
- Has the company changed the address? If yes, please provide a new one.

9) Does your company have a tax registration number? If so, please provide your new address
- Has your company got a new tax registration number (TIN)? If yes, provide a new one.

Support also offered to create a new account and make a transfer . But there is a requirement that "the application should not have current (or completed) offers of subscriptions to built-in purchases: automatically renewable, free or non-renewable."

We have 3 business tariffs inside and subscribers to them, what to do with them then? Therefore, rejected this option.

We sent everything we needed, after which we were simply refused. Rationale: Clouds Technologies LLC is a translation from Russian of the name of our company (Cloud Technologies LLC). The argument seemed strange to us, since there is nothing illegal in the correct translation of the company name.
