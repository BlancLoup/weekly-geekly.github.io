Olympiad FUPM MIPT programming for schoolchildren

Some habrayusers have already participated in programming contests.
For example, in May , ABBYY Cup . Now on the same server, the Olympiad of the Faculty of Management and Applied Mathematics of MIPT for schoolchildren is taking place. Students can also participate, but only out of competition, especially since the session is now. There will be other contests for students.

Winners will receive prizes and souvenirs from the faculty and sponsors. The tournament is available until January 15 .


At the moment, in the contest 20 problems, new ones will be added gradually. The Olympiad is held according to the Kirov system (that is, points are brought even by a solution that passes only part of the tests) at Ejudge. Languages: C, C ++, basic, yabasic compiler, perl, pascal, Java, C #, ruby, python, fortran. The source code of the program (not executable and not the project file) is sent for verification. The check is automatic, so no windows and input prompts are needed, but strict compliance with the data format specified in the task is required. Input from the standard input stream (from the keyboard), output to the standard output stream (on the screen).

Tasks of different levels from the simplest (from tests for beginners and trial rounds of Olympiads) to very complex (the level of the semi-finals of the ACM ICPC World Championship) for everyone to be interested. Contest compilers are coaches and part of the mipt_waterogers team, ACM ICPC finalist 2010-2011 and 2011-2012. All of us are graduate students and graduates of the Faculty of Management and Applied Mathematics of MIPT.

We wish you success and hope that you will enjoy the tasks! Participate (if you are a student) and invite familiar students of grades 7-11. Details and registration and instructions on the verification system with pictures on the site judge.mipt.ru

Update: Ask questions through the testing system.
