Telegram bots collection for geeks



Hello! Messengers demonstrate the highest level of user involvement compared to all other applications in other categories. In this regard, there are changes in the formed user experience paradigm. @GitHubBot




GitHub bot connects to your repository and notifies about all sorts of events in it. Moreover, it allows you to respond to comments without leaving Telegram. I also want to mention @gittrendsbot , which broadcasts the trend sheet of the github.

@GetStatsbot




Analytical bot that integrates with Google Analytics & Mixpanel and displays the necessary metrics directly to the chat. Statsbot supports all available dimensions, segments and custom targets. Works in inline mode. Want to know where the registrations came last week? - "signups last week by source". What countries were mobile device users from? - "users this country for mobile".

@Temp_mail_bo




Mega useful bot that performs one simple function: generates temporary mailboxes. Each address is deleted after 10 minutes.

@Pomodoro_timer_bot




Pomodoro is one of the most common techniques for increasing personal effectiveness and I use it myself. The essence is simple: the work is divided into periods of 30 minutes, which are called "tomatoes", where 25 minutes of work without distractions and 5 minutes of rest. Pomodoro Bot automates the control of this technique. He measures the "tomatoes", sends notifications and shows statistics for the day.

@Wolframbot




Bot search engine that will answer almost any of your questions, based on the knowledge base Wolfram Alpha. Wolframbot recognizes queries in natural language.

@Pollbot




A great solution for conducting a survey in a team. Pollbot easily allows you to create a survey in a group chat and provide statistics with the results of the answer.

@DrWebBot




Secure your chat from malicious files and links. When a virus is detected, the bot provides a link to the threat description. The file size limit is 10 MB.

@YTranslateBot


Bot-translator based on Yandex technologies. It is noteworthy that the bot works in inline mode, thanks to which it will help you understand a foreign language in any dialogue.
