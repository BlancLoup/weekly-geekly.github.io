China. Plant devices for "Smart Home". Learning and learning

The Russian market of professional automation equipment for home and building (aka Smart Home ) has always been filled with European and American brands. European companies whose products can be found in hardware stores - ABB, Schneider Electric, Legrand, Gira, JUNG - and American companies whose logos show off on touch panels in Class A offices - Crestron, AMX - have been working in this market for a long time, equipping expensive homes "Smart" features. But the market is growing, equipment needs a lot and production has to be moved to China. Not surprisingly, some of the Chinese companies that previously specialized in OEM manufacturing used the money they earned to create their own R & D units. And that means their own unique brand.

The HDL plant, which invited us to speak at its annual training for distributors and dealers (in Guangzhou), became the first Chinese brand to dilute American-European dominance in our market. In 2007, they began manufacturing their own line of Smart Home equipment, in 2009 they entered the Russian market (for them it was the first foreign experience). In 2015, their equipment is sold in 82 countries of the world, and their R & D unit of about 40 engineers releases new items more often than any competitor.

HDL is our good partner. The installers of this equipment are one of the fastest growing sections in our summary sales table. Some of these satisfied people are directly involved in the process.



This post is for those who have anything to do with the phrase "smart home" and are interested in how seriously the Chinese approach the issue of the production of appropriate equipment. This is not a record of updates or advertising anything. If you want to see what this plant does - go to its website or to the websites of Russian distributors.
