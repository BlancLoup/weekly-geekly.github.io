TrainCamp: Muscovites, going to BlogCamp together!

I suggest that BlogCamp participants in Moscow go to Kiev together, in one train - a compartment car. To do this, you need to check on the site of BlogCamp ( http://blogcamp.com.ua/ ) in the section "TrainCamp from Moscow". The earlier the better.

Objective: Meet and start the Camp itself is on the way. Moreover, those who wish and who love to sleep in trains will be disappointed. On the way, communication with Russian and Ukrainian border guards.

Departure - October 12 (shampoo, which pleases) in the evening from the Kiev railway station. The train and the type of car (coupe or reserved seat) will be selected based on the comments left in the corresponding section on the wiki. You can see the train options here .

The average cost of a reserved seat is 800 rubles, a coupe is 1,500 rubles. If the majority of reserved seats, then you need to buy in the coming days. If for a coupe - it waits another two weeks maximum. Go from 12 to 14 hours. Wi-Fi in trains of this direction has not yet been organized, but with GPRS it is normal - the network does not disappear very often. So communication can be optionally supported by live examples.

If there is uncertainty, you can buy a ticket, and then either hand it over, or exchange it to another participant (hand over and immediately buy in the name of another participant, loss - a hundred rubles if done beforehand).
