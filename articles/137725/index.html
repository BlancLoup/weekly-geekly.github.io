Image Catalyst 2.2

Hello Habr!
Adobe Photoshop CS5 (Save For Web) - 53.8 KB Image Catalyst (Xtreme) - 46.0 KB
Image Catalyst is a program for integrated optimization / compression of PNG and JPEG images without loss of quality within the same format.

Key changes


In the new version, basically, the changes affected the script of the project, and not the optimization of images. When creating a new version, we took into account the advice and desires of users. The main innovations can be considered:
  • dialog box support;
  • support of parallel operation of two or more copies of the project;
  • selection of a folder for saving optimized images with preserving the structure of subfolders.
For a complete list of changes, see Changelog.txt .
