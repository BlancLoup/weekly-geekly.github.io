Introducing Google Developers Live

Arkady Khachkovanyan, Marketing Manager, Google Russia
The annual Google I / O conference is an event where we talk about our latest developments. Google I / O 2012 will take place next week and will be held for three days, during which we will show our latest technology and people who have been developing them. For conference participants, we also organize meetings with Sandbox partners who develop their business using our API. Developers around the world will be able to follow the events of the conference thanks to the direct inclusions of I / O Live . We want to share knowledge, tools, and tips from experts in the Google developer community, not only for three days in June, but throughout the year.

We are sure that communication with Google experts and fellow developers can inspire you to create amazing applications. Therefore, especially for developers from around the world, we are launching live broadcasts from Google Developers Live . On the portal, you can find interactive programs that run live - from game shows for developers to events in the office, where you can chat with the engineers who created your favorite Google products.

As part of Google Developers Live, we will hold seminars for beginning developers on completely different topics - from Android to YouTube. This will allow you to prepare for more complex content, which we will present next week. If, after viewing these seminars, you have any questions, you can ask them to our speakers in a special time.

Join Google Developers Live - http://developers.google.com/live , to mark today on the calendar when you can chat with our engineers and speakers, see app reviews, presentations of a variety of Google products, and participate in programming sessions . It doesn't matter if you want to learn more about Android , Chrome , Google+ , Drive , Cloud Platform , Google Maps , YouTube , now you will have the opportunity to ask questions in video conferences all year long!
