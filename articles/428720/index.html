But one way or another, digital detox ideas are becoming more common, which manifest themselves from retreat and Vipassana to practical guidelines for improving the quality of interaction with devices , using monochrome and advice to build The Phone Stacking Game in meetings with friends.



Ofcome, the British Communications Regulator (Ofcome - a non-profit organization that regulates the communications industry, including the radio / television broadcasting, wireless networks and postal services) reports in its report that 95%
