The reasons for the partial inaccessibility of "Vkontakte" in Ukraine

Recently, in the light of recent events, the most popular Russian-language social network works intermittently in Ukraine. Although Pavel repeatedly declared his antipoliticality, tonight (apparently boiling) in his blog he published a post with the following content. Full quote below.

By the way, the VKontakte equipment in Kiev, which was seized by the state authorities of Ukraine in the summer of this year, was never returned. Officially, Ukrainian officials changed the reason why they seized our servers several times. Informally, in order to return our equipment to us, Ukrainian officials demanded a bribe in the amount of 100 thousand dollars.

As a result, the population of Ukraine lost quick access to VKontakte, and the Ukrainian economy lost millions of dollars in potential investments.

I hope that the current civilian speeches in Kiev will lead to positive changes. Obviously, this wonderful people deserve a higher intellectual and moral level of their rulers.

So far, there have been no replies from government bodies.
