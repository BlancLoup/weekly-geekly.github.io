Mobile WiMAX in Russia - October Results

Hello, hello. Please accept my regrets that the weekend is over.

Just over a month ago, I wrote a post about my first, at that time partisan, tests of Yota's WiMAX mobile technology, whose base stations began to slowly cover the centers of the two Capitals. Then I ran at night with a laptop and made a Skype call to distant countries, checked pings in various areas and alone drew a real map covering Moscow.
A month has passed since then. Technology has slowly entered my life, becoming a full-fledged tool both at work and outside it. But let's say, I have already ‚Äúplayed enough for the future‚Äù and now I can soberly evaluate the technology, its promotion to the market, its potential and service, and therefore often my opinion about what is happening is rather critical. I follow all official comments and statements, which by the way are not so many, and plan to monthly sum up some results for those who are interested in the process of becoming a mobile WiMAX in Russia, but do not have enough time to monitor the development dynamics every day.



1. Testing, which Yota announced on September 2, still started in mid-October. I myself (this time officially) received a modem on the 16th, which I wrote about in my personal blog on Habr√© . And maybe more than a thousand, after all it was officially planned to distribute 2000 modems.

2. Testing method. It turned out that there is no single official mechanism for testing network quality. There is a set of external test sites that can be used, but a step to the left, a step to the right, raised the glass in the car, lowered it - you get completely different numbers. And in fact, the essence of testing comes down to whether there is a network or not. There are no web applications that would automate the process, turning it into a kind of social game, where the end user tester would see that he was actually involved in the progress - does not exist. Tasks are sent to the mail in a spreadsheet file, and are processed manually. Visually, you can see the results of your work only on an unofficial site , and there the formation of a coverage map is a manual process, no testers were given any program scripts for usability (while we are writing our own, but the process is long. Can anyone connect?).

3. At the presentation, during the issuance of modems, it was confirmed that drivers for Linux and Macs will be available until the end of the year. Nonetheless, enthusiasts, on one side or the other, permanently declare temporary solutions and persistent problems with searching and connecting to the network.

4. Feedback formats for testers - this is a call-center, a single mail for everyone (according to the Yota-champion program), and the official LJ . Strangely, for some reason, the idea with Yota Guru, the representative of the Company, who would lead a group of testers, control the process and answer questions, has stalled. The human approach, it is always closer and clearer than a call to the call-center. On the other hand, the Company, in principle, did not envisage the creation of a platform for monitoring feedback and supporting the enthusiasm of its testers, which in the era of socialization and web2.0 is not just a miscalculation in promoting the service, but a strategic omission. People accumulate a lot of questions, ideas and suggestions, but there is simply nowhere to ask them, and most importantly, to discuss. Currently, due to weak activity in the blogosphere and, in particular, in the LJ Company, it is clear that the efficiency of supporting an ordinary user tester does not hold water. With competent work, from 1-2 thousand free, distributed gifts, the response should be much higher. October Summary:

Now you can firmly say that mobile WiMAX came to Russia. At the beginning of November, the Network is quite a working tool with excellent development potential, and with a large degree of confidence I will assume that by the New Year, the coverage can already be called stable. Voiced tariffs with high-quality coverage can be considered reasonable, since Yota will provide not only access to the Network, but also a number of other services, starting with content services (music, movies, etc.). Technically, a company develops qualitatively and at a very good pace, but from a marketing point of view, the behavior of officials often causes confusion and a lot of unanswered questions in the ranks of testers.
