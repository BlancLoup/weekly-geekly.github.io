Demo Left 4 Dead


In short: online FPS, four survivors in a city filled with the good old walking dead, you can play as survivors, and you can play for zombie bosses, in 4 vs. 4 mode. four.

AI-director, who ensures that the players would not be bored, and, at the same time, that it was not too difficult. That is, it monitors the health of the players, the number of cartridges, the skill, doses the number of zombies, decides which side they will attack, and whether or not to release the boss, scatters first-aid kits and cartridges around the map. In combination - he is a DJ, because he selects the appropriate moment for the music for each player separately.


The developer is Valve, which means work on the game will continue after release.
