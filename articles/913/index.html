In RuNet, a new data center has been launched - Datahouse.su.

Filanko, a project DataHouse.su , announces the launch of a new data storage and processing center.

The equipment design and commissioning works that ensure the functioning of the new center are now fully completed, and the center is ready to accept everyone who wants to host their servers or rent dedicated servers of the required configuration at the new site.
When constructing the Center, considerable attention was paid to creating solutions that ensure maximum accessibility and security of the systems located on this site. The company's specialists have developed a number of subsystems that allow solving the main tasks facing users of computer centers:

‚Ä¢ Uninterrupted power. It is provided with two independent inputs from two different sections of the substation, and together with the use of uninterruptible power supplies, the company provides reliable power supply. The ability to consume total power of up to 1 MW and the architecture of the internal power supply of the data center can provide power up to 10 kW per rack.
‚Ä¢ Cooling of the containment. With the increase in server power consumption, the tasks of heat removal become more complex. The overall solution for cooling the containment zone is built on the principle of the hot / cold zone and grows with increasing heat generation. Cooling solutions are based on Mitshubishi.

‚Ä¢ Fire fighting. The new data processing center of Filanko DataHouse.su is equipped with all the necessary gas fire extinguishing systems in accordance with GOST 12.1.004-76.SSBT. The systems include a fire alarm system consisting of fire sensors located around the perimeter of the room and a gas extinguishing system. The gas used in the system is harmless to equipment.

‚Ä¢ Ensuring physical security. Access to the containment is strictly regulated. Access control is provided by an authentication system. The complex has a video surveillance system that allows the security service to monitor in real time.

‚Ä¢ Availability of communication channels and information security.
Data center connectivity is provided by the CityTelecom.ru project, which has its own fiber optic link in Moscow. The network operation is provided by the active equipment of Cisco Systems. Security of customer information is the most important priority for DataHouse.su, therefore, upon customer request, information backup, traffic filtering and system administration of dedicated servers can be provided.

Thus, Filanko has developed the infrastructure and regulations that guarantee the availability of servers and the security of their data to customers. Of course, no one is insured against force majeure, but the experience of the company's specialists and its investment in building a stable solution will ensure the fulfillment of the most rigorous business requirements of the client.
