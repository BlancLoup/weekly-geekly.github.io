Meizu MX smartphones are now officially in Russia

Many fans of smartphones have probably heard about the company Meizu, which produces very interesting devices for a reasonable price. Meizu and Telecom InfoProject announced the start of official sales of Meizu MX smartphones on the Russian market. The new flagship of the company - the MX 16GB model - has all the advantages of a modern high-tech device in the segment of smartphones with touch screens.



Meizu MX 16GB is built on an Exynos platform with a dual-core processor with a clock frequency of 1.4 GHz, has 1 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal flash memory. The high-quality 4-inch display with a resolution of 960x640 pixels has a pixel density of 292PPI, is protected by Corning¬Æ Gorilla¬Æ Glass and transmits the smallest image shades through 16 million colors. Highest sound quality is achieved using a separate WolfsonWM8958 audio chip. The device is equipped with two cameras: a rear camera with a CMOS-matrix of 8 megapixels with backlighting (BSI) and LED flash, as well as a front VGA-camera for video calls.
Meizu MX will receive a Flyme OS firmware update (based on Android 4.0 ICS) to version 1.0 in June of this year.
