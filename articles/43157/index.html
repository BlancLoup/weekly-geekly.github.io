Monitor for youth

% Username%, you will be always young!
The main thing is to have at least a monitor with LED backlight at work.

A group of German scientists has proved: with prolonged sitting behind a monitor with LED-backlit, wrinkles begin to smooth out in a person. The effect is achieved in a few months with everyday use of these monitors.



Scientists explain the "health" effect by the fact that at a certain intensity of light in the human skin the molecular structure of water changes, which in turn causes a "rejuvenating" effect.
