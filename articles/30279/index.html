Ukraine has launched an analogue of the popular B2B service alibaba.com

SmartWeb has announced the official launch of the UAProm.net Internet service, which is designed to facilitate the search for partners between the business sector in the Ukrainian market.

UAProm.net is primarily focused on small and medium businesses, and allows them to significantly simplify their access to the Internet market. Now users will not need to register their own domain and create a website; all this can be done on UAProm.net.

As part of the service, a number of additional paid services were also implemented, aimed at attracting customers to the sites of service users who have the choice to engage in the promotion and placement of advertisements on their own, or to entrust it to UAProm.net employees. At the same time, the main functionality is free and will remain so in the future.

According to Nikolai Palienko, the executive director of UAProm.net, before the official launch, the service operated for three months in test mode, and already over this period more than 2000 representatives of domestic business registered with it.
