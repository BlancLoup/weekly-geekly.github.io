Weekly assembly Vivaldi 1.0.425.3 with a surprise

Hello!

Today, we have an unusual build. First, it is, as it were, unplanned. Secondly, in it you will find a new welcome page (we hope you will like it). And thirdly, for Windows, we have prepared an unusual experimental build, which is addressed to the creators of anti-virus programs, just adoring ochepyatki in words. :-)

But first things first.

New welcome page

For those who constantly use test builds, it is not a secret that during the development time our browser has acquired a lot of new features and configuration options. These users already know where to customize what to their liking and what options are available. But we are approaching the first final version of Vivaldi, which will surely be tried by those who have never worked with our browser before. For such newbies, probably, the abundance of Vivaldi features hidden in the depths of the settings will be inaccessible with a quick reference. In this case, we have prepared a new welcome page (vivaldi: // welcome), which opens after the first installation of the browser:


On this page, users can, in a couple of clicks, make the first interface settings according to their preferences, selecting the appropriate combinations from the presented thumbnails. We are waiting for your ideas to further improve this page. If you are not very familiar with the capabilities of the browser yet, you can visit the unofficial site vivalditips.com , created and maintained by our testers, Sopranos. There you can find a more complete description of the available browser features.

Antivirus - with love

After the release of the previous build, we received several messages from you that the antivirus program installed on your computer detected a virus in the browser code. First of all, we are in a hurry to inform you - we are healthy, we have no viruses! We also conducted some investigation of this incident and found that some anti-virus applications were very indifferent to the name of our company specified in the installer‚Äôs description. For example, if in the name of the company Vivaldi Technologies we remove one letter o (and get Vivaldi Technlogies ), the problem with the detection of viruses in Vivaldi magically disappears.

So that you could experiment on your own for the sake of interest, we have prepared a special assembly with errors in the words:


We hope that the databases of anti-virus programs will soon be updated taking into account our wishes, and most users simply will not notice this funny incident. In the end, we can not change the name of the company for each weekly assembly!

And now about the good

However, for this assembly, we not only intentionally created errors, but also intentionally corrected them. And also deliberately added new, long-awaited improvements. In short, you will find important in the list of fixes and improvements:

  • Fixed the creation of thumbnails of pages when placed on the Express panel;
  • Fixed opening of the window with a constantly activated side panel;
  • Fixed the inability to add sites on the Express panel, if the http protocol is not specified;
  • Fixed scrolling of tabs using the right button and the wheel over the tabs (the context menu was opened);
  • Fixed opening of incorrect addresses from the drop-down list of the address bar when using the keyboard;
  • Fixed the work of key combinations with the included non-English layout;
  • Fixed work with Lastpass;
  • Added the option to open bookmarks in a new tab or in the active one - for the bookmark manager and bookmark panels;
  • When sorting bookmarks, folders and bookmarks are not mixed;
  • Now you can remove the tab from the group (for now - via the context menu);

And much more. Full list of changes - at the end of the article.
