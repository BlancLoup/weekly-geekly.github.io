progg.ru - project for developers

Dear Hobrazhiteli, I offer you my own project progg.ru .

Some time ago I wondered about creating a project on which links to articles from around Runet would be centrally accumulated. Of course, with a restriction on the subject - the development of software and web sites. Including the theme of the site would include topics: start-ups and business, legal issues, software, design, typography, tools, and more.

And so, I would like to present the implementation of the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe project progg.ru . This project is an analogue of the popular project digg.com and works on a similar principle. After publishing a link to an article, project users can rate it, comment, get a link by RSS subscription.

Each blogger, a project participant, after placing an article in his blog, can place a link to his post in progg.ru , and then add a counter to the post that shows the assessment on the resource and allows him to go to the resource for evaluation.

Thus, progg.ru is a resource for developers, and all those who are somehow concerned with software development issues.

Features progg.ru
* a centralized source of interesting articles from all over the Internet;
* place of communication for different authors from different resources;
* social elements, such as rating articles and users;
* RSS subscription on numerous parameters, such as category, user, tag, search result, main tape, new entries.
* feedback from bloggers posts through the progg it counter;
* support for OpenId , Gravatar .
