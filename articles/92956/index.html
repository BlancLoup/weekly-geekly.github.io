The smallest nettop in the world


Nettops confidently gaining popularity in the market of electronics and computers. Their main advantage in size. They are very small and compact, they are convenient to take with you on trips. However, this model will surprise you. She is not just small, but miniature.


VIA has launched the ARTiGO A1100 nettop. It works on the basis of the VIA Nano processor with a clock frequency of 1.2GHz. Based on the pico-ITX motherboard. In addition, the mini-device is equipped with a VX855 media processor, VGA and HDMI outputs, gigabit Ethernet, five USB ports and optional 802.11b / g. This kid's dimensions are 146 x 52 x 99 mm and weighs about 0.6 kg.

Graphics processing performs the core Chrome9, which makes it possible to view HD-video, you will agree, not bad for such a miniature device.

Such a baby is ideal for those who live in small apartments, and the problem of arranging a workplace in a room in a minimal area is relevant. With the help of such a nettop you can surf the Internet, watch video and audio files, work with various programs.

According to the developers, ARTiGO A1100 is able to perform the functions of a home server or streaming media device.

Unfortunately, the memory and hard disk will have to be purchased separately.


Today, the ARTiGO A1100 can be ordered in the VIA online store for $ 243. Full sales are planned for the end of May this year.
