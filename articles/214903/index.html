Declassified Satosi Nakamoto's personality



Experienced journalist Newsweek managed to find Satoshi Nakamoto, the author of Bitcoin digital cryptocurrency. It turned out that his name is actually ... Satoshi Nakamoto and he lives modestly with his family in California, despite the 1 million BTC received during mining in the first year.

Gavin Andresen, who spoke with Satoshi almost from the first days of Bitcoin development, expressed dissatisfaction with the persistence of Newsweek journalist. She found the 64-year-old Nakamoto and published a dossier on him and his family, declassifying the home address in Temple City, publishing a photo of the house and other details received from former wives, brothers and children.

The Bitcoin community has traditionally respected Satoshi Nakamoto's anonymity, and it was not customary to talk about his personality.

It is difficult to say whether the owner is now threatened with a fortune of $ 400 + million in a small town. But it is certain that he will not be able to lead his former life. After all, even his family members did not know that it was he who created Bitcoin. Nakamoto himself agreed to say a few words to the journalist, and in the presence of the police.
