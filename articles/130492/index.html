As always, everything old is back ...

LG has decided to return to the Ukrainian market with a new line of laptops. As I remember, LG did not release laptops for 4-6 years (there is no exact data). On the one hand it is true, but on the other there isn‚Äôt. LG - led the hidden production of laptops. Why hidden? LG Display

For Apple fans, the signing of this contract means that the quality of displays in Apple devices will remain at the same high level. Even now, most of the monitors and displays of Apple - about 70% - are made at LG Display factories.
Printers

HP will supply printers for LG Electronics Inc., for subsequent sale under the LG brand, a Korean source said in September 2010, quoting people whose source the source did not disclose. Both companies are working on the details of a plan according to which HP can start supplying printers as early as next month, the newspaper said in Seoul. LG resumed the printer business at the end of last year, concluding a similar deal with Lexmark International Inc., the company first left the business in 1995 due to low profitability. According to the report, LG decided to partner with HP in order to expand its product line and increase its market share.
