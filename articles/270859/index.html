Facebook helps to find out if everything is good with your friends in case of emergency

In Paris today (November 13-14), 7 terrorist attacks occurred at night. Read about it here .

If you have friends in France, Facebook has turned on a special interface that allows you to find out if everything is good and if their tragedy has touched them.



https://www.facebook.com/safetycheck/

If you yourself are in Paris now (sincerely I hope that you have bypassed the tragedy), you can inform all your friends by the same link that you are fine. Notifications to friends will come in the form of Push notifications.

Also, there is an opportunity to celebrate friends with whom you have already managed to contact and know that they are safe.
