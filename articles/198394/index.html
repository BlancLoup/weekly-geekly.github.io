New certification from VMware - VCA (VMware Certified Associate) free delivery

In September, VMware announced the introduction of VCA (VMware Certified Associate) certification - this is the initial step in certification, currently it is available in three areas:

VMware Certified Associate - Datacenter Virtualization (VCA-DCV) - Exam blueprint - for assessing knowledge of infrastructure virtualization based on VMware vSphere.

VMware Certified Associate - Cloud (VCA-Cloud) - Exam blueprint - for assessing knowledge of infrastructure virtualization based on VMware vCloud Director.

VMware Certified Associate - Workforce Mobility (VCA-WM) - Exam blueprint - for the assessment of knowledge in VDI products (VMware Horizont View).

In the future, one more direction will be available - VMware Certified Associate - Network Virtualization (VCA-NV ) - this certification is not yet available, but it is known that it will be based on the knowledge of the new VMware NSX product.
