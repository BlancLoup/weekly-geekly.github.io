Unfortunately, not everything is as simple as it turned out almost immediately. Firstly, apparently not everyone in Google knows that payments are accepted in rubles through a bank. This is for example perfectly visible in the choice of payments .

An even more interesting point is that it is impossible to get payment details through the AdWords record, namely, the purpose of the payment is not decrypted (see screenshot). Numerous sufferings with code conversion in Stirlitz do not lead to anything.



At the forum, Google is already asking for help with payment , but so far no official response has been received.
