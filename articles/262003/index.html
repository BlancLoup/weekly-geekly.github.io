Free summer school on HPC in scientific and engineering problems - applications are accepted until July 24

From August 17 to August 24, the Sixth Summer School on High Performance Computing will be held at Innopolis University . The co-organizer of the event will be MIPT . The school is supported by the Russian Foundation for Basic Research . During the event, general lectures will be given on current problems of the mechanics of deformable media, as well as their applications to engineering problems.
