New Berlin: Lumia accessories

Now that gadget lovers have exhaled after a tense week of mobile premieres, it‚Äôs time to talk about some pretty new accessories that we presented at the #morelumia special event along with our latest Lumia 830/730 DS / 735 and Lumia Denim update.



HD-10 wireless media adapter for displaying Lumia images on large screens


It can be assumed that this device will find a response in the hearts of many owners of large televisions (and Lumia, of course), but most likely HD-10 will settle in the conversations of offices, press halls and other similar places. To some extent, the media adapter is another call to drop laptops in favor of mobility, since the rest of the office ecosystem in Lumia has been around for a long time.



The HD-10 media adapter consists of two parts: a removable NFC disk and the main unit, which is connected via a standard HDMI port to the TV, and via Micro USB to the charger.



Using a smartphone connected to the TV via HD-10, you can easily stream movies, Xbox games, PowerPoint presentations and other content via Bluetooth. To start the broadcast, it is enough to attach the smartphone to the disk and the image will immediately appear on the TV screen. After that, the user does not have to be close to the base or keep the NFC fire close at hand, the main thing is to stay within the range of the wireless connection. How it works in reality can be seen from the following video:



The adapter can transmit content with a maximum resolution of Full HD (1080p) and has support for Wi-Fi CERTIFIED Miracast. The preliminary cost of the adapter (excluding taxes and fees) is 79 euros.

Wireless Charging Panel DT-903


Our many favorite chargers are becoming not only more beautiful, but also intelligent. For example, the second-generation wireless panel has learned to understand and notify the user that the charge of Lumia is coming to an end.



The thing is that in addition to the already traditional standards of Qi and NFC, the new product features Bluetooth LE support, which is responsible for the constant radio communication between the smartphone and the charger.

It may seem to many that this is another ‚Äúdecoration‚Äù, but remember that the charger is usually in a prominent place, and the phone most often remains in your pocket, bag, or just disappears in the bowels of the apartment.



To notify the user about the need for charging (when the charge is less than 30%), as well as about new SMS, emails or missed calls, the DT-903 panel is equipped with an ambient indication (read as a backlight) located on the bottom of the device. In order to distinguish the type of notification from one another, users will be able to customize the scenarios of the display.



The intelligent wireless charging panel will be available in white, bright orange and bright green performance for about 59 euros.



Deliveries of DT-903 and HD-10 to Russia will begin in the 4th quarter of this year, retail prices will be announced additionally.

Housing for Lumia 735 with Wireless Charging Function - CC-3086


The possibility of wireless charging according to the Qi standard in the Lumia 735 is provided with an additional panel CC-3086.



Produced in the same shades as the Lumia, the cover with a built-in induction antenna is installed in the place of the native back panel and outwardly practically does not differ from it.

Color options: dark gray, bright green and bright orange. Estimated cost - about 14.9 euros.

Notebook Case for Nokia Lumia 830 with Wireless Charging Function - CP-627


For users who are concerned about the long-term preservation of the appearance of their Lumia 830, we have offered additional protection for the case - CP-627. A new vice-flagship smartphone clad in its strong arms will look no less impressive than with a standard lid.



The cover book fastens to the back wall of the smartphone. The soft microfiber lining protects the screen from dirt and light physical damage. The case, as well as the standard smartphone cover, is equipped with an induction antenna, so the Lumia 830 will not lose its support for wireless charging.

The estimated cost of one of the three color options (a choice of dark gray, bright orange and bright green) is approximately 24.9 euros.

Wireless Charging Panel T-601


Our smallest wireless charger for today.



Due to its compact size, the T-601 panel, working from any USB port, will be convenient for both home use, and for frequent trips or for constant carrying with you.

Charging is presented in both bright and classic colors: from black, white, yellow, blue, red and to the new orange. The recommended price for an orange charging panel is about 29 euros.
