Welcome in the new year, colleagues!

Next Thursday, January 16th, we are holding a webinar dedicated to the new lineup of Dell Venue Tablet PCs, which will be on sale in Russia very soon. These tablets are designed for both consumer and commercial segment. Thanks to Intel processors, these devices are characterized by high performance.
Under the cut, we briefly tell about the line itself, as well as tezisno about topics that will be discussed at the webinar.


Dell venue

  1. Dell Venue 8 Pro and Dell Venue 11 Pro tablets running Windows 8.1 provide a new level of performance and speed. They will be appreciated by both ordinary users and IT departments of companies that can easily integrate devices into the existing corporate environment and ensure full compatibility with existing Windows and Microsoft Office applications.
    • Dell Venue 8 Pro, running Windows 8.1, is equipped with an HD-capable IPS screen. Unlike competitive tablets, the device has a replaceable battery, and the large Full HD screen with wide viewing angles makes it easy to view and create content while remaining mobile. For the tablet additionally provides styluses and a keyboard.
