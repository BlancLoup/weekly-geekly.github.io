8 Tips of Cardinal Richelieu

There is a common misconception that the art of management belongs exclusively to the newest time. In fact, art is as old as the world. The manager is one of the oldest professions along with you know with what. Egyptian slaves would never have built the pyramids for the pharaohs if they had not been competently beaten with sticks. 1. Structure


"The weakness of the human being requires equilibrium in all things, and this is the foundation of truth."

The main key to the success of any enterprise is a properly built management structure. In relation to the SEO department, there are 2 types of structures that can be used explicitly or implicitly in organizations that specialize in SEO: functional and project-functional .

The functional structure usually develops naturally and takes place in small companies.



The main disadvantage of the functional structure is that with an increase in the number or pace of project implementation, the manager no longer has time to control the workflow.

The design and functional structure assumes allocation along with the head of a department of key posts of project managers.



Obvious advantages of the structure:

1) the opportunity for the head of the department to focus on strategic issues, the development of the department,

2) the possibility of quality management of a large number of projects. If in the functional structure a certain conditional "ceiling" is 10-15 projects, then the design-functional "ceiling" is proportional to the number of project managers.

The downside is that project managers must have, firstly, high qualifications, secondly, high motivation, and thirdly, a high level of responsibility. To find such specialists is quite difficult.

8. Professional growth


"A sovereign who wants to love being his subjects must occupy the main ranks and the first authorities of his state by persons so respectable from the whole world so that they can find the reason for his choice in dignity."

The only criterion for professionalism in the IT-sphere can be knowledge, not experience . The SEO market is subject to rapid changes, each month there are new ranking algorithms, new contextual advertising systems, new promotion mechanisms. A person who specializes in search engine optimization, can fill up all the work on the promotion of social networks, for example.

Hence the conclusion: promotion of an employee through the ranks is possible only if they pass a certain professional "exam". Only when he proves his competence in this field of specialization, it can be enhanced.
