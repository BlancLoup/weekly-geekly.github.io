Licensed movies and music on flash media.

Another idea that requires implementation (if not yet) is to organize the production and sale of movies / music (official legal versions) on flash cards (or usb media) of appropriate size and different standards (or with adapters) in 3gp or mp4 format - oriented to mobile phones or similar devices with an acceptable screen and support for such cards (most of which are becoming). If supplied with a usb reader, you can view it on computers.

Focused on geeks and just people forced to move frequently. It will be very convenient to take a handful of movies in your pocket with you on the road or buy them on the spot. Especially relevant to sales at airports and train stations, payment centers for mobile communications and sales with postal delivery. So far as I know, this category of persons is forced to independently create / copy pirated versions due to the lack of proposed alternatives.

The cost is likely to be slightly higher than standard disks, but justified by mobility, the lack of the need to convert films, ease of carrying and storage, and the ability to be cleaned and used as a regular storage device. The cost of the carrier itself tends to decrease, and even in retail sales, gigabyte cards cost about 200 rubles, while films of acceptable quality (for the indicated devices) will need less than 500 MB. I think in the end this direction of development will be one of the most interesting for promoting cinema-video audio products - the current official formats (DVD, CD ..) are too cumbersome and are not supported even by modern subnotebooks, not to mention phones and smartphones.
