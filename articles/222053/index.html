We built, built and finally built.

At the moment, we want to present to Habra a working prototype with the following characteristics:
IR matrix - two options are possible 32x31 and 80x60 and there, and there are the same viewing angles. With such matrices, we can construct a panorama of a room of approximately 160x155 and 400x300 pixels, respectively. IR image is complemented by a 1080p camera. This allows, if necessary, to make an interesting overlay image using interpolation , as in the imager for a smartphone, similarly made a picture of the router.

For communication, it is possible to use either Wi-Fi or USB modem of any Linux compatible, while using a cellular network, 3G / 4G coverage is optimal for high-quality online transmission of both video and IR data.

In the plans to develop the body, we have an example of how it could look for the end user, is presented at the very beginning of the post. There is a lot of work to be done both on the layout of electronic components and on the manufacturing technology of the case itself .

If you have read up to here, you are probably interested to find out, and what can he do that others cannot. Let's compare: there are expensive online monitoring systems on the market, there are thermal imagers for industrial vision systems, there are manual thermal imagers for personnel. All of them are used for comparative thermography wiki . To measure accurately the temperature value does not give us the coefficient of reflection, for each material it has its own. But this is not required if we have something to compare: getting a thermogram of the same place at different times can understand the beginning of the problem and respond in advance. All these systems have significant drawbacks - this is the price, difficulty in use, the need to buy expensive software. We make our device according to the principle ‚Äúeven my mother will be able to use‚Äù, and the price does not exceed 20,000 rubles. All you need to use CamIRa is a PC or smartphone connected to the Internet.



Possible cases of use now:

- Monitoring of temperature: we allocate a zone on the thermogram, for example, sockets with power supplies included in them, we indicate a temperature threshold of 60 degrees, that's all, CamIRa itself will alert you if the temperature rises above the limit value.

- Security of the room : we indicate the zone with all the available space, set the temperature range from 30 to 40, if a person appears in the zone, CamIRa will notify you.

-Control equipment / people: choose the equipment, set the temperature of its normal operation, for example, a welding machine in the workshop, CamIRa will track them and send a report on how much the equipment worked, how long it was idle.

This is not a complete list, if you have your case of application, write in the comments about it!
