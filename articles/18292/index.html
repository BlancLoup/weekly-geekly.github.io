Social Design is the development of community rules and default choices that motivate participants' desired behavior. Successful social architecture has become one of the decisive success factors for many projects: Wikipedia, Digg, Yahoo! Answers, Yahoo! Personals, Stumble Upon, del.icio.us, Facebook, Slashdot.org, Reader2.com, Eventful.com, Yelp.com, VKontakte.ru, News2.ru, Habr.ru, Yandex Blogs, Imhonet.Ru.

What components does social design consist of?
  • Rules of admission to the system. The system of engaging new users and retaining existing ones.
  • System of motivations. Psychological "winnings" and "encouragement." Business functions whose role is assumed by the community. Rules for determining reputation. Consequences of high and low values ‚Äã‚Äãof reputation.
  • Business model. Paid / free participation and individual functions. Earnings opportunities for participants. The presence and type of advertising.
  • Privacy The level of anonymity of participants. Access levels and their management system. The openness of the community to the outside world. The right to contact. Spam detection, user protection tools.
  • Informational dynamics. The rules governing the promotion and dissemination of information in the community.
  • Self-organization. The boundaries of the activity of an individual participant, his "territory".
