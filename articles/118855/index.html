Master class "Evolution of creativity" from Adobe. Special discount for habrovchan 70%

As part of the support for the new Adobe Creative Suite 5.5 package, Adobe will hold the Evolution of Creativity in Moscow on May 25 . All day international gurus of the digital industry will conduct master classes in two areas Design & Video and Web & Flash . Guest experts include Jason Levine (audio, video), Terry White (design), Greg Rewis (web), and Paul Train (mobile technology).

In the first stream, new features in Adobe CS5.5 Production Premium will be most actively discussed: changes in working with audio and HD files, the new Warp Stabilizer intellectual stabilization feature in After Effects, as well as everything related to design, photo and creation digital interactive magazines.

In the second, everything related to Flash and HTML5: development of interactive applications for Android, iOS and BlackBerry PlayBook, for example, Motorola Xoom and iPad 2, as well as optimization of digital content for smartphones and tablets using HTML5, CSS3 and the jQuery environment.

All the action will take place in the World Trade Center from 13.00 to 19.00.

Adobe is pleased to invite all of our customers to this event with a 70% discount. To do this, enter the promotional code BEGURU70% HBR when registering on the event website cs5evolution.ru/registration
