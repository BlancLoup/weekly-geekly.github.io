Juniper Workshop: JunOS as a second language



Workshops ‚ÄúJunOS as a second language‚Äù took place in TC MUK (Kiev) on July 30 and 31, 2015, where students learned in practice how to work with the main OS of Juniper Networks solutions.

These seminars were aimed at familiarizing with the JunOS operating system of network engineers who previously had experience only with Cisco-like CLIs (Cisco, Brocade, Huawei, ZTE, etc.). At the workshops, both theoretically and practically, the features and logic of the operating system, which is installed on all Juniper equipment lines, were considered.

It was not a simple seminar - in the form of a theoretical basis, but there was a real workshop in which the audience tried to do something with their own hands, to set up the SRX gateways.
