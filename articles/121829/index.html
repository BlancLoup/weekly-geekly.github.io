ActiveState acquired Phenona

Today, Phenona registered users received a letter from service creator Daniil Kulchenko (Daniil Kulchenko) with the announcement of the purchase of the service by ActiveState.

In the letter, Daniel Kulchenko says that despite the fact that he is still in school (I remind you that he is 15 years old), he will work on developing the service with the ActiveState team, but not full-time work.
I also recall that ActiveState has a Stackato - an end-to-end enterprise cloud platform for Perl and Python (which we talked about in the last YAPP ), all of whose users also received a letter with an announcement. It says that the technology, knowledge and talents of Phenona will expand the company's cloud strategy at the same time will accelerate development in the rapidly growing market of cloud services.
Stackato is currently in the Developer Preview stage (you can ask for an invite here ).
