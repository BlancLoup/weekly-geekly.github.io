Hello.

While the Internet is becoming more advanced, three-dimensional multiplayer online games, I decided to try to make the simplest game for two. Once upon a time there was something similar and it then dragged me down a lot, well, and decided to quickly do something similar.

This is a multiplayer likeness of the game "Tetris", where one player can influence the playing field of another as follows: when burning in his field 2, 3 or 4 lines at a time, the opponent is added respectively 1, 2 or 3 lines. The speed of the game increases over time so that it is not possible to play forever.

After the end of the game, the rating of players changes and, thus, some may have a desire to get into the top 15 players of the project.

Initially, the game technically worked through ajax, but then refused this. the server periodically started to slow down. But as I later understood, it was not entirely due to technology, but because of some of my mistakes, so maybe I will soon make support for the game via Ajax for those who sit at the proxy. In the meantime, the game works through a flash-bridge on sockets, so the game will require a normal Internet.

Do not throw stones - this is still an early beta version and I do not claim any laurels. This project is rather just a hobby. In the future, I plan to add the ability to view other people's games, somewhat improve the mechanism of Tetris itself (gliding, etc.), etc.

Also this project has an application in VKontakte , from where it is also possible to play.

I hope the big habr-effect will not be, since Most likely the hosting will not stand.
