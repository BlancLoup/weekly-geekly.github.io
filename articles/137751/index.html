Our solution was a slider that can be adapted for any issue and aspect of the company, set its dimensions, color scheme and embed it in any part of the site.



Here are the main advantages of our tool:
  • The ability to receive all reviews in private, in a private office, and not to keep them in sight of competitors, as is done in existing services.
