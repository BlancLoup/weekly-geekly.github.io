Pre-ordered Nokia Lumia 720

We are pleased to announce that the pre-order for the new Nokia Lumia 720, presented in February this year at MWC-2013 , has opened today. Let's see what this smartphone is.



Nokia Lumia 720 complements the line of models based on Windows Phone 8 and is a smartphone of the middle price segment, its cost at the start of sales will be 14,990 rubles.

What else is interesting for Nokia Lumia 720?



Design


The model is made in a unibody-case of polycarbonate, like its predecessors of the Lumia series - 800 and 900, the screen is protected by Gorilla Glass 2.

It is noticeably thinner than the Nokia Lumia 920 (9 mm versus 10.7 mm), the other dimensions are also slightly smaller than the flagship (width 67.5 mm versus 70.8 mm; length 127.9 mm versus 130.3 mm) . But these are the very few millimeters that significantly affect the feel of the phone in your hand. Nokia Lumia 720 will be comfortable to lie in both male and female palm.

At the start of sales, the device will be available in four colors: the classic white and black versions, plus bright yellow and red. This again makes it attractive for both male and female audience.

Iron


Nokia Lumia 720 is based on the same dual-core Snapdragon S4 Krait processor as the Lumia 920. The processor runs at 1 GHz.

Another attractive part of our new product is a 2000 mAh battery, the same as in the Lumia 920. Thanks to her, the smartphone can work for a very long time, up to 520 hours in standby mode and up to 79 hours in music listening mode.

Another feature of the Lumia 720 is an excellent front camera: it has a 1.3 MP widescreen lens, which is useful for Skype video calls or creating stylish self-portraits using the pre-installed GlamMe photo app. The main 6.7 megapixel camera is also equipped with a wide-angle lens and allows you to take great bright pictures.

In addition, unlike the Lumia 920, the 720 model has a slot for microSD memory cards. You can install a card of up to 64 GB in addition to the built-in 8 GB in your phone and store all your photos, videos and music library on your phone. And 7 GB of SkyDrive cloud storage will help you easily share your captured photo or important documents with friends.

Wireless charger


Like the older models, the Nokia Lumia 720 supports the Qi technology for wireless charging, to use it you will need an additional accessory - a special panel. Since, unlike the 820th model, the Lumia 720 case is not removable, a contact pad was placed on the back of the smartphone to connect this panel. The accessory will allow not only to charge the smartphone without using wires, but also additionally protect it from damage in case of accidental fall.

operating system


The device works on OS Windows Phone 8, which means that you will be able to customize the start screen with scalable live tiles, pre-installed exclusive applications from Nokia, as well as Here's geolocation services.

And last but not least: by pre-ordering the Nokia Lumia 720, you get the opportunity to become the owner of one of the three Nokia Wireless Headsets Purity Pro.
