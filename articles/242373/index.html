Pixel Perfect Precision: how good are your pixels?

Many of you who are interested in iOS / Android games have probably heard about Monument Valley. You may know that the game was developed by ustwo, an experienced digital studio. But you hardly know about the manual called Pixel Perfect Precision, which has now grown to two hundred pages and received serial number 3.

Pixel Perfect Precision - a special attitude and a look at how the company approaches to work. From general team collaboration tips to specific Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator guides.

Pixel Perfect Precision is a unique reference book on digital design, in which the authors tried to share their experience. Under the cut there is a small review of the changes of the third edition and links to download the Russian version.

Updated artwork and images optimized for retina


Vibrant colors, crisp fonts + images optimized for better display on retina displays. Reading on the iPad, for example, is very convenient. Moreover, the manual is available in .ibooks format. The overall design of the manual has also been updated. During the translation, we partially changed the fonts, but this did not affect the visibility.


New clear pictures and design

Design and development


An interesting chapter that will help build bridges between the designer and the developer. Valuable advice on the organization of the process of working together two diverse professionals. Authors will also share with you tips that will simplify the transition from design to code.


New chapter on design and development

More Pixel Perfect Principles


Now the fundamental principles of Pixel Perfect are even more: users, prototyping, movement.


New Pixel Perfect Principles

Detailed availability


Added error prevention guide, especially for mobile devices. In the new version this part has been redone: now it is easier to read, the authors also worked on the connectivity of the contents of the chapter.


New tutorial in chapter Accessibility

And of course, more about Photoshop


Since the release of the penultimate version of the guide, Adobe has released a Creative Cloud update with lots of goodies for interface designers: Live Shape Properties, Linked Smart Objects, and Mega-friendly Generator. We will talk about all this.


Updated tips for Photoshop
