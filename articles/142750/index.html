Running Google Drive



The long-awaited launch of the Google Drive service has just taken place .

Each Google Drive user is provided with 5Gb of space (with this offer, Google surpasses the standard conditions of Dropbox, but loses Yandex.Disk).

Drive is available at link drive.google.com (any browser is supported), as well as there are special clients for Windows, Mac and Android (a way to connect Google Drive for Linux: click ) has appeared. An application for iOS will appear soon.

"Goodies" service:
  • Sharing
  • Smart Tagging support
  • Support for 30 different formats for viewing directly in the browser
  • The possibility of buying additional space (up to 16Tb)
  • Promised uptime 99.9%
