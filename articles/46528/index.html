New search startup GetReview.Ru


We want to bring to your attention a search startup GetReview.Ru .

Let's start with the task.

Each of us somehow faced the problem of choosing goods. The range of products on the market is usually quite large and it often turns out that there are several products with the characteristics we need.

The responsible buyer, before making a decision, reads on the Internet reviews of goods of interest to it and makes a choice only after reworking a large number of review and comparative materials on the topic. We are doing a project that is designed to help such buyers in finding the necessary information.

The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthematic search engines is not new, but we saw that in this niche you can make an interesting and very useful project, which is not in this niche now. It is also an opportunity for us to practice and improve our level in search technologies. Another job in this niche allows you to look differently at the options for search results, to solve specialized tasks in the field of searching and analyzing textual information that search engines of a general orientation do not and will not solve.

What makes our project, and how is it different from others?

We index only specialized sites that publish technology reviews. The most famous such sites are 3dnews.ru , THG.ru , ixbt , zoom.cnews.ru , but there are many others. We index these sites with a special spider that downloads only reviews, highlights the dates of publication of reviews, can index multi-page reviews, sticking together several web pages into one review, can determine the link to the print version of the review. Having collected all this information, our service provides the ability to search among these sites, which excludes the possibility of search engine spam in the system. At the same time, the relevance of the issue depends on the date of publication of the review and on its size, number of photos and video materials in the review, etc., which allows us to improve the issue. Also, after the search, the user can filter the results by the most important attributes for him (the novelty of the review, its size, the presence of photos, etc.).

We differ from ordinary search engines in a more uniform and relevant issue, for example, we choose a 22-inch monitor and want to read what we have to choose.
