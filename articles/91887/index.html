Far Eastern Internet Forum 2010 May 19-21 May in Khabarovsk


From May 19 to May 21, the Far East Internet Forum will be held in Khabarovsk.

What is DVIF?


The Far Eastern Internet Forum (DVIF) is an event that brings together on one site managers of IT departments of companies and system administrators, leading providers and communications companies, representatives of educational and research institutions, eminent web designers and web developers, prominent programmers and government officials. authorities, business structures.

Technical program of the forum

1 day, May 19, 2010 (Wednesday)
The opening of the forum and the Internet marathon, day providers and network technologies.
  • 1.Opening of the forum, greetings to the participants
  • 2. Plenary session - Internet development in the Far East
  • 3.Sections “Internet Access”, “Modern Network Technologies”, “Mobile Access”, “Education and Work in the IT Field”, “Information Security”, etc.
  • 4.Sectors "State and the Internet", "Electronic Government"
  • 5. Section “Electronic medicine and telemedicine”
  • 6. Round table with providers

Day 2, May 20, 2010 (Thursday)
Day of the web developer.
  • 1. “Creating websites” sections, “Website content management systems”, “Internet projects design”, “Internet software”, “Domains”, etc.
  • 2.Sections “Social Networks”, “Internet Communities”, “Blogs”, etc.
  • 3. Round table with web-developers
  • 4. Award Ceremony "Miss Internet of the Far East - 2010"

Day 3, May 21, 2010 (Friday)
Internet advertising and PR day.
  • 1. “E-commerce”, “Internet advertising”, “SEO”, “Contextual advertising” sections, etc.
  • 2. Sections "Regional Information Projects", "Media on the Internet", etc.
  • 3. The ceremony of awarding the Far Eastern Internet Award "Sterkh-2010"
