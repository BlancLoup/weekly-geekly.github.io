Payments through the mobile at the point of sale of Privatbank

Privatbank apparently inspired by Flattr startup ( http://flattr.com ) decided to quickly make an alternative service at home, positioning it as a replacement for POS terminals. The seller sends a specific format with the payer's phone number to the service number 7440, while the payer sends an SMS to the same number 7440 with his card number + validity period + cvv2.
