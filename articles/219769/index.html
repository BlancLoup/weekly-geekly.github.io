Crank English with LinguaLeo: create thematic dictionaries, train your memory and train in a group!



In our work we are guided by the mission of the company - to make learning a foreign language convenient, engaging and effective. The last six months, the LinguaLeo team has analyzed user behavior and preferences in learning English. Based on this data, we have improved the product and developed several new features. As a result, today we are ready to announce three cool tools that will be useful both for self-study and for those who learn English in groups. Each of them takes into account the needs of the user and aims to achieve tangible results in a short time. So what are these tools?

Firstly, now each user can create and publish themed word sets on LinguaLeo. You can share them with everyone or send a link to the set to someone alone. Secondly, now all studied sets of words can be conducted through training the brainstorm . It is designed so that the user quickly remembers the meaning and spelling of words. Third, you can now learn English in LinguaLeo in a group .

Under the cut, we will tell you what happened and how it works.

The best result in the study of new words gives an integrated approach. You need to remember the meaning, spelling and sound, and then repeat the exercise after a while. With this approach, words are remembered quickly and permanently. Given this, we created a special training brainstorm . The process of learning new words


The secret of quick memorization is simple. It consists in intensive study of small groups of words. In one session, you will learn 6 words. First you memorize the translation, then the spelling and on the last exercise you recognize it by ear. Training continues until you succeed, having correctly completed all the exercises with each word. We will keep the cases in which there were mistakes so that you can return to them the next day. This is the first implementation of interval repetition, which will soon be developed.

The plans are to improve the user error correction algorithm, as well as train the memory. In other words, the service will take into account the incorrect execution and consistently explain the spelling or value with hints. There will also be taken into account the moment when you ‚Äúforget‚Äù the word: in a three-day, weekly or three-weekly time period. All data will be analyzed during the construction of the algorithm, and the user will eventually receive the most suitable program for him.

We create our group on LinguaLeo and add colleagues and brothers in the workshop



The third tool is aimed at simultaneously improving the language skills of several users. Ideal for training company employees and group course students. In the group version, you can assign an administrator who sets tasks and monitors the result.


Group Admin page on LinguaLeo


Situation one: the company enters the international market, and its employees need to learn phrases and expressions of business correspondence and terms related to the company profile. In this case, you can assign the HR-manager as an administrator, and he will assign tasks in accordance with the objectives of the company, for example, taking a business English course and a grammar course for the advanced .

Situation two: an English teacher who teaches several students at the same level. Ask your teacher to create a group for you on LinguaLeo. You can receive and perform tasks remotely. The teacher, in turn, will be able to monitor your progress and adjust the program.
