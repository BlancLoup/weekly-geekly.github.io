Resource-saving shredder for 100 thousand dollars

As an interesting curiosity for lovers of technology and all sorts of different machines, I propose the news about a machine that can process any type of office paper into toilet paper. It's simple - the worker came, downloaded a stack of last year's reports, and at the exit received a couple of rolls of toilet paper. True, an interesting idea?



That's just bad luck - this machine, which is named by the producers of the White Goat , costs nothing at all - $ 100,000. If you take the average cost of a roll of toilet paper for 60 cents, then in order to recoup this machine, you need 200 thousand (!) Rolls of toilet paper. This is enough for a month in a small town.
