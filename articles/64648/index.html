"Quintura - Search on the Site" offers tips on searching

Quintura introduces tooltips in the search box for service users. Using hints, Quintura users can quickly enter and more accurately formulate their request.

For the selection of tips Quintura uses user search queries and keywords highlighted from the pages of the site. Based on this data, when you enter a search query, the user is offered words and phrases, from which you can quickly continue your search.
