The Russian police in the near future will be able to remotely turn off the engine of any car

This opportunity will be provided by the ERA-GLONASS system, the installation of which becomes mandatory since 2017.


Photo: REUTERS / Sergei Karpukhin

Violators of traffic rules and hijackers may soon become virtually defenseless against the police: law enforcement officers at the touch of a button will be able to turn off the ignition at the pursued car. This opportunity will be provided by the ERA-GLONASS system , the installation of which has become mandatory since 2017. And not only in Russia - the system should be installed on all new cars that are produced in the territory of the countries of the Customs Union. On the secondary market for cars so far nothing is said.

About the new features of the system it became known during the on-site meeting of the interdepartmental working group on the development and production of the electronic component base, which was held in Zelenograd at the Angstrem-T enterprise, Izvestia writes . At the moment, ERA-GLONASS only transmits a signal about the accident with the establishment of the exact coordinates of the incident, and helps to establish two-way communication between the dispatcher and the passengers of the car that has had an accident. In the near future, the functions of the system will be expanded, and the police will be able to turn off the ignition in any car with ERA-GLONASS remotely.

In addition, from 2020, police will be able to determine the location of any vehicle with an accuracy of up to 50 cm (the accuracy of positioning objects using GLONASS should reach this level by 2020, the developers say). This means that violators of traffic rules, who, for example, go to the "oncoming", will not necessarily be caught in the place of violation.
