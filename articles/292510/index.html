According to a MasterCard study, two out of three users in America pay their bills using automatic payments.

Regular payments (or recurring payments) - automatic debiting of funds from a client's card according to a schedule determined by the client or the company. For example, monthly replenishment of a mobile phone on a specified date, payment of utilities, payment for hosting / Internet service providers or replenishment of various accounts. The service is already quite well known in Uanet, but even here there are subtleties in the implementation, as well as its pros and cons.




Implementation details and advantages

For an online business, using regular payments can be equated to regular profits. The payment from the client arrives in due time, the share of lost profits decreases. But everything is not always so simple, since the specifics and requirements of the business are different and everything depends on the correct implementation of automatic payments. For example, setting up regular payments may be available only on the client side - this is acceptable for automatic replenishment of a mobile phone, but this option is not quite suitable for paying for services that may have different amounts to be paid.
