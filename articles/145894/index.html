Teamlab Voices: First Webinar Experience

Webinar today, few people will surprise. However, despite the fairly widespread prevalence, not all companies use this feature. We, being in search of additional communication channels with our users, decided that everything should be tried, and today we want to share our experience with you.



Training

Confession: TeamLab users sometimes behave completely unpredictably, and we cannot always foresee their choice. That is the conclusion we came to during the survey conducted by VKontakte . Asking the users themselves to choose the topic of the first webinar, we were very surprised to receive as the winner the answer that we expected the least.

We didn‚Äôt want to receive as a result of ‚Äúregular_webner_annual_software_company - dreary, mechanical and inanimate‚Äù, therefore we decided that:

  • the webinar should last no more than 30-35 minutes;
  • it should be held in the form of a dialogue between two presenters acting as project manager and employee;
  • summarize the submitted material in the form of visual slides (standard but effective technique)


Program selection

Basic system requirements:

  • interface intuitiveness
  • minimum system requirements
  • support for both Russian and English
  • essential tools needed: desktop demo and chat


One by one we went through the services, marveling at the fact that some of them, in principle, refused to work! During testing, the following systems were considered:

Adobe connect
Brighttalk
Gotowebinar
Mirapolis
Onwebinar
Webinar.ru
Wiziq

As a result, the domestic Webinar.ru system and the product of the American developers GoToWebinar turned out to be optimal for us.

Webinar.ru turned out to be a very convenient program. You don't need to download anything at all, and you can start working almost immediately after registration. From the functionality - the ability to not only demonstrate the desktop, but also load presentations, videos and documents.

We stopped our final choice on GoToWebinar based on the fact that we are planning to hold webinars for an English-speaking audience.

A few words about GoToWebinar

The interface is really very simple and straightforward. The only negative is that there is no Russian language in the settings.
After registration, you are given a free 30-day trial period, after which users make the final choice. The cost of the program varies from $ 99 to $ 499 depending on the number of participants.
Everything that you can is customized: from the type of registration email to the ‚Äúwaiting room‚Äù - what participants see at the webinar ahead of time.





The climax is, of course, the webinar itself. On the desktop of the organizer there appears such a panel.



As you can see, the main feature - desktop demonstration - the displayed windows can also be configured by setting restrictions on pop-up dies and panels.

You can invite new participants during the webinar - just by providing a link.

Audience View registers the attention of participants to the webinar materials.

In the Questions section there are questions from the participants, which are visible only to you.

Chat - messages intended for everyone. We used, for example, to share links to the necessary pages.

Goal achieved

The goal was achieved - the participants showed up on time, were active, asked questions at the end and even asked for a record the next day. Moreover, thanks to the analytics provided by GoToWebinar, it is possible to determine the level of attention of each participant individually. (Interest Rating). This is generally great because no one wants to be a boring speaker, and if it can be seen that the majority of webinar visitors have a low rating, it‚Äôs not possible to keep the attention and, most likely, participants listened to the conference in the background while working in other browser windows.
