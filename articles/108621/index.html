The world record in 3DMark06 is conquered by Russian overclockers!

We have repeatedly shared news with the workers from the overclocking front, but recently the Russians have not spoiled us with records. Enlisting strong support from manufacturers, refilling 150 liters of liquid nitrogen, members of the OCClub.ru team represented by Smoke , slamms and FireVadim today gathered in the test lab of the computer magazine i to conquer new frontiers.



The result of the day was the absolute world record at 3DMark06 - 47,252 marks.



Test bench:
- Intel Xeon W3690 processor;
- RAM 2x2 Gb DDR3 Corsair Dominator GTX3;
- motherboard - EVGA Classified E760;
- 2x HIS Radeon HD5970 Crossfire @ 960/1170 MHz;
- power supply Antec TPQ 1200W;
- hard disk ADATA SSD 128Gb 596;
- operating system Windows Vista SP1.



Such a high result was achieved due to the excellent overclocking potential of the processor. In the absence of a coldbug processor, the 3DMark06 could pass Game Tests at a frequency of 6,500 MHz , and CPU Tests at 6,100 MHz .



To cool the processor, a glass of Finnish overclocking SF3D called Inflection was used.



And this was the world top-20 list to the record! As soon as processor support appears on the ORB, we will be able to officially confirm our result.
