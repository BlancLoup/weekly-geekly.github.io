T-Mobile introduced MyTouch 4G



T-Mobile officially unveiled the MyTouch 4G. The smartphone runs on Android and supports the fourth generation network. MyTouch 4G is equipped with a 3.7-inch WVGA touch screen and an 8 megapixel camera.

Separately, we can say about the camera support functions SweepShot, BurstShot, ClearShot HDR and equipped with dual LED flash.
It also comes with Android 2.3.3 Gingerbread with HTC Sense 3.0 and 1.2 GHz dual-core Qualcomm Snapdragon processor.
T-Mobile MyTouch 4G will be available in July 2011, priced from $ 199.99 (along with a two-year contract).
