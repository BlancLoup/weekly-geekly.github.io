Useful lessons for the programmer

Many probably are looking for such classes, which would be both pleasant for the person and useful in terms of the development of certain skills. For programmers, and indeed people with sedentary work, these classes should be their own.

1) Juggling.

Juggling is a wonderful thing, good for both health and soul. What is the use of juggling?
For a start, this is a good exercise for the eyes, removing tension from them, which is important, given the constant work at the computer. Then, it is physical exercise (albeit small) both on the arms and on the legs (in the beginning you will have to constantly bend down to lift the falling projectiles). Well, in the end, it is a moral satisfaction from the fact that you manage to manipulate objects in the air. Juggling does not require any fixtures. Three (for the beginning of two) items of such a shape are enough to make it convenient to catch and throw up, preferably not too light, but not heavy.
