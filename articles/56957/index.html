Russian operators are testing mobile chat

Good news for anyone who loves to correspond with friends.

The three largest mobile operators in Russia - MTS , Beeline and Megafon - announced the launch of a joint instant messaging service. The service is available to all subscribers of the Big Three and is available free of charge until July 1.

Mobile chat, if someone does not know, is an application that allows you to exchange free messages without using third-party services (like ICQ or Jabber).
