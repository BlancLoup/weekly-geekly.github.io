Master Startups


The presentation of the Master of Technology Entrepreneurship Master Program will begin at 6:00 pm on Thursday, June 9, at the VO Exchange Line, 14, in the QD Business Incubator.

The Master program is unique: in two years you will be able to develop your own technology business, working with a team in the QD student business incubator and get a Master's degree in technological entrepreneurship.
This is not like the classical higher education you are used to. Here you get only the necessary knowledge, there is no water and unnecessary items. All teachers are also business owners.

Open Day traditionally takes place in the format "Call to Action".


Call to Action - more than just an open door - is an immersion in the unique entrepreneurial atmosphere of the magistracy and the incubator.
At Call to Action, you can learn more about the program, features, opportunities, and prospects for studying under the MTE program, chat with undergraduates and teachers.
In a programme:

1. Presentation of the MTE program
2. MTE - life: a tour of the QD incubator
3. Coffee break
4. Questions to residents and undergraduates

It is not necessary to register for the event - it is enough to indicate your name, surname and contact phone number in the comments to the Vkontakte meeting or on the program website.

You can clarify any details by calling 984-18-57 - they will tell you everything: how, where and what will be.

15 budget and only 10 paid places are planned. All non-resident students are provided with a hostel.
