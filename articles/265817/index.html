"Carrot and stick"


We have introduced a system of rewards and punishments. The encouragement was "achivki." They were issued on various occasions, mainly for academic success. Accumulated achievements at the end of the summer school could be exchanged for any of the prizes (there were a lot of them). By the end of the summer school for achivki, a non-sick struggle unfolded, but the prizes went to ALL. For the guys who had 1-3 achivki, we gave a promotional sweetness - for this was purchased 150 chocolates. The punishments were very humane =) Late for the morning scrum had to be wrung out exactly as many times as it was late.
