4 Killer Conversion on Landing Page

Conversion of the Landing Page is a question that concerns both business owners and webmasters. There are thousands of articles on increasing conversion on the Internet. We will talk about four key errors that kill conversion through text, and how to fix them.





The killer number 1. Title

The first thing that sees the visitor Landing Page - header. If he does not cling, then say goodbye to a potential client. The correct title draws attention, prompts to remain on the page, increases conversion several times. How to write a headline that hit the spot? Using 4U technology - Usefulness, Ultra specificity, Urgency, Uniqueness.

Utility - video cameras in Moscow

Uniqueness - with the help of the online store "Excellent camcorders"

As a result, we obtain: Video cameras in Moscow with a 50% discount and a 3-year warranty for 1 hour, using the ‚ÄúExcellent video cameras‚Äù online store

Landing title advertising should be clear and eye-catching. Use word magnets, interrogative sentences, intrigue and numbers.

The killer number 2. First paragraph

Marketers felt that a person determines the benefit of a page in 5-7 seconds. It is important that the first paragraph of the text captivates the reader. Journalists and copywriters use the principle of an inverted pyramid. Its essence is that the author gives important and interesting information at the beginning. Killer number 3. Errors in the text

Spelling and stylistic mistakes are also deadly for a Landing Page conversion, just like a bad headline. Literate text inspires confidence and improves the image of your company. In order not to annoy customers, check the text for errors.

Dozens of sites and online services for spelling and punctuation are working on the Internet.

Killer number 4. The absence of a call to action

Each Landing has a goal: registration, call, order of goods or services. Without a call to action Landing Page does not look complete and ineffective. At the end of the text we have to push the visitor to the decision.
