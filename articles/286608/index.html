Over the past 3 years, the global share of encrypted traffic has increased 3 times (from 4% to 12%). And in China, Syria, Iran and Thailand, all anonymous traffic is blocked.

Since the register of prohibited sites began to work in Russia, 165,000 requests to block resources with illegal content were submitted to Roskomnadzor. Anonymizer programs allow anonymous access to resources that Roskomnadzor sets limits on. And the Tor network provides a proxy mechanism that allows you to establish a connection anonymously and protect against eavesdropping.

While Russia is still debating on this topic, in Belarus, measures on the pre-trial blocking of anonymizers and the Tor network have given the green light.
