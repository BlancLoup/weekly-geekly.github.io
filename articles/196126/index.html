IMotion - 3D motion controller with tactile feedback

You've probably seen how in many science fiction films the characters control the floating interface of the device with just a flick of a wrist? Judging by the new project on the kickstarter, the future is not far off.

iMotion is a versatile controller that combines precise motion recognition and tactile feedback. The controller supports existing applications and games on the PC (Windows, Mac, Linux), iOS, Android mobile applications, as well as Oculus Rift virtual reality glasses. In addition, iMotion can turn your old TV into a Smart TV by replacing the remote control with gesture control



IMotion creates a virtual touch screen that looks like a large space with motion sensitivity in front of you and the tactile feedback function provides a physical sense of touching a virtual object inside a game or application.

Imotion can be used with Any computer that can be controlled using the keyboard and mouse. You can play games and interact with 3D applications with your hands, moving, opening, rotating, and dragging 3D objects with high precision.



An SDK for developers will be available soon. Detailed information on the project page on kickstarter
