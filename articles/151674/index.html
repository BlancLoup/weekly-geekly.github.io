Over the past decade, mobile technologies have become firmly established in our lives, and mobile phones have become a luxury item for everyday communication. Why are mobile technologies so affordable? How will they develop further? Will the speed of mobile data continue to grow? In order to answer a lot of questions in detail, we have developed a cycle of webinars dedicated to mobile technologies.

On Thursday, September 20, during the introductory webinar, we will cover the following topics:
