Traffic police are going to equip computer systems with a Russian processor



Soon, the traffic police units and public service delivery points are going to be equipped with computer systems that operate on the basis of the "Baikal" processor. It is also planned to carry out a simultaneous transition to the Russian protected operating system, writes TASS.

"In addition to the installed servers with Elbrus processors , the traffic police units and public service delivery points will be equipped with workstations built on the Russian Baikal processor this year, with a simultaneous transition to a domestic protected operating system," the department said. According to representatives of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the transition to domestic software is carried out according to plan, in accordance with the instructions of the country's leadership.
