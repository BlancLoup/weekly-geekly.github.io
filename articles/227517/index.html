UPD 1 :
For convenience, I will describe how things are at the moment. If only the cost limit and weight are changed, it will be similar.

Customs duties are not subject to MPO with the following mandatory characteristics:
  • Products must be for personal use.
  • The total amount of goods per month should not exceed 1000 euros per recipient - an individual.
  • The total weight of goods per month should not exceed 31 kg per recipient - an individual.
  • In the case of receiving within one month of goods whose value exceeds 1000 euros and their total weight exceeds 31 kg, customs duties are applied to this excess.


Customs duties have cost and weight norms.
  • If the goods are worth more than the specified norms, then a duty in the amount of 30% of the excess of the standard cost is charged. Shipping costs are not counted.
  • If the imported goods weigh more than the specified norms, then a customs duty in the amount of 4 euros per 1 kg of excess weight is charged.

For example:
The total cost of goods per month is 1,200 euros. Total weight - 20 kg.
At the moment : The duty (1200-1000) * 30% = 200 * 0.3 = 60 euros.
If they accept the project : The duty (1200-150) * 30% = 1050 * 0.3 = 315 euros (by weight the tax is obtained: (20-10) * 4 = 40 euros, but the larger of the two is taken). Of course, you must take into account each individual package, and not the total cost. The norms do not cancel the total monthly limit, but add a restriction on one parcel.
The cost of the package is 130 euros. Package weight - 40 kg.
Currently : Fee (40-31) * 4 = 9 * 4 = 36 euros.
If they accept the project : The duty (40-10) * 4 = 30 * 4 = 120 euros
If both weight and cost are exceeded, the excess is calculated for both indicators and the maximum amount is taken.
