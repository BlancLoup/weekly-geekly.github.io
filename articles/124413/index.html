Abrakadabra - movie catalog ex.ua



I think many of you know about the resource ex.ua.

In order to find the film of interest in the required quality and the required translation, you have to spend a lot of time. The moment came when I was tired of this and decided to make a catalog of films with a filtration system.

A month ago, I launched the project abrakadabra.com.ua . Now, thanks to the filters, you can select for example fantastic comedies of the 90s in French in excellent quality. From the movie page you can go to the corresponding page of ex.ua or to the torrent search page. Now only rutracker.org and 0day.kiev.ua are added.

A couple of days ago they added the 1000th film. After 3-4 months we will finish work on the base ex.ua on films and cartoons. In a month, by September, we will launch a section on the series.

For rating use, as a foundation rating with kinopoisk.ru. That is, by voting on the site, the user increases the rating.

From the additional functionality at the moment there is an opportunity to subscribe to the update of the file list inside the film, that is, if there is no high-quality translation, the user can leave a request and receive a notification when a new file is added.
