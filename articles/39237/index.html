What is Apple preparing for us tomorrow?

Last Tuesday, Apple sent out invitations to an event that will take place on September 9th in San Francisco. Judging by the illustration, which was accompanied by the invitation, we can conclude that it will be entirely devoted to the presentation of a new line of the world's best players - iPod . However, it is likely that the "fruit" company is preparing us and a few surprises.



Let's hold a temporary parallel and refresh the memories of the September Apple events in recent years.

In 2005, the first iPod Nano was introduced. Revolutionary thin, working on a flash drive, he quickly gained popularity. At his presentation, Steve Jobs , well joked that the small pocket on all jeans was made specifically for the Nano .

In 2006, the company introduced the iPod Shuffle to the world, a revised and improved iPod Nano and a new iPod Video , which was a precursor to the concept of mobile video, which today is being successfully implemented on all iPods , with the exception of Shuffle .

Finally, in 2007, Steve Jobs delighted everyone with the Apple product user by updating the entire iPod line.
iPod Shuffle has become cheaper, has acquired many color options for the case. iPod Nano was transformed externally, got the opportunity to play video and got a screen of 320x240 pixels.
The standard iPod received the Classic suffix, became more capacious and, like the Nano , switched to a new firmware.
iPod Touch caused a lot of applause thanks to the revolutionary MultiTouch interface and became the younger brother of the phenomenal iPhone (the face is similar).

What to expect tomorrow?



Of course, the new iPod Nano , whose photos are full of Internet. Streamlined shape, increased display size - such assumptions can be made by looking at the picture, but, most likely, this is not all.

New iTunes 8 with interactive albums and amazing Magnetosphere visualizer.



Updated iPod Touch . Most likely, the changes will affect the design (will be closer to the iPhone 3G ), the amount of memory and the cost of the device.

Chances are low cost MacBook and other computers of the company. Apple has long been thinking about lowering prices in order to overcome the psychological barrier of $ 1,000 . Hopefully, if such an event occurs, it will most certainly affect the cost of beeches in Russia.

Many experts expect the appearance of a new firmware for the iPhone - 2.1 , which should, at a minimum, hide all the bugs present in the previous firmware (without generating new ones), as well as introduce some improvements concerning the work of the Push technology.

A new MacBook Pro may be unveiled tomorrow. Quite a long time waiting for the cardinal lineup of professional notebooks from Apple . Let me remind you that it was the MacBook Air that, before its release, was considered a new Pro , but its computational capabilities and the scarcity of external interfaces did not allow the full realization of these assumptions.

Conclusion


Whatever happens tomorrow, one can say with confidence that pleasant surprises await all of us, only their number varies.
