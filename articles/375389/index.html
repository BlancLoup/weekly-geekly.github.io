A batch of 20,000 tablets Nokia N1 was sold out in 4 minutes and 2 seconds



Journalists of Western technical publications drew attention to the post in Weibo (Chinese equivalent of Twitter), which states that a batch of 20,000 Nokia N1 tablets was sold out in 4 minutes and 2 seconds, there are at least half a million more people in the queue to buy didn't get it.

The tablet went on sale in China on January 7 at a price of 1,599 yuan (about $ 257) with Android 5.0 installed on board with a Nokia Z Launcher launcher. The screen size is 7.9 "with a resolution of 2048x1536 pixels under Gorilla Glass 3.0 glass. The N1 processor is a 64-bit Intel Atom Z3580 (4 cores) with a frequency of 2.3 GHz. The RAM size is 2 GB, there is no memory card support, instead of them there is an internal storage 32 GB, the main camera is 8 megapixel, the front one is 5 megapixel.All this is powered by a 5300 mAh battery. The tablet is equipped with a Type-C USB port that allows you to transfer data at a speed of up to 10 Gb / s; can any party.
