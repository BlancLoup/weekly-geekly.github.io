25-dollar Raspberry Pi went on sale in the USA



The mini PC Raspberry Pi is now more popular than ever. On the basis of this miniature computer, very interesting systems are created, some of which are described on Habr√©. The other day there was good news for fans of these devices: the fact is that the Raspberry Pi Model A went on sale in the USA. True, the reseller alliedelec all available copies dismantled within a few hours, so now you have to wait for the new batch of Raspberry Pi Model A.

It is worth recalling that the Raspberry Pi Model A is not so very different from its older brother, the Raspberry Pi Model B. But the 25-dollar model does not have an Ethernet port, there is only one USB port, plus half the RAM. However, this model consumes about 30% less energy than the Raspberry Pi Model B.

Earlier, the Raspberry Pi Model A went on sale in Europe (in February of that year). In Asia, this model appeared last week, and now representatives of the Habrasoobshchestvo living in the United States can purchase this device from themselves. True, as already mentioned, we will have to wait for the receipt of a new consignment of goods.

According to the developers, several thousand models of devices are being made a week now, so it won't take long to wait. At the moment, over 20 thousand Raspberry Pi Model A have already been sold.

As for the Raspberry Pi Model B, the milestone of 1 million units sold was broken back in January of this year.
