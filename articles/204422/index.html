The Neo900 project completed the first phase of development



The project to develop the Open Hardware version of the famous Nokia N900 , on the OpenMoko hardware platform, Neo900 , completed the first development phase, introducing the motherboard of the future device with LTE soldered modem , just one month after the announcement of the start of development.
The ultimate goal of the project is the ability to provide the owners of the original N900 with a hardware upgrade of their device, by replacing the native motherboard with a motherboard with modern components, an LTE modem, a faster processor and more memory. So in the plans, adding a compass and a gyroscope. Of course, the device must be fully compatible with the native Maemo 5 operating system for the N900.
