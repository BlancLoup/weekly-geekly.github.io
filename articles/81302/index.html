Alfresco integrates its solutions with IBM Lotus

Alfresco posted on its website a short demo video of the new Content Services for Lotus solution. The system is designed to manage content directly from Lotus Quickr, Lotus Notes and Lotus Connections. The beta should be released in February, at the moment Lotus users have access to a technical preview (simple registration is required), and in the form of implementation on AWS.
