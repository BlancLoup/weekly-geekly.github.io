The functionality of the controller KUB-Micro / 60

  • Log in "Counter" - readings from the pulse output of the meter electricity.

  • Established node voltage measurement station power 12 and 60 V.

  • Exit "Remote control" - restart of telecommunication equipment when hanging up or controlling various equipment from the control center.
  • Exit "Automatic control" - control equipment and restart in automatic mode.
  • RS232 / RS485 ‚Äúthrough channel‚Äù port - connection of intelligent equipment: electricity, water, heat metering devices with RS485 interface, power supply unit, photo recorder, etc.

The functionality of external expansion modules that can be connected to KUB-Micro / 60


So, as we have said, external expansion modules (BMP) can be connected to KUB-Micro / 60 to increase the number of control points or to expand the functionality of the monitoring system. VMPs are inexpensive, compact devices that do not have their own channel of communication with the center and transmit data to the software through the main controller, in this case, via KUB-Micro / 60. Up to 14 BMPs of the same or various types can be connected to KUB-Micro / 60 via RS485 communication interface. RS485 allows you to remove slaves from the KUB-Micro / 60 at a distance of up to 100 m.

Here we list only the most requested external extension modules we have developed. So let's get started:


IS485 (Intelligent Reader) provides security, authorization of access by a Chip-key or Proximity-card and controls the electric lock on the object. The IS485 is available in two versions: for indoor installation and for outdoor placement.

PST485 (Fire Alarm and Automatic Fire Extinguishing Installation Control Module) can be used both on unattended and on serviced sites. It is intended:

  • to control the fire,
  • to enable personnel alert devices (sirens, light displays),
  • for control of technological equipment in case of fire (for example, de-energization of an object),
  • for control and management of fire extinguishing installation on 1 direction.

In addition, PST485 can receive a signal from a third-party alarm system about arming / disarming and entering a control object in order to prevent exposure of people to a fire extinguishing agent.

DVT485 (Humidity and Temperature Sensor) is designed to measure temperature and humidity. Available in 3 versions: DVT485 for measuring temperature and relative humidity at one point, DVT485 in DT mode for measuring only the temperature at one point, DVT485 in 2 DT mode for measuring the temperature at 2 points.

PT485 (Temperature Controller) is used to control the temperature at the facility: when the critical temperature is reached, the system can automatically turn on the air conditioner or heater.

EPU485 (Control unit of energy parameters) is designed to control the operation of electrical conversion plants. It provides remote reading of readings from the pulse (telemetric) output of the electric meter, measurement of voltage and currents on a three-phase power input, as well as measurement of station supply voltage, charge current and discharge current of the battery.

AKB485 (Battery control unit) is designed to measure the key parameters of batteries - voltage and temperature - on monoblocks with voltages of 12V, 6V, 2V. Able to provide regular monitoring of a lead-acid battery or a chain of series-connected batteries located on a telecommunications facility. Together with AKB485, miniature ICA modules are used that are placed directly on the batteries.
