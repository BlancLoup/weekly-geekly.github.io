Google's official blog has been hacked by hackers

The official Google blog was hacked tonight. Hackers have posted a post in it that the company stops developing a click-to-call program.

Already deleted by administrators post was posted here . It was reported that the click-to-call program, developed by Google for use in contextual advertising and recently opened for use in the UK, is closed.

Despite the fact that the post was quickly removed, some users managed to read it on the pages of the blog and in their RSS-readers. The fact that it is - fake, says a large number of spelling and grammatical errors in the text.



This is not the first Google blog incident. In March 2006, one of the employees accidentally deleted the company's blog, with the result that someone even managed to register the blog address for themselves. As now, at that moment the problem was promptly resolved.
