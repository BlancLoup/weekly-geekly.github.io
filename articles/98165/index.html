I finally arrived at the end of April ordered by New IT GuruPlug Server Plus. They poisoned him as a result of all intersections with the volcano only in May, and he reached my post office only in July. Specifications


Sheeva processor:
  • 1.2 GHz
  • L1 cache: 16Kb instructions + 16Kb data
  • L2 cache: 256Kb

Hardware support for algorithms:
  • AES, DES, 3DES, SHA1 and MD5

Memory:
  • 512MB DDR2 400MHz
  • 512MB NAND flash downloadable

Periphery:
  • 2 1Gb Ethernet ports
  • 1 eSATA 2.0 port
  • 2 USB 2.0 ports
  • 1 microSD slot inside
  • 1 microSD slot outside
  • Wi-Fi 802.11b / g
  • Bluetooth: 2.1 / EDR
  • U-SNAP I / O
  • 7x GPIO for user 5 with 3.3VI / O, 2 with 1.8VI / O
