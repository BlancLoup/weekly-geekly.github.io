iPhone as a game console

For the past six months, I have acquired an iPhone and every day it makes me happy more and more. Today we talk about the iPhone, as a game console. About a year ago, games with a decent plot and good graphics could be counted on the fingers of one hand, but now the situation has changed radically. Game manufacturers realized that creating versions of their creations for the iPhone is quite profitable. And in the next couple of years, the Apple phone may well compete for the PSP. Enough talk, let's take a look at the new items over the past couple of months:

Well, probably the most anticipated racing simulator was Need For Speed ‚Äã‚ÄãUndercover . Electronic Arts mine squeezed the maximum phone capabilities.


Graphics, management, a wide range of sports cars - all at the highest level! There is the possibility of both tuning the speed capabilities of the car, and its appearance.


The game has a plot, and not just the same type of race, as in most racing simulators for the iPhone. To this add also great music and get candy. There is of course a fly in the ointment. On the passing game takes about half a day. But trust me, the game is worth its $ 10.

The next novelty is Assassin's Creed - Altair's Chronicles . Action game. You are transported to Jerusalem in the days of the Crusaders.


You play as Altair, who must kill 8 people to end the third crusade. The game is simply excellent graphics and well thought out gameplay. Deficiencies not seen. The game is clearly worth its $ 6.

And what to play our beautiful half. Just the other day, the Sims 3 was released.


This pleasure costs $ 10. The game was released quite recently, and already in the second place in the rating of paid programs in the App Store.

For fans of something simpler and cheaper, there are also lots of great games. For example Battleship , the toy is completely free.


There are also solitaires, poker, checkers, chess, they are either free or cost $ 1.

Of the inexpensive games, I would especially like to highlight 2 games.

The first is World Cup Ping Pong - a table tennis simulator. The developers have made a very convenient control. In World Cup Ping Pong you can play for 1 representative from the list of 8 countries.


You can choose the type of racket. There are 4 modes of difficulty. And 4 game modes: tournament, training, playing on the accuracy of the strike and the possibility of playing together against each other. The cost of the game is only $ 1. There is a stripped down free version.

The second game is StickWars . Also worth $ 1 and with a stripped-down free version. The goal of the game is to protect the fortress from the attack of a mob of enemies.


The game has several difficulty modes. The gameplay is simple but addictive. A large number of levels.

As you can see, the iPhone has a great advantage compared to the same phones from nokia, samsung, htc and others, in the form of a huge database of games at affordable prices collected in one place and presented in a user-friendly form. I hope Apple will continue to develop its product in this direction.
