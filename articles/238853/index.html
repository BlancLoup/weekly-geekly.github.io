English words with pictures and pronunciation from a native speaker


There are many options for learning foreign languages ‚Äã‚Äã- the use of a mobile application has recently appeared among them. However, most of them are either primitive dictionaries or regular textbook exercises translated into digital format. It is clear that in this way it is very difficult to get new knowledge, and you have to return to the time-tested courses and private lessons.


Nevertheless, there is a way to effectively get an education using a mobile device - EngCards mobile application is ready to help you in the difficult task of learning the most popular English words . In the application of their already 3500 thousand.


It is based on the author's technique of frequency repetition. Each word after it has been learned will be returned to the educational process after a certain optimal time and so several times. Until the word is deposited in the subconscious and remembered forever.

After starting the program, you will be asked to choose one of four modes:

It is advisable to start with Learn , that is, study. Your attention are cards with certain concepts that have a signature. If you have the opportunity to use the sound, then you can also listen to the correct pronunciation of the word. Please note - the program uses only the voice recording of the native speakers of the English language, and not the automatic "reader", as many programs to memorize English words. As the technique suggests, at the beginning the words will be repeated very often, so that you can memorize them better.


However, later the frequency of the appearance of certain cards will become less frequent, and, appearing on the screen 5 more times, they will completely disappear from the training program. Thus, the method of repetition is implemented.




Do you feel that you are already strong enough in English? Then you need to check your knowledge! The EngCards app (iOS and Android) has prepared three test modes at once in which you can confirm your strength. Dictation is useful to those who want to perceive the foreign language well by ear - in fact, the standard dictation, in which only the sound recording is played, and you have to specify the English word yourself. Want to train your memory at the same time? Then welcome to the Write section - you will be shown pictures under which you need to make the appropriate signatures.


Finally, you can use the test favorite of many students, which offers four possible answers, from which you must choose the right one.


But do not relax - the program selects only answers that are close in meaning, so it will not be very easy to choose the right one.

It is worth paying attention to the modes of operation that have appeared in the new version of the program. You can choose from four levels of knowledge corresponding to the classification developed by experts from Oxford :
  • Elementary
  • Pre-intermediate
  • Intermediate
  • Upper intermediate

In total, the program has 3500 different cards - this is quite enough to communicate with native speakers at a good level. If you are interested in a particular subject, then you can select the corresponding section using the menu - this function is useful for tourists who plan to visit a certain place. Here you can also choose parts of speech - not only nouns, adjectives, verbs, and others, but also numerals that are vital for the modern information society can be included in the selection.
