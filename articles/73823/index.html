Windows Live Custom Domains in Russian


In pursuit of the news about the launch of Yandex mail for domains. For some reason, almost no mention is made of the fact that the Russian version of Windows Live Custom Domains has been available since August 4 .

The service allows you to bind your domain to Windows Live Services and register user accounts in it.

An unlimited number of mailboxes are declared, 5 gigabytes each (500 are available immediately, more on request). Plus - the accounts will work in Live Messanger, yeah. If you do not need mail, you can connect the domain only for Live Messenger, leaving the mail on your server.



In addition, you can write your own applications to extend functions using the Windows Live Custom Domains SDK . Also, you can create domains with closed and open membership (anyone can register).
