Comparison of Internet payment acceptance services for individual entrepreneurs

Earlier I published two articles ( 1 , 2 ) on how to receive payments on the site. In previous articles there was one condition - connection to payment systems was made as an individual. Now I decided to get out of the twilight to do everything as honestly as possible, and I want to talk about how to connect to payment systems as an IP and legal withdrawal of money earned to a bank account.

Unfortunately, the process of connecting to payment systems as an IP is always overshadowed by the fact that in order to withdraw money (and sometimes just to go to your personal account), you need to sign an agreement and wait until it comes in paper form to the payment system. therefore, in this article in most cases I will describe only the theoretical and informational parts of cooperation with payment systems and aggregators.

In general, in order to connect the acceptance of payments, it is necessary to exchange an agreement with the payment system, so you cannot count on a quick connection. Plus - this agreement must be manually processed, so if you do not take into account the time spent on sending documents by Russian Post, then the connection takes from three days. The package of documents for all is about the same, but sometimes a copy of some document certified by a notary or, for example, a fresh extract from the EGRIP may be required.

In the case of IP, connecting to WebMoney is quite simple: you need to have a personal passport of the individual and ‚Äúbind‚Äù the existing WMID to the IP. The connection happens quite quickly, after filling in the data on the site, a letter from the WebMoney employee comes with instructions and a contract. The contract must be printed out, signed and sent by regular mail along with the rest of the documents. From small drawbacks: the contract must be self-stitched (for a programmer this may cause slight difficulties, but a chocolate bar and any familiar girlfriend solve this problem) and make a notarial copy of one of the documents. Otherwise, I did not have any problems, and the connection took 5 days, taking into account the delivery of documents by courier.

After checking the documents for the specified WMID, it is possible to withdraw money to the PI account. That is, in fact, connecting to WebMoney as an IP is no different from working as a physical. persons, except for the need to exchange documents. Money in the same way goes to WebMoney wallets, the withdrawal fee is completely the same - 0.8%. Automatic payment acceptance is configured completely in the same way - as for physical. faces.

Pros : low commission, fast connection
Cons : this is not an aggregator, but a payment system, therefore only WebMoney can be accepted



Yandex.Cassa - a project from Yandex.Money for receiving payments in various ways. In addition to Yandex.Money through the Cashier, you can pay using bank cards, WebMoney, through cash salons, from the balance of a mobile phone and through the Internet banking of some banks. After making the payment to the users, the money is transferred to the bank account on the next day. The commission at Yandex.Cash is rather high - from 2.8% to 5%. The percentage depends on the turnover and on the type of goods: if you sell services or digital content, the rate will be higher. The largest percentage (5%) is obtained when paying for digital content or Yandex.Money services, and this percentage does not depend on turnover.

As indicated on the Yandex.Cash website, you only need to provide an IP passport for the connection and usually the connection process takes no more than three days. After that, you must provide a signed contract and exchange acts once a month. From the ‚Äúcashier‚Äù cashier you can note the following:
  • Card binding: the user binds his card once and can make the following payments without re-entering data.
  • Repeat payments: you can set up automatic debiting of money from the card, if the service works by subscription.
  • Preauthorization: the ability to debit money not at the time of the transaction, but after some time.
  • Mobile terminal: the ability to accept payments from cards via smartphone.


Pros : the presence of interesting "buns", you only need a passport to connect
Cons : high commission when paying via Yandex.Money, there is no mobile application
