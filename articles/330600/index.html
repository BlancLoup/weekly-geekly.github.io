The third IT conference GeekDay - three days of free programming workshops


News for those who want to get an IT-profession: from 22 to 24 June will be an online IT-conference GeekDay # 3. During this time, you can listen to 20 free master classes in various areas of programming and development.


What is waiting for you on the third GeekDay?


  1. You will learn how to develop and customize an Android application, how to create several applications for iOS and a 2D game. Understand how to make the code concise and beautiful.
  2. At the master classes you will be able to chat with professional practicing programmers of the Senior level. Each speaker has a specialized education, solid professional experience and extensive experience in developing services and applications for large companies (Mail.Ru Group, MegaFon, Beeline).
  3. Communication and exchange of experience with experts will help you to formulate an idea and realize your project.

Conference speakers:


  • Sergey Kamyanetsky , Senior C # Developer. Together with Sergey, you will develop a 2D game and learn how to teach game objects to interact with the user and with each other.
  • Yuri Zhayvoronok , Senior Web Developer Mail.ru Group, worked on HYKL with Berkeley University. He will tell you how an ordinary programmer can succeed in IT.
  • Evgeny Kartavets , Senior .Net Developer, Team Lead, Head of GeekBrains Training. Teach the basics of the PLO and give recommendations for further development.
  • Igor Filimonov , head of web development at MacroIndex. From him you will learn the tricks of working with CMS-platforms and in an hour you will create your blog.
  • Alexey Kadochnikov , professional web developer. In his lecture, you will learn how to make an adaptive from a fixed layout, and you can put knowledge into practice.
  • Kirill Lukyanov , a professional iOS developer with more than 13 years of experience in IT. Teach you to create a task list and iOS application for video streaming.
  • Alexander Fisunov , software developer with many years of experience in large projects. Will tell about the possibilities of Java - the most popular programming language in the world.
  • Alexander Yerofeev , employee, teacher and graduate student of SPbPU. He will tell you how to create websites, applications, presentations and other IT objects using HTML / CSS.
  • Vladimir Yazykov , a professional web developer with experience in implementing large projects. You will learn about the BEM methodology and will be able to avoid the classic mistakes for a beginner.
  • Vasily Deryabin , creator and managing partner in the web-studio "Visario.ru". Teach you to create selling landings for startups without programming skills.
  • Roman Muratov , professional C # developer. Will tell about interactions with game objects and features of the implementation of gaming equipment.
  • Yury Afanasyev , Senior PHP Developer at Alpari. You will learn about ways to make the code concise and beautiful and get tips for developing from scratch to Senior level.

After signing up for participation in GeekDay, you will be able to attend free preparatory workshops to feel confident for the three days of the conference itself.


You can register for the conference or view the broadcast on the conference website . Waiting for you!
