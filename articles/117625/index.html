And what are you flying to work?



Finnish aviation engineer Aki Suokas (Aki Suokas) presented at the event Aero Friedrichshafen (International Aviation Exhibition) his aircraft FlyNano. The device weighs 70 kg, is completely made of carbon composite materials, lands and takes off from the surface of the water. There are three working options - a model with a 20-kilowatt electric motor and two models with a gasoline engine with a power of 24 and 35 hp. respectively. FlyNano can fly at a speed of 140 km / h, the ceiling height is 3 km. Even more striking is the price of the device - 27,000 EUR (for the evaluation it is worth recalling that the price of the AgustaWestland-139 helicopter, on which Ukrainian President Viktor Yanukovych is going to fly, is about $ 17 million).

Under the cut more details and photos of FlyNano.

The wingspan of the FlyNano is about 5 meters, the maximum weight that the device can lift is 200 kg, the range of flight speeds is from 70 km / h to 140 km / h, the distance it can fly after charging (or refueling) 70 km.



Since the weight of the FlyNano is 70 kg, in this class of aircraft the driver does not need a license for the driver .
