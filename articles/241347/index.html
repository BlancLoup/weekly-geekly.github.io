Introduction to the home automation system HDL

Technology "smart home" is designed to provide the maximum level of comfort for the owners of an apartment or house. It will help save time for doing household chores, because automation is now responsible for this, which controls and controls many household processes.

"Smart Home" - an integrated technical solution, implemented through software and hardware. These include: electricity consumers, household and technical appliances, various subsystems, engineering devices, which are combined into a single home control system.

We got into the hands of a demonstration set of the firm HDL, engaged in automated systems.

This device vividly demonstrates the functionality of the automation systems of the Smart Home:
  • light control (5 built-in dimmable LED lamps, 8 small light bulbs on the relay channels, motion sensor functions, light sensor, lighting scenarios);
  • climate control (climate pages on DLP, thermosensor on DLP, heating control through a relay, infrared control of air conditioning, climate control functions from the touch screen);
  • music and video control (Z-Audio, plays music from an SD card, FM tuner or from a local input, can be connected to a computer network, IR control of devices, RS232 / RS485 control of third-party devices);
  • security system (motion detection, security controller, installation of modes with DLP and touch screen), relay operation.

You can also program various combinations of device operation due to the Logic Module.
