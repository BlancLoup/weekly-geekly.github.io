How it all began.
I accidentally found out that competitions on snowmobiles are scheduled tomorrow. Usually I used a DSLR, but this time I wanted to have a shooting point that no one else has, and remembered the new phone with a good camera. I thought, why not make this relatively light phone, with no optical zoom, as close as possible to the snowmobiles, or even above them!
The most suitable object for an extension cord is a telescopic fishing pole: 6 meters in my case. It has a small folded size, lightweight, durable, and is designed for approximately the same and even greater weight of the fish.
How to combine the bait and phone? Time was running out.
The phone with a rubber band secured to a light aluminum plate left over from a broken antenna. The bar had to be deformed a bit to shift the center of gravity.
The site turned out simple, reliable and allowed to instantly install the phone.



How now to fix the bar on the bait so that the phone easily and automatically, without any sensors and actuators took the horizontal position?
It has been proven that in our country the law of world wide work worked long before its discovery by Newton, so you can safely apply this effect without any danger in that someone's patent will be violated.
A semicircular plug from the system unit came in handy. It secured the screw - the axis of rotation. The fate of the phone depends on the wide washer having an outer diameter slightly smaller than the diameter of the hole in the bar.
In order to reduce squeaks and improve sliding, screwed on two small textolite washers on the screw threads, at the same time they served as a gap stop.
Cap stitched to the third link of the telescopic fishing rod - the two top links are very flexible and I had to remove them inside ...



The installation process: the phone is fixed on the bar, then the hole in the bar is passed through the washer on the plug - everything is quick and easy.
The proportions of a millimeter of diameter difference theoretically do not allow the bar with the phone to fall out of the mount.
The phone has a good location for this type of shooting buttons. After mounting on the bar, the buttons are in the upper part, which protects them when the body touches snow or soil.
