The challenge for the competition is to write your own Hummingbird game . This should be done before the New Year 2015, Moscow time, i.e. until December 31, 2014 24:00 MSK

For the game, you can use any programming language - at least FASM (preferably), at least JAVA, at least Brainfuck .
