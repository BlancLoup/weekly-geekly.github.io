WineGame 0.0.1 RC

Quietly and quietly a few minutes ago, the WineGame 0.0.1 Release Candidate came out.

WineGame is a small program written using Qt4 that makes it easy to install various applications and games in Wine .

What can be installed now with its help:

- FIFA 10
- FIFA Manager 10
- PhotoShop CS4
- Age of Empires II / III
- NFS UnderCover
- Diablo II

and some other games.
Official site .

Sources (GPL3)
