Now Yota 4G Internet has become profitable

From October 18, all our customers will have the opportunity to choose for themselves the optimal combination of speed and cost of Internet access, as well as change the connection conditions at any time depending on their needs. You do not have to worry about the volume of downloaded traffic, the Internet from Yota is still unlimited.




We have created this service so that any client of our company can independently control the consumption of the Internet when it needs it.



In the same way, as it has become customary to adjust the water pressure or the brightness of the lighting in the room, the client can independently change the speed of Internet access and determine the cost of the service every second, depending on current needs. If you need to surf the sites, set a low speed and pay a smaller amount. If you want to quickly download a movie or watch videos online without delay, you can temporarily increase the speed, and after downloading you can return to its original position. You can change your speed through your Personal Account at any given time as many times as you like, without additional payment for the operation itself.

The cost of services is from 500 to 1400 rubles per month. The speed is from 320 Kbps to 10 Mbps. New terms of connection are available in all cities where there is Yota.

If you run out of money in your account, you will remain connected to the Internet at a speed of 64 downlink / 32 uplink without the ability to adjust the Yota 4G speed on the scale until you deposit money into your account again. You can also enjoy free internet access with the ability to turn on the maximum speed for 2 hours for 50 rubles. You can turn on the maximum speed an unlimited number of times. The speed will be unlimited.

The new offer will work on all devices. The exception is Jingle and Samsung modems, if they are purchased for the shares "1,400 rubles for the month of unlimited Internet and the Jingle modem included" and "2,800 rubles for two months of unlimited Internet and Samsung modem included". Participants of such shares will be able to connect to the Yota 4G offer only after the expiration of the promotion - a month or two, respectively.

All our services remain roaming as always. All conditions in your home region will apply in any other where Yota is.
