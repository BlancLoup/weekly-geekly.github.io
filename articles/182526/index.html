Bitbucket released a limited edition of branded T-shirts

Hello community!

In honor of its 1 million users, Bitbucket.org offers to buy a limited series of T-shirts with its brand with a 50% discount on a subsidiary of AtlassianSwag . The cost of such a t-shirt is $ 20. The service (bitbucket) gives a $ 10 discount coupon when inviting new users to the repository.



There are both male and female models with a full range of sizes.




I think that's pretty.

The only trouble is that you need to pay another $ 16.99 (USPS International) or $ 59.95 (UPS International Ground) for delivery, if you want to have an international tracking number of the parcel to track the package. Expensive for such joy =) Ordered (with delivery for $ 16.99 + $ 10), which is approximately as if in a store, but branded for fans of the whole computer and this service in particular.
