Free invitations to the Software People conference

On April 22-23, the second International Software People 2010 Conference will be held, dedicated to software development. Among the issues addressed, special attention is paid to: cloud computing, Agile (SCRUM, Lean), the future development of the F # language, GTD, MBO, application architecture, testing, usability, management of development teams, project management and software development risks, and much more.
Also within the framework of the conference in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Minsk a series of seminars devoted to the management of development teams will be held. Reads the seminar Peter Khrushchka - author of the famous book "Balding from adrenaline and zombie templates."

Participation in a seminar and a conference is paid, but for Habr users, the organizers play three free invitations to the conference and one invitation to the seminar in any of the three cities (your choice).
