Hello, I am your new Russian netbook



Alt Linux has spread the vigorous information that a new Russian netbook, Lynbuk Patriot Aquarius NS500, has appeared on the shelves.

It is indicated that the novelty is based on the ALT Linux operating system, it is compact in size (26x18x3 cm) and light in weight - 1.2 kg. The diagonal of the screen "Linbuka" - 10.2 inches. The netbook is equipped with an Intel Atom 1.6 GHz processor, 1 or 2 GB of RAM, and an 80, 120, or 250 GB hard drive, depending on configuration. In addition, the machine has three USB ports, an SD + MMC + MS card reader, a VGA output, an Ethernet (RJ45) port, stereo speakers, and microphone and headphone jacks. Optionally for use of a netbook in video conferencing, delivery with a high-resolution webcam is possible. The novelty supports the standard of wireless networks 802.11, as well as the Bluetooth interface. The model is given a 2 year warranty (1 year for batteries).

Everything would be wonderful, but ... firstly, the price for this miracle of the domestic netbook industry starts from 17,100 rubles (1 GB of memory, 80 GB of screws) and stretches to the level of 19,060 rubles (2 GB of memory, 250 GB screw). Of course, it is clear from all sides that real patriotism is expensive, but IMHO, 17 thousand is not expensive, it is expensive.
