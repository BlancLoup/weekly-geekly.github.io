RURU: Payment system from Alfa-Bank and Beeline

RURU, as a universal payment system, allows you to make payments from various sources of funds: the personal account of a Beeline subscriber (as well as other mobile operators), funds on VISA and MasterCard bank cards, Web Money purses (in the future - and other e-wallets), etc.

Payment of goods and services of the partners of the system will be possible with the help of the websites RURU and Beeline, through payment widgets on the partner websites of RURU and through a special application for mobile phones, including iPhone and communicators on Android OS. Commission for payments made will be from 0 to 5.95% of the transaction amount.
