Computer games are a new field of art. Who draws them? How are they created? How to become a game artist / designer? Listeners will find out the answers to these and many other questions during the lecture.

We are on the threshold of a new era, and this is the era of computer games and simulations. For a long time, psychologists have found that the human brain does not distinguish reality from fiction or memory, it experiences a certain life experience, and this experience is important for us, whatever the source.

One day, the best of those worlds that artists now create for games will take their place in the history of world art, fall into the galleries and exhibition spaces of the future and become the subject of controversy by art historians and cultural scientists.
