Reid against Pirate Bay changed Sweden

A lot has happened since the Swedish police tried to stop Piratbay. It's time to summarize and try to predict further events.

The Torrentfreak editors turned to Mathias Hellsten, author of the Piracy Unlimited blog, and a Swedish citizen for comment. Here is how he explains what is happening:

Firstly, the attack by the authorities was extremely unsuccessful - Pirate Bay remained inaccessible for only a couple of days, after which it resumed its work in full. He even strengthened his position - thanks to wide coverage of the situation in the media. Then, several sources reported that the American Cinema Association (MRAA) was the initiator of the persecution. She put pressure on Swedish government officials, in particular, on the Minister of Justice, trying to get his personal intervention in this particular situation, which is considered illegal in Sweden. When the Swedish television published documents confirming the stated version , intense discussions continued in the media, thanks to which the number of people sympathizing with the pirate movement increased. Suddenly, the party became the largest among the movements not represented in the current parliament. In Sweden, an organization gains parliamentary seats with four percent of the vote. General elections will be held at the end of September, so the passions around the election campaign are increasingly heating up.
