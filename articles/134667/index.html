People's Award Nevosoft

For the second year in a row we conduct online voting for the best casual game in Russian. The competition is attended by downloadable games that were released on the portal Nevosoft.ru over the past year. The prize is awarded to software companies in three main categories: Best Quest, Best Logic Game, Best Simulator / Time Management.




The popular vote takes place in two stages. The winners are awarded commemorative award signs (statues) containing the symbolism of the People's Award Nevosoft, the name of the company, the nomination and the winning game.

Hardcore players are usually skeptical (I think most of them are on Habr√©) - and in many respects because they never play them :) Modern casual games are far from Tetris.

The average budget for a high-quality downloadable game is from 100 thousand dollars, the duration of the gameplay is at least 4 hours. Often used 3D-graphics and professional voice.

In general, voting for the best games started: the first stage will last until December 21, the second - until December 26. Laureates, which will be the company-developers, will be announced on December 27.
