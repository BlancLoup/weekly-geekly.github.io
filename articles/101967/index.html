Evernote Gallery: Awesome Note Organizer

Posted by: David Pierce freelance browser Evernote



This moment has come. I reached the point where I can not remember anything without first writing it into the phone. If something is not in my calendar, I will not be there. If something is not on my to-do list, there is not a chance that it will be done.

Evernote is always at hand with me, it helps me to memorize and quickly find all my thoughts and notes, and I thought about how to embed it even deeper into my life aimed at solving problems. For example, I'm going to go shopping next week (I know about it because I wrote it on the calendar) - what about the shopping list? Or do I plan to get to the party on Friday - at what address does it go, and what should I take with me?

Rescues awesome note . This application for the iPhone, presented in the Evernote Gallery, which allows you to solve all these and many other issues. This is a program in which you can create notes, manage tasks and keep a calendar, and at the same time it can synchronize with Evernote, so that all your information becomes available, no matter where you are.

Why is it interesting


Awesome Note allows you to create notes, group them into folders and synchronize with Evernote. Individual notes can also be exported from one application to another. What impressed me most was how this synchronization was implemented. Awesome Note creates folders with names like ‚Äú[aNote] Folder Name‚Äù in your Evernote account, and any notes you put into these folders will be automatically synchronized between the two applications.

Awesome Note also integrated Evernote with a task list and calendar, allowing you to attach Evernote notes to created tasks and events. Thanks to this, one quick glance at a calendar or a task list is enough to get all the necessary information for further actions.

You can send notes to other Awesome Note users via Bluetooth or send text notes and tasks via email. Among other things, this is a very beautiful personalized application. You can give it any kind of paper, from a pile of paper to a chalk board, everything is done so that you can enjoy working with notes.

Evernote Gallery: Other Efficiency Apps (GTD)


In the Evernote Gallery, you can find all sorts of applications to increase efficiency from the Awesome Note presented here to tools like Nozbe and Reqall . Any of them in one way or another can work with your Evernote account. Just browse the Gallery to find the perfect app for yourself.
