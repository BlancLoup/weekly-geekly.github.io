Freeloot: is freelance free?

Welcoming all habrizhiteley, I want to talk about a project that will help make the world a better and fairer. No, this is not a donation site or a society of young communists. This is a new representative of the exchanges of distant work, in other words, freelancing. I, as one of the creators, will be grateful for the attention that you can give to this post, telling about our ideology and about our belief in free and free freelancing.


Homemade banner

Start


It all began last summer, when our main instigator, unknown to me until that moment, contacted me and started talking about remote work. And both of us faced a problem: freelance exchanges do not allow beginners to themselves, enclosing themselves with paid services and, to put it mildly, questionable interface.

There is a problem? We must solve it. I liked his idea and we started cooperation, which turned out to be very fruitful. After a month of work, there was also a young, self-seeking web designer. By the way, we attracted to the development of the project so once such beginner talents for her learning and replenishing their own experience. We must remember that if a designer comes to the team, everything will change dramatically. And so it happened: we changed the interface and added new non-boring wallpaper buns, spending even more time to develop.


So it was before ...


And so now.

And I understand that everything would have been done much faster and more qualitatively, more interesting and more beautiful, but our project lived only with the strength of our enthusiasm and the help of volunteers. So meet - the first free and completely free freelance exchange Freeloot spreads its wings.

Well aware that the idea will not be able to pull out the project, we are not without surprises. For freelancers, the response template features, a single account and the absence of any restrictions on responses will be useful. For customers, personal messages and task services will become indispensable, which allows you to see how the order is moving, what problems remain and that you are always ready to provide statistics on order fulfillment. All the rest will be pleased with not only a clean interface in the best traditions of Material, but also the expected applications for all platforms.

And once we have declared on Habr, then sin will not tell you about the technical stuffing. At least for the sake of tradition. The project uses one of the popular back-end frameworks, runs on a completely normal server, but has https , and, most importantly, we are preparing an API .

As a conclusion


We invite all interested Habrahbra users to participate in the development of the resource. Personally, I will be glad to see any of you in our team, be it a programmer or a manager. And especially we are valuable people who are not yet the gurus of their work, but they really want it. We will help them, giving invaluable experience with a real project.

Or just come to see and, perhaps, even leave a response to the order.

We believe that freelancing should be independent, not demanding money. For all and always.
