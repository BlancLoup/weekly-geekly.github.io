Ya.Subbotnik in Minsk

June 2, 2012 in Minsk will be held the second J. Subbotnik.

Employees of the Moscow, St. Petersburg, Simferopol and Minsk offices of Yandex will talk about technologies, solutions and useful practices that may be of interest to developers.

Come, listen, ask questions and chat on professional topics over a cup of coffee.

On our J. Subbotnik you will find out:
  • Pro methods for the classification of documents in the search;
  • Why do you need a search for the site, how to work with it and improve it;
  • About the history of the creation of the BEM methodology (Block. Element. Modifier);
  • About the use of custom fonts;
  • About AJAX in the search for the web;
  • How do we do code review;
  • How does the new JS API Yandeks.Kart 2.0;
  • About the plans and reality of the Yandex.Disk architecture;
  • How to start developing for WP7;
  • And how not to shoot yourself in the foot, localizing applications for iOS. :)

Bonus: the story of Alexei Sikorsky, head of development in Belarus, about the office in Minsk.

Participation in Ya. Subbotnik is free by appointment .

Registration for the event ends on June 1 at 19:00 Moscow time.
Limited number of seats.
June 2, Saturday, from 9:30 am we are waiting for the participants at the address: Minsk, ul. Kirov, 13, hotel Crowne Plaza, King Hall .

How to get to the venue of Ya.Subbotnik:
The Crowne Plaza Hotel is located in the city center, next to Ploshchad Lenina Metro Station and the Central Railway Station.

For those who do not fall into the number of participants or will not be able to personally attend Y.Subbotnik, an online broadcast will be organized.
