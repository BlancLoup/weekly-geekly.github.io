The official trailer for the movie The Pirate Bay Away From Keyboard



Last fall, it became aware of serious intentions from the Swedish director Simon Klose to make a film about Pirate Bay, the most famous and most recalcitrant bittorrent website of our Galaxy.

Financing of the production of the film was carried out on the principle of crowdfunding. You can assess the degree of interest in the project by the fact that the amount received exceeded the requested amount more than twice - Kickstarter users issued $ 51,424, and the initial timid request for 25 thousand was satisfied in just three days. Also, the film was sponsored by the Swedish Art Grants Committee in the amount of 200 thousand Swedish crowns (about 31 thousand dollars).

It is noticeable that the work is going on and the release of the film is just around the corner: in August of the past year, the first teaser came out, and a few hours ago the official movie trailer appeared above. In the latter, in contrast to the teaser, there are recordings of interviews and fragments of filming trials against the founders of Pirate Bay - Gottfried Svartholm, Frederick Neiyoma and Peter Sunde.

The name of the film beats the expression that the trio denotes the offline world - Away From Keyboard (away from the keyboard) instead of In Real Life (in real life), arguing that this is the reality of the Internet. The filmmakers focus on the fight against Hollywood as the world of traditional and outdated content distribution methods.

The release of the film will be held in the spring of 2013, but the exact date has not yet been named . The trailer is distributed under the license CC-BY-NC-SA , and it is likely to expect distribution of the film itself under a similar or the same Creative Commons license. In any case, it is precisely known that the film will be distributed absolutely free of charge through the Pirate Bay itself.
