Alexander Zharov asks Telegram to register in the registry in order to avoid blocking

Roskomnadzor again threatened to block the Telegram service if it did not send the questionnaire and did not register in the register of information dissemination organizers (ARI). By law, information dissemination organizers are required to store user data in the territory of the Russian Federation for one year and provide them at the request of special services.

This time, the head of Roskomnadzor, Alexander Zharov, delivered a whole open letter . But even from the text of this letter, it is clear that Roskomnadzor will be afraid to block Telegram without corresponding direct instructions from above (note the wording with the words ‚Äúmust be blocked‚Äù).

Good day!

I must admit that at the present moment all the existing possibilities of official communication with the team of the Telegram messenger have been exhausted.

Almost every day, Roskomnadzor sends letters to all available Telegram addresses explaining the requirements of the Russian law on information dissemination organizers.

The requirement is one, and it is simple: fill in the questionnaire sent with information about the company that manages Telegram. And officially sent to Roskomnadzor for inclusion of these data in the register of information dissemination organizers. However, so far we have not received any response from the administration of the messenger.
The inclusion of the messenger in this register means only the readiness of Telegram to work under Russian laws. The current legal status of Telegram in Russia will not change. Already now, by virtue of the law, and not the fact of inclusion in the appropriate register, Telegram must fulfill the duties of the organizer of the dissemination of information.

In the event of an actual refusal to perform the duties of the disseminator, the Telegram in Russia should be blocked. Until we get the required information.

The time allotted to Roskomnadzor by the law to make a decision expires.

Therefore, I publicly appeal to the Telegram team and personally to Pavel Valeryevich Durov: fulfill the Russian legislation!

The choice is yours.

Head of Roskomnadzor Alexander Zharov
