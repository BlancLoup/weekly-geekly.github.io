We are talking about the bill number 4629 from 05/10/2016, and 05/17/2016 filed for review.

In case of non-fulfillment, a fine from 500 to 1000 (344500-689000 UAH) of non-taxable minimum incomes of citizens is threatened, in case of repeated violation from 1000 to 2000 (689000-1378000 UAH).
