Illegal video content detected on seized Ukrainian Vkontakte servers

Remember, at the end of June, the Ukrainian police seized Vkontakte caching servers as part of a third-party investigation .

Today it was reported that the ministry of incomes and fees revealed on the Ukrainian servers of the social network "Vkontakte" illegal video content, including, including, child pornography, in connection with which on July 11 criminal proceedings were opened. This is stated in the document Mindokhodov, the text of which the agency "Interfax-Ukraine" has .


In particular, the document states that on July 10, a comprehensive examination was completed, which revealed that 265 files seized from an unidentified person who acted on behalf of the Vkontakte network contained information showing signs of pornographic products, including child pornography, also content with propaganda signs of the cult of violence and cruelty.

The examination found that the illegal information was on servers from December last year, only from 14 to 26 June, 31 million users viewed it.

The proceedings opened the investigative department of the State Tax Inspectorate in the Desnyansky district of Kiev.
