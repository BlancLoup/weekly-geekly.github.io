New educational project Ciklum Dynamics Academy in Minsk

In February 2014, the Ciklum Minsk office will launch a new educational project for senior students and graduates of technical universities - Ciklum Dynamics Academy !

Ciklum Dynamics Academy is a unique six-week training program for specialists in the field of Microsoft Dynamics AX , developed by leading experts of the Dynamics division of Ciklum.



Today, Microsoft Dynamics AX is one of the most popular solutions at the level of ERP systems for enterprise management, which allows to automate financial planning and control processes, customer interaction management, procurement and inventory management, etc.

The program will be held in the evening format 3 times a week for 2 academic hours in the training hall Ciklum (Minsk, Z. Byaduli St., 11, Azimut business center, 5th floor).
The students of the program will be offered: lectures and practical exercises with experienced AX-consultants from Belarus and Ukraine, special training materials for self-study of the main modules of the program, regular online support from teachers.
