DocFactor: technical documentation conference

Historically, Russian technical writers are somewhat deprived of professional events: there are quite a few conferences on technical documentation in Russia (at least, much less than on development or testing), and in Siberia there are practically none.

We decided that it was time to change this situation, so meet:




DocFactor is the first offline conference on technical documentation in Siberia.

In addition to technical writers, this conference may be of interest to system analysts, managers, and anyone who cares about documentation in their projects.

Our reports:

  • Mikhail Ostrogorsky (Philosoft, Moscow) will talk about the officially approved Russian professional standard "Technical Writer",
  • Olga Kirichenko (UNIGINE, Tomsk) will make a report on the career of a technical writer and documenting processes on the example of her company
  • Semyon Faktorovich (documentat.io, Novosibirsk) will share his experience in the field of search, selection and interview of technical writers
  • Dmitry Lishik (Signak, Novosibirsk) will tell about the system approach to technical communications
  • Pavel Semenov (Sbertekh, Novosibirsk) will talk about the principle of a single source and the tools for its implementation

Venue : Novosibirsk, Akademgorodok, a new building of the NSU ( Pirogov st., 1 ).

Date and time : November 4, 2016, 10: 00-18: 00.

Participation is free, but everyone who wants to participate must register on the conference website .

If you live far from Novosibirsk (or are not ready to spend the whole day off to participate in the conference), an online broadcast is at your service.

In addition, several additional seminars are offered to your attention in the days after the conference. All of them will also be held in the Novosibirsk Academgorodok.
