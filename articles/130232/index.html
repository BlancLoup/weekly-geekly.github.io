ABBYY CardHolder: how not to lose useful dating

Any two people on Earth are connected with each other through six or seven mutual friends - says a popular theory.

In the meantime, ABBYY is working on creating handy tools to save your dating history. Today we have released a new mobile application for storing business cards and working with contact information - ABBYY CardHolder for iPhone.

ABBYY CardHolder is a full-featured electronic business card holder in your phone that allows you to store business cards in a structured way and not clutter your iPhone address book. The application recognizes and saves images of business cards in its own archive, equipped with convenient search, grouping and sorting.




Working with ABBYY CardHolder is as easy as with the iPhone address book - to use contacts from recognized business cards (call, write a letter or SMS, go to the specified site) you just need to click on the button with the image of the corresponding action when viewing the business card.



In addition to working with recognized data, ABBYY CardHolder can export contacts from a vcf file, also known as a VCard. The format is supported by many applications: various mail clients, organizers, PIM, and so on. So contact information can be transferred to different devices: PC, Mac, iPhone, other smartphones.



ABBYY CardHolder is already available on the App Store , the cost of the basic version is $ 2.99.

If desired, the capabilities of the electronic business card holder can be expanded to the functionality of the business card application already familiar to our users - ABBYY Business Card Reader . This is done through the purchase directly from the application by tapu to one of the buttons with the lock or from the About section. The re-purchase price is $ 1.99.

In the purchase version, you can choose where to store information from business cards - in the iPhone address book, CardHolder or in both places at the same time, you can search for the contact profile on Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook, as well as view the address from the business card on Google or Yandex maps.



A little about the technical characteristics of business card holders.
