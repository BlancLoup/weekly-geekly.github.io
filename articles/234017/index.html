Summer School of High Performance Computing in Biology and Medicine Available Online


For the second year in a row , Innopolis University has been hosting the Summer School of High Performance Computing in the Field of Modern Biology and Medicine at its base. Co-organizer of the event - MIPT (Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology). This year the school will be held with the support of the Russian Foundation for Basic Research.
On August 21, classes will start for 40 students who have passed the competitive selection . We are waiting for participants from Moscow, Kazan, Rostov-on-Don, Nizhny Novgorod, Volgograd, Yekaterinburg, Voronezh, Omsk, Perm, Ufa, Krasnoyarsk, Perevalsk (Lugansk region of Ukraine), Almetyevsk, Elabugi, Insara, Soviet (Tula region). )

The Chair of the School Program Committee, Igor Rudolfovich Efimov (Eng. Igor R. Efimov), is a Russian-American scientist, professor of biomedical engineering, cell biology and physiology, and radiology at Washington University in St. Louis, Missouri. efimov.wustl.edu/

Guest Lecturer: Sofia Kovalevskaya Prize Winner Dmitry Fedosov (Juelich, Germany), specialist in the field of mathematical modeling of blood

The participation of the following lecturers is expected: Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Aliev R.R. Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Lobanov A.I., Ph.D., Associate Professor Karpov V.E., Ph.D., Associate Professor Konkov K.A. , Ph.D. Kolobov AV ... Practical classes will be conducted by A. Yu. Subbotina, Ph.D. Babichev S.L., Tsybulin I.V., Ph.D. Zhmurov A.A.

Lecture Topics:
  • Introduction to paralleling algorithms and programs
  • Basic mathematical models of biology and methods for the numerical solution of equations and systems of parabolic type
  • Mathematical modeling of proteins
  • Introduction to distributed computing
  • Simulation of the electrical activity of the heart
  • CUDA software model
  • Mathematical models in oncology
  • Mathematical modeling of blood


Topics for workshops:
  • Introduction to Linux OS
  • Introduction to MPI
  • Mathematical models in biology and medicine
  • High Performance Computing and GRID Computing


The summer school was made possible by the official mobile operator of the Innopolis University of MegaFon, as well as the sponsors of the School: the engineering company TESIS and DNA-Technology
View lectures online available at university.innopolis.ru/summer_school.
