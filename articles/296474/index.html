George Soros first invested in the Ukrainian IT market

The Ukrainian Redevelopment Fund LP, a George Soros Development Fund of Ukraine, acquired a stake in the Danish company Ciklum . Ciklum provides outsourcing services. Ukraine has its largest development office. It is known that the fund acquired less than half of the shares. He bought out the stake owned by Horizon Capital (it ranged from 10% to 25%), as well as part of the stake owned by Majgaard Holdings Ltd, the company Torben Maygaard, the founder of Ciklum. This transaction is expected to be completed by the end of 2015.
