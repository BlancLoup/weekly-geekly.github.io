In Yandex.Money, new limits and special commission

We have two good news - the first will be pleasant to many identified Yandex.Money users, the second will delight those who have switched to professional accounts.

Firstly, we have significantly increased the limits for withdrawing money from the wallet.
If yesterday the maximum amount of withdrawal was 300 thousand per month, today it is 3 million rubles . Also, the limits on a one-time withdrawal are higher. For example, if earlier you could transfer to Alfa-Click or CONTACT no more than 300 thousand rubles at a time, then from today - up to 500 thousand . Read more about the new withdrawal conditions on our website . Please note: for anonymous users, the limits will remain the same.

Secondly, today we have introduced a special commission for professional accounts . If you need to withdraw Yandex.Money to your bank account, then when transferring from 300 thousand rubles the commission will be 6 times less than usual - only 0.5% .
