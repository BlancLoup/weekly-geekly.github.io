Oversan in the clouds

Good day, Habrayuzer!

We decided to renew the old tradition of blogging company. And again we are ready to share our news and technical details.

Today we would like to talk about our cloud based on Microsoft's Hyper-V technology. This solution is perfect for both high-load web projects and for hosting your company's internal IT infrastructure. Due to the possibility of connecting the antiDDoS hardware protection and the F5 VIPRION balancing system together with Internet channels up to 1 Gbit / s per virtual server, any Internet project will feel calm. Corporate clients will appreciate the possibility of creating IPSEC / VPN and individual communication channels using MPLS technology from the office to the Cloud. Oversan Cloud Reliability is provided by high-end HP Proliant hardware, NetApp professional storage systems, and Cisco networking equipment. The cloud feature is that the default SLA is 99.9%!

Not inferior to the reliability and versatility of the platform: thanks to the support of a large number of operating systems (and win and * nix) and the possibility of renting Microsoft licenses for many products, it is suitable for projects of any level! So, besides Windows Server 2003 and 2008 (including version R2), the Cloud supports operating systems such as Ubuntu, CentOS, Debian, OpenSUSE, and other Linux-based systems. Even own Linux kernel is supported!

In addition, the status of the project is underlined by our official partnership with Microsoft, whose representatives provide full support to the project and are positive in their development. Do not forget that the Hyper-V platform is actively developing, and its closest release will add a huge number of features and capabilities, which we will definitely write about.

And in order not to be unfounded, we intend to continue to talk in more detail about Oversan Cloud. And this article opens a cycle of interesting publications about the functions of the Cloud and the technical features of their implementation.
For example, sometimes our clients are faced with a situation where, as part of a single project, they use both physical servers and cloud capacities. In most cases, it is problematic to combine power data with a common internal network. We have developed a ready-made solution that allows you to solve such problems quickly and easily. To learn how to combine these facilities into a single infrastructure, read about the complexity of implementation with visual schemes in our next article!
