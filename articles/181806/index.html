Tizen launched on Nexus 7 3G (video)



On Habre OS Tizen, based on Linux, and developed jointly by Samsung and Intel, was mentioned more than once. Now there are several alternative mobile operating systems, including Ubuntu Touch and Firefox OS, and we can expect the ready Tizen OS to appear on the market soon. Craftsmen-enthusiasts do not expect favors from Samsung with Intel, but they do everything themselves.

Not so long ago, the early port Tizen 2.0 was shown on the Nexus 7, now the updated port is loaded on the Nexus 7 3G. By the way, there is even a manual for porting Tizen with your own hands. You can get acquainted with the manual here (for beginners, it will probably be difficult to understand). If you do not want to port anything, but just want to look at the operating system, then all this is shown below:



Now it is too early to say that this port is suitable for daily use, but the principle of operation can be understood. By the way, the developer who uploaded the video to the Network used MultiROM to be able to download the necessary OSs one by one (Android and Tizen). By the way, the developers (official) are planning to make their OS suitable for use on both mobile devices and netbooks / laptops.
