Iron case will perform the role of a radiator

The iron case will play the role of a radiator - the lid tightly pressed against the processor "thermo chew". On the other hand, the point is quite heavy (2 kg) and not every false ceiling can withstand it.

On the motherboard 2 PCI slots 68-pin for connecting radio modules

On the motherboard, two PCI slots 68-pin (+ there is a seat for the 3rd, but ‚Äúforgot to solder‚Äù). Radio modules of standard type (like in a laptop). Traditionally, one radio module is tuned to 2.4 GHz, the second one to 5 GHz. But you can set either both to 2.4 or both to 5.

Our company uses the NetScout box for this. Oddly enough, but the software for motorola is more expensive than buying a separate Wi-Fi sensor.
