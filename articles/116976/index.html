Shop of useful services for owners of Toshiba laptops

Toshiba has opened an unusual online store. It does not sell products, but services that help protect laptop owners from the effects of force majeure. In addition, system owners can take advantage of the opportunity and extend the standard warranty service, or simply buy a package of services to protect a laptop from unforeseen circumstances.
