Automate the social activity of your Internet startup using ifttt.com

Let's start with the description ifttt.com



ifttt.com is a very promising startup, which in a nutshell: Lets You Hack Together Web Apps, Without Coding Skills . If more detailed, then this is a service that allows users, without immersing in the API a huge number of services and any knowledge of development \ programming languages, to mix and automate various activities with each other. In early 2012, we received a seed investment of $ 1.5M .

There are only a few entities in the system, such as tasks (these are the rules of your triggers or reactions to an external event), recipes (these are tasks that others have created and shared with everyone) and channels (this is what you can try to mix with by setting up different execution patterns tasks, each channel should be tied to ifttt.com).

Preamble: what goals should be pursued?



I think no one needs to explain the reasons for which the social presence and the main thing is adequate social activity, this is the most important attribute for any product (and it doesn‚Äôt matter whether it‚Äôs a start-up, an e-shop, or a site about insect rights). Through social networks, most often, we get:

  • Channel of attracting leads / early supporters ( early adopters )
  • Good feedback from real people.
  • Advertising and PR among potential users and / or customers, as well as among media and bloggers

But what interests us is not so much why, but how much to do it, and even automatically, and to set it up, but it worked reliably. This is what this article is about.

Well, what to automate?



Let us stop and think, and what social activity can we automate? What kind of social activity is usually taken to achieve the goals announced above? In addition to generating content that is not automated in fact, the rest can be automated almost everything!

So, a list of what is accepted, what is useful and what one could try to do in social networks to achieve their worthy goals:

  • Spread news about your product to the maximum number of social channels (WP blog, twitter, facebook (personal or page), tumblr, delicious, linkedin, etc.) from one source (for example a blog, although you can absolutely from any)
  • Repost \ retweet from your personal accounts any posts \ tweets made from official accounts of your products
  • Repost \ retweet from your product accounts news on your industry with selected resources
  • Twitter: Thanks to new followers, those who retweet you or people mentioning your account or any word you specify by auto reply or mention, post sample tweets with any frequency, mutual following and infinitely many etc.
  • Publish any documents (presentations, descriptions, studies) in all social networks you need just by putting the file in public dropbox or sending it by email
  • Receive any statuses or answers in the form of sms for free

Practical cases of using ifttt.com



In addition to an infinite number of personal goals, a la get sms warning that tomorrow it will rain, we will consider three practical cases.

We broadcast news to all social networks


Baseline conditions

  • Personally, I think that it is optimal to spread information from a blog about your product, with traffic landing either on the blog or on the website of your product.
  • Let our blog be on WordPress and that‚Äôs where we post the original post.
  • Do you have a facebook page and / or a personal account (or a fictional character), twitter account, linkedin, tumblr or another social service from the list


So, the scheme is simple:

  1. Create an account on ifttt.com
  2. Create ifttt tasks (or copy the following recipes)
  3. WP -> Facebook page post ( recipe )
  4. WP -> Facebook personal post ( recipe )
  5. WP -> Twitter tweet ( recipe )
  6. WP -> Linkedin status ( recipe )
  7. WP -> Tumblr status ( recipe )
  8. The task is done, now as soon as we have written something to the blog, information about it has spread to all the places we need.


The trivial task tell you? And I agree with you, there are a lot of native and third-party services that do something like this, if the clever schemes with information transfer ala WP -> RSS -> Twitter tweet -> Facebook personal post -> Linkedin status and this also works, but far less manageable and not always good looking. I find the broadcast using ifttt the most controlled and most importantly the most finely tuned.

We automate Twitter activity


Baseline conditions

  • We have twitter account of product for people.
  • We have a twitter bot account of the product for all kinds of automatic actions.

twitter account product
