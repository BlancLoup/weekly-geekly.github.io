Overview of 6 Budget Wheel Platforms for Ebay Robots

Good day.

Most recently, I thought that making a robot is expensive. Well, not so much a million, but 5-10 thousand for sure. That is why I did not think in this direction and did not even consider such an opportunity - to create something moving and reacting to the outside world. However, after this top of roboforum.ru/forum88/topic2214.html, my idea changed and I got an idea.

Attention is a brief description of 6 budget platforms for the robot up to 2000 rubles. The assessment is subjective, I will be glad if habrovchane throw links and examples to other budget platforms and opportunities that I missed.

For all examples, links to Ebay are given, at the time of the article, I chose the cheapest offers. Before you place an order - look at the seller's rating, read the reviews and make sure the shipping cost. And then I had precedents.

Platform of toys for 300 rubles




Pros: Cheap. The cheapest version of the wheeled platform that you can think of. Made from everything that can ride on wheels and has a motor. You need at least 2 motors to create a manageable platform.

Cons: Structural fragility, plastic, the need to think over the basis for the control board, incomprehensible motors, unknown gearbox parameters, the lack of normal mounting for sensors.

As an example, I cite the platform created a couple of days ago from a pair of phenolic tanks, just like in the article roboforum.ru/forum88/topic2214.html I want to note that even though I live in the city by the sea and the markets with the Chinese there is an ocean, but it took me a week to find a pair of phenolic tanks, 300 rubles per pair. Most likely you will not find such a toy in your city. The advantages of the platform are four-wheel drive, soft, high wheels - good grip. Simple motor fasteners to gearboxes, multi-stage reduction gearbox. After the platform is assembled, there are two speakers, sound chips that reproduce the sound, a pair of cases with compartments for 3 batteries - all this can be used both for the robot and in other projects. Of the minuses, I note the poor quality of plastics, the fragility. The load on the platform should be minimal, a couple of shields, batteries and everything. Conclusions about this platform, read at the end.
