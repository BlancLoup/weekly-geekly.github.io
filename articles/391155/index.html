Making the house smart with Xiaomi

Xiaomi was founded by seven partners on June 6, 2010, it is a very young company that could become the third smartphone manufacturer in the world in just a few years. Xiaomi smartphones makes it so that cheaper without sacrificing quality anymore . But this is not the entire business of the company: a significant part of the production is occupied by devices for the smart home.

About the air cleaner, water filter, security kit with body sensors and closing windows and doors, a tonometer and other Xiaomi home gadgets - read under the cut.

Lamp Xiaomi




Xiaomi Yeelight lamp will be a great gift. This is a nightlight on which you can adjust the brightness and choose a color from sixteen million. That is, the lamp can be adjusted * for any person perfectly.

Feel free to order your favorite. The possibility of such a setting for any mood she likes. Another option - children.

And you can also tell your wife that he is enhancing the Wi-Fi signal * - and pretending that you are not just playing, but setting up a network.

Wi-Fi router


Xiaomi Mini will be able to issue 1167 megabits per second. If your operator, of course, will provide such speed. A gigabit router * will cost you $ 28- $ 32 depending on the color. You can choose blue, white and black. It can be turned into a home server by connecting an external hard drive to the USB and writing video from the camera directly to it.

To enhance the signal from the router, you can use a small amplifier from Xiaomi. For $ 6.58 * .



Universal controller


This small device will replace all the remotes in the house, including the TV remote, audio systems and air conditioners. Xiaomi Universal IR Remote Controller * can control all gadgets within a radius of 20 meters from it. The main thing is that the smartphone was with Android not younger than 4.4.







Air purifier


The Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier Air Purifier will help control the presence of sulfur oxide, carbon monoxide and nitrous oxide in the air, as well as small particles up to 2.5 microns in size - the most dangerous that can penetrate into the lungs and cause chronic diseases. In the cleaner there is a pre-filter in the form of a grid, HEPA-filter filters fine particles, and in addition to this there is an adsorption filter from activated carbon. For an hour, Mi Air Purifier cleans 330 cubic meters of air * , it is designed for a room of 28-48 meters. You can control this device weighing 8 kg from your smartphone or using the buttons on the case.





Water filter


It is not enough to breathe clean air - you need to drink clean water. Xiaomi claims that you only need five minutes to install this four-stage filter * . A cotton filter cleans water from large particles of more than 5 microns, then a coconut activated carbon filter will remove the liquid from the bleach taste, a reverse osmosis filter will not let in heavy metals and bacteria, and another coal filter will improve the taste of water. About the purity of water can be found in the application on your smartphone. It is worth the gadget $ 217.99.





Water quality meter


The Xiaomi Mi TDS Pen is a miniature device that allows you to measure water quality * . The gadget reacts to organic components, metals and salts in water. With it, you can check the water for hardness. Price - $ 7.02.



Safe Home Kit


The starter kit for a smart and safe house from Xiaomi includes a sensor for opening and closing doors and windows, a human body sensor, a multifunctional gateway and a wireless switch. Application is limited only by imagination. You can configure the air cleaner to turn on when you open the window, or the lamp to turn on when you enter the room or get out of bed. The set costs $ 45.89 * .









Tonometer


Especially for our moms, dads, grandparents: a tonometer in the form of a docking station for a smartphone * . The gadget communicates with the phone on Android and iOS via Bluetooth and transmits all the data that can be shared with relatives. The device costs $ 42.





Smart scales


Smart scales from Xiaomi * work on 4 batteries, which are included in the kit, and show the result of weighing using LEDs and on a smartphone - on Android 4.4 b iOS 7.0. And they cost $ 17.85.

For each family member, you can create a profile, and the scales themselves determine who weighs. The device helps to track changes in weight and body mass index. All data can be viewed in the same application in which you manage the Mi Band bracelet.
