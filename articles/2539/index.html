Nokia patents voice tags

The manufacturer of the best-selling cell phones in Russia, the Finnish company Nokia, has patented a system of tagging photos with voice tags.

The technology described in the patent is as follows. When photographing with a cell phone, its owner can immediately set tags - keywords that characterize the image. A photo can be marked with one or several tags. The voice tag system can also be used to store photos on other digital media.

A distinctive feature of the system is that to use it you do not need to press any buttons: all commands are performed by voice.

Nokia patent is officially registered on January 16th.
