Google is considering buying Yahoo



The epic of buying / selling assets of Yahoo will not end. The management of this company has not yet confirmed its intention to sell the assets, but many companies are already counting money and are looking for partners, intending to make Yahoo "an offer that cannot be refused." Earlier it was written that Microsoft has found partners for joint purchase of Yahoo. Now, the authoritative publication of the Wall Street Journal announces that the management of the "Corporation of Good" is considering the possibility of acquiring Yahoo Inc.

So far, Google has made an offer to unite and buy Yahoo to several investment companies. But the names of these companies are still unknown, and negotiations are at a very early stage. In fact, for Google, buying Yahoo can be a good deal, since in the US, the mailer of this company is in first place in popularity, and in terms of attendance of all its services, Yahoo is one of the first places in the US.
