Which videos are ranked best: what to look for when preparing videos?
The main ranking factor is the indicator of audience retention. The longer the average time to watch a video and the more people watch it to the end, the higher YouTube puts this video in the list. How not to drain traffic from YouTube for nothing and to use annotations correctly?
There are several ways to output traffic from YouTube to your website:
  1. From the video viewing page: link in description, annotation with link, user prompt, overlay, site mention in the video itself + call to action.
  2. From the channel as a whole: you can add links to sites on the About Channel page, they will be displayed in the channel header. Also, a call to action can be placed in the description text on the ‚ÄúAbout the channel‚Äù page and in the description of playlists.


How to use annotations? First of all, remember about the technical features of YouTube and do not make such mistakes:
  1. External links in video annotations can only be put on sites linked to a YouTube channel (tricks can be circumvented using tricks, but we will not violate YouTube‚Äôs rules, right?).
  2. Annotations placed in the bottom of the channel's trailer player window can be overlaid by the video scroll bar, and in the right-hand side with a channel icon. This will make it impossible to click on such an annotation and follow the link.
  3. Annotations do not work on mobile devices. Custom hints work on all devices.
  4. Do not overlap with annotations the main part of the screen with information, this may cause irritation among viewers.

  6. Do not be intrusive, you do not need to force the audience to click on the link, open your website or place an order.
