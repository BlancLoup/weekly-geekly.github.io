Appearance and ergonomics


There are a screen on the front panel, two touch-sensitive ones (almost no visible light without them) and one hardkey button. Along the perimeter, the body is edged with black glossy plastic.

Above the screen is a speaker, front camera, two sensors and a warning LED. On the top there is a headphone jack and a power button.

At the bottom - MicroUSB connector, microphone hole and a slot, with which you can remove the back cover. A little higher, already on the back of the phone, lurked an external speaker and a brief information about the device.

Left - the volume rocker, without a separator and any identifying marks. Despite its simplicity, it has a clear tactile response, which makes it possible to use it when wearing a bumper.

Behind - the company logo, camera and LED flash, unfortunately - one.

The back cover is made of flexible plastic with soft-touch coating, which prevents the device from slipping out of the hands. The cover is sturdy and tight to the phone. In addition to black, there are also covers of white, orange, yellow, green and blue, but they are glossy.

It is fastened with 12 plastic latches along the entire perimeter of the smartphone.

In general, the appearance makes me only positive emotions. Brutal bar with high-quality assembly and, accordingly, without the slightest backlash or squeaks.

Hardware platform

Jiayu G2 is built on the base of the proven MTK6577 processor, which includes two Cortex-A9 processor cores, each with 1 GHz, a PowerVR SGX531 graphic core, 1 GB of RAM and 4 GB of flash drive (the user has 2.5 GB) . All this allows the smartphone to easily cope with viewing and recording video in HD-quality, three-dimensional games and other demanding applications.

It should be noted that those 2.5 GB are reserved for the internal memory of the smartphone and for its full work you will have to purchase a microSD memory card. For example, the camera will not work for you (save pictures) if a memory card is not inserted in the slot.

Operating system firmware

The model works on the basis of the operating system Android 4.0.4. I recommend immediately switching to the custom firmware All in one v. 1 by savyol . Just because there is already installed root access, cleared from the Chinese software and other pleasant things. There are several patches, including the one that is responsible for the correct operation of the three-color LED.

List of changes:
  • CWM recovery
  • Boot with root shell + ext2simg added to remove backup via adb
  • Root and superuser + rootexplorer
  • Removed Chinese software
  • Russian T9 in the dialer
  • Removed login and password from Russian PPSOS
  • Updated hosts
  • There is no JIAYU icon in the application menu, Chinese links to native ones are replaced in the browser
  • Replaced the MMS / SMS application (backup / recovery is available on the memory card)
  • Added engineering menu in the developer options.
  • Extended shutdown menu, there is a recovery item
  • Fix fonts

You can read the instructions for installing custom firmware in the topic on JIAYU G2 on the forum. In the cap, under the spoiler "Custom firmware".

Results

JIAYU G2 is the only 4-inch Android smartphone with 1 GB of RAM in the price category up to $ 200. Its main advantages include a high-quality IPS screen, a platform that is productive enough for comfortable work and, of course, a convenient size. Among the shortcomings can be noted the poor optimization of the firmware which quickly eats up the battery at 2000 mAh. Well, and yet I will not recommend this unit to people who want to "buy and use", given the price - this is more a toy for geeks or the first smartphone for a child.
