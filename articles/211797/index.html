Competition "Native Speech" - a week before the start!

Dear participants of the developer contest! Before the opening of access to the competitive database, there is only one week left!



You have seven more days to study the task, an example, ask all the interesting questions on the forum and tune in to the creative mood!
Do not forget to officially register on the portal m2ies.com: detailed instructions here .

We remind you that the contestants will have to create a workable algorithm for converting the recognized sequence of phonemes into text that meets the norms of the Russian language. More information about the competitive task can be found in our previous post .



The task is not trivial, and therefore the organizers will help participants at all stages of the competition. Your questions in the process of working on the algorithm for converting a sequence of phonemes into a readable text will be available on the forum .
