It was decided to make a whiteboard on the basis of a smooth white sheet with a film (usual white painted galvanized, which is glued over with a protective film for transportation). Smooth sheet was chosen in order to be able to use magnets. From a sheet of 2000 to 1250 mm, we got two marker boards.
