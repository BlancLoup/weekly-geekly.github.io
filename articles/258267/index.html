DevCon 2015. Join the broadcast today from 10:30! What is the first day we cook?

Dear friends! The wait is over, today, May 20, at 10:30, our big conference for developers, testers and software architects Microsoft DevCon 2015 begins .



We are pleased to invite you to the online broadcast of the conference. At 10:30 a direct opening from the online studio awaits you, and at 11:00 a large opening and plenary report will begin, within which all the most recent and most recent topics on software development on the platform and using Microsoft tools will be presented.

Immediately after the opening and a short break, we are waiting for 20 reports in five parallel tracks: the web and the cloud, Windows, Visual Studio and ALM, corporate development and, for the first time at the conference, game development. Examine the schedule and connect to the broadcast of selected reports.

On the first day, guest speakers, representatives of Microsoft, speakers and experts from the community, from Intel, Game Insight, VIAcode, Futurice and many others are waiting for you.

Today, May 20, the reports will be held on all topics from web development to the Internet of Things, from games to deep immersion in developer tools. It will be very interesting!

Join the broadcast !
