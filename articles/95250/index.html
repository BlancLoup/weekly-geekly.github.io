Android Training Seminars in St. Petersburg (registration completed)

Upd :
Streaming starts at 7:00 pm on June 7th, 10th and 14th: http://www.ustream.tv/channel/spb-gtug-3android-seminars
Hash Tag: #spbgtug

Welcome all interested in Android!

Recently, interest in this platform has become obvious, therefore, within the GTUG (Google Technology User Group), we also decided to focus on it and consider it in more detail in terms of development.
So remarkably it happened that Alexey Golubev and Dmitry Lukashev (working in the Yota company) offered to conduct a series of lectures and practical classes in Android programming. Lecturers have very serious development experience for Android (specifically, they are engaged in Android from its release, before that they were engaged in other mobile platforms).
It will not be a retelling of tutorials from developer.android.com, there are a lot of subtle points, examples, details in the presentations that came from the development experience. The seminars will present a course of three presentations lasting about 2 hours.
