Tablet review on Windows 8.1 (Vido w11c)



Hello!
Due to some lull among tablet-phone browsers, I decided to review the tablet on Windows 8.1 from China. I write after a couple of months of active use.

For those who do not like long words and objectivity - the tablet is good, but not a clear leader, the weakest point of the tablet is the keyboard case. Much better than anything that was previously presented in the Chinese market, and the stuffing is quite good for performing any office tasks and non-heavy games, but it does not reach the convenience of the laptop. However, under the cut I will tell everything in as much detail as possible.

So, the characteristics:

Minuses

Like the absolute majority of devices they are. But most of them are somehow connected with the case.



Spontaneous inclusion in a dream . Since there is no cover on the cover, as on laptops, the tablet cannot understand when it is necessary to turn off peripherals (touchpad and keyboard) and when the cover is closed, it can turn on spontaneously and discharge the battery. It is treated by a complete shutdown, hibernation, or a handwritten program that disables the periphery when it goes to sleep, and turns it on when it wakes up.

Inconvenient keyboard. Fn in place of Ctrl, F1-F12 only when you press Fn, and the lack of Cyrillic on the letter keys. From the recent moment on the market there are covers / keyboards with Russian layout
One position open tablet . It is impossible to fold the display to a specific angle in a laptop, this is a big minus of such covers.
Abrasion with time paint on the tablet in places of contact with the cover.
No full size USB .

pros

Great screen 1920x1200.
Full Windows 8.1 . Despite the complete discharge of Windows on desktop computers, the OS works perfectly on devices with a touch-screen OS. As if written for them =)
Convenience of working with a laptop and tablet , no difficulties with connecting / disconnecting. Tired of the cover, unhooked it and use it without it.
Compactness. It weighs less than a netbook and even less a laptop and takes up little space in the bag.
Sufficient tablet performance .
The installed OS takes up 3 gigabytes of space on the SSD. I do not know how they did it, but the available space is 61 gigabytes.
