Completing the patent war between Apple and HTC

Yesterday, November 10, Apple and HTC signed an agreement according to which they withdraw all claims filed against each other at the moment and sign a 10-year agreement on mutual licensing.

And we are talking about both existing technologies and all those that will be patented during this period of time.

HTC is pleased to resolve its dispute with Apple - from now on HTC will be able to focus on innovation, and not on litigation.

- HTC CEO Peter Chou


We are pleased to reach an agreement with HTC. We will continue to be extremely focused on innovation.

- Apple CEO Tim Cook


Apparently, all kinds of lawsuits are beginning to bother the largest companies, and very soon the news flow that X has sued Y $ 100M will come to naught.

Well, we wish the rest to follow their example. Make love, not war.
