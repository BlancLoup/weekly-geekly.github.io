A plastic surgeon died in an accident by tweeting a link to a photo of his dog.

Plastic surgeon Frank Ryan died on Monday in a car accident, posting on Twitter a link to a photo of his dog. His car fell off a cliff on one of the US Pacific Coast highways in the Malibu area. Before that, he wrote that he climbed to the top of a giant sand dune and that it is more difficult than it seems.

The surgeon's dog, whose link to the photo was his last tweet, remained alive.

Not so long ago, the UN Secretary General issued a statement: "I want every driver in the world to understand: correspondence by SMS-messages at the wheel kills." Using Twitter accordingly.
