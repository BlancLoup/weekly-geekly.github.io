17% of the websites of Ukrainian schools are hosted in Germany, and 20% in Russia

By the beginning of the school year, we conducted a small contextual study looking at the sites of Ukrainian schools. Interesting statistics came out, the largest percentage is 51%, of course, Ukraine, but in second place is Russia - 20%, and in third place is Germany - 17%, and further down: USA - 8%, France - 3 %, England - 1% *. As for hosting in Germany, most often it is low-cost hosters. In addition, the site itself is often not developed, but a free template is taken, or an inexpensive template is bought that no one modifies to the needs of the site.
