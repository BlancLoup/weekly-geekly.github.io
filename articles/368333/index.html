The project of the European solar telescope included in the program of science development for 2016



At the meeting of the European Strategic Forum on Research Infrastructures (ESFRI) in Amsterdam , it was decided to include the project to build the European Solar Telescope EST in the development program , along with twenty other projects, including five new enterprises.

The 4-meter European solar telescope will be located on the Canary Islands and will become the largest solar telescope in the world. He will study the magnetic properties of the star, sunspots and flares with an unprecedented resolution of several hundred meters of the surface of the Sun.

In connection with giving the project the status of a priority, the Spanish government has already expressed its readiness to provide all-round support in the construction of the telescope.
