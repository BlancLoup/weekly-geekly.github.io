The company RIM introduced the OS BlackBerry 10

Today, the Canadian corporation Research In Motion has unveiled a new milestone for its operating system, the BlackBerry 10, based on the QNX core, like the OS on a Playbook tablet. Now, the OS will also be available for communicators, the first of which will enter the market in the fall of 2012. Along with the announcement, the reference communicator for developers was announced, the details of the receipt of which are still unknown.


As for the development for the platform, Canadians have already submitted a beta version of the SDK, with which you can already try yourself in writing applications on HTML5 + JS + CSS3, and Qt-gurus can use Cascades SDK with QML or C ++.
