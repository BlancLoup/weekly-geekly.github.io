Functional and economic comparison of Russian cloud service providers. Version 2.0

Just over a year ago, I published a comparison of Russian cloud operators : I liked the article a lot and was helpful. About six months later, I began to receive a lot of feedback and requests that it would be nice to make a new review, updating the price data, adding additional services to the request and adding new companies to it. This time, the comparison included already 34 companies (last year there were more than 2 times less), including the foreign services Amazon and Rackspace, and the collection of information took more than two months.



As was the last time, a certain updated configuration of resources and services was chosen, also for a change and at the request of the workers, I added a number of new questions. Since I had experience in a company that provides integrated IT outsourcing services, the requested configuration was chosen based on the most popular services.

The review included 34 companies:

ActiveCloud, AWS (Amazon), b2b.beeline.ru, Clodo, Cloud One, Cloud4Y, CloudySky, Croc, DataFort, DataLine, De Novo, DepoCloud, e-Style Telecom, infoboxcloud.ru, ISPserver, IT Lite, IT-Grad , Linx, M1 Cloud, AITeko and MakeCloud *, Oncloud, Orange, Oversun, Parking, Rackspace, Rentacloud, Selectel, Softline, Outsourcing 24, Corus Consulting, Megaphone, Obloteka, Rostelecom O7, RTCOM.

* Ayteko and Makecloud provide services to one company - Servionika. The infrastructure is the same, but the cost of services is different .

Evaluation


The emphasis was placed on the complexity of providing services (the principle of one window) to cover the tasks of an average company in which the main operating system is Windows.

The evaluation was based on the number of pluses and the total cost. As a rule, it is most convenient for the customer to order services and work with one performer, therefore companies that did not have the requested services received a minus.

++ (super-excellent - functionality or solution that exceeds requests / expectations)
+ (excellent - the functionality or solution is present and fully satisfies the requests)
+ - (medium - the functionality or the solution is partially present, there are limitations)
- (bad - the functionality or solution is completely absent and does not satisfy the requests)
- (super-bad - the functionality or the solution does not satisfy the requests / expectations)
? (information is missing, that is, the question was asked, but the answer was not provided), since the concealment of the requested information is a negative factor in my assessment, in this case we put a minus -
