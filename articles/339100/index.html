The most important IT event of the fall: Dell EMC Forum 2017

Along with the beginning of autumn, Dell EMC Forum 2017 is approaching - one of the most important events of the company, which will be held on October 5 in Moscow at the Crocus Expo site. Details under the cut.



Each year the Forum theme changes following the latest global IT trends. This year, the Dell EMC Forum will be marked by ‚Äúdigital transformation‚Äù.

Most of the Russian business has adapted modern IT solutions only at a basic level, which hinders the development of companies, deprives them of flexibility and makes them vulnerable to unforeseen risks. In order to accelerate the transformation of your business, experts and industry leaders will talk about the most advanced products for IT-infrastructure of any scale during the day and will teach you how to use them effectively.

Participants will consider the most successful cases of digital transformation in recent years. You will get acquainted with the best practices of using both well-known and promising technologies - from Big Data to the Internet of Things.

You can prepare to upgrade your company's IT infrastructure, take a fresh look at the capabilities of cloud platforms and converged systems, and transform workplaces using the latest industry achievements.

Special attention is devoted to the topic of protection against data loss and equipment damage that does not lose its relevance. New hybrid cloud solutions for Microsoft Azure Stack will help organize secure cloud storage for backup. Data protection technologies will be demonstrated for Microsoft and Oracle applications popular in the Russian business environment. Dell EMC together with Intel announce ultra-protected devices for working in extreme environmental conditions and in hazardous production.

You can get acquainted with all the new products in the demo zone dedicated to the implementation and practical work with the presented solutions. At the Forum, you can chat with colleagues and experts from Dell Technologies and Dell EMC, ask questions and get advice on topics of interest.

Sessions are held throughout the day simultaneously in six sections. Thanks to this, after registration, you can create your personal Forum program by selecting the topics that are most important to you.

  • Digital Transformation . Get new, impressive business results using responsive software, the Internet of Things (IoT) and Big Data analytics.
  • IT transformation . Explore the latest platforms, converged systems and cloud technologies using the example of Microsoft Azure and SOHO solutions.
  • Transformation of jobs . Provide your employees with modern technology for working in the office and beyond.
  • Transformation of security systems . Learn about new ways to deal with IT threats, protect and restore infrastructure.

Date: October 5, 2017.
Venue: Crocus Expo exhibition complex.
