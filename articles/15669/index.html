3G WCDMA / UMTS from Ukrtelecom

The state-owned company Ukrtelecom announced the launch of the first commercial network WCDMA / UMTS on the territory of Ukraine under the Utel trademark.

At the moment, residents of Kiev, Dnepropetrovsk, Donetsk, Lviv, Odessa and Kharkov can enjoy. The rest of the territory is roaming in the Beeline network.

The company has prepared several categories of tariff plans - from prideid to no-limit. In principle, the focus is on business users, although I am sure that even active students will like the Internet at 3.6 Mbit / sec. Yes, and lovers can now see each other at a great distance, which is also cool.

You can immediately identify the great advantage of the offer from Ukrtelecom over today's only competitor - PeopleNET company - the Ukrtelecom network is supported by most modern devices, while CDMA-2000 EV-DO is only a few pieces (from officially supplied).
