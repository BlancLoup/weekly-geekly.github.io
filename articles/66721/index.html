Testing online store: looking for members

We invite users of websites of online stores of computer equipment to take part in online testing!

We write articles with the analysis of user qualities of online stores of computer equipment for the iTRate.ru project. We do this in order for the general public to learn that there is such a discipline as user interface design and how it helps to make digital products more user-friendly. We also hope that if the store employees pay attention to the article, the site will become better.

For the next article, we conduct remote testing of one online store. First of all, we are looking for gamers , but we also need users who do not play computer games.

This is not a commercial project, so we do not offer rewards. But if you leave interesting comments, they will most likely end up in an article ( see an example ). Testing will take about 40-50 minutes.

You can take part in testing at any convenient time until August 16 inclusive.

If you decide to take part in the project, please write to Irina: ipentsova@uidesign.ru. We will send you a link to the test.

Thank!

UPD: It was very interesting to read the feedback from the study participants. Sorry, not everything could be placed in the article (there were a lot of them). We hope you enjoy: analysis of the site of the online store Meijin for the project iTRate.ru .
