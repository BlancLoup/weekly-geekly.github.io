Android



No matter how strange it may sound, but Moverio works under Android 2.2. The control takes place with the help of a special remote with a touch pad (perceives 1 touch), how it works can be understood from the video.



In this console, and hides the entire filling Moverio. Here is the battery, which allows the glasses to work for about 5 hours without recharging.

There are also connectors for a flash card and headphones, there is a MicroUSB and a connector for connecting a charger.




Moverio has almost no internal memory.



In fact, working with the remote is not very convenient. It's good that there is a joystick, as the touchpad is not very responsive to touch, although you get used to it over time.

Inside, Moverio has a browser and several Android applications. The most interesting thing is that you can install Amazone Store here and support for APK files is also present here. For example, I launched Angry Birds on glasses (it works without brakes).

Glasses, albeit cumbersome, but convenient to work with them. They sit perfectly on the head and the ears with the nose get tired after 2 hours. This is a good result. The number of working hours of the battery also pleases - 5 hours. In any case, many will use glasses to watch only movies. Someone will go to the browser.
