1. Hit List

(social network, calendar, memo, diary, informer)

Planning and compiling lists of what needs to be done (see, hear, try, visit, buy), as well as lists like ‚Äú10 best *** for 2008‚Äù, has always been a popular activity, both in the network and in offline (magazines). But I have not met a single service combining such collections in runet.

Of the western projects, the most similar, and extremely popular (there) www.43things.com

Users can create and copy lists and individual events from each other, subscribe to new entries by other participants, comment on individual entries and lists.
Completed items are marked specials. marker.
Copied entries will be marked accordingly (the author will be able to see the list of participants who copied his entry).
In fact, copying someone else's record at the same time is a vote for it.
Based on this vote, uniform Hit-Sheets of the most popular events will be formed (in time), i.e. You can go and find out what they recommend to do (where to go, what to read, what to see ...) next weekend (month, season, year ...).

The project will integrate a system of widgets (users) - interactive images (banners), displaying the selected Hit Sheets and changes in them in real time.
Such a widget can be placed by the user in his diary, on the website, forum or any other page (the mechanism is implemented by us in the www.hate-list.ru project).
Such widgets are an additional means of attracting new visitors.
Interviews and the publication of hit lists of celebrities (for example, TV stars or popular bloggers), etc. are also expected.
