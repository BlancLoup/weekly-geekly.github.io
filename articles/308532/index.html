Selection of programming podcasts in Russian and English

Hello! This article contains some of the best programming podcasts in both Russian and English, which will keep you up to date with the latest news.

Podcasts are audio files that you can listen to at any time on your computer or other device (iPod, iPad, smartphone, etc.). This is the most portable way to consume content and learn something new. The popularity of podcasts has grown over the years and now they cover a very wide range of topics.

And yes, there are many interesting and popular podcasts for developers and programmers. Podcasts are incredibly useful, they will keep you abreast of everything that is happening in the field of interest to you, and also help you develop a broader view of the constantly developing field of information technology.

Let's get away with Russian-language podcasts, take a look at the list!

Radio T



Radio T is one of the first technical podcasts in Russian. Four people leading the podcast are sometimes invited to talk and other famous personalities. The podcast is focused on a wide technical audience and covers a variety of current trends and issues in IT, major news and events, and sometimes the hosts' point of view on the show may vary, which allows you to look at the subject from different angles, and besides, all the participants are enough professional, so that their point of view and thoughts are interesting to everyone, even if you can not share their opinions on any issue. Broadcast once a week, on Saturday.

Debriefing



The podcast is usually invited interesting guests, for example, developers from Google. They discuss the news of the IT world and new software products, in general, about technologies, gadgets and programming. Podcasts usually last at least 2 hours.

DevZen Podcast



A podcast in Russian about programming, administration and IT in general, which is published every week, specializes in technical topics and is not narrowly focused, for example, dedicated to one programming language or a stack of technologies. All leading DevZen are professional programmers and write a lot of code every day. In addition, guests regularly come to issues and talk about their experience working with certain technologies. And listeners offer interesting topics using comments on the site. New issues are recorded on Friday evening, and published on Saturday afternoon.

RadioJS



As you may have guessed, the podcast is about JS. Each issue discusses the latest news from the world of JS. From time to time, invited guests, notable professionals, so that they share their experiences. They talk about libraries and frameworks, the latest trends and techniques of the "old school". Each issue is 30 to 60 minutes long.

Software Development podCAST



This is one of the few IT podcasts in which only programming is discussed. 2 people take part in the recording of each issue - the presenter and the guest. Of the guests are: the director of one of the admin departments of Mail.ru, the developer of the frontend of Sberbank, the developer of the frontend ostrovok.ru and other interesting people. The art of programming



The podcast is aimed at programmers and those who are simply interested in this: students, novice developers who are just learning the world of patterns. In addition to such important problems for the developer, the author of the podcast reviews various books that you may find useful, covers interesting materials from the web. The podcast comes out once a week and lasts an average of 15-20 minutes.

Android dev



A podcast about development for Android and everything connected with it. Guests of the program are developers with great experience and experience, who remember Android still young and green when it was still version 1.5. Over the years they have written applications for millions of users around the world. In the podcast they talk about development in all its aspects, from cutting the design to assembling its own firmware.

Rwpod



A podcast about Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Lead Alexey Vasilyev and Alexander Chaplinsky , the developers of the company Railsware. Podcasts are released on Tuesdays and last from 30 to 60 minutes, currently 32 episodes are recorded.

Solo on .Net


Podcast is run by an experienced c # developer Dmitry Nesteruk. As the name implies, the most discussed are things related to programming on the .NET technology stack. However, there are often small branches in the direction of something IT, without being tied to the platform. The podcast comes out twice a month.

Frankly about IT careerism


Conversation show with interesting interlocutors, about programming, career and everything related to IT.
