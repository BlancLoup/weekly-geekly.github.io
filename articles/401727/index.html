Smart car charger Nonda ZUS Car Charger


To: technically advanced motorist


California startup Nonda has turned a banal car charger into a real smart device. ZUS Car Charger is inserted into the standard cigarette lighter, as well as less "brainy" counterparts. The case is quite large - 8.4 centimeters in length, made of dark gray plastic and has a stylish design that won the IF and RedDot awards in 2016.

The gadget works with an input voltage of 12 and 24 volts, and delivers a current of 2.4 amps with a voltage of 5 volts to two USB connectors. Nests are equipped with LED lighting and angled to each other so that the wires are not confused. The ports are reversible, so that the plug is inserted by either side - very convenient.



ZUS Car Charger connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth. Brand mobile application is available for devices based on iOS version 8.0 and above and Android 4.3 (Jelly Bean). After installing it, charging becomes a smart locator: when the engine turns off, it automatically remembers the position of the car. The car becomes very easy to find in the open parking area on the compass in the application. In addition, a parking timer and a monitor are available in the application for checking the voltage on the car battery.

Additional attractive features of the charger include ‚Äúindestructibility‚Äù. The developers tested the ZUS Car Charger at extreme temperatures, and the gadget failed only when heated to 140. Charging is equipped with a cooling system and complies with the military standard MIL-STD-810G.

RoboShar Sphero 2.0


To: a little geek, the head of a large family, the owner of a dog


Sphero 2.0 Robotic Ball is a great example of a device that encourages active interaction with other people through games.

Sphero 2.0 is a ball with a diameter of 74 mm and a weight of 168 grams, made of white polycarbonate. Inside are the ARM Cortex M4 processor, a Bluetooth module, an accelerometer, a gyroscope, a controller, LEDs, and other electronics. The surface of the ball is waterproof, but it is not shockproof, so it is better not to drop it from a height of more than 15 centimeters. The gadget independently rolls on any surface at a speed of 2 m / s and receives a signal at a distance of up to 30 meters.



Sphero is managed via a mobile application for Windows Phone, iOS and Android. Immediately available are two and a half dozen games, including multiplayer.

It is quite difficult to pilot a robo ball, but with a well-trained owner skill, Sphero is capable of unusual tricks. Robo shar races can be a favorite entertainment of a group of friends and a great reason to get out into nature. In the latter case, for Sphero worth buying a protective rubber case.

The battery lasts for an hour of work. Charging is carried out in an inductive way on a special stand, and will take about three hours with fully discharged batteries.

Virtual reality helmet HTC Vive


To: a friend or relative who has a powerful gaming PC



There is no sense in telling in detail about the virtual reality helmet HTC Vive. The legendary device received more than convincing coverage on Geektimes and needs no introduction.

The only point that I would like to highlight is the compatibility of the helmet. As we learned from experience at Madrobots, the minimum system requirements do not guarantee that the Vive will work the first time or will work in principle.
