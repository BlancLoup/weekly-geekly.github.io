Screen


Display Sony Xperia P deserves to stay on it separately. He even has his own name - WhiteMagic. Its uniqueness lies in the fourth added to the traditional three subpixels (red, green and blue) of the white. Usually, the white color on the screen is obtained by mixing the three primary colors, and, depending on the settings, the color may ‚Äúfade‚Äù with another shade. As a result, in Xperia P white is not achieved by mixing these three subpixels, but only by working white, which provides almost perfect color reproduction and at the same time saves battery consumption. In addition, thanks to a new subpixel, the Xperia P's display is very bright - the maximum brightness is 935 candles versus the usual 500 candles for smartphones, which ensures comfortable work in the sun.
