February 14: PM Weekend 3 with two project management experts

UPDATE: Record meeting posted (links in the first comment).

It seems that our online meetings for project managers are becoming traditional. February 14, we will hold the 3rd meeting of the online community PM Weekend:

This time we managed to invite the president of the St. Petersburg PMI branch, Yevgeny Bumagin. Therefore, before escaping an urgent search for a gift to congratulate the other half on Valentine's Day, you will have the opportunity to see:
  • Practical seminar "Simple steps for the implementation of the project office in the organization - practical tools", moderator - Yevgeny Bumagin.
  • Real case "Building a project office is not in an IT-company"
  • Interview on "Model of Project Management" on a standard assignment "(moderator - Ivan Selikhovkin)




Participation is absolutely free with prior registration:

PM Weekend (registration) >>

Dates: February 14, 12:00 - 14:00 Moscow time. The format of the meeting is online HD broadcast, so you can join the meeting from any place convenient to you.

Leading meetings:

Invited guest: Yevgeny Bumagin , President of the St. Petersburg branch of PMI, Director of Project Management at the largest companies in St. Petersburg.

Regular presenters: Ivan Selikhovkin (PMP, Deputy Project Director of NIPK Electron CJSC, Vice President of the St. Petersburg branch of PMI), Alexander Orlov (managing partner of Stratoplan School of Managers).

Good luck and see you on Saturday at PM Weekend!

PS Recording will traditionally be available to those who register for the meeting.
