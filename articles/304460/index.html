Announcement of the release of the SDcast podcast about OpenVZ

On the eve of the final release of OpenVZ 7, the company's employees decided to answer all the questions about changes in the company, the development of a new version, the future of the OpenVZ project in one of the next releases of the podcast about SDCast software development .
