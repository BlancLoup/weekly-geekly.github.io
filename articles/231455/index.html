Evacuated car? Notify owner


Currently, 305 tow trucks are operating in Moscow (by 2015 it will be increased to 440). About 800 cars are evacuated per day. According to unverified data, the plan of one tow truck is 5 cars per day. For some reason, the Moscow government refused to send an SMS at the time of the evacuation, the SMS is sent after the evacuation.
Let's help each other for yourself!

The simplest application for Android E112VA will allow you to send a message to the owner of the car that is being evacuated. The application is really simple - just enter the car number and press the button.

The owner of the car being evacuated will receive an SMS message ‚ÄúYour car will be evacuated!‚Äù (The application does not need to do this). He will have 10-15 minutes to remove a wrongly parked car, as according to the law, if the car has not yet been taken away, it must be returned to the owner (a reference to the relevant legislative act is sought ).

How it works

To work with the application, you must first register with the Unified Mobile Platform (EMP) of the Government of Moscow. Registration and sending messages are completely free . To register in the EMP through the application you will need to specify the number of your car. The car number will be tied to your phone number in the EMP. Already about 60'000 Moscow drivers tied the phone number to the car number in the EMP. A similar binding will be made on the application server - in case of unavailability of the EMP.

An Internet connection is not required for registration and for the application to work. Messaging occurs through free SMS.
