New Ubiquiti 4 Megapixel IP Camera Line - UniFi G3 Series



Introducing Ubiquiti's Next Generation HD UniFi Video Cameras, a new series of surveillance cameras. The short name of the UniFi G3 product line is represented by the UVC-G3 and UVC-G3-DOME models. Immediately, we note the key difference from the previous series of UVC cameras - this resolution, now in our hands, according to the sensor resolution, 4 megapixel 1080p camera (1920x1080) Full HD against 720p (1280 x 720), which was previously, support for HDR is also added, which means that the camera will work much better in the conditions of exposure of the frame or part of it, for example, by the sun's rays or vice versa, the lack of lighting (the period when the backlight is still early to turn on, and the quality of the picture is already falling due to the lack of lighting). And we also have more than 20% increased viewing angle, double the memory of 512 MB and an extended temperature range from -30 to 70 ¬∞ C. The cameras have become more compact, sleeker and more convenient to install with almost the same prices as previous generation cameras.
