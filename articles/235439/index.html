MOTA SmartRing: smart ring instead of smart watches



The number of manufacturers of smart watches, smart bracelets and other high-tech accessories is already going off scale. Every week, all new companies creating their own gadgets are added to the race for the production of such devices, and the excitement is noticeable both among the technological giants and among the non-manufacturer manufacturers.

As a result, the niche of the same smart watch is already full, and it is becoming closer, if I may say so. Therefore, manufacturers have to invent something new. Someone adds some unique features or unusual appearance to their own smart watches. Well, and someone creates a smart device of a new type. This is exactly what MOTA did, which announced the imminent launch of the ... smart ring.

Do smart watches seem too large and uncomfortable? Then you can try MOTA SmartRing.

Despite its size, the ring is equipped with a small display, Bluetooth wireless module and vibra. When connected to a smartphone (iOS or Android), the ring allows you to track notifications of incoming messages, and also shows the time.



MOTA SmartRing can track the receipt of email, text messages, calendar events and incoming calls. In addition, there is the possibility of receiving notifications about events in your Facebook or Twitter account. Upon receipt of a message, the ring vibrates.



Of course, the MOTA SmartRing case is waterproof.

This week, the device will be shown at the IFA Berlin Technological Show, where the technical specifications, price and release date of the device will be announced.



Actually, MOTA SmartRing is a very unusual micronische device. But knowing the love of many geeks and ordinary people to new products in the world of technology, one must think that this device will find its buyer.
