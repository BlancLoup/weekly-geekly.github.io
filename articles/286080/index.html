Masters abroad: grants and scholarship programs

In most cases, documents are sent online. You need to register on the university website, fill out the application form and attach the necessary documents to it. All data are considered by the commission, and then either a refusal or an offer to come for further processing comes.

To get a grant for training, you do not have to be the most talented or the smartest. However, you must be distinguished by your academic and / or professional accomplishments.
