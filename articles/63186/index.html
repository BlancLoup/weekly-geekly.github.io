Akado shows traffic jams live

It is strange that Akado herself did not post this news, and what is even more strange, no one before me had published it.

Akado has a nice resource that shows video cameras installed in various places in Moscow, which shoot carriageways, which allows you to really understand the situation on the road. Unlike GoogleMaps in Akado, the cameras show live video directly, and not a 20-minute freshness image, which is very nice.

I have long dreamed of such a service, to be honest, I hope that the responsible users of Akada will add more video cameras there. Let's ask them :)

UPD . Thought: But it is not so difficult to recognize the speed of movement of objects on the camera, tie it to a section of the road, and actually show traffic jams.
