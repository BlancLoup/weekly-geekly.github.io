The Swedes took to the demonstration in support of Pirate Bay

Translation of Ernesto's post from TorrentFreak.com.

More than a thousand people gathered yesterday in the square in the Swedish capital Stockholm to protest the sentence to the founders of The Pirate Bay . The demonstration was organized by the Swedish Pirate Party, which in June plans to run for the European Parliament.

The day before yesterday, the four founders of TPB were sentenced to a year in prison, and compensation for damage to the entertainment industry in the amount of $ 905,000 each. The defendants, in turn, said that an appeal would be filed, which means that the case could drag on for many years.

Many Swedes are outraged by the harsh sentence imposed by the court, and yesterday the Swedish Pirate Party organized a demonstration in support of the defendants. The demonstration was held in Stockholm, similar protests were held in other cities in Sweden.
