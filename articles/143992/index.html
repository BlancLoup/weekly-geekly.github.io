Study: Google+ users demonstrate low social activity

The efforts of the search giant in the field of social networks, according to reports by Google itself, are not brilliant, but the existing user base of approximately 170 million people fully satisfies the company, providing a monthly audience growth.

Nevertheless, whether in anticipation of Facebook's IPO, or just at this time, research company Fast Company published its document containing research on the quality of social activity of the Google+ user base. The research method was as follows: 40,000 public accounts were randomly selected, the behavior of which was analyzed by automated means.
