Dear Habr, I want you to better hear your usernames

TL; DR: github.com/limonte/dear-habr

Dear TM administration, you have created and maintain the best Russian-language IT blog. You do an excellent job with your task, but I believe that the time has come to take a step forward and become a little closer to your users.



These users are by nature very critical due to professional deformation. Every day we look for mistakes and very well know how to point out these mistakes, and sometimes with a bit of mockery.

We often forget to compliment what looks great and works, taking it for granted.

Filling this gap, I want to declare responsibly:
Habr, you are the best in functionality and UI not only in runet, you are the absolute champion. And let someone correct me in the comments, bringing an IT blog in any language that is more convenient for Habr.
