Briefly about the new: Samsung announced GALAXY Note 8.0

Good evening, Habrazhiteli!

Yesterday, Samsung decided to reveal the cards and announce the new tablet of the flagship mobile device line GALAXY Note 8.0 even before the official launch of the MWC 2013 exhibition in Barcelona. Today it will be possible to study it as it should be on the Samsung stand, but for now we have a short description of the technical characteristics of the new product.


Operating system: Android 4.1.2 (Jellybean)
Processor: Exynos 4 Quad (CPU Cortex-A9 - 1.6 GHz)
RAM: 2 GB
Internal memory: 16 or 32 GB (microSD memory cards up to 64 GB are supported)
Screen: 8.0 ", 1280x800 pixels, 189 DPI
Camera: 5 MP (main), 1.3 MP (front)
Sensors: accelerometer, digital compass and proximity sensor
Interfaces: Wi-Fi 802.11a / b / g / n (dual-mode, with support for Wi-Fi Direct and AllShareCast); Bluetooth 4.0; USB 2.0, A-GPS + GLONASS
Battery: standard lithium-ion, with a capacity of 4600 mA * h
Size: 210.8 x 135.9 x 7.95 mm
Weight: 338 grams (with 3G and Wi-Fi modules on board)

An important point is the fact that the device is equipped with a HSPA + 21 Mbit / s radio module (850, 900, 1900, 2100 MHz). We activate the micro-SIM card, and you can call from the tablet.

In the process of developing GALAXY Note 8.0, Samsung used the experience gained from previous flagship devices, in particular, the GALAXY S III and GALAXY Note II smartphones, as well as the GALAXY Note 10.1 tablet. Such a conclusion can be made based on the fact that the novelty received support from the branded electronic pen S Pen and the pre-installed applications S Note and S Planner. By the way, from now on Android virtual buttons will react to pressing using the S Pen - this feature has been waiting for a long time for Samsung mobile devices.

Another small, but curious feature that the new tablet got is the Smart Remote. With it, you can control home appliances through an infrared emitter built into the device.

Samsung GALAXY Note 8.0 will go on sale in the second quarter of 2013. The price of new items has not yet been disclosed.
