Smile to the world!



Being in a good mood is important not only for you, but, however banal it may sound, it is important for others: it is pleasant for people to communicate with a positive-minded person.


As they say, we can consider ourselves even kings, but to know who we really are.
Our prevailing mood is easy to learn - you need to keep a diary of your mood.

Maybe you have long wanted to be aware of the mood of an important person for you: be it your close or not.
Here, too, everything is simple: it is necessary that the diaries of the mood were kept by themselves and shared by them.

To simplify this whole process, a smileter was created. As you may have guessed: the mood diary service.

Good news for everyone: the site does not need registration. OpenID is used for your convenience.



Now you can track the mood of the entire planet!
To visualize the mood of the planet, GeoIP and GooleMaps are used.



Positive mood you.
Let our tiny blue ball, in these boundless open spaces of the universe, be lit up with smiles!
