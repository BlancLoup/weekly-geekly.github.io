Firefox OS smartphone went on sale for $ 33 (in India)

Mozilla proudly announced the start of sales of the Cloud FX smartphone running the Firefox OS. The Intex Technologies production smartphone is available to customers exclusively through Snapdeal.com at a price of 1999 rupees (US $ 33).



Dual SIM smartphone is equipped with a 2-megapixel camera, 3.5-inch display, 1 GHz processor, 1250 mAh battery, Bluetooth. The package does not include headphones and a flash card. The phone originally pre-installed several games, Facebook applications and Twitter. Naturally, all applications are written in HTML5.

Cloud FX is the first smartphone for Firefox OS, which is sold in India and in Asia in general. Mozilla makes a big bet on emerging markets and soon promises to start selling smartphones, including in Russia (at least, there were such plans before).
