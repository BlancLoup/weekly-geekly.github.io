The ability to bypass the lock screen iOS 7.0 and access the photo. And to the contacts!

Steps to reproduce:


  • We break the phone, lift up the widget access panel;
  • Open the stopwatch;
  • Choose an alarm clock;
  • Hold the shutdown button until "Cancel";
  • Click Cancel and quickly press "Home" twice;
  • If the camera was launched, select it and access the photo.
