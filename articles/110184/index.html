UJAM opens public alpha testing

UJAM is a web service that allows anyone to compose their own music . The capabilities of the service are really impressive - just sing into the microphone (on ‚Äúla-la-la-la‚Äù, ‚Äúaaaa‚Äù, or just mumble) or play the right melody on any musical instrument so that the web application itself records it as notes . Simply put, UJAM allows you to listen to how the melody just invented in your head will be played by an orchestra or a rock band.



UJAM works on two clouds, one in the USA, the second in Europe. Cloud selection is automatic, but you can switch manually.
