The materials of webinars conducted by Positive Technologies within the framework of the Practical Safety educational program are published.
The program is aimed at raising the awareness of specialists about the existing threats, methods and approaches to the analysis of security, as well as about managing compliance with standards and monitoring the effectiveness of remedies. The speakers are well-known information security experts - representatives of Positive Technologies and Positive Research research center.
In 2011, more than 10 webinars were held, bringing together more than 1,500 participants from around the world. There were reports on Web security, cloud computing, network infrastructure, VOIP networks, reviews on compliance monitoring standards, evaluating the effectiveness of information security tools and practical use of the security monitoring system and compliance standards MaxPatrol .
