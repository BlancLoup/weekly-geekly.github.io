E-tickets are around the corner

Perhaps in the near future, the Russians will be available the opportunity to purchase electronic tickets. The corresponding bill was approved in the first reading by the State Duma of the Russian Federation . Innovation, according to its authors, will increase the competitiveness of Russian air carriers and help them to more successfully compete with Western airlines. Well, the passengers, of course, will be more convenient, especially since in the West since the New Year there is a system of widespread sale of electronic tickets.

In addition, as stated by the head of the Duma Committee on Legislation, Pavel Krasheninnikov, legitimizing and introducing electronic air tickets is economically beneficial for the country, as it will significantly reduce the staff and reduce the cost of air travel.
