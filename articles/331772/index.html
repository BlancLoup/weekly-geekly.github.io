Introduction 3. Law 54. Ticket offices go online


The already existing Federal Law No. 54 prescribes that right from this Saturday, July 1, 2017, all cash offices in the Russian Federation must connect to the Internet and send electronic versions of checks to the tax. Instead of the usual ECL, the cash register should have a fiscal drive, information from which will be sent to the tax office through an intermediary - the fiscal data operator.
