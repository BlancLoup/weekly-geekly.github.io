ASUS GX1000 Gaming Mouse

Project management? Programming? Prototyping? Planners and meetings? Study? In your highly intellectual working life, you, habrazhitel, will probably take small breaks in order to unload your consciousness and fill a couple of dozen frags into a counter or take part in another battle shoulder to shoulder with other members of your clan in DotA.
And if we talk about efficiency, and since you have finished your business and intend to have a good rest (eat a deserved carrot), then your rest should also be useful. And a good mouse is one of the important components in your brain relaxation, because it depends on how the computer understands your teams, how quickly your virtual hero will hide from bullets, or how accurately get into the terrorists seated on the box in Temka.
ASUS has a separate player-oriented brand - Republic of Gamers. Laptops, motherboards, video cards, sound cards, headsets and mice are manufactured under this brand. The hero of this review will be the mouse GX1000 Eagle Eye.


Specifications


Sensor resolution: 8200 dpi
Number of buttons: 6 + scroll wheel
Cable length: 1.8 m
Dimensions: 43,5x65,5x128,5 mm
Weight: 150-175 g
Package size: 270x210x80 mm, weight 0.54 kg
Features:
  • high-precision laser sensor with adjustable resolution (from 50 to 8200 dpi);
  • durable ergonomic body with aluminum trim;
  • indicator of the selected profile with settings;
  • weight adjustment using the included weight;
  • convenient software interface to configure all device parameters.


Package and package


The size of the box in which the mouse is supplied is quite impressive. I even thought that besides the mouse there is something else.


On the front side there is the embossed Republic of Gamers logo, on the back side there are technical characteristics and designation of functions.




The inner part of the box is fixed with velcro - in order to open it, you need to pull the sidewall, and it will open with a characteristic crunch.


Under the transparent plastic - the mouse.


In general, the packaging creates a pleasant impression, as if in front of you a deluxe edition of some game. This box is not ashamed to give.




After unpacking the box, we see a rag mat and a sheet describing the main functions. There is no CD with software included. Distribution offer download from the site.


Appearance


The design of the mouse is attractive: it has something from sports cars, a sort of mixture of metal, smooth outlines and sharp corners.






The mouse is completely symmetrical, so that it is suitable for left-handers and right-handers.


But left-handers will not be very comfortable to press additional buttons with their little fingers.

The upper part of the mouse is covered with a metal insert. The left and right buttons are also made of metal. The remaining parts are made of matte and glossy plastic.

A laser sensor, sliding inserts and a profile change button are located on the bottom side of the mouse. Through the software, you can configure up to four different profiles that completely change the mouse settings. For example, a profile for a specific game or program. It will not be very convenient to constantly turn the mouse and press the button - you cannot do it with one hand. But, on the other hand, this can be done while loading the game.


As with most gaming mice, the GX1000 provides for weight control. Included is a small box the size of a flash drive with five weights, tablets of 5 g - only 25 g. Accordingly, you can increase the weight of the mouse from 150 to 175 g.




Click on the basket for weights and pull it out.




The mount looks like a cover on an old CD player - simple enough, but fragile, so be careful. For the correct insertion of the basket there are arrows.

In the main window, the profile number 1 is immediately displayed. There are many settings, you can reassign all the buttons, except for the profile change button.
