Mail.Ru holds the first Technology Forum.

Friends and colleagues



We are glad to inform you that on September 14, the Mail.Ru Technologies Forum will be held at the Central House of Entrepreneur . Start at 10 am

During the event, for the first time, we will share with the participants our most interesting technological secrets and innovations.

What is the Technology Forum?


Two parallel flow. Visitors of the first will be able to listen to the reports of leading technical specialists of the company and invited guests - about web development, high-load projects, unique architectural solutions.

The second stream is fully dedicated to social networking applications. The Technologies Forum will bring together Platform@Mail.Ru and creators of social applications to discuss the present and future of this industry.

Speakers are top managers and developers of key projects of one of the largest portals of Runet: Vice-President and Head of Mail.Ru communication services Anna Artamonova, Vice-President and Technical Director of the company Vladimir Gabrielyan, world-renowned PostgreSQL specialist and system architect of the portal Fedor Sigaev and a lot others.

And:

  • Special "star" guests - Tom Preston-Werner, Cofounder and CTO at GitHub, representative of ICQ Avi Zrachya, Director of Infrastructure and Operations;
  • Unlimited communication with the Runet guru;
  • The first meeting of the Club of developers of social applications, which will later become permanent and, we hope, will allow to establish closer contact between market participants;

You can register for participation on the official website techforum.mail.ru .

The first hours after its launch showed that interest in the event is extremely high. However, Habrayuzer applications are given priority: just indicate at registration that you learned about the Forum on your favorite resource.

Please note: participation in the Forum is completely free.

We are waiting for you!
