iTwister - the embodiment of twister on the iPhone / iTouch

Description




This game can bring you fame as a person with the most flexible and agile fingers. It is important to know how to control your body and always provide for subsequent actions.

You just need to start the game and enjoy the fun as one on the road or at home, and with a loved one.




Game Review




Recall the rules of the usual Twister: on the rug with colored circles are a few people. Pre-select the starting position. The roulette wheel is spinning, and thus the part of the body and the color to which this part of the body is to be put is selected. At the same time you can not tear off other parts of the body from the mat. Then the roulette wheel is spinning again, and another player is already making its move. The player who tears off the inactive part of the body loses and retires. The game continues until there is only one player left who becomes the winner.

The game on the iPhone is as similar as possible, playable and has not lost the interest of its progenitor. The rules are the same as in the "floor" version, except that you need to move part of the body without taking it off the screen - this makes the game more interesting and more difficult.

When you start the game, we see that the game is made in the street style.


Observe an intuitive menu:
Play - launches new game,
Rules - it is recommended to open before the first launch,
Records - here you can see all your records in various game modes,
Options - customize the game by yourself,
Info - information about the game, hidden in the lower left corner.


In the Options we are waiting for the settings on and the volume of music and sound effects, as well as vibration. It is possible to turn the finger marker on and off (they will always be under the fingers, no matter where they are). We also observe the choice of the game mode: Easy, Medium and Complicated, or "Game Together".


So let's get down to the game itself.

Click the menu item "Play", and watch the twister mat, but not simple. This is a piece of asphalt, decorated with balonchiki by old school fans.
We are welcomed by a notification of a new game that tells you how to start the game. It is necessary to place your fingers (well, or any other parts of the body, to whom, as it is convenient) in any circles - this will be your starting position. When place the specified number of fingers - notice that the frame will change its color from white to one of three: Orange, Green or Purple. The frame in the game plays the role of roulette from a regular twister. However, not one she shows us all the data. We notice that the marker of the installed finger starts spinning. This means that it is him who we must move to any free circle, painted in the color of the frame. If you successfully place your finger on a new place, the marker of the other finger starts spinning, and the frame uses a different color, although it can repeat the previous one. At first, moving your fingers, keeping them from the screen, and at the same time keeping other fingers in the field is quite difficult, but after 10-20 games it becomes easier.

This game is not only on sleight of hand and luck, but also on logic, because it all depends on where you put your finger in order to make it easier in the future.

If the game seems easy, then increase the difficulty level and try to play with one hand.
