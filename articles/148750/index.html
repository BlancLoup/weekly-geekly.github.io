10 of the most original and useful gadgets according to OViVO

Pillow Remote Control

Pillow-remote control recognizes the signal of 500 popular TV models. This gadget is for those who like to lie down at the TV, but regularly loses the remote.



Privacy monitor

A non-profit device for those who get annoyed when they look over their shoulders. For others - an impenetrable white screen, for you, who are sitting in special glasses, the information you want to keep confidential.



U Transfer

USB flash drive to quickly transfer information from a USB flash drive to a USB flash drive without using a computer. The device is equipped with a display for selecting the transmitted file and controlling the transfer. On one side of the gadget is a USB-plug, to the other through the connector is connected to the second flash drive.



Glasses to avoid dry eyes

A strange invention for those who spend a lot of time at the computer. Special glasses track the blink rate, and if the eye is not moistened for 5 seconds, the lens becomes cloudy.



The leap motion

Futuristic invention that allows you to manipulate a computer without the use of controllers. The device picks up the movements of all ten fingers. The device performs not only simple tasks like navigation on the desktop, but helps in the manipulation of 3d objects, designing; With the help of leap motion you can play.



G 182, smart vacuum cleaner

Actually, the home cleaning robot, equipped with a camera and Wi-Fi. Itself chooses a route of movement, avoids obstacles, after cleaning comes back to a place. You can control this marvelous invention with the help of a remote control, and via Wi-Fi, connect to the camera installed on it.



"Taponariki"

Night slippers with LED lamps on their toes will save you from breaking a finger when you go to the restroom at night. For children, there are color diodes: they help fight the fear of the dark.



Clocky running alarm

This invention is a student of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Gauri Nanda. After pressing the end button, the gadget moves off the nightstand and starts to drive on the floor, forcing you to get up and calm it down. Not only awakening, but also morning exercises are provided.



Point and Click Dictionary - portable translator

This small gadget is equipped with a retractable scanner, sent to a specific word in the printed text, and a display that displays the translation and interpretation. The tiny device is indispensable for translators and lovers to read the original literature. Plus, the device can be used as an MP3 player or voice recorder.



gTar, interactive learning guitar

The device combines an ordinary electric guitar and a tutorial for playing it. The device is based on the iPhone. You connect the phone, select the track in the application, after which the iPhone display will prompt each subsequent movement that you want to make. At the same time, the neck of the guitar is also highlighted, indicating where to hold the string. In general, gTar allows you to save your personal time and money on a tutor.
