And I love tenders!

Judge for yourself, how great it is to pay less and get more. Fantasy? Not at all. The whole hysterical howl around the state system. trades in the Russian Federation, the state procurement site and the gold saw are raised either by those who have their own sawing too small and have failed to stick to the juicy oil or minestre pie, or those who are not able to work on a competitive basis at all. 94-FZ guys - this is real gold! Whole, you can even say, Klondike. After all, the tender is not just a certain ritual. The main value is that it forces all the participants of the process to move with force of compulsion: the customer is obliged by law to annually evaluate the market in search of the best, and sellers have the right to challenge his choice and offer their options. And accounting does not whine. More precisely, whining, but it does not bother anyone at all.

Take, for example, our university, the oldest and largest in the region. In 2006, if memory fails, we paid for the Internet at 3 rubles per megabyte at a speed in the region of one megabit, and now we give 25,000 p. per month for 20 full Mbps. Great, right? Yes, not the word! Would this happen without annual service contests? I am not sure: it is unlikely that the accounting department would constantly renew the contract, finding a thousand reasons covering its own laziness. Yes, and providers regularly try to press or change the conditions in their favor. ... But here, like a little devil from a snuffbox, 94-FZ comes into the world and acts on someone better than other pills or medicine for greed.
