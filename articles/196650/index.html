Academy Huawei October 17, 2013. in Yekaterinburg

Landata invites partners to take part in the Academy of Huawei, which will be held on October 17, 2013. In Ekaterinburg

The Huawei Academy is focused both on sales support managers in areas such as networks and telecommunications, video conferencing, and on sales managers and managers. The main goal of the event is to provide partners with detailed information about the Huawei Enterprise products necessary for successful sales of Huawei solutions and the correct positioning of products on the market.

Participation in the Academy is free! Registration is required! Applications are accepted until October 11, 2013. Attention! Only representatives of system integrator companies are invited to participate. The application will be considered only in case of specifying a corporate e-mail.

In the program of the event:

Huawei Ethernet Switches - A Complete Solution for Corporate Business
WLAN. Access points and controllers
USG firewalls - Effective network protection
ARG3 - 3rd Generation Router
VRP operating system. Command syntax, sample configurations
UniStar tool for preparing equipment specifications
MCU new generation
Huawei video terminals. TE30 - Three-in-one terminal Full HD: codec + camera + microphone

During the event, it will also be possible to familiarize yourself with samples of the S5300 switches, AR1220 and AR531 routers, VP9610 video server and VP9030 and VP8033 video terminals.

For participation in the event, contact Yana Mordakina, Landata Marketing Specialist, at e-mail: mordakina@landata.ru.

Attention! From one company can register no more than 2 participants.
