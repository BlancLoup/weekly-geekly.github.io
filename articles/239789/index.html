October 21, watch the AzureConf 2014 online conference from Microsoft Azure experts!



Good day to all!

We are pleased to announce Azure Conf 2014 - one of the key events of the year for the Microsoft Azure community, to be held on October 21, 2014 .

The event will open with a speech by Scott Guthrie , and will continue with numerous sessions of representatives of the Azure community.

On Azure Conf, you will have the opportunity to get information about a whole range of services for building mobile applications, web applications and scalable multi-tier applications in the cloud.

The broadcast will take place on October 21 from 19.00 to 3.00 on Channel 9 .

You will see how, with the help of Azure, developers are building robust, highly scalable applications.

Azure experts from around the world will talk online about their own experience using the platform.
Among them: Michael Collier, Mike Martin, Rick Garibay, Chris Old, and other members of the community.

Are you just starting to use Microsoft Azure or are you already a professional? Get connected! The event will be interesting to a specialist of any level.

Viewing Azure Conf 2014 is available without registration .
The conference is held in English.
