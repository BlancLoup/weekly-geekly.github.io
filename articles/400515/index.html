Blockchenizer: advantages of the blockchain for those who do not like cryptocurrency

Have you forgotten about autoblockchain ? Nearly 200 comments on this post gave us a rather interesting feedback, so we decided to post announcements of our new developments even a little earlier. Meet blockchenizer: Blockchain as a Service.



Arguing about the benefits of the blockchain in different areas of life, we sometimes forget that, in addition to development problems, the implementation problem is even more important. Therefore, we decided to simplify as much as possible access to blockchain technologies so that, in principle, a blockchain wallet is not required.

We offer the possibility of storing NVS records up to 29 KB in the Blockchenizer, which is basically enough for almost any statistical, registration information as it is or for larger files in the form of a hash sum.

Development, like our previous projects, is based on the Emercoin blockchain.

Blockchenizer (the site is still in development) provides an API for blockchain operations with the option of paying with fiat money.

State and private organizations


The demand for the blockchain is growing all over the world, and Russia is no exception - for us, for example, we are drowning Sberbank and German Gref personally. But at the same time, almost all organizations that are already directly eager to implement the blockchain cannot and do not want to deal with cryptocurrencies as such.

It is ridiculous: the guys from Emercoin for the presentation of their technologies in public and private institutions had to redo their wallets, removing from there any mention of koin, calling them instead "blockchain editor". How to put it on balance - as a foreign currency or an asset?

Even if you assume that you took the risk and, not being afraid of questions from regulators, officially included cryptocurrency in the balance of your organization, new risks arise: if you control the cryptocurrency, you can always lose it - for example, the employee made a mistake in the translation. It is technically impossible to cancel a blockchain transaction (for this we both love and fear).

"Blockchenizer" removes all these problems entirely: the organization does not need to start the wallet itself and introduce the concept of cryptocurrency into its own accounting. Calculations with us are fiatnym money, and we already provide a service.
