How much do corporations spend on promoting their cloud products?



Major players in the cloud services market are trying to occupy a dominant position. At the same time, such companies spend billions of dollars a year to expand the capacity of their data centers in order to maintain the performance of their own services at a high level. At the same time, the budget of such projects increases from year to year.

Best of all, this trend can be seen in the example of Amazon with their Amazon Web Services. Everything is good here both in terms of costs and in terms of income received. So, in 2015, revenue for the first quarter amounted to about $ 1.57 billion. This is 50% more than the same period of time last year. Net income - $ 265 million. The company's revenue for the year shown in the first quarter of 2015 amounted to $ 5.16 billion.

At the same time, AWS increases profitability, so $ 5 billion is not the limit. However, AWS requires constant costs for the purchase of additional equipment, upgrading the infrastructure of data centers and other cost items. At the end of 2014, the cost of equipment, buildings and structures, the land on which AWS DCs are located, was $ 7 billion. The year before, the cost of all this was only $ 3.8 billion.
