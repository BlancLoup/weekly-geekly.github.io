The two complaints I hear most often about the Amazon Kindle are the price of the device and the fact that international users cannot use Whispernet (technology to connect Kindle to a network that is currently only available in the US). Amazon announced the Internation, the GSM version of the Kindle, which is currently available for orders at a price of 279 dollars. Also, the US version dropped in price to $ 259.

AT & T has allowed the use of VoIP software on iPhones for calls over 3G and 2G networks. Until recently, users could use Skype or Vonage only via WiFi. Verizon and Google have launched a partnership to create an open Android platform that will allow Google Voice to be launched. Information was confirmed on the launch of HTC Hero on Verizon's cellular network in November.

The company doubleTwist announced the launch of the Amazon MP3 Store , which will allow users to buy music and download it to almost any device.

There are new models of Gateway One , ZX4800 and ZX6800. The ZX6800 combines a computer and a high-resolution TV, 1920x1080.
