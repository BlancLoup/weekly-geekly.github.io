English for IT? Easy!

This topic does not relate to IT directly, but everyone knows that without it, nowhere. Unfortunately, not everyone has the opportunity to learn English with teachers. Well, let's try to do this at home and with maximum efficiency!


So, what is usually advised to those who want to learn English on their own? More to read and watch movies.

No matter how wrong!

This is the main mistake that almost everyone encounters, beginner level and below. The development of vocabulary will give at first very little use. And why?

For example, we all know that a few words in a different order can have completely different meanings. From the fact that we will memorize the translation of the word have (to have, to possess) - it will not be easier for us, because in combination with some other words (have to), it already has a completely different meaning. And you need to know.

So, where to start learning English? With grammar.
Unfortunately, not everyone is happy with textbooks like Murphy and Headway - if only because it is unrealistic for a beginner to understand what is written there because of a poor vocabulary.

So you need to look for resources in the open spaces of the Internet, of which there is plenty of today, but, unfortunately, not all of them are structured and have a simple and understandable way of presenting educational information.

I will try to provide a small plan, focusing on which, you can become familiar with grammar relatively quickly and fairly easily, and then continue to improve English by developing vocabulary.
