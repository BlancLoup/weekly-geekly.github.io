iPhone + iTunes: how to get an account to download applications

This note will be interesting to those who bought the iPhone but lives in a country where the iTunes Store does not work, as a result of which you can not register to the iTunes store to download additional programs to the phone.

I live in Belarus and the iPhone is not selling here. But you can buy it from neighbors, for example, they brought it to me from Warsaw, where they bought it without a contract in the store of the telephone operator Era . Work with the local operator Velcom began immediately after installing a SIM card in it (although I heard that they need to be unlocked, etc.).
