Reportedly , the cause of the accident was a virus that hit the airline's computer system. Because of this, the system could not identify the three technical problems of the aircraft, which, if detected, could have prevented the departure.



A preliminary investigation revealed that the aircraft had flaps and slats removed - and the pilots did not receive an audible message about this because they were unable to activate the warning system during takeoff.
