Commodore released Amiga Mini!

I do not believe my eyes! This is the Amiga comeback in a new form factor!
Commodore is pleased to inform us that the Amiga brand is objecting and represents the Amiga Mini and several other models.
Back in August 2010, the president and CEO of Commodore USA Barry Altman announced the release of several PCs all in one under the Amiga brand.

It is reported that Amiga officially returned as a gaming platform form factor Mini.
Declared specifications : 3.5 GHz Sandy Bridge Core i7 CPU, 16 GB RAM, 1 GB NVIDIA GeForce GT 430 GPU, 1 TB hard drive.
All this happiness running Linux-based Commodore OS Vision.
