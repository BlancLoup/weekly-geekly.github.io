Corel has included Firefox in the office suite.

Increasingly, Firefox is being distributed with third-party software.
The other day (November 9, 2006), Corel announced that the Firefox web browser will be included in the Corel WordPerfect Office Home Edition 2007 office suite, which will be released in January 2007 for $ 79. The package includes a text editor, spreadsheets, software for working with photos and video, to burn CD / DVD and utilities from Norton and Firefox.
