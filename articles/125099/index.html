Keyboard overview for PS3 controller

In principle, keyboard overlays for game controllers, they are Messenger Kit, they are also ThumbPads, they are ChatPads, are not the first subject of gaming necessity, because the text on PS3 can be entered using a regular gamepad: yes, difficult, yes Hemorrhoids, but still possible. However, if any game (say, Killzone 3) with a terrific multiplayer, which implies constant communication, has captured you completely and does not want to let go of its strong embrace, you should think about buying such a mega-device.

Certain irresponsible comrades will probably ask us a question: do you need a keyboard on the fig when PS3 has a great opportunity for both voice communication in the game and for sending voice messages? The answer is: of course, the importance of voice communication is difficult to overestimate. However, the Messenger Kit rules in two situations: first, it is necessary when voice communication is impossible or undesirable (for example, you decided to relax at the console in the evening after work, and your little child fell asleep). Secondly, ChatPad can provide a good service when you play online with foreigners, whose English sometimes has such a monstrous accent that you simply cannot do without ‚Äúsubtitles‚Äù. Firstly, ChatPads of other manufacturers in Russia are almost impossible to get, secondly, they do not have a Russian layout, and, thirdly, other devices are objectively more cumbersome. However, to the point.



The first thing the user finds when removing the keyboard from the box is the absence of any additional components. Externally, the device looks very stylish and high-quality, and so organically fits into the concept and visual style of the Sonivan gamepad, that after a couple of weeks of use, it is no longer possible to present them separately from each other. Fastened ThumbPad to the controller in the manner of fasteners, clips, which is very convenient.

The keyboard has two layouts: English and Russian, and the language of Shakespeare, for once, is in opposition to the great and mighty: Russian letters are twice as large as English letters. All the function keys that the user is used to on the computer keyboard are also present: Esc, BackSpace, CapcLock, Left and Right Shift, Enter, Space and function arrows. Some complain that the buttons on the Messenger Kit are too small, but in our opinion, typing text on it is just as convenient as, say, on a BlackBerry smartphone, and indeed on any mobile phone. A matter of habit and only.



A keyboard from a well-known world leader in the production of gaming devices would also seem like a good choice, if not for its monstrous dimensions. However, for those for whom the size does not matter, you may like the somewhat larger size of the keys, compared to the original Messenger Kit from Sony. This device also connects to the controller in the manner of a clip and works from a Bluetooth transmitter connected to the console via a USB port.

However, whether it is good or bad is up to the consumer.

Summary: objectively, the PS3 keyboard is the best one presented for the test. Both visually and functionally. Well, is this device for you a product from the category must have - you decide. But we would recommend!
