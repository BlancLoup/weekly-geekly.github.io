Greetings to Habra Residents from ServerClub



Good afternoon friends!
This post was created in order to acquaint the Habr community with our project and get feedback, what kind of materials would be interesting for you in the future.

How the project was created:


Using the Internet for about 80 years :), constantly paying bills from web hosts, it occurred to me that high-quality hosting of servers with a convenient admin panel was never seen on the web. Reliable - yes, cheap - sometimes, responsive support - it happened, but comfortable - never. Being a fan of the right interfaces, we started to talk and draw. That's what happened at the moment (click on the picture to not break the eyes):


We are constantly discussing with clients what else can be made more convenient in the admin area, I hope this process will never stop and the ServerClub team would be happy to connect the Habra community to this process.

ServerClub is an American company, business orientation - English-speaking clients around the world. But at the moment, each employee of our company is fluent in both Russian and English, so the support is carried out in both languages.

Our principles:



Only Premium bandwidth. No channel channels, no cogent. If required, we connect 10gigabit optics directly to the server.
Only branded iron. Currently, 80% of the server park is Dell. Network all on Cisco.
Because of these two points, we will never compete in price, for example, with Hetzner, where there are bad channels and desktop hardware, a completely different niche.

Maximum automation - order \ server cancellation, rebuses, OS installation, account statements, deposits, referral system, config upgrades, new ip request, etc.
Always available Instant setup - selection of a number of models and operating systems that can be accessed instantly (for many clients, the option of a credit line is open, that is, you get access to the server before you pay for it).
The lack of a wall between the authorities and customers - many customers have a personal mobile number of the most important thing, they are always allowed to call :). If you see the chat icon on the site, it means that an operator is sitting there right now and is ready to answer your questions, no bots, everything is fair.
Free administration assistance. Admins and even programmers are always happy to help in solving technical problems.
