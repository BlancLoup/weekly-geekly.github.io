Vangers have been reissued on Steam


Today, the game was released in re-released form on Steam under the auspices of KranX Productions.
The dream of a western journalist, who at the end of the 90s regretted that the game was published only in Russia and was not available to a Western player, came true.
Many do not know about its existence, but this game was one of those first Russian games, after which Western players and publishers paid attention to our game devs.
Now Kaliningrad is one of the centers of Russian game development. And this became possible not only thanks to a special economic zone, but also thanks to the example and frames of KD-Lab.
It is a great honor for me to be personally acquainted with some members of the KD-Lab team.
Congratulations to Crank and the entire KD-Lab team on this event!
And, of course, players who remember and love the Vangers!
Forward for the Scent, comrades!


The game engine was rewritten to support all current modern operating systems.
Buying a game, you can be sure that it will run wherever Steam works.
Thank you stalkerg for the work done.
