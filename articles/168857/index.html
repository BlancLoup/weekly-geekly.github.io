TW520 - watches and telephone in one person

It is unlikely that someone will argue that a cell phone is very, very much replaces the clock. I'm not talking about watches as an element of status, not about expensive, elite models. And about the usual tool that shows the time. Telephones are used so often that it is often unnecessary to carry another item with you (or rather, on yourself). Watches on the hand have become more rare than a mobile phone in your pocket, you cannot argue with this tendency.



But more recently, watch-combines, equipped with a variety of functions, began to appear: a communication module, a voice recorder, a player, a calculator, etc. In general, a whole set of ordinary cell phone. I would like to tell about this device.

To date, presented a huge number of models. When choosing, I was based on the appearance of the device and characteristics. The choice fell on the model TW520 . Watch phone cost me $ 109 with free shipping by Singapore Post.

  • Screen size: 1.54 inches
  • Type: TFT-LCD
  • Resolution: 240 x 240 px
  • Internal memory: 1MB + microSD slot with card support up to 32GB
  • Wireless: Bluetooth v3.0 with A2DP
  • Camera: Front - 1.3 MP
  • Interfaces: Microphone, Speaker, Micro USB, MicroSD, SIM
  • Java support: Yes
  • E-book: Support TXT format
  • Audio: MP3 / WAV / AMR / AWB
  • Video: 3GP / MPEG4
  • Photo: JPEG / BMP / GIF / PNG / GIF
  • Communication module: GSM 850/900/1800/1900 MHz
  • Internet: GPRS
  • Battery: 3.7V 450 mAh
  • Body Material: Aluminum. Strap - Rubber
  • Dimensions: 45.3 x 43.6 x 14.6 mm
  • Weight: 110 grams

Multimedia


Despite the fact that we are dealing with a modest-sized screen and basic functionality, there is also a multimedia component in the TW520. All applications responsible for it are in one submenu - Multimedia. Here is the button to activate the camera, camcorder and audio player.



The camera has a resolution of 1.3 megapixels. Most of all, it is suitable for creating self-portraits, everything else is given to her with great difficulty.



The music player can play music files in mp3, wav and awb formats. As for the video, there are only two supported formats, but they are very popular: they are 3gp and mp4. The device can also recognize graphic files saved in jpeg, bmp, gif and png formats.
