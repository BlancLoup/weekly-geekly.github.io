HotSpot @ Home from T-mobile USA: GSM + Wi-Fi = unlimited calls from home

The American operator T-Mobile has recently begun to provide a unique service HotSpot @ Home, thanks to which you can talk on the phone as part of the cellular network and via the Internet via Wi-Fi.

What you need and how it works:
1. An ordinary wi-fi router connected to the Internet is installed at home.
2. A phone that supports GSM and wi-fi is bought from T-mobile
3. When the home is in the radius of the router, all calls go through the Internet and are not limited in terms of payment by the tariff plan
4. As soon as the phone goes beyond the limits of home wi-fi, it automatically switches to the usual GSM connection at normal rates.

Visible advantages:
1. Savings with a significant number of calls from home.
2. The ability to get rid of home phone and call forwarding from home phone to mobile, as is often done in the United States.
3. The ability to use a mobile phone at home where there is no or poor GSM coverage.
