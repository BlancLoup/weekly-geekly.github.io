www.ratemyplacement.co.uk

www.ratemyplacement.co.uk was created by students for students and represents the first website in the UK entirely dedicated to evaluating a company as a potential employer.

The graduation year for a student, when he is faced with a choice of work, is the most problematic, but at the same time, the most exciting. The transition from student life to work is a huge step and the ratemyplacement.co.uk is designed to help prospective students make the right decision. For students who have already made such a choice, ratemyplacement.co.uk is a place where they can leave their opinions, both positive and negative, thereby really helping their colleagues make the right decision.

Mission ratemyplacement.co.uk
Become the best resource for all future / current students by building a comprehensive database of companies-employers.

Creators ratemyplacement.co.uk
Four friends who went through the agony of job search in 2005-2006. After that, they realized how even small information is important for students who are applying for a job. The creators hope that the information made available through ratemyplacement.co.uk will be useful. The resource is based on sincere and constructive reviews, and as thanks to the reviewers, the creators of the resource promise to distribute prizes.
