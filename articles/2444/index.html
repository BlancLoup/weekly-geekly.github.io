Match.com shared the secrets of attraction

The popular dating site Match.com has resorted to the help of a famous stylist Jay Manuel to share with the Internet users the secrets of creating a stylish and sexy network image, Reuters writes.

It is no secret that many people who want to find their other half or just a friend on the Internet, are often afraid to look stupid and consider their photo unsuccessful. Jay Manuel gave them some simple advice: first, you need to take a photo indoors with natural light, and second, clothing should be as dark as possible so as not to distract attention from the face. And to create the effect of "dynamic hair" you can use a regular hair dryer.
