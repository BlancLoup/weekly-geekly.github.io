IvBit Conference Report

We present to you a small report on the IvBit conference.
On October 19, 2013, the IvBit 2013 conference was held at the Textile Institute of the Voronezh State Pedagogical University in the framework of the III All-Russian Science Festival.
Despite the fact that it may be due to the fact that Ivanovo is located quite close to Moscow, we have a very poorly represented life of the IT community. Conferences, hackathons and other modest entertainment of IT-specialists are almost inaccessible to us. Conferences in universities? Unfortunately, very few people come to them, except for the students themselves and their teachers. Therefore, it was decided to organize and hold a conference devoted exclusively to IT topics. To attract attention, I will leave here the slides of the presentation of the first report:

About the conference

Now a few words about the event itself. The conference was held within the walls of the Institute of Textiles. The reason for this was the possibility of using kindly provided resources. The main task was to attract as participants not only students or university employees, but also specialists from IT companies who could share their experience. One of the main problems of the conference was the timing. Despite the small number of participants, IvBit was held in a warm, almost homely atmosphere.

PS We thank everyone who participated in the organization and work of the conference itself.
