Learn entrepreneurship with the Imagine Cup. Submission of applications until February 15, 2013

For the past 11 years, an international competition of student program projects Imagine Cup has been held. This year the competition has undergone significant changes , and student projects are expected in three categories: innovation , games and social projects . Regional competition finals will be held in more than 10 cities of Russia in March 2013, and the top 20 teams will be selected to participate in the Russian competition finals and a special educational accelerator , and the three best teams will receive funding from the Microsoft Seed Fund . For teams applying before February 15, an additional online training program will be organized in collaboration with ITMO and the Skolkovo Open University , which will include a technological and entrepreneurial component.


This year, the Imagine Cup is not only a competition of student projects, it is also an excellent educational initiative that helps to turn student ideas into concrete projects and technological start-ups. Starting from the technology schools Imagine Camp , which took place at about 20 points throughout Russia and online ( record ), ending with a unique online school on technology entrepreneurship , conducted for all students who have applied for the Imagine Cup and a special two-day accelerator for the participating teams Russian finals. In the course of the accelerator, we will try to individually pay attention to each project, help formulate and improve the business model, train participants in effective public speaking skills, give an opportunity to communicate with potential investors, coaches, other successful startups.
