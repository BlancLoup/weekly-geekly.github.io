Siclum supports User Experience Camp 2011

We at Siclum believe that the exchange of experience, knowledge and communication not only increases karma, gives inspiration, but also has a great effect on the improvement of professional skills and knowledge.

That is why we always try to support meetings, camps and other events of various professional development communities. For example, Chrome Hackathon & Contest or Alt.NET meet-up in Kiev, OpenTalk seminar in Kharkov , RubyShift in Dnepropetrovsk and many other events in cities of Ukraine, as well as in Belarus (for example, in Minsk ).

This time we decided to support the bar-camp User Experience Ukraine UX Camp 2011 in design and usability.


UX Camp is not a congress of speakers, but practical work in a team on an assigned task in a free style, without any frames or borders. According to the organizers, this time the bar-camp is intended for all who are interested in the field of usability and would like to share their experience or learn something new in this area. You can learn more about the format of the event at this link . During the camp, participants will work in a team on projects with the participation of moderators, clients of representatives and usability experts.

Date : Saturday, December 17
Place : Dnepropetrovsk, cozy cafe (details will be sent to registered participants)
For whom it is : both experienced UX specialists and beginners usually take part in UX Camp, as well as designers, project managers and programmers. Essentially, all those for whom convenient interfaces and user joy are important.

Attention! Participation in the event is paid. Details on the website of the organizers
