Watch Shock Clock: the discharge of electricity and fear - the key to success awakening



How hard is it for you to get up in the morning? For many people - very hard. Even if an important meeting is scheduled in the morning, you need to pass an exam or a quarterly report - not everyone can get up on time. Previously, for a quick wake-up, the usual mechanical alarm clock was set in an iron bowl with small change. In the morning there was such a roar that the neighbors in the nearby house sometimes woke up.

Now there are not so many mechanical alarms, but you still need to get up. And in order not to oversleep, you can use a really effective method - Shock Clock. This watch can start awakening with a vibration, a sound signal, and end with a shock or even three consecutive beats. This is no longer overslept.

The development of this watch is engaged in the company Pavlok. Interestingly, these watches are smart - for them there is an application for a mobile phone, so the settings in this case are simple.

Now the project is raising money with the help of crowdfunding, and if more than $ 50,000 is collected, the user will be able to use rather complicated configuration schemes. For example, a clock will be electrocuted every time a person visits an entertainment site while working, losing valuable time. Or you can customize your own IFTTT script.



Shock Clock is not just a concept. Some users, a small number of testers, were able to test the device in operation. And after a couple of days, such users jumped out of bed 10 minutes before the wake-up signal. Of course, who wants to get a shock, or even all three. Application settings allow you to block the alarm, so that it will beat the current and signal until the person gets up (determined with the help of a gyroscope).

This is a really working device, the effectiveness of which is higher than any other alarm clocks. Of course, the decision is extreme, but what if there is simply no other way out?
