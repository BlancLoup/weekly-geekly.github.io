3gCamp - barcamp mobile technology. July 24, St. Petersburg


If you are not yet fused in the summer heat and are ready for action, then this information is for you! :)
July 24 in St. Petersburg with the support of Beeline will be held 3gCamp - a conference in the format of barCamp. The event will bring together people associated with mobile technologies, ready to listen, discuss and tell useful information on the topic, gather enthusiasts, interested and professionals from St. Petersburg.

In the program: active communication and interaction, a lot of useful and interesting information, experts from Beeline and the opportunity to ask them questions, coffee breaks, prizes. It may be: flying UFOs to the Moon (without a return ticket), proof of Fermat's theorem using only one mobile, hilling melons in the garden near the venue :)
If you know what a mobile phone is, mobile Internet, know how unusual it is to apply, know tips & tricks, preach mobility in life, etc., then you are welcome!
Registration: 3gCamp.org

We remind you that the barcamp involves the preparation of reports on the topic by individual participants, so if you have something to tell, please write about it in the registration form, speakers are very necessary!
Attention, the number of places is strictly limited, do not delay the registration!
