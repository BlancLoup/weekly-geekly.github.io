What to do when you find yourself in a niche


The degree of difficulty in promoting a niche product directly depends on the state of its niche in the market:

  • a well-established niche is open, known, the main target audience is gathered in it, its coverage tools are known and work. Usually occupied by a more or less constant set of competing manufacturers;
  • the young niche is open, limited known, the target audience is known, but not structured, its coverage tools are known. The set of products is dynamic; Constantly new manufacturers come in with competing products;
  • a fresh niche is open, unknown or little known, the main target audience has not been studied, its tools do not work yet or are not created. It may be occupied by a single manufacturer whose product / invention discovered / created this niche - or a pair of quick followers / authors of parallel inventions / discoveries.
