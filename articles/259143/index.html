Yukon: 1st Annual Software Developer Conference

On June 20, 2015, for the first time in Saratov, a large-scale IT conference "Yukon" will be held. Representatives of leading companies in the software development industry, such as Microsoft, Zeptolab, JetBrains, Tinkoff, will gather at one site.



This is a completely new format of IT events for our city: 800 visitors and 20 speakers in one day!

The event will be organized 4 parallel tracks for reports on various topics: the business section and 3 technical sections.

On the business track you will hear about how to start your startup, success stories, as well as stories of failures.
Technical tracks are technologies, technologies and again technologies: DevOps, Java hardcore development, gamedev, mobile development, testing, functional programming, microservices and much more - everything that the software development industry now breathes.

For those who have a desire to speak at the conference, barcampes will be organized, so anyone with an interesting topic will find his audience. But that's not all.

On the site will be organized hackzone, where you can watch the battles of robots, participate in master classes and learn something interesting from the world of internet of things.

And of course, for an informal discussion of the reports you will be waiting for the afterparty in one of the cozy bars of the city.

Participation in the conference is free, registration on the site youcon.ru is already open!

See you at the conference "Yukon"!
