Varmanet and Renaissance Insurance entered into a partnership



It is finished! In the new version of Varmante 2.6 for iOS, all car owners can issue Casco insurance policies. The process of registration takes less than a minute, you get a preliminary cost of the policy and then a specialist contacts you to discuss the delivery of your new insurance. No queues and wasted time. This was made possible thanks to our partnership with Renaissance Insurance.


All this time we have been developing Varmane as a convenient and secure repository of documents. But we plan to make a big breakthrough and significantly increase the value of Varmane for our users, working not only on improving document storage, but also connecting the services behind each individual document. Indeed, in the era of rapid development of technology, documents, in the form in which we are accustomed to see them, still remain a relic of the past. A person should not bother with whether his passport, visa, insurance has expired, as well as keep track of the documents of his entire family. It is easy to lose sight of all these little things, and the consequences can be sad.



Service VKarmane learned to track the duration of documents and notify in advance of this, so that the user can take timely measures to replace. But now you do not have to get up from a soft couch to extend or arrange a new auto insurance. Thanks to the partnership with Renaissance Insurance, you can buy a new Casco policy as easy as installing a new application from the App Store.



The Casco policy was launched in test mode and is currently available to residents of Moscow, St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg.

In the near future, we will expand the possibilities of our partnership with Renaissance insurance and add to the application in the Varmante application services for the registration of CTP insurance, life insurance, travel insurance and others.

Technologies bring convenience to our lives, save our time and energy, but the changes do not concern the sphere of personal documents. With the help of Varmarme, we plan to improve the way people work with important documents. The future begins here and now.

Varmarme - all documents are always at hand!
