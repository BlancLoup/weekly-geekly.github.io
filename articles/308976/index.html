A gift for September 1 to all fans of digital circuitry and computer architecture



Lord Today, on September 1, the Day of Knowledge, a group of Ukrainian university professors, their graduate students, as well as Russian engineers, decided, in coordination with the British publishers Elsevier through Imagination Technologies, to make a small gift to all teachers of computer architecture and digital circuitry, especially who use the popular textbook by David Harris and Sarah Harris .

Just click here on this link and you will receive a zip-file with slides for lecturing on this textbook in Russian. And without registration, which is required for downloading a textbook as such .

Slides can be useful not only for teachers, but also for anyone interested who wants to learn how to build a microprocessor with their own hands, but does not want to make its way through the 1600 tablet pages of the book.
