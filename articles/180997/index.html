The collapse of anonymous payments. At rest goes LibertyReserve. Or not



The second day screams on the forums and backstage whispers do not subside - whether the patient is alive or rather is dead. LibertyReserve is the largest anonymous electronic payment system. Founded by a native of Ukraine, Artur Budovsky-Belanchuk, over the past five years, the system has achieved leading positions in all gray markets and began to be seriously quoted in the west, replacing the rest of e-Gold, eBullion and others. Long before BitCoin, Liberty became a thorn in the US Treasury, as served as an ideal means of hiding capital from taxes and contributed to the turnover of capital in transactions that violate US laws.

The turnover of LR over the past five years is calculated in billions of USD, the current balance of accounts is estimated at 70-150M.
