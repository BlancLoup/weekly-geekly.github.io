Presentation of new phones at the exhibition MWC-2013

Good afternoon, Habr!
Yesterday at Mobile World Congress 2013, we presented four new phones.
These are two new Lumia smartphones based on OS Windows Phone 8 and two phones - Nokia 105 and Nokia 301.
Let's look at them in more detail.








Nokia 301




Nokia 301 is similar to the Lumia series smartphones, while it is equipped with a 12-button keyboard, a 3.2 megapixel camera, Bluetooth, a 3.5 mm audio jack, and a micro USB connector for synchronizing with a computer. In addition, Nokia 301 supports 3G networking.
The phone has a slot for memory expansion with microSD format cards with a maximum capacity of up to 32 GB.

Specifications Nokia 301


Nokia 105




The Nokia 105 is the most inexpensive phone, the successor to the Nokia 1280 phone.
It is equipped with a color screen, built-in radio, flashlight and pre-installed games. And, thanks to its dust-proof keyboard, it is perfect both for children who do not always cherish such devices due to age, and adults who prefer not to part with communication in various extreme conditions, in the country, in the woods, while playing sports, and .d


Nokia 105 Photos





Specifications Nokia 105


Nokia Lumia 520




Nokia Lumia 520 is the most affordable of the Lumia series of phones, while the device has impressive characteristics. It is based on the Snapdragon S4 dual-core processor with a clock frequency of 1 GHz, supports third-generation networks, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 3.0 and is equipped with an ultra-sensitive screen, like its older counterparts. In addition, it is a very bright and youth unit with a removable body. The device supports microSD cards with a maximum capacity of up to 64 GB.


Lumia 520 photo














Specifications Nokia Lumia 520


Nokia Lumia 720




Nokia Lumia 720 - mid-range smartphone in a unibody package, equipped with a 6.7-megapixel camera with Carl Zeiss optics, supports third-generation networks. The smartphone has a super-sensitive ISP ClearBlack display with a WVGA (800x480) resolution. In the line of new Lumia smartphones, it stands between the 620th and 820th models. The smartphone supports wireless charging with an additional panel. Support for memory cards is implemented using a slot similar to the slot for a microsim card. Another highlight of this model is a widescreen front camera with a 1.3 megapixel sensor.


Lumia 720 Photo














Specifications Nokia Lumia 720


Accessories




A good addition to the Lumia series smartphones will be the accessories, also presented at the exhibition. Nokia Lumia 720 will be able to wirelessly charge with additional charging panels of white and black colors. If the phone itself has a glossy case, then the panels will be dull plastic-coated soft-touch.











Also on sale will go smart wireless charging cradle CR-200, which has long been waiting for motorists. The device has an NFC chip that allows you to run a predefined application when placing a smartphone in the cradle. In addition, the CR-200 will serve as a convenient holder for your smartphone.











Separately, it is necessary to talk about the new software part.


First, the navigation products have undergone rebranding and are now called HERE Maps, HERE Drive, HERE Transit and will be available to all users of devices based on Windows Phone 8.


Secondly, camera additions, so-called lenses, have been developed. For example, users will have access to the new Place Tag extension, which will allow them to place their location tags on their photos, thereby turning them into memorable postcards about places and sights visited.
