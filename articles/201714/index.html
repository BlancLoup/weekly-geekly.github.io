IT service performance audit. New opportunity for service companies



Have you ever deployed a professional commercial monitoring system or an APM system (application performance management)? If yes, then you know that vendors of such systems always or almost always categorically prohibit the use of their products for the provision of professional services (so as not to reduce sales). This is a principled position, and it is clearly prescribed in the license agreement.

Therefore, the new Network Instruments partner program - APM-Guru, Russia - can be called unprecedented. One of the world's leading manufacturers of APM systems offers Russian service IT companies to become authorized partners and not only provide professional services using the flagship product Network Instruments Observer Suite , but also receive a one-year license to Observer Suite for free . In addition, the partner receives a number of other important preferences.

Due to historical features, the IT services performance audit market in Russia is less developed than in Europe and the USA. Open source, semi-professional, or cracked software products are commonly used as tools. In most cases, they are unable to provide complete information about the operation of business applications in the company's corporate network. As a result, services are provided at a relatively low professional level, and therefore the demand for such services is also relatively small.
