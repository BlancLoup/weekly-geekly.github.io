How to spend a vacation IT professionals - infographics poll

July is on the calendar. Summer has firmly entered its rights, it is the height of the holidays. We decided to find out how IT specialists spend their time on vacation, and conducted a survey among users of Habr and My Circle.

A total of 3342 respondents answered questions.

It turned out that many people continue to program and improve their skills even during their holidays. For the most part, computer-aided professionals love to travel around Russia, mostly with a family or a couple. Many are willing to spend the last money on vacation and go on a journey at any time, not bothering to prepare and accumulate the necessary funds.

For details and answers to other burning questions - welcome under cat.






What is your specialty and experience?


The distribution of respondents by specialization roughly corresponds to the similar distribution of applicants on ‚ÄúMy Circle‚Äù: most of all we have programmers and layout designers. Most, however, are not new to their field and work for more than 3 years.

I wonder if there are any differences in the rest of specialists from different directions? Let's try to find out in the course of our humble research.







How many hours a week do you work?


The majority (67%) work strictly in accordance with the labor code. However, a quarter prefers to work overtime as well (18%), and even overtime (6%). Surely busy with their startups?





How many times a year do you need to rest, and how long does it take to rest after six months of active work?


Half of IT professionals are unanimous and quite conservative in matters of alternation of work and rest: they prefer to rest twice a year, for 2 weeks. However, it is curious that there is a significant group (more than 40%) who prefer to rest more often.







Are you relaxing alone or as a family / couple?



Most IT professionals rest with a couple or with a family.





What type of activity do you prefer on vacation?


As it turned out, the favorite type of activity depends little on the specialization of the IT specialist. Half is considered the best vacation travel. And for a third it will be communication with nature. But there are small nuances.



Inclined to travel during the holidays a little more among managers, designers and mobile application developers (60% of specialists of these profiles prefer this type of recreation).

Programmers and administrators like to rest in nature a little more than others (33-34% of them prefer this type of vacation). And least of all, nature lovers among designers and developers of mobile applications (23-24% prefer).

Testers are a bit more inclined to the extreme type of rest (17%), and marketers, managers and designers are less inclined (7-8%).

Almost half of IT professionals for recreation will choose Russia and its near-abroad countries. Another third prefer to rest in Europe.
