Participate in the Russian application week - Russian AppFest


From 10 to 17 November 2015, an action for mobile users is held - Russian AppFest Russian App Week. Partners of the action prepared a lot of surprises and announcements. Visit the Russian AppFest website and receive gifts from key companies in the Russian market! Only from 10 to 17 November you are waiting for discounts on movies and books, promotional codes for paid apps, bonuses in games and much more!

The MBLT} DEV Mobile Developers Conference will complete the Russian Application Week, which has been gathering top mobile market experts for the third year in a row. This year the conference program is divided into 2 tracks: for mobile developers and for managers of IT-companies. Guests of the event will hear reports from the best mobile developers, will attend seminars on cross-platform development, virtual reality and security of mobile devices. Participants will be able to exchange views with like-minded people and communicate in an informal setting with the speakers of the conference during the After-party.

Join the live webcast of the Mobile Developers Conference!

If you are interested in mobile applications, services and games, and you want to be the first to receive bonuses and special offers, as well as learn the news of the industry, go to the Russian AppFest site during the promotion!
