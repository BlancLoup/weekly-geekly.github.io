CardInfo - API for determining the logo, bank colors and other things by card number



Here is the demo and documentation: cardinfo.online This is the API. You give him the first 6 digits of a bank card, it gives you a link to the bank‚Äôs logo, its corporate colors, brand (Visa, MasterCard, etc.) and so on in JSON format. CardInfo is needed for those who create a form for accepting bank cards on their website in order to improve the UX (convenience, experience, sense of security) of users. Accepting payments directly on your site allows cloudpayments.ru in Russia and the CIS countries, and stripe.com throughout the rest of the world.

All major companies have made such forms. You can see a live example when paying for anything via Yandex.Cass or practically in any mobile application of the bank: when you start entering the card number, the form is repainted in the colors of this bank, and the logo appears next to it. Each of these large companies has implemented its own solution for determining the logo and colors. If this solution was implemented by all large companies, then there is value in it. If the large ones have already done, but the small ones have not yet, then it looks like an uptrend, which is reasonable to ride.

However, large companies have long made their forms so, but small ones still do not. Why? Creating such a solution for your form will take about 7 days of work by the programmer and 7 more works of the designer (pump out, process and redraw logos). Large companies can afford to spend on creating such a form. Small companies believe the value of this form is not sufficient to pay so much for it.

CardInfo will allow you to turn an already existing form for accepting bank cards into the same one as Yandex.Cash, for a half-day programmer. I suppose that after the release of CardInfo, the definition of the bank logo and bank colors for payment forms will become the standard. Because such forms are better. Because now such forms will be able to afford everything.

And by the way, notice that all the coolest accept payments right on your site, and not sent to external gaskets. For example Dodo Pizza dodopizza.ru

In general, accepting cards right on your site is cool, but it's even cooler to accept cards on your site through forms made with CardInfo.
