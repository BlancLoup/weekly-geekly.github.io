Technological beer opener

The German company TrekStor has released a useful "two-in-one" device. This is a USB flash drive, it is also a bottle opener. A flash drive is always with you at almost everyone. And that means open beer will not be a problem either.


The topic itself is certainly not new. But the device from TrekStor, firstly, looks quite decent (solid aluminum case, everything is done) and, secondly, these devices will be produced with onboard memory from 1 GB to 16 GB.

The cost of a gigabyte opener is only $ 10. A 16-gigabyte device will cost more - $ 71. The TrekStor flash drive will be available in October.
