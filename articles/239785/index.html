SQA Days-16. Eight reasons to participate


Hello!
We are glad to inform you that the SQA Days-16 conference program has been formed.
If you are not with us yet, we will give you 8 reasons to attend the conference:

  • Each report included in the program, passed a big competition and is worthy of attention.
  • Each report is rehearsed with the curator via video link.
  • The conference program is balanced and contains topics that will be of interest to all participants without exception. Also indicated are the levels of difficulty of the reports.
  • During the conference, you can rate the report online and provide feedback to the speaker.
  • During the conference, you can make appointments and chat with other participants.
  • On the evening of November 14th, there will be a QA party at which we will have some fun, take a break from the reports and, of course, we will also have additional conversations. Also you are waiting for contests and prizes.
  • At the end of the conference, we will draw prizes between speakers and participants.
  • In addition to a rich baggage of knowledge, you will take with you a charge of cheerfulness and positive!


We are waiting for you on SQA Days!


Recall the conference will be held November 14-15, 2014 in St. Petersburg in the hotel complex Park Inn by Radisson Pribaltiyskaya . Hurry, the places are running out!
