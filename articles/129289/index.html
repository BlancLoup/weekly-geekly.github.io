Android client for myshows.ru

Good day to all!
Surely many of you have heard about the site myshows.ru - the perfect site for fans of TV shows and fans of various TV shows. Resource is a handy tool that allows you to accompany the process of watching TV shows.

The authors of the resource, my very good friends, asked to write a client for the site under the Android platform, to provide even more convenient access for users. Actually, the application turned out, here is its main functionality:

  • Make your list of TV shows, put a rating;
  • Mark scanned series;
  • Learn about the release date for the next new episodes.
  • Find out what you have left to see;
  • View time spent statistics;
  • View friend's profile;
