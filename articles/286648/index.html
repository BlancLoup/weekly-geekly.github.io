In 2014, the Russian IT services market decreased by 15%

According to CNews, over the past year, the IT services market in Russia declined in dollar terms by 15%, to $ 6.57 billion. 2015 will open up new opportunities, but even in the rapidly developing IT sphere, companies will have a hard time.

However, in the ruble equivalent, the market grew by 2.2%. According to IDC, this is a consequence of the fall of the ruble. IDC analysts predicted a 4% market growth in 2014 dollar terms. But the forecast was made in August 2014, and then the further development of the adverse geopolitical situation in the world was not taken into account.

Nevertheless, as shown by the research of AMT Consult, 84% of Russian IT companies intend to raise the salaries of their employees. And only 8% plan to lower wages. In addition, research shows that IT companies are actively developing their business. In 2014, they increased the number of employees by 15%.

In the IT services market in Russia, the most common system integration services, which occupy 42% of the market. Outsourcing services reached a share of 18%. And the structure of expenditures on IT services allows us to conclude that in 2014 the leaders were financial enterprises, government institutions were second, and then telecommunications and the media.

IDC is confident that in 2015 most Russian IT suppliers will have a hard time. However, new trends in procurement and IT consumption will bring new opportunities that can be profitably used.

Compared with the crisis of 2009, when the Russian IT services market, according to IDC, fell by 31.2% in dollar terms, the current situation looks more optimistic.

However, there is another difference: in Russia, since 2014, the IT industry has taken steps to import substitution, which, if successful, will make the Russian IT market less dependent on foreign influence.
