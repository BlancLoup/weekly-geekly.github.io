Jelastic is now in Russia

You probably already know that Jelastic is a transnational company. We try to be closer to our customers, we cooperate with hosters from different countries and continents. Our service is available in the USA and in Europe.
It is no secret that Russia is a strategically important partner for us, since the founders of our company are Russians. Now popular Java hosting worldwide will be available in Russia from the hosting provider Rusonix .

We also made the interface of our site even more convenient, simple and understandable for you. Starting today, our localized version in Russian is available. We are waiting for your comments and suggestions on this. Feel free to let us know what you like, what not, what I would like to add, etc. Just visit our website jelastic.com/ru and share your impressions in the comments to this post. We will take into account all the wishes. Feel yourself a part of our team! Thank in advance.
