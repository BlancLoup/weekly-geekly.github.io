In the "Personal Account QIWI" 900 providers

Payment system "Personal Cabinet QIWI", which was launched in December 2007, continues to evolve.

Only during May 2009, QIWI Personal Cabinet users made payments towards 902 unique providers ! In total, users can pay for goods and services of more than 1,000 partners of the payment system.

QIWI Personal Cabinet is actively developing cooperation with online stores and software manufacturers. Only in May, payments were made towards 211 online stores , and the list of software was expanded by adding navigation systems, new anti-virus programs and other useful and popular software.

Cooperation with online games is expanding. Users of the "Personal Cabinet QIWI" got the opportunity to purchase time cards for such popular games as World of Warcraft, EVE-online, Warhammer and Lord of the Rings. In total, users can directly pay for more than 40 of the most popular online games .

In June, the active connection of partners continues. Only in the first 15 days of June, applications for connecting to the payment system of 20 new online stores were approved and more than 30 providers of various services were added.
