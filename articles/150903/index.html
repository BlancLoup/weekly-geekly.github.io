Chrome browser 4 years

Sundar Pichay, Senior Vice President, Chrome & Apps

4 years ago, when we launched the Chrome browser, most people used personal computers to access the Internet. Our goal was to make work on the world wide web even faster, easier and safer.

But it was then, what is happening now? Today, many users have several devices to access the Internet ‚Äî a smartphone, a tablet, one computer at work, and another at home.

The network is a single platform in which you can work in exactly the same way, no matter what device you use. And all this - thanks to the browser. We remembered this when we were working on improvements to Chrome: it‚Äôs very convenient to have everything you need on any of your devices.

Everyone has their own Internet, everyone has their own bookmarks, tabs, journals, passwords and more. All this, one way or another, reflects our life on the web. Today, Chrome allows you to access the Internet from any device and anywhere. It doesn't matter if you are using a computer on Windows , Mac OS , or Linux , a smartphone or tablet on Android or iOS - synchronization with your Chrome browser will allow you to use the same settings on all devices. Just log in to your account !



Since Chrome can now be used on more devices, we pay special attention to the safety of users on the network. For 4 years we have done a lot in this direction. For example, recent improvements, such as a reliable sandbox for running plug-ins and secure download , have made networking even more secure.
