Magazine "The Economist" called Yota breakthrough

Despite the fact that developed countries are still introducing third-generation (3G) wireless Internet access technology, in some countries they have already moved to the fourth (4G). Yota did not abandon the previously adopted technology in favor of the new, which indicates the company's foresight and sober calculation. Yota plans to expand business in developing countries.
