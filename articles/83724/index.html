Trojan Spy Eye eliminates competitors

The Internet is "conquering" the new Trojan called Spy Eye, whose ears, according to experts from Symantec, stick out from Russia. Actually, the salt is not in the appearance of the Trojan, but in the fact that its new modification has an interesting functionality. Spy Eye, due to the Kill Zeus feature, can remove the components of the competing Zeus system (also known as Zbot) from an infected computer.

Moreover, the Trojan can also intercept data sent by Zeus to the command and control servers of the botnet of this system. According to experts, it is precisely due to the new functionality that Spy Eye can quickly create its own botnet.
