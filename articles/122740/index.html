Baldur's Gate on Android: GemRB emulator in action

Hamsters and rangers everywhere, rejoice !!!

Optional personal preface

At the turn of the century, all progressive mankind with might and main cut through the Sword Coast: from Candlekeep to the very Gate of Baldur. And I - I played the right midfielder in the yard team and my percentage was 386, and the monitor was black and white. He joined the art of role-playing games only after 9 years. And the legendary Baldur's Gate did not obey me. Mouse can not turn the camera - uncomfortable! ..

So I would have lived, ashamed of my pops, if it were not good people - the creators of GemRB , a free open source implementation of Bioware Infinity Engine.

Materiel

Through the efforts of the St. Petersburg developer Dmitry Matveyev aka Beholder GemRB is ported to Android. Allows you to run games in the Baldur's Gate series, as well as - with some limitations - Icewind Dale and Planescape: Torment . Requires:
  • Android> = 1.6;
  • VGA, WVGA, HVGA or higher resolution;
  • 150 MB RAM;
  • availability of resources of the original game and space for them on a smartphone / tablet.


Installation

  2. We get the game itself (on Rutreker there is an excellent assembly of the Baldur Gate Trilogy - graphics from BG2 + continuous plot (!)
