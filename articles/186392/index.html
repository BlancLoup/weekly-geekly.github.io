Kim Dotcom (Mega, Megaupload) is going to launch a messenger and email protected from wiretapping



In general, you do not need to be Vanga to guess that after the disclosure of the methods of "wiretapping" used by special services of different countries, the active development of protected instant messengers will follow. Probably, about a dozen of such services are being developed right now, some of which we have already heard, and about the other part - to be heard. For example, a protected messenger from Peter Sunde, co-founder of The Pirate Bay, has already collected about 100 thousand dollars needed for development. In addition, the search service DuckDuckGo, where it is impossible to track user search queries, began to gain momentum recently. Well, Kim Dotcom, the founder of Mega, also announced that the protected messenger will be launched soon, as well as, later, email.
