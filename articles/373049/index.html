BT and MediaTek Announce Collaboration on Wireless Home Solutions


On January 4, 2017 at CES 2017 (Las Vegas, USA), MediaTek representatives announced that BT had chosen MediaTek's adaptive network solution as the basis for developing Wi-Fi for the whole house, which will provide wireless connectivity with full coating. To install the network does not require special knowledge, and the quality of communication guarantees a stable signal throughout the house.

‚ÄúUsers want wireless Internet to be simple and fast. With our investment in smart solutions that work with Wi-Fi, we will even exceed those expectations, ‚Äùsaid Erik Raphael, director of BT Devices. - By providing our users with home Wi-Fi, we try to make them get the best connection. Our technology "Wi-Fi for the whole house" guarantees fast and uninterrupted Internet for all devices throughout the house. "Wi-Fi for the whole house," based on MediaTek's adaptive network solution, allows users to set up a smart and secure network created for the modern Internet world. "

The adaptive network solution, which became the basis of Wi-Fi for the whole house technology, provides a high-quality wireless Internet connection with full coverage and the latest advances in security and smart connectivity. Users will be provided with a simple network installation and small repeaters that automatically conduct all traffic, providing fast and uninterrupted connection throughout the house. Advantages of MediaTek adaptive network:

  • fast and secure connection for full Wi-Fi coverage;
  • automatic configuration and grouping of repeaters;
  • reliable communication and fast roaming between them;
  • intelligent monitoring of traffic and guaranteed quality of service.

‚ÄúThe situation in the Internet world is changing rapidly, and people's demands for home wireless networks are changing,‚Äù said Joe Chen, executive vice president and chief operating officer of MediaTek. ‚ÄúThere are more and more devices in our homes, so we need to look for smarter wireless connectivity solutions around the house. MediaTek's adaptive network technology is specifically designed for this purpose. ‚Äù

The adaptive network is built on three MediaTek MT7621 + MT7615 + MT7615 chipsets and is a very reliable solution, supporting the most advanced technologies that exist today. Being currently the leader in the production of chipsets for Internet solutions for home use, smart devices and cars, MediaTek will try to continue to remain the center of our ever-growing Internet world.
