New European exchanges in Google Finance

Caroline Netolichka, product manager

Today we are just to inform you that we have added to Google Finance information about trading on the Scandinavian and Baltic divisions of the Nasdaq exchange, as well as on the Russian stock exchange MICEX. Thus, our service significantly expands the European coverage, making available data from players such as Gazprom , H & M , Nokia , Carlsberg and many others.

It is important that all current indicators of users in real time. Adding new stock exchanges is another step towards ensuring that as many market reports as possible from around the world come to you immediately.

A complete list of Google Finance stock exchanges is available here: http://www.google.com/googlefinance/disclaimer .
