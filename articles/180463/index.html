Week free courses on Creative Live



From June 3 to June 8, Creative Live launches a week of beginner courses in the following disciplines:
  • After Effects June 8
  • Photoshop June 7
  • Illustrator June 6
  • Dreamweaver June 5
  • InDesign June 4
  • Graphic Design Essentials June 3


Courses will be held in English, in real time, both with a live audience in the studio, and online from 9 am to 4 pm ( America / Los Angeles ).
Courses are free for viewing in real time , but recording the entire course will cost $ 199. or individually $ 59 each.


Event page

You are enrolled to watch the free workshop live
