The reasons for the total failure of Nokia Lumia and Windows Phone in particular

The following is a translation of the message from Slashdot with my-symbian.com (the original was found). Although the source text is not a complete article, it is worth it. I was in a bit of a hurry, so I will be glad of the corrections sent via private messages.

WP7.5 (Mango) is the second generation of this OS, but still has many limitations, prohibitions and serious problems with usability. This is a list of drawbacks compared to other mobile operating systems on the market. We do not compare special features or abilities, but only those that have become the norm for all smartphones, even for the simplest dialers. Android, Symbian or MeeGo have only a few of the listed drawbacks. The ability of the iPhone to transfer files also has limitations, but they still went far ahead of WP7.5. Even the Nokia N9 with the first generation of MeeGo OS is much better and does not have all these catastrophic problems that prevent the smartphone from being used normally.

6. There is no file manager. The file system is completely opaque.

7. Zune needed to transfer files. Zune can only transfer photos, videos and music. For regular files, you will have to use the Internet.

8. Your contact information is automatically transferred to the cloud, whether you like it or not.

9. Maximum screen resolution - 800x480.
