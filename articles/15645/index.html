Music from your iPhone in any iTunes!



Firefly Media Server is ported to iPhone. By installing it on your iPhone, you can listen to music from it in iTunes through sharing!
And in any iTunes: at home, at work, with friends.

For this you need
1) In Installer.app go to Sources-> Edit-> Add , enter the address home.mike.tl/iphone
2) In the list of applications, find and install FireflyMediaServer (in Multimedia or in the general list)
3) After installation, the program will index your library for 5-10 minutes

Everything! Now, opening iTunes, you will see your phone in the shared library section.
If not, enable the search for shared libraries in the ACCESS tab in the settings.

Already tested and works great!
Source: TUAW .


A password is put on the library - dottie is the default.
To change it, you need to go to Safari on iPhone at root : dottie@127.0.0.1: 3689 and change (or remove it altogether) in the Configuration item.
For those who like to delve into the settings - there are enough of them.

If you are listening via wi-fi:
1) If you are behind a firewall, you need to open port 3689
2) The library is only available when the iPhone is active, since wi-fi is turned off in the standby mode.
