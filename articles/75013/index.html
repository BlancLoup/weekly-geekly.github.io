Nokia starts selling N900



Maybe I was late with the news, then you can throw a stick at me. However, it became known that the company Nokia has released on the shelves of the new flagship line - N900. This phone has already been written many times on Habr√©, the most detailed overview of the characteristics was from the Budda40 habraouser here .

Let me just say that the Nokia N900 is based on the Linux-based operating system Maemo 5 (to which you can download the SDK for a long time). It has a total of 1 gigabyte of memory for applications (256 - RAM, the rest is swap), 32 gigabytes of storage, expandable up to 48 using microSD.

And most importantly: The prices will, as always, depend on whether you enter into a contract with a specific telecom operator, or you want to buy a device that is independent of the SIM card. The latter, as is known, is more expensive. For the N900, this bar is set at 500-550 euros. Expensive :)
