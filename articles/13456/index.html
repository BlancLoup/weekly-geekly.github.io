Yandex.Direct takes into account the interests of users

Internet, August 23, 2007. Yandex.Direct has begun to openly test the display of advertising, taking into account the interests of Yandex visitors. The new technology will allow users to see predominantly interesting promotional offers, and advertisers even more targeted to target audiences.

User preferences are determined by their behavior on Yandex and member sites of the Yandex Advertising Network. Based on the analysis of this behavior, advertising is broadcast on partner sites. The display of advertising, taking into account interests, essentially depends on the user himself: his preferences change - the advertising offer changes as well.
