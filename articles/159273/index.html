Russia wants to transfer to the UN the functions of ICANN and not only

The main issue at WCIT ( World Conference on International Telecommunications ), which will be held in Dubai this December, will be the discussion and revision of the principles of ITR ( International Telecommunications Regulations ). Adopted in 1988, before the wide spread of the Internet, these types of rules today do not correspond to existing realities.

The conference is expected to be attended by representatives of leading operators of the telecommunications market and governments of various countries. Although the proposals of the participants have not yet been officially announced, there are a lot of reports in the press about the intentions of the Russian side to transfer the authority to manage the domain system from ICANN to the UN.
