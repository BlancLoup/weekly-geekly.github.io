Collections of the best email marketing emails

Want your letters to be at the level of the best samples from the largest brands? No question - the experience of hundreds and thousands of advertising email campaigns is available on the Internet, and all you need is to study it carefully and think about how it can be applied in your business. In this post, we have collected several Russian and foreign sites that offer collections of examples of the most interesting letters, sorted by various topics. And as a bonus - several services that allow you to create letters based on ready-made templates.

Email samples


BEN - Beautiful Email Newsletters : a large collection of beautifully designed commercial letters.



Spam Meltdown : hundreds of screenshots of various emails, segmented by colors and design elements / techniques, as well as by subject and business areas.



Email Design Gallery : a collection of attractive emails that have been going on since 2004. All sample letters are conveniently grouped according to their structure and purpose / type.



Retail Email Blog : review emails from leading online stores. Each letter is accompanied by a small comment explaining what the pros and cons (if any) of this letter.



Email Gallery : a great site where you can get inspiration and ideas for your own letters.



Inbox Award : here are collected interesting samples of email from around the world (the site has not been updated since 2009).



Email Design Review : a blog that contains the best samples of emails. In the comments to each letter it is indicated that it stands out among others, than it is remarkable and why it works.



Beautiful Emails : here you can find letters with an unusual design, the main difference of which is successfully selected memorable images.



Email Institute : an impressive collection of emails from around the world. The main difference of the site is a convenient search, thanks to which you can quickly and easily find samples of letters of the desired type specifically for your business.



EmailSociety : emails from leading brands that are selected in real time.



EmailHub.ru is one of the best projects on this topic in the review; concise and convenient viewing of letters sorted by company, business segment and category:



Email Templates


A number of resources provides the ability to use ready-made templates for writing letters:

Campaign Monitor : 30 free templates + the ability to make your own unique design in just a few minutes using the template builder.



CakeMail : An interesting collection of templates - from the simplest to the unusual and original.



And of course, UniSender : a constantly growing collection of free templates for clients, available when creating a letter in your personal account:
