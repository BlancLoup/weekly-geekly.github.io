March 29, Moscow - Backend Stories 3.0

Next week, apart from mitap for JavaScript lovers, we will conduct a mitap for backend developers.

The place is the same, Corporate Innovations Hub, there will again be 3 speakers, burgers and prizes for the most interesting questions. As usual, the mitap is free + on the day of the event we will post the broadcast.

Let's talk about Selenium WebDriver and its benefits, touch upon ElasticSearch, and, of course, discuss microservice architecture.

The main thing - register in advance.

Selenium WebDriver in the service of a developer // Dmitry Kostichev


There is: the task of integration on a third-party Internet resource.
No: there is no time to sort it out.
Solution: Selenium.

Dmitry will tell you how to automate the work in the browser, while not leaving your service, using the example of your own project.

ElasticSearch potion 101 // Nikita Guchakov


Dragged Elastic into production, but is it still slow? Afraid to make an extra request, because the cluster falls for a few hours? Not sure how to decompose the data? Are your disks full?

Nikita will make a useful checklist for beginners, and also tell you how to reduce the mortality of your elastic.

We draw architecture from scratch // Alexander Kovalev


Using the example of his startup Mango Techsurance, Sasha will tell you how and why the current stack was chosen for the project, and also shows typical queries when building a microservice architecture in modern conditions.
