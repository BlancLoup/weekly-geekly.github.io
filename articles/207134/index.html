Who, when and how is going to fly to Mars

Now they say so often about flights to Mars that people sometimes confuse several independent projects. Sometimes you hear that Mars One is a NASA project, and Inspiration Mars is going to mine the gold that Curiosity has found. All this, of course, fiction. Therefore, I will try to decompose all Martian dreams on the shelves, so that there is a universal cheat sheet for all occasions.
I will make a reservation in advance: this review reflects the current situation. I do not exclude future evolution and possible mergers of the projects being described or the emergence of new ones.



So let's start in chronological order:

Mars direct


Cost : $ 30 billion.
Idea : Sending an expedition to Mars in two stages: first, an unmanned rocket flies, which is to return people from Mars. It has a supply of hydrogen, and the fuel for the return journey must create itself using local carbon from the atmosphere. The second descent module with pilots provides flight to Mars and life on the surface.
Implementation : within 10 years from the moment of approval of the program.



The first non-state project of a manned flight to Mars was the American Mars Direct, which was proposed by former engineer of Locheed Martin Robert Zubrin in the 90s.

This project is very detailed in the technical part, and is designed for implementation based on the current technical level of civilization. Although he has several "narrow" moments, but they are in principle solvable, if we apply science and ingenuity.
I will not describe it in detail, and all the details can be found in this film, translated by the habrip user paulpotseluev , and approved by Robert (he speaks a little Russian).


English original .

From myself I will note some of the characteristics of Mars Direct. Despite the non-state project, Robert Zubrin expects that it can be implemented only by the efforts of the national space agencies. For lobbying and inspiration by Mars potential voters of the United States, the Mars Society was created.

Zubrin almost succeeded when the Bush administration announced the launch of the ambitious Constellation program. The program included the development of a new interplanetary spacecraft, the construction of a base on the moon, and a flyby of Mars. Unfortunately, due to the exorbitant high cost of the Constellation and the budget crisis in the United States, the program was curtailed. All that was left of her was Orion, the SLS rocket, and some props for shooting parody clips:



Realizing that the United States alone Mars Direct will not pull, Robert Zubrin reoriented the idea to implement the entire world community. For these purposes, open international offices of the Martian community. He himself recently came to Russia, gave lectures , talked about the project, and his " We could do it with goosebumps ". However, he actually left with nothing , although he laid the foundations for the future creation of the Russian branch of the Martian community.

Despite the prospects of the project (this is one of the most robust and well-developed in the current list) Mars Direct makes me feel old-fashioned, I feel the approach of the 90s: hope for the state / va; multi-page, although talented inspirational, books; the lack of crowdfunding and crowdsourcing that the Internet provides today. In general, the project was born at the dawn of the Internet and practically does not provide for the implementation of its many resources. Against the background of a series of projects in recent years, Zubrin looks like a powerful dinosaur, which, although it is not going to die out, radiates powerful energy, but it feels like its era has passed.

Mars Inspiration


Cost : $ 3 billion.
Idea : Flight of a manned spacecraft to approach Mars along a flight path without landing, and return to Earth. The duration of the flight is 501 days.
Implementation : 2018



Dennis Tito is an old man in the commercial exploration of space (and he himself is not young). He became famous as the first space tourist when he flew in 2001 at the age of 60. On Earth, he is a multimillionaire and a major investor.

In early 2013, he announced his proposal, due to inspire the American nation to new achievements in space: Inspiration Mars (Inspiration Mars). A spacecraft, with two pilots, must fly in a heliocentric orbit, where the trajectory on one side crosses the near-earth orbit, and on the other, passes 160 km behind Mars. That is, travelers will fly over the night side of the Red Planet, and during the rendezvous and removal will be able to observe its phases:



This is the simplest, cheapest and most realizable program of the listed ( description , rationale ). True and the most boring. Useful for science only in assessing the state of the human body under the long influence of weightlessness and cosmic radiation.

The funny thing is that Inspiration Mars has already become part of conspirological mythology. Someone with a rich imagination and lack of basic knowledge, it seemed that this project appeared after the Curiosity rover found gold on Mars. The absurdity of this idea is obvious: he did not find gold; the cost of any pinch of Martian soil is orders of magnitude greater than the cost of earth gold, but even if Curiosity had found a turbine pyramid, Mars Inspiration would have helped neither the United States nor humanity. We can look from above and from satellites, and the pilots of the Inspiration will not be able to. However, this idea found supporters on both sides of the ocean.

Despite the obvious simplicity of Inspiration Mars, several serious obstacles stand in the way of its implementation: you need to start the device either in 2018 or in 2031 (or later). The launch is scheduled for 2018, but so far there is no ship, no rocket, no money. Recently, the reason why Tito has not announced fundraising since February and has not heard of any movement since February has become known. The businessman‚Äôs calculation was for NASA to take the project under its wing and start implementing it. Approval has not yet been received from the space agency. The reason is obvious: one of the main principles of NASA is the safety of the crew, and this mission requires people who are ready for the role of experimental kamikaze rabbits. However, Tito found such, but NASA is not ready to take responsibility for their death.
