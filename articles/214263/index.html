I am opposed to look at it in silence.

However, I am not a barbarian (no, well ... really), and therefore I suggest trying to fight barbarism in a civilized way.
Namely, I propose to clearly and targetedly inform the distinguished gentlemen lawmakers about:
  • Our attitude to these bills

  • One quite obvious fatal (for the needs of this post, we will assume the absolute sincerity of the legislators, and within the framework of such an assumption the disadvantage is fatal) of this package of bills, indicating the need to finalize this package.

But first things first.

Let's start with the simplest and fastest option - electronic.
