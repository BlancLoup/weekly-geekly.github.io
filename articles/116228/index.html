2. Reuse DLL in your program


Opportunities:
  • opening xls file
  • opening OpenOffice files
  • possibility of opening in hidden mode
  • reading data from a spreadsheet
  • full range of data manipulation: text insertion, numbers, formatting, merging cells, setting boundaries, setting column width
  • unloading xls tables to disk
  • The possibility of working under WINE, under the terms of installing OpenOffice under Wine
  • use of formulas
  • generation of version dependent RDB file on the fly (if necessary)
