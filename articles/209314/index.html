The Steam controller will remove the touchscreen, and change the configuration of the buttons


So, most likely, the updated Steam controller will look

On Habr√© many times already wrote about game consoles Steam, as well as about the controller for these consoles - it is very unusual. In fact, two touch pads on the sides, plus another touch-pad directly in the middle, allowing you to use several additional functions, such as virtual buttons on the TV screen.

It is worth recalling that all those photos and videos that were published earlier, showed all the same preliminary versions of the controller and consoles. The prefixes were tested by testers, which were played by gamers who submitted applications for participation in the test program.

Apparently, the testers were not very happy with the controller, because after Steam Dev Days, the Network received information from developers - participants of this event that the touchpad would still be removed from the controller. The reason - this site distracts the gamer from the game, and sometimes it just hinders.



In addition, the configuration of the buttons will also be changed towards the traditional rhombus - this is how the buttons are located in most controllers, including Sony and Xbox controllers.
