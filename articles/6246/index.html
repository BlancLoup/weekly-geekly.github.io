Published results of the rating "TOP-100 leading web studios Runet"

Hi, Habr! The project site published the results of the TOP-100 studios rating.

The first ten studios are as follows:
  1. Art. Lebedev Studio 658
  2. Actis Systems 552
  3. RBC-Soft 401
  4. Defa gruppe 393
  5. ADV / web-engineering 282
  6. X-Project 156
  7. Dot 135
  8. Promo Interactive 113
  9. Dominion 108
  10. PRIOR.ru 103


TOP-5
Art. Lebedev Studio did not give leadership to any of the closest competitors, gaining 658 points. This is logical - ALG remains the most promoted brand in the market for creating websites, a company with one of the highest market budgets and a consistently high, with rare exceptions, quality of work. It should be noted that the subsidiary structure of ALG - Teletype studio (16 points), which is not particularly active in the market, lost 34 positions in the rating and took a modest 52nd place.

The top five remained unchanged, Actis Systems firmly holds second place with a decent margin from competitors. Nevertheless, one permutation occurred in the top five - RBC Soft (401 points) was able to outperform Defa gruppe (393 points), although the difference is so insignificant that the two companies practically shared the 3rd and 4th places.

ADV / web-engineering, as in the past year, confidently closes the top five, but with a decent lag behind its closest competitors.
TOP-10
In the Top-10 of our rating, on the contrary, there have been very significant changes compared to last year.
The most significant events are the growth of the X-project from 10th to 6th place (156 points), as well as the rapid rise in the top ten of Samara‚Äôs Dominion studio to 9th place (108 points), which managed to bypass a number of major Moscow and All Petersburg studios. It should be said that in the growth of the Dominion there is a significant merit of its PR activity in the market, since, according to our estimates, the real turnover of the studio is still lower than most of the companies in the first twenty.
The DOT agency lost one position, taking a worthy 7th place (135 points).
Promo Interactive entered the top ten in 8th place (113 points), rising by 6 positions compared to last year.
The only representative of large networked companies in Armenia - PRIOR.RU showed a good growth rate, taking 10th place (103 points), having risen by as much as 12 positions compared to the previous year.
