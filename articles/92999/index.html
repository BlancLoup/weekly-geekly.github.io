DrupalCampKyiv 2010 - Transfer dates to June 10-12

As we have already reported earlier, the DrupalCampKyiv 2010 BarCamp conference will take place in Kiev.
Also, those interested can make their report, or select a topic from the existing plan .

Also, we want to inform you that 700+ people have already registered on the conference website.

But!


Attention attention!
We are forced to report the postponement of the conference dates to June 10-12 (Thursday-Saturday) . Apparently this year Drupal's karma does not contribute to the stability of decisions with the room, because we had to change the planned room at the end of April.

While we are at the final stage of solving this issue and we have several options even for the worst case, so this is the last and final postponement of dates. The most likely new venue for the conference will be the Kiev-Mohyla Academy.

We sincerely hope that you have not yet had time to buy train / plane tickets and this circumstance will not break your plans to go after them (and maybe even provide such an opportunity).

For any questions related to the transfer of dates, you can contact us as usual, we will try to help you.

Come! We will be glad to each of you.
