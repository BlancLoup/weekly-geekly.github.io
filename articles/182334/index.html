Meet Magento Russia 2013 Conference



For the first time in Russia, there will be an official international meeting, Meet Magento , dedicated to the most popular e-commerce platform in the world - Magento Commerce.

About Magento


Magento is a powerful e-commerce platform owned by eBay / PayPal.
Magento is leading both in the free e-commerce market and in the corporate segment.

On the basis of Magento, stores of many brands, such as shops of Philips, Auchan, Olympus, Tool King, Men's Health, Nike, Angry Birds and thousands of others, are built.

About the conference


The Meet Magento Russia conference will be held on August 24 in Moscow (Izmailovo).
Participation in the conference is completely free and requires only pre-registration .

The conference is organized with the support of representatives of Magento and its official partners in Russia, Ukraine and the Republic of Belarus.

Participation in the conference will be interesting for both Internet entrepreneurs and web developers and owners of web studios.

For online entrepreneurs:
  • The opportunity to talk with Magento experts, developers
  • Get comprehensive information about the capabilities of the Magento Enterprise and Magento Community platforms
  • The prospect of meeting with the future developers of your online store

For developers:
  • A unique opportunity to communicate with experienced Magento developers, creators of the Magento platform
  • The opportunity to share personal experience in the field of Magento and Zend development of electronic stores and portals
  • Acquisition of useful connections and new acquaintances in the professional field of web development

The geography of registered participants at the moment: Moscow, Kiev, Minsk, St. Petersburg, Volgograd, Ulyanovsk, Yekaterinburg, Nizhny Novgorod.
