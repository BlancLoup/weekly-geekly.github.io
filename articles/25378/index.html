Hand Phone. Concept

Italian designers from Biodomotica presented the concept of a futuristic phone called Hand Phone to the public. The device, as conceived by designers, should consist of the main part (a circle with a display and a pair of buttons mounted on the back of the palm), and the speaker and microphone, which are located on the thumb and little finger. In order to talk on such a phone you need to make a "flaring": bring the thumb to your ear and the little finger to your mouth.
