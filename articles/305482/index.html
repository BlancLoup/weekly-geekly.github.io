This summer, we, the iOS development team of the Rambler & Co holding, want to invite you to our Rambler.iOS # 7 meeting, which will take place on July 20 in our office at Danilovskaya Manufactory. At the request of the participants of our previous events at this meeting, special attention will be paid to the community communication with each other and with members of our team.
check in

But what we will talk about this time.

What should we build a table?

The story of the complex tabular interfaces.


Building an interface from many different cells is a common problem in the world of iOS development. In the first report, the solution of this problem will be analyzed on the example of a real project. How best to decompose, how to allocate class responsibilities correctly, as well as how the view models and the classic Builder pattern help us solve this problem, will tell Andrei Rezanov .



Hi, Siri! How to pay by credit card?

A detailed report on Internet acquiring and payment by bank cards in iOS applications.



Payment on the Internet using payment cards has long become an everyday decision. Alexey Sychev will tell you about how this process is organized and what problems you may encounter in his report .






The theme of the introduction of payments will continue and complement Alexander Sychev . His report is devoted to the existing possibilities of accepting bank cards when paying for goods and services in iOS applications.
