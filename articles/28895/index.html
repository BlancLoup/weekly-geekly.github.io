Creating thumbnails with Overflow

Controlling the size of thumbnails on your page. Those. limiting the default size, and showing their full size when you hover the mouse.

This is not a resizing of the image, but only a resizing of its visible part.



UPD: it turned out, but oh well ...

The idea is to put the image in a container (in this case it will be ). ( ) (position="relative"). (position="absolute"), top left . overflow hidden, , , . (a:hover) overflow visible .
