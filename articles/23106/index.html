Google Email Uploader

Google has released an open source email downloader .
To date, this software is available only to users of Windows XP / Vista. The main purpose is to transfer existing emails and contacts to your Google Apps Gmail account .

Instruction:
Download and install the Google Email Uploader, select one of your email clients: Microsoft Outlook, Outlook Express and / or Thunderbird to start the download. The images below explain the scheme in detail.
