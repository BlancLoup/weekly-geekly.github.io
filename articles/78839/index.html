The ice has broken Android on HTC TD2


Great news for the owners of the phone HTC TD2, finally there were shifts in the development of Androyd on our phone. About where to download and what works under the cut.

So you can read the whole info on xda-developers

Currently the port works and does not work:
  • Booting = YES
  • Touchscreen = YES
  • Keymapping = YES
  • WiFi = NO
  • Bluetooth = NO
  • GPS = NO
  • USB = NO
  • GSM Radio = YES
  • FMRadio = NO
  • SMS / MMS = Incoming SMS Only
  • 2g / 3g data = NO
  • G-Sensor = NO
  • Camera = NO
  • Power Off / Suspend = NO
  • Backlight Control = NO
  • Sound - Internal Speaker = YES
  • Sound - External Speakerphone = NO
  • Sound - Microphone = NO
  • Sound - Vibration = NO


Verified versions:
  • Android Eclair 2.0.1 = NO
  • Android Donut 1.6 = YES
  • Android Cupcake 1.5 = Untested


What can I say, at the moment the build is quite stable, although there are touchscreen response delays. Of course, the functionality is not complete but a week ago there was nothing, there was a strong breakthrough in development and I, like many readers of that topic on xda, very much want to get a stable working build for Christmas.
