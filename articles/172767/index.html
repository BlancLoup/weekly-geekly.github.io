HTML5 Camp online broadcast - March 15 from 10:00

Friends, we are glad to remind (and to tell someone) that tomorrow from 10:00 (Moscow Time) online translation of the third HTML5 Camp will be waiting for you.



During the conference we will have three tracks:
  • Business discussions + technology sessions
  • Web development technology sessions
  • Technology sessions for developing Windows 8 on HTML / JS.


Business sessions will be held in the format of panel discussions (moderated by Mikhail Chernomordikov, Microsoft):
  • Web Development Trends
    Participants: Grigory Bakunov (Yandex), Andrey Sumin (Mail.Ru Group), Vadim Makeev (Opera Software), Vladimir Gabriel (Business Environment)
  • Sites for devices, screens and fingers
    Participants: Platon Dneprovsky (UI Design), Irina Grandel (IVI.ru), Roman Shmelkin (Gismeteo), Alexey Markin (JetStyle)
  • Application against sites - who is more fun
    Participants: Roman Menyakin (Adobe Systems), Vadim Mityakin (Galssoft), Maria Piterskaya (Megaplan), Dmitry Tarasov (Tarasov Mobile)


Also you are waiting for technological reports from experts from Yandex, Adobe, Microsoft, Intel, Agima, JetBrains and Wincor Nixdorf.

You can watch the broadcast directly from the conference website: html5camp.ru
