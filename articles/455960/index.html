Why developers leave: 8 reasons



There is such a phrase ‚ÄúNo matter how well you raise a child, he will not care what to say to your psychoanalyst.‚Äù The same can be said about working with employees. No matter how well you work in the company, your employees will have something to say about the reasons for leaving the job interview - this is normal. But risks can always be minimized, and the life of an employee within a company can be increased.

We decided to analyze the main reasons for the departure of developers from previous places. This is not an in-depth study, but a short slice in the moment: we looked at 175 notes following the results of communication with developers, which are going on at different stages of our projects, chose the most vivid statements and grouped them into eight topics.

For each topic there are recommendations for representatives of companies and employees who will help the two parties or to establish cooperation, or to part with minimal negative consequences.

Unimplemented workflows


The leader of our collection. It occurs in more than 60% of the reasons when changing jobs. The peculiarity is that this is a fairly broad concept, which includes management errors, software flaws, and a shortage of specialists to accomplish the tasks set.

‚ÄúI left my last job at the end of last year, now I‚Äôm doing custom development. The development team had very few employees who could not find the right people for a long time, which was why there were problems with the work processes. ‚Äù
‚ÄúAt the current place, there are no well-established workflows for a long time: there is not enough testing department, all tasks have to be done in a short time without a deep dive, teams do not have time to write autotests.‚Äù
‚ÄúThere is no one who could competently build processes, you have to redo many things. There is a lack of specifics, I would like to work in a competent team and consult with professional colleagues in professional matters. ‚Äù
If you are an employer and want to understand how the outgoing employees perceive the company‚Äôs work processes, use the exit interview. It can be both personal and in the form of a survey. Its essence is to clarify the reasons for leaving through such questions: ‚Äúwhat convinced you to move to another company?‚Äù, ‚ÄúDid you have enough resources to work?‚Äù, ‚ÄúWhat would you miss in the new workplace?‚Äù. It is recommended to conduct such an interview, when the company does not owe anything to the employee (salary paid, work record given), so that the employee does not think that his answers may affect payments or other bonuses from the company.

If you are a developer , clarify with your guide before you even quit, what is the cause of the inefficiency of workflows, how can this be corrected. When interviewing new companies, you may be asked questions like: ‚Äúhave you tried to influence this somehow?‚Äù Or ‚Äúhave you spoken with management on this topic?‚Äù To understand how active you are in the event of problems.

Leadership problems


For this reason, there is a phrase that has managed to get bored: "They come to work in the company, but they leave the manager." We will supplement it: ‚Äúthey leave from a manager, several managers or the entire top management‚Äù. This is a process that cannot be influenced. For example, because of a change in top management, the company's general strategy is changing, a new positioning in the market is taking place, so the company can react with an outflow of employees even without the desire of the management to optimize the team itself.
‚ÄúI do not see any support from the management regarding changes and innovations (for example, to connect testers at the moment of architectural planning). Together with the team we are trying to establish processes. When I first came to the company, there was not enough experience and knowledge, but now the mess has become obvious: the attitude of management is demotivating. ‚Äù
‚ÄúI began to consider something new for myself, since they are not satisfied with the processes in the current location: the project manager commits with the management for a certain time, but does not take into account the opinion of the developers.‚Äù
‚ÄúI decided to look for a job because there are a lot of internal changes at the current place at the top management level, which entail the departure of developers by whole teams.‚Äù
If you are an employer , work on feedback tools. And accept the fact that in the event of a change of charismatic leader, part of the team can leave with him.

If you are a developer , try not to make a sharp decision about dismissal during rumors about a change of leadership - see how this actually affects the processes in the company. But for peace of mind, the labor market can always have time to monitor.

‚ÄúI decided to look for a job because since the new year, the company has gone with salary payments. ‚ÄúThe company doesn‚Äôt have the best situation with the business part, they don‚Äôt buy the developed solution, but I don‚Äôt want to write code‚Äú to the table ‚Äù.

Unfulfilled promises: expectations / reality


Sometimes at the interview so that the candidate accepts the offer, hiring managers can embellish the current situation or promise very attractive tasks / projects. If the choice of work will only take place according to a critical criterion, then, in case of his absence, the candidate will immediately resign. Therefore, experienced hiring managers do not rush to embellish workflows or tasks, and even talk about the difficulties encountered in their work, so that the candidate clearly understands what he agrees to, and the team did not suffer because of staff turnover.
‚ÄúI left company X due to the fact that the new mobile development department in the Moscow office delivered for revision the old project, although initially they promised to develop a new one. The American colleagues were given a new application to work. ‚Äù

‚ÄúI decided to start looking for a job, because at the current place they promised a strong team and interesting tasks, but as a result, the development department began to disband, many developers managed to leave, and the workflows were unimplemented. ‚ÄúI liked the work according to tasks, but the initial agreements on the conditions were not respected, which is why I had to change jobs.‚Äù
If you are an employer , do not make promises that are impossible to keep, or they have an extremely low probability to come true. For example, if you plan to open an office in the United States, but for this event it is necessary to overcome a certain profit margin, tell the candidate: ‚ÄúWe will open an office in the United States if we fulfill the sales plan by at least 90%‚Äù. It is not necessary to open all the numbers, it is enough transparency.

If you are a developer , it will be harder to know the real working conditions and the degree of attractiveness of the tasks. Word of mouth may help, but it is advisable to rely on several sources.

The criterion, which, in the case of the company's inflexibility, becomes the final point in the decision to change work.
‚ÄúI work remotely, I don‚Äôt have enough teamwork, I would like to find an interesting job in the office, I want more movement, sharing experience with colleagues and more involvement in the process.‚Äù
‚ÄúThe company cannot provide official employment, which also affected the change of job.‚Äù

A free work schedule allows employees to handle personal matters without compromising workflows. Although one of our examples above was a developer looking for work in an office, each candidate has his or her own set of requirements for an employer. And yes, warn about recycling if they occur.

If you are a developer , look for a job that matches your needs. If the conservative structure can affect the flexibility of working hours, then definitely not on the distance of work.

Instead of output
