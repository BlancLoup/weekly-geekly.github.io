Remote control for cat

In the online store editorial gg Think Geek appeared a device that can attract the attention of all who are not indifferent to cats. This is a remote control that allows you to control the actions of your caudate family member.


Cats do not know their ability to train and subordinate to the commands of their owners, in this case, Control-a-Cat Remote Control comes to the rescue!
The dimensions of the console are 14x5.5 cm, and 21 keys allow you to perform a lot of useful operations, including “stop shedding!”, “Curtail yourself!”, “Arch your back!”, “Get out of bed!”, “Stop sulking!”, “ to your toilet, infection! ”,“ catch the mouse ”,“ remain indifferent ”,“ show affection ”,“ purr ”,“ stop scratching furniture ”,“ hiss ”,“ remove claws! ”and“ land on the feet ”. The cost of the remote is low and is only 7.99 dollars. The power supply does not need batteries, rechargeable batteries or solar batteries - the device, according to the seller, is powered by positive thinking energy.

Using the remote control is also quite simple - you need to direct the remote control to a cat (or a cat - the gadget fully complies with the unisex concept and in no way promotes sexual superiority of any of the sexes), press the appropriate button and wait for your pet to execute this command. Ultimately, now it's your turn to make these proud and recalcitrant animals do what you need!
