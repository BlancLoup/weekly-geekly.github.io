Web 2.0 for raising babies

Exactly one year has passed since the launch of the excellent Trixie Tracker web service, which allows you to visualize the whole process of raising a baby: sleep intervals, feeding time and changing diapers. In the absence of the Internet, earlier all this was noted on a piece of paper, and it was impossible in principle to trace any trends or build charts, let alone compare them with the charts of thousands of other children. Web 2.0 came to the rescue.

A web program can show a lot of beautiful diagrams, calculate average indicators and the probability of falling asleep at a specified time (for a specific child or all children of this age), compare indicators for different periods and much more. Of course, complete statistics are kept.
