Skype 5.0 for Mac OS X

Today came the final version of Skype 5.0 for Mac . In comparison with the beta, according to numerous requests from the workers, the interface has been made more compact: the indents between the messages in the chat have decreased, the height of the lines in the contact list, the size of the side menu have decreased. The opportunity to expand video calls in full screen was returned and more convenient chat during a call was made. The call of the contact monitor is made more clearly - a new window for tracking the statuses of the most important interlocutors for you.
