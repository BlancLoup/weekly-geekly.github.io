Recently launched Russian site for registration of UAVs was unreal



A number of media outlets reported on April 5th that they launched a system for voluntary registration of unmanned vehicles weighing from 250 grams to 30 kg via the Internet. The project site indicated that it was developed by the Federation of Aeromodel Sport of Russia (FASR) and the Association of Operators and Developers of Unmanned Aviation Systems (ERBAS). However, after some time it became clear that the state bodies have nothing to do with the launch of the site.

The press service of the Ministry of Transport of the Russian Federation reported to the Fontanka publication that registration on the uavreg.ru portal is not provided for by current legislation. The ministry also denied the information from the site that it was created with the support of the Ministry of Transport.

According to whois, the site is registered to ERBAS non-profit organization. At the moment, he is no longer responding.
