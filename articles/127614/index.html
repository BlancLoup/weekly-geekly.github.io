The evolution of Internet browsers



By the third anniversary of the Chrome browser, Google has visualized the history of the development of technologies and programs for web browsing.

The colored bars on this infographic display a complex interweaving of web technologies and browsers, thanks to which we today have the opportunity to enjoy productive web applications.

On one page you can see two decades of technology of eight popular browsers: Mosaic, Netscape, Opera, Internet Explorer, Safari, Firefox, Chrome, and Chrome OS.
