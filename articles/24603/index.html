GTA walks through Europe

The retail sales data for the Grand Theft Auto 4 criminal action began to come in from the retail fronts. Analytical company Chart-Track reported that according to its calculations based on its own data and retailers' estimates, the project for the first day was sold in the UK with a record circulation of 609 thousand copies , beating the unshakable achievement of Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas in 2004 of 501 thousand copies sold per day.



The sales records for the first day on each of the new generation set-top boxes were also broken. Sales of the GTA 4 version for Xbox 360 amounted to 335 thousand copies against the autumn 266 thousand copies of the futuristic Halo 3 action. The PS3 version set a new record with an even more impressive margin of 274 thousand copies against 80 thousand copies sold recently released Gran Turismo Racing Project 5: Prologue. However, as noted by Chart-Track, sales data is still approximate; Analysts promise to present the specified information on May 6.
