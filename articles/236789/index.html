140 characters each: American online stores can trade on Twitter

This news on Saturday, September 13, Twitter reported in its newsletter to service users. Twitter has long been asked how it is going to make money - and it seems that the answer has been found: in the US, the functionality is being tested for purchases from some of the popular trading companies directly from Twitter.



To do this, users will need to provide information such as a credit card number and shipping address. Twitter will regulate the relationship of users with sellers, including responsibility for order fulfillment, delivery and return of goods.

Other policy updates concern countering spam and fraud - for this, Twitter gave itself the right to request additional information from the owners of suspicious accounts, as well as an explanation of what information about the user, including location information (for example, via IP address or nearby access points ) they collect.
