What is reintroduction?


This is when we soaked someone, then repented and delivered anew either from zoos or from another place where there is a lot of such animals.

For example, they somehow drove musk oxen to Taimyr from Greenland. True, in Taimyr, it was not we who ate the oxen, but they themselves had already smoked something long before the beginning of the industrial era.

The term is applicable to plants too.

One of the problems of reintroduction is that animals lose the necessary adaptive properties, for example, the skills of searching for food collection in the wild, defensive and attacking properties. Therefore, they are often released gradually. With bison, too, so it was planned, but they are quite heavy animals, so it does not always work with an aviary.

The second potential problem is low genetic diversity, because the zoo population is very small.

What is happening in the reserve now?


In the "Kaluga abatis" one of the most successful bison populations in the world. The reserve is such a long narrow forest of 120 kilometers in size.

Herds of bison live in different parts of it. Bulls go much further than the reserve, because the boys generally love to wander around the neighborhood, but cows and young heaps are around the feeding points.



Feeding points are needed not to maintain bison life, but to concentrate them in the reserve (they love cooked food) and to maintain fertility. Fat animals, satisfied with life, come to the hunt more often and reproduce much faster than those who are forced to look for something all day.



Without feeding points, they are quite capable of surviving, but the population in Vologda, for example, has stabilized at about 80 individuals and does not increase much.

There are no predators of the right size and the right skills in the forest. Wolves, in theory, are capable of blocking not the largest beast (there are two flocks in the reserve, 12 and 6 heads), but they simply do not know how. On the other hand, they perfectly learned how to hunt hunting dogs, and switched to beavers, as in other regions. Because there are a lot of beavers, and they are tasty, and after the swine plague there are few boars.
