Major IT companies will not allow AI to escape from the control of a person



Artificial intelligence - the subject of a large number of discussions in the scientific and world. Experts in many countries are working to create an artificial mind that could surpass its creator. Some scientists believe that AI is good. Other specialists are afraid of a strong form of artificial intelligence. Last year, businessmen, scientists, experts in the field of robotics and other fields signed an open letter , which warns weapons manufacturers from developing combat systems with 100% autonomy. Among the signatories are: Ilon Musk, Stephen Hawking, Noam Chomsky, Steve Wozniak.

Many aspects of the problem of artificial intelligence are displayed in the plot of a number of films, described in science fiction works. According to scientists, it is necessary to solve emerging issues before the appearance of a strong form of AI, that is, now. The largest technology companies, including Google, Microsoft, IBM are now working on the creation of artificial intelligence.
