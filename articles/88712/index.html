Announced the first Android-based smartphone with WiMAX - HTC Evo 4G

At CTIA 2010, which takes place in Las Vegas, mobile operator Sprint Nextel presented the first Android-based smartphone with WiMAX - it was the HTC Evo 4G (formerly known as "Supersonic").



Specifications:
  • WiMAX, CDMA (EV-DO RevA);
  • 4.3-inch capacitive WVGA display (480 x 800);
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon processor with 1 GHz;
  • 512 MB of RAM (RAM) and 1 GB of internal memory (ROM) + slot for microSD-card;
  • 8 megapixel camera with auto focus, dual LED flash and the ability to record HD video (720p);
  • 1.3 megapixel front camera;
  • GPS with Sprint Navigation support;
  • Wi-Fi 802.11b / g;
  • Bluetooth 2.1;
  • standard audio jack (3.5 mm);
  • HDMI port;
  • 1500 mAh battery;
  • software platform - Android 2.1 + HTC Sense UI.

The cost has not yet been announced. Home sales - this summer.
