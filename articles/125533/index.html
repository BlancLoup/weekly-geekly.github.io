Search for public transport in mobile 2GIS

To date, the mobile version of 2GIS is a guide to more than 1,000,000 organizations in Russia and a detailed 3D map indicating the number of floors of buildings, internal driveways, kiosks and fences. And now to this list was added the search for travel by public transport.



To search for travel is not necessary to know the names of public transport. It is enough to indicate on the map the starting and ending point of the route, or in the card of a specific organization in the context menu select ‚ÄúHow to get here?‚Äù Or ‚ÄúHow to get from here?‚Äù.

Your current location, which is determined by GPS, Wi-Fi or cell towers, can serve as the starting point of the route. The corresponding icon in the lower left corner of the screen unobtrusively suggests how your location is currently determined.

When calculating the route, 2GIS takes into account all types of urban transport: metro, buses, trolleybuses, trams, route taxis and electric trains. In addition, 2GIS also takes into account seasonal routes and rare types of transport such as, for example, metrotras in Volgograd or ferries in Vladivostok.

You can download mobile 2GIS from the Android Market or from our website (versions for WM and Symbian).

PS Version 2GIS for iPhone is planned for the end of this year. It will also be implemented search for travel by public transport.

PPS Mobile version 2GIS is installed in the phone and works completely autonomously . That is, does not require a permanent internet connection.

PPPS This functionality is not yet available for Nizhny Tagil, Belgorod and Tver, but this is a question for 2-3 months.
