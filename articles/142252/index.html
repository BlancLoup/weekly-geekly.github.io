Results of the initiative to support the development of Windows Phone applications in MCP-clubs and User Group

Today, the results of the support initiative for developers of Windows Phone applications in MCP clubs and User Group, launched on January 13, 2012, were summed up.

A few words about MCP clubs and User Groups. These are communities of IT specialists and developers who meet regularly. The program of each meeting of the MCP Club and UG includes the exchange of experience, reports, discussion of technologies and, of course, answers to burning questions. Currently there are more than 100 MCP clubs and User Groups, from Kaliningrad to Vladivostok. You can see if there is a MCP club near you or a User Group on www.ineta.ru .

As part of the initiative, its participants could get Nokia Lumia 800 phones, both in the "individual standings" and in the team - for the whole group.

In the initiative took part:
  • 11 communities
  • more than 20 developers
