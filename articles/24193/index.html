Chilirec to record individual tracks from Internet radio stations

The Swedes have launched a new project Chilirec , which solves an important problem for all fans of free music - recording tracks from Internet radio stations. However, it is necessary to use the list maintained by the developers - you cannot add your stations.

The procedure is very simple - choose a channel, click "Record". The service itself understands where the beginning of the song is, where the end is and writes them into your database, which is maintained online. Then you can listen to the tracks through a flash-player, or download everything together in mp3 format. The playlist is conveniently arranged, it is possible to search by artist name, title, genre or tags.
