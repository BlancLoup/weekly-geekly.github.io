Case: Paints Holi

Hi, Megamind! We have here a small case to increase calls from the site. KraskiHoli.rf provided us with call statistics before and after installing Rocket Callback.



A little about the company. Site KraskiHoli.rf . Engaged in the sale and supply of Indian holi colors used for festivals. On the market for about 3 years. We started preparing for the summer festivals since March - we checked several widgets, saw that they didn‚Äôt differ from each other, and so they chose just someone who offered the same quality at the lowest price - that is, us.

Rocket was installed on the site on April 1, 2015. As the company said, every year in April, interest in paints of holi rises sharply, as the season of festivals begins. At the same time, an advertising campaign is launched and, accordingly, site traffic is growing sharply.

Test 7 minutes were spent on the first day, after which the company first bought 50 minutes from us, then another 50, and then 4 times for 1000. We have call statistics for the period January-April 2015:

Numbers. The company bought 4,100 minutes from us, which cost it 56,000 rubles, taking into account the dollar rate at the beginning of April 2015. As can be seen from the graphs, attendance remained about the same, while the number of calls increased 18 times. If we take into account that in April the site was visited by 7800 people, then it turns out that approximately every fourth visitor used the widget in April. Each conversation lasted an average of 2-3 minutes.

Conversion. We ourselves were interested to know what kind of profit our Rocket brings in this particular case, but it was not possible to calculate the exact number of transactions closed through the widget - a person can call and ask today, and buy paint in a week. However, the company told us that if last year 58 tons of paint were sold, then this year this figure was almost reached, and about 120 transactions were closed remotely through Rocket - customers from distant regions, who were using the usual telephone conversation would have cost too much.

The reasons. Of course, Rocket could not catch such a number of calls in January or even February - there is no traffic, no one to call. However, if a warm visitor came to the site, who drove into the search engine ‚Äúto buy paints of holi‚Äù, then the probability that he would order a callback is rather high.
