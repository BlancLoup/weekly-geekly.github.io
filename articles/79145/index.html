IMAX 3D vs RealD vs Dolby 3D

This article does not pretend to a complete picture of the world, to objective information and scientific research. This is just an attempt to compare disparate information and summarize some statistics about current 3D technologies. In the last few days I was often asked about the differences between IMAX 3D, RealD and Dolby 3D technologies and which one to choose when watching a movie. There will be no definite choice, whoever wants to will be able to choose according to the facts provided.

Story. How it all began


For a long time, people, by virtue of the binocularity of view, sought to capture our world as we perceive it, i.e. three-dimensional. The first successful works, which are known at the moment, belong to the 19th century . In the 20th century, humanity did not stop at static stereo images. The film industry began to develop at a frantic pace, and to add zest, they began to shoot the first films in 3D, using anaglyph. The technology involves the use of two cameras, color coding is used for the display. She quickly gained popularity due to ease of implementation: you need to view a color-coded movie and glasses with red and blue light filters. Show such a movie can be in any cinema. Now you can watch these movies on your computer. The main disadvantage of this technology is incomplete color reproduction.
Then there were various technologies of alternating overlapping of the eye, but they are complex and not so interesting, about their history some other time.

The present.


At the moment, there are three main 3D display technologies in cinemas on the market, two of which are based on polarization, the third is an evolution of anaglyph technology: IMAX 3D, RealD and Dolby 3D.
