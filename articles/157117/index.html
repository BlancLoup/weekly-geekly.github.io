Photo Fair in St. Petersburg

From the 25th to the 28th of October in St. Petersburg was held PhotoFair. Surprised that so far no one has written a single post about such a major event. I only managed to attend the fair on the closing day. Yes, and I photographed there, mostly, only girls who advertise all sorts of new fashion gadgets. Therefore, I decided to publish this post on Friday.
Summary of the review:

Under the cut a lot of photos and little text.

Canon and Nikon showed off their top cameras and lenses. You could climb to the second floor, insert your memory card into the camera and feel several hundred thousand rubles in your hands.




Everywhere there were stormy sales of photo and video equipment.






Master classes were held in separate halls, where photo art masters shared their secrets. You could make quite unusual pictures or just watch a beautiful performance.




Wherever you look - everywhere signs and logos. Literally hang up on each other


On the second floor were exhibited works of famous photo artists.


Someone shot video reports.


At the Polaroid stand, one could see tricks with balls (except for an updated line of cameras with instant printing).


Two acrobats performed at the Samsung booth. I admit honestly - looked, having opened a mouth. The guys are doing such things. I remembered the camera when the performance was almost over.


Pentax products were advertised by people accustomed to an active lifestyle.


And of course, there were many beautiful girls at the fair. Girls in dresses, skirts, dancing, walking with guns, posing or just smiling.
