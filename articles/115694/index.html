SKB Kontur starts searching for startups through the community

Following Yandex, HeadHunter and Muz-TV, StartupPoint begins a partnership with a new strategic investor, SKB Kontur .

What will this collaboration give to projects?

You can apply and participate in the competitive selection of projects, which holds StartupPoint for SKB Kontur.
SKB Kontur supports start-up SaaS companies, start-ups that specialize in paid web services that are attractive to a very wide audience of businesses and are not highly specialized solutions.

What subjects of projects are interested in SKB Kontur?

Basically, these are solutions in the field of accounting and finance, automation of document flow.
The size of the proposed investment?

From $ 50,000. The size of the share in the project is negotiated individually with the project.


How is the procedure for submitting an application for participation in a contest for SKB Kontur?

You register on the site startuppoint.ru and add a description of the project . If you are already registered, send a letter of intent to participate in the contest skb@startuppoint.ru . After the pre-selection, you participate in the closed Pointe, which is held for SKB Kontur.

By email you send only a link to your profile on startuppoint.ru with a cover letter.

Attention! The nearest Point will be held in April 2011. Hurry up!

If you are not interested in receiving investments, participation in the selection may also be useful to you. The presentation in Point, in which representatives of SKB Kontur will participate, will give you (besides possible investments):
  • opportunity to improve your understanding of the project, its prospects for the market, adjust your strategy
  • negotiate a partnership with a company or other projects participating in the Point
  • Get data and figures about the market in which you work. SKB Kontur has data and statistics. What you have not yet tested and only assume may be a ready knowledge of this company.

About SKB Kontur company:

SKB Kontur has been working in the software development market since 1988. The company's solutions are able to automate a variety of processes:
  • prepare and submit reports via the Internet to the tax inspectorate and other regulatory bodies (FIU, FSS, Rosstat) - Kontur-Extern system, Kontur-PF Report service;
  • to adjust personnel records and accrue wages - the Kontur-Salary and Kontur-Personnel systems
  • exchange legally significant documents with counterparties through paperless legally significant document flow between enterprises;
  • clarify the legal aspects of accounting work - the system "Kontur-Standard"
