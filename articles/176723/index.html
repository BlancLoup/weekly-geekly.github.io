Google will change the appearance of search results for Europe, "give odds" to competitive services



For about three years, officials of the European Union have been analyzing the work of Google search service and its impact on the work of competitive services (Yelp, UrbanSpoon, TripAdvisor). If you remember, the companies that own thematic search services (tickets, restaurants and cafes, etc.) filed a joint lawsuit against Google, complaining of the impossibility of competing with the search giant. The corporation, after a series of court proceedings and work with EU officials, went to meet, and soon a new version of search results, so to speak, European will be presented.
