Evernote for Android appeared scanning business cards



Business Card Scanning, one of the popular premium features of Evernote , is now available on Android.

Download Evernote Android Update >>

Switch to the business card shooting mode in Evernote for Android and take a photo of the business card. Evernote instantly recognizes the text in the photo and places the contact information in the required fields.



Additional accuracy will ensure the connection of Evernote to your LinkedIn account. As soon as you do this, we will add information about the person from his profile, including a photo, to the digital business card, as well as provide an opportunity to contact him on LinkedIn.

Free access to features from LinkedIn
If you use the free version of Evernote, then by connecting your account to LinkedIn, you will get unlimited access to the scanning function of business cards for one year.

Features notes with business cards


Unlike most notes in your account, notes with scanned business cards have a clear structure. For example, when you open a note, you will see the phone icon next to the phone number, and the envelope next to the email address. Click on one of the desired icons to call or write to the business card holder. Click the edit icon to add notes or update information.
