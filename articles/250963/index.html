55 million users moving on



Do you know what warms us with long Scandinavian nights? Well, still Eastern European and many others, but still long and cold . The realization that we are working for a reason, our browser love and use it with pleasure. And the last numbers that we see only strengthen us in these thoughts. Today we are pleased to announce that we have as many as 55 million every month. We are talking here about unique users and this figure has grown by 8% since last year.

What is 55 million? This is more than the population of Ukraine and Belarus combined, for example.

Millionaires Club


But the matter is not only in the total number of users, the alignment of forces has also changed - the honorable club of millionaires has become more, that is, the countries in which more than a million people use our browser every month. Now there are eleven:

  • Belorussia
  • Brazil
  • Vietnam
  • Germany
  • India
  • Indonesia
  • Poland
  • Russia
  • USA
  • Turkey
  • Ukraine

In turn, Argentina, Brazil, Egypt, France, India, Indonesia, Mexico, Poland, Pakistan, Turkey and the United States showed the largest growth among the 25 most popular countries. It is very joyful to see Opera for computers gaining strength in Southeast Asia and Latin America, where until now it has been known for its mobile browsers.

It was a pleasant reminder that we are working for a reason. And we move on and we will be happy to see even more users in our family.
