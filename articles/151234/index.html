RailsClub'Moscow 2012. Conference program, afterparty

Good afternoon friends!

There are 4 days left until the next RailsClub'Moscow conference.

So, at the moment, the program of the event is fully formed.



In addition to foreign speakers, the speakers were joined by: Sergey Nartimov from brainspec.com , Yuri Sokolov from mail.ru , Nikolai Rekubratsky from undev.ru and Dmitry Galinsky from evrone.ru

Read more about all the reports here - railsclub.ru/reports

Traditionally, our conferences are divided into 2 parts. In the first part, we listen to the reports and gain knowledge. The second part is devoted to informal communication.

The last few RoR conferences were poorly organized from the point of view of the party after the conference. We decided to correct this situation and this time we chose one big bar for the afterparty. It is located in a cozy place in the center of Moscow, two steps from the Kremlin. Our goal is to give each of you the opportunity to hang out with the Ruby-people and informally communicate with our celebrity speakers. Details about the evening event will be additionally announced at the conference.

We will be waiting for you at the conference and afterparty.

You can buy tickets here - railsclub.timepad.ru/event/29192
