Games and SDK on Silverlight for MoyMir@Mail.Ru

Social networking applications have gained huge popularity lately. Who has not heard of Farmers in one form or another? Around the development of social applications are opening up new companies that make big money.

It's nice that the Russian networks have recently set their sights on opening their platform - in particular, from the end of last year, applications for MoYMir@Mail.Ru can be developed on any technology - including Silverlight.

Stanislav Shuvalov ported a very interesting application AudioFluids . This is a game of speed, where you need to place special objects on the stage, so that taking into account the laws of physics, particle flows go to the right targets. During the day, about a thousand people became users of this application. Try it and you!
