Qt Developer Conferences

I am happy to rush to share the news that no matter whether Nokia remembers or not, events in Europe and the USA will be held that are comparable in scale and format to Qt Developer Days.

Namely, in Europe, KDAB will hold the European Qt Developer Conference in mid-November in Berlin



And in the US in Silicon Valley, Integrated Computer Solutions Inc. December 5-7, will hold the North American Qt Developer Conference




Due to the well-known position of Nokia, so far this year the date of the most important event for all who work with the Qt framework, namely the date of Qt Developer Days, has not been announced.
