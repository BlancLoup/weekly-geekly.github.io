LinkedIn has agreed to take over Lynda.com for $ 1.5 billion

LinkedIn - the world's largest "professional social network", agreed to purchase the educational project Lynda.com for $ 1.5 billion. The amount will be paid in the second quarter of 2015, 52% in cash and 48% in shares of LinkedIn.


Lynda.com was created in 1995 by the spouses Barce Khiven and Linda Weinman.
