Fishermen-geeks of all countries, unite

The 3D simulator of winter fishing competitions ProPilkki has been known on the Web for a very long time, since 1999. At first it was simple, with several fixed locations, without playing over the Internet, but it was original - there were no other games dedicated to winter use. No wonder he immediately became popular. Then gradually there were additional cards, multiplayer, and dozens of servers in the world, on which there are always fans to shake the winter rod in the home heat.



But Finnish developers created the program (and continue) very slowly, completely free of charge for players, carefully testing the game before publishing. Update intervals are measured in semesters and years. Well, in the current millennium, new mobile devices, which are always in your pocket, allow you to make games for which there are no borders, neither geographic nor technological. In short, there was a desire to get a similar game, but ...

  • and in general - a more realistic simulator of the process of winter fishing, not 3D-pictures, but the process itself.
