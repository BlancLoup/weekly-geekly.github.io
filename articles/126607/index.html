Weather Layer Appears on Google Maps


Today in the official blog of Google there was an entry that the company added a layer of weather to its maps. As planned, the tool will show not only the temperature and weather conditions at specific points, but also the climate situation as a whole, which will allow you to plan your tourist adventures and outings more accurately. A little more under the cut.

To indicate the weather situation, special icons are used (rain, sun, etc.)



Cloud cover will be displayed on the US Naval Research Lab database.

To try a new product, just hover over the widget in the upper right corner and select the weather layer from the drop-down list. If you click on the weather icon in a particular city, more detailed information will appear, such as humidity, wind, and a forecast for the next 4 days.
