Asus PadFone 2 video review



Asus PadFone 2 is the second generation of a hybrid device that combines all the positive qualities of a smartphone and tablet. By installing the smartphone in a special dock, we get a tablet with all the relevant functionality. We have already reviewed the PadFone of the first generation, and now let's talk about what PadFone 2 offers us.

From the previous generation, PadFone 2 differs in several key features. First, the 4.3-inch Super AMOLED display was replaced with a Super IPS + display with a diagonal of 4.7 inches (the resolution also increased to 1280x720). Secondly, the device has got a quad-core Snapdragon S4 processor and twice the amount of RAM (2 GB). Thirdly, it was a cardinal reworked mechanism for installing a smartphone in the PadFone Station dock. And finally, fourth, the device received a new 13-pin connector for PC synchronization, charging or other functions.

In the second generation, PadFone lost the ability to connect a special keyboard dock that turned the device into a netbook. Also from the kit missing stylus headset. Android OS version remains the same - this is 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich.
