Pocket air hockey



Well, finally, now in order to play air hockey, you do not need to go to the nearest billiard room.

The toy sells for $ 14 and represents an inverted version of stationary hockey. That is, if in a normal game the air blows a puck through holes on the surface of the table, here the puck itself is equipped with an air cushion.

Any level surface is suitable for the game. Powered gadget from two AAA batteries.
