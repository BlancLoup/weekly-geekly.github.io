Yota changed CEO

Scartel General Director Anatoly Smorgonsky leaves his post after a year of work due to the expiration of the contract. The place of the head of the company will be occupied by the executive director of MegaFon on business development in the mass market, Mikhail Dubin.



Mikhail Dubin commented on Vedomosti that Megafon is pleased with the results of Scartel, achieved under the leadership of Smorgon. The mobile operator Yota was successfully launched on the market.

According to the source of "Vedomosti", the reason for the non-renewed contract was the dissatisfaction of shareholders with the work of Smorgon: he did not make the fourth federal operator from Yota.

In 2006, Denis Sverdlov and Sergey Adonyev decided to create the first Russian operator working on WiMAX. In the fall of 2008, Scartel was the first in Russia to deploy WiMAX networks in Moscow and St. Petersburg in the 2.5-2.7 GHz band. In 2010, Yota switched to LTE.

In the summer of 2013, MegaFon bought 100% of Scartel. The transaction amount was $ 1.18 billion.

In August 2014, Yota began to provide voice mobile services using the integrated network of Scartel and MegaFon. The Yota tariff feature is unlimited internet traffic. According to ItReviewer sources , in Q1 2015 the number of Yota subscribers is 1.5 million. In March 2015, 4G networks were launched into commercial operation in 39 regions of Russia.
