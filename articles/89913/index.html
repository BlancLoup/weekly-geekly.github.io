Who buys paid content on the Internet? Part 1

Our company ENAZA has been selling digital content (games, music, video, software, Antivirus) on the Internet for 3 years. We work with large Internet providers, for more than 1 million Internet users every month they buy games, videos, software, antiviruses on our portals turbodom.ru , shop.hitv.ru.

Not so long ago, we conducted a large study on how users buy paid content, what they decided to share with readers.

The survey was conducted among those who made at least one purchase on our portals, that is, we investigated the opinion of those who are ready to buy paid content and are not inclined to look for pirates on file sharing sites.


What is paid content?


In order for you, my friend, to understand what kind of content is involved, we will clearly define what we further understand by paid content, it is nothing more than:
  • Games ( casual games , popular games, online games)
  • Music
  • Video
  • Programs
  • Audiobooks
That is the entertainment content that is in demand and for which the user is willing to pay to get it. As well as the content that is now sold on DVD discs.

What have we got?


1. Age of content buyers



75% of paid content buyers are no longer students or schoolchildren, they are solvent network users. And at least 50% of buyers are the older generation, more than 25 years . It is also interesting that 10% of purchases are still made by adults for over 40 years.

2. The presence of a plastic card from buyers.



Important information to understand that the potential buyer has the opportunity to pay for the content. More than 55% of users have plastic cards , respectively, can pay for content almost instantly when the need arises. They do not need to run to replenish the account with Yandex Money or Webmoney, pay for the order in the payment terminals or replenish the account on the mobile phone to pay by SMS.

3. Email availability.



90% of buyers use e-mail - therefore, this method of communication with the buyer is the most effective. Weekly newsletters and notifications of special offers work very well, but the user should be given an opportunity to control the notifications and mailings he receives.

4. Are you ready to buy paid content?



Definitely YES, 56% are ready to buy paid content , if its quality is guaranteed better than that of a pirate with cracks or NO DVD.
