It all started last Thursday. I received a letter in which a woman said that she had to remove my program from her Android, since she has children, and porn banners are shown in the program. I immediately got into the AdMob settings and made sure that advertising for adults is prohibited (it is always prohibited by default). I apologized to the user and wrote a letter to AdMob support asking them to sort it out.

On Friday, I already received two letters stating that porn banners are on the program. Since I did not receive a response from AdMob, I sent them another email.

Saturday was quiet. But I did not receive a response from AdMob.

On Sunday, I received three emails from angry users. Moreover, like the previous three, all were from the United States. In one of the letters it was said that the banner pictures in the style of sado-maso. Of course, I once again wrote in support of AdMob, since I have not yet received a response from them.

Today is Tuesday. I never received a single letter from AdMob, but two more letters from angry users. Last night, after I learned the name of the problem (Flirt), I put a ban on the word Flirt in the AdMob text filter, but this, apparently, did not help.
