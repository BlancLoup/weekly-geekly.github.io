Earnings on cybersquatting

A type of online business that involves registering or acquiring registered domain names is called cybersquatting. Experts are still arguing about the legality of the activities of such businessmen.




Some believe that this type of business is completely legal from the legal side. And some experts claim that such a business is not legal.

The essence of cybersquatting


It is believed that at the beginning of an online business organization and domain registration, the latter must satisfy the following conditions:
  1. It must not be taken by another user to complete the registration process.
  2. It is desirable to have a certain semantic load.


About what cybersquatting is, how it makes money, and other ways of making money on the Internet, we talked to the Megazaboot project team. Cyberscotter is looking for domain names that will be of interest to entrepreneurs when entering the Internet market (or those businessmen who already operate, but want to expand the sphere of influence in the market), with a view to subsequent sale and earnings on the difference between the purchase price and the sale price .

Types of cybersquatting


Over the entire period of the existence of cybersquatting, it has been divided into several directions.

Industry


This type of business involves the registration of domains under the names that correspond to sectors of the economy or certain products. This type is the most practical, since there is a myth, the search engines initially give sites that are consonant with the names of goods and services. Therefore, entrepreneurs prefer this type of domain name, trying to stand out among many others.

Geographical


With this type of cybersquatter chooses a domain name associated with some name of the region. In this case, the profit is not as great as with the industry, but sometimes this method serves as a good source of income. The demand for territorial domains appears during regional business expansion. Names associated with the name of the territory are most important for the tourism business.

Brand and nominal


This type of cybersquatting often becomes a reason for recognizing it as illegal activity. The essence of brand cybersquatting is the assignment of domain names that are consonant with the names of brands (trademarks). When selecting domains for branded cybersquatting, domain names correspond to the names of famous people. You can earn a lot on this type of cybersquatting, but there are a lot of risks. The rightholder can go to court and then the domain will be withdrawn from the cybesquotter. Usually this does not happen, as the legal costs are too high, and it is easier for the right holder to purchase the domain directly. So the question is solved faster and much cheaper.

Typesquatting


This type is not associated with the classic registration of domains for further sale. When typosquatting domain is often difficult to sell, and it is not created for sale. This type is based on the incorrect search by users of known domain names. For example, some recruit pepci.ru instead of pepsi.ru. This is due to the fact that many people do not speak at a proper level English, which is widely used in the formation of the domain name.

Russian legislation is not yet able to recognize the actions of cybersquatting and typosquatting as illegal.
