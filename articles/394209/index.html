E1 M2 level of original Doom reproduced in new Doom IV



I think many readers of Geektimes have already managed to appreciate all the delights of the new Doom, published just a few days ago . The game has a lot of interesting, this applies to both single player and multiplayer. For fans of the latter, developers have added a tool SnapMap . It helps gamers develop their own levels, even on consoles.

The level map editor is quite understandable for the player, you can place objects, enemies and use scripts and triggers, which makes the new levels even more interesting. Users have already started making their cards. One of them is E1 M2 Tribute, a new incarnation of the E1 M2 level of the original Doom. It turned out quite a nothing.
