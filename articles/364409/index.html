Canadian band demands $ 666 thousand from the USA for using their music in Guantanamo

Canadian music band Skinny Puppy billed to the US government in the form of royalties for using music as an instrument of torture in the infamous Guantanamo prison .

On the Kevin Newman Live show, the keyboard player said:
We sent them the bill for our music services because they used music without our permission as a real weapon against someone.




Keyboardist Kevin Key announced that the band planned to post an account to the United States on the album cover, rather than send it. But after they learned that the government allegedly used their music without permission, they decided to file a lawsuit against the Ministry of Defense. He also noted that the members of the group were upset that their music helped harm the prisoners. The use of their music at Guantanamo was reported by the prison guard.

Kevin Key:
I would not want me to listen to loud music for six to twelve hours without a break.

If Skinny Puppy gets paid, musicians such as Metallica, Rage Against The Machine, Queen and Eminem can send bills to the US Department of Defense.
