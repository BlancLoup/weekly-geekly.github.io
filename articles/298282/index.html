Learning structure


Schooling is divided into three stages. At the first 100 students study. Of these, the top 30 fall into the second. On the third pass only 10. I will tell about the first step, because I deliberately did not go to the second and I do not know what is happening there.

The learning process is divided into weekly blocks. Every week text and video materials are laid out on a special school resource. Students study them and pass automated tests by 23:59 Fridays. If a student does not pass tests on time, there is practically no chance of getting to the second level.

First week


The first week of training for me coincided with a trip to Sadhu Sang . The materials seemed to me quite simple, until I began to take tests. In simple, seemingly tests, I could not score more than half of the possible points. The tests on the layout and the negotiations seemed to me particularly difficult: there are a lot of subjective answers. Those. you need to understand how the author of the test thinks in order to pass it successfully. According to the results of the first week, I went down in the ranking somewhere by 10 positions.

Second week


The trip to the south continued and training, too. In the second week, intensive training continued, the materials were piled up and superimposed on each other. I liked one of the teachers, Vladimir Belyaev . He has a tricky, exquisite facial expression and it seems that this is exactly what a lawyer should be. The test results were again disappointing: by the beginning of the third week I dropped to 51 seats.

Third and subsequent weeks


When I arrived in Cherepovets, Anton Sergeevich shared with me an excellent technique. You can print tests and think with paper in hand. It turned out to be very effective: it is possible to express some thoughts on the fields, to postpone a decision, without haste to turn to primary sources. In short, all the benefits of paper media. So over the next couple of weeks the situation in the rating has stabilized and even began to rise slightly.

Benefit



I will reveal here a little bit of what has greatly helped and continues to help.
  • Proximity theory. It is well disclosed in Lebedev and even more fully revealed in the school of Gorbunov. This is about the fact that the closer objects are visually, the greater is the connection between them. From proximity theory follows a number of special cases and rules that are useful to apply in projects.
  • Basic elements of layout. This is about how to make strong layouts from titles, texts, illustrations, signatures, hyperlinks and controls.
  • Layout pages. This topic is devoted to several weeks. From simple text pages to complex pages with many different elements. The section is well studied with Harrover's pre-read book.
  • Build interfaces. This is a whole section that leads Ilya Birman . Here I will note the methods of working with layers, the syntax of interface elements and navigation. We use these techniques every day and thank you for this Ilya.
  • Conversation. The whole discipline is based on the methodology of Jim Kemp and, in fact, the negotiating course is chewing this book with actual examples from the lives of designers and programmers. The course turned out to be very practical advice, for example, if according to your feelings, you are late for a meeting by 5 minutes - call and say that you are late by 10. You will arrive early and the person will be pleased that you arrived earlier. Often people do the opposite: they downplay the delay time, which is just annoying.
  • Design and right led sly Vladimir Belyaev. He told what plagiarism is, licenses, 16+, how to save on the conclusion of contracts, how to register a company and what to do if it is time to go to court.
  • Text and editing turned out to be the most difficult discipline for me. I got the worst grades on her. However, there are also many useful things there, for example, how to write a good promotional page. The Gorbunov bureau has its own invention: the information style. This is the style of writing simple and hedgehog texts. One of the characteristic techniques of the info-style is the removal of stop words .
  • Management and results. For me, the most interesting, relevant and simple section. I found for myself in it very important things. The principle of " do ‚â† do ", FFF and " not butt "
