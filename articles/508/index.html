Investors for everyone

Habrahabrovets lavrenko is creating a regularly reprinted reference book called the Russian Internet Equity Guide, which will tell potential and current investors about new promising sectors of the Internet market, companies, projects, and just good teams.

If you want your company, project or just a team to go to this directory and be noticed by investors - send a brief (non-confidential) information about yourself, your project, team.

It is no secret that the Russian Internet market, on the one hand, is rather opaque, and on the other hand, the number of well-known new Internet projects is rather small. We decided to fill this gap by structuring all our knowledge about the Russian Internet industry into a directory called the Russian Internet Equity Guide. It will help investors to find, if not a ready project for a purchase, then at least a team that can bring revolutionary ideas to life.

I think that many Habrahabroveans would be interested in their project being in this Guide, because having an investor and taking the right negotiating position, and as a result having obtained the investor as an equal partner, and not a stupid boss (because experience of really successful investing in the Internet), much stronger results can be achieved.

Of course, inclusion in this Guide is free, and there are no special requirements. If you want your project or team to be included in the directory - please write. No secret information is required (even the opposite is not welcome, because after inclusion in the directory it will become restrictedly accessible), just send me about the project / team all the information that you think you can about him / her tell without prejudice to the project.

In this topic, I propose to discuss ideas about which company projects could be included in the directory, what interesting information and ideas could be shared. Very welcome links to interesting projects.
