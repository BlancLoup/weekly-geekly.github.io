Clock with a linear time display



Division Furtive. A clock showing the time by means of LEDs (in the improved model, mechanisms with moving pins are used) located in a special lined compartment.



- days of the week
- date
- time zone
- Moon phases

There is a chronometer.

Activation works just like the lights on the G-Shock: a person tilts the clock by about 45 degrees.

Special attention is given to the process of setting the correct time - the clock is put on the smartphone screen. Special software makes the screen blink so that the light sensor on the clock can perceive light signals and synchronize the time on the clock with the time on the smartphone. Epilepsy prone clocks are not recommended, yes.

It is planned to make 2 models (Type40, using diodes, and Type46, using pins).
