AdWhirl - mobile advertising on all pairs



So I decided to share my discovery with the developers of mobile applications for Android and iPhone. AdWhirl is a service of the AdMob company, not so long ago acquired by the evil corporation Google, which specializes in mobile advertising. I think everyone who is somehow connected with mobile advertising should get acquainted with the possibilities of this, in my opinion, wonderful service.



The essence of the service to ingenious is simple. You no longer have to choose between advertising providers such as Admob, Quattro Wireless, JumpTap, etc. Instead, developers are encouraged to integrate the Adwhirl SDK into their applications, which in turn is responsible for distributing advertisements among all connected ad providers, including your own ad, or your own ad provider (!!!). The SDK itself is completely open source, as well as on the site you can find the AdWhirl server with open source and detailed configuration information, which will make it possible to become an advertising provider without much difficulty, unless of course you are confused by its performance exclusively on Amazon Web Cloud.
