The concept of the mechanism for correcting typos

When participating in any discussions on the Internet, you often come across the fact that it is forbidden to edit your own comments on the resource. Obviously, this is due to the fact that no one could refuse to say the words. But sometimes, editing a comment is not required to hide a different degree of shameful text, but simply to correct the silly typos that each person makes.

Sometimes with quick typing, you can confuse a couple of keys, confuse order, forget the letter, well, plus there are also other minor typos, all familiar. There are mistakes and rougher, but often they are explained not by the illiteracy of the author, but by many other factors. Here that would be useful mechanism for correcting typos. Below the cut, I would like to present my simple concept, which, in my opinion, is applicable on any resource.
