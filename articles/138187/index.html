The US Department of Justice approved the purchase of Motorola Mobility by Google



In principle, the "Corporation of Good" can already consider Motorola Mobility as its own unit. The fact is that on Monday it became known about the approval of the transaction by the US Department of Justice. In addition, the European Union also approved this deal. It seems that there will be no more special obstacles, and the critical points have already been overcome. It is worth recalling that the amount of the transaction is rather big even for Google: $ 12.5 billion was paid for Motorola Mobility.

This purchase will allow the corporation, firstly, to use Motorola Mobility patents to their advantage, resisting both patent trolls and some large companies accusing everyone and everything of plagiarism. In addition, the purchased unit will allow Google to manufacture its own devices under its own platform, rather than negotiate with third-party manufacturers. As many of you know, Motorola Mobility is one of the largest manufacturers of Android-based smartphones.

Recent statements made by one of the senior executives of the company make it possible to judge the future of the mobile unit. The statement said that the company, owning such facilities, and having the opportunity to produce its own mobile equipment, will be able to develop an almost ideal device for its operating system. The latter will work with maximum efficiency and impact, so to speak.

Previously, some analysts have expressed concerns regarding this transaction. The fears were not in vain - after all, purchases of this level are sometimes simply forbidden, and then nothing can be done. The acquisition of Motorola Mobility for Google is one of the first attempts to get into the mobile hardware market. It may well be that the actions of the corporation on this new market will entail new lawsuits from competitors. But all this is ahead, but for now, Google representatives may be glad that the most difficult stages of a deal are over.
