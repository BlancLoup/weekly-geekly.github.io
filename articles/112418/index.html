Estimated service Activepay

Good day!

Today I want to share our experience with the payment system Activepay .

Service opened not so long ago, but rather pompous .

The problems began with the fact that the subscriber received the answer ‚Äúaccount is blocked‚Äù for part of the payments by SMS, but the money was still withdrawn from his account.

When contacting Activepay support, there were no answers. We had to manually consider the problems of our customers and add funds to their balance.

Further more. Someone discovered an activepay vulnerability in which you can make a payment for an arbitrary amount via Webmoney, paying only 0.1 WMZ. I did not go into details, but there is a problem in the absence of validation of the actually paid amount in the HTTP request. In the Activepay control panel, all such payments are absolutely correct, so we are now waiting for the receipt of gigantic amounts in our checking account :)

Support, as expected, is not available. On the phone do not answer.

Moreover, Webmoney began to ban the wallets of users making payments for the service through Activepay. We had to immediately abandon this payment gateway.

As it turned out, the service was developed by the well-known innovation company Prime Labs .

Bypass this service side! :)
