So, the task: at the end of the month (or at the end of each day, how someone likes) the employee must fill out a report on the work done.

Solution: create a document form in Google Docs, of the following form:

In the form of the following important elements:
1. Select a full name (a list of all your collaborators)
2. Select a date (unfortunately, Google does not have a form for the date field - but you can simply write it in the format DD-MM-YYYY)
3. Select the type of work (here you will come up with 10-15 types of work that your employee may have)
