1. Key Collector



Everyone has already heard about the service https://wordstat.yandex.ru . But the Key Collector program in the business environment is much less known. In fact, this is the wordstat.yandex.ru parser. But in addition, the program has just a storehouse of interesting "chips" that will be of interest to you. For example:


  1. Parsing search hints.
  2. Collection of requests for which competitors are in the TOP10 (if you have an account, for example in spywords).
  3. Removal of positions.
  4. Definition of a relevant page. You can understand which page of your site leads to a specific request.
  5. Mass determination of the exact frequency of requests. In wordstat (without additional operators) you get the base frequency. If in a simple way, it can be tens of times smaller than the exact occurrences.

2. Automatic site verification services pr-cy.ru, xtool.ru/ and others



They can be used. But do not rely 100% on them.


An approximate use algorithm is as follows:


  1. Drove by pr-cy
  2. Found some kind of mistake
  3. Sent to a specialist, clarified why so

If this is a jamb of a specialist, he will most likely recognize and correct it (in the article I consider adequate specialists). If this is not critical, he will explain. Do not require the contractor to achieve 100% perfect results for these services. Perfectionism is redundant here. Try to drive a couple of giants sites like ozon.ru and see the result. It will be full of errors and shortcomings.


3. Analysis of site visibility



Visibility, if in a simple way, is the volume of requests for which you can be found in search engines from 1 to 100 pages. It often happens that a person checks his site in Yandex for the query "buy crushed stone in balashikha", sees it in the top ten, sees a number of competitors and forms on the basis of this his vision of delivery in general. This is not the right approach. As a rule, in this case one or two requests are regularly "monitored". For the big picture you need site visibility. There are various services for determining this indicator. As an option, https://ru.megaindex.com/visibility . Just enter the website address and see a rough picture.


4. Monitoring site positions
