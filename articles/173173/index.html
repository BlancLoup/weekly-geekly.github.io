Design HTC One

Last week, we introduced you to our new flagship HTC One. HTC One is strong not only technically, but also has a unique design, the creation of which took more than a year and required the efforts of the best specialists of the company.

In this article we will reveal the interesting aspects of creating a smartphone, as well as the ideas put in place by HTC professionals, thanks to which the smartphone appeared before you in the very form that we all admire now.

Being first is always difficult, and creating something truly innovative and unique on the market is doubly difficult. Designers and designers had to solve a number of non-trivial problems. For example, how to make the antenna work inside the metal case? How to create a metal case without a single seam? And finally, how to create a completely metal body from a single piece of aluminum?

Creative Director, Daniel Hundt:
The basic concept was to create a structure without a single gap, without moving parts and a transition between parts, a one-piece body, which is pleasant to hold in your hand.

Jethro Liou, senior director of industrial design, reveals the technical aspects of working on the HTC One and talks about a number of body building steps: electrochemical metal etching, using a special cutter to create grooves on the body, and other details of the work. His words are accompanied by visual frames of these processes.

Daniel explains:
Beauty is born in the joint work of designers and engineers, when they take risks, help and push each other to find the best solution, they try to create something truly innovative.
