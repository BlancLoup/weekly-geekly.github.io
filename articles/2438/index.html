Rambler released Rambler-ICQ 5.1

The Rambler company announced the release of a new version of the Rambler-ICQ 5.1 Internet pager, which combines the traditional messaging and access to the portal services.

The novelty has a rather large number of innovations:

- the opportunity to communicate through flash movies (the settings of the previous version are saved);
- Improved sound quality of voice communication;
- added the ability to play video Rambler-Vision using the service Vision-Xtraz;
- user status can be selected before connecting to the server;
- Added new statuses;
- important Rambler-ICQ menu items can be reached using shortcut buttons;

- there was a display of information about new entries in the diaries of friends, new comments and feedback.

And the most important innovation is the absence of the necessity of compulsory registration at the Rambler. The previous version refused to work if the user did not have his own Rambler account.
