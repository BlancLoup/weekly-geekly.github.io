Workplace in the field

Almost all descriptions of how to create a mobile workspace in the field are reduced to telecommunications and living conditions: how to access the Internet, which devices to place, etc. The fact that all this advanced technological beauty without power supply turns into a pumpkin pile of scrap metal is usually left behind the brackets. Fill this gap.

Mobility = speed

This is why a mobile work point is called that it can be quickly deployed and also quickly minimized. Elimination of accidents, geological exploration, open air events - everywhere you need to act quickly, sometimes the score goes on for minutes.

Mankind has not yet invented a faster way to electrify the site than installing a diesel power station. The diesel power station is compact, it can be quickly brought and taken away after completion of work. Some of them are produced on the chassis, which can be attached to the car. Reliability

Even a progressive rock concert is an extreme job for the organizers. Just because here even the slightest failure is synonymous with losses for many, many millions of rubles. An accidental breaking of the circuit, someone pulled the switch - and now the synchronous generator is demagnetized and the entire diesel power plant fails. The situation, alas, is not uncommon for generators with mechanical regulators of engine rotation, which, to save, many manufacturers continue to install.

To avoid such excesses, choose diesel power plants with electronic power switches equipped with thermal and electromagnetic releasers. The reasoning that the household automatic load device will cope with this will be referred to as amateur. And it is important that the power plant has an electronic engine management system. It will provide better control of the diesel engine during cold start or abrupt overload.

The second aspect of the reliability of a mobile power station is a controller with a protection function. It mates with sensors controlling the operation of a diesel engine and a synchronous generator in order to make an emergency stop when overloaded, overheated, lowered the level of oil, coolant or failure of the generator. Without an emergency stop, the diesel power plant will simply fail in the first emergency situation.

Noise problem

If during the work of emergency services, farms or oil rigs, the problem of noise is secondary, then for urban conditions it comes to the fore. A cheaper option - an open design on a steel frame - is unacceptable here, you need to choose a unit with a noise-protective casing, with it you can reduce the noise level to 65 dB.

terms of Use

When a diesel power station is used as an autonomous power source, it is worth thinking about protection from the external environment. And from the people, of course. More precisely, from the Vandals. To protect against weather conditions, generators are produced in containers, and for protection against vandals, there are options in 20-foot sea containers of increased strength. This protection is enough to safely leave a massive generator on the court.

What else to look for: synchronous and asynchronous generators
In simple terms, the difference between them is this: asynchronous generators are cheaper, but the accuracy of voltage support is about 10%, so they cannot be used to power high-precision equipment. That is, it is extremely undesirable to hold concerts with them or to feed the scientific equipment.

A synchronous generator in a diesel power station maintains voltage with an accuracy of 4-5%. They easily cope with the supply of power tools and electric motors, in which the reactive load reaches 65% of the nominal value.

Tricks of picking

So, you have decided on the parameters of a diesel power station, but when you buy you will find one unobvious trick. Many companies indicate an attractive price, modestly silent that it does not include mufflers and batteries, putting them in the category of additional options. But this is the same as selling a car, and offering wheels to it as an option. Therefore, when calculating a diesel power station, consider its cost along with a silencer, batteries, and also readiness for work: the station is filled with oil and all process fluids. Diligent seller indicates the price only in the complete set.
