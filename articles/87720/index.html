Win a ticket to an international technology symposium at Stanford University!

Global Techology Symposium, the BIT competition and the Yunova portal have agreed on cooperation. Now the readers of Yunov can get on the GTS for free.

How? It's simple - leave your request on the website www.unova.ru in the comments to the article by Alexandra Johnson - write down who you are and why you should get the lucky ticket from UNOVA. At the end of the week we will pass the names of the lucky ones to the organizing committee of the GTS.
More about the conditions of the promotion

GTS (Global Technology Symposium, Stanford University, USA). The annual International Technology Symposium at Stanford University is the leading conference on technology trends and investments, where much attention is traditionally paid to Russia and the countries of Eastern Europe. The target audience of the event is the leading investors in the United States and developing countries, top managers of high-tech companies, business angels, start-ups. The list of speakers includes well-known experts in the field of technology, business and politics. Official website of the symposium - www.globaltechsymposium.com
