Free e-book on design patterns in Ukrainian

Habravchane, allow you to present my small and modest work on the design patterns in the Ukrainian language. Sorry in advance if you are not from Ukraine.



Description
The book "Design patterns - just like doors" is a free Ukrainian-language book containing unique examples of design patterns. Thanks to her non-standard presentation of the material, it will allow you to easily master the basics of understanding the design of patterns or systematically and very quickly repeat them before the interview. The method of writing the book will allow you to spend the time allotted for its reading, without boredom, and sometimes even fascinating.

Refresh in your memory the forgotten design patterns!

Download a book

In a few words
I wrote the book with good intentions. I had several of them. I wanted to make sure I understood all the classic design patterns myself. There are a lot of resources for this, but I decided to implement these patterns on my own and invent my own examples. Thus, starting from January 16, 2010, I wrote blog posts, which somehow aroused interest among readers. To make this contribution to programming more clear, I had a desire to release a small book that would become a collection of these blog posts.

Visit the book site: http://designpatterns.andriybuday.com/
