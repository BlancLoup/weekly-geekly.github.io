Easterlin Paradox


There is a remarkable law formulated by Richard Easterlin (Richard Easterlin) in 1974 in his article “Does it improve the human lot? Some empirical evidance »


In English literature this law is called Easterlin paradox . But I, as a person of Russian culture, do not see any paradox ... a completely expected result, simply confirmed by research. Therefore, I propose to translate into Russian the “Easterlin paradox” as “Easterlin Law”


The growth of absolute, but not relative, income does not lead to an increase in life satisfaction
