Startup "auto parts PartMaster"

After more than six months of work on the design, development and improvement of the project PartMaster ( http://partmaster.com.ua ), finally launched in beta testing site: an online store and a catalog of auto parts with delivery.



Of the advantages: an automatic system for maintaining orders, money, bills, invoices. We also accept cash, bank transfer, webmoney. The plans include the provision of web services for posting our information on other sites and programs, an automatic service for depositing funds through online payment systems and plastic cards.

After some time, testing the site's engine on real orders - this will be one of the advanced resources of Ukraine and Russia for supplying auto parts directly from a supplier from OAU, Evropa, and Japan.

So, if you need a spare part for Auto - register on our website and order it. Our managers will do their best to make you satisfied with the work with us.
