Appiny - discounts for geeks!

Hello!
At the beginning of Augustus, Habr very positively met the idea of creating a discount service for geeks. Since then, more than 3 months have passed ...
After the publication, not only more than 1,500 people registered at once wishing to receive discounts on various products and services, but also the first partners and investors began to knock on us.
We did not disappear anywhere, and all this time we worked hard on launching Appiny , which we will try to tell about under the cut.

Attracting partners


Here we are faced with the fact that it was quite expected ...
When the potential of the market is unknown, nobody has heard about you, you have not yet had sales and, accordingly, there is no analytics for building sales forecasts, they look at your message very skeptically. It was especially difficult to negotiate with very large software companies. No wonder 37Signals in its sensational: Rework write that big companies are very slow :)
They swing very long.
But small companies turned out to be on the contrary more mobile and open to new distribution channels for their products, and perceived our offer much better.

One of these companies, Sparrow , which developed an excellent mail client for Mac OS X, can now buy it from us for just 192r. (with 40% discount)
