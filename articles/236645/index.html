8 Letter with reviews



What are you looking at first when you are considering purchasing a product on Amazon? Of course, these are reviews.
Social proof is not a new concept, but Amazon is the first company to prioritize and evaluate it. Every time someone makes a purchase, the site asks the client to leave a review. The latter, in turn, helps other buyers to make purchases wisely. All this improves customer service.
Notice that Amazon is very specific about what kind of feedback it needs. ‚ÄúHow fit is this size?‚Äù

A simple click on the desired review automatically adds information to the product page:



The user is redirected to a page where he can leave a review or set a rating on a five-star scale. Amazon, thanks to the help of people to each other, was able to evaluate this process and get a profit as an intermediary (by the way, we have already translated the article about how the feedback affects the conversion).

A nice addition to all this is the funny reviews that some people leave. Amazon actually tracks them and publishes the funniest ones.



This is a win-win.
