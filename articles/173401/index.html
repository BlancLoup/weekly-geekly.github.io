ImageCMS 4.3 release

Hello, habra users!
In this publication we will share news about the next release of our development: ImageCMS 4.3.

Work on this version has become a record in duration over the past six months - we have been preparing for release for a month and a half; also first conducted beta testing. Most of all, attention was paid to such aspects as speed and convenience in controlling the engine, absolutely all comments and recommendations from our users, customers and members of the forum were taken into account.
Highlights can be succinctly stated in the following paragraphs.

ImageCMS Shop version :
1. Changed the display of goods in the administrative part, made it more convenient.
2. Significantly changed the approach to creating templates:
  • reworked all the pages with the new layout, detailed all the narrow moments;
  • developed an adaptive system pattern using the Twitter Bootstrap framework.

3. In order to simplify work with the template, we added new widgets for the online store:
  • previously viewed products;
  • popular goods;
  • brands widget.

In addition, we have thought over the system of working useful blocks for further use.
4. Corrected the logic of the following modules:
  • Orders
  • Follow the price
  • Kolbeki
  • Filter products in the category
  • Currencies
  • Delivery Methods
  • Payment Methods
  • Similar products

5. Created a detailed step-by-step instructions on creating an online store, also updated the documentation .
