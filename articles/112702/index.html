Microsoft (MSFT): second quarter of 2011 - the most profitable in the industry

Disclaimer: This post was written by me as a private person. This post is part of a series of articles on IT company reporting.

The IT industry is growing rapidly after the crisis year, giving record numbers among all companies. Today Microsoft summed up the results of its second quarter of the fiscal year. The main questions before this were about whether the company will be able to maintain its growth rate and whether it will be possible to stay ahead of Apple in terms of net profit, especially after the new peaks of the latter. Microsoft succeeded in both.

In the second quarter, the company showed a record $ 19.95 billion in revenue, a growth of 15% over the year, and a net profit of $ 6.63 billion . Profit remains the highest in the industry, the main engine of which during this Christmas period was primarily the 8 million Kinect gaming devices sold. In addition, Microsoft set its new record of $ 0.77 in earnings per share and showed the sixth consecutive quarter growth operating margin.

As usual, there is a publicly available press release of the company, a conversation with investors, slides and digital indicators .

Today's conversation with investors was on duty, which was especially noticeable after announcements about new appointments last week with competing friends Apple and Google . Microsoft CTO Peter Kline led the discussion, and analysts' questions were only in the financial plane.

A brief report on the departments of the corporation:
  1. Windows and Windows Live

    This quarter is the year of the Windows 7 operating system. The main results are more than 300 million licenses sold and more than 20% of installations on computers connected to the Internet. 90% of organizations have at least begun the formal process of transition to a new OS.

    WWL's revenue was $ 5.05 billion, which is higher than the previous three quarters, but lower than the result of a year ago - then in connection with the launch of the product, the figure was $ 7.19 billion

    Traditionally, there is an overview of the entire PC market as a whole - the last quarter showed a new record of 90 million units.

    The business segment of new computers is growing faster than mass, the share of netbooks is decreasing due to new devices, laptops continue to grow at the same pace.
