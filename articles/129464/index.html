Record live video from iPad

purpose


Record live video gameplay iOS game.
As I have not tried to do this before, nothing good came of it.
The most difficult thing in this process is to make the camera and the device to be removed fixed.
As a result of several experiments, I could not do something less digestible, so the video was shot from the Windows version of my application.
But the other day, the question of having a live video came up very sharply and an uber system was invented for recording live video from the iPad.

Elements of the system


  • Stool
  • 2 sheets a4
  • iPad
  • Recorder (I used iPod Touch 4)
  • Glue
  • Scissors


A photo


1 - Stool
2 - iPad
3 - A sheet of paper with a slot under the camera iPod Touch 4, stretched between the struts of the stool


In the picture, the iPod Touch4 itself, which I wrote down, will advise (I took this picture on it); in the photo you can see a slot in the paper

In steps:
  • Take two sheets of paper and glue them
  • Cut a hole in the middle
  • We fix this paper on a stool (if the stool can be removed from the top, it will be even more convenient)
  • Put the recorder on paper so that his camera sees the iPad
  • We adjust the location of the iPad on the table so that the picture suits you
