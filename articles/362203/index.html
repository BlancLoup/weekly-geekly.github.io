Lumia 830


Smartphone Lumia 830 can be called a profitable alternative to the flagship device Lumia 930 . Especially for those who can replace the battery and expand (in our case as much as 128 GB) memory is more important than having a top-end processor and screen. But even in these parameters, the junior flagship is not much inferior to the head of the family: the smartphone is equipped with a 1.2-GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor with an Adreno 305 graphics chip, 1 GB of RAM and a five-inch IPS display with a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels (pixel density is 296 pixels /inch).
