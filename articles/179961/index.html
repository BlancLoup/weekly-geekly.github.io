iOS Saturday in Vinnitsa

Great news for developers of mobile applications in Vinnitsa!
On May 18th, the Saturday subbotnik of the Siclum company will be held for everyone!



Subbotics of Siklum dedicated to one or another technology stack or platform have been going on throughout Ukraine and abroad for several years. These events bring together professionals to share knowledge of ideas and professional networking, including between cities.

And this time we have some interesting speakers who will share their experiences with the participants:
1) Vladimir Kirillov, technical architect, Universal Mind (Kiev), hacker.
Subject: iOS Toolchain
I plan to make an excursion into the world of compiling and building iOS projects, and you will find out why the projects are simply collected on your MacBook, but it takes several hours to build them on CI. I will also tell you how to build without using Xcode.


2) Alexey Demedetsky, iOS developer, Ciklum Project Office
Topic: Advanced debugging techniques on iOS
I will share how to stop using NSLogs and breakpoint as the main weapon against bugs.

3) Andrei Kozak, developer Xamarin (Android), Ciklum (Vinnitsa)
Topic: Multiplatform development
I will present the Xamarin framework and demonstrate how to quickly develop multiplatform C # applications.

Participation is traditionally free, register here: goo.gl/Jpsjm
Start at 11:00
Venue: Office Siklum in Vinnitsa, Cathedral, 24

We are waiting for you this Saturday!
