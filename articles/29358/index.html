New level

Hello!

I think that St. Petersburg freelancers have heard more than once or twice about the New Level co-working center, but I would venture to write this post as well.

The fact is that we decided to offer you to use New Level as a platform for combining, as well as for receiving orders and assistance in work. I'll try to tell in order.

Union


I think that it will not be a revelation that many freelancers often need partners working in related fields (such as, for example, the situation with designers and layout makers), however, not everyone has them, they are not always free, and the quality of their work not always satisfied. Therefore, we suggest that you unite efforts under the auspices of New Level - the more people unite, the more choice there will be, the more total experience there will be, the better it will be for everyone.
In particular, it will be easier to assemble teams for large projects.
