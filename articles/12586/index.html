Ergonomic Logitech Keyboards

Logitech has announced the release of new Cordless Desktop Wave and Keyboards with increased ergonomics. The design of the device is made on the new technology Logitech Comfort Wave, which (according to the statement of Logitech), will significantly increase the comfort and even the productivity of users.


Innovations in the design of keyboards for three. This is a wavy form of the keyboard itself, a U-shaped curve with a constant radius and a soft contour lining under the hands. Due to the use of these elements, hands on the keyboard are located in a natural position, which provides convenience and comfort when typing.

On the technology of Wave Comfort "bonuses" of new keyboards do not end. They are equipped with several large in size and convenient "hot keys". With the help of special software, they can be configured to open directories, web pages and applications such as photo galleries and music players. If you have already installed Vista, these keys can be used to launch functions such as, for example, Flip 3D or Windows Sidebar.

As for the Logitech Cordless Desktop Wave, then this kit includes not only a wireless keyboard, but also a mouse into the load. The manipulator style copies the keyboard (for matching). Both devices are equipped with a battery charge indicator.
