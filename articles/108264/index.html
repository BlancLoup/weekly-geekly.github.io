Samsung Galaxy Tab. Unboxing and first impressions

I got not so long ago for tests Samsung Galaxy Tab - almost the only worthy tablet today on Android 2.2 OS with a capacitive screen. I plan to make a number of materials dedicated to this device - if it will be interesting to the audience. Sorry for the quality of photos - shot with your Nexus One
