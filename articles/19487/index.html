Yandex in 2007 - an increase of 130%

The Internet, January 29, 2008. Yandex summed up the preliminary financial results of 2007. Compared with 2006, revenues increased by about 130%.

The main source of income for the company is still contextual advertising. The ratio of contextual and banner (media) advertising in the revenue structure of Yandex for the year has not changed.
At the same time, in 2007, for the first time, a significant portion of revenue was obtained from the sale of advertisements on thousands of third-party sites participating in the Yandex Advertising Network.

In 2007, the search engine and portal yandex.ru turned 10 years old. During this year, Yandex launched a new search platform, Yandex.Fotki and satellite map search, significantly updated the image search and localized a number of its services for Ukraine, including search (yandex.ua). Thanks to the team transferred from Smartcom, Yandex has a new direction - mobile services (mobile.yandex.ru). In the spring, Yandex acquired a social network for professionals - Moy Krug (moikrug.yandex.ru). In the fall, the Yandex Local Network (local.yandex.ru) was announced - a program to improve access to all Yandex services from the regions of Russia and the CIS. Classes began at the School of Data Analysis, an educational institution created by Yandex for the training of specialists in the field of data analysis and fundamental scientific research.

Today, Yandex offices located in six cities have more than 1,000 employees, and their number continues to grow.
