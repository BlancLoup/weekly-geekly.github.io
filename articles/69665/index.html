Radio frequency processing stages (wi-fi, wi-max):

Immediately I warn in the article describes the process of registration of radio frequencies for the use of radio equipment outside buildings.


1) Obtaining a private Decision on the use of the frequency band

If you plan to use frequencies other than those for which there is a generalized solution of GKRCH (there is at 2.4 GHz, 40.5-43.5 GHz, 2.3-2.5 GHz in some cases). The terms are very long - up to six months and more.

2) Obtaining the conclusion of the GRRC review:

Once you have decided on the frequencies, you need to determine the equipment and locations of the BS and send the materials for examination to the FSUE "Main Radio Frequency Center". The conclusion of the examination is made out on specific points of placement and then you can no longer drag and drop your BS. Terms of 3 months or more.

3) Obtaining licenses for the provision of communication services using the radio frequency spectrum

If you plan to provide broadband radio access, you need to obtain licenses for telematic communication services and data transmission services using the radio frequency spectrum.

National tax:

Upon receipt of a new license:

- for consideration of an application for a license - 300 rubles.

- for the provision of a license using the radio frequency spectrum - 15,000 rubles. -

Dates 70-90 days.

4) Obtaining permission to use radio frequencies in the Federal Service for Supervision in the Field of Communications.

After you have an opinion on the HRCHT and a communication license, you must get permission to use radio frequencies. The deadline for obtaining permission is 120 days.

5) Registration of RES

In addition, after obtaining permission to use radio frequencies, you need to register your RES in the Territorial Administration of Roskomnadzor at the location.

Timing compared with all the other instances you have passed is a small 1-2 weeks.
