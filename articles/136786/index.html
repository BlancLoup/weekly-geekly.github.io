Yevgeny Savin: How to improve the Russian innovation ecosystem?

January 24 at 19.00 in St. Petersburg, in the QD business incubator will be held open discussion with Yevgeny Savin.

Evgeny Savin is the director of UNOVA Media, the leading Russian news resource in the field of innovation and venture investment.

Topics for discussion:
  • Let's talk about the Russian ecosystem: what is happening now, in what direction and how to develop it.
  • Let's compare Moscow and St. Petersburg in terms of the development of innovations, venture investments and start-up movements.
  • We will discuss what the necessary minimum needs to be implemented in order to improve the development of entrepreneurship in Moscow, St. Petersburg and the regions.

The event will be held as part of the QD Startup Speaker Series with the support of Game Changers and UNOVA Media .
We invite you to participate in the meeting!
Venue address: St. Petersburg, Birzhevaya Line, 14.

Attention! Participation requires mandatory registration on the event page .
