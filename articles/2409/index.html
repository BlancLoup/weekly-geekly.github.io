32.7% of Belarusians use the Internet

The Belarusian consulting center Satio conducted a sociological survey, which was attended by 1,500 respondents in all regions of Belarus.

According to the survey, it turned out that Internet users in Belarus are 37.2% of adult residents. Among the urban population, 38.1% of respondents use the Internet, and 15.8% among the rural population. Most often, Minsk residents use the Internet (62.3%), as well as residents of Minsk (37%), Grodno (32.6%) and Gomel (30.8%) regions. The share of Internet users among men and women is 36.9% and 29.2%, respectively.

The most famous sites among Belarusians are Tut.By (9.6% of the country's population), Yandex.ru (7.6%), Mail.ru (6.9%), Rambler.ru (6.0%) and Google ( 1.6%).
