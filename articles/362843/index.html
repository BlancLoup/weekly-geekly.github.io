Lumia 535 - the first Microsoft Lumia smartphone

In the line of our smart phones of the new generation (and this, we recall, smartphones based on Windows Phone 8.1) today is a landmark addition. In the morning, Lumia 535 was announced - the first smartphone under the Microsoft Lumia brand.



The novelty has a really good value for money / technical characteristics and a bright design - qualities that have already become the hallmark of Lumia models. A smartphone with a 5-inch screen and two 5-megapixel cameras (main and front) will be offered on the Russian market at an estimated price of $ 7,990. And now about everything in order.

Lumia 535 is not a continuation of the model Lumia 530, as one might think, but a kind of step aside: an entry-level smartphone for users who care about having a large screen and good cameras for taking regular photos and self portraits.
