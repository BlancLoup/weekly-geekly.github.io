Google Tasks



Not so long ago, Google announced the launch of a separate service - Google Tasks. However, before that, it existed in various forms in Gmail, Google Calendar.
Now this service is available from the phone.
But what to do with the browser version? A separate service has not officially appeared, and every time it is not always convenient to enter the mail or calendar.
Now this is not a problem. There was an expansion for chromium. It is a pop-up window in which your entire todo list is stored. While you do not need it, it peacefully hangs in a minimized form around the clock. With a simple movement of the hand, this little strip turns into a sheet of your affairs, which you can edit.

Gmail checker


Google has its own extension for Gmail. But it is not very informative, and it is not located in the most convenient place.
There is an alternative. Gmail Checker. It is located to the right of the address bar, displays the number of unread letters, you can see a list of these letters.

Vkontakte Toolbar


A small extension that shows the number of unread messages, photos in which you were marked, and the number of friends who requested "friendship." All updates are accompanied by a beep.

Cleeki


Very convenient extension. Works on the principle of "accelerators" in IE8. Select the desired text, an extension icon appears next to the cursor. Click on it, choose where you want to search for this fragment. Google, YouTube, translator, eBay, Amazon, well, many more places. There is a good extension customization system.
