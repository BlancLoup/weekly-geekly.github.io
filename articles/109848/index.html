The competition will be held in three online rounds, after which on March 11, 2011 the final will be held at the central office of Facebook. Registration begins December 20, and all three rounds will be held in January.

To begin with, a qualifying round will be held, which will begin on January 7, 2011 at 0:00 UTC and end on January 10, 2011 at 0:00 UTC. During these 72 hours, participants must solve at least one of the three proposed tasks in order to be admitted to the first round.
