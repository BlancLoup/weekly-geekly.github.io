Interesting video viewing experience: instead of VR - glasses with mirrors Ximmerse Visor-X

VR headsets, of course, make life more interesting: some content with them is perceived much more interesting. Even videos from Youtube, becoming a bit larger, literally take you to your personal, small, cozy cinema.

But the VR headset is full of flaws. We tried an alternative way - a helmet for Ximmerse Visor-X smartphones.



And he is full of virtues!

For a start, I want to honestly say: at home there are VR glasses, and not very often, but you have to resort to them. For a change. During the use of helmets, some claims against them emerged:

  • It's hard to watch for a long time, eyes get tired
  • No matter how the construction is arranged in terms of air flow, the space inside is still closed - part of the face is sweating
  • Even listening without headphones, you practically lose touch with reality (it is, of course, logical, but not always safe)
  • There are also restrictions on the use of helmets for people who wear glasses, but this is not our case. Bye pah-pah
  • Without a special controller, access to control the smartphone is closed

Ximmerse Visor-X helmet solves all these problems. It is also designed to be worn on the head, also provides a larger screen and immersive effect, but in a slightly different way.



At its core, it is a system of mirrors, one of which is before the user‚Äôs eyes, displaying a slightly curved display.



It is regulated, it can be set at the right level.



Everything is transmitted to the screen not directly from the display - but from the second mirror: it is inside.

As a result, you will have something like an enlarged screen in front of your eyes, and the viewing experience will be comparable to bulky videos with VR glasses.



But at the same time:

  • Eyes are not so tired, although the distance to the screen and small: about 50 mm
  • The space is not closed, you are still in the real world, you can react to external factors
  • Face doesn't sweat
  • Access to the smartphone is open: you can pause viewing
  • Users who wear glasses have fewer problems.

Separately, such a helmet is positioned in terms of convenience: in principle, it does not dictate a pose.
