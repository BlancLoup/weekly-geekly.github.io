With a flick of the wrist, the tablet turns into ... an additional monitor

After the publication of the topic with photos of workplaces habrovchan , I still waited for the reaction to the "Easter egg" in the photos of my cluttered workplace, namely questions like: "What is this tablet with Windows and why are there such small icons on it?"

Looking ahead, I would note that I would rate the level of comfort from using the program as 80-85%, but alternative solutions from the well-known AirDisplay and other manufacturers disappointed me a lot more.

As with any other additional monitor, the working area can be expanded to a second monitor or the image can be mirrored. To work, the program must be installed both on the tablet / smartphone and on the laptop / desktop. Well, both devices are located in the same Wi-Fi network.

At this stage, I am faced with completely unexpected difficulties.
