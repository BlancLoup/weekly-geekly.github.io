DropBox re-distributes to students / teachers



It has long been DropBox did not give out space for free to all cunning guys from the snowy Russia. Now we will get acquainted with the conditions and get 25 GB for 2 years.



To get started, read the terms of the action.

You need to register with an email in the domain of the school from a certain list. Or verify existing email on your account. I did not find the list of allowed educational institutions.
Your school receives 1 point for each registered in the action.
Your school will receive 2 points for each past Get Started .

Bonuses
For registration / verification of email you get 3GB of space for 2 years.
When registering a large number of participants from your school, these participants receive additional space (up to 22GB). For each admitted school, 3 points are set, at which space is added to the vault. The first threshold is 5GB, the second is 7GB, and the third is 10 GB.

Directly on the promotion page, it is proposed to enter a correct email, as well as see the top 10 educational institutions on points.

The campaign continues until December 10, so we have some time to find a way to get extra gigabytes.
