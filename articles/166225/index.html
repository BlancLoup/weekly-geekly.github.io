Freelance: order delivery and new rubricator

Hello! Under the cut is information about the update for those who are in full freelance or just about to start doing it.



Delivery of new orders
Shortly after launching, Freelansim acquired an RSS feed (content can be customized after registration), but agree, this tool is not as convenient and effective as notifications about new orders.


Website layout changed for screenshot

Go to the subscription settings and specify the sections that interest you. Then we select the appropriate type of distribution, there are two of the available:

Daily delivery. An option for those who have remote work is not the main income, but secondary. Letters with new orders will come once a day, without tiring their obsession.

Instant delivery. This is when the order has just been placed, and you already see it in your mail and, for example, by notification on the phone.





The cost of instant delivery is 1 fan wrapper per month, but you will be among the first to know about the new order, which may become a competitive advantage (especially if the order is urgent).

Update rubricator specializations
You sent us your wishes for improving the rubricator and we tried to take them into account as much as possible, but so that the rubricator itself did not grow to immense sizes.
