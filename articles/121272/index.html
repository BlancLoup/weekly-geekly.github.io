Game section on the 27th Point in Moscow in conjunction with Alawar Entertainment

Dear game developers,

StartupPoint is pleased to inform you about the start of cooperation of the startup community and the largest game publisher Alawar Entertainment!

StartupPoint will select promising startups dedicated to the development of desktop, mobile and social games for Alawar. Within the framework of cooperation with start-ups, Alawar offers two options for interaction - investing in the most promising development studios and publishing individual game projects.

As a publisher, Alawar plans to support young teams in both creating and distributing games for a mass audience, and in financing projects. Alawar will help to find the necessary resources for the project at an early stage of development, at the disposal of the projects also - qualified support of the company's production center in the field of game design, genre and technical features of the development. Work on the distribution and promotion of the game is included in the functions of Alawar as a publisher.

A distinctive feature of cooperation on the publishing model is the opportunity for a start-up to maintain independence. Within the framework of cooperation with StartupPoint, Alawar is ready to consider a closer integration of a startup into the company's current business with the provision of investments to the most promising development teams.

To participate in the selection of representatives of the strap-up, you must perform three simple steps:
  • Register a startup at StartupPoint.ru. Please note that it is possible to hide the project and not display it in the startup directory.
  • Get moderated by StartupPoint analytics
  • Participate in communication with representatives of Alawar Entertainment


You can show your project Alawar on June 23 in Moscow. StartupPoint holds a special gaming section on the 27th Pointe . To participate, click on the green "I will go" button on the event page!

Point is a discussion platform for entrepreneurs, activists who want to start their own business, experts and investors. Here you will always meet new people, find a partner, meet with investors and industry experts.
