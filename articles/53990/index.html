VKMusic - music and video easily and quickly.

A couple of months ago I was looking for the most convenient way to download music from the site vkontakte.ru. I think many people know what a huge archive of musical works has accumulated there thanks to users.



I found dozens of websites, browser plugins, but everything was ‚Äúsomething not very good‚Äù. And so, by chance, I came across an excellent VKMusic program ( http://vkmusic.citynov.ru/ )

Even if you have a powerful allergy to vkontakte.ru and social networks in general, still pay attention to this program :)



Enter:


We open the program, it asks for our login and password from Vkontakte.ru. If you are afraid for your account, you can get a "left", the program is poboku. From myself I note - the program in the ugly behavior regarding my account has not been noticed.



Are looking for:


Entered Enter the name of the desired song, or artist, click "Search". The program displays a list of found tracks with the specified name. Including all kinds of remixes, if any. You can sort the positions found by the number of file options found, name, the presence of words in id3 tags. We click on the desired composition, click "Add to download."



Choose the right:


After all the desired files have been added to the download list, we start the download. The program makes a selection of 50 files for each song, as a result you can filter out the trimmed (or vice versa, extended) options, choose the bitrate. After selecting, the song is downloaded to the folder specified in the settings, with the name of the form "Artist - name (bitrate) .mp3"



Artist Info


I really liked this feature. After searching for the song, click on the desired option and on the i button. Shows a photo artist, description, similar artists, the best albums and the most popular songs. If you click on an album, it opens its olozhku and the list of songs in it, by clicking on the song - adds it to the download.



Download video


The program also allows you to download videos from youtube, rutube, and, of course, vkontakte. When searching on VKontakte, only a list of videos is given, when searching on youtube, there is also a time, and on rutube - the name, time and size of the video. Sorry, sorting the list does not work, but oh well.



Conclusion


The program is great for downloading individual tracks, albums, unfortunately, using it is difficult to download. There are several errors in usability and simply flaws, which, nevertheless, do not interfere with the use of the program for its intended purpose.

Thanks to the author. I hope that the program will be updated regularly.
