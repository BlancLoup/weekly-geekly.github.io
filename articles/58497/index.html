Django Dash 2009 competition

Django Dash - competition for developers on Dzhanga. You are given only 48 hours to develop a web application! An excellent reason to try yourself in high-speed development.

rules


  • Blanks are prohibited
  • 48 hours
  • Maximum 3 people in command
  • You can use someone else's code (no more than 50% of the total)
  • Version Control System (Subversion) is hosted by the organizers.


schedule


Start registration May 2
Registration end May, 23rd
Start of competition May 30
End of competition May 31
Start of judging May 31
End of judging June 13
Winners announced June 13


Winners 2008



1st place - Aardvarkia


  • Benjamin pollack
  • Tyler hicks-wright

A text analysis tool integrated with Mercurial.

2nd place - Team Awesome


  • Eric Florenzano
  • Tony haber

RSS aggregator with ratings.

3rd place - Django Locales


  • Justin lilly

The site unites developers and translators, with the ability to evaluate translations and unite in groups
