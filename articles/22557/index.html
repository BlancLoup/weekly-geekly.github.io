HDPC All-in-One

MIU will soon launch on the Korean market its new portable hybrid device - HDPC (Hybrid Dual Portable Computer), which is a cross between a phone, a communicator and a PDA / UMPC. In addition to these functions, it can also serve as an MP3 / MP4 player, navigator, electronic dictionary, portable TV, etc. In addition, the user can even choose the operating system to his liking - Windows XP and Linux Qplus, because the device supports both .

Below are the technical specifications:

- processor: 500 MHz / 1 GHz VIA C7M ULV, 4 GB DDR2 memory;
- OS: Windows XP / Windows CE 5.0 or Linux Qplus;
- 4-inch, 800 x 480 touch retractable internal display;
- 1.3-inch 30/60 GB hard drive (or 4 GB SSD);
- miniSD / microSD slot;
- 802.11b / g WiFi;
- Bluetooth 2.0;
- 3 or 5 megapixel camera;
- full QWERTY-keyboard, two USB-ports, stereo speakers;
- DMB-TV and external GPS-receiver;
- 7 hours of battery life with intensive use and 90 hours - in stand-by.

This whole thing weighs a little, 387 g with a size of 163 x 67 x 24.5 mm. Its cost will be about $ 500. and it will be available in July this year.
