Night programmer. April 4-5, Nizhny Novgorod. IoT hakaton Microsoft and Intel


What can you do on Saturday night in April? There are plenty of options, of course, but we offer another one - probably the most original one. Just one night, from 4 to 5 April, Microsoft, with the participation of Intel, is hosting an intellectual party for programmers - a hackathon to develop solutions for the Internet of things based on Intel Galileo platform and Microsoft Azure cloud service.

Coding all night long in the company of like-minded enthusiasts - is this not happiness for a true developer?
Bring a laptop with the necessary development tools with you, a creative idea, so as not to waste time, and of course your head, and come to us.
Galileo is not necessary to bring - they will provide everyone. If you do not have special knowledge or have never worked with microcontrollers, our experts will help you to master this area and make your first IoT project.



The best 3 projects will receive prizes of the organizers, additionally will be awarded the prize of the company Intel.
Hackathon held at NSTU. Alekseeva (st. Minin, 24, building 1), beginning April 4 at 20:00.
In order to participate in the hackathon, you must register .
