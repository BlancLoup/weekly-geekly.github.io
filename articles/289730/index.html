AliExpress will open a representative office and store user data in the Russian Federation.

It became known about the meeting of employees of AliExpress, a member of the Alibaba holding, with the deputy. Head of Roskomnadzor Antonina Priezzhevoy. The representative of AliExpress in Russia and the CIS, Mark Zavadsky, along with the technical director of the company Dunbai Go was at the meeting.

Following the meeting, Alibaba announced the opening of an official representative office and the future storage of data on Russian users on the territory of the Russian Federation in order to meet the requirements that will come into force on September 1, 2015 under the Federal Law 242.
