International Information Protection Day


Although today is not Friday - but there is a reason to celebrate the professional feast of many Habra users.

History reference:
The holiday began to exist in 1998 (there is even a website since the holiday) because In 1988, the first mass epidemic of the worm, named after its creator, Morris, was recorded. The holiday exists and is recognized internationally thanks to the American Computer Equipment Association.

The purpose of this Day is to remind everyone of the need to protect computer information, and also to draw the attention of manufacturers and users of hardware and software to security problems.

PS It's funny when professional holidays coincide with birthday. I remember that I'm not the only one here.
