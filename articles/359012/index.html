Webinar: Planning Apache Ignite Cluster Capacity with Live Examples

In the previous post, we looked at fundamental approaches to estimating the capacity of a cluster and talked quite a bit about optimization. For those who like to look under the hood, Alexey Goncharuk will hold a webinar with live examples on May 29 :

  • Where does overhead come from when writing data;
  • Optimization techniques;
  • How to plan the capacity of the Apache Ignite cluster;
  • Improvements that await you in the next releases.

The webinar will be interesting for those who plan to use Apache Ignite in a real project and want to evaluate the hardware configuration or the amount of memory for storing a given amount of source data in Ignite.

We are waiting for you online on May 29 at 19:00 (Moscow time).
Registration is required .
