Symbiosis of ideas: examples of good combination of ideas in the design of logos and advertising

Hello, habr! Today I want to introduce to you all the dose of design inspiration for the brain.

The point is that, considering the good fruits of associative thinking, combining several ideas into one, to awaken these processes more strongly in your head.

What is it for


In our world, the successful combination of already existing ready-made things, ideas and methods is of great value. People easily recognize separate parts familiar to them in such a connection and, at the same time, perceive the whole as something interesting, new and unusual.

It is difficult to invent something useful and necessary from scratch, using extremely low-level data. From the point of view of experience, this is certainly useful, but often this is not required for those simple things that you have to deal with.

A strong idea + a simple (but, naturally, qualitative!) Implementation is no less effective than a complex implementation of the highest level with a familiar idea. And, in fact, a strong fresh idea can be found by combining the usual.

In short, more to the point. Only two global parts: ideological logos and ideological advertising.
