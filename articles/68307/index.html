Discussion: The Perfect Smartphone - What would you like to see in it?

Friends, many of us own smartphones. Someone still uses Vinmobile, someone moved to more modern Iphone Os or Android ...
Under the smarts there are already tens of thousands of programs written, but something is always missing ... Or maybe something is missing in the smarts themselves ...
So I put the question: What would I like to see in the "perfect" smartphone? What functions would I like it to perform?
In many ways, these reflections are more from the realm of science fiction, but given the speed of development of technology, I think that most of my wishes, at least, are quite feasible even with the current level of technology. I proceed from at least my preference to have 1 device that ‚Äúeverything can‚Äù, and not an army of them
Under the cut my wishes, and in the comments I would be glad to hear your wishes
PS: Write "brew coffee" or other more intimate desires, I think not worth it. Be realistic!

I will briefly describe my schedule, which, it is suspected, will be similar to the schedule of many of you:
I live at home (if not strange), at home there is high-speed Internet, he is at work.
The path from home to work takes at least 20 minutes, sometimes 40. I would like to use them with benefits ... I actively use: mail, RSS, twitter, LJ ...
  • So wish # 1: Rss reader, twitter client, mail client with voice synthesizer and voice control, which I could read the news on the way to work. For example, we read the headlines, and when we receive a voice command, we read the news completely. In general, the existing voice control in IMHO smarts is not very good, although apparently it is not far off.
  • Wish number 2: Built-in FM transmitter. Many of us store a lot of music on smarts (iPhones), and it would be nice to be able to listen to it in the car (and on the radio) without ultra-complicated adapters and cables + broadcast navigation commands through the car audio system (Mostly already implemented on the basis of Bluetouth)
  • Wish number 3 Using smart as a USB hub for transferring to it, for example, photos from a camera or files from a USB flash drive + when connected to Wi-Fi accessibility on the network as a media server (seemingly already implemented)
  • Wish number 4 (seemingly already implemented in some devices) - connect to TV to play HD video.
  • Wish number 5 Again sounding books. Recently there was a topic that on BB this has already been implemented quite at a normal level.
  • Wish number 6 (again seen in concepts) Projected onto any surface full-size keyboard with the ability to enter
  • Wish number 7 Built-in projector for presentations or just surfing on a larger screen
  • Wish number 8 Large amounts of internal memory. It seems that iPhones are already going with 32 gigs, they are going to release 60 gigabytes ... But we know how much a real maniac needs .... 120-160 gig as min!
  • Wish # 9 Wireless connection to a Home audio system as a media source.
  • Wish number 10 (arrogance of course, but you can also dream) adding support for DECT and switching to it with a smart forwarding call (mobile via mobile network, urban via urban, international via cyp), if you are within the range of the DECT network - I understand Google Voice does something similar, but unfortunately for us it is not yet available.

With respect and invitation to the discussion,
Arseny

PS I would also be grateful for the recommendations of existing programs that already implement these functions.
