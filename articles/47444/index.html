Idea


Make the project site StartupPoint.ru
Many have heard, and someone has already participated in meetings of startups held in Moscow. The first meeting took place in September, the last (already the fifth) on December 7. We can say that the idea is a success. But there is no site with decent functionality. Therefore, the first goal of the site: the online continuation of offline meetings, as well as expanding the idea of ‚Äã‚ÄãStartup Point to the regions and possibly the CIS.
The second goal: a highly specialized place to meet startups with startups (to share experience and discuss specific details of startups - business model, usability, code, design) and startups with investors (so that investors can see "live" projects, and startups show their project accordingly in all its glory before the investor).

Functionality:
* Presentations of startups and their discussion (blog structure)
* StartupWiki - a structured encyclopedia of startups, from creation to monetization, created by the editors and users, moderated by the editors
* Startup rating by users - reduced in the first version
* User Rating (analog of karma) - reduced in the first version

The target audience



Target groups and their needs (solved on the site):

Startups (beginners [a startup at the idea stage], practices [had time to launch something] and a guru [not 1 startup monetized]):
+ Presentation of your startup and yourself (announcement, review, writing articles = pr, demonstration of your knowledge, experience (GURU))
+ Communication with fellow startups (commenting, rating, discussion)
+ Getting quality feedback from the audience startuppoint.ru
+ Search for information (feature articles, reviews, information about competitors, statistics)
+ Search for employees, partners, outstaff among the audience of the portal

Investors (business angels, business incubators, venture funds)
+ Search for potential investment projects (based on reviews, announcements, reviews)
+ Communication with colleagues and startups (commenting, rating, discussion)
+ Presentation of yourself and invested projects (writing articles, reviews)
+ Search for information (feature articles, reviews, information about competitors, statistics)
+ Search for employees, partners

Internet specialists (project managers, programmers, designers, usability and others)
+ Partnership (participation in a startup)
+ Job search (temporary job search (outsourcing), permanent job search, offer of services)
+ Communication (discussion, commenting, rating, writing articles, reviews)
+ Search for information

Other Internet users (surfers):

+ Search for information (find and read interesting materials, learn about new projects and the process of their development)
+ Communication (discussion, commenting, rating)
