We invite you to the second hakaton Neurohack



On September 9, in Moscow, with the support of Mail.Ru Group, Neurohack 2.0 starts - this is a 48-hour marathon, during which you will be able to embody your ideas related to the topic of artificial intelligence and neural networks. The hackathon is carried out thanks to the community of leading scientists of Russia - Science Guide.

We invite:

  • programmers in the field of machine learning and neural networks;
  • scientists in the field of neurointerfaces, neuroscience, neurorehabilitation, higher nervous activity, neuroscience for sports, etc .;
  • engineers;
  • entrepreneurs and marketers.

At the hackathon, you can discuss your ideas, hear expert opinions about them, create a team of enthusiasts and interest investors. In other words, Neurohack 2.0 is a chance to create a startup of your dreams.

In addition to the hackathon, the program provides educational presentations on machine learning, neural networks, algorithms and modeling, as well as methods for measuring neurodata. Within 48 hours allocated for the development of projects, it is necessary to create a prototype based on the available or obtained data, analyze the possible market, develop a business model, presentation and plan to bring the product to market.
