Registration is now open for the third annual championship for developers of the Russian Developers Cup



We invite everyone to take part in the Russian Developers Cup Championship, in which IT-specialists in the field of web development, mobile services and games compete. Registration for the championship opens from today.

Here you can not only compete in the skill and knowledge in the field of development, but also find like-minded people and create project teams with them. The first championship of the Russian Developers Cup was held in 2013, then the first place was taken by the Funky Quest project, a service for creating and conducting games and quests on the ground. In 2014, the Wellness Team won, which in the course of the championship created a new online service for sports.

This year we have made important changes to the rules of the championship. Now all the teams registered in the system immediately get the opportunity to enjoy all the benefits of the IIDF preaccelerator (last year only the best projects got into it). And it gives access to educational materials and allows you to get in sight of more than a hundred investors watching the passage of RDC.

The championship partners will be the Internet Initiatives Development Fund (IIDF) and the Foundation for Assistance to the Development of Small Enterprises in the Scientific and Technical Sphere (Bortnik Fund). This year, the Bortnik Fund establishes a separate nomination, within which the best projects will receive grants of 400 thousand rubles each. under the program "UMNIK".

Also this year, the IIDF established a special prize - an invitation to an absentee accelerator, a two-month program for Internet projects, which makes it possible to pump the project together with the IIDF experts and increases the chances of receiving investments.

You can register for the championship and get detailed information about the rules and stages of the event on the official website .
