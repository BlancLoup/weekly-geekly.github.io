Announcement of the IDCEE 2012 conference



Dear, habrovchane!

We invite you to the international conference IDCEE 2012 ( Investor Day of Central and Eastern Europe 2012: Internet technologies and innovations ). This year the conference will be held in Kiev on October 18-19 at the same location - ACCO International Exhibition Center , which is located in the park to them. Pushkin. For the third year in a row, thanks to this event, the most respected headliners of the global IT industry are coming to the Ukrainian capital: the world's leading venture capital funds, actively investing business angels, professional industry experts, successful IT businessmen.

Of the new IDCEE 2012, it is worth noting workshops that will help uncover new talents and entrepreneurial spirit of the conference participants. In addition, 12 start-up finalists will be able to present their projects on the IDCEE main stage. Three winners will be determined by the Expert Commission, which includes representatives of global funds ( eVenture Capital Partners , Bessemer Venture Partners , Balderton Capital ).

Recall that last year IDCEE was visited by more than 2,000 participants from 35 countries, over 50 outstanding speakers, representatives of more than 50 venture funds and companies, as well as 150 teams of IT entrepreneurs from Eastern and Western Europe and the USA.
