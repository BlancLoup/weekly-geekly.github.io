What's new showed on Apple presentation



Less than an hour ago, the regular annual Apple Event ended. At the Steve Jobs Theater gathered journalists and technicians, thirsting for new information from the largest company on the planet and the first American trillionaire.


Part of the data on new devices Apple itself today accidentally browsed on its website. During its update , references to the iPhone Xs, iPhone Xs Max and iPhone Xr appeared in the code. In addition to the names "leaked" and some of the characteristics of new devices: screen size, memory size and body color. But the most interesting Tim Cook and his comrades still told directly at the event. In short, in three words: more expensive, more, more powerful.


Apple Watch


Began with the new Apple Watch 4. Cook believes that Apple's smart watches have come a long way to become the number one watch in the world in sales. And now the company is entering a new level. As expected, now smart watches will take care of our health with you. They will detect if the user's heart beats irregularly, and send him a message about it. Now, Apple Watch also knows how to make an ECG graph - you no longer need to consult a doctor for this.




The processor is twice as fast as the previous one; it is dual core 64 bit. The display has become a third more than in the previous generation. "Desktop" hours completely changed. Now you can bring to it, for example, your favorite photos. The dial can also be animated. The clock has improved the speaker and microphone, updated the cellular signal transmission system, added the function of the radio. Motion and orientation sensors began to work 8 times faster ... The watch became waterproof, and their battery should last 18 hours.




The coolest thing is that Apple Watch was taught to understand if a person fell, stumbled or slipped (they say, to find patterns, you had to sweat very seriously). If a person has fallen, a big red button will appear on the clock, by pressing which you can quickly call for help. Moreover, the watch can even call it independently if the user does not move within a minute after the fall.


Apple Watch Series 4 prices:


  • Series 4 with GPS - $ 399;
  • Series 4 with LTE - $ 499.

Start of sales - September 14. Shipments to the United States start on September 21.


iPhone Xs and iPhone Xs Max


Two new Apple smartphones, differing in display diagonal. It will be 5.8 inches for iPhone Xs, 6.5 inches for iPhone Xs Max. Display - OLED, resolution - 2436 x 1125 pixels. Both smartphones have become dvuhsimochnye. Both have two cameras with 12 megapixels. One - with a wide-angle lens and f / 1.8, the second - with a telephoto lens, double optical zoom and f / 2.4. The camcorder can record video in 4K and sound in good stereo thanks to four built-in microphones.




In front of iPhones - "the most durable glass in the world." They are waterproofed to IP68. Improved HDR-shots and 3D Touch, accelerated algorithms Face ID to determine the owner of the face. Apple continues to insist that it is the most reliable smartphone locking system in the world.


Inside both models - the most powerful A12 Bionic processor at 7 nm with 4 GPU cores and 6 CPU cores. Smartphones work 30% faster than previous models. The internal storage has also expanded: a model with 512 GB will be available.

iPhone Xr


Another smartphone, the main feature of which is the presence of an LCD display. Its diagonal will be 6.1 inches - something in between the iPhone Xs and iPhone Xs Max. The display goes from edge to edge for the first time; it is the most advanced LCD screen in the world of smartphones. Apple called it Liquid Retina. The resolution is 1792828 (326 PPI). The screen is larger than the iPhone 8 Plus (it was 5.5 inches), but in a much smaller package.


The smartphone is protected from dust and water according to the IP67 standard. It works with Face ID, A12 chip and all other iPhone Xs / iPhone Xs Max technologies.


The battery, according to Apple, is one and a half hours longer than the iPhone 8 Plus (before this, the iPhone 8 Plus was the leader among Apple smartphones, from 11-12 hours of active work without recharging).
