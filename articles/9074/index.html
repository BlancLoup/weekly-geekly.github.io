Study of Russian corporate blogs

Now they talk a lot about corporate blogs - they say much more than they do. And, interestingly, they say relying on some intuitive assessment of the corporate blogosphere. And it would be good to take and calculate everything.

Today, NetMind has published the first part of a study of Russian-language corporate blogs. . It contains quantitative data on Russian-language corporate blogs, which currently number more than a hundred.

A typical corporate blog is owned by a company operating in the field of information technology, the authors of this blog are several company employees who lead it as an addition to an existing corporate website.

The purpose of the study was to assess the objective situation with the Russian-language corporate blogging, count the number of blogs and estimate the growth rate of the corporate blogosphere.

A deeper assessment of corporate blogs, their effectiveness and peculiarities of use in RuNet will be presented at the second and third stages of the study.
