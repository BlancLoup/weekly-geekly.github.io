We start voting

Friends, we want to inform you that the first stage of the competition of ideas, conducted by the Innovation Laboratory, has ended.
We already wrote that the expert council selected some of the most promising ideas, recalling the list of authors: Evgeny Sinitsyn, Maxim Kulintsov, Jane Dukhopelnikova, Artyom Vasilenko, Tefery and Pavel P.

Then, it was decided to extend the competition until September 25, 2011, to give another opportunity to take part to all those who later came to their senses.

The reception of ideas was officially completed and, once again reviewing all the applications received, the expert council formed the final list of participants who move to the next round: Andrei Malolin, Yevgeny Sinitsin, Jane Dukhopelnikova, Ivan Boyko.

The final stage of the competition is voting on the participants of the short list indicated above. Voting takes place on our facebook page. We invite you all to take part and support the most attractive participant. The main winner will be determined by the expert council, but the results of the voting will be of considerable importance, so that your vote can really be decisive. Voting will last until 10/14/2011.
