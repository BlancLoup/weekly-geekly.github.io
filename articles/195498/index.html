Nvidia introduced Tegra Note


Finally, Nvidia opened the curtain and showed its long-awaited gadget - Tegra Note. Although not a consumer gadget, it will become a design model for their partners. Nvidia also notes that in India, the device will be known as Xolo.

Nvidia Tegra Note has a 7 "HD (1280x800) IPS display, a Tegra 4 processor with a 72-core GeForce GPU and a 4-core Cortex-A15 CPU. The device works as one would expect running Android with the ability to upgrade over the air. It has 5 megapixel rear camera and front VGA. The device will have 16GB of memory with expandable via microSD for another 32GB. The device promises to work up to 10 hours in HD video playback mode.

Other features of the device are DirectStylus technology for the use of styluses, PureAudio, camera application SmugMug known among iOS users, well, and well-known TegraZone representing access to games optimized for Tegra.

Commenting on the launch, Matt Wuebbling, director of product marketing at Tegra, said in his blog : ‚ÄúThis increases the capabilities of our partners with innovative and functional devices that they may not be able to produce themselves. In turn, Nvidia gets a huge market for these devices in various regions of the world. ‚Äù

The recommended price of this device will be $ 199. Deliveries of devices to partners will begin in the coming months.
