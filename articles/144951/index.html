HTML5 maps for mobile platforms

Recently, a team of HTML5 maps from Nokia provided API cards for smartphones and tablets .

Right off the bat!

You can try the link: bit.ly/html5map
Webkit browsers are currently supported.

Advantages and opportunities

  • Components
    Maps, traffic jams, search, geolocation, information about places of interest, navigation, walking routes, favorites.
  • Weight
    80 KB Javascript + 10 KB CSS
  • Performance
    Fast loading and smooth effects.
  • Readiness
    You can easily change colors, wrap in PhoneGap or QtWebkit and get a ready application.
  • WYSIWYG Assistant
    Helps to customize the configuration.




Responsive design

Works great on both smartphone and tablet screens.
IOS 4.x, iOS 5.0, Android 2.2, 2.3, 3.1, 3.2 (4.0), Meego, Blackberry Touch devices OS6, OS7 are supported. In the second half of the year, they will upgrade to Symbian, Windows Phone 8.0, WebOS 2+.

Cards

Four types of tiles are offered, including urban transport and traffic jams. And the geolocation button allows you to determine the current location.

Search

Full search for addresses and places with instant results. You can tie your data source. Search results contain ranking and distance to the place.

Interesting places

Nokia Maps offer information on thousands of locations in five categories around the world.

Detailed information about the place contains a description, photo, address, rating, reviews, working hours and adjacent places. The component also allows you to call, go to the site, get directions or share a link.

Navigation

Convenient step-by-step navigation with three types of routes: cars, pedestrian and urban transport. I consider walking routes through cities to be especially strong.
