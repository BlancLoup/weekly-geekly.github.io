Mobile marketing and application design on AppClub {build, monetize} # 6 Kyiv

The sixth meeting of AppClub {build, monetize} # 6 Kyiv will be held on June 5 (19:00 - 22:00). Topics of the meeting: prototyping and design of mobile applications, mobile marketing, presentations of startups.



What awaits the participants?

{build, monetize} Startup WithMyFriends: search for travel companions on airplanes and trips


Lvovyan Yaroslav Maksimovich managed to work in the role of both an entrepreneur and an investor, launched more than a dozen projects, two of which were successfully sold. In a recent contest from The Next Web was named "Best Founder".

Yaroslav will talk about his current project - WithMyFriends mobile application for searching for travel companions on airplanes and trains - you can watch advertisements for this particular project on some trains traveling across the expanses of Ukraine.

{build} From prototype to product. Develop HTML5 / JavaScript applications for Windows Phone and Windows 8


The report will talk about how to develop a cross-platform application for HTML5, how to make JavaScript friends with C # in Windows Phone 8 and Windows 8, how to work with touch events in IE10 and other browsers, and also look at how to work with LocalStorage in online / offline.

Speaker: Alexander Korobeynikov, .NET developer with web orientation, resident of IT Project Northern Europe (Sweden) and Startupreneurs AB . Alexander has published several applications for Windows Phone.

{monetize} 10 rules for successful launch of a mobile application


With more than three years of experience in promoting mobile applications since their launch and tracking the experience of a large number of developers, ComboApp has come close to understanding the processes needed for a successful release. In the report you will learn 10 rules for the successful launch of your mobile application.

Speaker: Sergey Kanishchev has been the head of ComboApp marketing department since 2012. In the company, Sergey is engaged in negotiations with key partners, strategy and placement optimization for large projects, and other issues related to the promotion of mobile applications. Previous experience includes more than 10 years of implementation of communication projects in international agencies DDB, Lowe & Partners, Ogilvy, marketing of the STB channel, as well as work in the European technology company Veracomp.

{build} Basic design errors for Windows Phone / Windows 8 applications


We will talk about the principles of interface design (aka metro) for Windows Phone and Windows 8, the basic design errors, as well as successful / not successful examples from the real world.

Information about speakers: Alexander Krakovetsky , @msugvnua, DevRain Solutions head. The author of the sites Windows Phone 7 Rocks! and Microsoft User Group Community , Microsoft Regional Director, Microsoft MVP, Telerik MVP, Ph.D. Information Technology, Forbes Columnist. Galushko Michael , devlanfear , co-founder of DevRain Solutions, author of the site Windows Phone 7 Rocks! . MCPD .NET 3.5, 4.0, Microsoft MVP.

UX / UI review of your applications


If you are a developer of Windows Phone and Windows 8 applications, you can apply for a UX / UI review of your application. During the meeting, you can get advice on improving the design and usability of your application. In order to get qualified advice, you need 1) personal attendance at a meeting 2) consent to show your application at a meeting 2) send a link to your Windows Phone or Windows 8 application to info@devrain.com.
