The new game for iOS will allow the user to become a Uber driver in real life

Uber has launched a game for users who want to try themselves as taxi drivers. This is a simulator of online taxi service. While he works on devices based on iOS.

The uberDRIVE game will allow users to start mastering the craft wisdom of taxi drivers without any real risk. According to the rules of the game, it is necessary to deliver the passenger to a given destination. The user's ability to comply with the criteria and rules of the road specified in the task is evaluated. The player must offer a safe and optimal route to the destination.

Gaining more points, the user can play cars of a higher class - Uber Black and Uber SUV. However, in the rules of the game there is one significant difference from the rules of service: the slower a player travels, the less he earns. In real life, of course, safety is more important.

If the player successfully completes the uberDrive mission, he will be able to get training materials and a step-by-step guide for the driver program in Uber. If desired, he can get trained and become a real Uber taxi driver, according to a company blog .

For now, only the San Francisco map is given in the game. uberDrive can be downloaded from the American Apple iTunes Store.
