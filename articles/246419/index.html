2. Accommodation rack / mi.


Consider what we need at the time of the move from the data center. Suppose you only have equipment and patch cables.

Service Fixed-place - is the placement of rack cabinets dimensions not exceeding some dimensions in width and in depth and summing a certain number of kilowatts. The sizes can be completely different, but usually they do not exceed 600x1100mm. All sizes, which are more than the given example, are considered non-standard and in many data centers the placement of those is impossible.

PDU rental service - Power Distribution Device or, even simpler, a unit with sockets for the equipment being placed. For example, APC AP8853. Mounted in a rack cabinet. Required item. There are managed and not managed. They can connect the monitoring system and see the current status of the power supply. Monitoring is useful for optimizing power consumption equipment.



The service provision for rent AVR - Power supply switching system. It is used when it is necessary to combine the backup power line and the main one in the equipment with one power supply unit.

Service provision for rent Racks (wardrobe) - the usual rack-mounted wardrobe. There may be any height, it all depends on the manufacturer. Manufacturer's example - MINKELS (standard Google racks).

In some data centers, it is possible to rent only a rack, without paying for anything else. This is applicable when, for example, you have a high-end storage system (it goes in your rack) or you use your racks to save on data center services.
It is important to pay attention to the required number of kilowatts. Those. if you count on growth in a budget data center, it will often be impossible to realize this. Only some data centers can provide from 8kW per rack. On average, they give 4-5kW per stand-up space. Calculations of food consumption are carried out individually. Proper PDU will help you save.

3. Services in the data center.


Having placed in a data center, we will need various services, among which there are mandatory with possible restrictions, and less in demand. Let's look at them.

3.1. Required services:



3.1.1. Round-the-clock access.


It is important that access to equipment is even at night. Often, to get into the machine room, the presence of an employee of the DC is required. It should think about this in advance.

3.1.2. Kitchen / dining room 24 * 7.


Work is work, but there is something you need, even during the deadline. This requirement is applicable in order to eat not above the server, and not far from the machine, without leaving the data center.

3.1.3. Room assembly / disassembly equipment.


Sometimes it takes a long time to assemble a server or unpack equipment, etc. For this there is a room where you can do dirty work in a convenient place.

3.1.4. Warehouse in the data center.


Often the equipment comes to the data center without engineers or there is so much of it that you can‚Äôt assemble it in a working day. In such cases, it is required to store the equipment for a long time or for a long time, if it is, for example, spare parts.

3.1.5. ‚ÄúPhysical support‚Äù or remote hands by data center employees.


KVM may not always help, and a specialist‚Äôs departure to the data center may not be possible. For this there is a support on duty from the data center. It can be anything. For example, see light bulbs or pull the power cord. The service can be, as a fee, and free.

Let's list the possible services in more detail:

A. Turn on / off hardware:
The engineering composition of the data center is present at the facility 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. If necessary, the employee within the application from the Client can turn on, turn off, reboot the equipment without leaving the Client‚Äôs representative in the data center. Thus, this service allows you to save on the time unavailability of a particular equipment, as well as the lack of engineering staff in the workplace. The ‚ÄúEquipment On / Off‚Äù service covers all equipment.
B. Cross / Patch Connection:
Work on the modernization, changing the connection equipment. Connect (cross-connect) equipment with patch cords within the rack.
C. Replacing components on equipment:
Works on installing and removing interface cards, replacing cassettes and other media for archiving data, replacing ‚Äúhot components‚Äù of equipment (hot-spot HDD, power units, cooler fans) that do not require, according to the technical instructions of the equipment manufacturer, power outages without the need to disassemble the equipment.
D. Technical support:
Work on the visual monitoring of health, providing visual confirmation when providing remote technical support, assistance in disassembling and unpacking equipment.

3.2. Not the most obligatory services, but the presence of which is sometimes critical:



3.2.1. Provision of the WHS service as a service.


This is a service for installing and configuring a video surveillance system (IOD) for continuous video monitoring of the Client‚Äôs equipment in real time. Recording can be carried out on the customer‚Äôs equipment, as well as centrally in the data center. The service is aimed at video recording of events with client racks.



3.2.2. The provision of access services for the rack, without additional. equipment, as a service.


This is a service to install or configure access control systems (ACS) on a rack with equipment. Further, its maintenance, logging and access control.
Possible functionality:
  1. Creation and maintenance of a database of employees;
  2. Determination of access areas and access levels for each employee, management of access rights at the level of user groups;
  3. Separate database management of employees and their access rights;
  4. Tracking the movements of employees with the help of the event log, which allows you to know when and how much time the employee spent in a given room;

  1. Providing data for the time attendance module
  2. Comparison using the photo module identifying images coming from the camera with a photo from the database;
  3. Full automation of the mechanism for managing access rights, including the creation of new access cards, changes in the rights of their holders, cancellation of cards;




3.2.3. Ability to organize the fence around the racks.


This service is an organization of the physical isolation of the racks inside the common machine room. The grille is installed from the concrete floor to the concrete ceiling. Electronic locks with contactless cards or coded mechanical built-in locks can be installed. The doors to the isolated zone can be made in the form of a sliding grille or rolling shutters.



3.2.4. Rental of premises for permanent stay of the Customer‚Äôs staff in the data center.


This service consists in renting a room to accommodate the Customer‚Äôs staff in the data center. Due to the presence in the data center building, customer engineers can have quick access to the equipment placed in the machine room. The service can be provided as a rental of the whole room, as well as its separate part, depending on the availability of technical capabilities and needs.
The room is equipped with: office furniture, SCS, OVN, ACS, Internet and electricity.
