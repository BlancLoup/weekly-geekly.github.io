Join the broadcast Sharepoint Conference 2011 Russia


Today, on June 20, at 10:00 Moscow time, the live broadcast of SharePoint Conference Russia 2011 will begin. Anyone can see the reports at msdn.microsoft.com/ru-ru/sharepoint/hh264214 or on the Conference website .

The program is divided into three parallel threads. In the two main streams, participants will be able to hear what the upcoming update pack will bring, what SharePoint Online is in Office 365 and why it is needed and why it is important not only for end customers, but also for solution developers, they will learn interesting details about corporate search, and the subtleties of SharePoint on the Internet and more. The main focus of the reports will be on practical experience and best practices for using SharePoint. The third stream will be presentations by partners of their own decisions in the extra-section.

The conference is organized by the Russian SharePoint User Group. Microsoft is the general partner of the event.

Use the hashtag #spconfru to share opinions and comments or find information on the conference on Twitter.
