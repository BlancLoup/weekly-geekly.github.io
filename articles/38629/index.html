How not to catch cold

In connection with the latest events in the weather, I was visited by the following idea, because we live in the 3rd millennium, and there is nothing complicated about what I offer.

The essence of the idea is to not give a man naked to go out into the cold.

To this end, it is proposed to create a complex of the following components:

Thermometer, or something more serious.
Monitor, or temperature indicator + speaker.
A bunch of small chips with a transmitter receiver (something like RFID).
Special lock or lotion to normal.
Any additional sensors

This is all placed in the following places:
Thermometer on the street, Indicator + speaker in front of the exit door, Chips in clothes, lock where it was before, sensors depending on destination.

How it works:
