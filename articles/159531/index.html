How to withdraw WebMoney Z using ePayService card



In the last article, we introduced you to our ePayService MasterCard card program and the service as a whole. Today we want to talk about the wmz withdrawal method, which is available to every ePayService client. If you occasionally encounter this quest, welcome under cat.

Historically, one of the most popular ways to pay for freelancers in the CIS countries is Gold WebMoney Transfer. For each country, the WebMoney system provides its own withdrawal options, which differ in fees, cash withdrawal rates and degree of difficulty. The method that we propose has relatively low costs and a single simple withdrawal scheme for all citizens of the CIS.

Initial inventory


1. Active member identifier of the WebMoney system (WMID).
2. Active account in the ePayService system with a rating of 2 or higher . An account with a rating of 2 or higher can be obtained by completing the verification procedure.
3. Active ePayService MasterCard . The ePayService card is available absolutely free of charge to any ePayService customer with an account rating of 2 or higher. Customers with an account rating of 1 can receive a card by completing the verification procedure.

Passage


1. Log in to your account in the online.ePayService payment system. Login is performed after authorization via SMS or QR code.
2. Follow the link "WebMoney Z Refill."
Screenshot 1

3. Enter the required amount, after which the system redirects us to WebMoney Merchant.
Screenshot 2

4. We pass the authorization procedure in the WebMoney system, confirm your payment.
5. The most difficult is behind - the account in ePayService is replenished.
Screenshot 3


Next, we have two options: withdraw all funds to the card here and now, returning to the main screen of your account in ePayService. Or replenish your card as needed through the ePayService app for Android and iOs .



I think everyone knows what to do with the card after replenishment, but just in case I will list the main ways of using it:
1. Pay for purchases and services in stores, restaurants, cash desks, etc. (no commission);
2. To cash out through any ATMs working with the MasterCard system (commission 1.5 USD per withdrawal);
3. Pay for purchases in online stores (no commission).

FAQ


How much do I lose on commissions when recharging an ePayService card with a transfer from the WebMoney system?
We do not charge a fee for replenishment of an ePayService card, so in this case your losses will be: 1.5% for replenishment of an account in ePayService + 0.8% for conducting a transaction in WebMoney system. Total 2.3% for a safe and fast way to cash out your earnings.

How much money can I withdraw at an ATM per day?
The standard limit on ATM withdrawals from an ePayService MasterCard card allows you to withdraw up to 10,000 USD per day and up to 300,000 USD per month. Shopping in stores and the Internet are subject to the same limits.

Is wmz limited to entering my ePayService account?
The standard limit on entering wmz into the system for all clients is 35,000 USD per month. However, at any time you can request an increase of this limit to the size you need through the form inside your account.

Can I withdraw funds from your card in rubles / hryvnias?
Yes, you can withdraw funds in any national currency, including euros, rubles, hryvnias, etc. The main currency of the card is USD.

How long does the whole process of transferring funds from WebMoney to ePayService card take?
All account replenishment operations in ePayService for wmz and replenishment of an ePayService MasterCard card occur instantly, in real time.

Can I replenish my card and make purchases through PayPal?
Yes, you can link our card to PayPal for online shopping.
