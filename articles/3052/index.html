Internet activity again in March 2006

The presidential elections are long gone, passions have subsided, and now I can easily publish it. Then, against the background of a general agiotage, it would not be entirely politically correct to do so.

Moreover, there is a reason - the activity of the Belarusians, which has fallen seriously after the elections, has again reached the March level.

I think habra people will be interested to know how it happens when the Internet is the only unregulated means of mass communication in the country.

The graph shows the daily number of sessions given by Akavita. The main peak is March 20, the day after the election. There is no point in drawing conclusions about the number of Internet users, but trends are clearly visible.

I leave the conclusions about the role of elections in the development of the Internet in Belarus to you. :)
