Released Worms Reloaded


In February, it became known that a new strategy about the cult "worms" - Worms Reloaded - is being developed. As then said the head of the company Team17, Martin Brown (Martyn Brown), the game will be two-dimensional, will be released only on personal computers, and you can buy it through the online service Steam .

And it became known that there was less than a month left to wait for Worms Reloaded - the release will take place on August 26, 2010. Players will be offered about 60 levels, more than 45 types of weapons, the Warzone campaign, as well as the Bodycount mode. Moreover, the authors will prepare and a level editor where you can create your own locations.

Support for Steam guarantees high-quality and convenient multiplayer modes - Worms Reloaded will be able to play up to 4 players in online or hot-screen matches with Steamworks support (updates, friend list, ratings, achievements and chat).
