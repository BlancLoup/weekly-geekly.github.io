Part 1. Fitness and pulse: Polar FT40





We start with a standard solution, hammered into the heads of city dwellers, with annoying advertising: a fitness subscription, for standard workouts with barbells and weights. The lesson is useful, but does not meet the main goal of training - strengthening the heart while maintaining shape. To track the level of workload from classes, the first device was purchased: Polar FT40 (see table). After a few months of training, the emphasis from the gym was shifted to a treadmill. Thanks to the device, I used to collect and monitor the statistics of classes, and with its help it was possible to track the progress in the development of the heart. The data stored on the site is as follows:



Kilometer has to be entered manually, since this parameter cannot be measured in the hall. If you open any workout, you can get detailed information:



If you continue classes in the hall - perhaps this is enough, but summer has come, and the legs themselves are carried out into the street - running around the park is much more pleasant than knocking your feet on a treadmill. Since FT40 does not allow to measure and save the distance traveled, I had to purchase a new device.

Part 3. Beautiful and free: applications for the phone Nike +, miCoach




In the process of training, there are many enthusiastic people and even amateur organizations that run for themselves and promote it with affordable means. In particular, I joined the Nike Race Club, which offers free training in Moscow parks, with the involvement of professional coaches.
Being engaged together with them, I decided to evaluate the solutions that are available for owners of smartphones equipped with a GPS receiver. I chose two applications from a huge variety: NikePlus from Nike, and adidas miCoach.
The main objective of both applications is to maintain statistics when passing the distance, as well as to increase motivation in any way possible: by handing virtual prizes, synchronization with the music library, colorful presentation of the saved activities.
Applications look very decent both on the phone screen and on the site, where data is automatically transferred after the workout is completed.

Deficiencies of applications (any and all including)
  • less reliability in comparison with specialized devices, sometimes due to failures when launching the application, or at a distance, the training is not saved correctly or is not saved at all;
  • It is difficult to control the speed in the process of training, usually the phone is deep in your pocket;
  • to save pulse data, you need to find and purchase an optional Bluetooth heart rate sensor, for example: Zephyr HxM ‚Ñ¢ BT Heart Rate Monitor ;

Application Benefits
  • availability, to start using requires only the presence of a smartphone;
  • colorful presentation of results;
  • additional motivation systems, in the form of a reminder of successes and achievements after each workout;

For each application, development companies offer a set of external sensors, but without them you can easily do.

Bottom line: to maintain physical fitness with the help of running, practically nothing is needed except desire and running shoes. The weather and the season are not a hindrance, to -15 it is quite comfortable to run, only the right clothes are needed.
Start with a free app, type in statistics and start working on improving results. To monitor the work of the heart, it is highly desirable to use a pulse sensor, and to control speed and distance, a GPS receiver is needed. You can purchase a specialized device that is more convenient and accurate during a workout, or buy a pulse sensor for a phone, and go to more advanced paid applications.
