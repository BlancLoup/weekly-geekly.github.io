(Kiev) May 26-27 seminar: Basic HP ProLiant skills from TC MUK

The purpose of the seminar is to provide technicians and system administrators with knowledge and skills on HP ProLiant servers in the most concise, accessible and practical, useful form.




Attendees of the seminar will not only improve the quality of their work in the field, but will also receive an excellent starting point for commercial training on HP servers and blades. For example, this applies to the HP BladeSystem Administration and Management courses or the introduction of HP ProLiant ML / DL / SL servers, where participants are initially expected to have a high level of knowledge of the tools and technologies that are part of the seminar program. Practical classes will be held on HP ProLiant servers installed in the laboratories of the training center.

The seminar is held for the first time and will be held with the sponsorship of the server unit of the HP office in Ukraine. Participants pay only 35% of the real cost of the course, which means paying 1090 hryvnia (including VAT). Under the cat a detailed program of the seminar.

You can register for the seminar by writing: training@muk.com.ua. Payment issues: CA Commercial Director Nikolay Evdochenko, nne@muk.ua.
