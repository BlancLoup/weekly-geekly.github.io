After a brief debriefing, it turned out that the google.com, google.com.ua, google.com, google.de, google.jp domains were not resolved (others were not checked).
Is it only here or has anyone noticed?
Interestingly, is it a communication problem , a planned shutdown of all (?) Data centers or an organized (unorganized) DDoS attack?
