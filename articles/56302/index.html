Minsk. Free public lecture "Software Requirements Management and Testing"

Next Thursday, April 9, in Minsk, at the University of Culture will be held a lecture on " Managing software requirements and testing ."
This is a continuation of a series of our educational events, which have already been written about in Habr√© ( here about hosting , here about creative in online advertising , here about the MOSS conference and here about our mini-conference on usability )
This time the lecture on software requirements management will be the head of the testing department of the IBA Internet division, Alexey Yakimovich . IBA is one of the three largest IT companies in Belarus.

The lecture will address those aspects of software requirements management that relate to testing and analyzing requirements. It will cover the process of organizing management and analysis of requirements, as well as managing the development process and testing requirements.
In addition, the organization of the process of reviewing documentation and software requirements in particular will be considered. This lecture may be of interest to testers and software analysts .

Participation in the event is free, but by prior registration .

This is the second lecture of Alexey. Materials of the first lecture, " Software Testing. Some practical lessons ", are available on the website. Including we filmed a lecture on a video that is also available.

In general, it will be 7 free lectures and 10 events (there were 3 more mini-conferences) that we, the portal IT-JOB.BY , arrange with the help of the Belarusian State University of Culture and Arts.
