Mobile patenting

Chetan Sharma Consulting has prepared a report on patenting in the US and Europe in the field of mobile technology.

Over the past few years, the number of applications for registration of patents for mobile technologies has increased significantly. In the US, the number of patents in this area increased by 17%.

The top three leaders in mobile technology patenting include IBM, Samsung and Qualcomm .
