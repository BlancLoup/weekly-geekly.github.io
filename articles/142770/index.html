Passengers of the Petersburg Metro will be able to pay for travel over the Internet

Passengers of the St. Petersburg metro, using a single electronic ticket "Plantain", have access to a new service - cashless replenishment of the ticket resource on the metro website ispp.spbmetropoliten.ru . You can replenish an electronic ticket using a bank card, mobile payments or electronic money WebMoney and Yandex.Money. In the future, it will also be possible to replenish other types of subway travel tickets on contactless smart cards.

For online replenishment of the travel card, it is enough to fill in the form on the website, indicating the contactless card number, means of payment and the amount of replenishment. To activate the replenishment of the resource, the card must be attached to the reader of the visualizer in the lobby of the metro, it can be done at any time after the replenishment of the ticket. The maximum amount for which the Plantain travel card can be replenished is 15,000 rubles, the minimum is the cost of one trip in ground passenger transport. The service of remote replenishment of travel tickets is provided by the Petersburg Metro in cooperation with the First Settlement Digital Center LLC, the technical solution provider is Processing Technologies.

The Internet system for receiving payments of the St. Petersburg metro is the second service for the remote replenishment of travel tickets, implemented jointly by Processing Technologies LLC and PRCC LLC. In December 2011, the Breeze Transport system was launched (the site oplatatransporta.ru ) for the online and SMS-replenishment of the Plantain, since then the number of users of the Breeze Transport services has increased monthly by an average of 20%. Passengers of urban transport appreciated the convenience of remote payments, which significantly save time when extending the ticket ticket resource.
