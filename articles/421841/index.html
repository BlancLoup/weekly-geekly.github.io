Robot cars are tested in test mode in the city of Phoenix, Arizona, USA. Residents of Phoenix are not too happy with the mobiles, claiming that they still have too many shortcomings for the service to be called complete. Among other problems are difficulties injecting traffic into the general traffic on the road, turning left and rounding off groups of people.
