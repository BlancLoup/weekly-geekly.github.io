Google launches office suite

The suite of office software from Google is called Google Docs & Spreadsheets . As usual, the system is released in beta. Although, in the case of Google, the term "beta version" almost corresponds to the term "official release".

Actually, Google Docs & Spreadsheets is nothing new. These are the same programs that were known before, namely Writely text editor (analogue of MS Word) and Spreadsheets spreadsheet (analogue of MS Excel). Now they are released as part of a single set of programs (similar to MS Office).

We are not talking about some kind of tight integration of a text editor and spreadsheets. Combining programs in a single package has more marketing value. This is another psychological blow to Microsoft.
