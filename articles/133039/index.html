Made an iPad version of our service

After months of hard work, we are happy to present a special application for iPad . Honestly, it was very difficult to do it. And not only by the fact that this is our first experience of developing an application completely, what is called in-house, but also because during the development of the application, Apple has significantly tightened the requirements for applications, and we have changed the understanding of what needs to be given to the user.

Under the cat description of the functionality of the application, video and:

Application functionality allows you to:

- Sync (download) device playlists and movies
- Create new and edit old playlists
- Search and add new movies or music to playlists
- Manage user account settings and subscriptions



The application can only be used by the owner of one of the Fidel paid subscriptions. For new users of the service or application, there is a demo mode, limited to 600 minutes. During this time, users have access to all the functionality of the application, which will make it possible to understand to what extent you need a service and whether you need it at all. All changes that you make during the demo mode (creating playlists, adding movies and so on) will be available even after it ends. After completing the demo mode, you will be asked to subscribe. When you subscribe to music through the application, you will be charged $ 9.99 a month (250 rubles if you subscribe to Fidel ) and $ 19.99 a month for a subscription to music and movies (599 rubles if you subscribe to Fidel ) .
