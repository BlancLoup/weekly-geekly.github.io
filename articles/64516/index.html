The biggest theft of corporate Twitter documents

For the second time this year, hackers gained access to the Twitter administrative account by finding out the password of one of the company's employees (other passwords were also revealed, including from the mailboxes of company executives). The attacker under the name Hacker Croll took full advantage of the opportunities. It is reported that a large archive of confidential documents, some of which are publicly available, was taken from Twitter. For example, here or here you can get acquainted with the documents yourself (these are primary sources, in the near future these servers will be overloaded), we publish something under the script. The leadership of Twitter, by the way, has already confirmed the fact of the hacker attack and the authenticity of these files.

Of course, the principles of journalistic ethics do not allow us to publish here all the information from these documents, including the list of employees and their salaries, passwords for combination locks in the office rooms and the names of all employees who were interviewed for the position of managers (secretly from their current employer). However, there are such documents that it makes sense to make public, especially since some foreign bloggers have already done so .

For example, here are estimates of the size of the user base from the Twitter business plan: 25 million users by the end of 2009, 100 million by the end of 2010, and 350 million by the end of 2011. The planned revenues are also indicated there. Profit is planned to receive from certified accounts, as well as contextual advertising (AdSense widget and advertising tweets).

In addition, it became known details about the TV show, which is scheduled to be shot on American TV in the near future.
