IT-companies will have to work in Crimea to participate in public procurement in Russia

The Ministry of Communications and the Internet Development Institute are simultaneously working on measures to support Russian software developers. The head of the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media at a meeting with representatives of the industry offered another measure: IT companies that do not work in Crimea may prohibit participation in public procurement, RBC reports . Until March 27, the draft department is under public discussion.
