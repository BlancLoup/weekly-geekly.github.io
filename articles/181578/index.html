Odessa Innovation Week


Company RootUA Media, Center for Supercomputer Computing and Free Software of ONU named after I.I. Mechnikov and Illichivsk Institute ONU named after I.I. Mechnikova invites you to take part in Odessa Innovation Week - the first week of innovative technologies, which will be held from 10 to 16 June in Odessa . Within 7 days, the participants will have a number of interesting events, in which specialists in the field of web technologies will discuss various professional topics, start-ups with experience will demonstrate their projects and receive feedback from mentors and coaches, and start-up entrepreneurs will have the opportunity to create their start-up in 54 hours under the guidance of domestic and foreign mentors, as well as make new acquaintances, find partners, and, of course, have a good rest by the sea.

In the framework of Odessa Innovation Week the following events will be held:

June 10 from 9.30 to 11.00. Odessa StartUP Coffee # 2 is the second Odessa meeting of startups in an informal setting. It will allow for a cup of coffee / tea to talk to fellow entrepreneurs; interested people will be able to get acquainted with the startup movement.
Venue: st. Greek, 1a; Hub Odessa.
All details at: on.fb.me/13YcXcB

June 11 from 1 pm to 4 pm. IAP - Incubators and Accelerators Presentation
For the first time in Odessa, leading Ukrainian incubators, accelerators, funds and grants will gather to acquaint everyone with their best start-ups, as well as the opportunities that they can give to budding entrepreneurs.
WannaBiz, Eastlabs, GrowthUP, Happy Farm and Global Technology Foundation have already confirmed their participation in the event, which was founded by venture funds such as Runa Capital, Almaz Capital, TA Venture and BIONIC Hill.
Venue: st. Dvoryanskaya, 2, ONU named after I.I. Mechnikov
Details and registration at: on.fb.me/12f7WSt

June 11 from 5 pm to 10 pm Business Dating in Odessa - the 8th business conference "Business Dating in Odessa" - a meeting of more than 120 businessmen and business people of Odessa to exchange contacts, business offers and find new customers and partners + the performance of TOP guests.
Venue: st. Greek, 1a; Hub Odessa
Details and registration at: networking.od.ua

June 12 - 13. WebCamp 2013 - IV specialized conference of web developers. On the first day, there will be reports in streams: web technologies and project management, usability and design, mobile technologies, internet marketing, startups, as well as a Startup Crash Test and an alley of startups. The second day will be entirely devoted to the master classes.
Yuriy Artyukh, Mathieu Bodier, Ruslan Shevchenko, Andrey Kozhokaru (EPAM Systems), Andrey Kushnarev (AK-IT Consulting), Nikolai Marzhan (PortaOne), Lydia Bogdanovich (Provectus), Roman Katerinchik (Artjoker), Alexey Denisyuk ( Luxoft), Simon Lobach, Pavel Kolodyazhny (Make), Olga Gorenko (UsabilityLab), Victor Shaburov (Opera Software), Dmitry Suslov (1C-Bitrix), Alexey Anikin (Global Technology Foundation), Sergey Beresnev (Fruitware SRL), Seregey Bahar (Netpeak), Vladimir Melnik (Tucha), Anna Khristyuk (AdLabs), Irina Bakhmut (TurboSeo), Dmitry Lyashenko (InWeb), Stanislav Matyushenko (I Marketing), Marina Onishchenko (SiteClinic.ru), etc. Gia.
Venue: st. French Boulevard, 24/26; ONU named after I.I. Mechnikov
You can register for the conference, as well as get acquainted with the topics of reports and master classes at: webcamp.in.ua

12 June. Alley startups conference WebCamp 2013 is a platform that is designed to communicate technical specialists of various projects. The main task: to give start-up developers an opportunity to share their experience in developing their own projects, talk about emerging problems and methods for solving them, find ways to solve technical problems that have not yet been resolved, and so on.
Projects such as: Spinbackup, Ready to Travel, Photohouse, Invitag.me, PayJoy, Remote Coach, Yellowbox.mobi, Jeapie, Lonely Walls have already confirmed their participation in the mall.
Venue: st. French Boulevard, 24/26; ONU named after I.I. Mechnikov
You can learn the details of the event, as well as register your startup at: bit.ly/11mPa3X Participation for start-up teams in the alleys and WebCamp 2013 conference is FREE .

12 June. Startup Crash Test Odessa # 3 is an event conducted to test the strength of business models, marketing strategies, distribution models and other necessary elements of IT start-ups. From the avenue of startups WebCamp 2013 for Startup Crash Test Odessa # 3 3 most interesting projects will be selected.
Venue: st. French Boulevard, 24/26; ONU named after I.I. Mechnikov

June 12 from 18.30 to 22.00. TEDxOdessa TEDxLive
TEDxLive is a broadcast of the session from TEDGlobal 2013, the annual TED event held in Edinburgh, Scotland, June 10-14, 2013.
TEDGlobal 2013 Theme - Think again!
In addition to the broadcast - live performances of local speakers!
Venue: st. Greek, 1a; Hub Odessa
Registration and details: tedxodessa.com/participate

June 14 - 16. Odessa Startup Weekend
Startup Weekend is a global social movement of active people who want to improve their entrepreneurship skills. In 54 hours of intensive work, you will not only be able to find a team of like-minded people, but turn your idea into a real startup that you present to the judges on the last day of the event. Startup Weekend is the largest community of entrepreneurs with more than 500 organized events in 100 countries. Join us!
Venue: st. Greek, 1a; Hub Odessa
Details and registration on the website: odessa.startupweekend.org

June 16th Odessa Innovation Week Party
In the finals of Odessa Innovation Week we will have a party where all participants will be able to relax and unwind after a fruitful and active week.
