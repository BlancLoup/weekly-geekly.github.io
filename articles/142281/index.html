TEST-Camp Petersburg: a workshop of applications for society

May 12 and 13 in St. Petersburg, we hold a test camp. The task of the event is to combine the knowledge of IT specialists and civil society activists to effectively address social issues. We turn to habra people who are interested in non-commercial or temporarily non-commercial start-ups and any other IT-initiatives.



TEST Camp is a creative lab format, something between Hackday and Social Innovation Camp, in 48 hours we create and think through applications.

To participate, you need:
  • Submit the application with your idea until May 8, 2012;
  • Enter the top five in the general voting;
  • Tip from KO: Come to the event.


For all teams, we offer fast hosting for the duration of the event, as well as access to the online interface prototyping panel. All code and content is laid out under a free license indicating the authorship of the team of teams. Further, the authors are free to do with their project what they want.
