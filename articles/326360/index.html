1. Fintech companies will compete with banks for experts in machine learning.




And it will not be a cosmic battle, but quite real. We see that the demand for machine learning specialists and those who were related to similar products is growing insanely.

Plus, sometimes cumbersome banks cannot quickly find and implement tools for analytics. But now banks are dramatically raising the bar - a wave of technological solutions, young minds and progressive ideas has covered the sphere.

There is a lot of data and there will be more and more, but so far there are not enough specialists (!) - now you know which profession is better to master in the coming year. Yes, analysts are welcome and grab like hot space cakes.
