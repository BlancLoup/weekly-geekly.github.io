New level editor for Portal 2

Former Google employee working on Google SketchUp, a program for quickly creating and editing 3D graphics. Works at Valve on a new level editor for Portal 2.



The main feature - the incredible simplicity of the interface and the process of creating levels. Isometric iconography, the location of objects by dragging and dropping from the library - all this will allow a lot more people to translate their ideas into a finished level. And to share this level, you also do not have to go through seven circles of hell with downloading archives is not clear where - Valve is working on a browser built into the levels of the game, which will allow you to download and download custom maps.

Translation news from the book The Final Hours of Portal 2 written by Valve. It tells about the process of creating this great game, from the very beginning of development to the release of the game.
