Google Offers Discount Service Expands



Despite the fact that Facebook's competitor Groupon was unsuccessful, Google Offers, a variant of the same Groupon from Google, is gradually developing. Recently, this service has become available to residents of US cities such as Austin, Boston, Washington, Denver and Seattle. In different cities, the service from Google offers various discounts, including food, books, and more.

For the convenience of users, developers have introduced the possibility of subscribing to news about discounts in various regions. At the moment, this service operates in eight different regions, and it seems that the service is quite successful. A complete list of regions where Google Offers works can be found here . Perhaps, for someone from the representatives of the community, this information will be useful.

Google Offers has been working since January of this year, gradually expanding. The expansion concerns not only the number of regions where services can be used, but also cooperation with various Internet companies.

In general, the "discount" service sector seems to be declining. At least the same Facebook and Yelp shut down related services. Even Groupon may have some difficulties. At least, this company has postponed access to the IPO, and it may well be that access to the stock exchange does not take place at all.
