Russian students are expected at the international competition of Microsoft in AI

We announce the launch of the International The Next AI Guardian competition for students. It will be held in 27 countries of the world, including Russia. The main goal of the competition is to propose solutions based on artificial intelligence, which will help to change the sphere of education for the better. The winners will receive a unique opportunity to attend a three-day intensive course on artificial intelligence in Warsaw, where they will be trained by leading experts in this field - including Microsoft engineers and the company's largest partners.
