January 21 - "Megaplan - a tool for effective business management", a free webinar Softkey and "Megaplan"

The Softkey software online supermarket and Megaplan hold an online seminar for specialists and organizations whose activities are based on the simultaneous management of many projects and tasks, as well as those who want to systematize and automate the workflow.

The webinar will be useful for managers of companies numbering up to 100 people, IT directors, heads of advertising and marketing departments, as well as anyone interested in project management.

Date: January 21, 2010
Time: from 11:00 to 12:00 (Moscow Time).
Theme: "Megaplan - a tool for effective business management."
Speaker: Mikhail Smolyanov, CEO of Megaplan.

Register for the webinar here .
The event itself will be held on a special platform Softkey .


In the webinar program:

The webinar will talk about the product for comfortable business management - Megaplan.

Megaplan is a business management system for medium and small companies: Task Manager, Intranet and Project Manager for any organization based on the simultaneous management of multiple projects and tasks.

The webinar will reveal and demonstrate the main features of the product in theory and in practice.

The practical part of the seminar will demonstrate the operation of the system on the example of a real company.
