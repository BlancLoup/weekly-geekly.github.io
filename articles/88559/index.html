ThickButtons

Dear Habra community, I want to bring to your attention the startup Thickbuttons , which was announced this night at the conference DEMO Spring 2010 . This is a new technology that allows you to conveniently and accurately type text on touchscreen phones, depending on the typed characters, Thickbuttons increases the buttons that are most likely to be pressed next. The application is already available on the Android Market.






Idea



The idea is that the application increases the useful buttons by reducing the "unnecessary". According to the founders, they decided to do it this way, because it is much easier to predict which symbols will not be needed exactly than to guess useful symbols or even a whole word, as other technologies do. As a result, after typing the first few characters, useful buttons increase by 15‚Äì20%, which, in essence, is equivalent to typing in text in horizontal mode.
