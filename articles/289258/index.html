Work on the draft on the preliminary filtering of the runet is suspended: it is unclear how to implement it and who will pay

Elena Mizulina, through her representative, transmitted to Vedomosti information about the suspension of work on the draft law on preliminary filtering of the RuNet. The main reasons are: it is not determined who will finance the implementation of the bill, and how to do it technically. If industry experts clarify these issues, the work will continue.

The chairman of the Duma Committee on Family, Women and Children Elena Mizulina in 2014 proposed filtering the default Internet to protect children from harmful information.
