    Nintendo, stubbornly following its "samurai" path, so different from the policies of other industrial leaders, can safely record 2006 in the list of the most successful in its long history.     In Western countries and in Asia, online gaming has been experiencing a real heyday for several years.     Merge AMD and ATi
    The largest event in the field of gaming "iron" occurred in July of this year, when the manufacturer of computer processors AMD acquired the Canadian company ATi, specializing in the creation of graphics chips. The amount of the transaction amounted to more than 5.4 billion, but it is unlikely that something will change for the players: the ATi brand and the famous Radeon video cards will be preserved. And a new alliance may soon offer an interesting novelty: a hybrid of graphic and conventional processor for computers.
