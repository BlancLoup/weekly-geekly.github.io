Three projects created in 2 days

Last weekend, a special Startup Weekend was held at the Digital October center in Moscow, dedicated to collecting teams. From the ideas presented in the morning by a general vote, the participants selected the 15 most interesting ones and divided them into teams.

We want to talk about three projects that not only managed to assemble a team, but also show the first results of their work in two days.

EpicHero


The idea of ‚Äã‚ÄãEpicHero was presented by Dmitry Lushnikov. EpicHero is a Kikstarter type mobile app for charity.

In EpicHero, the sources of the projects are foundations and non-profit organizations, they post their initiatives, offer a concrete solution to a social problem, and indicate the exact amount needed for implementation. You can choose any project and help it with any amount by making an in-app purchase using the payment method. In the "news" section you can observe the result of your participation and the progress of the project: you will see exactly how you helped and what specifically was done.

sRANEC


The project was transformed from the idea presented by Ekaterina Kotenko. sRanet is an application for recording, storing and distributing educational materials, the initial audience is schoolchildren and students.

You can record a lecture using a tablet or computer: enter formulas, draw charts and graphs, add audio and video files. The recorded lecture is synchronized with all authorized devices. You can exchange lectures with other participants: the more materials you upload, the more materials become available to you. Or you can buy a subscription (for special points) and get access to the entire archive of lectures.

There Food


The author of the service wished to save incognito. There Food is a geolocation service for finding food: you can find an institution closest to you and find out where to eat the cheapest.

By the way, in contrast to the Menutka service, already invested by Glavstart , where the choice of the establishment is based on your preferences and the specific dishes are recommended, in ‚ÄúThere Food‚Äù the emphasis is on the pricing policy of the establishments.
