Winners of the European awards ceremony for the best startups of The Europas 2011



Yesterday, November 17, the annual awards to the best startups, The Europas , took place. The choice of 22 nominations was carried out both on the basis of an expert vote, and on the basis of 40,000 votes from theeuropas.com visitors.

More than 500 guests attended the ceremony held in London this year. Presentations of participating companies and awarding of winners lasted all day, from 9:00 am and until 8:00 pm. This is the third event of this kind, and its scale continues to grow: the number of unique votes in the Internet voting has increased by 7,000 compared with last year.

We present you the list of winners in all nominations (you can familiarize yourself with the full list of participants at the link ):

Orrick is one of the world's largest law firms, with more than 1,100 lawyers in 23 offices around the world, representing over 800 companies, including young companies from GreenTech Media Top 50 tops Greentech Startups, The Wall Street Journal's Top 50 Venture-Backed Companies and BusinessWeek's 50 Best Tech Start-Ups.
