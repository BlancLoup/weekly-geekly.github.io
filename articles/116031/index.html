Great themes for ADW Launcher, Go Launcher EX, MIUI ROM

I present to you a free translation of the article " Outstanding Themes to Redesign Your Android "

Android customization is one of the most favorite features of this operating system. The ability to customize the appearance of all aspects of the operating system allows you to very finely and conveniently configure a mobile phone for each user. The easiest way is to set up the initial screen and many launchers have already been released for it. They all differ in terms of capabilities, performance and design, but they also borrow different functions from each other, allowing users to choose the best combination of functionality and features for themselves. Some of the launchers support themes - with the help of them you can change the set of visual effects, icons, widgets, and various background images.

ADW Launcher


ADW Launcher is one of the oldest and most popular launchers. In addition to the huge number of settings, ADW Launcher supports the change of themes. ADW can change everything on the main screen: wallpaper, dock icons, widgets. Most recently, a paid version of this lauchenr was released: ADW Launcher Ex. In the paid version of the launcher, you have even more options, the creation of several docks and new visual effects.

Go launcher ex


Go Launcher Ex (previously just Go Launcher) is one of the youngest launchers for Android, but already has a huge number of fans. True, he does not differ in anything radical from his competitors - rather, this is a collection of the best from his competitors, plus a couple of his ideas. This creates a very good balance between power, flexibility and speed.

The themes for Go Launcher Ex are much less than for ADW Launcher, but all of them are released by the developers of this launcher and their quality is at the height! At the moment, only 9 themes are available on the Android Market for Go Launcher Ex.

MIUI ROM


MIUI ROM is one of the ROM versions for Android, which combines the best of iOS and Android interfaces and adds a bit of magic to create a completely unique interface for your smartphone. Owners of Android smartphones can basically only drool because MIUI ROM is designed for the Chinese market (respectively, the language in this ROM is Chinese), but the developers are already working on translating MIUI ROM and the ability to use MIUI ROM outside of China on all Android smartphones .
