Simplify the life of the user , striving for ease of use of various services, designed Russian service Bestpersons.ru, which aims to unite social networks.

After a month and a half of development, we present to your attention an updated version of the service .





BestPersons.ru - allows you to combine all your accounts in social networks on one page.
You can read the posts of your friends (friends) from all their sites, as well as write posts to your blogs (crossposting).

What's new:
  • New design and intuitive interface;
  • convenient reading of the combined frendlents, AJAX;
  • convenient posting and cross-posting of entries in your blogs (livejournal, twitter, liveinternet, habrahabr and many others);
  • Add any sites to your profile and import posts (RSS) from them into your feed ;
  • adding friends from any sites to your friendlist at the address of their page, even those that are not yet registered with bestpersons;
  • simple and convenient instant messenger between users;
  • convenient search of friends by their nickname in any social network, e-mail, icq or address of their site, and also supports automatic search through Google Social Graph;
  • user statuses; you always know the mood of your friends and who is online now;
  • changeable access rights to view your sites, contacts and records, delete records imported to bp ;

  • https version for security concerns;
