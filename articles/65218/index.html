Do you steal? Get a discount on software

Microsoft has summed up the unprecedented decline in prices for licensed software in China. For example, the price of the MS Office package was reduced to $ 29 last September. The result of such an action turned out to be super-successful: for the year, sales of MS Office increased by 800%.

Steve Ballmer explained why it was China that lowered prices as low as possible. The fact is that the level of piracy on MS Office there is 95%. The logic is clear - the higher the level of piracy, the greater the discount must be given. Actually, this is the new Microsoft policy. For India and Brazil, they also lowered prices.
