Imagine Camps: January 26 and 31, 2013 Take the first step in youth entrepreneurship

Dear habrachiteli (and especially habrastudenty)!

We have repeatedly told you about the Imagine Cup competition , which for many students is an incentive to implement some of their software projects and thus make the first step in the direction of youth entrepreneurship. The application deadline for the competition expires on February 15, 2013 , and on the eve of this we are holding a traditional school called Imagine Camp . This time the school will be held in Moscow on January 26, 2013 , and also online - January 31, 2013 . Come to us - have a good time, meet the right people (first of all with each other), and also do something good over the weekend! See how similar events were held :



Registration at Imagine Camp January 26 in Moscow
Register for a virtual Imagine Camp January 31

Even if you have not yet begun to think about participating in the competition, you will quite have time to come up with a project, start its implementation and submit an application . Schools are held with the support of the Skolkovo Open University , and are the logical conclusion of a series of schools that were held in Tomsk, St. Petersburg, Vladivostok, and another 15 universities in Russia.

This event is for those who:
  • Already participated in the largest student competition Imagine Cup and wants this year to improve their success.
  • I heard about the Imagine Cup, but did not participate, because I could not come up with an idea, find a team, etc.
  • I have never heard of the Imagine Cup, but I want to learn how to get the most valuable knowledge and experience in creating software projects, an interesting line in the resume, as well as how to go from idea to full-fledged startup and get Microsoft support in implementing their ideas
  • Wants to learn how to program a wonderful Kinect controller, Windows Phone or Windows 8
  • He just wants to talk with the most active students of Moscow and Russia

Moscow Imagine Camp will take place on January 26, 2013 , at 11:00 in the Microsoft office in Krylatskoye. If possible, bring laptops with you to start working on projects right at the event. To participate, it is recommended to pre-register .

Highlights of the event:
  • Acquaintance , exchange of ideas , formation of teams
  • Secrets of participation in the Imagine Cup and the secret technology of inventing brilliant ideas
  • Amazing Kinect Tricks
  • Individual consultations with the organizers of the competition and members of the jury
  • Lecture on youth entrepreneurship

Starting from 10:30 from m. Krylatskoe (exit from the 1st car from the center, 2 times to the right), buses will go to the office where you can treat yourself to welcome coffee and start communicating. The event itself will begin at 11:00 and end at approximately 6: 00-19: 00. A pleasant surprise will be received by participants from the university where the most participants will come from! Therefore, invite your friends and come yourself - spend another day off with benefits!
For those who cannot participate personally and / or live far from Moscow - a virtual Imagine Camp will be held on January 31, 2013, at 11:00 Moscow time. To participate, it is also recommended to register , and the day before the event you will receive an e-mail link to participate. If you have not received the information, as well as on all operational issues - contact Dmitry Soshnikov on Twitter .

Imagine Camp is just the beginning! For competitors who submit their application for the competition , we envisage a larger training program , including technological and entrepreneurial training, which will allow you to create a product prototype and bring your idea closer to implementation, even if you are not yet able to create applications. But the first step is to do now!
