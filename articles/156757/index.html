48 cores for smartphones and tablets



Intel announced its intention to create a processor on 48 cores, focused on mobile devices.

Experts say that it will help to divide the intensive load between several cores. The presence of such a number of cores means improved multi-tasking capabilities, improved performance and optimized power consumption, compared to current processors on 2 and 4 cores.

Justin Ratner, Chief Technical Officer, notes that devices with such processors can appear on the market much earlier than predicted (as predicted in 10 years).
