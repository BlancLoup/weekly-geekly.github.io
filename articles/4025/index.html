OrangeHRM - open source personnel management system

OrangeHRM is said to be the world's first open source personnel management system.

Since the launch of the beta in January 2006, over 28,000 downloads from SourceForge have been registered. In January 2007, OrangeHRM entered the top 30 programs with the highest user rating. The system works both under Linux and under Windows and is distributed under the GPL license.

In the main office of the company in New Jersey, only 2 people work: CEO Sujee Saparamadu (Sujee Saparamadu) and his assistant. In general, the company operates worldwide. The Technical Director is in California, the main architect is in Texas. The marketing guys work in Denmark, and all research and development is done in Kolumbo (Sri Lanka).

The business model is such that in the future the product will be commercial. And now the company's strength is thrown on the creation of a community around the system.

This is a new software development model. Your people and your company are anywhere, and you use the Internet to put everything together.
