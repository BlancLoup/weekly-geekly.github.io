Superjob study


During the year from January 2015 to January 2016, the number of vacancies in the field of information technology has decreased by 24%. At the same time, the number of resumes increased by the same amount.

The company notes that if we compare the situation with 2008, when the activity in recruiting has dropped several times, now the situation is much more interesting - the structure of hiring itself is changing. The decrease in the number of vacancies in the IT industry was due to the mass segment.

‚ÄúThis is a business response to a change in the economy - hiring for solving problems of extensive growth is declining. At the same time, the importance of increasing efficiency is increasing, ‚Äùquoted analysts of the portal CNews.

Most of all - almost 2 times - the demand for IT specialists related to cellular, wireless and network technologies has decreased. At the same time, the demand for CRM specialists (+ 39%), mobile developers (+ 29%) and project management (+ 10%) increased.

Applicants are reorienting to areas with growing demand: the number of resumes in the development and administration of CRM systems increased by 71%, in mobile development - by 54%, in project management - by 63%.

Almost a quarter of vacancies for specialists in the field of information technology are in IT companies. The second place is in the demand for IT specialists - the production sector (18% of all vacancies for the IT sector), retail trade lags behind very little (17%).
