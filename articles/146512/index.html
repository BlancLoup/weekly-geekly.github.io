Microsoft buys Yammer for $ 1.2 billion

Rumors are fully confirmed: Microsoft buys Yammer corporate social network for $ 1.2 billion.
Yammer will join the Microsoft Office division, the company will continue to develop independently, under the guidance of the current CEO David Sachs.
At the time of purchase, Yammer has 5 million corporate users; 85% of Fortune 500 companies use the social network.
Yammer is expected to integrate with SharePoint, Office 365, Microsoft Dynamics and Skype.
When the transaction is completed at the moment no information is given.
