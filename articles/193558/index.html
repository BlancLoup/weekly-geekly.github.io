Sony SmartWatch 2 is available for pre-order.

A new model of smart watches from Sony - SmartWatch 2 has become available for pre-order.

They boast increased performance, as well as trend moisture resistance today (IP57). The 1.6-inch screen with a resolution of 220x176 perfectly shows itself in bright sunlight. The case is made of aluminum, and the strap is either silicone or stainless steel.



Key Features:
- Dimensions 42 x 9 x 41 mm
- Weight SmartWatch 2 + wrist bracelet (included): 122.5 g
- Multitouch, supporting up to three touches
- NFC support
- Replaceable straps
- Micro USB Charging
- Bluetooth 3.0

The watch is compatible with any smartphone based on Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) OS or later. With infrequent use, they promise up to seven days of work without recharging.
The package includes SmartWatch 2 SW2, Wrist bracelet, USB cable for charging Micro USB, Documentation.
The cost of the device is 5990 p.
