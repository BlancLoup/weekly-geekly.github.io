The report on the results of "My Circle" for November 2016, and the most popular vacancies of the month

And now in more detail about the responses to vacancies in the most popular areas of activity.





  • Most job seekers respond to design jobs, an average of 22-23 people per month.
  • An average of 14-15 specialists respond to layout vacancies.
  • On average, 12-13 specialists also respond to the vacancies of administrators .
  • On average, 11-12 specialists respond to managerial vacancies. In November, a record was set with almost 15 vacancy responses on average.
  • On the back of the programmers , an average of 8-9 people responds.
  • On average, 8-9 people respond to job testers . In November, a record was set with almost 11 vacancy responses on average.
  • On average, 6-7 specialists respond to vacancies of developers of mobile applications and software .
