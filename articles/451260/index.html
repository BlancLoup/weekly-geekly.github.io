10 thematic events at ITMO University

This is a selection for professionals, technical students and their younger colleagues. In this digest we will talk about the next thematic events (May, June and July).

1. Investment pitch session from iHarvest Angels and FT ITMO

When: May 22 (submission of applications until May 13)
What time: starting at 14:30
Where: Exchange Lin., D. 14, ITMO University, Aud. 611

The iHarvest Angels business angel club invests from 3 million rubles in projects with development prospects in the international market. In order to present your startup club to the Fitch Technologies business accelerator-based pitch session, you need to fill out a short questionnaire before May 13. We invite projects with an already formed team, ready-made prototype ( MVP ) and confirmed demand for your product (there are first customers / sales / partnership agreements, etc.). The pitch session will be held in 4x4 format: 4-minute presentations with an additional 4 minutes for answering expert questions.

2. Competition of projects from the Faculty of Physics and Technology


When: submission of applications until May 15

We support the project approach and provide an opportunity to implement your ideas on the basis of the ITMO University and the Sirius Educational Center. Our task is to search for projects in the field of physics, suitable for collaboration with schoolchildren and students as semester tasks. Both schoolchildren themselves and their teachers can participate, as well as university staff, students and graduate students from any region of the country. Deadline for submission of applications - May 15. Here you can find information on how to prepare them.

3. Festival for schoolchildren ITMO.START


When: May 19
What time: starting at 12:00
Where: st. Lomonosova, 9, ITMO University

We invite students of 5-10th grades and their parents to our thematic festival. We have prepared an interactive platform with the developments of our student laboratories, master classes and thematic lectures. The main objective of the event is the presentation of opportunities for schoolchildren at the ITMO University. Registration is required to participate.

4. The game on the models of social entrepreneurship "Good business"


When: May 25
What time: from 11:30 to 16:30
Where: Bolshaya Pushkarskaya ul., 10, art-space "Easy-Easy"

An open event for those who would like to try their hand at analytics - the development of sustainable monetization models and business modeling. The workshop will be conducted based on the Models of Impact Toolkit methodology. The participants will be assisted by experts: Grigori Martishin (ambassador of Models of Impact in Russia), Irina Vishnevskaya (director of the Social Innovation Center of the Leningrad Region), Elena Gavrilova (director of the Center for Entrepreneurship of ITMO University) and Anastasia Moskvina (expert of the Center for Social Entrepreneurship and Social Innovations of the Higher School of Economics) ).

7. International Conference of Young Scientists and Computer Modeling Specialists (YSC-2019)
