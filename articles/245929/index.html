Freelancers survey

One of the very first (and most important) questions that every freelancer has to face is the cost of your work. As a freelancer, how do you determine the value of your work hours? Do you check average market rates and rates of other freelancers? When calculating the cost of your work hours, you will certainly take into account your education, location, additional skills, competition in your segment, the target audience of customers and many, many other factors.



At the end of last week we launched a survey for freelancers, and already more than 10,000 people from around the world took part in it. When the survey is completed, we will collect and analyze the information received, presenting the results for relevant countries to your attention.

Does education affect the cost of work? Should you raise your bid, and how much do freelancers get with your skill set?
Participate in the survey and get your copy of the results in an email.

Link to participate: http://paynr.co/surveyru .
