Web Ready Contest has become GoTech Business Integrator

Web Ready - the largest independent competition of technology companies in Eastern Europe - not only changed the name, but also significantly revised its concept. From a traditional competition, GoTech has become an integrator of the best technology companies into a business of strategic partners. Call for applications opened on March 15, and the first results will be announced on October 6, 2016, when the GoTech Forum will be held as part of GoTech.



In essence, GoTech will not only collect applications and evaluate technology small and medium businesses, but also ‚Äúpack‚Äù it for the needs of strategic partners, as well as tracking projects interesting to strategists. Participants will receive additional funding opportunities and access to corporate resources, and strategic partners will receive technology projects prepared for implementation.

The first partner of GoTech was EMC corporation, which announced the collection of applications from companies developing projects in the field of Internet of things (IoT) and big data analytics (Big Data & Analytics) or creating technologies for a smart city (Smart City Tech). The winner of the nomination in addition to the cash prize will be able to launch a joint go-to-market project with EMC.

Together with Kaspersky Lab, a special nomination for Next Generation Security has been announced. We invite technology companies and projects that create products to protect against current or future cyber threats.

Companies that develop technology projects in the field of IT and related areas are invited to participate in the competition .

Dmitry Chikhachev (Runa Capital), Alexander Egorov (Reksoft), Konstantin Sinyushin (the Untitled VC), Vyacheslav Nesterov (EMC), Igor Kaloshin (Intel), Igor Bogachev (Skolkovo Foundation) joined the GoTech supervisory board.
