3. Citizens.
Citizens are a separate item of expenditure. Often considerable. It is not about customers, but about the attitude of people to property. Including the property of their neighbors.
The program for the overhaul of the housing stock initiated in 2006 by the state is a major headache that lasts for the fourth year.
People involved in the repair of soft roofs of high-rise buildings have a definitely barbaric mindset. Cut off all that is fixed and burn all that is chopped off - the main task.
The problem is solved in many ways. The main optical cables are placed as compact and high as possible, the plants in the room are organized at the maximum distance from the soft roof. House communications descend from roofs and facades to attics, and into panels. Where this is not possible, special remote fasteners (often manufactured by special order) are used to remove the cable system beyond the reach of flamethrowers and axes.

In houses, the cable system of which is laid on the plates, occasionally incidents occur and the cable insulation melts and the pairs are closed. Most often from cigarette butts, which neighbors extinguish shoving in the open shield. There were also cases of theft of a twisted-pair house and a number of active equipment located in shields.
Countermeasures are limited to calling the installation team, which hangs a lock and a sticker with a phone number on the door of the dashboard if someone suddenly needs it. A small branch is usually locked, in which there are no counters.
