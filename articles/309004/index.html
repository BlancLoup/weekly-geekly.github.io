Data Science Week 2016. Data Technologies Forum

Hello! We are glad to invite you to the second Data Science Week forum, which will be held on September 8, 9, 12 and 13 in Moscow. You will find more than 20 open workshops and lectures from specialists from such companies as Microsoft, Rambler & Co, Sberbank, Avito, DCA, E-Contenta, Segmento.

Each day will be devoted to a separate topic: customer relationship, internal optimization, Sberbank Data Day, artificial intelligence.



Day 1. Big Data - Customer Relationship

On the first day we will talk about how you can drastically build relationships with a client in a different way using big data. Companies that create products in the field of marketing and advertising will talk about what data can be collected about customers and how they can be used to increase customer satisfaction and profit growth. Experts will pay attention to creating personalized offers, developing recommender systems, and client-based data services.

Day 2. Big Data - Internal Optimization

The second day will be devoted to everything related to the internal optimization of the business. Companies from different industries will share their experience on how they were able to reduce personnel costs, reduce production risks, reduce fraud, and increase productivity. Business representatives will talk about the use of predictive models, the difficulties faced from the data collection stage to the implementation of the final models in production.

Day 3. Sberbank Data Day

Sberbank in a short time managed to acquire the image of a company striving for a high rate of change and the introduction of new technologies in its daily activities. Representatives of the bank itself, as well as its partners, will tell how their situation really is in this direction - what has already been done and implemented, and what remains as yet intentions.

Day 4. Artificial Intelligence

Participation is free, but registration is required.
