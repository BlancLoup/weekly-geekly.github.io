Zeo ZQ - sleep correction alarm clock



Zeo believes that not all people sleep correctly and offer to help. For the money, of course.

The alarm clock that will help you to see fearless dreams is called ZQ. At bedtime, the user attaches a wrap with sensors to his head and at night the machine records all the information about the behavior of your brain: how often the sleep phases alternate, how long you stay in them, and so on. Data is processed and recorded on the SD card. After that, they can be sent to the Zeo website, where graduates, based on them, will already give specific recommendations on how and what to do.

In general, everything would be fine, but confusing price. The first six months of "sleepy" training will cost $ 399, and every subsequent, six months, another 99.
