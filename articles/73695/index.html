Collecting friends about yourself

The opinion of those around him is always important to a person as a social creature. And to me, as the founder of a not-yet-launched project to collect the opinions of friends , it is important that the habrasoobschestva about this project.

After we started the project on dubbing articles , which turned out to be unclaimed, and the rest, it was decided to take up a long-standing idea to create a service to collect the opinions of friends about ourselves. The launch is on the way, and here I am ripe for a public story. It turned out in the presentation style for the investor, but I‚Äôll say at once that the investor is not required.

Relevance of the topic


It is important for people, the opinion of friends is interesting. Knowing something good from the opinions of friends, a man is proud of it. Knowing something that does not seem attractive, a person can try to change for the better. People like to compare themselves with others.
Yes, there are people who believe that the opinions of those around them are not important.

Decision


Service to collect opinions of friends about yourself.
  • The person is registered on the site
  • Receives a link and sends it to friends
  • Friends answer questions
  • A person gets an opinion of friends about himself

As arranged


Each answer to a question determines a person by some characteristic - a certain number of points (kindness, justice, beauty, intellect, etc. - 30 characteristics). After collecting a certain number of answers from a certain number of friends, the average score for each characteristic is calculated. Each characteristic has several text transcripts, depending on the number of points gained. These decryptions are collected in the text, and the text is issued to the user. The questionnaire was compiled by joint efforts of a professional copywriter, psychologist, proofreader under my leadership.

Chips

  • Cool, cool questions and answers.
  • Cool, cool decoding characteristics.
  • Decryption characteristics in the form of a certificate.
  • Virtual interlocutor - on the left, the character speaks depending on the question asked and the selected answer. + his picture changes - various facial expressions. + he can write something and he will answer.
  • You can see which questions were answered by friends (who exactly answered which question - no)
  • A brief description of a man of two or three words is given. (For example, "Friends think you're super modest handsome")
  • Lots of various other nice little things

Business model


Free: decoding only 2-3 characteristics from 30, and only 3-5 answers from friends.
For a fee, for SMS: full decoding and all answers of friends (who as answered has not been issued).

Technology


YII framework, exporting the questionnaire from their Excel file, SMS billing, where you need Ajax.

Promotion


Viral effect. This project is one of the few that spreads precisely because of the viral effect.
