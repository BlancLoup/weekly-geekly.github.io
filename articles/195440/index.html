Humble Weekly Sale: Kalypso Media



Less than a day after the release of the Humble Mobile Bundle 2 , the weekly offer was updated on the site: we meet the Humble Weekly Sale: Kalypso Media Bundle .

This time, the distribution includes games for Windows, and one game from the compilation is also available for Linux and Mac. Attention: the keys for Steam are issued only with a donation of more than $ 1, DRM-free is absent in the collection.

During the week you can get:
  • Tropico 3: Steam Special Edition ($ 199 / $ 5.99 * on Steam )
  • Sine Mora ($ 4 / $ 7.99 on Steam )
  • SkyDrift ($ 4 / $ 7.99 on Steam )
  • Anna - Extended Edition + Soundtrack ($ 4 / $ 7.99 on Steam , Windows, Linux and Mac versions)

And if you pay more than $ 6.00, you will receive:
  • Tropico 4: Steam Special Edition ($ 109 / $ 17.99 on Steam )
  • Jagged Alliance: Back in Action ($ 109 / $ 17.99 on Steam )

Thus, you can save up to 900 rubles if you need only four games.
Stay tuned for updates - not all Kalypso games presented on Steam are present in the compilation: adding is possible.

* prices are for Russia and the CIS respectively
