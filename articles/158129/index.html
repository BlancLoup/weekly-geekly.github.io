ASUS, Windows 8 and releases

ASUS launches Windows 8-based devices on the Russian market. These are ASUS Vivo Tab and ASUS Vivo Tab RT tablet computers (ASUS TF 810 and ASUS TF600, respectively), a series of ASUS Vivo Book laptops (X202, S400, U38), ultrabook ASUS TAICHI, ASUS Transformer Book - a laptop that transforms into a tablet PC, ASUS ZENBOOK Prime UX31A Touch is the first model in the ZENBOOK series equipped with a multi-touch display, as well as a series of 21.5-inch monoblock computers ASUS ET2220. ASUS updates the entire product line and launches new devices, a distinctive feature of which is the presence of a touch interface.

ASUS Vivo Tab / ASUS Vivo Tab RT



ASUS Vivo Tab is equipped with the latest Intel Atom processor, 2 GB of system memory and 64 GB user memory. The tablet is equipped with a mobile docking station, with which the Vivo Tab can be turned into a netbook with a keyboard and touchpad. In addition, the docking station adds a pair of USB ports and increases battery life due to an additional battery.

ASUS Vivo Tab will be available in late November and early December at a price of 40,000 rubles.

The hardware configuration of ASUS Vivo Tab RT includes a quad-core NVIDIA Tegra 3 processor with an integrated graphics core, 2 GB of system memory and 64 GB of user memory. The tablet runs under the operating system Windows RT. Like the Vivo Tab, it can be used with a mobile docking station that offers a keyboard, touchpad, USB port and extra battery.

The device ASUS Vivo Tab RT is already available at a price of 29,990 rubles.

ASUS VivoBook



A new series of laptops ASUS VivoBook (X202, S400, U38). Quality materials, a wide selection of color options and, most importantly, a convenient touch screen - these are the distinguishing features of the VivoBook series.

Laptops series X202 is already sold at a price of 18,000 rubles. Ultrabooks of the S400 series will be available this month at a price of 33,000 rubles, and laptops of the U38 series will appear in early December at a price of 28,000 rubles.

ASUS TAICHI



When opened, the TAICHI looks like any other ultrabook with a full-sized keyboard and touchpad. However, as soon as the lid closes, it instantly turns into a tablet computer with a multi-touch screen and stylus support. Despite the fact that both screens are connected to the same hardware configuration, they operate completely independently of each other, allowing two users to work simultaneously with TAICHI simultaneously. The device is powered by a third-generation Intel Core processor, equipped with a solid-state drive and a dual-band wireless module Wi-Fi 802.11n.

ASUS TAICHI will appear in early December and will be sold at a price of 52,000 rubles.

The hardware configuration of the Transformer Book includes the latest third-generation Intel Core i7 processor, solid-state drive and hard drive.

ASUS Transformer Book will be available in mid-December at a price of 60,000 rubles.

ASUS ZENBOOK Prime UX31A Touch



ASUS ZENBOOK Prime UX31A Touch is the first model in the ZENBOOK series, equipped with a multi-touch display. It has a third-generation Intel Core processor and a solid-state drive up to 256 GB

Among the hardware advantages of ZENBOOK Prime UX31A Touch, you can also mention the Super IPS + screen with wide viewing angles (178 ¬∞), USB 3.0 interface and a dual-band wireless module Wi-Fi 802.11n.

The appearance on the Russian market ASUS ZENBOOK Prime UX31A Touch is expected in mid-December. The device will be available at a price of 63,990 rubles.

A series of 21.5-inch monoblock computers ASUS ET2220



ASUS ET2220 series monoblock computers are enclosed in a compact case with a stylish design. The configuration includes a third-generation Intel Core i5 processor, up to four USB 3.0 ports, and a multi-touch screen.

ASUS ET2220 devices will appear in late December and will be sold at a price of 25,000 rubles.
