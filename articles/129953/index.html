KIA Rio destroyed Youtube

Not without the participation of DEFA Interaktiv, the new KIA Rio went beyond the framework of the usual player directly to the users of the popular video service.



Russian advertisers still rarely use non-standard features of large Internet portals. The ‚ÄúYoutube Mimicry‚Äù format is already well known to Western users and has earned a good reputation as a wow-tool. The essence of the solution lies in the unexpected departure of the video plot beyond the traditional viewing area.

The experience of using the format in Russia has already been, but the number of examples can be counted literally on the fingers of one hand. Now, an advertising campaign of the new KIA Rio on the channel www.youtube.com/KiaRioRus has been added to the number of the first Russian applications of the Youtube Mimicry format.
