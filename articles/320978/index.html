We invite you to Data Fest⁴ February 11 and 12



In almost two weeks, Data Fest⁴ will be held in our Moscow office - the fourth conference that brings together researchers, engineers and developers associated with Data Science in all its manifestations. You will find a rich program, a lot of theoretical and practical sections. Read the details under the cut.

Two-day program of lectures, workshops and interaktivov.
More than 30 speakers with advanced results and developments.
16 sections for every taste, with a sea of ​​new knowledge, skills and acquaintances.
Great company and even more networking with the data science community.
