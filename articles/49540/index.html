Perhaps the most annoying Microsoft ad



Everyone's favorite company Microsoft, so beautifully promoting its new OS Windows 7 (which bloggers have affectionately nicknamed "Semka") never ceases to amaze with the commercials of their creations. The last and, perhaps, subjectively, the worst advertising product is dedicated to the Songtastic application, with which it is supposed to record / create / sing along with the music.

And it's not even the Apple laptop, which is carefully sealed with stickers, anonymizing the apple product. And not in the horrific music, under which people sing along, objectively not having musical abilities, but in the aggregate of these factors. Not to mention the fact that for Microsoft, which is trying to create an analogue of the Garage Band, it was worth choosing a diametrically opposite policy (in my opinion), otherwise Songtastic will all relate to the program of little girls and bored office workers.

It remains to hope only that before putting this video into rotation on television, Microsoft will think about the expediency and consequences several times, otherwise it can turn out like with Seinfield .

If you are still going to watch the video to the end - do not say later that you were not warned.
