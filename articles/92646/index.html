And yet it is hacked!


How everyone has been waiting for this event ... And it happened!
Tonight, the famous hacker team Dev-Team has released its Jailbreak utility called Spirit (Spirit)!
It really works!

Most importantly, the Spirit works on all devices (iPad, iPhone or iPod touch) with firmware - 3.1.2, 3.1.3 or 3.2 .
And he also does unlocked hacking - i.e. You will no longer have to connect your iPhone or iPod to your computer for further work.

The whole process took less than 3 seconds on my iPod Touch. Then just download extra. programs (Cydia) directly from your phone. I want to note that there is a version for both Mac and Windows .

If you have been waiting for this - run and try ! It's really worth it: beautiful themes without OS 4.0, free games and many other advantages. Maybe Flash will be there!
