Meet IntelliJ IDEA 12 Leda Preview!

Good news for those who were waiting for new interesting features in IntelliJ IDEA. We are finally pleased to announce that IntelliJ IDEA 12's Early Access Program is officially open. The code name for the new release is Leda. So, meet IntelliJ IDEA 12 Leda!



To try IntelliJ IDEA Leda, you need to download the EAP distribution for your platform.

New features in IntelliJ IDEA 12:

  • Designer UI for Android
  • A completely new approach to compiling a project.
  • JBPM 5.0 support (BPMN 2.0)
  • Play Framework 1.2.4 support
  • Hibernate 4.0 support
  • Groovy 2.0 support
  • TomEE integration
  • IOS Simulator support for AIR Mobile
  • CloudFoundry integration
  • Improved support for Spring Framework for Core, Security and Web Flow
  • Improved CDI support
  • Numerous improvements for Subversion, Git and Perforce
  • Improved work with UML diagrams for Flash and Flex

More information about these and other new features will soon be published in our blog .

We are sure that the best developer tools can be created only by listening to the opinions of users. That is why it is important for us that you try new features as early as possible and tell us your wishes!

Please share your impressions on our EAP forum and submit information about errors found in YouTrack .

We remind you that many functions are under development. So make sure you save your project before opening it in the new version.

Develop with pleasure!
IntelliJ IDEA Team
