Image copy search service

Now piracy has become a fashionable and expensive problem on the Internet: I propose to implement a project providing the following services - searching for specified copies of pictures, photos, videos (there are companies and software that allow you to do this) + there are already variations, but you can add a search for pieces of text of any size (maybe slightly modified) music.

The mechanism is proposed as follows: the customer loads, for example, a photo through the form - the service finds its copies on the network + the possibility of tracking, i.e. show in the future only newly appeared copies. This can be done for free (for advertising), and you can ask for a penny for each copy found on the Internet.

Target audience: 1) direct authors and content providers - search for illegally sold / posted content, incl. text publications and photos of journalists, bloggers - for making claims (for the paid option). 2) different authors - determining the real number of citations with getting all the links where their information is posted, 3) users - for example, to reverse the definition of the author or the original source of a photo or text - often a situation arises, you find an interesting photo on your computer once saved and you cannot remember where it comes from, or on some site you see a photo and want to try to find its author. As far as I know in this volume, no one provides such search services.
