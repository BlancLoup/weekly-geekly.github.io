Cloud Connect-volume called a small add-in for Microsoft Office 2003, 2007 and 2010, which allows you to send your documents to Google Docs without leaving Microsoft Office. When you connect the plugin, an additional socket from Google appears at the top of the Microsoft Office applications, which allows you to share a document on your Google Docs account with one click.
