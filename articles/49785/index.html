Our future is in our hands. Second degree.

Dear friends!

This topic will be devoted to our future with you.
The question is simple: getting a second higher education, already having one crust about higher education behind your back.

What I encountered:

1. Interest and motivation to get a second higher education
2. Search for possible options
3. Possible problems
4. Life issues



I want to note that everything below is my vision of this issue. I, of course, can be wrong about something. :)

Having received the first higher education in SPSUITMO, we with group mates happily burnt numerous copies of diplomas in a nearby urn. We decided that everything! Basta! Free job search (who has not yet found by the time of graduation), career growth, wife, children and so on ... Each of us had concrete plans, if not for life, then for the near future for sure. The army didn't bother the guys, since we all went through the military department. Actually, many for the sake of this military department and went to study there.
A year has passed.

In the specialty, no one went to work, too little wages in the labor market in our profession. We have dispersed who where. Someone went to the car washers, someone to the sales representatives, someone got married, got married and was already waiting for children.
