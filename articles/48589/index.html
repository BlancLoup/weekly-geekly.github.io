Hosting 2.0

A competition of ideas for startups was recently held here, and this idea was invented specifically for him in one evening. Since she did not fit, I decided to put it in open access in the hope that someone would find it interesting and useful.

One of the criteria of the competition was a clear monetization scheme, so I decided to start the discussion by finding out what a person would pay for most of all. Despite the general distribution, the scheme for paying for the rent of software is not quite obvious and many disagree with it.

When I give my money, I need to know that every request for a service will require certain costs, which should be adequate to the cost of the service. You can tell me that this is the stone age, to which I will answer, that this is psychology. It is easier for a person to give money when he knows that this money will go not only to the fund for buying a regular yacht and maintaining the project‚Äôs performance, but will also cover the costs of fulfilling his request.

Go ahead - what could these costs be? In an automated project - computing power and disk space. If you connect people, then ... But most of all, we do not need such a project, for which we still need to hire people, so let's stop at the automated one. So the service will be either in the provision of disk space, or in the provision of computing power. Come on the other side. Who is willing to give money online? First of all, those who have such an opportunity - they have a virtual wallet and, accordingly, have experience of using it. It seems to me that these people belong to the majority of e-wallet owners, so for the time being we‚Äôll concentrate on them.

Now let's try to combine all this. The service should be to provide disk space to freelancers and their employers (often they are serious guys - businessmen).

So, why can you need a hosting for freelancers and businessmen? For the first - this is a portfolio, for the second - a web business card. How is this at the moment? If a person needs to publish his portfolio (business card) online, he hires people. Their job is to buy a domain, develop a design, write code, layout, etc. As you can see, this task requires knowledge in different areas, so usually several people do this kind of work - this raises its value. And in the end, a banal business card site will cost a tidy sum.

In my opinion this is unfair. So I propose to make a service that will simplify the process of creating a business card site or a portfolio (in fact, other not very clever sites) to such an extent that one person who is not familiar with the subtleties of web design can handle this. How it can look, further and the speech will go. But first I will clarify one detail - how should this project be positioned? The key point in this venture is the editor itself. Depends on the project. The editor must be flexible. So that on the one hand any housewife can make a page about herself in him, and on the other hand, so that the person initiated can show his knowledge and his taste.

The process of working with the editor should not resemble the compilation of an identikit or a trivial choice of a topic from the ten suggested.
