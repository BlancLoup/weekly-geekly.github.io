Old Compaq Armada 7730MT

Already for a long time I have been idle with an old laptop Compaq Armada 7730MT. Once upon a time Windows 98 stood on it and it was possible to play Starcraft, but at some point the hard ordered to live long and it was abandoned in the closet. Now, in connection with the planned relocation, old things, once abandoned in the closet, began to be thrown away, among which this curiosity was found. Naturally, it was a pity to throw it away, but there was simply no place to apply it. And so the idea was born to bring it into working condition (good for nothing but replacing hard with it), and it was decided to give it in good hands.

Perhaps one of you, or your friends, collects almost such museum exhibits or owns any collection of old equipment, like the museum of video cards in Kharkov - please unsubscribe in a personal. The laptop went to Ivano-Frankivsk to comrade monohrom , who owns an interesting museum . Thank you all!
