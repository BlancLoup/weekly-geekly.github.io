Windows RT running ... on HD2

On Twitter, well-known in narrow circles of the Cotulla character, interesting screenshots and photos of Windows RT, running on probably the most popular smartphone for developers, HTC HD2, appeared.



It seems that during the porting of Windows 8 he managed to start the UEFI bootloader.
Running Windows RT was only a matter of time, since both OSs use, in fact, one core.

Let me remind you, HTC HD2 is known as a smartphone that hit stores in 2010 with Windows Mobile 6.5 and, thanks to a huge number of developers, it allows you to run any mobile (and not so much) operating system that was just released.

I myself, as the owner of HD2, are really surprised by the fact that a lot of people are still engaged in porting the freshest products to it, including the latest Android, Windows Phone 7+ and so on. do not soar for various reasons.
I just want to say thank you so much and wish you success in your work, guys.
