Embrace smartwatch will help recognize the seizure

Most of the functions that are in modern bracelets can be found in the Embrace smart watch: activity tracker, compatibility with smartphones, time and others. And at the same time, Embrace has several unique features.



The main one is the ability to determine the attack in a person. This function will notify a loved one in time about the critical state of the body, which will allow for promptly providing medical assistance.

At the time of the attack, the relevant application sends a notification to the trustee or to any other person with an Embrace device that begins to vibrate. The app also tracks stress levels, activity throughout the day, and other information.



The smart watch consists of two parts: a strap made from Italian leather, which is attached to the wrist with a magnet and a smooth metal square with LEDs for determining the time. If you use the device solely as a clock, the battery will last for six months.

As part of the Indiegogo campaign, Embrace smart watches have already collected $ 261,000, which is more than twice the original goal of $ 100,000. A pair of Embrace devices will cost Becker $ 349. Simultaneously with the crowdfunding campaign, a charity program was launched: when buying a device, the company donates one device to children suffering from epilepsy.
