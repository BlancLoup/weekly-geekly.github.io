Social media aggregator Meople.net united in one place 10 basic social programs

Social networks through the eyes of the average user




I, like every normal user of the Network, have more than one, more precisely, more than two accounts in various social networks. Somewhere I am active, but somewhere I limit myself to the role of an observer, and have already safely forgotten some of my call signs.

To keep yourself up to date on social networking events, you need to open a separate window for each of them in the browser (this is if you are behind a stationary PC or laptop) or a couple of applications (if you are on a mobile device). And then to these windows / applications periodically return, so as not to fall out of the flow of news and events in the networks.

In principle, I do not lead a super active life in networks, there is someone there to do it. Mostly I observe, I absorb information from friends, from active users, from all kinds of communities, I write private messages, well, of course, photos, how can I show my own and others' views without it.

And if for news RSS-aggregators already exist for a long time, then for social programs such a product, so that it is convenient, has not yet been. Until‚Ä¶

Most recently, Belarusians launched the social networking aggregator Meople.net into the public testing stage .

The resource interface is made in the style of Metro UI. Everything is clear and accessible, but so far without minor bugs. You log in using the aggregator in the social networks you need, give permission to access sections of information and in one browser window you can see all your accounts at the same time.

How it works?

The use of this aggregator for social networks is in principle understandable; there is no inconvenience for a regular Internet user. The first time you just need to get used to the fact that all sources in one place.



In total, you can currently use accounts in 10 networks: LinkedIn, Facebook, Odnoklassniki, Vkontakte, Google+, Twitter, Instagram, Flickr, YouTube and Groupon.

You can manipulate information in Meople.net by dragging and dropping news from one network to another and it will be published there on your behalf. If you need someone very urgently, you can send him a message simultaneously to all networks in which both you and your addressee are. You can view all the albums of the person you are interested in in various social networks. At the same time there is a slide show function.
