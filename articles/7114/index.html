Holidays of all countries and denominations in thematic informers

A few hours ago, the Holiday Calendar project launched a new, curious service for bloggers and website owners - based on a unique database of holidays from different countries and denominations (the second such, by the way, there is no other language in the world) thematic informers for holidays were made.

Now on your website or profile of your LiveJournal, anyone can post a topical informer of one of several dozen types: "holidays of Russia" or "holidays of Ukraine", "Catholic holidays" or "Orthodox holidays", in a word, any holidays from the www .calend.ru , will be on your site.

Examples of informers:



about 40 different types of informers from different countries, religions, beliefs

The project had previously had an informer, but a consolidated one - all holidays for the current day:




today's new, by assumption, should be interesting to thematic blogs and sites. For example, a site about Italy - puts the informer "holidays of Italy", a blog about the ancient Slavs - the informer "holidays of Slavs", a fan of Buddhism - "holidays of Buddhism", etc.

You can get the code of the necessary informer on a specially trained page.
