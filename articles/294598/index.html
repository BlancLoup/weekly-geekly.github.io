Analysis of the convenience of viewing the site on mobile devices using Google tools

The update of Google‚Äôs ranking algorithms on April 21 was a signal for many webmasters to adapt their sites to mobile devices - this has officially become a significant factor in ranking a site in search results. Now, after 5 months, we can say that this trend has not only survived, but continues to evolve. And this means that it is high time to take measures to ensure that your site is ready for viewing on mobile devices.

Here are some facts you need to know about this update:

  • The robot scans page by page sequentially and checks them in real time.
  • Evaluation of readiness of the page for display on mobile devices is binary: either the page is being tested or not. There are no intermediate values.
  • Google provided tools to check the convenience of viewing on mobile devices.
  • Just waiting for this update increased the rate of responsive pages by 5% two months before the release.

If the page passes the test, it is marked with an icon in the mobile version of the search results. Many major western websites such as Reddit, NBC Sports, Vogue, SongLyrics, and Business Week did not have time to make changes and significantly lost ground.

So, what should be done in order not to repeat their sad experience?

As already mentioned, Google has launched several tools for analyzing the work of the site.
