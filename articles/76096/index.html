The first all-Russian student IT-championship. We train! We reward!



Friends! Here comes the long-awaited moment: the social network Campus.ru awards the winners of the First All-Russian Student IT-Championship! The participants of the competition are invited, as well as all those who want to listen to the master classes of the best employers in the IT industry, leave their CV to them, and most importantly - feel the atmosphere of the celebration!

The ceremony will be held on November 26 in Moscow, at the faculty of the VMK of Moscow State University (Leninskie Gory, 1 possession 52) from 16.30 to 20.30. The main prize - a home theater - will be drawn between the winners, and will be handed over by the legend of the Russian Internet, Anatoly Wasserman.
At the ceremony will be representatives of 13 companies - industry leaders. Microsoft and Softline will conduct master classes. Each company will award its winners - those who have successfully completed the tasks. And everyone will be able to freely communicate with employers, discuss professional topics, internship opportunities and practices.

If you wish, you can follow the ceremony without leaving your home thanks to an online broadcast, which will be conducted in real time on the campus of the championship: www.it.campus.ru .

Attention! To participate in the ceremony, you must register. Register here!

With any questions you can contact the organizers: Dmitry Mikhailov, tel: (495) 223-30-34, ext. 130, e-mail: dm@campus.ru.

We are waiting for you at the ceremony!
