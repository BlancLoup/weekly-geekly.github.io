Smart pot for plants Parrot Pot




Many home plants require serious care. They need to be watered at a certain time, and the water must be of a certain temperature (for some species). The most important criterion of plant well-being is the acidity of the soil, as well as the brightness of solar or artificial light. Plus, there are many other factors that novice gardeners hardly know.

It is quite realistic to follow this independently, but this task may not be for everyone. And if the owner of a demanding plant also regularly goes on business trips, the task of leaving becomes almost impossible. Nothing is impossible - the smart Parrot Pot flower pot will take most of the routine tasks of caring for the plant.

The pot has sensors of acidity, temperature, light and water jets, through which it removes life-giving moisture (up to 2.2 liters of water). Plus there is a wireless communication module that provides data transfer to the phone. A special application contains information about 8,000 different indoor plants. The program can not only determine the optimal conditions for the plant, but also give advice to the owner on how much fertilizer is needed and whether this particular flower loves sunlight.

In general, this is a great gift option for a geek - a smart pot for the Parrot Pot plants is useful both at home and in the office.
