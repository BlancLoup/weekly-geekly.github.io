Apoi.ru - navigator in the ocean of purchases and services

Each consumer thinks about how to find a proven organization or quality service, and for this you need to study a lot of information and structure it in your head. Resource Apoi.ru provides the ability to find all the necessary information in one place. The organization is interested in - find it in the catalog or on the map. Get ready for shopping - see discounts. Go on a trip - read the article. Go to the restaurant - look through the reviews. There was a question - ask consultants. And, of course, write reviews!

The portal Apoi.ru is a social search resource, thanks to which visitors can easily find companies, learn about the quality of their service with the help of reviews from other users, navigate prices for services, view news and information about discounts, get advice from professionals and share their personal experience. The site contains information about almost a million companies. Reviews, news and discounts of companies are published daily.



The site is based on the following sections:
  • Search - allows you to search for organizations by criteria: name, address, website, e-mail, phone, service, it is also possible to carry out a visual search on the map. For your convenience, it is possible to limit the request by region, name, verified organizations. And also with the help of additional filters by districts, working hours, user ratings. Search is carried out in all sections of the site.
  • Companies / Categories (navigation menu on the left) - allows you to quickly find the category of services you need.
  • Reviews - allows you to view the latest user reviews about companies. When a new review appears, the company about which the review was posted receives a notification and can respond promptly to the visitor's opinion.
  • News - contains the latest news from companies. They are collected from various sources automatically or published by company representatives.
  • Articles - in this section you can find reviews of the current situation in the selected area of ‚Äã‚Äãservices, a comparative analysis of prices and quality of service, examples from the personal experience of users.
  • Discounts - here are published current discounts and promotions from various companies, the location of which can be immediately seen on the map.
  • Consultations - if you need to find out more information about a service or product, in this section you can get advice on a question of interest to you in areas of interest both from professionals acting on behalf of companies and from individuals.
  • The forum is a place to discuss any questions you may have.

Each site material can be discussed, commented, increased its rating by adding a rating (like).

To help users developed a mobile application for devices based on Android OS. It allows you to find the necessary companies based on location or category. Read and write reviews, plan a route taking into account traffic jams, add photos, share information with friends.

The application contains interesting features: you can leave voice reviews, send information about organizations using SMS messages, as well as for the convenience of users, built-in functionality allows you to instantly transfer information from a computer screen to a mobile device using QR code technology.
