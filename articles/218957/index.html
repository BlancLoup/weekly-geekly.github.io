Roskomnadzor advised to host sites on domestic hosting sites

Today, Roskomnadzor issued a press release warning the website owners against using the popular foreign CDN service CloudFlare. According to the federal service, representatives of the service do not respond to requests to delete sites located on this service and posting content that violates applicable law. Therefore, many bona fide sites will be blocked by providers on the territory of the Russian Federation (that is, apparently, it was decided to block the entire CloudFlare).

Special interest should be given to the end of the press release. In it, Roskomnadzor recommends placing web-sites on the capacities of domestic hosting providers, who conscientiously comply with Russian legislation.
