School of Data "Beeline", on the Neva



Hi, Habr! You have already heard about the fact that we conduct machine learning and data analysis courses at the Beeline Data School . Today we are celebrating the 6th edition on our analytical course and the 4th on the course for managers . Only having time to release one course - we recruit a new one. After each new release, we collect feedback from our listeners, analyze it and make our program even more saturated with practice and examples from real business.

They write to us from all over the country and from outside of it with questions when the School of Data will appear in their city. We responded to these wishes and expand our presence.

Today we are pleased to announce the launch of our program in St. Petersburg ! We collected the best teachers in this glorious city, prepared a wonderful program and in this post we will tell all the details of the training.

The course starts on October 28th, will be held on Mondays and Fridays in the evening from 19.00 to 21.00 in the Beeline company office at Vasilievsky Island, 21 line, 6, letter A (the nearest metro station is Vasileostrovskaya).

Ahead of us waiting for 18 lessons (the course is designed for 9 weeks), in which we will try to cover the most important topics of data analysis.

A distinctive feature of our classes is their practical orientation.

Our country has always been famous for the school of mathematics and today many people who know how to build high-quality models win Kaggle competitions . But for our practice, we have repeatedly seen that this is not enough to make ready-made products that are capable of bringing a measurable money benefit to business.

It is important to have an idea about product metrics, it is important to know about the algorithm development process, how often and how to correctly retrain models and much more.
