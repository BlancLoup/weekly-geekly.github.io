For those who do not know, I explain that Amazon is carrying out a warranty free replacement of its famous reader, even if it is damaged due to your fault (most often, a reckless fall and a broken screen as a result). You could buy a reading room directly in Amazon, and could in any other store - this does not change matters. However, there are difficulties: free replacement is carried out only in the USA and Europe, so if you are a resident of other neighborhoods, welcome to habrakat.

1. We will get an address in the USA.


First we need a postal address in the United States. To do this, use the well-known service Shipito . When registering, we need to pay $ 8.5 for finding the mailing address. It will be valid for a month and you can send yourself one post through it.

To do this, we register on Yandex.Money , replenish the account in any convenient way and order a virtual bank card . When ordering a card, we set the amount that we put on the card balance, for the brave, uncheck the SMS alerts and - voila! It is necessary to add that we will not be given a $ 8.5 card, so it makes sense to calculate in advance all costs and immediately put the necessary amount. The card is still useful to us.

2. Register a reader on Amazon.com


If you have not registered it yet, it's time to do it. Go to the Amazon site, register . In your personal account we add information about a bank card in the Add a Credit or Debit Card section (we need this). In the Add a New Address section, add information about the address we received in Shipito . Next, go to the Manage Your Kindle page and select Register a Kindle in the left column. To register, you need the serial number of the device. If you have a second-generation Kindle, it is listed on the back of the device. If the third one, then it can be found either through Settings ‚Üí Device Information or on the original packaging. We pack the reader in the original packaging (I took it from friends), take the printed picture with bar codes (it identifies what the return is from you) and go to the international post office. 5. Registration of delivery in Russia


Three days later, Shipito received a notification: the parcel arrived at your address (even the photograph of the parcel is attached). In the personal account we indicate the address of delivery to Russia. Fill out the customs declaration at the link Customs Declaration. Choose a shipping method. I recommend USPS Priority Mail - in this case, you will receive a notification in your (real) mailbox with which you can go to the post office at your place of residence. USPS Express Mail provides delivery directly to the address.

Our shipment is small in mass - so a cheaper delivery is available to us - the USPS Priority Mail Flat-Rate Envelope. Its cost does not include insurance. Googling about the successful experiences of obtaining insurance with the USPS did not give anything, quite the contrary - the forums are full of messages that it is impossible to get insurance for electronic devices. Therefore, fingers crossed, choose USPS Priority Mail Flat-Rate Envelope.

My experience shows that at such a request the parcel is usually located (approximately another month) and is delivered.
