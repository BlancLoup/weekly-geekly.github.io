Made by Us! Site about production in Russia

They say that in Russia the industry is dying. They say that we already do not produce anything, but live only at the expense of oil and gas. All quietly laugh at the president‚Äôs calls for modernization. But where can we, we think, what nanotechnologies are there, no polymer left in Russia!



In my opinion, all this is not casual. There is an information war against Russia. It affects many aspects of our life, politics, religion, economy, our achievements, our history. Now one of the main blows is inflicted on the pride of Russia. As if someone is trying to make us think that we have nothing to be proud of in the present.

Therefore, I decided to collect information on production in Russia, on new plants, on modernization. Do not believe it, but it turns out that all of this is in Russia. And this is a lot! At first I collected this information in my LiveJournal, but I realized that I myself could not do it. Having written several reports on production in Russia, I found that by the time I finished the last report, at the time of starting over again, so much news I would like to talk about had appeared during that time. Therefore, I decided to create a platform where everyone can help to collect such information. On the engine of my own development, I created a website under the proud name " Made by Us! ". This site is about new productions in Russia, new products and services, modernization of production, innovations, discoveries, successes in the world markets, and other significant events in the production sphere of Russia.

The site has more than two months, and it is replenished daily. Mostly from open sources, but I also regularly write reviews, bringing together the most interesting news. Gradually, the site appears its own community (and by the way, we invite). We publish information not only about industry, but also about the construction of new medical centers, livestock farms, power plants, roads, bridges. While the president is calling for a country to modernize, modernization in the country is already long overdue!

Do not believe? Go to the site and make sure. We have much to be proud of!
