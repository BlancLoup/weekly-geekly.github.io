IPad users complain about the quality of the wi-fi connection

Many lucky people have already managed to spend the weekend with the iPad in their hands. And there were the first complaints.
A topic on wifi signal quality was found on the Apple support forum. The signal quality indicator is constantly changing its value. Being in the same place, one can observe how the indicator of a wi-fi connection drops from four divisions to one, and then again gives full power by itself. At the same time, MacBooks and iPhones working alongside do not show any signs of signal quality deterioration. Nevertheless, it is reassuring that there were no communication breaks and there is an assumption that the matter is in the indicator's work. It simply reacts incorrectly to network activity.

In the next branch of the same forum, users complain that the iPad can not get an address via DHCP and thus gain access to the network. If someone has already encountered such a problem, Apple recommends reading the iPad user manual (PDF format) and paying particular attention to page 117.
