Drupal 7 release date announced



The site drupal7releasedate.com has information about the release date of Drupal 7 and also a link to the message from the project founder Dries Buytaert.

The release is scheduled for January 5th.

Drupal 7 is the result of many years of efforts by more than 1000 project participants. Errors that will be detected before this date and after, promise to fix in future versions 7.1 and above. This version of Drupal is the most tested version in the history of the project. They promise that Drupal 7 will be more stable compared to Drupal 6.0

During the 3 weeks remaining, during the New Year holidays, the developers promise to make final adjustments.

In honor of the release of the new version, the Drupal community organizes worldwide parties on Friday, January 7, about which is contained on drupal7releaseparty.org

Cheers, comrades! :)
