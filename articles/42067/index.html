Double-click on Windows: what it affects.

It turns out that double-click in Windows works as a universal determinant of user reaction speed, reflex meter. This value (time) is used as one of the parameters of the delay between events. For example, it affects how long a baloon or tooltip is visible; also affects the speed of the drop-down menu.
In general, this time is involved in many user events.
