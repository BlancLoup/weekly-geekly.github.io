The first thing I would like to note is a clear upward trend in interest in cloud solutions. We cannot say that at the beginning of 2015, no one was interested in virtual PBXs or CRM integrations, there was steady demand, we regularly connected new users and implemented integrated solutions, but we believed that a virtual PBX should live in a box and only in a boxed one. Based on IP SIM, we plan to create several solutions for different business segments, but for now we will briefly show the solutions that have already been created.



The office in the Pocket is a cloud-based PBX on mobile phones. To use this solution, there is absolutely no need to use softphones or IP phones. IP SIMs connect to the cloud PBX directly, like the most common office phones and you can set up all the necessary call processing scenarios without the use of wires and redirects. Calls from mobiles are included in the PBX statistics, they are recorded, mobile phones have short numbers, you can transfer calls from one cell to another, while outgoing from the cell phone number is not determined, but a single company number is determined.

Something similar already existed on the market before us, but in those previous solutions there was a clear separation: cellular worked as if in two networks: corporate mobile and office. In our case, this division disappeared and corporate cellular became the most common office phones.



Employees of a fully mobile company do not need to have a permanent place in the office or an interface in the cloud system. You only need to set up all call scenarios once, download the IVR and assign short numbers to the mobile. After that, you can run away and work on cell phones, while in constant motion. From our point of view, this is an excellent solution for micro-businesses that do not want to spend money on the office and cloud PBXs, but want to look like serious and big companies with all the attributes of success: a beautiful incoming number, electronic secretaries, call transfers and voice alerts .

All calls from all announcements come to a single SIM card, are recorded in CRM and upon incoming call directly on the smartphone you can see by what announcement the call came. The communication history is stored in CRM, which allows you to collect call statistics, evaluate the effectiveness of ads for each of the products, and evaluate the conversion of advertising companies.

If you add a mobile conferencing service to our integration and solutions, you can get a good tool for automating small business: mobile phones, CRM, cloud PBX are now components of a single information system with the ability to create, invent, try almost infinitely.
