Expensive store in the bourgeois Allods

European and American publisher Allods Online gPotato at the weekend opened a game store. What struck most players to the depths of the soul. And not by the fact of discovery, but by exorbitant prices. Players at first thought that the publisher simply made a mistake by adding an extra zero at the end of the price tag.

For example, one healing potion costs $ 13.5, a bag for inventory for six extra beds - $ 20. A rune of the 10th level is sold for almost $ 7,000 (brrr). In the "original" prices are much lower. Not surprisingly, many players decided to boycott the store, which for a publisher can mean a lot of problems with the monetization of the game. However, gPotato seems to be happy with everything. The only thing the publisher apologized for so far is the opening of a store without an announcement.
