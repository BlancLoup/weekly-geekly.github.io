Video from LIR training course


We promised - we do. Video from the training course, which took place in St. Petersburg at the end of July 2011.

Conditions from the NCC: the course is intended only for the LIR, so if you are not an employee of an organization with Local Internet Registry status, then you are not allowed to view this video.

Language: English with funny splashes of Russian.

Subjects: RIPE, RIPE NCC operation, address allocation rules, creation of network numbering plans in ripe, etc. The second lesson is entirely about ipv6, not how ipv6 works, but how ipv6 addresses are distributed, how to choose addresses for equipment and servers, what size of network to distribute to clients, differences in conservation and aggregation policy between ipv4 and ipv6 PI to IPv6, etc.

First course: RIPE NCC LIR Training Course (ipv4 with reference to ipv6): video.yandex.ru/users/selectelru/collection/1

Second course: RIPE NCC IPv6 Training Course (ipv6 with reference to ipv4): video.yandex.ru/users/selectelru/collection/2
