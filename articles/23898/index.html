OpenID for your domain

The myopenid.com service offers everyone to use OpenID on their domain without installing any additional software. To do this, just register on the site, make small changes in the DNS for your domain and confirm that you own the domain.

Details and points

1. We inform about your desire to register on the site and get your OpenID for the domain.



2. Specify the domain name, as well as how our OpenID will look like. Option two: username.yourdomain.ru or openid.yourdomain.ru/username .



3. If you already have an account, then go. If not, then we are waiting for a simple registration.







4. We are told how to configure the DNS. Just add a CNAME record for your domain.



5. Interior, domain settings. Please note that there are two ways to confirm that you are the owner of a domain name: create a page with a code or specify some data in the DNS settings.



6. End result



Website: myopenid.com
OpenID for domain: myopenid.com/product_domains

Living examples: openid.skurudo.ru/skurudo and pavel.galkin.su
