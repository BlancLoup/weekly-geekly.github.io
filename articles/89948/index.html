SpySystem Device - Wireless Spy Camera Pen with DVR

I decided to share with you information about a spy ballpoint pen-camera with a wi-fi loudspeaker.
This device from the company 3rd Eye fell into my hands relatively recently, although it is far from being a novelty and a wonder.
The kit includes a speaker, charger, TV-out cable, USB / miniUSB cord, 3.5mm headphones and instructions,

and, in fact, the very ballpoint pen with a built-in camera.
All of this is packaged in a small neat box.
So how does it work? The handle is equipped with a Li-ion battery that charges from USB. The 2Mpx-camera in a ballpoint pen is activated when the pen is switched to letter readiness mode. The signal at 2.4GHz frequency from the pen goes to one of the DVR channels (there are several of them), where in real time we see everything that is happening in front of the camera. Directly in the functions of the DVR, you can turn off the reproduction of the sound, for a lot of interference (you understand). I think it is not necessary to mention such simple functions of Digital Video Recorder as the ability to record and save video. The DVR itself can be used as a regular audio and video player, storage medium, voice recorder, etc. That is, to some extent, it is an independent device. The DVR has a 3.5-inch TFT display, 2Gb of internal memory, as well as an expansion slot microSD (up to 16Gb), 3.5mm AV-out, miniUSB, and control buttons on the side panel. Supported video formats: Mpeg-4, AVI, 3GP.

Charges the DVR from USB. The charge lasts for 1.5 hours of reproduction. The range, as stated, is 100m, but I have not checked it yet.

The photos were borrowed from DIYTrade, due to the lack of a camera.
