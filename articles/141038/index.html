Lack of standard interaction of online stores and their suppliers


E-commerce in Russia is gaining momentum. Yesterday's
small problems grow into global ones.

Online stores are getting bigger, small online stores are breeding like mushrooms. The main reason is the ability to trade in goods that do not need to be bought in advance.
It is enough to make a showcase with a qualitative description of the product and agree with the supplier on prices and conditions. The goods will need to be redeemed from the supplier only if the customer pays for it.

Online Store Problems


Leftovers
So that there is no dissatisfaction on the part of the retail buyer, there should be as much as possible of the goods that the supplier has in the storefront of the online store! But the supplier works with a huge number of such online stores, plus delivers goods to the regions, networks and offline retail. This means that the supplier‚Äôs inventory balances are updated hourly. The online store should as often as possible request balances from suppliers and update the product on the site.

Reserves
For example, in the morning the online store received information from suppliers on the presence of certain goods. He has updated the shop window on the site and is waiting for orders. After the order is received, it is necessary to urgently inform the supplier of the article number of the goods, so that he put it in reserve. And this applies to every order.

Confirmation of reserves
At the end of the day, the online store needs to send each supplier information about the goods for which he will arrive the next day. Unfortunately, most suppliers have their own requirements for the file format and the presentation of information on orders in it, which creates additional inconvenience for online stores.
