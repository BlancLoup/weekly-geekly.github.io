LEGO MP3 Player

This small device is not a brick from the famous LEGO set. This brick is a real MP3 player. The unusual design and very light weight, only 26 grams, will allow you to listen to your favorite music anywhere.



Features:
* Support for MP3 and WMA;
* Supports microSD up to 2 GB;
* Powered by rechargeable battery (time is not specified);
* Supports Windows 2000 / XP / Vista;
* Available colors: blue, yellow, red, white, green;
* Size: 28 x 58 x 17 mm;
* Weight: 26 grams;

The cost of the device is 45 US dollars.

The full name of the device: HOMADE MP3 i-Player .
