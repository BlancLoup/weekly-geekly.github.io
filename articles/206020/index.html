Sony has released a universal USB flash drive for PC / mobile devices



The Japanese company Sony recently announced an interesting USB flash lineup. These flash drives can be called universal, since they can work with both laptops / PCs and mobile devices with micro USB. The drive itself is two-way.

On the one hand, this is quite a normal connector for a USB connector. On the other hand, if you remove the cap, a micro-USB connector appears. With this device almost all tablets and phones with Android OS work.

In general, a very convenient way to quickly copy files from a smartphone to a laptop and back, without the need to connect all these data cables or mess around with setting up a wireless connection. Among other things, a flash drive of this kind can be used as an additional drive for your mobile device - for example, if you need to take a lot of photos or videos.
