Russian companies are ready to take responsibility for the development and support of the Mandriva operating system

In 2007-2011, a full-scale infrastructure for the development, development, and technical support of free software, produced under the Mandriva brand, was created in Russia. Companies such as GNU / Linuxcenter and PingVin Software (technical support), ROSA (development) and Mandriva.ru (distribution) participate in it. These companies are ready to develop and maintain the distribution, to form the product line of the brand, to provide its technical support and distribution in Russia. Moreover, the free software model allows you to use all the previously made developments.
