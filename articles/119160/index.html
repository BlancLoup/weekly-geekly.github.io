Google Chromebook from Samsung and Acer



On Wednesday, the company introduced the first laptops running Chrome OS on Google I / O - two models from Samsung and one from Acer.

Chromebooks are designed for continuous use in the cloud. This means that their disk space will not be particularly impressive, but they will be loaded very quickly, thanks to the dual-core Intel Atom.

Let's take a closer look at the models presented.

Samsung Series 5 Chrome OS Laptop




Samsung offers a Chromebook in white (see picture) or silver.
Both will have a 12.1-inch display, weigh 1.48 kg cars and have built-in Wi-Fi and optional world-mode 3G. The laptop has 2 USB 2.0 ports, a 4-in-1 card reader, a mini-VGA port, an HD webcam and a full-size keyboard with a touchpad. A model with Wi-Fi will be priced at $ 429, a model with Wi-Fi and 3G will cost $ 499.

Acer Chromebook With 11.6-Inch LED-Backlit LCD Display




Acer's Chromebook is slightly cheaper and less expensive than Samsung. The Chromebook has a 11.6-inch display and weighs 1.34 kg. It also comes with built-in Wi-Fi and optional world-mode 3G.

Features: HD webcam, HD audio support, 2 USB 2.0 ports, 4-in-1 card reader, HDMI port and full-size touchpad keyboard. The Acer Wi-Fi version will cost about $ 349.
