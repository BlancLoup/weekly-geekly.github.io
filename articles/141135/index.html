Black Isle Studios may be reborn

Brian Fargo, the man who raises money for the Wasteland 2 project on Kickstarter and has already collected $ 1,750,000 (with the required $ 900,000) said that if he had finances, he would not mind reviving Black Isle Studios. A little later, Chris Avelon (CEO of Obsidian Entertaiment and also one of the leaders of Black Isle Studios) said that he would like to work together with Brian Fargo.



And so, yesterday Brian said that if he collected $ 2,100,000, the Obsidian team would join the development team together with Chris, and this is almost the full composition of the founders of Fallout (Only Leonard Boyarsky is missing, but he does Diablo III). It seems that if successful, Wasteland 2 will be the revival of the very Black Isle Studios in the old line-up. Especially considering that Obsidian is now experiencing financial difficulties . Maybe after Wastland 2, Planescape 2 and new projects of one of the most creative studios in game development will be waiting for us.
