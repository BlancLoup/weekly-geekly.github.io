All domestic projects on Kickstarter in one post. //Part 2

In this post I collected all the Ukrainian, Belarusian, Russian projects that have ever been launched on Kickstarter. Recently, not only computer games were launched from our developers, but also several gadgets and interesting projects in the design category.
I have already done a large selection in this Habrapost , then there were 39 projects, now there are already 65 of them!
All projects with useful data and links are available in the table via googlebox. I hope that the article will be useful to everyone who is interested in crowdfunding both in the role of a buyer, and in the role of the author of his own project.



This list cannot be complete, since the projects should be launched from a US, Canada, UK resident (and soon Australia and New Zealand), so it‚Äôs difficult to keep track of domestic projects, unless the authors themselves go to contact through the media. 15. Septikon is a board game that has already been released in Russia from the publishing house Mir Hobby. I worked on a team preparing and running this project (an unforgettable experience).

18. TapTap is a smart bracelet that comes with a range of sensors and can vibrate. It supports out of the box, such as an alarm clock. In addition, the bracelet supports IFTTT , and therefore gestures and all applications that can be controlled with them.
The project, by the way, from the engineering side is carried out in St. Petersburg, and the developers have already successfully distributed the products of the previous company Lightpack, from which they collected as much as $ 500k!

24. Steam Squad - a game from St. Petersburg developers who made a couple of habraposts about their creation. Very strange they were fees. The average payment on the backer is $ 127, which is quite a lot compared to the average Kickstarter payment of $ 70 per person.

25. Universum: War Front - a game with interlacing heaps of genres. It is reported that it makes one Ukrainian guy. It is difficult to believe in it, especially for me with experience of running the project on Kickstarter.

26. Knock-knock - a toy in the genre of horror and survival. She collected money for a long time, but about a month ago she came out in Steam, where everyone can buy it and evaluate it.

27. Oceania - the second attempt of this game on the COP. Knight with letters VIP on the shield and in full armor against the backdrop of palm trees and the beach looks wild. 30. SKYJACKER Space Combat Sim - space pirate shooter. This is the fourth project at the COP under the SKYJACKER. So far only one was successful, where money was collected for the designer of the spacecraft. The designer, by the way, not so long ago came the Beckers, seemingly satisfied. 54. iblazr - diode flash for smartphones. Very stylish and versatile flash that can be inserted directly into the jack, and onto a flexible leg. It has its own battery, so it can be a simple flashlight, and can illuminate an object for macro photography. Charges say that the device is in demand.
