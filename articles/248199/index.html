7 secrets of successful trading on Amazon

Why is it profitable to sell on Amazon?

The Internet has blurred the boundaries between countries and continents, allowing you to conduct business from anywhere in the world or buy online from any continent. For example, you can right now buy one of the 2000, located around the world, online stores service kubish.ru , and get cashback for your purchase, while being in Russia. It is senseless to be limited to local markets considering these conditions. We present to your attention a series of articles on e-commerce, from which you can learn a lot about foreign markets and the mechanics that are used there. Today we will talk about how to start selling on Amazon.


Secret number 1. Content quality


Pictures
- Basic images - white background, without watermarks or text
- 1000 px or more for zoom mode (zoom feature)
- At least 500px on the longest side (main picture)
- Prefer JPEG format
- Photos from different sides at different angles
- Verify your category requirements for product photos



Headlines
- One of the most important elements in any market
- The components of your header are used in the search for Amazon
- Clothing / shoes have their own requirements
- Original name = no more than 80 characters
- Create your title, including the main parameters of the product
- Notify Amazon if the catalog headers do not match your product



Options
- For the most convenient selection of basic parameters (such as sizes, colors, etc.), they must be in the same list

- Explore what options are available in your category.
Example: Size, Color, Item Package Quantity

- Use the parameters that are used on Amazon
Example: Blue, not Dark Blue

- Make sure the children are unique
Example: Men's T-shirt, Blue XL; Men's T-shirt, Blue Large; Men's T-shirt, Blue Medium.

Product information
Make sure that your product is described in detail and qualitatively.

- Recommendations for the description of the main characteristics of the product:
  • The text of each paragraph should be short and informative.
  • It is advisable not to use more than five items in the list.
  • The list of characteristics is located directly next to the picture.


- Recommendations for the description of the product itself
  • Do not use special characters in the description.
  • The description must be true and exactly match the product.
  • Use unique attributes in the product description (recommendation of Amazon)

Keywords are used by buyers to search for products on Amazon. Using the most relevant keywords, you will help buyers see your product in the top lines of the Amazon product issue.

1. Put yourself in the buyer's place - think about how they are looking for a product?
2. Experiment
3. Use single keywords instead of phrases
4. According to the rules, you can use no more than five keywords for each product.
5. Avoid keywords that are already used in headlines.

Secret number 4. Make the most of the FBA program.

The program Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) - a program that allows you to sell more goods, do not worry about its storage in warehouses and forget about the problems with delivery!
How does FBA work:
- Bulk send goods to Amazon (you can send any quantity of goods)
- The goods will be stored in the Amazon Fulfillment center nearest to your customers.
- Amazon will pack and deliver your goods worldwide - Amazon will take care of customer service 24 hours a day, which in turn can increase your customer service rating
