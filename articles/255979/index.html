Watch the live broadcast of Imagine Day on April 18



Dear friends!
We invite you to the main student event of this spring - IMAGINE DAY !
Imagine Day is the Russian final of the Imagine Cup and the Student Day . The event will take place on April 18, 2015, from 9:00 to 18:00, in the Technopolis Moscow Congress Center. For those who live far away and will not be able to come to Moscow, we will organize an online broadcast of reports and interviews with teams on the site imaginecup.ru . The broadcast starts at 10:00 (Moscow time) . You are waiting for live broadcasts from the site, interviews with participants, as well as conversations with the jury!

We have prepared for you a rich and interesting program:
  • You can see the performances of the top 20 student teams of the Imagine Cup from all over Russia!
  • Listen to interesting performances about technology and not only!
  • Take part in a mini-competition!




Russian final of Imagine Cup 2015
During the Russian final of the Imagine Cup 2015, we will recognize the winners in three categories (Games, Social projects, Innovations), which take place in the international online semi-final.
The winner of the international online semifinal will represent Russia at the international final of the competition in Seattle, will get a chance to compete for the title of champions and win a prize of 50,000 dollars.
