Replenishment of Webmoney wallet from Beeline SIM card balance

The other day I needed to pay VDS, I usually pay via Webmoney, but this time there was not enough money in my wallet. It would be possible to reach the nearest terminal and replenish the wallet, but it is autumn already on the street (the article was written in the autumn, but there was no karma to publish) and once again it did not want to go out in the rain and wind. Fortunately, there was enough money on the balance of my phone, it was necessary only to find a way to transfer the necessary amount to Webmoney.

Internet search did not bring anything useful, only a bunch of artisan exchangers with incomprehensible tariffs, each other more suspicious. Then I went to the Beeline website and after quite a long time, following the intricate chain: Mobile payment> Rent> Payment> Other services> QIWI Visa Virtual (??!), I got to be able to buy a virtual QIWI Visa card by paying it via SMS (http://kvartplata.beeline.ru/pf.do?prv=944). After a whole heap of preliminary sms, they sent me an SMS with the card details. Well, the map is already progress, from it and to Webmoney is not far. Having read ‚ÄúQuestions and Answers‚Äù ‚Äã‚Äãon the QIWI Visa website (http://visa.qiwi.ru/qa.html), I realized that first I need to withdraw funds from the card to my QIWI account. To do this, you need to register with the same phone number that I used to purchase the card on the site w.qiwi.ru. After registering, a VISA item appeared in the menu, through which I transferred money to my personal account, and transferred money from my personal account to Webmoney. After 2 hours, the money was in my ruble wallet and I paid for VDS. The general commission turned out somewhere around 10%.

In general, here is the procedure:

  1. Go to kvartplata.beeline.ru/pf.do?prv=944 and buy QIWI Visa Virtual
  2. After receiving the details go to w.qiwi.ru and either log in with the same phone that was used to buy the card, or register with it.
