First budget phablet

By tradition, I always take phones no more than 10 thousand. They are not so pathetic to break, drown, crush a cow under the hoof (and this has happened), and in the past few years the development went at such a pace that yesterday's flagships instantly passed into the category of antediluvian. In general, the approach is fully justified. There was only one problem: the size of the screen.

Therefore, it was necessary to appear available Megafon Login + phablet, I immediately became interested in them and immediately took it.
