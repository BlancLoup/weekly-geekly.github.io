Turning off the strip above the tabs and dragging a new assembly for the tab bar Vivaldi 1.0.231.3

Hello!

Despite the vacation time, we continue to work on the browser, albeit in an abbreviated format. Today's build Vivaldi 1.0.231.3 will delight you with some new features, as well as a list of bug fixes.

Tab bar enhancements

There were many complaints from users about the inability to move the browser window using the free space on the tab bar. Well, we are always happy to meet your wishes - now we have added this opportunity. True, it was necessary to abandon another popular feature - creating a new tab by clicking the middle mouse button on the tab bar. But this is a temporary inconvenience . We will definitely come up with a way to return this convenient function.

Another popular query was about turning off the narrow band above the tabs. It is designed so that you can move the browser window, even if the entire tab bar is occupied by open pages. In previous builds, we added the ability to disable this bar above tabs, but this was due to the rejection of tab grouping. Today we are pleased to announce a new solution: we have divided these settings, so now you can turn off the bar at the top, but you still have the option of grouping tabs:
