Welcome to Java Meetup at Raiffeisenbank UPD Broadcast

Raiffeisenbank's internal Java development community invites you to its first open meeting, which will be held at the site of the Moscow office in Nagatino on June 28 .



It is very important for us to share experience and knowledge with other colleagues, so we are actively developing the direction of communities. If you have something to share and you want to make a report at one of our events, please fill out the form.

In the Java program, a mitap is waiting for us four reports from the speakers of Raiffeisenbank and colleagues from Infobip. The reports start at 18:00 , registration will open at 17:30 .
