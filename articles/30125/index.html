5. Customs.


If you live in Ukraine - this article is not for you, buy further Levi's from baryg with peace of mind, or change the law.

If you live in Russia - everything is simple, you can deliver the goods once a week for an amount of no more than 5,000 rubles by courier services (DHL, FedEX, UPS), or for 10,000 rubles by state mail (including EMS).

If you exceed the amount, you will have to meet with customs officials. They are not scary and do not bite, bite DHL and FedEX.
Customs officers will be asked to pay customs duty and VAT (most often - 18% + 20%, that is, dohrena). If DHL or UPS delivered - you will have to pay the entire amount of the goods (well, for example, on Monday delivered 4,000 rubles, and on Friday 4,000 rubles - 8,000 a week came, that's from this amount and pay all fees).

If the state mail delivered - only from the difference between 10,000 rubles and the amount of your goods (another example - delivered goods by 12,000 rubles by mail - pay already 30%, but not from 12,000 rubles, but from 2,000 rubles, that is, the difference between the value of the goods and 10,000 rubles). More details in which the devil itself is hidden.

How do customs officers determine the value of your cargo, does it exceed 5,000 rubles or not?
There is such a term: customs value.
So, by law, this cost includes the cost of the purchase, plus the cost of insurance of the goods, plus the cost of shipping the goods. The cost of the purchase is determined on the basis of the Invoice (invoice), which goes stuck with adhesive tape to the box with your purchase.

Some good shops can safely lower the cost of goods in the Invoice by half. Some shops from their kindness reach senility and write "Donation" (Gift). So, if the seller (do not worry about online shopping, this usually does not happen, but on eBay - very often) wrote suddenly in the Invoice Gift, the Russian Customs officer is already eagerly rubbing his hands.
The fact is that in our legislation there is no Gift concept, so the price is determined independently by the customs officer. This price can be determined on the basis of catalogs (most often outdated;)), the market price on the Internet, or simply the guys will require an extract from your bank card to confirm expenses (and then pay for delivery immediately). That is, there are a lot of chances to stumble upon customs duties in the case of Gift.

If you greatly underestimate the cost of the goods (for example, MacBookAir is new in the package, estimate at $ 100) - the customs officer will not believe you and will be right in his own way. After that, a standard procedure for determining the market price and payment of customs duties by you.

Now, about the cost of insurance and delivery. If legal entities cannot avoid including the cost of delivery in the customs value, then everything is simple for individuals - if the cost of delivery is included in the invoice, then it is taken into account when calculating customs duties, if not included in the invoice - then it is not taken into account.
