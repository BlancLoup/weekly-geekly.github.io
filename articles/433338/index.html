Creality 3D 3D Printer Manufacturer Overview



Hello! Today we talk about the manufacturer of 3D-printers Creality 3D and show the video that was shot for this publication in China.

history of the company


Shenzhen Creality 3D Technology Co., Ltd began as a small 3D printer production in 2014 in Shenzhen. During this time, the company was able to significantly increase production capacity and become a significant player in the market of low-cost 3D printers.

Company currently


Not only are warehouses and assembly lines growing. Now the company is actively investing in development - more than 30 engineers are creating new 3D printers and making improvements to existing models.



With a production volume of 30,000 printers per month, it is important to monitor the quality of products, so Creality has a large staff of specialists who are responsible for testing the assembled devices.



Product line


Creality 3D focuses on popular models such as the Ender 3 and CR-10 .



The company tries itself in other niches. For example, in its product line there are industrial models DP-001 and DP-002 .



In the summer of this year, Creality announced its first personal photopolymer printer LD-001 .



Manufacturing process


3D printers are assembled by the company's employees on four assembly lines operating around the clock. The whole process, from installation and configuration to testing, is carried out directly in the walls of Creality 3D. The quality of work and compliance with the plans are tightly controlled.



Finished products are delivered to a large warehouse complex where they are carefully packed and shipped to customers.



The company has a large, multiplex showroom, where potential partners can familiarize themselves with the current line of 3D printers.



Such an asset is especially important, given the ambitious plans - Creality 3D plans to ship over 250,000 devices to buyers by the end of 2018.
