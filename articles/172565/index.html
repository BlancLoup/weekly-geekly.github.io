Google Glass can be worn with ordinary glasses / glasses



On Habr√© news about "smart" glasses from Google repeatedly appeared. And in most cases it was a question that there would be no glasses with such glasses. It is clear that this whole system without glasses looks rather strange, plus the whole question arises - how to wear this mini-PC to people who wear glasses due to vision problems? The other day, device developers reassured all carriers of ordinary glasses: Google Glass can be worn right on the rim of such glasses.

The fact is that Google Glass will have (or already have) a modular design, so that you can add the desired frame and lenses to the system. The announcement picture was taken by one of the developers of the Google Glass team, who wears this device along with the usual glasses. So far this is a preliminary version of the design, but the final version is just around the corner (although Google loves to experiment, you can't take it away).

So far there is no information about how much this version of the device will cost, and what other types of design for Google Glass will be available. Nevertheless, already happy news that the device is quite a universal in terms of choosing the basis for wearing. This whole system looks almost normal, I think that a person unfamiliar with Google Glass will not pay attention to another person, on the frame of whose glasses there is some kind of thickening.

On the other hand, such glasses clearly attract the attention of all those who are familiar with the project. But what to do, new technology has always attracted attention.
