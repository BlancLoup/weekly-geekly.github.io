Swiss presentation knife



As you know, the company Victorinox Swiss Army is famous for its folding knives. Models with flash drives in its assortment appeared a few years ago, but this year the Swiss people simply surpassed themselves. The model of 2009 shown at CES really surprises with its IT orientation.

Let's start in order:
1) The memory of the Swiss knife has grown to 32 GB;
2) The built-in flash drive received 256-bit encryption, as well as a fingerprint scanner;
3) The Bluetooth module is now built into the knife;
4) There are buttons "back and forth" for managing presentations;
5) Built-in laser pointer.
6) Software package includes utilities for backup, synchronization with Outlook, etc.

In addition to all of the above, the knife also has the actual knife, scissors and nail file. In general, now with the help of the famous gadget, you can not only cut the sausage, pick it up in the nails, cut the paper, but also hold a full-fledged presentation in field conditions.

Depending on the amount of memory (8-32 GB), the novelty will be sold at prices ranging from 170 to 335 dollars.
