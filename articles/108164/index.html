Looking for projects on the 4th Startup Crash Test


On November 19, the Moscow Startup Crash Test will be held final in the autumn season. The organizers, the GreenfieldProject team and Gleb Kaplun, are looking for strong start-ups that are ready to test their business models in half-hour crash tests for experts and audiences, or to be presented as part of two-minute pitch tests without feedback. Projects wishing to speak can now write at sctmoscow@gmail.com .

SCT guests will receive a report and a panel discussion - details and topics are being clarified. Crash-test projects will come experienced experts: investors, entrepreneurs, gurus in the field of IT, project management, PR, branding, etc.
