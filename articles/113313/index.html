About the club


The Computer Science Club was established in St. Petersburg in September 2007 with the support of Anton Likhodedov, and today the club is supported by Anton Likhodedov (Deutsche Bank) and Yury Bogdanov (Uralkali). The main goal is to provide students with an opportunity to get an education in the field of computer science. Lectures are given by local and visiting teachers at POMI RAS (Fontanka, 27). Entrance to the lectures is free. During the existence of the club, it was visited by more than a thousand students from various universities. On average, about forty students attend the lecture, including not only students, but also graduate students, graduates of universities. The most popular lectures gather more than a hundred people.

In September 2010, a branch of the club was opened in Yekaterinburg. The club is supported by Anton Lihodedov (Deutsche Bank), SKB Kontur and Yandex .
