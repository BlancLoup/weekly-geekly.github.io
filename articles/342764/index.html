Russian Startups Go Global 2017 - new global opportunities for Russian IT entrepreneurs



Hello!

November 29, we hold a Russian Startups Go Global 2017 conference for IT entrepreneurs with the potential for development and scaling in the international market. In this post we will describe how the event of last year passed, which blocks the Russian Startups Go Global 2017 program is divided into, and which foreign guests can be heard at the event.

As it was in 2016


Last year, we held Russian Startups Go Global 2016 - the first international event of the IIDF for IT founders of Russia who want to conquer new horizons. Guests from 500 Startups, Seedcamp, Cherry Ventures and Maxfield Capital, Ideal Machine, as well as representatives of the embassies of Great Britain, Ireland, France and Germany came to us, and the founders of IT start-ups could communicate with Russian and foreign investors in a one-on-one format . As it was, you can look at the video:



Then we got a lot of feedback:



We saw the value of this event for entrepreneurs - and this year we decided to continue to acquaint Russian IT start-ups with opportunities for development in the international market. We are waiting for everyone who is interested in the global development of their company, on Russian Startups Go Global 2017 ! The event will be held November 29 in Moscow in Izvestia Hall , register and come!



How will it be in 2017


The main program of the conference consists of three blocks: "Business", "Technology" and "Fundraising and M & A". Speakers at Russian Startups Go Global will talk about how to adapt a product for a foreign market, how B2B sales in Russia and the USA differ, how to build a strategy for working with investors to increase the company's capitalization, how to attract investments abroad, and share cases from their practice. .
