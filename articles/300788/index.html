Android'mania: over four years, Google Play revenues have grown more than 35 times


Source: App Annie

Google Play shows rapid growth in revenue and downloads: the number of downloads has increased by almost 4 times, and revenues from the sale of applications have increased by 35 times. Last year, the store outperformed its main competitor iOS in the US.

Such a conclusion was made by analysts App Annie in his study. They prepared a report on the most popular and profitable applications in this store.

According to the number of downloads for all the time of observation among the gaming applications, Subway Serfers ‚Äúendless runner‚Äù leads . In second and third place is the Candy Crash Saga and the alien pet simulator Pou . It is followed by Temple Run 2 and Hill Climb Racing . In the first place in terms of profitability is a Japanese puzzle with RPG elements Puzzle & Dragons , in second place is Clash of Clans , in the third one is another Japanese game Monster Strike , also a puzzle with RPG elements. This is followed by Game of War: Fire Age .

Casual games took 9 out of 10 positions in the rating of Google Play games for all time, which confirms the hot interest in similar games around the world - both from gamers and casual players, the App Annie report says .

Among non-gaming applications, Facebook and WhatsApp were the leaders in downloads (in the iOS ranking, they did not make it to the top lines, although they were in the top ten). Emerging markets have become one of the growth drivers of Google Play, thanks to the development of infrastructure and the increasing popularity of smartphones, the researchers note. According to the authors of the study, Android-based phones dominate in East Asia and the culture of mobile games is very well developed.
App Annie app store revenue data is received from publishers, it does not include advertising revenue in apps.
