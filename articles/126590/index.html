Farm Frenzy 2 - the first Russian game from Microsoft Game Studios

This week a historic event happened - the first Russian game will be released by Microsoft Game Studios on Windows Phone 7! The announcement was made at the Gamescom international gaming conference in Cologne.

Farm Frenzy 2 ( Farm Frenzy 2 ) from Alawar Entertainment is one of the most famous and popular games, translated from 2007 into more than 20 languages ‚Äã‚Äãand sold to more than 3 million copies on mobile platforms and social networks. Alawar , one of the leading international game developers, originally from Novosibirsk, becomes the first Russian partner of Microsoft Game Studios.

Farm Frenzy 2 will appear as an Xbox LIVE title in the app store soon and will be accompanied by the appropriate Microsoft marketing support. Let me remind you that MGS publishes games such as Angry Birds and Fruit Ninja.

Many Russian developers want to cooperate with MGS, but until today it seemed impossible for many of them. There is confidence that this is not the last Russian game on a special game "shelf" in the Windows Phone Marketplace.

All information is available in the official press release . A list of all announced games is in the Windows Phone blog .
