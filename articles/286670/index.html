No time to explain! I have been following and writing for a long time about the development of guys from the ComfortWay company ( http://4tourist.com ), who work in the segment of roaming-free communication technologies, virtual SIMs, and are ahead of the curve. Today, dry news from them and comments why the news is great. News on the results of their participation in the GSMA World Mobile Congress.


News # 1
Colleagues set up an international association of companies and entrepreneurs working in the provision of virtual communication services.
VSCA (Virtual SIM Card Alliance).

News # 2
The Alliance is working to create a cloud service for managing the virtual SIM cards ComfortWay Virtual SIM Cloud.
and actually became the first global independent provider of SIM profiles from the cloud.
Cloud functionality:
  • secure storage of virtual SIM-card images of participating operators (in partnership with leading manufacturers of SIM cards)
  • billing - a single user balance and dynamic management of device tariffs or SIM cards
  • Secure transfer of profiles on SIM cards according to GSMA standards
  • integration with partner services - Internet portals providing travel services to potential customers
  • SIM protection implementation

i.e., in fact, the cloud assumes the functions of a connector or hub between operators, end users, and manufacturers of wearable devices, and this is beneficial for everyone and allows you to smooth out the conflicts of interests of the parties - mobile device manufacturers do not want to produce devices with a limited pool, operators do not provide, want to release products that are ready to work around the world without additional user actions, and operators want to expand the customer base at the expense of visiting tourists and the market of M2M devices .
Scenario of using a cloud by a mobile operator
A virtual SIM card can be installed into any wearable device or a chipset with a built-in SIM chip. The choice of the operator is made by the user from the application. To ensure security, operator profiles are stored on certified platforms of SIM card manufacturers or in the IPVS cloud - an independent virtual SIM provider. The profile can be filled (selected from the application) SIM-cards of any operator who has implemented the ability to transfer independently or decided to work with an independent supplier of SIM profiles from the cloud.

# News 4
ComfortWay implements the Remote SIM Provisioning technology (remote downloading of SIM-card profiles to a smartphone), which was approved by the GSMA international association in the summer of 2014. In fact, the technology allows using virtual SIM-cards instead of installing physical SIMs in a smartphone.
About virtual sim
The main function of a classic SIM card is to store identification information about the owner, which allows the subscriber to easily and quickly change cellular devices without changing their account, but simply moving their SIM card to another phone. Virtual SIM can be tied to a specific device in the form of a built-in SIM-chip, which at any time you can pour the profile of the local operator.
