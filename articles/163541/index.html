Getting a passport through the portal gosuslugi.ru

In the pre-New Year rush, the value of a quick application for a passport increases many times - it is convenient to do this without spending time on the traditional queues of fellow countrymen planning their vacations.

Getting a foreign passport (or rather, applying for a receipt) is the most popular service on the portal gosuslugi.ru , to date, about 20% of passports are issued by electronic applications.

The service is provided only to registered users. Therefore, take care of registration in advance.


Preparation of documents

In order to apply for a passport, you will need the following documents:

1. Passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation
2. Documents confirming the basis for the urgent registration of a foreign passport *
3. Child's birth certificate
4. Documents certifying the presence of citizenship of the Russian Federation for persons under the age of 14
5. Documents confirming the rights of legal representative **
6. Foreign passport issued earlier (if any)
7. Receipts for payment of state duty
8. Photos of the applicant
9. Application form for the issuance of a passport abroad ***
10. Employment record, an extract from it or a certificate of employment
11. Permission of command issued in accordance with the procedure established by the Government of the Russian Federation ****
12. Military ID or relevant certificate of the military commissariat at the place of registration
13. Petition of the organization sending a citizen outside the territory of the Russian Federation

Application submission

Choose a service in the catalog. It is located in the first section of the Federal Migration Service. You can also simply use the search in the upper right corner.


Next - press the button "Get the service", from the bottom or from the right - as it is more convenient.


And we proceed to filling out the proposed form:


It is necessary to fill in the application form, indicating the information about yourself, spouse, parents, about being previously in foreign citizenship, about the place of appeal, etc.
At the end of the data entry, the system will process and send your request to the state structures for completion of the registration, verification of the application and the final decision.
In case of successful implementation of the service at all stages, the system will give you a positive response, and government agencies will inform you about the need to come to the territorial office of the department to receive the documents. In case of refusal at any stage of the service, the system will give a negative answer, and the state structures will inform you about the reasons for the refusal.

Passport Issuance

The deadline for issuing a passport does not exceed one month from the date of filing the application at the place of residence and four months when submitting the application at the place of stay.

Passport can be issued and faster - as soon as it is ready, you will receive a notification.

* The reason for the urgent (within three working days) consideration of the application for issuing a passport is:

a letter from the health authority confirming the need for an urgent trip for treatment outside the territory of the Russian Federation;
a letter from a foreign medical organization about the need for emergency treatment (at the same time, the medical report of the medical institution at the place of residence of the patient is submitted);
a telegraph message received from outside the territory of the Russian Federation and certified in accordance with the laws of the state of residence (residence), confirming the fact of a serious illness or death of a close relative or spouse.

** Required if you plan to receive a passport through a representative.
The following documents are additionally submitted to confirm the rights of the legal representative:

birth certificate;
certificate of adoption (adoption);
court decision establishing guardianship;
the act of the local government on the establishment of guardianship;
documents proving the identity and citizenship of the adoptive parents or sole adopter.
if one of the adoptive parents is stateless, also a document of this adoptive parent certifying the status of the stateless person.
