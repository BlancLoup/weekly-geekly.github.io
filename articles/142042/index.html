Boeing is going to release a super-reliable smartphone



Some people and companies do not fit the standard offers of manufacturers of mobile devices, laptops and tablets. This is not about moisture, dust and shock protection. No, it's about more prosaic things - stealing information and listening. Regular phone specialist specialist profile will not be difficult to listen. In addition, standard devices are poorly protected from theft of private user data. And there are not so many variants of protected devices, and they cost about 15-20 thousand dollars.

The other day, the company Boeing announced its intention to create a secure smartphone based on the Android operating system. This was reported not by anyone, but by the president of the company. Currently, the device is already being developed, and in a few months it will be presented to the general public. The device will be called accordingly - The Boeing Phone.

According to the manual, such a device will cost much less than the protected gadgets of other companies. Typically, a smartphone, protected from listening and stealing personal information, costs quite a lot - up to several tens of thousands of dollars. The Boeing Phone will cost less - but how much, until announced.

According to management, the device will become popular with the military, with corporate users and other categories of mobile device users. Unfortunately, the characteristics of the device are not reported yet either - they will be published on the company's website a bit later.
