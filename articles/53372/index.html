WM Keeper Mini - can not be easier

WebMoney Transfer has a new way to manage your account - WebMoney Keeper Mini . The new keeper is not as powerful as the usual Classic and Light, but very simple and convenient for novice users. He, like WM Light, does not require special installation and works directly through the browser.


Novice users will appreciate its simplicity and ease of use: the registration procedure in the Mini is easier and faster than in Classic, Light or even Mobile, and you can log in to the Keeper Mini using your login and password.

You can enter Keeper Mini using your login and password, or E-num- authorization.

Top up your mobile phone, an account with an Internet provider, buy an access code for a popular online game or e-book game - all this Mini allows you to do in just a few clicks.

The new keeper is adapted for devices with a small screen (PDA, mobile phones) and can be used in any OS.
