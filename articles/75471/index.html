AIR 2.0 beta release

On the Adobe Labs website they posted an AIR 2.0 distribution, one of Adobe‚Äôs most anticipated products. Versions for all operating systems (Windows, Mac and Linux), as well as for x86 netbooks have beta status.

Simultaneously with the development platform, a new version of Flash Player 10.1 has been released .
