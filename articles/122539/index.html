In the Ukrainian Internet will start working new domains.

From July 1, 2011, registration in the new domain - .kharkiv.ua will begin in the Ukrainian, address Internet space.

.Kharkiv.ua - is positioned as a regional domain of the Kharkov region.

After opening registrations in the .Kharkiv.ua domain, all current domain owners in .Kharkov.ua will receive equivalent names in .Kharkiv.ua for free. At the same time, the expiration date of the free-provided domains in .Kharkiv.ua will be +2 months to the expiration date of the parent domain (the date specified in Record expired) .Kharkov.ua.

In addition, from July 1, 2011 registration in the domain .sumy.ua will be paid. In this regard, all domains that are registered before June 29, 2011, from July 1, will need to be transferred to the service to the official registrar. Names in the domain .sumy.ua, which are registered before June 29, 2011, will continue to work until the date specified in the status field, if the existing domain is not transferred to the official registrar before this date - the domain is frozen (becomes Frozen) and no less than 30 days, such a domain is removed from the database of existing domain names.

If you have registered names in the domain .sumy.ua - starting July 1, 2011, you can transfer them to the Internet Name Center of Ukraine ( Ukrnames ) for free .

On July 1, 2011, all owners of registered names in the domain .sumy.ua will receive equivalent names in the domain .sm.ua for free. At the same time, the expiration date of the free-provided domains in .sm.ua will be +2 months to the expiration date of the parent domain (the date indicated in the status field) .sumy.ua.

On the expiration date, the owners of the free-provided .kharkiv.ua and .sm.ua domains will decide for themselves what to do with additional domains - to renew or not.

The Eastern Ukrainian Internet Coordination Center (Coordinator), which currently serves a number of regional domains, will serve the new domains.

The price for registration in the domains .Kharkiv.ua, .Sumy.ua and .Sm.ua for clients. The Center for Internet Names of Ukraine will be the same as for the other regional domains of the Coordinator. In addition to domains in Latin, Cyrillic domain names are available for registration.
The Center for Internet Names of Ukraine is the official registrar of Ukrainian regional domains.
