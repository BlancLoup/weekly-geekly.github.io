Autumn smartphone: Chinese "Galaxy Note 7" and other news from Xiaomi



The company Xiaomi produces a lot of smartphones. You could even say a lot. However, unlike devices from other Chinese manufacturers, each of its products still deserves separate attention. This year, Xiaomi cleared up and just in the fall held several presentations, where she showed many smartphones - from her own Galaxy Note 7 to the upgraded Mi5.

At the end of September, the Xiaomi Mi5 smartphone received its portion of updates. Not to say that they were long-awaited, as the Mi5 itself was only introduced in February of this year. Apparently, the company decided not to wait for the annual update cycle, and judging by the appearance and characteristics of the new Mi 5s and Mi 5s Plus - for good reason.

Mi 5s - the one we were waiting for




In general, Xiaomi rarely manages to surprise at their presentations, because the details about its new devices, as a rule, surface in the Web in a few days, if not in a week. Numerous leaks indicated that outwardly the Mi 5s would be almost indistinguishable from the Mi5, but here the company still kept the intrigue.

Firstly, the materials of which the body is made have undergone changes. Unlike the Mi5, which is completely glass on both sides, the Mi 5s has an all-metal case with plastic inserts for antennas. Do you know the description? Yes, these were the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus. Sometimes it proves that everything new is well forgotten old.





The branded curved shape of the back cover has not gone away, the camera with a flash is located in the usual upper left corner, right above the antennas. Xiaomi, by the way, was the first to install a completely new Sony IMX378 camera sensor in the Mi 5s, which has an f / 2.0 aperture and a 1.55 micron pixel.

The camera is equipped with a four-axis optical image stabilization system, six lenses, phase autofocus, support for slow-motion at 120 frames per second and, of course, can record video in 4K format! At its presentation, Xiaomi compared the images with the Mi 5s with photos taken on the iPhone 6s Plus and Galaxy S7.



Real tests show that this is true: we offer a look at how Mi 5s shoots in life.







They did not forget about the technical characteristics of the company, having made Mi 5s one of the most powerful smartphones running Android. That only costs the new quad-core Snapdragon 821, 3 GB or 4 GB of LPDDR4 RAM and built-in high-frequency UFS 2.0 memory of 64 GB or 128 GB.

Yes, the diagonal of the display remained the same (5.15 inches, resolution 1920 x 1080), but it became noticeably better than in the Mi5. The maximum brightness increased to 600 nits, the coverage of the NTSC color gamut is 94.4%, and the contrast ratio is 1500: 1. The most important thing is the presence of sensitivity to the force of pressing, just like 3D Touch. It works, however, so far only with standard applications.

A nice bonus to the rear camera was the two-color LED flash. And with a front four megapixel camera with 2 microns pixels and an aperture of f / 2.0, the selfie will be even better. For fingerprint scanning in this smartphone meets the ultrasound scanner from Qualcomm. The main thing is its location: right behind the touch home button below the screen. Yes, the button is no longer mechanical, and the scanner itself assures that Qualcomm can work correctly even with dirty and wet fingers.



Battery Mi 5s is not big, but not small - 3 200 mAh, and is able to quickly charge with the help of technology Quick Charge 3.0. As for the interfaces, everything is as we used to: Wi-Fi 802.11ac (MU-MIMO), Bluetooth version 4.2, support for GPS / GLONASS / Beidou, NFC and USB Type-C port for charging a smartphone.

Well, Mi 5s turned out decent work on the errors Xiaomi Mi5. But what about the Mi 5s Plus?



Xiaomi Mi 5s Plus - for those who love more


Xiaomi Mi 5s Plus primarily differs from the Mi 5s display with a diagonal of 5.7 inches, a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels, a contrast of 1300: 1 and a brightness of 550 nit. Displays 94% of the NTSC color gamut and provides an incredibly bright and juicy picture compared to the same Mi5.



The chipset here is the same Snapdragon 821, but already with a higher core clock frequency (2x2.35 GHz Kryo + 2x1.6 GHz Kryo). RAM also threw - now you can choose between 4 GB and 6 GB of LPDDR4 memory. UFS 2.0 internal memory is still available in two versions: 64 GB and 128 GB. The battery also pleases - 3800 mAh instead of 3200 mAh in Mi 5s.

Xiaomi sees the trend and plays on it, though someone sees the similarity with the design of smartphones from HTC. To the camera, we will return.

Xiaomi Mi Note 2 - no, does not explode


"The best product in the history of Xiaomi", according to the company representatives themselves, we specifically left for dessert. In this case, many leaks were confirmed, including the presence of a large 5.7-inch OLED display bent on both sides. This screen occupies more than 77% of the front panel of the smartphone, due to which the dimensions of the device are comparable with 5.5-inch gadgets of competitors.

It turned out that on the front side, the Mi Note 2, presented a few days ago, is frankly similar to the Galaxy Note 7 - yes, the very one, which Samsung had to stop production due to numerous instances of smartphone ignition.





The back panel is also made of curved 3D glass, which also found a 23-megapixel camera and LED flash.

Under the hood, the Mi Note 2 has a Qualcomm Snapdragon 821 quad-core processor with a clock frequency of 2.35 GHz, 4 or 6 GB of LPDDR4 RAM to choose from and 64 GB / 128 GB of internal memory.

The main (rear) camera of the Mi Note 2 is not just a 23-megapixel camera, it has an electronic stabilization system and a dual LED flash - you can not worry about a photo in poor light.
