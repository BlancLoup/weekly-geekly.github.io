How do web studios and agencies earn in partnership with online services?


How can a web-studio or an advertising agency earn in addition to creating and promoting websites? What types of affiliate programs for web studios exist today and what is their scheme? For specific examples, we consider some types of partnership offers from Internet services, enabling web developers to replenish their services and gain additional (and perhaps even basic) revenue.


Web Hosting Affiliate Programs


The first thing any developer should think about is to establish close contacts with one of the hosting sites. The range of affiliate programs offered by hosters is quite wide: from bonuses that you receive from new customers who have come through the referral link to interest on each transfer for hosting.
In the simplest case, you yourself pay for hosting services much less, if you don‚Äôt even host your own website for free. If you regularly bring to the hoster new clients and its revenue due to you exceeds a certain amount - you get interest from each transaction. There are a lot of options, and you can choose the most suitable one.

As a rule, the host is chosen based on the technical requirements of the site, reputation, technical support capabilities, and so on. The most interesting partner programs for web studios today are associated with the use of virtualization and cloud services. For example, Infobox in exchange for the listed clients of virtual hosting, VPS, IaaS platform, Virtual Secretary service offers not only payments from 10 to 30% from each payment, but also free use of VPS and the cloud platform for hosting the agency‚Äôs website as well as and for development. In addition, there are separate programs for webmasters and IT-integrators.

Web development, design and CMS


Another well-known area that allows web studios not only to quickly and simply solve their own problems, but also to earn extra money on this. Perhaps the richest range of proposals of this kind can be found in 1C-Bitrix , one of the most popular commercial Russian content management systems. CMS sublicensing for customers with a big discount (up to 40%), participation in the service of requests for website development, the ability to sell custom modules, templates and entire websites created for 1C-Bitrix are just a partial list of the sources of additional income offered to developers.

A more promising area is the development of serious projects based on specific platforms designed for e-commerce and providing for virtually everything that a client may require. One of these platforms is InSales : the service offers two main types of affiliate programs - referral and reseller. The referral program allows you to receive a percentage of the deductions from your clients (25-40%). CRM and project management systems


It is hard to imagine modern Internet marketing and e-commerce without the use of customer relationship management systems, newsletters and other online business tools. Can you handle this task? If you develop websites on one of the ready-made CMS, then there is a chance that there are ready-made modules for them that you can use. But most often their functionality is very limited, and rarely suits customers. Which means - you need to integrate more advanced solutions. Why not get extra profit from the developers of these solutions?

Under the affiliate program, you not only get a discount of up to 50% depending on the product version, but also the opportunity to offer discounts to your customers up to 15%. Under the terms of the affiliate program, you get a percentage of sales made on your recommendation (from 20 to 50% from each sales). Profitable? Of course. And the demand for such services will only grow over time, even in the regions.

Online consultants


Whether the site sells real estate, travel vouchers, web hosting or insurance services - the built-in chat will help increase trust, promptly answer questions from potential customers, ensure their loyalty, and therefore improve sales. Website owners understand this, and the built-in online consulting system today is a common part of the site development specification. Online accounting


Cloud technologies and mobile devices have become a reality and significantly change the world around. Today, thanks to SaaS, companies can do without renting offices, buying equipment and professional software packages. Cloud accounting services for small and medium businesses are becoming increasingly popular. Just yesterday, online CRM and project management systems seemed fantastic, today they are commonplace. So why developing a website for a startup does not offer the customer to become a client of online accounting?
The benefits of the client are obvious: he saves on the purchase of accounting software and equipment, removes the problems associated with the maintenance of the IT department and provides himself with flexibility in work, freedom and security. And as an affiliate program participant, you will receive bonuses offered by the SaaS service provider.

For example, My Case under the program for regional representatives offers a reward for the initial sale of access to the service (50% of the tariff cost) and a reward for the renewal of access.
Already today, the site is an integrated into a unified system of content management tools, customer accounting, social services and project management systems. Tomorrow financial management systems will become part of the system, and you, developers, should think about it now.
