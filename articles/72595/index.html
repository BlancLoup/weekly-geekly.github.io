Gallery of cases at the exhibition WEB-Studio 2009

At the exhibition WEB-Studio 2009 there will be presented a gallery of cases (the best works) of CMS studios and developers. The main idea is to collect the most technologically complex, interesting and non-standard projects implemented by web developers, and present them in the form of a gallery at the exhibition.

Important: The sent cases will also be placed in a special section on CMS Magazine , which is currently being developed.

One case (project) will be a 2.5 x 1 meter poster.

Schematic images of the placement of the gallery and poster:







The number of projects is limited by the exhibition space (no more than 20 projects will be placed).

The cost of placing a single project in the gallery is 3,500 rubles.
For participants - 2900 rub.
For partners - one project for free, the rest - 1500 rubles.

Applications for participation are accepted until October 19 inclusive.

If there are more than 20 applications, preference will be given to applications submitted first.

Participants must provide the following information:

1.Number of hosted projects
2. Stand number
3. For each project:

1. Project title
2. Name of the client
3. Client's logo (if any) in vector
4. Developer Name

9.From 1st to 6 screenshots at the highest possible resolution
10.Contact developer information (Phone, e-mail and website)
11.URL project.

We are pleased to accept any comments and comments on this event.
