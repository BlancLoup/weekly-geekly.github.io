Who owns Bynet?

I think that it is no secret to anyone in Bynet that for a long time the Open Contact LLC, which served as the technical administrator of the .by zone, was also the largest squatter of the zone. All more or less valuable domain names, free for a certain date or vacated, fell into the stop list of "Open Contact".

This stop list included keywords such as auto.by, travel.by, sport.by, sex.by. It is clear that to hide this fact after the transfer of the functions of a technical administrator and the opening of the whois service would be difficult, and Open Contract LLC was to find a way out of the current situation. Perhaps they decided that the best way out would be the mass release of these domains and their registration by everyone, perhaps the mass release and registration for nominees, perhaps I am mistaken in my speculation, and the mass release of beautiful domains this summer was just an accident.
