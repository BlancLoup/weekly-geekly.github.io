Some details of the collaboration of Google and NASA

Everything that the fantasy writers of the last century wrote so vividly is becoming a reality - from now on, the most successful company in the world, Google, will be engaged in space research not only uniting the minds of the best scientists but also pursuing innovative goals in any branch of human knowledge. According to NASA representatives, the first steps of Google will be aimed at popularizing the most interesting information collected in the agency over the many years of its existence on the Internet.

The ambitious plan for joint work of Google and NASA to create a natural model of human communication with a computer that allows you to work with the machine without the participation of classical communication interfaces such as a mouse and keyboard deserves special attention.
