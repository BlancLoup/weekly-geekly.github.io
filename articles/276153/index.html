How much did Google pay for the lost google.com domain?


Sanmai ved

On September 29, 2015, the hacker and former googler Sanmay Ved (Sanmay Ved) brought a rustle when he managed to buy the Google.com domain for one minute . The domain was free through Google Domains at the standard price of $ 12 per year.

Google realized its mistake within a minute and within a minute took back the most visited domain on the Internet. Now the details of that story have become known, namely the amount of the ransom (reward) to the hacker.
