XIAOMI Yi 1080P Car WiFi DVR - and the Chelyabinsk meteorite will shoot




Everyone was waiting for XIAOMI to release its DVR that they did not specifically buy other devices - $ 60 for a ready-made solution with the ability to record video in Full HD 60 fps sounds very tasty. Let's see why the XIAOMI Yi recorder is so good and why it is worth paying attention to.


First of all, it is worth noting the support of night shooting, and a rather high-quality one, thanks to a high resolution lens with a f / 1.8 aperture and a photosensitive matrix. Therefore, this DVR works even in low light conditions, and a wide viewing angle (165 degrees) allows you to capture all the events that occur with the car, since it covers three lanes at once. Of course, there is no way out of the blind zones, but there will be much less of them here. Also an important factor is the support of the smart system ADAS. This is a real gift for car enthusiasts - the DVR detects the deviation of the car from the marking line in real time, controls the distance between your vehicle and other road users, and warns you in case of dangerous situations. If the crash could not be avoided, a special sensor will record the force of impact.


The device is equipped with a 2.7-inch LCD display (16: 9, 426x240), has an integrated Wi-Fi 2.4G wireless module and an ultra-high resolution of 1296p UHD (30 fps). Wi-Fi support allows you to view videos using the application in real time and earlier recordings.


What is iron? All this is controlled by a dual-core A12 chip (40 nm), which works in symbiosis with the 4.0 V Lux-sec light sensor. Aptina Ar0230 with a dynamic interval is used as an image sensor. The lens consists of six optical lenses and is equipped with an infrared light filter.

The case is painted with high-quality varnish, which causes pleasant tactile sensations. The control of the DVR is carried out by four function buttons. It is noteworthy that the recording is not interrupted even after filling the memory card (microSD up to 64 GB is supported), the recorder automatically deletes old entries to free up space. Micro USB is used for power supply, the recorder itself is fixed with the help of suction cups on the glass.
