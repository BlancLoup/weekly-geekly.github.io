Drupal day December 3



The Runet community of the Drupal web platform ( www.drupal.ru ) is holding a Drupal Day seminar in Moscow on December 3, participation is free, the program and details on the place and time are posted on the website moscow.drupalcamp.ru . Registration is required to participate - on the site. Due to the limitations of the premises, the seminar is designed for exactly one hundred participants.

Here is a partial list of Drupal Day reports:
  • Let's look into the future: Drupal 7
  • Example of communication with third-party scripts: integration with PHPBB3
  • VDS and DS: Stereotypes and Optimization
  • Project prototyping on Drupal
  • Work with Simpletest
  • Examples of using Drupal: about creating a website forbesrussia.ru
  • Overview of developer tools and webmasters on unix-desktop

There will also be reports on the work of jQuery / JavaScript / AJAX, the creation of themes in Drupal, work with Google Maps and a session of five-minute blitz-reports on various topics is scheduled. The speakers are members of the Runet community drupal.ru.

Objectives of the event:
  • Improving the skills of Drupal developers and users.
  • Involving new developers in working with the Drupal platform and the Drupal development project.
  • Attracting attention web studios to Drupal.
  • Contacts between developers and employers.
  • Drawing attention to the movement of open source software in Russia.


Sponsor Drupal Day December 3 - the company Masterhost. Information sponsorship of events today is provided by: Analytical portal CMS Magazine , Webplanet , Habrahabr , Service 1PS.RU , NetNews.by News Resource , Open Systems Publishers , Computerworld Magazine, PC Magazine Magazine , Designcollector Magazine .

More information on the website moscow.drupalcamp.ru .
