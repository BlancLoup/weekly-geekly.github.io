How will the Runet develop in the coming years? Internet Development Institute is preparing its plan


Photo: Denis Vyshinsky / TASS

The Institute for Internet Development ( IRI ) is an organization established in the spring of this year following a meeting of representatives of IT companies with the first deputy head of the presidential administration, Vyacheslav Volodin. IRI unites several funds and unions at once, including the Russian Association of Electronic Communications (RAEC), the Internet Initiatives Development Foundation (IIDF), the Media Communication Union and the Regional Public Center for Internet Technologies (ROCIT), RBC reports . Now Iran is preparing a plan for the development of the Russian segment of the Network, so that the elements of this plan are included in the program, calculated until 2025. The program is being developed on the instructions of President Vladimir Putin.

Proposals from the IRI will be submitted already on October 5, at a meeting with Volodin, as well as Minister of the Ministry of Communications Nikolai Nikiforov. Now the preliminary version of this document is ready, and it consists of 137 pages. In terms of there are tips not only on the development of the Internet, but also on the modernization of other industries.

The plan records four directions for the development of the Runet: in relation to society, government agencies, business, the media and communications sector.

Thus, for medical institutions, an aggregation of case histories and clinical studies is provided in a single database. Plus it is proposed to develop such services as remote diagnostics and consultations, as well as to develop an electronic prescription system. Such a system will allow you to buy drugs on the web.

As for education, here the industry representatives recommend creating a system of distance school and vocational education. Among other proposals is the development of an online platform that allows educational institutions to consult with businesses on the qualities of graduates necessary for commercial structures. It also emphasizes the need for training IT professionals.

For industry, it is recommended to create a system for collecting data from mining and processing enterprises.
