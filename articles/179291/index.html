Universal phosphors with a long afterglow

In continuation of posts about cold neon and phosphors and my online store of phosphors . I promised to do infographics.

For those who do not know what phosphors are and why they are needed: phosphors are substances that can accumulate energy and radiate it. It will be a question of photoluminophors that emit visible light. In a simple way - substances and things that glow in the dark.

At first, I wanted to make an infographic in the form of a photograph: I collected various items - watches, pens, rulers, phosphor, pigments and already finished glowing things, laid it all out on the fabric. Everything looked very impressive, but the shooting did not work, because the photographic equipment available to me and the experience do not allow us to convey all the magnificence that opens to the eyes in the dark.

As a result, I abandoned the original idea and collected photographic material from the Web. Then I studied several services for creating infographics and stopped at www.piktochart.com .

Under the cut - the result of three hours of work on collecting material and writing texts - my first infographics. I am not a designer and will listen attentively to expert advice and criticism.
