Tetris


June 6, 2016 Tetris turned 32 years old. This game has visited almost all gaming platforms, as well as graphical calculators and media players. In its motives in China they want to make a science fiction feature film. And this will deal with the American producer Lawrence Kazanov, responsible for the film "True Lies", as well as taking part in several animated television projects for Lego.


Why are there a calculator, in Tetris it was possible to play on the wall of a skyscraper

The game was created by Soviet programmer Alexey Pajitnov. The name comes from "tetramino" and "tennis." "Tetramino" are geometric shapes of four squares. There are seven such figures in Tetris. The figures fall into a beaker with a width of 10 and a height of 20 cells. The goal is to collect a horizontal row of 10 cells, which then disappear.

In the history of the game, it was not only ported to all sorts of platforms, but also changed the rules, complicated, refined. Tetris did the volume, it was color and black and white, with a variety of musical accompaniment. Even the "physical" Tetris sell. In 2009, designer Andrew Liszewski (Andrew Liszewski) began selling his 360-degree version in which the figure appears in the center, and then falls to any side that the player chooses, turning the console.

The rights to use the game in the machines bought by Atari. Nintendo got the rights to port to NES and Game Boy. Then, in the late 1980s, the game hit the Sega. And in 1996, Pajitnov founded the Tetris Company in order to start getting money for his invention. In 2014, Tetris Company announced a deal to port the game to XboX One and PlayStation 4.

For 2014, the number of mobile installations of the game was 425 million, 35 million - for the Game Boy, plus about 70 million copies were sold. It is difficult to say how many unlicensed cartridges and devices with this game were sold.

So Tetris is not just a leader, but an absolute leader with a huge advantage.
