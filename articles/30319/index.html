A preliminary reception of questions for the Q & A session with the presenters began.

leader of the PostgreSQL community, an expert of EnterpriseDB.
* Maxim Boguk , leading database administrator for Rambler and
Masterhost, renowned PostgreSQL specialist.
* Fedor Sigaev , developer of the full-text search subsystem, systems
