EZTV torrent tracker back in service



While The Pirate Bay gives mysterious signs of life , another major torrent portal, EZTV , has fully recovered and is already offering fresh, still hot, content.

As you all know, a couple of weeks ago, as a result of a raid by the Swedish police in the city of Naka, near Stockholm, the largest file-sharing resources stopped working . Were confiscated servers of several large sites. The first of them was EZTV - the traditional place for sharing fresh series of television series. The site was opened in 2005 (albeit with periods of complete inaccessibility) and became one of the most popular similar portals.

Although the site did not work for 13 days, the content distributor group took a break for just one day. While the site did not work, the torrents were distributed through ExtraTorrent.cc and Kickass.so.

Now, albeit with some periodic slowdowns, EZTV is working. We are waiting for the rest of the file sharing participants. We love, we miss.
