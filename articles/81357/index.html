9-year-old girl became Microsoft Certified System Engineer

I remember how, on April 1, there appeared a story about a 12-year system administrator working in an American school. That story seemed to be a joke, but now there is a new, true story of a 9-year-old girl who has become the youngest certified Microsoft system engineer.



The girl's name is Marco Kalasan , and she lives in Macedonia. By the way, she received the first system analyst certificate from Microsoft at the age of 6; this was the first step in her current career as an IT specialist.

The head of the Microsoft branch of Slovenia, Matei Potokar, confirmed the information, adding the following: ‚ÄúI have to say that in terms of technology knowledge, Marco is definitely a wonderful child.‚Äù

Why does the head of the Slovenian office comment on the situation? Yes, because the girl was proving her right to be MCSE (Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer) precisely in Slovenia. She spoke to a team of the corporation, giving a presentation to Active Directory, talking about the benefits of this technology.

Now Marco is already working in his specialty, helping several non-profit organizations to manage their base of computers (the girl works remotely). The employees of these organizations are very pleased with their system administrator. By the way, the girl sits at the PC for no more than 4 hours a day, although she says she sometimes spends more than 10 hours a day at the computer. What to do - the profession requires.

And by the way, an interesting point - Marco does not play computer games. She says that she prefers to play with friends on the street, and the computer itself is perceived solely as a tool for work, not for entertainment.

In general, an interesting girl. I would really like to know who she will become in the future ...
