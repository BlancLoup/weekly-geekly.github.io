Greetings to all habravchan!

We are an Epson company, and these are our first steps here.

Epson often emphasizes its connection with Japan, and not only because the company is from there. Most of us at least once in our lives managed to appreciate the uniqueness and reliability of Japanese technology, be it a Japanese car or a kitchen knife. So, speaking Epson, we definitely mean innovation and uncompromising quality - by the way, all our products are developed on the basis of our own technological solutions.

If you dig deeper, then having eyes, may he see our continuous improvement!
This is precisely the principle that came to Epson from the philosophical doctrines of the samurai, and it remained to live, taking deep roots not only in the headquarters, but also in the 94th Epson offices around the world. We try to improve ourselves every day in order to be wiser tomorrow than today and not to allow past mistakes in the present and future!

At HabraHabr, we will study with you. We promise to be honest and objective in relation to both our successes and failures. We hope for your support, because these are our first steps.

Over the years we have accumulated so much interesting and useful material that we are simply obliged to share this knowledge and experience with you!

With faith in a bright future
Your Epson
