Bloomberg terminals around the world out of order

Bloomberg's terminals, used by traders around the world to track real-time quotes and news, as well as to make deals, have failed in all countries.

The company quickly confirmed the failure and stated that it was already carrying out restoration work, figuring out the cause of the incident. Some users have already been able to reconnect to Bloomberg servers.

More than 315,000 clients use Bloomberg terminals in paid mode, access to the system costs about $ 1,800 per month.

Analysts predict a fall in daily trading volumes on all leading areas with securities and raw materials, as well as currency, because of this emergency situation.
