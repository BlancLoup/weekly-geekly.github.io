5. How Blog Search Works

Anton Volnukhin, Roman Ivanov

Advertising and educational report about blog search. Quite quality and most consistent with the audience. In the program it was written ‚Äúhow the blog search works ‚Äù, but it wasn‚Äôt just said about the internal search device, the report is actually about how the blog search works : at what intervals it indexes, who gets to the top, with what blog services.

I learned that:
  • Yandex has an agreement with LJ (and others): as soon as you publish something, LJ notifies Yandex, and a blog search indexes a new publication.
  • If the publication has once been made publicly available, then it will remain in the blog search no matter how many times you make it closed. (If you really really need to make it closed, then write in support of blog search)
  • Blogs have 1 billion publications, this is 1/5 of the whole RuNet, but the publications themselves are very small.
  • From one blog, only one entry can be in the top.
  • Top updated every 20 min.
  • Publications not older than 8 hours have a double rating.
  • As soon as a publication hits the main page, it begins to lose (spend) the rating every hour.
  • Habr's comments search blogs and indexes, but the articles themselves are not.
