In testing the main quality

Hello, Habrazhiteli!

In the last couple / three weeks we have done a lot of work on the site for testing sites and software ( Fixber ) as part of the task of improving the quality of the testing provided.



All improvements made solely to improve the existing functionality of the site.



Briefly and in the case:

1. The customer may invite testers from his whitelist to the project - fixber.com/client/blackwhite,



2. A tester can display a list of bugs on his / her page to show the customer the quality of his work by example - fixber.com/tester/buglist,



3. On the tester profile page, even more information is displayed that will help the customer to quickly determine the choice of performers for testing their project,

4. the customer can export the purchased bugs in xml, csv and xls (for example, fixber.com/client/project/xxxx/bugs/exp , where xxxx is the project id) + import file for the pivotaltracker.com tracker,



5. the ability to leave notes on the tester / customer on the user details page (for example, fixber.com/testers/xxxx , where xxxx is the user id),



6. quick access to information about bugs (for example, fixber.com/tester/project/xxxx/bugs , where xxxx is the project id),



7. introductory test to test the basic knowledge of testers,

8. A common system of communication between users and those is available. support - fixber.com/workflow .
