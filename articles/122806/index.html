Preparation for the 3rd .NET Developer Conference has begun



We started organizing the 3rd .NET Developer Conference in Chelyabinsk. The official site http://www.dotnetconf.ru , meeting VKontakte and twitter #dotnetconf + @dotnetconf .

Admission is free after registration, registration is open now. The conference is designed for 400 seats and will consist of two parts.



1. Two-day course Agile Requirements Analysis


September 22-23

Dealing with requirements in Agile has its own characteristics. The process of planning and collecting requirements becomes iterative, the requirements are constantly changing. Despite this, we should be able to specify the exact release date. From the course you will learn how to decompose features into stories, you will learn to develop a product incrementally, you will learn what skills the owner of a product in Agile should possess. Details on the conference website http://www.dotnetconf.ru/Training

September 24-25

Main directions:
  • .NET Framework, ASP.NET MVC, WPF, WCF and other technologies
