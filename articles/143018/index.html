Incredibox Music Designer, second version



The designer Incredibox allows you to "compose" the music yourself by selecting the melody, rhythm, chorus and effects from the set. It seems there are few sounds, but in fact this set is enough for 137,980 different combinations.



In the second version a game interface appeared, and now it is not just a music generator, but a real game with three levels.

Incredibox works in two modes: Manual and Shuffle. In the first case, you select the sounds to your taste, and in the second they change endlessly in random order.
