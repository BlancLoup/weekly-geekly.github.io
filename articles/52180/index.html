3 Skype news at the MWC in Barcelona

1. Cooperation with Nokia
Skype solutions will be integrated into Nokia phones, for a start - the N-series. The flagship of the line, Nokia N97, will be the first phone in which Skype will be integrated in the third quarter of 2009. The main point of cooperation is that Skype will not just be pre-installed on the phone, but will be integrated into the Nokia N97 address book, allowing you to display information about the status of your contacts on Skype, send messages to them, and on 3G or Wi Fi networks and call as on Skype, as well as landline and mobile phones at Skype rates.

2. Skype for Windows Mobile 2.5
The Windows Mobile version comes out of beta testing and the final version 2.5 is uploaded on Skype for download . At the same time, beta version 3.0 was announced, public testing of which should begin in March.

3. Skype Panel for SonyEricsson Xperia X1
Well, in addition to this, Skype presented its proprietary panel for the communicator SonyEricsson Xperia X1, which allows you to easily and quickly see information about the status of your contacts in Skype.
