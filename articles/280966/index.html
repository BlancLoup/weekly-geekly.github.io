How to transfer business to the cloud

From the translator.
Hello! The first two of my translations ( first , second ) had many views, so I decided to post another article. Today I want to present a translation of a small text on how to transfer business to the cloud. I really hope that the article will be useful.


If you use an online email service, such as Gmail or Hotmail, then you are using cloud technologies. Similarly, if you used services such as PayPal to make purchases over the Internet, then you also used cloud technologies. In short, the cloud can be used for remote storage and retrieval of data, as well as a remote service that is not controlled by you directly.

Benefits of moving to the cloud


Cloud technologies offer organizations a number of benefits.
First of all, servicing servers and software is provided by the cloud service provider, and you will not have to hire additional IT specialists.
In addition, your data will be available anywhere in the world, no matter where you are - in the office or on a business trip, you can log in, access the necessary data, make and save changes.
Finally, most cloud software is scalable, which means that it is available on various devices, including PCs, tablets and smartphones.

Making the transition


The article ‚ÄúTraditional data center storage systems have been moved to the cloud‚Äù refers to the fact that many companies have decided to make the transition.
In many cases, the process of switching from traditional storage to cloud storage is quite simple. Moreover, many providers offer services for the automatic transfer of files and databases to cloud storage.
In case your provider does not provide such services, you may need some time to manually transfer data. Fortunately, this will need to be done only once and after all the data has been uploaded to the storage, your work is finished.

Choosing a Cloud Service Provider


When choosing a cloud service provider, particular attention should be paid to security.
