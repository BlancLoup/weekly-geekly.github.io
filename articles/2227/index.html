Tut.By rent DVDs

The Belarusian portal Tut.By opened a DVD rental service . The site, created in conjunction with Belprimenergo, offers to rent license discs from the extensive collection of the site without leaving your home, informs IT in Belarus .

The service is available for residents of Minsk. Currently, movie lovers are offered three tariff plans that allow you to view from 6 to 12 DVDs per month. The cost of renting a license disk is an average of 3,000 Belarusian rubles (about 40 Russian rubles).
The discs ordered on the website are delivered free of charge by courier home or to the office at a convenient time for customers. He brings new drives, takes viewed and accepts payment.

In the future, the creators of DVD.Tut.By plan to expand the list of discs in the film library to 1,000 titles and hold promotions with prizes and discounts on connection to the service.
