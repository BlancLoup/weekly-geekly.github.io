MS Empower Initiative

The topic is not an attempt to ignite another war of supporters of paid or free software and is addressed to those who need Microsoft software products, including development tools.

It is no secret that the relevant departments of law enforcement agencies in the past few months (and somewhere even years) have dramatically stepped up their work in catching and bringing to justice organizations that use unlicensed software. The main software-bait in this hunt, of course, is the production of the industry leader.

Buy a set of Windows + Office costs, how easy it is to calculate, in the amount of about 10 tr. on one workplace. If we consider the server operating system, the costs and even more. However, for many, MS Empower will save a lot of money and become useful.

Conditions are simple and clear.
A legal entity engaged in the production of software based on MS technologies and planning to release it in the near future can join the initiative. There are no special requirements for software.
Available:
Up to five software licenses for internal use:
o Microsoft Windows Vista Business or Enterprise, or Microsoft Windows XP;
o Microsoft Office 2007 or 2003.

One server license and up to five client (CAL) licenses for internal use:
o Microsoft Windows Server 2003 (Standard or Enterprise Edition),
o Microsoft Exchange 2003 Server (Standard or Enterprise Edition),
o Microsoft SQL Server 2000 (Standard or Enterprise Edition),
o Microsoft SQL Server 2005 (Standard or Enterprise Edition),
o Microsoft SharePoint Portal Server 2007 or 2003.

Microsoft Visual Studio 2005 Professional Edition and MSDN Premium Subscription Universal (5 licenses for development and testing).

Five MSDN Premium Subscription licenses and MSDN Premium DVDs.


What is needed for that?
1. Participate in the Microsoft Partner Program at the Registered Member Level
2. Have and maintain a website
3. Develop at least 1 replicable software product using MS technology.
4. Pay an annual membership fee of $ 420 (excluding VAT).

Under certain conditions, you can extend the participation for another year.
