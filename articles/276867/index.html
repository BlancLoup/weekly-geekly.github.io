Study of virtual hosting tariffs

To begin with, compared to the number of tariffs for virtual servers , the rates for virtual hosting are represented less. If for virtual servers we have 1982 offers from 219 hosters (on average, 9.05 each), then for shared hosting this figure is almost two times less (on average 5.15 ).
