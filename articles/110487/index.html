Holiday sale of games for iPhone

In honor of the holiday resource IndieSale set a price of 99 cents for 6 popular games.


The six games include Canabalt, Solipskier, Spider: The Secret of Bryce Manor, Osmos, Eliss, Drop7.

Canabalt is a runner game. The whole gameplay consists of running and jumping.


SolipSkier - a simple game - a skier simulator, so to speak. The main plus - you can create your own tracks.


Spider: The Secret of Bryce Manor - a game about a spider. The task is to cling to the whole house with a web, along the way having solved some secrets of the owner of this house.

Awards:
IGF iPhone Game of the Year - 2009
TouchArcade iPhone Game of the Year - 2009
IGN Top 5 iPhone Games - 2009


Osmos is a pretty famous game. You play as an amoeba, absorbing smaller organisms and do not get large.

Awards:
Independent Games Festival Winner


Eliss . You control balls of different colors on the iPhone screen. They can be combined and divided. The challenge is to remove them from the screen.


Drop7 - puzzle, a mixture of Tetris and Sudoku.

Awards:
- Time Magazine's Top 10 iPhone Games
- Esquire's Best Downloadable Games for Any Phone, Console & PDA
- PCWorld's Nine Essential iPhone Apps
- Edge Magazine's # 2 Best iPhone Game of All Time


Part of the funds raised will go to charity.
