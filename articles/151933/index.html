ASUS introduced two tablet computers with Windows 8: the models ASUS Vivo Tab and ASUS Vivo Tab RT (previously known as ASUS Tablet 810 and ASUS Tablet 600, respectively).

ASUS Vivo Tab

ASUS Vivo Tab (the name is derived from the Latin "live") is equipped with an Intel Atom processor, 2 GB of system memory and user memory of 64 GB.

Like the ‚Äútransformers‚Äù, the tablet is equipped with a docking station, which adds a pair of USB ports and increases battery life due to an additional battery.

Vivo Tab has a 11.6-inch Super IPS + display with a resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels and a multi-touch interface with support for Wacom digital pen. The thickness of the tablet is 8.7 mm, and the weight is 675 g. There are two cameras: a front 2 megapixel and a rear 8 megapixel autofocus and LED flash. ASUS Vivo Tab RT

The hardware configuration of ASUS Vivo Tab RT includes a quad-core NVIDIA Tegra 3 processor with an integrated graphics core, 2 GB of system memory and 32 GB of user memory. Works under the operating system Windows RT. Its weight is 520 g, body thickness - 8.3 mm. Like the older model, the Vivo Tab RT is equipped with two cameras and an NFC sensor.

Both tablets will be available immediately after the release of Windows 8.
