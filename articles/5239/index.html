Thai computer game teaches goodness

The Thai company has released a computer game called Ethics Game, designed to free a person from the thirst for violence inherent in many running shooters. In contrast, the game promotes the five commandments of Buddhism: not to kill, not to steal, not to commit adultery, not to deceive and not to drink alcohol.

The plot of the game is simple: three children make pilgrimages in company with an old wise monk. On their way, obstacles of varying difficulty arise, which must be overcome so as not to violate the five commandments.

The game can be downloaded from the site of developers . True, it is desirable to know the Thai language ...
