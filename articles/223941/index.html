HDR comes to Windows Phone


As you know, HDR (high dynamic range shooting) is a shooting technique that allows you to take images with a high dynamic range by combining multiple shots: with low (underexposed), normal and large (overexposed) exposure. As a result, images are obtained where the bright zones become darker and the dark ones become brighter.



The 4Blend HDR application takes this process to a slightly different level. If other applications to work with HDR apply filters only upon completion of the process of combining images, the Sebastiano application does this individually for each image, and then only combines them. The result is more natural and high-quality looking pictures, compared to other applications.
