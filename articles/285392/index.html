First steps to China

Good day, dear Habrasoobschestvo.

I want to write my story about how to escape from Rashi, not even having a tractor to leave Russia for China.

Documents receiving

Without a couple of pieces of paper, they won't let us go anywhere from Russia. So take a lot of time, patience and go to collect documents for a passport.

For those who do not know, you need to apply for a passport: Fill out a passport application, a photocopy of your work, the original Russian passport + photocopies, 4 photos, for the military obliged certificate from the military office or military ID and of course the certificate of payment of duty (usually around 1000R ). We give the documents to the FMS and wait. While we are waiting, we can look for where to make a visa, preferably a tourist one, they want less money for it and if something goes wrong, you can always return.
I did a visa at a travel agency, giving away my passport. Passport, a completed application form (given in the agency), 2pcs color photo, and money return itself. I paid for a visa somewhere 3000r per month.

The tourist visa has a gap of three months. During this time, you need to have time to enter the country, and what else would be in reserve 30 days, otherwise you may have problems.

If you can, it is better to take a visa for 3 months. It is necessary to make a certificate that you are NOT HIV positive, it may not be necessary, but our customs is famous for its unpredictability.

Work searches

Of course, without any hope, a ray of light or friends, you should not try to stick to China. For without knowing the language, you cannot do anything.

Since my friend is studying in Harbin, I began to look for work there. There is a whole resource dedicated to China, and everything connected with it. Wherever I could, I left my resume. Needless to say, no matter what level you are, it is useless to wait for what they write to you right away. I probably waited a month or two and here they wrote me on Skype and told me that there was work at the Russian restaurant. Yes Yes. To work as the system administrator at restaurant. I also accidentally gave a hint that I can work with sound and light equipment, it was a mistake, but more on that later. Since I wanted to leave, I agreed. And just my visa was almost ready.

In general, it is difficult to find an IT job, but it is possible.
