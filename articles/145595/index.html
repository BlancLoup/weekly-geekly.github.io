Welcome to Euro 2012


As you may know, the European Football Championship 2012 began the other day in Ukraine and Poland (by the way, the Russian team has already managed to beat the Czech Republic with a score of 4: 1). We decided (against the background of any incidents ) that we could make the guests' stay in our country a bit more comfortable and released special free applications for them under iOs, Android and Facebook. Nothing too clever, just information about:

  • Embassies (addresses, phone numbers, position on the map)
  • emergency services (police, ambulance, fire protection)
  • hospitals and clinics
  • airports
  • collection of useful links (information portals of Euro-cities, transport, tourist portals, currency exchange)
  • A collection of links to other useful Euro-related applications (match calendars, tournament tables, UEFA applications, maps of participating cities, etc.)
