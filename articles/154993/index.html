A selection of services to enhance your Dropbox

The Dropbox service is loved by many habrovchanami, so I offer you a selection of utilities for working with this cloud service.

Backup Box can synchronize different cloud services, as well as backup sites (FTP and MySQL).



IFTTT is a popular service for creating triggers between various services, including Dropbox.



Wappwolf Dropbox Automator allows you to create rules by linking Evernote, Facebook, Flickr and other services with Dropbox.



BoxCryptor encrypts all files in Dropbox and makes them available on your computer (or iOS and Android device).



Dropbox Folder Sync Addon is an addon for Windows that allows you to synchronize any folder on your computer with Dropbox. MacDropAny - analog for Mac.



JotForm allows you to create web forms for the site, the results of which are sent to you in Dropbox.



Dropboxifier helps to synchronize program data or game saves between computers.



DropboxPortableAHK makes Dropbox portable.



DROPitTOme allows other users to upload files to your Dropbox.



DropTunes allows you to listen to music from Dropbox on iPhone.



DropVox is a voice recorder for iOS that stores audio files in Dropbox.



Otixo - connects several cloud services: Dropbox, SkyDrive, Box and Google Drive.



Writebox is an online text editor that saves text to Dropbox.



Send To Dropbox - sending attachments in letters to Dropbox.

Gimme Bar - a bar for the browser, where you can drag pictures, tweets, screenshots, content to save them in Dropbox.

SideCLOUDload can download files to Dropbox itself via the link provided .

SortMyBox sorts files into Dropbox according to user rules.

Hosting on Dropbox: DropPages , KISSr , Scriptogr.Am , Site44 .
