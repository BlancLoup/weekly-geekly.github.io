ROBOTICS TXT controller with computer vision functions



ROBOTICS TXT controller is based on two microprocessors with ARM architecture: TI AM3352 and STM32F105. The solution using two microprocessors allows you to unload the main processor on which Linux is running, from tasks related to processing input and output signals in real time.

The controller is programmed in the ROBO Pro environment or in C, C ++, C # and VB languages.

Characteristics of the ROBOTICS TXT controller:
  • ARM Cortex A8 main processor: two cores, 32 bit, 600 MHz
  • Peripheral Processor: Cortex M3
  • Memory: 128 MB DDR3 RAM, 64 MB FLASH
  • Display: 2.4 inch color touch (320x240 pixels)
  • External memory: microSD flash memory card
  • Universal inputs: 8 pieces of digital / analog 0-9V or 0-5 kŒ©
  • Counting inputs: 4 pieces (F <b> 1 kHz)
  • Bluetoot / WiFi combined wireless module: BT 2.1 EDR + 4.0, WLAN 802.11 b / g / n
  • IR photodetector for the remote control from the kit "Set for remote control"
  • USB mini-USB connector for PC connection
  • USB connector USB-A with host controller function for connecting USB-camera, USB-flash drive and other devices.
  • Connecting a video camera via USB, driver for Linux OS
  • 10 pin connector for connecting expansion modules with I2C interface
  • Built-in speaker
  • Built-in real-time clock with a replaceable battery for measurements at specified intervals
  • Connecting to smartphones / tablets via Bluetooth or WiFi allows you to use them as a terminal for the controller.
  • Power supply: from battery via standard fischertechnik 2.5 mm plugs or from a 9 V power supply unit via a 3.45 mm cylindrical connector.
  • Dimensions: 90x90x25 mm

Designer Mini Bots



Designer Mini Bots . These are robots for the little ones themselves. It has a control unit that does not need to be programmed. Everything is already programmed. Simply select the desired algorithm using the switch.



Basics of pneumatics



Basics of pneumatics . Demonstrates the basics of pneumatics during the game. With it, children will learn the behavior of pneumatic systems, learn how a pneumatic pump, cylinders and manual distribution valves work.

Compressed air is forced into the pneumatic system using a hand pump. It then enters the pneumatic cylinders, which drive various mechanisms. Small manual valves are used to distribute compressed air.

From the designer you can assemble an excavator and four more models.




Solar cars


Solar cars . Playing with the models of this set, the children will see how the sunlight turns into electrical energy and further into the movement of the models that they collected with their own hands.

They will be able to use the children's pool or pond in the garden and compete on a catamaran and boat, whose base is made of high-quality foam. Children will learn how a car and a fan powered by solar energy function.
