The Windows Vista Resource Kit is available for download.


Microsoft has opened access to the free download of the electronic version of the book Windows Vista Resource Kit. This book, on its 1693 pages, talks about the administration of the operating system Windows Vista Service Pack 1, in addition, it also tells about IPv6. The authors are Mitch Tulloch, Tony Northrup and Jerry Honeycutt in cooperation with system developers. The price of a printed book in stores, approximately 2000 rubles. Download the English version in PDF format, here , it is enough to have a Windows Live ID. The size is 44.8 MB.
