‚ñå Opinion blogger about GALAXY Camera


Before judging the camera, you should have a clear understanding of its concept. The image quality is not as good as in mirrorless cameras, and the size and functionality of the device is not quite suitable for use as a smartphone.



But this is not the main thing. Currently, most people who take pictures want to share their pictures in social media, and not just store photos on computers. Smartphones began to acquire good cameras, because of this, many have ceased to use separate cameras. But, in my experience, for really good pictures, you still need a full-fledged camera. On the other hand, modern devices lack the convenient and fast functions for transferring files, uploading photos to social networks. networks, photobanks or cloud services. GALAXY Camera allows you to do both without the hassle.

I spend a lot of time visiting and reviewing restaurants and other public places in various social services. network services: believe it or not, the popularity of my reviews is quite high. To be sure that I publish my reviews and reviews among the first, it is extremely important for me to be able to quickly upload the best photos. This creates the need for better quality photos and high download speeds. Probably many bloggers need the same thing.



GALAXY Camera is made for people like me. The device automatically uploads high-quality photos to cloud services faster than others.

One of the drawbacks of this camera is that it cannot replace a telephone. Telecommunications companies want to sell similar devices with limited functionality. But time will tell that it is meaningless.

In addition, the image quality does not compare with the quality that mirrorless cameras give out, like the NX1000, which also has a Wi-Fi connection (although not so convenient to use).



Samsung representatives never said that they would not make mirrorless cameras with Android (such a device would make me happy). This is the final part of my review. Conclusion about GALAXY Camera do you. My recommendations? If you get a chance to try out this new product, do not miss it!
