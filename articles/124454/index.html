Meeting of system administrators 2011 (Oryol region)

From July 28, 2011 to July 31, 2011, near the city of Oryol, an All-Russian gathering of system administrators will be held, dedicated to the celebration of the day of the system administrator.





The purpose of the Meeting is informal communication on professional topics, uniting people whose interests are related to information technology. This is a great opportunity to gather in tents in nature and share experiences with colleagues, establish personal contacts between specialists, discuss software development prospects, initiate new projects, as well as participate in sports tournaments and professional contests, themed "round tables" and just have a great time. . Invited system administrators and IT-specialists, heads of IT-departments and technical directors of enterprises, developers and project managers, also welcome the participation of their friends, relatives and relatives. We invite IT specialists from anywhere in Russia and other countries!

The System Administrator Journal (# 6-2011) contains an article about our meeting.

Participation in the meeting is absolutely free. Registration and familiarization with the Rules of the rally is required!

The age of participants allowed to the event from 18 years. Children and adolescents up to 18 years old - entrance without registration, and strictly when accompanied by parents. An on-line video broadcast is planned from the venue of the rally, thanks to which Internet users will be able to see everything that happens at the rally - a concert, a bonfire and much more. At our meeting there will be WiFi and access to video cameras located in a parking lot and a clearing. Only at our meeting you will see and be able to participate in the game Admin DOOM .
