Microsoft and IIDF invite to a series of student hackathons Imagine Cup

Imagine Cup hackathons are unique two-day events organized by Microsoft in cooperation with the Internet Initiatives Development Foundation (IIDF) specifically for those who would like to learn how to create modern mobile and cloud applications. In the next couple of months, we will visit 8 different cities of Russia to help novice developers to invent and create new applications on the Microsoft platform, as well as to spend an unforgettable night in a company with computers and like-minded people.




What can participants do on the hackathon?


  1. Quickly learn the basics of developing modern applications and games (in C #).
  2. Come up with your own idea, which can be implemented in a few hours. If you have no idea yet - we will help!
  3. Program the prototype of the application using the knowledge gained and a library of examples. If you have difficulties - we will help, and even sometimes we will write code for you!
  4. Look at the ideas and applications of other students, participate in a mini-competition based on the results of the hackathon, and get acquainted with the community of active student-developers in your city.
  5. Apply for the Imagine Cup competition or for participating in a Microsoft student partner program.
  6. Learn about business models of modern applications and how you can make a startup from the idea and prototype of an application.
  7. Spend an exciting night with a team of friends for a laptop!


An application developed on a hackathon can be the basis for a project submitted for the Imagine Cup competition, either the start of its startup or just a good line on the resume (not every IT specialist can boast of his application in one of the app stores).

Where and when?


We plan to visit 8 cities of Russia. The estimated schedule is shown below. It may vary depending on the circumstances and the success of pre-registration for events.
  • October 11-12 - Perm
  • October 18-19 - Krasnoyarsk
  • October 25-26 - Tomsk
  • October 25-26 - Astrakhan
  • November 7-8 - Nizhny Novgorod
  • November 15-16 - Kazan
  • November 28-29 - St. Petersburg
  • November 29-30 - Tolyatti


What to take with you


To successfully develop your application, you need your laptop (at least one laptop per team) with pre-installed software: at least, this is Visual Studio, but it also makes sense to install Unity and Monogame (if you want to develop games). Read more about the required software here .
