Become a representative of Opera in your university



The university tours organized as part of the new educational program Opera Software are in full swing. At the same time, new articles are published in which high-class specialists share their web development experience using HTML, CSS, JavaSvript and DOM. Meanwhile, the educational program continues to evolve in a rather interesting direction. A couple of days ago, a new section was opened on the project website - Opera Campus Crew . Speaking in human terms, now any student (or teacher - why not?) Can become the official representative of Opera Software in his educational institution. Naturally, like any official representative, you have both duties and more pleasant concerns. The first are:

- assistance in organizing visits of company representatives to your university;
- organization of Opera users meetings among students;
- assistance in the introduction of educational aids in the educational process, created as part of the Opera Education Program project (yes, those on dev.opera.com );
- promotion (by convincing officials) of the Opera browser on university computers, as well as students and teachers computers, cell phones and smartphones (I remind you that the license for Opera Mobile is completely free in this case);
- help novice opera users to work with the browser.

In exchange, the Norwegians also will not remain in debt. They promise:

- prizes and gifts to the best university teams / representatives every semester;
- individual only for opera representatives of T-shirts and other "attributes of power";
- A special document from the company, giving the status of "University Business Partner";
- the opportunity to discuss new ideas and ideas with the University Opera team in a personal blog / forum;
- obtaining good skills in organizing events, work experience as a company representative, interaction with the media, etc .;
- you will learn much more about the company itself, and about web standards, as well as be able to share your knowledge with others;
- it is possible that you will become a participant and organizer of memorable meetings with people who are widely known in the software world;
- and, most importantly, take a personal part in improving the Internet and then you can honestly say that "the Internet has become better and thanks to me too."

In general, here is such an interesting idea. Anyone can simply register as a member of the program. We are waiting for new opera representatives in all universities of Russia and countries of the former USSR!
