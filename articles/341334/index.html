DotNet-community again in touch. I hasten to inform you that the .NET community is still living and developing! Since everyone already knows about our .NET communities, we will not spread the thought on the tree, but let's get straight to the point!

DotNext, Moscow 2017


Recently, DotNext has begun to practice two-day conferences and another conference, which will be held in Moscow, according to tradition in the Slavyanskaya Radisson hotel will not be an exception. The most hardcore .NET conference will take place November 12-13.



The program, as always, is expected to be dense and stellar. Andrei Akinshin, Dino Esposito (who became a regular DotNext), the Goldstein family and many equally famous people will perform. Separately, I would like to say that this time it will be possible to meet with myself ... Mark Siman, who for a long time could not be dragged onto DotNext.
