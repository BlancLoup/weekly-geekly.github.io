iNew V8 - special, but not perfect

In early July, the Chinese company iNew (created in 2012) announced the V8 model, a clone of the famous OPPO N1 smartphone, famous for its 180 degree rotating camera.



On closer comparison, we see that the similarity is rather only external, because the filling of the V8 does not resemble the filling of the OPPO model. Therefore, the smartphone will be considered as a separate full-fledged model, without affecting its origin in terms of design and main features. In this case, the smartphone has another "chip", which I will tell you later in the review.

‚ñå TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS


iNew V8 is equipped with a new six-core processor from the company MediaTek called MT6591, the cores operate at 1.5 GHz. Inside there are 1 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal memory with the possibility of expansion with a microSD card. Display 5.5 inches with HD resolution (1280x720 pixels) and IPS matrix, covered with Corning Gorilla Glass 2 protective glass. 13 megapixel camera, Sony's second-generation sensor with a F2.0 aperture. V8 works on the Android 4.4.2 KitKat operating system. There is also a wireless connection using NFC technology, support for 2 SIM cards and a special rear touch panel.

‚ñå DESIGN, DIMENSIONS, CONTROL ELEMENTS


iNew V8 has a very interesting and non-standard design. Sizes of the smartphone: 155.7776.8 mm. Weight: 166 grams.



Now almost all such devices, the front of the phone consists of a single monolithic glass frame with a frame around it. Immediately we see the top of the phone, consisting of a beautiful white non-glossy plastic, which smoothly passes into the front of the frame of the device. Immediately are the sensors of lighting, proximity and conversational speaker.



From the bottom, on the front of the smartphone, we see three touch control buttons: menu, home, back. They are highlighted, so there will be no problems with their use at night. Such buttons are present on almost all android smartphones, so everyone will be accustomed to using them.



On the right side of the iNew V8 there are volume rockers and an on / off button of the device. The arrangement of these buttons is good, they fit well under the thumb and are pressed with a little effort. It is also excellent tactile sensations, so there will be no problems with finding these keys with your finger.



On the left side there are no keys, just a beautiful frame for the entire perimeter. I think that the manufacturer did the right thing, moving all the control buttons to the right side, since With such a large diagonal, it will be difficult to press something on the left side.



At the bottom of the phone is a connector for charging and connecting to a microUSB PC, a speaking microphone and a 3.5 mm audio jack. Through native headphones on a standard player, a good balanced sound, not refined, of course, but good. The cables are well inserted, I did not observe any problems with connecting to these connectors.



On the upper part, there is nothing but a pivoting camera mechanism. There is nothing more and should not be, since all the alleged parts of the control are on the right side.



The back is made of soft-touch white plastic. In the middle is the inscription iNew. Below the inscription is a square touch panel that can be used to fit your needs. It is slightly highlighted on the plastic, so as not to lose its location. Below is the speaker, its volume is large enough, it is enough to hear the call in the noisiest place. Just above the dynamics is the marking of the smartphone. Above is the mechanism by which we can rotate the camera by as much as 207 degrees. It has a camera eye and a dual LED flash. Under the camera is a ribbed surface, for which I need it, I did not understand. It is possible so that the device does not slip out of the user's hands.



Under the back cover we see slots for SIM, microSIM and microSD. The battery on the phone at 2400 mAh. Hot swap is not provided. On the reverse side is the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe touch panel, which is connected to the phone when closing and the area for wireless communication through the NFC module.



It is safe to say that iNew V8 is built with high quality. Nothing creaks and does not play. A sort of monolithic block, which looks spectacular and relatively easy to use, given the size of its screen. But still, such a screen is considered unnecessarily large for mobile devices. Also one of the downsides is the absence of the LED event indicator. I do not know why the manufacturer chose this solution, because it is a very convenient common feature. The display processes up to 5 touches, no problems with the touchscreen is noticed. All touches are well recognized, no false positives.



The screen is very high quality and juicy. Knowing that the display diagonal is 5.5 ‚Äù, and the resolution is only 1280x720, I was preparing for the apparent graininess. Brightness margin is very good. I saw a lot of different phones and their behavior in the sun. This display fades, but not as much as it could. At the very sunny day, it still turns out to read information from it, but not as easily as we would like.



Viewing angles at the maximum display. If you look at the display from the sides, you can see that on one side the image turns yellow, and on the other it turns blue.

The display is not bad, in general, it pleased me.
