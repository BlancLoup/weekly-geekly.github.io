How to get started on the stock exchange and how to buy Tesla shares



Most of the questions from novice investors arise at the stage of acquaintance with the stock market - in our country a considerable amount of myths and delusions have formed around it.

In our blog, we already talked about how exchange trading in Russia is organized , compared investments on the exchange with financial instruments of financial preservation offered by banks , and studied the possibilities of buying shares of famous IT companies .

In continuation of the topic, we organize educational seminars and webinars dedicated to the issues of starting work in the stock market and investing in shares of international companies.

The main thing is to start correctly.


Novice traders are forced to process a huge amount of information that may not directly relate to the execution of transactions - the legal subtleties of investment, the choice of software and even the stock exchange ( go to Forex or not ), brokers commission and stock exchange.

During the seminar, led by ITinvest expert Vasiliy Oleinik, students will learn how to develop their own working style in the stock market, get acquainted with various financial instruments and technical indicators, learn the SmartX trading terminal and learn many more important aspects of working on the stock exchange.

A detailed description of the seminar is available on our website .
