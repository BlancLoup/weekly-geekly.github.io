Let me explain some points:

> Why one day a week, and not, for example, a whole week once a month?

Unfortunately, commercial activity does not allow to stop the production process immediately for a week.

> Why is tuesday?

As working practice shows, this day can be thrown out without losing the rhythm of working with the customer. For example, on Friday or Monday will often fall on the days of delivery of results.

> Why just 1 day?

The point is not to allocate exactly working time. It is clear that such projects will be completed after work and on weekends. Depending on fanaticism. It is important to give one day freedom from the cares of the whole Studio at once. So that everyone could stop at some point and thoroughly discuss the idea, the script, the graphics.
