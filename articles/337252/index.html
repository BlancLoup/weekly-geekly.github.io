Epic Growth Conference - grocery marketing conference in Moscow

September 27 in Moscow, Mobio and Getloyal, with the support of AppsFlyer and myTarget, will organize a conference on product marketing Epic Growth Conference .

  • Alexey Moiseenkov (CEO, Prisma Labs)
  • Evgeny Lisovsky (CEO, Maps.me)
  • Mikhail Trutnev (COO, Ultimate Guitar)
  • Bayram Annakov (CEO, App in the Air)
  • Denis Litvinov (COO, FunCorp)

You can apply for participation on the website egconf.ru for free. The number of participants is limited, manual selection of applications is conducted.
