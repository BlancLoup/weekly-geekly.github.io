In this mode, it is worthwhile to stay in more detail. Since the frame can stand both vertically and horizontally, it is obvious that this function should rotate all photos on its side (relative to the position of the frame by default). But in reality it is not so - only those images that originally had a portrait orientation are turned.

At first glance, this seems unreasonable, but in fact it is quite a logical decision, since when you turn images with landscape orientation, they become too small for easy viewing. In other words, the images need to be selected for one particular frame of only one orientation, if you want to get a good impression of the images.

However, individual photos can still be rotated to any degree by calling the context menu when directly displaying a slide.

Mode "Mini Monitor"

In this mode, the frame can work as an ordinary second monitor. To do this, you need to install the Frame manager program. Personally, I tried this feature on Windows 7, and noticed only one drawback - when you turn off the device, the application that was displayed on the second desktop does not return to the first. Most likely this problem is related to the operating system than to the software of the device itself, but I don‚Äôt claim 100%.



Mode "Storage"

In this mode, the frame is defined on the computer as removable media (in the photo frame mode of the new device is not added), and you can work with it as with a regular USB flash drive. It is possible to split photos into folders; in the menu, as described above, it is possible to play images only from a specific directory.

A special point: as long as the computer does not complete the procedure for safely disconnecting the device, the frame will not respond to button presses. In general, this is an ordinary situation.

Impressions and debriefing


Initially, the frame makes an excellent impression: a stylish case, convenient operation, an excellent screen made using LED technology (described in previous articles. In short, the backlight of the LCD panel is not made by lamps, but by LEDs located around the device‚Äôs perimeter) - A great gift to anyone with photos :)

But a closer acquaintance reveals details that cloud the first impression. The first thing that personally caught my eye was a somewhat localized curve, as a result of which individual menu items might be incomprehensible to inexperienced users (it was written about them above). Also, the calendar has not been translated yet.
The second point is unnecessary, in my opinion, playback options. Hours over the image interfere with the frame to perform its main task, and two additional views made with playing around the image of the photo from Polaroid seem to be just a fun option that does not carry meaning. Although it is possible that they will find their user.

After downloading a large number of photos, it turned out that the 16: 9 form factor is not the most common among my photos and images at the moment :) On the other hand, there is nothing to grieve about - the screen diagonally is 8 inches, and therefore this form the factor is more likely to be assessed as an additional opportunity to watch widescreen images than the inability to expand your collection of images "full screen", so to speak.

It is probably not worth describing the quality of the image in detail, since it‚Äôs more likely to see it once than hear a hundred times. The photos below also do not convey the joy of viewing your favorite pictures and images.

The timer, seemingly seemingly unnecessary trifle (because the frame does not know how to play sounds, which means it cannot be an alarm clock), in the process of using the device is very helpful - if you put the frame in the bedroom, at night it will shine pretty much, even with set to 50% brightness.

The size of the internal storage personally suits me completely, all the more so since, especially for such use, photographs can be cropped into suitable sizes and thus significantly reduce the consumption of memory photos.

A couple of minor drawbacks - the buttons need to be developed, since right after the purchase of the device they go pretty tight, and the device menu cannot be called the fastest in the world. Apparently affects the size of the photos, as I said above.

As it became clear from the above, the frame does not know how to play video and sound. This dignity or disadvantage is up to you.

In general, the frame made a great impression. All the disadvantages are small, and the main function of the photo frame does not apply. Moreover, they are known to the developers, who soon promise to improve the situation for the better.

As far as I know, at the moment the frame is available in two colors: black and red (as in my photos) and just black.
