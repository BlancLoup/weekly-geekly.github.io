Sony's March premiere: Meet Xperia Sola



Sony today announced the new Xperia Sola . In contrast to the three already well-known new products of this year's NXT series, Sola belongs to a separate line, which can be seen from the absence of the already familiar transparent strip under the display. The diagonal of the screen is 3.7 ", so the model is exactly the size exactly between the 3.5-inch Xperia U and the 4-inch Xperia P recently announced .

The main feature of the novelty is the touch screen control without touch. The smartphone fixes the position of the finger above the display and highlights the necessary links, to navigate through which a short tapa is sufficient.
