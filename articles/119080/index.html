Meet the speakers of the school workshop Founders Bootcamp (part 2)

We continue to acquaint you with American business angels who will take part in the school-seminar Founders Bootcamp, which will be held from 2 to 5 June in the MSU science park.



Alan D. Lucas, a member of the Mass Medical Angels Association (MA2). Alan's career includes various senior positions in business development and marketing, as well as advising start-ups and developing companies that supply medical equipment. He worked as a top manager to increase the investment value, and he has extensive experience in successful IPOs and private placement of securities. His track record includes signing contracts with suppliers, establishing relationships with distributors and entering into marketing license agreements, managing marketing research, writing technical requirements, launching products and selling. Alan is a member of the Mass Medical Angels, Massachusetts Medical Device Industry Council and Medical Development Group, a mentor at MassChallenge and the Technology Development Bureau of Boston University. In addition, he is an Entrepreneur-In-Residence at First Founders and a partner at CMC Consulting Boston.

Inessa Obenhuber, business angel and startup consultant, member of the Seraph Group. Inessa is currently an active member and investor in the Seraph Group, an association of business angels with a focus on global investment. Seraph Group unites more than 135 representatives in 20 cities that specialize in information technology, healthcare, consumer goods, financial services, biotechnology and the Internet industry. Such a network allows you to effectively apply the experience and communication of representatives of Seraph. In addition, Inessa is the founder of Kiventures and was the first managing partner of the IVO Technology Fund. Investments: Silicon Pipe (acquired by TPL Group), Tukaroo (acquired by Ask Jeeves), FrontBridge Technologies (acquired by Microsoft), IronKey, Droplet Technologies, Silver Tail Systems, PBWorks, Clean Filtration Technologies, Icon Aircraft, Adina World, HMicro, Garrison Enterprises, VitaminD, Charles Chocolate, iSpionage, LifeVantage (public company), Searchlight Minerals Corp (public company), Linear Technology (public company). Inessa is the board of several portfolio companies. Her name is among the inventors in four patent applications in the United States.
