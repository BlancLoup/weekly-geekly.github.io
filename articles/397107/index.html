Caution: Knee-applied DIY

Gadget requirements:
  • Counting the number of strokes
  • Counting the "approximate" impact force based on the displacement and deflection angle.
  • Training mode with the ability to set the intervals from and to between the alarm about the need to strike
  • Verification mode Formation of the progress chart and the average time lag between the signal and the impact.
  • Send information via Bluetooth to your Android device.
  • The possibility of differentiated light indication of the need to make various blows, for example, with legs or hands.


I assembled a prototype of what turned out to be in the pantry:
  • Arduino Mega 2560
  • Gyro MPU-6050 GY-521
  • Piezo emitter
  • LED matrix
  • Battery
  • Bluetoth module HC-05


KickBrick is a sports gadget that allows you to train the speed of reaction and the speed of impact, the gadget is mounted on a punching bag with a belt. Setting up and receiving information about the success of a workout is done when synchronizing with a mobile phone or tablet via Bluetooth, KickBrick uses light and sound indication to signal the need for a strike. Sensors in the device record the moment of impact and the time lag since the alarm.
