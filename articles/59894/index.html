The Book of Total Commander

Authors: Vlasov K. A., Kazakov V. Yu.
Name: Total Commander: effective work with files and archives
Series: In the Original
Publisher: BHV-Petersburg

Format: 70 * 100 1/16, paperback
Volume: 752 pages
ISBN 978-5-9775-0022-7
Circulation: 2000 copies.
Price: from 400 rubles.

annotation

You can work with files and directories in different ways. Someone does not know anything except the standard Explorer, and even simple operations turn out to be long and inconvenient for him. And someone has mastered other, more powerful and convenient tools and can cope with any task effortlessly. Want to be able to work with files as effectively? Then this book is for you! After reading it, you will learn how to use Total Commander's remarkable file manager, which will turn you into the full owner of your computer and make it simple and enjoyable what previously seemed incredibly difficult or even completely unthinkable.

This is the first and only publication in Russian today, completely dedicated to Total Commander. If you are new to the computer world, it will help you to join the inexhaustible variety of opportunities provided by the computer. Experienced users will be able to find for themselves a lot of ways to solve various tasks related to file operations - from fairly simple to the most cunning and unobvious.

On the enclosed CD-ROM you will find a trial version of the Total Commander distribution, a Russification package for it, as well as a set of useful plug-ins and utilities described in the book.
