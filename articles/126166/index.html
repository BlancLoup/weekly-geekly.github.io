Determining positions for pros

Sescout.com is an automated, hourly, updated, neat and beautiful search engine positioning service used by top pros.

- Alerts by e-mail, sms, Push notifications when the site gets to the first page of issue.
- PDF and CSV reports.
- Super interface.

5 tariff plans.
- Free . 10 keywords and 10 domains.
- Lite $ 14.97 per month. 50 keywords and 50 domains.
- Basic $ 29.97 per month. 200 keywords and 200 domains.
