Numberama. Reviving your favorite game on iOS



Remember this game? They called it differently: numbers, numbers, seeds, 19, and there was no official name. The rules are simple. At the beginning of the game, numbers from 1 to 19 (except 10) are written out on a piece of paper in a check. It is necessary to cross out the same or giving a total of 10 digits, which are next to or through the crossed out digits. When all possible pairs are crossed off, you rewrite the remaining numbers at the end. Victory is the complete deletion of all numbers.

Despite the simple gameplay, the game was popular because:
- any schoolchild or student is always at hand has a notebook and pen;
- One game can be played for a very long time. The fans could find several glued sheets cut to the width of the field, that is, such tapes, reaching a meter or more.
- the game itself is interesting and addictive. 40 minutes of a lesson - no problem, two pairs for 3 hours is also nonsense. It is more difficult to break away from the game to go for a break. :)
