Europe overtook America on the mobile Internet

The Old World is actively using mobile devices to access the Network, reports eMarketer . The results of a survey of residents of the UK, Germany, Spain, Italy and France indicate that today about 29% of the European population prefer the mobile Internet. For comparison, in the States, this figure is only 19%.

The most active users are the British, Germans and Italians (in each of these countries, about 34% of the population go online using mobile devices), followed by the French and Spaniards, and the Americans solemnly close the "parade".
