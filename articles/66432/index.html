Reliable safe ... in your pocket

Not everyone knows that the data in the notes, encrypted on Evernote's desktop clients, can also be opened in the iPhone version by entering the appropriate password. Thus, you can constantly, even when you are not at your computer, have access to important protected data, such as various pin codes, passwords and other information that it makes sense to protect from prying eyes.



To access the encrypted data, simply tap the area with hidden content and enter the password in the message window that appears. The data will be open until you close the note.

By the way, when encrypting fragments in Evernote, the cryptographic algorithm RC2 is used . Without knowing the master password, it is impossible to recover the contents of the encrypted fragment. Try to remember this password, otherwise even Evernote technical support staff will not be able to recover your data.

PS We want to remind that for the convenience of users, we have created public notebooks, which we regularly update. Notes with examples of using the service are available at http://www.evernote.com/pub/evernote/ru_tips , and answers to frequently asked questions about the service can be found at http://www.evernote.com/pub/evernote/ru_faq .
