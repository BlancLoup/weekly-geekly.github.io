How to count

The site says it is magic. In fact, it is not, and the formula is. We take into account the distance to various infrastructure facilities of the city and their impact on the standard of living.

Of course, we do not take into account the parameters of the car: neither the remoteness from the center of the city, nor the condition of the house, and certainly we do not know about the "cheerful" neighbors. We simply assumed that the better the infrastructure is developed, the more likely it is that all this is indirectly at a normal level.
