MediaTek Labs conducts another free seminar online.

On September 8, 2016, MediaTek Labs conducts another free seminar online. The meeting will be devoted to prototyping the system of energy-efficient devices for the Internet of things using a secure connection using Wi-Fi based on the new LinkIt 7687 HDK development board. Register for the webinar on the link .


The new MediaTek LinkIt breadboard for real-time operating system (RTOS) is based on the ARM Cortex-M4 MCU processor and provides excellent opportunities to develop various network devices and smart devices, home and office automation elements, control modules for the Internet of things.

The MediaTek LinkIt 7687 HDK development board (hardware development tools) was originally developed by Silicon Application Corp. Group (SAC) for supporting the LinkIt platform for RTOS and based on MediaTek's reference design. This breadboard with Wi-Fi module can be used to quickly connect devices in a smart home or office.

During a 30-minute webinar, MediaTek Labs technical consultant Philip Handshin will show you how to get started with LinkIt 7687 HDK, which allows you to develop a quick prototype of connecting devices for the Internet of things using a secure Wi-Fi connection. Then Philip will answer the questions of those present at the webinar.
