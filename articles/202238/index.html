[POLL] How do you feel about the selection of Q & A in a separate project?

As, probably, everyone already knows, recently the Q & A section on Habr√© has turned into an independent project - Toaster .
Perceived it was ambiguous.
I personally already had a few drawbacks compared to what was before:

- Harder to pick tags
Previously, questions were divided into hubs. As a memory, I naturally did not remember everything, but it was possible to open the list and scroll through, checking if there was something like that. But those hubs that interested me, I knew. Now the questions are cataloged using tags. No need to be a rocket scientist in order to understand what resource the tracing paper was taken from :) It may, over time, be more convenient, but now it is more difficult for me to use.

- Complicated search
Earlier If I was looking for something in Habr√©, usually there were a couple of necessary posts and a couple of questions. In sum, they pretty well covered the topic of interest to me. Now on the need to make a separate search. By the way, at the moment the relevant search on the Toaster is not so hot.

- No connection accounts
Previously, as already mentioned by people in the comments, it was possible to quickly get out of the minus, giving good answers to questions. Now this opportunity is lost. Do not you think that someone will go to search for the profile of the one who gave the answer to put a virtual plus sign?

It may be a matter of time, but for the time being I am not comfortable using it. Previously, questions could be added to favorites and they were stored in the same place as posts. Now you can subscribe to the question (and the favorites have moved there), but all the favorites in one place are much better.
