Russian companies and Finnish Jolla discuss the possibility of releasing a YotaPhone based on Sailfish OS

The developers of the Finnish operating system Sailfish and representatives of the companies STC ROSA and Alt Linux have discussed the possibility of serial production of YotaPhone based on Sailfish. So far, YotaPhone devices have an operating system based on Google Android .

A test copy of this smartphone based on Sailfish is available from Russian Communications Minister Nikolai Nikiforov. However, this configuration is not sufficiently prepared to enter the market. Sailfish supports Android applications, but so far it does not properly interact with the YotaPhone at the hardware level.

Jolla (developer of Sailfish) was one of the two main candidates for state support among software developers. His competitor in the category of mobile operating systems was the Samsung company with the Tizen operating system.
