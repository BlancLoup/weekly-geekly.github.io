Afisha-Mir has opened a service for travelers

Last night Afisha Mir opened the public beta version of the mir.travel Internet service. This site and the mobile applications for iPhone and Android that work with it help travelers understand what to see and where to go in any city in the world.

The last few months in testing the service could take part by joining a closed group on Facebook. Now the site mir.travel is open to all comers. Free apps can be found in the App Store and in the Android Market.
