Hyper-6


Extremely simple and compact abstract tactics about space. The plot - you need to transfer the fleet to the other end of the galaxy for a decisive battle. You and your opponents are moving in groups of ships to the black hole that you need to use for the jump. Who will fly first - wins. Chips of ships are cubes with different numbers of faces. The game uses them both as chips and as random number generators (after a hyperjump, the cube must be thrown - the relativistic speed changes randomly), and as variables (on the cube the current speed of the ship, and you can change the cube by one division). The strategy of the game is to use your own and other people's ships as mass anchors for hyperjumps in a converging orbit.
