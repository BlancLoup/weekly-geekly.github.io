What can Fishing FanCam


The float weighing 46 grams called Fishing FanCam is equipped with a compact video camera, thanks to which the fisherman gets a unique opportunity to view and record in the high-definition video all the ‚Äúevents‚Äù occurring in the water column at the casting site.
