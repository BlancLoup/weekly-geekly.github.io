The first cars with iOS on board will be shown at the Geneva Motor Show

Apple has announced CarPlay , an interface for using the iPhone while driving.



So far there is no talk about Apple replacing the e-stuffing of cars - CarPlay means only support for iOS devices at the hardware level: the Siri call key will be placed on the steering wheel, and iOS 7 icons will be displayed on the touch display of the car system.

The first cars with CarPlay will be Ferrari, Volvo and Mercedes-Benz; the cars will be shown at the Geneva Motor Show, which will open on March 6, writes Lenta.ru.
