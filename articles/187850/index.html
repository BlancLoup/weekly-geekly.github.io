Does Russia need data centers in the regions, and what should they be?

The Filanko Group of Companies with the DataHouse.ru project found its answer to this question by offering a unique solution that allows medium and small businesses that provide services in regional and local markets to place equipment in highly reliable, but small and inexpensive regional data centers. . In 2006, as part of the DataHouse.ru project, the first data center was built in Moscow at Nizhnyaya Syromyatnicheskaya, whose areas were quickly filled. In 2009, another capital data center was launched - at Burakova, and in 2010 - a data center in St. Petersburg.
