Oil Rush: official release


OilRush - real-time strategy from Russian developers from Tomsk.
Today was the release of versions for Linux, Mac OS X and Windows.
It should be noted that the project is one of the first technological games released for Linux.

The game is a mixture of real-time strategy and Tower Wars genre: you control the development of offshore platforms: oil-producing and producing, as well as their defense by creating defenses, while attacking enemy platforms from the sea and air. An additional variety of strategies brings advanced technology tree.

In terms of the setting, the game resembles the film ‚ÄúThe Water World‚Äù - a flooded world after a global catastrophe, in which few survivors fiercely fight for the remnants of the available resources.

Features:

  • Lack of DRM
  • Story campaign
  • Network play (LAN, Internet)
  • Availability of Russian and English versions
  • Availability of deb and rpm packages for 32 and 64 bit distributions
  • The ability to activate on Steam and Desura *
  • High-quality graphics. DirectX 11 and OpenGL 4.0

The game is developed on the Unigine engine, which is available for Microsoft Windows, Linux, Mac OS X, PlayStation 3, Android platforms.
Supported technologies: hardware tessellation, SSAO, SSDO, DirectCompute, OpenCL 1.1 and Shader Model 5.0.

You can buy the game in the Unigine Store, Desura and the Ubuntu Software Center.
