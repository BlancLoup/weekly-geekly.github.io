Here map service is offered for sale. Its owner, the Finnish company Nokia, considers Uber taxi service, a group of car companies from Germany, as well as private investors as potential buyers. Nokia plans to focus on developing wireless networks.

In connection with this news, Nokia shares rose by 5.6% and reached the price of 7.58 euros. In the fourth quarter of last year, sales in the Here division increased by 15% and reached 292 million euros.

In 2013, the company sold its mobile division of Microsoft for $ 7.5 billion. After that, Nokia had 3 areas of development: the development of network infrastructure, the Here service and the technology licensing department. Here service is estimated at 2 billion euros. However, in 2008, when Nokia was just starting work on Here, it bought out the manufacturer of digital maps Navteq for $ 8.1 billion. So, the price of Here is relatively small.

Nokia plans to acquire Alcatel-Lucent, a French telecommunications company. The price of its shares on the background of information on the possible sale of Here increased by 4.8%, as reported by Bloomberg.

Last year, the quarterly revenue of the network division of Nokia Networks grew by 8% and reached 3.37 billion euros. The company's revenue amounted to 12.7 billion euros and remained at the 2013 level.
