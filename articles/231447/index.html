Portable Wind Turbine Windpax

On Kickstarter, the WindPax project has already collected more than 80 thousand dollars out of the declared 50. This is a wind turbine that you can take with you on a hike, so that your beautiful mobile phone or camera will not let you down at the most inopportune moment. After all, until now the weak point of most gadgets is their battery.

Perhaps the main disadvantage of this device is its size. And the plus is that you can attach it even to a table set in the park, even to a tree in the forest.



Windpax is designed as a vertical wind turbine. The generator inside the gadget generates energy, which will help you use a pen-battery with USB and Mini-USB ports. The battery is removable, in addition it is possible to charge the gadget directly from the turbine, while this battery charges your other devices.



This video shows how to mount the turbine.



WindPax for donators will be presented in several colors.



Wind turbine fins are made of plastic to avoid injury. The developers have also considered the protection of the gadget itself: it meets the requirements of IPX-6 protection against water and the fifth level - to protect against dust. So you can use WindPax in rainy weather.
