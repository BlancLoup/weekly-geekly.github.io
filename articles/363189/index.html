Wearable device concept

This cry of the soul is published in order to draw the attention of interested people to the possible development vector of wearable electronics. I offer my vision of the development of these devices and in fact agree with the concept outlined by one science fiction writer (mentioned in the material).



Who is interested in my nonsense, welcome under the cat.

Not so long ago, some ten years ago, we could not imagine how mobile equipment and mobile devices would develop. I remember the first PDA, bluetooth connection with the phone to gain access to the network, communicators on windows mobile. Against that background, the iPhone that came out after was a space rocket. One of the last communicators BC (before the era of iOS and Android) is still in the closet. The battery is charged at 50%, a piece of paper is laid between the contacts of the phone and the battery in order to avoid discharge. I will store as much as I can, the device is already almost a dinosaur. It would be necessary to fill the original firmware as long as possible.

But this is the lyrics. Devices and operating systems developed at a rapid pace and continue to do so. New functions are added, iron becomes more powerful. The niche of these devices is highly controversial. And, finally, timid attempts to make additional smart devices began to appear, and the clock boom has recently begun.

I would like to talk about the future of these systems. Just want to say, behind the smart watch is only marketing. The technological component is given a place somewhere there, just to work. Such a clock is stupid to position for people of those professions and hobbies that force them to be separated from civilization for some time. The socket is needed for hours, the connection to the Internet. That is, travelers, rescuers and the like in the passage. Maybe then their niche office laborers? Again, no. So, the general concept is as follows. There is a box the size of a smartphone. No screen, with a SIM card slot, memory card. With charging connectors. Main unit. This approach has both advantages and disadvantages. Plus the fact that peripherals are cheaper. They do not need memory, communication modules of various types (except the communication module with the main device), SIM card modules, memory cards, etc. It's not scary to lose such a device, since there is no data on it. In case of failure, it is easy to replace. The physical dimensions of the devices decrease, the service life of a single charge increases, since they do not need to constantly go online and synchronize data.

The downside is that the main device will have to be controlled and not lost. Although in the case of normal data protection and encryption, the risk of damage is minimal in the case of data synchronization with the cloud.
