Oval Elephant - another competitor to Raspberry Pi



In general, after the success of the Raspberry Pi, it was already clear that competitive models soon released by other companies would appear. It was not long to wait: almost immediately after the announcement of this computer flash drive, other models of mini-computers began to appear, and some turned out to be surprisingly successful. As an example of such devices, you can bring those that have already been published on Habr√©: here , here and here . Now another member of the mini-PC family has appeared.

This is Oval Elephant, computer flash drive with Android OS on board. Despite the name, the device is neither oval nor elephant-like. As a hardware platform, manufacturers have equipped the device with an AllWinner A10 Cortex A8 processor with a core frequency of 1.5 GHz. The graphics system is Mali 400. The user has 4 GB of internal memory, plus support for microSD cards up to 64 gigabytes. There is also a wireless 802.11b / g / n WiFi module.

The developers have provided 1 GB of RAM, which, along with the specified characteristics, makes it quite productive. The cost of a computer flash drive is not very high, although it is higher than that of the Raspberry Pi. Its price is 71.49 US dollars. After 1000 pre-orders have been collected, the cost will be reduced to $ 65.

Deliveries will begin on July 12 - this is the time of sending the first batch, and on July 20 - the second batch.
