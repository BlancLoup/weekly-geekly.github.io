Derby 0.6 - FAQ


Recently, due to the lack of normal documentation for derby version 0.6, I have often been asked by mail and through various services.

For information to accumulate, I started a separate repository for this - derby-faq . Now there are a couple of dozen questions with detailed answers and code examples. I suggest everyone to participate in its development.

How can I do that?
  • First, you can ask questions derby, I will answer and, if the questions are interesting in my opinion to everyone, post them in a turnip.
  • Secondly, if you yourself dug into something and found the answer, you can make a fork, add your information and make a pull request.
  • Thirdly, we need volunteers to translate this FAQ into English, our French colleagues asked for this, although they would translate it from English into French.
