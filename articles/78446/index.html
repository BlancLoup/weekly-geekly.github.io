I present to your attention "Rejector" - http://rejector.ru .

This is a smart DNS service with a number of advanced, customizable features. Its goal is to help you effortlessly set up filtering and protection of your traffic, speed up the response time of sites and facilitate access to the Internet for inexperienced users.
Of course, we are not Google, and we can‚Äôt get IP that can be remembered at a glance, but we can provide certain services to our users:

- filters by category;

- correction of incorrect input of addresses;
- filtering phishing domains;
- statistics of all requests made;
- as well as DNS-based services that you can come up with and tell us.


Filters by category.


Each existing domain in our database may fall into one or several categories (social networks, cars, file exchangers, etc.). Our base is still quite small and has about 500,000 domains. We hope for the active help of our users in its replenishment.

There are 44 categories of domains and so far only 5 preset access groups - safe, office, children, non-blocking and individual. In the individual group, you can select categories manually.

Personal access lists.


Everything is clear: you can deny or allow access to a specific domain. The number is not limited. Filtering by masks is not yet available, added to TODO.

Bookmarks or short names.


This service allows you to convert a short site name to any URL. For example: access to the address http://habrahabr.ru/blogs/startup/ on the short name startup .

Works at the level of HTTP redirects, i.e. It‚Äôs not possible to match your short name with an IP address. Add to TODO.

Correction of incorrect input addresses.


Entering the "rfikukg" in the address bar and going to this address, you will automatically get to the page where you will be informed about the error and offer the correct option. Provided that this domain does not exist in the Cyrillic representation, of course. Also requests are checked in ‚Äúpunycode‚Äù encoding.

An invalid query is reversed in the layout, and also sent to Google search. Correction works without registration.

Filtering phishing domains.


The database on fraudulent sites is updated once an hour using the PhishTank service. If you select the Phishing category, all requests for fraudulent sites will be denied.

Statistics of all requests made.


All sent requests are saved for your analysis in the following sections: Correct, Invalid, and Blocked. For the report, you can choose any time period times the day.
