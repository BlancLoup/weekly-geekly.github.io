GPS Navigator with augmented reality



The company Pioneer Japan announced the release of automotive GPS-navigators AVIC-VH09CS, AVIC-VH09, AVIC-ZH09CS and AVIC-ZH09, which for the first time in the world implemented augmented reality navigation system.

A wireless video camera on the windshield captures the video and broadcasts it to the navigator screen, where instructions for the route are overlaid in real time: where and where to turn. The system adequately estimates the distance to other objects, including the cars ahead.



New navigators will be available in Japan at the end of May, the cost has not yet been announced.
