Youtube.com removed from zapret-info.gov.ru

So far, you can quit trying to start your tractor, because youtube.com domain is excluded from the registry of prohibited sites. It was originally about the link to this video, which was really successfully blocked by the administration of the resource for our country.



Lenta.ru kindly posted a banned video on its Vkontakte page and did not forget to give a link to the registry dump , which contains the entire video hosting address and a link to the video itself. Not for the first time, serious doubts arise about how reasonably the mechanism for blocking unwanted pages or sites works. Fortunately, the blocking of the domain address (or a specific link to the video) by the providers did not have time to start because of a registry error.
