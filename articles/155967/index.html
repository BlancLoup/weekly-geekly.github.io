Acer Iconia Tab A701 - Return of the Jedi

The tablet. This is the trend of 2011-2012. Everyone already if did not get a tablet, then at least thought about its acquisition.
Giants of the market multiply the entire line of tablets. Acer has updated the 2011 model range with the following models: Acer Iconia Tab A200, A510, A701. The first is a simple tablet, with a USB host, without any frills, with a simple filling (Tegra2), nice design. Acer Iconia Tab A510 - the continuation of the hit A500 - there is already Tegra3, high performance and autonomy, reasonable price. But Acer Iconia Tab A701 opens a new niche for the line - the niche of flagship tablets. Powerful stuffing, FullHD display and low price. What happened - under the cut.

Appearance


The back panel is silver plastic with pits (something like a golf ball). The use of plastic is sad because it is not metal. Solidity and strength is not enough. But such a decision is also reasonable and understandable. Moreover, the plastic is not creaky and pleasant tactile.


The glass that covers the display is tempered. Alas, not a gorilla and not something strongly well protected. Scratches remain, prints are visible.


But the display itself is a fairy tale in the world of tablets on the android.
Viewing angles are very good. Brightness and clarity are amazing. No wonder - this is an IPS matrix.

But the most important thing in the display is its resolution - honest FullHD (1920 * 1200). Even the android notification panel takes those 120 pixels, turning the effective area into 1920 * 1080 pixels.
Thanks to this resolution, you can forget about the grain and concessions. The film in FullHD (and the filling allows it) to watch not just cool, but also incredibly nice! Paints juicy, detailing - the best. In general, I advise you to try once than read a thousand times.

Bottom line


Connector for accessories, charging and other things - at the bottom, on the end. Not very convenient, but no one forbids you to turn the device upside down.

In addition to the universal microUSB connector, in which you can plug in the charging, there is also a reset button on the bottom of the device. To the credit of the company Acer I will say - never had a chance to check it.

On the right side is the microHDMI connector (yes, we need an adapter), as well as a plug.

Under the cap, there are slots for microSD card and SIM card (full, not micro and not nano).


On the top side - the volume rocker, orientation lock lever and microphone (optional, for noise reduction).

operating system


Initially, the tablet appeared on the market with Ice Cream Sandwitch, however, after the appearance of Jelly Bean, Acer immediately announced that they would update their devices in 2012 to the new product. This is what happened.
Android 4.1.1 is currently installed.
The innovations compared to ICS are as follows (in addition to minor fixes):
- A full-fledged Google Now appeared, with voice input and search (also offline).
- Added Acer Cloud integration.
- Standard browser supplemented with Google Chrome browser.
- Increased smoothness of work.
- Fixed major firmware bugs A701 - namely, the low sensitivity of the sensor and overheating. Now everything works as expected.
- In addition, according to my personal feelings - besides smoothness, the speed of the interface was added.

Pre-installed software - not so much. It is very good! Acer Print, Amazon Kindle Store, AUPEIO !, EquiView are also installed. As well as the HD games EA Monopoly and Real Racing.
