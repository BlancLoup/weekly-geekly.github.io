Avito X: people - people

This year Avito is 10 years old. Perhaps one of you noticed our big advertising campaign under the slogan "People - People." Or maybe even saw the results of the anniversary contest of ads, when artists painted the most original ads online. This is all part of the holiday that could be seen outside. And what's inside? If you want to look behind the scenes and find out a few details about the life of the classifier leading in Russia - click on the cat!



Beginning of Avito History


Avito was founded by two entrepreneurs from Sweden. Jonas Nordlander came to Russia for the first time in 2006 after he sold his project to eBay corporation, the online auction Tradera.com. At that time, he had a successful project Bilannonser.se, which was also sold to eBay. In 2007 they became partners with Philip Engelbert - then he invested in the Swedish holding Kontakt East Holding (KEH), which bought the famous offline directory Yellow Pages. Together they came up with Avito.


Today, the question of how the name of the company appeared, the founders answer - an accident.

Chronology


This section briefly describes the major milestones in Avito development.


2007 - 2008


The first version of Avito, launched on October 15, 2007, provided for the possibility of placing not only private ads, but also auctions of private sellers.


2009


In 2009, the auction was decided to abandon and focus on working with ads.


2010


In 2010, Avito launched a large-scale Avito advertising campaign with the slogan ‚ÄúHow much is your mess?‚Äù. The monthly audience has increased to 10 million unique visitors. Then there was a moderation of ads. Avito has become possible to view from a mobile phone.


2011


Mobile applications and the ‚ÄúShops‚Äù service were launched, allowing professional sellers to create their own storefronts with goods. Avito entered the TOP-5 of the most visited resources of the Runet.


2012


In 2012, a special section of the Personal Account appeared, providing quick access to pay for Avito additional services and history of operations - Avito Wallet.


2013


The company's business grew, and in 2013, Avito merged with competing ad sites OLX.ru and Slando.ru under the Avito brand. After the transaction, the site entered the top three largest online classifiers in the world. Inside the Avito, the ‚Äúmycelium‚Äù DWH began to grow.


2014


In 2014, the Domofond.ru project was opened, a PRO-office for professional users appeared, and Startup started working. There was a site redesign.


2015


In 2015, an important event occurred - a controlling stake in Avito was sold to the South African holding Naspers. The Avito Context service started working, the Checkout project was included in the company, the opportunity appeared to correspond on the site with the help of Avito Messenger. Services for professionals entered the market - ActiAgent and ActiDealer .


2016


In 2016, Autoteka appeared - a car inspection service via VIN and Avito Subscriptions - a service in which we combined our Avito Shop with the ability to place ads from a renewable package of placements.


2017


In 2017, the search for Avito was updated. A public profile has appeared - this is the personal page of the Avito site user, accessible to all visitors, which contains information about the seller and his ads. In mobile applications, a lot of cool features have been added: search for ads by product photo, Vision for hiding faces and numbers of cars to serve ads, Voice Over, 3D Touch.
