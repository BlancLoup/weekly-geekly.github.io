To raise or not to raise - that is the question


I am not a business owner. And not an executive director. I, as well as all programmers, the hired worker. Therefore, the decision to raise the salaries of their subordinates is not made by me. But I give recommendations. And listen to them or not.

I reason like this.

How much should a programmer pay? As a rule, the market "fork" of remuneration for a specific qualification is known and ranges from X to 1.5 * X. You can take the risk and pay for the lower bar - X. However, the possibility of getting 1.5 times more for the same work is likely to outweigh all the other motives that keep the fighter on my team.
