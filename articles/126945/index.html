Original texts: Yandex against copy-paste

Yandex offered webmasters an alpha version of the new tool. The new service is designed for those sites whose texts copy other sites. At the same time, garbage sites that steal content sometimes manage to do it so successfully that they rank highly in search results, are better indexed and have time to be the first to provide other content to search engines.

Yandex offers webmasters before you post the original text on your site, upload it through a special form in Yandex.Webmaster. In this case, of course, the text should not be published anywhere else before. The advantage of this service is that you can publish content on the site immediately after sending it to Yandex.

The text should be from 2000 to 32000 characters, this opportunity is available for sites with a TIC of at least 10. You can read more here .

Undoubtedly, the problem that the service is intended to solve is very urgent. On Habr√© even offered to give out content simultaneously with the arrival of the robot on the site. And now you can send your text to Yandex and publish it boldly - its affiliation to your site will already be confirmed. The only potential problem may occur in those sites that have not yet used this system. After all, then all the content of such a site can "stake out" the attacker and place it with impunity. But, I think, to the release of all these nuances will be taken into account by the developers.

Let's hope that the new feature from Yandex will deliver a significant blow to copy-pasters and will contribute to the development of original sites with really useful content.
