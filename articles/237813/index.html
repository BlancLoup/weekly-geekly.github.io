What to draw MindMap? A detailed overview of the 6 most popular mindmap drawing programs

Structure of the review


1. Entry;
2. Comparison of the main 5 programs in popularity, price, compatibility, availability on different platforms;
3. Detailed description of the functionality and features of the 5 main programs.

Introduction


Mindmapping (drawing tree maps or mental maps) has firmly entered the lives of many people with the advent of the appropriate software. Using mindmap, technical tasks are done, analytical consultants make projects, designers come up with concepts, trainers make presentations, managers make plans, etc.

The purpose of this article is not mindmapping propaganda. The tree view has its advantages and its limitations. The discussion was already in the comments to the publication "+10 to the intellect" .

This publication is for those who want to choose a handy tool and learn some new chips that they did not know about yet.

For me, mindmap is a separate chapter of my life, planning, structuring, generating ideas, analyzing, etc. When, sitting at any conference, I write everything down in the mindmap, then those sitting behind and seeing the process always shove me business cards with requests to send these files. And sitting only at the 1st conference, you can make 15-20 cards. Over the last 5 years I have done more than 1,000 mindmaps (1043 files with mmap resolution, xmind, imx, etc. I found on my disks and in the ‚Äúclouds‚Äù) and all this was done in 5 different programs.

XMind


Popularity: high
Price: 0 - $ 99
Platforms: Windows, MacOS, Linux
Product site: www.xmind.net

The main feature: it has a supported free desktop version.
He also has only sheets (tabs) inside 1 file. Very comfortably.

The possibilities are generally average. XMind copes with the task of creating a mindmap, with a basic set of links, markers, tags, etc. In addition to the price and tabs - there is nothing that XMind would remember.

The Pro version has a presentation mode.

Mindjet Mindmanager


So we got to the mindmap tool, the most popular in Russia.



Popularity: above average, in Russia the leader in popularity
Price: from $ 400 (from $ 275 subscription per year)
Platforms: Windows, MacOS, iOS, Android
Product site: mindjet.com

Mindjet is undoubtedly the leader of the corporate market. And in general, it is not a tool for drawing a mindmap, but a tool for corporate management. Extensive database capabilities, writing macros, exporting to MS Project and many other functions.

For me personally, the most useful feature is the export of mindmap to MS Project. This allows literally 10-15 minutes to distribute the task tree in Mindjet, and then export it in a couple of clicks to Project. It is important that in Project the hierarchy of tasks is preserved and it takes 3-4 times less time to compile the project timing.

Also one of the interesting features of Mindjet is a completely different version under MacOS. After Mindjet for Windows, the version for MacOS looks very simple and unfinished.

Conclusion: a good player for the corporate market, rather a management tool, rather than a mindmap. Very expensive.

Mindmeister


In fourth place in the world in popularity is the young, but very brash Mindmeister.



Popularity: above average
Price: 0 - $ 72 - $ 120 per year
Platforms: Web, iOS, Android
Product site: www.mindmeister.com/?r=2029
(I allowed myself to insert an affiliate link)

Cloudy, in my opinion, ideally designed design, export and import, humane pricing policy - all this makes it one of the most promising players in the market. Mindmaps in the cloud is a smart idea. The opportunity to work together is also a very good idea. In addition, mindjet and imindmap have online mindmap editors, but they are so slow and miserable that we can assume that they are not.

Up to 3 mindmaps can be done in free mode. The export / import mode in the free mode does not work. Recently, the minimum period, how much you can pay - six months (instead of a month earlier).

Personally, at one time I switched to 100% on Mindmeister. But, alas, the desire to work offline returned me to traditional desktop solutions. Even in Moscow, at some conference with not very good internet, I want to record in mindmap. Mindmeister allows you to do this only by downloading it in advance and going offline in the browser.

iMindMap





Popularity: low
Price: from $ 245
Platforms: Windows, MacOS, iOS, Android
Product Website: thinkbuzan.com

The most stylish, designer, creative, creative, bright, unusual. All these epithets are exactly about iMindMap. 4 years ago iMindMap won me over with its bright design. It has a very functional presentation mode. The presentation itself is bright, dynamic, animated, which allows you to keep the audience in good shape almost all the time.

One of the most key features is the ability to write text along branches, with branches of any shape. If you need a more official style - you can not write along the branches, and do the same as in MindJet and Xmind.

Experimentally verified: iMind animated presentation holds listeners' attention much better than the same one as PPT or MindJet. Want bright emotions, unusual presentation - I recommend.

But here, of course, it is worth noting that a professional presentation is better to do in something like Prezi, but in terms of the speed of creating an animated presentation iMind ahead of the rest.

Mindindode




Popularity: low
Price: $ 19.99
Platforms: MacOS, iOS
Product Site: mindnode.com

I first learned about MindNode when I saw this picture:



The conciseness and simplicity pleased me, as well as surprised by the fact that I did not know the tool, where MindMap was made. Literally a month later, I moved to MacOs and discovered MindNode. Here, the same text is written without frames, along the branches, which is convenient and concise, and the form is not as distracting as in iMindMap.

I love this tool and on Mac now I mostly work in it. Nothing extra, simple and convenient. But in order to share the file, you have to do a double export (first in Freemind format, and then from it with the help of mindmeister in * .xmind or * .mmap).
