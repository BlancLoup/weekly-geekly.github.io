Concept "Smart home and caring iPhone"


The problem of energy saving appeals to us not only from Moscow billboards. New York studio Kaleidoscope , following the global trend, decided on the concept of "Smart Home", where the main role is played by the iPhone.

How does everything work? For each outlet in a residential area, a special adapter is purchased, which via Bluetooth transmits to iPhone such parameters as voltage, amperage, power consumption in watts.

With the help of the necessary software, the phone translates everything into a graphic look, understandable to the owner of the house.

With two clicks, you can control the total network load, turn off the devices, assign timers to individual sockets, and much more. You look, accounts from ZhEKA will decrease.
