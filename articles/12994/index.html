The Japanese have released a mobile phone with three huge displays

KDDI Japan employees have always been distinguished by their love for creating unusual mobile phones. However, Japanese designers and engineers came up with this for the first time, although the idea that there is no need to conceal is not new and even banal.


Unusual mobile phone is made in the form factor "rakadushka" and has not one or even two, but three whole displays. And all three huge screens, apparently, are sensitive to touch.



The technical characteristics of the device remained behind the scenes (only the camera is known), as well as the plans of KDDI Japan for its mass production. However, in this idea and in the video in particular, there is something that does not allow you to completely believe in the real existence of the device.
