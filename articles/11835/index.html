25 iPhone Applications

The iPhone came out on Friday, but many developers have already written some interesting web applications for it. Of the variety, we selected the 25 most-most, and wrote a list with their description. These applications are:


iPhoneChat
One of the expected, but as a result, missing features of the iPhone is the online messenger. iPhoneChat allows you to use AIM.


Google reader
Without a doubt, the main advantage of current phones is the ability to receive information anytime, anywhere. The Google Reader app takes RSS reading to a new level.


iphonedigg
For iPhone owners reading Digg , this is a great program. It uses a sliding interface to maximize the small available space and displays the number of diggs for the read news.


OneTrip Shopping List
With this application you can create a shopping list. The OneTrip Shopping List has a nice simple interface, as well as organizing by category.


iPhlickr (iPhone + Flickr)
As the name implies, this service allows you to view iPhone photos from Flickr from your iPhone screen.


Hahlo
If you like Twitter , then this is the best mobile app for you. You can chat right from your iPhone using a quality interface.


iZoho
iPhone - a good device for doing things. And Zoho is one of the best online office suites used daily. iZoho allows you to combine these two components together and make it possible to create documents, as well as view tables and presentations from your Zoho account.


Gas.app
Enter the zip code and find out the cost of gasoline. The service works only in the USA, however, like the iPhone :)


Mymetar
Weather service optimized for iPhone.
Necessary locations can be saved.


Apple reader
Apple RSS feed reader.


iPhoneTravel
If you need to book a hotel room, plane ticket, etc. from your iPhone, then iPhoneTravel will help you. It should be noted that the application uses travel.ian.com to find prices.


iActu
Steve Jobs at the January presentation, read the New York Times using the iPhone, showing the browser. iActu allows you to read the headlines of another 5 most popular US newspapers.


iBookmark
iBookmark ‚Äî a bookmark management service that syncs iPhone bookmarks with your base del.icio.us.


101 Cookbooks
If you like to eat and do not mind cooking, then 101 Cookbooks will help you, as it is a virtual book of recipes.


YouTube
YouTube on the iPhone? Of course! Who followed all the news knows that Apple had an agreement with Google.


goMovies
Have you met your girlfriend and decided to go to the cinema with her? Find out all about new movies on the way to the cinema with goMovies.


Listingly
Service for creating various lists with the possibility of "sharing" and saving for future use. Can be used as a simple list of tasks or purchases.


iPling
Social network of interest. The service helps to find people with similar interests, after which you can send a message with a proposal to meet in the real world :)


iChess
Do you like to play chess? Then this program is created for you. So that it is not boring or insulting :), you can select the desired level of difficulty of the game.


NYTimes River
NYTimes River reformats news from the New York Times for easy reading. Now you do not have to scale and rotate the screen to read this newspaper from their site , as did Steve Jobs.


CheapMF
If you go to buy something, it will not be superfluous to compare prices. CheapMF is looking for new and used merchandise prices on Amazon.com. Also using this service you can get reviews.


iTouch
iTouch is an iPhone game that tests your reaction based on how quickly you click on a series of dots appearing on the screen.


Avalche
Another game, this time similar to Tetris. I will not explain in detail - it is better to follow the link and see for yourself. Parting words: the faster you click on the squares, the more points you get. But do not forget that a figure consisting of less than three cubes will not disappear.


Expense view
This application allows you to enter into your iPhone various expenses, then come home and find out what and where you spent. It is possible to sort expenses by category.
