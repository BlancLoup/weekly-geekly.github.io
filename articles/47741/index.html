The uniqueness of the competition is that regional projects will be able to get federal publicity and declare themselves in all their glory.


The organizers of the New Reality Festival are the Regional Public Center for Internet Technologies (ROCIT) and TTK. The festival is supported by the All-Russian Internet MARATHON.

The competition for the best Internet resources at the regional level is held for the third time with the support of ROCIT and has already managed to become a landmark event for the entire Russian Internet industry. It is worth noting that since 2008 the format of the ‚ÄúNew Reality‚Äù Festival has undergone some changes compared to previous years: the festival takes place at a different time, the criteria for evaluating nominees will be changed and supplemented, but most importantly, the contest nominations have been reformulated with the latest Internet community.
There are still ten nominations, but they sound like this:
1. DEVELOPMENT OF THE INTERNET IN THE REGIONS (only ORGANIZATIONS)
The personal award is given to a leader in the region who forms and develops the IT community. The award is awarded to the regional leader by decision of the jury.
2. BUSINESS ON THE INTERNET
Nomination, in which the winner is the best business-oriented project, commercial organizations that conduct business on the Internet are invited to nominate.
3. SCIENCE AND EDUCATION
Internet resources that develop science and education on the Internet are nominated.
4. CULTURE
Nomination, designed to determine the best online resources in the field of culture: museums, library collections, theaters, etc.
5. MEDIA AND MASS COMMUNICATIONS
The nomination involved the websites of the media and media communication.
6. REGIONAL AND URBAN INTERNET RESOURCES
Websites are invited for nomination, the main content of which is aimed at the development and popularization of a particular region, district, city, etc.
7. WEB 2.0
Nomination, in which Internet resources are estimated, which use the newest means of forming the user environment.
8. NON-COMMERCIAL INTERNET PROJECTS
In this nomination, non-commercial Internet projects take part, the main task of which is to solve the problems of society or to popularize socially significant ideas, projects, etc.
9. INTERNET FOR CHILDREN
Projects and resources intended specifically for children and their parents are invited for nomination.
10. ENTERTAINMENT
In this nomination, the best entertainment internet projects will be revealed: games, posters, Internet communities, etc.
An undoubted innovation is the moment that the terms of the Festival "New Reality" are now extended, it allows you to cover a greater number of Internet projects.
