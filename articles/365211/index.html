#AppleWatchOr Toyota Corolla? Or how Pebble troll Apple on Twitter

Yesterday, Apple introduced new products at Apple Spring Forward, a text translation of the event is available at Geektimes . Among the new products - the long-awaited Apple Watch in several versions of the case.

The watch maker Pebble, the record holder of the Kickstarter crowdfunding platform, decided to roll out Apple, including because of the price of new devices. Twitter users with the hashtag #AppleWatchOr wrote about what you can spend the amount of $ 17,000, which the company wants for one of the Apple Watch models with 18-carat gold. Options included a Toyota Corolla, a horse, a boat, a wedding, and a hundred Pebble watches.

And what would you spend $ 17 thousand?

The Pebble team on Monday watched the presentation of Apple's new products in the office. On Twitter, Pebble expressed her opinion and joked about the number of watches and their capabilities, while talking about herself - for example, recalling how to wash Pebble watches in water. Pebble retouched stinging messages from other users about new gadgets: for example, that it is better to burn $ 10,000, or that some parents will be able to send only one child to college, because for the rest of money dad will buy himself a new watch.
