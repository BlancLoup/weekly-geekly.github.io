Bird-talker from Sega Toys


Having released robots-chickens , the company Sega Toys plans to expand the range of such animals. DreamPet series was conceived as a series of toys for children who are allergic to pets: allergies to wool and down of robots should not occur. The release of each toy was preceded by a detailed study of the habits of animals; as a result, the animal robots will behave almost the same as the real ones.
