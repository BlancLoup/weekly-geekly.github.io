Personal 3D printers

A 3D printer, if you do not consider commercial and industrial use, is a tool for materializing ideas in art or in everyday life. Toys, souvenirs, mechanisms, large-scale and radio-controlled models, robots, jewelry and useful trivia are tasks for a personal 3D printer.

3D scanner

The bulk of 3D-scanners refers to high-precision professional equipment with a corresponding cost. There are also simpler devices that can be used in everyday life for scanning relatively large objects.

  • scanning area: up to 200 x 200 x 200 cm;

Hand scanner Sense is designed to scan people and other objects of similar dimensions. The device itself is compact, the software is not difficult to master.

3D pens

Utilitarian use of it, too, there will be, paired with a 3D-printer - to correct printing flaws, fill the seams when connecting elements of large models.
