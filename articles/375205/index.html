A private Russian company proposes to build a lunar base for 10 years.



According to TASS , Alexander Ilyin, the general designer of the Russian company Lin Industrial , which develops the Taimyr ultra-light rocket, declared its readiness to create a base on the Moon within 10 years after the adoption of the relevant decision. The cost of the base, according to preliminary estimates, will be 550 billion rubles. In the draft program, aimed at approval by the Cabinet of Ministers, it is proposed to allocate about 2 trillion rubles for the lunar program in 2014-2025. At the same time, manned expeditions to the moon and the deployment of the first elements of the lunar infrastructure are planned after 2030.

The project involves the use of existing equipment and tools that can be created in the next five years. Thus, it is proposed to use a modernized heavy Angara as a launch vehicle, the first flight of which took place in late December. The ship, according to Ilyin, can be created on the basis of the buildings of the descent vehicle and the domestic compartment of Soyuz, and the landing lunar module can be created on the basis of the Fregat upper stage. To maintain the life of the base, it will take 37 starts within five years.
