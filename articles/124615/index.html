Discontinued Google Toolbar for Firefox



Another Google product is being phased out. This time, such a fate befell the Google Toolbar extension for the Firefox browser. An unnecessary repetition of functionality is cited as a reason, since many of the functions of the Google Toolbar are already built into the browser or implemented as third-party extensions.

The help section reports that the Google Toolbar will not be updated for Firefox 5 and later versions of Fire Fox. As a replacement, they offer a long list of extensions that can replace each of the Google Toolbar functions with links to relevant search results in the Mozilla extension directory: form auto-completion , translation , grammar check , social bookmarks , bookmarked site preview , Gmail notifications .

However, after the release of the official Google Toolbar for Firefox, the update to the third-party extension was discontinued.
