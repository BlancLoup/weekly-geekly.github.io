Cool758 - mobile phone with a built-in electric razor



It is even strange that no one thought of this before. Especially since the release of the Razr V3. Cool758 - a Chinaphon with a built-in electric razor - savory spittle into Motorola's soul.

No one is surprised when manufacturers release women's mobile phones in the form of pendants (even a beautiful chest can turn even a beeping piece of plastic into an object of worship). But the men have traditionally been cheated. And as you know, with bluetooth and built-in megapixels you will not get a Brit.

Cool758 is both a joke, and a gift and an indispensable thing for travelers sent to the wilderness.

The razor is located under the end cover and is not at all a joke - the power is enough for daily facial care. Thus, Cool758 becomes an ideal travel-business phone. It is a pity, the camera let us down.

In addition to the razor, are embedded in the mobile phone:
2.5-inch touch screen (240x320 pixels, 260 thousand colors);
506 KB of memory;
Support for microSD memory cards up to 8 GB;
0.3 megapixel camera;
FM tuner, Bluetooth, 64-tone polyphony;
The battery capacity of 2800 mAh (not built, but the kit is included).

The handset handles the three-day stubble, supports two SIM cards, works on GSM 900/1800 MHz, has dimensions of 120x52x19 mm and is sold in China for about 112 dollars .
