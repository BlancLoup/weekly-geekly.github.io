More than 11,000 users signed a petition for Google - asking to release the Linux-client Google Drive



At the moment on the site change.org more than 11,000 users have signed the petition "Google: Create A Native Linux Google Drive Application".

Interestingly, native clients of the search giant's cloud service exist for Windows, OS X; You can also take into account the mobile version of the Drive for Android and iOS. Thus, the absence of a similar client for Linux looks at least strange. And given the fact that during the launch of Google Drive, the release of such a client was discussed, it turns out that the search giant at least put the idea aside.
