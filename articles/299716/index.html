Periscope shared statistics in honor of the year since the launch of the service



Last Saturday, exactly one year has passed since the launch of the Periscope video streaming service. During this period, there were several peaks in popularity of the service and today Periscope shared some data on the activity of its users.

In just one year, Periscope users spent more than 200 million broadcasts, and every day the service watches videos in time equivalent for 110 years. Recently, there has been a particularly rapid growth - only seven months ago, users watched videos of just 40 years a day in time equivalent. This indicator does not take into account views through the web version of the service and pasting to Twitter - the company considers only views in applications for iOS and Android.
