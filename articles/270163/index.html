As we for the first time in Ukraine held a security event on a grand scale

Hello to all IB's and hackers, here is a HackIT report with interesting details.

Surely, many have heard that Ukraine is officially recruiting for cyber police for the position of inspector and special agent. So, this is not an accident.

This event, which we have been preparing since May 2015, meant holding a forum in 3 sections of 21 speakers and a separate hall for holding the Olympiad in CTF format, where the 9 best scoreboards were awarded prizes.

I hope this post is not considered for advertising, because the event has already passed, and the purpose of the publication is to collect not indifferent people to cyber security in Ukraine.



Next - photos and videos.

A week before the event, our site was hacked, or rather zadefeysili.



This was expected and we, of course, made it public and published officially

The deface stood for exactly 2 minutes. The site was at that time in development.

Everything was within the framework of ethical hacking. The craftsman called, introduced himself and notified the developers with the organizers about the incident, for which he received his prize and public fame.



We have gathered surprisingly many people. Pupils, students, information security experts and teachers gathered.

For the time being, speakers amazed listeners who were incomprehensible, even for IT people, with words like: sniffing, skimmer, de-anonymization, quad-vpn and others. A fierce battle was taking place for the search for flags among the participants of the CTF Olympiad.





It was established 15 wifi points connected to the gigabit channel. Trays with cookies were constantly updated and coffee with delicious teas was poured, and in the middle of the day there was a lunch in the format of a buffet table so that you could take a break and gain strength.

A rather difficult task was to make an unforgettable and interesting show out of the event for the inhabitants who are far from cybersecurity and IT, since outwardly, nothing interesting happens: the ‚Äúcomputer people‚Äù sit and click on the keyboard. But the guys from the event agencies who helped us in the organization came up with many interesting stands, as well as the broadcast of the Olympiad together with the scoreboard in the common hall.
