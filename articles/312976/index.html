Ruby Hero Russia Award 2016

Hello!
There are only 4 days left for the RailsClub'2016 conference, and everyone is already waiting for a meeting with our celebrity speakers.

As we have repeatedly said, the purpose of the conference is to popularize the Ruby language in Russia and in the world. As part of the Rails Club, we want to get together, listen to great reports, and also identify and encourage the work of those of us who make a great contribution to the development of native technologies. Therefore, several months ago, we announced the 2016 Ruby Hero Russia Award for contributing to the development of Ruby and open-source technologies related to Ruby (for our compatriots, of course).

All these months you vigorously nominated applicants for the award. It is time to determine the winner!

So the nominees for the Ruby Hero Russia 2016 award are:

Vitaly Kurennov



He created and successfully develops the Russian-language online training school for RoR-developers THINKNETICA. School courses have already passed more than one hundred people.

Anton Davydov



For contributions to Hanami and Sidekiq, popularizing open-source and organizing meetings moscow.rb.

Nikita Shilnikov



For his contribution to the development of dry-rb, rom and popularization of the right approaches to the design and development of object-oriented software for Ruby.

Vladimir Dementiev



Actively involved in the life of the open-source community, the committer in rails, rubocop, author of the AnyCable gem. A good tutor for novice developers.

It is very easy to vote for the one you liked and the most worthy, just go to the rubyheroes.ru website and vote by logging in via GitHub.

We will vote until the announcement of the winner from the scene, so the nominees are allowed to campaign for themselves at the conference. I remind you that the winner will be awarded on the main stage this Saturday.

Ruby, choose your hero! Show your friends and let them choose their hero too!
In the question of the choice of heroes can not stand aside! Urgently everyone fulfill their civic duty on the choice of heroes!

Do you want to see the reward live? Do not forget to buy a ticket to the conference on railsclub.ru .

PS Voting starts with the publication of this article.
