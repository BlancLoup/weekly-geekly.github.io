Monument to old technologies

In Yekaterinburg, as many know, there is a monument to the keyboard. Now we have our own monument in the city of old technologies.















The monument is located in the city of Kurgan in the Central Park of Culture and Leisure. The height of the monument is 4 meters. For the construction 34 system units, 3 monitors, about a dozen keyboards and 3 boxes of ADSL modems were used.
