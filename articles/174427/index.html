WebHostingHub Glyphs - font update to 1446 icons



The recently released WebHostingHub Glyphs icon set has added over 300 new icons.

Icons are available in png format (white and black) and, most importantly, in the form of a font in SVG, EOT and TTF formats. We will be glad to hear any wishes for the further development of the set.
