Every now and then I come across articles, arguments or interviews of various economists, political scientists or just ordinary people about what will happen when our limited natural resources, such as oil, gas, coal, etc., run out. But I have never come across a single article about natural resources, which in our modern life is no less important and even more limited than all natural resources. This resource is our radio frequency spectrum.

After all, with the rapid development of wireless communication technologies, after several decades, the full frequency spectrum of the radio frequency spectrum may occur, and the lack of free radio frequencies will simply hamper the development of the telecommunications technology market.
We all remember the difficulty with which 3G communication appeared in Moscow, how long the frequencies used for the needs of the Ministry of Defense could not be released. Now imagine that for any new technology introduced in the advanced countries of the world, in our country there will simply be no free frequencies. To take away from the frequency of holders of the procedure is not simple and lengthy, and there may simply not be free in the necessary range. Progress does not stand still, new wireless access technologies will appear, and in order for us and you to enjoy these technologies, our valuable and limited resource - the radio frequency spectrum must have adequate attention and attitude.

In conclusion, I would like to congratulate everyone on the wonderful holiday "Day of Radio"! And wish that all the delights of wireless communication bring you only benefit and joy .
