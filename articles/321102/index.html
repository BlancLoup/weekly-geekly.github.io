"Clock Signal Component Issue" or again a massive marriage in Cisco devices

A few years ago, JDima wrote about a marriage at one of the suppliers of memory for Cisco network devices, leading to their early demise.

Now a problem has been discovered with the Clock Signal Component component, which may fail prematurely after 18 months of operation and the device will stop loading.

The problem affected:

  • ASA 5506, 5506W, 5506H, 5508, and 5516
  • ISA3000
  • ISR4331, ISR4321, ISR4351
  • UCS-E120
  • IR809 / IR829
  • NCS5500 Line Cards
  • NCS1K-CNTLR
  • N9K-C9504-FM-E / N9K-C9508-FM-E / N9K-X9732C-EX
  • Meraki MX 84
  • Meraki MS350 Series

If you have a warranty or contract valid on November 16, 2016, you can contact the TAC for a proactive device replacement, priority will be given to devices with more time. Detailed description and FAQ: Clock Signal Component Issue
