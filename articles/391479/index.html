Google Play and AppStore agreed to pre-trial blocking of content

To avoid blocking their sites on the territory of the Russian Federation, Google and Apple have agreed with Roskomnadzor on the mechanism of pre-trial blocking of applications and other content in the Google Play and AppStore directories, Izvestia writes . Thus, the content will be deleted immediately after the complaint of the copyright holder.
