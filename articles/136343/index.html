Select a color palette from a photo

Designer Jessica from the company Seed Design Consultancy is engaged in the selection of original color palettes for sites and offers several methods, including on an arbitrary color, on the subject of topics ( autumn , minerals , nature ). Now she came up with another way that is easy to implement on your own using statistical analysis.

It's very simple: find a photo that you really like, and calculate the main colors in it. Since the photo is initially harmonious, then the selected colors will be combined with each other. And do not need any paid advice.
