Orthodox blogging

In the comments to the previous post of the blog "Orthodox Christianity on the Internet" the idea arose to make a social network of Orthodox subjects.
This is a very interesting idea. Moreover, I-nete has a lot of active blogs with Orthodox themes, and sometimes they are interesting not only to a narrow circle of believers. This is me to the fact that the Orthodox community is quite active in the open spaces of I-neta. But it is not strange, I have never met reviews devoted specifically to the Orthodox blogosphere.
Today I would like to introduce the venerable public with some Orthodox blogs, maybe for someone it will be the discovery of a new planet. Or maybe even a review of the Orthodox blogosphere :)
Orthodox blogs on LiveJournal:
1) community.livejournal.com/pravoslav_ru is the whole community of Orthodoxy_ru bloggers
2) pr-daniil.livejournal.com - this is a popular blog of one of the most active Orthodox priests;
3) dmitrijpakhomov.livejournal.com - this blog is probably one of the most active Orthodox bloggers;
Orthodox blogs on Mail.ru:
4) blogs.mail.ru/mail/podosinkina - this is one of the most active Orthodox blogs on Mail.ru;
5) blogs.mail.ru/community/orthodox is an Orthodox community of blogs on Mail.ru;
Orthodox blogs are even on Yahoo! 360:
6) blog.360.yahoo.com/blog-uZ.B6YIherBS6LmQ7fB5ClKTn4QSSyXN8Q--?cq=1 - for example, this blog of one very active Bulgarian priest.
