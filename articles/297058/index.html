Catch up and overtake: does Russia need to accelerate the development of innovative business

In our country, the topic of innovation development remains topical. Most experts discuss the problems and prospects of this business line. Basically, everyone agrees that innovation must be developed more actively. But the expediency of accelerated development of innovative business in Russia from the point of view of market needs is much less often questioned.

According to a study by the Economist Intelligence Unit , in Russia only 37% of the surveyed top managers cited the introduction of innovative technologies, the creation of new products and services as a priority for the company in the coming years.

Vladimir Ukolov, a professor at the MIRBIS Institute, discusses this topic:
Entrepreneurship is different: one thing when it focuses on trade, services, and quite another when it generates innovation for large-scale industrial production, which allows a technological breakthrough. Or, for example, it serves global corporations by fulfilling their orders, without which it is impossible to maintain competitive advantages and lead in international markets.
