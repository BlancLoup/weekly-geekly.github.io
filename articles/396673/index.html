For example, since the 19th century there is a well-known model of the monetary system Frei Geld. A system designed to accelerate economic processes in a crisis economy. The system is based on the desire of people to get rid of cheapening monetary units that cannot be saved. According to the author, Silvio Gezel, this system removes the accumulative function from the concept of money, which forces people not to save bills in a pillow, removing them from the economy for years and reducing the volume of bills available to other people.
