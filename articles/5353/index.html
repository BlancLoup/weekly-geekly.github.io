Kaspersky Anti-Virus 7.0

Today, the first prototype of Kaspersky Anti-Virus 7.0 was presented at the official forum of Kaspersky Lab (LK).

According to the developers, this version is the first public build of Kaspersky Anti-Virus of the seventh generation and so far includes only the most basic innovations:
- passage of leak-tests (BITStester, Breakout2, CPILSuite (# 3), Osfwbypass, Surfer);
- Passing a keylogger test aklt # 3;
- detection of rootkits during the scan. Verdict "Hidden Objects". Testing took place on instances of Unreal.A, Rustock, EliteKeylogger.

Later, the program functionality will be expanded, incl. A new graphical interface will be added.

Screenshot Kaspersky Anti-Virus 7.0. Discussion of the new version, as well as links for downloading are on the official form of LC.
