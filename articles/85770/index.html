What ASUS is preparing for us

Every year in Hanover, the world's largest international exhibition devoted to information and telecommunication technologies is held - simply CeBIT . This year the event can not be avoided - it is a pity that not everyone has the opportunity to get there;)



Recently, ASUS held a preCeBIT meeting in Moscow, at which it showed some of the new products. Under the cut - what awaits us in the near future. Traffic !

Perhaps the most interesting novelty is the ASUS NX90 laptop. This is really something new in the notebook industry - at least look at the design.



While it is known that some very same acoustics will be installed in SOMETHING, two touchpads for the new interface with the ability to control with two hands will be placed on the sides of the keyboard.





Aluminum case, 18.4 "-inch screen with a resolution of 1920x1080, Intel Core i7, Nvidia GT 336M, up to 12GB of RAM and up to 1280GB on two disks. In general, a powerful machine, and ... and very stylish!



The second surprise will be the G73 gaming laptop, the design elements of which are borrowed from the stealth stealth aircraft. Accordingly, not less productive stuffing.





As for other gaming laptops - the model range will also be updated, including devices with the ability to show three-dimensional images.




Some series of laptops will disappear (for example, UX), but new ones will appear - I will write about this a little later. Different types of surfaces will appear, in particular, bamboo styling will return.

In netbooks there will be new fighters, and the old ones will be updated. The new netbooks will have a slightly larger screen (12 ") and ... oh, yes, matte surfaces !!!

Low-profile video card Bravo 220 for installation in media centers and just small compics. On board fully passive cooling.



The direction of network multimedia players O! Play will also develop - I have already reviewed the first model. On the evolutionary path - the ability to install a drive, USB3.0, WiFi, Internet services and something else.

Despite not the richest experience in the production of network equipment, ASUS managed to quickly enough to gain a large market share and user confidence. And then there will be only more:
