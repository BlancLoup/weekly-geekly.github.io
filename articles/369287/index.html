Andy Weir, author of The Martian, writes a book about a woman on the moon

In an interview with the Huffington Post, the author of the best-selling book Martian, Andy Weir, shared his plans for his new book, which, according to the plans of the publisher, should be released at the end of 2016 or early 2017. The need to participate in the promotion and advertising of "Martian" takes Weir a lot of time, so it is terribly behind the schedule established by the publishing house Random House.

As the writer said, the protagonist of the new book, the name of which is still unknown, will be a woman whose main task will be to study the moon. Since Weir did not have to write on behalf of a woman before, he was a little worried about whether the statement would be as plausible as it was in Martian.
