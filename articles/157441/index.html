Dell XPS 14 Laptop Review

Dell has set the course for a complete update of the lines of laptops. Updated laptop design. Today we will talk about one such update - Dell XPS 14.



A novelty has been made in a style that is already beginning to claim the title of a firm (for a line of ultrabooks). At the same time, the case is very compact and is closer in size to 13-inch laptops. The weight of the laptop is close to 2 kg, but this is due to the presence of a solid aluminum body. Due to this, the strength and scratch resistance of the XPS 14 case will not have any complaints.



The novelty is equipped with a 14 ‚Äùwidescreen TFT-display with HD + resolution, 1600x900 pixels ... The screen coating is glossy. Thanks to this, the picture is good, with vivid and pleasant shades for the eyes. The screen is protected by tempered glass Gorilla Glass, which allows not to be afraid of scratches and scuffs during operation. I liked the display with a good margin of brightness (420 cd / m¬≤), high visual clarity and the lack of noticeable graininess. Built-in frame display webcam with a resolution of 1.3 MP, as well as a microphone.



The keyboard is made on an island type, the keys are separated from each other by a stock of empty space, the key travel is shallow, but comfortable, without clicks. On the keys there are barely noticeable grooves that should help position the fingers. The keyboard does not bend even when strongly pressed.



In addition, it is protected from moisture and has a key backlight, which can be turned off manually or on timeout after a certain period of inactivity on the part of the user.



Touch touchpad responds well to touch throughout its area. There are standard multi-touch gestures that are supported by the operating system.



The novelty uses a productive cost-effective hardware platform from Intel based on cost-effective Ivy Bridge processors. Integrated into the processor, the graphics core, Intel HD Graphics 4000, supports DirectX 11 and provides good performance with low power consumption. Most interestingly, the new product has a discrete Nvidia GeForce GT 630M video adapter with 1 GB of fast GDDR5 video memory. Storage capacity - up to 500 GB. Optionally available solid-state drive up to 512 GB.

The laptop is equipped with all the necessary ports for everyday use. Their location can be found below.



On the left side are the power connector, Ethernet (LAN), HDMI, mini-DisplayPort, 2x USB 3.0.


On the right are the audio, card reader, Kensington.

The Intel Centrino Advanced-N 6235 adapter supports WiFi 802.11a / b / g / n and Bluetooth 4.0.

The claimed autonomy of the novelty reaches 12 hours.

Dell XPS 14 is a good laptop, which absorbed all the advantages of modern laptops of the company. It is worth noting the stated battery life, as well as the good performance of the dedicated Nvidia video card - a rarity in the ultrabook segment.
