Because of the new law, popular torrent trackers of Kazakhstan have stopped working

Now, according to the new amendments to the Criminal Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, illegal use of copyright or related rights objects by organizing, creating Internet resources for further access to the exchange, storage, movement of copies of works or objects of related rights in an electronic digital format, causing significant damage, is punishable by a fine in the amount of from 600 to 800 MCI (from 970,800 tenge to 1,294,400 tenge), either by engaging in public works for a period of 200 to 240 hours, or by restraint of liberty for up to 1 year.

Three departments will immediately monitor the execution of the law - the Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan and the Financial Police.
