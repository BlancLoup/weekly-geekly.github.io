Palm Centro: GSM option

Released in September 2007, the CDMA version of the Palm Centro smartphone was awarded the 4th place in the list of the best gadgets of the past year according to Time magazine. It is a decent smart, and sold in the United States at a price of $ 99, given the conclusion of a two-year contract with the operator (for CDMA is Sprint), I am sure, was on this list by right.

And now, just the other day, Palm has released a GSM version of this device. Available "new" will be in two versions of the color of the body (black and white). The GSM Centro will not differ from the CDMA option. As for the price. Costing in the US (when connected to the AT & T service for two years) a smart will cost the same $ 99.
