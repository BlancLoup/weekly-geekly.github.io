So, Vox.io is a service that allows you to call to mobile numbers and Vox.io accounts directly from the browser (both with and without video). And also make group calls (read, newsgroups), calls to e-mail (below) and send sms. Perhaps, while I was writing, something else appeared - the project is very lively.



On average, the price of calls in Russia is 2 rubles for a subscription and 4 rubles for "just calls". Upon registration, you will be immediately given 10 rubles (25 euro cents), and for each invited person you will be charged another 6 rubles (15 euro cents).

Below, I will talk in more detail about the 3 main Vox.io utilities that ‚Äúhooked‚Äù me.

1. Calls


Actually, the calls themselves. Specify the phone number (with country code) or the name of the user, click on Enter , call. The quality of communication suits me perfectly, the prices are at the level of local operators.



As mentioned above, standard calls (without subscription) are charged at 4 rubles per minute. Calls between users are, of course, free. Extremely simple, convenient and understandable.

All calls come from the number associated with the account, so the recipient of the call does not notice anything unusual. The limit of attached numbers is not voiced (or it simply does not exist).

The potential of using this terribly useful feature is limited only by fantasy. I managed to do it both for technical support and simple communication with friends. I'm happy, I will continue.
