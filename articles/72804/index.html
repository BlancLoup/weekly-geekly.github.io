ServerFault in Russian

Probably many IT specialists are aware of such sites as stackoverflow.com and serverfault.com , but not everyone knows English well, someone is just lazy to translate and there are quite a few of them. I am one of those.

By the will of fate, I work as a sysadmin / programmer, and I lacked such a Russian-language resource as serverfault.com, without hesitation, I decided to make the Russian equivalent of serverfault.ru . I do not pretend to be individual, while everything is in the stage of thought. I need the help of the IT development community, I think this resource is needed not only for me.

The resource is not commercial and it is not planned to become such, while in development on a test hosting, in the near future I will transfer it to M9 hosting.

We need your opinion that you need to take from your foreign ancestor, what is there too much, what functionality would you like to see?
