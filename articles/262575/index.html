Free online school of Android developers. Last days of registration

Three and a half months ago, together with Google, we launched our first free online school of Android developers LearnDroid . And on Friday we release the final test, after passing which you will receive a certificate from Google. The test will be able to pass only those who registered until Thursday July 16, 23:59.

During these 3.5 months, we issued 14 lectures in which lecturers from Kaspersky Lab, Tinkoff Bank, Parallels, JetBrains, Google, e-Legion, ABBYY, Mail.Ru, OK.RU, Aviasales, LinguaLeo revealed the secrets of development under Android. In total, more than 22,000 people from Russia and the CIS registered at the school.



Details about the final test and certificate under the cut.

Final test
The test will consist of 25 questions, 22 of which must be answered correctly in order to receive a certificate. The test is given 2 attempts and 60 minutes (for each attempt). In order to get access to the final test, you need to look at all the lectures and pass tests after them.
You will need to pass the test before the end of August. Lectures will remain freely available.

How to get a certificate
After successfully passing the test, you will receive a certificate that can be downloaded from the site. The first 500 who passed the test, we will issue a paper certificate, which can be picked up in Google Developers' Groups throughout Russia and in St. Petersburg in our office.
