Salaries of IT-specialists in the USA



Mashable published a curious infographic created by the OnwardSearch job search service, which analyzed and ranked the salaries of IT specialists in the United States.

In total, there are 10 positions in the ranking, which were formed according to the most frequently placed vacancies by employers. A cursory analysis shows that the highest chances of finding a job in the United States are for professionals working in the field of the Internet ‚Äî there are three such positions explicitly associated with the web: Web developer, Web designer, Front end developer. Those working in these areas receive an average of $ 82,000, $ 62,000 and $ 85,000, respectively.

The highest salary is in a position called the Information arhitect - its owner earns $ 99,000.

It is curious that graphic designers stand at the end of the top-dozens of vacancies with a salary of $ 47,000, but copywriters are ahead of them quite significantly - they get $ 56,000.
