6 services that make life easier for freelancers



Well, if you still decide to become a freelancer and join the 53 million people around the world who independently build their lives, then this article is for you. If you see advantages in this format of work and are ready to regulate your working day yourself, you will need services to simplify the routine and tedious work, whether it is finding new customers or invoicing. Now, technological progress allows remote workers to earn more, it is easier to find customers and easier to navigate in the tasks. So, if you want to work more productively, turn your attention to these free services for freelancers:

Sighted


This is a website and mobile account management application that allows you to track expenses. The software is designed specifically for freelancers and independent entrepreneurs. The service combines all possible payment options and profit and loss statements, but it is very easy to use. All the necessary functionality can be configured in 5 minutes, and the accounts look as if their creation took hours.

Weebly


If you don't have a website yet, then this service is just for you.
Weebly is a convenient service for creating a simple and easy site without coding skills - just drag and drop, turn on and off the necessary blocks. Here you have collected a large number of topics for creating a website, a wide platform management toolkit, SEO and marketing tools, all you need to successfully sell your product.

Buildfire


In the modern world, each site needs a mobile version, but being only at the beginning of your career path, you may simply not know all the details of its creation. In Buildfire, everyone simplified and made an incredibly simple service for creating a mobile version of the site. This site allows you to synchronize with almost all platforms and has many features, such as integration with social networks in mobile applications, mobile stores and the ability to add your own HTML-code.

Brand24


Monitoring and analyzing references to your brand can be a long and laborious process, which in turn takes a very long time. Brand24 gives you a toolkit that helps you explore your product from all angles and see how loyal the audience is to your brand. You can check the marketing analytics, the influence of people mentioning your brand and conduct a mood analysis.

Dribbble

Social network for freelance designers. Here you can place and demonstrate your work and skills. The community helps to find like-minded people and work on projects with a large team.

Wunderlist


An application that helps to organize your work day. Just add tasks to the list, assign people responsible, or add lists inside the task. The application exists both in the web version and as a mobile application, which will allow you to work from anywhere in the world.
