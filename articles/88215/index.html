Thermaltake BlacX Duet - a cozy place for two HDD

After buying a laptop there was a problem - where to store information? The 320GB Winchester was quickly over, and a pair of abandoned 3.5 HDDs lay at home. First, a docking station for one hard drive was purchased. But the two docking stations on the table are too much. And after that, I accidentally saw a docking station from a well-known manufacturer. So - Thermaltake BlacX Duet.


Thermaltake BlacX Duet Specs



  1. Model: ST0015.
  2. Types of supported drives: 2.5 "and 3.5" SATA HDD.
  3. Weight: 312 grams.
  4. Dimensions: 140x120x170 mm.
  5. Body material: plastic.
  6. Interfaces: SATA -> eSATA and SATA -> USB 2.0.
  7. SATA Compatibility: SATA1 and SATA2 (up to 1.5 Gbit / s and up to 3 Gbit / s, respectively)
  8. Transfer speed: USB 2.0 up to 480 Mbit / s, eSATA up to 3 Gbit / s.
  9. Food: from the power supply unit of 12 V, 3 A.
  10. OS Support: Windows and Mac OS.


Features:



  1. Simultaneous work of 2 hard drives.
  2. Support for all 2.5 ‚Äùand 3.5‚Äù SATA hard drives up to 2 TB (per slot).
  3. The ability to hot swap hard drives.
  4. Special buttons for removing hard drives.
  5. SSD support.
  6. LED indicators for switching on the device and operation of each of the disks.
  7. Includes 2 silicone cases for hard drives (one for 3.5 "and 2.5" HDD).
  8. Also included are power supply, eSATA and USB cables.

Stuffing and equipment.


Pleased with how the device is packaged. All the wires in the bags and in separate cardboard boxes. The docking station itself is rigidly fixed inside the box with the help of foam rubber.

Equipment:

The photograph is not the whole kit, since the docking station is already in use for about two months.


Dock station.


The docking station is made of high quality plastic. Produces a pleasant feeling - after the Chinese "thing" that I had - heaven and earth.


At the station one button - power. On it is an indicator of activity - one for each hard. Indicators blink when accessing a hard disk.

On the reverse side of the docking station are connectors - eSata, USB, power.


Own feelings


I really liked the device. It stands on the same table with a laptop - no vibrations are felt, it makes almost no noise. Although the noise level depends on the hard drives.
