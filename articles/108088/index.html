C-world-by-thread

Hi, habra people!

In the coming days, we are opening the first platform in RuNet for financing creative projects. In the meantime, the last preparatory work is going on, we want to tell you a little about what it is and listen to your opinion.

The C-to-world- site (as it will be called) has a long history, which we will briefly tell you.

Prehistory



We had several projects. We have been looking for money to launch them for a long time, and after going a very long way, we realized that the seed is not working in Russia today. Business incubators, angel investors, investment funds - if something from this somewhere worked in Russia, it is more like an exception to the rule.

And we came up with another way - to create s-miru-po-nitke.ru. Rather, learn from the experience of developed countries and bring it to you. Here everyone can talk about their project and ask for help from people. As in a fairy tale, we help, as much as you can, and from what happens, each young startup manager will be able to do something. And this may be enough for the idea to start living. To live not only in the heads of their creators, but also in the heads of other people, to gain energy, transform and translate into reality.

It is important for us that a young singer, a programmer, an artist, a financier, an oil industry worker, or someone else could start doing what he wanted.

When the idea took shape, we put our affairs aside and set to work. A couple of weeks, and the site is ready to open its doors to the first projects. And we continue to work to make it easier, faster and more pleasant.

And now the story itself!
