Customer review for Twitter for Android!

Good afternoon, Habrovchane. I decided at my leisure to deal with clients for Twitter on Android, the benefit is there to choose from, the Market is still teeming with them. There are both excellent, worthwhile things, and applications are not very good, apparently the policy of moderating Market, or rather, its almost complete absence, affects.

In this article I will consider: Seesmic, Twidroid, Swift, Twicca .

First, I will describe their interface with screenshots, then at the end I will give a comparative table, which will indicate the capabilities of each application.

ATTENTION! VERY MANY PICTURES !!!


Seesmic


The client is wonderful, it was not for nothing that I returned to him twice, and now I continue to use it. Recently, we launched support for several accounts and the ability to remember the position of the last read tweet after downloading a new content.


Login screen. Here you can select a default account.




Accounts Clicking on the account will open his timeline. A long press on the account will open a menu where you can delete the account, go to the timeline, see the profile or write a tweet (compose). By clicking on the Menu button you can add an account or go to settings.




Timeline. Clicking on any post will open it in full screen, which will include, except for the tweet itself, the full name of the follower, the date of sending the record and the client from which the sending was made.
As you can see, users, hashtags and links are highlighted. Links open, hashtags and users - read. You can go to the sender's profile by clicking on it. Also there are buttons for the answer to the record, retweet, direct messages, add to favorites.

A long press on a tweet in the timeline will open a menu where everything is possible, as with the usual click + the ability to share this tweet (you can send it by e-mail, mms, to other Twitter clients or to Facebook).




Replais . Here you can see all the posts in which you were mentioned. The remaining functions are identical with the timeline.




Direct messages. Clicking on a tweet opens the conversation history with the follower. A long press opens a menu where you can delete the correspondence.




Profile. Here you can see all the information about a person, the number of tweets and followers written, the presence of selected messages. You can also fold or block a person, as well as see his location on the map (if he indicated the coordinates in the profile).




Lists . In order to view your lists for reading, you can use your profile or simply press the Menu button and select Lists, and lists of other people look through their profile.




Send me a tweet . At the bottom of the button: the first one adds a photo or video, by the way, you can also shoot directly from the program, the second one - a location, or an address, or a link to maps, the third one - shortens links. The application works fine with the clipboard. If you have several accounts, you can select from which / which ones to write by clicking on @% username% at the top of the screen.




Search. Here you can search for everything, users or view trends. There is also a function to save the search or delete it, and the ability to load the saved searches from the site Twitter.




Settings.
  • Default Account - here is the choice of the primary account.
  • Number of Tweets - the number of displayed tweets in the timeline (from 20 to 150).
  • Display full name - display the full name, not the username.
  • Remember Position - the ability to remember the position of the last read tweet, after downloading a new content.
  • Text size - sets the text size from small to VERY large.
  • Notification - notification settings (vibro, ringtone, flashing green light), update interval and what to update.
  • Photo service - photo resolution service. yFrog, Twitpic or MobyPicture.
  • Video service - the same for video only. yFrog, Twitvid, YouTube. If you have a YouTube account, you can specify it in the settings.
  • URL Shortening - link compression. J.mp, Tinyurl or Bit.ly (if you specify the API or Bit.ly username in the following menus, you can see the statistics on your links on their website)

Twicca



In my opinion, the thing is very successful, it is a pity that so far only beta. At least, I think something worthwhile will come of it. It is worth noting that Twicca is available only under Android OS 1.6+.


Login screen. Well what can I say? Minimalism.




Timeline . If you have a lot of followers, I think this program will be useful to you. Perhaps the screenshot is clear, it can display in the timeline the posts of the people you marked in any color. Here you can do everything: answer, retweet, quote, write a direct message, see the chat history (similar to Show conversetion in Swift), see the user profile, follow the links, add to favorites, send reading lists, set the display color of the user or share a tweet functions, you can assign a default action).
