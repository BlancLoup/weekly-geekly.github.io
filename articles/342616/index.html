Let's argue about Dart and Flutter at the meeting of the Russian-speaking community Dart in St. Petersburg


November 30 in St. Petersburg will be the annual meeting of Dart-developers . What it is?
First, it is an opportunity to learn something new (if you have never heard of Dart). Secondly, this is a reason to have a good time, especially since this time everything will be not quite usual.

What hasn't changed is that Wrike has been writing its product on Dart for 3 years . We changed several frameworks (now we write on the Dart + Angular bundle), reworked the legacy mountains and even tried the Dart on the backend.

About all willing to talk, argue and respond to jokes and trolling.



This time the meeting will be held in the center of the city, in the space of Golitsyn Hall. Getting there will be no problem, and the hall will accommodate up to 200 people.

We were supported by language developers from Google, so wait for a report from the Dart Lang Dev Team about Flutter (take your cell phone colleagues to the meeting) and answers to questions about Dart from the creators of the language. By the way, you can leave your question to the language development team in this form .

The guys from the friendly craft brewery prepared 4 varieties of Wrike + Dart beer specially for the event.
