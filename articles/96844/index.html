In the world of opensource, there are now active debates about the fact that cloud computing can destroy the very concept of open source software . Does the provision of services really require cloud providers to hide sources, as Forbes suggests ?

Recently, I went to talk with the best expert on this issue that I could find - to Martin Mikos .
