4. Competition of projects Unilever Technical Startup Project

When: accepting applications until June 23
Where: online

The international corporation Unilever arranges a competition of projects in order to integrate the most worthy of them into their production cycle. Invited to participate technological startups, the development of which may be useful in the field of industrial automation.

The competition is held in four areas: AR-technology, industrial robotics, automation of internal logistics (automatic vehicles and drones), and digital workflow optimization. The experts will select the finalists who will have the opportunity to develop a prototype and test it on Unilever sites.
