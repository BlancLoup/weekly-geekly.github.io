And about the numbers

However, this is not interesting to me - when we were preparing for release, we looked at the numbers for all applications. Since the stories about attendance, recurrence and other mobile applications in Russia are rarely told (or we are blind and miss everything), even referring to presentations on the performance indicators of the American market, we decided to briefly tell.

- In January 2013 we recorded 1 million 100 thousand installations of mobile applications "Posters". 70% of users who installed the application will use it later. The distribution by platform is: iOS - 77%, Android - 18%, Windows Phone - 5%. The dominance of iOS, however, is largely due to the fact that the application for this platform was launched first of all - in October 2009, for Android - in June 2011, and for Windows Phone - less than a year ago, in May 2012.

- The monthly active audience of applications in January 2013 amounted to 550 thousand unique devices. On average, each user enters the application 5 times a month. The ratio of the number of installations to the active monthly audience is the same for all platforms.

- Over the year, the audience of Afisha mobile apps grew by more than 2 times. The fastest growth app shows for Android. The growth rate of its audience is 1.5 times higher than that of an iOS application.

- Finally, users of mobile applications are increasingly buying tickets with their help. On average, every 4th cinema ticket sold by Afisha was purchased using applications. At the same time, owners of gadgets with iOS buy tickets more actively - about 5 times more often than Android fans. Honestly, I can't imagine why - maybe, in principle, we are used to buying more.
