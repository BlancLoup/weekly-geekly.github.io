Global DevOps Bootcamp 2019 in Moscow

In previous years of the Global DevOps Bootcamp, the guys from MVP talked about developing modern applications using Continuous Delivery and other DevOps practices. This year, June 15, there will be a transition to the next level of learning the principles of DevOps, and concentrating on the following phases:

  • Detection. Getting information about how your system works to inform you and your users about any anomalies.
  • Response. Communication with users and informing about problems that have occurred.
  • Recovery. Troubleshoot and return the system to normal operation.
