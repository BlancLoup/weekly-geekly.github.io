ScribbleBot

Have you ever wanted to draw something that another person could draw, but you don't have the ability to draw well? Then this gadget is for you. ScribbleBot records the movement of the writing tool (for example, a marker), which can then be posted somewhere on the Internet and if someone wants to draw the same thing, he simply downloads the recording of the movement of ScribbleBot and rewrites it on the gadget. Then just press the button on the device and it will reproduce someone else's picture.
Designer: Jon Cumberpatch
