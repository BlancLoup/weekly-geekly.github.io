Personal personal documents will appear in Google personal search results.



Google search service tries to change the user's personal output to make the search even more convenient. The search engine developers reported that personal documents, including emails from Gmail and files from Google Drive, will now appear in the personal search results of a user who is looking for information on a specific request.

For convenience, personal documents will appear in the right side (probably, under the AdWords contextual advertising banner). Of course, only those documents that are relevant to the request submitted by the user will be displayed.

It should be noted that attempts to integrate the general and personal search are conducted by the corporation for a long time. According to the developers, the addition of documents from Google Drive is the result of numerous users who liked the search function in Gmail messages. Currently, a new personalized search can be obtained only by users who work with the English-language search engine interface. Inclusion is made on this page .



It may well be that in the case of positive feedback from users about the new feature, it will be expanded and transferred to all interfaces of the search service, and not just to English.
