How to maintain a Telegram channel with 20,000 subscribers? Interview with the creator of All-in-One Person


In his channel All-in-One Person Konstantin Dokuchaev talks about interesting services and programs that are designed to simplify our lives. Often publishes discounts on all sorts of cool things, or talks about tricks to set up. More on this - in an interview with the author.

How did the All-in-One Person channel appear?


The channel appeared on the basis of my site . It was just autoposting from RSS, first to the bot, and then to the channel.

Like a real 90s oldfag, I consume news via RSS, not social media feeds. Therefore, in the morning I have breakfast and look through the tape during the day, and if I see something suitable for the channel, I prepare a post. 90% of the content I plan is in the morning before work, and posts are published automatically throughout the day.



What content to post, and whether the audience likes 5 posts per day?


The audience with the number of posts can not cope, of course. This is the main feature of my channel and its problem. There are about 5 posts per day, and the coverage of one publication reaches approximately 8,000 views, when the channel has more than 23,000 subscribers. My channel is a directory, and many people read the channel once every few days or less. Content is clearly structured by tags, and you can always find the desired service or program.
