TeamLab: results of 2011, plans for 2012

2011 was a very busy and productive year for the TeamLab project. We were able to strengthen our position in the markets of Europe and the USA, increased the number of users six times and competed with the largest business applications. In our final post, we want to tell what specifically was done and what updates are waiting for users in the next year.




"Five-Year Plan for the Year" or Updates 2011

March - the release of the " Documents " module

May - the release of the mobile version of the "Community"

June - adding functionality for access rights

June - creation of detailed help from the TeamLab center

July - online edition of text documents, spreadsheets and presentations

September - the release of the mobile version of "Projects"

October - calendar release

October - the release of the full mobile version of the "Documents"

November - release of the CRM system

December - translation of portals into Chinese, Polish and Czech

Immediate plans for 2012

At the very beginning of the year, the Documents module will be updated. Major innovations:

- display content across the width of the entire workspace at 1000px;

- add filtering option with search and sorting;

- Adding vector graphics format .svg to create and edit documents;

- access to files by reference, even for a user without registration;

- the possibility of reducing references to the document in the form of teamlab.co/***

You can take a look at the new interface right now:



Then comes the release of the TeamLab API :

It will be a REST API with JSON | XML data formats for access to all TeamLab modules, thanks to which experienced programmers will be able to import / export necessary data, create applications that work with TeamLab, and integrate TeamLab into their services.

2012 is also scheduled to release its own Email client and a mobile version of CRM. But the most unexpected and 100% unique innovation, which can not yet boast of any similar service, we will present in the second quarter. We really want to work ahead of the curve, as they say. So, stay with us, we will pleasantly surprise;)

You can write more "mnogabukv" about what a wonderful team we are, and how we always strive to meet the wishes of users, but you probably already have time for the New Year corporate parties, and I would like more specifics. And it will surely be - next year we will tell about how different divisions of our project work, for example, how our support manages to respond promptly and always to everyone. We also remember about the promise to tell how we attract users outside Russia and the CIS. In the meantime - all a good rest in the holidays and productive work in the 2012m!
