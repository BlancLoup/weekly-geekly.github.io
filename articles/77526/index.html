In rune crouched censorship

All probably already read, that Yota blocks access to some Internet resources. I checked the availability of sites from the federal list of extremist materials and they are also not available to me! Through my provider, Corbin, the site http://www.kavkazcenter.com does not open, but loads through a proxy server. When censorship begins on the Internet, this is the beginning of the end of freedom! The Internet has always been valued for its freedom. Today, they have blocked the site with extremist information, and tomorrow, under the same pretext, they ban a resource that is undesirable for the authorities. I do not mind the fact that the Ministry of Justice is struggling with the spread of extremist information. Only it is necessary not to block access to the site, because the one who needs it is still accessible through a proxy server, and to seek the closure of resources and bring the creators to justice.
