After that, he tested it several times at conferences and in person with the owners of this device. In short about HoloLens


This is a small computer that is worn on the head and does not require any binding to external devices. Before the eyes is a translucent screen, which serves visible objects that create the illusion of the real presence of an object in the world. The screen - waveguide lenses - lenses with a wavelike prismatic structure, which properly refract and send images to our eyes from microdisplays located on the sides. A detailed description of the optics and specifications here .


A strong difference from other augmented / mixed / virtual reality devices is that HoloLens are completely autonomous and their work is not tied to either a smartphone, or a computer, or to a box on a belt . Will ignite or not
