Competition of mobile applications from IMI.VC


Investment company IMI.VC announces a competition of projects in the field of mobile applications. The competition will be held in the framework of Live Mobile! Russian mobile congress, which will be held November 21-22, 2012 at the Digital October center, Moscow.

Details about the participation and stages of the competition on the site livemobilecongress.com .

Three finalists will be awarded prizes - modern mobile devices.

The winner will be given the opportunity to conduct a more detailed presentation at the meeting with the investment company IMI.VC at the end of the congress.

All questions about the conditions of the competition should be sent to : live.mobile@imi.vc .
