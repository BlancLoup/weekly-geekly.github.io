High-capacity 3600mAh HLI-820XL Battery for Nokia Lumia 820

Nokia Lumia 820, as it turns out, is quite voracious, as hinted at by the test data of the regular Nokia Lumia 820 battery (GSM Arena):
1. Talk time: 7:09
2. Web Browsing: 4:24
3. Video playback: 6:25

We created a high-capacity 1800mAh standard-sized battery (suitable for the standard battery compartment), which increases capacity by 9%, but many of our customers let us know that it would be great if we could create a more capacious battery that would increase its capacity more than 2 times.

We are pleased to announce that the 3600 mAh HLI-820XL battery came out, which has a capacity 2.18 times more than the regular BP-5T (1650 mAh) battery for the Nokia Lumia 820.

Included with the battery is the back cover, available in 4 colors: Red, Black, White and Yellow.



Specifications:
Item Number (SKU): HLI-820XL
Battery Type: Li-ION
Voltage: 3.7v
Capacity (mAh): 3600
Back cover: Included
Replaces: BP-5T (1650 mAh)
