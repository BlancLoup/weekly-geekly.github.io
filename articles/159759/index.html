Modern Internet platform for civil initiatives


Today, on November 22, 2012, a new ambitious project in the field of e-democracy and civil initiatives was launched - Yopolis (http://yopolis.ru) .

Yopolis is a new politically neutral online platform with which people who are not indifferent to the problems of their city can find each other, unite to solve these problems and interact with the authorities. The founder and ideologist of the project is the president of Svyaznoy group of companies, Maxim Nogotkov.

Yopolis combines the whole set of functions that will allow every citizen to connect to the life of his city. These could be proposals to the authorities for the development of the district, the urban infrastructure, and the search for and unification of like-minded people. Yopolis gives you the opportunity to prove yourself with real actions and reasonable suggestions, to enlist the support of neighbors, residents of your district, city or even an entire country.

The authorities can use Yopolis in order to make their work more transparent and comprehensible to voters, receive feedback, support for various projects and decisions from the public, hold polls, referendums.

What is Yopolis?

We all want to live better. Live in comfortable homes. To live in beautiful, comfortable and thoughtful cities. Live in a prosperous and friendly country. Create something together and understand each other. Yopolis was created to make these dreams true together. It is designed to help citizens learn to be more conscious and active, use their voice, knowledge and experience to influence the life around them. For example, arrange parking near the house or change the legislation of the country.

With the help of Yopolis, you can quickly and safely organize any action, including to raise money and prove yourself in real business.
