10 most recent and promising startups from Founders Den

Founders Den, a private business club from San Francisco, held a demo day for its most notable startups - the top 10 projects presented themselves to journalists and investors.

It is noteworthy that the startups given below are not just ideas, but really working and earning services, naturally received the support of experienced investors. Their business models have already passed the first test of strength and have proven their worth. If you want to try to create something similar in Russia, treat the components of the business model especially carefully!


So here they are:

Screenleap is a graduate of Y Combinator. The project, whose task is to improve the process of "sharing" the screen. Screenleap has reached 100,000 Shair, and their number is growing at an average of 19% per week.

AnyPerk is another graduate of Y Combinator, offering start-ups and small companies an easy way to increase the number of benefits for employees. The AnyPerks platform combines various discounts and promotions for users. The creators say that 90% of the $ 5 billion discount industry consists of start-ups and small companies, but the market as a whole is not functioning well enough, because existing operators cannot find the right interaction model.
AnyPerks is currently working with small companies and plans to eventually move to work with large clients. At the moment, the platform is used by more than 2000 customers.

DrawChat - as the name implies, is an application for drawing and chat. Given the small size of the screens of mobile devices, the pictures do not look the best (those who have tried it will understand), but they still allow you to visualize the correspondence. BitDeli is a platform for processing and storing data in real time. As conceived by the creators of the project, the system tracks "data that is relevant to your business - including what is important to you personally." Clients write BitDeli TK for analytics and visualization, after which the company takes over the entire data processing process.

The company claims that analytics is currently divided into two different types: there are unique custom products built for large enterprises, and there are global turnkey solutions like Google Analytics. BitDeli meets the needs of those who want something average, but high-quality and inexpensive. To illustrate how easy it is to create a custom dashboard, a product was shown that was created in less than 30 minutes and consisted of less than 100 lines of Python code.

GameFace.me - a platform that combines the game and data from social networks. For example, instead of saving a virtual princess, you can fight for your beloved girlfriend, and instead of zombies you can shoot at your colleagues or friends. The creators launched a concept project ZombieFace, after which they noticed 65% of user outflows the next day and 20% outflows within a month after users began to use the faces of friends instead of zombies.

SocialPandas - converts contacts in social networks into potential customers. Recently, True Ventures invested about $ 1.5 million in them. At the moment, the company received about 1000 requests for access to the working version, and it plans to launch the product in 2013.

Streak is a CRM system for Gmail that allows you to transfer used email to databases, sorting them into various categories. The project is also a graduate of Y Combinator.
