Why so secret: a crypto retreat for the leading representatives of the bitcoin industry will be held in February

You can send your application for participation in a closed crypto retreat (which will be held on February 26-28 in a secret place) to members@satoshiroundtable.org, however, ‚Äúeven if you are an honored worker in the field of cryptotechnology and innovation, your chances receive an invitation are small, "- said on the event website .



The organizers of the retreat apologize in advance to those who do not fall into the lists. The number of invitees is partly classified and strictly limited by the organizers: no more than 50 people will be allowed to participate in the Satoshi round table. Based on the hint of the organizers, the best way to get an invitation is to provide recommendations to registered participants.

What is Satoshi Roundtable?


This is a narrow circle of people consisting of enthusiasts involved in development in the field of blockchain, cryptocurrency, heads of Bitcoin companies, investors, ideologues and early Bitcoin followers from around the world who gather at the Satoshi Round Table for secret negotiations.

This year the event will be held from February 26 to February 28 in a secret place. To date, the list of participants published on the Satoshi roundtable website already includes the names of such industry representatives as Roger Wer ( Bitcoin.com ), Patrick Byrne, head of Overstock, main developer of Bitcoin Foundation, Jeff Garzik, head of BTC China, Bobby Lee, and Dash's creator of cryptocurrency, Evan Duffield, who did not resist the temptation to share a photo of his invitation in the corporate blog, adding that the upcoming meeting would be an excellent opportunity to explain the technological approach of Dash.

Judging by the photo posted in the corporate blog Dash , the round table Satoshi will be held on one of the islands in the western Atlantic Ocean.



This is the second such crypto retreat in the history of Bitcoin. The first was held in 2015. The purpose of the meeting will again be to discuss the future development of the sector.
