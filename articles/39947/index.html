Alternative file managers: CubicExplorer

It just so happens that digging up all sorts of alternatives
(and often more convenient) programs for personal use for some time turned into a kind of hobby.

In my collection there are various tools for various applications. Today I want to elaborate on alternative file managers for all my beloved windows.

And in particular, I want to tell you about CubicExplorer .

It is interesting because with its small size of 2 mb., It can be quite a lot.

List of main features:
  • Tab support
  • Extensive search capabilities
  • Support for skins (skins) and transparency
  • Built-in image viewer
  • Built-in text editor with syntax highlighting
  • Flexible session management
  • Very handy Bookmark and Storage features (DropBox)

From the big advantages, I will immediately note portability (all settings are stored in the program directory in xml files) and the availability of the ability to translate the interface into any language . In particular, I myself translated it into Russian. Here you can pick up the version with my translation. I think that soon my translation will appear on the office too .

Built-in tools are also very convenient. After sitting a couple of days under it was very lack of its functionality. Bookmarks and Storage - this is something! You get into the folders you need with unprecedented speed. And about viewing pictures right in it, I generally keep quiet.

It is very useful to have such a thing on a flash drive and not really strain yourself, for example, at a party. Immediately begin to ask: what do you have this thing so beautiful?

Better to try it yourself once, than looking at pictures.

I have to say: I am not a supporter of explorer-like file managers, but this one seemed to me quite interesting, and, oddly enough, very convenient to use.

Replacing the standard conductor from the megacorporation - I recommend, despite some dampness of the program.
