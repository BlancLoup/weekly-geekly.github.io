Free Software Packaging Training Workshop in Zhytomyr

On August 5, 2013, the first training workshop on Software Packaging starts in Zhytomyr. The program will be trained on the basis of Infopulse with the support of BIONIC University. We invite everyone!



About the course


The program is aimed at the fastest and most effective training in the basics of Software Packaging: 173 training hours within 20 training days. For novice programmers. The classes will be conducted by the main experts of Infopulse in this area. There will be a little theory and a lot of practice. Students will be able to see and try how everything works in real deliveries, as well as learn about the use and development of innovative solutions for automating routine tasks.

The program consists of 3 parts:

路 Microsoft Windows internal device
路 Creation of installation packages using Microsoft Installer (MSI) technology,
路 Creating virtual packages using Microsoft App-V technology.

Requirements for students:

路 Knowledge of English at a level not lower than Intermediate,
路 Basic knowledge and skills in desktop administration,
路 Knowledge of the registry and file system is desirable
路 Successful completion of the qualifying test.

After completing the training, students will receive a BIONIC University certificate and the opportunity to join one of the largest Software Packaging teams in Ukraine (about 100 professionals who have been supplying 30+ customers from Europe and Scandinavia since 2006) - at Infopulse in Zhytomyr and Lugansk.

Enrollment

Registration is open until August 1. Limited number of seats.
