Microsoft Web Security Information Security

On April 9, at 11:00 am Moscow time, a webcast will be held on the results of the sixth global research in the field of information security Microsoft Security Intelligence Report (SIR) ( link to the connection ). Microsoft experts promise to reveal the most current details about the latest software vulnerabilities, exploits and gaps in security systems. They will present the results of research conducted in the field of information security, will consider in detail the dynamics of growth in the level of computer threats and crimes, as well as the number of malicious programs in the second half of 2008.

The leading web caste is Vladimir Mamykin (director of information security at Microsoft in Russia) and Renat Menazhdinov (head of IT and Microsoft Marketing at Microsoft). In addition, independent information security market experts are invited to participate in the conference.

You can register and check the quality of communication today. A test connection runs throughout the day.

The webcast will be carried out using the Microsoft Live Meeting program, so for the connection it is necessary to download software (it works under Windows 98, NT 4.0, 2000 and XP).
