Widgets for maps from 2GIS

Now 2GIS online maps can be embedded on the websites of organizations using a convenient tool - widget designer . In just 3 simple steps, the user receives a widget that indicates the location of all the branches of the company, as well as the most important information on each of them - the address, contact details and working hours.

The integrated 2GIS map allows you to quickly and easily determine where in the city there are branches of the company, which bus stops are nearby. 2GIS maps also show intra-quarter passages - just understand how to shorten the path to the office by car or on foot. A reference part tells about the time of work, methods of payment and all contact information.
