Cloud services are the priority of e-Style Telecom

This year, e-Style Telecom headed for the "cloud" . Since mid-January 2012, the company began implementing projects based on true cloud technologies.


To date, e-Style Telecom already has experience in implementing private, public and hybrid clouds for projects with different loads. This is due to the increasing interest of the company‚Äôs customers in cloud technologies.

The third generation of virtualization technology allows you to create ready-made infrastructure solutions and provide them as a service (IaaS). The client independently manages his cloud and solves issues that the implementation on a virtual hosting requires the participation of a manager.
For example, installing and selecting arbitrary software, creating / copying and deleting virtual machines, distributing server roles (application server, database server, etc.), restoring previously created servers from the image, as well as routing and filtering traffic between networks and much more.

The client receives a complete set of solutions and services already deployed and ready to work.

The cloud environment of e-Style Telecom is deployed on its own technology sites in Moscow and is based on VMware technologies. In the fall of 2011, e-Style Telecom joined the VMware Partner Network.

The generation of cloud-based virtual servers allows you to optimize customer costs in the IT sector and keep business running continuously.

Information on e-Style Telecom cloud services can be clarified in the company's sales department.
