Whether there will be consequences for physical. individuals for using a proxy?


Artem Kozlyuk:
A normal user who uses a VPN or proxy is not in danger. Currently, Russian legislation does not provide for liability for the use of anonymizers and any other traffic tunneling systems. Even the owners of VPN servers are not responsible. The maximum that our legislation allows to do is blocking. A punishment for the owners and even more so - users - is not provided.

Nevertheless, we are taking big steps to ensure that such laws appear in our country. First, they introduced categories of prohibited information, then laws appeared that limit the tools for circumventing censorship. The next logical step on the part of the state may be new laws that introduce penalties for users themselves for attempting to use such tools.

Artyom Gavrichenkov:
I asked this question to a lawyer. At the moment, the legislation does not provide for exactly any sanctions for individuals or legal entities for using methods of circumventing the blocking. There is a restriction only on the provision of such services.
