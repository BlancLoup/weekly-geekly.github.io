Undergraduate Computer Science (AI) / Software Engineering. Hurry to get a grant

University Innopolis announces the start of the competition among students of 3-5 courses of leading universities in Russia and abroad in the field of IT. The winners of the competition - a group of 30-40 students will receive a target grant for training under this program.







Hurry up to apply for our program, the number of places is limited!

Registration for the program

We are ready to answer any questions you may have about the program - ugrad@innopolis.edu.ru

Timing of the program
- Call for applications on the website of Innopolis University: January 31 - March 29, 2014
- Arrival of candidates for competitive selection in Kazan: April 5-6 and April 12-13, 2014 - 2 groups of 30 people each
- Announcement of the admission group (30-40 people) on the basis of competitive selection - April 23, 2014
- Fall semester: August 18 - November 30, 2014
- Winter session: December 8-21, 2014
- Winter Holidays: December 22 - January 11, 2015
- Winter internship: January 12 - February 8, 2015
- Spring semester: February 9 - May 31, 2015 (with a break of 1 week)
- Spring session: June 1 - June 29, 2015
- End of the school year: June 29, 2015

Training
- Training will be held at Innopolis University in Kazan
- Language of study - English
- Admission to the 4 course on a competitive basis. Winners of the competition receive a target grant to study at Innopolis University
- For students there are scholarships and subsidies for rental housing in Kazan
