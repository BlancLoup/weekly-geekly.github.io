Phase Two Calls for Papers of PHDays III Forum Launches

Two and a half months remains until the Positive Hack Days information security forum, which will be held on May 23 and 24 at the Moscow International Trade Center . This year, the forum will be attended by 2000 experts in the field of information security from around the world. If you want to share the results of your research, if you have something to tell the community, then we are waiting for you on PHDays as a speaker.

You can apply for participation in the PHDays III program until the end of the second stage of Call for Papers , which will end on April 14, 2013. After the first stage of Call For Papers, the first participants became known to speak at the forum.
