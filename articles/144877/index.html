Turn on Yandex.Music on iPhone

It used to be difficult with music. Have you found the times when cassettes with records of fashionable groups were passed from hand to hand?



Today all the music is in your phone. Our new application for the iPhone allows you to put your favorite tracks anywhere, anytime, even if there is no computer or internet nearby. Include good songs, walking in the park or waiting in line at the passport office. Listen at home and at work. Now Yandex.Music will solve both the one and the other task.

The mobile application is a logical and long-awaited addition to our web service, which for several years now has been offering to listen to licensed music of good quality from a wide variety of genres and styles. Many people create playlists on the service - by themes, moods, performers, any other criteria - and now all these personal selections will be close to the iPhone or iPod Touch.



If you save a playlist or a separate track to your device, you will not need to connect to the Internet in the future to listen to it. At the same time, the amount of stored music is limited only by the memory of your device.

The service and the application communicate perfectly with each other - if you add a song or playlist to one device, it will appear in the other, you don‚Äôt need to do anything else.

The first two weeks of using the application - for free. To use it constantly, subscribe. It will cost you 199 rubles (if you pay on the web service in your account) or $ 6.99 (if paid through the application) for 30 days. In order not to forget to renew your subscription, there are indicators in the appendix that will warn you about the expiration of the subscription period.

This is the first version of mobile music. We will definitely develop and improve it - and not only for the iPhone, but also for other mobile platforms. Your feedback will be very valuable - please tell us how it all sounds, sings, plays and shakes.
