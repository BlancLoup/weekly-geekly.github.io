CJSC "CNT" presents the Android version of the navigation program "Navitel Navigator" with the support of the free service "Navitel.Probki".

CJSC "CNT" announces the release of the navigation program "Navitel Navigator" for the Android operating system.

Navitel Navigator 3 for Android OS is a unique and accurate navigation system that includes the free Navitel service. Traffic jams, data about traffic police surveillance cameras (SPEEDCAM), three-dimensional models of road junctions and significant buildings of architecture. Navitel Navigator includes the most detailed map of Russia - 45,380 cities and towns, 580 cities and towns with address plans and a road network.
