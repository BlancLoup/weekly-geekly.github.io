YouTrack 5.0: Your Language. Your Agile. Your rules




Where should you start the story about YouTrack 5.0?

YouTrack 5.0 , codenamed 'Gentle', was created to satisfy all your needs. Now YouTrack is also available in Russian!


Salut! Hallo! Hello! Hola!

Let's start with the most expected YouTrack feature: now our bug tracker speaks German, French, Russian and Spanish, and in future releases in many other languages.

To change the language in YouTrack , you need to go to the settings: Administration> Settings (for this you must have administrator rights) and select one of the four available languages ‚Äã‚Äã(in addition to English, of course):


And what's more, if your language is not in the list of available ones, then you can translate YouTrack yourself or even create your own YouTrack using slang. All detailed in the manual how to translate YouTrack UI .


Updated UI: New from the outside and familiar from the inside.

When creating a new version of the bug tracker, we tried to maintain a balance between form and content. Now work on the tasks has become faster, understandable and simple. We tried to rework the screens of working with the task , creating the task and editing the task , spice it up with a new look-and-feel, in order to manage the tasks could be faster and more interesting.

The most important improvements and new features of the fifth YouTrack:

  • Unified design and field layout for all main screens: full screen task , creating and editing task
  • Now field names are always visible.
  • Attaching files has become faster and more fun: you can simply drag and drop files onto the task creation or editing screen
  • Create a URL link to the task template and send it to your customers or colleagues. Or use a template for yourself when creating future tasks.
  • Clear and concise new kind of comments
  • Updated menu icon design
  • New design buttons "Edit" and "Delete"
