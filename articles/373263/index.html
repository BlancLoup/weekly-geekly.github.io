Mpow Thor: Everyday Wired Wireless Headphones

Mpow Thor - seems to be a regular headset, made on a solid four. However, one of its features seemed to me quite practical, and therefore I decided to tell a few words about headphones.



According to its structure, these are closed headphones, which, according to tests, reproduce frequencies from 17-17000 Hz. The main part of the construction is made of plastic.



Overlay ear cushions, apparently polyurethane.



And the sound emitter is covered with fabric lining.



The speaker has the shape of a circle and does not try to repeat the oval of the ear, but the pillow is soft enough so that it does not create discomfort.

There is also a relaxed part in the place of direct contact of the headset with the head: it is made of rubber, under which is a strip of foam rubber.



So, as not all parts of the wire are retracted inside the structure, for reliability, the fabric wire is. This is also done so that it does not grind during the bending of the structure.
