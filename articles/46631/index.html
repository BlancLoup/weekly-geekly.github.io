Here is a sad list of leaders (the number of downloads is indicated in brackets):


1. Spore (1700000);
2. The Sims 2 (1150000);
3. Assassins Creed (10,70000);
4. Crysis (940000);
5. Command & Conquer 3 (860,000);
6. Call of Duty 4 (830,000);
