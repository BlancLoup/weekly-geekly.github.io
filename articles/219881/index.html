Tizen Developer Lab in St. Petersburg

Good afternoon, Habr!

We invite everyone to the Tizen Developer Lab in St. Petersburg. This is a one-day open and free training conducted by the Association FRUCT and Samsung, based on the St. Petersburg National Research University ITMO .



Tizen DevLab will be held on April 22, 2014 (Tuesday) at ITMO St. Petersburg National Research University, Vasilyevsky Island, Birzhevaya Line 14 (this is the very center of the city, 15 minutes walk from Vasileostrovskaya or Sportivnaya) in the conference hall 515. If you have questions about finding a place on the days of events, call 8-962-692-3481 (Sergey).

By participating in DevLab, you will have the opportunity to listen to lectures and master classes by Samsung and FRUCT specialists, as well as to receive individual advice on developing a Tizen system. Each registered training participant will receive cool and useful Tizen souvenirs.

To fully participate in the training, you need to have a laptop with you that meets the minimum system requirements , on which it is strongly recommended to install the appropriate version of the Tizen SDK in advance. We also highly recommend that you familiarize yourself with the SDK before starting the training, this will allow you to better understand the material and increase the chances of winning prizes.

To register at DevLab, fill out the form at the bottom of the page at this link . Limited number of seats!

Tizen DevLab Program
08:30 30 min Registration of participants: the issuance of materials DevLab
09:00 30 min Tizen platform overview
09:30 30 min Tizen SDK Review: Introduction and Review of Using the Tizen SDK
10:00 30 min Tizen HTML5 development
10:30 30 min Coffee break, communication of participants, questions to trainers and the end of the SDK installation
11:00 60 min Tizen Web Runtime and Device APIs Overview: Introduction to HTML5 web runtime and Device APIs
12:00 30 min Tizen # 1 Lab: First Practical Development Experience for Tizen using the SDK
12:30 30 min Tizen Store: opportunities for developers. Place the first application
13:00 30 min Coffee break, communication of participants and individual consultations with trainers
13:30 Tizen DevLab Completion

Coach, employee of the FRUCT Association in St. Petersburg.

Denis Voloshko

Kirill Danilov
