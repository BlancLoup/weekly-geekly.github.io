Weborama now for GTA San Andreas

Having received tons of feedback with a question: ‚ÄúWhy is there no version for GTA SA?‚Äù After the announcement of the WebMoney for GTA3 (a mod that allows you to play random music from the site weborama.ru instead of an MP3 player), we realized our mistake and were quick to correct. Namely, to speed up the process of developing this mod for GTA SA, (although initially we wanted to freeze the development before the release of GTA IV on PC).



Initially, the release of the mod for GTA SA was supposed to take place on Monday last week, but a number of fatal circumstances prevented us.



With GTA SA, everything turned out a bit more complicated for the following reasons:

1. A series of diseases, which poured our technologists in turn, left no time for optional tasks.
2. As it turned out, there is an N-th number of localizations of GTA SA, and to each of which it was necessary to write a patch to display the radio station in the game. (We think that far from all localizations and versions are covered).
3. It took a long time to find a way to replace the tracks during the game, since GTA SA for some reason keeps them all at once.

But, having overcome all these difficulties, we roll out the first beta version of GTARama for GTA SA. The principle of operation does not differ from GTARam For GTA III , except for the following points:

1. For proper operation, the mod executable must be in the same folder as the GTA executable.
2. Before you start GTARam, go to GTA and change the settings of the custom radio. Switch the playback mode to sequential and scan user tracks.

Mod can be downloaded or viewed source (Delphi 7, distributed under the GPL).

NB Since we may not have found all the localizations and versions of GTA SA, we opened the GXT-files receiving point , where you can send the archived text folder from the root of the game.
