Sony Xperia ZR sales start

Wonderful news for lovers of active lifestyle, underwater photography and those who read Habr in the bathroom. We are pleased to announce the start of sales of the secure smartphone Sony Xperia ZR.





It boasts a 4.6-inch Reality Display screen with a resolution of 1280 x 720 and a special anti-reflective coating OptiContrast, a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 Pro quad-core processor, two gigabytes of RAM, a fast 13-megapixel camera, a removable battery, an LTE module and, finally, the highest level protection against dust and moisture conforming to IP55 and IP58 standards. In this way, he will not let you down either in the tropics or in the desert. The phone will be available at the end of June, the recommended price will be 23,990 rubles.
