Free groups on C ++ and web programming in Moscow for high school students!

The Palace of Creativity on Sparrow Hills invites pupils of grades 7-10 to IT courses in the following areas:
  • Programming sites: HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL;
  • Programming in C / C ++.

Education is free.

In the first year of study, the theory is studied from scratch. In the second year, children implement their own projects (programs, websites) under the guidance of a teacher. Successful (efficient (-:) projects are sent in spring to conferences, contests and exhibitions: urban, Russian and international. Examples of projects are on the site.
The classroom is equipped with modern computers and a projector. Classes are, as a rule, students of technical universities.



Classes in all groups are held 2 times a week : on weekdays and on Saturday afternoons, on Sunday morning or afternoon. Load - 4 hours per week + independent work using distance learning (Moodle). The schedule will be known in the second half of September. Classes are from September to May.

Age
It is advisable to register in grades 7-9. Those enrolled in grade 11 will not be able to remain in their second year of study due to graduation But usually we try to take everyone 13-17 years old.

Admission to the group is carried out according to the results of testing for the possession of basic computer skills. Testing is conducted after September 10th.

How to join the group

The first option : you can come to the Palace (see the address below), chat with the staff of the sector and join the group. Time: from August 30 to September 10 on weekdays from 16 to 19 hours, on Saturday and Sunday from 12 to 16 hours. You can also enroll in other groups of the Palace of creativity and department . There are many directions in the Palace: from the English language and financial management to shooting and military training.
The second option : you can fill out the form on the department website . We will contact you.

In the 2012-2013 account year introduced mandatory registration in institutions of additional education. Please read the information on the site.
