Post for Japan

Google could not ignore recent events in Japan and created a dedicated website messagesforjapan.com , on which any Internet user can leave a message with words of support in their own language, which will be automatically translated into Japanese using Google Translate. Also, the writer can mark his location and his message will be attached to a specific location in the form of a pink ball on a world map (Google Maps).



More importantly, the site has requisites for donations to help those affected by the disaster, and anyone can make a small contribution to restoring the Japanese nation.
