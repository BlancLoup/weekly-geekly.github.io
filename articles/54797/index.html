Robo-q

In the world there are a huge variety of ideas for office entertainment, however, it seems to me, very few had the idea to arrange a football match on the coffee table.

Of course, it is not necessary to get on the table with the whole department, especially considering that the average IT score is very well-fed. It is enough to release a team of miniature radio-controlled robots on the table and instruct them to deal with the team from the neighboring department :)

The growth of each robot is only 3.4 centimeters, but in spite of this they can walk, do an excellent job with the execution of commands that the robot owner provides, and also move independently in automatic mode. In addition, each robot is equipped with several IR sensors, which allows the robot to move independently without running into obstacles. After the match, the robots are charged from the radio remote control.



Find this miracle of Japanese technology on the following site:

- www.geekstuff4u.com/robo-q-the-thumb-sized-robot.html
