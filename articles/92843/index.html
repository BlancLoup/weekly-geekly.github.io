Your RSS feed on Yandex, Rambler, Google and LI news

Everyone knows the news processing systems Yandex.News, Rambler News and Google.News, many use these portals to search and read news.

Your website can also be a source for these news sites.

The article will focus not on the technical side of publishing RSS feeds (many CMS already have all the necessary tools), but on how to get the opportunity to export your materials to the largest news sites based on the experience of a company that is not a media outlet.

What does publishing on news sites give? First of all, a significant increase in attendance and an increase in citation by serious online publications. By experience, if your news gets into the TOP - it can give an increase in attendance by several orders of magnitude. Even if your site is not news, but the site news covers the target audience of the users of your services - the benefits of this are certain.

Publishing news on your own website shows potential consumers the seriousness of your attitude to their work, provides additional convenience to visitors, and gives a significant increase in search results.

How to become a partner of Yandex. News

Yandex.News is the first in Russia service of automatic processing and systematization of news, according to official data, attendance is 1-5 million per day, and a significant portion of visitors are purposefully looking for news of a certain subject.

The site displays headlines and news annotations (full texts of the news are involved in the search), links to the full text of the news lead to Yandex.News partner sites.

Registration procedure in the Yandex.News system

1. Read the Media Information.
2. Prepare at least 10 news items. Please note that Yandex.News is very sensitive to the following points:
  • news should not contain advertising of your site and the goods and services you provide;
  • news must be unique, no copy-paste, it may be translated materials;
  • If your site already has an RSS feed, for example, internal site news, select a separate channel for Yandex.News that does not contain this information;
  • It is recommended to publish news at least two or three times a week;
  • news headline should be attractive, for example, instead of ‚ÄúDjango 1.2‚Äù, it‚Äôs better to write ‚ÄúNew features of Django 1.2 are announced‚Äù;
  • exclude from the channel news, older than 8 days, they can not be processed by their robot;
  • the <yandex: full-text> tag should contain the full text of the message;
  • It is necessary to remove information from the message that does not relate directly to the topic of the news, for example, signatures, ‚Äúmore‚Äù, ‚Äúaccording to materials‚Äù, etc .;
  • The source of the message can be specified in the context of the message, for example: ‚ÄúAccording to the official website (link), a new release of Django 1.2 RC1 has been published.‚Äù;
  • information should be purely news, announcements of upcoming events, press releases, as well as news posted by users of the site are not allowed to be published.

3. If the channel is prepared, write to info@news.yandex.ru in free form, be sure to specify the contact person and his position, the name of your publication, which topic your channel is dedicated to, and also provide your Yandex account login (you can use an existing box) .

If everything went well and there are no questions from the employees of Yandex.News, you will get access to partner.news.yandex.ru - the partner interface within 1-2 days, and your news will automatically be retrieved from the RSS feed.

The partner interface contains conversion statistics, a form for your publication, a duplicate search service, and legal documents (Information Cooperation Agreement). Please note that the documents to sign and send by mail is a must.

Using the duplicate search service, you can always check who reaped the benefits of your work on their sites.

If there are problems with the technical side or you are politely refused, eliminate the comments and contact again. Attentive employees work in Yandex (and this applies not only to this service) - and if you have fulfilled all the requirements - your RSS feed will appear with the name news.yandex.ru/smi/ channel_name

News.LiveInternet

LiveInternet is more familiar as an excellent statistics collection service, however there is News . LiveInternet. At the time of writing the article LI. News contain 905 sources.

To add your channel, you must register by entering your email address.
You will receive a login and password for access to the section for partners: news.liveinternet.ru/partner
The section provides the ability to add RSS feeds, view conversion statistics and get the code to place on your LI.News site.
If you have prepared channels for Rambler or Yandex.News, they are perfect for LiveInternet.
The main requirement - the publication of full-text news edited news publication. That is, if your site is not a news resource - your application will be rejected.
