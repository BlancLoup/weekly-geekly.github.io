It is possible that the web version of WhatsApp is being prepared.

In addition to statistics on the number of users and registrations, Durov also suggested that his immediate competitor in the market - WhatsApp messenger - should soon launch the web version, because "... they [WhatsApp tutorial] tried to hire our web developers."
