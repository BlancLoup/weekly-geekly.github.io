Apple and Google will try to predict user behavior

Two technology giants - Apple and Google are now developing a technology that should allow them to anticipate the actions of mobile device users. Perhaps this function will work in September this year, writes "RBC". The new technology will allow to deliver information to smartphone users even before users request such data on their own.

Interestingly, Apple and Google use different methods to develop. Thus, Apple's portable assistant, which will appear in the ninth version of the company's software platform, will offer information analyzing the data that is already stored in the smartphone.
