Android 4.4 for Xperia T2 Ultra

Owners of smartphones Xperia T2 Ultra and T2 Ultra Dual will be happy to know that a firmware update has been released for these models. In addition to general optimization, improving performance and eliminating bugs, as well as innovations specific to Android 4.4, the update adds new customization features to the quick settings panel, integration of Walkman, Album and Movies applications with the Sony Entertainment Network cloud service, as well as the ability to download more than 280 interface design options in the framework of the project Xperia Themes.



In addition, support has been added for Sony's latest wearable electronics, including the SmartBand tracker bracelet.

A similar update for the Xperia E1, M2 and M2 Dual will be released soon. Follow the news!
