Toshiba removed from Nikkei after reporting scandal

The official report of the index of the Tokyo Stock Exchange Nikkei 400 reported the exclusion of shares of Toshiba. The reason - the scandal with the reporting , because of which the resignation had to leave not only the general director of the company, but also top managers .

The Nikkei 400 was launched in 2014 and contains 400 blue chips on the Japanese stock exchange. The most famous Japanese index remains the Nikkei 225, consisting of 225 actively traded companies of the first section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange.
