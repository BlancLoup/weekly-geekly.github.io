Video review ultrabook (tablet) Asus Taichi 21



Asus Taichi 21 is another hybrid of a tablet and an ultrabook, but if for other similar devices, the screen either detaches from the keyboard dock, or turns around on the other side, the creators of Taichi 21 decided to go a more original way. The device is equipped with two FullHD IPS-displays located on both sides of the cover. The first display is matte, and the second is glossy with support for touch input. Both screens can work simultaneously or separately.

The hardware basis of the new products is low-voltage processors Intel Core i5 or i7. The amount of RAM is 4 GB, and for storing data using solid-state drives with a capacity of 128 or 256 GB.

In addition to the laptop itself, the kit also includes a cover, USB-LAN and miniVGA-VGA adapters. Also in some supplies there is a stylus.
