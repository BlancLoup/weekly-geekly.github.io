Motorola Droid - new analog iPhone with OS Android



Soon the Apple iPhone will have another competitor in the face of the Motorola Droid with the Android operating system. The whole anti-iPhone company was deployed with the creation of a video site www.DroidDoes.com . Emphasis is placed on the lack of iPhone flash, removable battery, physical keyboard, etc.

Motorola Droid will combine in itself:
  • 5 megapixel camera (with flash),
  • Google Android 2.0 operating system,
  • Multitasking,
  • Physical keyboard and touch screen.
Coupled with a stylish design, the Motorola brainchild may not be the killer of the iconic iPhone, but the face of the Android OS is fine. (some pictures under the cut)
