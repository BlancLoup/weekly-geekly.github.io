How we conducted the Summer School for High Performance Computing


From 21 to 30 August, the VI Summer School of High Performance Computing in the field of modern biology and medicine was held at the Kazan site of the new Russian IT University Innopolis . The co-organizer was the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology . The event was held with the financial support of the Russian Foundation for Basic Research, grant No. 14-37-10076 mol_g.
We are ready to share the presentation materials of visiting professors and recording from online broadcasts. Details under the cut.

This year, 40 talented children from all over Russia went to the Summer School of High Performance Computing : participants came from Moscow, Kazan, Rostov-on-Don, Nizhny Novgorod, Volgograd, Yekaterinburg, Voronezh, Omsk, Perm, Ufa, Krasnoyarsk, Almetyevsk, Elabugi, Insara, Sovetsk (Tula region). Of the 40 children, 35 received certificates, and 25 of them apply for a certificate of advanced training (deadline for practical projects is September 30).

Guest Lecturer, Sofia Kovalevskaya Prize winner Dmitry Fedosov (Juelich, Germany), specialist in the field of mathematical modeling of blood.

Teaching staff of the VI Summer School: Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Aliev RR Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Professor Lobanov A.I., Ph.D., Associate Professor Karpov V.E., Ph.D., Associate Professor Konkov K.A. , Ph.D. Kolobov A.V.
