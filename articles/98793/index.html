About the "forgetfulness" of web services

Almost every service that has a user registration, at the entrance, makes it possible to remember the user. This is implemented through cookies, for the user - only a modest tick "remember me" in the form of authorization and / or registration. A simple technology designed to simplify the work of users with the service , convenient and already familiar.

However, it is annoying when the service suddenly ‚Äúforgets me‚Äù at a certain moment - the term for which the cookies were installed from the very moment when the user ticked and entered is just coming to an end . Both methods are simple, obvious and fix the problem - I hope to see the implementation more often.
