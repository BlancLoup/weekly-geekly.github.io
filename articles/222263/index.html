The fifth international application development forum Apps4All will be held in Moscow



On May 19, 2014, the Apps4All development community , together with Polylog agency, Samsung Electronics and Sberbank of Russia, will bring together over 1000 representatives of the application ecosystem, specialists from leading IT corporations and leading experts in the field of mobile technologies at the Digital October site.

The fifth anniversary forum of Apps4All will include a conference consisting of four tracks of the business program, an exhibition of high technologies and devices, the final of the Annual Apps4All Developer Contest, as well as a traditional buffet for participants and guests. The event will be held with the support of the Higher School of Business Informatics and the Startup Academy Skolkovo .

The business program of the event includes sections on application promotion, user retention, market analytics and mobile advertising. In the discussion panels, the conference participants, along with experts, will be able to discuss the requirements for the process and the results of project delivery, industry standards, mobile content, as well as future technologies and the venture capital investment market. Market leaders and experts such as Google, Huawei, Intel, Unity, Embarcadero, RedMadRobot, GalsSoft, ZeptoLab, Ostrovok, Aviasales, TNS, AdWatch / Isobar, Video International, Articul Media, AdCamp, AdInch and other companies will present their new products and successful cases.



Representatives of the Google EMEA headquarters (Europe, the Middle East and Africa) from Ireland will talk about the secrets of monetization of applications on the Android platform and hold master classes for interested developers.

Special attention will be paid to ecosystems and developer communities: Dan Romescu (Droidcon, Germany) will talk about the latest trends from the European developer community.

The business program will end with a tallying up and a solemn rewarding of the winners of the Annual contest of the best application developers according to Apps4All. The competition is held in 4 main nominations: "Best Developer-Outsourcer", "Best Developer of Own Products", "Best Game Developer" and "Best Digital Publisher" . Winners will be selected by the Expert Council based on the Apps4All Index.

Samsung Electronics will share the latest developments in the field of Samsung Smart TV.

At the end of the day, there will be a raffle of unusual prizes from partners and sponsors of the event and an evening reception in honor of the anniversary international developer forum Apps4All.

In honor of the anniversary, participation in the V International Apps Developer Forum Apps4All is free by pre-registration * for all developers and ecosystem participants wishing to plunge into the world of applications and technologies.

All information about the event, as well as the registration form - forum.apps4all.ru

* Participation in the V International Forum is free of charge and is held on pre-moderation. Applications are reviewed by the Forum Organizing Committee.
