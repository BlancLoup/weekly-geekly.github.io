Ideas behind open production - individual needs

The phenomenon of open production , along with the phenomenon of open source software (open source), is based on certain social, economic, psychological and technical prerequisites. Previously, the issue of decomposition was considered. After the idea of decomposition, second in importance, it makes sense to name a shift in the structure of demand towards the individualization of products, in order to meet the specific needs of customers.
Again, referring to the socio-economic roots of the phenomenon of the transition from mass production and industrial economy to a post-industrial society and the growth of the information component in each product and, accordingly, to the transition to custom-made individual production. I understand that I quote common truths, but is it necessary to reinforce that point of view with something?
Why is this trend fundamentally important for open production? Obviously, reducing the number of units in a series of products eliminates all the advantages of mass production, respectively, the price of products in the framework of open production systems may even be lower than the price of products manufactured by traditional economic recipes. The following factors serve to reduce costs and increase attractiveness.
- the possibility of placing (selection) of production in close proximity to the buyer
- a high degree of competition among manufacturers, which makes it possible for the buyer to choose the appropriate quality of the product (of course, not lower than the pledged developers), while maintaining a reasonable price
- ability to remove intermediaries
- implementation of exactly those properties of the product that are required
- reducing the cost of the product by eliminating the cost of intellectual property, or choosing an intellectual property service provider to customize the product of their choice
-the possibility of direct participation in the supplement of the project at its discretion (which is especially important for "our brother", the rapid development of the popularity of DIY on Habr√© confirms this once again). Accordingly, the replacement of foreign intellectual property of their own.
Thus, with the observance of standards, the formation of a culture of interaction between carriers of intellectual assets and product manufacturers, a new economic niche is formed.
A prerequisite for practical implementation is an open information system that allows you to implement typical UseCase of all participants.

I will repeat the appeal once again: who is interested in the subject of open production to leave their feedback and coordinates, indicating the scope of practical interests. There is a definition of boundaries and clarification of the essence of a startup, communication with relevant specialists is crucial.
PS I want to note in advance that the goals of monetization of a startup as such are not pursued, otherwise it is not possible to maintain the spirit of the idea itself, which presupposes complete openness.
