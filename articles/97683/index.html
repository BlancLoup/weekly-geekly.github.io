Help in search

Today we begin to openly test the new prompter in the search. This service is the result of the work of the winners of our competition in C. Right now they are interning in Vkontakte.



The developed prompter performs the functions of an individual search on the VK page: it allows you to get quick access to your friends, meetings and all the content.



The system takes into account the probability of a non-switched language, understands transliteration, searches for prefixes:



In addition, search results are generated taking into account those objects of the page that the user most often refers to. When you enter a symbol ignored by the ( ;, $, etc. ) character, you can see a list of the objects you use most often.

And if you select an audio recording from search results, the system will redirect to the appropriate section and automatically start playing.

Now this service is launched for the first million users. If the load tests are successful, then within a few hours we will open it to a wide audience.
