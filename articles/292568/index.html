Attracting targeted traffic with new development objects


In any large city of Russia, new objects under construction constantly appear. Developers are beginning to invest in advertising - this is where you need to catch the benefits for your site.

1. Find new development objects.
2. We collect all the information about these objects.
3. Create landing pages of these objects on your agency website.
4. We promote these pages in search engines.

Since the objects are new and there will be very little information about them, the key requests for promotion will be mostly low-frequency. In these conditions it will be easy to bring the site to the top. Well, then everything will develop according to the scenario: the more developers invest money in advertising, the more popular the object becomes, and you will already be in the TOP - a substantial part of the traffic will go to your website.
