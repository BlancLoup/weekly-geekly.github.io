How to get a DUNS number for free



DUNS number - international identifier of legal entities. In Russia, it is voluntarily mined in one case: when you need to register a developer company in the AppStore.

We‚Äôll tell you how to get this number for free and save 12,240.

Why do I need a number


Apple uses the DUNS number to make sure your company exists. The assignment of numbers deals with the firm D & B, to which Apple has entrusted all the responsibilities for the verification of legal entities.

Until you get the DUNS number, the registration process for an account with a legal entity cannot be continued. Getting a number will take from seven to thirty working days.

You can get paid for five days


In Russia, there is a representative office of D & B, which helps to arrange the DUNS number. They will be pleased to issue a number for 12,240 rubles , you only need to leave a request on the site.

You can arrange for free for 30 days


Spoiler: it took us 13 days.

We decided to conduct an experiment and get a number for one of our customers, who was just about to publish an application from a legal entity. On the official website of D & B and asked if you can get a room for free. The representative of the agency first wanted to transfer us to the Russian-speaking office, but we clarified that Interfax-Dan and Bradstreet offers only paid services. Five minutes later they became the owner of the link for getting the number for free .

Go step by step.

1. Follow the link. We are registered on the site .



2. Accept the terms of the user agreement



3. Click on the blue button to get a number for free



4. We try to find a company in the registry. We did not find the right one - we tried to add it to the database by clicking on the button at the bottom of the page.



The site did not allow to change the region from America to Russia, the field was inactive. Therefore, they wrote to the support service at applecs@dnb.com.

5. On the same day a letter arrived with instructions. We were asked to send information about the company:

Company Name
Street Address (Cannot be a PO Box, but the physical location of this company)
City
State / Province
Zip code
Country
Company Phone Number (Local telephone number that is monitored during business hours)
Contact Name, Email and Phone
An Attached Copy Of The Business Certification For Verification

They answered questions in English and attached a scan of the OGRN. Please note that the names of your company on Apple and D & B should be exactly the same.

6. For two weeks nothing happened, we sent a clarifying letter to D & B. The answer came that the application will be considered for another ten days. And the next day they sent a DUNS number to the mail.
