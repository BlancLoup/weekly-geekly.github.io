100,000,000 Americans are watching YouTube

The audience of all video sites on the Internet continues to grow rapidly. Over the last year in the USA it has increased by one and a half times, and in Russia, probably, by three to five times. The undisputed leader is YouTube , which controls about half of the market.

In October 2009, the number of visitors to YouTube broke another record. In a month, 100 million people came to watch videos (this is a unique monthly audience in the United States, according to comScore). The total monthly audience of all video services amounted to 147 million people or 77% of the total Internet audience.

For a month, Americans watched 13.5 billion videos, including 5.4 billion on Youtube. On average, each accounts for 92 videos per month (9 minutes of video per day).
