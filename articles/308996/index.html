TargetSummit Moscow 2016 in a week



On September 8, the third International Mobile Marketing Conference TargetSummit Moscow 2016 will be held in Moscow.

The program is built around product stories from representatives of applications with a million audience. New attraction channels, growth-hacks, deep analytics and other topics will be raised by the representatives of Snapchat, Shazam, Aviasales, Dubsmash, My.com, Prisma, LinguaLeo, Maps.me, etc.

In parallel threads there will be discussions and workshops on ASO, analytics, User Acqusition, monetization and growth-hacking. And after the business program, all participants will have a party at the Progress Bar.

Program and registration on the official site .

TargetSummit partners: MyTarget, AppMetrika, Affise, PMA Network, Branch, Mobio, Appness, DetaLead and Looksery.
