This machine has simply unlimited possibilities for creating complex parts.



Amazing product of machine tools. In fact, this technique is a strategically important product. Dynamic projection on moving surfaces



Directly space technology: some (two?) Projectors project the image on two planes moving in space. They move not only in different planes, but also change the angle of inclination, rotate. In this image, which we see, is not distorted. A magnificent mathematical apparatus is behind this development, not to mention the technical component: dynamic projectors that track the position of screens in space, changing the direction and size of the beam perfectly synchronously.
