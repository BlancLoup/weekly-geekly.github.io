Nevertheless, the device can be in demand among architects, graphic designers, civil engineers and representatives of a large number of other specialties. The other day, the iFixit service team disassembled the tablet, and assessed its maintainability.

Before looking at what happened as a result, it is worth remembering what the tablet is, its characteristics.

And this is a 7-inch touchscreen display (resolution 1920x1080), NVIDIA Tegra K1 processor with a frequency of 2.3 GHz, 128 GB of internal memory, 4 GB of RAM, two cameras with a viewing angle of 120 and 170 degrees (front and rear cameras, respectively).
