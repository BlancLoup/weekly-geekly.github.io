WHDI and other wireless technologies

Thanks to all who expressed their opinion on the device FLOWMASTER. Be sure to take into account all the wishes of a more detailed information about the work of the gadget. We will talk in order about everything that will cause the greatest interest.

Obviously, the most active statements are related to the cost of the FLOWMASTER models. This product is, indeed, not available to every consumer, but is focused on those who are interested in new products and can afford them. However, we do not give up hope that an increase in the interest and demand for the product will entail a reduction in the cost of components (which neither Chinese nor Russian craftsmen have yet learned to do) - this is about the Israeli Amimon chipset, which is still the only chip maker on WHDI technology. According to all the laws of the genre, the cost of developing a new technology cannot be low.

Now we will briefly talk about WHDI and other technologies known to us that allow wireless signal transmission.

The most famous technology is Wi-Fi 802.11n. The transmission speed is 600 Mbit / s, the transmission distance is up to 50 meters. When transmitting a signal, compression is used, because of what, firstly, there is a delay (from 20 ms), and secondly, rather massive equipment is used. The cost of devices operating on this technology varies from 7 to 17.5 thousand rubles. (in the Russian Federation).

One of the varieties of the above Wi-Fi technology - Intel WiDi. Using a device with an Intel chip, you can transfer content with a delay of up to 0.5 seconds, transfer is carried out only from some laptops that meet certain requirements of Intel, you need special software to work. The cost of the device in Russia is 6-8 thousand rubles.

The next technology is WirelessHD (WiHD), it is based on SiBEAM chips. The technology boasts high data rates with no delay and no compression. The downside is that it transmits up to 15 meters only in the line of sight, moreover, the antenna units are sufficiently massive. The cost of such devices in Russia is also unlikely to be recorded in the benefits, they can be purchased from 15 to 45 thousand rubles.

The WHDI technology is built on the basis of Amimon chips, capable of transmitting a signal of maximum FullHD quality with 3D support through walls at a distance of up to 30 meters. No signal compression, signal delay less than 1 ms. Devices can have a compact size, in addition it is possible to alternately use several transmitters with one receiver. Wireless channel 3 gigabit. Price from 7.5 to 17.5 thousand rubles.
