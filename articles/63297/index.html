Worldwide Real-Time Firefox Downloads

The guys from Mozilla never cease to delight fans of their browser with various "chips" accompanying the release, from year to year.

This time they made a map of the world, in which real-time information about downloading fresh Firefox 3.5 is displayed! Here, admire yourself.

downloadstats.mozilla.com
