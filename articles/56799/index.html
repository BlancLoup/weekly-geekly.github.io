How to make a cover for the disc, if there is no empty box at hand

Not always for a freshly recorded disc there is a free box at hand. What to do?
The simple way out is to print the envelopes yourself from a regular A4 sheet.
Next, you will see how to create an envelope cover for discs in origami style.

Instruction:
  1. Download and install a free program for printing origami envelopes.
    http://www.ronyasoft.com/products/cd-dvd-label-maker/files/ronyasoft-cd-dvd-label-maker.zip
  2. Create and print a blank for origami envelope on a regular A4 sheet.
  3. Collect the envelope:

    1. Flip the printout on the back side and bend the side strips inward
    2. Bend the lower part inwards and insert the disc.
    3. Bend the corners
    4. Bend the upper part
    5. Insert the top of the bottom


All envelope is ready!
