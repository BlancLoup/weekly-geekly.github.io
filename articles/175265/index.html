LG Smart TV Apps Contest - Final and Award

Remember, last year, right in the middle of autumn, we announced the LG Smart TV Apps Contest program? And then, in December, they wrote that the terms of the competition had been revised, and eight weeks were given to those who wanted to add and refine their ideas.

Eight weeks have passed, the competition has moved to an active stage, active tours, testing and voting of the jury and various communities have passed, and, as we promised, on April 5 the winners received prizes.



To be honest, we did not expect such a return from the competition, so time after time to sift out the contestants was not easy. It so happened that the date of the award ceremony coincided with the III International Forum Apps4All held in Moscow, and we decided to combine our event with the forum held. About the winners, prizes and discussions can be read by clicking

Top places


First Place - Top Applications - vTelek

Special application for Smart TV, allowing one-click to send videos from the social network vk.com and other video sequences directly to your TV screen.

Second place - Best apps - Clever TV

The application analyzes the viewer's preferences, viewing history, selects interesting content for the viewer and dynamically forms a queue of video using VoD and Live media transmission media.

Third place - Best applications - Simple Smart IPTV

The application allows you to watch IPTV channels provided to the user by his provider. The base of the application contains almost fifty providers, over three thousand unique sources of video content. It does not require the purchase of the console or rent it from the operator.

The best socially significant application - Smart Job

Quite an unusual application for TV, offering smart TV owners to use a large base of vacancies.

The best educational app - Smart Bookshelf

An even more unusual application, offering to read e-books from the TV screen; tired of reading, the user can switch to the listening mode. Fortunately, all LG models that support Smart TV have excellent picture quality and resolution that is optimal for their diagonal. We hope that users will appreciate the quality of the text display in this application.

The best entertainment app - Football

A game in which the player is asked to hold the ball in the air for as long as possible using the LG Magic Motion remote.

Additional nomination - Best interface solution - Smart IPTV

This application took the third place, but won the hearts of the jury with its fresh look at the interface of such programs.

Ideas Contest - Legendary Adventures in 3D: Carrebian Islands

Again the game, which this time is a fencing simulator with elements of a shooting gallery. As a controller, it is proposed to use the Magic Motion console, a low bow (and an excellent bonus as a prize) to the author for such an interesting approach to our console.
