Kaspersky Lab patented a way to recognize graphic spam

According to Cnews , Kaspersky Lab has patented technology for detecting spam in raster images without the need for machine recognition of graphic images. The decision whether the image contains text is made on the basis of the nature of the location of the probable graphic images of words and lines, as well as the content of the identified images of letters and words.
