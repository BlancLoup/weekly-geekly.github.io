Automobile plants and Kaluga

A small provincial town two hundred kilometers from Moscow with a population of 350 thousand people, obviously, will soon become the capital of the Russian auto industry. Over the past 2 years in the region begins construction of the fourth plant. Three production sites - Volkswagen, Volvo, Vorsino industrial park, which included the Samsung plant - is in the process of completion.
