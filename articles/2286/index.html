Yandex. Cards looking for partners

As part of the service Yandex. Postcards organized a special affiliate program. From now on, any visitor to the portal can offer its original virtual postcard to Internet users.

Listing those who can become a member of the affiliate program, Yandex calls, in particular, designers, photographers, manufacturers of postcards. The service accepts postcards (pictures or photos, static or animated images) with copyright confirmation. In addition, she is ready to consider proposals for the creation of cards to order.

Each partner will receive a personal gallery on Yandex. Postcards with the ability to place a link to your site there. Partner will be paid for the right to use the card.

The Yandex. Postcards service has also changed in content, structure and design. "For the festive season, we present a new face of Yandex. Postcards, and we hope that our visitors will find it pretty," says Anton Popov, head of the company's entertainment projects.
