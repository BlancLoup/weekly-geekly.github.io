The design of the usual things. Fully automated restaurant Baggers`s Nuremberg (Germany)



For those who have not heard of an unusual institution in Nuremberg, it will be interesting to learn about the fully automated restaurant that has opened there. Its visitors browse the menu and make an order using the touchscreens built into the tables. Then he goes to the kitchen, located right under the roof of the building, where the person already manages, not the car. The finished dish is placed in a container and labeled with a color label to indicate the customer's table. For its delivery, there is an original system, reminiscent of the famous ride ‚ÄúRoller coaster‚Äù: plates with food go down the rails, which are connected to each place.

To keep visitors from waiting for the order, the touch tables provided the function of sending SMS messages and e-mail. Payment of the order also occurs without the participation of outsiders. Above each table is a device for reading bank cards.

Automated restaurant - the brainchild of local businessman Michael Mack. He said that the idea to create a cafe without waiters appeared to him for quite a long time, and its embodiment, it must be admitted, is popular with the townspeople. After all, such a system saves money not only for the owner of the restaurant, but also for his clients who do not have to spend money on tips.
