Tesla Motors is developing a robotic charger for their electric vehicles.



Tesla Motors is now working on the implementation of an interesting idea: an automatic charger for their cars, which does not require manual connection. The idea, in fact, is very simple: to charge the car, you just need to drive up to the station, and the charger itself comes out of the charging unit, finds the connection jack and connects to the jack.

In the same way, the charger will be disconnected when the battery of the electric vehicle is replenished. According to Ilona Mask, the charger will be like a metal snake. It is reported that the device will be compatible with all Model S.

However, it is not yet known exactly when such a charger will appear on the market.
