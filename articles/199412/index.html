Anonymous claim to have hacked the mail of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry



Continuing the topic of hackers, the group Anonymous stated that it had hacked into a Ukrainian provider, thanks to which they were able to gain access to letters that went through the domain of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry mfa.gov.ua.

Hackers write that many letters concern the head of Naftogaz of Ukraine Bakulin and his relations with the FSB. Many documents are related to the Ukrainian ministers, the parliament, relations with the UAE and Azerbaijani oil groups, and the shipment of military goods.

To convince the truthfulness of the information presented, hackers posted 24 screenshots .

Hackers offer to get acquainted with the full archive 250 MB (773 files). Link posted on their website. But, be careful, when downloading the archive you may be offered to install plugins for your browser - and who knows if it is not viruses or software to spy on you.

The blog's signature ends with the words from the national anthem of Ukraine "Sche ne vmerla Ukraina!". It is possible that Ukrainian specialists participated in the hacking.
