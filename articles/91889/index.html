Russian section on Enetri.com

A couple of days ago the Enetri beta was published in Russian: en.etri.com

The resource, in its essence, is a platform for collective blogging, and to the habrovans in many ways will seem very familiar. But, apart from the banal deja vu with a lot of different social media, the following original and rather unusual things are available:
  • In addition to IT itself, a sufficiently large number of different thematic sections are supported: Automobiles, Business, Science, Sport, etc.
  • In the rating system, cheating as a class is practically excluded (by special prudent mathematics). No less than equalization at voting.
  • Accordingly, the disease of "minuscience" is also reduced to quite reasonable limits (sic!)
  • No UFO . Instead, the principle of public self-government
  • Cloud implementation (on the Google AppEngine platform)




For everyone who is interested in personal participation in open beta testing - you are welcome, now the website just hosts the Open Doors Days. Completely free probably register without invitations, just Gmail-box or account from Google: http://en.enetri.com/login/google/
