Google will increase the salary of all employees by 10%

According to Business Insider, from January 1, 2011, Google will increase salaries to all its employees by 10%. All employees will also receive a premium of $ 1,000, the tax with which the company assumes. In addition, part of the annual bonuses will be included in the basic part of the salary, that is, it will become permanent.

Considering that Google employs about 20,000 people, the one thousand dollar premium will cost the company $ 20 million. A salary increase will cost Google about a billion a year.

Business Insider writes that these changes suggest that Google is still a great place to work. And everything indicates that the company's financial performance is still very high.

In The Wall Street Journal, however, believe that the whole thing in opposition to competitors, especially Facebook. According to the WSJ, about 10% of current Facebook employees are veterans of Google.
