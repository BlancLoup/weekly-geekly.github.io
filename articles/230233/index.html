Rebranding "Radioelectronic Technologies" will show the world in Farnborough

At the Farnborough 2014 Air Show in 2014 in the UK, Rostech's Radio Electronics Technologies Group is performing for the first time under a new name and a new brand.



Now KRET is the largest Russian developer of modern electronic equipment, systems and means of electronic warfare (EW), civil and military avionics. KRET includes 97 organizations of the military-industrial complex, which employ about 50 thousand specialists. The company's partners are such brands as Thales, Raytheon, General Dynamics, BAE Systems, Lockheed Martin and AirbusDS.

At the same time, the company is profitable: last year KRET's profitability was 8.5% - 6.6 billion rubles with revenues of 77.3 billion rubles - one and a half times higher than in 2012.

The exposition of KRET in Farnborough demonstrates the products of the holding in the areas of on-board radio-electronic equipment, systems and means of electronic warfare and state identification. Against the background of aviation novelties, it is more difficult to capture the attention of the public "entrails", therefore the company presented its products in the form of transparent models of aviation technology, which show where certain avionics components are installed.
