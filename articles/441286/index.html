The Yandex neural network co-wrote a play for viola and orchestra



In the last season of the Simpsons, the main character learned that in order to charm geeks, you need to start a song that was composed by the neural network. Everyone will be fascinated to listen and dance. The Yandex company has almost implemented this idea.

The composer Kuzma Bodrov said that his last work - the Composition for viola and orchestra - was written not by one, but in collaboration with the Yandex network. The neural network, specially trained in classical music, generated a musical series on the basis of which Kuzma could write a melody and develop it into a whole play. The musical premiere of the opus will take place at a gala concert at the closing ceremony of the XII Winter International Art Festival in Sochi.
