OpenGift Developer Weekly Contest



Become an OpenGift online hackathon!


The challenge of the next contest will be open on August 18 at 10:00 GMT, the prize fund of $ 10,000 in platform tokens!

Hackathon rules

1. Task details are announced to all participants simultaneously.
2. On the implementation of the task is given 24 hours
3. Results are published simultaneously as links to an open repository.
4. The best result is selected by a jury vote.
5. The winner receives the entire amount of the prize fund.

Requirements for participants and results

1. Anyone can participate in the hackathon
2. The result must be published in the open repository.
3. The result should have a demonstration in the form of a web interface (for tasks related to the web) or a video recording of the result (for other tasks)

Possible stacks: PHP + JS, Python, Ruby

Subsequent hackathons will be held on Saturdays, the prize fund will be announced separately and may include platform tokens (they can be exchanged for other currencies within the system at a fixed rate) and Fiat.

Registration is required to participate.

You can follow new hackathon announcements in the group .
