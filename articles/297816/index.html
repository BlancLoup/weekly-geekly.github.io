Roskomnadzor will check Microsoft, Samsung and a thousand other companies to comply with the law on the storage of personal data

Roskomnadzor will inspect the storage of personal data of Russians in Russia by Microsoft, Ozon, LaModa, Wildberries, Vkontakte, Ostrovok.ru, Soup Media. In total, over a thousand inspections are scheduled for 2016, Kommersant reports.

Not all companies are prepared for the entry into force of the law on personal data. According to Telecom Exchange and 42Future, on average only 10% of the clients of the twenty largest data center providers in Russia completed the data transfer to the country in order to comply with the law On Personal Data, which entered into force on September 1, 2015.

Under the law in Russia fell more than two and a half million organizations. The Chinese company Alibaba Group and the American Apple were among the first to agree with the new legislation and prepare for the transfer of data, while Facebook initially did not consider it necessary and possible to transfer user data to Russia.
