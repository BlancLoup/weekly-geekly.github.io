Sony KDL-24EX320 TV Review



Why Sony KDL-24EX320?

After repairing the kitchen, I decided to update the TV. Immediately set myself a few mandatory characteristics that must be implemented in the new device.

1) Diagonal: 22 to 26 inches. Of course, I wanted more, but space is limited.

2) Wi-Fi module for playing content from network storage. In his role is the NETGEAR WNR3500L router with the Tomato firmware, which runs the Transmission torrent client, FTP, Samba and DLNA server. After all, it is much nicer at dinner to see a freshly downloaded series of the Theory of the Big Bang than to stick into House 2 interspersed with advertising.

I began to study Yandex.market and was surprised to find that none of the manufacturers, except Sony, had realized the ability to connect a small diagonal to the TV network. All models with network connectivity start at 32 inches. The choice is great here. I also had to choose from three (KDL-22EX320, KDL-24EX320, KDL-26EX320) Sony models. All of them are completely identical in characteristics, the difference is only in the size of the screen. Although not only in size. With some fright in the 26 inch model, the resolution is 1366x768, and in 24 inch 1920x1080. Where is the logic? Because of this, stopped at 24EX320.

Wi-Fi: 802.11n

We are technically savvy people who know that there are several Wi-Fi standards in nature. The most common is b \ g \ n. For some reason, this was not indicated on the product page on Yandex.Market and on the official Sony website . Simply stated that Wi-fi is. And what he is no longer clear. How come? In my opinion on the official website of the manufacturer should be given full technical information. I am writing to Sony support (info@sony.ru) and get the answer:

Good day!
First of all, let me thank you for contacting us and for your interest in SONY products.
We inform you that the built-in wi-fi module on the KDL-24EX320 TV does not support the n standard.

Well, here we are: (2012 is in the yard. My entire home technical zoo (which is not the very first freshness), consisting of Macbook laptops, Asus F8, Sony Vaio Z11, Google Nexus One phone and Samsung Galaxy Tab tablet has Wi-Fi n The router has n only mode of operation for greater performance. I had to accept the fact that it would be necessary to change the mode to Auto.

Analog TV, Network, DLNA, Browser, Skype, YouTube
