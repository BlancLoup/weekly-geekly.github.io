Overview of expansion boards of the communication platform Aastra A400 (A470)

In this article we will talk about a new (relatively) communication platform from the company Aastra - Aastra 400. In particular, we consider what modules (boards) expansion has this solution for the older model in the line - A470.

Aastra 400 consists of three communication servers for servicing companies of different sizes.

A415 / 430


Aastra 415 and Aastra 430 systems can be used in smaller companies and can handle up to 12 and / or 50 employees.

The standard Aastra 430 provides more connections and a total of four instead of two expansion slots.

Both systems have a modular and basically the same design:
  • 1.5U server contains Call Manager, IP gateway and 2/4 expansion slots;
  • built-in: e-Voice Mail, IVR;
  • UCC and vertical market applications are installed on an external server;
  • installation options: 19 ‚Ä≥ rack, on the wall, on the table;
  • All 400 Series Communication Server Platforms ship with the same software.

A470


The standard version of Aastra 470 is designed for 36 users. The capacity of the communication server can be expanded to service up to 256 subscribers.

In addition, the Aastra 470 platform can be equipped with an embedded application server (CPU2). This server provides the ability to effectively integrate innovative communication services into the A400 system.

A470 is:
  • complete communication solution: Call Manager, IP gateway, DHCP server and application server
  • color display, 3 x Gigabit Ethernet, 1 x 100MB, FXS interfaces;
  • built-in Voice Mail, e-Voice Mail, IVR;
  • UCC applications are installed on the embedded server or can be installed on an external server;
  • when configuring more than 36 subscribers, an Expansion Aastra 470 license is required.

The CPU1 board contains the main call control server software.

  6. Analog subscriber ports (FXS)

PRI


PRI - ISDN Primary Rate Interface (30B + D digital trunks).
PRI cards are designed to connect Aastra 470 with public or departmental ISDN networks. Two types of boards: 1PRI, 2PRI



For configurations> 10 B-channels, licensing is required. PRI cards can contain 1 or 2 IP media modules (EIP1).

Protocols:
  • ISDN-DSS1;
  • QSIG.

Compatibility Standards:
  • ETSI ETS 300 011 / A2 ed.1;
  • ETSI TBR 004 / A1 ed.1.


FXO


FXO - Foreign Exchange Office (analog trunks)



There are three options for implementing the FXO card:
  • 16 ports;
  • 8 ports;
  • 4 ports.


Bri


BRI - ISDN Basic Rate Interface (2B + D digital trunks).
BRI cards are designed to connect Aastra 470 with ISDN networks or with ISDN devices. Two types of boards: 4BRI, 8BRI.



8BRI board with 4 configurable ports (from ISDN-T to S); 4 ports are fixed to T-Protocols ISDN S-bus:
  • ETSI;
  • EXTERN S;
  • S-bus short or long (max. 500 m);
  • S-bus point-to-point (max. 1000 m).


DSI


DSI - Digital Station Interface (digital subscriber lines)
DSI boards are used to connect to the Aastra 470 digital subscribers: Aastra 5300, Dialog 4200.



Three types of boards: 8DSI, 16DSI, 32DSI:
  • Line length - up to 1200m;
  • Power 48V;
  • Automatic adjustment of power, depending on the length of the line.


Fxs


FXS - Foreign Exchange Station (analog subscriber lines).
FXS boards are used to connect to the Aastra 470 analog devices:
  • Analog phones, modems, answering machines, G3 faxes, etc. Supported tone and pulse dialing mode;
  • Music sources on hold;
  • Sound alarm systems;
  • Call Generator.




Four types of boards: 4FXS, 8FXS, 16FXS, 32FXS (up to 7 boards max.)
  • Line length - up to 4000 m (√ò 0.6);
  • The first two ports are used to connect remote subscribers.



Fop


The FOP panel is used in Aastra 470 to connect multiple terminals. Port capacity cards with more than 8 ports can be used with FOP.



The FOP panel has 5 modules with 10 RJ45 connectors each, for a total of 40 ports.

16 port cards are connected to the 10xRJ45 unit
  • ports 1 to 8 (white) are cabled directly, ports 9 to 16 (blue) are embroidered with parts


32 port cards are connected to the 8xRJ45 unit
  • all ports 1 to 32 (blue) partially sewn




Patch cables are available in two types: 1m and 2m long.

EIP1


Designed for IP connections with non-IP devices. Two types of EIP1-8, EIP1-32 modules with different VoIP capacity

Functions:
  • VoIP channels G.711, G.729A;
  • Fax over IP (T.38 protocol);
  • SRTP voice encryption;
  • DTMF transmission mode according to RFC2833;
  • DTMF tone generation;
  • Echo cancellation G.168;
  • Detect fax tones.

EIP1 modules are located on the CPU1 card (1), or on the PRI (1 or 2) cards. EIP1 media channels are not licensed.


DSP


SM-DSPX modules are used for various functions, such as encoding / decoding audio signals. DSP modules are located on the CPU1 board. Two types of modules are available: SM-DSPX1 and SM-DSPX2 with 1 or 2 DSP chips.

DSP (firmware) functions are configured using AMS or WebAdmin:
  • DECT, Voice Mail, GSM, VoIP, secure VoIP, FoIP, FXO;
  • VoIP G.711, G.729 with adaptive G.168 echo cancellation.
