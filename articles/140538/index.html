Alternative ending Mass Effect 3 - a dangerous example for the gaming industry



March 6 came the last part of the legendary sci-fi series Mass Effect - according to some estimates, the most anticipated game of 2012. Now many have gone through the game to the end and have been, so to speak, slightly disappointed. What can I say, the current ending of Mass Effect 3 caused a real storm of indignation among the fans.

Therefore, everyone will be pleased to learn that the company Bioware intends to make an alternative ending for Mass Effect 3. The official co-founder of the company Bioware published an official blog Bioware . Ray Muzyka says that they consider ME3 to be their best game, and therefore it is very painful to receive feedback from the most loyal players that the ending does not meet their expectations.

However, the developers decided to listen to the fans. Further quotation:

Executive Producer Casey Hudson and the development team are working hard on several game content options to solve the problem for players wanting to complete their game journey more clearly. Additional information about this will appear in April. We are working hard to preserve the artistic integrity of the original plot and at the same time satisfy the fans' claims. This is an addition to our plans to continue releasing new content for Mass Effect and new individual games, so be sure that your journey in the Mass Effect universe will continue.

At the same time, a conspiracy theory appeared among fans that Bioware made such an ending on purpose to provoke the release and sale of additional content.

Some experts warn that the release of an alternative ending for an additional fee could be a dangerous precedent and a negative example for other game developers.
