Seminar "ERP-systems in the cloud: debriefing"



October 9, DataLine invites IT managers and specialists to an OST data center at a seminar on the characteristics of ERP systems in the cloud.

In a programme:

  • real cases of virtualized ERP systems in retail, e-commerce and industry;
  • features of the database in the cloud on the example of Oracle E-Business Suite, SAP ERP and Microsoft Dynamics AX ;
  • requirements for the virtual infrastructure in which it is planned to locate the ERP system;
  • possible problems and their solutions, tested on their own experience DataLine;
  • answers to all your questions.


After the seminar, all guests will have an excursion around the OST data center and a tasty lunch.

Cases DataLine will be presented by Gregory Atrepiev, Kirill Shadsky will lead the tour.

Beginning at 10.30.

Participation in the seminar is free, but by prior registration

Hurry up, space is limited!

Any questions about the seminar can be asked by mail at event@dtln.ru.
