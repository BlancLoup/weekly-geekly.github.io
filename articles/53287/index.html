A single law enforcement portal has opened in RuNet


A single law enforcement portal was opened in Runet. A law enforcement portal of the Russian Federation opened at www.112.ru on Sunday, in the creation of which the FSB, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Emergencies Ministry, the Federal Migration Service, the Federal Customs Service, the Federal Prosecutor General's Office, and the Ministry of Justice took part.

Users will be able, in particular, to familiarize themselves with the lists of missing persons and persons on a wanted list for involvement in crimes, to find the coordinates of local policemen, download some forms of documents of law enforcement agencies, as well as report offenses.

As of Sunday evening, not all sections of the site were filled. In particular, while the pages about the search for people and objects of art are empty, there is no information about the activities of law enforcement agencies and about the rules of interaction with them. In addition, there are no news in the corresponding section.

The first page of the site contains a warning that unauthorized attempts to distort the information posted on the portal, or suspend its work are considered as opposing the work of law enforcement officers and are prosecuted.

Recall that since the end of 2008 in Russia, the telephone number 112 is the single number of all emergency services. The same numbers are used in some European countries.
