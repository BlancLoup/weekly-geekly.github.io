Video review smartphones HTC One X and HTC One V



HTC One X (HTC Endeavor, HTC Edge) is the flagship smartphone with a quad-core NVIDIA Tegra 3 processor (1.5 GHz), equipped with 1 GB of RAM, with a 4.7-inch display.

HTC One V - the entry-level smartphone, the diagonal of its display - 3.7 inches. The hardware-based smartphone is a single-core 1 GHz processor supported by 512 MB of RAM.

Both smartphones are running the operating system Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) with the user interface Sense 4.0.

The estimated cost of the devices is 28 and 14 thousand rubles, respectively.
