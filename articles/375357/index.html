Car dealers continue to try to ban Tesla from selling electric cars in Georgia, Michigan and Missouri



Companies that sell cars with internal combustion engines, lead their own mini-war with Tesla Motors, trying to ban this company to sell electric cars in some US states. At the same time, the attack method is quite simple - the use of the car sale law In some US states, auto manufacturers are allowed to sell their cars only through dealer networks.

Well, since Tesla has no dealers, the company does not have the right to sell its cars. That is how the sellers of conventional cars were able to suspend sales of Tesla in Michigan. Those states where there are no laws on the need to sell cars through dealers, issue amendments to the laws under the influence of the lobby of car dealers.
