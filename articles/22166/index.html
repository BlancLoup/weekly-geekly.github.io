Google Adwords now takes demographic data into account.

Google Adwords developers in their blog reported on the introduction of a new feature. Creating advertising campaigns will be even more effective, thanks to the introduction of new parameters - demographic data of the audience. Now, when creating an ad, you can specify (or ban) the target group not only by country and region, but also by age and sex of the audience. The new feature will make your advertising campaign to the maximum aimed at the result you want.
For example, you sell women's sports sneakers and your target audience is women aged 18-24. By setting these demographic parameters in the ad settings, you, therefore, not only filter traffic, but also specify your request.

Demographic bidding is currently available to a limited number of AdWords users.
