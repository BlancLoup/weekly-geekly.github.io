Wrist Pip-Boy for Fallout 4 may not be enough for everyone



In November, Bethesda is going to release the next part of Fallout, this is already the fourth part. To attract the attention of fans of the series, the company releases a certain amount of plastic Pip-Boy. Wrist mini-PC will be included in the collection series. Now it turned out that Pip-Boy might not be enough for everyone.

The fact is that Bethesda, before ordering a batch of these "computers", conducted a rather thorough analysis of the needs of potential buyers. As a result, we found out that we need a certain quantity, which was ordered. But after the announcement of the game there was a huge number of new people who wanted to buy a collector's edition. As a result, not everyone will get Pip-Boy.

Of course, on eBay there will be a mass of them, as is usually the case. But the price will be different.

But to order a new batch of wrist computers is no longer possible - the enterprises that carried out the order refused to Bethesda, saying that the production lines were already working at full capacity, and it would not work to increase the batch (meaning, by November 10).
