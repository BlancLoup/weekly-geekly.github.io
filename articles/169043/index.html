Leap Motion. Unpacking and small overview

Instead of the preface


Finally, I waited for the device. The problem was that I ordered it at my home address, and not at work and the courier did not catch me when I was at home. As some of you may have guessed, it will be a question of Leap Motion .



For those who would like to remember or even learn about the capabilities of Leap Motion can read the link or watch a short video.




Next will go photos with a little description of me.


The box itself, as it flew across the oceans.


Inside is a black box with a version.




Inside the black box was the device itself, a cable (USB - microUSB) and a letter from the creators of the device.


Leap Motion is small, about 7.5 cm long and 3 cm wide.


On the one hand there are miniUSB and microUSB connectors perfectly combining both of them. As suggested by Firz , this is still USB 3.0.


The front side is covered with glass, but the back is rubberized, which allows you not to slide on the table and lie steadily.

Impression


In general, the impressions of the device remained ambiguous. The design itself is remarkable, but there are a few nuances:
  • The supplied wire is too hard and you can accidentally move the device by touching it.
  • Very few examples of ready-made applications. When you make a new device and a new market, it makes sense to attend to its support.
  • Not enough instructions and tips. How best to place the device, at what height is best to interact with it, how to interact with it.


I am very supportive of this project and I hope that soon it will go to the masses and we will see new ways of interaction. In the meantime, I will continue to pick the SDK and wait for updates.
