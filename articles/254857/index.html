The story of the unofficial application what3words for BlackBerry



Today, 3 word addresses exist for every place on the planet. We want to do everything so that the use of what3words addressing system is available to everyone, and developers from all over the world who integrate what3words into their applications or are inspired by our idea and create something new using the what3words API help us in this . Our list of partners is constantly growing, but we hardly appreciate the efforts of individual developers who make a product for themselves. Today we want to share one such example.

Oleg Derevenets, a developer from Russia, as he himself admitted, is a big fan of BlackBerry and its products. In the BlackBerry World app store, Oleg's programs are held in good places, and are popular in the United States, Canada, and Western Europe, the main markets for BlackBerry.

In early December of last year, Oleg signed up for the key to the API what3words, and just a week later, specialized websites and forums of BlackBerry smartphone fans told the whole world about the new unofficial 3Words client already available in the app store. We contacted Oleg, and asked him to share his impressions about our API and application download statistics.

The 3Words application replicates the capabilities of the what3words official mobile clients available for iOS and Android, and our team members are happy to use Oleg's application on their BlackBerry smartphones. This is the easiest way to find a location and its address.
