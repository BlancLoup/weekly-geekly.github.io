Nokia XL sales start


On May 15, the sales of the ‚Äúbig‚Äù model on the Nokia X platform (a modernized Android Open Source Project) - the Nokia XL Dual Sim - officially started. The cost of new items is 6,990 rubles , which is 2,000 rubles more than the younger brother.



For this amount, the buyer receives a device with a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 dual-core processor operating at 1 GHz. The amount of RAM is 768 MB (for Nokia X - 512 MB), and the built-in memory is 4 GB and can be expanded with a microSD memory card of up to 32 GB.

The main advantages of the Nokia XL are a five-inch touchscreen IPS-display with WVGA resolution (800 x 480 pixels), a main 5 megapixel camera (1/4 inch sensor, maximum aperture F / 2.8, autofocus) with LED flash, and a front-facing camera with a resolution of 1 , 9 megapixels. For comparison: the Nokia X has a four-inch IPS display with the same resolution, a 3 megapixel main camera without flash and no front camera.



As is clear from the name of the model, the Nokia XL Dual Sim can work with two SIM cards. The smartphone supports data transfer over 3G, Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g / n and Bluetooth 3.0 + HS.

With dimensions of 141.4 x 77.7 x 10.9 mm, the weight of the device is 190 g. The Nokia XL is equipped with a replaceable 2000 mAh battery.
