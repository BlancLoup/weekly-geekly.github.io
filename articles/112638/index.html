Interactive tv

A new TV format with augmented reality was presented this week jointly by junaio and the TV show ‚ÄúGalileo‚Äù. True, not in Russia, but in Germany.



In order to take part in this show, it was necessary to download the free browser of augmented reality junaio and to include the Galileo show at a certain time. Viewers were asked to test their intellectual abilities by passing an online test. During the transfer, questions appeared on the screen with three or four possible answers. The viewer had to bring his smartphone to the TV screen and select the desired answer by clicking on a specific area on the phone screen.
