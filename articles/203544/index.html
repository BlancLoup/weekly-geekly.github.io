How to transfer data from Yandex.Direct and Google AdWords advertising systems to Google Analytics

We have repeatedly written and talked about the enormous possibilities of Google Analytics. By setting up the necessary reports, you can monitor and analyze online and offline campaigns, as well as learn a lot about site visitors and improve interaction with them qualitatively. In the comments on GA past material , we were asked to explain in detail how to link the Google Analytics account with Yandex.Direct and Google AdWords advertising systems.

Transferring data from AdWords


As you know, Google Analytics and AdWords are products of one company, so they are easy to integrate and perfectly complement each other. Linking the accounts of two systems allows you to analyze the actions of visitors on the site, including how they interact with the resource after switching from an advertisement: what rate of refusals, how much time they spent on the site, number of pages viewed, how many visitors came to the site for the first time, and t .d As a result, you will understand how much of the traffic and sales are obtained thanks to AdWords. You will be able to evaluate the effectiveness of your advertisements and more efficiently manage your campaign.

After linking Google Analytics and AdWords accounts, they will be able to exchange data, and you will be able to:
  • Import Google Analytics goals and transactions into AdWords
  • view Google Analytics data about driving traffic to your site in AdWords;
  • Create remarketing lists in Google Analytics and use them in AdWords to target a specific audience.
  • Automatically view AdWords data about clicks and costs next to Google Analytics data about driving traffic to your site.

We transfer data to Google Analytics from Yandex.Direct


If you run an advertising campaign in Yandex.Direct, you can also link it to your Google Analytics account. This will allow you to track the number of clicks on advertisements, analyze the effectiveness of the campaign as a whole. There is an opinion that the data on advertising in Yandex.Direct is better and more accurately represented in Yandex.Metrica . But according to our observations, both analytics systems provide approximately the same data with a slight error.
