What is waiting for Yandex? Or Yandex.Store status

Special features
The store is an alternative to Google Play - the official program store for Android, which is operated by Google. Using the Yandex.Store service, users can download and install free and paid applications on their device.
As of the beginning of 2014, over 100,000 applications are available in Yandex.Store.
