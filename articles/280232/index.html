Microsoft DevCon 2016 - computer vision, SQL Server 2016, Data Science and more



Friends, I am pleased to share with you the announcements of several master classes that await participants on the first day of the DevCon 2016 conference!

The new conference format offers you to plunge not just into the atmosphere of Microsoft technologies, but with the help of our experts to touch on the most topical issues of development for Windows, use of Azure services, features of the new release of SQL Server 2016 and data analytics.

We are pleased to introduce the themes of the master classes and introduce you to the experts:

Computer vision technologies on the Windows platform from Dmitry Andreyev, a technological evangelist from Microsoft, the main Windows ideologist of the track, at the conference this year.

During the master class, students will be told about the breakthroughs achieved by computer science in this area, and using practical examples will demonstrate how to create applications using these technologies. You will learn how to connect the OpenCV-3.0 library to your universal (UWP) Windows 10 application, how to create universal (UWP) applications of augmented reality on markers and much more.

Microsoft and Open Source is a story of friendship from Alexander Belotserkovsky, who is also a technological evangelist of Microsoft Russia, who forms the Azure program and the Tracks community.

Microsoft loves Linux - this famous slide was shown in 2015, but the history of friendship between Microsoft and Open Source began long before that. Together with Mikhail Gavrikov from Rubbles, the conference participants will be able to immerse themselves in machine learning and Data Science .
