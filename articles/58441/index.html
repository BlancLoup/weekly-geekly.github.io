The exhibition - the phenomenon of the Internet Art WWW



I want to invite the habrasoobschestvo to the exhibition of contemporary art, which is devoted to the topic of the Internet, and the vision of contemporary artists. The Internet as a source of creativity, as its theme and as its result.


At the Art WWW exhibition, for the first time, the viewer will be able to observe with his own eyes the complex interweaving of Virtuality and Reality. Artists working in traditional genres depict how the Internet is able to influence our lives. Features Web 2.0 and the global web allow you to create completely new interactive visual images. The Internet is turning into a new language of art.

Virtual to real


The ArtWWW exhibition will feature works by the famous French artist and theorist of interactive arts Fred Forest , cult web journalist Mark America, one of the pioneers of Net Art art Olga Lyalina , as well as interactive projects by young Russian artists: Timur Akhmetov, Denis Ponarin , and Tim Yarzhombek .

Real to virtual


The ArtWWW exhibition will also feature works by artists of traditional art related to the problems of the global web: Gleb Solntsev, Daria Rychkova, Maria Krasnova-Shabayeva, Andrey Scha, Tim Yarzhombek .












Art WWW exhibition will be held from April 24 to May 3 at the Art Banking Club
