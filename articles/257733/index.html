Wolfram Language (Mathematica) Virtual Tutorial

Download textbook in Russian
Download the textbook in Ukrainian
A virtual tutorial is built into the Wolfram Mathematica system documentation, which details the basic principles of the Wolfram Language , and also shows how it can be used in many different areas of knowledge with many examples.

This textbook contains 356 articles , the total amount of which is several thousand printed pages.

I am happy to announce that this textbook has now been translated into Ukrainian and Russian .

The translation of the textbook was done for quite a long time by Andrei Mikhailovich Zelenitsa (employee of the official distributor of Wolfram Research products in Ukraine, Bakotech ).

The Wolfram Language ( Mathematica ) virtual tutorial works directly in Wolfram Mathematica , while all documents, like the Documentation Center online help documents , are fully interactive ‚Äî they allow you to copy any text, calculate the code inside the tutorial, change it, etc.

The textbook is an interactive table of contents containing links to articles. Each article is connected by a collection of links contained in it itself, with other articles that are close to her on the subject.
