May there be innovation!

In this habraposta, I want to inform you about the strange ways in which Internet technologies are developing in our country. There is such a company, Scartel (aka Yota), not so long ago, it announced the introduction of a new communication standard LTE on the Russian market. In the press, there was information about the fact that equipment has already been purchased and brought, that soon all of us will have an even faster and more stable Internet connection than 4G. It only remained to wait for the decision of Roskomnadzor on the designated issue.

The latter made the decision that licenses for LTE should be obtained separately. As I understand it, here begins the bureaucratic line of our narrative, the details of which we will not go into, for it is of little interest.

We better look at what the Big Three operators are doing at this time. Taking advantage of the pause, which arose at Scartel through the fault of officials, they also declare in chorus that they are going to make LTE.
