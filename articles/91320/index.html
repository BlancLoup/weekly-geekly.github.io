Defender Galaxy 4710 - Classic Does Not Die



What could be better than a conventional classic keyboard? That's right, just an unusual classic keyboard. I will tell you about such a copy.



There have been many reviews of super-keyboards at great prices, for example the backlit Razer Lycosa . I will tell about the keyboard from a price range to 1000 rub.

At first glance, Defender Galaxy 4710 is an unremarkable keyboard, just like everyone else. But, I will not reveal all the secrets at once. I'll start from the beginning.

As the theater begins with a hanger, familiarity with the device begins with the box:



Its content is simple and relaxed: keyboard, mini-description and warranty card.
No drivers and extra information. This is good, because With the installation of the keyboard, there will be no problems - Plug & Play (or, as they say with us - Stuck and works). Although on the other hand, this means that the additional buttons are permanently attached and you can only reassign them using third-party utilities. But, the buttons are tied to the most necessary functions, so I think you will not have to redo them.

We get the keyboard out of the box:



Let's pass to technical characteristics:



The appearance of the device is very nice. Gray glossy body and white slightly transparent keys on the same white background, located on the standard layout. The buttons are slightly rough, so the fingers do not slip and it is very convenient to work with the keyboard. All symbols are black, but this does not interfere with perception. Overall very beautiful.

More details about the add. buttons.

There are 15 of them, 3 pieces per block, only 5 blocks:



-Browser
-Mail client
-Conductor

-Back
-Forward
-Stop

-Play / Pause
-Previous
-Next

Hush

And finally:
-My computer
-Calculator
Sleep mode

As I said, the buttons are tied like that by default, but you can redo them with the help of programs that are in bulk on the Internet.

Well, the characteristics learned, looks looked, it's time to go to the goodies.

So, the first thing you liked was the low-profile keys that provide accurate and quiet tactile feedback, the move is average. Quiet keys will be useful if you are not alone in the room and someone knocks the keys.

And the most important thing, in fact, what distinguishes this keyboard from the usual classical keyboard is the soft blue backlight:



The keys are not illuminated one by one, but all at once using the backlight of the transparent panel below them. Therefore, the light is even and, as it were, streaming, which ensures good visibility of the keys at any time of the day.



This light is much more convenient than the key illumination from the inside, and looks good for the eyes.
Turning on and off the backlight prozvoditsya using the El On / Off key, it is also - Scroll Lock.

The Scroll, Caps and Num Lock indicators are located in the upper right side of the keyboard.
