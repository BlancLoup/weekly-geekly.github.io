Education in IT, the growing importance of self-education and specialist self-esteem

Dear Habrasoobschestvo. I decided to raise the question - how do you monitor your vocational education?

On the other hand, with each lecture, you become increasingly aware that this is not enough. That no matter how you laid out, for a normal level you need not a course of lectures for one semester, not 50-60 hours, but much more.
