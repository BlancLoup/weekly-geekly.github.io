Today at 10-00 am the webcast of the WebProfessionals conference in Samara


Today, April 3 at 10-00 in the city of Samara will host a conference WebProfessionals . For those developers who failed to get to the conference itself, we organized a live online broadcast. We invite you to watch the broadcast and leave your opinion about the reports on Twitter with the hash tag #mswebprof .

You can watch the conference on the conference website at http://www.webprofessionals.ru/webconference .

14 reports and a plenary report on the following topics are waiting for visitors and online viewers of the conference:
  • Windows Server 8 and innovations in IIS 8 for web projects;
  • work with projects, frameworks, CMS on ASP.NET/PHP/Python/Ruby/Node.JS on IIS and Windows Azure;
  • new versions of Visual Studio web development tools and ASP.NET platform;
  • stories about practical examples of high-loaded solutions;
  • cloud platform Windows Azure;
  • Special report by Denis Kortunov (Parcsis / Turbomilk) on Metro UI style design.

In addition, at the conference, visitors will be able to communicate with leading experts from Microsoft and invited experts from various companies: Abbyy, 1C-Bitrix, Parcsis / Turbomilk, Inln, and the Drupal community in Russia. A full list of speakers can be found on this page .

Special guest of the conference will be Pranav Rastodzhi - a developer from Redmond, USA, who participates in the development of Microsoft web platform tools. Pranav will make three presentations on the topics: ASP.NET 4.5, Visual Studio 11, WebMatrix 2, Windows Azure and Node.JS. One of the Pranava sessions will be presented in the form of live communication with the audience.
