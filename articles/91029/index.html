Idea:

Collect on one portal all interesting travel offers. This may be an extreme route on the Amazon, and maybe a trip with children to Disneyland, the main thing is that it differs from standard offers - ‚ÄúBodrum, first line, 5 stars, all inclusive‚Äù.



Solved problems of tourists:
  • Search for ideas for an interesting holiday on a single site. Google is not adapted for this, because the search query ‚Äúan interesting journey ‚Äù makes little sense;
  • Access to regional operators and offers from private organizers;
  • Unified interface for receiving payments and delivering documents (especially important for the regions);

Solved problems of travel organizers (guides, small operators):
  • Small and medium-sized operators can not successfully sell their products through ordinary agencies that can only sell mass tours. On HipWay, the operator has direct contact with the consumer;
  • Travel portals work on a content-advertising model - pay for impressions or clicks, clicks on yandex direct cost up to $ 4! per click. In general, online advertising from operators does not pay off. HipWay offers pay-per-sale payment, which completely removes risks from operators;
