We read with our fingers. Texts Braille Alphabet DIY

Have you ever seen a business card suitable for understanding a blind person? And the brochure? A bus schedule at the bus stop?


in title transcript


Although I can not (can not) print brochures, and no one will allow me to stop with such an initiative either, but I wanted to do at least something to help.

I did (or rather, my colleague helped me most actively). I bring to your attention Braille.Ru .

Now it is being finalized, including in terms of layout, which is now executed in a technical form.

What the converter can do now:
1. in the Russian part - convert Cyrillic and Latin, numbers and some symbols;
2. in English, similarly, but without Cyrillic;
3. while the test is going on, the limit of characters is limited only by the capabilities of the virtual server, in the future it will be reduced to 1000 characters, which is more than enough for a business card and a brochure;
4. with the exception of some problems, the braille font is visible in all popular browsers ( there are problems in Windows Opera );

What are the plans:
1. reverse conversion;
2. support more and more languages;
3. simple API.
