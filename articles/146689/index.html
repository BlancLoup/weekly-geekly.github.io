Taxi is waiting for you at taxi.yandex.ru

Last year, we launched the Yandex.Taxi mobile app for iPhone and Android. And today Taxi has also become a web service and is open to anyone who wants to book a taxi in Moscow. All the advantages and possibilities of a mobile application - searching for the nearest cars, choosing a car by the specified parameters, tracking the driver's movement on the map - are also on the Yandex.Taxi website .



Yandex.Taxi works with a variety of taxi services in Moscow - if you want to leave as soon as possible, the order is sent immediately to all partners. In this case, you do not need to call anywhere - you will see on the screen when one of the drivers accepts your order and goes to you.

As a rule, there is always a car nearby, so you will not have to wait long. According to our estimates, the average filing time is 12 minutes.

If you need to leave in an hour or two or even the next day, choose a specific taxi service and fare. As soon as the time of the order comes, the dispatcher will call you and clarify the application.

In order to use the service, log in to Yandex, and when sending the order, specify the mobile number. The service will remember this number and then will offer it automatically. The same applies to the routes - just specify it once, and it will be saved in the history of travel. About how the application works, see our screencast .

And also watch our promo video, which is dedicated to everyone who likes to stay up late with friends.
