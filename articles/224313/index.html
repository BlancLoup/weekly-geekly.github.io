Watch the online broadcast of the DevCon 2014 conference



Dear friends!

On May 28 and 29, join the online broadcast of the largest Microsoft conference in Russia on the development and testing of software DevCon 2014 !

DevCon 2014 is a unique opportunity to learn about where the Microsoft platform is heading, to hear about the first results of the introduction and use of new technologies, as well as to ask questions live by Microsoft experts and development gurus.



The conference will be opened by a two-hour plenary report , within which, according to tradition, a general overview of all company news for developers over the last year since the last conference will be given.

On the first day, together with the plenary report, participants and online viewers of the conference will be able to attend 20 technical sessions , see live broadcasts from the site, interviews with the main speakers and conference participants. In addition, you will have the opportunity to give questions to the speakers online, as well as live, we will show a lot of new devices and tell them all the most interesting!

We are trying to make DevCon for you not only a unique event, but also a fascinating two-day journey into the world of Development and Technology . Therefore, this year DevCon will be the leading voice of the main geek company of our time, known for dubbing popular TV shows - Denis Kolesnikov , aka K-k-courage-Bambay! All two days, Denis will lead our conference, hang out with the participants on the site and create a c-to-c-mood! See live broadcasts!

Follow the news on the site www.msdevcon.ru , communicate in the twitter-tape (#msdevcon) and connect to the broadcast on May 28 at 11:00 (MSK).



Respectfully,
DevCon 2014 conference organizers
