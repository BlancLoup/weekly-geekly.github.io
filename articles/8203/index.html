Beta testing Blog.Ru

Today, May 10, 2007, we are opening a service that we have been developing for a long time - Blog.Ru.

Blog.ru is a new blogging system, which we hope you will like more than what you are used to. This is not a competitor to Habra, of course, but we will definitely try to defeat all the ugly and decrepit things that are in runet (I will not talk about who I mean).

Meanwhile, we believe that, first of all, Blog.ru should be designed for its user, and not its developer. Therefore, before we really open up, we would like to receive feedback and criticism from people who, we think, can help make the service great. We would really like these people to tell you what you think about our work and help make it better.

So, Blog.Ru !
