This was done, in particular, to solve problems with the "attack" on the rating. The term "attack" refers to the publication of a large number of negative reviews in order to lower the rating of the game.

According to the developers, the changes should give each player the opportunity to comment on a particular game and its purchase. In the end, this will lead to the formation of a rating that can tell customers whether they will like the game or not.

Since the introduction of changes Valve, according to representatives of the company, tried to listen to the views of the players, and to developers. Both the first and second aware of the benefits or harm that can cause negative reviews, and in some situations, this tool is still used.

Valve has developed analysis tools that allow you to monitor reviews. After the data and opinions of users were received and studied, in Valve came to the opinion about readiness for new changes.
