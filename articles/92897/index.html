State registration of absolutely all Internet resources announced in Belarus

The resolution of the Council of Ministers in Belarus on May 1 announced the state registration of all Internet resources. Since July 1, the operation of any site in the territory of the country that has not been registered will be considered illegal.

The decisions taken the day before regarding online stores and Internet cafes are no comparison in importance with the announcement of the official state registration of all Internet resources. The only exceptions are those resources that contain state secrets.

As indicated in the Council of Ministers Resolution No. 644, the decision was made to improve the use of the national segment of the global computer network and pursuant to paragraph 5 of sub-paragraph 14.1 of paragraph 14 of the Presidential Decree of February 1, 2010 No. 60 ‚ÄúOn measures to improve the use of the national segment of the Internet‚Äù .

According to this paragraph, the Council of Ministers should, in coordination with the Operational Analytical Center (OAC) under the President, determine the procedure for state registration of information networks, systems and resources of the national Internet segment located in the Republic of Belarus, the list and forms provided by Internet service providers for state registration of documents. Everyone waited for this moment with great fears and now it has arrived.

The registration itself will take place according to the application principle and, it seems, does not imply a refusal. The only reason for which a refusal in registration can be obtained is non-compliance with the requirements established for the form or the content of the application. But just set the form and content of the registration application make you think. In a statement, the owner of the resource will have to specify literally everything, starting with his own name and ending with the technical nuances of the site‚Äôs work.

According to the decree Somina, de facto registration has already begun on May 1 and should end by July 1 - the deadlines are incredibly tight. All owners of resources who want to register and work from July 1 within the framework of the law must submit to the Ministry of Communications and Informatization of Belarus or to its authorized body a statement in accordance with the approved form, in which to indicate a lot of confidential information:

- name of the legal entity or full name of the individual entrepreneur who is the owner of the resource;

- country code (meaning the country in which the domain name is registered);

- Full name of the head;

- the location of the legal entity, the address of the individual entrepreneur, contact phone number, e-mail address;

- information from the Unified State Register of Legal Entities and Individual Entrepreneurs (USR);

- The contract of the owner of the resource with an Internet service provider;

- data on the rights of the legal entity or individual entrepreneur to the specified resources;

- for the Internet site, the following information is specified: description of the resource, used network (IP) addresses, domain names of the resource, registration number of the data processing center hosting the resource, type of hosting used by the resource;

- the ports used by the transport protocols of the open systems interaction model (for the network address space a list of network addresses is indicated), as well as a host of other information.

Such registration applications will be filed not only by site owners, but also by Internet service providers, as well as data center owners. For them, the filing of additional technical information.
