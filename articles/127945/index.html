Toshiba AT200


I have to admit the technological superiority of the iPad over the other tablets, but I still want to have some kind of alternative. Therefore, when it is possible to find something interesting without a bitten apple on the back cover, we immediately want to tell about it!



The Toshiba AT200 tablet is very thin - only 7.7 mm. Therefore, almost all the connectors with which it is equipped have a micro prefix. There is a micro-USB, microSD and micro-HDMI.



In addition to micro connectors, there is, of course, a 3.5 mm audio output and a wide proprietary charging connector.



The entire front panel of the AT200 is glossy. The diagonal of the screen is 10.1 inches, the resolution is 1280 x 800 pixels, and inside it has a TI OMAP 4430 processor with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz, a gigabyte of RAM and up to 64 GB of main memory. The weight of the device is 558 grams.



On the back of the metal cover there was a place for a 5 megapixel camera with a small LED flash.
