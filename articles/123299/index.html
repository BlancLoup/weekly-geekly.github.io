Playing torrents in your browser with Magic Player

Torrent Stream Magic Player is a completely new add-on that allows users to play streams of video and music, through torrents, directly in their browser. Magic Player works with Chrome, Firefox, Opera and supports many popular sites, including The Pirate Bay, isoHunt, BTjunkie and EZTV (rutracker, etc.). This is one of the first solutions for creating true on-demand video directly in the browser, using the BitTorrent protocol.

While millions of people still use BitTorrent every day to download videos, streaming online video sites are becoming increasingly dominant for online entertainment. Almost every video file accessible via BitTorrent can be already found somewhere on the online video sites, as it is paid and completely free.

Although BitTorrent was one of the first technologies to make it very easy to download very large video files, it must now catch up a bit. Many people want the video and music to be on demand, and if BitTorrent wants to remain a major player, then today it should be able to do it.

To this end, in recent years, many torrent clients have begun to add streaming playback to their functionality, and there are also several Internet services that offer similar features.
