Expert comment


Andrei Yunisov , managing partner of Webprofiters Internet marketing agency :


- From the point of view of the interface, the new project is very similar to the recently launched sapato.ru. From the point of view of classical marketing, the store is made quite well - the general advantages of buying are well communicated.

It is not entirely clear what is unique and whether the store is going to compete with something other than products and prices with the same sapato.ru. There are representational problems with non-food benefits. There are 2 catalogs implemented on the site, which speaks about the reasonableness of the site structure for the search logic. However, the design of robots.txt suffers, which can cause duplication of cards and general problems of internal project optimization and, as a result, problems with the recruitment of SEO traffic to the site. The interface of the product cards is clear and understandable, but the basket interface suffers - a lot of distracting elements, which generates a ‚Äúspreading‚Äù of clicks. The interface of the cart is fairly standard and is subject to the study of user behavior in it. The project is actively using SMM to recruit traffic, but it is clearly not up to SEO in my opinion. This is strange, given that SEO and contextual advertising serve created demand, and SMM more forms it. However, aspects of the project‚Äôs advertising strategy are unknown.
