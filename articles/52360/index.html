How is the rating formed?

The rating is based on the assessments of the expert council which includes full-time analysts of StartupIndex and independent experts with knowledge in relevant fields. Among them:
  • Maxim Spiridonov , Head of the Production Center " Roiber ";
  • Maxim Krainov , entrepreneur and independent consultant;
  • Roman Mandrik , general director of the agency " KSAN ";
  • Dmitry Demenchuk, game producer.
How can a startup get a rating?
  • Register the project on the website startupindex.ru ;
  • Attach additional materials: presentation, business plan, research results, etc. (these documents will be available only to members of the expert council and moderator of the site);
  • Fill in the startup questionnaire;
  • Wait for the letter from the StartupIndex employee confirming the start of the examination process.
In case the final rating turns out to be BBB or higher, you will be offered to host the project in the next issue of the StartupIndex Review. And this is just one of the reasons to get a rating.
