All Wordpress San Francisco office staff started working remotely


Image of Automattic website

WordPress developer Automattic will no longer spend money on maintaining a San Francisco office, reports Business Insider.

The fact is that most staff members prefer to work at home, in a cafe or anywhere, except for the office itself. Therefore, the general director of the company, Matt M√ºhlenweg, made a decision on such cost optimization.

The Automattic company is ready to pay employees even air tickets if the need arises to hold face-to-face meetings. Moreover, the company also conducts all stages of the interview online.
