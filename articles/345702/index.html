The story of the emergence of financial accounting in my life

I was born in a simple Soviet family: my mother is a physical education teacher, my father is a military civil engineer. He graduated from the Pedagogical University, worked as a programmer. The stash was not in the bank, but simply in the table, because then I had no experience with plastic cards. Sometimes, getting shallowly into the money I put away, I made myself a notch about the new volume of the nest egg. But after a couple of months, I realized that I did not take into account more than half of the notches and the amount there was two times less than it should have been according to my calculations. The first steps

I decided to keep track of expenses. The goal was one - to understand where the money goes. There was no higher goal. It was a difficult task to convince of the need for accounting for my wife. Since we accustomed to make expenses right away, we needed an application on the android, since my wife and I had a phone on it. Another requirement was to manage the list of items of expenditure. I started setting up the application, introduced expenses, set up synchronization between my phone and my wife, set up the currency. And here the first problem was waiting for me, I set up the currency, everything is in rubles, but dollars are set by default. I reconfigured everything, forwarded, overloaded, again dollars. After the third attempt, I decided that dollars are rubles and began to calmly keep records.

In the process of using the initial idea to customize the reading of SMS for automatic generation of records, I decided to refuse. Firstly, because it needs to be configured, but I did not find an easy and convenient way to do this in the application. And secondly, because it is impossible to automatically determine the type of consumption by SMS. For those who say that it is possible - determine to which category of consumption you will take SMS from the hypermarket, if you have both products, alcohol, and life, and possibly clothes, etc. in the check. Immediately came the idea that for one SMS you can make a sign of expenses by category, but no application, including the one I use, has this functionality from the word at all.

Dual costs

In the process of keeping records, ambiguous situations appeared. Let's look at them in more detail. We have a friend Nikita, to whom we gladly go to the country. Bath, barbecues, alcohol, beshbarmak, etc. All this costs, which on the one hand can be attributed to the profile of products, alcohol, household goods, the same gasoline on the car, and on the other hand it is entertainment, recreation. Entering the flow into two categories at once is nonsense, since this mechanism, if it divides the amount in half, does not reflect reality, and if it writes it down into both categories, it simply doubles the amount. As a result, for ourselves we decided to write everything down according to the articles, as it should be, that is, food, alcohol, gasoline, etc. And ideally, I decided for myself that a tag system is needed, when you can mark the expense, build a report, and find out how much money was spent on a trip to the cottage to our friend Nikita.

By the way, travel expenses can be added to this problem, but we decided for ourselves that all expenses during a journey, regardless of destination, are products, a service, we fall into one category - travel.

After a couple of months, we already had some statistics. But winter was approaching and it was understood that our statistics are incomplete - there are few expenses in an important category - gifts. And we decided to wait until the end of winter with the collection of statistics.

Statistics

The winter passed and collecting data for the first analysis, we found out that the main expenditures are 50%. Expenses in small records gain another 50%. We thought that somewhere a mistake, that part of the money is lost and something else, but the fact that small categories are recruited by 50% was a shock for us. And then it became clear that the initial habit of recording all expenses helped us a lot. Consumption, even at 10 rubles, we make and this allows us to see the whole picture.

In the spring we bought a dog and again we added an item of expenses. Again, we need to collect statistics to understand how our expenses have changed. To buy a dog, we were a bit shaken up in our expenses and I got the idea to keep a record of income. Initially, the idea was to choose the perfect moment, for example from the beginning of the year. But this is all deception - there is no perfect moment. Therefore, you just need to take and start doing.

Planning

By the end of the year, it was understood that I had on hand our full annual statistics. And you can try to predict the cost of next year. Resolved - done. He took annual expenses, added 10% and took it as a plan for the year. No breakdown by month, just a total. In the application, I did not find this functionality in the free version, so I had to duplicate the account and start the file in Google disk, with total monthly indicators.

I adjusted the plan for the year approximately every month, because there appeared unaccounted moments, new projects. But this did not upset me, since I had already seen the next step - planning major purchases. That's what I did. I learned about what my darling wanted, added what I wanted, counted and was upset. For a part of the costs it was not possible to fulfill them, it was necessary to decide what we would do and what would not. Taking the data started a difficult conversation with his wife. They argued, even quarreled a little, but found a way out. If there is a goal, then for this purpose you can save.

Experiment

To ensure purchases, it was decided to cut the budget by 30% for two months. We stopped buying excess food, began to look more at the products, read the composition, compare analogues and somewhere later switched to a cheaper brand. As a result, we met the new budget. But I will say at once, if it were not for the purpose for which we were making these savings, then nothing would have happened. That is, if you need to save some money, then immediately set a goal - why. Saving for the sake of saving is not a goal, it does not work. And if it works, then you have the wrong budget.

findings

First, we came to the conclusion that clothes should be bought expensive, because on the horizon in a couple of years, it is cheaper, that is, it is more economical. Same with shoes. I have a problem with her at all - with height 185 cm, weight 114 kg and size 48 I have shoes for one season only. In order to save, they decided to switch to an expensive brand. I will say in advance - the idea with expensive clothes and shoes fully justified itself, and allowed saving up to 20-40 thousand per person per year.
Secondly, as I wrote above, we decided to conduct an experiment in order to optimize costs. And according to the results, they made a decision that we will sometimes arrange such experiments, as they allow shaking up expenses.
Third, make all budget decisions together, since you are a family and live on these incomes and expenses together.
Fourth, subcategories are required to account for expenses. Take for example the same costs for a car: maintenance, taxes, fines, tire, OSAGO, gasoline, etc. Having collected all this in one article - you see one amount and you do not understand anything. Looking at her becomes scary. And having broken everything into subcategories, you understand why the consumption varies from month to month. It is also easier to plan for subcategories. If you have everything without force majeure, then twice a year tire fitting, once OSAGO, one or two THAT.
Fifthly, it is necessary to plan for a year, but with monthly details. The reason for this step is well understood from the previous paragraph.
Sixth, lay down additional expenses over and above the plan in order to be more prepared for unplanned situations. And if everything goes according to plan, then you can send the released money to a deposit, or distribute it during the adjustment to other articles.
Seventh, it is sometimes morally difficult because everything is distributed among you and cannot be spent on something else. In this case, take and spend money on anything. Have fun, get distracted. But of course then write down these costs. Yes, they will be unscheduled, but very necessary. And the money for this expense, we laid in the previous paragraph.

What else happened in our life

My wife and I returned to the category of training. We looked at how much we drank, we ate and became ashamed. We are both educators and have stopped studying. Now we have a budget and the category of education is already larger than entertainment and is close in scale to a cafe.
We began to consider debts. Yes, we treat those people who sometimes lend. And thanks to accounting, I can understand, can I lend, may I lose this money.
We keep a shopping list for every trip to the store and a list of large purchases.

I perfectly understand that it is necessary to live without reducing expenses, but increasing income. But if an increase requires investment, then the best way to find this investment is to look into your budget and he will give you the answers.

Now my wife and I are setting goals for the year ahead. So far there are few of them, and they are not ambitious, but we are only at the beginning of the road and I know that we will succeed.
