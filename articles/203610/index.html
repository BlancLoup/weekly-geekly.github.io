Ideal site - TK as the basis of the site, built on the basis of competent software solutions



Imagine that you, as the owner of a certain company, are ordering the company's website from a developer‚Äôs studio. The situation is quite standard and developing under the standard scenario.

You need:

CMS, written so that even the new office manager can easily understand how and what to do to add a section or article.

The design is such that even from a mobile phone or tablet, the user can see everything that the company offers him. Attractive user-friendly design.

The user's account, allowing you to see what he was already doing on the site, where he went and what he was interested in. Standard solution for a standard company website.



But if you think about it, then why not connect to the creation of the site at least at the level of writing TK Marketing. After all, he is engaged in the development of the project. Marketers create activity and turn the site into a site that leads customers. Agree, it sounds reasonable. Let us have a marketer .

SEOshnik here, too, would not interfere. He has to work out the project structure. Make it a reference for the search engine. To register meta tags, to work out the system of cross internal links.

So you gather a team and turn your website into a unique project that has only an indirect relation to the standard you have planned. Let's try to imagine the TK, written as a result of such TeamWork.



So, what would she want?


First, the fields for meta tags. They should be obligatory for each material, section and in general any element containing content.
Then he will clearly intervene in the work of the designer, analyzing a couple of dozen top sites, thus highlighting the areas of attention of users and insisting on the placement in these areas of the main elements containing keywords. For example, dynamically updated news catalogs or a list of key sections of the site, menu modules.

Since SEO is based on statistics of user behavior, he immediately warns the designer and IT specialist that after the launch he will insist on changing the locations of certain modules. It turns out that the site will start and then immediately receive a redesign, in the name of improved usability and S. For example, imagine a project like CMS visualization of linking. This solution will be very useful, as it will help you get rid of unnecessary internal links and always have a visualized site map, no matter how it grows. Automatic generation and submission of website templates to Webmasters / Analytics is also not the last function, which helps to always provide updated information about a website to a search engine. It is also very convenient if the CMS will allow you to edit .htacces and robots.txt, without leaving the environment and automatically saving copies. Augmented tinyMCE or another text editor can allow any text to be analyzed for entries according to given keywords according to the algorithm developed by SEOschennik.

Marketer, undoubtedly, will also have their own ideas.


The marketer will be pleased to add control over the subscriptions of users to the TZ with maintaining the database of e-mail addresses and the ability to sort users by their requests. For example, in the online store it will be sorting by products sold by users, by price of goods, by frequency of site visits ... He is a marketer, he can come up with a lot ...
