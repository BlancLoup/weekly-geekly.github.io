Ask a question to the author of the language Lua



This Sunday, the Mail.ru Group office hosted the Lua in Moscow 2019 conference. The guest star was Roberto of Jerusalem, one of the creators of the Lua language, a leading architect and author of educational materials. Under his strict guidance, the language has been developing since the distant 1993, so Roberto can be called one of the pillars of the Lua community. He has already spoken with us three times: Lua Workshop 2014 , Mitap in Mail.ru 2016 , Lua in Moscow 2017 .

And this year, Roberto did not fly home immediately after the conference, but will come to visit us on March 6 at the invitation of the Tarantool team.
