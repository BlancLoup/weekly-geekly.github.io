Good morning woodpecker it's time to get up



Developers of alarm clocks often go for various tricks to draw attention to their product. We can recall the alarm clocks that need to be thrown at the wall, which leave on wheels under the bed so that the owner does not get it, alarm clocks, puzzles, etc. But designer Natalie Duckett seems to have decided to go further. Her concept can literally zadolbat owner, simply because it mimics the sound of a woodpecker. Special hammers knock on a wooden base - it turns out very similar.

Unlike many other design ideas, this one is already on its way to industrial production. On the renderers you can see how the clock will look, in the form below - to listen to how they sound.
