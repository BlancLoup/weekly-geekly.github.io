We are looking for speakers at the Front-End MeetUp March 28



Our internal Front-end community in Raiffeisenbank is actively developing, our colleagues regularly meet to exchange experience and useful useful knowledge. We know how important it is to communicate with people from other teams and projects, to have the opportunity to ask for advice, discuss just emerging technologies and share experiences. Therefore, on March 28, we will organize Front-End MeetUp. We invite speakers ! Details under the cut.

We are interested in reports:

  • Frontend architecture (ATM, for example)
  • New practices and approaches in the development
  • Automation and testing tools
  • Blockchain and machine learning on js
  • Developer Experience
  • And everything else

Tell us about your work! Suggest a topic that you would like to share, and we will assist you in the preparation. The structure of the report should be 20-25 minutes for the report itself and 5 minutes for questions. Presentations at the application stage do not have to be sent.

To submit an application, fill out the form ! Applications are accepted until March 20 inclusive.
