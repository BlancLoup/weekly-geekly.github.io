Wi-Fi cufflinks for comfortable work



Manufacturers of all sorts of devices and accessories to them from the skin climb to create something original, something that can become popular and popular. Some succeed, others do not. But, probably, cufflinks with the function of an access point and a flash drive are simply doomed to success, despite the relatively high cost. For the first time, these devices were presented at the Brookstone site, and right there the news about them was distributed to most of the gadget portals (and not only).

So, these cufflinks look like cufflinks until there is a need for an access point or storage device. Each cufflink can work as a flash drive, and as an access point. In order to secure a wireless Internet, you need to insert a cufflink into the USB port of a computer or laptop, install specialized software from the manufacturer, and everything can work. Well, the information downloaded from the Network can be saved ... also on a cufflink.

Each of them has a capacity of 2 gigabytes. Actually, this idea is not new, but right now this idea has received a worthy implementation. Despite the fact that a pair of cufflinks costs $ 250, they are likely to be popular - after all, a hybrid accessory for clothing, plus a pair of practical gadgets, you may need a lot of people. Maybe some of the geeks will even start wearing classic shirts - just to be able to use the cufflinks.

Even the price of 250 does not seem so high, well, and the practicality of such an idea is unlikely anyone will have doubts, right? Here is a little English-language video about this from Mashable.
