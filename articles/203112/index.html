We invite developers to visit!

If you have great ideas for mobile and cloud applications, or you are developing technologies on the Autodesk platform and want to find out how Autodesk envisions the interaction of desktop software and cloud technologies, then this information may be of interest to you.

We invite you to dive deep into the world of Autodesk technology at DevDays 2013 with us. The event will take place on December 18, 2013 at the Moscow office of Autodesk. Come to visit!

Here you can share your bright ideas with the company, as well as learn first-hand about the new generation of Autodesk products that will appear only next year.

If earlier DevDays conferences were held only for a narrow circle of partners of the Autodesk Developer Network (ADN), then this year everything will be different. The Autodesk ecosystem is seriously expanding, and we need new partners with extraordinary ideas, primarily in the field of mobile and cloud solutions. The conference program is under the NDA, we do not publish it in open access, but after signing a non-disclosure agreement (or joining the ADN without paying a partnership fee), we are ready to share all the secrets!

So, the theme of DevDays 2013: Revolution - the meeting of the desktop PC with the cloud. It will announce the release of several new APIs for web services from Autodesk. You will see how these new services expand the capabilities of desktop PCs by providing simple interaction conditions, unlimited computing resources and data storage capabilities. At DevDays, you will learn about the lineup of network APIs based on cloud platforms and tailored specifically to the needs of machine builders, architects, infrastructure specialists, and other Autodesk related specialties. And, of course, we will tell you what is waiting for you in the next versions of desktop products (AutoCAD, Autodesk Revit, Autodesk Inventor, AutoCAD Civil 3D and so on).

If you are working with Autodesk technologies or are engaged in the integration of your solutions with Autodesk, but for some reason we are not yet familiar with you, it is time to correct this unfortunate omission! Tell us about yourself and your ideas on the development of mobile and cloud services at DevDays 2013. If you only plan to develop for our platforms - it's time to also tell us about it.

If you want to take part in DevDays, write to Yaroslav Reshetnikov at Yaroslav.reshetnikov@autodesk.com. Yaroslav is waiting for letters from everyone who is interested in the event.

Respectfully,
ADN Team
