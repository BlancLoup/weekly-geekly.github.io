Responsibilities of the game designer



What do you really have to do? In general, about the profession - the game designer comes up with every detail of the game from the combat system and progress, ending with how the "Back" button behaves on each of the interface screens.

Imagine that you are the lead designer on a project. Even in this case, besides you, the project will most likely be the producer, product manager, product owner (the substructure of high-level companies), and if you work in a branch and the development is controlled from the main office of the company, then there may be more people involved. And the more people on the project, the smaller your role, your influence and your involvement in the creation of one or another part of the game.

Now imagine that you are a junior designer and have just recently started working. Given the people mentioned, your influence on the project turns out to be completely insignificant until you prove your worth. And the structure of some projects is completely amazing - for example, Farmville 2 from Zynga was developed by a team of more than 100 people, of whom at least 12 were designers. Imagine how small were the areas of responsibility of each of them?

In fact, the junior designers do the work for which more experienced designers do not have enough time or who do not require specific skills. For example, inventing descriptions for objects and characters, or a banal transfer of numbers from an Excel table to a project configuration. Each designer went through this and on some projects that are managed by a single designer ‚Äî many continue to do such things even after 5 years of work in a game maiden. So you should not be afraid, but you should understand that you will not be able to shine with your ideas from day one. And if you are lucky with a good leader, he will trust you more and more responsibility.

How to find a job?



Let's go back to how to get a job? Unfortunately, in Russia there is no education as such, which will help you in this, except for some schools that we will not call with prices that do not fit in the article. So, there are two ways that will help you realize yourself in this area.

The first way, obviously, is to learn everything yourself. At the same time, it is very difficult to start doing something yourself, and even more so to learn something, if there is no one who tells you what to do. When applying for a job, first of all, they will evaluate your understanding of game design as a whole and your ability to compose design documents or concepts. And the most important thing is that in order to understand what is good and what is bad, you need to be able to analyze. Analyze other games: successes, failures, consider mechanics separately and try to understand if they work, and if so, why?

In this case, finding a job without experience is quite difficult, and if it turns out that you are in the position of a junior designer, who is not in every company. This does not mean that it is not possible, many people do just that, but for this you need to first prepare and master the skills that will help you get an interview.

The second option is a bit simpler, but probably longer. It depends on your commitment and opportunities within the company. This way is to get a tester. It may seem uninteresting to someone, but when I started, I was extremely happy to touch the development of games at least by testing them.

Do not forget that first of all, you need to show yourself as a good tester, and only after trying to figure out what is happening around you. Do not do anything to the detriment of your basic duties. And in your free time, watch what's going on in the project: how do you spell design, how does it come to life, what mistakes are made? All this will help you in the future. Be active, express your ideas, take more responsibility. In general, show that you do a good job with your work and can also cope with design tasks.

But be patient, in the most successful set of circumstances it will take six months, and in some cases it may take a year or more. Letting the matter take its course, you can stay in the testers three years later.

Have you made your choice? Now you need to find the right company for growth. First of all, try not to settle in small companies. As a rule, they have few employees, they rarely expand and there are few opportunities. If there is no other way, well, this is still an experience that will be useful to you in the future, do not miss this opportunity, if there is nothing better.

Looking at the current picture of game development in Russia, we can safely say that 90% of developers make mobile and social games. Many dream of making big games like World of Warcraft, Assassin's Creed, Gears of War, right? Unfortunately, in Russia there are only a few developers on the console and PC, and it will be difficult to get there. But do not worry, look at the situation from the other side.

The mobile market is now the most growing and to work in it from the very beginning is a great start for a career. It may seem to you that this is not as interesting as making real large AAA projects, and on the one hand this is true. But! In mobile development, there is much more responsibility and much depends on you. If, working in Blizzard, you will be engaged in the first 5 years of building houses on locations and other minor tasks, then making a small game, you will get much more interesting things. Imagine how it is to make a game for 3 years or 5 years? But in mobile games there are no such development terms, every year (on average), you will make sequels to the same game for four years, start something new, and discover new horizons for yourself.

Development cycle



Consider briefly how the work looks like a game designer during the development cycle. It all starts with the concept of the game, in which you describe all the most important thing that distinguishes it from other games, but also importantly, what games does it look like, what is Emulation Target (a game that yours will orient and borrow some elements of) . In serious cases, after that, a business case is compiled, which is a developed version of the concept, with comparative metrics of other games, what do you focus on, what to expect from your game in terms of audience and profit.

Further development of the game begins, or rather, its prototyping, at this stage it is necessary to determine the entire basic design of the game, what the gameplay will look like, in general, the game should take the form of a raw but playable product that can be used to determine whether your idea will work. If this does not happen, then development is often not worth it.
