Phone to write to


Often when talking on the phone there is a situation when you need to write down some important information, and you don‚Äôt have a piece of paper at hand, or there are, for example, stickers that are lost at the exact moment when they are needed most. Some people even write on their own palms!

For those who often encounter a similar problem, a simple and elegant solution has finally emerged. This is not a touch screen or electronic paper, but a simple phone, the case of which is a stack of paper, with numeric keys in the center. So, if you want to record something during a telephone conversation, a note can be made right on the phone itself. And if you tear off the top sheet, then the phone will become white and clean again.

No unnecessary parts, color display, touch screen or answering machine - just a phone. Very elegant and in some way a revolutionary solution to a common problem.
