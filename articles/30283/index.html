Pushup the web, do not forget to update

Let's unanimously ask the users of our sites to forget to update their browsers! To do this, Pushup will help us. By installing a small pushup script on your site, a user who uses an outdated version of the browser will receive an unobtrusive notification that his browser is outdated and needs to be updated with an update link. The user can either immediately go and upgrade or ask to remind him about it later, the reminder time is indicated in the script options.
