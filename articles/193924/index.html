Perfect smartphone. Moto X Review



Good day, dear readers Habrahabr. I was lucky to test Moto X for a whole week, without a twinge of conscience I want to say that this is the best Android smartphone I have had to use and I seriously think about buying it. Full review with tests under the cut.

Caution, a lot of pictures and video



I would highly recommend watching the video to all those who can do it, since the text part does not fully reveal the full potential of the phone and certainly will not show how fast it works and how beautiful it gives a glare in the light.

Let's start with the characteristics :

- Android 4.2.2
- 4.7 "screen, 1280 x 720, 316 ppi
- Rear Camera Clear Pixel 10 MP
- 4-core Motorola X8 1.7 GHz
- Video Adreno 320
- 2 GB RAM
- 16/32 GB of internal memory, no microSD slot
- NFC, GPS
- 2200 mAh battery
- Dimensions: 129.3 x 65.3 x 10.4 mm
- Weight: 130 g

The main issue here is the relevance of the Moto Moto X and the comparison with the Nexus 4, since the Motorola product costs $ 575 for the 16 GB version, and the Nexus costs $ 249 for the same gigabytes.

Initially, it seemed to me that the Moto X is a failure device, since the price does not look justified. I was wrong, the phone has unique features and excellent ergonomics.



Most of you know that the Moto X options are full, you can build your X on the MotoMaker website the way you want: a sleek matte or carbon body, a black or white front panel, 18 options for the back panel colors, 7 colors for frames and buttons.



And at the end of 2013, Moto X made of wood will go on sale. Exclusively for the operator AT & T. Such a phone will cost $ 50 more than a regular model. Previously it is known that 4 versions will be on sale: from bamboo, teak, ebony and rosewood.

I have never paid attention to such a detail as a box, as I believe that in 2013 it should be a priori perfect. But in Moto X, the packaging really liked it: it is bright, beautiful and at the same time looks expensive.



Included are all standard: phone, headphones of average quality, clip for SIM-card, USB, charging.



The front of the Moto X is covered with Magic Glass, the next generation of Gorilla glasses. Perhaps this is self-hypnosis or just a new device, but to the touch this glass is very nice, much nicer than on my Nexus 4 and iPhone 5.



Taking into account that the screen of Moto X and Nexus is identical in size - 4.7 inches, in my hand the Motorola phone feels noticeably smaller. And this is good news, since working with one hand in it is really convenient (unlike the Nexus 4). Such ergonomics is achieved due to the small frames and reducing the thickness of the body at the edges.



I tested the carbon black version. It is very soft and very pleasant.

Unique Moto X features. Voice control

Android has voice control - there is Google Now. But in order to turn it on, you must click on the screen. In Moto X, you do not need to press anything, you just need to say ‚ÄúOk, Google Now‚Äù and the service will start. Even if the phone is locked, the service will start.

It is noteworthy that in Moto X there is no Russian, but Google Now works in Russian.

Active display

I liked this feature the most. Active Display is when all the necessary notifications hang on the phone and do not spend the charge. We remember this perfectly on the old Nokia S60-series phones. Here it works like this: the phone is lying, nobody touches it. If you take it in hand, it will immediately show how many SMS are missed, calls and whether there is mail. In the pocket, the proximity sensor works, so the screen will not light up just there.



Camera

Another interesting feature is the inclusion of the camera. To do this, you need to sharply shake the phone left-right and the camera will turn on. It works in the lock mode. Many wrote that the function is not properly activated, but I did not find any shortcomings. Moreover, I also really liked it: you take out the phone and quickly turn on the camera. Perfectly.

As for the photos themselves, Clear Pixel technology is used here, which allows you to take good photos in low light. In fact, I did not see anything like it.
