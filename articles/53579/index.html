Co.cc domain name registrar carelessly applies to privacy



The domain name registrant co.cc is quite well known.
Registers domains of the third level for free and provides a convenient control panel. Beautiful names sells for money.
But in this, if I may say so, a barrel of honey has a fly in the ointment. Namely - using a certain technique, anyone can see the data specified during registration, even if the user wishes to hide them.



Here is a page with their Whois service.
