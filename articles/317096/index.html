How many stars shine? Habrainterview with players, developers and publisher of "Space Rangers"



It is difficult to write about a little-known computer game, because The reader immediately has a legitimate question: with what kind of joy the author chose this particular game from thousands of equally unknown? But it is equally difficult to write about a very famous game, because The reader immediately has a legitimate question: is it possible to say something new, when has so much been said about this? A possible way out is an interview: firstly, with the game fans, and secondly, with the developers and the publisher. Let them explain why they devoted many years to this particular game. Here we choose this path, because it's not just about the famous game, but about the very, very famous series of games ‚ÄúSpace Rangers‚Äù (KR) of the 1C publisher. Just think: these games are an enviable success for 14 years - this is truly a unique case. Moreover, the game continues to evolve - a new game of this series was recently announced 1C: "Space Rangers: Legacy" . So how many stars shine?

Let's talk better about the future of the game. It is clear that for this it is worth trying to figure out the secrets of her success. In the first part of our interview, the most active members of the independent forum of fans of the Milky Way Ranger Center game (CRL) will answer the questions. Among them are the champions who occupy the top lines of the KR Table of Records (TP), noted in the Celebrity Table (TK), the authors of the passing methods, the organizers of the championships. Another part of our interview is the developer and publisher responses.

But, first of all, brief explanations should be made to innocent readers in the Kyrgyz Republic. The CD is not a multiplayer, but from the first CD to the present, each player can send his own record to the game site ( SNK - game developers "Space Rangers HD: Revolution"), where this record is recorded in the TR according to the number of points scored. In addition, TK was opened, where the maximum average speed of the ship for the whole game, the maximum number of awards, etc. are entered. In addition, Steam also captures as many as 82 achievements during the game. Thus, there was a competitive context. This context has been enhanced by championships, where participants are offered a starting save game of the game, by downloading which, they must complete a number of tasks specific to each championship. As an example, you can see the section of the championships held at the CRL forum. There is also a Player Ranking Table , where the rating is calculated using a formula that depends on a record in TR. Players are grouped into teams , receive merit and victory awards and titles. And the teams themselves get points for winning their players in the TR and in the championships. Thus, constant competition is provided not only between players, but also between teams. This system was worked out over the years, both by the developers and the players of the respective online communities; now it can be argued that it has recommended itself and makes a significant contribution to maintaining interest in the game. It is possible that developers and publishers of other games should learn from this experience. Of course, not everything is as simple as it may seem from the brief explanation given. There are many pitfalls. Note two of them. The game must have good protection against hacking. A special structure is needed for the championships - a commission on championships .
