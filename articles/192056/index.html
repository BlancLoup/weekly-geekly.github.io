October 27-29, 2013: Samsung Developer Conference

Hi, Habr!

We are pleased to announce that starting August 26, registration is open for the first annual developer conference, which is organized by Samsung Electronics and will be held in San Francisco at Westin St. Francis Hotel in the period from 27 to 29 October 2013.



During the conference, the best specialists from all over the world will speak with dozens of technical sessions on the following topics:

  • New Samsung development tools, such as the S Pen, and new features;
  • Immersion in the Samsung Service Platform, ChatON API, Group Play, Samsung AdHub, Context Aware and much more;
  • Master classes in developing applications for Smart TV with support for displays of various sizes and resolutions;
  • New features in game development;
  • Cross-platform development of HTML5 applications;
  • Web development for Tizen;
  • Special sessions for B2B developers, where they will talk about the program for mobile partners, the creation of certified applications, KNOX, Enterprise SDK and much more.

The cost of participation in the conference is $ 299. Details can be found on the official website of the event: samsungdevcon.com A preliminary program of the event is also already available.

We invite everyone to take part in this momentous event!
