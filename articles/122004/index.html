Smartphone with two cores. LG Optimus 2X video review

Today we will talk about the most powerful smartphone from LG officially represented in Russia at the moment. Optimus 2X was the first non-tablet to put the Nvidia Tegra 2 dual-core chip. In this video, we want to share with you our experience of using this gadget and take a look at its features:



In addition to the powerful processor included is an excellent 4-inch screen from LG and 8 GB of internal memory. The Optimus 2X case looks solid and powerful, and the dimensions of the smartphone themselves are rather big.

Despite the strong hardware component, the smartphone does not demonstrate any superiority in performance. The effect of the Tegra 2 chip is noticeable except in games that are really good, especially in terms of graphics. In general, the device does not work faster than analogs on Android, and sometimes even shows a strange reaction to touch. True, the latter is most likely related to software. By the way, the gadget works on Android 2.2.2 (upgrade to 2.3 in the plans) with a proprietary interface on top.

After looking at the LG Optimus 2X, we can say that the company has done a lot of work over the past year and is striving to catch up with its eastern competitors: HTC and Samsung.

PS: At the time of recording the review of the official VKontakte application for Android was not yet :)
