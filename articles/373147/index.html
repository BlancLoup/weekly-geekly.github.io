Backpack for geek: several options from Madrobots



Geeks most often carry their favorite gadgets with them. Usually we are talking about a laptop, tablet, smartphone, camera and various trifles. Some of them are necessary in daily work, and some are simply because they want to (why not?). In general, the universal means of work and entertainment is now just a laptop, you can connect to the network almost anywhere. Therefore, it is him who most often takes with him to study, work, coworking, a cafe or even a friendly meeting.

Carrying everything in your bag is not the best option, so most geeks lean toward backpacks. And here the flour of choice begins. Besides the fact that the backpack for equipment must be protected from the weather, it must also be convenient for long-term movement around the city or outside the settlement. Plus, there are still many criteria, each of which is purely individual. Today we are talking about backpacks that are optimized for comfortable carrying gadgets and meet the additional requirements of geeks.

The selection itself does not pretend to objectivity, in general, we would really like to hear that you, first of all, appreciate in your (or not your) backpacks. Well, in this collection are examples of backpacks, which can be called a good solution for the geek. Some of these backpacks are sold in our store ( more than 50 models are available), and some can be purchased from the manufacturer. We talk about the most comfortable backpacks according to Madrobots.
