SmartSwipe or secure online shopping


Shopping has changed significantly since it became possible to make purchases without getting up from the chair. Perhaps the only problem not solved when buying over the Internet remains the information security of the buyer. NetSecure SmartSwipe is a device designed to protect a credit card from unscrupulous handymen. When using this gadget, data from the card is not entered from the keyboard, but is transmitted in secure mode via the SmartSwipe terminal. The device connects to the computer via the USB port and is ready for use immediately after downloading the necessary software. SmartSwipe works with Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover cards and is compatible with Internet Explorer6 and higher. Cost $ 100.
