Weekday Sysadmin: 17 typical situations

If you work in the IT field, you probably encounter problems that other people do not even understand. Needless to say about the processing, incidents of users, people who do not even try to understand what you are doing at work ... not everyone is ready to share your headaches.

Our team has prepared a free Friday translation of beautiful comics about the banal life situations of system administrators. Do you have it too?
