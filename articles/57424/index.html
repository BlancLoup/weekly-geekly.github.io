May Symphony for PHP Orchestra



What is it?


With the support of WorldWebStudio and the Ukrainian community on symfony , a conference of developers writing on this framework is scheduled for May 16 in Kiev.

The conference is held to popularize Symfony among Ukrainian developers, share experiences on using the framework in real-world tasks, and discuss typical problems during development.

The conference starts at 10.00.

Program


Sample event program:
  • Joining symfony A brief tour for beginners
  • The routing system in symfony 1.2
  • Publishing a symfony project on the web and building up new functionality without losing performance
  • Development of high-load systems, performance optimization methods
  • Functionally and unit testing in symfony
  • Benchmarking symfony and Rails
  • Innovations in symfony2
  • ...

Where?


The event will be held at: Kiev, st. Predslavinskaya, 12 (Center "Pre-Slavin") in the conference hall " Wall-Street ".



How to register?


Just write me on habraposhtu. It is advisable to specify the name, company, your contact e-mail

I want to make a presentation


Of course, we want to cover more topics and are looking for speakers. If you want to make a report - write to me at habraposhta

To come and listen - not at all.
