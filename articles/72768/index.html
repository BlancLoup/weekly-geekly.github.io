Obder.ru - Accounting for the payment of meals and any common expenses

Lunch is approaching and it's time to introduce you to a new service - obeder.ru

Originally made it for personal use. Tired of confusing when paying for dinner, who is to pay today and who owes whom.

The idea is simple:

Pays one, the amount is equally divided into all those who had dinner.
The one who paid the balance rises in plus, the others in minus.
Who has the "deepest" minus - pays the next time.

It is possible to take into account differences in the price of lunch, the absence of some "diners", to distribute debts. A log with the ability to roll back.

It is convenient to use in mobile browsers, and of course you can take into account any common expenses.
