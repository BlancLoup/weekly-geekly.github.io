Linux. Console mini distribution.


Over time, studying and mastering the console in Linux a little, sometimes it becomes necessary to boot the system on another computer. The search for console mini-distributions did not bring good results, everyone encountered appeared to have an embedded X-server. The best option I managed to find is gentoo install-minimal.
