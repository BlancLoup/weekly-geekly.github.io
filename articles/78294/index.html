Now I will try to tell you how things are in Russia with the guarantee for ASUS laptops.

All ASUS laptops come with a 2 year warranty. The exception was the line of laptops K-series. Which was originally designed as a budget (inexpensive) series, but productive. By the way, the price reduction for the K-series just went due to the fact that the year of warranty was removed.

In the beginning, the first batch of laptops was nevertheless produced with a sticker, where 24 months warranty was written and it corresponded to this. But then all the K40, K50 laptops went with the year of the ganthy.

And then my friend, having decided to please herself with a laptop before the New Year, brought me to several shops selling laptops. And to my surprise, in several of them, where the k61ic laptop was installed - the warranty was 2 years. At first I thought it was more like a typo on the price tag or the seller mixed up with another series.
It turned out not. The warranty was 2 years and by serial number.

Therefore, I will tell you how to accurately determine the warranty and what are the main problems encountered with it.

How to determine?


There is a sticker with a number of months warranty on the laptop. Global warranty 24 months or Global warranty 12 months.
Which just says about how much warranty for a laptop.
As much as the guarantee is written on the warranty card, but not in all stores it lies next to the laptop, so the simplest one is still on the sticker.
Or by phone, call those. ASUS support (8 (800) 100-27-87) . There, the operators will look at the serial number and say how much the warranty.

What could be the pitfalls.


The first and largest stone.
Now the new year is approaching and firms are starting to arrange laptop sales and it is possible to lower prices. Not bona fide sellers can sell a stale product on this occasion, and then it turns out that you have less warranty for a year.
In your head, count by serial number how much the laptop is with them.
Only 6 months is given for implementation, so if it lays longer with them, the guarantee will be reduced by the difference in term.
