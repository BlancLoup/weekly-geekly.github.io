March 8 every fifth Internet user will go to a restaurant

Every fifth Internet user prepares to celebrate March 8 with a romantic dinner in a restaurant. It is reported by Romir Monitoring , who interviewed 416 respondents aged 16 years of age at the beginning of this month. Approximately equal shares of users stated that they would go on a visit (11%) or invite guests to their place (10%). Another 11% do not honor March 8 for the holiday and just rest on a day off. 5% of respondents will go to a nightclub, theater or cinema, 3% - to play bowling with friends.

About Clara Zetkin as the initiator of the International Women's Day 80% of respondents know.
