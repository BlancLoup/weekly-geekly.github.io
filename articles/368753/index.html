3D printing and safe sex toys at home (DIY) (18+)



Many of the 3D industry have already had time to joke on this topic, and some even publicly.



But the guys from SexShop3D brought this topic to a serious level by creating a service that deals with the additive production of high-grade sex products.


The service offers a really large assortment of finished products, as well as the opportunity to personally produce an exclusive sex toy in the conditions of peace and comfort of your home. Production is possible in two different ways:

Method One

A 3D printer is taken (in particular, on the video - Ultimaker2 ), on which a two-part blank of the future product is printed. For printing using standard PLA .

Next, the parts of the workpiece tightly connected to each other and filled with silicone from the company Smooth-On . Siloxane rubber called The Dragon Skin 2 silicone rubber material , used SexShop3D, does not contain phthalate , is non-toxic and generally has a high hygienic, which makes it the optimal material for the production of such products.



When mixed, a flesh-colored dye is added to it, with the result that at the output we get nothing indistinguishable from the factory blank for the future sex toy.

Second method

PLA is not used at all - instead, the product is immediately printed using silicone. Through the same 3D printer on which a special extruder is installed, a fully silicone mold is created. The principle, however, is the same. The only difference is that in this case the material does not linger in the extruder, but is applied very smoothly, with the result that we, layer by layer, get an impeccable model of the desired product.



In addition to the commercial direction of the service makes instructions for DIY toys at home.

By shutting its eyes to all ethical prejudices, the company positions itself as a service based on innovative 3D technologies, and time and again proves that even sex products can be more affordable and specialized if they are manufactured under conditions of additive manufacturing.

In a matter of minutes, customers are able to select and adjust to the required parameters a product that will satisfy the highest requirements. Thus, the industry for adults keeps up with the times, and 3D-technologies occupy another niche in the market of goods and services.
