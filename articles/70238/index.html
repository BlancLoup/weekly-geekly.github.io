Payment of your orders from Beeline account

For those who already cooperate with the payment system " Personal Cabinet QIWI " there is an interesting news. We are glad to inform you that a new opportunity to pay for goods / services appears for customers of your business (for example, an online store). Now, invoices can be paid not only in QIWI terminals (QIWI), but also directly from the account of the mobile operator Beeline.


Payment example :
  • User has filled his ‚Äúcart‚Äù and is ready to pay
  • From the list of payment options, the client selects "Mobile Payment" * (Payment from the balance of the mobile phone Beeline).
  • In the form of payment that appears, the client enters his phone number and presses the button to pay:


  • After that (usually within a minute), SMS is received from 8464 with a request to confirm the order and an indication of the total amount of the deduction from the balance.
  • The client will need to send a reply SMS with the number 1 to confirm the payment or with the number 0 to cancel. SMS to number 8464 for free.
  • If the client has confirmed the payment, then within a minute (usually) he will receive an SMS with the payment confirmation and the order number.
Payment of the bill from the balance is not free for the user. The commission charged by Beeline will be from 3% and higher of the payment amount.

Ps. The service works in test mode. At the moment, in the manner described above, you can pay the bills of several dozen stores that Beeline has chosen. In the future, most of the stores that work with the Personal Cabinet QIWI will receive this opportunity.
