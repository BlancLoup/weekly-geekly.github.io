Inversion compact skin v2

For those who are in the subject, briefly: after the autumn upgrade, the Habr-user-style has flown off - I had to rewrite it again.



One-click style makes Habr more comfortable and convenient:
- compact hat
- user menu on the right side (who still remembers, it was originally there ...)
- the sizes of indicators of a karma / rating / index are reduced - after all they are not the main thing on Habr√©
- Replaced the appearing points near the comments on the indent size of the avatar + thin line to determine the level of nesting
- replaced the multiple "answer" with underscore strokes on the modest link that appears when the mouse hover
- removed rounded corners from avatars
- if the voting period for a comment has expired, then only the rating is displayed, without gray buttons
- turned down the width of the right column (sidebar)

Who does not know what it is about - the prehistory: season 1, season 2 .
