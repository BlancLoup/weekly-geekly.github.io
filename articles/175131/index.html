IT exhibition MUK-EXPO 2013 in 2 days



We remind you that in 2 days the IT exhibition MUK-Expo 2013 will take place.

The stands will feature solutions from such manufacturers: Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, VMware, Cisco, Dell, Hitachi, Oracle, TE Connectivity, Grandstream, Extreme Networks, OKI, ZyXEL, Asus, Dell, LifeSize, Check Point, IBM, Radware, Aruba Networks, Tripp-Lite, APC by Schneider Electric, Alcatel-Lucent, EMC, VCE.

The exhibition will have three formats:
1) the actual exhibition, which will present stands with various options for solving current problems for enterprises of any scale with a live demonstration of the operation of these technologies. There will be demonstrations of solutions on the stands, a total of 10 demonstrations. Schedule site .
All demos will be recorded on video and posted here: www.youtube.com/user/MediaMuk

2) and the conference, on the most topical and hot topics, where you can listen to reports from experts of the companies that produce solutions, and
also meet with representatives of vendors and technical experts of the MUK group of companies.
Schedule and description of the reports (most with video announcements about what the report will be) here : choose which report to visit - only 13 reports.

3) as well as within the framework of the exhibition, Softverdey Conference - at which successful implementations of software products will be presented. Demonstrations will show how certain customer tasks are solved by the example of the infrastructure of real organizations. In this session, solutions will be presented: Cisco, Check Point, EMC, HP, IBM, Microsoft, Oracle, Veeam and Vmware . The highlight of Software Day will be speeches devoted to the risks of using unlicensed software in the company's infrastructure . The discussion will focus on the legal consequences of using pirated copies of software products, in particular, about the prospects of appearing as a defendant in court on the claim of copyright holders and payment of compensation for copyright infringement under current legislation. One of the invited experts, Alexander Urdenko, an expert in computer forensics at the institute of forensic examinations, will tell about the theft schemes and methods of work of cybercriminals, as well as the possible consequences of real companies and criminal cases . More details .
