Farminers: in the morning you present a startup, in the evening you get 150,000 dollars of investment!

Farminers Academy of Projects offers a unique format for Russia to get seed investments for startups - in the morning you present your project at our round table, the same evening we announce whether we are ready to invest $ 150,000 in your project.

What you need to do to get investment for your project?

1. You send a presentation of the project through this form;
2. During the week we make a decision on inviting your project to a round table ( read more about the principles for selecting projects);
3. You talk about the project at the round table to academicians and other candidates;
4. We listen to you and make a decision whether to invest in your project or not.

Timeline:

At 13.00 you come to visit us at the round table. By 19.00 you already know the decision to invest in your project.

Attention! We do not wait and do not insist that the teams decide to work in our incubator on the same day. We give them time to consult among themselves, with lawyers, with mothers and wives, with superiors or friends. But leaving us, you will know for sure whether we are ready to allocate $ 150,000 to your project.

About the preliminary results:

We have held 3 round tables in which more than 90 projects participated. According to the results of 14 projects proposed investment Farminers. The nearest round tables will be held on October 1 and 15. Come!
