New contests for developers on Windows Phone 8

Hi, Habr!
We are happy to share interesting news for developers.

By the release of the Nokia Lumia 1020 and the new Imaging SDK, we timed the launch of two contests: Nokia Future / Capture and Nokia Imaging Wiki Competition 2013Q3 , which are designed to expand the possibilities of working with images and, of course, encourage the best developers.

For all interested in creating applications on Windows Phone and wishing to take part in competitions, welcome under cat.




Nokia Future / Capture


At this competition, we expect developers to develop innovative applications or innovative approaches to implement any Nokia Lumia 1020 function. They should take full advantage of the technological capabilities of the new camera installed in our smartphone .

Applications offered for the contest can be based both on the new Nokia Imaging SDK and on the native Windows Phone API for working with the camera and applications. Each participant is allowed no more than three ideas.

Immediately warn that the jury will strictly screen out incoming proposals on the subject of "used" ideas and concepts. We want each of the contestants to give free rein to their imagination and offer exactly the idea that will really surprise users.

Such a strict approach is justified by the prize fund of the competition. At the end of August, ten developers whose ideas will be the most interesting will go to the Swedish city of Lund to attend the Nokia Future / Capture Hackathon event. Here, the contestants will have the opportunity to realize their concept and create an application for Windows Phone 8 with the functionality that they have in mind.

The developer, who won the first place, will be invited to a special event, where his application will be officially presented to the public. The winning application will be promoted through Nokia‚Äôs official channels, and the Windows Phone Store will appear among the recommended ones. In addition, Hackathon participants, who took first and second place, will receive new Nokia Lumia 1020 smartphones for personal use.

Application for participation in the contest with a description of the application can be submitted on the official page of the competition .

Please note, there is very little time left: the deadline for registration of participants is July 31, 2013 . So it is worth hurry!

The page with the rules of the competition is available here . And the official FAQ can be found here .

Nokia Imaging Wiki Competition 2013Q3


This competition involves the competition of author articles, manuals and other reference materials, revealing the possibility of working with a camera and images in Lumia smartphones. In particular, articles can tell about the features of working with the new Nokia Imaging SDK toolkit or the native Camera API and Windows Phone API to create the best graphics applications. In addition to articles and tutorials, the jury will also evaluate developer feedback on the new Nokia Imaging SDK in a special topic on the Nokia Developers forum.

To make the competition even more interesting, we divided it into 3 segments. The finals of each segment with the definition of winners will be held in August and September 2013. Works that did not win in the first stage of the competition will take part in subsequent stages. Therefore, the earlier the authors publish their work, the more opportunities they will have to win.

Topics for writing articles may affect a particular area in the development of graphical applications. For example:
  • Visualization and presentation of applications - new approaches to sorting, displaying and grouping photos and videos in the gallery
  • Demonstration of the development of new graphic filters using the SDK
  • HDR photography
  • Aspects of monetization - how to make money selling graphic applications, mobile photos and finding an effective approach to advertising in graphic applications
  • Augmented Reality: games using the camera.
  • Print files from device.

The authors of the four best articles and one best review about the new SDK, according to the jury of Nokia Developer Administrators , will receive the personal use of the Nokia Lumia 1020 and access to the Nokia Premium Developer Program for Lumia developer program.

To participate in the competition, the author of the article should leave a link to it in the comments section on the official page of the competition .

Read the rules and read the details of the competition can also be on the above page.
Good luck!
