Now a few words about the control system itself.
As a rule, DCS (distributed control systems) are applied at such level objects. The main requirement for DCS is fault tolerance. For her, the refusal, and accordingly the shutdown of the technological process, is unacceptable. High fault tolerance is achieved by reserving (as a rule, duplication) hardware and software components of the system, using components of increased reliability, introducing advanced diagnostic tools, as well as through maintenance and continuous monitoring by the person.
