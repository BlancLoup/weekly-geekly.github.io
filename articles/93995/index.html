OpenBSD 4.7 released

Today, May 19, the release of version 4.7 of the OpenBSD operating system has been reported . This is the 27th release on CD-ROM (and the 28th release on FTP).

A complete list of changes can be found here . There is also a separate page describing the differences between versions 4.6 and 4.7.

You can buy a CD-ROM with the system, or you can download it from one of the FTP servers . In the latter case, the developers urge to make a donation .
