Android application Sixaxis Controller allows you to play on your smartphone using Sixaxis or DualShock 3 controllers



In general, the really interesting news is that Dancing Pixel Studios has developed Android software that allows you to play mobile games using, for example, a PS3 controller. Now there are a lot of emulators in the Market that allow you to play old games for different types of consoles. So why not use a controller for the game? It is for this purpose that the application Sixaxis Controller was developed.

Unfortunately, this application and its compatibility with different devices are still far from ideal. For example, Sixaxis Controller will not work on most devices from the NTS and with a number of new smartphones from Samsung. In addition, in order to connect the controller to compatible models, you must first perform a number of intermediate steps, for example, connect the device to a Windows PC.

All this is quite difficult, but if someone needs it and / or is interested, then all the steps can be performed without problems. I remember how about 7 years ago my friends and I soldered the PS1 controllers to connect an ordinary PC to the LPT port. It was also not so easy, needed soldering skills, plus the appropriate software. But everything turned out well, and after a month most of the acquaintances had already used these controllers to play the usual "personalk".

So it is here - if the idea interests someone, then all intermediate steps are no more than an annoying trifle, no more. By the way, the cost of the Sixaxis Controller is 1.67 USD But you can first download the Sixaxis Compatibility Checker application to check the compatibility of your device and the Sixaxis Controller. The verification application is free.
