Google launches mobile shopping app

The new application from Google for Android devices is called Google Shopper , the equivalent of an existing ShopSavvy.



The application recognizes the covers of books, CDs and DVDs by the image on them, or by barcode, there is the possibility of voice search.

Google Shopper allows you to view the prices of products, their descriptions and comments of other users, as well as the ability to add your favorite products to your favorites and share them with friends.

Unfortunately, the prices in the application are shown only for the US
