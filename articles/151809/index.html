HTC introduced smartphones on Windows Phone 8


Today, HTC has introduced its new smartphones on the operating system - Windows Phone 8 together with Steve Ballmer.
The names of two smartphones are Windows Phone 8X by HTC and Windows Phone 8S by HTC .

Windows Phone 8X by HTC is a flagship model with a large 4.3-inch Super LCD2 display with a resolution of 1280x720 pixels. The density is 341 ppi. The smartphone, in addition to the screen, boasts an eight-megapixel camera, a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 dual-core processor with a clock frequency of 1.5 GHz, and 16 GB of flash memory.

Windows Phone 8S by HTC is a more budget model, with a 4-inch Super LCD display with a resolution of 800x480 pixels, a 5-megapixel camera, a Qualcomm S4 dual-core processor with 1 GHz and 4 GB of flash memory. Also here the smartphone supports microSD, in contrast to 8X.

Both support microSIM
