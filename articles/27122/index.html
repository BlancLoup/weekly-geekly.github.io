Meet iPhone 3G

So it happened. iPhone 3G was introduced at WWDC'08.



What is so wonderful about him?

Specification:

  • Screen diagonal: 3.5 inches
  • Screen resolution: 480 by 320 pixels (163 ppi)
  • Input Device: Multi-Touch
  • Memory: 8GB or 16GB
  • Cellular technology support:
    UMTS / HSDPA (850, 1900, 2100 MHz),
    GSM / EDGE (850, 900, 1800, 1900 MHz)
  • Wireless data transfer:
    Wi-Fi (802.11b / g)
    UMTS / HSDPA (850, 1900, 2100 MHz)
    EDGE (850, 900, 1800, 1900 MHz)
    Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR
  • Built - in A-GPS
  • Camera: 2.0 megapixels
  • Battery life:
    Conversation:
    up to 5 hours on 3G;
    up to 10 hours on 2G networks
    Standby time: up to 300 hours
    Work on the Internet:
    up to 5 hours when using 3G;
    Up to 6 when using Wi-Fi
    video playback: up to 7 hours
    audio playback: up to 24 hours
  • Dimensions:
    height: 115.5 mm,
    width: 62.1 mm,
    thickness: 12.3 mm .
