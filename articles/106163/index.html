Parking.ru BONUS! - bonus program from Parking.ru

October 15, 2010 , the Parking.ru service provider launches the Parking.ru BONUS bonus program ! Under this program, all our customers will receive bonuses and discounts for using services.

The first for the action of the program Parking.ru BONUS! Microsoft WebsiteSpark members are eligible to receive discounts of up to 30% on dedicated server hosting and Slidebar cloud hosting. To do this, you need to register in the WebsiteSpark program and start using Parking.ru services.



The discount amount depends on the amount of VDS paid at any time (Hyper-V / HyperCMS / Slidebar):

- One VDS - 18%
- Two VDS - 21%
- From three to ten VDS - 24%
- More than ten VDS - 30%

In order to receive a discount, the participant must:

1. Place at least 1 publicly accessible website on each VDS / SlideBar paid by it.

2. Specify the URL and go through the procedure of validation of all sites hosted by it on all virtual machines in your account on the WebsiteSpark program site .

3. Send a letter to the support service with information about the company that was registered in the program, and the URL of the sites specified in the personal account, to verify the information.
After that, you will be given a discount of up to 30% in accordance with the above scheme.

Full terms of the program: parking.ru/special/websitespark
