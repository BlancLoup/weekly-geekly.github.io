If the world were a village of a hundred people

So, a case study that summarizes UN statistics. In fact, people are skeptical about statistics. However, the data offered by researchers are obviously objective, because no one can use them for any mercenary interests. We sort of squeeze the whole world down to the size of one small village, and the entire population of the planet - up to 100 people.

If the world were a village of a hundred people:
Computers: 7 - have computers, 93 - do not have.




Age: 70 - adults, 30 - children.


Skin color: 30 - white, 70 - not white.


Nationality: 61 - Asian, 13 - would inhabit North and South America, 13 - Africans, 12 - Europeans, 1 - Oceanian.


Electricity: 76 - has electricity, 24 - no.


Gender: 52 - women, 48 - men.


Language: 17 would speak Chinese, 9 - English, 8 - Hindi, 6 - Russian, 6 - Spanish, 4 - Arabic, 50 - other languages.


Birth / death rate (per year): 2 - will be born, 1 - will die.


Freedom: 48 - cannot speak or act in accordance with their faith and conscience due to oppression, imprisonment, torture or death; 52 - free in this regard.


Sexual orientation: 90 - heterosexuals, 10 - homosexuals.


Education: 99 - do not have higher education, 1 - has.


Food: 50 - do not have a reliable source of food and are hungry all the time (sometimes), 30 - always get enough food, 20 - are undernourished, 15 - are overweight, 1 - die of hunger.


Water: 83 - have a clean, safe water source; 17 - do not have.


Fears: 80 - have no fears, 20 - live in fear of death, armed with attacks, mines, rape or abduction by armed groups.


Literacy: 86 - can read, 14 - can not.


Energy: 80 - consume 20% of energy, 20 - consume 80% of energy.


HIV: 1 - infected with HIV, 99 - do not get sick with HIV.


Air: 68 - breathe clean air, 32 - polluted.


Religion: 33 - Christians, 19 - Muslims, 13 - Hindu religion, 6 - Buddhists, 24 - atheists (other religions), 5 - believe that spirits live in everything alive.
