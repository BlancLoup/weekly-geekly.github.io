What not to do if your phone was stolen

Previously, each time I saw reports that a person had a phone stolen, I always wondered: why? Well, seriously, why steal smartphones if:

  • it can be tracked by IMEI, and changing IMEI is clearly not free;
  • locks can be installed on the phone, which can not be removed for free;
  • There are applications with the "Anti-theft" mode, which transmit photos, video and audio;
  • even the built-in capabilities of Android (and, I suppose, iOS) have the geolocation function of the missing device;
  • the device may have special features that are quite difficult to eliminate ‚Äúfor free‚Äù;
  • for sure somewhere there are surveillance cameras, witnesses, etc .;

Realizing all this, I really did not understand, why steal phones, if, in fact, they are easy to find, and you cannot earn money on it? I did not understand until February 8, 2019, until my phone was stolen from me.
