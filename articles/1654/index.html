Review of the best Russian projects Web 2.0

Anton Antich has compiled a brief overview of Russian Web 2.0 projects. He did this for a foreign audience, but we are also interested to see which projects Anton considers most worthy of attention.

A review of Russian Web 2.0 was compiled at the request of Richard McManus, author of the popular blog ReadWrireWeb with an audience of about 24,000 readers. In addition to Anton Antich, Alexander Dodonov participated in the work.

Russia is far from the first country for which Richard McManus publishes such reviews. Earlier similar reviews were published in Germany , Holland , Poland , Korea and the UK . Information on other countries will appear in the near future, so the ReadWrireWeb blog is a real find for a researcher studying foreign markets.
