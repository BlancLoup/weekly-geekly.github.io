HTC One M8 with WP 8.1 consumes battery more economically than Android version

HTC has demonstrated a good example of ingenuity and the search for additional ways to increase the sales of its flagship smartphones. In particular, the last HTC One M8 - in New York last night, the company introduced a version of the top model in a variation with Windows Phone 8.1. Compared to the Android version, there are no special differences (about autonomy - later), with one exception - the new product will be sold only in the version with 32 GB of memory.



While WP-smartphone will be sold only on the terms of a contract with the US operator Verizon, that is, in an exclusive order.



However, the "monopoly" will not be eternal, the head of HTC in North and South America, Jason Mackenzie, has already found out: later this year, the model will appear in other operators. On the other hand, there were no encouraging clarifications on sales outside the United States.





At the same time, Jason called the event a landmark event - they say that the same device on different operating systems is worth offering, having managed to avoid compromises.
