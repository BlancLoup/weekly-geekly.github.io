Analysis of tasks of the Russian Design Cup 2013. Task about smart watches

On the eve of the Russian Design Cup 2014, we offer to recall and analyze the tasks and solutions of last year's championship. In particular, we will tell about the most interesting works and their authors.

The power of Habr is that his community consists of many different specialists: designers and web designers, front-end and backend programmers. We invite you to discuss the best problem solving with RDC members. Designers will tell about their findings and how they came to them, about the difficulties they encountered. We will go through the three most interesting tasks of the Cup, and today we will start with the task of the clock.

‚ÄúLarge companies presented new devices, so-called smart watches. They interact with smartphones via Bluetooth LE. It is also known that many buyers already return them back, not finding their use. Imagine the concept of an application of any social network for such hours that people do not want to return them. What features and capabilities will you put on the watch? Ok, about notifications about new messages, we already know. "

1. Ayrat Gafiyatullin , top 10 participants.



Full version of the solution

It was a simple task. Given the fact that I am quite skeptical about using watches for something else besides showing time, it was not difficult to discard inappropriate services and social networks. Vkontaktiki and facebooks immediately flew into the furnace, there was a thought about Instagram, but I imagined how stupid it would be to build a frame looking at the clock, and abandoned this idea, although I saw that some guys really believe in it. He stopped at the Foursquare chekin, as the simplest and most suitable functionality for watches. In the appendage made Badu, to demonstrate to others "state of the soul." If I had been performing the task now, I would have thrown out the second solution about Badu and left only Foursquare. The main thing that I wanted to lay in the application - simplicity, adequacy and feasibility.

From the work of competitors liked the decision of Julia Matyunina .

Full version of the solution

Full version of the solution
