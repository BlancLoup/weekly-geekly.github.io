Beautiful words about the game


First of all I will give a link to the game site and translate the description from off.site: http://thebridgeisblackandwhite.com/

The Bridge is a two-dimensional puzzle game that forces the player to overestimate his understanding of physics and perspectives. This is the meeting of Isaac Newton and Maurits Cornelis Escher. Control the forces of gravity to move across the ceiling, as if on the floor, traveling through impossible architectural objects. Explore the worlds that with each step become more complex and diverse, full of unique details and bringing intellectual satisfaction to completion. The Bridge presents the game as an art, with exquisite hand-made graphics, made in black and white lithography technique.

From the outset, The Bridge was conceived as an affordable game and intellectual occupation. The minimum set of character and world controls allows anyone to concentrate on the game, whether you are a gamer or not. Each stage of the game opens up new nontrivial tasks, built on concepts, presented in previous levels. The stunning design of the game, the unison of the fantastic visual world and the addictive environment resulted in a dozen awards and nominations from international gaming competitions. The Bridge is still the subject of attention of the gaming press.

  • 48 puzzles unlike each other, requiring a new approach to solving
  • Gravity control, vortexing, parallel measurements and other concepts in a world where the laws of physics appear in a new form
  • Esher worlds with non-existent architecture in the style of black and white lithography
  • An alternative version of the game opens upon completion of the main story, including alternative advanced versions of 24 previous puzzles
  • The system of unwinding reduces the fear of making a mistake when passing and try any idea.
