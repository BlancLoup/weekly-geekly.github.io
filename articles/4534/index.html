A hacker from Serov convicted of extortion

The company "Bitrix" , which develops software and creates integrated systems for managing Internet projects, told the law enforcement authorities about a person trying to extort money from her.

According to ITAR-TASS Ural , the attacker was a 23-year-old Serov resident Igor Ushakov, who illegally gained access to the company's internal archives, finding vulnerabilities in the demo version of one of the programs. The hacker broke into the administrative panel of the site and copied the archive with the commercial version of the program, after which he wrote to the management of Bitrix a letter demanding $ 3,000 and further payment of $ 1,000 per month. In case of refusal, Ushakov threatened to put the stolen archive into open access.

In order to delay the period of negotiations with the hacker, employees of the company transferred $ 100 to his electronic account. The hacker failed to make a larger sum.
