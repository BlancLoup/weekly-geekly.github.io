6 ways to protect copyright



Copyright protects products of intellectual work: website content, software, movies, audio recordings, articles, artistic and scientific works.

Copyright does not allow other people to use the product of your creative activity without your permission. It allows you to charge for the right to use your product.

Exceptions

1. Ideas (novel plot)
2. Pure facts
3. Anything that is not fixed (is in someone's head, and not stored on storage media)
4. Short names: names, titles, or marketing slogans

To protect your copyright work, no lengthy preparation is necessary, as is the case with a patent. You just have to save your work on any material information carrier and use the following signature after any of your work:

Copyright your name, date or your name, date

It would seem simple. But the other side of the coin is that it is extremely difficult to prove your authorship.

I would like to share a few tips that may be useful to you:

1. Simple irrefutable proof

I called this method because it is extremely simple and works in almost every corner of the world. Wherever there is a state post. Please note that a private company will no longer be such irrefutable evidence.

You take the fruit of your intellectual work, indicate the necessary symbolism of copyright protection, refer to the mail on any information carrier, preferably as long as possible, buy an envelope and ... send a letter to yourself.

On your envelope will be a stamp of the postal service with the date.

This envelope will be irrefutable proof of your authorship in any court of the world, while litigation on the theft of intellectual property from websites can last for years.

2. Influential acquaintance

In our society, authority sometimes means more than evidence. If you work with a large customer, or you have an uncle in Washington, their testimonies and support in your case against intruders can be very important. Anyone with connections who has information about your involvement in the product of intellectual labor can act as an influential acquaintance.

4. ISBN

What is ISBN? This is an international standard book number. This is the official, internationally recognized registration form used by publishers, booksellers and libraries for ordering, listing and inventory control.

In essence, this is just an inventory number. But I know some self-published novelist writers who defended their work with this issue.

5. Companies offering copyright registration

In most countries, there are no state or semi-state copyright registration authorities. The exception is the United States of America. They have a website www. copyright.gov

There is an intellectual property office in the UK - www.ipo.gov.uk - this is an incredibly useful site, but it does not act as a registrar.

There are many independent commercial organizations that you can find on the Internet that offer to act as your copyright registrar for a small fee. Some call themselves official organizations, using the word ‚ÄúAuthority‚Äù in the name of their company, and some do not even ask you to send them a copy of your work - registration and payment is required of you. If you decide to enlist the support of an online registrar, I advise you to check the guarantees they offer. The cost of this service you will be about 50-60 dollars for five years of registration. And as a matter of fact, their job is to start work on the first three points above, on your first request.

Due to the growing popularity and importance of the music industry, a number of companies have appeared, offering services to remove pirated content. They became so popular and successful that even publishers actively use their services.

Unlike the companies that we talked about before, offering copyright registration, they offer more active actions to protect your materials. Instead of simply registering your copyrights, they are actively fighting to prevent your material from being distributed online. Some went even further. Without your direct participation and decision-making, they monitor the possible phenomenon of violation of your rights around the clock. A search on the Internet will provide you with a huge selection of such companies.
