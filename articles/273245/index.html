Announcement of the JPoint 2016 Java Conference



On April 22 and 23, a Java-conference JPoint 2016 , the largest Java-conference in Russia, will be held in Moscow at the Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel. Over the two days of the conference, participants will receive more than 40 reports from developers of the Java platform and leading Java experts from Russia, USA, UK, Israel, Ukraine, Estonia, Latvia and other countries.

The JPoint will be held for the fourth time, and for the first time in a two-day format!

Two days are:
  • 2 times more reports ;
  • 2 times more speakers ;
  • 2 times more communication with experts and colleagues from other companies.

Who are the speakers?


The following experts have already confirmed their presentations:
  • Aleksey Shipilev , Oracle Engineer, Java Performance Team, Benchmarking Expert, Program Performance and Optimization Methodologies;
  • Andrei Pangin , Odnoklassniki leading social network developer, specialist in JVM and high loads;
  • Nikolay Alimenkov , specialist of EPAM in the field of design patterns, modern frameworks and development techniques;
  • Baruh Sadogursky , Developer Advocate of JFrog (Bintray, Artifactory), expert in Groovy, dependency management and distribution of software components;
  • Viktor Gamov , Solution Architect at Hazelcast , a specialist in distributed data warehouses, a leader and permanent host of the podcast "Debriefing";
  • Vladimir Sitnikov , Performance Engineer, NetCracker , Full Stack Optimization Expert;
  • Anton Arkhipov , Product Manager of ZeroTurnaround (JRebel, XRebel), one of the founders of the Tallinn Devclub developer community, an expert on Classloaders and Java software profiling;
  • Kirill Tolkachev and Alexander Tarasov , leading developers of Alfa-Bank , experts in containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes) and micro-service architecture;
  • Oleg Anastasiev , Odnoklassniki leading social network developer, expert in building distributed fault-tolerant and high-loaded systems, Cassandra project committer;
  • Sergey Kuksenko , Oracle Engineer from the Java Performance team, an expert in program performance optimization, tuning Garbage Collectors and modern processors micro-architecture;
  • Andrei Breslav , leader and leading architect of the Kotlin language at JetBrains .


Topics are gradually refined.
