Fastest Intel Core

We blow away the dust of centuries from the manuals on liquid cooling systems, buy the most fashionable cases and the most top-rated video cards! Intel company produces the most powerful processor for PC in its history - Intel Core i7-5960 Extreme Edition. Like all products with the EE index, the new processor is designed for fans of extreme overclocking and enthusiastic gamers who yearn for new cores and the prospects for their merciless exploitation.

The fourth-generation Intel Core i7-5960X processor (microarchitecture codename - Haswell-E) has 8 cores (16 threads), regularly operating at 3 GHz (3.5 GHz in turbo mode). It is not necessary, probably, to say that the nuclei are unlocked by multiplier. In general, the opportunities for overclocking are quite impressive: during the preliminary tests of engineering samples, the Russian overclocker, known in the community as TaPaKaH, achieved a frequency of 6.25 GHz.
