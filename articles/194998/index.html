Visiting the switch

Hello, inquisitive! In Ufa, we visited a tall communications tower , which, as it turned out, is connected into one large network with neighboring turrets. All these facilities sooner or later originate from the switch - the heart of any operator, without which subscribers can only talk live. Objects of similar importance are protected accordingly, however, after watching a couple of spy movies in advance, I learned how to forge fingerprints, pass through lasers, and so on. A small photo report from the site visit.



The premise is called Avtozal, there are two of them at the company's Kazan office.



The internal areas of the auto-hall are not much different from the usual data centers (I was in a couple, there is something to compare):



The room is fresh as in a frosty winter morning, quite noisy - due to the work of numerous coolers. The cooling is mainly air, so there is nothing particularly interesting under the raised floor. In general, everything is neat and tidy :)
