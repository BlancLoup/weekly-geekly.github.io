LitKult!



LitCult is a project for people who are not indifferent to modern literature and culture . For beginner authors and experienced writers, this site can be a good platform for publishing works, and the reader will find here works of a completely different nature, a genre, and will be able to express their opinion about what they read. LitKult is open to writers from different regions and countries who speak Russian.
The purpose of the project is to unite people who create literature of a new generation, and those who want to read and discuss it , as well as those interested in music, cinema and other arts.

The life of the portal LitCult has its continuation offline: regular meetings are held with literary contests, reciting of poems, prose, and performances of musical groups. Events can take place in different cities. Photo reports on these meetings are published on the site.

Much to talk about the site makes no sense, read, write, listen.
