How we bought reviews of bloggers through NoRetype or how to get a visitor at a price of 333 rubles apiece

The NoRetype project contributes to the appearance of reviews about your web service on the pages of popular blogs - it is written on the website NoRetype.ru . As far as facilitates, we decided to test experimentally.
The object of advertising was the new social network Limp .
The resource is aimed at a youth audience. Based on this, the NoRetype manager was asked to pick up popular bloggers from www.mail.ru , www.liveinternet.ru + a small number of IT bloggers, including those from autonomous blogs.
Judging by the answer that came from the manager, a large number of more or less popular bloggers

Two bloggers refused to write reviews (including the one that ranked 47th), one of them was replaced - as a result, 7 bloggers wrote a review about limpa.ru. These reviews cost us 6000 rubles.
There was also a top blogger for number 1 according to Yandex blogs for only 4000 rubles, but we refused.
All, except for one review, were detailed, the authors actually got acquainted with the site to write the text. Selected blogs did not have a narrow topic.
What did the reviews give us? With 7 reviews from not very popular blogs, we received 18 visitors. Thus, 1 visitor cost us 333 rubles 33 kopecks . We expected a bad result, but did not expect the result to be virtually zero.

What moral of this mini-experiment I learned:
1. Buy reviews on general blogs - throw money down the drain. No traffic, no result.
2. More effective post on narrow topic blog, offering a highly specialized product. For example, on the blog seo offer seo-software. (If you have any personal experience of such advertising - please share.)
3. The NoRetype service does not yet have a large base of bloggers. We, for example, could not offer blogs with mail.ru, although we wanted to.
