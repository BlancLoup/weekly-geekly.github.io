New features and New Year's Eve promotion at Ping-Admin.Ru

To begin with about New Year. New Year is coming. We decided not to wait for January 1, but to do something nice to users right now. From December 20 to December 31, when replenishing an account in our monitoring service for $ 5 or more, users receive a bonus of 20% of the payment amount.

It is doubly beneficial, because Before the New Year holidays, many people throw money into the account so that the checks do not stop due to a lack of funds in the account, and then do not find out on January 11th that it turns out that all the holidays the site did not work.

We also have RSS-notifications. You can add an RSS with service check information to an RSS reader, access this RSS every minute and find out almost instantly about problems with the service being checked.

There was also a separate setting for the check frequency for checking the service when it is not working. For example, we usually check every 30 minutes, and when the error happens once every 5 minutes in order to find out more quickly when the error disappears.

Well, let's finish this post with an intriguing announcement (especially for Habr) :-)
January 1, all users Pingadmin waiting for a small gift. Particularly satisfied will be those who took part in the pre-Christmas campaign, about which he wrote at the beginning of the post.
