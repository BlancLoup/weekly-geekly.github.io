Rating of domain zones according to Verisign

Good day!
Last Sunday, an article was published on cybersecurity.ru with a report from Verisign on the development of the domain industry in the first quarter of 2010.
According to the report, the total number of domain registrations on the Internet continues to grow. At the end of the first quarter, there were more than 193 million domain names on the Internet, which is about 11 million more than in the same period of 2009.
In the first quarter of last year, the Chinese with the .cn domain headed the ranking in terms of the number of registrations among national domains. Growth rates exceeded even growth in the .com zone. But in 2010, due to the tightening of the rules for registration and renewal, the growth rate decreased. And now the Chinese domain takes the honorable 4th place.
The number of registered domains in the .com and .net zones, on the contrary, increased by 7% compared to last year and their total amount is 99.3 million.
According to Pat Kane, vice president of Verisign, ‚Äúover the next three years, we expect the number of Internet users to grow from 500 million to 2.2 billion, and the number of devices with Internet access from 1.6 billion up to 2.7 billion. "
And this is what the ten largest domains look like (in decreasing order of the number of domains): com, .de, .net, .cn, uk, .org, .info, .nl, .eu and .ru.
