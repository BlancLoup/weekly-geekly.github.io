Kazan space



Got a few days in Kazan. According to his custom, he went in search of space. Previously, the capital of Tatarstan was never seen, so familiarity with the space Kazan took place in parallel with the earthly Kazan.

The first place in which you can join the cosmos in the Kremlin.

Museum of Natural History of Tatarstan

Contrary to the name, this museum tells more about the history of the planet Earth and even a little about its neighbors. The exhibition begins with the birth of the solar system. On one wall you can compare the scale of the sun and the planets.



Here you can see and touch the real parts of space - meteorites. Both meteorites in their natural appearance and thin sections are available for viewing, allowing you to look at the internal structure and metallic crystals of iron-nickel meteorites. Unfortunately, the most beautiful type - pallasites - are lined in one piece, so it is impossible to admire them in the light. A lot of attention in the museum is paid to geology - my second interest after space.

In the hall dedicated to the birth of life there was a place even for Mars. More precisely for a piece of meteorite ALH84001.



At first I, frankly, ofigel, thought: "Wow, Tataria is cool, snatched a piece of ALH84001 ". Then I googled, and nowhere did I find information that a piece of this meteorite was being transmitted to Kazan, therefore, most likely there is a replica here. However, one can not praise the museum for such a wide coverage of the topic.

In general, the museum has a lot of interactivity, a lot can be touched, and you can excavate fossil dinosaurs:



Mixed feelings are caused by the realization that here such toothy lizards of the Permian period are the ancestors of everything hairy and feathered that ever ran, flew and wrote posts on Earth in subsequent millions of years of evolution.
