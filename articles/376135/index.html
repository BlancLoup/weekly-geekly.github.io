Some recommendations on choosing a bank


1) Decide what is most important to you. Service cost, manufacturability, or individual services. If you work from a tablet or mobile phone, choose a bank in which you can send payments from mobile devices. If you want to accept payments on the website, see if the bank has Internet acquiring. If you work with cash, pay attention to the fee for receiving and issuing cash and the cost of a cash card (analogue of a checkbook).

2) Carefully study the rates. When opening a current account, you will need a card with signature samples. She can be assured at the notary or in the bank. The costs of the certification of the card and the necessary documents are not included in the cost of opening an account and are paid separately. The cost of connecting the Internet bank usually does not include issuing a certificate and issuing a USB key. They are also paid separately.

3) Leave a request for opening an account on the bank's website. This will help to shorten the time for the security service to verify documents

4) Specify the list of documents required for opening an account. If you need an extract from the USRIP, then order it in advance, since after registration, it is no longer issued. Regular statement is made 5 working days.

5) If you plan to open a plastic card in the same bank, then look at the rates for transferring money from the settlement account to the card. For example, in Alfa-Bank transfers to a personal card up to 150,000 rubles. per month are free. After that, a commission of 0.5% of the amount of the transfer, at least 100 rubles.
