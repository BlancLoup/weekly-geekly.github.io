Nobody canceled invitations to Gmail

A message about the complete cancellation of invitations to register with the Gmail mail service was premature. The Digg website published a refutation of the application for entering free registration in Gmail. The list of countries in which free registration of mailboxes is possible has indeed increased by one country - at the expense of Egypt. Now residents of 4 countries: Egypt, Japan, Australia and New Zealand have free access to registration in the postal service from Google . Residents of the US and Europe can have mailboxes in Gmail only by invitation or by sms request, that is, in these regions, the registration rules have not changed.

Yesterday, December 5, 2006, a message appeared on Digg about the cancellation of invitations to register with Gmail. The comments to the post revealed that the notorious lifting of restrictions does not work for residents of Canada, Mexico, as well as residents of several European countries.
