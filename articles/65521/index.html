Rating of the most popular mobile devices from 2005 to 2009

Published ranking of the most popular mobile devices from 2005 to 2009.
Resource w3bsit3-dns.com.ru, the Russian-speaking community of owners of mobile gadget users (PDA smartphones-smartphones), has published a popularity rating of mobile devices based on 4 years of work from 2005 to 2009.


Popularity rating is based on the user registration database in the resource forum. On July 22, 2009, the registered audience of the w3bsit3-dns.com forums was more than 639,000 people. Of these, 371,895 people indicated their devices (~ 60%). The rating contains 676 different models of mobile devices.

In conclusion, it should be pointed out that this study is not absolute: it should be considered solely as the popularity rating of mobile gadgets among active users of mobile platforms.
