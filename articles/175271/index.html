Announcement: Denis Kortunov and Egor Gilev from Turbomilk will hold a design school within DevCon 2013



Dear software developers and developers!

On behalf of the DevCon 2013 team, I am pleased to announce the holding of a design school as part of the conference.
"Design of interfaces for non-designers" from design experts and the founders of the studio Turbomilk
Denis Kortunov and Yegor Gilyov .

Would you like to learn more about modern application design? Want to understand what the work of the user interface designer and UX is? Are you interested in modern trends in how the user interface interacts? Then the design school from Denis Kortunov and Yegor Gilyov will be extremely useful for you!

School goal


The work of the UI-designer does not make sense without developers who implement the interface. But often designers and programmers do not understand each other.

The main goals of the school:

  • Introduce conference participants to the basics of interface design.
  • Consider the main problems of interaction between designers and developers and their solutions
  • Share modern trends and ways of user interface interaction.

School Lecturers




Yegor Gilyov

Co-founder of the design studio Turbomilk. Designer of icons and interfaces with ten years of experience. Winner of the Pixelpalooza 2004 icon design competition. Time management evangelist, SnailRider blog author.



Denis Kortunov

Co-founder of the design studio Turbomilk. Engaged in the design of sites and interfaces for over 13 years. In the past, he had the status of Microsoft MVP in the direction of Expression. Organizer of the annual " Festival 404 " in Samara.
