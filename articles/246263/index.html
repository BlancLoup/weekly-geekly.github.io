5 improvements for the site, which can increase sales by 3 times

Corporate site today is a vital thing. Most companies have websites, but only a few use all their opportunities to increase sales and increase revenue. And this is annoying, because a lot of time and money are invested in the site, and in order for the site to remain relevant, you have to work a lot.

Fortunately, there are a few relatively simple things that you can do to help your website attract and retain customers, as well as increase sales, revenue and customer loyalty. These steps do not require hundreds of thousands of dollars in investments, but small companies and corporations can take them.



We in Alconost translated these tips for you and even added a few words from ourselves in the comments. True, the figures of the projected sales growth indicated by the author for each of the tips seemed to us insufficiently substantiated - but the point is not in them.


1. Add a video


Customers love the video. What are you waiting for?

Our brain processes visual information 60 thousand times faster than textual. So give up wordy descriptions of products in favor of video content that your site visitors could interact with. Landing page videos can increase conversion by 86%. At the same time, 44% of customers make more purchases on the site with informative videos. And these figures are only growing.

On video sites, customers also tend to spend more time, and that's not all: customers are more likely to return to such sites. Involving product videos, customer reviews, and even a video tour of your workspace can help increase conversions.

Possible perspective: sales growth up to 86%.

2. Go beyond one culture


If the content of your site is offered only in English, you do not use as much as 2/3 of its marketing potential . By making the site accessible to a multilingual - and multi-cultural - audience, you will get a chance to increase your market share by doubling the demand.

Choose a translation management system that can be integrated into your website. This is much more efficient than manual translation, which often requires lengthy correspondence with translators. New translation services simplify the implementation of translated text and site management in the long term.

Make sure that you do not allow the same blunders as Pepsi, for example, whose light blue gamma of branding has cut off the entire market, whose customers associated this color with death. Having made your website international, you open a whole world of new opportunities for your business, and literally.

Minute "brakes" could cost the company a tidy sum; similar to your site. If the site does not load quickly enough, you can lose customers before they even look at the products. In fact, 57% of visitors leave the site if it takes more than 3 seconds to load it. It is important that the site easily adapts to the high load, since 24% of respondents called the slow loading of the site the reason why they abandoned their virtual baskets with unpaid purchases.

4. Use responsive design


Most customers visit sites from more than one device. Use responsive website interface design to visit and use it conveniently from any device. This year it is expected that 29% of all electronic transactions will be carried out from a mobile device or tablet. Adaptive design allows you to take advantage of this type of sales, as well as simplifies search engine promotion and site database management.

Make sure you use a modern content management platform, such as WordPress or Drupal, which makes it easier to set up a responsive design. Still in doubt? Consider that responsive design is not just different sizes of windows and screens. This is a tool that will make your website in demand in the future on new devices, the popularity of which, in accordance with the concept of " Internet of things ", is gaining momentum.

Possible perspective: sales growth of 29%.

5. Post customer reviews


For visitors to the site to go all the way to the purchase, the site content must have this. And one of the most important things about this content is the feedback from your customers. Why? Get acquainted with the experience of the service Angie's List : it is based on the idea that when looking for reliable suppliers, people trust the opinions of their friends and acquaintances.

According to Forrester Research , relative to the brand, customers trust the recommendations of friends in 70% of cases, and advertising - only in 10% of cases. Customer reviews on the site act as a kind of friendly recommendation. If there are customer reviews on your site, you can expect an 18% increase in online sales .

Possible perspective: sales growth by 18%.

Conclusion


The improvements described above can significantly help you achieve your ultimate business goals. Do not be afraid to try new methods and techniques to make the content more attractive, and the site is more profitable. But the most important thing to remember is, in fact, your client. Making your website as accessible as possible is the key to sales success and revenue growth.
