Google buys GreenBorder - internet surfing protection software

Google acquired GreenBorder , a Californian company that creates data protection software designed to protect your computer while surfing the Web.

According to Silicon Valley Watcher , GreenBorder is developing ‚Äúeasy solutions against viruses, spyware and trojans, by isolating each Internet session from the software part of the computer and earlier Internet sessions. The beauty of Green Border Pro lies in the absence of the need to update the software to combat new viruses and other "evil" software. Green Border Pro creates an isolated virtual Internet session, and when you stop it, it is removed from the cache and temporary files. ‚Äù

Initially, Green Border Pro was a sandbox (protection mechanism, executing applications obtained from the Web within an isolated environment) for Internet Explorer, however, Firefox is now supported.
