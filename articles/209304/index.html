Robots-cars from BMW showed their capabilities at CES-2014



Probably, almost all car manufacturers have their own versions of robotic cars. Someone focuses on driving on the roads at all without human intervention, someone - to help the driver. In general, there is no doubt that such cars will appear on the roads of the whole world after only a few years.

At the current exhibition, CES-2014, BMW showed that its cars are now able to, by recording the demonstration on video.

The demonstration was held by Vegas Motor Speedway, and the cars were really able to surprise the audience. For example, without the participation of the driver, the car-robot managed to go around all the obstacles on the road, which, for greater difficulty, were also moistened.

The BMW 2-series was used as a test sample. "Drivers" also sat in the auto folded on his knees, they could only watch what was happening. If something went wrong, the driver would take control. But nothing happened, and people practically did not take part in the demonstration race.
