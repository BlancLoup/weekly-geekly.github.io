Domain 2014 Olympics sold for $ 1.8 million

On the night when Sochi was declared the capital of the 2014 Winter Olympics, about 50 domains with the name of this city were registered on the Internet. Domain olimpic-sochi.ru was sold for $ 1.8 million , sochi.pro - for $ 300 thousand. Reports ski.ru about it.

Now the sochi.info domains are put up for auction, with a starting price of $ 773,000. Also at the auction are the domains: sochi-life.com (about $ 1 million), olimpiada.sochi.su ($ 696 thousand), sochiolymp.info ($ 417 thousand).
