Stream TV is under bureaucratic threat

On July 13, the press center of the Office of the Federal Service for Supervision in the Sphere of Communications (Rossvyaznadzor) received a message that during the inspection, a violation was discovered in the activities of MTU-Intel . The essence of the violation is the broadcasting of television programs under the Stream-TV brand without the necessary license. Specified, in particular, that the company has not received a license to provide "communication services for cable broadcasting."

"MTU-Intel" issued an order to immediately eliminate the violation of the legislation on communications, and the protocol on the case was sent to the court, which will consider the incident as an administrative offense.

Referring to the Law on Telecommunications, Rossvyaznadzor points to clause 1 of Article 29, according to which, the activity of paid telecommunications services is carried out only on the basis of a corresponding license. According to the state agency, the lists of services and licensing conditions contain a description of the service that corresponds to the essence of Stream-TV, namely, a description of ‚Äúcommunication services for cable broadcasting purposes‚Äù.
