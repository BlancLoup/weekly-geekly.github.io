MSI GE40 laptop video review



MSI GE40 is a portable gaming laptop with a screen diagonal of 14 inches with a resolution of 1600x900 (matte). The weight of the laptop is less than 2 kg. Despite its small size, the device boasts impressive performance: it runs on an Intel Core i7-4702MQ processor, and the graphics performance is the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 760M graphics card.

The amount of RAM is 8 GB. The disk subsystem is represented by a tandem of a 750 GB hard disk (7200 rpm) and a 128 GB mSATA SSD (there is also a second mSATA slot for installing a second drive).

It should also be noted quite impressive for a similar laptop battery life - about 7 hours of wifi internet surfing (in games, battery life itself goes down dramatically to a half to two hours). The warranty on the laptop is 2 years.
