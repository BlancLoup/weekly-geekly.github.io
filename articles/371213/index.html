XYZprinting 3D Color Printers Overview with Formnext 2017



In this article, we talk about 3D color XYZprinting 3D printers with Formnext 2017.
Among the full-color XYZ, there are now two devices - a desktop one, which many people already know about, and a more serious one, with a large print area.



At the time of filming, the exact cost of the devices was unknown, current information, see the links below.

XYZprinting da Vinci Color



  • Technology: FDM
  • Working chamber: 200x200x150 mm
  • Layer thickness from: 100 microns
  • Dimensions, mm: 600x581x640
  • Weight, kg: 32.3
  • Management Connection Interfaces: SD Card, USB
  • File Format: STL
  • Supported materials: PLA
  • Display: color, LCD




A full-color FDM printer, which was not particularly impressed at the time of the first announcement, now seems worthy of attention. The prints look very decent, as if the usual FDM prints are hand-painted by the artist, but in fact they are painted by the printer.



The head supports the filing of not only the filament, but also the dye, right during printing. Paint becomes part of the structure of the material, due to which durability is achieved staining. Such prints are interesting for use in advertising and in art.

It is delivered at an affordable price for professional use. Given the rarity of full-color 3D printers and the variety of possible uses, it is a reasonable price.
