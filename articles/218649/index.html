FundingTo - fundraising according to your rules. Part two



We continue the description of our crowdfunding solution - Funding.to . Last time, we already talked about the fact that this is an incredibly flexible tool that greatly simplifies the process of raising funds for your project. As already mentioned, the main difference of Funding.to is that it provides a wide range of conditions for fundraising, allowing you to choose the best options. You can use both the traditional scheme (a specific amount for a specific date) and other options: a specific amount, but without a fixed period; the amount that will be collected by the set date; in-line financing is a permanently open account that is not limited in amount and time. Today we will tell about other features of Funding.to.



Our tool allows in the shortest possible time to prepare everything necessary so that any site can become a resource for fundraising. In addition to the flexibility and ease of handling, Funding.to has several advantages:


- A wide selection of payment methods that become available immediately after creating a project in the system. The user can pay for any of his convenient systems, which significantly increases the chance of donations in comparison with a project that uses a small number of payment systems.

- Availability of service for individuals and legal entities. The project on Funding.to can become an official source of funding, since each payment is accompanied by a complete document flow, which allows you to record all receipts, as well as withdrawals from the project account.

- You can withdraw the received funds to a bank card or webmoney wallet. You receive your funds immediately after the expiration of the payment reservation period - 3 days (21 days for bank card payments). You do not need to go through complicated procedures, money is simply transferred to the requisites indicated by you.
