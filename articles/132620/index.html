Electronic bear pillow helps to stop snoring



What manufacturers just do not come up with in order to help different people with different problems. The other day, one of the companies presented a toy bear that helps to get rid of snoring at night. True, the bear does not relieve the cause of snoring, but simply wakes up a snoring person. Of course, it may appeal to those who suffer from snore, but for those who snore it is unlikely that everyone will like it - it will be very difficult to sleep. Created device specialists from the University of Wasaeda Univeristy. It is clear that for now this is only a test model, which was presented last week in Tokyo, at the International Exhibition of Robots.

The bear is quite large, it can and should be used as a pillow. Automatics is built into the soft shell that monitors, firstly, the sound level, and secondly, the oxygen level (in the blood, as far as can be understood). There are no wires, monitoring is ensured by the presence of sensitive sensors.

As soon as the toy "understands" that a person snores, the sleeper gets a paw across the face. Actually, this is not a blow, but more like a tickle. Perhaps the snoring person will not wake up completely, but will stop snoring. True, there are some fairly uncomfortable things. First, you need to sleep within the reach of the bear's paw. Secondly, as far as can be understood from the video, the finger must be placed in a special sensor that measures the level of oxygen in the blood.

That is, sleep, in fact, will have in one position - on the back, "soldier". It is unlikely that all this will contribute to a good dream. In such a position, it will be a problem to fall asleep, what to say about a peaceful sleep during the whole night. But the device is created, and it means, is it necessary for someone?
