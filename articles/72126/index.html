DIY: KAP (Kite aerial photography)

Not so long ago I found a site on the network gallery with pictures and panoramas made from a kite. And then another . And one more ...
In general, I was eager to buy the necessary equipment and try to remove myself. The system consists of a snake (requires a special snake, providing significant and stable traction - I chose FlowForm), a robot suspension with 3 servomotors (horizontal, vertical, and camera release), a control microcontroller, and, finally, a camera. However, it quickly became clear that this hobby is widespread mainly in the US, it is not very cheap and, most importantly, almost all online stores sell their goods only in the US (Canada - for very extra money ...) Those who are all still trades in Europe, very much utter prices break up. Those. the numbers are the same as in the USA, but ... not dollars, but euros! However, as a rule, not a complete system is sold (a finished one costs 2-3 thousand green presidents not including a camera and a snake), but a set of parts. This prompted me to buy only the most necessary things, and do the rest myself, with the children. Fortunately, hands grow from where it is necessary, and there is considerable experience in electronics and programming.
