Free school iOS developer

Hi, Habrayuzer!
Can you still freelance on php projects and create next web 2.0 mega-super-duper startups? Or even trying to use any .Net for the mass Internet market? Maybe you just got interested in the green bitten apple? ;)

Do you feel that the mobile trend is passing you? Not afraid to stay in the back of the story?
The success of Apple with its platform is invisible already only for the most lazy. The value of its products is difficult to deny, just remember how the iPad created a new market, and the iPhone in its time shook up the smartphone market.

You have literally the last chance to jump on the outgoing train of iPhone and iPad development. We are opening a free (sic!) School of iOS development for active, young and goal-oriented developers who are familiar with the basics of programming, designing and developing, but have not had time to learn the platform from Apple.

Why us? This school has grown out of an optional iPhone course of development based on the HSE School of Business Informatics, which was held this year for software engineering students. In just ten classes, the guys learned not only the basics of development, but also the specific aspects of creating a real product.

What can you get from participating in this school? First, learn the basics of the Objective-C language and the development platform. Secondly, to gain real experience in developing iOS applications and aspects of their implementation (MVC application, interface implementation, and much more). Third, get into the community of young iPhone developers and create a team.

However, be prepared for some barriers. Classes will be held in the business center, a 15-minute walk from Kaluzhskaya metro station. They will pass in the evening on a weekday every week. And to participate you need to pass a test of knowledge of the very basics of programming.
Not afraid? Then fill out the form here and wait for our response.
