Smart card: a new word in the environment of loyalty cards

New Year, Christmas and a lot of wonderful holidays are coming, which await us next year. On the eve of all these events, a shopping spree begins with the goal of preparing everything for the holidays and get gifts in advance for all loved ones. And it was very well at this time that the universal electronic card Dadjet came into my hands, which is designed to save space in your pocket and save money. How it turns out, see under the cut. Well, who is too lazy to read - at the end of the material there will be a video where I will step by step tell about the work of the card.



The idea to collect all the bonus and discount cards in one place soared for a long time. Special wallets for cards are very popular. But to carry with you, in addition to documents, rights, keys to the house / car / work, there is also a separate wallet with discount cards? No, thank you! Therefore, I was left without discounts more and more when, in obedience to the impulse, I went to the store for a thing I liked. Everything was decided literally in one day, when I received the coveted package from the courier's hands. In the package was a small box with a device inside.

The kit includes a card reader with a cable.



The card itself has a programmable magnetic stripe.



And the instruction from one tiny sheet. Working with the device is intuitive and even a computer is not required for these simple actions. All you need is all available loyalty cards, a USB port with power (suitable for charging the phone) and a smartphone with Android or iOS on board.

Cut the cards to one

Start by installing the application. It is easy to find by the name of Cardberry.


During the registration of the application, you are required to provide your number, as it is used for binding. In the case of a phone change, it will be enough to log in using the old number and get a list of all available cards. When registering send SMS with confirmation code.


Next, in a few steps, add a new map. First we photograph from the application from two sides. This will then identify it visually, although you can search by name.


The next step is to choose the method of identification cards in the store: it is a bar code and / or magnetic stripe. Put the appropriate checkboxes and the scanner window appears. If the bar code is simply scanned by the camera, then you must contact the card reader to read the magnetic strip. It must be connected to power and we just roll the card. On the screen we get a QR code and it is read by the camera of the smartphone.



Cards can be classified by type (discount, accumulative, bonus, other) and purpose (food, car parts, etc.). Gradually, the list will be updated and the application will look like this.


How to behave in the store



What to do with this card next? And now you can take with you only one card and go shopping. Before purchasing, it is enough to turn on the map with a button (one second to see the flashing blue LED), launch the application and select the desired card. The smartphone will communicate with the card via Bluetooth and transfer the data to the chip, but the latter will already magnetize the tape, as required. If the map is only with a bar code, it is enough to show the screen of the smartphone - the bar code is displayed on it. Security

Everyone immediately has a question: Is it possible to clone a bank card? I answer: you can. But such a card instead of a bank will immediately cause a lot of questions and most likely you will be asked to pay in cash. In addition, most of the cards currently being produced are chip-based and, after carrying out a magnetic stripe, the terminal will ask you to use a card chip. And the card manufacturers themselves do not recommend cloning a bank card, since the issue of storing a clone of a card remains open. In general, it is better not to joke with money.
