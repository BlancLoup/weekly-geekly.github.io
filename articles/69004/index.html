HTC Tattoo HTC's first budget Android smartphone



While everyone was keen on discussing the new products that the Finnish company rolled out on Nokia World 09, the details regarding the new HTC smartphone, codenamed Click, became known. Since that time, Click has changed its name to a more fashionable Tattoo, and also got a stylish interface HTC Sense UI.

Tattoo will be based on a System-On-Chip Qualcomm MSM7225 with a frequency of 528 MHz, equipped with 512 MB ROM and 256 MB RAM + 64 MB RAM in a baseband processor. The device will have a 2.8 " capacitive resistive screen and a resolution of 240x320 pixels. Bluetooth, WiFI 802.11b / g, GPS, accelerometer, digital compass also included. The dimensions of the device are 106 x 55.2 x 14 mm, weight - 113 grams.

The novelty will appear in retail in the beginning of October. It is likely that Tattoo is designed to replace the Touch, Touch 3G and other budget smartphones HTC.
