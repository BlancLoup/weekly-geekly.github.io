Technology Forum: 18 reports, high loads and Kai Voight

As many probably already know, on November 16, the second Mail.Ru Group Technologies Forum will be held at the Infospace exhibition center. What is it and why is it?

Everything is simple - at the Forum we tell about the internal structure of our projects and technologies, we answer questions.

A lot of topics will be touched upon: experience in implementing and using distributed data storage systems, Rich Web client testing technology, interface development through guidelines and much, much more.

Like last year, it will not do without a special guest - this time they were Kai Voight, an instructor at Cloudera, the developer of Hadoop and a number of related projects.

Participation in the Forum is still completely free, but due to the limited number of places, a system of pre-registration through the site operates.

As a result, participants will have the opportunity to receive a certificate of Cloudera Certified Professional. The workshop was organized by us specifically for its employees, but ultimately we persuaded ourselves not to be greedy;)
