One receipt



On this receipt flights are docked.

You can fly with a transfer with one ticket (more precisely, a ticket receipt), and you can - with several.

Different receipts



These are two different segments, and they are not related to each other.

There are no guarantees between them: ticket 1 guarantees that you will reach the intermediate point, and ticket 2 guarantees that you will reach the final point if you take the flight on time at the intermediate point. Between such connections it is necessary to get luggage, and then take it to the next segment of the path.
