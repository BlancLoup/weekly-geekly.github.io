How much does Amazon Web Services earn?



Amazon Web Services has become a good revenue generator for Amazon. Over the past year, AWS revenues have grown almost one and a half times (by 49%), and over the past 12 months, the division has received more than $ 5 billion in revenue. The service, once launched for internal use, is now the fastest growing division of Amazon.

CEO Jeff Bezos for the first time disclosed the amount of revenue Amazon Web Services yesterday at a conference for investors. He called the numbers for 12 months, ending March 2015. If business growth continues at the same pace, then AWS can end the calendar year with $ 7 billion.

According to some analysts, the total turnover of Amazon for the year will be $ 102 billion, so it turns out that AWS brings the company about 7% of total revenue.
