Major trends that will continue to change life


IoT Internet of Things - sensors and data


It is customary to consider the Internet of Things as a set of devices that transmit information, which is then processed by information and analytical systems. Someone perceives IoT as the first step to chipping people on the screen, someone only treats it as entertainment, someone predicts an explosive growth in the number of various sensors, both in private and in production.

Banks, like trading, are still suspicious of IoT due to the lack of standards for protecting such networks. A pioneer in the use of IoT steel utilities (in Russia and the CIS have global projects to collect data on energy consumption), automotive concerns, companies and warehouses.

Undoubtedly, IoT will develop. Now mobile operators launch special networks to connect such devices to the Internet. And it will only be necessary to conclude an agreement with the operator and install sensors that will connect to the Internet via the mobile network. This is especially important for projects such as automated parking and unmanned vehicles. Yes, unmanned cars - this is also part of IoT.

IoT will become the basis, firstly, for the unmanned movement of vehicles, and secondly, for global automation in the production of goods and products, in the energy sector and, of course, in trade. In Russia, shops will surely appear in which products can be bought without queuing at the cash desk, cash and wallet. By the way, Artezio has developed a project for a fully automated store that will compete with Amazon Go.

Artificial intelligence as a companion of natural


Artificial intelligence is a rather vague concept that includes many technical developments: neural networks, robotics, pattern recognition, algorithms, etc. But still it is the intellect, that is, in essence, a trained computing structure. Its difference from the human mind is narrow specialization and the inability to switch between profiles of mental activity.

AI has enormous prospects for application in all spheres of life. The use of artificial intelligence makes it possible to create dynamic, flexible and relatively autonomous systems. This means that these systems should be used where, for example, a person cannot cope with accuracy (in industry) or encounters threats to life and safety (in atomic energy, in underwater work, in the military sphere). And in medicine, where they expect a breakthrough from AI, its role is still advisory. AI is used for diagnosis, but on the basis of the conclusion of AI alone, they will not be diagnosed - because of the risk that the AI ‚Äã‚Äãdid not take into account the symptoms, details and circumstances. To do this, you still need a flexible thinking person.

Blockchain is time to untie from bitcoin


Wherever you talk about the blockchain, the first association with it is the bitcoins. We are not going to talk about the fate of virtual currency (although our financiers see all the signs of a bubble in the hype), but we can talk about the blockchain itself.

In 2018, the importance of the blockchain as a technology will only grow. Already today one can see many examples when with the help of the blockchain business processes between many companies are rebuilt, intermediaries in relationships are eliminated, new business models are built. Blockchain becomes the infrastructure for a reliable, decentralized registry of shared facts and knowledge about all sorts of digital assets and their movements between ecosystem participants.

And if we have already heard about the first blockchain transactions in banks and exchanges, then today the technology is rapidly spreading to health care, education, logistics, and even government relations.

In 2018, we will see even more new implementations and applications of the blockchain, as well as examples of technology testing in combat mode and the first industrial implementations. But do not forget that the blockchain is still at the very beginning of its journey and there is still no technological platform that would be the undisputed leader. The blockchain platform market is now more like the search engine market before Google, or social networks before Facebook, or mobile platforms before iOS and Android.

And therefore the task of the next year is to find business cases that without the use of the blockchain would be simply impossible.

Cloud technology continues to conquer the world


It would seem that the clouds do in the forecast for the future, when they have become commonplace and even boring present. Nevertheless, this technology will remain mega-dynamic over the next few years. The value of clouds is in their reliability, low cost for customers, and the absence of capital expenditures. The cloud provides easy scaling and allows you to create and deploy service-oriented models, to guarantee computing power for business purposes. In addition, clouds have penetrated into the lives of private users.

And GLONASS, and GPS, and the navigator in one tangle, which brought so many heroes to the right path

But the complete victory of the clouds in 2018, as in the following years, should not be expected - business, especially medium and large, prudently relates to the storage of data and customer bases in the cloud.According to the Dimensiondata report, 55% of the companies' representatives surveyed stated that the main reason that keeps them from using leased facilities is a security issue. 46% consider cost management the most difficult in cloud infrastructure. This means that it is too early for cloud providers to rest on their laurels - it is time to increase the security of services and develop mechanisms to combat cyber threats. We think that the peak of creating small data centers and data centers has passed, and large, reliable centers will be formed on the market. They also have the mission of creating systems for the physical protection and protection of information.

We have tracked this trend, equipping and deploying the largest data centers.

One example is the construction of a data center in Skolkovo, the largest in Russia and one of the largest in Europe. A year and a half ago, on June 4, 2016, INSISTEMS scored the first pile, and the delivery of the first stage of the facility took place on December 16, 2017. It is extremely fast. On average, such data centers surrender in 36 months, but if very quickly, in 24-25 months. INSISTEMS makes in 18 months. The company was able to reduce the time because it used the factory premises (rented) to assemble parts of the system. Then these parts are mounted. This reduces installation time: assembling systems from prefabricated parts faster than installing everything directly at the site. Improving the quality of systems.
