Bluesmart: a suitcase can also be smart



Our houses and apartments are gradually getting smarter. Why are there at home - even clothes, and she becomes smart. Why not make smart and bags with suitcases?

That is, probably, the creators of the Bluesmart suitcase argued. This is a hi-tech suitcase that can do a lot of things that other suitcases are not capable of.

What does he do?



For example, show your own coordinates - a GPS module is sewn into the fabric of the suitcase, which transmits the position of the owner's baggage to the mobile application.

In addition, the suitcase can also serve as an autonomous power supply system - inside it has a sufficiently capacious battery. The developers claim that the battery capacity is enough to replenish the charge of the smartphone battery six times. If you take the average mobile battery capacity for 2.5-3 thousand mAh, it turns out that the capacity of the backup battery of the suitcase is 15-18 thousand mAh.



The developers have equipped the device and radio tag . If the distance to the suitcase suddenly begins to increase (for example, a stranger tries to carry the luggage), a notification will immediately come to the smartphone.

The lock on the suitcase is not ordinary, but electronic. If the smartphone is out of range of the suitcase, the lock is locked. And in this case it is impossible to open the suitcase. As soon as the smartphone reappears in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe suitcase, the lock can be opened without problems.

Finally, one of the most useful features is the built-in scale . The suitcase can measure the weight of the baggage, and sends the measurement results to the smartphone. Travelers who fly frequently need this feature.

Additional amenities



Of the additional benefits can be noted the presence of a special niche for electronic devices. It is located in such a way that all devices can be easily obtained at customs, in just a few seconds.



If the movers at the airport are too rude to deal with a suitcase, then after the restoration of communication with him, the corresponding notifications will come to the smartphone, indicating the time of the incident. And if something is broken - you can already make a complaint to the company - the carrier.

Price



Hardly anyone expects such a suitcase to cost 20-30 dollars, right? The price of Bluesmart is equal to the price of a good ordinary suitcase of a more or less well-known manufacturer. While there is a fundraising for Kickstarter (already collected 20 times more than planned - $ 1 million instead of 50 thousand), Bluesmart can be ordered for $ 235-330.



After the release of Bluesmart to the market, its price will be $ 450. The first shipment is scheduled for August 2015.
