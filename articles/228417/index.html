At the moment, the following important points on the state and trends of development of the search promotion segment can be highlighted:

  • The SEO promotion industry remains the most closed segment of the digital communications market in Russia.

  • In recent years, the model of cooperation with pay per action / result (CPL, CPA, CPO - models) has been actively developed.
  • One of the important advantages of the leaders is the level of customer service and well-established processes within the agency.
