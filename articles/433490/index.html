Description

Creality CR-X - a model with a large desktop. At the base is a square with a side of 300 mm. In 2018 this can hardly surprise anyone. The second feature will be more interesting - it is printing at once with two materials.



In the CR-X model, a lot was applied by the company for the first time. This is the first printer from Creality, all electronic components of which, including the power supply, are in a single package.



Also in this printer, for the first time among the products of the company, control using a touch screen is used, which makes it easier to work with the printer than its predecessors.



The most important and most interesting innovation of this model is the ability to print with two materials at once. This is the first printer company that supports this type of printing.



An important feature of Creality CR-X is the combination of two extruders and one nozzle. This allows you to make the transition between different materials imperceptible, and the printing unit itself becomes more compact.



Starting with the CR-X model, the company's engineers began to use new tables that hold the part well on themselves, but make it easy to remove the finished printout when it cools.



Since the Creality printers, not least of all, are designed for 3D printing enthusiasts, they are still supplied not only in assembled form, but also as a set-designer.



That is why the developer seeks to simplify all connections as much as possible. In Creality CR-X, the filament feeder and extruder are connected to the mainboard with one cable. This simplifies assembly and protects against incorrect connection.



Since Creality focuses on the mass market, the price of their devices is quite democratic. Plus, the company listens to the community opinion and makes changes to the design of 3D printers based on user comments.



Total

Creality CR-X is an excellent choice for those who need a large working volume, printing with standard materials and small but pleasant amenities - a touchscreen display and a practical desk. It is perfect for use in architecture, advertising, layout, manufacture of souvenirs and toys, education, prototyping.
