The only question is how to implement it technically.

The deputy head of the Federal Financial Monitoring Service (Rosfinmonitoring) Pavel Livadny suggested that the best way to prohibit anonymous payments is to link e-wallets to a client‚Äôs bank account.

The corresponding bill is planned to be developed by the end of this year .
