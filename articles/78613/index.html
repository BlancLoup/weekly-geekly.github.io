Beta Flash CS5 will not

(note - In general, several paragraphs about the same - there will be no Beta )
Adobe no longer plans to release a public beta of Adobe Flash Professional CS5. This is contrary to our plans, announced in October at Adobe MAX 2009.
From the moment of the announcement, we saw that everyone is very interested in Flash CS5 and the development tools for the iPhone included in it.
Developers participating in the pre-release program continue to send excellent feedback and test new features. We also saw several new applications created using ActionScript 3 and exhibited in the App Store.
And, in response to this extremely positive feedback, we change our plans. We want to make sure that we can provide an army of designers and developers with the final version of Flash CS5 as soon as possible.
We understand that someone will be very upset. Many of us were in favor of the beta. But in the end, we decided that it was much more important to give you the final release as soon as possible.
