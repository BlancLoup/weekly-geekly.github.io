The small dragonfly was capable of intercontinental migration.

When you see Pantala flavescens next time, know - before you is the greatest traveler in the world.

Photo: Greg Lasley

Dragonflies of the Pantala flavescens species, as it turned out, are the most capable travelers in the world among all the flying animals of the planet. An insect that is barely 4 cm long can fly across the oceans, migrating from continent to continent. This was stated by biologists from Rutgers University. Unfortunately, scientists could not observe the migration of insects directly. Instead, they analyzed the Pantala flavescens dragonfly genome in Texas, Canada, Japan, Korea, India and South America. As it turned out, the genetic profiles of various insects are so similar that there is only one explanation.
