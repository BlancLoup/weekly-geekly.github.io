Public transport in 8 new cities

The routing service on public transport has arrived in 8 cities - 4 Russian and 4 Ukrainian. These are Tver , Izhevsk , Lipetsk , Bryansk , Vinnitsa , Sumy , Ternopil and Uzhgorod .

Just specify the starting and ending points of your path, and in response you will see one to three travel options. Which one suits you best - choose for yourself. For example, from Lugovaya Street to New Dawn in Tver can be reached by two shuttles - with one transfer.



This update is also available for mobile Yandex.Maps running on iOS or Android platforms.
