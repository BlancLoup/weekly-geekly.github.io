Kindle Unlimited: $ 9.99 monthly subscription to books from Amazon



Amazon continues to terrorize the publishing industry by dumping an incredible scale. But this is a real treat for the book lover.

Amazon accidentally (or by chance) prematurely published a page advertising the new Kindle Unlimited service. The main page has already been cut out, but there are copies in the cache and a promotional video .

A subscription cost of $ 9.99 per month access to 600,000 books in the Amazon catalog? This is an extremely advantageous offer for anyone who is used to buying on Amazon. Just imagine how much students who need dozens of textbooks, teachers, technicians can save! Right during the lecture, you can download the desired textbook, not "steaming" about its cost.

$ 10 per month is a subscription to the largest library, which is right in your hands. This can only dream of. And, by the way, many experts have long said that with such a monopoly position in the market and subjugating publishers, Amazon could launch such a service for a long time. Even in Russia there are several digital libraries with paid access to popular books. And with the ability of Amazon to launch such a service - a sacred thing.

The only embarrassment is that the 5 largest publishers still resist and refuse to participate in the Amazon mega-project.



It should be noted that even with such a dumping service still can increase profits and Amazon, and the authors of books. This is on condition that it will be possible to sign up for those people who are now buying for an amount less than $ 9.99 per month, but at the same time like to read - and still want to get unlimited access. By purchasing a "library subscription," they themselves stimulate to read more. Everyone will win: Amazon, readers, and authors of books.
