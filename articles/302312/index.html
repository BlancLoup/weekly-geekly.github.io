Formula for ultimate success



1) Know your goal


If you want to achieve something in life , you need to know what the result or goal is to achieve . The more specifics in your definition, the higher the probability of obtaining the desired. Strength is exactly.

2) Understand why you need it.


Having dealt with your goal, you need to find out WHY you want it. First the reasons, then the answers.

You must do what you strive for, MANDATORY, so that you are absolutely serious about achieving what. Good reasons are fuel and momentum that force you to do what leads to the desired outcome.

3) Take large-scale actions.


Realizing what you want and why you need it, you have to take the next step - go to GRAND OPERATIONS . Not minor matters, but it is SCALE ACTS. You need to create an action plan and execute it.

4) Stay on top of your achievements.


When taking large-scale actions, you need to pay attention to what you get from them. It is very important sharpness of feelings. Are you moving towards your goal? Are you approaching or moving away from her? Does what you do work?

5) Change your approach


What if what you do is not working? Change tactics. What if everything is wrong again? Change the approach again. The key is to change methods up to and fro until you get what you want.

This is what most successful people do, from Thomas Edison, who invents the light bulb, to Colonel Sanders, who sells his chicken dishes.
