TV program at hand

In general, this article does not claim to be super nouhausovsky. It is even possible that they already wrote about it three hundred and forty times a thousand times. Personally, I just got the idea.

Okay, from words to other words. So, if someone is not in the subject line, the UEFA Cup starts (or rather, resumes). I, as usual, got into the TV program to somehow plan my day and arrange with friends to watch the match CSKA on my new telly (well, at the same time and find out how much pizza with beer to order). To do this, I used a TV program from Yandex (due to the fact that I was climbing there the other day), and finding the events I needed there, as if by chance, I decided to add them to the calendar (Yandex allows you to do this, provided you have a registered account). What was my indescribable surprise when I saw that when adding an event, Yandex suggests adding all the similar ones, i.e. all subsequent cup matches!
