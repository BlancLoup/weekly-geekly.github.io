The most important thing: the hardware platform


The role of the hardware platform in the Alcatel One Touch Hero 8 D820x is played by the MediaTek MT8392. This, as I said above, is the world's first eight-core chipset designed specifically for tablets. In fact, this is an analogue of the notorious MT6592. For example, in the Android assembly supplied with the chipset: in the case of MT6592, it is designed for screen resolutions of 1920 x 1080 and 1280 x 720, and in the case of MT8392 - 1920 x 1200 and 1280 x 800. Also, the chipset supports multi-window mode, about which we will tell more below.

What good is MediaTek MT8392? At least by the fact that it is fast, economical and at the same time inexpensive. I strongly suspect that due to the low cost of the chipset compared with the current Qualcomm solutions, the Alcatel One Touch Hero 8 D820x price in the domestic market will hardly exceed 20 thousand rubles. The launch of the camera takes a second, a dozen open tabs in Chrome is not a question, the desktops scroll without delay, the multi-window mode works like a clock, everything is fine with games ...

Here I will clarify that the tablet is equipped with a non-removable battery with a capacity of 4,060 mAh.

For long-term work is to thank the MediaPek CorePilot technology, which serves to achieve a balance of power consumption, heat dissipation and performance. Its essence is as follows: CorePilot activates the number of cores necessary for solving a specific task at a frequency sufficient for this. For example, in standby mode, one core with a minimum frequency of 768 MHz will function. When watching a video, four cores turn on, and in games the system uses all eight. And if the chipset realizes that for a smooth drawing of graphics, the frequency of 1.4 GHz is sufficient, then it will not accelerate the cores to a maximum of 1.7 GHz. The technology allows you to save up to 20% charge compared to chipsets, where it is not.
