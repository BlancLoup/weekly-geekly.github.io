French Bering Strait Bridge Project



OFF Architecture specialists were able to organically combine the commercial conditions of cargo transportation with minimal intervention in the ecology of the environment.
The project includes roads and railways, an exotic underwater hotel and a natural water park. Part of the bridge will pass underwater, where numerous underwater channels are provided for whales and other marine inhabitants. This decision is necessary in order not to interfere with the maritime vessels.
