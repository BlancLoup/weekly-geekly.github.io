The official extension for Google Chrome. English at your fingertips!


Listen to English words and phrases on web pages?
Translate and instantly add them to your personal dictionary?
View vocabulary articles in famous online dictionaries?
Easy!
The official LinguaLeo extension is available for installation in the Google Web Store.



Expansion options

  • Showing variations and popularity of translations of selected text or an individual word.
  • Save the selected translation along with the context to a personal dictionary.
  • Change the current translation for words from a personal dictionary.
  • Enter your own translation.
  • High-quality voice acting of words and individual phrases.
  • Show the number of untrained words.
  • View dictionary entries in any of the well-known online dictionaries:
    Google Translate, Multitran, Abbyy Lingvo, theDictionary.com, theFreeDictionary.com



The extension interface is localized into 2 languages: Russian and English.
The language is set automatically depending on the current browser language.
About all noticed errors in the work of expansion and suggestions for improvement,
please write to chrome@lingualeo.ru .

Very simple.
