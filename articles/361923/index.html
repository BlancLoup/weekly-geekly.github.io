Google introduced the Nexus 6

Internet giant announced online its first "phablet" Nexus 6, designed to show the world what a new version of Android is capable of.



Unlike its two predecessors, the Nexus 6 was created in partnership with Motorola, which is no longer a mobile division of Google . The new device has become the largest in the line of Nexus smartphones, its 5.96-inch display has a larger diagonal than the previous flagships of competing companies: iPhone 6 Plus and Galaxy Note 4.
Phablet got the latest version of Android, which was presented with the devices; A 5.96-inch Quad HD 2560 x 1440 (2K) display (493 ppi), protected by Gorilla Glass 3. The ‚Äúheart‚Äù of the device was a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 805 processor with a clock frequency of 2.7 GHz.



Nexus 6 is equipped with main and front cameras. The main 13 megapixel camera with dual LED flash can shoot 4K video, the front camera boasts only 2.1MP and the ability to write video at 720p.



Google announced the use of technology Motorola Turbo Charger. The company claims that 15 minutes of charge is enough for 6 hours of operation, and a full charge of 3220mAh battery is enough for 24 hours.



The model is available in cloudy white and dark blue colors. The amount of internal memory is 32GB or 64GB, there is no expansion slot.
Start of sales is scheduled for November, pre-order will be available in late October. Device price: $ 649 for the 32GB version.
