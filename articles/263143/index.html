Geek online vacations of a modern child

How can a modern child spend long summer days online doing something really technologically advanced? We were puzzled with this question when we started preparing our online program for the summer. We share with you the resources that we especially remember.

You can play both online and simply installing the game on the tablet. By the way, in the latter case, an Internet connection is no longer required. Why do we like her so much? The most popular and successful option for teaching programming, after all, is programming on Scratch. Summer online schools and camps


Summer Camp Online is a new alternative to traditional offline camps.

In this collection you can see interesting options for summer schools - How to take a child in the summer . Some of them are free, some are paid - at the current exchange rate of the dollar is not entirely profitable, and, of course, in English.

However, one of them really deserves special attention - Maker Camp.

Maker Camp ( makercamp.com )


It was organized by Make and Google. This is an absolutely free camp, where every day a child is waiting for different tasks collected on the best resources of the Internet. Most often they are really connected with work by hands and invention. Less often - with programming. All materials are open! Every day new projects. At the beginning of the video appeal from the leading (which I, frankly, always miss and turn to the study of today's projects).

Just take it and do it - I wonder how it is not used? The same thing happened when Google posted its study materials and detailed lesson plans for free on the Internet.

The only time is all in English, but Google Translator will help you.



Edu Kraft Summer Online Camp ( www.edu-craft.ru )


In Russian, the first online robotics camp this year is organized by Edu Craft Center. Their format differs from foreign ones in that the classes are taught by mentors in real time and the child can ask, clarify and ask for help at any moment of the lesson. Also, in an online lesson, children can meet and see children from different parts of our country, expanding their understanding of the structure of the world and creating a network of useful contacts for developing future projects. Moreover, the camp serves as a supplement to the summer days.

For programming of robots in the online camp, innovative development of Russian developers is used - TRIK Studio. Its capabilities include a simulation of the work of the robot in conditions close to real. Thus, it gives the opportunity to evaluate the algorithms of the robot in different environments of stay and look at its behavior, reducing the time for software development and in some cases keeping the real robot safe and sound.

The organizers did not bypass the question of invention (or maker, as it is now fashionable to call it) and included in the course the creation of models of robots from improvised "flexible" materials.
