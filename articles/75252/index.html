SLEEPBOX - a comfortable overnight stay anywhere in the city

Arch Group shared their innovative development " SLEEPBOX ".

" We believe that urban infrastructure should be more convenient for people ," the architects explained.

The room is rented for a period of fifteen minutes and a few hours, SLEEPBOX provides a restful sleep and rest from the city.
