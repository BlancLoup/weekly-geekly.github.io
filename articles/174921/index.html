Windows Embedded Day 2013. April 18th. Moscow. Infospace

Date: April 18, 2013
Venue: Moscow, Infospace, 1st Zachatievsky Pereulok, 4
Organizer: Quart Technology
Price: Free
Detailed information: www.embeddedday.ru

This is the 6th annual conference, the main theme of which is the production of modern smart devices and solutions used in various industries, such as industrial automation, services, retail, banking, medicine, training, entertainment, advertising, etc. .

Several new products will be launched this year: Windows Embedded 8 Standard , Windows Embedded 8 Professional , Windows Embedded 8 Industry , Windows Embedded Compact 2013 . About these systems will be most of the reports. This will not be ignored the embedded server solutions: Windows Embedded Server 2012 , Windows Storage Server 2012 .

Just a device with an operating system very few people can come in handy. Most of the value of the device add the applications that work on them, so attention will be paid to the development of modern applications.

Speakers from the European Embedded division of Microsoft will talk about how Microsoft sees the future of embedded technologies and what business prospects open up for developers, taking into account the main market trends that can no longer be ignored.

Intel will talk about new products for the embedded market, reflecting current trends and present their roadmap.

Advantech (Taiwan) will talk about reliable and secure embedded computers that are successfully used in projects of Russian companies in the field of industrial automation.

Solutions on the ARM architecture will be presented by the TI report .

There will also be reports on the development of interfaces, applications, protection of Emebdded devices, licensing, training opportunities, real-time scenarios .

Russian partners will share their experiences using Windows Embedded to create smart devices with rich functionality in a short time.

Traditionally, the conference will feature an exhibition of devices from Russian manufacturers, as well as components for creating embedded solutions. Developers, system integrators, manufacturers will show the latest innovations and developments in the field of embedded devices: thin clients, industrial computers, kiosks, terminals, Digital Signage systems, medical equipment and much more.

Participation in the conference is free. Registration on the website: www.embeddedday.ru

By the way, the official conference application is available for Windows 8: apps.microsoft.com/windows/en-ru/app/windows-embedded-day/6bbbc5a8-5a26-46e4-8f8e-086f0a12ffff
