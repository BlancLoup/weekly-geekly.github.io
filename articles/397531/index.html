Physics in the animal world: the toucan and its beak



Even those people who are not at all orientated in the species of birds, and do not know what bird looks like, can easily recognize the representative of the Tukanov family . Toucans live in the plain and mountain tropical forests of North and South America, usually in the hollows of trees. Most birds of this family have a disproportionately large beak. It is usually very brightly colored.

Such a beak looks even a little threatening. But the bird itself does not pose any threat to anyone (except for the beast that attacked the chicks). Toucans are herbivorous birds that feed on berries and fruits. A large beak is needed by a toucan in order to pluck the fruits from thin branches that the weight of the bird cannot bear. Thanks to the long beak, the toucan can reach the fruit, which hangs inaccessible far to most other herbivorous birds. Beak design


As mentioned above, the beak of a toucan is not at all as heavy as it might seem. The bill is very durable, but it is made of lightweight material with a large number of pneumatic cavities. Meyers, a materials science specialist at the University of California.
