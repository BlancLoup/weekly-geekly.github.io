Nexus 5: Unboxing



Most recently, the Nexus 5 smartphone was introduced . Many lucky ones who bought the phone within a few hours from the start of sales have already received it. Got it today and me. Those who only think to order it, alas, most likely, will have to wait a couple of weeks. By the way, if you are in the CIS countries, then quite good instructions for buying are posted here .
