Outsourcing is not a business model for newbies

I decided to share my thoughts on IT outsourcing.

According to my modest observations, outsourcing in IT is not the business that new companies should deal with.

What is outsourcing: the provision of services for Western employers.

Why you should not build a business on this now?

  • To earn more - you need more people
  • Recruitment from the open labor market for new projects is a big risk
  • Finding good programmers for the PO that you are willing to pay is not so easy.
  • Growing competition from large IT-companies, in which the RFP, and stability and social. the package is good, and the experience can be quickly acquired there (although this is a controversial point)
  • IT employees open their companies and recruit people - which ultimately leads to a decrease in free human resources
