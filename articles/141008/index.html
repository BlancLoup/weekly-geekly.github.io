RosYam 2.1

First of all, I want to thank the habra people for the stress testing of the RosYam site that was done last time . Work was done on the bugs, as a result of which we found a terrible jamb in the application code, which ruthlessly put everything on the handbrake. I also want to say thanks for the many valuable suggestions made in the comments (words) and on the githab (code). For example, all configuration information (passwords, API keys and OpenID) is moved to a separate file.

To the point. We have another major update.

The ability to monitor a specific territory and write statements on other people's pits, added the ability to write one application on several pits simultaneously. Of course, both in their own and in others, located in the "controlled area". So we are slowly but surely moving towards separation of functions: citizens simply fix holes, and willing activists write statements about them.

Those interested can familiarize themselves with the details of the update (with pictures) in my LJ .

Future plans include the withdrawal of a part of the pits to the archive, the addition of a large array of new template problems, cooperation with google rusdorogi.ru and Perm streetjournal.org and many other interesting things.
