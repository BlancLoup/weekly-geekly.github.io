You were filmed by a hidden camera.

What we used to see in spy movies and secretly jealous, has recently become a reality. The Chinese company Ajoka presented several developments in the field of "spyware" technology - hidden cameras.

Admiration is caused by a belt buckle with an integrated camera.



Dimensions of the device 64x37x15 mm.
The camera records video in the resolution of 176x144 and sound.
There is no built-in memory, the storage medium is a microSD card of up to 2 GB. Synchronization with the computer and camera charging - via USB connector.

Here is the second extremely interesting technology that we have already seen in many films.



Video recording goes through the camera, built-in sunglasses, using the world's smallest AV-recorder size of 90x65x20 mm. The camera does not need additional energy; it is connected to the recorder, as seen in the figure, using audio-video cables.
Storage media - SD card up to 1 GB.
On the 1GB card, you can record video for 4 hours.
A nice bonus is the ability to listen to and record MP3 files (through headphones connected to the recorder)
