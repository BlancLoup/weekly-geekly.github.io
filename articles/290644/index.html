Ministry of Communications plans to create a Russian mobile OS based on Sailfish OS

On May 14, at a meeting of representatives of the Ministry of Communications and the Finnish company Jolla , Russia announced its plans to create its own mobile operating system. Jolla was founded by former Nokia employees. And recently, its shareholder was the famous Russian businessman Grigory Berezkin.

The base for the Russian OS will be the operating system Sailfish . It was created by the developers of Jolla, and some of its components are distributed open source.
