Summer School of System Programming (SSS'13)

The company ksys labs with the support of Genode Labs and the community of developers and researchers of micronuclear systems organizes the Summer School of System Programming ( SSS'13 ). As part of the School, you will be able to get acquainted with current scientific projects in the field of system programming, virtualization and reliability.

Participation in the School is free, but the number of participants is limited. Lectures will be held in English and Russian. According to the results of the Summer School, an internship with further employment is provided.

Summer school directions


  • Microkernel OS and their security
  • Virtualization
  • Reliability


Lecturers


  • Bjoern Doebel, PhD Student in Operating Systems at TU Dresden, Germany
  • Sartakov Vasily, ksys labs, Russia
  • Dr.-Ing. Norman Feske, Genode Labs, Germany
  • Udo Steinberg, Research Scientist at Intel, Germany


Key Dates


June 01 Start receiving applications
July 15 Deadline for applications
July 25 Publication of selection results
5-6 August Summer School

Selections


In order to participate in the School, it is necessary to fill out a questionnaire and complete a practical task. The practical task is a few tasks from the last Summer School, which allows you to demonstrate knowledge of operating systems and programming skills.
