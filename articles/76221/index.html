The fans of the game Mount & Blade have a long-awaited reason for joy. 1C sent to print the addition of "Fire and Sword", created by Sich studio with the active participation of Snowberry Connection and the developer of the original M & B company TaleWorlds.

As the name suggests, the project is dedicated to the era of cruel wars, civil strife and power struggles in Eastern Europe of the seventeenth century, sung by Senkevich.

From Mount & Blade "With Fire and Sword" you can expect a detailed study of the historical component, a decent story (which, by the way, plainly was not in Mount & Blade itself) and the professional performance of other game elements, including a unique combat system designed for horse and foot battles; detailed map of Eastern Europe, recreated on the basis of real topographical plans of the time, etc.

The release of the new project will take place on December 4th. Look forward to. In the meantime, you can read interviews with developers.
