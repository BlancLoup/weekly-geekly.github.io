Enthusiast designed a homemade 8-bit processor in Minecraft

An enthusiast hiding under the nickname Dj Sapsan, created in Minecraft a real processor that can run the simplest programs!



Detailed video describing and demonstrating the work under the cut.





Processor Parameters:
- 8 bits
- 256 bytes memory
- stack of 9 bytes
- I / O ports
- frequency ~ 1 Hz

The author of the project allows to create any arbitrary works on the basis of his project, and, in general, to do with it everything, provided that one condition is observed - the indication of the original authorship and the installation of a link to its channel.
