Approved professional standard manager of IT products

The Minister of Labor of the Russian Federation approved the professional standard of the Product Manager in the field of information technology, which in 2013 was developed by the working group for the creation of professional standard, Denis Beskov said on the website of the School of Product Management.

The standard can be the basis for the development of:

  • Job descriptions;
  • Job texts;
  • Corporate competency profiles;
  • Professional development plans;
  • Certification programs and tests;
  • Commercial training programs;
  • Federal educational programs.


The text of the standard can be found at the link (* .pdf).

Worked on the standard: Vladimir Bataev (EsperantoXL), Denis Beskov (School of Product Management), Mikhail Butlitsky (KamaGames), Alexey Zhuk (Enkata), Mikhail Karpov (Yandex), Anton Katkov (Flexis), Roman Sedykh (Whitescape), Andrey Sytsko (Anturis), Dmitry Shkolnikov (Mail.ru), Vladimir Chervonenko (NPTV / Digital October).
