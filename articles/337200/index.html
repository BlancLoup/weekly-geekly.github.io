Step by Step Plan for Upwork # 2



The previous part .

I hope that you have already registered and filled out the profile, if not, then you are in the last article. And we will begin.

Before registering, I advise you to carefully read the rules of the exchange, otherwise as it was in my case - you can fly into the ban "without-a-mes-bottom".

If you have a bad communication with the customer, then the freelance on Upwork is clearly not for you, the only way out is agencies.

Fixed Price and Hourly


There are two types of payment: hourly and fixed payment. With a fixed payment you will receive money at the end of the project or part of it. Mostly use millestone. What is millestone? This is a way of paying for work that gives the greatest guarantees. You start the project, the employer deposits the first part of the amount, that is, the money is now not just a figure - this is real money that is somewhere, the customer cannot take it without your consent or the support of the support, but you cannot confirm it without the customer‚Äôs confirmation or support will receive. With hourly things are different - you install an upwork tracker on a laptop or PC (what you work there for). When you start working, you start the tracker every 10 minutes, it takes a screen, a snapshot of your face through a webcam, counts the number of keyboard and mouse clicks and sends it to the exchange. Every Monday, the customer can see what you did there and pay for the work. See more here .

Search for good orders


The basis is orders, and finding truly good orders is not easy. You have connections - this is the currency on the exchange, for which you can apply for a project, basically the cost of the application is 2 connections, but it can go up to 5. There are 60 connections per month on the base account (up to 30 applications), which is quite enough. Therefore, you can not click on the first post. By the way, spend all the connections and not get the order - fly away to the ban, forever . Need to choose carefully. For a newbie, getting a project on which more than 15 people have applied for is extremely difficult. First, you should choose projects up to 200 USD. But it is worthwhile to have the concept of the minimum amount, that is, projects whose amount below this one is not interesting to you. A crowd of Indians rush to cheap projects. Personally, I always keep to the principle: The more the customer first talks about the project, the more pleasant it will be to work with him. Therefore, it makes sense to look for orders with a great description, especially for beginners, because only this way you can be confident in your abilities, and only in this way can you write a good cover letter. Communication with the customer


It is an extremely stressful process, because you need to show your professionalism to a person who does not really understand this. The only way to do this is to show how you will solve the problem. Sometimes for this it is worth resorting to diagrams (here is an excellent service for drawing diagrams). Many are frightened by the language barrier, because not everyone has knowledge of English at the highest level. But customers treat this with understanding, and do not pay much attention, if the level of English must be high - this is indicated in the project.

Memo freelancer:

  • Do not work for review
  • Do not put the price below the baseboard - call the real one. Some people take the order putting the price higher than indicated in the project (That's it - this is not fl.ru).
  • On the proposal to work around the project, made in correspondence at the very upwork, you must immediately refuse and immediately complain.
  • If it turns out that you do not have enough knowledge for this project, it is better to immediately deviate and leave with apologies, although something can be learned along the way.
  • Perform a project with high quality and on time, if you break the deadline, make a discount so that you can somehow block your mistake
  • In a conflict situation, they will try to solve everything on the spot, and only if a compromise was not reached, contact the support.
  • Before starting work, negotiate at least some minimum TK

Withdrawal of money


When you earn the first money, you will want to pick it up. In total there are 4 statuses:

  1. Work in Progress. Work in progress (open milestone, or money for hours already worked)
  2. Overview. The customer confirms that you must receive this money and sends it
  3. Pending Upwork carefully eats away its commission and begins the countdown (security period) when the customer or freelancer can scribble on each other with a request to return the money / get more
  4. Avaliable. Finally they can be removed
