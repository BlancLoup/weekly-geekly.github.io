In Ukraine, Chernihiv Distillery began production in bottles of unusual shape - in the form of a male member. Vodka is swept from the shelves, apparently preparing for the upcoming holidays - Defender of the Fatherland Day on February 23 and International Women's Day on March 8, Factnews reports with reference to Sian-ua.info.





As the sellers of the stores where this souvenir vodka is sold under the name "Garmata" ("Gun") are noted, the goods are in high demand among buyers. People buy vodka as a gift to friends and relatives.

Chernigov Vodka Chernigiv Distillery JSC (TM Vagma-Lux) has been producing strong alcoholic beverages for 30 years - classic and special vodka, bitter and sweet liqueurs. Plant technologists are constantly working to increase the demand for products. And they succeed - Chernigov vodka is sold throughout Ukraine.

The company offers original vodka souvenirs with historical and literary overtones under the names: "Vakula", "Khmelnitsky", "Cossack with a barrel", "Mace", various sabers and pistols, "Golden Fleece" and others.
