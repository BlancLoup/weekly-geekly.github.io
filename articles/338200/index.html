GeekUniversity opens a set of game development faculty



In our online university for programmers opened a new faculty of game development. During the year of study, students will learn to write games in C #, reaching the level of middle.

GeekUniversity is a joint educational project of the Mail.Ru Group and the IT-portal GeekBrains. The curriculum and special courses for the faculty are developed by Avito, Alfa Bank, MTS, Tinkoff, DeliveryClub.

To enter the game development department, you must pass the C # theory of language test. Those who do not cope with the entrance test will be offered to complete preparatory courses - eight lessons in which you can master object-oriented programming and basic C # constructions. The faculty program is designed for a year, and the intensity of classes is three to four times a week. During their studies, students implement a number of projects that they can add to their portfolio.

As part of the training, students will learn C #, SQL, classical algorithms and data structures, take courses in augmented reality, architecture and design patterns, and learn the basics of 3D modeling - this will allow them to create simple models and transfer them to Unity3D.
