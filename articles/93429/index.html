Self-weighting luggage



The main problem at the airport (well, except for being late for the flight, of course) is the weight of the baggage. If the norm established by the company is exceeded, it is necessary to pay extra. To some extent, the Self-Weighing Suitcase allows this problem to be solved. It has built-in scales and an LCD monitor that shows the weight of the baggage. So you can determine what to take, and what can be left not at the weighing counter, but still at home during the training. The bag is sold in the Expertverdict.com store for 69.95 pounds.
