We have great news! Already on sale book " Universal Design Methods ." This book is a continuation of the series "Universal Design Principles". In this book, among other things, at each turn in the left part, a brief text description of the method is given, accompanied by references to the works of the authors.



Prototype: Universal Methods of Design: 100 Ways to Research and Development, Effective Solutions
Publisher: Publisher: ROCKPORT

Annotation:


In this book, 100 of the most popular and effective methods of creating high-quality design work, without which no modern designer can do, in whatever field he may apply his skills.



Bruce Hanington, professor of applied design at Carnegie University (USA), and well-known practicing designer and consultant Bella Martin gathered in this book 100 of the most popular and effective methods of creating high-quality design work, without which no modern designer can do in any field applied his skill. Beautiful photos illustrate each concept. The book is intended both for students studying design, and for practitioners.

" Universal Design Methods ":
  • destroy the myth that consumer and user research methods are complex, expensive and time consuming;
  • create a common terminological space for various professionals involved in the design process;
  • illustrate methods with visual materials and cases;
  • give the basic characteristic of methods;
  • show in which cases it is better to apply a particular method to simplify the prioritization of the design process.
