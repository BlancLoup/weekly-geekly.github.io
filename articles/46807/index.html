This can be interesting, first of all, to regional companies that are engaged in the development of sites and want to assess their market. Believe me, the data will surprise you.
All of the below analytics refers to Chelyabinsk and the region

My colleagues, when they studied the results of the annual analysis of the number of domains in Chelyabinsk for 2008 , added very interesting criteria by which they made a sample. This is the binding of the domain to the region by the region's digit (we have the digit ‚Äú74‚Äù) and the transliteration of the name of the city of Chelyabinsk (looks like ‚Äúchel‚Äù). See what we have: for the past 2008 (without December), 15101 domains have been registered for the share of Chelyabinsk and the region. In this total mass there are domains that were not registered to do Chelyabinsk sites with them (domains). They made general or industry websites out of them, that is, it is not necessary that it was a Chelyabinsk office that made a website for itself (just a company website). But sampling by region and transliteration of the city name is the most likely case of a client order (company website). According to the data obtained in our 2877 domains out of 15101 have the prefix "74" in the name and 1539 domains have the prefix "chel". Total of such 4416 domains.

Let's now determine the price of developing the site. This price has a wide variation. From 5000 rubles to infinity, in fact. I was told that students in Chelyabinsk and for 1000 rubles some sites do, I did not take this into account, because those who understand what a site development is, also understand the order of works and their cost.
I have data from a small pre-crisis marketing research. According to this study, the average Chelyabinsk company is ready to spend on the development of a site for its company no more than 20,000-30000 rubles. In the understanding of the average Chelyabinsk client, this is the most normal amount for the implementation of their website. There are, of course, such companies that understand what they want; they understand the price level for their whims, because they go to professional developers (they do not start with students) and that price range is already an order of magnitude higher. But there are few such companies and, for a negligible number of such companies, these data can be neglected.
So, we believe that the bulk is ready to pay an average of 25,000 rubles for the development of the site.
I Consider "in the forehead": by not complicated work 4416 * 25000 = 110.400.000 rubles. It turned out a little over a hundred million rubles. But it should be understood that of these 4,416 people who have registered domains, not all are representatives of the very layer about which I wrote above.
