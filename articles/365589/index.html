Become Wolverine at Home

Advancer Technologies showed Wolverine's claws, which are advanced on command from the arm muscles. The device is controlled by the MyoWare sensor - it is presented on KickStarter . This sensor is designed for various devices and prostheses.



In 2014, inventor and stuntman Colin Furze introduced his version of Logan's claws. The most important thing in such experiments with the claws of Wolverine is not to hurt yourself .



The version from Advencer Technologies is controlled by the MyoWare sensor. To release the claws, you need to strain the muscles of the arms - clench your fist.



MyoWare Muscle Sensor v3 is the third version of the sensor that measures muscle activity using electromyography . The device is designed to control prostheses , robots and gadgets compatible with Arduino.





An example of the use of electromyography in a prosthesis is the hand of Iron Man, which Robert Downey Jr. presented to seven-year-old Alex in March 2014. The developer of MyoWare is a sponsor of the Limbitless Solutions project that made this gift for the boy.



Detailed instructions are published on Makezine.com . You will need a 3D printer, Arduino and MyoWare sensor.
