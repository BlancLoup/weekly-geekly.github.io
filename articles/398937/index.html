11.11. World Day of "fake" discounts

A bit of prose


The annual sale on Aliexpress, which takes place on November 11 of each year, is a very remarkable event. The folk holiday, the Bachelor's Day, which is celebrated annually on November 11 in China, in 2013 received international publicity and became akin to the American ‚ÄúBlack Friday‚Äù. Customers are promised big discounts, bonuses and gifts for all sorts of necessary and useful things, many people are waiting for this day all year.

However, after last year's similar sales, many buyers still had doubts about the reality of the discounts that the sellers were able to offer.

This year we decided to check how fair the discounts are for the buyers on the 11.11 sales day and to monitor the pricing situation.

First swallows


At the time of the start of monitoring, the number of goods that were sold at a discount was more than 70% of the total number of tracked items. Discounts appeared somewhere, disappeared somewhere, but the overall result was about the same.

However, 11 days before the sale (i.e., 01.11.16), the discounts started to ‚Äúdisappear‚Äù, so 30.10 of goods with a discount was 388 606, and today (06.11.16) the quantity of goods with a discount is only 424.

At the same time, a list of promotional items and their future value appeared on the website of Aliexpress. An official promotion of the upcoming sale was launched.

Conclusion: before the sale almost all discounts were removed, even those that existed from the day of the start of price monitoring.

How to identify "fake" discounts


We recorded all the above price fluctuations, compared with the prices laid out for 11.11 and set up the following filters:

  • the product on sale will cost 10% or more lower than the minimum price * for the last month - a real discount ;

  • the product will be cheaper, but it will not be cheaper than the minimum price * for the last month - a discount is lime ;

  • the product will be cheaper, but the discount will be from 0 to 10% compared with the minimum price * for the last month - a slight discount - this is also a negative result. Why do I need to declare large discounts, and sell the product with minor or even zero discounts.

* Minimum price with current discounts (if any). For example, the price of a product is 1200 rubles without a discount, and with a discount of 896 rubles, this means that the minimum price is 896 rubles.
