Great idea for fans to clone English-language projects.

Parkingspots.com is a Canadian startup launched this year that brings together those who would like to rent or rent a parking space. Holders of parking spaces can place their ads, indicate the desired rental price, as well as the period for which the space is rented. Those who want to rent, respectively, have the opportunity to choose the most suitable option. Only one ad can be posted free of charge; a paid subscription is provided for landlords with a large number of parking spaces.

For Russia, this issue is becoming more and more relevant, and it would be foolish not to begin to learn from the experience of Canadian colleagues.
