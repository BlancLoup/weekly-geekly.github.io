GTA: San Andreas on mobile devices in December

Rockstar Games officially announced the launch of San Andreas on iOS, Android and Windows Phone platforms yesterday. It will not be just a port of the desktop version - Rockstar claim that they reworked the graphic part: improved shadows, drawing distance, and also worked on models of people and machines. The touch control will also be changed: it will dynamically change, although it is not clear how. The list of improvements also added an improved checkpoint system, designed to facilitate the passage of missions.


Grand Threft Auto: San Andreas was released on PS2 in October 2004, was subsequently ported to Xbox, PC (Windows, Mac), Xbox360 and PS3. This is the third game of the GTA 3 series and the sixth in the entire series. The story takes place in 1992, when the glavgeroy, African American Karl Jones, nicknamed CJ, returns to Los Santos for his mother's funeral. This is the only game in the series where the map is not one detached city, but a whole state with three megalopolises: Los Santos (type of Los Angeles), San Fierro (San Francisco), Las Venturas (Las Vegas) , a dozen small villages and a bunch of analogues of the sights of the west coast: the Hoover Dam, Mount Diablo, Zone 51 and many others.
The release of Gta: San Andreas to mobile platforms was expected: in December 2011, GTA 3 was released on iOS, in December 2012, GTA: Vice City was released and both were very successful.
