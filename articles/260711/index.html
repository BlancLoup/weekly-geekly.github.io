Friends!

We are pleased to announce that Kerio Technologies and Softline are inviting you on June 25, 2015. take part in a free seminar: "Kerio Control: Everything you wanted to know, but did not know who to ask."

Interesting? Details under the cut ...

Use the Kerio Control solution, but you cannot fully understand the intricacies of the settings, or maybe you are looking for a solution for organizing secure and easily-controlled Internet access? then this seminar is for you!
