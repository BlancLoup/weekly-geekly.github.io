On the final day of such a long working week, we want to congratulate you on the upcoming holiday - March 8 ! We could not take advantage of such a wonderful occasion, and decided to tell you about the most influential and successful women in the world of mobile and Internet technologies, the real women of the industry.

Marissa Mayer, Google.
Vice President, Search Products & User Satisfaction.



Marissa leads the direction of managing the most important Google projects - search . In her charge are launches associated with the main services of the company: web search, Images, News, Books, Google Maps, Google Earth, Toolbar and others. She joined the team in 1999 and was the first female engineer. In addition, Marissa has been reading popular lectures on programming at Stanford University for a long time.

Sher Wong, HTC.
Chairman of the Board of Directors, one of the founders of the company.



Cher was very lucky with her relatives, which, of course, does not negate her personal outstanding qualities and achievements. Her father was the richest Taiwanese long-lived entrepreneur who led the empire of the plastics and chemical industries. Husband Cher - CEO of VIA Technologies , which manufactures processors, motherboards and memory modules. Cher herself has been involved in the life of HTC since its inception.

Elena Shmatova, VimpelCom (Beeline).
CEO.



She graduated from the economics department of the Moscow Institute of Communications. Elena became the general director of one of the Big Three operators in June 2010, having worked in the company since 1999 in major financial positions.

Mary McDowell, Nokia.
Executive Vice President. Mobile phone division.



During her career, Mary managed to work in companies such as Compaq and Hewlett-Packard . In 2004, she began working at Nokia, and now she has all the tasks of promoting and creating mobile products for the company worldwide.

We hope such a powerful list will inspire both men and women to new achievements and ideas. Once again we congratulate you on the upcoming holiday! Editorial Droider.ru .
