Cinema Park invests about 19 million dollars in cinema halls of 4DX format

This fall, anyone will be able to look into the cinema of the 4DX format, where spicy smells, rain and even mysterious fog await him. In October-November, the first cinema hall in 4DX format will be launched in the shopping center Metropolis. In just 5 years, Cinema Park and the South Korean CJ 4DPlex promise to open about 19 such "4DX-apartments" in Russia.



Unlike 4D-format, which creates the effect of movement of chairs during a session, 4DX equipment performs longitudinal and transverse tilt depending on what is happening on the screens, and most importantly, all this is accompanied by "the wonders of Mother Nature":
  • sharp gusts of wind;
  • splashing water in the face;
  • smells of dinosaurs and other representatives of flora and fauna;
  • fog, cloudy weather and lightning are provided for you




Upon request, you will be able to disable some of these options, and some will be set by default, so you will not be able to get out of the water.

The first movie that the audience saw with 4DX technology was Titanic. I wonder how much water CJ 4DPlex Co needed to create a splash effect while watching this movie?

According to specialists of this company, there are already agreements with Hollywood producers, who have begun to create films in this format. To date, 10 new films have been released in 4DX format.

For the first time the 4DX room was opened in 2009, by 2016 the Korean company expects to bring their number to 800.
