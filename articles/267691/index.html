Live broadcast Droidcon Moscow 2015

Hi, Habr! Today at 10:00 (Moscow time) on droidcon.moscow will begin the broadcast of one of the largest annual Android conferences - Droidcon. This time the event will be divided into the following thematic blocks: Tech Day, Business Day, Community Day. The main themes of Droidcon Moscow 2015 will be: New Android M features, wearable devices and development for them, cloud, Internet of things (IoT), Material Design, Android TV, virtual and augmented reality (AR / VR), Open API.

Join us and be aware of all the events!



25.09 - Tech Day


On Tech Day, September 25, professional developers will tell in their reports about new features in Play and Android; will provide a checklist of what everyone should do now for the stable operation of Android Marshmallow applications; share experience solving problems arising in the interaction between threads in Android; they will describe the look at the design from the developer‚Äôs side and show how, without understanding the design, to make pretty applications; and visitors will learn how to integrate NFC payments and how to live with Dagger 2 in combat.



26.09 - Business Day


Business Day, September 26, will be held in the format of reports devoted to building a business, marketing, management, creation, development and promotion of products in the Android ecosystem.



09/27 - Community Day


A feature of Community Day, September 27, will be live communication between producers, programmers and testers, as well as representatives of game projects and potential investors with the aim of sharing experience, life hacking, contacts and ideas.



BARCAMP and DEMOCAMP


BARCAMP takes place in the afternoon of the third day: the developers in a free format present their successful cases, projects, ideas and discuss them with the conference participants. At DEMOCAMP, previously selected teams present their projects to the best experts and VC. For each speech given 2 minutes + questions and advice from experts and investors.
