Facebook Developer Hack in Moscow

Finally in Moscow will be a real Facebook Hack! Yes, in the format in which Facebook hackathons are held worldwide.



Facebook Developer Hack Moscow will be the final event in the World Hack series.
So - October 1, Moscow , you and Facebook. This is your chance to go to California!

The event starts with a technical session. Leading Facebook engineers - Simon ross, Ali Parr (games), Stephane Crozatier and Connor Treacy (open graph), Tom Elliott (mobile) will tell you about the Facebook Open Graph, the mobile platform, and help you prepare for the hack session.

For the next 6 hours, you or your team are working on creating a cool app that can be your ticket to San Francisco.
Facebook Developers Hack is a chance to meet and work with your colleagues, as well as to have a live chat with a team of engineers from Facebook.

At the end of the event there will be a demonstration of the participants' works and the awarding of the winners who created the best applications in various categories.
