Fur.ly - create a presentation from sites


Three days ago I came across a curious link reduction service - Fur.ly. The search on Habra did not give any results, so I decided to write a small topic that demonstrates the capabilities of the link reducer. The task of the service is to provide users with the ability to make small presentations from sites, reducing several links into one. Then all the links will be presented in the form of a small online presentation.
