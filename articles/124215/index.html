Fast site creation with .NET Forge CMS and Web Platform Installer, part 1



More recently, 1C-Bitrix announced the release of a new content management system called .NET Forge CMS , which is presented in two editions, including in the form of a free community version. This system has powerful tools for creating modern fast, functional sites for commercial and other activities on the Internet.

Other advantages of the new CMS include the following: ASP.NET platform, templates for an online store, corporate website, blog, community website, visual editor, Hermitage interface, website advertising management system, ratings, polls, galleries, forums, blogs , proactive protection against threats and hacking attempts, morphological search and built-in update system.

I am pleased to announce that .NET Forge CMS has become available for a quick unattended installation through the Web Platform Installer, among many other content management systems. This means that installing and configuring your own site has become much easier and is now available to any user.

The following describes how to use the Web Platform Installer in the shortest possible time to deploy and configure a completely ready own corporate website.
