Collect donations with donateMe (Part I)

The PaySto payment system, through which payments are made on the Internet, has a number of features due to which it can be used for various purposes. One such feature is the ability to raise funds online, in other words, donations. Donations can be collected both by individual users, for their personal purposes, and by entire organizations.


What is the general idea - to collect funds from the Internet with complete strangers who are not connected with the initiator of fees by any obligations? In fact, there are a lot of reasons for fees. This assistance to sick children or orphanages, and donations to other noble goals. There are also startups on the Web who gather people with a dream on their websites. Users describe their dreams and indicate the amount needed for their implementation. Other community members can help them with money and in this way contribute to the implementation of plans.


In order to put the fees from Internet users in this way into life, websites need to integrate some kind of tool that would help transfer money from donor accounts to recipient accounts. It is the creators of PaySto that offer such a tool ‚Äî donateMe service for individual donations and FundingTo for group donations.

It is important that PaySto employees focus on the ease of use of the service and on the fact that for people who collect donations it is only necessary to integrate the tool into their websites. They do not need to have special knowledge in programming and creating new web pages.

Those who wish to help with money for a particular charitable idea can choose the method of payment and the type of currency convenient for them when they make a contribution. Already in the PaySto system, the funds will be converted into the currency that the recipients need.


On the recipient's website, PaySto will help to integrate a special button or form, according to which donators can go to the donateMe / FundingTo page (depending on the type of donations and the recipient's needs). If the user needs to collect charitable contributions for a noble purpose, and he does not have his own web resource, then PaySto will help to generate a separate page on the Internet to which donators will go and transfer funds.


In order to use one of the tools for receiving donations on the Internet from the PaySto system, the user must be registered in the system itself, create its own Personal Account - an account. Let me remind you that both registration and access to PaySto are free. PaySto charges a fee for using the service, which is paid by the donor and does not constitute more than 7.5% of the payment amount.

As soon as the collector creates his PaySto account, donateMe will immediately become available to him.
