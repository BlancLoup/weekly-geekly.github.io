Xiaomi 3D Printer - First Meet

The first photos of the Xiaomi brand, famous for its low-cost productive gadgets that successfully compete with the giants of the mobile market, have appeared on the network.

The estimated price of the future device is not yet advertised, as well as its technical characteristics.

However, the reputation of Xiaomi allows us to expect that a 3D printer will be no worse than quality and more affordable than competing counterparts. And its compact and rather nice design assumes, most likely, purchase by fans for house use.


Considering the specifics of the manufacturer, one should not exclude some probability that a new 3D printer will be able to work under the control of mobile technology on the Android platform, directly - via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. But this, for now, is only an assumption. Wait and see.


Perhaps in the near future we will witness the emergence of a series of 3D printers from companies that had previously become famous in completely different areas.
