Remembering childhood ...

Let's dive again into the exciting moments of the purchase of the first Prefix ...
But I want everything in order.





In the days of my youth, the game halls were very popular. For example, in Gorky Park near my house there were as many as two halls, one - a trailer on eternally lowered wheels, and the second - a blue iron structure. Strange, but in the winter it was not cold in them, and in the summer it was not hot. In both "halls" were the dreams of each teenager - the coveted prefixes "SEGA MEGADRIVE II", and huge queues. Now, I am writing about it and remembering that the joysticks then seemed to be the height of ergonomics. Particularly appreciated consoles with joysticks, on which there were 6, and not 3, buttons. The additional buttons for A, B, and C (large and black) were Z, Y, and X, small and gray.


In the halls, spending unmeasured amount of pocket money, sat around the clock. When the money ran out, they stood behind the player, watched, and rejoiced with him. They prompted, sometimes they sat down next to each other and asked to play in pairs.
But at some point, realizing that it was more interesting to play at home, I asked my parents for such a prefix. They counted the amount of money spent in the halls, were horrified and decided to buy a prefix home. Bought, by very cunning manipulations with the Soviet TV, connected, and the Games began ...


And the most favorite game, when the console was already at my house, was the Comix Zone. I want her to give you a "play". The game is free, cheats and codes do not deliberately cite - in Google they are searched for once or twice. Try to go without saving, as on the console. :)
