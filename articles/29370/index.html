Meeting with Opera Software in Moscow

In connection with the arrival at the Russian iCamp, representatives of the Opera Software company took time to meet with users of the Opera browser (or generally with users of any browsers - there will be something to argue about;)). From the company will be David Storey (the leading web evangelist of the company, the kernel and development manager for Opera and Dragonfly), Alexey Feldgendler (the developer of the desktop version) and me.
