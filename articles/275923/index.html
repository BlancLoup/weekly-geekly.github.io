Welcome to the meeting Moscow.pm February 4



On February 4, Perl-programmers of the Moscow.pm community will meet again in our Moscow office. Members of the worldwide Perl Mongers Association meet regularly to discuss current issues, talk about interesting cases from practice and share experiences.
