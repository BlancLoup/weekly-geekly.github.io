Russian Conference Wolfram Technologies and Hackathon 2019



It is with great pleasure that we would like to invite you to the Russian Wolfram Technologies Conference and the hackathon, which will be held on June 10 and 11, 2019 in St. Petersburg .

Do not miss your chance to meet the developers of Wolfram Technologies and exchange ideas with other Wolfram users. The presentations will discuss the use of the Wolfram Language to increase productivity, the scalability and flexibility of Mathematica, the development of practical applications, and the integration of Wolfram technologies such as Wolfram Cloud, Wolfram | Alpha Pro, and Wolfram SystemModeler into your workflow.

Students and schoolchildren are also invited to take part in the second All-Russian Wolfram Hackath on June 10 - 11. Hackathon themes are machine learning, creative use of the Wolfram Cloud, big data.
