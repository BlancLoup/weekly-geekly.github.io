‚ÄúMicrosoft founder Bill Gates on January 6 at CES in Las Vegas gave the last keynote speech as chairman of the board of directors of the corporation. Gates from 17 years working at Microsoft full time. In July, he will transfer most of his duties to others. ‚Äù


The reasons for Gates' retirement are currently unknown. It is believed that Bill will be replaced by two people at once - Ray Ozzie, head of software architecture, and Craig Mundi, head of strategy and research. The place of the general director Bill gave to Ballmer 8 more years ago.

Without Gates, managers have a lot to do - support Windows Vista, release Windows 7, upgrade IE to the next version, etc. The last major product released under Bill, Windows Vista, was accepted without enthusiasm, Vista won the first place in the list of technical disappointments 2007.

Gates devoted his last speech to his favorite hobby - predictions. In a speech, he described the "second digital decade." During this period, all attention will be focused on connections between people, services will work on all platforms - PC, PDA, mobile phone, Internet TV, etc. Gates prophesies the serious development of high-definition video technology.
The main technology is computers mounted on a table whose surface will become a touchscreen display (Microsoft is already developing such a technology).

Information will be easier to transfer from device to device, and the user interface will become increasingly natural. The dominance of the mouse and keyboard from 1995 to 2005 will be followed by the popularity of touch control devices.

So, Bill Gates in any case did a lot for the development of information technology from the early 90s to our days. The Gates Plan will affect the corporation for a few more years, until Vista and other new products become obsolete.
