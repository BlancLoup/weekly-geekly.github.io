The publication of AACS keys is underway.

He looked into the water: websites began to appear on which keys of discs protected by AACS technology embedded in BlueRay and in HD DVD are published.

It will take only a very short time, and disk images will be distributed in P2P networks along with the keys.

One can only guess how much more money production corporations will agree to throw to the wind, before realizing the fundamental impossibility of protection from playful pens with a key that is given in these pens.

It does not matter if the key is built into the iron player, or into the program. Sooner or later it will be found and published.
