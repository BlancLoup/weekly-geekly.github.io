Some people believe that a deduction is when the calculated tax can be reduced by some amount. For whom life is good


Before we talk about how to save on taxes due to legal deductions, we make a reservation right away: deductions on personal income tax are given only for income taxed at a rate of 13%. Those. if you are an entrepreneur on a simplified payment system (UPDF), and you do not have other income, then you cannot claim a tax deduction for the apartment purchased. Or if you are one of the owners of an LLC and receive dividends from which you honestly pay 9% of income tax, you cannot take advantage of deductions. But if you work somewhere for hire (labor contract or civil law is not important), then you can get the whole range of tax breaks from the amount of earnings. And there are a lot of them. And also, if you are a citizen of another state (hello to friends from Belarus and Kazakhstan;) and live in Russia for at least 183 calendar days, then you can also receive Russian tax deductions.

Children are the flowers of life


If you have children, in addition to the standard deduction, you can get a standard deduction "for children" at work. I bought an apartment in the mortgage


Property deduction "to buy an apartment" - probably the most tasty morsel for the taxpayer. You can get it only once in your life. Apartments in residential houses, shares in houses under construction, private houses, land plots for construction fall under deduction.

If an apartment is acquired as a common property, then the deduction can be distributed in any shares between the owners. Only packages of documents will have to be collected as much as claimants for deduction.

The deduction can include expenses:
  • for the purchase of an apartment in an already built or under construction house, house, plot;

  • for the purchase of materials for finishing the apartment and for finishing work.
If you have a mortgage, then in addition to a deduction for an apartment, you can receive a deduction to pay interest on the loan, and in full without restrictions.

The maximum amount of deduction when buying an apartment - 2 000 000 p. plus the amount paid for each year of interest on the mortgage. But we must remember that the deduction for the year can not be more than you earned. The balance of the unused deduction will be carried forward to each subsequent year.
Those. if in 2009 you bought an apartment for 2,000,000 p., paid interest of 200,000 p., and in 2010 100,000 p., then in 2011, you claim a deduction of 2,000,000 p. for an apartment plus paid for 2 years 300 000 p. percent. Must return the tax 299 000 p., But in 2010 you officially earned 420 000 p. and a deduction you will be able to provide only 420 000 p. Therefore, only 56 400 p. Next year they will return the next portion of the tax, but the application procedure will have to be repeated. And so every year, until you choose the full amount of the deduction.
For simplicity of calculations, you can first choose the amount of the main deduction for an apartment, and then go to the bank every year, take inquiries about the interest paid and return only them.

You can get a deduction in two ways: in the tax (see the end of the topic - as) or from your employer.

To receive a deduction from the employer, you must again go to the tax with the same package of documents and write an application for receiving the Notice of the right to receive a property tax deduction. Next you need to write a statement in the name of the employer and with the notification attributed to work. The employer will no longer withhold income tax from your salary and will pay you 100% of the money earned. Documents required for deducting:

  • certificate of registration of ownership;
