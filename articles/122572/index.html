Get acquainted? .TEL

Dear Habravchane, we remind you that the registration of numeric and short (two-character) domains in the .TEL zone is open, which were previously unavailable for registration. New features in the .TEL domain allow you to register phone numbers, city codes and even car license plates; ) Do you adore your car? So dedicate an entire site to her! We invite you to take advantage of a 10% discount for registering domains in the .TEL zone from REG.RU.

Recall that the registration of domain names in the zone. TEL, there are some restrictions:
1. domain name must contain from 2 to 63 characters;
2. Names containing only numbers should not consist of more than 7 digits, including a hyphen. Register domains like 123456789.tel or 123-4567.tel, alas, will not succeed;
3. two-letter domains that match the name of the national first-level domains, for example, ru.tel, are not available for registration.

More than your imagination is not limited. You can even use a PIN from a credit card as a domain name, but in this case we do not guarantee that your finances will remain there for a long time;)

Instructions for obtaining a discount on registration .TEL in REG.RU :
In the special field, enter the promo code WEEK-TEL and click "Activate". Select the account to which you want to apply the discount, and click "Apply discount". You can use the promotional code from June 23 to June 30, 2011.
