About the report


Before the report and during the break, I was able to meet and talk a bit with the heroes of the previous meeting , Kirill and Alexander. The report consisted of two and a half parts with a break between them. Everything was told by Alexey (I assumed that the first part would do one, the second - the other), Artyom talked to the audience during the break and helped answer questions at the end of the report.

The first part was given a little theory and prerequisites for distributed load testing - PTDD (Performance Test Driven Development), test scripts and metrics, a stack of tools. It is told about the self-developed library NanoCloud . Much more about her, Alexey told half a year ago (June 4, 2015), also on jug.msk.ru , video part 1 and part 2 .



The second part included a review of various possible test scenarios with the display of code examples. This was followed by answers to questions from the audience. Finally, it was proposed to join the development of projects laid out on GitHub (links to them are given on the penultimate slide of the presentation).
