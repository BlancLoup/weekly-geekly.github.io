Choosing a name for a new Russian operating system

I propose to think about the name for the new operating system, about plans to create which the government regularly declares. I propose to call the new system "MedvedOS 10"!

1) The image of a bear both inside and outside the country is perfect as a symbol of Russia.

2) The word form "Medved" will give the bear image a unique computer-Internet orientation.

3) Dmitry Medvedev regularly demonstrates his enthusiasm for high-tech topics to the public: iPhone, iPad, digital cameras. He even went to visit Kaspersky!

Therefore, I believe that the meaningful hint in the name of the new operating system for the ex-president of Russia will also be in the subject.

4) Why 10? Firstly, there have already been several known attempts to create your own OS in Russia. And secondly, this is a clear reminder of the price offered to pay for its creation to Russian IT companies .
