Steven Spielberg engaged in video games

Eminent filmmaker Steven Spielberg (Steven Spielberg) decided to try out a new industry for himself - soon as many as three video games will be created under his authority and with the assistance of Electronic Arts.

The first is called LMNO and is designed for the Xbox 360 and PlayStation3 gaming consoles. In it, the player will have to become a woman (more precisely, her educator), whose abilities will develop depending on interaction with the outside world. A truly mysterious description.

Another game, called PQRS, will be released for Wii consoles and will be similar to the board game Jenga , in which the players' task is to build turrets from rectangular wooden blocks, and the loser is the one with the collapse (probably the closest Russian version flywheels ).

Details about new games may appear on July 16.
