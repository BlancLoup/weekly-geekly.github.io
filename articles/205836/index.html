Servers free until the end of the year

We decided to do what we had never done before, in honor of the upcoming holidays, to give Habr users the opportunity to test our Intel Xeon E3-1230 / 8GB DDR3 / 4x1TB SATA2 / 100Mbps Unmetered DC Configuration Server EvoSwitch (Netherlands) by the end of this year for free. Rules - under the cut.

The terms are simple. You register in our billing and write in the comments to this post your login and operating system that you need (or the standard configuration: CentOS 6 + RAID10 + ISPmanager Lite). Within 1-2 days (depending on the current download), we will prepare the server, tie it up in the billing and issue access. Validity will be set to 01/01/2014. If you decide to continue using the equipment - just replenish the account and renew. If you do not want to - just notify us, and we will release the server. All servers that are not renewed as of January 1, 2014 will be disabled.

Special conditions

  1. Only servers of the Unix family can be installed on the servers provided for the promotion.
  2. RAID is configured only for the standard configuration with ISPmanager, the other servers are provided without RAID.
  3. When ordering any paid add-ons (iron upgrades, additional IPs, etc.), the free test period is considered complete, and the user must pay according to the standard conditions from the date of the upgrade.
  4. If during the free use period there are complaints about the server provided, we reserve the right to:
    • upon receipt of the third complaint concerning copyright infringement or entering into any black lists, refuse to provide equipment free of charge and demand payment of the balance of the days for the current month and the next month according to our rules;
    • upon receipt of a complaint related to spamming, attacks of any kind, etc. request payment according to our rules or completely refuse to provide the service (depending on the seriousness of the complaint).
  5. The number of servers offered for the promotion is limited.

We also want to draw attention to the fact that Leaseweb plans to change the configuration of the servers based on the Intel Xeon E3-1230 processor from January and to offer them with 4x500GB disks instead of 4x1TB. According to preliminary data, the price will remain unchanged. Hurry up to get the best configuration for the same money.

UPD.: Due to the fact that some of the servers are now on upgrade, today servers will be issued up to the user DonecVlad. Up to 35 more servers will be issued within the next week.
