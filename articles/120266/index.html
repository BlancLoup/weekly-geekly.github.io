Assembly and adjustment of iron


Suppose that everything above you already have and you can start drilling, soldering and twisting.
We make rama who is in that much, the main thing is that the distance from the center to the ends of the rays is the same, the propellers do not touch each other and the central part (plywood) as they rotate, as there will be brains, sensors and possibly a camera on it. It is better to install the sensors on a thick adhesive tape, silicone or rubber, to reduce vibration. And now the attention of the shoals, about which you can stumble out of ignorance and the necessary information:
  • The receiver must be paired with the transmitter - it is written in the instructions
  • Speed ‚Äã‚Äãcontrollers (ESC) need to be calibrated! To do this, we connect together all 4 ESC: ground (black) and signal wire (white or yellow) and leave ONE power wire (red), then insert it into the third channel of the receiver, and also connect the power wires and connect the motors to the regulators. After that, with the transmitter turned on, we put the gas knob (the third channel) to the maximum position (up) and connect the power supply to the speed regulators, the motors cheerfully squeak, then lower the gas knob down and the motors squeak again - hurray, we calibrated the regulator
  • Often, the speed regulators need to be programmed, read the instructions for their regulators, the main point is to increase the reaction speed by increasing the frequency and setting the instant response mode, rather than a gradual set of revolutions. This can be done both by listening to endless squeaking, and using special cards for programming .
  • To the motors rotated in the other direction, you need to change any two wires in some places
  • Last but not least, put the propellers - they cut their hands very well
  • Motors, like propellers, often require balancing, to reduce vibration, to do this, take the usual cable ties and rotate 1-2 on the motor and turn it on until you feel the minimum vibration. In more detail, as well as the video is better to read on a specialized forum
