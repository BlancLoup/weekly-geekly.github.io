/ dev / null as a Service

In the modern world, everything is a service. You can create documents , upload photos , deploy computers , but what happens to the garbage? It accumulates and takes everything around. That is why we run / dev / null in the cloud.

IAAS , SAAS , PAAS ?
We call our brainchild DAAS.

You no longer have to get rid of unnecessary data yourself. Use our distributed service located in more than 380 locations! It does not matter whether you use it for personal purposes or for business. Tariffs are available even for entire corporations.

Who are we?
We are a young, dynamic company consisting of careless data-scientists who could not get a job.
Our experience in deleting data is more than amazing! Over the years, we have deleted a lot of important data at home and even at work. Photos from the rest, important invoices, registration data - everything was lost!
Therefore, we are doing what we are exactly good at.

How to use it?
We provide a simple, understandable and well-documented API. Just make an HTTP POST request to our REST server, and our BigData distributed cluster will take care of your data! 
$ curl -d @invoice.pdf -X POST http://devnull-as-a-service.com/dev/null

Or, if you prefer wget, use: 
 $ wget -O- -v --post-file=backups.tar.gz http://devnull-as-a-service.com/dev/null
