It turns out that the telecom operator can change the terms of the contract only by concluding an additional agreement in writing or by such subscriber actions that explicitly indicate agreement with the changes. The operator cannot change the terms of the contract unilaterally.

Section 18. Advertising Distributed by Telecommunication Networks
