GLONASS supports all mobile devices on Snapdragon S2 and Snapdragon S3 platforms.

Not so long ago, the Internet was excited by the news that the Iphone 4S supports GLONASS.
After that, discussions were actively conducted on numerous websites and forums - is it true, incomprehensible PR or outright nonsense.
And finally, comrades from Qualcomm said that the GLONASS satellite navigation system is supported by all mobile devices on the Snapdragon S2 and Snapdragon S3 platforms. The chipsets of these families are dual-system and can work with both GPS and GLONASS. However, the final decision on whether to include support for the Russian system on the device is made by the manufacturer.
The Snapdragon-based GLONASS satellites are called upon when the GPS signal is not strong enough. This scheme allows you to save battery power, as well as improve the accuracy of positioning up to two meters.
Some Qualcomm communication chipsets can also work with the GLONASS system - in particular, the MDM6610, which is used in the iPhone 4S smartphone.
