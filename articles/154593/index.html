Lego will release your designer if 10,000 fans vote for him

The project Ideas.Lego was created so that anyone could offer their designer. And if he gets more than 10,000 likes, Lego will release it. The author will receive 1% of sales.



Habrayuzer, like normal geeks, like Lego . In the crisis years for the company from 1993 to 2004, it was the geeks who saved lego, buying up Mindstorms . The company does not stand still and all the time comes up with something new, including in the field of computers, on the Internet and in social networks.

It's nice that our favorite company accepts crowdsourcing as a modern tool.
 

Short story


The project was launched in 2008. Since then, only 3 constructors have been released:
  • Shinkai 6500 , the Japanese adore their scientific submarine , capable of diving to a depth of 6500 meters,

Several projects scored the required 10,000 votes, but were rejected by Lego. For example: the sets for the film "Zombie by the name of Sean" and the TV series "Firefly".

There are several projects that have scored 10,000 votes but have not yet passed the test of the company. rules




Participation rules are as follows:
  1. We first register on the site so that you can vote, discuss and add your projects.
  2. Then we invent our project and upload it to the site.
  3. How do we want to collect 10,000 votes from registered on the site (you can register through accounts on Facebook or Twitter).
  4. If the project has scored ten thousand, it does not mean that the designer goes into production. Lego team must check and decide whether to release or not.

A constructor may be rejected for several reasons:
  • the presence in the plot of a positive image of bad habits, such as alcoholism (the film "Zombie named Sean"),
  • violence and other non-childish joys,
  • but the main indicator is the lack of playability.
