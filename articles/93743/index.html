GeForce GTX465 test results

A few days ago, the Chinese from the eNet resource tested the GeForce GTX465. From the site itself, the test results were quickly removed, but the pictures managed to crawl through the Internet.

The new product from nVidia, of course, was immediately compared with its competitors. According to the tests, it turned out that the card, which is expected to appear on the shelves on June 1, is slightly faster than HD 5870 in Far Cry 2, but slower than HD 5830 in Crysis Warhead. In Unigine's Heaven benchmark, the map ranked between HD 5850 and HD 5870.

The video card has 352 stream processors and 1 GB of GDDR5 memory. The core, shader and memory clock frequencies are 607, 1215 and 3206 MHz.
