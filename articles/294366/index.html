Why don't they read me: 12 ways to make the audience love the site



We understand how media and content-oriented platforms should improve efficiency.

Make a mobile version

In 2015, it is a shame not to have a mobile version of the site. More than half of all Internet users in the world surf from a smartphone, phone or tablet. In Russia, the Internet is used monthly by 82 million people. Of these, 50 million use mobile gadgets. 14% of Runet users access the network only from mobile. Without a mobile version, you lose millions of potential users.

Give content

Wrote 100 articles - select the top 10, issue an e-book and give it in exchange for a like, sharing a page on social networks or in exchange for an e-mail address.



The method is called content upgrades. Its effectiveness is confirmed by many successful cases. Site owners increased the conversion by 100 times , and some even more .

Get the newsletter

Once a week, send to subscribers all the new things that appear on your site. If you publish a lot of content, choose the best. This channel does not take you much time and effort, but will increase attendance. Megaplan receives almost 17% of all traffic to the site from email .

Always add a picture

Posts with pictures get 42% more clicks than posts without pictures.



When sharing on Facebook or VKontakte, a link with a picture will automatically turn into a snippet - a large image, on clicking on which the site opens. Go over it more convenient.
