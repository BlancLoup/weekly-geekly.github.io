The story of how I saw the light


For 10 years I wore lenses with myopia -4 diopters and thought about the correction, but at one moment clicked, made, pleased as an elephant. With my myopia it costs up to 100 mp.

Turnkey SuperFemtoLASIK (May 2019g). But happiness full pants.

Immediately I will make a disclaimer: I do not have medical education, a wealth of experience in operations and commercial motivation to write this article. I describe this experience for people who still doubt and are looking for answers to their problems on the Internet.
I will also use common abbreviations. Information about the essence of the methods is complete on other resources. He also did not call the medication, so that no one would guess to prescribe it for himself.

Prerequisites


Glasses put on me in elementary school (-2 diopters), by the ninth grade I have already switched to two-week lenses (-3.5 diopters). At the end of the institute, relatives / acquaintances began to incite correction, but then it seemed to me to spend the cost of 3-4 smartphones, or a good trip to Europe is too much. So I went to lick, until the changes began: a close relative seriously ill and the treatment of oncology cost a lot of money. Two years later, everything ended well, but another fell ill and everything started anew. At this point in the brain clicked that health is not something to save on. My 30 years are already behind and it's time to do my own health. At the same time began to strain the wearing of contact lenses. A state of emergency happened once a quarter: when I accidentally scratched my eyelid, the lens ran through the eyeball and it was hard to get it, not to mention the sensations at that moment. One time it was driving, another time away from home and it was difficult to return. The sensation of weakness was frustrating.

And I will never have again:

  • I can not go to the airport at carsharing at 5 am, because unable to stick lenses in eyes.
  • I can not stay overnight, because did not take the solution or spare kit
  • I can not nap during the day / on the road, because the lenses stick and must be soaked after waking up
  • I can not drive, because sick and can't wear lenses
  • see clearly more than 12 hours a day without eye injury
  • emerge without sight and think which side is the coast.

Of course, I can no longer stand in the smoke of the fire. But I think I will survive.

Training


I came across a series of articles about SMILE - a new generation technique, and most importantly requiring mandatory certification of doctors, caught fire on her. Found a list of accredited clinics, considering it a panacea, and began to wool prices. It turned out to do in Moscow time more expensive by 20-30% (there are exceptions). He came to NN on the morning swallow and immediately to the clinic, there are 4-5 hours of examination: checking the alphabet, electronic checking (picture with a balloon), a few more devices, one of which abruptly puffed air into the eye. Rehabilitation period


Day after surgery - to survive, endure, sleep. Usually, everything is much simpler and even try to blunt the cell phone.

Three days - sleep on your back, avoid screens, do not wash your hair (I washed gently), drip, sleep.

Two weeks - do not lift weights (5-10 kg), no sports, gymnastics, avoid high pressure, dust, aerosols, steam.

Month - a strict ban on baths, saunas, gravity, swimming. Hypothermia, colds, any viral infections. Injuries, contact sports.

Then you can all!
