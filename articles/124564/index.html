Idea


The idea does not pretend to be unique, but I wanted to create my own youth Internet radio, where besides music there will be live communication, where they will have their RJs with good diction and high-quality sense of humor, where there will be no rigid censorship framework and there will be little advertising.

Music

One of the most important aspects when creating your own radio station is the style of music. Since I and my like-minded people like dance music, we chose this direction accordingly, it is quite extensive and if you turn all the tracks in a row, you would have received a vinaigrette, and this could kill the listeners' interest. Therefore, it was decided to split the day into different styles of dance music:
  • in the afternoon - popular club music (Electro, House);
  • in the evening - music, under which it works well and surfs on the Internet (Trance);
  • early at night (23:00 - 01:00), when listeners are going to sleep - calm, relaxing music (Lounge, Dub);
  • late at night - music to stay awake (Drum'n'Bass, Techno, Hard Style);
  • morning - the immortal hits of the 80s, 90s, 00x with modern processing or without it.

Radio show

The focus is on high-quality music, but much attention should also be paid to radio programs, because we are creating an interactive radio station. Conducting the show should be with a humor. Gear Ideas:
  • Greetings and congratulations (2 broadcasts every day);
  • News feed about club events (1 time per week);
  • Radio show with DJ known and not very (2-3 times a week);
  • Humorous program about cars, in the style of "Top Gear" (1 time per week);
  • For those over 20 (once a week, after midnight).

Technical implementation

To start the radio station use a dedicated server on which the software for broadcasting non-stop music with the ability to connect to live broadcasts will be installed.
On a computer that is live, software must be installed with the ability to work with a microphone. Make the possibility of calls to the studio (Skype, mobile phone), communication in ICQ, as well as through the feedback form on the site.
Creating a site on which there will be a grid of airs, voting for the tracks, a poster, as well as a feedback form to communicate with the radio host.
The main criterion when choosing a provider for a dedicated server is a wide return channel. When broadcasting with a quality of 192 kbps. for 1000 listeners, the channel must be at least 188 Mbit / s.

Money issue

We all understand that you have to pay for everything. From servers and domain name to salary RJ. With bare enthusiasm and with an empty wallet, something is unlikely to work out, so at first we were ready to pay money from our own pockets and work in the ‚Äúminus‚Äù until advertisers appear on the horizon. Here it is important to promote, to bring listeners, and the work of RJ, so that the listener wants to return. We understood this and tried to approach the work with responsibility.
The main goal we set to let all the money in the development of the radio station, and not the purchase of Mercedes and Lexus.
