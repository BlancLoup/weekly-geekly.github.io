Pizza Hut introduced the concept of a touchscreen table for ordering pizza (video)


Ordering pizza at a pizzeria is a fairly routine process. It is necessary to study the menu, agree with friends, what kind of pizza to take. Oh yeah, you still need to wait for the waiter to take your order from you. Or no longer needed?
In the official YouTube account of the famous Pizza Hut chain of pizzerias, a video showing a touch table for pizza order has appeared. The buyer will be able to choose the size of the pizza, the stuffing and everything else. You just need to drive your fingers on the table, no slow waiters, no queues (except to this very table). The project is carried out jointly with Chaotic Moon Studios . It is not clear when such tables will appear in a nearby diner, but the technology is already existing. So in the near future, coming to the cafe / pizzeria, it will be quite possible to stumble upon something similar. But besides convenience, such a table is an excellent marketing ploy! Will you go to such a pizzeria? I would go, for example.
