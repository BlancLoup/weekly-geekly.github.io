T-shirt for ECG from Russia

At an exhibition held in Moscow, the forum came across an interesting project from the category of wearable electronics. This is a T-shirt that makes an electrocardiogram. I have written more than once about similar projects (for example, here ) but about Russian projects for the first time.



Appearance - not the most glamorous. But the T-shirt is intended not for athletes, but for medical purposes - for example, for people who suffer from heart problems, obesity, and so on. For patient clinics. In the first photo - the cheapest version, disposable T-shirt for 500 rubles.


These - reusable, about they can be washed in the machine. Theoretically, it can be made more beautiful, but more expensive.



Not without the main device, the ECG apparatus itself, fastened to the belt. It is not particularly large, but costs about 3 thousand rubles.



All data is sent when connected to the computer's usb port in the patient profile, where it is correlated with other medical research results. Unfortunately, blood tests and other tests made in any clinics will have to be driven in manually.
