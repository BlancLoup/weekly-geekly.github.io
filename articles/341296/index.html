Construction of the park "Zaryadie" began in 2015. In Moscow, this is the first large-scale project in more than 50 years to create a unique space near the Kremlin walls at the junction of a diverse natural landscape, high technology and architecture. Zaryadie, the first park built within the boundaries of the Boulevard Ring, has already become the center of attraction for residents and guests of the capital.

History of Charges
The name of the park comes from the name of one of the oldest districts of Moscow, Zaryadya, whose history goes back over 8 centuries. At various times, Zaryadie was the center of commerce, the embassy quarter, and even slums.

In the twentieth century it was supposed to place here one of Moscow's skyscrapers, but the project was not completed. In 1967, the Hotel Russia was built on the foundation laid, at that time the largest in the world.

At the beginning of the XXI century, the Moscow government decided to dismantle a huge, heavily dilapidated and obsolete building. In 2006 the hotel was closed and dismantled. In its place it was planned to build a new hotel and office complex with parking. But in early 2012, Russian President Vladimir Putin announced that a modern park would be set up in the historic Zaryadye district.

In the first stage of the international competition for the creation of a landscape-architectural concept of the park, applications from 27 countries, from 420 companies united in 90 consortia, were considered. The competition jury included leading specialists in architecture and urban planning, among them Saskia Sassen, a professor at Columbia University, Anthony Vives-i-Thomas, vice-mayor of Barcelona, ‚Äã‚Äãand Marta Thorn, executive director of the Pritzker Prize. The victory was won by an international consortium, which included architects from the Diller Scofidio + Renfro bureau (New York), landscape workshop specialists from Hargreaves Associates (New York), and urbanists from Citymakers (Moscow).
Construction of the park "Zaryadie" began in 2015. In 2017, the construction of the park was completed.
