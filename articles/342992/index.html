TgGram - website creation service for / from telegram channels

Good day.

As you know, the content published in telegrams is not indexed by search engines. I developed a service, tggram.com , which automatically creates sites for telegram channels.

Just a few examples:

The site is created in one click. You need to go to telegrams, and add the @BlogFatherBot bot as an administrator, with a minimum set of rights. The bot will automatically track activity in your channel and publish new posts on username.tggram.com

On the site, in addition to the tape posts are available:

- rss feed for reading the channel
- the possibility of sharing any post
- the ability to add your site to Yandex.DZen
- widget likes / comments on social networks
- also, good sites, growing - continuously rotated in the catalog telegram channels, on tggram.com

All worries about hosting and website promotion are on the service.

- Your channel will be able to read by users who do not have telegrams.
- Your channel will be able to find users who have telegrams
- The site will increase the audience of your channel and the number of subscribers.

The service is free. And I promise that annoying casino banners will not appear.

How it works?


After adding a bot to the channel ... nothing happens)
The bot sits in the channel and patiently waits for the next post. No need to write a test test, unfortunately the bot does not see the removal of posts, only the addition and editing of posts.

After the next publication in the channel - the site will be created (as soon as the first post appears)
The next day, the site will be added to the main page of the tggram.com catalog.
After some time, the site will be indexed and start working on the channel.
