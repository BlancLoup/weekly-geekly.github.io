FRIA plans to launch a training course on how to conduct Internet business in more than 100 Russian universities

The Internet Initiatives Development Foundation ( IIDF ) has developed a special training course on launching and managing Internet projects. This course will be launched in September of this year in 22 universities of our country.

Among them are universities such as Moscow State University. Lomonosov Moscow State Technical University. Bauman, HSE , ITMO University , Ural Federal University , Financial University under the Government of the Russian Federation and other educational institutions of Russia. The FRIA will also be supported by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation.
