Anonymous will launch their MegaUpload

Anonymous, who stirred up the whole world with attacks on computer systems of such serious institutions as the FBI and the US Department of Justice (and a large number of others), are going to launch their analogue MegaUpload file-exchange service in the near future, the closure of which caused the actual information war.

Yesterday, anonymous tweet appeared on the official twitter,

from which, in fact, it became known about the launch of the project AnonyUpload . Now it has Donate-button installed and everyone is invited to contribute to the creation of the site. At the same time, its study makes it possible to note a curious feature: the group plans, in order to avoid harassment by the US authorities, to host the project‚Äôs servers in Russia. At the same time, the role of the arrested head of MegaUpload, Kim Dotcom, is recognized as so significant that a separate block on the website is dedicated to him with gratitude for his work.

So far, only FAQ is working on the site, from which the details of the future project and the motives of its creators are clarified. First of all, the project will be free, anonymous (refers to access via FTP) and without advertising, because hacktivists believe that file sharing should be free. Secondly, the appearance of some kind of Anon-Manager software is expected, whose functions are not yet clear (one can only guess from the name). And thirdly, precisely because the site does not plan to make money, Anonymous and asked to assist them in the preparation of its infrastructure.
