Charging

The battery on the 3400 keeps the phone in working condition for three days. How the battery works with full load - read below. Perhaps this can immediately close the topic, except that it is necessary to tell about two small nuances. First, I noticed that with active use, the last 10% of the battery capacity is practically not charged. So upon reaching 90% you can safely turn off. The difference is small. And secondly, with the upgrade to Window Phone 8.1, the phone began to complain about the insufficient power of the current source, regardless of whether the device was powered from the outlet, from the MacBook or from the power bank. What is the reason I did not understand. Charged, worked. The rest was no problem.

Photo and video shooting

Results

Nokia Lumia 1520 is really a high-quality and thoughtful device. I, unfortunately, will not take it myself, but I recommend it to those who want to leave the camp of iOS fans.
I myself will refrain why: out of the obvious flaws for today - the keyboard "point" and an extremely small library of applications and games in the Windows Market. Perhaps these are two key factors for me personally.

But the advantages of the phone are much greater. This is a popular form factor, high-quality build, nice design, stable and easy to learn multitasking operating system, excellent battery, great camera; and standard applications cover 99% of the needs of the average user.
